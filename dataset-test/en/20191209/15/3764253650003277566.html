Tonto Dikeh shares wish to be with child, says 'it's time to get pregnant'

09 Dec 2019, 15:26

- Nigerian actress Tonto Dikeh has stated that she wants to be pregnant again

- The actress shared her wish on the issue on her Instagram page

- Dikeh noted that she was pure when she was pregnant with her first child

Popular Nigerian actress Tonto Dikeh has taken to social media to talk about her desire to have a child again. The actress shared this desire on a post on her instagram page.

The actress shared a no-makeup photo of herself showing a pimple on her face. In her caption, she noted that her first pregnancy took away all her impurities.

According to her, she was super excited and grateful for the impurities her first pregnancy took away. She said that now that she is without a child she is back to fighting a very stubborn pimple.
