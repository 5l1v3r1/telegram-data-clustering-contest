Ant McPartlin and Anne-Marie Corbett enjoy date in Oz ahead of I'm A Celeb final

The I'm A Celeb presenter enjoyed a well-deserved afternoon away from the jungle as he treated his girlfriend Anne-Marie to a takeaway lunch date

09 Dec 2019, 15:04 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin finally took a break from his busy I'm A Celebrity jungle presenting duties to enjoy some well-deserved alone time with his girlfriend.

The TV presenter, 44, was snapped enjoying a romantic lunch date with his girlfriend Anne-Marie Corbett, 42, as he headed out ahead of Sunday night's I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! final.

The couple ventured slightly out of town from the set of I'm A Celeb and enjoyed a takeaway Japanese meal in the seaside town of Carbarita in New South Wales, Australia.

The telly favourite made the most of his TV downtime as he treated his girlfriend to a relaxed beach day in the sun-soaked holiday destination.

Photographers snapped away as Ant affectionately placed his hand on Anne-Marie's back as they patiently waited for their food and happily chatted.

The loved-up pair looked beach-ready as they sat on a wall outside the Japanese food stop, waiting for their lunch to be cooked.

Ant showed evidence of their chilled day on the golden beach as sand covered his legs, while he looked as if he had been for a dip in the sea, wearing floral-patterned swimwear.

The presenter protected himself from Australian's blistering heat with a casual T-shirt, while he wore a pair of designer Gucci flip-flops on his feet.

Anne-Marie also opted for fashionable beach gear as she showed glimpses of a red bikini hidden under a casual blue shirt dress with a subtle floral embroidered design.

The beauty kept her golden locks away from her face with a pretty plaited bun while she shielded her eyes from the sunshine with a pair of stylish-looking aviator shades.

Ant puffed on his vape while he kicked back on the wall and waited for his lunch.

According to The Sun , the couple then headed to their car before taking the 50-minute journey back to the palatial Versace Hotel where the celebs and their families have been staying while Down Under for the ITV jungle adventure.

Ant and Anne-Marie touched down in Oz nearly one month ago as he prepared to reprise his presenting role alongside his partner Dec Donnelly after he stepped down from his TV duties last year amid his mental health battle.

This Morning sweetheart Holly Willoughby stepped up to the challenge when she braved the snake-infested jungle as Ant's replacement.

Ant and Anne-Marie have been inseparable since sparks started flying between the duo.

Their love has been going strong but they keep their romance out of the limelight where possible.
