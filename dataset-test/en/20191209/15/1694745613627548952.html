Jennifer Lopez Brought Her Versace Jungle Dress Back *Again* For Her SNL Monologue

09 Dec 2019, 15:10 by Karenna Meredith

Jennifer Lopez fully celebrated her unbelievable 2019 during Saturday Night Live on Dec. 7, and of course, she couldn't forget to mention her iconic Versace jungle-print dress, which she resurrected earlier this year. While delivering her funny monologue, the 50-year-old pop star and actress cracked jokes about the fashion moment that shook the internet.

"I walked the runway in Milan for the first time wearing a dress I wore 20 years ago. Some people said I look better now than I did then!" she said. "I'm not bragging! That's just, you know, gossip." To top it all off, she made a quick change into the actual gown while singing "Santa Claus Is Coming to Town" with the Rockettes. See? This is exactly why J Lo can't be beat!

Jennifer's homage to her green gown is a throwback in more ways than one. She actually wore the statement outfit when she hosted SNL for the first time, back in 2001.
