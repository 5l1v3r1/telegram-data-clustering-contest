Pirates To Spend The Week Away  

09 Dec 2019, 15:20 by Soccer Laduma

Following Orlando Pirates’ 2-2 draw with Baroka FC, which according to coach Rulani Mokwena felt like a loss, the Buccaneers will spend the rest of the week away from Gauteng to prepare for their last match of the year.

Pirates played Baroka in Limpopo at the Peter Mokaba Stadium on Saturday. As confirmed to the Siya crew by sources in the know, Bucs will spend the week in Mbombela as part of the team's preparations for the last match of the year, which will be against Black Leopards next Saturday.

Bucs will be expected to return to Johannesburg at the end of the week to finalise preparations for the match at Orlando Stadium.

“Pirates have travelled to Mpumalanga to prepare for their last match of the year. You can call it a team building exercise of some sort,” said the source.
