The Dubai Mall is holding a huge sale this weekend

Expect up to 75 percent off at the three-day super sale

09 Dec 2019, 15:37 by Darragh Murphy

Still got plenty of Christmas shopping to do? You’re in luck, as malls across Dubai are hosting a massive sale this weekend.

The first edition of Dubai Festivals and Retail Establishment’s (DFRE) ‘Gift Season’ is offering up to 75 percent off across 1,500 shops, along with a host of promotions and activities to celebrate the festive season.

Kicking off on Thursday December 12 until Saturday December 14, shops around some of Dubai’s biggest malls will have discounts ranging from 25 percent to 75 percent, with items including fashion and furniture, to cosmetics and electronics.

While the stores have yet to be revealed, a number of stores have confirmed their participation, according to DFRE.

This is the first Gift Season event in Dubai, which comes perfectly-timed before Christmas. Now you can get gifts for family and friends without draining your bank account.

Up for more Christmas festivities? If you’re after a bit of exercise, check out Dubai Festival City’s Santa Run this weekend.
