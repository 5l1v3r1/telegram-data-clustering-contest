Shweta Basu Prasad Officially Announces Her Split With Husband, Rohit Mittal, Exactly A Year after Marriage

09 Dec 2019, 15:22 by Gretel Sequeira

While B-town has been witnessing a lot of break-ups and separations, yet another marriage has come to an end. Makdee actress Shweta Basu Prasad who tied the knot with fiancé Rohit Mittal in December last year took to her social media to officially announce her split

Actress Shweta Basu Prasad who has charmed us with her roles as a child actor in many films has won hearts. For a long time, the actress has made a big name on television and constantly keeps fans posted about her personal life. In the world of social media, the Makdee actress who recently got married in December last year has announced her split with husband Rohit Mittal on social media. While B-town has been witnessing a lot of break-up and separations, yet another marriage has come to an end, and Shweta wishes the best for Rohit Mittal in his life.

SpotboyE.com had exclusively broke the news of Shweta Basu Prasad getting engaged to Rohit Mittal last year. While the couple tied the knot on December 13, last year, not even a year into marriage, Shweta Basu Prasad took to her Instagram to announce the news of her separation to husband Rohit Mittal. Breaking out the news to fans on social media, the Badrinath Ki Dulhania actress shared that she and husband Rohit Mittal have mutually decided to part ways and end their marriage and wrote, “ Hi everyone, Rohit Mittal and I have mutually decided to part our ways and end our marriage.” Also Read: Shweta Basu Prasad Is Now Mrs Rohit Mittal- Wedding Pics Inside

Shweta, then added how after months of contemplation, she and her better half arrived at this decision, and wrote, “After months of contemplation, we arrived at this decision a few months ago in each other’s best interests, as individuals.” Hinting towards her broken marriage and acceptance over it, the actress wrote, “Not every book is supposed to be read cover to cover, that doesn't mean the book is bad, or one can’t read, some things are just best unfinished. Thank you Rohit for the irreplaceable memories and always inspiring me. Have a great life ahead, forever your Cheerleader,” thanking her husband Rohit Mittal about inspiring her, the actress wished for him having a great life ahead.
