Old Dominion hires Penn State assistant Rahne as head coach

09 Dec 2019, 15:25 by RALPH D. RUSSO

Old Dominion has hired Penn State offensive coordinator Ricky Rahne as its head coach.

Rahne has been offensive coordinator at Penn State the last two seasons and on coach James Franklinâ€™s staffs with the Nittany Lions and Vanderbilt since 2011.

The 39-year-old former Cornell quarterback has never been a head coach.
