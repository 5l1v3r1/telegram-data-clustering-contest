BJP MP Gautam Gambhir Writes to Delhi Government Over Anaj Mandi Fire Incident

The BJP lawmaker on Sunday had said that he did not want to get into politics, but somebody has to take responsibility for the fire incident.

09 Dec 2019, 15:39

New Delhi: A day after the tragic fire incident killed 43 people in Delhi’s Anaj Mandi area, BJP MP from East Delhi Gautam Gambhir on Monday wrote a letter to Delhi Government, saying ‘all the commercial and industrial establishments running in East Delhi constituency should suffice all licences pertaining to safety and security’.
