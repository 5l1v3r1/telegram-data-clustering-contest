All the 2020 Golden Globe nominations

09 Dec 2019, 15:46

Cape Town - The nominees for the 2019 Golden Globes were announced in Los Angeles on Monday, 9 December.

The nominees were announced at the Beverly Hilton Hotel and was streamed live on the Golden Globes Facebook page and their website.

Presenters included Dakota Fanning, Susan Kelechi Watson and Tim Allen. The stars were joined by Golden Globe ambassadors Dylan and Paris Brosnan, and Hollywood Foreign Press Association president, Lorenzo Soria.

Tom Hanks is the recipient of the 2020 Cecile B. DeMille award and Ellen DeGeneres the 2020 Carol Burnett award.

Ricky Gervais will return to host the 77th edition of the glitzy ceremony on Sunday 5 January.
