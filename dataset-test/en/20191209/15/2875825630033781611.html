Kelvin Fletcher's wife addresses her hubby's sexy dances ahead of Strictly final

The Strictly Come Dancing finalist opened up about the competition as his wife revealed the sizzling 'sensual' routine don't bother her

09 Dec 2019, 15:00 by Irishmirror.ie

Kelvin Fletcher 's wife Elizabeth Marsland has finally had her say on her husband's sizzling Strictly routines with pro dance Oti Mabuse ahead of this weekend's final.

After topping the Strictly Come Dancing leaderboard with his first 40 of the series during the semi-final, former Emmerdale star Kelvin, 35, revealed that winning is now "anyone's game."

The soap star opened up about his Strictly experience and his wife finally addressed her hubby's sensual routines during a festive family photoshoot with Hello! magazine this week.

Kelvin flashed his dazzling smile at the camera as he sat in front of a stunning Christmas tree with his beautiful wife Elizabeth, their daughter Marnie, three and adorable baby Milo, who celebrated his first birthday on Thursday.

The former Emmerdale star's dances with professional Oti have often left the judges, who have praised his "sensual hip action", flustered on the hit BBC One show.

Kelvin's wife Liz told the publication what she really thought about the routines.

She shockingly insisted: "It doesn't bother me. I'm used to seeing him kiss Britain's sexiest females on Emmerdale.

"I know the biggest part of a dance is the story and making it believable. I understand it's a performance."

The brunette beauty's understanding of her hubby's performances continued as she explained: "I can imagine it's difficult for people who aren't in this industry."

Asked about the possibility of winning the dance competition in this weekend's final, Fletcher said any of the final three are in within a chance of winning the coveted glitterball trophy.

He nervously said: "I genuinely believe it's anyone's game but it would be absolutely incredible."

The father-of-two added: "It would be the biggest accolade I've ever received."

Elizabeth, who tied the knot with Kelvin in a romantic wedding ceremony in 2015, will be supporting her hunky hubby this weekend as he battles it out on the dancefloor in the Strictly Come Dancing final.

The semi-final saw comedian Chris Ramsey narrowly miss out on the final after losing to Karim Zeroual in the dreaded Strictly dance-off.

So Kelvin will now face the final against soap star Emma Barton and her pro partner Anton du Beke and CBBC presenter Karim Zeroual and his dance partner Amy Dowden.

In the final, the remaining three couples will be tasked with performing two of their favourite routines from their time on the show.
