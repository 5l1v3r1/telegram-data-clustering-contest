December Indie World direct will be tomorrow to show new Nindies

09 Dec 2019, 15:17 by Lewis White

Nintendo’s new Nindies will be shown off tomorrow in an all-new December Indie World direct.

Starting tomorrow at 18:00 UK time / 19:00 CET, Nintendo will be showing off roughly twenty minutes of new indie games for Nintendo Switch.

The Indie World – 10.12.19 livestream will, like always, be livestreamed on the official Nintendo Switch YouTube channel.

While not a full Nintendo Direct with AAA announcements, the smaller Indie World presentations are lovely additions by Nintendo. Highlighting some of the Nintendo Switch’s most innovative and inventive video games, the Indie World directs are great companion streams for Nintendo Switch gamers.

“Tune in on December 10th for a new Indie World showcase livestreaming featuring roughly 20 minutes of information on some upcoming indie games for Nintendo Switch,” reads the description.
