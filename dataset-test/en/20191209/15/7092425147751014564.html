Duran Duran return to Ireland to play St. Anne's Park next summer

09 Dec 2019, 15:33 by Nick Fitzgerald

Tickets go on sale this Friday, December 13

Duran Duran are making a return to Ireland next summer when they play at St. Annes Park in Raheny.

The performance is scheduled for Sunday, June 7, 2020 with tickets going on sale this Friday, December 13 at 9am.

Simon Le Bon, Nick Rhodes, John Taylor and Roger Taylor announced the date in support of their next studio album set for release in spring.

The band has sold over 100 million records since their rise to fame in the 1980s. Duran Duran's Paper Gods album released in 2015 was the lads highest-charting release in 22 years.

It has been hailed by critics and fans alike as their best body of work in more than two decades.
