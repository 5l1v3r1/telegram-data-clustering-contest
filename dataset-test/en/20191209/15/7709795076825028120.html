Sporting Life Premier League Weekly Podcast: Tom Carnduff, George Pitts and Dale Tempest joined by Paul Parker

09 Dec 2019, 15:57 by Sporting Life

Sporting Life's Tom Carnduff and George Pitts are joined by Sky Bet's Dale Tempest to review all the latest Premier League events.

Duncan Ferguson had an immediate impact at Everton as they beat Chelsea 3-1 at Goodison Park. Should he get the job on a full-time basis? Or is it important that they don't get carried away in the moment?

It's been a great week for Manchester United after they secured bragging rights in the derby. We hear from former United defender Paul Parker who gives us his thoughts on the current situation.

Leicester keep on winning. Are they serious title contenders? Or would you back Liverpool going the entire season unbeaten?

We discuss all that and much more.
