Mauricio Pochettino returns home to visit Maradona despite links with United, Arsenal

09 Dec 2019, 15:38

- Mauricio Pochettino was received by Diego Maradona before a league match in the Argentine league

- The 47-year-old was relieved of his duties as Tottenham manager after poor string of results

- Pochettino has been linked with several clubs including Bayern Munich, Man United, and Arsenal

Mauricio Pochettino paid a courtesy visit to Argentine legend and former international teammate Diego Maradona.

The ex-Tottenham manager returned to his country to watch Maradona's side Gimnasia defeat Cordoba at the weekend.

Both managers greeted each other before kick off as Gimnasia earned a much-needed win having been in the bottom two of the Argentine Super League prior to the match.
