Police looking for suspects in Kitchener robbery (3 photos)

It happened Sunday at a convenience store on Doon Village Road

09 Dec 2019, 15:00 by KitchenerToday Staff

Waterloo Regional Police are looking for three suspects connected to a robbery in Kitchener.

It happened Sunday at a convenience store on Doon Village Road.

Police say two men entered the store, while a third man stood in the doorway. 

While in the store, the suspects stole an undisclosed amount of money. 

Anyone with information is asked to call police at 519-570-9777 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477.
