From In/Spectre to Plunderer: Your Winter 2020 Anime Preview Guide

09 Dec 2019, 15:12 by Keana Aguila Labra

It can be hard deciding which shows to watch for the upcoming anime season, especially with a plethora of options sure to please everyone. With that in mind, here is a Winter 2020 anime preview guide to help start your watchlist, including the likes of In/Spectre, Darwin’s Game, and The Case Files of Jeweler Richard.

Plunderer

Funimation announced on Twitter that Plunderer will premiere in January 2020 with FunimationNow under director Hiroyuki Kanbe. Plunderer is an action sci-fi series based on the manga by Suu Minazuki, who also penned Heaven's Lost Property. The anime will be produced by Geek Toys with music from Junichi Matsumoto. The official trailer can be found on Funimation's YouTube.
