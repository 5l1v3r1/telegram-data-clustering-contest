I'm A Celebrity 2019: The awkward question Lorraine asked Roman Kemp

Cringe

09 Dec 2019, 15:04 by Sian Filcher, Andrew McQuarrie

Lorraine Kelly triggered a slightly awkward moment during a chat with I'm A Celebrity contestant Roman Kemp.

The TV presenter was interviewing 26-year-old Kemp on her breakfast show this morning after he came third in the popular programme.

According to Hull Live, Lorraine was keen to find out what Kemp got up to with his girlfriend Anne-Sophie Flury after his three weeks in the jungle.

The Scottish presenter asked: “Tell me, as soon as you got back to that hotel, and you gave Anne-Sophie a hug, what else did you do?”

Before Roman had a chance to answer, Lorraine cut back in, saying: “Oh! You don’t have to tell me as much... I meant food and things like that.”

Roman jokingly replied: “Yeah, Lorraine, come on.”

The radio host then admitted he has been in a state of "pure greed" since the show ended.

He said: “There was pizza ordered, fries ordered, just pure greed and elation at the same time.

“I kept touching the floor and I was like, ‘It’s carpet, actual carpet’.

“I turned on the shower and it’s hot water, and I’m looking at Soph going ‘it’s hot'!".
