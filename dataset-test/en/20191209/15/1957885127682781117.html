Vanquish and Bayonetta remasters will run at 4K 60FPS on Xbox One X

UPDATE: First footage released.

09 Dec 2019, 15:58 by Tom Phillips

UPDATE 9/12/19: Platinum's pair of Vanquish and Bayonetta remasters are now official. As we reported last week, both will launch on 18th February 2020 with a 10th Anniversary Bundle containing both games.

On PlayStation 4, digital versions of the game will also unlock a pair of static console themes.
