Bayonetta and Vanquish bundle launches in February 2020 (update)

The leaked bundle exists.

09 Dec 2019, 15:56 by Asher Madan

Source: Sega

What you need to know

  • In 2017, a Bayonetta and Vanquish bundle leaked.
  • Today, the bundle was discovered on the Microsoft Store.
  • It includes remastered versions of Bayonetta and Vanquish.
  • The games should look spectacular on Xbox One X.

*Updated December 9, 2019:** Sega confirmed that the bundle should launch on February 18, 2020.
