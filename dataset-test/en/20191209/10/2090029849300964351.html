I’ll bring home Grammy Award, then we’ll celebrate – Burna Boy

09 Dec 2019, 10:49 by Sanya Agunbiade

Nigerian singer, Burna Boy has stated that he will bring home the Grammy Awards and it will call for celebration with his fans.

The international award ceremony is set to hold in January, 2020, and Burna Boy has allayed the fears of his well-wishers, who are fervently hoping that he would become the first Nigerian to move past the nomination level, and actually bring the award home.

During a recent performance at international rapper, Cardi B’s concert, in Lagos, Nigeria, Burna Boy pledged he would bring the award home and after that there would be a cause for celebration.

In his words, he said:

“I go carry the Grammy comeback and when I come back, we go reason.”

Burna’s album African Giant, was nominated in the World Best Music Album, alongside fellow African legendary singer, Angelique Kidjo.
