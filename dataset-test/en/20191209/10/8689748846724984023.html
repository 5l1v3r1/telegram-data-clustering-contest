Ghanaians react to celebrities’ failed ‘Meet and Greet’ with Cardi B

09 Dec 2019, 10:07 by Nana Yaw

American rapper, Cardi B’s arrival in the nation’s capital has really left most celebrities and Ghanaians alike in pains as some of them were snubbed in a supposed ‘meet and greet’ with her at the Kempinski hotel.

Self-acclaimed Ghanaian celebrities like Afia Schwarzenegger, Akuapem poloo, Hajia4real, and others who could not meet the international star after over five hours took to social media to express their displeasure but Cardi B in a response via Instagram live poured out her frustration after she was wrongly accused of being arrogant by the so-called slay queens disguised under the mask of celebrities.

During the ongoing brouhaha, Ghanaians trooped micro-blogging platform, Twitter to express their disappointment in the slayqueens for disgracing Ghana in such a manner.
