We've had thousands of gamers register for the Twelve Days of Christmas 2019 Community Challenge event and it's now time to reveal the challenge for Day 8:

Challenge 8: Eight Redone Actions

For the eighth challenge, you need to unlock eight achievements with either the Cumulative+ flag or the Cumulative - flag applied.

The key points are:

  • You can click the Valid Achievements button on the 12 Days panel on your homepage to see achievements you need in games in your collection that are valid for this challenge
  • You can find a full list of achievements with the Cumulative + or Cumulative - flags applied here
  • You have until the end of December 31st to do this and all of the other 11 challenges

Challenge 9 will be revealed at 10am (UTC) tomorrow.

Good luck!
