Pati Patni Aur Woh Box-Office Collection Day 3: Kartik Aaryan-Bhumi-Ananya Panday Starrer Has A Solid Weekend

09 Dec 2019, 10:09 by Gretel Sequeira

After having a phenomenal Saturday by collecting Rs 12.33 crores on Day 2, Pati Patni Aur Woh starring Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday has had a solid weekend overall. The film manages to mint a total of Rs 35.94 crore making it Kartik’s biggest 3-day opener by far

Pati Patni Aur Woh released this Friday, December 6, and the film has received great reviews from the critics. Following a phenomenal response from the critics, this Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar, and Ananya Panday starrer has been winning hearts. Now, after having a great Saturday by collecting Rs 12.33 crores on Day 2, this Mudassar Aziz film has had a solid weekend. The film manages to mint a total of Rs 35.94 crore making it Kartik’s biggest 3-day opener by far.

The film Pati Patni Aur Woh released on Friday along with the Ashutosh Gowariker period drama Panipat. As the Kartik Aaryan starrer has had a stable growth on Day 2, Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon’s Panipat struggled to make a significant mark. Today, sharing the box-office collections of Pati Patni Aur Woh, Trade Analyst Taran Adarsh took to his Instagram and wrote, “#PatiPatniAurWoh has a solid weekend... Day-wise growth - despite the division of screen space [#Panipat] - is a plus and should ensure strong biz on weekdays...” The film has clearly been receiving a lot of love from the audiences and has managed to mint a total of Rs 35.94 crores.
