Chrissy Teigen answers all our questions on celebs and their lavish lifestyles

09 Dec 2019, 10:01 by Zara Woodcock

Chrissy Teigen gave us a glimpse into the elite lives of A-list celebrities by revealing their secret perks.

The Lip Sync Battle star took to Twitter to complete a Q&A on everything normal people wanted to know about ‘being a super huge A list celebrity’.

Retweeting a fans question asking whether rich people have a ‘junk drawer where they throw random cords and pens’, the 34-year-old responded: ‘it’s celebrity question asking time! Ask me, your favorite A list celebrity anything you would like to know about being a super huge A list celebrity’.

Chrissy – who is married to John Legend – must have regretted giving fans the opportunity because thousands of questions came flooding in immediately.

One fan asked: ‘Do you pay restaurants $ to let you cut the line? Or do they just do it automatically?’ to which Chrissy replied: ‘I make reservations and don’t give them the option to say no. “Hi it’s Chrissy Teigen and id like to make a reservation for 2 for John legend, do you have any availability?” if you just say “hi can I make a reservation for 2?” they say no. gotta say it all at once’.

Pro-tip, if you want to make a reservation, just pretend you’re a celebrity and hope the waiters never figure out you’re lying.
