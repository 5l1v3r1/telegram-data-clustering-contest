You hired a helicopter just to make Cardi B recognize you – Archipalago to Shatta Wale

09 Dec 2019, 10:53 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Archipalago aka Palago Mufasa has attacked Shatta Wale for hiring a chopper just to get Cardi B’s attention.

Dancehall artiste Shatta Wale yesterday pulled a surprise after he showed up at the Accra Sports stadium for his soundcheck with a helicopter before heading to his hotel room to rest ahead of the concert later in the evening.

Some few hours after this, Shatta Wale took to social media to laugh at the celebrities who had gone to the Kempinski Grand Hotel to have a ‘Meet and Greet’ session with Cardi B but were disappointed.

That didn’t end there as he posted on his page again hammering on the fact that Ghana is still a village.

He posted: “WHEN I SAY GHANA BE VILLAGE…..DEM GO TALK SAY WHY”

But Archipalago who has been trolling the celebrities after the incident sent an epic respond to Shatta Wale

He responded: “U made it look more village by hiring a helicopter just for #CardiB to recognize you ???Kwasia Banku Abeteku”
