Netflix reveals Chilling Adventures of Sabrina Part 3 will return next January

09 Dec 2019, 10:43 by Ciara Walsh

See you next January witches.

What's up witches, Netflix have revealed Chilling Adventures of Sabrina will return for its third season on January 24.

They announced the news on the show's official Twitter account over the weekend.

"Knock knock knock on the gates of hell. See you Jan 24, witches."
