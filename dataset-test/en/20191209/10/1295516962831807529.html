Crisis: Black Lightning Promo Includes Familiar Clues for CW Crossover

09 Dec 2019, 10:12 by Tim Adams

A new promo for the next episode of Black Lightning features clues for the "Crisis on Infinite Earths" crossover event.

The promo for "The Book Of Resistance: Chapter Four" focuses on China Anne McClain's Jennifer Pierce, who appears to be having issues with her growing powers. The first scene shows three different Jennifers: one in her Lightning costume, another in a Blackbird suit and the other in an all-white ensemble worn by prisoners of the ASA's Pit. As the skies above Freeland turn to red, Gambi tells Jefferson Pierce and Lynn Stewart that they're dealing with parallel universes.
