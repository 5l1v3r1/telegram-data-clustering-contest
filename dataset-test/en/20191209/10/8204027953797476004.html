No injuries after a reported vehicle crashed into a building on Brandt Pike

09 Dec 2019, 10:24 by Breaking News Staff

HUBER HEIGHTS — UPDATE @ 6:30 p.m.:

No one was injured in the incident where a vehicle reportedly crashed into a building.

Crews have cleared the scene of the crash.

INITIAL REPORT:

Crews are responding to a report of a vehicle that has crashed into a building at 5390 Brandt Pike, according to dispatch.

During the incident the vehicle burst a pipe that is now leaking water.

We are working to learn more.
