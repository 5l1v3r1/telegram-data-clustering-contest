Robbie Williams is up for I'm A Celebrity 2020 - but wants a record breaking fee

The singer teased that he hopes to bag a record appearance fee worth of millions to sign up as he chatted to jungle hosts Ant and Dec

09 Dec 2019, 10:20 by Katie Dickinson

The finalists for this year's I'm a Celebrity were barely out of the jungle before rumours started about their successors in 2020.

And it was a big name who joked that he would be willing to appear in 12 months' time.

Robbie Williams teased that he hopes to bag a record appearance fee of millions to sign up for next year.

Appearing on Not The Robbie Williams Christmas Show on ITV, Robbie chatted to jungle hosts Ant & Dec via video link.

He jokingly added: "So a really famous singer will get paid millions for going on I'm A Celeb right?"
