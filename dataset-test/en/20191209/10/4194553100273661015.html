This would be a tricky one for the Kop to blare out in unison, but how good would it sound?!

But how about this remix of Gerry Cinnammon’s classic ‘Belter’ as seen in the video below?

It’s a quality song, and suits Robbo perfectly as the original singer is a very well-loved Scot, too.
