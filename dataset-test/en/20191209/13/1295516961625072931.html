The Witcher: Meet Geralt, Ciri and Yennefer in New Featurettes

09 Dec 2019, 13:12 by Narayan Liu

With all the buzz surrounding Netflix's upcoming fantasy series, The Witcher, audiences might be growing increasingly curious about the characters standing front and center in the show's marketing material. Who is Geralt of Rivia? Ciri? Yennefer of Vengerberg?

Those who have read Andrzej Sapkowski's novels or played the video games will of course know these characters well. For those who aren't as familiar, Netflix has released three new featurettes, each one introducing one of the three central protagonists. Each clip shows off the essentials of Geralt, Yennefer and Ciri, while the actors provide a brief outline of the roles they play.
