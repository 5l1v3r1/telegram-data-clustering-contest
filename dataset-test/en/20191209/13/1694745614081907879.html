No matter what store they're in, fashion girls always find the best pieces. Whether it's a vintage dig or shopping spree at a big retailer, you can be sure they're scouting the most on-trend styles and adding them to their cart faster than you can drink your trendy oak milk latte. The only thing better than being that girl who's known for her pro shopping habits is being the one who always scores a deal in the process. One of the best places to shop affordable, on-trend Fall and Winter fashion right now? Kohl's!

From sleek (and buttery soft) faux-leather to romantic tops and croc-effect accessories, Kohl's is stocked with the latest trends, all of which you can shop for under $100! Feminine tastes will adore the romantic sweaters from Nine West and the LC Lauren Conrad 10th anniversary collection while holiday-focused shoppers will love party-perfect attire from buzzy, new-to-Kohl's brands like Elizabeth and James and JW Jason Wu (the satin dresses and minimal-chic jumpsuits are G-O-O-D). If you're about that cozy-girl life, you'll hit the chunky sneaker, retro fleece, high-rise jeans, and fuzzy coat jackpot, too.

From boots to coats and holiday dresses, ahead, shop our edit of under-$100 fashion-girl pieces from Kohl's.
