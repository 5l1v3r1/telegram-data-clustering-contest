Build your confidence & stop telling me ‘I’m ‘Too-Known’ – Sarkodie

09 Dec 2019, 13:25 by Adu Sarpeah

Ghana’s finest contemporary rapper, Sarkodie for once has added his voice to the popular perception about him that his life is built on pride and arrogance.

To the best knowledge of Sarkodie, it’s high time those with the erroneous perception about him considered building self-confidence. To him, anyone with self-confidence thinks differently perhaps in a mature manner.

Sarkodie tweeted that;

”Yo quick one: y’all allow females call men “Too Known” it’s cute but no man n I repeat no man should tell a fellow man “You are too known” … You violating G code big time … that’s some girly sh## build your confidence as a man and you will see things differently.“

“How on earth a man with balls standing in a corner looking at a fellow man saying *with a soft voice “This guy he de see en body oo” Build your confidence oo nny3 saa someone de cough sef you go bore,” – Sarkodie added.
