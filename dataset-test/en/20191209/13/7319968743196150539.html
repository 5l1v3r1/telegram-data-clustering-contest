"I may be Irish but I'm not stupid" — Joe Biden's latest gaffe

09 Dec 2019, 13:19 by IrishCentral Staff

“I may be Irish but I’m not stupid.”

Former Vice President Joe Biden, who is well known for his off-the-cuff remarks and slips of the tongue, recently made a controversial comment about his Irish heritage.

The Irish American 2020 Democrat candidate reportedly made the cringe-worthy remark to a crowd in Iowa, JOE.ie reports.

According to John Mulholland, Editor of The Guardian US, Biden told the audience: "I may be Irish, but I'm not stupid." 

Mulholland, who is originally from Dublin, noted in his tweet: "Wonder what Irish people (like me) would make of that."
