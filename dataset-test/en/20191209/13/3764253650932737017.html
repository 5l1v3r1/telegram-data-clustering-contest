Cardi B’s concert in Ghana: Angry fans throw bottles at stage (video)

09 Dec 2019, 13:26

- Cardi B’s visit to Ghana appears to be a complete contrast to the days she spent in Nigeria

- A disturbing video from the rapper’s concert in the country has surfaced online

- The clip captures the moment members of the audience threw bottles at the stage

America rapper Cardi B left Nigeria for Ghana on Sunday, November 8, but from indications, her visit to the neighbouring country seems to be a complete contrast of her experience in Nigeria.

Recall that hours after she landed in Ghana, social media was set abuzz after some aggrieved celebrities in the country vented their anger at the superstar for not honouring a meet-and-greet that had been scheduled with her, despite her presence at the venue.

Well, it did not just stop there. A video is currently making the rounds online that captured the moment some members of the audience started throwing bottles at the stage.
