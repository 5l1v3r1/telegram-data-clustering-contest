Crisis on Infinite Earths Episode 1 – What Did You Think?!

09 Dec 2019, 13:11 by Blair Marnell

The first episode of The Flash began the five year buildup to Crisis on Infinite Earths. Last night on Supergirl, the end of everything finally arrived…and we want to hear what the Superhero Hype community thinks about it!

This was a very Supergirl heavy episode, since Kara Zor-El lost almost everything she had. But at least she’s alive, which is more than Green Arrow can say. Oliver Queen has been fated to die in this crossover for almost a year. However, we’re not convinced that this is the last appearance of Stephen Amell in the event. After all, we’ve already seen more than one Oliver!

Additionally, the opening moments touched upon a few unexpected cameos from the Titans and Tim Burton’s Batman. The beloved world of Batman ’66 may have even made an appearance if Burt Ward was playing a much older Dick Grayson. However, those Earths may have already been destroyed by the Anti-Monitor. Now, Argo City and Earth-38 have also fallen to the antimatter wave. At this rate, Oliver won’t be the only hero to fall. And there may not be much of a multiverse left to save…

Crisis on Infinite Earths episode 2 will be tonight’s episode of Batwoman on The CW.
