Elephant crosses train tracks by lifting level crossing gate in Jalpaiguri. Internet is worried

An elephant crossed train tracks by lifting a level crossing gate in North Bengal. The video has gone viral.

09 Dec 2019, 13:43

A video of an elephant crossing train tracks by lifting a level crossing gate surfaced on Twitter and has gone viral ever since. The over-one minute clip has been shared on Twitter by Susanta Nanda of the Indian Forest Service or IFS.

The elephant made a risky attempt to cross the train tracks in Jalpaiguri and fortunately succeeded. It appears from the video that nobody was present to operate the level crossing gate near the tracks. That didn't deter the elephant's attempt at all because it effortlessly lifted the level crossing gate and crossed the tracks.

It, thereafter, crossed the second level-crossing gate as well. In his caption for the video, Susanta Nanda said, "Level crossing or the train line won't stop this elephant to migrate. They remember their routes very well, passed from one generation to another. Interestingly, different techniques at both the ends."
