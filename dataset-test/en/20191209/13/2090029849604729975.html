Adorable photos from Chizzy Alichi traditional wedding

09 Dec 2019, 13:45 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Nollywood actress, Chizzy Alichi and her heartthrob, Chike Ugochukwu have on Saturday, December 7, 2019, traditionally tied the knot.

The actress who got engaged to her fiancee in September 2019, had her traditional marriage at her hometown in Enugu State and her colleagues turned up in their numbers to support her.

Recall that the talented movie star had earlier announced that she can’t wait to be addressed as ‘Mrs’, before the end of 2019. She had earlier last week shared her pre-wedding photo and while at that clashed with a troll who wished her ills in her marriage.

Some of her colleagues graced the wonderful occasion, celebrities, such as Judy Austin, Eve Esin, Iheoma Nancy, Patience Ozokwor and many others were spotted at the grand ceremony.
