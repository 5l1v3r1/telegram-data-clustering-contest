Jacqueline Jossa not doing interviews following reports about husband Dan Osborne

09 Dec 2019, 13:55 by Ciara Walsh

Jacqueline Jossa was crowned Queen of the Jungle on I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here 2019. 

I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here 2019 winner Jacqueline Jossa won't be doing interviews following recent reports about husband Dan Osborne.

Usually, the winner of the ITV series gives an interview on breakfast talk shows like Lorraine or Good Morning Britain, but today, Lorraine confirmed this won't be the case with Jacqueline.

On Monday's episode, Lorraine was interviewing radio host Roman Kemp, who finished third on the series, when she asked: "Can we talk about little Jacqueline who's Queen of the Jungle?"

Roman replied: "I know she's not doing interviews, I sincerely hope she's in a bubble bath with a glass of something fizzy."

Lorraine's comment comes after recent claims that Dan had a threesome with former Celebrity Big Brother co-stars Natalie Nunn and Chloe Ayling last year.

He denied the allegations at the time and is planning on taking legal action against the tabloid reports.

This isn't the first time Jacqueline and Dan have had a setback in their marriage.

Recent rumours have also suggested that Dan had a fling with Myles Stephenson's ex-girlfriend Gabby Allen.

Dan and Gabby have denied the reports at the time, with Gabby recently saying that she confronted Jacqueline directly about the claims.

I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here will return to TV screens next year.
