AC Milan make approach for Barcelona defender Jean-Clair Todibo?

AC Milan have reportedly identified Barcelona defender Jean-Clair Todibo as a target for the January transfer window.

09 Dec 2019, 13:42 by Darren Plant

As a result of breaching FFP regulations, Milan find themselves in a situation where they may have to make player sales at the beginning of 2020.

Should the club part ways with key men, Stefano Pioli will want replacements to be signed, and it appears that the Italian giants are ready to bring in at least one fresh face.

According to Sport, talks have already been held regarding a deal involving Todibo, who has struggled for game time at Camp Nou.

Despite spending almost a year with the Catalan giants, the 19-year-old has made just three starts and one substitute outing in La Liga.

The report claims that Barcelona are prepared to accept a bid of €10m (£8.42m), providing that a buy-back clause is included in any proposal.
