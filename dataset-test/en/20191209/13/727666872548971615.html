Are Iggy Azalea & Playboi Carti Expecting Their First Child?

09 Dec 2019, 13:55 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Rumors suggest a baby is coming in 2020.

(AllHipHop News) Celebrity couple Iggy Azalea and Playboi Carti began dating in 2018. There are now reports that the "Fancy" rapper is pregnant with the "Magnolia" rhymer's child.

Hollywood Unlocked published an article claiming the couple is expecting their first baby. The gossip outlet cited unnamed sources confirming that Iggy is six months pregnant.

Neither Azalea nor Carti have publicly corroborated the news of the alleged pregnancy. Over the last 24 hours, Iggy has tweeted mostly about her Wicked Lips EP and wanting 3D French nails.

The Australian's Wicked Lips is a 4-track project which includes the single "Lola" featuring Alice Chater. Playboi Carti fans have been patiently waiting for his Whole Lotta Red project. The Atlanta native dropped his well-received Die Lit album in 2018.
