BREAKING New Zealand volcano: Fears for three Brits after eruption kills at least five

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

09 Dec 2019, 13:13 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

There are fears for for three British nationals after a volcano erupted on an island in New Zealand, killing at least five people.

Three Britons are named on a Red Cross list, which allows family and friends concerned about loved ones to register an inquiry.

It is not an official list and it is possible that those named were nowhere near White Island when the volcano exploded on Monday afternoon.
