09 Dec 2019, 13:26 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

This is the staggering moment Boris Johnson took a reporter's phone to avoid a photo of a four-year-old boy being forced to sleep on a hospital floor.

ITV reporter Joe Pike has said Boris Johnson took his phone off him when he showed him a picture of Jack Williment-Barr, the four-year-old with suspected pneumonia forced to lie on a pile of coats on the floor of a Leeds hospital.

The incident came as he was interviewing the PM.

He tweeted: "The PM grabbed my phone and put it in his pocket."

Shadow Education Secretary Angela Rayner said the clip of Mr Johnson was an "utter disgrace", adding: "Do you really want this man running the country for next 5 years?"

Responding to Mr Johnson's reaction after being confronted with the picture Jeremy Corbyn tweeted: "He just doesn't care."
