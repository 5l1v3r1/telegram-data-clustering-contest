Gospel Star, Diana Antwi-Hamilton Shows Off Twin Children As They Mark 9th Birthday

09 Dec 2019, 13:08 by Ghbase New Writer

Gospel musician, Diana Antwi-Hamilton has shared a photo of her twin children as they mark their 9th birthday today- December 9.

The award winning artists penned a heartfelt message to express her profound gratitude for having such rare species in her life while bestowing onto them all the good things life has to offer.

She posted;

“9 years ago, you both came into my life and everything changed for the better. I got to be called mother. My joy increased and hasn’t stopped.

Happy birthday to my amazing children M & M Now on this note, I pray for everyone requiring fruitfulness in any area of their lives. You shall abound in every good thing. In Jesus name!”
