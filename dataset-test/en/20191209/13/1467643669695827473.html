09 Dec 2019, 13:03 by valsala

Moscow:  Russia banned from international sports event for four years by the world Anti-Doping Agencies (WADA). Russia would likely to be banned from international sports events including 2020 Tokyo Olympics, Food ball world cup in Qatar and 2022 winter Olympics in Beijing.

The decision is taken at a meeting of the World Doping Agency (wada) in Lausanne in Switzerland. Prior to the ban, Wada decided to ban Russian athletes from competing at the Olympics.

Though the ban sustains, Russia can participate   Euro-2020 foot ball Championship.
