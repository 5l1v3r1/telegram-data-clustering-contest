Penny Lancaster forced to Google Rod Stewart's age after mixing him up with her dad

Loose Women star Penny Lancaster was joined by husband Rod Stewart on the show on Monday, but things got pretty awkward

09 Dec 2019, 13:55 by Irishmirror.ie

Rod Stewart was left unimpressed with his Loose Women panellist wife Penny Lancaster on Monday, as she got his age wrong.

To make matters worse, she got him mixed up with her father as she told him he'd made a mistake when telling the Loose ladies how old he was.

Rod was sharing his gratitude to still be singing at the age of 74, with fans as loud as ever at his shows.

But as Rod opened up to Jane Moore, Andrea McLean and Brenda Edwards, Penny interrupted him to reveal he'd made a mistake.

Believing he'd forgotten his own age, Penny advised him he was almost 76 in super hilarious yet awkward scenes.

But she was then forced to Google search her own husband's age live on air, after realising it was actually her who was in the wrong.

Rod had said: "I'm sitting there thinking you know, I'm 74..."

Penny quickly said, leaving the audience in hysterics: "Darling, you're 75... you're 76 in January."

Rod quickly shouted back: "No I'm not, shut up... no I'm not, I'm 74 now..."

Penny then realised she'd actually got her husband mixed up with her dad, who is a year older than him.

She quickly hugged him, telling him: "Oh maybe I'm talking about my dad, cos he's one year older!

"Sorry, my mistake, we all make mistakes darling, sorry."

She then Googled how old her husband was for clarification, with Andrea in hysterics telling the panel: "She's literally Googled how old is Rod Stewart, and he's right."

This wasn't the only mistake during the interview, as Rod was left swearing when he made a blunder with the auto cue.

As he said "roll VT" which wasn't supposed to be read out, he was shown saying: "Oh s**t."

Andrea was quick to apologise to fans at the end of the show, blaming it on him being "a rockstar".

Loose Women airs weekdays at 12:30pm on ITV.
