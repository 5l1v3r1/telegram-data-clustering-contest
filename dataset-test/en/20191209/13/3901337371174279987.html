You Choose Your Team Of The Week

09 Dec 2019, 13:46 by Soccer Laduma

Over the past weekend, the Premier Soccer League took to the stage.   

The weekend's PSL action started on Friday as SuperSport United defeated Highlands Park 2-0, to climb up the table to second place. 

Orlando Pirates threw away a 2-0 lead against Baroka, snatching two late goals, leaving Rulani Mokwena frustrated in Polokwane. 

The match of the weekend was played in Durban, between Kaizer Chiefs and Bloemfontein Celtic. A frantic end-to-end encounter saw Serbian sharpshooter, Samir Nurkovic, score the first hat-trick of the season. The Glamour Boys maintained their 10 point lead at the top of the table, as the match ended 5-3.

And Sunday saw Maritzburg United scrape a 1-0 victory, courtesy a brilliant Yusuf Jappie free-kick, as Polokwane City were condemned to their 9th loss of the season.
