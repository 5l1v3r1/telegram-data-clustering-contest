West Ham United considering Darren Randolph reunion?

Middlesbrough goalkeeper Darren Randolph has reportedly emerged as a transfer target for West Ham United.

09 Dec 2019, 13:40 by Darren Plant

The 32-year-old has previously represented the Hammers, making 42 appearances in all competitions before departing for the Riverside Stadium in 2017.

However, according to 90 min, the Premier League outfit have contacted Middlesbrough regarding a deal for the Republic of Ireland international.

Having made a number of high-profile mistakes since joining West Ham in the summer, Sergi Roberto is likely to be moved on in January, providing that the east Londoners can sign a replacement.

While Randolph currently has just over 18 months remaining on his contract, Boro may have to consider cashing in on the experienced stopper.

The north-east team currently sit in 19th position in the Championship standings.
