Punta Gorda Christmas Parade ready to hit the street this weekend

09 Dec 2019, 13:00 by WFTX Digital Team

PUNTA GORDA, Fla. -- Southwest Florida residents and visitors are invited to come out and enjoy a Punta Gorda holiday tradition this weekend.

On Saturday, December 14th, the Charlotte County Chamber of Commerce will be hosting the 41st Annual Christmas Parade in Punta Gorda.

The parade will begin at the Charlotte Performing Arts Center (CPAC) at 12:00 PM and will travel on Carmalita Street to Taylor Road, ending at the Charlotte Harbor Event & Conference Center.

Motorists should expect travel delays during the parade and are encouraged to find an alternative route between 12:00 PM and 1:30 PM if possible.
