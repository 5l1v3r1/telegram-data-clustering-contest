Arsenal 'will not recall Eddie Nketiah from Leeds United'

Arsenal reportedly have no intention of recalling Eddie Nketiah from his loan spell at Leeds United.

09 Dec 2019, 11:00 by Darren Plant

Despite being sent to Elland Road to be provided with regular first-team football, the 20-year-old has been given just 12 substitute appearances in the Championship.

The lack of game time has led to speculation over the striker's immediate future, with it being suggested that the Gunners could send the England Under-21 international to another club.

However, The Sun claims that Nketiah will see out the campaign at Leeds, who are attempting to secure promotion to the Premier League.

Nkeitah has scored two goals from 289 minutes of football in the Championship, while another three strikes have come from two starts in the EFL Cup.
