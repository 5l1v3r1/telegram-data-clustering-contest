John Prine touring in 2020, playing The Apollo

09 Dec 2019, 11:12 by Andrew Sacher

The legendary John Prine is wrapping up 2019 with a New Year’s Eve show at the Grand Ole Opry House in Nashville with Marty Stuart and Secret Sisters, and he continues to add more dates to his 2020 schedule as well. Newly-added shows include an appearance at MerleFest (which also includes Willie Nelson, Alison Krauss, Colin Hay, and more), and a show at Harlem’s historic Apollo Theater on June 20. Tickets for the Apollo show go on sale Friday (12/13) at 10 AM with an artist presale starting Tuesday (12/10) at 10 AM and an AmEx presale starting Wednesday (12/11) at 10 AM.

Prine also has Europe shows with Ian Noe, two Louisville shows with Kelsey Waldon, Philly and Virginia shows with Emmylou Harris, and more. All dates here.
