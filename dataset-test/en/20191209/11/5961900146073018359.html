Here’s Lizzo Twerking In A G-String At An NBA Game After Professing Her Love For A Player

09 Dec 2019, 11:11 by By Lavender Baj

The LA Lakers hosted the Minnesota Timberwolves in an NBA game on Sunday night, but the only reason I know that is because Lizzo absolutely stole the show.

The ‘Truth Hurts’ singer gave a stellar court side interview, in which she professed her love for Timberwolves player Karl-Anthony Towns.

“Let me tell you something, both teams are great,” she said in the interview. “I’m personally cheering for 32.”

The interviewer later clarified if she meant Karl-Anthony Towns, to which she replied a sassy “mmhmm!”

“That’s my baby!”

The 8-time Grammy nominee eventually admitted that she hasn’t actually met the NBA player. But she’s Lizzo, I’m sure somebody could facilitate a meeting.

But her wee little crush on Karl-Anthony Towns isn’t the only thing Lizzo made headlines for during Sunday’s game.

No.

In true Lizzo (real name Melissa Viviane Jefferson) fashion, she couldn’t help but bust a move when the Lakers cheerleaders danced to her hit song ‘Juice.’

The 31-year-old ‘Good as Hell’ singer twerked along to her own song before turning around to reveal her g-string.

Yes, a g-string. At a Lakers game.
