Ronnie Fieg Teases Forthcoming Rainbow-Tinged adidas TERREX

Continuing their long-standing relationship.

09 Dec 2019, 11:44 by Mallory Chin

Frequent collaborators Ronnie Fieg and adidas are once again teaming up on a collaborative silhouette, this time using the latter’s TERREX model.

Teased via Fieg’s social media accounts, the forthcoming KITH x adidas TERREX is dressed almost entirely in a fan favorite all-black colorway from its GORE-TEX-infused upper to its BOOST-assisted midsole. Embellishing the heel is a reflective rainbow-tinged heel counter, which helps to elevate this technical silhouette. Coloful KITH branding can also be spotted on the sides, along with matching lace loops.

According to the caption, the sneaker will release soon. Keep it locked here for official photos and a release date when they surface.

Recently KITH debuted its Winter 2019 menswear range which serves up bold patterns and extreme comfort. Additionally Ronnie Fieg teased an upcoming KITH x Nike Air Force 1 collaboration.
