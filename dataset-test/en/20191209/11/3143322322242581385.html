Enchanting Christmas performance by the Drakensberg Boys Choir

The 100-strong Drakensberg Boys Choir made a turn in the city at the end of November, as part of its annual Noel Tour.

09 Dec 2019, 11:00 by Charl Pienaar

MBOMBELA – With it the choir celebrated the essence of yuletide across a host of musical styles, from a heavenly classical choral repertoire, to festive carols, wrapped in shiny new arrangements.

The joyful performance full of Christmas cheer, lots of smiles and a touch of sentiment took place at Communio Church on Friday evening.

The choir tours at least twice a year, both nationally and internationally. It has just returned from a tour to the UK where it performed as a special act during The Lion King 20th anniversary celebrations at the Lyceum Theatre, with South African music legend, Lebo M.

With the Noel Tour, the Drakensberg Boys Choir certainly spread the joy of Christmas.
