Tame Impala announce 2020 tour with Perfume Genius

09 Dec 2019, 11:31 by Bill Pearis

Tame Impala have announced a summer North American tour which will have them out with Perfume Genius. The tour begins in Chicago on May 29 with stops in Detroit, Toronto, Montreal, DC, Atlanta, Miami, Tulsa, Austin, Houston, Denver, Portland, Vancouver and more. (There are currently no NYC stops on the tour but they did play Madison Square Garden back in August.) Tickets for the dates with Perfume Genius go on-sale Friday, December 13 at 10 AM local time, with presales beginning Wednesday (12/11) at 10 AM local.

The tour is in support of Tame Impala’s new album, The Slow Rush, which is out February 14. Tame Impala also have West Coast and Mexico shows in March with Clairo and MGMT. All dates are listed below.
