Cork to meet Tipperary in Munster Ladies Football semi-final

Holders Cork will play Tipperary in the 2020 TG4 Munster Senior Championship semi-final, with Kerry to play 2019 runners-up Waterford on the other side of the provincial draw.

Tipperary regained senior status by winning the All-Ireland Intermediate Championship and will face the Leesiders again in the last four in Munster, as the semi-final pairings are identical to the 2018 last-four ties.

That year, Cork beat Tipperary and Kerry overcame Waterford in the semi-finals, with Cork going on to claim provincial glory.

In 2020, Kerry will take on Waterford in the first semi-final over the weekend of May 23/24, with Cork up against Tipperary on the weekend of May 30/31.

The minor championships will take place from late February to early May, with Cork, Kerry, and Tipperary competing in the A Championship, and Clare, Limerick, and Waterford in the B grade.

Senior Championship: Semi-finals: Kerry v Waterford, May 23/24. Replay date: May 30/31. Cork v Tipperary, May 30/31. Replay date: June 6/7. Final: June 20/21. Replay date: June 27/28

Minor A Championship: Group stage: Kerry v Tipperary, February 29 (Kerry Venue), or March 1 if there's a Minor Camogie clash. Cork v Tipperary, March 14 (Tipperary Venue), or March 15 if there's a PPS clash. Cork v Kerry, April 4 (Cork Venue). Semi-final: 2nd v 3rd, April 19. Final: May 4.

Minor B Championship: Group stage: Clare v Limerick, February 29 (Clare Venue), or March 1 if there's a Minor Camogie clash. Limerick v Waterford, March 14 (Limerick Venue), or March 15 if there's a PPS clash. Clare v Waterford, April 4 (Neutral Venue). Semi-final: 2nd v 3rd, April 19. Final: May 4.

Replay Date (A & B finals): May 10. Minor C Final: May 10.
