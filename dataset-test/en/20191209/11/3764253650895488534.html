Anthony Joshua: heavyweight champ vows to beat Deontay Wilder and Tyson Fury

09 Dec 2019, 11:22

Heavyweight champion, Anthony Joshua, has relished the challenge of fighting Deontay Wilder and Tyson Fury after his victory over Andy Ruiz.

Joshua defeated the Mexican star on Saturday, December 7, in Saudi Arabia in an epoch-making bout that saw the Brit put in blood and breath to reclaim his heavyweight belts.

The 30-year-old star was immediately ordered to face WBO challenger, Oleksandr Usyk within 180 days or risk losing his belt.

But Joshua, who earlier welcomed the challenge of fighting the Ukrainian, seems to be keen on possible fight with American star or his countryman.
