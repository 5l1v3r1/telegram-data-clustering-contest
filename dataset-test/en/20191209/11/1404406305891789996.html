Update: Juice Wrld Took Pills Before Seizure, 70 Lbs of Weed Found on Jet

09 Dec 2019, 11:09 by VladTV

Update 12/09/2019 10:42am:

People that were on the plane with Juice Wrld prior to landing in Chicago, where he suffered a seizure and later died, say that he was taking pills during the flight. An associate of the "Lucid Dreams" rapper told TMZ that he has a Percocet problem.

In addition, the FBI seized 70 pounds of marijuana on the private jet, which was stored in luggage, and they also found a bottle of codeine inside. Back in July, Juice Wrld wrote on Twitter about wanting to quit Lean after scaring his girlfriend, "Bae I’m sorry I be tweaking, you’ve put up with more than ppl know I know I be scaring you, f**k Codeine I’m done. I love you and im letting it be known publicly that ain’t shit f**king up the real love I found. Learn from this everyone. Addiction kills all but you can overcome."  

Police also found three guns on the plane, leading to the arrest of Chris Long and Henry Dean. Dean was released without bail, while Long was released after posting $1,500 bail. 

The Cook County Medical Examiner is expected to conduct an autopsy on Monday (December 8) to determine a cause of death for Juice Wrld. 

Source: TMZ

 

Original 12/08/2019 9:52am:

In a tragedy that is going to sadden the world, platinum rapper Juice Wrld (Jarad Anthony Higgins) is dead at the young age of 21.  Juice Wrld had just landed at Chicago Midway Airport and was walking through the terminal when he suffered a seizure.  When an ambulance arrived he was still conscious but bleeding from his mouth.  Paramedics rushed him to the hospital he died shortly afterward.  

Juice Wrld recently had a breakout year, garnering over a billion streams on Spotify.  His new song 'Bandit' with NBA Youngboy is currently on the top 10 charts.

Our condolences to his family, friends and fans worldwide.
