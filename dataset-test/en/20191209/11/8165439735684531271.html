Ashley Tisdale Admits She Didn't Get Along With High School Musical Co-Star Lucas Grabeel

09 Dec 2019, 11:06 by Tara Walsh

Ashley Tisdale and Lucas Grabeel

High School Musical's Ashley Tisdale says she and co-star Lucas Grabeel didn't get on during filming the first movie.

She makes the revelation during a chat with Andy Cohen last week.

"Lucas and I didn't get along at first..."

The pair played onscreen siblings Sharpay and Ryan in all three movies.

She tells the host, "Lucas and I didn't get along at first, because when we were screen testing, I was giving him line readings."

"And it's not an actor thing to do."
