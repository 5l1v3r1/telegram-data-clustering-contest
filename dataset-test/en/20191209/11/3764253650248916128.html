Adunni Ade meets Cardi B, says 'I'll rather appreciate a real person than a liar'

09 Dec 2019, 11:43

- Adunni Ade has praised rapper Cardi B on social media

- The actress praised Cardi B for always being a real person

- Adunni shared her thoughts about Cardi B after her trip to a club in Nigeria gained mixed reactions

Popular Nigerian actress and model Adunni Ade has praised international rapper Cardi B on social media. The actress shared a lengthy post on her Instagram page, talking about the rapper's character.

Cardi B recently visited Nigeria for LiveSpot Festival which took place on Saturday, December 7. The rapper's arrival sparked mixed reactions on social media with many discussing her experience in Nigeria.

The actress had flooded her Instagram story with videos of herself at a club twerking with some ladies. The videos had gained reactions where Nigerians praised her. However, the praise did not go down well with some people, who stated that a Nigerian celebrity would have been criticised for doing what Cardi did.
