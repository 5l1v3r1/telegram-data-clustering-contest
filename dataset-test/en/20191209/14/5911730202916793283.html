Comedian Jeff Foxworthy will perform 2 shows at Tampa’s Hard Rock Hotel and Casino in 2020

09 Dec 2019, 14:30 by Zack Perry

TAMPA, Fla. — Comedian Jeff Foxworthy will be performing two standup shows at Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Tampa in 2020.

Shows will kickoff on Thursday, February 20 at 7 p.m. and 9:30 p.m.

Fans may purchase pre-sale tickets beginning at 9 a.m. on Monday, December 9 via the property’s social media outlets using the password TEMPO. Tickets priced between $70 and $100 go on sale Friday, December 13 at 9 a.m. and can be purchased online at Ticketmaster.com or seminolehardrocktampa.com.

Foxworthy is one of the most respected and successful comedians in the country. He is the largest selling comedy-recording artist in history, a multiple Grammy Award nominee and best-selling author of more than 26 books.

Widely known for his redneck jokes, Foxworthy’s act goes well beyond that to explore the humor in everyday family interactions and human nature, a style that has been compared to Mark Twain’s.
