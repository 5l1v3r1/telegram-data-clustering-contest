Jennifer Aniston Accepts She Might Get Another Pet Dog After Losing Dolly, She Had With Justin Theroux

09 Dec 2019, 14:35 by Gretel Sequeira

Jennifer Aniston and ex-husband Justin Theroux lost beloved pet dog 'Dolly' in July. Recently in an interview, ‘The Morning Show’ actress accepted that she might get another pet dog after losing her dearest Dolly

Jennifer Aniston has been currently basking in success after she was named as 2019’s People Of The Year with Jennifer Lopez, Michelle Obama, and Taylor Swift. She also just got nominated for Golden Globes 2020 for The Morning Show. The actress has come out a long way with her accomplishments and continues to charm us with her stellar performances. While Jennifer has been winning hearts for long, the actress split with ex-husband Justin Theroux in 2017. In the two years of marriage, Jennifer and Justin shared a white German Shepherd ‘Dolly’. As the couple lost the pet dog in July, Aniston recently accepted in an interview that she might get another pet dog.

The Friends actress rose to fame with her popular TV show and has wowed fans with her character as ‘Rachel Green’. After being named as 2019’s People of the Year, recently in an interview with People’s magazine, Jennifer spoke about how she might get a new furry friend. As Jennifer and ex-husband shared a white German shepherd ‘Dolly’, the couple lost the dog in July. Jennifer in the interview shared how she has come very close to getting another. The Morning Show actress further shared how friend Ellen DeGeneres constantly bombards her with pictures of animals that need homes. Jennifer also shared how she would have a llama, a pig, an alpaca, sheep, goats, but went on to add that she has to be very sensitive to her dogs Clyde and Sophie because it’s all about whether they are going to be happy and hence is still deciding.

Meanwhile, on the work front, Jennifer Aniston has had an amazing year with her work. The actress starred alongside Adam Sandler in the Netflix film ‘The Murder Mystery’. 

The actress is currently working on her Apple+ Tv series The Morning Show co-starring Steve Carell and Reese Witherspoon. Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux announced their separation after two years of marriage and seven years of being a couple.
