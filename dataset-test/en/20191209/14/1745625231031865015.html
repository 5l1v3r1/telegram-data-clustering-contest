Victoria’s Secret model Lorena Rae showed some serious skin in a very plunging bikini in a seriously hot new shot recently shared to Instagram. The beauty – who walked her first Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show last year – wowed her followers in the gorgeous new photo as she soaked up the sun in the Sunshine State and showcased her flawless all-over tan.

The photo showed the 25-year-old beauty as she hit the beach at Soho Beach House on the Miami coast.

Lorena stunned in her revealing electric blue bikini top that featured very thin strings that crisscrossed across her chest to hold together the piece of material which stretched around her back.

She paired the unique top with a pair of equally skimpy bottoms. Rather than opting for a matching blue pair, she instead switched things up with bottoms featuring a yellow pattern with different shapes.

The bottoms sat well below her bellybutton to flash even more skin and perfectly highlighted her flat, toned torso and mile-long legs with a high-cut design that stretched almost all the way up to her waist.

Lorena lay on her back as she did a little tanning in Florida. She was snapped relaxing on a blue and white striped towel on the beach, while the gorgeous blue ocean was just visible behind her.

The German model posed with her right hand up as she brushed her hair away from her face and looked off into the distance. She had her light brown locks straight and flowing down her back.

Lorena glowed as she enjoyed a day at the coast and rocked a pair of small gold hoop earrings in her ears and several bracelets on both wrists. She also kept an eye on the time with a rose gold watch and a light blue ribbon on her left wrist.

The beauty also kept things tropical as she sipped on a coconut during her sunny beach day. The model stuck a blue and white straw – which perfectly matched her beach towel – straight into the drupe.

The comments section of the hot bikini upload was overrun with praise from her 1.7 million followers.

One fan called her a “beach babe” with two eye heart emoji.

Another Instagram user said, “beautiful” with eye heart, fire, and kissing emoji.
