Want to buy an old fire station? South Florida county has one on auction block

09 Dec 2019, 14:36 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

STUART, Fla. -

Officials from a South Florida county are preparing to auction several parcels of real estate -- including an old firehouse.

Martin County officials will auction a fire station that was built in Jensen Beach in 1976, TCPalm reported. The building has been rezoned for limited commercial use and will be sold at noon Tuesday, according to Elliot Paul & Company Auctions, which is handling the transaction.
