Kabaddi: Indian men’s and women’s teams win gold to extend their dominance at South Asian Games

09 Dec 2019, 14:01 by Scroll Staff

Indian men’s and women’s kabaddi teams won gold medals to extend their dominance at the South Asian Games in Kathmandu on Monday.

The men’s team led by Deepak Niwas Hooda defeated Sri Lanka 51-18 in the final to win their tenth gold medal at the South Asian Games. The women’s team meanwhile defeated hosts Nepal 50-13 to clinch their third gold medal.
