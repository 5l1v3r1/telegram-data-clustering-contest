Lizzo Twerks in a Thong to Her Hit Song "Juice" Courtside at Lakers Game

09 Dec 2019, 14:30 by VladTV

Lizzo showed up to the Lakers game against the Timberwolves on Sunday (December 8) wearing a t-shirt dress that was cut out in the back to show off her thong and fishnet stockings. 

Whle sitting courtside at the game, the Laker Girls danced to her hit song "Juice," and Lizzo stood up to sing along and twerk to the track. The crowd cheered for the Grammy-nominated singer, who dropped it low during the chorus. 

During an interview with Fox Sports, Lizzo stated, "Both teams are great. I’m personally cheering for No. 32," referring to Timberwolves baller Karl-Anthony Towns. She added, "That’s my baby!"
