Breaking: Nigeria has suffered over 8 air crashes since 2015 — Gbajabiamila 

09 Dec 2019, 14:16 by Bankole

…as Reps probe Nigerian Airforce

By Levinus Nwabughiogu

Speaker of the House of Representatives, Femi Gbajabimaila has regretted incessant military air crashes in the country, placing the number at 8 between 2015 and 2019. The speaker spoke at the ongoing investigative hearing of the House on “Incessant Non-Combatant Related Air Crashes by the Nigerian Air Force” at the National Assembly complex in Abuja.
