Vehicle crash causes roadblock in North Fort Myers Monday afternoon

09 Dec 2019, 14:40 by WFTX Digital Team

NORTH FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Deputies say a vehicle crash in North Fort Myers is causing roadblock on two roads Monday afternoon.

According to the Lee County Sheriff's Office, it's at the intersection of N. Cleveland Avenue and Littleton Road.

The crash has forced the closure of northbound U.S. 41/Cleveland Avenue and eastbound Littleton Road.

Drivers should seek alternate routes if possible.
