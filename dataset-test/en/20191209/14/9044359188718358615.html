Gazini Ganados actually won the Miss Universe National Costume competition

09 Dec 2019, 14:12

Hold your tears just a minute because Gazini Ganados actually won the Miss Universe National Costume competition.

The organization clarified the mix-up on Twitter saying host Steve Harvey had it right all along.

"Miss Universe Philippines Gazini Ganados is the winner of the Miss Universe National Costume competition. Congratulations, Gazini!" the organization said in a tweet.
