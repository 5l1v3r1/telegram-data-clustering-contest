Motorcyclist crash in Cape Coral causes roadblock on Country Club Boulevard

09 Dec 2019, 14:31 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Police are on the scene of a traffic crash involving a vehicle and motorcycle that has northbound Country Club Boulevard.

It's at the intersection with Archer Parkway.

All northbound lanes will be closed for the next couple of hours.

Drivers should avoid the area and use an alternate route.
