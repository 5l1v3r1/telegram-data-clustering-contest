Mpengesi All Smiles As Record-Breaking Chippa Surge

09 Dec 2019, 14:42 by Soccer Laduma

Siviwe ‘Chippa’ Mpengesi – the chairman of Chippa United, was seen all smiles as the Eastern Cape club continued with their impressive form at the weekend.

The Chilli Boys beat Cape Town City 1-0 at the Athlone Stadium on Saturday to move out of relegation zone for the first time this season.

The Norman Mapeza-coached outfit has moved to 11th on the Absa Premiership log standings, after collecting 10 points from their last four matches.

In addition, Chippa have gone four matches without conceding a goal for the first time in the club’s history in Mzansi’s top-flight.
