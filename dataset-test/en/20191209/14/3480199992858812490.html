Here is a full list of nominees for the 2020 Golden Globe Awards

09 Dec 2019, 14:30

It’s been a good year for British talent, which has scored a number of nominations.

The nominations for the 77th Golden Globe awards have been announced, with Marriage Story and Once Upon A Time… In Hollywood leading the way.

Martin Scorsese’s The Irishman has picked up five nominations.

In addition to the names announced today, Hollywood A-lister Tom Hanks will will be honoured with the Cecil B DeMille Award. Talk show host Ellen DeGeneres will receive the TV counterpart to Hanks’ award, the newly-created Carol Burnett Award.
