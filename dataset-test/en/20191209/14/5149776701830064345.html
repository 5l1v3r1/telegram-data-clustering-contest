Nottingham Forest fans have mixed feelings over potential striker transfer

Nottingham Forest transfer news | The Reds have been linked with the Charlton forward ahead of the January transfer window

09 Dec 2019, 14:14 by Mark Wakefield

Nottingham Forest fans have been reacting to the rumour linking their club with Charlton Athletic striker Lyle Taylor.

The Reds have been credited with an interest in the Addicks striker, along with three Premier League and two Championship sides.

Burnley, Sheffield United and Norwich City have also been linked with the 29-year-old, as have Brentford and Bristol City.

Reports also say that Charlton are hoping to tie Taylor down to an extension on his current deal, which is due to expire at the end of the season.

Here is what a selection of Forest fans have had to say about the latest player linked with a move to the City Ground.

Scott Lee Helmkay: “See what’s in it...but I’d take Lyle Taylor.”

Jack: “Lyle Taylor would be an outstanding signing for cheap.”

Steve: “Taylor lad from Charlton be a good signing.”
