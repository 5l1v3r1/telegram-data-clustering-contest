Seamus Golf Unveils Collaboration With Nike

The Seamus x Nike Golf Air Zoom Victory Tour can be worn on and off the course.

09 Dec 2019, 14:00 by Emily Jensen

Golfing goods company Seamus Golf has teamed up with Nike for a shoe suitable for golf enthusiasts and sneakerheads alike. The result of the collaboration is the Seamus x Nike Golf Air Zoom Victory Tour, which mixes leather and wool for an updated take on classic golf style.

The shoe’s outer features brown leather with woodland camouflage waxed canvas on the upper and tongue, while the interior is lined with Pendleton® Tucson Black wool. The shoe also includes a foam sock liner and Nike’s signature Zoom Air unit for extra cushioning. To add even more grip and traction, the sneaker features removable spikes around the sides and heel.

The overall look of the shoe is meant to evoke the Pacific Northwest in homage to Seamus Golf’s Oregon roots, with its mix of materials and textures inspired by a custom golf bag from the company’s founder Akbar Chisti.

“We built our reputation by creating unique, made to order products of impeccable style and quality,” Chisti explains in a press release. “When you see the originality and character of our products in person, it elevates the ‘Wow’ factor to another level. This shoe is no exception. We’re humbled to have inspired this one of a kind golf shoe.”

The Seamus x Nike Golf Air Zoom Victory Tour will launch on Thursday, December 12 at 7 a.m. PST for $200 USD, and will be available from both Nike and Seamus Golf‘s online stores.
