Man and woman arrested over murder of man in Kildare in 2018

09 Dec 2019, 14:31 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

A man and a woman have been arrested in relation to the fatal shooting of a man in Leixlip, Co.Kildare that occurred last year.

50-year-old Clive Staunton was treated at the scene for serious injuries but was later pronounced dead.

Today, Gardai announced that two people, a man and a woman, were arrested in relation to the incident.

They are being detained in relation to the murder under section 50 of the Criminal Justice Act 2007 in Kildare Garda Stations.
