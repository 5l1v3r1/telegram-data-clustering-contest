Patriots Losing At Home To Chiefs Had The Fan Base Fighting Each Other In the Stands (VIDEO)

09 Dec 2019, 14:56 by Darrelle Lincoln

The New England Patriots lost to the Kansas City Chiefs 23-16 on Sunday, which was the Pats’ first loss at home in 21 games.

The home win streak in the regular season and playoffs was tied for the longest in team history. It’s safe to say that Patriots fans had become accustomed to their team winning at home anytime they showed up.

Well, when that looked to be ending on Sunday, a few Patriots fans took their frustrations out on other Pats fans and violence ensued in the stands.
