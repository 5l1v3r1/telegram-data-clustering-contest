Nick Saban’s Final Coaches’ Poll Top 25 Ballot Has Been Revealed

09 Dec 2019, 14:20 by Andrew Holleran

Alabama won’t be heading to the College Football Playoff this postseason. The Crimson Tide aren’t playing in a New Year’s Six game, either.

Nick Saban’s team, which finished No. 13 in the final College Football Playoff rankings, is playing in the Citrus Bowl against Michigan.

If Alabama would have finished a couple of spots higher in the final playoff rankings, perhaps they’d be playing in the Sugar Bowl or Orange Bowl.

Saban had them ranked high enough in his Coaches’ Poll top 25 ballot. Unlike the playoff committee, he had Alabama all the way at No. 5.
