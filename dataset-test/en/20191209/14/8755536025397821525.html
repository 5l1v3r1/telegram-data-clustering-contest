Robbie Williams BEGS Ant and Dec to sign him up for I’m A Celeb 2020

"How am I looking for the next series?"

09 Dec 2019, 14:55 by Sarah Magliocco

Robbie Williams has asked Ant and Dec if they will take him on for I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here 2020.

The singer tried to strike a deal with the TV presenters during his show Not The Robbie Williams Christmas Show on ITV.

“What about the next series?” he asked the duo via a Skype chat.

“Any guests lined up?”

The singer tried to secure a multi-million pound deal for his appearance on the series next year, joking:

“So a really famous singer will get paid millions for going on I’m A Celeb right?”

Not one to give up easily, he continued:

“Boys what do you say? How am I looking for the next series?”

However, Ant and Dec remained tight lipped about their prospective celeb contestants for the next year’s series.

The 2019 series has just wrapped up, with soap star Jacqueline Jossa securing the title of queen of the jungle.
