Sarileru Neekevvaru: Mahesh Babu Releases Soulful Melody Track Surudivo Chandrudivo Crooned By B Praak

Surudivo Chandrudivo is a soft soulful melody track sung by Bollywood playback singer B Praak.

09 Dec 2019, 14:36 by Koimoi.com Team

Tollywood superstar Mahesh Babu starrer Sarileru Neekevvaru happens to be in talks all over. The buzz related to the film is very high following the teaser which was unveiled a couple of weeks back.

Post garnering overwhelming response for the first track from the film, a super massy track, Mindblock, Mahesh released the second track.

The song that we are talking about has been titled Surudivo Chandrudivo.

Mahesh shared the song on Twitter along with a tweet that read: “One of my personal favourites !! Enjoy : )”
