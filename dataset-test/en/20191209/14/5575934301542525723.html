The Mac Pro is finally coming on December 10 — Apple's super-powerful computer that starts at a whopping $6,000

09 Dec 2019, 14:49 by Lisa Eadicicco
  • Apple’s new $US6,000 Mac Pro will be available to order on December 10, according to a notification sent to customers.
  • Apple previously announced that the computer would launch in December, but has not shared a specific date.
  • The new Mac Pro has been in the spotlight recently since Apple said it would manufacture the computer in the United States, a decision that comes as the US has been locked in a trade war with China.
  • Visit Business Insider’s homepage for more stories.

Apple’s $US6,000 professional-grade Mac Pro desktop will be available to order starting December 10. That’s according to a notification sent to customers, which YouTube personality and tech reviewer Marques Brownlee spotted.

Apple previously announced that the Mac Pro and the $US5,000 Pro Display XDR it’s selling to go along with it would be available in December, but hasn’t revealed a specific date. The Mac Pro was initially expected to be released in the fall.
