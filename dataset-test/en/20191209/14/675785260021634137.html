BREAKING Lucky UK EuroMillions winner still not claimed whopping £40m jackpot

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

09 Dec 2019, 14:58 by Amber Hicks, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

A lucky UK punter who won £40m in the EuroMillions has still not come forward to collect the life-changing prize.

Whoever won the jackpot in Tuesday’s 3 December EuroMillions draw, may not have even realised they’re a multi-millionaire yet.

The search is on to find the owner of the winning ticket, who matched all five main numbers and two Lucky Stars in the EuroMillions draw last Tuesday.

The winning EuroMillions numbers on that date were 18, 31, 32, 38 and 48, with the Lucky Stars 4 and 12.
