Join Dorsett's carnival to raise funds for children in need

09 Dec 2019, 14:07 by Aznim Ruhana Md Yusup

Dorsett Hospitality International with its group of hotels is hosting the TLC Carnival Day on Dec 14 and 15 at Dorsett Grand Subang, to raise funds for children in need of long-term medication.

The event is also joined by Dorsett’s strategic partners including the Asean Youth Chamber of Commerce, Kiwanis Club of Bukit Kiara, Lee Kum Kee and Mayland Properties, the developer for the Dorsett Waterfront Subang project.

The carnival this weekend starts at 10am and will have close to 70 pop-up stalls selling food, festive gifts and homemade jewellery as well as a stall selling pre-loved items of Miss World Malaysia 2019 Alexis Sue Ann. 

There will also be activities for kids including zumba, face painting and picture sessions with Anna and Elsa from Frozen. There are prizes up for grabs for the many games organised, including a 3D/2N stay at Dorsett Chengdu and a 4D/3N stay at Dorsett City London. 

Transaction are only via coupons, sold at RM50 per booklet. The coupons can purchased online at www.dorsettbooking.com/tlc and picked up on the day of the event at Dorsett Grand Subang.

Funds raised from the carnival will be used to give financial assistance to children seeking treatment at PPUKM. Media Prima-NSTP Humanitarian Fund is supporting this initiative by evaluating each patient application and disbursing the funds to qualified cases.
