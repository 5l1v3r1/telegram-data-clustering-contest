Gwent expansion Merchants of Ofir out now along with patch 5.0

09 Dec 2019, 12:58 by Aran Suddi

CD Projekt RED has released a new expansion for its Witcher based card game Gwent, with the Merchants of Ofir dropping today with over 70 cards on PC and iOS. Along with the over 70 cards there are new stratagems card types that can give your army a unique bonus at the start of a match. Players that log in before December 16th will get a free Merchants of Ofir keg which will grant five cards from the expansion.
