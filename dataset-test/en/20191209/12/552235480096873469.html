Man rushed to hospital after being assaulted inside flat close in Glasgow

Emergency services swooped the scene at Hawthorn Street during the early hours of Monday morning.

09 Dec 2019, 12:46 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

A man has been rushed to hospital after an assault inside the close of a block of Glasgow flats.

Emergency services rushed to Hawthorn Street, in the Springburn area of the city, after reports that a man had been found injured in the close at around 5.30am on Monday.

A 35-year-old was rushed to Glasgow Royal Infirmary and remains in a stable condition.

Police say they have now established that the man was assaulted.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: “Enquiries are continuing.”
