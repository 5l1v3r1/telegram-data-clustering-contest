Apple Mac Pro can be pre-ordered on 10th December for ~RM24971

09 Dec 2019, 12:11

Apple announced last month that the new Mac Pro would be coming soon and now we have a date. According to Engadget, the Mac Pro and Pro Display XDR will be available starting 10th December 2019. Prices for both have been confirmed to start from 5999 USD (~RM24971) and 4999 USD (~RM20808).

In terms of tech specs, the Mac Pro is expected to come with an octa-core Intel Xeon CPU, 32GB of RAM, an AMD Radeon Pro 580X GPU, and 256 GB of SSD storage. Of course, there are higher-end configurations available for customers to choose from but expect to pay even more for those. You can visit the Mac Pro's product page to find out more about that.

As for the Pro Display XDR, it is a high-end professional monitor with 6016x3384 resolution and 10-bit colour. If that's not enough, customers can also go for the more expensive variant at 5999 USD (~RM24971) that features nano-texture glass instead. In addition, there's an optional stand that costs 999 USD (~RM4158).
