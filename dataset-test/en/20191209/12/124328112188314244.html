Moment an out-of-control car screeches off the road before smashing into a wall and barrel-rolling into a front yard - but the driver escapes with only minor injuries

09 Dec 2019, 12:55 by Milly Vincent For Mailonline
  • The black Ford Focus can been seen speeding down a 30mph residential street
  • It skids off the road flipping upside down before landing in a front garden
  • The incident took place in Redhill, Surrey, with the driver sustaining minor injury

Terrifying surveillance footage captures the moment a speeding car skids off the road towards a house, flipping upside down into the front yard. 

The black Ford Focus is seen speeding down a 30mph residential street in Redhill, Surrey, on Saturday as it loses control. 

It leaves the road, flying through the air and hitting a wall, before dramatically landing immediately in front of the home.

Somehow the driver escaped with minor injuries but the home was damaged. 

The horror crash was caught on  the CCTV of a neighbour over the road and shared by resident Clare Payne-Hanlon. 

Ms Payne-Hanlon tagged a builder in the post commenting 'We might be needing you back to do our wall!!!' 

Adding: 'Last night there was a terrible accident in Ladbroke Road, Redhill. This could have been so much worse.

'Luckily we were out as were our neighbours.'

Viewers mocked the driver in the video and called for a tougher speed limit on the road.   

Kelly Earlswood said: 'This is awful! How fast that car is going is ridiculous!' 

And Munir Choudhury joked: 'Sure the driver doesn’t live there? It was almost perfect parking tbh.'

Another resident called on the local council to implement a 20mph speed limit.

A Surrey Police spokesman appealed for witnesses and said: 'We were called following a one-vehicle collision in Ladbroke Road, Redhill, around 5.20pm on Saturday.

'The car, a black Ford Focus, overturned as a result of the incident.

'The driver suffered minor injuries. Some damage was caused to a house following the collision.'
