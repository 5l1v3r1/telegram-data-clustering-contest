Diane Russet clears the air about her kissing scene with Iyanya

09 Dec 2019, 12:25

2019 Big Brother Naija ex-housemate, Diane Yashim fondly referred to as the 'Queen of the North', has opened up on her viral kissing scene with singer, Iyanya Mbuk.

Recall, the duo trended online for a while after they shared a passionate kiss in one of the scenes of the short film produced by the reality star titled, ‘The Therapist’.

The kiss gave way to numerous speculations about the true nature of the relationship between the two stars. Well, the reality star has come out to speak on the kiss, breaking the hearts of many fans who were perhaps hopeful about a budding relationship.
