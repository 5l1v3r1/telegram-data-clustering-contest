Corruption biggest hurdle in country's progress: Chaudhry Sarwar

09 Dec 2019, 12:46 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – Punjab Governor Chaudhry Muhammad Sarwar on Monday said corruption is the biggest hurdle in country’s progress and prosperity, and is the second name of lie.

In his message on the International Anti-Corruption Day which is being observed today (December 9), the Punjab governor asserted that Prime Minister Imran Khan has made all anti-graft watchdogs independent.

“Condemnation of corruption is indispensable at every forum. Moral turpitude is in fact the base of financial corruption and we must get rid of the menace of corruption.

“Our government has zero tolerance policy against corruption. We want a corruption-free Pakistan. All of us must pledge today to raise voice against this evil at every platform.”
