Rapper Zlatan begs Anthony Joshua to leave DJ Cuppy alone

09 Dec 2019, 12:49

- Rapper Zlatan has called on Nigerian professional boxer, Anthony Joshua, to leave DJ Cuppy alone

- The rapper made the statement after he won N10k from Joshua's rematch with Andy Ruiz

- Zlatan also thanked Joshua for his N10k win

Popular Nigerian rapper Zlatan Ibile has called on Nigeria's talented professional boxer, Anthony Joshua, to leave billionaire's daughter Ifeoluwa Florence Otedola, popularly known as DJ Cuppy, alone.

The rapper took to the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter, to make the statement. He described DJ Cuppy as his bae.

In the tweet, the rapper stated that he won N10,000 because of Anthony Joshua. Zlatan won the cash after Joshua won his rematch with Andy Ruiz.
