Ghostbusters: Afterlife German Trailer Reveals Extra Proton Pack Footage

09 Dec 2019, 12:12 by Sam Stone

Sony has officially unveiled the first trailer for Ghostbusters: Afterlife, with a German-language version providing fans with even more footage, including a look at a new piece of equipment in action.

The German trailer for the upcoming revival -- under the alternate title Ghostbusters: Legacy -- includes a coda not present in the standard, English-language trailer, with Mckenna Grace's new character Phoebe testing out a proton pack with her classmate, portrayed by Logan Kim.
