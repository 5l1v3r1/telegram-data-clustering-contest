In pics: Ranbir Kapoor is obsessed with making this heart emoji for the paps

09 Dec 2019, 12:24 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Actors have their particular gestures to make whenever they step out for a public appearance. Sara Ali Khan greeting paps with a namaste, Alia Bhatt sweetly waving a hand, Janhvi Kapoor flashing her million-dollar smile, Varun Dhawan or Ranveer Singh showing thumb's up and the list just goes on. Likewise, today Ranbir Kapoor was papped in the city showing a heart emoji to the paps. Looking at the pictures it instantly clicked us that the actor has shown this emoji to the shutterbugs many times in the past while he got clicked at various events.

Just a few hours ago, Ranbir was spotted outside a clinic in the city. The star was seen sporting a yellow tee and ripped jeans. He had teamed up his look with a cap, black shades and white shoes. While coming out of the clinic he got clicked showing a heart emoji to the paparazzi.

Looks like RK is obsessed with this gesture! From weddings to award functions to Durga pandals- here are the times when Ranbir was seen making heart emoji for the cameras.

It's been quite a while that Ranbir Kapoor has been away from the big screen. The actor who was last seen in 'Sanju' is currently busy with his upcoming sci-fi adventure 'Brahmastra' which is scheduled to release next year.
