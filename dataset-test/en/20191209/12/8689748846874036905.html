‘You Made Ghana A Village When You Hired Helicopter So Cardi B Could See You’- Archipalago Jabs Shatta Wale

09 Dec 2019, 12:41 by Ghbase New Writer

US based Ghanaian social media commentator and part time musician, Archipalago has fired some salvos at familiar foe, Shatta Wale for mocking the celebs who went to meet Cardi B in her hotel room in the supposed Meet & Greet session.

Shatta Wale jabbed the Ghanaian celebs who were ‘browned’ by Cardi B after she uninternationally failed to meet them in a prearranged Meet and Greet Session to reinforce his infamous Ghana is a village cliché.

The SM boss said what the female celebrities did by lining up to greet Cardi B proves that we live in a village.

He posted on Twitter:

“WHEN I SAY GHANA BE VILLAGE……DEM GO TALK SAY WHY ”

But Archipelago disagreed with his assertion, saying he rather made Ghana a village when be decided to cause a scene by flying in a chopper over to his hotel room so Cardi B will know he’s a big guy.
