Pep Guardiola encourages Man United star Fred after being racially abused

09 Dec 2019, 12:44

- Pep Guardiola offers Man United star Fred words of encouragement

- The Brazilian was racially abused during Red Devils' victory over Man City

- English FA are investigating the matter in a bid to punish the offender

Manchester City boss Pep Guardiola showed how compassionate he is after offering Manchester United midfielder Fred some encouraging words after being racially abused in last weekend's city derby.

A goal each from Marcus Rashford and Anthony Martial helped the Red Devils compound City's woes after going 14 points behind current table-toppers Liverpool.

The Brazilian was said to have been abused by a 41-year-old City fan - who denies he was being racist during the game.
