PICTURES: More than 600 Santas take to streets of Perth for charity run

A sea of red and white flowed through the streets of Perth at the weekend as hundreds took part in the annual Santa run.

09 Dec 2019, 12:52 by Jamie Buchan

The 5k dash was launched as part of Perth’s Winter Festival in 2014 and has grown in stature to become one of the most anticipated events on the seasonal calendar.

More than 600 people signed up to race through the city on Saturday, while dressed as Father Christmas and raising money for individual causes.

A huge inflatable Santa was installed at the start line on Mill Street.

The route took runners over Queen’s Bridge and Perth Bridge and into North Inch.
