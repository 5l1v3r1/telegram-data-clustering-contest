Romeo Beckham enjoys festive trip to Winter Wonderland with dad David and family

The youngster joined his famous family for their annual trip to London's Christmas attraction while he captured the festivities with hilarious upside down snaps

09 Dec 2019, 12:47 by Irishmirror.ie

The Beckhams were out in force last night as Romeo Beckham joined dad David and his younger siblings Cruz and Harper for a family festive trip to Winter Wonderland.

David Beckham and his three younger children - Romeo, 17, Cruz, 14, and Harper, eight, kicked off the festive season with a trip to Hyde Park's iconic Christmas attraction on Sunday afternoon.

The A-lister family share a series of sweet photos from the outing on both Romeo and David's Instagram stories.

Earlier in the day, fashion icon Victoria Beckham treated the couple's youngest child Harper to a Christmas party at private members' club, George.

Harper appeared excited as she arrived at the festive grotto and magically stood with Father Christmas. Former Spice Girl Victoria captured the moment, writing “Harper is on the good list!”

Various snaps from Winter Wonderland showed David pulling funny faces as he posed in front of a colourful fairground backdrop, while another snap saw Cruz looking totally cool chilling out in a knitted hat.

The 17-year-old also took to Instagram to mark the family's extravagant festive adventure and sent fans into hysterics as he created fun poses in the Upside Down house.

Romeo captured the festivities of the zero-gravity illusion experience in Winter Wonderland's Upside Down house.

Posh and Becks' second oldest son perfected the ultimate hilarious poses to make it look like he was balancing on his head and lying on the ceiling in a series of clever snaps.

The teen looked ready to brave London's freezing temperatures as he donned a khaki and black puffer jacket with a vibrant yellow hoody and black jeans.

David was seen riding the dodgems with his kids, as well as taking them on all the roller coasters and attractions.

It looks like Becks put his fairground games skills to the test and came out on top, much to the delight of daughter Harper.

Things got a bit wild for the Beckham clan as they decided to seek some thrills on the fairground rides.

Becks and the kids had some fun as they had a go on the rollercoaster, waltzers and the swings.

The family trip comes just after David hit headlines after coming under fire for his parenting skills where trolls slammed the footballer for kissing his own daughter on the lips again.

The photo was deemed "inappropriate" by some with GMB star Piers Morgan also blasting the football star.

During a chat about the controversial photo on Good Morning Britain , Piers argued: "It's just weird right? Who does that with their kids? Who kisses their kids on the lips."

Piers said it was "quite creepy", while co-host Susanna Reid suggested that celebrities shouldn't post pictures if they didn't want "the scrutiny".
