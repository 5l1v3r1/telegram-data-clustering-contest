Natalie Nunn begs husband to return amid Dan Osborne sex slurs after I'm A Celeb concludes

Natalie Nunn has posted a declaration of love addressed to her husband, who reportedly dumped her after claims she had a threesome with Dan Osborne

09 Dec 2019, 12:31 by Irishmirror.ie

Natalie Nunn is desperate to win her husband back after he ditched her following claims she cheated on him with Dan Osborne .

The Bad Girls Club actress posted a declaration of love to her husband, American football player Jacob Payne, on her Instagram story.

The post simply said "I love you @mrjacobpayne" and showed love heart emojis set against a black background.

He reportedly left her after shocking claims emerged about Natalie and her CelebrityBig Brother co stars.

Model Chloe Ayling claims she had a threesome with Natalie and Dan - who is married to I'm a Celeb winner Jacqueline Jossa.

She claimed to The Sun that after a night of boozing and partying - which saw the stars papped together - they went back to a hotel and engaged in sexual activity together.

Chloe claims that she watched Dan have full sex with Natalie, who was married to Jacob at the time, before kissing and performing a sex act on Chloe.

Jacqueline's campmate Myles Stephenson also told her he believed Dan had cheated with Myles' ex Gabby Allen .

Dan has firmly denied all claims, as has Gabby.

While they did go out partying together that night, Dan says that absolutely nothing happened between them and denies that he was unfaithful to Jacqueline, with whom he shares daughters Ella, four, and Mia, one.

A representative for Dan told Mirror Online: "This story is not true and Dan will be seeking legal advice."

Dan has now been reunited with Jacqueline as I'm a Celeb finished last night.

On Sunday, Natalie's husband Jacob was spotted looking glum as he moved his belongings out of their house in the US.

It came after it was reported that he'd dumped Natalie after seven years of marriage.

They also share a two-year-old daughter called Journey.

A source told The Sun : "Jacob was so angry when the story came out last night.

"He has gone to stay with friends and says he needs some time alone.

"It's not looking good and the scandal has broken up their marriage. Natalie is devastated and cannot believe this is happening."
