Photos: Sara Ali Khan flaunts her infectious smile as she sizzles in a black and white shimmery babycon dress with a bow

09 Dec 2019, 12:40 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sara Ali Khan might be just two films old but the diva has already carved a niche for herself in the industry with her spectacular performances on the silver screen. The millennial diva, who is also an avid social media user took to Instagram to share a series of pictures from her latest smouldering photo shoot.

Dressed in a black and white babycon dress with a bow at the back, Sara can be seen flaunting her outfit with an infectious smile on display. Sara, who is often spotting around the city keeps on sharing pictures and videos on Instagram to keep her millions of fans entertained.

Sara, who is the daughter of Saif Ali Khan and Amrita Singh has been grabbing a lot of attention due to her bond with co-star Kartik Aaryan. While the two are reported to have moved on in their respective lives, the buzz making rounds on the internet suggests that Kartik had recently avoided shooting with her for some patchwork which was needed for Imtiaz Ali's 'Aaj Kal'.

The Imtiaz Ali directorial is slated to release on 14th February next year and currently, Sara has just returned from her New York vacay and resumed the shoot of the remake of 'Coolie No. 1' starring Varun Dhawan.
