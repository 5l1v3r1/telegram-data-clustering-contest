Irish actress Saoirse Ronan nominated for Golden Globe for role in Little Women

The Carlow native is up for Best Actress in a Motion Picture - Drama

09 Dec 2019, 16:52 by Ailbhe Daly

Saoirse Ronan has been nominated for a Golden Globe for her role in Little Women.

The Carlow native is up for Best Actress in a Motion Picture - Drama.

Ms Ronan is no stranger to the famous award show, having already bagged herself a Golden Globe for her part in Ladybird.

And this is the second time the 25-year-old has been nominated for a role in a Greta Gerwig movie.

She faces stiff competition in a strong category with Cynthio Erivo (Harriet), Scarlett Johansson (Marriage Story), Charlize Theron (Bombshell) and Renee Zellweger (Judy).

The 77th annual Golden Globes, presented by British comedian Ricky Gervais, will take place on January 5.
