Tekken 7 patch 3.10 rolling out now, full update notes here

09 Dec 2019, 16:59 by Aran Suddi

A brand new patch for Tekken 7 is rolling out across PS4, Xbox One, and PC which makes a number of adjustments for characters, along with customisation options and new illustrations in the gallery. Patch 3.10 also helps prepare the way for Leroy Smith and Ganryu, two of the characters of season pass 3, who will be available from December 10th.

You can check out the full patch notes for Tekken 7 3.10 below.
