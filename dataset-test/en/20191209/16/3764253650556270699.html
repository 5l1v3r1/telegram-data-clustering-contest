Toke Makinwa drags Yomi Black after he dared her to explain how she affords her lifestyle

09 Dec 2019, 16:34

It appears that December 2019 in the Nigerian entertainment industry would be one to remember for a very long time going by the number of drama fans have experienced in the lives of celebrities on social media.

Nigerian media personality and self-acclaimed baby girl for life, Toke Makinwa, recently dropped her crown for a few minutes to call out film maker, Yomi Black, after he had dragged her on social media.

It all started when Toke agreed with a post that claimed Nigerians are hypocrites for hailing Cardi B but slamming their own celebs who would dare to live life the way the American rapper does.

Well, Yomi Black then took to his own Instagram page to share a caption on how Toke’s post is the most hypocritical in 2019. The post had dared the media personality to beat her chest and boast of how she makes money to afford her luxurious lifestyle.

The post had also warned Toke not to claim that she makes her money from endorsements or from being a media personality.

However, it seems Toke was very well prepared as she brought guns to a knife fight after she called out Yomi Black in a now deleted post on her Instagram page.

The media girl shared a DM Yomi had sent her since 2017 and she recounted all the times she had helped him with his career despite him not having the money to pay back. Toke proceeded to calling him an ungrateful fool and asking who he has ever helped in his life.

Who have you supported? it’s always been the other way round, you couldn’t get a freebie and now i am your enemy?
