'No point waiting' - Dean Smith has been urged to make this difficult Aston Villa decision

Aston Villa news | After back-to-back defeats against Chelsea and Leicester, pressure is mounting on several of AVFC's starting Xi

09 Dec 2019, 16:17 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Aston Villa fans feel the time is right for Dean Smith to give John McGinn a much-deserved rest.

The Scotland international, started the season in stunning form, scoring three times in the opening month and a half of the campaign.

However, since his goal at Burnley on September 28, McGinn has contributed just a solitary assist, in the defeat to Liverpool.

McGinn has been an ever present for Smith thus far, playing every minute of Villa’s opening 16 games.

However, his form has tailed off in recent weeks and struggled to make any impact as Villa were dismantled 4-1 by Leicester City on Sunday.

In our weekly Q and A with Villa reporter Ashley Preece, the question was asked whether McGinn should be taken out of the firing line.

Ashley answered: “McGinn hasn’t been doing it ever since the international break. He looks shattered and, dare I say it, he’s been really ineffective in recent weeks.

“It would be nice to take him out, but who comes in? Hourihane or Lansbury?

“Given it’s all-action Sheffield United on Saturday, I think Villa will need McGinn in there, even though he’s looking sapped of energy. There’s no midweek game this week, which is a bonus.”

And the fans on social media seem to agree, with many of them asking Smith to give the former Hibs man a break before the busy Chritstmas schedule.
