You can now get your name engraved on a Harry Potter wand at intu Trafford Centre

And seven other gifts that have your name written all over them this Christmas...

09 Dec 2019, 16:16 by Danielle Elton

Finding your loved ones that perfect Christmas gift is never easy.

Do you buy them something practical or sentimental? Is it best to go funny or thoughtful?

With so much choice available festive shopping can often be a nightmare, especially if you've left it last minute.

If you're stuck for ideas this Christmas, and a pair of socks just won't cut it, then why not go for something personalised.

Find unique gifts under one roof with a huge range of new and exclusive personalisation services at intu Trafford Centre .

From bespoke KitKats to Harry Potter wands you’re bound to find something extra special and truly personal for even the trickiest of recipients.

After hunting out those special presents why not treat yourself to a Yorkshire pudding wrap or mug of hot chocolate in the centre's Alpine Village .

Alison Niven, centre director at intu Trafford Centre, said: "A personalised present instantly adds more heart to the simplest of gifts.

"So if you’re on the hunt for something with a personal touch to make your loved one smile on Christmas Day, you’ll find plenty of unique gifts at intu Trafford Centre."

Avoid unwanted presents this Christmas with these eight fantastic personalised gift ideas.

1. KitKat at John Lewis

Here's one for the chocoholics in your life.

You may have heard of a personalised chocolate bar but how about a personalised KitKat?

Lovers of this classic bar can go to town designing a bespoke eight-finger KitKat bar. Choose from milk, white, dark or ruby chocolate and add a whole range of ingredients, from rose petals to salted caramel chunks.

Once you've made your selection, in-house chocolatiers will get to work creating your bespoke treat.

Alternatively, pick and mix eight individual KitKat fingers from a selection of flavours, which will then be packed into a personalised box.

Create your own eight-finger KitKat for £14.

2. Veuve Clicquot at Selfridges

Do you have a friend that appreciates the finer things in life?

If you know someone with a taste for champagne, gifting it in a personalised tin will be a thoughtful touch.

Veuve Clicquot champagne is a popular classic but this personalised tin makes it extra special.

Go the extra mile by personalising it with your hometown, a special city or the name of your favourite person for a gift to treasure long after the bubbles have popped.

A bottle of Veuve Clicquot with a personalised tin costs £59.

3. Harry Potter at Debenhams

New and exclusive for 2019 Harry Potter fans will love Debenham's new personalisation service.

Everyone's favourite wizard is still as popular now as he ever was, even years after the last film was released.

With wands, money boxes, mugs, clothing, water bottles and drinking glasses, you'll find a personalised gift for any Harry Potter fan, no matter what age.

4. Quality Street at John Lewis

You know it's Christmas when the tins of Quality Street and Roses start hitting the shelves and they're often a staple over the festive period.

Come January you can always tell which flavours went down well with a tin of empty wrappers and stash of orange cremes or coconut eclairs.

But not only can you personalise the tin, you can also fill it with your favourite chocolates too.

Another fantastic gift for those with a sweet tooth. The tin will probably stick around much longer than the chocolates.

A personalised tin filled with your favourites will cost you £15. If you have your tin from last year, you can fill it up for £10.

5. Littlesmith at Selfridges

A gift that will be truly unique, is there anything better than receiving a stunning piece of jewellery on Christmas Day?

Littlesmith is dedicated to helping you get your message across in the most stylish way possible.

Each piece is hand-stamped using the old-fashioned method of lettering, meaning that no two pieces of jewellery are the same.

Personalise your necklace with wording, letters and different shapes.

6. Fever Tree at John Lewis

Forget a silly hat and a lame joke in your stocking, with this fantastic Christmas gift you'll find gin.

Choose from eight ‘perfect pairing’ cracker combinations from a variety of iconic gin brands including Edinburgh Gin and Tanqueray.

These are teamed with Fever Tree's six tonic flavours including the Clementine winter limited edition to create the perfect gin inspired gift.

Prices start from £8.

7.  Nutella at Debenhams

Lashing of chocolate spread on toast, it's the breakfast of dreams.

Christmas is the perfect time for a little indulgence so if starting your day with chocolate isn't acceptable at Christmas, then when is it?

With large and small jars available, personalise a delicious jar of Nutella with a name of your choice on the front.

Personalised jars are available in either 400g or 1kg and cost £4 or £7.

8. Beauty Stockings at Selfridges

Gone are the days when your stocking would contain a piece of coal as now you can fill it to the brim with gifts and treats.

At Selfridges, you can even make a tailor-made stocking or gift box packed with miniature versions of their favourite beauty must-haves.

If you're buying for someone who loves to be pampered, they will love this stocking.
