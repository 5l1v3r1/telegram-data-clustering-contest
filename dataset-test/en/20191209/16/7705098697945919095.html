Harley Needs a Crew in New DC Universe Harley Quinn Episode 3 Promo

09 Dec 2019, 16:42 by Tudor Leonte

DC Universe has released a new promo for the upcoming Harley Quinn Episode 3. It seems that things are going a bit harder than expected for Harley. In order to join the Legion of Doom, she needs a gang to watch her back.

The synopsis of the next episode reads:

Realizing she needs a crew to pull off heists that will attract the Legion of Doom’s attention, Harley attempts to recruit Dr. Psycho (Tony Hale) and Clayface (Alan Tudyk).

You can check the new Harley Quinn Episode 3 promo in the player below. Warning: extremely NSFW language.

The Big Bang Theory‘s Kaley Cuoco will voice the titular character. The voice cast also includes Lake Bell (Childrens Hospital) as Poison Ivy , Alan Tudyk (Rogue One: A Star Wars Story) as Joker, Ron Funches (Powerless) as King Shark, JB Smoove (Curb Your Enthusiasm) as Frank the Plant, Jason Alexander (Seinfeld) as Sy Borgman, Wanda Sykes (Black-ish) as Queen of Fables, Giancarlo Esposito (Breaking Bad) as Lex Luthor, Natalie Morales (Bojack Horseman), Jim Rash (Mike Tyson Mysteries) as Riddler, Diedrich Bader (Veep) as Batman, as well as Tony Hale (Arrested Development, Veep) as Dr. Psycho, Chris Meloni (Happy!) as Commissioner Gordon, Rahul Kohli (iZombie) as Scarecrow, Sanaa Lathan (The Twilight Zone) as Catwoman, as well as Vanessa Marshall as Wonder Woman and Jacob Tremblay as Robin.

The series will follow Harley Quinn‘s adventures after her break with the Clown Prince of Crime. The story will focus on Harley along with Poison Ivy and other DC castoffs as they try to help each other in order to earn a seat at the biggest table in villainy: the Legion of the Doom.

The series is produced by Ehsugadee Productions in association with Warner Bros. Animation and Cuoco’s Yes, Norman Productions.
