Vladimir Tarasenko injury update: St. Louis Blues GM confident forward will return for playoff run

09 Dec 2019, 16:07 by Tommy McArdle

St. Louis Blues general manager Doug Armstrong recently told ESPN's Emily Kaplan he is confident injured forward Vladimir Tarasenko will re-join the team for its playoff run in the spring.

"He's diligent with his rehab," Armstrong told ESPN. "I know that he works very hard. We actually tried to slow him down a little bit. We told him to spend time with family, take vacations, because when we get into January and he can start going full barrel, we want him to."

Tarasenko, 27, underwent surgery on his left shoulder after he was injured in an Oct. 24 game against the Los Angeles Kings and was originally stated to be out for at least five months.
