Asaduddin Owaisi Tears Citizenship Amendment Bill, Says it Will Make Muslims 'Stateless'

Calling the Bill ‘arbitrary in nature’, Asaduddin Owaisi said secularism is the basic structure of the Constitution and the Bill was in violation of fundamental rights.

09 Dec 2019, 16:04

New Delhi: Expressing strong resentment over the Citizenship Amendment Bill 2019 (CAB), All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) chief Asaduddin Owaisi on Monday tore a copy of the Bill in Lok Sabha, saying the Bill was aimed at making Muslims ‘stateless’ and will lead to another partition.

The AIMIM leader said that the Bill is against the Constitution of India and is a disrespect to the freedom fighters of the country.

“Ye aur ek partition hone ja raha hai (this will lead to partition). This bill is against the Constitution of India and disrespect to our freedom fighters. I tear the bill, it is trying to divide our country,” Owaisi said.
