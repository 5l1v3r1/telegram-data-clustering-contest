OnePlus is celebrating its 6th anniversary this month. As part of the festive deal, the company is offering up to Rs. 10,000 (~$141) discounts on the OnePlus 7T and the 7 Pro. The sale went live on December 6 and will continue till December 17 on Amazon India. The company is also offering instant discounts of Rs. 1,500 (~$21), Rs. 2,000 (~$28) and Rs. 3,000 (~$42) on the OnePlus 7 Pro, OnePlus 7T and the OnePlus 7T Pro respectively.

The BBK Electronics-owned smartphone giant was established in 2013 and has managed to carve a niche market out of itself over the years. OnePlus is the most sought-after premium smartphone brand in quite a few Asian markets, including India, where it claims to be number one. Potential Indian customers can also avail No Cost EMI offers up to six months on the official OnePlus website, authorized dealers and Amazon India.

OnePlus 6th Anniversary Discount and Benefits

As part of the 6th Anniversary celebratory offer, the 8GB + 128GB variant of the OnePlus 7T is available at Rs. 34,999 (~$492), down from the usual Rs. 37,999 (~$534). The 8GB + 256GB variant is priced at Rs. 37,999 (~$534). Customers are entitled to an additional discount of Rs. 1,500 (~$21) using an HDFC bank card.

The 8GB + 256GB of the OnePlus 7 Pro is now priced at Rs. 42,999 (~$604). The device was launched for Rs. 52,999 (~$745). The 6GB + 128GB variant sports a Rs. 39,999 (~$562) price tag instead of the Rs. 48,999 (~$689) sticker price at the time of launch. Potential customers can also avail additional discounts worth Rs. 2,000 (~$28) using an HDFC Bank Credit or Debit card.

Potential customers interested in owning the OnePlus 7T Pro can avail Rs. 3,000 (~$42) instant discount. OnePlus is also offering additional discounts up to Rs. 2,000 (~$28) on the exchange of old OnePlus devices in lieu of 7-series smartphones.

OnePlus 7T and OnePlus 7 Pro Specifications and Features

The OnePlus 7T flaunts a 6.55-inch Fluid AMOLED capacitive display with a resolution of 2400 x 1080 pixels. The device draws power from an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 855+ SoC with 8GB of RAM and 128GB/256GB of storage. The triple-camera on the rear consists of a 48-megapixel primary sensor, a 12-megapixel sensor with 2x optical zoom, and a 16-megapixel ultrawide sensor. The smartphone features a 16-megapixel selfie camera along with an in-display fingerprint sensor. The device draws power from a 3,800 mAh battery with 30W Warp Charge support, and runs on Android 10-based OxygenOS 10.

The OnePlus 7 Pro flaunts a 6.67-inch Optic AMOLED display with a resolution of 3120 x 1440 pixels. The device draws power from an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 chipset with up to 12 GB of RAM and 256GB of storage. The rear camera features a 48-megapixel primary sensor with a 16-megapixel lens and an 8-megapixel sensor. The pop-up selfie camera uses a 16-megapixel lens. The smartphone draws power from a 4,000 mAh battery with Warp Charge support and runs on Android 9.0 Pie-based OxygenOS 9.
