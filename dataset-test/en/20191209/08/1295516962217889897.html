Harley Quinn & Poison Ivy Find a Dinosaur Theme Park Occupied By Villains

09 Dec 2019, 08:12 by L.D. Nolan

As Harley Quinn and Poison Ivy attempt to hide out from Jason Woodrue/Floronic Man, issue #4 sees their their journey bring them to an abandoned dinosaur-themed amusement park that's home to a mysterious gang. DC Comics has given CBR an exclusive first look at the issue, which is by Jody Houser, Adriana Melo and Mark Morales, that shows the two temporarily facing off against the gang before going to meet their mysterious leader.

You can see CBR's exclusive preview of the issue below along with the solicitation text for it.

  • art by ADRIANA MELO and MARK MORALES
