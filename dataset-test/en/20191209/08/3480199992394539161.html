Peter Purves ‘shocked’ and ‘hurt’ by Crufts axe

09 Dec 2019, 08:15

The presenter said he was told Channel 4 is ‘refreshing’ its line-up.

Veteran television presenter Peter Purves has said he is “shocked” and “hurt” after being dropped as a commentator from Channel 4’s coverage of the Crufts dog show.

The former Blue Peter host and Doctor Who star said he was told the broadcaster is “refreshing” its line-up.

The 80-year-old told ITV’s Good Morning Britain: “I got a phone call saying ‘There is no easy way to say this but actually you’re surplus to requirements, we don’t need you’.
