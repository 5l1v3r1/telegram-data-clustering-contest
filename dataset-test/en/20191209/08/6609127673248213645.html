CHEEKY DIG

Celtic hero Chris Sutton trolls Rangers as he tweets ‘and they are still coming’

09 Dec 2019, 08:01 by Aidan Scott

CELTIC legend Chris Sutton has trolled Rangers as he insisted 'they are still coming'.

The Hoops claimed a TENTH successive domestic trophy yesterday as they beat the Light Blues in the Betfred Cup Final.

And BT Sport pundit Sutton couldn't resist having a dig at the Parkhead club's Old Firm rivals last night.

He simply tweeted: "And they are still coming..."

A reference to a phrase commonly used by Rangers fans, it has become a punchline for their Celtic counterparts in recent years.

And many Hoops supporters loved Sutton's latest dig at the Ibrox club.
