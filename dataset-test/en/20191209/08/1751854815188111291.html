Android 10 has been out for a couple of months at this point. Apart from Google, some phone makers have been able to offer the update relatively quickly. Honor have been making a decent effort to push updates faster, and now they’re looking for Honor 9X Pro and Honor 9X users to beta test EMUI 10 based on Android 10.

The company is looking for 500 users of the log version and 1500 users of the non-log version of its beta program. You can sign up via this link right over here. The signup deadline is on December 15, and users are required to run EMUI 9.1.1.160 or above to be eligible for the EMUI 10 beta program.

EMUI 10 features various new advancements and performance tweaks. It comes with a whole new UX inspired by a Magazine style layout depicting cleaner UI elements, new screen transitions, menus and notifications panel. The new colourful Always-On-Display brings a unique charm to the device.

  • Dark Mode support
  • Enhanced security with a distributed system.
  • Mutual aid sharing to improve hardware capabilities.
  • One-Touch Screen Sharing
  • Multi-Screen Sharing collaboration
  • Huawei HiCar
  • ARK Compiler
  • GPU Turbo
  • Super File System
  • Lin Turbo

Also, it finally brings Android 10 to the devices, which will improve the overall performance and security of the device. It’s good to see Huawei and Honor pushing forward with updates for their devices, and we’re looking forward to seeing Android 10 come to more devices in their long list.
