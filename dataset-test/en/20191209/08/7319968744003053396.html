Touching video is a love letter to Irish emigrants at Christmas

09 Dec 2019, 08:10 by IrishCentral Staff

Dublin documentary maker's moving film wishes a Merry Christmas to all those Irish who can't make it home this December

Cathal Kenna, a Dublin documentary filmmaker, made a moving video wishing Irish emigrants around the world a Happy Christmas.
