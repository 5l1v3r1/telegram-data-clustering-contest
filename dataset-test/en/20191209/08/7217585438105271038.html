Watch: ‘Wonder Woman 1984’ gets its first official trailer and release date

09 Dec 2019, 08:00 by Shereesa Moodley

Warner Bros on Monday dropped the official trailer for Wonder Woman 1984, the second stand-alone movie featuring the Amazonian DC superhero.

“Fast forward to the 1980s as Wonder Woman’s next big-screen adventure finds her facing two all-new foes: Max Lord and The Cheetah,” reads the trailer description.

As the title suggests, the movie takes place in the heart of the 80s and features nostalgic references to the booming era.

Along with Gal Gadot returning as Wonder Woman, the movie also brings Chris Pine’s character Steve Trevor back from the 2017 original as well, which was set during World War 1.
