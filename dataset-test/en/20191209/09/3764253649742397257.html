Eberechi Eze scores brace as QPR beat Preston 2-0 in EFL Championship

09 Dec 2019, 09:06

Eberechi Eze was Queens Park Rangers hero when they defeated Peston North End by 2-0 in an EFL Championship game played at Loftus Road.

The Nigerian-born England midfielder for the second time in a week improved his goalscoring form for the London club.

Eze broke the deadlock in the 17th minute as he latched on to a through ball as he shot straight at the goalkeeper and pounced on the second attempt to make it 1-0.
