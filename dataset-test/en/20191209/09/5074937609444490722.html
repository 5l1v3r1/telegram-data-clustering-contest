President Akufo-Addo attends ACP summit in Nairobi, Kenya

09 Dec 2019, 09:26 by Ken Sackey, GNA

Accra, Dec. 8, GNA - President Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo Sunday left Ghana to attend the 9th Summit of the Heads of State and Government of the African, Caribbean and Pacific Group of States (ACP Group), being held in Nairobi, Kenya.

President Akufo-Addo is scheduled to deliver the opening remarks on Monday, December 9, 2019 at the two-day Summit on behalf of the Africa Group, and participate in the general debate of the Heads of State and Governments.

He is expected to return to Ghana on Tuesday, December 10, 2019. Vice President Dr Mahamudu Bawumia will act in his stead.
