Anthony Joshua: Heavyweight champion raps as he show skills outside boxing

09 Dec 2019, 09:56

- Anthony Joshua raps passionately as he shows skills outside boxing

- The heavyweight star holds the mic like pro before sending the audience into jubilation

- Joshua defeated Andy Ruiz to reclaim his heavyweight belts in Saudi Arabia on Saturday night

Anthony Joshua proves he is more than boxing after the heavyweight champion was seen showing rapping skill in an heartwarming scene that defines him as multi-talented ambitious young man.

The British-Nigerian born recorded a resounding victory on Saturday night, December 7, as he majestically reclaimed his heavyweight belts in a brutal action-packed 12th round defeat of Andy Ruiz in Diriyah Arena, Saudi Arabia.

Earlier written off by critics after suffering first career defeat to Ruiz in June, the Nigerian-born boxing star put in breath and blood from intense six-month hard training to enthrone himself as king of heavyweight division again.
