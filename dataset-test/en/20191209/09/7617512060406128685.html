Robbery suspect pulls knife when confronted by store owner: Oak Bay police

09 Dec 2019, 09:40 by Jeff Bell

Oak Bay police say a robbery suspect pulled a knife when he was approached just after noon Saturday by the owner of a store at Eastdowne Road and Dalhousie Street.

The man had taken food items.

After the encounter, the suspect rode off on a bicycle.

He is described as tall, thin and in his 20s with a dark complexion.
