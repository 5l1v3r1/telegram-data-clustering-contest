Maurizio Sarri slams Juventus players after disappointing defeat to Lazio

09 Dec 2019, 09:19

- Maurizio Sarri blames his players for disappointing defeat to Lazio

- Cristiano Ronaldo scored his seventh goal of the season

- A goal each from Luiz Felipe, Sergej Milinkovic-Savic and Felipe Caicedo handed the Old Lady the first defeat of the season

Juventus boss Maurizio Sarri has slammed his players for after suffering his first Serie A defeat to SS Lazio at the Stadio Olimpico last weekend.

Cristiano Ronaldo's goal failed to count after the hosts came from one goal down to beat the defending champions 3-1 in a thrilling encounter.

A goal each from Luiz Felipe, Sergej Milinkovic-Savic and Felipe Caicedo rendered the 34-year-old's a mere consolation at the end of the meeting.
