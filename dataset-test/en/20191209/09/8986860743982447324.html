I had a little dilemma in Ghana; but still had fun – Cardi B

09 Dec 2019, 09:50 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar known to many as Cardi B name would be on the lips of Ghanaian entertainment lovers for long after the little brouhaha that happened after she touched down on the motherland.

Her show which came off successfully at the Sports stadium got more people jamming and singing along to her songs as well as other top artistes in the country.

After her show, she took to her photo-sharing site Instagram and posted a picture of herself in a made in Ghana bikini with the caption talking about her short stay in Ghana.

She posted: “I was in Ghana for less then 24 hours .Went thru a lil dilemma but I still had fun”
