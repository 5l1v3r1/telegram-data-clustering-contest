size? Adds Heritage Fox Brothers Materials to adidas Originals Campus 80

Featuring three different flannels.

09 Dec 2019, 09:28 by Jack Stanley

size? has partnered with adidas Originals and British heritage textile company Fox Brothers for a unique new take on the Campus 80 silhouette. The sneaker is constructed from three Fox Brothers flannels, which are mirrored from one side of the shoe to the other.

The materials begin with a West of England Grey flannel, appearing on the exterior of each shoe, followed with an Old English Blue Chalkstripe flannel running from the toe box up to the tongue and, finally, a Glen Check on the medial walls. Other detailing includes orange Three Stripe and Fo Brothers branding on the medial walls, with white stripes and gold “CAMPUS” branding on the lateral side. Finally, the collaborative sneaker is finished with co-branded footbeds and tongue tabs.

Take a look at the size? x Fox Brothers x adidas Originals Campus 80 in the gallery above, and expect the sneaker to arrive via the size? web store and in all physical locations on December 13.
