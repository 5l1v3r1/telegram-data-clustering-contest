Virat Kohli gestures angrily as MS Dhoni chants follow Rishabh Pant’s dropped catch

09 Dec 2019, 09:12 by Sports Desk

By: Sports Desk | Updated: December 9, 2019 2:42:37 pm

Virat Kohli gestures to the crowd during the 2nd T20I between India and West Indies in Thiruvananthapuram on Sunday (Screengrab)

Virat Kohli was not impressed with India’s fielding efforts as West Indies won by 8 wickets in the 2nd T20I in Thiruvananthapuram on Sunday, but he had something to say when the crowd reacted to a missed catch by wicketkeeper Rishabh Pant by chanting MS Dhoni’s name.
