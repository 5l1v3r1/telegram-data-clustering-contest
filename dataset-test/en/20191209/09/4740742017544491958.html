Huge prediction made in Championship race with Leeds United set for promotion in boost for Nottingham Forest and Bristol City

The experts have updated their opinions

09 Dec 2019, 09:06 by David Byrom

Bristol City's win over Fulham at the weekend has given both them - and Leeds United - a boost, according to a huge new Championship prediction.

Data website Five Thirty Eight has a model to predict the outcome of the Championship this season and Leeds United are predicted to come top.

In-form West Brom will run them close for the title - Slaven Bilic's side are predicted to finish just three points behind them in second.

Yet Leeds have a 54 per cent chance of winning the title, with West Brom being given a 32 per cent possibility.

Leeds have been boosted by the Robins' win at Craven Cottage, though - they now have a massive 84 per cent chance of getting promoted, and are predicted to finish 15 points clear of Fulham.

The Robins' victory also sees them strengthen their claim for the play-off places.

City are predicted to finish sixth, in the final play-off spot, two points ahead of Sheffield Wednesday.

The Robins have been given a 39 per cent chance of making the top six, and a 14 per cent chance of being promoted.

Also involved in the play-off places are Fulham, Brentford and Nottingham Forest.

If the website's prediction rings true, City will face Fulham in the play-off semi-finals.

At the other end of the table, Barnsley, Wigan Athletic and Luton Town are predicted to be relegated.
