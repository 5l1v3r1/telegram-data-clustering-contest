Mega Millions jackpot rises to $314 million for Tuesday's drawing

09 Dec 2019, 09:51 by WFTX Digital Team

TALLAHASSEE, Fla. -- The Mega Millions lottery game has seen its jackpot exceed $300 million for the 3rd time this year.

Tuesday night's jackpot is worth an estimated $314 million, according to the Florida Lottery.

The last winner was on September 24th, when an anonymous Texas player won $225 million.

Other winners this year have included a $522 million winner in San Diego in June, and a $437 million winner in New York in January.

The next drawing will be on Tuesday night at 11 p.m. But good luck; your chances of winning are 1 in 302,575,350.

If you miss out on that huge jackpot on Tuesday, there's always the PowerBall drawing the next night, with an estimated jackpot of $140 million.

As always, you can watch the drawings live on Fox 4.
