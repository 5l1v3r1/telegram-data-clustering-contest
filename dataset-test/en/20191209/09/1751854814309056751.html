Samsung Vietnam will be holding a launch event on Dec. 12 to announce a new 2020 Galaxy A-series phone. The launch teaser had revealed that the new Galaxy A phone will be arriving with a display that features a punch-hole at the top-center position. This suggested that the company may announce the Galaxy A51 or Galaxy A71 or both phones on Dec. 12 since these devices are rumored to arrive with aforementioned punch-hole styled display. A leaked promo video of the Galaxy A51 smartphone has appeared to confirm on its design, color variants and key specifications
