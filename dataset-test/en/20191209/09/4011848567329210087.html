Top 5 Chromebook Laptops to Have in 2020

09 Dec 2019, 09:24 by Olive Marie

Looking forward to a more productive 2020? Then, you should be more aggressive in grasping new technologies. Start with a new laptop. There are lots of laptops you'll find, be it online or at the physical stores. They vary in brand, features, specifications, and price. If you have a hard time which brand to buy, consider the Chromebook laptops. You might be wondering what difference do Chromebooks have from the other laptops. 

As in any other personal computer, you can think of the Chromebook as, more often than not, Internet-reliant, starting up ultra-fast and performs snappily. Chromebook laptops are quite different as their applications focus on their services and applications of Google, including YouTube, Chrome, and Maps. Moreover, Chromebook laptops don't run on the Windows operating system. Instead, they are powered by Chrome OS.

Five Best Chromebook Laptops You can Find at Amazon

You don't have to feel confused on which Chromebook laptop to choose and buy. All five brands listed here offer different features and specs that definitely match your preferences and needs. But don't settle for these five laptops alone. There are other brands you can find on Amazon. Meanwhile, scroll down and see if you'll find the perfect Chromebook computer that you're looking for. 

1.       Google Pixelbook Go

(Photo : Photo: Amazon)

Google retains its tradition of developing the world's best Chromebooks. While its older Pixelbook laptops occupied the Amazon's top spot in its Chromebooks list, it has been replaced by this Chromebook laptop-Google's latest Chromebook, so far. The Google Pixel Go features an impressive battery life, not to mention a brilliant keyboard, which makes typing easy and such a pleasure. 

2.       Acer Chromebook Spin 13

(Photo : Photo: Amazon)

With Google's Chromebook Spin 13, Acer aims for its Chromebooks synonymous as well, with business. Attractively built from aluminum, along with a stunning QHD screen, this is a perfect fit in any office setup. One more thing to look forward to, in this Chromebook laptop is packed with, as described on Amazon, "full-fat Ultrabook processors," to get work done, be it in school or at work.

3.       HP Chromebook 14

(Photo : Photo: Amazon)

If you think buying a new Chromebook laptop will cost you a lot, think again because the HP Chromebook 14 is among the reasonably priced devices without its efficiency and usability being compromised. This may be a bit similar to one of the Acer Chromebooks in many ways, but this 14-inch Chromebook is quite more compact. And, even if this device's battery life and performance are just normal, it is considered one of the best Chromebooks today.  
 4.       Asus Chromebook Flip

(Photo : Photo: Amazon)

Prior to Google's launch of the Pixelbook, and presented exactly how the best Chromebook should be, this Chromebook laptop was already the personal computer, many Google laptops aimed to be. Featuring a full-HD display and Intel Core processor, the Asus Chromebook Flip was, as reviewed on Amazon, "undoubtedly a game-changer." Like the HP Chromebook 14, if you are currently on a budget constraint, this one's a perfect choice to consider.

5.       Dell Inspiration Chromebook 11

This perfectly suits your style, preference, and, most of all, your budget. Probably, this is not the most powerful Chromebook you'd find in the market, but the Dell Inspiration Chromebook 11 is everything this laptop brand ought to be: durable, easy to use, and, most of all, reasonably priced.
