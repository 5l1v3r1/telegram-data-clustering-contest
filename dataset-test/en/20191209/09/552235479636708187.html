Currys PC World reveals this year’s top tech Christmas gifts

The top gifts of the year were decided by the brand’s new customer testing initiative, the Customer Collective

09 Dec 2019, 09:41 by Linda Howard

Currys PC World has revealed its top must-have Christmas tech gifts which have been voted for by customers, as part of the technology retailer’s newly launched Customer Collective.

The popular electrical retailer has created the Customer Collective to answer real-life tech challenges and conundrums faced by customers every day.

Made up of people from across the country, the first challenge centred around finding the must-have tech gifts for Christmas2019, as tried-and-tested by those who know best – customers.

This unique initiative has revealed insights about the way people are approaching gifting this Christmas.

This year’s trends are all about being practical; giving gifts that feel generous, but will be useful, functional and have life after December 25.

The Customer Collective’s first challenge was to test the best in Christmas tech and has resulted in the creation of the Currys PC World’s Customer Choice Christmas Gift of the Year 2019. 100 customers from across the country came to a central London location to interact with, test and assess the Currys PC World top tech gift ideas.

Dan Rubel, Customer Communications & Brand Director, Dixons Carphone said: “Currys PC World is all about helping everybody enjoy amazing tech, so this Christmas we’ve challenged our new Customer Collective to work out the very best tech gifts in order to guide our customers through the decision making process surrounding all the amazing tech out there."
