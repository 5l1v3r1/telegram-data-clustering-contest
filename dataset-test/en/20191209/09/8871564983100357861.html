A fight over A&W seats? Two arrested over brawl outside Jewel Changi Airport outlet (Video)

09 Dec 2019, 09:42 by Coconuts Singapore

Two men have been arrested over a brawl that broke out just outside the A&W fast food outlet at Jewel Changi Airport on Sunday. 

Police were alerted to the incident at around 11am, and subsequently nabbed the unidentified men on suspicion of affray, or fighting in a public place. 

Both men sustained minor injuries, police told reporters today. 

The fight appeared to involve around 15 people, some of who seemed to be family members, and was captured, at least in part,  in a 15-second clip now circulating online.

The video opens in media res, so to speak, with the group already in the thick of the brawl and several participants engaged in their own one-on-one bouts as passersby looked on.
