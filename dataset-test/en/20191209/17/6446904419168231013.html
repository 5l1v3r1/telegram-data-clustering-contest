Woman missing from Castlebar found safe and well

Gardaí said no further action was required.

09 Dec 2019, 17:05 by Sean Murray

GARDAÍ IN MAYO who had been loking to trace the whereabouts of a 29-year-old woman say she has been found safe and well. 

The woman had gone missing from the Castlebar area on Saturday 7 December. 

In a statement, gardaí thanked the public for its assistance and said that no further action was required.
