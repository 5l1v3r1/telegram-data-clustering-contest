Big claim from France that PSG could swoop for Liverpool star

09 Dec 2019, 17:20 by Sport Witness Team

This week’s edition of France Football has a special feature on potential PSG transfers. It’s mostly about players who could leave the club and there’s nothing hugely dramatic.

There’s also a small section on potential incomings, and that’s where Sadio Mane gets a mention.

Ahead of the last summer transfer window the Liverpool star was linked to Real Madrid a couple of times, and it’s possible that PSG will take that role ahead of future markets.

France Football report Mane’s name is becoming more popular with those in charge at PSG, especially in the scenario that Neymar leaves the Ligue 1 champions.

Should the Brazilian leave then the Liverpool player is believed to be the ‘perfect’ candidate to replace him.

There’s no explanation about what Mane himself or the Reds would think about a potential Anfield exit. Unless the player pushed for a transfer it’s unlikely Jurgen Klopp would be happy to see the back of him.

Mane’s contract runs until the end of June 2023.
