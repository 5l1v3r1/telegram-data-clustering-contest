Bayonetta & Vanquish 4K Remaster Bundle Officially Announced for Xbox One and PS4

09 Dec 2019, 17:35 by Nathan Birch

Existence of the remasters seemingly leaked last week, but now Sega has made it official – the Bayonetta & Vanquish 10th Anniversary Bundle is coming to Xbox One and PS4 early next year! This is the first time these two Platinum Games classics have been available on Microsoft and Sony’s current consoles (both came to PC in 2017 and Bayo hit Switch in 2018). These new remastered ports of the games will run in 4K at 60fps on both the PS4 Pro and Xbox One X.
