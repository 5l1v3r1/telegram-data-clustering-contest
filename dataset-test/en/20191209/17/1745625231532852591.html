Nina Serebrova started off her week by soaking up some sun in a tiny little bikini while in Miami, Florida. The Bang Energy model shared the photo and a video for her Instagram fans to enjoy on Monday morning.

In the steamy post, Nina is seen sporting a neon orange string bikini as she relaxes on a yellow and white lounge chair out on a balcony. The brunette beauty showcased her ample cleavage, toned arms, flat tummy, curvy hips, and long, lean legs in the skimpy two-piece as she told her fans in the caption that they should do Monday like she does.

Nina wore her long, dark hair pushed to the side and styled in loose waves that fell down her body. She donned a pair of oversize sunglasses to shield her eyes from the sun’s rays as she laid with her arms behind her head looking comfortable.

The model also appeared to wear a more natural makeup look for the shots, showing off defined eyebrows, a shimmering glow on her face, pink blush to bring out her cheekbones, and a pink tint to her full lips.

In the background of the photo a city scene is visible, as well as a gorgeous blue sky complete with white fluffy clouds.

Meanwhile, many of Nina’s over 2.7 million followers were left stunned by the jaw-dropping post, and quickly showed their support for the snaps by clicking the like button over 5,700 times and leaving more than 80 comments in less than 20 minutes after the post went live on the network.

“Orange bikini looks amazing on you,” one of Nina’s Instagram followers wrote in the comments section of the post.

“I hope you have a great day and wonderful week ahead,” a second admirer commented.

“Always so gorgeous Nina,” another fan said.
