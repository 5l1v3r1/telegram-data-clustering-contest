Russia's Response to Olympic Ban: 'Chronic Anti-Russian Hysteria'

09 Dec 2019, 17:38

The World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) on Dec. 9 announced a four-year ban on Russia competing in or hosting Olympic Games and other major world sporting events.

WADA said its Executive Committee unanimously endorsed the ban following a lengthy investigation of Russia athletes’ “state sponsored” doping.
