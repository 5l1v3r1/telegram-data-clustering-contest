The End Is Near in the ‘Watchmen’ Season Finale Promo

09 Dec 2019, 17:45 by Genevieve Scarano

The season finale of HBO’s Watchmen airs this Sunday and it teases an intense countdown for all involved.

Last night’s episode was not easy to watch: An emotional character confession and a showdown with the Seventh Kavalry ended things on a tragic note, while setting up the last episode of the season.
