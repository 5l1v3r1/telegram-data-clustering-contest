This is how Cassper Nyovest is bringing back his popular #FILLUP concert before the end of the year

09 Dec 2019, 17:25

Johannesburg – South African Hip Hop sensation, Cassper Nyovest is set to fill up another stadium this year, and Standard Bank is rallying behind him for the second time. 

Taking place in the North West, his home province at the Royal Bafokeng stadium in Rustenburg on 15 December 2019, the show is set to be one for the books.

After four years of a successful #FillUp series of concerts where he filled up The Dome, Orlando Stadium, FNB stadium, and most recently, Moses Mabhida stadium in Durban, Cassper is ready to return home to #FillUpRoyalBafokeng stadium.

About the professional collaboration, the bank said: "Standard Bank is excited to be partnering with a young visionary like Cassper Nyovest. He is a great example of pushing through adversity and proving that the possibilities are endless if you put your mind to it, which directly aligns to our Still Good to Go positioning," said Keno Smith, Head of Retail Marketing at Standard Bank.
