FIFA 20 Team of the Week (TOTW) 13 predictions with Liverpool midfielder and Kylian Mbappe

Here are our predictions for the FIFA 20 Ultimate Team TOTW 13 lineup including players from Liverpool, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Barcelona and PSG.

09 Dec 2019, 17:19 by Nathan Bliss

After scoring, assisting and being named Man of the Match in Liverpool's 0-3 win over Bournemouth on Saturday, Reds midfielder Naby Keita has a great chance of being named in the FIFA 20 Ultimate Team TOTW 13 lineup, alongside the likes of Kylian Mbappe and Lionel Messi this week.

Mbappe scored one and assisted another in PSG's 3-1 win over Montpellier in Ligue 1, but could lose out on a TOTW place thanks to Neymar's performance, with the Brazilian scoring a wonderful free-kick and registering an assist in the same game, and you can't have two players from the same team in a TOTW squad.

From around the world, Lionel Messi, Jadon Sancho, Luis Suarez, Heung-min Son, Raphael Varane, Marcus Rashford and Timo Werner all have a good chance of being named in the FUT TOTW 13 lineup, after some incredible individual performances.

It's very hard to call this one.

This is just a FUT 20 TOTW prediction, so there is no guarantee that any players mentioned will be included.

The FUT20 TOTW line-up is picked on single performances only, so goals and assists are not added accumulatively within the TOTW period.
