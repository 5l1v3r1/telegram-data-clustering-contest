Khloe Kardashian playfully kisses daughter True in cute snap to celebrate Thanksgiving

KUWTK star Khloe Kardashian marked the American celebrations by dedicating a heartwarming social media post to her one-year-old daughter True

29 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Irishmirror.ie

Reality sensation Khloe Kardashian has posted an adorable snap on her Instagram , showing her sharing a sweet kiss with her young daughter True through a glass window.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians star, 35, looked lovingly at her darling daughter as she crouched down to dramatically pout her lips and plant a sweet kiss to True through the glass.

The adorable one-year-old appeared to have spotted her mum through the window as she sweetly reached up to the glass to give her a kiss back.

Blonde bombshell Khloe posted the snap with her 101million followers to mark Thanksgiving celebrations and shared a sweet message alongside the photo.

She wrote: "Everyday I give thanks that I was chosen to be your mommy! I’ll never ever take this role for granted!

"Thank you Tutu! I will easily love you until the end of time."

Khloe dedicated her love and thanks to her little girl as she insisted how grateful she is to "be her mummy."

The reality TV queen finished the post with hashtags for the thankful season 'Thanksgiving' and 'Thankful.'

In the cute snap, the little one looked adorable dressed in a fashionable leopard-print swimsuit and white frilly lace shorts, while Khloe appeared to be dressed in her gymwear and carrying a bag of shopping ahead of cooking her annual Turkey dinner.

Earlier in the day of celebrations, the Revenge Body star, revealed she started her Thanksgiving morning with a 6am workout, which she shared on her Instagram Story.

In a candid mirror selfie, Khloe kneeled in a room beside fitness equipment wearing grey leggings and a black hoodie.

"#Mood," Khloé wrote over the post, adding the time — 6:19am — and the sticker "Happy Thanksgiving!"

Khloe shares her adorable tot with her ex, pro-basketball beau Tristan Thompson , 28.

The beauty recently revealed she is not ready to date yet after Tristan was caught cheating on her with her sister Kylie's best friend Jordyn Woods.

Speaking on Jay Shetty's On Purpose podcast, Khloe addressed the speculation of the cheating scandal, saying: "About eight months ago, I had a breakup and so figuring out, I think people are so good at distracting and I've really chosen to not distract myself or try to not distract myself.

"Maybe people would say True would be a distraction, but if anything, she's a healthy distraction."

Khloe is now blossoming in single life and recently admitted that she is "so in love" with her daughter True, that she doesn't need another man to muscle in on her affections.

She said while she has forgiven him for being unfaithful and that she puts her daughter, True, first when it comes to co parenting, she isn't jumping back into the dating scene.

The mum-of-one explained: "I haven't jumped into dating. I don't care to date right now. I'm not in a rush for it,.

"I feel so good in my life and I don't really need much else."
