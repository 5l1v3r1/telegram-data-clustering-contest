Jacqueline Jossa's mum and Dan Osborne ignore each other despite sharing hotel

Dan Osborne and his daughters are staying in Australia with Jacqueline Jossa's family as they support her I'm A Celebrity stint

29 Nov 2019, 15:25 by Irishmirror.ie

Dan Osborne has been accused of ignoring his mother-in-law despite sharing hotel with her and the rest of Jacqueline Jossa's family as they wait for her to finish her I'm A Celebrity stint.

Even though Dan and Selina Jossa have been sharing childcare of Dan and Jac's two daughters, Mia and Ella, they've each kept their social media feeds free of each other.

Selina and her husband John Jossa have been sharing snaps of themselves hanging out at the plush five-star Palazzo Versace, where Dan is also staying.

But even though they all arrived on the same flight to Brisbane airport, Selina and Dan have pointedly not included each other in their Instagram updates.

Both, however, have been sharing plenty of snaps of the girls.

After Wednesday night's episode, Jacqueline's parents posed outside the Palazzo's sunny courtyard and wrote: "We're here for #junglejac come on our girl your doing great."

The family also posed for a snap on the plane from Heathrow Airport with their granddaughters, but Dan was missing from the social media snap.

He has been busy updating his own followers with posts of him supporting Jacqueline through her various ordeals in camp.

During the nightly episodes, Dan has taken to his Instagram to share his live reactions with fans and keep them updated on how he's coping without his wife-of-two years.

The EastEnders actress suffered major heartbreak during her time on the show after campmate Myles Stephenson, 28, told her Dan, 28, had a fling with Love Island star Gabby Allen last year, something both deny.

But as he arrived at Brisbane aiport this week, Dan insisted Jacqueline had "nothing to worry about".

"Listen, she is my wife, I have got nothing to worry about, nothing to think about. I am just coming out to support her and that is the only reason I am here," he told the Mirror.

"I am just excited to see her and the last person on my mind is Myles or anyone else."

Asked if he had anything to say to Myles, who dated Gabby, 27, Dan said: "Nah. I have got nothing to say to him or anything else, only my wife."
