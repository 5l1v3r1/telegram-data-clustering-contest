Leave Mmebusem alone -Mzbel hits hard at critics

29 Nov 2019, 15:52 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Popular Ghanaian musician, Belinda Nana Ekua Amoah known in the showbiz circles as Mzbel has reacted to a recent post by the founder and leader of London Prayer Center, Pastor Brian Boatemg & actress Joselyn Dumas over Mmebusem Ghana Jesus.

Reacting to the post, the “16 years” hitmaker indicated that Jesus should fight his own fight if indeed Mmebusem is mocking him.

The musician added that the comic actor should be left alone to continue with his hilarious works.

Mzbel wrote; “If this is truly a mockery let Jesus fight his own fight! Live Mmebusem alone ah!”

This comes after Pastor Brian Boateng in am Instagram post warmed Mmebusem to put an end to this madness because he is making a mockery of Jesus.

Renowned actress and television personality Joselyn Dumas also supported the pastor’s comment describing it as blasphemous.
