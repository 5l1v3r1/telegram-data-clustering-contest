Meister Launches 10 New Colorways of the Ultra Slim Watch for Black Friday

The perfect unisex gift.

29 Nov 2019, 15:45 by Nate Louis

Fresh off its collaboration with Marvel for an Avengers: Endgame capsule collection, Meister is launching 10 new colorways of the refined Ultra Slim watch for its Winter 2019 collection. A special offering for Black Friday, each new colorway of the Ultra Slim will be limited to only 50 pieces.

The Ultra Slim watch serves as an important mark for the evolution the Meister brand. The design is based off the flagship Ambassador model, updated with 37mm case slimmed down to just 8.6mm thick. The accessory is a versatile unisex timepiece, with a clean looking aesthetic, stainless steel or leather band and roman numerals for hour markers. The water resistant watch will now feature a multitude of colorways, so whether you’re looking for the perfect gift or shopping for yourself this Black Friday, there’s something for everybody.

Don’t miss out on your chance to cop any of the 10 new colorways of Meister’s Ultra Slim watch, which are available now on the brand’s website with prices starting at $150 USD.
