Akshay Kumar And Badshah Express Their Anguish on Priyanka Reddy's Brutal Rape And Killing

29 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Amruta Patel

After news of 26-year-old Hyderabad based veterinarian Dr. Priyanka Reddy being brutally murdered and charred to death made headlines, many actors have been expressing their anguish over the same. Akshay Kumar and rapper Badshah too showed their anger and disappointment on the social media handle

Once again the social media platform has been fired up with anger, asking for justice for an inhumane act meted out to 26-year-old veterinarian Dr. Priyanka Reddy. A Hyderabad based vet was charred to death after being raped on the Hyderabad-Bengaluru highway on Thursday and has awakened and united Indians on the social media platform. According to reports on various portals, the deceased was sexually assaulted before she was murdered. This brutal act also got attention from Bollywood celebs, raising their voice against it.

Joining the ongoing issue, actor Akshay Kumar and Rapper Badshah have voiced their opinion and shared their disappointment towards the same. Kumar in his tweet recalling some similar incidences in the past seeks for the laws of the nation to be stricter. His Tweet read, “Whether it is #PriyankaReddy in Hyderabad, #Roja in Tamil Nadu or the law student gangraped in Ranchi,we seem to be losing it as a society.
