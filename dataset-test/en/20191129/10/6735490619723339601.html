‘Stranger Things’ Kids Have A Twitter War Over Rudolph The Red-Nosed Reindeer

29 Nov 2019, 10:31 by Brent Furdyk

An imaginary reindeer led to a real-life disagreement between two “Stranger Things” stars and their fans are loving it.

It all started when Gaten Matarazzo, who plays Dustin on Netflix’s supernatural hit, shared a video on Twitter in which he opines about Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer.

“No one likes Rudolph, alright?” rants Matarazzo in that clip, hilariously putting on the persona of a disgruntled, complaining “Grandpa Gaten.” “He has a shiny nose, that’s all he’s got going for him.”
