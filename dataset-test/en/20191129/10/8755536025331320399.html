Lauren Conrad shares first photo of her baby son Charlie on Thanksgiving

So cute!

29 Nov 2019, 10:24 by Kendra Becker

Lauren Conrad has shared the first photo of her baby son Charlie Wolf, as she marked her first Thanksgiving as a mother-of-two.

Taking to Instagram, the former reality star shared a sweet photo of her holding her one-month-old son on the beach.

The 33-year-old posed alongside her husband William Tell, and their two-year-old son Liam James.

Lauran captioned the post: “So much to be thankful for this year 💛 Wishing everyone a wonderful holiday!”

Lauren and William announced Charlie’s arrival back in October, but she hasn’t shared a photo of him until now.

The couple tied the knot back in 2014.
