How South Africans really feel about Black Friday, according to Twitter

29 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Shereesa Moodley

It’s finally Black Friday, and while some are maxing out their credit cards at both real and virtual shopping centres, others are trolling social media with their thoughts and feelings about the day.

The funniest tweets to come out of the #BlackFriday2019 trend all seem to agree that celebrating the day is just not worth it.
