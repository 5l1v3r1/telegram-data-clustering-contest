Our Legacy Launches Sustainable WORK SHOP Scheme Online

Shop the limited-edition upcycled pieces while stocks last.

29 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Eric Brain

Stockholm-based mainstay Our Legacy has expanded its WORK SHOP division to its online platform, introducing its reworked products to a global audience.

The WORK SHOP initiative is sustainable as it gives new life to old fabrics and garments, with each piece being reworked or upcycled into a new piece. By deconstructing, over-dyeing, screen printing and more, Our Legacy can offer truly unique pieces designed in its Stockholm WORK SHOP atelier that ranges from T-shirts and sweatshirts to more standout pieces.

Key items releasing online include a pair of Converse complete with a fitting vintage finish, a selection of retro-futuristic sunglasses, a leather vest sporting a rear reptile graphic, and most notably, a stunning black-and-brown mohair jacket.
