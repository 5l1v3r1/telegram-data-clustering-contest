Garda graduation to take place in Templemore

29 Nov 2019, 10:06 by Reporter

The latest cohort of probationer gardai will graduate from the Garda College Templemore this afternoon following eight months of training. 

The passing out ceremony of Intake 191 will begin shortly after 11am on Friday, November 29.

An Taoiseach Leo Varadkar and Minister for Justice and Equality Charles Flanagan are expected to be in attendance alongside Garda Commissioner Drew Harris.
