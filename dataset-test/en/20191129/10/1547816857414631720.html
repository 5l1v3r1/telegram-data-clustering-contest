Taapsee Pannu's Romantic Thriller Gets A Hero In Vikrant Massey

29 Nov 2019, 10:09 by Amruta Patel

Actor Vikrant Massey acting prowess, is bagging him lucrative projects. According to reports the actor has been considered to play a male lead opposite Taapsee Pannu in Vinil Mathew’s next. Said to be a romantic thriller, the film will be going on floors in January 2020

Actor Vikrant Massey acting prowess is scoring him films with some of the A-listed actresses in the entertainment business. Marking his beginning in 2020, would be Meghna Gulzar’s Chhapaak allowing the actor to share screen space with Deepika Padukone, reports suggest, the Half Girlfriend actor has been considered opposite Taapsee Pannu to be helmed by Vinil Mathew. Good going BOY!  The makers have heard to be zeroing on him to play the male lead in the film opposite the Saand Ki Aankh actress. 

As per reports in Mumbai Mirror, this venture helmed by Vinil, will be a romantic thriller going on floors in January 2020. Reason of the makers to consider Vikrant in this pivotal role, was their desire to present a fresh pair on the celluloid and thus, letting the audience watch the two sharing screen without any pre-conceived notions.
