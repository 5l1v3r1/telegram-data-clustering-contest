Gemma Collins gives us all the festive feels as she braves the ice rink again

29 Nov 2019, 10:34

It’s not even December yet, but Gemma Collins won’t let that stop her from getting into the Christmas spirit.

Celebrating the opening of a new ice rink at Eastwell Manor Champneys Health and Spa, on Thursday night, the Towie star rocked on up to the venue in a bright red Santa hat covered in sequins.

And while getting everyone into the festive mood, the 38-year-old threw on her skates before braving the ice rink once again.

Earlier this year, the TV personality appeared on ITV’s hit show Dancing On Ice where she attempted to show off her moves with her partner Matt Evers.

However, despite giving it her best shot, the GC ended up falling flat on her face not once but twice when entertaining the audience during the live shows.

This hasn’t put her off the ice though, as she glided around the rink and was even pictured stopping for selfies with fans at last night’s event.

The star is pretty much the skating pro and will be returning to the much-loved competition for their annual Christmas special.

A source told The Sun Online: ‘[Gemma] wants to be the showstopper for the Christmas special, and is proving to producers she is ready for it.

‘She’ll be giving a jaw-dropping performance when she returns to the rink alongside [former Dancing on Ice winners] Ray Quinn and Jake Quickenden.’
