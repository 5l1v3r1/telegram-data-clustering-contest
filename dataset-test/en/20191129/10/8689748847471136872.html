Joselyn Dumas descends on Mmebusem; says his actions are blasphemy at the highest level

29 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Adu Sarpeah

Ghanaian entertainer Mmebusem, regularly known as “Ghana Jesus” has come under intense criticism by religious folks taking into consideration his comical philosophy – mimicking the works of Jesus Christ.

Following his viral video, Veteran Ghanaian gospel musician Cindy Thompson advised the actor to stop using Jesus’ name in vain but Mmebusem clapped back accordingly saying Cindy doesn’t even know how Jesus looks like.

READ ALSO:–Cindy Thompson condemns Mmebusem’s latest video

Now, it seems actress Joselyn Dumas is the newest person to join forces to fight against Mmebusem. It all started when the president of Brian Jones Outreach Ministries headquartered in London admonished Mmebusem to stop mimicking Jesus in the name of comedy.

Fast forward, actress Joselyn Dumas from no where commented and her response shows that she’s is not in support of the creativity of Mmebusem.

“Enough of this nonsense. I don’t support this….Christian Youth of Ghana, our God cannot be mocked in the name of comedy….” Brian Amoateng wrote on Facebook.

While commenting under Brian’s post, Joselyn Dumas wrote: “Thank you. We are now condoning blasphemy All in the name of comedy smh.”
