YAY! Jennifer Aniston’s Thanksgiving celebrations were all about reunions, as her ex-husband Justin Theroux, along with FRIENDS’ co-star Courtney Cox, joined the fun

Yesterday marked Thanksgiving 2019 across and all our stars celebrated it with utmost enthusiasm. One such feast took place at Rachel AKA Jennifer Aniston’s residence and it was all about reunions for the actress! The celebrations weren’t only attended by FRIENDS co-star and her good friend Courtney Cox, but her ex-husband Justin Theroux, too, was a part of the memorable night. It saw Justin smiling wide and spreading his arm our wider with Jason alongside him, while Jennifer leaned onto his shoulder for the picture. Courtney Cox and company were all smiles at the back. In his caption, Justin described the celebrations as ‘fakesgiving’ and thanked for such friends and such nights.
