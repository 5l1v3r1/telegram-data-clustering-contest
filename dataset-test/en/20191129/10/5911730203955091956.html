Shop small at Seminole Heights Holiday Shop Hop Saturday

29 Nov 2019, 10:07 by WFTS Digital Staff

With all the madness of Black Friday and Cyber Monday, don't forget to support local businesses for Small Business Saturday.

Several businesses in Seminole Heights are taking part in a Holiday Shop Hop Saturday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. that wraps up with a block party at 6:30 p.m.

You can win prizes by getting a bingo card stamped by six businesses.

Experts say when you shop small, 67 cents of every dollar stays in your community, and business owners say they rely on this support.
