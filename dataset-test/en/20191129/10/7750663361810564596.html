Wolves to rival Liverpool, Manchester United for Leeds United loanee Ben White?

Brighton & Hove Albion defender Ben White has reportedly emerged as a potential transfer target for Wolverhampton Wanderers.

29 Nov 2019, 10:19 by Darren Plant

White has been a huge success on loan at Leeds United this season, contributing to a backline which has conceded just 10 goals from 18 matches in the Championship.

As it stands, the 22-year-old is expected to return to Brighton at the end of the campaign to compete for a first-team spot at the Amex Stadium.

However, according to The Athletic, there are an increasing amount of clubs who are interested in signing the centre-back.
