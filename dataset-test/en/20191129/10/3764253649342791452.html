Fikayo Tomori, Chelsea star, says he likes Burna Boy's song 'Anybody'

29 Nov 2019, 10:54

- Fikayo Tomori has stated clearly that he likes Burna Boy's song 'Anybody'

- The Chelsea star said that the music is capable of getting him to dance-floor

- Chelsea are currently occupying fourth position on the Premier League standings

Chelsea superstar Fikayo Tomori recently snubbed the Super Eagles of Nigeria to play for England national team, but the defender still hold the nation of his father high in his heart.

Fikayo Tomori explained recently that he chose England over Nigeria because the Three Lions were the first to invite him into the national team.

And now, the 21-year-old Fikayo Tomori has revealed his love for popular Nigerian musician Burna Boy.
