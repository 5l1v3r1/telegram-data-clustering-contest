Black Friday Settings in Zim…..Customers flood Edgars since 3am

The black Friday specials have sent Zimbaweans into a flurry of activity in a bid to get good discounts on all goods.

Black Friday is the name given to the shopping day after Thanksgiving in the US where retailers give huge discounts.

It was originally called Black Friday because the volume of shoppers created traffic accidents and sometimes even violence however there’s a more sinister version involving the slave trade.

Police coined the phrase to describe the mayhem surrounding the congestion of pedestrian and auto traffic in downtown shopping areas.
