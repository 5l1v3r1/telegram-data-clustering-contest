Lando Norris has new special helmet design for 2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix

29 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Bobby Vincent

McLaren driver Lando Norris has a new special helmet design for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix this weekend, with the face of his performance engineer Andrew Jarvis on the helmet. Can Norris get in the points this weekend in the final race of his rookie Formula 1 season?
