Do you know this man? RCMP looking for suspect in Colwood theft

29 Nov 2019, 10:37

West Shore RCMP have released a surveillance photo of a suspect in a theft at the Petro-Canada gas station at 1879 Island Hwy in Colwood.

The theft happened Nov. 21 at 6:47 p.m.

Police said a store employee was doing some repairs and had put a tool bag on the floor. A man in the store took the tool bag as he left.

The suspect is described as 30 to 40 years old. He was wearing a black jacket, blue jeans and black shoes.

Call West Shore RCMP at 250-474-2264 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-TIPS (8477) with any information.
