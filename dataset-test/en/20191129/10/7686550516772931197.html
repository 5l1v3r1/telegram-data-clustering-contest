Carphone Warehouse is giving away FREE Nintendo Switch Lites for Black Friday

Shoppers who take out a Samsung or iPhone contract can get a free gaming console

29 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Jonathan Yates

Carphone Warehouse is giving away free Nintendo Switch Lites for Black Friday.

The phone company is offering customers a free gift worth up to £269 when they take out a contract as part of its sale.

This includes the hugely popular Nintendo Switch Lite which is likely to be at the top of plenty of Christmas lists this year.

To get the free gaming console Black Friday shoppers need to take out a contract on a phone with Carphone Warehouse.

The models included are iPhone 8, 7 and 6s, Samsung Galaxy A20e, A40 and A70, Huawei P30 Lite and Doro 6520.

The best offer is a £25.99 per month contract with the Samsung A40 model, and you would get a free Nintendo Switch Lite.

If you're after the Nintendo Switch Lite Blue, then that colour comes free with a number of Samsung and Huawei models as well as the Doro.

As well as the Nintendo Switch Lite customers can also get Apple Airpods, PS4 Bundle, Google Nest Hub and other headphones, speakers and games as part of the offer.
