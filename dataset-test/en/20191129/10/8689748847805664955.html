Movie producer Evander wins big at Zafaa Awards 2019 with ‘Asantewaa’ movie (+ Photos

29 Nov 2019, 10:57 by Adu Sarpeah

As earlier reported by ghbase.com, Evander Kwame Agyemang who doubles as cinematographer and director was cited by Zulu Africa Film Academy Awards (ZAFAA) in the name of his self-directed movie christened “Asantewaa”.

The above-mentioned movie received 9 solid nominations and the good news is that Evander Kwame Agyemang didn’t return from the awards show which took place inside Transcorp Hilton hotel at Abuja Nigeria on 24th November 2019 empty-handed. He won in five different categories. Namely; best film, best producer, best actor – male, Best actor – female as well as best supporting – male.

Speaking to the press after the awards, Evander Kwame Agyemang made it emphatic that he’s indebted to the Ultimate Reality or God for giving him the creative mind to do anything movie. Besides, he sounded optimistic that the awards won will motivate him to do better as more glories are ahead.

Touching on what movie enthusiasts especially his fans should expect, Evander Kwame Agyemang said that a lot more in the pipeline although he can’t disclose. But one thing he can say is that movie lovers should expect greater works in 2020.

For clarity sake, Evander Kwame Agyeman is a Ghanaian filmmaker who has over the years prove beyond reasonable doubt that the popular saying “experience is the best teacher” is indeed true. Evander as popularly called has humbly served and learned film making for 18yrs with experience from both international and local bodies in terms of theory (School) and practical (Work).
