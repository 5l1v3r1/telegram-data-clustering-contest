Banned driver caught in uninsured car in Telford

29 Nov 2019, 13:03 by Rob Smith

A banned driver who was caught in an uninsured car in Telford was stopped and arrested.

Police stopped the Ford Fiesta in Southall Road, Dawley, this afternoon and after checking the driver's license discovered that he was disqualified from driving.

He was subsequently arrested and the car was seized.
