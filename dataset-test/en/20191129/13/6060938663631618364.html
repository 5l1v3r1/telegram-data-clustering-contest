When asked if my cup is half-full or half-empty, my only response is that I’m thankful I have a cup: Preity Zinta on Thanksgiving

29 Nov 2019, 13:13 by TNN

Bollywood actress Preity Zinta reached out to her 6.8 million followers on Instagram to share her Thanksgiving wishes. In the video, the actress is seen with her husband Gene Goodenough and best friend Shagun Khanna at a mall in London.

“When asked if my cup is half-full or half-empty my only response is that I am thankful I have a cup ! Today I’m even more grateful for the life I have & for all the wonderful people that make my life special. People I love & people who love me. So this Thanksgiving I want to say a big thank you from the bottom of my heart to the Universe, my friends & family & to my fans for making my life so incredibly special. Thank you, I’m so grateful & humbled #Happythanksgiving to everyone out there 😘 #gratitute #grateful #love #ting (sic),” read the caption. Check it out!
