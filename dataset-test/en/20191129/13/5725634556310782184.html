Alta Fox Opportunities Fund, L.P. Q3 2019 Quarterly Letter

29 Nov 2019, 13:10 by Fund Letters

Summary

  • Alta Fox Capital is a long/short hedge fund.
  • In Q3 2019, the fund decreased 2.21% gross and 2.10% net.
  • This compared favorably to the -2.40% return for the Russell 2000 and -5.4% return for the Russell Microcap index, but unfavorably to the S&P 500's 1.70% return.
