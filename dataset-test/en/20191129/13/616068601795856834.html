Have you seen Bill?

29 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Jon Manchester

RCMP are requesting the public's help in locating a missing Kelowna resident. 

William Byron Williams was last seen leaving his residence Nov. 24, and has since failed to return.

Williams also goes by the name “Bill” and “Bear.”

He is described as a Caucasian male, 63 years old, six feet two inches tall, 300 pounds, with grey hair, a full beard, and brown eyes. He wears glasses, and frequently uses a cane or walker.

"Police are concerned for William’s well-being," says Const. Solana Paré. "His friends report that it is out of character for him to be out of contact for this long."

Anyone with information is urged to contact their local police, or CrimeStoppers at 1-800-222-8477 (TIPS).
