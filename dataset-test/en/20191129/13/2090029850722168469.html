Man shares how he was ‘healed’ from HIV, miraculously by God

29 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

A man identified as King D has shared an extraordinary news with people, thanking God for healing him from HIV.

According to King D, he had HIV last year and had been living with it until he got healed by God. King D said, he went for a medical test a month ago and realised that he tested negative to the virus.

Thanking God for healing him, King D assured others that God truly heals. He also went on to reveal his intentions to get married and have kids.

Read his post below:

Good morning, A year ago from today, I found out that I had HIV…A month ago I found out that its no where in my bloodstreams, I can have kids and I can’t give it to anyone. God heals I am thankful. This is the best thanksgiving. Thank you Jesus. Keep fighting.
