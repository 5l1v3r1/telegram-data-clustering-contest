Badshah Got Dumped By His Ex For Choosing To Rap As His Profession

29 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Amruta Patel

Popular and celebrated rapper in Bollywood and Indian music industry, Badshah revealed in his latest interaction that he got dumped by his now ex-GF for opting for a career in the field of music. What a loss lady! Coming from a conservative family, the rapper also revealed having a hard time with his family to convince them to let him pursue his career

‘Kuch pane ke liye, kuch khona padta hai’ this phrase stands true in case of the popular and successful rapper, Badshah. The rapper became a household name in the last few years, with his back to back hits; creating a fanbase for himself for his cool and happening numbers in Bollywood movies as well as his singles. But the rapper in his recent interview with an entertainment portal, Pinkvilla.com, revealed making several scarifies and also being dumped by his girlfriend, for choosing this profession of rapping to earn his livelihood.

The rapper revealed loving his now ex-GF from the age of 3, confessing about loving her from an early age, did warm the cockles of our heart. But she turned her back on him due to his profession, as according to her that was not a suitable profession. “I was madly in love with this girl. I was probably three years old when I fell in love with her.
