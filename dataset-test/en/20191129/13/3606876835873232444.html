MPs brother brutally murdered by Nephew

29 Nov 2019, 13:17 by Mumbi Mutuko

MP's brother killed in cold blood

Residents in Kanduyi Constituency were left shocked after the brother of area MP Athanas Misiko Wafula Wamunyinyi was murdered.

The deceased, Joseph Wamalwa Wekesa, is alleged to have been stabbed on the chest by his nephew.

Witnesses claimed that the two had a fought earlier during the preparation of a memorial of one of their kin.

Vandalized home

The nephew identified as Nicholas Simiyu Wafula is alleged to have stabbed his uncle who had earlier injured his nephew's thumb.

Bungoma South OCS George Odongo stated that Wamalwa had also injured his nephew on the head.

After the fight, Wafula took a kitchen knife and followed his uncle to his home where he stabbed him on the chest severally.

The nephew is also alleged to have vandalized Wamalwa's home and killed his dogs.
