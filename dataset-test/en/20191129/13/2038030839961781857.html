'Vikings' spin-off series ordered by Netflix

29 Nov 2019, 13:07 by Thinus Ferreira

Cape Town – Vikings, that was shown on DStv and Showmax in South Africa, will conclude with its sixth and final season, set to start streaming on the popular platform on Thursday, 5 December, but, good news for fans: the popular show is getting a spin-off!

Netflix has commissioned MGM Television to create and produce Vikings: Valhalla as a sequel drama series that will be set 100 years after the original Vikings series.
