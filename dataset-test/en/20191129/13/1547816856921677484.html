Former Bigg Boss Contestant Nehha Pendse Confirms Tying The Knot With Shardul Singh Byas

29 Nov 2019, 13:34 by Amruta Patel

Wedding bells are ringing for actress Nehha Pendse, the actress after her hush-hush engagement confirmed taking the plunge with her fiancé Shardul Singh Byas on January 5, 2020. The nuptials will take place in Pune in the presence of her near and dear ones

Wedding Bells are Ringing! Former Bigg Boss contestant Nehha Pendse is soon going to be a bride. Becoming a household name post her stay in Bigg Boss house during the 12th season and post her TV show, May I Come In Madam, the actress is set to take the plunge with her longtime beau. Exchanging rings with her beau Shardul Singh Byas in a hush-hush ceremony in the month of August, the actress spill the beans about taking her relationship to next level by tying the knot to Shardul in 2020.

In an interview with Bollywoodlife.com, the actress did not give out much deets about her D-day but revealed going around the holy fire on January 5, 2020. To be held in Pune, this special day in the actress’ life would be a hush-hush affair in the presence of her family members and close friends. Making an official announcement of her engagement, she had posted an image from her vacay in Milan, posing with Shardul and a diamond on her ring finger.
