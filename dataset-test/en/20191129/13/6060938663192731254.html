Ajay Devgn and Kajol flaunt their impeccable chemistry on the cover of Filmfare

29 Nov 2019, 13:12 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

One of Bollywood's most adorable power couple Ajay Devgn and Kajol have been giving their fans major couple goals since the time they have come together. The couple, who is happily married now and even parents to two adorable kids: Nysa and Yug have come together to end the year with a bang.

Featuring on the cover of Filmfare's December issue, Ajay and Kajol look chic and elegant as they pose together in complimenting back outfits. While the 'Golmaal Again' actor can be seen donning a sophisticated black tuxedo, Kajol, on the other hand, is a sight to behold in a black shimmery outfit.
