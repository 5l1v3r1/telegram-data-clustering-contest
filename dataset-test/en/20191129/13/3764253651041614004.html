Unai Emery's sack: Arsenal hold talks with ex-Juventus boss Massimiliano Allegri

29 Nov 2019, 13:55

- Unai Emery's sack has been linked with Arsenal hiring former Juventus manager Max Allegri

- It is understood that the Gunners hierarchy has been in talks with the Italian gaffer

- Allegri won five Serie A titles with the Bianconeri and qualified for two Champions League finals

Unai Emery's sack has got Arsenal talking with Massimiliano Allegri as their next manager, according to reports

The Spanish manager was sacked by the London outfit on Friday, November 29, after series of poor results.

Arsenal have now gone without a win in seven matches, their worst run in 27 years and their latest 2-1 loss to Frankfurt in the Europa League ended Emery's 18-month term with the Gunners.
