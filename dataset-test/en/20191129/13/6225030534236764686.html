Take two: Instagrand returning to action at Del Mar

29 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Mary Schweitzer

The talented Instagrand looks set to kickstart his career again when he faces four rivals in Del Mar’s fourth race on Sunday. The contest is an allowance/optional claiming race for 3-year-olds and up going 6 1/2 furlongs with post time is scheduled for 4:30 p.m. ET.

The race will mark the first start back for Instagrand since he underwent surgery in May to repair a fractured sesamoid in his left-hind leg. The injury was discovered after the colt finished eighth in the May 4 Pat Day Mile (G3) at Churchill Downs.

On Twitter, trainer Don Chatlos expressed his relief that a comeback race finally filled for this talented charge after a previous comeback attempt was rained out last month at Belmont Park.
