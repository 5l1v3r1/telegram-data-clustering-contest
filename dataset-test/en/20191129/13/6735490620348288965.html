Weather Man Gets Contradicted By Siri During Broadcast

29 Nov 2019, 13:29 by Corey Atad

It doesn’t get more awkward than your digital voice assistant being triggered on live TV.

On Thursday, BBC meteorologist Tomasz Schafernaker was in the middle of a report predicting snow in the forecast for parts of the U.S. Midwest when he got interrupted by Siri.

The voice coming through on his Apple Watch read its own weather report, contradicting him by saying there was no snow expected.
