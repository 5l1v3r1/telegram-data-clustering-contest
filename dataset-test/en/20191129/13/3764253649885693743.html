Unai Emery: Fans believe Arsenal intentionally sacked former boss on Black Friday

29 Nov 2019, 13:52

- Unai Emery's sack has brought different reactions from Arsenal fans on social media

- Some felt sorry for the Spanish gaffer while other had strong words for the ex-PSG boss

- But some section of the club faithfuls believes the sack intentionally happened on Black Friday

Unai Emery's sack happened on Black Friday because the club would only have to pay half his contract, according to some Arsenal fans.

The north Londoners announced the sacking of the Spaniard a day after the Gunners lost 2-1 to Eintracht Frankfurt in the Europa League.

But Arsenal faithfuls had a different reaction on social media for the former Paris saint-Germain manager.
