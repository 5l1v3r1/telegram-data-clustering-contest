Rahim Yar Khan: Policemen among 15 people injured in clash between two groups

29 Nov 2019, 11:13 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

RAHIM YAR KHAN (Dunya News) – At least 15 people including police personnel have sustained severe injuries in a scuffle between two groups over land dispute in Rahim Yar Khan today (Friday).

According to details, the incident took place in the presence of police officers who later fled the scene as the situation intensified.

Moreover, the injured have been shifted to the nearby hospital for medical assistance.
