Kerala: Class 11 student accuses sub-inspector of sexually assaulting her

In a shocking incident, a Class 11th girl has alleged that a sub-inspector touched her inappropriately in Thiruvananthapuram. 

29 Nov 2019, 11:48 by P S Gopikrishnan Unnithan

In a shocking incident, a Class 11th girl has alleged that a sub-inspector touched her inappropriately in Thiruvananthapuram.

Police have filed a case of sexual assault under POCSO Act against the accused and a probe is underway.

The victim was familiar with the accused and his children who lived in the same neighborhood.

Meanwhile, the accused is absconding.
