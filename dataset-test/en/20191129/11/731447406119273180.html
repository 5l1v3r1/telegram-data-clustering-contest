RE-TEXTURED festival returns to London in 2020

dBridge, SOPHIE, Shackleton and Helena Hauff are all booked for krankbrother's early April event.

29 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Mark Smith

krankbrother's RE-TEXTURED festival will return to London next year.

The four-day event will run from April 2nd to the 5th across seven venues including Tobacco Dock, Southbank Centre and BFI. Covering the cross-section between architecture, the arts and electronic music, the festival will explore the theme "Connection" in six areas called re-connect, re-play, re-write, re-see, re-mind and re-think.

The first lineup announcement has a host of live performances from artists like Donato Dozzy, SOPHIE, British Murder Boys, dBridge, Shackleton, Hiro Kone and Actress. Other booked acts include JASSS, Silvia Kastel, Helena Hauff, Ben UFO, Mala and Rebekah.
