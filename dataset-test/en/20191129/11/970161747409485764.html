What is Aldi's Kevin the Carrot and how much is the toy now selling for?

29 Nov 2019, 11:48

Aldi was one of the first supermarkets to release their much-anticipated Christmas TV advert for 2019 and the store did not disappoint, with another adventure for Kevin the Carrot bought to life on our screens.

The supermarket chain took inspiration from show Peaky Blinders for the ad, with Russell Sprout and the Leafy Blinders threatening to take back Christmas.

Despite starting the advert in hot water, Kevin prevailed and closed out the 70 second segment with a The Greatest Showman inspired, veg-filled routine to the tune of Let Me Entertain You by Robbie Williams.

What is Aldi’s Kevin the Carrot?

Aldi’s Kevin the Carrot is not only the star of their Christmas TV advert this year, but he has been a staple in their festive ads since 2016.

In the past he’s featured in an Orient Express themed advert, as well as the supermarket’s fairy-tale inspired offering from last year.
