New Years Day Announce UK And Europe 2020 Tour

New Years Day are coming back to the UK and Europe at the start of 2020

29 Nov 2019, 11:44

Fresh off tour with Halestorm and In This Moment, New Years Day have revealed a headline tour of the UK and Europe for next February and March.

Kicking off at The Key Club in Leeds, UK, on February 18, the continental run will end at the Legend Club in Milan, Italy, on March 8.

“We are so excited to headline in the UK and Europe for real!” says vocalist Ash Costello. “We loved being the opener for Halestorm and In This Moment, but we are ready now to bring the full New Years Day experience to our extended UK and Europe family!”
