BREAKING Man who shot dead heavily pregnant ex wife with crossbow jailed for life

Ramanodge Unmathallegadoo, 51, will serve at least 33 years behind bars for the "brutal and evil" attack

29 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Dave Burke

A man who killed his pregnant ex-wife with a crossbow in a "brutal and evil" attack has been jailed for life.

Ramanodge Unmathallegadoo, 51, will serve at least 33 years behind bars after being sentenced at the Old Bailey.
