Amazon's Black Friday Sale Cuts the Price of Beats Solo3 Headphones by Over £100

29 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Shabana Arif

Yes, they launched in 2016, but they're still sporting a £250 price tag which has been almost cut in half for today only.

The Beats Solo3 Wireless On-Ear Headphones are currently £139 on Amazon just for today, although the 44 per cent discount only applies to the Indigo, Magenta, and Violet colourways which is fine because they're amongst the best colours on offer. I have spoken.

If you disagree, then you'll have to pony up a bit more cash depending on which variant floats your boat. Satin Gold is 32 per cent off and will set you back £169, Red has been discounted by 36 per cent and is £159 until the sale wraps up at the end of the day, Matte Gold is 30 per cent off bringing the price down to £175, while Gloss White is £179.99 with 28 per cent off.

Even thought they're three years old, the headphones are decent enough for the cheaper price, but let's be honest - you're mostly paying for the brand. If you're not fussed about noise cancelling, you can't go too wrong with these.
