Missing child found

29 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Darren Handschuh

UPDATE 12:12 p.m.

Surrey RCMP said the three-year-old boy who went missing earlier this morning has been located and is safe. 

ORIGINAL STORY

A frantic search is underway to find a missing three-year-old boy.

Surrey RCMP said Christian McDonald was last seen at approximately 10:45 a.m. this morning at his home in the 6900 block of 135 Street in Surrey .

McDonald is a three-year-old Caucasian child, with blonde hair and may be wearing a grey and white, hooded winter jacket, and green dinosaur boots.

The child may have wandered away from his home.

If located please call 911.
