Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi to name alliance General Secretary in days

29 Nov 2019, 11:02

Mubasher: Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi on Friday said that it will name a general secretary in the upcoming days as a first step towards boosting the operational efficiency of the Franco-Japanese auto alliance, according to a press release.

The new executive will be a major part of coordinating and facilitating several projects undertaken, the alliance said after an operating board meeting.

The general secretary is expected to report to the Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi operating board and chief executives.
