Flashback – Masango's World-Class Panenka Against Pirates

29 Nov 2019, 11:59 by SL Social

In his prime, Mandla Masango was considered one of the best players in the PSL while plying his trade with Kaizer Chiefs. His confidence was perhaps best illustrated when he pulled off a Panenka penalty in a Carling Black Label Cup match a few years ago. Check it out!

Now 30, Masango finds himself clubless after leaving Danish outfit Randers FC in 2018. Before departing for Europe, however, he was a vital cog in Stuart Baxter's Amakhosi outfit in 2015 and came good as a crowd favourite in the process.
