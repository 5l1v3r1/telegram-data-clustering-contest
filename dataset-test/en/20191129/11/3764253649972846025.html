Unai Emery sets Arsenal's worst winless run in 27 years after Frankfurt defeat

29 Nov 2019, 11:47

- Unai Emery has set Arsenal's worst run of form since 1992 after loss to Frankfurt

- The Spanish boss has not won seven games, drawing five and losing two of them

- Arsenal will travel to Norwich, then play Brighton before taking on West Ham in their next three fixtures

Unai Emery side's loss to Eintracht Frankfurt makes it Arsenal's seventh straight game without a win, their worst run before Arsene Wenger was hired.

The Bundesliga side came from a goal down to win the match by 2-1 as fans at the Emirates booed the team and other calling for the Spanish manager's sack.

The Gunners have not won a single match in their last seven outings, recording five draws and two losses, the worst run before legendary manager Wenger was appointed in 1996.
