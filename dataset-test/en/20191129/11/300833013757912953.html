How To Fix An iPhone That Won’t Ring

29 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Elizabeth Kartini

Do you miss lots of phone calls because your iPhone won’t ring? Don’t worry, you are not the only user who experienced this problem as it bothered many people. The issue is quite annoying because you will see missed calls on the screen even though the phone is right next to you, but it may also be confusing especially if you are sure the ringer is on, the phone is not muted and you didn’t hear any sound. In this article, we are going to discuss the reasons why this issue occurs, and we are going to share several solutions to help you fix iPhone not ringing problem.

Why My iPhone Doesn’t Ring?

Let’s list a few reasons why this issue may appear. Perhaps you made some changes in iPhone settings or you let your kids play with your phone? Maybe you enabled “Do not disturb”? A software glitch can be responsible for the problem, but this annoying issue can also appear right after you update the iOS software on your iPhone. Water damage or the fact you dropped your device, are among the reasons that can cause iPhone not ringing issue.
