Nokia 2.3 (TA-1206) passes certification in Egypt before the official launch

29 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Nayan

Before its expected launch on December 5, the Nokia 2.3 has been appearing in various certifications including Malaysia and Canada recently. The Dual-SIM variant TA-1206 that we have originally seen appearing in FCC certification and a distributor database has now passed certification in Egypt.

This is significant because the Nokia smartphone launch event is scheduled to happen in Cairo, capital of Egypt and will be live-streamed on the official YouTube channel.

Nokia 2.3 Dual-SIM variant TA-1206 earlier appeared in Russian and FCC certifications thereby revealing many details about itself including the Single-SIM support. Coming to the leaked details, Nokia 2.3 may pack a display size of greater than 6.1-inch with HD resolution.

It features a bigger battery of 3920 mAH capacity as compared to Nokia 2.2. Nokia 2.3 also comes with Bluetooth 5.0 as compared to Bluetooth 4.2 on Nokia 2.2.
