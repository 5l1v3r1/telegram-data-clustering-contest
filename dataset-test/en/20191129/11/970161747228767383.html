I'm A Celebrity's Vanessa White says she 'lost feeling in her legs' from hunger

29 Nov 2019, 11:04

Former I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here star Vanessa White reportedly begged TV bosses for food after she lost the feeling in her legs from being so hungry.

Speaking at the Amazon’s Home of Black Friday launch event, the former Saturday’s singer revealed that she mainly struggled with hunger during her time in the jungle.

‘When I was on the show I lost the feeling in my legs because I was just so hungry,’ she began.

‘I begged the producers for food so they ended up giving me a Jelly Bean.’

Vanessa appeared on the show in 2017, and was the fourth celeb to be evicted from the jungle.

The former pop star’s most famous moment was during a Bushtucker trial with Corrie star Jenny McAlpine.

The duo took part in the task titled Car Cruel Karaoke, where they had to hold live critters in their mouths while humming a song that the other camper had to guess.

Although shockingly, Vanessa wasn’t very good at it as she failed to guess a song by the Spice Girls.

The show also came under fire from viewers after they branded the trial cruel.

On this year’s I’m A Celeb the topic of conversation among the campmates has been centred around food – or the lack of it.

To be fair they haven’t faired too badly with the trials and they seem to be keeping their bellies relatively full.
