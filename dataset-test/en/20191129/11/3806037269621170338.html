Stormzy Announces 'H.I.T.H.' World Tour, UK Dates, & Pre-Sale Information

Heading to an arena near you soon.

29 Nov 2019, 11:46 by Eric Brain

Stormzy has announced his H.I.T.H. World Tour dates ahead of the release of his upcoming album, Heavy Is The Head.

The album is set to feature Aitch, Burna Boy, Ed Sheeran, Headie One, H.E.R., Tiana Major9, and YEBBA with production from Fred Gibson, Fraser T Smith, T-Minus and many others. It’s Stormzy’s most creative and largest project to date, with four of the sixteen tracks already released.

His hit “Own It” featuring Ed Sheeran and Burna Boy leads the album’s pre-success, and has created much fanfare around the upcoming release. The multi-award-winning UK rapper will be heading up the UK with a huge list of arena dates, and will be continuing the H.I.T.H. World Tour in North America, Asia, Australia and New Zealand, Africa and throughout Europe.

The tour follows on from Stormzy’s impressive year of success, which included the Croydon-born MC becoming the first British rapper to ever headline Glastonbury. You can find the dates and arenas for the H.I.T.H. World Tour below.

For those looking to get tickets early, pre-order Heavy Is The Head on the official Stormzy website, and head over to the dedicated album pre-order website to ensure you get your copy both digitally and physically when the sophomore project releases on December 13. General tickets will go on sale December 6 at 9 a.m. GMT and will be available from gigst.rs/Stormzy.

In other news, The Weeknd has released his new disco-tinged single “Blinding Lights.”
