Three fatalities on Mayo roads so far this year

29 Nov 2019, 11:41

THREE deaths have occurred as a result of accidents on Mayo roads so far this year.

Twenty-nine people have suffered serious injuries in accidents, figures circulated at a meeting of Mayo County Council roads and transportation strategic policy committee reveal.

The report issued by the authority's road safety office made for positive reading, with deaths on the county's roads decreasing from nine last year to just three to date this year.

Twelve fatalities were recorded the previous year (2017).

The breakdown of fatalities on the roads in Mayo over the period from 2008 to date are:

2008: 10

2009: 10

2010: 8

2011: 12

2012: 7

2013: 6

2014: 11

2015: 13

2016: 4

2017: 12

2018: 9

2019 (to date): 3
