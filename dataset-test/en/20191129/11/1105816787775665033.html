Salman Khan's upcoming item song 'Munna Badnaam Hua' promises to be a treat for 'Dabangg' fans

29 Nov 2019, 11:36 by Entertainment Desk

Get ready, Chulbul Pandey fans! Munna is about to get very, very badnaam!

Confused? Listen up! With Dabangg 3 right around the corner, Salman Khan has finally revealed the first look from the biggest song of the upcoming film. And guess what it’s called? Yup, you’re right. Munna Badnaam Hua!

While the entire song is not out as yet, the 20-second teaser the actor has shared on his Instagram is sure to get all Dabangg fans excited. There’s colours, there’s action and of course, there’s the indomitable Salman Khan replacing Malaika Arora in the item song himself!

“Aa rahe hain bohut jald with the most bad ass song: Munna Badnaam Hua (I will be coming soon with the most bad ass song: Munna Badnaam Hua),” Khan captioned his post.
