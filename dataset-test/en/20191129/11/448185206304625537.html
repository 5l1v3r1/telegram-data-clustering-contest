IIT Guwahati Job for Junior Research Fellow

Applications are invited for a Walk-in-interview for the following post(s) in the project entitled, “Structural and functional investigation of mammalian cell entry (MCE) proteins from human pathogens: development of structure-based lead molecules” at the Department of Biosciences & Bioengineering, IIT Guwahati.

  • Post Name:         Junior Research Fellow
  • Qualification:       M.Sc, B.Tech/B.E, BVSC, B.Pharma, Any Graduate, Any Post Graduate
  • Vacancies:         01
  • Salary:    Rs. 30,250/-Per Month
  • Experience:      Fresher
  • Job Location:       Guwahati
  • Walk-In Date:      07/12/2019

Address: Conference room, BSBE, Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, Assam

Candidates have to appear in the Walk-in-Interview along with an application/CV on plain paper giving details of all educational qualifications, experience, contact address, phone no., Email etc. and submit photocopies of relevant documents at the time of interview on 07 Dec 2019 (Saturday) at 10 AM. Venue: Conference room, BSBE. Selection will be based on the performance of the candidate in the interview. Candidates will not be sent any call letters separately. An advance copy of CV may be sent to the Principal Investigator. For any clarification, contact: Shankar Prasad Kanaujia (Principal Investigator) Email: spkanaujia@iitg.ac.in
