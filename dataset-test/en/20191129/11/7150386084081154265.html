'Why didn't She Call 100?' Telangana Minister Blames Victim in Gruesome Rape and Murder Case

"This is an unfortunate incident. But why didn't she call up 100? She was an educated woman," the minister said.

29 Nov 2019, 11:29

New Delhi: A day after the burnt body of a veterinarian was found near Hyderabad raising shock and concerns over women safety, Telangana home minister kicked up a storm as he commented that the woman should have called 100, instead of police. Police is alert. She could have been saved. “This is an unfortunate incident. But why didn’t she call up 100? She was an educated woman,” the minister said which in a way shifts the blame on the victim.
