After Trump’s statement, Taliban say 'way too early' to speak of resuming talks with US

29 Nov 2019, 14:26

"The Taliban wants to make a deal and we're meeting with them and we're saying it has to be a ceasefire," Trump said during his recent visit to Afghanistan.

The Taliban said Friday it was "way too early" to speak of resuming direct talks with Washington, a day after President Donald Trump suggested negotiations to end America's longest war were back on track during a surprise visit to Afghanistan.

"It is way too early to talk about the resumption of talks for now. We will give our official reaction later," the Islamist group's official spokesman, Zabihullah Mujahid, told AFP in a WhatsApp message.

Trump on Thursday had said the Taliban "wants to make a deal".
