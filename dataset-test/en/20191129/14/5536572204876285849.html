Eagles Superfan Sammy Draper Celebrated the Cowboys Loss by Shaking It On Instagram (VIDEO + PICS)

29 Nov 2019, 14:46 by Darrelle Lincoln

What a wonderful Thanksgiving day for everybody who isn’t a fan of the Dallas Cowboys.

On Thursday afternoon, the two teams met on the AT&T Stadium field, and although the game was close after one half, it would get out of hand for much of the second half en route to a Buffalo Bills victory.

Not only did Bills fans enjoy the win, but so did the Philadelphia Eagles that needed them to lose to keep pace in the NFC East.

Eagles superfan Sammy Draper loved that L so much, that she took to Instagram and danced seductively in the least minimal amount of clothes she could find.
