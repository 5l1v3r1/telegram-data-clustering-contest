Enhance the Internal Storage on your PC with Solid State Drives

29 Nov 2019, 14:23 by Review Team

Internal storage in a personal computer is critical for storing your work onboard the machine, though you have the option to store in external storage disks as well. If your computer came with limited internal storage, you can enhance the storage by adding the SSD cards of 500 GB, 1 TB or 2 TB depending on how much storage you need and how much your PC can accommodate.

WD Blue SN500 500 GB NVMe Internal SSD

This WD Blue SN500 500 GB NVMeinternal SSD, as the name suggests, is rated at 500 GB and has substantial write and read speeds that are reasonably good. The range of reading speed goes up to 1,700 MB per second and write speed can go up to 1,450 MB per second. The solid-state drive works on low power requirements without compromising on its performance. The company, Western Digital, has built this SSD with its in-house controller and 3D NAND. The internal storage drive enjoys the limited warranty of 5 years from the manufacturer.
