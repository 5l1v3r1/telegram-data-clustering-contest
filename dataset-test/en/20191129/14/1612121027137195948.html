Sign up for OPPO’s ColorOS 7 beta program to test Android 10 on your OPPO Reno and Reno 10x Zoom

29 Nov 2019, 14:25 by Peter Holden

We reviewed the Reno 10x Zoom Edition from OPPO earlier this which impressed us in almost every aspect apart from the ColorOS software experience that felt a little clunky at times with missing features such as dark mode and full icon customization. OPPO would appear to have taken the complaints on board because it recently announced ColorOS 7 that is based on Android 10 with the promise of a lightweight visual approach as well as the inclusion of the all-important dark mode. If you have a Reno or Reno 10x Zoom you can get in on the ColorOS 7 action because OPPO has announced that beta programs for the two phones have gone live. Join us after the break for more details.
