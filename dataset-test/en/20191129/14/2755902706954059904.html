WATCH: Police block lawyers from handing over petition against brutality on Civilians

29 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Sharon Chirisa

Police block lawyers from handing over petition

The Zimbabwe Republic Police have blocked protesting lawyers from entering the police headquarters where they wanted to submit a petition in protest to police brutality against civilians.

The protest comes after police, fearing anarchy, unleashed violence on MDC supporters who were gathered at Morgan Tsvangirai House last week in Harare where MDC leader, Nelson Chamisa was delivering his Hope of the Nation Address.
