17 Simple Ways To Express Your Love To Your Partner

29 Nov 2019, 14:40 by Sofiya Golovnya

Any relationship can’t exist without partners showing their affection to each other. While telling your significant other that you love them is very important, there are a lot of different ways to express how you feel. Here are some simple things you can do to show your love to someone.

Do Something They Love

Even if you’re not a fan of it, it’s nice to do something your partner loves, like dancing or exercising together. Besides, you might change your mind in the process.

Compliment Them

We all like compliments, but they are especially meaningful when received from someone you love. Tell your partner something sweet to remind them how you feel.

Ask Them About Their Day

It doesn’t seem like a lot, but asking a loved one how their day went can make a huge difference. This shows that you truly care about your partner.

Do the Little Things

Romance is nice, but managing to show your love through everyday activities is so meaningful. If you see your loved one cleaning or working, ask if they need your help.

Listen to Them

Listening is an essential skill that can be used in both your personal and professional life. Paying close attention to what your partner is saying will show how much you care about them.

Tell Them Something Personal

You are truly close to someone only if you know everything about them. Don’t be scared to share; it will definitely show that you trust your partner with anything.

Hold Their Hand

Physical touch expressed through gestures like holding hands is essential when in a relationship. It helps you to get connected to your loved one.

Do Something Together

Spending time together helps people bond, and doing something like cooking, going on a hike, or traveling somewhere together is going to bring you and your partner closer to each other.

Tell How Important They Are to You

Sometimes, you can forget to tell your partner that you appreciate their presence in your life. Make sure to remind them how much they mean to you regularly.

Introduce Them to Your Family

Introducing your partner to your family might seem like a huge step, but it’s worth doing if you really love someone. This will show that you’re serious about your intentions.

Consider How They Show Affection

We all show our love in a different way, and sometimes partners can disagree on how they do it to each other. But, if someone is truly important to you, make sure to return the signs of affection they show.

Show Your Emotions

It might be hard to show how you feel at times, and some of us tend to bottle up our emotions. This is a terrible habit, so try to get rid of it and make sure to let your loved one know how you feel.

Consider Their Opinion

A healthy relationship can’t exist without occasional disagreements, but try to avoid turning these into big fights. To achieve this, always consider your partner’s opinion.

Leave a Love Note

This small, yet mighty gesture can be very meaningful, especially in the age of technology and social media. A sweet note will show your partner how much you love them.

Use the Word We Instead of I

This might feel scary at first, especially to the more introverted of us, but using the word ‘we’ can have such a positive impact on your relationship. By doing this, you declare the importance of your connection.

Laugh Together

Humor is one of the best ways to get closer to people. In a relationship, laughing together with your partner makes a huge difference. It shows you’re genuinely comfortable with each other.

Give Them Space

While you might want to be around your partner 24/7, it’s important to give them some space if they want it. Besides, this lets you understand how much you miss your loved one while away from them.
