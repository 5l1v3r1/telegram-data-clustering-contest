Police rush to London Bridge amid reports of shots fired

29 Nov 2019, 14:26 by James Cox

UPDATE: Police have told Sky News that a man has been detained following an incident at London Bridge.

Metropolitan Police have said as many as five people have been injured in the incident.

Police have rushed to London Bridge amid fears of a shooting.

Sky News are reporting that armed police officers have shot a suspect dead.

Metropolitan Police have confirmed they are in the 'early stages' of dealing with an incident.

People have been urged to avoid the area around London Bridge.
