Reports: Man Utd Could Sign £100m-Rated Star In January

29 Nov 2019, 14:28 by Kurt Buckerfield

After yet another disciplinary issue, Borussia Dortmund have reportedly made the decision to sell England international Jadon Sancho in January, and Manchester United and Liverpool are believed to be after his signature.

The 19-year-old winger was named on the bench for Dortmund's away loss to FC Barcelona in the UEFA Champions League on Wednesday, after he reportedly arrived late for a team briefing and training session in the build-up to the game.

This is not the first time Sancho has been reprimanded for an indiscretion, which has reportedly led to a massive breakdown in the relationship between the player and the club's management.

In October, the Manchester City academy product was handed a suspension by the club for returning late from international duty.
