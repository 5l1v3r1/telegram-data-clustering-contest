Social Media Erupts As Gunners Sack Emery

29 Nov 2019, 14:58 by SL Social

After just 18 months at Arsenal, Unai Emery was sacked as the Gunners manager on Friday afternoon. He has since become a trending topic online as fans took to social media to react to the news. Check it out!

After a series of poor results and performances, the Arsenal board made the decision to let the Spaniard go, with the club taking to its official website to explain the former Paris Saint-Germain mentor's sacking.

In the statement, Gunners owner Josh Kroenke said: "Our most sincere thanks go to Unai and his colleagues who were unrelenting in their efforts to get the club back to competing at the level we all expect and demand. We wish Unai and his team nothing but future success.

"The decision has been taken due to results and performances not being at the level required."
