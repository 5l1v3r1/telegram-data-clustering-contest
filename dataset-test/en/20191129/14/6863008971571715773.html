JFO HQ reveals enemy death toll in Donbas over past week

Ten enemy troops were killed in action.

29 Nov 2019, 14:20

The Joint Forces Operation Headquarters (JFO HQ) has disclosed the enemy death toll in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, over the past week.

"On November 22-28, 2019, Russian occupying forces had 25 casualties, namely: 10 were killed, and another 15 wounded," the JFO HQ wrote on Facebook.
