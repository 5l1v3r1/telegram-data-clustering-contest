When is Christmas Jumper Day 2019?

29 Nov 2019, 12:00

Now that Christmas television ads are fully underway, Black Friday has landed and office parties are in the diary, we have officially entered the festive season.

Which of course means that another festive rite of passage is just around the corner: that’s right, Christmas Jumper Day.

When is it and where you can you score the best Christmas jumper?

We’re glad you asked.

When is Christmas Jumper Day and who organises it?

Christmas Jumper Day takes place on Friday 13 December.

The campaign – run by Save the Children – urges people to participate by donning their ‘silliest sweater’ for the day.

You can sign up via the Save the Children website to get your free fundraising kit.

They recommend a donation of £2, or £1 if you’re a school.
