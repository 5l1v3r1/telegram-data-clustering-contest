Student sit-ins and Black Friday crowds: Friday's best photos

29 Nov 2019, 12:26 by Guy Lane

The Guardian’s picture editors select photo highlights from around the world

Lesbos, Greece

A girl walks on a muddy path in a makeshift camp next to the overcrowded Moria refugee camp near the capital, Mytilene

Photograph: Aris Messinis/AFP/Getty Images

Turin, Italy

Fridays for Future protesters stage a sit-in to draw attention to the climate emergency

Photograph: Marco Bertorello/AFP/Getty Images

Sydney, Australia

A student activist takes part in a ‘solidarity sit-down’ protest outside the offices of the Liberal party

Photograph: Steven Saphore/EPA

Bogotá, Colombia

Protesters play drums during a demonstration. Under the slogan ‘ Tambores a las Calle’ (Drums in the street), hundreds of percussionists gathered to protest against the government of the president, Iván Duque Márquez

Photograph: Mauricio Dueñas Castañeda/EPA

São Paulo, Brazil

Shoppers scramble to buy TV sets at a supermarket during a Black Friday sale

Photograph: Miguel Schincariol/AFP/Getty Images

Geneva, Switzerland

A girl looks out from the Rigot accommodation centre for asylum seekers, where people can stay with their families while their applications are being processed

Photograph: Sergei Bobylev/Tass

Kiev, Ukraine

Visitors attend the Wrapped in Plastic exhibition by the Ukrainian Vladi Yudi. The director used plastic in various shapes and forms to create settings for a horror cartoon

Photograph: Gleb Garanich/Reuters

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Visitors display their tattoos during the International Malaysia Tattoo Expo, a gathering of more than 100 tattoo artists from 40 countries

Photograph: Mohd Rasfan/AFP/Getty Images

Ullapool, Scotland

Simon Eltingham (right) and Gordon Wink put the finishing touches to a 16ft Christmas tree they built on the harbourside. It is made from 340 fishing creels used for catching prawns and crabs

Photograph: Jane Barlow/PA

Seoul, South Korea

Children wait to perform during a ceremony to launch a Salvation Army fundraising campaign for underprivileged people

Photograph: Jung Yeon-Je/AFP/Getty Images

London, England

Tate Britain is decorated with a sculptural installation by Anne Hardy. For this commission, the artist has transformed the building’s facade into a marooned temple, in an exploration of the natural rhythms of the earth, tides, and the winter solstice

Photograph: Chris J Ratcliffe/Getty Images

London, England

John Lennon’s sunglasses are displayed at Sotheby’s, where they are estimated to sell for £8,000 at auction

Photograph: Chris J Ratcliffe/Sotheby's/PA

Chester, England

A newborn orangutan, a member of a critically endangered Sumatran species, is cradled by its mother at the city’s zoo

Photograph: Chester Zoo/Reuters
