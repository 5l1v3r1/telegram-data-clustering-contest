Dunphy, Giles & Brady To Reunite For Christmas Premier League Coverage

29 Nov 2019, 12:44 by Marisa Kennedy

The iconic trio of Eamon Dunphy, John Giles and Liam Brady will return to our television screens this Christmas period for coverage of the Premier League.

The famed pundits are expected to feature on Premier Sports for a five-game stint beginning next week, according to the Irish Independent.

Dunphy, Giles and Brady first began working together in RTÉ in 1998 but only the former Arsenal star remains with the national broadcaster. Giles departed RTÉ at the end of their Euro 2016 coverage while Dunphy followed suit two year’s later.

They will reform their punditry next Tuesday on Premier Sport for Manchester City’s trip to Burnley while they will also cover the Merseyside Derby between Liverpool and Everton at Anfield on Wednesday, December 4.

It is reported that Ivan Yates will host the coverage.

According to the media outlet, their five-match stint will also comprise of Arsenal against Brighton on Thursday as well as two fixtures on St Stephen’s Day.
