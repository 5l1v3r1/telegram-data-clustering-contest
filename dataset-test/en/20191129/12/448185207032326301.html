CISF Recruitment 2019 for Head Constable (Rs. 25,500 – Rs. 81,100/-Per Month)

Applications are invited from the Male/Female citizens of India to fill up 300 vacancies of HEAD CONSTABLE (GENERAL DUTY) in Central Industrial Security Force against SPORTS QUOTA for the year 2019. The discipline wise detail of vacancies are as under:-

  • Post Name:    Head Constable
  • Qualification:       12TH
  • Vacancies:        300
  • Salary:    Rs. 25,500 – Rs. 81,100/-Per Month
  • Experience:         Fresher
  • Job Location:      Across India
  • Last Date:        17/12/2019
  • Age: 18 to 23 years
  • Application Fees: Rs. 100
  • Address: Block No.13, CGO Complex, Lodhi Road, New Delhi-110003

To Apply:-

  1. Only those meritorious sportsmen/sportswomen who fulfilling the above eligibility criteria and have participated in the championships during the last edition of any Tournament / Competition/ Games (as applicable) held on or after 16.11.2017, will be eligible for applying the post. However, it has to be necessarily the last edition of tournament/competition/games. Those candidates willing to compete through the selection procedure as mentioned in Para-7 above, may forward their applications in the prescribed proforma as per ANNEXURE-“1” to the following DIsG/CISF, so as to reach latest by 17.12.2019 (05:00 P.M) and in case of residents of North East region by 24.12.2019 (05:00 P.M) along with attested photocopies of certificates.
  2. The application should be sent so as to reach the concerned application receiving DIsG of CISF as shown in the above-mentioned para-8 by 17.12.2019 (05:00 P.M) and in case of residents of North East region by 24.12.2019 (05:00 P.M) along with attested photocopies of certificates.
  3. Application received after the due date/ incomplete/ unsigned/ without a photograph or proper enclosure will be summarily rejected. The person serving in Government/Semi-Government department should produce a “No objection Certificate” from their present employer as per format.
