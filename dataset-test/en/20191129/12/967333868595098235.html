Tesla Cybertruck to Join Dubai Police Luxury Vehicle Fleet

29 Nov 2019, 12:13

Last week, Tesla introduced its long-awaited Cybertruck at an event in Los Angeles, followed by the launch of preorders though the start of production is not scheduled to begin until 2021 or late 2022.

The Dubai police have unveiled a new car for its luxury vehicle fleet, the recently introduced Tesla Cybertruck.

According to the chief official of the Dubai police Major General Abdullah Khalifa Al Marri, the Cybertrack will help secure tourist attractions such as the Burj Khalifa, Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard, Jumeirah Beach Residence, and La Mer.
