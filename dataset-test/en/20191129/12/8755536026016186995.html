Jennifer Aniston celebrates ‘Friendsgiving’ with ex-husband Justin Theroux

The pair were joined by a host of their famous pals

29 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Kendra Becker

Jennifer Aniston celebrated ‘Friendsgiving’ with her ex-husband Justin Theroux, just months after finalising their divorce.

The former couple were joined by a host of their celebrity pals as Jen hosted a Friendsgiving feast, the day before actual Thanksgiving.

Stars including Courteney Cox, Jimmy Kimmel, Jason Bateman and Will Arnett were all spotted at the dinner party, in photos and videos posted by Justin on Instagram.

 

Jennifer also shared photos from their Friendsgiving party, including a snap of her making enchiladas for chat show host Jimmy.

Last year on his show, Jimmy said people should only eat Mexican food on Friendsgiving, to save the traditional turkey and stuffing for Thanksgiving day.

Poking fun at Jimmy’s request, the actress made Jimmy a popular Mexican dish, and named it, “Jimmy’s f**king enchiladas.”
