Chris Wood Shows His Support For Wife Melissa Benoist Following Her Domestic Violence Reveal

29 Nov 2019, 12:15

Chris Wood is opening up and sharing all his support for wife Melissa Benoist, following her eye-opening video describing the domestic violence she went through in a previous relationship.

The 31-year-old actor, who once starred with Melissa on Supergirl, shared a message on social media for Thanksgiving (November 28).

“Happy Thanksgiving! I’m going to kiss my wife and hold her tenderly. All day. And every day. How do YOU show love? #IStandWithMelissa,” he posted on his Twitter account, using the hashtag many fans and stars trended to voice their support.

If you didn’t know, Melissa revealed in an emotional video on Wednesday (November 27) that she is a survivor of domestic violence, and detailed the abuse she experienced in a past relationship.

You can also see more messages of support that Melissa has received from other celebrities.
