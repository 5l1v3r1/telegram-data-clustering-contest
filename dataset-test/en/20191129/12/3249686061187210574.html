For the brightest star in the Universe: BTS V fans dedicate stars,an acre of a planet and donations for the center of our Mikrokosmos

29 Nov 2019, 12:40 by thepopscene

There are a lot of unique gifts that fans come up with  to make their beloved idol's birthday a meaningful one; and countless ways that love have been manifested. BTS V fans, for example, year after year, have created significant and creative events from charity involvement, donations, to advertisements-all for expressing how important the multi-talented artist means to them, and making history each time.

For BTS V's birthday this year the anticipation builds higher as fans came together to give not just a star under his name- but seemingly, the whole universe for Kim Taehyung.
