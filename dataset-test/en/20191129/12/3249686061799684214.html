The only 2019 songs with Perfect-All-Kills

29 Nov 2019, 12:59 by scent_of_rain

Source: SBS PopAsia HQ

There's only been five artists (but 6 songs) this year!

For the uninitiated, a Perfect All Kill is when a song hits No.1 across the daily and realtime South Korean music charts.

The charts that are used to count the PAK include Melon, Genie, Bugs, Soribada, Flo realtime chart and Instiz iChart's weekly and realtime charts.

To get a PAK is to basically say that your song is the biggest thing in South Korea on release, and comparative to previous years, 2019 has been a bit slower on the Perfect All Kill scales. Only five songs have achieved Perfect All Kills, and here they are...

1. BTS ft. Halsey: "Boy With Luv"

They've taken over the world so of course they took over the charts with this release! "Boy With Luv" marked BTS' second perfect all kill, the previous being 2018's "Fake Love". BTS were also the first artists of 2019 to achieve a PAK.
