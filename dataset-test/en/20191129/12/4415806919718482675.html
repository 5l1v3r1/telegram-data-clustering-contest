Comedian Joanne McNally and author Jo Wood joining Ray D'Arcy this Saturday

29 Nov 2019, 12:28 by By Digital Desk staff

Comedian Joanne McNally and author Jo Wood will join Ray D'Arcy this Saturday night, along with Ireland's only female steeplejack Angela Collins O'Mahony and showband star Roy Taylor.

The Kaiser Chiefs will also perform their hit song 'I Predict A Riot' and chat about their new album on tomorrow's show.

Joanne McNally will be talking life in London and online dating, while Jo Wood will be discussing her new book 'Stoned', charting her rock and roll life as part of the Rolling Stones family. Wood was married to Ronnie Wood for over 25 years and will also give viewers a behind-the-scenes glimpse into life on the road with the band.

Angela Collins O'Mahony, Ireland's only female steeplejack, will be in studio with Ray to talk about her life and her last abseil, off Limerick's Thomond Park, at the age of 75.

And Roy Taylor, a star of the showband era who represented Ireland at the 1988 Eurovision song contest, will share his story of hope and recovery after previously telling Ray on the show about his Motor Neurone Disease diagnosis.
