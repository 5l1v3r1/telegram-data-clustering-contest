Read Lordina’s sweet message to John Mahama as he celebrates his 61st birthday

29 Nov 2019, 12:42 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Today, 29th Movember 2019 marks the 61st birthday of the former President of Ghana, John Dramani Mahama.

As usual, the former president received lots of birthday messages from family, friends and loved ones but one message that caught our attention was that of his wife, Lordina Mahama.

The former first lady shared lovely pictures of herself and her husband with am adorable message om Twitter.

She tweeted; “You stole my heart and I’ve never wanted it back. Your captivating love & care for us, has left no doubt that I’m the luckiest woman alive to be loved by you. Happy birthday my love. I promise to stand by you for many more birthdays to come. #JMat61 #HappyBirthdayJM”
