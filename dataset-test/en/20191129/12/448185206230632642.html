Gauhati University, Recruitment for Guest Faculty Commerce

Applications in plain paper accompanied by a CV are invited from the candidates having M.Com Degree with the specializations as mentioned below for temporary engagement as ‘Guest Faculty’ in M.Com and Five Year Integrated M.Com Course, Department of Commerce, Gauhati University for the academic session January to June 2020

  1. Post Name: Guest Faculty
  2. Vacancies: 12
  3. Department: Commerce
  4. Qualification: As per UGC Norms
  5. Job Type: Full Time
  6. Interview Type: Written-test
  7. Last Date 01 Dec 19

Important Note: The remuneration will be as per University rules. The qualification shall be as per the latest UGC norms. The application should reach the undersigned within ten (10) days of the date of publication of this advertisement. The prospective Guest Teacher will have to appear for an interaction at his/her own cost at Gauhati University as per the appointed time and date which will be intimated over phone and e-mail. However, retired faculty from university/college/professional organizations will be exempted from the interactive session.
The applications received after the last date shall not be entertained under any circumstances. The ‘Nome of the post-applied-for’ must be clearly mentioned on the envelope containing the application
