AC/DC UK get ready to rock Torquay

AC/DC UK will be playing the gig from 7:00 pm on Friday, December 6 at The Foundry

29 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Lewis Clarke

Get ready to rock as one of the world’s leading AC/DC tribute bands heads to Torquay.

AC/DC UK will be playing the gig from 7:00 pm on Friday, December 6 at The Foundry. They will be supported by Priest Unleashed, a tribute to Judas Priest. Tickets cost £12.50.

Gav Bolt, the lead singer for AC/DC UK, said: “We are the worldʼs most authentic tribute to the Brian Johnson era AC/DC will be embarking on their biggest run of shows in 2020.

“In addition, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Back In Black tour, the band will be performing the album in full at selected shows throughout 2020.”

Gav added that you could expect to see fully firing cannons, an inflatable Rosie, and iconic Angus and Brian chemistry.

“AC/DC UK churn out all of the biggest hits alongside a few fan favourites at every show. The band have delivered on the biggest of stages such as Graspop Metal Meeting (2018), Stone Free (2016), Herning Rocker (2019) and more, featuring on the same billing as rock titans including Guns Nʼ Roses, Iron Maiden, Alice Cooper, The Darkness, D-A-D and many many more.

“The band regularly travel all over Europe and beyond, playing in excess of 20 countries worldwide in the past decade. 2020 promises to be AC/DC UKʼs biggest year to date, with the band fully committed to building on the reputation they have already built. Back in Black, and ready to Shoot To Thrill in 2020; We Salute You All.”
