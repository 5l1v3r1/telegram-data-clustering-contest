How to find the best VPN deal for Mac

VPN subscriptions are usually bundled into two or three-year periods, though you can often choose to pay as you go month-by-month as well. Typically, longer subscription commitments reduce the monthly rate, saving you overall. Prices rarely exceed £6/US$7 per month with multi-year subscriptions.

Many major VPN providers offer discounts through the year, and sometimes even offer permanent discounts. These discounts can look impressive at first. It's not uncommon to see a blaring "70% off" sign and an ever-receding count down ticking to when the deal ends – but such deals may be regular.

Of course even with year-round discounts, the percentage off can (and do) fluctuate – in which case it's possible you can pick up a better deal at certain times over others. Yet large discounts aren't too surprising. This makes it all the more important to look into the various providers and what each has to offer, before jumping into a long term commitment.

As with most tech and software, we'd recommend looking for seasonal discounts. With Halloween due at the end of the month, you might find VPN services offering more money off than usual. Apart from Halloween, Black Friday and – you guessed it – Cyber Monday are pivotal sales seasons to look out for.
