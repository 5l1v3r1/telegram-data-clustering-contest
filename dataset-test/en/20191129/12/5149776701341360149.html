The best Black Friday deals at Nottingham's intu Victoria Centre

The centre will be open until 8pm

29 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Simon Wilson

Black Friday has become a firm staple in the calendars of bargain hunters across the UK and this year is set to be no exception.

Thousands of shoppers will be heading to the intu Victoria Centre today as many stories and food outlets are getting involved, offering discounts and deals.

“While no longer a mad-cap hunt for bargains, Black Friday has become part of the UK’s Christmas shopping calendar, with the sales period kicking off many people’s festive experiences," said centre director Nigel Wheatley.

“This week has already seen the launch of some great deals from our retailers but there are even more offers available today and throughout the weekend, which we’ll also be promoting on our digital screens and across our social media channels too.

“Since last year, we’ve welcomed a string of new additions to the centre including popular beauty brand, Morphe, and health food restaurant, Clean Cut Kitchen, meaning there’s even more choice when it comes to Black Friday deals."

He added: "We’re also pleased to welcome back our life-sized toy soldiers who will be on hand to add a little something extra to the experience by spreading festive cheer."

The centre will be extending its opening hours today until 8pm.
