Japan: Nintendo issues apology for Ring Fit Adventure shortages

29 Nov 2019, 12:31 by My Nintendo News Admin

Japanese consumers have been lapping up Ring Fit Adventure on the Nintendo Switch and subsequently there have been severe stock shortages of the fitness title. Nintendo told its followers on Twitter that shipments will be coming in each week and that they will ramp up supply towards the end of the year. Nintendo also advises potential purchasers that they should check instore to see whether they can reserve copies.
