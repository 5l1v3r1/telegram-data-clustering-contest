Bailee Madison Announces End of 'Just Between Us' Podcast With Sister Kaitlin Vilasuso

29 Nov 2019, 12:10

JJJ is incredibly sad over the fact that Bailee Madison and sister Kaitlin Vilasuso are ending their podcast, Just Between Us.

In a note on her Instagram, the 20-year-old actress revealed that the duo just released the final episode.

“Yesterday we released our final @justbtweenus episode,” she started. “@kaitlinvilasuso & I had no idea what to expect when we decided to go on this year journey with @podcastone (Thank you guys!!), but boy can we both agree now that it couldn’t have been more special or memorable.”

Bailee continues, “Thank you so much for welcoming our little show into your lives, sharing moments and stories with us, and for listening to us every week ❤️ it has meant the world to Katie and I and let us just say… we are so excited for what’s ahead. @kaitlinvilasuso – you’re the best podcast host and partner & this is just the beginning.”

“To all of our friends- old and new: thank you so much for coming on our show, sitting in our living room and speaking honestly and openly. We hope you’ll join us for whatever our next sister adventure is!!!!!!! We love you guys!!”
