Full extent of Shropshire maternity scandal 'may never be known'

29 Nov 2019, 12:43 by Lisa O'Brien

The full extent of the maternity scandal at Shropshire's major hospitals may never be known, according to the senior midwife leading the independent inquiry.

Donna Ockenden, who is leading the investigation into baby deaths at Shrewsbury and Telford Hospital NHS Trust, has warned it is unlikely records more than two decades old will be accessible to the inquiry.
