VIDEO: Deepika Padukone Asks Papps If They Don’t Get Tired; Netizens Go All Praises

Deepika was recently spotted exiting the airport and her sweet gesture towards the paparazzi is winning the internet now.

29 Nov 2019, 11:14 by Koimoi.com Team

Deepika Padukone is the queen of many hearts and time and again the actress has proven the same. People are in awe of her acting and beauty but the actress never fails to give them more reason to fall in love with her. Deepika was recently spotted exiting the airport and her sweet gesture towards the paparazzi is winning the internet now.

Deepika was returning to Mumbai after some work in Delhi and as usual, the shutterbugs were ready to capture the beauty in their lens. Deepika stepped out in a pink suit and was looking pretty as usual. The actress walked towards her car and smiled for the paps and as she was reaching for her car, the actress asked, “Aap log thak nahi jate ho? (Dont you all get tired?” and walks away with a smile.

This absolutely won the heart of the fans. The clip got lakhs of views in less than a day and people could not stop gushing about Deepika’s down-to-earth nature. “She is a good heart woman indeed; Queen of heart Deepika Padukone,” one Instagram user commented, while another wrote, “Deepika is so sweet”.

Workwise, Deepika is awaiting the release of Meghna Gulzar’s Chhapaak, which is based on the journey of acid attack survivor Laxmi Agarwal. The film also stars Vikrant Massey and is all set to hit the theatres on January 10. Chhapaak also marks Deepika’s debut as a producer.

At an event last month, Deepika was quoted by PTI saying that Chhapaak was a draining experience for her. “The process was exhausting. We had to get the prosthetics done for like three hours and another hour to take it off. Emotionally, I have never been as burnt out as I felt after that,” she had said.

Apart from Chhapaak, the actress will be also be seen in Ranveer Singh starrer ’83. She will essay the role of Rumi Dev, Kapil Dev’s wife in the film.
