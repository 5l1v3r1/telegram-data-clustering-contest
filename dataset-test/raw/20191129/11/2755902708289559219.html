NUST Slay Queen reveals why she walked around campus semi nude ‘Zodwa Wabantu’ style

NOBHEKIMPILO Mbovora, popularly known as Vivica, has opened up on her controversial dressing which sparked a social media frenzy recently after she turned up in a risque outfit for a lecture at the National University of Science and Technology (Nust).

Vivica said she pulled the act  to fight for women’s rights.

“Women in my hood are raped and abused by men but in the name of umendo (marriage), they’d rather suffer in pain and I’m here to speak out against such abuse.

Vivica revealed that she was a feminist, determined to stand for all women who are deprived of their rights and she was determined to achieve her dream through modelling.

“Men shouldn’t be affected by how we dress, if it was a man half-naked, it wouldn’t be an issue but just because I am a woman, it becomes an issue, it’s high time the society stops treating women as possessions,” said Vivica, who was dressed in a bum short.

Vivica’s pictures and videos which went viral on social media made her a target of verbal abuse as people in her class called her names.
