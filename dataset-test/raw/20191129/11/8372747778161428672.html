Mega Millions jackpot now up to $243 million in Friday night's drawing

29 Nov 2019, 11:33 by WFTX Digital Team

TALLAHASSEE, Fla. -- Lottery players will have a chance to win nearly a quarter billion dollars in a Florida Lottery drawing Friday night.

Friday night's Mega Millions jackpot is worth an estimated $243 million, according to the Florida Lottery.

The last winner was on September 24th, when an anonymous Texas player won $225 million.

The next drawing will be on Friday night at 11 p.m. But good luck; your chances of winning are 1 in 302,575,350.

If you miss out on that jackpot, Fridays night's PowerBall drawing is worth an estimated $110 million.

And as always, you can catch these lottery drawings live on Fox 4 at 11 p.m.
