VIDEO: Delays to Irish Rail services after truck crashes into barriers

29 Nov 2019, 11:50 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

Irish Rail services between Sandymount and Lansdowne in Dublin were suspended earlier this morning after a truck crashed into the barriers.

The incident occurred at the barriers at Serpentine Avenue in Dublin 4, leaving commuters without the busy service for a lengthy period of time.

Irish Rail released a statement on their Twitter account this morning regarding the incident along with footage of the crash.

The video shows a white truck stop at the level crossing before driven through, causing significant damage to the barrier.
