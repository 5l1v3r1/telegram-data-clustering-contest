Air quality improves in Delhi, light rains predicted

29 Nov 2019, 11:34 by PTI

The air quality in the national capital was recorded in the "satisfactory" category on Friday morning.

The Air Quality Index stood at 79.

The MeT department has predicted light rains in later parts of the day.

An AQI between 0-50 is considered 'good', 51-100 'satisfactory', 101-200 'moderate', 201-300 'poor', 301-400 'very poor' and 401-500 'severe'. An AQI above 500 falls in the 'severe plus' category.
