Child's body recovered from sewage pit in Oman

29 Nov 2019, 11:18 by Times News Service

Muscat: Water rescue teams of the Public Authority of Civil Defense and Ambulance (PACDA) pulled out the body of a child who had fallen into a sewage pit..

PACDA said in a statement online: "Water Rescue Team at the Civil Defense and Ambulance Department in Al Dhahirah Governorate, pulled out a child fallen in a sewage pit in wilayat of Ibri."
