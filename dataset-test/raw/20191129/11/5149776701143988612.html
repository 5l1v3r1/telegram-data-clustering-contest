Tom Hanks reads out Nottingham woman's tweet about her cat

'You named your cat Chips? Was there another one named Poker?'

29 Nov 2019, 11:07 by David Whitfield

When you tweet about something that's happened in in your everyday life, you probably hope it will get a few likes and maybe the occasional retweet.

What you don't expect is for Tom Hanks to read it out in a video which is seen by more than eight million people.

But that's exactly what has happened to a woman from Nottingham who posted a message about her cat six months ago.

Nottinghamshire Live reported back in May how Karen Miele had tweeted about a lady named Jean, who had taken a photo of Karen's cat, and then posted it through her letterbox for her.

The note read: "For you. Your lovely cat I always look for him. Jean."

Karen's tweet about her cat Chips and Jean went viral. And now Tom Hanks has read out the tweet in a collection of some of the nicest and best moments that can be found on Twitter around the world.
