Unai Emery's sack: I’m not going to answer - Nuno Espirito Santo

29 Nov 2019, 11:08

- Unai Emery's sacking has sparked off speculation Nuno Espirito Santo will take over as boss

- The Portuguese gaffer has been recording impressive results since he joined Wolves last season

- Arsenal suffered an embarrassing 2-1 defeat to Eintracht Frankfurt in the Europa League

Unai Emery's sack has brought about Nuno Espirito Santo being tipped to take over from the Spanish boss at the Emirates.

But the Portuguese manager has refused to comment on speculation linking him with the job after Emery was shown the door.

The Wolves manager is the number one candidate listed to succeed Emery after his impressive results at the Molineux.

Emery was shown the door after losing 2-1 to Eintracht Frankfurt at the Emirates and extended his winless run with Arsenal to seven matches.
