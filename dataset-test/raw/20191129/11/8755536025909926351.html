Caitlyn Jenner reveals how much Kylie spends on security – after obsessed fan showed up at her home

The reality star spends a huge amount of money in order to feel safe

29 Nov 2019, 11:17 by Kendra Becker

Caitlyn Jenner has revealed that her daughter Kylie spends up to $400,000 on security every month.

The 22-year-old, who was named the world’s youngest self-made billionaire earlier this year, needs security “everywhere” she goes.

Speaking about her daughter on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here, Caitlyn said: “I bet you Kylie will spend anywhere between 300,000 and 400,000 dollars a month on security, everywhere she goes. It’s a big operation.”

Fellow campmate Roman Kemp then asked: “How does that make you feel that your daughters have to go through that just to go to the shops?”

The 70-year-old replied: “It’s sad, but they have been doing it forever, they like having the security guys there.”

Despite spending so much money on security, guards at Kylie’s house failed to stop an obsessed fan from getting to her front door last month.

 

The reality star was forced to seek a restraining order against a man in October, after he managed to sneak into her gated community.

The man allegedly knocked on Kylie’s front door “aggressively”, and claimed that he was there to visit her.

He was quickly escorted away by her security team, and was later arrested.

According to court documents obtained by E! News, Kylie said: “I have suffered, and continue to suffer emotional distress.”

The reality star also expressed concern for her one-year-old daughter, Stormi, by stating: “I have a young child and also fear for her safety.”

The man has since been ordered to stay 100 yards away from Kylie, her property, workplace and car.
