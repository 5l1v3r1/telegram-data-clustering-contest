TikTok sensation Aashika Bhatia is back with her ex-beau Satvik Sankhyan. The actress confirmed it to SpotboyE.com

Now, they are back together and their recent social media posts with each other hint the obvious. So we contacted the actress and she indeed confirmed it to us.
