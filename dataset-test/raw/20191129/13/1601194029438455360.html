PMCO 2019 Global Finals: Day 1 Results and Standings; Entity in top 3; Bigetron leads the board

29 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Rabia

Day 1 of the biggest PUBG Mobile Esports tournament, PUBG Mobile Club Open Fall Split Global Finals 2019 is finally over. A total of 16 teams are competing for the title and a prize pool of 500,000 USD. A total of 4 games were played on Day 1. All the teams played put up a gripping game play throughout the day.

Entity Gaming and SouL are representing South Asia at PMCO Fall Split Global Finals 2019. The teams were placed 3rd and 11th respectively after Day 1 on the leader board. Bigetron RA topped the leader board with one chicken dinner and 68 points and Team Queso followed them with 60 points. Team Soul won the 3rd match of the day.
