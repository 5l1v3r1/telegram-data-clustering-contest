Nokia 2720 Flip available on Amazon India before official launch

29 Nov 2019, 13:35 by Nayan

In a good news for Nokia fans, the smart feature phone Nokia 2720 Flip 4G  has become available on Amazon India. It is a bit surprising as the HMD has to launch it officially yet, but one can now buy the device in India.

Nokia 2720 Flip 4G is priced at Rs 9990 in India but retailers are offering it for the lowest price of Rs 9240 in India. Only the Black model is available so far.

Nokia 2720 Flip 4G is powered by Qualcomm 205 Mobile processor coupled with 512 MB RAM. The 2 MP rear camera comes with flash. Featuring a  2.8” (7.1 cm) QVGA main display and 1.3″(3.3 cm) front display, the smartphone has built-in Wi-Fi, LTE, two nano-SIM card slot, and a 3.5mm headphone jack.
