Brussels' Listen! Festival announces 2020 lineup

Beautiful Swimmers, Skee Mask, Terrence Dixon, Yu Su and more will play the fifth edition beginning March 25th.

29 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Marissa Cetin

The first names for Listen! 2020 are in.

The Belgian festival has invited a host of local and international names for the fifth edition, which will run from March 25th through the 30th in Brussels. Beautiful Swimmers, Skee Mask, Yu Su, Lefto, Jane Fitz, Antal, Orpheu The Wizard, Walrus and Hessle Audio's Ben UFO, Pearson Sound and Pangaea are among those down to DJ. There will also be live sets from the likes of Terrence Dixon, Violet, Dengue Dengue Dengue!, Suzi Analogue and Cabasa.

The festival will take place at various venues across the capital including C12, VK, Lavallée, La Cabane and FFORMATT.
