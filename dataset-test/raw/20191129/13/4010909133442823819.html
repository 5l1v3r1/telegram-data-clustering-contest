Now you can have Astro & Broadband for RM99

29 Nov 2019, 13:31

Astro has just announced a new offer for its Astro & Broadband service. You can now sign up for a 30Mbps internet connection plus a subscription for Astro's Family Pack for just RM99. All existing Astro and NJOI customers can sign up for this package and save a nice RM30 for their monthly subscription.

By signing up for the Family Pack, you get to tune into programmes like Maharaja Lawak Mega 2019, The Good Doctor, Miss Astro Chinese International Pageant 2019 and more. You will also get access to Astro GO for free, allowing you to stream videos at your own convenience.

If you want access to more content, you can go for the Super Pack that costs RM250/month. This pack gives you a broadband connection speed of 100Mbps and Astro's new Ultra Box, which was launched a few weeks ago. The Ultra Box provides advanced features like 4K playback, an improved UI, and Cloud Recording - sweet! Other plans include the RM119 subscription that upgrades you to a Starter Pack and the RM159 plan that bumps up the broadband connection to 100Mbps.
