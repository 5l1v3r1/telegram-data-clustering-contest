Hollard excludes SAA from insolvency coverage  

This comes after a crippling strike at the airline.

29 Nov 2019, 13:28 by Alexander Winning, Reuters

South African insurer Hollard excluded struggling state airline South African Airways from its travel supplier insolvency coverage earlier this month, the managing director of the company that administers Hollard Travel Insurance told Reuters.

Read: SAA dropped by leading travel insurer

Uriah Jansen, managing director of Oojah Travel Protection, said Hollard took the decision after a crippling strike started at SAA on November 15 and the airline warned it might not be able to pay salaries.

Another large South African travel insurer, Travel Insurance Consultants, has also stopped covering tickets issued by SAA against insolvency.
