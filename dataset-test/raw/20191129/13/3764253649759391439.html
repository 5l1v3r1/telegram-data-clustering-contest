Wilfred Ndidi: Maddison says Super Eagles star is Premier League best midfielder

29 Nov 2019, 13:30

- Wilfred Ndidi has been hailed as Premier League's best holding midfielder by James Maddison

- The Nigerian sensation has been in superb form for the former England champions Leicester City

- Ndidi sealed a move to England in £17 million transfer deal from Belgian top side Genk FC in 2017

Super Eagles star, Wilfred Ndidi, has been hailed by Leicester City ace James Maddison as the best midfielder in the Premier League.

Ndidi has been in superb form for the Premier League giants since arriving England from Genk in a £17 million deal.

The midfielder, who hit another landmark in the European top five leagues with highest tackles and interceptions, was an instrumental figure as Foxes demolished Southampton 9-0.
