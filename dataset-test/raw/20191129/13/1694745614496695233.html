Why run yourself ragged trying to find a million different presents for all your friends and family when you can just invest in a giant gift set and divide up all the goodies? It's a trick as old as time! We've already rounded up the greatest skincare gift sets, the best gift sets from Sephora, and our favorite beauty sets from Ulta, but what if you're in the market for something supersize? In the case that you're planning to rip through some pretty packaging to get to the good stuff and individually wrap it for all your friends, we've gathered your best bets down below. Some of these would provide great stocking stuffer items, while other full-size products are impressive standalone gifts. Take a gander . . .
