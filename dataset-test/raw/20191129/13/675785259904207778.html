How to get the best Black Friday clothing deals

While it might seem simple to just snap up whatever catches your eye, there are many ways to ensure you are bagging the best clothing deals on Black Friday. Here are our top tips.

  • Pick out some of the items you'd like to buy and then use price comparison website like PriceSpy to find out the cheapest prices. So when the big day arrives, you'll know how much you should be paying.
  • Opt for free in-store collection to avoid paying delivery charges.
  • If you're shopping on your phone or PC, make sure you have a decent data or WiFi connection for a quick and simple shopping experience.
