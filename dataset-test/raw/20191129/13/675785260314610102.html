First I'm A Celebrity star leaves tonight - with fears Caitlyn Jenner might go

I'm A Celebrity hosts Ant and Dec are convinced Cliff Parisi will be voted out on Friday night - but Alison Hammond worries there will be a surprise exit

29 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Kyle O'Sullivan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/kyle-osullivan/

It feels like I'm A Celebrity has only just begun - but the first contestant will leave the jungle tonight.

The celebs have been competing for immunity during the 'Reckoning' trials other the last few days.

Those that fail to do so will be up for the public vote to decide who will get that unwanted wooden spoon.

Their friends and family have been piling into the famous Versace Hotel and enjoying the facilities while their loved ones struggle on beans and rice.

No one wants to be the first to go - and there are serious concerns that Caitlyn Jenner may leave in a shock exit.

Speaking on today's This Morning, Alison Hammond pleaded with viewers to vote to save Caitlyn even though she is a firm favourite to win the series.

Alison is concerned because Deal Or No Deal star Noel Edmonds was the surprise first casualty last year.

She said: "Last year the surprise was Noel. No one thought he would leave. If you donâ€™t vote for your favourite you could lose Caitlyn."

At the moment, Radio DJ Adele Roberts and comedian Andrew Maxwell are the bookies' favourites to be given the chop.

They are closely followed by former EastEnders star Cliff Parisi, which Alison believes is because he "sleeps all the time".

Presenters Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly confirmed who they think will be first to leave on Thursday night's Extra Camp.

When asked by Joel Dommett who they think will be the first to go - both Ant and Dec said that Cliff could find himself struggling to stay.

Ant said: "I think it is fair to say that Cliff is in danger as he's not doing a lot."

In agreement, Dec added: "I think he is struggling a bit and his energy levels have dropped and he's not been in the show a huge amount."
