Dabangg 3: After Controversy, Fans Trend #AwaitingDabangg3 Proving That There’s No Fandom Like Salman Khan’s

29 Nov 2019, 13:39 by Lubna Khan

The title track of Dabangg 3, 'Hud Hud Dabangg' had come under fire recently for hurting religious sentiments, and Chulbul Pandey's fans came to the rescue. Salman Khan’s fans started trending #AwaitingDabangg3 on social media to support the movie proving that there is no fandom like Salman Khan’s!

Dabangg 3 is one of the most anticipated releases of this year. Salman Khan as Chulbul Pandey is back once again in the third installment of the hit franchise Dabangg. There's no fandom quite like Salman Khan's! Salman Khan is without a doubt, one of the most loved superstars in India, the actor is all set to for the release of his next movie Dabangg 3. The title track of the movie 'Hud Hud Dabangg' had come under fire recently for hurting religious sentiments, and Chulbul Pandey's fans came to the rescue.

The fans started trending #AwaitingDabangg3 on social media to support the movie and it shows just how crazy the fan following of Salman Khan is. In less than a day, the hashtag #AwaitingDabangg3 has been used by the fans over 1.2 lakh times. The song will be launched in a grand event in Mumbai by Chulbul Pandey and Prabhu Deva this week, and will see both of them groove onstage. For those unaware, the song Hud Hud Dabangg ended up facing the wrath of Hindu Janajagruti Samiti.
