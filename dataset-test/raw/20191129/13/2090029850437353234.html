B-Red welcomes baby boy with his partner, Faith (Photo)

29 Nov 2019, 13:51 by Damilola Ismail

Bayo Adeleke otherwise known as B-Red has welcomed a baby boy with his partner, Faith Johnson.

B-red took to his social media page to announce the birth of his son, Jordan Adetayo Osioke Adeleke with his “wifeyy” Faith Johnson.

He wrote;

I WELCOME MY SON JORDAN ADETAYO OSHIOKE ADELEKE @thefaithjohnson_ YOU CARRIED MY BABY FOR 9 MONTHS,I LOVE YOU WIFEYYY 🙏🏾🙏🏾🙏🏾🙌🏾🙌🏾🙏🏾🔥💪🏽💪🏽💪🏽❤️❤️

It is obviously is raining babies in the Adeleke family, this good news comes a few weeks after Davido and his fiance Chioma welcomed son, Ifeanyi David Jnr Adeleke.

B-Red and his partner tried all they could to keep the pregnancy a secret. He even at some point deactivated his social media page after which there were speculations the two had tied the knot.

Faith was thrown a surprise baby shower sometime in August.

Congratulations to the couple!
