Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey to move to Africa in 2020

29 Nov 2019, 15:55 by Caitlin Yilek

Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey said he plans to spend part of 2020 living in Africa.

Dorsey spent November visiting with entrepreneurs in Ethiopia, Ghana, Nigeria, and South Africa.

“Sad to be leaving the continent…for now,” Dorsey tweeted Wednesday. “Africa will define the future (especially the bitcoin one!). Not sure where yet, but I’ll be living here for 3-6 months mid 2020.”
