Virgin Media is Giving Away a Lot of Stuff this Christmas

29 Nov 2019, 15:40 by Holly Brockwell

Cheryl Hole, the fabulous Essex-based drag queen from RuPaul's Drag Race UK, takes on the role of a fairy 'Giftmother' in Virgin Media's new campaign to give away a fucktonne of stuff before Christmas.

Slightly cringily called Now That’s Christmas-ing, the campaign is a sort of advent calendar of prizes, with daily prizes from the 1st to the 25th of December.

The 25 days of prizes are on offer for all customers of both Virgin Media and Virgin Mobile, and start this Sunday (which incredibly is somehow December).
