Refuge plots three fundraisers in Berlin, Bristol

Featuring Gideön, Leif, LNS and more, the events will all take place before the year is out.

29 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Carlos Hawthorn

Berlin-based party Refuge is hosting three fundraisers across November and December.

The first two, on November 30th and December 14th, will take place in Berlin, with Gideön headlining Sameheads this weekend before LNS, M.E.S.H., Clouds, DJ Fart in the Club, Nathan Micay, Derek Plaslaiko, Telephones and more take over Griessmuehle from Saturday through Monday.

The final date is being held in Bristol—a Refuge first. Leif, Peverelist and two TBA guests (who will go back-to-back) are confirmed for The Love Inn on Thursday, December 19th.

Refuge has chosen seven charities and causes to support, including International Women Space, Frauenzimmer and Caring In Bristol. All proceeds from the nights will be donated.
