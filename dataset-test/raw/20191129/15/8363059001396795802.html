Apple's Not-Black Friday Shopping Event Offers Up to £160 in Gift Cards for Buying Apple Stuff

29 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Tom Pritchard

Apple is known for only ever doing things its own way, and to hell with what everyone else thinks. Or at least that's what happens when dealing with the public, because who the hell knows what happens inside the UFO they call a headquarters. So When Black Friday comes around Apple takes part, but doesn't call it 'Black Friday'

Instead Apple is having a four-day 'shopping event' this weekend, and it's just coincidentally at the same time as Black Friday and Cyber Monday. Also because Apple doesn't really do discounts they're giving away up to £160 of gift cards instead. Assuming you buy Apple products, obviously.

Here's what you can get:

  • £40 gift card with any iPhone XR and iPhone 8
  • £20 gift card with any Apple Watch Series 3
  • £20 gift card with any AirPods or AirPods with Wireless Charging Case
  • £80 fit card with any iPad Pro
  • £40 gift card with the iPad Air or iPad Mini
  • £160 Gift Card With 13-inch MacBook Pro
  • £80 gift card with the MacBook Air
  • £160 gift card with the iMac or iMac Pro
  • £20 gift card with the Apple TV HD and Apple TV 4K
  • £40 gift card with the HomePod
  • £40 gift card with the Powerbeats Pro, Powerbeats3 or Solo3 headphones
  • £80 gift card with the Beats Studio3 Wireless Headphones
