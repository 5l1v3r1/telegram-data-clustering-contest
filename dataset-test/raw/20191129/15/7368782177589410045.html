Yanzu-yanzu: Kotu ta baiwa Mompha beli N100m

29 Nov 2019, 15:53

Babban kotun tarayya dake Legas ta baiwa shahararren matashin nan mai alfahari da daloli kuma ake zargi da damfara, Ismaila Mustapha, beli.

Kotun ta baiwa matashin da akafi sani da suna 'Mompha' belin N100m.

An gurfanar da Mompha ne kan zargin damfarar N33billion.

Hukumar hana almundahana da yiwa tattalin arzikin kasa zagon kasa wato EFCC ta gurfanar da Ismaila Mustapha, alias Mompha, da ake zargi da damfara a babban kotun tarayya dake jihar Legas.

EFCC na zargin Mompha da laifukan damfarar yanar gizo da safarar kudade daban-daban har 14.

Mukaddashin shugaban hukumar EFCC, Ibrahim Magu ya bayyana cewa zuwa yanzu sun kaddamar da cikakken bincike a kan Ismaila Mustapha wanda ya shahara wajen damfara da satar kudi ta yanar gizo.
