Irish model Karena Graham announces engagement to sports agent Damien O’Donohoe

Amazing news!

29 Nov 2019, 15:44 by Kendra Becker

Irish model Karena Graham has announced her engagement to top sports agent Damien O’Donohoe.

The couple have been dating since 2015 and have welcomed two kids together – sons Alfie and Dougie.

Taking to Instagram, Karena showed off her new bling in a selfie with her fiancé.

She captioned the post: “It only took five years and two kids to get him down on one knee!! 😆”

“The best way ever to end a magical year… 💍👨‍👩‍👦‍👦🎄❤️ #NYC,” she added.

Karena is well-known in the Irish modelling scene, and used to pose for quirky photocalls with fellow model pals Georgia Penna (nee Salpa) and Sara Kavanagh.

Although she’s pursued other ventures since then, including training to be a pilates instructor, Karena is still signed as a model with Andrea Roche’s agency.
