Georgian mansion transformed into luxury homes for bargain price

Your chance to own a piece of history

29 Nov 2019, 15:43 by Lottie Gibbons

Now's your chance to own a piece of history as a Georgian mansion has been transformed into three newly refurbished homes.

Redrow Homes has transformed a Grade II listed building, Fremont House in West Derby , into a pair of three-bedroom houses and a two-bedroom duplex apartment.

The historic lodge house, in the grounds of the former Holly Lodge Girls' College, has been returned to its former glory with smart painted brickwork and a pillared entrance porch.

Inside, period features are plentiful including bay windows and detailed architraves.

However, the old has been blended with the new.

Homes boast a range of modern conveniences including new shaker-style kitchens, flooring throughout and elegant contemporary bathroom suites.

The Mellor, priced at £375,000 is a spacious three-bedroom duplex with a private entrance taking you into an open-plan kitchen and dining area.

The separate lounge has a deep bay window with views over the gardens. On the first floor, there are three generous bedrooms, including a master with bay window and en-suite, and a family bathroom.

The Heyworth, priced at £400,000 has an impressive L-shaped entrance hall, dual aspect lounge, and open plan kitchen/dining room.

There is also outdoor access through a second hallway. All three bedrooms are doubles, two of which boast en-suites.

The final property, The Sandfield, is a two-bedroom apartment, with a private entrance and two floors of living space, priced at £190,000.

It includes a large hallway with a downstairs cloakroom. The open-plan kitchen/dining/lounge is triple aspect while upstairs there are two bedrooms and a family bathroom.

The properties are available to move into now, with Redrow offering to cover stamp duty for buyers who reserve before the end of November.

Catherine Lacey at Sutton Kersh said: "The properties are unique. I have been selling property in West Derby Village for over 30 years, and Fremont House is a hidden gem.

"Offering spacious, secluded accommodation and an enviable lifestyle.

"The wider development was in high demand and, although part of the development, Fremont House enjoys a private entrance set behind gates, and two of the properties come with double garages."
