Jerry Jones Became a Perfect SpongeBob Meme During Cowboys Thanksgiving Day Loss vs. Bills (TWEETS)

29 Nov 2019, 15:43 by Howard Cosmell

Jerry Jones has lived through a grand total of 78 Thanksgivings, but I can almost guarantee that none of them were worse than his 2019 Thanksgiving Day festivities.

Not only did his team drastically hurt their playoff chances by losing to the Buffalo Bills, 26-15, but the Cowboys owner was also seen with tears in his eyes while speaking to the media about the loss after the game.

And if that wasn’t already bad enough, 2018 will also mark the Thanksgiving when Twitter turned Jerry Jones into a SpongeBob meme.
