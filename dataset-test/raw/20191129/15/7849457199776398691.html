Bonifacio Monument Circle to be closed to traffic Saturday morning

29 Nov 2019, 15:07 by Minka Klaudia Tiangco

The Caloocan City government announced road closures for vehicles in line with the observance of the 156th birth anniversary of Andres Bonifacio.

The Bonifacio Monument Circle will be closed to traffic on Saturday from 12:01 a.m. onwards.

Motorists are advised to take alternate routes and to expect heavy traffic, the local government said.

The annual wreath-laying ceremony at the Bonifacio Monument Circle will be held at 4 p.m.

President Duterte and some of Bonifacio’s descendants are expected to grace the event.
