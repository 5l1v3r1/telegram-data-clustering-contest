Black Friday is here once again and Ulefone has prepared large discounts for its rugged Armor series and entry-level S series on its Aliexpress store. So, it would be a perfect time to grab a rugged phone on the cheap. Let’s take a look at what they have to offer.

Ulefone Armor 7 flagship

Ulefone Armor 7 is this year’s flagship of the company and is powered by Helio P90 octa-core SoC backed by 8GB RAM and 128GB of flash storage. Also, there is a triple AI camera on the back with a 48MP main sensor as well as a 16MP selfie shooter. In addition, there is a 6.3” FHD+ waterdrop screen, 5500mAh battery with Qi 10W wireless charging, face & fingerprint unlock, NFC with Google Pay, and Android 9.0 Pie. You can get it 25% off for just $369.99 here.

Ulefone Armor 3W/3WT

If you prefer the combination of rugged features with a huge battery, then take a look at Armor 3W/Armor 3WT, which come with a massive 10300mAh battery. They are powered by the Helio P70 AI SoC accompanied by 6GB of RAM and 64GB of storage and they sport a 21MP Sony camera on the back and an 8MP front shooter. They are rather compact since they come with a 5.7″ 18:9 FHD+ screen while other features include face/fingerprint unlock, dual-4G/dual-VoLTE, NFC with Google Pay, global network bands, and Android 9.0 Pie. The Armor 3W costs just $224.99 while its walkie-talkie version, Armor 3WT costs just $259.99.

Ulefone Armor 6E

If you prefer something cheaper then you should consider the Armor 6E with Helio P70 SoC at the discounted price of $182.49. It comes with a 6.2″ FHD+ notch screen, 4GB/64GB memory configuration, 16MP/2MP dual rear camera, an 8MP selfie camera, 5000mAh battery with Qi 10W wireless charging, face/fingerprint unlock, and Android 9.0 Pie. You can find it here.

Ulefone Note 7

On the other hand, if rugged isn’t what you are looking for, you can get the super-affordable Ulefone Note 7 at just $49.99! It is powered by the MT6580 4-core SoC coupled with 1GB of RAM and 16GB of storage. Also, it sports a triple camera setup with an 8MP main sensor while the display is 6.1″ with 19.2:9 aspect ratio and waterdrop notch. What’s more, there is a 3500mAh battery, 3 card slots for 2 SIMS and 1 microSD card, Face recognition and Android 9 Pie Go. You can get it here.

Lastly, there are other devices available too like the rugged entry-level Ulefone Armor X3 with 5000mAh battery priced at only $71.24, and its 4G version Armor X5 for just $89.99. For more details about the products and promotions, you can visit Ulefone’s Aliexpress store or their official website.
