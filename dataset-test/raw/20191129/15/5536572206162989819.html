Cole Beasley Demanded That Dak Prescott Give Him His Jersey “NOW” After Bills-Cowboys Game (VIDEO)

29 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Howard Cosmell

People tend to have mixed reviews about these post-game jersey swaps that have become a new fad across the NFL, but did you ever wonder how these players choose who they’ll swap with?

In some cases, they probably have pre-arranged agreements with friends from the other team. And in other cases, like after yesterday’s Thanksgiving contest between the Bills and Cowboys, the players have no choice but to flat-out demand a player give them the jersey off their back.

Following the Bills’ 26-15 victory over Dallas, former Cowboys and current Bills receiver Cole Beasley took the latter approach in getting Dak Prescott’s jersey.

Beasley clearly wasn’t taking “no” for an answer, as he told Dak to “Give me your jersey NOW!”
