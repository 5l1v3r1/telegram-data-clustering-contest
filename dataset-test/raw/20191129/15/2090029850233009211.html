Ghana welcomes Tacha in grand style (Video)

29 Nov 2019, 15:43 by Damilola Ismail

Former BBNaija housemate, Tacha was welcomed into Ghana for her homecoming in grand style from the Kotoka International Airport in Ghana.

Tacha stepped out to see cultural dancers at the airport dancing to welcome her into Ghana who she ended up joining and learning some of their dance moves.

Her fans Titans in Ghana came out to welcome her at the airport wearing Titan-branded tees at the airport, chanting “no leave no transfer, we full grown, we pin, we brekete” and dancing.

She then headed in an entourage, with her seated in a limousine as she headed to he Black Star Square as she tours some parts of Accra .
