Trevor Noah And Lupita Nyongo Spark Dating Rumor

Ever since funnyman Trevor Noah shared to the world his views on marriage and how he does not believe in staying together after marriage, the comedian seems to be doing some bit of damage control and has been seen to post subtle hints on his love life. Trevor has shared a couple of pictures with him and Kenyan born Hollywood star, Lupita Nyongo.

The internet went abuzz that the two might be dating, after all, this is after Trevor Noah posted the photo with what he captioned “dear friends”, this made the Twitter universe go into detective mode with some hinting that there could be more than “dear friend” between the two. The “couple” have in the past been seen together and have shown up at many functions together.

“When I look back on my favourite moments the smiles will without a doubt be from the friends that were there at each milestone along the way. Throwback to me and my dear friend @lupitanyongo backstage at my MSG show (sic),” he captioned the photo.

Their “friendship” is so deep that Lupita will actually be playing Trevor Noah’s mother in a movie about the actor’s life.  This might also explain why the two have been together a lot of times.  Lupita herself has also been posting cryptic messages regarding Trevor, once calling him his “Best friend after he went out of his busy schedule to meet Lupita’s mom.
