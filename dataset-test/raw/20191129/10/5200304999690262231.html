Overnights: Star Plus stays in lead on Thursday in UK

29 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Raj Baddhan

Star Plus remained the No.1 Asian TV broadcaster in UK ratings on Thursday, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Plus delivered a share of 0.11%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television with 0.09%, Colors with 0.07%, New Vision TV with 0.06%, Hum TV and PTC Punjabi with 0.05%, Star Gold, Star Bharat, Colors Rishtey and ZEE Cinema with 0.03%, ZEE TV, Geo News, B4U Movies, Sony SAB TV and Sony MAX with 0.02% and Geo TV with 0.01%.

Star Plus’ top attractions were ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ at 19:30 with 54,400 viewers, followed by ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ at 22:00 with 44,500 viewers, ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 40,900 viewers, ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 35,400 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 34,300 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 27,400 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 23,500 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 16,100 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 15,500 viewers and ‘Namah’ at 18:30 under 1,000 viewers.

On Sony TV, ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ topped ratings with 68,200 viewers at 21:00, followed by ‘Mere Dad Ki Dulhan’ at 22:00 with 21,200 viewers, ‘Mere Sai’ at 19:00 with 20,800 viewers and ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 with 15,300 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ delivered 32,500 viewers at 21:30, followed by the repeat at 13:00 with 22,100 viewers and ‘Choti Sarrdaarni’ at 19:30 with 9,200 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Kun Faya Kun’ led with 33,300 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘Tum Mere He Rehna’ at 21:00 with 13,100 viewers and ‘Soya Mera Naseeb’ at 19:30 with 10,000 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ was watched by 20,600 viewers at 18:30.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
