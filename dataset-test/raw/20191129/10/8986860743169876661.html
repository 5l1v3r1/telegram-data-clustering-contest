29 Nov 2019, 10:02 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Actress Joselyn Dumas has become the lastest person to bare teeth at Kumawood actor Justice Hymns aka Mmebusem but now known popularly as Ghana Jesus.

“Enough of this nonsense. I don’t support this….Christian Youth of Ghana, our God cannot be mocked in the name of comedy….” he posted

“Thank you. We are now condoning blasphemy All in the name of comedy smh.”
