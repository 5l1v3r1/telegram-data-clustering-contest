Best advent calendars for children and adults in 2019, from Harrods chocolate to LEGO

29 Nov 2019, 10:57 by Precious Adesina

Like many of the popular Christmas practices we engage in today, the tradition of counting down the days until Christmas is of German origin. Prior to the early 1850s, this was simply done by lighting candles or marking walls. By the early 20th century, advent calendars had the small doors that are now known worldwide.

While advent calendars filled with chocolate are both common and fun, there are so many other options worth considering - including cheese, gin and sweets. These days you can get beauty calendars full of lovely scented perfume and lotions in advent calendars for adults, or pieces of railway or lego which you build as you go in advent calendars for kids. 

It's only a few days before the Christmas countdown begins, so we've picked what we believe are some of the best advent calendars of 2019 so you don't have to hunt around. This list is full of variety so there's something for everyone. Hopefully you'll enjoy the 24 days before Christmas as much as we will.
