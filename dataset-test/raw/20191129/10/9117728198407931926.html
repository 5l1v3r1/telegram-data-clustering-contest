Watch: Daniel Ricciardo suffers oil leak in FP1 of Abu Dhabi GP!

29 Nov 2019, 10:06 by Bobby Vincent

Daniel Ricciardo was on a hot lap with just 30 minutes remaining of the first free practice session of the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix weekend, his car suffered a mini explosion with a suspected oil leak. The Aussie driver does not know the impact this will have on Renault's weekend just yet. The session was haulted due to a red flag.
