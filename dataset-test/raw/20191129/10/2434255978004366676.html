Harvey Norman offering huge 50% sale for Black Friday

There is serious money to be saved here!

29 Nov 2019, 10:04 by Blathin de Paor

Harvey Norman have launched a fantastic Black Friday discount event on plenty of products, just in time for Christmas shopping!

The retailer has confirmed it will be offering up to 50% off in the biggest sales event of the year from gadgets to furniture and homeware.

The sale includes up to 30% off TVs and smartphones, up to 50% off headphones, and up to €400 off laptops among other deals in store and online.

And the store will even stay open until midnight to meet demand, as shoppers hunt for their bargains.

Peter Hearn, CEO, Harvey Norman Ireland said: “While the Black Friday sales period has got longer, there are still deals available only on Friday November 29.

"There is a great atmosphere in-store and we are extending our opening hours to midnight on Friday.

"Shoppers know to keep an eye out online and to visit in-store. Black Friday promotions this year will be better than ever.”
