PM Modi clicks uber-cool selfie at Mohena Kumari Singh and Suyesh Rawat’s wedding reception

Prime Minister Narendra Modi attended Mohena Kumari Singh’s wedding reception in Delhi.

29 Nov 2019, 10:11

Mohena Kumari Singh, the Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai fame actress and her husband Suyesh Rawat threw a grand wedding reception in Delhi on November 28. Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi attended the wedding reception and since then, Mohena is on top of the world. A few videos and pictures of the Prime Minister attending their lavish wedding reception are doing the rounds on the internet.

In fact, the actress took to her official Instagram handle to share a picture of her and her husband along with PM Modi. Mohena wrote "Truly blessed by the gracious presence of our honourable and admired Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi Ji at our Reception. And a huge big thank you for this Uber Cool Selfie! Koti Koti Pranaam @narendramodi ji. @satpalmaharajofficial @suyeshrawat @vasundhrarajlaxmi"

In the latest video, Mohena Kumari Singh, Suyesh Rawat and PM Narendra Modi, along with the couple's family members can be seen on the stage, striking a pose for the shutterbugs. Mohena and Suyesh greeted the PM and the two then clicked a selfie - group-fie, if you will - with him. After that, PM Narendra Modi is seen congratulating the couple.

The Prime Minister donned white kurta-pyjama and teamed it up with his signature Modi jacket in brown. While Mohena Kumari wore a heavy baby-pink embroidered Anarkali suit, her husband Suyesh was in a white pathani suit with a colorful turban. Mohena Kumari Singh, the Princess of Rewa, tied the knot with Suyesh Rawat on October 14 this year. Before their wedding reception in Delhi, Mohena had also organised a grand reception in her hometown Rewa. It was attended by several renowned people including government dignitaries.

On the work front, Mohena is known for her role as Keerti Singhania in the popular TV show Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai. The actress is also a dancer and has participated in various reality shows.
