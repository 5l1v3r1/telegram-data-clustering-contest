Prophet Cosmos Walker makes shocking prophecy about Otumfuo Osei Tutu II

29 Nov 2019, 10:30 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Popular Ghanaian prophet His Grace Cosmos Walker Affran has made a yet shocking prophecy about the Asantehene Otumfuo Nana Osei Tutu II.

According to the popular Prophet, some people are planning to plant cocaine into the Asantehene’s bag.

He suggested that Ghanaians pray to avert the evil plans by people he failed to mention.

He wrote on Facebook; “They are going to plant cocaine in Otumfuo Nana Osei Tutu II, bag. #pray #Behind #Elephants’s. Is never a good bye. ‘Thou saith the Lord’ Prophecy by Prophet Cosmos Walker Affran”.

His Grace Cosmos Walker Affran is known for predicting some prophecies that came to pass.

He predicted the Ethiopian plane crash that killed everyone on board.

However, he has predicted a win for the National Democratic Congress in the upcoming 2020 elections.
