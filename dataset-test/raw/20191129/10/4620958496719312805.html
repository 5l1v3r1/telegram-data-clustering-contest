Vancouver police release photo of suspect in attempted robbery

29 Nov 2019, 10:35 by Vancouver Courier

Vancouver police are looking for help identifying a suspect in an attempted robbery.

Just before 6 p.m. on Oct. 21, a man walked into the 888 Mini Market near Beach Avenue and Hornby Street and attempted to rob the store.

After assaulting the clerk, a 61-year-old Vancouver man, the suspect fled west on Beach Avenue.

The suspect is described as white with a slim build. He is believed to be in his mid-30s and was wearing a black baseball hat with a white logo, a black sweatshirt and black and grey plaid pajama pants.
