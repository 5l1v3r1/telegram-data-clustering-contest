Arsenal Fan TV star DT slams Ray Parlour for claiming supporters want team to lo

29 Nov 2019, 10:14

Ray Parlour has been branded a hypocrite by AFTV regular MR DT after the Arsenal legend claimed supporters were happy when the team loses.

Unai Emery is facing the sack after last night’s farcical 2-1 Europa League defeat against Eintracht Frankfurt, played out in front of a half empty Emirates Stadium.

The Spaniard has presided over a calamitous run of just one win from the last nine matches and looks set to be given his marching orders today, less than a month after he was given a vote of confidence by the club’s board.

AFTV, a YouTube channel with over one million subscribers, has established its reputation as the outspoken, unofficial, voice of the club’s fanbase with cult personalities such as DT regularly attracting over 100,000 views.

Indeed, his post match verdict on last night’s defeat, during which he called on the board to end Emery’s reign, has already been played on 421,000 occasions.
