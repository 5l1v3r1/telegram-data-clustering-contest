Discover Tokyo and Japan 

Tokyo is the largest city in Japan and offers seemingly endless things to see and do. From breathtaking temples and shrines to delectable, modern eateries, vibrant nightlife to peaceful gardens, there is something for everyone to enjoy. What’s more, the city itself has a vibrant, bustling atmosphere, and many locals don extravagant styes on its streets. For instance, the Harajuku style includes a variety of brightly coloured clothes, garish costumes, teased hair and sky-high shoes. Visitors may also explore Mount Fuji, the Ghibli Museum, the Zao Fox Village, Tokyo’s Robot Restaurant, and much more.
