Balenciaga Pays Homage to Paris With Eiffel Tower-Indebted Items

Featuring playful prints.

29 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Mallory Chin

Spanish fashion house Balenciaga is paying homage to the City of Love, and its Paris home, with a new set of items that feature the Eiffel Tower.

The Eiffel Tower, which has become synonymous with Paris, is featured throughout a range of long-sleeved tees, pullover hoodies, jackets and accessories. On the black tee, a colorful “Paris” motif is embellished across the front chest which wittily replaces the “A” with the Eiffel Tower. Over on the “i,” a heart replaces the dot. Elsewhere the black jacket sports contrasting white decals of Parisian monuments and the words “Paris,” “France” and “Balenciaga.” Finally the red hoodie is printed with a monogram mixing the Eiffel Tower and the house’s “BB” insignia.

The new items are available to purchase now via Balenciaga boutiques and balenciaga.com.
