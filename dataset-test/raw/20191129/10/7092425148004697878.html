Stormzy has just announced a massive Dublin gig for next September

29 Nov 2019, 10:03 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

Stormzy has just announced a huge 55-date ‘H.I.T.H. World Tour’ which includes a massive gig in Ireland next September.

With shows spanning across Europe, Asia, Australia, New Zealand, North America, UK & Ireland and Africa, the tour marks Stormzy’s biggest live offering to date.

Kicking off the tour in Dubai on February  7 and concluding in Africa in October, the tour will also see him make his solo debut at London’s O2 Arena for two nights on September 3 and 4, marking his first solo shows in the capital since his 3-day stint at Brixton Academy in May 2017.

Subsequent to London’s O2 Arena, Stormzy will grace the stages of 11 more arenas throughout the UK and Ireland, including a stop at Dublin's 3Arena on September 8.

Tickets for the Dublin gig start from €49.50 and go on sale at 9am next Friday (December 6).
