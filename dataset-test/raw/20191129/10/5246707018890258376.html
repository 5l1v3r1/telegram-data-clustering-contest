New Pokémon Sword And Shield Galar Plush Release Next Month, Including Wooloo

A lovely set of lovely 'mon

29 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Ryan Craddock

The very first plush toys for Pokémon Sword and Shield that aren't your typical Starter or Legendary Pokémon are set to be released into the world next month.

All of the designs found below will launch at Pokémon Center Japan on 14th December. Eager fans will be able to get their hands on Gossifleur, Eldegoss, Wooloo, Corviknight, and Drednaw with prices ranging from 1,815 to 2,200 yen (approx. £13 to £15.50).

For those of you not based in Japan and therefore unable to get to a Pokémon Center, never fear. All five toys will be available to pre-order from Amazon Japan on the very same day, with international shipping available. The products haven't appeared just yet so we don't know any pricing details, but make sure to check back here if you're interested.

Now then, who wants to get us a Wooloo?
