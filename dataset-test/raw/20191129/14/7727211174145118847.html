FIFA 20 Ultimate Team Black Friday Best of TOTW squad revealed

The latest FUT 20 promo has been unveiled - the Black Friday Best of TOTW squad featuring Sadio Mane, Lionel Messi and Kylian Mbappe.

29 Nov 2019, 14:52 by Nathan Bliss

It's Black Friday, and for FIFA Ultimate Team players that can only mean one thing, a very special FUT promo.

EA SPORTS have made a habit of releasing a special edition squad to celebrate Black Friday on FIFA Ultimate Team over the last few years, with a Best of TOTW squad normally revealed - and this year is no different.

This year's squad is very special indeed, with some of the world's best players included in the latest Black Friday FUT Promo, including Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, Kylian Mbappe, Sadio Mane, Robert Lewandowski and many more.

EA SPORTS haven't confirmed how long these previous TOTW cards will stay in packs, they've just said it's for a limited time only, but they are in packs as of right now!

Check out the full FIFA 20 Ultimate Team Black Friday Best of TOTW squad below, it's a stunner.

FUT 20 Black Friday Best of TOTW squad

RW: Lionel Messi (Barcelona) - 94 OVR

LW: Cristiano Ronaldo (Piemonte Calcio) - 94 OVR

LF: Eden Hazard (Real Madrid) - 92 OVR

CM: Kevin De Bruyne (Man City) - 92 OVR

ST: Robert Lewandowski (Bayern Munich) - 91 OVR

LW: Kylian Mbappe (PSG) - 90 OVR

ST: Sadio Mane (Liverpool) - 89 OVR

ST: Paulo Dybala (Piemonte Calcio) - 89 OVR

ST: Dries Mertens (Napoli) - 88 OVR

CAM: Radja Nainggolan (Genoa) - 86 OVR

ST: Wissam Ben Yedder (AS Monaco) - 85 OVR

These cards are now available in packs for a limited time, so you better be quick!
