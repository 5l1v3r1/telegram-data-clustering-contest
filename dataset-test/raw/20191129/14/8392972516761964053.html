Aldi is selling this flashing Christmas Candle Snow Spinner for only £5.99

Giving homes a glittery glow this Christmas

29 Nov 2019, 14:26 by Robert Leigh

Excited festive shoppers have shone a light on an excellent Christmas decoration guaranteed to light up the home.

A member of the Extreme Coupling and Bargains group on Facebook recently highlighted a purchase of an impressive candle from Aldi - and other bargain hunters loved it.

The shopper will be giving their home a glittery glow this Christmas after picking up the flashing, snow globe-styled light for under £6.

They captioned images and a video posted to the social media group: "£5.99 from Aldi, it's beautiful, 3 triple A batteries included with it."

The candle comes with an LED colour-changing function and glittering snow-swirls effect causing it to flash in colours such as blue, green, red, yellow, pink and purple.

Filled with water and glitter, the candle has dimensions of 25cm by 9cm and is made of plastic.

Really calming and adds the wow factor.

It is also battery-powered, meaning it can be displayed on window sills, fireplaces and cupboard to give any room an immediate festive feel.

Several would-be buyers insisted in the Facebook group they would be snapping one up as soon as they next make a trip to the budget supermarket.
