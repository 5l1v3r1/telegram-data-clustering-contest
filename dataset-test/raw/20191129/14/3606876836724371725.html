There's no denying that the keto diet dominated health and wellness trends in 2019. The super low-carb, moderate protein, and high-fat diet helped countless people achieve their weight-loss and health goals this year, so what's not to love? Well, there's that whole part about completely cutting out carbs...I'm talking fruit, grains, and yes...BREAD.

That's why Women's Health and Delish partnered up to create Keto for Carb Lovers, a mouth-watering keto cookbook complete with a 21-day meal plan to kick-start your weight loss sans deprivation.

Ready to start that low-carb lifestyle? Keto for Carb Lovers is currently on sale for 32 percent off through Wednesday, 12/4.

This book is chock-full of yummy keto-friendly recipes that mimic classic carb-filled foods, including: cookie dough keto fat bombs, carrot cake keto balls, Brussels sprouts "chips," keto pancakes, cauliflower "toast," cauliflower "benedict," keto taco cups, cheese taco shells, keto lasagna, keto peanut butter cookies, and keto pizza.

Whatever your carb craving, this cookbook basically covers it.

Reminder: Keto dieters typically eat no more than 50 grams of carbs a day (some strict keto dieters even opt for just 20 grams a day)which is why these carb swaps are so clutch.
