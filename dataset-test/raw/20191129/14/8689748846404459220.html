Yvonne Nelson sends an interesting birthday message to John Mahama as he clocks 61

29 Nov 2019, 14:45 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian actress and movie producer, Yvonne Nelson has sent an interesting birthday message to Ghana’s ex-president, John Dramani Mahama as he celebrates his 61st birthday today.

It seems her message has come as a shock to Ghanaians as she has for long been a critic of the former head of state.

According to Yvonne who has always criticised John Mahama of his bad governance, there were some particular things the ex-president did that she really liked.

In a post on twitter, the Heels and Sneakers actress praised John Mahama thanking God for keeping him and also thanking the ex-president for his developmental projects such as New terminal and the Ridge Hospital.

“Happy birthday you impressed me with our new terminal and the ridge hospital good job! #development is what Gh needs!” she wrote.
