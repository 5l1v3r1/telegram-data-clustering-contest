The Weeknd's 'Blinding Lights' Lyrics Seem to Address His Feelings for Bella Hadid

29 Nov 2019, 14:10 by Alyssa Bailey

The Weeknd gave everyone an extra helping of his new music era last night, with the Thanksgiving day release of his second single, "Blinding Lights." While "Heartless" lyrically explored how The Weeknd went back to his old playboy ways following his split from Selena Gomez, "Blinding Lights" seems to address how fast he got over playing the field because of the reemergence of his on-off girlfriend Bella Hadid.

The Weeknd hinted at the end of "Heartless" that Hadid had just come back: "I lost my heart and my mind / I try to always do right / I thought I lost you this time / You just came back in my life / You never gave up on me (Why don't you?) / I'll never know what you see (Why won't you?) / I don't do well when alone (Oh yeah) / You hear it clear in my tone."

"Blinding Lights" builds on that and his feelings for the woman who's consistently been there for him.

The Weeknd and Hadid, in real life, have been in an on-off relationship since April 2015. The two broke up in November 2016; The Weeknd dated Gomez from January 2017 to October 2017; then The Weeknd and Hadid started hanging out again in November 2017. They were back to full-fledge couple status in the summer of 2018.

In August 2019, they were reported to break up again but remain friendly. Sources hinted there's a possibility they could get back together.

Here, the lyrics to "Blinding Lights," via Genius, with possible references to Hadid explained. Listen to the song below too.

Hadid lives in New York City and as a model, travels a lot for work, meaning some of their relationship (and communication during their off-periods) was long-distance. The last time the two dated, things got more serious though: She and The Weeknd ultimately moved in together in October 2018 before they split this summer. Distance was said to be a big contributor to their latest split. In August, a source told E!, "They are in different places right now, physically and mentally. Bella is prepping for her fashion week commitments and Abel is working on his music and his upcoming acting debut [in the Safdie Brothers' film, Uncut Gems.]"

During Hadid's birthday in October 2019, E! reported The Weeknd called her to wish her a happy birthday and then ultimately met up with her in New York City. A source told the outlet then that “The Weeknd did reach out to Bella during the day to wish her a ‘Happy Birthday’ and they have been in touch. There is no bad blood between the two and they are on good terms right now. There isn’t any chance of them getting back together right now, but Bella never wants to have any anger or resentment towards anyone.” When reports emerged shortly after of the two getting back together, The Weeknd's rep shut them down. The Weeknd and Hadid "attended [her birthday party] as friends," the rep said. They haven't been photographed hanging out together since then.
