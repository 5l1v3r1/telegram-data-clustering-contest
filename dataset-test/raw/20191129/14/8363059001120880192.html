Exploding Kittens' Bonkers Card Games Are On Offer for Black Friday

29 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Holly Brockwell

As you might expect from a company called Exploding Kittens LLC, there are some quite unusual card game ideas in this particular Black Friday sale.

As well as Exploding Kittens itself and its expansions – the first of which is inevitably called Imploding Kittens – there's:

  • You've Got Crabs, in which you get to yell "YOU'VE GOT CRABS" at people, now £17.49, and the Imitation Crab Expansion Kit, £17.06
  • Bears vs Babies, in which you make terrifying but hilarious monsters out of random bits of other creatures, now £14.99
  • Throw Throw Burrito, in which you literally get to throw foam burritos at friends and loved ones, now £19.99

And more, plus the NSFW version of Exploding Kittens and the Party Pack game.

If the art style looks familiar, it's because it's by the same people as The Oatmeal webcomic.
