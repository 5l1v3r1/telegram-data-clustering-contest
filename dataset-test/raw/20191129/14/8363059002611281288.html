The Fitbit Versa Lite Hits the Lowest Ever Price for Black Friday, Now Just £90

29 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Tom Pritchard

At £150 the Fitbit Versa Lite was never particularly expensive, certainly not compared to some of the other watches in the Fitbit portfolio, but when the discounts come about we're not going to turn them away for no good reason. So if you fancied yourself a Fitbit watch but didn't want to spend a lot, this is the perfect time to do it.

The Versa Lite has never been so cheap now that Amazon has knocked £60 off the asking price in its Black Friday sale. So now it's just £90, which is basically stocking-filler money as far as tech is concerned.

Though it could be cheaper, because we always want things to drop to 'Christmas cracker present' levels of affordability, and if you want to spend just £40 you can get the Fitbit Inspire instead. That's down from £70, and makes Fitbit's most affordable tracker even more appealing. Just bear in mind this one does not have a heart rate monitor. That model costs £73 at the moment.
