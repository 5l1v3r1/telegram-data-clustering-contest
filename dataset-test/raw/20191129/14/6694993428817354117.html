Aldi shopper spots what could be the worst 'Black Friday deal' of all time

'Bargain of the day'

29 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Rachel Pugh, https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/authors/rachel-pugh/

Black Friday is now in full swing, and shoppers have been inundated with deals on pretty much every single product you can shake a stick at.

From Apple AirPods, to Now TV sticks and Build-A-Bear teddies , there really is something to keep everyone happy in the sales.

But while there are many fabulous deals to be had, one shopper has spotted the best one yet - and it comes courtesy of Aldi .

Posting a picture of a pile of McVitie's biscuits on Twitter, Spod1000 pointed out the phenomenal offer.

Under the picture, Spod1000 wrote: "Black Friday is here. Bargain of the day."

It has to be seen to be believed, but Aldi has slashed an incredible ONE PENCE off the biscuits.

Calm down, Aldi, don't be spoiling us too much now.

The Manchester Evening News has contacted Aldi for comment on the bargain find, and we'll update you when we can.

Yesterday shoppers rushed to Aldi stores and were seen queuing in store car parks - not to get their hands on the £3.99 biscuits, but to try and nab some goodies from the Kevin the Carrot toy range.

However, scenes of chaos quickly began, as people became disgruntled with the lengthy waiting times.

Taking to Twitter, one frustrated shopper expressed their anger: "Thought I’d get up early and go out to see if I could get one of those Kevin the carrot toys at Aldi.

"Got there and the queue was snaking back past the store with people still coming. All that for a toy!"

"I am in a queue at  #aldi  to buy fluffy sprouts..." said somebody else.

Another said: "Queues are forming over a carrot! A plush carrot at that!  #kevinthecarrot#Aldi#Worksop ."

"Stalybridge Aldi sold out of Kevin’s in 20 minutes," another said.
