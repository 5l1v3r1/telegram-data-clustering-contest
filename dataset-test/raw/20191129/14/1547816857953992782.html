Janhvi Kapoor Feels ‘Too Much Khushi’ As She Indulges In Some Yummy Fries With Her Sister In The US

29 Nov 2019, 14:24 by Lubna Khan

Janhvi Kapoor, who recently announced the wrap up of Dostana 2’s Punjab shooting schedule, is currently in the USA with her sister Khushi Kapoor. She took to Instagram to share pictures of the two at the patio seating of a restaurant, gorging on some yummy fries

Janhvi Kapoor is one of the most active Bollywood celebrities on social media. We love seeing her post new things daily on her Instagram feed as well as stories, just as much she loves posting them. The Dhadak actress has a huge following on Instagram- 6.7 million to be precise, and with all the entertaining things she posts, we’re sure they’re all hooked to her social media. She keeps fans updated about her vacations, outings as well as her personal life. Her sister Khushi Kapoor appears regularly on her Instagram and once again, Janhvi is reunited with Khushi Kapoor and posted pictures to let her fans know about it.

Janhvi Kapoor has made a trip to the US and took to Instagram to share several cute pictures and boomerangs while she is with her sister Khushi Kapoor. In one of her stories, the cute boomerang clip shows Khushi Kapoor dancing and Janhvi is clearly very happy after being reunited with her sister. She wrote, “Too much Khushi!!!” The two were at the patio seating of a restaurant, gorging on some yummy fries. Sharing another boomerang of her fries, she wrote, “ Life feels complete.” Another picture shows Janhvi indulging in the yummy fries and she can be seen with her eyes closed.
