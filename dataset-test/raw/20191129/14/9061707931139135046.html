Police concerned for safety of missing Derbyshire man

Lee Campion was last seen in Mickleover

29 Nov 2019, 14:10 by John Grant

Derbyshire police have launched an appeal to find a missing 27-year-old man.

Lee Campion was last spotted in Mickleover at around 10.40am yesterday. (November 28)

He was wearing dark jeans, trainers and a grey hood with a fluorescent emblem on the front shoulder, says Derbyshire Constabulary.

Mr Campion, of Langley Mill, is slimly-built, around 5ft 7ins tall and has short hair.

Officers have asked anyone with information on his whereabouts to contact them on 101 with reference number 199-291119.
