Wolves fans react online to Nuno Arsenal links after Emery exit

With the news that Unai Emery has been sacked as Arsenal's head coach, social media has been awash with speculation about who will replace him at The Emirates.

29 Nov 2019, 14:51

Wolves manager Nuno has emerged as one of the bookies favourites for the job, alongside former Juventus boss Massimiliano Allegri and Manchester City assistant Mikel Arteta.

But Wolves fans have been quick to express their contempt online.
