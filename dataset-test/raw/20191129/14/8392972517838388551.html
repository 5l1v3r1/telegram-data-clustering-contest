Coronation Street SPOILERS: Jade 'loses it' in shocking scenes TONIGHT

She's after revenge!

29 Nov 2019, 14:32 by Helen Fear

Jade Rowan will 'lose it' in shocking scenes to air in tonight's Coronation Street.

Earlier this week, we discovered that Hope and Ruby's nanny had been hiding the mother of all secrets since she arrived in Weatherfield...

As some viewers had long suspected, the shifty childminder is actually related to Corrie killer John Stape - she's his daughter.

And it seems Jade has revenge on her mind, appearing to blame Fiz for her dad's death.

This episode is the one where she fully loses it - I can't wait for it to play out.

Jade will snap and lose her temper on Friday (November 29).

The single white female will be seen losing control after being told that Fiz and Tyrone no longer need her services.

Screaming with rage and trashing things, Jade becomes distraught that her plans are falling apart and fans will see her snap for the first time - exposing her unhinged side.

The scenes follow a dangerous fire, which Tyrone tried to blame Hope for - but fans think "twisted" Jade was responsible.

Actress Lottie Henshall, who plays Jade, told Metro.co.uk: "You will really see her lose it tonight. We've seen her start to lose control, but this episode is the one where she fully loses it - I can't wait for it to play out.

"Every single episode, we're trying to make it different. We want to confuse viewers about what her end game is."

She teased: "There's so much more for her to come. There are so many episodes where she shows vulnerability. But is she being sincere or is she still lying?"

She continued: "You can't trust her and she is dangerous, but there is vulnerability.

"I don't know where it's going. I honestly live for getting the scripts. What's she doing this week!? I wish I could tell you!"

Reports tell us that Jade's vengeance will take a disturbing turn, when she reports Fiz and Tyrone to social services.

We've already seen her painting bruises on Hope's skin, but in upcoming scenes Fiz and Tyrone have their children taken away from them and put into care.
