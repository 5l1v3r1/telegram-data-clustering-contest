Courteney Cox shares rare snap with teenage daughter Coco to celebrate Thanksgiving

The iconic actress took to social media to share a heartwarming mother-daughter snap as they celebrated Thanksgiving Day - it's ultimate family goals

29 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Irishmirror.ie

Courteney Cox has shared a glimpse into her enviable mother-daughter relationship with mini-me Coco Arquette after posting a heartwarming photo to Instagram to celebrate Thanksgiving.

The iconic actress, 55, shared a sweet selfie with her lookalike daughter Coco, 15, on Friday and proudly revealed how thankful she was.

To mark Thanksgiving and point at her gratitude towards her teenage daughter, Courteney shared the photo with her 6.3 millions Instagram followers alongside a heartfelt caption writing: "So thankful!!!"

The Monica Geller actress looked sensational as she smiled softly while snuggling up to her pretty teenage daughter, who she shares with ex-husband David Arquette .

The sitcom legend embraced autumnal colours as she upped the glamour in a burnt-orange toned shirt dress and accessorised with a subtle silver necklace.

The raven-haired beauties looked almost identical as they cosied together for the family snap and showed off their gorgeously glossy locks.

The Friends star accentuated her natural beauty for Thanksgiving with a natural make-up look and a smokey eyeshadow.

The A-lister also shared another snap dedicated to her Thanksgiving celebrations as she relaxed in a plush sofa setting alongside her three dogs.

Courteney's former Friends co-star Jennifer Aniston instantly commented on her loving family selfie and hinted at her close-knit bond with the duo, commenting: " Coco! You’re growing up way too fast.

"Or am I protecting? I love you deep," followed by a series of love heart emojis.

Courteney continued to celebrate the occasion and joined bestie Jennifer for her annual Friendsgiving party with a line-up of their star-studded pals.

Courteney and Jennifer were snapped alongside close friends Jimmy Kimmel, Will Arnett and Jason Bateman for the Rachel Green actress's yearly bash the night before Thanksgiving.

Jennifer, 50, laid on a feast that was full of piles of meats, different types of potatoes and ditched tradition by cooking up the Mexican dish of enchiladas.

Justin Theroux shared a glimpse into the evening by capturing a snippet of Cox’s heartfelt speech to Aniston, before Courteney shouted: "Justin, please stop filming me!"

In the video, Cox told her real life friend, "Jen, I love you so much."
