Fabulous new Dunnes Stores Jetland in Limerick celebrates opening next weekend

There’ll be fashion, food, homewares, giveaways and fun for all the family!

29 Nov 2019, 14:45 by Rsvplive.ie

Ireland’s top food retailer Dunnes Stores is opening a brand new store at Jetland Shopping Centre, Limerick next weekend.

The all-new Dunnes Stores Jetland will offer some of the leading Irish names in food, fashion and homeware – all under one roof!

The new state-of-the-art premises will be open seven days a week from 8am to 10pm Monday to Saturday and 9am to 9pm on Sunday – with extended hours in the run up to the festive season.

Its unveiling will take place from Friday 6th December to Sunday 8th December.

To celebrate the launch of the store, there will be opportunities to meet some of Ireland’s best fashion, beauty and home designers throughout the weekend including: Paul Costelloe, Carolyn Donnelly, Brendan Courtney, Sonya Lennon, Leigh Tucker, Diarmuid Gavin, Derrick Carberry and Ireland’s favourite chef Neven Maguire.

Limerick’s own style icon Celia Holman Lee will also host some fab fashion shows and there’ll be plenty of  giveaways and family fun all weekend!

Watch out too for events in the Food Hall, where some of Ireland’s leading food producers will be on hand for a chat and for shoppers to sample some of their award-winning products.

The world class Food Hall will include: Sheridans Cheesemongers, James Whelan Butchers, Alternative Bread Company and Nourish Health Food Store.

There will also be a gardening store which will feature the complete collection from Diarmuid Gavin’s gardening range ‘Outer Spaces’, exclusive to Dunnes Stores.

A full list of events for the ‘Making Christmas for Everyone’ weekend at Dunnes Stores Jetland is below:

Friday 6th December:

  • Fashion shows and makeovers with Brendan Courtney and Sonya Lennon with Ireland’s top make-up artist Derrick Carberry
  • Neven Maguire - Live Cookery Demonstration – 6pm
  • Official Opening of Nostalgic Limerick Photographic Exhibition by local author Paddy Brennan
  • Unveiling of RSVP Live Style Session Competition Winners

Saturday 7th December:

  • Live fashion shows hosted by Limerick’s own Celia Holman Lee – From Cashmere to Partywear
  • Live performances with Dolmen String Quartet

Sunday 8th December:

  • Live on stage ‘Outer Spaces’  gardening workshops with Diarmuid Gavin
  • Live baby led feeding demonstrations with Aileen Cox Blundell of baby led feeding
  • Meet the Designer Paul Costelloe on stage showcasing the Cashmere Collection with Celia Holman Lee

Plus throughout the weekend:

  • Opportunities to meet the designers, buyers and food producers, including Carolyn Donnelly, Leigh Tucker and Patricia Martinez of Savida
  • Pop-up shops from Cadburys, Quality Street and Butler’s Chocolates
  • Santa Claus Experience and plenty of kids’ activities and entertainment
  • Lots of spot prizes and giveaways all weekend!

The ‘Make Christmas for Everyone’ weekend at Dunnes Stores Jetland runs from Friday 6 December through to Sunday 8 December. Don't miss it!
