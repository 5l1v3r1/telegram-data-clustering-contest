The Girl On The Train: Parineeti Chopra’s Hindi Adaptation Finally Gets A Release Date

29 Nov 2019, 14:41 by Gretel Sequeira

Parineeti Chopra has been working day and nights for her upcoming film ‘The Girl On The Train.’ As the film is a Hindi adaptation of the Emily Blunt film by the same name, Parineeti’s movie gets a release date. The film is set to hit theatres next year

Parineeti Chopra has many films in the pipeline and the actress is busy with her work commitments. Earlier this year, Parineeti constantly shared pictures and videos from the shooting sets of her upcoming film, ‘The Girl On The Train’. As the film, ‘The Girl On The Train’ is a Hindi adaptation of the famous novel of the same name, the actress has been gearing up hard for her upcoming flick. While the film will showcase Parineeti in her best, the Hindi adaptation of ‘The Girl On The Train’ finally gets a release date and will hit theatres next year.

The Parineeti Chopra film ‘The Girl On The Train’ is set to release on May 8, 2020. Today, the makers of the film finally released the official dates for the film to hit the big-screens and we just can’t wait. While Parineeti Chopra has been gearing up for her upcoming flick, the film releases next year in May and is surely going to release with a bang. As the film is a Hindi adaptation of the 2015 released psychological thriller novel ‘The Girl On The Train’ by British author Paula Hawkins, it gives out the narratives from the three different women about relationship troubles and binge drinking. The novel was soon adapted into a film of the same name released in 2016, starring Hollywood actress Emily Blunt in pivotal roles. In the Hindi adaptation of ‘The Girl On The Train’ Parineeti Chopra will be facing a tough challenge as she will step into the shoes of Emily Blunt. While Chopra has been working hard on delivering her role, the pictures featuring the actress in different situations from the film, are gripping and impressive.
