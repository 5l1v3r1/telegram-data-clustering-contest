Hailey Baldwin and Justin Bieber went for dinner at Komodo in Miami, showing off their coordinated outfits when they left the restaurant. While the newlyweds are typically both casual dressers in their own right, Justin was decidedly dressed down in his Drew House hoodie, ripped jeans, slippers, and pink socks, while Hailey was fully ready for a night out. I mean, who wouldn't want to go dancing in her Opening Ceremony Space Shirt with its breezy arm cutouts and those square-toe sandals? Hailey's slouchy jeans also provided optimal room for movement and her Balenciaga minibag wouldn't be much of a burden while holding a cocktail. Justin, please put on your sneakers and take Hailey to the club!

I mean, you'd be crazy not to play up a crop top like the one she's wearing, so we don't blame her for braiding her hair back and putting this shirt on full display.
