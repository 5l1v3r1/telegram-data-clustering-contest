Magnetic Mix 067: LP Giobbi

LP Giobbi is ready for the weekend and so should you with this mix.

29 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Ryan Middleton

It’s the weekend everyone. In the US, you have been enjoying too much food and booze since Wednesday night into Thursday. Now it is time to carry on debauchery with a special mix from LP Giobbi, the self-described “producer-DJ-music director-synth warrior goddess.” Born in Oregon and currently in LA, she has toured with the likes of Hotel Garuda, Autograf and Sofi Tukker, while also heading up FEMMEHOUSE, running her own apparel business and releasing music on her own. She is also the co-founder of touring party Animal Talk Collective, which fits perfectly with the theme of this Magnetic Mix.

She sets the scene perfectly for this mix, that runs through over 40 tracks in 80 minutes and drops records from the likes of Eli Brown, KiNK, PAWSA and of course plenty in her discography.
