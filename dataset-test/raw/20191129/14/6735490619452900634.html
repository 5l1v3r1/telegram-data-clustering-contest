Dwayne Johnson Picks His Own Pro-Wrestling Mount Rushmore

29 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Corey Atad

Dwayne Johnson just revealed his top wrestlers of all time.

The actor and former WWE star responded to a meme image this week featuring a Photoshopped version of Mount Rushmore with the faces of famous wrestlers, including The Rock himself.

Johnson shared his own list of wrestlers in a tweet.
