The official launch of Star Awards is scheduled for tomorrow, November 30

29 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Adu Sarpeah

The wait is almost over as Ghana is going to witness the birth of Star Awards basically to recognise, appreciate and reward industry people who believe in excellence.

The launch of the maiden edition of Star Awards is scheduled to take place tomorrow, November 30, 29019 at Queens Sports Bar and Restaurant at Ahodwo Nhyieso-Kumasi starting 2:00.p.m sharp!

The scheme, which seeks to award excellence in the creative industry, will be the first major awards scheme which has its root in Kumasi.

Industry players are expected to troop to Queens Sports Bar and Restaurant at Ahodwo Nhyieso-Kumasi, where the event will be held. DJ slim, Austin Woode, Zack GH, Y Pee, MrKaxtro, Atuahene amongst other media dignitaries are expected to be in attendance.

More importantly, the main event is slated for June 2020 at a yet to be disclosed location. Like earlier said, Star Awards is organised by Professional Media with Emcee Lento Bebe as CEO of the outfit. Be there in order to be part of the history makers.
