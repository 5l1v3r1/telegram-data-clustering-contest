BREAKING: Appeal Court dismisses APC’s appeal, affirms Bauchi governor’s election 

29 Nov 2019, 12:58 by Armstrong Bakam

ARMSTRONG BAKAM, Bauchi

The Court of Appeal, Jos Division, on Friday, has affirmed the victory of Governor Bala Mohammed and dismissed the All Progressives Congress and its candidate, former Governor Mohammed Abubakar’s appeal challenging his victory as the duly elected governor of Bauchi State.
