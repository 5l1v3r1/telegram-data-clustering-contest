Reekado Banks Releases Colorful Video For New Single “Put In Pressure”

29 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Violet Johnson

Nigerian music star, Ayoleyi Hanniel Solomon popularly known as Reekado Banks has released the official video to his latest single, Put In Pressure.

The song which is an automatic invite to a dance party taps the catchy up-tempo rhythm dominant on the streets of Lagos. It is a follow-up to his hit single Rora and the second single from his upcoming sophomore album.

Since everyone is now looking towards “Detty December” which is almost upon us, this track appears to be the ultimate soundtrack.

Put In Pressure was produced by Kel P, while the colourful visual was created by Clarence Peters.
