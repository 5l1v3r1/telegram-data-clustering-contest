Stormzy tour 2020 - Where to get tickets as rapper announces world tour?

29 Nov 2019, 12:38

Stormzy has announced a 2020 world tour – this is not a drill.

Rapper Stormzy, who earlier this year made history by being the first black British solo artist to headline Glastonbury, is embarking on a mammoth world tour next year.

Here’s what we know so far about when and how you can get tickets.

How to get tickets for Stormzy’s 2020 tour

General sale for the Heavy Is The Head world tour will be opening at 9am on Friday, 6 December.

Tickets will go on sale in Dubai at 8am on Wednesday, 4 December.
