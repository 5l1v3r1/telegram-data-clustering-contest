GOG’s Black Friday sale feels like the old Steam sales

29 Nov 2019, 12:57 by Carrie Talbot

You might have noticed that Black Friday is here, which means there are big sales going on offering discounts on some of the best PC games out there. There’s an Epic Games store Black Friday sale in full swing, plus a Steam autumn sale to check out. Now, there’s a GOG.com Black Friday sale too, offering big discounts on some big games.

The GOG.com Black Friday sale offers deals on over 2,000 games, but highlights include 70% off CDPR’s fantasy RPG The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (Game of the Year Edition) and Obsidian’s post-apocalyptic adventure Fallout: New Vegas (Ultimate Edition) respectively, as well as 50% off A Plague Tale: Innocence.

There’s also 50% off Warhammer 40.000: Mechanicus and The Bard’s Tale IV: Director’s Cut (Standard Edition), 20% off Bloodstained: Ritual of the Night, and a 15% discount on Diablo + Hellfire on offer in the sale.

There are also some deals included for newer releases, such as 20% off Disco Elysium and Trine 4: The Nightmare Prince. You might find our Disco Elysium review handy if you’re keen to jump in and want to find out a bit more.

There are plenty more game deals to discover, though – head over to GOG.com if you’re tempted to pick any up while they’re on sale and browse what else is on offer in the event. The Black Friday sale runs until 14:00 UTC on December 3.

Elsewhere, if you’re looking for some hardware discounts, check out our list of the Black Friday 2019 PC deals on offer – there are some sweet deals on AMD CPUs, graphics cards, gaming laptops to be found.
