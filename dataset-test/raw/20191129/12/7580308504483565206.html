Kanye West Releases “Closed On Sunday” Music Video

29 Nov 2019, 12:19 by Modupeoluwa Adekanye

From his sprawling 4000 plus acres ranch in Wyoming, Kanye West serves the video for “Closed On Sunday”, the second video from ‘Jesus is King” album.

The music video opens alongside Kim Kardashian and their kids North, Saint, Chicago, and Psalm, sitting in the cleft of a rock.

Kourtney and Kris are not also left out in this video, seems like a family reunion.

Kanye brings out his Sunday Service Choir, they surround him as he stands on higher ground in the middle of the crowd.

There are some very dramatic and beautiful visuals of the rugged Wyoming landscape-mountains, rocky terrain, grass-covered plains, and ATVs barreling through the expanse of land.
