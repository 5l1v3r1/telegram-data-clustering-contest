Nike Celebrates the 2020 NBA Paris Game With Special Capsule

The event kicks off on January 24.

29 Nov 2019, 12:34 by Mallory Chin

To celebrate the forthcoming 2020 NBA Paris Game, Nike has just debuted a capsule paying homage to the event.

The range, which fans got a sneak peek at in September, is inspired by the French capital and the city’s basketball culture. Varsity Jackets, hoodies, tees, tracksuits and accessories have each been emblazoned with a special “Paris” motif that sees the “i” replaced by the NBA’s Jerry West-inspired logo. Elsewhere additional NBA Paris insignias are featured throughout the items. The color palette keeps to a more demure black and white for the most part, while other looks have been dressed in loud vibrant prints. Check out the items in the lookbook above.

Pieces from Nike’s 2020 NBA Paris Game capsule are available to purchase now at select Nike retailers and nike.com.

The 2020 NBA Paris Game will take place at the AccorHotels Arena in France, with the Charlotte Hornets and the Milwaukee Bucks set to hit the court on January 24.
