Here’s why all the top brands want to have Ayushmann Khurrana on board

29 Nov 2019, 12:08 by Filmfare

Ayushmann has always been experimental and unconventional with his choice of roles right from the beginning of his career. From his debut as a sperm donor in Shoojit Sircar’s Vicky Donor to Bala where played the role of a bald guy battling premature balding. The Andhadhun actor now has a genre to his name in Bollywood called ‘The Ayushmann Khurrana Genre’.

The year 2019 has been a great year for Ayushmann with two of his movies joining the 100 crore club the actor is on a different high. Reports suggest that the Bala actor has seen a 200 per cent hike in the number of his endorsement deals. Ayushmann is currently endorsing 20 brands. From real estate to mobile, from wafers to eyewear, Khurrana has managed to sign lucrative deals with all of them. 

All of Ayuhsmann’s movies have been loved by both critics and the audience. Hence, there’s no doubt why the actor has all these deals coming his way. A report also stated, "Ayushmann is a huge icon of the Indian youth and every top brand wants to bring him on board because seldom do you find a star that evokes all these qualities!”
