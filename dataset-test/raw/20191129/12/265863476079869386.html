Gabrielle Union Has A Heartfelt Message For Her Supporters Following The Latest Controversy Involving ‘America’s Got Talent’

29 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Alexis Stone

You probably already know the fact that Gabrielle Union has not been asked to return to the show America’s Got Talent. Dwayne Wade stepped in and defended his wife, showing her tons of love and respect on social media.

He penned some emotional thoughts on Twitter, and his followers stepped in and praised his love, respect, and loyalty.

‘“Men lie, Women lie, numbers don’t” Over this past year I’ve been approached by many people saying that my wife @itsgabrielleu is the main reason they’ve started watching #AGT or that they love her insight and sincerity on the show,’ Dwayne posted on his Twitter account, continuing with more messages.

Now, Gabrielle shared a gratitude message for Thanksgiving Day on her own social media account.

‘Grateful ❤❤❤ Happy Thanksgiving good people. To all the friends and family that have my back and all the friends and family, I’ve never met who show love and support when everyone is looking and when no one is looking…All praise, gratitude, and thankfulness,’ Gabrielle captioned her post.

Someone said: ‘Happy Thanksgiving, my friend! Thankful for your friendship! ❤️🤗’ while another follower posted this: ‘Zion is so bold in a midriff for Thanksgiving! Haha, love you guys!!’

Ome other commenter wrote: ‘Aaaaw, Gab we got you ✊🏾✊🏾. Thank you for standing up for what’s right.Happy Thanksgiving to you & yours. 🙏🏾’

Another follower praised Gabrielle: ‘I so respect you and your story. I wish you all the best, and I believe you will not be silenced. God has you!’

As you can see, fans and followers appreciate Gabrielle and have her back.
