Stormzy is coming to the East Midlands - and tickets are on sale soon

The master of grime will be here next year

29 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Simon Wilson, Christopher Harper

Grime star Stormzy has announced a world tour - and he's coming to the East Midlands.

The Heavy Is The Head world tour will kick off next year and he will stop off at the Motorpoint Arena in Nottingham on Friday, September 18, 2020.

Tickets go on sale at 9am on Friday, December 6, with prices starting from Â£40.40 at ticketmaster.co.uk, seetickets.com or motorpointarenanottingham.com, reports NottinghamshireLive.

The announcement follows news that the 26-year-old will be releasing a brand new album called Heavy Is The Head, a record which has been produced by the musician himself.

The album also features vocals from Aitch, Burna Boy, Ed Sheeran, Headie One, H.E.R., Tiana Major9 and YEBBA with production from Fred Gibson, Fraser T Smith and T-Minus.

The rapper, who famously wore a Banksy bullet-proof vest on stage at Glastonbury this summer, was last in Nottingham back in 2017 when he played to a sold out Rock City.
