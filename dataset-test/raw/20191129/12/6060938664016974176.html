Deepika Padukone wants to learn the 'Dheeme Dheeme' hook step from Kartik Aaryan

29 Nov 2019, 12:38 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Deepika Padukone is the epitome of beauty, who enjoys a huge fan following across the globe. She is not only the top A-listed actress in Bollywood but has even carved a niche for herself in Hollywood as well.

But what has come as a surprise is her latest wish to participate in the '#dheemedheemechallenge' which has been going viral on the internet. The dance challenge which has been started by Kartik Aaryan has caught the attention of Deepika and taking to Instagram stories she requested him to teach her the hook step so that even she could partake in the challenge.

The 'Padmaavat' actress who has millions of followers on Instagram, took to the social platform and wrote, "@kartikaaryan will you please teach me the Dheeme Dheeme step!? I want to take part in the #dheemedheemechallenge !!!"

Well, this request is sure to catch the millennial sensation's attention and we are eagerly awaiting to see how Kartik react to it. Meanwhile, Kartik is busy promoting his film 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' with his co-stars Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday.

On the other hand, Deepika is all set to entertain the audience once again with her upcoming films 'Chhapaak' and '83' after staying away from the celluloid in 2019. She will be seen sharing screen space with her husband Ranveer Singh in Kabir Khan's sports drama '83' for the first time post their marriage.
