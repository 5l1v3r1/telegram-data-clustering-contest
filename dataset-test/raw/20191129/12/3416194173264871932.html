Austin Butler is Joined by 'Elvis' Co-Star Olivia DeJonge at GQ Men of the Year Awards 2019

29 Nov 2019, 12:38

Austin Butler and Olivia DeJonge attend the 2019 GQ Men of the Year Awards together on Thursday night (November 28) in Sydney, Australia.

The actors, who will be starring in the upcoming Elvis Presley movie, coordinated in black outfits as they stepped out for the event.

Joining the two at the event was their movie’s director Baz Luhrmann.

Austin and Olivia will be playing Elvis Presley and wife Priscilla in an upcoming movie about the life of the late King of Rock and Roll.
