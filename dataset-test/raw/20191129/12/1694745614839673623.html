These 30+ Mini Gifts From Sephora Will Bring Big Smiles

29 Nov 2019, 12:10 by Morgan Ashley Parker

As POPSUGAR editors, we independently select and write about stuff we love and think you'll like too. POPSUGAR has affiliate and advertising partnerships so we get revenue from sharing this content and from your purchase.

Gifts don't need to be expensive to be good. And, anyway, good things do come in small packages . . . and we're not just talking diamonds. These present ideas from Sephora may be in mini form, but that doesn't make them any less appealing (like a little La Mer face cream, for example). The items are all TSA-approved-for-carry-on small, which means that if your loved ones travel light and are flying back home after the holidays, they'll be aboard with your gift.

Some of these things are cute for stockings, others are sets that are cute for stocking up, and the rest make for a perfect way to let a loved one dabble in something new. There are solo stars that shine alone, like that amethyst face roller, and others who perform better in a pack, like a shampoo-conditioner set.

If you've ever been to a Sephora store, you're most likely familiar with the checkout area lined with small things you never knew you always needed. Consider this list a little of that, too.

Here are 30+ of the best mini items from Sephora that can still get a big grin this holiday season.

You're welcome.

IGK Flight Club Dry Shampoo Travel Set

The first rule of Flight Club . . . is that you definitely share it with your friends. Gift frequent fliers, or infrequent shampooers, the IGK Flight Club Dry Shampoo Travel Set ($28) this season. The lightest option (Jet Lag) has finely milled volcanic ash to refresh a scalp; the style-extending Direct Flight uses turmeric to gently cleanse; and the most powerful — called First Class, naturally — uses charcoal powder for that deep detox.

Peter Thomas Roth Mix, Mask & Hydrate

The five little masks and one moisturizer in the Peter Thomas Roth Mix, Mask & Hydrate ($75) kit range from a soothing cucumber one for the eyes to one infused with actual gold for a glow boost. There's something for everyone, or perhaps everything for someone? Merry masking!

Kiehl's Smooth Skin Delights

Even though it may seem like Winter weather is public enemy No. 1, this trio of Kiehl's Smooth Skin Delights ($35) comes to the rescue with moisturizing shea butter and avocado oil. Take that, dry skin.

Caudalie Vinosource Face Cracker

When your loved ones pop these holiday crackers open on Christmas morning, their faces are the ones really getting gifted: there's a mini serum, a mini moisturizer, and a mini micellar cleansing water in this Caudalie Vinosource Face Cracker ($16).

Benefit Cosmetics Gimme the Goods! Customizable Set

Did you ever see a set and think, "It could be so perfect if only that one shade actually matched my hair or skin color"? Well, that's not the case with the Benefit Cosmetics Gimme the Goods! Customizable Set ($29), as you can pick from eyebrow gel shades from blond to black when scooping up one of these kits. (Oh, and there's also a mini mascara, a mini bronzer, and a mini primer, too.)

Mario Badescu Facial Spray Travel Trio

Long flight? No problem. The Mario Badescu Facial Spray Travel Trio ($15) includes three airplane-friendly, aloe-vera-based face sprays for hydration on the go. Hey, maybe they can use it to make a new friend on the flight home?

Moroccanoil Beauty Vault

There are seven travel-size, argan-oil-infused goodies locked up in the Moroccanoil Beauty Vault ($45). Set them free by gifting the set of bestselling products to someone you love. There's a hand cream, a moisturizing soufflé, and a dry oil for their body, alongside three protecting products for their hair.

Fenty Beauty by Rihanna Mini Fairy Bomb

Giving someone the Fenty Beauty by Rihanna Mini Fairy Bomb ($10, originally $20) is like dusting someone with soft pink snow . . . but in the most the luminous way possible. Let it shine on the tree then glimmer on their body, as the puff is packed with iridescent shimmer.

La Mer Crème de la Mer Mini

Arguably one of the most iconic skincare brands, La Mer definitely makes many beauty-product bucket lists. It doesn't come cheap, but the delight will be huge if you pop a La Mer Crème de la Mer Mini ($90) jar under the tree for someone special this year.

Lancôme Mystery Makeup Mini Set

Let them pick a fave when trying two mini mascaras in the Lancôme Mystery Makeup Mini Set ($24). There's also eye makeup remover to wipe the slate clean and one of two primers as the surprise item. Mystery solved.

Milk Makeup MVPs Set

There's a team of Milk Makeup hard hitters in this Milk Makeup MVPs Set ($39). First up is a little version of the brand's popular Kush Mascara (fortified with cannabis seed oil). Rounding out the starting lineup — a stick of cooling water (yes, water in stick form) to depuff their undereyes and a dual-tasking glow oil for cheeks and lips, among other favorites.

Natasha Denona Mini Gold Eyeshadow Palette

The golds and greens in the Natasha Denona Mini Gold Eyeshadow Palette ($25) evoke a decorated Christmas tree, but this set is not just for show. With five high-pigment colors in both matte and shimmer, it's a great little set to add to the (holiday gift) mix.

Herbivore Jewel Box Mini Facial Oil + Serum Set

So, they've tried face oils, and they're hooked. Well, the limited-edition Herbivore Jewel Box Mini Facial Oil + Serum Set ($58) has five more to add to their collection, including the popular Prism one (with AHAs and BHAs) and the Emerald option with hemp seed oil.

Glow Recipe Watermelon + AHA Glow Sleeping Mask Mini

If you count yourself among the many, many fans of Glow Recipe's first, most iconic mask, introduce it to someone you love with the smaller version, the Glow Recipe Watermelon + AHA Glow Sleeping Mask Mini ($22). Its watermelon extract hydrates and soothes irritation while they're sleeping.

Drunk Elephant T.L.C. Sukari Babyfacial Mask Mini

It's like a facial in a bottle, because very few people get to go to the spa every week. The Drunk Elephant T.L.C. Sukari Babyfacial Mask Mini ($28) can be used that often, and before they know it, they have dewy skin . . . without needing to change out of pajamas. Oh, and there's a bonus marula facial oil to protect against environmental stresses when they do need to leave the house.

Clinique's Best-Selling Minis

As the name hints, Clinique's Best-Selling Minis ($25) include five of the brand's most popular products. Let a loved one sample an exfoliating cleanser, an eye cream, a mask, a moisturizer, and a makeup remover — it's like a full beauty routine in one gift box.

Mount Lai Amethyst Roller Mini

The Mount Lai Amethyst Roller Mini ($30) doesn't just look pretty. Fans of face rollers sing its praises for everything from relieving tension to promoting lymphatic drainage, so why not add a pretty purple one to the mix?

Fresh Minty Magic Gift Set

Don't let chapped lips bah humbug the holidays — offer up the Fresh Minty Magic Gift Set ($19) to make that a thing of Christmas past. It comes with a clear advanced-therapy lip treatment and a matching mint one.

Tweezerman Mini Nail Rescue Kit

Sudden snaggy nail or cracked cuticle? Friends don't let friends have a nail emergency. Gift the Tweezerman Mini Nail Rescue Kit ($22) that's small enough to go along with them and comes with the right tools to get their nails sorted.

Briogeo Next Level Hair Dry Hair Duo

The Briogeo Next Level Hair Dry Hair Duo ($16) comes with a carry-on-size tube of moisturizing shampoo with mineral- and antioxidant-rich algae extract, and a hydrating deep-conditioning mask as well.

Belif Ho-Ho-Holiday Kit

Five little minis packed into a waterproof pouch means that the Belif Ho-Ho-Holiday Kit ($23) is a stocking stuffer that can travel home with them after the holidays. Plus, the included Aqua Bomb Sleeping Mask can work wonders to battle dry airplane air before they pop it into the fridge back home.

Murad Mini De-Stress Set

If you know your friends are stressed but you don't think you can do anything about it, show your support with the Murad Mini De-Stress Set ($30). Yves Saint Laurent Mini Black Opium & Mon Paris Eau de Parfum Duo

Supergoop! Everyday Sunscreen For Face & Body Broad Spectrum SPF 30 PA++++ Mini

Everyday Sunscreen For Face & Body Broad Spectrum SPF 30 PA++++ Mini ($19), because you're just looking out for them and their skin.

Clinique Almost Lipstick Mini Duo: For You For Me

Jack Black Major Minis

The Jack Black Major Minis ($56) are a major deal, as they can pretty much form their own toiletry kit. There's cleanser for their body and face, alongside deodorant, moisturizer, serum, and lip balm.

Sephora Favorites Iconic Hair
