1 dead, 2 wounded in Florida Thanksgiving strip club shooting

29 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Associated Press

WEST PALM BEACH, Fla. (AP) — A Thanksgiving Day fight at a Florida strip club left one man dead and two others injured.

Palm Beach County Sheriff’s deputies found three men with gunshot wounds when they arrived at The Rose Gentlemen’s Club after 3 a.m. Once was pronounced dead at the scene and two others were taken to the hospital.

The Palm Beach Post reports the infamous strip club has had several shootings in the past.

A fight in 2016 ended with a 20-year-old fatally shot in the club’s parking lot. Another shooting in 2013 injured two men. And a club manager opened fire on two robbers in 2005.
