Steven Gerrard: Liverpool legend backs 'faultless' Van Dijk for Ballon d'Or

29 Nov 2019, 12:54

- Steven Gerrard believes Virgil van Dijk deserves to win 2019 Ballon d'Or award

- The Liverpool legend says he is convinced the Dutchman star had a remarkable 2019 above others

- Van Dijk will face stiff competition from Juventus star Cristiano Ronaldo and Argentine forward Lionel Messi

Steven Gerrard has tipped Liverpool center back, Virgil van Dijk, for the Ballon d'Or 2019 award glory ahead of Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo.

The Dutch star faces stiff competition from the Juventus forward and Barcelona star for this year's edition of the award slated for December.

Van Dijk failed to win Premier League but enjoyed a prosperous season under Jurgen Klopp as he was an integral part of the indomitable Liverpool squad that won Champions League.
