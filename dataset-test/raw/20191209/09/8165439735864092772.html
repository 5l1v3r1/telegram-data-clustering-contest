Natalie Nunn's Husband Moves Out Following Reports She Had A Threesome With Dan Osborne

09 Dec 2019, 09:12 by Tara Walsh

Natalie Nunn's husband moves out

Chloe Ayling's admission to having a threesome with Natalie Nunn and Dan Osborne has caused a lot of problems.

It's been reported that Natalie's husband Jacob Payne has moved out of their family home.

'He has gone to stay with friends and says he needs some time alone.'

Celebrity Big Brother star Chloe Ayling revealed to The Sun last week that she had a threesome with Natalie and Dan last year.

While Chloe was very much single at the time, Natalie and Dan were in committed relationships.

The American reality star has been married to Jacob Payne for seven years.

In pictures obtained by MailOnline, Jacob is seen placing cardboard boxes into a moving van.

A source tells The Sun, 'Jacob was so angry when the story came out last night.'

'He has gone to stay with friends and says he needs some time alone.'

The insider continues, 'It's not looking good and the scandal has broken up their marriage.'

'Natalie is devastated and cannot believe this is happening.'

Natalie pays tribute

The reality star takes to Instagram to pay tribute to Jacob shortly after Chloe makes her admission.

She writes, 'You’re my bestfriend! My husband the father of my child!'

'No matter what in this world you sit on the throne you’re my [king]!'

'This world is full of craziness and you’ve always been my peace!'

'This storm too shall pass but in the meantime I pray we continue to just dance in the rain together !'

'I love you'

Natalie's close friend Heidi Montag also takes to Twitter to address the reports.

'Spencer [Pratt] and I are very close to the Payne’s and praying everything is ok with them and this storm will pass.'

'They are one of our favorite couples and have the most beautiful love and family.'
