Watch Video: Rajkummar Rao grooves like Leonardo DiCaprio; leaves fans impressed with his moves

09 Dec 2019, 09:18 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Rajkummar Rao has crafted a niche for himself with giving back to back stellar performances on the celluloid which has been loved by the critics as well as the audience. The versatile actor, who is hailed as one of the best performers of today's generation took to social media recently to share a fan-made video of comparing his dance moves with Hollywood stalwart Leonardo DiCaprio.

The 'Made in China' actor, who is gearing up for his next release took to Twitter and shared a funny video where his dance moves from Anurag Kashyap's film 'Gangs of Wasseypur – Part 2' is compared with DiCaprio's moves from highly acclaimed 'The Wolf of Wall Street'. Terming the video as '#TheWolfofWasseypur', Rajkummar has got his fans excited and left them impressed with his dance.
