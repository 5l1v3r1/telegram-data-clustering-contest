ROYAL DRAMA

I’m A Celeb winner Jacqueline Jossa and Dan Osborne kiss before she reunites with her children at Versace hotel

09 Dec 2019, 09:09 by Tom Capon

I'M A Celebrity winner Jacqueline Jossa and husband Dan Osborne kissed outside the Versace hotel before being whisked away for crisis talks.

The former EastEnders actress, 27, beat Corrie star Andy Whyment to be crowned Queen of the Jungle on Sunday night.

Jac and Dan, 28, put on a united front as they shared a kiss, after The Sun revealed the former Towie star cheated on his wife last year in a wild threesome with Chloe Ayling, 22, and Natalie Nunn, 34.

He led his wife into the hotel, where their daughters, Ella, four, and Mia, one, waited for their mum in matching dresses.

The family were all smiles as she picked them both up for a hug.

Her mum and dad - Sarah and John Jossa - were waiting for the actress in the hotel, alongside her I'm A Celeb campmates.

Despite the loving display, a show insider told how the troubled couple headed straight for crisis talks to discuss the cheating allegations.

"It feels like Jacqueline has grown and is stronger than when she went in there," a source told The Mirror.

"She loves Dan but they have split up several times before so it would not be a huge surprise if they split up again in the future, despite Dan denying all the allegations and insisting he is not worried about the situation. Time will tell."

This will be the first time the family are reunited since fellow campmate Myles Stephenson told Jac Dan had cheated on her with Love Island's Gabby Allen.

The revelation caused Jacqueline, who married Dan in 2017, to remove her wedding ring and threaten to quit.

Just days before Jac left the jungle, Chloe Ayling told The Sun how her, Dan and Natalie had a steamy group sex session after the end of last year's Celebrity Big Brother following a boozy night out in London.

Chloe said: “Dan cheated on Jacqueline with me and Natalie. We’d all had a lot to drink and a threesome happened.

“He had full sex with Natalie in in front of me in the bathroom, and then kissed and performed a sex act on me.

“It was a debauched night – we were drunk and we let loose."

ITV bosses dramatically axed an early reunion on I'm A Celeb between Dan and Jac amid fears she would break down in tears.

A source told The Sun: "The visit would have been really awkward for her.

"Bosses couldn’t give her advance notice as it would ruin the surprise, which is the whole point of the reunion segment.

"But if they didn’t give her a heads-up and she hated that Dan had been sent in it could’ve really upset her.

"ITV are all about duty of care. They decided that Dan and Jacqueline’s first proper conversation since the dramas emerged should be off- camera and not in front of millions of viewers."

Meanwhile, Jac's parents stayed far away from Dan on the I'm A Celebrity bus to the finale last night.

They shared a photo surrounded by other celebrities and their families travelling to the camp ahead of the crowning.

Selina captioned the picture: "On our way to get you @jacjossa [you're] our winner no matter what. Good luck everyone."

But eagle-eyed fans spotted they had chosen to sit four rows away from their son-in-law.
