Exclusive Star Wars Rise of Skywalker clip to air inside Fortnite

09 Dec 2019, 09:21 by Steve Wright

Are you ready to brave an early morning (and Fortnite) for a look at Rise of Skywalker?

An exclusive Star Wars Rise of Skywalker clip will be shown inside a virtual theatre in Fortnite, it was recently confirmed.

The exclusive scene will be available inside Fortnite from 15 December, days ahead of the movie’s launch in theatres worldwide. Already, in-game advertising screens are heralding the debut event, which is set to kick off at 5.30 am AEDT on 15 December ahead of the actual screening at 6.00 am AEDT.

The screening will take place at the Risky Reels in-game drive-in, located in the northeast corner of Fortnite’s battle royale map.

Star Wars Rise of Skywalker heads to Australian theatres on 19 December. Fortnite is available on Windows PC, Xbox One, PS4, Switch, iOS and Android devices.
