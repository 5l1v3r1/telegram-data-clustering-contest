Netflix Announces 'Chilling Adventures of Sabrina' Season 3 Release Date

“Welcome to Hell.”

09 Dec 2019, 09:51 by Mallory Chin

Although Part 2 of Chilling Adventures of Sabrina only aired earlier this year in April, Netflix is gearing up to release a new batch of episodes from the hit series.

Warning: This post contains spoilers.

In the super short teaser trailer shared by the streaming giant over the weekend, Sabrina Spellman (portrayed by Kiernan Shipka) is seen wearing a red hooded robe while walking through the fog. “Welcome to Hell,” a witchy narrator voice whispers in the trailer. The clip then reveals the upcoming season’s release date of January 24, 2020.

According to the show synopsis, even though Sabrina defeated her father Lucifer Morningstar/The Dark Lord (Luke Cook) last season, he remains trapped within the human body of her boyfriend, Nick Scratch (Gavin Leatherwood). Nick is suffering, burning in hell under Madam Satan’s (Michelle Gomez) supervision. Sabrina and her mortal friends must then ban together to help save Nick from eternal damnation.

The show’s inaugural season debuted in October of 2018, followed by a Christmas special in December of the same year. Season 3 of Chilling Adventures of Sabrina will contain around eight episodes.
