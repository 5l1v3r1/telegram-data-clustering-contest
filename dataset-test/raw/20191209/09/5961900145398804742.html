NZ Police Confirm 5 Dead In Volcanic Eruption With “At Least A Double Digit” Still Missing

09 Dec 2019, 09:25 by By Lavender Baj

Five people have been confirmed dead as a result of a volcanic eruption on New Zealand’s Whakaari/White Island on Monday afternoon, The Guardian reports. This number is expected to rise in the coming hours as police and rescue workers further investigate the scene.

Whakaari Island, which is known as New Zealand’s “most active volcano” erupted at around 2.15pm local time on Monday.
