Caitlyn Jenner celebrates 'sweet friend' Jacqueline Jossa's I'm A Celebrity win

09 Dec 2019, 09:24 by Cydney Yeates

Caitlyn Jenner was seriously stoked that her ‘sweet friend’ Jacqueline Jossa was crowned the winner of I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! last night.

Jacqueline was named Queen of the Jungle, beating the likes of Coronation Street’s Andy Whyment and Roman Kemp in the nail-biting finale, and the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star celebrated her fellow campmate’s victory in the most lovely way.

Showing her love for the former EastEnders actress, Caitlyn shared a selfie of the pair together and posted it to her 9.8million Instagram followers.

Captioning the pic, she wrote: ‘Congratulations to my sweet friend @jacjossa witnessing your growth on this show was amazing! Congrats Jungle Jack!’

We bet Jacqueline will be made up by the heartwarming post.

Caitlyn and Jacqueline became good friends while living it up in the I’m A Celebrity jungle.

Early on in the series, Caitlyn compared the soap star to her daughter Kylie Jenner after she’d smashed her Bushtucker Trial.

Giving her encouragement, especially after she got 12 stars for the camp, Caitlyn told Jacqueline: ‘After you came back and you were all happy and that good feeling you should be and you reminded me of Kylie.

‘Enthusiastic, like wow, here we go. We’re doing this, wow that was fun, you know. It’s very much that way.’

Naturally, Jacqueline was bowled over by Caitlyn’s remark and excitedly exclaimed in the Bush Telegraph: ‘How cool is that?!

‘Caitlyn Jenner just casually said, “oh you remind me of Kylie.” Winning. Winning! She always notices when I’m down, she notices when I need picking up, and she’s just really, really an amazing person.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! is available for catch-up on the ITV Hub.
