Juice Wrld dead: Tonto Dikeh reacts – See how he predicted his own death

09 Dec 2019, 09:57 by Sanya Agunbiade

Actress Tonto Dikeh, while reacting to US rapper Juice Wrld death, has emphasized on speaking the right words into one’s life.

The rapper reportedly died yesterday, just some few days after he celebrated his 21st birthday. He reportedly suffered a seizure on his way to the airport.

It was so unfortunate that the rapper mentioned in one of his lyrics that he might not make it beyond the age of 21

Reacting to his death, Tonto Dikeh shared:

“He said he would die at 21..
Yesterday he died at 21..

I can’t OVER emphasis how important it is to speak of your self positively wether it be thru songs, poems, write up or straight Talk..

If you don’t learn this for your own good, Atleast do so for the ones who love and cherish you!!
GREATEST WEAPON GOD GAVE MAN, “YOUR MOUTH”.
