Kangana Ranaut’s Sister Blasts Alia Bhatt For Taking Her Star Screen Awards Before The Award, Hints At Fixing The Same

The actress is all smiles in the video while this conversation is happening.

09 Dec 2019, 09:28 by Koimoi.com Team

The Star Screen Awards 2019 function was held in Mumbai last night. A lot of stars attended the award show, like Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh, Taapsee Pannu, Ayushmann Khurrana, Sara Ali Khan, Shahid Kapoor and many others. Alia Bhatt had to skip the awards night due to prior commitments.

While Bhatt didn’t attend the function, she made sure to collect her trophy before the main event. The Raazi actress was papped leaving with her award which she won for her performance in Gully Boy. In a video that’s gone viral on the internet, Alia Bhatt’s manager asks if they are planning to share the pics now, to which a cameraman said, “No, no, after an hour.”

Her manager further tells the cameraperson to share the photos post 8 pm to which he agrees. The actress is all smiles in the video while this conversation is happening. Now, Kangana Ranaut’s sister Rangoli Chandel has bashed Alia for the same.

Rangoli took to her Twitter page and wrote, “Chalo itni honesty toh hai ki yeh kaam chup ke kar rahi ho, sabke samne nahin, achcha laga kuch toh bacha hai andar abhi bhi jo rok raha hai”.
