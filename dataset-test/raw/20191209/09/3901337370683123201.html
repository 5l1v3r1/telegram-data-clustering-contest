Where Does Middendorp Rank Among Top Coaches In The World?

09 Dec 2019, 09:06 by Soccer Laduma

With Pitso Mosimane ranked in the top 100 coaches in the world, Soccer Laduma takes a look at where his counterpart, Ernst Middendorp is positioned.

According to clubworldranking.com, Mosimane has been ranked in 70th position, amongst several well-known European coaches in the world, even ranked above the likes of Steven Gerrard and Roy Hodgson.

Read more: Pitso Above Gerrard On Coaches Ranking

But where does clubworldranking.com place Head Coach Middendorp?

The Kaizer Chiefs tactician is ranked at 241, with Gavin Hunt also following in 281st place and Kaitano Tembo at 287.

Here are the rankings of some of the other Absa Premiership (and former) coaches:

Steve Komphela – 301st

Owen da Gama – 315th

Benni McCarthy – 342nd

Eric Tinkler – 368th

Jozef Vukusic – 380th
