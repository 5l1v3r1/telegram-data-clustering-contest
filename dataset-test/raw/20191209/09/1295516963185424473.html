Stargirl Teaser Reveals ANOTHER New Logo Ahead of DC Universe Show's Launch

09 Dec 2019, 09:12 by Noah Dominguez

DC Universe has released a (very) short teaser trailer for Stargirl -- and with it, the latest iteration of the upcoming superhero series' title logo.

The teaser -- which appears to be a shortened version of the now-removed trailer previously shared by DC -- boasts the series' connection to prolific creator Geoff Johns and promises to introduce "a new generation of superheroes." Shortly thereafter, the clip displays the new Stargirl logo, which depicts the show's title written out in sheeny blue, red and yellow letters. The teaser ends with a brief shot of Brec Bassinger's Stargirl wielding the Cosmic Staff.
