Maruti Suzuki Baleno, Ciaz, Ignis, S-Cross: Offers up to Rs 1.13 lakh in December 2019

Maruti Suzuki India is offering benefits up to Rs 1.13 lakh on Baleno, Ciaz, Ignis and S-Cross.

09 Dec 2019, 09:06 by Varun Singh

HIGHLIGHTS

  • The offers on Baleno, Ciaz, Ignis and S-Cross are valid till December 31, 2019.
  • Baleno, Ciaz, Ignis and S-Cross are sold through the Nexa channel of Maruti Suzuki India.
  • The benefits on Baleno, Ciaz, Ignis and S-Cross may vary from variant to variant.

The year 2019 has been quite difficult for the Indian automobile sector with most of the top manufacturers witnessing a decline in sales month-after-month. Now that we are in December, it is natural that the major carmakers will come out with hefty discounts to add to their sales. The country's largest carmaker, Maruti Suzuki India, is providing massive benefits this month on the products it sells through the Nexa dealerships.

Among the vehicles sold through the Nexa channel are Maruti Suzuki Baleno, Maruti Suzuki Ciaz, Maruti Suzuki Ignis and Maruti Suzuki S-Cross. Maruti Suzuki India is furnishing benefits up to Rs 1.13 lakh on these models. The offers are valid until December 31. Below are all the relevant details.

Maruti Suzuki Baleno (Petrol)

The petrol variant of Maruti Suzuki Baleno has total benefits up to Rs 45,000. There is a consumer discount of Rs 25,000, exchange offer of Rs 15,000 and corporate offer of Rs 5,000.

Maruti Suzuki Baleno (Diesel)

You will save as much as Rs 67,400 on the diesel trim of Maruti Suzuki Baleno. While the consumer discount is Rs 25,000, the exchange and corporate offers are Rs 15,000 and Rs 10,000, respectively. The carmaker is providing a 5-year warranty free of cost.

Maruti Suzuki Ciaz (Petrol)

The petrol variant of Maruti Suzuki Ciaz has total benefits of Rs 75,000, with the consumer discount being Rs 25,000, exchange offer Rs 40,000 and corporate offer Rs 10,000.

Maruti Suzuki Ciaz (Diesel)

One can avail benefits up to Rs 1.13 lakh on the diesel trim of Maruti Suzuki Ciaz. The vehicle has a consumer discount of Rs 40,000, exchange offer of Rs 40,000 and corporate offer of Rs 10,000. The buyer will also get a 5-year warranty without paying any extra amount.

Maruti Suzuki Ignis (Petrol)

Maruti Suzuki Ignis is only available in a petrol trim and you can save as much as Rs 65,000 on it. There is a consumer discount of Rs 30,000 on the SUV-styled hatchback. The exchange offer is Rs 25,000, while the corporate offer is Rs 10,000.

Maruti Suzuki S-Cross (Diesel)

Maruti Suzuki S-Cross comes in a diesel variant only. You can save as much as Rs 1.13 lakh on it. The vehicle has a consumer discount of Rs 50,000, exchange offer of Rs 30,000 and Rs 10,000. It also comes with a free of cost 5-year warranty.

According to Maruti Suzuki India, the offers may vary from variant to variant. Also, there is an additional discount of Rs 10,000 on S-Cross in Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Telangana and Odisha.
