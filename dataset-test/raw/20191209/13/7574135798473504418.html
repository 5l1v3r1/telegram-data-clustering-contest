Rory Scovel & more going on “The Comedians Following Tool on Tour: The Tour”

09 Dec 2019, 13:06 by Bill Pearis

Comedian Rory Scovel is a massive Tool fan and he and fellow obsessives and comedians Freddy Scott and Nick Youssef have come up with a way to see a bunch of shows on the Fear Inoculum tour and do shows along the way. They’re calling it “the Comedians Following Tool on Tour: The Tour” and in January they’ll perform either the night before or after Tool on their Fear Inoculum tour in San Diego, Fresno, Phoenix, Houston, Austin, Dallas, Atlanta and Memphis.

All dates are listed below and tickets are here. Tool’s tour hit Brooklyn back in November.
