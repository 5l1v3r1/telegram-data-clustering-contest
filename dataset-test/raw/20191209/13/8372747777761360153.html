Cape Coral to discuss noise ordinance again

09 Dec 2019, 13:39 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- City council members are expected to vote on a noise ordinance Monday night in Cape Coral.

There is also a public hearing scheduled.

Back in July, the discussion was tabled because the members wanted more public input.

The ordinance calls for a $150 fine for loud noises within 50 feet of your car and 100 feet of your home.

The meeting is scheduled for 4:30pm in the council chambers at 1015 Cultural Park Boulevard.
