Unai Emery: Everton reportedly approach Spaniard for manager job

09 Dec 2019, 13:43

- Unai Emery has been linked with a move to become new Everton manager

- He was recently sacked at Premier League side Arsenal after poor performances

- Everton are looking for an experienced manager who can replace Marco Silva

Premier League side Everton have reportedly approached Spanish football coach Unai Emery about the possibility of him becoming their next manager.

This latest approach comes ten days after the former Paris-Saint-Germain handler was sacked at Arsenal due to unimpressive performance this season in all competitions.

According to the report on Sky Sports, an informal meeting has been held in London, but Unai Emery explained that he needs more time for him to think about his next move.

Everton are currently looking for another manager after recently sacking Marco Silva and they are currently occupying 14th position on the Premier League standings.
