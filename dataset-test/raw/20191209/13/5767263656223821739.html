Bruce Willis and Demi Moore’s 3 Daughters Grew Up to Become the Spitting Images of Their Parents

09 Dec 2019, 13:30

Bruce Willis and Demi Moore were 2 of the world’s brightest Hollywood actors in the 1980s and 1990s. Willis, a role model of masculinity, and Moore, sex symbol of several generations, were once a beautiful couple, today they are still good friends who are raising 3 beautiful daughters together — Rumer, Scout, and Tallulah. And they all look exactly like Demi and Bruce combined!

Here at Bright Side we were so blown away by their resemblance, that we decided to find out a little more about their amazing family.

Demi and Bruce got married in 1987. Willis was 32 and it was his first marriage, and the 25-year-old Moore already had one divorce behind her. The age difference didn`t stop them from living happily together for 13 years — during this time the careers of both actors rose up to the international level.

Both actors often took their daughters with them to film sets, and even allowed them to have small roles in movies. Thus the elder Rumer appeared in the films Now and Then (1995) and the legendary Striptease (1996), when she was 8 years old. Scout and Tallulah first appeared in The Scarlet Letter (1995) at the ages of 4 and 1.

Despite the fact that all 3 daughters were actively engaged in acting, the couple decided to raise their children in the small city of Hailey, Idaho, away from Hollywood — so the girls could have a more or less normal childhood, surrounded by the stunning nature of Idaho.

Each of their 3 daughters, to some extent, was always in the public eye while they were growing up. And fans of the Willis-Moore family noticed that each kid is very similar to both parents, but their appearances are still very different from each other.

All 3 girls are grown up now and no longer live with their parents, nevertheless they love to post throwbacks and share memories about their childhood.

Despite their divorce in 2000, that was very peaceful, the whole family still appears together on the red carpet, and also spends weekends and holidays as a whole, according to the daughters` Instagram posts.

This big family often hits tabloids with their sweet photos together. And we want to tell you a little bit about the daughters separately:

Rumer, 31, is the oldest daughter of Bruce and Demi. Now she’s a famous American actress, having dozens of movies and musicals under her belt, including a part in the latest Quentin Tarantino film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood and the main role in the Chicago musical on Broadway.

Scout, 27, has also participated in several film projects, but decided to not follow in her parents footsteps. Nowadays she’s actively involved in social activities, singing, and fashion. This young lady has grown up and turned into a beautiful and glowing copy of her gorgeous parents — many note her striking resemblance to her father and we couldn’t agree more.

Tallulah, 25, is the youngest of the Willis-Moore daughters. She’s a modern illustrator. In 2016 she launched her own clothing label Buuski, named after her childhood nickname. It’s based on shirts and sweaters featuring her own illustrations.

Bruce and his second wife, Emma Heming, have 2 more daughters — Mabel (7) and Evelyn (5). All 5 daughters get along well and spend a lot of time together. Rumer’s recent Instagram post is proof of this. She wrote, “The 4 most important beings on this planet. I love you all with every fiber of my being and will be there to protect you day or night forever. Your Big Sister.”

In September 2019, Demi Moore released her memoir Inside Out, in which she admitted that she was far from a perfect mother and wife, and there was a time when she did not speak to her ex-husband and even her daughters. In the book, she talks about how she managed to build relationships with her family anew. So today the Willis-Moore family bond seems to be stronger than before.

The sisters often publish photos of them spending time together, and we definitely can see that all 3 beauties inherited their mother’s slim figure.

It’s always such a pleasure to see a big and loving family, especially when the kids are spitting images of their parents. Bruce Willis and Demi Moore must be proud of raising such amazing, different, yet very alike daughters! Do you know any other children of celebrities that look just like their parents?
