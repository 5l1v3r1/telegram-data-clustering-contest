Anthony Joshua flaunts his adorable daughter after winning against Andy Ruiz

09 Dec 2019, 13:49

- Anthony Joshua flaunts his little daughter after the bout against Mexican boxer Andy Ruiz Jnr.

- After winning via unanimous decision, Joshua brought over his daughter to the boxing ring, carrying her on his shoulder while granting the interview

- Anthony Joshua has regained his titles from Ruiz who took them from him in a boxing bout six months ago

Heavyweight champion Anthony Joshua has shown his little daughter to the world after winning his match in Saudi Arabia.

In a video making waves on social media, the reigning holder of four title belts was seen showing off his daughter after beating Andy Ruiz Jnr.

Joshua was captured carrying his daughter on the shoulder while talking about his willingness to continue boxing and meet other boxers.

The little girl, on the other hand, held his father's shoulder as she starred at the parked auditorium with innocence.

"I took my L, and I bounced back, anyone could do it. Life is like a roller coaster, what do you want me to do, give up?" he quizzed.

"I hear a man say I should retire, I should retire, come on, I love this sports," he bragged, calling on Andy Ruiz to ask if he is willing to retire.

Andy Ruiz who defeated Joshua six months ago but lost on the night responded, saying "I won't retire! I want to see the third fight."

Patrons of the much-attended boxing match cheered the two boxers up with a loud noise, as the journalist asked them to talk about the future.
