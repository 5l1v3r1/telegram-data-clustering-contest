English football legend Harry Redknapp is coming to Dubai

Expect an evening of sporting anecdotes and plenty of laughs

09 Dec 2019, 13:54 by Reema Rahman

Football fan? Harry Redknapp needs no introduction then.

With a career spanning more than 50 years, the legendary footballer went on to manage teams such as A.F.C. Bournemouth, West Ham United, Portsmouth, Southampton, Tottenham Hotspur, Queens Park Rangers and Birmingham City.

In 2018, Redknapp won the popular British survival reality television game show, I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!, making him the king of the jungle, too.

The former English football player, manager and TV star is bringing his sell-out UK tour ‘An Audience with Harry Redknapp’ to Dubai alongside comedian Noel Brodie, who will be hosting the evening – it set to be an evening packed full of stories, jungle experiences and plenty of laughs.

The special evening is taking place on Friday January 31 at Mövenpick Hotel Jumeirah Beach in JBR and tickets are on sale now here.

Standard tickets cost Dhs295, and if you fancy a tipple you can also pair the evening with free-flowing drinks from 7pm until 10pm for Dhs595.

Super-fan? There’s also the VIP ticket package for Dhs1,200, which includes a private bar, VIP seating, a three-course exclusive dinner with Harry after the show with a question and answer session between courses and a meet-and-greet with a photographer.
