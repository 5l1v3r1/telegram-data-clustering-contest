Ruletheworld claims Salarjung Cup

Suraj Narredu becomes the first Indian jockey to notch up 2000 wins

09 Dec 2019, 13:18

Deshmukh-trained Ruletheworld (Suraj Narredu up) won the Salarjung Cup, the feature event of the races held here on Monday (Dec. 9). The winner is the property of Miss Ameeta Mehra, M/s. Mukul Amratlal Sonawala & Dilip Ramanlal Thacker.

By winning the Crown Treasure Plate, astride Guiding Force, Suraj Narredu became the first Indian jockey to ride 2000 winners.
