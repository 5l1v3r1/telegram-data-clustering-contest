Shoppers go wild for Lidl's melting dark chocolate and salted caramel cottage

Christmas treats don't come much better than this!

09 Dec 2019, 13:11 by Nancy Brown

Shoppers have been going wild for bargain supermarket chain Lidl's range of Christmas desserts.

And top of their shopping list is the Deluxe Dark Chocolate & Salted Caramel Cottage.

It costs just £6.99 and is set to provide a festive showstopper on Christmas dinner tables up and down the country.

And the Christmas dessert actually melts!

Designed in the shape of a cottage, the treat is filled with chocolate mousse and a salted caramel sauce.

This is absolutely 100 per cent needed!

It's covered in a gold lustre and topped with icing sugar snow.

But the best part is the chocolate pouring sauce that comes with it.

Simply drizzle the warmed sauce through the chimney and watch as the house melts.
