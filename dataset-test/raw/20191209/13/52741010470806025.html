Guelph Police investigating after man struck and killed by train

Police say it happened late Sunday night

09 Dec 2019, 13:26 by KitchenerToday Staff

Police in Guelph are investigating after a man was struck and killed by a train.

Police say it happened just before 10:30 p.m. on Sunday, when the man was hit by the train travelling eastbound on a set of tracks east of Victoria Road North near Suburban Avenue.

There's been no further information released.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Guelph Police.
