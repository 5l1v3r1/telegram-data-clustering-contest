Atlantis Attacks: Jeehyung Lee Variant Teases a Cold War (Exclusive)

09 Dec 2019, 13:12 by Jon Arvedon

In January, Marvel Comics will launch Altantis Attacks, a five-issue miniseries by Greg Pak and Ario Anindito to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the 1989 event of the same name. Now, the publisher has provided CBR with an exclusive first look at Jeehyung Lee's variant cover for Issue #1.

The cover features Namora facing off with Luna Snow, the fan-favorite South Korean K-pop sensation who debuted in the popular mobile role-playing game Marvel Future Fight in 2018.

Atlantis Attacks is a continuation of Pak's run on Agents of Atlas, which began during War of the Realms. The official synopsis for the miniseries is below:

In Atlantis Attacks, Amadeus Cho, Silk, Wave, Shang-Chi, and all the heroes of Atlas head for a showdown destined to shake allegiances and break hearts! When a terrible secret incurs the wrath of Prince Namor, questions arise over who is a friend... and who is an enemy? Jimmy Woo also makes his shocking return -- as well as the original Agents of Atlas!

Atlantis Attacks #1, by Greg Pak and Ario Anindito, goes on sale Jan. 22, 2020 from Marvel Comics.
