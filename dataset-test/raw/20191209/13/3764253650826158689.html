Singer and dad of 3, Davido, seen feeding his newborn baby Ifeanyi (video)

09 Dec 2019, 13:40

- Popular singer Davido has been spotted online feeding his son Ifeanyi

- The singer was seen trying to type on his phone and feed his son at the same time

- Davido also shared cute videos of himself, his son and his beautiful wife-to-be

Nigerian singer David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, has been spotted in a video shared on social media feeding his newborn son, Ifeanyi David Adeleke Junior.

The singer, who is a father to three beautiful children, was seen feeding his third child and first son in the cute video.

In the video, the singer was seen bottle-feeding his son while also trying to type on his phone. Davido, who was filmed by a friend, started to smile immediately he realised that he was being filmed.
