Cape Coral Police make three DUI arrests in weekend traffic operations

09 Dec 2019, 13:13 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Police held high-visibility traffic operations over the weekend and made three DUI arrests.

According to Cape Cora Police, officers made a total of 17 total traffic stops.

Among the results were:

3 DUI arrests
11 Warnings
2 Speeding citations
1 Non-moving citation
1 Criminal citation
