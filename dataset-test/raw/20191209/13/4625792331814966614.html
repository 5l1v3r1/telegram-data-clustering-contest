Sacramento State student appears to slap former College Republicans' president following Facebook argument

09 Dec 2019, 13:17 by Dominick Mastrangelo

Video of a student assaulting the former president of the Sacramento State College Republicans has gone viral.

The confrontation between Floyd Johnson II and his attacker, Keaton Hill, began after an argument on a mutual friend's anti-abortion Facebook post, according to the Sacramento Bee.

Hill confronted Johnson, a fellow political science student, about the online argument in person before apparently slapping him in the face at least once, the video shows.
