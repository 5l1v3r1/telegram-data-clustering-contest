All the Liverpool polling stations for the 2019 General Election

Everywhere you can vote in Liverpool on Thursday

09 Dec 2019, 13:53 by Christopher McKeon

Liverpool heads to the polls on December 12 to elect the city's five MPs in the 2019 General Election .

On the day itself, anyone without a postal vote will need to go to a polling station to cast their ballot in person.

Each polling station will be open from 7am until 10pm, and most are located in public buildings like schools or church halls.

If you have already registered, you should have been sent a polling card in the post telling you where to cast your vote.

Below is a list of each constituency's polling stations. But bear in mind, your nearest polling station may not be the one you need to vote at.
