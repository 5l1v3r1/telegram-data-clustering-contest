Aldi is selling Prosecco for just £3.99 a bottle

Rude not to...

09 Dec 2019, 13:21 by James Rodger, Max Channon

Christmas has just come early at Aldi - the budget supermarket has slashed the price of its Prosecco to just £3.99 a bottle.

Customers at the German budget supermarket, which has stores all over Devon and Cornwall, can pick up a bottle of Aldi's Prosecco for just £3.99 right now.

The supermarket cut the price of its festive fizz on Friday, December 6, so customers can now snap up a bottle of Costellore Prosecco Frizzante DOC  at a  bargain price, reports Birmingham Live.

It contains a 10.5% alcohol by volume (ABV) and comes in a 75cl bottle - which provides around six servings.

Whilst the Prosecco is currently sold out online, Aldi has more than 830 stores in the UK where you could potentially buy one - but we'd advise calling up your nearest store to check if it's available before making a trip.

The offer won't be around for long, though, so you'll need to be quick if you want to get your hands on a bottle in time for Christmas and New Year.
