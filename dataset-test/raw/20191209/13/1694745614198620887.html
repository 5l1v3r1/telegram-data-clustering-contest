Here Are All the 2020 Golden Globe Nominees!

09 Dec 2019, 13:45 by Mekishana Pierre

Award season is just kicking into high gear, and what better way to celebrate our favorites than with the Golden Globe nominations? On the morning of Dec. 9, the Golden Globes announced which films and TV series would be up for awards in 2020 and we have a feeling fans will be happy to see some of their favorites this time around. Check out the nominees below, and find out who wins during the 77th annual ceremony, which will be hosted by Ricky Gervais for the fifth time on Jan. 5.
