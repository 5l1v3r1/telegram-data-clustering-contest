'The Irishman' leads film nominees for Critics' Choice awards

09 Dec 2019, 13:48

Cape Town – The Irishman by Martin Scorsese leads the field of films with 14 nominations for the 25th annual Critics' Choice Awards.

Quentin Tarantino's Once Upon a Time in Hollywood was close behind with 12 nominations, followed by Little Women with nine, 1917 and Marriage Story with eight and Jojo Rabbit, Joker and Parasite with seven.

On the television front Netflix's When They See Us leads with six nominations followed by This is Us and Schitt's Creek with five nominations.

The awards ceremony will be hosted by Taye Diggs and will air on the CW Network on Sunday, 12 January 2020.
