'Irishman,' 'Dolemite,' 'Morning Show' score top Golden Globe nominations

09 Dec 2019, 13:59 by Karen Butler

Dec. 9 (UPI) -- The Irishman, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Dolemite is My Name and The Morning Show emerged as top contenders when Golden Globe nominations were announced in Los Angeles on Monday.

Tim Allen, Dakota Fanning and Susan Kelechi Watson read aloud the names of the nominees in the film and television categories.

Winners of the awards are to be announced at a Jan. 5 gala hosted by comedian Ricky Gervais. The ceremony will air on NBC.
