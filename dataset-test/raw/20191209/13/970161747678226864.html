Disgusting moment creepy marathon runner gropes female TV reporter live on air

09 Dec 2019, 13:29 by Jimmy McCloskey

A TV reporter has hit out at a creepy marathon runner who left her lost for words after groping her live on air.

Alexandrea Bozarjian was assaulted while covering a race in Savannah, Georgia, on Saturday morning for local TV station WSAV.

A man in sunglasses could be seen smirking as he violated the young journalist, who was stunned into momentary silence by the incident.

Bozarjian has since taken to Twitter to hit out at her attacker, saying: ‘To the man who smacked my butt on live TV this morning: You violated, objectified, and embarrassed me.

‘No woman should EVER have to put up with this at work or anywhere!! Do better.’
