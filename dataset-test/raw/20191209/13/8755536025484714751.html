Here is a FULL list of all the 2020 Golden Globe nominations

09 Dec 2019, 13:53 by Clodagh Meaney

Today the Hollywood Foreign Press Association announced the nominations for the 77th Annual Golden Globe Awards, taking place on January 5th 2020.

From film to TV, some of the biggest names in the Hollywood business have been honoured with a nomination.
