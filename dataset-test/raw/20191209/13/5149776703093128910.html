How to create a slap-up family Christmas dinner for less than £50

It includes Bucks Fizz and Christmas cake

09 Dec 2019, 13:33 by James Andrews

Christmas dinner is probably one of the most loved meals of the year.

But when you add up the cost of the meat, all the trimmings and a sweet treat, it can also be an extremely expensive meal.

So financial app Wagestream went on a hunt for all the Christmas dinner essentials and where you can buy them cheapest.  

They found a way to cook the meal for four people for less than £50, MirrorOnline reports.

Wagestream chief executive Peter Briffett said: "Christmas can be hugely stressful for many households, as there is massive pressure to deliver the perfect day for all the family.

"We thought it would be nice to do some research to show that you don’t have to spend a fortune to have a great Christmas — and you don’t need to scrimp on the simple pleasures either."

Christmas dinner comes with all the trimmings - from sprouts and carrots to Chipolatas and parsnips.

In fact, the most expensive bit of the whole meal is the turkey, which costs £9.99 from Lidl.
