Jacqueline Jossa breaks silence in video with wedding ring back on as she swerves party

I'm a Celeb winner Jacqueline Jossa has finally spoken out on social media while wearing her wedding ring again after her marriage was rocked by several cheating claims

09 Dec 2019, 13:22 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm a Celeb winner Jacqueline Jossa has finally broken her silence to update her fans on how she is doing - and her wedding ring is firmly back on.

The newly-crowned Queen of the Jungle is still in Australia but swerved the afterparty for the show - leaving early after saying a quick hello to her campmates.

Instead, she opted to spend time with her daughters, Ella, four, and Mia, one, and her husband Dan Osborne .

Dan was hit by several cheating claims while Jacqueline was in the jungle.

One came from her campmate Myles Stephenson, who told her he believed Dan had cheated with Myles' ex Gabby Allen .

Shortly after that, model Chloe Ayling who starred on CelebrityBig Brother with him and Natalie Nunn , claimed they had a threesome after a boozy night out.

Dan has denied both claims, and Gabby has also denied anything happened between them.

But Jacqueline put on a brave face as she cuddled her daughters and filmed them playing with Dan.

She thanked her fans for their support, and insisted she was doing well despite the drama.

Beaming to the camera, she said she couldn't believe she had won.

"I just want you all to know that I am absolutely fine.

It has been the best experience of my whole entire life."

She also filmed Dan picking up a delighted Ella and high-fiving her as they hung out together in a hotel.

Jacqueline was crowned last night after a public vote.

Corrie star Andy Whyment came second, while radio presenter Roman Kemp came in third.
