Eamonn Holmes impresses fans with 'stunning' portrait for his 60th birthday

The likeness is uncanny

09 Dec 2019, 13:39 by Richard Bell

Fans of Eamonn Holmes were deeply impressed when the presenter shared snaps of a stunning portrait an artist painted for his 60th birthday.

On Instagram, the This Morning favourite uploaded pics of a realistic portrait produced by artist Colin Davidson - and his followers think the likeness is "amazing" and "stunning".

The snaps, shared on Monday (09.12.19), show Eamonn and his wife and co-presenter Ruth Langsford standing with painter Colin.

Eamonn has a half-smiling, half-serious expression in the painting and wears his signature dark suit and white open-collar shirt.

Last week, Eamonn delighted fans with a sweet snap of himself and Ruth on holiday in Vienna to celebrate his birthday.

In the caption of a photo from the trip posted to Instagram, Eamonn revealed that the holiday was a surprise present from his wife.

Not only did he have no idea they were going to Vienna, but when he arrived in the Austrian capital, his grown-up children were all waiting for him there!

Eamonn wrote alongside the pic, which showed him beaming next to Ruth in front of a festive scene: "Whisked off by Ruth on a complete mystery trip for my birthday. Ended up in Vienna with my children waiting there for me! Never been before - can't wait to come back. Great time in a truly majestic city."

On the day of his birthday, Eamonn got a lot of love from his family and friends.
