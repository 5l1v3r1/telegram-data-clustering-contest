Is League of Legends’ next champion Sett? Here’s everything we know

09 Dec 2019, 13:19 by Carrie Talbot

Hot on the heels of the newest League of Legends Champions Senna and Aphelios‘ arrivals, it looks like we could have an idea who the next Champion heading to LoL’s roster might be – and now, what abilities he’ll have. Following a loot asset hinting that the next character will be ‘Sett,’ players have been sent teasers pointing to what the mysterious hero will have up their sleeves.

A couple of weeks back, Surrenderat20 (via Dot Esports) revealed a loot asset called ‘Sett’s calling card’ had appeared in the game as part of the current League of Legends patch 9.24 PBE testing cycle. This is reportedly “gained by impressing Sett. Openable on January 14, 2020 at 11:00 PT.”

Now, players are reporting developer Riot Games has sent them emails containing some teasers about a new Champion. The teasers cover the Champion’s four abilities – W, Q, R, and E – which carry slogans like “Fist, meet face,” “Catch these hands,” and “Bust ‘em up,” and show images of various Champions engaging in a bit of a scuffle. Each image bears the statement: “We know you like to fight. Get ready for a new contender.”

According to Dexerto the emails were sent to LoL players who have played their fair share of Bruisers, Juggernauts, and top laners, which could point to what kind of Champion this new addition will be. While it’s not certain what their abilities might be from the clues given, it looks like strength and physical prowess in combat will likely be their style.

The teasers could also possibly point to it being Sett, as each image includes what seems to be his calling card.

Riot Games previously announced that a “smokin’ hot new juggernaut” is in the works. Emerging from the Ionian underground, “this new Champion will thrive in the centre of a brawl, laughing as they take hit after hit then furiously punching back, releasing all that stored aggression on their enemies’ faces.”
