Leicester City among clubs keen on Charlton Athletic forward Macauley Bonne?

Charlton Athletic forward Macauley Bonne has reportedly emerged as a transfer target for three clubs ahead of the January transfer window.

09 Dec 2019, 13:41 by Darren Plant

Having left Leyton Orient for Charlton in the summer, Bonne has contributed six goals and one assist from 14 appearances in the Championship.

Despite being less than six months into a three-year contract at The Valley, talkSPORT claims that Leicester City are one of three teams who are monitoring the 24-year-old's development.

While Jamie Vardy and Kelechi Iheanacho are established as the top two strikers at the King Power Stadium, it is said that Brendan Rodgers is looking to bolster his options in the final third.

However, with three weeks still remaining before the start of 2020, the report says that Newcastle United are currently considered to be the marginal frontrunners for Bonne.

Fulham are also ready to offer the attacker an opportunity to make the short switch across London.
