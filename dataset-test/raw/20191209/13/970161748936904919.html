Golden Globes 2020 nominations: The Irishman, Marriage Story and 1917 honoured

09 Dec 2019, 13:26 by Emma Kelly

The Irishman and Once Upon A Time In Hollywood have dominated the nominations for the Golden Globes 2020.

The nominees for the first big movie and TV awards show of the season have been announced, and Martin Scorsese’s three-hour drama The Irishman is running away with the nods.

The Golden Globes take place on 5 January 2020.
