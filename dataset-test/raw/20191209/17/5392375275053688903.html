The Galaxy S10 Lite and Galaxy Note 10 Lite continues to leak. While the launch is imminent, an online retailer has unveiled the price of both smartphones in Europe.

A leak came to lift the veil on the price of the Galaxy S10 Lite and Note 10 Lite in Europe as reported by our colleagues at GSMArena this December 9. Datart, an online retailer based in Slovakia, has mistakenly put online the prices in euros for both smartphones.

Galaxy S10 Lite and Galaxy Note 10 Lite prices

The Galaxy S10 Lite, renamed Galaxy A91 in some markets, will be visibly marketed at a price of 669 euros ($740). Attention, the real price in other European countries can obviously vary of some tens of euros. Not surprisingly, the device is a little cheaper than the Galaxy S10e , which is sold for 749 euros ($830). As a reminder, the S10 Lite is equipped with a 6.7-inch Full HD AMOLED display, a Snapdragon 855 SoC, 8 GB of RAM, 128 GB of internal storage and a 4500 mAh battery compatible with fast charging of 45 W.

For its part, the Galaxy Note 10 Lite, also called Galaxy A81, will be sold at a price of 609 euros ($675). It is much cheaper than the standard Galaxy Note 10, offered at a price of 959 euros ($1060) on the European market. The smartphone is obviously distinguished by its stylus S-Pen. Side technical sheet, there is a Snapdragon 730/735 SoC supported by 8 GB RAM and internal storage of 128 GB.

The price of the Galaxy A71 and Galaxy A51

In the process, Datart also unveiled the European price of Galaxy A71 and Galaxy A51, new midrange phones. The Galaxy A71 will be sold at a price of 469 euros ($520). Against 375 euros ($415) for the Galaxy A51. Both smartphones come with a punch-hole for the front camera like the Galaxy Note 10. According to the online retailer, the four Samsung smartphones will land on the market on December 27, 2019. We will tell you more as soon as possible.
