Microsoft to kill Wunderlist apps on May 6th, 2020

09 Dec 2019, 17:26 by Pradeep

Microsoft today announced that it will shut down Wunderlist on May 6th, 2020. For the past few years, Microsoft has been working on a brand-new task management app called To Do. Microsoft To Do will be the successor to Wunderlist app and you can import your lists from Wunderlist to To Do in few steps. From today, new users will not be able to sign-up for Wunderlist.
