Kim Kardashian threatens to sack sister Kourtney from KUWTK

09 Dec 2019, 17:27 by Lisa Higgins

They don’t seem to go very long without falling out – but this time Kim Kardashian is fuming with her sister Kourtney and has threatened to boot her off Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

And it’s all over Kourtney not towing the reality line and spilling all about her personal life.

On Sunday’s episode, Kim, 39, criticised her 40-year-old sister for avoiding opening up about certain aspects of her personal life and being ‘so secretive’.

‘Kourtney spends a lot of time off-camera,’ said Kim.

‘She won’t talk about her relationship, but you’ll see paparazzi photos of her and her guy that I can’t say his name on the show because Kourtney doesn’t want to.

‘I’ll see things or read about it online and wonder like, did this really happen? Is she talking to this person?

‘And realise that it’s true, but she’s so secretive with us that she won’t even tell us.’

Younger sister Khloe, 35, agreed that being transparent with viewers is all part of the Kardashians’ ‘job’, but Kourtney argued there is a ‘fine line’.

Kim explained that she and Khloe have had to overcompensate for their sibling’s decision to hold things back.

‘Our job is being open and honest and sharing a lot of ourselves and it just seems like for the past few years now, Kourtney hasn’t really been open about her personal life on camera,’ she said.

‘So all of the days that Kourtney isn’t filming, Khloé and I are picking up the slack and having to share more. Because if we’re not sharing our lives, then what is the show?’

Shockingly, when Kim and Khloe called Kourtney to discuss the issue, Kim suggested: ‘We have a proposal for you: It starts with an F and ends with a D.’

She later continued: ‘She doesn’t want to show up, she doesn’t want to work, she has too many f***ing boundaries, she’s out.’

This is not the first time the sisters have clashed. Their rivalry hotted up in September when they had their ‘biggest fight’.

Meanwhile, Kourtney, who has children Mason, nine, Penelope, seven, and Reign, four, with ex Scott Disick, posted a clip on Instagram of her telling her side of the story.

Kourtney recently revealed she would be taking some time away from the reality show to focus on her brood.

She said: ‘I just decided to spend more time as a mom and put more of my energy there. But I’m not saying goodbye.’

It’s going to be a tense Christmas!
