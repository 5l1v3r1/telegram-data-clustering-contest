Sip on some holiday cheer at this Christmas pop-up bar in Echo Park

09 Dec 2019, 17:13 by Sophie Flay

ECHO PARK (KABC) -- Grab your ugly Christmas sweater and check out Santa Baby LA, a Christmas pop-up bar in Echo Park.

Tickets to the bar cost $5.

The bar is open Thursday-Saturday now until December 31st.
