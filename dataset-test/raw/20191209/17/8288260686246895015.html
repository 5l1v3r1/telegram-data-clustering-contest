Wolverhampton Wanderers v Besiktas: TV details, form, betting odds

Nuno Espirito Santo's men are in Europa League group action against Turkish opposition at Molineux

09 Dec 2019, 17:13 by Chris Watson

Wolverhampton Wanderers are back in Europa League action this week when they take on Turkish side Besiktas.

Wolves have already qualified for the knockout stages of the competition, securing their passage through courtesy of a 3-3 draw away to Braga last time out.

Nuno Espirito Santo's men - unbeaten in eight in all competitions following Sunday's 2-2 draw at Brighton in the Premier League - round off their Group K campaign at home to Besiktas.

Here's what you need to know ahead of the game...

When is the game?

Wolverhampton Wanderers v Besiktas is taking place on Thursday (December 12).

The match at Molineux is due to kick off at 8pm.

Is it on TV?

Yes. The match is being shown live on BT Sport ESPN, with coverage due to get under way at 7.45pm.
