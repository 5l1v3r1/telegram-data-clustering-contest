Watch: David Ehrlich’s 25 Best Films of 2019 Video Countdown

09 Dec 2019, 17:40 by Jordan Raup

As Film Twitter quarrels over who or what did or didn’t get nominated for a certain award, it’s always a refreshing breath of air towards the end of the year to get the annual video countdown from David Ehrlich. Both a celebration of the year in cinema as well as an excellent list of films to seek out if one hasn’t yet, 2019’s edition is no different.

Giving unexpected new emotional heft to some Disney and Sondheim tunes, as well as some favorite tracks from the year in film, these new juxtapositions are a brilliant way to re-appreciate some of our favorite movie moments of the last twelve months. We won’t spoil any of the magic in this one, so start watching below. Also, a good amount of the selections are currently available to stream, so get watching.
