Amir arrives at Kigali

09 Dec 2019, 17:09

Kigali: The Amir H H Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al-Thani arrived Monday in Kigali, capital of Rwanda.

On his arrival at Kigali International Airport, His Highness was received by HE President of Rwanda Paul Kagame, and HE Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation Vincent Biruta, and Mayor of Kigali Pudence Rubingisa, and HE Ambassador of Qatar to Rwanda Abdullah bin Mohammed Al Sayed.
