Report: Florida State Makes Offensive Coordinator Hire

09 Dec 2019, 17:18 by Tzvi Machlin

Newly-minted Florida State head coach Mike Norvell is wasting no time putting his coaching staff together. It’s only his first day on the job and he’s already picked out his offensive coordinator.

According to Pete Thamel of Yahoo! Sports, Florida State is hiring Auburn offensive coordinator Kenny Dillingham to fill the same role with the ‘Noles. 247Sports has confirmed the news.

Dillingham is in his first year with Auburn after previously working under Norvell at Memphis, and at Arizona State before that. In 2017, he served as quarterbacks and tight ends coach, and helped put together an offense that set numerous school records moving the ball.
