Sainsbury's, Asda and Tesco are urgently recalling these items

These supermarkets are recalling food items due to health concerns and the list includes Pot Noodle, Oxfordshire Tea Co and Oumph!

09 Dec 2019, 17:12 by Kali Lindsay, Jillian MacMath

Major supermarkets have issued an urgent recall on several food items.

Sainsbury's , Tesco and Asda are recalling items such as Beef & Tomato pot noodles from Unilever and Sainsbury's Hot Buffalo Dip.

According to Wales Online , the Food Standards Agency has urged the public not to consume any items on the list and return them to the supermarket for a refund.

The entire list of recalled products:

Unilever Pot Noodle Beef and Tomato

Unilever is recalling a batch of Beef & Tomato pot noodles as it contains celery and mustard, food items that are not specified on the label.

This could pose a risk to anyone with allergies to these ingredients.

Allergens: Mustard and celery

Pack size: 90g

Batch code: L9282

Best before: July 2020

Oumph! The Chunk

Due to a packaging error, Oumph! are recalling a batch of 'The Chunk'.

Some products contain mustard but this is not specified on the food label, which could pose a health risk to anyone with allergies.

The product is sold in ASDA, Holland & Barrett and Tesco stores only.

Allergens: Mustard

Pack size: 280g

Batch code: 180919

Best before: March 18, 2021

Asda Meatball Marinara meal

ASDA is recalling a meatball marinara meal as the product contains milk; this is not specified on the label.

This is a health risk for anyone with an intolerance to milk or milk substitutes.

Allergens : Milk

Pack size: 160g

Use by: December 8, 2019

Oxfordshire Tea Co. Butter Shortbread

TJ Morris is recalling Oxfordshire Tea Co. Butter Shortbread due to an inaccurate allergy label.

The product has been labelled as 'gluten-free' but it contains wheat (gluten), meaning the product poses a health risk for anyone with an intolerance or allergy to wheat or gluten.

The shortbread is only sold in home bargains stores.

Darwin's Dairy milk and cream products

Darwin's Dairy has issued an urgent recall on every milk and cream products as they may not have been effectively pasteurised.

The products might contain harmful bacteria such as E. coli.

These products are sold in a variety of packaging, including glass bottles, plastic bottles and carton.

Darwin's Dairy products being recalled include: Double cream, whipping cream, semi-skimmed milk, skimmed milk, whole milk, NP and DJ Darwin Ltd double cream, NP and DJ Darwin Ltd whipping cream, NP and DJ Darwin Ltd semi-skimmed milk, NP and DJ Darwin Ltd skimmed milk, NP and DJ Darwin Ltd whole milk.

Pack size: All

Use by: All dates up to and including November 29, 2019

Sainsbury's Hot Buffalo Dip

Sainsbury's is recalling this dip as it contains egg, an ingredient that is not specified on the label.

Some packs might contain a garlic and herb dip, meaning the product poses a health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to egg.

Allergen: Egg

pack size: 90g

Use by: December 6, 2019
