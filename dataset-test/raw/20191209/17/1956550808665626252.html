LUVYA full weekend line-up schedule revealed

Find out when your favourite artists are playing at the huge three-day festival

09 Dec 2019, 17:33 by Darragh Murphy

The massive LUVYA three-day festival at FIVE Palm Jumeirah Dubai is fast approaching, and if you’re wondering when your favourite artists are playing, look no further.

There will be three stages hosting top acts across the weekend, held on the brand-new 150m private Beach by FIVE, Time Out Dubai award-winning rooftop bar The Penthouse, as well as cool underground club Secret Room.

Beach by FIVE
So, who’s playing first at the Beach by FIVE? On Thursday December 12, first up is Chris Wright from 1pm until 2.30pm, followed by Arado from 2.30pm until 4pm, and Icarus from 4pm until 5pm.

As the evening turns to night, Klingande will be first up from 5pm until 6.30pm, Bjones from 6.30pm until 8.30pm, and to finish off the night, prepare to groove to Lost Frequencies,

As for Friday December 13, expect Madds to kick off from 2.30pm until 3.30pm, DJ Licious from 3.30pm until 4.30pm, then Offiah from 4.30pm until 5.30pm.

Then Disciples are up from 5.30pm until 7pm, followed by Craig David Presents TS5 from 7pm until 8.30pm and the hugely-anticipated duo Dimitri Vegas and Like Mike to finish the night.

Finally on Saturday December 14, partygoers can expect NSI from 2pm until 3.30pm, Giolo and Assia from 3.30pm until 5.30pm, Mishka from 5.30pm until 7pm, and Lannka to complete the night from 7pm onwards.

The Penthouse
Missed any of the artists at the beach? Not to worry, you’ll find some of them here along with some other top artists.

On Thursday December 12, there’s Arado from 5pm until 8pm, NSI from 8pm until 10pm, and Adrian Eftimie from 10pm until midnight.

Party doesn’t stop there. From midnight until 2am, expect Eli and Fur to be blasting tunes, and Lexlay to finish off the night from 2am until 4am.

On Friday December 13, expect Arado from 5pm until 7pm, NSI from 7pm until 9.30pm, along with Caffola and Engel from 9.30pm until midnight.

Keep the party going with Paris House Addicts from midnight until 2.30am, and finish off with Adrian Eftimie to finish off the night.

Finally, on Saturday December 14, the parry will kick off with the Candypants White Party from noon until 7pm, and Arado from 7.30pm until 9.30pm.

As for the final two, there’s Miguel Bastida from 9.30pm until 12.30am, and NSI bringing his tunes to finish off the weekend with a bang.

More than 40 top artists will perform, plus there will be top dishes to try from Japanese and Chinese to Italian and international street food.

And of course you’ll get unbeatable views across to the Dubai Marina skyline, making for an awesome backdrop to the super-cool new festival.
