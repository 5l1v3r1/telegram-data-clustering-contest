Green Monday 2019 mobile tech deals

09 Dec 2019, 17:12 by Brad Linder

Another day, another excuse for internet retailers to offer deep discounts in an attempt to sell you more stuff.

This time it’s Green Monday, a name invented by eBay more than a decade ago, but which has been adopted by a number of other stores in recent years.

The long and short of it is that if you didn’t find what you were looking for on Black Friday, Cyber Monday, or any other day in the past few weeks, now’s another chance to score cheap deals on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other devices.
