Bat for Lashes shares stripped-down piano version of “Desert Man”

09 Dec 2019, 16:56 by Bill Pearis

Bat for Lashes has just offered up an alternate version of “Desert Man” from her recent album Lost Girls. Stripped back almost entirely to just Natasha Khan and her piano, the song may be be all the more powerful for it. Listen to this new version and the lush album version below.

You can catch Bat for Lashes on tour in North America starting in early February and including a NYC stop at Town Hall on 2/19 (tickets).
