High school football: Big 10 Conference announces 2019 All-Conference selections

09 Dec 2019, 16:58 by Cameron Jourdan

The Big 10 Conference announced Monday its selections for the 2019 All-Conference football team.

Putnam North was named conference champion, and coach Ryan Laverty was named Coach of the Year.

Check out the All-Conference awards here:

Quarterbacks: Tyrece Romesburg, Putnam City; Eric Wiley, Lawton; Thad Williams, Choctaw

Running backs: Emahdre Hightower, Putnam West; Omari John, Midwest City; Trentavious Mitchell, Enid; Deysean Moore, Choctaw; Trevaugn Walton, Lawton Eisenhower

Wide receivers: Eddie Austin, Lawton, Gabe Johnson, Chateau Reed, Lawton; Cade Sanford, Choctaw

Tight end: Wesley Dedmon, Midwest City

Offensive linemen: Andy Beabout, Choctaw, Donovan Forest, Putnam North; Jack Funderburgh, Choctaw; Reggie Gaither, Jayston King, Putnam North; Midwest City; Jake Lovinggood, Choctaw; Kaleb Stanley, Enid

Defensive linemen: DJ Flowers, Putnam North; Erik Hughes, Putnam West; Dakari Mason, Putnam City; Kaleb Morgan, Midwest City; Trinity Pewewardy, Lawton Eisenhower

Linebackers: Zach Dortch, Putnam North; Caleb Hukill, Putnam City; Jacobi Johnson, Midwest City, Jeremiah Magee, Lawton; Devante Moncrief, Lawton Ike; Jeff Roberson, Choctaw; Kaden Romero, Putnam North

Defensive backs: Angel Ackerman, Choctaw; DeMarcus Andrews, Lawton; Jaden DiMaricut, Enid; Tryston Murphy, Midwest City; Tyrese Neal, Enid; Elijah Reyes, Lawton; Tavion Robinson, Choctaw; Tyreque Romesburg, Putnam North; Davione Tipton, Midwest City

Athlete: Ethan Roberts, Lawton Eisenhower
