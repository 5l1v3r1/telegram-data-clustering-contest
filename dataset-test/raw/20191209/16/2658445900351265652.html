Rising musical act, Teni Akpata set to serenade fans with a live concert in Lagos

09 Dec 2019, 16:11

Our Reporter

Teni Akpata popularly known as Teni Makanaki & the Entertainer will be live in concert as she thrills fans to an unforgettable experience this December in Lagos with the Billionaire Experience Concert.

The event organized by S&S entertainment in partnership with Bet9ja will bring more excitement on the scene this festive period. Many have been yearning and are thrilled with the news of this opportunity to see the 26year-old perform live in Lagos.

Teni is an extraordinary artist, since coming on the music scene with lyrical songs that have consistently been loved by fans and many globally; she has earned her place and name as a true entertainer. The singer who is full of life with an amazing personality has quickly warmed her way up in the hearts of many with her electrifying voice and unconventional style of music.

The Billionaire Experience is packed with delightful entertainment for attendees; S&S Entertainment in partnership with Bet9Ja is leaving no stones unturned as they are set to host music lovers with the Teni Billionaire Experience, 6pm on Monday December 16, 2019 at the Eko Convention Center, Eko Hotel & Suites, Victoria Island, Lagos.

From Music, comedy, millionaire quiz, every detail promises to be fun and rewarding for the concert goers.

A major highlight leading up to the concert is the making of a Millionaire with the Teni Who wants to be a Millionaire Quiz competition.

One lucky fan will be presented with One million naira at the concert and many more side attractions and entertainment.

This definitely is a mouthwatering concert with a difference and one to look forward to when looking to have a really good time in Lagos this December.
