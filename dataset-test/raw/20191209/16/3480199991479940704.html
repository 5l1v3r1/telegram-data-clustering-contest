First Ghostbusters: Afterlife trailer sees return of film favourites

09 Dec 2019, 16:00

The first trailer for the new Ghostbusters film sees newcomers Paul Rudd and Finn Wolfhard making their franchise debuts.

The first trailer for the new Ghostbusters sequel sees the return of franchise favourites Slimer and the Ecto-1 car alongside a new cast of new stars.

Ghostbusters: Afterlife, which will be released next summer, is directed by Jason Reitman, the son of the original film’s director Ivan Reitman.

The supernatural comedy features new cast members including Ant-Man star Paul Rudd, Stranger Things actor Finn Wolfhard, The Leftovers star Carrie Coon and Designated Survivor actress Mckenna Grace.
