‘I Am Jesus Christ’ Is the Ultimate God Game

09 Dec 2019, 16:55 by Jordan Minor

There have been plenty of video games based on the Bible. Most of them are bad, sometimes hilariously so. Religion has inspired some of history’s greatest works of art. But so far video games have yet to receive that divine touch, from Christianity at least. It’s about time that changed.

For YEARS I’ve been pitching a game where you either play as Jesus or fight Jesus. It’s such a logical endpoint for a franchise like Assassin’s Creed or God of War. But whereas AAA publishers are too cowardly and full of sin to make the ultimate god game, this weekend our salvation was revealed in the form of an indie game.

The first trailer for I Am Jesus Christ has risen.
