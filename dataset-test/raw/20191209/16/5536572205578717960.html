Ex-NFL GM Says Dak Prescott Will Sign Massive $400M Deal With Cowboys

09 Dec 2019, 16:27 by Darrelle Lincoln

Say what, now???

The Dallas Cowboys are 13 games into the 2019 season and the talk surrounding Dak Prescott getting a new deal has been quiet. That was until Sunday afternoon when The Athletic reporter Jon Machota took to Twitter and dropped some big news.

He relayed information he heard from former NFL GM Mike Tannenbaum who stated on ESPN radio that Dak Prescott would end up signing a massive $400M deal with the Cowboys.
