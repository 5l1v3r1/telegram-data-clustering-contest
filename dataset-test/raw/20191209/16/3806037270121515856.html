Très Bien and SNEEZE Magazine Team up on Material-Focused Capsule Collection

Featuring unexpected SNEEZE logo placements.

09 Dec 2019, 16:52 by Emily Engle

Très Bien and poster-sized publication SNEEZE Magazine have collaborated on a clothing and accessory capsule collection that aims to join Très Bien’s sportswear aesthetic with SNEEZE’s artistic eye. The capsule consists of pieces that take classic items a step further in material and design, from a leather anorak and bucket hat to a ruby shirt with bold, offset stripes. SNEEZE’s iconic outline logo graces many pieces in the collection, most unexpectedly on a pair of plaid wool trousers that come in either grey or navy.

Some of the more traditional offerings in the collection stand out based on their of-the-moment color palettes, including SNEEZE logo crewnecks in stone blue and oatmeal and a bold block rugby shirt with hints of a blue similar to Pantone’s Color of the Year 2020, Classic Blue. A navy fleece vest is also upgraded with a navy and orange oversized chest pouch pocket. Three different T-shirts, two with Très Bien’s logo and one with SNEEZE’s logo, round out the capsule.

The full SNEEZE x Très Bien capsule collection will be available over on Très Bien’s website and SNEEZE’s website beginning December 13. To celebrate the collection, a launch party will be taking place in London on December 11. RSVP information can be found in the Instagram post below.
