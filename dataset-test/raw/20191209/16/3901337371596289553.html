The ex-Mamelodi Sundowns man was in attendance at the Teqball World Championships, and took to Instagram to reveal his whereabouts.

Teqball is a sport played between two players, or teams of three or four, which is played on a curved table. In fact, many football teams around Europe enjoy a game of Teqball in their respective dressing rooms after training sessions.

That being said, Barcelona legends Carles Puyol and Ronaldinho were also in attendance, and were later seen hanging out with the former Bafana Bafana star.
