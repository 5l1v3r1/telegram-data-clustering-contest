Nintendo Switch indie livestream tomorrow

From 6pm UK time.

09 Dec 2019, 16:31 by Tom Phillips

Nintendo will hold an indie game focused presentation tomorrow, 10th December, at 6pm UK time.

Named Indie World, it'll be a 20-minute long Nintendo Direct-style broadcast focusing on Switch indie games.

You'll be able to watch it via the video embed below, and we'll of course report on all the big announcements shortly after.

This week is a busy one for game announcements. Things kick off at 2pm with PlayStation's own version of a Nintendo Direct, State of Play.

Then, on Friday, you can join us in the wee hours of the morning (from 1.30am) for The Game Awards. We're expecting a couple of things from Nintendo there, too.
