Liz Hurley gets ready for the holidays by wrapping up as a Christmas present

09 Dec 2019, 16:23 by Zara Woodcock

Liz Hurley transformed herself into a racy present just in time for the Christmas holidays.

The star shared a raunchy snap of herself basically wrapped as an almost-nude present to her 1.4 million followers.

She captioned her post: ‘I wear lots of red but Chinese red, aka Valentino red, is by far and away my favourite shade. And perfect for Xmas.’

That’s a lot of names for the same shade of red.

Covered in only a red satin piece of fabric while sitting on her living room floor, it’s safe to say her heating bills are through the roof.

However, Liz is no stranger to posting semi-nude but classy photos on the internet.

Her fans clearly appreciated the post, with one writing: ‘So beautiful,’ along with a love heart.

The star goes all out for holidays, as Halloween had the 54-year-old dressed up in her best Kill Bill costume and gave Uma Thurman a run for her money.

Last month, Liz blessed us with her skin routine while out in the sun.

Taking to Instagram, the actress sent fans into meltdown with the racy upload, showing her stripping off while enjoying a quick dip.

As. You. Do.

‘LA morning wake up (PS I only go in the sun before 9am and after 6pm- otherwise, it’s under a large umbrella),’ she captioned the shot. And, right on cue, they were quick to flood her post with all the praise.

‘This surely is “How it should be done!!!!!”,’ a fan posted. ‘Absolutely stunning, seriously fabulous, amazingly beautiful.’

‘Love this photo,’ an Instagram user said. ‘That’s why your skin looks so good!!

The next holiday is Valentine’s Day, so everyone needs to get ready for a saucy themed photo, one where she poses covered in roses or standing next to endless love hearts, perhaps?
