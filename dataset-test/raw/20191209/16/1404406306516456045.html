Kentucky Judge Accused Of Having Threesomes With Staff in Courthouse

09 Dec 2019, 16:37 by VladTV

According to reports, a Kentucky judge named Dawn Gentry has been accused of pressuring lawyers into having threesomes with her and a pastor who used to be her lover.

Allegedly, Gentry would not give the lawyers preferential treatment in their cases if they denied her request. The Kentucky Judicial Conduct Commission alleged that Gentry used Snapchat to flirt with lawyers, and Gentry has also been accused of letting employees get drunk while on the job.

The 38-year old judge has denied the allegations against her so far. Stay tuned for more details in this situation.
