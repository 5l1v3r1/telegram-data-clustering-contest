Sexual Battery investigation underway in North Port

09 Dec 2019, 16:46 by WFTX Digital Team

NORTH PORT, Fla. — North Port Police are searching for a suspect accused of Sexual Battery.

The incident took place on December 8th between 1 AM and 7 AM on Tamiami Trail, near Pan American Boulevard.

The suspect is described as a black male, medium build, possibly in his 60’s, with short twists in his hair.

The suspect is described as wearing a cream-colored long sleeve shirt/sweater with a brown stripe across the chest and flannel pants with white spots, wearing sneakers.

The suspect was also reported to be riding a bicycle with blue and green striped fenders over the wheels.

This is an ongoing investigation. More details will be released when available. If you have any information which might be relevant to this case, please contact Detective James Keller at 941-429-7322 or jkeller@northportpd.com.
