Here Are the 2020 Golden Globe Nominations in Full

09 Dec 2019, 16:36

Awards season is finally here, and it's starting with a bang. Favorites like The Crown, Fleabag, and Big Little Lies lead the list of 2020 Golden Globe television nominees. Major motion productions like Martin Scorsese's The Irishman and Quentin Tarantino's Once Upon a Time… in Hollywood are also top contenders, with both men nominated for Best Director as well. A category some have already noted is seriously lacking in female directors, Best Director has historically followed suit. Since the Golden Globes' inception, it has nominated only seven women for the honor, according to the Los Angeles Times.

We'll all find out the winners come next year, with the ceremony set for January 5. In the meantime, place your bets on this season's biggest winners. Here are the 2020 Golden Globe nominees.
