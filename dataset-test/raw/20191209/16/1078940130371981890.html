1971 War Veteran hero Lt Colonel Muhammad Suleiman passes away

09 Dec 2019, 16:13 by Mati

RAWALPINDI, Pakistan: Lt Colonel Muhammad Suleiman, veteran hero of 1971 War, breathed his last in Lahore on Monday, the Inter Services Public Relations (ISPR) said.

Known as “Suleiman the magnificent” the Officer was originally from the Special Services Group (SSG).

The ISPR said that Lt Colonel Muhammad Suleiman under challenging environment rescued his injured companion Major PD Khan from battlefield in the face of enemy and brought him back safely.

In a statement, the Chief of Army Staff (COAS) General Qamar Javed Bajwa has expressed heartfelt grief on his demise while paying tribute to his matchless bravery.
