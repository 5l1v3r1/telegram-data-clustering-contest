Boris Johnson pockets journalist's phone instead of looking at photo of child sleeping on hospital floor

Johnson then took the phone and said it is a “a terrible, terrible photo”.

09 Dec 2019, 16:11 by Press Association

UK PRIME MINISTER Boris took a reporter’s phone and put it in his pocket after refusing to look at a photo of a child who had to sleep on a hospital floor.

Johnson was being interviewed by ITV News political correspondent Joe Pike, who asked the PM to look at a photo of four-year-old Jack, whose mother Sarah Williment covered him with coats to keep warm as he waited for a bed at Leeds General Infirmary.

She had taken him there last Tuesday fearing he had pneumonia.

Johnson did not look down at the photo on the reporter’s phone, instead saying he would “study it later” as he attempted to steer the conversation on to Tory investment in the NHS.
