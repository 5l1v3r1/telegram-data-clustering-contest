Reports: Ronaldo Tells Friends His Big Regret

09 Dec 2019, 16:26 by Kurt Buckerfield

With things not going very well for superstar Cristiano Ronaldo at Juventus this season, the former Real Madrid talisman has reportedly opened up to his friends about a major regret of his.

Despite scoring in his past two Serie A matches, the 34-year-old has endured a tough time in Italy in recent months.

After his relationship with manager Maurizio Sarri took strain recently, Ronaldo is believed to be unhappy at the Bianconeri, whom he joined from Los Blancos in 2018.

Following Real's win over Liverpool in the 2017/18 UEFA Champions League final last year, the Portuguese shocked many when he suggested it may have been his last match for the club, which eventually turned out to be true.

Now, according to ABC newspaper, Ronaldo has told close friends and family that he wishes he had not left the Spanish giants, and that his decision to move on might have been a mistake.
