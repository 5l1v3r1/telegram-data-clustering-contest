Jacqueline Jossa breaks her silence in Instagram video wearing her wedding ring

She told her fans: 'I just want you all to know that I am absolutely fine'

09 Dec 2019, 16:45 by Bronte Howard

I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! winner Jacqueline Jossa has updated fans on how she's doing in an adorable Instagram video with her two daughters - and she's wearing her wedding ring again.

The 27-year-old triumphed over Coronation Street star Andy Whyment and DJ Roman Kemp in the live final on Sunday night after spending three weeks in the jungle.

Jacqueline was sweetly met by her husband Dan Osborne at the end of the bridge amid cheating claims and when she saw her daughters, she broke into a run to scoop them up and the waiting crowds cheered.

While the I'm a Celeb runners-up have already appeared on various television shows talking about their time in the jungle, Jacqueline reportedly cancelled all TV appearances - with a close source to the star telling MirrorOnline it was so she could spend time with her family.

But earlier today, Jacqueline, who is still in Australia, broke her silence in an Instagram video featuring her two daughters, Ella, four, and one-year-old Mia.

She told her fans: "Hello everybody. Thank you so much for voting for me. If you voted, it means the absolute world I cannot quite believe it's happening to me.

"I just want you all to know that I am absolutely fine.

"It has been the best experience of my whole entire life. And my babies are here."

Jacqueline's time in the jungle was overshadowed by rumours TOWIE star Dan had cheated on her but eagle-eyed fans would have spotted that her wedding ring was firmly back on her finger.

While she was in the jungle, campmate and Rak-Su star Myles Stephenson reportedly told her that Osborne cheated on her with Stephenson's ex-girlfriend Gabby Allen.

The pair were previously in the Celebrity Big Brother house together.

Amid speculation over the status of their relationship, Dan, who supported his wife throughout her stint in the jungle, proudly posted a photo of the pair on Sunday night.

He captioned the image: "My jungle Queen!! ❤️ words can not describe how proud I am of this woman! She absolutely smashed it from day 1 and deserves this sooo much!! Well done baby ❤️❤️❤."
