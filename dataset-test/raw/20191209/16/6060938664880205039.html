'Panipat' was released on December 06 and has been the talk of the town since the announcement. However, the film is facing criticism over the portrayal of Bharatpur's Maharaja Surajmal. Several cinema halls have stopped the screening of the film in Jaipur.

A theatre official said in a statement, "As per instructions of the authorities, we have suspended the screening of the movie at our multiplex in Jaipur. The show will remain suspended until further notice."
