Video: Fan’s Reaction To LeBron Cursing Is Going Viral

09 Dec 2019, 16:52 by Tzvi Machlin

Athletes cursing during a game is nothing new. It might actually be harder to find an athlete who doesn’t swear out loud during any given game.

But one fan seemed especially peeved to see Los Angeles Lakers megastar LeBron James cursing.

During yesterday’s game between the Lakers and the Minnesota Timberwolves, LeBron was seen cursing along the sideline. As the camera panned across with him, a fan wearing a red shirt could be seen mouthing the words “Watch your mouth.”

The video has quickly gone viral with thousands of likes and hundreds of retweets in the minutes since it was released.
