Crime Stoppers Crime of the Week: Aggravated Robbery

09 Dec 2019, 16:39 by Johnny Thrash

Crime Stoppers could use your help solving a recent aggravated robbery.

The crime occurred on Wednesday, October 30 at Abels General Store 2, located at 1335 Central Freeway.

At around 4:20 pm, a tall, black male wearing an orange/red colored beanie and sunglasses with brown tint lenses entered the store. The suspect then jabbed a box cutter at the clerk several times, demanding money. The man fled the scene after the clerk gave him money from the register.
