What to do in Oklahoma on Dec. 9, 2019: Hear Texas singer-songwriter Isaac Hoskins at JJ's Alley Bricktown Pub

09 Dec 2019, 16:57 by Brandy McDonnell

Today's featured event:

Hear Denton, Texas-based singer-songwriter Isaac Hoskins play at 9:30 tonight at JJ's Alley Bricktown Pub, 212 E Sheridan.

It will be Hoskins' debut at the Bricktown bar, and there will be no cover.
