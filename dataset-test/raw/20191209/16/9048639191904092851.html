Memorial services set for KNOE investigative reporter Ken Booth

09 Dec 2019, 16:26 by KNOE

MONROE, La. (KNOE) - Memorial services have been set for longtime KNOE investigative reporter Ken Booth.

According to Mulhearn Funeral Home, visitation is set for Tuesday, December 10, 2019, from 1 p.m. to 2 p.m. A memorial service will follow.

The address is 2308 Sterlington Road, Monroe, LA 71203.

Booth died on December 6, 2019, after being diagnosed with lung cancer.
