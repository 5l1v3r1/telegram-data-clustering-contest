Paul Reubens Announces Tour To Celebrate 35th Anniversary Of ‘Pee-Wee’s Big Adventure’

09 Dec 2019, 16:40 by Aynslee Darmon

Pee-wee is heading out on tour.

Paul Reubens, the creator and star of “Pee-wee’s Playhouse” and three Pee-wee Herman films, announced a tour to celebrate the 35th anniversary of 1985’s “Pee-wee’s Big Adventure”.

The show will feature a screening of the hit film, as well as live behind-the-scenes stories from Reubens himself.

“Pee-wee’s Big Adventure” marked the directorial debut for Tim Burton. It was composed by Danny Elfman.

The 20-city tour will kick off Feb. 14 in Portland, Oregon, and runs through March 29 in Washington, DC. No Canadian dates have been announced.

In 2016, Netflix released the most recent Pee-wee film, “Pee-wee’s Big Holiday”.

Tickets will go on sale to the general public on Dec. 13.
