Davido joins shatta wale to mock Bullet over Cardi B ‘meet and greet’ snub

09 Dec 2019, 16:06 by Adu Sarpeah

Most popular personalities otherwise known as celebrities in Ghana have become subject of mockery following Cardi B ‘meet and greet’ snub.

Remember that Cardi B, the Grammy-winning American female rapper, was billed to headline Livespot X Festival in Accra, Ghana on Sunday, December 8th.

Ahead of the concert, the organizers arranged for a meet and greet with local celebrities. However, things couldn’t go as scheduled for obvious reasons.

Bullet, Afia Schwar, Efia Odo, Jackie Appiah, Yvonne Nelson, Lydia Forson, Hajia4real, Wendy Shay, Akuapem Poloo, Victoria Lebene Moesha Boduong, et Al. aligned themselves at Kempinski Hotel to meet and greet Cardi.

But as a result of the snub, the above-mentioned personalities have become subject of mockery among social media users.

Well, Shatta Wale reacting to Cardi B ‘meet and greet’ snub shared a picture of Bullet queuing up to meet Cardi B on his Twitter page captioned it “Cardi G”. Fast forward, Davido after seeing the post responded to the with ‘lmfao’
