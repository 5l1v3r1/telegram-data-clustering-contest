Manchester United fans identify full strength team when everyone's fit

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer could soon have a dilemma when Paul Pogba returns from injury and other Man Utd players are available.

09 Dec 2019, 16:45 by Dominic Booth, http://www.facebook.com/DominicBoothMEN/

Manchester United fans believe Ole Gunnar Solskjaer is close to discovering his first choice team after two recent victories.

United have been plagued by inconsistent form for much of the campaign, but wins over Tottenham Hotspur and Manchester City in the past week have provided a timely boost for Solskjaer and his squad.

And it seems many supporters think that the return of Paul Pogba from injury could give United yet another boost.

The Frenchman has been missing since September 30th with an ankle injury. And although United have coped well — particularly the midfield duo of Scott McTominay and Fred — many fans think Pogba should return in the No.10 role.

Some fans also want to see youngster Brandon Williams reinstated at left-back, despite positive displays from Luke Shaw against City and Ashley Young versus Spurs. Shaw appears to be the current first choice on the left of defence.

Solskjaer's dilemma will be whether to bring back Pogba into a deeper midfield position and changed United's shape from the 4-2-3-1 that has been effective or late. Axel Tuanzebe and Williams are also pushing for regular inclusion, as is Mason Greenwood who started against Tottenham in Anthony Martial's absence.

As well as Pogba, United's other current injury worries are over Diogo Dalot, Eric Bailly, Timothy Fosu-Mensah and Marcos Rojo, though none would break into the strongest XI as things stand.

Here's the team that some fans want to see picked by Solskjaer on a regular basis — when all players are fit.
