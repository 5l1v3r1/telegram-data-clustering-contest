Bayonetta and Vanquish anniversary bundle is coming to PS4 and Xbox One next year

09 Dec 2019, 16:32 by Emily Gera

The Bayonetta and Vanquish anniversary bundle is set to arrive February 18, 2020.

Ten years after their debut, Bayonetta and Vanquish are slated to reappear as part of a new anniversary bundle from developer Platinumgames and publisher Sega.

Coming to PlayStation 4 and Xbox One, the Bayonetta & Vanquish 10th Anniversary Bundle includes both the original Bayonetta and Vanquished, remastered in 4K resolution and at 60 fps in digital and physical format for $39.99.

In addition, all physical copies of the bundle will include an embossed SteelBook with new art for both games, created by artist Dan Dussault with supervision from Bayonetta director Hideki Kamiya.
