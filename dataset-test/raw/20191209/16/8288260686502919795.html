Serie A outfit contact Wolves and Everton over January transfers - reports

The young star has made just three Premier League starts, with eight appearances off the bench - and has scored twice

09 Dec 2019, 16:35 by Brian Dick, https://www.facebook.com/blueswriterbriandick/?fref=ts

Bologna have contacted Wolves with a view to taking Patrick Cutrone back to Italy, according to reports.

The 21-year-old has had to wait for his opportunities since his £16million summer move from AC Milan.

He has made just three Premier League starts, with eight appearances off the bench - and has scored twice.

His most recent goal came in the 2-0 win over West Ham a week ago, but with the transfer window around the corner speculation has started.

Italian outlet La Repubblica has claimed Bologna’s search for striking reinforcements has taken them to Molineux and Goodison Park.

They reckon they have enquired about Cutrone and Everton’s Moise Kean, both of whom have struggled to make an impact.
