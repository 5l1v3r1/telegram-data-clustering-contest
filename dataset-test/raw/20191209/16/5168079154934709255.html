Three held in Oman for drugs

09 Dec 2019, 16:47 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Royal Oman Police in Dhofar have arrested three people on charges of possessing and using narcotic drugs.

In a statement issued by ROP on Monday said:“ Dhofar Governorate Police Command arrested three people for possession and use of narcotic drugs. “

Royal Oman Police confirmed that legal procedures are taken against them and the case is underway.
