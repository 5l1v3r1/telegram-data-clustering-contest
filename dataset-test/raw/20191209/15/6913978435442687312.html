SPA, ATA sign cooperation MoU

09 Dec 2019, 15:04

Saudi Gazette report

RIYADH â€” Abdullah Bin Fahd Al-Hussein, president of the Saudi Press Agency (SPA), and Ms. Armela Krasniqi, director general of the Albanian Telegraphic Agency (ATA), signed a memorandum of understanding for cooperation and exchange of news between the two agencies.

This came during a meeting held at SPA headquarters in Riyadh between Al-Hussein and the ATA director general and her delegation comprising Romelda Rapolari, director of human resources division and Arian Jorgaqi, director of international news division in addition to SPA Vice President Ahmed Bin Ibrahim Al-Awadh, Saudi Press Agency reported.

Later the ATA chief, guided by Al-Hussein, toured SPA departments including the editing desk, e-media, photography department, TV production and media center

Ms. Krasniqi was briefed on the mechanisms of work at the Saudi broadcasting station, as well as the Saudi TV, including their well-equipped channels, studios and main control rooms.

Ms. Krasniqi also paid a get-acquainted visit to the headquarters of Al-Ikhbaria TV channel, listened to an explanation about the mechanism of the channel's work, and viewed its modern studios and technical devices.
