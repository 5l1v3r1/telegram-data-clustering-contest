Newly Engaged Couple Emma Stone and Dave McCary Hold Hands During Sweet NYC Date

09 Dec 2019, 15:38 by Brea Cubit

Emma Stone and Dave McCary have the look of love! On Dec. 7, the newly engaged couple cozied up while heading to a Saturday Night Live afterparty in New York City. Emma, who rocked a navy coat with boots, looked especially smitten as she flashed a bright smile alongside Dave, who wore a jean jacket with dark pants.

Their outing came a few days after the La La Land actress and the SNL writer — who began dating in October 2017 — announced their engagement on Instagram. Dave shared a sweet snapshot of Emma cuddling with him and showing off her gorgeous engagement ring. In honor of the exciting news, look ahead to see more pictures from their date night!
