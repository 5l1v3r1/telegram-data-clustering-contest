Coronation Street episodes will be an hour long this week!

There is a reason for the change!

09 Dec 2019, 15:42 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Listen up, soap fans: episodes of Coronation Street will be an hour long this week!

The soap usually airs two episodes on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays at 7:30pm till 8pm then 8:30 till 9pm but this week the schedule will be a bit different.

On Monday, Wednesday and Friday, hour-long episodes will air at 7:30pm and finish at 8:30pm.

The schedule change is due to new four part series The X Factor: The Bands, which will be airing at 8:30pm.

Despite the schedule change, Corrie fans won't be getting any fewer episodes and there's still plenty of drama to look forward to.

In tonight's episode of the ITV soap (December 9, 2019) Geoff and Yasmeen arrive back from their trip to Las Vegas with some bombshell news - they got married!

However their announcement is soon leaving Tim worried.
