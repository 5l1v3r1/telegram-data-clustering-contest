Thief jumps into canal in Wednesfield

A thief jumped into a canal in an attempt to flee police in Wednesfield.

09 Dec 2019, 15:30 by Jordan Reynolds

Five alleged shoplifters were arrested in total at Bentley Bridge Retail Park yesterday and hundreds of pounds worth of goods were recovered.

Wednesfield Police tweeted saying: "The team have been busy today @BBShoppingPark.

"Five shoplifters arrested. One even jumped into the canal in a failed attempt to get away.

"Hundreds of pounds worth of goods recovered.

"Three of the offenders arrested were also wanted for multiple offences.

"Also big thanks to @ResponseWMP."
