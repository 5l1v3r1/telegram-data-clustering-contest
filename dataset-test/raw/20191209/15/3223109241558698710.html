Watch: What To Expect From ‘Doctor Who’ Season 12

09 Dec 2019, 15:25 by Stephanie Mlot

Only 23 more sleeps until the Doctor Who season premiere. But what can we expect from the new series?

A lot of excitement, according to the show’s cast and crew.

“It’s got action. It’s got scares. It’s got humor. It’s got warmth,” showrunner Chris Chibnall teased in a new video. “And it’s got the TARDIS.”

What more could a geek want?

Well, for starters, how about some more footage of the upcoming series.

The new clip—featuring Chibnall, Jodie Whittaker, Tosin Cole, Mandip Gill, and Bradley Walsh—recycles content from last month’s trailer mixed with talking-head extracts.

“The season is about … the individual characters going on their own journey,” according to Whittaker. “And particularly for the Doctor, it’s a series about self discovery.” 

“It was exciting to revisit certain relationships,” she said, possibly alluding to the return of familiar adversaries like the Cybermen, Judoon, and Racnoss (or maybe just her traveling companions). “But also to have new ones as well.”
