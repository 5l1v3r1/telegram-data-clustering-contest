Lane Kiffin Holds Fan’s Baby, Guy Tells Him To Get A “Burner Phone” (VIDEO)

09 Dec 2019, 15:26 by Darrelle Lincoln

Ole Miss fans are loving their new head coach.

A good number of them showed up to greet new football coach Lane Kiffin when he landed in Oxford on Sunday evening. A fan in attendance handed over his baby to Kiffin in order to take a picture.

Following that picture, Kiffin hanbded the baby back and receives some advice from a guy.

“Get you a burner phone, alright?” the fan said to Kiffin.
