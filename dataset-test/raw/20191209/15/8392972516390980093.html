Former Corrie star Lucy-Jo Hudson says co-parenting with ex-husband Alan Halsall is 'difficult'

She's currently expecting a baby with her new man

09 Dec 2019, 15:58 by Nancy Brown

She's expecting her first baby with new love Lewis Devine and now former Corrie star Lucy-Jo Hudson has opened up about co-parenting with her ex-husband Alan Halsall.

Alan and Lucy-Jo split in 2018 and share six-year-old daughter Sienna-Rae.

Pregnant Lucy-Jo is due to give birth in February, with little Sienna-Rae seemingly thrilled at the prospect of welcoming a baby brother.

However, it appears that it hasn't all been plain sailing for Alan and Lucy-Jo post-split, with the actress revealing that sharing parenting duties with her ex can be "difficult".
