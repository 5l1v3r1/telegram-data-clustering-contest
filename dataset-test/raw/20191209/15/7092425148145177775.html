Woman dies after getting into difficulty while swimming off Antrim coast

09 Dec 2019, 15:12 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

A woman has died after a group of swimmers got into difficulty in the sea off the Antrim coast this morning.

The woman died in the incident off a beach at Ballycastle just after 8.30am.

Another woman is in a stable condition in Coleraine Hospital after being treated for the effects of hypothermia.

Police, fire and rescue service, ambulance crews and the air ambulance were all involved in the emergency response.
