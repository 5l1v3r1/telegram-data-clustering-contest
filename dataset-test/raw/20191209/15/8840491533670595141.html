Mayo garda appeal issued over missing person

09 Dec 2019, 15:01

Gardai in Mayo are appealing for information to help them trace a missing person.

Rachel Dervin (29), originally from Ballina, has been missing from the Castlebar area since Saturday and her family is concerned for her welfare.

She may have travelled to Dublin over the weekend. 

Anyone who has seen Rachel on a bus or train is asked to contact Castlebar Garda Station at 094 - 9038200.
