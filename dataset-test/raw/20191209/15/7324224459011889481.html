Coronation Street star heads to Wednesbury for Christmas lights switch-on - in pictures

Crowds flocked to Wednesbury this weekend for Gallagher Park's first Christmas lights switch-on event.

09 Dec 2019, 15:10 by Rebecca Sayce

Coronation Street star Sam Aston was in attendance, as well as Wednesbury North Councillor Elaine Costigan.

Visitors could enjoy live music acts on stage, as well as inflatables and stalls.
