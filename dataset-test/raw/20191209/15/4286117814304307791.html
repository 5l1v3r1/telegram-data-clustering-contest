Golden Globes 2020 nominations full list: The Crown gets 4 nods, Marriage Story leads with 6

The nominations of the Golden Globes 2020 are here. Marriage Story, The Irishman, The Crown, Chernobyl and Unbelievable have received maximum nominations.

09 Dec 2019, 15:08

The nominations of the 77th Golden Globe Awards have been announced. Olivia Colman's The Crown has secured for nominations. The much talked about TV shows like Chernobyl and Unbelievable too has got four nominations.

In films, it is Scarlett Johannson and Adam Driver's Mariiage Story and Martin Scorsese's The Irishman that has got maximum nominations. Marriage has got six nominations and The Irishman garnered five.
