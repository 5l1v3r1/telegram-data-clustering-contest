UPDATE: NB I-75 reopened in Dayton after earlier multi-vehicle crash

09 Dec 2019, 15:08 by Breaking News Staff

DAYTON — ﻿UPDATE @ 7:50 a.m.: 

Dayton police reopened all lanes of northbound I-75 at North Main Street around 7:50 a.m. following a crash involving multiple vehicles. 

﻿LIVE TRAFFIC UPDATES: Listen to 1290 and 95.7 WHIO

Additional details were not immediately available. 

﻿>>Dayton-area traffic conditions from the WHIO Traffic Center

﻿FIRST REPORT

A crash involving multiple vehicles has shut down all northbound lanes of Interstate 75 at the North Main Street exit in Dayton. 

The crash was reported around 7:15 a.m. Monday with at least three vehicles involved in the crash, according to initial reports. 

At least one person reported injuries in the crash, however their condition was not immediately known. 

Police have blocked all northbound lanes on I-75, however traffic is still able to exit off the highway onto North Main Street. 

We’ll continue to update this story as we learn more.
