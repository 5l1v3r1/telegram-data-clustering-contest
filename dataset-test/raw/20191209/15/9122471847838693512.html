Tom Brady Posts Simple Message After Loss To The Chiefs

09 Dec 2019, 15:49 by Alek Arend

If there’s one thing we all know about Patriots QB Tom Brady, it’s that he won’t let a loss detour him from the team’s goals. New England’s defeat to the Kansas City Chiefs on Sunday was ugly. But Brady is bound to come back better than ever.

The Patriots’ quarterback took to Twitter to post a simple message after the loss. Brady’s clearly already moved on and is ready to get back to winning.

“We’re on to Cincinnati.”
