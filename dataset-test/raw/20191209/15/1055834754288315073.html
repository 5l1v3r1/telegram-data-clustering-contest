New Nintendo Indie World showcase happening on Tuesday

09 Dec 2019, 15:06 by My Nintendo News Admin

Tune in on Tuesday December 10th at 18:00 UK time / 19:00 CET for a new Indie World showcase live stream, featuring roughly 20 minutes of information on some upcoming indie games for Nintendo Switch.

To tune in, check out the Indie World – 10.12.19 (Nintendo Switch) live stream, where the broadcast will be streamed.

Further information about the library of indie titles available for Nintendo Switch can be found on the Indie World Hub on the official Nintendo website.

Source: Nintendo UK
