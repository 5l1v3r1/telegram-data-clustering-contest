One man killed after vehicle rolls on Erastus Durbin Road in Mercer County

09 Dec 2019, 15:08 by Breaking News Staff

MERCER COUNTY — One person was killed after a single-vehicle crash on Erastus Durbin Road near state Route 119 early this morning.

Just before 5 a.m., a 2006 Toyota Tundra was traveling northbound on Erastus Durbin Road near state Route 119 when it went off the west side of the road and rolled, according to reports from the Mercer County Sheriff’s Office.

The driver, Travis Fullenkamp, 39, of Fort Recovery, was thrown from the car and pronounced dead on the scene.

The crash remains under investigation by the Mercer County Sheriff’s Office.
