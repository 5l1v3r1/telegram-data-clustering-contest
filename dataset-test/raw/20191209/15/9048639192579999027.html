Disabled man dies in Franklin Parish fire

09 Dec 2019, 15:52 by KNOE

WISNER, La. (KNOE) - Authorities have identified the victim of a fatal fire in Franklin Parish.

According to the state fire marshal's office, the victim was 72-year-old Ronald H. Bixler of Wisner.

The fire happened in Wisner in the 400 block of Hope Street at 6:30 a.m. on Sunday, December 8, 2019.

Bixler is disabled and was still inside his home when firefighters arrived. Firefighters went inside and found him on the living room floor next to his overturned walker. He was taken to the hospital where he later died.

The case remains open and the fire is still under investigation.
