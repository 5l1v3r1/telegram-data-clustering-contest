Netflix land a HUGE number of 2020 Golden Globe nominations

The streaming service garnered 17 nominations

09 Dec 2019, 14:33 by Clodagh Meaney

Internet streaming platform Netflix are leading the 2020 Golden Globe nominations with a total of 17 nominations.

Marriage Story garnered 6 nominations including Best Motion Picture, Best Performance By An Actress In A Motion Picture, and Best Performance By An Actor In A Motion Picture.

The nominations see Adam Driver nominated for his 2nd Golden Globe and Scarlett Johnson nominated for her 6th.

Martin Scorsese’s The Irishman received 5 nominations including Best Motion Picture.

Both Joe Pesci and Al Pacino were nominated for Best Performance By An Actor In A Motion Picture.

The film was also nominated for Best Director and Best Screenplay.

Another Netflix Original, The Two Popes starring Anthony Hopkins landed 4 nominations.

The film was nominated for Best Motion picture, and also saw Jonathan Pryce nominated for Best Performance By An Actor In A Motion Picture.

Anthony Hopkins was also nominated for a Golden Globe for his role as supporting actor in the film.

Dolemite Is My Name landed two nominations, with Eddie Murphy securing his 5th from the Globes.

The actor was nominated for Best Actor In A Comedy Or Musical, and the film itself landed on the shortlist of nominations for Best Motion Picture.

The Golden Globes take place on January 5th 2020 in L.A.
