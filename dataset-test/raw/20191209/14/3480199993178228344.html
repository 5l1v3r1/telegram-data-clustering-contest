Burglars cause 'horrific' damage at Market Drayton pub - to steal a wheel of cheese

09 Dec 2019, 14:06 by Rory Smith

Burglars ripped out the alarm system to a pub in Market Drayton before prying open doors with a spade.

Thieves targeted The Red Lion Inn on Great Hales Street sometime last night.

Market Drayton Safer Neighbourhood Team reported a wheel of cheese had been stolen, but workers at the pub said parts of the premises had been damaged beyond repair.
