All the Newcastle United rumours as the Magpies look ahead to the winter window under Steve Bruce

09 Dec 2019, 14:45 by Andrew Musgrove

Newcastle United finished the summer transfer window with five new signings joining the club.

Joelinton, Allan Saint-Maximin, Jetro Willems, Emil Krafth and Andy Carroll all joined the Magpies
