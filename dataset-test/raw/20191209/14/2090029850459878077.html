Man bags two-year jail term for stealing boxers

09 Dec 2019, 14:01 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

One Tobechukwu Anyanwu has been sentenced to two years imprisonment for stealing 34 pieces of boxers.

According to reports, the 24-year-old was arraigned for stealing male underpants. He was sentenced by A Magistrate Court in Isolo, Lagos.

Anyanwu reportedly stole 34 pieces of boxers valued N30,600 at the rate of N900 each from Globus Supermarket, Ago-Palace Way, Isolo LCDA and was caught by the security officer on Monday, 25th of November.

He was subsequently handed over to the police officers of Ago Divisional Police Station, Isolo from where he was charged to court.

Anyanwu later pleaded guilty to the offense punishable under section 287 criminal law, volume 44 of Lagos State 2015.

The presiding Magistrate, Olufunmilayo Teluwo sentenced him to two years imprisonment.
