How Nillan’s 3rd birthday party went down in Photos

09 Dec 2019, 14:19 by Pauline Katethya

Can you even!

South Africa based Ugandan business woman Zari Hassan, held an exquisite birthday party for his son Prince Nillan who was turning three years old.

The all white themed party was an invite only ceremony that was attended by Zari’s close friends and relatives.

Judging from the photos, the birthday party looked classy, expensive and glamorous. We are yet to confirm the location of the birthday party held in South Africa.

Nillan is the last born son to Zari and the second of her kids with Tanzania singer Diamond Platnumz.

Diamond and Zari have not been in good terms since February 2018 when they parted ways over a number of issues including cheating allegations. Despite of their differences, it's not yet clear why Diamond didn't attend his son's birthday party.
