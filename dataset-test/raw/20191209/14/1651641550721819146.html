Here is how much the Galaxy S10 Lite, Note10 Lite, Galaxy A71 and A51 will cost

09 Dec 2019, 14:15 by Rahul

Samsung is expected to launch four new Galaxy smartphones, which are Galaxy Note 10 Lite, Galaxy S10 Lite, Galaxy A71, and Galaxy A51, on Thursday this week. While the Galaxy Note 10 Lite and the S10 Lite are going to be more like a flagship smartphone, the Galaxy A71, and Galaxy A51 are going to be what you call “budget-friendly smartphones,” according to a Slovakian shop, where the prices of the Samsung’s December releases leaked.

The price leak in the Slovakian shop suggests that the Galaxy S10 Lite is going to be the most expensive phone, which will be followed by Note 10 Lite. The Galaxy A51 will be the most affordable smartphone, while the A71 will cost a bit more than the A51. You can see the price tag of the upcoming Galaxy smartphones.

  • Galaxy A51: €375(~$415)
  • GalaxyA71: €469(~$519)
  • Galaxy S10 Lite: €669(~$741)
  • Galaxy Note 10 Lite: €609(~$675)

All these three smartphones will be launched on December 12 and are expected to go on sale from December 27. You can read more about the Galaxy S10 Lite, Note 10 Lite, Galaxy A71 by clicking on the links. If you’re interested in the Galaxy A51 you can watch the below video.
