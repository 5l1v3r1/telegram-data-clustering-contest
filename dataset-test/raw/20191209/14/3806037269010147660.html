Here's the Complete List of 2020 Golden Globes Nominees

‘Joker’ lands four nominations including Best Picture.

09 Dec 2019, 14:56 by Mallory Chin

The nominees for the 77th Golden Globes Awards were announced Monday morning from the Beverly Hilton in Hollywood, California, which honor the best films, TV shows, actors, actresses and directors that stood out from the past year. Among this year’s picks for possible Best Film are Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Joker, Marriage Story, The Irishman, and Dolemite Is My Name.

Along with a Best Picture – Drama nod, Todd Phillips’ divisive Joker was also recognised in the Best Actor, Best Director and and Best Original Score categories. On the other-hand Netflix led in total nominations, thanks to a strong showing from the films Marriage Story and The Irishman. The dramas were nominated for six and five awards, respectively.

Over on the small screen, Chernobyl, The Crown, and Unbelievable all led with four nominations each.

View the complete list of 2020 Golden Globes nominees below and let us know your thoughts. The ceremony is set to premiere on January 5 at 8 p.m. EST on NBC and will be hosted by veteran Ricky Gervais.
