Cristiano Ronaldo's son wins Cavour Trophy with Juventus' U9 side

09 Dec 2019, 14:39

- Cristiano Ronaldo's son has won the Cavour Trophy with Juventus' Under 9 side

- The eldest son of the Portugal international scored three goals in this tournament

- He was also named as the best striker by the organizers of the championship

Juventus forward Cristiano Ronaldo will be proud of his son after winning youth tournament with the Old Lady's Under 9 team where he was also named the best striker.

Since Cristiano Jr joined the Juventus' youth team, he has been impressive and has also made headlines many times.

By the end of October, he was sitting on 58 goals and had a further 18 assists in just 28 appearances this season.

Ronaldo's son has now added to his brilliant campaign by winning the Cavour Trophy after beating Chieri 3-2 in the final.

According to the report on Soccer Laduma, Cristiano Jr scored three goals in this championship which earned him the best striker award.
