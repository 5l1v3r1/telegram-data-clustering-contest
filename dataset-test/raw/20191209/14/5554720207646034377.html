Alia Bhatt left frightened after Dad Mahesh Bhatt snaps at reporter, mother Soni Razdan cringes and sister Shaheen’s desperate ‘Papa’ plea falls on deaf ears

09 Dec 2019, 14:14 by JKR Staff

Actress Alia Bhatt was left stunned after her filmmaker father lost his at a reporter during a press conference to discuss sister Shaheen Bhatt’s book I’ve Never Been (Un)Happier. Alia was so scared witnessing her father’s extraordinary outbursts that she was even heard saying, “I warned you that this was gonna happen.” While Shaheen made a desperate plea for Bhatt to calm down as she said ‘Papa,’ Alia’s mother Soni Razdan looked visibly uncomfortable sitting next to her husband. The video of Mahesh Bhatt’s unexpected outbursts has now gone viral.

In the video, a reporter is heard asking the renowned filmmaker, “So you don’t really want answers.” This appeared to evoke an angry response from him as he yelled, “There are no answers! There are pretenders who claim to have answers and they institutionalise those answers and they impose those answers with the butt-end of a gun.”

Mahesh Bhatt continued, “There are no answers. Mankind doesn’t have answers. You push your children. You don’t follow things that you pretend to preach to them…I never pretended to my children that I was in possession of the virtues that people flaunt.”

As Mahesh Bhatt continued with his non-stop diatribe, Soni Razdan was seen cringing with discomfort.
