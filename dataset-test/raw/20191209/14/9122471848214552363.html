Kirk Herbstreit’s Message For Fans Of Oregon, Wisconsin

09 Dec 2019, 14:42 by Andrew Holleran

There is no more picturesque setting in sports than the Rose Bowl on New Year’s Day. Oregon and Wisconsin fans will get to experience that setting this year.

The Ducks and the Badgers are set to meet in the Rose Bowl on Jan. 1. Oregon is coming off a win in the Pac-12 Championship Game, while Wisconsin is coming off a close loss to Ohio State.

ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit tweeted a message for Oregon and Wisconsin fans ahead of the game.

“Please do yourself a favor (and) go to this magical location to watch your team in the best setting sports. It’s my favorite assignment I get every year!” he wrote.
