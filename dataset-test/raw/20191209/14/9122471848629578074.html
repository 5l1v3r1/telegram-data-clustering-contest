ESPN’s Score Predictions For The College Football Playoff

09 Dec 2019, 14:12 by Andrew Holleran

Bowl season is here. The college football’s postseason schedule was announced in full on Sunday. The College Football Playoff will of course be the highlight of the season.

LSU is set to play Oklahoma in the Peach Bowl, while Ohio State will take on Clemson in the Fiesta Bowl. The winners will meet in the national title game.

The Tigers are a sizable favorite in their contest, while the Buckeyes opened as a small favorite in theirs.

ESPN’s Adam Rittenberg has made his score predictions for every bowl game. Here’s what he’s got in the playoff games.

LSU vs. Oklahoma

Score prediction: LSU 42, Oklahoma 27

No. 2 Ohio State vs. No. 3 Clemson

Score prediction: Clemson 34, Ohio State 30
