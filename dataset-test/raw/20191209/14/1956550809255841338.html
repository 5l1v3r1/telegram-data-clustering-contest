The world's first Warner Bros Hotel is opening on Abu Dhabi’s Yas Island

The hotel will be open in 2021

09 Dec 2019, 14:01

The world’s first Warner Bros. themed hotel will open in Abu Dhabi in 2021.

Work on the WB Hotel on Yas Island is already underway, and is almost 40 percent complete.

Located next to the record-breaking Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi theme park, the hotel will offer tourists and residents the chance to enjoy a unique stay in Abu Dhabi.

Designed and themed throughout with all of Warner Bros. most famous characters, the hotel itself will be another attraction on Yas Island that will gain attention in the region and across the world.

The WB Hotel will be home to 250 rooms over eight levels and will feature Warner Bros.-themed restaurants, a spa, fitness club, ballroom and rooftop pool.

“Following the successful debut of Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi, we are thrilled to expand our presence in the region with the studio’s first-ever branded hotel,” said Pam Lifford, president of Warner Bros. Global Brands and Experiences.

“This new venture will pay tribute to Warner Bros.’ legacy of rich entertainment, offering fans a unique way to experience their favourite brands and characters.”

More announcements and details are expected in the coming months and Time Out will keep you up to date with all the developments as the hotel nears its opening date.
