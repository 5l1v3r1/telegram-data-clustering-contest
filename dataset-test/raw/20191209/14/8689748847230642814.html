KiDi reveals the ‘identity’ of his baby mama

09 Dec 2019, 14:21 by Adu Sarpeah

KiDi of Lynx Entertainment fame most recently made the internet colorful when he uploaded photos of his 3-year-old son on his birthday celebration.

KiDi shared a photo of the child with a caption;

“Happy birthday to my Mini-Me. Young Sugar. 3 years on and you’ve brought me nothing but happiness and blessings. I bless the day you came into my life. Daddy loves you to the moon and back.”

Fast forward, fans of KiDi started asking and yearning to know more about the lucky lady in his life. Detailing about his baby mama in the studios of Hitz Fm with Andy Dosty, KiDi posited that the mother of his son who’s older than him in age doesn’t stay in Ghana.

More importantly, KiDi made it emphatic that they aren’t together but are co-parenting in order to give the child the best of upbringing. According to him, his baby mama is in abroad; reason he has not gotten the opportunity to take a photo with him.
