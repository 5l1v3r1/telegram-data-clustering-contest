eFootball PES 2020 Data Pack 3.0 is now available for download

09 Dec 2019, 11:50 by John Papadopoulos

Konami has released a brand new Data Pack for its latest soccer title, eFootball PES 2020. According to the team, Data Pack 3 adds new stadium updates, new National kits, player faces and other in-game content.

Going into more details, the eFootball PES 2020 Data Pack 3.0 adds multiple new player faces, providing over 30 stars with realistic and highly detailed updates. Furthermore, it brings multiple stadium updates – including improvements to Cicero Pompeu de Toldeo (São Paulo), Old Trafford (Manchester United) and Allianz Stadium (Juventus FC). Not only that, but it adds the Arena do Grêmio.

Data Pack 3.0 also includes multiple new player faces, providing over 30 stars with realistic and highly detailed updates. Moreover, it brings updates to National uniforms. For instance, Germany, Spain, Japan, Northern Ireland, Wales, Belgium and Scotland all receive their new Adidas first team kits.

Last but not least, Data Pack 3 adds four new adidas boots.

As always, Steam will download this pack the next time you launch its client.

Enjoy!
