Godzilla Vs. Kong Leak Shows the Iconic Giants Fighting

09 Dec 2019, 11:12 by Sundi Rose

Warner Bros. debuted footage from Godzilla vs. Kong at Comic Con Experience in Brazil, which was subsequently leaked online and then promptly removed. However, a screencap from said footage is still making the rounds.

The image features both Godzilla and Kong facing off on top of what looks like an aircraft carrier, but it seems like Kong has grown quite significantly since his appearance in Kong: Skull Island. Fans might remember seeing Kong's parents' bones in that film, which -- coupled with a line of expository dialogue -- alluded to the gorilla still having some growing to do.
