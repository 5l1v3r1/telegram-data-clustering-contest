Arjun Suravaram Leads The Second Weekend, Emerges Success

09 Dec 2019, 11:56 by Siddartha Toleti

For the second weekend in a row, the Nikhil starrer Arjun Suravaram has emerged as box office topper. It stood as first, including all the new releases that hit cinemas this past Friday.

90 ML starring Karthikeya was supposed to emerge as the number one choice. It opened decently on the first day. However, subsequently, it had a crash due to poor word of mouth. It was across the board. The Sunday too was flat with no significant jump.

Arjun Suravaram, on the other hand, dropped at the end of its first week. The second Friday maintained at low levels, but it jumped again on the second Sunday. The momentum was maintained on Sunday as well. Overall, it has achieved a good total and breakeven status in a few areas. It makes the long-delayed movie a success.
