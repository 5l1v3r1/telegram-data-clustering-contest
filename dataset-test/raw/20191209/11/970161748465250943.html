Raheem Sterling’s emotional speech at Great Gatsby themed 25th birthday party

09 Dec 2019, 11:11 by Alicia Adejobi

Raheem Sterling threw a lavish Great Gatsby themed party to celebrate his 25th birthday this weekend and, of course, it looked like no expenses were spared to make it a night to remember.

The Manchester City player, who earns an estimated £300,000 per week, hosted the bash at Manchester’s Hilton Hotel on Sunday night.

Posing on the red carpet with his fiancee Paige Milian, Raheem looked particularly dapper as he channelled 1920s dress in a white suit with a waistcoat and a fur-trimmed coat. Paige, with whom he shares two children, looked stunning in a champagne-coloured flapper-style gown with a white feather in her hair.

Among the A-listers in attendance including rap duo Krept & Konan, and Raheem’s Man City teammates including Gabriel Jesus who arrived with his model girlfriend Fernanda Queiroz, Fernandinho and his wife Glaucia Roza, Ederson and his pregnant wife Lais, and Ilkay Gundogan and Kyle Walker.

Inside the bash, photos from Raheem’s memorable photoshoot with GQ magazine decorated the venue, while waiters and waitresses dressed in flapper-style outfits were on-hand to dish out the bubbles. Guests could also entertain themselves in the casino room.

Joining the DJ in one room was a violinist while a live band with trumpets and drums performed on the dancefloor as Raheem showed his famous dance moves.

Somehow, he managed to avoid knocking over his nearby four-tier birthday cake while dancing and picking up money which had been sprayed on the dancefloor.

Later, the footballer delivered a speech thanking his nearest and dearest for having his back.

‘I swear to god I thank the Lord for my life, I thank the Lord for my kids’ life, Thiago, Melody, Thai-Cruz, Paige Milian, big p your bumbaclart,’ Raheem said mentioning his immediate family.

He added: ‘Of course, like everybody else, your mums are the most important thing.

‘My mum showed me the way, how to be strong, how to be wise, how to live up. Hold your way.’

Despite Man City’s defeat earlier in the day, Raheem has lots to celebrate as he and Paige recently announced they had secretly welcomed their second child together, Thai-Cruz, 11 months ago.

The couple is also parents to their eldest son Thiago, three, while Raheem has a daughter, Melody, from a previous relationship.

In a sweet tribute to her fiance on his birthday, Paige posted on Instagram: ‘HAPPY 25TH BIRTHDAYYYY BABE @sterling7 no need for a paragraph yuh done know how di ting go already. LOVE YOU MORE THAN YOU’LL EVER KNOW [sic].’

The pair got engaged in 2018 and are expected to get married in 2020.
