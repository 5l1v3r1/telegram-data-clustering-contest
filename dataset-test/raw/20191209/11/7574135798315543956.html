Demetri Martin announces tour dates; Ali Wong adds 6th NYC show

09 Dec 2019, 11:22 by Bill Pearis

Demetri Martin is continuing his Wandering Mind Tour into 2020, with dates in January, February and April in North American cities including Fort Lauderdale, Atlantic City, Baltimore, New Haven, Toronto, Providence, Albany and more. There are two Brooklyn shows at The Bell House on April 7 & 8. Tickets for The Bell House shows go on sale Wednesday, December 11 at 10 AM.

All dates are listed, along with a trailer for Demetri’s 2018 Netflix special The Overthinker, below.

Meanwhile, Ali Wong is continuing her Milk & Money Tour in 2020 as well, and she’s added a sixth NYC show to it, which happens at Beacon Theatre on March 30. Tickets go on sale Friday December 13 at 10 AM, with various presales starting Tuesday December 10 at 10 AM.
