STUNNER OR BUMMER: Katrina Kaif, Alia Bhatt, Janhvi Kapoor, Vedhika Kumar Or Sunny Leone?

09 Dec 2019, 11:17 by Aditi Singh

Meet the ladies who kept the fashion quotient high and those who turned off the heat

 Katrina Kaif was spotted at the private airport with Salman Khan as the duo landed in the city. Vedhika Kumar was out for the promotions of her film, The Body, with Emraan Hashmi. Alia Bhatt's pictures dressed in all ethnic wear, made way to the Internet while Janhvi Kapoor was papped as she headed towards her car. 

Sunny Leone looked pleasant as she waved to the shutterbugs in the city. So, let's find out who were the stunners and bummers of the day: 

KATRINA KAIF- STUNNER- Kaif's latex pants with a loose black top is quite sexy, especially the high ponytail. 

VEDHIKA KUMAR- BUMMER- Vedhika has got the look wrong in that short frilly frock. 

SUNNY LEONE- STUNNER- Sunny keeps it casual and cool in green tee and loose denims.

ALIA BHATT- BUMMER- Alia's polka dotted green Sabyasachi suit is way too much polka dots in the eye. The jewellery is also non-flattery.

JANHVI KAPOOR- STUNNER- Janvhi rocks the short white dress.
