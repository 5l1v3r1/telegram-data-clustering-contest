Crisis: Ralph Dibny Jokingly References Classic DC Superteam in New Footage

09 Dec 2019, 11:12 by Sam Stone

One episode in and "Crisis on Infinite Earths" has already lived up to its promise as the biggest, most ambitious television crossover event of all time, gathering the heroes from across DC's entire history of TV and film.

Now, the CW has released a new behind-the-scenes featurette showing the various casts assembling and comparing notes and costumes interspersed with short clips of previously unseen footage. Among them is an off-the-cuff remark from Ralph Dibney as he presumably meets Kingdom Come Superman for the first time.
