Funke Akindele loses dad

09 Dec 2019, 11:16 by Sanya Agunbiade

Funke Akindele loses dad: Nollywood actress, Funke Akindele has just announced the death of her father.

Taking to her Instagram page, the actress revealed that she did her best to ensure that her father lived, but unfortunately he was snatched by the cold hands of death.

Though the actress didn’t disclose the cause of death, she mentioned that God knows best and how she wished she could turn back the hands of time.

She wrote:

”Dad!!! May your soul Rest In Peace!! I tried Dad! I did!! We love you but God knows best!!! #iwish i can turn back the hands of time”.
