Star Screen Awards 2019 Winners' List; Ranveer Singh, Alia Bhatt, Ayushmann Khurrana Bag Awards

09 Dec 2019, 11:47 by Waiz Ahmed

The Star Screen Awards 2019 was held yesterday (December 8) in Mumbai. The prestigious award ceremony was graced by the biggest stars and dignitaries of the film industry. The tinsel town fraternity was gathered together to celebrate and honour the best of cinema from 2019. The list of winners of Star Screen Awards 2019 are as follows
