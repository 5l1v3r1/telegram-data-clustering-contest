Battle of Ohio: Browns win third straight over Bengals

09 Dec 2019, 11:10 by John Boyle - Staff Writer

The Cleveland Browns won their third straight game over the Cincinnati Bengals, scoring a 27-19 victory Sunday at FirstEnergy Stadium.

Nick Chubb rushed for 106 yards and Kareem Hunt added touchdown run as the Browns (6-7) kept their faint playoff hopes alive.

Denzel Ward returned an interception 61 yards for a score and the Browns defense limited the Bengals (1-12) to three field goals and a touchdown on five red-zone trips. The Browns stopped Andy Dalton on a fourth-and-goal quarterback draw from the 5 in the fourth quarter.

Cincinnati’s Joe Mixon rushed for 146 yards and a TD.

It was the Bengals’ ninth straight AFC North loss and their 11th straight road loss.

The teams meet again in the final game of the regular season in Cincinnati.
