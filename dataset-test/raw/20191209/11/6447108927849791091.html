A Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker scene is to be shown in Fortnite

09 Dec 2019, 11:32 by Tuffcub

Fortnite players will be able to view an exclusive scene from Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker next weekend. The scene, the contents of which have not been revealed, will play at 7pm UK time on Saturday 14th December, and ‘doors open’ thirty minutes earlier so you can reserve your place and stock up on popcorn.

This not the first tie in Fortnite has had with the upcoming movie, a Star Destroyer was spotted in the the sky above the island and you can buy a Stormtrooper outfit in the game.
