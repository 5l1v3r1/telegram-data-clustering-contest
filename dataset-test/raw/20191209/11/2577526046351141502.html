Fortnite Will Show an Exclusive Star Wars Scene This Weekend

09 Dec 2019, 11:26 by Alistair Jones

I’m still trekking across the galaxy with Cal Kestis and pals in Jedi: Fallen Order, which means that the upcoming release of The Rise of Skywalker has largely snuck up on me. To help boost the hype, however, Fortnite will be showing off an exclusive scene from the movie at its in-game cinema.

In a tweet over the weekend, the battle royale’s official account confirmed that the Stormtrooper and Star Destroyer appearances would be followed up by the scene premiere on December 14. The event will take place at Risky Reels, Fortnite’s drive-in movie theatre, at 7pm GMT, but doors will open 30 minutes beforehand.
