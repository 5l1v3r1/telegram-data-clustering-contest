Amanda Holden addresses 'issues' with Phillip Schofield amid This Morning row

09 Dec 2019, 11:50 by Emma Kelly

Amanda Holden has thrown major shade at Phillip Schofield amid claims that multiple This Morning presenters have complained about his attitude.

According to reports by The Sun, Ruth Langsford made an official complaint about the 57-year-old, while his relationship with co-host Holly Willoughby has allegedly become strained.

The report claims eight big ITV presenters have spoken to them about the ‘toxic’ atmosphere at This Morning, with one household name saying: ‘For so long people have been silenced because they are scared for their job. If they speak out publicly about the atmosphere they fear there could be repercussions.’

And amid the drama, Amanda – who has had her share of run-ins with Phil in the past – has said she ‘admires’ anybody who sticks up for themselves.

The Britain’s Got Talent star was hosting Heart Breakfast alongside Jamie Theakston and Ashley Roberts when the subject of the ITV wars came up.
