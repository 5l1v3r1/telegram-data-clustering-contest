Wales to play Austria to kick off Euro 2020 preparations

Wales will take on Austria in March 2020

09 Dec 2019, 11:31 by Katie Sands

Wales will face Austria in a friendly next March to step up preparations for Euro 2020, it's been announced.

Ryan Giggs' men will take on the Austrians on Friday, March 27, 2020, in their "first preparation match of the year" ahead of facing Switzerland, Turkey and Italy in the European Championships.

It will allow the Wales manager a chance to shape his team in time for his first tournament as Wales boss.
