Watch: Manipur’s Rex Singh grabs eight wickets on opening day of Ranji Trophy’s new season

09 Dec 2019, 11:59 by Scroll Staff

Manipur fast bowler Rex Singh is once again making heads turn. On Monday, the left-arm pacer starred with the ball during Manipur’s match against Mizoram on the opening day of 2019-’20 Ranji Trophy season at Jadavpur University Ground in Salt Lake on Monday.

Batting first in the Plate Group match, Mizoram were bundled out for just 65 in just under an hour with Rex Singh being the wrecker-in-chief. The 19-year-old registered figures of 8-4-22-8 and five of the batsmen who he dismissed, were out for nought.
