Watchmen: The End Is Nigh in Season Finale Promo

09 Dec 2019, 11:12 by Ian Cardona

WARNING: The following contains spoilers for Watchmen Episode 8, "A God Walks Into Abar," which premiered Sunday on HBO.

HBO has released the promo for the upcoming Season 1 finale of Watchmen, "See How They Fly."

The trailer doesn't offer any dialogue teases and instead paces everything to the rhythm of a ticking clock. As all of the threads that have been running throughout the season come together, the promo teases the series is counting down to something massive.
