I’m A Celeb winner Jacqueline Jossa returns to social media for the first time with Dan Osborne

"I cannot quite believe that it's happening"

09 Dec 2019, 11:32 by Sarah Magliocco

I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here winner Jacqueline Jossa has returned to social media.

The star was not allowed access to her phone while in the jungle, and has been off social media for three weeks.

The star shared videos reuniting with her children after her 23 day stint on the reality show.

“Hello everybody, thank you so much for voting for me. If you voted it means the absolute world. I cannot quite believe that it’s happening,” she told her followers.”

“I just want to let you all know I am absolutely fine and it’s been the best experience of my whole entire life… and my babies are here!” she exclaimed, showing her children on camera.

She then shared a video of husband Dan Osborne playing with their two daughters.

While Jacqueline was in the jungle, Dan was forced to deny multiple cheating scandal rumours.

The star was first alleged to have had a fling with Love Island star Gabby Allen. Both Dan and Gabby have denied the claims.

Last week, model Chloe Ayling claimed that she had a threesome with Dan and fellow model Natalie Nunn. Both Natalie and Dan have denied the allegations.
