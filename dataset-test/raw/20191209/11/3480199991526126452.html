Shrewsbury woman, 21, charged over Co-op robbery

09 Dec 2019, 11:04 by Dominic Robertson

A 21-year-old Shrewsbury woman has been charged with robbing a Co-op store and possessing a fake gun.

Adina McLean, was charged by police in connection with an incident at around 7.30pm on Saturday, November 30, at the Co-op store in Shrewsbury Road, Bomere Heath.

A spokesman for West Mercia Police said McLean, 21, of Crowmere Green, Shrewsbury, had been charged with robbery and possession of an imitation firearm.

McLean appeared at Kidderminster Magistrates Court today.

She was remanded in custody and will be produced to crown court in the next two days.
