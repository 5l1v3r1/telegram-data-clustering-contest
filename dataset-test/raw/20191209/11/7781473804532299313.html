2020 Tata Altroz: Variants explained

09 Dec 2019, 11:09 by Team OD  

We've just driven the newest offering from Tata Motors, the Tata Altroz hatchback. The Altroz is a premium sub-four-metre hatchback that competes with the likes of the Maruti Suzuki Baleno, Honda Jazz and the Hyundai i20.

The new Tata offering comes with two engine options. Both BSVI compliant. The 1.5-litre Revotorq turbo-diesel 4-cylinder is a detuned version of the one found in the Nexon and makes 90PS at 4,000rpm and 200Nm at 1,250-3,000rpm. The petrol motor is the 1.2-litre Revotron 3-cylinder petrol, as seen on the Tiago, with 86PS at 6,000rpm and 113Nm at 3,300rpm. Both come paired with a 5-speed manual gearbox.

There are four variants on offer, on both powertrain options. Further, you can choose numerous feature packages with some variants.
