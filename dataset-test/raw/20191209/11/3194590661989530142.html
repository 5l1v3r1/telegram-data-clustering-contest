PHOTOS: Lai Mohammed, Omotola Jalade grace EbonyLife’s ‘Your Excellency’ movie premiere

09 Dec 2019, 11:09 by Adedayo Ade-Rufus

‘Your Excellency’, a movie by Mo Abudu, Nigerian film producer, held its grand premiere on Sunday at the EbonyLife place, a newly-built cinema in Victoria Island.

The political drama also doubles as Funke Akindele’s directorial debut.

Celebrities dazzled on the red carpet of the movie premiere with distinguished looks that included tuxedos, ball gowns and traditional attires in different arrays of colours and designs.

Some of the guests in attendance include Lai Mohammed, minister of information and culture, Abdulrasheed ‘JJC Skillz’ Bello, Ramsey Nouah, Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde, Deyemi Okanlawon, Alexx Ekubo, Eku Edewor, Osas Ighodaro, Chioma ‘Chigul’ Omeruah, Funke Akindele-Bello.

The movie will be available in cinemas from December 13, 2019.
