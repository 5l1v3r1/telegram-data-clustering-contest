Radiohead’s Ed O’Brien playing North American shows

09 Dec 2019, 12:16 by Amanda Hatfield

Radiohead guitarist Ed O’Brien is preparing to release a solo album as EOB early in 2020, and he’s just announced his first North American tour dates supporting it. In February, he’ll play four shows, stopping in Toronto, Chicago, NYC, and Los Angeles. The NYC show happens at Le Poisson Rouge on February 10, and tickets go on general sale Friday, December 13 at 10 AM, with a Spotify presale starting Wednesday December 11 at 10 AM.
