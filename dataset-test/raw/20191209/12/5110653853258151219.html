College football bowl schedule: Dates, times, TV channels for all 40 FBS bowls in 2019-20

09 Dec 2019, 12:34 by Bill Bender

The 2019-20 college football bowl schedule featuring 40 games between FBS teams — and the Celebration Bowl — is here.

This year's College Football Playoff semifinal games will be on Dec. 28 in the Peach and Fiesta bowls. The College Football Playoff championship game is Jan. 13 at the Mercedes-Benz Superdome in New Orleans.

Sporting News has the schedule for all 40 bowls, with teams, dates, times and TV here.
