Forget Half Life Alyx: Twin Peaks VR is here and it’s as weird as you’d expect

09 Dec 2019, 12:16 by George Storr

Twin Peaks VR - Image credit: WelcomeToTwinPeaks.com on YouTube , Showtime and Collider Games.

The official trailer for the Twin Peaks Virtual Reality game is here and as you would expect it is very, very strange.  

Following its announcement last year, fans of Twin Peaks have been eagerly anticipating the franchise’s VR game. The short, confusing trailer promises that players will “collect Easter eggs” and “discover hidden secrets”.

One of the “hidden secrets” we’re keen to uncover is: What on earth is going on in this trailer?
