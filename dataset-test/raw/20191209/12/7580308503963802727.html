Funke Akindele Loses Dad

09 Dec 2019, 12:15 by Michael Bamidele

Nigerian actress and filmmaker Funke Akindele has lost her dad.

The “Jenifa Diary” actress took to her Instagram page today to announce the passing away of her father. She posted a photo of herself with her late father who was a retired school principal, along with the caption:

“Dad!!! May your soul Rest In Peace!! I tried Dad! I did!! We love you but God knows best!!! #iwishicanturnbackthehandsoftime”

Funke Akindele has over 100 films to her credit as actress, writer, and producer. She came into the limelight after starring in popular United Nations Population Fund (UNFPA)-sponsored sitcom “I Need to Know”, which ran from 1998 to 2002.

Her big break came in 2008 when she appeared in the film “Jenifa” which has spurn the comedy series “Jenifa’s Diary”.
