Dakota Fanning Announces the 2020 Golden Globe Nominees - See The Full List Now!

09 Dec 2019, 12:41

Dakota Fanning reads out some of the nominees during the 2020 Golden Globe Awards Nominations Announcement held at The Beverly Hilton Hotel on Monday morning (December 9) in Beverly Hills, Calif.

The 25-year-old actress announced the nominations alongside Tim Allen and This is Us star Susan Kelechi Watson. Golden Globes Ambassadors Dylan and Paris Brosnan were also at the event.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Dakota Fanning

JJJ faves Joey King and Kaitlyn Dever are nominees!

The annual award show is set to air on January 5th, 2020, with Ricky Gervais taking on hosting duties once again.
