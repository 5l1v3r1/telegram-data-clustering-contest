Shah Rukh Khan Congratulates Indonesian Actor Who Dedicated His Award To King Khan; SRK Says ‘Will Meet Soon’

09 Dec 2019, 12:51 by Lubna Khan

An Indonesian actor Muhammad Khan dedicated his award to Shah Rukh Khan and gave a speech in which he mentioned that SRK is the reason why he became an actor and that he hopes to meet him. The video reached King Khan and he congratulated him, saying that he would meet soon

Shah Rukh Khan is one of the most popular stars not just in India, but all over the world. He has a massive fan following and his fans go to great lengths to just get a glimpse of him. His fans wait for King Khan outside his house Mannat for hours, waiting for him to come out and greet them. While we didn’t get to see SRK on the big screen this year, fans are eagerly waiting for him to announce his next. He was last seen in Aanand L Rai’s Zero, also starring Katrina Kaif and Anushka Sharma. However, the actor keeps updating his fans on social media, and is also often seen responding to his fans, or wishing them on certain occasions. Now, King Khan came across the video of an Indonesian actor, who is a big SRK fan, and congratulated him.

An Indonesian actor Muhammad Khan dedicated his award to Shah Rukh Khan and gave a speech during the award ceremony in which he mentioned that SRK is the reason why he became an actor and that he hopes to meet him. The video also shows him singing one of his songs, Mere Mehboob Mere Sanam from his film Duplicate. Now, the video reached Shah Rukh Khan through one of the fan pages, and Kin Khan shared it. The video’s caption reads, “I dedicate this award to King Khan. You are the reason why I became an actor. I hope I can meet you": Muhammad Khan (an Indonesian new actor who won Piala Citra for Best Actor in a Leading Role).
