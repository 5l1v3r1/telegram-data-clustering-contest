Saoirse Ronan & 'Little Women' Cast Step Out in NYC After Golden Globes Nominations!

09 Dec 2019, 12:32

Saoirse Ronan has scored another nomination!

The 25-year-old actress and her co-stars – Florence Pugh, Eliza Scanlen, Laura Dern and director Greta Gerwig – all got together to make an appearance on SiriusXM’s Town Hall in support of their film Little Women hosted by SiriusXM’s Jess Cagle on Monday (December 9) in New York City.

Saoirse Ronan and the ladies all sat down for an interview moments after it was announced that she had just scored a Best Actress – Drama nomination for her role in the film at the 2020 Golden Globes.

While Saoirse scored a nomination for her performance, Greta was snubbed in her category for Best Director.
