Utility work to close two Marco Island roads next week

09 Dec 2019, 12:43 by WFTX Digital Team

MARCO ISLAND, Fla. -- The City of Marco Island is warning drivers of two road closures that begin next Monday.

The city says the closures will begin at 9 a.m. on Monday, December 16, and last through Wednesday, December 18 at 5 p.m.

The closed roads will be 6th Avenue (between E. Elkcam Circle and Marco Lake Drive); and E. Elkcam Circle (between Collier Boulevard and Tallwood Street).

Both lanes will be closed to traffic during that period.

The closures are scheduled to allow crews to work on a newly constructed sanitary sewer forcemain.
