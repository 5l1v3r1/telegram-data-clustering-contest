Dubai’s Dido concert moved to Coca-Cola Arena

The gig on Friday (December 13) has been relocated

09 Dec 2019, 12:44 by Amy Mathieson

British singer Dido is coming to Dubai this Friday (December 13) – and her gig will now take place at the Coca-Cola Arena.

The singer was previously scheduled to take to the stage at Dubai Media City Amphitheatre, but the gig has today (Monday December 9) been relocated due to unforeseen weather.

It will be the first time the singer will be visiting the emirate, as part as her “Still On My Mind’ comeback tour.

And after 15 years off the touring circuit, it’s sure to be a welcome return for fans. The British singer has sold more than 40 million records worldwide, and hit the spotlight in 1999 with her debut album No Angel, which sold more than 21 million copies. It also won her several awards including Best British Female and Best British Album at the BRITS. She has won four BRIT Awards in total.

It’s the final stop on a global tour for the singer, and Dubai fans can look forward to more than two decades worth of tunes, including Thank You, Life For Rent, No Angel, Here With Me and White Flag.
