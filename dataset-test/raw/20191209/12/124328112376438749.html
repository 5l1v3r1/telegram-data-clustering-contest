Tottenham Hotspur's £65,000-a-week football star Davinson Sanchez Mina must pay £276 after being caught speeding in his Land Rover

09 Dec 2019, 12:57 by James Gant For Mailonline
  • The Colombian defender, 23, was clocked on a speed camera at 11pm, February 2
  • Willesden Magistrates Court heard £42m player was driving 62mph in a 50 zone
  • Sanchez, of Barnet, fined £146, with £100 costs and to pay £30 victim surcharge

Tottenham Hotspur star Davinson Sanchez Mina has been given three points on his driver's licence after he was caught speeding in his Land Rover.

The Colombian defender, 23, was caught driving his three-litre diesel 4x4 over the speed limit on February 2.

Willesden Magistrates Court heard the £42million signing was recorded driving at 62mph on the 50mph North Circular Road in North Finchley at 11.00pm.

Sanchez Mina, of Barnet was fined £146, with £100 costs and ordered to pay a £30 victim surcharge as well as his licence being endorsed with three penalty points.

The centre back was snapped up by Spurs in 2017 and was a record signing at the time.

He is paid £65,000 a week, which works out just over £3million a year.

Sanchez Mina was landed in hot water earlier this year when he kicked out at Arsenal defender Laurent Koscielny during a north London derby.

But he did not face retrospective punishment from the Football Association for his actions in the March game.
