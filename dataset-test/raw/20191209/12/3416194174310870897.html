DC Universe's 'Stargirl' Gets First Teaser Trailer - Watch Here!

09 Dec 2019, 12:52

The first little teaser trailer for Stargirl is here!

The new visual introduces Courtney (Brec Bassinger) and her pals with a voiceover of Courtney saying to her friends that “the staff chose me, and I choose you’.

If you don’t know, Stargirl centers on Courtney Whitmore, who inspires an unlikely group of young heroes to stop the villains of the past.

The series will premiere on DC Universe in the second quarter of 2020 with new episodes released weekly, and after each episode airs there, it will also be broadcast on The CW.
