09 Dec 2019, 12:07 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Malaika Arora is sure to impress you with pictures from her latest photoshoot where she is playing with pop colours while she shines in a mesmerising silver outfit. The Bollywood diva, who known for her sense of fashion took to social media to share a handful of glimpses which are screaming glamour.

The 46-year-old diva still gives young actresses a run for their money with her well-toned body and impeccable sense of style. She even grabs everyone's attention with chic athleisure whenever she steps out for her workout sessions.

In her latest photoshoot, she can be seen donning a shimmery silver outfit with statement earrings and tresses all set back in a pony. She completed her with similar coloured metallic shoes.

Meanwhile, the diva is also grabbing a lot of attention for her relationship with Arjun Kapoor, whose recent release 'Panipat' has been doing wonders at the box office. While rumours about their marriage are making rounds on social media but the stars have rubbished marriage rumours saying that they are in no hurry to take the next step.
