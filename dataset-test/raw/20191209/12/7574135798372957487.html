Kaytranada releasing LP ft. Pharrell, Tinashe, Mick Jenkins & more on Friday; “10%” ft. Kali Uchis out now

09 Dec 2019, 12:50 by Amanda Hatfield

Montreal producer Kaytranada has remained busy over the past few years, but he still hasn’t released an album since his great 2016 debut 99.9% As he just announced, that will finally change this Friday (12/13) with the release of his of his sophomore album Bubba, due via RCA Records. That’s the cover art above, and you can see the tracklisting below.

It features a ton of cool guests, including Pharrell Williams, Tinashe, GoldLink, Charlotte Day Wilson, Estelle, VanJess, Masego, Mick Jenkins, SiR, Iman Omaari, Masego, and more. Along with the announcement, he shared the Kali Uchis collab “10%,” a head-nod-inducing dose of shuffling, thumping R&B. Listen below.
