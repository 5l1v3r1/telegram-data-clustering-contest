Sarkodie, Shatta Wale, and Patoranking failed to perform at Cardi B’s concert

09 Dec 2019, 12:05 by Qwame Benedict

Cardi B has finally left the shores of Ghana but news about her concert keeps coming every second.

According to a new report coming in and fast going viral, some artistes that were billed to perform at the show yesternight where actually bounced and couldn’t mount the stage to perform to the huge crowd that had gathered at the stadium.

The source revealed that Nigerian superstar Patoranking alongside Ghana’s Sarkodie and Shatta Wale failed to mount the stage without giving the reason why.

A member of Sarkodie’s Sarknative posted; “Sarkodie couldn’t perform at the Cardi B show but this reaction from the crowd when “Saara” was being played is everything ?? #BlackLove”.

As it stands now, no one from Shatta Wale’s camp has said anything on why their boss after hiring a helicopter to the venue for soundcheck failed to perform.

On the part of Patoranking, he was seen angrily leaving the venue after he was also prevented from performing.
