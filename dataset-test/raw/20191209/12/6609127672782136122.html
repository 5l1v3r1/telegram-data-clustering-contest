Rangers vs Celtic: Liam Burt shuts down Gers fan who called him ‘dirty fenian b******’

09 Dec 2019, 12:59 by Aidan Scott

CELTIC ace Liam Burt has shut down a Rangers fan who aimed sectarian abuse at him.

The midfielder, 20, crossed the Old Firm divide earlier this year after being released by the Light Blues.

And the boyhood Hoops fan celebrated their Betfred Cup victory over his former club yesterday afternoon.

Sharing Celtic's tweet about being champions, he said: "Beautiful Sunday."

However, a Rangers fan named Davie, with the username @Coplandfront54, then responded by aiming sectarian abuse at Burt.

He tweeted: "Dirty fenian b******."

 

But Burt was quick to reply as he simply said: "And I love it."

The Rangers fan's tweet is no longer available and he appears to have deleted his account in the hours since the abuse.

While the Celtic ace has made no further reference to it since then.

Meanwhile, talkSPORT host Alan Brazil has branded Rangers star Alfredo Morelos a 'cheat'.

The breakfast show presenter claimed the Colombian striker falls over too easily during games.

He said: "He's a cheat. Simple as that. He just falls over too easily.

"He's a good player but he's got to stop cheating. Simple as that."
