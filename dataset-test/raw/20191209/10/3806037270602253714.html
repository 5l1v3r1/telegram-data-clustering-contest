Cozy up in Converse's Shearling-Lined Suede Color-Split Chuck 70 Hi

With hits of “Navy Blue,” “Baby Pink” and brown all around.

09 Dec 2019, 10:18 by Eric Brain

Converse has updated its iconic Chuck 70 Hi with a Winter-ready construction of “Navy Blue” and “Baby Pink” shearling and suede.

The classic silhouette is offered up using premium materials throughout, from its multicolored suede upper to the brown leather debossed All Star branding. Most notably, this Chuck 70 Hi sports a split colorway, with “Navy Blue” adorning the lateral, “Baby Pink” encompassing the medial side, and brown suede breaking the two apart by taking over the tongue, toe box and heel stripe.

Converse has also implemented a cozy shearling lining that matches the sneaker’s muted tri-color palette, wrapping around the inside respective of each shade used. Keeping things original is an off-white sole unit sporting a black running stripe all around the midsole, as well as a black license plate and a brown outsole. Completing this pair is a set of silver-toned eyelets with white laces running up the tongue.

Take a look at the Converse Chuck 70 Hi “Navy Blue/Baby Pink” Shearling in the gallery above. You can pick up a pair for yourself on December 14 for £109 GBP (approx. $143 USD) via retailers such as Sneakersnstuff.
