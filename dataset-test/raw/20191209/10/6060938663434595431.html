Photo: Can you recognize Deepika Padukone in THIS childhood picture

09 Dec 2019, 10:08 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Fans just can't get enough of Deepika Padukone and her Instagram handle updates, they just keep on wanting more. Not just her Insta updates but special comments by her hubby Ranveer on her posts is also something fans await for with breathed breathe. Today, the actress through her social media handle, announced the trailer release of her upcoming film, 'Chhapaak' which is happening tomorrow. However, before the announcement, she treated her fans with a few throwback pictures which are noteworthy.

One of the throwback pictures which she shared has Deepika posing with her two besties and we bet you will not recognize the 'Padmaavat' actress at a first glance.

In the picture, we can see the three childhood friends, posing together. Interestingly, three of them are dressed in white and are sporting a similar hair cut which is making it more difficult for the fans to make out Deepika from the frame. However, a true Deepika fan will make out that the third girl with a sparkling smile is Deepika, who is leaning on her friends' shoulder.

This particular picture earned praises from the fans and we are waiting for Ranveer to comment on this super adorable picture of hers.

On the professional front, Deepika will be seen in Meghna Gulzar's 'Chhapaak' opposite Vikrant Massey. The film is about acid attack survivor Laxmi Aggarwal. Moreover, she will also be seen in Kabir Khan's '83' as Kapil Dev's wife Romi Bhatia.
