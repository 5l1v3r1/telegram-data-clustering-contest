Autohub opens first Ssangyong-Changan dealership in Metro Manila

New Ssangyong-Changan Otis dealership is now open to serve customers

09 Dec 2019, 10:01 by AutoIndustriya.com

SsangYong Berjaya Motors Philippines (SBMP) and Berjaya Auto Asia Inc. (BAAI), the official distributor of Ssangyong and Changan vehicles in the country, has just opened their newest dealership in the heart of Metro Manila. The new Ssangyong-Changan Dealership also known as Auto Asia Otis will be operated by the Autohub Group of Companies. The Autohub Group has also been awarded the exclusive dealership franchise in Metro Manila and is eyeing to expand in Luzon and Mindanao by 2020.

“We are proud to partner with the Autohub Group in bringing Auto Asia to Manila. Their strong dealership presence in this area gives us confidence in the long term success of the two brands that we carry,” says Japheth Castillo, president of SBMP/BAAI.

All Ssangyong models offered by Auto Asia Otis will be diesel variants. The Tivoli which is a subcompact SUV and the Musso Grand, SsangYong’s entry to the pickup segment, both start at Php 1,180,000. It is essentially the same crossover offered today but features a Korean flag painted on the roof. All SsangYong models on sale come with 5-years warranty, V-Kool Window Film, and a free flight voucher to selected Asian destinations.

In order to commemorate the dealership’s launch, the group also announced the arrival of the Tivoli Launch Edition. Media personnel got to try the Launch Editon, as well as other Ssangyong models on a short trip to the historic Paco Park together with the Changan models.

Speaking of Changan, their models start at Php 585,000 for their 7-seat MPV with am affordable financing program under Php 12,000 for 60 months. The Chinese automaker also has light-duty trucks that range from Php 435,000 to Php 488,000.00, all below Php 10,000 for 60 months.

Auto Asia, Otis Manila is looking forward to providing the most cost-efficient service maintenance to its patrons and is very excited to open its doors to the current and future SsangYong and Changan owners this December 2019.

For more information on their promos, offers, and vehicle lineup, you may call 8-561-0973 / 09175284442, or you can follow SsangYong Otis, Manila and Changan Otis, Manila on Facebook and Instagram.
