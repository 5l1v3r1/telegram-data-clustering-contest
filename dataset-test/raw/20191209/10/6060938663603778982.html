Happy Birthday Dia Mirza: Alia Bhatt, Sonali Bendre and others shower wishes for the actress on her special day

09 Dec 2019, 10:35 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The actress turned a year older today and social media is buzzing with birthday wishes and rare and unseen pictures of the actress shared by her fan clubs.
