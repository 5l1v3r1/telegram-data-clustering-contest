Mallika Sherawat beats Monday blues with a difficult yoga pose; fans say 'sexy feets'

09 Dec 2019, 10:58 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

With millions of followers on social media, Mallika Sherawat has crafted a route to connect with her fans. The diva might be away from all the limelight and paparazzi but takes to her social media handles to give her fans a glimpse of her life.

Taking to Instagram on Monday, Mallika shared a Monday motivation picture of herself performing a difficult yoga pose. She captioned the picture as "Beat the Monday blues with yoga #mondayblues #yoga #health #fitness #fitindia"

The quintessential actress has been sharing motivational as well as fashionable posts on the picture-sharing platform. Not only that but a scroll through her account will give you a glimpse of her exotic vacays and pictures of the diva posing with Hollywood celebrities like Nicole Kidman, Bill Gates and others.

The 43-year-old beautiful actress has managed to keep her body in great shape and has also been setting the temperature soaring by sharing bikini pictures of herself as well. Meanwhile, on the work front, the 'Murder' actress was seen in a web series and is yet to announce her next project.
