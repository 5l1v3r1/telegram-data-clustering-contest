Tommy Fury strips off to tease his bulging muscles in cheeky snap

The topless hunk stripped off to a pair of designer swim shorts and posed for pictures to show off his extremely toned physique, while catching the attention of his blonde bombshell girlfriend

09 Dec 2019, 10:56 by Irishmirror.ie

Tommy Fury marked the start of his sizzling spa day by stripping off for a half-naked snap.

The Love Island runner up, 21, left little to the imagination as he shared a cheeky photo with his 2.9 million Instagram followers on Sunday afternoon.

The professional boxer appeared to be enjoying a relaxing day in the spa, and it seems he wanted to show off his extremely toned physique and bulging muscles - sending pulses racing.

The hunky reality star is currently preparing for his highly-anticipated return to the ring after he took to social media to announce his comeback fight with a video captioned: "Are you ready 21.12.19."

The fight, with his opponent still unknown, will take place at the Copper Box Arena in London on 'The Fight Before Christmas' card on BT Sport.

Tommy's latest snap proves the TV star has been training hard in the gym with his chiseled six pack, defined shoulders and huge biceps stealing the photo's attention.

He captioned the snap, writing: "Spa nights."

The handsome star's Love Island girlfriend Molly-Mae Hague complimented her beau's recent snap with a hint of PDA as she took to Instagram to post on his pic.

Teasing her approval of Tommy's snap, the blonde bombshell, 20, replied: "Wowwww."

Meanwhile, Tommy's saucy snap also caught the attention of his fellow Love Island contestant Michel Griffiths.

The former firefighter, 28, recognised Tommy's gym success, commenting: "Abs coming through there son."

Tommy's post-Love Island schedule has been hectic, with the star also busy filming for The Boxer & the Ballroom Dancer, alongside best pal Curtis Pritchard .

Love Island 2019's favourite mates have teamed up for a new spin-off of the hit ITV2 show which sees the boys switch their day jobs, and it will consist of three 60-minute episodes which highlight their unbreakable bromance.

Curtis, 23, will put on the boxing gloves as Tommy learns to cut some moves on the dancefloor.

As a surprise, their girlfriends Maura Higgins and Molly-Mae will star in the series along the way as they root for their boyfriends with support.

*The Boxer & the Ballroom Dancer will air on ITV2 later this year.
