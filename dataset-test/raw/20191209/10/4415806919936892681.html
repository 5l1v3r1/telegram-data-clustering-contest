Gardaí appeal for help in locating woman, 31, missing from Dublin

09 Dec 2019, 10:53 by By Digital Desk staff

Gardaí in Dublin are appealing for help in locating a missing 31-year-old woman.

Nikita Mooney has been missing from the Kilmore West area of Dublin 5 since Saturday, December 7.

Nikita is described as being around 5'6" in height with a slim build.

She has bright purple hair and blue eyes.

Anyone who may have seen or has information on Nikita's whereabouts are asked to contact Ballymun Garda Station on 01 6664400, the Garda Confidential Line on 1800 666 111 or any garda station.
