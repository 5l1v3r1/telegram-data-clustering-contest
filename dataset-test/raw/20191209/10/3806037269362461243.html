BAPE & GOD SELECTION XXX Join Forces on a Graphic-Heavy Collection

The exclusive capsule releases this weekend.

09 Dec 2019, 10:35 by Mallory Chin

Two of Japan’s lauded labels, underground streetwear brand GOD SELECTION XXX and streetwear stalwart BAPE, are joining forces on a range of essential apparel items.

The range is comprised of a bevy of T-shirts, a Shark Hoodie, snowboard jacket and trucker hat, each emblazoned with a special camo print that merges BAPE’s Ape Head insignia with GOD SELECTION XXX’s triple “X” motif. BAPE’s lovable sidekick Baby Milo even makes an appearance on one of the tees, where he can be seen holding up a “XXX” box logo. The standout items of the collaborative capsule include the black Shark Hoodie featuring a signature Shark Face motif and “WGM” terrycloth letters on the right portion of the hood, along with the snowboarding jacket dressed fully in the aforementioned special co-branded camo print.

Along with BAPE, GOD SELECTION XXX has also formed collaborative capsules with Hiroshi Fujiwara’s fragment design, CASETiFY and MEDICOM Toy.

Items from the BAPE x GOD SELECTION XXX capsule will be available this Saturday, December 14 exclusively at BAPE China, Hong Kong and Taipei.

In related news, BAPE and UNKNWN unveiled their collaborative capsule late last week.
