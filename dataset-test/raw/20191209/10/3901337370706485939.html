Nike & Mbappe Release Exclusive Signature Boot Range

09 Dec 2019, 10:58 by SL Social

Having a signature boot range as a footballer is considered a dream to many. Paris Saint-Germain star Kylian Mbappe is the latest to launch his very own boot range, with Nike. Check it out!

In today's game, players such as Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi play in their own boots while legends such as Ronaldinho and Pele have been privileged enough to run the blades of grass in their signature togs too.

That being said, Nike recently showcased former AS Monaco star Mbappe's signature boots, named the "Bondy Dreams" Mercurial. Bondy is an area in the north-east of Paris, a place where Mbappe says he fell in love with football.

The Nike X Mbappe Bondy selection is exclusive to Europe only, and includes the Air Max 98, a hoodie, a T-shirt, a Nike FC Jersey, a cap and a football.
