09 Dec 2019, 10:02 by Timothy Ndoro

Centurion Goes UNDERWATER In South Africa

Heavy rains have devasted South Africa’s Gauteng Province after massive flash floods resulted in parts of the City of Centurion going underwater. Midrand and Pretoria have also been negatively affected, but the flooding is not quite as bad as that in Centurion.

The rains which have been incessant for the past 5 days are so bad that the Centurion Lake Hotel has actually been flooded.
