Andy Whyment confirms Jaqueline Jossa had a ‘tough time’ in the I’m A Celeb camp

It comes after claims her husband had an affair with Love Islands star Gabby Allen

09 Dec 2019, 10:56 by Clodagh Meaney

I’m A Celebrity, Get Me Out Of Here runner up, Andy Whyment has confirmed that Jaqueline Jossa had a “tough time” following allegations her husband cheated on her.

Jaqueline was last night crowned as Queen of The Jungle after her fellow camp mate Myles Stephenson allegedly confirmed her suspicions about husband Dan Osborne.

The couple previously took a break after rumours swirled that the TOWIE star slept with Love Island’s Gabby Allen.

Speaking on Good Morning Britain this morning, Andy appeared alongside Kate Garraway to give the inside scoop on the show’s winner.

“I think she’s fine. I was really pleased for her,” he said of Jaqueline.

“She had a tough time in there at times. She’s done really well. I am pleased for her.”

Last night Jaqueline was crowned the winner of the ITV show after 23 days in camp.

Andy Whyment came in 2nd, with radio presenter Roman Kemp finishing in 3rd.
Dan has denied claims he had an affair behind his wife’s back.
Further claims have emerged that the Big Brother star had a threesome with his fellow housemates Chloe Ayling and Natalie Nunn while on a night out in London.
He has also denied these allegations and has threatened legal action, branding the story as fake.
