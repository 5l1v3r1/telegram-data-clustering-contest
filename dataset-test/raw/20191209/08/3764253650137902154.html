Beautiful photos of Davido's brother Adewale Adeleke with his family at his wedding introduction

09 Dec 2019, 08:01

- Singer Davido's brother, Adewale Adeleke, has done his wedding introduction on Saturday, December 8

- Adewale was joined by his family and friends who attended his wedding introduction

- Photos and video from the ceremony surfaced on social media

- The family flew to Cross River in a private jet for the introduction

Adewale Adeleke, the brother of Nigerian singer David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, has done his wedding introduction in Calabar, Cross River state. The ceremony took place on Saturday, December 8.

The young groom-to-be was joined by his billionaire father, Adedeji Adeleke, and other family members for his wedding introduction with his fiancee, Kani.

Adeleke family flew with friends and other family members all the way from Lagos to Cross River state in their private jet to celebrate the couple.
