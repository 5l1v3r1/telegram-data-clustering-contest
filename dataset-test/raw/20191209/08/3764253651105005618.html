Burna Boy confidently says he is going to win his Grammy nomination (video)

09 Dec 2019, 08:37

- Nigerian singer Burna Boy is gearing up for the 2020 Grammy Awards

- The music star recently allayed the fears of many that want him to win his nominated category

- Burna Boy wants his fans to know that he is bringing home the award

Social media was set abuzz some weeks ago after the exciting news of Nigerian singer, Damini Ogulu aka Burna Boy’s Grammy Award nomination became public.

The music star’s eponymous album African Giant was nominated in the World Best Music Album, alongside fellow African legendary singer, Angelique Kidjo.

Well, the weeks are drawing nearer for the international award ceremony which is set to hold in January 2020, and Burna Boy has allayed the fears of his well-wishers, who are fervently hoping that he would become the first Nigerian to move past the nomination level, and actually bring the award home.
