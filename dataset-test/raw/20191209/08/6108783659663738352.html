New Wonder Woman 1984 trailer is deliciously 80s

09 Dec 2019, 08:30 by Steve Wright

The savior of the DC Cinematic Universe returns.

The first proper Wonder Woman 1984 trailer has dropped, and it’s a good’un.

The trailer, below, debuted today at CCXP Brazil. In it, you’ll get great glimpses of Gal Gadot as Wonder Woman, Chris Pine as (surprise!) a returned-from-the-dead (?) Steve Trevor and a look at Kristen Wiig pre-Cheetah as Barbara Minerva.

As you’ll also be able to see (and hear), there’s a huge 80s vibe going down… and is that the same mall from Stranger Things or do they all seriously just look the same?

Wonder Woman 1984 heads to theatres in the northern hemisphere summer of 2020 (so winter here in Australia).
