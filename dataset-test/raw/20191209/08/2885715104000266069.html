Shares, Commodity, Rupee Market LIVE Updates: Sensex up 100 points, Nifty over 11,950 led by auto, metal stocks

09 Dec 2019, 08:08

Share Market, Sensex, Nifty Today Live Updates: After witnessing a see-saw trade in the morning session, the frontline equity market indices on the BSE and National Stock Exchange (NSE) are now trading higher in the early afternoon deals on Monday led by gains in automobiles and metal stocks.

At 12:33 pm, the S&P BSE Sensex was trading at 40,552.91, up 107.76 points or 0.27 per cent, while the broader Nifty 50 gained 33.20 points or 0.28 per cent at 11,954.70.

Among sectoral indices on the NSE, the Nifty Auto and Nifty Metal indices continued to be the top gainers, they were up 1.24 per cent and 1.11 per cent respectively.

Broader market indices were mixed in the early afternoon session of trade. The S&P BSE MidCap index was up 83.09 points or 0.57 per cent at 14,750.46, while on the other hand, the S&P BSE SmallCap index was at 13,349.34, up by just 9.99 points or 0.07 per cent.
