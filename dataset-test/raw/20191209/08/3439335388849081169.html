Man, 33, arrested after allegedly threatening to shoot Waikiki workers | Honolulu Star-Advertiser

09 Dec 2019, 08:00

A 33-year-old man was arrested early Saturday after threatening to shoot employees at a Waikiki store if he didn’t get the items he wanted, according to the Honolulu Police Department.

The incident occurred at 6:20 a.m. Saturday at an unidentified retail establishment. Police were called and managed to stop the man and arrest him at 7 a.m.

The case was classified as a robbery in the second degree. The man was being held in custody pending further investigation.
