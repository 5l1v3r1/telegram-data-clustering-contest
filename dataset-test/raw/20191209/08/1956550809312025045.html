Ring in the festive season at FIVE Palm Jumeirah Dubai

Sponsored: Christmas brunches, fine-dining set menus and some of the best New Year’s Eve parties in the city

09 Dec 2019, 08:46 by Time Out Dubai staff

The festive season is just around the corner and if you’re looking to celebrate Christmas and New Year’s Eve in style with top food and drink offers, FIVE Palm Jumeirah Dubai is the place to be.

From delicious food to some of the biggest parties of the season, take your pick from the list below and enjoy.

Al fresco dining at Cinque
Looking for a sophisticated and classy Christmas? Modern Italian ristorante Cinque invites foodies to an al fresco dining experience featuring a five-course gourmet menu inspired by the authentic flavours of the Amalfi Coast. From lobster cannelloni, herb-crusted lamb, to a selection of delicious festive pastries, there is something for the whole family, all accompanied by lively entertainment. For brunchgoers, the chic spot is also throwing a unique Italian-style Christmas Day brunch. Experience Christmas the Italian way and enjoy a roast turkey, slow-cooked salted cod and other tasty treats.
Dhs380 (soft drinks), Dhs550 (house beverages), Dhs190 (five-11 years old). Tue Dec 24-Wed Dec 25, 7pm-11.30pm. Dhs349 (soft drinks), Dhs499 (house beverages), Dhs175 (five-11 years old). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4.30pm.

Festive delights at BLVD on One
Get into the festive spirit at Blvd on One this Christmas. With an array of cooking stations including a live carving station and a delicious dessert selection, there is something for even the fussiest of eaters. Sit back and enjoy the live entertainment and all the festive fun with the family. On the big day itself, get ready to fill up on festive treats at the Christmas Day brunch. There’s going to be a slap-up spread featuring 15 international and traditional cooking stations and a big sing-along with festive carols.
Dhs249 (adults – soft drinks), Dhs125 (five-11 years old). Tue Dec 24, 6.30pm-11pm, Tue Dec 24-Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4.30pm, 6.30pm-11pm.

License to thrill at The Penthouse
Our Bar of the Year is a prime spot to watch all New Year’s fireworks – and with a theme that is not to be missed. Join in the extravaganza at one of Dubai’s hottest spots and ring in the New Year James Bond-style. There’s also set to be a surprise DJ, drinks and top grub including a caviar and oyster station, mushroom-truffle risotto and Peking duck.
Dhs1,500 (Marina side), Dhs2,000 (city side). Tue Dec 31, 8pm-2am.

New Year’s Eve at Cinque
Ring in the New Year with the finer things in life at Italian eatery Cinque. The perfect spot for free-flowing beverages and a five-course gourmet dinner including oysters, prawn carpaccio, foie gras and wagyu, and all accompanied with a live entertainment and prime views of the Dubai Marina fireworks.
Dhs1,500 (house beverages), Dhs750 (12-20 years old), Dhs375 (five-11 years old). Tue Dec 31, 7.30pm-1am.

New Year’s Eve feast at BLVD on One
Head down with the little ones in tow for a family-style New Year’s Eve buffet at BLVD on One with plenty of live entertainment, 15 food stations featuring dishes from all around the world, a chocolate fountain and top views of the city’s fireworks.
Dhs1,000 (house beverages), Dhs500 (five-11 years old). Tue Dec 31, 7.30pm-1am.

New Year’s Eve mega brunch at Maiden Shanghai
Top party planning group Candypants are throwing a mega party brunch at Maiden Shanghai. Expect delicious Chinese cuisine along with a crack party atmosphere.
Brunch: Dhs899 (house beverages – first and second floor), Dhs1,200 (house beverages – third floor outdoor terrace). Tue Dec 31, 8pm-3am. Drunch: Dhs399, Wed Jan 1, 12.30am-2.30am.

New Year’s Eve at Beach by FIVE
Dine under the stars and party on the beach under Dubai’s fireworks. With live entertainment and a Mediterranean menu, Beach by FIVE is set to be a top spot to celebrate the New Year.
Dhs1,250, Dhs625 (five-11 years old). Tue Dec 31, 8pm-3am.

Praia presents Carnival 2020
Praia presents Carnival 2020 – expect live Latin music, flamenco dancers, fire performers and illusionists for a lively vibe. Dance the night away and enjoy prime views of Dubai’s firework displays. This year, partygoers can customise their experience, starting with a four course dinner with unlimited house beverages from 7pm until 1am, or upgrade the night with premium drinks, bubbly and beluga caviar.
Dhs1,400 (four-course dinner package), Dhs2,200 (bubbly and caviar package), Dhs700 (14 years old and under). Tue Dec 31, 7pm-4am.

To book your spot today visit palmjumeirah.fivehotelsandresorts.com or call 04 455 9999.
