Bigg Boss 13: Ankita Lokhande Supports Rashami Desai After The Arhaan Khan Mess; Asks Her To Decide ‘Wisely’

09 Dec 2019, 08:14 by Divya Ramnani

After Devoleena Bhattacharjee and Kamya Panjabi, Ankita Lokhande comes out in support of Bigg Boss 13’s Rashami Desai post-Arhaan Khan’s big reveal

Ever since host Salman Khan exposed Rashami Desai’s boyfriend Arhaan Khan, there has been havoc inside the Bigg Boss 13 house, as well across the internet. Saturday’s episode showed how Arhaan Khan hid the news of his marriage and a kid with Rashami, whom he proposed to after reentering the house. And soon after Rashami came to know the truth, she broke down and was speechless most of the times. As a consequence of the same, Salman Khan had to enter the house and console the actress, he also confronted Arhaan Khan.

A lot of celebrities and fans have been extending their support and sympathy to Rashami. The list also includes her good friend and actress Ankita Lokhande. Taking to her Instagram story, the Pavitra Rishta actress shared a glimpse of the part where Salman Khan is talking to Rashami and Arhaan. In her caption, Lokhande expressed her support towards Rashami and advised her to decide wisely. She wrote, “God bless you. Rashu be strong and take your decision wisely baby,” followed with a heart.
