Christmas gift ideas that give back to farmers in need

While we’re busy ticking things off our Christmas gift lists, there’s a Facebook group posting hundreds of ideas to help take the stress out of Christmas shopping.

Buy From the Bush is a page that showcases beautiful and unique gifts on offer from retailers in rural communities who are battling through our country’s devastating drought.
