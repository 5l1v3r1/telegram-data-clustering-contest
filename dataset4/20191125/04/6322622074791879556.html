Hong Kong pro-democracy parties take 390 of 452 district council seats -RTHK

25 Nov 2019, 04:09 by Reuters

(Nov 25): Hong Kong pro-democracy parties took 390 of 452 district council seats, or nearly 90%, local broadcaster RTHK said on Monday, a day after residents turned out in record numbers to vote following six months of anti-government protests.
