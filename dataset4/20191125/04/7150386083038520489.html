Bigg Boss 13: Siddharth Shukla And Rashami Desai's Steamy Dance Sets Screens on Fire

Siddharth Shukla and Rashami Desai get intimate, dance inside the pool and create magic with their steamy chemistry inside the Bigg Boss 13 house. Watch the promo here.

25 Nov 2019, 04:52

Bigg Boss 13 is going to get spiced up with Rashami Desai and Siddharth Shukla‘s chemistry. Seems like the makers finally decided to listen to the fans who wanted to see some hot chemistry between two of the most popular contestants inside the house. In the new promo released by the channel on social media, Siddharth and Rashami are seen performing in a romantic video as part of a task. However, their performance seems so genuine it’s absolutely drool-worthy.

It all started when Bigg Boss assigned a task to Siddharth and Rashami by asking them to make a romantic video. Interestingly, the video is being directed by Shehnaz Gill while Devoleena Bhattacharjee and Arti Singh are seen helping the couple. The video published by the channel on its Twitter handle shows Siddharth and Rashami getting intimate on the tunes of Neend Udi Udi from the film Saathiya.
