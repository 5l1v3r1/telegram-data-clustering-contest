Taapsee Pannu wants to star in THIS Hrithik Roshan starrer

25 Nov 2019, 04:43 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Taapsee Pannu has slowly but gradually made her way up the success ladder in Bollywood. The actress has impressed one and all with her stunning performances in her past few films. The actress recently spoke about her love for superhero films. She reportedly said that she wishes to play an Indian superhero in ‘Avengers’. She also added that she would love to do Hrithik Roshan starrer ‘Krrish’ if offered.

While Taapsee has been a part of some noteworthy films like, ‘Pink’ and ‘Naam Shabana’, she has also been part of a multi-starrer like, ‘Mission Mangal’. Speaking about what appeals to her in a script, she reportedly said that although she knew it is not her film, she believed that people will remember her when they walk out of the theatres.

According to her, the screen time does not matter. If she is offered a small but significant role, she will take it up if her character doers a change in the narrative.

Meanwhile, on the work front, she was last seen in ‘Saand Ki Aankh’ along with Bhumi Pednekar. The film received positive reviews from audience and critics alike.

She will next be seen in ‘Rashmi Rocket’ and ‘Thappad’.
