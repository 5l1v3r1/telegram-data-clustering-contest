Sensex at record closing high amid strong gains in metal, banking & telecom stocks; Nifty ends at 12,074

25 Nov 2019, 04:15 by Agencies

Highlights

  • Top gainers in the sensex pack include Airtel, Tata Steel, IndusInd Bank, Axis Bank and HDFC with their stocks rising as much as 7.20 per cent
  • On the other hand, Yes Bank and ONGC slipped as much as 2.17 per cent
  • During the late deals, sensex also touched an all-time intra-day high of 40,932

MUMBAI: Market benchmark BSE sensex jumped over 500 points on Monday amid strong gains in metal, banking and telecom stocks on positive cues from global equities. The 30-share BSE index surged 530 points or 1.31 per cent to close at 40,889, while the broader NSE Nifty moved 159 points or 1.34 per cent higher to settle at 12,074.

Top gainers in the sensex pack include Bharti Airtel, Tata Steel, IndusInd Bank, Axis Bank, HDFC, Maruti and Vedanta with their stocks rising as much as 7.20 per cent.

On the other hand, Yes Bank and ONGC slipped as much as 2.17 per cent. During the late deals, sensex also touched an all-time intra-day high of 40,932.

On NSE, except for Nifty Media, all other sub-indices finished in green with Nifty Metal and Nifty Auto rising as much as 3.09 per cent.

According to traders, domestic benchmarks followed global equities which were enthused by fresh optimism over a trade truce between the US and China amid reports that an initial deal may be signed by the end of this year.

Bourses in Shanghai, Hong Kong, Seoul and Tokyo were trading up to 1.50 per cent higher.

Stock exchanges in Europe also opened on a positive note.

On the currency front, the rupee appreciated marginally against the US dollar to 71.69 in early session.

Brent futures, the global oil benchmark, rose 0.21 per cent to $62.50 per barrel.

Meanwhile, foreign institutional investors (FIIs) bought shares worth Rs 305.72 crore in the capital market in the previous session, while domestic institutional investors (DIIs) sold equities worth Rs 177.37 crore, data available with stock exchange showed.
