Poo thrown at bus driver in Victoria

25 Nov 2019, 04:04 by AAP

A bag filled with human poo has been thrown at a Melbourne bus driver before exploding over his face, hitting his eye and splattering across his driver’s seat.

The local bus driver stopped to let a passenger off in Wollert at 9.40pm on November 13 when a bearded Caucasian man, aged 30 to 40, stepped onto the bus.

The high-vis-clad man lobbed a plastic bag of poo at the driver before running off.

“Obviously the faeces has gone all over the driver,” Acting Sergeant Todd Little told reporters on Monday.

“The driver believes that it is human faeces.

“It is quite runny so it has splattered throughout the driver compartment of the vehicle and it has gone onto the driver’s face and into his eye.”

A number of small rocks were found inside the bag after it hit the safety security mesh and splattered throughout the driver’s compartment.

The attacker was wearing a beige beanie, a yellow and black high-visibility jumper with its hood up, and dark pants with a white stripe down each leg.

The driver remains distressed and is yet to return to work, and is receiving ongoing medical and psychological help.

“It is an absolutely disgusting act and we are doing our best to identify the male and hold him to account,” Acting Sgt Todd said.

Police wish to speak to anyone who saw the incident or the man described.
