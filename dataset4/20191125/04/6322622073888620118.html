Apex Healthcare will gain from strong commercial production : Affin Hwang

25 Nov 2019, 04:40 by Bloomberg / Dow Jones Newswires

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 25): Apex Healthcare's commercial production from its new plant is paying off, despite higher start-up costs, says Affin Hwang Capital. Performance for upcoming quarters are expected to be stronger after Apex Healthcare's 3Q revenue growth of 9% on year, the brokerage says.

Stronger performance is mostly expected to come from higher commercial production, says Affin Hwang, adding that it likes Apex Healthcare for its solid growth prospects and established delivery network.

Affin Hwang upgrades their call on the stock to buy from hold and raises its target price to RM2.67 from RM2.11.
