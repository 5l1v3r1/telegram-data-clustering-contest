Nokia Smart TV leaks in images. Packs Dolby Audio & DTS TruSurround Sound too

25 Nov 2019, 04:27 by Nayan

The Nokia Smart TV has now leaked in two images that reveal the “Sound by JBL” branding and also gives an idea about how it will look like. Though these images (Above and below) don’t completely reveal the design of the TV.

Retailer Flipkart is bringing Nokia Smart TVs as a brand licensee to Nokia and has shared these images with “Digit“. It also seems that the JBL speakers will be complemented with Dolby Audio & DTS TruSurround Sound support for bets-in-class aaudio quality. The leak also re-confirms the probable launch timings of early Decmeber for the smart TV.

We last reported about Indian retailer Flipkart planning to launch a Nokia-branded Smart TV in India. More details had emerged later about the Nokia Smart TV.

Nokia Smart TV Specs & Features:

  • 50 inch-plus size
  • 4K ultra-HD LED panel
  • JBL’s audio technology
  • Intelligent Dimming technology with auto-tuning for contrast and deeper black levels
  • Android 9.0
  • Dolby Audio & DTS TruSurround Sound
