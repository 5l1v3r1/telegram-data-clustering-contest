Bala Box Office Day 18: Changes Gears Further On Sunday

The Ayushmann Khurranna, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar starrer would come close to the 110 crores mark by the end of week

25 Nov 2019, 04:40 by Joginder Tuteja

With not much competition coming from Hindi films, except for the fact that it has lost a major count of screens, Bala changed gears further on Sunday. The film collected 3.22 crores more and that has elevated its score further to 105.87 crores.

The Ayushmann Khurranna, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar starrer would come close to the 110 crores mark by the end of week and from there a lifetime in the vicinity of 120 crores would be a possibility.
