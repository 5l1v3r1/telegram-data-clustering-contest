Cab driver found dead in his car in Gurgaon

Police said they identified the man, Yusuf, after they issued an advertisement in a local newspaper and were approached by his family.

25 Nov 2019, 04:14 by Express News Service

A day after a 22-year-old cab driver went missing from Gurgaon’s Pataudi, police found his body in his car near a dried-up drain in Pataudi’s Khor village Saturday morning.

Police said they identified the man, Yusuf, after they issued an advertisement in a local newspaper and were approached by his family.

According to police, Yusuf’s family told them that he left home Friday morning to pick up a passenger from Pataudi and didn’t return home.

Yusuf’s brother Farrukh said, “On Saturday, I couldn’t find him at his home or with his friends. Later, we learned that Yusuf’s car was found near a drain in Sohna district. Police had seized the car and recovered his body. I went to the hospital and identified his body later.”

Yusuf lived in a rented accommodation in Imam Nagar in Gurgaon.

A case of murder and causing disappearance of evidence was registered against unknown persons by Pataudi police after they found injuries on the victim’s head and back.

Police suspect that he was killed in a robbery gone wrong and his body may have been dumped in the car later.

A post-mortem was conducted and police are awaiting its result.

Veer Singh, ACP, Pataudi, said, “A case was registered Sunday and we are checking the victim’s phone for possible leads. We haven’t identified suspects yet and are probing the matter.”
