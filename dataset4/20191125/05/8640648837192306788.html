LETTER: Schiff’s impeachment circus cluttering up the boob tube

25 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Alan Bianco, Las Vegas

Why do all the major networks, plus the three news networks, have to carry the impeachment proceedings? According to ABC News, only 13 million people tuned in. That’s about 0.5 percent of the U.S. population. People do not care and are tired of it, especially after the Mueller hearings.

Why don’t they take turns or let MSNBC, CNN and Fox carry it? We can get info online, from the Review-Journal or from streaming if we’re interested. Please give us our TV back.
