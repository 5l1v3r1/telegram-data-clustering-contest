BBB offers special holiday season tips for businesses and consumers

25 Nov 2019, 05:01 by Eddie Roach

Better Business Bureau of Central Oklahoma wants everyone to know they are more than just a place to report businesses for poor performance.

While business reviews and complaints are an important part of their service, they offer a wide range of other services for businesses and consumers, including special holiday season planning resources so businesses can make the most of the busiest shopping season.

“We want people to look at us as a resource,” said Kitt Letcher, President and CEO. “Black Friday and Small Business Saturday are fast approaching. As shoppers are gearing up to fulfill their holiday shopping lists, we are offering tips on best practices to prepare for the holiday shopping weekend.”

Some of the ideas, advice, tips include:

  • Make sure online listings, social media platforms and other extensions of your website are up to date and accurate.
  • If your office is closed during the holiday shopping weekend, consider partnering with neighboring businesses by providing coupons or handouts, that neighboring stores could pass out, on your behalf.
  • Decorate your store/place of business and don’t shy away from a theme.

“We have a special holiday website which includes many more tips for businesses,” Letcher added.
