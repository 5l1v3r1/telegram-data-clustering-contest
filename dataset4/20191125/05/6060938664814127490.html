Taapsee Pannu gives a befitting reply to an attendee who asked her to speak in Hindi at an event

25 Nov 2019, 05:41 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The video of Taapsee Pannu giving a befitting reply to an audience member, who tried to troll her for not speaking in Hindi at a recent event in Goa, is going viral on the Internet since yesterday.

Taapsee Pannu was invited as a guest speaker at the event where she was interrupted by a person sitting in the audience who asked her to speak in Hindi as 'she is a Hindi film actress.'

As soon as the attendee said this, Taapsee was quick to reply, "Sir, main to pura Hindi me baat karsakti hoon, par kya yahan sabko Hindi samajh aayegi?" (Sir, I can speak in Hindi for the entire session but will the audience understand Hindi?)

She further went on to say, "Main south Indian actress bhi hoon... Tamil aur Telugu main bhi baat karu?" (I'm also a south Indian actress, so, should I speak in Tamil and Telugu too?)

Taapsee was applauded for her response and reportedly the rest of the session went on without interruptions. Since yesterday #TappseeOnFire has been trending on Twitter for the actress' best reply to the attendee. " You slay like a boss," one fan commented, while another wrote, "#TaapseeOnFire! She sIayed a vicious troll who was trying to school her... Taapsee you are a staaaa (that's star but with an accent)."
