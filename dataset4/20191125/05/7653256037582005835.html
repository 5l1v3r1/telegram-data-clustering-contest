Priyanka accuses BJP of disregarding Constitution in Maharashtra

25 Nov 2019, 05:12 by PTI

New Delhi: Congress leader Priyanka Gandhi Vadra on Monday accused the BJP of disregarding the Constitution in Maharashtra and repeating the "game" it played in Karnataka.

The Congress had accused the BJP of poaching MLAs to form government in Karnataka earlier this year.
