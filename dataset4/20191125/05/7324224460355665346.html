Fans nostalgic after Kesha performs one of her biggest hits at the AMAs

The pop star was joined on stage by New Orleans musician Big Freedia.

25 Nov 2019, 05:15

Kesha made a triumphant return to the American Music Awards following a six-year absence.

The pop star, 32, was joined on stage by New Orleans musician Big Freedia for a performance of Raising Hell, the first single from Kesha’s upcoming album.
