Sydney bracing for 90km/h winds, severe storms

25 Nov 2019, 05:11 by Matt Bungard

The northern and western parts of Sydney are expected to be lashed by heavy thunderstorms and massive winds as the Bureau of Meteorology issued a severe weather warning on Monday afternoon.

Hornsby and surrounding suburbs are set to be the heaviest hit, but meteorologist Katarina Kovacevic said that "later on this afternoon and into the evening, the storms could extend".

"If you drew a line from Camden to the CBD, probably north of that line [is the area that will be affected]," she said.

Hail fell in the north-west suburb of Galston in the afternoon and could spread further.
