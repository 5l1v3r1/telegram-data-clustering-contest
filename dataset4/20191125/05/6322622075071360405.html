RHB Bank 3Q net profit up 6.4% to RM615.83m

25 Nov 2019, 05:31 by Surin Murugiah

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 25): RHB Bank Bhd's net profit for the third quarter ended Sept 30, 2019 rose 6.4% to RM615.83 million from RM578.69 million a year earlier, lifted by higher non-fund based income and lower expected credit losses for loans.

In a Bursa Malaysia filing today, the bank said revenue for the quarter rose to RM3.34 billion from RM3.2 billion a year earlier.

Earnings per share for the quarter rose to 15.36 sen versus 14.43 sen.

For the nine months ended Sept 30, RHB's net profit rose to RM1.86 billion from RM1.74 billion a year earlier. Revenue for the period was higher at RM10.1 billion compared to RM9.38 billion previously.

In a separate statement, RHB Bank group managing director Datuk Khairussaleh Ramli said the group demonstrated resilience by delivering higher net profit for the first nine months of 2019, amidst challenging operating environment.

"We remain prudent in growing our business, while we continue to adopt measures to accelerate efforts to manage asset quality and exercise discipline in cost management, supported by our strong fundamentals.

"We continue to make good progress in the execution of our FIT22 strategic roadmap, which includes investments in technology infrastructure and enhancing digital capabilities, as well as the adoption of the AGILE work culture across the group, driven by our focus to serve our customers better," he said.

At the midday break, RHB gained 1.05% or 6 sen to RM5.76, valuing it RM23.09 billion.
