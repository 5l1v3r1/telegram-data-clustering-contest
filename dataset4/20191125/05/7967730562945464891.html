Man throws bagful of faeces at bus driver in Wollert

25 Nov 2019, 05:21 by Christine McGinn

A bag filled with human excrement has been thrown at a bus driver in Melbourne's north before exploding over his face, hitting his eye and splattering across his driver's seat.

The bus driver stopped to let a passenger off in Wollert at 9.40pm on November 13 when a bearded Caucasian man, aged 30 to 40, stepped onto the bus.

The high-vis-clad man lobbed a plastic bag of poo at the driver before running off.

"Obviously the faeces has gone all over the driver," acting Sergeant Todd Little told reporters on Monday.

"The driver believes that it is human faeces.

"It is quite runny so it has splattered throughout the driver compartment of the vehicle and it has gone onto the driver's face and into his eye."
