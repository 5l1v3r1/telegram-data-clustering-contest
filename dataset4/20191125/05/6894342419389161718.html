PlayStation 5 Might Be Released On 20 Nov 2020 For US$499; To Come With 2TB SSD

25 Nov 2019, 05:50 by Ian Chee

The Sony PlayStation 5 (PS5) is scheduled to be announced at the end of next year. It’s a broad window, but now a leaker has claimed to know the exact date of the console’s launch. Not only that, they also claim to know the price tag that will accompany the console at launch.

Twitter user @PSErebus has posted a series of tweets which relate to the history of the console series of sorts. But two of them stand out. One says that the PS5 will be available in North America on 20 November 2020 for US$499 (~RM2084). The other states the launch price in Europe and the UK, as well as the claim that the PS5 will launch with a 2TB SSD.
