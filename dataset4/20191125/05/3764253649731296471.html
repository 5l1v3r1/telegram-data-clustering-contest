25 Nov 2019, 05:47

Vivo S5 is a new smartphone released by the Chinese technology company called Vivo. The handset comes with an attractive design and impressive features that make it stand out in the market. The device is the third in the S series after Vivo S1 and S1 Pro.

The Chinese company is known to produce good Android smartphones that are pocket-friendly. So, what Vivo S5 details should you know? Is it worth buying?

Vivo S5 Overview

The new device comes with its pros and cons that anyone planning to buy it should know.

The good

  • It comes with a large display suitable for watching videos and playing games.
  • The phone has high battery capacity that can last for long without charging.
  • It also has one of the best rear and selfie cameras in the market.
  • The handset has a microSD card slot to expand memory.
  • It comes in three colour options, Icelandic Blue, Phantom Blue, and Star Black.

Design

Generally, Vivo phones come with gorgeous designs, and the latest S5 is no exception. The handset has a similar design to the previous S1. It has a 3D surface grip that makes it more appealing. The punch-hole camera and the four cameras on the back also set it apart from other phones in the series.

When it comes to its measurements, the phone is 8.66 mm in thickness, 73.9 mm in width, and 157.9 mm in length. It weighs 188g, which means you can feel some weight when holding it.

Vivo S5 display

The smartphone also comes with a 6.44-inch AMOLED display with Full HD+ resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels and 409 PPI (Pixels Per-square Inch). It has a screen-to-body ratio of 85.8% and a TUV Rhineland certification which means the phone is safe for your eyes.

The handset comes in three different colours for buyers to select their favourite. The three Vivo S5 colours are Icelandic Blue, Phantom Blue, and Star Black.

Processor, storage & performance

The phone uses the Qualcomm SDM712 Snapdragon 712 Octa-core (2×2.3 GHz Kryo 360 Gold + 6×1.7 GHz Kryo 360 Silver) processor which provides a user-friendly experience. The Adreno 616 GPU ensures fast and efficient graphics performance.

The ROM of the new device is 128GB, and with a microSD slot, you can increase the memory up to 128GB. It has a RAM of 8GB.

Vivo S5 battery

One of the standout features of the device is its long battery life. Not many handsets have a battery capacity of 4010mAh within its price range. It also has fast charging technology.

Speaker and audio quality

The speaker produces good sound for entertainment. You can also plug in an earphone to listen to your favourite song.

Software

One of the standout software features of the new handset is its powerful lenses for both its selfie and primary cameras.

Operating system

The smartphone runs on Android 9.0 (Pie), which is fast and efficient. Other similar brand phones running on the same OS are Vivo V17, Y19, and Y5s.

Vivo S5 camera

The front and rear cameras capture quality and clear images and videos. The rear camera is 48 MP alongside an 8MP ultrawide, 5MP depth, and 2MP macro units arranged in a diamond formation.

The rear camera takes videos of both 2160p@30fps and 1080p@30fps while the selfie camera takes videos of 1080p@ 30fps. For selfies, users can enjoy the 32 MP front camera, which is suitable for photography.

Vivo S5 release date

The phone was officially launched on Nov 14, 2019, in Beijing, China. It is now available in some countries at an affordable price. Vivo S5 price ranges from one nation to the other. In Nigeria, it is expected to start retailing at about N140,000.
