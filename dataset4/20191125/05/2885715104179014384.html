American Music Awards 2019: Winners list

Who won what at the American Music Awards 2019 held today at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

25 Nov 2019, 05:22 by AP

A list of the winners at American Music Awards 2019 held today at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

Artiste of the year: Taylor Swift

New artiste of the year: Billie Eilish

Collaboration of the year: Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello, “Senorita”

Favorite pop/rock female artiste: Taylor Swift

Favorite pop/rock male artiste: Khalid

Favorite pop/rock duo or group: BTS

Favorite pop/rock song: Halsey, “Without Me”

Favorite pop/rock album: Taylor Swift, “Lover”

Favorite rap/hip-hop artiste: Cardi B

Favorite rap/hip-hop song: Lil Nas X featuring Billy Ray Cyrus, “Old Town Road”

Favorite rap/hip-hop album: Post Malone, “Hollywood’s Bleeding”

Favorite soul/R&B female artiste: Beyoncé

Favorite soul/R&B male artiste: Bruno Mars

Favorite soul/R&B song: Khalid, “Talk”

Favorite soul/R&B album: Khalid, “Free Spirit”

Favorite country female artiste: Carrie Underwood

Favorite country male artiste: Kane Brown

Favorite country duo or group: Dan + Shay

Favorite country song: Dan + Shay, “Speechless”

Favorite country album: Carrie Underwood, “Cry Pretty”

Favorite alternative rock artiste: Billie Eilish

Favorite adult contemporary artiste: Taylor Swift

Favorite Latin artiste: J Balvin

Favorite contemporary inspirational artiste: Lauren Daigle

Favorite electronic dance music artiste: Marshmello

Favorite social artiste: BTS

Favorite music video: Taylor Swift, “You Need to Calm Down”

Artiste of the decade: Taylor Swift

Tour of the year: BTS

Favorite soundtrack: “Bohemian Rhapsody”
