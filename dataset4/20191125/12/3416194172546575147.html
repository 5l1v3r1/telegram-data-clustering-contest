Would Josephine Langford Work With Sister Katherine Langford On A Project? Find Out Her Answer Here!

25 Nov 2019, 12:11

Josephine Langford is opening up about whether she’d ever work with sister Katherine on a project – and her answer was an astounding yes.

During an interview with Hollywire at the recent People’s Choice Awards, where After picked up The Drama Movie of 2019, the 21-year-old actress dished about if she join a project with Katherine, who is best known for 13 Reasons Why.

“I would love to!” Josephine shared in her on-carpet interview. “It just hasn’t popped up.”

She continues, “But it would be fun.”

Just recently, Katherine opened up on if she’d watched Josephine‘s movie.
