Palace Ultimo 2019 Outerwear

From camouflage parks to Polartec fleeces.

25 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Jack Stanley

Palace has debuted its full range of winter-ready outerwear from its Ultimo 2019 collection, which first appeared in the brand’s recent lookbook. With the coldest months being underway, the new collection sees the London-based label deliver a range of weather-appropriate pieces.

Arguably the stand-out item is the P-3B Parka, which is set to release in black as well as a number of different camouflage iterations. Elsewhere, the Ultimo 2019 collection also feature a puffa jacket constructed from a fuse ripstop fabric, and a selection of Polartec fleece options.

The first pieces will arrive in store and online on November 29. For more from the collection, make sure to check out the range in the following categories -

- Lookbook
- Tracksuits
- Tops & Sweatshirts
- Tees
- Hats
- Footwear, Accessories & Hardware
