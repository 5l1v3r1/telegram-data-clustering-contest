Shahid Kapoor to head to Chandigarh to shoot for Jersey

25 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Akash Nihalani

Shahid Kapoor last release Kabir Singh, which was a remake of South hit Arjun Reddy went on to become one of the biggest hits of 2019. And now, the actor will be seen in another remake of a South film titled Jersey. Shahid will be playing the role of a cricketer in the film and has already begun with brushing up his cricket skills by spending time on the nets. 

As per a report in a leading daily, the actor will soon be heading to Chandigarh to shoot for the film. He’ll be shooting in Chandigarh from December 2 to December 30 after a month-long schedule. Reportedly set in the ’90s, the film will showcase the high and lows of this struggling cricketer. A source close to the project had this to say, “After prepping for almost two months on turf in suburban Mumbai, Shahid will start shooting from December 2. The month-long schedule stretches right up to December 30. After that, the team will take a break for around four-five days for the second schedule. The Jersey team will be moving in and out of Chandigarh a lot in the coming months. They will shoot at real locations, including a beautiful row house in the heart of the city which will be shown as Shahid’s house.”

Recently, the makers announced that they’ve signed Mrunal Thakur to star opposite Shahid in the film. Jersey is slated to release on August 28, 2020.
