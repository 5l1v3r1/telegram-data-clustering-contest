Body discovered near school in Ballymun, Co Dublin

Gardai say they are at the 'scene of an incident'

25 Nov 2019, 12:02 by Brynmor PattisonDeputy Online Editor, John Patrick Kierans

A body was discovered at a laneway near a school in Dublin this morning.

Gardai say they are at the "scene of an incident" at Balbutcher Lane in Ballymun.

The Irish Mirror understands that a body was discovered there this morning.

The discovery was made just yards from a local school.

A garda spokesman said: "Gardai are currently at the scene of an incident at Balbutcher Lane area of Ballymun.

"A scene is currently in place. Further updates will issue."

Just last Monday, there was a 'shooting' scare in the area.

Gardai responded to reports of a loud bang from a place known locally as 'The Hill' near St Joseph's School at around 3pm on Monday, November 18.

It was feared that a gun had gone off - however no evidence was found to suggest there was a shooting.

Locals told Dublin Live last week how the area has been known as an anti-social blackspot, describing it as a "no-go area".

One said: "There's always trouble there, residents are sick. There's open drug dealing and fighting and we're scared someone will be hurt."
