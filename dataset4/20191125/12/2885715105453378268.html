IBPS SO 2020 application process for 1163 posts closing soon: Here’s how to apply

IBPS SO 2020: A total of 1,163 vacancies are to be filled through this recruitment process. The preliminary online exam will be held on December 28 and 29.

25 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Careers Desk

IBPS SO 2020: The last date to apply for the post of a specialist officer (SO) at the Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) is tomorrow – November 26. A total of 1,163 vacancies are to be filled through this recruitment process. Candidates who wish to apply for these posts can do so from the official website, ibps.in.

To be selected for these posts, candidates will have to pass the three-tier exam –  prelims, mains and interview round. The preliminary online exam will be held on December 28 and 29. Those who clear it will be called for IBPS SO Mains to be held on January 25, 2020. Merit list will be created based on marks obtained in prelims and mains.

IBPS SO 2020: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website, ibps.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘CRP specialist officer’ link in the left-hand panel
Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page, click on the link ‘Common Recruitment Process for the specialist officer – IX’
Step 4: In the new page, click on the online application link
Step 5: Click on ‘click here for new registration’
Step 6: Fill details, verify
Step 7: Log-in using registration number, fill the form
Step 8: Make the payment, submit

IBPS SO 2020: Fee

Candidates need to pay Rd 600 as application fee, however, for reserved category candidate including PwD candidates, the fee is Rs 100.

Selected candidates will be hired at the post of IT Officer (Scale I), Agricultural Field Officer (Scale I), Rajbhasha Adhikari (Scale I), Law Officer (Scale I), HR/Personnel Officer (Scale I), Marketing Officer (Scale I).
