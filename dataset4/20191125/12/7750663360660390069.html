Raheem Sterling stalling on new Manchester City contract?

Raheem Sterling reportedly has no plans to sign a further extension to his contract at Manchester City for the foreseeable future.

25 Nov 2019, 12:58 by Darren Plant

Despite only penning a fresh deal late last year, interest from Real Madrid has led to City wanting to hand improved terms to the England international.

However, according to The Sun, the 24-year-old is happy with his current contract at the Etihad Stadium.

The report suggests that the winger will delay making a decision on the next stage of his career until knowing whether Pep Guardiola will prolong his stay at the Premier League champions.

Sterling still has three-and-a-half years left on his current deal at the north-west outfit.
