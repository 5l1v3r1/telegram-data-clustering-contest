Taylor Swift's Green AMAs Dress Has A Deeper Meaning Than You Might Have Thought, According to Fans

25 Nov 2019, 12:46

Taylor Swift surprised us all when she hit the red carpet at the 2019 American Music Awards over the weekend in a stunning olive green dress.

The look was definitely unexpected, given her colorful Lover era, but according to fans, the shade was perfect for the night where she received the Artist of the Decade honor.

Some fans were getting Reputation vibes, while others claimed she wore green as the symbol for money.

Meanwhile, the biggest fans knew what the green truly meant – it symbolized the beginning of her career, which was perfect given the honor Taylor was picking up.
