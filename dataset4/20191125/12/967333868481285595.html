Audience Thrash Tipsy Singer for Singing out of Tune during a Religious Event in India - Video

25 Nov 2019, 12:33

New Delhi (Sputnik): The audience at a religious turned violent after a singer appeared in intoxicated and started singing out of tune. The incident happened in Gujarat, one of five states in India where the manufacture, sale and consumption of alcohol is prohibited.

In the video clip, the singer can be heard singing out of tune and slurring his words. The mood in the audience quickly turned ugly and a man jumped up on stage to beat up the inebriated singer.
