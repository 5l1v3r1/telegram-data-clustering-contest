Texas Southern beats University of Hawaii Wahine basketball team | Honolulu Star-Advertiser

25 Nov 2019, 12:41

Richelle Velez scored 25 points and Niya Mitchell 24 as visiting Texas Southern beat Hawaii 74-57 in a women’s college basketball game Sunday at the Stan Sheriff Center.

Amy Atwell led the Rainbow Wahine with 15 points.

Hawaii fell to 3-2. Texas Southern improved to 2-3.
