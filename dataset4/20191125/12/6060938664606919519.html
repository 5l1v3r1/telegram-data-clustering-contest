Photos of the day: From Ranbir Kapoor-Alia Bhatt heading to Manali to Shahid Kapoor prepping for 'Jersey'; here’s what made headlines today

25 Nov 2019, 12:48 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

No day is a quiet day in the glamorous business of Bollywood as celebs are often spotted around the city. While some were spotted post their exhausting workout sessions at the gym, others were snapped sporting fashionable outfits while promoting their film in the city. Not only that as the airport is also an important part of Bollywood as B-town celebs get papped entering or leaving the city.

Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt head to Manali

Fans were treated with pictures of lovebirds Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt early in the morning as the two were spotted at the airport heading to Manali along with their co-star Mouni Roy. While all were dressed in their casual best, what grabbed everyone's attention was RK sporting an arm cast. While fans took to social media to ask their favourite actor to 'take care' and 'get well soon', reports making rounds on the internet suggests that the versatile actor sprained his hand while playing football last evening.

Shahid Kapoor preps for 'Jersey'

If Ranbir-Alia's pictures weren't enough then 'Kabir Singh' star Shahid Kapoor was snapped by the paparazzi while he left the gym after a gruelling workout session. Shahid has been prepping very hard to get into shape for his next film 'Jersey' and has also been spending a lot of time on the ground, polishing his cricketing skills.

Out and about in the city

Janhvi Kapoor, Mira Rajput and Amrita Arora were spotted outside their gyms while they headed towards their vehicles. While Mira looked in a delightful mood post working out, Janhvi flaunted her washboard abs while sporting funky athleisure. Varun Dhawan was also snapped later in the day walking out of the gym in a fashionable sporty outfit.

Promotional activities

It was another day of promotions for Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar as their upcoming film ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ inches closer to its release date. While Bhumi stunned in all-black attire, Kartik-Ananya continued to have fun while promoting their film.

Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon also posed for the shutterbugs as they promote their upcoming periodic drama 'Panipat'. While Kriti opted for an all-white look, Arjun looked dashing in a printed outfit.
