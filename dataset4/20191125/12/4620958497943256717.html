How a rat caused a massive Metro Vancouver power outage today

Richmond News

25 Nov 2019, 12:37 by Maria Rantanen

A pesky rodent managed to shut off power to 10,850 BC Hydro customers on Monday morning.

The rodent contacted the transformer and caused a converter fault at the Cambie substation, explained BC Hydro spokesperson Tanya Fish, causing power to go out in large areas of central and east Richmond.

Power was reported out around 5:30 a.m. but crews were able to get it restored by 7 a.m.

Fish said it was a relatively “quick fix” once crews got on site to the Cambie substation.

To report an outage, call BC Hydro at 1-800-224-9376.
