Pals of missing Govan man Kyle Hay launch frantic search after he disappears on works night out

Kyle Hay, of Govan, was last seen out with his work pals over the weekend.

25 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Lynn Love

The family of a missingGlasgow man who disappeared after a night have launched a frantic search.

Kyle Hay , of Govan, was last seen out with his work pals over the weekend.

He has not been in contact with friends or family since.

Friend Jamie Bey Jnr took to Facebook to help track him down this morning.

He wrote: If anyone has seen or heard from Kyle Hay please message me. If everyone can share this status as well it will be appreciated."

When asked when he was last seen by a concerened family member, Jamie said: "He was on a works night out his work and colleagues have all been contacted."

Police Scotland have been contacted for comment.
