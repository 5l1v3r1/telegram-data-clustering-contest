Raheem Sterling tells Man City he'll sign new deal unless Guardiola stays

25 Nov 2019, 12:06

- Raheem Sterling has reportedly claimed he wants confirmation about Guardiola's stay at Man City before signing a new deal

- The England international has been offered a whopping £450,000-per-week wage by the Etihad outfit

- Sterling has improved massively since Guardiola's joined the Premier League side in 2016-17 season

Raheem Sterling will not commit his long term future to Manchester City until Pep Guardiola extends his stay at the Etihad.

The Spanish boss recently claimed he will not be leaving the Premier League champions next summer but the Englishman wants an official confirmation before signing a £450-per-week deal.

This means Guardiola's departure could lead to the 24-year-old leaving the club to play for his manager with another team.
