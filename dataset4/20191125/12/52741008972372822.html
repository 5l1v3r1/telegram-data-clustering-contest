Stolen vehicle in Kitchener leads to arrest

Regional Police stopped a suspected stolen vehicle on Highland Road over the weekend, and seized weapons and cash

25 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Mark Pare

Regional Police have charged a man after stopping a suspected stolen vehicle over the weekend.

Police say sometime on Saturday, they conducted a traffic stop on Highland Road.

While investigating the vehicle, police say they seized a prohibited weapon, three knives and a large sum of Canadian currency.

The man, who was not named, is facing a number of charges.
