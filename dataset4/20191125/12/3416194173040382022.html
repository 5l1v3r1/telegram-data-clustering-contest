Liam Payne Parties with His Angel & Charlie's Angels at the Same Time

25 Nov 2019, 12:41

Liam Payne had a lot of angels by his side over the weekend – his real-life angel Maya Henry and the stars of the new film Charlie’s Angels!

They were all in attendance at the Business of Fashion Voices Gala Dinner to help honor British Vogue‘s editor-in-chief Edward Enninful on Friday (November 22) in Oxfordshire, England.

Charlie’s Angels stars Ella Balinska and Naomi Scott (with husband Jordan Spence) were there, as well as model Jourdan Dunn.

FYI: Maya is wearing an Alberta Ferretti dress. Naomi is wearing a Jason Wu look. Ella is wearing Prada. Jourdan is wearing Alexander McQueen.
