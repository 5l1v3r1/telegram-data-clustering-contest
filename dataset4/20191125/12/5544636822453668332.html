People Think I’m Jobless- DJ Cuppy

25 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Female disc jockey and singer, Florence Otedola, better known as DJ Cuppy has revealed that most people think she is just a billionaire’s daughter who does nothing.

DJ Cuppy, daughter of Nigerian billionaire, Femi Otedola in a tweet revealed how someone asked if she was truly a disc jockey.

According to the singer, people think she is just a a billionaire daughter that takes gelato (ice cream) all day.

DJ Cuppy tweeted “someone just came up to me asking if I’m actually a DJ, so people legit think I’m a professional pink-haired billionaire heiress that eats gelato all day? LMAO”

Few days ago, the dj took to social media to brag about being the most followed African DJ in the world.

On her Instagram and Twitter handles, the billionaire’s daughter stated that she just made the discovery and decided to share her excitement on the platforms.

She wrote: “OMG! Just found out I’m the MOST FOLLOWED African DJ in The WORLD!

Earlier this year, DJ Cuppy, noted that many people think she is joking when she tells them she is going around the world doing the work of a DJ.
