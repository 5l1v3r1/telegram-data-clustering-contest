Vidya Actor Namish Taneja HOSPITALISED After Getting A Severe Electric Shock While Shooting For His Show

25 Nov 2019, 12:29 by Divya Ramnani

Reel-life tragedy turns into real-life tragedy after TV actor Namish Taneja gets electrocuted while shooting for a sequence for his show Vidya!

TV actor Namish Taneja, who is currently seen in the lead role in Colors TV’s Vidya, was admitted to hospital after getting an electric shock during the shoot. As per a report in Telly Chakkar, a reel scene turned out to be real tragedy while Namish was shooting for a hospital sequence. For that particular scene, Namish, as Vivek, was supposed to receive an electric shock. However, it happened for real and the actor was left injured. Soon after the incident, Namish was immediately rushed to a nearby hospital and underwent his treatment. (ALSO READ - Swaragini Actor Namish Taneja Becomes Victim Of ATM Fraud, Duped Of Rs 50,000)

In fact, the portal also tried to get in touch with Namish Taneja to know what led to such incident; however, he remained unavailable to respond. As soon as this news hit social media, a lot of fans took to their accounts and prayed for Namish’s speedy recovery. “Wishing you a speed Recovery @namishtaneja ! All our prayers are with you. Get well soon,” wrote a fan, while another tweeted, “Pray for Nami… He got electrocuted while shooting and is in ICU… PLEASE GET WELL SOON #NamishTaneja.”

It was in the year 2014 that Namish Taneja forayed into the television industry with the show Ekk Nayi Pehchaan. He was 25 years old back then. Further, Namish has been a part of several popular TV shows like Swaragini and Mein Maike Chali Jaungi Tum Dekhte Rahiyo. Coming to his ongoing show Vidya, it commenced this September and hasn’t managed to score a good TRP rating. The show co-stars Meera Deosthale opposite Namish.
