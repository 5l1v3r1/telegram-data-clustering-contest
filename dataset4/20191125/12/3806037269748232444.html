Palace Ultimo 2019 Hats

Standouts include Polartec fleece-equipped offerings & chenille patch six-panels.

25 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Eric Brain

London-imprint Palace recently unveiled its Ultimo 2019 collection in a Letty Schmiterlow-shot lookbook featuring skaters Lucien Clarke and Dino Da Silva.

For its latest capsule collection, Palace has gone hat-heavy with a selection of Polartec-indebted pieces, a myriad of six-panel caps, and a variation of beanies all ready for the cold Winter months approaching.

Kicking off the selection of hats for Palace Ultimo 2019 is its “Hot Head” Polartec hat, which features a nylon outer, a warm Polartec sherpa lining, and a side zip. Popper buttons fasten the hat around the neck, while Palace’s moniker subtly graces the front.

Elsewhere, the London-imprint delivers a fleece and sherpa mix beanie complete with drawcords, a collegiate-inspired fleece beanie, and three other offerings ranging from “Alpine” to a ribbed “R-Knit” offering, a waffled “Mellow One” iteration and the finely-knitted patch beanie.

Asides from the Winter-ready headwear is a selection of six-panel caps. Notable pieces include the P 6-Panel ‘The Final Frontier” cap, which comes complete with lined flap-down ear covers, as well as the diamond-fused PERTEX quantum fabric cap. Palace continues on from its selection of scarves with the Polartec Go-Go cap, and round off its capsule with various bold six-panels sporting chenille patches, embroidery and large branded patches.

Take a look at all of Palace’s Ultimo 2019 hats in the gallery above, and pick up a selection of pieces on November 29 from its stores in London, New York, Los Angeles and Toyko, as well as Dover Street Market London and online.
