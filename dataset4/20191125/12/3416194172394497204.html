Stephen Amell & Wife Cassandra Jean Support the L.A. Rams!

25 Nov 2019, 12:28

Stephen Amell and his wife Cassandra Jean are sporting their L.A. Rams gear on their way to a game!

The 38-year-old Arrow star was seen supporting his favorite team while making the trek from Palm Springs to Los Angeles for the game on Monday (November 25).

The Rams are playing the Baltimore Ravens for the big game on ESPN. At the time of this posting, the game is still in progress, but things aren’t looking good for the Rams as they’re down by a lot!
