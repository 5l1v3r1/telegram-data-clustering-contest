Nokia is Teasing a New Phone to Be Revealed Next Month

25 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Shabana Arif

Nokia will be announcing a new smartphone to close out the year that will be unveiled on December 5.

The impending announcement was teased via the company's official Twitter account with a brief video that has diddly squat to do with a handset. The stylised clip shows an outdoorsy type running through a mountain range, scaling cliff faces, and generally having a whale of a time, before it cuts to roiling clouds and sneakily flashes the outline of the mystery phone.
