Second Major Update to ‘Monster Hunter World: Iceborne’ Adds Stygian Zinogre, Launches December 5

25 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Mike Wilson

Capcom has announced that the second major title update for the Monster Hunter World: Iceborne expansion will add Stygian Zinogre as a huntable monster and launch on December 5.

The update will also include new weapons and armour sets, a new region in the Guiding Lands (Tundra Region), the launch of the first Astera Fest following the launch of the expansion, and for PlayStation 4 users, the second and third Horizon Zero Dawn: The Frozen Wilds collaboration quests. Capcom will also be releasing a developer diary on December 4th that fully details the update.

Monster Hunter: World is available now for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC, while the Monster Hunter World: Iceborne expansion is available now for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. The PC release of the expansion is due out on January 9, 2020.
