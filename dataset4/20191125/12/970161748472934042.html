AMAs: Taylor and Billy Porter adorably react to Shawn and Camila's almost-kiss

25 Nov 2019, 12:53

When Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello almost kissed at the end of their American Music Awards performance last night, Taylor Swift and Billy Porter couldn’t contain their excitement.

And honestly, same.

The loved up couple were singing their hit-duet Señorita and things were certainly heating up on stage.

But at the end – much like their VMA performance a couple of months ago – they lean in and look like they’re about to kiss… then they don’t.

It’s a real rollercoaster of emotions and it looked like we weren’t the only ones feeling it.

Just as Shawn and Camila look like they’re about to lock lips, the camera cuts to Taylor, 29, and Billy, 50, who are in peak fangirl mode in the audience.

Taylor held onto Billy’s shoulder in anticipation, as Billy raised his hand to his mouth in shock.
