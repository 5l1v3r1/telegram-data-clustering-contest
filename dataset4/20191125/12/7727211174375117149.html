Adidas launch its early Black Friday sale with 50% off trainers and clothing

There are more than 6,000 products included in its sale

25 Nov 2019, 12:36 by Catherine Murphy

Black Friday doesn't officially take place until Friday, November 29 but that hasn't stopped some retailer launching deals early.

Adidas is the latest brand to begin offering its customers discount, with up to 50% off more than 6,000 items.

The discount is available through its website with everything from clothing to trainers included.

There are more than 600 items which currently have 50% off the price and around 30 with 60% off.

Stand out deals include the Germany Mens Away Primeknit Sweatshirt which was £219.95 now £107.97, Nemeziz 18.1 Firm ground boots reduced from £179.95 now £89.97 and the FC Bayern Adidas Z.N.E Hooded jacket was £109.95 now £54.98.

Women's trainers, clothing and accessories are also included.

Adidas are yet to reveal how long its savings will be running for, but its deals can be found here.

The sports brand has followed Asda , B&M , Smyths , Amazon and Argos who have already released Black Friday offers.
