Akshay Kumar Mourns The Sudden Death Of His Housefull 4 Sound Technician Nimish Pilankar

25 Nov 2019, 12:48 by Amruta Patel

Taking to his Twitter handle, Akshay Kumar mourned the death of his Housefull 4 sound technician Nimish Pilankar. The actor paid his condolence to the family. Nimish passed away at the age of 29 after he suffered high blood pressure resulting in brain hemorrhage

Akshay Kumar mourned the death of sound technician Nimish Pilankar, in his recent Twitter post. The actor took to his Twitter handle, shared the news of Nimish Pilankar's demise, and offered his condolences to the sound technician’s family. Nimish was the sound technician of Akshay’s Diwali release, Housefull 4.  According to reports in India Today, he suffered high blood pressure, leading to brain hemorrhage. Nimish began his career in the stream of entertainment with Salman Khan’s film Race 3, followed by which we worked on Bypass Road and Marjaavan and few others in recent times.

Akki’s tweet read, “Very sad to learn about the passing away of Nimish Pilankar, that too at such a young age. My heart goes out to his family at this difficult time.” Nimish who aged 29, was a resident of Mumbai and his early death also triggered a lot of discussion on the social media platforms, about the work pressure on sound technicians, employed in showbiz. Though not many renowned faces of the industry spoke about Nimish’s sudden early death, actor Vipin Sharma was seen joining the discussion.
