8 of the best Christmas gifts for adventurers, handpicked by Bear Grylls

25 Nov 2019, 12:13 by Telegraph Writers
Survival expert Bear Grylls has been shocking the fainthearted with his daredevil antics for many years. From world records to book deals to multiple hit TV shows, he is one of the most popular men in his field. Here he chooses his favourite 2019 gifts for adventurers.

Some of Bear Grylls' earliest memories are of him and his late father climbing the sea cliffs of the Isle of Wight. He would later win the Guinness World Record as the youngest British person to successfully scale Mount Everest at only 23-years-old (not long after breaking his spine in a parachuting accident and going through intense rehab). These experiences would set the foundation for his life as one of the world's most famous survival experts.

Today, the world has watched Grylls get dropped into the some of the world's most inhospitable places in Man vs Wild, showing off his expert survival skills, and later taking prominent celebrities on these journeys with him on Running Wild with Bear Grylls. 

Grylls knows what it takes to have a thrilling adventure and create an exciting widlife show, but he also appreciates the joys of quality family time at home. For him, the perfect Christmas is "mad family members, fires, cosy all together at home in the UK – December is always a fun month. For us, it’s a chance to hole up for a bit and recharge."
