Death of man found in Ballymun a ‘personal tragedy’

The body was found in a container on Balbutcher Lane on Monday morning

25 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Conor Gallagher

Gardaí are treating the death of a man whose body was found in a container in Ballymun, Dublin as a “personal tragedy”.

The remains of the man in his 40s have  been taken to the Dublin City Morgue where a postmortem examination will be carried out on Tuesday.

The body was found in a container on Balbutcher Lane in Ballymun at about 10.50am on Monday.

In a statement the Garda Press Office said: “Pending the outcome of the postmortem, An Garda Síochána are treating this case as a personal tragedy at this time. “
