Prince Kaybee on his plans to retire at 40

25 Nov 2019, 12:45

Mbalenhle Zuma

Cape Town - Award-winning DJ and producer Prince Kaybee – real name Kabelo Motsamai –  recently announced that he plans to retire at the age of 40.

He also shared just how much pressure he feels to achieve all his dreams and desires within the next ten years.

The Fetch your life hitmaker took to Twitter to share his thoughts with his fans.

“Knowing that I’m retiring at 40, that's only ten years from now. I really need to shift gears quickly so that I achieve all the things I want in my career. Damn from next year it will be nine years left.”

Kaybee recently turned 30 and has had a great 2019 musically and produced a lot of club bangers.
