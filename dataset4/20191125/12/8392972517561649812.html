Jaqueline Jossa's mum pleads with fans to stop voting her for Bushtucker trials

They think she has already proven herself and someone else should be voted.

25 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Georgia Logan

Jacqueline Jossa's mum has begged I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! viewers to stop voting her for Bushtucker Trials.

Concerned mother Selina has followed calls from the former EastEnders star's husband Dan Osborne after he pleaded with fans to back off, and now she has urged people to "vote for someone else".

She told the Mirror Online: "It is a lot for her. I think she has proven herself so they could vote for someone else.

"There are trials where they all take part and she is doing them as well as the other ones. She needs a little break."

However, Selina thinks she could be partly to blame for this as she admitted they have such a "big family" who were all told to vote for Jacqueline.

She admitted: "It is partly my fault because we have got a really big family in Ireland and I think they all keep voting for her because I told them to at the start."

Selina's comments come after Dan made a similar plea on social media as he asked fans to stop choosing her for horrible tasks on the jungle show.

In a video posted online, he said: "Give her a break please because she's getting upset now. I know I said you all vote for Jac cos she's ain't in there to change lightbulbs - but I didn't mean every single trial.

"Give her a break now, come on. But she keeps smashing them all. She's worried about it but she'll smash it.

"She'll keep proving, everyone. Let her do this one then give her a break please because she's getting upset now and it ain't nice. But she'll get all the stars, smash it and then go back and have a nice dinner."

His comments come after Jacqueline was voted to do the Crevice of Cruelty trial.
