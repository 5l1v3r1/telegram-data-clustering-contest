Selena's New Tattoo Is Almost Identical To Ex Justin's

25 Nov 2019, 12:44 by Jessica Maciel

Selena's Matching Tatt

This weekend as she was prepping to head to the American Music Awards, Selena Gomez uploaded a few pictures of her outfit to Instagram.

The cute Polaroid collage featured the singers evening dress but that was not the only thing she included.

In the bottom left hand corner of the post she included what appears to be a new tattoo.

The 27 year old already has a few tattoos but fans spotted something different about this one.

The tattoo on her upper thigh is almost identical to her ex-boyfriend Justin Bieber's leg tattoo.

Both tattoos are of clasped praying hands with the only difference being that Selena chose to include rosary beads in hers.

It's a popular tattoo design but there is no doubting that this is a pretty big coincidence.

The song mashup

Only a few weeks ago fans were swooning over a mashup that appeared on YouTube of one of Selena and Justin's songs.

The mix included Gomez's new single Lose You To Love Me and Bieber's hit single Sorry.
