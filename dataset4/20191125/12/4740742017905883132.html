'Non-starter' - What Bristol City, Leeds United and Aston Villa are saying about Liverpool striker set for loan

He is set to depart Anfield in January

25 Nov 2019, 12:46 by David Byrom

Liverpool are set to allow Rhian Brewster to leave the club on loan in January.

Game time has been limited for the 19-year-old this season, with the striker managing just two appearances in the EFL Cup.

Bristol City have long been linked with the highly-rated starlet, but the Robins face plenty of competition for his signature.

Leeds United and Aston Villa are the latest clubs to be credited with an interest in Brewster, although Swansea City and Crystal Palace are also said to be monitoring his situation.

Here is what fans of Bristol City, Liverpool, Leeds and Aston Villa are saying about the deal.
