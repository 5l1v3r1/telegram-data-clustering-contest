Bhogle vs Manjrekar: Was Sanjay Manjrekar really wrong?

Former India cricketer Sanjay Manjrekar has been facing backlash after his audio clip of on-air spat with Harsha Bhogle regarding the visibility of the pink ball went viral after India's maiden Pink ball Test.

25 Nov 2019, 12:50

Sanjay Manjrekar was severely criticized after his on-air spat with Harsha Bhogle during India's crushing victory over Bangladesh in the historic Day-night Test in Kolkata.

It is not often two Indian commentators spar on air. But it turned ugly enough for Manjrekar, who played 37 Tests and 74 ODIs for India to remind Bhogle, who was the more established cricket expert.

The Day-night Test captured the imagination of the Indian cricket circuit for many reasons. And the pink ball was obviously the talk of the town. How would it behave under lights? Would there be a prodigious swing? Would the pronounced seam make it easier for the spinners?

Harsha Bhogle, a seasoned commentator, suggested a post-mortem and reckoned India's cricketers needed to be asked about the visibility of the pink ball under twilight.

"Well there a post-mortem should be done on this game. Visibility of that ball will be a big factor against the big white sight-screen," Bhogle said.

Manjrekar was quick to respond.
