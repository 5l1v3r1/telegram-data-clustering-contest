Cam'ron's Girlfriend Tawasa Harris Passed Away

25 Nov 2019, 12:45 by VladTV

Cam'ron's girlfriend Tawasa Harris has reportedly passed away, which was confirmed by her close friend in a Facebook post. Media outlets were reporting that Harris is the mother of Cam'ron's son, referencing an Instagram tribute the rapper made in 2018. However, Harris is not the mother of Cam'ron's son. 

Cam'ron has yet to comment on Harris' death, but we'll be sure to update you when more information is available.
