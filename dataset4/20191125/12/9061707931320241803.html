Burton curry house hopes regulars will return after fixing hygiene problems

'We wanted a clean slate', bosses have said

25 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Rhea Turner

A well-known Burton curry house that went downhill after a damning hygiene inspection has turned its fortunes around.

Manzil hit the headlines for the wrong reasons in March when inspectors found a risk of "cross-contamination" in its kitchen and gave it a one-star hygiene rating.

Since then new director Abdul Muhit has taken the reins - and a recent re-inspection has seen the Guild Street restaurant given five stars.

Mr Muhit, 26, said the business suffered as a result of the previous inspection, which also raised issues including food left uncovered and bare chipboard exposed on shelves.

But after closing for a major refurbishment, it reopened as Manzil Gold on July 22 and has made major improvements.

Mr Muhit, who took over on July 8, said: "We have been working really hard to show customers what we have to offer but a lot of people have been waiting for our re-inspection.

"They are happy with the food and the service but they were not happy with the hygiene rating.

"The restaurant has always had a really good reputation but that rating tainted it and we were really suffering as a result. We wanted a clean slate."

Mr Muhit believes the new hygiene rating will see more customers coming through the door.

He said: "The business went downhill after that report was published and they couldn’t recover. They couldn’t survive and run it after that.

"I think the previous regulars felt let down and I think they will be coming back through the doors now.

"We feel like we have waited so long for this. I'm really happy that the inspection went well and we finally have our five stars which we deserve.

"I hope now people will see we have worked hard to turn it back around and they will start coming back to see what we have to offer."

Inspectors who visited Manzil Gold on November 18 found only one issue that needed rectifying within a month.

The report read: "There were some gaps/holes noted in the dry goods store rooms and a hole was also noted in the bar area next to the wash hand basin."
