2019 American Music Awards: Taylor Swift – Artist Of The Decade Award To Billie Eilish – New Artist Of The Year, Winners’ List REVEALED

The big night was hosted by Ciara and had a few memorable speeches by Taylor Swift and Billie Eilish. Swift, who was nominated in five categories, broke the record for most AMA wins of all time

25 Nov 2019, 12:41 by Koimoi.com Team

2019 American Music Awards were one hell of a night. From Taylor Swift winning Artist Of The Year to Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello winning Best collaboration Of The Year award. The night was one star studded affair attended by Halsey, Lizzo, Billie Eilish and Lil Nas X.

The big night was hosted by Ciara and had a few memorable speeches by Taylor Swift and Billie Eilish. Swift, who was nominated in five categories, broke the record for most AMA wins of all time; she’s already won 23 AMAs throughout her career and tonight’s win has made history. She has crossed Michael Jackson’s record too.
