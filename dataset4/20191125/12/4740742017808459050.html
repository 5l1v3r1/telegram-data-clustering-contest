Fried chicken restaurant Slim Chickens opening in Cabot Circus

It will serve the flavours of Southern USA and specialise in "life-changing chicken"

25 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Robin Murray

Fans of fried chicken will soon have another option to choose from in Bristol city centre when a new restaurant opens in Cabot Circus.

Slim Chickens, launching on the top floor of the shopping centre, will serve the flavours of the southern states of the USA and specialise in "life-changing chicken", according to a spokesperson for the chain.

It will step into the unit occupied by Giraffe.

The branch will be the sixth Slim Chickens to open in the UK - with the other five sites in London, Cardiff and Birmingham.

The spokesperson added its restaurants offer "100 per cent British, fresh, buttermilk-marinated, hand-breaded and cooked-to-order premium chicken".

The menu will feature the brand’s most popular chicken dishes alongside a selection of 13 dipping sauces including garlic parmesan, honey BBQ, ranch and Slim’s own best-selling signature sauce.

To top it off, diners can choose from a selection of five signature sides and treat themselves to an indulgent milkshake or dessert.

Bristol's Slim Chickens restaurant opens on Friday, November 29.

About Slim Chickens

From testing chicken recipes in a garage, Tom Gordon and Greg Smart went on to open the first Slim Chickens in Fayetteville, Arkansas, in 2003 with the ambition of creating freshly cooked chicken for guests served fast.

Over a decade later, the business has grown to more than 80 sites in the US alone.

Slim Chickens is operated by the Boparan Restaurant Group, which is expanding throughout the UK over the next two years.

Tom Crowley, CEO at Boparan Restaurant Group, said: "The positive reaction we have received to Slim Chickens from customers across the UK has been phenomenal.

"We’re really excited to be opening in Bristol, there’s already been a lot of excitement and anticipation.
