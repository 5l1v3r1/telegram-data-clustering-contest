Slaying in black: Bhumi Pednekar is the new fashionista in town and these pictures are proof of it

25 Nov 2019, 12:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

With the release date of 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' coming closer, leading actors Bhumi Pednekar, Kartik Aaryan and Ananya Panday are leaving no stone unturned while promoting their film. The trio has been having a gala time promoting the film but what grabbed our attention was Bhumi's promotional outfit.

The 30-year-old actress turned up in a sizzling back outfit and posed for the shutterbugs with the right attitude of a diva. Her transformation has been noteworthy and many critics have hailed her on-screen persona as well.

With a bow at the front and a fluffy sleeved knee-length black dress, Bhumi has been making heads turn as she completed her look by opting for her tresses to be tied behind in a bun and sported a cat-eye sunnies as well.

Meanwhile, on the other hand, Kartik and Ananya donned spunky casual as the trio stepped out to promote their film 'Pati Patni Aur Woh'. The film is slated to release on 6th December and will clash with Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon starrer period drama 'Panipat'.
