Post Malone Wins 2019 AMA For Favorite Rap/Hip-Hop Album

25 Nov 2019, 12:35 by VladTV

Votes from the public reportedly determine who wins the American Music Awards, and now the public has decided that Post Malone should be given the ‘Favorite Rap/Hip Hop Album’ award for his project ‘Hollywood Bleeding.’

This left many on social media upset and confused, mainly because Post Malone’s album barely contains any hip hop. His album beat out other projects that were nominated in the category like Meek Mill’s ‘Championships’ as well as Travis Scott’s ‘Astroworld.’
