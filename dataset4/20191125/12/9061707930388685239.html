When Santa will be calling in at your street in Swadlincote

He will be meeting good boys and girls

25 Nov 2019, 12:46 by Beth Pridding

Santa will be hopping in his sleigh to visit all the good boys and girls from around Swadlincote from next week.

The big man himself will be joined by his helpful elves from Swadlincote Round Table as volunteers travel around town to spread festive cheer and raise money for good causes.

This year, Father Christmas will be travelling around estates in Newhall, Midway, Gresley, Swadlincote and Woodville to find out what youngsters would like to see under their Christmas tree.

A spokesman for Swadlincote Round Table said: "On week nights we try to visit between 6pm and 8pm, except on Fridays, when we continue until 8.30pm.

"Please understand that whilst we try to visit at times suitable for children it isn't possible for us to be outside everyone's house at a time that is ideal for them.

"Wherever possible we will try to stick to this schedule, but time and weather may mean that we have to adapt as we go through December.

"We will be keeping Monday, November 23, free as an optional extra night to cover any areas that time or weather prevent us getting to as planned."
