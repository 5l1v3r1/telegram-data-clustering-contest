The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt Comes to Xbox Game Pass on December 19

By Sean Carey, 25 Nov 2019
Comments
It was announced at X019 that The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt would be joining the already stacked out Xbox Game Pass lineup; however, we didn't have an official date as to when it would arrive, only "Holiday 2019". Now the Xbox Game Pass app is showing The Witcher 3 as coming to the service on December 19.
