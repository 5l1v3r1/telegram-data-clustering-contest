John Dumelo and son hang out with former President John Dramani Mahama (Photo)

25 Nov 2019, 12:34 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Ghanaian actor turned politician John Dumelo together with his son John Dumelo Jnr. has been spotted hanging out with the NDC flagbearer for the 2020 elections John Dramani Mahama.

Though much is not known about the visit, it is believed that the Johns’ met to look at the way forward in seizing power from the Akuffo Addo led NPP in the next elections which is just a year away.

Actor John Dumelo is seeking to be elected as a Member of Parliament to represent the people of Ayawaso West Wuogon on the ticket of the NDC.

Former President John Dramani Mahama, on the other hand, is seeking to be elected for the second time to head the affairs of the country after losing massively to the Nana Akuffo Addo in the 2016 Elections.
