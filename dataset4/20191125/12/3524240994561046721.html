Ubah advocates sustainable peace in Anambra communities

25 Nov 2019, 12:59 by Sunday Aborisade

Sunday Aborisade, Abuja

The Senator representing Anambra South Senatorial District, Ifeanyi Ubah, has urged the various communities in the state to sustain the peace currently being enjoyed in the state.

Uba, who is the Vice Chairman of the Senate Committee on Petroleum Upstream, also assured them that he would always support their efforts in promoting peace and national unity.

The Anambra South Senator, who is also the National Leader of the Young Progressive Party, according to a statement by his media office on Monday, stated this when he hosted members of the Old Aguata Union, Nnewi branch during their 2019 end of the year party.

The statement explained that the Old Aguata Union is made up of indigenes of Orumba North, Orumba South and Aguata Local Government Areas.

The LGs were created out of the old Aguata Local Government Area of Anambra state.

Ubah urged the traditional rulers and titleholders of the Local Government Areas of the union to sustain the existing peace in their various communities.

The event took place at the Nnewi residence of the Anambra South Senator.

It was attended by Nnewi members of the Union, their traditional rulers and stakeholders.
