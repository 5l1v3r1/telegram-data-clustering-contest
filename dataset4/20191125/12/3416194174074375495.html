Nathan Kress Bids Farewell To 'Henry Danger' With Sweet New Family Pic

25 Nov 2019, 12:11

Nathan Kress is saying goodbye to another long running Nickelodeon show – Henry Danger.

Taking to social media this weekend, the now 27-year-old actor and director shared his goodbyes to the show with an updated family picture featuring his wife, London and their young daughter, Rosie.

“5 years. A lot has changed. Not just the fact that I can’t fit into those khakis any more…,” Nathan wrote. “Congrats to the Henry Danger team on wrapping their fifth and final season, spanning a massive 120+ episodes. It’s been an honor and a privilege!”

Nathan directed 10 of the show’s episodes from 2015 until the end of series.

Series star Jace Norman first hinted at the end of the show earlier this fall.
