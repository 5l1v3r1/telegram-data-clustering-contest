Shah Rukh Khan and Abhishek Bachchan join hands for Kahaani spin-off Bob Biswas

Bob Biswas, a spin-off to the popular character from 2012 film Kahaani will star Abhishek Bachchan. Diya Annapurna Ghosh will make her directorial debut with the film that'll be co-produced by Shah Rukh Khan and Sujoy Ghosh.

25 Nov 2019, 12:21 by Entertainment Desk

Shah Rukh Khan on Monday announced his next production venture titled Bob Biswas, starring Abhishek Bachchan in the lead role. Filmmaker Sujoy Ghosh’s daughter Diya Annapurna Ghosh is set to make her directorial debut with the thriller, which will be co-produced by SRK’s Red Chillies Entertainment and Bound Script Production.

Bob Biswas is a spin-off of the poker-faced contract killer by the same name from 2012 hit Kahaani. The film will depict the different facets of his dual nature. It will go on floors in early 2020 and will release in the same year.

The official Twitter handle of Red Chillies Entertainment revealed the news with a tweet that read, “Nomoshkar! Thrilled to announce our upcoming film, #BobBiswas in association with Bound Script Production; starring @juniorbachchan and directed by Diya Annapurna Ghosh. @gaurikhan @iamsrk @sujoy_g @_GauravVerma”
