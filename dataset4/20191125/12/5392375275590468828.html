Xiaomi is all set to introduce its brand-new Mi Note 10 Pro in Malaysia in the coming days. Apart from Malaysia, the smartphone is also making its way to the Indian market. Xiaomi India Head, Manu Jain, recently confirmed the same on his official Twitter account. According to his recent post, a smartphone with 108MP primary camera will soon be launching in India.
