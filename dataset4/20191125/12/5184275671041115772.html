Ileana D’Cruz On Body Shaming: “I’ve Been Harassed By Body Type”

On the work front, she will be next seen in The Big Bull opposite Abhishek Bachchan.

25 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Koimoi.com Team

Ileana D’Cruz made her comeback with Anees Bazmee’s Pagalpanti which was released last week after almost a year. She was last seen in Raid opposite Ajay Devgn. The film gained pretty good numbers at the box office.

Meanwhile, on a personal level, Ileana has been going through a lot. She recently broke up with her longtime boyfriend, Andrew Kneebone. In an interview with Pinkvilla, she spoke about body shaming and how people called her abnormal.

“I get annoyed when there are warped pictures of me, where they will enhance certain things and make me look bigger than I actually am. If I have put on weight, it’s fine. But I have dealt with bullying since I was 13-14. That’s a vulnerable and sensitive age because you’re just starting to talk to boys. I have been harassed by my body type so much that I think I have heard it all. I just didn’t like it because I was looking at myself from that person’s perspective. People would be like, ‘This isn’t normal’, why do you have a body like that?’ I have reached that point where I’m happy. It’s entertaining and funny.”

She further added, “I recently read a comment which said, ‘I don’t like her arms’. And I’m like, I don’t like them too, but they aren’t that bad.”

On the work front, she will be next seen in The Big Bull opposite Abhishek Bachchan.
