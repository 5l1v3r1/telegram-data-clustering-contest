Bernie Sanders pens scathing letter to Rob Manfred, condemns plan to contract Minor League Baseball

25 Nov 2019, 20:10 by Joe Rivera

Bernie Sanders is standing up for MiLB.

The U.S. senator and presidential hopeful published a letterMonday, addressed toMLB commissioner Rob Manfred, condemning the proposed plan to eliminate 42 minor league squads across the country, citing "greed" as themain motive behind the plan.
