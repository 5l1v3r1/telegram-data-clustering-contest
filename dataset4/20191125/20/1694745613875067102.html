Presenting All the Winners of the 2019 American Music Awards

25 Nov 2019, 20:40 by Mekishana Pierre

Sunday night was full of high energy and incredible performances, thanks to the American Music Awards, and watching our favorite artists win big only made it more exciting. The night's big winners included first-time winners Billie Eilish and Halsey, and, of course, the guest of honor, Taylor Swift. Take a look at the winners below!
