Davante Adams Issues Message To Fans After Blowout Loss

25 Nov 2019, 20:44 by Alek Arend

The Packers suffered a shocking blowout loss to the San Francisco 49ers on Sunday night. The game was expected to be a close battle with QB Aaron Rodgers trying to out-duel the San Francisco defense. But Rodgers had minimal time to pass as the 49ers’ defensive line got pressure all game long.

Since the Packers’ Sunday night loss, major concerns are starting to arise. Is Green Bay in serious trouble?

Packers WR Davante Adams took to Twitter on Monday to reassure fans the team will get better because of the loss.

“We gotta be better!! And we will,” Adams said on his person Twitter account. “Love my guys thru thick n thin.”
