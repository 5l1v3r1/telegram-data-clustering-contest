Camila Cabello says she loves Shawn Mendes after AMAs performance

25 Nov 2019, 20:39

Camila Cabello dropped the L-bomb on Shawn Mendes after their triumphant night at yesterday’s American Music Awards.

The Senorita singers performed an explosive rendition of their duet on the night, before winning the collaboration of the year gong.

After all the excitement, Camila took to Instagram to express how she really feels about her pop star boyfriend.

‘I love u @shawnmendes,’ she gushed.

She went there.

‘Thank u for being so there for me yesterday and every other day,’ she went on.

‘U have the most caring and beautiful heart in the world! thanks to all our fans for voting, we love and appreciate you guys so much and are so thankful for you.’

Too cute.

Camila, 22, also recently opened up about her relationship on Lorraine, saying: ‘I’m so happy.

‘I’ve known him for such a long time and I don’t know, he just feels like home to me. Yeah, I’m really happy.’

She also confirmed that some of the songs on her upcoming second studio album Romance are about the Stitches singer – and that she never had time to fall in love before meeting Shawn.

Well, she did join Fifth Harmony on X Factor USA at the tender age of 16.

‘Like, from my first album to now, it’s probably what’s influenced my entire album,’ the Havana singer explained. ‘And it being called Romance and it being about falling in love, I literally did not have time to fall in love before the age of 20, because I was just working all the time.’

‘Everything from my first album was drawn from my imagination,’ she added. ‘I was single for like 20 years!’
