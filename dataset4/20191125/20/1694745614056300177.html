Sally Hansen Just Launched Its First Nail Polish Line in Years, and It Was Worth the Wait

25 Nov 2019, 20:15 by Amanda Elser Murray

As POPSUGAR editors, we independently select and write about stuff we love and think you'll like too. POPSUGAR has affiliate and advertising partnerships so we get revenue from sharing this content and from your purchase.

2019 was a big year for my beauty routine. I slowly, but consciously, made the decision to incorporate cleaner products into my routine — something that required a lot of researching, and for my pits, detoxing. But one area that had yet to be affected by my clean-sweep? My weekly manicure appointments. Call me high-maintenance, but there was something about my little nail salon ritual that I couldn't kick, even knowing that it wasn't aligned with my new clean-beauty initiative. Cue Good.Kind.Pure., Sally Hansen's newest plant based, 100 percent vegan nail polish line available at Target.

Yup, the entire line — 30 shades to be exact — of clean nail polish is formulated with plant-based, 100 percent vegan ingredients. The shades are appropriately inspired by nature. Neutral Earthy tones and natural pops of color, there is literally a shade for every season. (I'll be rocking Meteorite this Thanksgiving!) The packaging also include a new plant-based brush, which is optimized to work with the new formula.

And while that's all fine and dandy, what about performance? Well, for the same reason I love Sally Hansen's Salon Perfect line for my at-home manicures, Good.Kind.Pure. makes giving yourself a good manicure at home easy. Two coats, no base coat, and nails looks heal their and stronger. The new line includes a Hardener to improve the look of ridges and seal fragile nails to protect against breaking, cracking and splitting. But if you want a touch more shine and longer finish, the line also comes with its own Good.Kind.Pure. Top Coat.
