PDP pens down inspiring message as Atiku clocks 73

25 Nov 2019, 20:46

- PDP has congratulated Atiku on his 73rd birthday

- Atiku clocks 73 years of age on earth on Monday, November 25

- The party described the former vice president as an exceptional democrat

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) has congratulated its presidential candidate for the 2019 general elections, Atiku Abubakar, on the occasion of his 73rd birthday.

Legit.ng reports that the party in a statement issued on Monday, November 25, by its national publicity secretary, Kola Ologbondiyan, described the former vice president as an exceptional patriot who leads light and incontrovertible symbol of the collective quest by Nigerians for justice for all.

The statement read: "Atiku Abubakar is an exceptional patriot, quintessential democrat, leading light and incontrovertible symbol of the collective quest by Nigerians for a united, peaceful, secure, democratic and prosperous nation governed by the rule of law, equity and justice for all.

"Indeed, Atiku Abubakar leads the trail in the hope for a much-desired national rebirth, anchored on purposeful leadership where the overall interest of the people is over and above selfish personal interests."
