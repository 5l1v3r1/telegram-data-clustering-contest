Lewis Capaldi to hold LIVE ‘Big Fat Sexy Q&A’ with fans before Glasgow gigs next month

25 Nov 2019, 20:31 by Paul Smith

LEWIS Capaldi will treat fans to a live Q&A session before his Glasgow gigs next month.

The Scots singing sensation, 23, from West Lothian, will take to the stage at the Scottish Exhibition Centre on December 7 and 8 before his gigs at the O2 Academy.

The sessions, which will last around half an hour, will give fans the chance to ask him more about his life.

Lewis has called the event 'Big Fat Sexy Q&A'.

But it seems some Capaldi lovers are having difficulty getting their hands on tickets - with social media users are reporting problems purchasing them.

One person tweeted: "I have received my code for the Lewis Capaldi Q&A but it doesn't seem to be working.

"It's unlocking the tickets but when I go to check out it says there is an error."

Another said: "Literally had second row tickets for the Q&A, went to pay and then it stopped working great."
