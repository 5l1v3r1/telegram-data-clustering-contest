Strictly's AJ Pritchard slams judges for undermarking Saffron Barker

25 Nov 2019, 20:23

Strictly Come Dancing’s AJ Pritchard has blamed the judges for his and Saffron Barker’s elimination this weekend after they underscored their samba routine.

The pair scored just 26 out of 40 for their samba dance to Walking On Sunshine, placing joint bottom of the leaderboard at the end of Saturday’s live show.

After failing to get the votes from the public to stay, the pair landed in the dance-off with Karim Zeroual and Amy Dowden, with the judges voting unanimously to save Karim and Amy.

Speaking on It Takes Two this evening to discuss their exit, AJ made no secret of the fact that he thought that they should’ve earned a higher mark.

‘[Saffron] did amazing, you attacked it so positively and for me the dance was great,’ he said, adding pointedly, ‘Which I felt was underscored.’

He added: ‘Our fans saved us definitely, I feel like the judges sent home. It was a sad evening.’

‘I didn’t see any of them technical issues, I’m going to throw it out there,’ he told Rylan when discussing the critiques they received.

‘Obviously the five [score] from Craig, oh Jesus, I took that one to the heart,’ he added.

Saffron was left in tears on Sunday night’s show after being told she wouldn’t be going through to the quarter finals musicals week next week.

When the pair were sent home, Saffron told Tess Daly: ‘I’ve had the absolute time of my life and it’s all down to him [AJ]. It’s been amazing.

‘I’d like to thank AJ so much, when I look at what I was like on week one I was so embarrassed and shy.’

She added: ‘He’s really taken me out of my shell. Thank you so much for everything.’
