West Ham fans react as report suggests Chris Wilder interest

25 Nov 2019, 20:15 by Jordan Harris

A number of West Ham United fans have taken to Twitter to comment on reports from the Daily Mirror suggesting that the Hammers are interested in Chris Wilder.

Manuel Pellegrini appears to be coming under increasing pressure at the London Stadium. The Irons have not won a game since September, and they have now lost their last three. They have conceded nine in the process.

The Daily Mirror suggests that a list of potential candidates has been put together in case Pellegrini does depart.

The Mirror suggests that both David Moyes and Chris Hughton are on the club’s radar. But it is also suggested that the Hammers are interested in Sheffield United boss Wilder.

Wilder has done a fantastic job at Bramall Lane. He has won two promotions in three seasons. And United now sit sixth in their first campaign back in the Premier League.

West Ham fans pessimistic

It is no surprise that other top flight sides are taking an interest in Wilder. And it appears that plenty of West Ham fans would happily see Wilder replace Pellegrini.

However, it is mentioned more than once that they cannot see why Wilder would choose to leave Sheffield United for West Ham. There is a large gap between the two in the table right now, and the Hammers supporters are clearly worried that their club is not moving in the right direction.
