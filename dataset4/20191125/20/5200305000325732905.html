UK Ratings: ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ on Hum TV tops Pakistani TV genre

25 Nov 2019, 20:21 by Raj Baddhan

Hum TV shows continued to lead in UK ratings, with ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ remaining the most watched programme across all Pakistani TV shows in official BARB data for week ending Sunday 17th November 2019.

1. Ehd-E-Wafa (Hum TV)
Audience: 73,600 viewers

2. Ishq Zah-e-Naseeb (Hum TV)
Audience: 66,200 viewers

3. Daasi (Hum TV)
Audience: 62,600 viewers

4. Main Na Janu (Hum TV)
Audience: 59,000 viewers

5. Yeh Dil Mera (Hum TV)
Audience: 50,700 viewers
