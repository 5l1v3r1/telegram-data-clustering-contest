How can you vote for the Bushtucker Trials using the I’m a Celebrity app?

25 Nov 2019, 20:01

Part of the joy of I’m a Celebrity each year is watching stars embarrass, challenge and push themselves to do the unthinkable, all from the comfort of your sofa.

A football hero screaming for his mum? Check. A reality icon chowing on a cow’s eye? Done.

Viewers have the power to choose which stars face the Bushtucker Trials – a power Dan Osborne is asking fans to wield with more kindness for his wife Jacqueline Jossa.

How do you vote for the Bushtucker Trials?

How to vote for the Bushtucker trials

You can use the official I’m a Celebrity voting app to choose which celeb you want to see tackle the Bushtucker Trial.

The app can be downloaded from the iTunes store or Google Play.

You’ll need iOS 9 or later, and Android 4.1 or later.

You won’t be charged for downloading or voting through it but data charges may still apply.

To vote, download the app and navigate the user face to get to the voting section.

You must register before you can start casting votes and you’ll need a valid iTunes account to register.

Fans will have to wait until Ant and Dec announce the vote is open during the live show – once it’s open, fans will only have a few minutes to open the app and cast up to five votes.

If you can’t download the app – or don’t want to – you can still vote in the Bushtucker Trials.
