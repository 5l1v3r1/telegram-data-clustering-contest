Injury Update For Oakland Raiders WR Hunter Renfrow

25 Nov 2019, 20:32 by Matt Hladik

Not only did the Oakland Raiders get blown out by the New York Jets on Sunday, but they also lost promising rookie wide receiver Hunter Renfrow to an injury.

Renfrow took a big hit from Jets rookie cornerback Blessuan Austin in the second quarter of yesterday’s loss. He was clearly in pain on the ground after the play.

While the hit from Austin was clean and legal, many thought it might have constituted pass interference. Either way, he laid a lick on Renfrow.
