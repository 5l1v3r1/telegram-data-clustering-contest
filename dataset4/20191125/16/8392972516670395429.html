Christmas Santa duvet sets are available from Wowcher for £12.99

Usual price is nearly fifty quid!

25 Nov 2019, 16:21 by Robert Leigh

Good news for those who are already starting to live, sleep and breathe Christmas - you can actually now be all festive while you're slumbering!

It may not even be December yet but these duvet sets found on Wowcher should bring a bit of Christmas cheer to the bedrooms of all festive fans.

This fun bedding - available as 'single Boy', 'single Girl' and 'couples' - allows customers to celebrate the season all from the comfort of their own bed.

The single options cost £12.99 and the couples version is priced at £16.99.

But elsewhere online they can retail for nearly £50 - so this really is a Christmas present!

The couples version portrays Father Christmas and Mrs Christmas holding hands as snowflakes fall against a pale grey background.

The duvet illustrations do, however, lack faces and heads - and that's where napping bed residents come in as they rest on their pillows!

The reversible furnishing is also 52% polyester and 48% cotton and should be washed at 40 degrees.
