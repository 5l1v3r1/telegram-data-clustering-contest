Miami Heat unveil new ViceWave City jersey

25 Nov 2019, 16:23

The Miami Heat unveiled their new ViceWave City Edition uniform on Monday.

The new look is inspired by sun-drenched South Beach afternoons, according to the team. The Heat says the new color scheme is a shift from the neon colors in their previous alternative uniforms that represented the city's famous nightlife.

The new uniform's primary color is blue gale with pink, black and white lettering and numbers.

The uniform was designed by the Heat's in-house creative department.
