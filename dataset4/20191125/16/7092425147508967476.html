A number of classic Christmas movies are returning to Irish cinemas next month

25 Nov 2019, 16:56 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

With just one month to go until Christmas Day, Omniplex Cinemas are cranking up a notch on nostalgia in a seasonal run of their popular #BringItBack classic movie screenings.

Omniplex Cinemas #BringItBack Christmas season kicks off with the one, the only, Buddy the Elf!
