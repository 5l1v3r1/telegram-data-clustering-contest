YSR Congress MLA Knocks Supreme Court on Cash for Vote Case

25 Nov 2019, 16:38 by Sridhar Raavi

YSR Congress seems to be desperate to see Chandrababu Naidu behind the bars. They have now brought Cash for Vote case once again into the limelight. YSR Congress Controversial MLA, Alla Ramakrishna Reddy approached a petition in Supreme Court seeking an early hearing in Cash for Vote case of May 2015.

Revanth Reddy, who is now an MP from Congress was arrested on a charge of offering Rs 50 Lakh to nominated MLC Elvis Stephenson to vote for the TDP candidate in Telangana MLC polls. A call record was also surfaced with a voice similar to Chandrababu assuring all that is promised to Stephenson.

However, the case did not progress after the initial Hungama. On the other side, the ACB court also agreed to revive a 2005 disproportionate assets case on Chandrababu Naidu filed by Lakshmi Parvathi who is now a YSR Congress Leader.

YSR Congress is hopeful that Chandrababu Naidu will go behind the bars in at least one of these cases.
