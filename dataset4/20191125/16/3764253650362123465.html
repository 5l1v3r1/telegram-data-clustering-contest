Paul Pogba planning to quit Man United and join Real Madrid in January

25 Nov 2019, 16:06

- Paul Pogba could finally join Real Madrid this winter

- The Frenchman is said to be plotting to force a move away from Manchester United

- Pogba has been linked with a move to join Los Blancos in the past months

Manchester United record signing Paul Pogba is reportedly planning to force his way out of Old Trafford in January.

The Frenchman has been endlessly linked with a move to Real Madrid but the deal was blocked by the Premier League side last summer.

Although the 2018 FIFA World Cup star has been out of action since September after picking an injury in the Red Devils 1-1 draw against Arsenal.

Pogba, who is currently recovering from the knock was spotted in Maimi spending time with rapper Pharrell Williams and NBA star Jimmy Butler in between gym sessions.
