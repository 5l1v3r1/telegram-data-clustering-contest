Kylian Mbappe: Zidane in love with PSG star ahead of UCL clash

25 Nov 2019, 16:16

- Kylian Mbappe is highly rated by Real Madrid boss Zinedine Zidane

- Zidane says he is 'already in love' with the striker fueling speculation of signing him in the summer

- Real Madrid and PSG will meet in the Champions League at the Santiago Bernabeu

Kylian Mbappe has been praised by Real Madrid boss Zinedine Zidane ahead of their crunch Champions League tie at the Santiago Bernabeu.

It is understood Los Blancos are preparing a mega bid for the France international in next summer's transfer window.

Zidane's side will be looking to avenge their 4-0 first leg defeat at Parc des Princes in September and the French boss spoke about the possibility of signing the 20-year-old who is valued at £370million.
