Emmerdale's Claire King hints at Graham Foster's exit

Kim usually finds a way to get what she wants

25 Nov 2019, 16:46 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Actress Claire King, who plays villain Kim Tate in Emmerdale, has hinted at Graham Foster's exit.

Recently Graham got on the wrong side of boss and on-and-off lover Kim when he revealed he was in a relationship with vet Rhona Goskirk.

However, it looks like a jealous Kim may have a plan up her sleeve to get rid of them both.

As reported in Metro, talking about Kim and Graham's relationship, Claire said: "I personally always thought that Graham was the one in love with Kim - and Kim loved him but wasn't in love with him.

"They've both had their dalliances in the past and they've allowed that because they had an open relationship.

"But this seems to be different and I think Kim is very worried that Graham might be falling in love with Rhona.

"That's a bit taboo, as far as the Kim/Graham relationship is concerned.

"Kim is now starting to feel threatened and I think she's suddenly realising that she might have deeper feelings for Graham than she thought.

"In an ideal world, Kim expects Graham to keep running back. He has done that every single time in the past.

"They usually get over it, move on and it's business as usual."

Claire then reveals she thinks Kim will try to get Graham and Rhona out of the village.

"But I don't think Graham wants that this time. He just wants a quieter life. Kim feels threatened by this. She'll no doubt try to get them out of the village so the relationship is out of sight."
