- Blogger Stella Dimoko Korkus and actress Tonto Dikeh are currently in an online messy fight

- Nigerians have reacted to the on-going war between the two

The Nigerian Twitter and the Instagram platform is currently buzzing with the on-going drama between actress Tonto Dikeh and blogger, Stella Dimoko Korkus. The fight between the two has got Nigerians talking.

How the online war of words began between the two is still unclear, but it has now become a topic of discussion after some alleged secrets were revealed to the public.

During their fight, Korkus had claimed that Dikeh sleeps with pastors and travels to Dubai to eat poo. She also claimed that Dikeh usually posts empty shopping bags taken from Nigeria abroad to pretend like she shops.
