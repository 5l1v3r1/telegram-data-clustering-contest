New ‘The Walking Dead’ Spin-Off Series Gets an Official Title in a New Teaser

25 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Ethan Anderton

Back in October, when the first trailer for the new The Walking Dead spin-off was revealed, the series still didn’t have a title. All we knew was that the series would focus on two young female protagonists as part of the first generation to come-of-age in the wake of the zombie apocalypse as they venture out into a world that is still full of danger and undead. Now a new teaser not only shows more of the series, but it finally gives the series a title.
