'a star'

Tributes to former Glasgow Uni student Breffni O’Connor who died after falling from yacht in Italy following night out

25 Nov 2019, 16:36 by Abbi Garton

A FORMER student president at the University of Glasgow sadly died in a yachting incident.

Breffni O’Connor passed away while working as a crewhand on a superyacht named Iman in the port of Ancona in Italy.

The 30-year-old, originally from Co Limerick in Ireland, died after returning to the ship following a night out at a local disco on Saturday.

The former zoology and animal biology student is understood to have slipped into the water where the ship was docked, after losing her balance walking the gangway to the boat.

Following the news of her tragic passing tributes flooded in for Breffni, who was described as “a star”.
