7 best-dressed divas from the week gone by

25 Nov 2019, 16:59 by Poonam Jain

Bollywood celebs are always under the spotlight. Hence, they keep their best fashion foot forward at every public appearance. From their hair to makeup, everything about them becomes a fashion statement. Be it starry B-Town parties, their dinner outings or movie promotions -- each time they step out, our Bollywood divas manage to catch all our attention. Kick-starting our Monday on a high, here we pick the most glamorous looks from the week gone by.

1. Kareena Kapoor Khan

Wearing - Dion lee

Styled by - Rhea Kapoor

Kareena Kapoor Khan showed up in this beautiful pastel yellow dress at the promotions of her upcoming movie Good Newwz and won over everyone with her effortless chic. Keeping her hair in soft waves, she completed the look with a pair of sunnies and tan heels.

2.  Sonam Kapoor Ahuja

Wearing - Fatma Shaikh

Styled by - Chandini Whabi

Sonam Kapoor Ahuja on her vacation in LA looks gorgeous in this two-piece olive dress. Her hair was tied in a low bun and her red lips complemented the green dress to a T. Sonam opted for gold hoops and dark brown loafers to complete her look. Sonam’s look is great to draw inspiration from for a day out with your girls.

3. Ananya Panday

Wearing - Floret.Al

Styled by -  Ami Patel

Ananya Panday looked like a stunner in this look from last week. Complementing her all-black attire, the actress went with nude makeup and soft loose curls. Ananya rounded off her look with a pair of black high heels.

4.  Janhvi Kapoor

Dressed in a pink body-con dress, Jahnvi Kapoor at Kartik Aaryan's birthday looked too pretty to miss. She paired her pink dress with sparkly pumps and rocked a red pout.

5. Kiara Advani 

Styled by - Lakshmi Lehr

Kiara Advani totally defines the happy girls look pretty in this look here. She looked extremely elegant in a one-shoulder candy pink dress for the promotions of her movie Good Newwz. Kiara opted for a fresh and glowing look and completed the look with blow-dried hair.

6. Bhumi Pednekar

Wearing – Dhruv Kapoor

Styled by – Pranita Shetty

Bhumi Pednekar wore a shimmer pantsuit with a black bralette at Arpita Khan's bash. She left her hair in loose curls and opted for a brown matte lip to complete her look.

7. Katrina KaifWearing - Self- Portrait 

Katrina Kaif was her usual best at Arpita Khan's bash last week. She looked extremely chic in a small LBD. She opted for minimal makeup  and completed her look with matching black heels.
