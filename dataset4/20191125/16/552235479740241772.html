West Dunbartonshire residents urged to register to vote in general election

You have until midnight on Tuesday, November 26 to make sure you get your say on December 12.

25 Nov 2019, 16:34 by Fraser Clarke, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/fraser-clarke/

Residents across the country have until midnight on Tuesday to make sure that their views are held in next month’s general election.

The deadline for registering to vote is 11.59pm on Tuesday, November 26 and registering takes just five minutes online.

Candidates from across all parties are urging locals to ensure they sign up, with West Dunbartonshire recording a turnout of just 65 percent in 2017.

SNP candidate Martin Docherty tweeted: “Don’t let cold or the rain stop you from voting, so sign up now” whilst Labour rival Jean Anne Mitchell said: “It couldn’t be more important to make sure your voice is heard on December 12.”
