Don't Forget Black Friday Also Applies to the Amazon Warehouse

25 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Tom Pritchard

The Amazon Warehouse is filled with discounted gear, in very limited quantities, because they can't be sold on the main Amazon website. Usually because they've been used and get a discount, or some other reason. Despite being cheaper than normal, Amazon is still adding Black Friday discounts to all that stock.

The sale is on for the same length as the regular Black Friday sale, having started last week and ending at 23:59 on 2nd December. Rather than variable discounts based on the products, this sale knocks 20 per cent off all the products being included. And those products include all sorts. Watches, vacuums, lighting, monitors, toys, and so on.

You can check out everything that's on offer here, and make sure to check back to the main Amazon site to make sure you're not getting ripped off.
