Fans Finally Agree on Star Wars Again, Because It's Playing It Safe

25 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Anthony Gramuglia

With The Mandalorian, Star Wars fandom has seen something it hasn't seen in years: total consensus. Sure, some will complain, but with only a few episodes out, the fans following the events of the galaxy far, far away seem to agree on something: The Mandalorian is good.

It feels like madness to say that, of course. The Star Wars fandom notoriously never agrees on anything, especially under the Disney era, where a vocal minority of fans scream at every decision Disney makes. Chris Stuckman, a widely beloved film critic, put it perfectly in this tweet.
