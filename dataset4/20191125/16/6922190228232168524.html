ACNB Co. (ACNB) To Go Ex-Dividend on November 27th

25 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Michael Walen

ACNB Co. (NASDAQ:ACNB) declared a dividend on Tuesday, October 22nd, Fidelity reports. Shareholders of record on Friday, November 29th will be paid a dividend of 0.25 per share by the bank on Friday, December 13th. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Wednesday, November 27th.

ACNB stock traded up $0.01 during trading hours on Monday, hitting $35.23. The stock had a trading volume of 2,900 shares, compared to its average volume of 6,760. The company has a quick ratio of 0.95, a current ratio of 0.96 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 0.39. The stock has a market capitalization of $249.16 million, a P/E ratio of 10.21 and a beta of 0.17. ACNB has a 12-month low of $32.29 and a 12-month high of $41.00. The stock’s 50-day moving average price is $34.67 and its 200-day moving average price is $35.89.

Several brokerages have issued reports on ACNB. BidaskClub raised shares of ACNB from a “sell” rating to a “hold” rating in a report on Saturday, September 14th. ValuEngine upgraded shares of ACNB from a “sell” rating to a “hold” rating in a research note on Tuesday, October 15th.

About ACNB

ACNB Corporation, a financial holding company, provides banking, insurance, and financial services to individual, business, and government customers in the United States. The company offers checking, savings, and money market deposit accounts, as well as time deposits and debit cards. It also provides commercial lending products, such as commercial mortgages, real estate development and construction loans, accounts receivable and inventory financing, and agricultural and governmental loans; consumer lending products comprising home equity loans and lines of credit, automobile and recreational vehicle loans, manufactured housing loans, and personal lines of credit; and mortgage lending programs consisting of personal residential mortgages, and residential construction and investment mortgage loans.
