Is Frozen 2 better than the original? Fans think the impossible has happened

25 Nov 2019, 16:43

Frozen 2 has beaten the box office debut of its predecessor, taking in $350 million worldwide in its opening weekend.

In fact, it’s the highest opening for an animated film of all time. So, kind of a big deal.

But is it better than the original?

We know, we know – a pretty impossible question to answer!

While the first Frozen film – which was released in 2013 and grossed $1.276 billion – scored a 90% ‘Fresh’ rating on Rotten Tomatoes, Frozen 2 has scored a little lower.

The follow up, which once again features the voices of Kristen Bell, Idina Menzel and Jonathan Groff, is currently on 75%, as per the film reviews aggregator.

But there’s plenty of evidence on social media that Frozen 2 is just as good as, if not better than, Frozen.

(And given some of the thought that’s gone into these fan reviews, we think film critics had better start looking over their shoulder. And they’re spoiler-free too!)
