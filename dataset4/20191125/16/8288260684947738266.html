'12-0 victory' - How Newcastle United fans see tonight's game with Aston Villa

Aston Villa v Newcastle | The Magpies make the long trip to the Midlands to face Villa, looking to continue their positive start under Steve Bruce

25 Nov 2019, 16:27 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Newcastle United fans have been having their say ahead of the pivotal looking clash with Aston Villa tonight.

The Magpies make the trip to B6 in good form - winning the last two before the international break.

Villa meanwhile lost three on the spin before the 15 day break, with defeat at Wolves labelled as one of the worst under Dean Smith.

Newcastle come into the game in 13th place, with 15 points from their opening dozen games, four points ahead of Villa.

With a tricky looking run of fixtures for Smith's side on the horizon, Villa will be desperate to earn three points in front of the Sky Sports cameras.

It will also be Steve Bruce's Villa Park return, the Newcastle United boss hasn't been in a dugout on B6 since his unceremonious sacking from the club in 2018.

Ahead of the game, we've taken a look on social media and on fan forums, to get a taste of how the away end are feeling:
