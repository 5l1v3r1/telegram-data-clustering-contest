25 Nov 2019, 16:16

Hailakandi: To improve Health services in Hailakandi District two CSR ambulances were provided to two different Health Institute of Hailakandi dist today. Under the CSR (community social responsibility) project of North East Transmission Company Limited (NETC) one each ambulance was provided to SK Roy Civil Hospital and Lala Community Health Center, Hailakandi.

Deputy commissioner Hailakandi, Keerthi Jalli formally handed over the two ambulances to the two hospital superintendent present on this occasion.

Speaking on this occasion, Chief guest of this handing over ceremony, DC Jalli hoped the ambulance would be able to provide health care to distressed patients. She also lauded the effort of NETC authorities for their noble cause which will provide service at the time of medical emergencies.

She also urged locals to use the ambulance with care.

On the occasion, ADC Health E. L . Firen, Hilakandi SK Roy Civil Hospital Superintendent Dr. Sudip Chakraborty, two NETC officers Bikash Kumar Naik and Sudhir Kumar Panit were also present.
