GRID Season One will be starting in early December

25 Nov 2019, 16:01 by Aran Suddi

Codemasters has confirmed GRID’s Season One content will start becoming available from December 4th with the release of the Hot Hatch Showdown pack. This will pack will add four cars to the vehicle roster including the Renault Clio s1600, Mini Hatch JCW, Lancia Delta HF Integrale Super Hatch, and Audi S1 Quattro Concept. Most of this content will only be available to those who bought the Ultimate Edition of GRID or choose to upgrade to that version, but the Renault will be available as a loan car in free play multiplayer. Season One is adding an additional 33 challenges too.
