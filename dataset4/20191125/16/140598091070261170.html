Homemade Christmas gifts 2019: Easy and inexpensive present ideas you can make from home

25 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Precious Adesina

Why buy into all that consumerist malarkey during the festive period when you can make your own Christmas gifts from scratch?

Not only will your 2019 homemade Christmas gifts save you a ton of  money, but it may seem a little more thoughtful than readymade items you can purchase within seconds. 

Imagine your loved ones' surprise when they realise how much effort you put into their DIY present. Nothing can top that. Or even add these gifts to your Christmas Eve box to make this year's one extra special. 

The joy of homemade Christmas gifts is that you get to have fun while you make them (whether that's alone or with friends) or even learn a new skill. Here, we've got DIY gift ideas for all sorts of crafty people, from budding chocolatiers to jewellers, along with some top tips on how to make perfect homemade gifts.
