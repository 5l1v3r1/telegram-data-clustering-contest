Maps of roads closed for trucks and buses during Gulf Cup

25 Nov 2019, 16:55

Traffic department has announced that some roads in the country will be closed for trucks and passenger vehicles (buses) during the 24th Arabian Gulf Cup which will kick off tomorrow.

The department published maps of the “forbidden roads” for all kind of trucks and passenger vehicles during 24th Arabian Gulf Cup from November 26, 2019 to December 08, 2019.
