Red Axes to release new EP, Voom, on Dark Entries

The latest project from the Tel Aviv duo is out December 6th.

25 Nov 2019, 16:46 by Kiana Mickles

Dark Entries has announced Red Axes's next release.

Tel Aviv-based artists Dori Sadovnik and Niv Arzi are putting out the four-track EP, Voom, on December 6th. The record, which covers electro and techno, also sees contributions from George Horn, who mastered the tracks at Berkeley's Fantasy Studios.

Red Axes are the electronic music duo and label heads of Garzen Records. Earlier this year, Life And Death released their collaborative double-LP, Redrago, with label founder DJ Tennis. Their most recent solo album was 2017's The Beach Goths, released via their own imprint.
