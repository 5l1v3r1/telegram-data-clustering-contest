WATCH | Ugly Fight Breaks Out Between Punjab Police And PNB Players During Nehru Hockey Final

IOA President Narinder Batra, who is also the chief of International Hockey Federation (FIH), also called on HI to take strict action against the players and officials involved.

25 Nov 2019, 16:04

Indian hockey stooped to a new low on Monday when an embarrassing incident happened during the 56th Nehru Hockey Tournament final. Players of Punjab Police and Punjab National Bank got involved in an ugly fight after the scuffle broke out during the title clash in Delhi.  It forced the national federation – Hockey India to seek a detailed report from the tournament organisers.

The brawl started while PNB was on the attack inside the Punjab Police circle. Both teams were locked at 3-3 at that point of time. The players exchanged blows and fought with sticks inside the turf for a while before the tournament officials rushed to douse the fire.
