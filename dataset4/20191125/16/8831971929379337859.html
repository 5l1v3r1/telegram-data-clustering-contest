Best Black Friday Travel Deals Of 2019

25 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Constance Kalpakis

Black Friday is not only a day of deals, but also the time of year where we are traveling home for the holidays and planning future new and exciting excursions. However, traveling can get majorly expensive. Here are some awesome deals going on Black Friday that’ll help you save when planning your next adventure.

Boston, Massachusetts

Beacon Hill’s, The Whitney Hotel is located in a great neighborhood being that it’s walking distance to everything you’d want to see and any public transportation to get you elsewhere. The hotel is offering a great sale from November 22, 2019 until December 5, 2019 of up to 40% off on stays through September 2020.

Trafalgar

This guided tour service is offering 20% off between November 29 and December 2, 2019 on over 40 select trips. Their trusted and awarded services are a perfect way to enhance your trip wherever you are!

San Juan, Puerto Rico

If you’ve always wanted to go to Puerto Rico, The Condado Vanderbilt Hotel is a great choice of stay in beautiful sunny San Juan. Book your stay for January 3 to December 1, 2020 to take advantage of their offer of 19% off suites (for at least two-nights), a $100 hotel credit with your suite booking, and 19% off the Chef Tasting Menu at their hotel restaurant, 1919.

Sedona, Arizona

If you need a nature oriented place to unplug and relax, Sedona, Arizona is perfect for you. And lavish desert resort, L’Auberge de Sedona is the place to stay!  From November 22 to November 30, 2019 you can score 30% off on stays from November 22, 2019 through December 21, 2020, and at least $75 of daily resort credit.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is one of the world’s most desired travel destinations. Located on Palm Jumeirah, The Atlantis Dubai is an extremely beautiful and extravagant resort that makes this deal all the more exciting. From November 29 to December 3, 2019, you can book a stays for a room or suite for up to 35% off until September 30, 2020. The deal also includes admission to their Aquaventure Waterpark and Lost Chambers Aquarium.

New York, New York

If your visiting the iconic city of New York, consider booking a water taxi trip with Circle Line Cruises. Enjoy the gorgeous NYC skyline for less with their offer of 50% off your booking with when you buy your tickets between November 27 and December 3, 2019, including All-Day Access Passes and 2-Day Access Passes when using code CYBER19 at checkout.

Los Angeles, California

If you’ve been looking for a reason to getaway to sunny LA, here’s your sign. The Hollywood Roosevelt is offering 50% off Tower Suites and Cabana Rooms on Thanksgiving Day through Cyber Monday for a stay between November 28, 2019 to June 30, 2020 when using CYBERDEAL at checkout!

Mendoza, Argentina

Entre Cielos Wine Hotel + Spa is place of relaxation and aesthetic getaways. The hotel allows for Argentinian wines and unwinding spa treatments in the gorgeous scenery of Mendoza. Book between November 29 and December 1, 2019 for stays between May 1 and October 9, 2020, and you will receive 40% off original room rates for at least a two-night stay.

Charleston, South Carolina

South Carolina is an underrated escape for a simple and enjoyable time away from home. The King Charles Inn is offering 35% off on stays that are booked through February 11, 2020, and 25% off stays booked through the end of 2020. The location offers the charming town of Charleston filled with shopping, dining, rooftop bars, and much more!

Palm Beach, Aruba

If you’re looking for a place to drink and tan, Aruba is for you. The Aruba Mariott & Stellaris Casino is offering a great deal of 30% off your room when traveling between January 2 and June 30, 2020 as long as you book on Black Friday or Cyber Monday.
