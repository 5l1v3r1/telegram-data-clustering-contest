Sister Rangoli Chandel defends Kangana Ranaut's Jayalalithaa look against trollers

25 Nov 2019, 16:56 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The first look of Kangana Ranaut's 'Thalaivi' has been the talk of the town. It received a lot of appreciation and love from all the quarters while some social media users trolled Kangana's look as late Tamil Nadu chief minister J. Jayalalithaa.

And now, Kangana's sister Rangoli Chandel has come out in support of the actress. Rangoli who is also Kangana's manager, stated that those who got eyes can see the fantastic work done by the prosthetic team.

She tweeted, "Anyone who has got eyes can see the brilliance of prosthetic work baki samosa gang is there jo din ko raat aur raat ko din kehte hain, they are inconsequential 😁🙏".
