Cheryl 'sobs' over I'm A Celebrity rumours alleging she plotted against Nadine Coyle

Cheryl has been left upset by the rumours about her and Girls Aloud bandmates Kimberley and Nicola being against Nadine

25 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Simon Duke

Cheryl has been driven to tears over malicious rumours against her and her Girls Aloud bandmates.

Over the weekend, it was reported that Cheryl and friends Kimberley Walsh and Nicola Roberts had set up a What's App group to plot against their former bandmate Nadine Coyle , while the Irish star is in the jungle on I'm A Celebrity.

The rumour was that the trio wanted to see suffer and vote for her to do all the Bushtucker Trials, but the girls were left so incensed by the reports that all three of them made statements to rubbish them, with Cheryl saying "We only want to see Nadine do well, and regardless of any differences we might have had, we will always all have love for each other.

"She text me saying 'I'm absolutely crying my eyes out'. I don't like the negativity. I'm impressed [with Nadine]. She's doing so great!"
