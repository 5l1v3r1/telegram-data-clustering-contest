Woman killed in morning crash on Interstate 20

25 Nov 2019, 17:46 by KNOE

RAYVILLE, La. (KNOE) - Authorities say a woman was killed Monday morning in a crash on Interstate 20.

According to Louisiana State Police, it happened around 8:40 a.m. just west of Rayville.

Police say 50-year-old Regina Watson, of Pioneer, was traveling west on Interstate 20 when she veered into the median. They say she overcorrected, traveled back across both lanes of travel and began to overturn.

Watson was wearing her seatbelt but did not survive the crash. She was pronounced dead at the scene.

The crash remains under investigation.
