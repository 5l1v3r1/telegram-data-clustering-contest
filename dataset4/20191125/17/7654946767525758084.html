Teen slashed outside of Brooklyn high school: cops

25 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Daniel Cassady

A 15-year-old boy was slashed in the neck and arm outside of a Brooklyn high school on Monday morning, cops said.

The brutal attack happened at around 8:45 a.m. in front of George Westinghouse High School in Downtown Brooklyn, according to cops.

The assault came after a dispute between the boy and an unknown person, and the attacker fled the scene, police said. It was not immediately clear whether either was a student at the school, cops said.

The boy was taken to Brooklyn Hospital Center where he was in stable condition, police said.
