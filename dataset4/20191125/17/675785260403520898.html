Richard and Judy were seen flying out of Australia looking glum amid a dramatic couple of days for their family.

The TV presenting duo, who have been married for 33 years, were snapped leaving a luxury hotel in Kingscliff, New South Wales.

Their outing comes after Mirror Online exclusively revealed that the couple's son-in-law James Haskell made a secret sex tape of his best mate romping with a schoolgirl who didn't know she was being filmed.

James, who is wed to Richard and Judy's daughter Chloe Madeley, hid a camera in a cupboard to film best pal Paul Doran-Jones having sex with a naked sixth former before brazenly showing the pornographic footage to pals.

Haskell, who was 17 and a student at Wellington College in Crowthorne, Berks at the time, was later suspended for his filming - pal Doran-Jones was expelled.

The story seemed to weigh heavy on the couple as they looked downcast on their outing.

Richard, 63, pushed along the pair's suitcases and kept his face covered with a big pair of sunglasses.

He dressed casually in a grey t-shirt, dark blue jeans and a pair of black trainers.

Judy, 71, kept covered in black striped trousers, a matching t-shirt and a pink patterned shawl draped over her shoulders.

She carried a straw bag in one hand and a notebook and iPad in the other.

Richard and Judy are yet to comment on their son-in-law's sex tape.
