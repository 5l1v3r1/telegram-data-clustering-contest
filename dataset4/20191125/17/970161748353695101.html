Piers Morgan turns down Gemma Collins' sex offer

25 Nov 2019, 17:15

Piers Morgan doubled down on never turning vegan after Gemma Collins offered to have sex with him to see if he ‘lasts longer’.

It looks like the Good Morning Britain host wasn’t done attacking veganism after the show reported on Netflix documentary The Game Changer which claimed veganism can improve your sex life and helps men ‘last three to four times longer’.

He branded the entire study ‘utter nonsense’ and Gemma, being the Good Samaritan she is, was willing to ‘have sex’ with Piers to see if he lasts longer.

Although the comment had him laughing and blushing on the show, Piers took to Twitter to comment: ‘This is unlikely to turn me vegan’.

Sorry, Gemma.

The TV personality is very passionate about being anti-vegan and will stop at nothing to make sure everyone knows it.

During the segment this morning, host Susanna asked him:  ‘Why don’t you do a compare and contrast, you can go vegan for a while?’

Piers responded: ‘I don’t need to. But who’s going to try me out?’

Shocking everyone in the studio, Gemma excitedly answered: ‘Me!’

The entire exchange in the conversation had everyone wondering how a debate on vegan diets turned to having sex with Piers.

Yeah, not the sentence we wanted to write today.

Piers was wondering the same thing, apparently, and said: ‘How have we got to me having sex with Susanna and Gemma Collins to test if meat-eating is good or bad for your virility?’

This entire topic is a train wreck but we’re here for it.

‘After a meat-free meal, the men’s night-time…lasted. It’s not that they lasted longer, it’s that the…interest lasted longer,’ Susanna explained to Piers on how the diet helps, evidently not allowed to say ‘erection’ on breakfast telly.

Quoting the study, she continued: ‘This is not a scientifically-validated study but the results that we’re seeing [are] very exciting.’
