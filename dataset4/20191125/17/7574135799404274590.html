Mac Demarco directed Iggy Pop’s new video (watch)

25 Nov 2019, 17:02 by Bill Pearis

Iggy Pop has shared a video for “Sonali,” from his jazzy new album, Free, and for it he got Mac DeMarco to direct. Even though it’s posted on Iggy’s official YouTube, it’s called an “unofficial video,” and given who’s behind the camera, you might not be surprised to learn it’s pretty weird. Mac is clearly still on the lizard man kick, with friends Kiera McNally and Tommy Midnight starring as a reptilian power couple. And being set in Los Angeles, there’s also a pink, fur-covered convertible and a saxophone solo. Iggy is nowhere to be seen but watch the video below.
