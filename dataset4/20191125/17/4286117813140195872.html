Another heart-wrenching incident has emerged from Mumbai where a three-year-old female dog was mercilessly beaten to death by a security guard in the Phoolpada area of Virar (east).

Her fault? She took shelter from the rain at the apartment and was sleeping there.

One of the security guards at the apartment, identified as Rakesh Sahani, allegedly hit her repeatedly with a wooden pole while she was sleeping.

The barbaric act was caught on the CCTV camera installed at the apartment and the dog, named Sweety, could be seen wriggling in pain.

This comes after a similar incident, where a dog named Lucky was killed by a security guard, outraged Mumbaikars. Even the Bollywood fraternity had come out demanding punishment for the culprit.

The dog was rushed to the hospital. However, she succumbed to the injuries.

A case has been lodged against the accused, who has gone missing since the incident came to the public eye.

One of the reasons many cited for the increasing number of animal cruelty cases is the negligible penalty for those found guilty.
