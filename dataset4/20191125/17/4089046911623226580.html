Investigation underway after man found with gunshot wound in Andersonville

25 Nov 2019, 17:40 by WVLT News

ANDERSONVILLE, Tenn. (WVLT) â€” Anderson County Sheriff's Office is investigating after a man was shot in the leg just after 10 a.m. Monday.

It happened on Defoe Road in Andersonville.

An off duty deputy heard the call and responded to the scene where he found a man with a large amount of blood loss due to a gunshot wound to his leg.

The deputy quickly used a tourniquet to help stop the bleeding until emergency crews arrived, officials said.

The victim was taken to UT Medical Center where he underwent surgery. No updates on the man's condition have been released.

Deputies are working to learn the circumstances surrounding the shooting.
