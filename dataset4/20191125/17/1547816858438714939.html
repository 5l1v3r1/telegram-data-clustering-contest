Binge Or Cringe: Is Living With Yourself Worth Your Time?

25 Nov 2019, 17:21 by SpotboyE Team

Netflix’s latest offering, is a comedy series called Living With Yourself that stars Friends actor Paul Rudd in the lead role. Let’s find out if the series manages to keep us entertained throughout

Netflix’s latest offering, is a comedy series called Living With Yourself that stars Friends actor Paul Rudd in the lead role. The show delves into a unique and interesting concept. Paul Rudd’s character of Miles Eliott is struggling in his personal and professional lives.

To bring about change and good luck, he decides to visit a special kind of spa which could help his life turn around. He finds an exact clone version of himself who’s quite better than him. So, his life takes a huge turn and what happens next is something you would only discover once you watch the show.

 Living With Yourself boasts of some steamy scenes, strong language and violence. We suggest you to possibly watch this with your clone and not with your family.

The show is quite breezy as it comprises of just 8 episodes that are of 30 minutes each. Paud Rudd’s performance is quite impressive and makes the show even more interesting to watch. The show will raise questions in your mind- Do you really need a better version of yourself?
