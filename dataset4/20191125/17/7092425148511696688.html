Major search launched after plane disappears over the Irish Sea

25 Nov 2019, 17:07 by Buzz Contributor

A search operation is underway after a light aircraft was believed to have crashed in the Irish Sea just off Puffin Island, Anglesey, North Wales.

Police were called at 12.59pm to reports of a "possible crash". The plane is believed to be a light aircraft which is most likely privately owned.

The UK coastguard has confirmed that they launched a search after reports that the plane had “disappeared from radar contact”.
