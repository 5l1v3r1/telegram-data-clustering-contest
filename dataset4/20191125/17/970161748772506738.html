Shenmue 3 fails to crack top 10 in latest UK sales chart

25 Nov 2019, 17:11

Despite the decades long wait Shenmue 3 has failed to attract a wider audience, as it’s outsold in the new charts by Football Manager 2020.

It’s very easy for more hardcore gamers to get entirely the wrong idea about what games are and are not popular. Shenmue 3 has been dreamt about for years, but now that it’s actually out it’s made barely a ripple in the UK physical sales charts.

The best it could manage was a debut at number 17, selling only half as much as last year’s Shenmue 1 & 2 remaster. Although that was multiformat, whereas Shenmue 3 is a PlayStation 4 and PC exclusive.

A percentage of sales will also have been digital, which aren’t counted on this chart, but that’s the same for any game. Indeed, being a PC exclusive the sales for Football Manager 2020 would be expected to be almost entirely digital, but it still managed to come in ahead of Shenmue 3 at number 16.

Of course, Shenmue 3 was a Kickstarter project, and so the 70,000 or so that backed it will have got their copies already – which also aren’t counted in this chart – but it suggests that interest from the wider public is minimal.
