Selena Gomez Had a Panic Attack Before Her AMA Performance

25 Nov 2019, 17:52 by Shivani Singh

On Sunday, many stars performed at the 2019 American Music Awards (AMA), including Selena Gomez. She opened the show with her newest hit songs “Lose You to Love Me” and “Look at Her Now.” Even though her performance was well executed and fans were loving it, Gomez had “put a lot of pressure” on herself backstage just moments before. It was her first time performing at the AMAs in two years.

A source revealed to E! News, “Selena definitely had anxiety and a panic attack before she went out and was putting a lot of pressure on herself.” Billboard also confirms that there were technical issues while Gomez was performing, “…the press room is having a hard time hearing anything with the volume turned way down on the monitors. Thankfully, the sound gets turned up for the very end of Gomez’s triumphant return to the stage.” Despite all the issues that Gomez faced prior to and during her performance, she pulled through and delivered a stellar performance.

Gomez constantly puts on a brave face. She received support from her fans and her friend, Taylor Swift, during her performance as they cheered her on.
