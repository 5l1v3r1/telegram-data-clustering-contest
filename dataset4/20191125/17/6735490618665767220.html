Jim Carrey Announces New Book: ‘Memoirs And Misinformation’

25 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Aynslee Darmon

Jim Carrey is writing another book.

The Canadian comedian icon, 57, took to Twitter on Monday to reveal his new Memoirs And Misinformation novel, which is semi-autobiographical.

“When we were writing my memoir/novel together, Dana Vachon gave me a blind taste test with book covers,” he tweeted about the new book. “And the only ones I liked — like, ALL OF THEM — were done by @chipkidd. So I asked him to do my cover.”
