Best Black Friday 2019 deals: Early sales and discounts from Amazon, Currys and John Lewis so far

25 Nov 2019, 17:51 by Telegraph Reporters

Even though Black Friday 2019 officially starts this coming Friday, November 29, the retailers have got a jump-start this year. On Friday, exactly a week before the official start date, Amazon, Currys PC World and John Lewis launched their Black Friday deals, and there some are serious savings to be made.

So far, there are big discounts on TVs, tech, fashion and everything in between. Last year, customers saved £40 million on Amazon alone, and, from looking at the deals that have launched today, it is easy to see why. 

With so many deals already on offer, sale-loving shoppers are truly spoilt for choice this year. But before you start feeling overwhelmed by this choice, we have prepared the following guide on where to find the best Black Friday deals this year.

Where can I find the best Black Friday deals - which retailers have offers?

It's no surprise that Amazon leads the pack - the retailer started its Black Friday deals last week. Amazon is practically synonymous with Black Friday as it not only brought Black Friday to the UK, but it offers the widest range of deals in the widest range of categories - including TVs, tech, mobile phones, appliances, laptops, tablets, consoles, smart devices and more. 

Both John Lewis and Currys PC World have also started their offers - the stores both price-match many of their competitors' best deals, making Currys an easy one-stop-shop for tech and John Lewis a worthwhile destination for higher-end products.

Argos is another of the big retailers getting in on the act early. The store is a good go-to for smart tech and consoles, and last year's Black Friday sale marked its biggest-ever day for video gaming sales.

It's also worth keeping an eye on other cross-category retailers, such as Very and AO, which work hard to beat Amazon's prices every year - especially for home appliances.
