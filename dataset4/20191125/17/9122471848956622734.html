Jim Harbaugh Comments On Saturday’s Game vs. Ohio State

25 Nov 2019, 17:52 by Alek Arend

Rivalry week is finally here. No. 2 Ohio State and No. 13 Michigan are set to face off this Saturday in Ann Arbor. For the first time in a few seasons, many anticipate a close game this weekend.

Wolverines head coach Jim Harbaugh certainly seems confident heading into game week. In fact, he’s “excited” for the massive rivalry contest.

“I’m excited for the game,” Harbaugh said, via Brandon Brown of Wolverine Insider. “Up for it and ready for the challenge.”
