CJCSC Gen. Zubair Hayat pays farewell call on PM

25 Nov 2019, 17:58 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews, https://facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) - Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee Gen. Zubair Mehmood Hayat on Monday paid farewell call on Prime Minister Imran Khan.

The Prime Minister appreciated his long meritorious service for the country. The Prime Minister wished the outgoing CJCSC well for his post retirement life.

Earlier, Prime Minister Imran Khan appointed Lt Gen Nadeem Raza as the new Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee (CJCSC), a statement issued by the premier s office announced on Thursday.

According to the press release, Lt Gen Raza s appointment will take effect from November 27.
