Dungeons & Dragons books are buy 2, get 1 free on Amazon

Follow Polygon online:

25 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Charlie Hall

If you’re new to Dungeons & Dragons or just looking to kick off a new campaign setting in 2020, there’s a tremendous deal on Amazon right now in honor of Black Friday. Over a dozen D&D sourcebooks are now buy 2, get 1 free.

The promotion includes the all-new Eberron: Rising from the Last War, Baldur’s Gate: Descent into Avernus, the excellent Waterdeep: Dragon Heist, or the entire core rulebooks set. The offer also extends to Dungeons & Dragons Art & Arcana, a tremendous art book that we excerpted here last year.
