Jay McKee out as coach of Rangers

General Manager Mike McKenzie will take over as head coach in the interim

25 Nov 2019, 17:35 by KitchenerToday Staff

The struggling Kitchener Rangers have parted ways with head coach Jay McKee.

The team announced in a release Monday afternoon that McKee had been relieved of his duties.

General Manager Mike McKenzie will take on the role of interim head coach until further notice.

The Rangers have lost four straight games and currently sit last in the Western Conference.
