Katie Price shocks fans with extreme new look as she preps for TV interview with Harvey

Katie Price, 41, took to her Instagram stories to post a picture of herself getting ready for a TV appearance with her son Harvey

25 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Katie Price has always been known for changing up her looks as a self-confessed fan of plastic surgery, but her new image will shock even her most loyal fans.

The former glamour model, 41, took to her Instagram stories to post a picture of herself getting ready for a TV appearance with her son Harvey, 17, tonight.

In the photo she appeared to be having her microphone adjusted as she stared moodily down the camera lens.

But she looked worlds away from her usual self in the snap, with her eyes looking strangely bright and her skin looking extrordinarily smooth.

Katie captioned the post: "Catch me on Channel 5 news this evening with my main man Harvey Price and Anna Kennedy OBE."

She also posted another photo of herself and Harvey hanging out with members of the crew.

Even in that photo she looked completely different to the one posted on her stories.

It comes after Katie was accused of sharing old pictures of her children to make it look like she was spending time with them.

She posted a snap of Princess saying they were having a "day out together" but it seems to be an old photo from a previous photo shoot for her fashion brand.

It followed the time her estranged husband - and Bunny's dad - Kieran Hayler hinted he was upset with Katie for posting a picture of Bunny and brother Jett, six, that he'd already shared.

"Never be intimidated by someone who copies you," a quote he shared read, "The original is always better than the copy."

Katie also baffled fans with a post of Jett in the garden with big brother and Katie's eldest Harvey, writing: "Love Sunday mornings with my boys."

Jett also appeared on Kieran's Instagram , prompting one fan to mock: "Those kids are remarkable; two places at once."
