Ibaka out again for Raptors vs. 76ers, Thomas has finger injury

25 Nov 2019, 17:57 by Sportsnet Staff

Forward Serge Ibaka has been ruled out of Monday’s game against the Philadelphia 76ers, while guard Matt Thomas has been diagnosed with a fractured finger, the team announced.

Ibaka is still dealing with a sprained ankle, but he could be back next week.
