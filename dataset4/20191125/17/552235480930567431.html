Ayr's Kyle Academy named as best performing school in South Ayrshire

The official rankings have listed schools in order of percentage of kids passing a certain number of exams.

25 Nov 2019, 17:07 by Stuart Wilson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/stuart-wilson/

Kyle Academy has been named the top performing state secondary school in South Ayrshire.

Official rankings of Scotland's top 100 state secondary  schools  have been revealed.

Rankings were based on the performance of pupils across the country with schools ordered on the percentage of kids passing a certain number of exams.

The table - published today in the  The Sunday Times  ' Parent Power rankings -  was also affected by the number of pupils who got two Advanced Highers or five or more National 5s.

Top spot was retained by Jordanhill School in  Glasgow  with the west end secondary boasting 83 per cent of pupils getting five or more awards at SCQF Level 6, the equivalent of Highers.

Kyle Academy in Ayr was the best performing South Ayrshire school, bagging 57th place on the list.

Prestwick Academy was next in at 78th on the rankings.

Queen Margaret Academy was named 93rd, while Belmont Academy rounded out the top 100.
