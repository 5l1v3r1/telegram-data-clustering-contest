What time and TV channel is Tottenham vs Olympiacos on in Ireland? Kick-off time and more for the Champions League clash

Jose Mourinho takes the reigns for his first Champions League game as Spurs boss.

25 Nov 2019, 17:28 by Connor McCarthy

Tottenham host Olympiacos in the penultimate game of the Champions League Group Stage.

Spurs have the chance to seal their spot in the Round of 16 draw with a win against the Greek side.

Here's everything you need to know about the game:

Where and when is it?

The match takes place at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium in London on Tuesday, November 26.

What time is kick-off?

It will get underway at 8pm Irish time.

What channel is it on?

BT Sport 2 will show the clash live, with coverage getting underway at 7pm.

Quotes corner

New Spurs manager Jose Mourinho

When asked about taking charge of his first match at home as Spurs boss: "I've been in the new stadium to watch the Newcastle game and I felt it, the relationship between the fans and the players".

"With these boys I will never be afraid of a Champions League match that we face.
"But we need to qualify first."

When asked about a potential move for free-agent Zlatan Ibrahimovic: "I have more than a connection, amazing player, amazing guy, but I would say no chance.

"We have the best striker in England. It doesn't make sense to sign him when we have Harry Kane."
