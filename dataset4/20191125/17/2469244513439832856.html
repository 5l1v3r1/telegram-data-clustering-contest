HGV driver arrested on suspicion of drink driving in Exeter city centre

Police described the incident as 'truly shocking'

25 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Howard Lloyd

The driver of an HGV has been arrested on suspicion of drink driving in the centre of Exeter.

The 'truly shocking' incident happened earlier today in the city, and police say the driver appears to be so far over the limit that he will have to wait until the morning to be interviewed.

"It shouldn’t happen but does and is truly shocking!," tweeted Dan Brenchley, a response sergeant for Devon and Cornwall Police.

"@PCNaomiCox arrested a HGV driver for drink driving in the city centre. He’s that over the limit that he’ll be waiting for us in the morning to be interviewed #police#Exeter."
