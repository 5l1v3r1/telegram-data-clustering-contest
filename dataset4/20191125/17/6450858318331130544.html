New Microsoft Surface Pro 7 has up to £375 off in Black Friday bundle

Microsoft just launched an early Black Friday discount on the Surface Pro 7 and Type Cover bundle which slashes up to £375 off the tablet.

25 Nov 2019, 17:52 by Somrata Sarkar

Black Friday deals are upon us, and Microsoft is flexing its muscles. The company just launched an early Black Friday deal that cuts up to £375 off the brand new Surface Pro 7, which only hit stores late last month. Microsoft's incredible discounts even leave Amazon, which launched its Black Friday sale last Friday.

Microsoft's early Black Friday discount brings the price of the base Surface Pro 7 (with Core i3, 4GB RAM and 128GB Storage) to £574.01 from the original £799; this shaves off a formidable £224.99. Though for the highest discount – and best value – you can get £375 off the model with a Core i5 processor, 16GB RAM and 256GB storage, which is otherwise £1,399 (now £1,024).

Keep in mind there isn't a discount on the Type Cover so you will pay full price for it – which is either £124.999 or £149.99 depending on the model you choose, but the discount earned on even the lowest model of the Surface tablet more than covers this cost.

If you prefer, you can also buy the Surface Pro without the bundle, with up to £225 in savings.
