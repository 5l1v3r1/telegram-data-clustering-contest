Mr Eazi blast Headies for not giving him his ‘Next rated’ award car gift

25 Nov 2019, 06:44

Nigerian singer, Oluwatosin Oluwole Ajibade, better known by his stage name, Mr Eazi, has taken to social media to blast the organisers of the Headies Awards for not giving him the car gift for an award he won three years ago.

Recall back in 2016, the singer's career grew impressively and it came as no surprise when he was honoured with the Next Rated award at the 2016 Headies Award show, an award which comes with a car gift.

The controversy already surrounding the award show might have just gotten worse with the singer's new claims of not yet receiving the car gift even though it has been three years since he won the award.
