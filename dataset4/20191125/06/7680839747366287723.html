Here are all the 2019 American Music Awards winners

25 Nov 2019, 06:37 by Callie Ahlgrim
  • The 47th annual American Music Awards is aired live on Sunday, November 24, 2019.
  • Post Malone was the most-nominated artist this year with seven nods, but only came away with one award for favorite rap/hip-hop album.
  • Taylor Swift was honored as the artist of the decade, but also swept all five of her nominations and performed a medley of her beloved songs.
  • Visit Insider’s homepage for more stories.

The 47th annual American Music Awards, self-described as “the world’s biggest fan-voted award show,” aired live on Sunday.

Post Malone was the most-nominated artist this year with seven nods, but only came away with one award. Conversely, not only was Taylor Swift was honored as the artist of the decade, she swept all five of her nominations and performed a medley of her beloved songs.

Keep reading for the complete list of nominees and winners (*in bold*).
