How to Use the Tile Trackers

Tile's Bluetooth-enabled devices and handy app make everything findable, from the smallest electronic devices to the bulkiest backpacks. Simply follow these easy steps:

1. Place your Tile Essentials on your valuables. Tile currently offers four varieties, which are:

  • The Sticker: A small finder with a 150-feet range perfect for finding for electronic devices, like remote controls
  • The Slim: A sleek finder with a 200-feet range perfect for finding wallets and passports
  • The Pro: A high-performance finder with a 400-feet range perfect for finding car keys
  • The Mate: A versatile finder with a 400-feet range perfect for finding car keys and backpacks

2. Download the Tile app.

3. To see if your stuff is nearby, tap the 'Find' button on the app.

4. To find your phone, double press the Tile button on your Tile device. Your phone will ring even when it's on silent.

5. To locate your stuff, check the location history on your app. The map will show the last time and place it was updated.

6. To get help when your stuff is lost, activate the Tile community, and every Tile user can help you find it.

BONUS: If you take advantage of the Amazon deals above, you can find your stuff with a simple voice command through your Echo Dot. Simply say, "Alexa, ask Tile to find my [stuff]."
