Lindsay Lohan ex Harry Morton found dead aged 38

25 Nov 2019, 06:35 by Buzz Contributor

Lindsay Lohan 's ex Harry Morton has been found dead at his home, passing away at the age of 38.

TMZ reports that the Pink Taco founder has died after being found unresponsive at his home by a relative on Saturday.

Harry was pronounced dead at his Beverly Hills home, according to the publication.

It's said that foul play is not suspected and an autopsy will be carried out to determine the cause of his death.

Harry famously dated Mean Girls actress Lindsay in 2006, but the relationship fizzled out.

The businessman was also romantically linked to Demi Moore, Britney Spears and Paris Hilton at various stages.

Reports also linked him to Jennifer Aniston and Hayden Panettiere, according to TMZ.

Harry also bought The Viper Room in 2008, where actor River Phoenix died of an overdose in 1993, but is said to have sold it.

He was the son of Hard Rock Cafe co-founder Peter Morton and his grandfather Arnie was also in the restaurant business and co-founded Morton's Steakhouse back in 1978.

Harry founded Pink Taco when he was just 18 years old.

Harry also owned Elvis Presley's former Beverly Hills estate, but it is not believed that Harry died at that residence.

Following his death Lindsay's mom Dina is said to have posted an image of her daughter with Harry to Instagram, writing: "RIP Harry God has an angel, we love you!

"Prayers to the family."

Lindsay is yet to respond to the news of her ex-boyfriend's passing.
