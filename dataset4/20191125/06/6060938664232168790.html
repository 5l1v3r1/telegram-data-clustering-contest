Impossible to outrun Ravindra Jadeja, tweets Virat Kohli

25 Nov 2019, 06:20 by ANI

NEW DELHI: India skipper Virat Kohli on Monday said that it is impossible to beat allrounder Ravindra Jadeja in the conditioning sessions.

Taking to Twitter, Kohli shared a picture in which he, along with Jadeja and Rishabh Pant, is running during a practice session. Jadeja can be seen as the front-runner, followed by Kohli and Pant.

"Love group conditioning sessions. And when Jaddu is in the group, it's almost impossible to outrun him. @RishabhPant17 @imjadeja," Kohli captioned the post.
