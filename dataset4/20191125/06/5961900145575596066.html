Please Enjoy This Not At All Dramatic Look Back At The Phases One Direction Went Through

25 Nov 2019, 06:38 by By Steffanie Tan

So one night in bed when I couldn’t sleep, I decided to enter a YouTube hell hole. I thought maybe true crime ‘cos I was in the middle of Mindhunter season 2 but somehow I ended up watching One Direction music videos. Happens to the best of us. Said YouTube hole made me yearn for another time, a simpler time, when all I thought about was how I wished Niall Horan would make eye contact with me from about a gazillion metres away, in the dark, at a concert, and fall in love with me. BRING IT BACK. I WANT IT BACK.

Quality memz aside, it’s time we look back at One Direction. But not in a “look at the change in music #growth” way, I want to look at their lewks through the years. That, and how Niall’s hair has barely even changed.

The Foetus Phase

MY GOD, I threw myself on the 1D bandwagon quite late in the game, like late 2012 so I completely missed this entire phase. Well, not really. I went to an all-girls school which meant I was still one hundo per cent aware of the shenanigans. Girls literally cried when they missed out on tickets to 1D’s first tour of Australia. ANYWAY, what the FRICK. Why did people find this attractive? I did not vibe with 1D in 2010 and it is absolutely because they were all foetuses.

I just did not love the scarf look at all and Niall only stepped out in supra shoes, which did my head in.
