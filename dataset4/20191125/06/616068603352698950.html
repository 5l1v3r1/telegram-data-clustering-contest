Rogen to join Price is Right - Entertainment News

25 Nov 2019, 06:57 by WENN

Seth Rogen and David Boreanaz will put their game show skills to the test for a star-studded Christmas edition of TV favourite The Price is Right next month.

The stars will join host Drew Carey for The Price Is Right at Night: A Holiday Extravaganza on Dec. 22 and 23.

Boreanaz will be playing to benefit charity the Headstrong Project, which provides mental health treatment for post-9/11 veterans and their families, while Rogen will be hoping to raise cash for Hilarity for Charity, the Alzheimer's research and support non-profit he founded with his wife, Lauren Miller Rogen.
