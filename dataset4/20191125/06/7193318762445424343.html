OU vs. Stanford men's basketball: How to watch, things to know, projected starters

25 Nov 2019, 06:03 by Abby Bitterman

Oklahoma vs. Stanford

When: 8:30 p.m., Monday

Where: Sprint Center, Kansas City, Missouri

TV: ESPN2 (Cox 28)

Radio: KRXO-FM 107.7

Three things to know

• Oklahoma and Stanford have faced two common opponents ahead of this game. They both played Maryland Eastern Shore and William & Mary. Oklahoma beat William & Mary 75-70 and UMES 91-64, while Stanford won both games 81-50 and 76-55, respectively.

• Monday’s game will be the fourth meeting in history between Oklahoma and Stanford. The last meeting between the two was in March 1997. The Sooners are 1-2 against the Cardinal.

• This game is part of the Hall of Fame Classic, a four team tournament. The winner will play the victor of Butler-Missouri in a championship game, and the losers will play will play for third place. Both second round games will be held on Tuesday.
