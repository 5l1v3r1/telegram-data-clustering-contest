Overnights: ‘Indian Idol 11’ puts Sony TV in front on Sunday in UK

25 Nov 2019, 11:36 by Raj Baddhan

Sony Entertainment Television retained the No.1 position on Sunday in UK ratings, on the back of the success of ‘Indian Idol 11’, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Sony TV delivered a share of 0.12%, followed by PTC Punjabi with 0.09%, Colors, Sony MAX and ZEE Cinema with 0.08%, Star Plus with 0.06%, New Vision TV with 0.05%, Star Bharat with 0.04%, Hum TV, Sony SAB TV and Colors Cineplex with 0.03%, Hum TV, Geo TV, B4U Music, NDTV 24×7 and Sony MAX 2 with 0.02%.

Sony TV’s top attractions were ‘Indian Idol 11’ with 61,300 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ at 21:30 with 30,300 viewrrs and the repeat of the episode at 12:30 with 19,000 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13 Weekend Ka Vaar’ topped ratings with 59,500 viewers at 21:30, followed by repeat at 12:45 with 10,600 viewers and the repeat at 17:00 with 6,200 viewers.

On Star Plus, ‘Dance+ 5’ raked in 31,000 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘TED Talks India Nayi Baat’ at 19:00 with 23,300 viewers and ‘Divya Drishti’ at 21:30 with 10,700 viewers. Film ‘Mission Mangal’ at 16:00 delivered 5,900 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ attracted 21,800 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘Gul-e-Rana’ repeat at 16:15 with 6,200 viewers.

On ZEE TV, movie ‘Dhadak’ led with 10,800 viewers.

On Sony MAX, ‘Silsila’ was watched by 25,400 viewers and premiere of ‘Khandani Shafakhana’ raked in 10,000 viewers at 17:00.

On ZEE Cinema, premiere of ‘Badla’ pulled in 18,700 viewers at 18:00.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
