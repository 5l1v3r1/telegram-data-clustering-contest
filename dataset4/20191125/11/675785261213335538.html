How to find Amazon Black Friday deals?

Lots of coveted items and products with the biggest price cuts tend to sell out fast, so it's advisable to hop online early to avoid missing out on the limited discounted stock.

Popular categories include:

  • Amazon devices
  • Toys
  • Gaming
  • Appliances
  • Computers
  • Fashion
  • Baby

Shoppers can also sign up to Amazon's Daily Deals newsletter or you can download the Amazon mobile app or check the main deals page to stay up to date on the latest offers as they go live.

How to get Amazon Prime for free?

Amazon Prime offers some amazing perks that includes same-day delivery, access unlimited access music, TV and films streaming services.

Amazon customers can sign up to a 30-day free trial to take advantage of Prime perks, like 30-minutes exclusive early access to all Lightning Deals during Black Friday.

This means they'll avoid paying the £7.99 monthly fee (or £79 upfront annual cost). But users must remember to set a reminder to cancel before the subscription ends - or risk being charged the full fee.
