Shah Rukh Khan’s latest airport look is all things cool and classy

25 Nov 2019, 11:40 by Vedanshi Pathak

Shah Rukh Khan was last seen in Aanand L Rai’s Zero.  After that, the actor took a long break from the movies. Now, it looks like the superstar is gearing back into action. Reports suggest that the actor will play a special part in Brahmastra and earlier today was snapped at the airport soon after Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt were clicked leaving for Chandigarh. 

Now is this a mere coincidence or has the megastar left to shoot for Brahmastra is something only time will tell, but we can’t wait to watch him on the big screen again.
