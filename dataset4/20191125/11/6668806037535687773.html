Pictures: Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor head to Chandigarh

25 Nov 2019, 11:16 by Vedanshi Pathak

Lovebirds Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor are all set to be seen together on the big screen for the first time in Ayan Mukerji’s Brahmastra. Alia and Ranbir were snapped together this morning as they left for Chandigarh. Alia looked radiant in a pair of white pants and an azure coloured shirt, while Ranbir opted for a comfy all-black look. What also caught our attention was Ranbir being injured as the actor had his hand in a cast as he left for Chandigarh. Scroll through for pictures.
