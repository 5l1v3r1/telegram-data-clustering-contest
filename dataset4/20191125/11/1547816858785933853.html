STUNNER OR BUMMER: Mouni Roy, Alia Bhatt, Ananya Panday, Shilpa Shetty Or Raveena Tandon?

25 Nov 2019, 11:47 by Aditi Singh

Here are the ones who kept the weekend fashionable and the one who turned out to be the bummer of the day

Mumbai airport was buzzing with star power as we spotted a number of celebrities here. Mouni Roy and Alia Bhatt flew out of the city for the shoot of their film, Brahmastra. 

Shilpa Shetty arrived back in Mumbai and was snapped with hubby Raj Kundra. Raveena Tandon attended an event in the city while Ananya Panday visited Manish Malhotra's house. 

So, let's see who were the stunners and who was the bummer of the day: 

MOUNI ROY- STUNNER- Mouni looks quite chic in the beige overcoat. We love what we see. 

SHILPA SHETTY- BUMMER- Shilpa never disappoints us with her fashion game but this travel attire is not giving out style vibes at all.

ALIA BHATT- STUNNER- Alia will take away your Monday blues with this cute and comfortable blue top!

RAVEENA TANDON- STUNNER- Raveena's Indian look is pleasant and the actress looks beautiful in it. 

ANANYA PANDAY- STUNNER- Ananya looks hot in a white tank top which she paired with white shorts.
