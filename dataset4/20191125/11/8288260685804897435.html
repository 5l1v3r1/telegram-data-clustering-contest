Smoke billows from Wolverhampton One Stop shop after booze raid

West Midlands Police were called to Griffiths Drive, Ashmore Park, at 2.30am on Monday, November 25

25 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Jordan Coussins, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/

Burglars fled with stolen booze as a store's anti-theft system sent smoke billowing out into the street.

They smashed through the front of the One Stop store in Griffiths Drive, Wolverhampton, at around 2.30am on Monday, November 25.

Pictures from the scene showed smoke billowing from the store and officers at the scene.

It was not known if anything else was taken but police enquires continued, a spokesman for the force confirmed.
