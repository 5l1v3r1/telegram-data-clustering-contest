EXCLUSIVE: The Legion of Doom Won't Let Superman Surrender in Action Comics

25 Nov 2019, 11:11 by L.D. Nolan

Empowered by Perpetua, Lex Luthor and the Legion of Doom have done major damage to the Justice League. Brian Michael Bendis and John Romita Jr.'s Action Comics #1017 will continue exploring the conflict between the two groups, and DC has provided CBR with an exclusive preview showing Superman attempting -- and failing -- to surrender.

In the exclusive preview, which you can see below alongside the solicitation information, Superman attempts to surrender to the Legion of Doom, though they reject the notion, not believing the Man of Steel will actually quit. Instead, what they want is "genuine surrender."

Action Comics #1017

  • written by BRIAN MICHAEL BENDIS
  • art by JOHN ROMITA JR.

  • “Invisible War” part one! It’s one of the biggest creator reunions in years as writer Brian Michael Bendis and artist John Romita Jr. join forces for the first time since their blockbuster turns on Avengers and Avengers Vs. X-Men. In this issue, it’s all-out war on the streets of Metropolis-and at the end of this epic there will be a huge change to the status quo of the world of Superman-you do not want to miss this!
