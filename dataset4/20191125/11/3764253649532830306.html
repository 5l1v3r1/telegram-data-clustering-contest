Actor Saidi Balogun loses mom, says 'good night mama' (photos)

25 Nov 2019, 11:19

- Actor Saidi Balogun has lost his mother, Jolade

- The actor took to social media to pay respect to his mother

- Balogun shared a photo of himself with his mother on his Instagram page

Popular Nigerian actor, Saidi Balogun, has paid tribute to his mother after she passed away. The actor took to social media to pay respect to his mother, Jolade, who recently passed away.

The popular actor shared photos of himself with his mother on Instagram. Balogun wished his mother good night and praised her. Balogun also wished his mother a peaceful rest.

Balogun is a 52-year-old veteran Nigerian actor, film-maker, director and producer. He is a father to two children, Khalid, and Aliyah Balogun.
