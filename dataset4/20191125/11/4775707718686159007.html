It's not a big surprise that the big Black Friday doorbuster for Xbox players this year is the Xbox One X, with a new console set to release this time next year. What's a little more surprising is that Microsoft and Amazon are offering pretty much the same deep discount on the console bundle range. Amazon UK has a slight edge in bundling additional games like RAGE 2 and Call of Duty: Black Ops 4. Hilariously, Xbox are bundling in a code for Anthem instead. GAME in the UK have a variety of bundle options available, while Amazon US has little to offer ahead of its Black Friday event properly kicking off later in the week.

Below we have a list of the current Xbox One X Black Friday deals for Amazon and Microsoft in the US and UK, plus the range of Xbox One X Black Friday deals offered by GAME in the UK. There's a considerable variety of options, particularly for UK buyers, so be sure to take a look through.

Microsoft US are throwing Anthem in with every Xbox One X console deal they have on site for Black Friday – it's also on sale in the Xbox Black Friday game sale, so it's not a huge saving and, obviously, it's Anthem. Still, Microsoft have managed to match Amazon on the basic discount amount, even if the bundled extra game isn't necessarily what everyone would want.
