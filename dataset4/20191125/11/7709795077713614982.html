NFL Week 12 highlights & reports: 49ers beat the Packers & Patriots beat Cowboys as both press Super Bowl claims

25 Nov 2019, 11:49 by Paul Higham

The San Francisco 49ers confirmed themselves as the team to beat in the NFC with a dominant 37-8 victory over the Green Bay Packers, while the New England Patriots edged out the Dallas Cowboys in a soaking wet arm wrestle.

The 49ers have been the surprise package of the season so far, winning just four games last year but hitting ten wins already as they shut down the visitors en route to going to 10-1 for the season.

Jimmy Garoppolo got far more change out of the Packers defence than counterpart Aaron Rodgers could muster out of the Niners – admittedly the league’s best passing defence this season.
