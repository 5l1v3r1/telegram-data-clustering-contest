You can get a huge discount at all Yas Island theme parks this weekend

Enter any of the Abu Dhabi attractions for under Dhs150

25 Nov 2019, 11:27 by Colin Armstrong

Yas Island is going to be the place to be over the next few weeks.

As the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix comes to town a whole host events will take place - from crazy auctions, big parties, pop-up events, brunches, pool parties, concerts and more.

Giving you even more options for entertainment, all Yas Island theme parks are offering residents a huge discount on ticket prices for National Day.

From November 28 to December 2, tickets to Yas Island theme parks including Yas Waterworld, Ferrari World Abu Dhabi and Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi will be offering a 48 percent discount to celebrate the UAE’s 48th National Day.

Tickets are available to book on each of the park’s websites.

With the discount, entry to Ferrari World Abu Dhabi and Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi will be reduced from Dhs295 to Dhs150.

Yas Waterworld tickets will be reduced from Dhs250 to Dhs130 per person. Plus, the UAE’s biggest water fight is set to take place on December 2, so there’s even more reasons to visit.

A valid Emirates ID will need to be presented at the parks for proof of residency status to redeem the offer.
