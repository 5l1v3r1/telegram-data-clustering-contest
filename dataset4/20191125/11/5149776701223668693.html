Choose Cosmo for your festive get-together this year

Alongside global banqueting options, there's also a full carvery

25 Nov 2019, 11:18 by Jane McFarlane

Nothing says Christmas like a good old fashioned turkey dinner with family and friends.

And if you're looking for somewhere special to celebrate this year, Cosmo on Milton Street has got to be your go-to venue.

Yes, Cosmo. The perennially popular all-you-can-eat buffet restaurant famed for its global banqueting options and Pan-Asian cooking stands.

Because in case you didn't know, during the festive period you can also get a carvery with all the trimmings at Cosmo - alongside all your other favourite dishes!

Restaurant manager David Cheung is very proud of the Christmas fayre at Cosmo, saying: "Around Christmas we put out a selection of roast meats and vegetables with all the trimmings so if you're after a traditional turkey dinner, you can have one.

"And that's not at the expense of our other dishes either - it's an extra!"

Cosmo has 17 restaurants across the country from Edinburgh and Glasgow to Bristol, Dublin and Belfast with a branch soon to open in Paris.

David added: "We are very popular with groups and with families because we have such a wide choice of dishes. Families like to come to us because it's a chance for children to taste lots of new foods while groups like the fact we can easily accommodate lots of people together."

Prices for Christmas start at £10.99 for lunch and £17.99 for dinner - where else will you get all that choice for such great value?

David said: "Come to Cosmo this Christmas for more fun, more festivities and more choice."

To book a table at Cosmo, at First Floor 29A Milton Street (Opposite John Lewis), Nottingham NG1 3EN, call 0115 950 7755
