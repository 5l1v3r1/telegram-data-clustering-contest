Westlife is HEADLINING this huge new music festival

Having the band play "is a real coup" for the festival

25 Nov 2019, 11:50 by Sarah Magliocco

Westlife have been named as the headline acts at the brand new York Music Festival.

Alongside the iconic Irish pop band are headliners Lionel Richie and Madness.

The Irish band will perform at the musical event, taking place in York in the UK’s Yorkshire county.

The three-day event will be held at York Sports Club between Friday June 19th and Sunday June 21st 2020.

Director Peter Taylor told YorkMix.com: “This is the very first York Festival so we wanted to make this a very special debut year.”

“To have Lionel Richie, Westlife and Madness as headliners – alongside many other brilliant chart-topping artists – is a real coup.”

“We feel this is the perfect line-up for the first year of what we hope will become a major annual event in this wonderful and historic city.”

Westlife will headline on the Saturday night as part of their UK-wide stadium tour.

All Saints have also been announced for the line up.
