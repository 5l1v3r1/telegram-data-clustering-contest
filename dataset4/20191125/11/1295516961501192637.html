Black Widow Will Have Two New Costumes in Her Solo Film

25 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Sundi Rose

Marvel Studios Director of Visual Development Andy Park shared concept art of Natasha Romanoff from Avengers: Endgame and in doing so, also revealed she'll get two new costumes in the upcoming Black Widow solo film.

Park has designed all seven incarnations of Black Widow's costume since 2012's The Avengers. In his Instagram post, he said, "I can’t wait for you all to see her 8th & 9th look in her solo film soon!"

Photos from the D23 Expo in August revealed a new white costume, but Park didn't reveal if this is among the two new designs he created for the film, which will launch a whole new franchise in the MCU. The white suit evokes memories of Black Widow's costume from the comics, complete with all the belts, straps and accessories you'd expect a Black Widow costume to have.

Park also wished the Black Widow herself, Scarlett Johansson, a happy belated birthday, ending the post with the "#whateverittakes."

Directed by Cate Shortland, Black Widow stars Scarlett Johansson, David Harbour, Florence Pugh, O-T Fagbenle and Rachel Weisz. The film arrives in theaters on May 1, 2020.
