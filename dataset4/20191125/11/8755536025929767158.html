Ant and Dec respond to backlash after last nights ‘impossible’ bush tucker trial

Fans of the show were NOT happy with the trial

25 Nov 2019, 11:31 by Clodagh Meaney

Fans took to social media in their droves to complain about last night’s bush tucker trial on I’m A Celebrity, Get Me Out of Here.

The task saw James Haskell and Roman Kemp lay on a bed in a Love Island-themed challenge.

The stars had to battle a 90-second timer to unscrew stars placed in boxes, on either side of the bed, filled with spiders, crabs and cockroaches.
