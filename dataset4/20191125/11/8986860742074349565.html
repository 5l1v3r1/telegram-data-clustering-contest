Tracey Boakye puts her heavy ‘watermelons’ on display at Bro. Sammy album launch (Video)

25 Nov 2019, 11:52 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Actress cum producer and the CEO of Shakira films Tracey Boakye became the center of attention among her peers and patrons who had gathered to witness the launch of Gospel singer Brother Sammy’s Album.

The heavily endowed actress got the people around talking about her big watermelons while she looked on with smiles all over her face.

Brother Sammy’s ‘Aduro Biaa Nka Dem’ album was launched at the Dichemso Assemblies of God Church in Kumasi on Sunday, November 24, 2019, and had Rev. Obofour of Anointed Palace Chapel (APC) buying the first copy for Ghc 100,000.00.
