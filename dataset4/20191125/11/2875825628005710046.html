Dublin footballers honoured at Celtic Park following five-in-a-row success

A number of Dublin stars were welcomed onto the pitch at half-time

25 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Robert Hynes

Dublin's footballers were honoured at Celtic Park on Saturday following their five-in-a-row success.

A number of Dublin stars were welcomed onto the famous pitch at half-time during the Hoops' 4-0 win over Livingston.

Jack McCaffrey, Brian Fenton, Niall Scully and Paddy Andrews were among the players to attend the match in Glasgow.

They presented Celtic with a signed Dublin jersey to help with their Christmas charity appeal.
