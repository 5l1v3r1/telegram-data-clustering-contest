Outgoing CJCSC General Zubair Mahmood Hayat meets PM Imran Khan

25 Nov 2019, 11:07 by Mati

ISLAMABAD, Pakistan: The outgoing Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee (CJCSC) General Zubair Mahmood Hayat paid a farewell call on Prime Minister Imran Khan in Islamabad on Monday.

The prime minister appreciated General Zubair Mahmood Hayat’s long meritorious service for the Country.

Imran Khan wished the outgoing CJCSC well for his post retirement life.

The CJCSC General Zubair Mahmood Hayat is set to retire on November 28, 2019, three years after he assumed the Command as the 17th Head of Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee.
