EE's free Nintendo Switch deal for Black Friday ends today

The deal has been one of the biggest hits of the Black Friday run in but it will not even make it to the day itself

25 Nov 2019, 11:24 by Stephen Hurrell

EE will end its free Nintendo Switch giveaway just days before Black Friday begins.

The network provider launched the eye-catching Black Friday deal at the beginning of November as part of an early Black Friday sale but the deal itself will not last to the day itself on 29 November.

EE is giving away a free Nintendo Switch console or a 43-inch Toshiba TV to anybody who takes out a phone contract on EE before the end of today (Monday 25 November).

The deal is available on EE's Pay Monthly Deals. Limited time offers are also available on EE's SIM-Only deals, Upgrade Deals, Pay-As-You-Go Deals and Broadband Deals.

However, rival network Three has a competitive alternative Black Friday offer for those looking for SIM-only deals.

Money Saving Expert Martin Lewis has picked out Three's £8 SIM card with 8gb of data and unlimited texts and calls. The deal has been praised as one of the best value mobile deals ever and is available on the Three website.

Three has also launched a £20 SIM deal that offers users unlimited data. The deal is £10 for the first six months of the 24-month contract.
