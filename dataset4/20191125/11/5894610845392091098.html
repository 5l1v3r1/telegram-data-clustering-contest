Gardai investigating after body of man found in container in Dublin

25 Nov 2019, 11:51 by Ken Foy

Gardai are keeping an open mind into the discovery of a man’s body in a container in north Dublin this morning.

The deceased, who has not yet been formally identified, was found at 10.50am on wasteland beside Balbutcher Lane in Ballymun.

He was pronounced dead at the scene and members of the Garda Technical Bureau are carrying out a forensic examination of the scene.

A senior source said that the man had visible wounds, consistent with having been assaulted and possibly stabbed.

However, gardai are awaiting the confirmation of a post mortem examination to establish how he suffered these wounds as well as the cause of death.

The scene at Ballbutcher Lane was been cordoned off with uniformed and plain clothed detectives present.

Senior investigating officers have also attended the scene, which is behind a metal railing but is accessible over a small mound.

A derelict building as well as three containers are visible from the roadway. The male’s body was discovered in one of the containers this morning.
