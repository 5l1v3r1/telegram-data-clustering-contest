Dbanj 'happened' so Wizkid and Burna Boy could rise - Nigerians praise singer

25 Nov 2019, 11:27

- Nigerian music star Dbanj is currently trending on Twitter for his contribution to the music industry

- Many music lovers credited the singer for paving the way for Wizkid, Burna Boy and Davido

- Fans of the singer also recounted some of the best moments of the Kokomaster’s prime

Popular Nigerian singer Dapo Oyebanjo professional known as Dbanj is currently trending on popular micro blogging platform, Twitter.

The music star became a topic of discussion after some music lovers delved into the matter of the Nigerian entertainment scene and some artistes that have paved way for the music industry to become what it is at the moment.

As expected, Dbanj’s name surfaced as one of the top entertainers that managed to pave way for Nigerian music, especially letting younger stars like Wizkid, Davido, Burna Boy and others take their craft to a whole different level.
