Twenty One Pilots announce Dublin gig

25 Nov 2019, 11:35 by James Cox

Twenty One Pilots are set to play another Dublin gig after their sold out 3 Arena Gig last year.

The duo will play at the RDS Arena on Thursday, June 25, 2020.

Tickets, priced from €59.50, will go on sale on Friday, November 29 at 9am via Ticketmaster.
