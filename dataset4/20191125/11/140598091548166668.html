Frozen Christmas gift guide: the best merchandise, from Frozen 2 toys to Elsa dolls

25 Nov 2019, 11:44 by Precious Adesina

When the original Frozen movie came out, it quickly became the highest grossing animated movie of all time, spawning a hit song, a slew of merchandise and a generation of girls who no longer believed that 'true love's kiss' had to be delivered by a lousy prince. 

That year, the Elsa doll was the must-have Christmas toy, with Disney later switching manufacturer from Mattel to Hasbro to keep up with demand. 

Six years on, Frozen 2 has finally arrived  and forecasters predict it will earn more than $100 million at the US box office when it opens this weekend. And with it, of course, comes a new set of toys and merchandise for kids to fight over. If the first movie is anything to go by,  these will be sell-outs by Christmas.

Here's our pick of the top Frozen 2 merchandise.
