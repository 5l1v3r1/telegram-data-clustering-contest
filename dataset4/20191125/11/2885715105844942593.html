2 killed, 20 injured as bus skids off Pune Bangalore Highway

The incident took place near Shindewadi village, around 20 kilometres from Pune.

25 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Express News Service

Two people were killed and at least 20 injured after a Maharashtra State Transport bus veered off the road and landed 50 feet below the Pune Bangalore Highway on Monday.

According to the police, around 1.30 pm, the driver of the Sangli-bound bus lost control of the vehicle. The bus fell off the road and turned on its side. The incident took place near Shindewadi village, around 20 kilometres from Pune.

Local villagers and the Pune fire brigade launched a rescue effort and the passengers were taken to local hospitals.  “Out of 40 persons on the bus, at least two persons have been killed and more than 20 were were injured in the accident. The injured are being treated at local hospitals.” said inspector Dattatray Darade of Rajgad police station.
