Spoilers: Robert to kill Corrie's Vicky in wedding day revenge?

25 Nov 2019, 11:31

Robert Preston (Tristan Gemmill) is set to find his while world falling apart next week as Michelle Connor (Kym Marsh) prepares to execute her revenge on her cheating groom on their wedding day in Coronation Street. But things will get even darker when Robert is accused of murder – has he killed his pregnant secret lover Vicky Jeffries (Kerri Quinn) in a fit of rage?

Not knowing that Michelle is already aware of his scandalous double life, Robert is horrified when Vicky turns up at the bistro during pre-wedding celebrations to confront him over his lies. Fearing he will lose everything, his rage at Vicky will know no bounds.

So when Tyler later reveals that Vicky is missing in action and accuses Robert of getting rid of her, he has some explaining to do. Will he lose both Michelle and his liberty as he starts to discover the consequences of what he has done?

New pictures of the wedding and the drama surrounding it have been released on ITV’s Corrie website and accompanying captions tease: ‘Under Michelle’s orders, Robert goes to Vicky’s house to ban her from the ceremony.

‘But Vicky interrupts the Connor’s pre-wedding celebrations in the Bistro anyway! Will she ruin Michelle’s revenge before she can put it into action? Robert quickly gets rid of Vicky while Michelle makes excuses to the guests that he’s just helping out Tyler.’
