Hideo Kojima Hints at a Return to Horror Following Death Stranding Release

25 Nov 2019, 11:48 by Nathan Birch

What’s next for Hideo Kojima? The Japanese auteur just released the much-anticipated, and somewhat divisive, Death Stranding, and while he’ll probably be focusing on updates for that for a while, it seems like he might be planning to return to the horror genre for his next game.

Of course, before Hideo Kojima broke away from Konami to create Death Stranding, he was working on Silent Hills with Guillermo del Toro and Norman Reedus. That project was cancelled when Kojima left Konami, which a lot of fans are still getting over, given how promising the project sounded and how legit terrifying its “P.T.” teaser demo was.
