Landmark Mayo pub to go under the hammer

25 Nov 2019, 11:55

The landmark public house premises known as Stack’s Bar is to go for sale by public auction next month.

The property, which is located at the junction of Thomas Street and Upper Charles Street, will be auctioned at 12 noon on December 13 at the Harbour Hotel, New Dock Road, Galway City.

The package, as well as a ground floor licensed premises, has three apartments to the rear.

The selling agents are O’Donnellan & Joyce Auctioneers, Galway.

Stacks is described by the auctioneers as being located in an area which is extremely commercial in character with a number of licensed premises and retail units all in the immediate vicinity.
