How to get tickets for the big Little Mix gigs in Wales next summer

The girls are set to hit two big stages in Wales in 2020

25 Nov 2019, 11:17 by Katie-Ann Gupwell

The big news is out... the world's biggest girl band Little Mix are heading back to Wales in 2020.

Perrie Edwards, Jesy Nelson, Leigh-Anne Pinnock and Jade Thirlwall will visit Cardiff City Stadium and Stadiwm Zip World in Colwyn Bay next year, and lots of people are wondering how to get tickets.

The hugely popular girl group met and became an act on The X Factor in 2011 which they went on to win.

Now they're set to perform in Cardiff on Sunday, June 28 and in Colwyn Bay on Saturday, July 11 .

Announcing their tour, the group said: "Performing live is our favourite thing to do as a band, we love it.

“Our last summer tour in 2018 was one of our favourites ever, so we can’t wait for some more brilliant outdoor shows next year.

"We want everyone to come party with us in the sunshine."

How do I get tickets?

Tickets for the shows will go on sale on Thursday, November 28 .

They will be available to purchase from Ticketmaster.co.uk and Venuecymru.co.uk .

Alternatively, you can also call 01492 872000.

At least 75p from each ticket sale will be donated to charity, Nordoff Robbins, that uses music therapy to help people affected by life limiting illness, isolation or disability.
