Kitchener property taxes could go up 2.2%: report

Kitchener city council will open up discussions on their operating budget Monday morning

25 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Mark Pare

A lengthy discussion is set to take place at Kitchener city hall, as council takes a glance at their operating budget for next year.

Plans won't likely be finalized until a budget meeting set for January 20th, 2020. Â But as it stands, there is a proposed property tax increase of 2.2 per cent for 2020.

That would work out to being $24 extra for the average homeowner for the year, while increases to water utilities and gas would put the total increase to $81 on average for homeowners.
