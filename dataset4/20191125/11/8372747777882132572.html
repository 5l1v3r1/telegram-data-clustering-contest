3 teens arrested after Indiana woman's body found in abandoned school

25 Nov 2019, 11:03 by WRTV Staff

GARY, Ind. â€” Three teenage boys are in police custody Monday morning in connection with the death of a missing northwest Indiana woman.

The body of Adriana Saucedo, 27, was found in an abandoned elementary school in Gary.

Police say Saucedo met a 15-year-old boy on Facebook. Tuesday night, he and two other teens picked her up from her home in Portage.

Investigators say once she was in the car, the teens shot her, then dumped her body at the school.

So far, no charges have been filed in the case.
