Small fire at a Fort Myers apartment complex Sunday night

25 Nov 2019, 11:02 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Fire investigators will go back to work Monday, trying to figure out what caused a small fire at a Fort Myers apartment complex.

Fox 4 viewers sent in pictures from the Retreat at Vista Lake, which is off Winkler Avenue.

The people who live there say they can smell a lot of smoke, but nobody had to leave their homes.
