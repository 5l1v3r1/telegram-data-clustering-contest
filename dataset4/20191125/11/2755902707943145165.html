This is How Much a 10kg Bag Of Mealie Meal Costs in Zimbabwe

The struggle continues for impoverished Zimbabweans following the removal of subsidies on grain by The Finance Minister Mthuli Ncube in The 2020 Budget. The new policy means Millers are now purchasing at market price from The Grain Marketing Board. Ordinary citizens are feeling the brunt of the change as a 10kg bag of Mealie Meal now costs ZWL102 or the equivalent of USD 5. Given that citizens are earning ZWL2000 and below, Mealie Meal will soon become a challenge for many.

The Government of Zimbabwe has with immediate effect removed subsidies on grain, resulting to the price of GMB maize to ZWL 4,000.00/MT.
1. ROLLER MEAL In light of the above price movement, the price of roller meal shall be circa RTGS 89.96 per 10 kg bag. The traditional margin for miller prices to retailers is 13%, has giving a retail price of circa RTGS 101.66 per 10 kg bag.
