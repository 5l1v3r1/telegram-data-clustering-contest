Naira Marley is the hottest Naija artiste - Rapper Ycee says

25 Nov 2019, 09:56

- Rapper Ycee has praised Naira Marley on social media

- The rapper stated that Marley is the hottest artiste in Nigeria

- Ycee added that no one would agree that he is the hottest artiste in Nigeria

Nigerian rapper Oludemilade Martin Alejo, popularly known by his stage name Ycee, has praised fellow rapper, Naira Marley, on social media. The rapper shared his thoughts about Marley on social media.

The rapper took to the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter, to praise Marley. He stated that Marley is the hottest artiste in Nigeria.

Ycee further noted that many people would not agree that Marley is the hottest Nigerian artiste because of the industry.
