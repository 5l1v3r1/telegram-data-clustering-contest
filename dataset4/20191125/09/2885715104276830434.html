Krushna Abhishek: Bigg Boss is not my cup of tea

Krushna Abhishek on hosting OMG! Yeh Mera India season 6 and his sister Arti Singh's stint inside the Bigg Boss 13 house.

25 Nov 2019, 09:34 by Sana Farzeen

Krushna Abhishek is back as the host of OMG! Yeh Mera India season 6. The factual entertainment show presents extraordinary stories of ordinary Indians. The 10-episode series airs every Monday at 8 pm on History TV18.

Krushna, who is also seen in The Kapil Sharma Show, playing the hilarious Sapna, recently sat down for an exclusive chat with indianexpress.com about hosting the latest season of OMG! Yeh Mera India. The actor also opened up about his sister Arti Singh, who is presently locked in the Bigg Boss 13 house. While applauding her efforts, Krushna also mentioned that he will never participate in the controversial show.

Excerpts from the conversation:

1. What can we expect from OMG! Yeh Mera India 6?

Honestly, it’s one of my most favourite shows. Everywhere I go, people keep chanting ‘OMG OMG’, even when I am on stage for another show. The entire team works really hard to bring some interesting facts about people from across the country. Every time I read the script, I am amazed by the research. I am sure the audience loves it too. In this season too, the audience will be treated to a lot of surprising elements.
