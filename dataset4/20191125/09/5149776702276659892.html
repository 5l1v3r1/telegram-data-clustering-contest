Picture released after reports of woman being racially abusive towards taxi driver

Do you recognise the woman pictured?

25 Nov 2019, 09:39 by Phoebe Ram

Pictures have been released after police were called to reports of a woman being racially abusive towards a taxi driver after damaging a car.

The incident happened around 4am on Saturday, October 26, in Lower Parliament Street near the NG-One nightclub.

Nottinghamshire Police would like to speak to the woman pictured in connection with the incident.

Neighbourhood Inspector for Nottingham City, Paul Gummer, said: “Racism has no place in Nottinghamshire and we are committed to ensuring that all reports are investigated thoroughly.

“If you recognise the woman pictured, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us via a 101 quoting incident number 168 of 26 October 2019.”
