A Better Look at the Off-White™ x Nike Dunk Low “Pine Green”

Set to release this month.

25 Nov 2019, 09:10 by Mallory Chin

Back in August, Virgil Abloh confirmed three Off-White™ x Nike Dunk Lows were on their way. Taking to social media earlier today, Virg gave fans another teaser of the highly-anticipated model, which is now expected to release on December 20.

Thanks to Stadium Goods, we now have our best look yet at the forthcoming “Pine Green” colorway dressing the aforementioned sneaker. The collaborative silhouette sports a green and white upper, finished off with a matching white midsole and green outsole. Off-White™’s signature additions include the zip ties dressed in full white hanging off of the orange hiking-inspired lacing system, and orange accents featured on the lateral Swoosh insignias and tongue tab branding. Finally Off-White™’s boldface iconography is embellished on the medial sides.

Along with the “Pine Green” colorway, a “University Red” and Michigan-fitting “University Gold” color scheme are also slated to drop next month each retailing at $170 USD per pair.

Head over to Stadium Goods to be notified when the Off-White™ x Nike Dunk Low “Pine Green” drops.
