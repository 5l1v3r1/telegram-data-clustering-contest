COZA: She Is Frustrated Idiot, Journalist Comes For Busola Dakolo

25 Nov 2019, 09:04 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Popular controversial journalist, Kemi Olunloyo has again blasted celebrity photographer, Busola Dakolo, describing her as ‘a frustrated idiot’

Olunloyo, in an Instagram post explained how much she enjoys sermon by Modele, wife of COZA pastor, Biodun Fatoyinbo.

“Pastor @modelefatoyinbo @biodunfatoyinbo’s wife is KILLING IT @cozaglobal. Jesus! The woman can even preach harder than any female pastor I’ve EVER seen and she’s very attractive too. Damn she can preach? “I declare success in my life in 2020” she wrote.

Responding, a follower attested to Olunloyo’s claim while saying that Busola’s rape allegation against COZA pastor disrupted Modele’s sermon.

“She’s amazing i use to attend some of their programs like 7DG, obviously this year’s program was disrupted because of busola. God will judge her for that….Pastor MO is such an amazing preacher and she’s underrated,” the follower said.

Reacting to the comment, Olunloyo stated that Busola is a frustrated idiot.

The celebrity photographer had accused Fatoyinbo of raping her on two different occasions in 2002 in her family house when she was a teenager.

Recall that Olunloyo waded into the rape claims  by Busola, believing that the photographer laid false accusations on Fatoyinbo.

The celebrity photographer had sued Fatoyinbo, demanding that the COZA Pastor publishes an apology to her on the front page of at least two national newspapers, noting that the apology should also run on two national television stations for seven days running consecutively.
