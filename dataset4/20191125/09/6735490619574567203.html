Songwriter Accuses Madonna Of Failing To Credit Him On ‘Madame X’ Track: ‘They F**ked Me Over!’

25 Nov 2019, 09:19 by Becca Longmire

Songwriter Casey Spooner has slammed Madonna, accusing her of failing to credit and pay him properly for his work on the Madame X song “God Control”.

Spooner has been sharing numerous social media posts about his alleged ordeal, writing in one caption: “I have had enough. I’ve gotten no credit and no compensation.

“While you’re galavanting around on stage I’m completely broke in Berlin. Robbed, ignored and delayed.”

He also said he’d been offered a $25,000 advance, but after legal fees and taxes it would end up more like $10,000.

The musician, part of the recently split electronic pop group Fischerspooner, claimed he worked with “God Control” producer Mirwais back in 2017 for his solo album. However, it was not released.

Bits of their work did however end up in the Madonna song but he wasn’t told beforehand.

Spooner wrote he should be paid one per cent of touring profits.

He shared, “There is no money in record sales. Period. Not even for Madonna.”
