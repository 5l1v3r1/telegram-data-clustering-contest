sacai x Nike Rumored to Drop PEG Vaporfly SP Collab Fall 2020

Reportedly three colorways.

25 Nov 2019, 09:52 by Sophie Caraan

Following the possible reveal of the forthcoming sacai x Nike LDWaffle iterations, rumors are now pointing to yet another release slated for late 2020. According to reports, sacai and Nike‘s next project will take on the hybrid PEG (Pegasus) Vaporfly SP silhouette and will be offered in three colorways — a neutral “Black/Summit White/Pure Platinum” iteration along with the vibrant “Sail/Sport Fuchsia/Light Bone/Game Royal” and “String/Black-Villain Red/Neptune Green” models. Neither renders nor official images have hit the Internet at the moment, but sources are claiming a Fall 2020 release date for the rumored collaboration.

While the PEG Vaporfly SP stirs in the rumor pot, more photos of sacai and Nike’s upcoming LDWaffle has surfaced on the internet. The rumored offerings are expected to arrive in tonal black and triple white iterations, utilizing a luxe combination of materials including leather, suede and mesh. While an exact release date and prices are yet to be revealed, the two models will most likely arrive between March and April 2020.

Stay tuned for more information including imagery, pricing details and release date.
