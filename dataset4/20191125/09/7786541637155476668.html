American Music Awards 2019: Taylor Swift performs 'Love Story,' 'Blank Space,' other past hits

25 Nov 2019, 09:29 by Michelle Ewing, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

LOS ANGELES -

Following a headline-making spat with ex-label Big Machine Records, pop diva Taylor Swift broke out several of her past hits Sunday during her performance at the 2019 American Music Awards.

These were the six songs featured in her medley:

1. "The Man" (from "Lover," 2019)

In what "Entertainment Tonight" described as a nod to her spat with Big Machine's Scott Borchetta and Scooter Braun, Swift, clad in a jacket bearing her old album titles, kicked off her performance with an excerpt of her new song "The Man," which includes the lyrics: "And I'm so sick of them coming at me again / 'Cause if I was a man, then I'd be the man."
