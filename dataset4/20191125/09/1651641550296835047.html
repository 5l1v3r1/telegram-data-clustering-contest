Hideo Kojima hasn’t given up on horror, wants to make “the scariest horror game”

25 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Lewis White

Despite the cancellation of P.T/Silent Hills and the shifting focus towards Death Stranding, game director Hideo Kojima hasn’t given up on creating horror games. In fact, Hideo Kojima wants to make “the scariest horror game”.

Even with the memory of Silent Hills fresh in everyone’s minds, Kojima is still adamant that he will create the world’s scariest horror game.

With Death Stranding out in the wild and playable on PS4 –with a PC port in the works – Kojima is ready to move onto his next project. It’s probably horror themed.

“As to make the scariest horror game, I’ll watch the scary movies in order to awaken my horror soul,” Kojima wrote on Twitter. “THE EYE is the Thai horror movie I rent when making PT but was too scary to finish watching. The package is scary so I rented the disc only. Will I be able to finish watching?”
