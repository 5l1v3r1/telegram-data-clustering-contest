French Montana Still In ICU 6 Days After Being Rushed To Hospital

25 Nov 2019, 09:53 by Kyle Eustice

Calabasas, CA – Doctors are reportedly scrambling to figure out what has sidelined French Montana in a San Fernando Valley hospital. According to TMZ, medical personnel still haven’t pinpointed a reason for French’s stomach pain, nausea and elevated heart rate.

The Los Angeles-based rapper was rushed to the hospital by ambulance last Thursday (November 21) after police investigating an alleged robbery at his Calabasas home noticed he wasn’t acting right.

On Monday morning (November 25), French shared a video clip to his Twitter account, capturing his surroundings.

“6 days in icu,” he wrote.
