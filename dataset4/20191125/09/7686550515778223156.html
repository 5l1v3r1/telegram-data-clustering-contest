All of the Christmas films to watch on Netflix

Snuggle up on the sofa and watch a heartwarming movie

25 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Lea Nakache

'Tis the season for hot chocolates, log fires and lazy afternoon's spent curling up on the sofa and watching a festive film.

As always, Netflix has released a whole host of original movies to keep you and the family entertained throughout November and December.

Here's a round-up of what you can watch in the lead of up to the big day.

Holiday in the Wild

If the cold weather isn't your cup of hot cocoa, this movie under the African sun is for you.

Newly-divorced Kate decides to go solo on her second honeymoon, where she meets handsome Derek, who will show her the delight of the continent, giving Kate a new hope.

In summary, it’s all about festive fun with Rob Lowe and elephants.

Let It Snow
