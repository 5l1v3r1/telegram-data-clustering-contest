2 killed in shooting outside of North Carolina medical center

25 Nov 2019, 09:38 by Scripps National

Two people were killed Monday in a shooting outside of a medical center in Durham, North Carolina, according to WRAL-TV in Raleigh.

Citing sources with the Durham police department, WRAL reported that two people — a man and a woman — were shot and killed outside of the University of North Carolina Medical Center at about 8 a.m.

Police have not given any information about a suspect.

The medical center remains on lockdown.

WRAL reports that Monday morning's shooting was the third in the city in a 24-hour span.
