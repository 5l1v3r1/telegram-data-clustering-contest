I-675 North now open after single-vehicle rollover crash

25 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Breaking News Staff

CLARK COUNTY — ﻿Multiple medic units were requested to the scene of a single-vehicle rollover crash on I-675 North near mile marker 26.

I-675 was briefly shut down near I-70 as crews worked to clear the scene of the crash. 

The crash was reported around 7:15 p.m. on Saturday night.

The extent of the injuries were unclear.
