Jenna Dewan did not shade Camila Cabello at the AMAs, ok?

25 Nov 2019, 09:38

In case you were wondering, no Jenna Dewan did not throw shade at Camila Cabello as she performed at Sunday’s American Music Awards.

After eagle-eyed punters thought they’d caught the pregnant dancer commenting on Camila’s Senorita routine, Jenna was quick to dismiss the claims.

Earlier in the night, in a clip shared online, social media followers believed Jenna leant into BFF Brad Goreski and whispered ‘she’s so extra’ the way of Camila, who was performing alongside Shawn Mendes.

While being labelled as ‘extra’ isn’t always seen as a bad thing, Jenna’s apparent eye roll caused fans to wonder whether it was at all a complimentary comment.

They were all, oh no she didn’t!

Turns out, well, no she actually didn’t.

Setting the record straight as she left the venue, held at the Microsoft Theatre on Los Angeles, Jenna said: ‘I’m getting a lot of texts messages from people wondering if I was shading Camila. No! I love her, I love her so much.

‘I’m such a big fan of hers.

‘I don’t understand what that’s about but no, I love her, love her, love her.’
