Leo binds Spirit of death over Bambam and Teddy A

25 Nov 2019, 09:55 by Sanya Agunbiade

Big Brother Naija 2018 star, Leo Da Silva, while responding to a tweet on social media, tried to bind the spirit of death over Teddy A and Bambam.

Leo’s prayer was necessary after an innocent tweet suggested a deeper meaning/consequence because BamTeddy were likened to Romeo and Juliet.

DSTV Nigeria had made a tweet on the newly wedded couple, asking why twitter users have not used the couple’s photo for a Romeo and Juliet meme.

Responding to the tweet, Leo wrote:

I reject it for them in Jesus name. They will not die but live to declare the praises of our Lord Jesus Christ in the land of the living.

It is worthy of note to remember that Romeo and Juliet, although a love story, was a tragic one that ended in tears… sorry death.
