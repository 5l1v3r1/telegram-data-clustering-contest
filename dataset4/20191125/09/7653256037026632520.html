25 Nov 2019, 09:32 by Anandi Chandrashekhar

MUMBAI: Softbank backed InMobi has acquired video content platform Roposo as it looks to increase the number of curated content on its platform Glance in vernacular languages in India.

InMobi also plans to take Roposo to Southeast Asia, where it launched Glance early this year. Though the company did not comment on the deal value, industry trackers peg the cash and stock deal at $20 million.

Glance will also benefit from Roposo's popular vernacular language influencers, content creators and over 42 million users,InMobi group founder Naveen Tewari told ET. Glance was recently launched in Bahasa, Indonesia by leveraging the local creator market, he added. The company will now also take Roposo to the Indonesian market. Glance has also has presence in Malaysia, Thailand and Philippines.

"We are attempting to bring together quality content from variety of sources to be part of Glance and we found that Roposo has access to professional influencers who create high quality video content," said Tewari.

The acquisition gives Glance ownership of Roposo's video technology, platform and brand. Roposo, was founded in 2014 by IIT Delhi alumni, Mayank Bhangadia, Avinash Saxena and Kaushal Shubhank. Roposo was started initially as a fashion social network and then transformed into a platform that
offers a TV-like interface with content from regional language influencers. It had raised $10 million from Tiger Global Management and Bertelsmann India Investments earlier last year.

"Glance is a disruptive platform and we are very excited to be a part of it," said Mayank Bhangadia, Founder of Roposo. "We hope to bring our experience and expertise in the area of short-form video platform to deliver engaging video experiences and make Glance the dominant vernacular video platform in India and the world." Glance, which is installed in nearly two thirds of all new smartphones sold in India, has been able to scale up to 60 million daily average users (DAU) users in the last 18 months to become a popular consumer property in India. The company will also expand its presence to Gurgaon with Roposo's 60 member team.
