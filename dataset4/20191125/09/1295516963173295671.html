Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker Shares an Emotional Special Look

25 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Noah Dominguez

As the build-up to Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker enters its final month, Lucasfilm has revealed a new special look at the final chapter in the "Skywalker Saga," looking back on the monumental cultural influence Star Wars has had over the past four decades.

The promo contains countless examples of the beloved Space opera franchise's influence, from the Star Wars fever that first swept the nation back in the '70s and '80s, to The Force Awakens and The Rise of Skywalker director J.J Abrams recalling the first time he saw the words "Star Wars," to various times the series has been referenced in other pieces of pop culture.
