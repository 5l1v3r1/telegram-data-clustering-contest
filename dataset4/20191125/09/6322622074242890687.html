Uber will not receive new London operating license: LBC

25 Nov 2019, 09:29 by Reuters

LONDON (Nov 25): Uber will not receive a new operating license in London from the city’s transport regulator, LBC radio reported on Monday citing unnamed sources, on the day the taxi app’s right to take rides in Britain’s capital city is due to expire.

Transport for London said no decision has been made.

Uber declined to comment.
