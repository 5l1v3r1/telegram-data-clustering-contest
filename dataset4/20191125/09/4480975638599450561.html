German IFO Business Climate Index rises to 95.0 in November, EUR/USD unmoved

25 Nov 2019, 09:01 by Dhwani Mehta
  • German IFO Business Climate Index came in at 95.0 in November.
  • German IFO Current Economic Assessment stood at 97.9 in November.
  • German IFO Expectations Index arrived at 92.1 in November.

The headline German IFO Business Climate Index came in at 95.0 in November, firmer than last month's 94.6 and meeting the consensus estimates pointing to a reading of 95.0.
