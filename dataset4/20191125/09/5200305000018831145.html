Kareena, Sonam, Swara & Shikha to reunite for ‘Veere Di Wedding’ sequel?

25 Nov 2019, 09:05 by Raj Baddhan

After the super success of female-centric film ‘Veere Di Wedding’ (2018), plans for the sequel are already underway.

According to Mid-Day, the original four stars Kareena Kapoor Khan, Sonam Kapoor Ahuja, Swara Bhasker and Shikha Talsania, will reunite for the second installment.

A source told the publication, “The first film did well at the box office. Discussions about a sequel have been on for a while, and the makers wanted to take the story forward from where part one ended. The second instalment will explore how the girls’ relationships evolve.”
