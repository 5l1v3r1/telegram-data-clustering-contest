Future Awards: Burna Boy Wins Big (See Complete List)

25 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Afro-fusion singer, Damini Ogulu better known as Burna Boy has bagged two awards at the 14th Future Awards held in Lagos on Sunday.

Concise News understands that Burna Boy bagged The Young Person of the Year and The Future Award Prize for Music.

This year, the award which was themed ‘Nigeria’s New Tribe’, was hosted by ex-BBNaija housemate Khafi Kareem and media personality Taymesan.

The nominees represented outstanding young talents, innovators, entrepreneurs, and community advocates who are breaking boundaries in various industries.

The audience were thrilled with musical performances by Timi Dakolo and Johnny Drille.
