Couple gets married on flight after they couldn’t agree on venue

Australian groom David Valliantand his New Zealand bride Cathy Rolfe got married halfway between Australia and New Zealand on the Australian budget airline's Flight 201.

25 Nov 2019, 09:58 by Trends Desk

An Australian man and New Zealand woman got married 34,000 feet in the air between their home nations, because they said they couldn’t decide on a venue.

David Valliant and his New Zealand bride Cathy Rolfe got married halfway between Australia and New Zealand on flight 201 of Australian budget airline Jetstar. The video of the wedding was posted on Jetstar’s YouTube channel, and it went viral soon.
