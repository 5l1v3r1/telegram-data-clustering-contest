Star Wars: First Rise of Skywalker Clip Shows the Jet Troopers in Action

25 Nov 2019, 09:11 by L.D. Nolan

Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker's first clip is here, and it show the movie's jet troopers in action.

The scene was released by the Star Wars Twitter and shows Rey, Finn, Poe Dameron, Chewbacca and C-3PO attempting to escape a team of jet troopers. As the two exchange fire, the jet troopers take to the air, much to the shock and dismay of the heroes.
