Realme is a phenomenon and there’s no way to deny this fact. The company which started in May 2018 with Realme 1 is now the fastest-growing smartphone brand and crowns itself as one of the top 10 smartphone manufacturers in the world. The smartphone brand that started as Oppo’s subsidiary, can now be considered a sister company, having its own powerful slice of the market and a large number of users. The company started its activities in India and during its short life period already has 14.3% of the Indian market.
