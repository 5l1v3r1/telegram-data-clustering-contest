Lizzo carries the smallest little bag to the American Music Awards 2019

25 Nov 2019, 01:38

Lizzo was surprisingly outshone on the American Music Awards red carpet, after arriving at the bash carrying what is possibly the world’s smallest handbag.

Joining some of music’s biggest stars on Sunday, the Truth Hurts singer strutted along the red carpet in a pretty ruffled one-shoulder minidress, but even the bright orange hue wasn’t enough to distract from her unusual accessory.

The 31-year-old casually posed on the red carpet at Los Angeles’ Microsoft Theatre holding up a tiny white handbag between her fingers.

While it may be small in appearance, the teeny purse was designed by a fashion powerhouse courtesy of Valentino.

So what can Lizzo’s tiny bag fit exactly? Her phone? Lipstick? Snacks for the show?

Nope, none of the above but something much better.

‘@maisonvalentino Bag big enough for my f***s to give. Big body b***h in a Valentin-HO custom look for @amas.’

Brilliant. How do we get one?

Ariana Grande gave the teeny accessory her seal of approval and commented on Instagram: ‘Cutest.’

During her red carpet interview, Lizzo revealed there are only three of the little handbags in the world but they’ll no doubt be snapped up sharpish after tonight.

As for what she packed for the evening, the singer joked: ‘I got tampons in here, a flask of tequila, condoms.’

All the essentials, then.

Lizzo, real name Melissa Jefferson, will be hoping to bag one of the three awards she’s nominated for, which are: best new artist, soul/R&B song of the year and soul/R&B female artist of the year.

Last week, the Juice singer earned eight Grammy nominations including song of the year for Truth Hurts and album of the year for Cuz I Love You.

It’s been a stellar year for Lizzo who spent seven weeks at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart with Truth Hurts, which became one of the longest-running chart-toppers by a solo female rapper.

The AMAs are taking place at the Microsoft Theatre in Los Angeles tonight and will air live on ABC at 8pm ET.
