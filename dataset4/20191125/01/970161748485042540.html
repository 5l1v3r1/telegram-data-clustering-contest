Camila Cabello drops new music video just hours before AMAs performance

25 Nov 2019, 01:25

Camila Cabello gave fans a little taste of what they can expect from her American Music Awards performance, as she dropped her new music video just hours before.

Taking to Instagram, Camila revealed that the video to her new track Living Proof is out right now and the steamy vid sees her channelling her inner fairy nymph while getting hot and heavy with a shirtless man.
