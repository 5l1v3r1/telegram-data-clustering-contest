American Music Awards: Taylor Swift stuns on arrival as fans' wait nearly over

The 29-year-old singer had taken aim at ex-manager Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta after a row over being able to sing her hits at the awards at the Microsoft Theater in LA

25 Nov 2019, 01:15 by Sam Elliott, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sam-elliott/

Flawless Taylor Swift arrived on the red carpet looking incredible as fans wait to find out if she speaks out after a public row with Scooter Braun.

Taylor and her former manager have been at war over rights to her hits.

The megastar accused her former label manager and a prominent music industry figure of "exercising tyrannical control" over her music.

The 29-year-old Shake It Off singer had taken aim at Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta in a social media rant as her feud with them reached new heights.

She said she had been blocked from performing her own songs on TV during the 2019 American Music Awards.

The matter seems to have been resolved but fans want to know what happens next.

She arrived at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles in an incredible green dress, showing off her leg with the sleek number on the red carpet.

Taylor may be tempted to open up on the subject on stage - never being scared of using awards to make points.

A statement from Big Machine Label Group and Dick Clark Productions later said Taylor can perform her own songs and doesn't need their approval for live performances.

Following this development in the saga, Taylor's former record label manager has taken to Instagram to share his own public message for the pop diva.

The dad shared his concerns over death threats his family have received following his row with the Red singer.
