Spain Clinches Sixth Davis Cup With Nadal Win Over Canada's Shapovalov

25 Nov 2019, 01:55

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - Rafael Nadal guided the Spanish national tennis team to its sixth Davis Cup on Sunday with a win against Canadian Denis Shapovalov, taking a 2-0 tally over the Canadian national tennis team.

Playing on home soil, at La Caja Magica stadium in Madrid, Nadal maintained an upper hand throughout, with the final scoresheet reading 6-3, 7-6, (9-7).

Earlier on Sunday, Roberto Bautista Agut defeated 19-year-old Canadian Felix Augier-Aliassime with a score of 7-6, (7-3), 6-3 in a hard-fought match.
