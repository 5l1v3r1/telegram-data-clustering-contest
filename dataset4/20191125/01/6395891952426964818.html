Missing Boston 13-year-olds found safe, police say

25 Nov 2019, 01:19 by Manoella Macedo

BOSTON - Boston Police have issued an update, saying the two missing 13-year-olds have been found safe.

According to police, both teens had last been seen with their families on Saturday.

Elisabeth Sanchez, of Dorchester, and Brian Vila, of Brighton, are believed to be together and have been known to frequent the area of Downtown Crossing.

Sanchez is described as a white-Hispanic female, about 5'0", 100 lbs., with dark brown hair, brown eyes with an unknown clothing description. 

Vila is described as a Hispanic male, about 5'6", 160 lbs., with black hair and brown eyes, last seen wearing a black Nike zip-up sweatshirt, blue jeans and white Nike high-top sneakers.
