Selena Gomez Opens the AMAs With the Most Stunning Outfit Change

25 Nov 2019, 01:21 by Sarah Wasilak

Selena Gomez opened the American Music Awards with a serious tone, singing her hit "Lose You to Love Me" in a black turtleneck gown, and then, just like that, she tore off her dress to reveal a mosaic crystal bodysuit by Dior. The mood completely changed when Selena broke it down and danced to "Look at Her Now," emitting all the confidence in the world. Selena had walked the red carpet in a highlighter-green Versace minidress just moments before, then suddenly, here she was showing us she can own the stage, too.

We love that Selena kept on her Roberto Coin diamond necklaces for her performance, because her stage was deserving of complete and total sparkle and shine. Selena capped off the look with black suede Stuart Weitzman Lesley thigh-high boots, which are currently available for $795.
