One person dies in fire at retirement complex in Walnut Creek

25 Nov 2019, 01:30 by Tatiana Sanchez

Updated 5:32 pm PST, Sunday, November 24, 2019

One person died Saturday night in a fire at the Rossmoor retirement community in Walnut Creek, authorities said.

The fire erupted in an eight-unit residential building around 6:20 p.m. Firefighters contained the blaze to a single unit, but smoke and water damage also displaced at least four residents of three neighboring apartments.
