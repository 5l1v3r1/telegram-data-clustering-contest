Darren Rovell Has An Update On Willie Taggart’s Contract Situation With Florida State

25 Nov 2019, 22:15 by Matt Hladik

Florida State parted ways with head coach Willie Taggart earlier this season after less than two years on the job. In doing so, it seemed they were willing to pay his gargantuan buyout.

However, earlier today, a story from Matt Baker of the Tampa Bay Times stated that Taggart and Florida State never actually signed an official contract. As a result, there looked like a possible way for the school to get out of the $18 million it owed him.

Now, a new report from Action Network’s Darren Rovell claims Taggart actually signed his contract with FSU, but the school did not. Rovell says Taggart should get his money.
