Disney Channel and Disney Junior Will Air New Holiday-Themed Episodes Starting Nov. 30!

25 Nov 2019, 22:05 by Alessia Santoro

The holiday season is about to infiltrate both Disney Channel and Disney Junior come Nov. 30! Disney will be featuring brand-new holiday-themed episodes of your kids' favorite shows between Nov. 30 and Dec. 7 that will air throughout the festive season. Additionally, DisneyNOW will feature a collection of new and existing holiday episodes beginning Nov. 29.

Keep scrolling to see the full holiday lineups on Disney Junior and Disney Channel, and set your DVRs now!
