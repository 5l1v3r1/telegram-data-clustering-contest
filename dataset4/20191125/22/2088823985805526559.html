Buffalo gives shoppers the gift of free parking this holiday season

25 Nov 2019, 22:02

Small Business Saturday is coming up, and to encourage residents and visitors to shop in Buffalo's retail districts throughout the holiday season, the city is offering free parking in specific locations. It is also offering a temporary break on overnight parking restrictions.

Free parking will be available on Black Friday, Nov. 29, and from Dec. 21 to Jan. 1 at metered, on-street spots in retail districts citywide as well as in city lots at 198 Allen St., 301 Bryant St., 304 W. Utica St. and 569 Forest Ave.

Also, on New Year's Eve, free parking will be available at the Fernbach Ramp, located at 123 Franklin St., said Mayor Byron W. Brown during a press conference Monday at the Market Arcade.

And due to the favorable short-term weather forecast, winter parking restrictions on bus routes from 1:30 to 7 a.m. have been lifted through Sunday, he said.

Brown also proclaimed Nov. 30 as Small Business Saturday in Buffalo.
