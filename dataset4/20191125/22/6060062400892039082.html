On Sunday, Kim Kardashian West posted a video on Twitter and on her Instagram Story where she shared a food hack that she calls her "secret of life."

In the video, Kardashian West explained why she swears by microwaving M&Ms before eating them.

"This is how you eat M&M's! Microwave them for 30 seconds then enjoy the melted magic!" she wrote in her tweet.
