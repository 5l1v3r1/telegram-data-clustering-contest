I'm A Celebrity's Kiosk Keith 'wants his job back from Kiosk Kev'

25 Nov 2019, 22:37

I’m a Celebrity’s former proprietor of the Outback Shack, Kiosk Keith, is apparently desperate for his old job back after being fired for being drunk on the job.

Keith, real name Raymond Grant, was pulled from the show in January 2018 after reports he had been drinking while filming the show.

The father-of-four also apparently ‘behaved inappropriately towards a female member of the team’.

But according to his ex-wife Donna, Raymond has turned himself around since getting the boot from the show, and now wants a new shot at the series.

‘He’s in a really good health and loves going out to work every day,’ she told The Sun.

‘Raymond said he’d never go back to I’m A Celebrity as a labourer but he would like to back in the role of Kiosk Keith.’

Currently, Kev’s bearded and just-as-mopey replacement Kiosk Kev has stepped into his new role of the protector of extra coveted treats for the celebrity campmates.

In each episode, Kiosk Kev trades in earned ‘dingo dollars’ for treats for the camp, quizzing them on UK surveys before handing the goods over.

He’s played by Survivor star Mark Herlaar, who became known on the series for his love of yoga and arts.

Announcing his return to the show this year, he said: ‘What an honour to be part of such an epic show @imacelebrity UK.

‘I love working on this show great people great site and of course I’m always at home in any jungle .. so if you’re not familiar with the show give ‘em a follow and stick around it be fun.

‘A special hello of course to my fans in England love your support and positive feed back its amazing,’ he continued. ‘Enjoy guys . I’m having a great time being #kioskkev on #imacelebrity’
