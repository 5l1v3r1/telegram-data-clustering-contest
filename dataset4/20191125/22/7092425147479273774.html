Aston Villa vs Newcastle player ratings as Hourihane gets on the scoresheet in 2-0 win

25 Nov 2019, 22:29 by Buzz Contributor

Aston Villa secured a much-needed win at the expense of Newcastle at Villa Park this evening thanks to goals from Conor Hourihane and Anwar El Ghazi.

Both strikes came in the first-half, both from set-pieces after two sloppy fouls from Steve Bruce's side.

Jack Grealish ran the show for the home team, causing Newcastle's defence real problems, while Corkman Hourihane bagged a goal and an assist on the night.

But it was another game where the Magpies' attackers failed to score or make an impact.

Buzz Sport takes a look at who impressed and who flopped in the Midlands.
