TV Contract

25 Nov 2019, 22:47 by BRIEF

LOUISVILLE, Ky. – Strategic Wealth Designers sued Sinclair Media in Jefferson County, Kentucky, on Monday for airing a competitor’s financial news show despite a contract to exclusively broadcast and promote Strategic Wealth’s weekly program on the local WKRC television station.
