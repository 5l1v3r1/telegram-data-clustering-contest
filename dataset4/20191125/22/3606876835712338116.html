David Beckham Once known for his long blond locks, Beckham famously shaved his head in 2000 after a major upset at the 1998 World Cup. The haircut is claimed to have cost more than $500. Shaun Botterill - Getty Images

Kristin Stewart In March 2017, Steward shaved her long brown locks and colored her cropped hair bleach blonde. The look, which Steward called practical for her role in Underwater, was also a style the actor wanted to try out personally. Alberto E. Rodriguez - Getty Images

Halsey Singer Halsey is known for her chameleon-like hair changes, including dabbling in shaved styles. The biracial singer told Nylon in May 2017 that hair has been a big indicator of racial issues in her life and that shaving her head was a way to prove that she could still love herself without her long locks. Lester Cohen - Getty Images

Millie Bobbie Brown Before she was a household name, Brown shaved her head for her role as Eleven in Stranger Things. On Instagram, Brown called shaving her head the most empowering moment of [her] whole life. Curtis Baker/Netflix

Zayn Malik Malik has rocked cropped hair from time to time. But back in Sept. 2017, the singer went completely bald, shocking fans. Malik told BBC Radio 1 he shaved all of his hair off because he bleached it too many times and it was a bit destroyed. Jason Kempin - Getty Images

Matthew McConaughey Even with a shaved head, McConaughey looks alright, alright, alright. The actor shaved his head after rocking a questionable balding hairstyle in the film Gold. Lester Cohen - Getty Images

Justin Timberlake Once known for his curly blond locks in his boy band days, Timberlake took a buzzer to his head for the first time in 2001. Now, the look is so synonymous with Timberlake, its hard to imagine his do any other way. Christopher Polk - Getty Images

Cara DeLavigne Against the advice of her agents and managers, DeLavigne shaved her head in 2017 for a movie role playing a cancer survivor. She called the cut liberating , saying it proved to her that you dont need hair to be beautiful. David M. Benett - Getty Images

Chris Hemsworth Thor is no stranger to a shaved head. Rocking the no-hair look early in his career and at various points thereafter, Hemsworth proves some dudes look good with any do. Brian To - Getty Images

Amandla Stenberg In January 2017, Stenberg shaved their head for a role in Where Hands Touch, which follows the story of biracial children in Nazi Germany. I feel so aerodynamic now, Stenberg joked about the haircut. Ive achieved ultimate speed Brandon Williams - Getty Images
