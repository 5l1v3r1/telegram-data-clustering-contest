Pick up Christmas poinsettias at Orban's Nursery in Bradenton

25 Nov 2019, 22:45 by Zack Perry

BRADENTON, Fla. — Orban's Nursery in Bradenton is a fourth-generation family business that’s been growing poinsettias for more than 70 years.

You can find these colorful holiday plants at Publix and other select stores and garden centers throughout Florida.

But if you're wanting to pick up some straight from Orban's Nursery, visit the annual Poinsettia Drive-Thru Sale on Saturday, November 30 from 9 a.m. - 3 p.m.

Come see over 200,000 blooming poinsettias before they are shipped out for the holidays.

This is a rare opportunity for the community to visit the nursery, take photos and buy holiday plants.

Check out Orban's Nursery at 9601 9th Ave NW in Bradenton.
