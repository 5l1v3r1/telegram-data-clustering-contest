Woman pulled from beneath train in North Knoxville, officials say

25 Nov 2019, 22:08 by WVLT News

KNOXVILLE, Tenn (WVLT) -- Knoxville officials said a 33-year-old woman was hit by a train near Cedar Lane and Fennel Road Monday evening.

The woman was reportedly hit approximately 1,000 yards off the roadway. Knoxville fire crews rescued the victim from under the train. The woman was reportedly conscious and alert at the time of rescue.

KPD officials said the conductor followed standard procedure and stopped the train after he realized the train hit the woman.

Officials said the women was transported to the University of Tennessee with severe injuries.

Norfolk officials were on the scene and will conduct their own investigation of the incident, according to KPD. Initial reports said the person had been in a vehicle.

In October, a pedestrian was hit and killed by a train in the same area.
