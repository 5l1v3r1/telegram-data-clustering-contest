A Former Cricketer Is A Big Chance Of Landing A Role At The Adelaide Crows

25 Nov 2019, 22:39

A former Redback could be in line for a new job at the Adelaide Crows, reports Stephen Rowe.

Rowey told FIVEaa Breakfast on Tuesday:

“Greg Chappell we know has retired as an Aussie selector and George Bailey, the Tasmanian’s won that role.

“Now why do I mention that? Because it’s got impacts on the Crows culture and leadership role. Ex Redback Michael Klinger -- I know he may get the Melbourne Renegades job, but he missed out on that one -- he’s up to his eyeballs for that culture and leadership role…

“So expect an announcement in the next seven days there.”
