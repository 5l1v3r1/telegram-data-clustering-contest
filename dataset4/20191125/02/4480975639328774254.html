Japan PM Abe-China’s Pres. Xi meeting likely to be in Spring 2020 - Reuters

25 Nov 2019, 02:01 by Dhwani Mehta

Latest headlines are crossing the wires, via Reuters, citing that Japanese PM Abe has been in meetings with the top Chinese diplomat, Wang Yi, working for a State visit from China's President Xi in early 2020.

The report said the date is likely to be in the Japanese spring of 2020.
