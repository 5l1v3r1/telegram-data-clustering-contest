NCP tries to win over Ajit Pawar who asks party to follow him

25 Nov 2019, 02:38 by Krishna Thevar

MUMBAI: NCP on Sunday made renewed attempts to convince Ajit Pawar to resign as deputy CM. Senior NCP leaders Jayant Patil and Dilip Walse Patil met Ajit on Sunday urging him to resign from the BJP-led government and obey the instructions of ‘Pawar saheb’.

However, Ajit told them that there was no question of resigning and the party should fall in line and support BJP to avoid a split, EThas learnt. Ajit also told them that though MLAs were ‘holed up’ with Sharad Pawar, they will switch sides during the trust vote.

Ahead of his meeting with Ajit Pawar, Jayant said: “Almost all our MLAs are with us, only Ajit has left. We are trying to convince him.”

Soon after the meeting, Ajit Pawar tweeted: “I am in NCP and shall always be in NCP and Sharad Pawar is our leader. Our BJP-NCP alliance shall provide a stable government in Maharashtra for the next five years.”
