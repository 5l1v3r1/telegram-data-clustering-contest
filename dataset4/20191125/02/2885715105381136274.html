CAT 2019 paper analysis: Verbal Ability section ‘tough’, check candidates’ reactions

CAT 2019: The candidates who have appeared for the b-school entrance examinations rated the overall paper as moderate but the difficulty level in English section as high

25 Nov 2019, 02:18 by Arnab Mitra

CAT 2019: The Common Admission Test (CAT) is being conducted throughout the country on Friday, November 24, 2019. The first slot of the CAT just concluded while the second slot will begin at 2:30 pm. The candidates who have appeared for the B-school entrance examinations rated the overall paper as moderate but the difficulty level in English section as high. The Quantitative Ability and Data Interpretation section was moderate, they said.

Speaking to indianexpress.com, Gautam Bawa, Vice President, Career Launcher said, “The questions asked in the verbal section was quite tricky, it is hard to answer reading the passage once. The Quantitative Ability and Data Interpretation sections were easier to solve. The difficulty level of the paper was moderate.”
