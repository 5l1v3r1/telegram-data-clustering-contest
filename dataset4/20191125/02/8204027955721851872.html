Five-car crash reported at Salem Avenue and Free Pike

25 Nov 2019, 02:19 by Breaking News Staff

DAYTON — A five-car crash has been reported at Salem Avenue and Free Pike.

Regional dispatch indicated that three people have been injured and one person is unconscious. The extent of their injuries is unknown.

Scanner traffic indicates a moving truck may have been involved.

The crash was reported around 1:20 p.m.
