Asus Zenfone 6 hardware

What are the hardware features of this device?

Design

One of the most incredible design features of this new release is its flip camera, which handles the issue of the front and rear one being separate and having a difference in quality. It weighs 190 g and has an aluminium frame. There is a protective Gorilla Glass 6 screen in front and back, which prevents the device from scratches.

The device's full model name is Asus Zenfone 6 ZS630KL. It comes in Midnight Black and Twilight Silver. Is the Asus Zenfone 6 waterproof? Unfortunately, it is not, though it offers other great features.

Display

This gadget has a 6.4-inch display with a 19:5:9 ratio, which is compact and convenient for usage. It is an LCD capacitive touchscreen with a 1080 x 2340 resolution and is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 6, just like the body.

Processor, storage, and performance

This new release has a powerful Qualcomm SDM855 Snapdragon 855, which is undoubtedly one of the strongest features. It also has an octa-core CPU and an Adreno 640 GPU. With such a reliable processor, the performance is guaranteed to be top-notch.

It has three memory options: 64 GB, 128 GB, or 256 GB. The first two come with 6GB of RAM, while the last one has 8GB of RAM. There is also a microSD slot, which you can use for expandable memory up to 1 TB.

Battery

The device is powered with a non-removable Li-Po 5000 mAh battery, which is another reason why it is widely praised. It also has a Quick Charge 4.0 technology and 18W fast battery charging. Asus Zenfone 6 battery can last up to 624 hours in a standby state, and the user can spend up to 33 hours talking.

Speaker and call quality

If you purchase this device, you will get a powerful loudspeaker with stereo speakers. Additionally, it has a 3.5mm jack, 24-bit/192kHz audio, and active noise cancellation with dedicated mic. The calls are very clear on this device.

Asus Zenfone 6 software

What about the software features?

OS and features

The gadget runs on Android 9.0 (Pie) and ZenUI 6 operating systems. You will also get regular updates and security patches. Some of the features that come with the device are fingerprint, accelerometer, proximity, compass, and gyro sensors.

Camera, video and image quality

The flip camera is one of the inventions that Asus company takes pride in. In the device, the rear and front cameras the same, with the option of flipping the little module around. The flip camera has a 48 MP wide and a 13 MP ultrawide camera.

With this flip camera, the user can take fantastic photos. It has fast autofocus, nice colours, and good flash use results. It can also be used for recording videos of 2160p quality at 30 or 60 fps, 1080p at 30, 60 or 240 fps, and 720p at 480fps. As a result, it is a great choice for those who cannot imagine their lives without creating videos and taking pictures of brilliant quality.
