IN-Y vs AF-Y Dream11 Match Prediction : India U19 vs Afghanistan U19 Best Dream 11 Team For Today’s Match

25 Nov 2019, 21:36 by Arya Sekhar Chakraborty

IN-Y vs AF-Y Dream11 : The Indian U-19 team will look to win the 5 match ODI series against Afghanistan when they meet the Asian minnows in the 3rd game of the series in Lucknow.

The Men in Blue has been dominating in the series so far and stands a chance to wrap up the series later today. Afghanistan, on the other hand, will look to keep the series alive by registering their first win of the series.

They gave a valiant effort in the 2nd youth ODI but it wasn’t enough to stop India from registering their 3rd victory against Afghanistan from as many matches. Afghanistan will have to play out of their skins if thy harbor hopes of winning their 1st ODI against the Sri Lankan youth team.

Match Conditions and Toss

The Ekana Cricket Stadium B will host another match of the series and the tradition of the pitch is expected to remain the same. Teams batting first at this venue has put up 230+ only once in the last 8 matches and teams have preferred chasing at this venue. As the game has progressed the pitch has become batting-friendly which has allowed the team batting 2nd to come out triumphant on most occasions.

IND-Y vs AF-Y Dream 11 Batsmen

Yasashvi Jaiswal will be our first pick in this segment. He has looked in great touch with the bat and scored a quickfire 38 of 25 deliveries in the previous clash. Jaiswal will definitely look to continue his exploits and put up another substantial score.

Imran Mir will be the next pick in this segment. He hasn’t been in the best of touches against India but has been a top-class batsman for the Afghanistan youth team. Mir will look to get his act back together in the all-important clash.

Priyam Garg will be the 3rd pick of this segment. He scored 32 in the previous clash against the Afghans. He was also in good form during the U 19 Challenger Trophy.

Tilak Verma will definitely look to make an impact in the 3rd youth ODI. He scored a quickfire 29 in the previous clash and also chipped in with two crucial wickets. Hence he will be an important option in the Dream XI team.

Rahmanullah Zadran will be the next pick in this segment. He has been among runs in the series so far and scored a fluent 47 in the previous clash against India U 19.

IND-Y vs AF-Y Dream 11 All-rounders

Shafiqullah Ghafari will be our first pick in this segment. He has been a star performer for Afghanistan U 19 team. Though he scored a duck in the previous clash, his all-round skills is very important for a dream XI team and more often than not he will fetch you the points.

Divyansh Joshi will be the last pick in this segment. He smashed 10 of just 3 deliveries in the previous clash and also chipped in with an important wicket.

IND-Y vs AF-Y Dream 11 Bowlers

The first pick in this segment will be CTL Rakshan from the Indian youth team. He has been among the wickets in the recent past and bagged a couple in the 2nd ODI against the Afghans. He will definitely look to make an impact later today.

Akash Singh, the relatively inexperienced guy will be our next pick. He bowled in great rhythm in the previous clash and bagged a wicket for just 22 runs in his 8 overs. Akash will surely look to grab a few more in the 3rd ODI.

The last pick of this segment will be Noor Ahmad. He bagged two important wickets for Afganistan in the previous clash and will look to bowl his side to a famous victory in the 3rd encounter.
