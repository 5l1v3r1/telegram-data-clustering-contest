Play on the Go With the Best Nintendo Switch Lite Cases

25 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Jordan Minor

While the whole hook of the Nintendo Switch is that you can either play it as a home console or powerful handheld, some players only treated it as a home console. They constantly leave it docked and enjoy the latest Nintendo games exclusively on a television. So players like that never needed to get a Switch case.

However, the new and very cool Nintendo Switch Lite is only a powerful handheld. It’s only made for playing on the go. And while that means the design is a bit more durable compared to the original Switch, it also means that Nintendo Switch Lite owners definitely do need to get a case.

Here are some of our picks for the best Nintendo Switch Lite cases.

Nintendo Switch Lite Flip Cover

If you’re willing to wait a few extra weeks, you can get Nintendo’s own official Nintendo Switch Lite case. The Nintendo Switch Lite Flip Cover and Screen Protector ($40) is a thin case that doesn’t add much bulk to the slimmer device. It also includes a protective sheet for the all-important LCD screen.
