Pacific Motorway crash: Multi-vehicle crash causing delays inbound

25 Nov 2019, 21:42

A multi-vehicle crash on the Pacific Motorway is causing significant delays for inbound traffic.

The incident involving four vehicles occurred on the Pacific Motorway northbound at Slacks Creek about 7.15am.

Paramedics are on the scene and delays are expected to continue.

Earlier, Brisbane commuters faced very slow start to the day after a multi-vehicle crash on Gympie Arterial Road at Carseldine blocked two lanes of traffic.

The crash occurred about 5.20am in the inbound lanes just before the intersection with Beams Road, but it has since been cleared.

Delays for inbound vehicles stretched back past to the Gateway Motorway turn-off at one stage, but while the crash has been cleared, some residual delays remain.
