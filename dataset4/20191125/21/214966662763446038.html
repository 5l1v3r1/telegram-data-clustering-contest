Massive M1 crash delays northbound traffic

25 Nov 2019, 21:38 by Monique StClair

If you’re heading into Brisbane this morning, be prepared for some lengthy delays.

A four car crash was reported around 7.15am, between the Hyperdome and Loganlea exit.

It happened in the right hand lane, causing traffic across all lanes to slow right down.

Witnesses say it looked as if all the drivers were ok, though at least one of the vehicles was seriously damaged.

Paramedics have just arrived on the scene, and report that everyone was out of their cars.

It’s understood several assessments are underway.

At the time of writing, heavy delays were already back to Loganholme.
