Prostitution Will Decrease The Rate Of Unemployment Amongst The Youth, It Should Be Legalized – Singer Advises (+Video)

25 Nov 2019, 21:59 by vanessa nordzi

Fast rising Ghanaian songstress, Naf Kassi has backed Fuse ODG on the need for the Government to decriminalize prostitution.

Speaking in an interview with Zionfelix on the ‘Uncut Show’, Naf Kassi revealed how beneficial it will be if Prostitutes are allowed to work without any harassment from the police.

According to Naf Kassi, apart from reducing the unemployment rate in the country, it will decrease the number of men cheating on their partners.

Naf Kassi asserted that men seeking the services of prostitutes would reduce the number of “side chicks” in the system.

However, Naf Kassi believes that adequate health screening tests must be done for these prostitutes to help reduce the spread of sexually transmitted diseases.
