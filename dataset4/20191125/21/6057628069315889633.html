Garuruwa kusan 800 na fuskantar karancin abinci a Nijar

25 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Michael Kuduson

Rahotanni daga Jamhuriyar Nijar sun ce sakamakon damunar bana a Jihar Damagaram tazo da matsala, wanda ya shafi garuruwa 798 duk da taimakon gwamnati da na masu hannu da shuni.
Wannan matsala dai ta shafi akalla mutane 659,182 wadanda ya zama musu dole su nemi wasu hanyoyin samun abinci.
Daga Damagaram,ga rahoton Ibrahim Malam Tchillo.
