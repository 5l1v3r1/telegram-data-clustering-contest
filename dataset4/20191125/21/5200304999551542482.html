Star Gold UK to broadcast West Indies tour of India cricket in December

25 Nov 2019, 21:07 by Raj Baddhan

Star TV UK has confirmed the broadcast of West Indies cricket tour of India in December.

The tour, which consists of three Twenty20 matches and three One Day International matches, will kick off on Friday 6th December and conclude on Sunday 22nd December.

All matches will be broadcast live and exclusive on Star Gold UK, along with digital platform, Hotstar.

Star Gold UK has been broadcasting non-stop cricket this year with coverage of the Indian Premier League, followed by South Africa’s tour of India and Bangladesh’s tour of India. The broadcaster has promised even further cricket coverage in 2020.
