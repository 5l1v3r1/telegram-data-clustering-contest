Asim Riaz bares heart for fellow housemate Himanshi Khurana, promises to protect her on Bigg Boss show

25 Nov 2019, 19:11 by JKR Staff

If the Monday episode of Bigg Boss sought to recreate the on-screen chemistry of Siddharth Shukla and Rashami Desai, another blossoming relationship is also in the offing. What appears to be catching the attention of housemates and the audience is the blooming romance between Asim Riaz and Himanshi Khurana. Both Asim and Himanshi were seen discussing their developing love for each other.

The upcoming episode will see Himanshi and Asim express their feelings to each other. The promo shown by Colors TV has Himanshi saying to Asim, “Everyone is saying that you have strong feelings for me.” Asim seems to reply in the affirmative. Himanshi also reciprocates positively.

Earlier in the show on Monday night, Asim was seen comforting Himanshi after she had a brief altercation with Siddharth. Asim pulled her out of a group and walked alongside her by putting his arm around her shoulder. He told her, “Your heart is of gold. What to do! You’ve come to this show. You have me here. Asim means protector.”
