HBO Releases Trailer For Nick Saban, Bill Belichick Documentary

25 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Alek Arend

Nick Saban and Bill Belichick are two of football’s limited coaching legends. The two have mastered coaching, helping their teams have great success each and every season.

As a result of both coach’s successes, HBO will be airing a new documentary about the two individuals. It will certainly be a must-watch for all football fans, regardless of fanhood.

The 90-minute feature will be released Dec. 10th on HBO. Check out the newly released trailer below.

The two coaching legends will discuss their time together with the Cleveland Browns – and the struggles both faced during that time. It’s clear that the challenges both have been through have shaped who they are today.

Saban spent several seasons as the defensive coordinator for the Cleveland Browns when Belichick served as the team’s head coach. They clearly share a great respect for each other despite plenty of hardships.

It’ll be interesting to hear the two talk about their philosophies of life, coaching, football and what’s made them so successful today.

“There’s a lot of people that will take the challenge to climb the mountain,” Saban says in the trailer. “But when you get to the top of the mountain, you become the mountain.”

Check out the new documentary on Dec. 10 on HBO.
