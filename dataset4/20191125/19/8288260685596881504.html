'Going nowhere' - Wolves fans hit back after Nuno is tipped for Arsenal job

The Wanderers boss has emerged as the bookies' favourite to become the next Gunners manager

25 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Chris Watson

Wolverhampton Wanderers fans have been reacting to the news that Nuno Espirito Santo is the bookies' favourite to become the next Arsenal manager.

The Wolves chief has emerged as the early front runner to succeed under-pressure Unai Emery if the Spaniard is relieved of his duties.

After a run of two wins in 11 Premier League matches, Arsenal are eighth in the table, a point behind fifth-placed Wanderers.

And the Molineux faithful are desperate for Nuno to remain at the club.

Here is what some fans had to say about the situation on Twitter ...

Matt: Nuno isn't going anywhere, We signed better players then Arsenal have the last 2 years, with Fosun backed Jorge Mendes help, plus João Moutinho signing a New 3 Year Deal!  Sure he wouldn't of signed unless he knew Nuno was staying.

Neil Ford: Guo Guangchang is in the top 4 of wealthiest owners in the @premierleague #nuno won't be going anywhere.

Jack: While Nuno is a top manager, there's no way he's going to Arsenal. Wolves are developing in to a top English side & he'd be stupid to leave.

Jake Longworth: It was only a matter of time before a manager of Nuno’s calibre and class started attracting interest from the likes of Arsenal etc. They’d be mad not to consider luring him! I still reckon he’ll stay at Wolves though.
