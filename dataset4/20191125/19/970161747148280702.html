I'm a Celeb's Adele Robert's girlfriend posts sweet snap as she heads to Oz

25 Nov 2019, 19:56

Adele Roberts’ girlfriend Kate Holderness has begun her trek to join her other half in Australia as the I’m a Celebrity elimination days loom.

The actress and vlogger posted a sweet snap of her and Adele ahead of her trip over to the other side of the world, sharing her excitement at being reunited with her ‘queen’.

Alongside the picture, she wrote: ‘10,307 miles, I’m coming for ya!! Adventure starts tomorrow! Can’t WAIT to get on that bloody bridge & get my little Jungle Queen!!!!! #TeamAdele #ImACeleb’

Adele has been keeping viewers entertained over the past week as she grafts in with the Bushtucker Trials, which have included some gruesome eating trials.

Among the disgusting concoctions she’s taken on is pig uterus which left her feeling ill, and the gag-inducing vomit fruit.

It’s a far cry from her and Kate’s YouTube series Friday Night Fakeaway, where they recreate much-loved takeaway treats with a healthy twist.

She’s also been sharing her love of Jane McDonald with the nation,  something which, unknown to her, has been reciprocated by her idol back here in the UK.

Kate in the meantime, has been sharing insights into their adorable relationship – sharing anecdotes about where the love of Jane came from, to that time they almost had an awkward run-in with Adele’s new campmate, Kate Garraway.

But now she’s got a new means to fight for survival as the first elimination looms over the camp.

Currently, Caitlyn Jenner and Andy Whyment have got the bookies’ backings to win the series.

I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here continues nightly at 9pm on ITV.
