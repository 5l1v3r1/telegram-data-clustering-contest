Manager Buzz: Bucs decision could be coming soon

25 Nov 2019, 19:03

(Jim Rogash/Getty Images)

MLB.com is keeping track of all the latest news and rumors surrounding potential manager changes around the Majors.

Pirates aim to pick manager by Thanksgiving

Nov. 25: The Pirates have narrowed their managerial search to Twins bench coach Derek Shelton and Rays bench coach Matt Quatraro, according to a report from MLB Network insider Jon Heyman. The plan within the organization is to make a decision before Thanksgiving if possible, according to an additional report from MLB Network insider Joel Sherman. The Pirates are the only team that has yet to hire a manager for the 2020 season.

With Cherington in as GM, Pirates turn attention to skipper search

Nov. 15: The Pirates offered Ben Cherington their general manager job Friday, and he has accepted, a source told MLB.com's Adam Berry.

The Pittsburgh Post-Gazette's Jason Mackey and MLB Network insider Jon Heyman were first to report that the Bucs presented Cherington with the role.
