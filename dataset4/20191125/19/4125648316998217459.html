Video footage of massive explosions over eastern Aleppo

25 Nov 2019, 19:03 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (10:45 P.M.) – Heavy explosions were reported over the eastern countryside of the Aleppo Governorate this evening after unknown warplanes launched several airstrikes in the Al-Bab District.

According to the latest reports, at least a half dozen airstrikes targeted Al-Bab, while another three strikes targeted the Jarabulus District near the Turkish border.
