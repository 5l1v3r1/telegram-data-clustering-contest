Rivlin Meets in UK on Fund Initiatives in Israel

25 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Hana Levi Julian

President Rivlin has arrived in London, England where he is on a working visit at the invitation of the Portland Trust, where he is to participate in a meeting of heads of impact funds which advance projects and initiatives in Israel and the Middle East, including the Portland Trust and Yad HaNadiv.

At the meeting participants are to discuss a number of initiatives that are under consideration for the coming year, in the spirit of the ‘Four Tribes’ speech and in relation to social dynamics in Israel and the region, according to the president’s spokesperson.

The projects being presented are to focus on advancing shared existence, social diversity and confidence-building across society.

President Rivlin is slated to speak at the opening of the conference, as will Chair of Yad HaNadiv-The Rothschild Foundation Lord Jacob Rothschild, Chair of the Portland Trust Sir Ronald Cohen, CEO of the Portland Trust Douglas Krikler and Chair of UJIA Louise Jacobs.
