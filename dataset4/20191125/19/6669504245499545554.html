Figure skating heroes return to Kelowna for Stars On Ice

25 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Parker Crook

Some of the world's best figure skaters will grace the ice at Prospera Place in Kelowna in May.

The Stars On Ice tour, which began in the United States in 1986, will bring famed athletes who are set to compete in the upcoming World Figure Skating Championships to Kelowna May 8 at 7:30 p.m., according to a media release.

The 2020 starstudded tour lineup, comprised of Olympians, world champions and Canadian champions brings Kurt Browning, Elvis Stojko, Patrick Chan, Meryl Davis, Charlie White, Kaetlyn Osmond, Kaitlyn Weaver and Andrew Poje among others.

Tickets start at $27.50 and go on sale Dec. 6 at 10 a.m. with special on-ice seating and will be available through the Select Your Tickets website here.
