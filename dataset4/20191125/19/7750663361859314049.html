Three clubs keen on Wolverhampton Wanderers youngster?

Three high-profile European clubs are reportedly showing an interest in Wolverhampton Wanderers youngster Owen Otasowie.

25 Nov 2019, 19:05 by Darren Plant

While the club's senior team are currently impressing in the Premier League, every effort is being made to put together a strong youth squad at Molineux.

However, a consequence of focus being placed on the Under-23s and Under-18 setups is their hot prospects attracting plenty of attention.

According to The Sun, Arsenal, Liverpool and Paris Saint-Germain have become admirers of efender Otasowie.

The 18-year-old made an impression as Wolves recorded a 1-1 draw with PSG in a youth fixture held in the West Midlands last week.

Otasowie is currently due to be out of contract in the summer.
