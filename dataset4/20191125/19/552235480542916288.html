Kilmarnock firefighters deal with incident in wooded area

Emergency service crew were in Bellfield Estate, a largely wooded area with parks all around.

25 Nov 2019, 19:31 by Lochlin Highet, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/lochlin-highet/

Firefighters have been dealing with an incident this evening in Kilmarnock. 

Emergency service crew were in Bellfield Estate, a largely wooded area with parks all around. 

Firefighters were dealing with an incident and were in the area at around 6.30pm. 

The extent of the incident is not yet known, we will keep you updated on the Kilmarnock Standard Facebook page as we get more information.
