T.J. Brodie injury update: Calgary Flames defenseman could make return to lineup against Penguins

25 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Tommy McArdle

Eleven days after collapsing at practice, Calgary Flames defenseman T.J. Brodie wason the icewith the team at morning skateonMonday. The team activated himahead of their game against the Pittsburgh Penguins and according to Postmedia's Wes Gilbertson, he must be cleared by the medical staff before suiting up.

Sportsnet's Derek Wills reported that Brodie skated with Mark Giordano on the top defensive pair at the morning skateand looks ready to return to the lineup.
