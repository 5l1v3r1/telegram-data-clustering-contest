'Very surprised' - Aston Villa fans react as Jack Grealish returns for Newcastle United clash

Aston Villa v Newcastle | Dean Smith's men will be looking to arrest a run of form that has seen them lose their last three Premier League games

25 Nov 2019, 19:24 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Aston Villa fans have been having their say on Dean Smith's big selection calls for the game against Newcastle United.

Jack Grealish is finally back in a Villa shirt as the skipper makes his long-awaited return to match action against Newcastle.

Dean Smith, has brought Grealish straight back into the starting line-up with Trezeguet dropping out, despite scoring in the last two.

Elsewhere, Tom Heaton has shaken off a calf knock of his own - sustained during the 2-1 defeat to Liverpool - as the No.1 claims his starting shirt back.

Bjorn Engels misses out with Ezri Konsa again partnering Tyrone Mings at the hart of Villa's defence.

Conor Hourihane also starts in midfield alongside in-form John McGinn and Douglas Luiz, the latter tasked with protecting Villa's defence. Marvelous Nakamba surprisingly drops out.
