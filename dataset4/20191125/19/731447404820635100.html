DJs Pareja's new EP for Schalen is 'a manifesto for the club'

Tecno Inferno is out December 6th.

25 Nov 2019, 19:30 by Marissa Cetin

DJs Pareja's next EP is coming out on Schalen next month.

Out December 6th, Tecno Inferno "is a manifesto for the club," according to the Cologne-based imprint. It's Mariano Caloso and Diego Irasusta second four-track EP of the year, following The After—their first release in four years—on Honey Soundsystem.

Schalen launched in June by the team behind Magazine with an EP from cofounder Barnt.
