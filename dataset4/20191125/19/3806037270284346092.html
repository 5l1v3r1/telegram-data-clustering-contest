Gucci's Leather Ace Sneakers Get Starry Embroidered Update

Crafted in clean white leather.

25 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Nia Groce

Gucci has created several iterations of its classic Ace sneakers, often mixing up the signature side-stripes with new embroidered designs. While its familiar bee and snake imagery have often taken the spotlight on the shoes, this time around, the Italian label switches things up with a colorful three-star pattern and bold “Gucci Band” branding.

This release sees the Ace model crafted in white leather along the upper with a tonal rubber sole. The brand’s usual red and green house colors come into play on the Web stripes at the side panel and on the back of the heel, the latter of which has been printed with metallic gold logos. White laces round it all out to finish off the style with a streamlined touch.

Priced at $670 USD, Gucci’s White Ace Leather Sneakers are available now from retailers such as Browns.

For more from the brand, take a look at the seasonal “Beige Wool GG” edition of its Dublin sneakers.
