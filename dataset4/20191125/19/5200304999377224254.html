UK TV Reach: B4U Music jumps to No.3 behind Star Plus & Sony TV

25 Nov 2019, 19:48 by Raj Baddhan

While all Asian TV channels dropped below the 500k mark in weekly reach data, B4U Movies improved its position by taking the No.3 spot in overall numbers, putting it behind Star Plus and Sony TV. It was just ahead of Colors, with a reach of 336,000 viewers, according to data for week ending Sunday 17th November 2019.
