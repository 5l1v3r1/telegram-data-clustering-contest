‘I’ll Listen To Kelvyn Boy’s ‘Coffee’ Song To Chop My Woman’ – Pastor Reveals

25 Nov 2019, 19:18 by Ghbase New Writer

The Founder and General Overseer of Charismatic Anointing Ministries, Prophet Bernard Owusu Ansah has made a bizarre revelation (from the perspective of Christians) about the kind of songs he listens to.

He told blogger Zion Felix on his Uncut show that there is nothing wrong in listening to secular songs that will boost his appetite for sekz.

The Man of God said there are certain songs that enhance the relationship couples share in the bedroom and as a Pastor he relies heavily on such songs to make love to his woman.

He asserted that songs like Kelvyn Boy ‘Coffee’ is very good for ‘bedmatics’ and that’s why he won’t stop listening to it.

He stressed how good love songs are, and added that Jesus was a fan of love songs.

Though he claims to be selective when listening to secular songs, Pastor Bernard said Coffee will continue to be his favourite.
