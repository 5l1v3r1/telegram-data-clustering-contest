Gemma Collins demands Arg propose to her live on Good Morning Britain

She said she needs the "big bling on the finger"

25 Nov 2019, 14:29 by Rebecca Carter

Reality TV star Gemma Collins has urged on-off boyfriend James 'Arg' Argent to propose to her soon.

The Dancing On Ice star appeared on Monday's Good Morning Britain and was asked about her love life.

Gemma, 38, admitted she's waiting for Arg to pop the question but said she "can't wait much longer" and it's "either soon or I'm out of there".

Host Piers Morgan asked her: "How's your love life which I keep reading about. On-off, on-off, on-off. Is it on or off?"

Gemma replied: "It's good. It's very much on, Piers. James are you up out of bed yet?"

I can't wait much longer, it's either soon or I'm out of there.

Piers asked: "You are back on?" to which Gemma insisted: "Yes, well, yes. Last time I spoke to him we were."

Piers asked: "When was that?" as Gemma replied: "Last night but you never know, things could be different this morning... no I'm only joking. Everything is good, everything's good. I'm happy."

The presenter asked: "Any wedding bells? Has he ever asked you?" to which Gemma said: "Well, I'm waiting. No [he hasn't]."
