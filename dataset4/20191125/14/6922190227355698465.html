Entercom Communications Corp. Plans Quarterly Dividend of $0.02 (NYSE:ETM)

25 Nov 2019, 14:44 by Danessa Lincoln

Entercom Communications Corp. (NYSE:ETM) declared a quarterly dividend on Friday, November 15th, Zacks reports. Stockholders of record on Friday, November 29th will be paid a dividend of 0.02 per share on Monday, December 16th. This represents a $0.08 dividend on an annualized basis and a dividend yield of 1.78%. The ex-dividend date is Wednesday, November 27th.

Shares of NYSE ETM opened at $4.49 on Monday. The firm has a market cap of $639.83 million, a P/E ratio of 5.35 and a beta of 1.27. The company has a current ratio of 1.92, a quick ratio of 1.92 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.45. Entercom Communications has a 1-year low of $2.94 and a 1-year high of $7.94. The business’s 50 day moving average is $3.72 and its 200-day moving average is $4.74.
