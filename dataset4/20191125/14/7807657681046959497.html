Black Friday is upon us again, bringing the warm glow of unsustainable capitalism to every storefront, glass screen, and tech site on earth. Hurrah! Seriously, though, it's the best time of year to do gift shopping for loved ones ahead of Christmas, and also a great time to snipe some upgrades to your gaming experience.

Xbox consoles, accessories, and games are all going to be steeply discounted. There will be no better time to snag a deal than Black Friday 2019, and Microsoft has already begun the festivities with huge discounts and deals on dozens of Xbox games, products, and services.

Here's a range of the best early bird deals we've found so far, as well as some products that you should definitely keep an eye on as we head towards the big day.

Best budget option: Xbox One S All-Digital

$150 during Black Friday

Microsoft's Xbox One S All-Digital console is the cheapest option out there and will be just $150 during Black Friday. It drops the disc drive for additional savings, but retains the sleek aesthetic and is an excellent option for 1080p gaming.

  • $230 at Amazon
  • $250 at Microsoft Store

For newcomers: Xbox One S Starter Bundle

Up to $150 off during Black Friday

If you're new to the ecosystem, keep an eye on the price of the starter bundle, which is expected to gain anywhere up to $150 off (although it may be less on the day). The starter bundle comes with Xbox Game Pass, providing access to dozens upon dozens of games out of the box, as well as UHD movie playback.

$299 at Microsoft Store

4K power house: Xbox One X

Up to $150 off during Black Friday

Currently the world's most powerful console, the Xbox One X, is the ideal partner for anyone with a 4K HDR TV (or anyone planning to grab one in the Black Friday sales). UHD gaming and movies, the X could be anywhere up to $150 off during Black Friday.

  • $460 at Amazon
  • $500 at Microsoft Store

A sexier console: Gears 5 Limited Edition Xbox One X

Up to $150 off during Black Friday

If you're a big Gears 5 fan, there's every chance Microsoft may discount the stunning Limited Edition Xbox One X during Black Friday. The Gears 5 Xbox One X comes with unique sound effects, a vivid icy finish, and a custom controller.

  • $450 at Amazon
  • $500 at Microsoft Store

Best Xbox One Black Friday 2019 deals: Games

Microsoft already has a huge sale going on for games, which will last between now and November 25. Microsoft will also run a separate Black Friday sale on November 25 through to December 2. Here are some of the best games already discounted on the Microsoft Store, and a few more recent games that will likely get discounts during the Black Friday sale.

The Old West: Red Dead Redemption 2

Up to 50% off during Black Friday

This is arguably the best game on Xbox One, set towards the end of the "Wild West" era in a fictional county in the United States. During Black Friday, Red Dead Redemption 2 will be 50 percent cheaper.

  • $60 from Amazon (Disc)
  • $60 from Microsoft (Digital)

Editor's Pick: The Outer Worlds

Up to 25% off during Black Friday

The Outer Worlds is an incredible first-person RPG set in a declining colony on the fringes of frontier space. During Black Friday, the game will be up to 25 percent off, which is a great deal considering it only just came out.

  • $60 at Amazon (Physical)
  • $60 at Microsoft (Digital)

Leading RPG: The Witcher 3

Up to 75% off during Black Friday

The Witcher 3 is one of the best games on Xbox One, and it's due for a steep discount during Black Friday. Set in a fantasy world based on novels of the same name, The Witcher series is full of political intrigue, mythical beasts, and epic battles.

  • $50 at Amazon (Disc)
  • $50 at Microsoft (Digital)

Best Competitive Shooter: Rainbow Six Siege

Up to 70% off during Black Friday

Rainbow Six Siege is an industry-leading tactical shooter, and it will enjoy some steep discounts to all versions during the Black Friday festivities. Few games have been supported as well as this one post-launch in terms of DLC, so be sure to give it a look.

  • From $20 at Amazon (Disc)
  • From $40 at Microsoft (Digital)

Our favorite wireless headset
