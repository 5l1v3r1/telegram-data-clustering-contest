HTET answer key 2019: Last date to file objections extended

HTET answer key 2019: The result will be based on the final answer key. The final answer key will be released after considering all the objections, as per the standard procedure.

25 Nov 2019, 14:23 by Careers Desk

HTET answer key 2019: The Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE) has extended the last date of raising objections against the Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test (HTET) from November 25 to November 27, 2019. Candidates can file objections against HTET answer key from the official website – bseh.org.in or htetonline.com.

Once the link for raising the objections against the answer keys is closed, the board will release the final answer keys after considering all the objections. The result will be based on the final answer key.

HTET answer key 2019: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website, htetonline.nic.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link ‘answer key’
Step 3: A PDF will open, check the answer key

To qualify HTET, the candidates need to have at least 90 marks or 60 per cent of the total marks. The reserved category candidates will have to score a total of 55 per cent or 82 marks, according to the rules. The candidates who want to teach in classes 1 to 5 will have to qualify paper I while those who clear the trained graduate teacher (TGT) will be eligible to teach up to class 12.

As many as 2,83,878 lakh candidates have applied for the HTET of which 1.77 lakh appeared for the exams. Of the total, 99621 candidates appeared for level 2 of the examination while 78,334 appeared for level 1.

The HTET was conducted on November 17, 18 and 19, 2019 in various centres across the state. Earlier, the candidates were allowed to raise objections, if any in the answer key from November 21 to 25, 2019.  The deadline, however, has now been extended.
