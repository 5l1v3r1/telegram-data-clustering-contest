Victoria Beckham says her son used her to get more social media followers

"He used me!"

25 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Sarah Magliocco

Victoria Beckham has revealed that one of her children used her fame to garner more social media clout.

Speaking on The Ellen DeGeneres show, Victoria explained that 17-year-old son Romeo recently joined teen video app TikTok, and wanted to launch his profile with a band,

Victoria said that her son asked if she would do a dance with him for the app.

“What should we dance to? He said ‘the Spice Girls,'” Victoria said in the clip.

“And then he took my phone, he posted it on my Instagram and said ‘That’ll get my numbers up’… He used me!”

The pair danced to Spice Up Your Life in the kitchen of their home.
