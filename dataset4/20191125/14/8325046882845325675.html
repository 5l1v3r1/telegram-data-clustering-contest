Here are all the 2019 American Music Awards winners

25 Nov 2019, 14:33 by Callie Ahlgrim
  • The 47th annual American Music Awards aired live on Sunday.
  • Post Malone was the most-nominated artist this year with seven nods, but only came away with one award for favorite rap/hip-hop album.
  • Taylor Swift was honored as the artist of the decade, but also swept all five of her nominations and performed a medley of her beloved songs.
  • Visit Insider’s homepage for more stories.

The 47th annual American Music Awards, referred to by it organizers as “the world’s biggest fan-voted award show,” aired live on Sunday from the Microsoft Theater in LA.

Post Malone was the most-nominated artist this year with seven nods, but only came away with one award. Conversely, not only was Taylor Swift honored as the artist of the decade, she swept all five of her nominations and performed a medley of her beloved songs.
