Housefull 4 Cast Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Sanon Dance To Panipat Track Mann Mein Shiva-VIDEO

25 Nov 2019, 14:12 by Lubna Khan

Housefull 4 cast Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Sanon, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde, Kriti Kharbanda came together for a success party and were seen dancing to Mann Mein Shiva song from Panipat, which also stars Kriti Sanon. Take a look at the video

Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Sanon, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde, Kriti Kharbanda are currently basking in the success of their latest film Housefull 4. The film had released on Diwali and performed well at the box-office, having crossed 200 crores. To celebrate the same, the entire team came together for a success party and were seen dancing to Mann Mein Shiva song from Panipat, which also stars Kriti Sanon. The team promoted their co-star Kriti Sanon’s upcoming film and going by the video, they had a blast as they danced together.

Mann Mein Shiva is the latest track from Arjun Kapoor starrer Panipat and it released just two days ago. The track originally features Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon enjoying their victory with their family members as well as the Praja. Now, recreating the hook step, the entire Housefull 4 team and Pulkit Samrat too rocked to the victory song. Panipat stars Arjun Kapoor, Kriti Sanon and Sanjay Dutt in important roles and narrates the story of the  great Battle Of Panipat which was fought between the Marathas and Ahmad Shah Abdali. Helmed by Ashutosh Gowariker, the period-drama also stars Mohnish Bahl as Nana Saheb Peshwa, Padmini Kolhapure as Gopika Bai, Zeenat Aman as Sakina Begum, Sahil Salathia as Shamsher Bahadur and many more. Panipat is set to release on December 6, 2019.
