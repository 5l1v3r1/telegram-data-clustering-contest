Spurs stars went to Pochettino's house to console sacked boss

25 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Buzz Contributor

Several Tottenham players said their goodbyes to Mauricio Pochettino at the Argentine's house after he was axed as manager last week.

The north London club severed ties with the 47-year-old after five years at the helm following a poor run of results that dates back to January.

A breakdown in relations between Pochettino and Tottenham chiefs was clear, but the timing of the decision still bought about plenty of shock.

Harry Kane, who was a major beneficiary of Pochettino's tenure, went to console his former mentor and concedes the club have asked to keep quiet about how he was informed, reports The Mirror.

“They’ve asked me not to talk about it," said the Tottenham marksman.

But he added: “I went round his house and caught up with him the next day.

“It was a shock for everyone, so I wanted to go and see him and we had a chat for a couple of hours and it was nice to do that before the new manager came in.”

Eric Dier, who had lost his way under Pochettino but is expected to be a favourite under Jose Mourinho, also spoke to the Argentine following his dismissal.

But he insists, while emotion was present, the players had to remain focused ahead of the weekend's action.

He said: “We found out that night [through the club]. And obviously it was quite emotional.

“It was important for everyone to be able to go to see him. I spoke with him and it was good to be able to do that.

“It’s been a week I’ve never really had in my career before. A big shock on Tuesday night for everyone, the players included.

“Then it was a quick turnaround - all of a sudden we got a new manager, one of the best managers there has been in the game.

“So you have to automatically have to turn your head towards that and focus on the game. Obviously, a lot of emotion, a lot of energy, but since the gaffer came in it was all about: ‘Just prepare for Saturday, get yourselves ready, you don’t have to impress me on days one and two, just run about and waste your energy.’

“So it was about going out there and performing and thankfully we got the win.”
