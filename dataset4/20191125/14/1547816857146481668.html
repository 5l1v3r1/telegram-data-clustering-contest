Kylie Jenner just shared a picture of herself and Kris Jenner twinning in black outfits and sipping on wine, even posing the same way! Calling it a mood, Kylie asked her fans to caption the picture, and received some really interesting captions

Kylie Jenner, the youngest self-made billionaire, has a huge following on social media. She has 151 million followers on Instagram alone, and fans go gaga over every picture that she posts. Her Instagram posts range from her vacation pictures to promoting her makeup collection, to sharing pictures with her daughter. She definitely knows how to get her fans’ attention and sets her Insta on fire with some of these posts. Now, the makeup mogul has shared a picture with her mom Kris Jenner and sought her fans’ help to caption the picture.

Kylie Jenner took to Instagram and shared a picture of herself and Kris Jenner twinning in black outfits and sipping on wine, even posing the same way! Calling it a mood, Kylie asked her fans to caption the picture, and received some really interesting captions. She wrote, “mood. caption this”. Fans were quick to respond and some of the replies have us laughing out loud! While one fan suggested, “us after any slight inconvenience,” another fan wrote, “The girl you both hate enters the room.” Others captioned it based on their similar looks and one comment read, “Like mother, like daughter!
