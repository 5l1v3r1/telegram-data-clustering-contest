Arizona police officers save kittens doused with gasoline

25 Nov 2019, 14:05 by Bob D’Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

PHOENIX — A pair of Arizona police officers helped rescue two kittens that were soaked with gasoline and thrown from a plastic bag, authorities said.

The two members of the Phoenix Police Department found the kittens and took them to the Arizona Humane Society for care, the Arizona Republic reported.
