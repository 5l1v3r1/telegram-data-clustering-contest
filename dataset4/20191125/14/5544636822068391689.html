Over 20 Million People Ready To Die For Me- CDQ

25 Nov 2019, 14:52 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Indigenous rapper, Sodiq Yusuf aka CDQ has taken to his social media handle to boast that there are over twenty millions of people who are willing to die for him.

Concise News reports this comes after CDQ got series of congratulatory messages for having over two million followers on Instagram.

The rapper said he was not freaked about having such number of followers, as their are many more people willing to lay down lives for him in the real world.

CDQ took to his Instagram page to write “Everyone be sending me congratulatory messages over “MILLION followers on IG but sorry i’m not freaky about that that.

“Let’s fall out to the real world and you know I’ve got over 20 million people out there ready to die for CDQ anytime any-day. Woss #Masun.”
