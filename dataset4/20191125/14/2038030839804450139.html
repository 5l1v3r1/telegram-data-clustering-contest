Reports: R Kelly's girlfriend Joycelyn Savage makes a series of serious allegations against incarcerated singer

25 Nov 2019, 14:10

Cape Town - Joycelyn Savage, R. Kelly's alleged live-in girlfriend has reportedly gone on to make even more bombshell claims against him in a reported post on Patreon according to The Chicago Sun-Times.

The embattled artist has been in jail since July, following his arrest on charges including child pornography and enticement of a minor.

According to the Chicago publication, Joycelyn writes in her post: "I realised I was pregnant by this monster ... Eventually, I ended up getting an abortion I was forced to get the surgery done at his house."
