Legends of Tomorrow's Season 5 Picks Up in the Wake of Crisis

25 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Colin Hickson

With DC's Legends of Tomorrow's Season 5 premiere airing after a special episode that ties into the upcoming "Crisis on Infinite Earths" crossover, fans might be wondering when exactly the season takes place. However, according to "Crisis" showrunner Marc Guggenheim, the premiere, as well as the following six episodes of the season, all take place before the event.

On Twitter, a fan asked Guggenheim about whether the first seven episodes take place before or after "Crisis," to which he replied, "Before. It will be very clear."
