Carer found dead in front yard 'was savaged by wild hogs'

25 Nov 2019, 14:09

Police say a carer found dead on a doorstep is feared to have been savaged by wild hogs.

Christine Rollins, 59, was found dead outside the home of an elderly couple she cares for in Anahuac, Texas on Sunday.

The Chambers County Sheriff’s Office said that Rollins appeared to have been attacked by the animals.
