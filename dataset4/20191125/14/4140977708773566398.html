PMCO Global Finals Date 2019 and Teams: PMCO Fall Split 2019 Grand finals schedule and timings as final 16 teams are ready for the showdown.

With the end of the PMCO Fall Split 2019 Global prelims, the final 16 teams are disclosed for the Grand finals stage scheduled to kick off on 29th November till December 1st.

The tournament will span over 18 rounds and 6 rounds will be played on each day of the tournament. Earlier, 13 teams gained direct qualification to the finals from the regional qualifiers played and last three spots were left for the teams participating in prelims.

Timing for PMCO Fall SPlit Grand finals will be same as Prelims. First Round Will Start at 1:30 pm IST and last round will start at 6:30 pm IST

With the completion of the preliminary round, Mega Esports, Bigetron RA and Orange Esports have booked their places in the Grand Finals of the tournament.

TeamIND was close to gain a qualification but in the end, they succumbed to the pressure mounted on them in the last rounds, and they ended up at the 8th place by the end of the prelims.

If TeamIND had gained a qualification, there would have been three teams in the grand finals of the PMCO, which could have bolstered Indian contingent in the grand finals.

Nevertheless, a decent performance by TeamIND who won two rounds in the competition. Meanwhile, SynerGE and Team INS were having troubles for the most part of the competition but showed their skills in the last rounds.

This leaves Team Soul and Entity Gaming from India to vie for the title in the Grand finals. Amidst all of this let’s look at the details ahead of the Global finals of PMCO.
