Cowboys Reportedly Make Decision On Potential Staff Changes After Loss To Patriots

25 Nov 2019, 14:59 by Matt Lombardi

The Dallas Cowboys lost to the New England Patriots on the road on Sunday, falling to 6-5 on the season. In the aftermath, many have been wondering if the team will make any coaching changes – especially since team owner Jerry Jones expressed his frustration after the game.

According to ESPN’s Ed Werder, the Cowboys will not be making any changes just yet. Werder reports that there will be no “immediate” changes to the staff – including on special teams.
