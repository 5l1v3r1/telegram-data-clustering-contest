Exclusive: Peyton Gilliland premieres 'Drag Me Under' music video 

R&B singer-songwriter Peyton Gilliland premieres her new music video for "Drag Me Under" exclusively on Digital Journal.

25 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Markos Papadatos

A rising teen artist, Gilliland co-wrote the song with songwriters Craig Wilson and John Salaway. The song "Drag Me Under" is about finally becoming self-aware in a bad relationship and realizing that the other person is drowning you with all the lies and self-doubt that follow; moreover, "Drag Me Under" is about determining one's own self-worth and realizing one deserves better.

"I've had this vision in my head for the video since we first wrote 'Drag Me Under'," she told Digital Journal. "While the song is intense and numbing, I knew I wanted to capture that through the visuals in the video," she explained.
