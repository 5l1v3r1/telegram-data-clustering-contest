10 sites that have ALREADY started their Black Friday sale

A complete round up of sites who have started their Black Friday sales

25 Nov 2019, 14:25 by Clodagh Meaney

While Black Friday isn’t technically until, well, Friday, some sites have already started their sales.

The annual event which takes place at the end of November every year sees stores kick start the Christmas shopping season by offering customers some epic offers and discounts.

While some stores started last week, even more have begun to drop their prices in the lead up to the sale event.

Boots.ie

With epic deals on beauty, fragrance and health, Boots.ie has got to be your first port of call for some bargains.

There are great reductions and deals on brands such as Real Techniques, Benefit and even Fenty.

Littlewoods Ireland

Online retailer Littlewoods is the home to big brands galore.

Here you’ll find savings on everything from shoes and clothes to watches, beds and appliances.

Easons

Book retailer, Easons are offering amazing savings this Black Friday.

For 30% off almost everything from Easons, simply pop the promo code “BLACK” in at check out.

Euronics

The Euronics Black Friday sale has up to 50% off TVs, up to 30% off laptops and up to 25% off tablets, and phones.

Not to mention they also have everything you need from fridges and cookers to fit bits and headphones.

Smyths Toys

If you’re shopping for little ones, Smyth’s Toys Black Friday sale is already in full swing.

Available online and instore, there are savings on everything for toddlers right up to teens.

Boohoo

While it’s not quite a fully-fledged Black Friday sale yet, Boohoo have launched their warm-up sale.

We have to say, if those savings are just a warm-up, we know that Friday’s discounts are going to be incredible.

Harvey Norman

Promising to match all their competitor’s prices, Harvey Norman’s Black Friday sale is a great place whether you’re looking for a present or something for yourself.

With everything from homewares, to kitchen appliances and electricals, Harvey Norman is a great place for some bargain shopping.

Debenhams

The fabulous My NY perfume by DKNY is just one of many fragrances on sale in Debenhams, this Black Friday.

Other brands with huge discounts include YSL, Estee Lauder and Dior.

D.I.D

Another fantastic spot for sorting your personal, and gifting needs, D.I.D is the perfect spot for bargain hunters.

These Toshiba Bluetooth headphones are only €19.99, saving you €40.

So Sue by SJ

This stunning Dripping Gold set is just one of the many bargains you can bag from So Sue by SJ this Black Friday.

The beauty brand also have huge savings on brush sets, and we’re sure the discounts will continue as the week goes on.
