I'm A Celebrity: Jacqueline Jossa fears she'll 'break' if public keep voting for her to do the trials

Husband Dan asks fans to stop voting for her

25 Nov 2019, 14:37 by Nancy Brown

Jacqueline Jossa fears she will "break" in the I'm A Celebrity jungle if the public keep voting for her to do the show's Bushtucker Trials.

After being voted to take part in yet another trial, Jacqueline headed into the Bush Telegraph to pour her heart out.

She said: "You worry and think why are people voting for me, there’s only so much you can do before you do break."

She added: "I hope I don’t break. I hope I don’t break."
