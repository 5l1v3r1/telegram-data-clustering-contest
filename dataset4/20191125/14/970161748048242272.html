Courtney Act, Dick and Dom dream dinner guests in Christmas Come Dine With Me

25 Nov 2019, 14:48

Celebrity Christmas Come Dine With Me has the weirdest line up in store for us this year.

Drag Race star Courtney Act, Strictly Come Dancing finalist Debbie McGee, and children’s TV presenters Richard McCourt and Dominic Wood – better known as Dick and Dom – are going to rustle up dinner for each other this Christmas.

They will be joined by TV personality Charlotte Dawson, daughter of comedian Les Dawson, and internet entertainer Stevo The Madman.

Like any other CDWM, the celeb version will run for five days from 16 December to 20 December on E4 to get you in the festive mood.

The random dinner guests will display their culinary skills, or lack of, to each other for a good cause.

Courtney is hoping to whip up a pink themed vegan menu, Debbie is going to go posh for her black tie evening with some pretty special entertainment – a professional ballroom dance demonstration inside a massive snow globe. Fingers crossed for an appearance from her Strictly partner Giovanni Pernice dropping in to lend a hand.

We’re also fully expecting Dick and Dom’s evening to be as chaotic as their hilarious TV show In Da Bungalow.

It will launch the new series of Celebrity Come Dine With Me that will air throughout 2020.

Simone Haywood, executive producer of Celebrity Come Dine With Me said: ‘We are delighted to be able to take our successful format to a brand new audience on E4, as part of our ever-growing slate of productions across the country and across every genre.

‘Come Dine With Me has always been hugely popular with younger audiences and we’re excited to give it a fresh new twist with our Celebrity Specials.’

Celebrity Christmas Come Dine With Me starts 16 December on Channel 4.
