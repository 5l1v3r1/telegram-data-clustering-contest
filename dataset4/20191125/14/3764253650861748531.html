Lionel Messi spotted playing with his kid in adorable video

25 Nov 2019, 14:24

Lionel Messi has over the decade proved his amazing superhuman on the pitch to the joy of football fans but nothing warms hearts than the Barcelona star's fatherhood experience.

The Argentine football captain was recently spotted playing with his child Ciro and that experience shows how lenient Messi can be with kids.

In a short clip shared by his wife Antonella Roccuzzo, the 32-year-old was seen playing with Ciro in what appears to be a soft duel between a loving father and son.
