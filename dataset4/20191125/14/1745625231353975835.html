Shania Twain & Taylor Swift Look ‘Pretty In Pink’ Together At The 2019 American Music Awards

Fabio Magnocavallo

Shania Twain and Taylor Swift were two of many huge names that attended the American Music Awards last night in Los Angeles, California, at the Microsoft Theater and posed for a photo together at the ceremony. Twain shared a shot of the duo to her Instagram account where they both rocked pink ensembles.

Swift owned a glittery gold garment which she paired a sheer pink cape over the top. The “We Are Never Getting Back Together” hitmaker sported her blond hair behind her ears and applied a red lip. Shania, on the other hand, also stunned in a pink cape-style dress with fishnet tights. She owned her curly hair down and appeared to be very happy to be in the photo with Taylor.

For her caption, Twain described the photo as “pretty in pink,” attaching a red heart emoji.

In the span of six hours, her post racked up more than 42,000 likes and over 660 comments, proving to be popular with her 1 million followers.

“Diamond selling crossover legends,” one user wrote.

“You are such a Queen Shania Twain,” another shared.

“TWO ICONIC QUEENS, LETS GO GIRLS,” a third fan remarked passionately in capital letters.

“Wow!!! It only seems like yesterday that you were the young ingenue, and now you’re an inspiration for our future stars. The circle of life indeed,” a fourth follower commented.

On the night, Twain performed a medley of her greatest hits — “Man! I Feel Like A Woman,” “Any Man Of Mine,” “You’re Still The One,” and “That Don’t Impress Me Much” — and covered Taylor Swift’s “Shake It Off,” Twenty One Pilots’ “Stressed Out,” Drake’s “God’s Plan,” and Post Malone’s “Rockstar,” per the Daily Mail.

As previously reported by The Inquisitr, Taylor also performed as she was honored with the Artist of the Decade title. She was joined on stage by “Without Me” chart-topper Halsey and “Crying in the Club” songstress Camila Cabello.

According to Billboard, Swift also picked up trophies for Artist of the Year, Favorite Artist – Adult Contemporary, Favorite Female Artist – Pop/Rock, Favorite Music Video for “You Need To Calm Down.”

At next year’s Grammy Awards, Swift might pick up a couple more awards. According to the BBC, the “Love Story” singer is up for Best Pop Solo Performance for “You Need To Calm Down” and Song of the Year for “Lover.” The 62nd annual ceremony will be hosted by Alicia Keys again and will take place on January 26 at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, California, and will air live on CBS.
