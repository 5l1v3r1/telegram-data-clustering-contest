OAP Dotun source of income questioned as he acquires new house

25 Nov 2019, 07:52 by Sanya Agunbiade

Popular Nigerian OAP Dotun of Cool FM’s source of income was questioned by a troll after he announced he just acquired a new house in Lagos.

The vibrant OAP had taken to his Instagram page to make the announcement, while at the same time trying to motivate his followers with some inspiring quotes.

After sharing the photo, he captioned it

“#BeInspired .. Exalt GOD…I mean it…Exalt his holy name always & work super HARD!”

Reacting to the good news, his celebrity friends wrote on the post:

” Congratulations, you have done well! ” Daddy Freeze wrote.

” Wow wow wow!!!!! Congratulations brother ” Yomi Casual wrote.

” Congrats bro 🙌🙌🙌 2 much thanks for the tour of the beautiful spot” Sound Sultan wrote

But as expected, a certain troll was not convinced he got he house with proceeds from his OAP job. Well, the OAP did not waste time in putting the unbeliever in his place.
