Spoilers: Terrifying illness relapse for Lisa in EastEnders?

25 Nov 2019, 07:00

It’s been an incredibly traumatic few months for Lisa Fowler (Lucy Benjamin), who returned to EastEnders earlier this year to acquire the help of Mel Owen (Tamzin Outhwaite) in getting Louise Mitchell (Tilly Keeper) and Keanu Taylor (Danny Walters) out of London. When her plan went belly-up, she decided to stay put in Walford — and has found herself embroiled in plenty of drama ever since — but is she about to have a terrifying illness relapse?

Between clashing with Phil (Steve McFadden) over the part she played in harbouring Keanu and Louise, to helping Mel with her plan to get Hunter (Charlie Winter) safely out of the country, it’s been an incredibly stressful time for Lisa. Factor in almost losing her daughter — and grandchild — in the siege, and it’s no wonder she was intent on getting out of Walford.

Following Hunter’s death, Lisa went out of her way to support Mel, and the pair planned on moving away to Portugal together. However, after Mel discovered Sharon’s (Letitia Dean) secret affair with Keanu, her primary objective became revenge — something which resulted in plenty of conflict between the two best friends.
