Fans brand I'm a Celebrity live trial 'impossible' after James and Roman fail to win last three stars

It means that Ian, Cliff and Andy miss out on a Sunday roast dinner

25 Nov 2019, 07:25 by Cathy Owen

Fans of I'm A Celebrity are outraged after the live trial, claiming it had been made impossible on purpose to create divisions in the camp.

The trial, based on another of ITV's reality smashes Love Island, saw the celebrities take on a number of challenges, which included downing blended vomit fruit and finding a star in a pot of mealworms using only the mouth, to win a roast dinner for each of the contestants.

Earlier there had been tensions as they had to decide in which order they were going to get their meals.

Former rugby player James Haskell and DJ Roman Kemp were the last two up for a challenge but they failed to win their stars, Ian Wright, Cliff Parisi and Andy Whyment would all have to make do with some rice.

James and Roman had to reach into boxes filled with all sorts of critters and unscrew their stars - but they didn't get them in time.

When Ant and Dec asked James what had gone wrong, he gave a very sassy reply: "The very fact that there was a giant crab in the way that you wanted me to carefully caress in a different direction while I was trying to unscrew a star in its face."

Dec mocked: "Alright, somebody's getting a bit prickly!"

"The competitive spirit!" James laughed.

But fans of the show were not happy about the outcome saying it had been impossible from the start.
