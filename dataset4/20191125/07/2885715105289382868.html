Baashha to re-release in select theaters on December 11

Also starring Nagma, Janagaraj, Vijayakumar and Raghuvaran, Baashha was originally released in January 1995.

25 Nov 2019, 07:28 by Entertainment Desk

In 2017, the makers of Baashha had digitally remastered the popular Rajinikanth-starrer, releasing it in 80 theaters across Tamil Nadu. After two years, here is a chance for Thalaivar fans to revisit the film.

The Suresh Krissna-directorial will be re-released on December 11 ahead of Rajinikanth’s birthday. The official handle of Sathya Movies revealed Baashaa will be released in select cities worldwide.
