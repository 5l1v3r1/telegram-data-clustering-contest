Fans can't get over Bhuvan Bam's uncanny resemblance to Alia Bhatt

25 Nov 2019, 07:58 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

We’ve seen several doppelgangers of Bollywood stars, but Alia Bhatt's lookalike takes the cake. Comedian Bhuvan Bam shared an old photograph of himself which went viral on social media for his "uncanny" resemblance to his "crush".

The comedian shared the photo and asked Alia out on a coffee date saying, "@aliaa08 Please let's go for a coffee date because I've started looking like my crush!"
