I'm A Celeb's James Haskell loses his cool with Ant and Dec

25 Nov 2019, 07:38

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! contestant James Haskell was a little antsy after undertaking an ‘impossible’ Bushtucker Trial with Roman Kemp and snapped at Ant and Dec.

In scenes that aired last night, the former rugby player and the radio presenter attempted a Love Island-themed task to win themselves a roast dinner, however, it all proved to be a bit too difficult with audiences demanding justice for the duo.

James and Roman had to unscrew stars from one side of a bed and re-screw them on the other side with just 90 seconds to get all three moved over.

Oh, and if that wasn’t enough, they also had snakes lying beside them and each star was in a box filled with critters, including a snappy crab which caused problems for James.

After the buzzer sounded, James seemed to be irritated by his defeat, causing Dec to ask: ‘That was unlucky. What was it, James?’

‘Well, what was it?’ James replied. ‘Maybe the fact there was a giant crab in the way which you wanted me to carefully caress in a different direction while I was trying to undo a star in his face.’

‘Alright, somebody’s getting a bit prickly!’ Dec gasped, making eyes at Ant.

James laughed: ‘Competitive spirit!

‘It was difficult and the crab wouldn’t get out the way and you had to unscrew it at the top. The cockroaches were a little bit easier but getting it back down with those crabs…’
