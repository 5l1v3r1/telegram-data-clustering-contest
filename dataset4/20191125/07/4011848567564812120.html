Thanksgiving Chaos: How to Prevent Holiday Headaches Using Apps

25 Nov 2019, 07:57 by Jamie P.

Being the 'host' for this year's Thanksgiving is quite challenging—it may give headaches. From cooking humongous set of meals for your long-lost friends and relatives to talking with them since you are the 'host' for this year's Thanksgiving—the effort to make it a perfect and flawless holiday is quite a huge responsibility. Luckily, there are mobile applications that you can easily download to help you prepare for this year's Thanksgiving Day. 

Useful Apps to Survive Thanksgiving

1. Boxed

Let's get stuck in a box. Boxed app is one of the handiest Thanksgiving apps you can ever imagine. 

Another thing that hinders everyone in making a smooth Holiday is the shopping day. As Thanksgiving is just a few days away, you do not want to be stuck in terrible holiday traffic while shopping for your recipes. This is where Boxed app enters the room. 

Create that account and push that add-to-cart button as items from your recipe list can be delivered right in front of your home. Settle in and have some beer while waiting for your package to make you stress-free even before the holidays. 

2. Google Nest Hub Max

Having your own assistant while preparing for Thanksgiving is like having a genie in a bottle. Sounds unreal? Not until Google Nest Hub Max grants your wishes in no time. Make your Google Assistant part of your Thanksgiving Day with this application that vows to answer each of your questions—in terms of cooking, handling all your calls, and watching videos for that crispy and aromatic Turkey of yours. 

3. Food Network Kitchen

No one wants to be recognized as 'the worst chef of the last year's Thanksgiving Day.' That is why having new recipes for your Thanksgiving Day through Food Network Kitchen is what you must do before the event. 

From classic Turkey recipes to your most famous cranberries, Food Network Kitchen app will help you reach that five-star hotel like-recipes for your guests. Win their hearts with your meals as you win this app in Google play store. 

4. Multi-Timer Stop Watch

Do not aim to stop the Thanksgiving Day but aim to download this Multi-Timer Stop Watch to have each of your meals on time. You do not want your guests to wait for a long time to eat your specialties since this app is a match for your simultaneous cooking. 

Throw that old-timer of yours and use technology with the multi-timer stop watch—perfect to time your Turkey while cooking other meals in your menu. 

5. TaskRabbit

Though we do appreciate our Moms taking control of everything we need when Thanksgiving comes, seeking help from other people is also an option. This is what TaskRabbit offers to you. Pay people from TaskRabbit willing to cook, to clean, and even to shop for everything you need for the holidays. 

They will be thankful for you as you thanked them for their services. 

Enjoy this Thanksgiving Day and be the 'best host for this year's holiday.'
