Gh ladies will go after my man if I flaunt him on social media – Emelia Brobbey

25 Nov 2019, 07:01 by Adu Sarpeah

Lately, young guys and ladies in relationships take delight in flaunting their partners on social for obvious reasons.

To some, displaying your lover on social media is a mark of showing how much you love your partner, but to others, hell no. Thus, never will they flaunt their partners because doing so will make them lose them, the perception of Emelia Brobbbey.

To the best knowledge of the above-mentioned Kumawood actress, she is not ready to show off her man on social media because of the fear that other Ghanaian ladies might go after him.

“I will not say whether I am dating or not because the two have its own consequences but I am not searching,” she said in an interview with Sammy Kay.
