Post Malone Brought Southern Style to the AMAs in a Checkered Suit and Classy Bolo Tie

25 Nov 2019, 07:25 by Chanel Vargas

Post Malone fans know that the "Take What You Want" singer cleans up nicely, and his red carpet look at the 2019 American Music Awards is proof. Post showed up in a checkered black-and-white suit paired with a silver bolo tie and a matching black-and-white cowboy hat patterned with barbed wire. From head to toe, the look was a sophisticated spin on Post's unique style, and we are loving it. Check out his suit from all angles ahead.
