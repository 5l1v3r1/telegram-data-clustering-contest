Lewis Hamilton leads BBC Sports Personality of the Year nominees

25 Nov 2019, 23:25

Lewis Hamilton and Raheem Sterling lead this list year’s BBC Sports Personality of the Year nominees following a tremendous year of achievements.

Formula One racer Lewis was among the six shortlisted names announced on Monday after a stellar year, which saw him win his sixth world title, placing him just one title behind the legendary Michael Schumacher.

It could be the second time Lewis wins the coveted award after scooping the honour for the first time back in 2014.

Raheem, 24, has been nominated for his work with Manchester City, who claimed a treble this year winning the Premier League, FA Cup and the Carabao Cup. In personal news, the footballer secretly became a father for the third time with his girlfriend, Paige Milian, sharing the happy news of their 11-month-old son Thai-Cruz’s arrival last week.

Elsewhere on the nominations list, athletics star Katarina Johnson Thompson was recognised after winning gold in the heptathlon at the World Championships in Doha where she set the British record.

Popstar Ed Sheeran earlier announced cricketer Ben Stokes as a shortlisted name while rapper Stormzy did the honours of revealing Dina Asher Smith as another nominee. Welsh rugby player Alun Wyn Jones is also up for the prestigious award.

It comes hours after Lewis, 34, spoke about the positive impact he wanted to have on the younger generation, following in the footsteps of his hero Ayrton Senna.

‘I am trying to have a positive impact,’ Lewis told reporters in Monaco according to PA Media.

‘I looked at Ayrton Senna. He was a star in the sky and I want to somehow get up there.

‘It is important to find people like that and that is what I am trying to be.

‘If you look at young kids they are pure spirits. We are not born to hate or have negativity in our hearts.

‘That is something we learn over time. It is passed to us by our parents, and whatever issues they have going on, and through people you interact with.

‘It is always really important to stay true to your core values, and I am really fortunate that I have this amazing platform, that I never thought I would have. I am trying to be the best person I can.’

BBC Sports Personality of the Year nominees

Ben Stokes

Dina Asher-Smith

Raheem Sterling

Alun Wyn Jones

Lewis Hamilton

Katarina Johnson-Thompson

The BBC Sports Personality of the Year awards are set to take place at the P&J Live in Aberdeen on 15 December. The ceremony airs on BBC One at 7pm.
