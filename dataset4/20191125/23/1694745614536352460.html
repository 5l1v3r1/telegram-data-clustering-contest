Heads Up, Parents — There’s an Elf on the Shelf Advent Calendar, and It Comes With 27 Ornaments

25 Nov 2019, 23:15 by Rebecca Brown

If Elf on the Shelf is the type of thing that takes over your free time every December, we don't necessarily have a way for you to get out of the commitment — you have to move Buddy! — but we did just find something that might take some of the pressure off you to get super creative: an advent calendar dedicated to the guy himself!

The Elf on the Shelf Orna-POP! Advent Countdown ($15) comes with 27 mini mylar ornaments with fun prompts and jokes written on them for the kids, plus they can pop each one open for a surprise! It's truly fun for the whole family this holiday season.
