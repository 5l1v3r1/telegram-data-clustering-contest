Kirk Herbstreit Reveals His Top 5 Teams Of Week 13

25 Nov 2019, 23:54 by Alek Arend

College football’s Week 13 is in the books. This past week’s slate of games didn’t feature many highly-anticipated contests, but it still brought about several shocking results and upsets.

As he does each week, ESPN’s Kirk Herbstreit has revealed his top 5 (or in this case, six) teams of the previous weekend. To no surprise, the Arizona State Sun Devils claim the top spot on the list after upsetting No. 6 Oregon in dramatic fashion.

ASU quarterback Jayden Daniels had a monster game, throwing for 408 yards and three touchdowns. The Sun Devils jumped out to a 24-7 lead late in the fourth quarter before the Ducks attempted a ferocious comeback – but it was all too late. ASU held on to beat Oregon 31-28, knocking the Ducks out of the College Football Playoff race.

Here’s the rest of Herbstreit’s top 5 teams of Week 13:

  1. Arizona State
  2. FIU
  3. Charlotte
  4. Cal
  5. Minnesota and Baylor

The FIU Panthers shocked Miami on Saturday night, coming away with a 30-24 victory. Not only was it a huge win for the Panthers, but it’s sparked questions surrounding Manny Diaz’s future with the Hurricanes.

Charlotte football knocked off Marshall 24-13, becoming bowl eligible for the first time in school history in the process.
