Benjamin Damage signs to Feel My Bicep for new EP

Boss Rhythm / Niio Rhythm follows this month's four-track effort for Figure.

25 Nov 2019, 23:34 by Matt McDermott

Benjamin Damage will release an EP called Boss Rhythm / Niio Rhythm on Feel My Bicep December 6th.

The new 12-inch, which comes with two originals and download only "Dub" and "Tool" versions of "Boss Rhythm," sees the Welsh artists collaborating with anonymous label mates under the BDB alias. It follows the Overton Window EP, just released on Len Faki's Figure. Damage also has a 12-inch for ARTS planned for early 2020.
