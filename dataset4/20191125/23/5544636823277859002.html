N-Power Latest News Headlines Updates Today

N-Power: Federal Govt. To Train 3000 Young Nigerians

President Muhammadu Buhari has said about three thousand Nigerian youths would be trained by his government in N-Power Creative, Concise News reports.

This news medium understands that Buhari said this over the weekend at the graduation ceremony of Senior Executive Course 41 of the National Institute of Policy and Strategic Studies (NIPSS) in Kuru near Jos.

He was represented at the event by Vice President Yemi Osinbajo who noted that the present government wants to create jobs for youths with technology. Details here.

N-Power: Beneficiaries Losing Sleep Over October Stipend Payment

Beneficiaries of the N-Power scheme are still grumbling over the non-payment of the October 2019 stipend, Concise News reports.

This news medium understands that this is the longest period beneficiaries of the scheme have stayed without payment of their stipends.

There has been no official statement on why the October 2019 stipends have not been paid by handlers of the N-Power scheme.

The beneficiaries are angry with the handlers as rumours make the rounds that the scheme would end soon due to government’s inability to paid the N30, 000 monthly stipends. Read more here.
