Altagas Ltd (TSE:ALA) Receives C$21.38 Average PT from Analysts

25 Nov 2019, 23:18 by Mitch Edgeman

Altagas Ltd (TSE:ALA) has earned an average recommendation of “Buy” from the ten ratings firms that are currently covering the firm, MarketBeat reports. Four investment analysts have rated the stock with a hold recommendation and four have assigned a buy recommendation to the company. The average twelve-month price target among brokers that have issued a report on the stock in the last year is C$21.50.

A number of equities analysts have issued reports on the company. Raymond James reaffirmed a “market perform” rating and set a C$19.00 price target on shares of Altagas in a research note on Thursday, October 31st. BMO Capital Markets reaffirmed an “outperform” rating and set a C$23.00 price target on shares of Altagas in a research note on Tuesday, November 19th. CIBC raised Altagas from a “neutral” rating to an “outperform” rating in a research note on Thursday, October 31st. TD Securities increased their price target on Altagas from C$22.00 to C$23.00 and gave the company a “buy” rating in a research note on Friday, August 2nd. Finally, National Bank Financial increased their price target on Altagas from C$20.00 to C$21.00 and gave the company a “sector perform” rating in a research note on Thursday, October 31st.

TSE ALA traded up C$0.05 on Monday, reaching C$20.03. 602,587 shares of the stock traded hands, compared to its average volume of 880,784. The firm’s fifty day simple moving average is C$19.41 and its 200 day simple moving average is C$19.39. The company has a quick ratio of 0.26, a current ratio of 0.50 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 102.83. The company has a market cap of $5.54 billion and a price-to-earnings ratio of 5.28. Altagas has a 12 month low of C$11.87 and a 12 month high of C$20.87.

The business also recently disclosed a monthly dividend, which was paid on Friday, November 15th. Stockholders of record on Friday, October 25th were given a dividend of $0.08 per share. The ex-dividend date of this dividend was Thursday, October 24th. This represents a $0.96 dividend on an annualized basis and a dividend yield of 4.79%. Altagas’s dividend payout ratio (DPR) is presently 30.70%.

In related news, Director Allan Leslie Edgeworth acquired 5,000 shares of the business’s stock in a transaction dated Friday, November 1st. The stock was bought at an average price of C$19.10 per share, for a total transaction of C$95,500.00. Following the transaction, the director now owns 5,896 shares of the company’s stock, valued at C$112,613.60.

About Altagas

AltaGas Ltd. operates as a diversified energy infrastructure company in North America. The company operates through three segments: Utilities, Midstream, and Power. The Utilities segment owns and operates regulated natural gas distribution utilities in Michigan, Alaska, the District of Columbia, Maryland, and Virginia; and 2 regulated natural gas storage utilities in the United States serving approximately 1.6 million customers.
