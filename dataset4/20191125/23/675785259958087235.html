Faye Brookes makes red carpet debut with personal trainer Joe Davies after Gareth Gates split

Faye Brookes cosied up to her personal trainer Joe Davis at a premiere in London tonight following her split from Gareth Gates

25 Nov 2019, 23:38 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Faye Brookes and Gareth Gates shocked fans when they split earlier this year, but the soap starlet appears to be moving on.

She made her first red carpet appearance with her personal trainer Joe Davis in London tonight.

The ex Coronation Street actress, 32, looked very cosy as she cuddled up to the blonde hunk at the White Christmas musical's press night in the capital.

Joe, who Faye was first linked to several months ago, wrapped an arm around her as they posed for photos together.

Faye looked chic in a green dress that she'd hidden under a black coat with a fur collar.

She braved the cold with bare legs and a pair of black stilettos, which she matched with her clutch bag.

Joe looked dapper in a crisp white shirt, teaming it with a grey jumper, dark blue jeans and a warm black coat.

Back in August it was reported that the duo had "grown close" following Faye's split from fiancé Gareth.

Faye denied that there was anything romantic between them, saying she'd started working out more to distract her mind from the split.

She tweeted: "It’s been a really weird week…

"I've been throwing myself into the gym pretty much everyday, anyone who knows me knows how much I love training and keeping fit!"

She went on: "It’s great for your physical and mental health. I’m a member of several gyms in Manchester including the fab Barry’s Bootcamp.

"So (yesterday) there was a pap outside the gym when I got there, taking pictures of me & getting very excited with his camera because I walked in with one of the trainers from the gym – who is a friend."

Gareth was said to be "heartbroken" by the reports.
