Spoilers: Darren supports Luke in Hollyoaks as he worries about his health

25 Nov 2019, 23:59

Luke Morgan (Gary Lucy) has had plenty on his mind since returning to the village in Hollyoaks earlier this year, but his recent trip to the hospital has left him incredibly concerned about his health. In the coming episodes, he decides to put his mind at rest by making a hospital appointment — and he asks Darren Osborne (Ashley Taylor Dawson) to accompany him.

After his altercation with Darren left him in need of medical attention, Luke was devastated to discover that he might have an underlying health condition.

In the coming episodes, he continues to worry about his health, as his hand keeps seizing up.

Sienna (Anna Passey) notices his hand, and proceeds to tell him about her dad, who had motor neurone disease. Luke decides to book himself an appointment at the hospital, and asks if Darren wouldn’t mind going with him.
