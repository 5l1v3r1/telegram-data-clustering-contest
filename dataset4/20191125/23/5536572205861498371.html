Colin Kaepernick Drops Insane Hype Video From Workout (VIDEO)

25 Nov 2019, 23:40 by Darrelle Lincoln

Colin Kaepernick was hoping to drum up some interest from teams around the league after his workout almost two weeks ago, but so far, nobody has contacted his representatives to possibly sign him to a team.

To get their attention once again, the free agent quarterback dropped an insane hype video of his workout at a local high school.
