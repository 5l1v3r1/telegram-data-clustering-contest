Baronsmead Venture Trust PLC (LON:BVT) Plans Dividend of GBX 3.50

25 Nov 2019, 23:16 by Michael Walen

Baronsmead Venture Trust PLC (LON:BVT) declared a dividend on Monday, November 25th, Upcoming.Co.Uk reports. Investors of record on Thursday, February 6th will be paid a dividend of GBX 3.50 ($0.05) per share on Friday, March 6th. This represents a yield of 4.93%. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Thursday, February 6th. This is a positive change from Baronsmead Venture Trust’s previous dividend of $3.00. About Baronsmead Venture Trust

Baronsmead Venture Trust Plc is a venture capital trust managed by ISIS Equity Partners LLP. It invests in a portfolio of unquoted companies in the United Kingdom, which raise expansion capital or are the subject of a management buy-out or buy-in. The trust primarily invests in the media, business services, consumer, information technology support services, and healthcare sectors.
