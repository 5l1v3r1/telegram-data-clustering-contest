Spoilers: Callum warns Whitney against Leo in EastEnders — but he has a plan

25 Nov 2019, 23:59

In spite of their relationship having ended in disaster, Callum ‘Halfway’ Highway (Tony Clay) has demonstrated that he still cares greatly for Whitney Dean (Shona McGarty) in EastEnders. Similarly, Whitney has proven that she still loves Callum — and it appeared as if the pair may have embarked on a friendship had it not been for the evil Leo King’s (Tom Wells) interference.

Yes, viewers will remember that, after Leo set his sights on Whitney, it didn’t take him long to realise that Callum was going to be something of a problem. With Whitney still holding the young man in a high regard, it was nearly impossible for Leo to get close to her, and thus he set about getting Callum out of the picture.

After acquiring an old video that didn’t paint Callum in a positive light, Leo showed the clip to Whitney, who proceeded to publicly call him out. As a result, the young man’s heroic reputation was damaged, and thus Leo was able to swoop in and carry on with his plan.
