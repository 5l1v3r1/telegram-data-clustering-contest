Why this membership pack has got fans questioning whether new crowd rules are on the way…

25 Nov 2019, 23:28 by 3aw mornings

Is the AFL about to introduce stricter new crowd rules?

3AW listener Ross got his 2020 St Kilda membership pack recently, which came in a see-through, clear, plastic bag.

His wife wondered whether the AFL was about to make that a rule for fans entering venues.

The idea for clear backpacks was floated back in 2016, in a bid to help speed up security checks at AFL venues.
