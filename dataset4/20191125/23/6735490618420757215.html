James Van Der Beek And Kimberly Return To ‘DWTS’ Finale

25 Nov 2019, 23:01 by Shakiel Mahjouri

James Van Der Beek and Kimberly Van Der Beek got a lot of love at the “Dancing with the Stars” finale.

The “Dawson’s Creek” alum and his wife have spoken candidly about a recent miscarriage they suffered. Van Der Beek was eliminated from “DWTS” in the penultimate episode right after he shared the tragic news of his wife’s miscarriage

When the couple arrived, “a crew member just greeted her [Kimberly] and gave her a long hug,” a source told Us Weekly.

Former “The Bachelorette” star and “DWTS” winner Hannah Brown “saw Kimberly from the dancer’s box and reached her arm over to where Kimberly was sitting.”

“They held each other’s hands as they spoke and looked happy to see each other again,” the source added. “Hannah also waved to Kimberley’s daughter, Olivia, who was excited to see Hannah.”

Van Der Beek performed a big dance number alongside the season 28 “DWTS” contestants to a song by Pitbull and Ne-Yo.

“First off I want to thank everybody for all the support and the love that you guys have sent my way and my family’s way,” Van Der Beek told his Instagram followers. “It really helps. We so appreciated hearing all the messages of support, of love.”
