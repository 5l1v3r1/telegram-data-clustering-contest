‘Servant’ for Apple TV+ has been renewed for a second season

25 Nov 2019, 23:14 by Evan Selleck

One of the more interesting projects coming to Apple TV+ is called Servant and now we know we’re getting more than just one season.

Servant is from M. Night Shyamalan (The Sixth Sense), and it tells a very creepy story about a family trying to make their way through a traumatic event, but who, ultimately, allow a not-so-savory presence into their home while trying to do that. It stars Toby Kebbell (Dawn of the Planet of the Apes) and Lauren Ambrose (Six Feet Under) as Sean and Dorothy, respectively, and welcomes newcomer Nell Tiger Free for good measure.

Rupert Grint (from the Harry Potter films) is also present.

According to a report from SlashFilm, Apple has renewed Servant for a second season. And if you thought maybe you had somehow missed the series debuting on Apple’s fledgling video streaming service, fear not, because you didn’t. Servant is set to debut on Thursday, November 28.
