In Pic: Karan Johar shares a sweet selfie with Sara Ali Khan on Instagram

25 Nov 2019, 03:29 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ace filmmaker Karan Johar who is currently in the US for some work commitment share an adorable picture with Sara Ali Khan who is already vacaying in New York. The actress has been sharing pictures and videos from her holidays on her social media account.

Just a while ago, KJo took to his Instagram stories to share a sweet selfie with Sara Ali Khan. He captioned the photo by dropping in a heart and a lovestruck emoji. Later, Sara also reposted the same image on her Insta stories too.
