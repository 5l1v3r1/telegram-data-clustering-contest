Photos: Shah Rukh Khan papped at the airport sporting a cool and casual look

25 Nov 2019, 03:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Fans have been eagerly waiting for Shah Rukh Khan's return to the big screen. It's been almost more than a year that his last outing 'Zero' released. This year, on his birthday, SRK announced that soon he will make some announcements regarding his upcoming projects.

Meanwhile, he is often papped in the city by the paparazzi attending various events, parties and spending some quality time with his kids.

Today early morning, the King of Bollywood was snapped at the Mumbai airport while he was set to jet off to an unknown destination.

In the pics, Shah Rukh is seen sporting cool casuals- white tee and blue jeans teamed up with a checkered shirt on top of the tee, sneakers and a pair of sunglasses. SRK was all smiles as he posed for the shutterbugs stationed at the airport

Reportedly, the superstar was supposed to join Alia and Ranbir on the 'Brahamastra's' Manali schedule. Just a few hours ago, Alia and Ranbir have also jetted off to an unknown destination which left all SRKians wondering if they were all heading to shoot the fantasy drama.

On the professional front, SRK will be collaborating with 'Bigil' director Atlee for his upcoming film. As per the buzz, the film is slated to go on the floors soon.
