Everyone Is Obsessed With Lizzo’s Ridiculously Tiny Handbag On The AMAs Red Carpet

25 Nov 2019, 03:10 by Junkee

The American Music Awards are currently taking place in Los Angeles’ Microsoft Theatre.

So far, Billie Eilish has been the big early winner of the night, taking home the gongs for Best New Artist, and Favourite Artist for Alternative/Rock — oh, and she casually set the stage on fire during her performance of ‘all the good girls go to hell‘.

She even tried to win the red carpet by wearing what appeared to be a bejewelled bee keeper helmet on the red carpet — although she was eclipsed by Lizzo, who stayed away from helmets, but instead brought along the world’s tiniest bag. Custom Valentino, no less.

“It’s a Valentino bag, it’s custom — there’s only three in the world,” Lizzo told the bemused red carpet hosts. “I’ve got tampons in here, a flask of tequila, some condoms.”
