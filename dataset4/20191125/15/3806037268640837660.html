Dunkin' Donuts Imagines the Original Blend, Old Fashioned & Peppermint Mocha in Candle Form

With help from candle makers Homesick.

25 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Eric Brain

Dunkin’ Donuts and candle makers Homesick have come together on a limited-edition collection of home scents, perfect for the cozy months ahead.

Dunkin’s culinary team and Homesick have captured some of America’s — and the world’s — favorite scents with their “Original Blend,” “Old Fashioned” donut and “Peppermint Mocha” coffee candles, which are all made with natural coconut wax.

“Original Blend” delivers wafts of freshly made coffee alongside an undertone of sweet creaminess, with a top note that combines espresso bean with cold brew. “Old Fashioned” is based on the signature Dunkin’ donut, blending its sweetness with warm spices such as cinnamon and nutmeg. Lastly, “Peppermint Mocha” brings memories of the festive season approaching thanks to its vanilla, peppermint, coffee, dark chocolate, and whipped cream mixture, making for a candle that’s just as decadent as the drink.

All of Homesick’s candles are hand-poured in the US and burn for 60-80 hours inside the brand’s signature minimal jars. Branding is left minimalistic on the front, with only a small nod to the collaborator on the label, while the box goes all-out with a Dunkin’ Donuts theme.

Take a look at the Dunkin’ Donuts x Homesick collection in the gallery above, and purchase all three online now for $29.95 USD each.
