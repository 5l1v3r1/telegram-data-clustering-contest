Audi parked in Dundee driveway vandalised using ‘corrosive substance’

Police are hunting a vandal who caused over £1000 worth of damage to a parked vehicle in Dundee.

25 Nov 2019, 15:33 by Jake Keith

The blue Audi, which was parked in a driveway on Loftus Road, was severely damaged by a “corrosive substance” between 7pm on Wednesday November 20 and 11am the next day.

It did not belong to the residents of the house and no other nearby cars were damaged.

Anyone with information that could assist police should call 101 or speak to any officer.

Information can be passed anonymously through Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. The reference is incident 1054 of November 21.
