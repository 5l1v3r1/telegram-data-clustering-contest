Maina: Court Grants Ex-pension Reform Boss N1bn Bail

25 Nov 2019, 15:24 by Donatus Anichukwueze

The chairman of the defunct pension reform task team, Abdulrashid Maina, has been granted bail by a Federal High Court in Abuja in the sum of one billion naira with two sureties in like sum.

The court ordered that both sureties must be serving Nigerian Senators with no criminal cases before it.

Furthermore, both sureties must have fully developed landed property in Maitama or Asokoro district of Abuja.

In its ruling on Monday, the also said the sureties must submit 3-years tax clearance certificates and must appear at all further court rulings with the 1st defendant.

The sureties must file an affidavit to show they can pay the penance sum.
