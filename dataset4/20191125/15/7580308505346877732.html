Court grants Maina N1bn bail

25 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Editor

A Federal High Court sitting in Abuja has granted the former chairman of the defunct pension reform task team Abdulrasheed Maina bail in the sum of one billion Naira with two sureties in like sum.

The court ruled that both sureties must be serving Nigerian Senators with no criminal cases before the court and that the sureties must file an affidavit to show they can pay the penance sum.

The sureties must also submit 3-years tax clearance certificates and must appear at all further court rulings with Maina.

Also, the court said both sureties must have fully developed landed property in Maitama or Asokoro district of Abuja, Nigeria’s capital city.
