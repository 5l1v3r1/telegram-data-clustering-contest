Man crushed to death by rollercoaster carriage at Christmas market in Berlin

25 Nov 2019, 15:10

A man has died after he was crushed beneath a rollercoaster carriage at a Christmas market.

The man, believed to be a ride attendant, was treated at the scene in Berlin but could not be saved.

Firefighters were called just after 7pm on Saturday, the cityâ€™s fire department said.

They said the cause of the accident was not yet known.

A family of four who had been on the ride at the time were given counselling.

German police said they did not suspect foul play, Deutsche Welle reported.

Fire department spokesman Jens-Peter Wilke said the market was closed after the incident, having just opened on Friday.

The market was pitched on a large car park on Landsberger Allee in the northeast of the city.
