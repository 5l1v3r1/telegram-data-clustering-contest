Pagalpanti has a decent start at the box-office

25 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Akash Nihalani

The multi-starrer madcap comedy Pagalpanti has just completed its first weekend at the box-office and has gone on to have a decent run so far. Talking about the film’s collection, Pagalpanti had managed to collect a total of Rs 5 crore on the first day. The numbers saw a slight rise on Saturday and Sunday as the film collected Rs. 6.25 crore and between Rs. 7-8 crore respectively. The film’s total collections now stand at Rs 18 crore. Pagalpanti is also facing stiff completion from Frozen 2 which has managed to collect over Rs.10 crore over the weekend.

Directed by Anees Bazmee, the film stars John Abraham, Anil Kapoor, Ileana D’cruz, Arshad Warsi, Kriti Kharbanda, Pulkit Samrat and Urvashi Rautela.
