The latest development in Spurs and Crystal Palace's pursuit of West Brom star - report

West Bromwich Albion transfer latest | The Baggies have offered Nathan Ferguson a new long-term contract - but

25 Nov 2019, 15:21 by Josh Murray

West Bromwich Albion could reportedly consider selling starlet Nathan Ferguson to a Premier League suitor to avoid him heading abroad at the end of the season.

The 19-year-old defender has been one of the big success stories of an eye-catching start to the season at the Hawthorns, making 13 appearances to date in his maiden Championship campaign.

Ferguson's current contract expires in the summer, and he has been offered a new five-year deal.

Should Ferguson move abroad, the Baggies will reportedly only be entitled to around £300,000 compensation under FIFA guidelines - with Atletico Madrid and Juventus among the European clubs linked.
