Plane crash off Anglesey coast sparks search and rescue mission

Police, the Coastguard and lifeboats are searching the water off Puffin Island, Anglesey, North Wales

25 Nov 2019, 15:19 by James Rodger, Sarah Hodgson, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

A frantic search and rescue is underway amid reports of a plane crash off the coast of Anglesey.

Police, the Coastguard and lifeboats are searching the water off Puffin Island, Anglesey, North Wales.

The search comes after a light aircraft was said to have crashed this afternoon (Monday, November 26).

Police were called at 12.59pm to reports of a "possible crash".
