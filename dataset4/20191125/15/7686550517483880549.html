GAME launch Black Friday sale on PS4, Xbox One and the Nintendo Switch

The Black Friday sale at GAME has started and there are some cheap consoles.

25 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Stephen Hurrell

Bundle deals on all major consoles have been reduced in the GAME Black Friday sale .

Launched today, Monday 25 November, in the build-up to the big day at the end of the week, GAME promise to add new deals daily.

Reductions have already been placed on PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Nintendo Switch bundle deals that come complete with the latest titles including Star Wars Jedi and Call of Duty: Modern Warfare.

But how do they stack up against some of the best game bundles on offer for Black Friday?

GAME has cut the price of a PS4 to just £299. The bundle is for the Pro version of the console and includes Call of Duty Modern Warefare and NowTV. A PS4 5000gb console complete with FIFA 20 is priced at £249.99 at Argos if you are looking for a cheaper option.

It also has an Xbox One X deal priced at £299 with Star Wars Jedi Fallen Order and NowTV for three months. An Xbox One X console is currently £319.99 on Amazon . The deal includes Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order, Rage 2 and a gaming headset.

Among the pick of the deals, GAME are currently selling a Nintendo Switch complete with the Nintendo Labo for a combined price of £279.

Meanwhile, one of the indisputed 'Games of The Year', Red Dead Redemption 2, is now on offer for less than £25 on both Xbox One and PS4.

Lastly, other savings can be made on accessories including headsets and controllers.

Other deals, currently 'locked', will be made available later in the week.

GAME's offers aren't restricted to consoles, accessories and games. TV deals are also available – as too are savings on the latest mobile phones.

To check out everything in the GAME Black Friday sale head to the GAME website.
