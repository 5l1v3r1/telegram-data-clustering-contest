See Usher at WHITE Dubai

Sponsored: Hip-hop legend to be at Meydan this weekend

25 Nov 2019, 15:12 by Time Out Dubai staff

US superstar and R&B legend Usher has confirmed he’ll be in Dubai this weekend.

The rapper, singer and hip-hop icon will be hosted by WHITE Dubai at Meydan on Saturday November 30, and having most recently played in Dubai back in 2012, it marks a long-overdue return to the emirate for the star.

Now 41, Usher’s career began way back in 1994 aged just 16, with the release of debut album Usher, before his career took off big time with the release of My Way, and US and US chart-topping albums 8701 in 2001 and Confessions in 2004.His following three albums topped the charts in the US and to date he’s sold nearly 70 million records worldwide.

You’ll know him for his biggest hits Caught Up, U Got It Bad, Pop Ya Collar, U Remind Me, U-Turn, Yeah! and so many more.
