BBC Sports Personality of the Year nominees: Raheem Sterling, Ben Stokes & Dina Asher-Smith nominated for 2019 award

25 Nov 2019, 15:41 by Press Association

England and Manchester City forward Raheem Sterling has been announced as a nominee for the BBC Sports Personality of the Year award.

Sterling, whose nomination was announced by England and Manchester City Women striker Ellen White, played a key role as City secured the domestic treble of Premier League, FA Cup and League Cup in 2019, while he has also been a standout performer for the national team.

He has won widespread praise for his stand against racism in the game.
