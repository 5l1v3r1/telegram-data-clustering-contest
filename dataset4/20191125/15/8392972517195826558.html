I’m A Celebrity viewers demand Ant and Dec host next series of Love Island

Shall they replace Caroline Flack?

25 Nov 2019, 15:12 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity reckon Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly should host the next series of Love Island.

During last night's episode of the jungle-themed show, a live Bushtucker trial took place and it was based on the ITV2 reality programme.

The trial saw the campmates take on a string of different challenges as the jungle was set up to look like the Love Island villa.

And it seems viewers loved seeing Ant and Dec in that setting so much, they want the duo to replace Caroline Flack on the reality series next year.

One person said on Twitter: "Petition for Ant & Dec to present Love Island 2020."
