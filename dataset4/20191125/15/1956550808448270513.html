Homegrown mobile app StyleCity is the fastest downloaded game in the UAE

Putting Dubai gamer’s fashion sense to the ultimate test

25 Nov 2019, 15:55 by Darragh Murphy

Think you have what it takes to conquer the fashion world of Dubai? Well, before you start, you might want to first do it virtually.

Homegrown AAA mobile gaming brand Dubzplay, has launched StyleCity, an already chart-topping App Store game created in the region, for the region.

With over 200,000 downloads, StyleCity is the fastest downloaded game in the UAE, having topped the charts within the first two days of launch.

In the game, players will explore Dubai’s trendiest spots in real-time, with hundreds of different venues, shops and events to visit and over 1,500 unique items of clothing and accessories to style characters.

In single-player mode, gamers will be pitted in different scenarios where they are ‘judged for their ability to pick the perfect look’. There’s also a daily multiplayer competition for other players to duke it out – the fashion way.

Founded in 2015, Dubzplay also has contracts with some of the biggest celebrity names in the region, including The Real Fouz, Nohastyleicon and Model Roz, featured in the game, and upcoming applications, too.

There’s plenty of reason to keep playing, as Dubzplay will grow its roster of regional celebrities, and is developing three more products to be released in the coming two years.

“Our applications place a strong emphasis on celebrity presence and seasonality, appealing to users in the MENA region and globally,” said CEO at Dubzplay Mohannad Khallouf.

“With a large network of passionate and talented employees, we take a bold and energetic approach to social applications, combining cool and original content with state-of-the-art graphics.”

StyleCity is available right now on Android and iOS, and the game is completely free to download, with in-game purchases available.
