Commercial National Financial Corp (OTCMKTS:CNAF) Plans Quarterly Dividend of $0.76

25 Nov 2019, 15:28 by Michael Walen

Commercial National Financial Corp (OTCMKTS:CNAF) declared a quarterly dividend on Tuesday, November 19th, Zacks reports. Stockholders of record on Friday, November 29th will be given a dividend of 0.76 per share by the bank on Friday, December 6th. This represents a $3.04 dividend on an annualized basis and a dividend yield of 14.69%. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Wednesday, November 27th. This is an increase from Commercial National Financial’s previous quarterly dividend of $0.26.

Shares of OTCMKTS CNAF remained flat at $$20.69 during mid-day trading on Monday. Commercial National Financial has a fifty-two week low of $19.95 and a fifty-two week high of $24.65. The stock has a market cap of $59.17 million, a price-to-earnings ratio of 13.18 and a beta of 0.32. The stock has a 50-day simple moving average of $20.75 and a 200 day simple moving average of $21.73.

Separately, ValuEngine raised shares of Commercial National Financial from a “sell” rating to a “hold” rating in a research report on Wednesday, October 2nd.

Commercial National Financial Company Profile

Commercial National Financial Corporation operates as a bank holding company for Commercial Bank & Trust of PA that provides various banking services in Pennsylvania. The company offers checking, money market investment, and savings accounts; and business-time deposits, as well as certificates of deposit.
