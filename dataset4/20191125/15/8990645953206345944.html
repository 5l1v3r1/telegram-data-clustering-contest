City of Wichita Falls Thanksgiving 2019 Trash Schedule

25 Nov 2019, 15:08 by Johnny Thrash

City offices will be closed in observation of the Thanksgiving holiday on Thursday, November 28 and Friday, November 29.

As a result, there will be no organic reuse collection this Wednesday.

Instead, Thursday’s trash pickup schedule will shift to Wednesday while Friday’s normally scheduled pickup will move back a day to Saturday.

The landfill and transfer station will be closed Thursday and Friday, but will return to normal hours on Saturday.
