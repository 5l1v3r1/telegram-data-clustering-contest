Michelle Wolf's New Netflix Special Joke Show Has a Trailer

25 Nov 2019, 15:28 by Rachita Vasandani  

Michelle Wolf’s Joke Show has a trailer and new key art. The latest Netflix special from the 2018 White House Correspondents’ Dinner host tackles the differences between men and women, and explores gender equality. As Netflix notes, Wolf “wants to fight for women’s equality … but not in, like, that annoying way.” The trailer, meanwhile, focuses more on animal consent and labradoodles, so it would appear Joke Show contains multitudes.

Michelle Wolf: Joke Show starts streaming on Dec. 10, exclusively on Netflix. See the trailer and key art below.
