FATAL CRASH

A74(M) crash – Lorry driver dies in smash near Lockerbie as second crash sees van driver cut free and rushed to hospital

25 Nov 2019, 15:25 by Blair Meikle

A LORRY driver has died after a horror smash on the A74(M) this morning.

Emergency services raced to the scene near junction 20 on the, near Lockerbie, at around 5.50am this morning.

The articulated lorry smashed into a barrier, in one of two crashed which occurred in the area.

Cops say the driver was pronounced dead at the scene.

The road was locked down in both directions for several hours while the vehicle was recovered.

The northbound carriageway remains closed.

Cops probing the accident are appealing for any witnesses to come forward.
