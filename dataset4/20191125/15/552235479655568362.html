Road between Dumbarton and Bonhill will be closed for overnight works

Stirling Road will be shut between Lomondgate Roundabout to Lionsgate Roundabout from tonight.

25 Nov 2019, 15:40 by Jenny Foulds, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/jenny-foulds/

A key road between Dumbarton and Bonhill be closed for overnight works this week.

To facilitate road works by West Dunbartonshire Council contractors, Stirling Road will be shut from Lomondgate Roundabout to Lionsgate Roundabout at the Vale of Leven Industrial Estate.

The works began last night (November 25) and will take up to five days.

The closure is expected to take place between 9pm and 6pm.

Further information is available at http://www.west-dunbarton.gov.uk/stirlingrd
