Dreamers Delayed

25 Nov 2019, 15:55 by BRIEF

BROWNSVILLE, Texas — U.S. District Judge Andrew Hanen agreed defendant-intervenor New Jersey et al.’s request to stay Texas v. United States, which seeks to have the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program declared illegal, pending Supreme Court review of similar issues.
