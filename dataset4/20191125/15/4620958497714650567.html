Popular street party gets festive with one-time holiday pop-up in December

25 Nov 2019, 15:32 by New West Record

New Westminster’s favourite street party is returning, and it’s feeling festive.

Fridays on Front: the Holiday Edition returns on Friday, Dec. 13 from 5 to 9 p.m. Everyone’s invited to join in fun under the parkade on Front Street.

Fridays on Front features a lineup of fan-favourite food trucks – including such faves as Mo Bacon, Frying Pan, Boca’o, Cannoli King, Bread and Cheese and Mr. Arancino, just to name a sampling.

You’ll also be able to sip on mulled wine by Pacific Breeze Winery and enjoy award-winning local beer by Steel & Oak, all while you browse through the New West Craft artisan holiday market.

There will also be fun, family-friendly activities. And, since it wouldn’t be a Christmas market without music, the Arts Council of New Westminster will be on hand to provide the holiday tunes.

Fridays on Front: The Holiday Edition

When: Fri. Dec. 13 from 5-9 p.m.
Where: Front Street in New Westminster
Cost: Admission is FREE
