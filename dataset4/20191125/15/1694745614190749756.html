Emily Ratajkowski and her husband, Sebastian Bear-McClard, are vacationing at the Waldorf Astoria in the Maldives and Emily is providing us with some serious wardrobe envy while they're at it. I mean, we already knew she owned sexy swimsuits, many of which are part of her own line, Inamorata Swim, but Emily's cover-ups and date night dresses are taking things to the next level.

She posed for a picture on Sebastian's shoulders wearing the George's suit (yes, one of her own creations), and later that night opted for a silk white slip with a strappy back, complete with a dazzling diamond choker. Emily also coordinated her leopard Las Olas top with a printed red wrap skirt, showing that particular look off selfie style. Even her simple black triangle top was accessorized smartly, with some gold jewelry and an adorable black bucket hat.

It's safe to say Emily's pics are going to make you want to book a getaway STAT, so we rounded up her essentials and shopped them out for you — that way you too can be the sexiest babe on the beach, or in the water, playing Maldivian chicken with your SO.
