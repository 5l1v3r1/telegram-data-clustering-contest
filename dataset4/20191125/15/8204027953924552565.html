Ohio State Highway Patrol investigating deadly crash in Fairborn

25 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Breaking News Staff

BATH TWP. — Fairborn troopers from the Xenia post of the Ohio State Highway Patrol are investigating a deadly crash that occurred on state Route 235 near Sandhill Road.

According to reports from OSP, the crash occurred around 3:20 a.m. Saturday morning.

A red 2013 Nissan Sentra was traveling southbound on state Route 235 when it drove left of center and struck a silver 2007 Ford Focus traveling northbound head on.

Chantal M. Baldwin, 25, of Huber Heights, was operating the Nissan Sentra. She was pronounced dead on the scene, according to a release.

David L. Wilson, 57, of Fairborn, was operating the Ford Focus. He was transported to Miami Valley Hospital with serious injuries.

OSP is still investigating the cause of the crash. Alcohol involvement and seat belt usage is still unknown.
