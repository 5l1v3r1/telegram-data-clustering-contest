ThaiBev posts 30% rise in FY19 earnings to S$1.05 bil on higher revenue

25 Nov 2019, 15:24 by Stanislaus Jude Chan

SINGAPORE (Nov 25): Thai Beverage posted a 30% jump in full-year earnings to 23.27 billion baht (S$1.05 billion) for FY2019 ended September, from 17.94 billion baht a year ago.

This translated to earnings per share (EPS) of 0.93 baht in FY2019, compared to EPS of 0.71 baht in FY2018.
