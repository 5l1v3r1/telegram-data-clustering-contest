Burglar forces way in through front door, steals laptop, police say

25 Nov 2019, 15:15 by Tabatha Wethal

MADISON, Wis. - A burglar forced their way into an east Madison home Sunday night, according to police. 

The Madison Police Department said the resident, who lives in the 5900 block of Milwaukee Street, wasn't at home when the burglar broke into the home at about 11 p.m. 

Police said the burglar entered through the front door by forcing it in and stole a silver Chromebook laptop computer.
