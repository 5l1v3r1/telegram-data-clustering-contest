Joe Theismann Rips Dwayne Haskins For Taking Selfies With Fans: “That’s Unprofessional & Wrong” (TWEET)

25 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Darrelle Lincoln

Dwayne Haskins was simply excited about getting his first win of his career as well as celebrating the team’s second win of the year.

The Washington Redskins beat the Detroit Lions, 19-16, on Sunday, but before the game was actually over, Haskins was seen on the sidelines near fans and taking selfies with them.

He wasn’t available for the final victory kneel down snap as Case Keenum took the field.
