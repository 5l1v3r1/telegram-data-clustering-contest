Tater tot wrap restaurant opens on State Street

Madison location is fifth for company

25 Nov 2019, 15:34 by Maija Inveiss, Logan Rude

Conrad's Grill, an East Lansing-based tater tot wrap restaurant, opened a new location on State Street, the fifth for the company.

Founded in 2007, the restaurant serves up a variety of tater tot wraps. Regardless of the wrap's other ingredients, each wrap has tater tots included.

Conrad's has three locations in the East Lansing area, where it originally started, and one in Chicago.

The location in Madison is the company's first in Wisconsin. Conrad's Grill is located in the hub building, in the former location of Glaze Teriyaki.
