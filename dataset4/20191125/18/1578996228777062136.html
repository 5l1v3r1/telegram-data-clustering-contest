Prada and Adidas Unveil Its First Collaboration: A Limited-Edition Superstar Sneaker and Bowling Bag

25 Nov 2019, 18:59 by Ariana Yaptangco

After a lot of hype, Prada and Adidas dropped their first collaboration with two limited edition releases. Talks of a collaboration first started in October, and was finally confirmed earlier this month, with the powerhouse brands teasing a possible sneaker release on Instagram.

The first piece from Prada x Adidas is a take on the classic Adidas Superstar sneaker, crafted in Italy by Prada. Made of full-grain leather, the sneaker has the iconic shell toe and signature triple stripes, while the Prada logo is emblazoned on both heels. The second is a surprise addition: a Prada bowling bag for Adidas made of calf leather with a nylon strap. Both come in the colorway Optic White.

Adidas is no stranger to collaborations, working in the past with designers like Raf Simmons and celebrities like Kanye West. This is Prada's first collab with the brand.

The collection is extremely exclusive, with availability limited to 700 pieces and each numbered with a unique serial number. Starting December 4, the collaboration will retail globally across 19 Prada stores, in cities like Paris, London, Beijing, and Tokyo. It's also available in the U.S. at Prada's stores in New York, Miami, and Los Angeles, and online at Prada.com and adidas.com. Pricing information is not available yet, but stay tuned to Prada and Adidas's websites for updates.
