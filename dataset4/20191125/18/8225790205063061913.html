Pedestrian in crosswalk critically injured following Kalihi crash

25 Nov 2019, 18:33 by HNN Staff

HONOLULU, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) - A 57-year-old woman was critically injured Monday morning when she was struck while trying to cross North School Street.

The crash happened about 6:30 a.m. when the woman was in a marked crosswalk.

Police said the driver who hit her was on Gulick Avenue and had made a left turn on North School Street. Investigators said it appeared the driver didn’t see the woman.

At this time, it doesn’t appear that speed, drugs or alcohol were factors in the crash.

The victim was taken to the Queen’s Medical Center in critical condition. The driver was uninjured.
