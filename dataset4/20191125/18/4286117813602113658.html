Have support of 162 MLAs, total 158 present at Hyatt meeting today, says Shiv Sena's Priyanka Chaturvedi

Shiv Sena Deputy Leader Priyanka Chaturvedi on Monday tweeted that a total of 158 MLAs were physically present at Hotel Grand Hyatt in Mumbai where they took a pledge not to defect to the BJP.

25 Nov 2019, 18:20

Shiv Sena Deputy Leader Priyanka Chaturvedi on Monday tweeted that a total of 158 MLAs were physically present at Hotel Grand Hyatt in Mumbai where they took a pledge not to defect to the BJP.

She said that a total of 158 MLAs were present at the venue physically while four other MLAs kept in touch even though they could not make it to the Mumbai 5-star hotel.

She tweeted, "Those interested in head count/ exact numbers, here goes: Total 158 present at Hyatt meeting today NCP: Zirval ji just reached, Dharmarao Atram ji is in the hospital unwell but in touch Cong: Prithviraj Chavan ji in Delhi, Sunil Kedar ji in Nagpur for ZP elections."
