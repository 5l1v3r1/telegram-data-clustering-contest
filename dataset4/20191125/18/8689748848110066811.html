Video Of Rapper Edem Preparing Banku Hits The Internet

25 Nov 2019, 18:44 by Ghbase New Writer

Rapper Edem has lighted up the internet after a video of him preparing banku or probably ‘akple’ hit the net.

Apparently the rapper, real name Denning Edem Hotor has some great culinary skills as he stirs the popular local dish to the delight of his friends who were videoing him.

One of his friends even joked that it’s only a real man who knows how to cook that can ride a woman in bed.

Banku or its variant Akple is a Ghanaian dish which is cooked by a proportionate mixture of fermented corn and cassava dough in hot water into a smooth, whitish paste, served with soup, stew or a pepper sauce with fish.
