2019 Has Been Good To Me – Strongman Discloses

25 Nov 2019, 18:59 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian “diss” song rapper Vincent Osei popularly known as Strongman doesn’t seem to share in the bad fate 2019 has been for most Ghanaians as he says the year has been the best year in his music career.

Speaking in an interview on Akoma FM in Kumasi, Strongman said : “Although the year has not ended yet, I would say 2019 has been the best year in my music career. I believe there are better years to come”.

After his contract with Sarkcess Music expired, Strongman has gone ahead to win the hearts of many Ghanaians with his tracks and also won a couple of awards.

It would be recalled that, less than a month after announcing the birth of his daughter, Strongman acquired a new house and a new car which he shared on his social media page.

Strongman was recently in Kumasi to promote his new single ‘Mmaa’ which is targeted at treating women with the best of love and also campaign against domestic violence.
Well, we wish Strongman all the best in his future endeavors.
