Russian venue Stackenschneider launches label with 12-inch series featuring club residents

Tripmastaz and Ki.Mi. contribute to the first release, STK01, out now.

25 Nov 2019, 18:48 by Kiana Mickles

Russian club Stackenschneider has launched a new label with a various artists series.

The first installment, STK01, features tracks by club residents Tripmastaz, Ki.Mi., Deepcase and Pavel ludin. Out now on vinyl, the house- and electro-heavy EP was mastered by UK producer Rob Small.

Located in Saint Petersburg, Stackenschneider is a nightclub that promotes underground electronic music from Russia and beyond.
