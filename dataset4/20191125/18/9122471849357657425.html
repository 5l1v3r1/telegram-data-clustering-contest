Willie Taggart, Florida State Reportedly Never Signed A Formal Contract

25 Nov 2019, 18:07 by Matt Lombardi

Florida State fired Willie Taggart midway through the 2019 season, despite the fact that it has to pay him approximately an $18 million buyout. But a contract snafu could wind up saving the Seminoles a whole heck of a lot of money.

Matt Baker of the Tampa Bay Times reports Monday that Taggart and Florida State never actually signed a formal contract. There was an initial letter – but the two sides never actually signed a contract later on.

Baker reports that experts say it’s possible Florida State could consider Taggart an at-will employee – meaning it wouldn’t have to pay out his buyout.

It’s a complicated matter, and you can be sure that Taggart will sue if he isn’t paid his buyout. Apparently, there is some precedent here too.
