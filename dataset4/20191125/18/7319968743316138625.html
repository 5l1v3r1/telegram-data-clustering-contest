Dream job! You can apply to be an Irish Coffee taste tester in NYC this Christmas

25 Nov 2019, 18:56 by IrishCentral Staff

Love Irish Coffee? One travel site is looking to hire an official taste tester in New York City next month. 

Is there anything better than sipping a piping hot Irish coffee during the holiday season? Turns out there is, as Reservations.com is looking to hire an official Irish Coffee taste tester for December in New York City.

“One of the quintessential pick-me-ups during the cold and chaos of the holidays is a comforting Irish coffee,” says Reservations.com who is now on a mission to find the best Irish Coffee in New York City.

That's where you come in. If you want to be considered for Reservations.com role of official Irish Coffee taste tester, you can submit a 150 - 200-word description of your favorite holiday beverage and setting on their website.

The lucky winning applicant will be tasked with visiting eight bars and restaurants in New York City between December 10 - 17 in order to taste test each establishment’s Irish Coffee and critique each establishment's atmosphere. Afterward, the taste tester will write up a 300-word review of each of the Irish Coffees and rank their favorites.

The official taste tester will receive $500 for the drinks and food, and a travel and writing stipend of $1,500 for a total of $2,000. Their final reviews will also be published on Reservations.com.

Of course, applicants must be at least 21 years old.
