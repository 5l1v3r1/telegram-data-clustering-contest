Keith Urban to headline 2020 Rockin’ River Music festival in Merritt

25 Nov 2019, 18:01 by Karen Edwards

The Rockin’ River Festival 2020 has announced country music star Keith Urban will headline the country music festival in Merritt this summer.

Other country music stars scheduled to peform include John Pardi, Travis Tritt, Chris Janson, Kenny Hess, Chase Rice, and Aaron Pritchett among others.

The 2020 festival will run from July 30 to August 2 in Merritt.
