New ‘GTFO’ Trailer Explains “The Rundown”

25 Nov 2019, 18:09 by Mike Wilson

10 Chambers Collective are still working away at their squad-based FPS GTFO, which is scheduled to hit Steam Early Access before the year is out. To get the hype train going, the devs have released a new trailer showing what’s referred to as “The Rundown”.

The Rundown is basically the menu you’ll get upon launching GTFO, showing what your objectives are for the mission. As you know, in GTFO, players are captives in an underground complex who are tasked with a work order every time they log into the game. There’ll be different tiers, and the deeper you go into the underground complex, the harder the missions will be.

Additionally, there’ll be a countdown timer for the Rundown. After the countdown ends, the objectives will be replaced by new ones, leaving the previous Rundown objectives unavailable. The goal is to provide a dynamic environment for players while also focusing on a common set of missions at a time.
