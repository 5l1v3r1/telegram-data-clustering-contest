‘Give me my car’ — Mr Eazi shades Headies over 2016 ‘Next Rated’ award gift

25 Nov 2019, 18:46 by James Ojo

Oluwatosin Ajibade, Nigerian Afrobeats singer better known as Mr Eazi, has aimed a dig at organisers of the Headies Awards for allegedly not giving him a car he won at the 2016 edition of its annual event.

The award-winning singer took to his Twitter page to express his annoyance while reacting to a post by HipTv, a media partner and major organiser of the Headies.

The post ordinarily seemed to be an appreciation of Mr Eazi’s outfit and that of Temi Otedola, his girlfriend.

“@jtofashion x @mreazi Feeling like Tony Montana and Elvira both in that new #JTOxOC collection,” HipTv wrote.

But rather than reciprocate the gesture, the singer disclosed a shocking revelation in his response.

His reaction suggests he might not have received the car he won in 2016 where he emerged the ‘Next Rated’ artiste — beating the likes of Humblesmith, Aramide, and Ycee to clinch the coveted award.

“Instead make u gimme my car u dey here dey tweet couple goals,” the singer replied.
