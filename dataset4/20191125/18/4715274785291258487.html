FCF Show

25 Nov 2019, 18:03 by Ben Moses Ebreo

A worker of FCF Minerals Corp. shows off his firefighting skills during a competition in the 66th Philippine Mine Safety and Environment Association conference at Camp John Hay in Baguio City.

Earlier, other FCF workers joined other mining companies in a parade on the streets of Baguio, which has hosted the annual gathering over the last half-century.
