Teen 'tied girl down and raped her after she withdrew consent during foreplay'

25 Nov 2019, 18:08

A 19 year-old boy tied a 17 year-old girl down and raped her after she withdrew consent for sex during foreplay, police say.

Jamar Brandon Dulaney was arrested Friday, three months after he and his alleged victim had initiated consensual sexual contact at his home in Montgomery, Alabama.

The girl, who has not been named to protect her identity, then changed her mind during the August 7 encounter, it is alleged.

That prompted Dulaney to tie her hands and legs together and have sex with her against her will, court papers say.

Further details of how the attack ended and how Dulaney’s victim escaped have not yet been made public.

It is unclear whether Dulaney’s alleged victim reported him to police at the time, or waited until just before his arrest last week.

He was apprehended by police within hours of the warrant being issued.
