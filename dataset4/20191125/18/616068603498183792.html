Brakes hit on speed limit

25 Nov 2019, 18:00 by John Lawless

The decision to change the speed limit on Commonage Road came to a grinding halt at council this afternoon.

The motion to upgrade the speed to 70 km/h was voted down, and the limit will remain at 60 km/h.

"If the speed limit is 70 km/h then you're probably going to end up driving 80 km/h," said Councillor Brian Quiring. "then you're just screaming coming into town and that's not safe."

Quiring had support from other councillors on that side of the argument.

"This increase would make absolutely no sense," said Councillor Kari Gares.

There was a petition with over 300 signatures in favour of the increase, but the motion was ultimately turned down.
