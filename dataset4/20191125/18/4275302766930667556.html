PICTURES: Big crowds flock to Angus town centres for Christmas lights extravaganzas

It was the big switch-on on Sunday night as revellers in Carnoustie and Monifieth came out to enjoy local Christmas lights displays.

25 Nov 2019, 18:16 by Reporter

In Monifieth there was some festive-themed entertainment from the Tolmie Highland Dance School.

In Carnoustie there was entertainment for the crowds as they waited for Santa’s arrival, with girls from the Angus Centre for Performing Arts putting on a display.
