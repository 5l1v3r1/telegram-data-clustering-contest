The 11 players linked with Derby County ahead of January transfer window

Latest Derby County transfer news | A regularly updated list of all the players linked with a move to Pride Park ahead of the fast-approaching January transfer window

25 Nov 2019, 18:01 by Chris Watson, Mark Wakefield, Jamie Kemble

Derby County have been linked with a host of players as the January transfer window edges ever closer.

The Rams have already secured the services of former England star Wayne Rooney, who is joining the club in the new year.

And manager Phillip Cocu could look to further bolster his squad during the first month of 2020.

Cocu's side are currently in 11th place in the Sky Bet Championship table following their 1-0 victory over Preston North End.

Will there be more new arrivals at Pride Park to give the team a lift going into the second half of the campaign?

We have put together a regularly updated list of all the players tipped to sign for Derby.

Take a look below...

Scott Sinclair

Derby County are being linked with a January move for Celtic winger Scott Sinclair.

The Rams were said to be considering a deadline-day move for Sinclair back in August, before they secured a season-long loan deal for Bristol City's Jamie Paterson.

Sinclair remained at the Scottish champions, for whom he scored 17 goals in 55 appearances last season.

But the ex-Swansea City and Aston Villa man is yet to start a League game for Neil Lennon's side in 2019-20 - and Football Insider are now reporting that the Rams are considering a January deal for the 30-year-old.

Sinclair is out of contract at Celtic in the summer.

Luke McCowan

Derby County have been linked with a move for young Scottish attacking midfielder Luke McCowan.

The Rams and Sky Bet Championship rivals Cardiff City are closely monitoring the Ayr United prospect, according to Football Insider.

The report also claims that an unnamed Premier League is keeping tabs on the player.

Full story here

Cameron Dawson

A number of Championship clubs including Derby County are keeping an eye on Sheffield Wednesday goalkeeper Cameron Dawson.

Blackburn Rovers and Preston North End are also believed to be keen, as are Scottish club Rangers, according to the Sheffield Star.

Dawson's current contract is up at the end of this season.

Full story here

Sam Cosgrove

Sam Cosgrove has been catching the eye with his goalscoring exploits in Scotland this season.

The 22-year-old has netted 15 goals in only 17 appearances for Scottish Premiership side Aberdeen, to add to his tally of 21 from last term.

That form is said to have attracted the attention of several Championship clubs, including Derby County, Middlesbrough, Brentford and Charlton.

It has not all been plain sailing for Cosgrove, though.

Full story here

Kyle Dempsey

Derby County have been linked with Fleetwood Town central midfielder Kyle Dempsey.

The Rams have been keeping a close eye on the 24-year-old, according to Football Insider.

Fellow Sky Bet Championship clubs Swansea City and Huddersfield Town are also said to be interested.

Citing a Fleetwood source, the report says the three clubs have been impressed by the player's recent form and have been "making regular trips" to watch him in action.

Full story here

Uche Ikpeazu

Derby County have been linked with a move for Hearts striker Uche Ikpeazu.

According to West London Sport, Queens Park Rangers are monitoring the 24-year-old.

It is reported Derby's Championship rivals Wigan Athletic and Bristol City have scouted the former Reading man along with Phillip Cocu's side.

Ikpeazu, who began his career at Reading, has scored ten goals for Hearts since his arrival from Cambridge United in 2018 on a free transfer.

Full story here

Jamie Hamilton

Derby County have been linked with a move for young Scottish defender Jamie Hamilton.

The highly-rated centre-back has come through the youth ranks at Hamilton Academical and has been identified as one for the future.

According to the Daily Maill, Derby, along with four Premier League clubs, have been taking a closer look at the 17-year-old.

Hamilton came to the fore last season, when he impressed for Scotland Under-17s and also in the UEFA Youth League. He has since been called up to Scotland’s under-19 squad.

Ivan Toney and Marcus Maddison

Peterborough United striker Ivan Toney says he does not listen to transfer speculation amid reports that he is set to be part of a Derby County double transfer swoop.

Toney, who has scored eight goals in 11 appearances this season, has been linked with January a move to Pride Park along with midfielder Marcus Maddison.

The duo have proved instrumental in the Posh's recent climb up the table in League One, both notching goals on a run that saw Darren Ferguson's side beat MK Dons, Sunderland, Northampton and Rochdale without conceding a single goal and scoring 15 along the way.

Full story here

Lyndon Dykes

Derby County are amongst at least half-a-dozen Championship clubs being linked with a move for Livingston frontman Lyndon Dykes.

The Australian-born 24-year-old has netted five goals in 14 games for the Scottish Premiership club, and that has sparked interest south of the border just a few weeks before the opening of the January transfer window..

According to a TeamTalk “exclusive”, the Rams, Middlesbrough, Cardiff, Swansea, Bristol City and Blackburn have all made enquiries for the 6ft 2in forward, who can play anywhere across the front-line.

The website claims all six Championship clubs have been to see Dykes in action in recent weeks, and says Livingston are ready to sell in January.

Full story here.

Armand Gnanduillet

Derby County are being linked with a move for Blackpool striker Armand Gnanduillet ahead of the January transfer window.

According to Football Insider , the Rams are casting an eye over the striker as they prepare to strengthen their forward options during the upcoming transfer window.

The report also adds that Derby are in the market for reinforcements in the striker department in the New Year, and have identified Gnanduillet as a potential option.

Clubs across England have also reportedly shown an interest in the 27-year-old’s services, with the player’s current deal at Bloomfield Road due to expire next summer.

It is believed that Gnanduillet could be available for a cut-price fee in January as the Tangerines look to cash in on the player.
