NEW DELHI: India's information technology minister Ravi Shankar Prasad said on Monday Apple Inc would expand its operations in the country and the iPhone maker's supplier Salcomp would make components at a plant near Chennai in southern India.

Salcomp will invest 20 billion rupees (US$278.67 million) over the next five years to make chargers and other components at the plant and the move will also create 10,000 jobs, Prasad said.
