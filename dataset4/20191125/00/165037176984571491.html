Juventus back in for Juan Miranda

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Juan Miranda is having a complicated year at Schalke 04. He is on loan from Barcelona and hasn't even played for them. Barca plan to recall him, but Juventus want to sign him. According to 'La Gazzetta dello Sport', Juve have reactivated their interest in the Spanish fullback.

Barcelona could recall Juan Miranda in January due to the complicated situation he is experiencing at Schalke. The fullback was loaned to the German club to get game time, but he hasn't even played for them.

It was recently made known that Barca were planning to recall the player and loan him to Mallorca, but his future might not necessarily lie in Spain.

According to 'La Gazzetta dello Sport', Juventus are back on the scene and are following Juan Miranda's situation closely. They were already an option in January and they have reappeared as the Andalusian fullback looks for a solution to his future.

Then Juventus didn't have a merely passing interest. The Italians included Miranda on their agenda after his performance in the U19 Euros. Now they have reactivated their interest in him.
