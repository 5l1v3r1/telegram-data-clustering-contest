Man who allegedly murdered wife last Wednesday charged by Police

25 Nov 2019 by Vijay Narayan

A man who allegedly murdered his 32-year-old wife last Wednesday has been charged by Police.

The woman was found dead in her home in Lautoka last week.

The man has been charged with one count of murder and one count of breach of the Domestic Violence Restraining Order.

He will be produced in the Lautoka Magistrates Court today.
