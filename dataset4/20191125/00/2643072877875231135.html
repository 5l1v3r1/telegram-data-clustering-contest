Nollywood Actor, Saidi Balogun Loses Mother To Death

25 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Saidi Balogun’s Mother Is Dead

Popular Nollywood star actor and producer, Saidi Balogun has announced the death of his mother.

Mr. Balogun, also known as Walata, announced the sad news of his mother’s passing away on Monday via his Instagram account.

He shared a picture of himself and his mother and captioned it “Good night Mama mi Jolade Omo Olomu Aperan. Rest in peace.”
