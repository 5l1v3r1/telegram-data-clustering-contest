‘Governor With Swag’ – Onoja Praises Yahaya Bello Of Kogi State

25 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Onoja Eulogizes Bello

The Deputy Governor of Kogi State, Edward Onoja, has heaped praises on the Executive Governor of Kogi State, Yahaya Bello.

In a tweet on Sunday, Onoja described Yahaya Bello as a “governor with swag”.

The ‘swag’ description was only one of the many things Onoja said to eulogize the Governor. He also described him as a “boss, leader and friend”.

And he also added in his tweet: “My boss, my leader my friend. Governor with swag! Who want tamper there?”

Naija News reports until last month, Onoja was the chief of staff to Bello but he emerged the deputy governor of the state following the controversial removal of Simon Achuba by the Kogi State house of assembly.
