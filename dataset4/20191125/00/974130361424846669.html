21 Of The Best Images From The Weekend's Club GAA Action

25 Nov 2019 by PJ Browne

Sportsfile photographers were at grounds across the country to capture the highs and lows of the weekend's club GAA action.

All three of the All-Ireland ladies football club championships finals took place as did the Munster hurling final, the Connacht football final and the Leinster football semi-finals.
