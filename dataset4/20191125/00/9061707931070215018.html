Tansley Ladies raise money for the DLRAA

Group spent 12 months fund-raising

25 Nov 2019 by Jane McFarlane

Tansley Ladies Group donated £1,580 to Derbyshire, Leicestershire & Rutland Air Ambulance (DLRAA) and £1,580 to The Helen Trust

Diana Dakin, chairperson of the group 2018/2019, presented Richard Fletcher, Derbyshire volunteer community representative for DLRAA, with the cheque.

The money was raised over 12 months, launching with a coffee morning and quickly followed with a host of activities which included open evenings, a carol evening, soup bread and cheese lunch, quiz nights, hot cross bun coffee morning. They even had a member of the group complete a sponsored run.

Some other members did an evening walk with a twist and it was finished in August with a Mega Tombola at the Gate in Tansley.
