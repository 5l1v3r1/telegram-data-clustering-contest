Motorcycle crash near Southern Motorway

25 Nov 2019 by Elena McPhee

Police have been called to a motorbike crash near a Dunedin Southern Motorway off-ramp.

A spokeswoman said the person was injured, and it was understood to be a one-vehicle crash.

The crash happened near Abbotsford on Dunedin's Main South Rd.

Emergency services were called about 7.45am.
