Yemi Osinbajo And Wife, Dolapo Mark 30th Wedding Anniversary

25 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

The Vice President of Nigeria, Yemi Osinbajo and his wife, Dolapo, are marking their 30th wedding anniversary today.

The wife of the vice president took to her Instagram account to share a picture and captioned her post, ‘Hand in hand.’

Mrs Dolapo Osinbajo wrote, “Hand in hand on the 25th November 1989. It’s our 30th wedding anniversary to the glory of God.”

She also posted some pictures down the memory lane.
