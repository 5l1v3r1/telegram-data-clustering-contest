Netherlands Queen Maxima to arrive in Pakistan today | Pakistan - Geo.tv

25 Nov 2019 by Web Desk

ISLAMABAD: Netherlands Queen Maxima will arrive in Pakistan today for a three-day visit as the UN Secretary General’s Special Advocate for Inclusive Finance for Development.

During the visit, the queen will meet President Dr Arif Alvi and Prime Minister Imran Khan besides holding talks with a range of stakeholders from the public and private sectors. 

It may be added that inclusive finance for development is one of the key priorities of the government. Queen Maxima will also attend the launch of the Micro Payment Gateway, an initiative of the State Bank of Pakistan. 

The gateway reducing cost of small payments and boosting digital transactions to benefit people and promote financial inclusion. The Queen has been the UN Secretary General’s Special Advocate for Inclusive Finance for Development since 2009.

She is engaged worldwide in promoting access of individuals and enterprises to financial services at a reasonable cost with the aim of improving economic and social development opportunities. Queen Maxima had earlier visited Pakistan in February 2016.
