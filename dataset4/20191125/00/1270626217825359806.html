Luke Nasetava further remanded in custody for alleged murder of his 34-year-old wife

25 Nov 2019 by Semi Turaga

34-year-old Luke Nasetava who allegedly murdered his 34-year-old wife in Walu Bay on the 31st of August this year, has been further remanded in custody.

He was produced before High Court Judge, Justice Salesi Temo.

Police had earlier said that according to initial information gathered, the woman was allegedly stabbed after allegedly refusing to talk to the husband.

The case will be called again on Friday.
