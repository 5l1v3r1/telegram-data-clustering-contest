Eden Hazard admits he wants Kylian Mbappe at Real Madrid

Eden Hazard has admitted that he wants Paris Saint-Germain forward Kylian Mbappe to line up alongside him at Real Madrid, claiming that he expects him to become of the greatest footballers of all time.

25 Nov 2019, 00:05 by Andrew Sinclair

The 20-year-old has missed a number of games this season with injury but he has still scored five goals and recorded three assists in eight league games for the French champions.

In an interview with Le Parisien, Hazard praised Mbappe's quality and expressed his desire for the two to play for the same side, saying: "In a few years, he will certainly be the best player in the world.

"This title, there are a lot of players who fight for it, but if he continues like this, Kylian will be one of the greatest footballers in the history of the world.

"A footballer constantly dreams of playing alongside the best.

"If tomorrow I could bring Kylian to Real Madrid, I would try. But not only is it not just me who can decide this, on top of that I don't even think I would be asked for my opinion."
