Tamannaah's opens on Rajni- Kamal political entry! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

25 Nov 2019

Actress Tamannaah was seen recently in Vishal starrer Action directed by Sundar C, and is now acting in few movies in Tamil and Telugu. The actress had recently been to an event in Trichy, where she opened up on Rajni - Kamal's political entry.

Tamannaah expressed that she's been enjoying the films of Rajnikanth and Kamal Haasan as a fan, and considers them as Gods of cinema. Saying that nobody will refuse an opportunity to act with them, she said that she's ready to act with both the stars.

Tamannaah also said about their political entry that both Rajni and Kamal want to do something good for the nation and the people, and that their political entry is a good thing. When questioned on her marriage, Tamannaah told media to find a bridegroom for her and added that she's ready to marry a person from Tamil Nadu.
