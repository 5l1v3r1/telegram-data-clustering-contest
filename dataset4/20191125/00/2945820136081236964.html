Fernandez joins EG 0,0 Marc VDS for 2020

The 22-year-old Spaniard replaces the seat vacated by Alex Marquez in Moto2™ for the upcoming campaign

25 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

After enjoying a fantastic season in which he scored five podiums and three victories, the 22-year-old Spanish rider Augusto Fernandez joins EG 0,0 Marc VDS to join Sam Lowes for the 2020 season. 

Despite missing two rounds through injury last season, Fernandez shone to finish P5 in the overall standings, collecting 207 points with his three victories coming in Assen, Silverstone and Misano. Now, one of the sport's brightest prospects joins the reigning Moto2™ Champions to replace MotoGP™-bound Alex Marquez.
