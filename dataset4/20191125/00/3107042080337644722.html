Lawnmower comes off trailer, injures one

25 Nov 2019

One person has been injured after a ride-on lawnmower fell off a trailer on State Highway 1 in Christchurch on this morning.

The incident about 9.35am also caused the south-bound lane on the Western Belfast Bypass to close while the clean up took place.'

St John spokesman Gerard Campbell said one person was treated for minor injuries at the scene.

Contractors were on site to clear the lane, but road users are advised to take extra care and expect delays.
