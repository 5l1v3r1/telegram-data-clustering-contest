We Are Not After Auditor–General – Gov’t

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The government has said that they do not have plans or any intention to remove Auditor-General, Mr. Daniel Domelevo from office.

According to the Deputy Information Minister, they are fully cooperating with the Auditor-General to support his works.

Speaking on Okay FM’s 'Ade Akye Abia' program, Deputy Information Minister, Pius Enam Hadzide explained that the opposition NDC has plans to pitch the Auditor-General against the government and as such want the public to believe that government is after him.

"It is very scandalous to suggest that the government is witch-hunting the Auditor-General," he said, adding that it is a silly suggestion that should not be encouraged.

He assured Ghanaians that the government has no intention to hound the Auditor-General out of office.
