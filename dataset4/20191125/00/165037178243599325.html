La Liga top scorers 19-20

25 Nov 2019 by Aidan Shearer

Benzema is currently top of the table after his excellent start to the season. However, he is closely followed by Leo Messi who is 1 goal behind.

After matchday 14, Benzema finds himself top with 10 goals. The Frenchman has had a fine start to the season and got his 10th as Real Madrid came from behind to beat Real Sociedad 3-1.

He is closely followed by Messi, Morón and Moreno who all have 8 goals. Messi has been on fire since he came back from injury. He even got a hat-trick against Celta Vigo and will be hoping to add to his tally in next week's match against Atlético Madrid.
