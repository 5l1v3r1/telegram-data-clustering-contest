Photos: Ghanaian Man Weds Three Women On The Same Day

25 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Naija News earlier reported that a Ghanaian man, simply known as Hafiz was set to wed three women on the same day.

The wedding ceremony of Hafiz and his wives Yidana Sikena, Ali Karima and Yakubu Huzaima was held over the weekend at Walewale in the North East Region of Ghana.

Naija News learnt that the event lasted for about an hour and a half.
