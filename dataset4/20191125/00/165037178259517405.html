"Neymar or Mbappe? I'm not going to choose"

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Zinedine Zidane spoke in the pre-match press conference before Real Madrid's match with PSG. The Frenchman spoke about his team's situation and the ins and outs of a busy week at the Bernabeu.

Zinedine Zidane analysed the moment in which Real Madrid come into the match against Paris Saint-Germain. After getting a few wins, the atmosphere at the Bernabeu seemed different to that of a month back, before playing against Galatasaray, although the French coach is not getting carried away. 

"I know feelings can change again. It depends on our capacity to play good matches, play well, not concede. We know what we must do and personally, I'm happy because I think my team is better now," Zidane commented. 

Ahead of him, a PSG side who arrive as always, with plenty to talk about. Questioned on whether he likes Neymar or Mbappe more, he did not give his opinion. "I'm not going to choose because tomorrow those who are going to play will play. They are all ready and we will see. We are all ready and we are going to go with the best players. 

He did not want to give more details on Mbappe: "Nothing, Mbappe comes tomorrow as a rival. What most bothers me is what we will do, we will see what they do. We know the player that he is, the importance in to his team and that is."

On the rumours of Neymar being on the bench, Zidane is not reading too much into that: "Honestly, for me it doesn't change anything because I think Neymar is going to play and so is Mbappe, so that doesn't change anything. We are ready to play against any team and against any player. PSG are having a magnificent season and we are going to see a great match."

Gareth Bale trained again with Real Madrid after returning from his controversial return for Wales. On the footballer's situation, Zidane treated it normally: "You have to ask Bale directly. He is with us and he is ready to play a game of football."
