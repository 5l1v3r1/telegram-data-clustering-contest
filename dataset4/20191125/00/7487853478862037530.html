WSL Roundup: Chelsea Stay Top After Huge Win - But Man City & Arsenal Keep Pressure on

25 Nov 2019 by Jamie Spencer

​Chelsea kept their place at the top of the Women’s Super League and remain the only still unbeaten team so far this season after thrashing Birmingham 6-0 away from home on Sunday in the latest round of top flight fixtures.

Just two points separated third place Chelsea and fourth place Birmingham at the end of last season, but the visitors made short work of the hosts on their trip to the west Midlands and had raced into their 6-0 lead shortly after the hour mark.
