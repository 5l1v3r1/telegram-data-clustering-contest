GASTONIA, N.C. (WBTV) - A person has died after a car ran into the back of a tractor-trailer in Gastonia on Sunday.

The wreck occurred around 6:50 p.m. at the intersection of East Ozark Avenue and Pear Street, near Interstate 85, Gaston County Communications said.

One person was pronounced dead at the scene, according to Gaston Emergency Medical Services.
