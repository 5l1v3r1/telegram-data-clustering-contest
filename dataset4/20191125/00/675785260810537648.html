Fury after drunken naked tourist drives scooter into swimming pool in Bali

The man, known as Raymond RB, 23, posted the viral video to his Instagram account, followed by a second which showed the scooter being hauled out of the pool in Bali, Indonesia

25 Nov 2019, 00:40 by Brendan McFadden

An naked Australian tourist who drove a scooter into a swimming pool in Bali with a pal insists they "were not disrespecting locals".

The driver, known only as Raymond RB, 23, posted a video of the bizarre stunt on his Instagram page, followed by a second which showed the scooter being hauled out of the pool.

When the video, which is going viral, was shared to the Bell Tower Times Facebook page, it received more than 2,400 comments, with many Balinese complaining that the pair had been disrespectful.

Explaining his actions to 9News, he said: "My reason for doing it was an alcohol-influenced situation."

He dismissed claims there would be public outrage over his behaviour.

Raymond also insisted he owns the scooter because he spends four months in Bali every year.

He said: "I'm not too bothered by people saying I'm immature.
