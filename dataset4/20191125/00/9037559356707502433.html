Pak Navy invites applications to join force as doctor | Pakistan - Geo.tv

25 Nov 2019 by Web Desk

Pakistan Navy has invited applications to join the force as doctor through (M) Cadet Scheme (5th Batch).

Candidates studying in the 4th (Male and Females) and 5th (Males only) year of MBBS in government medical colleges recognized by the PMDC/relevant authority as on 1st July 20 are eligible to apply.

The candidates' age should be 26 years as of 1st July 2020.

Candidates may register themselves by visiting Pakistan Navy official website or nearest PM Recruitment and Selection Centre.

Applications sent directly to Recruitment Directorate at Naval Headquarters will not be entertained.

Candidates can apply from November 24 to December 08, 2019.
