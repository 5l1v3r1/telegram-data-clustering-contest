Chris Smalling Follows in David Beckham's Footsteps With Outstanding Serie A Showings

25 Nov 2019 by Tom Gott

​All around the football world, one of the biggest surprises of the season has been Chris Smalling's phenomenal form on loan with Roma.

The centre-back, who had spent years being ridiculed with Manchester United, has exploded onto the scene in the Serie A. Smalling has been incredible defensively and played a starring role in his side's 3-0 win over Brescia on Sunday.
