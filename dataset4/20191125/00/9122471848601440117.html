Dez Bryant Has Telling Comment On Cowboys-Patriots Game

25 Nov 2019, 00:10 by Andrew Holleran

Former Dallas Cowboys wide receiver Dez Bryant has weighed in on this evening’s game against the New England Patriots. His comment is probably going to sit well with much of the fan base.

The Cowboys are trailing the Patriots, 13-9, late in the fourth quarter. It’s been a mistake-filled game for Dallas, which has made some puzzling coaching decisions, too.

Bryant believes one team has the better players, while the other has the better coaches. You probably don’t need more than one guess here…

“What I see from the game so far…. Dallas has the better players but New England has the better coaches and game plan,” he tweeted.
