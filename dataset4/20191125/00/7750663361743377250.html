West Ham United to offload Carlos Sanchez in January?

West Ham United are reportedly considering letting midfielder Carlos Sanchez leave the club on loan in January.

25 Nov 2019, 00:12 by Andrew Sinclair

The 33-year-old came off the bench against Tottenham Hotspur on Saturday; his first league appearance for the Hammers since late August and one that took his total minutes played in the top flight this season to 40.

Sanchez's contract at the Olympic Stadium expires at the end of the season and now the Daily Mail reports that West Ham would be prepared to entertain loan offers for him in the January transfer window.
