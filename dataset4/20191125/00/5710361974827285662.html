Palo Alto misses quarterly revenue estimates

25 Nov 2019 by Reuters

(Reuters) - Cybersecurity firm Palo Alto Networks Inc reported lower-than-expected quarterly revenue due to lower demand for its products, sending its shares down about 8% in extended trading on Monday.

Revenue rose 18% to $771.9 million in the first quarter ended Oct. 31, but missed analysts' expectations of $767.8 million, according to IBES data from Refinitiv.

Net loss widened to $59.6 million, or 62 cents per share, from $38.3 million, or 41 cents per share, a year earlier.
