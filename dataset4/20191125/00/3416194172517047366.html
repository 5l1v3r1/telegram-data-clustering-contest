Camila Cabello Is 'An Unbelievable Human Being,' Shawn Mendes Says After AMAs 2019

25 Nov 2019, 00:31

Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello are such a cute couple and they were among the winners at the 2019 American Music Awards on Sunday (November 24) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

After the couple won the Collaboration of the Year award for “Senorita,” Shawn took to Instagram to send some praise to Camila.

“@amas thank you everyone thank you so much!! ❤️ also @camila_cabello i admire you so much you unbelievable human being thank you for being you ❤️❤️ we love u guys,” he captioned the below photo of them on stage together.
