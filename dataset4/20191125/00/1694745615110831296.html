If You Thought Lizzo's Tiny Purse Made a Statement, Wait Till You See Her Crystal Nails

25 Nov 2019, 00:59 by Danielle Jackson

While we were busy focusing all of our attention on Lizzo's tiny white Valentino purse at the American Music Awards on Sunday, she had another statement-making detail in her ensemble: her nails! The "Juice" singer hit the red carpet at the award show sporting a crystal manicure that basically made her look like the gem emoji come to life.

According to one of her recent Instagram Stories, the look was created by NYC-based nail artist Eri Ishizu, who's been Lizzo's go-to manicurist as of late. To get the look, Ishizu painted Lizzo's nails with a glitter polish from OPI before decking out her fingers in Swarovski crystals. Read ahead to get a closer look at Lizzo's manicure.
