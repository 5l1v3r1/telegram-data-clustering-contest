Lele Pons Dishes On Who She's Crushing On Right Now

25 Nov 2019, 00:58

Lele Pons steps out in a spacey white suit for her tarte cosmetics x Lele Pons collection meet and greet held at Macy’s Herald Square in New York City over the weekend.

The 23-year-old creator and singer recently spoke with ET and opened up about her dating life.

Lele admitted that she finds it hard to make time for a personal life, because she loves working so much.

However, “there is someone I like but I can’t say that we’re together or not,” she shared.

Lele continues, “I just see him from afar, but I don’t know. It’s just, the thing is, I fall in love very quickly, but I also fall out of love very quickly too.”

So what kind of partner is Lele on the lookout for? “He has to make me laugh, he has to be someone ambitious, someone that I admire, someone that has a lot of values, is a family person, supportive.”
