Ciara Rocks NINE Different Looks To Host American Music Awards...

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

She'd promised to include 'some really cool moments of fashion' for her hosting gig at Sunday's American Music Awards in Los Angeles.

And Ciara did not disappoint as she shoehorned her fabulous figure into a total of nine different outfits during the live telecast from the Microsoft Theater.
The 34-year-old singer also treated the audience to a performance of her brand new single Melanin and even included her two young children in the show.
