LVMH reaches agreement to buy Tiffany for $135 per share

25 Nov 2019 by Reuters

PARIS (Reuters) - French luxury goods group LVMH said on Monday it has reached a definitive agreement to buy U.S. jewelry chain Tiffany & Co for $135 per share in an all-cash offer, in a $16.2 billion deal.

The Louis Vuitton owner also said in a statement it expects to close the transaction in the middle of next year.
