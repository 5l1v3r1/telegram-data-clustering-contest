Stream Kanye West’s New Opera ‘Nebuchadnezzar’ Right Here

25 Nov 2019 by Caitlin Kelley

Kanye West is debuting his first opera, Nebuchadnezzar at LA’s Hollywood Bowl on Nov. 24. A Tidal-exclusive broadcast is streaming live at 4 p.m. PST. Subscribers will have access to the Vanessa Beecroft-directed show, which features music by the Sunday Service, Peter Collins, and Infinity’s Song. Tidal is promoting a 60-day free trial for those who aren’t signed up to the music service.

A press release explained the origins of the opera:

West conceived of this opera as an expression of his commitment to both his faith and ever-expanding musical palette. Nebuchadnezzar brings together elements from different worlds, including opera, fine art, modern dance, and gospel music, to create an innovative performance structure. West’s sound is the bridge between these artforms.
