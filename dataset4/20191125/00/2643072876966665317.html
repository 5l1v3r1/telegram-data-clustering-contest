Full List Of Winners From The 2019 America Music Awards

25 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Taylor Swift Wins Big At AMA

The 47th edition of the America Music Award held on Sunday, November 24 in Los Angeles.

The show was hosted by Ciara and featured performances from Taylor Swift, Eilish, Shania Twain, and many others.

Naija News reports that with her win on the night, Taylor Swift who had five wins, has now overtaken Michael Jackson as the artist with the most AMAs ever.
