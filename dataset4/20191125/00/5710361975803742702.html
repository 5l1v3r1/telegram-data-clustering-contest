EBay to sell ticketing unit StubHub for $4.05 billion

25 Nov 2019 by Reuters

(Reuters) - EBay Inc (O:EBAY) will sell its ticketing unit, StubHub, for $4.05 billion to ticket reseller Viagogo Ltd in an all-cash deal, the companies said on Monday.

Shares of eBay were up more than 5% before the opening bell.

The deal is expected to close by the end of the first quarter of 2020.
