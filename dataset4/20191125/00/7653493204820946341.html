Amazon Black Friday Sneak Peek & Starlight Donation

25 Nov 2019 by Elliot Nash

You won’t have to wait until Friday to get your Black Friday discounts as Amazon Australia is giving customers a sneak peek at its early holiday deals with $5 from every toy purchased going to Starlight Children’s Foundation.

Amazon Australia is offering customers a massive selection of early holiday deals, including Amazon Music, Audible and Amazon Books subscriptions.

Not to mention the discounts on Amazon devices, fashion, electronics, toys, household, kitchen and home furnishings, garden, video, pets, sports and outdoors, beauty products, health and personal care, baby products and products from startups and entrepreneurs through the Amazon Launchpad.

Amazon Australia will be bringing new deals all day, every day with online retailer promising thousands of ‘amazing deals’ across every shopping category.

For Amazon Prime members you will be able to access Lightning deals 30 minutes ahead of time.

Amazon Australia’s Black Friday shopping event begins at midnight Friday 29 November until Sunday 11:59 PM, with Cyber Monday kicking off just one minute later until 11:59 PM on Monday.

US Black Friday and Cyber Sunday deals will also be available starting 7 PM Thursday 28 November and Cyber Monda Deals until 6:59 PM 3 December.

Better yet, amongst the deals and discounts, Amazon is also bringing smiles to the Starlight Children’s Foundation with $5 donated for every toy purchased during Black Friday and Cyber Mondy shopping events, on top of the $40,000 worth of toys, games and crafts already being given.

In fact, when looking into the fine print, Amazon.com.au has guaranteed a minimum total donation of $50,000!
