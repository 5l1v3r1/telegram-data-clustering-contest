Three clubs after Cédric Soares

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The RB Cédric Soares' contract at Southampton ends in the summer and there are several clubs that are after him. According to 'Teamtalk', Arsenal, West Ham and Wolves are fighting for the signature of the 28-year-old Portuguese.

Cédric Soares's contract ends this season and it doesn't seem that he's going to renew it. Faced with this situation, Southampton are willing to accept an offer for him in January in order to get some money for him.

The RB has become a wanted player in the market. At 28, he has played 33 times for Portugal and his price will be less because of his contract situation.

According to 'Teamtalk', up to 3 Premier League clubs want him: Arsenal, West Ham and Wolves. All of them are willing to negotiate in January and sign him, or if there's no deal, sign a pre-contract for next season.

Cédric arrived at Southampton in the summer of 2015 from Sporting Lisbon. It was also rumoured that they would be interested in getting him back after 4 years in England interrupted by a brief loan spell at Inter.
