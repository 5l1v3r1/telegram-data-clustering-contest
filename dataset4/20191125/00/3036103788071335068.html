New Takealot Black Friday tech deals unveiled

25 Nov 2019 by Jamie McKane

Takealot’s 2019 Blue Dot Sale takes place from 29 November to 3 December, but the company has already unveiled a number of deals as part of its Early Access Sale.

Takealot’s Blue Dot Sale is the biggest Black Friday sale in South Africa, offering shoppers up to 60% on technology products, TVs, appliances, toys, and gaming products.

During the Blue Dot Sale, new deals will also be added daily and will cover the Black Friday weekend, Cyber Monday, and Takealot Tuesday.

In the build-up to its Blue Dot Sale, Takealot is holding an Early Access Sale which will run from 24-28 November.

Deals available during the Takealot Blue Dot Early Access sale will offer great discounts that the retailer said will not be beaten over the Black Friday period.

These deals will also change on a daily basis, which means customers should take advantage of attractive discounts while they remain available.

The company launched app-only Early Access Blue Dot Sale specials on Sunday 24 November 2019, and it has now launched new deals available through its website.
