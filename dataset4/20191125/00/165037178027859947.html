Leeds United join the race to sign Brewster on loan

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

According to the 'Daily Star', Leeds United have joined the race to sign Liverpool striker Rhian Brewster on loan.

The Liverpool youth academy graduate is one of the big sensations in British football. At just 19, he is being tipped to make a difference in a not so distant future.

The striker has already made his Liverpool debut this season and it is normal to see him train with the first team.
