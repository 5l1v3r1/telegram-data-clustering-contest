The Hollow Road in the Curragh Camp closed at times this week

Facilitating training exercises

25 Nov 2019 by Leader Reporter

The Hollow Road through the Curragh Camp will be closed temporarily at times this week.

According to the Department of Defence, the closures will take place between 10am and 2pm, daily, until Friday, 29 November.

The temporary closure is to facilitate Defence Forces training exercises in the area.
