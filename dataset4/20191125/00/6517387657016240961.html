Lewis Capaldi on course for third Number 1 on the Official Irish Singles Chart with Before You Go

Can Lewis cap off an incredible year with another chart-topper?

25 Nov 2019 by Jack White

Lewis Capaldi could top off an incredible 2019 by claiming his third Number 1 on the Official Irish Singles Chart.

Tallying downloads and streams from across the weekend, the Scotsman's latest track Before You Go is at Number 1 on today's chart update, some 900 units ahead the closest competition at the halfway point of the week.

Before You Go debuted at Number 11 on last Friday's chart after less than three days on sale. Earlier in 2019, Someone You Loved spent six weeks leading the Top 50, plus Hold Me While You Wait spent one week at the top in May, making Ireland the only country that the song peaked at Number 1.
