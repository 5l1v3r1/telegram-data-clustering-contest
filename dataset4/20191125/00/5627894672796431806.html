Man dies in confrontation with sheriffs deputies

25 Nov 2019

MORONGO VALLEY  (INT)  Sheriffs deputies say they were defending themselves when they shot and killed a Morongo Valley man outside his home last Tuesday.

Several hours earlier, deputies had responded to reports that Jacob Cato, 34, had been shooting at another home on Cactus Flower Road.

At one point, Cato fired shots into the air as a sheriffs helicopter flew overhead. Cato was overheard on a video he recorded saying those were warning shots to get that helicopter out of this airspace.

When Cato walked out of his home, shot at deputies but wounded none. Officers returned fire and died at the scene.
