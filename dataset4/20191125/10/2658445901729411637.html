BREAKING: N33b fraud: I'm not guilty, says Mompha

25 Nov 2019, 10:57 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

By Robert Egbe

An Internet celebrity, Ismaila Mustapha, also known as Mompha, has denied involvement in a N33billion money laundering charge filed against him by the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC).

Mompha pleaded not guilty to a 14-count charge bordering on the offences following his arraignment at the Federal High Court, Ikoyi, Lagos by the EFCC.
