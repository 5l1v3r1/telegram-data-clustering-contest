BT: 11 tracks that blew my mind - "I heard this and literally pulled the car over and wept"

Last month, Grammy-nominated, multi-platinum recording artist BT’s new ambient set Between Here and You (Black Hole Recordings) was released and quickly soared to  number 1 on the iTunes Electronic chart. 

The companion album, the experimental Everything You’re Searching for is on the Other Side of Fear, is due out 13 December, also via Black Hole Recordings.

Groundbreaking electronic music pioneer BT has written and produced for such widely-acclaimed and diverse artists as Death Cab for Cutie, Peter Gabriel, Howard Jones, Tiesto, Depeche Mode, David Bowie, Armin Van Buuren, Sting, NSYNC, Blake Lewis, The Roots, Madonna, Britney Spears and more.  

We asked BT to give us the 10 tracks that blew his mind. He gave us 11!  

1. Manuel Göttsching - E2 to E4
