They’ll snatch my man – Emelia Brobbey on why she keeps her relationship private

25 Nov 2019, 10:51 by MyNewsGH

Actress, Emelia Brobbey has been reported to have said she will not make public the man she’s dating for fear colleagues may snatch him.

According to her, Ghanaian ladies are sharp and will chase him immediately she discloses his identity, the reason everything is still under wraps

She made this known when she spoke to Sammy Kay Media in an interview.

The actress said she’s not in search of a man as many perceive.

“I will not say whether I am dating or not because the two has its own consequences but I am not searching.”
