Kelowna's most wanted - Crime Stoppers

25 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Crime Stoppers

Crime Stoppers is asking the public’s assistance in locating the following female who is wanted on a province-wide warrant in effect as of November 25, 2019.

Call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-TIPS or go to www.crimestoppers.net.
