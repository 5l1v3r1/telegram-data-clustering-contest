EuroLeague All-Decade Nominee: Paulius Jankunas

25 Nov 2019, 10:06

Well-deserved honor for Zalgiris Kaunas’ captain, Paulius Jankunas.

By Eurohoops Team/ info@eurohoops.net

The list of nominees for the 2010-2020 All-Decade Team now includes Paulius Jankunas, EuroLeague announced on Monday. Counting 314 games, 200 this decade with Zalgiris Kaunas, the 35-year-old forward center has demonstrated determination and professionalism in every aspect of his career.
