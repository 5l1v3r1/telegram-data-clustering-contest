The I'm-A-Celebrity-meets-Love-Island trial was horrific

25 Nov 2019, 10:05

I’m A Celebrity stars James Haskell, Roman Kemp,Cliff Parisi and Adele Roberts got stuck in to a grim Love Island style trial.

In Sunday’s episode the celebs were gathered around the fire pit, just like in the ITV2 dating show.

But instead of deciding who to couple up with, the celebrities had 90 seconds to pass pig testicles to each other using only their mouths.

The fact they were sat in a hot tub did little to make things any better as the foursome got stuck in.

Luckily for us I’m A Celebrity have released high quality shots of the disgusting challenge, capturing the moment the stars grimace as a slippery, wet pig testicle gets handed off into their mouths.

James wanted to make sure his target landed as he passed the ball to Roman – the pictures are quite something – while Cliff and Adele also gave it their best shot.

If that wasn’t enough for the campers, Andrew Maxwell had to drink a blended cockroach milkshake, and Caitlyn was put through the ringer again as she had to knock back a blended vomit fruit concoction.

Ian Wright mean while was given a milkshake made of the other bit of the pig’s unmentionables in order to win his roast dinner. That was the slime and cockroaches pie, by the way.

Even after watching all that, the Love Island recoupling ceremonies are still just a tiny little bit worse (they’re so tense!).

Also in the episode, it was a bad day for James Haskell as he snapped at Ant and Dec, clashed with Kate Garraway about chivalry and fell victim to a seemingly ‘impossible’ trial with Roman Kemp.
