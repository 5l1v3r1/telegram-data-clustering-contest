WWE's Ricochet Dons Schway Batman Beyond Ring Gear for Survivor Series

25 Nov 2019, 10:11 by C.K. Stewart

WWE's resident superhero Ricochet debuted new comic-book-inspired gear at Sunday night's Survivor Series pay-per-view, as the high flying superstar joined Team Raw in black-and-red gear harkening back to Terry McGinnis' iconic Batman Beyond costume.

Ricochet (real name Trevor Mann) joined WWE Raw stars Randy Orton, Seth Rollins, Kevin Owens and Drew McIntyre to compete for the red brand in the annual men's Survivor Series match. Unfortunately, the gear didn't help Ricochet get the win, as he was eliminated via pinfall by Team SmackDown's Baron Corbin.

This isn't the first time Ricochet sported superheroic styles in the ring. At October's Crown Jewel PPV, he dressed as My Hero Academia's All Might, and he wore Nightwing-inspired gear in a United States title match against AJ Styles in August. His passion for comics has been on display since his days in NXT, where he paid homage to Spider-Man/Miles Morales at January's TakeOver: Phoenix.

Ultimately, Team SmackDown emerged victorious in the men's Survivor Series match, with captain Roman Reigns emerging as the last man standing after eliminating NXT breakout star Keith Lee as the final elimination of the match.
