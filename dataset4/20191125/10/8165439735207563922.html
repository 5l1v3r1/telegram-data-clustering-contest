What Happened On I'm A Celeb? Weekend Catch Up

25 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Tuathla Lucey

Here's all the action you missed over the last few days of I'm A Celeb.

Your votes sent Jacqueline down to Ol' Dingo Town to enter the Snake Hotel - and she chooses Myles to join her. While the Rak-Su star finds himself banished to a trunk full of snakes, Jacqueline is left to make her way through the hotel alone... almost. But dozens of sneaky snakes don't stop her finding all the stars - and the keys to free Myles.
