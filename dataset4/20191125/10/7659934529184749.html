King Salman Receives Kingdom’s Mufti, Religious Scholars in Riyadh

25 Nov 2019, 10:30

Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz received in Riyadh on Monday the Kingdom’s Grand Mufti Sheikh Abdulaziz Al al-Sheikh and several religious scholars, reported the Saudi Press Agency.

Several princes and citizens also attended the reception to convey their greetings to King Salman.
