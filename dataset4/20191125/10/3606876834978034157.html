Akothee’s message to Janet Mbugua, Lillian Muli and Zari from her from Hospital Bed

25 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Dennis Milimo

Quick Recovery Madam Boss

Singer Esther Akoth aka Akothee sent a special message to people who reached out to check on her, stating that she had learnt a lot while in her Hospital bed.

The mother of five, who is still admitted at Karen Hospital, took to social media to thank media personalities Janet Mbugua, Lillian Muli and Zari Hassan for being there for her.

“Thank you @officialjanetmbugua for keeping up with me @lilian_muli love felt , @a_opondo mom, I already know who will quickly type on their wall rest in peace and those who will actually feel the pain and make a step to come and witness , my fans are life I love you to death, thanks for praying with me , I learnt a lot on this bed ,I will be a good girl !( think ) @zarithebosslady thank you mommy ,your words of encouragement was out of this world, your mind quickly went to our children, my daughter cried when. She saw your text messages God bless mama Tee,” reads part of Akothee’s post.

Hardship allowance

The Rollam hit maker added that her manager Nelly Oaks deserves hardship allowance for the things he has been through, to ensure she is back no her feet.

“NELSON OYUGI OGANGO DESERVE HARDSHIP ALLOWANCE @nellyoaks for the fans who came ,thank you sooo much even though you dint get to see me ,I felt your love big time, continue with the spirit be blessed. My INSURANCE COMPANY IS LIFE ,Who are you insured with ?. I fought the battle came out alive, thank you @the_karen_hospital for the wonderful hospitality ,day 5, I know I gave you hell, but your staffs are Gold , very patient, Thank you for those who made it to me ,you are my family My children @veshashaillan @rue.baby ,@peptang_ke @weston_hotel @silverstoneairservices @jalangoo, my cousin Sheril and July, visit me more, thank you @oketch_patrick15 I felt your love,” added Akothee.

Akothee was hospitalized on Thursday last week, days after jetting back in the country from Dubai for the one Africa music festival.

‘So if you didn’t work late yesterday, I would have died in the bathtub slaying on Instagram live. I have to agree I am tired ‘. Doctors result: fatigue 90% flu 10%," said Akothee.
