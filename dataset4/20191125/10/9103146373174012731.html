Uganda news in pictures - November 25

25 Nov 2019, 10:13 by Admin

New Vision brings you a series of pictures taken by our journalists as the news unfolds.
EDDIE SSEJJOBA’S LENS AT SHERATON HOTEL
 

A fundraising drive for Quiin Abenakyo Foundation hosted by the former Miss Uganda Abenakyo, is underway at Sheraton Kampala.

The ceremony has been graced by President Yoweri Kaguta Museveni, Speaker Rebecca Kadaga, and the Nnabagereka of Buganda Queen Sylvia Nagginda among other dignitaries.

REGINAH NALUNGA’S LENS IN WANDEGEYA 

Standstill between the Police and traders at the Kampala Capital City Authority (KCCA) market in Wandegya market this morning. 

The traders were protesting the leadership of their chairperson Jonathan Gitta.
