First look: brand-new Beach by FIVE opens on Dubai’s Palm Jumeirah

Expect a swish beach club with a Mediterranean menu

25 Nov 2019, 10:34 by Amy Mathieson

There’s a brand-new beach club opening on Dubai’s the Palm– and it looks set to be an ace new addition to the party scene.

Beach by FIVE is now open at popular party hotel FIVE Palm Jumeirah Dubai and Time Out Dubai's been down for a look around. And it’s safe to say it’s looking super.

Spanning 150m, the beach features double sunloungers, cabanas and a stunning Mediterranean deck for dining. But that’s not all – as you’d expect from the trendy hotel, there’s an extremely Instagrammable glass-sided pool starring a one-of-a-kind chandelier.

There’s also a range of signature drinks to sip while you soak up the sun.

Weekday access is Dhs150, which is full redeemable on food and drink, while weekends will set you back Dhs200, which you’ll get back on food and drinks.

Beach by FIVE has a restaurant menu packed with food from Spain, Greece, Turkey and the Middle East with dishes including shrimp or veggie tacos, prawn pitas, chicken souvlaki, salads, mezze, Greek salad, burratina, hummus, grilled prawns, sea break, sushi and more. Plus desserts including churros, lemon tacos or crème gourmand.

Fancy a day lazing on the beach? It’s time to put on your smartest swimwear, polish those sunnies and saunter along to Beach by FIVE this weekend.
