CGPSC state services exam notification 2019 released: Check eligibility, vacancy details, salary

CGPSC state services exam notification 2019: online applications will begin from noon of December 6 and will be closed by midnight of January 4, 2020. A total of 199 posts are to be filled through this recruitment process.

25 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Careers Desk

CGPSC state services exam notification 2019: The Chhattisgarh Public Service Commission (CGPSC), Raipur has released the notification for the state services exam 2019 at its official website, psc.cg.gov.in. The online applications will begin from noon of December 6 and will be closed by midnight of January 4, 2020. A total of 199 posts are to be filled through this recruitment process.

To be eligible for the jobs, candidates will have to pass three-tier exams. The preliminary exams will be conducted on February 9, 2020 in three shifts while the CGPSC Mains will be conducted from June 17 to 19, 2020. Those who clear both will be called for the interview round. For some posts, the physical fitness test will also be conducted.

CGPSC state services exam notification 2019: Eligibility

Education: Candidates should have at least a graduate-level degree to be eligible to apply.

Age: Applicant should be at least 21 years of age while the upper age limit is capped at 28 years. For reserved category candidates, there is relaxation in the upper age limit. Age will be calculated as on January 1, 2020.

CGPSC state services exam notification 2019: Fee

Candidates will have to pay Rs 400 as registration fee. For candidates who are natives of Chhatisgarh or reserved category candidates, the fee is Rs 300.
