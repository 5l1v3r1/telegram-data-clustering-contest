25 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Koimoi.com Team

Superstar Mahesh Babu’s fans just can’t keep their excitement following the teaser release of their star’s upcoming release Sarileru Neekevvaru. The teaser which is on record-breaking spree went on to become the most viewed Telugu teaser in 24 hours after its release with a whopping 18 million views.

The latest news related to Sarileru Neekevvaru is, fans of Mahesh Babu erected a huge 81 feet tall cut of the superstar outside the famous Sudarshan 35mm theatre in Hyderabad post the teaser release.

The pictures of the cut out have already been gaining all the attention on the internet, all thanks to Mahesh fans.

Talking about the film, the makers and star cast are busy these days wrapping up the final portions of the action drama. The film is expected to be wrapped up by December.

Sarileru Neekevvaru has gorgeous Rashmika Mandanna opposite Mahesh in lead.

The film also stars veteran actors Vijayashanti and Prakashraj in pivotal roles.

The Mahesh Babu starrer is being helmed by filmmaker Anil Ravipudi and it is being produced under Sri Venkateswara Creations. The music for the film is been composed of Devi Sri Prasad aka DSP.

Sarileru Neekevvaru will hit big screens on 11th January 2020. So far the title track, teaser, and posters of the film have been very well received by the audience.
