LTO sets road safety seminar

25 Nov 2019, 10:06 by Christian D. Supnad

To further ensure the safety of the public, the Land Transportation Office in Bataan will conduct a road safety seminar on December 6.

This was disclosed by LTO chief Ronld Dabu who said that a lot of participants have been invited to attend the said event.

Regarded in high esteem in the agency and a lecturer, Dabu said that among those invited to attend are senior high school students, two motorcycle federations in Bataan, Tricycle Operators and Drivers Association (TODA), and various government agencies, among others.

Invited guests are LTO Director Eduardo C. de Guzman, Gov Abet S. Garcia, Cong. Joet S Garcia, Mayor Charlie Pizarro and other top government officials.

The launching of the safety seminar came following a series of fatal road accidents along Roman expresseway, caused mostly by human error, it was learned.

Alarmed by the high volume of road mishap victims, Gov. Garcia has installed lights along the highway, initiating road widenings with the support of the Dept of Public Works.
