Pune: One dead, 17 others injured as MSRTC bus overturns near Shindewadi village

The bus was on its way to Sangli from Pune when the incident happened at Shindewadi village near Katraj

25 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Chaitraly Deshmukh

In a major accident that took place near Pune, a state transport bus fell into 50 feet deep quarry killing one person, leaving another critically injured, while 17 passengers suffered minor injuries. The MSRTC 'Shivshahi' bus was on its way to Sangli from Pune when the incident happened at Shindewadi village near Katraj, some 10 kilometres from Pune.
