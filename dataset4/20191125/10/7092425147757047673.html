Maurice Robinson pleads guilty to plotting to assist illegal immigration

25 Nov 2019, 10:23 by James Cox

Northern Irish lorry driver Maurice Robinson, 25, who is accused over the deaths of 39 migrants found in the back of a lorry in Essex, has pleaded guilty to plotting to assist illegal immigration.

Robinson entered the guilty plea at the Old Bailey in London via video link from Belmarsh Prison in east London.

The County Armagh native did not enter a plea to 41 other charges, including 39 counts of manslaughter.
