Ayr dinner lady hangs up her apron after 26 years

Belmont Academy’s Katherine McMillan waved goodbye to her colleagues after 26 years as a catering assistant at the school.

25 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Ayrshire Post, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/ayrshire-post/

A dinner lady is hanging up her apron after over two decades serving youngsters their school lunches.

Belmont Academy’s Katherine McMillan waved goodbye to her colleagues after 26 years as a catering assistant at the school.

The fun loving lunch lady is looking forward to some well deserved time to herself, putting her feet up and running after the grandkids.

Katherine celebrated her retirement with her work colleagues who threw her a party to wave her off.
