Ahead Of Brahmastra Shoot Alia Bhatt And Ranbir Kapoor Get A Warm Manali Welcome By The Locals

25 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Oshine Koul

This morning, Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor were spotted at the airport as they dashed off to Manali for Brahmastra's shoot schedule. Now pictures of them receiving a warm welcome in Manali are going viral. Check them out

After completing the shooting schedules in Bulgaria and Varanasi, lovebirds Alia Bhatt, and Ranbir Kapoor have now dashed off to Manali to complete the last leg of their upcoming supernatural film, Brahmastra. The film, which is scheduled to release in Summer next year, also stars an ensemble cast of Mouni Roy, Nagarjuna and Amitabh Bachchan and is helmed to be a trilogy. Alia and Ranbir took to an early morning flight to jet off to Manali and we are sure the lovebirds are going to get a lot of personal time as well.

As soon as the couple landed, pictures of them receiving a warm welcome in Manali are going viral on the internet. As seen in the pictures, Alia and Ranbir can be seen holding flower bouquets while wearing the tradition Pahadi multi-colour cap also called Pahari Topi. Ranbir and Alia can be seen posing happily for the cameras with the localities.
