Star Wars Jedi Fallen Order terrarium guide: Find all seeds

25 Nov 2019, 10:34 by Steve Wright

Fancy a bit of gardening?

Star Wars Jedi Fallen Order is out now, and one of its Achievments or Trophies tasks young Jedi Knights with finding terrarium seeds in order to help out pilot Greez. This guide will show you how to do just that!

All up, there are 10 seeds that not only have to be planted in the terrarium on the Mantis, but given enough time to grow. Doing all that will net you the Green Thumb Achievement or Trophy.

Seed locations are below, listed in the order they appear in the terrarium.

Fetherfern seed location

This is on Bogano in the Fractured Plain. Look for a plant with purple flowers.

Kalpi seed location

This is also on Bogano in the Subterranean Refuge. Look to the lower ground of a hub area and head towards a plant with a large stalk.

Dreamwort seed location

Dreamwort is found on Zeffo in the Crash Site area. This is on the large area in the middle, on the coast just off a wrecked ship. Watch out for the baddies before you try to collect this plant with yellow flowers.

Royal Fuzz seed location

This is on Zeffo in the Weathered Monument area. This is near a chest that you’ll need BD-1 to slice. Look for a purple flowered plant.

Gillypod seed location

This is at Zeffo in the Windswept Ruins. Look for a red flowering plant at the top of a raised stone area.

Milk Grass seed location

This is on Kashyyyk in the Gloomroot Hollow area. Look for a giant plant with thick, green stalks.

Mushbloom seed location

This is on Kashyyyk in the Origin Tree area. From a mediation point, climb up a nearby ledge and look to yellow, bulbous plants at the base of one of those green plants you can use as a trampoline.

Bonshyyyr seed location

This is on Kashyyyk in the Imperial Refinery. You’ll find this near a mediation spot where Stormtroopers are fighting Giant Spiders. This is a small green plant nestled against some dead ferns.

Bleeding Gut seed location

The remaining two plants are found on Dathomir. The first is in the Swamp of Sacrifice. Head towards the bottom of the big central hub area and follow a red vine to a giant red flower.

Mushling seed location

The final plant is also on Dathomir in the Swamp of Sacrifice. This one’s in the same relative area; look to the wall near a cliff that’s populated with Nightsister pods.
