Deco receives over 800 complaints against packaged goods

25 Nov 2019, 10:39

Consumer watchdog Deco has received over 800 complaints against packaged goods.

The '#Plasticoamais' (too much plastic) campaign, launched by DECO, has brought about many consumer complaints, the majority relating to excessive plastic packaging on fresh produce and groceries.

The initiative, developed by DECO and launched in June, has garnered a total of 835 consumer complaints
