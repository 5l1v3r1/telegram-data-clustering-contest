Sani Emmanuel receives N1.4m from Ogenyi Onazi for medical treatment

25 Nov 2019, 10:03

- Sani Emmanuel has been given the sum of N1.4m by Ogenyi Onazi for his surgery

- The former Nigerian Under 17 star confirmed this on his official Instagram page

- Emmanuel was forced to quit football because of the injury he suffered years back

Super Eagles midfielder Ogenyi Onazi has given the sum of N1.4m to former Nigeria youth international Sani Emmanuel for his medical treatment in the hospital.

Sani Emmanuel was among the players who played for Nigeria at the 2009 FIFA Under 17 World Cup before he was forced to quit football due to injury.

And since he got injured, Sani Emmanuel has been battling for survival on how to resuscitate his career and also how to undergo surgery.

He recently solicited for help on social media where many Nigerians felt bad about his current ordeal.
