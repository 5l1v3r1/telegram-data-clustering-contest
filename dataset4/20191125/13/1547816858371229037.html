Kasautii Zindagii Kay star Erica Fernandes took to Instagram and shared a few fun clips, giving her fans a glimpse into the madness and fun she has on sets. The videos have got us laughing out loud, check it out!

Kasautii Zindagii Kay star Erica Fernandes is one of the most fun and entertaining television personalities to follow on Instagram. Right from her photoshoots to her personal life to her postcard-worthy vacation pictures, the actress shares it all on her Instagram. Her recent posts though, are a few fun clips, giving her fans a glimpse into the madness and fun she has on sets. The BTS videos have got us laughing out loud, and are unmissable. Check them out!

Erica Fernandes took to Instagram to share a hilarious video of her dancing around to songs such as Bijli girane, Ghoomar, Bala, Kamariya on set. She wrote,"Welcome to my world , my madness and a sneak peek of Erica on set.” Well, the video is sure to leave you in splits. Ekta Kapoor commented, “I wannna have wat ur having.” Apart from this video, Erica also shared another video from the set of Kasautii Zindagii Kay, in which she comes to shoot for a scene, but forgets to wear her ghagra and her baby bump, making everyone ROFL. She captioned it as, “Directing the director this time. P.s :- my entry on sets now a days".
