Manchester Derby News: Sergio Aguero to miss Manchester United derby

25 Nov 2019, 13:47 by Tanish Chachra

Manchester Derby News: Sergio Aguero reportedly set to miss the match against Manchester United on the 15th game-week of the Premier League.

Sergio Aguero set to miss Manchester derby scheduled on the 15th game-week of the Premier League, in the press conference attended by Pep Guardiola ahead of the champions league match tomorrow, he has informed that the Argentine will be out for few games.

He has not specified the timeline of his recovery but by his words, it is almost confirmed that Aguero is not going to be in the lineups of the Citizens for a while.

“We will miss Sergio. We have had some problems with injuries but we will wait for him and meanwhile, we have [Jesus] and Sterling who can play in that position. We will live without him for a while.” said Guardiola at the press conference.

“He’ll be out for a few games, a few weeks. Exactly I don’t know, it’s his thigh.  He’ll miss the derby.” he further added.
