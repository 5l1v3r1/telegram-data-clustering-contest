Kim Kardashian says she can relate to Meghan Markle’s need for privacy

“I can definitely empathize with their need for having a secure, safe place"

25 Nov 2019, 13:57 by Sarah Magliocco

Kim Kardashian has revealed that she empathizes with Meghan Markle.

The reality star said that she understands the Duchess of Sussex’s push to have more privacy from the glare of the royal spotlight.

However, Kim told Aussie show The Sunday Project that “no one” could fully understand the pressures of being a royal.

Kim said: “I think everyone has their own journey.”

“I think their journey is extremely personal, with what [Harry’s] mother experienced and how her life was so affected by the spotlight and by paparazzi.”

“I don’t think anyone can really understand what that’s like except for them.”

However, Kim added that having children changes a public figure’s attitude to paparazzi and privacy entirely.

“But I think as I’m older and as I have kids, and what I would want for them is just the safest place.”

“I can definitely empathize with their need for having a secure, safe place and taking time for themselves and having privacy when they need it.”

Meghan has come under scrutiny following a recent documentary she filmed with husband Prince Harry, in which she admitted to struggling in the royal spotlight.
