Three's Black Friday Pixel 4 Deal Has a Huge Data Cap at an Unmissable Price

25 Nov 2019, 13:30 by Shabana Arif

If you've been waiting for a price drop to kick in for Google's Pixel 4 and 4 XL, you're in luck with Three's Black Friday deals.

The Pixel 4 only just launched, and while you wouldn't usually expect to see it discounted just yet, the gods of Black Friday have intervened over at Three to bring you a sweet little deal on the Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL. When the handset launched on the network, the 64GB Pixel 4 had a £49 upfront fee and was available on three tariffs; a 30GB plan that cost £47 a month, and two unlimited plans costing £49 and £69 a month.

Meanwhile the Pixel 4 XL was available on five tariffs. Four had an upfront cost of £49 with data ranging from 4GB at £48 a month, up to unlimited data for £56 a month, while the unlimited deal scratched the initial payment for the phone and charged £75 a month instead.

With Three's Black Friday deal, you can pick up a 64GB Pixel 4 with 100GB of data, and unlimited minutes and text on a 24 month contract for £40 a month and an upfront cost of £29. The Pixel 4 XL is on offer for £40 a month with the same upfront cost of £29. And you can knock an extra fiver off your monthly payment by setting up a recurring payment method like direct debit, so you're looking at potential monthly costs of £35 for the Pixel 4, and £40 for the Pixel 4 XL.

100GB is a huge amount of data, so if you guzzle it up to the point where you need this much, this might be just the deal for you.

It's only the start of Black Friday week - not to be confused with Black Friday month which has been raging for what feels like forever now - so keep up to speed with all of the offers that have dropped and will be dropping over on our Black Friday hub. [TechRadar]
