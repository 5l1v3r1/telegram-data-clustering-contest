Strictly's Shirley Ballas looks smitten with hunky toyboy as they dance in street

Strictly Come Dancing judge Shirley Ballas danced around and held hands with her new younger lover

25 Nov 2019, 13:12 by Verity Sulway, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/verity-sulway/

Strictly Come Dancing judge Shirley Ballas appears to have a new lease of life.

She moved from LA to England, had her breast implants removed after they caused health issues, and has a younger partner.

Shirley, 59, has been dating actor Daniel Taylor, 47, since last year.

And judging by new photos of the loved up couple turning on the Christmas lights in Darlington, they've never been happier together.

Shirley and Daniel were pictured beaming, holding hands and dancing in the street and on stage together.

They couldn't keep their hands off each other as they posed for photos with fans.

Her happiness is all the more touching given Shirley had a rocky road to finding love.

The professional dancer was married to Sammy Stopford from 1980 until 1984, and she married second husband Corky Ballas a year later, but they divorced in 2007.

Shirley told Mirror Online her relationship with current beau Daniel is far more relaxed than with her ex husband.

When asked what she had learnt about herself from being with Daniel, she replied: "That it’s OK to be just me. I don’t always have to be on point. I don’t feel anxious any more, as he calms me.

That’s the opposite to anything I’ve had before, so it’s been quite revealing.

"I don’t have to be dolled up 24/7.

My ex-husband had a thing where I couldn’t get into bed if my toe polish was chipped. It’s nice that I don’t have to do that."

Last month, Shirley had her DD breast implants removed after nurses told her they obscured their view during a mammogram.

Shirley had a family history of breast cancer, and so decided to have them removed.
