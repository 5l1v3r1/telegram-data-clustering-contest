Amazon Touts Big Google Pixel 4 And Pixel 4 XL Discounts For Black Friday

25 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Shane McGlaun

Amazon has unveiled some new Black Friday deals this week with big discounts on Google's latest smartphones - thePixel 4 and the Pixel 4 XL. These two smartphones haven't been out that long, and enthusiasts who want a pure Android experience might want to check out the deals.

Amazon is offering the Pixel 4 with 64GB of storage space for $599, a discount of $200 off the normal $799 price tag. The version with 128GB of storage is on sale for the same $200 discount making it $699. All three available colors are eligible for the deal, including the Just Black, Clearly White, and Oh So Orange. The orange color is only available in the 64GB version.

  • Google Pixel 4 Smartphone, 64GB - $200 off at $599

Pixel 4 fans wanting the larger screen Pixel 4 XL can get that smartphone with a similar discount of $200 off the regular $899 list price for a 64GB version making it $699. The 64GB device is offered in Clearly White, Just Black, and Oh So Orange. Those wanting the version with 128GB of storage can get that one in Clearly White or Just Black for $799, which is $200 off the regular price of $999.

  • Google Pixel 4 XL, 64GB - $200 off at $699

Amazon has been having impressive discounts leading up to Black Friday later this week. On Saturday, it launched some big discounts on Amazon hardware like the Echo Dot, Fire tablets, and Ring doorbell. On Thursday, Amazon announced some deals on Fire TV devices, Echo, Echo Show, and several other products.
