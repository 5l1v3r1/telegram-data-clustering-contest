Lidl's Black Friday deals - TVs, XBox One S and Fitbit among products on offer

The XBox One S with three games could be the cheapest deal for the console this year

25 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Molly Holt, Emma Flanagan

As one of the biggest budget supermarkets in the UK, Lidl are known for their bargains.

So it should come as no surprise the retailer is fully on board with one of the biggest days for discounts, Black Friday.

But while you would be forgiven for thinking their offers would be limited to mince pies and mystery items from the middle aisle the supermarket is actually focusing its attention on electricals, reports The Mirror.

And they are slashing the prices on top brands as well including XBox, Fitbit and Russell Hobbs.

The best deal of the lot is surely their Xbox bundle, which nets you the Xbox One S Digital 1TB (without a disk drive), along with three digital games for £129, down from £199.99.

Elsewhere online, the same Xbox One S console - minus any additional games - will still set you back a hefty  £189.99 at Amazon,£199 at John Lewis  and  £160 on eBay.

But being Lidl, there's likely to be limited stock, with customers warned that they'll be limited to one per person, so we recommend getting to the story very early on the day it goes live, on Black Friday itself (Friday 29 November).

Unveiled in the retailer's weekly magazine and spotted by bargain hunters on deals forum  HotUKDeals  , Lidl has revealed that it will be offering price cuts on everything from Xbox consoles, to coffee machines, to electric toothbrushes, hoovers and even Prosecco. We've picked out some more of our favourite deals from Lidl's Black Friday sale event below, along with how much they cost elsewhere.

  • Bosch Tassimo Happy Single Serve Coffee Machine,  £34.99 at Lidl -  same price on Amazon
  • Russell Hobbs 6l Slow Cooker, £29.99 at Lidl -  £44.99 on eBay
  • George Foreman 10 person grill, £29.99 at Lidl -  £35 on AO.com
  • Fitbit Inspire, £49.99 at Lidl -  £59.49 on Amazon
  • Oral-B Pro 650 3D electric toothbrush, £19.99 at Lidl -  £23.89 on Amazon
