Jose Mourinho responds to rumours he wants Tottenham to sign Zlatan Ibrahimovic

25 Nov 2019, 13:56

Jose Mourinho has dismissed rumours he wants his new club Tottenham to sign Zlatan Ibrahimovic.

Prolific striker Ibrahimovic worked under Mourinho at Manchester United and has been linked with a move to Spurs since the Portuguese manager took over from Mauricio Pochettino.

But Mourinho says it does not make sense for Tottenham to move for Ibrahimovic as the north Londoners already have ‘top striker’ Harry Kane.

‘I would say we have one of the top strikers in England,’ Mourinho said ahead of Tottenham’s Champions League clash against Olympiacos.

‘It doesn’t make any sense of a striker of Zlatan’s dimension to come to a club where we have Harry Kane.’
