Kate Henshaw shares message she got from 'worried fan' about getting married

25 Nov 2019, 13:58

- Nigerian actress Kate Henshaw recently shared her encounter with a concerned follower on social media

- The actress posted a screenshot of the fan’s message to her on her Instagram DM channel

- Henshaw’s fan had expressed concern because she is yet to get married

Much loved Nollywood actress Kate Henshaw recently took to micro blogging platform, Twitter, to recount her experience with an Instagram follower, who sent a message to her direct messaging channel, on the photo-sharing app.

From the screenshot shared by the actress, the individual in his message had expressed concern because the movie star is yet to tie the knot. He noted that Henshaw is a beautiful woman and he would have loved to help her settle down.
