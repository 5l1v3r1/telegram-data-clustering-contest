Russian, Syrian forces pound foreign jihadist positions in northeast Latakia: video

25 Nov 2019, 13:50 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (5:30 P.M.) – The Russian and Syrian armed forces have recently stepped up their attacks against the jihadist rebels in northeastern Latakia.

In a video released by the Sputnik News Agency, the Syrian and Russian forces can be seen pounding the positions of the Turkestan Islamic Party (TIP) near the key town of Kabani.
