Tourist tries clicking selfie during Venice floods, falls into water. Viral video has 5 lakh views

A hilarious video of a tourist shot during the Venice floods has gone viral on social media.

25 Nov 2019, 13:08

A hilarious video of a tourist in Venice - shot during the floods in the Italian city - has gone crazy viral on social media. Since the beginning of November, Venice battled difficult conditions as the city was hit by its highest tide in more than 50 years.

The four-second clip has been shared on Twitter by StanceGrounded and has garnered over five lakh views so far. Of course, because it is funny. The clip features a man wading through a flooded area in Venice with a selfie stick and guess what happened next?
