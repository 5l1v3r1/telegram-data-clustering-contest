PartyNextDoor Teases Release Date For His Third Studio Album

25 Nov 2019, 13:55 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Are you ready for a full-length effort from the R&B star?

(AllHipHop News) Fans of PartyNextDoor finally got new music from the singer-songwriter. A two-pack with "The News" and "Loyal" Featuring Drake dropped on Friday.

Following those records hitting DSPs, people began asking when PND would release his next body of work. He partially answered that question on Twitter last night.

"Album in January," tweeted PartyNextDoor at 9:14 pm. The OVO Sound recording artist did not provide an exact date for the upcoming LP.

Party has let loose two official studio albums so far. PartyNextDoor Two came out in 2013. That was followed by PartyNextDoor 3 in 2016. He has also released the PartyNextDoor, Colours, Colours 2, and Seven Days EPs.
