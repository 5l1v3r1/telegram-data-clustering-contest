One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 is coming to the PC on March 27th

25 Nov 2019, 13:33 by John Papadopoulos

Bandai Namco has announced that One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 will be coming to the PC on March 27th, 2020. In order to celebrate this announcement, the publisher has also released a new trailer that you can find below.

One Piece: Pirate Warriors 4 will combine the characters from the One Piece series with the furious action of the Dynasty Warriors franchise. Therefore, you can expect to be fighting numerous enemies on screen (in a typical Dynasty Warrior way).

As the publisher noted, the game will feature an original ending as the manga is still running.
