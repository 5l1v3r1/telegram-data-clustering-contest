Expedia launches Black Friday flight and holiday sale

The holiday website has cut the price of flights and holidays - and revealed the date of the full Black Friday sale

25 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Neil Bennett

Holiday company Expedia have today thrown their hat into the Black Friday ring with the announcement on big savings on hotels and holidays around the world.

Prices on selected hotels have dropped by up to 60% while other discounts have also been placed on flights, car hires, package holidays and holiday activities.

The Expedia Black Friday sale includes 40% off trips to Dubai, 65% off holidays in Paris and discounts on Bangkok, Phuket, San Francisco and Bophut.

The holiday site has also launched flight deals including London to Singapore from £567 and flights to Dubai from £327. Other trips include London to Toronto from £344 and London to Shanghai return from £865.

However, Expedia says the holiday deals are only a warm up to the main event, when massive discounts will be revealed at 9am on Black Friday.

Expedia are also encouraging customers to create an Expedia account and download their app – available now on Google Play and the App Store – where further, exclusive Black Friday offers are being promised.

Deals available now on the Expedia website will run through until 'Cyber Monday' next week on December 2.

Elsewhere, one of Expedia's rivals in the holiday booking site market, Skyscanner, have also today launched their Black Friday sale.
