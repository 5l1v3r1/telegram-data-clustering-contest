Woman struck at Kitchener crosswalk, suffers serious injuries

Regional Police say a woman was crossing on the crosswalk at Union and Belmont over the weekend

25 Nov 2019, 13:18 by Mark Pare

Regional Police are on the hunt for a vehicle that fled the scene of a pedestrian crash Saturday evening in Kitchener.

Police say around 7:15 p.m., a "dark coloured four-door sedan with a modified spoiler" was going westbound on Union and made a left turn onto Belmont Avenue West.

However, the vehicle struck a woman that was crossing the street on a crosswalk at the time.

The vehicle fled the scene at a high rate of speed, police say.

The woman suffered serious, non-life threatening injuries and was taken to hospital.

If you have any information, or witnessed the crash, you're asked to call Regional Police or Crime Stoppers.
