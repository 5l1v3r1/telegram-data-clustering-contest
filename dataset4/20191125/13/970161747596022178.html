How the EastEnders characters voted in the Brexit referendum - and why

25 Nov 2019, 13:46

EastEnders has never been shy at tentatively dipping its toe into the world of politics, with the show inserting topical scenes seeing characters discuss major events such as the Brexit referendum. But while we got quite a stilted and balanced conversation at the Queen Vic after the vote to leave was announced, how would the characters have actually voted?

Comedian Sooz Kempner (@SoozUK) has delved through the roster of characters to have a look at just that – and her observations are utterly on point. From Dot Branning’s (June Brown) confusion over racial issues and Grant Mitchell (Ross Kemp) Brexiting from abroad through to Sonia Fowler (Natalie Cassidy) constantly reminding everyone that she is an NHS nurse and Stacey Fowler (Lacey Turner) voting remain because pizzas come from Italy, just about every response you’ve ever heard any vote here is represented by the Walford constituents.
