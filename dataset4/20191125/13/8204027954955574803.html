Police looking for man who stole woman’s car at gunpoint at the Dayton Mall

25 Nov 2019, 13:17 by Breaking News Staff

DAYTON — Investigators are looking for a man who took a woman’s car at gunpoint in the parking lot of the Dayton Mall Saturday afternoon, according to dispatch.

﻿>> Live Doppler 7 Radar | INTERACTIVE

﻿Police were contacted by witnesses when they saw the incident occur.

The 911 call indicates that the woman dropped her purse and when she went to pick it up the man was there with a gun demanding the keys to her black Buick Regal.

The woman told police she does not know the man who took her car.
