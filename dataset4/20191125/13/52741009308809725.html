Kitchener man arrested after robberies involving food delivery drivers

Police have received 11 reports of robberies since May 2019, all targeting food delivery drivers in Kitchener

25 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Mark Pare

A 22-year old Kitchener man has been arrested in connection to a slew of robberies in Kitchener.

Regional Police say they have received 11 reports of robbery since May 2019, all of them targeting people delivering food in the Kitchener area.

Police made the arrest sometime on Friday.

No name has been released.
