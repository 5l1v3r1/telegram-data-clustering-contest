UAE football team arrives in Doha for 24th Arabian Gulf Cup

25 Nov 2019, 13:19

Doha: The UAE national football team arrived in the Qatari capital to participate in the 24th edition of the Arabian Gulf Cup, which will be held from November 26 to December 8.

The UAE team received an official reception on arrival at Hamad International Airport before heading to their accommodation. Later in the evening, the team will start its first training sessions in one of the stadiums in preparation for tomorrow's match against Yemeni counterpart.
