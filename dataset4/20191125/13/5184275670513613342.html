Not Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone Says She Would Have Been Very Happy To Marry A South Indian Guy

Film Companion just released the hundred great performances of the decade actors and had called Deepika Padukone for Piku, Ranveer Singh for Bajirao Mastani

25 Nov 2019, 13:31 by Koimoi.com Team

Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone just celebrated their first anniversary together. They went to Tirupati Balaji first and then to the Golden Temple, Amritsar to seek blessings from the god. Their lovely pictures went viral on the internet in no time and they looked ethereal in them.

Film Companion just released the hundred great performances of the decade and had called Deepika Padukone for Piku, Ranveer Singh for Bajirao Mastani, Vijay Deverakonda for Arjun Reddy, Vijay Sethupathi for Super Deluxe, Parvathy Thiruvothu for Uyare, Ayushmann Khurrana for Andhadhun, Alia Bhatt for Highway and Manoj Bajpayee for Gangs Of Wasseypur.

Talking about Ranveer Singh’s career graph, he started with Band Baaja Baaraat and portrayed the character of a young Delhi guy. He was so much into the skin that Ayushmann thought to himself, that he’s a Punjabi from Delhi. Everyone laughed at this and appreciated the fact that even though he was from Bandra, Mumbai; he cracked the accent and nailed the role.

Deepika then revealed that when she met him afterwards for the first time, she always thought that he was from Delhi and was shocked to know that he was from Bandra to which Ranveer funnily replied, “that’s what got her hooked’. Deepika immediately responded by saying, “I would have been very happy with a south Indian guy” and laughs off.

Ranveer then laughs and says, “Just wait for two days, I’m going to Tirupati in a dhoti!” and everyone starts laughing!
