Star Wars The Rise of Skywalker teaser will tug at your heartstrings

Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker will wrap up the tale of the Skywalkers that began with 1977's original Star Wars. The film hits theaters on December 20, 2019.

25 Nov 2019, 08:11 by Entertainment Desk

A new teaser for Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker, the final film in the Star Wars’ sequel trilogy, has been released. The two-and-a-half-minute clip features snippets from the previous films in the franchise, behind-the-scenes footage and basically sets up The Rise of Skywalkers as this grand conclusion of the series.

The Rise of Skywalker will wrap up the tale of the Skywalkers that began with 1977’s original Star Wars.

The teaser also hammers home the immense popularity and influence of the franchise on pop culture, and we are shown even short clips from several Star Wars parodies.
