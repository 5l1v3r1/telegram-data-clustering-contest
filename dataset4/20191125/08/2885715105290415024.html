JEE Main January admit card 2020 to release on this date

JEE Main admit card 2020: The admit card will be available to download at the websites- nta.ac.in, jeemain.nta.nic.in. The examination will be conducted in shifts from January 6 to 11, 2020

25 Nov 2019, 08:59 by Education Desk

JEE Main admit card 2020: The National Testing Agency (NTA) will release the admit card for the JEE Main January session examination on Friday, December 6, as per the official notification. The candidates who will appear in the examination can download the admit card through the websites- nta.ac.in, jeemain.nta.nic.in.  The engineering entrance exam will be conducted in shifts from January 6 to 11, 2020.

The JEE Main hall ticket will have details of the exam venue, time slot. The three-hour exam will be conducted online for both paper-I for admission to engineering courses and paper-II for admission to architecture courses. This year, a different paper for B.Plan will also be conducted. There will be two shifts, morning shift will begin at 9:30 am and the afternoon shift will begin at 2:30 pm.
