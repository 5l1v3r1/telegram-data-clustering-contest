New medical centre offering free ‘mole mapping’

25 Nov 2019, 08:35 by Staff Writer

A NEW Perth medical centre is celebrating its opening by offering free ‘mole mapping’ to its first 100 skin check patients.

Floreat Forum General Practice was officially opened on Saturday.

And to celebrate the launch, the team is offering free mole mapping using their leading edge machine.

The head-to-toe check, valued at $200, provides a comprehensive scan of your skin allowing the doctors to thoroughly assess and diagnose suspicious spots and record any changes over time.

The new medical centre is family owned, offers bulk billing for all patients and extended operating hours, opening seven days a week, for both appointments and walk-ins.

The new facility has nine consulting rooms for doctors, a dedicated Clinipath pathology centre and a separate surgery room for minor medical procedures.

Floreat Forum General Practice is located in the centre of the main mall at Floreat Forum Shopping Centre, with convenient external access off the Floreat Avenue lane-way and a new internal entrance directly into the shopping mall opposite Lenard’s.

Floreat Forum General Practice operates Monday to Thursday, 8am-9pm, Friday, 8am-6pm and 9am-1pm on weekends.

The practice can be contacted on 9269 9029.
