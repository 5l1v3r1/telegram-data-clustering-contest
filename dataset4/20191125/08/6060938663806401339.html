'Frozen 2' box office collection day 3: Disney's animated fantasy film weaves its magic at the ticket counters

25 Nov 2019, 08:55 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Six years after 'Frozen' set the box office ringing with a stupendous collection, Disney has come out with the sequel of the animated fantasy film which revolves around the lives of Elsa, Anna and Olaf. Their breathtaking adventures and soothing visual effects have helped the film at the domestic box office as well.

According to the latest report on Boxofficeindia.com, the film has managed to rake in Rs 8.25 on its first Sunday. While the film opened to a mere collection of Rs 3.25 crore, its collections doubled on Saturday as it minted Rs 6.75 crore. With a total of Rs 18.25 crore from its first weekend, the animated film looks to grow at the Indian box office as well.

While the plot of the film revolves around a princess, who sets off on an adventurous journey to find her estranged sister whose powers have accidentally trapped their entire kingdom in endless winter.

'Frozen 2' has been facing tough competition from John Abraham and Ileana D'Cruz multi-starrer 'Pagalpanti' at the ticket counter but despite that Elsa and Anna's story has managed to weave its magic.
