Abhishek Bachchan starrer 'Kahaani' spin-off titled 'Bob Biswas', Shah Rukh Khan to produce

25 Nov 2019, 08:19 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

While there were reports doing rounds in the media that junior Bachchan is about to play a negative character in the prequel of 'Kahaani'. Today an official announcement from the film's production team and the actor himself made it more clear. Accordingly, the film is titled as 'Bob Biswas' and will be produced by none other than Shah Rukh Khan and Gauri Khan's Red Chillies Entertainment. Yes, you heard it right.
