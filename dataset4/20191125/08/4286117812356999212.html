Elephant breaks electric fence using a brilliant technique. Viral video leaves Internet stunned

In a viral video, an elephant can be seen using a brilliant technique to break an electric fence with its foot. The clip has left the Internet stunned.

25 Nov 2019, 08:37

Elephants are intelligent animals and there are innumerable videos on the Internet to prove the same. Remember the viral video of an elephant pulling down a wooden electric fence to cross over to the forest?

Another video of an elephant breaking electric fence is also leaving the Internet in awe of the animal’s intelligence.

The video was shared online by Twitter user Dr. PM Dhakate with the caption, "Gajraj, The Elephant, is one of the most intelligent animals in the animal kingdom. An amazing video, showing how the elephant first checks for electricity flowing in the wires, breaks two barriers, keeping itself safe in the process and thereby dispersing in the forest!"
