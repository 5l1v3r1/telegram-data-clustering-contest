Employer tests the patience of job seekers for hours, Nigerians react

25 Nov 2019, 08:47

- A Nigerian man recently shared the news of how an employer conducted a job interview

- According to the individual, the employer carried out a test of patience on his prospective employees

- Social media users received the story with mixed reactions

A social media user identified as @jerrydoubles on micro blogging platform, Twitter, recently shared a story of how an employer conducted an interview for job seekers.

According to him, the employer had invited six candidates for an interview and slated the time for 7am.

However, after waiting for several hours, three out of six of the candidates took their exit from the venue.

@jerrydoubles said the employer did not show up at the venue until 6pm when there were just two candidates left waiting.
