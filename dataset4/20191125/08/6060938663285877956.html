Assam government joins hands with Times of India Group to host the 65th Filmfare Awards in Guwahati

25 Nov 2019, 08:17 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The prestigious black lady is all set to enthrall Bollywood stars yet again as the 65th Filmfare Awards were announced today. For the first time ever the prestigious award function will be hosted outside Mumbai. Assam government has signed an MoU with the Times Group to host the extravagant Bollywood event in Guwahati next year.
