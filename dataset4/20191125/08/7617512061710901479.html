Oak Bay police issue warning after rash of thefts from vehicles last week

25 Nov 2019, 08:24

Oak Bay Police responded to five reports of thefts from vehicles last week.

Police are urging residents to lock their vehicles and keep valuables out of sight, following thefts on Nov. 19 through Nov. 24.

The thefts occurred throughout Oak Bay on Cadboro Bay Road, Central Avenue, Beach Drive and two separate incidents on Musgrave Street on different nights.

In the case on Central Avenue, the rear window of the vehicle was smashed and several items stolen. That investigation is ongoing.
