The industry has no room for an ungrateful person like Kelvyn Boy – KOD fires

25 Nov 2019, 08:31 by Adu Sarpeah

Analysing the happenings in our entertainment industry, underground musicians or better still up and coming acts will see real struggling and hustling in terms of getting external support.

This above-mentioned projection comes at the back of the back and forth issues that normally happen between budding artistes and their managers after parting ways, the case of Kelvyn Boy and Stonebwoy.

READ ALSO:– Kelvyn Boy Suggests He Would Play A Ghc7 Show Over Stonebwoy’s Ashaiman To World Concert

Y’all know how issues pertaining to Kelvyn Boy and Stonebwoy have taken over the media landscape lately with each throwing indirect shots. Following discussions, broadcaster Kofi Okyere Darko alias KOD feels Kelvyn Boy has been ungrateful to Stonebwoy.

According to him, ingrates like Kelvyn Boy has no room in the Ghanaian music industry. KOD believes despite the sudden “beef” that has risen between him and his former boss, it’s not the right thing for him to make comments and gesticulations aimed at ridiculing Stonebwoy.

Kofi Okyere Darko (KOD) who is the host of Starr Drive made this statement when he joined a live discussion section on Facebook with blogger George Britton.
