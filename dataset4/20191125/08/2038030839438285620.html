Who won big at The American Music Awards 2019? We've got the full list!

25 Nov 2019, 08:00

Cape Town – Singer and songwriter Taylor Swift became the artist with the most AMAs ever, after she won five awards at the ceremony which took on Sunday 24 November.

The Lover hitmaker also was named the artist of the decade at the ceremony.

Talented young artist Billie Eilish won new artist of the year, as well as a favourite alternative rock artist. The event was hosted by Ciara.

SEE THE FULL WINNERS LIST HERE:

ARTIST OF THE YEAR

Taylor Swift

NEW ARTIST OF THE YEAR

Billie Eilish

COLLABORATION OF THE YEAR

Shawn Mendes & Camila Cabello - Señorita

TOUR OF THE YEAR

BTS

FAVOURITE MUSIC VIDEO

Taylor Swift - You Need to Calm Down
