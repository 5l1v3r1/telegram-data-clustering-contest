I'm A Celeb's Ian Wright 'fuming' as football memorabilia sold on eBay

Football legend Ian Wright's most prized sporting possessions are up for sale on eBay - but the I'm A Celeb star is not the seller

25 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Sara-Aisha Kent, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sara-aisha-kent/

I'm A Celebrity's Ian Wright, 56, is 'fuming' after his personal football memorabilia - including signed shirts, trophies and his 1992 Premier League Golden Boot - is being auctioned off on eBay.

The former sporting star's accolades were in storage in Orlando, Florida, according to The Sun, where the unit was up for grabs at a low price after Wright's ex-wife Deborah stopped paying the monthly fees for the space.

The shed load of fortune making goods were snapped up by church worker Kurt Wojchiechowski, 24, who is now auctioning off the lot for $150,000.00.

Kurt, who resides in Wisconsin claims he has offered to sell the sporting gear back to Wright but the price was not right.

He told the Sun on Sunday: "I gave him multiple opportunities to buy it back already. Now all his stuff is securely locked away and I am waiting for the right moment to sell it. It feels like now is the time.

“Hopefully Ian will win I'm A Celebrity and I’ll get loads of offers.”

However, Kurt states on his online advertisement that he is looking for a nearby buyer, as he posted: "Local Pick up only. I could potentially work out a deal to ship it if needed."

Sport commentator Ian is reportedly 'fuming' at the loss of the items and memories of his sporting success.

A source told the publication: “Ian’s footballing life was in that storage facility — everything that reminded him of his playing days.

"He’s fuming that his items could be scattered around the world."

The contents of the unit were up for purchase after payments were not made on the unit which was then seized by local officials.

A Florida native brought the unit two years ago and then sold it onto Kurt.

Under US law if storage space is repossessed the contents belong to the new owner.
