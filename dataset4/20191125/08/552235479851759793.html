Presale tickets to see Texas perform anniversary gig at Glasgow Hydro are on sale - here’s where to get them

Scottish rockers Texas will be celebrating the 30th anniversary of their debut album 'Southside' with a show at the Hydro next year

25 Nov 2019, 08:43 by Linda Howard

Presale tickets to see Glasgow heroes Texas perform an anniversary concert at the SSE Hydro next year are on sale today.

The Scottish rockers will be celebrating the 30th anniversary of their debut album 'Southside' with the special performance on Saturday, October 17, 2020.

The early release tickets are on sale from 9am through Ticketmaster here .

The band, fronted by Sharleen Spiteri and famous for hits such as 'I Don't Want A Lover' and 'Black Eyed Boy', have sold over 40 million records during the course of their career, since forming in 1986.

The Hydro show comes on the back of a sold-out performance to celebrate the band’s 30 years in music - set to take place on December 15 at SWG3 in Glasgow.

There are several ticket presales for the SSE Hydro show happening over Monday and Tuesday before going on general sale at 9am on Wednesday, November 27 - you can get them via Ticketmaster here .
