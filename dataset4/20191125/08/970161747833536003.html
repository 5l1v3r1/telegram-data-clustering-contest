Declan Donnelly hits back at James Haskell after awkward I'm A Celebrity clash

25 Nov 2019, 08:03

It was all kicking off on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! last night, but no one could have foreseen the spat between fiercely competitive James Haskell and Ant and Dec.

During last night’s live trial, James and Roman Kemp failed to win stars – aka a roast dinner – for Ian Wright, Andy Whyment and Cliff Parisi in what viewers called an ‘impossible task’.

The pair were stuck when a crab refused to let go of a star which threw the entire Bushtucker Trial into disarray.

However, James unexpectedly took his frustration out on Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly, which was painfully awkward for everyone involved.

‘That was unlucky. What was it James?’ Dec, very cool and calmly, asked the rugby ace.

‘What was it?’ James snapped. ‘It was the very fact there was a giant crab in the way that you wanted me to carefully caress in a different direction as I tried to unscrew a star in its face.’

Naturally, the Ant and Dec were still reeling from the clash on Extra Camp.

‘How angry did James get at the end there?’ gasped host Emily Atack.

Ant chuckled: ‘He had a little go at Dec, didn’t he.’

Doing an impression of James, which he would no doubt never do to the athlete’s face, Dec moaned: ‘Well it’s all about the crab you wanted me to caress!’

But that wasn’t the only source of tension last night as James also locked horns with Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway, who didn’t want preferential treatment going to the live trial because she’s a woman.

And then Ian and Andrew Maxwell had to have a bizarre bicker over what appeared to be nothing, just because.

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! Continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
