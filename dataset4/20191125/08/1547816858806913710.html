Bigg Boss 13: Asim Riaz Deep In Love With Himanshi Khurana; Sings 'Kyun Hota Hai Pyaar, Na Tum Jaano Na Hum'

25 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Nikita Thakkar

Looks like love is in the air of Bigg Boss 13 house. Asim Riaz seems to have grown fond of Himanshi Khurana and is now singing love songs too

Bigg Boss 13 has been all about fights so far, but it seems, the environment is going to change. Looks like love is taking over the Bigg Boss 13 house. Asim Riaz, who showed off his aggressive side last week, is now a little mellow as he has fallen in 'love' with Himanshi Khurana. Hey, we are not making this up, he has confessed it himself. Last week, Shefali had sort of given a hint about Asim's growing fondness for Himanshi and now, the handsome hunk is even singing love songs inside the house.

A recent Bigg Boss 13 update states that Asim will confess about his love for Himanshi Khurana to Arti Singh. And then he'll sing "Kyun Hota Hai Pyaar, Na Tum Jaano Na Hum" from Hrithik Roshan's Kaho Na Pyaar Hai. Well, well, that's some great bonding happening. BUT, let us also tell you that Himanshi is engaged and has spoken about her partner to Asim. So he knows pretty well that uska kuch ho nahi sakta. How much ever we love their chemistry inside the Bigg Boss 13 house, Asim and Himanshi will remain to be just 'good friends'. Asim's one-sided love will have to take a backseat.
