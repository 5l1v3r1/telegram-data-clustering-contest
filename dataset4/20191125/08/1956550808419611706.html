The UAE has a new baby and toddler food delivery service

Parents can order nutritious meals for their little ones

25 Nov 2019, 08:50 by Kelly Johnstone

A brand-new service has launched that delivers baby and toddler food to families across the UAE.

Lil' Tots prepares and delivers a range of healthy food for tiny tots taking the stress out of meal times for mums and dads.

Most parents begin to wean their little ones around the age of six months, but reaching this important milestone brings with it a whole new set of parental concerns (the worry never goes away) from worrying that they aren't eating enough, to worrying that they aren't getting enough of the right things.

Lil' Tots has worked alongside leading dietitians and chefs to create a menu that features wholesome, well-balanced meals that contain all the right nutrients for growing babies and toddlers.

The Lil’ Tots Lil’ Blends range meals are all handmade in small batches, using farm-fresh ingredients that are free from anything artificial, preservatives, sugar or salt.

Savoury dishes on offer for tiny taste buds include ratatouille, a Moroccan feast and a chicken dish, while desert takes the form of a tropical pudding amongst other tasty sweet treats.

The set menus, which are planned for week-by-week from Sunday to Thursday and include a variety of ingredients from all five food groups with more than 20 seasonal fruits and vegetables, are recommended from eight months old, with subscription plans starting from Dhs420.

Gift vouchers are also available, plus Lil' Tots can cater to birthday parties and other special occasions.

Once you've registered on the website, chosen your meal plan and paid the subscription fee, your little ones' meals will arrive within 48 hours - speedy.
