Kelowna woman murdered

25 Nov 2019, 08:41 by Rob Gibson

RCMP in Spruce Grove Alberta, just outside of Edmonton continue to investigate the murder of a former Kelowna woman.

Parkland RCMP responded to a call of a woman in distress on a rural road just off Highway 16 in the early morning of November 17th, 2019.

Police say the woman was deceased when they arrived.  An autopsy determined that 33 year old Saladina Vivacos died as the result of a homicide.

Friends and family of Vivacos have started a GoFundMe page, "our sister Saladina Vivancos is no longer with us, it feels as if we are living a nightmare that we can’t awaken from."

The post says, "we are shattered, we are broken, still in disbelief that this could be a reality."

Funds raised will be used to bring Vivancos home from Alberta to B.C., for the funeral expenses and to help the family with last-minute travel expenses.
