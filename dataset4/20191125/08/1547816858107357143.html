25 Nov 2019, 08:57 by Aditi Singh

Athiya Shetty was our guest on Fashion Vashion And All That. The actress, whose film Motichoor Chaknachoor released a week back, spoke about her style mantra and more

We're back with another episode of Fashion Vashion And All That. Our guest this time was Athiya Shetty. The actress had a lot of interesting dope to share on her style files. 

We started the conversation by asking how has she dealt with the pressures in Bollywood. The actress, dressed in a blush pink pantsuit said she has become calmer with time.
