SRK ropes in Abhishek Bachchan for next production ‘Bob Biswas’

25 Nov 2019, 08:55 by Raj Baddhan

Shah Rukh Khan has confirmed his next film production, ‘Bob Biswas’ starring Abhishek Bachchan.

The thriller is a prequel to the popular ‘Kahanani’ franchise, helmed by Sujoy Ghosh. ‘Bob Biswas’ will be about one of the mysterious characters from ‘Kahaani’.
