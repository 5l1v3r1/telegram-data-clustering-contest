Weyco Group, Inc. (WEYS) To Go Ex-Dividend on November 27th

25 Nov 2019, 08:48 by Renee Jackson

Weyco Group, Inc. (NASDAQ:WEYS) declared a quarterly dividend on Tuesday, November 5th, Zacks reports. Shareholders of record on Friday, November 29th will be given a dividend of 0.24 per share by the textile maker on Thursday, January 2nd. This represents a $0.96 dividend on an annualized basis and a dividend yield of 4.14%. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Wednesday, November 27th.

Shares of NASDAQ WEYS opened at $23.19 on Monday. Weyco Group has a 1 year low of $21.80 and a 1 year high of $34.46. The company has a debt-to-equity ratio of 0.07, a current ratio of 3.59 and a quick ratio of 1.73. The company’s 50-day moving average is $24.44 and its 200 day moving average is $25.58. The stock has a market capitalization of $229.48 million, a P/E ratio of 10.85 and a beta of 0.74.

Separately, BidaskClub upgraded shares of Weyco Group from a “sell” rating to a “hold” rating in a research report on Tuesday, July 30th.

About Weyco Group

Weyco Group, Inc, together with its subsidiaries, designs and distributes footwear. The company operates through two segments, North American Wholesale and North American Retail. It engages in the design and marketing footwear for men, women, and children under the Florsheim, Nunn Bush, Stacy Adams, BOGS, and Rafters brand names.
