Dog of the Week: Shady

24 Nov 2019, 09:12

Source: Spark Rescue

Meet Shady a 2 year old German Shepard/ Doxie/ Basset Hound mix ( best guess)? Weighing in at 80 pounds this adorable low rider is in need of a home now! We were told Shady is great with everyone, including kids and dogs! We are still waiting on more information but wanted to get him posted as we were told his family can no longer keep him. If you are able to foster Shady please let us know by submitting a foster application.

If you are interested in adopting Shady please fill out an adoption application on our website www.sparkrescue.org.
