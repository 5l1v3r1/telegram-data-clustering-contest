Police close road near Dunkeld after tree falls in landslip

A country road in Highland Perthshire has been closed after being blocked by a tree which fell in a landslip.

24 Nov 2019, 09:34 by Ross Gardiner

The tree, which fell in the early hours of this morning, is blocking the A822 at Trochry, near Dunkeld.

A police spokesperson said: “Police Scotland would like to advise motorists that there is a road closure on the A822 at Trochry, near to Dunkeld, due to a fallen tree and landslip.

“Diversions are in place and drivers are asked to avoid the area if possible and thanked for their patience.”
