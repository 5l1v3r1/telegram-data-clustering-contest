9 Taliban militants killed, detained in Special Forces raids in Farah, Herat and Kabul

24 Nov 2019, 09:14 by Khaama Press

The Special Forces killed 5 Taliban militants and arrested 4 others during the operations in Farah, Herat and Kabul provinces.

The military officials said Sunday the Special Forces killed 5 Taliban militants and destroyed a cache of weapons in Pusht-e Rod district of Farah.

The officials further added that the Special Forces arrested 3 Taliban militants and destroyed a small cache of weapons during a similar raid in Ghoriyan district of Herat.

The Special Forces also arrested a Taliban militant during a raid in Chahar Asyab district of Kabul, the officials added.

The Taliban group has not commented regarding the operations so far.
