Strictly Come Dancing viewers blast Craig Revel Horwood over 'ridiculous' scores

Fans were "screaming" at their screens!

24 Nov 2019, 09:46 by Nancy Brown

Viewers of Strictly Come Dancing have slammed judge Craig Revel Horwood for his "ridiculous" scores.

Craig has never been the easiest judge to please, but last night he angered viewers as he took things to a whole new level.

The judge awarded Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard a five for their samba – much to fellow judge Motsi Mabusi's shock.
