JOZ Vs TST Dream11, Probable Playing 11, Toss, Pitch And Match Report: horrid Jozi Stars make for dire viewing with the side unable to snap out of their ordeal  

Every match where we have had to sit down and document Jozi Stars’ campaign in the ongoing edition of the Mzansi Super League has only lead us towards one narrative. It’s been a torrid one with the side’s travesty being easy to document given the way the side have been broken down and decimated to find themselves right in the trenches of the points tally.

Sitting in sixth place with five defeats from five matches, for Jozi Stars, this edition of the showpiece league is turning out to be one to forget. What has made for a particularly dismal sight is not just the defeats but the horrid manner in which they have come about for the side with the team being comprehensively and clinically thwarted in all segments of the game to all but bring an end to their season this edition.

The side come into this encounter in the wake of a 4 wicket defeat at the hands of Paarl Rocks, a match which saw their 130 run target being peeled off with almost four wickets to spare. Up against such a dwindling outfit, Tshwane Spartans will be looking to register a much needed win with the side having had to bear the brunt of inclement weather, one which has seen three of their encounters being truncated due to incessant rainfall.

It was a discourse which was emanated the last time around as well with the side seeing what looked to be a certain win being snatched away from them. Making 141 while batting first, the side had Durban Heat on the mat at 21-3 before rain decided to leave them infuriated. 

Pitch Conditions And Toss

There are seldom few tracks better than this one in the country for the batsmen with this surface being a runs galore one. The batting unit is going to love its time out in the middle and with their being a slight chance of rain marring the match, the side winning the toss is bound to come out and chase here.
