FIND HER

Cobainy Whittet missing: Fife woman disappears for second time in a week sparking desperate police search

24 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Ewan Mowat

CONCERNS are growing for a Fife woman who has gone missing for the second time in a week.

Cobainy Whittet was last seen in the Springfield area of Cupar last night around 11.40pm.

It is believed she travelled there from Kirkcaldy.

Loved ones are growing increasingly worried for the 23-year-old who also disappeared last week.

Cobainy went missing from Kirkcaldy on Monday but was traced the following day.

She is described as being 5ft4in with long, brown hair.

Cobainy was wearing dark jeans and a dark hoody when she was last seen.

Her friends and family are concerned about her disappearance - which has sparked a desperate police search.

Sergeant Ian Moffat from Cupar Police station said: "Cobainy has been missing for a number of hours now and her friends are understandably worried about it.

"We have a number of police resources out looking for Cobainy and I am now looking for the assistance from the public."

If you believe you have seen Cobainy or have any information please contact 101 quoting incident no. 0114 of the 23/11/2019.
