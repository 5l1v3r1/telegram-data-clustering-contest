4.9 Magnitude Earthquake Strikes Off Tonga Coast - USGS

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - A 4.9 magnitude earthquake has been registered off the coast of the Polynesian kingdom of Tonga in the southern part of the Pacific Ocean, the US Geological Survey (USGS) said.

24 Nov 2019, 09:03

The quake was registered at 04:48 GMT with the epicentre located at a depth of 56.5 kilometres (35 miles). There have been no immediate reports about damage or casualties due to the earthquake and no tsunami threat has been reported.
