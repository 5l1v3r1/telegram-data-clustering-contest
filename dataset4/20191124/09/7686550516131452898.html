The 26 people currently reported missing across Wales

The Missing People charity is encouraging people to get in touch if they have information

24 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Joshua Knapman

There are currently 26 reported missing people in Wales.

The charity Missing People keeps a comprehensive list of people from all over the UK who have gone missing and have not been found.

Missing People has details about each of them on their website, and after contacting the four Welsh police forces, we can confirm that in Wales there are 26 different missing people.

A number of these cases are as recent as this year, while some stretch back to the 1970s.

The Missing People website has a 24-hour helpline providing emotional support. They also encourage all of the missing people to get in touch.

You can find details on their website .

These are Wales’ missing people:

Jordan Moray

Jordan has been missing since July 24 this year.

The 32-year-old from Aberdare was reportedly seen by a friend on July 29 in Cwmbach but a mystery letter has sparked a fresh appeal from police.

Read more here .

Keith Price

Keith was described as "potentially vulnerable" when he was reported missing in August 2019.

The 63-year-old is a diabetic and was last seen at around 10am on Tuesday, August 13 at his home in Maesteg , Bridgend.

His family made an emotional appeal begging him to come home, which can be found here .

Andrew Mahoney

Andrew has been missing since July 9, 2019.

The 46-year-old was last seen in Cardiff.

Jac Jones

According to South Wales Police, Jac's investigation is now with Essex Police who confirmed he is still missing.

The 16-year-old has been missing from Swansea since November 2, 2019.

Christopher Buckler

Christopher, 29, was last seen in Barry.

He's been missing since June 30, 2019.

Tapiwa Matuwi

IT graduate Tapiwa went missing at the age of 21. He's since turned 22 and his family have made fresh appeals to bring him home.

He's been missing from Swansea since February 7, 2019.

Cuong Tran

Cuong has been missing from Cardiff since September 12, 2019.

Robert Williams

Robert was 15 years old when he went missing 17 years ago.

He was last seen in Neath on March 22, 2002.

Lorraine Ridout

Lorraine was last seen at a Premier shop in Gabalfa, Cardiff, on January 31, 2016.

Police dogs were used to search the area and the River Taff was searched using sonar equipment in an attempt to find her body but so far she has not been found.

She was 57 at the age of her disappearance.

Haitham Abaker

Haitham has been missing since February 24, 2016.

He was 28 at the time of his disappearance and was last seen in Cardiff.

Christine Bracken

Christine, also known as 'Sister Jacinta', was 39 when she disappeared.

She's been missing from the Swansea area since May 19, 1979.

Michael Cox

Michael was living in Caerau, Bridgend, when he was last seen on November 18, 2011.

He is 5ft 10in with brown hair and blue eyes and was 45 at the time of his disappearance.

Kevin Thomas

Kevin Thomas was reported missing on Tuesday, March 1, 2011.

Police officers believe he was last seen at the Ladbrokes in Swansea city centre’s Union Street the day before.

He was 45 at the time of his disappearance, is 5ft 8in with grey hair was last seen wearing his council work uniform.

Parvin Singh

On March 24, 2011, Parvin disappeared from Cardiff.

He was 53 at the time.

William Davies

William went missing from Perthcelyn in February 1997.

His age at the time of his disappearance was just 16.

Andrew Rosser

Andrew John Rosser went missing on June 24, 2008 when he was aged 34.

He was last seen at his home address in Melyn, Neath.

Fond of walks in the Brecon Beacons and Neath Valley, Andrew is described as 5ft 8in with brown hair and green eyes.

Dobir Miah

Dobir went missing on June 8, 2002.

He was 29 at the time and was last seen in Uplands, Swansea.

David Jones

Aged 38 at the time of his disappearance, David has been missing since December 8, 2010.

He was last seen in Carmarthenshire.

Na Dang

Vietnamese teenager Na Dang went missing in Sedbury on April 27, 2015 but police suspect she was heading for Chepstow.

Na is 5ft 2in tall and was last seen in leopard-print pyjama bottoms and a navy blue puffer coat.

Nam Vo

Nam went missing on May 15, 2018 from Blackwood.

At the time he was 17.

Jaymie James

Missing from Pontypool, Jaymie was 49 at the time of his disappearance.

He went missing in October 2017.

Tim Evans

Tim was 58 when he went missing from Llandudno on March 29, 2018.

Tony Haigh

Tony was 49 years old when he was reported missing in Holyhead.

He's been missing since February 23, 3018.

Justin Mathis

Justin was last seen drinking with friends at the George and Dragon in Conwy in the early hours of February 4, 2011.

He lived with his partner in Penmaenmawr but that night he did not returned home.

Search operations in the area were unsuccessful, he was 28 at the time.

Thomas Constance

Thomas was 69 years when he was reported missing from Conwy on December 3, 2018.

David Clwyd Davies

David was 69 at the time of his disappearance.

He's been missing since April 3, 2013 and was last seen in Wrexham.
