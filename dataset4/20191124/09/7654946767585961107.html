Prince Andrew and Ghislaine Maxwell had secret Buckingham rendezvous

24 Nov 2019, 09:50 by Laura Italiano

Prince Andrew apparently just can’t quit accused Jeffrey Epstein procurer Ghislaine Maxwell.

The reckless royal secretly met with Epstein’s former gal pal at Buckingham palace in early June, the Daily Mail reported.

The hush-hush tete-a-tete came just two weeks after US prosecutors announced they were reopening an inquiry into Epstein’s alleged sex-trafficking operation — a probe that could well involve both Andrew and Maxwell.

The FBI wants to interview Andrew about his ties to Epstein, who committed suicide in a Manhattan federal prison in August.

Maxwell, who has been named in civil suits by multiple Epstein accusers as helping to keep him supplied with young women and underage girls, is assumed to be in hiding.
