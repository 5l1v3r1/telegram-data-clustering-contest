RnB star NE-YO is coming to Abu Dhabi for F1 gig

Another huge name is coming to MAD on Yas Island

24 Nov 2019, 09:34 by Colin Armstrong

It’s going to be a huge week in Abu Dhabi.

During the Formula 1 weekend, and the wider Race Week celebrations, you can expect amazing concerts, super car auctions and parties, and the little matter of a race at Yas Marina Circuit.

Fresh from announcing that Usher will be performing this weekend, MAD on Yas Island has announced another huge concert on Yas Island.

Taking to the stage on Saturday November 30, NE-YO will be performing for fans in the capital.

The Grammy Award-winning singer and songwriter joins a big line-up of acts to perform in the UAE capital during race week.

Ne-Yo is probably best known for his hits Miss Independent, Because of You, Another Love Song and So Sick.

The Yas Island Club is open late so we wouldn’t expect to see the American star perform until the wee hours.

At least it’s a long weekend in the UAE so you won’t have to be too tired in work the next day or if you’re heading to the big race.
