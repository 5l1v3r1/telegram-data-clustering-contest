Srinagar flea market abuzz with shoppers

Few shops were also open in areas around the commercial hub of Lal Chowk.

24 Nov 2019, 09:35 by PTI

The weekly flea market in Srinagar witnessed a huge rush of customers on Sunday, four days after threatening posters had enforced a shutdown in most parts of Kashmir, officials said.

Shops opened on Sunday morning at some places in the Kashmir valley, including here in civil lines areas of the city and mini buses plied, the officials said.

They said few shops were also open in areas around the commercial hub of Lal Chowk.
