Netizens call Bhuvan Bam the male version of Alia Bhatt, minus his beard. Well, it looks like the comedian has accepted it, and his recent coffee proposal to Alia is proof!

As funny as it sounds, the netizens can’t stop but comparing the Student Of The Year actress with Bhuvan Bam, after a certain picture of him went viral on social media.
