Monroe church feeds seniors Thanksgiving meal

24 Nov 2019, 10:22 by Reggie Wells

MONROE, La. (KNOE) - One Monroe church fed seniors in its community on Saturday. The Greater Realness Cathedral served 70 seniors at the Booker T. Washington Senior Village a thanksgiving meal just in time for the holiday.

Church leaders say the church also has other events in the community as well like bingo nights and bible study.
