From Peter Andre to Barbara Windsor - Christmas light switch-ons through the years in Bristol

From Peter Andre in 2014 to Josie Gibson in 2010 - Bristol's had quite the array of Christmas light switch-ons

24 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Briana Millett

Christmas is a mere month away, with Bristol well and truly geared up for the big day.

The lights are switched on, people are preparing to buy their Christmas trees, and the shops are busy with prepared people buying their loved-ones gifts.

But how much has Christmas changed in Bristol over the years?

We've been delving through the archives to find some festive snaps from Christmas light switch-ons of years gone by.

There was Big Brother star Josie Gibson at the Yate switch-on in 2010, while Peter Andre doing it not once but twice - in 2009 and 2014.
