Watch now: Priyanka Chopra grooves to Ghungroo with Vaani Kapoor

24 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Kajol Mathur

Priyanka Chopra is currently in Mumbai and has been keeping busy with her work schedules. However, amidst all this she makes sure to catch up with her friends from B-town and have all the fun in the world. Speaking of that, we have now got our hands on a video in which she can be seen grooving to popular dance number – Ghungroo from War which featured Hrithik Roshan and Vaani Kapoor. The viral video is from a bash which happened recently and one can see Priyanka burning the dance floor with Vaani. It was posted by Kapoor on her Instagram story and she captioned it as “ain't nobody like you PC”.
