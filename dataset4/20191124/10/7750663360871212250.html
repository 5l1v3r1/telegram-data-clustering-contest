Borussia Dortmund 'join race for Tottenham Hotspur youngster Troy Parrott'

Borussia Dortmund have reportedly entered the race to sign Tottenham Hotspur youngster Troy Parrott next summer.

24 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Leo Nieboer

The 17-year-old, who moved to North London in 2017, made his senior debut against Colchester United in October.

Moreover, Parrott also received his first senior cap for the Republic of Ireland during a 3-1 win over New Zealand this month.

According to The Mirror, Dortmund are the latest club to demonstrate an interest in the striker, who has been compared with Harry Kane.

It is claimed that Bayern Munich, Juventus and Real Madrid have all expressed an interest in Parrott as well.
