Kim Kardashian has Caitlyn Jenner's 'rise and shine' I'm A Celebrity catchphrase as her alarm

Loves her dad's motivational words

24 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Paul Hirons

Reality superstar Kim Kardashian has taken to Twitter to comment on Caitlyn Jenner's 'rise and shine' catchphrase on I'm A Celebrity.

Kim, 39, took to the social media site to joke that she had recorded Caitlyn's motivational message and set it as her alarm alert.
