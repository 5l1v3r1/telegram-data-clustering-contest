Ajit Pawar posts thank you tweets in first public comments after ditching uncle Sharad

Ajit Pawar has gone on a thanking spree on Twitter after being sworn-in as the deputy chief minister of Maharashtra on Saturday.

24 Nov 2019, 10:57

Even after he was sidelined by the NCP after a rift with uncle Sharad Pawar, Ajit Pawar has gone on a thanking spree on Twitter after being sworn-in as the deputy chief minister of Maharashtra on Saturday.

He has also changed his Twitter bio to Deputy Chief Minister of Maharashtra amid the ongoing huddle in the state.

Ajit Pawar has thanked Prime Minister Narendra Modi and assured him of forming a stable government in the state. Replying to the Prime Minister's congratulatory message on Twitter, Ajit wrote, "Thank you Hon. Prime Minister @narendramodi ji. We will ensure a stable Government that will work hard for the welfare of the people of Maharashtra."
