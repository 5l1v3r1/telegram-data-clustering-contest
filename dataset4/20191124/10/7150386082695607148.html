‘Grand Ram Temple Will be Built in Ayodhya, no World Power Can Stop it,’ Says Rajnath in Jharkhand

Defence Minister Rajnath Singh, who is in Jharkhand to address a number of rallies for the state’s upcoming assembly polls, talked about the procurement of Rafale fighter jets from France and said it will destroy the terror camps across the border.

24 Nov 2019, 10:55

New Delhi: Union Defence Minister Rajnath Singh on Sunday addressed an election rally in Palamu district in Jharkhand and said a grand Ram temple will soon be built in Ayodhya and no power is there in the world can stop it. “The path for the construction of the Ram temple has been cleared by the Supreme Court and we will soon have a temple there,” he added.

The Defence Minister, who is in Jharkhand to address a number of rallies for the state’s upcoming assembly polls, talked about the procurement of Rafale fighter jets from France and said it will destroy the terror camps across the border.
