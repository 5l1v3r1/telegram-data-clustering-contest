Where was The Crown filmed?

24 Nov 2019, 10:50

The Crown season three launched a week ago, and has probably already been binged in its entirety by millions of fans.

The season has been met with overwhelming praise, from Olivia Colman stepping into the role of Queen Elizabeth to the sensitive portrayal of the real-life Aberfan disaster.

But just where is the series filmed and is that actually Buckingham Palace in the show?

Here’s what you need to know…

Where do they film Buckingham Palace scenes for the Crown?

Obviously the Royal Family weren’t going to permit Olivia Colman and co to come traipsing around their house, dressed as them.

A majority of the scenes set within the palace are filmed at Lancaster House, which is owned by the Foreign Office and isn’t too far from the real Buckingham Palace.

Wilton House in Wiltshire has also been used as a stand-in for parts of the Palace.

Other interior scenes have been filmed at Waddeston Manor.

For the recently-released season three, the University of Greenwich was used for the scene where Lord Mountbatten (Charles Dance) visits Prince Charles (Josh O’Connor).

Where was the Investiture of the Prince of Wales filmed?

This is one of the occasions where the real life location was used.

The Investiture of the Prince of Wales took place at Caernarfon Castle on 1 July 1969.

Josh O’Connor filmed the scenes at the same location for season three.

Where was Churchill’s funeral filmed?

In season three, viewers watch the Queen attending the funeral of Prime Minister Winston Churchill.

In real life, this funeral was held at St Paul’s Cathedral. Churchill was given a State Funeral – an honour usually reserved for Royals.

The Crown shot this funeral scene in Winchester Cathedral in Hampshire instead.

Where are the White House scenes filmed?

Season three sees Helena Bonham Carter’s Princess Margaret travel to Washington DC to meet with President Lyndon B Johnson.

Rather than send Helena overseas, the White House scenes were filmed at The Hylands House in Essex – which used to host V Festival.
