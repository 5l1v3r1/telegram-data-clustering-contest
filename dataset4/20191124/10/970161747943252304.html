I'm A Celeb's Nadine Coyle imitates Gillian McKeith's fainting spell

24 Nov 2019, 10:16

Nadine Coyle just won I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! by impersonating Gillian McKeith’s unforgettable fainting spell in hilarious scenes.

In last night’s episode, the Girls Aloud star was faced with holding live cockroaches in her mouth as part of a savage Bushtucker Trial and as she stepped up to the challenge she imitated the iconic moment from the 2010 series.

‘Oh my God,’ Nadine said as she heard what gruesome task she was about to take on.

‘What did Gillian McKeith do? She just went like this?’

The singer then pretended to drop to the floor, causing presenters And McPartlin and Declan Donnelly and her campmates to erupt with laughter.

Viewers were in bits over Nadine’s reference on Twitter, with one writing: ‘Nadine talking about Gillian McKeith is one of the highlights so far.’

Meanwhile, another branded the moment ‘iconic.’

Despite not being keen to face the cockroaches, Nadine managed to successfully complete the trial and was applauded by the rest of the squad.
