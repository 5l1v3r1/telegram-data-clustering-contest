Who left X Factor: Celebrity last night in the semi-final?

24 Nov 2019, 10:36

The X Factor: Celebrity whittled down its remaining six acts to its four finalists on Saturday night – meaning we had to bid farewell to two more acts.

Following last weekend’s show, when Vinnie Jones pleaded with Simon Cowell to send him home instead of Glee’s Kevin McHale, there was everything to play for ahead of next weekend’s grand final.

So who fell at the final hurdle in the movie-themed show?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who left X Factor: Celebrity last night in the semi-final?

Last night’s X Factor: Celebrity saw Vinnie Jones and rugby trio Try Star miss out on a place in next weekend’s final.

Vinnie lost out after finishing bottom of the public vote – and said his time on the show was  ‘absolutely fantastic.’
