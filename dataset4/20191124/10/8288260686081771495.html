'He was our life' man dies after he was hit by a bus in Sedgley

The man was rushed to hospital via an air ambulance following the tragic crash in September

24 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Charlotte Regen, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/

A heartbroken family have spoken of their grief after a man, who was hit by a bus in Sedgley, died.

Medics and police rushed to the scene on Wolverhampton Road, on September 21 after the man was struck just before 8.20am.

He was rushed to hospital via an air ambulance following the crash where he was in a critical condition .

However, the 53-year-old sadly died on September 30, police revealed.

A spokesman for West Midlands Police said: "A man who was taken to hospital after he was hit by a bus in Sedgley on Saturday, September 21, has sadly passed away.

"The man, in his 50s, was in a critical condition following the collision just before 8.20am at the junction of High Park Crescent and Wolverhampton Road."

The family of the 53-year-old have released a tribute following his death on September 30: “We are utterly devastated to have lost our loving son; he was our life.

“We are a very private family and wish to grieve quietly and in privacy; we request that this is respected so that we can try and come to terms with this terrible shock to us."

Police say the circumstances of the incident have been investigated and the driver of the bus was found not to be at fault. The matter has been referred to the coroner.
