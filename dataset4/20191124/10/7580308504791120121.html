Chimamanda Adichie Is First Nigerian, Youngest African to Get UN Foundation’s Global Leadership Award

24 Nov 2019, 10:06 by Editor

The United Nations Foundation on the 20th of November, 2019 honoured Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie, amongst other global leaders in its annual awards ceremony. This year’s ceremony held on the eve of the United Nations’ 75th anniversary and called We the Peoples to honour the founding ideals and vision articulated in the UN Charter.

The honorees – advocates, business leaders, UNhumanitarian workers, and more – strive for a more equitable and sustainable world.
