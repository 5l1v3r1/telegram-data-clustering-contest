Philippine Navy officer guns down retired cop in Cavite

24 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Zaida Delos Reyes

POLICE arrested a Philippine Navy (PN) officer shortly after he allegedly shot dead a retired policeman who tried to stop them from creating so much noise while drinking, Saturday night in Indang, Cavite.

Arrested was Petty Officer 2nd Class Dimple Feranil.

He was nabbed for shooting the victim identified as Jose Alcantara a retired member of the Criminal Investigation and Detection Group (CIDG).

Reports reaching Camp Crame showed that the incident occurred at around 9:30 p.m. at Bgy. Poblacion 3, Indang, Cavite.

Initial investigation showed that the victim went to the area to ask the suspect and his friends who were already drunk to lower their voices since they were creating so much noise in the area.

Feranil and Alcantara engaged in a heated argument, drew their guns and exchanged fire.

Alcantara died on the spot, while Feranil was rushed to the hospital due to gunshot wound in his leg.

Authorities are now conducting a follow-up investigation on the incident.
