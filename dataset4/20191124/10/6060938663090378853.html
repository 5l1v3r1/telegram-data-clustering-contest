India consolidate top spot in WTC rankings after 2-0 series rout of Bangladesh

24 Nov 2019, 10:04 by PTI

Highlights

  1. India, who are yet to drop a point in the nine-team championship, reached 360 points after win vs Bangladesh
  2. India won 2-0 in the Windies and beat SA 3-0 in a home series before completing a third series win in D/N Kolkata Test
  3. Australia are on 116 points after winning the first match of its ongoing home Test series vsPakistan

KOLKATA: India on Sunday consolidated its position at the top of ICC World Test Championship after completing a 2-0 series victory over Bangladesh.

World Test Championship Points Table

India, who are yet to drop a point in the nine-team championship, reached 360 points after the comprehensive innings victory in the pink ball Test against Bangladesh at the Eden Gardens.

India won 2-0 in the West Indies and beat South Africa 3-0 in a home series before completing a third series win in the day-night Kolkata Test.

Each series is worth 120 points with the points distributed evenly over the number of matches in a series. The points range from 60 points for each match of a two-Test series to 24 for each match of a five-Test series.
