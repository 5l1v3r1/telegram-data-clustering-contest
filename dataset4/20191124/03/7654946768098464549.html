Sixth ‘Dear John’ reader receives free Lively hearing aides after touching letter

24 Nov 2019, 03:55 by John Crudele

Readers: Here is the sixth lucky winner of the free $1,600 hearing aids given out by Lively (listenlively.com), a company that I challenged to prove its claim that its devices were fabulous at a lower price than its competitors’. The winners were selected randomly by Lively from people who emailed me and asked to be considered.

Dear John: I am 83 years old, born April Fools Day 1933, and live in the East Village. 

I’ve been using hearing aids over 20 years. I started with Dunshaw in late ’90s at 654 Madison. 

In the last two years, it changed its name to I Love Hearing, and recently moved to 30 E. 60th St., Suite 605. 

I live on Social Security, with little savings in a rent-stabilized apartment. 

Every four years it costs me $7,000 for a new pair of hearing aids, and Social Security does not cover it. 

I limit my use of the hearing aids to make them last longer. 

Connecting with traumatized people and teaching the craft of acting to people of all ages for free is my major way of being of value in this world. 

Free hearing aids would allow me more time with that. L.L.
