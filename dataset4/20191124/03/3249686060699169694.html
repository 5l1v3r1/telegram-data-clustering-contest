Jung Joon Young's sentencing trial gets delayed

24 Nov 2019, 03:02 by jennywill

Jung Joon Young's sentencing has been delayed.

According to the court, instead of the sentencing, there will be another trial on the 27th at 11AM. Originally, the sentencing was to be handed down on the 29th. However, on the 21st, prosecutors issued a supervision order for the 'Jung Joon Young group', and that will have to be litigated.
