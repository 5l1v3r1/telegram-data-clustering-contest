Winter Weather Advisory issued November 24 at 3:42AM EST until November 24 at 1:00PM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

24 Nov 2019, 03:42

Winter Weather Advisory: Bradford; Wyoming until 1:00 PM

* WHAT…Mixed precipitation changing to snow expected. Additional
snow accumulations of 1 to 3 inches along with a light glaze of
ice.

* WHERE…Bradford and Wyoming counties.

* WHEN…Until 1 PM EST this afternoon. Steadiest snow expected 7
am until noon.

* IMPACTS…Plan on slippery road conditions.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS…A mix of rain, sleet and higher elevation
freezing rain will transition to mainly wet snow for a time mid
to late morning. The snow ends from west to east by early
afternoon. Temperatures will be hovering between 30 to 35
degrees through the event.
Slow down and use caution while traveling.

The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can
be obtained by calling 5 1 1.

The Pennsylvania Department of Transportation and Pennsylvania
Turnpike Commission remind motorist to adjust speeds based on
driving conditions as winter weather impacts Pennsylvania
