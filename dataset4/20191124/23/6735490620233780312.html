Shania Twain Returns To AMAs Stage With Explosive Greatest Hits Medley

24 Nov 2019, 23:20 by Katie Colley

Let’s go girls! Shania Twain has made her return to the AMAs stage and she did not disappoint.

Performing at the awards show for the first time in 16 years, the country queen delivered a flawless career-spanning medley of a few of her greatest hits, including “Any Man of Mine”, “Man! I Feel Like A Woman!” and “That Don’t Impress Me Much”.
