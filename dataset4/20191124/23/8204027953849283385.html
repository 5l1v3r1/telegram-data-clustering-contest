Helicopter called to aid search in area of Dayton-Cincinnati Pike crash

24 Nov 2019, 23:18 by Breaking News Staff

MIAMI TWP. — ﻿Crews responded to a crash at Shepherd Road and Dayton-Cincinnati Pike around 4:35 a.m. Reports indicated that a car had crashed into a wooded area.

Reports indicated that officers were unable to find the driver of the vehicle, who was believed to have been ejected from the vehicle. However, Montgomery County dispatch records indicated that the vehicle was stolen and the driver may have fled the scene.

Our crew on scene reported seeing a helicopter searching for the driver and there were emergency crews working in the brush with saws.

It is unknown at this time if the driver was ever found.
