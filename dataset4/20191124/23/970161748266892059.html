I'm A Celebrity fans demand justice for James and Roman after 'impossible' trial

24 Nov 2019, 23:03

I’m A Celebrity viewers are demanding justice for Roman Kemp and James Haskell for being given ‘impossible’ task during today’s live trial.

Eight of the celebrities were put to the test for a roast dinner on the live trial, called Jungle Love Island (complete with Ant and Dec getting texts and everything!).

With 12 stars on the line for those in camp, the team were going strong until the last round.

Lying on a bed filled with snakes, Roman and James were tasked with unscrewing stars from one side of the bed and rescrewing them on the other – with 90 seconds to get all three moved over.

On top of the snakes already on the bed with them, each star was in a box filled with critters and animals including a snappy crab that caused havoc for James.

Unable to complete the trial, a clearly annoyed James snapped at Ant and Dec when they asked him what went wrong.

While normally most people vehemently defend the Geordie duo, this time they backed up rugby star Haskell, claiming it was impossible for the pair of them to complete the challenge in the allotted time slot.

Ant and Dec later defended the trial task, and said: ‘The stand ins did it when we tested it #ImACeleb’ 

Taking to Twitter, they demanded justice for the three stars who would now miss out on a roast dinner meal in camp tonight, after a week of meagre rations.
