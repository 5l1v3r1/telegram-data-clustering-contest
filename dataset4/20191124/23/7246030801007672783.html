Netanyahu Tours Northern Sector, Says Israel Will Foil Iran’s Attack Plans

24 Nov 2019, 23:36 by Hana Levi Julian

Iran is again planning to attack Israel, according to Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, but will not find the IDF asleep on the job whenever the attempt takes place.

Netanyahu said during a security tour of the northern sector on Sunday (Nov. 24) that Iran has not confined its aggression to Israel alone; Tehran is also attacking its own citizens as well.

Netanyahu was briefed on Mt. Avital by GOC Northern Command Maj.-Gen. Amir Baram and the commander of the IDF Bashan Division, Brig.-Gen. Amit Fisher. Defense Minister Naftali Bennett and IDF Deputy Chief-of-Staff Maj.-Gen. Eyal Zamir also attended the tour.
