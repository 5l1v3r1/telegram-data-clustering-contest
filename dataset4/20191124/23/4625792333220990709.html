‘He picked the wrong house’: 82-year-old bodybuilder takes out home invader, 29, with table

24 Nov 2019, 23:17 by Madison Dibble

An 82-year-old woman clocked a 29-year-old man over the head with a table after he attempted to break into her home.

Willie Murphy was in her Rochester, New York, home last Thursday when she heard someone trying to break in. She called 911, but she didn’t wait for someone else to come and rescue her.

Murphy grabbed her kitchen table and swung it at the man, knocking him to the floor and shattering the table. Once he was down, she took a broom and started whacking him with it. When it looked as though he wouldn’t be able to get up and fight back, Murphy grabbed a nearby bottle of shampoo and poured it on his face, leaving him in a painful mess on the floor until police arrived.

During an interview with WHAM, Murphy explained that she's old, but that doesn’t mean she’s not tough.
