Saints’ Vonn Bell Punches Christian McCaffrey In The Facemask Trying To Go For Ball (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 19:33 by Darrelle Lincoln

The New Orleans Saints seem to be in a punching mood on Sunday.

During the matchup against the Carolina Panthers — RB Christian McCaffrey was getting tackled down to the ground from behind when Saints’ Vonn Bell came up swinging and hitting him with a fist in his facemask.
