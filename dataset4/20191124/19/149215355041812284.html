GOP senator blasted for floating Ukraine hacking theory on Fox News

24 Nov 2019, 19:13

Sen. John Kennedy (R-La.) on Sunday told host Chris Wallace during an appearance on Fox News Sunday that he doesn't know if it was Russia or Ukraine who hacked the Democratic National Committee's server in 2016. "Nor do you, nor do any of us," Kennedy said.

Wallace countered that the U.S. intelligence community stands behind the assertion that it was Russia, but Kennedy apparently doesn't think that's enough and continued to give a platform to the theory that it was Ukraine.

Former National Security Council official Fiona Hill dismissed the notion during her impeachment inquiry testimony Friday, calling it a "fictional narrative." Kennedy told Wallace that Hill is entitled to her opinion, but cited the fact that "no rebuttal evidence was allowed to be offered" during the impeachment process.
