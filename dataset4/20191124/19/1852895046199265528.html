Harry Morton: 5 Things About Pink Taco Founder Who Died At 38

24 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Erin Silvia

Pink Taco founder Harry Morton, who is the son of businessman Peter Morton, has reportedly died at the age of 38 after being found unresponsive in his Beverly Hills home on Nov. 23.

Harry Morton, 38, who is best known as the founder of the popular hotspot Pink Taco, has sadly died, according to TMZ. The successful business owner is the son of businessman Peter Morton, 72, who is the co-founder of the Hard Rock Cafe, and was reportedly found unresponsive in his Beverly Hills, CA home on the afternoon of Nov. 23. Although paramedics were called, Harry was pronounced dead at the scene and then transported to the local coroner’s office, where he will undergo an autopsy, TMZ further reported. No foul play was suspected at the time he was found, sources told the outlet.

Here are five things you should know about Harry in the wake of his unexpected passing.

1.) Pink Taco, which he founded in 1999, is one of many restaurants he helped start and run. Harry was part of a famous family of restaurateurs who created the popular Morton’s Steakhouse and the Hard Rock Cafe and Hotel. He was just 18 when he created Pink Taco.

2.) He bought the infamous Viper Room in 2008. Previously owned by actor Johnny Depp, the club and music venue which is located on Sunset Blvd in West Hollywood, CA is perhaps best remembered as the place where actor River Phoenix overdosed and died at the age of 23 in 1993.

3.) He owned a Beverly Hills residence that used to belong to the late Elvis Presley. Harry made headlines for buying the estate, which Elvis previously owned with wife Priscilla Presley, in Mar. He paid about $25.46 million for it, according to the Los Angeles Times. Harry’s father, Peter, also owned the home for two years before selling it for $14.5 million in 2014. Although Harry owned this home at the time of his death, he was found dead in a different Beverly Hills home, according to TMZ.

4.) He’s been romantically linked to more than one Hollywood starlet. He reportedly dated actress Lindsay Lohan back in the 2000s. Her mother, Dina Lohan, took to Instagram after news of his death made its way around the internet and posted a photo of him with Lindsay and the caption, “RIP Harry God has an angel, we love you! Prayers to the family.” Harry’s was also romantically linked to Demi Moore after her breakup from Ashton Kutcher as well as Britney Spears, Paris Hilton, and Jennifer Aniston. At the time of his death, he didn’t appear to be married or have any kids.

5.) The West Hollywood Sheriff’s Station honored him as Reserve Detective of the Year. He was a volunteer sheriff’s deputy in and around Los Angeles, according to TMZ, and the station confirmed he put in hundreds of hours a year supporting its Detective Bureau.

Our healing thoughts go out to all those affected by Harry’s passing.
