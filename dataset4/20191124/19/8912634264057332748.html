Riyadh maritime forum discusses ways of protecting Mideast region’s sea lanes

24 Nov 2019, 19:16

RIYADH: Saudi Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Adel Al-Jubeir met German ambassadors and consuls to the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries and Yemen, in Riyadh on Sunday.

The officials are currently in Saudi Arabia for their regular meeting, held this year in Riyadh.

Also on Sunday, Al-Jubeir met German Minister of State at the Federal Foreign Office Niels Annen. The meeting was attended by a number of senior Saudi and German officials. Ambassadors and consul generals in the GCC and Yemen also attended.
