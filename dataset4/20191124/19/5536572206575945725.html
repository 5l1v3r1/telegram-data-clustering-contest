Steelers Bench Mason Rudolph for Devlin Hodges, New QB Immediately Throws Touchdown (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Darrelle Lincoln

Mason Rudolph is having a rough few weeks.

During the Steelers last game against the Browns, he got his helmet snatched off and hit over the head with it and would receive a $50K fine from the league.

On Sunday, he got benched for playing badly and his back-up in Devlin Hodges showed what he had almost immediately as he tossed a 79-yard TD.
