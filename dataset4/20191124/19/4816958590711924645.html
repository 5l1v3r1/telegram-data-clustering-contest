Italy's SIAS says no casualties reported after Savona motorway bridge collapse

24 Nov 2019, 19:16 by Reuters

MILAN — Toll road operator SIAS said there were no reports of casualties so far after a motorway bridge collapsed earlier on Sunday near the costal town of Savona in northwestern Italy.

SIAS said in a statement that it was making an effort to confirm that that was the case.
