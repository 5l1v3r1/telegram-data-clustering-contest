Full List Of Nominees For 2019 CAF African Player Of The Year

24 Nov 2019, 19:46 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

The Confederation of African Football (CAF) has released the shortlist; nominees for the 2019 African Player of the Year award on Sunday, Concise News reports.

Concise News understands that in the 30-man shortlist for the 2019 African Player of the Year award, two Nigerians – Victor Osimhen and Wilfred Ndidi – are in the battle for the prize.

Also included in the CAF 2019 AFPOTY shortlist are Egyptian winger Mohamed Salah, Percy Tau of South Africa, Sadio Mane and Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang.

Salah had beaten Sane his Liverpool teammate to win the 2018 CAF Player of the Year prize.

Nigeria’s Odion Ighalo who was the top scorer at the 2019 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) made the shortlist for the CAF Player of the Year.

2016 winner Riyad Mahrez and Atletico Madrid midfielder Thomas Partey are also gunning for the prize.

Concise News understands that the CAF Player of the Year prize will be awarded on Tuesday, January 7 2020, at Citadel Azure, Hurghada, Egypt.
