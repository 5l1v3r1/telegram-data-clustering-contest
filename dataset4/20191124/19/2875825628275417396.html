Harry Morton dead: Lindsay Lohan's ex and Pink Taco founder dies aged 38

The businessman and son of Hard Rock Cafe founder Peter Morton has died after being found unresponsive at his home

24 Nov 2019, 19:51 by Irishmirror.ie

Businessman Harry Morton, who famously dated Lindsay Lohan, has been found dead at the age of 38.

The Pink Taco founder has died after being found unresponsive at his home by a relative on Saturday, TMZ reports.

Harry was the son of Hard Rock Cafe co-founder Peter Morton.

It's reported that Harry was pronounced dead at his Beverly Hills home.

It's said that foul play is not suspected and an autopsy will be carried out to determine the cause of his death.

Harry founded popular US restaurant chain Pink Taco when he was just 18-years-old.

His grandfather Arnie was also in the restaurant business and co-founded Morton's Steakhouse back in 1978.

Harry famously dated Mean Girls actress Lindsay in the 2000s.

The businessman was also romantically linked to Demi Moore, Britney Spears and Paris Hilton at various stages.

Harry had begun working for his dad at the Hard Rock Cafe before branching out on his own.

He also bought the Viper Room nightclub in 2008.

The Hollywood club is the place where actor River Phoenix infamously died of a drug overdose in 1993.

Harry also owned Elvis Presley's former Beverly Hills estate, but it is not believed that Harry died at that residence.

Harry, who is survived by his parents Petr and Paulene, was recently named Reserve Detective of the Year by the West Hollywood Sheriff's Station.
