'LOOKING LIKE A GODDESS'

Karen Gillan stuns in yellow suit as she channels TMNT’s April O’Neil for Jumanji press day

24 Nov 2019, 19:16 by Rachel Macpherson

SCOTS actress Karen Gillan has channelled her inner April O'Neil in a yellow suit as she geared up for a Jumanji press day.

The Avengers actress posed for the camera - as fans begged her to play the famous Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles character.

Taking to Instagram, Karen wrote: "April O’Neil goes to the Jumanji Cabo press day 3!"

The stunning snaps racked up thousands of likes online while followers hailed the actress as a "goddess".

One fan said: "Looking like a goddess."

While another wrote: "You would be awesome as April."

And one Instagram user added: "Hell yeah let’s go teenage mutant ninja turtles."

Another follower commented: "You are so perfect."

It comes as the actress promotes her new film Jumanji The Next Level.

The film follows the 2017 film Jumanji Welcome To The Jungle, and will see Karen reprising her role as Ruby Roundhouse.

This weekend will also see the anticipated release of Karen's new film The Party's Just Beginning - which is set to hit cinemas on December 1.

Earlier this month, we told how the actress almost missed out on her role as Marvel character Nebula.

It came after Karen was spotted partying at the Belladrum Festival in August.
