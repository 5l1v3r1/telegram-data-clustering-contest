Ayrshire sides are patiently waiting to find out their Scottish Cup fourth-round opponents.

Both Kilmarnock and Ayr United have entered at this stage, but Auchinleck Talbot still have work to do as they need to see off Arbroath in their third-round replay on Wednesday night.

Tommy Sloan's men took the lead through Dwayne Hyslop, but a late penalty earned Dick Campbell's side a draw.

The draw takes place from 7.15pm on BBC Scotland.
