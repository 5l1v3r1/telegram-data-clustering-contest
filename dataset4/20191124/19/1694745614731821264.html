You have two choices when it comes to your home's entryway. One, you can treat it as a boring pass-through space and, design-wise, pretty much pretend it doesn't exist. Or two, you can give it the style respect it deserves (after all, it is a guest's first introduction to your home and your personal dumping ground for shoes, mail, bags, and packages). Not surprisingly, we highly suggest the latter option and have enlisted Amazon to help.

Amazon offers a huge selection of chic decor items ready to amp up your entryway both in terms of style and functionality. From cool rugs, mod mirrors, and purposeful wall art to benches, storage solutions, hooks, and more, Amazon is ready to transform your entryway, no matter how big or small it might be, from an afterthought to the front-and-center space it should be — without blowing your budget. Keep reading to find the 24 Amazon home decor items your entryway needs now.
