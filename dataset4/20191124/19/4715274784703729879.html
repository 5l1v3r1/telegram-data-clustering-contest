Chef's Corner: Restaurant Verbena welcomes new executive chef

24 Nov 2019, 19:55 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

After five years of lending his talent to the award-winning 22 Prime Steakhouse, Executive Chef Gerwin Bailon heads to Tagaytay to lead Discovery Country Suites’ Restaurant Verbena. 

Chef Gerwin has since been hard at work bringing his expertise to the steak-hungry Tagaytay market. Restaurant Verbena recently introduced 22 Prime items such as USDA Prime Rib Eye, Surf and Turf, and New York Striploin.

Chef Gerwin’s latest brainchild, Unli Steak & Eggs, a lunch event on November 30, brings together the best of both flagship establishments with unlimited USDA Prime Rib, a live egg station, and an array of sides such as Mushroom Pilaf Rice, Truffle Potato Gratin, Tagaytay Vegetables, and Farfale Pasta with Sundried Tomato Sauce. 

Restaurant Verbena is at Discovery Country Suites along 300 Calamba Road, San Jose, Tagaytay City. Call (02) 8529-8172 or email reservations@dcs.discovery.com.ph for inquiries and reservations.
