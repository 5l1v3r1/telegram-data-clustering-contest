Former Crystal Palace chairman Simon Jordan is doubtful that Jose Mourinho will be a success at Tottenham Hotspur.

Spurs chairman Daniel Levy decides to part company with Mauricio Pochettino on Tuesday night after five and a half years in charge.
 
Just months after guiding the club to the Champions final, Spurs had struggled under the Argentine this season having managed to win just three of their opening twelve Premier League matches.
 
With Jose Mourinho appointed as his successor, the Portuguese is tasked with getting his side to climb the table.
 
Jordan believes that while Spurs are right to sack Pochettino, he’s unsure whether Mourinho can bring success back to North London.
