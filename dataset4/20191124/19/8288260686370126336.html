‘Inevitable’ - The Leeds United claim that will surprise West Brom fans

Latest West Brom news includes former Leeds striker's bold promotion prediction

24 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Leeds United are winning the ‘mental’ side of the Championship promotion battle – and it’s ‘inevitable’ they will knock West Bromwich Albion off top spot soon.

That’s the view of former Leeds striker Jermaine Beckford who thinks the Whites have got the upper hand despite Slaven Bilic’s side enjoying a two-point lead over them.

The Baggies kept their place at the Championship’s summit with a dramatic victory over Sheffield Wednesday at The Hawthorns.

A goal apiece for strike pairing Hal Robson-Kanu and Charlie Austin at either end of the game saw off the 10-man Owls, who equalised through a penalty from Steven Fletcher.

Leeds ensured they kept up on the pressure on Albion with a late win of their own, Matt Pearson's late own goal condemning Luton Town to a defeat.

Albion have been beaten only once in the league so far - by Leeds back in October - and Beckford reckons they will be worried by the form of their leading promotion rival.

Beckford said on LUTV: "You know what, West Brom will be looking over their shoulders thinking 'wow, what more can we do?'

"They will be worrying about what's happening over here, rather than us worrying about what's happening down there.

"I think in terms of the mental game, Leeds United are winning all day long.

"West Brom, I think are going to go through a blip and when they do, they are really going to start worrying because [Preston], although they lost today, are a team that have been on form as well as us.

"So it's inevitable in my opinion that we're going to go top and Preston are going to end up putting a lot of pressure on West Brom as well."
