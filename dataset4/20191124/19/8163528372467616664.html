American Music Awards 2019: Complete List of Nominees and Winners

24 Nov 2019, 19:53 by SPIN Staff

The biggest stars and VIPs of the music industry are celebrating another year in music at the American Music Awards on Sunday, November 24. Ciara hosted the ceremony, which was held at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

Among the performers were Green Day, who celebrated the 25th anniversary of their album Dookie by playing one of the record’s biggest hits, “Basket Case,” as well as the title track to their upcoming album, “Father of All….” Billie Eilish, Post Malone with Ozzy Osbourne and Travis Scott, Taylor Swift, Lizzo, and many others also took the stage.

Post Malone led the pack with a total of seven nominations, while first-time nominee Eilish and Ariana Grande both had six nods, and Lil Nas X and Swift are tied at five. See the full list of nominees and winners below.
