Winter Wonderland acrobat in 'horrific' fall 30ft in front of shocked families

The performer at Cirque Berserk at Hyde Park's Winter Wonderland in central London is reported to have slipped from her harness during a performance this evening

24 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Tom Davidson, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/tom-davidson/

An acrobat performing at Winter Wonderland in Hyde Park fell 30ft in front of horrified families this evening.

The performer at Zippos Cirque Berserk, 35-year-old Jackie Armstrong, is said to have slipped from her harness during the 6pm show.

Pictures posted online show an ambulance outside the circus.

In a statement Zippos Cirus said that Jackie, a mum-of-one, is a highly-experienced circus artist with 10 years in the ring.

The added: "Jackie was immediately attended to on the scene by first aiders and an ambulance called.

"She was taken to hospital by ambulance and further information on the nature of her injuries is awaited."

One witness told Mirror Online: "To see her fall was horrific and we hope that she is well and recovers soon".

They added: "They are all genuinely lovely people.

"I saw her falling and instantly it went silent all you heard was her hit the floor and everyone gasp."

A witness told Sun Online: “It was horrific. I thought it was part of the act but when she didn’t get back up I knew something was wrong.

“A group of people ran on to help her and people were shouting for a paramedic.

“I thought I heard her scream or wail, so hopefully she is conscious."

They added that they saw paramedics rush into the arena as they were leaving.

Circus Founder and Director Martin Burton commented, “Jackie is a very experienced artist whom we value dearly as part of our circus family.

"Clearly we are concerned and offering her and her family our full support and sympathy at this time.

"At this stage we cannot comment further as we have no information on any injuries she may have sustained.”

After the fall audience members were asked to leave the Big Top and offered tickets for the next performance that evening.

Zippos Circus is a popular attraction at Hyde Park Winter Wonderland in the heart of London.
