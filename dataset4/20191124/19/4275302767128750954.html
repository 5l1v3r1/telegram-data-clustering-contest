Scottish Cup draw hands both Dundee clubs Premiership opposition at home

Dundee United and Dundee were both handed ties against Premiership opposition when the fourth round draw of the William Hill Scottish Cup was made.

24 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Ian Roache

The Tangerines will play Hibs at Tannadice, while the Dark Blues have been drawn against Motherwell at Dens.

The fixtures will have to be spread over the weekend of January 18-19 and you imagine the TV companies will be interested in one or both games.

St Johnstone, fresh from their controversial league clash against Aberdeen, came out of the hat along with the winners of the Brora Rangers v Greenock Morton replay. That match will be in Perth.

Like the Dundee sides, Raith Rovers have been given Premiership opposition but they will have to travel to Livingston.

Should Arbroath win their replay against Auchinleck Talbot then they will host Falkirk at Gayfield.
