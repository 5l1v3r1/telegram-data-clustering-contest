Former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg is running for president

24 Nov 2019, 19:07 by Nick Fitzgerald

His participation is specifically a stand against Donald Trump

After much deliberation, former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg has announced he is officially running for president.

The 77-year-old billionaire has been mulling the idea back and forth for quite some time. It was only back in March he said he would not make a 2020 run for the White House.

However, the tides have changed, with Bloomberg hitting out at the Donald, saying he is specifically running for president to defeat Trump and "rebuild America".
