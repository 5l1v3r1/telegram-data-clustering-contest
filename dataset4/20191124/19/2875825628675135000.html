Fair City's Johnny Ward hints he may find love in 2020 after splitting with girlfriend

The Dancing With The Stars pin-up recently split up with Rachel after six years together

24 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Jim Gallagher

Soap star Johnny Ward has hinted 2020 may be the year his love life blossoms - after losing his girlfriend of six years.

The actor’s career has gone into overdrive, meaning he’s been much too busy for romance.

But he reckons next year that could change.

The Dancing With The Stars pin-up, who has shot down suggestions he’s seeing his former dance partner Emily Barker, said: “Maybe I will meet someone in 2020. Who knows what will happen?”

The Fair City and Love/Hate actor added: “I’ve had no time for a love life.”

The 32-year-old star surprised fans earlier this year when he and girlfriend Rachel split up after six years.

It came after the death of his beloved father John while he was taking part in DWTS and then his mother Kathy suffered a heart attack.

Johnny added: “With myself and Rachel, it was just really unfortunate. But my relationship and that of my two sisters all ended.

“Since my father passed it was like something came into the house and ripped everything apart.

“I never saw it coming. I was very, very happy and I would love to be able to tell you all these horrible things about Rachel but I can’t because she is an absolutely incredible person.

“She is someone who I would love to still have contact with but I haven’t spoken to her in a very long time and we didn’t end on bad terms at all.

“I just needed to move back in with my mum and be there for her and it was just a very difficult time.

“I suppose that thing with my father – he made me promise that no matter what happened, I had to stay in the competition and do it for him – made it very difficult. I think I became slightly obsessed with it.

“Everyone else seemed to enjoy it but I didn’t because that’s all I was thinking about and I just needed to be on my own for a long time. It’s still a bit like that.

“I would love to say I’m back out dating but I’ve had no time. I went straight into the show, Copper Face Jacks, after double jobbing with Fair City and Dancing With the Stars rehearsals.

“It’s been so busy but I wouldn’t have it any other way. It’s a good distraction and now I’m preparing for the panto, Aladdin, in Limerick University Concert Hall.

“These are all little stepping stones to keeping busy, it’s a little distraction after everything that’s happened.”

The star revealed over the weekend that he was not seeing his 22-year-old dance partner Emily and that he looked on her like his “little sister”.
