Fremantle star Jesse Hogan hospitalised after car crash in Perth

24 Nov 2019, 19:52

Fremantle forward Jesse Hogan has been hospitalised after he was involved in a car accident in Perth on Sunday morning.

A club spokesperson confirmed Hogan was taken to Fiona Stanley Hospital as a precaution but has since been released.

The key forward did not sustain serious injuries in the crash, which saw him hit a parked car in the Perth town of Bicton at around 11pm AWDT.

It is understood the 24-year-old’s air bag deployed, with a witness reportedly calling an ambulance to the scene..

He will not train with the Dockers tomorrow, with the club confirming he has been given the day off.

A Fremantle team doctor was with him at the time while he was in hospital.

His manager Jason Dover told the Herald Sun Hogan would make a quick recovery.

“He is fine. He presented to hospital and they were worried about concussion symptoms after a member of the public rang the ambulance, but he was discharged quickly and he has been cleared by the doctors,” Dover said.
