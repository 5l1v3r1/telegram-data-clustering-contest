John Boyega on hanging out with Robert Downey Jr.

24 Nov 2019, 17:59

John Boyega has opened up about his sweet friendship with fellow film star Robert Downey Jr. in a new interview.

In fact the Star Wars: Rise of Skywalker star says the Avengers: Endgame icon has become something of a mentor to him.

John, 27, told The Times: ‘When I was starting out, I didn’t really know what to think and I wanted to get some advice.

‘I was a huge fan of [Robert’s]. I think our agents spoke, and then he called me up out of nowhere and we had a good long chat.

What happened next is pretty amazing.

‘He was, like, “Next time you’re in LA, come to the house,”‘ John explained. ‘And I went to his frikkin’ amazing house — and Orlando Bloom was there eating pancakes. I stayed there all Sunday eating good food with nice people.’

Robert’s reputation as one of the nicest guys in Hollywood continues. What we wouldn’t have given to have been a fly on the wall at that particular brunch…

As well as the Star Wars films, John has appeared in movies such as Detroit, The Circle and Half of a Yellow Sun.

Meanwhile Robert’s film credits include Sherlock Holmes, Kiss Kiss Bang Bang and Zodiac.

Elsewhere in the interview, John, who is single, discussed whether or not thirsty fans slide into his DMs.

‘My DMs are dry!’ he joked. ‘The other day, my mates were, like, “Let’s go in your DMs, let’s see what it’s like [to be famous].” My boy scrolls through, like [he grimaces], “Bruv, this person is asking you what planet Tatooine is on.”’

Classic.
