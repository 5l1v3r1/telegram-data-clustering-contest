Two Found Dead in South-Eastern France Following Heavy Storms, Floods – Reports

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - Two people were found dead in separate locations in south-eastern France after storms and floods battered the region, France Bleu reported on Sunday.

24 Nov 2019, 17:04

The first victim was a 40-year-old man who went missing a day prior when a rescue boat he was on capsised in Le Muy commune, according to local radio station France Bleu.

The second man, according to the radio station, was a 50-year-old local hunter who was found deceased in his car in the Cabasse commune. Rescuers suspect the car was flooded with the man inside.
