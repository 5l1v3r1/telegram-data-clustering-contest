Grand Chess Tour 2019: Magnus Carlsen defeats Viswanathan Anand in round 7

Viswanathan Anand’s 19th move with Rook to e8 was a blunder that allowed Magnus Carlsen to grab the initiative.

24 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Sports Desk

Five-time world champion Viswanathan Anand failed to utilise most of his opportunities in the final game as he finished joint sixth while current World Champion Magnus Carlsen stamped his authority in the rapid chess to win by a huge margin in the Tata Steel Rapid and Blitz chess tournament on Sunday.

Day three of the India leg of the Grand Chess Tour witnessed the highly-awaited round seven clash between Carlsen and Anand, who were facing off on Indian soil for the first time since their World Championship match in Chennai in 2013.
