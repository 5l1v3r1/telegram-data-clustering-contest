Cleveland Browns Fans Use Helmet To Hit A Mason Rudolph Pinata Before Game (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Darrelle Lincoln

Cleveland Browns fans are taking the fight between Myles Garrett and Mason Rudolph to hilarious levels.

Before the team was slated to face off against the Miami Dolphins on Sunday, the fan base got a Mason Rudolph pinata, a Steelers helmet, and blindfolded a guy as he tried to hit it.
