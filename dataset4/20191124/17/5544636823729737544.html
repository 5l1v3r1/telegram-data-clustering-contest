Full Shortlist For Nigerian Navy 27 DSSC 2019 Recruitment Aptitude Test (PDF)

24 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

The Nigerian Navy has released the shortlist (PDF) for the 2019 DSSC recruitment with the aptitude test set to take place on November 30th, 2019.

When Is Nigerian Navy DSSC 27 Aptitude Test 2019?

Concise News understands that the Nigerian Navy 27 DSSC recruitment aptitude test will take place in nine centers in the country by (7:30 am).

All candidates who made the Nigerian Navy DSSC 27 2019 recruitment shortlist are to immediately check for their aptitude test centers.

Note, however, that there are two centers for the Nigerian Navy DSSC 27 aptitude test 2019 in Lagos, Abuja and Port-Harcourt.

Requirements for Nigerian Navy DSSC 27 2019 Aptitude Test

According to a statement by the Nigerian Navy, those who made the shortlist for the aptitude test for the DSSC 27 should follow these requirements; instructions”

1.  Only those on a centre’s list would be allowed into the centre for the test.
2. Candidates are to come along to the Aptitude Test Centres with the following:
a. Parents/Guardian Consent Form.
b. Photocopies of Degree/HND certificate.
c. NYSC Discharge/Exemption Certificate (Compulsory. Serving Personnel to present a letter from unit /ID card).
d. Writing materials (2B pencil, eraser and sharpener only)
3. Mobile phones and any other forms of communication devices are prohibited from the examination halls.

The aptitude test for the Nigerian Navy DSSC is free of charge just as the selection interview.
