Alavés cruise to a 0-2 victory over Eibar at Ipurua Municipal Stadium

After this win, Asier Garitano's squad has 18 points from 14 games

24 Nov 2019, 17:14 by SPORT.es

Alavés staggered past Eibar in a 0-2 victory on Sunday at Ipurua Municipal Stadium. Eibar faced the match with a desire to make up their league after losing against Real Madrid 0-4 while Alavés won 3-0 the last league match against Real Valladolid. At the moment, Eibar is 16th in the LaLiga table with 15 points from 14 matches, while Alavés sits 13th with 18 points after 14 matches.

There were no goals during the first half and the first 45 minutes ended 0-0.

Alavés took the lead in the second half, with a goal from Joselu 85 minutes in the game. Later, Alavés scored once more and increased their lead 0-2 thanks to another goal from Joselu, scoring his second goal in the 90th minute just before the end of the second half, finalising the game with a 0-2.

For Eibar Charles, Pedro Bigas and Ivan Ramis came on for Sergi Enrich, Jose Angel and Pape Diop, while Alavés brought on Pere Pons and Joselu to replace Borja Sainz and Lucas Perez.

The referee booked Anaitz Arbilla from Eibar, while for Alavés Navarro Ximo and Mubarak Wakaso saw a yellow card as well.

Eibar with their next fixture against Real Sociedad at away stadium, while Alavés will face Real Madrid at home stadium.
