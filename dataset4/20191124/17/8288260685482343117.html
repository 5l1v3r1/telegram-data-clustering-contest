SUSPENSIONS

Harlee Dean will serve the final match of his three game ban.

Dean was shown a straight red card by referee Andrew Madley in the 4-2 defeat to Cardiff City.

Jake Clarke-Salter stepped in as his replacement against Fulham and Huddersfield Town.

INJURIES

Maikel Kieftenbeld

Injury

Knee

Details

The Blues midfielder went down in pain during the second half of the 1-0 win against Leeds in April and had to be taken off the pitch on a stretcher.

Then boss Garry Monk admitted afterwards that it didn’t look good and a scan confirmed Kieftenbeld would be set for a lengthy spell on the sidelines.

The Dutchman damaged the anterior cruciate ligament in his right knee - he might not be back until Christmas.

Last match: Birmingham City 1 Leeds United 0 - Saturday, April 6

What Clotet said: "From the reports that I have we are expecting him to try to join us in training for January or February, we are not putting much pressure on it because every knee is different but I do know he is doing very well in his recovery and probably the timing will be around that.

“Normally it’s around six to eight months to be on the grass so he is going to hit that because he is a very good pro and he has done everything by the book with his knee. He is on the right track.”

Potential return date: New Year

Gary Gardner

Injury

Knee

Details

Gary Gardner has undergone a ‘minor operation’ on a knee injury that will keep him out until at least after the international break.

The Birmingham City midfielder hurt himself as he took a late free kick in the 1-0 defeat at Leeds and has missed every game since.

Last match: Leeds United 1-0 Birmingham City - October 19

What Clotet said: Clotet was asked if Gardner will be available the Huddersfield game: “I don’t know if that will be a lot of pressure for him, they said four to six weeks, I was speaking to him last week, he looks much better, he looks fine and stable and has been doing a lot of work.

“I don’t want to put that pressure on him to be available, it needs to be when it is sorted, but it’s going very well – so possibly.”

Potential return date: Soon
