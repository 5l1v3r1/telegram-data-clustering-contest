Silly Phishing Spotlight: Login to Unblock Microsoft Excel

24 Nov 2019, 17:34 by Lawrence Abrams

As part of our ongoing series to educate users about some of the more silly phishing scams out there, we bring a new one that states Excel is blocked unless you login and verify your details.

As people get more educated about phishing scams and how to spot them, we continue to see scammers create outlandish campaigns in order to bait people into entering their login credentials.

Such is the case with this new phishing email that states you wont be able to use you Excel due to a "system delay" unless you first login.

The full text of this email is below.

YOU WILL NOT BE ABLE TO OPEN EXCEL OR ANY DOCUMENT FILE DUE TO SYSTEM DELAY. 
KINDLY CLICK BELOW AND COMPLETE THE DETAIL TO VERIFY YOUR ACCOUNT.  

       CONFIGURE EXCEL AND PDF SETTINGS

If a user clicks on the "Configure Excel and PDF Settings", they will be brought to a login form hosted on SharePoint that pretends to be an Excel shared document.

If a user enters their credentials, the information will be saved so that attackers can access and it used for BEC scams or take over other accounts the victim owns.

As always, if you find a suspicious email related to your email accounts or login credentials, before clicking any links or entering your credentials, be sure to contact your system administrator.
