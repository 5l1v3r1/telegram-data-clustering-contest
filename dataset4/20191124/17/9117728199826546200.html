Horner: Team is always the loser in an incident like Leclerc and Vettel

24 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Tom Mason

Aston Martin Red Bull Racing boss Christian Horner believes that the team is the biggest loser when two drivers collide. This comes after Ferrari duo Charles Leclerc and Sebastian Vettel collided towards the end of the Brazillian Grand Prix.

“It depends on the scenario and each team has a different approach, and different people have different approaches, so it's difficult for me to judge Ferrari's situation,” Horner told Motorsport.com

“But the loser is always the team in that situation. It is a tricky one.”

“I'm glad it's not my drivers,” he said. “It's always a difficult situation.

Horner saw a similar clash also with Vettel when he collided with teammate Mark Webber at the Turkish Grand Prix in 2010.
