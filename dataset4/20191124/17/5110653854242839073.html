Browns fans are hitting a Mason Rudolph piñata with a Steelers helmet

24 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Tadd Haislop

Whether or not they should be, Browns fans are having a fiesta with the Cleveland-Pittsburgh brawl story.

A few days after the NFL's indefinite suspension ofBrowns defensive end Myles Garrettfor his role in last week's fight was upheld, some of the Cleveland faithful were filmed swinging a Steelers helmet at a Mason Rudolph piñata before their team's Week 13 home game against the Dolphins.

Seriously. Cleveland.com shared the video Sunday morning.
