New Year’s Eve in Dubai 2019: Dubai’s Soho Garden to throw double New Year’s Eve party

One ticket, two parties, unlimited drinks…

24 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Reema Rahman

Dubai party people – it’s time to starting planning the biggest night out of the year.

New Year’s Eve is fast approaching (where has the time gone…) and if you’re looking to enter 2020 with a bang, listen up, because Soho Garden is throwing not one, but two mega parties.

The giant entertainment complex at Meydan Racecourse, which recently underwent a revamp, will be hosting Berlin-based house and techno DJ Dixon plus international party brand elrow who will be welcoming Solardo for the occasion.

It will be the second year in a row that elrow takeover Soho Beach for New Year’s Eve and if last year is anything to go by, you’re in for a cracking night.

World-renowned for its epic fiesta style parties, the famed festival concept will be returning with its Sambowdromo do Brasil edition which promises more music, more decorations and most importantly, more fun.

Meanwhile Dixon, who’s known for wowing crowds at some of the world’s biggest clubs and festivals, will play at Soho Garden.

Each bash can accommodate up to 6,000 people with the entry ticket granting you access to both. The night kicks off at 9pm and tickets cost Dhs799 which include unlimited drinks all night long.
