One hospitalized in crash

24 Nov 2019, 17:18 by Laura Brookes

Kamloops RCMP are investigating the cause of a head-on collision involving two motor vehicles at Halston Ave and 14th St.

The crash occurred at approximately 6:10 a.m. on Sunday morning. Police, fire and ambulance services responded to the collision. 

Two male individuals were rescued from the vehicles and transported to local medical facilities for treatment. 

“A 35 year old Kamloops man was transported by ambulance in serious but stable condition, while a 28 year old Calgary man was briefly treated and released from medical care.” says Sgt. Brandon Buliziuk. 

“Alcohol and icy road surfaces are believed to have been factors in the collision and an investigation is ongoing accordingly. Upon completion, information will be forwarded to the British Columbia Prosecution Service for assessment and approval of applicable charges, if any.”
