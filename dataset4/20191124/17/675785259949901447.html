Maura Higgins risks flashing everything in dramatic dress at Radio 1 Teen Awards

The Love Island star joined the likes of Strictly's Saffron Barker, Little Mix's Jade Thirlwall and Perrie Edwards and fellow Islander Amber Gill at the showbiz bash

24 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Maura Higgins dared to bare on the Radio 1 Teen Awards red carpet as she risked flashing everything.

The Love Island beauty looked incredible as she headed into the annual showbiz bash at London's Television Centre.

The Irish reality babe wore a white dress with a dramatic plunging neckline and an equally-dramatic thigh split that went right up to her stomach.

Her dress had long sleeves that were trimmed with feathers that made her stand out from the crowd even more.

She posed up on the red carpet with her boyfriend Curtis, who looked smart in a crisp white shirt and fitted trousers with black loafers.

The happy couple wrapped their arms around each other as they smiled for photographers.

Curtis also posed up with his big brother, Strictly Come Dancing professional AJ Pritchard.

AJ opted for an all-black look of fitted trousers, a T-shirt and chunky lace up boots.

Little Mix beauties Jade Thirlwall and Perrie Edwards also hit the red carpet, although they were there without their bandmates Jesy Nelson and Leigh-Anne Pinnock.

The girls held hands as they posed for snappers.

Jade bared her legs in a gorgeous asymmetric blazer dress and strappy heels, while Perrie wore sheer black tights underneath a stunning strappy black dress that was trimmed with diamontes.

Jade let her long hair hang free in loose curls while Perrie opted to pull her blonde tresses back in a classic up do.

Strictly's Saffron Barker was there too, braving the cold in a strapless black minidress with a scarf wrapped around her shoulders.

Love Island's Amber drew attention to her enviable pins in a cute corset dress with a sheer polka dot skirt and matching sleeves.

She posed on the red carpet with co-star and pal Ovie Soko.

Now that they're both single, people are dying to see them couple up.

But Amber has described the hunky basketball player as her "brother".

Strictly's Dev Griffin and Diane Buswell reunited as they attended the event together.

Diane accentuated her signature red locks with a shimmering mauve one-shouldered dress, that she paired with polka dot tights and heels.

Dev looked smart in navy checked trousers and a red velvet jacket on top of a black T-shirt.

TV presenter Maya Jama posed up on the carpet with former Saturdays star Mollie King.

Maya opted for a green dress with a dramatic thigh split and boots that matched her gown, while Mollie went for a yellow qipao-style dress.

TOWIE and X Factor : Celebrity star Megan McKenna was there too.

She stood out from the crowd in silver glitterball trousers and a white top an blazer.
