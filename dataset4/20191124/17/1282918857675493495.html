'ALL IS WELL': Ajit Pawar Appeals To NCP For A Little Patience; Opens Up On Sharad Pawar

24 Nov 2019, 17:32 by Navashree Nandini

Politics

Making intent clear & hinting at an alliance of BJP-NCP in Maharashtra, new Deputy Chief Minister Ajit Pawar said that both parties will provide stable govt.

Written By Navashree Nandini | Mumbai | Updated On: November 24, 2019 17:32 IST

Making his intent clear and hinting at an alliance of BJP and NCP in Maharashtra, new Deputy Chief Minister Ajit Pawar on Sunday said that both parties will provide a stable government in the state. Taking to Twitter right after thanking the BJP leaders, he said that there is no need to worry as 'all is well'. He maintained that he is an NCP leader and Sharad Pawar is their leader. 

Even as all the MLAs believed to be at his side, are said to have returned to Sharad Pawar, the use of the term "our" by Ajit Pawar raises many questions. The deputy CM also said that he will always be an NCP leader and both parties will work towards the "welfare of the State and its people." He thanked the people of the state for their support and urged for patience.
