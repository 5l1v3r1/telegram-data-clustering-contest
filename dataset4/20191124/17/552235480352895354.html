Woman rushed to hospital after being struck by car in Fife crash

The pedestrian was knocked down on Saturday night and rushed to hospital.

24 Nov 2019, 17:58 by Kathleen Speirs

A woman suffered serious injuries after being hit by a car in Fife.

The 49-year-old was knocked down on Church Street in Glenrothes at around 6.45pm on Saturday.

Emergency services raced the woman to Ninewells Hospital .

Medics say she is now in a stable condition.

Police probing the incident are appealing for any witnesses to come forward.

Sgt Alastair Purvis said: “The woman was taken to hospital with serious injuries but is now in stable condition.

"We are appealing for anyone who may have witnessed the crash to come forward.”

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 3299 of November 23.
