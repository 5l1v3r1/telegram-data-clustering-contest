Motorcyclist airlifted to hospital following collision in Sooke

24 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Roxanne Egan-Elliott

One person was airlifted to hospital after a motorcycle crash in Sooke Sunday afternoon.

B.C. Emergency Health Services received a call at 2:36 p.m. about an accident on West Coast Road near the intersection of Atherley Close, just down the road from an ambulance station. Paramedics were on the scene with two ambulances and an air ambulance in about a minute.
