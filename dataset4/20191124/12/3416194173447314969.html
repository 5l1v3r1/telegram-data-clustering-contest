Here's How Taylor Swift Addressed Feud with Scooter Braun at AMAs 2019

24 Nov 2019, 12:27

This is the moment we were all waiting for at the 2019 American Music Awards – Taylor Swift addressed the feud she is in with Scooter Braun and Big Machine’s Scott Borchetta.

The 29-year-old singer won the Artist of the Decade Award and kicked off her career-spanning performance by addressing the situation on Sunday (November 24) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

You’re probably aware that Taylor told fans that she was blocked from performing old songs during her Artist of the Decade performance as her old master recordings are owned by Big Machine. While the label said she can perform anything she wants, the real issue would be the distribution of the performance on platforms like YouTube and others.

Taylor kicked off the performance wearing a shirt that featured the titles of her past albums, Taylor Swift, Fearless, Speak Now, Red, 1989, and Reputation. She left off the one album from her new label, Lover.

While singing “The Man,” a song that imagines how the media would treat her if she were a man, Taylor was joined on stage by kids who also wore the same shirt. She then ripped off the shirt to perform old songs like “Love Story” and “I Knew You Were Trouble.”
