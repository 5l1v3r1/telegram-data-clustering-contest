Watch Taylor Swift's Artist of the Decade Speech at AMAs 2019 (Video)

24 Nov 2019, 12:53

Taylor Swift focused only on positivity during her acceptance speech at the 2019 American Music Awards while being presented with the Artist of the Decade Award.

The 29-year-old singer dedicated the special win to the legendary artist Carole King, who presented the award, on Sunday (November 24) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

Taylor thanked Carole for showing that her songs “could transcend so many different changes in people’s lives.”

“All any of the artists in this room want is to create something that will last,” she said. “And all that matters to me is the memories that I have had with you, the fans, over the years, because we’ve had fun, incredible exhilarating times together. Thank you for being the reason I am on this stage.”

Many fans were expecting Taylor to address her feud with Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta during the speech, but she only addressed it with a subtle moment during her performance.
