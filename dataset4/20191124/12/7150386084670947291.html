Saina Nehwal Pulls Out of Premier Badminton League to Prepare For Next Season

Saina, who played for North Eastern Warriors in last PBL last, will not be seen in action in the fifth edition to be played between January 20 and February 9.

24 Nov 2019, 12:07

Plenty of injuries this season has forced Indian badminton star Saina Nehwal to pull out of the Premier Badminton League (PBL) this year. The 29-year-old Saina, who played for North Eastern Warriors in last PBL last, will not be seen in action in the fifth edition to be played between January 20 and February 9.

“Hey everyone, I won’t be part of the PBL Season 5. I haven’t been well most part of the year due to pancreatitis and injuries and I would like to take time during the PBL to prepare better,” Saina said in a tweet on Sunday.
