Kim Kardashian compares her celebrity life struggles to Prince Harry and Meghan Markle

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians star took time out of making an advert for Uber Eats to talk with Australia's The Project TV show

24 Nov 2019, 12:46 by Mirror .co.uk

Kim Kardashian says she knows how it feels to be a member of the Royal family as she compared her overexposed reality star life to that of Prince Harry and Meghan Markel, the Duchess of Sussex.

The 39-year-old reality star shot to fame after a sex tape went viral in 2007 preceding the launch of reality show Keeping Up With The Kardashians later that same year.

Now Kim - who has actively sought out fame at any cost - agrees herself and her famous siblings and parents are the only people in the world on a par with the Royal Family in terms of recognisability.

Speaking to Australian entertainment show The Project, Kim drew parallels between her own life and that of Prince Harry, the Duke of Sussex, 35, and his 38-year-old American wife, Meghan, Duchess of Sussex.

“I think everyone has their own journey and I think their journey is extremely personal with what his mother experienced and how her life was so affected by the spotlight and by paparazzi,” Kimmy told Lisa Wilkinson.

“I don’t think anyone can know what that is like except for them,” Kim went on.

“I think, as I’m older and i have kids, and what I would want for them, is just the safest place. I can definitely empathise with their need for having a secure safe place and taking time for themselves and having privacy when they need it,” she continued.

Kim then wanted to make it clear she wasn’t complaining about being in the spotlight herself.

“I’m not one of those people that will complain and be like ‘I don’t have any privacy’ or ‘my life is so different’,” she said.

Going on, she revealed her biggest struggle in life is being able to keep in touch with friends.

“I think the hardest part is keeping up with my friends. I have the best friends I could ever have wished for and they’re all girls I grew up with. But you do have to put in work to maintain those relationships,” she said.

Over the years, Kim has given fans and followers full access to her entire life - despite no one asking to see it.

She has shared a sex tape she made with Ray J, home life with husband Kanye West and their four children, North, Saint, Chicago and Psalm, and fly on the wall footage for her reality shows alongside her siblings, mother Kris Jenner, and former step-father Caitlyn Jenner.

More recently, Kim has appeared to attempt to make more of herself than being simply a product of a reality show, by studying law and campaigning for prison reform in America.

There continues to be a threat she will one day become First Lady of America as her husband continues to tease he will run for President in 2024.
