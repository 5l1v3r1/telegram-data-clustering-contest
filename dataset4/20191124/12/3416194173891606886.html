Taylor Swift To Become Most Awarded Artist at American Music Awards

24 Nov 2019, 12:18

Taylor Swift will break the record for most award wins at the American Music Awards at tonight’s ceremony!

The 29-year-old musician is nominated for five awards at the 2019 American Music Awards, airing on Sunday (November 24), and she is set to receive the Artist of the Decade award.

Taylor and Michael Jackson are currently tied in the top spot with 24 wins each.

She is up for Favorite Artist Adult Contemporary, Favorite Album Pop/Rock for “Lover”, Favorite Female Artist Pop/Rock, Favorite Music Video for “You Need To Calm Down”, and Artist of the Year.

The “ME!” singer is also set to put on a fierce, powerful performance after ongoing battle over her older music.
