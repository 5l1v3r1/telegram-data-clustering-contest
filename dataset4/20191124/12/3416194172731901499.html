Camila Cabello's 'Living Proof' Video Is Out Now - Watch Here!

24 Nov 2019, 12:06

Camila Cabello just dropped the new music video for her latest song release, “Living Proof“!

The 22-year-old “Liar” singer revealed the surprise release earlier in the day on Sunday (November 24).

“the video for living proof is OUT NOW 😩😊😊😊 I loved pretending I was a little fairy nymph cuz that’s all I wanna be !!!! watch the #LivingProofVideo on @YouTubeMusic and tell me what u think,” she wrote on Instagram.

Camila will not only perform her song at the 2019 American Music Awards, but she’s also set to perform her duet “Señorita” with boyfriend Shawn Mendes. They’re even nominated for Collaboration of the Year!
