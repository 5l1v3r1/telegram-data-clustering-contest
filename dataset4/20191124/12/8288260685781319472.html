'Difficult time' - Liverpool fans agreed on young star amid Aston Villa and Leeds United links

Aston Villa transfer news | Villa are being linked with a loan move for the Reds youngster - and Liverpool supporters think a temporary move could work for their England age-group striker

24 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Josh Murray

Liverpool starlet Rhian Brewster is being linked with a January loan move to Aston Villa.

The 19-year-old striker is reportedly on Villa's radar ahead of the transfer window reopening in the new year, according to the Mirror - and Crystal Palace, Swansea City and Leeds United are also credited with an interest.

Brewster - a Golden Boot winner during England's Under-17 World Cup triumph in 2017 - made his senior Liverpool debut earlier this season in a Carabao Cup win over MK Dons.

He is yet to make a Premier League appearance for Jurgen Klopp's side, but he is expected to feature against Villa in the quarter-finals of the Carabao Cup next month.
