Two-foot Tall Man Gets Married to Six-feet Tall Pakistani Bride, Videos And Pictures go Viral

The two-foot tall man has a grand wedding reception as he gets married to 6-feel tall Pakistani bride - Watch viral videos.

24 Nov 2019, 12:46

Wedding pictures and videos of a two-foot-tall man named Burhan Chishti have taken the internet by storm and he has become an overnight sensation. Chishti, affectionately known as Bobo, got married to a girl named Fauzia. Chishti is a polio survivor and is wheelchair-bound but lives his life to the fullest.

In the wedding videos, Bobo can be seen dancing to the tunes of Punjabi song at his wedding reception that went viral on social media. He can be seen carried by his friends in their arms so that he can dance to Punjabi tunes. He can also be seen taking a selfie with his wife Fauzia and enters the wedding reception holding her hand while driving his electric wheelchair.

The wedding that was held in Oslo recently was attended by nationals from thirteen different countries.
