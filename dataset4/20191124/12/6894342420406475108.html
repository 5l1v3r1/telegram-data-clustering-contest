iPhone 11 Smart Battery Case To Be Available For RM 539; Features Dedicated Camera Button

24 Nov 2019, 12:21 by Chief Chapree

While the battery life is one of the most talked improvements within iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, and iPhone 11 Pro Max, it doesn’t stop Apple from coming out with Smart Battery Cases for these three devices. While the case might look identical to the cases that the company has made for iPhone XR, iPhone XS, and iPhone XS Max, there is one major difference though.

Specifically, the Smart Battery Cases for iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, and iPhone 11 Pro Max is equipped with a dedicated camera button. Able to support both rear and front camera, the button can also be used to record QuickTake video.

Just like the Smart Battery Cases for previous generation iPhones, the new models also come with wireless charging capability. While their actual battery capacities were not revealed by Apple, the company did point out that the Smart Battery Cases are able to extend iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, and iPhone 11 Pro Max’s battery life up to 50%.

Even though they are not yet available for purchase, these Smart Battery Cases for iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, and iPhone 11 Pro Max are priced at RM 539 each. For more information, head on to Apple Malaysia’s official website right here.
