Ex-stripper claims Prince Andrew 'motorboated her breasts' at Epstein's party

24 Nov 2019, 12:12

A former lapdancer has claimed Prince Andrew ‘motorboated’ her breasts during a party thrown by Jeffrey Epstein.

The woman, who gave evidence against Epstein under the name ‘Tiffany Doe’, alleged the Prince of York liked ‘curvy girls, like his ex-wife, Fergie’.

She claims she was paid by convicted paedophile Epstein, who died in August this year, to attend the party and make sure Andrew was looked after.

According to her, there were also several young women and girls attending the party, held in Epstein’s mansion, some of who were homeless, runaways, or the little sisters of strippers.

She claims girls who ‘couldn’t look 18 or 19’ were kept upstairs in bedrooms during the night.

There is no suggestion the duke was aware of any under-age girls being at the party.

The woman, now 47, told the The Mirror Andrew had appeared in ‘good spirits’ during their meeting, stating that she ‘always thought’ he liked her breasts.

She continued: ‘He leaned forward and put his face on them and blew a raspberry. I was shocked, but I laughed, thinking “Wow what was that?”

‘He laughed, too. He’d never done it before and I thought, “It’s going to be a good night”. I guess being a prince gives you those privileges.’

The former stripper stated that the duke had failed to remember the term for his actions, asking her if it was a ‘motor…something?’

She described him as ‘quite awkward, quite clumsy’, and alleged that he had also told her she should be in ‘a magazine for big-breasted women’.

The woman said she saw him at parties a ‘couple of times a year’, adding: ‘He would hold my hands, put his arm around my waist, stroke my cheek.

‘He used royalty, his celebrity, to his advantage.’

The woman said Ghislaine Maxwell, 57, was also at the parties, ‘keeping an eye over what was happening and mingling with the guests’.

Ms Maxwell has continuously denied allegations of wrongdoing and has not been charged with any crime.

The former stripper added that Epstein would record his guests on cameras so that he could ‘have leverage’ over them in the future.

Epstein, 66, was found dead in his cell in August earlier this year while awaiting trial for sex trafficking charges.

Two jail workers who were on duty at the time have since been arrested for allegedly failing to check on him every half an hour as their job required.

They have been taken into custody in New York and are expected to appear in the US District Court in Manhattan.

The Duke of York has continuously denied any wrongdoing during his friendship with Epstein, including witnessing any criminal activity.

He denies having sex with Virginia Roberts, who claimed that she had slept with the prince three times, including one occasion when she was 17.

During an interview with Newsnight, he stated that he had been at Pizza Express in Woking on March 10 2001, when the allegations are alleged to have taken place.

He also said he was also previously unable to sweat, disputing Ms Roberts claims that he had had to take a bath because he was perspiring so much.

The prince told presenter Emily Maitlis: ‘I didn’t sweat at the time because I had suffered what I would describe as an overdose of adrenaline in the Falklands War when I was shot at.

‘It was almost impossible for me to sweat.’

Andrew has since stepped down from royal duties after he was dropped by a string of charities and organisations.
