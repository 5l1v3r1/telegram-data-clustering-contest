RY EYES CUP

Rangers star Ryan Kent says Celtic Betfred Cup final will be biggest moment of his career

24 Nov 2019, 12:22 by Ryan McDonald

RANGERS star Ryan Kent admits next month’s blockbuster Old Firm final will be the biggest moment of his career.

Steven Gerrard’s side lock horns with rivals Celtic at Hampden on December 8 as both sides battle for Betfred Cup glory.

While Neil Lennon’s quadruple treble chasers have dominated Scottish football over the last eight years, Gers haven’t won any major trophies since 2011.

The Light Blues suffered semi-final misery against Aberdeen at Hampden last year but after reaching the final this time around, Kent is desperate to end the club’s trophy drought.

He told BT Sport: “I was here last season to realise how much it affected us as a team and as a club.

“To lose in the semi-finals last year was a big disappointment.

“To reach a final, it’s no secret how massive that is for the club and for each individual in the team as well.

“Everybody is really excited for that. It will definitely be the biggest moment of my career, to play in that game.

“If I was to come away with silverware, it would be huge.”
