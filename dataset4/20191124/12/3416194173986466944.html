Shay Mitchell Watches 'Bumblebee' at Drive-In Movie Event

24 Nov 2019, 12:58

Shay Mitchell wears shiny leggings to the Volkswagen Drive-in Movie Event held at the Petersen Automotive Museum late last week in Los Angeles.

The 32-year-old actress and new mom hosted the event alongside the car company, where guests watched a screening of Bumblebee.

“New girls’ night out in an old school #VWPartner way… had so much fun watching Bumblebee at the #VWDriveIn last night 🎥 Oh and the 2020 Atlas Cross Sport is pretty beautiful too 😉,” Shay shared on Instagram after the event, along with a cute photo.

The Bachelorette‘s Tyler Cameron was also in attendance.
