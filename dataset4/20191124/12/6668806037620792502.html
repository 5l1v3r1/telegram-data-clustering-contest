Photos: Kunal Kemmu and Soha Ali Khan step out for a romantic dinner

24 Nov 2019, 12:08 by Kajol Mathur

Kunal Kemmu and Soha Ali Khan have been married for four years now and have a beautiful daughter – Inaaya Naumi Kemmu. The two are quite active on social media and share glimpses of their personal life on their Instagram handles. We snapped the adorable couple on a romantic date last night.

They headed out for dinner at a popular restaurant in the city and were seen posing for our shutterbugs present there. While Soha looked gorgeous in a red suit which she paired with grey dupatta, Kunal looked dapper in a black tee, denims and a snapback.
