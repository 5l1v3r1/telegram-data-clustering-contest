Drama as boy shares residential address of an anti-Titan for calling Titans unintelligent

24 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Damilola Ismail

A boy has shared the residential address of a social media user who called BBNaija Tacha fans,Titans unintelligent in his recent post.

The boy in an attempt to get back at the anti-titan for calling Titans unintelligent, posted the address of the anti-Titan, saying he hopes Titans do no hurt him.

The anti-Tacha fan, Alhaji Royz however got hold of the post, confirmed the address is him and warned anyone who tried anything funny would have themselves to blame. He wrote;

When I said I have never seen an intelligent Titan before, the others that I don’t know came at ,e to show they are unintelligent too.

But now they over reached. They want the smoke, they shall get the smoke. I’m not even tripping.

So i just heard one of the Titans I blocked put up my address for other Titans.

I just wanna say that’s my real address, and whenever you are coming, you should come with a lot of back up. Cause i swear, niggas gon bleed.

You’ll see the difference between twitter and real life.

I’ll also make sure I have it on record so i can post it back here to let y’all know not to fuck wrong with me.
