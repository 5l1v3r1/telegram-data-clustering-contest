Pichichi latest as Benzema threatens to take Messi's crown

Messi has won the prize in each of the last three seasons

24 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Nino Vàzquez R.

Lionel Messi is in the race of the Pichichi once again but he failed to score on Saturday against Leganes and lost ground on Karim Benzema.

Real Madrid striker Benzema was on target against Real Sociedad as his side moved back level at the top of La Liga with Barcelona on 28 points.

He is now two goals clear of Messi, although has played more minutes. Messi missed several games injured earlier this season.
