Breaking! Pitso & Downs Duo Nominated For CAF Awards!

24 Nov 2019, 12:26 by Soccer Laduma

Mamelodi Sundowns coach Pitso Mosimane has been nominated for the 2019 CAF Coach of the Year award.

Click through the gallery to see some of the coaches nominated for the Men's Coach of the Year award.

Mosimane helped Sundowns to another Absa Premiership title last season while the club made it to the semi-finals of the CAF Champions League, where they lost out to Wydad Casablanca.

The Sundowns tactician will go up against the likes of Senegal coach Alious Cisse, Nigeria's Gernot Rohr and Africa Cup of Nations winner Djamel Belmadi.
