Aldi shoppers spend hours in queues for Kevin the Carrot 2019 range online

"What. The. Actual. Hell."

24 Nov 2019, 12:15 by Emilia Bona

Aldi shoppers were left devastated after spending hours in online queues for the Kevin the Carrot range - only to come away empty-handed.

The festive range is so popular there were  huge queues  at Aldi’s Liverpool stores when it went on sale last year and items sold out within 24 hours.

Aldi's Kevin the Carrot range is back in stores from Thursday, November 28 at 8am - but shoppers had the chance to avoid the chaos and order online today.

But the demand for the plush toys, pyjamas and tree decorations was so high that shoppers were left in online queues thousands of customers long.

Anyone trying to access the Aldi website this morning was met with a message saying: "You're in the queue... We're very sorry, but there are currently lots of people trying to access the Aldi website.

"We've automatically placed you in a queue. Don't worry about refreshing - this page will update automatically as soon as you can enter the site."

Queue positions went from around 2,000 at 8am to an unbelievable 10,000 at 10am, as thousands of shoppers tried to get on the site.

Plenty of frustrated shoppers took to social media to share their disappointment at missing out on the range.

One Twitter user with the handle 'YNWA' tweeted: "Queued for five hours last night for #KevinTheCarrot merchandise on the Aldi website. Fortunately I got everything I wanted, but I feel so sorry for people queuing at the moment because most things have sold out and they have no idea."

Rachel Jones tweeted: "Just went looking for something on the aldi website and did not realise it was Kevin the Carrot release day and I'm in a queue of 20,000 people to access the site. what. the. actual. hell."

Sam Garstang Heath said: "Don’t bother all sold out was on the web at midnight till 1.15am nothing on it, kept saying we are updating won’t be long, so we gave up. Went on this morning and they're sold out."

Molly Tweeted a screenshot of her queue position at 19,919 saying: "Was going to pre-order a Kevin the Carrot from Aldi because the boys love the advert... maybe not."  

Karen Swan said: "Its 6.30am on Sunday and theres no Kevins left......?! Sort it out @AldiUK #kevinthecarrot #ineedone"

Sam tweeted: "Anyone actually managed to access the @AldiUK website to see if Kevin The Carrot is there. My daughter missed out last year and it looks like she will again."

An Aldi spokesperson said: “We’re sorry that some shoppers were unable to get their hands on these toys online this morning, however, demand has been extremely high. 

“The range will be stocked in-store from 28 November but, as with all of our Specialbuy products, these will also only be available while stocks last.”

Hope isn't completely lost for those who missed out online though, with the range set to drop in stores across the country on Thursday.

Based on the budget supermarket’s  Christmas advert , fans can buy a giant one metre tall Kevin and Katie carrot toys, along with their carrot kids. New for this year are Russell Sprout and Tiny Tom ‘Leafy Blinders’ toys, who also star in this year’s advert.
