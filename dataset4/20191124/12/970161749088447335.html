K-Pop star Goo Hara has reportedly been found dead at her home, according to Korean media.

Police said the singer – who found fame as a member of the girl group Kara – was found on Saturday evening at her home in Cheongdam, in the Gangnam district of South Korean capital Seoul.

The tragic news comes after Hara had signed with a Japanese production company.

She had released her solo mini album Midnight Queen earlier this month.
