Bolts gain final four

24 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Theodore P. Jurado

ALLEN Durham completed a double-double as Meralco eliminated Alaska, 94-84, to barge into the PBA Governors' Cup semifinals at the Smart Araneta Coliseum last night.

Durham had 28 points and 16 rebounds to go with eight assists as the second-ranked Bolts needed just one game to end the Aces' first conference under new coach Jeffrey Cariaso.

Allein Maliksi, who was the top local scorer with 23 points, prouduced key baskets in Meralco's fourth quarter run that kept Alaska at bay.

“They really gave us a scare,” said Bolts coach Norman Black.

Maliksi iced the game with back-to-back baskets to push the Bolts’ lead, 92-81, with 1:02 left in the ballgame that broke the backs of the Aces

JV Casio put Alaska within five, 77-82, off banked shot floater  at the 5:25 mark.

Casio took the scoring mantle for the Aces with 20 points while Simon Enciso chipped in 16 points.
