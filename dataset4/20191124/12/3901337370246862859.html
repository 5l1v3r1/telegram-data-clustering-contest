Tau, Salah & Mane Nominated For CAF Player Of The Year Award

24 Nov 2019, 12:51 by SL International

Bafana Bafana star Percy Tau has been short-listed for the CAF Player Of The Year award, along with another PSL star.

Click through the gallery to see some of the big names nominated for the award.

Tau has enjoyed an impressive 2019 where he netted 13 goals and 13 appearances during his loan spell for Belgian outfit Union Saint Gilloise.

The 25-year-old was also instrumental in Bafana Bafana's Africa Cup of Nations qualifying campaign in 2019, where he netted four goals in eight appearances.

Africa Cup of Nations champions Algeria dominate the list with four players, while Nigeria and Senegal both have three players nominated.

Mamelod Sundowns goalkeeper Denis Onyango is the only South-African based player nominated on the list following his showings for the Absa Premiership champions and Uganda over the past year.
