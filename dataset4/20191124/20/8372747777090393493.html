The Coast Guard is searching for a man who went missing around Knapps Point in Sanibel on Sunday.

The Coast Guard confirms the man took his raft into the water near Knapps Point around 11:30 Sunday morning, but after missing for some time, his family contacted Sanibel Police and the Coast Guard.

Crews are actively searching for the man. The Coast Guard has marine units and a helicopter searching the area.
