When is War of the Worlds set and where was it filmed?

24 Nov 2019, 20:46

BBC’s hotly-anticipated War of the Worlds aired its first episode last week.

The series, starring Poldark’s Eleanor Tomlinson, Rafe Spall and Robert Carlyle, re-tells what’s considered the first ever tale of a conflict between mankind and extra-terrestrial life.

Where was the show filmed?

When is the War of the Worlds set?

In the original book written by HG Wells, the majority of the story takes place in Woking, Surrey where Wells was based at the time of writing.
