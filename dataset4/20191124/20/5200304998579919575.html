Overnights: ‘The Kapil Sharma’ gives Sony TV a huge leap on Saturday in UK

24 Nov 2019, 20:41 by Raj Baddhan

A huge boost by ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ on Saturday, gave Sony Entertainment Television a massive leap over its rivals, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Sony TV delivered a whopping share of 0.21%, followed by Star Gold with 0.15%, Star Plus and PTC Punjabi with 0.09%, Colors with 0.07%, ZEE Cinema with 0.06%, Star Bharat with 0.05%, Sony MAX, New Vision TV, BritAsia TV and Hum TV with 0.04%, Sony SAB TV, B4U Movies, NDTV 24×7, Geo TV and Geo News with 0.03%, and ARY Family and ZEE TV with 0.01%.

Sony TV’s biggest attractions were ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ at 21:30 with 97,700 viewers – peaking at 114,400 viewers, followed by ‘Indian Idol 11’ at 20:00 with 59,300 viewers, ‘The Kapil Sharma Show’ repeat at 18:30 with 39,700 viewers and ‘Crime Patrol’ at 11:30 with 31,500 viewers.

On Star Plus, ‘Dance+ 5’ was watched by 49,300 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘TED Talks India Nayi Baat’ at 19:00 with 27,200 viewers and ‘Divya Drishti’ at 21:30 with 21,500 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13 Weekend Ka Vaar’ led with 53,400 viewers at 21:30.

On Hum TV, ‘Deewar-e-Shab’ pulled in 24,500 viewers, followed by ‘Gul-e-Rana’ at 21:00 with 11,900 viewers.

On ZEE TV, movie ‘Mulk’ drew 4,000 viewers at 15:00.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
