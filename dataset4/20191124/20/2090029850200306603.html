Nigerian doctor buys house 3 years after migrating to UK – How he did it!

24 Nov 2019, 20:35 by Sanya Agunbiade

Popular online medical doctor, Dr. Harvey Olufunmilayo who migrated to the United Kingdom, UK 3 years ago, has just bought a house.

Revealing how it was made possible, Dr. Harvey mentioned that he cultivated a saving culture, and was able to save as much as 500-1000£ every month.

He wrote:

2019, God gave me a house.
Not rented. My own home.
4bedroom. With a garden. In England.

For a boy that only came to UK with 2travelling bags in 2016, I’m in tears.

My prayer for everyone that follows me or sees this is that God will grant all your heart’s greatest desires

And to those asking-
The interiors were totally all designed by me. The choice of the colours, furniture, paints and wallpapers were all picked by me.

The walls were all white when the house was sold. I felt it was a little boring and it would be nice to spice things a little.

And all this happened since September 2019. But I’ve had a few things going on in my life to sort and a major exam in October also- which I just passed last week.

1. I’m not married.
2. People’s responsibilities differ.
3. I’ve had a culture of saving between 500-1000£ every month from my earnings for about 3years.
4. It was a plan I had and worked towards seeing friends who achieved same.

That’s the simplified explanation.

I pray that this goodnews encourages someone to not give up and keep trying. Your dreams are valid.

God bless you.
