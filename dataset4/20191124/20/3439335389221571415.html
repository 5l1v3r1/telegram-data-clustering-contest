Police seek suspect involved in Waianae shooting | Honolulu Star-Advertiser

24 Nov 2019, 20:40

Honolulu police reported an attempted murder in the second-degree in Waianae on Saturday that involved two males.

Police say an unidentified male brandished a handgun at a 37-year-old male after a traffic incident and shot at his vehicle at 3:30 p.m. The shooter then fled the scene.

No injuries were reported and no arrests have been made.
