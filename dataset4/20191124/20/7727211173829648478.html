Cinemas showing festive films in Liverpool this Christmas

There is something for children and adults and films to go to watch as a family

24 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Mark Johnson

A whole range of Christmas-themed movies will be shown at Merseyside cinemas this festive season.

You can take your loved ones or go with a friend to see your favourite festive films.

There is something for people of all ages to enjoy, including old time classics and children's hits.

You can enjoy festive films such as Elf, Jack Frost, It's a Wonderful Life, Love Actually, Die Hard, Les Miserables and The Holiday, Home Alone, The Grinch, Muppets Christmas Carol, The Polar Express and more.

The venues include The Plaza in Waterloo, The Palm House at Sefton Park, the floating cinema at Albert Dock, Woolton Picture House and St George's Hall.
