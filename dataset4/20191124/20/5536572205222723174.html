Tom Brady Shows Off His Version of The ‘Dak Dance’ (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 20:14 by Darrelle Lincoln

Following the Sunday night battle between the Dallas Cowboys and the Minnesota Vikings, the NFL world was introduced to a pre-game workout by Dak Prescott that has been dubbed the “Dak dance.”

His hip-shaking warmup is not new or rare, but his version got everybody making jokes about it.

New England Patriots quarterback Tom Brady recently revealed he does it as well, but a less violent version.

“He does a good job of it,” Brady said about Prescott’s warmup. “He’s got those loose hips and then he keeps his … I know exactly what he’s doing, and I like that he’s doing that. It’s good training for him.”
