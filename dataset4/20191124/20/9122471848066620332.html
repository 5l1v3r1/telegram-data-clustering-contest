Tom Brady Gives Honest Response To Question About Future With Patriots

24 Nov 2019, 20:12 by Tzvi Machlin

Tom Brady has accomplished everything there is to accomplish in professional football – all with the New England Patriots. So it’s not unfair to ask if Brady would want to try to add to his legacy by winning with another team.

Considering that Brady will be out of contract at the end of this year, that speculation is only going to get louder.

So in a recent interview, Brady answered the question of what his future holds.

In an interview with FOX, Brady asserterted that he can’t predict what happens next in his career. But he maintained that he plans to play longer no matter what.

“I think the reality is there is no prediction. I have always said I have a plan to play longer and I will figure that out when the time comes.”
