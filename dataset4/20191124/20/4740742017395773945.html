McDonald's customers slam festive drinks added to menu

A spokesperson for the chain addressed the complaints

24 Nov 2019, 20:06 by Jessica Sansome, Andrew McQuarrie

Some McDonald's customers have been left disappointed by festive drinks on offer - with one punter claiming their day was "ruined" by a beverage.

The fast-food chain's Christmas menu includes a Millionaire's Latte and a Toffee Latte.

But some customers have revealed they were less than impressed with the products, according to the Manchester Evening News.

One angry social media user wrote: "Fuming mate, second Millionaire Latte from  @McDonalds  WITHOUT cream. No it hadn't melted and no it hadn't stuck to the lid. Day, ruined."
