Strictly Come Dancing fans ridicule Motsi Mabuse's new 'cycling shorts' look

Fans took to Twitter as they went into meltdown over the judge's bold new look during this evening's results show

24 Nov 2019, 20:14 by Irishmirror.ie

Strictly Come Dancing fans have been sent into overdrive by judge Motsi Mabuse's bold fashion choice.

As the judges came out for tonight's results show, fans flocked to social media with confused posts about her quirky ensemble.

"Has Motsi forgotten to put her skirt on? #Bodyshaper #Strictly," one penned.

Another laughed: "So @MOTSI_MABUSE is wearing velvet cycling shorts and I'm here for it #Strictly."

A third wondered if Motsi had been taking fashion heads ups from co-host Tess Daly.

"Has Motsi been raiding Tess' wardrobe? #onesleeve #strictly," they asked.

A fourth was just as baffled, but insisted Motsi was the one to make it work.

They wrote: "I am baffled by black velvet cycling shorts, but if anyone can pull them off it's Motsi #Strictly."

Others oozed positivity about the look.

A dedicated fan said: "@MOTSI_MABUSE single handedly making cycling shorts look amazing!! #Strictly."
