Swartbooi says once in power LPM will root out corruption

24 Nov 2019, 20:22

The Landless Peoples Movement's (LPM) leader, Bernadus Swartbooi, says that once in power, they will root out corruption and stop the ongoing acts of personal enrichment, which he says, is one of the reasons the country finds itself in financial problems.
Swartbooi made the remarks at a packed J Stephanus Stadium in Keetmanshoop where the party held its final rally.
