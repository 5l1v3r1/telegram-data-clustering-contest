Injured Youngstown State QB gets help from teammates on victory kneel, last play of his college career

24 Nov 2019, 20:29 by Zac Al-Khateeb

Youngstown State quarterback Nathan Mays may not have got the senior season he wanted this year, but his teammates on Saturday ensured his last game would be unforgettable nonetheless.

Mays, whose career ended after he broke his leg and dislocated his ankle in his team's Nov. 2 matchup vs. North Dakota State, was helped onto the field by teammates on Saturday so he could take the victory kneel in a 21-3 win over No. 7 Illinois State.
