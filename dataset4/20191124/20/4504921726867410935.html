Geingob says SWAPO is the only party that can guarantee positive growth

24 Nov 2019, 20:40

President Hage Geingob says SWAPO's track record in terms of freedom and socio-economic development shows that it is the only party that can guarantee a continued positive growth trajectory for the country.
The ruling party held its final rally in Windhoek, where Geingob told members and supporters that SWAPO stabilised the economy, even in times of global economic recession.
