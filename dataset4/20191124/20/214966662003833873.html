Three charged after various drugs found in police raid

24 Nov 2019, 20:55 by Monique StClair

Three men have been charged after a major drug bust in Broadbeach Waters last week.

It’s understood police were tipped off to suspicious activity at a home on Rebecca Court, by a member of the public.

Officers executed a search warrant on Friday morning – when they allegedly uncovered scores of dangerous drugs, including cocaine, cannabis and testosterone.

A 25-year-old, 29-year-old and a 47-year-old were all charged with various drug offences.

The two men in their 20s will appear at Southport Magistrates Court next month while the 47-year-old man will appear in January.

Detective Inspector Craig McGrath from Major and Organised Crime Squad encourages people who observe suspicious activities in their neighbourhood to contact Crimestoppers.

“Information from the community assists greatly in identifying and disrupting these illegal activities,” Detective Inspector McGrath said.

If you have information for Police, please contact Policelink on 131 444 or provide information using their online form 24 hours per day.

You can also report information about crime anonymously to Crime Stoppers by calling 1800 333 000 or via crimestoppersqld.com.au 24 hours per day. Crime Stoppers is a registered charity and community volunteer organisation.
