Nicole Scherzinger voted Thom Evans off X Factor: Celebrity to save their romance following ‘unbearable’ mocking on show

24 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Ellie Henman

NICOLE Scherzinger voted Thom Evans off The X Factor: Celebrity to save their romance.

Insiders said the singer, 41, couldn’t bear to have their relationship mocked on the show.

She ditched his band Try Star, and his bandmates Levi Davis and Ben Foden, during last night’s episode.

A source said: “Nicole is really into Thom and she didn’t want jokes being made about them.

“She chose to get rid of his group Try Star because it would stop people scrutinising what she has with Tom. And she knew they were the weaker act.”

Former Scots rugby player Thom, 34, didn’t harbour any hard feelings against Nicole who stole the show in a barely-there pink dress.

The smitten pair were pictured partying together in London following the elimination on Saturday night.
