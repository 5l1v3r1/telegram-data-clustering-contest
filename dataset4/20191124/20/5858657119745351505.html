Richmond RCMP searching for missing 75-year old woman

24 Nov 2019, 20:55 by Nikitha Martins

Summary

Feng Qin Zhou was last seen in Richmond on Thursday

Those with information about Feng Qin's whereabouts are to contact the Richmond RCMP

RICHMOND (NEWS 1130) – Since Thursday, 75-year-old Feng Qin Zhou has been missing and Mounties are concerned for her safety.

In a tweet on Friday, Richmond RMCP say Feng Qin, “may appear confused.”

“Zhou’s family and the police are concerned for her well-being. Anyone with information as to Zhou’s whereabouts is urged to contact their local police,” Cpl. Dennis Hwang of the Richmond RCMP says.

The senior was last seen near Firbridge Way and Ackroyd road around 1:00 p.m. Thursday.

She was wearing a pink long sleeve shirt, long black hooded coat, black pants, and pink shoes.
She is described by police as an Asian women with brown eyes and white hair. She is 5′ 2″ and about 138 pounds.

The senior only speaks Mandarin but if you have information you can contact the Richmond RCMP at 604-278-1212 or call 911. If you wish to remain anonymous dial 1-800-222-8477 or visit www.solvecrime.ca, and quote file number 2019-38195.
