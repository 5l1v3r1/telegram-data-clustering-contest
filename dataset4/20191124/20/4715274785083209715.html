5 unusual fast food item combinations

24 Nov 2019, 20:20 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

At fast-food joints, most, if everyone, always give in and order their usual food combo. There’s no harm in getting what’s tried and tested, right?  American fast-food chain Popeyes helps diners beat the food rut with the following combinations—featuring its menu items—which may appear unusual but actually work.  Popeyes Chicken + honey

Tired of the usual chicken + gravy combo? Popeyes Chicken with a dash of honey offers that new kind of flavor. The sweetness of honey balances the distinct Cajun flavors of Popeyes Chicken. Diners don’t have to order biscuits to get honey as the fast food chain now have ala carte honey packets in the menu.  Fish Burger + Shrimp Burger

For those who can’t get enough of seafood, the fish burger plus shrimp burger will surely satisfy that craving. Just order one of each burger and stack the patties, effectively making one beast of a burger. Dip the excess buns into Popeyes’ creamy crab soup, the perfect side to this seafood-centric entrée. Cajun Rice + Creamy Crab Soup

Imagine this: a bowl of flavorful and slightly spicy rice mixed into a thick and savory crab soup. All of those ingredients, spices, and aroma come together to give a delicious mashup with distinctive Cajun flavors. Rice lovers out there will surely go bonkers for this one. Classic Honey Biscuit + ice cream

A twist on the ice cream sandwich, this one features the distinct salty, sweet, and buttery taste of the Classic Honey Biscuit. Just cut up the biscuit right down the middle and slather on chocolate, caramel, or strawberry sundae for an explosion of flavors.  Cajun Fries + Popeyes Spaghetti Sauce

Fries go with almost anything—catsup, gravy, ice cream, and leftover spaghetti sauce from Popeyes’ sweet, savory, and cheesy Spaghetti.
