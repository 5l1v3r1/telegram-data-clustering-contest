Celtic to face Partick Thistle in Scottish Cup fourth round as Rangers drawn against Stranraer

The Hoops will travel to Firhill as they take on Ian McCall's side for the second time this season

24 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Graeme Young, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/graeme-young/

Holders Celtic will face Partick Thistle in an all-Glasgow clash after being drawn against their city rivals in the Scottish Cup fourth round.

The Hoops will travel to Firhill as they take on Ian McCall's side for the second time this season after sweeping them aside 5-0 in the Betfred Cup in October. 

Neil Lennon's Celtic side are eyeing a fourth consecutive Scottish Cup triumph after coming out on top in each of the last three seasons.

Title rivals Rangers will take on Stranraer at Ibrox as the Steven Gerrard looks to make inroads in the tournament which came to a shuddering halt last season against Aberdeen at the quarter-final stage.

Derek McInnes' side, on the back of Dave Cormack being confirmed as chairman, will take on League One side Dumbarton at Pittodrie.

Lowland League side East Kilbride missed out on a money-spinning clash as they take on BSC Glasgow who are eyeing progression to the next round after stunning East Fife. 

Managerless Hearts could face a tricky task against League One leaders Airdrie.

The fourth round ties will be played on the weekend of January 18/19 when the Premiership teams come back from their winter break.
