Little Mix announce summer 2020 tour including appearance at 'legendary' London festival

The wildly successful girl group are about to start on on their new album and will appear at the British Summer Time festival in Hyde Park next year

24 Nov 2019, 20:03 by Hannah Graham

It's big news for Little Mix fans as the group announce their next tour.

The girls, including South Shields' own Perrie Edwards and Jade Thirlwall, will be hitting the road in the summer of 2020.

And revealing the news to fans, they teased one very exciting tour date.

In a video on the group's shared Twitter page, group member Jesy Nelson said: "We have some huge news that we are so excited about, that we want to share with you guys.

"We are heading back on the road next year for our summer 2020 tour. This will include a date playing the legendary American Express Presents BST Hyde Park in London."

Leigh-Anne Pinnock added: "Tickets go on sale on Thursday the 28th - get your tickets guys!"

The rest of the group's appearances are yet to be confirmed - they're expected to be unveiled tomorrow.

Tickets go on sale at 9am on Thursday, November 28 via ticketmaster.co.uk, axs.com or seetickets.com.

The girls are certainly hard-working, having only just finished their latest tour, for the LM5 album.

And appearing at the Radio One Teen Awards they revealed they'll be going back into the studio next week to make a start on their new album.

Meanwhile their first ever Christmas single, One I've Been Missing, was released last week.
