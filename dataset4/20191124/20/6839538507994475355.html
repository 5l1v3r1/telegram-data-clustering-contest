Policewoman found dead on duty northwest of Melbourne

24 Nov 2019, 20:53

The death of a policewoman whose body was found while she was on duty in Malmsbury is being investigated by homicide detectives but is not being treated as suspicious.

The body of the sergeant, from the northwest region, was found in central Victoria.

A Victoria Police statement said she was located near Mollison St in Malmsbury at 9am.

“Her death is not being treated as suspicious,” the statement said.

Detectives from the homicide squad will investigate the death, overseen by members from professional standards command.
