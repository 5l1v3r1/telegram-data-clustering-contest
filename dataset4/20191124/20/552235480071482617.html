Scottish Cup draw: Rangers clash at Ibrox lying in wait for former Kilmarnock star

former Rugby Park utility man Jamie Hamill will grace the pitch at Rangers' home on January 18 as Stranraer take them on.

24 Nov 2019, 20:19 by Lochlin Highet, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/lochlin-highet/

Former Kilmarnock star Jamie Hamill is getting set to head to Ibrox in the Scottish Cup.

The former Rugby Park utility man will grace the pitch at Rangers' home on January 18 as Stranraer take them on.

He left Killie in 2011 to head to Hearts, before returning to Rugby Park for a second spell between 2014 and 2016.

He has made 101 appearances for Stranraer since joining in 2017.

Meanwhile his former side Killie will take on Queen's Park at Rugby Park.

They are managed by former Killie player Mark Roberts who will be delighted to make his return to Rugby Park considering he still stays round the corner.
