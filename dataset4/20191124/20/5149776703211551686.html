SUSPENSIONS

Forest don't currently have any suspensions.

Though, Samba Sow is just one yellow card away from a one-game suspension with four yellows to his name, and Sammy Ameobi is two away with three accumulated.

INJURIES

Tendayi Darikwa

Injury: ACL

Darikwa suffered a devastating blow, rupturing his Anterior Cruciate Ligament in the lead up to the new season.

He won't be back until some time next year.

Last match played: Zimbabwe 0-4 DR Congo - 30 June, 2019

Potential return date: 2020

What Lamouchi said in August: “Darikwa is having an operation, so we will be missing him for about six months.”

Alfa Semedo

Injury: Back

Alfa Semedo suffered an injury in Forest's win over Barnsley.

Back in full training but likely to miss the visit to Ashton Gate.

Last match played: Nottingham Forest 1-0 Barnsley - 21, September 2019

Expected return date: Any time now.

What Lamouchi said: “It has been a very good week, with players back, like Alfa Semedo, Carl Jenkinson, Yohan Benalouane and Michael Hefele."

Samba Sow

Injury: Hamstring

Sow, who has impressed so far this season, picked up a hamstring injury in the win over Brentford earlier this month.

Forest will have fingers crossed his return isn't too far into the distant future. The player himself has issued a message on social media.

Last match played: Nottingham Forest 1-0 Brentford - 5, October 2019

Carl Jenkinson

Injury: Ankle

Jenkinson missed the entirety of September after suffering an ankle injury in the draw against Preston.

Lamouchi has said that the full-back is "close" to returning to a comeback.

Last match played: Forest 1-1 Preston - 31, August 2019

Expected return date: Back in training

What Lamouchi said: “Dawson, Semedo, Jenkinson, Benalouane and Hefele, they made all the sessions with us this week. But it is so early for them.”

Yohan Benalouane

Injury: Hip

Benalouane hasn't featured for Forest since April following a hip injury.

But he is set to return in the coming weeks, and Lamuchi has confirmed that the defender has returned to training after surgery.

Last match played: Sheffield United 2-0 Nottingham Forest - 19, April 2019

Expected return date: After the international break

What Lamouchi said: “Dawson, Semedo, Jenkinson, Benalouane and Hefele, they made all the sessions with us this week. But it is so early for them.”
