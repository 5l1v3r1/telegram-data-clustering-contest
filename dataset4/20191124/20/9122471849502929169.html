Danny Kanell Shakes Up His Top 8 After Week 13

24 Nov 2019, 20:41 by Matt Hladik

Week 13 of the college football season is in the books, and Danny Kanell has updated his top eight rankings accordingly.

Kanell’s weekly rankings are always fun to analyze, particularly because they typically cause consternation for some fan bases who feel their teams are being slighted. Usually, those fan bases are from the SEC.

Today, fans of Alabama and Georgia might actually be somewhat happy with Kanell. He finally put their teams back in the top eight.

Kanell’s top five looks exactly how it did one week ago. Oregon has been dropped after losing to Arizona State, as has Penn State. Georgia and Alabama replaced them.
