'Come on Jews, Let's Go Camps': Students Make Nazi Music Video Parody to 'Barbie Girl'

Argentinian teacher who gave the project a 9 out of 10 was laid off, and the school issued an apology over anti-Semitic, homophobic and racist video

24 Nov 2019, 20:19 by JTA

Students in a history class in a private secondary school in a northwest province of Argentina made a music video featuring singing Nazis as a class assignment.

The Nazi music video received 9 points out of 10 points from the teacher at the Escuela Modelo of San Juan, or Model School, whose website says that “we mould people with integrity, solidarity and entrepreneurship.”
