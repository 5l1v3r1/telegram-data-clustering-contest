Overnights: India V Bangladesh puts Star Gold at No.2 on Saturday in UK

24 Nov 2019, 20:54 by Raj Baddhan

Star Gold emerged as a winner in UK ratings on Saturday, on the back of its coverage of the ongoing India V Bangladesh cricket series, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Gold was placed at No.2 with a share of 0.15%, just behind leader Sony TV, which garnered 0.21% share. Star Gold was also ahead in the movie genre with ZEE Cinema raking in 0.06% and Sony MAX fetching 0.04%.

Star Gold’s cricket coverage was watched by 41,300 viewers at 10:15 – it continued its good run until 14:45. The movie ‘Hero’ starring Sooraj Pancholi did well on the channel also with 14,800 viewers at 16:00.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
