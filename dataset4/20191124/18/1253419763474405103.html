The Witcher 3 Enhanced Edition V4.75 released, updates friendly HUD, fixes various issues and more

24 Nov 2019, 18:13 by John Papadopoulos

The Dedicated W3EE Mod Team has released a brand new version of The Witcher 3 Enhanced Edition. In case you weren’t aware of, this is a complete and comprehensive rework of The Witcher 3, offering a much higher quality experience than the original game ever could.

Version 4.75 updates the friendly HUD, makes toxicity fever blur deactivate in cutscenes and makes free Aard casts not drain NPC stamina.

This latest version also comes with a number of fixes. For instance, it fixes 3D marker toggle keybind not being in the input settings file. It also fixes alt yrden not working on flying creatures and the incorrect white raffard potion toxicity levels. Furthermore, it fixes the meteorite silver armor abilities.

Those of you who want to overhaul the game’s gameplay mechanics can download the Enahnced Edition mod from here. As a bonus, you can also download the latest version of the Immersive Lighting mod.

Immersive Lighting is a complete overhaul of lighting in base game and the two DLCs. Its purposes were to achieve pleasable effect, give fantasy vibe at day and realistic during nights, eliminate ugly lights put into cutscenes, and extend weather system in all regions.
