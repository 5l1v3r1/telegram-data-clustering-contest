Foreign National Killed, Five Injured as Grenade Hurled at UN Vehicle in Afghan Capital Kabul

According to Afghanistan's Interior Ministry spokesman Nasrat Rahimi, five other people, including two Afghan personnel, were injured in the incident.

24 Nov 2019, 18:19

A foreign national has been killed and at least five people sustained injuries after a grenade was thrown at a United Nations vehicle in Afghanistan's capital city of Kabul, the country's Interior Ministry spokesman Nasrat Rahimi said on Sunday.
