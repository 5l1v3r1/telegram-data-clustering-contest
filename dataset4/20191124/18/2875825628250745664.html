Kieran Hayler hints he's upset Katie Price stole snap of kids Bunny and Jett

Kieran urged fans to remember that 'the original is always better than the copy' after estranged wife Katie posted the same picture that he had of their two kids together, Bunny and Jett

24 Nov 2019, 18:04 by Irishmirror.ie

Katie Price's estranged husband Kieran Hayler has hinted he's not got a cool head after the former glamour model took one of his pictures of their kids for her own Instagram .

Kieran, 32, posted a snap of Bunny, five and Jett, six, looking adorable dressed up in old timey clothes.

Mum Katie, 41, followed suit - the day after, according to Instagram's time stamp - posting the cute snap with a string of heart emojis.

Kieran might not be alright with that, though, as he shared a quote with his fans that read: "Never be intimidated by someone who copies you. The original is always better than the copy."

Katie and her third husband have had a turbulent and troubled relationship, meeting online back in 2012.

They got hitched in an intimate Bahamas ceremony the year after, with Katie announcing she was expecting her fourth child just a month later - Jett was born in August 2013.

She'd been married twice before - to I'm A Celeb co-star Peter Andre from 2005 to 2009 and stripper Alex Reid from 2010 to 2012.

Days after Katie revealed she was pregnant again - with Bunny - Kieran's cheating came to light.

It was made all the more dramatic by the fact it had been with one of Pricey's closest pals and a bridesmaid at their wedding, Jane Pountney.

Kieran headed to sex addiction therapy around the same time that another of his affairs was revealed....with another of Katie's friends.

She told fans she'd be divorcing Kieran in August 2017 after she claimed she found emails showing his affair with their nanny Nikki Brown.

Katie's had an up and down love life since then - she's now back with toyboy lover Kris Boyson.

The mum of five had initially dumped Kris by text while she partied away with her new flame Charles Drury.

That soon came to an end and Katie reunited with Kris.

She hasn't been shy about their relationship, revealing in a radio interview that she might even be walking down the aisle again next year.

"Next year maybe a baby and a wedding," she told Flex FM soon after the rumours of their reunion first began to swirl.

It's now claimed that Katie's keen to get hitched again to rile ex-husband Peter Andre.

A source told Closer : "Katie does genuinely love Kris and wants to marry him but she can't help seeing the wedding as a way of getting one over on Pete, too.

"Her life has been a mess recently and she hasn't been able to stand the thought of Pete pitying her while he's got a 'perfect' home life with his wife Emily."
