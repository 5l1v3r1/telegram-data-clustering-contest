Abu Dhabi T10 League 2019: Maratha Arabians crowned champions of season 2

24 Nov 2019, 18:01 by Vinay Chhabaria

The Maratha Arabians crushed the Deccan Gladiators in the final of Abu Dhabi T10 League 2019 to emerge as the winners of the tournament. Chadwick Walton and Dwayne Bravo starred for the Arabians as they won the match by 8 wickets.

The champions had won the toss and elected to bowl first at the Sheikh Zayed Stadium. Mitchell McClenaghan (1/12) struck for the Arabians in the first over itself as he removed the dangerous Shane Watson (1). Lasith Malinga (1/18) got the better of Mohammad Shahzad (14) in the next over as both the Deccan openers were back in the hut in the mandatory powerplay.

Daniel Lawrence (4) and Kieron Pollard (5) tried their best to get going in the middle however, Bravo (2/16) and Kasun Rajitha (1/15) claimed their wickets, respectively.
