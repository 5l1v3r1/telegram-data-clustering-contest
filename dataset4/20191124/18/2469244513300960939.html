Injured man rescued from under ride on lawn mower

Ambulance and fire crews were sent to the A377 in Umberleigh after the call came in at 4:15pm.

24 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Richard Booth

Emergency service rushed to North Devon on Sunday afternoon after a man became injured and stuck under a ride on lawn mower.

Ambulance and fire crews were sent to the A377 in Umberleigh after the call came in at 4:15pm.

One fire engine from Chulmleigh and one from South Molton were sent to the scene.

Devon & Somerset Fire & Rescue Service said they were called to a report of a male stuck beneath a ride on lawn mower.
