Knowledge Drop: Kanye West Made A T-Pain Diss Track While They Worked On ‘808s & Heartbreak’ Together

24 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Grant Rindner

T-Pain helped Kanye West on his massively influential fourth studio album, 808s & Heartbreak—which celebrates its 11th anniversary on November 24—but their relationship was sometimes fraught. As T-Pain explained n a 2014 interview with DJ Vlad, West even went so far as to make up a diss song about him while they were in the studio together.
