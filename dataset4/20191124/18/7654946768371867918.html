Prince Andrew buried his face in stripper’s breasts at Jeffrey Epstein party: report

24 Nov 2019, 18:03 by Kate Sheehy

A former lap dancer says Prince Andrew once greeted her at Jeffrey Epstein’s Manhattan pad by burying his face between her breasts and blowing a raspberry.

“I was shocked, but I laughed, thinking, ‘Wow, what was that?’ … I guess being a prince gives you those privileges,” the woman told the Sunday Mirror of Britain.

“Anyone doing that in a bar or in the street would have got a punch in the face, believe me.”

The ex-exotic dancer said she had been paid by Epstein, a multimillionaire pedophile, to hire young women for the party, including those specifically tailored to Andrew’s tastes — big-breasted and curvy, like his former wife, Sarah Ferguson.

“They were mostly older teens paid $200, $300,” the woman said, adding that there were rooms upstairs in the posh townhouse set up for sex.

“It was a big night, really important for Jeffrey. The girls had been specially selected to try to please Andrew,” said the woman, now 47, who was only identified as “Tiffany Doe.”

“Epstein made it clear he wanted to make Andrew happy. I was told to make sure he was looked after, that he had a drink, that he was OK.

“The duke seemed in good spirits and was out to enjoy himself. I always thought Andrew liked my breasts. When he started to say hello, he looked at them first rather than me,” the woman said.

“He leaned forward and put his face on them and blew a raspberry.”

She said she laughed and that the prince “laughed, too. He’d never done it before, and I thought, ‘It’s going to be a good night.’

“He said I should be in a magazine for big-breasted women.”

The woman said such raspberry-blowing is known as “motor-boating,” although the “Randy Andy” royal fumbled when he tried to recall the term.

“He didn’t quite know the term ‘motorboat.’ He said, ‘Motor… something?’

“He was trying to come off cool, but he didn’t know the words. I found him quite awkward, quite clumsy,” she said.

It wasn’t clear exactly when the party was, but the woman said she had been to previous gigs that Andrew attended.

“I saw Andrew at parties a couple of times a year, if not more. I would flirt with him and he would hit on me and was handsy. He would stand in close proximity, looking at my breasts,” the woman said.

“He would hold my hands, put his arm around my waist, stroke my cheek. He used royalty, his celebrity, to his advantage.”

Prince Andrew has come under fire for his ties to Epstein, who authorities have said committed suicide in a Manhattan jail cell in August while awaiting trial on sex-trafficking charges.

Andrew has been accused of raping an underage Epstein “sex slave,” who was allegedly 17 at the time, and knowing about at least some of his financier pal’s behavior.

The prince has vehemently denied any wrongdoing, including in a disastrous TV interview earlier this month in which he offered outrageous claims about the two men’s time together.
