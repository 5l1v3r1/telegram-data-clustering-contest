Irish Trio In Thick Of The Action As Blades & Man United Play Out 3-3 Draw

24 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Oisin McQueirns

Irish internationals David McGoldrick, Enda Stevens and Callum Robinson were in the thick of a crazy game at Bramall Lane as Sheffield United and Manchester United drew 3-3.

From the off the home side looked the more comfortable and it was McGoldrick who almost gave them the lead as he searched for his first Premier League goal.

John Lundstram’s strike from the edge of the area was well saved by David De Gea, but came out to Stevens who found his international teammate McGoldrick with a brilliant cross only for the Spaniard to react excellently and keep his effort out.
