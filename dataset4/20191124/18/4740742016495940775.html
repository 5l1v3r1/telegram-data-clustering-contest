These are the most viewed Bristol places to rent on Zoopla

They range from a four-bedroom Hotwells house to a double room in Kingswood

24 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Conor Gogarty

The most viewed Bristol rental listings on property website Zoopla have been revealed.

These include a four-bedroom house in Hotwells available for £1,700 a month and a Kingswood room you can rent for just £450 a month.

The 10 properties on the list are the most viewed Bristol rentals on Zoopla over the past month.

Zoopla spokeswoman Laura Howard said: "If you want to see fascinating properties in your local area, refining your Zoopla search by ‘most popular’ is a great way to see what’s really grabbing attention.”
