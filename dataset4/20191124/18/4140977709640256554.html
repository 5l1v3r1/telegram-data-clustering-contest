Sheffield United Vs Manchester United: Watch Marcus Rashford make Manchester United comeback after 2 goal deficit against the Blades.

Manchester United had a thrilling night at the Bramall Lane as they have made a sensational comeback against Sheffield United to avoid a defeat even though they were under a deficit of 2 goals for the majority of the game.

The Red Devils were cursed with constant attacking threats by Sheffield United in the first 60 minutes of the game, in which the Blades were able to seal 2 goals with their brilliance.

From there it looked that Manchester United had no coming back from this but Ole Solskjaer’s boys did not bend and put pressure on the hosts in the last phase of the game.

It allowed Manchester United to bag three goals in less than 8 minutes to snatch a victory away from the hands of Sheffield United. It was a strike by Brandon Williams in the 72nd minute which motivated his teammates to believe.

Later, Mason Greenwood also sent the ball inside the box and it was assisted by Rashford. However, the highlight was Rashford’s goal in the process as in the 79th minute the English striker found the net after receiving a ball from Daniel James from the left-flank.
