Better, brighter Noel Bazaar

24 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Manila Standard Showbiz

Noel Bazaar, the metro’s longest-running Christmas shopping extravaganza, is all geared up for a better and brighter holiday series with over 500 merchants on board to delight shoppers.

Marking its 19th year, the iconic bazaar series is continuously committed to being a one-stop holiday shopping destination where shoppers can discover affordable gift options, sample delicious treats, and enjoy cashless shopping during the most wonderful time of the year. It is also co-located with the International Food Feast Bazaar, showcasing mouth-watering holiday eats such as baked ham, desserts, and a curated selection of signature food finds.

 In partnership with GMA Network, Noel Bazaar features an exciting line-up of special events which features Kapuso stars as this year’s ambassadors – Heart Evangelista, Benjamin Alves, Camille Pratts, Sanya Lopez, Althea Ablan, and Sofia Pablo. 

Visitors will enjoy the heart-warming sound of Christmas music and see live performances while strolling the shopping village. They also get a chance to bring home pre-loved clothing, luxury bags, shoes, and accessories of their favorite Kapuso personalities via the Celebrity Ukay-Ukay. Celebrity Auction and variety shows are also held every weekend at the Bazaar’s World Trade Center and SMX Convention Center runs. 

 Proceeds from the Celebrity Ukay-Ukay will support GMA Kapuso Foundation’s educational projects such as Unang Hakbang sa Kinabukasan and the Kapuso School Development Program.

To kick the season of giving into high gear, the team behind Noel Bazaar has prepared online contests and raffle prizes which include limited edition Noel Bazaar merchandise and other special items to be given away during the event.

Catch Noel Bazaar on the following dates: Nov. 27 to Dec. 1 at the Filinvest Tent Alabang; and Dec.19 to 22 at the SMX Convention Center.
