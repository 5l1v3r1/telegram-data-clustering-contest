BREAKING: Man left in critical condition following shooting in North Dublin

24 Nov 2019, 18:25 by Nick Fitzgerald

The incident took place in Coolock

A man in his 20s has been left critically injured following a shooting that occurred in Coolock in north Dublin.

The incident took place on Clonshaugh Avenue in Dublin 17 at approximately 5:15pm on Sunday.

Gardai and emergency services were called to the scene where they discovered the man with "a number of apparent gunshot wounds."

He was treated at the scene and has since been removed by ambulance to Beaumont Hospital.

His condition is understood to be critical.
