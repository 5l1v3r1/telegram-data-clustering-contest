How Birmingham City's promotion chances was affected by Huddersfield Town draw

Birmingham City latest | A regularly-updated list of the Championship odds from the league title to relegation after 1-1 draw with Huddersfield Town

24 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Jamie Kemble, Mark Wakefield

Birmingham City resume their Sky Bet Championship campaign on Saturday as they were held to a 1-1 draw with Huddersfield Town.

The clash at the John Smith's Stadium was Blues' first match after the November international break.

That result leaves Blues 15th in the division - six points off the play-off places and nine clear of the relegation zone.

But what do the bookmakers think about their chances of going up or indeed down this term ahead of their return to action at Sheffield Wednesday in midweek?
