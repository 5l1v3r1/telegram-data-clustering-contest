Davido's fan talks about A Good Time, says album is full of trash songs

24 Nov 2019, 16:55

- Davido's fan on Twitter has stated his disappointment about the singer's latest album A Good Time

- The fan noted that the songs on the album were all trash

- However, he still added that he loves the singer and he is a fan.

A fan of singer David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, has shared his thoughts about the singer's latest album A Good Time on the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter.

Despite the fact that Davido's latest album has been gaining positive reactions all over, a fan recently expressed disappointment about the album. The disappointed fan stated that the album is full of trash songs.

Legit.ng had reported that fans had praised Davido's work, saying that he had evolved. However, this fan identified as @realiyanu stated that songs on the album the not please him.
