Tuck into a brilliant Saturday roast at Gordon Ramsay Hell’s Kitchen

Sponsored: From delicious tender-aged beef to classic Yorkshire pudding, this one’s a no-brainer

24 Nov 2019, 16:08 by Time Out staff

A fiery Saturday awaits all those who dare to enter Gordon Ramsay Hell’s Kitchen at Caesars Palace Bluewaters Dubai. Those who do? You’re in for a sizzling treat…

The world-class chefs at Gordon Ramsay’s famed restaurant are cooking up a right storm every Saturday at the immersive restaurant's weekly Saturday Roast.

Taking place from noon until 4pm, foodies around Dubai can expect the restaurant’s signature tomato-based mixed drink, along with one of the finest prawn cocktails around to kick off the roast.
