Man arrested at Cloverhill Prison following recent death

24 Nov 2019, 16:04 by Nick Fitzgerald

The male in his 50s was arrested on suspicion of murder

A man in his 50s has been arrested by gardai at Cloverhill Prison, following the death of a 30-year-old inmate yesterday.

He was arrested this morning, Sunday, November 24 on suspicion of murder.

Shortly after midnight, in the early hours of Saturday, November 23, gardai were called to a cell at Cloverhill Prison, following an incident involving two male prisoners.

A 30-year-old was found with life-threatening injuries, which emergency services attempted to treat, however, he was pronounced dead at the scene a short time later.

State Pathologist Doctor Margaret Bolster completed a post-mortem of the body yesterday but gardai have said the results will not be released for operational reasons.

The man in his 50s, held on suspicion of murder, is currently detained at Clondalkin Garda Station under Section 4 of the Criminal Justice Act 1984.
