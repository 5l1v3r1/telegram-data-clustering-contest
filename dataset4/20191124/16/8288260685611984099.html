'Shocking' - Huddersfield fans have mixed feelings after Birmingham City stalemate

Latest Birmingham City news | Blues had to settle for a point in their Sky Bet Championship clash against the Terriers

24 Nov 2019, 16:37 by Mark Wakefield

Huddersfield Town fans have been giving their verdict on the 1-1 draw with Birmingham City on Saturday.

Blues came from a goal behind to earn a point at the John Smith’s Stadium in their first game back after the November international break.

Fraizer Campbell opened the scoring for the Terriers before Marc Roberts equalised for Blues with just over 10 minutes left to play.

Blues now lie in 15th place in the Championship table going into Wednesday night’s trip to face Sheffield Wednesday at Hillsborough.

Here is what Huddersfield supporters had to say after the final whistle at the John Smith’s Stadium.

Daniel Peckett: “Lack of leaders on the pitch going forward once Campbell and Pritchard were subbed. O’Brien was a natural at left back!”

Mike Shackleton: “Good performance, should have won, but that’s football!”

Heather Whitehead: “Saw some beautiful football being played by the terriers, Hoggy, O'Brien and Pritchard were class. Hoggy is a machine.”
