NRL 2020: Valentine Holmes signs long-term deal with North Queensland Cowboys

24 Nov 2019, 16:32

Valentine Holmes will return to the NRL in 2020, after signing with North Queensland on a six-year deal.

The Cowboys wasted no time snapping up the former Cronulla flyer, inking a deal until the end of the 2025 season, 24 hours after Holmes was officially released by the NFL's New York Jets.

Townsville-born Holmes, 24, is tipped to don the No.1 jersey for North Queensland, after signing the lucrative long term deal, reportedly worth almost AU$1 million a season.

While known for his prowess on the wing, Holmes played most of his games in the 2018 NRL season at fullback for the Sharks, scoring 22 tries in 26 games.
