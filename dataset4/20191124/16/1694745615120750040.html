Kaia Gerber and Pete Davidson really seem to be enjoying their time together in Miami. They looked adorable hanging out by the pool, then later attended a friend's wedding, holding hands as they made their way into the party. Kaia wore the Reformation Sigmund Dress in Splatter print for the occasion. The navy blue midi comes complete with a slit at the leg, but has a modest neckline and pouf sleeves. It looked great accessorized simply with a delicate necklace and small hoops, as this dress deserves the major shoe moment that Kaia gave it with her strappy leather sandals.

The 18-year-old supermodel also toted a red mini bag for a pop of color. Miami calls for a bold little touch like that, now doesn't it? While Kaia's particular pattern is sold out at the moment, the silhouette is still available in a floral pattern and plain black, and I'm already picturing it hanging in my closet, just begging to be worn. It's not only versatile and appropriate for so many events, it's also pretty season-less. Scroll down to see Kaia's outfit from every angle, then shop the exact design, along with similar pieces we found that fit the bill. Bonus points if you take a tip from Kaia and make mixing navy and black look this good.
