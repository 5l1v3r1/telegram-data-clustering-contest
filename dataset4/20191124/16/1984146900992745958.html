When is Sunderland vs Burton Albion, is it on TV, what time is kick-off and who is in the teams?

The Black Cats welcome the Brewers to the Stadium of Light on Tuesday - here is how you can follow it live

24 Nov 2019, 16:13 by Sean McCormick

Sunderland welcome Burton Albion to the Stadium of Light on Tuesday as they look to kickstart their promotion charge.

Phil Parkinson's difficult start to life as Sunderland boss continued on Saturday as they stumbled to a 1-1 draw at home to Coventry.

Parkinson has won just two of his opening 10 games as Black Cats boss and the draw left Sunderland in tenth place in the League One table - eight points adrift of the automatic promotion places and 13 points behind league leaders Wycombe.

But a win over Burton on Tuesday would put Sunderland back into the play-off picture and they welcome a Brewers side that sit in 15th place in the League One table.

Nigel Clough's travel to Wearside out of form themselves and are without a win in their last seven games in all competitions.

When is Sunderland vs Burton Albion?

Sunderland take on Burton Albion on Tuesday, November 26. It is one of four League One fixtures taking place on Tuesday evening.

Is Sunderland vs Burton Albion on TV? Can I follow it live?

It is not on TV but the game will likely be streamed on Sunderland's official website. Details are yet to be confirmed, though.
