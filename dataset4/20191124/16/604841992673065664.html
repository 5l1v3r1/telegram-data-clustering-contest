You can't beat Bohermeen for style

24 Nov 2019, 16:02 by Conall Collier

Local woman Niamh Rispin was the winner of Winter Ladies Day which was an off-track feature at Navan Races Troytown Day at Proudstown this afternoon.

Judged by Mary Kennedy (RTE Nationwide) who said - "I was so delighted when Navan Racecourse approached me to be their Winter Ladies Day judge and I was just delighted to accept."

Sponsored by Navan Town Centre, the winner won't have far to travel as she hails from Bohermeen.

Niamh wore a jumpsuit from Penneys and her hat was by Milliner Suzanne Ryan and she won a €1,600 shopping voucher for the Navan Town Centre while the runners-up each got €100 vouchers for Navan Town Centre.

Pat Mullaney from Navan Town Centre said: “Navan Town Centre is delighted to continue its partnership with Navan Racecourse and their Winter Ladies Day for the seventh year and congratulations to our winner.
