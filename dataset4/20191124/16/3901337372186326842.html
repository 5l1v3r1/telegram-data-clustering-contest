City Confirm Progress Of One More Midfielder

24 Nov 2019, 16:19 by Soccer Laduma

Cape Town City have confirmed that another one of their midfielders is in the final stage of rehabilitation.

Abbubaker Mobara had a disappointing start to his career at the Citizens, after an injury has left him on the sidelines for the past two months.
