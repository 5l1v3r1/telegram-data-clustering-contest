IPL: Naft Masjed Solyman Stuns Persepolis [VIDEO]

24 Nov 2019, 16:54 by Mir Farhad Ali Khan

Tasnim – TEHRAN, Naft Masjed Soleyman football team defeated Persepolis 1-0 here at the Azadi Stadium on Friday.

Alireza Alizadeh scored the only goal of the match in the 76th minute.

Persepolis created several scoring chances in the final minutes of the match but its strikers lacked the cutting edge.

Titleholder Persepolis remained in third place with 19 points, four points behind IPL leader Shahr Khodro who has one match in hand.
