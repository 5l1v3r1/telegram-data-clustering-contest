LOOK: Previews for Every Marvel Comic Arriving Wednesday, Nov. 27

24 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Samuel Gelman

Marvel Comics is set to continue some of its most popular series this week, as the publisher will be releasing 17 different books.

Of the new books releasing this week, there are new installments in a number of popular continuing series, including Venom, Avengers, Ghost Spider and Ironheart. Jane Foster will also continue her adventures as Valkyrie in Valkyrie: Jane Foster.  Of course, there is also something new for Star Wars fans with a new issue of Star Wars: Doctor Aphra. This week will also see the sophomore issues of Fallen Angels, Fantastic Four: Grand Design, New Mutants, Yondu and X-Force. Finally, also this week, Marvel kicked off its 2099 storyline for the Punisher and Conan with Punisher 2099 and Conan 2099.

You can see all of the books releasing this Wednesday below, with links to the previews that will show you what's to come from Marvel Comics.
