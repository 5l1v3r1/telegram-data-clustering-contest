NIC OF TIME

Nicole Scherzinger parties with Thom Evans until the small hours in dress showing her incredible abs

24 Nov 2019, 16:14 by Adam Nutburn

NICOLE Scherzinger partied with Thom Evans until the early hours of this morning.

The X Factor judge, 41, fuelled rumours she's dating the hunky Scots sportsman as the pair hit the town last night.

Former Pussycat Doll Nicole wore stylish pink dress that showed off her abs, which she paired with a pair of silver heels.

Thom and Nicole spent time together after she sent Thom's band, Try Star, home from The X Factor: Celebrity.

After filming, Nicole went to burlesque themed nightclub Cirque Le Soir. She arrived at the venue just before midnight and Thom wasn't far behind, according to the Mirror.

A source said: "Thom looked worse for wear when he left the nightclub after 2:30am, with liquid covering the left side of his body."

Nicole reportedly left the venue at 3:30am.

Show insiders previously told The Sun how the pair flirted and texted for weeks before Thom, 34, finally asked out 41-year-old singer Nicole.

An ITV source said: “Thom is completely Nicole’s type. And for his part, Thom was smitten early on — he’s always said Nicole is one of the most beautiful women in the world.

"He fancied her long before meeting her on the show. He was initially a bit nervous about starting something up, and ruining his chances on the series, but they had great chemistry from the off.

“And after weeks of texting, Thom finally plucked up the courage to ask her out, and took her for dinner."

The source added: “They met up with friends, all had drinks, and were laughing and joking the whole night. They looked to be having the time of their lives.

"They both want to focus on the series for now though, and then see what may or may not progress once the show has wrapped.

"But Nicole deserves to meet someone lovely and everyone is hopeful this will work out.”
