Tamar Braxton Pens An Emotional Message For David Adefeso’s 50th Birthday

24 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Ron Collins

Tamar Braxton celebrated her man, David Adefeso’s 50th birthday, this weekend. She made sure to share an emotional message on her social media account, just to mark this important event.

‘It’s not too many times that you meet someone and your world shakes. That happened to me when we locked eyes at Gene’s birthday party almost two years ago. You were a young man then, 😂 and also then, I knew we we would grow old together. You are my BEST friend and I thank you for so many things. Mostly for loving my son just like he’s your own. I can’t wait to turn up at your very first birthday party🥰 happy 50th bday @david.adefeso you may be an old man but you are still fine, Rich, and ALL MINE🤣🤣😂😂’ Tamar captioned her post.
