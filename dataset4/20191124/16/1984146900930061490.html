Revealed: Newcastle United's goal of the season so far as chosen by fans

Hunders of votes were cast by ChronicleLive readers - and we can now reveal Newcastle United's goal of the season so far

24 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Chris Knight

Newcastle United have remarkably relied on defenders for most of their goals so far this campaign.

The Magpies have scored just 11 times in their opening 12 Premier League encounters, with centre-backs such as Ciaran Clark, Federico Fernandez, Jamaal Lascelles, and Fabian Schar all chipping in.

But despite United's struggles in the attacking third, Steve Bruce's side have already notched several memorable strikes for supporters to savour.

We decided to select four of these and put it to a vote during the international break to see which goal would be considered the fans' favourite so far.

The four contenders read as follows:

  • Joelinton's first United goal against Tottenham
  • Jetro Willems' thunderbolt at Liverpool
  • Matty Longstaff's winner against Manchester United
  • Jonjo Shelvey's free-kick at West Ham.

Hundreds of votes were cast by ChronicleLive readers, and two strikes stood out as clear favourites among supporters.

But - perhaps unsurprisingly - Matty Longstaff's memorable goal against the Red Devils in front of the Gallowgate came out on top for the best goal of the season so far.

The 19-year-old secured 45% of the vote - edging out Willems' surprise opener at Anfield which was backed by 42% of supporters.

Shelvey's screamer at the London Stadium - which ultimately proved to be the winner for the Magpies - rounded out the podium positions after being backed by 10% of fans.

Joelinton's winner at Tottenham following a 17-pass move was selected by just 4% of supporters.
