LSU Football Star Has A Message For Texas A&M

24 Nov 2019, 16:13 by Andrew Holleran

LSU and Texas A&M played the craziest game of the 2018 college football season. The Tigers and the Aggies went to seven overtimes. Jimbo Fisher’s team ended up winning, 74-72, in the wildest contest of the year.

We’ll have the rematch on Saturday.

LSU is set to host Texas A&M in Baton Rouge on Saturday night.

Tigers’ star linebacker K’Lavon Chaisson had a message for the Aggies following his team’s win over Arkansas.

“We owe them,” he tweeted.
