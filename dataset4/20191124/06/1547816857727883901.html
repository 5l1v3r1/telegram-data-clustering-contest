Madhuri Dixit Strums The Guitar And Jams To John Legend’s All Of Me With Shriram Nene; Color Us Impressed- VIDEO

24 Nov 2019, 06:25 by Lubna Khan

In the latest Instagram post by Madhuri Dixit, she can be seen singing the super hit song ‘All of Me’ by the multiple Grammy-winning musician John Legend. Along with Madhuri, her husband Dr. Shriram Nene and musician Zac D’Souza can be seen playing guitars as well, and we’re impressed!

We’ve known Madhuri Dixit for a lot of her talents in all these years including her acting and her dancing and she has managed to surprise her fans even after decades of being in the limelight by displaying a new talent altogether, that is, singing. In a recent video posted on Instagram by her, she can be seen strumming a guitar and singing and was actually quite good at it. Along with her in the video, her husband Dr. Shriram Nene and musician Zac D’Souza can be seen playing guitars as well.

Madhuri Dixit posted the small clip on her official Instagram handle captioning it “Family jam session! It was fun trying my hand at playing the guitar with @drneneofficial ‘cuz “All of me, loves all of you...” #WeekendJam #AllOfMe @zacdsouzaa.” She can be seen singing the super hit song ‘All of Me’ by the multiple Grammy-winning musician John Legend. Well, color us impressed because now we know for sure that there’s nothing this diva cannot do!  Madhuri Dixit has been active on Instagram recently and was also seen wishing choreographer Saroj Khan on her birthday with a heartfelt post on the platform.

On the professional front, Madhuri Dixit was last seen in the movie Kalank alongside Varun Dhawan, Alia Bhatt, Aditya Roy Kapoor, Sonakshi Sinha, Sanjay Dutt, and Kunal Khemu. She has also forayed into production and recently produced a Marathi movie titled ‘Panchak’ along with her husband Shriram Nene.
