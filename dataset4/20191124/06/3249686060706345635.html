VERIVERY reveal their fun official colors

24 Nov 2019, 06:08 by jennywill

VERIVERY has revealed their official colors.

The three colors are PANTONE 7649 C, PANTONE 136 C, and Glittering Silver. The colors mean that VERIVERY will relay their warm sincerity to VERRERs (their fans) even through the flashiness, and that VERIVERY and VERRERs will be a shining existence to each other.
