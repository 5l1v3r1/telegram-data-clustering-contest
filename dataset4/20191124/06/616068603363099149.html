Credit unions join forces

24 Nov 2019, 06:35 by Kirk Penton

Prospera Credit Union and Westminster Savings are officially united.

The members of both credit unions voted overwhelmingly in favour of the merger, which was proposed by the boards of directors of both companies after talks began 18 months ago.

The merger, which is the largest in Canadian credit union history, will become official on Jan. 1. At least two-thirds of both memberships had to vote in favour of the coalition for it to go ahead.
