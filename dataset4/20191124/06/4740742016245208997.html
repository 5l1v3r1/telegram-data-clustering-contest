How to get a free mince pie and drink at Morrisons

It's for one day only - and only for certain customers

24 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Molly Holt, Alexander Brock

Morrisons is giving away a free mince pie and drink to all OAPs ahead of Christmas this year.

Senior citizens can get the free refreshments in the store's café between 2.30pm and 5.30pm on December 3, Mirror Online reports.

The supermarket said the deal will provide the perfect opportunity for older customers to interact with each other during the festive period.

A spokesperson for Morrisons said: "Nobody should have to be alone over the festive season.

"To show our older customers how much we value them — and spread a little festive cheer — we’re offering all OAPs a free hot drink and mince pie in our cafes between 2.30pm and 5pm on Tuesday, December 3."

A similar deal has been run by the supermarket before, at both Christmas and at Easter, when Morrisons gave out free hot cross buns and cups of tea.
