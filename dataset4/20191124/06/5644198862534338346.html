Flamengo lift Copa Libertadores with 2-1 win over River Plate

24 Nov 2019, 06:05

Flamengo won the Copa Libertadores for the first time in 38 years with two goals in the last three minutes to come from behind and beat River Plate 2-1 on Saturday.

LIMA: Flamengo won the Copa Libertadores for the first time in 38 years with two goals in the last three minutes to come from behind and beat River Plate 2-1 on Saturday.
