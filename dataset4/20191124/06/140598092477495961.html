8 of the best beauty gifts for Christmas, handpicked by make-up guru Mary Greenwell

24 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Telegraph Writers

There are few famous faces left in Hollywood on which Mary Greenwell has not graced with her beauty expertise. The make-up artist is regularly booked by stars such as Cate Blanchett, Amanda Seyfried and Lily James. Her most monumental career highlight is arguably working with Princess Diana. The pair first met in 1991, during a Vogue cover shoot and Greenwell continued to be her beauty right-hand-woman throughout her life. 

Though her career has had a 30 life span thus far, her approach to make-up is somewhat refreshing. In a time which strongly focuses on picture perfection, be it for a cover of a magazine or an Instagram post, Greenwell focuses her attention on highlighting her client's beauty, not masking their flaws.  

For Mary, Christmas 2019 will be a non-traditional one. "I will be in Lamu, in Kenya and we'll be eating traditional Swahili food. This year, it will be a great big gorgeous red snapper."
