People are going wild for Lidl's Christmas food - including pigs in blanket crisps

And they're vegetarian

24 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Danny Shaw

Christmas really is just around the corner, and now Lidl has launched a range of festive snacks to get you in the mood.

The new range could be perfect for any family gatherings or Christmas parties - and it includes pigs in blanket flavoured crisps - which are suitable for vegetarians.

The budget supermarket is also selling Wiltshire guinea fowl and black truffle crisps, but it's the pigs in blanket flavour fans are really loving.

One fan said on social media: "Today I bought Lidl's pigs in blankets crisps, and they actually taste like pigs in blankets."
