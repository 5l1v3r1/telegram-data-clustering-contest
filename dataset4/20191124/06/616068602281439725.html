OK Builders site goes retail

24 Nov 2019, 06:40 by Wayne Moore

Kelowna city council will review a plan to rezone the former OK Builders site on Ellis Street from industrial to business commercial use.

The site has been vacant since the company shut down valleywide operations in January.

The property has a future land use designation of mixed use residential, which staff say would support the zoning change.

According to the proposal before council, the owner of the property plans to lease the existing buildings to a new tenant or tenants with a more "retail-oriented focus."

A company did recently use the building for a brief sale of Persian rugs.

In the long-term, the owner does expect to apply to redevelop the site, and wants zoning in place consistent with their vision.
