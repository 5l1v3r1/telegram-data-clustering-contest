Predicting The College Football AP Poll After Week 13 Of The 2019 Season

24 Nov 2019, 04:08 by Matt Lombardi

This Saturday was light on big-time matchups, but that doesn’t mean there wasn’t drama. No. 6 Oregon, thought to be a College Football Playoff contender, fell on the road to Arizona State in an absolute stunner.

Ohio State, meanwhile, clinched the Big Ten East and a spot in the league’s title game with a win over Penn State. The Buckeyes continue to look like one of the best teams in the country.

LSU, the No. 1 team in the playoff rankings, had little trouble with Arkansas. Elsewhere in the SEC, Alabama crushed Western Carolina while Georgia held off a late Texas A&M comeback effort.

At this point, we’re realistically down to only eight or nine teams that have a shot at the playoff. It’ll be fascinating to see how it all plays out.
