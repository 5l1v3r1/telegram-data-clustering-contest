Special Weather Statement issued November 24 at 4:16AM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

24 Nov 2019, 04:16

Special Weather Statement: Tompkins, Cortland, Tioga and 1 more area until 9:00 AM

Wintry mix will change to snow this morning, which may still cause
untreated roads to become slick, mainly at higher elevations this
morning. 1 to 2 inches of snow accumulation will be possible in
the higher terrain, with lesser amounts of slushy accumulation in
the valleys. Exercise caution if traveling this morning.
