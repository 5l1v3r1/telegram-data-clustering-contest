Buick's Coolest Model Receives Huge Discount This Month

But the clock is ticking.

24 Nov 2019, 21:35 by Jay Traugott

Buick had done an excellent job of turning itself into an upmarket premium brand just short of all-out luxury. The latter duty is handled by GM's Cadillac division. Meanwhile, Buick has proven to be a formidable competitor against the likes of Acura and Lincoln, mainly in the crossover segments. But there's still yet another current Buick model that goes too unnoticed way too often. The 2020 Buick Regal TourX wagon is one of the Subaru Outback's main competitors thanks to its standard all-wheel drive, sufficient power, and cool styling.

Like the Outback, the Regal TourX is the less expensive alternative to the Audi A4 allroad, which carries a base MSRP of $45,700. The Regal TourX? Just under $30,000. And now Buick is offering up to a 20 percent discount on most 2020 Regal TourX wagons this month, but considering we're already heading into Thanksgiving this week, time is limited to take advantage of this offer.

If you're a non-GM owner or current competitive owner, then you're eligible for up to 20 percent off MSRP, which equates to nearly $8,000 in savings for "most" Regal TourX models. The offer does not state exactly what "most" means in this case, but Buick does provide the following example: a Regal TourX Essence model with an MSRP of $39,675 receives a $1,364 price reduction below MSRPR along with a $6,571 purchase allowance, adding up to $7,935 in savings for a new total of $31,740.

The Regal TourX Essence also happens to be the highest trim level. There's also the base TourX 1SV and mid-level Preferred, with MSRPs of $29,995 and $33,595, respectively. The second Regal TourX offer this month is $1,000 cash plus 0 percent APR for 72 months. Both offers, however, must be with GM Financial.

Along with standard AWD, all Regal TourX wagons come powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four good for 250 horsepower and paired to an eight-speed automatic transmission. Features such as dual-zone climate control are now standard for 2020. This current offer specifically states buyers must take delivery by December 2, 2019.
