Eagles Defense Celebrates Turnover With Team Photo Down 17-3 In 4th Quarter (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Philadelphia Eagles are on their way to being 5-6 on the season and the team’s offense decided to not show up in their game against the Seattle Seahawks.

Late in the 4th quarter, the team’s defense recovered a fumble and proceeded to run to the end zone and celebrate with a team photo.

The Eagles were down 14 points with a little over 9 minutes to go in the game.
