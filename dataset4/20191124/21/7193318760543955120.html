Video: Tulsa native Tim Blake Nelson talks about his role, Oklahoma setting on HBO's 'Watchmen'

24 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Brandy McDonnell

Last week's fifth episode of the critically acclaimed HBO series "Watchmen" revealed the origins of Tulsa native Tim Blake Nelson's character, Tulsa Police Detective Wade Tillman, who wears a reflective mask and goes by the alter ego Looking Glass.

As previously reported, Damon Lindelof’s (“Lost,” “The Leftovers”) spin-off on “Watchmen,” the trailblazing 1986-87 DC comic book by writer Alan Moore and artist Dave Gibbons, is set in an alternate-reality version of Tulsa, with the 1921 Tulsa Race Massacre playing a key role in the series.
