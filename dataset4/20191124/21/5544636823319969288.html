Full List Of Lagos Experience 2019 House On Rock Guest Artists

24 Nov 2019, 21:53 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

The Lagos Experience 2019 of the House on the Rock will take place on December 6th at the T.B Square with many guest artists set to attend.

Concise News reports that the theme of the 2019 Lagos Experience is “Let’s Worship Jesus,” according to a statement by the organisers.

The Lagos Experience 2019 is an all-night musical show involving top music stars in and outside Nigeria.

Experience Lagos 2019 Guest Artist 

As noted earlier, the Experience Lagos 2019 will have some top gospel artists in Nigeria and outside the country in attendance.
