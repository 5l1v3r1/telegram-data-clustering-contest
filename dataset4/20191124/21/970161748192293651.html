Kim Kardashian says Rob Kardashian is doing 'great'

24 Nov 2019, 21:08

Kim Kardashian has opened up about her brother Rob Kardashian, who tends to avoid the showbiz spotlight, saying he’s doing ‘great.’

This makes us happy.

The Keeping up with the Kardashians star also clarified that Rob still hangs out with the family ‘a lot’, but seemingly off camera, given he’s never seen on the show that made him and his sisters famous.

‘He’ll be around for Thanksgiving,’ the fashion and beauty guru told ET. ‘He’s been around a lot – I’d say for the past year or two.’

She then added of the 32-year-old: ‘We love having him around. He’s doing really good.’

Glad to hear it.

Kim’s update follows sister Khloe recently telling the same publication that Rob is ‘doing really great, he is,’ and adding: ‘You know he’s been working out…

‘He goes to Kim’s, he’s worked out for like the past five days or so and he’s kicking a**.’

She added: ‘I just want Rob to do what’s best for him and what makes him happy. And if that makes him happy, then we will all support him.’

Last year it was reported that Rob had lost three and a half stone since quitting Keeping Up with the Kardashians.

Good for you, Rob.

The girls’ positive words about their brother follow Kim sharing a cute picture of her and daughter Chicago and Rob’s daughter Dream on social media earlier this month.

Rob is also seen in the background of the shot, which was taken to celebrate Dream’s third birthday.

She’s growing up so fast.
