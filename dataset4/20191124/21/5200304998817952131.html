Karan Patel confirms date of ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ final episode

24 Nov 2019, 21:03 by BizAsia Correspondent

Television star Karan Patel has opened up the lid on the finale of Star Plus’ popular series ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’.

Confirming the news about the show ending, Patel also gave details about the telecast date of the show’s final episode. He said, “Yes, sadly YHM is actually going off-air this time. It is good that the show is ending at its peak rather than running for many more years and retire at its low. We are still shooting for a few days. The show will air its last episode on December 20.”

Patel also confirmed that its replacement, ‘Yeh Hai Chahatein’ – a spin-off series of ‘Yeh Hai Mohababtein’ will take its place from Monday 23rd December 2019.

‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ has been Star Plus’ second longest running show (six years), after ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’.
