Logan Paul hits LA club after losing KSI boxing match

24 Nov 2019, 21:04

Logan Paul isn’t letting his recent boxing match defeat at the hands of KSI (or his subsequent wish for the result to be appealed) dampen his mood.

Indeed, the controversial YouTuber put his sporting woes to one side as he was spotted partying at an LA night spot last night.

The 24-year-old was kicking back at Hyde nightclub, weeks after the boxing extravaganza on 9 November at the Staples Center.

Also spotted at the nightspot was I Can’t Feel My Face singer The Weeknd and Kylie Jenner’s ex Tyga.

Logan’s night out follows comments in a recent video posted to social media that he’s not a ‘sore loser’ since losing the high profile fight.

Explaining why he was launching an official appeal against the result after being deducted two points (‘the punishment simply did not fit the crime’), Logan said: ‘My team and I have decided to file an official appeal for the fight because we don’t believe the outcome is right.

‘It just resonates with me to my core. One of my biggest fears about this is being a sore loser and that’s why I made this video a week after the fight.

‘I really had to check myself and make sure I wasn’t doing this because I was afraid of losing.’

He went on: ‘Historically I’ve taken big Ls. By no means am I afraid of losing.

‘I welcome it because it’s the only way I tend to experience growth in my life.

‘But I truthfully, 100 per cent believe I did not lost this fight.’

Logan’s appeal is now being formally investigated.
