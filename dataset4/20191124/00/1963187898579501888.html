Shikabala & Sassi return to Zamalek training

24 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

Mohamed Abdel Razek "Shikabala" returned to training Zamalek again after the end of his physical program, which he underwent recently to recover from injury.

Shikabala took part in the full Zamalek training and was confirmed to be ready for the upcoming matches with the white team.

Zamalek midfielder Mohamed Hassan has also returned to participate in group training after the end of his qualifying program.

Egypt's goalkeeper Mohamed Sobhi appeared in training after the end of the African Under-23 Nations Championship, and the goalkeeper coach Dragan held a session and praised his level after winning the championship and qualifying for the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games.

Mohamed Sobhi did not take part in the practice because of the stress after the tournament.

Imam Ashour, the Olympic team's team player, joined in the team training as soon as he returned to the white team.

Meanwhile, midfielder Ferjani Sassi has undergone qualifying training at the Gymnasium to recover from the injury he suffered during his participation with Tunisia a few days ago.

Olympic team duo Moustafa Mohamed and Mohamed Abdel Salam were given a break from group training with the permission of the coaching staff to recover from stress.

The duo Tarek Hamed and Ashraf Bencharki performed rehabilitative training at the Gymnasium on the sidelines of the training in order to prepare him to participate in the games normally.

And only Ibrahim Ibrahim, "Obama" rehabilitative training in the gym because of a cold.

Finally, left-back Abdullah Juma was given a break from training with the permission of Meizhou's coaching staff.

Zamalek play on Monday against Enppi in the Egyptian league, then fly to Congo to face Mazembe in the opening of the African Champions League groups.
