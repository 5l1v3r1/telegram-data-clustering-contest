Micah Parsons Gives Justin Fields Props On Instagram: “Respect Earned”

24 Nov 2019, 00:21 by Matt Lombardi

Ohio State took down Penn State on Saturday to clinch the Big Ten East, and it looks like the Justin Fields hype train got a new passenger in the process. Nittany Lions star defensive end Micah Parsons was apparently impressed by what he saw from the Buckeyes’ signal-caller.

Fields, who finished the game 16-of-22 for 188 yards with two touchdowns, took to Instagram after the game to celebrate the victory. Parsons commented on the post.

“Earned…I salute you…respect earned.”

Fields also added 68 yards on the ground. He was a problem for Penn State’s defense all day.

 

Parsons is a great player in his own right, and it’s likely that he winds up being a top NFL pick after his junior season.

Ohio State will face either Minnesota or Wisconsin in the Big Ten title game in two weeks. The Buckeyes have their rivalry game with Michigan next Saturday.

Penn State, meanwhile, will finish up its season against Rutgers. The Nittany Lions will likely finish the year with a 10-2 record.
