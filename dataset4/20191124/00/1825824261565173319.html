Game Thread: Raptors vs. Hawks updates, TV info, and more

24 Nov 2019 by Jacob M. Mack

The Raptors inch closer and closer to normalcy. They’ve endured a tough stretch of schedule despite injuries. Now they’ll endure an easy stretch of schedule with injuries, and soon, knock on wood, there won’t even be any injuries to endure at all.

Serge Ibaka, initially ruled out indefinitely following his ankle sprain way back at the start of the Raptors’ west coast road trip is listed as doubtful for tonight’s contest against the Hawks, but doubtful is better than out even if he’s for sure out tonight. With Ibaka ahead of schedule and Kyle Lowry due back at the start of December it’s looking like the Raptors may finally be due for a stretch of smooth sailing.
