Al Ahly give rest to Salah Mohsen & Ammar Hamdy

24 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

Al-Ahly announced the rest of the duo Salah Mohsen and Ammar Hamdi for three days.

The coach decided to rest Mohsen and Hamdi for three days after the end of the evening training on Saturday.

The duo participated in the winning of Egypt's Olympic team in the African Under-23 Cup over Cote d'Ivoire on Friday evening.

Therefore, the two players will miss El Gouna in the Egyptian league next Monday.

Salah Mohsen had participated in the practice normally, while Ramadan Sobhi and Ammar Hamdi underwent qualifying training.

Al Ahly will conclude their preparations to face El Gouna on Sunday before flying to the Red Sea province in the afternoon
