Clay Helton Has Message For USC Athletic Director Following Win

24 Nov 2019, 00:57 by Andrew Holleran

Few head coaches have been on a hotter seat this season than Clay Helton. Almost everyone has thought that the Trojans would part ways with Helton following the year. USC has been linked to Urban Meyer, James Franklin and others for months.

Is USC actually going to fire Helton, though?

We might be trending in the opposite direction of that right now.

USC topped UCLA tonight, 52-35, to improve to 8-4 on the season. The Trojans’ president actually congratulated the team on the win. You’re probably not going to fire your head coach right after publicly congratulating him.
