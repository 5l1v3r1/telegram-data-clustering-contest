The 'top 10' of teams with the most international titles

24 Nov 2019 by Cecilia

River lost in the final to Flamengo in their attempt to win a new Copa Libertadores, but the team already ranks among the ten clubs with the most international titles in the world.

10. River Plate (12 titles)

1 Intercontinental; 4 Libertadores; 3 Recopas Sudamericanas; 1 Copa Sudamericana; 1 Copa Interamericana; 1 Supercopa Sudamericana; 1 Copa Suruga Bank.

9. Sao Paulo (12 titles)

1 Club World Cup; 2 Intercontinentales; 3 Libertadores; 1 Copa Sudamericana; 2 Recopas Sudamericanas; 1 Supercopa Sudamericana; 1 CONMEBOL Cup; 1 CONMEBOL Master Cup. 

8. Zamalek (13 titles)

5 CAF Champions League, 1 CAF Confederations Cup; 3 African Supercups; 1 African Cup Winners' Cup); 2 Afro-Asian Club Championship; 1 Arab Club Championship. 

7. Liverpool (13 titles)

6 Champions League/European Cups; 3 Europa League/UEFA; 4 European Supercups. 

6. Barcelona (17 titles)

3 Club World Cups; 5 Champions League/European CUp; 5 Supercopas de Europa; 4 European Winner's Cup. No Inter-Cities Fairs Cup are counted, as they are not recognised by UEFA.
