Caitlyn Jenner's romance with James Haskell over after I'm A Celeb camp leadership row

The former England rugby hero has been chosen to lead the camp forward - but that may mean any love in the air between James and Caitlyn will have to wait!

24 Nov 2019, 00:36 by Sam Elliott, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sam-elliott/

Caitlyn Jenner has been teasing James Haskell about their blossoming romance but it could be all over before it's even begun!

The rugby star is the camp's first leader - and all his loyal subjects have been falling into line.

After the votes were counted, Roman Kemp revealed the results with James scoring five and was put at the helm of the jungle.

A round of applause went round and the jokes soon started.

James said “Thank you, that’s very kind of you all.”

Caitlyn said playfully: “You better be good or we’re going to fire you.”

James laughed, adding: “Don’t start with me, you won’t win.”

Caitlyn smirked: “I can’t, you’re the King.”

James walked into camp and everyone cheered “All hail the leader.” He later asked Jacqueline to join him by the creek.

James soon told everyone that Jacqueline was his new deputy and that they both were now exempt from all camp chores.

Caitlyn joked “Our romance is over.... you picked someone else.”

James laughed and said: “No, no, the leader always has time for all the women in his life, don’t worry about that.”

Meanwhile, Andrew Maxwell broke down in tears and threatened to walk out on Saturday's I'm A Celebrity.

The comedian confessed he had "hit a wall" as he became "overwhelmed" with jungle life.

Andrew said: "I honestly thought that I was gonna walk out, but I haven’t, when you’re tired, you just say ‘have I done the right thing’."

The camp-mates duties:

Chefs – Myles and Nadine

Washing Up – Caitlyn and Andrew

Cleaners – Cliff and Ian

Maintenance – Roman and Andy

Gas Pump – Kate and Adele
