Barcelona's half -time spat against Leganés

24 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

'As' reports there were discussions in the dressing room during the half time break, when Barcelona were losing 0-1.

Barcelona managed a win against Leganés but it wasn't without a fair share of hiccups and suffering. Valverde's men began losing 1-0 after a great goal from En-Nesyri, but things changed after the break. 

AS' reviews the half-time conversations that went on in the dressing room, with several groups forming. 

The source says Valverde tried to lift his team's spirits, alongside second-placed Jon Aspiazu. He invited them to push harder and press more in order to create chances. Additionally, there were also some who complained and seemed disapproving.

"What are we playing at," were the reported words from one of the team's heavyweights. The source highlights laments and complaints of some members of the first squad, while many opted for silence.

Apparently, the Frenchmen of the team, Griezmann, Umtiti and Dembele, discussed on their own in relation to the team's lack of cohesion in the first half.

Barcelona's second half was not much different, but a reported speech by Piqué and Luis Suárez in the tunnel. They scored early and made sure to beat Leganés despite their below-average performance.
