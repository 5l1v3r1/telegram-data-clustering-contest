All Women Should Join NPP’s 'Women On The Move' Conference At Takoradi- Matilda Asare

24 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The Agenda 1more4Nana urge all Ghanaian women whose wards are enjoying from the NPP’s free SHS to join the upcoming conference of the NPP Women’s wing at Takoradi.

We believe this will form part of the collective appreciation we could offer the NPP government for delivering on the free SHS promise.

The NPP government have been true to their words and have pleased our expectations. So it’s important we support the cause of the NPP at every given time.

The women’s conference is equally aimed at mobilizing votes for the NPP and the quest to retain President Akufo-Addo and the NPP beyond 2020. So it’s an important quest to partake.

The programme comes off at Takoradi on the 29th of November, 2019. And it is important that the Ghanaian should show gratitude to President Akufo-Addo by attending in their numbers.

As a group, there are a lot we could be appreciative of to the NPP government, in that order we have decided to grace the occasion in our numbers.

So, we appeal to all women to join the National Women’s Wing of the NPP as they bring women together from the length and breadth of the country to the women on the move conference.


Signed
Matilda Asare Agenda 1more4Nana
