Turns Out DJ Earworm Still Exists, And His Best Of The 2010s Mashup Kinda Slaps

24 Nov 2019, 00:41 by By Alasdair Duncan

The mashup craze may have well and truly come and gone, but it turns out that DJ Earworm is still out there, condensing all your favourite bangers into one giant banger.

The musician, otherwise known as Jordan Roseman, famously whittles entire years’ worth of hits down to a few choice hooks and choruses in his United States Of Pop mixes.

This week, he unveiled his Decade of Pop mashup, bringing together 100 songs from the 2010s, with a track list ranging from certified bops to a few that you probably forgot even existed.
