Ian Book Asked About His Future At Notre Dame Following Win

24 Nov 2019, 00:11 by Alek Arend

Notre Dame came a away with a big time 40-7 win over Boston College on Saturday. The Fighting Irish are still vying for a NY6 Bowl game selection. Saturday’s outcome should held ND move up in next week’s rankings.

The Fighting Irish now shift their attention to next Saturday’s contest against Stanford. But quarterback Ian Book is having to face a few more distractions. Saturday’s Boston College game was Notre Dame’s last home contest of the season. As a result after the game, a reporter asked Book if this was his last home game as a part of the Fighting Irish.

The Notre Dame quarterback had an interesting response: “We’ll see,” via Tyler James of ND Insider.
