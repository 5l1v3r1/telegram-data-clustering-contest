Two dead in Bay of Plenty crash

24 Nov 2019

Two people are dead after a crash near Opotiki in the Bay of Plenty this morning.

The two-vehicle crash happened at 10.10am on State Highway 2, Waiotahi Beach Rd.

Police confirmed two people died in the crash and four others were injured.

The Serious Crash Unit is attending and the road is expected to be closed for several hours.
