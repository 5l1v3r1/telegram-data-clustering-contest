Top Nigerian Newspaper Headlines For Today, Sunday, 24th November, 2019

24 Nov 2019 by Philips Sunday

Good morning, Nigeria, welcome to Naija News roundup of top Newspaper Headlines in Nigeria for today Sunday, 24th November 2019.

Here Are The Major Nigerian Newspaper Headlines.

1. President Buhari ‘Storms’ Out Of APC NEC Meeting

President Muhammadu Buhari on Friday walked out of the All Progressives Congress (APC) National Executive Committee (NEC) meeting. Naija News reports that the NEC meeting was held at the national secretariat of the APC in Abuja.

2. Herdsmen Kill Many In Latest Adamawa Attack

Suspected Fulani herdsmen have killed an unspecified number of people in fresh attack on a village in Adamawa State. Naija News reports that the gunmen attacked Shuws-Kala’a village in Hong LGA on Saturday.

3. US Charge Air Peace Boss, Allen Onyema With $20m Fraud

The Chairman of Air Peace, Allen Onyema, has been charged with bank fraud and money laundering for moving more than $20 million from Nigeria through United States bank accounts in a scheme involving false documents based on the purchase of aeroplanes. In a statement posted on its website on Friday, the US State Department of Justice said Onyema’s Chief of Administration and Finance, Ejiroghene Eghagha, has also been charged with bank fraud and committing aggravated identity theft in connection with the scheme.

4. Air Peace Boss Reacts To US Money Laundering Indictment

The Chairman of Air Peace, Allen Onyema, has dismissed reports of his indictment for financial scam, fraud and money laundering by the United States Government. Naija News reports that Onyema was charged with bank fraud and money laundering for moving more than $20 million from Nigeria through United States bank accounts in a scheme involving false documents based on the purchase of aeroplanes.

5. Allen Onyema: Fani-Kayode Speaks On Air Peace Boss Fraud Indictment

Former Minister of Aviation Femi Fani-Kayode has said the Chairman of Air Peace, Allen Onyema, is innocent until proven guilty. Naija News reports that Onyema was charged with bank fraud and money laundering for moving more than $20 million from Nigeria through United States bank accounts in a scheme involving false documents based on the purchase of aeroplanes.

6. Why Buhari, APC Governors, Others Are Meeting In Plateau

President Muhammadu Buhari will meet with All Progressives Congress (APC)Governors to discuss economic diversification, anti-corruption and nation’s security. Naija News reports that the parley is scheduled to hold on Sunday, 24th to Monday, 25th November 2019.

7. Adeyemi vs Dino Melaye: APC Kogi West Candidate Sends Message To Supporters

Senator Smart Adeyemi has urged members of the All Progressives Congress (APC) to work for the victory of the party in next Saturday’s Kogi West supplementary poll. Naija News reports that the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has fixed Saturday, November 30, for the conduct of the Kogi West supplementary rerun election.

8. Dino Melaye Will Lose Like Saraki, Shehu Sani, Others – Oshiomhole

The National Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Adams Oshiomhole, has mocked Senator Dino Melaye, saying he won’t return to the Nigerian Senate. Oshiomhole made this known on Friday while speaking at the National Executive Council (NEC) meeting of the APC, chaired by President Muhammadu Buhari.

9. Zamfara: Why Yari May Be Arrested – Matawalle

Governor Bello Matawalle of Zamfara Statehas said he could order the arrest of his predecessor Abdulaziz Yari if killings continue in his state. The governor made the accusation at government house, Gusau, while receiving some firearms that some “repentant bandits” surrendered, the Punch reports.

10. EPL: Arsenal Need Intelligent English Players – Pires

Former Arsenal attacker, Robert Pires, has called on manager Unai Emery to sign intelligent English players to help the team. Naija News reports that Arsenal are eight points off Champions League places after 12 fixtures, with only four wins on their record to date,
