Mane, Salah and Van Dijk, Hazard's favourites for the Ballon d'Or

24 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Belgian, Eden Hazard, revealed (two days before Real Madrid take on PSG in the Champions League) who are his favourite footballers that deserve to win the Ballon d'Or this year. For him, any Liverpool player deserves it a lot more than Messi.

Hazard also demanded the return of his team-mate Karim Benzema to the French national team. "I can only judge him by his performances, the rest is the French's problem," he started saying in this respect.

"It is not normal that they haven't named Karim in the squad because he is the best striker at the moment. He scores, he plays at the best club in the world. France is already strong without Karim but with him they would be even stronger".

Hazard would not give the Ballon d'Or to Barcelona player, Leo Messi because he thinks that "a Liverpool player" deserves it. His candidates would be Mane, Salah and Van Dijk.

"If Sadio had won the African Cup it wouldn't be a debate and Salah is also my friend", Eden Hazard explained, in a long interview with 'Le Parisien', in which he also spoke for a long time about Kylian Mbappe, who for him, could also be a future Ballon d'Or winner.
