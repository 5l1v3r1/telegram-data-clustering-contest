BREAKING: Mia Yim attacked before TakeOver: WarGames; Replaced in WarGames match

24 Nov 2019, 00:08 by Kevin C. Sullivan

It seems that getting attacked before NXT programming is becoming a thing these days.

The NXT TakeOver: WarGames pre-show opened up tonight with the news that member of Rhea Ripley's WarGames team, Mia Yim, was attacked backstage. Footage was shown of medical personnel checking on Yim, with Ripley in the background - and she didn't look happy.
