Genoa - On 14 August 2018 the collapse of the Morandi bridge in Genoa caused the death of 43 people.

24 Nov 2019

Genoa - The “Madonna del Monte” highway viaduct collapsed on the A6 Torino-Savona motorway in northern Italy. The section between the junction of the A6 and the A10 has been closed. The exact point of the accident is located between the port city of Savona, near Genoa, and the town of Altare, in the direction of Turin. Ongoing checks on the stability of the other section of the bridge. On 14 August 2018 the collapse of the Morandi bridge in Genoa caused the death of 43 people.
