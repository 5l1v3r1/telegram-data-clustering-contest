De Fire flags new record label, signs two artistes - The Nation Newspaper

24 Nov 2019, 00:53

Poised to help upcoming acts realize their potentials, newly launched record label, Basic Entertainment, has made a debut to the Nigerian entertainment scene with two artistes, Eze Emmanuel simply known as Skul Feez and Ogbonnaya Kennedy Chukwudi aka Elgo Veen.

Speaking at the unveiling in Lagos, the CEO of Basic Entertainment, Ugbuefi Udochukwu aka De Fire revealed that the two artistes were selected based on their talents, versatility, and creativity.

De Fire boasted that his signees will take over the music industry with their uniqueness.

He continued: “Our vision is to hunt for young talents and introduce them to the world. Basic Entertainment is here to stay and we will be focusing on promoting, marketing and management of musical talents across Nigeria. We want to change the narrative of the record labels in Nigeria for better.”

The newly signed artistes have recorded many songs featuring some of the rave-making musicians including DMW act, B-Red, Zoro among others. The singles and videos will be release soon.

Both artistes, hail from Anambra state, assured that we will attain great height by the time drop their songs. They also expressed gratitude to almighty God and the record management for the opportunity given to them.
