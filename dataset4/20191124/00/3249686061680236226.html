BLACKPINK's dance practice video for 'Kill This Love' hits 200M views on YouTube

24 Nov 2019, 00:01 by olmal

BLACKPINK's dance practice video for 'Kill This Love' has hit 200M views on YouTube. The dance practice video was released on April 8. The group's M/V for the song has over 658M views.
