Portway Infant School says thank you to 'Mr Geoff'

Geoff Barker has been a volunteer at Portway for 25 years

24 Nov 2019 by Jane McFarlane

Portway Infant school in Allestree celebrated a very special volunteer at the school.

Geoff Barker has been volunteering twice a week at the school for 25 years!

Mr Barker, who the children and staff fondly refer to as ‘Mr Geoff’ has become a well-loved, friendly and hardworking volunteer at the school and the children and staff alike look forward to him coming in each week.

Mr Geoff listens to readers regularly but also comes in to talk to the children about topic work , the children loved hearing about his life as a submariner and about his thoughts during the moon landing!

Three of the governing body, staff and children presented Mr Geoff with a card and present and thanked him for his service.
