La Rochelle Player Sent Off For Horrific Eye Gouge On Sale's Tom Curry

24 Nov 2019 by Gary Connaughton

It has been a middling Champions Cup for French teams over the first couple of weeks of the tournament, with a number of disappointing results. Clermont Auvergne, Lyon, and Montepellier have all lost games already, with La Rochelle adding another defeat to that list this afternoon.

They would fall to a 25-15 defeat in Sale with the English side coming away rather convincing winners.

La Rochelle's cause certainly was not helped by the fact they had to play all of the second half with only 14 men after Pierre Bourgarit was given his marching orders just before halftime.

The hooker performed a sickening eye gouge on World Cup star Tom Curry, leaving the referee with no choice but to produce the red card. Coach Ronan O'Gara certainly didn't seemed pleased.
