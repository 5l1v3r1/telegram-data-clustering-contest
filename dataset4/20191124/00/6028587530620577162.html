The Best Alternative Advent Calendars Available

24 Nov 2019 by Paul Armstrong

Chocolate isn’t exactly a health food and yet as this time of year we eat tons of the stuff. Advent calendars are big part of this, and big business for food brands, but they aren’t the only game in town. From biohacking to movie buffs, here is a selection of some of the best and quirkiest advent calendars to ‘self-gift’ or buy for loved ones. For the health nut - The Pro-Ho-Ho-Ho-Tein Calendar is the GMO, gluten, soy-free must-have for anyone who wants to keep going and not just drink or feast on chocolate. Snack strong! ($25.71/theproteinball.co)

For the movie buff - Joseph’s have got you covered with +10 varieties of their gourmet popcorn. From salted caramel to the more unusual raspberry cheesecake, this calendar is perfect for the Apple+/Amazon Prime Video/Netflix addict in your life. ($32.08/josephs.co.uk)

For the beauty expert - Beauty calendars have exploded this year and Amazon’s has gotten the most inches so far. Stocked full of items worth over $250, it’s a huge present for anyone. The John Lewis Beauty advent calendar is another beauty calendar that’s getting rave reviews ($58.16/amazon.com).

For the tech head - Revell has created a build your own remote-controlled helicopter advent calendar. Educational, fun and teaching patience (!), kids of all ages can enjoy seeing this come to life. ($42.33/amazon.com)

For the sci-fi nut - Funko created the ultimate sci-fi calendar with 25 mini figurines to collect and play with throughout December and beyond. From Venom, Captain Marvel, the gang is all here and super cute. Perfect for children young (and not so young!). ($46.30/amazon.com)

For the young (or young at heart) - LEGO isn’t just for kids anymore and their range of advent calendars demonstrates this. From Star Wars to LEGO City, Harry Potter to Friends, LEGO gets surprise and also storytelling but the STEM angle isn’t far behind either. ($various/lego.com)
