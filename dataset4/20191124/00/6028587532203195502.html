Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Most Beautifully Packaged Gift Sets

24 Nov 2019 by Natasha Wolff

From advent calendars featuring the best in beauty to stationary, condiments, flowers, board games and linens, there's a specially packaged gift set or box for everyone on your list this season.

Nécessaire The Body Essentials in Eucalyptus, $60

New Los Angeles-based clean, sustainable beauty company Nécessaire has cornered the market on body and self-care products. Founded by beauty and fashion editor Nick Axelrod and former Estée Lauder executive Randi Christiansen, the duo has debuted chic and luxurious gift sets in eucalyptus, sandalwood and no scent at all.

Charlotte Tilbury’s Glittering Galaxy of Make-up Magic Advent Calendar, $200

Featuring several full-sized palettes for eyes and lips along with mini versions of its signature skincare products and mascara, Charlotte Tilbury’s celestial-themed advent calendar is out of this world.

Gro-To Little Squirts New Mum gift set, $85

Clean, Aussie skincare brand Go-To’s new baby line features the same great quality in gentle formulations for little tykes. From bubble bath to calming body lotion and luxurious baby oil, this gift is perfect for the new mom in your life.

Maison Christian Dior Advent Calendar, $520

French illustrator Vincent Darré has created a wonderland inside 30 Avenue Montaigne, the Dior atelier in Paris, featuring 24 special candles, scents and lotions inside every vignette.

Il Buco Alimentari Olive Oil Gift Trio, $110

Downtown New York City restaurant and marketplace Il Buco Alimentaria is known for its Italian specialties but for the holidays, its extra virgin olive oil trio features rich varietals from Sicily and Umbria.

Chantecaille Wild Pairs Cheek and Lip Duo, $88

Chantecaille deepens its commitment to philanthropy by offering make-up sets whose proceeds benefit the organizations Space for Giants, which protects endangered elephants in Africa, and The Xerces Society, which conserves bees. This one features a coral blush and soft pink lipstick.

Corpus The Minis Box Set, $56

This on-the-go mini deodorant box set features six natural scents like Cedar Flora and Third Rose which makes packing your toiletries less of a chore.

Milk Makeup MVPs Set, $39

Mini versions of the brand’s best-selling mascara, primer, under-eye de-puffing stick, serum, highlighter, bronzer and lip and cheek glimmer in a clear make-up bag.

Grown Alchemist Holiday Intensive Body Hydration Kit, $59

Australia’s cult organic skincare brand Grown Alchemist gets a silver makeover making for a glamorous, metallic moment under the tree. Its Intensive hand and body lotions will keep dry, flaky winter skin at bay.

La Prairie White Caviar Illuminating Ritual Set, $1,710

Showcasing four of La Prairie’s hero White Caviar products—Illuminating Pearl Infusion, Eye Serum and Eye Cream and Crème Extraordinaire—this ritual is luxury to the max. Perfect for any tired moms on your list who need some glow.

Hill House Home Bathe Me Kit, $100

New York City-based direct-to-consumer bedding and linen brand Hill House Home, founded by Nell Diamond, has just launched a kids collection. We love this rainbow-edged bath robe, hooded towel and washcloth combo.

Tom Ford Noir Extreme Eau de Parfum Gift Set, $180

Tom Ford’s sexy amber and woody oriental fragrance Noir Extreme is unisex and but not for the faint of heart. This disco-glam set features the body spray and eau de parfum.

Dr. Barbara Sturm Advent Calendar, $495

Dr. Barbara Sturm’s German-engineered, science-based skincare line (beloved by Gwyneth Paltrow and Kate Moss) has unveiled its advent calendar and inside each of the 24 windows are full-sized and 22 deluxe-sized product offerings.

Dr. Jart+ Holiday Experiment Kits, $25-$50

Korean skincare brand Dr. Jart+ has just launched four, targeted experiment kits each featuring formulas to treat any skin issue you may have from dryness to redness. It’s a great way to check out the brand if you haven’t yet.

Omorovicza holiday gift sets, $40-$595

This Hungarian skincare brand harnesses the minerals of the country’s famed thermal waters for its indulgent products that promise to smooth and decongest the complexion and diminish the appearance of fine lines. This season, they’re offering sets for every skin type and price point.

LoveShackFancy holiday crackers, $45 for four

Rebecca Hessel Cohen’s newest collection features her signature, bohemian LoveShackFancy florals on table linens and, for the holidays, she’s launched Christmas crackers for a festive tabletop accent. The line is available in store and online in early December.

Jo Malone London English Pear & Freesia Collection set, $125

This delicate and mellow yet ripe and woody fragrance from Jo Malone London now comes in a verdant holiday box sex featuring a cologne, body and hand wash and a body cream.

Dear Annabelle stationary gift set, $80 for ten cards and envelopes

This frosty themed stationary set from Dear Annabelle, a new New York City-based paper goods company started by Marcie Pantzer, features wintry imagery like snowflakes, hats, skis, pine trees and snowmen on its chic notecards and lined envelopes. Perfect to use to write your loved ones an appreciative message.

Fresh 6 Days of Surprises Gift Set, $99

Six days of skincare surprises include Fresh’s iconic Rose Face Mask and Soy Face Cleanser products.

Caswell-Massey Master Collection Year of Soap, $98

Twelve triple-milled soaps—one for each month—from America’s original apothecary and perfumery, Rhode Island-based Caswell-Massey.

Diptyque Advent Calendar, $425

Optimistic symbols like ladybugs and maneki-nekoes will have you ending 2019 on a high note. Diptyque’s advent calendar features 25 products and lucky charms that will delight each day.

AERIN Shagreen Card Case, $425

A deck of cards has never looked this good. Aerin Lauder’s shagreen and brass card case holds two decks of playing cards and will keep you entertained all season.

Venus ET Fleur 12 Days of Roses, $249

New York’s first rose retailer Venus ET Fleur’s 12 Days of Roses gift set is filled with 12 spray roses and is lined in velvet for an elegant and unique gift idea.

Molton Brown Advent Calendar, $250

Twenty-four treats to celebrate the month of December including British fragrance house Molton Brown’s distinctive body, hair and home products.
