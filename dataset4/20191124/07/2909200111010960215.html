Ghanaians are fools – Shatta Wale

24 Nov 2019, 07:21 by MyNewsGH

Self-acclaimed Dancehall King, Shatta Wale born Charles Nii Armah Mensah insists Ghanaians are fools though there is some level of peace in the country

The artiste after his GRAMMY AWARD nomination has been sending jabs to people who say he has not earned a nomination although the album he featured on with Beyonce was nominated.

In series of tweets, the “Level” hit maker mentioned that although Ghana is known to be peaceful, all citizens in there are fools and that has dragged down the country and its development.

He said “This country is very peaceful but the people for inside fool too much and after they will be blaming the government for their foolishness !! #3tw3fuor”
