Josh Richardson stars as the Philadelphia 76ers make Miami feel the heat

The Lakers beat the Grizzlies in a tight contest and the Bucks were too good for the Pistons.

24 Nov 2019, 07:25

Josh Richardson scored a season-high 32 points to help the Philadelphia 76ers to a 113-86 victory against his former team the Miami Heat.

Richardson sunk six three-pointers as the Heat were left reeling in a defeat which sees them slip down to third in the Eastern Conference.

In a game that took the 76ers’ winning streak to four, Joel Embiid added 23 points and 11 rebounds, Tobias Harris scored 19 and Al Horford got 16.

Tyler Herro top-scored for the Heat with 20 points and Goran Dragic had 18.
