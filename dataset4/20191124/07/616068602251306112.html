Mel B to pay $1.8 million - Entertainment News

24 Nov 2019, 07:08 by WENN

Mel B will pay $1.8 million to end a defamation spat with a former nanny.

Lorraine Gilles, 27, filed a suit against Mel in 2017, claiming the Spice Girls star defamed her by implying she was a "prostitute" who fell pregnant with her ex-husband, Stephen Belafonte's baby.

The out-of-court settlement means the matter will not go to trial and private details about Mel's marriage will not be discussed.

A source tells The Mirror Mel B and Gilles have agreed to "go their separate ways and not bad mouth each other," adding, "Mel was just happy to clear this mess up once and for all.

"Things were said in court papers in the heat of a divorce, which exploded into this lawsuit. But in recent weeks their frost has thawed, with them even meeting in the same room to work out their differences."
