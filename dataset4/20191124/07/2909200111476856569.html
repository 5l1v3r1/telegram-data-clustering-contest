GRAMMY AWARDS: Yvonne Nelson blast media for hating on Shatta Wale – Yvonne Nelson

24 Nov 2019, 07:03 by MyNewsGH

Actress, Yvonne Nelson says the hate in the entertainment industry is sickening and worrying.

This comes on the back of some media personalities insisting Shatta Wale is not a GRAMMY AWARD nominee after Beyonce’s “Lion King” which featured Shatta Wale got nominated.

Reacting to the discussions ongoing, she noted that it’s sad Ghanaians do not like to see their compatriots succeed in what they do.

She said “The HATE in this industry SHATTA WALE has put GH on the map!!! Y’all should be happy for him. I cant believe even radio discussions are trying to discredit him, what at all should one do?? Ghana is proud of you SHATTA! Congrats”.
