Bates 'grateful' to be alive - Entertainment News

24 Nov 2019, 07:03 by WENN

Kathy Bates is feeling "grateful" she's still alive after the actress fought a number of health scares.

Ahead of Thanksgiving, the star spoke with U.S. TV show Extra at the 2019 American Film Institute Fest world premiere of her newest film Richard Jewell, where she confessed she'll be celebrating her life over the holidays.

"I'm grateful that I'm still alive and I'm grateful to be supporting millions of Americans who are suffering with lymphedema and to use my platform to draw attention and hopefully very soon we'll find a cure," the American Horror Story actress emotionally shared.

In addition to suffering from lymphedema - swelling that generally occurs in one of your arms or legs - she is also a two-time cancer survivor.

Her new movie, Richard Jewell, about the eponymous security guard who was initially celebrated as a hero for saving lives in the bombing of the 1996 Summer Olympics, then vilified when the press reported he was a suspect, opens in theaters 13 December.
