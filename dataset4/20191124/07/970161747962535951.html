Sheffield Utd vs Man Utd time, TV channel, live stream, team news and odds

24 Nov 2019, 07:51

The only Premier League game of the day sees Manchester United travel to South Yorkshire to take on Sheffield United at Bramall Lane.

The Blades have been the surprise package of the season so far, sitting eighth in the table going into this game, with three points taking them fifth.

The Red Devils are only one point behind their opponents and can also go fifth with a victory, although they have only won four of their 12 league games so far this campaign.

Man Utd won their last Premier League outing, at home to Brighton, but have not won back-to-back league matches since the beginning of March.

What time is Sheffield United vs Manchester United?

The match kicks off at 4.30pm on Sunday 24 November at Bramall Lane.

Team news

John Egan is likely to miss the game for the Blades, while Dean Henderson is ineligible because he is on loan from Manchester United.

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer will be without Scott McTominay, Paul Pogba, Nemanja Matic, Eric Bailly, Marcos Rojo, Diogo Dalot and Luke Shaw.
