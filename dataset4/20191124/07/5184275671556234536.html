Mann Mein Shiva From Panipat On ‘How’s The Hype?’: BLOCKBUSTER Or Lackluster?

Set in 1761, Panipat is about the Maratha Empire which had reached its zenith and their grip on Hindustan reigned supreme with no-one to challenge them.

24 Nov 2019, 07:38 by Koimoi.com Team

Arjun Kapoor, Kriti Sanon, Sanjay Dutt and the team of Panipat is geared up for the release of their film as it is just a few days away from its release. The makers are leaving no stone unturned in keeping the buzz on as they released a new song titled Mann Mein Shiva and it has made it to our How’s The Hype section.

Composed by singer and composer duo Ajay-Atul, the songs is sung by Kunal Ganjawala, Deepanshi Nagar and Padmanabh Gaikwad. The song is a slow number penned by Javed Akhtar and is a boosting devotional number. The song features an ensemble that has Arjun, Kriti and Girish Mahajani in the highlight. With a simple choreography the song is fun watch and gives the period vibe nicely. A grand set and men and women clad in era appropriate outfits are the high point.
