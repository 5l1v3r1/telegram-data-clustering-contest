Daisy Ridley, Noah Cyrus, Tom Hiddleston, And Bernie Sanders Are Coming To The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon Thanksgiving Week

24 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Charisse Van Horn

It’s Thanksgiving week and that means specials are coming to television. Those who watch The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon will find some reruns will play towards the end of the week, but there are plenty of guest stars coming pre-Thanksgiving. Jimmy Fallon sat with John Legend on Friday and the EGOT winner also performed on the show as the musical guest. Coming up Monday, Jimmy Fallon will have Star Wars star Daisy Ridley, Marvel’s Loki Tom Hiddleston and musical guest Noah Cyrus and Leon Bridges.

Star Wars’ fans are hoping Daisy will dish on her character Rey and the upcoming movie Star Wars Episode IX: Rise of the Skywalkers.

Tom Hiddleston plays Loki in Marvel’s Avengers and Thor films and he’ll be starring in a new series titled Loki that’s coming exclusively to Disney Plus in 2021. He’s currently working on the show and fans can’t wait until it airs.

Tom is also working on the upcoming Disney Plus series What If…? where he will appear as Loki.
Undoubtedly, Monday is going to be a fan-favorite episode as Jimmy Fallon speaks to both Daisy and Tom who have enormous fan bases.

Noah Cyrus and Leon Bridges released an acoustic version of her hit song “July” that has gone viral and fans are thrilled the two will appear on the show as Monday’s musical guest.

On Tuesday, Jimmy Fallon welcomes presidential hopeful Senator Bernie Sanders, Katherine Langford and Gary Vaynerchuk.

Known for the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why, Katherine is currently filming an animated series for the streaming media giant that she stars in: Cursed.

Gary Vaynerchuck is an American entrepreneur and a social media personality with a huge following. You may hear Noah Cyrus featuring Leon Bridges perform the song “July” in the video player below.
