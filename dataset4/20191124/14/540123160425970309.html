A Non-Jewish Brooklynite Visits Hasidic Crown Heights

24 Nov 2019, 14:32 by Video Manager

A gentile who lives a few blocks from a Hasidic Jewish community in Crown Heights, Brooklyn that has unfortunately been the target of a rise in hate crimes over the past year. His friend Rabbi Yoni Katz shows me around his community as we talk about finding one’s purpose and fighting hate by getting to know each other.
