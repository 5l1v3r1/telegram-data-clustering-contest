PM Johnson says: I haven't lost my 'tax-cutting zeal'

24 Nov 2019, 14:49 by Reuters

TELFORD — Prime Minister Boris Johnson said on Sunday he had not lost his tax cutting zeal but that Britain needed to maintain fiscal discipline.

“We are maintaining fiscal discipline and we will keep debt coming down,” Johnson said. “We are doing this sensibly.”

“I haven’t lost any of my tax-cutting zeal,” Johnson said. (Reporting by William James and Kylie MacLellan; writing by Guy Faulconbridge, editing by Elizabeth Piper)
