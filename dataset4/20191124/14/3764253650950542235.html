Nollywood actor Theodore Anyanji is dead

24 Nov 2019, 14:53

- Nigerian actor and movie director Theodore Anyanji has reportedly passed away

- The actor's death was announced on social media by his colleagues and friends

- Friends and colleagues of the deceased actor took to social media to pay tribute to him

The Nollywood industry has tragically lost another member. It was recently reported that actor and movie director Theodore Anyanji passed away.

Legit.ng came across the sad news after colleagues and friends of the late Nollywood legend took to social media to pay respect to him.

According to Information Nigeria, Anyanji passed away on Saturday, November 23, at a prayer healing church in Festac, Lagos state. He was said to have been battling heart and kidney problems before he passed away. It was also revealed that he has been taken to Umuaka, Imo state for burial.
