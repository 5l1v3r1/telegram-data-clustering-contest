Photo: Joe Burrow Had A Special Guest At LSU’s Game Last Night

24 Nov 2019, 14:25 by Andrew Holleran

Joe Burrow and the LSU Tigers continued their perfect season on Saturday night, dominating Arkansas at Tiger Stadium, 56-20, to move to 11-0 on the season. Burrow was his usual great self, throwing for 327 yards and three touchdowns on 23 of 28 passing.

The former Ohio State quarterback, who transferred to LSU two years ago, had a special guest in attendance on Saturday night.

J.T. Barrett, who played with Burrow at Ohio State, was at Tiger Stadium supporting his former teammate. He was at the game with another former Buckeyes quarterback, Stephen Collier.

The former Ohio State star QB was wearing a Burrow No. 9 LSU jersey.
