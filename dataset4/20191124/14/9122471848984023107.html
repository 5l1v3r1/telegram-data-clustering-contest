Herm Edwards’ Postgame Interview Went Viral After Win vs. Oregon

24 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Andrew Holleran

Herm Edwards was fired up following Arizona State’s big upset win over Oregon on Saturday night. He had a right to be, after all, as his Sun Devils had just secured one of the most-surprising wins of the 2019 college football season.

Arizona State dominated the game from the start and did not let up, beating Oregon, 31-28, to guarantee a bowl berth at 6-5 on the year. The Sun Devils shook up the College Football Playoff race, too, knocking the Ducks out.

Edwards was interviewed on the field by ESPN’s Maria Taylor following the game.

The interview has quickly gone viral.

“We made it good for television,” the former ESPN employee told Taylor. “So even though you came to cover a 5-5 team, we made it good for television.”
