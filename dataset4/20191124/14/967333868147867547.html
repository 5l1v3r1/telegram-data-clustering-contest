Twitter Users Heartbroken as K-Pop Star Goo Hara Found Dead at Her Home

The singer was a former member of the popular girl band Kara and also starred in television dramas. She was 28 years old.

24 Nov 2019, 14:22

K-pop singer and actress Goo Hara was found dead at her home in the Cheongdam neighbourhood of Seoul on Saturday evening, according to the Gangnam Police Department.

"We received a report on 24 November, 6:30 pm KST about a death", the police said in a statement. "We are currently investigating the cause".

A day before her demise, she made a post in Instagram captioned "Good night".

The singer earlier attempted suicide at her home in May. She was found unconscious by her manager and taken to a hospital. She later apologised to fans for trying to take her own life.

The singer's fans, devastated by the loss, rushed to Twitter to mourn and express their condolences after receiving the news of their beloved star's death.
