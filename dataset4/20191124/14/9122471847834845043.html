ESPN’s Kirk Herbstreit Releases His New Top 6

24 Nov 2019, 14:31 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit has updated his personal rankings following Week 13 of the 2019 regular season. There’s been a bit of a shakeup.

The most-notable result of Week 13 came late on Saturday night, when No. 6 Oregon was upset by Arizona State. The Ducks are now out of the College Football Playoff race.

LSU, Ohio State and Clemson have a strong hold on the playoff’s top three spots, but the No. 4 spot is pretty wide open. Oregon’s loss could open the door for a team like Alabama or Oklahoma.

Herbstreit has updated his rankings following Saturday night:

  1. LSU
  2. Ohio State
  3. Clemson
  4. Georgia
  5. Alabama
  6. Utah
