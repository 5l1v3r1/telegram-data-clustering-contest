Blast in Kabul Leaves One Foreign National Dead

The Ministry of Interior confirmed that the target of the attack was a UN vehicle.

24 Nov 2019, 14:55 by TOLOnews.com

A hand grenade attack occurred in Kabul’s police district 9 (PD9) near Makrorayan area on Sunday evening, targeting a United Nations Development Program (UNDP) vehicle, the Ministry of Interior confirmed.

A spokesman for the ministry, Nusrat Rahimi, said five people were wounded and one foreign national was killed.

Eyewitnesses said the number of casualties was higher than reported by the ministry.

The UN mission in Afghanistan, UNAMA, in a statement confirmed that an employee of the organization was killed and two others were injured in the attack in Kabul this evening.
