Who is in the final of X Factor: Celebrity and when is it?

24 Nov 2019, 14:30

X Factor: Celebrity has been keeping fans of the show gripped since October after a bunch of famous faces took to the stage for this spin-off of the main series.

And after several weeks of great performances, shock exits and some seriously emotional celebrities, we finally know the four who’ll be battling it out in the final.

Like regular X Factor, there’s a lot at stake, with a record contract awaiting the winning act – but who has made it to the final and who’s the favourite to take the title?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who is in the final of X Factor: Celebrity?

These are the four acts who have made it through to the final:

Megan McKenna

Odds of winning: 1/3

Megan McKenna is heading into the final as the hot favourite following a string of impressive performances – potentially handing victory to Louis Walsh as the last remaining act in his Unders category. ‘Megan’s home and hosed as far as the odds are concerned,’ said Ladbrokes Jessica O’Reilly of the reality star’s chances.

Max and Harvey

Odds of winning: 5/1

Social media stars Max and Harvey are second favourites for the title – and could well emerge victorious after impressing the panel and audience with performances of such songs as One Direction’s Kiss You and Panic! At The Disco’s High Hopes

Jenny Ryan

Odds of winning: 6/1

The Chase’s Jenny Ryan – aka The Vixen – almost didn’t make the live shows but was added in by Nicole Scherzinger at the last minute. Which turned out to be a good decision as she’s now in the final and third favourite to win it following epic performances of songs including Rise Like A Phoenix and Skyfall.

V5

Odds of winning: 16/1

Girl group V5 – the second of Simon Cowell’s groups to make it through – won their place in the final after winning Saturday night’s sing-off against Try Star. The girls – social media stars Alondra Martinez, Renata Vaca, Sofia Oliveira, Wendii Sarmiento, and Laura Buitrago – are currently the outsiders for the series title.

When is the final of X Factor: Celebrity?

The final airs next Saturday night on ITV from 7.10pm.
