Ambati Rayudu advises HCA President Azharuddin to isolate himself from seasoned crooks

Ambati Rayudu had broken his Twitter silence on Saturday urging Telangana's Industry and Municipal Administration Minister, KT Rama Rao to look into the "rampant corruption prevailing in HCA."

24 Nov 2019, 14:58 by Sports Desk

Ambati Rayudu, in his follow-up to alleging corruption in Hyderabad Cricket Association (HCA), advised its president Mohammad Azharuddin on Sunday to not make it a personal tussle with him and isolate himself from “seasoned crooks”.
