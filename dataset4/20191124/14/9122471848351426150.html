ESPN’s Paul Finebaum Releases His New Top 6

24 Nov 2019, 14:59 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN college football analyst Paul Finebaum has updated his personal rankings following Week 13 of the 2019 regular season.

The ESPN analyst has an SEC-heavy top five following Saturday’s slate of games. The only truly notable results were Ohio State’s big home win over Penn State and Oregon’s surprising upset loss to Arizona State.

The race for the No. 4 spot in the College Football Playoff is getting more interesting by the day now that the Ducks are out. Could Alabama actually sneak back in? It’s very possible.

Here’s Finebaum’s latest top six. He’s got Alabama up there pretty high…

  1. LSU
  2. Ohio State
  3. Clemson
  4. Georgia
  5. Alabama
  6. Utah
