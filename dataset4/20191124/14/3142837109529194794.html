Brokerages Set Pearson plc (LON:PSON) Price Target at GBX 807

24 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Doug Wharley

Pearson plc (LON:PSON) has been assigned a consensus rating of “Hold” from the eleven ratings firms that are currently covering the firm, MarketBeat reports. Five investment analysts have rated the stock with a sell rating, three have given a hold rating and three have issued a buy rating on the company. The average twelve-month price objective among analysts that have issued a report on the stock in the last year is GBX 804.50 ($10.51).

A number of equities analysts have recently weighed in on the company. Shore Capital downgraded Pearson to a “hold” rating in a report on Friday, October 4th. Barclays boosted their price target on Pearson from GBX 790 ($10.32) to GBX 795 ($10.39) and gave the company an “underweight” rating in a research note on Tuesday, July 30th. Citigroup boosted their price target on Pearson from GBX 975 ($12.74) to GBX 1,050 ($13.72) and gave the company a “buy” rating in a research note on Tuesday, July 30th. JPMorgan Chase & Co. set a GBX 1,115 ($14.57) price target on Pearson and gave the company a “buy” rating in a research note on Wednesday, August 28th. Finally, Goldman Sachs Group boosted their price target on Pearson from GBX 683 ($8.92) to GBX 696 ($9.09) and gave the company a “sell” rating in a research note on Tuesday, August 6th.

Shares of PSON traded up GBX 11 ($0.14) during trading hours on Tuesday, reaching GBX 651 ($8.51). The company’s stock had a trading volume of 2,401,346 shares, compared to its average volume of 4,520,000. The firm has a market cap of $5.09 billion and a price-to-earnings ratio of 11.30. Pearson has a 1-year low of GBX 6.87 ($0.09) and a 1-year high of GBX 1,030 ($13.46). The company has a quick ratio of 1.20, a current ratio of 1.95 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 48.18. The company has a 50 day moving average of GBX 687.52 and a two-hundred day moving average of GBX 791.30.

In related news, insider Graeme Pitkethly bought 285 shares of the firm’s stock in a transaction on Monday, September 30th. The stock was purchased at an average price of GBX 736 ($9.62) per share, with a total value of £2,097.60 ($2,740.89).

About Pearson

Pearson plc provides educational products and services to institutions, governments, professional bodies, and individual learners worldwide. The company operates through North America, Core, and Growth segments. It offers courseware services, including curriculum materials provided in book form and/or through access to digital content; and assessments, such as test development, processing, and scoring services.
