Children among 13 migrants rescued from boat in English Channel

24 Nov 2019, 14:52

Thirteen people have been rescued from a small boat crossing the English Channel today.

Children were among those believed to be migrants who were met near Folkestone in Kent.

Border Force and HM Coastguard were alerted to the boat at around 9.41am.

They were deployed and met the boat off the coast, where those on board were brought ashore.

The people rescued are in the process of being transferred to the Kent Intake Unit.

An intake unit is where people can make a claim for asylum, and will be medically checked and questioned.
