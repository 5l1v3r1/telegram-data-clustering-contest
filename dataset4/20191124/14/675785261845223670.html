Cody Simpson wishes Miley Cyrus a happy birthday with cosy video amid split rumours

The Australian singer squashed all break-up rumours after posting 'loved-up' video

24 Nov 2019, 14:10 by Mirror .co.uk

Cody Simpson, 22, has wished his ‘baby’ Miley Cyrus , 27, a happy birthday on Instagram sending fans wild after break-up rumours circled the new couple.

The Australian singer last night posted a sweet video to his 3.8 million followers captioned: “Happy birthday baby. Thanks for being you.”

The video shows the pair giggling while Simpson lip-syncs a song with the lyrics, “I felt love.”

The post came as a surprise to fans of the couple, who have been dating since October, after split rumours plagued the pair in recent weeks .

It’s the first birthday the Tennessee singer is spending as a divorcee, after her infamous split from ex-husband Liam Hemsworth in August.

On Saturday, Simpson also shared a selfie of the two on his Instagram Story writing, “B’day angel.”

The pair is rumoured to have celebrated Miley’s birthday in her home state of Tennessee, where she purchased a home in 2017.

The couple were also snapped at dinner together while they celebrated the special occasion.

It comes as the couple have decided that "having separate lives" and living in different states works for their blossoming relationship .

"Miley and Cody have their separate lives. Cody had to stay in LA but has visited Miley in Tennessee," a source told People.

They added: "They have a very easy relationship. They see each other when they can, and when they are apart, things are good too. This works for both of them.''

Musician Cody recently revealed he has had a crush on Miley since he was just 12 years old.

Miley’ spent her birthday sharing fan tributes and childhood photos on her own Instagram Stories on Saturday.

The singer’s family family posted their birthday blessings, as dad Billy Ray Cyrus wrote: “Happy birthday, Miley Cyrus!!! Love you so much.”

Miley’s sister Noah also took to Instagram to wish her a happy birthday, writing: “Happy birthday sissy!!! love you SO MUCH.”

However no signs of a happy birthday post from recent ex Liam, 29, who is " taking it slow" with new flame Maddison Brown, 22 .

The two were snapped in New York together, and fans are going wild speculating about the blossoming relationship.
