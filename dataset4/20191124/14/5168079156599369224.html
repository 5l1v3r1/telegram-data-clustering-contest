New multipurpose sports hall opened in Oman

24 Nov 2019, 14:12 by Times News Service

Muscat: With donations of OMR 15,000, Bani Bu Ali School in south Sharqiyah governorate celebrated the inauguration of a multipurpose sports hall.

According to the school, the project allows students to practice various sports in a healthy environment.

Maryam Al-Sunaidi, Principal of Bani Bu Ali School, said: "Thank you to all the teachers, administrators, companies and local institutions who contributed to the project. The cost of the project reached OMR 15,000. "

The hall will serve the students and the administrative and teaching staff of the school and the local community.
