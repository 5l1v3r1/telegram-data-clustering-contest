Rumhi wins best minister award in the field of energy

24 Nov 2019, 14:46 by Times News Service

Muscat: His Excellency Mohammed bin Hamad Al Rumhi, Minister of Oil and Gas has won the award for the best minister in the field of energy in the world, according to the Petroleum Economist magazine in London.

In a statement issued online by Ministry of Oil and Gas said: “ Ministry of Oil and Gas congrats H.E Mohammed bin Hamad Al Rumhi, Minister of Oil and Gas, on the occasion of receiving the award for the best minister in the field of energy in the world from the Petroleum Economist magazine in London. “
