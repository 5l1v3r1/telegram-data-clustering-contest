Ryan Day Has Thank You Message For Ohio State Fans

24 Nov 2019, 01:01 by Alek Arend

Ohio State conquered one of its biggest obstacles on Saturday with a win over Penn State. The Buckeyes responded to any and all adversity throughout the game and walked away with a 28-17 win.

One of the most impressive aspects of the game was the Ohio State crowd. Buckeyes fans have always been some of the loudest and most passionate in college football. But on Saturday, they took it to a whole new level with a top 10 team in town.

As a result, Ohio State head coach Ryan Day tweeted out a message thanking his team’s fans for their superb showing on Saturday.

“Thank you Buckeye Nation for bringing it today,” Day said on Twitter. “You made a huge impact on the game and we love you! Let’s keep fighting!!!”
