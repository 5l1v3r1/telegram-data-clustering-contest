I'm A Celebrity Bosses Have Upped Security After A Man Was Found Dead Nearby

24 Nov 2019, 01:34 by SPIN

I’m A Celebrity bosses have “tightened security”

The I’m A Celebrity team has reportedly had to tightened security after a man was found dead in the area.

On Friday it was reported that New South Wales police launched a manhunt after a man was found dead under suspicious circumstances.

I’m A Celeb is filmed in far-north eastern New South Wales on an unused Banana plantation.

A source told The Sun The celebs are being watched 24 hours a day by a team of staff and security measures are incredibly tight.

So there is zero chance of anyone sneaking into camp. But it is very unsettling to know that there is someone out there, running from the police, who has committed such a horrible crime.
Investigations are ongoing into the death.
This comes after threats of bushfires in the area earlier this year.
