All Our November Must Haves Are Under $200 — Yup, Even the Cashmere

24 Nov 2019, 01:14 by Sarah Wasilak

As POPSUGAR editors, we independently select and write about stuff we love and think you'll like too. POPSUGAR has affiliate and advertising partnerships so we get revenue from sharing this content and from your purchase.

Although we do splurge on quality fashion items that we trust we'll wear for many years to come, it's always nice to shop sensibly, limiting ourselves to a specific price point. This way, we feel better about picking up multiple items, and naturally we'll have even more outfits to wear, isn't that great? For the month of November, we're not shopping a single item over $200 (the holidays lie ahead, as does gift giving)! We rounded up shoes, must-have tees, belts, and beanies that we're collecting for the end of Fall and Winter months to come.

My personal favorite pick is these Charles & Keith pumps that ring in under $50 and come to a point with a square-shaped, low heel. But if you're all about height and love to stand out, Shop and Fashion director Hannah included a pair of marigold Zara boots that will look unbelievable with your dark denim. In the casual department, we've got muscle tees and live-in cropped sweaters, but editor in chief Lisa upped the ante with a $90 (yes, $90) Everlane cashmere shirt. How's that for sweater dressing? Read ahead for the full lineup of products we're into and find out why they're essentials. The fair price is just a bonus.
