Kym Marsh almost blinded by chihuahua after pet claws her in the eye

The star, who has played troubled Michelle Connor in Coronation Street for nearly 15 years, was given some bad news by doctors after Rico become too playful

24 Nov 2019, 01:21 by Irishmirror.ie

Worried Kym Marsh was almost blinded by her pet chihuahua after she got a little too close to her beloved pup.

The Coronation Street star, 43, had to dash to the hospital after the dog accidentally clawed her.

She revealed in her OK! magazine column that playful Rico scratched her eye so badly that he needed emergency treatment.

Kym, who is leaving the Cobbles in the coming weeks, recalled the moment fear set in - and the bad news doctors told her.

She wrote: “As many of you know I’ve got a little chihuahua puppy name Rico.

“He’s very playful and likes to stroke your face with his paws. But he accidentally pawed my eye.

“At first it didn’t really hurt but my eye was stinging and when I looked in the mirror the left side was bright red.

“I went to the opticians and they told me there was a small lump on the eye. They put me on antibiotic drops but over the next few days, things weren’t improving.”

Doctors told her she had been left with a cyst that may never go away.

She added: “I ended up going to the eye hospital.

"After sitting in the waiting room for hours and hours I was the last patient to be seen.

“The staff were fantastic. They diagnosed me with a conjunctival cyst and gave me steroid drops but warned me the cyst may never go away, which is scary.

“You take your eyes for granted so it’s really nerve-racking when something like this happens.

“I’ve just got my fingers crossed that the drops will work.”

Kym has played troubled Michelle Connor in the soap for 14 years.

But she won't be killed off - and that means she is free to return in the future.

When asked whether her character would die, she replied: "No. The door is open so that’s good."
