Barça have scored the most goals through substitutes in La Liga

Arturo Vidal & Suárez (2), Ansu, Arthur & Busquets have all netted off the bench

24 Nov 2019, 11:34 by sport.es

Barcelona have scored 35 goals in 13 league games this season and seven have come from players who have come off the bench, the latest being Arturo Vidal, who scored the winner against Leganes on Saturday.

Barça have scored more goals through substitutes than any other team in La Liga, ahead of Getafe, Levante and Real Madrid who have all scored five.

Stretching the boundary to the rest of Europe, Barça also lead the way, with Atalanta (six) and Paris Saint-Germain and Parma (five each) their closes challengers on the continent.

The players that have scored off the bench for Barça are Arturo Vidal (two) and Luis Suarez (two), as well as Ansu Fari, Arthur and Sergio Busquets.

Vidal came on to score against Real Betis and Leganes, while Suarez scored twice against Valencia after coming on after 60 minutes.

Ansu bagged against Osasuna, heading home a Nelson Semedo cross to make it 1-1, and Arthur also came off the bench in Pamplona to make it 2-1, although the game ended 2-2.

Busquets, meanwhile, came on early against Celta Vigo and was on target in the 4-1 win.
