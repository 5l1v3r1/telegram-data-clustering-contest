Nine authors to sign at Best of Books anniversary event

24 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Staff reports

Best of Books will include nine author signings and other events in its "35/5" anniversary celebration on Small Business Saturday, which is coming up this week.

The bookstore at 1313 E Danforth Rd. in Edmond has been in business for 35 years; the past five years, it has been owned by the Hight family. Hence the 35/5 anniversary.

From 9 a.m. to noon Saturday, all non-book items such as shirts, socks, games, toys, puzzles and candles will be 25 percent off. For the rest of the day, non-book items will be 10 percent off. All day buyers will get a 20 percent discount on special book sections, including gift books, travel, calendars, science fiction, reference and cooking.

Santa Claus will host a story time and take free photos with children at 11 a.m. Saturday. If interested, arrive early to ensure a line number.

Book signings begin at noon with store owner Joe Hight ("Unnecessary Sorrow"), Randy Krehbiel ("Tulsa, 1921: Reporting a Massacre), Hunter Cates ("Oklahoma's Atticus" and Noah Milligan ("Into Captivity They Will Go").

Another round of signings begins at 1 p.m. and includes Wendy Spinale ("Everland"), Brad McLelland ("Legends of the Lost Causes" and Kim Ventrella ("Skeleton Tree").

Children's authors Tammi Sauer ("Nugget and Fang Go to School") and Gaye Sanders ("The Survivor Tree") will start signing books at 1:30 p.m.
