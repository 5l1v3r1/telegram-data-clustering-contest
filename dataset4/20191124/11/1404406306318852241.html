Adam 22 Agrees With Vlad, Wouldn't Pay Lil Reese $10,000 for Interview

24 Nov 2019, 11:51 by VladTV

Lil Reese recently spoke on the possibility of DJ Vlad reaching out to him for an interview, and said: “Tell him I need a million for this story this s**t ain’t fake or free s**t bigger [than] 50 Rick Ross and whoever [else].” Vlad responded by saying, “Very happy that you survived that shooting. But nobody’s going to pay you even 1% of $1 million for that interview.”

Since then, Adam 22 of No Jumper spoke on the topic of Lil Reese requesting $1 million for an interview and sided with Vlad on the matter, noting he wouldn’t pay the 1% either which amounts to $10,000. Watch above.
