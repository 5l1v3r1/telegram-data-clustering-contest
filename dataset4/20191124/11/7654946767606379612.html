Queen Elizabeth scraps Prince Andrew’s lavish birthday bash

24 Nov 2019, 11:01 by Laura Italiano

Queen Elizabeth has scrapped plans for a lavish 60th-birthday bash for her son Prince Andrew.

Instead, she’ll mark the Feb. 19 occasion with a small family dinner, The Sunday Times of London reported.

The downsizing comes a day after she stripped Andrew of his royal duties and booted his staff from Buckingham Palace in the wake of his disastrous recent BBC interview on his friendship with pedophile financier Jeffrey Epstein.
