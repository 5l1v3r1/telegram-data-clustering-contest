Gareth Bale greeted with boos on his Real Madrid return after Wales flag controversy

24 Nov 2019, 11:21 by Archie Blade

Gareth Bale greeted with boos on his Real Madrid return after Wales flag controversy. His teammates have let it go as banter but the fans are having none of it.

During the international break Gareth Bale and his teammates waved a flag with the words “Wales, golf, Real Madrid – in that order.” While Bale’s agent insists that the action was meant for the media rather than the club or the fans. However, fans have taken grave offence with it.

On Saturday, Gareth Bale returned to the Spanish Capital and the fans let him know what they think of him. The winger started on the wings but the fans began booing him before the match had even begun when his name was read out on the speakers.
