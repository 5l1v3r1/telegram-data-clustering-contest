Woody Cook shares adorable message to mum Zoe Ball for her 49th birthday

24 Nov 2019, 11:25

Zoe Ball’s son Woody Cook paid an adorable tribute to his mother while she enjoys a birthday getaway.

The Circle star Woody shared some cute selfies of him and his mother for her 49th birthday and apologised for missing her special day.

The 18-year-old posted: ‘Wishing a wonderful Birthday to the best Mum I’ve ever had!! Sorry couldn’t be there today but I love you lots!!!!!! xx #legend’

Zoe responded to his sweet message with an eager: ‘LOVE YOU WOO BEAR’ along with a bear emoji.

The Radio 2 presenter shares Woody with musician Norman Cook (or better known as Fatboy Slim) and celebrated her birthday at the Lime Wood Hotel in New Forest, being showered with gifts and cake.

She uploaded a snap of the gifts she received, and it’s safe to say she had a ball.

Zoe was presented with endless balloons, a homemade cake, cards, a walrus plush, flowers and make up.

Sharing the gifts on Instagram, she wrote: ‘Thank you for the birthday love peeps. Being spoilt rotten #limewoodhotel #newforest #hideaway #escapetothecountry #countrymonkeys #cakeface’ along with a cake emoji and present emoji.

Meanwhile, Zoe has recently split from her boyfriend Michael Reed after nearly two years together, according to reports.

It marked the first public relationship the It Takes Two presenter had since the tragic suicide of her boyfriend Billy Yates in 2017.

According to sources, Zoe called time on the romance with construction boss Michael amid claims she was ‘growing uncomfortable’ with reports he was using her fame for his own benefit.

‘Zoe knew deep down the relationship wasn’t going anywhere,’ a friend told The Sun. ‘She enjoyed her time with Michael but came to the conclusion he wasn’t a keeper, despite him believing they were getting serious.

‘There were certain things she grew uncomfortable with, such as him wanting to become a model and act. Her close circle feared he was using her to enhance his own career.’
