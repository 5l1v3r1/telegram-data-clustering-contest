Ajit Pawar changes Twitter bio to 'Deputy CM, Maharashtra', says Sharad Pawar still his leader

24 Nov 2019, 11:52 by BusinessToday.In

On Sunday, Ajit Pawar, in a series of tweet, thanked all BJP veteran leaders who had wished him for showing support to their party

Ajit Pawar has changed his Twitter bio to 'Deputy Chief Minister, Maharashtra'. The Nationalist Congress Party Leader (NCP) leader also responded to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's congratulatory message after his sudden swearing-in as Deputy CM on Saturday. Pawar in a tweet thanked the prime minister and also wrote that he would work hard for people of the state.
