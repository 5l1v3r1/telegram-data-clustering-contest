Wayne Rooney whisks wife Coleen off on first romantic mini-break after WAGatha Christie

Forget WagAgathaChristie - the Rooneys are closer than ever on a mini break

24 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Irishmirror.ie

Wayne Rooney has whisked wife Coleen off on their first childless romantic break after the WAGatha Christie drama.

The pair shared a romantic snap from their childless break without sons Kai, 10, Klay, six, Kit, three, and Cass, 21 months.

It comes after a tough few months for the mum who waged war on Rebecca Vardy by accusing the star's Instagram account of leaking fake stories about her.

Coleen claimed she laid a carefully set trap where only Rebecca Vardy's Instagram could view her Instagram stories which consequently ended up being branded the WAGatha Christie drama.

Wayne shared the romantic picture on Sunday from Dubai's luxiourious Mandarin Oriental Jumeira. He captioned the snap: "Nice break in Dubai with Coleen Rooney. Thanks to all the staff for looking after us."

Coleen opted for a sassy scoop neck thigh skimming dress which showed off her Dubai tan to perfection.

The break comes after the pairs first date night earlier this month.

Captioning the date night snap Coleen wrote: "It’s been a while.... Great to be back to weekday date nights and meeting up with friends."

This Christmas will mark the Rooney's first in their new £20million home so no wonder they have got the festive fever already.
