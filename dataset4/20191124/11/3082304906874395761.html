Al Hilal see off Urawa Red Diamonds to win AFC Champions League 2019

24 Nov 2019, 11:54 by Gabriel Tan

Al Hilal have ended a 19-year wait to be crowned champions of Asia again as they won AFC Champions League 2019 on Sunday after beating Urawa Red Diamonds 2-0 to claim a 3-0 aggregate final triumph.

Having claimed a slender victory on home soil a fortnight ago courtesy of an Andre Carrillo effort, Al Hilal backed up that performance at Saitama Stadium 2002 as second-half goals by Salem Al-Dawsari and Bafetimbi Gomis sealed their first ACL crown since 2000.
