Sharad Pawar my leader, BJP-NCP alliance will provide stable govt: Ajit Pawar

Justifying his joining hands with the BJP, Ajit Pawar said that NCP's alliance with the saffron party would provide a stable government in Maharashtra for next five years.

24 Nov 2019, 11:42

Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) leader Ajit Pawar has tweeted that party chief Sharad Pawar is his leader. In a tweet, Maharashtra Deputy Chief Minister Ajit Pawar also said that he would always remain in the NCP.

This is Ajit Pawar's first response to the high drama in Maharashtra after he joined hands with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and took oath as the Deputy Chief Minister of the state.

Taking to Twitter, Ajit Pawar said, "I am in the NCP and shall always be in the NCP and @PawarSpeaks Saheb is our leader."

Justifying his joining hands with the BJP, Ajit Pawar said that NCP's alliance with the saffron party would provide a stable government in Maharashtra for next five years.

Ajit Pawar said, "Our BJP-NCP alliance shall provide a stable Government in Maharashtra for the next five years which will work sincerely for the welfare of the State and its people."
