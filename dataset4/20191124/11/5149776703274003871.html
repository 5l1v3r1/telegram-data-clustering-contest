Police called to pub after reports of two men 'entering with a firearm and assaulting staff member'

A 29-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of robbery.

24 Nov 2019, 11:41 by Phoebe Ram

Officers were called to a pub in Huthwaite after two men are alleged to have 'entered with a firearm'.

Nottinghamshire Police responded just before 6.45am on Saturday, November 24 to The Woodend pub in Chesterfield Road, Huthwaite near Sutton-in-Ashfield.

It was reported two men had entered the pub with a firearm and assaulted a member of the pub's cleaning staff.

Two bottles of spirits and a charity collection box were also reported stolen during the incident.

A 29-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of robbery.

If you saw what happened, you should call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 195 of 23 November 2019.
