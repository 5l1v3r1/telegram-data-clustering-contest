1 killed, 3 hurt in grenade blast

24 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Arlene Rivera

A MAN was killed while three others were injured after a grenade planted on the motorcycle of a policeman exploded yesterday morning in Mandaluyong City.

Mandaluyong police chief Col. Remigio Sedanto, identified the fatality as Roger Barcelo of Barangay Barangka, Mandaluyong City while the injured victims were identified as  Rogelio Tolentino, 32; Merlina Bag-o, 52; and Edwin Rodas, 34.

Initial investigation showed that Police Staff Sergeant Aldin Saligo, a member of the Mandaluyong Police Station Drug Enforcement Unit (SDEU), was traversing F. Martinez Street in Barangay Addition Hills and heading to the police station when the incident happened at around 6:30 a.m.

As Saligo rode on  by a road hump, a hand grenade surreptitiously  tied under his motorcycle fell and exploded.

The blast hit Barcelo who was also on a motorcycle and passing the area. The other victims who were walking on the sidewalk were hit with shrapnel. Barcelo was rushed to the nearest hospital but was declared dead on arrival.

Sedanto said Saligo had been receiving several death threats prior to the incident.
