Van stolen in Market Drayton

24 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Nick Humphreys

A van has been stolen in Market Drayton.

The white Ford Transit was taken from Buntingsdale on Friday evening between around 5pm and 9pm.

Market Drayton SNT tweeted: "White Ford Transit has been stolen from Buntingsdale, Market Drayton on Friday 22nd. Believed to have been taken between 17:00 and 20:57hrs. Any information please call 101."
