How long has Justin Timberlake been married to Jessica Biel?

24 Nov 2019, 11:34

Justin Timberlake has hit the headlines after he was spotted holding hands with the co-star of his forthcoming movie Palmer earlier this week.

The 38-year-old pop star and actor was spotted with Alisha Wainwright, who appears with him in the football drama – sparking speculation although a source told Mail Online there is ‘nothing going on’ between the two of them.

Justin is of course married to actress Jessica Biel, – who fell for the former NSYNC singer despite admitting she wasn’t a fan of the boyband.

But how long have they been together – and who has Justin dated in the past?

Here’s what you need to know…

How long have Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel been married?

The two superstars started dating in January 2007.

The two had a temporary break in March 2011, releasing a statement to People magazine insisting they’ll ‘remain friends and continue to hold the highest level of love and respect for each other.’

You can’t clip the wings of two true love birds, though and they reconciled around June.

They became engaged in December 2011 and married on October 19 2012 at the Borgo Egnazia resort in Fasano, Italy.

In 2015, the two welcomed their son Silas Randall.

And perhaps their most important milestone – in 2019, Jessica Biel won Halloween, dressing as her hubby in his NSYNC get-up.

Justin Timberlake’s dating history

Britney Spears

How could anyone forget Britney and Justin?

Meeting as youngsters in Disney’s Mickey Mouse Club, the two grew up together and dated from 1998 to 2002.

During their tenure as America’s Sweethearts, they gave us such iconic moments as the matching denim outfit – which is still recreated by the likes of Kendall Jenner, Katy Perry, and Jake Paul and Tana Mongeau.

When they first broke up, cheating rumours were banded around – with Timberlake’s own Cry Me a River adding fuel to the fire.
