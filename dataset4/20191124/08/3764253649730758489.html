Prophet Chukwuemeka Odumeje bags doctorate degree at Gregory University in Abia state

24 Nov 2019, 08:40

- Nigerian pastor, Prophet Chukwuemeka Odumeje, has bagged a doctorate degree

- The pastor was awarded the degree at Gregory University Uturu in Abia state

- Odumeje bagged a doctorate degree in humanity

Popular Nigerian pastor, Prophet Chukwuemeka Odumeje, has become the latest PhD holder in town. The pastor recently bagged a doctorate degree from Gregory University Uturu in Abia state.

It was gathered that the pastor was awarded a doctorate degree in humanity. In photos shared on social media, he was spotted giving a speech at the school podium after he was given his degree.

The photos were shared on the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter, by users congratulating him on his achievement.
