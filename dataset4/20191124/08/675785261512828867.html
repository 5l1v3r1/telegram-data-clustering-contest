How much every Premier League side will pay in squad salaries this season

With top-level teams spending more than ever on wages for their stars they are left with an astronomical salary bill at the end of every season

24 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Alex Milne

Spending in football has never been higher, and Premier League stars are now among the most affluent earners in world sport.

Top players can expect to take home hundreds of thousands of pounds every week, and contract offers seem to only be on the increase.

As a result, clubs are left with a titanic outlay over the course of the season, with some truly staggering amounts being splashed out on retaining their key assets.

Here is a look at how much every team will spend on salaries this season according to the website Spotrac, along with the highest earner at each club.
