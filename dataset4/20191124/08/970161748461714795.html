Dan Osborne begs I'm A Celebrity fans to give Jacqueline Jossa 'a break'

24 Nov 2019, 08:53

Dan Osborne has asked I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here! viewers to go easy on his wife Jacqueline Jossa after she was selected for another bushtucker trial.

In Saturday night’s episode Jacqueline and Ian Wright were chosen as the next pair to face a trial to collect stars for camp.

The former EastEnders star became very upset and had to be comforted by her camp mates as she said: ‘I don’t want to do this any more’.

Her husband took to Instagram and urged viewers to give her ‘a break’.

Dan, who married Jacqueline in 2015, said: ‘I know I said to you all vote for Jac because she ain’t here to change light bulbs. I didn’t mean every single trial. Give her a break now. Come on.

‘She keeps smashing them all. She’s worried about it but she’ll smash it. She’ll keep proving everyone . And she’s doing it with Ian Wright as well which is good. He’ll look after her.

‘Let her do this one then just give her a break please. She’s getting upset now. It ain’t nice. But she’ll be good. She’ll smash it. She’ll get the stars, go back and have a nice dinner.’

He also had praise for reality TV star Caitlyn Jenner and radio DJ Adele Roberts.

‘We were just saying how great Caitlyn is and she’s smashing it. And Adele. Solid,’ Dan said in the Instagram video.

Jacqueline has smashed trials in the past, facing her worst fear of spiders and doing it in record time.

On Saturday it was Caitlyn’s turn for a trial, gamely chomping on a cow’s eye in a gruesome challenge.

Meanwhile Adele and Nadine Coyle were chosen to eat a cow’s anus. Despite retching, Nadine managed to scoff hers. Adele asked the other celebs to sing to encourage her and given the boost she managed to eat her anus too.

I’m A Celebrity continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
