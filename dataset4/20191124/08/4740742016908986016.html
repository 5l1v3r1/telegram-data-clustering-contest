Inside the stunning penthouse for sale in Bristol that'll set you back £1m

The Grade-II listed property has had a stunning makeover

24 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Danny Shaw

Imagine if you could enjoy the comfort of a stylish, Grade-II listed property within the liveliness of Bristol’s city centre.

Well, now you can... if you have a spare £1million lying around to splash on this gorgeous apartment.

This penthouse is one of three converted flats located on the King Edward VII wing of the former Bristol General Hospital.

The flats fuse traditional exteriors with contemporary living spaces, providing a luxurious retreat, just minutes away from the hustle and bustle of the city centre.

Look inside the penthouse here

The property is located on Guinea Street, around half a mile from Bristol Temple Meads.

The penthouse spans three floors, has three bedrooms, with the en-suite master bedroom boasting a walk-in dressing room.

You can even enjoy breath-taking views of the harbour from the comfort of a deep bath.

The living room/dining area has soaring ceilings and windows that ensure this is a light and airy space.

Double doors connect the impressive living space to the spacious kitchen. The kitchen itself has fully-integrated Neff appliances.

There’s also a family bathroom, an office area and a utility room.

Cars and bikes are also safe and secure in an underground car park and designated bike shed.
