Saina Nehwal pulls out of Premier Badminton League

24 Nov 2019, 08:19 by ANI

Highlights

  • Saina revealed that she has not been well throughout the year as pancreatitis and injuries have troubled her.
  • Saina was last seen in action in the Hong Kong Open where she had to face a first-round exit.
  • Saina has now faced exits from a tournament in the first round five times.

NEW DELHI: Ace Indian shuttler Saina Nehwal on Sunday announced that she would not be participating in the upcoming fifth edition of Premier Badminton League (PBL).

She revealed that she has not been well throughout the year as pancreatitis and injuries have troubled her so she would like to take some time off the game.

"Hey everyone, I won't be part of the PBL Season 5 . I haven't been well most part of the year due to pancreatitis and injuries and I would like to take time during the PBL to prepare better . I want to say sorry to all my fans and I hope to be part of the next season of PBL," Saina tweeted.
