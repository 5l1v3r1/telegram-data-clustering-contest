Vipers split the weekend

24 Nov 2019, 08:17 by John Lawless

The Vernon Vipers left this weekend with a split, starting off with a win at home on Friday.

The Vipers narrowly edged the Salmon Arm Silverbacks 2-1 in a shootout. Drew Bennett had the lone regulation goal for the Vipers, and Reilly Herbst made 30 saves. Connor Marritt and Logan Cash were both successful in the shootout.

Game two of their back to back homestand wasn't the same story, as they fell 3-1 to the Langley Rivermen. Connor Sleath scored for the Vipers, and Hunter Donohoe and Matt Kowalski chipped in with the assists.

This weekend has Vernon locked in a tie for fourth place in the tough Interior Division with the Wentachee Wild. They are 10 points back of the Trail Smoke Eaters with three games at hand.

The Vipers will be looking to bounce back on Friday when they welcome the Surrey Eagles to town, who are last place in the Mainland Division.
