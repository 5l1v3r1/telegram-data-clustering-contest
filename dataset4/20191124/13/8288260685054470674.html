Two stabbed and receive 'serious injuries' in vicious attack

Officers seal off road after double knifing in Villa Road Lozells - and make three arrests

24 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Stephanie Balloo, https://www.facebook.com/MissBalloo/

Police shut down city road after two men were stabbed in the early hours of Sunday morning.

Officers swooped to Villa Road, Lozells at 3.15am at the junction with Hamstead Road.

Two men suffered serious wounds in the incident, which has also seen three people arrested.

A spokesman for West Midlands Police said: “Three men have been arrested after two men were stabbed in Lozells this morning.

“Police were called to Hamstead Road just after 3.15am. One victim, aged 23, was left with a serious hand injury and taken to hospital. A second man suffered a serious back injury.

“Three men were arrested, and police remain at the scene this morning.”

At the scene there are yellow markers along with a forensics van as officers carry out their investigations.

Community activist Desmond Jaddoo has been down at Villa Road this morning and says forensics officers are there.

He said: “On my way through I went to the scene, the stretch between Heathfield Road and Hamstead Road is blocked, and Hunters Road too.

“I understand there’s been an incident early hours of the morning, police have turned up and blocked off the road.

“One shopkeeper this morning, he lives ten miles away, said he arrived to open his shop only to be told he can’t open because of the cordon.”

A police cordon can be seen at the junction of Hamstead Road this morning. Anyone with information has been asked to contact officers via Live Chat at west-midlands.police.uk or via 101, or via Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111. Quote log 429 of 24 November
