PMCO Fall Split 2019, Prelims Day 2 Match 11: IND finishes #5, INS finishes #9, Bigetron wins Game 11

24 Nov 2019, 13:02 by Kuldeep

PMCO Fall Split 2019 Prelims for Global Finals are being held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The 11th match of Prelims Day 2 took place in the map Vikendi, from a third-person perspective. The flight path for this match started from the north-west part of the map and went towards the north-east part of the map. The first play-zone formed near the north-west side of the map, and it circled hot-priority locations such as Los Dobro Mesto, Krichas, and Goroka.

Party Gaming's Jihun eliminated AustinX from SynerGE to secure the first kill of the game. IND's Kratos with a superb play was able to kill two members of Futbolist. Futbolist was unaware when Kratos sneaked on them from behind. IND then went on to eliminate SynerGE and Orange Esports from the game. An exceptional grenade from Red Canids wiped out two members of INS. IND's impressive run ended after the Indian team was sandwiched by Bigetron and Asterion Myth. Bigetron won the game after they eliminated 2 members from Red Canids and 2 members from Asterion Myth in a chaotic battle. They secured 18 kills in the 11th game of PMCO Fall Split Prelims for Global Finals.

Match 11- Vikendi TPP

  1. Bigetron: 18 kills and 38 points
  2. Red Canids: 6 kills and 20 points
  3. Asterion Myth: 6 kills and 126 points
