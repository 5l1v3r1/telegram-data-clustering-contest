CAF Awards: Ighalo, Ndidi, Osimhen shortlisted in 30-man list for African best player

24 Nov 2019, 13:27

- CAF awards 2019 is set to be staged next year and three Nigerians have been shortlisted in a 30-man list for African player of the year

- Odion Ighalo, Wilfred Ndidi, and Victor Osimhen will battle 27 other players for the award

- Man City's Riyad Mahrez and Liverpool duo Mohammed Salah and Sadio Mane top the list

Nigeria's Odion Ighalo, Wilfred Ndidi, and Victor Osimhen have been shortlisted in a 30-man list for African player of the year.

In a list released by CAF, the three players were nominated alongside 27 other African stars with Man City's Riyad Mahrez and Liverpool duo Mohammed Salah and Sadio Mane topping the list.
