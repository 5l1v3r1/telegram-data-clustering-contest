Car collides with traffic light in Bilston

Part of Oxford Street was blocked following the smash on Friday afternoon

24 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Jordan Coussins, Charlotte Regen, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/

Commuters were faced with travel chaos after a dramatic smash blocked off a busy Black Country junction.

Dramatic pictures from the scene, which were taken by Black Country Live and nearby motorists, showed the damaged car at the side of the Oxford Street, Bilston.

A traffic light was also been knocked onto its side following the smash at around 4pm on Friday, November 22.

It is not known if anyone was injured but motorists spoke of travel misery – as part of the major junction was closed off.

An eyewitness, who did not want to be named, said the junction is notorious for crashes and urged people to slow down as the bad weather continues.

“The junction has been awful lately,” one said. “There are always bumps and shunts – it’s only a matter of time before something worse happens.

“I hope everyone is ok. The cars looked a bit of a mess.”
