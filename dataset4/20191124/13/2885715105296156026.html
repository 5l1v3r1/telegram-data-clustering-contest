Ranveer Singh or Rani Mukerji: Who wore this Sabyasachi outfit better?

Both Rani Mukerji and Ranveer Singh were spotted wearing a similar Sabyasachi ensemble. Who do you think looked better?

24 Nov 2019, 13:31 by Lifestyle Desk

Rani Mukerji, who is busy with promotions of her latest film, Mardaani 2, was recently spotted in a Sabyasachi ensemble. The actor was seen in a floral printed kurta which was teamed with a matching churidar. This was paired with a sheer dupatta and the look was rounded out with dark kohl eyes and hair tied.

In case you think you have seen this outfit somewhere, well you are not wrong. Recently Ranveer Singh had stepped out wearing a very similar ensemble from the same designer while visiting the Golden Temple. He was with Deepika Padukone celebrating their first wedding anniversary. The Gully Boy actor was spotted in a floral printed kurta and churidar which he paired with a waistcoat. Singh is known for his flamboyant fashion choices and mostly for blurring the line between what women wear and what men do. And it was no different this time.

While it is refreshing to note that outfits are no longer considered to be gender-specific, if there was a comparison between both the actor, we guess it is Singh who wins this round.
