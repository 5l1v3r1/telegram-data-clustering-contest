Espanyol drop points against Getafe 1-1 at Rcde Stadium on Sunday

24 Nov 2019, 13:24 by SPORT.es

Espanyol and Getafe draw 1-1 on Sunday at Rcde Stadium. Espanyol arrived with the intention of regaining victory after losing 3-1 against Atl√©tico de Madrid while Getafe had to settle for a draw in the last match played of the competition. As of today, Espanyol and Getafe occupy position 19th (9 points) and 6th (21 points) respectively after playing a total of 14 matches.

The first half started in favour of Getafe, thanks to a good early goal from Jaime Mata in the 3rd minute. However, Espanyol just before the end of the 45 minutes gave an equalizer thanks to a goal from Wu Lei in the 45th minute, and the first half ended 1-1.

The second half ended with little further action at either end and goalless.

As for substitutions, both coaches used full substitutions. For Espanyol, Matias Vargas, Esteban Granero and Kevin Soni came on for Victor Sanchez, Matias Vargas and Victor Campuzano Bonilla, and Getafe brought on Jorge Molina, Robert Kenedy and Francisco Portillo for Angel Rodriguez, Marc Cucurella and Jason.

The referee booked Wu Lei, Marc Roca and Esteban Granero from Espanyol, while for Getafe Jaime Mata, Mauro Arambarri, Marc Cucurella and Jorge Molina saw a yellow card as well.

Getafe and Espanyol will play at home stadium against Levante and Osasuna respectively.
