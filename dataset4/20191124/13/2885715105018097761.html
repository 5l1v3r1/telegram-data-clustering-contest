PM Modi launches fitness grading system for schools across India

The Fit India School rankings have been divided into three categories - Fit India Schools, which is the first level of ranking, Fit India School (3 star) and Fit India School (5 star) - depending on the level of importance the school gives to fitness.

24 Nov 2019, 13:07 by PTI

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday launched the Fit India School grading system in schools across the country.

The Fit India School rankings have been divided into three categories — The Fit India Schools, which is the first level of ranking, Fit India School (3 star) and Fit India School (5 star).
