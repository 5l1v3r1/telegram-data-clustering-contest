Arsenal chiefs 'have no plans to sack Unai Emery'

Arsenal allegedly have no plans to make a quick-fire decision on the future of manager Unai Emery.

24 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Darren Plant

With the Gunners having only recorded two wins from their last 10 matches in the Premier League, there are increasing calls for Emery to be removed from his position.

However, despite the negative atmosphere at the Emirates Stadium, The Independent claims that the Spaniard remains safe for the foreseeable future.

The report says that Raul Sanllehi and Edu intend to give Emery at least another three weeks to turn around the club's fortunes.

The north Londoners next face fixtures away at Norwich City and at home to Brighton & Hove Albion as they bid to close the gap on the top four in the standings.

Arsenal currently sit in seventh position, eight points adrift of city rivals Chelsea.
