CAF Awards: Chukwueze, Osimhen nominated in 10-man list for Africa's best young player

24 Nov 2019, 13:50

- CAF awards 2019 is set to be staged next year and two Nigerians have been shortlisted in a 10-man list for African young player of the year

- Samuel Chukwueze and Victor Osimhen will battle eight other players for the award

- Borussia Dortmund and Morocco star Achraf Hakimi tops the list

Nigeria's Samuel Chukwueze and Victor Osimhen have been shortlisted in a 10-man list for African young player of the year.

In a list released by CAF, the two players were nominated alongside eight other African stars with Borussia Dortmund and Morocco star Achraf Hakimi topping the list.

The CAF African Young Player of the Year category recognizes the best young player from Africa in the year under review, in this case, 2019.
