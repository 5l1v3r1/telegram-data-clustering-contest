'Too many times' - Bournemouth fans have questions after defeat to Wolves

Wolverhampton Wanderers latest | Wolves' 2-1 triumph at the Vitality Stadium lifted them up to fifth - but what did Bournemouth's supporters make of their side's performance?

24 Nov 2019, 13:51 by Josh Murray

Bournemouth are the eighth side in a row to fail to beat Wolverhampton Wanderers in a Premier League game - as Nuno Espirito Santo's side continue their impressive upward trajectory.

Wolves have not lost a League game since September 14, and Saturday's 2-1 triumph over the Cherries was the fourth in their last seven outings.

Joao Moutinho and Raul Jimenez got the first-half goals to put Nuno's men in control, with Simon Francis' red card - on his first League start in 11 months - aiding the visitors' cause.

Steve Cook's header ensured some nerves towards the end of the game, but Wolves saw the contest out to move up to fifth in the table.
