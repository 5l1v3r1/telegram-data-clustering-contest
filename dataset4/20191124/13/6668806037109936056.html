John Abraham and Anil Kapoor starrer Pagalpanti has a slow start at the box-office

24 Nov 2019, 13:55 by Kajol Mathur

Anees Bazmee’s mad comedy Pagalpanti starring John Abraham, Ileana D'Cruz, Anil Kapoor, Pulkit Samrat, Kriti Kharbanda and Urvashi Rautela has finally hit the screens. The film opened to mixed reviews and is competing with the sequel of Frozen at the box-office. 

Speaking of film’s box-office collections, Pagalpanti managed to collect a total of Rs. 5 crore nett on its opening day followed by 25 per cent growth and earning Rs 6.25 crore on day two at the box-office. The film’s total collection currently rests at Rs.11.25 crore 

Pagalpanti is produced by Bhushan Kumar, Krishan Kumar, Abhishek Pathak and Kumar Mangat Pathak under T-Series banner and Panorama Studios.
