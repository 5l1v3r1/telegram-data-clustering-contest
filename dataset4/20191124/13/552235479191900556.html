Teen arrested by armed cops after report of man with gun and knife at Scots shopping mall

Officers were called to the Kingdom Shopping Centre in Glenrothes late yesterday afternoon.

24 Nov 2019, 13:37 by Record Reporter

Armed cops were called to a shopping mall amid reports of a man carrying a gun and a knife.

Police confirmed they arrested a teenager at Kingdom Shopping Centre in Glenrothes at 5.25pm on Saturday.

A spokesman said: "A knife and replica firearm were recovered and a 16-year-old male has been arrested in connection with the incident.

"There was no risk to the wider public."
