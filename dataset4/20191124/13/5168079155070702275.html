Want to watch Gulf Cup in Qatar? You can buy tickets from these locations in Oman

24 Nov 2019, 13:48 by Times News Service

Muscat: People living in Oman who wish to travel to Qatar to watch the Gulf Cup first-hand can buy their tickets in Oman.

The tickets are being sold at two locations in the Sultanate – one in Muscat, and one in Sohar – and can also be purchased online at www.gulfcup2019.qa.

A statement from the Qatar Football Association said, “Tickets are available in Oman at Muscat Grand Mall in Muscat and Safeer Mall in Sohar. Get your tickets, mark the dates and support your national team at the Gulf Cup 2019!”

Tickets are sold in the mall daily from 4pm to 10pm. The Gulf Cup begins on 26 November and ends on the 8th of December.

Additionally, those who live in Kuwait and wish to buy their tickets can do so at The Avenues Mall, while in Qatar, tickets are being sold at Souq Waqif, Katara, Villaggio, the Mall of Qatar and Doha Festival City.
