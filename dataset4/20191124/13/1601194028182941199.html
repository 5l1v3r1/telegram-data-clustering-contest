Bangla Tigers vs Qalandars Live Cricket Score: Ball by ball scorecard and match updates

24 Nov 2019, 13:07 by Vinay Chhabaria

The final night of the Abu Dhabi T10 league will begin with a battle between Bangla Tigers and Qalandars for third place. After losing their qualifying matches, both sides will now battle for the third position in the league. Dawid Malan will lead the Qalandars for this battle Thisara Perera will head the Bangla Tigers.

The match will begin at 7.15 PM IST at the Sheikh Zayed Stadium. The fans are excited about this contest because this is the first time the Qalandars will lock horns with the Tigers this year.

You can follow ball-by-ball scorecard updates of the match right here.

Dawid Malan, Philip Salt and Samit Patel will be the 3 top player to look out from the Qalandars. Malan has aggregated 151 runs in 8 matches so far while Samit Patel had impressed the cricket universe with his performance against the Deccan Gladiators. The former England player had bagged 3 wickets for 26 runs in his 2 overs.

On the other side, Rilee Rossouw, Colin Ingram and Thisara Perera will look to give the Qalandars a run for their money. Rossouw has been the leading run-scorer from his side as with 195 runs in 7 matches. It will be intriguing to see which side finishes at the third position and ends the tournament on a happy note.
