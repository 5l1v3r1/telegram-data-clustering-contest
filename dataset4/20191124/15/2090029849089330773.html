Calling a woman Ashawo means she’s successful – Afia Schwarzenegger

24 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Sanya Agunbiade

Media personality Afia Schwarzenegger has said that most men chose to call a successful woman ashawo when they see her progressing in her career.

In a post she made on social media, Afia wrote that men need to come up with more creative insults as Ashawo (whore) no longer stings.

She wrote:

Ashawo in 2019 means : congratulations you are a very successful hard working female entrepreneur !!!!
Guys pls be creative n come up with another insult wae ….hahahahahaha hahahahahahahahahahahaha
Your Mother!!!!!
