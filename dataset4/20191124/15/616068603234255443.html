Man dies in motorbike crash

24 Nov 2019, 15:26 by Laura Brookes

Vancouver Police are investigating after a 44-year-old Vancouver man died in a motorcycle crash downtown today (Nov. 24). 

The driver of the motorcycle was heading east on Nelson Street near Granville Street when he lost control of the motorcycle and hit a tree. 

The driver was taken to hospital, where he was pronounced dead. 

No other vehicles were involved in the crash.

It is unknown how or why he lost control of his motorcycle, but police believe speed was likely a factor.

Vancouver Police Department investigators are working to determine the cause of the crash.  

Anyone who witnessed this incident, or has video, is asked to call VPD's Collision Investigation Unit at 604-717-3012.
