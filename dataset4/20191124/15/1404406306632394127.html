Chicago Rapper Killa Kellz Pleads Guilty to Meth Trafficking, Facing 15 Years

24 Nov 2019, 15:01 by VladTV

According to reports, Chicago rapper Killa Kellz pleaded guilty to charges that stem from 2017, and now the rapper is reportedly facing a 15-year prison sentence.

The rapper was booked for drug trafficking in 2017 in northern Missouri. While in court, the rapper, whose real name is Kevin Phyfiher, pled guilty to having almost a kilogram of meth with the intent to distribute. In his plea deal, his other charges were dropped as a result.

The rapper’s plea deal reveals he is facing a possibility of 15 years in federal prison without parole. Stay tuned for more details.
