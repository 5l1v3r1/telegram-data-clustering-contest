Priyanka Chopra visits Shabana Azmi to offer her condolences

24 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Kajol Mathur

Bollywood veteran Shabana Azmi’s mother Shaukat Kaifi passed away recently and ever since then friends from the movie fraternity are sending out love and support to the family. While yesterday we saw several celebs including Amitabh Bachchan, Rishi Kapoor, Rekha, Sudhir Mishra and more paying their last respects, today we snapped Priyanka Chopra at Shabana Azmi’s Mumbai residence. The actress was accompanied by brother Siddharth Chopra.

Priyanka donned a white shirt and paired with blue denims and black loafers. She rounded off her look with a high bun and black tinted sunnies.
