Dubai’s Cavalli Club to throw Winterland New Year’s Eve party

Check out the ice lounge and robot bar at this swanky Dubai club

24 Nov 2019, 15:27 by Reema Rahman

Having already thrown a decade’s worth of New Year’s Eve parties, Cavalli Club Dubai is set to go all out for its eleventh year – ensuring the new decade starts off with a bang.

The longstanding flashy nightclub is going to be transformed into a “frosted dream” which is aptly being called Cava11i Winterland.

There are going to be stunning performances on the centre stage, beverages made by an actual robot (and no, we’re not kidding – click here to find out here) and make sure to bring a coat for the ice lounge bar which is a freezing -18 degrees.

You can make a night out of it and head down for dinner from 9pm until 11.30pm and enjoy a four-course set menu along with free-flowing select beverages for Dhs1,650 per person.

There are also tickets available for the lounge area which are Dhs900 for ladies and Dhs1,100 for gents and include free-flowing beverages until 3am.

This luxurious nightspot is as cool as it gets and recently underwent a multi-million dollar make-over – so to call it flashy would be a massive understatement.

What a way to welcome in 2020.
