The Weeknd Drops $21 Million on New Los Angeles Penthouse

24 Nov 2019, 15:56 by VladTV

According to reports, The Weeknd has picked up a $21 million penthouse at the Beverly West in Los Angeles. This is reportedly one of the highest condo sales ever recorded in Los Angeles.

His entire residence occupies the 18th floor of the building, and is named the “Mogul.”  The building project manager—Eric Jencks, said: “This penthouse was designed for a mogul, and it’s a mogul who is buying it.” He also said, “The Beverly West has no common areas besides the amenities floor.
