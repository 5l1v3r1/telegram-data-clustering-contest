John Turturro Joins The Batman as Mobster Carmine Falcone

24 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Cheryl Eddy

Just when you thought The Batman could not possibly cram any more characters into its cast, along comes this bit of news: John Turturro has signed on to play Bat-foe Carmine Falcone.

The news was shared in Variety and confirmed by The Batman director Matt Reeves on Twitter, using a GIF of what’s probably the best-known role – soon to be reprised in the Turturro-directed The Jesus Rolls – in Turturro’s long and varied career.
