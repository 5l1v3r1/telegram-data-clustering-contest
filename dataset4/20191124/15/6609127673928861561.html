BACK HOME

Mekala Osborne – Scots personal trainer who was in coma on Vietnam ‘back in Edinburgh’ after two month ordeal

24 Nov 2019, 15:31 by Aidan Smith

A SCOTS personal trainer left fighting for her life in Vietnam after falling ill has returned home, according to reports.

Mekala Osborne, 22, was placed in an induced coma after developing pneumonia while travelling in South East Asia.

Mekala has spent months in intensive care in a hospital in Hoi An, Da Nang.

Mum Yvonne and stepdad Jimmy McAulay, 64, both jetted out to be at her side.

Now according to Edinburgh Evening News Mekala has returned to Scotland's capital.

She flew back to the UK via Manchester before an ambulance drove her up the road to Scotland.

Mr McAulay, a taxi driver, said: “Mekala is safely home, with no problems. We are all emotional wrecks — but in a good way.

"Mekala's mum and I are so excited that she's home and safe, although there's still a long way to go.

Mrs McAulay told followers on Facebook: "Mekala has arrived safely in the ERI and is comfortable. She will just be resting tonight as the wee scone is shattered."

Well-wishers have raised over £12,000 to help support Mekala's family with the costs of travel and treatment.
