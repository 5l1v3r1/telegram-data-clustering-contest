Here's why Priyanka Chopra will not sing a duet with husband Nick Jonas

24 Nov 2019, 15:41 by Kajol Mathur

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas have been married for over a year now and we just can’t get enough of them. With the two of them being popular global stars, fans are excited about them collaborating in future. It doesn’t really surprise us because other than being an actor, Priyanka is also a talented singer. Being married to one of the most popular names in the international music circuit, one would expect that she might sing a duet soon with him but nothing is on the cards as of now.

When asked to comment on the same,  Priyanka said, “I love music, but I don't have that kind of education in the field”. She then described her husband as a “child prodigy” and said he does everything from making songs, writing lyrics and creating music, however, she just likes music. The superstar further added that she wouldn’t even try singing with him.

Priyanka had started her music career with her first single In my city in 2012 which also featured American rapper Will.I.Am. Post that, she had her another hit number Exotic in 2013 with Pitbull. 

On the acting front, she last appeared in Shonali Bose’s The Sky Is Pink and is currently shooting for Netflix’s The White Tiger.
