Club World Cup Fixtures: When will Liverpool play FIFA Club World Cup 2019 matches

24 Nov 2019, 15:21 by Tanish Chachra

Club World Cup Fixtures: When will Liverpool play FIFA club world cup matches as the list of fixtures are released for the tournament.

Club World Cup is about to kick-off in Qatar and 7 elite teams from all the continents of the world will be vying for the title in the final rounds, scheduled to start from 11th December.

Liverpool will represent Europe and they are the sure favourites to clinch the title considering the quality of the team and they have to play a lesser number of matches in the tournament as compared to other teams.

Liverpool will enter into the competition in the semi-final round of the tournament and will face one of the winners from round 2. The Reds are in a phenomenal form and have won only last only one match in all the matches they have played this season across all the tournaments.

Now, Jurgen Klopp’s men have to travel to Doha in order to put the icing on the cake, as this tournament is mostly dominated by the European teams in the recent past.

With Flamengo winning the Copa Libertadores, the tournament has finalized its final 7 teams, the set number of teams which battle in this competition every year.

Similar to Liverpool, Flamengo will also enter into the competition in the semi-final round and will face either Al-Hilal or Esperance de Tunis, who will compete against each other on 14th December.

Amidst all of this, let’s look at the schedule and venue of Liverpool matches in the FIFA club world cup 2019.
