Giants Reportedly “Would Have Serious Interest” In Jason Garrett If Not Retained By Cowboys (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 15:09 by Darrelle Lincoln

If The Dallas Cowboys falter for the remainder of the season and don’t accomplish something bigger in the postseason, Jason Garrett could seriously find himself on the outside looking in.

That may not necessarily mean he would be out of the NFC East because another team seems interested in his services.

On Sunday’s NFL GameDay morning show, Ian Rapoport of NFL Network dropped a damning report that stated the New York Giants would interested in Jason Garrett.
