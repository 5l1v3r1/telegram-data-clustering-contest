The Walking Dead: Third Series' Title Revealed in New Promo

24 Nov 2019, 22:11 by Noah Dominguez

Nearly two months after its first trailer was revealed, the the third scripted series to be released as part of AMC's Walking Dead Universe finally has its official title.

A new promo for the series has been released by AMC through the official Rotten Tomatoes Twitter page. The promo briefly highlights the new Walking Dead show's characters, as well as the challenges they will encounter on their journey, before concluding with the reveal of its name: The Walking Dead: World Beyond.
