YOUNG MUM TRAGEDY

Glasgow dad’s anguish as ‘beautiful’ daughter, 26, dies suddenly giving birth to baby boy

24 Nov 2019, 22:07 by Connor Boylan

A HEARTBROKEN dad has paid an anguished tribute to his “beautiful” daughter who tragically died after giving birth.

Stacy Louise Joyce, 26, from Drumchapel, Glasgow, passed away after giving birth to a baby boy on Saturday.

Distraught dad John Joyce shared the devastating news on social media as he led tributes to his “princess”.

John Joyce posted: “How the f**k can this world be so f*****g cruel with such sad devastating news.

"Our world has come crashing down like we as a whole family could never imagine.

"Our daughter, sister, granddaughter, niece, cousin, mummy, Stacey Louise Joyce was sadly taken from us while giving birth to a perfect wee, over eight pounds, healthy baby boy.

"We promise you one thing, Stacy, that wee stunner will be looked after like you'll never know.

"I promise you that with every breath I'll ever take.

"Fly high my princess.

"Words will never describe how we're all feeling or will ever feel again.

 

"Love you more than you'll ever know."

In another post, John added: "How can someone so special give me this perfect grandson and leave us with so much devastation Stacy???

"You'll never believe how much I miss you already n love this wee guy so much things will never ever be the same. How the f*** does anybody deal with this??? Please give your mum, son, brother, sister the strength to get through this nightmare."

And shattered friends and family posted heartfelt messages of support, describing Stacy as “beautiful inside and out”.

Sarah Jane Smith said: "Stacy Louise Joyce thinking of your baby boy and family at this heartbreaking time.

"RIP you were the most beautiful girl inside and out."

Kimberly Hall said: "My thoughts, love and prayers to John Joyce and the whole family on a day which should be the best day, ever becoming grandparents and gaining a new family member, to losing their child."
