The Weeknd Teases New Single “Blinding Lights”

24 Nov 2019, 22:38 by Caitlin Kelley

The Weeknd just teased his upcoming single, “Blinding Lights”—and it appears that the song will drop on Black Friday.

A snippet of the new track soundtracked a Mercedez-Benz commercial in Germany. By the end of the clip, the date Nov. 29 appears on the dashboard of the EQC electric SUV.
