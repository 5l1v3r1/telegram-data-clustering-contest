I'm A Celeb's Jacqueline traumatised and in floods of tears over Bushtucker Trial

The mum-of-two cried her eyes out after it was announced she would face the Crevice of Cruelty trial alongside Arsenal legend Ian Wright

24 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Irishmirror.ie

Jacqueline Jossa couldn't fight back her disappointment as she broke down in floods of tears after learning she would face the Crevice Of Cruelty Bushtucker Trial during tonight's episode of I'm A Celebrity .

The mum-of-two was seen bursting into tears and crying out "I don't want to do it anymore man" as legendary TV duo Ant and Dec revealed Jacqueline and Ian Wright were selected by the public to attempt the latest trial.

Soap star Jacqueline, 27, appeared totally devastated at the news and insisted she didn't want to do it while loudly crying as the campmates surrounded her.

Fellow campmate Andrew Maxwell commented on the soap star's upset and scared reaction.

He said: "Jacqueline was shook when she got the news she was nominated again.

"There is literally nothing you can say that will make her feel better, frankly you will annoy her, or by you I mean me, I annoy her."

Jacqueline cried her eyes out before admitting that she "worries and thinks why are people voting me".

The emotional star told the Bush Telegraph that there is only so much you can do before you break and "I hope I don't break".

The mum-of-two then enjoyed a pep talk from hunky camp leader James Haskell, who encouraged her to try her best in the trial

The actress recollected herself and told the cameras: "I took myself off and had a little moment.

I looked at my kids in my pillow and that's the reason I'm doing this."

While Jacqueline revealed she felt better with Ian by her side, the duo agreed to give the Bushtucker trial a go.

Ant and Dec made a hilarious joke at Jacqueline's emotional reaction to being selected by sarcastically saying she was 'subtle' in showing her emotions .

Viewers instantly took to Twitter to comment on Jacqueline's floods of tears prior to doing the Bushtucker Trial.

One wrote: "They probably filled the trial up with Jacqueline's tears."

While another shared: "Why does Jacqueline get so upset over trials? She absolutely smashes them."

A third seemed sceptical: "I'm not having that Jacqueline is crying and sobbing about doing these tasks.... Clearly all an act as there's no evidence of it at all when she actually does them!"

Another agreed by writing: "This proves Jacqueline is playing up to the camera's. Constantly crying then smashing the trials".

As Jacqueline and Ian entered the Trials clearing, they spoke to Ant and Dec about why they thought they had been voted for the Trial.

Football legend Ian joked: "Because they love us", while Jacqueline simply giggled through the sensitive subject.

Ian continued by saying the public wanted to see people that they love "going through stuff" so they come through the other side and smile.
