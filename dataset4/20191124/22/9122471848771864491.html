Video: LSU QB Joe Burrow Is Excited For Saturday Night

24 Nov 2019, 22:12 by Andrew Holleran

LSU’s football program appears to be excited for Saturday night’s game against Texas A&M. The Tigers have already secured a spot in the SEC Championship Game, though this weekend’s contest against the Aggies will be personal.

The 2018 edition of the LSU-Texas A&M game was wild, with the Aggies winning in seven overtimes. Emotions were flying on the field following that game, too.

Ed Orgeron made it clear that the Tigers will be better this time around.

“There’s no question. There’s no question about that,” Orgeron said after LSU’s win over Arkansas. “It’s going to be on.”

“I’ll never forget that game last year. We’re going to be ready.”

Joe Burrow appears to be excited, too.
