Sevilla cruise to a 0-1 victory over Real Valladolid at José Zorrilla

Sevilla earned a valuable away win and three points to Real Valladolid at José Zorrilla on Sunday

24 Nov 2019, 22:14 by SPORT.es

Real Valladolid on Sunday lost to Sevilla on a home defeat at José Zorrilla. Real Valladolid wanted to improve their position in the tournament after losing against Alavés 3-0 while Sevilla came from winning 1-2 against Real Betis in the last match played. Following today's result, Real Valladolid and Sevilla occupy position 14th (17 points) and 3rd (27 points) respectively after playing a total of 14 matches.

Good start of the game for Sevilla, thanks to a goal from Ever Banega in the 13th minute, thereby seeing the first half to a 0-1.

There were no goals during the second half but the match ended on a 0-1 win.

As for substitutions, both coaches used full substitutions. For Valladolid, Toni Villa, Jorge De Frutos Sebastian and Enes Ünal came on for Pablo Hervias, Sandro Ramirez and Mohammed Salisu, while Sevilla brought on Oliver Torres, Franco Vazquez and Nemanja Gudelj to replace Nolito, Ever Banega and Jesus Navas.

The referee booked Jordi Masip, Nacho Martinez and Toni Villa from Valladolid, while for Sevilla Lucas Ocampos, Luuk De Jong and Fernando saw a yellow card and Lucas Ocampos (2 yellow cards) saw the red card.

Real Valladolid will play against Celta at away stadium, while Sevilla will face Leganés at home stadium.
