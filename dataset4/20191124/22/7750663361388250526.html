Sheffield United 3-3 Man Utd: How the two managers fared head to head

The spoils were shared as Chris Wilder's Sheffield United played out an entertaining 3-3 draw with Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's Manchester United at Bramall Lane.

24 Nov 2019, 22:05

The draw extends the Blades' unbeaten run to six Premier League matches and lifts Manchester United up to ninth in the table.

Here, the PA news agency takes a look at the head-to-head battle between the two managers.
