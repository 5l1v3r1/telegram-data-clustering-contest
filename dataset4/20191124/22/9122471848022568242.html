Browns Player Has Brutally Honest Comment On QB Mason Rudolph

24 Nov 2019, 22:13 by Matt Hladik

The Pittsburgh Steelers benched Mason Rudolph during today’s 16-10 win over the Cincinnati Bengals. It is unclear whether he or Devlin Hodges will start against the Browns next weekend.

At least one Cleveland player is hoping Rudolph keeps his job. Defensive tackle Sheldon Richardson was asked about Rudolph’s benching after the Browns’ game today.

He pulled no punches with his response.
