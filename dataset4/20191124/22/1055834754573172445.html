Video: Liam Robertson takes a look at unreleased Nintendo peripherals

24 Nov 2019, 22:10 by ssf1991

Nintendo is constantly experimenting with hardware. If they are not coming up with wacky things for their consoles, the company is experimenting with accessories and peripherals. Some of them make it to the market, but some do not. The latter is the focus for a new video by Liam Robertson, which was published on the Did You Know Gaming? YouTube channel.
