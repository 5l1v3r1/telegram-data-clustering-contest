I'm A Celeb's Andrew Maxwell gets in yet another spat as he slams 'shambolic' camp

Tensions threatened to boil over in camp as the camp was given the opportunity to win a treat with a multiple choice question, but Ian didn't like the way camp went about the task

24 Nov 2019, 22:29 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity saw resident melodrama king Andrew Maxwell find himself in yet another verbal sparring match this evening as tensions rose in camp.

The comedian found himself facing off against England rugby legend and newly elected Camp Leader James Haskell as camp voted on the answer to a question that could win them a treat.

As Kate Garraway and Roman Kemp were presented a choice between salted nuts and jelly beans at the Outback Shack, the rest of camp was asked a question that would win them one of the two.

Kisok Kev rang up camp and asked them: "What percentage of adults do not trust politicians?

"A - 59% or B - 79%?"

James called for a show of hands for each, and the camp went for 79%, despite Andrew insisting: "Not everyone put their hands up."

"They did," James snapped back.

At that point, Andrew threw another sulk and moaned: "It's just as shambolic as ever. We're not getting chocolate."

Cliff tried to cheer up the atmosphere by giving his rendition of musical Frozen's iconic track Let It Go.

Speaking in the Bus Telegraph, James confided: "Everyone's tired, everyone's a bit chippy.

"Everyone's had a long day, everyone's had a long day. Things happen.

"I'm just mortified if [Andrew] think I just closed him down."

The pair cleared the air during a face to face confrontation, and hugged it out.

"I said my piece," Andrew said in the Bush Telegraph, "Everyone said their piece and big James gave me a hug. Job done."

Roman and Kate humbled Andrew further when they came back with the bowl of jelly beans.

"Thank God for Adele," he said, "Thank God people didn't listen to me."

He also clashed with Ian Wright as he accused the former footballer of shouting at him.

Roman confessed he was confused by the whole situation and why the pair were snapping at eachother.

"I'm sorry," he told Ian after, "I'm really tired. I'm sorry. I don't know."

They gave each other a squeeze as Ian threatened he might shed some more tears.

Tonight's episode also saw Jacqueline Jossa squeal with delight as Caitlyn Jenner compared her to her daughter Kylie Jenner .

"So how cool is that?" she gushed, "Caitlyn Jenner just casually said, 'Oh, you remind me of Kylie.'

"Winning."
