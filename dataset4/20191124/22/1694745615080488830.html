64 Sex-Filled Films to Stream on Netflix For a Steamy Night In

24 Nov 2019, 22:55 by Tara Block

We've found the sexiest movies on Netflix in 2019 for when you want some hot, steamy onscreen lovin' that's not porn. There are racy foreign flicks, sexy romantic comedies, erotic period pieces, and everything in between (the sheets) — perfect for solo watching or date night. Whether you're in the mood to appreciate your favorite sexy actor or enjoy a hot and heavy romance, hop into bed with one of these raunchy movies tonight. Fair warning: they bring the heat!

Looking for even more Netflix recs? Check out more movies and TV shows that are available on Netflix in November here.

— Additional reporting by Lauren Harano, Corinne Sullivan, and Hannah Abrams

1 Compulsion

When an erotic novelist's ex-boyfriend invites her to an Italian villa, she finds him there with another woman and quickly starts falling for her. Compulsion, from 2016, centers around love, lies, and ultrasteamy encounters.

2 High Society

The 2018 movie High Society follows a couple who will do whatever it takes to become part of the elite community. Their race to the top includes sex, betrayal, and so much more.

3 Immoral Tales

Immoral Tales, a French film from 1973, tells four different erotic stories that involve everything from the loss of purity to the dark depths of incest.

4 After

The 2019 romantic drama After follows the life of Tessa Young, a freshman in college. After she meets an alluring man named Hardin, their romantic relationship takes twists and turns she never saw coming.

5 The Lake House

Starring Keanu Reeves, Sandra Bullock, and Christopher Plummer, this movie is already off to a good start. The Lake House centers around an architect and a doctor who lived in his house two years prior. The two meet through letters left in the mailbox at the lake house they have both lived in at separate points in time.

6 Lovesong

The romantic film Lovesong tells the story of two best friends who experience an intimate moment together one night while on a road trip. After years pass, they are forced to face their feelings, and each other.

7 A Perfect Ending

A Perfect Ending is the story of Rebecca, an older woman who is in search of her first orgasm. When she meets a call girl, she finds that as well as a whole new meaning of self.

8 Sorry Angel

Sorry Angel is set in France in 1933 and follows Jacques (Pierre Deladonchamps), a single father in his late 30s who has a sour outlook on life, and Arthur (Vincent Lacoste), an aspiring filmmaker in his early 20s, who together explore a new sexual relationship and ultimately a new way to live their lives as one.

9 Enemy

When Jake Gyllenhaal finds an actor who looks just like him, he takes a deep dive into the other man's personal affairs in this 2013 movie, Enemy.

10 Goodbye Lover

Patricia Arquette may look like a nice girl next door, but in Goodbye Lover she is much naughtier than that. Need we say more?

11 Nymphomaniac: Volume I and II

Director Lars von Trier filmed his actors — which included Shia LaBeouf, Uma Thurman, and Charlotte Gainsbourg, among others — having actual sex in his two-part drama art film Nymphomaniac: Volume I and Nymphomaniac: Volume II. The films follow a self-diagnosed nymphomaniac, so naturally they include many graphic sex scenes.

12 Concussion

Concussion tells the steamy story of a housewife who feels trapped in her marriage and develops a new interest in female sex workers.

13 Zipper

A prosecutor could lose everything as he falls further into the world of high-class escorts in the political thriller Zipper.

14 The Exception

The Exception follows a German solider during World War II who is on a mission to find out if spies are collecting information on the old monarch. While he is on his assignment, he finds himself falling for a young Jewish housekeeper, and steamy scenes ensue.

15 Blue Valentine

Ryan Gosling and Michelle Williams have plenty of hot and heavy scenes together in the sad tale of love falling apart Blue Valentine.

16 God's Own Country

You heard plenty of buzz about Call Me by Your Name in 2017, but you may not have heard about its very indie sibling, God's Own Country. It's a beautiful, captivating look at one man's struggle with his sexuality, and it earned a directing award at Sundance, as well as a BAFTA nomination.

17 Y Tu Mamá También

In Y Tu Mamá También, two teens take an older woman on a road trip — sexual excapades ensue.

18 Below Her Mouth

An engaged fashion editor determinedly pursues a lovelorn roofer, and their salacious affair blooms into something more in Below Her Mouth.

19 Blue Is the Warmest Color

Blue Is the Warmest Color is a sexual coming-of-age story about two teen girls falling in love. With its NC-17 rating, it's no surprise there are some very graphic lesbian sex scenes.

20 Newness

The world would be a better place if more movies starred a sexy Nicholas Hoult. Newness, set in Los Angeles, tells the story of two millennials trying to find their way in the app-driven dating world.

21 Sex Doll

Sex Doll is a 2016 erotic thriller about a high-end French escort in London who falls for a man trying to help her escape the business.

22 Lust Stories

Love, lust, sex, and modern relationships are all explored in the 2018 film Lust Stories from the viewpoint of four Indian women.

23 People You May Know

When a wife becomes pregnant by her husband's gay best friend, People You May Know only just begins to heat up.

24 The Lives of Others

This German Oscar-winning drama, The Lives of Others, tells the story of a dedicated Stasi officer who decides to spy on a playwright and his famous actress girlfriend when he doubts their loyalty to the Communist party, but the longer the officer watches them, the more he warms to them — a sentiment that becomes complicated when his superior orders him to eliminate the playwright so he can have the beautiful actress for himself.

25 Across the Universe

Starring Rachel Evan Wood and Jim Sturgess as Lucy and Jude, the trippy Beatles-themed musical romance Across the Universe places two star-crossed lovers in the midst of the Vietnam War, the turbulence of the late 1960s, and the most incredible soundtrack you'll ever hear.

26 Battle

If you can't get enough of Step Up and Save the Last Dance, then you'll love Battle. This new movie is about an affluent dancer whose father's bankruptcy means the end of her formal dance training — and her introduction to a hip-hop dancer who wants to teach her all new moves.

27 American Honey

The dreamy romance American Honey revolves around Star (played by Sasha Lane), a young woman who runs away from her troubled home life to drive across the Midwest, and Jake (played by Shia LaBeouf), the hard-partying guy who lures Star into a world of debauchery.

28 The Ugly Truth

Katherine Heigl stars in this rom-com, The Ugly Truth, as an unlucky-in-love TV producer named Abby, who's desperate to woo the sexy doctor who just moved next door, while Gerald Butler costars as the crass TV celebrity who's determined to help Abby land the doctor by proving just how little she understands about men.

29 Indiscretion

Steamy crime drama Indiscretion stars Mira Sorvino as Veronica, a politician's wife who thinks she's just engaging in a harmless weekend fling with a tortured young artist, but instead she becomes the target of an insanely intense obsession that slowly infiltrates her life.

30 Ex Machina

In perhaps one of the sexiest modern sci-fi films, Ex Machina portrays Tomb Raider star Alicia Vikander and Crazy Rich Asians actress, model, and ballerina Sonoya Mizuno as two female humanoid robots desperate to experience the outside world, even if that means killing their creator.

31 Berlin Syndrome

You'll never want to date again after watching Berlin Syndrome. The psychological thriller follows a young Australian backpacker (Teresa Palmer) exploring Berlin on a solo trip who decides to join a sexy local at his place for a one-night stand. It all seems to be going well — until the next morning, when she discovers that her new Berlin lover has no intention of letting her leave.

32 Perfect Stranger

When a journalist goes undercover to find evidence that a suspicious advertising executive killed her best friend, she finds herself in the middle of a scheme more complicated than she had ever imagined, but here's what we never imagined: that Halle Berry and Bruce Willis would have such steamy onscreen chemistry in Perfect Stranger.

33 All the Boys Love Mandy Lane

Starring Amber Heard, this teen slasher film All the Boys Love Mandy Lane is about a high-schooler who reluctantly accepts an invitation to a rural weekend party, only to find her many male suitors murdered one by one.

34 In Darkness

Game of Thrones hottie Natalie Dormer both wrote and stars in 2018's In darkness, a thriller about a blind pianist who accidentally finds herself witness to a murder that takes place in the apartment above hers. She's also joined by Emily Ratajkowski as her neighbor, who even makes getting murdered look hot.

35 The Aviator

The Aviator depicts the early years of director and aviation tycoon Howard Hughes (played by Leonardo DiCaprio) who hides his dark side with his successful public persona. Cate Blanchett and Kate Beckinsale round out the cast as Hughes's lovers Katharine Hepburn and Ava Gardner.

36 Someone Great

Starring Jane the Virgin's Gina Rodriguez, Pitch Perfect's Brittany Snow, and She's Gotta Have It's DeWanda Wise, Someone Great tells the story of aspiring music journalist Jenny (played by Rodriguez), whose boyfriend of nine years unceremoniously dumps her as soon as she lands her dream job in San Francisco because he's not willing to make long distance work. However, her two BFFs, Blair and Erin (played by Snow and Wise, respectively), are there to help her get over her ex and give her one last crazy (and sexy) weekend in New York City before she moves across the country.

37 Mother and Child

Don't let the title Mother and Child fool you. This steamy (and emotional) movie ties in family, adoption, and some sexy scenes.

38 Studio 54

Not to be confused with the late-'90s flick 54 starring Ryan Phillippe, Salma Hayek, and Neve Campbell, this documentary-style movie Studio 54 offers an inside look at the real Studio 54, the pleasure-seeking epicenter of 1970s New York City that would become known as the greatest (and sexiest) nightclub of all time.

39 Porto

When an American expat and a French student share a steamy encounter together, they are haunted by the sexual connection they share years later. What happened in Porto sure didn't stay there.

40 Sleeping With Other People

In the film Sleeping With Other People, when Jason Sudeikis and Alison Brie run into each other twelve years after having a one-night stand, they try to keep a platonic relationship while still having a sexual connection.

41 The Black Room

The Black Room is a chillingly sexual movie which revolves around an evil and (very) lustful spirit living in a couple's new home. Watch it only if you think you can handle it.

42 Two Lovers and a Bear

When two burning souls come together near the North Pole, Two Lovers and a Bear makes the snowy setting so much hotter.

43 Under the Skin

The sexy extraterrestrial Scarlett Johansson disguises herself and preys on men in the steamy and out of this world film Under the Skin.

44 Winter of Our Dreams

When a bookstore owner finds an unlikely friend in a heroin-addicted sex worker, he finds himself drawn to the troubled woman in the 1981 movie Winter of Our Dreams.

45 Carol

When a wealthy married woman develops a forbidden affair with a female department store clerk, a complicated love affair ensues in the 2015 movie Carol.

46 Loving Annabelle

After Annabelle is sent to a Roman Catholic school for bad behavior, she develops a scandalous relationship with her female poetry teacher. A feverish affair makes Loving Annabelle a hot, hot flick.

47 The New Romantic

Best known for playing Alyssa in The End of the F***ing World, Jessica Barden stars in The New Romantic as a journalism student in need of some inspiration (and some cash) who decides to masquerade as a sugar baby for a wealthy older man, hoping the resulting story will earn her a win in an upcoming journalism competition. Luckily, Camila Mendes is happy to show her the ropes.

48 Last Night

Keira Knightley and Sam Worthington star in this lustful drama where they are both tempted one night when they are apart. See if there marriage will make it or if their night away will ruin everything in Last Night.

49 White Girl

When a wild college freshman meets a drug dealer, she almost loses it all in the romantic drama White Girl.

50 Princess Cyd

When a 16-year-old girl visits her aunt in Chicago for the summer, she meets another girl who flips her world upside in the movie Princess Cyd.

51 Desire

The 2017 movie Desire is an erotic thriller in which a woman falls in love with the one man off limits. Her sister's fiance.

52 Alex Strangelove

In the romantic drama Alex Strangelove, a young boy who has planned on losing his virginity to his girlfriend is taken by surprise when he develops a romantic interest in a charming boy.

53 The Model

When an aspiring model falls for a photographer, things become more than they can handle. Watch how a love affair becomes a lustful obsession in The Model.

54 The Graduate

A college graduate gets seduced by a bored housewife and then falls for her daughter in this dramatic and sexy 1967 movie The Graduate.

55 The Tree of Blood

In The Tree of Blood, a young couple visits an old farmhouse that belonged to the woman's family, and it is there that they discover their shared genealogical roots and family histories, among other things. This steamy Spanish movie will heat up any night.

56 One Day

One Day stars Jim Sturgess and Anne Hathaway, who fall in love and make this movie so steamy.

57 Cam

This psychological horror film Cam is set in the world of webcam pornography where an erotic cam girl who finds her popular channel hijacked by a look-alike.

58 Her

A lonely writer develops a steamy relationship with an artificial entity in this futuristic film. Her will change the way you interact with technology.

59 An Education

In An Education, a sheltered teen gets charmed by a much older suitor, and things get hot and heavy.

60 Us and Them

Us and Them follows the love story between two strangers who meet on a train and have a serious bond afterwards.

61 Dare to Be Wild

Dare to Be Wild is based on the true story of an Irish landscaper who gets a chance to compete at the prestigious Chelsea Flower Show and then falls in love with a sexy builder while she is there.

62 Nights in Rodanthe

Adrienne Willis (Diane Lane) is living a life of chaos when she decides to help run a friend's inn in a small town in North Carolina. When a storm hits, she and Dr. Paul Flanner (Richard Gere), the only guest who is staying at the inn that weekend, find themselves in a whirlwind romance that changes both of their lives forever in Nights in Rodanthe.

63 Who's That Knocking at My Door

The 1967 drama Who's That Knocking at My Door tells the story of a tumultuous relationship between a couple who must try to understand how to fully love each other, despite what has happened to them.

64 Love

Love, a 2015 French film, is pure sex, literally. And in 3D, no less! It follows a twisty, hypersexualized relationship.
