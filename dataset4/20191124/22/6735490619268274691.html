Taylor Swift Performs Medley Of Her Songs Before Accepting Artist Of The Decade

24 Nov 2019, 22:25 by Jamie Samhan

It was the performance that almost didn’t happen and was certainly heard around the world as things got heated before the American Music Awards.

In short, Swift’s former label Big Machine Records wouldn’t let her play her songs that she had recorded while she was with them. An agreement was eventually reached but not without some drama.

And thank goodness an agreement was found because she rocked the stage singing “The Man”, “Lover”, “Blank Space”, “Shake It Off”, “Love Story” and “I Knew You Were Trouble”.
