Ciara’s Kids Have The Cutest Response To Her Epic American Music Awards Performance

24 Nov 2019, 22:02 by Aynslee Darmon

Ciara had two of the cutest fans in the American Music Awards audience Sunday night.

The singer, who also acted as host for the 2019 awards show, took the stage to perform her hit “Melanin”, joined by La La Anthony, and brought the entire crowd to their feet.

But after her performance, she jumped off stage to chat with her kids.

“As a performer, CEO, wife and mom, Future, how did I do?” she asked her son. “Good,” he responded while in the audience. Daughter Sienna agreed, she also gave two thumbs up while shouting “good” into her mom’s microphone.

“Awww, thank you so much my babies,” Ciara later gushed. “I’m here to tell you that women can do it all.”

Ciara shares son Future, 5, with ex Future and daughter Sienna, 2, with Seattle Seahawks superstar Russell Wilson.
