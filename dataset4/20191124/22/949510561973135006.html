Special Weather Statement issued November 24 at 10:25PM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

24 Nov 2019, 22:25

Special Weather Statement: Northern Wayne, Otsego, Broome and 4 more areas until 7:00 AM

Temperatures have dropped below freezing in many areas this
evening. This may cause any moisture on untreated roads and
walkways to freeze resulting in slippery conditions. If you will
be out overnight, watch for icy patches on roads and sidewalks,
especially over the higher elevations and in rural areas.
