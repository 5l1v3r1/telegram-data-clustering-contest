Watch: Trisha Yearwood delivers heartbreaking performance of 'Love You Anyway' on 'Good Morning America'

24 Nov 2019, 02:31 by Brandy McDonnell

Country music star and former Owasso resident Trisha Yearwood appeared on "Good Morning America" this week to chat about her new album, "Every Girl," as well her upcoming hosting gig on the "CMA Country Christmas."

As previously reported, Yearwood will host the 10th annual “CMA Country Christmas,” which will air at 8 p.m. Tuesday, Dec. 3 on ABC.
