Urban Meyer Has Heartfelt Message For Buckeye Nation

24 Nov 2019, 02:02 by Dan Lyons

Today was a pretty special day in Columbus. The eyes of college football turned on the city ahead of this weekend’s biggest game, Ohio State vs. Penn State. It also featured the return of a few notable former Buckeyes, including national championship-winning head coach Urban Meyer, who is in his first year as an analyst for FOX.

ESPN’s College GameDay was on hand to preview the game. Former Buckeye Kirk Herbstreit said that Ohio State fans made it an incredible atmosphere for the show.

Columbus also played host to FOX’s big new pregame show. Before the network’s “Big Noon Saturday” presentation of the game, Meyer and company previewed the game. He had a message for Buckeye fans very similar to Herbstreit’s.
