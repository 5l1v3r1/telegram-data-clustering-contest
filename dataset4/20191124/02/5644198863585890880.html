US and China can still clinch phase 1 deal by end year: US national security adviser

24 Nov 2019, 02:10

HALIFAX, Nova Scotia: United States national security adviser Robert O'Brien said on Saturday (Nov 23) that a phase 1 trade agreement with China is still possible by the end of the year, while warning that President Donald Trump would not turn a blind eye to what happens in Hong Kong.

"We were hoping to have (a phase 1) deal done by the end of the year. I still think that's possible," O'Brien told reporters at a security conference in Halifax.
