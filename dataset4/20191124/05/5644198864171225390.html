Elon Musk: About 150,000 orders thus far for Tesla Cybertruck

24 Nov 2019, 05:30

REUTERS: Tesla Inc Chief Executive Elon Musk said on Saturday (Nov 23) that there have been about 150,000 orders thus far for the electric carmaker's Cybertruck, which was unveiled on Friday.

"146k Cybertruck orders so far, with 42 per cent choosing dual, 41 per cent tri & 17 per cent single motor", Musk said in a tweet, adding separately that the orders were achieved without any advertising or paid endorsements.
