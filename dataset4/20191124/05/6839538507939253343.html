Woman, children missing from Sunshine Coast

24 Nov 2019, 05:35

An urgent public appeal has been issued for help to find a woman who has disappeared with her two girls.

The 32-year-old woman and her two daughters, aged two and three, left a Maltman St address around 9am on Saturday.

The three have not been seen or heard of since and police have expressed concerns for their safety and wellbeing.

The woman is described as Caucasian, 170cm tall, proportionate build with dark hair and brown eyes.

The three-year-old girl is described as Caucasian, 110cm tall, slim build with blue eyes and long curly blonde hair.

The two-year-old girl is described as Caucasian, 80cm tall, slim build with brown eyes and short light brown curly hair.

Police believe the woman often visits the Caloundra, Kallangur and Caboolture areas.

Police urge anyone who has information regarding the whereabouts of the woman and the children to immediately contact police.
