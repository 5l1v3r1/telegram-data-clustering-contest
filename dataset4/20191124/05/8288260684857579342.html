You could own two homes for £40k in Great Barr - and they're next door to each other

The semi-detached properties, located on Ennerdale Road, are in walking distance of a primary school and Hamstead train station

24 Nov 2019, 05:30 by Stephanie Balloo, https://www.facebook.com/MissBalloo/

You could own TWO three-bedroom homes for less than a fifth of the price of an average city home.

A pair of adjoining semi-detached properties, located on Ennerdale Road in Great Barr, could both be yours for just £40,000 - in theory.

The vacant freehold houses are currently listed on Rightmove and Zoopla for a bargain guide price of £20,000 each.

Set back from the road and situated near the junction of Langdale Road, the homes are in walking distance of St Mark's Catholic Primary School and Hamstead train station.

Buyers will have the opportunity to bid on the houses when they go to auction next month.

House 1 (left)

The home on the left of the image boasts a downstairs toilet, a reasonably-sized kitchen diner, a front garden and a driveway on the ground floor.

Upstairs, a landing leads off to three bedrooms and a bathroom with a second toilet fitted.

With dated carpets and wallpaper in some rooms, it's in need of a little TLC.

But it could prove a wise investment with nearby three-bed homes up for sale for as much as £250,000.

The Rightmove mortgage calculator estimates monthly payments of just £80 with a £2,000 deposit on the property.

The advert adds: "The property benefits from having a gas fired central heating system and UPVC double glazing."

House 2 (right)

The adjoining property also has three bedrooms and a bathroom stemming from a landing upstairs, but the Zoopla advert boasts a front and rear garden.

It is said to have two reception rooms, a rear lobby, a downstairs toilet, a hall and a kitchen downstairs.

Both properties will be up for auction on December 11, 2019 at the Holte Suite, Aston Villa FC, Trinity Rd, Birmingham B6 6HE.
