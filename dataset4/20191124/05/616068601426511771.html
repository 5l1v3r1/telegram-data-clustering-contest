Affordable housing help?

24 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Chelsea Powrie

Oliver town council will be considering waiving or reducing the development cost charges associated with an affordable housing complex near the airport. 

The proposed affordable housing project at 5931 Airport Street is requesting a waiving or reduction of the $225,000 developmental cost charges payable at time of building permit issuance. 

Council will discuss Monday whether to waive the DCCs for the proposed complex or provide a discount, given that they have requested reduced fees in their capacity as a low income housing project. 

The Airport Street development is set to commence work in early 2020, prior to the adoption of the new DCC bylaw in mid to late 2020.

Council will have the chance to weigh in on the matter this week.

A staff report states they will be looking at ways to ensure an agreement " would ensure that the form of tenure of the housing units are limited to rental tenure," to contribute to solutions to the ongoing housing crisis. 

Oliver council will discuss the issue at Monday's meeting.
