NXT TakeOver: WarGames 2019 results

24 Nov 2019, 05:12 by WWE.com

Check out full WWE NXT TakeOver: WarGames 2019 results below. Streaming live from Allstate Arena in Chicago, Ill., the WWE Network special event features two WarGames Matches, including a Women’s WarGames Match for the first time ever, as well as Finn Bálor return to NXT action and a match to determine who will challenge NXT Champion Adam Cole at Survivor Series.
