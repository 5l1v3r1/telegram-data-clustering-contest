Vietnam captain Que Ngoc Hai linked with transfer to Thai League 1 giants

23 Nov 2019, 02:37

Vietnam international defender Que Ngoc Hai is currently in the rumour books and has been linked with a sensational move to Thai League 1 ahead of the next season.

The 26-year-old has 48 international caps for Vietnam and has scored three goals for the Golden Dragons including in their 3-1 win over Indonesia in the 2022 FIFA World Cup Qualifiers last month.
