Mumbai: Cop among first to enter Taj during 26/11 dies

Kakde had gone for a run at Marine Drive to prepare for an upcoming marathon Friday morning when he collapsed.

23 Nov 2019, 02:24 by Express News Service

Nitin Kakde (45), an assistant police inspector who was among the first to enter the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel during the 26/11 terror attacks in 2008, died from a heart attack on Friday.

Kakde had gone for a run at Marine Drive to prepare for an upcoming marathon Friday morning when he collapsed.

He was taken to a nearby hospital where he was declared dead. Posted at the LT Marg police station, Kakde had received a gallantry medal from the President. He is survived by his wife and two children.
