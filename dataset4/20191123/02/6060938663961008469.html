From 'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior' trailer to Ranu Mondal's fake make-up picture: Here are the news-makers of this week

23 Nov 2019, 02:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

As we all know there is no dull day in B-town. Like always this week also turned out to be one of the most happening weeks of the year. From many new promising trailer launches, special birthdays and interesting updates of celebrities' life, this past week had everything just like some masala movie.

The week started with the trailer launch of Akshay Kumar and Kareena Kapoor's light-hearted film, 'Good Newwz'. The trailer was well-received by the audiences and its light comedy and an appealing unique concept has impressed the audience. Then came the trailer launch of Ajay Devgn's magnum opus film, 'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior'. The trailer received praising from all over the nation and how. The grandeur of the movie, the scale and the VFX along with the outstanding star cast has heightened the excitement of the masses to witness this period drama at the earliest.

Next in the news came the singing sensation Ranu Mondal, Ranu left netizens shocked after she went for a makeover. As soon as her pictures hit the internet, Twitterati started trolling Ranu. Moreover Ranu's makeover led to a hilarious meme fest, later in the week an make-up artiste who did her make-up came to her rescue with sharing her real makeover picture. Not only Ranu, but Ajay Devgn and Kajol's daughter Nysa also got trolled over her dressing sense.

B-town was also hit by a sad news as Bollywood's celebrity designer Manish Malhotra lost his father early this week. B-town extended their condolences for the family.

Later in the week, Arjun Rampal and Mehr Jesias were granted divorce by the court on Tuesday to finally part their ways after 21 years of their marriage.

One more news that caught major eyeballs this week was of Arpita Khan delivering her second child on her brother Salman Khan's birthday, i.e on December 27.

One of the much awaited songs from 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' was released by the star cast of the film and the song received mixed reviews from the audiences. Finally on Friday, heartthrob Kartik Aaryan celebrated his birthday with his parents.
