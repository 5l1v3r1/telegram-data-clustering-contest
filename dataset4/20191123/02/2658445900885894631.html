Southern Sun marks anniversary - The Nation Newspaper

23 Nov 2019, 02:52

Activities have been lined up to mark the 10th anniversary of one the leading hotels in Nigeria, Southern Sun Hotel, Ikoyi,  Lagos. The celebration will kick off with a VIP cocktail next Wednesday,  November 19, 2019. There will also be a golf tournament at the prestigious Lakowe Golf course and Resort on Friday, November 22.

Southern Sun Hotel opened its doors to customers in 2009 and has within the last ten years built a strong reputation for quality hospitality service delivery.
