B&M selling a yard of Jägermeister with 10 mini bottles for less than £10

Bargain shop B&M is selling the herbal liqueur in a pack of 10 miniatures as a perfect party gift this Christmas. For less than £10, you can get an equivalent of a 200ml bottle

23 Nov 2019, 02:57 by Tiffany Lo

Shot-lovers can now get their hands full with a 'yard of Jäger' - for under a tenner.

Bargain shop B&M is selling a pack of 10 miniature Jägermeister with each bottle containing 20ml of the digestif.

The famous German liqueur was spotted by a Facebook page 'Money Saver Online' on Thursday.

The post has soon attracted over 17,000 comments as web users claimed their 'Christmas presents sorted'.

One said: "I am going there on Friday/ Saturday if anyone wants any."

Another one wrote: "Bargain...stock up on these miniatures for wedding favours eh."

A third said: "That's your Christmas pressies sorted."

But some frugal web users did the calculations and said it's a money trap.

"Don't get mugged off! Clearly says 10 x 2cl minis. That's 200ml for a tenner when a 700ml bottle's less than £20!" one commented.

One said: "Got this last year they r only small bottles got four drunks out of it. I rather buy a big bottle lasts longer."

Waitrose is selling a 50cl (500ml) Jägermeister for £13 and Sainsbury's is selling a 70cl (700ml) one for £16.

The same product is also available at Jagershop and Bargain Booze for the same price.

Apparently, this is not the first time the spirit company launched the product during festive seasons.

Jägermeister first posted the 'yard of Jägermeister' in December 2017 and it was priced under £10.

The herbal liqueur is made from 56 herbs and spices at a strength of 35 per cent alcohol by volume.
