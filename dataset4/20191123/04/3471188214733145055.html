BlueAnt has launched 2 new Bluetooth portable wireless speakers – the X1 and X2 speakers

23 Nov 2019, 04:01 by Alex Dennis

BlueAnt, an Aussie tech audio company has launched 2 new Bluetooth portable wireless speakers, the X1 and X2 speakers just in time for Christmas.

The new X-series Bluetooth speakers have been designed and made capable of producing bass in the same form factor as other similar larger speakers fail to deliver that are currently in the marketplace.

Taisen Maddern, CEO and Founder of BlueAnt has said:

“The X1 and X2 deliver sound you can literally see; the deep bass is evident the moment you turn the device on and holds through any listening experience,” and “If sound is what people are after, they can now turn to the X1 or X2 which deliver a premium experience at the best price point on the market.”

Spec wise, the two ranges of speakers are more alike than different, but there are some key differences, so lets have a look at what you get for the price for both :

Devices/Item X1 Bluetooth Speaker X2 Bluetooth Speaker
Wattage 14 watt 20 watt
Battery 10 hours 12 hours
Bluetooth Version 4.2 4.2
Audio play-back Options Bluetooth and Aux playback Bluetooth and Aux Playback
IP Water Rating IP56 splashproof IP56 splashproof
Audio Drivers/Radiators 2 x 40mm drivers, 2 x passive radiators 2 x 45mm drivers, 2 x passive radiators
Assistant Integration Siri/Google Integration Siri/Google Integration
Colours Available Black, Blue, Red Black, Blue, Red
Price $79.95 $99.95
Retail availability Officeworks, The Iconic, Amazon Officeworks, Amazon, The iconic

“We’re betting on bass and both the X1 and the X2 deliver exactly that: pure, deep bass in a form factor and design that can fit in anywhere they’re taken,”

As stated, the BlueAnt X1 and X2 Bluetooth portable speakers are available from Officeworks, The Iconic and Amazon for $79.95 for the X1 Speaker and $99.95 for the X2 Speakers right now.
