Edo is prepared for industries, says Obaseki

23 Nov 2019, 04:02 by Agency Report

The Edo State Governor, Mr Godwin Obaseki, has said his administration will not relent in transforming the state into a hub for industries, which will improve the economic fortunes of residents.

The governor, who was represented by Head, Edo State Investment Promotion Office, Mr Kelvin Uwaibi, said this while delivering a paper at the Equipment Leasing Association of Nigeria’s 17th National Lease Conference, themed ‘Promoting leasing for inclusive growth’.

He said the state government would continue to work to improve the ease of doing business, with policies aimed at creating a conducive and favourable business environment for foreign and local investors.

Obaseki said, “In the last three years, Edo State has created an enabling environment to attract investors.

“The state is Nigeria’s best-kept secret with an all-year-round climate for cultivation of crops, among other endowments.”

He added that Edo State was open for investors and businesses and would continue to improve the ease of doing business.
