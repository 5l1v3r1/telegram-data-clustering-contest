Heavy congestion on Gateway Motorway after two accidents

23 Nov 2019, 04:03

Two traffic accidents in less than hour have created heavy congestion in both directions on the Gateway Motorway this afternoon.

The first crash incident occurred just after 1pm when a bus hit a barrier at Boondall, heading northbound.

It was followed by two-vehicle crash in the southbound lanes in the same area about 1.40pm.

Police have asked motorists to avoid the area if possible.
