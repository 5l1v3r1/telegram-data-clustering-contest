Stone Cold opens up on WWE and AEW competing against each other

23 Nov 2019, 04:32 by Abhilash Mendhe

WWE Hall of Famer Stone Cold Steve Austin recently sat down with Sports Illustrated to promote the premiere of "Broken Skull Sessions", featuring WWE legend The Undertaker. Austin opened up on AEW and WWE competing against each other and stated that both promotions are in competition despite not admitting to the same.

AEW's arrival on the Pro Wrestling scene was met with excitement on the fans' end, as many had been clamoring for the same ever since WCW folded, back in 2001. AEW presented its first show, "Double Or Nothing" in May, and hasn't looked back since.

The company runs its own weekly show named AEW Dynamite, which goes head to head with WWE's NXT. This week was the first time since Dynamite's debut that NXT managed to trump the former in the ratings.
