Rumors of a Tom Sachs x NikeCraft Mars Yard 2.0 Restock Surfaces

Apparently featuring a slightly updated design.

23 Nov 2019, 04:44 by Nicolaus Li

Consistently standing as a sneakerhead favorite, the Tom Sachs x NikeCraft Mars Yard 2.0 is now rumored to be set for a restock.

Although no solid information has surfaced, sneaker insiders are noting that the restock will feature a slightly updated design and limited to 8000 pairs worldwide. Insiders are also saying that the redesign is enough for the re-release to receive a new model code. The model code note is also leading many to begin calling the release 3.0 iteration of the Tom Sachs x NikeCraft Mars Yard.

Rumored to be priced at $200 USD, stay tuned for more details regarding the rumored updated Tom Sachs x NikeCraft Mars Yard 2.0.
