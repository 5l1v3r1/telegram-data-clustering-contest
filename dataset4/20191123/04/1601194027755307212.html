Toni Storm reacts to appearing on SmackDown, trending on Twitter and Survivor Series

23 Nov 2019, 04:31 by Greg Bush

NXT introduces women's team?

On tonight's episode of SmackDown, NXT finally revealed one of their teams for Survivor Series. Up until now, the black and gold brand has kept their representatives secret. However, with one final showdown between the women of each brand taking place, it seems that Triple H and the rest of the brand felt that tonight was the best time to shock the world.

Not only was Rhea Ripley, one of the breakout stars of this storyline, named captain of the team, but all four of her allies accompanied her to the ring. Mia Yim, Tegan Nox, and Candice LeRae, three more of NXT's best and brightest, stood shoulder to shoulder with the Mosh Pit Kid. There was one final surprise, though, that took the WWE Universe by STORM!
