Mum of Billyjoe Bates urges Glasgow gangsters to stop protecting son's killer

Jackie Wilson has begged people in Milton, including gangland figures, to stop protecting Billyjoe’s murderer.

23 Nov 2019, 04:30 by Mark McGivern, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/mark-mcgivern/

A mother has issued an emotional appeal to catch her son’s killer on the first anniversary of his death.

Jackie Wilson yesterday begged people in Milton, Glasgow, including gangland figures, to stop protecting Billyjoe Bates’s murderer.

She said: “I am appealing to anyone who has information about the murder of my son to come forward, even if you think it’s irrelevant.

“After a year has passed, I still have no answers to why this has happened. How a coward can do this to someone and leave them lying dying is beyond me.

“I’m devastated and heartbroken.

“You know what you have done.

“Others must know too, so it’s time for people to stop protecting you.”

Seriously injured Billyjoe, 28, was found lying on Ashfield Street, where it meets Kippen Street, on November 18 last year. He died in hospital four days later.

Police have issued a CCTV image of a man buying cream soda and milk in a Londis shop close to the scene.

Officers believe he may have witnessed a key detail in the crime but stressed they aren’t connecting the shopper with Billyjoe’s death.

Reports have suggested the killer is a man known to Billyjoe and the knife attack happened after an altercation at a party.

It was claimed the killer had fled to Spain.

Crimestoppers, which can be contacted on 0800 555 111, has offere£10,000 reward for information leading to an arrest and conviction.
