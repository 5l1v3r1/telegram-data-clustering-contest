No. 16 Badgers fall behind early, can’t recover in loss to Minnesota

23 Nov 2019, 04:25 by FOX Sports Wisconsin

No. 16 Wisconsin gave up a pair of early goals and never recovered in a 4-1 loss to Minnesota on Friday night in Minneapolis.

The Gophers scored back-to-back goals in the early minutes of the first period to jump out to a 2-0 lead.

Sean Dhooghe got the Badgers on the board midway through the second period after a nice feed from Cole Caufield to make it a 2-1 game, but the Gophers responded with a pair of goals in the third to seal the win.

Daniel Lebedeff got the start in goal, making 25 saves.

The two teams meet for the border battle series finale on Saturday at 7 p.m. on FOX Sports Wisconsin.
