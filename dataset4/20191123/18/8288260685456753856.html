Roman Kemp's ludicrous body transformation before I'm A Celebrity - and how he managed it

The son of Spandau Ballet star Martin has become a favourite among viewers since the show launched last Sunday (November 17)

23 Nov 2019, 18:57 by James Rodger, Jessica Sansome, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Roman Kemp continues his I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! stint tonight, as the show airs on ITV1.

The son of Spandau Ballet star Martin has become a favourite among viewers since the show launched last Sunday (November 17).

And, as the Manchester Evening News reports , the 26-year-old went through a serious body transformation before heading Down Under.

The Capital FM Breakfast presenter spent 12-weeks dedicating himself to his health and fitness with the help of Caroline Flack's personal trainer, Bradley Simmonds.

Showcasing the fruits of his labour on Instagram, the radio host impressed his social media followers with before and after snaps.

He wrote on Instagram back in August: "Not one for posting a photo like this but I wanted to say a huge thank you to @bradleysimmonds for convincing me to do his #12weektransformation.

"Best training I've ever had and provided me with the results I wanted. Thank you mate."
