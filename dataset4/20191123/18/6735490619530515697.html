Meghan McCain Says The ‘Abuse And Threats’ Have Gone ‘Too Far’ As She Disables Instagram Comments

23 Nov 2019, 18:29 by Jamie Samhan

Meghan McCain has had enough.

“The View” co-host announced on Friday that she has disabled Instagram comments after receiving an excessive amount of “abuse and threats.”

“Turning off commenting on Instagram for the time being,” she said in a post. “I have family and young people in my life who can read it. You can disagree with my politics all you want but the abuse and threats is too far.”

As the daughter of U.S. senator John McCain, the TV host has very opinionated views on politics.

She frequently gets in arguments with her co-hosts, most recently over the impeachment hearing of Donald Trump or going head to head with Donald Trump Jr. when he was on the show.

Her Instagram posts are also dividing like her throwback photo of her prepping for the “zombie apocalypse” while at a shooting range.
