Rema returns to the stage reloaded

23 Nov 2019, 18:53 by Mary Nambwayo

By her own confession, she said she was a new Rema. "Sili muyembe (I am not a mango fruit). I am a new person." The show that took place in Namugongo,was her first since her glamorous introduction ceremony and honeymoon.
