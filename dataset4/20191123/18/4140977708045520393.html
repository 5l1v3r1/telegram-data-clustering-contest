Manchester City Vs Chelsea: Watch N’Golo Kante finish after splendid pass by Mateo Kovacic

23 Nov 2019, 18:25 by Tanish Chachra

Manchester City Vs Chelsea: Watch N’Golo Kante making a fine finish after a terrific pass from Mateo Kovacic to shock the Citizens.

Manchester City is having a crucial game against Chelsea, as Pep Guardiola’s side is slowly losing grip for the title race. Thus, this match is necessary for Manchester City to keep their fight alive.

Moreover, considering Chelsea is just one point ahead of Manchester City for the third spot, Manchester City has to win the game to advance in the table as they were dropped to 4th with a loss against Liverpool in the last game-week.

Amidst all of this, Chelsea shocked the defending champions in the 21st minute, after the Blues stunned them with an impressive finish by N’Golo Kante to make it 1-0 in the game.

But something which stood out in this goal was the ball provided by Mateo Kovacic, the Croatian midfielder sniped a long ball directly inside the 18-yard-box, which defeated the high-line defence of Manchester City, giving Kante enough room to defeat Ederson and Benjamin Mendy.
