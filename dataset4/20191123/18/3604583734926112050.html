Everything you need to know about Ballarat's springfest Market Sunday

23 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Jolyon Attwooll

What is it?

One of Ballarat's best known spring events, this takes place every year with around 530 stalls, including food, crafts, art and music, and much more. There are events and activities for all ages.

When is it?

It runs from 10am to 4pm on Lake Wendouree this Sunday (November 24).

Where is it?

Spread six kilometres all the way around the shores of Lake Wendouree.
