Bollywood stars mourn the demise of Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi

23 Nov 2019, 18:12 by Akash Nihalani

Veteran actress Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi passed away due to a cardiac arrest on Friday. While designer Manish Malhotra was one of the first to reach, many Bollywood stars from the industry like Amitabh Bachchan, Rekha, Urmila Matondkar, Prasoon Joshi, Vindu Dara Singh, Tabu, Rishi Kapoor too turned up to share their condolences with the family.
