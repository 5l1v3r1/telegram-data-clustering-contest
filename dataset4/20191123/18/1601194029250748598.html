Cody Rhodes reacts to WWE changing the Intercontinental Championship

23 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Nicky Pags

Cody Rhodes weighs-in on new WWE IC title

This week's episode of WWE SmackDown, the final episode of the show before this Sunday's WWE Survivor Series, featuring the somewhat shocking debut of not a new WWE Superstar, but a new version of the Intercontinental Championship belt.

The title is arguably one the most prestigious in pro wrestling history, and while the color of the belt strap has varied over the years, the plates have remained the same, and are considered legendary at this point.

All that changed on WWE SmackDown this week, when Sami Zayn awarded current IC Champion Shinsuke Nakamura with a new version of the belt, and the new title design has been met with mostly positive reviews from fans online, with some fans also reject the idea that WWE would change such a historic title belt.
