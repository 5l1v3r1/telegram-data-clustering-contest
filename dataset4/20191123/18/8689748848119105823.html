People Call Me And Ask Me To Divorce My Wife Because They Think I Don’t Deserve Her -Oteele Discloses(+Video)

23 Nov 2019, 18:09 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian comic actor, Yao Mawunyo popularly known as Oteele  has recounted how people who are envious of his marriage with his wife and as a result are advising him to divorce her.

According to Oteele in an interview with Adinkra Tv, most people wish for his marriage to collapse.

Oteele who tied the knot to his long time girlfriend has been constantly lambasted about his size and his “fooling”. His wife in a interview with Zionfelix sometime ago said that she was aware of her husband’s foolishness before agreeing to marry.

This clearly indicates that the bond between the young couple is stronger than criticism from the public.

Oteele  further noted that some people can’t hide their jealousy and hatred.

“it used to be spiritual warfare, but they couldn’t win, so now they attack us physically with a malicious, well-calculated plot to tarnish our reputation. What amuses me most is that I  didn’t become great by myself. People call and discourage me to divorce my wife.”
