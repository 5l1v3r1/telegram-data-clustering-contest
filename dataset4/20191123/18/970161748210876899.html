Katie Price pushes aside Emily Atack feud to enjoy day out with kids

23 Nov 2019, 18:11

Katie Price has put her feud with Emily Atack to the back of her mind in order to enjoy a day out with her kids.

That’s right, after hitting back at the Extra Camp host’s STI jibe, the 41-year-old has spent her Saturday out and about with her daughters Princess, 12, and Bunny, five.

Taking to Instagram, the proud mum shared a picture of her brood, who could be seen all wrapped up as they played with two dogs.

In the caption, the former glamour model wrote: ‘Saturdays with my girls, wearing their own range @princessandbunny.co.uk coats & hats.’

Fans were quick to comment and couldn’t help but coo over the little ones.

‘Bunny is getting so big,’ one wrote while another said: ‘Stunning girls. Princess has the best hair she could be in a commercial for Pantene.’

Someone else picked up on the fact the youngest was wearing pink fluffy slip ons, adding: ‘Bunny’s feet must be freezing.’

The star shares Bunny with her ex-husband Keiran Hayler, as well as the tot’s older brother, Jett.

Princess is from her previous marriage to Peter Andre, who she shares 14-year-old son Junior with. Katie is also mum to Harvey Price, 16, who she had while in a relationship with Dwight Yorke.

Katie’s post comes just one day after the mum-of-five took aim at Emily Atack after the actress made a remark about Katie’s sex life on I’m A celebrity’s sister show.

The Inbetweeners star joked that presenters Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas needed to get tested for diseases after plunging into the same pool Katie and peter Andre first kissed in.

And this didn’t sit right with the reality star, who shared a video on Instagram hitting back at the quip.

Threatening to come to the jungle herself, the 41-year-old spat at the camera: ‘So Emily Atack, you think it’s clever do you to slag me off? Who are you anyway?’

‘Because if you’ve got something to say don’t say it behind my back on telly say it to me. Because all the men you’ve been with don’t have good things to say about you.’
