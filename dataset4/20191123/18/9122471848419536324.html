Chase Young, Micah Parsons Got Into It At Halftime Of Ohio State vs. Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 18:46 by Matt Lombardi

About an hour before kickoff at Saturday’s Ohio State vs. Penn State matchup, players on both sides were seen jawing at midfield in anticipation of the contest. It doesn’t look like they’ve calmed down after one half of football.

FOX just aired video of Ohio State’s Chase Young and Penn State’s Micah Parsons going back-and-forth as the two teams headed to the tunnel for halftime. It looks like it was shut down before it got physical.

Young has been a monster for Ohio State thus far. The Buckeyes are shutting out the Nittany Lions to the tune of a 14-0 score.
