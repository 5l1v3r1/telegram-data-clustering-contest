A Frozen Christmas at Hong Kong Disneyland

23 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

The Fantasy Gardens at Hong Kong Disneyland turns into the magical kingdom of Arendelle this Yuletide season as the resort features a Frozen-themed winter wonderland. 

Elsa, Anna, and Olaf enchant guests amid twinkling lights, sparkling ornaments, glittering snowflakes, and shimmering trees at the Frozen Fantasy Gardens Village. 

Aside from hobnobbing with guests, Anna and Elsa appear at “A Magical Frozen Snowfall” show. 

“As dusk falls, a citizen of Arendelle will announce the arrival of Princess Anna. A series of performers will lead a royal processional for Anna and will then be joined by Olaf, who will make a comical arrival!

“Queen Elsa will join everyone and cast her magic to make beautiful snow magically fall. Guests will be mesmerized by Arendelle’s royal sisters and can capture winter-perfect moments,” HKDL said in a statement. 

In addition to Frozen-themed attractions, classic Christmas celebrations, well-loved festive activities, and magical moments delight guests of all ages. 

The Castle Hub Stage is once again hosting the “Mickey and Friends Christmastime Ball,” a fantastic Yuletide musical celebration in which beloved Disney characters will take the stage to spread holiday joy through songs, dance, and magic. 

Guests can take memorable Christmas photos with Santa Mickey at The Annex or send personalized Christmas wishes to loved ones all around the world with newly designed collectible postcards at Santa Mickey’s Holiday Wishes Mailbox. 

Santa Stitch makes his debut at Comet Café in Tomorrowland where he’s giving cuddly hugs to guests.

While enjoying a magical Christmas experience at HKDL, guests can savor a selection of over 70 Christmas-themed food and beverages. Those celebrating Christmas or New Year’s Eve may partake of an exquisite Christmas and New Year’s Eve Set Dinner and sip on a delightful Snow Time drink at Walt’s Cafe.

Part of the over 300 seasonal items available at the resort’s stores are more than 90 Frozen-themed merchandise items. 

To complete the Frozen journey, check in at the Kingdom Club Frozen Suites whose picturesque Frozen-inspired décor immerses guests in the enchanted world of Anna and Elsa. 

Filipinos booking a Hong Kong Disneyland Advance Purchase Room Offer can save up to 20 percent on guest rooms at either Disney Explorers Lodge, Hong Kong Disneyland Hotel, or Disney’s Hollywood Hotel. They also get a special offer for a two-day park ticket.
