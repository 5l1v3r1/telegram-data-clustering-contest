Pete Davidson and Kaia Gerber pack on the PDA as they pucker up on poolside date

23 Nov 2019, 20:54

Pete Davidson and Kaia Gerber did not hold back on a spot of PDA as the couple were seen enjoying a poolside date in Miami.

The two were spotted in the Florida city as they soaked up the sunshine, and were not afraid to pucker up while they were at it.

So they are pretty much official now, right?

Kaia sported a black bikini and a huge pair of shades, while Pete looked casual in shorts and a graphic t-shirt, while he also wore his own pair of sunglasses.

The couple could be seen relaxing on the sunbeds before they both decided to lean in for a kiss, and at one point, their tongues were clearly on display.

And they were also spotted looking very smitten as they later enjoyed a dip in the pool together, as the funnyman carried a drink in his hand.

Pete first sparked rumours he was dating the daughter of Cindy Crawford after the two were spotted enjoying a lunch date in October.

The comedian also reportedly visited Kaia at her apartment that same day, although a source told PageSix that the two were simply ‘just friends’.

A few weeks later, Kaia attended the Women’s Guild Cedars-Sinai Annual Luncheon at the Beverly Wiltshire Hotel, and notably wore a necklace with the letter ‘P’ written on it.

Last week, the 18-year-old model surprised Pete with a personally-delivered birthday cake, as the SNL star celebrated turning 26.

She was seen leaving the famous Magnolia Bakery in New York City carrying a white paper bag, and she was papped later that evening entering the NBC Studios (where Pete films Saturday Night Live) carrying the exact same bag.

And this week, the two enjoyed some more time together as they headed to see Charlotte Lawrence perform at a concert in the city, before jetting off to Miami.

Prior to Pete, Kaia had been linked to EastEnders star Patsy Palmer’s son Fenton Markell, and fellow model Wellington Grant.

Pete had also previously briefly dated Once Upon A Time In Hollywood star Margaret Qualley during a two-month romance, before they split in October.
