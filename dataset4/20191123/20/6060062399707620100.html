Eyebrow marker claims to give brows a microbladed look

23 Nov 2019, 20:52 by Celia Skvaril
  • Microbladed eyebrows are still incredibly popular, but not accessible to everyone.
  • They are expensive and semi-permanent. Ciaté London is just one of the many companies to release an eyebrow marker that is supposed to mimic the fluffy, natural, microbladed look.
