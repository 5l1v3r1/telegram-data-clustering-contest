Cardi B’s Daughter Kulture Is Her Mom’s Biggest Fan As She Dances To ‘Clout’

23 Nov 2019, 20:09 by Jamie Samhan

Cardi B and Offset’s daughter Kulture is a big fan of her parent’s music.

Fresh off the Grammy-nomination for their collaboration “Clout”, Cardi posted a video of Kulture, 16 months, dancing along to the song.

“Happy Friday!!! KK fav song got nominated.❤️….She so New York but was born in Georgia,” the rapper captioned the clip.

The adorable little girl has always been a big fan of her mama’s music as Cardi has posted many videos of the toddler dancing along.

Perhaps one day fans will get to see a three-way collab between Cardi, Offset and Kulture when she grows up.
