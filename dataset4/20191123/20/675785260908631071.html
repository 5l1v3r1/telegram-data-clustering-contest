Strictly's tearful Karim Zeroual runs to crying mum after emotional routine

Strictly Come Dancing viewers were 'in pieces' when emotional Karim Zeroual hugged his mum and sister after paying tribute to them in his couples' choice dance

23 Nov 2019, 20:27 by Kyle O'Sullivan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/kyle-osullivan/

Tearful Karim Zeroual ran over to his crying mum and sister tonight after his emotional Strictly Come Dancing routine.

The CBBC star paid tribute to his family during his contemporary couples' choice dance - which had Strictly viewers 'in pieces'.

Before he took to the dance floor, Karim said: "It's about not having many people in your life but the few you do have can make you. Your dreams can come true and anything is possible."

After Karim and professional partner Amy Dowden's touching performance - the CBBC presenter rushed over to hug his tearful mum Carol.

Wiping away tears, Karim said: "My mum and sister are everything. Two strong women in my life. Amy represented that in the dance holding me up."

Craig Revel Horwood praised Karim for his "dynamic and engaging" performance - but admitted it was not enough for a 10.

He said: "I thought it had great leaps and turns but I have to say I think that you're a technician and not an actor and that bothers me. There could have been a lot more story. I think it's lacking emotional conent.

"I want you to fly. I'm desperate to get a 10 out for you but I'm battling with the fact your technique is overpowering what you're trying to say."

Bruno hit back at Craig and claimed he loved the "freedom of flowing movement".

Motsi Mabuse said: "As a dancer I'm just amazed at what you bring to the show. Your technique is unbelievable. Those spins in the air and jumps. I love you always give 110%. To watch you is like a gift."

Shirley Ballas added: "Family is everything and mums are the best."

Strictly Come Dancing viewers took to Twitter after watching the emotional moment.

One fan said: "God I LOVE how Karim isn’t afraid to show emotion and I LOVE also the obvious love and respect he has for his mum and sister. An absolutely beautiful man."

"Karim’s backstory already had me in complete pieces & then he went danced stunningly & then he hugged his crying mum & sister. I can’t cope," added another.

A third said: "I was raised by a single mum, I myself am a single mum. Mums ARE the best and children who do you proud are a blessing. You go, Karim! Show the world that girl power of your mum and sister!"

"Normally hate the filler fluff between the dances but #Karim ‘s story nearly made me cry. And then I was in floods when he went to his mum afterwards," agreed a fourth.

*Strictly Come Dancing airs Saturdays on BBC One
