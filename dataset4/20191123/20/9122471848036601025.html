FOX Releases Epic Ohio State-Michigan Hype Video

23 Nov 2019, 20:32 by Alek Arend

All anyone could think about before, during and after Ohio State’s win over Penn State on Saturday was the Buckeyes’ contest against Michigan next week. The massive rivalry carries heavy implications this season. So much so that Fox Sports aired an OSU-Michigan hype video during Saturday’s game.

The video’s commentary highlights both team’s fans and the hatred for each other. Is this the best rivalry in all of sports?

“It’s complicated. On the outside you act like they don’t even exist. But on the inside, they’re all you think about. Because without that school, we don’t get to have that day – the day you wait all year for. You’re outside might not say their name. But your inside can’t imagine life without them.”
