Chaos as drivers face two-hour wait to get out of Newcastle car park, with queues up to 12th floor

There were queues back to the 12th floor of the NCP multi-storey on John Dobson Street on Saturday night

23 Nov 2019, 20:38 by Kali Lindsay

Motorists were faced with two-hour delays trying to leave a Newcastle city centre car park.

There was a huge queue at the NCP on John Dobson Street, with traffic tailed back to the 12th floor on Saturday night.

Motorists said they had taken 45 minutes to get down just two floors, while others said they had come earlier and left again hoping traffic would have eased.

Ollie and Katie Little, who were still waiting after 7pm, first came to the car park at 4pm but were told there was a delay.

Ollie, 35, said: "We were told there was an hour and a half delay so decided to go for something to eat.

"We came back and it was just as bad. It was kicking off with people honking their horns.

"It has been like this since 4pm. It seems like everyone who has come for the market has come to one place."

One man said: "I came at 5pm and queued for bit and then I parked for 30 mintues.

"I thought it was easing off so decided to try and leave again, but it is just as bad."

Two women initially came but when they saw the queue left for a coffee before trying again.

"We asked someone how long they had been waiting for and they said one hour," the woman said. "We decided to go away and come back. It is ridiculous."

"I am supposed to be going out with friends," the other said.

Another man said: "We have been queuing for 45 minutes and have only gone down two floors.

"There were people further down who said they have been waiting for two hours."

It comes after motorists faced lengthy delays getting into the city centre car parks before heading for Christmas shopping.

Queues of traffic could be seen outside Eldon Garden Car Park on Saturday afternoon.
