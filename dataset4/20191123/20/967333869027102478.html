France Declares 'Red Alert' for Southeastern Coastal Departments Amid Flash Floods

23 Nov 2019, 20:33

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - France has issued the highest "red" alert for two of its departments located on the southeastern Mediterranean coast in light of heavy rains and flash floods in the region, Interior Minister Christophe Castaner said on Saturday.

"Red level in Var and Alpes-Maritimes. If you live in these departments, be vigilant, warn your relatives and friends and follow recommendations of the authorities", Castaner wrote on Twitter.

He added that relevant agencies were working at the scene to help people.
