Russell Westbrook’s Brother Escorted Out of Staples Center For Trying To Fight Clippers’ Montrezl Harrell

23 Nov 2019, 20:22 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Houston Rockets lost a close game to the Los Angeles Clippers on Friday night, but things almost got physical, not between the teams, but between a family member of the Rockets and a player on the Clippers.

After that buzzer, Russell Westbrook‘s brother Raynard reportedly rushed the court in an attempt to confront Montrezl Harrell and was promptly escorted out of the arena by security, according to Yahoo Sports.
