Ajit Pawar sacked as NCP's legislature party leader

'The decision to sack Ajit Pawar was taken at the meeting of the party's MLAs,' NCP party sources said.

23 Nov 2019, 20:14

MUMBAI: The NCP on Saturday removed Ajit Pawar as its legislature party leader after he joined hands with the BJP to form government and took oath as the deputy chief minister of the state.
