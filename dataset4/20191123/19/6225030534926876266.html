MIdcourt makes Native Diver 4th straight win; Roadster runs last

23 Nov 2019, 19:27 by Brad Stephens

Midcourt continued his hot streak Saturday for trainer John Shirreffs, wiring the Grade 3, $100,000 Native Diver at Del Mar.

The win was a fourth straight for Midcourt, a 4-year-old Midnight Lute gelding, and came in his first try in a graded stakes race. He entered off a neck win over Two Thirty Five – one of the three rivals he faced Saturday – in the black-type Comma to the Top Stakes on Oct. 27 at Santa Anita Park.

There was no such close finish Saturday for Midcourt. He broke from the No. 2 post and went to the front, setting fractions of :23.55, :47.61 and 1:11.54.

Jockey Victor Espinoza pushed the button on Midcourt through the far turn of the 1 1/8-mile dirt feature. The C R K Stable runner drew away from there, then cruised home to score by five lengths over runner-up Extra Hope for a win that returned $7.60.
