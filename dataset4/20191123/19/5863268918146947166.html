Ali Rehman Khan finally comes up with an explanation over viral video

23 Nov 2019, 19:42 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – Renowned actor Ali Rehman Khan posted a video statement and clarified his ill-mannered behavior towards an employee of McDonald’s, dubbing it as an awareness campaign regarding the VIP culture in Pakistan.

In a leaked video, Ali Rehman was seen screaming at a cashier saying, “"Apko pata hai main kaun hoon? Apne kabhi mujhe TV pe dekha hai? Aap employee hain, aapka kaam hai meri baat maan’na."
