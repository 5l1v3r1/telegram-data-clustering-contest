What has Kim Kardashian said about Caitlyn Jenner's I'm A Celebrity stint?

23 Nov 2019, 19:00

Kim Kardashian has broken her silence on Caitlyn Jenner’s stint on I’m A Celebrity.

The Kardashian clan had kept noticeably silent on Caitlyn’s appearance in the jungle – until now.

Kim, who was raised by Caitlyn after her mother married her in 1991, took to Twitter to share a video of the Olympic gold medalist delivering a rousing speech to her fellow celebrities.

The 70-year-old told her fellow campmates: ‘Let’s go team! Not just another day, another day to excel, another day to be great.

‘Rise and shine,’ she added, in an apparent reference to the Rise and Shine song her daughter Kylie Jenner has sang to her baby Stormi.

‘I literally recorded this and set this as my alarm a few weeks ago,’ Kim said of the video, and honestly, we can’t blame her.

This tweet might not seem like much, but it’s the first time that someone from the Kardashian family has spoken publically about Caitlyn being in the jungle.

This includes Caitlyn’s biological daughters Kendall and Kylie, who have yet to reference her time on the show.
