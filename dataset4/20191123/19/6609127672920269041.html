FIND HIM

Cops launch urgent appeal for missing Dumfries and Galloway teen, 15, who vanished in September

23 Nov 2019, 19:02 by Aidan Smith

COPS have launched an urgent online appeal to trace a missing 15-year-old from Dumfries and Galloway.

Maraj Karimi, 15, was last seen on Sunday 22 September 2019 in the Newton Stewart area.

Cops now believe he may have travelled to Belfast in Northern Ireland by ferry around 6am on September 22 and thereafter, potentially to the Dublin area via train.

Maraj is described as Afghan, around 5 ft 4in tall, of slim build and has short black hair.

When last seen, he was wearing a pink/red and white polo shirt and a black bomber style jacket.

A Police Scotland statement added: "We believe he has relatives in the Cork area and it's possible he may have travelled into the Republic of Ireland in order to reunite with his family.

"Officers are seeking any information which could assist them in locating Maraj to ensure he is safe and well."

If anyone has seen Maraj they are urged to call police in Dumfries, Scotland, via 101, quoting incident number 2608 of 22 September 2019.
