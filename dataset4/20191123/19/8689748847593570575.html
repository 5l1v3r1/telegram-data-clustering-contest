I’ve never cheated on my wife since we married – Brother Sammy

23 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Adu Sarpeah

The wait is over as Brother Sammy has reacted to the allegation levelled up against him that he has been having intercourse with Rosemond Brown a.k.a Akuapem Poloo.

Remember that Xandy Kamel most recently alleged that the gospel artiste has been “using, abusing and misusing” Akuapem Poloo in the area of intercourse at the blind side of Bro Sammy’s wife.

But Brother Sammy reacting to the buzz in an interview with blogger Zion Felix described the allegation masterminded by Xandy Kamel as null and void adding that he has never cheated on his wife since they got married.

He continued by saying that Akuapem Poloo doesn’t know his house and they only had a business encounter on set. Brother Sammy reiterated that he becomes sad whenever he’s tagged with infidelity as he has remained faithful to his wife.
