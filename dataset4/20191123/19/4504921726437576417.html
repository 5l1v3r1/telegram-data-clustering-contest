UDF Government to review all mining contracts

23 Nov 2019, 19:03

United Democratic Front of Namibia (UDF) president Apius Auxab said if the party is elected into power they will review mining contracts as well as elevating education to be top priority nationally.
Auxab also said the UDF is not a Damara party but a national party.
He made these remarks at the party's rally held on Saturday at Uis.
