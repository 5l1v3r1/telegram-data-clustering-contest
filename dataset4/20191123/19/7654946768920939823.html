Rudy Giuliani: Joe Biden’s jab at Lindsey Graham reminds me of a ‘mafia case’

23 Nov 2019, 19:01 by Mary Kay Linge

Democratic presidential candidate Joe Biden lashed out at Senate Republicans for their plans to investigate his son Hunter’s questionable business dealings in Ukraine — and Rudy Giuliani responded by comparing the former veep to a mafia don.

Biden said he is “embarrassed” for Sen. Lindsey Graham (R-South Carolina), the chairman of the Senate Judiciary Committee, in a Friday interview with CNN.

“Lindsey is about to go down in a way that I think he’s going to regret his whole life,” Biden said, in response to news that Graham asked the State Department for documents related to the Bidens’ communications with former Ukrainian and U.S. officials over Burisma, a company that hired Hunter Biden for a cushy job while his father was vice president.

“They have him under their thumb right now,” Biden continued. “They know he knows, if he comes out against Trump, he’s got a real tough road for re-election.”

Giuliani, Trump’s personal lawyer, leaped to Graham’s defense.

“Now a threat to a US Senator,” he tweeted. “This is getting to be more and more like my old mafia cases. They sure do sound like crooks.”
