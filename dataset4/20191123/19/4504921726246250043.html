RDP vice president says the party is alive and not losing support

23 Nov 2019, 19:53

As political parties wrap up their campaigns in the build-up to elections next week, the Rally for Democracy and Progress' (RDP) vice president Kennedy Shekupakela says the party remains alive, arguing it is not losing support as widely perceived.
RDP held its Khomas Star Rally at Ombili in Windhoek today.
