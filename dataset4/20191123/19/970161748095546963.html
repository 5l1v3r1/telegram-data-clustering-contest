Meet the partners of the I'm a Celebrity 2019 contestants

23 Nov 2019, 19:45

I’m a Celebrity 2019 is well underway, with Caitlyn Jenner cracking up the nation by falling out of a window and Andrew Maxwell’s budgie smugglers coming for Helen Flanagan’s ‘sexy shower’ crown.

But who in the camp has got a special someone rooting for them back home?

Read on to find out about the celebrities’ other halves…

Caitlyn Jenner and Sophia Hutchins

70-year-old Caitlyn is dating 23-year-old Sophia Hutchins.

Sophia, also a transgendered woman, is a budding entrepreneur, having invented an odourless SPF 50+ sunscreen mist that can be applied after makeup.

Caitlyn has been married three times before, most famously to Kardashian matriarch Kris Jenner.

On top of being a step-parent to Kim, Kourtney and Khloe, Caitlyn has six biological children including Kendall and Kylie Jenner and The Hills’ Brody Jenner.

Roman Kemp and Sophie-Anne Flury

The radio DJ and son of Martin Kemp has been dating Sophie-Anne Flury since 2018.

Anne-Sophie is a neuroscientist and is a budding business mogul, launching Shu Gum – a cleaning product to help keep your shoes fresh – this year.

The two have a Chihuahua together – and you can follow the dog’s adventures on its Instagram, @PoochiebyGucci.

Jacqueline Jossa and Dan Osborne

Jacqueline has been with ex-TOWIE star Dan Osborne since 2014 and they share daughters Ella and Mia.

Though they’ve had a few ups and downs over the years, the two seem stronger than ever.

Dan recently uploaded a cute photo of their two daughters and his son from a previous relationship cheering her on in the jungle.

Adele Roberts and Kate Holderness

Radio 1 DJ Adele is dating actress Kate Holderness.

Kate has starred in episodes of Coronation Street, the BBC’s Henry VIII and his Six Wives, playing Catherine Parr, and the BBC’s Elizabeth I in 2017.

Kate Garraway and Derek Draper

Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway is married to political advisor Derek Draper.

The pair married in 2005 and have two children together – daughter Darcey and son Bill.
