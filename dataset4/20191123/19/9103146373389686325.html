Namatovu returns with honours

23 Nov 2019, 19:21 by Mary Nambwayo

She became the second woman in history to represent Uganda in Miss International.

EVELYN Namatovu who scoped second runners up position in the Miss International pageant has come back home holding an award.

Held in Tokyo, Japan on Saturday, Miss International is one of the top four most popular pageants in the world.

The crown was handed to Sireethorn Leearamwat. She is Miss International 2019 and, becomes the first-ever winner of Miss International from Thailand.

Mexico's Andrea Toscano was 1st runner-up, followed by Uganda's Evelyn Namatovu as 2nd runner up.
Colombia's Maria Alejandra Vengoechea and United Kingdom's Harriette Lane finished as 3rd and 4th runners up respectively.

The 23- year-old Namatovu is a student at Makerere University Business School, awaiting graduation in Bachelors of Entrepreneurship and Business Management. She became the second woman in history to represent Uganda in Miss International.

Uganda made its debut in Miss International in 2009 with the five-foot-nine and a half Pierra Akwero 22-years-old at the time, flying the national flag at the 49th Miss International pageant, held in Chengdu Sichuan China.
