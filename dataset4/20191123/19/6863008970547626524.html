Ukrainian officer reveals enemy death toll in Donbas on Nov 22

Two invaders were killed on Friday.

23 Nov 2019, 19:15

Officer of the 53rd Separate Mechanized Brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Anatoliy Shtefan has disclosed the enemy death toll in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, over the past day.

"On November 22, 2019, the losses of Russian occupying forces totaled three people, namely: two people (from the 4th and the 3rd separate motorized rifle brigades) were killed, and another one from the 1st separate motorized rifle brigade was wounded," he wrote on Telegram.

Shtefan monitors reports on enemy losses on social media.

Russia's hybrid military forces on November 22 mounted 14 attacks on Ukrainian army positions in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, with one Ukrainian soldier reported as wounded in action, while another one was killed in a road accident.
