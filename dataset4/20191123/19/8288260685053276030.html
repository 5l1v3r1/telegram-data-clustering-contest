Stephen Mulhern threatens to 'smash' Ant and Dec's faces in after brutal I'm A Celebrity jibe

Ant McPartlin has returned to TV to present I'm A Celeb alongside Declan Donnelly, after Holly Willoughby replaced him last year while he dealt with personal issues

23 Nov 2019, 19:37 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Stephen Mulhern has warned Ant and Dec: "I'll smash your faces in".

The comments from Stephen, who is also a present on ITV, came after the cheeky Geordie duo made a swipe at him on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!

Ant McPartlin has returned to TV to present I'm A Celeb alongside Declan Donnelly, after Holly Willoughby replaced him last year while he dealt with personal issues.

However, the pair's 'cruel' joke on I'm A Celebrity has left Stephen incensed - and he issued a brutal response to the pair's brazen cheekiness.

The cheeky presenters joked that Stephen should front a 'cheap, one dimensional and very low rent show' based on a game invented in the jungle by the I'm A Celebrity campmates.

Dec joked: "Don’t miss Stephen Mulhern’s Quick Draw next Saturday only on ITV."

Ant added: "He’ll present anything."

And turning to face the camera, Dec said: "Hi Stevie."

The duo tweeted to ask if Stephen was watching and he replied drily: "Didn’t see the show, as was too busy filming the Xmas special of In for A Penny, making more money for you two millionaires #happychristmas. Mention me again and I’ll smash your faces in."

Fans found the exchange hilarious, reports the Manchester Evening News .

"You two are a cruel pair," tweeted one with a crying-with-laughter emoji.

"And the funny thing is, he’s busy because of them," laughed another.
