This Is Us star Justin Hartley files for divorce from wife Chrishell Hartley

23 Nov 2019, 19:22

This Is Us star Justin Hartley has filed for divorce from his wife, Chrishell Hartley, after two years of marriage.

The 42-year-old officially filed the paperwork on Friday in Los Angeles, with the reason cited as ‘irreconcilable differences’ and ‘dissolution without minor children’, according to Us Weekly.

Although Justin has listed their split as 8 July, the two have been seen together on a number of occasions, and more recently at a pre-Golden Globes party just last week.

The actor also took the time out to wish his now ex-wife well on her birthday at the end of July.

‘Let’s all wish this young gem of a human a very happy birthday!’ he wrote on Instagram.

‘To many more years of love and laughs!!! HAPPY BIRTHDAY @chrishellhartley!!!!!!!!’

Justin and Chrishell had begun dating in January 2014, with Justin proposing to his future wife in July 2016, before the two wed the following year in October 2017.

Prior to that, the actor was previously married to his Passions co-star, Lindsay Hartley, between 2004 and 2012, with the couple sharing a daughter, 15-year-old Isabella.

Chrishell had previously opened up about holding off on expanding her family with Justin and having another child.

‘We don’t know [when] yet,’ she told Us Weekly back in May. ‘I have a stepdaughter (Justin has a daughter), so we’ve got our hands full with high school.’

She also joked that Justin was nothing like his This Is Us character, Kevin Pearson: ‘Thank God because he wouldn’t be a very good husband.

‘There’s parts of Kevin that are good, but I don’t think I’d want to be married to him, you know?

‘So luckily, Kevin gets all of his charm, but I get both. I also get the good husband’s side. So I win!’

Metro.co.uk have contacted reps for Justin and Chrishell for comment.
