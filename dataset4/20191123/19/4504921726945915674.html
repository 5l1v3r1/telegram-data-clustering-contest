NUDO calls on voters not to give SWAPO their votes

23 Nov 2019, 19:51

President of the National Unity Democratic Organisation (NUDO), Esther Muinjangue is calling on voters not to give SWAPO their votes, after it has nearly 30 years to fulfil its promises to the nation.
Muinjangue was addressing NUDO's final rally in Windhoek.
