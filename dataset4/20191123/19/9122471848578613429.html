Nebraska Announces Update For RB Wan’Dale Robinson

23 Nov 2019, 19:55 by Chris Rosvoglou

When the Nebraska Cornhuskers take the field this afternoon against the Maryland Terrapins, they’ll be without standout running back/wide receiver Wan’Dale Robinson.

Nebraska didn’t have Robinson last weekend against Wisconsin, which certainly hurt the team’s production on offense.

Robinson has been one of the top playmakers for the Huskers this season. His 326 rushing yards and five total touchdowns are third on the team, while his 453 receiving yards are second on the team.

Unfortunately for the Cornhuskers, Robinson is not even on the team’s travel roster for today’s contest.
