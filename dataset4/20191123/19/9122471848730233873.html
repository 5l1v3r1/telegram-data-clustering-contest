Texas Announces 2 Key Players Won’t Play vs. Baylor

23 Nov 2019, 19:29 by Alek Arend

Despite a 6-4 record which includes three losses in Big 12 play, the Texas Longhorns still have a shot to make the conference title game. First things first, the Longhorns must beat No. 13 Baylor on Saturday.

That’ll be difficult to do as a recent report suggest two key Texas players will not play against the Bears. The Longhorns will be without WR Collin Johnson and DB D.J. Foster. Johnson continues to deal with a hamstring injury while Foster is dealing with a neck injury.
