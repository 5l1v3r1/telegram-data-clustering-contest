Car crashes into home

23 Nov 2019, 14:36 by WFTX Digital Team

Immokalee Firefighters responded to a vehicle accident on Durso Court late Friday night.

Firefighters say a pickup truck struck parked cars in front of a home, causing one of the cars to be pushed into the home.
