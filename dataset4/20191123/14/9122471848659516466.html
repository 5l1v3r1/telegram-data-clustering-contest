ESPN’s Paul Finebaum Makes His Pick For Ohio State-Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 14:10 by Andrew Holleran

The biggest game on college football’s Week 13 slate is taking place in Columbus, Ohio. The undefeated Ohio State Buckeyes are hosting Penn State at Ohio Stadium.

Today’s contest is essentially a Big Ten Championship Game play-in game. Ohio State clinches the Big Ten East with a win, while Penn State would just need to beat Rutgers next week (which is a virtual lock).

Ohio State enters today’s game as a sizable favorite.

ESPN college football analyst Paul Finebaum made his pick for the game.

The SEC guru is taking Ohio State to beat Penn State today, he said on SportsCenter. The Buckeyes will move to 11-0 with a win over the Nittany Lions.

Ohio State will have Chase Young back for this afternoon’s contest. He appears to be ready to go.

Ohio State and Penn State will kick off at 12 p.m. E.T.

The game will be on FOX.
