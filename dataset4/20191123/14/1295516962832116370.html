WWE: Smackdown Reveals Great Look at New Intercontinental Championship Belt

23 Nov 2019, 14:11 by C.K. Stewart

Just two days before Survivor Series, WWE has unveiled a new Intercontinental Championship.

Sami Zayn presented current IC Champion Shinsuke Nakamura with the new belt on this Friday's WWE SmackDown. The celebration was short-lived, however, when NXT's Undisputed Era (Adam Cole, Bobby Fish, Kyle O'Reilly, and one of Nakamura's Sunday opponents, Roderick Strong) attacked the SmackDown superstars.
