Aston Villa 'keen' on Argentine forward impressing in Premier League

Aston Villa latest news | Sky Sports say the 22-year-old Norwich City forward has attracted the attention of Villa boss Dean Smith

23 Nov 2019, 14:00 by James Pallat

Aston Villa are said to be "keen" on Emiliano Buendia.

Sky Sports report the 22-year-old Norwich City forward has attracted the attention of Villa boss Dean Smith ahead of the January transfer window.

Argentine Buendia has started all 12 of Norwich's matches in the Premier League so far this season, having played a key role in their promotion to the top flight as champions of the Championship last season.

The Canaries, who are rock bottom of the Premier League, bought him for £1.4m from Getafe in July 2018, Buendia signing a four-year deal at Carrow Road.

Sky Sports also say Villa "remain interested" in Brentford winger Said Benrahma, having failed to strike a deal with the Bees for the £20m-rated Algerian during the summer transfer window.
