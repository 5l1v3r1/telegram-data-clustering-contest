Joel Klatt Has Message For People Criticizing Alabama’s Schedule

23 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Andrew McCarty

In just a few hours, Alabama will take the field for a contest against an FCS opponent.

Western Carolina comes to town for a battle with the Crimson Tide. It’s a nice payday for the school, but they have no chance to win the game.

As for Alabama, fans across the country aren’t happy with the school’s decision to schedule a cupcake game before the Iron Bowl. Now, Auburn is doing the same – playing Samford – so the Tide aren’t alone on this front.

However, that doesn’t change the fact that fans aren’t happy with Alabama’s schedule. As other top programs are playing legitimate conference games, the Tide can pick up an easy win.

College football analyst Joel Klatt is just fine with that. He had a message for fans complaining about Alabama’s schedule.

“Everyone is upset with Alabama for their schedule this week…However, why should they change? They shouldn’t…It’s not their job to make things equitable in CFB. If I was their AD I would do the same thing,” Klatt said.
