Ciroc confirms partnership deal with Tacha

23 Nov 2019, 14:15

Fans of controversial BBNaija star, Tacha, have been thrown into excitement following Ciroc's confirmation of their partnership with the Rivers born reality star.

Earlier on, the reality star and entrepreneur had taken to her Instagram page to announce in a rather interesting way, that she had signed a partnership deal with the French brand.

While her fans rejoiced over the new endorsement deal, a confirmation from the horse's mouth has left them more than ecstatic and may have given them reasons to shade other housemates.

A fan of Tacha, had inquired to know when the variety of the liquor named after Tacha would be available for purchase. Replying to the question, the official account of the liquor brand confirmed Tacha's partnership deal.
