Maharashtra: SC to Hear Sena-NCP-Congress' Joint Plea Against Governor Koshyari at 11:30 AM Today

Maharashtra Political Twist: The parties claimed that all the MLA's of the Shiv Sena, NCP and Congress are completely with the alliance except for Ajit Pawar. 

23 Nov 2019, 14:43

New Delhi: The Shiv Sena, Nationalist Congress Party and Congress have approached the Supreme Court, hours after Maharashtra governor BS Koshyari administered oath of office to Devendra Fadnavis as the new CM of state and NCP leader Ajit Pawar as his Deputy.  The court will hear the petition at 11.30 am on Sunday.
