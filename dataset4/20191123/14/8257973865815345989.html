Apple TV+ renews Lauren Ambrose-Rupert Grint show 'Servant' for Season 2

23 Nov 2019, 14:07 by Karen Butler

Nov. 23 (UPI) -- Apple TV+ has renewed its suspense drama Servant for a second season.

Produced by The Sixth Sense and Split filmmaker M. Night Shyamalan, the show is to debut on Thursday. It takes place in Philadelphia and stars Lauren Ambrose, Toby Kebbell, Nell Tiger Free and Rupert Grint.
