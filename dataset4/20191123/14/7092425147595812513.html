Former Girls Aloud bandmates slam rumours they have a group chat slating Nadine Coyle

23 Nov 2019, 14:56 by Nick Fitzgerald

"We only want to see Nadine do well" - Cheryl

Former Girls Aloud bandmates Cheryl Cole, Kimberley Walsh and Nicola Roberts are slamming rumours they have a WhatsApp group chat dedicated to slating Nadine Coyle.

The girls also deny they had been "voting repeatedly" for Nadine to do gruesome Bushtucker Trials as part of her participation in this year's show of I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here.

Speaking to Mail Online, Cheryl said:

"We only want to see Nadine do well, and regardless of any differences we might have had, we will always all have love for each other.

"We did not enjoy watching her distressed when jumping out of the plane. And we don’t have time for group chats regarding the trials on the show!"

Kimberley also dismissed the claims, adding she thinks her former bandmate is "doing great" in the jungle.

Nicola chimed in too, denying any existence of a WhatsApp group chat slating Nadine.

"We don’t have a group chat about I’m A Celebrity, this just isn’t true. We only wish Nadine the best in the jungle and hope she does well on the show," she said.

The news comes as Nadine recently rubbished rumours of a feud with Cheryl. The Northern Irish lass branded these rumours as "fake news" and said "It wasn't a thing."
