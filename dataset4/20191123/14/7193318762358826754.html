What to do in Oklahoma on Nov. 23, 2019: Hear Ellis Paul at the Blue Door

23 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Brandy McDonnell

Today's featured event:

Hear folk singer-songwriter Ellis Paul at 8 tonight at the Blue Door, 2805 N McKinley.

A regular at Okemah's Woody Guthrie Folk Festival, Paul will bring with him new songs from “Storyteller’s Suitcase,” the album he released in May on his own Rosella Records label. Recorded in Woodstock, New York, the album’s title track is stuffed with recollections and lessons from his life as a traveling troubadour.
