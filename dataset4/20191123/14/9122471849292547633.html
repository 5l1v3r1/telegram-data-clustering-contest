Photo: Ohio State Fan’s Jim Harbaugh Sign Goes Viral At College GameDay

23 Nov 2019, 14:29 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday morning, ESPN’s College GameDay kicked off from Columbus ahead of a huge match-up between No. 2 Ohio State and No. 8 Penn State.

Before the game kicks off, Kirk Herbstreit and company took the stage to break down the day’s slate of games. as the crew previewed the biggest game of the Big Ten season, the cameras panned out to the crowd.

Of course, no College GameDay would be complete without a litany of signs.

One Ohio State fan’s sign stuck out.

“I’m thankful for Jim Harbaugh,” the sign read.
