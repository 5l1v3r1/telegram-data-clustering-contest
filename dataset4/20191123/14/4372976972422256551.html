21 cars and motorcycles from Paul Walker's collection are headed to auction

Photos of all the cars are now available

23 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Autoblog Staff

Barrett-Jackson will auction 21 vehicles from the late Paul Walker's car collection at its massive event in Scottsdale, Arizona, that runs from January 11-19, 2020. The vehicles cover a wide range of years and styles — everything from a 1963 Chevy Nova Wagon to a 2013 Ford Mustang Boss 302S race car — but the highlights of the auction are five 1995 BMW M3 Lightweight models.

BMW produced only 125 of the M3 Lightweights. Each one features an aggressive rear wing, front spoiler and an immediately recognizable Alpine White paint scheme highlighted by checkered flag livery. According to the auction house, this will be the largest collection of E36 Lightweights ever sold at a single auction. In addition to the Lightweights, Walker's collection includes a pair of E30 M3s that will also be auctioned.
