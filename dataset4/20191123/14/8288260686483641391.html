'Big call' - Birmingham City fans react to Pep Clotet's huge shake-up

Huddersfield Town v Birmingham City build-up | Pep Clotet made three changes to his Blues side for the game away to Huddersfield – and the fans have had their say

23 Nov 2019, 14:37 by Jonny Bonell

Birmingham City boss Pep Clotet made three changes to his starting 11 for the game away to Huddersfield Town.

Blues return to Championship action after a two-week international break with a game against Danny Cowley’s Terriers.

Lee Camp’s error against Fulham sees him replaced by Connal Trueman, who has come through the  Academy, with Jacques Maghoma and Jeremie Bela coming in for Fran Villalba and Kerim Mrabti.

Here’s how the Birmingham fans reacted to the shake-up...
