The Cardiff City player ratings against Charlton as defence is dire again but two forwards make big impact

This is how the Bluebirds rated in their 2-2 draw with the Addicks at The Valley

23 Nov 2019, 14:40 by Glen Williams

Neil Etheridge 6

Couldn't really do anything with either of the goals conceded. Made a flurry of brilliant second-half saves.

Lee Peltier 5

Has to take his share of the blame for an ordinary day at the back for Cardiff City's defenders. Made a goal-saving block late on.

Aden Flint 4

Far too cumbersome in possession and too many gaps between him and Nelson. Didn't make his physicality known in the opposition box, either.

Curtis Nelson 4

Beaten far, far too easily out wide for the first goal, allowing the winger to cut inside. One half of a disjointed centre-back pairing.

Joe Bennett 6

Didn't do a great deal wrong and put in a couple of lovely crosses in the opposition box. A nice assist for Tomlin, too.

Nathaniel Mendez-Laing 7 (star man)

Never shied away from taking on the defence and took his chance well. Getting back to his marauding best.

Marlon Pack 5

He struggled with his lack of pace to play in a midfield two and it afforded the hosts acres of space in the middle of the park.

Leandro Bacuna 6

Was forced to work incredibly hard but was also culpable for the first goal. Distribution continues to improve.

Junior Hoilett 5

Showed willing and creativity but his final product was definitely missing. Missed an ultimately crucial penalty, too.

Callum Paterson 5

Was unlucky not to have been awarded a penalty, but eventually won one in the second half. Otherwise relatively ineffective.

Gary Madine 5

Had very little service but always made himself available and was a nuisance in the air at times.

SUBS

Lee Tomlin (on for Hoilett) 7

Another brilliant cameo from the playmaker, took his chance incredibly well to level the tie.

Omar Bogle (on for Madine) 6

Hustled and harried in attack but had limited impact. Certainly made himself known and had a good shot blocked late one.

Will Vaulks (on for Paterson) NA
