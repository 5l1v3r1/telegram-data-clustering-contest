Comedian Funnybone opens up on what will stop him from marrying

23 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Sanya Agunbiade

Nigerian comedian Funnybone has opened up on what might stop him from marrying and staying single for the rest of his life.

While speaking in a new interview with The Sun, the comic actor mentioned that comparing his mom to ladies of nowadays may render him single for life.

According to him, it is quite rare to find a woman who is exactly like his mother.

He said:

“I wish to marry a woman like my mother, but if I compare my mother with the women I see around now, I won’t marry.

You can’t find her breed again. I had a picture of what she wanted for me and I would work with that.

She wants unity; someone that would solidify the love my siblings and I share.”

The comedian lost his mother in October 2019.
