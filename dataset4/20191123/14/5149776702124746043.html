Nottingham Forest fans react as injury forces Sabri Lamouchi to make change for Bristol City clash

Yuri Ribeiro was left out of the Reds squad because of an injury

23 Nov 2019, 14:40 by James Pallatt

Nottingham Forest fans have been reacting to today's team news at Ashton Gate.

Reds boss Sabri Lamouchi made one change to his side for the Championship clash with Bristol City, with Jack Robinson replacing Yuri Ribeiro at left-back.

Ribeiro was left out of the squad because of an injury, but it is not thought the Portuguese defender has a serious problem.

Michael Dawson also notably returned to the Forest squad, following his recovery from injury.

Here's the Forest line-up...

Forest: Samba, Cash, Worrall, Figueiredo, Robinson, Yates, Watson, Lolley, Silva, Ameobi, Grabban. Subs: Muric, Carvalho, Bostock, Mir, Dawson, Chema, Adomah.
