Sergio Busquets banned for Barcelona's trip to Atletico on Dec. 1

The midfielder picked up his fifth booking of the season against Leganes

23 Nov 2019, 14:21 by Sport.es

Sergio Busquets picked up his fifth booking of the season on Saturday against Leganes for a late challenge on Roque Mesa in the first half.

The Barcelona midfielder will, therefore, miss next weekend's tough trip to Atletico Madrid in the league through suspension.

Busquets started at Leganes as part of a two-man midfield, alongside Frenkie de Jong, as Ernesto Valverde opted for a 4-2-3-1 formation.

The Spain international didn't have the best afternoon, though, getting booked before being taken off early in the second half for Ivan Rakitic.

At that point, Valverde switched to a midfield three, with De Jong and Rakitic joined by Arturo Vidal, who replaced forward Antoine Griezmann.

Barcelona won the game 2-1 thanks to goals from Luis Suarez and Vidal to remain top of the table with 28 points.
