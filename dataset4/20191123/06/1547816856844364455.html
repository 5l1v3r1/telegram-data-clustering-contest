Devendra Fadnavis Takes Oath As Maharashtra CM; Netizens Can't Help But Compare It To The Game Of Thrones Climax

23 Nov 2019, 06:14 by Saheli Maity

After a sudden change of events Devendra Fadnavis took oath as the Maharashtra Chief Minister again, Twitterati started pouring in some creative memes and started comparing this dramatic Maharashtra government politics to HBO’s hit show Game Of Thrones. Scroll down to see some amazing memes

Today morning, the BJP completely took everyone by surprise when it was announced that Devendra Fadnavis is back again as Maharashtra Chief Minister while Ajit Pawar as his deputy. Earlier, during the Maharashtra elections phase, Fadnavis had resigned from his post when no party was able to form the government with a majority. This surprise swearing-in early today morning is now being described as a political masterstroke of Sharad Pawar, who until Thursday night had said there was an agreement between the NCP, Congress and Shiv Sena that Uddhav Thackeray should lead the new government. 

However, with Devendra Fadnavis back to his position, it not only stunned Shiv Sena and Congress but also the whole nation. Devendra Fadnavis took oath early morning in front of the government leaders and official media. As soon as the decision broke in the media, Twitterati started losing their mind on this political masterstroke and started calling this climax just as HBO’s hit show, Game Of Thrones.
