Derbyshire house with huge garden up for auction for just £55k - but it needs some work

A perfect opportunity for someone to invest in

23 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Hayley Watson

A Derbyshire home could be snapped up for a bargain price at auction next month.

The end of terrace house in Swadlincote has been listed for auction with SDL Auctions - with a starting price of just £55,000.

According to Rightmove, the average price of semi-detached properties sold in the town over the past year was £160,833.

The 'larger-than-average' property could make an ideal family home, with a generously-sized garden, offering the potential for an extention.

However, pictures show it will require some improvement before anyone moves in.

In an online listing for the property, the auctioneers say: "Requiring some improvement and upgrading is a larger than average two double bedroomed end-terraced property with the advantage of a driveway to the side and potential for an extension (subject to planning consent).

"Internally the property has the benefit of gas central heating and is mostly double glazed and in brief comprises generous sized lounge through diner measuring 27'6" x 13'5", fitted kitchen and walk-in pantry."

It continues: "To the first floor the passaged leads to two genuine double bedrooms and a spacious bathroom with three piece suite and shower.

"Outside there is a driveway to the side leading to a good sized enclosed garden enjoying a southerly aspect.

"The property is situated in the context of an established residential location always popular with first time buyers, young families and landlords.

"Swadlincote town centre is a short commute along with Burton town centre. Excellent freehold investment opportunity."

If a bit of home decor doesn't put you off, 78 Regent Street will feature in SDL Auction's upcoming event in Derby on December 13.

The auction takes place at The Pedigree Suite at Pride Park Stadium, starting at 11:30am.
