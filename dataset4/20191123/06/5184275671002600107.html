Thalapathy 64: Vijay Sethupathi To Start Shooting For His Role In The Action-Thriller From December

The film is being bankrolled by Xavier Britto under the XB Film Creators production house.

23 Nov 2019, 06:53 by Koimoi.com Team

Filmmaker Lokesh Kanagaraj’s upcoming directorial venture which has been tentatively titled Thalapathy 64 has been trending all across the Internet for a long time. The film starring Kollywood superstar Thalapathy Vijay is one of the most awaited releases for the year 2020.

The latest news related to the action thriller is, as per a report from onlookersmedia.in, Super Deluxe actor Vijay Sethupathi who will be playing the villainous character in Thalapathy 64 will kick start his portions from December. Also joining the shoot will be Mollywood actor Anthony Varghese.

Reportedly, Anthony Varghese who is well known for his acting in Malayalam films like Angamaly Diaries and Jallikkattu will be seen in a character with grey shades.

Talking about Thalapathy 64, the first schedule of the film was shot in Chennai. The second schedule of the action thriller is being filmed at a brisk pace in Delhi.

Thalapathy 64 also stars Malavika Mohanan, Andrea Jeremiah, Shantanu Bhagyaraj along with others in pivotal roles.

If reports are to be believed, one can expect the makers to unveil the film’s title and first look poster of Thalapathy Vijay on New Year.

The film is being bankrolled by Xavier Britto under the XB Film Creators production house.

The music for the action thriller will be composed by Anirudh Ravichander.

The Thalapathy Vijay starrer will big screens in April 2020.
