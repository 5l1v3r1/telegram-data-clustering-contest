Congress feels betrayed, says illegitimate Maharashtra govt formation will self-destruct

Hitting out at the BJP, Congress has said the party has betrayed people's mandate in Maharashtra and will self-destruct.

23 Nov 2019, 06:28

The Congress hit out at Devendra Fadnavis being sworn-in as the Maharashtra chief minister on Saturday, saying it was a "betrayal of people's mandate" and the "illegitimate" government formation will self-destruct.

The month-long political impasse in Maharashtra ended dramatically on Saturday with the BJP's Fadnavis returning as the chief minister, backed by the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP).

NCP's Ajit Pawar was sworn-in as the deputy chief minister.
