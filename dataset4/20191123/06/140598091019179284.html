8 of the best Christmas gifts for foodies, handpicked by Yotam Ottolenghi

23 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Telegraph Writers
The renowned Israeli-English chef, restaurant owner and food writer Yotam Ottolenghi handpicks his favourite foodie gifts for Christmas 2019 

Ottolenghi has become a household name when it comes to cooking. More of us are reaching for the Israeli-born chef's words of culinary wisdom, be it from his debut recipe collection of The Cookbook first published in 2008 or last year's bestseller, Simple. 

Why? Perhaps it's because of his inventive attitude to food. His recipes are bold, yet hearty, achievable yet time-conscious - appealing for the modern day connoisseur. 

Dedicated fans also enjoy dining in one of his many London based restaurants, where the 'Ottolinghi Effect' is at its strongest. Whether they pop in for a sweet treat in Notting Hill or seasonal inspired pre-theatre dinner in Soho, diners continue to return to enjoy old and trusted favourites, the Ottolenghi way.  

As to what the chef does for Christmas, he tells us,"We keep it fairly traditional, with turkey, stuffing and roast potatoes, although aubergine, tahini and lemons make a visit to the Christmas table as well - my take on the Three Wise Men." Here he handpicks 8 of his favourite gifts for 2019:
