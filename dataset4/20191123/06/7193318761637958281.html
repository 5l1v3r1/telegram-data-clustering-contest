Nicoma Park police officer accused of child sexual abuse

23 Nov 2019, 06:05 by Josh Dulaney

NICOMA PARK — A Nicoma Park police officer was arrested Friday and is accused of child sexual abuse.

Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation agents arrested Douglas Spencer, 51, after the Nicoma Park Police Department requested assistance on a report of alleged inappropriate touching of a minor child by one of their officers.

The alleged incidents were several years apart and involved two 14-year-old females, according to OSBI.

Spencer was arrested Friday morning at the Nicoma Park Police Department and booked into the Oklahoma County jail on a $450,000 bond.

He is accused of rape in the first degree by instrumentation and indecent or lewd acts to a child under 16.

The OSBI investigation is ongoing.
