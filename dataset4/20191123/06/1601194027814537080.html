Triple H and Shawn Michaels comment on NXT's SmackDown invasion

23 Nov 2019, 06:47 by Arunava Ghoshal

The final episode of Friday Night SmackDown before WWE Survivor Series was full of chaos owing to the repeated rundowns from the NXT and RAW roster.

In the first match of the night, Rhea Ripley managed to gain a victory over RAW's Charlotte Flair and SmackDown's Sasha Banks in a triple threat battle. This was followed by the debut match of The Undisputed Era on the Blue brand as they collided against The New Day and Heavy Machinery. Once again, the representatives of the Black & Gold brand stood tall on SmackDown with Roderick Strong pinning Otis. NXT Women's Champion Shayna Baszler also showed up on the show and engaged in a brawl with the SmackDown Women's Champion Bayley.

The show concluded with Triple H, Shawn Michaels and Road Dogg leading the NXT roster to takeover SmackDown en route to Survivor Series.

DX reunites

Triple H and company recreated the DX invasion from the days of Attitude Era on tonight's SmackDown. Like the original instance, they were successful tonight as well. The WWE COO took to Twitter to reflect on the same after SmackDown went off-air.
