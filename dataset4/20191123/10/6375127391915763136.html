Speeding car runs over couple

23 Nov 2019, 10:23 by Alfred P. Dalizon

A couple was killed when they were hit by a speeding car yesterday morning in Sablayan, Occidentl Mindoro.

This was confirmed yesterday by Police Regional Office (PRO) Mimaropa spokesperson Police Lt.Col. Imelda Tolentino.

Tolentino identified the fatalities as Ricardo Ladringan, 45, village watchman and Maribel Ladringan, 42, vendor, both residents of Sitio Payompon, Bgy.Sta Lucia, Sablayan, Occidental Mindoro.

Taken to the nearest hospital due to minor injuries was Roberto Bernabe San Jose, 56, Minister of Iglesia Ni Cristo (INC) and resident of Bgy. Pag-asa, San Jose, Occidental Mindoro.

Reports reaching Camp Crame said the accident occurred at around 6 a.m. along the road at Sitio Payompon, Bgy.Sta Lucia in the said province.

Initial investigation showed that San Jose was driving his Toyota Innova with plate numbet ZKU-238 going to North direction and tried to overtake a bus but lost control of the vehicle and hit the couple who were standing in the area.

The victims were rushed to San Sebastian District Hospital but were pronounced dead on arrival by attending physician due to severe injuries.

The driver of the vehicle also sustained injuries and was rushed to the hospital.

After the treatment, the driver was brought to Sablayan Municipal Police Station for proper disposition.
