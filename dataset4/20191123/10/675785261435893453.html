Chanelle Hayes debuts darker locks and sleek new look as she celebrates weight loss

The 32-year-old debuted her darker locks on Instagram as she continues to slim down

23 Nov 2019, 10:47 by Mirror.co.uk

She is super proud after dropping two dress sizes - and it appears Chanelle Hayes quest for glamour hasn't ended there as she debuted a super sleek look on Istagram.

Showing off her extra glamorous look to her 100k Instagram follows Chanelle had added some dark brows and flattering blush nude lipstick.

Her darker locks perfectly complemented her sassy new look as she pledged to up her vitamin intake with a new pack of Feel multiviatmins.

Sharing her new haircut and colour on Instagram Chanelle wrote: "I’m staying brunette for a while but my end goal over the next few years is to go gingery-red-auburn."

Fans loved her new look, with one gushing on Instagram: "You’re one of these lucky people who suit both light and dark hair. It looks beautiful!"

Another added: "WOW........you look amazing, the hair is gorg & colour is lovely too."

The Instagram sensation recently updated her fans about her weight loss and she gushed it had given her "confidence" as well as "happiness".

Chanelle did admit she had been putting in a lot of hard work to maintain her fitness levels.

"In losing all that excess weight I have gained more confidence and happiness," she wrote.

"If I had been EXTREME with my eating choices I am sure I could be much smaller and lighter than I am now however I have been working HARDDDD at keeping (and increasing) my muscle mass.

"Over the rest of this year I will be cutting down my body fat slowly and steadily. I want to make a point that I am choosing to take my time in order to keep my muscle."

Chanelle made a promise to herself to lose weight when Ryan Oates broke her heart in November two years ago in 2017.

Her ex walked out on her just weeks after she gave birth to her second child Frankie.

Chanelle also has a son Blakely from a previous romance with Matthew Bates.

The CBB star has struggled with her weight while she has been in the limelight as she has yo-yoed between a size 20 and a size eight.
