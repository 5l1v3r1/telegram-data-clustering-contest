Akshay Kumar as Varun Batra shares his medical report from ‘Good Newwz’, it’s sure to leave you in splits

23 Nov 2019, 10:26 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood hunk Akshay Kumar who is all set for the release of his comedy film ‘Good Newwz’, has taken to his social media handle to serve a dose of laughter. He has shared his character Varun Batra’s medical reports and the condition as well as the treatment mentioned in the same is sure to crack you up.

In the report, Varun Batra is said to have symptoms of being annoyed by goof ups, the condition he is suffering from is top quality bearer and the treatment prescribed to him is always listening to his wife. This report is complemented with a quirky picture of Varun Batra, and the caption reads, “#MeetTheBatras - Varun Batra's appointment is confirmed for 27th Dec! #GoodNewwz”

Slated to release on December 27, ‘Good Newwz’ is a Raj Mehta directorial. It’s a story of one medical goof up that changes the lives of two couples who are trying to conceive a baby.

In the movie, alongside Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani will be seen playing the lead roles.
