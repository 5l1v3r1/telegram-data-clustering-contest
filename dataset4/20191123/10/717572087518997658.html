Nokia smartphones getting Chat features in Android messaging app

23 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Nayan

Many Nokia smartphone users have reported availability of new “Chat features” in the native Android messaging app. We also received the chat feature in couple of our daily drivers.

The chat feature works on both Wi-Fi and mobile data and allows to send high-quality media to those with chat features. You can also use the messaging feature with out the chat features aftre disabling it if you don’t want.

You can send read recepts and let others know that you are typing. You can also set auto-download of media over mobile data or Wi-Fi.

If you haven’t receiived the feature yet perhaps you need to update the messaging app via Google Play store.
