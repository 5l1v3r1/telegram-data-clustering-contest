Early spring gardening workshops set

23 Nov 2019, 10:17

The Oklahoma County Master Gardeners will present the three-day workshop for novices and experienced gardeners alike, Home Gardening 101, on three successive Saturdays in early spring.

Topics will include working with and improving Oklahoma soils; home composting; turfgrass management; smart irrigation and water conservation; vegetable and flower gardening; and insects and plant diseases. The workshop will be from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. on three Saturdays — Feb. 22, Feb. 29, and March 7 — at the OSU Extension Conference Center at 2500 NE 63. Bringing a sack lunch is suggested.

Registration in advance is required. Cost is $75. Organizers note that tuition makes a good Christmas gift for a gardening friend or relative. Register by mailing contact information (name, complete address, phone number and email) with a check for $75 made payable to OSU Extension to OSU Extension Center, Attn: HG 101, 2500 NE 63, Oklahoma City, OK 73111.

For more information, call 713-1125 and ask for Diana.
