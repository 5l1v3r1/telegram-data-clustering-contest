Thalaivi FIRST LOOK: Shape Shifter Kangana Ranaut Is Now Jayalalithaa And We Are Mighty Impressed - WATCH VIDEO

23 Nov 2019, 10:20 by Gretel Sequeira

The first look of Kangana Ranaut from the much-anticipated film Thalaivi is finally out. While the actress looks unrecognizable, Kangana’s first looks clearly bring out the character of Jayalalithaa that she is set to play in the film and she is impressive

Kangana Ranaut has been busy gearing up and prepping for her role in the upcoming former Tamil Nadu Chief Minister J Jayalalithaa’s biopic. Since months now, the actress has been taking dance lessons and undergoing prosthetic testing in order to fit in the role of the character she is set to play. While Kangana is busy working hard for her role in the film Thalaivi, the first look from the film is finally out and the actress has once again left us impressed. As Kangana who has been a shapeshifter in her films earlier, the actress has perfectly tuned in to the character of the former Tamil Nadu Chief Minister J Jayalalithaa and looks unrecognizable.

Kangana Ranaut’s team took to Twitter to share the first look of the actress from her upcoming film Thalaivi. While Kangana looks completely unrecognizable in the first look poster of the film, the actress has managed well to fit into the role of the former Tamil Nadu Chief Minister. With her first look from the film, the elegance and poise Kangana has brought in to the look of the character she is playing is equally impressive. The actress has nailed it to excellence and has proved true to the role she is set to play in the film.
