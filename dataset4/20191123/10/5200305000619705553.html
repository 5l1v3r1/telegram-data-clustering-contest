Box Office: Ayushmann Khurrana’s ‘Bala’ joins Rs. 100 crore club

23 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Raj Baddhan

Ayushmann Khurrana’s latest release ‘Bala’ has become the latest film to enter the lucrative Rs. 100 crore club in India.

The film based on premature balding, is the third Khurrana film to enter the club after ‘Badhaai Ho’ and ‘Dream Girl’. ‘Bala’ released three weeks ago and surpassed the Rs. 100 crore mark, after it grabbed another Rs. 1.35 crore on Friday, totalling its business to Rs. 100.15 crore.

‘Bala’ has crossed Rs. 150 crore in global business. On Friday, actress Bhumi Pednekar expressed her happiness on social media. She said,”#Bala crosses another milestone. And it’s a big one!
