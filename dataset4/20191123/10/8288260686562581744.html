Heartbreaking - Little girl's blackened bedroom as tower block fire completely destroys family flat

The child's bedroom can be seen ruined, along with her pink princess castle, in a devastating picture from Edgbaston

23 Nov 2019, 10:38 by Stephanie Balloo, https://www.facebook.com/MissBalloo/

A blackened princess castle sits in the darkness of a smoke-damaged room following a devastating flat fire in the city.

It is the heartbreaking image which captures the stark aftermath of a tower block blaze on Friday afternoon (November 22).

Fortunately no one was injured as flames tore through a 12th floor flat off Priory Road in Edgbaston.

But pictures shared by West Midlands Fire Service show the destruction of a child's bedroom, the kitchen and the hallway.

Several panicked residents raised the alarm with the fire service - with thirty firefighters then racing to the scene around 12.30pm.

Thankfully, the six crews managed to get the blaze under control before it affected other floors.

Ladywood Watch Commander, Mike Long, shared images of the incident and said: "Flat fire on the 12th floor of a block of flats in Edgbaston, quickly under control thanks to rapid intervention of crews today.

"Note the added danger of fallen cables."

It is not known what sparked the inferno.

A spokeswoman for West Midlands Fire Service added: "We had the call at 12.30pm to the 12th floor of a block of flats just off Priory Road.

"We had six appliances attending. We had quite a lot of repeat calls about this one. Not believed to be any injuries.

"We came away at 3.45pm."
