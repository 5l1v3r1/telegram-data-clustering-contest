Ambati Rayudu Accuses Hyderabad CA of Indulging in Corrupt Practices

Rayudu’s outrage comes a few months after he made a U-turn on his retirement from all forms of cricket to play for Hyderabad in domestic cricket.

23 Nov 2019, 10:19

Ambati Rayudu on Saturday lashed out at the Hyderabad Cricket Association (HCA) of indulging in corrupt practices and appealed. He appealed to Telangana’s Minister for Industry and Municipal Administration KT Rama Rao to look into the matter.

The former India middle order batsman alleged corruption in the HCA one day after taking a break for a few weeks from playing first-class cricket for Hyderabad.
