Nokia 3.2, 5, 6, 6.1, 5.1 Plus getting Google Assistant’s Ambient mode in India now

23 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Nayan

We have today received many tips from our readers about Nokia smartphones including Nokia 3.2, 5, 6, 6.1, 5.1 Plus receiving the Google Assistant’s Ambient mode feature in India. You can check for the feature availability by going to Settings–>Google–>Account services–>Search, Assistant & Voice–>Google Assistant–>Assistant–>Scroll down to Assitant devices–>Phone.

While Nokia 6.2 and 7.2 launched with the Google Assistant’s Ambient mode as an exclusive feature , it was surprisingly unavailable on even these two devices in India. But readers from other markets started reporting that they were now able to use “Ambient mode” on their Nokia smartphones in what looked like a sever-side roll-out of the feature by Google. So, seems that Google has finally made it available in India too.

Google describes Ambient Mode as a new visual overview that gets triggered when devices are plugged in. The Ambient Mode makes it easier to see notifications and reminders, start a playlist and control smart home devices on the lock screen of your Nokia smartphone.

While your mobile device is charging on a nightstand, living room table, or kitchen counter, it can still help you stay on top of your day.
