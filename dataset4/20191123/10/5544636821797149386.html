“Leave Me Alone”, Davido’s Chioma Tells Her Followers

23 Nov 2019, 10:54 by Oyindamola Olubajo

 

Davido‘s fiancee, Chioma Rowland has shunned some of her Instagram followers who kept lecturing her on how to raise her son, David junior.

Concise News reports that she shared a video of herself bottle feeding her son while listening to a song off Davido’s new album ‘A good time’ when her followers advised her to breastfeed him often.

A follower said: “absolutely beautiful experience, but please let him suck directly for close bonding” after which she responded by saying she does that often.

Another follower, “bottle feeding does not create a bond, also it is not healthy”

In her reaction, Chioma who seemingly lost patience asked the followers to let her be.

She said “Bruh you people should leave me alone”

Davido had announced the birth of his son, David Adedeji Ifeanyi Adeleke Jr in the early hours of Sunday, October 20.

The DMW chief shared the news on his verified Twitter handle, describing his wife-to-be, Chioma Rowland, as “strong.”

Accompanied with a picture of him carrying the newborn, Davido wrote: “OMOBA TI DE!!! DAVID ADEDEJI ADELEKE Jr I !! D PRINCE IS HERE!!!! 20 – 10 – 2019 !!! Love you my STRONG WIFE!!! I LOVE YOU!!!!!”
