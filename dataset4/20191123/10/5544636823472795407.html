Dino Melaye bags First Cinema Movie Role

23 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Kogi state senator, Dino Melaye has got his first cinema movie role in a new Nollywood blockbuster titled ‘Lemonade’ set to premiere December 11, this year.

Lemonade, a production of Joy Media Productions and directed by prolific film maker, Lummie Edevibe of Filmcorp, tells the story of a single mum and aspiring writer who finds courage to leave a toxic relationship, and to pursue her dreams on her own.
