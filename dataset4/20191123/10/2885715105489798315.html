Pagalpanti box office collection Day 1: Anil Kapoor starrer mints Rs 4.75 crore

Pagalpanti box office collection Day 1: This Anees Bazmee directorial, starring Anil Kapoor, John Abraham, Ileana D'Cruz, Arshad Warsi, Urvashi Rautela, Pulkit Samrat, Kriti Kharbanda and Saurabh Shukla, minted Rs 4.75 crore on its opening day.

23 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Entertainment Desk

Bollywood comedy Pagalpanti opened with Rs 4.75 crore on its opening day, according to Bollywood Hungama. The Anees Bazmee directorial stars Anil Kapoor, John Abraham, Ileana D’Cruz, Arshad Warsi, Urvashi Rautela, Pulkit Samrat, Kriti Kharbanda and Saurabh Shukla.

Trade analyst Girish Johar had projected the film’s first-day total to be around Rs 9 to 10 crore.

Johar told Indianexpress.com earlier, “Pagalpanti is a quirky family comedy directed by Anees Bazmee. It has an ensemble cast of Anil Kapoor, John Abraham, Pulkit Samrat and Arshad Warsi among others supporting it. All the actors, with their fan following, have the power to pull the audience to the theaters.”

He added, “The film looks like a clean commercial comedy. After a long time, a film has come where you can keep your brains out and just enjoy some time with your family. The trailer is also being liked and songs have created awareness among the audience. So, I think Pagalpanti should open at around Rs 9-10 crore. It will probably have a good weekend at the box office.”
