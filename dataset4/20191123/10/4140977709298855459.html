LEG Vs BAR Dream11 : Mismatch sees table toppers Barcelona and languishing bottom side Leganes faceoff in Saturday’s opening tie.

As Barcelona get ready to engulf themselves back into the gruelling and arduous title challenge in La Liga, the table toppers couldn’t have asked for a more easy to tie to resume proceedings in the league. When the side get ready to step out to middle today, they’ll be squaring off against last placed Leganes, a side who should be nothing more than mere pushovers for them.

With just the one wicket from 13 matches, this Leganes unit is undoubtedly the worst side in the league. The side have put in appalling, abysmal and apathy filled displays, performances which have seen their ardent supporters be left perplexed and exasperated with the brand of football they have been doled out on a persistent basis by the side.

Hammered down dead into last place, the writing on the wall for the side when Barcelona come calling today looks blatantly clear. For the home side, this encounter is a foregone conclusion, a result which looks set to have only one ending with Leganes’ aspirations of salvaging anything from this encounter begin negligible.

Elsewhere, Barcelona will be looking to not only win this one but thump Leganes in this clash as they look to lay down a marker and put in a pleasing to the eye display, one which sees them mount the pressure on Real Madrid who are persistently snapping on their heels. With both the sides level on 25 points ahead of today’s impending clash, a colossal win for Barcelona would massively spur their title fight, something we envisage the side doing today.
