2 Key Players Ruled Out For Sundowns

23 Nov 2019, 10:55 by Soccer Laduma

The Brazilians will lock horns with Golden Arrows at the Sugar Ray Xulu Stadium today, although still have some of their key players ruled of action.

Sundowns and Arrows will face off in the first Telkom Knockout semi-final fixture this afternoon, with the Brazilians needing to do one better after losing 3-2 to Steve Komphela’s side in the Absa Premiership.

However, the club has since confirmed that they will be going in to the encounter without the services of Sibusiso Vilakazi and Themba Zwane as the duo are still nursing injuries.

Zwane is currently still nursing a hamstring injury while Vilakazi injured his foot.

On the other hand, Anthony Laffor, Lucky Mohomi and Oupa Manyisa are still nursing their long-term injuries, with Phakamani Mahlambi also undergoing ankle rehabilitation.
