Amitabh Bachchan on Kangana Ranaut: “Very beautiful, No.1 & famous artist”

23 Nov 2019, 10:31 by Sahar Junejo

Amitabh Bachchan was all praise for Kangana Ranaut during a recent episode of his hit gameshow ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati’.

After a question about Bollywood actresses, including Ranaut, came up, Bachchan shared “Kangana Ranaut Ji is a very beautiful, number one and famous artist. She has won many awards including National Awards and Filmfare Awards.”, The Asian Age reported.

Ranaut is currently hard at work preparing for her upcoming trilingual film ‘Thalaivi’, which is a biopic on actress and politician Jayalalithaa.
