Occupant treated for smoke inhalation after small kitchen fire in Dundee

One person was treated by paramedics after a small kitchen fire in Dundee this morning.

23 Nov 2019, 10:43 by Jake Keith

The blaze, which took place in a flat on Stirling Avenue, occurred at 7.40am.

The occupant was treated for smoke inhalation but is not believed to have suffered serious injury.

A spokesperson for the Scottish Fire and Rescue Service said the stop messaged was received at 8.12am.
