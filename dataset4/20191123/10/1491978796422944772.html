Catching big waves at Nazaré - in pictures

23 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Olivier Morin/AFP via Getty Images

Nazaré in Portugal hosts one the two World Surfing League’s Big Wave surfing contests, with waves reaching up to 30 metres during winter. For anyone seeking heart-pounding action, Big Wave is a place where humans constantly push the boundaries of what’s possible

Surfers keep an eye on early-morning conditions before a free surfing session.

Legend of big waves Garrett McNamara. The 52-year-old American is known for discovering the biggest waves in the world at Nazaré. He is the eight-times Guinness World Record holder for the largest wave ever surfed.
