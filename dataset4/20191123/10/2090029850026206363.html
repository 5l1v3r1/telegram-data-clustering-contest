Between Zlatan Ibile and a fan begging for school fees

23 Nov 2019, 10:08 by Damilola Ismail

Zlatan Ibile has dished a sub to a fan who begged for financial assistance from him to pay his school fees.

The request for school fees from the fan via social media led to Zlatan Ibile revealing he did not complete his tertiary education.

The social media user, @HMphotographyx revealed he had been waiting to get this admission for 6 years and he now that he got it, he doesn’t have the admission fee and is ready to show proof of his admission. He wrote;

“I swear I just got admission into Yabatech after waiting for six years, admission fee and school fee him be problem now, I have proof of admission”

The tweet got Zlatan’s attention but his response was not what the fan must have anticipated. He told said God probably does not want him to go to school, adding that he too didn’t complete his education. He wrote;

“Maybe God no want make you go school, me sef no finish”
