Hotel Mumbai Song 'Bharat Salaam': An ode to Unsung Heroes of 26/11 Attacks

Bharat Salaam song pays homage to the martyrs of the 26/11 Taj Mahal Palace attacks. Watch here.

23 Nov 2019, 10:54

Ahead of Hotel Mumbai’s release, the makers of the film have launched a new song – Bharat Salaam. It is a song created by the makers as an homage to the martyrs of the 26/11 Taj Mahal Palace attacks. The track seems a poignant reminder of thanking the unsung heroes of the unfaithful 26/11 attacks.

Composed and sung by Mithoon along with Sunidhi Chauhan and BPraak, it’s a touching number that reiterates people’s belief in humanity. The overarching theme of the film is to celebrate the human spirit which triumphed in the face of adversity on the fateful series of events that started on November 26, 2008.

The song encapsulates the same sentiment and has been written by Mithoon himself. The song also features a choir of 40 singers alongside the notable singers. Presented by Zee Studios and Purpose Entertainment, Hotel Mumbai will be released on November 29 in English, Hindi, Tamil and Telugu languages.
