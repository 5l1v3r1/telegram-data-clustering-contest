Maharashtra government formation twist leave people shocked. Twitter calls it best season of GoT

The immense shock that people got after Devendra Fadnavis took oath as the CM of Maharashtra has led to Twitter comparing the situation to the hit series, Game of Thrones.

23 Nov 2019, 10:53

There are some memes that you relate to on a different level. The most awkward and unexplainable situations can easily be explained by memes and that is exactly what all the memers are doing right now with the Maharashtra government formation on Twitter.

The President's rule ended in Maharashtra in the morning as Bharatiya Janata Party's Devendra Fadnavis took oath as the Chief Minister with NCP leader Ajit Pawar as his deputy CM.

Till last night a lot of people were sure that Uddhav Thackray will be the chosen one to run the state. However, it is this relationship between BJP and Ajit Pawar that nobody had foreseen. These shocking turn of events were difficult for a lot of people to digest.

All this confusion and sharp twists led to Twitterati comparing the current scenario in Maharashtra to the hit TV series, Game of Thrones which is famous for its suspense and jaw-dropping moments.

Maharashtra Politics started trending on Twitter and as a result, the memes that were generated were just mind-blowing. Have a look.
