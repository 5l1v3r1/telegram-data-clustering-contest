Revolt Within The Family? Ajit’s Decision To Support BJP Is ‘Personal’, Not Endorsed By NCP, Says Sharad Pawar

23 Nov 2019, 10:25 by Swarajya Staff

Nationalist Congress Party Sharad Pawar on Saturday (23 November) said that the move by the NCP Group Leader in Maharashtra Legislature to form a government with the Bharatiya Janata Party was "his personal decision and not of the NCP".

"We place on record that we do not support or endorse this decision of his," Sharad Pawar said, indicating a vertical split in the NCP, though the complete details are yet to emerge.
