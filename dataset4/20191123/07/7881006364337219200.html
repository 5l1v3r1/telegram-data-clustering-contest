‘Not NCP’s decision’: Sharad Pawar as nephew Ajit props up BJP in govt

Sharad Pawar also tweeted: 'We place on record that we do not support or endorse this decision of his.'

23 Nov 2019, 07:38 by GARGI CHAUDHRY

Mumbai: Responding to the drastic twist in Maharashtra politics, the Nationalist Congress Party chief Sharad Pawar on Saturday said that it was Ajit Pawar's decision to support the BJP to form the Maharashtra government.
