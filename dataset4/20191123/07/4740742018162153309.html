Ten unique apprenticeships available from ghost hunter to chocolate maker

Whatever your interests, there is an apprenticeship out there for you

23 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Nathalie Gannon

You are never too old to start an apprenticeship. 

If you are aged 16 or above, and not in full time education, you can take part in an apprenticeship scheme. They are the perfect opportunity to start down a new career path, and they give you the freedom to learn, work, and get paid.

Apprenticeships are a convenient route into a profession that doesn’t land you with a hefty student debt, and there are thousands of different schemes to choose from that could take you down many diverse paths.

And we mean thousands. Many people think apprentices only cater to a handful of different career options but that couldn’t be further from the truth.

And here are ten unique apprenticeships on offer to prove it:

Nuclear Scientist

Looking for a brand spanking nu-clear career? This apprenticeship lands you with a degree and some cash when it’s all finished (it even offers annual pay increases). If you’re a science or maths whizz there are loads of apprenticeships that offer you money to study.

Puppy Pamperer

Love a wet nose and the pitter-patter of tiny paws? Being an animal groomer doesn’t sound too ruff. This apprenticeship will bring you the most loveable clientele, that will always be happy to see you, and will have bundles of pawsitivity. 

Martial Artist

Tae Kwon-Do or Tae Kwon-Don’t? Take that fighting spirit and apply it to an apprenticeship that will condition both your body and mind and give you the opportunity to transform into a role model for others in the process.

Sandwich Artist

Are you someone that goes against the (whole)grain? If you fancy yourself a Dali with the deli meats and have great customer service skills, then an apprenticeship as a Sandwich Artist might be for you.

Guitar Maker

Fret-ting about your career? Don’t get strung up looking for a degree and learn a niche craft. This independent company offers courses in guitar building and apprenticeships in making precision tools for guitar making. 

Submarine Engineer

Student debt can sometimes get you into deep water. This apprenticeship does the complete opposite, offering a healthy salary with a lot of growth opportunity. A varied role with multiple responsibilities, this is one apprenticeship in which you could rise to the top.

Pizzaiolo

Do you know your prosciutto from your pepperoni? Get a pizza the action and earn some dough! This is an apprenticeship that will definitely make you popular amongst your friends, because pizza is life.

Chocolate Maker

Satisfy your sweet tooth and choose from a multitude of roles on offer from Nestlé. They are one of the world’s largest food and drink producers, and have a range of apprenticeship roles from factory floor to corporate finance.

Boat Maker

Knot shore what you want out of a career? Maybe boatmaking will be your lega-sea. This apprenticeship might float your boat and earn you qualifications in marine engineering.

Ghost Hunter

If you aren’t afraid of things that go bump in the night, and like the sound of being a specter spectator this might be the one for you. Gloucester’s resident Ghost Hunter was searching for an apprentice, and although that role has now been filled, you can see that trainee paranormal investigators have avoided landing in a dead-end job!

As you can see, the number of opportunities available are limitless! If these roles have peaked your interest have an explore of the different industries available and earn whilst you learn!
