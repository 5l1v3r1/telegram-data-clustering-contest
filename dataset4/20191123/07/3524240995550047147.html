Rihanna’s ex, Chris Brown, welcomes second child with ex-girlfriend

23 Nov 2019, 07:32 by Agency Report

American singer and song writer, Christopher Maurice Brown, a.k.a Chris Brown, has welcomed his second child with another ex-girlfriend, Ammika Harris.

According to TMZ, the music star and his girlfriend welcomed a baby boy.

Although the singer has not come out, outright, to announce the good news, Chris posted a cryptic message on his Instagram page @ Chrisbrownofficial sharing a photo of himself looking down and smiling at what is seeming his baby boy.

The 30-year old singer went on to caption the photo with a quote of a date that appears to be the date of the arrival of the baby.
