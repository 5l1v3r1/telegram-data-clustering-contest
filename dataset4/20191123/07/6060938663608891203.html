‘Housefull 4’ box office collection Week 5: The Akshay Kumar starrer sees a 75% drop in collections on fifth Friday

23 Nov 2019, 07:56 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After four smashing weeks at the box office, Akshay Kumar's multi-starrer 'Housefull 4' is showing signs of slowing down at the domestic box office.

Housefull 4 Movie Review : Not so full of comedy

The film that entered the 200-crore club recently, saw a huge 75 % drop in collections from last week, according to a report on BoxofficeIndia.com. The film earned an estimated Rs 10-12 lakh nett on its fifth Friday, thus taking the overall total to approximately Rs 204.17 crore.

The Fahad Samji directorial which collected Rs 135.86 crore in week 1, followed it up with collections of Rs 48.07 crore, Rs 16.27 crore and Rs 3.85 crore in weeks 2, 3, and 4 respectively.

The film’s collections have trade experts predicting that Akshay may just be on the brink of cashing in a 1000-crore collection at the box office with consecutive back-to-back hit films in 2019.

'Housefull 4' which also stars Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde in the lead roles released along with Rajkummar Rao's 'Made In China' and Taapsee Pannu-Bhumi Pednekar's 'Saand Ki Aankh' but managed to dominate them and continued its impressive run for weeks.

Meanwhile, Akshay is now getting ready for the launch of 'Good Newwz' that is slated for a Christmas 2019 release. The film’s trailer featuring Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani was well received by fans who have been eagerly awaiting its big release.
