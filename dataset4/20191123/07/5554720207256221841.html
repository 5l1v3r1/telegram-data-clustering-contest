Nita Ambani’s daughter-in-law Shloka Mehta gets playful as Mukesh Ambani’s nephew dances to Sara Ali Khan’s Simmba track

23 Nov 2019, 07:42 by JKR Staff

Nita Ambani’s daughter-in-law Shloka Mehta was the star attraction at the wedding function of Arjun Kothari, the nephew of Mukesh Ambani. First, she dazzled in a ‘red-hot’ avatar during the pre-wedding party hosted by Mukesh and Nita Ambani at their official south Mumbai residence Antilia. But, what has set the internet on fire all over again is a video showing Shloka in a playful mood as she fanned her brother-in-law groom Arjun.

In the video shared by one of her fan pages on Instagram, Shloka was seen dressed in a stunning embellished lenhga choli. She completed her look with a red-stone long necklace and a diamond choker bangles, earrings and maang tika to match. While Arjun danced to the beat of Seeti Bajaaye track from Sara Ali Khan’s Simmba, Shloka playfully fanned the groom. The sweet gesture by Shloka Mehta has left her fans awestruck. Fans are drooling over not just her outfit but also Shloka’s gesture as a ‘sweet bhabhi.’ 

In another video, Shloka was seen blowing the kiss as she greeted the bride during the wedding ceremony. Also standing next to her was Mukesh Ambani’s niece Isheta Salgaocar and Radhika Merchant, who is set to marry Anant Ambani.

Arjun Kothari is the son of Mukesh Ambani’s sister Nina Kothari. India’s richest businessman had hosted a pre-wedding party at his house on 10 November when a host of Bollywood celebrities including Shahid Kapoor and Mira Rajput, Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai and others had joined the celebrations.

Isha Ambani too was also a star attraction at her cousin’s wedding as she flaunted her shimmery gold lehenga with colourful 3D floral embellishments. Fashin designers Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla, who designed her outfit, took to Instagram to share Isha’s photos.  Isha chose to keep her makeup simple but accessorised it with a pair of diamond earrings.

Arjun Kothari’s wedding may be over but Shloka’s style statement and playful performances will be remembered for a long time.
