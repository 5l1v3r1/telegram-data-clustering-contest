Tiwa Savage Serves New Song “Owo Mi Da”

23 Nov 2019, 07:53 by Michael Bamidele

How would you turn up for “detty December” if your account is red while your debtors are living la vida loca on the gram with your money?

Well, Nigerian Afrobeats artist Tiwa Savage has released a new song titled “Owo Mi Da” which will get you dancing and in the mood to retrieve all outstanding credit.

“Owo Mi Da” is a follow-up to Savage’s previous hit track “44-99”.
