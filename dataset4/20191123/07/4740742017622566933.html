Tesco is running a fuel deal to get you 10p off per litre - how to get it

The deal will be available until the end of this month

23 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Emma Munbodh, Alexander Brock

Tesco customers will be able to save money while buying groceries this month as part of the supermarket's new fuel deal.

Shoppers can get 10p off every litre when they spent £60 in stores.

The discount will be available until December 1 and applies to a single shop either in-store or online, Mirror Online reports.

If you shop in-store, your receipt will automatically include a money-off fuel coupon if you spend at least £60.

Online shoppers will instead get a fuel coupon when their shopping is delivered.

Their coupons can be redeemed at any UK Tesco fuel station kiosk.

However, the deal excludes anything you buy at any café, tobacco kiosk, opticians, phone store, concessions, travel money or Tesco Bank.

When you receive your fuel coupons, you have two weeks to redeem it.

Online shoppers have until December 15 to use their coupons however.
