Liverpool are up against their former manager Roy Hodgson when they take on Crystal Palace this weekend.

The Reds are unbeaten in the league this season and hold an eight-point lead at the top after a 3-1 win over champions Manchester City in their last game.

Palace, on the other hand, have lost three of their last four top-flight games and will be desperate to avoid a fourth consecutive home league defeat by the Reds.

It is sure to be an entertaining encounter as both teams look to return from the international break in impressive fashion.
