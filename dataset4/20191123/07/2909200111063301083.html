As a Boss I’ve learnt that failure will always come – DBlack

23 Nov 2019, 07:34 by MyNewsGH

Rapper D Black has opened up on some challenges he’s had to overcome as an entrepreneur.

In a tweet, the rapper said he has come to accept the fact that failure is inevitable in business.

He mentioned that it’s important to persevere as n entrepreneur in order to attain the height to intend attaining.

He said ” As a boss, as a CEO, I’ve learned that failure will always come, obstacles and set backs will always come, you can’t avoid them.

I’ve learned to give it a smile, humble myself to it and then use it as a springboard to send me on my way to strength, more success, and fulfillment”.
