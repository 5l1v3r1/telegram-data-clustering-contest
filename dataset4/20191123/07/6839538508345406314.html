33-year-old Balga man and 57-year-old Mirrabooka man charged after Australian Federal Police intercept kilogram of methamphetamine

23 Nov 2019, 07:44

Two men have appeared in court today after a drug bust targeting methylamphetamine imported from Canada.

Australian Border Force intercepted nearly one kilo of meth at an air cargo depot on Monday, concealed in the floor of a cardboard box.

The investigation resulted in charges against a 33-year-old Balga man and a 57-year-old Mirrabooka man.

The pair were charged with attempting to possess an illegal drug with intent to supply.

The second man was also charged over an alleged $4500 cash also seized during the searches.

They are next due to appear in court in early December.
