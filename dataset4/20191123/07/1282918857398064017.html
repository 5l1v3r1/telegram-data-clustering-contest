Bank Loan Fraud: Ex-Bhushan Power And Steel Ltd CMD Sanjay Singal Arrested

23 Nov 2019, 07:44 by Press Trust Of India

General News

The ED on Friday arrested former Bhushan Power and Steel Ltd CMD Sanjay Singal in connection with its probe in a multi-crore money laundering case

Written By Press Trust Of India | Mumbai | Updated On: November 23, 2019 07:44 IST

The ED on Friday arrested former Bhushan Power and Steel Ltd CMD Sanjay Singal in connection with its probe in a multi-crore money laundering case linked to an alleged bank loan fraud, officials said. They said Singal was placed under arrest, under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA), after he was questioned in connection with the case.

Assets worth Rs 4,025 crore attached

He will be produced before a court here on Saturday for seeking further custody, the officials said. He was arrested as he was not cooperating in the probe, they said. The ED has recently attached assets worth Rs 4,025 crore of Bhushan Power and Steel Limited (BPSL).

"An amount of Rs 695.14 crore was introduced as the capital by Sanjay Singal (the then CMD of the company) and his family members in BPSL out of artificially generated long-term capital gains (LTCG) by the diversion of bank loans fund of BPSL," the ED had said.
