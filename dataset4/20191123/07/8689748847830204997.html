Singer Raquel Now An Actress, Stars In First Movie Titled ‘Gold Coast Lounge’

23 Nov 2019, 07:58 by BB Jones

Ghanaian singer, Raquel who is remembered for her relatively hit song, ‘Sweetie o’ ft. Sarkodie, has starred in her first movie titled Gold Coast Lounge.

Raquel plays ‘Black Rose’, a young singer full of dreams and ambition in the movie. This is a film by former music video director Pascal Aka.

‘Gold Coast Lounge’ also features actors Alphonse Menyo, Adjetey Anang, Zynnell Zuh, Cina Soul and many more.

After watching the trailer myself some weeks back, I was convinced this is a beautiful movie that could earn an international recognition.

The concept, dialogue and costume which was set in the past (during the time Ghana was called Gold Coast) is so on point.

The movie will be premiered on January 3, 2020 at Silverbird Cinemas, Accra Mall and January 4, 2020 at Silverbird Cinemas, West Hills Mall.
