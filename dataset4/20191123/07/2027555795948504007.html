Speak out: 23/11/2019

23 Nov 2019, 07:53 by DHNS

Yoga Guru Baba Ramdev said his statements on Periyar E V Ramaswamy were being manipulated to mislead the dalits on social media. The hashtag, #ArrestRamdev, began trending on Twitter on Sunday after Baba Ramdev in a recent TV interview had declared that the followers of social activist Periyar E V Ramaswamy were following intellectual terrorism.
