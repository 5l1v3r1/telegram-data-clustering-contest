Forfar man drove at five times the limit because he wanted fish and chips

A drink-driver who got behind the wheel at almost five times the legal limit because he fancied a chippie has been fined and banned.

23 Nov 2019, 07:44 by Graham Brown

Vladimir Matousek, of Nursery Street, Forfar, was caught in the town’s West High Street on October 1 with a breath alcohol reading of 104 microgrammes, against a legal limit of 22.

Sheriff Derek Reekie told the 60-year-old: “When I heard you decided to drive to the chip shop that is even more alarming.”

Matousek, who pled guilty, was fined £400 and banned from driving for 18 months.
