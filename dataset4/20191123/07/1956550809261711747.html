Dubai’s Stars 'N' Bars launches Utopia Sunset Sessions

Expect stellar tunes, drinks deals and festival vibes at the day-to-night party

23 Nov 2019, 07:03 by Reema Rahman

If you’ve yet to make any plans and are on the hunt for different things to do in Dubai this weekend, why not head to a brand new ‘Tulum style’ sunset party?

Stars 'N' Bars, which can be found at Dubai’s La Mer, has a new Utopia Sunset Sessions, which is held every Saturday.

The day-to-night party kicks off at 4pm and DJs Adam Cotier and Reece Baker will be hitting the decks alongside special guests and international acts.

Between 5pm and 7pm you’ll be able to enjoy select Italian mixed drinks for Dhs39 or sip on free-flowing bubbly and orange juice mixed drinks for Dhs99, all while watching the sun go down from the terrace.

Dressing up is encouraged so don’t be shy about turning up in your best festival-inspired garb.
