Taylor Swift is a person of many talents; she's a singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. But perhaps her most impressive skill is influencing me to want to wear a bodysuit during the Winter. Taylor has been spotted in lots of bodysuits recently, and she posted a picture to Instagram of herself wearing this Reformation Nisa Bodysuit ($68). I have so many questions, namely, how did she make a bodysuit look so cozy and seasonally appropriate, and what lip color is she wearing? Though the jury's still out on the lipstick, I would hurry and pick up the long-sleeved white henley bodysuit before it inevitably sells out.

Long sleeves are a great way to make a bodysuit feel more acceptable during the Winter, and they're an ideal layering option because they sit so smoothly under clothes. I can't believe I never thought to wear one before, but that's all changed now. Plus, it's $68! We love an affordable fashion moment. Get yourself this cute little top, and thank me (and Taylor) later.
