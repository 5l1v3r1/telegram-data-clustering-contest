Atletico Madrid held to 1-1 draw away at high flying Granada in La Liga

Atlético de Madrid failed to seal all three points despite taking the lead

23 Nov 2019, 22:36 by SPORT.es

Granada was held down on Saturday at Los Carmenes by Atlético de Madrid as the game ended 1-1. Granada arrived with the intention of regaining victory after losing 2-0 against Valencia while Atlético de Madrid came from winning 3-1 against Espanyol in the last match played. At the moment, Granada is 6th in the LaLiga, having garnered 21 points from 14 matches, while Atlético de Madrid sits at 3rd with 25 points after playing a total of 14 matches.

Both sides were struggling to produce anything in the first part and the teams went to the dressing room locked in a 0-0.

Atlético de Madrid boosted the game in the second half, with a goal from Renan Lodi 60 minutes in the game. Granada gave an equalizer thanks to a goal from Germán Sánchez 67 minutes in the game to establish the 1-1, finalising the game with a 1-1.

Both coaches made their full set of substitutions. For Granada Carlos Fernández Luna, Darwin Machís and José Antonio Martínez came on for Ramón Azeez, Álvaro Vadillo and Germán Sánchez, while Atlético brought on Thomas Lemar, Félix Joao and Dario Poveda to replace Ángel Correa, Marcos Llorente and Vitolo.

The referee booked Roberto Soldado, Ángel Montoro, Antonio Puertas and Maxime Gonalons from Granada, while for Atlético de Madrid Ángel Correa, Felipe and Marcos Llorente saw a yellow card as well.

Granada will play against Athletic at away stadium, while Atlético de Madrid will face Barcelona at home stadium.
