Manchester City vs Chelsea: Blues armies suffer 2-1 defeat to defending champions

23 Nov 2019, 22:04

- Manchester City vs Chelsea ended in favour of the Citizens

- The defending champions strived for maximum points to get their title race back on track

- Their next Premier League fixture is against Newcastle United at Saint James Park

Manchester City returned to winning ways in the Premier League following their 2-1 victory over Chelsea at the Etihad Stadium.

The defending champions needed the victory to revive their chances of retaining the EPL title at the end of the season.

Although, they currently rank second on the log with nine points adrift of current table-toppers Liverpool who are yet to lose a game so far this campaign.
