Real Madrid 3-1 Real Sociedad: Los Blancos come from behind as Bale jeered

Imanol Alguacil's squad let the match slip from their hands against Madrid at Santiago Bernabeu on Saturday

23 Nov 2019, 22:40 by SPORT.es

Real Madrid recovered from Sergio Ramos' early mistake to beat Real Sociedad 3-1 on Saturday on a night when Los Blancos' supporters turned against Gareth Bale.

Ramos' misplaced backpass allowed La Real to take a surprise lead inside two minutes. Willian Jose latched on to the loose ball and rounded Thibaut Courtois.

Madrid levelled before the break when Karim Benzema scored his 10th goal of the season, using his chest to turn home Luka Modric's free-kick.

Zinedine Zidane's side took the lead after the break when Fede Valverde's speculative effort was deflected home, before Modric added the gloss to the win.

Gareth Bale, meanwhile, made his first appearance in almost two months and was jeered by his own supporters when he came on.

Supporters chanted 'Out, out, out' having been angered by his celebrations with Wales in midweek upon qualifying for Euro 2020 when he danced behind a flag with the slogan: 'Wales, gold, Madrid. In that order.'

Madrid's win moved them level with Barcelona on 28 points at the top of La Liga.
