Nigerian farmers have made the country food sufficient - Buhari

23 Nov 2019, 22:20

- President Muhammadu Buhari says farmers have helped the country save billions of dollars yearly, which would have been spent on food importation

- The Nigerian president discloses that the saved funds are now deployed to other developmental projects, particularly infrastructure

- Buhari notes that the real wealth of our country lies primarily in farming, livestock, foresting and fisheries

President Muhammadu Buhari has commended farmers in the country for making Nigeria sufficient in food.

The Nigerian president in a goodwill message to mark the farmers’ day celebrated in Obie, Rivers state said farmers in the country have helped the country save billions of dollars yearly, which would have been spent on food importation.

Buhari said the saved funds are now been deployed to other developmental projects, particularly infrastructure.

The president stated that farming had brought fortune to millions of Nigerians who embraced it, as it has turned around their social and economic status. He said the real wealth of the country lies primarily in farming, livestock, foresting and fisheries.
