School teacher delivers a classic performance on Ranveer Singh's 'Malhari; watch the viral video here

23 Nov 2019, 22:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sanjay Leela Bhansali's 'Bajirao Mastani' was a huge hit at the box office. The cast of the film, Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, Priyanka Chopra and all the other delivered a stellar performance!

Ranveer was lauded for his Peshwa Bajirao act in the film. Not one can forget his energetic dance in 'Malhari'.

And now, a video of a teacher grooving to tunes of 'Malhaari' has been doing rounds the internet. Reportedly, on November 14 a school teacher put up a stupendous dance performance to a Ranveer's song for their students.

During the performance, she also called her students to join her for the same.
