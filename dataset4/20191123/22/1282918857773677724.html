Former Fortis Promoter Malvinder, Ex-CMD Godhwani Sent To Judicial Custody

23 Nov 2019, 22:12 by Press Trust Of India

General News

Delhi court on Saturday sent former Fortis Healthcare promoter Malvinder Singh and former CMD of Religare Enterprises Limited Sunil Godhwani to judicial custody

Written By Press Trust Of India | Mumbai | Updated On: November 23, 2019 22:12 IST

A Delhi court on Saturday sent former Fortis Healthcare promoter Malvinder Singh and former CMD of Religare Enterprises Limited Sunil Godhwani to judicial custody in a money laundering case related to alleged misappropriation of funds at Religare Finvest Limited (RFL). Special Judge Sandeep Yadav sent them to custody till December 7 after they were produced before a court on expiry of their custodial interrogation by the Enforcement Directorate (ED).

Court dismisses application

ED special public prosecutor Nitesh Rana sought further custodial interrogation, saying more information has emerged and they need to be quizzed. However, the court dismissed the application saying "further custodial interrogation is neither required nor warranted."

The agency had taken both into custody on November 14 from Tihar jail, where they had been lodged due to a case filed by the Delhi Police in relation to the alleged scam, advocate A R Aditya, also appearing for the agency, said. Singh and Godhwani have been accused of laundering money, punishable under Sections 3 and 4 of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, the central agency added.
