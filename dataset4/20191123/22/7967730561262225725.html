Man dead after late-night pub fight near Ballarat

23 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Ashleigh McMillan

A man is in police custody following the death of another man at a hotel in Creswick, 15 kilometres north of Ballarat, overnight.

Homicide Squad detectives believe an altercation between two men took place at the Farmers Arms Hotel on Albert Street just before 2am.

A 39-year-old Creswick man died at the scene.

A man presented at a local police station about 6.30am and is expected to be interviewed by police later this morning.

The investigation remains ongoing and anyone with information is urged to contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000 or submit a confidential report at www.crimestoppersvic.com.au
