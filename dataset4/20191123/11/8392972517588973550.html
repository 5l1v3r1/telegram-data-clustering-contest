Gogglebox's Jenny shocks viewers by munching on Brussels sprouts for a snack

An interesting choice, Jenny!

23 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Entertainment Daily

Jenny Newby and best friend Lee Riley have always been a hugely popular pair after joining hit Channel 4 show Gogglebox in 2014.

But while viewers are big fans of the Hull friends, they don't seem to be too taken by Jenny's choice of TV snack.

On last night's show, Jenny was happily tucking into a plate of Brussels sprouts, which also appeared to be completely raw.

As she bit down on one, Lee turned to her and hilariously asked: "Are you taking the [bleep]?"

"No, I love 'em. It's like eating cabbage," Jenny replied, to which Lee quipped, "Get stuffed. Not exactly coleslaw is it?"

One stunned fan asked: "Jenny why are you eating Brussels sprouts like that?"

A second shocked Gogglebox viewer remarked: "Sprouts! Seriously! They are little farts wrapped in cabbage leaves. Yak yak yak! Not mad, just disappointed in loving sprouts."

Another also pointed out the potential health hazard of eating uncooked sprouts, sharing: "Jenny you can't eat them raw... full of bacteria."
