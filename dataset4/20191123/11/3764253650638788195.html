Pep Guardiola, Man City manager, welcomes Jose Mourinho back to Premier League

23 Nov 2019, 11:04

- Pep Guardiola has welcomed Jose Mourinho back to the Premier League

- The Manchester City manager also sent best wishes to Mauricio Pochettino

- Guardiola and his wards lost to Tottenham last season in the Champions League

Manchester City manager Pep Guardiola has expressed shock over the sacking of Mauricio Pochettino at Tottenham as he welcomed his old rival Mourinho back to Premier League.

Mauricio Pochettino was recently sacked at Tottenham after poor run of form this season in the Premier League and was replaced with Jose Mourinho.

Jose Mourinho was also last year December fired at Manchester United after his side's Premier League defeat against Liverpool.

The former Chelsea manager has however been out of job since then before getting Tottenham position although he was doing some punditry work.
