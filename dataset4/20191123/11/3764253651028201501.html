Alex Iwobi: I learned about Everton's interests while on holiday in Dubai

23 Nov 2019, 11:58

- Alex Iwobi has revealed that he never planned on leaving Arsenal in the summer

- The winger revealed he was in Dubai for holiday when he was called to return and sign on deadline day

- The Super Eagles star admits he is happy with his present club

Nigerian football star Alex Iwobi made a deadline switch from Arsenal to Everton on a five-year deal valued around €32 million.

The move was least expected for the Super Eagles winger who was on holiday after helping his national team win bronze at the 2019 Africa Cup of Nations championship in Egypt this summer.

Since his move to the Goodison Park this campaign, the 23-year-old has managed just one goal in ten Premier League appearances but, he has expressed satisfaction with the move

Iwobi revealed that he was in the United Arab Emirates on holiday with his family when he was contacted about the Toffees interests'.
