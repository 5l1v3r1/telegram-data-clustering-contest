Westlife hailed Gay Byrne "the grandad of Ireland" on Late Late appearance

23 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Nick Fitzgerald

"He was a special, special man."

Last night, Westlife made their first Irish television appearance in years as the say opposite Ryan Tubridy on Friday's Late Late Show.

The band performed Flying Without Wings, which they dedicated to the late Gay Byrne and his family.
