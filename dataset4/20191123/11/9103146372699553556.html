ACCIDENT

WAKISO - One person has died and two others injured in a fatal accident that occurred at Matugga, along Gulu highway.

This accident happened on Friday when a vehicle, registration number UBB 032W lost control.

According to Bukenya Wasswa, a resident, the vehicle rolled several times and hit an electricity pole.

He blamed the accident on speeding and drunk driving because they found empty beer and waragi bottles.

Kampala Metropolitan Police Spokesperson, Luke Owoyesigyire confirmed that the two are in critical condition and appealed to drivers to stop speeding and driving while drunk.

The deceased and injured were not identified by press time.

The car wreckage was towed to Matugga police station.
