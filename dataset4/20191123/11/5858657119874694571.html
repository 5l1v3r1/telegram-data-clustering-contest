Battle over B.C. teachers' new contract continues at NDP convention

23 Nov 2019, 11:50 by Liza Yuzda

Summary

Teacher's Federation president Teri Mooring says they need to make gains

Premier John Horgan says he's confident there is a solution as there's so much common ground

VICTORIA (NEWS 1130) — Teachers are protesting outside the annual BC NDP Convention in Victoria Saturday as contract talks drag on.

It’s been nine months of contract debates, and Teacher’s Federation president Teri Mooring says they need to make gains.
