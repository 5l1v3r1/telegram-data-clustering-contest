Gabby Allen 'bombarded with messages' from women about I'm a Celebrity star Myles Stephenson

Gabby and Myles Stephenson split in August

23 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Olivia Tobin

Love Island star Gabby Allen says she has been "bombarded by women" who claim her ex Myles Stephenson sent flirty texts and explicit photos while they were together.

Gabby and the I'm a Celebrity star split up in August after she said the Rak Su singer cheated on her.

It is claimed the 25-year-old received a load of messages from women saying Myles messaged them while the pair were a couple.

A source close to the personal trainer told Mirror Online: "A barrage of women have stepped forward since Myles appeared on I'm A Celeb, she has been bombarded.

"They have been sending proof of the messages, sext and d*** pics Myles sent to them when he was with Gabby."

The source also claimed Gabby has been informed Myles was "two-timing" her throughout their entire relationship after the woman whose texts with the singer caused the end of their romance recently "smugly" revealed that Myles was seeing her the whole time.

Before Myles entered the I'm a Celeb jungle, he claimed he came clean to Gabby about texting his ex "because he loved her".

He also said the pair were on a break.

Gabby confirmed the couple split in August on her Instagram story.

She wrote: "The newspaper articles are true.

"I am no longer in a relationship. I hope youse have all had a better week than me."
