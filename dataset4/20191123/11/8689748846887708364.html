“Unnecessary beefing is your hobby but we don’t have time for you” – Social media users blast Manifest for teaming up with Kelvyn Boy to diss Stonebwoy

23 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Adu Sarpeah

Manifest for some time now has literary become a “lawyer of beef” who is hired by artistes to bring down the hard-earned reputation of other artistes into disrepute.

For the purpose of clarity, let me take you back to the time Strongman and rapper Medikal engaged themselves in a fierce lyrical war. In the course of the banter, Manifest teamed up with Strongman and came out with a song titled “Ups & Down”.

Although the lyrical content of “Ups & Downs” has an element of positivity, however no one can dispute the fact that it has an element of diss aimed at Medikal.

Now, Strongman and Manifest beef have been quashed as we speak, but issues about Kelvyn Boy and Stonebwoy have become the talk of Ghana. At the blind side of Ghanaians, Manifest has maneuvered his ways to team up with Kelvyn Boy on a song.
