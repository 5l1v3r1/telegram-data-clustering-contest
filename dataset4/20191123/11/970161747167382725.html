Jose Mourinho unsure over Christian Eriksen's Tottenham future

23 Nov 2019, 11:47

Jose Mourinho has made three changes to the Tottenham side ahead of his first match in charge against West Ham and has left Christian Eriksen on the bench.

Moussa Sissoko, as well as summer signings Giovani Lo Celso and Tanguy Ndombele, are also omitted from the Spurs starting XI with Eric Dier stepping into midfield alongside the recalled Harry Winks.

Lucas Moura also returns, having started on the bench in the 1-1 draw against Sheffield United which turned out to be Mauricio Pochettino’s last match in charge, but there was no room for unsettle star Eriksen.

The Denmark international’s contract is due to expire at the end of the season and he struggled to showcase his best form since admitting he wanted to leave the club in the summer.
