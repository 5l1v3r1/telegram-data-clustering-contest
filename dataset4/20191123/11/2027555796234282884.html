Neymar returns to help PSG beat Lille

Brazilian forward Neymar returns from injury to help PSG beat Lille in Ligue 1.

23 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Reuters

Paris St Germain' Brazil forward Neymar made his comeback from injury as they enjoyed a 2-0 home win over Lille on Friday to move a provisional 11 points clear at the top of Ligue 1.

First-half goals from Mauro Icardi and Angel Di Maria put the hosts ahead and Lille never seriously threatened a comeback.

PSG moved to 33 points ahead of second-placed Olympique Marseille, who visit second-bottom Toulouse on Sunday, while Lille are seventh on 19.
