Nollywood’ll be compared to Hollywood in two years –Scott Roberts

23 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Olajide Samuel

Olajide Samuel

Actor, Scott Roberts, has revealed that he sometimes gets weird messages from fans. In an interview with Saturday Beats, he said a fan once said she would love to kiss him.

“I manage my female fans. Sometimes I act like I don’t see some of these messages they send to me, and if it’s not so annoying, I simply say ‘hello’.

“A fan once sent me a message, saying, ‘I would love to kiss you’. Some of them say they would love to spend time with me. It makes me laugh.”

Asked why he plays more of lover boy roles, Roberts said: “I don’t want to believe I have been typecast as a lover boy because there are a lot of movies I have appeared in that have nothing to with playing lover boy roles. We are all lover boys and lover girls.”

Speaking further, he denied taking a break from acting.

“I didn’t take a break from acting; I have been doing a lot of other things. I have a movie that just came out titled, ‘The Good’. Sola Sobowale, Alex Usifo and others starred in the movie.

Commenting on the growth of Nollywood, the actor said, “The movie industry is actually better now. Between now and the next two years, we can actually be compared to Hollywood.”
