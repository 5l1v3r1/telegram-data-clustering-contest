Japan: Latest Famitsu most wanted Nintendo charts

23 Nov 2019, 11:36 by My Nintendo News Admin

The latest Famitsu readers most wanted Nintendo video games are here and there’s a big discrepancy between No.1 and No.2 on the charts this week. The most wanted game by readers is unsurprisingly Animal Crossing: New Horizons for the Nintendo Switch system which is due out sooner than both The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild 2 which is at No.2 and Bayonetta 3 which is at No.3. Neither of these two titles have been given a release date, not even a tentative one. Here’s the most wanted games this week in Japan according to Famitsu readers:

  1. Animal Crossing: New Horizons (Switch) – 508
  2. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild 2 (Switch) – 292
  3. Bayonetta 3 (Switch) – 278
  4. Shin Megami Tensei V (Switch) – 231
  5. Ushiro (Switch) – 205
  6. Rune Factory 5 (Switch) – 184
  7. SD Gundam G Generation Cross Rays (Switch) – 133
  8. TRIALS of MANA (Switch) – 131
  9. Momotaro Dentetsu ～Showa Heisei, Reiwa mo Teiban!～ (Switch) – 111
  10. Yo-kai Watch 4++ (Switch) – 92
  11. Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition (Switch) – below 82
  12. Tokyo Mirage Sessions #FE Encore (Switch) – below 82
  13. Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA Mega Mix (Switch) – below 82
