West Brom 2 Sheffield Wednesday 1: Albion fans have their say as Baggies keep bouncing - VIDEO

Luke Hatfield spoke with Albion fans following their side's 2-1 win over Sheffield Wednesday at The Hawthorns.

23 Nov 2019, 17:58 by Luke Hatfield

Hal Robson-Kanu got the Baggies off to a brilliant start after slotting home after Matheus Pereira's delightful pass.

However, the game turned on its head in the second half, with Wednesday battling back level via the penalty spot, after Filip Krovinovic was deemed to have hauled down Kieran Lee.

The game was on a knife-edge until Pereira was clipped by Keiren Westwood in the box, giving Charlie Austin the chance to put Albion ahead - which he did emphatically to ensure Albion secured all three points.
