IISc to set up research park to help start-ups with infra, finances

The research park will come up with Rs 50-crore investment

23 Nov 2019, 17:29 by BS Reporter

The Indian Institute of Science (IISc) is setting up a research park on its Bengaluru campus to provide infrastructure and financial support to start-ups. The facility — I-Hub — will be spread across 150,000 square feet and is likely to be comple-ted in two years. The research park will come up with Rs 50-crore investment .
