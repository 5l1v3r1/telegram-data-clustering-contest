Woman fights off maniac attempting to swipe baby from stroller

23 Nov 2019, 17:23 by Ruth Weissmann

A crazed creep tried to snatch a 10-month-old girl from a stroller as her mother walked down a Queens street, police said Saturday.

The woman was pushing the tot in a stroller near 43rd Ave. and 11th St. in Corona Park at about 10:30 p.m. Thursday when the deranged man ran up to her and grabbed the stroller, authorities said.

“Give me the baby!” the stranger screamed repeatedly at the mother as they wrestled for the infant.

The mom fought off the attacker, who ran off, police said.

Investigators believe the man was drunk when he demanded she hand over the child.

Images released by police show the would-be kidnapper dining at a nearby Roosevelt Ave restaurant just after the incident.

He is described as in his 20s, approximately 5-foot-5, with brown eyes and black hair, and was last seen wearing a green hooded sweatshirt and dark pants, authorities said.
