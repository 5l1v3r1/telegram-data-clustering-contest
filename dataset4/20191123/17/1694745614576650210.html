If you've watched Euphoria, odds are you've at least thought about recreating one of the many stunning and intricate makeup looks from the show. From the neon eye shadow to bold rhinestones (we're looking at you, Maddy), each one is so memorable. Even if bright colors and sparkles on sparkles aren't your thing, the more subtle looks from the show are effortlessly gorgeous.

Should you have a friend who's equally obsessed with Jules, Rue, and Kat, but you're not sure what to get them this holiday season, we've got you. Ahead, we've rounded up some of the best Euphoria-inspired beauty products, so everyone can try out some of the most iconic looks themselves.
