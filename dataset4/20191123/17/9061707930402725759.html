Phillip Cocu expresses delight over Preston victory but admits disappointment

Derby County 1-0 Preston North End | A Martyn Waghorn goal secured all three points for the Rams at Pride Park Stadium

23 Nov 2019, 17:58 by Steve Nicholson

Phillip Cocu was delighted with Derby County's performance in their 1-0 victory over Preston North End this afternoon.

Substitute Martyn Waghorn scored the only goal after 33 minutes of the Championship fixture at Pride Park Stadium.

"I think we played very well - the best 45 minutes I have seen (from us)," Cocu said.

"The last 15 minutes was more about getting the 1-0 win because we could not get that second goal.

"But the way we wanted to start, the tactics, worked - a great job.

"The only disappointment was we did not get more goals.

"If you get that second goal the belief is there and it is very hard for Preston to come back into the game."

This was Derby's fifth consecutive home win and they have moved up to 11th.
