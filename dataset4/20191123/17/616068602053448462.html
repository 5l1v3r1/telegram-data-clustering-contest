Hwy 1 fully reopened

23 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Castanet Staff

UPDATE: 5:20 p.m.

The Trans-Canada Highway has been fully reopened after a serious crash closed it near Chase Saturday afternoon. 

UPDATE: 5 p.m.

The Trans-Canada Highway has reopened to single-lane, alternating traffic Saturday afternoon, after a serious crash closed the highway for several hours. 

The condition of those involved in the crash is unknown at this time, but several vehicles were involved.

UPDATE: 2 p.m.

Highway 1 remains closed near Chase Saturday afternoon, but highways contractor AIM Roads says they have "medium confidence" the Trans-Canada will be reopened by 4 p.m.

The nature of the crash remains unclear at this time. 

ORIGINAL: 12:45 p.m.

A serious accident has closed Highway 1 near Chase.

According to DriveBC, there is a multi-vehicle incident between Old Highway and Shuswap-Chase Creek Road west of Chase.

Few details are available, but one person is believed to be critically injured.
