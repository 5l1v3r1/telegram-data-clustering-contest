Beck shuts down Scientologist claims as singer explains he's 'focused on work'

23 Nov 2019, 17:03

Beck has shut down claims he’s a Scientologist despite identifying as one back in 2005.

The singer opened up about his faith recently, when chatting to Saturday’s The Sydney Morning Herald.

And while his father David Campbell practices the religion, it turns out the Loser hitmaker does not.

Talking to the publication, he explained: ‘I think there’s a misconception that I am a Scientologist. I’m not a Scientologist.

‘My father has been a Scientologist for a long time, but I’ve pretty much just focused on my music and my work for most of my life, and tended to do my own thing.’

The 49-year-old added: ‘I think it’s just something people ran with.’

Almost 15 years ago, the star spoke about his beliefs to the Irish Sunday Tribune, where he openly confessed he’s a Scientologist.

The star said: ‘Yeah, I’m a Scientologist. My father has been a Scientologist for about 35 years, so I grew up in and around it and stuff.’

He then went onto describe what that means, adding: ‘What it actually is is just sort of, you know, I think it’s about philosophy.

‘And sort of, all these kinds of, you know, ideals that are common to a lot of religions.’

Earlier this year, TMZ claimed that Beck had filed a divorce from his wife Marissa, after almost 15 years of marriage.

The duo share two children together, son Cosmo Henri, 14, and daughter Tuesday, 11.

It’s thought they tied the knot in 2004, when the mother-of-two was pregnant with their first child.

At the time it was claimed that they said their vows at San Ysidro Ranch in Montecito, California

This was the same iconic location that Chris Martin and Gwyneth Paltrow wed.
