Russell Westbrook-Patrick Beverley beef continues with trash talk at end of Rockets-Clippers game

23 Nov 2019, 17:15 by Jordan Greer

Rockets star Russell Westbrook and Clippers guard Patrick Beverley added another chapter to their longstanding beef after both players fouled out of Friday night's exciting game in Los Angeles.

With less than 10 seconds remainingand the Clippers leading by one point, Paul George left Westbrook to double-team James Harden, givingWestbrooka potential game-winning 3-pointer. Westbrook bricked the shot, then fouled George to stop the clock, sending him to the bench for the restof regulation. The Clippers went on to win by a final score of 122-119.
