Photo Shows How Many Letters Tua Received From Alabama Fans

23 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Andrew Holleran

Tua Tagovailoa has been extremely upbeat throughout his early recovery process from his season-ending hip injury. Video showed the Alabama quarterback in a good mood at the hospital and he’s since returned to the Crimson Tide, interacting with his teammates and Nick Saban.

The star quarterback likely won’t play for Alabama again, as he’ll probably declare for the 2020 NFL Draft, where he’s expected to be a high pick.

The love Alabama fans have for their QB isn’t going away, though.

Tagovailoa shared a photo on Instagram of the amount of letters he’s received from fans since his injury. It’s a lot.
