Salman Khan spills bean, says creative team of Bigg Boss orchestrates fights among contestants as part of reality show’s format

23 Nov 2019, 17:46 by JKR Staff

Bigg Boss host Salman Khan on Saturday night minced no words while admonishing Siddharth Shukla and Asim Riaz for their repeated violence in the show last week. However, the biggest revelation on the show was Salman spilling the beans on how the creative team of the Colors TV reality show orchestrated fights among housemates.

Salman started the show by expressing his disgust for the housemates adding that the week gone by was the worst week in the history of the Bigg Boss show. Revealing how the format of the show entailed its creative team orchestrating fights among the contestants, Salman said, “This is the format of the Bigg Boss show. If you don’t fight, the creative team will make you fight. But this time the creative team is not making you fight. You are doing it on your own.”

Continuing his tirade, Salman said, “Hurl abuses to each other’s mothers and sisters… Call each other names..This would affect me earlier, but this doesn’t bother me any longer..This is your life, destroy your life if you want…You are not 15-16-year-old kids. You are not in school and I am not here to act as your principal. Is this a remand home? Not really.”
