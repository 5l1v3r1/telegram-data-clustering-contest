Teaser Talk 2: More Fun And New Angle Revealed

23 Nov 2019, 17:37 by Siddartha Toleti

The second teaser of Venky Mama is out. This time it concentrates on “Alludu” as its Naga Chaitanya’s birthday today.

The fun aspect is once again visible. We have another love story, and that comes from ‘Alludu’ Naga Chaitanya. Glimpses of his track with Rashi Khanna is lovely but typical of the commercial romantic dramas the star usually does. The addition of Venkatesh in the routine become variety here. Of course, it was the case on the opposite end as well, with Venkatesh and his love story.

However, it is the first revelation of the military angle that adds a new dimension to the whole tease. Naga Chaitanya is seen as Captain Karthik Sivaram Veeramachineni. Also, we see Venkatesh in a rugged older look. This portion looks serious and intriguing as to what could be the story behind it.

Everything is fine, but the makers continue not openly to come up with the release date. December 13th is unofficially confirmed for now what with the heroine posting it on her social media. Still, an official confirmation is required, though.

Check out the teaser below. Bobby aka KS Ravindra of Power and Jai Lava Kusa fame directs Venky Mama. S Thaman provides the music. Here’s wishing Naga Chaitanya a very happy birthday and a blockbuster success ahead.
