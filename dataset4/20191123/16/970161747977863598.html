Kylie Jenner's daughter Stormi channels father Travis Scott as she rocks braids

23 Nov 2019, 16:02

Kylie Jenner’s daughter, Stormi Webster, continues to take fashion inspiration from her famous parents – and most notably her father, Travis Scott.

The makeup mogul took to Instagram to share an adorable picture of Stormi wearing a colourful tie-dye t-shirt with matching Converse shoes, with her hair up in braids – seemingly channelling her rapper father.

‘Coolest girl I know,’ Kylie captioned the post, while adding a heart emoji.

Big sis Kim Kardashian replied in the comments: ‘Obsessed w her hair [sic].’

‘The cutest too,’ Khloe Kardashian added.

And fans also took to the comments to shower the one-year-old with praise.

‘The cutest baby I ever saw,’ one fan wrote.

‘She knows how to dress,’ another added, complimenting her fashion sense.

‘When a one year one is by far cooler than I’ll ever be,’ another fan commented.

This also isn’t the first time that Stormi wore her hair up in braids inspired by her father – with Travis sharing another snap of her donning the same hairstyle just a few days prior on his own Instagram.

She also made reference to the rapper’s number one album ASTROWORLD, as she could also be seen wearing a necklace which said ‘Stormi’s World’.

Kylie and Travis have mastered co-parenting pretty well since they split back in October, with Kylie even attending the rapper’s Astroworld Festival earlier this month.

‘Kylie flew to Houston with a group of friends. She returns to LA this morning,’ a source shared to People.

‘It was important for her to support Travis’s festival. They are still very close and often hang out with Stormi.’

During the festival, the 27-year-old also took to the stage and referred to Kylie as his ‘wife’, sparking rumours of a reconciliation between the two.

Travis was also gifted a set of flowers ahead of his performance at the festival, which were apparently sent by Kylie, and this could prove to be a hint that the two may be back on.
