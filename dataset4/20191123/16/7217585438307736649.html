Another tornado reported near Ulundi, SAWS issues more severe storm warnings

23 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Andy Walker

KwaZulu-Natal is fast becoming the tornado capital of South Africa, after the second tornado of the weekend was reported in Ulundi on Saturday.

Images published by Twitter user @sakhilemagalela, and retweeted by the South African Weather Service, shows the whirlwind spinning near eDlebe Village, near the former provincial capital.
