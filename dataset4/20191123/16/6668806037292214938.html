Team Bala celebrates the film’s dream run at the box-office

23 Nov 2019, 16:07 by Akash Nihalani

Ayushmann Khurrana is on a roll. His last film Bala marked his seventh consecutive hit at the box-office. Now, that is a remarkable achievement for any actor in the industry. Ayushmann, through his films, has tried to shed light on various topics that are considered a taboo in our society. With Bala, we are given a glimpse of how our society is obsessed with physical appearance. While Ayushmann plays a prematurely balding man in the film, Bhumi Pednekar's character has to battle discrimination against darker skin.

With the film becoming the latest entrant to Rs.100 crore club in 2019, the team got together and celebrated the success along with some of their friends and family.  Apart from the leading cast including Ayushmann, Bhumi and Yami Gautam, Tahira Kashyap, Aparshakti Khurrana, Akriti Khurrana, Rajkummar Rao, Patralekhaa, Kriti Sanon, Boney Kapoor, Dinesh Vijan, Abhishek Banerjee and Diana Penty too were present at the celebrations.
