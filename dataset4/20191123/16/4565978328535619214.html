First Day of the 2019-2020 Sugar Harvest in Las Tunas (+Photo gallery)

23 Nov 2019, 16:31 by Reynaldo López Peña

The Majibacoa sugar mill gave yesterday "the initial whistle" of the harvest in Las Tunas. The main authorities of the Cuban Communist Party and the Government in the province accompanied its workers and directives.
