Beverley Knight lined up for Ibiza Classics night

One of Wolverhampton's best-known performers will be part of a night of electronic dance classics.

23 Nov 2019, 16:47 by James Vukmirovic

Beverley Knight will be performing with Britain's most influential figures in electronic dance music, Pete Tong, as part of Ibiza Classics, a night celebrating the legacy of dance music.

The event will take place on December 11 at Birmingham Arena and will feature the 65-piece Heritage Orchestra and conductor John Buckley putting on a fresh show of Ibiza Classics, as well as sets from DJs such as Andy Baxter, Robert Owens and Phil Gifford.

Tickets are on sale now via www.gigsandtours.com, www.ticketmaster.co.uk and www.ibizaclassics.com.
