Pink Lloyd scores a hat trick in Woodbine's Kennedy Road

23 Nov 2019, 16:51 by HRN Staff

Pink Lloyd successfully defended his title when he charged from last-to-first to win the Grade 2, $175,000 Kennedy Road Stakes at Woodbine on Saturday.
It was the third straight win in the race for the 7-year-old gelding by Old Forester, who improved his overall record to 22 wins in 27 starts.
Pink Lloyd was sent off the heavy 3-5 favorite in the field of eight. Ridden by jockey Eurico Da Silva, Pink Lloyd was a bit keen through the first furlong, and eventually was settled at the rear of the field as Richiesinthehouse led the field through the early stages of the race.
As the field raced into the far turn, Pink Lloyd began to close in on the leaders, making a bold, sweeping move while racing wide. In the stretch it was all Pink Lloyd, as he took command and pulled away to win by just over a length.
