Upcoming Samsung Galaxy A81 could come with S-Pen support

23 Nov 2019, 16:27

Based on a statement by gizmochina, it seems that Samsung’s upcoming Galaxy A81 smartphone could come with S-Pen support in the future. As of now, The only smartphone series by Samsung to support the S-Pen is the Galaxy Note series and Samsung could be planning to have the S-Pen available on more of their smartphones in the future.

Other than what was mentioned, there is no other information in regards to the Malaysia release date, local price as well as exact tech specs for now. With all that said, do take every bit of information with a grain of salt, due to the fact that Samsung has not released any statement in regards to the Samsung Galaxy A81.
