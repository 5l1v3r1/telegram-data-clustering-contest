Gabrielle Union and Julianne Hough axed from America's Got Talent

23 Nov 2019, 16:14

Gabrielle Union and Julianne Hough are set to leave America’s Got Talent after replacing judges Mel B and Heidi Klum.

The duo replaced Scary Spice and Heidi during the show’s 14 season, but it has been reported that they won’t be returning for series 15.

Music mogul Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel and host Terry Crews will remain on the show as filming has already started.

The pair were revealed to be replacing Mel B and Heidi back in March, joining resident panelist Simon Cowell and Howie Mandel.

However, it has now been confirmed by Variety that they won’t be returning for another series.

Fans seemed to love the new additions to the talent show as Gabrielle pressed her first ever Golden Buzzer for singer Kodi Lee who went on to win the entire show.

Blind singer Kodi was announced as the winner of America’s Got Talent season 14 after an epic finale show that saw music legend Cher put on a devastatingly fabulous performance.

Kodi bought the judges, including Simon Cowell, to tears with his incredible performances throughout the series.
