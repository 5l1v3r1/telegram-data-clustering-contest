Steph McGovern's newborn daughter gets custom made baby gifts and it's too cute

23 Nov 2019, 16:08

Steph McGovern and her partner recently welcomed their newborn daughter into the world – and it looks like she’s being spoiled rotten with all the baby gifts.

Taking to Twitter, the proud mum shared a snap of the tot’s presents from a number of family members and friends – and it’s too cute.

Perfectly displayed for the snap, the presenter showed off a collection of custom made baby grows and bibs – with the likes of ‘my mam’s the business’ printed across the front of one, while another reads: ‘BBC Breakfast baby correspondent’.

Writing alongside the picture, the 37-year-old said: ‘My mates + family have really excelled on the slogans/branding for my baby daughter’s clothes.

‘Here are some of my faves: @BBCBreakfast @Boro #perfectpresents  #luckylittlelady.’

Fans were quick to comment and couldn’t help but coo over the tiny pieces as they laughed along at the slogans.

‘Love them especially the BBC breakfast,’ one wrote while another added: ‘Love these! ‘Shy bairns get nowt’ was one of my granda’s sayings!’

Someone else said: ‘These are lovely, my favourite is the baby correspondent one. Hope all is going well.’

The journalist welcomed her bundle of joy into the world earlier this month, and confirmed the news on Twitter.

Writing to her 387,000 followers, the first-time-mum said: ‘Well hello world, just surfaced to let you know that we now have a daughter!

‘Born on Monday morning just as @BBCBreakfast was wrapping up. We’re all fine and dandy.’

She added: ‘Just getting used to the eau de sick/poo I am now regularly wearing. #babylife.’

She had previously spoken of suffering from morning sickness during the early stages of her pregnancy, almost vomiting over one of her co-hosts at one point.

Telling Jon Kay on BBC Breakfast that she had had a ‘bit of a rough start.’

She explained: ‘Charlie [Stayt] nearly got covered in watermelon one morning.’
