Superstar Usher coming to Abu Dhabi for F1 gig

The singer will perform at MAD on Yas Island during Race Week

23 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Time Out staff

It's the time of year when celebrities and superstars descend on Abu Dhabi.

During the Formula 1 weekend, and the wider Race Week celebrations, you can expect amazing concerts, super car auctions and parties, and the little matter of a race at Yas Marina Circuit.

The latest name to be added to the line-up of concerts is a big one - US RnB star Usher.

The singer (and dancer, don't forget the dancing) is perhaps best known for his smash hits Yeah, My Boo and U Got It Bad.

He'll be appearing at superclub MAD on Yas Island on the night after the F1 race itself - Sunday December 1.

It's Dhs500 to get in for men, which includes two drinks, and Dhs200 for women (with one drink). Table packages will also be available.

One thing we do know if Usher will take to the stage late, MAD is open until around 5am during the F1 weekend.

There will be more concerts at MAD announced over the coming days, so watch this space for more information.
