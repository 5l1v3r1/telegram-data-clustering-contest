(Video) Roberto Firmino scores colossal goal for Liverpool after goalmouth scramble

23 Nov 2019, 16:54 by Matt Addison

Just as Crystal Palace had equalised late on through Wilfried Zaha, Roberto Firmino put the Reds back in front again at Selhurst Park in the Premier League – making it 2-1 to Liverpool.

Trent Alexander-Arnold swung in a brilliant corner kick that forced a goalmouth scramble, and then the Brazilian was there to poke home his fourth goal of the season so far.
