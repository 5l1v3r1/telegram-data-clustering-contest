Liam Hemsworth heads out after Elsa Pataky’s comments that he’s ‘doing well’

23 Nov 2019, 16:51

Liam Hemsworth has been spotted doing his grocery shopping in Malibu following comments from his sister-in-law Elsa Pataky that he’s ‘doing well’ following his split from Miley Cyrus.

The actor, 29, was seen doing the food shop in Whole Foods in Malibu, California, as he spent the week surfing with his brother, Chris Hemsworth.

He looked lost in thought as he stocked up on some essentials, in his rare time off from his busy work schedule.

It comes after Chris’s wife, Elsa, spoke out about how Liam is doing following the split from his wife, Miley Cyrus, in August.

She said at a press conference in Madrid this week: ‘My brother-in-law, well… after a relationship that you’ve dedicated ten years to, he’s a little bit down, but he’s coping well.

‘He’s a strong boy and he deserves the best. I think he deserves much better.’

Liam and Chris have always been close, and Elsa says Liam has been leaning on his brother while going through the divorce.

She explained: ‘You always find support in your family, and he’s joined at the hip with [Chris], who has been there to give all the strength he needed.’

Elsa’s comments surprised fans after she and Miley had seemed close while the couple were together, even getting matching tattoos as a tribute to their friendship three years ago.

Liam and Miley confirmed their split after eight months of marriage – following 10 years of an on-off relationship – over the summer.

Miley was soon spotted kissing Kaitlynn Carter on a boat while on holiday, but that short-lived rebound romance lasted just six weeks.

She then got together with Cody Simpson, with the pair being rumoured to have split last week, but have since claimed they are together but have an ‘easy’ relationship where they can spend extended time apart.
