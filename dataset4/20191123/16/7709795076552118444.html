Lostintranslation sinks Bristol in Betfair Chase at Haydock

23 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Sporting Life

Lostintranslation got the better of king of Haydock Bristol De Mai in the Betfair Chase for Colin Tizzard and Robbie Power.

Nigel Twiston-Davies' grey was going for his third Betfair Chase win in a row under Daryl Jacob, but both he and the winner were sent off 5/4 joint-favourites as the market struggled to separate the duo.

It was difficult to separate them after the last, too, as they went head to head in a thrilling duel, but Lostintranslation pulled out more when asked to win by one-and-a-half lengths.

Lostintranslation is the new 9/2 favourite for the Gold Cup with Sky Bet.

Bristol De Mai cut out the running in his customary front-running style, with Lostintranslation held up off the pace by Power with Frodon and Ballyoptic racing in between.

At the top of the straight Lostintranslation had loomed up into contention and it soon became a match race with Ballyoptic and Frodon dropping away.

Lostintranslation was travelling the better approaching the second last, but the slightest of errors there gave Bristol De Mai added impetus and the whip was drawn on Tizzard's horse for the first time.

He nosed ahead after the last and pulled away late on, with Bristol De Mai going down on his sword with an excellent effort in defeat.
