Sunil Chhetri scores in Bengaluru’s 1-0 win over Kerala Blasters

Though Kerala Blasters battled valiantly, Sunil Chhetri scored the winner with a brilliant header in the 55th minute to preserve Bengaluru's unbeaten record in the ISL.

23 Nov 2019, 16:44 by PTI

Talismanic captain Sunil Chhetri scored the winning goal as Bengaluru FC beat Kerala Blasters 1-0 in an action-packed Indian Super League match at the Sree Kanteerava stadium here on Saturday.
