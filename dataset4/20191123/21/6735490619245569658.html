Tyler Perry Pays Mexican Hospital Bill For Couple Who Weren’t Allowed To Leave Until They Came Up With $14,000

23 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Jamie Samhan

Tyler Perry’s heart just keeps getting bigger as he just helped a couple that wasn’t allowed to leave a Mexican hospital after not being able to pay the bill.

Tori Austin and her fiance, Stephen Johnson, were on a cruise when Johnson fell ill with pancreatitis, diabetes and a kidney infection reports TMZ. They ended up in Centro Medico Americano in Progreso in the Yucatan where he was treated.

However, the couple was told they couldn’t leave until they paid the $14,000 bill which included dialysis. They started a GoFundMe page but it didn’t even come close to covering the hospital bill.

After their story was on the local news, it caught the attention of mega-producer Perry who stepped in. He reportedly tried to pay over the phone with a credit card but was told he would have to wire money instead which should arrive this Monday.

At this point, Johnson will be released on Tuesday but doctors were concerned a medevac plane would be needed. Another item that Perry offered to pay for.

ET Canada has reached out to Perry’s rep for a comment.

Perry is known for lending a helping hand including sending his personal plane to bring aid to the Bahamas after Hurricane Dorian.
