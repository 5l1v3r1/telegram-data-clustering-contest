This Fast-Paced Billie Eilish Dance Routine Is "Everything I Wanted" and More

23 Nov 2019, 21:55 by Brea Cubit

Billie Eilish's "Everything I Wanted" dropped on Nov. 13, and choreographer Matt Steffanina has already shared an incredible routine to the song. Known for his isolations and smooth movements, Matt doesn't disappoint as he and a group of talented dancers break it down to a remix of Billie's new tune. The music's rapid rhythm might make you wonder if the performers can keep up, but no one misses a single beat. These are trained professionals, people!
