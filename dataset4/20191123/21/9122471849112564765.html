Video: Dancing Alabama Fan Goes Viral During Blowout Win

23 Nov 2019, 21:44 by Chris Rosvoglou

Every week there’s a fan in college football that goes viral for something out of the ordinary. While there are most likely multiple fans that have gone viral this afternoon, none top the Alabama fan that showcased a series of dance moves at Bryant-Denny Stadium.

Senior day was a success for Alabama as it destroyed Western Carolina. Honestly, the game was over before it even began.

With the game well out of reach in the second half, one fan decided to display her dancing skills for the rest of the world to see.

Thankfully, the broadcast crew at ESPN caught her moves on camera.
