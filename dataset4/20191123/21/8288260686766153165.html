Vinnie Jones has been eliminated from The X Factor: Celebrity - days after viewers threatened a boycott when Simon Cowell ignored his plea.

The former footballer received the fewest votes from the public, and leaves the competition ahead of the live final next week.

Jones said his experience on the show has been "absolutely fantastic" and he thanked show boss Simon Cowell for "inviting me to be part of this".

Jones, whose wife Tanya died earlier this year, added: "You've really helped me with my personal life bringing me onto this show, and I'll always be in debt to you."

It comes a week after fans have threatened to boycott the show after Simon Cowell ignored Vinnie Jones' heartbreaking plea to leave.

Taking to social media on Saturday evening, Twitter and Facebook users had their say as scores furiously threatened to boycott the show.

Vinnie performed a version of Galway Shawl, and the former Chelsea and Wimbledon star told Simon, Louis Walsh and Nicole Scherzinger that he had had a "fantastic time" but that he wanted to leave.

Cowell, 60, refused to respect Vinnie's wishes as he landed in the bottom two with Kevin McHale.

"It's been everything I needed since my wife passed," Vinnie admitted.
