Sickening blows in Kolkata raise question of pink ball's visibility

23 Nov 2019, 21:37 by Amlan Chakraborty

Two more Bangladesh batsmen sustained sickening blows to the helmet on Saturday, raising questions about the visibility of the pink ball used for their maiden day-night test against India at Kolkata's Eden Gardens.

The tourists, facing a heavy defeat, were already grappling with a personnel crisis after Liton Das and Nayeem Hasan were hit on the head by short deliveries from Mohammed Shami in their first innings on Friday.

That forced Bangladesh to bring in Mehidy Hasan Miraz and Taijul Islam as concussion substitutes.
