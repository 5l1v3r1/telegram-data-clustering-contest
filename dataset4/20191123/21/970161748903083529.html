X Factor: Celebrity fans baffled after Nicole voted off Try Star

23 Nov 2019, 21:38

X Factor fans (and Simon Cowell) were left a bit baffled following Saturday night’s show after Nicole Sherzinger decided to vote off Try Star.

Vinnie Jones, Try Star and V5 were all in the bottom three – with Vinnie being booted off by the public with the lowest amount of votes.

Both bands then had to battle out in the sing-off.

Making her decision, Nicole Scherzinger said: ‘This is so hard. Boys, I have so much respect for you. Girls, you know I love you and have loved you from day one. And I’m all about girl power.

‘The act that I’m sending home is Try Star.’

Whilst Simon Cowell added: ‘Wow, that was a surprise. Like I said earlier, it’s a popularity competition and a talent competition. Boys, in the weeks you had available and obviously given that you weren’t singers before, the progress is incredible. Girls, in seven days, you were put together, which is remarkable. If I’m judging it purely on what I call the real world, I have to send home Try Star.’
