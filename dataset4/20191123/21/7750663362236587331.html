Real Madrid to beat Barcelona to Dani Olmo deal?

Real Madrid are reportedly weighing up a move for Dinamo Zagreb attacker Dani Olmo.

23 Nov 2019, 21:07 by Matt Law

The 21-year-old has enjoyed a productive start to the season for Dinamo, scoring six times and registering six assists in 18 appearances during the 2019-20 campaign.

The Croatian club are seemingly facing a battle to keep hold of the Spain international, though, with a number of clubs credited with an interest.

Manchester City and Juventus are two teams to be linked with a move, while Barcelona are also thought to be keen on bringing the attacker back to Camp Nou.

However, according to reports in Spain, Madrid are now in the race for the Spaniard, and Zinedine Zidane's side are determined to beat their Clasico rivals to his signature either in January or at the end of the campaign.
