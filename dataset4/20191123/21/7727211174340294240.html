I'm A Celebrity Andrew Maxwell breaks down in tears and threatens to quit

Andrew sobbed on Jacqueline in the leader's cabin

23 Nov 2019, 21:59 by Sophie McCoid

It's been a tough week in the Australian jungle and tonight one of the I'm A Celebrity camp mates broke down and threatened to quit the show.

Tonight saw James Haskell voted leader of the camp, with Jacqueline Jossa as his deputy.

In the episode Jacqueline and James were sat on their comfy beds in the Leaders Cabin and Andrew came up to them to chat.

Talking in the Bush Telegraph, Andrew said: “I was up in the Leaders Cabin, I was chatting to James and Jacqueline and I was just so tired, I just hit a wall.”

James went for a shower and Andrew sat on his bed.

He turned to Jacqueline and said: “I honestly thought that I was gonna walk out, but I haven’t, when you’re tired, you just say ‘have I done the right thing’.”

Jacqueline said: “You’d never leave, I can tell that, you’re just tired.”

Andrew began to cry, when James came back and asked if he was OK and Jacqueline said that Andrew was tired.

James said: “Everyone has a bad day” as Jacqueline hugged Andrew and said: “He’s been sleeping on the floor, he’s had six nights on the floor.”

James said: “You’re an important part of the group and we need you.”

James and Jacqueline advised him to eat some food in the form of beans.

Andrew said: “I’m just so tired.”

Jacqueline said: “You’ve spent over two weeks away from your family, it’s a long time.”

Andrew said: “I’m just a little bit overwhelmed right now.”

And James encouraged him and said: “You’re brilliant, you bring so much energy and laughter.”

James then said he’d look after him in the camp.
