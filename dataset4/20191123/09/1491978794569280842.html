Aston Villa v Newcastle United: match preview

23 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Guardian Staff

Despite the summer of turbulence on Tyneside, Newcastle find themselves in a respectable 13th place in the table and could potentially win their third game in a row on Steve Bruce’s first return to Villa Park since his dismissal last October. Villa on the other hand, have lost their last three, albeit two of those were to Manchester City and Liverpool. Jack Grealish should be a welcome return to Dean Smith’s side after a calf injury.
