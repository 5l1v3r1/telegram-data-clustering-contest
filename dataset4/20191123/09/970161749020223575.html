Gabby Allen ‘bombarded by women’ claiming I’m A Celeb’s Myles ‘sexted them'

23 Nov 2019, 09:23

Myles Stephenson’s ex Gabby Allen is thought to have been ‘bombarded’ with claims of his cheating, following his stint in the I’m A Celebrity jungle.

The pair dated for 11 months before splitting earlier this year, amid claims the X Factor winner, 28, was unfaithful – something he fiercely denied.

It has now been claimed the Love Island favourite has been sent messages and proof of his infidelity, including ‘sexts and explicit pictures’.

‘A barrage of women have stepped forward since Myles appeared on I’m A Celeb, she has been bombarded,’ an insider said.

‘They have been sending proof of the messages, sext and d**k pics Myles sent to them when he was with Gabby.’

The source also told the Mirror that Gabby was informed Myles had been ‘two-timing’ her throughout their relationship, with an unnamed woman reportedly claiming she had also been seeing the ITV star ‘the entire time’.

Myles has previously addressed accusations that he cheated on the 27-year-old after he was caught messaging his ex-girlfriend.

But he insisted they weren’t together at the time, and that ‘nothing happened’ with his ex.

‘We were on a break at the time when I messaged this girl, a previous ex,’ he told the Sun.

‘I shouldn’t have done it, even though me and Gabby weren’t speaking at the time and I moved home, but it was a general conversation.

‘It was a stupid mistake and I stopped speaking to her when we started getting back together, but by that time she’s messaged Gabby and said we’d been speaking.’

Viewers recently saw the Rak-Su singer opening up about his love life to campmates Ian Wright and Roman Kemp, admitting that he wanted to find something a bit more ‘meaningful’.

And, despite Gabby being abroad in Thailand, she couldn’t help but react to his words on Instagram.

Taking aim at the I’m Feeling You singer, she wrote: ‘Apparently he was on about “meaningful relationships”. I’m not sure the guy knows what he’s on about tbh.

‘I didn’t know cheating, lying and a whole load of other [poo emoji] came under “meaningful”. Who knows? Each to their own?’
