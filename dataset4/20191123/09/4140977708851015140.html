MAR vs QAL Dream11 Match Prediction : Maratha Arabians Vs Qalandars Best Dream 11 Team for Qualifier 1 Match of T10 League 2019

23 Nov 2019, 09:39 by Jatin Hasija

MAR Vs QAL Dream11 Team : The league moves into its business end as the table toppers get ready to battle it out against each other.

Gruelling, rigorous and tedious. The group stage encounters, ensured by the Super League matches were those and everything more as the eight sides partaking in the marquee league indulged themselves in a testing brand of cricket to give us the final four sides to play in the qualifier and eliminator today.

And getting things underway will be the two sides who ended up sitting in the annuls of the points tally at the culmination of the Super League yesterday courtesy of their enigmatic and enterprising band of cricket. Both Maratha Arabians and Qalandars will be facing off in the first qualifier with the winner moving onto the summit clash whilst the loser will be accorded a second chance to move onto the second qualifier.

With the four wins from six matches, Maratha Arabians find themselves making an advent into today’s enthralling encounter having finished atop of the league standings with just the lone defeat in the Super League. The side imbedded in place the inch perfect run chase yesterday as they ended up cantering their way to a 7 wicket win in pursuit of the 107 run target at hand.

It was a an emphatic and profound performance by the side, one which will make Qalandars extremely cautious when they square off against the stalwarts after having gotten the better of Delhi Bulls yesterday. It was far from a convincing display though with their bowling unit being torn apart in the death overs and their bats just about managing to scram through to a win by 3 wickets in pursuit of 99 after some disgruntled cricket by Delhi Bulls in the culminating stages of the match.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

A fresh track with this being the first match of the day to be contested over here means we are going to be in for resplendent batting conditions. There is little, if anything at all over here for the bowlers with the surface maintaining its identity across the 40 overs to lead the side winning the toss opting to field first.

MAR Vs QAL Dream11 Batsmen

We had to begin this facet of our side with Maratha Arabians’ opener Chris Lynn. Over the course of the league, the player has been responsible for seeing his side taste such consummate success, coming into the wake of this intriguing encounter with some resplendent performances in his artillery along with the 23 in just 9 balls yesterday.

Adam Lyth will be partnering him up for this encounter with the opener giving him apt company along with Najibullah Zadran who ended up playing a match winning knock of 36 in a mere 15 balls to take his side past the finishing line with ease. Qalandars meanwhile have just the lone player being picked up by us in the form of Dawid Malan, their most consistent and persistent bat, someone the side will be heavily relying on today to take them past the finishing line. 

MAR Vs QAL Dream11 All-Rounders

MAR Vs QAL Dream11 Bowling Order
