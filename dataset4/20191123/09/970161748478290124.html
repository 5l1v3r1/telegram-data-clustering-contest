Elizabeth Banks shocks Graham Norton Show viewers with Prince Andrew joke

23 Nov 2019, 09:07

Elizabeth Banks shocked viewers watching The Graham Norton Show with a totally unexpected joke about Prince Andrew.

Appearing on the show alongside Ricky Gervais, Kylie Minogue and Lewis Hamilton, Graham attempted to embarrass the actress with a hilarious throwback photo from her teens, sporting a stylish white polo neck with matching white hoops with her hair in its naturally curly state.

‘That’s my hair,’ Elizabeth laughed, with Ricky Gervais joking: ‘Sorry, that’s a tree behind you?’

‘That’s Marge Simpson hair,’ Graham added.

‘But look how proud I am,’ Elizabeth added. ‘I made that necklace myself, I was not really allowed to wear hoop earrings until I was older, those were probably some of my first hoop earrings.’

Pointing at the hand lurking in the picture, Graham said: ‘I’ve only just noticed this hand, is it someone saying, “you’ll regret this photograph.”’

‘That’s Prince Andrew’s hand,’ the 45-year-old actress shot back, prompting gasps from her fellow guest Kylie Minogue and Ricky while the audience offered her uproarious laughter.

‘Oh God, Jesus,’ Ricky said, while Graham insisted: ‘Photoshop, it’s just Photoshop.’

And it seems the viewers at home were just as impressed with Elizabeth’s edgy humour as the live audience.

‘”That’s Prince Andrew’s hand!” Oh @ElizabethBanks you are bloody hysterical #GrahamNortonShow,’ a fan wrote, while another posted: ‘Her comment about Prince Andrew love that.’
