People Can’t Hold Back Their Tears As Popular Slay Queen Afia Wale Dies In A Car Crash (+Photos)

23 Nov 2019, 09:34 by BB Jones

A sad news that has surfaced on the internet indicates that a beautiful young woman identified as Afia Wale, real GenahLina Owusu Ansah  has died from a car accident.

The fatal accident reportedly occurred at the Suhum -Kumasi highway last week.

The report adds that Afia boarded a bus to Kumasi on Monday, November 18th but they, unfortunately, had an accident on the way where she lost her dear life.

Photos of her one week memorial service has popped up on the internet and people can’t stop shedding tears.

 

One of her friends called Efyagighh Starr broke the news on Facebook.
