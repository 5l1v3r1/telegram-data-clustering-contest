Inside Kimberley Walsh's wild birthday with Cheryl after being forced to deny Nadine testicle plot

Kimberley Walsh celebrated her 38th birthday with former Girls Aloud buddies Cheryl and Nicola Roberts

23 Nov 2019, 09:19 by Lucy Buckland, http://www.facebook.com/MirrorCeleb

Kimberley Walsh made sure she celebrated her 38th birthday in style by swigging vodka, wild dancing and noshing down a very creamy birthday cake.

Pop princess turned musical theatre star Kimberley made sure she invited ex Girls Aloud bandmates and best pals Cheryl and Nicola Roberts.

Awkwardly, Kimberley's birthday bash fell on the evening a wild rumour flooded the internet, claiming the three Girls Aloud stars connived to set up a Whats App group plotting Nadine Coyle's downfall on I'm A Celebrity.

Although the reports were denied in a rare joint statement from Cheryl, Nicola and Kimberley the details of the alledged plot were so embarassing they must have played on their mind during the wild bash.

The most inflammatory false accusation involved the trio, who admitted they haven't spoken to Nadine since the group split, plotting to get their former pal to eat Turkey testicles.

But ignoring the cringe slurs the trio made sure Kimberley had the time of her life at the bash in Bagatelle in Mayfair.

Cheryl shaed a video of Kimberley swigging from a gaint bottle of vodka and abandoning her sky high silver stilletos.

Nicola was spotted dancing in her sassy bralet and kneelength tailored skirt as Cheryl posed up for snaps.

In a statement to MailOnline Cheryl said: "We only want to see Nadine do well, and regardless of any differences we might have had, we will always all have love for each other.

"We did not enjoy watching her distressed when jumping out of the plane. And we don’t have time for group chats regarding the trials on the show!"

Kimberley also dismissed the claim and that when Nadine jumped from the helicopter in the launch episode, her heart went out to her.

She said: "I felt really emotional watching Nadine jump out of the plane and texted Cheryl saying how hard it was to watch. I think she’s doing great in there."

This was echoed by Nicola Roberts, who also slammed the reports that the Girls Aloud singers were hoping for bad things to happen to Nadine in the Australian jungle.

She also dismissed reports that there was a private group chat where they tear into Nadine behind her back.

Nicola said: 'We don’t have a group chat about I’m A Celebrity, this just isn’t true. We only wish Nadine the best in the jungle and hope she does well on the show.'

A spokesperson for the band also said the reports were "fabricated", telling MailOnline: "This story is completely fabricated and made up.

"Cheryl, Nicola and Kimberley have not been 'plotting' to get Nadine to do trials in I’m A Celebrity. To suggest they have been is quite frankly ridiculous."

There had been rumours that Nadine and Cheryl had a longstanding disagreement but Nadine denied this when asked about by Jacqueline Jossa.
