I’m Most Followed African DJ In World, Cuppy Boasts

23 Nov 2019, 09:19 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Popular female Disc Jockey and singer, Cuppy has taken to social media to brag about being the most followed African DJ in the world.

On her Instagram and Twitter handles, the billionaire’s daughter stated that she just made the discovery and decided to share her excitement on the platforms.

She wrote: “OMG! Just found out I’m the MOST FOLLOWED African DJ in The WORLD!

Earlier this year, DJ Cuppy, noted that many people think she is joking when she tells them she is going around the world doing the work of a DJ.

This news medium understands that in a post on her official Instagram page recently, DJ Cuppy who is the daughter of billionaire Femi Otedola, noted that her career is now on a whole new level.

“I think people are stating to realise this is not a joke… I’m getting FLEWED [sic] out to DJ around the world!” she wrote
