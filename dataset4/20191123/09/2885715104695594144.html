23 Nov 2019, 09:21 by Express News Service

Punjabi singer Karan Aujla landed himself into controversy, after he and his fans created nuisance on the airport road and violated traffic rules, on Friday.

The district police said that they will take action after verifying the CCTV footage and the videos made by the singer.

According to information, Karan Aujla landed at the Chandigarh International Airport on Friday afternoon where his fans were waiting for him. When he left the the airport, his fans started following his vehicle and Aujla made videos of the same.

The traffic flow was disrupted due to the large number of vehicles on the busy airport road which is also an accident prone area.

The video in which of Aujla and his fans are seen creating nuisance, went viral on social media, eventually reaching the police.

DSP (Traffic) Gur Iqbal Singh said that they will take action against Aujla if he is found guilty of violating any traffic rules.

The DSP said that they received some videos in which Aujla was seen making videos while standing in his car and his fans creating nuisance.

Karan Aujla was not available for comments.
