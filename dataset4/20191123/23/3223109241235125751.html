Adidas Jumps To Hyperspace With Their Newest Star Wars Sneakers

23 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Tres Dean

Adidas came out hot earlier this month with the first wave of their new Star Wars kicks. The line, which drew inspiration from lightsabers, was primarily incorporated into the sneaker and apparel brand’s basketball line, seeing the signature sneakers of NBA superstars like James Harden and Damian Lillard get some slick blue, green, purple, and red colorways. It made for a strong start to the brand’s 2019 Star Wars line, largely timed to coincide with the veritable Star Wars Season we’ve got going on right now with the release of The Mandalorian, Jedi: Fallen Order, and The Rise of Skywalker happening in such quick succession.

While they’ve yet to announce a full line of both sneakers and clothing centered around my son/best friend/Patronus/number one boy Baby Yoda (it’s gonna happen eventually, mark my words) the newest line dropped this week. If you’re a fan of adidas’ running output, you’re in for a treat with this one.
