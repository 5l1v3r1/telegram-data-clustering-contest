Megan The Stallion Denies Love Affair With Tristan Thompson

23 Nov 2019, 23:10 by AllHipHop Staff

Meg has finally come forward to shoot down growing speculation she's involved with Tristan Thompson.

(AllHipHop News) Megan Thee Stallion has hit back at rumors she's dating Khloe Kardashian's ex, basketball player Tristan Thompson.

Fans had speculated the pair were involved romantically after the Hot Girl Summer hitmaker reportedly received a private message from the NBA star inviting her to the Cleveland Cavaliers' game against the New York Knicks.

Although the rapper did attend the game, she hit back at fans on Twitter who were fueling the relationship speculation.

"Lol they literally made up a whole LIE I was at knicks game with my manager and my friend Ej," the star tweeted, explaining her appearance at the game.

When another fan joked that Megan “wore that boy out” after it was reported Tristan wouldn’t be playing in the next game against the Miami Heat, she added: “What in the f##k are you talking about. I wish y’all would stop making s##t up I don’t even know that n###a."

The star later posted: "If I ain’t claiming him he ain’t mine ...SIMPLE."

Tristan and Khloe split after it emerged the sportsman had kissed family friend Jordyn Woods at a house party in earlier this year. They share one daughter, 19-month-old True.
