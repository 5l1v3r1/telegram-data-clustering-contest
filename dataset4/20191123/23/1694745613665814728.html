All the New Holiday Movies, TV Shows, and Specials Coming to Hulu This December

23 Nov 2019, 23:25 by Corinne Sullivan

Just when you thought Hulu couldn't possibly get any more great holiday programming, it's adding just a few more options. After all, Netflix isn't the only streaming service with an incredible selection of movies perfect for the season. From star-studded Christmas specials to sweet family-friendly films, the service is providing even more selections in December, just in case you've run out of options (though, TBH, we'd be impressed if you made it through all the holiday flicks available to stream right now). Hulu is the gift that keeps on giving, and here are its last few gifts for you to enjoy this holiday season.

1 The Great Christmas Light Fight, Season 7

In the seventh season of this Christmas decorating competition, families bring even more blinking bulbs and extravagant designs to light up their homes for the holidays.

When it's available: Dec. 3

2 CMA Country Christmas

This annual two-hour holiday special — hosted for the first time by Trisha Yearwood — features plenty of holiday cheer, plus special performers such as Chris Young, Brett Young, Rascal Flatts, Lady Antebellum, and Chris Janson, among many others.

When it's available: Dec. 4

3 Into the Dark: A Nasty Piece of Work

Hulu's holiday horror anthology series is premiering its second Christmas special, which revolves around a midlevel employee at a reputable company who finds out he's not getting the Christmas bonus he was expecting, leading to an epic (and violent) competition with his professional rival.

When it's available: Dec. 6

4 Holidays With the Houghs

Sister-brother duo Julianne Hough and Derek Hough host this hour-long holiday special, which includes guest performances, comedy sketches, and new takes on several holiday classics.

When it's available: Dec. 17

5 Prancer

In this 1989 family-friendly flick, a young girl who refuses to lose faith in Santa Claus discovers a wounded reindeer in the woods. Believing him to be Prancer, she works with a kindly vet to bring the animal back to health.

When it's available: Dec. 1
