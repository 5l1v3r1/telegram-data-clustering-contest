This new video that Cardi shared with the world was no exception!

Kulture showed off her smooth dance moves as her mother kept on cheering on her and laughing which also made the 17 month old’s face light up.

She’s definitely heard her parents’ song many times judging by her cute toothy grin as she listens to her dad spit some bars.

At the time, however, she could barely walk so it’s obvious she’d done a lot of growing up and she’s also much more coordinated now, just two months later!

The only thing that hasn’t changed is how much little Kulture enjoys this song! She is also just as adorable!
