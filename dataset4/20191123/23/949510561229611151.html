Winter Weather Advisory issued November 23 at 11:07PM EST until November 24 at 3:00PM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

23 Nov 2019, 23:07

Winter Weather Advisory: Otsego; Delaware until 3:00 PM

* WHAT…Mixed precipitation expected. Total snow accumulations
of 3 to 6 inches and ice accumulations of a light glaze.

* WHERE…Otsego and Delaware counties.

* WHEN…Until 3 PM EST Sunday.

* IMPACTS…Plan on slippery road conditions.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS…Expect a mix of rain and freezing rain to
continue into the overnight hours. The rain and freezing rain
will change to snow Sunday morning. A brief period of heavy snow
will be possible from Sunday morning into early Sunday
afternoon. Snowfall rates could exceed over an inch per hour.
Slow down and use caution while traveling.

The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can
be obtained by calling 5 1 1.
