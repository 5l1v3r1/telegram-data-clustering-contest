23 Nov 2019, 23:07

Winter Weather Advisory: Cortland, Tioga, Steuben and 6 more areas until 5:00 AM

* WHAT…Freezing rain over the higher terrain. Expect a light
glaze of ice over the higher terrain. Mainly rain will fall in
the lower elevations.

* WHERE…In Pennsylvania, Bradford, Susquehanna and Wyoming
counties. In New York, Steuben, Chemung, Cortland, Chenango,
Tioga and Broome counties.

* WHEN…Until 5 AM EST Sunday.

* IMPACTS…Plan on slippery road conditions especially over the
higher terrain.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS…A mix of rain and freezing rain will
continue into the overnight hours and change to snow by 5 am.
The freezing rain will be mainly over the higher terrain.
Slow down and use caution while traveling.
