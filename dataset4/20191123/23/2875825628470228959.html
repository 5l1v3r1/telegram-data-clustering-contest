I'm A Celeb's Nadine Coyle mocks Gillian McKeith fainting during Bushtucker Trial

The Girls Aloud hitmaker hilariously recreated Gillian McKeith's dramatic fainting moment from I'm A Celeb 2010 as she endured a Bushtucker Trial

Nadine Coyle left viewers in hysterics after she recreated Gillian McKeith 's iconic fainting moment from I'm A Celebrity 2010 during tonight's episode.

The Girls Aloud singer, 34, hilariously screamed and pretended to faint as she prepared to take on the challenge of putting live cockroaches in her mouth.
