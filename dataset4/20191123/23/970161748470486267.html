Hailey Baldwin enjoys 'low-key' birthday after Justin Bieber teases babies

23 Nov 2019, 23:15

Hailey Baldwin has revealed that she is the ‘happiest girl in the world’ as she enjoyed some low-key birthday celebrations – after her husband, Justin Bieber, teased that he wants to have babies with her.

The model recently celebrated her 23rd birthday and rather than have a wild night out or a big birthday party with her pals, she instead decided to spend some quality time with her husband in the comfort of their own home.

After all, she already had made sure to party hard at her hen party before getting married to Justin back in September.

Taking to Instagram stories, Hailey showed off a sneak peek of her romantic-looking dinner, with treats such as doughnuts and cupcakes also present on the table, as well as a cake with ‘Happy Birthday Hailey’ written on top in pink icing.

And if that wasn’t enough, she was also gifted with a bottle of an alcoholic drink with her man’s name written on it.

‘Nothing I want more, best birthday,’ she wrote.

The model also posted a message to thank all of her ‘beautiful friends and family’ for wishing her well on her special day.

‘Thank you so much to all my beautiful friends and family for all the love today!’ she wrote.

‘I’m the happiest birthday girl in the world.’

The quiet birthday celebrations make sense after she recently revealed that she was ‘too tired’ to host a party for her big day – despite originally having plans to host a Michael Jordan themed bash to mark the fact she was turning 23, as 23 had been the number on the basketball player’s back throughout his career.

It also comes after the Biebs hinted that he would like to have children with his wife, as he posted a sweet birthday tribute to her on his Instagram.

Sharing a set of professional photos from their wedding day, he wrote: ‘Happy birthday babes! You make me want to be better everyday!

‘The way you live your life is so attractive.. ps you turn me on in every way. Next season BABIES.’

So could we be expecting a baby Bieber very soon? We hope so.
