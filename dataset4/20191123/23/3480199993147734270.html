Jacqueline Jossa weeps after learning of her fate in I’m A Celebrity

23 Nov 2019, 23:00

The former EastEnders star has been picked by the viewing public before to do a trial.

Jacqueline Jossa burst into tears when she learned she had been chosen to do the next Bushtucker Trial in I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here!.

The ex-EastEnders star was voted by the public to do the trial, along with former footballer Ian Wright.

The celebrities learned of their fate from hosts Ant and Dec during the live portion of Saturday’s episode of the jungle-based show.
