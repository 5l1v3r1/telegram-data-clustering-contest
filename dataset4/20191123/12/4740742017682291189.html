Sainsbury's launching Lindor Lindt pick 'n' mix across UK stores - including one in Bristol

The perfect mini stocking fillers for chocoholics

23 Nov 2019, 12:31 by Alexander Brock

Sainsbury's has revealed it will be launching Lindor Lindt pick 'n' mix stations across the UK this Christmas.

The shop in Winterstoke Road in Bristol will be one of 72 stores nationwide offering the festive treats.

Speaking to  The Sun, the supermarket giant said that a roll-out is set to begin in December following a successful trial in five stores earlier this year.

It confirmed 67 more stores will open across the country by December in addition to these five existing trial stores.

Prices will start at £3 for 8 Lindt balls, and will go up to £5 for 14 balls, or £10 for 28 balls - an offer which will be sure to tempt many chocoholics in Bristol.

They will have to wait until Monday (November 25) however, until the pick 'n' mix stand becomes available in the Winterstoke Road store.
