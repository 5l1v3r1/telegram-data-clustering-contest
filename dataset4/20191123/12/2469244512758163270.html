Gemma Zoe HUMPHRIS

Address given: Hayne Close, Tipton St. John.

Age: 37.

Offence: On 20/09/2019 at Taunton in the County of Somerset drove a motor vehicle, namely VW Tiguan on a road, namely Parkfield Road, after consuming so much alcohol that the proportion of it in your breath, namely 143 microgrammes of alcohol in 100 millilitres of breath, exceeded the prescribed limit.

Outcome: Committed to prison for 18 weeks suspended for 24 months.

Reason for custody: it was a very high reading, the defendant drove a significant distance and intended to drive further to collect a passenger, she was involved in an accident and stopped near a school. The Court is prepared to suspend the sentence on the basis of the defendant's strong personal mitigation no previous convictions and considerable remorse shown. Additionally she has shown a willingness to engage with probation and there is a real prospect of rehabilitation.

To pay a surcharge to fund victim services of £122. To pay costs of £85 to the Crown Prosecution Service. Disqualified from holding or obtaining a driving licence for 34 month(s).
