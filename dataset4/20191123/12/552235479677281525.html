Cops hunt hooded gang responsible for terrifying Alloa home attack

The 47-year-old victim was left with a significant injury to his face following the brutal assault.

23 Nov 2019, 12:21 by Conor Riordan

A man has suffered significant injuries in an attack by a gang in his home.

The incident happened at around 8.25pm on Tuesday evening in the house on Argyll Street, Alloa , Clackmannanshire.

Police said three men entered the property and attacked the 47-year-old.

He was taken to Glasgow Royal Infirmary for treatment to a significant facial injury, but staff describe his condition as stable.

The first suspect is of slim build and between 5ft 6in and 5ft 9in.

He was wearing a dark long-sleeved hooded top with the hood up, trousers and dark trainers.

The second suspect is of slim build and between 5ft 6in and 5ft 9in.

He was wearing a two-toned long-sleeved top with the hood up. The top is a darker colour on the arms, shoulders and hood, and lighter on the chest and torso.

He was also wearing dark trousers and trainers and is described as walking with a distinguished gait.

The third suspect is of medium build and is about the same height as the other attackers.

He was wearing a dark hooded top with a lighter stripe down the arms, jogging bottoms and trainers.

Officers have been carrying out inquiries in the area and are studying CCTV footage to gather more information.
