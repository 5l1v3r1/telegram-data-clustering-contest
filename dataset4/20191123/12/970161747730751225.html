Dermot O'Leary claims Nicole is 'most committed' X Factor: Celebrity judge

23 Nov 2019, 12:36

Dermot O’Leary has revealed his pick for the most committed judge on The X Factor: Celebrity – and if you’re Simon Cowell or Louis Walsh, look away now please.

Apparently, according to the long-serving host himself, Nicole Scherzinger is the most ‘committed’ judge on the show.

A maker of bops and an excellent reality TV show judge? Is there anything this woman can’t do?

Speaking to the former Pussycat Doll on his Radio 2 show today, Dermot said: ‘You’re THE judge. Like if I went on that show and I got in someone else’s category I’d just walk.’

‘You are by far and away the best judge on that show.

‘Because Simon [Cowell]’s always doing, like, 50 million things, Louis [Walsh]’s not even in the room.

‘You are the most committed. And you’re fun.’

Nicole, ever the voice of reason, decided to take on more of a diplomatic approach to the host’s claims. After all, you don’t want your P45 handed to you by Simon Cowell of all people.

‘I think I’ve seen Louis in the room more often,’ she added. ‘And I think I’ve seen Simon a little bit. You know, he’s Simon, he’s got a million things going on.’

You’re right, Nicole, he really really has, including the upcoming launch of X Factor: The Band in the coming weeks, after he gave contestants just days to apply.

The show follows a similar format to one already announced by Little Mix on the BBC, and Cowell has said he’s putting ‘pressure’ on himself to beat his former proteges.

Let’s hope things between the three judges stay cordial, however, as The X Factor: Celebrity airs its live semi-final tonight.

This comes, of course, after quite the controversy last weekend as contestant Vinnie Jones threatened to quit the show live on air.

The Syco mogul was having none of it, however,and Vinnie has confirmed he will be taking to the stage tonight.

The X Factor: Celebrity airs tonight at 7.40pm on ITV.
