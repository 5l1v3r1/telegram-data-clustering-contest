Harry Styles Features an Ex-Girlfriend's Voice On His New Album

23 Nov 2019, 12:00

One of Harry Styles‘ ex-girlfriends is going to be featured on his new album and fans have a pretty good idea who it is!

In a new interview, the 25-year-old singer explained that he added a snippet of a voicemail from the ex on his new song “Cherry,” which is about a breakup.

“That was my ex-girlfriend. It got added in later on, and it felt so part of the song. It just felt like it needed it. We’re friends and stuff, so I asked her if it was okay. And she was okay with it. I think she liked it,” Harry said in an interview with New Music Daily with Zane Lowe on Apple Music’s Beats 1.

While Harry didn’t say the ex by name, fans are assuming the voice will be Camille Rowe, who he reportedly split with in 2018.

To add to the speculation, in Harry‘s Rolling Stone cover interview, his pal Tom Hull admitted the split with Camille had a “big impact on him” before writing the album.
