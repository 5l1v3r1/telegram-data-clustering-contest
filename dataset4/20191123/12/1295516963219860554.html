Star Wars: The Knights of Ren Assemble in Rise of Skywalker Promo

23 Nov 2019, 12:11 by L.D. Nolan

A new promo for Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker shows the Knights of Ren assembled together as the First Order and its overwhelming forces prepare to take on the Resistance.

The TV spot, titled "Fate," was posted to the Star Wars YouTube channel. The footage shows the various major players of the movie and gives additional context to some previously seen scenes from the film. In the TV spot, the Knights of Ren are gathered together on a plateau. Sith Troopers also appear briefly towards the end of the footage.
