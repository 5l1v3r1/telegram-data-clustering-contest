JoJo Siwa Ran Into Another Famous Nickelodeon Star in LA!

23 Nov 2019, 12:44

JoJo Siwa just ran into another one of our favorite Nickelodeon stars!

The 16-year-old YouTuber and musician bumped into former Zoey 101 actress Jamie Lynn Spears while making a stop at Child Star Academy in Los Angeles.

Jamie Lynn was also joined by her mom Lynn and her one-year-old daughter Ivey.

After the interaction, JoJo and Jamie Lynn took to their Instagrams to share photos from the meet up.

“Just another day in the office!😱 So this happened!! Hi @jamielynnspears and her amazing mom!! It was so nice to meet you all!!💕✨” JoJo wrote.

Jamie Lynn added, “Casual run-ins at Child Star Academy 👩🏼‍🎓. p.s. she and her mom are lovely♥️”

We love to see our favorite Nick stars, past and present, together!
