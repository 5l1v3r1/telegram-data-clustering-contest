Clear and cold tonight, warmer Monday afternoon

23 Nov 2019, 12:32 by Kyle Grainger

KNOXVILLE, Ten. (WVLT) -- Monday morning comes with patchy fog, some frost and a cold temperature of 33 degrees. As you head out on Monday, at the bus stop or to work, make sure you have a jacket and give your car time to warm up. Leave some extra time so you can be cautious around foggy areas.

Light the Park in Farragut will have a clear sky, but the temperatures will fall fast as the sun sets. At 5 while the Concord Brass performs your holiday favorites, temperatures will be near 60. By the time the switch is thrown at 6:15, we'll be in the low 50's. Take a few layers to put on as the temperatures drop.

If you are traveling for the Thanksgiving week, Monday and Tuesday are the best days to travel. For East Tennessee, late Tuesday into Wednesday brings our best chances for scattered rain.

Tuesday we'll have a high near 62. We're even warmer on Wednesday at 64 as the rain pushes east and becomes a travel problem into the Carolina's. If you are traveling west, you'll be all clear.

Thanksgiving Day will have a mix of sun and clouds, isolated rain chances, especially in the afternoon. We'll be cooler on Thursday in the low to mid 50's.

Shopping plans Friday keeps a spotty rain chance in the forecast and temperatures in the mid 50's.
