Conor McGregor UFC news: Notorious makes big statement ahead of fight return

The Dubliner announced that he will make his long-awaited return to the octagon on January 18

23 Nov 2019, 12:25 by Eamon Doggett

Conor McGregor has laid down the gauntlet to the biggest names in the UFC saying he will fight them all.

The Notorious has announced that he will make his long-awaited return to the octagon on January 18 next year.

But the identity of his opponent still hasn't been confirmed with Donald Cerrone highly mooted.

ESPN MMA tweeted a graphic on Friday of potential fights for both McGregor and Jorge Masvidal in a web of interconnected bouts that included Khabib Nurmagomedov, Tony Ferguson, Nate Diaz, Nick Diaz and the victor of Colby Covington v Kamaru Usman.
