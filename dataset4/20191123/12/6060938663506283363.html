Photo: Salman Khan's sister Arpita Khan Sharma's adorable bond with Katrina Kaif and Alvira Agnihotri is unmissable

23 Nov 2019, 12:50 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Arpita Khan Sharma is expecting her second baby and if reports are to be believed then she might be giving birth to her second child on 27th December which also marks Salman Khan's birthday as well. But looks like she is currently enjoying her current time with her baby Alvira Agnihotri and love Katrina Kaif.

The trio came together for a beautiful picture at a recent party and Arpita took to social media to share it with her fans. She captioned this picture as "Some relationships will always be special. Only love for @katrinakaif & baby ♥️"

Even though Salman and Katrina have parted ways but the duo still remains good friends and at the same time the later also shares a good bond with Salman's family as well. They recently even featured together in 'Bharat' which turned out to be a blockbuster at the box office.

Meanwhile, talking about Arpita and her husband Aayush Sharma's fondness for Salman, the couple has decided to undergo c-section so that the Bhaijaan and the newborn share the same birth date.
