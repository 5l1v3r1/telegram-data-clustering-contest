Kylie Jenner puts Christmas lights up in November and we stan an organised queen

23 Nov 2019, 12:03

Kylie Jenner has already got her Christmas lights up more than a month before the big day, ready for some festive fun.

We stan an organised queen.

The 22-year-old gave fans a glimpse into her holiday decorations, sharing a video of the festivities on Instagram.

In a short clip on her story, the mum-of-one told her 151million followers: ‘Christmas lights going up.’

Fairy lights could just about be seen hanging off one of the many trees in the Lip Kit mogul’s driveway.

She also showed off lights in bushes on her property, and a wreath placed above her windows.

While the vibe is a little more subtle than we’re used to the Kardashian-Jenner gang gracing us with, we’re still living for the early Christmas goodness.

And yes, there are still four more weeks until Christmas Day, but any Scrooges who have complaints can humbug off.

Ky isn’t the only famous face to make a start on decorating her house, as Jodie Marsh popped up her tree in October.

Sheridan Smith also got in on the action, whacking her tree up as she moved into her new home.

Kylie’s video comes after she had a bumper pay day earlier this week, offloading her Kylie Cosmetics company for a casual $600million (£463m).

Just a year after she was named the youngest self-made billionaire, the make-up entrepreneur has sold 51% of her brand to Coty – joining the likes of MaxFactor, Clairol and CoverGirl.

In a joint statement, Coty and Kylie confirmed that she will continue to lead all creative efforts and ‘communications initiatives’.

‘I’m excited to partner with Coty to continue to reach even more fans of Kylie Cosmetics and Kylie Skin around the world,’ the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star said.

‘I look forward to continuing the creativity and ingenuity for each collection that consumers have come to expect and engaging with my fans across social media.

‘This partnership will allow me and my team to stay focused on the creation and development of each product while building the brand into an international beauty powerhouse.’
