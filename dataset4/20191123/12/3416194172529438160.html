Meet Charli & Dixie D'Amelio - TikTok Stars to Know!

23 Nov 2019, 12:51

Charli D’Amelio and her older sister Dixie may be your typical teens but online, they’re super famous TikTok!

The duo have skyrocketed to fame on the app in just a matter of months and 15-year-old Charli has already gathered over 5 million followers!

Charli and Dixie post primarily dance videos and fun clips of their everyday lives but Charli‘s not sure what caused the surge in followers.

“I wish I could give everyone an explanation as to what happened, but I have no idea…I’m just doing what I do every day and posting it, I guess. It’s very insane to me, as it is for everyone else watching,” Charli recently told MEL Magazine.

Since Charli‘s TikTok’s have become so popular, she decided to create her own YouTube channel. In her first vid, she recapped her first meet and greet which she hosted in order to raise money for a local charity.
