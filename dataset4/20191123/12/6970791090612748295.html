2 Chainz Wants To Know How Bad You Want A Future Collabo: “What Should We Call It”

23 Nov 2019, 12:46 by Rosario Harper

Atlanta rapper 2 Chainz is ready to put some serious audio power together. The hip-hop veteran has hinted at a possible Future collaboration brewing up.

This week, Deuce jumped on Instagram with a shot of himself flexing alongside Future Hendrix.

In August, rap star T.I. co-signed the possibility of Future and Chainz linking up for new tunes.

The same week, Chainz teased fans with pics of himself alongside Future.

Back in 2011, Future and 2 Chainz talked about working together on a collaborative project.
