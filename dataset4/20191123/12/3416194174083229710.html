Louis Tomlinson Drops New Ballad 'Don't Let It Break Your Heart' - Listen Now!

23 Nov 2019, 12:57

Louis Tomlinson has released yet another song from his upcoming album Walls and this time it’s a touching ballad called “Don’t Let It Break Your Heart.”

The 27-year-old singer is one of the co-writers on the new track!

The song is the third part of the story that ran through Louis‘s recent “Kill My Mind” and “We Made It” videos.

“Don’t Let It Break Your Heart is a song I’m really proud of. It’s a song about hope and seeing the glass as half full,” Louis said in a statement.
