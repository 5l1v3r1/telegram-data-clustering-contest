Gardai investigating death of Thomas Carton in Kerry "anxious" for witnesses to come forward

23 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Nick Fitzgerald

The 55-year-old's body was discovered at a house in Killarney on Wednesday, November 20

Gardai are "anxious" for anyone with information on the death of 55-year-old Thomas Carton to come forward.

Shortly after 10pm, gardai attended the scene and the man deceased.

His body was removed and a post mortem was carried out at University Hospital Kerry on Thursday, November 21.

Gardai have said the results of the post mortem are "not being released for operational reasons."

The scene at Torc Terrace is currently preserved and a technical examination by the local scenes of crime unit is ongoing.

A garda spokesperson has said detectives are "continuing to keep an open mind in relation to this matter.

Gardai are not investigating this incident as a murder at this time but are trying to establish the movements of Thomas Carton in the days leading up to his death.

"Gardai are particularly anxious to hear from people who may have seen or spoken to Thomas in recent days or who may have any information whatsoever in relation to his death," the garda spokesperson continued.

Anyone who has any information about this incident is asked to contact Killarney Garda Station on 064 - 667 1160, the Garda Confidential Line on 1800-666-111 or any Garda Station.
