Jon Jones vs. Dominick Reyes official for UFC PPV event this February in Houston

23 Nov 2019, 00:20 by Dan Hiergesell

Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) has officially booked Jon Jones’ next light heavyweight title fight, as “Bones” will take on undefeated contender Dominick Reyes on Feb. 8 live on ESPN+ PPV from inside Toyota Center in Houston, Texas.

The light heavyweight title fight was originally reported by BJPenn.com last week prior to ESPN’s Brett Okamoto confirming the news with UFC president Dana White.
