Mourinho's Back! Twitter Reacts as Son, Moura & Kane Get Tottenham's New Era Off to a Perfect Start

23 Nov 2019 by Ben Carter

Goals from ​Son Heung-min, Lucas Moura and Harry Kane gave José Mourinho the perfect start to his career at Tottenham thanks to a 3-0 win away at London rivals West Ham.

It took less than three minutes for Mourinho to be off his feet celebrating his first goal in charge of Tottenham, but Harry Kane's early strike was ruled out for offside.

Dele Alli went on a fantastic run to create space in the final third, but Kane just couldn't get himself back onside and was denied a seventh goal this season.
