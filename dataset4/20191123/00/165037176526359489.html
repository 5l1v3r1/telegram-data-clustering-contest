Bernabéu will review its future stars

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

In a matter of days, two future Real Madrid stars will play at the Bernabeú. First, it will be Martin Odegaard, and mid-week it will be Kylian Mbappés turn.

The first one has the definitive opportunity to impress a team that is already dazzled, to the point of seeming convinced that they will recall him at the end of this season, no matter what it takes.

Days later it will be the turn of the great desire, Mbappé, who will play at the Bernabéu in the Champions League with PSG. In less than a week, 2 promising players, one of them who is sure to play for Real Madrid sooner rather than later, will be reviewed by the Bernabéu.

Those who may mark the future of Madrid will face those who today are called to embody that future, like Rodrygo and Vinicius, under Hazard's wing.

Odegaard and Mbappé will play their respective matches with different objectives. Both will try to win, but only one will do it to impress what will be his future club. Mbappé does not need it, but it is important not to forget that he is still at PSG.
