Watch: Éire Óg Score Stunning Goal Straight From Throw-In Against Portlaoise

23 Nov 2019 by Gary Connaughton

Éire Óg are hoping to end their long wait for a Leinster club title this year, having gone 20 years without one since their run of five in seven years in the 1990s.

They faced Portlaoise in a mouthwatering semi-final clash this evening, with the Carlow side getting off to the perfect start. They would score a goal right from the throw-in, with Chris Blake providing a stunning finish to steer it on off the post.
