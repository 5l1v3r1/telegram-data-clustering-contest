Celades focused on the game against Chelsea

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Valencia coach, Albert Celades, stated after the defeat against Betis, that "now he is only thinking about the next game that comes, which is in the Champions League against Chelsea, next Wednesday at Mestalla.

Celades stated that his team did not go into the game at Benito Villamarin thinking about the Champions League clash with Chelsea, "because the best way to go into a game is by winning the last".
