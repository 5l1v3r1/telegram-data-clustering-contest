Busquets gets booked and will miss match at Wand

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Bad news for Barcelona. Half an hour into the match against Leganés, CM Sergio Busquets got his 5th yellow card of the season, which means he will be suspended for 1 match. And this couldn't come at a worse time...

Busquets has hit the yellow card limit. Against leganés he got his 5th of the season, so he will be suspended for Barcelona's next league match.

And this will be none other than the intense match against Atlético. Thus, with Barcelona down to the bare bones, Ernesto Valverde will have to face the match at the Wanda with one more absentee.
