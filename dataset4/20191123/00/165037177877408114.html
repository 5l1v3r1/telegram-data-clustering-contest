"I didn't expect much from Neymar on his return"

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

PSG manager, Thomas Tuchel, analysed the 60 minutes Neymar got after his return from injury in the match against Lille.

Neymar started again for PSG against Lille after a month and a half out. The Brazilian was grey, but his manager said that he didn't expect much more after being out for so long.

"No, I'm not worried about his performance. I don't know if it's normal, but at the same time, I didn't expect much from him because he's missed six weeks. It's a lot for a player like him", Tuchel explained.
