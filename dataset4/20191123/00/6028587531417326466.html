23 Nov 2019 by Felicity Carter

Fill her stocking to the brim with these top treats. From nourishing hair masques to stylish phone cases, sparkly makeup and jewelry for the party season, here are the stocking selects for women this holiday season:

Charlotte Tilbury The Gift Of Pillow Talk Lips

Charlotte Tilbury is one of the best makeup artists in the world and her “Pillow Talk” range is one of her bestsellers. This makeup set features the coveted Matte Revolution Lipstick and matching lip pencil to achieve fuller lips for the perfect pout. The lipstick is hydrating thanks to the orchid extracts and is highly pigmented for a color that stays put.
