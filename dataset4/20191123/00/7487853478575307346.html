Sheffield Utd vs Man Utd: 6 Key Facts and Stats to Impress Your Mates Ahead of Premier League Clash

23 Nov 2019 by Charlie Haffenden

The time has arrived. Sheffield vs Manchester. The battle of the two Uniteds.​

​Sheffield United have had a fortnight or so to sit back and appreciate their incredibly impressive start to the season, as they occupy fifth position in the ​Premier League heading into week 13.

Meanwhile, a point behind in seventh place, ​Manchester United haven't quite enjoyed the international break to the same extent as they ponder, embarrassed, as to how they can make up an eight-point deficit to the top four.
