Could this be the final design of the Samsung Galaxy S11?

23 Nov 2019, 00:35

Recently, a Twitter user by the name of @OnLeaks has recently posted a tweet which includes pictures of what seems to be the upcoming Samsung Galaxy S11 series smartphone. Based on the image, it looks like the upcoming Galaxy device will come with a rectangle camera module which houses around 5 image sensors as well as one LED flash.

Not only that, but it also seems that the smartphone will not be coming with an under-display camera. Instead, it will feature a hole-punch notch towards the middle top of the screen, similar to that of the Samsung Galaxy Note 10 series.

Furthermore, the renders hints that there will only be a USB Type-C port at the bottom, while the 3.5mm audio jack slot seems to be missing. In terms of the sizes can expect to see, there will be a 6.3-inch as well as a 6.7-inch variant of the aforementioned smartphone.

With all that said, do take all the information provided with a grain of salt due to the fact that Samsung did not provide an official statement in regards to the leaked renders.
