Official Animal Crossing account now focusing on Animal Crossing: New Horizons

23 Nov 2019, 00:34 by alba

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is still a few months away from launch, but it’s never too early to get ready for it. In anticipation of the highly-anticipated title, the official Animal Crossing account on Twitter will soon begin pushing out content for the upcoming Nintendo Switch game. To coincide with this, Nintendo has created a separate Twitter account called @Pocket_Camp dedicated to Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp. “Welcome to Animal Crossing,” reads the main account’s description. “Stay tuned for more on Animal Crossing: New Horizons & be sure to follow @Pocket_Camp for more on Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp.” Exciting times ahead for Animal Crossing fans!
