WATCH: Over 4,000 solar panels on roof of new Lidl distribution centre in Newbridge

23 Nov 2019 by Senan Hogan

The new Lidl Regional Distribution Centre in Newbridge has over 4,000 solar roof panels - enough to power 250 homes.

The panels worth €1m were installed by Enerpower.

They will produce 1 million kilowatt hours of energy, enough power for 240 homes - and reduce the carbon output by some 473 tonnes annually.
