Domini Gee interview: Authoring Camera Anima

Camera Anima is an indie point and click title currently in development. We speak with creator Domini Gee at Reboot Develop Red.

23 Nov 2019, 00:15 by Chris Jarrard

Reboot Develop offers developers and industry folks a way to connect and share that future of independent video games. In 2019, the conference was hosted in two different countries several months apart. Reboot Develop Blue was hosted in Croatia back in April of this year and recently, Reboot Develop Red was hosted in Canada at the end of October.Â 

Shacknews had its traveling video crew on-location for both exhibitions of Reboot Develop in 2019. While in Canuckistan for Reboot Develop Red, we got the chance to speak with Domini Gee, an independent developer and project lead on Camera Anima, a point and click visual novel.
