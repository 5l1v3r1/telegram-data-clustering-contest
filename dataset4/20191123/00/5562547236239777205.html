Zach LaVine: Lack of Trust With Bulls Coach Jim Boylen

23 Nov 2019 by Marcel Mutoni

Jim Boylen cited “three egregious defensive mistakes” as his justification for benching Zach LaVine early from Chicago’s 116-108 Friday night loss to the visiting Miami Heat.

LaVine responded by telling a reporter that he feels a lack of trust with the Bulls’ head coach.
