Huawei nova 5T Pro specs and design revealed through Android Enterprise listing

23 Nov 2019 by Sagar

Huawei announced the nova 5T back in August and it seems the company will soon follow it up with a Pro variant that has appeared on Google's Android Enterprise website.

The listing includes the nova 5T Pro's image which reveals the phone looks similar to the vanilla 5T.

The listing also reveals a few specs of the nova 5T Pro - 5.5" screen, 8GB RAM, and Android Pie.

The smartphone will come with an NFC chip and a fingerprint reader which is likely located on the right side like the 5T, and it will be available in two storage options - 128GB and 256GB.

The nova 5T is a rebranded Honor 20 and a Google site back in June listed the Honor 20 Pro as the nova 5T Pro, which means it's likely a rebadged 20 Pro and will arrive with a Kirin 980 SoC, 48MP quad camera, and a 4,000 mAh battery with 22.5W fast charging support.

However, the Honor 20 Pro sports a 6.26" display while the Enterprise listing of the 5T Pro says it will feature a 5.5" screen.

We'll have to wait for more details to surface to be sure that nova 5T Pro is indeed a rebranded Honor 20 Pro.
