Pakistani teenager girl accuses father and his four friends for gang-rape

23 Nov 2019 by Web Desk

The police arrested a man and four of his friends after the man’s daughter submitted a petition against them, accusing them of rape.

Although a medical report has yet to be released, the girl, aged 13, had alleged in the petition that her father and four of his friends raped her.

The police have since registered an FIR against the man and his friends, taking them into custody until a medical report ascertains more facts.

Earlier in October, a man from Shujabad was arrested for the murder of his two sons, his daughter, and his grandson, following a long-running dispute between him and his children over his consumption of drugs.
