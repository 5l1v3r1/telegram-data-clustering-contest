Texas band Shane Smith & The Saints plays on in OKC tonight after bus fire

23 Nov 2019, 00:02 by Brandy McDonnell

Austin, Texas-based Shane Smith & The Saints survived a harrowing bus fire en route to their Thursday night show in Lubbock, Texas. The band escaped uninjured, but their bus and trailer were completely destroyed along with their instruments, merchandise, and personal possessions.

Still, the band is playing on in Oklahoma City, performing their previously scheduled concert at 8 tonight at the Tower Theatre, 425 NW 23. Doors open at 7 p.m. For tickets and information, go to www.towertheatreokc.com.
