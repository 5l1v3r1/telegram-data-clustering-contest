You don’t want to miss it

23 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

Day 2 of FiveSixZero promises to be as exciting as the inaugural day (read below). For starters, Ashish Pherwani, partner, media & entertainment, Ernst & Young, will deliver the keynote. Then music makers, label owners, promoters and event managers will take over, one panel after another, to talk about: ‘The evolution of underground music’, ‘The Starmakers Forum: Journey of an Artist’, ‘Effective Brand Alliances: What Works, What Doesn’t, And Why’, ‘Women In The Lead’ by the She Said So team, ‘Signing The Future’. On the sidelines of these talks, Third Party, a British progressive house DJ duo, will host a masterclass on the art of music production.

Plus, they have set aside a forum called Pitch Demo for you to drop your demo music and get feedback from experts at Release Recordings, Monstercat, Qilla Records, IncInk, and Armada.

.

Once all the talking and listening is done, go, let your hair down at the concert on Day 3, which will also feature a flea market and a chance for you to have brunch with a select musicians. Homegrown DJs such as Ivan, Martin Dsouza, Shine, Rishav Chhabra, Roan Shenoy, and Fake Tattoos will open the show with back-to-back sets, you know, just to warm things up. Later in the evening, post 7pm, the concert will play across three different stages. The Main Stage is dedicated to commercial music and will be headlined by Krewella and Nucleya; De:Vision – The Underground Stage, will cover the underground side of techno, deep-house and tech-house music and will be headlined by Sal O Cin, and the She Said So Stage will feature four female DJs – Jennifer Cardini, Anushka, Shri and Noni Mouse.
