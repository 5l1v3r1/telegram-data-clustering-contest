Jose Mourinho Wins First Spurs Match

23 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Jose Mourinho starts life at Spurs with a 3-2 victory over West Ham at London Stadium - but the scoreline did not reflect the dominance shown by the visitors.

Heung-min Son and Lucas Moura put Spurs two goals ahead by half-time, and Harry Kane's header shortly after the restart appeared to end the game as a contest.

But West Ham rallied with a fine Michael Antonio goal before Declan Rice saw an effort ruled out for offside - and Angelo Ogbonna's late effort proved too little, too late to deny Mourinho three points.
