GOT THE BUGG

Jake Bugg’s new single Kiss Like The Sun recorded live for first time at BBC Quay Sessions in Glasgow

23 Nov 2019, 00:22 by Ewan Mowat

JAKE Bugg wowed the crowd with his new single at an intimate BBC Quay Sessions gig in Glasgow.

Just hours after he brought the house down with a headline show at the Barrowlands - the rocker's Kiss Like The Sun was recorded live for the first time.

Bugg, 25, treated fans to a personal performance at the BBC's Clydeside home on Monday morning.

The live recording was shared online - with the full set to follow next Tuesday.
