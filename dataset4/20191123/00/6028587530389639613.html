Stadia Vs. xCloud In 150 Words Or Less

23 Nov 2019 by Kevin Murnane

I bought a copy of Shadow of the Tomb Raider from Google so I could compare the game on Stadia, Xbox One X and xCloud. It’s a great game that I’ve enjoyed playing but I wasn’t happy to spend $60 on a game I already own or having to start all over from the beginning. Earlier I had discovered Battle Chasers: Nightwar when I was sampling games in the xCloud beta. I enjoyed it so much I wanted to buy it for my console but discovered it was free to download through Game Pass. When I launched the console version it continued where I left off on xCloud.

So, $60 I didn’t want to spend on a game I already had vs. $0 for a game I didn’t own and wanted to buy. Starting from the beginning vs. starting from where I left off.

Advantage xCloud.
