David Beckham bragged about meeting the 'Mother of Dragons'

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

David Beckham one of the great symbols of football beyond the pitch. And one of the actresses with the most impact in recent years is Emilia Clarke, for her famous character Daenerys Targaryen in the series Game of Thrones. Now, the two stars have joined forces on Instagram, creating a huge frenzy.

Two of the most attractive people on earth, both with a legion of fans. Conclusion: the photo became viral. 

All because the former English footballer and current owner of Inter Miami wanted to share his happy run-in with his Instagram, which boasts almost 60 million followers.
