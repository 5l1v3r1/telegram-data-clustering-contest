Kellyanne Conway ‘must have done some bad things’ to husband: Trump

23 Nov 2019, 00:07 by Tamar Lapin

President Trump quipped in a television interview Friday that White House counselor Kellyanne Conway “must have done some bad things” to her husband to make him act like a “whack job.”

Trump was asked on “Fox & Friends” about George Conway’s speculation that former US Ambassador Nikki Haley is fighting to be on the 2020 Republican ticket.

“Well, first of all, Kellyanne is great, but she’s married to a total whack job,” Trump said.

“I think she must have done some number on him, Ainsley,” Trump told co-host Ainsley Earhardt.

George Conway is a notoriously vocal Trump critic, despite his wife working for the commander in chief.

“I don’t know what Kellyanne did to that guy …,” Trump said. “He’s got to be some kind of a nut job. She must have done some bad things to him because that guy is crazy.”

Trump then reiterated that Vice President Mike Pence will remain on the ticket.

About Haley, he said: “Nikki will absolutely be involved. She’s a friend of mine.”

“She endorsed me with the most beautiful endorsement you’ve ever heard, she did a great job at the U.N., she’s now in the private sector,” Trump said. “I assume she’s doing very well. But, Nikki will be back in some form because she’s great, and just she’s my friend.”

It’s not the first time that Trump has come after George Conway, calling him a “stone cold LOSER & husband from hell!” on Twitter in March.
