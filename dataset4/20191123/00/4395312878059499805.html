Sandro Carosella is a well-known Canadian entrepreneur who was born on 20th November 1998. Originally, he belongs to Canada but based on Florida and Montreal. However, he always has been an entrepreneurial spirit. He always wanted to become an entrepreneur since his childhood. Therefore, he had done everything from setting up gift shops at multiple corporate events to buying and selling clothes.

However, later he has been involved in multiple startup programs and this genuinely has a long list to go. However, if you are going to have a look at the early life of Sandro Carosella then you will get to know that Sandro Carosella is from a Canadian suburb Montreal and he completed his graduation from Heritage Regional High School. After completing his graduation, Sandro Carosella decided to leave further education and planned to pursue his dream of becoming a well-known entrepreneur. He preferred to created online business so he can boost his career in the best possible way. As a person of the present century, he knew the fact that online business will have more potential and can help him to be on the way of achieving his life goals in a more effective way. 

“Live Trading Mentor”, The Most Prominent Achievement of Sandro Carosella

In the 2017’s Fall, Sandro Carosella decided to get more from his life and decided to go on his own way instead of going to school for further education. However, by going through multiple hardships and after having various trials and errors, he fell upon an educational platform that was meant to help the people of the entire world learning the facts and trends of currency trading. While the name of this platform was “Live Trading Mentor”.

Most interestingly, the company “Live Trading Mentor” was one of the very first companies which came up with the educational courses of online currency trading courses. With the help of this amazing platform, Sandro Carosella has played a vital role as a mentor in the lives of thousands of students all around the globe. The basic aim of this platform was to train and create an enclosed trading community that will be able to build wealth. However, the huge part to market this program was to focus more on the use of influencer marketing.

However, to make this program successful, they met a thought leader and entrepreneur named Daniel Lethermy and asked him to utilize his network of social media based on more than 20 million people and show these online courses to his millions of users to start it to kickoff.

Besides this, they also ran plenty of social media ad campaigns. They bought social media ad spaces on themed influencer accounts for up to 24 hours. For their very first Instagram ad, they paid $150 for a 24-hour period and earned back about $2,000 from this. This ad campaign ended up with amazing success and “Live Trading Mentor” became a sensation among people all around the world.

Most amazingly, within the duration of six months, this platform racked up $470,000 and ultimately, Sandro Carosella became featured in Mic and La Presse CNBC. Courage, passion, hard work and intelligence of Sandro Carosella paid him with the best.

Other Startups of Sandro Carosella

Along with being the founder of “Live Trading Mentor”, Sandro is also involved in multiple other startups. Mauro Clothing Inc. is his very first clothing brand which is a custom made for the people in London and amazingly, every month it would have shipped about 25,000+ clients of him around the world. Besides this, he also has found an application to vest Real-estate brokers and to help them with their files and prospects. More than 30,000 brokers are using this application daily at the present time. Besides “Live Trading Mentor”, most prominent founding of Sandro includes Dryo Delux, iCurrency Inc., and Mauro Clothing.

Sandro Carosella is an amazingly intelligent entrepreneur who knows how to figure out the best ways to change the world around him. He believes in the strategy of one step at a time and cleverly changing his surroundings in a more amazing way. He actually knows how to play a card to get success. He is an inspiration of the students who are living with the dream of becoming an entrepreneur.
