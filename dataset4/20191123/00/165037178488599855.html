Madrid looking for alternatives for Fede Valverde and Casemiro

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Zidane has a wide range of options for almost all positions, but there is one where there are not so many. The CDM position is played by Casemiro and Fede Valverde, but it needs more players. Real Madrid are now looking for alternatives.

The Madrid fans are starting to tremble only with the possibility that Casemiro or Fede Valverde will miss a match. Both have proved to be irreplaceable, the pillars on which Zidane's team stands.

They are proving pivotal for Real Madrid to work this season and the young Uruguayan has won the spot from the veterans Kroos and Modric.
