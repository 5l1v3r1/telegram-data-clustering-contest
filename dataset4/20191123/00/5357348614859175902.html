Awardee remarks: Alicia Rose — MegaMart awardee moved many to tears in her heartfelt response

23 Nov 2019

Most honourable, distinguished guests, ladies and gentlemen, my fellow MegaMart team members, friends of MegaMart, good evening. As one of the awardees I am happy for this opportunity to share with you some of my experiences at MegaMart.

Growing up, it was always my desire to work with a company like MegaMart. I remember each day I would pass the store in Portmore and I'd say, “Mi want to work at dat big building.”

I got the opportunity in 2004 when I started working at MegaMart Portmore as a part-time receptionist/relief operator. I was eventually made a full-time receptionist. In 2006, I was promoted to the Accounts Department and began studying for my Associate Degree in Accounts and Finance.

To say I've grown with the organisation is an understatement. I have been promoted four times within the Accounts Department and I continue to advance towards my personal goals. My 15 years with MegaMart has really been a roller-coaster ride but I have achieved much because my mottos have always been: “The brave may fall, but never yield,” and “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me!”

I have accomplished a lot during my time with the company. I am married with two children, own my own home and I've managed to qualify for a Bachelor's Degree in Accounts and Finance.

Many have asked how I managed to combine work, school, family and church so successfully. What I always say is, “If you want good, your nose have to run.”

Ladies and gentlemen, I thank God for MegaMart. It has moulded me into the woman I am today: a mother, a wife and an accounting professional. MegaMart has taught me how to stand up and work hard to achieve my goals. If I can make it, I know anyone can, because with God all things are possible.

To Mr Gassan Azan Jr and all the MegaMart team, I say a special thank you, and I say thanks to everyone who has turned out this evening to support us on this very special occasion.
