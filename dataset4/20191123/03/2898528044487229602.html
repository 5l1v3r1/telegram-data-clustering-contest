Video: Will Spann III opens CFFC 80 with stunning head kick KO

23 Nov 2019, 03:05 by MMA Fighting Newswire

He might be an amateur, but Will Spann III’s picked up a finish on Friday that was worthy of the pros.

Less than 20 seconds into his opening flyweight bout opposite Jordan DeJesus at Cage Fury Fighting Championships 80 in Hampton, Va., Spann landed a head kick right on the jaw that flattened DeJesus.
