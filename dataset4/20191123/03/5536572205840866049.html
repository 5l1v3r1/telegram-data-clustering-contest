Kemba Walker Stretchered Off Court After Frightening Injury (VIDEO)

23 Nov 2019, 03:27 by Howard Cosmell

A scary scene unfolded in Denver tonight during the Celtics-Nuggets game as Boston’s star point guard Kemba Walker was stretchered off the court with what appeared to be a neck injury.

Walker appeared to be reaching for a lose ball when he stumbled forward and went head first into a teammate’s body. He immediately dropped to the floor and was attended to by medics for several minutes before being stretchered off.
