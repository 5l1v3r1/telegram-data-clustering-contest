Hrithik Roshan Set To Hike His Fee After The Success Of WAR And Super 30; Details Inside

23 Nov 2019, 03:30 by Gretel Sequeira

Hrithik Roshan has been basking in the success of his films Super 30 and WAR. Post the massive success of his films, Roshan is now set to Hike his fees for his upcoming films

Hrithik Roshan has carved his niche over the years with his talented performances in many films. While Hrithik featured in two films this year, the actor has been winning praises for his acting skills in both his films. Similarly, the year has proved to go well for the actor in terms of his films Super 30 and War having done great numbers at the Box-office. As Hrithik has been basking in the success of his films Super 30 and WAR, Post the massive success of his films, Roshan is now set to Hike his fees for his upcoming films.

Hrithik Roshan has had a great year on the Box-office front with his back-to-back hits Super 30 and WAR. According to reports in a section of the media, Hrithik is now in huge demand after his stellar performance and back to back hits at the Box-office. With the said demand for Hrithik, the actor has hiked his fees for his upcoming projects. The reports also state that with any new project the War actor signs, including any other sequels to his films from the past, Hrithik’s fee during the signing of the project will be hiked. After the repeated hits of the actor, Bollywood top directors Aanand L Rai, Aditya Chopra, Sanjay Leela Bhansali, Karan Johar, and a few others have reached out to Hrithik Roshan in regards to their upcoming projects. (ALSO READ:- Baby Hrithik Roshan Busts Some Killer Dance Moves In This Throwback Clip - SENSATIONAL VIDEO INSIDE )

While Hrithik’s first release this year was Super 30, the actor was seen playing the character of the famous Bihar based Mathematician Anand Kumar, with the film making it to the 100 Crore club. On the other hand, in WAR, Hrithik played the role of an intelligence officer and was seen performing some high-octane action-packed sequences with the film minting 300 crores at the Box-office.

Meanwhile, SpotboyE.com exclusively told you that Hrithik Roshan will be starring in Amitabh Bachchan film Satte Pe Satta alongside Anushka Sharma. SpotboyE.com also broke the news that Hrithik might be reconsidering his decision and is having doubts about it.
