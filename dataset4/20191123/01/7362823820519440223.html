'This Is Us' star Justin Hartley files for divorce from Chrishell Stause after 2 years of marriage: report

23 Nov 2019, 01:03 by Jessica Bennett

“This is Us” star Justin Hartley has filed for divorce from actress Chrishell Stause after two years of marriage.

Hartley is citing irreconcilable differences as his reason for the split, according to court documents obtained by TMZ.
