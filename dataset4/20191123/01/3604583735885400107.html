Western Freeway crash at Bacchus Marsh leads to delays

23 Nov 2019, 01:30

The Western Freeway suffered severe delays on Saturday morning after a man crashed into the side of the road at Bacchus Marsh.

A police media spokesperson confirmed a man towing a trailer suffered a blown tyre before crashing, on the Ballarat-bound side just after 10am.

Lengthy delays, which may have affected people heading to the Ballarat Cup, were seen as crews worked to clear the scene.
