WATCH: Chelsea star shares old video of him copying Cristiano Ronaldo’s freekick technique and scoring

23 Nov 2019, 01:08

Chelsea under Frank Lampard have been one of the best sides in the Premier League so far this season. The Blues have accumulated twenty-six points from twelve matches and sit third in the table. Several academy stars have joined the first team as well and one of the shared an old video of himself copying Cristiano Ronaldo. 

Chelsea star Mason Mount shared an old video of his on social media, in which he can be seen copying Cristiano Ronaldo’s free-kick technique. The youngster explains the routine first before executing it perfectly.
