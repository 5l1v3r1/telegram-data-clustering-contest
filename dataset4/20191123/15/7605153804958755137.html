Quins beat Oilers to pick first Kenya Cup win

23 Nov 2019, 15:23 by ALEX ISABOKE

NAIROBI, Kenya, Nov 23 – Kenya Harlequin got off the bottom of the Kenya Cup standing after edging out Oilers 25-15 in a match played at the RFUEA Ground on Saturday.

Kenya Harlequin got off the bottom of the Kenya Cup standing after edging out Oilers 25-15 in a match played at the RFUEA Ground on Saturday.

In other results, Mwamba were 27-17 winners over Nondescript, champions KCB beat Homeboyz 27-24 to stop the dejeeyz unbeaten run, Kisumu lost 5-41 to Impala, Kabras Sugar thrashed Blakblad 57-3 while Western Bulls fell 10-23 to Nakuru.

Kenya 7s international Herman Humwa went over twice for Quins with Brighton Mwalawa also bagging a try as Lyle Asiligwa sent in two penalties and two conversions.
