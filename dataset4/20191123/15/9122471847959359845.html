The Latest On KJ Hamler Before Kickoff vs. Ohio State

23 Nov 2019, 15:43 by Chris Rosvoglou

We’re less than two hours away from kickoff for this year’s Ohio State-Penn State matchup. Although his status isn’t official yet, it appears that KJ Hamler will be ready to play.

Penn State was dealt unfortunate injury news on Friday involving its offense. Starting center Michal Menet will not be available due to an undisclosed injury.

Hamler entered this week as questionable because of an injury to his head/neck area. James Franklin admit earlier in the week that he was hopeful that his star wideout would be active, but he wasn’t sure yet.

Fortunately for the Nittany Lions, Hamler is warming up on the field at Ohio Stadium.
