Chemical spill sends 5 to hospital with throat irritation

23 Nov 2019, 15:57 by EndPlay

HOPKINTON, Mass.Â (AP) - Five people were treated for throat irritation following a chemical spill at a manufacturing facility in Massachusetts.

Emergency crews from multiple agencies responded to the leak Friday night at Hutchinson Aerospace and Industry in Hopkinton.

The chemical was identified as trichloroethylene, a substance commonly used as an industrial solvent.

WHDH-TV reports that crews quickly stopped the spill.

Those exposed to the chemical are expected to suffer no long-term effects.
