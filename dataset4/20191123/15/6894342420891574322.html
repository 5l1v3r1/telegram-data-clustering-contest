Huawei Mate30 Series Have Sold 1 Million Units Within 3 Hours in China, Available Now for RM2799 in Malaysia

23 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Heirul Kamel

Huawei recently announced that the new Huawei Mate 30 series has sold a record breaking figure of 1 million units in China. On the other hand, the company also revealed that the smartphone’s popularity is growing ever higher among Malaysians, with over 70% of the Mate 30 units already received by its customers just a mere few days after its launch.

So what makes Huawei and its Mate 30 smartphone series a favourite among Malaysian users in 2019? From offering high performance cameras to excellent quality-of-life features, here are the list of highlights:

Capture Outstanding Photos And Videos With The SuperSensing Cine Camera

The Mate 30 is equipped not with just one, but two best-in-class 40MP main cameras for photography and videography. One of which is the 40MP SuperSensing Cine Camera, a first to be featured on a smartphone, that features an ISO range of up to 51200 and is capable of capturing 4K videos at 60fps. On top of that, it also provides several professional video modes including 4K HDR+ Cinematic Time-Lapse, real-time video bokeh effects, and a new super slow-motion video capture of up to 7680fps – the highest frame rate ever to be recorded on a smartphone. 

Full Interactivity With AI Gesture Control and Side-Touch Functions

The Mate 30’s front 3D Depth Sensing Camera and Gesture Sensor is designed to fully identify your facial features in 3D, and also detect the movement of your hands. With this real-time information received in the Kirin 990’s Tiny-Core NPU provides the phone’s AI Gesture Control to a variety commands such as unlocking, scrolling, slide-changing, and screen capture – all by just moving your hands without even having to touch the phone itself.

Aside from AI Gesture Controls, the Mate 30 series’ ultra-curved screen not only offers you with a beautifully immense viewing experience, it also provides more interactivity options for its users. Instead of relying on physical buttons, you can control certain functions such as volume by using the side-touch functions. Furthermore, you could also take your gaming experience to the next level with this function by adding trigger-like buttons on the Huawei Mate 30’s curved screen.

Taking Privacy To The Next Level With AI Private View 

Mate 30 series’ facial recognition is also empowered with Huawei’s new “AI Private View” mode. Sensitive information such as private messages are immediately hidden from prying eyes if it does not recognize the face of the person who is accessing your phone. The AI Private View feature brings a new meaning to the phrase “for your eyes only.”

Viewing It Right With AI Auto-Rotate

Another cool feature from the front 3D Depth Sensor Camera on the Mate 30 series is the AI Auto-Rotate feature can determine the perfect screen orientation for your viewing pleasure. It achieves this by identifying the orientation of your eyes, without relying on the gyroscope like other smartphones. With that said, it is even able to track you and adjust the screen accordingly when you are walking around the phone.

A Screen Worth Sharing

With the latest EMUI 10, you could pair a Mate 30 smartphone and a MateBook laptop and transform them into one super device with a unified screen by just a tap of a button. The Multi-screen Collaboration feature enables you to extend your phone’s screen to a laptop without the need of an internet connection. You can drag and drop files in between the two devices with ease, and even transferring or continuing music playback from one device to the other.

World’s First 27W Wireless Charging

Fully utilise the Mate 30 Pro throughout the day on a full charge, thanks to its 4500mAh battery and efficient power distribution from its Kirin 990 SoC. Even on low battery, expect to have the phone up and ready to go in to time thanks to its 40W wired charging, as well as Huawei’s new 27W wireless charging technology which is clocked as the highest speed recorded for wireless charging in the world.

An Ever Growing Huawei Ecosystem

Huawei is aware of your concerns in regards to specific software and services which are unfortunately unavailable on its devices at this time being. With that in mind, the company is continuously enhancing the development of its Huawei AppGallery store to provide you with more apps and services for a greater user experience.

To this date, the number of daily activities in the Huawei AppGallery has surged to 1.5 million, and 24 commonly used apps have been launched in the store in just one month, with more to follow soon after. Maybank and Hong Leong Bank are among the many banking apps that are joining the AppGallery, as well as others that cover various aspects such as social media and shopping. A total of 200,000 new users have joined the Huawei AppGallery between September and October this year, with over 100 million total app downloads recorded from January to October 2019.

Amazing Benefits And After-Sales Services

Always wanting to provide the best in customer service and experience, Huawei continues to offer you with a variety of customer benefits. Chief among these is the recently launched 50GB cloud storage, and the “Screen Protection Warranty” and “Extended Warranty” services for your HUAWEI Mate 30 smartphones.

An Advanced Smartphone At Affordable Prices

The Huawei Mate 30 is an advanced smartphone which packs a large number of astounding features, but what sets it apart from competitors is its incredible affordability. Aside from picking the phone up directly from official stores nationwide, you could also own the new Huawei Mate 30 and Mate 30 Pro through packaged bundles from various service providers in Malaysia. With packaged deals from telcos such as Celcom, Maxis, and Digi, you could actually get your own Mate 30 along with a plan from as low as RM188 per month. This establishes the Huawei Mate 30 series to be the most affordable option among the flagship smartphones that are also offered in these packaged plans.

The new Huawei Mate 30 smartphone retails from RM2799 and  is now available in Malaysia at official Huawei retail stores, Huawei Online Store, and authorised resellers.
