Lee Corso: Today Is Clay Helton’s Last Game At USC

23 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday morning, ESPN’s College GameDay kicked off from Columbus ahead of the biggest game of the weekend. No. 2 Ohio State hosts No. 8 Penn State in a battle of College Football Playoff contenders.

Before Saturday’s slate of games kicks off, the GameDay crew broke down the coming action.

While most the attention is on the Ohio State-Penn State game, the crew made sure to look at other games as well. A big rivalry game takes place in Southern California between USC and UCLA.

During their breakdown of the game, ESPN’s Lee Corso suggested this will be Clay Helton’s last game as the Trojans head coach.

“No matter what, this is Clay Helton’s last game,” Corso said.
