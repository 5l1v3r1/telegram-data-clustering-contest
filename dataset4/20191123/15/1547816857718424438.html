Looks Like John Cena Has Had A Pulp Fiction Kind Of A Morning; At Least His Post Says So

23 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Gretel Sequeira

WWE star and Fast and Furious 9 actor John Cena has been quite active on social media as he often shares pictures. As Cena makes sure he keeps fans entertained with his posts, the star shared his pulp fiction kind of morning with Samuel L Jackson

John Cena who is pretty busy with his work commitments somehow manages to find time out to connect with his fans worldwide. While the WWE star has a routine of sharing motivational posts on one of his social media accounts, his other social media accounts speak completely different of the star, which leaves many fans wondering about Cena’s sense of humour. As the Fast and Furious 9 actor wants to keep fans entertained with his posts, John took to his social media to share his pulp fiction kind of morning with Samuel L Jackson

John Cena took to his Instagram to share a picture of legendary actor Samuel L Jackson. The Fast and Furious actor shared Jackson’s picture from the 1994 crime drama Pulp Fiction which starred Samuel L Jackson in it. As Cena shared the picture of the actor sipping a drink from the film, the WWE star clearly proves how keen he is on entertaining fans with his pulp fiction kind of morning. Sharing his morning with fans, John Cena’s quirky post featuring Samuel L Jackson is winning the hearts of his fan as he reminisces the memories of the iconic Quentin Tarantino directorial. The WWE star also shared his daily motivational post with fans and spoke about knowing the difference between managing life. On Twitter, John Cena tweeted, “Know the difference between managing life so you enjoy it and your values are intact, and becoming consumed and self-absorbed. One will show you the beauty of the world and keep your mind open, the other is a breeding ground for entitlement, complacency, and false fulfilment." John also spoke about learning to be self-absorbed and becoming consumed and explained how one will know the beauty of the world if you keep your mind open.
