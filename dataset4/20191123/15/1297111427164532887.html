'Looney Lyrics' and silly songs abound in Regina Lyric Singers' upcoming show

The Regina Lyrics Singers perform Looney Lyrics: Comical and Novelty Songs From Broadway and Pop on Nov. 29 and 30 at MacKenzie Art Gallery.

23 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Ashley Martin

The Regina Lyrics Singers are getting “Looney” with their upcoming concert. With that in mind, a few of the singers reflected on their favourite funny tunes, which you can hear on Nov. 29 and 30 during Looney Lyrics: Comical and Novelty Songs From Broadway and Pop.

‘Laughing Matters’ from When Pigs Fly (1996)

Jane Ursan, artistic director, conductor, and founder of the Lyric Singers in 2012

“My favourite song is ‘Laughing Matters,’ which is about why I chose humour as the concert’s theme.” (The song begins, “Live at five and CNN keep us all abreast / Of breaking stories that can tend to make us anxious and depressed.”)

Stephanie Streifel

“Over 20 years later, the message of ‘human spirits need to be leavened by a little levity’ seems more important than ever.”

Laurie Fisher

“Its lyrics remind us that we mustn’t lose our humour despite all the chaos, malady and discontent around us — a message that’s certainly applicable in current times.”
