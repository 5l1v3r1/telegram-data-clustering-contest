Penn State & Ohio State Players Almost Brawl Before Huge Matchup (VIDEO)

23 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Darrelle Lincoln

One of the biggest games of the 2019 college football season is another top-10 showdown between Ohio State and Penn State when the two meet Saturday afternoon.

It’s a huge game with Big Ten East and College Football Playoff implications and the players certainly know that.

Hours before the game, players were out on the field and things almost got crazy when a huge brawl almost broke between the teams.
