Playboi Carti and Iggy Azalea’s Rental Home Burglarized, Thieves Take Over $350,000 in Jewelry: Report

23 Nov 2019, 15:59 by C. Vernon Coleman II

A home in the Buckhead section of Atlanta rented by Playboi Carti and Iggy Azalea was recently burglarized and thieves made off with a substantial amount of jewelry.

The incident occurred on Nov. 14, reports the Atlanta Journal Constitution. However, it was not reported until Nov. 17. Iggy told authorities she believes she was in the house when the whole thing happened. During the time, she reported she was on the first floor and heard suspicious footsteps on the level above her. She initially believed the footsteps where her boyfriend Playboi Carti, because she had left a back door of the home unlocked so he could enter.
