Ohio State, Penn State Players Get Into It During Warmups

23 Nov 2019, 15:58 by Matt Lombardi

Ohio State vs. Penn State is set to kick off at Noon ET, but it looks like players on both sides are ready for the contest to start a little early. FOX just aired some interesting footage during warmups on the field.

In the clip, you can see players on both sides jawing at each other about an hour and a half before kickoff. It looks like both teams are ready to go for the all-important contest.

The game was big enough to draw both ESPN College GameDay and FOX’s Big Noon Kickoff. It’s a huge contest for both the Big Ten and the College Football Playoff race.
