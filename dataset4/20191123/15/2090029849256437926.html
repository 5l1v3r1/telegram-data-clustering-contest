Serial POS scammer caught in Lagos, says it’s 1st time (Video)

23 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Sanya Agunbiade

A serial POS scammer was caught this morning in the Ayobo area of Lagos.

The man who identified himself as Raph and reported to be notorious in the art of scamming POS shops around Lagos, said he is from Benin State and this was his first time of engaging in the act.

A lady who posted the video on social media wrote:

People please be very careful, this man was caught in my area, (Bada in Ayobo Ipaja) he goes about sending fake alert to POS shops and receiving cash from them.. He has done this act severally in a friends POS shop and has succeeded but was caught on this faithful day. Plsss take note of his face in order not to fall victim to his scam.

He said he does the act because he is asthmatic, he claim he came all the way from Benin which obvious is a lie because he seems Yoruba. He also claim its his first time performing this act which is also a lie because he has done it and succeeded severally more than three POS out let. He is even a car owner bt claims he picked his car key on the ground.

He has been handed over to the police (Ayobo division) for proper questioning.
