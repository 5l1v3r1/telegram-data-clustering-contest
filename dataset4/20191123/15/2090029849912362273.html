I’m getting married in January 2020 – Harrysong (Video)

23 Nov 2019, 15:40 by Sanya Agunbiade

Nigerian singer Harrysong has just announced he is getting married next year January.

The singer made the announcement at the wedding ceremony of his former manager, Desmond Ike Chima, which is currently holding today in Lagos.

Harrysong has been on the radar of most of his fans who have always wanted to know if he is getting married anytime soon.

Although some few days back, the Alterplate record label founder said God has revealed the name of his wife-to-be to him, calling her Isioma.

He shared a short video of his plans ahead of the 2020 marriage to Isioma, insisting that God showed him the lady he would marry and also her name.

According to the award-winning songwriter, it is always difficult for celebrities to find love, but he believs with the lady God showed to him, there wouldn’t be much stress for him getting a wife.

He said:

“finding love for many celebrities could be hard but with Isioma, he was certain of God’s plan.”
