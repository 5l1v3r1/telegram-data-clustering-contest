Marnie Simpson and Casey Johnson reveal baby son’s name and first photo

23 Nov 2019, 15:47

Marnie Simpson and Casey Johnson have finally revealed their baby son’s name three weeks after his birth – and also the names they decided against.

The couple have decided to call their little one Rox, and they admitted it was the only name they both agreed on.

Marnie, 27, told OK!: ‘I came up with it. It popped up quite near the end. I like that it’s unusual for a boy.’

She also revealed what they almost named the baby: ‘The other names I liked were Columbus, Diego, and Emmett.

‘Ultimately, Casey would have let me choose the name, but I wanted him to enjoy it, too.’

Casey, 24, said he had to put his foot down when Marnie came up with a middle name he thought their child would be mocked for at school.

He explained: ‘Marnie wanted his middle name to be Star but I had to put my foot down! I thought it was silly. He would have had the p**s taken out of him at school.’

Marnie and Casey welcomed Rox on 29 October, and the newborn weighed 8lbs 1oz, after arriving in dramatic fashion.

The reality star revealed that she had had a ‘traumatic’ birth after being induced when she was two weeks over due.

She was in labour for 28 hours and had to have a vacuum-assisted delivery, ending up losing two pints of blood after having a postpartum haemorrhage.

Marnie told her followers about the scary experience: ‘Obviously, it was a very traumatic experience for me and it wasn’t the easiest labour, but I’m just so thankful that everyone’s so supportive and [has] been there for us.’

She added: ‘I had a bad birth experience and a traumatic labour. It did not go to plan which is really upsetting. As long as baby is healthy that’s all that matters.

‘I can’t believe that I’m a mam now. I can’t wait to show you guys the baby. I just can’t wait to show you all him. He’s worth every bit of pain that I went through.’
