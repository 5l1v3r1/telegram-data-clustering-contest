Jose Mourinho rates Dele Alli's performance as Tottenham win at West Ham

23 Nov 2019, 15:06

Jose Mourinho praised the contribution of Dele Alli as Tottenham enjoyed their first away win in the Premier League since January.

In his first match in charge since replacing the outgoing Mauricio Pochettino earlier this week, Spurs raced into a two-goal advantage and made the game safe early in the second half thanks to Harry Kane’s header.

West Ham rallied late on to reduce the margin of defeat to a single goal, but it was Alli, playing in a more advanced role, who caught the eye of his new manager.

Mourinho said: ‘I’m happy with him, I spent a few minutes with him in training and outside the pitch. And we were saying that the best Dele Alli has to be back.

‘He’s too good to not be one of the best players in the world and not playing with the national team.
