Meek Mill hilariously photobombed a fan and she had no idea he was there

23 Nov 2019, 15:22

There’s nothing quite like spotting something in the back of your Instagram photo that you didn’t know was there, and one Meek Mill fan got a bit more than she bargained for this week.

The fan, who goes by the Twitter username of Terrzilla, shared a photo of her friend hiking at Runyon Canyon in Los Angeles, and at first glace, it’s a totally cute, influencer-worthy shot.

Until you zoom in, that is…

She posted: ‘my friend went hiking at runyon canyon & @meekmill f**king photobombed her & she didn’t know til after’.

In the background of the photo, they found Meek Mill on a hike of his own, throwing up both of his middle fingers in the most epic photobomb.

He replied to the tweet, saying: ‘The funny part is I really knew y’all didn’t see me do this … I said she gone see it later’.
