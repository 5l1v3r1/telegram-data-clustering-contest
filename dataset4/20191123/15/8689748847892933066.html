Meet The Three Beautiful Ibrahim Sisters (+Photos)

23 Nov 2019, 15:46 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian curvaceous actress, model, singer and entrepreneur Juliet Ibrahim has time without number stand out as one of Africa’s most beautiful celebrities. Until you are told, it is difficult to believe Juliet Ibrahim is a Ghanaian. A critical look at her sisters will prove to you that, beauty is indeed in the eyes of the beholder.

The popular actress is born to Lebanese father Mr Hussein Ibrahim and Ghanaian mother Sarah Elizabeth Ibrahim. Juliet has two lovely sisters. Sonia Ibrahim and Nadia Ibrahim. The two sisters are already following  in Juliet Ibrahim’s footsteps  by being in the limelight as her. Both of them are up-and-coming actresses and models and are quite good at what they do.

It’s is without a doubt that beauty and talent indeed runs in the family. Well, the youngest of the sisters, Nadia recently celebrated her birthday and flooded the internet with cute photos. Her two sisters were there to make her celebrate her birthday in grand style.
