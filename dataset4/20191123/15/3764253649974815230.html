West ham vs Tottenham: Jose Mourinho begins Spurs reign with 3-2 win

23 Nov 2019, 15:13

- West ham vs Tottenham Premier League clash ended in favour of the visitors

- Jose Mourinho got his reign as the coach of the club on a winning note

- A goal each from Kane, Moura and Son handed Spurs the victory

- Ogbona and Antonio scored but not enough for West Ham to get a draw

The Portuguese who took over at the club earlier this week after the sack of former handler Mauricio Pochettino.
