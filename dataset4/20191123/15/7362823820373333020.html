'Real Housewives of Atlanta' star Kandi Burruss welcomes baby girl via surrogate

23 Nov 2019, 15:44 by Jessica Bennett

Congratulations are in order for “Real Housewives of Atlanta” star Kandi Burruss and husband Todd Tucker, who have just welcomed daughter, Blaze Tucker, via surrogate.

Baby Blaze was born Friday at 6:54 p.m., weighing in at 8 lbs., 1 oz. according to ET.

Burruss, 43, and Tucker — who wed in 2014 — share 3-year-old son Ace and daughters from previous relationships: Kandi’s daughter Riley, 17, and Todd’s daughter Kaela, 22. Their surrogacy journey was documented for “RHOA,” with Kandi sharing that while she wanted more children, her high-risk pregnancy with Ace and history with fibroids (noncancerous growths in the uterus) made her hesitant to carry a child this time around.

The Tuckers also revealed during the “RHOA” season 12 premiere that their surrogate was initially pregnant with twins, but one of the embryos didn’t make it.
