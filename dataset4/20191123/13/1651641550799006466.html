Tesla Cybertruck in Black to be available

23 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Surur

The Tesla Cybertruck is made of Ultra-Hard 30X Cold-Rolled stainless-steel which does not need any painting or surface treatment, and it initially appeared that this will be the only option available.

On twitter today Elon Musk, however, confirmed that at the very least the Tesla Cybertruck will also be available in Matte Black.
