Boston 25 Weather Coat Drop-Off Day Dec. 7th

23 Nov 2019, 13:12 by EndPlay

The 2019-2020 Coats For Kids Drive is underway.

The goal is simple — to provide a warm winter coat to anyone who needs one.

Now in its 25th year, the Anton's Cleaners program has collected, cleaned and distributed more than 1 million coats to local children and adults in need.

So where do all those coats come from? Donors like you!

Help us reach our goal of collecting 50,000 warm winter coats.

Join the Boston 25 News Weather Team and JAMN 94.5 at Jordan's Furniture in Avon on Saturday, Dec. 7 from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. for Coat Drop-Off Day. We will be accepting kids and adult size coats in good condition.

Additional drop-off sites include all Anton's Cleaners, Enterprise Bank, and Jordan's Furniture locations through Jan. 12, 2020. For more information about Coats For Kids, click here.

Thank you for helping keep our neighbors warm!
