2 NFL Teams Are Reportedly Interested In QB Colin Kaepernick

23 Nov 2019, 13:17 by Andrew Holleran

Colin Kaepernick remains unsigned following his workout for NFL teams in Atlanta last weekend, though there reportedly is some interest developing for the talented quarterback.

Mark Geragos, the attorney for the quarterback, said on The Adam Carolla Podcast this week that there are two NFL teams interested in Kaepernick.

One of the teams is reportedly in desperate need of some help at the quarterback position, while the other team has an owner who could be willing to pull the trigger on a move.

“One who is legitimately in need of a quarterback who had a very poor showing on Sunday and has got an owner that I don’t think gives a sh-t,” he said.

There’s speculation that one of those teams is the Carolina Panthers, who have an injured Cam Newton and a struggling Kyle Allen at the quarterback position. They also have one of Kaepernick’s former teammates and biggest supporters in Eric Reid.
