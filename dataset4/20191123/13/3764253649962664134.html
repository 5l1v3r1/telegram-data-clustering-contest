Appeal court judgement: APC heads to Supreme Court

23 Nov 2019, 13:48

- The All Progressives Congress (APC) in Sokoto state is heading to the Supreme Court

- The PDP won the APC at the state governorship tribunal and the appeal court

- APC's lawyer, Barrister Bashir Jodi said the party has very strong reasons to appeal the judgement

The All Progressives Congress (APC) in Sokoto state says it will appeal the judgment of the court of appeal which upheld the election of Governor Aminu Tambuwal.

APC's lawyer, Barrister Bashir Jodi said the party has very strong reasons to appeal the judgement and will be heading to the supreme court.

Addressing journalists in Sokoto on behalf of the APC legal team, Jodi said: “Legally, the battle has just begun and there is no cause for alarm. We have very strong reasons to appeal this judgment to the Supreme Court.

“With the help of the Almighty Allah, we are optimistic that, we will in Sha Allahu succeed at the end.”
