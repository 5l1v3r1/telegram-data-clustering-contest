Starting XI: Arrows v Sundowns

23 Nov 2019, 13:31 by Soccer Laduma

Telkom Knockout Semi-Final

Teams: Golden Arrows v Mamelodi Sundowns

Date: 23 November 2019

Venue: Sugar Ray Xulu Stadium

Mamelodi Sundowns and Golden Arrows will be eyeing a spot in the Telkom Knockout final when they clash at the Sugar Ray Xulu Stadium on Saturday afternoon.

The match is scheduled to start at 15h00

Masandawana have not won a match in open play in their last four competitive outings and Pitso Mosimane will be hoping his charges regain their form after a welcomed international break, with influential midfielder Gaston Sirino coming back from injury.

The Brazilians got to this stage of the tournament after beating Chippa United on penalties following a 2-2 draw in extra time in the quarter-finals.

The reigning back-to-back Absa Premiership champions will be hoping to get a revenge over Arrows after suffering a 3-2 defeat to Steve Komphela’s troops at the same stadium just before the FIFA break.

Sundowns have won the League Cup three times but they have captured the Telkom Knockout only once in it’s current guise in 2015, after thrashing Komphela's Kaizer Chiefs 3-1 in the final.

Meanwhile, Abafana Bes’thende will be hoping to reach their first League Cup final this time around, after reaching the quarter-final stage last season.

With Maritzburg United facing Kaizer Chiefs on Sunday, Arrows will be hoping for an all KwaZulu-Natal final at the Moses Mabhida Stadium.
