Alabama Shares Heartwarming Video Of Tua Tagovailoa Before Today’s Game

23 Nov 2019, 13:28 by Andrew Holleran

Tua Tagovailoa has returned to Alabama following his early-week surgery on his hip injury, suffered last Saturday against Mississippi State. The Crimson Tide quarterback appears to be in good spirits.

Alabama’s football program shared a video of Tagovailoa greeting his teammates and coaches ahead of today’s game.

Tagovailoa was smiling and appeared to be very happy to see everyone.

The Crimson Tide’s quarterback also had a message to his teammates about Mac Jones. He wants them to have Jones’ back like he does theirs.
