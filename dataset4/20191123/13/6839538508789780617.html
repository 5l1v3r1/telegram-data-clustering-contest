Queensland police charge woman with murder after children aged one and two found dead in car at Logan

23 Nov 2019, 13:16

Queensland Police have charged a woman with murder following the investigation into the deaths of two toddlers who were found dead in a car.

The children, two girls, showed signs of exposure to extreme heat, Queensland police say.

Paramedics tried to revive the children, aged one and two, after being called to a home in Waterford West about 1.35pm on Saturday.

Police declared a crime scene around a black station wagon parked in the driveway at the front of the property on Logan Reserve Road.

A 27-year-old Waterford West woman, who is known to the girls, has been charged with two counts of murder and will appear at the Brisbane Magistrates Court on Monday November 25.
