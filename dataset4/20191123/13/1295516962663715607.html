Kevin Smith Summarizes Three Seasons Of The Expanse In Under 15 Minutes

23 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Chad Denton

Director and writer Kevin Smith gave a crash course on the first three seasons of the Amazon Prime sci-fi series The Expanse in advance of Season 4's release.

In a spoiler-filled YouTube video titled "Kevin Smith Explains The Expanse," the filmmaker lays down the basics of the show's premise, the setting, the technology, the politics and the characters of The Expanse. There's also a brief sneak peek at Season 4.
