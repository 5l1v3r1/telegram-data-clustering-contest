Kemba Walker injury update: Celtics guard discharged from hospital after collision

23 Nov 2019, 13:56 by Peter Thompson

Kemba Walker has been discharged from the hospital and will be assessed again on Saturday after suffering "concussion-like symptoms" in the Celtics' defeat to theNuggets.

The three-time All-Star point guard was wheeled off on a stretcherat the Pepsi Center following a collision with his team-mateSemi Ojeleye on Friday.

Walker was treated for more than eight minutes on court after plowing into Ojeleye's midriff head first. The 29-year-old underwent tests at Denver Hospital, but was allowed to rejoin his team-mates before they returned to Boston.

Walker will face further checks ahead of Boston's game against the Kings at TD Garden on Monday.
