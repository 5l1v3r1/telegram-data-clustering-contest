Mourinho Reveals Where He Will Retire

23 Nov 2019, 13:58 by SL International

Just days after being appointed new Tottenham Hotspur manager, Jose Mourinho has revealed where he plans to retire.

Mourinho was appointed new Spurs boss on Thursday, replacing the outgoing Mauricio Pochettino who had been relieved of his duties following a poor run of form. 

The Tottenham job is the Portuguese tactician's fourth in the Premier League, following two stints with London rivals Chelsea and one with Manchester United, the latter of which was his most recent, and he was recently asked if it would be his last in England.

“I would love that to be the case. I would love to stay for many years," Mourinho said, as reported by FourFourTwo.

Mourinho also revealed that he hopes to emulate his predecessor's time at the club, before returning to Portugal where he could retire at home-town club Vitoria Setubal.
