BLAZE DRAMA

Man hurt after fire breaks out at block of flats in Perth as emergency crews swoop on scene

23 Nov 2019, 13:55 by Jonathan Whitelaw

A MAN was left hurt after a fire broke out at a block fo flats in Perth.

Emergency services raced to the scene on Scott Street after the alarm was raised around 1.09pm this afteroon.

The blaze broke out in the second floor of the block.

And one man was being treated for smoke inhalation.

Crews were stood down around 1.33pm this afternoon.

It's understood that local residents were checked but not evacuated.

A spokesperson for the SFRS said: "Crews were called to reports of a fire in a block of flats in Scott Street, Perth around 1.09pm this afternoon.

"One male casualty was treated for smoke inhalation at the scene.

"We were stood down around 1.33pm."
