Concern for Derbyshire woman who went missing earlier today

She was last seen in Alfreton

23 Nov 2019, 13:03 by Zena Hawley

Derbyshire police have said there are concerns for the safety of a missing woman.

Bethanie Reddington was last seen in Alfreton at around 3am on Saturday morning wearing a white padded jacket.

The 19-year-old is 5ft 2ins tall, slim and with blonde hair. She has links to both the Alfreton and Heanor areas.

Anyone with any information about her whereabouts is asked to contact Derbyshire police using any of the following methods, including reference 236-231119 in any correspondence:

  • Facebook – send a private message to /DerbyshireConstabulary
  • Twitter – direct message the contact centre on @DerPolContact
  • Website – complete the online contact form www.derbyshire.police.uk/Contact-Us
  • Phone – call on 101.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
