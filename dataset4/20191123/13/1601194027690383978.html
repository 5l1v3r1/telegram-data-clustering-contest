PMCO Fall Split 2019 Global Prelims Day 1 Match 4: INS finishes #3, IND finishes #6, Tempo Storm wins the game

23 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Kuldeep

PMCO Fall Split 2019 Prelims for Global Finals are being held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The 4th match of Prelims Day 1 took place in the map Erangel, from a third-person perspective. The flight path for this match started from the west part of the map and went towards the East part of the map. The first play-zone formed near the west side of the map, and it circled hot-priority locations such as Quarry, Hospital, and Gatka.

Tempo Storm wiped out Mega Esports to secure the first squad wipe of the game. Temp storm then eliminated three members of Asterion, continuing their kill spree in the game. IND and Victory five collided where IND killed two members of Victory Five, but INS played a party spoiler as they took two members of IND. The end game was very close where INS. Temp Storm, Futbolist and Party Gaming were trading blows left and right. INS was the first team to exit the final zone, followed by Party Gaming and Futbolist. Tempo Storm won the Chicken Dinner with 18 kills claiming the 2nd position in overall standings.

Match 4- Miramar TPP

  1. Temp Storm: 18 kills and 38 points
  2. Futbolist: 8 kills and 22 points
  3. INS: 8 kills and 16 points
