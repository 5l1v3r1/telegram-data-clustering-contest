Five hairstyles for a perfect bridesmaid look this wedding season

With the onset of the wedding extravaganza, it is useful to keep a few tips and tricks handy for a good hairdo and to a complete look.

23 Nov 2019, 13:39 by Lifestyle Desk

Winter weddings are exceptionally romantic and undoubtedly so exciting for all the bridesmaid out there. With the onset of the wedding extravaganza, it is useful to keep a few tips and tricks handy for a good hairdo and to a complete look. While the attire will finally dictate the hairstyle, however few easy, simple patterns and accessories will help carry a simple yet elegant look.

Step-up your hair game this wedding season with these five easy-to-do hairdos.

Hairbands or Headbands

A headband is a perfect accessory to complete your hairstyle and add glam to your overall look. Embellished, leather or flowery hairbands are easy to carry and can complement different looks and hair types. Try keeping the headband type and colour in contrast with your outfit for an edgy yet chic look.

Flowers

Floral hairstyles and buns have been in trend for quite some time now. Fresh and colourful flowers add on to the grace and uplift the hairdo, giving a fresh yet elegant look. Few easy ways to style your look with flowers can be a roundup bun with gajra or seasonal flowers, a hint of delicate flowers to braids and hair updo to complete your look.

Stones

Add a bit of dazzle to your look with a few subtle stones tucked in your hairdo for an outstanding hairstyle.
Though an uncommon hair accessory, they complement a lot of hairdos when coordinated well with the outfit with the day.

Half Updo

Getting a half-updo style is quick yet elegant. Either twist your hair on the front or make small puffs on either side and seal them with bobby pins. This style combined with soft curls for the loose hair helps you achieve an effortless look. You can also finish the look with fancy hair clips and pins.

Side Braid

Go beyond your usual braids and amp up your look with side braid this wedding season. Side braid complements your neck and enhances your jawline. For a voluminous look, keep some strands loose near your temples and curl them a bit for a final touch. This easy-to-do hairstyle works best for medium to long hair type.

“Bridesmaid should have a different hairstyle than that of the bride, but it should have a connect with the overall wedding theme”, says Sanket Shah, CEO & Managing Director, Advanced Hair Studio.
