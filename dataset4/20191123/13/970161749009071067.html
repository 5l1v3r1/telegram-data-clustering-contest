One dead and three injured after stabbing in east London

23 Nov 2019, 13:54

A man has died and three others have been injured after a violent stabbing on Saturday morning.

Police were called to the scene just before 9am to reports of a stabbing and found the wounded men.

The victim, in his 20s, was found at the scene with fatal stab wounds in Whitechapel, east London.

Despite the efforts of paramedics, he was pronounced dead shortly after. Enquiries are ongoing to inform his next of kin.

Three other men were rushed to hospital after being treated by emergency services at the scene for their stab injuries. Their condition is not yet known.

Met Police said two people have been arrested on suspicion of GBH and officers remain at the scene.
