Hawelia Group obtains Occupancy Certificate

23 Nov 2019, 13:45

Guwahati: Hawelia Group, a name synonymous with quality construction, obtained Occupancy Certificate from Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority (GNIDA) for its project -‘Hawelia Valenova Park’. The project is located in Greater Noida West (Noida Extension). ‘Hawelia Valenova Park’ has received Occupancy Certificate for 460 flats (5 tower) and commercial complex, stated a press release.

Commenting on this, Rattan Hawelia, founder and chairman, Hawelia Group said, “It is really very welcome news for getting Occupancy Certificate and looking at the happiness of our home buyers shifting to their home is a great moment of pride for us. We are overwhelmed and happy to offer the possession to our valued customers and all set to welcome the all home buyers. Our entire team, thank you all from the bottom of our heart for the well wishes and standing you had with us”.
