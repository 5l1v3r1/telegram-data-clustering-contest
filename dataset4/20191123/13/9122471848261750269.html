Patriots Announce Injury Update For QB Tom Brady

23 Nov 2019, 13:34 by Andrew Holleran

The New England Patriots released their injury report for Sunday’s game against the Cowboys late on Friday night. One name on the report stood out: Tom Brady.

Brady is listed as “questionable” for Sunday’s game against the Cowboys with elbow soreness.

Is there actually any chance of Brady not playing on Sunday afternoon against Dallas, though?

No, there does not appear to be any chance of that.

“Tom Brady, who was listed as questionable on the injury report, is expected to play, source said. More managing the elbow and taking care of it than anything,” NFL Network insider Ian Rapoport tweeted.
