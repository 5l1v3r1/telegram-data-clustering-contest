Rainbow Six Siege has launched its Black Friday sale

23 Nov 2019, 13:06 by Ian Boudreau

Years ago, Black Friday was a specific day of the year, but now it’s more a state of mind. And Rainbow Six Siege has entered the holiday shopping spirit with a big Black Friday sale that covers just about anything you could want in terms of Siege stuff: the game itself, the current season pass, DLC, and merchandise are all on sale at the Ubisoft Store.

The Rainbow Six Siege base game and Deluxe Edition are 60% off during the sale, dropping them down to $8 USD and $12 respectively. The Deluxe Edition starts you out with the original eight Year 1 operators unlocked right from the start. The Ultimate Edition, which includes instant access to all 24 add-on operators from years one through three and the Year 4 Pass, is marked down 55% to $45 USD. The Gold Edition is also discounted 55%, down to $27.

Year 4 is heading into its final season, and you can pick up the Year 4 Pass at a 30% discount, putting it at $20.99. That’s includes all six new operators released so far this year, plus Kali and Wamai, who are arriving with Operation Shifting Tides in a couple short weeks.

There’s plenty of Siege merchandise on sale as well. The adorable operator chibi figurines are all half off ($9.99), and there are a selection of shirts, hoodies, and hats that can be had at a discount.

You can also pick up some classic Rainbow Six games in the sale. The game that started it all, Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six, is on sale for a dollar, while Rainbow Six Vegas 2, Rainbow Six Lockdown, and Rainbow Six 3 are all 66% off.

Rainbow Six Siege is also free to play today and tomorrow on Uplay, which means you can give it a whirl before making a purchase. In any event, there’s plenty to pick from here, whether you’re looking to fill out your collection or searching for some Siege-themed gifts for the holidays.
