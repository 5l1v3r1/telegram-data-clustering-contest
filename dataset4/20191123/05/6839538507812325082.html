Family of Craig Hegarty issue appeal for help finding missing man

23 Nov 2019, 05:27

The family of a 41-year-old Roxburgh Park who has not been seen in nearly two months have joined police in appealing for the public’s help finding him.

The family of Mr Hegarty, who has been missing for more than a month, are concerned for his welfare due to an ongoing medical condition and are appealing for assistance from members of the public to help locate him.

He is said to possibly drive a charcoal grey Holden Commodore sedan with registration ZNL 010.

Anyone with information about Mr Hegarty should contact Epping Police on 9409 8100.
