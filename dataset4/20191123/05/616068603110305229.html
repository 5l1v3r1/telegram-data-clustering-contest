Penticton kicks off holidays

23 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Colin Dacre

 

The Downtown Penticton Association kicked off the holiday season Friday night with its annual Tree Light Up celebration.

Crowds of families filled Nanaimo Square taking part in a variety of activities like crafts, photo booths, letters to Santa and more. Apple cider, hot chocolate and cookies were consumed while Christmas carols were performed.

The big man himself made an appearance, and the switch was flicked on the lights on the tree in Nanaimo Square.

Castanet photographer Mike Biden was there to capture the evening.
