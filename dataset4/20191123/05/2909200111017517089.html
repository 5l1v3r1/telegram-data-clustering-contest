Stupid people invested in MenzGold – David Osei

23 Nov 2019, 05:32 by MyNewsGH

Actor, Prince David Osei says he’s not stupid to have invested his money in defunct MenzGold.

According to him, he’s a smart man and cannot be swindled by Nana Appiah Mensah.

He mentioned that there’s no investment that will give you the kind of returns promised by MenzGold but people were blinded by the lies they told them.

He indicated that he advised most of hi friends against investing in the sham of an organization but they refused to listen but rather saw him as an envy to their success.
