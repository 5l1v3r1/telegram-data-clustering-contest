BREAKING: General Chiwenga arrives in Harare...ZANU PF snubs him

23 Nov 2019, 05:31 by Mandla Ndlovu

Vice President Rt General Constantino Chiwenga arrived in the country on Saturday morning and was welcomed by China's Deputy Ambassador to Zimbabwe, Mr Zhao Baogang upon his arrival at the Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport.

On the welcoming delegation was also his elder brother Onisimo and Son Tawanda.

No ZANU PF or government official was present to welcome him. There have been several reports that Chiwenga is working with China to plot a coup against President Emmerson Mnangagwa.

Commenting on Chiwenga's arrival Professor Jonathan Moyo said, "General Chiwenga is back and looking fit. He was received at RGM Airport in Harare early this morning by Chinese Deputy Ambassador, Zhao Baogang. While Zimpapers were on duty to capture the arrival, no government or ZanuPF officials were seen save for Chiwenga's relatives and security!"
