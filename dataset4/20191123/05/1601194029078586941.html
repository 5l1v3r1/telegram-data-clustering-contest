Exclusive footage of the brawl between all the three brands after WWE SmackDown went off the air (Video)

23 Nov 2019, 05:46 by Shubham Roy

The final episode of WWE SmackDown before Sunday's Survivor Series emanated from Chicago's Allstate Arena. During the closing moments of the show, an all-out brawl broke out between the Superstars of RAW, SmackDown and NXT.

The show went off the air with the brawl still ensuing in and out of the ring. WWE.com later released exclusive footage of the chaos that the WWE Universe didn't get to see after SmackDown went off the air.
