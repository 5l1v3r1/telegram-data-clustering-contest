5 mistakes that Vince McMahon and WWE cannot afford to make at Survivor Series 2019

23 Nov 2019, 05:51 by Riju Dasgupta

Survivor Series is only days away and the excitement is quite palpable. I'm certain that I wasn't the only one who popped when Triple H, Shawn Michaels, and Road Dogg led Team NXT, DX style to invade the SmackDown roster.

That said, while the pieces are all in place for a great pay-per-view event, I do honestly believe that there is also the possibility of things going awfully wrong as well. I am a mere writer while Vince McMahon is a multi-billionaire at the very top of his game, but as someone who's followed his product for over two decades now, I will suggest a small checklist so that the show goes off with no glitch.

As always, I invite you to weigh in and leave a comment with regard to whether or not, you'd want one or more of these things to potentially take place. Even if you disagree with my assessment, you are welcome to voice your opinions, folks!
