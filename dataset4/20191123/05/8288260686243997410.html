'Really tough' - Huddersfield Town boss points out Birmingham City's biggest threat

Birmingham City latest - Huddersfield Town boss Danny Cowley has been discussing Blues ahead of Saturday's meeting at the John Smith's Stadium.

23 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Jamie Kemble

Huddersfield Town boss Danny Cowley has pointed out what he believes to be Birmingham City's biggest threat.

Blues head to Yorkshire to take on the Terriers on Saturday afternoon, and the home team are looking to bounce back from their first defeat in eight games.

Huddersfield slipped up against high-flyers Preston before the international break, and they will have to be on their guard against City to ensure they don't suffer back-to-back defeats for the first time since September.

That's according to manager Danny Cowley, who said in his pre-match press conference: "I think it will be a really tough game.
