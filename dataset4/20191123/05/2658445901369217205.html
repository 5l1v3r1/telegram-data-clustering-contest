Egbado College old students elect new officers today - The Nation Newspaper

23 Nov 2019, 05:53

The national general election and extra-ordinary general meeting of  Egbado (Yewa) College Old Students Association, Ilaro, Ogun State, will be held today at the school premises.

A release signed by the Chairman of the Electoral Committee, Sola Ogunyemi  said that contestants would present their manifestos while voting would commence by 12 PM and end by 3PM. “There will be collation and counting of votes from 3PM to 4PM. This will be followed by pronouncement of results and winners by the chairman of electoral committee chairman.
