Joey B Now Brand Ambassador For FanYogo Ghana

23 Nov 2019, 08:50 by BB Jones

Endorsement deals means a lot to artist globally as it’s another avenue to rake in revenue for themselves.

Rapper Joey B is the newest ambassador of FanYogo Ghana and he can’t hide his joy.

The rapper broke the good news to his fans on Instagram and he was duly praised by them for making strides in his career.

He wrote: “Happy to announce that we’re officially friends with Darryl! Say hello to the GOAT and latest addition to the FanYogo family.”

Founded by a Danish entrepreneur, Fan Milk Limited has been in existence since 1962. The company was converted to a public limited liability in 1969, and is currently engaged in the production and distribution of milk-based and fruit-based products.

The milk-based products consist of a range of frozen ice creams, chocolate and yoghurt, and they include such brands as FanYogo (yoghurt), FanChoco (chocolate), FanIce (ice cream). Our fruit-based products include FanDango (fruit drink) and FanPop.
