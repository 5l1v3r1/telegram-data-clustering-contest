Dean Smith tipped to controversially drop Aston Villa star for Newcastle United clash

Latest Aston Villa news | Jack Grealish is fit to face Newcastle and pundit makes selection prediction

23 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Dean Smith will sacrifice Marvelous Nakamba to bring Jack Grealish back into the Aston Villa side, according to Darren Bent.

Grealish has missed the last three games in all competitions with a calf injury picked up in the defeat at Manchester City in October.

Villa beat Wolves in the Carabao Cup – a match which Grealish would have sat out anyway – but suffered Premier League defeats to Nuno Espirito Santo’s side and Liverpool without their talisman.

Having been eased back into training, Grealish is fit to play against Newcastle United on Monday night.

Boss Dean Smith said: "Jack's been training with us, but I am a bit ill-at-ease to say he's been available because I've said it the last couple of weeks and he's not played, such has been his calf injury.

"But he's trained with us for the last week so I have no doubts he will be there and in his happy place at Villa Park playing in front of the fans."

Grealish excelled in a wide position in wins over Norwich City and Brighton before his injury yet Bent reckons Smith will bring the 24-year-old back into a central role in midfield.
