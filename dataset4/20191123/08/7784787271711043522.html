Scores one, saves one: Wellington Phoenix captain Steven Taylor produces Premier League-calibre moment of magic

23 Nov 2019, 08:06 by Staff Writers

Steven Taylor just proved why he was such a valuable asset to Newcastle United during his lengthy stint in the Premier League.

The Wellington Phoenix captain produced two potential matchwinning moments within the first 15 minutes against Brisbane Roar.

After scoring the opener with a bullet header, the Geordie great made an emphatic goal line clearance just five minutes later to maintain his side’s lead.

After a mix up at the back for Wellington, Roar must have thought they’d scored when

Jordan Courtney-Perkins picked up the ball with an open goal in sight.

But a recovering Taylor made a last-ditch, goal line challenge to deny Brisbane an all-important equaliser.

“It was a Roy of the Rovers moment,” Fox Football expert Mark Bosnich said during half time.

“Scoring a goal and clearing one off the line!”
