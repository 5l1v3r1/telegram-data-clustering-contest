Unused 'Endgame' Concept Art Shows Doctor Strange in the Iron Man Suit

The Eye of Agamotto replaces the Arc Reactor.

23 Nov 2019, 08:18 by Nicolaus Li

As comic fans continue to wait for What If Disney+ series, Marvel Studios Head of Visual Development Ryan Meinerding has been revealed unused Avengers: Endgame concept art on Twitter.

The special looks reveal some potential designs for beloved heroes that were eventually scrapped. The different iterations offer some insight into some of the alternative routes the studio experimented with before coming to its final choices.

One of the more standout unused concepts includes Doctor Strange donning the Iron Man suit. The concept shows Stephen Strange wearing the advanced nanotechnology armor and channeling his powers of the Search Sorcerer Supreme. A closer look shows that the Eye of Agamotto surrounded by a transmutation circle replaces Tony Stark’s Arc Reactor as the main power source of the suit.
