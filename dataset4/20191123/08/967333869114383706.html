Why Unrealistic US Assessment of China's Might Pushing the Two Countries to Cold War

23 Nov 2019, 08:20

The US and China still can improve their relations if Washington stops beating the war drum about China's "threat" and correctly assesses its geopolitical competitor, says Chinese scholar Zhu Feng, commenting on Henry Kissinger's warning that Washington and Beijing are heading to a new Cold War.

Speaking at the New Economy Forum on Thursday in Beijing ex-US Secretary of State Henry Kissinger said that the US and China were in the "foothills of a Cold War", adding that it's not too late to mend fences.

"The First World War broke out because a relatively minor crisis could not be mastered", Kissinger warned.
