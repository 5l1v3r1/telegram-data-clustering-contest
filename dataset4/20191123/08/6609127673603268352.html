WEEKSPEND STARTS HERE

From gadgets to toys and kitchens… Save up to £3,262 with our Black Friday guide to the hottest deals

23 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Daniel Jones

SAVVY shoppers will splurge £1.5billion today as “Black Saturday” sees stores offering a slew of early deals.

After slow autumn trading, retailers are slashing prices the weekend before Black Friday to lure bargain-hunters.

So expect a packed High Street. And those buying on their laptop may face virtual queues. All items here would have cost £7,846 in total but you can get them for £3,262 less.

Professor Joshua Bamfield, of the Centre for Retail Research, said: “Stores are encouraging shoppers to spend after a quiet spell by expanding Black Friday into two weeks.”
