Kim Kardashian cried when Robin Williams compared Met Gala look to Mrs Doubtfire

23 Nov 2019, 08:41

Kim Kardashian has opened up about feeling ‘insecure’ about her 2013 Met Gala dress, admitting she cried when she saw memes about it.

Safe to say, the memes were a lot less kind than Rise and Shine.

The star, who is known for her bold looks at the event, was worried about how she would look because she was pregnant with her daughter North.

‘I was very pregnant, very puffy and bloated and I was like, “Oh god, of course the first time I go I’m gonna be huge,”‘ she said, explaining that she wasn’t actually invited but went as her husband Kanye West’s plus one.

Designer Riccardo Tisci made sure she was wearing a stretchy fabric to be comfortable, but the public reaction wasn’t quite what she expected.

Of course, memes began to circulate, with even celebs like Robin Williams getting in on the joke.

‘I think Robin Williams even tweeted it, said I looked like Mrs Doubtfire,’ Kim told Vogue. ‘I was crying the whole way home because I just couldn’t believe it.’

Adding that she would have rather gone with the plain black version of the dress, she said: ‘I could never really speak up at that point because I so shy and just wanted to make everybody happy.’

However, despite the public generally loving her outfit this year, it still wasn’t without a bit of stress on her part, due to a disagreement with Kanye.

A recent episode of Keeping Up With The Kardashians showed a row between Kim and Kanye about the sexiness of the custom corseted Thierry Mugler dress for May’s fashion gala.

The mother-of-four said: ‘Well, the dress initially, since I was wet coming out of the water, there were nipples attached to it. I think with editing I don’t know, I’m not sure if we actually put that part into that fight.

‘So, he was really certain that he didn’t want that on the dress and had mentioned it multiple times. And that was still on there so we did have that discussion.’
