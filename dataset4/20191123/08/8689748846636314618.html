Ghana2Pac aka Supa is back, threatens to fight Shatta Bandle for taking his shine

23 Nov 2019, 08:52 by Nana Yaw

It seems Ghana is about to witness a war between two social media sensations as Ghana2Pac aka Supa who’s been of the radar for some time has resurfaced to fight for his spot with Shatta Bandle.

In a series of videos sighted by Ghbase.com, Supa who seem fumed by the outburst of Shatta Bandle’s fame threatened to take shine back by all means.

In one of the video, Supa in his famous rap rhymes stated that he will buy GHC5 airtime to bundle Shatta Bandle. In another video, he revealed that he’s not perturbed with Shatta Bandle featuring in Ruebwoy’s music video as he has featured D Khaled and Cardi B in a song.
