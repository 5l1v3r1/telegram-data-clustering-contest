Jonathan – Dickson Feud: Here’s What Nnamdi Kanu’s Counsel Says

A Counsel to the leader of the outlawed Indigenous People of Biafra (IPOB), Nnamdi Kanu, Barrister Aloy Ejimakor has expressed his reservations about the reported feud between former President Goodluck Jonathan and sitting governor of Bayelsa state, Seriake Dickson.

The legal practitioner went proverbial, warning that “when two grasshoppers fight to death, it is the crow that gets to feast on their carcass.”

Ejimakor wrote on his known Twitter handle on Thursday: “Goodluck Jonathan & Gov Dickson should’ve known that when 2 brothers fight to death, a STRANGER inherits their property or when 2 grasshoppers fight to death, it is the crow that gets to feast on their carcass”. That’s the summary of the electoral self-immolation in Bayelsa.” Read more here.

Hate Speech Bill: Are You Mad? – Nnamdi Kanu’s Counsel Asks Buhari

A Counsel to the leader of the outlawed Indigenous People of Biafra (IPOB), Nnamdi Kanu, Barrister Aloy Ejimakor has berated the Muhammadu Buhari-administration for initiating the hate speech bill.

The legal practitioner criticised the Nigerian president’s “tribalism” while also accusing his government of “murdering innocent IPOB members”.

Ejimakor wrote on his known Twitter handle on Friday: “Buhari’s crass TRIBALISM promotes HATE more than the worst form of any #HateSpeech. You promote hate by murdering the INNOCENTS (#IPOB) while pampering the TERRORISTS. You hate & persecute Ndigbo, and when they react with strong words, you call it Hate Speech. Are you mad?” Read more here.
