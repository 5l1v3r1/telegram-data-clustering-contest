Panipat Song Mann Mein Shiva: Arjun Kapoor - Kriti Sanon Impress With Their Victory Dance In This Thumping Song

23 Nov 2019, 08:15 by Nikita Thakkar

A new song from Ashutosh Gowariker's Panipat titled Mann Mein Shiva is out now. Featuring Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon, it is a victory song that has the members of the royal families celebrating with their Praja

Arjun Kapoor as Sadashivrao Bhau and Kriti Sanon as Parvati Bai are bringing forth the great story of Battle of Panipat helmed by ace filmmaker Ashutosh Gowariker. The film has many supporting characters and a new song titled Mann Mein Shiva is out now.  Titled Mann Mein Shiva, it features Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon enjoying their victory with their family members as well as the Praja. While the music of this song is good, we are pretty impressed with Kriti and Arjun's dancing skills.

The song that appears to be a victory song is sung by Kunal Ganjawala, Deepanshi Nagar and Padmanabh Gaikwad. The music has been given by Ajay Atul and the lyrics have been written by Javed Akhtar. Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon who are husband and wife in the film are celebrating their victory as well as motivating their Praja - villagers by dancing with them. We love the choreography which is quite simple yet powerful. Arjun and Kriti's chemistry too appears to be pretty impressive.
