Waitrose, Cadbury, Lidl, Harvey Nichols and Costa are urgently recalling these products

The product recalls, all issued courtesy of the Food Standards Agency (FSA), mean you could be entitled to a full refund

23 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Katie Dickinson

Retailers are urgently recalling these products over safety fears.

The product recalls, all issued courtesy of the Food Standards Agency (FSA), mean you could be entitled to a full refund.

Cadbury is recalling one product over undeclared nut allergens, while some Pots & Co desserts may contain pieces of glass.

Here's the latest recalls - as well as older recalls that are still relevant.

Waitrose Carrot & Coriander Soup

Waitrose is recalling its Carrot & Coriander Soup because it contains barley (gluten) and celery which are not mentioned on the label.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to barley (gluten) and/or an allergy to celery.

Some pots have been filled with the incorrect soup.

If you have bought the above product and have an allergy or intolerance to barley (gluten) and/or an allergy to celery do not eat it. Instead return it to the store from where it was bought for a full refund.

Pack size: 600g

Batch code: 27 November 2019

Allergens: Celery, Gluten (barley)

Welsh cottage cakes

 

Welsh Cottage Cakes is recalling its Spelt & Fruit Cake because it contains wheat which is not mentioned on the label.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to wheat or gluten.

Pack size: 550g and 360g

Batch code: 190920, 191004, 191017, 191025, 191108

Best before: November 23, 2019; December 7, 2019; December 20, 2019; December 28, 2019;  January 11, 2020.

Cadbury Dairy Milk Little Robins

 

Mondelez UK is recalling Cadbury Dairy Milk Little Robins because some packs contain almonds which are not mentioned on the label.

The product has been incorrectly packed with Cadbury Dairy Milk Little Robins Daim Chocolate.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy to nuts.

Mondelez UK is now recalling this product from customers. Those who have bought the chocolate are advised to return to to the store they bought it from full a full refund or contact Mondelez UK on 0800 818181.

Pack size: 93g

Batch code: CIS3092812 & CIS3092822

Best before : March 31, 2020

Allergens : Nuts (almond)

Pots & Co

Pots & Co is recalling its lighter range of Colombian Chocolate Pot, Mango & Passion Fruit Pot and Summer Berry Pot because some jars may contain pieces of glass.

Products: Pots & Co Colombian Chocolate Pot, Pots & Co Mango & Passionfruit Pot, Pots & Co Summer Berry Pot.

If you have bought any of these products, do not eat them. Instead, return them to the store from where they were bought for a full refund.

Pack size: 65g

Batch codes: Barcode 5060253701920, barcode 5060253701906, barcode 5060253702224, barcode 5060253701913

Use by date: All date codes up to and including 23 November 2019

La Boulangere Brioche Hot-Dog Buns

La Boulangere is recalling its Brioche Hot-Dog Buns because they contain milk and wheat (gluten) which are not mentioned on the label in English.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to milk or milk constituents or an allergy or intolerance to wheat or gluten.

Pack size: 270g

Best before: 9 November 2019, 23 November 2019 and 30 November 2019

Harvey Nichols Dark Chocolate Gingers

Harvey Nichols is recalling its own brand Dark Chocolate Gingers because the product contains pistachio (nuts) which is not mentioned on the label.

The product has been incorrectly packed with Dark Chocolate Pistachios. This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy to nuts.

Pack size: 125g

Batch code: 190916

Best before: 30 August 2020

Allergens: Nuts (pistachio nut)

Costa Coffee Millionaire Mallow Bites

Costa Coffee is recalling Millionaire Mallow Bites because some of the products may not have the correct allergen labelling information.

Some of this product has been mistakenly labelled as Cornflake Cluster, therefore does not mention that they contain egg and wheat which means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to egg, wheat or gluten.

Batch code: 19289

Best before: 7 and 8 January 2020

Allergens: Egg, Gluten (wheat)

LITALY Wafer Roll products

TJX Europe (TK Maxx and Homesense) are recalling LITALY Wafer Roll products because they contain egg which is not mentioned on the label.

This means the products are a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to egg.

If you have bought one of the below products and have an allergy or intolerance to egg, do not eat it. Instead return it to the store from where it was bought for a full refund.

LITALY Wafer Rolls (Vanilla flavour)

Pack size: 400g

Batch code: All

Best before: All date codes

Allergens: Egg

LITALY Wafer Rolls (Cocoa flavour)

Pack size: 400g

Batch code: All

Best before: All date codes

Allergens: Egg

LITALY Wafer Rolls (Hazelnut flavour)

Pack size: 400g

Batch code: All

Best before: All date codes

Allergens: Egg

STUBB’S products

McCormick France is recalling various STUBB’S products because they contain mustard which is not mentioned on the label. This means the products are a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy to mustard.

If you have bought the below products and have an allergy to mustard, do not eat it. Instead return it to the store from where it was bought for a full refund.

STUBB’S Legendary Original Bar B-Q sauce

Pack size: 510g

Batch code: 734756000020

Best before: 5 December 2019, 4 March 2020, 25 April 2020, 30 April 2020, 15 January 2021, 26 February 2021, 3 June 2021, 29 January 2022 and 21 April 2022

Allergens: Mustard

STUBB’S Citrus and Onion Chicken Marinade

Pack size: 300ml

Batch code: 734756002024

Best before: 21 April 2021, 16 March 2022 and 23 June 2022

Allergens: Mustard

STUBB’S Chilli Lime and Ginger Pork Marinade

Pack size: 300ml

Batch code: 734756002031

Allergens: Mustard

Lidl GB Baresa Green Pesto Alla Genovese

Lidl GB is recalling Baresa Green Pesto Alla Genovese 190g because it contains peanut which is not mentioned on the label.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy to peanuts.

Pack size: 190g

Batch code: IIF103, IIF183 and IIF186

Best before: end: June 2022
