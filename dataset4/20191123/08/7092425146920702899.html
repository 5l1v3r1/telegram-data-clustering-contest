Man found dead after incident at Cloverhill Prison

23 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Nick Fitzgerald

Gardai have confirmed they are following a definite line of enquiry

A 30-year-old man has been found dead in his cell at Cloverhill Prison in Dublin, following an incident involving two male prisoners in custody at the prison.

Shortly after midnight, gardai and emergency services were called by prison authorities who rushed to the cell.

Emergency services attempted to treat the man's injuries but he was pronounced dead at the scene a short time later.

The body of the deceased remains at the scene which is currently preserved pending a full forensic and technical examination.

The coroner has been notified and the services of the State Pathologist and Garda Technical Bureau have been requested.

Gardai have confirmed they are following a definite line of enquiry and the investigation is ongoing.
