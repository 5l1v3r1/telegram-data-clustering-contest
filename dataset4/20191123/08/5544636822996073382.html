What Zlatan Told Fan Begging For School Fees

23 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Controversial Nigerian singer Zlatan Ibile has revealed that he did not complete his tertiary education, after a fan begged him for school fees.

Concise News reports that the fan, identified as @HMphotographyx on Twitter had tweeted, asking the ‘Zanku’ crooner to assist him financially, as he just gained admission after waiting for six years.

He tweeted “I swear I just got admission into Yabatech after waiting for six years, admission fee and school fee him be problem now, I have proof of admission”

In his response Zlatan swayed the fan, stating that God probably does not want him to go to school, adding that he too didn’t complete his education.

The singer wrote “maybe God no want make you go school, me sef no finish”

What Zlatan Ibile Did Before Becoming Music Star

Zlatan recently recounted his journey to stardom in a series of tweets on Monday, October 28.

Zlatan explained how he did some menial jobs in his early years.

According to one of his tweets, he had worn only a pair of black jean back then in school.

“One of my friend just remind me of when we get only one black jean trouser for skul then !! All we need to do to make it look fresh na just hot water and 20 naira dye” the rapper tweeted.

In 2014, Zlatan who is popularly known for his catchphrase, “Kapachumarumarichipaco” won the Airtel One Mic talent show held in Abeokuta, Ogun state.
