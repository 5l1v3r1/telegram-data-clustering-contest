Oshoala 2019 football championship kicks off December 4

22 Nov 2019, 03:35 by Alex Monye

A football competition put in place by Super Falcons striker, Asisat Oshoala, for girls is slated to kick off at the Campus mini stadium, Lagos Island next month.

According to the organisers, the first phase of the event will take place from December 4 to 6, while the second phase will hold between December 21 and 24.

Oshoala declared that her major aim is to ensure that the girl child gets well educated and at the same time attain any height in sports.

According to her, the football championship is to enable girls, who are talented in football horn their skills at the grassroots level.

She stated that the championship would also be a mentorship programme to put Nigeria girls on the right pact.
