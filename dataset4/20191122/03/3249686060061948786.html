Park Bom reportedly working on solo comeback next month

22 Nov 2019, 03:15 by beansss

According to an exclusive report on November 22, singer Park Bom is currently preparing for her solo comeback next month. 

Park Bom is expected to return some time in December with an emotional single perfect for the cold winter weather. This will mark her first music release in approximately 7 months, as well as her first comeback after wrapping up Mnet's 'Queendom'.
