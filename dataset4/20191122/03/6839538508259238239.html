Fire takes hold at Ferndale property

22 Nov 2019, 03:07

A fire has engulfed a home in Ferndale and threatened another.

A fire is understood to have sparked in the patio of a single-storey brick home on Metcalfe Road near the intersection of Clovelly Crescent just before 9.50am.

“The body of the fire looks to have started in the patio or garage area,” a spokesman said.

They said a house next door had briefly caught fire, but that had been put out.

A St John Ambulance spokesperson said an elderly man was treated at the scene for minor smoke inhalation but was not transported to hospital
