Police investigating hit and run involving crossing guard in Waterloo

It happened Thursday afternoon near Cedarbrae Public School

22 Nov 2019, 03:24 by Erin Anderson

Police are investigating after a crossing guard was hit by a vehicle near a public school in Waterloo. 

The incident happened at Cedarbrae Avenue and Sunnydale Place on Thursday, around 3:40 p.m.

According to Waterloo Regional Police, a crossing guard was hit by a pick-up truck while helping students cross the road.

A 55-year-old woman suffered serious, but non-life threatening injuries. No kids were injured.

Police say the involved truck didn’t stop and fled the area. 

Anyone with information, including witnesses, should call police or Crime Stoppers.
