Star Wars: Rise of Skywalker Image Offers New Look at the Knights of Ren

22 Nov 2019, 18:11 by L.D. Nolan

A magazine cover gives a new look at the Knights of Ren in the upcoming Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker.

The image shows Kylo Ren standing in front of six of the Knights of Ren and is one of three covers for Empire magazine-exclusive covers. One of the other covers features Lando Calrissian, Poe Dameron, General Leia Organa, R2-D2 and C-3PO, while the third -- which is not shown below -- features Finn, Rey, Rose Tico, Chewbacca and new character Jannah.
