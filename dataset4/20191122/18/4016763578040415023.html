Chance The Rapper Calls Shia LaBeouf One Of The Best Freestylers Of All Time

22 Nov 2019, 18:22 by Caitlin Kelley

Shia LaBeouf can freestyle with the best of them, if Chance the Rapper has anything to say about it. The Big Day artist recently appeared on Complex’s Hot Ones, where he gave the Transformers actor the nod.

“Shia LaBeouf is one of the best freestylists of all time… I mean, like, period,” the Chicago rapper said. “He’s fire. He can put ’em together.”

LaBeouf previously took on Sway in the Morning’s “Five Fingers of Death” challenge three years ago, where he spit a memorable freestyle:

Never thought my rhyme style was eligible
Rhymes are negligible, unintelligible
Now I’m exceptional, unquestionable
No question I’m full, my cup, it runneth over
This shit’s goin' viral, yup, I’m takin' over

He also has tattoos of his favorite MCs: 2Pac, The Notorious B.I.G., and Missy Elliott.
