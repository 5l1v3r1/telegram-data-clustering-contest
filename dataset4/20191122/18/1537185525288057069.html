Nazareth man killed after crashing into guard rail near Childress Thursday night

22 Nov 2019, 18:11 by Kaitlin Johnson

CHILDRESS COUNTY, Texas (KFDA) - One person has died after a crash near Childress on Thursday night.

Around 10:35 p.m., DPS officials say 59-year-old Glenn Earl Waldo of Nazareth was driving east on US 287 when he lost control of his vehicle for an unknown reason.

His pickup struck a guard rail head on.

He was thrown from the pickup and pronounced dead on the scene.

DPS officials say he was not wearing a seat belt.
