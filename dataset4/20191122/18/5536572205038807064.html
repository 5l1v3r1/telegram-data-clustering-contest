XFL Reveals More Ex-NFL, College Players That Will Be Playing In The League In 2020 (TWEETS)

22 Nov 2019, 18:52 by Darrelle Lincoln

The start of the XFL is just some less than three months away, as it’s slated to kick off the weekend after Super Bowl LIV is held on February 2nd.

The eight XFL teams will be located in Dallas, Houston, Los Angeles, New York, Seattle, St. Louis, Tampa, and Washington D.C.
