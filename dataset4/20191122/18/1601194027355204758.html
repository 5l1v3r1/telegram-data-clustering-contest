WWE Survivor Series 2019: 5 Superstars who will miss the PPV (and the reasons why)

22 Nov 2019, 18:05 by Danny Hart

A total of 48 Superstars will compete in matches at the 2019 WWE Survivor Series pay-per-view, including 16 from the NXT brand.

The featured matches at the upcoming event include Brock Lesnar vs. Rey Mysterio (WWE Championship), “The Fiend” Bray Wyatt vs. Daniel Bryan (Universal Championship) and Adam Cole vs. TBC (NXT Championship), while five RAW vs. SmackDown vs. NXT matches are also scheduled for the show.

Despite the large amount of Superstars who have been booked for this year’s battle of brand supremacy, there are still several high-profile names who are set to miss out on the event for a variety of reasons.

In this article, let’s count down five Superstars who have been omitted from Survivor Series and take a look at the likely reasons why they will not be competing on the show.
