Air Travel

22 Nov 2019, 18:08 by BRIEF

MINNEAPOLIS — A black man claims that Sun Country Airlines refused to let him and two black friends fly from Los Angeles to Minneapolis on their first-class tickets, and that they were the only black folks in first class, in Hennepin County Court.
