Salman Khan issues chilling warning to Siddharth Shukla, dares him for physical fight either inside or outside Bigg Boss house

22 Nov 2019, 18:36 by JKR Staff

Bollywood megastar and Bigg Boss show’s host Salman Khan has reportedly lashed out at contestant Siddharth Shukla and reminded him of the consequences of his bad behaviour. Salman also reminded him of consequences if he wants to stay in the ‘industry.’ In another extraordinary development, Salman challenged Siddharth for a fight either inside the Bigg Boss house or outside.

An angry Salman told Siddharth bluntly that if he was so keen to flaunt his muscle power, then he should try that on the former either inside the reality show or outside. “Shukla ji, you have plenty of anger and you have a huge physique. Would you like to try it (on me) at some point? Come out and try it (on me). Or call me inside the house,” said Salman menacingly.

Salman also reportedly asked Siddharth if he had no intention to work in the industry, implying that he will have no work if his violent behaviour continued.
