Person of Interest in Stephanie Parze’s Disappearance Has Died by Suicide, Prosecutors Say

22 Nov 2019, 18:22 by Alberto Luperon

New Jersey man John Ozbilgen, a defendant in a child pornography case who was also a person of interest in the disappearance of ex-girlfriend Stephanie Parze, was found dead at his home Friday morning, prosecutors say. Authorities said Ozbilgen died by suicide.
