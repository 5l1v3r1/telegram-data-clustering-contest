How to find the best Black Friday deals on memory cards for your Amazon Fire Tablet

Memory cards (or microSD cards) are pretty simple gadgets. They add more storage to your device, giving you more space to store movies, music, games, and more. The higher the number on the card you buy, the more digital content you'll be able to store. A 16GB microSD card will be alright if you just need a little more space, but if you plan on loading your Fire Tablet up with as many movies as possible, you'll want to splurge for something roomier — like a 128GB or 200GB card. In addition to the storage size, each microSD card also comes with different transfer speeds. This determines how fast your content is loaded onto the card. The faster the transfer speeds are, the more quickly you'll be able to load the card up with all of your files.
