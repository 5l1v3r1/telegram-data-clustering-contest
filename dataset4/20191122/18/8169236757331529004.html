Confirmed Barcelona squad vs Leganes: Key man ruled out, these fans upset over snub in midfield

22 Nov 2019, 18:38 by Tom McNeil

This should be about as easy as they come for Barcelona tomorrow. Yes they are away from home, but they go into the game top of La Liga and face Leganes who are rock bottom of the division.

That said, the Catalan giants have looked vulnerable away from home this season with losses to Athletic Bilbao and Levante, while they could only manage a draw away to Osasuna earlier in the season as well. That does suggest this might not be as straight-forward a result as you might expect.
