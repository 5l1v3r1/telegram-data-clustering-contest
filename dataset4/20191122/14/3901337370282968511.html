Orlando Pirates New Lobster & Lemonade Swag!

22 Nov 2019, 14:33 by SL Social

Fire new headwear! The sick swag features Orlando Pirates branding and geometric patterns.

Pirates’ have teamed up with German brand, Lobster & Lemonade, to release two new caps and two new bucket hats. 

Check out the gallery above to see the dope new Pirates merch!

Modelled on the Orlando Pirates official Instagram account by winger Vincent Pule and centre-forward Tshegofatso Mabasa, the new release is sure to impress supporters and fashionistas alike. 

Who is Lobster & Lemonade?

Based near Frankfurt, Germany, headwear brand Lobster & Lemonade have added a South African team to their collection for the first time! The Orlando Pirates collection can be seen alongside the likes of Burnley FC, FC Zurich and Slavia Praha on their official website.

A Fresh Look!

Images revealed by Lobster & Lemonade reveal the new Pirates collection to be a welcome change from the usual and simple branding of sports merchandise. 

The first of two bucket hats feature a wild geometric print and slogan 'Up The Bucs!' under the brim, which is sure to turn heads in the street. The second bucket hat has a more classic look, all black with the Pirates logo on the front. The top, however, is a unique and practical style choice. Mesh allows the hat to ventilate your top on a hot day. Branded with a large Pirates skull, this hat is the smart choice for match day. 

On the cap side of things, a snapback and baseball cap conclude this collection. The snapback is simple, with Pirates’ branding on the front and the 'Up The Bucs!' slogan on the side, and a cool twist of colour on the back. 

Check out Pule and Mabasa rocking the sick Lobster & Lemonade collection in the gallery above!

The baseball cap completes the release in style. 

The simple black the cap doesn’t stand out at first, but this toned down look with embroidered 'Pirates' on the front allows you to make it a part of any outfit. Flex your favourite team in style. The cap features a Pirates skull and crossbones patch on the back, along with stylish white patterning on the adjustment tab.
