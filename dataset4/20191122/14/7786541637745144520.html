High winds blow trailer on top of pickup truck bed

22 Nov 2019, 14:53 by Jared Leone, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

CHADRON, Neb. -

High winds blew a trailer onto the bed of a pickup truck that was driving along a highway near the border of South Dakota and Nebraska.

>> Read more trending news  

Nebraska State Patrol shared a photo Wednesday of the trailer balancing on the back of the pickup truck. 

"High winds and a lightweight trailer resulted in this unlikely predicament," a trooper said on social media.
