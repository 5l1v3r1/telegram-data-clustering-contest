Amazon Black Friday 2019 sale sees Le Creuset casserole dishes get huge price cut

Amazon kicked off its Black Friday sale today and it includes savings on popular (and pricey) Le Creuset kitchenware - but shoppers need to act fast as the deal isn't on for long

22 Nov 2019, 14:52 by Chantelle Symester, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

If you're a fan of the premium Le Creuset kitchenware you may want to check out one of Amazon's amazing Black Friday offer.

Selected round casserole dishes in a number of sizes are now half price in the Black Friday discounts, which means home cooks can pick up 20cm Le Creuset dish from £89.99 - saving a whopping £95 off the normal £185 price tag.

There's also a larger 24cm option that is now £119.99, reduced from £225 - a saving of 41 per cent and a shallow 26cm version for £99.99 (was £210). But shoppers should be aware the lightning deal means the incredible price cuts are only on until midnight, while stock lasts.

The popular cookware items would make the perfect gifts for anyone who loves cooking for Christmas and comes in a few stylish colours, including a nice dark purple, midnight grey and navy.

The deal looks to be the cheapest we've spotted online, with rival retailer John Lewis only offering up to 30% off selected Le Creuset items. A 24cm dish will set shoppers back between £150 and £157.50, depending on colour, and prices for the smaller 20cm dish starts from £129.50.

House of Fraser is even more costly, with its selection of cast iron dishes starting from £172 for selected 24cm dishes.

Amazon Black Friday sale 2019
