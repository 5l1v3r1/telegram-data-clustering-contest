BREAKING: Buhari, Osinbajo, governors, party leaders attend APC NEC

22 Nov 2019, 14:36 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

Bolaji Ogundele, Abuja

The National Executive Committee (NEC) of the All Progressives Congress (APC) convened at the party’s National Secretariat Friday afternoon.

As at about 3pm when the meeting opened, most of members of the NEC had arrived the venue, waiting for the arrival of President Muhammadu Buhari, who came in at about 3:15pm.

Governors, members of the National Assembly as well as members of the party’s executive were all part of the meeting.
