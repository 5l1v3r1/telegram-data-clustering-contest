Missing Cavan teen found safe and well

Gardaí issued an appeal for information earlier today.

22 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Hayley Halpin

A TEENAGE BOY who had been missing since Wednesday has been located.

17-year-old Reece Murphy had last been seen in the Ballyjamesduff area of Co Cavan. Gardaí issued an appeal for information earlier today.

The boy has now been found safe and well. 

Gardaí thanked the public for their assistance.
