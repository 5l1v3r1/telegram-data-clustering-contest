PICS: “Evil Cop” Who Vigorously Beat Up Civilians In Brutal Assault By Police UNMASKED

22 Nov 2019, 14:13 by Timothy Ndoro

Cop Who Vigorously Beat Up Civilians Unmasked

One of the ZRP officers who vigorously and enthusiastically beat up civilians near the MDC Headquarters has been unmasked.  The police reacted in a heavy-handed manner and beat up all civilians who were near the Morgan Richard Tsvangirai House (former Harvest House) indiscriminately.  The beatings started a few minutes after opposition leader Nelson Chamisa arrived to deliver his Hope of The Nation Address (HONA).

According to online publication Zimlive, the officer who brutally tripped a woman who was trying to run away from the chaos is Officer Ndawanahama Mtambisi from Marimba Police Station.

Outraged people from across the world have called on human rights lawyers to sue the police force as well as the individual police officers in their individual capacities for the excessive force that they used is dispersing peaceful people who were not resisting. The police’s actions were condemned around the world.
