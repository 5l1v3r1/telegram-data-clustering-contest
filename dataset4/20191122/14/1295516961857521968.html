Three Jokers Tease Teams Batman and Jason Todd

22 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Jon Arvedon

Batman: Three Jokers artist Jason Fabok has shared yet another tease from his and Geoff Johns' upcoming three-issue miniseries under DC's Black Label.

The black-and-white image features the Caped Crusader and Red Hood/Jason Todd "going for a little drive in the rain" in the Batmobile.
