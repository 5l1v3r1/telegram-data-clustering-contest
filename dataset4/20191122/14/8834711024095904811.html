Liberty Founder Donald Gordon Dies

22 Nov 2019, 14:55 by SG Editor

Liberty founder Sir Donald Gordon has died at the age of 89.

On Friday, the financial services company announced Gordon passed away after a short illness.

He founded Liberty in 1957 on principles born of a deep belief in humanity and the desire to help people to leave a legacy for their families.

Liberty said it would continue to honour his vision.
