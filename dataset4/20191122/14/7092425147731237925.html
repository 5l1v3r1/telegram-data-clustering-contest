Grandmother ends climate change hunger strike outside Dail Eireann

22 Nov 2019, 14:10 by Ciara Walsh

The 78-year-old began protesting outside Dail Eireann on Wednesday, November 20.

A grandmother who has been on hunger strike for three days over climate change is ending her protest today.

Patricia Devlin is joining the Extinction Rebellion rally around now.

Mrs Devlin even met with Minister Bruton to air her grievances.
