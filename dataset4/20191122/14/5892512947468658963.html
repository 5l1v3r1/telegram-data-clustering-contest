Video: Fists start flying at Bellator weigh ins during chaotic ‘London’ scuffle

22 Nov 2019, 14:56 by stevejuon, Jesse Holland

The weigh ins for Bellator London 2 went down earlier today (Fri., Nov. 22, 2019) ahead of tomorrow night’s “MVP vs. Melillo” mixed martial arts (MMA) event inside SSE Arena in London, England, on Channel 5.

“Venom” will be unleashed as Michael Page (15-1) takes on Giovanni Melillo (13-4).

Unfortunately for the welterweight headliners, they’ve been upstaged for the second-straight day thanks to yet another pre-fight scuffle, this time between middleweight rivals Fabian Edwards and Mike Shipman.
