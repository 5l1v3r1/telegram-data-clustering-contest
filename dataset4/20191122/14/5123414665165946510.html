Richie Hawtin presents CLOSE this Saturday at the Palladium (Los Angeles)

Catch a very rare appearance of Richie Hawtin this Saturday at the Palladium!

22 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Danny Kaufman

After nearly a four-year absence, Richie Hawtin returns to the City of Angels this Saturday night for a rare, one-night performance of his critically-acclaimed audio/visual set-up, CLOSE. A show which earned him the award for best live performance at the DJ Awards back in 2017 and he debuted it that same year just a few hours east of L.A. at Coachella. Don't fret if you were there for that though because the performance is said to be like no other thanks to a 100 percent improvised format guaranteeing that no two shows can be alike.
