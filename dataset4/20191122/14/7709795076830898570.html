Angels Breath 14/1 for Arkle with Sky Bet following chasing debut win at Ascot

22 Nov 2019, 14:04 by Matt Brocklebank

The Nicky Henderson-trained Angels Breath earned a 14/1 Arkle quote with Sky Bet following a taking first start over fences at Ascot on Friday.

The grey five-year-old was one of two highly-regarded horses from the Seven Barrows yard in the Sky Sports Racing Novices' Chase and the betting got it spot on regarding the pair, with Angels Breath a well-backed 5/4 chance under Nico De Boinville.

Stablemate Birchdale, the initial favourite, proved easy to back and ran as though the experience was needed, ending up a well-held third of four runners.

Second went to the front-running First Flow for Kim Bailey and David Bass, but he was a spent force coming to the second last fence and ended up eight lengths adrift of the impressive winner, who popped the last and was eased down on the run-in.

Sky Bet cut Angels Breath to 14/1 from 25/1 for the Racing Post Arkle Trophy at Cheltenham in March and made the same antepost alteration for the JLT Novices' Chase at the Festival.

Angels Breath was taking his Ascot record to two-from-two, having made a winning hurdling debut in a Grade Two contest at the same venue last December, after which he was second at Kempton before finishing seventh behind Klassical Dream when 6/1 for the Sky Bet Supreme Novices' Hurdle.
