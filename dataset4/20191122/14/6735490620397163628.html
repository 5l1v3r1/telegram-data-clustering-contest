Kenan Thompson Jokes About The Wedding Gift He Has In Mind For ‘SNL’ Co-Star Colin Jost And Fiancée Scarlett Johansson

22 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Brent Furdyk

Kenan Thompson was on hand for Thursday night’s 2019 American Museum of Natural History Gala in New York City, and Us Weekly caught up with the “Saturday Night Live” star to ask a very important question: what does he plan to get his co-star Colin Jost and his fiancée, Scarlett Johansson, as a wedding gift?

“I think a Range Rover,” quipped Thompson. “I think we’re all chipping in on a Range Rover. They need that for the kiddies coming — nice, roomy.”

Us Weekly also spoke with “SNL” creator Lorne Michaels, who told the magazine that he and wife Alice Berry were brainstorming some gift ideas for the couple, who announced their engagement in May.

“I’ll see Colin later tonight, and Scarlett is hosting the show in a couple of weeks,” said Michaels. “I love them both. I’m happy for them both.”

Johansson opened up about Jost’s proposal during a recent appearance on “The Ellen DeGeneres Show”.

“He killed it,” she told DeGeneres of Jost popping the question. “He did, like, a whole James Bond situation. He’s got a lot behind that news desk. He’s very charming and very thoughtful and romantic. I was surprised… When someone tells you they want to share the rest of their life with you, that is just a lovely, special thing.”

Johansson will host the Dec. 14 edition of “Saturday Night Live”, airing Saturdays at 11:30 p.m. ET/8:30 p.m. PT on Global.
