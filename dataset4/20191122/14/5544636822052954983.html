Chris Brown Welcomes Second Baby

22 Nov 2019, 14:55 by Oyindamola Olubajo

American superstar, Chris Brown has welcomed his second baby from his ex, Ammika Harris, Concise News understands.

The news of the baby’s arrival was shared by Brown and Harris on their social media handles on Thursday.

TMZ, reports that this would be the first child the singer and his ex welcomed.

Brown shared a photo of himself smiling and looking down at something off-camera with the caption of the date 11-20-19.

Shortly after, he shared a pic of himself wearing a hoodie with the word “BORN” embroidered on it.

Harris also on her Instagram Story, wrote “I was in love when I first saw you,” with a heart emoji.

Brown however has a 5-year-old daughter named Royalty with Nia Guzman.

The pregnancy news was revealed earlier this summer in a cryptic Instagram comment posted by No Guidance Brown.

Although news of the pregnancy with Harris first broke in June, fans caught on as early as May.

Ammika is an aspiring model and was Chris Brown’s ex before becoming the baby mama. The pregnancy news was revealed earlier this summer in a cryptic Instagram comment posted by Brown.
