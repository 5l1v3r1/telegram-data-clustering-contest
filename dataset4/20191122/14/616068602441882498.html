'Classic' a magnificent beast

22 Nov 2019, 14:04 by Nicholas Johansen

For the car collector out there with a taste for the classics, a West Kelowna seller has a deal for you.

Described as a “magnificent beast” that “runs like a Porsche,” the seller of a harvest gold 1980 Honda Civic had some fun with the listing in Castanet's classifieds section.

“I have to sell it because I'm being hounded by all the single moms at my kid's soccer games as well as 1974 Miss Playmate of the year who somehow discovered I was the owner of this magnificent beast,” reads the ad.

The classic two-door “sports car” comes loaded with four seats and a steering wheel, along with gas, brake, and clutch pedals, all for just $1,200.

“Is it fuel efficient while offering a beautiful purr that only a four-cylinder engine from the '80s can provide? Indeed.”

For no extra charge, the lucky buyer will also get an interior covered in dog hair, along with “a pretty good chance it comes with a small family of squirrels who now reside in the back.”

The car could be the perfect purchase for the person who loves to get their hands dirty, it requires a passenger-side mirror, a new windshield, new battery, an oil change, spark plugs, and “a little tree air freshener to hang off the rear view mirror.”

“All that is negated though because it comes with a CD player to play your favourite Ace of Bass CDs with the windows down at any car show you should choose to win a gold medal in,” the ad states.

While the seller doesn't know exactly how many kilometres are on the car, the ad says “let's assume 40 million.”

“Good luck to the successful buyer of this prize. I will need your address and phone number upon sale so that I can get 1974 Miss Playmate to start bothering you at all hours instead of myself. She's relentless.”
