Molly-Mae Hague debuts short hair in defiant clapback after bum drama

Love Island's Molly-Mae Hague showed off her fresh makeover after a cute date night with boyfriend Tommy Fury

22 Nov 2019, 14:47 by Emmeline Saunders

Molly-Mae Hague has shown off her brand new winter makeover with shorter hair, after hitting back at the haters over unairbrushed pictures of her bikini body .

The Love Island 2019 star unveiled her chic new look on Instagram, sharing a snap of herself posing in a white-tiled bathroom with her fresh blonde locks.

"So this happened. Hey winter hair...." she captioned the pouting pic.

Fans flocked to gush over her sleek and straightened shorter style, with one commenting: "OMG didn't realise u needed this til now."

Molly replied: "Hahaha same."

Instagram model Jess Hunt told her pal, "I love it so much", and Molly wrote back: "Thanks my girl."

Molly-Mae also shared clips of her enjoying her new look on a cute date night with her reality star boyfriend Tommy Fury, who reacted in a typically male way by focusing on his meal.

Molly had the makeover as part of a fashion shoot for PrettyLittleThing, for who she's a collaborator and model.

It comes after the 20-year-old influencer shared a defiant clapback after receiving cruel comments about her body.

The blonde bombshell had stripped down to a white bikini for a photoshoot in Barbados earlier this week, but nasty commenters felt the need to tear her apart over her natural shape.

In response, Molly published a picture of her bum covered in white netted trousers yesterday, captioning it: "To the people that are kind, thank you."

It comes after ITV sources confirmed this year's Love Island Christmas Reunion special won't be filmed, with some stars of this summer's series said to be annoyed they've been 'ignored' by bosses.

Since Caroline Flack brought back the series in 2015, only in 2018 have the stars of the summer show got together again for a special December episode.

It was full of drama as warring exes who have been slating each other over Twitter and Instagram set eyes on each other for the first time since leaving Majorca.

But ITV bosses have quietly axed the one-off special.

"The cast don't think there are any plans for a Christmas reunion like last year, but they haven't been given a straight answer," an insider told OK! Online .

"A few of the islanders have seen the producers since leaving the villa and there was a bit of a reunion at the ITV Palooza last week and the topic was brought up but quickly shut down.

"Questions were asked about the possibility of an on-screen reunion, but they were all ignored by producers."

Instead, ITV is focusing on its brand new Love Island Winter series, which starts filming in South Africa in January, hosted by the Flackster.
