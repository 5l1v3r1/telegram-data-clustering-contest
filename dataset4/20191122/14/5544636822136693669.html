EPL: Solskjaer Speaks On Pochettino ‘Displacing’ Him As Man Utd Manager

22 Nov 2019, 14:18 by Ridwan Yusuf

Manchester United boss Ole Gunnar Solskjaer says every manager would want his job but he feels no extra pressure in the English Premier League (EPL) after Mauricio Pochettino became available following his sacking by Tottenham Hotspur, Concise News reports.

Pochettino, who was sacked by Spurs on Tuesday and replaced by former United manager Jose Mourinho, was linked with the Old Trafford job by British media following the Portuguese’s departure last season.
