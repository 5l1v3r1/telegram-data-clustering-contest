Lunachicks add second NYC show & Punk Rock Bowling

22 Nov 2019, 14:58 by Amanda Hatfield

NYC punk rockers Lunachicks announced their first show in over a decade, happening at Webster Hall on April 11, earlier this week. That show is now sold out, so the band has added a second the next night, on April 12 at Webster Hall. Tickets are on sale now, and Toilet Boys open.

In addition to the second NYC date, Lunachicks are also on the lineup of the just-announced 2020 edition of Punk Rock Bowling in Las Vegas in May, which also features UK ska icons Madness, a reunited Circle Jerks, Cock Sparrer, and more.
