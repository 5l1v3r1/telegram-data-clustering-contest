Big Rod: A Closer Look At Rama G's Elegant SUV

22 Nov 2019, 14:15 by SL Social

Rodney Ramagalela’s big ride befits his big match temperament, somewhat of a match made in motoring heaven. Take a closer look at the Highlands Park star’s classy SUV.

Injury and a lack of game-time this season meant that Ramagelela is yet to find the back of the net for the Lions of the North, but the 30-year-old is back in training and raring to go.

Off the field, the former Mamelodi Sundowns and Polokwane City striker is not one for the spotlight.
