Nokia coming to SLUSH 2019 together with Telia and OP

22 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Marin

The SLUSH event is becoming the main event for all the tech-oriented startup companies to share their achievements and try to find partners to do business. Nokia used the SLUSH to launch its Nokia N1 tablet a few years ago, and since then it was present at the event. This year the visitors will also be able to find Nokia people there but as a partner to Finnish operator Telia. They will be demonstrating the recognition based biometric authentication payment over 5G that was developed in cooperation with the Finnish company OP. Kf you are coming to Slush, you can reach them at the booth 6C.8!
