Roku TV Makes Its UK Debut This Black Friday

22 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Shabana Arif

The Hisense Roku TV rolls out in Argos next week, with four models to choose from.

First announced for the UK at IFA 2019, the TV will be launching on Friday, November 29, in four sizes; 43”, 50”, 55” and 65”. Roku's licensing program has already been in effect in the US for a while, but the UK is the first European market to have the rollout.

"We are incredibly proud to launch the first Roku TV models in the UK," said Arun Bhatoye, senior marketing manager at Hisense . "These new 4K TVs offer stellar picture quality, endless entertainment and an affordable price. We are now just days away from the UK’s first Roku TV models to hit the stands; it’s a very exciting time.”

If you're wondering just how affordable the tellies are, the pricing has also been detailed at last:

  • 43” 4K HDR Hisense Roku TV – £329
  • 50” 4K HDR Hisense Roku TV – £379
  • 55” 4K HDR Hisense Roku TV – £479
  • 65” 4K HDR Hisense Roku TV – £649
