I'm A Celeb's Stanley Johnson checked up on Rebekah Vardy over Coleen Rooney row

22 Nov 2019, 14:38

Stanley Johnson reached out to former I’m A Celebrity campmate Rebekah Vardy over her feud with Coleen Rooney.

Boris Johnson’s dad and Jamie Vardy’s wife became close back in 2017 when they headed to the Australian jungle together for the ITV series.

And, during a stint on Loose Women, the 79-year-old explained he sent her a sweet message to make sure she was okay, after Wayne Rooney’s wife shared a bombshell post accusing her of leaking stories to the press – something she firmly denied.

Speaking to the panel about the drama, he said: ‘I sent her a WhatsApp, she sent me such a nice WhatsApp back.

‘I said to Becky, “Look, I don’t at all understand, but I just want you to know that we’re all rooting for you”.

‘This lady is about to have a baby, and that baby is going to be a wonderful thing for Becky to have.’

When asked whether the mum-of-four replied, Stanley continued: ‘[She said], “Thank you so much for your lovely, lovely message.”’

Stanley also let slip that the I’m A Celeb class of 2017 are in a group chat together – and we desperately want in.

‘There is actually a WhatsApp group,’ he added. ‘And I don’t want to give away state secrets, but it’s called the Bush Tucker group’.

Becky has had a tough few months, after finding herself engaged in an almighty feud with Coleen Rooney that basically broke the internet.

It all kicked off when the mum-of-four shared a bombshell post on Instagram last month, accusing her pal of leaking stories about her to the press.

In Coleen’s upload, which quickly went viral, she detailed how she planted fake posts on her private social media page – which were only viewable by Becky – in a bid to find out who had been selling the information.

However, Becky fiercely denied she was the person behind the leak, and hired a forensic expert to get to the bottom of the accusations.

Loose Women continues on weekdays, at 12.30, on ITV.
