O'Neills launch new Derry Girls jersey - and it's bound to be a huge hit with fans

Fans will be delighted

22 Nov 2019, 14:47 by Edel Hughes

O'Neills have launched a new Derry Girls jersey and it's bound to be a huge hit with fans.

The sports manufacturer launched a range of five GAA jerseys featuring images and slogans from the hit series, including "I'm a Derry Girl", "Sweet suffering Jehovah" and "Catch yerself on" earlier this year.

The jerseys have been a bestseller for the brand since being launched in August.

The company also saved jobs when they took over Derry's last shirt-making factory to manufacture the clothing. five jerseys featuring images and slogans from the hit series, including "I'm a Derry Girl", "Sweet suffering Jehovah" and "Catch yerself on".

Four months after the runaway success of the collection, O’Neills have released the new addition featuring that memorable quip, ‘Being a Derry Girl is a state of mind’.

The new Derry Girls top featuring a pink and marl grey colour way, is likely to prove just as popular with fans and it’s available in both adult and kids’ sizes.

Managing Director of O'Neills, Kieran Kennedy said:“The level of demand has been remarkable to date.

"We’re delighted that Derry Girls’ fans at home and abroad have also taken the O’Neills jerseys to their hearts, especially when they are made in Derry, sustaining local employment and keeping the city’s centuries old stitching tradition alive and well.”
