Police say no foul play suspected in dead body last week in Fort Myers

22 Nov 2019, 14:50 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Police have identified the man found dead in a Fort Myers field last week.

According to Fort Myers Police, the body was found November 13th in a field near Palm Avenue and Metro Parkway.

They now say he was 51-year-old Curtis Thomas.

There is no suspicion of foul play and next of kin have been notified.
