Harry Styles reveals why he stayed away from drugs while he was in One Direction

The singer has since dabbled in hallucinogenics

22 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Kendra Becker

Harry Styles has revealed that he stayed away from drugs during his time in One Direction, because he didn’t want to “f*** up” their success.

Back in August, the 25-year-old admitted that he took magic mushrooms while he was recording his new album, Fine Line.

During an interview with Zane Lowe on his Apple Music radio show, the singer said he decided to experiment with drugs once he became a solo artist, because he was too afraid to before.

“When I was in the band, it was like, to me it felt like it was so much bigger than any of us that I kind of felt like, I’m not going to be the one who f**** it up,” he confessed.
