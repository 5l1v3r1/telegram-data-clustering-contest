UPSC releases results for various recruitment exams, check direct link

UPSC recruitment exam results 2019: The candidates can check the results through the website- upsc.gov.in

22 Nov 2019, 14:32 by Careers Desk

UPSC recruitment exam results 2019: The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) released the results for recruitment examinations for the posts of Assistant Hydro geologist in Central Ground Water Board, and Medical Officer in Department of Health and Family Welfare. The candidates can check the results through the website- upsc.gov.in.

The commission earlier advertised for the 50 vacancies for the posts of Assistant Hydro geologist in Central Ground Water Board, and 327 vacancies for the posts of Medical Officer.

UPSC written exam results 2019: How to check

Step 1: Visit the official website- upsc.gov.in

Step 2: Click on the download result link for relevant posts

Step 3: A pdf file with roll number of the selected candidates will appear on screen

Step 4: Download it, and take a print out for further reference.

The commission will release the marks of the unsuccessful candidates after the conclusion of the recruitment process. “The marks of candidates who have not been shortlisted in the recruitment test and the category-wise cut off marks etc. will be uploaded on the commission’s website within 30 days from the publication of final result or completion of the recruitment process, whichever is later,” read the official notification.
