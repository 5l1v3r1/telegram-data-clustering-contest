Amazon's Fitbit Charge 3 tracker for £75 is a great Black Friday 2019 deal

22 Nov 2019, 14:43

Anyone looking to get their hands on a discounted Fitbit activity tracker for Black Friday may have had their prayers answered.

Black Friday itself may be a whole week away, but Amazon has already got into the sales spirit.

The online retailer is offering the Fitbit Charge 3 with over £50 knocked off the price for a limited time only. The deal brings the price down from £130 to £75 but is only running for the next nine hours (at time of writing).

The Charge 3 is the best of the business when it comes to Fitbit’s wristband line-up.

It’s waterproof up to 50 meters and lets you track your swimming as you would a run or a cycle. It’s also got a bigger battery which (Fitbit says) will net you up to seven days of life on a single charge. Last year’s Charge 2 could only manage five.

There’s also a (slightly) refined design with an OLED screen that’s nearly 40% lighter and brighter than the Charge 2. It’s also now made from aluminium to make it lighter but Fitbit has kept the interchangeable straps so you can add a bit of personalisation.

The Charge 3 has got added notifications when paired with your phone. You’ll be able to see alerts from select apps, calls, calendar reminders and texts.

Fitbit itself has also been in the news recently after agreeing to be acquired by Google.

So if you want to get a head start on any New Year fitness ambitions, this is a deal well worth taking advantage of.
