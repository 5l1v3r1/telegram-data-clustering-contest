5 outfits that are perfect for any Christmas party

Time to shine

22 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Marguerite Kiely

We love the festive season. What other time of the year do you get to dress up like a Christmas tree, top to toe in sequins and sparkles?

As much as we enjoy the parties, wondering what to wear can give you a headache. With that in mind, we have selected five stylish sparkley outfits that will turn heads and take the pain out of party wear. You can thank us later.

The maxi dress

Want full on glam? A maxi is the way to go. A full length dress will create a dramatic look and there are loads on the high street to choose from this season. If you really want to knock it out of the park add some sequins and all eyes are guaranteed to be on you. We adore this sleeveless oversized midi dress from Zara.  You can get it here.

The midi dress

The rise of midi was a major trend this year and shows no sign of slowing down. Pleats were another big story over the past twelve months and when you add the two together you've got a look that's seriously stylish and up to date. This black, sequined midi is effortlessly glam, buy it here.

Winter white

There is nothing we like more than wearing white in winter. It instantly adds elegance to any outfit. This sequin, balloon sleeved top is seriously sophisticated and would look fantastic paired with jeans. Alternatively,  double down on the sparkles and team it with a white sequined skirt. It's available in River Island, here.

Gothic Queen

The LBD is always in style but why not do it with a twist this Christmas? The autumn has seen an influx of black lace, tulle, and sheer fabrics on the high street. We are on board with the gothic glam trend and this limited edition ruffled, textured dress from Zara would be perfect for any Christmas party. it's selling out fast though, so you will need to be quick! Buy it here.  

Day to night

Most of us will be dashing straight from the office to the bar this festive season so will need an outfit that will take us from day to night. A little bit of sparkle paired with some super sexy leather pants or your favourite pair of jeans should do the trick. This River Island green embellished top has you covered. You can pick it up here.
