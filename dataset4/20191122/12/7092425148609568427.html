A pasta dish will be available for free next week in one Dublin restaurant

22 Nov 2019, 12:57 by Nick Fitzgerald

Sprezzatura are giving away their coeliac friendly Buckwheat Fettuccine with Pesto dish all day for absolutely nothing on Monday, November 25

To mark their new gluten free addition to their menu, Sprezzatura are giving away a coeliac-friendly dish for absolutely nothing all day on Monday, November 25.

And the dish on offer is none other than Buckwheat Fettuccine with Pesto. As seen below, it looks simply delish.

Located at 5/6 Camden Market on Grantham Street, walk-ins will be welcomed throughout the day but bookings can also be made through the website sprezzatura.ie with payment taken by card only.

Going forward, the Italian restaurant will be featuring one gluten-free dish on its menu each week.

All thanks to head chef Alexandre Braz, the focus will be on great flavours and seasonal produce.

This is a dish surely not to miss for all you pasta lovers out there. See you on Monday!
