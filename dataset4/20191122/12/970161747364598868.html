Eamonn Holmes channels Elvis in front of Priscilla Presley to middling results

22 Nov 2019, 12:44

Eamonn Holmes made a childhood dream come true on This Morning as he dusted off his best Elvis impression…in front of the actual Priscilla Presley.

Safe to say, Ruth Langsford was absolutely mortified and couldn’t stop apologising to the American icon.

Crossing from the other side of the sofa, the 59 year-old turned up his collars (quite literally), sat himself right next to the actual wife of the actual Elvis Presley…and gave a little rendition of Suspicious Minds.

Eamonn looked like he was a having blast, Ruth looked like she wanted the ground to swallow her up and Priscilla looked like she wasn’t sure if she was in some kind of twisted dream.

‘I’m so sorry,’ the presenter’s long-suffering wife said as she dried her eyes. ‘Oh my goodness. I’m so sorry Priscilla.’

Although ITV’s newest Elvis impersonator didn’t stop, looking down both cameras and giving a show for the ages.

‘Security!’ the Loose Women anchor said finally. ‘Please take this man away.’

Priscilla herself just had one piece of advice for Ruth: ‘Help him.’

Oh, trust us, Priscilla. We’re sure she’s tried.

The married couple were also joined on the sofa by The X Factor: Celebrity contestant Vinnie Jones, who discussed the one surprise his late wife Tanya arranged for her family before her death.

‘She planned [something for] New Year’s Eve,’ Vinnie revealed to the presenting duo. ‘I’m flying everybody over to Los Angeles, I have a house in Palm Springs.

‘I’m bringing everyone there to celebrate our 25th anniversary, she wanted to put it on. She made all the arrangements. We have to fulfill them.’

Vinnie was also embroiled in one of the most controversial X Factor moments in years last weekend after he attempted to quit the competition after finding himself in the sing-off.

In the bottom two against Glee’s Kevin McHale, the Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels star pleaded with Simon Cowell to allow him to exit the competition for McHale to continue.

Unfortunately, the music mogul was having none of it and McHale was eliminated.

Despite the controversy, Vinnie has confirmed that he will be returning to the stage this weekend.
