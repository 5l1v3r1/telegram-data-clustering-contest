Britain's Got Talent auditions taking place at The Galleries in Bristol

Reckon you've got what it takes? Here's how to audition

22 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Robin Murray

People in Bristol will have the chance to show off their talents next month at auditions for Britain's Got Talent.

The televised talent show, hosted by Ant and Dec, launched in 2007 and is now on the lookout for entrants for its fourteenth series.

Previous winners of the hit show, which is the UK's biggest television talent competition with viewing figures of 9.9 million viewers per series, include Colin Thackery, Spellbound and Paul Potts.

Among the show’s successes, Susan Boyle has sold more than 25 million records worldwide, Diversity are about to embark on their 10 year anniversary tour and multi-platinum selling artist Paul Potts has sold six million records worldwide.

Could you be the next success story? There's only one way to find out.

On December 15, hopefuls for the ITV competition from Bristol will be able to audition at an event held at The Galleries.

Executive producer Charlie Irwin said: "Britain’s Got Talent 2019 was another fantastic series showcasing the best talent and variety from all over the UK.

"Colin Thackery triumphed, becoming the oldest ever winner of BGT proving the point - whatever age you are, or what talent you have - anything is possible. 

"Whether you’re an acrobat, comedian, a dancer or even a puppet - we can’t wait to see what talent the public will bring in 2020.

"Anyone can apply - there is no age limit and we are looking for all kinds of talents. So, if you feel you have something special to show us, no matter what it is, then make sure you apply, you never know where it might take you!"

How to audition

Auditions will be taking place at The Galleries on Sunday, December 15 between 11am – 5pm. 

All potential hopefuls need to do is attend the shopping centre on the day and audition.

Successful acts will then be in with a chance of securing a place at one of the judges’ auditions in 2020.

The show is open to any performer of any age, with any talent - all you need is a skill and star quality which you think will impress.
