West Bengal Police Constable PET/ PMT admit card released, check schedule

WB Police Constable PET, PMT admit card 2019: The candidates can download the admit card through the website- policewb.gov.in. The recruitment examination will be conducted from December 3 to January 7, 2020

22 Nov 2019, 12:53 by Careers Desk

WB Police Constable PET, PMT admit card 2019: The West Bengal Police Recruitment Board (WBPRB) has released the admit card for the Physical Measurement Test (PMT)/ Physical Efficiency Test (PET) for the posts of Constable on Friday, November 22, 2019. The candidates can download the admit card through the website- policewb.gov.in.

The recruitment examinations for Physical Measurement Test (PMT), and Physical Efficiency Test (PET) is scheduled to be conducted from December 3 to January 7, 2020.

West Bengal Police Constable PET, PMT admit card: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website- policewb.gov.in

Step 2: Click on the ‘download admit card’ link

Step 3: Enter registration number/ roll number

Step 4: Admit card will appear on screen ‘

Step 5: Download it, and take a print out for further reference.

WB Police Constable PET/ PMT admit card 2019: Check zone wise schedule

Murshidabad Range Recruitment Board: December 3 to 28, 2019

Jalpaiguri Range Recruitment Board: December 3 to 20, 2019

Mednipur Range Recruitment Board: December 3 to 26, 2019

Burdwan Range Recruitment Board: December 3 to 20, 2019

Presidency Range Recruitment Board-I: December 26 to January 7, 2020

Presidency Range Recruitment Board-II: December 26 to January 7, 2020.

Earlier, the results of the preliminary examinations for constable post was released on November 19, 2019. Candidates who clear all the recruitment rounds will be recruited at a monthly remuneration of Rs 5,400 to Rs 25,200 with grade pay of Rs 2,600.
