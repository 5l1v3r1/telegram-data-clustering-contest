This father mimicked his daughter’s ‘milk-drunk’ expressions and it has gone viral

Comedian Josh Hawkins from Sydney recently became a father and decided to record all his daughter's expressions. He then created a spoof in which he imitated all her 'milk-drunk' expressions and the video is now breaking the internet.

22 Nov 2019, 12:58 by Trends Desk

One father decided to imitate the many moods of his newborn daughter and the compilation of his various expressions has gone viral. Comedian Josh Hawkins from Sydney in Australia recently became a father and taking care of daughter Billie is now his full-time job. At some point, he decided to imitate his daughter’s adorable ‘milk drunk faces’.

“Copying my daughter’s milk drunk faces is my new full time job”, the pastor-turned-comedian wrote while sharing the hilarious videos. The videos have taken social media by storm, and have been viewed nearly 50 million times on his Facebook page alone. Hawkins even wore headbands and bibs to match those his daughter was wearing while making the expression.
