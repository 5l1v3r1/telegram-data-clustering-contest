Duterte sets trip to Busan for summit

22 Nov 2019, 12:25 by Cristina Lee-Pisco

THE Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA) yesterday confirmed that President Rodrigo Duterte is attending the two-day Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN)–Republic of Korea (ROK) Commemorative Summit and other side events next week in Busan, South Korea.

Duterte and the other ASEAN leaders will discuss with South Korean President Moon Jae-in issues and cooperation of mutual concern.

“The ASEAN-ROK Commemorative Summit will be attended by heads of governments or states of ASEAN member-states and the Republic of Korea’s President Moon Jae-in,” DFA Assistant Secretary for ASEAN Office Affairs Junever Mahilum-West said.

Duterte is expected to discuss regional security issues, including the Korean Peninsula.

He will be accompanied by Foreign Secretary Teodoro Locsin Jr., Trade Secretary Ramon Lopez, and Social Welfare Secretary Rolando Bautista and other members of the Cabinet.

This is the second time that the President is going to South Korea, the first was in June 2018.
