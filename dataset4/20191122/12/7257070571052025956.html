Didal na-‘torture’ sa seag training

22 Nov 2019, 12:17

MAY mga bagong trick si skateboarder Margielyn Didal, umaasa siyang ginto ang ibibigay nito sa kanya sa 30th SEA Games.

“I’ve also got some new tricks prepared but will stick to the game plan,” ani Didal.

“I’m always prepared and ready to compete, no matter where it is. That’s why I recently spent time in Rio de Janeiro/Los Angeles to get the best place training possible.”

Naging malaking puntos naman para sa Cebuana-skater ang maging parte ng Red Bull na nagsilbing training niya bago sumabak sa paparating na biennial event.

Aminado siya na mahirap ang pinagdaanan niya sa Red Bull High Performance Training Center dahil natamo niya ang ‘very severe ankle sprain’ na naka-apekto sa kanyang training.

“The best part about them (Red Bull) is when they would torture me during rehab,” pahayag ni Didal.

“But really, they have the best team, therapist and equipment. They helped so much – especially with my ankle concerns and other key areas. Now we’re focusing on a lot of ways to improve my skills.” (Aivan Episcope)
