Billie Eilish Hilariously Interviews Kids About Their Sleeping Rituals - Watch!

22 Nov 2019, 12:30

Billie Eilish is asking kids where they believe we go when we sleep!

The 17-year-old “bad guy” crooner recently stopped by Jimmy Kimmel Live for the segment on Thursday (November 21) in Los Angeles.

Inspired by her album When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?, Billie interviewed the kids about their dreams and nighttime rituals.

“You can pretty much go anywhere,” one boy said, naming the desert, India, the old west, and “the mummy times” as examples.

One boy said that when his parents go to sleep, “They just stay in their bed and sometimes when they just want to take off their pajama shirts they just…” He then pretended to take his shirt off.
