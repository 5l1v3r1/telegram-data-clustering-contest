Michelle Visage was lined up for EastEnders: 'I believe I am Kat's cousin'

22 Nov 2019, 12:31

Michelle Visage has revealed she was once asked to join EastEnders for a guest role and suddenly we’ve never wanted anything more.

The Drag Race superstar – who finally cleared up her supposed ‘feud’ with Cheryl this week – is an icon wherever she steps foot and would no doubt go down a storm with the residents of Walford.

Could Michelle be Kat Moon’s long lost American twin? Easily, and the Strictly Come Dancing star revealed her cameo was agonisingly close to happening until former showrunner Dominic Treadwell-Collins stepped down as EastEnders boss.

However, the offer is still on the table and she’s ready for her soap debut.

She told Good Housekeeping magazine that she prefers EastEnders to Coronation Street because ‘I believe I am Kat [Moon’s] American cousin.’

‘I’m still vying for that guest role,’ she teased.

Naturally, Jessie Wallace is totally on board too and tweeted: ‘I believe this too! @michellevisage.’
