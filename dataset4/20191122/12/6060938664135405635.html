Photos of the day: From Salman Khan celebrating Helen’s birthday to Kartik Aaryan promoting ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday, here’s what made headlines

22 Nov 2019, 12:26 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Woh’ Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday, here’s what made headlines It was an eventful day today as B-town was abuzz with birthday celebrations and film promotions. Many Bollywood celebrities were also all out and about in the city. From veteran actress Helen’s birthday bash at the Khan residency to Kartik Aaryan promoting his film, ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ with co-stars Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday on his birthday, here are pictures that grabbed headlines today.

Helen rings in her 80th birthday with family
Veteran actress Helen rang in her 80th birthday with her family. Everyone from Salman Khan, Sohail Khan, Alvira and Arpita joined in the celebrations as the birthday girl cut into her elegant two-tiered cake with a message that read, ‘Amazing At 80’.

Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar promotes film in a college
Kartik Aaryan, who turned a year older today, was seen promoting his upcoming film, ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ on his special day. Along with his co-stars Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar, Kartik was seen entertaining college students in the city. He was also seen celebrating his birthday there by cutting a cake.

Airport spotting
Airports have become a common place for celebrity spotting and one can always see paparazzi stationed right outside to click pictures of them. Recently to get papped at the airport was none other than Karan Johar and Hrithik Roshan.

Out and about in the city
B-town celebrities were all out and about in the city today. While Varun Dhawan and Shraddha Kapoor were seen outside a dubbing studio in the city, Shanaya Kapoor was snapped in Bandra .

Paparazzi also spotted the new love-birds Pulkit Samrat and Kriti Kharbanda goofing around. Kriti Sanon was also snapped by the shutterbugs with her gym trainer.

Anushka Sharma visits Manish Malhotra at his residence

After Priyanka Chopra, Anushka Sharma too was snapped at his residence. Dressed in a white traditional attire, the actress was seen coming out of his residence today. With her hair tied into a messy bun and matching juttis, she looked pretty as a peach.
