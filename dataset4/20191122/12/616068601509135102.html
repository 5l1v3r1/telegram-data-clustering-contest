SUV, truck crash at Ethel

22 Nov 2019, 12:40 by Wayne Moore

UPDATE: 12:40 p.m.

Castanet reporter Nich Johansen says one person inside a lifted Chevrolet pickup has been loaded into an ambulance and taken to Kelowna General Hospital.

The condition of the person taken to hospital is not known.

Two ambulances, two fire trucks and several RCMP are on the scene of the collision at Harvey Avenue and Ethel Street.

The crash involved the pickup and a silver Acura MDX SUV.

Traffic is not moving along Ethel across the highway in either direction. Harvey is down to just a single lane northbound.

No indication as to the the cause of the collision.

ORIGINAL: 12:30 p.m.

A two-vehicle crash at Ethel Street and Harvey Avenue in Kelowna has slowed traffic moving in all directions.

Numerous emergency vehicles are on the scene of the crash, effectively blocking traffic in both directions on Ethel. Northbound traffic on Harvey is also being impacted.

The crash, shortly before 12:30 p.m., involved a silver SUV and a dark-coloured pickup.

The SUV suffered extensive front-end damage.

No word on the extent of any injuries or a cause.
