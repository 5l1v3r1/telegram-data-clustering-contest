Emmerdale's Matthew Wolfenden hits back at troll who accuses him of being a bad actor

He was forced to defend himself...

22 Nov 2019, 12:56 by Helen Fear

Matthew Wolfenden has felt the need to defend himself, after the Emmerdale star was trolled by a follower who accused him of being a bad actor.

On Friday morning (November 22), Matthew - who portrays David Metcalfe in the ITV soap - lashed out at a Twitter user who attacked him personally for no reason.

David was in a playful mood when one Twitter user asked him, in response to an Emmerdale storyline: "Don't people in soap land practice safe sex?"

It's kinda hurtful and, to be fair, needn't be posted on here.

Matthew, ever the joker, replied: "YES! If we 'do it' outside, we always wear high vis jackets. If we 'do it' on a top bunk, we always make sure the sides are up and finally if we 'do it' on a building site, we always make sure we have a hat hard on."

But one follower was displeased, replying: "Your comments are as bad as your acting!"
