Colors to extend ‘Bigg Boss 13’ by another 3-4 weeks?

22 Nov 2019, 12:30 by BizAsia Correspondent

Popular reality show ‘Bigg Boss 13’ is likely to get an extension of 3-4 weeks by broadcaster, Colors, according to reports.

SpotBoye reports that the show, which was expected to end in mid-January 2020, is now likely to end in February 2020. The current run of the show has sparked a number of arguments, leading to coverage in the media around the world.

‘Bigg Boss 13’ has also entered the Top 10 most watched shows of the week in latest TV ratings in India. The last couple of weeks of ‘Bigg Boss 13’ have been much talked about with Siddharth Shukla and Asim Riaz, being the centre of much debate in the house.

At the time of filing this article, BizAsiaLive.com was awaiting a response from Colors.
