Marks & Spencer releases cheese advent calendar – and it's on sale now for just £15!

And there's a cheesy Santa for Christmas Day!

22 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Nancy Brown

If you're a savoury person, advent calendars have probably been pretty much off limits. Up until now, that is.

Marks & Spencer has just unveiled a mouth-watering cheese advent calendar.

And the best news is it's available in store now for just £15!

The Collection Cheese Advent Calendar comes with 24 mini bars of the brand's best cheddar, made up of a variety of M&S Medium Cove Cheddar, M&S Mature Cove Cheddar and its award-winning M&S Cornish Cruncher Cheddar.

Not to spoil the surprise come December 25, but there's even a cheese Santa to enjoy on Christmas Day.

The calendar, of course, comes with cheesy jokes and facts to help you brush up on your knowledge of all things cheese.
