Nina Dobrev Named Dior Beauty's New Brand Partner

22 Nov 2019, 12:41

Nina Dobrev is opening up about her love for Dior Beauty, just after she was named the beauty brand’s newest ambassador.

“I always looked up to Dior. It felt so timeless and classy and elegant in every way,” the 30-year-old actress shared with Harper’s Bazaar. “I think the Miss Dior scent is what I always associated with that grace and timelessness. That was probably the first product I recognized, purchased, and aspired to as a little girl.”

Nina also opened up about her fave beauty items in the brand – she loves the “999″ Rouge Dior Ultra Care Lipstick, and Forever Foundation – and what she’s looking forward to in the coming year.

“2019 was a whirlwind it had a lot of great moments and some tough moments and that’s all part of being a human,” she says. “I am looking forward to a fresh start and fresh slate, new year. I set some new intentions and start this partnership that I am super excited about with Dior and see what the future has in store.”
