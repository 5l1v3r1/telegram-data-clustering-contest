Camila Cabello will bring The Romance Tour to Resorts World Arena Birmingham in 2020 - here’s how to get tickets

The Señorita singer will light up Resorts World Arena on Monday 1st June 2020

22 Nov 2019, 12:37 by Scott Dickinson, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/scott-dickinson/

The ex-Fifth Harmony member will make a triumphant return to Birmingham in the summer of 2020.

The highly anticipated Romance tour will ride off her upcoming album of the same title which is already expected to break record sales.

Camila’s 2020 tour will come to Birmingham Resorts World Arena on Monday 1st June 2020.

Following a four year stint with X Factor USA runners up Fifth Harmony, Cabello departed the group in 2016 to pursue her own solo projects.

Tickets for Cabello's tour will go on general sale at 9am on Friday 29th November 2019 and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

Nearly three years and three Grammy Award nominations later, Cabello now helms the crown of the most listened to female artist in the world on Spotify. She is the first female artist since Adele to triple up atop Billboard’s Hot 100, Billboard 200 & Artist 100 charts.

The first single from her debut album, "Havana," took the world by storm earning over 1.1 billion on-demand U.S streams and reaching number one on countless charts worldwide.

Camila followed her RIAA platinum-certified album, CAMILA, with a Spanish influenced tune with beau Shawn Mendes. Señorita has reached number one in 28 countries in Europe and remains the most streamed song of any song released on Spotify this year globally.

We can expect her chart topping songs such as “Shameless,” “Liar,” “Cry For Me,” and “Easy” to feature in the show and her sophomore album, "Romance."

Fans will be able to access presale tickets to her tour on 26th November 2019 when pre-ordering the upcoming record which releases 6th December 2019.
