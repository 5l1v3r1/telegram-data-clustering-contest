I’m A Celebrity fans demand ITV launches new series with ordinary people

Should there be two versions of the show?

22 Nov 2019, 12:52 by Rebecca Carter

Fans of I'm A Celebrity are demanding another series but with ordinary people rather than famous faces.

The show, hosted by Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly, currently sees 12 celebrities go into the jungle and try to survive three weeks Down Under.

But viewers are now calling for ITV to launch a whole new series featuring non-famous people as they think it would be an instant hit.

One person said on Twitter: "I'm definitely backing an #imacelebrity show but for normal people, I so wanna goooo."

Another wrote: "They should really do I'm NOT A celebrity.. Get Me Out Of Here! Let us normal none-celebrities have a go!!"

A third tweeted: "Petition to do a I’m A Celeb but with normal people and call it I’m Not A Celeb? I think I’m onto to something here, Dragon Den hello I’d like to make a suggestion."
