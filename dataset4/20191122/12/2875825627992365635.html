Former I'm A Celebrity winner Toff slams Jacqueline and Nadine for 'pulling out'

Toff, 25, branded the girls 'naughty' after they refused to put their names in the hat to be sent to I'm A Celeb's Jungle Jailhouse

22 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Irishmirror.ie

Former I'm A Celebrity winner Georgia 'Toff' Toffolo has hit out at Jacqueline Jossa and Nadine Coyle for sparking the first camp fight.

The Made in Chelsea star, 25, branded the girls "naughty" after they refused to put their names in the hat to be sent to the Jungle Jailhouse.

Speaking on This Morning, Toff said that she had been "shouting at the TV" over the awkward moment.

After achieving eight stars in their Bushtucker Trial, new campmates Cliff Parisi and Andy Whyment became Outlaws and were sent to the Jungle Jailhouse.

The main camp had the opportunity to be "good, bad or downright ugly" - and either keep all the food themselves, or choose to feed the outlaws with their share of food.

The twist with the second option was that two campmates would have to join the outlaws in the Jailhouse - but tempers flared over a difference in opinion.

When former footballer Ian suggested that they draw straws, they all picked bottles out named water bottles to see who would go.

However Jacqueline and Nadine both refused to go, prompting discord in camp.

"As a former campmate I think my judgement might be a bit different to everyone," Toff said. "I thought it was really naughty of them!

"Because you work as a team in there and I know it sounds so silly, but your day revolves around working together.

However host Ruth said that she loved their "honesty".

"It is, but I'm just not sure about that," Toff said.
