Man arrested over alleged broad daylight rape in Dundee released without charge

22 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Aidan Smith

A MAN arrested over an alleged rape in a Dundee flat has been released without charge.

Tayside cops yesterday confirmed that they had arrested a 31-year-old man in connection with the alleged incident.

But they now say he has been released without charge.

They are continuing their enquiries.

The alleged rape is said to have taken place in Balunie Crescent in Dundee.

A Police Scotland statement read: "Officers in Dundee can confirm that a 31-year-old man arrested yesterday in connection with a serious sexual assault which happened in Balunie Crescent on Saturday, 19, October has been released without charge.

"Enquiries are continuing."

Cops had previously called on the public to come forward with any information as they launched a probe.

A dedicated team of both uniformed and detective officers were called in for the investigation.
