Jose Mourinho wants Adrien Rabiot at Tottenham Hotspur?

New Tottenham Hotspur boss Jose Mourinho is reportedly eyeing a move for Juventus midfielder Adrien Rabiot.

22 Nov 2019, 12:32 by Matt Law

The 24-year-old joined Juve on a free transfer over the summer after running down his contract with Paris Saint-Germain.

The Old Lady are believed to have beaten a number of English clubs - including Tottenham - to the signature of the Frenchman on a contract until the summer of 2023.

Rabiot has struggled to earn a starting role for Maurizio Sarri's side this season, though, and has completed the 90 minutes in just one of their 12 league matches to date.

According to Calciomercato, Mourinho is an admirer of the midfielder, and the new Spurs manager wants his club to make a move in the January transfer window.

Rabiot is yet to score or provide an assist in nine outings for Juve this season.
