Some of my favourite cricket memories have come at Eden Gardens: Harbhajan Singh

22 Nov 2019, 12:51 by ANI

KOLKATA: India spinner Harbhajan Singh on Friday said that some of his favourite cricketing memories have come at the Eden Gardens and it was unbelievable to relive them on the same turf.

Harbhajan is present at the Eden Gardens to witness India's first-ever day-night Test against Bangladesh.

The spinner was seen having a chat with former India cricketers VVS Laxman, Sachin Tendulkar, and Anil Kumble during the lunch break and all four relived some of their greatest cricketing memories.

"Some of my favourite cricket memories have come at the Eden Gardens - Today, to relive them on the same turf was unbelievable .. @sachin_rt @VVSLaxman281 @anilkumble1074 thanks @StarSportsIndia and our very own @jatinsapru," Harbhajan tweeted.
