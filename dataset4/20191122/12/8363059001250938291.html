BT Sport Ultimate Raises the Bar on Live Telly Once More

22 Nov 2019, 12:40 by Shabana Arif

BT Sport Ultimate, formerly known as the BT Sport 4K UHD channel, is bringing live matches to you in 4K HDR for the first time in Europe.

The channel always seems to be announcing sports-related 4K UHD HDR blah blah updates, with the last one being the live broadcasting of the footie in HDR back in August. This week's announcement sees the channel making its "biggest live matches" available in 4K and HDR on more devices as of today, including Samsung smart TVs, Xbox One consoles, and Chromecast Ultra streaming devices.

That's good news for those of you hotly anticipating upcoming events like the UEFA Champions League matches featuring Tottenham Hotspur v Olympiacos on Tuesday, November 26 at 8pm, and Liverpool v Napoli on Wednesday, November 27 at 8pm.
