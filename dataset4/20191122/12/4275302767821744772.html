Man released without charge over serious sexual assault in Dundee

A 31-year old man who was arrested yesterday in relation to a serious sexual assault in Balunie Crescent, Dundee, has been released without charge.

22 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Scott Milne

The incident occurred on October 19. Police were previously criticised for keeping local residents in the dark over the nature of the assault.

A force spokesman said: “Enquiries are continuing.”
