I'm A Celebrity's Jacqueline has terrifying encounter in bed with giant snake

22 Nov 2019, 10:46

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! Champ Jacqueline Jossa has the fright of her life tonight when she lifts up the bed covers to find a humongous python chilling on a pillow during tonight’s Bushtucker trial.

The former EastEnders star came under fire from viewers and comedian Andrew Maxwell – who also clashed with Ian Wright over crocodile feet, obviously – last night after she refused to take part in a draw to be banished to the Jailhouse.

However, tonight’s she more than proves she’s got the prowess to deliver as she battles through a chamber of serpents to win food for camp, while Rak-Su singer Myles Stephenson is locked in a box with some snakes of his own.

Will the camp go hungry tonight or will Jacqueline silence her doubters with a full bag of stars for camp?

The 27-year-old broke down in tears last night after admitting she was homesick. The actress has left behind her two daughters Ella, four, and Mia, one, with husband Dan Osborne.

I just woke up feeling a bit like “mmmm” when you’re just looking up at pure Jungle and you think, “Wow, I’m so far away from home,”’ she sobbed.

‘The thing is, everyone is missing their families, I just feel like it’s too soon to be crying missing your family…and we all smell so bad, we just stink, I stink, the smelliest I’ve ever been in my life.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
