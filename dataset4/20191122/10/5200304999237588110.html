BARC India’s Jagdeep Dighe passes away

22 Nov 2019, 10:26 by BizAsia Correspondent

Jagdeep Dighe, Senior vice president, Marcom and Business Development Sales at BARC India, has passed away.

Dighe, 54, was associated with BARC India since its inception. Prior to his work at BARC India, Dighe was Senior Vice President of Client Servicing with Sony Pictures Networks India for more than 13 years. He also worked at ZEE TV and The Indian Express.

BizAsiaLive.com extends its condolences to Dighe’s family and friends.
