When is the Euro 2020 playoff draw? Live stream and start time details

Scotland will discover who stands in their way as they hope to reach their first major tournament since 1998.

22 Nov 2019, 10:40 by Chris Doyle, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/chris-doyle/

Scotland are potentially 180 minutes away from reaching Euro 2020 and their first major tournament since 1998.

Steve Clarke 's reign started off slow, but there are signs of progress after two wins against Cyprus and Kazakhstan. At least, it's something build on as they look ahead to the playoffs in March.

Scotland already know that they will face either Bulgaria, Hungary, Romania or Israel in the semi-final and if they get through that, they face a stiff task of going up against Norway or Serbia.

There's only a few permutations but fans will be eager to know how it will all play out.

Here's all the details you need to know ahead of the draw.

When is the draw?

The draw will take place on Friday, November 22 in Nyon, Switzerland.

It will start at 11am.

How can I follow it live?

Unfortunately, the draw will not be televised in the UK.

UEFA will stream the draw on their website, allowing you to watch it live.

But make sure to stick with us as we will be following all the action as it happens with our LIVE blog on dailyrecord.co.uk .
