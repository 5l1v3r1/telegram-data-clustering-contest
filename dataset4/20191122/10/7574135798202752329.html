Decibel Philly Fest finalizes lineup with Midnight, Gatecreeper & Necrot

22 Nov 2019, 10:35 by Andrew Sacher

The lineup for the 2020 Philly edition of Decibel Metal & Beer Fest has been finalized, with Midnight, Gatecreeper, and Necrot joining the previously announced lineup of Converge (playing Jane Doe), Pig Destroyer (playing Prowler In The Yard), Napalm Death (playing Harmony Corruption and Utopia Banished), Mayhem, Satan, Abbath, Idle Hands, Night Demon, Haunt, Magic Circle, and Un.

Participating breweries include Broken Goblet Brewing (PA) (who are presenting the beer lineup), Brimming Horn Meadery (DE), Lionshead (PA), Metal Monkey (IL), Nightmare Brewing Company (NY), Cosmic Eye Brewing (NE), Zeroday Brewing Company (PA) and Soundgrowler Brewing Co. (IL), Wake Brewing (IL). Three Floyds (IN), Hammerheart (MN), Tired Hands (PA), Adroit Theory (VA), Blake’s Hard Cider (MI), Burial Beer Co. (NC), Oliver Brewing Co. (MD), Yards (PA), and Goldhorn Brewery (OH).

The fest goes down April 3-4 at The Fillmore. Tickets are still available. The fest also doubles as the Philly stop of the 2020 Decibel tour, which Mayhem and Abbath are headlining.

Gatecreeper (who just toured through Philly with Necrot), Mayhem, Abbath, Idle Hands, Haunt, and Magic Circle are also all on Decibel’s Top 40 Albums of 2019.
