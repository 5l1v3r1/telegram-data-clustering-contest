China embraces 5G with open, collaborative mentality: ZTE chairman

22 Nov 2019, 10:48 by Global Times

Photo: VCG

China embraces 5G with an open, collaborative mentality, and only by creating indigenous core competitiveness can we survive in an open environment, ZTE chairman Li Zixue said at the New Economy Forum in Beijing on Friday.

For China, 5G means a new industrial revolution, Li said, noting that the cutting-edge technology will bring transformative changes to various sectors, including information transmission and computing.

"China's telecommunications firms, equipment suppliers and customers are making preparations for the explosive application of 5G technology," he said.

"We telecommunications equipment manufacturers may cooperate with the US, but this must be based on win-win outcomes. We need competitive semiconductors to build our own competitiveness and create value for our clients," Li said.

Sanjay Mehrotra, CEO of Micro Technology, expressed his excitement regarding the opportunity of 5G, saying, "China is a very important market."
