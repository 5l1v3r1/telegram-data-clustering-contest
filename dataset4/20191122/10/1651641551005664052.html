Microsoft To Do for Android now lets you print tasks and more

22 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Rahul

Microsoft has pushed an update to its task management app, Microsoft To Do on Android. The update brings a couple of new features, bug fixes, and performance improvements.

Talking about new features, To Do app for Android will now let you print your lists of tasks, the option which can be found on the list menu. And aside from print lists, you’ll also be able to hide completed tasks.

Microsoft also improved some graphics in the app and the improvement is visible in both light and dark mode. You can see the official changelog below.

Changelog

   •  You can now print your lists! Find the option in your list menu.
   •  We’ve changed some of our graphics so that they look better with light mode & dark mode.
   •  We’ve added the option to show or hide completed tasks in your Important list.
   •  We made several smaller fixes & improvements.

Microsoft To Do app for Windows 10 recently picked up the ability to print lists. A similar feature is now available on Microsoft To Do for Android.
