R3bn Lotto Winner Buys His Favourite Club

22 Nov 2019, 10:30 by David Kappel

A lifelong fan has bought a majority share in his favourite club after winning an incredible R3 billion in the EuroMillions lottery.

Colin Weir has completed a takeover of Scottish Championship side Partick Thistle after buying 55% of the club's shares, as reported by ESPN.

The die-hard Thistle fan also announced that he would give his shares to the club's fans for free in 2020.

"(Partick Thistle supporters organisation) Thistle for Ever planted the thought of fan ownership as a real option in my mind," Weir said in a statement.
