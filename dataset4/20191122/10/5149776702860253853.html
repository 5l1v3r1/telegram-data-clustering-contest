Nottingham Forest on TV: Kick off time and TV details for Bristol City game

Nottingham Forest latest | Here's a full round-up of when you can see the Reds live on Sky Sports in the coming months

22 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Ben Husband

Nottingham Forest have regularly had the Sky Sports cameras at their matches in the Championship this season.

Reds fans will have more opportunities to watch their team on the box in the weeks to comes as we approach Christmas and New Year.

Clashes against Derby County, West Bromwich Albion, Leeds United, Stoke City and Wigan Athletic have all been selected for TV coverage so far this season, and there are more to come.

They were not included in the last Sky selections for December and early January, though.

All midweek Championship games are screened as part of a deal with the EFL which began last season.

Red button events are not available via Sky Go or the Sky Q app - but you can watch midweek Sky Bet Championship matches via the Sky Sports app.

Here are the Sky Sports matches featuring Forest:

Nottingham Forest TV fixtures

QPR v Nottingham Forest - Wednesday, 27th November (19.45)

Millwall v Nottingham Forest - Friday, December 6 (7.45pm)

2019 mid-week red button fixtures (Sky Sports)

Nottingham Forest v Middlesborough - Tuesday, 10th December (7.45pm)

Next game

Bristol City (A) November 23, 15:00

This one won't be on TV on Saturday, but you'll be able to follow all the action as it happens from our Live Blog live from Ashton Gate.

Watching Championship highlights

Where can you watch Forest highlights?

Championship highlights are shown on Quest.

It is part of a four year deal which will bring 90 minutes of match highlights, hosted by Colin Murray, at 9pm every Saturday night.

Quest are also broadcasting 10 hour-long highlights shows capturing the action from every round of midweek fixtures.

In addition, they have been presenting eight, hour-long highlights shows from the Carabao Cup and Checkatrade Trophy as part of the arrangement.

Quest is available in 27 million homes across Freeview, Freesat, Sky, Virgin Media and BT TV. With a monthly reach of 12.4 million, Quest is Discovery’s largest channel in the UK.

The EFL highlights will headline Quest’s line-up of sports events throughout the year which are delivered in conjunction with Eurosport.

Each show will feature every goal from every EFL fixture plus analysis from a selection of pundits.
