Krack: Ravi Teja, Shruti Haasan Commence Shoot For Their Action Thriller

Reportedly, the film is based on true life incidents that occurred in Telugu speaking states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

22 Nov 2019, 10:01 by Koimoi.com Team

Tollywood superstar Ravi Teja is in news following his upcoming release Disco Raja, and his next, Krack which went on floors yesterday. The veteran actor along with his co-star Shruti Haasan shot for the film’s first scene yesterday at Ramoji Film City in Hyderabad.

Krack which was launched last month with a muhurat pooja is an action thriller that has Ravi Teja as a cop. It was only last week when the makers unveiled the film’s name and first look poster of Ravi.

The action-thriller is being helmed by Gopichand Malineni. It is for the third time in their careers where the director-actor duo of Gopichand and Ravi have teamed up for a film.

The duo has earlier worked twice together for their earlier projects, 2013 released Balupu and 2010 released Don Seenu.

Apart from Ravi and Shruti, Krack also has actress Varalaxmi Sarathkumar in major roles.

Reportedly, the film is based on true life incidents that occurred in Telugu speaking states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. The Ravi Teja starrer is slated to release in summer 2020.

Talking about his upcoming release Disco Raja, the film is one of the most awaited releases in Tollywood for the year 2020. The Sci-Fi thriller is been helmed by Vi Anand and it is been produced by Ram Talluri under the banner of SRT Entertainments.

The Ravi Teja starrer has Paayal Rajput, Nabha Natesh, Tanya Hope, Bobby Simha among others in pivotal roles. The music for the Sci-Fi venture is been composed by S.Thaman.

Disco Raja will hit big screens on 24th January.
