India Vs Bangladesh: Twitter Turns Rohit Sharma's Catch Into Hilarious Memes

He dismissed Bangladesh skipper Mominul Haque in the 11th over.

22 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Akshita Jain

Bangladesh was reduced to 73 for 6 at lunch on the opening day of the Day/Night Test at Eden Gardens and Rohit Sharma’s stunning catch left Twitter obsessed. 

He dismissed Bangladesh skipper Mominul Haque in the 11th over. The ball was going towards captain Virat Kohli when Sharma jumped in and took a one-hand catch.
