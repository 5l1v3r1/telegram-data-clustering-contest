I'm A Celebrity viewers concerned for Jacqueline Jossa after she's picked for Bushtucker trial

She will try and win meals for camp in Snake Hotel

22 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity have expressed concerns for Jacqueline Jossa as she was picked to do a Bushtucker trial.

On Thursday night's episode, the former EastEnders actress was voted by the public to do tonight's trial, Snake Hotel.

And she didn't take it well when hosts Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly revealed the news to camp.
