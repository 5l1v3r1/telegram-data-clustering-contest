Stormzy releases new song with Ed Sheeran

This is definitely going to be a chart topper

22 Nov 2019, 10:59 by Kendra Becker

Stormzy has released his brand new single ‘Own It’ featuring musical heavyweights, Ed Sheeran and Burna Boy.

The rapper’s new release was accompanied by a slick music video, directed by British director Nathan James Tettey, which was shot across a plethora of London locations.

Stormzy’s forthcoming album, ‘Heavy Is The Head’ marks a hugely exciting return for the multi award-winning phenomenon.
