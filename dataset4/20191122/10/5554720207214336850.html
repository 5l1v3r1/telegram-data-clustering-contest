Siddharth Shukla evicted from Bigg Boss house? Social media abuzz with rumours as support for Asim Riaz grows

22 Nov 2019, 10:48 by JKR Staff

Social media platform Twitter is abuzz with rumours that Bigg Boss contestant Siddharth Shukla has been kicked out of the show. The microblogging site exploded after a Twitter handle, known for leaking information from inside the Colors TV’s reality show, claimed that Siddharth had faced eviction for repeatedly indulging in violence against Asim Riaz.

Twitter handle ‘Khabri’ wrote, “Makers are planning something really very big for #SiddhartShukla. High chances of his eviction in #WeekendKaVaar.” It also reported that Khesari Lal Yadav had been evicted already adding that the early eviction of the Bhojpuri star was to ensure that Salman Khan pays full attention to the fight between Siddharth and Asim.
