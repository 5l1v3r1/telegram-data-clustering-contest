P680K shabu seized from HVT

22 Nov 2019, 10:23 by Bernard Galang

ANGELES CITY – Some P680,000 worth of shabu was confiscated from a high value target during a buy-bust operation in Barangay Balibago here the other day.

Col. Joyce Patrick Sangalang, city police director here, said that the illegal drugs were seized from Basco Kamal, 31, listed as level 1 high value target in the city police’s list of suspected drug personalities.

The suspect, a resident of Quezon City, was arrested in broad daylight along MacArthur Highway by operatives from the Police Station 4, City Drug Enforcement Unit and City Mobile Force Company.

Armed with coordination document from the Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency,  the policemen used a poseur-buyer who bought a medium-sized sachet of shabu from the suspect with a marked peso bill mixed with boodle money.

Some 100 grams of shabu with street value of P680,000 were confiscated from the suspect.

Meanwhile, policemen arrested recently an American national in Barangay Malabanias here for alleged rape.
