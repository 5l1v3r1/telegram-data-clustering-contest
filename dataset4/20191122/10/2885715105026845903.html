Sunny Leone to put on her dancing shoes for Ragini MMS: Returns 2

Sunny Leone shared the teaser of the song "Hello Ji". In the video, the actor says she will add some salt to the glamorous world of AltBalaji's web series Ragini MMS: Returns season two.

22 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Entertainment Desk

Ekta Kapoor and Sunny Leone are collaborating one more time after setting the screens on fire with their first project Ragini MMS 2. Sunny will make an appearance in the second season of ALTBalaji’s erotic-horror series Ragini MMS: Returns. She has been signed for a special song titled “Hello Ji” in the series.

On Friday, Sunny shared a teaser of the song on social media. In the video, the actor says she will add some salt to the glamorous world of Ragini MMS: Returns season two. “Sunny ke bina #RaginiMMSreturnss ?? No way!! I’m coming to double up those sexy thrills! #HelloJi, care to join me? ALT aur Zee pe mein aa rahi hu to add the chamak, dhamak and lots of namak!” read the caption of the video. Sharing the same video, Ekta mentioned, “You’re in for a special treat that is – Xtremely sexy. Xtremely sensational. Xtremely seductive.”
