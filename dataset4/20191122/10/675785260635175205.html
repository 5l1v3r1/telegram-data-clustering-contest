Air Transat Black Friday deals launch early with return flights to Canada from £299pp

Air Transat's Black Friday deals are kicking off a week early with return flights to Canada in 2020 up for grabs from £299pp

22 Nov 2019, 10:32 by Julie Delahaye, https://www.facebook.com/juliedjourno

Canada is on most people's bucket lists, but it's not exactly the cheapest of destinations to get to for Brits.

Luckily, Air Transat is helping make it a little bit more budget-friendly for 2020 holidays as the airline launches its Black Friday sale a week early.

As part of the deals, there are currently return flights to Toronto from £299pp up for grabs, with departures from London Gatwick or Manchester .

If you've never been to Toronto then you may want to check out our guide to the best things to see and do while you're there!

Toronto isn't the only destination on offer.

There's good news if Vancouver has always been on your travel wishlist, as you can find return flights from £329pp from London Gatwick , and £408pp from Manchester .

Thinking of exploring Montreal instead? You can find returns from £363pp from Gatwick and £388pp from Manchester .

All of the deals are already available to book on the Air Transat website but you'll need to do so by Friday 29th November (the actual Black Friday) when the sale ends.

There's quite a generous window of travel dates to choose from too, as the deals are valid on selected dates from January to April 2020.
