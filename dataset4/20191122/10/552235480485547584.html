'Two hurt' after bus crashes into car outside school in West Lothian

Emergency services rushed to the scene of the smash on Armadale's West Main Street just after 8am.

22 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

Two people have reportedly been hurt after a bus crashed into a car outside a school in West Lothian .

Emergency services rushed to the scene of the smash on Armadale's West Main Street just after 8am on Friday November 22.

Scottish fire and rescue sent three appliances and a heavy rescue unit to collision, which took place just outside Armadale Academy.

Locals report two people have been injured. Their condition is not known at this time.

It is believed the bus was empty at the time and on the way to collect children at St Kentigern's Academy in Blackburn.

Police Scotland tweeted that the road has been closed while they deal with the crash.

They posted: "A89 closed at Armadale Police Station and along full stretch from Blackridge. This is due to a road traffic collision. Thank you for your patience."

A Scottish Fire and Rescue spokesperson said: "We were called to the scene of a collision between a bus and a car outside Armadale Academy at 8.01am.

"Thee appliances and a heavy rescue unit were mobilised."
