Real Madrid 'hope to secure £69m Milan Skriniar deal'

Real Madrid are reportedly hopeful that they will be able to sign Inter Milan defender Milan Skriniar for £69m.

22 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Matt Law

Earlier this week, the Slovakia international's agent revealed that Madrid and Barcelona were competing for the signature of his client, who is worth £86m.

However, according to reports in Spain, Los Blancos are keen to complete a deal for a figure in the region of £69m either in January or at the end of the 2019-20 campaign.

The 24-year-old was linked with moves to Manchester City and Manchester United over the summer transfer window before ultimately signing a new deal at San Siro.

The defender's contract, which runs until the end of the 2022-23 campaign, reportedly does not include a release clause despite various suggestions.

Skriniar has made 102 appearances for the Black and Blues in all competitions since arriving in 2017.
