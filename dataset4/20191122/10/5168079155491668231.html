Body of missing boy recovered in Oman

22 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Times News Service

Muscat: Rescue teams found the body of the missing boy in wadi Al Hoqain, the Public Authority of Civil Defence and Ambulance (PACDA) announced.

PACDA said in a statement: "Referring to a report of the loss of a 15-year-old child in Wadi al-Hoqain, in wilayat of Rustaq, rescue teams were able to find his body."

The child went missing on Thursday at Al Batinah south governorate after the overflowing of wadi Al Hoqain due to heavy rainfall in the wilayat.
