I’m A Celeb’s Adele Roberts reveals how she achieved two stone weight loss

"It’s never too late to change"

22 Nov 2019, 10:47 by Blathin de Paor

I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! star Adele Roberts lost two stone in the year before she entered the jungle.

She Radio One presenter had previously opened up about her lifestyle change, after she discovered her weight had risen to 12 and a half stone.

She then revealed among the secrets to her weight loss had been swopping  chocolate bars for protein bars and replaced white baps for sourdough bread.

Sharing a throwback picture of herself sitting beside a cake stand looking heavier she told her followers that one day she "woke the f**k up", writing: “Hi everybody! Past Adele just wanted to wish you a very happy #throwbackthursday

"I’ve just told her she’s going to run two marathons in the future. She’s just pissed herself laughing and gone straight to the bottom shelf on that cake stand.

"It’s never too late to change. Going to take her back to this very spot one day and recreate this photo. Just need to find that bloody t-shirt. It was my favourite. Wore it for Kate’s birthday. Thought I was being dead fancy because it’s got Nike in gold on it.

She added: "Currently telling the story of how this little cherub went from cake stands to WAKING THE F*CK UP.” [sic]

Adele also shared the ten year throwback photo challenge on Instagram before entering the jungle, showing her wearing very similar outfits, but looking visibly different.

I’m A Celebrity …Get Me Out Of Here air at 9pm on Virgin Media One.
