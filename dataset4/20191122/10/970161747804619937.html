Nicole Scherzinger slays Gay Times Honours bash amid Thom Evans romance claims

22 Nov 2019, 10:06

Nicole Scherzinger is rumoured to be dating X Factor: Celebrity star Thom Evans, after the pair were spotted looking all kinds of cosy.

But the Pussycat Dolls singer ignored all those romance rumours like a pro as she headed to the Gay Times Honours 500 bash.

The 41-year-old slayed as she rocked up to Magazine London on Thursday night, opting for an off-white, cut-out mini dress for the event.

She completed the look with all of the jewellery, and a pair of thigh-high beige boots.

And the Don’t Cha singer debuted her new hairstyle on the red carpet, scooping her hair up into a ponytail to emphasise her snazzy new fringe.

Nicole was joined on the night by her X Factor: Celebrity co-star Kevin McHale, as well as Love Island’s Megan Barton Hanson, Munroe Bergdorf and Vanessa White.

Her appearance comes after Thom Evans broke his silence on rumours they are secretly dating – after they were seen together on a romantic night out.

During a stint on Loose Women alongside his Try Star bandmates, the rugby player did absolutely nothing to discourage the rumour mill.

In fact, the 34-year-old got very flustered when questioned about their ‘relationship’.

‘Nicole is lovely and you know, I… But I’ve been concentrating on the bromance… And getting to the final,’ the 34-year-old told the panel who couldn’t contain their giggles over his denial.

‘Who wouldn’t want to date Nicole? She’s lovely and, ermmmm…’

Smooth, hun.

An insider recently claimed Thom was completely smitten with Nicole, and plucked up the courage to ask her out this week.

‘Thom is completely Nicole’s type. And for his part, Thom was smitten early on — he’s always said Nicole is one of the most beautiful women in the world,’ an insider claimed to The Sun.

‘He fancied her long before meeting her on the show. He was initially a bit nervous about starting something up, and ruining his chances on the series, but they had great chemistry from the off.
