Serious three-vehicle crash closes M54 between Cannock and Wolverhampton

A serious crash has closed a stretch of the M54 between Cannock and Wolverhampton this morning.

22 Nov 2019, 10:27 by Dayna Farrington

Emergency services are currently at the scene of the smash involving two cars and a van between junctions one and two of the M54.

Two people are believed to be trapped in their vehicles, with firefighters using cutting equipment to release them.

Two children were released prior to emergency services to follow.

The M54 has been closed at junction one, heading towards the M6 while emergency services are at the scene.

A spokesman from West Midlands Ambulance Service said: "We were called to the M54 at around 9am this morning by Staffordshire Fire and Rescue Service to reports of a road traffic collision between junctions one and two.
