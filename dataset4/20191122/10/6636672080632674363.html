Elon’s truck

22 Nov 2019, 10:24 by David Manners

It may not look much like a truck but this is Tesla’s electric pick-up truck announced yesterday in Los Angeles.

Called the Cybertruck it costs $40/50/70k depending on spec.

The top spec model has a 500 mile range. Production is scheduled for late 2021.

“The top three selling vehicles in America are pickup trucks, said Musk at the launch, “to solve sustainable energy, we have to have a pickup truck.”

The body is made from stainless steel with windows made from armoured glass.

Musk has said that the design was partly influenced by 007’s submarine-capable Lotus Esprit.
