13 NPA rebs charged over CAFGU man’s murder

22 Nov 2019, 10:32 by Alfred P. Dalizon

POLICE the other day filed a murder case against 13 suspected members of the New People’s Army accused of killing a member of the Citizens Armed Forces Geographical Unit in Valencia City in Bukidnon last October 27, the Police Regional Office 10 reported.

Charged with murder, robbery, carnapping and violation of the International Human Rights Law were Reynante Tumbalang alias ‘Ka War/Jigger,’ Dionisio Micabalo alias ‘Ka Muling/Cardo/BF/William, Abelardo Navarro alias ‘Ka Canoy//Narra,’ Julia Cagadas alias ‘Ka Toyang/Rheda,’  Bonifacio Lutawan alias ‘Ka Budo/Tado/Marvin/Tads/Dodong, Lorna Micabalo alias ‘Ka Ating/Adel/Sarah,’ Allan Morales alias ‘Ka Budlat/Dante,’ Judith Jaron alias ‘Ka Geelah/Lalai,’ Precy Locsin alias ‘Ka Jian/Geraldine,’ Michael Fat alias ‘Ka As/Nonoy,’ Pedro Lumantas alias ‘Ka Andoy/ Mosong,’ Beverly Sinunta alias ‘Ka Toyang/Ayang/Raya,,’ one alias ‘Ka Jian’ and an alias Ka Pon.’

PRO10 director, Brigadier General Rolando B. Anduyan said that the criminal charges against the suspects were filed before the Valencia City Prosecutor’s Office on Thursday.

The charges stemmed from the killing of Pedro Omblero Jr., a militiaman assigned at the Lumbayao Patrol Base of the Army’s 88th Infantry Battalion last October 27.

Witnesses identified the suspects as the killers of the CAFGU man in Barangay Batangan.
