Crystal Palace 'want January deal for Olivier Giroud'

Crystal Palace are reportedly keen on a January deal for Chelsea forward Olivier Giroud.

22 Nov 2019, 10:56 by Matt Law

The France international - also linked with the likes of Inter Milan and Borussia Dortmund - has only made four Premier League appearances for Chelsea during the 2019-20 campaign.

The 33-year-old will see his contract at Stamford Bridge expire this summer, and it is widely thought that the experienced striker will seek pastures new at the start of 2020.

According to The Express, Palace want to complete a January deal for the 2018 World Cup winner, but the Eagles will struggle to afford the Frenchman's salary.
