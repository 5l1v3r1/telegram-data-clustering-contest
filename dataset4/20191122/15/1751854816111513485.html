Honor V30 and V30 Pro 5G phones are slated to go official on Nov. 26. With few days remaining for the unveil event, the rumor mill is now actively spilling more information on the Honor V30 series phones. Today, fresh information on the specs of the V30 and V30 Pro phones have surfaced ahead of the next week’s launch.

Honor V30 and V30 Pro Specifications (Rumored)

The leak has revealed that the Honor V30 and V30 Pro are equipped with a 6.57-inch IPS LCD display. Honor has revealed the present of a dual selfie camera system that will be made available a pill-shaped cutout on the screen. Probably, both phones arrive with same display design. The leak states that the Honor V30 and V30 Pro will offer a screen-to-body ratio of 91.46 percent.

The Kirin 990 5G chipset that will bring support SA (Standalone) and NSA (Non-Standalone) dual-mode 5G connectivity will be powering the Honor V30 and Honor V30 Pro smartphones. The Honor V30 series will be the first 5G phones from the company. The Honor V30 may come in variants like 6 GB RAM + 128 GB storage and 8 GB RAM + 128 GB storage. The Pro model could be coming to China in variants like 8 GB RAM + 128 GB storage and 8 GB RAM + 256 GB storage which has been benchmarked on AnTuTu with an impressive score.

The leak further reveals that the Honor V30 and V30 Pro will both feature three rear-mounted cameras. The Honor V30 may feature a 40-megapixel Sony IMX600 main lens, an 8-megapixel telephoto lens and an OIS enabled 8-megapixel wide-angle senor. The Honor V30 Pro could be featuring the same primary lens and telephoto lens along with an OIS assisted 12-megpixel lens for videography.

The Honor V30 may come with a 4,200mAh battery and V30 Pro could be packed with a slightly smaller 4,100mAh battery. The V30 could be coming with 40W fast charging whereas the V30 Pro may arrive with 40W rapid charging, 27W fast charging and 7.5W reverse wireless charging. Both phones may feature a side-mounted fingerprint reader. The V30 series are also expected to feature liquid cooling technology. The V30 and V30 Pro could be available in colors like Icelandic Fantasy, Magic Night Star River, Charm Star Blue and Twilight Orange. The leak has information on the pricing of the Honor V30 and V30 Pro phones.
