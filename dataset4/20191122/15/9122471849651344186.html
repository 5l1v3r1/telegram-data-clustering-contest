Alabama vs. Western Carolina: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

22 Nov 2019, 15:13 by Chris Rosvoglou

Alabama quickly found itself back in the win column last weekend, but it came at a price. Tua Tagovailoa went down with a season-ending hip injury against Mississippi State.

Losing a player as talented as Tagovailoa is certainly a tough blow for the Crimson Tide. However, the selection committee still has Alabama in the top five of the College Football Playoff.

It’ll be on Alabama to show the committee it can still be a dominant team without its star quarterback. That means Mac Jones will have to be impressive in the team’s final two games.

Jones will start this Saturday against Western Carolina, which should give him a nice chance to build up confidence before the Iron Bowl.

Western Carolina is just 3-8 this season as is coming off a double-digit loss to Samford.
