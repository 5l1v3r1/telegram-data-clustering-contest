PMCO Prelims Schedule: PMCO Fall Split 2019 prelims teams, date, time and map

22 Nov 2019, 15:08 by Tanish Chachra

PMCO Prelims Schedule: PUBG Mobile club open to host prelims for global finals of fall split to fill the last posts in a 16-team competition.

PMCO Fall split is reaching to its final phase, as the prelim round for the global final is set to kick-off on coming Saturday, 16 teams will vie against each other to grab final three spots in the grand finals of the tournament.

From India, two teams have already qualified for the finals, meanwhile, three teams will be in the prelims against the teams from the other regions to join Team Soul and Entity gaming.

All the 16 teams in the preliminary round will be competing across 18 rounds and the best three teams with the aggregate score will proceed further in the final stage of the competition.

This will be for the first time when an Indian team will be competing in the preliminary round, as last time INS and IND were not able to compete despite being placed for the preliminary round due to visa issues.

But this time both the teams will be competing in the prelims held in Malaysia. Meanwhile, Team Soul was the only Indian team who represented the nation in the global finals at that time.

Now, with more than one team from India, there would be better chances of accomplishment in the final tournament. Let’s look at the details ahead of the final tournament.
