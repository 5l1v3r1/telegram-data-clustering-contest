Taskmaster poached by Channel 4 from Dave as hit game show makes big move

22 Nov 2019, 15:09

Get ready for UK TV’s biggest channel hop since The Great British Bake Off departed the BBC in 2017.

Taskmaster, the beloved and very silly Dave show where celebrities and comedians are set a series of increasingly bizarre tasks, has been bought by Channel 4.

The Avalon Television-produced series will be screened on 4 from 2020, with a six-series deal across the next three years having been confirmed. Repeats will air on E4.

Its ninth series aired on Dave back in September, with Greg Davies and Taskmaster creator Alex Horne serving as hosts.
