Back after a hiatus of two weeks as we got done with the final round of qualifiers for Euro 2020, Ligue 1 return with a resounding clash which will feature league leaders PSG in play. Undoubtedly the most well rounded and complete setup in the land of the league, the side are befittingly sitting in the echelons of the points tally, a spot they’ll be looking to hammer down in their favour come today.

Currently having an eight point lead in their artillery, PSG make an advent into today’s contest looking to further extend their advantage against a dwindling Lille side. In fifth place ahead of today’s impending encounter, Lille have floundering recently, regressing in form to go on a downward spiral to garner just the two wins from their last five outings.

The run of form has seen them fall from challenging for a spot in the annuls of the league to clutching at straws with their most recent encounter seeing them draw 0-0 with Metz to be handed out their fourth draw in 13 encounters. Elsewhere, PSG managed to turnover their 2-1 defeat to Dijon with a scrappy 2-1 win over Brest the last time around, a result which showed that the side are far from the complete package.

With their being blatant gaping holes in PSG’s setup, Lille will be well aware that the slightest bit of hammer on their opponents today can make this an edge of the seat thriller and see the side salvage something from the contest. With the ramifications for both the sides enormous from this contest, this match looks to be a closely contested one with the finest of margins coming into play here.
