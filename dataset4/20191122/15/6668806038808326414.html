Taapsee Pannu names two of her worst co-stars

22 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Filmfare

Taapsee Pannu was last seen in Saand Ki Aankh and with that film, she proved that there’s nothing she can do. The actress has featured in movies ranging from intense dramas like Pink to comedies like Judwaa 2. Taapsee is also known to speak her mind. She recently featured on one of the episodes of No Filter with Neha, with the host Neha Dhupia where she answered a series of fun questions about herself. On being quizzed about who were her worst co-stars, Taapsee, to everyone’s shock named Vicky Kaushal and Jacqueline Fernandez.

She went on to further explain, “I'll say two of them - Jacqueline because she is so freaking hot which I was struggling to match up to it in Judwaa 2 and I was like I just hope I don’t put myself to shame. And the other one which I had a difficult time with was, Vicky because I was always so conscious that I should not lose out to him in the scene because he is just so good."

Well, we couldn’t agree more with your reasons, Taapsee.
