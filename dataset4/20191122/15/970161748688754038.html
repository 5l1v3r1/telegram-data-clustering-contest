Jennifer Garner spends 25 minutes hunting down her lost car and it's too much

22 Nov 2019, 15:46

Jennifer Garner lost her car in a one-story car park and spent over 25 minutes trying to find it.

Yes, if you think you’re having a bad day then think again, as the 47-year-old had to get her friend to drive her around the other vehicles in search of her ride.

Taking to Instagram, the actress shared a video of herself hunting down the car – and it’s too much.

Upon entering the carpark, she told the camera that they were looking for the car in the section labeled Y.

But despite walking for 10 whole minutes, they were unsuccessful.

Pressing the button on her key fob, the 13 Going On 30 star sighed: ‘You know what’s happening? We’re out of batteries.’

She laughed: ‘I stopped to tie my shoe, I should be on CSI: Miami.’

The video then shoots to the pair driving around the carpark looking completely lost.

‘Oh gosh,’ Jenner said: ‘This is where it gets complicated. Now I remember thinking about what to do.’

Driving up to a junction she explained: ‘It won’t take us long just to go and do that loop, because I might have done that, or I might not have, I can’t tell you.’

Getting frustrated, Jen added: ‘It’s been 25 minutes of looking for a car on a one-level parking lot.’

However, shortly after a huge smile appeared on her face as she gushed: ‘I see it.’

Howling down the lens, the mother-of-three said: ‘It’s nothing to do with that I said it was! I said it was that way in Y.’

Her buddy sounded disappointed in the background and flipped the camera to a sign next to the car, which read ‘F’.

‘It’s so rude to just move someone’s car to F,’ Jennifer jokes as they finally reach her car. Oh, Jen.
