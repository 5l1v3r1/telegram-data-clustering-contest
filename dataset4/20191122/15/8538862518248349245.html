Monster Hunter and Horizon Cross Paths Once Again in Today's Iceborne Update on PS4

Into the Frozen Wilds

22 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Stephen Tailby

Horizon: Zero Dawn and Monster Hunter: World are pretty different games, but they both have one thing in common -- huge, exciting battles against gigantic creatures. It's no wonder that Aloy is returning to Capcom's best-slaying RPG in a brand new crossover event. From today, Iceborne players will be able to play a brand new event quest and unlock some cool Horizon-themed gear.

The first quest in a "multi-part crossover" is called Into the Frozen Wilds, and gives hunters the chance to craft the Stormslinger Prototype, a new Light Bowgun based on Aloy's weapon in the Horizon expansion. You'll face off against a Zinogre in Hoarfrost Reach, and you'll need to be Hunter Rank 24 or above to stand a chance. Completing the quest will also reward you with a weapon pendant fashioned after Aloy's Focus device. The quest is available from now until 10th January, so there's lots of time to give it a shot.
