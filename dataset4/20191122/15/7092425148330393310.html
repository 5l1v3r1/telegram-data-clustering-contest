22 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Ciara Walsh

The Always Sunny gang will return to Netflix next January.

Just days after the record-equalling season ended in the US - Always Sunny is now one of the longest-running comedy shows of all time - Netflix have confirmed that Mac, Frank, Dennis, Dee, and Charlie will be breaking in the new year in style.

Season 14 of Always Sunny arrives on Netflix on January 19, 2020.

The good news doesn't appear to be stopping there, however, as earlier this year, FX CEO John Landgraf said: "As long as we can come to business terms that fit both our needs and they can continue to make episodes as good as they have been making, there is a good chance it may go past season 14".
