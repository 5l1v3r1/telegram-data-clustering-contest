Road closure postponed

22 Nov 2019, 15:50 by Colin Dacre

A previously announced  temporary closure of Eastside Road in Okanagan Falls next week has been cancelled. 

The region’s road contractor, AIM Roads, said in a tweet Friday the four-day closure of Eastside Road between McLean Creek Road and Maple Street has been postponed. 

The maintenance work has been rescheduled for spring 2020 due to weather.
