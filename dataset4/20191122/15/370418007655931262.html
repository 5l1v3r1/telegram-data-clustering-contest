Watch Panic! At The Disco’s New Video For Into The Unknown

Brendon Urie’s Frozen 2 cover has now gotten a lovely, shiny music video.

22 Nov 2019, 15:59

To coincide with the release of Disney’s new Frozen 2 movie, Panic! At The Disco have unveiled a video for their cover of Into The Unknown.

Brendon Urie’s rendition of the Disney banger is taken from the end credits of the movie, while in the actual film it’s performed by Idina Menzel (who plays Elsa).
