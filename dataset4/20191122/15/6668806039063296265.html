Hrithik Roshan to hike his fee post the success of Super 30 and War

22 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Filmfare

Hrithik Roshan has had a great year at the box-office with two back to back hits this year. His first release, Super 30, was based on the life of renowned mathematician Anand Kumar from Patna and joined the 100 crore club. War, on the other hand, in which he was seen as an intelligence officer, went on to make 300 crores at the box-office. The film also starred the dashing Tiger Shroff and had some high intense action scenes that impressed fans. 

Reports suggested that with back to back hits, the star is now in demand. Apparently, top directors like Aanand L Rai, Aditya Chopra, Sanjay Leela Bhansali, Karan Johar and a few others have reached out to Hrithik for their projects. With all the hype, it is rumoured that the actor has hiked his fee substantially. Any new project that he will sign, including any sequels to his movies from the past will be signed with a hiked fee.  

Well, considering the year he’s had at the box-office, we believe this is well and truly deserved.
