Most followed DJ in Africa – DJ Cuppy brags

22 Nov 2019, 15:51 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Florence Otedola popularly known as DJ Cuppy has taken to her social media page to gush over herself upon discovering that she is the most followed Dj in Africa.

Cuppy, the daughter of billionaire oil magnet Femi Otedola was overly excited and could not hold her excitment as she rushed to her social media page to share the news with fans. She has been flaunting the title “Most followed DJ in Africa” on her posts since she made the announcement.

She posted:

“OMG! Just found out I’m the MOST FOLLOWED African DJ in The WORLD! RETWEET AND CELEBRATE WITH ME!”
