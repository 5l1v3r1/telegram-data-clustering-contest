iPhone XS Max price & availability

The Max is a premium phone and even a year on from launch you shouldn't expect it to be cheap. Still, it should cost significantly less than the £1,099/$1,099 the 64GB model cost in autumn 2018.

(The higher-storage models cost £1,249/$1,249 for 256GB and £1,449/$1,449 for 512GB at launch. Again, you shouldn't buy at anything like those prices now.)
