Ricky Ponting fumes as umpires miss 21 no-balls during Australia v Pakistan Test

22 Nov 2019, 15:37

Ricky Ponting says the current state is ‘not good enough’ after 21 no-balls went uncalled on day two of the opening Test between Australia and Pakistan.

Four front foot no-balls have been called so far in the first Test at the Gabba but footage from Australian sports network 7Sport has highlighted a number of no-balls that went unpunished.

Pakistan’s teenage debutant Naseem Shah had his maiden Test scalp chalked off for overstepping and former Australia captain Ponting has bemoaned the current approach to calling no-balls.
