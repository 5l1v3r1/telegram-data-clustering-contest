Soap fans face a dilemma as EastEnders clashes with I'm A Celebrity TONIGHT

It's a clash of the titans!

22 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Helen Fear

TV fans will face a dilemma tonight, when EastEnders airs one hour later than usual.

On Friday (November 22), the BBC soap will air at 9pm to make way for an extended edition of Question Time.

From 7pm to 9pm, presenter Fiona Bruce will introduce a live debate from Sheffield, with Labour Party leader Jeremy Corbyn, Leader of the Scottish National Party Nicola Sturgeon MSP, Leader of the Liberal Democrats Jo Swinson and Prime Minister Boris Johnson.

The debate is an important one in the run-up to the election on December 12.

But the big decision for some viewers won't be who to vote for... It'll be what to watch afterwards at 9pm!
