Sisters missing 2 weeks

22 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Elana Shepert/Glacier Media

RCMP are seeking the public's help in locating two Surrey teens who haven’t been seen for more than two weeks.

Romanian sisters Elizabeth and Denisa Stana were last seen Nov. 7 on 192nd Street in Surrey. 

Elizabeth, 15, is described as five-foot-five, 88 pounds, with black hair and brown eyes, last seen wearing a black jacket.

Densia, 12, five-foot-three, 99 pounds, with blond hair and brown eyes, last scene wearing a white sweater and white T-shirt.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Surrey RCMP at 604-599-0502, or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477 or www.solvecrime.ca, quoting file number 2019-180851.
