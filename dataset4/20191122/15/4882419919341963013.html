Right-back shortage sees Dani Morer train with Barça pre-Leganes

The Barceloma B right-back is having a cracking season in the third division

22 Nov 2019, 15:32 by German Bona

Barcelona B right-back Dani Morer trained with the first team on Friday ahead of this weekend's trip to Leganes in La Liga.

Ansu Fati, Carles Perez and goalkeeper Sergi Puig were also involved in the session as Ernesto Valverde prepared for the return of domestic football following the international break.

Morer has been called up due to a full-back shortage. Nelson Semedo is injured and Sergi Roberto is suspended on the right side, while Jordi Alba's injured on the left.

Junior Firpo is likely to keep his place in Alba's absence, while Moussa Wague could make his first appearance of the season for the club.

Right-back Morer is likely to make the squad as cover in the full-back positions having impressed for the B team in Segunda B this season.

Ernesto Valverde, though, also suggested that he could change the system to make up for missing so many full-backs for the game in Madrid.
