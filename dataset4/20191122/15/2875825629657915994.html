Black Friday 2019: Currys PC World launch sale with tonnes of discounts on gadgets

The tech retailer is offering tonnes of deals on a range of gadgets

22 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Ian Mangan

Tech retailer Currys PC World has today, November 22, launched their massive Black Tag sale ahead of Black Friday next week.

To mark the festive season, punters can bag themselves discounts on a range of products online or in stores around the country.

Shoppers looking to get their hands on some discounted gadgets need look no further with the shop offering a range of deals on TVs, headphones, coffee machines and more.

The company has said they expect over 30 online orders 54 sales in stores per minute this year for Black Friday.
