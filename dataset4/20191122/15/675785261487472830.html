YouTuber nearly chokes to death during social media chili challenge

Mexican internet star Hugo Lara was hospitalised after encountering severe breathing problems whilst filming himself eating one of the world's hottest chillies

22 Nov 2019, 15:25 by Sam Elliott, Louise Newton, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sam-elliott/, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-newton/

YouTuber Hugo Lara realised he had bit off more than he could chew when a social media chili challenge went very wrong.

The popular Mexican comedian filmed himself scoffing two whole habanero chillies - one of the hottest around.

But he choked and was was hushed to hospital after falling ill.

But that didn't stop the influencer uploading the video to his YouTube channel with nearly 500,000 watching his very close call.

The clips hows him with his sister Yazmin and two other women as he begins to munch on the hot peppers.

He then gets through two whole chillies before beginning to visibly struggle.

Lara can be seen coughing heavily as he appears to starting choking. He dropped onto the sofa and seems to be having difficulties breathing.

One of the women in the video rush to help him, and pat him on the back as he continues coughing.

His sister and co-star Yazmin released a statement saying her brother is on the mend.

She wrote: "Hugo Lara is in stable condition. We didn't think that this would get so out of control, and we will show you the video we recorded... so that you can see what really happened."

The YouTuber wrote to fans on Facebook and said he is “feeling better”.

An account called 'lylapas' said: “I am glad to hear you are well, if I were your mother I would slap you after scaring me."

Another user name Angeles Duran wrote: “Nobody broadcasts these challenges live because they are edited, you lack experience.”
