Dog left alone in car drives around in circles for an hour

22 Nov 2019, 15:24

A dog left alone in its owner’s car managed to drive the vehicle around in circles for an entire hour.

Residents of a street in Port St Lucie, Florida, were concerned after they saw the vehicle reversing around and around outside their homes.

And they were stunned when they went outside and realized what was behind the wheel.

Eyewitness Anne Sabol said: ‘First I thought I saw somebody backing up but then they kept going and I’m like OK what are they doing.

‘The cops came and I’m like OK.

‘And when the cops got the door open a black dog jumped out I was like they should give that thing a license.’

The doggy driver struck a mailbox, garbage cans and bricks left laying out in front of several houses, WPBF reported.

But both animal and car left largely unscathed, with locals still mystified as to how the dog managed to get behind the wheel.

Port St Lucie Police believe the dog may have been able to joyride after the car’s owner stepped out of the vehicle and accidentally left it in reverse.
