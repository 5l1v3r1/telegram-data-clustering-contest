Golden retriever looks very sad about Mufasa's death in The Lion King

22 Nov 2019, 15:00

An adorable golden retriever appeared to get incredibly worried while watching Disney favorite Simba’s dad Mufasa die in ‘The Lion King.’

The footage taken by Sam Stolly shows his one-year-old dog, Khaleesi, nervously watching the Disney movie from Stolly’s kitchen before walking up for a better view.

As the heartbreaking scene unfolds, Khaleesi takes a seat right in front of the screen and does not look away as Simba tries to wake his father up.

Alongside the video, Stolly wrote: ‘Someone was very concerned.’

According to the American Kennel Society, dogs can recognize action on the television – especially when it depicts another dog, even more so when it’s their breed.

Even though Simba is a cartoon lion, Khaleesi may be perceiving him as another golden retriever in distress due to his golden yellow fur and white chest.

Sam shared his footage online, where his empathetic dog’s behavior has since won the hearts of thousands of viewers.
