The Pixel 4's slim size means that even a heavier duty case can still feel sleek in hand, and when combined with a slim but grippy case like the Spigen Air Armor it feels absolutely fantastic. The Air Armor seems like a light-duty case, but I've worn them for months, and they're all the protection you'll need unless you're an outdoorsman.

On the more robust end of the scale, the Tudia Merge Series and OtterBox Symmetry Series are both solid heavy-duty cases with long pedigrees and subtle sophistication. The Tudia's hybrid style is easier to get on and off, but the Sapphire Secret is just the best color ever. Both will serve your Pixel 4 well, as would any case in this roundup.

About buying cases on Black Friday

Considering most cases retail for $10-$20, you can't expect to see the huge markdowns that we'd see on phones themselves, but that doesn't mean you can't find good deals on cases; you just need to adjust your discount expectations. If you see a case that's $20-$30 off on Black Friday, chances are it's a case that was $60-$90, to begin with, which means that's you're not necessarily getting a great deal so much as just not getting ripped off for a high-end name-brand case.

When looking for case deals around Black Friday, we'll be covering the one we come across in our Black Friday coverage, but with items this low-priced to begin with, a lot can fall through the cracks. If you want to hunt for case deals the way I do, make yourself a short list of case features that are important to you. Include features like extra-wide port openings, good button covers, or a high lip around the camera. Remember to check the case maker's site as well as major retailers like Amazon and Best Buy. Both will be having sales, but the manufacturer might have steeper discounts than bigger chains that are discounting a wider variety of products.

You'll also want to check frequently. Cases wind up in Lightning deals all the time, and those deals tend to go very quickly for name-brand cases like Spigen, Otterbox, and Ringke, especially for the good colors like that Sapphire Secret Otterbox Symmetry Series.
