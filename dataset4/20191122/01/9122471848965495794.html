Michigan Fans Won’t Be Happy With This Graphic From Ohio State

22 Nov 2019, 01:46 by Andrew McCarty

In just a few days, the Ohio State Buckeyes will host the Penn State Nittany Lions in a massive game for the Big Ten.

All eyes will be on Saturday afternoon’s game as the pregame shows for FOX, ESPN and the Big Ten Network will all be in Columbus for the game.

Before the game kicks off, Ohio State released a graphic showing the number of people expected to tune into those respective shows. The Buckeyes expect more than 3.5 million people to be watching the pre-game action.

In the graphic, Ohio State made sure former Michigan star and ESPN College GameDay analyst Desmond Howard was nowhere to be seen.

Howard can be seen sporting Brutus the Buckeye’s head.
