BENGALURU: CasaOne, the US and India-based furniture rental startup, has raised $16 million (Rs 114 crore) in its series-B funding led by existing investor Accel Partners.

The India fund and the US fund of Accel have invested in the two-year-old startup, valued at $116 million (Rs 832 crore) after the fundraise.

WeWork, US-based office manufacturer HNI, Quiet Capital have joined this round along with angel investors including SoftBank Vision Fund partner Lydia Jett. CasaOne’s existing investors — JLL Spark, Freestyle Capital, NextWorld Capital, and Array Ventures have also participated in this round.

Of its 110-strong team, 70 are based in Bengaluru, largely working on technology and product. The India headcount would cross 100 in coming months as the company expands operations in the US.
