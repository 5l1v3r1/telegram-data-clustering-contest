Auba postpones Arsenal contract talks

22 Nov 2019, 01:04 by Agency Report

Arsenal striker Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang has reportedly postponed talks over a new contract with the North London club.

The Gabon international is said to be considering a move away from the Emirates next summer amid interest from Barcelona, who are looking for a long-term replacement for Luis Suarez.

Reports have suggested that Aubameyang will explore his options next summer if Arsenal once again fail to reach the Champions League.

According to Sport, the striker is keen to know more about Barcelona’s interest before committing himself to a long-term deal at Arsenal.

Aubameyang has also recently responded angrily to criticism of his relationship with AFTV personality ‘Troopz’, describing the comments as “bulls**t”.

The 30-year-old, who has eight Premier League goals this term, replaced Granit Xhaka as the club’s permanent captain earlier this month.
