Gurmeet Choudhary Pays A Surprise Visit To A Fan At Her Wedding Reception Party In Mumbai

22 Nov 2019, 01:30 by Gretel Sequeira

Paltan actor Gurmeet Choudhary has a huge fan-base and makes sure he fulfills the wishes of his die-hard fans. To reciprocate his love and admiration for a fan, the Paltan actor recently surprised one of his fans by attending her wedding reception party in Mumbai

It is not by chance that Gurmeet Choudhary became a household name after arriving on the small screen. It was the result of continuous auditions, dedication to fitness and acting mettle. Gurmeet Choudhary is known to have a strong female fan base right from his first television stint. His social media accounts are proof that they are only growing. To reciprocate his love and admiration for a fan, the actor recently surprised one of his fans by attending her wedding reception party in Mumbai.

Gurmeet Choudhary who Talking about it, the actor says, “She has been an active fan right from the time I marked my debut on television. She is from Mumbai and has always supported me and my work. For every film promotion or meet-and-greet contest, she would always come and see me. Gurmeet, who was last seen in Paltan (2018) remarks that his fans keep him on his toes. “We work hard to win their hearts and we are everything for them. If they can be happy for us, why can’t we become part of their happiness And there is no bigger happiness than getting married,” he shares.
