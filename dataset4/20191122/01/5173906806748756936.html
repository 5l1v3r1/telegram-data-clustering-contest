Mild autumn weather for Portugal

22 Nov 2019, 01:00

Rain is predicted in the north with drier weather the further south you travel, while temperatures are to remain mild.

Lisbon: Saturday in the capital is set to be mainly dry however the rain clouds are to return on Sunday and are to remain for the rest of the week. Cloud cover will help to keep the temperatures up this week with average daily highs of 17 degrees and nightly lows of 12 degrees.

The South: The weekend should remain dry in the south with intermittent cloud cover expected and only a slight chance of rain the following week on Tuesday. Temperatures are to remain mild with daily highs of 19 degrees and nightly lows of 11 degrees.

The North: Rain is set to continue throughout the weekend and for the rest of the week in the north. Temperatures are to average daily highs of 15 degrees and nightly lows of 11 degrees. Winds are to be southerly until Thursday when they are to change to westerly.
