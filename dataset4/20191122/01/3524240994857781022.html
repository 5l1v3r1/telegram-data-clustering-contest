Businessman wins Ogunbote’s daughter’s heart

22 Nov 2019, 01:22 by Johnny Edward

Johnny Edward

Lobi Stars coach Gbenga Ogunbote will on Saturday give out the hand of one of his daughters in marriage in Ajah, Lagos State.

Ogunbote, who was appointed as coach of the Markurdi-based outfit at the start of the Nigerian Professional Football season is marrying out his last daughter Anuoluwapo.

Sports Extra gathered that Anuoluwapo will be getting married to businessman, Adewale Oriyomi.

The strictly-by-invitation wedding starts with a church service at the Celestial Church of Christ at Ajah, Eti-Osa in Lagos by 10.00 am.

The reception and engagement holds at the Labora Event Centre, Ajah, while another reception holds at the bride’s father’s residence for the exclusive guests.
