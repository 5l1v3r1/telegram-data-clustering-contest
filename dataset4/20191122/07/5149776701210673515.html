Man arrested after sexual assault investigation on Sandiacre park

The area was cordoned off yesterday

22 Nov 2019, 07:34 by Christopher Harper, Rachel Sutherland

A man has been arrested after an alleged sexual assault took place near a children's park.

Police were called to reports of a sexual assault in Sandiacre, at 1am on the morning of Thursday, November 21.

Derbyshire police were at the scene yesterday morning and a section of the park next to a children's play area was cordoned off, reports DerbyshireLive.

The victim was not physically harmed during the incident and officers believe it was an isolated event.

Officers would like to hear from anyone who was in the area between midnight and 1am and anyone who may have information that could help them with their investigation.
