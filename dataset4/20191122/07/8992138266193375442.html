Chaos or Hoax? Binance, Bithumb Deny Claims of Shanghai Office Raid

22 Nov 2019, 07:17 by Arnab Shome

The crypto industry took a setback recently with reports from China about raids in the offices of Binance and Bithumb’s Shanghai offices by the local authorities.

However, both the exchanges came out to deny any such raids, claiming these as attempts to tarnish their reputation in the country.

No office in Shanghai

A spokesperson from Binance told Cointelegraph that no such offices in Shanghai even exist.

“The Binance team is a global movement consisting of people working in a decentralized manner wherever they are in the world. Binance has no fixed offices in Shanghai or China, so it makes no sense that police raided on any offices and shut them down,” the spokesperson said.
