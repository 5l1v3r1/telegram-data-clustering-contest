Man arrested over Maltese journalist murder free on bail

22 Nov 2019, 07:55 by EUobserver

Businessman Yorgen Fenech, a "person of interest" in the investigation over the murder of Maltese journalist Daphne Caruana Galizia arrested earlier this week by the authorities, has been released on bail, although he is expected to face further questioning on Friday, The Times of Malta writes. According to the newspaper, authorities have ordered 24-hour police surveillance as a precondition for the police bail.
