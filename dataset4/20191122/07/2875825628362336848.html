I'm A Celebrity's Jacqueline Jossa snaps at Caitlyn Jenner after awkward soap slip-up

Reality TV star Caitlyn Jenner was not impressed when she saw that more soap stars were entering I'm A Celeb

22 Nov 2019, 07:51 by Irishmirror.ie

Caitlyn Jenner accidentally put her foot in it after voicing her frustration at the number of soap stars in the jungle this year... to former soap star Jacqueline Jossa.

Last night on I'm A Celebrity, it was revealed that ex-EastEnders actor Cliff Parisi, aka Minty, and Coronation Street's Andy Whyment were this year's late arrivals.

Looking at their pictures on a poster, Caitlyn asked whether anyone in the camp knew who they were.

Roman Kemp replied: "You know the one on the left it’s a guy from Coronation Street which is another big soap in the UK."

Caitlyn immediately quipped: "What’s the deal with all these f***ing soap people?"

Overhearing her, Jacqueline said: "Oi!"

Grinning sheepishly, Caitlyn added as an apology: "Oh sorry."

While Roman laughed over the awkward moment, Jacqueline looked less than impressed.

And fans found the moment utterly hilarious.
