AM DRAMA

Tay Road Bridge locked down after early morning ‘police incident’

22 Nov 2019, 07:33 by Jonathan Whitelaw

A BUSY Scots bridge was locked down first thing this morning after a "police incident" in the area.

Emergency services were scrambled to the scene on the Tay Road Bridge after the alarm was raised around 6.33am this morning.

It's not clear what sparked the emergency.

But bridge bosses said the crossing was reopened again just before 7am.

Both the north and southbound carriageways were locked down first thing this morning.

And early morning commuters were warned of the closure on Twitter.

Local bus services were disrupted as a result.

Police Scotland has been approached for information.
