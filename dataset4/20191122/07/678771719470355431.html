Market Pulse on November 21st

22 Nov 2019, 07:42 by Olivier Rigot, EMC Gestion de Fortune

S&P 500 3103,54 -4,92.

Stock prices are holding fairly well above the 3100 mark and, for the time being, the correction/consolidation unfolds smoothly although the cumulative advance/decline line has started to lose momentum. Systematic models are on the verge to turn down and that may attract selling pressure from market participants using this approach. Daily sentiment indicators paint a mixed picture and hedging activity is still subdued.
