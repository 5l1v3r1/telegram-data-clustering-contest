Dead man found inside car in Beltola area of Guwahati city

22 Nov 2019, 07:29

STAFF REPORTER

GUWAHATI: A parked car (AS 01 BM 8136) at Beltola Tiniali with the driver inside caught the attention of the local people when it did not move for many hours. The driver appeared to be unconscious. Basistha police was contacted and the driver, identified as Rajkumar Deori of Dispur, was taken to GMCH but was declared dead on arrival.
