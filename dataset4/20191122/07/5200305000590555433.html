Krystle D’Souza finalised for Bollywood debut in ‘Chehre’?

22 Nov 2019, 07:25 by Priyanka Borua

Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi starrer ‘Chehre’ made headlines this week as the original leading lady Kriti Kharbanda is being recast. Names like Ankita Lokhande and Mouni Roy have been said to be considered for the role. Although, the latest rumors suggest that Krystle D’Souza has been finalised to star alongside Bachchan and Hashmi after her audition for the same.

There has been no confirmation from the makers of ‘Chehre’ but if cast, D’Souza will be making her Bollywood debut after a successful run on the television and digital space.

D’Souza was recently in the digital show ‘Fittrat’.
