Mix Of The Day: Bufiman

A live set from the Düsseldorf mainstay, recorded for Fault Radio at San Francisco's Vinyl Dreams record shop.

22 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Resident Advisor

Bufiman (AKA Jan Schulte) came through with an hour-plus mix of psyched-out downtempo jams at the Haight Street. His debut album, Albumsi, drops Monday via Dekmantel.
