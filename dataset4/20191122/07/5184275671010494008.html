Yu Karke From Dabangg 3: Salman Khan’s Quirky Steps & Sonakshi Sinha’s Appeal Add Up To The Heat!

The fact that Yu Karke has vocals by Salman Khan himself just adds to the fun.

22 Nov 2019, 07:13 by Koimoi.com Team

The makers of Dabangg 3 are surely taking the entertainment and fun quotient really high with the firecracker of a song that released today, Yu Karke, featuring Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha. The video that came out today is sure to be a treat to all the fans with the out and about chemistry along with the naughty avatar of Chulbul Pandey and his ‘sexy’ Rajjo.

The fact that the song has vocals by Chulbul Pandey himself just adds to the fun. Yes, you read that right! The actor has crooned for the song himself, and that once increases the buzz further.

Bringing us Salman Khan and Sonakshi ‘s dance moves with the perfect dose of Chulbul and Rajjo to make our day, the song is a treat to sore eyes. A visually colourful and vibrant number, the video teaser itself that was released yesterday prepared us for a Friday bonanza from the most daring, badass and lovely cop, Chulbul today!
