Doctor Who promises 'space for all' with series 12 teaser

22 Nov 2019, 07:42

Doctor Who is gearing up for the release of its twelfth series and a new teaser image promises it will have ‘space for all’.

This will be Jodie Whittaker’s second run in charge of the Tardis as the Thirteenth Doctor.

Taking to Twitter, the BBC America account tweeted out the newest teaser image for the new instalment, which sees the Doctor reunited with her three companions Graham (Bradley Walsh), Yaz (Mandip Gill) and Ryan (Toisin Cole).

The picture teases that the show’s return will have ‘space for all,’ so we’re looking forward to this being the most inclusive and far-reaching part of the long-running sci-fi saga yet.
