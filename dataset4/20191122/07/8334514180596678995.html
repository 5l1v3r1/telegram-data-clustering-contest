China makes representation to U.S. over ships in South China Sea

22 Nov 2019, 07:42

BEIJING (Reuters) - China lodged stern representations with the United States after U.S. navy ships sailed in the South China Sea near islands claimed by Beijing, foreign ministry spokesman Geng Shuang told a daily briefing on Friday.
