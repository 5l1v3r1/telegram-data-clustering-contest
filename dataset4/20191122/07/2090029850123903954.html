Police officer allegedly leaves daughter bleeding after beating her mercilessly

22 Nov 2019, 07:20 by Damilola Ismail

A Police officer has been called out after he allegedly beat up his daughter mercilessly, leaving her bleeding on her head.

According to an eye witness, the Police officer always beats up his wife or children whenever he gets back to work leaving the little girl with so much marks disfiguring her back and an injured head.

The story was brought to the public by Unkle Jet-Wize Udoma who posted pictures of the wounded girl and her disfigured back in an attempt to put an end to this assault.

He wrote;

Every time this police officer comes back from work, he’ll either vent his anger/frustration on his wife or this innocent girl (his daughter).

This nonsense has to stop.
Please, help me repost.
The idiot lives on Abak Road here in Uyo.

For more info, call me on: 08034905433

The attention of the Police force is being called to investigate and save the lives of this child.

According to Mmanti Umoh, the girl has been taken into custody by FIDA Akwa Ibom while investigations are still ongoing. She wrote;

Dear PoliceNG, this is now your turn to ensure and insist that law and order please starts at the bench. This report got to me 30 Mins ago. I have reached #FIDAAkwaIbom lead and the child will be taken into their custody ASAP. Tomorrow i’ll post his name & division
