Investigators search wooded area of Fort Myers overnight

22 Nov 2019, 07:14 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. – We’re working to get more information on an active scene overnight in a wooded area of Fort Myers.

Investigators from both the Fort Myers Police Department and the Lee County Sheriff's Office were out searching in the woods off Billy Creek Drive, around 1:30 a.m. Friday. That's just off Ortiz Avenue north of Luckett Road.

The Fox 4 Breaking News Tracker was the first media on the scene as officials combed the area.

Once we get an update on the situation, we'll be sure to bring that to you.
