1029 Hot Tomato’s Splashing Into Summer

thanks to Wet’n’Wild

22 Nov 2019, 07:00

Everyone’s favourite Dive’n’Movies is back at Wet’n’Wild!

To celebrate, 1029 Hot Tomato are Splashing Into Summer and giving away family passes to Wet’n’Wild, so you can check out your favourite flick while floating in the famous Giant Wave Pool!

Slide into the night from Friday 27 December with some rides operating until 8:30pm! Take the whole family, grab one of the many delicious summer treats and cocktails before diving-in!

We’ll be handing out family passes every morning at 8:30am with Flan, Emily Jade & Christo, across the day while you work and again with Galey, Mal and Moyra for the drive home!

Presented by Coca-Cola, Dive’n’Movies are returning to Wet’n’Wild from 27 December – 19 January!

Let the good times roll with a line-up of blockbuster films which you can enjoy in the Giant Wave Pool by hiring a tube or on surrounding deck chairs!

Here are just some of the films hitting the big screen:

  • AVENGERS: Infinity War
  • AVENGERS ENDGAME
  • Bumble Bee
  • The Lego Movie 2: The Second Part
  • Home Alone
  • Finding Nemo
  • ALADDIN
  • Fate of the Furious

There will also be a range of mouth-watering food and drink options available for purchase to indulge in whilst you watch the movie.

You can also slide into the night with some of your favourite attractions including Super 8 Aqua Racer, AquaLoop, River Rapids, Constrictor, Wet’n’Wild Junior, Whirlpool Springs, Giant Wave Pool, Kamikaze, Tornado and Black Hole operating until 8:30pm!

Dive’n’Movies schedule subject to change. Slides and attractions are subject to change to enable maintenance and some slides and attractions will not operate during inclement weather conditions. Please note queue lines may close earlier to allow slides to finish operating at 8:30pm.
