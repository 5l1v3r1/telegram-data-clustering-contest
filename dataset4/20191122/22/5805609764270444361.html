At that time, we don’t usually have many options left apart from dealing with the annoying low battery notifications with LED light. Well, disabling low battery notifications is not always the best option, but you can always set low battery notification ringtone to make it less annoying.

Actually, lots of people search for ‘how to set low battery notification ringtone in Android’. Since Android is an open-source operating system, you can actually set a low battery notification ringtone on your smartphone. How to Set Low Battery Notification Ringtone On Android

Below, we have shared two best methods that would help you set low battery notifications on any Android smartphone 2019. So, let’s explore how to set low battery notifications on Android 2019.

#1 Using Battery Notifier BT Free

Step 1. First of all download and install the app “Battery Notifier BT Free” on your Android device. You can get this app from the Google Play store. Install it, and grant all permissions that the app asks.

Step 2. After installing the app, open it and click on the Settings option which you shall see up easily in the middle of your screen.

 

Step 3. You will now see a list of options inside the app, you just have to select the option “Low battery charge options“. From here onwards, you will be able to set the various aspects of your device when the battery comes to certain low level.

Step 4. Now set up the options Low battery, Repeat interval, Vibration mode, Sound mode and at last the Notification ringtone from there. For changing the low battery notification, tap on ‘Notification Ringtone’ and then select the ringtone of your choice.

After setting the ringtone from the options or the menu, just save it and then close the app. From now on, your device will play the ringtone that you have set as the low battery notification whenever it alerts for the purpose.

#2 Set Low Battery Notification Ringtone (Android 9 Pie)

Well, if your smartphone is running on Android 9 operating system, then you don’t need to rely on any third-party app. Below, we have shared a working method that would help you to set low battery notification ringtone on Android Pie operating system.

Step 1. First of all, open Settings on your Android device.

Step 2. From the Settings, you need to select the ‘Apps’. There you will see the list of all installed apps.

Step 3. You need to search and open the ‘System UI’ from the list.

Step 4. Now tap on the ‘Notifications’

Step 5. Now you need to tap on the ‘System UI’ and then select ‘Sound’

Step 6. From there you can set system ringtones. You can also choose custom ringtones from your internal storage.

That’s it, you are done! This is how you can set low battery notification ringtone on Android 9 Pie operating system.
