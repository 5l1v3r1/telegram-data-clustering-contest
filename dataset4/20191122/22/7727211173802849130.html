Andrew Maxwell sparks health concern as he chokes on I'm A Celebrity

Kate and Andy had to come to Andrew's rescue

22 Nov 2019, 22:04 by Lottie Gibbons

Andrew Maxwell sparked health concerns on Twitter as he choked on tonight's I'm A Celebrity.

The outlaws tucked into their food which was won earlier in the Bushtucker Trial.

They had camel fillet, purple congos, karkalia and asparagus shoots for lunch with bananas for dessert.

Myles cooked the six star meal for his jailhouse campmates and they all tucked in.

But as comedian Andrew ate a large part of camel and started to choke as it got stuck in his throat.

Kate and Andy started to hit his back as he knelt down, eventually the meat came out and Jacqueline got him some water.

Myles said: “Chew your food, mate you scared us."

Kate said: “You went bright red, I was like ‘oh my god’.”

Later, Myles admitted: "I can’t believe I nearly killed Andrew Maxwell”.

Andrew replied: “You didn’t, I nearly killed me.”

The camp thanked Myles for cooking the food as Andrew said: “Let that be a lesson to you kids, chew your camel.”

Andrew stepped into the sunlight and raised his arms out and said: “Not today Lord, not today, thank you for the second chance.”

Users took to Twitter, commenting on their concern for Andrew.

Jack said: "Yikes, Andrew nearly choked on his life!"

Alison tweeted: "Did Andrew nearly just die?!"

Alex said: "Broooooo Andrew nearly choked live on air."

And Georgia said: "Omg I’ve just s*** thinking Andrew was going to die."
