I’m A Celebrity viewers left in hysterics as Caitlyn Jenner falls out a window

22 Nov 2019, 22:39

I’m A Celebrity viewers couldn’t control their laughter after Caitlyn Jenner was filmed falling out of a window.

Don’t worry, it’s not as dramatic as it sounds – although the clip does get funnier the more times you watch it.

The Olympic champion was taking part in a bushtucker trial with her fellow campmate James Haskell, which saw the duo take on an escape room challenge filled with critters.

After successfully unlocking the code to get to the money, the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star could be heard telling her buddy: ‘Let’s get the money and get out of here.’

After stuffing the coins into her bag, the A-listers ran towards the window where they hoped to make a graceful escape.

However, it wasn’t graceful at all, as despite the rugby player giving the 70-year-old a leg up, she still managed to fall from the windowsill.

Talking about the moment, the sportsman said: ‘We rummaged it like a couple of proper burglars, got the dingo dollars…’

Trying not to laugh, he added: ‘Caitlyn fell out of the window… And I followed.’

Back in the studio, Ant and Dec remained straight-faced as they told the viewers at home: ‘If you’re the kind of person who thinks watching Caitlyn Jenner fall out of a window is funny, then you’ll enjoy this action replay.’

After watching the clip once more, Dec said: ‘It’s funnier the second time around.’

However, he was keen to reassure everyone that Caitlyn was absolutely fine despite her slip.

Taking to Twitter, fans revealed how they’d been left in hysterics over the iconic moment.

‘I’m crying at Caitlyn Jenner falling out that window,’ one wrote while another added: ‘Caitlyn falling out the window has got to be my highlight of this series so far tv gold.’

Someone else said: ‘Replayed Caitlyn falling out the window like 5 times, thought I got over it, then they replayed it and I’m laughing all over again.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! continues tomorrow at 9pm on ITV.
