Ohio State vs. Penn State Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

22 Nov 2019, 22:35 by Andrew McCarty

Arguably the biggest game of the college football weekend will kick off from Columbus on Saturday afternoon.

No. 2 Ohio State hosts No. 8 Penn State in the biggest game of the Big Ten season thus far. The Buckeyes have a clear path to the College Football Playoff, if they can take down the Nittany Lions tomorrow. However, Penn State can insert itself back into the playoff conversation with a huge upset win over Ohio State as well.

Ryan Day and company are the No. 2 team in the nation, but rank first in offense and defense. They can finally prove they’re the top team in the country by demolishing a very capable Penn State team.

Here’s everything you need to know about the Big Ten clash:

Game Time: 12:00 p.m. ET

Channel: FOX

Spread: Ohio State -18.5

Over/Under: 57.5

Announcers: Gus Johnson, Joel Klatt, Jenny Taft

Ohio State enters as a heavy favorite. However, if the last three games between these two teams are any indication, it should be closer than the experts think.

The Buckeyes have won each of the past two games by a total of two points. Penn State took down Ohio State in 2016, by just three points.

Can the Nittany Lions keep this one close?
