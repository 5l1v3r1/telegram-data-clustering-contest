WWE cancels tonight's episode of 205 Live

22 Nov 2019, 22:21 by Gary Cassidy

WWE have announced that tonight's scheduled airing of 205 Live has been cancelled, and confirmed that the WWE Network will instead show a repeat of this week's episode of NXT following SmackDown instead.

The official statement was released less than four hours before SmackDown takes place live from Chicago, and less than six hours before 205 Live would have been scheduled to air live on the WWE Network.

No reason for the cancellation has been given thus far.
