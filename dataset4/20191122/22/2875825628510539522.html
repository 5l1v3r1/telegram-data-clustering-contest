I'm A Celebrity's Kate Garraway has viewers in tears after astonishing innuendo

Kate Garraway made an offer in the jungle that was enough to bring blushes to the cheeks of the male campmates in I'm A Celebrity after Andrew Maxwell began choking

22 Nov 2019, 22:51 by Irishmirror.ie

Kate Garraway has brought tears to the eyes of viewers at home after she made an astonishing offer in the I'm A Celebrity camp.

The 52-year-old Good Morning Britain presenter had just helped comedian Andrew Maxwell from choking on camel steak, when she propositioned him with an extraordinary offer.

The pair were part of the group at the Jailhouse, who were celebrating soap star Jacqueline Jossa winning them a full meal alongside with former X Factor winner, Myles Stephenson.

There was drama though, as Andrew was so pleased to be eating a meal after rice and beans and basic rations, that he seemed to shovel it into his mouth at a rate of knots.

A piece of camel meat then got lodged in his throat, dropping him to his knees as the fellow campmates rushed to his assistance.

After he was saved from a brush with death, former EastEnders star Cliff Parisi offered to chew Andrew's food first to save him from suffering like that again.

Kate Garraway then piped in, offering: "I can chew your meat anytime you need!"

Realising her faux-pas, Kate quickly tried to retract that offer, saying that it had come out wrong but viewers at home were already in hysterics at the classic innuendo.

Twitter was flooded with those watching who wanted to share their delight at Kate's offer.

One viewer tweeted: "'I can eat your meat any time you need' God help us!!!! Hilarious, @kategarraway #imaceleb2019"

Another wrote: "@kategarraway I will chew on your meat anytime made me actually made me cry with laughter #imaceleb2019"

A third tweeted: "'I can chew your meat anytime you need' Kate Garraway you wee minx"

While Kate may be away from the main camp, she has certainly leaped up the rankings as to who will win I'm A Celebrity, as viewers have warmed to her personality.
