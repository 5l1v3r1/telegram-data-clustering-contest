South Africa’s ‘KFC couple’ will get married on New Year’s Eve

22 Nov 2019, 08:33 by Shereesa Moodley

South Africans are once again fawning over the couple that got engaged at a KFC a few weeks ago after the pair announced their second wedding date.

Fondly known as “The National Couple”, Hector Mkansi and Nonhlanhla Soldaat first made headlines when KFC tweeted a video of the former getting down on one knee at a branch.

“Mzansi please help us find this beautiful couple,” the company tweeted in hopes of getting in touch with “a finger-lickin’ Good surprise”.
