No matter who you have on your holiday shopping list, there’s no better time to shop and no better place to do it than next weekend at Metropolis at Metrotown.

From November 29 – December 1, the beloved retail destination will host their annual Black Friday event, complete with huge savings and extended hours all weekend long. With 375 stores and several retailers offering early bird door crasher specials, bargain hunters won’t need to go far to find the best deals of the holiday season.

To celebrate the true beginning of holiday shopping, we’ve partnered with Metropolis at Metrotown to  award one lucky shopper a $500 gift card. You can enter via the contest form below:

The $500 gift card can be used at most of Metropolis’ stores, including stores such as H&M, Hudson’s Bay, Indigo, Old Navy, Sport Chek, Toys “R” Us, Uniqlo, Urban Behavior, Tommy Hilfiger, Michael Kors, Coach, RYU, Aritzia, Walmart, Winners HomeSense, and more!

Want to line up early for your favourite deals? Early morning Black Friday mall access will be available through the entrance closest to Customer Service Ground Level, near NYX Professional Makeup, which will open at 6:00 am on November 29th. All other mall entrances will open at 6:30 am.

Select retailers are opening early for Black Friday! For more information on Black Friday Weekend and for a complete list of all participating retailers, hours and deals please visit metropolisatmetrotown.com.

Black Friday Weekend at Metropolis at Metrotown

When: November 29th – December 1st (Friday: 8:00am to 10:00pm, Saturday: 10:00am to 9:00pm, Sunday: 10:00am to 7:00pm)

Where: Metropolis at Metrotown, 4700 Kingsway, Burnaby
