NBA roundup: Giannis Antetokounmpo leads Milwaukee Bucks to sixth straight win

A triple-double from Giannis Antetokounmpo powered Milwaukee Bucks to a sixth straight win as they beat Portland Trail Blazers 137-129 on Thursday night.

22 Nov 2019, 08:10

Last season's MVP collected 24 points, 19 rebounds and a career-high 15 assists while Eric Bledsoe scored 30 points for the Bucks, who won for the 10th time in 11 games.
