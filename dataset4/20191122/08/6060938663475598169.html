'Housefull 4' box office collection Week 4: Akshay Kumar's multi-starrer continues to grow giving 'Bala' and 'Marjaavaan' a run for their money

22 Nov 2019, 08:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Akshay Kumar's multi-starrer 'Housefull 4' has been doing an exceptionally good at the box office and giving its contemporaries a run for their money. The film which recently entered the 200 crore club has been continuing to prove its mettle at the ticket counter in the fourth week as well.

According to the latest report on Boxofficeindia.com, the comic caper film has managed to mint another Rs 3.85 crore in its fourth week in the domestic circuit, giving a tough competition to 'Bala' and 'Marjaavaan'. The Fahad Samji directorial which collected Rs 135.86 crore, Rs 48.07 crore and Rs 16.27 crore in its previous weeks respectively and now the film stands strong at the box office with Rs 204.05 crore in total.

'Housefull 4' which also stars Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde in the lead roles released along with Rajkummar Rao's 'Made In China' and Taapsee Pannu-Bhumi Pednekar's 'Saand Ki Aankh' but managed to dominate them and continued its impressive run for weeks.

Meanwhile, with 'Good Newwz' also set to release this year, Akshay might become the first Bollywood actor to collect Rs 1000 crore in a single year.
