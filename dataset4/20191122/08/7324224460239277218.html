Boxers chasing British glory

Brooklands ABC duo Kian McDermott and James Willis aim to box clever this weekend when they continue their push for glory in the NAGBC’s Championships.

22 Nov 2019, 08:46

The teenagers from the Woodsetton-based club have already been crowned Midlands champions and are now just one fight away from a shot at the British title.

Lions BC’s Osama Mohamed will also be in action in Solihull as he chases a second national accolade.
