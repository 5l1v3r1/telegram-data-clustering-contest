Bigg Boss 13: Sidharth Shukla And Asim Riaz Are Fighting But Netizens Are Feeling Sorry For Rashami Desai

22 Nov 2019, 08:08 by Amruta Patel

After Twitter stood divided into two groups - one supporting Bigg Boss 13's Sidharth Shukla and the other Asim Riaz, now Rashami Desai’s fans too have sprung into action. #StayStrongRashami is trending on the microblogging site. Users are appreciating Rashami for her fair play

With the ongoing chaos in the Bigg Boss 13 house, due to housemates Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz constant fallouts, one of the strongest contenders in the house Rashami Desai, at times suffers because of these altercations. After #StayStrongSidharth and #AsimWinningHearts trends, comes yet another hashtag, this time for Rashami from her Twitter followers, #StayStrongRashami. Despite her team winning the task, her team is in disagreement to let her be the captain of the house, ultimately Rashami is looking out for support in the house to save herself from being evicted. 

While Rashami won’t be aware of it, darling you don’t have to worry if you are supported by the housemates, your social media fans are in complete support of you. Many of her Twitter fans, emphasizing with Rashami asked to stay strong and are taking potshots at the broadcaster, Colors TV in their Tweets for being biased.
