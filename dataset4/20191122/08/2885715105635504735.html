Watch: Sikkim teacher’s viral performance of ‘Malhaari’ for Children’s Day

The teacher, who was identified as Tshering Doma Bhutia, is a post-graduate teacher and teaches political science and culture in the Melli Senior Secondary School in South Sikkim.

22 Nov 2019, 08:15 by Trends Desk

A teacher’s amazing dance performance for her students on the occasion of Children’s Day is breaking the internet. The teacher, who dances to Ranveer Singh’s popular number ‘Malhaari‘ from the film Bajirao Mastani, has impressed everyone with her incredible moves.

The teacher, who was identified as Tshering Doma Bhutia, is a post-graduate teacher and teaches political science and culture in the Melli Senior Secondary School in South Sikkim.

The video shows her pull off the complex steps with relative east and by the end of the performance even her students joined her.
