Latest job vacancies

22 Nov 2019, 08:18 by Olushola Odeyinde

Our medium is one of Nigeria’s foremost Online publishing platforms. It is currently
expanding its frontiers with the launch of an online health vertical, which is envisaged to
become Nigeria’s go-to website in health sector coverage. We have therefore declared the
following vacancies open:

1. Assistant Editor, Health (2 persons)
Functional responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
 Research and write stories
 Manage reporters on specific assignments
 Edit articles
 Determine a story’s emphasis, length, and format, and organise material
accordingly
 Work under the direction of a Senior Editor as assigned
 Attend regular editorial meetings
 Other duties as assigned

Requirements
 At least 10 years of proven work experience as a Health Writer, or similar role
 Portfolio of published articles
 Strong, proven editing ability, involving writing, line editing, substantive editing,
copyediting, tone, and headline creation in English
 Knowledge of health and wellness
 Hands-on experience with Content Management Systems (e.g. WordPress)
 Ability to fact-check long-form content pieces
 Time-management skills
 Familiarity with SEO

Education/Experience/Language requirements
 To be successful in this role, you should have experience with health reporting
and leads for investigative stories
 First degree or equivalent

2. Investigative Reporters: Three (3) persons
Responsibilities:
 News hunting: Makes plans for special coverage of news
 Special Reporting: S/he will engage in in-depth and investigative reporting of health
sector, as it requires experience and expertise
 To mobilize reporters and see the activities are covered properly and reporters are
missing nothing
 Review copy and correct errors in content, grammar, and punctuation, following
prescribed editorial style and formatting guidelines

 Comparative study of competitors: S/he analyses the works of competitors and is
fully aware of their activities to make for further improvement in their work.

Education/Experience/Language requirements

 At least eight (8) years of active investigative journalism (in addition to
the above)

3. Curators and aggregators (2 persons)
Responsibilities:
 Gather information for news or human-interest stories on health issues
 Utilise several sources to gain different perspectives and facts
 Scour the internet for valuable content
 Ability to read social media posts thoroughly and create short stories for our
website

Education/Experience/Language requirements
 One or two years of job experience
 Ability to speak and write English Language fluently
 Social media savvy

4. Reporters (Four persons)
Responsibilities:

 In-depth researching and reporting on the health beat
 Receiving assignments and evaluating leads and tips in order to develop story
ideas
 Discuss issues with editors in order to establish priorities and positions

Education/Experience/Language requirements
At least eight (8) years of on-the-job experience (In addition to same as above)

5. Multimedia & Infographics (2 persons)
Duties and responsibilities:
 Creating multimedia designs in liaison with animators, programmers, writers,
video producers, sound engineers and artists

Education/Experience/Language requirements
 At least two years of on-the-job experience
 Familiarity with relevant software

6. Developers (2 persons)
Writing well designed, testable, efficient code by using best software development
practices. Creating website layout/user interfaces by using standard HTML/CSS
practices. Integrating data from various back-end services and databases.

7. Copy Editors (Two persons)
This job requires candidates to be able to perform duties that include the following:
 Proofread text and correct spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors
 Verify factual correctness of information, such as dates and statistics
 Check text for style, readability, and adherence to editorial policies
 Arrange page layouts of photos, articles, and advertisements
 Rewrite text to improve clarity and readability
 Computer literacy
