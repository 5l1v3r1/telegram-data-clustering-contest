Tickets to see Harry Styles, The Killers and Bryan Ferry are on sale today - here's how to get them

Tickets are also dropping today for Katie Melua, Ant Middleton, Barenaked Ladies, Thunder and Dermot Kennedy

22 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Linda Howard

Forget all the hype about Black Friday, Scotland is playing host to more talent than you can shake an Amazon Fire Stick at over the next few months.

Tickets go on sale today for a whole trolley-load of talent including Harry Styles, Bryan Ferry, The Killers, Katie Melua, Barenaked Ladies, Ant Middleton, Thunder and Dermot Kennedy .

And Monday sees the start of presales on tickets to see Scottish rockers, Texas, celebrate the 30th anniversary of their debut album 'Southside' with a special show at the SSE Hydro in Glasgow next October - full details here .

 

You'll find everything you need to know about dates, venues and tickets for more than 50 shows coming to Scotland below.
