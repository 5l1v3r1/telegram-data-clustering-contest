Sona Mohapatra reacts to Anu Malik’s exit from Indian Idol 11; composer says he is on a three-week break

Anu Malik, who has been accused of sexual misconduct by multiple women, met the creators of Sony TV's show Indian Idol 11 and told them about his decision of leaving his position of judge on the show.

22 Nov 2019, 08:18 by Entertainment Desk

After Anu Malik stepped down as the judge of singing reality show Indian Idol 11, singer Sona Mohapatra thanked everyone who supported her in her campaign “#MoveOutMalik”. But, Malik on Friday said he will return to the show after clearing his name and is only on a three-week break.

Sona tweeted on Thursday, “Thank you to all the women & men & the media of India who supported our campaign #MoveOutMalik . A repeat offender like Anu Malik on national tv triggered a lot of trauma, pain & stress in many of our lives. I have been ill for a while & I hope to sleep well tonight.”
