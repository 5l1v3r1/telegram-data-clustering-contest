Meme game: Here's what desi Twitterati will tell their kids and so should you

22 Nov 2019, 08:25 by Entertainment Desk

Now, we’ve all seen those “I’m going to tell my kids” memes flooding social media. It’s become evident that the current generation plans on feeding their children with hilarious misrepresentations of the world as it is right now, and well, have a lot of them.

We are talking about the generation that came up with memes in the first place; the generation that coined the terms ‘nibbas’ and ‘nibbis’, the very generation that gave a whole new definition to Iqbal’s Shaheens and made sure Pooja behaved. So there’s no denying that this generation is bound to twist history more than there forefathers ever could with their history books.

Let’s just take a look at how misinformed our future generations are going to be.
