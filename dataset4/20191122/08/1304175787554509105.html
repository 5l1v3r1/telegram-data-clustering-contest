New gem in the crown of the Lotte Hotels & Resorts Group

22 Nov 2019, 08:52 by Tatiana Rokou

Lotte Hotel Moscow features first-class dining options showcasing internationally renowned brand names.
 

DUBAI, UNITED ARAB EMIRATES – The five-star property conveniently located on Novinskiy Boulevard - the heart of Moscow’s financial and shopping districts - offers guests 300 spacious rooms and suites.

Apart from distinguished accommodation and facilities, Lotte Hotel Moscow features first-class dining options showcasing internationally renowned brand names. Among them, two superb restaurants – OVO restaurant by the world-famous Michelin-starred chef and TV-star of the culinary reality show “Hell`s Kitchen” Carlo Cracco, and MEGUmi, a Japanese fusion restaurant. 

Furthermore, the hotel features a Wedding & Event Bureau – Russia’s first and only luxury hotel event boutique, capable of accommodating banquets and business events of any size and format. The hotel also houses an innovative club lounge for business travelers, the world-famous Mandara Spa, a Fitness Club and a state-of-the-art swimming pool. 

The ten-story building also boasts substantial underground parking and close proximity to main city sights and entertainment. Extensive facilities coupled with Lotte Hotel’s outstanding client-oriented service aimed at anticipating guests’ needs are certain to exceed your expectations.

Lotte Hotel Moscow belongs to Korea’s largest luxury hotel chain, Lotte Hotels & Resorts, which operates 30 properties throughout the largest cities and regions of South Korea

Seoul and its suburbs Mapo and Gimpo, Ulsan, Busan, Buyeo, Guro, Daejeon, Myeongdong and on Jeju Island, as well as across Vietnam, Uzbekistan, Myanmar, Japan, and America (New York and Guam island). The chain was founded in 1972 with the construction of Lotte Hotel Seoul, Korea’s first world-class hotel. Exhibiting excellence in every detail, Lotte Hotels now provide an opulence which places them in line with the best-rated international properties. For more than 35 years, Lotte Hotels have been operating on the basic principle of being a home away from home, so comfortable that guests wish to return time and time again. Lotte Hotel Seoul, the flagship of South Korean hotels, is an award-winning property, voted Best Individual Hotel in Asia by Global Traveler, prestigious international travel magazine.

The rapid development of Lotte Hotels in Asia will continue, setting the highest standards of excellence and allowing guests to experience genuine Korean warmth and hospitality while overseas. In 2011 Lotte cut the ribbon on a luxurious hotel in Shenyang, China, and a business hotel in Gimpo. At the end of 2013, the first Lotte Hotel was opened in the Middle East. The following year will see four new hotels added in Jamsil, Busan, Gimhae, and Seocho-dong (Seoul). As such, Lotte Hotels are building an international network and in doing so, are taking a leap to become a truly global hotel chain representing Korea.
Lotte Hotels & Resorts are run by management company - Lotte Group, South Korea’s largest conglomerate. It consists of over 60 affiliated companies employing more than 60,000 people. engaged in such diverse industries as candy manufacturing, beverages, hotels, fast food, retail, financial services, heavy chemicals, electronics, IT, construction, publishing, and entertainment. The company possesses major operations in South Korea, Japan, China, the Philippines, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, India, USA, and Russia and continues to expand.  

With regards to Russia, Lotte Group has been represented by subsidiary Lotte Rus since 1997. Development started in October 2002 with the construction of Lotte Plaza, deluxe shopping mall and business centre, which opened in September 2007 on Novinskiy Boulevard. The department store - housing the world’s best brands in sumptuous surroundings – and the first- class business centre are manifestations of state-of-the-art Korean facilities and service. The million-dollar Lotte Hotel Moscow has also joined this impressive portfolio, along with a confectionery plant in Kaluga city. "We are not going to stop here but will proceed with expanding our retail and tourist activities in Russia. Lotte Hotels & Resorts opened a new hotel in St. Petersburg in September 2017, our next hotel was opened in the city of Samara in May 2018, and the next opening took place in July 2018 in the city of Vladivostok" says Lotte Rus General Director.
