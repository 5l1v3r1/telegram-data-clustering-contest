Elsa Pataky says Liam Hemsworth 'deserves more' after split from Miley Cyrus

22 Nov 2019, 08:49

Liam Hemsworth’s sister-in-law Elsa Pataky has claimed the actor ‘deserves more’ following his split from Miley Cyrus.

Liam, 29, and Miley, 26, confirmed in August that they had split after eight months of marriage and 10 years of dating on and off.

While The Hunger Games star Liam went back to Australia to spend time with his big brother Chris, Miley embarked on two new relationships – first with Kaitlynn Carter, then with Cody Simpson.

And Chris’s wife Elsa has made it clear she’s on Team Liam.

According to Hola! magazine, the 43-year-old told press at the Madrid launch of Women’s Secret’s KissMas Time range: ‘My brother in law, well, after a relationship where he dedicated 10 years, he’s discouraged … but he’s taking it well.

‘He’s a strong guy and he deserves the best. I think he deserves much more.’

Elsa – who has three kids with Avengers star Chris – went on to discuss how the Hemsworths had been supporting Liam during the rough time.

She said: ‘Family is always there to protect you. He and his brother are so close and he’s really been there for him to give him the strength he needs.’

Elsa reportedly isn’t the only Hemsworth with hard feelings towards Liam’s ex-wife, with a source previously telling Hollywood Life that the star’s mum Leonie never approved of the Wrecking Ball singer.

They said: ‘His mom is feeling so relieved that Liam made the decision to end the relationship as his entire family, but her especially, always struggled approving Miley.’

Earlier this year, Miley’s rep confirmed: ‘Liam and Miley have agreed to separate at this time.

‘Ever-evolving, changing as partners and individuals, they have decided this is what’s best while they both focus on themselves and careers.

‘They still remain dedicated parents to all of their animals they share while lovingly taking this time apart. Please respect their process and privacy.’

The couple had only tied the knot in December 2018, in an intimate ceremony at their home.

While Miley packed on the PDA with Kaitlynn, Liam retreated to Byron Bay, where he was spotted surfing and spending time with his big brother.

However, the actor seems to be moving on, having been pictured on a date with Dynasty actress Maddison Brown.
