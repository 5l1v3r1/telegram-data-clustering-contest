Amazon launches amazing deal on the Echo Dot for Black Friday

The retailer has discounts on Echo Dots, Fire TV Sticks and Kindles

22 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Jonathan Yates

Amazon has launched its Black Friday sale with big discounts on its own devices.

The online retailer has kicked off its sale a week ahead of the official day of deals on November 29.

Customers can get their hands on cheaper Kindles, Fire Sticks and more as its week-long sale officially starts.

This includes discounts on a number of its Echo models including more than half price off the Echo Dot which is now £22 in the sale.

The Echo Dot with a clock which is priced at £34.99 (was £59.99) and the Echo Show 5, which comes with a screen, is priced at £49.99 (was £79.99).

 

Other newly launched deals including the 2019 edition of the Kindle which is priced at £54.99 reduced from £69.99, or the Kindle Paperwhite which is priced at £84.99 with a saving of £35.

Meanwhile the Fire TV Stick with Alexa Voice Remote is priced at £19.99 (was £39.99) and the Fire TV Stick 4K is priced at £29.99 (was £49.99).

To see all of the Amazon Black Friday deals go to amazon.co.uk.

The retailer is expected to roll out more deals across the next week, with Friday, November 29 being the biggest day for discounted products.

Some of the most popular items include TVs, vacuum cleaners and top toy of the year the Nintendo Switch, so if you're after something in particular keep an eye out on Amazon's daily deals.

Plus other stores includes Curry's PC World, Very and Littlewoods are also taking part in Black Friday with discounts on electronics and more.
