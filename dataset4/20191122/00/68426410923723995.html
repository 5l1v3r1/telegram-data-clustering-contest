Badger Daylighting insiders buy after bad news

Ideally, we want to see insider buying as that suggests market reaction may be overdone and that the depressed stock price could represent a buying opportunity. This is what we have seen at Badger Daylighting Ltd. after the company lowered 2019 adjusted EBITDA guidance on Nov. 5. Insiders have spent $1.2-million buying shares in the public market since the bad news was announced.
