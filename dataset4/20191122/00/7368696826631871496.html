All you need to do to grab Serial Cleaner is head on over to this link (create a free Humble Store account if you don’t have one already) and claim the game for free. You do need to sign up to the Humble Bundle newsletter to grab the game, but the newsletter isn’t spammy at all so it isn’t a big deal.

Created by indie developer Draw Distance, Serial Cleaner is a story-driven, fast-paced stealth/action game that has seen some great reviews and currently sits at a “Very Postive” user review rating on Steam. The game will normally set you back over R150. Grab it for free before 23 November 2019 at 20:00 SAST and it will be yours to keep and play forever.

If you really need more convincing to grab Serial Cleaner, check out a description and trailer below.

Serial Cleaner is a story-driven, fast-paced stealth/action game, set in 1970s America. You are a professional cleaner. Your job is to clean up murder scenes by disposing of bodies, cleaning up blood stains and hiding incriminating evidence. A good cleaner never gets caught so you have to make sure you get in, clean up and get out without leaving a trace of evidence for the police to find.

Game Features:

  • 70s-inspired Setting and Funky Soundtrack
  • Real-World Data to modify levels according to the current time of day in the player’s whereabouts
  • Full story-driven campaign
  • Challenge modes
