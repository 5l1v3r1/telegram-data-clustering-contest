Baileys release chocolate reindeer 'mug' to make the ultimate indulgent Christmas drink

22 Nov 2019 by Rachael O'Connor

WE CAN feel the Christmas weight piling on already.

Juts when you think Baileys can't possibly come up with any more ideas-- we already have Baileys Strawberry & Cream, Baileys Chocolat Luxe, Baileys Almond, Baileys Salted Caramel and Baileys Yule Log-- the drinks company goes ahead and creates thick milk chocolate reindeer 'mugs'.

If you're like me, you're thinking you'll probably never drink Bailey's any other way again, but unfortunately these limited edition reindeers are only available for the Christmas period.

Which, as hard as it is to admit, is probably for the best.

The company suggest you can make the most of your chocolate reindeer by filling it with 50ml of Baileys before smothering it with whipped cream, chocolate sprinkles-- and the most important part, the golden paper straws which make up the antlers.

The mouthwatering chocolate reindeer is a collaboration between Baileys Irish Cream and Lir Chocolates, an Irish chocolate brand who are already well-known for making delectable Baileys chocolate treats.

If you're just dying to try this scrumptious creation, you can pick up your very own chocolate reindeer at Tesco in Ireland or Asda in the UK-- but as they're exclusive to these two stores you won't find them anywhere else.

But if you don't manage to get your hands on one, don't despair-- check out some of these deliciously indulgent Baileys recipes instead!
