Arsenal, the latest team to be interested in Haaland

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

One after the other, more interested parties for Erling Haaland follow one another. At the moment, the player's father has gone to Arsenal to visit their facilities.

One more to choose from. A very busy summer awaits Salzburg striker, Erling Haaland. With the possibility of staying at the Austrian team practically ruled out, the player will now have to choose which is the best option for him.

The footballer's father does not want his son to rush and to make a decision lightly and that's why he is being a key figure in the professional development of the Norweigan footballer.
