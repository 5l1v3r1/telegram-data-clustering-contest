Campaign Infiltration

22 Nov 2019, 00:09 by BRIEF

COLUMBUS, Ohio – A federal court in Ohio denied the nonprofit investigative journalists’ motion for a preliminary injunction in a dispute with the state’s election commission, finding that an anti-infiltration statute is constitutional even though it prohibits journalists from gaining information on political candidates by making undercover recordings.
