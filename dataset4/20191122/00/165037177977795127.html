Suarez already sees Liverpool as Premier League champions!

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

FC Barcelona striker and former Liverpool player, Luis Suarez, this Friday said that for him it is "difficult" to imagine Liverpool letting the Premier League title escape them.

"Winning the Premier League is difficult because the season is very long. But at this time, they have a good advantage," the 33-year-old Uruguayan commented to 'ESPN FC' during a presention of branded boots that he sponsors.

"But we all know what football is like. Last season was a similar advantage at one point and, in the end, Manchester City won the title (in the last game)," he continued.

Suarez, who played for Liverpool for three and a half seasons (he arrived in the winter market from Ajax for 26.5 million euros), was very close to getting Liverpool their first league title since 1990, a little before signing for Barcelona.

"We almost won the Premier League with young players. We almost did so without the budget that they have now. considering everything that Liverpool is at the moment, winning then would have been more of a merit," the top scorer for Uruguay stated (59 goals).
