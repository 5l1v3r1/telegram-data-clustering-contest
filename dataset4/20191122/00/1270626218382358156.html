Jale Baba changes plea to guilty in the Suva Magistrates Court

22 Nov 2019 by Rashika Kumar

Jale Baba has changed his plea from not guilty to guilty in the Suva Magistrates Court.

Baba has pleaded guilty to publishing falsified Voter Lists and other false information related to that list on Facebook last year, which he knew was untrue. 

He had denied the charge in August but had later indicated to the court through his counsel that he wanted to change his plea. 

The matter has been adjourned to the 6th of January next year.
