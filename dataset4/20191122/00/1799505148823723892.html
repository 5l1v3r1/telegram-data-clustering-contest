Boxer’s Injury

22 Nov 2019, 00:23 by BRIEF

PASADENA, Calif. – The Ninth Circuit ruled that the lower court properly dismissed several putative class actions stemming from the Mayweather vs. Pacquiao fight. The class members claim the boxers and others concealed Pacquiao’s preexisting injury and that they would not have purchased tickets to the event had they known about the injury.
