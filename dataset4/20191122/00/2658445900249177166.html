Efe Nathan, Bidemi, other top gospel stars for BOW

22 Nov 2019, 00:32

By Adeola Ogunlade

Pop gospel stars, Efe Nathan, Bidemi Olaoba, Dare Justified, 9Tingale, Yemi Olufemi also known as Ypiono among others are set to perform at the 8th edition of Best of Worship (BOW) slated for today at the Redeemed Christian Church of God, (RCCG) l, Redemption Close, China Town, Lagos.

Others guest artistes are Bada, Samuel Essien and Miaor Ese.

According to the convener of BOW, Pastor Paul Mashote, Best of Worship is an international musical concert with a specific vision of creating a platform of undiluted worship.

“The phenomenal annual worship gathering has recorded hundreds of worshipers from within and outside the shore of Nigeria,” he said.

He noted that over the years, BOW has featured music artistes like Pastor Kunle Ajayi, Pastor Kenny Koore, Gbenga Salako and Frank Edward.
