Dr. Sid to host Beatz Awards - The Nation Newspaper

22 Nov 2019, 00:28

By Sampson Unamka

Come this Sunday, Mavin records Singer, Dr Sid will host the fifth edition of the Beatz Awards which is created to celebrate the unsung heroes behind the music scene.

The award which holds by 5pm in the Shell Hall of MUSON Centre, Onikan, Lagos, aims to celebrate creative professionals in the entertainment industry in 29 categories.

The chief Idea Merchant of The Beatz Awards, Elijah John, said that the award will be “a memorable night celebrating an incredible year of triumphs in the careers of our dear professionals working behind the scene.”

This year’s edition has 25 voting categories and four honorary Awards (Non-voting). It is produced by Eliworld International Limited and supported by MTN.
