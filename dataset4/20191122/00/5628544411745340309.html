Trailer drops for Shia LaBeouf’s Honey Boy

22 Nov 2019 by Ryan Adams

Trailer for Amazon’s Honey Boy appears a day after it receives 4 Independent Spirit Award nominations:

BEST DIRECTOR – Alma Har’el
BEST SUPPORTING MALE – Shia LaBeouf
BEST SUPPORTING MALE – Noah Jupe
BEST CINEMATOGRAPHY – Natasha Braier

“From a screenplay by Shia LaBeouf, based on his own experiences, award-winning filmmaker Alma Har’el brings to life a young actor’s stormy childhood and early adult years as he struggles to reconcile with his father through cinema and dreams. Fictionalizing his childhood’s ascent to stardom, and subsequent adult crash-landing into rehab and recovery.”
