Billie Eilish scores her first Number 1 on the Official Irish Singles Chart with Everything I Wanted

Lewis Capaldi's Before You Go debuts just outside the Top 10 with just two and a half days of sales.

22 Nov 2019 by Jack White

Billie Eilish is Number 1 on the Official Irish Singles Chart for the very first time.

The 17-year-old phenomenon charges to Number 1 this week with Everything I Wanted. Released midweek last week, the song charted outside the Top 50 at Number 53 last Friday based on two days of sales.

Everything I Wanted is Billie’s fifth Top 10 single on Irish soil and joins When The Party’s Over (7), Bury A Friend (2), Wish You Were Gay (6) and Bad Guy (2) as top flight hits.

Last week’s Number 1 Dua Lipa narrowly misses out on a third week at the top with Don’t Start Now at Number 2.
