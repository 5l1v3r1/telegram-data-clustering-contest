U-23 AFCON: Ghana's Starting Line Up Against South Africa

22 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Ghana's U-23 coach Ibrahim Tanko made just one change in his starting lineup against South Africa in the third place game of the U-23 AFCON.

Kwabena Owusu returns to the Black Meteors starting lineup as he replaces Robin Polley. The Cordoba striker was benched in the two previous games against Mali and Cote d'Ivoire. Owusu has been a key figure for the Black Meteors side in the competition and Coach Tanko will rely on his experience and quality in this afternoon's game.

The Black Meteors are in search of a win in today's game to guarantee them a place in the Tokyo 2020 Olympic football tournament.
