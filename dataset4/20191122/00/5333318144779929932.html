SIF19 @ #COP25

22 Nov 2019 by Staff reporter

We are media partners with the upcoming Sustainable Innovation Forum 2019 (SIF19) in Madrid – a key side event of COP25. Accelerating Solutions for Climate Action is the tagline of the event, which will bring together public and private stakeholders keen to forge new impactful partnerships across a range of sectors, including in clean power, where the race is on to fast-track the transition to smarter, cleaner energy systems.

Our Editor-at-large Marc Buckley and contributing climate columnist, Eva Keretic, will be at the Forum. Get a 15% discount to attend the event on 10 and 11 at the Novotel Madrid Campo de las Naciones by using the code InnovatorsMag15 at the checkout to redeem this special delegate pass.
