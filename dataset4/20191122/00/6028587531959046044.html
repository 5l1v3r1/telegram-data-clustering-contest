Here’s GameStop’s Black Friday 2019 Ad: Big Deals On Nintendo Switch, PS4, Xbox One, ‘Anthem’

22 Nov 2019 by Dave Thier

Video Games are a big part of Black Friday, and there’s still no US video game retailer quite like GameStop. It’s always a major part of Black Friday and a big destination for people picking up new video game hardware or software, and this year it’s kept its ad until the very last moment. But here it is, for your perusing pleasure. There are some execellent deals in the mix here, some we’ve seen elsewhere but solid nonetheless. For the full ad, go to www.GameStop.com/BlackFriday.For one thing, Anthem is on sale. Note to the moderately curious: maybe don’t buy Anthem, even at $5.

Doorbusters: Some good stuff in here, with those retailer-specific cashback bonuses that can sweeten even standard deals. You can get store credit for a Nintendo Switch Lite, and there are precious few deals on that thing this season. There are also some solid software deals, as well as a super cheap Xbox One. A refurbished one, naturally, but that’s fine if we’re being honest. Here are the doorbusters:
