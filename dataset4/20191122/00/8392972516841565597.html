Soap SPOILERS: Here's what's happening in tonight's Emmerdale, Coronation Street and EastEnders

No one's very happy

22 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Carena Crawford

Tonight viewers can watch all their favourite soaps with the regular Friday outings to Emmerdale, a Coronation Street double, and EastEnders.

Rhona's not happy in the Dales, Martin's not happy on the Square, and Paul isn't happy in Weatherfield.

Here are the eight biggest storylines from the soaps on Friday November 22.

Emmerdale, ITV, 7pm

1. Rhona and Marlon clash

Rhona is concerned when she finds out Graham tried to speak to Marlon about seeing Leo.

Marlon's not at all happy about it, but will Rhona give him what for?

2. Liam is dimissive

Following his arrest for hitting Jacob, Liam is dismissive over the charges.

Will he go to prison for assault? And is his future with Bernice in jeopardy?

Coronation Street, ITV, 7.30pm and 8.30pm

1. Paul confesses the truth about Kel

As Paul and Gemma realise they may have been wrong about Bernie and apologise to her, Gemma and Bernie realise Kel also had access to Billy's diary.

They break into his flat and find Daniel's laptop - as well as a stack of old photos of Paul and some other kids. Gemma quickly realises their significance.

Paul is horrified when they return with the laptop and the photos and realises they have discovered his past.

He emotionally recounts his childhood experiences with Kel, and reveals Kel sexually abused him.

Gemma wants Paul to go to the police, but it's too painful and he refuses.

2. Beth comforts Daniel

Daniel wants to make a start on cleaning the flat after the robbery, but it's too much when he finds Sinead's broken mug on the floor and he breaks down.

Beth calls at the flat and offers him comfort as he sobs in her arms.

Later, Peter and Carla take Bertie so Daniel can have some 'me time', but they're concerned when he returns to No.1, worse for wear and highly strung.

3. Michael tries to see his daughter

Michael tells his parents about seeing his ex-girlfriend and daughter, but is stunned when Grace turns up at the door.

She tells him their little girl needs a responsible dad and Michael insists it can be him. Grace leaves without promising anything.

But when she later finds out Aggie has consulted a solicitor about Michael's parental rights, Grace is fuming and storms out.

Michael's left furious with his mother.

EastEnders, BBC One, 8pm

1. Martin makes a horrifying discovery

Martin feels like a weight has been lifted after Ben set him free, but it's clear Kush knows something that could upset that - what is he hiding?

After failing to get hold of Stacey to explain the whole sorry mess to her, Martin heads to the Slaters', but is shocked at what he finds...

Jean and Mo are packing Stacey's things.

And to make matters worse, he's only minutes away from making another horrifying discovery...

2. Max proposes to Ruby!

Max wants to prove to Ruby he's serious about their relationship so decides to propose.

He asks Bobby for a favour regarding the proposal, but Ian overhears and takes great joy in winding Max up.

Ian engineers it so that everyone is there to witness the proposal, and Max only realises what's happening when it's too late.

Forced to pop the question, how will Ruby react?

3. Things are on the up for Callum and Ben

Callum is thrilled that Ben has given up crime for him.

Ben thanks Kathy for making him see sense, and mother and son share a tender moment.
