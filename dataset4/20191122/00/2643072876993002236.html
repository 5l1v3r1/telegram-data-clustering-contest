Davido Releases New Album

Super star Nigerian singer and entertainer, David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido on Friday, November 22 dropped a brand new music album.

The Afro-pop star released the album titled ‘A GOOD TIME’ to much jubilation from his fans.

Naija News reports the album has seventeen tracks, with ten of the songs being solo works of Davido, while the other seven featured collaboration with other local and international artists.

Some of those featured in the new album include Popcaan, Peruzzi, Zlatan, Naira Marley, WurlD, Summer Walker, Chris Brown, Gunna, Dremo, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie and Yonda.

Music producers that worked on the album include Shizzi, Kiddominant, Speroach Beatz, Tekno, etc.
