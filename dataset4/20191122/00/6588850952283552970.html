Actress Vicky Zugah To Marry On December 28?

22 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Ghanaian actress, Vicky Zugah has caused a stir on social media after she gave a hint about her supposed impending marriage.

A post sighted by zionfelix.net on Facebook and the actress’ WhatsApp status announced December 28 as a memorable date.

Details on the ‘Save the date’ photo were not enough to ascertain who she will get married to.

Venue for the ceremony was also unannounced, making many people question whether it is real or a promotional photo for a movie.

We cannot wait to see what this special day will offer in the life Vicky Zugah.
Zionfelix.net will not relent in giving updates about this story.
