Again, Edo State Polytechnic Expels Two Students For Having Sex In Classroom

22 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Just recently, a 300level female student at Babcock University was expelled from the university, after her sex tape got leaked on social media.

Just while the public is recovering from that news, another has occurred in Edo State Polytechnic.

Naija News learnt that two students of the Edo State Polytechnic have also been expelled after they were reportedly caught having sex in a lecture room.
