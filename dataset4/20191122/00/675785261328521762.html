How does Never Knowingly Undersold work during Black Friday?

John Lewis pledge to price match competitors all year-round is even in place during the Black Friday sale.

This means if a rival retailer drops the price on a coveted TV or designer bag, John Lewis will also lower its prices.

Some offers may not be highlighted in store so shoppers are advised to check pricing online if visiting a John Lewis & Partners branch and the retailer will honour the online price.
