Crimes, incidents of terrorism decrease in 2019 in KP: officials | Pakistan - Geo.tv

22 Nov 2019 by The News

PESHAWAR: Incidents of terrorism and crimes have recorded a decrease in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa during the current year as compared to the corresponding years, officials said on Thursday.

According to the official, during the last 10 months of 2019, there were six incidents of kidnapping for ransom reported as compared to 11 in 2018.

The incidents of kidnapping for ransom dropped by 45 per cent as compared to last year, the official said, adding, 18,011 proclaimed offenders have been arrested during the year 2019 as compared to 16,613 in the year 2018 which are 1,398 more in numbers.

Similarly, a total of 1,568 incidents of theft and 73 extortion cases occurred during the current year as compared to 1,587 theft and 75 extortion cases last year.

Likewise, 23 cases of vehicles snatching occurred during the current year as compared to 28 cases last year.

The official further said, 4,896 incidents of aerial firing were reported during the year 2019 as compared to 5,693 incidents of the year 2018.

During the current year, 72 incidents of terrorism took place as compared to 75 incidents last year which, showing a four per cent decrease in the terrorism incidents.

Furthermore, a total of 19,291.681kg narcotics (charas, heroin, ice) were recovered during the current year as compared to 16,221.632kg narcotics of last year, an increase of 3,070.049 kg more in numbers.
