Details Of What APC Discussed At Its National Caucus Meeting Emerges

22 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

APC Hold National Caucus Meeting In Abuja

Naija News has obtained details of what the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC) discussed at its national caucus meeting which held on Thursday.

It will be recalled that the APC held its national caucus meeting on Thursday at the Aso Rock Villa in Abuja.
