Why Cabot Microelectronics Stock Has Rallied 40% This Year

22 Nov 2019 by Trefis Team

Cabot Microelectronics (NASDAQ: CCMP), a supplier of polishing slurries and polishing pads used in the semiconductor industry, has seen its stock rally by over 40% this year to about $135. There are multiple factors driving the increase. While the semiconductor market has seen headwinds over the last year or so, on account of lower pricing and tepid demand, there have been some signs that things could stabilize. Moreover, demand for the company’s consumables could rise in the interim, driven by higher semiconductor content and more complex chips coming in with the advent of technologies such as 5G and AI. Separately, in late 2018, the company closed a deal to buy KMG Chemicals, a company that sells specialty chemicals for various sectors, including the semiconductor industry. The deal should allow Cabott to bolster its product portfolio and increase its geographic reach.We ‘step back’ from these recent swings to review Cabot Microelectronics’ performance over the last few years, as a context for what might come next. The context for the last few years:

A closer look At Cabot Microelectronics’ Total Revenues over the last few years and the outlook

Total Revenues for Cabot Microelectronics significantly increased from $0.6 Bil in 2018 to $1 Bil in 2019, an increase of about 76%, on account of the company’s acquisition of KMG Chemicals and the growth of its CMP pads business.

This compares with Total Revenues growth of:

  • -2.49% in 2015
  • 3.95% in 2016
  • 17.8% in 2017
  • 16.4% in 2018

We expect Total Revenues growth to be 12.7% in 2019.

A closer look At Cabot Microelectronics’ Total Expenses over the last few years and the outlook

Total Expense for Cabot Microelectronics increased from $428 million in 2018 to $975 million in 2019, an increase of 127.5%.

This compares with Total Expense growth of:

  • -3.70% in 2015
  • 4.99% in 2016
  • 10.5% in 2017
  • 7.7% in 2018

We expect Total Expense growth to be 7.7% in 2019.
