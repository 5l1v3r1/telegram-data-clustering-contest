4 minors held for torture of 12-yr-old

22 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

Four youths were arrested for torturing a 12-year-old boy under the pretext of teaching him how to swim inside Sampangiram Nagar lake near Kanteerava Stadium. The four youths pushed him into the water and recorded him while he was struggling to come outside.

Police said that among the four who have been arrested, three are minors and the fourth accused has been identified as Sandeep, 18.


They were arrested on Friday and sent to juvenile home while Sandeep was sent to judicial custody. In the video, the boy was seen crying and pleading them to leave him alone. The four are seen hitting him and throwing him inside the water.
