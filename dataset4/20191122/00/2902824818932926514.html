Rocky Valente releases 'Rebirth' EP

The four-track EP is out on Pig & Dan's ELEVATE label

22 Nov 2019 by Max Penny-Barrow

Lisbon-based producer Rocky Valente has released the 'Rebirth' EP.

The four-track EP is out on Pig & Dan's ELEVATE label. The duo saw her play at Dance Valley and snapped her up for the release.

Read this next: Pig & Dan in The Lab LA

'Rebirth' focuses on melodic techno, bringing together dreamy synths and soulful vocals on opener 'Unapologetic' and trance influences on 'Negotiation'.

The EP comes after a strong summer for the Portuguese artist, who played Beachland and Free Your Mind festival.

'Rebirth' is available digitally and on vinyl.

A: Unapologetic
B: Under The Radar
C: Background Story
D: Negotiation
