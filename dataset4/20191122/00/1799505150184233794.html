Huggin’ Bear

22 Nov 2019, 00:17 by BRIEF

NEW ORLEANS – A toy company’s trademark suit claiming Disney was not authorized to use the “Lots-O-Huggin’ Bear,” also known as “Lotso,” in the movie Toy Story 3 was properly dismissed for lack of jurisdiction, the Fifth Circuit ruled.
