Selena Gomez announces new album and release date

22 Nov 2019, 00:46

Selena Gomez has announced the release date for her latest album.

The pop star’s as-yet-untitled third record will arrive on January 10 next year, she said on Twitter.

It will be Gomez’s first album since 2015’s Revival. Gomez said: “This album is my diary from the past few years and I can’t wait for you to hear it.”
