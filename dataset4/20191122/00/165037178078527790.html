Mourinho's arrival could reactivate some star's renewals!

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Jose Mourinho's arrival has generated an earthquake in Tottenham. The signing of the Portuguese to manage the team may have caused footballers such as Eriksen, Alderweireld and Vertonghen to change their minds and now renew their contracts with the team.

Despite all the controversy he has caused, if there is one thing Mourinho guarantees in his teams, it is character and competitivity, something which footballers often like. 

The Portuguese coach has unleashed madness after his signing for Tottenham and his arrival could have an affect on the sporting level beyond imagination. 

Mauricio Pochettino's presence as the coach made a number of players reluctant to continue in the ranks of the London team, but the hiring of the Portuguese has totally changed the situation.

According to the 'Daily Mirror', Mourinho's signing has been well received by Christian Eriksen, Toby Alderweireld and Jan Vertonghen, three of the team's big players.

The information indicates that this group of players would now consider a renewal by the English team, because they think they can be important with the Portuguese and hope that this raises the level of the club.
