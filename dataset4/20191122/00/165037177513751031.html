Mourinho: Woodward one of first to congratulate me

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Tottenham appointed Jose Mourinho as their new boss on Wednesday and Manchester United chief Ed Woodward was quick to congratulate him.

Jose Mourinho revealed he received a "special" message from Manchester United executive vice-chairman Ed Woodward after being named Tottenham head coach.

Three-time Premier League title winner Mourinho was appointed by Tottenham on Wednesday, less than 12 hours on from Mauricio Pochettino's departure. Mourinho had been out of work since being sacked by United last December following a poor run of form that left the club sixth in the Premier League. Despite his acrimonious split with United, Mourinho was congratulated by Woodward for landing a new job.

"I received messages from everybody, everywhere [from my former clubs]," he told Sky Sports News.
