Attenborough honoured for ocean pollution fight

22 Nov 2019

The Queen has presented British broadcaster Sir David Attenborough with an award for his hit television nature series Blue Planet II, which raised public awareness around the world of the danger of plastic pollution in oceans.

Attenborough, who like the monarch is 93 years old, was awarded the Chatham House prize on Thursday along with the BBC Studios Natural History Unit for "the galvanising impact" of the series, the London-based international affairs think tank said.
