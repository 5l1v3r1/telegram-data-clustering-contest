Business case approved for two-way, all-day GO train service to Kitchener

It was unanimously approved by Metrolinx at their board meeting on Friday

22 Nov 2019, 21:29 by KitchenerToday Staff

Although the service is still years away, Kitchener is another step closer to two-way, all-day GO train service.

The initial business case for the extension received unanimous approval at Metrolinx's board meeting on Friday.

The hope is to have the service in place by 2025.
