Adam Cole on grueling Survivor Series, dating Britt Baker and Bullet Club reunion

22 Nov 2019, 21:17 by Joseph Staszewski

NXT champion Adam Cole is in the midst of a grueling five-day stretch that will see him wrestle three times, including at NXT TakeOver: WarGames (Saturday, 7 p.m.) and then defend his title at Survivor Series (Sunday, 7 p.m.), both live on WWE Network. The leader of the Undisputed Era took some time from his busy schedule for some Q&A with The Post’s Joseph Staszewski. The interview has been edited for brevity.

Q: How would you describe this challenging stretch that you are in the midst of, wrestling a ladder match on Wednesday, a WarGames match Saturday and then at Survivor Series Sunday?

A: That’s tough. At the same time, there is so much adrenaline and excitement going through my body that I’m just ready for the challenge. This is exactly why I got into pro wrestling, to be put in situations like this, high-profile situations night after night. I’m going to make the absolute most of it. My body may not agree with me, but mentally I’m in a really good place and really excited.

Q: Is there a way to keep your body ready as it’s going on?

A: There are little things you can do. I know this sounds really silly to say, but this is going to be difficult for me, but trying to get as much sleep as I possibly can. I’m a big yoga guy, so I’m going to be doing a ton of yoga throughout the week, whether it’s before the show or in my hotel room when I get back. Any sort of stretching and moving around, keeping the blood flowing. And besides that, ibuprofen and some prayers.

Q: What do you have planned for Monday or Tuesday when it’s over?

A: It will be a combination of laying on my couch, playing a video game or two and then catching a movie or going to get some dinner with my girlfriend [AEW star Britt Baker]. A very, very low-key day.
