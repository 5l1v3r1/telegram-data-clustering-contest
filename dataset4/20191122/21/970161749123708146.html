I'm A Celebrity's Kate offers to 'chew' Andrew's meat after he chokes on camel

22 Nov 2019, 21:44

Things got a little out of hand on I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here after Myles Stephenson almost ‘killed’ Andrew Maxwell with his cooking.

As the outlaws in the jailhouse camp tucked into their evening meal of camel fillet, Andrew ate a large part of camel and started to choke as it got stuck in his throat.

Kate Garraway and Andy Whyment came to the rescue as they started to hit the comedian on the back, and eventually they managed to get the meat out

‘Chew your food, mate you scared us,’ exclaimed Myles as Andrew thanked everyone.

Kate continued: ‘You went bright red, I was like “oh my god”.’

Andy then pleaded with Andrew ‘not to do that again’ while Cliff offered to chew his meat for him going forwards.

Kate then joked: ‘I can chew your meat anytime you need.’

The camp burst out laughing as Kate added:  ‘I didn’t mean it like that.’

‘I can’t believe I nearly killed Andrew Maxwell,’ Myles added.

Andrew responded: ‘You didn’t, I nearly killed me.’

The camp thanked Myles for cooking the food as Andrew said: ‘Let that be a lesson to you kids, chew your camel.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! airs weekdays at 9pm on ITV.
