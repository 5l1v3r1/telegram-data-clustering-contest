Nawaz Sharif to undergo more medical tests in London

22 Nov 2019, 21:30 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LONDON (Dunya News) - Former Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif will undergo further tests at a private clinic in London while his PET scan is due next week.

Family sources said that Nawaz Sharif’s PET scan will help in diagnosing the disease. Two therapies of the former premier were done at his house. The team of specialist doctors is trying to recover the health of the Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) supremo.

Meanwhile, Nawaz Sharif’s personal physician Dr Adnan expressed that it might take months to fully recover Nawaz Sharif’s health. Reports of various tests at Guy’s Hospital will be received within a week.

Family sources told that all medical facilities have been provided to Nawaz Sharif at the Avenfield House on Park Lane.
