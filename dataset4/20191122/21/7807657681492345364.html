How to beat Nydak Alpha in Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

Attack the back.

22 Nov 2019, 21:22 by Asher Madan

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an Xbox One, PC, and PlayStation 4 game that features exploration and a lot of Dark Souls-like combat. There are a lot of challenging bosses in the game, but Nydak Alpha on Dathomir is one of the worst. It's a massive, crazed, dog-like monster that packs quite a punch. You have to dodge again and again, as well as keep on hitting it from behind.

The video below tells you exactly what you need to do. Keep in mind that you'll have to dodge a number of times because Nydak Alpha does a series of sloppy attacks where it rolls around on the floor, almost.
