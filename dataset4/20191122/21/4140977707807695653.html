NMG Vs DUR Dream11 : Table toppers Nelson Mandela Bay Giants look to further hammer down first place and stretch their lead at the top today.  

The only side in the ongoing edition of the showpiece Mzansi Super League to remain speckle free and unblemished, Nelson Mandela Bay Giants have been in flawless touch. The side are rightfully sitting atop of the league standings with the three wins and one truncated tie from four matches, a position which is a testament to their enter enigmatic, vibrant and radiant brand of cricket.

Their pristine and exceptional form has exulted their ardent supporters in a bedlam, with the side stepping out into today’s clash against Durban Heat looking to emulate the same rousing showing. The side come into the wake of this clash having defeat Cape Town Blitz by five wickets as they chased down what should have been an extremely dicey 187 run target with relative ease.

When Durban Heat take on a side of such calibre, the side who currently occupy a spot in fifth place will be well aware that they can’t afford to slip up at any stage if they are to rise from the agonising spot they occupy at the moment. The position they find themselves slumped in isn’t of their own making though with the side having to face the brunt of widespread rainfall, one which has resulted in their matches being cut short.

With four of the five encounters the side were slated to pay in being marred and mauled by rain to be abandoned, the one defeat the side faced in their only encounter leaves them needing to start winning matches at the earliest. Their previous outing saw their pursuit of 142 set out by Tshwane Spartans being brought an end to at just 21 as rain decided to intervene and leave them infuriated once again.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

En extremely fine track for the batsmen with everything prevelant on this wicket conducive for the batsmen, both the side are concurrently going to thrive on this track. This promises to be a runs galore encounter, one which caters to our quest of watching a high scoring match with the side winning the toss set to come out and bat first in this one.
