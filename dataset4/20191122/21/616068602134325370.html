RECAP: Heat dropped by big-brother Thunderbirds at home - UBCO Heat

22 Nov 2019, 21:00 by UBC Okanagan

The UBCO men's team's improved second-half play was not enough to overcome a 40-point halftime deficit on homecourt as the UBC Thunderbirds rallied to a convincing (112-57) win Friday night at the UBC Okanagan Gymnasium.
