I'm A Celebrity stars rush to help as Andrew Maxwell chokes on camel meat

I'm A Celebrity stars Kate Garraway and Andy Whyment rushed to help Andrew Maxwell, as he began choking

22 Nov 2019, 21:47 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity 2019 star Andrew Maxwell horrified his campmates as he began choking on his dinner in the latest episode - with Andrew even crediting his co-stars to saving his life.

As the comedian attempted to digest camel meat, it got lodged in his throat leaving him struggling.

Andrew began choking, with a "scared" Kate Garraway and Andy Whyment rushing over to help him as he sat on his knees trying to bring the food up.

As they slapped his back, they helped him release the meat with him able to spit it out.

Jacqueline Jossa quickly got him some water as he laughed off the panicking moment in the jungle jail.

His co-stars failed to see the funny side, relieved to see him no longer choking but confessing they were worried for him.

He even ate the same piece of meat after.

Camp cook Myles Stephenson even feared he'd killed him, but Andrew was quick to take the blame.

Andrew had attempted to swallow a huge chunk of chewy camel fillet, which had been won by Jacqueline and Myles in the latest Bushtucker Trial.

Myles told him as he recovered: "Chew your food, mate you scared us."

As Andrew laughed, Kate said: "You went bright red, I was like ‘oh my god’."

Andy said after helping the campmate: "Please don’t do that again Andrew.”

It's then that Rak-Su singer Myles confessed: "I can’t believe I nearly killed Andrew Maxwell," with Andrew replying: "You didn’t, I nearly killed me."

The stars are currently in the jungle jail, with newcomers Andy and Cliff yet to enter the main camp.

Jacqueline and Myles were told after their trial that they would be joining the separate camp.

They then had to decide who the food should go too, before eventually deciding the jail via a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

I'm A Celebrity airs nightly at 9pm on ITV.
