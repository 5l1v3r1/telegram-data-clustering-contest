Attleboro Police seeking help in finding missing teen

22 Nov 2019, 21:07 by Manoella Macedo

ATTLEBORO, Mass. - Police in Attleboro are asking for the public's help in finding a missing 17-year-old boy.

According to officials, Courtney Pinkins Jr. was last seen going to school in Attleboro on Wednesday morning.

Pinkins has ties to Malden and Boston, but it is unclear where he could have gone.
