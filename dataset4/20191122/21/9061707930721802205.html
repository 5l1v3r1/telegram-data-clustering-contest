Body found close to River Derwent in Derby

His family has been informed

22 Nov 2019, 21:11 by Nigel Slater

A body of a man was found in Derby earlier today.

Officers were sent to Chester Green this morning amid reports of a body being found.

Formal identification of the man is yet to take place, although his family have been informed.

Pictures sent to Derbyshire Live show a police cordon and blue tent at the scene.

Police say they don't believe the death to be suspicious.

A statement from Derbyshire police said: "Officers were called to reports that the body of a man had been found close to the River Derwent on the east bank of the river, close to the bowls club off Old Chester Road, this morning at 9.55am.

"Investigations are ongoing, however, there are not believed to be any suspicious circumstances at this time.

"Formal identification has yet to take place; however, the family of the man have been made aware."
