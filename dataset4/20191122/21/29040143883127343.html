How to find the best Black Friday deals on Moto G7 cases

If you're looking to protect your Moto G7 with a case, we have a few buying tips to keep in mind that'll help you find the case that's just right. Our most prominent rule, and the main thing we hope you take away from all of this, is to buy a case that fits your own particular needs. Don't worry about what's popular with your friends or what you think will get noticed the most — buy a case that'll serve you as good as it can. If you're someone that drops their phone a lot, for example, it's best to get a Moto G7 case that is rugged enough to withstand your daily use. That may make your G7 a bit chunky, but a chunky phone is better than a shattered one. Perhaps you're careful with your phone and want something with a unique design? You're probably safe to get something more slim and lightweight. Everyone has different needs, and thankfully, there are cases out there to serve every single one.
