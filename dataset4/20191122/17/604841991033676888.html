Glory day in the Pairc for primary school kids

22 Nov 2019, 17:19 by Fergal Lynch

There was double celebration for Dunshaughlin schools in Pairc Tailteann this afternoon as St Seachnall's claimed the Meath Primary Schools Boys Div 1 title, while their neighbours in Gaelscoil na Rithe won the Girls Div 2/3 Shield crown.
Day of the Meath Primary Schools finals featured four finals with the Boys and Girls Div 1 Shields up for grabs along with the Boys and Girls Div2/3 Shields.
The opening game of the day was a saw nine goals, six of them going to Duleek as Megan O'Connor top scored with an amazing 5-7 to help Duleek GNS see off St Paul's from Ratoath by 6-8 to 3-4 to claim the Girls Div 1 Shield.
