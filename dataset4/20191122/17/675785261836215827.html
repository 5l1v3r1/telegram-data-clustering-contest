Lewis Bloor openly sniffs white powder on wild night out after weeing on building

Pictures show ex-TOWIE star Lewis Bloor holding and snorting a suspicious white powder on a night out with friends

22 Nov 2019, 17:49 by Sara-Aisha Kent, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sara-aisha-kent/

Former TOWIE and Celebrity Big Brother star Lewis Bloor has been pictured snorting a suspicious white powder on a night out with pals.

In the snaps, taken in central London on Thursday evening, the ex reality TV personality, 29, is seen sitting in the back of a vehicle with two men sitting in the front.

After he acquired the powder, Bloor can be seen standing in the street with his friends as he snorts some of the powder and rubs it on his gums.

In another photo he seems to inspect the packet of powder in his hands.

The friends seemed in high spirits as they fell about laughing in the street illuminated by lamp posts.

Earlier on in the evening, Bloor was also pictured urinating outside a homeless charity in public at around 10.30pm.

Standing next to a railing and a red postbox, Lewis can be seen looking behind him with his hand on his hip while he relieves himself.

MirrorOnline has reached out to a rep for Bloor for comment.

Bloor, who is from Chingford, joined The Only Way is Essex in 2013, appearing on the ITV show for three years.

He quit during the 17th series of the show, after falling out with several of his mates for boasting about his Lothario ways with some of the female members of the cast.

Lewis then starred in CelebrityBig Brother in 2016, and he was the fifth housemate to be evicted, coming tenth overall.

He has released a fitness app which promises to help users get a six pack.
