InfoWars boss Alex Jones 'storms' Apple factory in a 'battle tank'

22 Nov 2019, 17:12

The controversial right-wing shock jock Alex Jones has ‘stormed’ an Apple factory in the ‘InfoWars Battle Tank’.

Jones, who is banned from Facebook, Twitter and Youtube, rocked up outside a facility in Texas which builds Mac Pro computers.

The factory employs thousands of people and is part of Apple’s $1 billion plan to make more of its products in the USA.

‘When you build it in the United States, you don’t have to worry about tariffs,’ Trump said after touring the building.

Outside, Alex Jones squared off against anti-Trump protesters.

Driving an armoured car and yelling through a bullhorn, Alex Jones slammed left-wingers and called for free speech to be protected.
