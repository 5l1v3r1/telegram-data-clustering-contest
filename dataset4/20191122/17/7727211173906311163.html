Inside the million pound all-white mansion that's ultra modern

It may look like it belongs to an A-list celebrity but it's actually on our doorstep

22 Nov 2019, 17:17 by Lottie Gibbons

This mansion may look like it belongs to one of Hollywood's A-list celebrities but in fact it's just come on the market in Wirral.

The all-white house boasts trendy minimalist decor, an impressive kitchen and views of the Dee Estuary.

Located in Thurstaston , 2 Rangers House has recently undergone a full renovation which prioritises light and space.

The owners have implemented all the amenities and conveniences that you would expect in a luxury 21st century home.

And with four bedrooms, there's plenty of options to transform this mansion into a family home or modern pad.

On the market with Savills for £1m, the house on Station Road is close to Thurstaston beach and The Wirral Way.

Charlie Kannreuther, head of residential sales at Savills in the North West said: "2 Rangers House has recently undergone a full renovation in which the current owners have applied a great deal of care and attention to detail.

"The result is a striking and spacious family home with plenty of natural light and beautifully landscaped outside space."

Photos on Rightmove offer a glimpse inside the luxury property.

The impressive kitchen and diner is filled with a range of high quality appliances, two sets of bi-folding doors which lead to the landscaped gardens.

This open plan living space provides a wonderful sociable area for entertaining. 

Adjacent to the kitchen is the impressive sitting room with its marbled floor and bespoke, eye-catching staircase.

The double aspect patio doors ensure this living space is filled with natural light flooding in from the southerly/westerly aspect.

And a feature fire place provides the room with a focal point for colder evenings.

A second reception room gives the buyer the opportunity to use this area to suit their own requirements; whether that be a TV room or study.

Completing the ground floor is the utility room, boiler room and toilet.

On the first floor there are four large large bedrooms and a family bathroom - three of the four bedrooms have luxury appointed en suite facilities.

A large balcony in the master bedroom suite is a particularly impressive feature of the property - being large enough for al fresco breakfasts on a sunny day.

The property also comprises of a detached triple garage and sweeping driveway affords amble parking space for family and guests alike.
