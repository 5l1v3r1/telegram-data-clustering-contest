Caroline Flack is an absolute mood as she sips champagne while lying in bath

22 Nov 2019, 17:20

Caroline Flack was living her absolute best life as she stripped down to enjoy a glass of champagne in a steaming hot bubble bath as part of her latest photoshoot.

And she was clearly unafraid to show it off on Instagram.

The Love Island presenter shared a video to her 2.3 million followers of her drinking champagne while in the bath, posing up a storm as she had her picture taken for the camera.

It’s possible she might be a Lady Gaga fan, as her hit single Just Dance could be heard playing in the background. Or perhaps Caroline was taking inspiration from the singer herself, after she previously showed off her ice cold baths on her own Instagram page?

‘I take baths really seriously, shoot TBC,’ she captioned the post.

It wasn’t long until fans rushed to the comments to compliment Caroline on her looks.

‘Whoa this is steamy,’ one fan wrote.

‘Oh Caroline!! Stop it,’ another added.

‘Go on girl!!’ another fan commented.

While some fans seemed concerned she was about to spill her champagne into the bath.

‘I was so worried you were going to spill your drink!’ one said.

Another chipped in: ‘I couldn’t stop thinking…… don’t spill ur drink in the bath!’

But thankfully, the drink remained intact.

Caroline previously teased she was shooting something ‘exciting’, as she also shared a behind the scenes glamour shot from the shoot to Instagram.

‘Shooting something so so exciting today,’ she wrote.

Asides from exciting photoshoots, the 40-year-old has also given us another reason to be excited: she recently flew to Cape Town to begin filming for the upcoming winter series of Love Island.

Although she previously told Metro.co.uk that she is not looking forward to flying back and forth from South Africa once the series commences.

‘It’s a 13 hour flight [to South Africa],’ she said. ‘I’ve got to go back and forth the whole time do Aftersun over here.’

She also added that it’s a ‘long old flight just do that walking’ – but we’re convinced it will be worth it for her iconic slow-mo walks.
