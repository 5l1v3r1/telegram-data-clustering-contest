Photos: Kartik Aaryan visits Mukteshwar temple with his parents in Juhu on his birthday

22 Nov 2019, 17:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan marked his debut with Luv Ranjan's ' Pyaar Ka Punchnama' in 2011. He has made a place for himself in everyone's hearts with his classic performances in various films. The actor has several films lined up ahead. He will be next seen in Mudassar Aziz's 'Pati Patni Aur Woh'.

And today, the actor turned a year older and Kartik along with his parents visited Mukteshwar temple in Juhu on his big day to seek almighty's blessings. He was sporting a white t-shirt teamed with black denim and white sneakers. He was all smiles for the cameras and even posed with his fans as he exited the venue.

Meanwhile, 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' is helmed by Mudassar Aziz and stars Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar as the leading ladies. The film has been slated to hit the theatres on December 06. Post that, he will feature in films like Imtiaz Ali's romantic drama, 'Dostana 2' and 'Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2'.
