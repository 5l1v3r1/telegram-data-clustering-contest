Work start date confirmed for Beeston's new multi-million-pound cinema site

Broxtowe Borough Council has said the project - between Styring Street, Middle Street and Station Street - remains on schedule to open in Christmas 2020

22 Nov 2019, 17:31 by Ben Reid

The start date for work on a new multi-million-pound cinema for Beeston has been confirmed.

Work will start on the eight-screen cinema planned for the Beeston Square development on Monday, November 25.

The Beeston Square development will also bring number of food and drink outlets and 132 homes.

As part of the works, a path that runs between Tesco and the bus and tram interchange will be shut from Monday.

Broxtowe Borough Council has said the project - between Styring Street, Middle Street and Station Street - remains on schedule to open in Christmas 2020.

A statement on the authority's Facebook page said: "Exciting news: work will start on the site of the new cinema in Beeston next week.

"Unfortunately, in order to allow the works to take place, the path that runs between Tesco and the bus/tram interchange will be closed permanently from Monday, November 25.

"This will allow access to the utilities that run underneath it so they can be diverted to make way for the main construction work.

"We ran a consultation on this earlier this year. A new replacement path will be created as part of the new development."
