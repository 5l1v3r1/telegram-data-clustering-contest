Police search for missing teenage girl believed to be in Derby

She was last seen on Tuesday night

22 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Ben Lyons

Police are concerned for the safety of a missing teenage girl who is believed to be in Derby.

Officers are searching for Kyra Priest Levett, who was last seen in Glossop, in the High Peak, at 10pm on Tuesday, November 19.

Miss Priest Levett is described as 5ft 5ins tall with dark brown hair and brown eyes.

Officers believe the 14-year-old is currently in the Derby area.

Anyone who may have seen her, or has information about her current whereabouts, is asked to contact Derbyshire police by calling 101, quoting reference number 200-201119.

You can also call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
