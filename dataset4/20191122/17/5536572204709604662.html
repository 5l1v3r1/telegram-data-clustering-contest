Nelson Agholor Denies Having a Burner Account That Rips QB Carson Wentz (VIDEO + TWEET)

22 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Darrelle Lincoln

Internet detectives seemed to have found another burner account on Twitter and all signs seemed to point at Nelson Agholor.

The burner account criticized Carson Wentz and said Nick Foles throws a better football. Agholor, who has been ripped all season for dropping catches, vehemently denied having a burner account when asked by reporters.
