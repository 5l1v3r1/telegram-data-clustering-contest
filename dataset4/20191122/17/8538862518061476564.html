Devolver Digital and Nomada Studio have announced the beautiful 2D side-scroller GRIS is coming to PlayStation 4. This follows PEGI, the European games rating board, listing the game for Sony's platform last month.

The game is a narrative adventure with a wonderful painterly art style, which will be amped up on PS4. On the PlayStation Blog, lead programmer Roger Mendoza talks about the art team increasing the resolution of all the game's assets by up to four times on PS4 Pro. The music is also less compressed than on other versions, which will result in a crisper sound. Interestingly, the devs have hidden a "PlayStation-exclusive secret homage" in this port, so it'll be cool to see what that easter egg is.

GRIS comes to PS4 digitally on 26th November -- that's next Tuesday. Will you be taking it for a spin? Paint us a picture in the comments below.
