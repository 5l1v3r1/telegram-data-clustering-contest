It can be said that Kaizer Chiefs goalkeeper Itumeleng Khune had a whirlwind of a week, that's if his activity and reactions on social media was anything to go by. We take a look at how crazy Khune's week has been online. Check it out!

From Monday onwards, Khune's name was arguably on every fan's lips and was a trending topic online for a large chunk of the week. Even on Friday, the Bafana Bafana star was part of the South African trends list on Twitter after Chiefs declared "Itu" fit to play again.
