Abu Dhabi T10 League 2019: Yuvraj Singh, Najibullah Zadran guide Maratha Arabians to the top of the table

22 Nov 2019, 17:01 by Vinay Chhabaria

The penultimate league match of T10 League 2019 witnessed a clash between the tournament's two most successful teams, Bangla Tigers and Maratha Arabians. The Arabians sealed their place at the top of the points table with a resounding 7-wicket win over their opponents.

The Tigers had won the toss and elected to bat first on a flat track at the Sheikh Zayed Stadium. Thisara Perera's men got off to a disastrous start as they lost their top three batsmen inside the first 13 balls of the innings. The Sri Lankan duo of Kasun Rajitha (2/18) and Lasith Malinga (1/25) grabbed the three wickets.

Wicket-keeper batsman Tom Moores (43*) held one end however, he received no support from the other batsmen. Ultimately, Robbie Frylinck's (18) cameo helped the Tigers post 106 runs on the board. Dwayne Bravo (2/24) starred for the Maratha side with his variations.
