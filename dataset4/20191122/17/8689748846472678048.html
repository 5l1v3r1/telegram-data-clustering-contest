Brother Sammy Has Denied Rumors Of Sleeping With Tracey Boakye(+Video)

22 Nov 2019, 17:40 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian gospel musician, Brother Sammy has denied allegations of sleeping with Kumawood actress and entrepreneur Tracey Boakye.

Brother Sammy also debunked rumors of pimping Tracey Boakye to other pastor.

Speaking to Zionfelix on the uncut show, Brother Sammy questioned how possible it would be for him to have a sexual affair with the actress/producer and pass her over to someone else.

He added that Tracey Boakye is not a cheap woman for him to just pass her off.

Preparation is currently underway for Brother Sammy to launch his fourth album ‘Aduro Biaa Nkadem’ at the Dichemso Assemblies of God church..
