When it comes to PC gaming, there’s an almost unlimited number of peripherals, games, and components to buy. This makes Black Friday the perfect time of year to catch up on all the PC accessories you’ve been meaning to pick up all year.

To help you figured out where to look for the best deals, we’ve put together a few of the highlights from around the internet. As usual, peripherals like keyboards, mice, and headphones are on sale in quite a few places, especially for the bigger brands like Logitech and Razer.

If you’re on the hunt for hardware, this year has a solid selection of gaming laptops available, along with some sales on GPUs — but don’t expect huge savings in that department. Finally there are also some great deals on games through Green Man Gaming this year, which will give you a few free games along with a reduced price on some of the most popular PC games from this year.
