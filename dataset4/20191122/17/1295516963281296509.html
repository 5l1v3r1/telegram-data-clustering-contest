Doomsday Clock #12 Preview Features Superman & Dr. Manhattan's Epic Clash

22 Nov 2019, 17:11 by L.D. Nolan

Writer Geoff Johns and artist Gary Frank's Doomsday Clock has seen some of the biggest characters from Watchmen entering DC's main universe, resulting in disaster for many of the publisher's biggest heroes. Clark Kent/Superman has been particularly affected. As such, the entire series has been leading to a showdown between Superman and Doctor Manhattan.

DC has now released a first look at Doomsday Clock #12 and the beginning of the battle between the two powerful characters. You can see the preview of Doomsday Clock #12 along with the solicitation information for the issue below.

Doomsday Clock #12

  • Written by Geoff Johns
  • Interior, Cover and Variant Cover art by Gary Frank and Brad Anderson
  • Lettering by Rob Leigh
  • This is it! The final showdown between Dr. Manhattan and Superman shakes up the DC Universe to its very core! The conclusion to Geoff Johns, Gary Frank, Brad Anderson and Rob Leigh’s Doomsday Clock hits shelves December 18th!
  • In Shops: Dec 18, 2019
  • Final Orders Due: Nov 25, 2019
  • SRP: $5.99

The first issue of Doomsday Clock released in November 2017. The 12-issue series, which has seen long and frequent delays, serves as a sequel to Alan Moore and Dave Gibbons' Watchmen.

Doomsday Clock #12 goes on sale Dec. 18 from DC Comics.
