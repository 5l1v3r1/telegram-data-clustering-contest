Here Are the Best Black Friday Gadget Deals From Amazon, Target, and Best Buy

Just because the holiday shopping season is shorter this year doesn't mean the deals are any smaller.

22 Nov 2019, 04:00 by Jason Aten

Black Friday is a week away, which means that the holiday shopping season is about to kick into overdrive. If you're a retailer, you can hardly wait, since this is easily your most important time of year. If you're a gadget-lover, or even a shopper looking for a great deal, this is definitely the best time of year.

Here are a few of the very best tech gadgets that will be on sale this Black Friday (and a few that are already on sale today) everywhere from Best Buy, to Amazon, to Target, and Walmart :

Beats Solo3 Wireless Headphones - $129 (Target)

If you need a quality pair of wireless headphones with solid battery life, the Beats Solo3 are a great choice. There are certainly headphones that sound better, and you can similarly-priced models with noise-cancellation, but at $170 off at Target, you won't find a better deal. 

XBox One Star Wars Bundle - $350 (Best Buy)

There's a similar deal at Target, but this one's better. Save $150 off this deal which includes the full-game download of Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order Deluxe Edition, a month of EA access, the Xbox One X 1TB console, and an Xbox wireless controller, along with a month of Xbox Live Gold.

Apple Watch Series 3 - $129 (Walmart)

Apple kept the Series 3 around as a lower-cost option, and it's a steal this Black Friday for $70 off at Walmart. You can actually get the deal as early as Wednesday at 10 p.m. EST online, and on Thanksgiving day in-store. This one doesn't include the EKG feature, or the always-on display, but aside from also being a little smaller overall, it's a great deal on the most popular smartwatch you can get.

iPhone 11 - Free (when you buy an iPhone 11 Pro from Verizon)

If you're looking for a great deal on the latest iPhone models, Verizon is offering a buy one/get one style deal that includes a free iPhone 11 when you buy an iPhone 11 Pro or Pro Max. If you don't need two new phones, Best Buy is offering up to $500 off the iPhone 11 Pro with activation and a trade-in.

Samsung 70" Class 4K Smart TV - $1,499 (Best Buy)

Saving $500 on a flagship 4K Smart TV is a pretty good deal. Of course, if you don't need something quite that large, Best Buy has a slew of Samsung LED TVs on sale for Black Friday. 

Target also has a Samsung 65" Smart 4K UHD HDR TV for $300 off, making it a sweet $599 deal starting on Thanksgiving day.

Amazon Echo Dot - $22 (Amazon)

If you're ready to take the smart speaker plunge, you can save $27.99 on the Amazon Echo Dot, or get a set of three for $64.97. The Dot isn't the world's most amazing-sounding speaker, but Amazon's most recent version is a marked improvement in that area. You can also save $25 off the Echo Dot with Clock if you'd rather not have to always ask for the time.

10.2" iPad - $249.99 (Target & Walmart)

Apple's base model of its iconic tablet is $80 off for Black Friday. Running iPadOS and now compatible with the Smart Folio Keyboard, the 10.2" iPad is not only a great tablet, it's a great deal at both Target and Walmart.

Microsoft Surface Pro 7 - $599 (Best Buy)

You can get Microsoft's newest Surface Pro for $360 off, or get a bundle that includes the Type Cover and pen and save $429.99. That's a pretty good deal especially if you're looking at getting Microsoft's 2-in-1 device for a graduating student, or just for your own personal computing needs.

iTunes & App Store Gift Cards - Save 20% (Amazon)

In addition to offering a ton of deals, especially on Amazon products, one of the best deals you can get on the site for Black Friday is a 20 percent discount on gift cards at many of the places you love to shop. That includes Apple iTunes and App Store, as well as popular restaurants like Applebees.
