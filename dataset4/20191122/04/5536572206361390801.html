Colts Get Screwed When Clear Fumble By Deshaun Watson Doesn’t Get Reviewed (VIDEO)

22 Nov 2019, 04:27 by Darrelle Lincoln

With a little under two minutes to go in the 4th quarter, Deshaun Watson hiked and kept the ball for a designed run.

He got hit pretty hard to the point where the ball clearly came out for a fumble.

Even with the Texans calling a timeout, the play wasn’t reviewed and the Texans would go on to run the clock out.
