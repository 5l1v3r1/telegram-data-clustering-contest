BREAKING Grace Millane: Man found guilty of murdering British backpacker in New Zealand

The 22-year-old was killed by the defendant after returning to his hotel room in Auckland, New Zealand, last December after meeting him on Tinder

22 Nov 2019, 04:46 by Matthew Dresch, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/matthew-dresch/

A 27-year-old man has been found guilty of murdering British backpacker Grace Millane after a Tinder date in New Zealand.

The 22-year-old was killed by the defendant when she returned to his hotel room in Auckland, New Zealand, last December.

Prosecutors claimed he deliberately strangled her, although the man argued that Grace died accidentally during rough sex.

Auckland High Court heard how the man Googled 'flesh-eating birds' and 'are there vultures in New Zealand' as he researched how to dispose of her body.

He then bought two suitcases and stuffed her body into one of them, before burying it in a shallow grave outside Auckland.

The defendant also arranged another Tinder date while Grace lay dead in his hotel room.

His defence team said he panicked after finding Grace dead in his room and buried her body because he did not think anyone would believe he was innocent of murder.

Grace's parents have sat in court over the past three weeks listening to the harrowing details of their daughter's death.

Just before the verdict came in the judge was asked a question by the jury, who asked for clarity around the sustained pressure to the neck during her death.

That indicated that a decision would be imminent.
