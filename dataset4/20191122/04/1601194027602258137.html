WWE RAW Superstar says Lana's acting is awful

22 Nov 2019, 04:09 by Abhilash Mendhe

WWE RAW Superstars Zelina Vega and Lana recently got into a Twitter feud after the latter took exception to Vega calling herself the best dressed WWE talent.

Zelina Vega isn't exactly known for staying silent when it comes to social media altercations with fellow Superstars. She recently posted a photo featuring herself and Andrade on her official Twitter handle, and referred to the duo as the best-dressed and best-looking talent on the roster.

Fellow RAW Superstar Lana noticed the tweet and didn't seem to agree with Vega's statements. She responded by stating that her Vercase attire made her the best-dressed star on Monday Night RAW.
