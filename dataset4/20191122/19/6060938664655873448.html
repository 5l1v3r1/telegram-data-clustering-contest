Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi passes away; Boney Kapoor, Tabu, Urmila Matondkar and other celebs arrive to pay their last respects

22 Nov 2019, 19:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Veteran actor and Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi passed away in Mumbai on Friday evening due to age-related illness at the age of 93.

Kaifi's son-in-law, veteran writer-lyricist Javed Akhtar, confirmed the news of the demise of the theatre and film actor.

And now, celebs like Boney Kapoor, Urmila Matondkar, Saiyami Kher, Satish Kaushik, Tabu, Manish Malhotra and others arrived at Shabana Azmi's house to pay their last respects.

"She was 93 and she was having one problem after another. She was admitted to Kokikaben Dhirubhai Ambani hospital, for certain days she was in the ICU and then she was out of ICU. It was all age-related," Javed Akhtar said.

"Ultimately they brought her home. She wanted to come back to her own room she stayed for a day or two and then passed away. Shabana is in Mumbai," Akhtar, who is in the US, told PTI.

The last rites will be held Saturday noon.
