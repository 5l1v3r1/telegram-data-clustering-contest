Príncipe Discos sister label HOLUZAM to release two new record

New music from Luís Fernandes and Moreno Ácido & Diogo comes out next week.

22 Nov 2019, 19:33 by Kiana Mickles

Príncipe Discos has announced two releases arriving via HOLUZAM next week.

First, Moreno Ácido and Diogo join on the Roçadas EP> Spanning four tracks, the project draws from Brazilian funk samples and acid techno. The second release, Seis Peças Sintetizadas, comes from Luís Fernandes (pictured). His six-track record is arranged from ambient sounds, organ synthesizers and industrial backings.

Last year, Príncipe Discos launched their sister label HOLUZAM, an imprint with a "jazz and avant-garde focus."

Listen to both EPs via Bandcamp, and watch a video from Moreno Ácido & Diogo.
