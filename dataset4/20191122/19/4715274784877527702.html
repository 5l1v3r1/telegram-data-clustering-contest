EJK, war on drugs in spotlight

22 Nov 2019, 19:40 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

Over 70 artworks by sculptors, painters, mixed media, and performance artists are featured in BANTA sa UP, an art exhibit at the new UP CFA Atrium, ongoing until Dec. 15. 

Artists from Davao, Lucena, Laguna, and Angono join their peers in Metro Manila for the show. 

The show’s theme focuses on extra-judicial killings, war on drugs, and the West Philippine Sea dispute.

In addition to the artworks on display, a mural made by children orphaned by the government’s war on drugs is featured. 

The show is open to the public from Monday to Saturday.
