Ben Vida releasing four-hour album on Shelter Press

Reducing The Tempo To Zero is out in December.

22 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Andrew Ryce

Ben Vida's next album comes out on Shelter Press on December 6th.

Reducing The Tempo To Zero is a four-hour drone album inspired by the American artist's daily ritual of ultra-focused deep listening and music making. It was composed and edited across four years, from 2015 through 2019, and is a spiritual successor to Vida's 2016 album, Damaged Particulates. It comes in digital formats as well as a USB card.

Vida has previously released on Shelter Press, as well as labels like iDEAL, PAN and Nihilist.
