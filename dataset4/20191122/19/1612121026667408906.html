Call of Duty Mobile update adds Zombie mode, controller support, and new maps

22 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Peter Holden

I haven’t played CoD since Modern Warfare 2 around a decade ago, but dang it, the mobile game that launched a few weeks ago has got me hooked, bigtime. Call of Duty Mobile is a game that harks back to the best of the CoD franchise, with something for everyone from hard-charging assaulters, campers snipers, and the sneaky-sneaky types, along with some iconic maps. And now, an update is adding controller support, new maps, a new Battle Pass, and perhaps more importantly, Zombie mode.
