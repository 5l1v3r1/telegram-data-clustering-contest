How old are I’m A Celebrity star Kate Garraway’s children?

22 Nov 2019, 19:30

Kate Garraway’s husband Derek Draper and their two children Darcey and William appeared on Good Morning Britain to talk about her time on I’m A Celebrity so far.

Derek, 52, sat next to the young ‘uns that Kate was previously seen getting emotional over in the jungle when she spoke about how she was missing them.

Derek quipped: ‘I never get a mention by the way. “Oh I miss the kids”, what about me?’

With Kate’s time in the jungle having only just started, let’s take a closer look at the family she’s been missing so much.

How old are Kate Garraway’s children?

Kate Garraway daughter’s Darcey is 13 and her son William is 10.

On this show, Kate was seen getting emotional when she spoke about how much she missed her children, saying she could no longer ‘remember what their heads smelled like’.

When the family appeared on Good Morning Britain, Derek, a former political lobbyist, gushed about his wife, saying: ‘Take it from someone who knows – she never looks better than first thing in the morning.
