The Xiaomi Mi CC9 Pro that has a 108-megapixel penta-lens camera setup was recently made outside of China as Xiaomi Mi Note 10. According to a new report on 91mobiles, the Chinese manufacturer is planning to launch the Mi Note 10 in India. The Xiaomi Mi 5 flagship phone that was unveiled in India in 2016 had received lukewarm reception. Since then it hasn’t launched a high-end or flagship Mi series phone in India. Beginning from 2017, the Chinese firm has been launching Android One based Mi series phones in India each year. The Mi Note 10 is expected to rival with the likes of Realme X2 Pro and OnePlus 7 in its price segment.

The source that revealed the arrival of Mi Note 10 to India has claimed that the top executives of the Chinese firm is think about the right time to bring it to the country. The Mi Note 10 was previously planned to break cover in India alongside the Xiaomi Mi MIX Alpha concept phone in December. The plan has been reportedly dropped.

It remains to be seen when Xiaomi will bring the Mi Note 10 in India. As far as the Mi MIX Alpha is concerned, it is expected to arrive in limited quantities. The Mi Note 10 is a flagship-grade phone and the only thing that people may not like about it is that it does not feature a flagship chipset, but the Snapdragon 730G SoC. Since Xiaomi is leading the Indian smartphone market, it is likely that the Mi Note 10 will be met with positive reception in the country.

Xiaomi Mi Note 10 Specifications

The Xiaomi Mi Note 10 has a waterdrop notch enabled 6.47-inch AMOLED display that offers full HD+ resolution. The front and rear sides of the phone is flanked with Gorilla Glass 5 panels. The Snapdragon 730G SoC is coupled with 6 GB of RAM and 128 GB of UFS 2.1 storage.

The MIUI 11 based Android 9 Pie OS runs on the Mi Note 10. It has a front-facing camera of 32-megapixel. The rear shell of the phone features a penta-lens camera system that is accompanied by a pair of dual LED flash units. The camera system has an OIS assisted 108-megapixel primary lens, a 12-megapixel portrait lens, a 20-megapixel ultrawide lens with 117-degree field-of-view, an OIS enabled periscope 5-megapixel lens with 5x optical zoom and a 2-megapixel macro unit.

The Mi Note 10 has a hefty 5,260mAh battery. The phone supports 30W fast charging that can fully charge the device in 65 minutes. The phone also features an in-screen fingerprint reader. It is available in Europe with a price tag of 549 euros (~$605).
