Missing Summerville teenager found safe

22 Nov 2019, 19:59 by Live 5 Web Staff

SUMMERVILLE, S.C. (WCSC) - Police have announced that a missing Summerville teenager has been found safe.

Summerville police say 15-year-old Alyssa Williams returned to her home on Friday.

Williams was last seen on Wednesday when she was shopping with her mother.

Police said Williams told her mother she had to use the restroom, but did not return.
