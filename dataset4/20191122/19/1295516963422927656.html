Legacies Promises a 'Surprisingly Violent' Christmas Episode

22 Nov 2019, 19:11 by Sundi Rose

Legacies -- The CW's latest contribution to The Vampire Diaries franchise -- is promising viewers a good bit of holiday violence in the title of the upcoming Christmas episode. While viewers can usually expect a fair amount of blood and gore from these vampire shows, when the show, itself, promises some "surprising" violence, then fans know they are in for a shock.

The promise comes straight from a newly released synopsis that reveals the title of the Dec. 12 episode, "This Christmas Was Surprisingly Violent." In addition its titled, The CW reveals the holiday episode will feature a Christmas monster that uses holiday spirit to infiltrate the Salvatore School. Also, Landon offers some surprise news about his family to Rafael. Meanwhile, Sebastian teams up with Lizzie for her latest mission.

Per the full synopsis for "This Christmas Was Surprisingly Violent,"

IT’S CHRISTMAS IN MYSTIC FALLS — Hope (Danielle Rose Russell) joins forces with an unlikely ally to take down a Christmas monster, who’s using holiday cheer to infiltrate the Salvatore School. Elsewhere, Landon (Aria Shahghasemi) surprises Rafael (Peyton Alex Smith) with news about his family lineage, and Sebastian (guest star Thomas Doherty) accompanies Lizzie (Jenny Boyd) on her latest mission. Kaylee Bryant and Matthew Davis also star. Brett Matthews directed the episode written by Brett Matthews & Hannah Rosner (#208). Original airdate 12/12/2019.

Airing Thursdays at 9 pm ET on The CW, Legacies stars Danielle Rose Russell as Hope Mikaelson, Jenny Boyd as Lizzie Saltzman, Kaylee Bryant as Josie Saltzman, Quincy Fouse as Milton Greasley/MG, Aria Shahghasemi as Landon Kirby, Peyton Alex Smith as Rafael Waithe and Matt Davis as Alaric Saltzman.
