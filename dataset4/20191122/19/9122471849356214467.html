NFL Insider Updates Status Of Bengals Star WR A.J. Green

22 Nov 2019, 19:02 by Chris Rosvoglou

For those waiting for A.J. Green to make his debut this season, you’ll have to wait a little longer. Once again, the star wideout on the Cincinnati Bengals will have to sit out due to his ankle injury.

Green suffered the injury during training camp. The Bengals expected him to miss a significant period of time, but they were hopeful he would return after the bye week.

Unfortunately for Green, he re-aggravated his injury while practicing a few weeks ago, and remains out indefinitely. With another divisional matchup set for this weekend, Cincinnati will have to play without its top offensive weapon.

According to ESPN insider Adam Schefter, the Bengals have ruled out Green for this Sunday against the Steelers.
