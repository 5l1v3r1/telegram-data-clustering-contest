Score free tickets to U2 concert in Manila

22 Nov 2019, 19:50 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

Heads up, U2 fans!

Telecommunications company Smart gives subscribers an opportunity to score tickets to this year’s biggest concert, the Joshua Tree Tour, the first ever concert of U2 in Manila.

The one-night only show on Dec. 11 at the Philippine Arena is expected to bring together music fans across generations with the iconic Irish rock band’s hits including “Where The Streets Have No Name,” “Pride (In The Name of Love),” “I Still Haven’t Found What I’m Looking For,” “With or Without You,” and a lot more.

Through the new Smart MVP Rewards program, prepaid and postpaid subscribers can win free tickets. 

Simply download the MVP Rewards app in the App Store or Play Store and register your details. Then enroll your Smart account to start earning stars.

Click on your stars to visit the Marketplace where you can click on the U2 raffle icon and participate for free.

All customers who click on the U2 raffle icon will only have one e-raffle ticket for the entire promo duration.

Those who will enter the free Smart MVP Rewards U2 Raffle Promo get a chance to win two tickets to the U2 The Joshua Tree Tour concert, plus access to the Smart Music Live VIP Lounge during the concert proper.

The promo is ongoing until Dec. 5. Winners will be drawn on Dec. 6.
