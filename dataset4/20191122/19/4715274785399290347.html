Education partner

22 Nov 2019, 19:54

The Philippine Climate Change Commission has engaged iACADEMY, a school in Makati that specializes in technology and design, to produce digital artwork to help increase awareness on climate change and communicate the need to act, now. The CCC is celebrating Climate Change Consciousness week at One Esplanade, J.W. Diokno Blvd, Pasay City. The public exhibit which coincides with the week-long event features 30 art works from iACADEMY’s 2nd year Multimedia Arts students.
