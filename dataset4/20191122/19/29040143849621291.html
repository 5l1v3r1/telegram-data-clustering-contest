How to find the best Black Friday deals on Galaxy S10+ cases

When looking into cases for your Galaxy S10+, there are a few things to consider to ensure you find something that's perfect for you and your lifestyle. Above all else, think about what you're looking to get out of a case. Are you someone that drops their phone? Like, a lot? If so, you should probably look into a rugged case that's been designed to withstand endless drops and falls. On the other hand, if you're more careful with your tech and just want something to keep the S10+ safe against daily wear and tear, you're probably fine getting a slimmer, lightweight case. There are also different features to think about. Some phone cases have things like a built-in kickstand, while others have a folio design and can be used for storing your credit cards. Advanced goodies like this often cost more money, but for some people, they can be well worth it.
