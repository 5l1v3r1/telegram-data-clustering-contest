MAMAMOO executes electric performances on 'Yoo Hee Yeol's Sketchbook'

22 Nov 2019, 19:34 by sl278

MAMAMOO recently appeared on the November 22 broadcast of the show where they showcased a number of electric performances and entertained the audience with their wit. 

The girls prepared a number of stages for the audience and performed their newest title track "HIT". They also impressed with their beautiful vocals as they performed their ballad tracks "I Miss You" and "Hello Mama".
