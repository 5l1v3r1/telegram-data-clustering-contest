Microsoft releases Office Insider Build 16.0.12228.20168 for Insiders on Android, here is what’s new

22 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Rahul

Microsoft has released a new Office Build for Office Insiders on Android. The new Build 16.0.12228.20168 brings a couple of important new features to Outlook and Excel. You can read the official changelog below.

Changelog

Outlook
Classification, labeling, and protection
For Office 365 customers who have configured their organization’s sensitivity labels in the Office 365 Security and Compliance Center or the Microsoft 365 Compliance center, labels will be available to be applied in Outlook for Android email messages.  People in your organization will be able to apply a pre-defined label to an email and have that label and any associated protection persist when sent to others. The different actions and protection, including encryption, will depend on the policies your organization defined in the Security & Compliance center.

Meeting Insights from event details page
Tired of scrambling for information before or during a meeting? Files and documents relevant to your meeting will now appear in the event description so you’re always prepared. 
Galaxy Watch support 
See all your mail and act on it while on the go with the all new Outlook app now available on on Samsung watches!

Excel
Mobile optimized sort and filter options 
What’s your favorite color? Sort and filter cells and items based on text and cell color using format based filters. The Sort and Filter pane has been optimized for mobile, and you can now filter columns and items by ascending vs. descending, conditional filters, and view a tree list for dates.
Word
There are no new features to highlight at this time.
