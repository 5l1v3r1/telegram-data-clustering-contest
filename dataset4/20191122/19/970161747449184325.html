Andrew Whyment is already the favourite to win I’m A Celebrity 2019 despite the fact that he has only just entered the jungle.

How much is Andrew Whyment paid for I’m A Celebrity?

Soap star Andrew Whyment reportedly got paid £100,000 to appear on I’m A Celebrity 2019, which according to the Manchester Evening News makes him one of the better paid stars on this year’s show.

They report that that Good Morning Britain host Kate Garraway and Eastenders actor Jacqueline Jossa are both taking him less than Andy at £75,000 each and rugby player James Haskell and Radio One DJ Adele Roberts are both getting less again at £50,000.

American star Caitlyn Jenner is reportedly earning the most from her stint on the show this year, pocketing an impressive £500,000 for her appearance, which works out at roughly £25,000 a day.

While that sum is an eye-watering amount, it’s still not the most that has ever been paid to a celebrity for their involvement. That coveted title goes to TV host Noel Edmonds who is said to have become the highest ever paid celebrity to appear on the show having been apparently paid £600,000 to enter the jungle.

Who is Andy Whyment’s wife?

Andy Whyment’s wife is called Nichola and the couple have two children together named Thomas and Hollie.

The couple have been married for 12 years and before entering the jungle Andy said in an interview with OK! Magazine that he believes he has been: ‘punching above my weight for years’ on account of his wife.

‘She gorgeous!’ he gushed to the mag.

‘I’m a very lucky bloke. We’ve been together 14 years and we just don’t argue. We get on so well.’
