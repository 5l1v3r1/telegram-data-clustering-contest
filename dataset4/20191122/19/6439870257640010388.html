Man wanted on attempted murder charges turns himself in, Pineville police say

22 Nov 2019, 19:18 by WBTV Web Staff

PINEVILLE, N.C. (WBTV) - Pineville police say a man accused of firing a gun into an occupied vehicle has turned himself in Friday evening.

Police say the incident occurred at a parking lot off of South Polk Street around 7:00 p.m. According to police, Dashawn Derrell Crawford, 31, fired multiple shots into the vehicle following a verbal argument.

No one was reported injured or struck by the bullets but Crawford left the scene. He has been charged with attempted first degree murder, possession of a firearm by a felon, discharging a weapon in a vehicle and assault by pointing a gun.

Pineville police say Crawford turned himself into the Mecklenburg County Jail Friday evening based on the active warrants.
