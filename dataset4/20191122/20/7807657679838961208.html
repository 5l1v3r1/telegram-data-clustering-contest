What’s the difference between the HP ENVY and the HP ENVY x360?

22 Nov 2019, 20:56 by Camille Sanghera

Source: HP via YouTube

Best Answer: The HP ENVY laptops are equipped with integrated fingerprint readers that allow for a fast login. This feature also acts as an additional security measure. If you're looking for superb graphics, the HP ENVY laptops range from NVIDIA Geforce 250MX to NVIDIA Geforce 940MX graphic display, depending on the model.

The HP ENVY x360 laptops are convertible and can be manipulated 360 degrees to fit your most productive position. The ENVY x360 laptops also come prepared to work with four different modes including laptop, tablet, tent, or stand. You can also choose between an Intel Core or an AMD Ryzen processor to suit the demands of your career or passion.

Both the HP ENVY and the HP ENVY x360 have touchscreen options available with select models.

HP ENVY Laptop - 13t

Source: Daniel Rubino / Windows Central

Available in a natural silver or pale-gold color, the HP ENVY Laptop - 13t is ideal for anyone who is in the photography or videography business. This lap computer is Windows 10 compatible and features an 8th Generation Intel Core i7 processor. This means that it packs a lot of power and can handle the multi-tasking needs of your profession.

Equipped with 8 GB of memory and 256 GB SSD storage space, this 13-inch laptop can store all of your media and data files safely and with ease. In fact, the ENVY - 13t makes safety a priority and comes prepared with an integrated fingerprint reader. It also safely secures and shields your files from corruption through its built-in HP BIOS Recovery software.

Don't be fooled by this laptop's ulta-sleek and lightweight design because although it only weighs around 2.59 pounds, it is both powerful and durable. This capable laptop computer features NVIDIA Geforce MX250 graphics, which means that you can edit videos and photos or immerse yourself in the world of gaming through high-resolution three-way micro-edge display, all while enjoying a fast performance.

If your passion requires you to indulge for hours on end, then you'll love knowing that the HP ENVY Laptop - 13t features a battery that supports fast-charge. In fact, your laptop will be at approximately 50 percent of its full power potential after only being plugged in for 40 minutes. A fully charged battery will run for 14 hours.

This laptop comes with one headphone and microphone port, two USB 3.1 Gen 1 ports, and 1 USB 3.1 Type-C port. One of the USB 3.1 Gen 1 ports can be used to charge devices when the laptop is in sleep mode. The other USB 3.1 Gen 1 port is for data transfer only.

HP ENVY Laptop - 17t

Source: HP

If 13.3 inches of diagonal FHD display isn't big enough for you, then you can opt for the 17-inch HP ENVY Laptop. Not only is this touchscreen laptop bigger, but it comes equipped with 4K Ultra High Definition (UHD) display that is abundantly clearer than the HP ENVY - 13t. If you are looking for more display options, you can actually add a second external display to the HP ENVY - 17t as it is fully supported within its hardware. This second external display can even reach up to 4K resolution.

Similar to the HP ENVY - 13t, the HP ENVY - 17t is excellent for photographers, videographers, movie-lovers, and entry-level gamers. However, unlike the HP Envy 13t, the HP ENVY - 17t features an NVIDIA GeForce 940MX graphics card (GPU). This means that it performs up to four times faster with its graphics, making editing images and competing while gaming incredibly engaging.

The HP ENVY - 17t weighs approximately 6.14 pounds and offers up to 10 hours of battery life. It features a 10th Generation Intel Core i5 processor and comes prepared with 8 GB of memory and 1TB of HDD storage.

You can also opt for 16 GB of Intel Optane Memory. This smart technology cuts down wait time when loading up your laptop as it remembers what you've been working on and fast-tracks the responsiveness of your laptop.

The HP ENVY - 17t comes with one USB 3.1 Gen 1 Type-C port that can transfer data at a rate of 5 GB per second, one USB 3.1 Gen 1 Type-A port that is HP sleep and charge compatible, and two USB 2.0 Gen 1 Type-A ports that are for data transfer only.

HP ENVY x360

Source: HP via YouTube

Known as the convertible laptop, the HP ENVY x360 can be manipulated to rest in many unconventional positions. In fact, you can turn the screen 360 degrees to fit perfectly within the guidelines of your creative process at any given time.

Whether your main goal is to use the laptop as a tablet to increase productivity, or if your needs include tent and stand features to elevate conference calling, video chats, and entertainment, there is a mode for you. In fact, toggle between four different modes depending on your current activity: laptop, tablet, tent, or stand. Each mode comes prepared with different settings to enhance your experience.

Available in 13.3-inch or 15.6-inch diagonal display, the HP ENVY x360 laptop has enough power to fuel your creativity, gaming, or productivity needs. However, the 15.6-inch is touchscreen and the display is equipped with a wider range of optional features when it comes to performance. With the 15-inch and 13-inch ENVY x360, you have the option of opting for an Intel Core or an AMD Ryzen processor.

If you're looking for a gaming laptop, then the AMD Ryzen processor may be the right choice for you as these processors enhance performance without consuming more power. This allows you to run programs flawlessly while gaming.

The HP ENVY x360 - 13z comes with one headphone and microphone port, one USB 3.1 Gen 1 port that is HP Sleep and Charge compatible, two USB 3.1 Gen 1 that are intended for data transfer only, and one USB 3.1 Type-C Gen 1 port for data transfer.

The HP ENVY x360 - 15t comes with one headphone and microphone port, one USB 3.1 Type-C Gen 1 port that can transfer data at a rate of 5 GB per second and offer power delivery, two USB 3.1 Gen 1 ports, and one HDMI 2.0 port.
