Supernatural Synopsis Teases The Arrival of More Unlikely Allies

22 Nov 2019, 20:11 by L.D. Nolan

Supernatural's Sam Winchester, Dean Winchester and Castiel are going to get new allies in their fight against Chuck/God.

The CW has released its synopsis for Season 15, Episode #15, titled "Our Father, Who Aren't in Heaven." Although the summary does not make clear just who will assist in the fight against God, there are a lot of possible candidates considering how many people and entities the brother's have encountered over the course of the series.

SUPERNATURAL“Our Father, Who Aren’t in Heaven” — (8:00-9:00 p.m. ET) (TV-14, LV) (HDTV)

GOD BLESSES HIM WHO HELPS HIS BROTHER – Sam (Jared Padalecki), Dean (Jensen Ackles) and Castiel’s (Misha Collins) continued search for a way to defeat Chuck (guest star Rob Benedict) leads them to unexpected places and toward unlikely allies. Richard Speight, Jr. directed the episode written by Eugenie Ross-Leming & Brad Buckner (#1508). Original Airdate 12/12/2019.
