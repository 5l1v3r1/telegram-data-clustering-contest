Kristen Stewart goes hell for leather with Charlie's Angels cast in London

22 Nov 2019, 20:20

Kristen Stewart and the cast of Charlie’s Angels kept things coordinated on Friday morning as they sported matching leather looks while promoting the film’s UK release.

The Angels wowed during Wednesday evening’s London premiere of the action movie, but opted for more casual outfits on Friday as they visited radio station Kiss FM.

With all three of the girls opting for black leather items of clothing (if there’s one thing we want from a kickass group of women, it’s coordinated outfits), Kristen Stewart led the way in perhaps the most weather appropriate outfit for a chilly November morning.

Wrapping up warm in a leather aviator jacket, distressed boyfriend jeans and a baseball cap, she was joined by Aladdin star Naomi Scott, who also opted for trousers in light wash denim jeans.

The 26-year-old actress paired her look with a black embossed leather tank top, layering a fur lined denim jacket and patent coat over the top which she offset with some tiny black sunglasses.

Talk about preparing for all possible temperatures…

Meanwhile, newcomer Ella Balinska gave us serious spy vibes – but then again, isn’t the most obvious spy the one that doesn’t look like a spy at all? – in a black leather jumpsuit while sporting a pair of black sunnies.

The girls are currently visiting the UK ahead of the release of Elizabeth Banks’ reboot of the action comedy classic Charlie’s Angels.

We suspect that the cast are hoping the film’s international debut will help boost sales after the film grossed just $3.1 million in its US opening weekend despite receiving good reviews.

Not great for a project that cost $48 million to make…

However, actress Elizabeth Banks, who wrote, directed, produced and starred in the project (very impressive) seemed to be taking this bad news in her stride.

‘Well, if you’re going to have a flop, make sure your name is on it at least 4x,’ she wrote on Twitter, ‘I’m proud of #CharliesAngels and happy it’s in the world.’

We’re glad to hear it, Elizabeth.

Charlie’s Angels is released in UK theatres on Friday 29 September.
