Alleged Fraud: EFCC Hits Mompha With 14-count Charge

22 Nov 2019, 20:07 by Donatus Anichukwueze

The Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) has slammed a 14 charge of alleged fraud and money laundering against internet celebrity, Ismaila Mustapha, popularly known as Mompha.

The anti-graft agency said it will arraign Mompha before Justice Mohammad Liman of the Federal High Court, Ikoyi Lagos on Monday, November 25.

The EFCC, in the first charge, said that between 2015 & 2018, Mompha procured Ismalob Global Investment Ltd to retain the sum of N18m in its account, a sum suspected to be proceeds of crime.

Mompha had yesterday filed a Fundamental Rights Enforcement Suit before the Lagos High Court to challenge his continued detention by the EFCC.

The internet celebrity asked the court to award him N5m in damages against the EFCC for what he called his unlawful detention.
