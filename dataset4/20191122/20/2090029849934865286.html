Davido appears on New York’s Times Square

22 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Popularly Nigerian singer David Adeleke, aka Davido is in high spirits as he celebrates yet another achievement just a day after his birthday.

Hours after releasing his latest and second album,’ A Good Time’, it was proudly displayed on New York’s Times Square.

Davido released his second album at the early hour of Friday, November 22, and by evening time on the same day, his album was already displayed on New York’s Times Square screens.

According to reports, Davido’s new Album hit a staggering number of 87million streams as at 5am this morning. The Album has broken into the charts of 38 countries according to Apple Music.

The proud singer took to his Instagram page to share a photo of him in the New York Times Square, he captioned the post:

“Having a good time.”
