Photos: Kiara Advani, Ananya Panday, Janhvi Kapoor and other celebs attend Kartik Aaryan's birthday bash

22 Nov 2019, 20:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan turned a year older on 22nd November and a lot of birthday wishes poured in for the actor on social media. And late in the night, the actor hosted a party at a restaurant in Mumbai.

The bash was attended by his 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' co-star Ananya Panday, Kiara Advani, Janhvi Kapoor, Lakshya, Luv Ranjan, Ramesh Taurani, Jackky Bhagnani and more. For the night, Kartik wore a white striped shirt teamed with black pants and boots and looked absolutely cool in it. While Ananya opted for a black ensemble and she rocked in it! Janhvi, on the other hand, made heads turn in a pink tube top and was all smiles with her 'Dostana 2' co-star Lakshya.
