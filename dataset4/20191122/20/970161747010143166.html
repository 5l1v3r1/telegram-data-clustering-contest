Lizzo jets off to Disneyland as she celebrates Grammy nods and we're not jealous

22 Nov 2019, 20:34

Lizzo is clearly living her best life, as she has taken herself off to Disneyland following her eight Grammy nods.

Sporting Minnie Mouse ears and polka dot socks, the Truth Hurts hitmaker threw her hands in the air as she arrived at the magical resort.

And it looks as though she is having a blast, as she took to Instagram to share her time away.

‘I’m at the happiest place on earth…’ the 31-year-old said before adding: ‘And I’m at Disneyland.’

Our hearts just can’t deal!

Swiping to see the rest of her pictures, the singer quietly trolled herself as she shared those candid shots of herself on a number of rides.

And she does not look impressed, as the Good As Hell songstress squinted her eyes while clutching her face.

Fans were quick to comment and showered their goddess with praise.

‘Credits for that caption,’ one wrote while another added: ‘You are legit so gorgeous!’

Someone else said: ‘I wanna go to Disneyland on the same day LIZZO goes.’ Same girl, same.

Her post comes after it was announced the star received a whopping eight Grammy nominations including album of the year for Cuz I Love You, best new artist and song of the year for Truth Hurts.

Billie Eilish followed close behind along with Little Nas X who have scooped six nods each.

Freaking out over the news, the Old Town Road singer took to Twitter where he simply wrote: ‘NO F**KING WAY.’

Fans were quick to congratulate the star as they showered him with praise under his post.

‘Congrats King. I’m so proud of you!!!’ one wrote while another added: ‘GRAMMY NOMINATED NAS IS WHAT THEY CALLIN YOU NOW.’
