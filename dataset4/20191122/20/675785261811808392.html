Gabby Allen 'bombarded by women' claiming ex Myles Stephenson 'sexted them'

EXCLUSIVE: Love Island beauty Gabby Allen has been contacted by a number of women claiming her ex I'm A Celeb's Myles Stephenson 'sexted them' whilst he was with her

22 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Sara-Aisha Kent, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sara-aisha-kent/

I'm a Celeb's Myles Stephenson, 28, hasn't only got to concern himself with creepy crawlies in the jungle - as a number of worms have come out of the woodwork since he appeared on the ITV show.

The Rak-Su singer's ex-girlfriend Gabby Allen , 25, who split from Myles after she discovered he was messaging a previous girlfriend, has been "bombarded by women" claiming they received flirty messages, sexts and explicit photos from him whilst he was coupled up with the Love Island beauty.

A source close to the pretty personal trainer exclusively told Mirror Online: "A barrage of women have stepped forward since Myles appeared on I'm A Celeb, she has been bombarded.

"They have been sending proof of the messages, sext and d*** pics Myles sent to them when he was with Gabby."

They source also shared that Gabby has been informed that Myles was "two-timing" her throughout their entire relationship after the woman whose texts with the singer caused the end of their romance recently "smugly" revealed that Myles was seeing her the whole time.

Prior to entering the Australian Bush, in an interview with The Sun, Myles claimed he came clean to Gabby about texting his ex "because he loved her" but it was not technically cheating as they "were on a break. "

"We were on a break at the time when I messaged this girl, a previous ex", he said.

"I shouldn't have done it, even though me and Gabby weren't speaking at the time and I moved home, but it was a general conversation.

“I was a stupid mistake and I stopped speaking to her when we started getting back together, but by that time she's messaged Gabby and said we’d been speaking."

Gabby appeared on Love Island in 2017, and dated Marcel Somerville before they split, due to his cheating.

She then starting dating Myles last September and they were together for almost a year, but split following reports she caught him messaging other women.
