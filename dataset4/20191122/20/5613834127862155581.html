The First Ever “Thanksgiving of Horror” Episode of “The Simpsons” Airs This Sunday! [Exclusive Clip]

22 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Brad Miska

If you thought the Halloween fun was over, think again. “The Simpsons” have even more treats in store for you in their special Thanksgiving edition of “Treehouse of Horror”!

Bloody Disgusting has scored a first look at this Sunday’s first ever “Thanksgiving of Horror” episode, wherein the Simpsons are forced to face various Thanksgiving nightmares, including:

The first Thanksgiving, an A.I. mishap and a dangerous space mission complicated by a sentient cranberry sauce.

We have an exclusive clip from the last segment that appears to spoof Life with Jake Gyllenhaal and Ryan Reynolds. In the clip, Bart and Milhouse create a living blob from cranberry sauce.

Check it out below, along with images and poster art underneath!

The special will air Sunday, Nov. 24 (8:00-8:30 PM ET/PT) on FOX.
