Kirk Herbstreit Has Brutally Honest Response To SEC’s Schedule This Weekend

22 Nov 2019, 23:09 by Andrew McCarty

On Friday afternoon, ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit sent out a tweet saying how excited he is for a major weekend of college football.

“Nothing like CFB in November. Just when you think ya know the answers the underdogs change the question! Time of the yr where you can make assumptions & yet the pressure gets excessive & some teams can’t handle it-Expect the unexpected the next 2 weeks-Here we go!” Herbstreit said.

A Michigan radio host weighed in calling out the SEC’s top teams playing weaker opponents this weekend.

He noted the opponents of Alabama, Auburn and LSU who play Western Carolina, Samford and Arkansas.

Herbie responded, noting he’s not happy with the schedules either.

“Sucks,” he said on Twitter.
