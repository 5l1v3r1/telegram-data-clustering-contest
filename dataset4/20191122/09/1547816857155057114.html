Pati Patni Aur Woh Song Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare: Kartik-Ananya-Bhumi's Peppy Track Trends On Number 2 In India

22 Nov 2019, 09:46 by Lubna Khan

Two days ago, makers of Pati Patni Aur Woh released a new song titled Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare. The remix version of the song starring Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar, Ananya Panday is now trending on number 2 in India, with 22 million views in just 2 days!

Two days ago, makers of Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday starrer Pati Patni Aur Who released a new song from their film titled ‘Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare.’  The peppy track features all three stars and it is a remix of the classic old song Akhiyon Se Goli Mare that originally featured Govinda. The original song had been a massive hit and is one remembered by people even now. The bar was set high since the ‘90s song was such a hit and now, looks like this remake is also a big win among the audience as is it now trending on number 2 in India.

Ankhiyon se goli mare has been trending on number 2 in India, with 22 million views in just 2 days, and that is a LOT! Looks like remixes are the flavor of the season and this one, in particular, is winning hearts. Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar’s energetic dance moves have also been praised by many . While this peppy song from Pati Patni Aur Who trends, fans are now more excited than ever before to watch the film.
