Nigerian man shares proof of verbal abuse he suffers from his sister

22 Nov 2019, 09:57

- A Nigerian man has taken to social media to share proof of the verbal abuse he suffers from his sister

- The man identified as Victor O Cheedoh shared some abusive messages his sister constantly sends to him

- In his Facebook post, he revealed he has been going through the abuse for a decade

While most of the spotlight is shone on cases of domestic violence, abusive relationships continue to take on different forms that more often than not, leave the victims broken.

A Nigerian man identified as Victor O Cheedoh recently left many people shocked after he took to the Facebook platform to reveal how his own sister constantly abuses him verbally. He shared screenshots of text messages from her in which she constantly insults him.

He wrote: "Someone called me a useless responsible ingrate eating like a starved dog also a disrespectful untrained fatherless human being. Apparently all I do is sleep, smoke and drink to stupor. Oh and I’m a disappointment as well. To cut the long tale short.
