Kim Kardashian fumes as Versace dress doesn’t fit, still manages to look banging

22 Nov 2019, 09:31

Kim Kardashian was left fuming when she tried on a Versace dress that didn’t quite fit – but still decided to bless us with a picture of it anyway.

And we lowkey love her for it.

In a photo shared with her 152million followers, the 39-year-old could be seen posing in a floor-length, deep pink, sparkly gown, with clothes strewn all over the floor.

A (sort of) relatable queen.

‘How good would this @versace dress have been if it fit,’ the mum-of-four captioned the upload, alongside a crying emoji.

But, judging by the unimpressed look on her face, she might want to swap that crying emoji for an angry one…

Even though the dress didn’t fit, Kanye West’s wife still looked incredible in it – and fans flooded to praise the upload.

‘WOW VERSACE NEVER LOOKED BETTERRRR,’ an Instagram user screamed.

‘Kim you look amazing,’ a follower replied.

‘It looks amazing as is,’ one posted. ‘I thought the middle part was designed that way.’

While many, many more suggested she send the dress over to them instead, as they could make it fit…

Kim recently made headlines after opening up about gaining 18 pounds over the last year, explaining how hard she was working to get in shape to reach her ‘goal weight’.

In a video shared on her Instagram story, the reality star could be seen taking on an evening workout with personal trainer Melissa Alcantara before dinner.

‘Okay, so we’re doing a night workout before my dinner, which I never do, with this one, but I figured we have to get into it at some point,’ she told fans.

‘Sometimes you fall off and sometimes you’ve really got to get it together and this is one of my fallen off times where I am, I think, 18 pounds up from what I was about a year ago, a year and a half ago.’

Speaking about turning 40, she revealed that she just wants to ‘feel good’ and has a goal weight in mind.

‘I just want to get it like, a really good goal weight and so that means working out at all costs,’ she added.
