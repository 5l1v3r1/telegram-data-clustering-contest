Victoria Beckham Keeps Up with Kardashians as she cosies up to Kim and Kourtney

22 Nov 2019, 09:16

Victoria Beckham is all about Keeping Up With The Kardashians as she partied with Kim and Kourtney on Thursday.

And we’re not at all jealous of the whole thing…

Taking to Instagram, the 45-year-old shared a snap of herself posing with reality royalty as they commemorated her latest VB Beauty launch.

‘So much fun celebrating the launch of my new @augustinusbader @victoriabeckhambeauty Priming Moisturizer with @kourtneykardash @kimkardashian @violetgrey x Kisses VB,’ she captioned the shot.

Thanks for the invite, hun.

The Posh Spice singer was the very definition of chic as she rocked a crisp white suit, with black shoes.

Side note: We would’ve spilled rose and seven canapés all over that blazer way before the picture was taken…

While Kim opted for an off-the-shoulder black velvet dress coupled with matching boots for the bash, and Kourtney also got the all-black memo in a long overcoat and PVC boots.

Right on cue, fans went into total meltdown over the snap which, let’s be honest, should probably be printed out and displayed in a museum.

‘Power women I love it,’ one commented, as another agreed: ‘This is. THE best photo.’

While an Instagram user asked the question on all our minds: ‘How do we get into this girl gang?’

Over to you, VB…

Victoria has pretty much conquered the world, thanks to her turn in the Spice Girls, starting up her own fashion label and recently moving over into the beauty industry.

Basically, she’s total goals.

But the singer – who shares four children with husband David Beckham – revealed she’s most proud of being a good mum to their brood, Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper.

During a chat with Vogue, she opened up on fame, admitting her ‘two most important roles’ are being a mum and running her own business.

‘I think if you are famous and you can use that for a good cause, if you can use that and use your voice in a positive way, then I think fame is great,’ she told the publication.

‘If I can inspire anybody to just follow their dreams, however many times you’re told “No”, then that’s my job done.’
