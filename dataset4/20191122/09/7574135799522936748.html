tix for Elton John’s newly-added 2020 tour dates on sale

22 Nov 2019, 09:22 by Andrew Sacher

Tickets for all 24 newly-added Elton John 2020 North American tour dates go on sale today (11/22) at 10 AM local time.

That includes shows in Toronto, Montreal, Hershey, Columbus, Tampa, Orlando, Miami, St. Paul, Chicago, New Orleans, Houston, St. Louis, Kansas City, and more. Some tickets are still available for his previously announced shows (including seven NYC-area shows) at that same link. All dates and more info here.
