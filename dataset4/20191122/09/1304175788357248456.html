SpringHill Suites by Marriott is now open in Estero, Florida

22 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Vicky Karantzavelou

The SpringHill Suites Fort Myers Estero is located just seven miles from Southwest Florida International Airport and 20 minutes from the pristine Gulf beaches.
 

ESTERO, FL – Marriott International announced that the SpringHill Suites by Marriott in Estero, Florida is scheduled to open today. The 100-suite hotel, located at 10370 Corkscrew Commons Drive, will operate as a Marriott franchise, owned by Jagi Estero, LLC and managed by Janus Hotels & Resorts of Boca Raton, Florida.

The SpringHill Suites Fort Myers Estero is located just seven miles from Southwest Florida International Airport and 20 minutes from the pristine Gulf beaches. The hotel offers guests convenient access to JetBlue Park – the spring training home of the Boston Red Sox, Hertz Arena and Florida Gulf Coast University.

“As a brand opening a new hotel every 10 days on average, we are delighted that the SpringHill Suites Fort Myers Estero is the latest addition to our growing number of properties across the United States and Canada,” said Janis Milham, Senior Vice President and Global Brand Leader, Classic Select Brands at Marriott International. “Our all-suite offering and convenient amenities offer guests a little extra to help them enjoy their stay.”

Ideal for both business and leisure travelers, the new hotel offers suites that are larger than a typical hotel room with separate live, work and sleep spaces to give guests flexibility and functionality. In addition to comfortable beds, soft linens and plush pillows, allowing for an optimum night’s sleep, each suite also features the SpringHill Suites’ custom, three-in-one West Elm sofa which converts from a sofa to a daybed to a trundle bed, along with a stylish West Elm Swivel Chair for additional relaxation space. The designated work space includes a well-lit desk space to ensure maximum productivity.

From guest rooms to lobby, every aspect of design, from furniture and lighting to colors and fabrics, has been carefully selected to offer calm and refreshing spaces for guests to relax and recharge. The lobby also includes a brand-new, sophisticated design that will accommodate casual meetings by day and an enhanced social setting in the evening.

To make sure guests have a good start to the day, SpringHill Suites’ complimentary breakfast is filled with hot, healthy and indulgent choices including fresh strawberries, steel cut oatmeal, cold cuts, cheeses and much more. Other amenities include business services, complimentary Wi-Fi, same-day dry cleaning, a meeting room, guest laundry facilities, 24/7 Market, outdoor swimming pool and a well-equipped fitness center.
