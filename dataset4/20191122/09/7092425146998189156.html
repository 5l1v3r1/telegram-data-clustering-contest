The Killers announce additional show for Malahide Castle next summer

22 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Nick Fitzgerald

Tickets are available right now!

Due to phenomenal demand, The Killers have announced an additional show for Malahide Castle next summer for June 17, 2020.

Tickets are on sale right now at a price of €69.90 including booking fee. You can purchase them online through Ticketmaster.

Last week, it was revealed the band would play in Malahide on June 16, 2020. the extra date allows fans to grab tickets before they sell out fast.

The news comes as The Killers announced last Friday that their hotly anticipated sixth studio album, Imploding The Mirage, will be released in Spring 2020.

As part of their Imploding The Mirage Tour, fans of The Killers will now be graced with two dates one day after the other next year.

The lads will be joined at Malahide Castle by special guest Sam Fender.

In what has been an incredible year for the American rock band, their most recent appearance was headlining Glastonbury 2019 where they were joined by the Pet Shop Boys and Johnny Marr.

Since the release of their 2004 debut, Hot Fuss, with its now-classic breakthrough singles Mr Brightside and Somebody Told Me, Las Vegas’ The Killers have gone on to sell 28 million albums, win countless Grammy, Brit, Q, NME nominations and awards and headline stadia and festivals around the world.
