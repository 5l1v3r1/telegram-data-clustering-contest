EPL: Pochettino Sends ‘Touching’ Farewell Message To Tottenham Players

22 Nov 2019, 09:23 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

Mauricio Pochettino has told Tottenham players that they would always be in his heart following his sack by the Premier League side on Tuesday.

Pochettino was replaced by a former Manchester United coach Jose Mourinho on Wednesday morning by the North London side.

In a farewell message as shared by his assistant Jesus Perez, Pochettino wrote on a tactics board in the players’ dressing room:

“Big thanks to you all.  We can’t say goodbye… you will always be in our hearts.”

The pictures were posted on Perez’ Twitter account with the caption: “COYS.”
