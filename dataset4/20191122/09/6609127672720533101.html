SOLID GOLD

Inside WWE legend Bill Goldberg’s stunning mansion up for sale for £2.5m including horse stable and vineyard

22 Nov 2019, 09:03 by Dave Fraser

WWE legend Bill Goldberg has put his stunning San Diego mansion up for sale for a whopping £2.5million.

The former wrestling superstar, 52, looks to be on the move after his 20-acre California pad popped up on the market recently.

While the £2.5m price-tag might seem steep at first, the property boasts a hoard of incredible features.

Most notably, the grounds - which span 20 acres - include fully irrigated wine slopes.

With the perfect California climate, any potential buyers could turn the slopes into a fully functioning vineyard in no time.

As you'd expect, the muscle-bound star - who wrestled between 1997 and 2004, before making a comeback between 2016 and 2019 - has a monster gym at his home.

The four-bedroom, four-bathroom home also has a spacious living room, kitchen, dining room and even an office.

The back garden is perfect for the sunny West Coast days, complete with an impressive pool - with a waterfall and slide - as well as a spa.

Outside, the gated home can be entered via a long driveway - while there is also a basketball court, a fire pit and a horse stable.

But the piece de resistance outside is the humongous garage, large enough to fit more than 20 vehicles.

The huge building has even featured on American TV shows Garage Dreams and Garage Mahal for its one-of-a-kind appeal.

Since retiring from wrestling, the former WCW World Heavyweight Championship winner, has gone into acting.

As well as appearing in Adam Sandler film The Longest Yard in 2005 amongst others, Goldberg has appeared in a host of TV shows.

Along with appearances on reality programmes such as Garage Mahal and Punk'd, the American has provided voiceovers in Family Guy and Kim Possible, as well as roles in Law & Order and NCIS.

More recently, Goldberg played PE coach Nick Mellor in hit show, The Goldbergs...
