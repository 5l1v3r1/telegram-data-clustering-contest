Edinburgh has a six week Christmas spectacular lined up for families to enjoy

Christmas Magic sees buildings come to life during the festive season complete with light displays and choirs

22 Nov 2019, 09:58 by Lorraine Howard, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/lorraine-howard/

Edinburgh’s Christmas is the perfect place to get into the festive spirit with a spectacular, six-week event for people of all ages to enjoy, nestled in the heart of Edinburgh.

In East Princes Street Gardens you can enjoy traditional markets, complete with incredible food and drink, rides and attractions, including the Forth 1 Big Wheel, where you’ll get dazzling views of the city.

Christmas just wouldn’t be Christmas without a visit to see Santa, so head down to Castle Street to meet the man himself, listen to a magical story and receive a wee gift.

Add to the fun with a ride on the Helter Skelter and then warm up with some delicious mulled wine or hot chocolate from the bar below. Santa will be on Castle St until Christmas Eve.

New for 2019 is Community Christmas. Edinburgh’s Christmas magic will be heading to twelve locations over twelve nights around Edinburgh, bringing a building to life in each locality for one night only with winter projections accompanied by local choirs performing some of their best-loved festive tunes for their local community.

Edinburgh’s Christmas is spreading the festive cheer from until Saturday, January 4, so head to Edinburgh Christmas and book to beat the queues.

Plus, EH postcode holders can get 20 per cent off rides and attractions.
