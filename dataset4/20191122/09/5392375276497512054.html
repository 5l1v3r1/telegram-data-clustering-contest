Twitter now lets you hide the replies to your tweets
5 (100%) 1 vote

After the ability to schedule your tweets, Twitter continues to roll out new features for its platform users. Tested for months, especially in Canada, the new feature to hide replies to a tweet is being rolled out to all users.

The feature is available to all users, both on the web and on mobile apps, including Twitter Lite. The ability to hide replies to a tweet is intended to prevent conversations from slipping or taking a negative turn.
