Loveable mutt needs help

22 Nov 2019, 09:56 by Darren Handschuh

Teki is in need of some human help.

The loveable senior dog needs the public's help to cover medical costs.

Teki had surgery to remove two painful lumps and has received treatment for a severe dental disease.

At almost 13 years old, Teki arrived at the SPCA underweight, but has been slowly gaining weight and recovering her strength.

“Teki loves people and gets along well with other dogs. She is a very laid-back girl who loves nothing more than to fall asleep on the couch in front of a warm fire,” said Chelsea Taylor, SPCA Vernon and District Branch manager.

Her estimated cost of care is $1,690.

“We need to raise funds for Teki and the many other senior animals that end up in shelters in their twilight years,” said Taylor. “Teki is such a sweetie and deserves an awesome retirement home.”

Staff are hopeful that Teki will be able to spend the holidays in her new forever home and she is currently available for adoption.
