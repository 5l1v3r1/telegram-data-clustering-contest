I found out my boyfriend was married during my pregnancy – Lady recounts her ordeal

22 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Damilola Ismail

A lady has recounted her experience with her boyfriend who she got pregnant for, only to find out that he was married.

According to her, she feel into an early labour after she found out her boyfriend was married during her pregnancy and this led to her having their baby early.

After a few days of delivery via a Cesarean section, their son died as a result of difficulty in breathing. On reaching out to her boyfriend to share the sad news, he immediately asked her to stop calling him because his wife was beginning to suspect.

She shared;

I found out during my pregnancy my boyfriend was married, I went into labour too early and had to have cesarean birth my son was only alive for a few days before having breathing difficulties. told him he’s son died and he said he’s wife already suspicious I should stop calling.
