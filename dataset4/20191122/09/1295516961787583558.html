Transformers: Super7 Goes Old School with New ReAction Figures

22 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Noah Dominguez

Pop culture design house and collectible prouder Super7 has launched a new series of Transformers action figures, which harken back to the original toys that first hit shelved back in the 1980s.

Dubbed the "ReAction Figures," this collection of 3.75" retro-style figures faithfully recreates the Robots in Disguise as they appeared in the original Transformers cartoon. Each figure comes blister-packaged on a classic-style card back, which features brand-new '80s-inspired artwork.

The first generation of Transformers ReAction Figures includes some of the most iconic Autobots and Decepticons old school fans of the franchise know and love. Representing the good guys are Optimus Prime, Jazz and Bumblebee. The villainous Megatron, Soundwave and Starscream are present and accounted for as well. It should be noted that the ReAction Figures' packing makes it clear that these blasts from the past are collectibles, not toys. Moreover, the figures do not actually transform. As such, they are recommended for collectors at least 14 years of age.

Super7's first generation of Transformers ReAction Figures are available for purchase now. Each figure retails for $18 USD and comes with an accessory.
