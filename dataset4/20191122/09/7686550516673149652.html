Welsh darts star Gerwyn Price has an unlikely fan in Hollywood A-list actor Matthew McConaughey

Star of The Wolf of Wall Street delivers out of the blue thumbs-up for former Welsh rugby player turned darts ace Gerwyn Price

22 Nov 2019, 09:55 by Anthony Woolford

Gerwyn Price's new found fame and fortune as a PDC darts star is attracting the attention of the Hollywood glitterati.

The Welshman, who successfully defended his Grand Slam of Darts title with an emphatic 16-6 win over Peter Wright last weekend, has a new member of his growing fan club in American film star Matthew McConaughey.

And the former Neath and Cross Keys hooker could well find the A-lister, whose list of film credits includes Tropic Thunder, The Wedding Planner, The Lincoln Lawyer and The Wolf of Wall Street, roaring him on after the PDC announced their World Series will land at Madison Square Garden, New York in June next year.

The news came on the day McConaughey gave a shout out to world number three Price after defending his Grand Slam of Darts title in Wolverhampton.

The actor tweeted out: "Big ups to the Iceman, Gezzy Price for taking out MVG and the Eagle to win his second consecutive Grand Slam of Darts."
