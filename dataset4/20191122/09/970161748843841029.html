Spoilers: EastEnders' Jake Wood reveals 'spectacular' proposal for Max and Ruby

22 Nov 2019, 09:00

Max Branning (Jake Wood) and Ruby Allen (Louisa Lytton) have only been dating a couple of months in EastEnders, but Max is determined to hold on to his girlfriend, and thus he resolves to take their relationship to the next level by popping the big question — a task he undertakes during tonight’s episode.

Max has experienced a lot of heartache over the past couple of years — and this year has been one of his toughest yet, as he found himself going head-to-head with Rainie (Tanya Franks) over custody of baby Abi. Amid all of the devastation, however, he’s managed to find a little bit of happiness in his relationship with Ruby.

Speaking about Max’s journey over the last couple of months, actor Jake Wood told Metro.co.uk: ‘I think this last year — year and a half — has been about Max finding his feet really after everything he’s been through. You know, trying to get custody of Abi.’

‘I think at the minute it feels like Max is getting settled and getting his feet on the ground, and getting back into normal life, if you like.’

Last night, viewers saw Max become embarrassed when a punter in E20 attempted to buy Ruby a drink, as he’d assumed that Max was simply Ruby’s dad.

The conversation got Max thinking, and — after making light of the situation — Jack (Scott Maslen) suggested that Max needs to up his game if he wants to keep Ruby.

Tonight, he takes it upon himself to pop the question, but how exactly does he come to this conclusion?

Jake told us: ‘He thinks that the right thing to do is to propose. He’s not exactly sure what Ruby wants, but I’m not exactly sure he knows what he wants. I think he just wants to make a commitment to her.’
