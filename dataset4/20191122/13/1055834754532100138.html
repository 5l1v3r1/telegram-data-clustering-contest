Super Smash Bros Ultimate: Pokemon Sword & Shield spirit event live

22 Nov 2019, 13:46 by My Nintendo News Admin

The new Pokemon Sword & Shield Spirit event is now live for the engrossing Super Smash Bros Ultimate. Players of the game will be tasked with getting Spirits based on Pokemon which can be found in the Galar Region. Those Pokemon are as follows: Zacian & Zamazenta, Grookey, Scorbunny and Sobble, Morpeko and Corviknight. The event is currently up and running in Super Smash Bros Ultimate and will end in 5 days. Enjoy!
