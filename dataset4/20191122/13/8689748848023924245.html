Joey B Cautions Men To Be Wary Of Women, Money

22 Nov 2019, 13:21 by Ghbase New Writer

Women and money are men’s weakness and there are a lot of nonfiction stories to substantiate this theory.

Rapper Joey B is much conscious of this too and has subsequently warned all men not be reduced to nothing when they have them in abundance.

The former Black Avenue Muzik signee said women and money can make or unmake a man hence men should be wary on how to deal with them.

He sounded this warning during an interview session with Andy Dosty on Hitz FM: “You are bound to fail if you cannot control women and money.

Money and Women can make and unmake you if you allow these two to control you”, he said.
