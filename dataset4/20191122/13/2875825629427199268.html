Take Me Out plunges into toilet humour with disgusting 'hairy poo flirting'

Eyebrow raising scenes see guest Charlie get asked if his poo is really hairy by potential date Chloe

22 Nov 2019, 13:18 by Irishmirror.ie

Take Me Out plummets into toilet humour with gruesome "hair poo" flirting.

In eyebrow raising scenes, potential date Chloe asks guest Charlie if his poo is "really hairy" when they speak for the first time on the dating show.

In his VT, the guest's mum divulges her son's bizarre habit of perfectly shaping his eyebrows and devouring all the hairs.

As always, the audience of girls waiting to go on a date with the handsome sports therapist had a lot of questions.

But Chloe only has one thing on her mind. She says: "I’ve got a question, is your poo really hairy?"

Take Me Out host Paddy McGuinness jaw hits the floor when he watches on in horror.

Feeling the embarrassment, the TV host crawls into the lift and pleads for it to go up.

He says: "Take me up, anything! That’s a vision I’ll never be able to unsee!"

Already, Paddy is left feeling sick by the idea of Charlie munching on his eyebrows.

The dad gags and pretends to be sick after the VT plays out on the screen for the potential pool of dates.

He says: "I went with the egg whites Charlie but I’m not having that! [to Chloe] I mean, Chloe, I’m gonna ask you why you turned off but I think we know why."

Charlie's mum let her son's secret out the bag as she attempts to get him a date by painting a realistic picture of the sports therapist.

And as it turns out, she leaves nothing out.

Charlie’s mum could be heard saying: "My son Charlie, his eyebrows are always on point. But he picks them himself and he has an unusual way of disposing of the remains – he eats them.

"So girls, if Charlie’s eyebrow munching doesn’t make you frown, he could be the one for you."

Take Me Out will air on Saturday, 6:25pm on ITV.
