Black Friday PS4 game deals up to 73% off - Spider-Man for £16, GTA 5 for £8

22 Nov 2019, 13:30

Sony’s official PlayStation Store Black Friday deals are looking pretty good, with FIFA 20 for £40 and Borderlands 3 for £35.

By next year it’s probably going to be called Black November, not Black Friday, so it’ll come as no surprise to find that Sony’s Black Friday PlayStation deals have already started.

The full list of deals is available via the PlayStation Blog, but amongst the most tempting are FIFA 20 for just £39.99, NBA 2K20 for £29.99, Marvel’s Spider-Man for £15.99, and GTA V Premium Online Edition for just £7.99.

There’s also Borderlands 3 for £34.99, Crash Team Racing Nitro-Fueled for £22.74, Days Gone for £34.99, God of War Digital Deluxe Edition for £24.99, and both Devil May Cry 5 and Metro Exodus for a miniscule £15.99.

The biggest discount though is poor old Anthem, which, at 73% off, is also just £15.99. Although the considerably better received Persona 5 Ultimate Edition is reduced by the same percentage to £19.99 (the standard edition is also just £14.99).
