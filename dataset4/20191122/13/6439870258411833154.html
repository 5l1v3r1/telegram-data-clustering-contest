Rowan County road closed for hours after crash

ROWAN COUNTY, N.C. (WBTV) - One person was killed and two others were injured in a crash on Highway 150 in Rowan County Friday morning.

The wreck happened Friday morning on Highway 150 (Mooresville Road) near Jones Road when a car and SUV collided. Troopers say the driver of the car died at the scene.
