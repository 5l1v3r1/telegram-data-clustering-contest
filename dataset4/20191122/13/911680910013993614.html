St. Mary's Food Bank looking for donations of Thanksgiving turkeys

22 Nov 2019, 13:37 by abc15.com staff

PHOENIX — Wanted: 10,000 turkeys for families in need.

St. Mary’s Food Bank is asking for help reaching its goal of 10,000 turkeys to distribute to families this Thanksgiving.

On Saturday, November 23, St. Mary’s is hosting its 13th annual Super Saturday Turkey Drive. The event goes from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. at locations around the Valley.

Last year, the food bank provided 11,000 holiday food boxes in the three days leading up to Thanksgiving. Their hope is to provide families with a turkey and other holiday dinner items.

Thanksgiving food box distribution will happen this year from Monday, November 25 through Wednesday, November 27. Families who wish to receive a box will need to bring an ID and are asked to only visit once per family. Read time and location details on distribution here.

What to donate on Super Saturday:

  • Turkeys
  • Other food (both perishable and non-perishable)
  • Money
