WATCH: Lorry ploughs through level crossing barrier causing train delays

Footage has been released showing the moment a lorry ploughed through a level crossing barrier, causing delays for trains across the West Midlands.

22 Nov 2019, 13:47 by Thomas Parkes

One barrier was completely destroyed after the vehicle smashed into it at Hartlebury station, south of Kidderminster, this morning.

All lines were disrupted between Stourbridge Junction and Droitwich Spa in the aftermath, with cancellations and delays of up to 60 minutes as a result.
