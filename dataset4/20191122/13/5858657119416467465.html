ABBOTSFORD (NEWS 1130) — Police have now identified the man who rented an SUV that was found burnt out with a man’s body inside last weekend in Abbotsford.

Crews were called to a vehicle fire on Bates Road between Townshipline Road and Harris Road around 10:30 p.m. on Nov. 15.

Once the fire was out, a body was discovered inside. Homicide investigators were called in by the Abbotsford Police Department.

Investigators say that the SUV, a black Chevy Tahoe, was a rented to a 48-year-old man named Sukhdev Dhaliwal.
