DOWNLOAD: Timi Dakolo drops 11-track album ‘Merry Christmas Darling’

22 Nov 2019, 13:34 by Stephen Charles Kenechukwu

Timi Dakolo, Nigerian gospel singer, has put out his much-anticipated album entitled ‘Merry Christmas Darling’ — ahead of the 2019 Yuletide season.

The 11-track body of work, which was released on Friday, features Emeli Sande, British singer and songwriter, and comes shortly after the 38-year-old award-winning musician had put out ‘Merry Christmas Darling‘, the lead track off the project.

The album also comes not long after the singer signed a record deal with Virgin EMI, a subsidiary label under the Universal Music Group (UMG).
