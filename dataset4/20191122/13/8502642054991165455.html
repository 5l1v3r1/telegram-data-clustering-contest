Ukraine grain exports up 36% yr/yr -ministry

22 Nov 2019, 13:28

KIEV, Nov 22 (Reuters) - Ukraine has increased its grain exports by around 36% to 23.1 million tonnes in the 2019/20 July-June season helped by higher wheat and corn exports, the ministry of economy and agriculture said on Friday.

Wheat exports rose to 12.9 million tonnes from 8.9 million a year earlier, the ministry said in a statement.

Ukraine has exported 3.5 million tonnes of barley and 6.5 million tonnes of corn, it said. (Reporting by Pavel Polityuk; editing by Jason Neely)
