Retirement Advisor: Between A Lump Sum And An Annuity (Podcast)

22 Nov 2019, 13:30 by SA For FAs

Summary

  • The Brookings Institution’s Retirement Security Project suggests that managed-payout funds might be the Goldilocks option between lump sums and annuities.
  • Unlike target-date funds, which seek capital appreciation, mitigated by an age-based formula for reducing exposure to market risk, managed-payout funds offer a distribution target, such as 4%.
  • Investors don’t get the guarantee of an annuity, but they get a conservatively managed fund, providing a retirement paycheck, and their capital does not die with them.

This podcast (6:44) notes that Brookings’ policy wonks are proposing very much the sort of retirement portfolio that many financial advisors seek to put in place – a growth portfolio, a monthly paycheck, an emergency fund and longevity protection.
