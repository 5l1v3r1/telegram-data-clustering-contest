Bellator: Fabian Edwards sparks melee with Mike Shipman and MVP at weigh-ins

22 Nov 2019, 13:02

Fabian Edwards and Mike Shipman had to be separated by security as a heated face-off turned physical on Friday morning.

The middleweights were weighing in for their clash at Bellator London and tempers boiled over.

Edwards didn’t take his eyes off Shipman when he entered the room to step on the scales and then accused his rival of looking scared.

‘Why you flinching?’ Edwards said before moving his hand towards the face of Shipman.
