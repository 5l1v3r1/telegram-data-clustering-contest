Man recounts encounter with former university mate who became a taxi driver

22 Nov 2019, 13:02

- A Nigerian man identified as Ehi Onyibe has recounted his experience with his former university mate on Twitter

- Ehi was at the mall when he met his old school mate and they chatted for a while before parting ways

- Th eventually had to order a cab home and the taxi driver ended up being the school mate he had just chatted with

- Rather than wait to take the order, the cab driver drove off after spotting Ehi

Having a good education and being without a job for so long can be a dent in the confidence of many people and they resort to doing business in order to make ends meet.

These days, many promising graduates work as taxi drivers pending the time they get another job or they just sustain it and hope for the best. Well, a young Nigerian man identified as Ehi Onyibe on Twitter has taken to the site to share his experience with a former university mate turned cab driver who ran away after spotting him.

Ehi had visited the mall in Ikeja, Lagos, with his family when he bumped into one of his mates from Covenant University. They exchanged greetings and went their separate ways.
