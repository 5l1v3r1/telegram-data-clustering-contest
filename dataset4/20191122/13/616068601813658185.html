3-car crash in drive-thru

22 Nov 2019, 13:48 by Chelsea Powrie

A few drivers may have been in too much of a McHurry for food at Penticton's McDonalds, ending up in a three-car crash. 

Mid-morning Friday, some hungry travellers crashed into each other in the lineup for the drive-thru window. 

Penticton deputy fire chief Chris Forster confirmed crews responded to the incident, saying three cars were involved in the rear-enders. 

Nobody was hurt, and it is not known whether those drivers are still hungry.
