Caitlyn Jenner reveals the sweet nickname her grandchildren have for her

The reality star has almost 20 grandchildren

22 Nov 2019, 13:17 by Kendra Becker

Caitlyn Jenner has revealed the sweet nickname her grandchildren have for her.

The transgender star, formerly known as Bruce, split from Kardashian matriarch Kris Jenner back in 2013 – but she still sees herself as a grandparent to the Kardashian kids.

During a chat with her campmates in the jungle, Caitlyn revealed she’ll have 20 grandchildren in December.

Kate Garraway then asked what her grandkids call her, and she said: “They came up with Boom Boom. It kinda stuck, it was easy for them to say.”
