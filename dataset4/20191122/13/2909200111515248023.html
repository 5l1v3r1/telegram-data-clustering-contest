Maths teacher arrested for ‘chopping’ Sawla SHS student in Staff Common room

22 Nov 2019, 13:14 by MyNewsGH

A Maths teacher of the Sawla Senior High School (SHS) in the Sawla-Tuna-Kalba District of the Savannah Region is in the firm grips of the police after he was caught making love to a female student of the school in the Staff Common Room.

Authorities of the school claim the teacher raped the 19-year-old female student in question when classes were not in session but latest information emerging indicates that suspect, Ernest Ocloo and the victim have been engaged in amorous sexual relationship for some time now.

Police have confirmed the arrest of the teacher who doubles as the Entertainment Master of the school and revealed that he is currently in their custody assisting with investigations.

Snippets of information gathered by MyNewsGh.com indicate that the randy mathematics teacher subjected his student fiancé to several bouts of sex using the table in the staff common room as a makeshift bed until the victim became unconscious.

He was said to have sought the assistance of a colleague to resuscitate the partially naked unconscious female student which leaked the information leading to the headmaster lodging an official complaint with the police and he was subsequently arrested.

The victim (name withheld) in her caution statement to the polive  insists she was not raped as claimed by the authorities of the school indicating that they had consensual sex but she passed out as a result of the excitement.

Colleague Tutors at the Sawla Senior High School suspect a frame up by the authorities just to get the teacher out of the school over unknown reasons.
