US: Amazon opens official Nintendo Merch storefront

22 Nov 2019, 13:33 by My Nintendo News Admin

Popular North American retailer Amazon has worked alongside Nintendo to open a dedicated storefront for Nintendo merchandise. There’s a wealth of lovely goodies available including cute Yoshi t-shirts, Zelda hoodies, figures and plushies. It is well worth having a browse if you are looking to add more Nintendo merchandise to your collection as you don’t know what you will find.
