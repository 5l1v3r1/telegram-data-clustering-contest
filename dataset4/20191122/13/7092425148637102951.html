Gardai seeking the public's assistance in tracing Reece Murphy

22 Nov 2019, 13:50 by Nick Fitzgerald

The 17-year-old was last seen in the Ballyjamesduff area of County Cavan on Wednesday, November 20

Gardai are seeking the public's assistance in tracing the whereabouts of Reece Murphy who was last seen in the Ballyjamesduff area of County Cavan on Wednesday, November 20.

The 17-year-old is being described as being 6 foot in height, of slim build with black hair.

When Reece was last seen he was wearing grey tracksuit bottoms, a bright blue hoody, black runners and black stud earrings.
