With Reduced Time And Modern Amenities, Vande Bharat Express A Big Hit With Indian Railway Passengers

22 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Swarajya Staff

Railways Minister Piyush Goyal said that Vande Bharat Express trains have become a hit and Indian Railways are aiming to introduce a new era of world-class train travel experience, report Financial Express.

Vande Bharat Express has been well received by passengers with one train running between Delhi-Varanasi and the another between Delhi-Sri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra.

Reportedly, the Railway Ministry informed that train number 22435/36 Delhi-Varanasi-Delhi Vande Bharat Express saw an occupancy of 115.71%, while train number 22439/40 Delhi-Sri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra-Delhi Vande Bharat Express saw an occupancy of 99.27%.

The occupancy recorded by both these trains is inclusive of bookings done for en-route/intermediate stations.

These ‘Make in India’ trains have made commuting between Delhi and Varanasi, Delhi and Katra very comfortable and travel time has come down to just 8 hours.

Both trains are semi-high speed trains with modern facilities and amenities including European-style seats, modular toilets, on-board hotspot WiFi, GPS-based audio-visual passenger information system, LED lighting, automatic doors, etc.
