Kanye West and Dr Dre collaborating on Jesus Is King II album

22 Nov 2019, 13:31

- Hip-hop fans are in for a treat as two of the genre's greats are teaming up for a collaboration album

- Rapper Kanye West and legendary producer Dr Dre announced that they are working on a joint album

- The album is to be titled Jesus Is King Part II, paying homage to West's first gospel record Jesus Is King

Kanye West announced a joint project with Dr Dre on his Twitter account late yesterday evening.

He posted a picture of himself and Dr Dre in the studio. West captioned the picture, "Ye and Dre Jesus is King Part II coming soon."

This will, surprisingly, be the first time the two stars work together.

But then again, Dre is quite the recluse these days and hardly releases new music or performs. That's what will make this project extra special for the pair's fans.
