Video: Real Madrid ace mocks Gareth Bale with golf swing after he messes up in training

22 Nov 2019, 13:57 by Tom McNeil

If there were any hopes from Real Madrid that they might be able to sweep the latest Gareth Bale controversy under the carpet, they are now long gone after some of the players have started to reference it.

A video from AS showed a clip from Real Madrid training where Mariano Diaz mocked Bale with a golf swing celebration after the Welshman messed up during a drill.
