22 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Nollywood actor, Blossom Chukwujekwu’s estranged wife, Maureen Esisi took to her social space to reveal her biggest lesson this year.

The biggest lesson I’ve learnt this year is not to force anything – Blossom Chukwujekwu’s estranged wife, Maureen writesAccording to Maureen Esisi, she learnt never to force anything which includes relationships, conversation, love and others. She added that whatever is forced is not worth fighting for.

However, Maureen Esisi was dragged into an exchange with a troll shortly after she shared the post. The troll interpreted the post as “Maureen missing her husband” but Maureen was not having any of that. She fired back.
