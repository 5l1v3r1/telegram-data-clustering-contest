ST sampling 4Mbit serial EEPROM

22 Nov 2019, 06:00 by David Manners

ST is sampling 4Mbit serial EEPROMs made on ST’s 110nm process.

The memories can write 512 bytes in 5ms.

The chips come in 8-pin SO8N and TSSOP8 packages with the option of an 8-bump WLCSP package.

The EEPROMs, designated M95M04, have a supply range of 1.8V to 5.5V.

Data retention is 40-years.

ST sees the chips as useful in small devices which require storage via the SPI serial bus such as smart meters, portable medical devices, wearables and network switches.

The 1000-up price is $2.50.
