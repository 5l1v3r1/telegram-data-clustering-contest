Harry hurt: more bad news for the Blues

22 Nov 2019, 06:43 by 3AW Football

Carlton has copped another injury blow to one of its young guns, with Harry McKay dealing with a groin issue.

The Blues announced on Friday he’d be on a modified training program until at least Christmas.

Carlton is still coming to terms with news of a significant setback to Charlie Curnow.

Curnow has undergone surgery to repair a fractured patella in his right knee.

He’ll miss three to four months of training.
