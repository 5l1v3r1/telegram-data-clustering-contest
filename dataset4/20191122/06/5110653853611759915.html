Steven Stamkos injury update: Tampa Bay Lightning star (lower body) misses most of win

22 Nov 2019, 06:29 by Rudi Schuller

The Tampa Bay Lightning won their game Thursday vs. the Chicago Blackhawksbut lost their captain in the process.

Steven Stamkos left the 4-2 victory after suffering a lower-body injury in the first period, according to Tampa Bay coach Jon Cooper. Stamkos played just over five minutes in the contestbut remained on the bench for the second and third periods.
