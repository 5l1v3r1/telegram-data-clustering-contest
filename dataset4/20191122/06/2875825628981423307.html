There are four horse racing meetings to get stuck into on Friday.

There's action at Catterick, Ascot, Ffos Las and Newcastle - and our man Newsboy has scoured the form to bring you the best bets and tips.

With some favourites and a few better value bets, as well as a more ambitious daily double, there is plenty to interest the punters.

We've also got the NAP and Next Best bet - have a look and see what tickles your interest, but please remember to always gamble responsibly.

Footpad began his season with a bang to earn a quote of 8-1 for the Ryanair Chase at Cheltenham in March, writes David Yates .

The Willie Mullins seven-year-old was a perfect five from five as a novice chase during the 2017-18 campaign - but last term was a damp squib with defeats in all three starts.

Sent off at 4-5 for the two-mile, six-furlong Listed Boomerang.ie Chase at Thurles, Daryl Jacob’s mount strolled home by 10 lengths.

“It is lovely to see the horse back on the racetrack and he had plenty of enthusiasm and jumped and travelled very, very well,” enthused the winning rider.

“It's a great starting point for him and he never missed a beat the whole way around.

“Today's trip wasn't a problem for him and ideally going a little bit quicker early doors would have suited him, but he is nice and fresh and it was good to see a performance like that this early in the year.

“It was mission accomplished today,” added Jacob, whose mount is 12-1 with Paddy Power for the King George VI Chase at Kempton Park on Boxing Day.

Hollie Doyle became the third female jockey to rack up 100 winners in a calendar year when Perfect Crown made a victorious debut at Chelmsford City yesterday.

DOUBLE: RED FORCE ONE and PERFECT SWISS

RED FORCE ONE gets a confident vote to atone for a narrow defeat over course and distance 10 days ago with a victory in the concluding Rewarding Ownership With The ROA Handicap Hurdle (3.45).

The Paul Nicholls four-year-old went under by a neck to all-the-way scorer Gumball - the winner ran a cracker when second in the Greatwood Hurdle last Sunday - and a 4lb rise in the weights for that run looks within range.
