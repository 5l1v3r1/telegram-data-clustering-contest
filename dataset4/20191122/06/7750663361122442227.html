Friday's Chelsea transfer talk news roundup: Ben Chilwell, Olivier Giroud

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Chelsea eye possible reinforcements.

22 Nov 2019, 06:53 by Andy Brent

The Blues are currently under a transfer embargo, which has forced them to turn to youngsters who have impressed during the first half of the season, but they are hopeful of overturning the ban in time to sign players in January.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest news on possible comings and goings from Stamford Bridge.
