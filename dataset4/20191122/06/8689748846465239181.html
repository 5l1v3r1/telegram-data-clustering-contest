‘Your Mothers’- Shatta Wale Loses His Cool On Ghanaian Bloggers

22 Nov 2019, 06:22 by BB Jones

Shatta Wale has lost his cool on journalists and bloggers who continue to discredit him on the back of the trending Grammy Awards nomination.

It emerged yesterday that blogger and radio presenter NYDJ said he personally sent an email to the organisers of the Grammys for clarification on the Shatta Wale Grammy Award brouhaha and reply was sent to him to that effect.

“According to a mail received from the Grammy Awards, only Artists who have worked on over 51% playing time of the pop vocal album would be nominees.

Any featured artist with less than 51% would not be a nominee. That settles it. Shatta Wale is not a Grammy Nominee.”

The issue has generated a lot of debate on social media and as we know, SM fans are not happy with the attitude of Ghanaian journalists for this propaganda.

The issue has attracted the attention of Shatta Wale and it has annoyed him to the core.

He responded in a Snapchat saying: “So Shatta Wale doesn’t deserve the best erh? your mother waa!!
