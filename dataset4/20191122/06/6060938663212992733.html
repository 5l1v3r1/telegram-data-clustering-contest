Trolls attacking Nysa Devgan over wearing ‘inappropriate dress’ in temple should shut up

22 Nov 2019, 06:51 by TrollSlayer

Bollywood actors Kajol and Ajay Devgan’s daughter Nysa recently got trolled after a photo of her visiting a temple with her father. She invited wrath for wearing a cropped top in a religious place.

Here’s what trolls said and why they should shut up:

Troll Attack 1: Such inappropriate clothes that expose the waist of a woman shouldn’t be worn in a temple

What we’re going to tell you is a done-to-death analogy. But just for the sake of these seemingly illiterate trolls, let’s just leave this here - “The two inch gap in saree is traditional and graceful but one inch gap in cropped tops is shameless”. Doesn’t make any sense, right?

Where is it written that only traditional clothes ‘covering’ your entire body have to be worn to a temple?

God is everywhere, not just in a temple or a mosque or a church. So by trolls’ flawed logic women shouldn’t be wearing crop tops, swimsuits, bikinis or skirts anywhere, because what if “God gets upset?”

Troll Attack 2: “Is she going to a temple or a gym?”

Is there a dress code in our constitution for these places? Did she violate any law by wearing what she wore? Isn’t the entire idea of going to a religious place to experience some peace within? Then what’s the harm if Nysa wanted to wear clothes she felt comfortable in?

It’d be good if men going to temples and gyms concentrated on why they’re there instead of leering at women and focusing on what they’re wearing. If even in a temple you can’t help but gaze at a young girl, it’s better you don’t go to a temple at all.

Troll Attack 3: “My father would’ve slapped me for wearing that in a temple”

Well then, Nysa should thank her stars that she’s blessed with a liberal, understanding and sweet father who respects and supports her choices and lets her be. Hope all fathers will learn a thing or two from Ajay.
