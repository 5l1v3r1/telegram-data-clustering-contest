It’s better to exchange sex for movie roles- Prince David Osei

22 Nov 2019, 06:16 by MyNewsGH

Actor, Prince David Osei says it’s better to exchange sex for movie roles than ice creams and the pack of friend rice.

According to him, there is the need to stop judging people who are engaged in the act

He said “Even though I don’t have evidence for these claims, I don’t think it is a big deal if two adults have consensual sex. Sex happens everywhere and the practice is not peculiar to the movie or entertainment sector alone.

“Some ladies have exchanged sex for a bowl of fried rice, ice cream and some get payment for as low as GH¢10 so if another wants to do it to secure a role, I don’t see anything wrong with it.

“However, you should be talented so that you ride on that to achieve your target or dream as an actor.”
