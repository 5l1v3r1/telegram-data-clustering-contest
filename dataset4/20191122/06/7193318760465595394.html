OSU vs. Western Michigan basketball: Lineups, tip-off time and TV info

22 Nov 2019, 06:04 by Jacob Unruh

Oklahoma State vs. Western Michigan

When: 7 p.m. Friday

Where: Gallagher-Iba Arena, Stillwater

TV: ESPN+/Big 12 Now (online only)

Radio: KXXY-FM 96.1

Three things to know

• OSU point guard Isaac Likekele ranks in the top 20 of the Big 12 in four categories — 12.8 points (No. 17), 5.8 rebounds (No. 12), 4.8 assists (No. 5) and 2.0 steals (No. 6) per game. In his past 11 games dating to last season, Likekele has averaged 11.9 points, 5.0 rebounds, 4.7 assists and 1.5 steals.

• At 4-0, the Cowboys are off to their best start since the 2016-17 season. A win would give OSU its best start since opening with seven straight victories in the 2014-15 season.

• This is OSU’s first matchup with Western Michigan in program history and the first time a Mid-American Conference team has visited Gallagher-Iba Arena. The Cowboys are 4-2 all-time against MAC opponents.
