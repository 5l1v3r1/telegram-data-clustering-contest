'Bernie vs. Bernie': Presidential Hopeful Sanders Interviews ‘Himself’

22 Nov 2019, 06:05

From time to time every person feels the need to have a talk with a broadminded and intelligent conversational partner, having in mind themselves, of course. That was possibly one of the motives behind US presidential candidate Bernie Sanders' idea.

US Democratic senator and presidential candidate in the 2020 US elections, Bernie Sanders, appeared in a video interview titled “Bernie vs. Bernie” with his well-known comedian impersonator James Adomian.

"What I said to myself, was, 'we don't get a fair shot in the mainstream media, so I said, what the heck, what the hay, I would interview myself'. So here we are", said the impersonator at the beginning of the interview.
