Honolulu Acting Prosecuting Attorney Dwight Nadamoto appears before grand jury for 3 hours | Honolulu Star-Advertiser

22 Nov 2019, 16:46

Honolulu Acting Prosecuting Attorney Dwight Nadamoto spent roughly three hours before a grand jury in U.S. District Court today and emerged with little to say to reporters.

Nadamoto said he cannot provide details of what federal prosecutors asked him, citing the ongoing investigation, but did say that he would need to appear again.
