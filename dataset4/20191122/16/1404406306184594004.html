Michael Jackson's Estate Approves Biopic That Will Cover His Entire Life

22 Nov 2019, 16:45 by VladTV

Michael Jackson's estate has signed off on a biopic that will be produced by Graham King, who previously produced the Freddie Mercury biopic Bohemian Rhapsody, according to Deadline. King’s GK Films has retained three-time Oscar-nominated writer John Logan to write the script, while the estate has allowed access to Jackson's entire catalog of music. 

While the details are still coming together on the project, the film will cover Jackson's full life, including some of the more complex details that were reported throughout his life.
