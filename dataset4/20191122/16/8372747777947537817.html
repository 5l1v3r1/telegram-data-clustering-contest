Friday night's Mega Millions drawing worth $208 million

22 Nov 2019, 16:10 by WFTX Digital Team

TALLAHASSEE, Fla. -- The jackpot in the Mega Millions lottery game continues to grow.

Friday night's jackpot is worth an estimated $208 million, according to the Florida Lottery.

The last winner was on September 24th, when an anonymous Texas player won $225 million.

The next drawing will be on Friday night at 11 p.m. But good luck; your chances of winning are 1 in 302,575,350.

If you miss out on that jackpot, Fridays night's PowerBall drawing is worth an estimated $90 million.

And as always, you can catch these lottery drawings live on Fox 4 at 11 p.m.
