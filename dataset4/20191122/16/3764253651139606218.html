Diego Maradona returns to Gimnasia 2 days after quitting the club

22 Nov 2019, 16:03

- Diego Maradona has decided to return to Argentine club Gimnasia

- He left the club two days ago after being in charge of eight games winning three of those ties

- Maradona took to social media to announce his latest decision on Friday, November 22

Argentine football legend Diego Maradona has announced that he will return to Gimnasia which is a club in his country two days after quitting the side.

Maradona left the club after eight matches where Diego Maradona was only able to win three games and lost five encounters.

Diego Maradona however announced on Friday, November 22, that he will return to Gimnasia immediately.

His contract was due to run until the end of the League season on December 8, but he walked away from his post with three fixtures remaining.
