#WelcometoJakartaEXO trending worldwide as EXO lands in Jakarta for their 'EXO PLANET #5 – EXplOration' tour

22 Nov 2019, 16:36 by sl278

EXO fans in Jakarta are greeting the group with a massive welcome by trending the hashtag #WelcometoJakartaEXO as the boys arrived at the airport for a stretch of their 'EXO PLANET #5 – EXplOration' tour.

Excited fans are sharing footage of the boys arriving at the airport. The videos show large crowds at and outside the airport as fans lined up to get a glimpse of the members.
