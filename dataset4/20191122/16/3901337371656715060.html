Strong Starting Line-Up As SA Chase Olympic Dream

22 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Soccer Laduma

The crucial match between the South African Under-23 team and Ghana is currently underway as both teams are in pursuit of qualifying for the 2020 Tokyo Olympics.

The two sides will battle it out in the third/fourth place play-off match of the U23 Africa Cup of Nations, with winning side from the tie set to qualify for next year’s Olympics.  

SA U23 coach has lined up his side:

Mondli Mpoto, Keagan Johannes, Katlego Mohamme, Thendo Mukumela, Tercious Malepe, Athenkosi Dlala, Teboho Mokoena, Grant Margeman, Luther Singh, Keletso Makgalwa, Lyle Foster.
