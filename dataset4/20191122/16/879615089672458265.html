Cara Delevingne and Olivier Rousteing Fête Their Puma x Balmain Collab In L.A.

22 Nov 2019, 16:36 by The Daily Front Row

Thursday, November 21

Puma and Balmain Throw a Star-Studded Launch Party
Last night, Cara Delevingne and Olivier Rousteing celebrated their Puma x Balmain collection in L.A. Guests like Alessandra Ambrosio, Eva Longoria, Amber Heard, Bella Thorne, Christian Combs, Devon Windsor, and Slick Woods got to enjoy a boxing-themed party that drew from the collab. The evening ended with a live performance featuring the clothes.

Sony Pictures Classics and The Cinema Society Screens The Song of Names
Clive Owen and director François Girard premiered their new film, The Song of Names, in New York last night. After the screening guests — including Tory Burch and Gina Gershon — headed to Omar’s La Boite and sipped Qui tequila cocktails based on the movie.

MATCHESFASHION Throws a Garden Party for Emilia Wickstead
After celebrating Emilia Wickstead’s nightwear collection earlier this week, MATCHESFASHION threw a second party for the designer. Yesterday’s soiree at the Robinson Gardens in Beverly Hills celebrated Wickstead’s Safari collection and Zoë de Givenchy’s tabletop capsule.
