Shreya Ghoshal will perform at Arena Birmingham - here’s how to get tickets

The world renowned vocalist will come to Birmingham Sunday 22 March 2020

22 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Scott Dickinson, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/scott-dickinson/

Shreya Ghoshal will treat us to some Bollywood belters next year as she takes to Arena Birmingham for a highly anticipated gig.

The singer is one of the most respected playback artists in the world, having received countless awards including four National Film Awards and singing for masses of A-list stars on screen.

You can catch Shreya Goshal at Arena Birmingham on Sunday 22 March 2020. Tickets will go on sale at 10am on Friday 29th November 2019 and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

Having started performing when she was only four years old, she eventually buckled up the courage to enter and win Sa Re Ga Ma, an Indian reality TV show.

Shreya’s career consequently took off and she has now featured in some of the most iconic Bollywood films of all time.

Yet her achievements are far from exclusively filmic as her performance of the song “Jaadu Hai Nasha Hai” shot up the charts and into the mainstream music scene.

She has also become notorious for her incredible live shows, having performed in America, Canada, Australia, New Zealand and the UK which she visited in 2013 to celebrate Bollywood's 100th birthday.

Now she will return to the UK for only two dates for an exclusive set of shows for the ages.

Shreya Goshal will come to Arena Birmingham on Sunday 22 March 2020.

Tickets will go on sale for the show at 10am on Friday 29 November 2019 and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.
