Stourbridge man killed in fatal smash in Tenbury Wells is named

Paul Springer, from Stourbridge, died after the smash on the A456

22 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Jordan Coussins, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/

A man who was killed after a horrific two-car crash near to Tenbury Wells has been named by investigating officers.

Paul Springer, from Stourbridge, died after the smash on the Tenbury Wells (A456) as he was travelling towards Worcester on Wednesday, November 20.

West Mercia Police says the 88-year-old retired chartered engineer suffered a medical episode which led to the fatal smash.

He was described as a private, family man and was the father of two daughters and six grandchildren.

Emergency services were called at around 3.10pm and closed the road for a number of hours.

Anyone who witnessed the incident or has any information in relation to it should contact West Mercia Police on 101 quoting reference 434s of November 20, 2019.
