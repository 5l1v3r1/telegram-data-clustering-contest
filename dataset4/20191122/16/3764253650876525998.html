Buhari, Osinbajo, governors, party leaders attend APC's NEC meeting

22 Nov 2019, 16:15

President Muhammadu Buhari and his vice, Yemi Osinbajo, on Friday, November 22, attended the National Executive Committee (NEC) of the All Progressives Congress (APC) at the party’s national secretariat in Abuja.

The APC's NEC meeting was also attended by state governors, members of the National Assembly delegates of the party’s executive wing.
