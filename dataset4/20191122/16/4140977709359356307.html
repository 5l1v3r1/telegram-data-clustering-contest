LET Vs MRC Dream11 Team, Probable Playing 11 And Match Report: Two struggling sides strive to rejuvenate their inclement campaigns when they go at loggerheads come today.

As La Liga returns to the footballing fraternity with the international set of fixtures behind us, the predicament for Mallorca returns as well. With a mere 4 wins in 13 matches, the side find themselves struggling and languishing in 16th place in the league standings, a position which perfectly alludes to their bewilderment this campaign.

However, what will buoy the side ahead of today’s gameweek 14 opener in the league is not only the identity of their opponents but the win the side managed to garner up in their foregone contest as well. It was only their second defeat in five matches but a pivotal one as they thumped Villarreal 3-1, a result they’ll be wishing serves as a revival to their remorse filled season when they face off against Levante today.

And Levante who are only the three points above Mallorca as things stand will know that they can’t afford to undermine Mallorca for all their malicious showings. The 2-1 defeat the side suffered at the hands of Athletic Bilbao was a damaging one not only owing to the two match winning spree it curtailed for the side but also with it being their sixth defeat in 13 matches to see the side left yearning for consistency.
