Toke Makinwa Sends Message To BBNaija Fans

22 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Media mogul, Toke Makinwa has admonished the fans of BBNaija reality TV show to stop putting pressures on their favourite housemates.

This follows series of social media fights and how fans continue to create enmity between the top housemates.

According to Makinwa, it’s been a long time she expressed her views on such issues, but BBNaija fans need to focus more on better things.

She tweeted “Been so long since I aired my thoughts on social issues cos I’m busy building my empire but you BBrothernaija fans really need to stop tripping and putting so much pressure on your favourite housemates…”
