Photos! Anushka Sharma pays a visit to Manish Malhotra

22 Nov 2019, 16:26 by Analita Seth

Manish Malhotra is going through a rough patch right now after his father’s demise last weekend. The eminent designer is well-liked in the film community and has a host of friends in B-Town. We saw many celebrities visiting Manish to offer condolences after the loss of his father. From close pals Karan Johar, Gauri Khan to Priyanka Chopra, everyone came to offer their support in this difficult time.

Anushka Sharma was snapped outside Manish’s residence today. She paid a visit to the designer to offer condolences for his loss. Following are the pictures of the actress heading out after her visit.
