Watch: Woman Sarpanch from Rajasthan climbs over JCB machine to stop encroachment

The land was allegedly encroached by a man named Vagha Ram who also started construction there to which Rekha Devi opposed.

22 Nov 2019, 16:43 by ANI

Jalore: In a viral video, a woman was seen attempting to climb over a JCB machine through its front loader end.

The woman identified as Rekha Devi, Sarpanch of Mandawala village was trying to stop an alleged encroachment.
