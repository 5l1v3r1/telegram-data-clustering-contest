Pink ball Test: What we saw at Eden Gardens huge step forward for Test cricket, says Sourav Ganguly

India vs Bangladesh: Legendary Indian cricketers and sporting heroes, including PV Sindhu and Mary Kom, were felicitated in a star-studded ceremony after the end of the 1st day's play at Eden Gardens on Friday.

22 Nov 2019, 16:16

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Sourav Ganguly led the felicitation ceremony at Eden Gardens on Friday evening
  • Bangladesh PM Sheik Hasina headlined the ceremony after Day 1
  • Tendulkar, Dravid, Sindhu, Mary among sporting heroes who were felicitated

BCCI president Sourav Ganguly thanked Bangladesh Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina, West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee and other dignitaries who joined India's Pink ball carnival on Day 1 of the ongoing 2nd Test against Bangladesh on Friday at Eden Gardens, Kolkata.

More than 50,000 fans were at Eden Gardens on Friday as the stands at the iconic venue were packed to the rafters.

Bangladesh PM Sheikh Hasina, West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee, legendary India cricketers Sachin Tendulkar, VVS Laxman, Rahul Dravid, Anil Kumble, Harbhajan Singh were part of the star-studded guest list on Day 1 of India's 1st ever Pink ball Test.

Apart from the cricketing legends, India's sporting heroes PV Sindhu, Mary Kom, Sania Mirza, Mithali Raj, Jhulan Goswami among others were also felicitated after the end of the 1st day's play in Kolkata.
