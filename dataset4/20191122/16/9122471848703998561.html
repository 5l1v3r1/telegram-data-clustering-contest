Georgia vs. Texas A&M: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

22 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Tzvi Machlin

A pair of SEC powerhouses square off this weekend as the No. 4 Georgia Bulldogs take on the Texas A&M Aggies at Sanford Stadium.

The Bulldogs secured their spot in the SEC Championship Game last week, but still need to win out in order to get into the College Football Playoff. Outside of one stumble against South Carolina, they’ve largely dominated teams thanks to a No. 2 ranked defense that allows less than 11 points per game.

The Aggies, on the other hand, are 0-3 against ranked opponents this year, losing to Clemson, Alabama and Auburn. But they’ve beaten all of their other opponents – including South Carolina – thanks to an offense scoring 34 points per game.

The difference in defenses between the two is going to be the real indicator in this game. Bettors have Georgia favored by double-digits – almost exactly the difference between the number of points per game the two teams have allowed.
