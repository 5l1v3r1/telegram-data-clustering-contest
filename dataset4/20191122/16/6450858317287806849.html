Does Frozen 2 have a post-credits scene?

Frozen 2 is out in cinemas now - find out everything you need to know before you go, including whether or not there's a post-credits scene

22 Nov 2019, 16:54 by Hannah Cowton

After the incredible popularity of the first Frozen movie, the sequel is finally here. With the return of Anna, Elsa, Olaf and Kristoff, Frozen 2 was anticipated to be among the biggest movies of 2019. 

If you've not yet grabbed your tickets to see it, you can get them from Cineworld if you're in the UK, and Fandango if you're in the US.

Does Frozen 2 have a post-credits scene?

Make sure you stick around through the credits, because Frozen 2 DOES have a bonus scene that may be of interest! If you'd like to know what it is, take a look below.

Spoilers ahead - you have been warned!

Olaf recaps the events of the movie (this recalls a scene earlier in the film where he recaps the first movie). He says that his theory that “water has memory” was correct. The camera then cuts to his audience which includes the snow monster from the first Frozen film and hundreds of baby snowmen.
