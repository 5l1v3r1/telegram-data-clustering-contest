Today in 2015 – Abena Korkor named big men forcing to sleep with her

22 Nov 2019, 05:45 by MyNewsGH

The Teaching Assistant at the University of Cape Coast (UCC) Abena Korkor in 2015 today exposed big men here in Ghana trying to have their way with her.

According to Nana Abena Korkor Addo. some prominent people either seek to have an affair with her or still sleeping with her. She even further narrated on her facebook page that even though the Central regional minister, Mr. Aquinas Tawiah Quansah (MP) was fond of sleeping with younger girls, he was better of than Mr. Stephen Asamoah-Boateng of the (Asa B) who she was busily sharing beds with.

She went on to further caution people like Kwame Buafo Akuffo, Alfred Mahama and Mr. Derrick Asare Lokko, of her impending leak. She lamented on how Kwame Buafo Akuffo kept pursuing her for sex.

Mr. Derrick Asare Lokko, a husband of Thelma Jane Lokko, sought to silence the young lady through many messages but on all occasions, Nana Abena objected.
