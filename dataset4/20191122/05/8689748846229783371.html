Yvonne Nelson Drops Freestyle Rap Skills On Live TV (Video)

22 Nov 2019, 05:14 by BB Jones

Actress and producer Yvonne Nelson showed her other talent as a rapper when she dropped some punchlines on live TV in the studios of Kwese TV.

The mother of one was a guest on the TV network TV to promote her upcoming movie ‘Fix Us’ premiering on December 6 at Accra Mall.

In order to convince movie fanatics why they should be at the cinema to watch the movie, Yvonne dropped some lines in raps to propagate her message.

Yvonne was joined by Efia Odo as they rapped and made gestures associated with rappers.

Yvonne rapped telling viewers of the show to purchase a ticket and join her at the premiere of the much anticipated movie.

It didn’t take long for her to prove she can really rap and the studio guests loved every bit of her mini concert.
