Ghana bride arrives at her wedding in a coffin

22 Nov 2019, 05:11

A Ghana bride has turned up to her wedding in a coffin.

The unnamed woman decided to take an unconventional approach when it came to arriving for her big day as she made her entrance in a large white casket.

In a video uploaded online, guests can be heard cheering as friends open up the coffin, with the bride smiling and posing for photos.
