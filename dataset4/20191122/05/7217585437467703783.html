29 times Twitter users started an argument with just four words

22 Nov 2019, 05:30 by Andy Walker

Have an opinion on Twitter — be it right, wrong, morally-challenged or just plain daft — and someone will leap at the chance to argue with you.

Their challenge may not be fair, but it is what it is, and it seems that Twitter users are really, really pushing the limits of this notion.

On Thursday, a dangerous hashtag appeared and trended worldwide for a few hours. #StartAnArgumentIn4Words is exactly what it says on the tin, and we bet that among these 29 arguments, you’ll find at least one you’ll want to argue against.

Oh, we found plenty. (Don’t even get us started on the correct orientation of toilet paper.)
