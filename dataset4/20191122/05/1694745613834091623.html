The 9 Best Chocolate-Covered Foods You Should Get at Trader Joe's

22 Nov 2019, 05:35 by Erin Cullum

The seemingly endless wall of chocolate-covered foods at Trader Joe's is one of the most magical places on earth. If you're like me, you've spent an embarrassing amount of time making your selection, because the number of choices is almost too high. If you need inspiration for your next chocolate purchase, look no further than these editor-approved favorites. Keep reading to find out which chocolate-covered Trader Joe's products POPSUGAR employees love and why.

Prices and products may vary by Trader Joe's location.

1 Trader Joe's Milk Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups ($4)

So creamy and delicious. Hands down, 1,000 times better than Reese's Peanut Butter Cups.

— Kelly Schwarze

2 Trader Joe's Sea Salt and Turbinado Sugar Dark Chocolate Almonds ($4)

These are so different from any other chocolate-covered almond I've had because of the sprinkle of coarse sea salt and turbinado sugar. Biting into the thick chocolate coating before crunching into the roasted almonds is so satisfying, and these are rich enough that the container lasts me a while.

— Erin Cullum

3 Trader Joe's Milk Chocolate Smashing S'mores ($4)

This treat from TJ's will make you wonder why you ever went through the trouble of campfire s'mores. Well, that may be stretching things, but it certainly is worth snacking on. Don't forget to microwave the treat for a few seconds to make the mallow center ooey-gooey.

— Anna Monette Roberts

4 Trader Joe's Gone Bananas ($2)

TJ's makes these awesome individual sliced bananas covered in chocolate. They're amazing and are great for when you're trying to eat healthier but have an ice cream flavor.

— Sarah Siegel

5 Trader Joe's Dark-Chocolate-Covered Pomegranate Seeds ($4)

I love Trader Joe's dark-chocolate-covered pomegranate seeds. They're tart and sweet, which is much more exciting than munching on plain chocolate chips. I like to throw them in oatmeal instead of sweetening it with syrup or sugar. The hot cereal makes the chocolate get a little melty — and who doesn't wanted melted chocolate for breakfast?!

— Rebecca Firkser

6 Trader Joe's Dark-Chocolate-Covered Blueberries ($4)

The choco-covered blueberries! They're the perfect combo of two of my favorite things — it's hard to stop at just a few.

— Lisette Mejia

7 Trader Joe's Dark-Chocolate-Covered Pretzel Slims ($3)

These are salty, sweet, chocolatey, and perfect for anyone with a sweet tooth. I have made myself stop buying them, because I eat them too fast.

— Aemilia Madden

8 Trader Joe's Dark-Chocolate-Covered Powerberries ($4)

You know Brookside chocolate-covered acai berries? These are just like those but even better, and you get the added bonuses of all the fruits (acai, pomegranate, cranberry, and blueberry) in one bag.

— EC

9 Trader Joe's Mini Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups ($3)

Everything is better as a mini, right? The mini chocolate peanut butter cups are just as good as the regular size, but their bite-size form makes them even more appealing.

— EC
