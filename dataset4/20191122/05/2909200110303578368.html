My baby’s mama tormented my life – Akwaboah on break up

22 Nov 2019, 05:58 by MyNewsGH

Singer, Akwaboah says he broke up with his baby’s mama because the latter tormented his life and never gave peace of mind.

“We were in the relationship for 7 months and I didn’t have peace so I have to let it go though we have a beautiful baby together.” He revealed

He noted that although he and other musicians sing about forever love, their relationships are not perfect.

“Most artistes are not as perfect as what they sing about. We might be talking about a forever love affair in our songs but in real life, we are suffering in our relationships. Because of this perception, people presume we are the best to be with. But when they come into our lives and realize we are different from our songs or not the perfect guy in the music they end up breaking up.”
