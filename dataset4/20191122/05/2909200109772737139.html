Being ditched by two MEN is my biggest life CHALLENGE – Juliet Ibrahim

22 Nov 2019, 05:12 by MyNewsGH

Actress, Juliet Ibrahim says her biggest challenge she has been faced with in her life was dealing with being ditched by two men.

The actress says she’s a private person so after her divorce with Kwadwo Safo Kantanka Junior  and subsequent failed relationship with Iceberg Slim failed, it had a great toll on her life.

The actress said ” For me, it is a tie between a huge, public divorce that I went through with my ex husband and my breakup with another African celebrity that had too much press behind it.

I am a private person when it comes to my personal affairs so those two moments were a bit uncomfortable for me.

Those experiences I also spoke about in my book “A Toast to Life” because I needed to let people know more about who I am as a woman outside of the paparazzi headlines and how I struggled and survived it all”.
