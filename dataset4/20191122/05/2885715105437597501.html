BELTRON DEO admit cards 2019 released, check steps to download

BELTRON DEO admit cards 2019: The selection of the candidates will be done on the basis of their computer skills and typing test. The admit card for the online exam has been released.

22 Nov 2019, 05:56 by Careers Desk

BELTRON DEO admit cards 2019: Bihar State Electronics Development Corporation Ltd. has released the hall tickets for the recruitment exam to be conducted for the post of data entry operator. Candidates who will appear for the exam can download their admit cards or hall ticket from the official website-http://www.bsedc.bihar.gov.in.

Candidates will require their registration number and date of birth to download the admit cards. The selection of the candidates will be done on the basis of their computer skills and typing. The typing test will be conducted in two languages –  Hindi and English.

BELTRON DEO admit cards 2019: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website-http://www.bsedc.bihar.gov.in

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘Downloading link for admit cards for Data Entry Operator’

Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page.

Step 4: On the page that opens, enter the details asked like registration number and date of birth and click on submit.

Step 5: The admit cards will be displayed on the screen.

Step 6: Check the details on the admit cards and download for future reference.

The online applications were accepted from March 15, 2019 and the last date to apply online was April 26, 2019. The selected candidates will enter in an agreement with the agency, to offer services at mutually agreed terms.
