China’s Pres. Xi: Long-term improving trend for china's economy will not change

22 Nov 2019, 05:17 by Dhwani Mehta

Xinhua reports the latest comments from China’s President Xi Jinping is on the wires now, via Reuters, with the key headlines found below.

The long-term improving trend for china's economy will not change.

China's economy has great resilience, potential and room for manoeuvre.

Have full confidence in china's development.
