5 Players To Boost Pirates!

22 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Soccer Laduma

Orlando Pirates have been boosted with return of at least five players in their preparation for next Tuesday’s match against Polokwane City.

During the FIFA break, the Buccaneers had a number of stars on national duty – the likes of Thembinkosi Lorch and Innocent Maela (Bafana Bafana), Justin Shonga and Augustine Mulenga (Zambia), as well as Gabadinho Mhango (Malawi) in the Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers.

All five players are expected to play a role in helping Rulani Mokwena and the team in pursuit of enough points as the team will be aiming for improvement on the log in the last three matches of 2019. 

With South African U23 team still in pursuit of qualifying for the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, Thabiso Monyane is unlikely to be available for Bucs’ next match.
