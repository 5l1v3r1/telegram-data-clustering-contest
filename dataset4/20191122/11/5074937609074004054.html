Registration opens for StarTimes' Bundesliga Football School

22 Nov 2019, 11:09 by Simon Asare, GNA

Accra, Nov. 21, GNA – StarTimes in collaboration with the German Bundesliga, have opened registration for its new football talents development programme dubbed the Bundesliga Football School (BLFS).

Young Boys and Girls between the ages of 13 and 17 can register by downloading the StarTimes On App from the Google Play Store and follow the necessary steps with deadline for registration set for December 1, 2019.

The BLFS slated for December 14 – 18, 2019 would see 100 selected young footballers receive assistance from top World Stars, in a series of compact training sessions and workshops, enabling them to showcase their talent.

Former German international Renate Lingor, two time World Cup winner, two time European Champion and three time Bronze Medal Olympic Games winner together with other UEFA license A coaches such as Tobias Süveges of SV Werder Bremen, Arne Stratmann of TSG 1899 Hoffenheim, Bjoern Nass of Bayer 04 Leverkusen and head coach of the Bundesliga Football school, Birger J. Nass would lead the one-week course.

It would be the second time the BLFS would be hosted in Africa with the maiden edition held in South Africa.
