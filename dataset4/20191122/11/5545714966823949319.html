Uncharted's Amy Hennig partnering with Skydance Media to create new studio

'Story-focused experiences' in the works

22 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Chris Moyse

Uncharted writer and co-creator Amy Hennig is collaborating with Skydance Media (the production team behind movies such as Annihilation, as well as the Mission: Impossible and Terminator franchises) to found a new video game development studio.

Hennig will work alongside her former Electronic Arts comrade-in-arms Julian Beak, with the as-yet-unnamed studio developing "story-focused experiences... designed to reach gamers and non-games alike." Skydance notes that the team's upcoming projects will be cultivated for "emerging streaming platforms."

"The interactive media landscape is continually changing and Amy and Julian are creative and visionary leaders of this evolution," said Skydance Media CEO David Ellison in an official statement. "Together, we will create within this new sphere the same type of event-level entertainment experiences that Skydance is known for in features and television."

Aside from being a key figure in the Uncharted series, Hennig has previously been involved with the Jak & Daxter and Soul Reaver series. Beak is known for his work on Battlefield: Hardline, Army of Two: The Devil's Cartel, and the 2015 Need for Speed reboot.

"Julian and I are thrilled to be part of the Skydance creative team, and excited to partner with Skydance to explore this new frontier in entertainment while pioneering new ways to tell immersive stories through technology," said Hennig. "Our goal is to create inviting and innovative experiences with the high production values and visual fidelity that will set the standard in this new media landscape."
