STUNNER OR BUMMER: Nora Fatehi, Janhvi Kapoor, Malaika Arora, Nushrat Bharucha Or Genelia Dsouza?

22 Nov 2019, 11:34 by Prajakta Ajgaonkar

Here are the ladies who kept the fashion meter high in the city and those who turned out to be not so stylish

Nushrat Bharucha, Nora Fatehi and Genelia Dsouza were snapped at the success bash of Marjaavaan at a popular hotspot in Mumbai last night. Malaika Arora was spotted post her workout session this afternoon. Janhvi Kapoor was also papped post her pilates class today. Now, let’s have a look at the stunners and bummers for the day:

NORA FATEHI-BUMMER- Nora fails to impress us with her satin blue wrap-dress. The long loose sleeves are a turn off.

JANHVI KAPOOR-STUNNER- Janhvi Kapoor is a total stunner in an aqua blue bralet and tight black pants.

MALAIKA ARORA-STUNNER- Malaika is a babe in a grey sports bra and hot pants.

NUSHRAT BHARUCHA- STUNNER- Nushrat looks smoking hot in a black one-shoulder dress which has a thigh-high slit.

GENELIA DSOUZA-BUMMER- Genelia’s draped dress is too confusing to say the least!
