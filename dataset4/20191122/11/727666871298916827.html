Billie Eilish, Trippie Redd & More Appear In The Trailer For XXXTentacion's 'Bad Vibes Forever' Album

22 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Watch music artists reflect on the 2018 BET Hip-Hop Awards Best New Artist winner.

(AllHipHop News) Jahseh "XXXTentacion" Onfroy's final full-length album, Bad Vibes Forever, is scheduled for release on December 6. A trailer for the late musician's forthcoming project was published on YouTube.

In the video, Billie Eilish, Trippie Redd, PnB Rock, and more reflect on XXX’s impact. The Grammy-nominated Eilish called Onfroy "a beam of light" and "the most selfless kid" she ever met.

Bad Vibes Forever will also include the previously released singles "Hearteater" and “Royalty” featuring Ky-Mani Marley, Stefflon Don, and Vybz Kartel. The project follows 2018's chart-topping ? and Skins. His debut studio album, 17, came out in 2017.

"Music saved me as an individual, because I was literally haunted by myself,” XXXTentacion was quoted saying before he was murdered on June 18, 2018. “As a person, I was lost. It gave me a purpose. Within that purpose, I became able to display my pain for others. Initially, it just felt good. After a while, I wanted to be like a therapist for these kids."

He continued, "Anyone that was even slightly depressed could feel it. I was doing this for them as much as I was for myself. I gave them something to feed on. I gained attention from networking, marketing myself, and being an idiot on the internet, but the cult following is purely because of the songs.”
