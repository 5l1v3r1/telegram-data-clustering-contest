Rosanna Davison shares sweet snap of baby girl Sophia Rose

22 Nov 2019, 11:46 by Ciara Walsh

The former Miss World welcomed her first child with husband Wes Quirke on Thursday, November 21. 

Rosanna Davison has shared an adorable snap of her new bundle of joy, baby Sophia Rose.

The former Miss World and her husband Wes Quirke became parents yesterday when their surrogate gave birth in the US.

The new mum said someday her little girl will "know just how loved and longed for she is" after the family were flooded with congratulations at their news yesterday.

Sharing a sweet snap of husband Wes holding the tot's hand, Rosanna said: "Pure, true love I've been Sophia's mum for almost 24 hours and while she's spent the majority of it asleep, I've mostly stayed up staring at every inch of her!

"Thank you all so much for the gorgeous messages to welcome her into the world - someday she'll know just how loved and longed for she is."

The 35-year-old said the pair were "completely besotted" with their little girl as they thanked fans and pals for their kind words.

Announcing Sophia's birth yesterday: "And the dreams that you dare to dream really do come true.

"Today, November 21 2019 at 11.57am, our beautiful and healthy daughter Sophia Rose Quirke arrived safely into the world weighing six pounds eight ounces, she's absolutely perfect.

"We're completely overwhelmed with love and gratitude for our incredible gestational surrogate, who has given us the greatest gift of all and made our family dream a reality.

Her husband also shared an adorable picture on his Instagram with the same message.

The pair have been sharing the surrogacy journey from the beginning when they announced that they were expecting earlier this year.

Rosanna and Wes tied the knot in 2014, celebrating their nuptials with three wedding parties in Ibiza, the Merrion Hotel in Dublin and Powerscourt in Enniskerry, Co Wicklow.

The power couple - who dated for eight years before they married - originally met on the social networking site Bebo.

Last July, Rosanna confirmed that she and Wes would welcome their first child by gestational surrogate after suffering multiple miscarriages.

She said how it felt like "the longest week" after heading to the States to await the birth of their little girl.

The nutritionist vowed not to post snaps of her new baby girl online until she's old enough to choose for herself, so far only sharing snaps of her little hands and feet.
