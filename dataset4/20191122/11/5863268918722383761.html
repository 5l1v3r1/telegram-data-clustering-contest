Karachi: Police open fire on car, kill one person

22 Nov 2019, 11:12 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

KARACHI (Dunya News) – Karachi police opened fire on a car near the Cantonment Railway Station killing one person and injuring another, Dunya News reported on Friday.

District South SSP Sheraz Nazir told that all three officials involved in the incident are arrested and initial statement of the wounded man, Raza Imam, has been recorded. The deceased has been identified as 40-year-old Nabeel.

Sheraz Nazir maintained that the policemen were chasing the car and opened fire on the vehicle after stopping it near the Frere Hall. The investigation officers have told that 9mm pistol was used in firing.

Raza Imam said in his statement that he and his friend Nabeel used to do import-export business. We were coming from Defence area at night and did not realize when the police started chasing us, he added.
