Bigg Boss 13: After Sidharth Shukla, Gauahar Khan Lashes Out At Asim Riaz; Says ‘He Is No Saint’

22 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Divya Ramnani

Soon after slamming Sidharth Shukla, Gauahar Khan has now taken to Twitter to lash out at Asim Riaz and take his class and wrote that he is no saint in the Bigg Boss 13 house. Looks like even the previous contestants cannot decide whom to support

Gauahar Khan, who happens to be the winner of Bigg Boss 7, is one avid Bigg Boss 13 watcher and her live tweets on the same are proof. Earlier in the day, the actress had slammed Sidharth Shukla for being over aggressive towards Asim Riaz and pushing him so bad. Well, Khan has now moved onto Asim Riaz and Paras Chhabra and is taking their class through her tweets. As per her, if Sidharth Shukla was wrong, Asim, too, is at fault.

In her tweet, Gauahar Khan wrote how Asim is no saint and that his busy playing his own game. According to Gauahar, Asim is contradicting his self as just like Sid, he too has spoken ill with girls in the past. “Asim is no saint ! He has played his game too ! He knows the nerves to pinch ! I remember when he was getting aggressive n smashin the mud balls on the ladder he said to the girls toh phir hatt jao,! He has spoken badly to the girls too , n now he was preaching to Siddharth,” wrote Gauahar Khan.
