Palace Ultimo 2019 Lookbook

Featuring winter-ready parkas and a range of camouflage pieces.

22 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Jack Stanley

Following on from the final drop of its Winter 2019 collection, London’s Palace has returned with the lookbook for its Ultimo 2019 collection. The capsule features a number of classic Palace touches, including eye-catching outerwear and referential graphics.

Stand-out themes from the Ultimo 2019 collection include camouflage, which features in a number of different formats, as well as cold weather-ready pieces such as fleeces, tonal scarves and lightweight puffa jackets. Elsewhere, key items include denim with red stitch detailing, repeated “P” logo hoodies and camo parkas.

Take a first look at the collection in the Letty Schmiterlow-shot lookbook — featuring Palace skaters Lucien Clarke and Dino Da Silva — in the gallery above. The first pieces from the collection will release online and in-store at 11AM on November 29, before releasing in-store and online in Japan a day later.
