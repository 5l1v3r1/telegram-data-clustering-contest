Sarileru Neekevvaru Teaser: Mahesh Babu packs a punch as an army commando in this pakka action entertainer

22 Nov 2019, 11:45 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After much anticipation and hype from the past few days, the teaser of Mahesh Babu’s upcoming actioner Sarileru Neekevvaru has been finally unveiled on social media a few moments. Directed by successful director Anil Ravipudi, the action entertainer is loaded with hero-elevating sequences, thumping BGM, striking dialogues, high-octane action and the rocking screen-presence of Mahesh Babu who literally steals the show. Along with the Superstar, seasoned actor Prakash Raj and veteran actress Vijayashanthi make an impact.
