Canary Wharf and Canada Water evacuated after 'dust storm'

22 Nov 2019, 11:45

Passengers were evacuated from two London Underground stations after a ‘dust storm’ in rush hour this morning.

Canary Wharf and Canada Water were both shut after the air was filled with dust from a tunnel fan.

Footage shows passengers covering their mouths as they left the stations in the haze.

The two stations were closed for about 10 minutes shortly after 9.15am.

Owen Smith, who was at Canada Water during the evacuation, said he thought there was ‘some sort of gas or smoke’.
