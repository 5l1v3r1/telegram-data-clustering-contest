Scotland’s Euro 2020 play-off fixtures and schedule revealed

Scotland’s Euro 2020 play-off fixture dates have been revealed.

22 Nov 2019, 11:26 by Stephen Eighteen

Steve Clarke’s men will play Israel in a one-legged semi-final at Hampden on March 26, before a final away game against Norway or Serbia on March 31.

Scotland – who failed to gain automatic qualification from Group I, ending their campaign against Kazakhstan on Tuesday – were in the same group as Israel in the UEFA Nations League, losing 2-1 away from home before winning 3-2 in Glasgow and topping the pool.

Should Scotland win both play-off games they will qualify for a first major tournament since the 1998 World Cup in France.

The draw was made at UEFA headquarters in Nyon.
