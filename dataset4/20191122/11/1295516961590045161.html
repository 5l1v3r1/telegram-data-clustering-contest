Marvel Resurrects Force Works for Iron Man 2020 Tie-In

22 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Jon Arvedon

Force Works is returning in February 2020, courtesy of Matthew Rosenberg and Juanan Ramirez.

In Marvel's solicit information for product shipping February 2020, the publisher revealed Rosenberg and Ramirez will helm Force Works 2020, a three-issue miniseries that ties into the upcoming Iron Man 2020 event.

The original Force Works was created by Dan Abnett, Andy Lanning and Tom Tenney and debuted in 1994's Force Works #1. The team was formed by Iron Man as a replacement of sorts to the West Coast Avengers and the original lineup consisted of Scarlet Witch, Iron Man, Wonder Man, Spider-Woman, and U.S. Agent.

The cover art and solicitation text for Force Works 2020 is below:

Force Works 2020 #1, by Matthew Rosenberg and Juanan Ramirez, goes on sale in February 2020 from Marvel Comics.
