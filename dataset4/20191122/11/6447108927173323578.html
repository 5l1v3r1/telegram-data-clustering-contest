The EU PlayStation Store Black Friday sale is now live, includes 25% off PlayStation Plus

22 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Tuffcub

Black Friday continues to try and be a thing over this side of the pond even though we don’t have Thanksgiving, but if that means cheap games then you can’t really complain.

Following the sale on the US Store and the Xbox One Black Friday sale, the EU PlayStation Store have got their sale up and it includes 25% off PlayStation Plus which takes the price down to £37.49. Remember, you can stack up your subscriptions so even if you PS+ is not due you can buy this deal and it will be added on to the end of your current subscription.

According to the PS Store, “PlayStation®Plus is home to the world’s largest community of new-gen players.” – New gen? Really?

As usual there are load of games on sale with over 60% wiped off the price of some of them, and some movies on the PlayStation Store are also discounted. We are also expecting hardware discounts to be announced for consoles and headsets very shortly.
