PartyNextDoor Returns With "The News" & "Loyal" Featuring Drake

22 Nov 2019, 11:22 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Listen to PND's new two-pack of songs.

(AllHipHop News) The elusive PartyNextDoor dropped off two new tunes overnight. One song is titled "The News." The second record is called "Loyal" and it features Drake.

PND tapped into his Jamaican roots for a dancehall rhythm on "Loyal." "The News" is more of an atmospheric slow jam. Thanks to his return to releasing music, PartyNextDoor's name became a Twitter trending topic on Thursday night/Friday morning.

This is the OVO Sound recording artist's first solo music since his 2017 Seven Days EP. PartyNextDoor's discography also includes his self-titled series. Plus, the Canadian singer-songwriter put out the Colours and Colours 2 EPs.
