Chocolate Orange Shreddies are back - just in time for Christmas

22 Nov 2019, 11:54

The festive season just got sweeter – thanks to the return of one particular cereal.

Nestle’s Coco Orange Shreddies are back on the shelves – for a limited time only.

They’re available to buy in Morrisons stores now and are priced at £2 (reduced from £2.75) for a 500g box.

There’s just one catch – the moreish cereal will only be available until 4 December.

So you have just under two weeks to grab yourself a box… or five.

Nestle announced the comeback on the official Shreddies UK Facebook page.

The post said: ‘You asked for it, so it’s here! LIMITED EDITION Coco Orange Shreddies is back! Get them in Morrisons stores while you can!’

Cereal lovers were quick to comment on the news of the relaunch.

One Facebook user commented: ‘What a time to be alive, they’re back’

Another cereal fan said: ‘Got my 15 boxes this morning. That’s me sorted. Early bird always catches the worm’

While others were keen to stress that the move should be long-lasting, posting: ‘YESSSSSS! Thank you, please keep it a permanent thing!’

But the confectionery brand has stressed that the Coco Orange Shreddies will not be heading to the shelves permanently – well, not yet anyway.

Nestle said: We have brought it back as Shreddies fans tell us that they love our flavour experimenting.

‘It is again available for a limited time only though, so that our flavour development team can continue to experiment and constantly bring new and exciting flavours to the breakfast tables of Shreddies fans.’

The festive flavour was last available back in 2012.

Let’s hope it won’t be another seven years until it comes around again.
