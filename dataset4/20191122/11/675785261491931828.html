Disneyland Paris breaks are currently going for £139pp with park tickets

Disneyland Paris breaks including your hotel, Eurostar and park tickets are currently on offer in Wowcher's early Black Friday deals

22 Nov 2019, 11:27 by Julie Delahaye, https://www.facebook.com/juliedjourno

Disneyland Paris may not have a reputation for being the cheapest of breaks, but Mickey Mouse's Parisian home is a bit more budget-friendly in a new offer!

That's because Wowcher has kicked off its Black Friday deals early and is offering up two-night breaks at the theme parks from £139pp with Travel Center.

That includes your hotel stay, a one-day two-park ticket as well as your return Eurostar.

You won't be staying at a Disney hotel, but you will be staying at the nearby Campanile Val de France - and your transfers from the hotel to the parks is included as part of the package.

You can book the Disneyland Paris deals here until Sunday 1st December with daily deals up for grabs.

Want to make a longer trip of it? You can also find three-night stays from £199pp and four-night stays from £249pp.

When we took a look, the £139pp packages were on offer for travel dates through February and March - which happens to coincide with Disneyland Paris' Frozen Celebration if you're a big fan of Elsa, Anna and Olaf.

We're talking a brand new float, the fun new attraction Frozen: A Musical Invitation and heaps of new foodie treats and merchandise.

There are other deals to be had in January with prices from £199pp, as well as packages from £239pp in April.
