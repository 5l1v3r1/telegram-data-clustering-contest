Resident Evil 3 remake due next year claim rumours - could it be next gen?

22 Nov 2019, 11:35

Given the huge success of Resident Evil 2 a Resident Evil 3 remake is inevitable but it may be out sooner than you expect.

Considering it was just named Ultimate Game of the Year by the Golden Joysticks and is one of the Game of the Year nominees for The Game Awards 2019, it seems fair to say that the Resident Evil 2 remake has been a success. Especially as its sales greatly exceeded Capcom’s expectations.

That puts a remake of Resident Evil 3: Nemesis on the same level as FIFA 21 in terms of dead certs, even though Capcom themselves has never confirmed they’re working on it (although they have hinted at it).

But according to YouTuber Spawn Wave the remake is now far enough along that it’s being prepped for a 2020 release.
