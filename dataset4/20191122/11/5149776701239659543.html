New gourmet burger joint Buns to open in Nottingham

There's even coloured buns to match the branding

22 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Lynette Pinchess

After months of teasing on Instagram, a mouth-watering gourmet burger joint will open its doors on Monday, November 25 promising to "blow away your taste buds."

Buns has had its 1,000+ followers salivating at the thought of juicy burgers since first announcing its arrival in August, saying "something tasty is coming to Nottingham."

Three months on and the independent business, in the heart of student land, will unleash its menu of tasty burgers, chicken wings and milkshakes on locals.

Founder Wasim Ali believes it will fulfill a gap in the market for a "proper" takeaway burger joint.

"We believe the Nottingham takeaway food scene was missing out on fresh handmade smashed beef burgers, proper buttermilk chicken wings/strips, loaded fries and milkshakes.

"We have recruited chefs from London to fulfil our dreams to create something amazing for the people of Nottingham.

"It's quality, fresh and the best cuts of meat. The decor is modern and vibrant."

The new Lenton business, with distinctive pink and black branding, graffiti art and hashtags galore (#bitemebaby #shakeyourbuns) has taken over a former chip shop, which has been empty for some time on the corner of Ilkeston Road and Derby Grove.

Burger lovers can choose from beef, chicken or veggie in brioche buns.

There's the classic beef with melted American cheese and "secret" sauce, a smoky variation with turkey rashers, a crispy one with onion rings, a hot spicy one and Philly Cheese Steak with strips of Angus steak, grilled onions, peppers, mushrooms and cheese.

And if you want your burger to match Buns' colour scheme, pink and black buns are available for an extra couple of quid.

Chicken burgers also come with smoky and spicy flavours, or teriyaki and peri peri. Veggie eaters can tuck into Beyond Meat patties, all plant-based.

Really hungry customers can go super-size with Yo! Big Beef - a whopping three patties made from Angus beef.

You can't have burgers without chips and Buns have them in abundance, from rustic and crinkle-cut to masala and sweet potato fries.

Chicken wings, tender strips and bites are on sale too with five sauces to spice them up or 'naked' Louisiana style.

To wash it all down there are milkshakes in vanilla, chocolate and strawberry, full-on chocoholic heaven with big name brands such as Oreo and Reese's Peanut Butter shakes or American Fanta in flavours including strawberry and grape.

With thousands of students living nearby, including those at Radford Mill directly opposite, there was no shortage of job applicants to fill the posts at the takeaway.

There's seating for eight inside and after Christmas home delivery will become available across Nottingham.

Buns will be open from 11am to midnight seven days a week.
