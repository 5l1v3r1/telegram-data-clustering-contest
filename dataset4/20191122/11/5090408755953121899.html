Billie Eilish asked kids where they think we go when we fall asleep, and their answers are the best thing you'll see today, Business Insider - Business Insider Singapore

22 Nov 2019, 11:46 by Alison Millington
  • Billie Eilish broke a Grammy record this week by becoming the youngest artist to be nominated in all of the show’s top categories.
  • Among her nominations were album of the year and best pop vocal album nods for her debut album “When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?”
  • Appearing on “Jimmy Kimmel Live” on Thursday, Eilish asked a group of kids where they think we go when we fall asleep.
  • Responses ranged from “the Mummy Times” to “kicking the butts” of zombies.
  • One boy said that when his parents go to sleep, they “just stay in their bed and take off their pyjama shirt.”
  • Another described a dream involving Pennywise the clown: “He’s sitting on your head and eating candy and he’s eating a cat and he’s eating a dog and a duck and a fox and a person. And he’s drinking a grandmother.”
