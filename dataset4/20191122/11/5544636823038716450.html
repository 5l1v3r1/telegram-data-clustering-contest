Stormzy Features Burna Boy, Ed- Sheeran In New Single (Audio)

22 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Oyindamola Olubajo

British rapper Stormzy has released a new single “Own It” featuring afro-fusion star Burna Boy and award-winning singer Ed Sheeran.

The collaborative track, was released in the early hours of Friday after it was earlier announced on Thursday.

The is a track off Stormzy’s forthcoming second studio album titled ‘Heavy is The Head’ which will be released on December 13.

The album is the U.K. rapper’s follow-up to his 2017 debut “Gang Signs & Prayer”.

Stormzy, whose real name is Michael Ebenazer Kwadjo Omari Owuo, came to the limelight through his ‘Wicked Skengman’ series of freestyles over classic grime beats.
