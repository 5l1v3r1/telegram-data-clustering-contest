Priyanka Chopra Visits Manish Malhotra To Offer Her Condolences

22 Nov 2019, 11:41 by Amruta Patel

Manish Malhotra lost his father Suraj Prakash Malhotra a few days ago and he found support in his industry colleagues, who stood by him in his weakest phase. After tons of Bollywood stars, global star Priyanka Chopra visited Manish Malhotra’s residence to offer her condolences to the designer late last night
