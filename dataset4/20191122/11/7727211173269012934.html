Chocolate lorry fire leads to hundreds of jokes and most of them are terrible

Once everyone knew there were no injuries, people couldn't help themselves

22 Nov 2019, 11:35 by Rebecca Koncienzcy

Last night a major road was closed when a lorry containing chocolate burst into flames.

Emergency services were called to the huge blaze on the A556, near the M56 towards the M6, at Knutsford to reports that an HGV was on fire.

On Twitter, North West Motorway Police tweeted to say that the road was blocked just before the motorway due to the chocolate laden lorry fire.
