London's Pokémon Center Will Return in 2020 (UPDATE)

Filled with exclusive merch.

22 Nov 2019, 11:43 by Mallory Chin

UPDATE (November 22, 2019): After a wildly successful run, which saw thousands of fans lining up for hours just to step inside, London‘s pop-up Pokémon center is set to return in 2020.

Although an exact date has yet to be shared, the pop-up will likely coincide with the 2020 Pokémon World Championships set to take place in the city next summer. And like all Pokémon center pop-ups, expect exclusive items to be up for grabs. Maybe even the coveted Bowler Hat Pikachu will make a return. Keep it locked here for more details when they surface.
