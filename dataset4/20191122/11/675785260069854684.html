Robbie Savage's Premier League predictions as Man Utd tipped for surprise result

Man Utd face Sheffield United, while Man City and Chelsea is the biggest game of the weekend. Liverpool travel to Crystal Palace as Jose Mourinho starts at Tottenham

22 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Alex Smith

Robbie Savage has predicted Manchester United to drop points to Sheffield United on Sunday.

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's side travel to Bramell Lane hoping to extend their winning streak to three matches, having beaten Partizan Belgrade and Brighton before the international break.

But Savage reckons that the Red Devils could struggle against the Blades - who are two places and a point ahead of them in the table after a stunning start to the season following their promotion from the Championship.

The former Birmingham midfielder has plucked for a score draw in the clash to leave United's hopes of qualifying for next season's Champions League in tatters.

The biggest game of the weekend comes at the Etihad Stadium, where Manchester City host Chelsea .

And the reigning champions are forecast to suffer a second defeat in a row and lose yet more ground on table toppers Liverpool .

City lost 3-1 to the Reds before the international break but haven't tasted defeat at home since April 2018.

Jose Mourinho makes his debut in the Tottenham dugout on Saturday lunchtime, having taken over from Mauricio Pochettino earlier this week - but West Ham are predicted to win the London derby and stun the Portuguese's big return.

Meanwhile, Arsenal and Leicester are both expected to win and keep up their battle for the top four, with Everton's resurgence continuing versus Norwich.

There will also be draws for Bournemouth and Wolves , Watford and Burnley , and Aston Villa and Newcastle .

Here's how Sav sees the weekend action panning out...
