22 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Luke Matthews, Amardeep Bassey

A couple who posted a creepy looking image of their baby on social media have been congratulated on their "bundle of demonic energy."

The baby monitor image shows the child lying in the cot, with dark shadows and the photographic flash in the infant's eyes making a scene scary enough to give people 'nightmares'.

Now the parents saying buying the monitor was a 'mistake' after the post attracted more than 400,000 likes and 3,000 comments, as people congratulated them on their 'bundle of demonic energy'.

The parent had captioned the picture: "We got a new video baby monitor and I think that was a mistake," reports the Mirror .

After one wrote: "Is your baby baptized? If so, might want to try it again. First time clearly didn't take," the parent then brilliantly replied: "We did but it turned the water to blood, the priest started climbing up the walls, it was a whole thing."

Another said: "Can you return it? The baby, I mean return the baby."

A third replied: "Throw the whole kid away."

In an attempt to calm everyone down, the parent then posted a follow-up photo to show their son is nothing to be scared of in daylight - and failed miserably.
