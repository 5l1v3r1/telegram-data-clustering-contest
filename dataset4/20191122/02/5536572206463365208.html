Map Shows Data On Which States Support Colin Kaepernick The Most (PIC)

22 Nov 2019, 02:23 by Darrelle Lincoln

Colin Kaepernick hasn’t played in the league since the end of the 2016 season, yet he remains one of the most talked about figures surrounding the sport.

Even more so now after his controversial workout in Atlanta over the weekend that had him suddenly changing the venue because of a waiver, and instead of having 25 NFL teams represented, it turned into just about seven or eight teams showing up.

Just a simple mention of his name would show the many people who like and dislike him, and they are loud on both sides.

Thanks to Betonline.ag, we now know which states support or do not support the former 49ers quarterback.

The below map was created by using geotagged Twitter data.

Illinois turned out to be the most supportive state, with Georgia and Orgeon following closely behind.

The former San Francisco 49ers quarterback was basically blackballed out of the league after he took a stance to kneel in protest of police brutality during the pregame national anthem in 2016.

The 32-year-old is still trying to get back into the league.
