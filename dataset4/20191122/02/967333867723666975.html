Porn Star Teanna Trump Claims Oklahoma Thunder Player Owes Her Money - Report

Teanna Trump’s new allegation became the talk of the NBA this week. Earlier in March, the porn star claimed that the person who “got [her] into porn” was an Indiana Pacers player.

22 Nov 2019, 02:29

In a Sunday tweet, porn star Teanna Trump (no relation to US President Donald Trump), alleged, tagging the official account of Oklahoma City Thunder, that one of the basketball team's players owes her, according a screenshot of a since-deleted tweet obtained by SportsGossip.com

“Your player needs to PAY ME what he owes me. @okcthunder", Trump wrote.
