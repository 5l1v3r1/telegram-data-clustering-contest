Seventeen receive first ever standing ovation in 'Blue Dragon Film Awards' history for their congratulatory performance

22 Nov 2019, 02:26 by jaehaedae_seojeo

On November 21, Seventeen heated up the festivities with an irreplaceable performance at 'The 40th Blue Dragon Film Awards', broadcasted live on SBS. 

Seventeen were recently named this year's K-Pop representatives for the annual 'Blue Dragon Film Awards', and on this day, the group delivered a memorable performance which dominated real-time searches on various portal sites immediately after the broadcast. The group received praise from media outlets such as, “There has been NO STAGE like this until now”, from the famous line of actor Ryu Seung Ryong in 'Extreme Job'. 

In fact, Seventeen became the first performing artist in the history of the 'Blue Dragon Film Awards' to earn a standing ovation from the audience members after their energetic stage, performing songs like "HIT" and "Very Nice". 

Seventeen's stellar stage presence managed to impress top actors in attendance at the ceremony including Jung Woo Sung, Jeon Do Yeon, Go Ah Sung, Ryu Seung Ryong, Honey Lee, Lee Kwang Soo, and more, and many actors were spotted applauding along merrily to the lively music. 

It has been approximately 10 years since a male idol group was invited to perform at the 'Blue Dragon Film Awards', the last time being 2PM in 2009. Check out the legendary stage for yourself above!
