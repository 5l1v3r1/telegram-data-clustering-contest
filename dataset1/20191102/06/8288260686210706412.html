'We need to be ready' - Liverpool fans on whether key man should be risked against Aston Villa

Aston Villa latest - Liverpool fans have been having their say on whether key midfielder Fabinho should start at Villa Park on Saturday.

02 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Jamie Kemble

Liverpool fans have been having their say on whether midfielder Fabinho should start against Aston Villa.

The Brazilian is just one booking away from a yellow card with a crucial top-of-the-table clash against Manchester City to come next week.

That means if Fabinho plays and is booked against Villa on Saturday afternoon, he will miss that all-important match-up at Anfield.

Stuck in a dilemma, Jurgen Klopp spoke about the situation in his pre-match press conference.

"I don't know yet," the Liverpool boss replied when asked if he will be risking Fabinho.
