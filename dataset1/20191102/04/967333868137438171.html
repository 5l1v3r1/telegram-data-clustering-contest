Tesla chief Elon Musk Says He is Quitting Twitter

02 Nov 2019, 04:08

Such a move is at odds with Musk's habit of using his Twitter account so often that it once provoked disapproval on the part of the US Securities and Exchange Commission, which blamed him for giving wrong information on the platform.

Tesla head Elon Musk said in a tweet on Friday that he is leaving Twitter, perhaps, for Reddit - an American social news aggregation, web content rating, and discussion website.
