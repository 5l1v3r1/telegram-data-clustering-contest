IPL: Persepolis and Sepahan both held [VIDEO]

02 Nov 2019, 12:39 by Mir Farhad Ali Khan

Tasnim – TEHRAN,  Persepolis and Sepahan football teams were held by their opponents in Matchday 9 of Iran Professional League (IPL) on Thursday.

Persepolis drew 0-0 with Machine Sazi in Tehran’s Azadi Stadium.

Persepolis forward Ali Alipour missed a penalty in 90th penalty.

In Abadan, Pars Jonoubi and Sepahan shared the spoils. Reza Esmaeili gave a lead the visiting team and Pars Jonoubi captain Mohammad Nouri equalized the match before the interval.

Naft Masjed Soleyman and Foolad played out a goalless draw in Masjed Soleyman.

Nassaji Mazandaran was held to a 0-0 draw by Sanat Naft.

And Shahr Khodro Mashhad also drew 0-0 with Saipa in Tehran.
