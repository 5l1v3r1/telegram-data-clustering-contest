Florida woman accused of suffocating 10-month-old son with pillow

02 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

A Florida woman is accused of intentionally suffocating her 10-month-old son with a pillow, authorities said. 

Victoria Grace Jackson, 24, of Clearwater, is charged with first-degree murder in the death earlier this year of her son, Malachi Jackson, according to arrest records.

Police arrested Jackson on Friday night, the Tampa Bay Times reported.

According to police, Jackson called 911 on May 24 and said she found her son "unresponsive" in his crib, the newspaper reported. Paramedics took the baby to an area hospital, where he was pronounced dead, the Times reported.
