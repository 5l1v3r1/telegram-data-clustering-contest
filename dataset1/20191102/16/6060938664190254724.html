Hrithik Roshan makes a surprise appearance to wish fans celebrating Chhath Puja

02 Nov 2019, 16:51 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood star Hrithik Roshan has quite a few unsuspecting fans star-struck when he peeked beyond his compound wall to greet them as they spent their day by the beach. The star was all smiles as fans quickly flocked around to get photos of him.

Sharing a click of the sweet interaction, the star also extended greetings to the people celebrating the Chhath festival.

“Happy Chhath Puja to all the devotees who are undergoing the tough rituals of this great festival. Sending my love to all of you and my thanks to those who took a moment to interact with me at the beach today,” he captioned the photo.
