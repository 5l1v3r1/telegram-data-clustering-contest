Essex lorry deaths: Woman who sent tragic texts to mum was one of 39 victims

The family of Pham Thi Tra My, 26, say they received the heartbreaking news in a call from England

02 Nov 2019, 19:35 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

A Vietnamese woman who texted her mum to say "I'm dying" has been confirmed as one of the 39 migrants found dead in a lorry trailer in Essex.

The family of Pham Thi Tra My, 26, say they received the heartbreaking news in a call from England on Saturday.
