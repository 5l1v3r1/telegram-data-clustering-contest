Boston Police announce they've found missing 29-year-old man

02 Nov 2019, 21:28 by Manoella Macedo

BOSTON - Boston Police have located a missing 29-year-old man, they announced on Saturday afternoon. The finding comes hours after police asked the public's help in locating the man late Friday night. 

According to police, John Sessions of Braintree was found safe and sound after last being seen in the area of 180 Canal Street in Boston on Thursday at around 7:30 p.m.

Sessions was described as being about 5-foot-7 and weighing about 185 pounds with short brown hair and blue eyes. He had last been seen wearing a blue vest over a white button-down shirt with purple stripes and tan/khaki pants before being located.

In a release, police said Sessions, "ha[d] exhibited behavior that ha[d] caused concern for his well-being."

Anyone with any information regarding the whereabouts of Mr. Sessions was asked to contact District A-1 Detectives at 617-343-4248.
