Watch Video: Special birthday message for Shah Rukh Khan lights up Burj Khalifa

02 Nov 2019, 17:13 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

In a first, the world famous Burj Khalifa lit up with a special message for Shah Rukh Khan on his 54th birthday.

“Happy birthday to the King of Bollywood Shah Rukh Khan” were the words that lit up the iconic tower in Dubai.
