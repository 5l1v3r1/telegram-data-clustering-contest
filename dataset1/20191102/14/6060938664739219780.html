Akshay Kumar’s ‘Laxmmi Bomb’ set to clash with Salman Khan’s ‘Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai’ on Eid 2020

02 Nov 2019, 14:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Come Eid 2020, it is going to be a clash of the titans as Akshay Kumar is set to take on Salman Khan at the box office.

The makers officially announced that the release of ‘Laxmmi Bomb’ has been advanced and will now hit screens on Eid, 2020, a date Salman picked for the release of 'Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai'.

“EID 2020 ko, saare desh mein machega shor, jab fatega #LaxmmiBomb aapke nazdeeki cinema gharon mein,” read a tweet from the film’s official handle.

The makers even shared a poster of sorts that featured Akshay’s first look along with the release date in bold at the bottom.
