Pedestrian hospitalized with life-threatening injuries after being hit by car

02 Nov 2019, 22:24 by EndPlay

BOSTON - A pedestrian was struck by a car in the area of Tremont Street and Whittier Street in Boston, local police confirmed to Boston 25 News. That victim is an elderly male and was hospitalized with life-threatening injuries.

The incident occurred around 4:30 p.m. on Saturday afternoon. The car involved in the crash did remain on scene, per police.
