Double win for Mulranny Park Hotel

02 Nov 2019, 09:00

MULRANNY Park Hotel was a double award winner at the Hotel Awards Ireland 2019, picking up the Luxury Hotel of the Year and Connaught Wedding Hotel of the Year awards.

Delighted general manager Dermot Madigan said the awards were 'testament to the hard work of the team here in the hotel that their hard work and their willingness to go that extra mile is being recognised'.

The Mulranny Park Hotel will be hosting a wedding viewing open weekend on November 23 and 24 where the Grainne Uaile Suite will be fully dressed and newly upgraded accommodation will be available for viewing with guided show arounds with the team.

For more details, visit www.mulrannyparkhotel.ie or call Asta on (098) 36704.
