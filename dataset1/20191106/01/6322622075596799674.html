Ringgit slips against greenback at opening

06 Nov 2019, 01:53 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 6): The ringgit slipped at the opening today on lack of demand for the local currency amid the lower crude oil price, said a dealer.

At 9.10 am, the ringgit was quoted at 4.1320/1350 against the greenback from 4.1290/1320 recorded yesterday. - Bernama
