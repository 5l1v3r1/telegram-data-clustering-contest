All Cats Purr: Weddell Seal Sings in Antarctica

Weddell seals are medium-sized with a circumpolar population surrounding Antarctica that move regionally to follow the distribution of breathing holes in the ice between seasons.

06 Nov 2019, 13:48

A Weddel seal has been captured while purring in Paradise Harbour, Antarctica. The seal performed strange vocalisations that are reportedly used to communicate with other seals underwater.
