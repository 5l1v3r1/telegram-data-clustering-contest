Watch Video: Rishi Kapoor lashes out at the paparazzi for screaming

06 Nov 2019, 08:10 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Veteran actor Rishi Kapoor, who has returned to the bay after his cancer treatment in New York was spotted with his entire family. The Kapoor clan comprising of Rishi, Ranbir, Neetu, Riddhima and little Samara had headed for some intimate dinner together in Mumbai.

A gala of shutterbugs had gathered outside the popular restaurant to snap the Kapoor clan together but what grabbed everyone's attention was Rishi lashing out at the paparazzi for shouting for pictures. The veteran actor said 'Chillane ka nahi' to them as the sea of asked the Kapoor family for pictures.

While the Kapoor's posed for pictures together, this video of Rishi scolding the paparazzi has gone viral on the internet. This isn't the first time that Rishi has lashed out at the shutterbugs as he had asked them to back off and stop making noise outside Ekta Kapoor's residence while he attended the grand Diwali bash.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Rishi's son Ranbir is all set to entertain the audience with his girlfriend Alia Bhatt in Ayan Mukerji's mythological drama 'Brahmastra' which is slated to release next year.
