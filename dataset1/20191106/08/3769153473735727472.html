Assalamu'alaikum. Hi guys! My name is Widhi Muttaqien from Expose Academy.

In this tutorial video, I’m going to show you how to create a "crown molding" easily in Blender 2.8 or above. This method actually works also for older versions of Blender. If you wonder what “crown molding” is, basically it is the decorative trim used to transition from wall to ceiling often seen in classic-style interiors. People in my country usually just call them “ceiling profiles”.

Basically we will use 2 curve objects and the bevel feature. One curve object will be used for the room layout. And another curve is used for the bevel profile.
