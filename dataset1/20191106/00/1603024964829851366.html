Redington Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 4,796.92 crore, up 15% Y-o-Y

06 Nov 2019

Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Redington (India) are:

Net Sales at Rs 4,796.92 crore in September 2019 up 15% from Rs. 4,171.14 crore in September 2018.

Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 162.27 crore in September 2019 up 237.99% from Rs. 48.01 crore in September 2018.

EBITDA stands at Rs. 233.06 crore in September 2019 up 147.46% from Rs. 94.18 crore in September 2018.

Redington EPS has increased to Rs. 4.17 in September 2019 from Rs. 1.20 in September 2018.
