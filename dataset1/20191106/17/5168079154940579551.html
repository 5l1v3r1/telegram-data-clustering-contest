Food outlets in Oman asked to abide by rules

06 Nov 2019, 17:39 by Times News Service

Muscat: In an advisory, the Regional Municipalities and Water Resources Ministry has asked all food establishments to abide by the application of Article 85 of Ministerial Decree No. 29/2016 which deals with health requirements related to public health activities.

The Ministry emphasized the need to restrict food establishments and food stores for the unavailability of a device to measure the temperature at the store.
