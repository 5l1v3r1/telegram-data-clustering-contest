‘Panipat’: Arjun Kapoor ‘honoured, flattered and speechless’ over Shah Rukh Khan, Anil Kapoor and Alia Bhatt’s comments on trailer

06 Nov 2019, 12:01 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The trailer of Arjun Kapoor, Sanjay Dutt, and Kriti Sanon-starrer 'Panipat' released on Tuesday. Packed with powerful dialogues, gory battle scenes and some subtle and soft moments between the lead the pair, the three-minute-fourteen-second trailer had something for everyone.

The film that aims to take viewers back in time to 1761, when the Maratha army waged war against the invading Afghans, that culminated in the epic third battle of Panipat.

Soon after the release, fans and several Bollywood stars including Shah Rukh Khan, Karan Johar, Alia Bhatt, Akshay Kumar, Ranveer Singh, Anil Kapoor, Janhvi Kapoor took to social media to praise the trailer and laud the team for their efforts.

Today, lead actor Arjun, who was humbled by the kind words that came his way, took to social media to personally thank his fellow b-town friends for their support.
