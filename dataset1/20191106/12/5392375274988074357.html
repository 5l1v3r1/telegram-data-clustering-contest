The Redmi Note 8 Pro is currently sold with Android 9 Pie and MIUI 10 out of the box. It is one of the most recent Xiaomi devices and also one of the first to receive updates. Users of this phone may be in luck, because MIUI 11 is already rolling out.

MIUI 11 is now available for the Redmi Note 8 Pro, but only in China

Indeed, the MIUI 11 update has been released in China for the Redmi Note 8 Pro. This device has only been on the market for a few weeks. And is already receiving its ration of the new customization layer. Of course, it is the Chinese version of MIUI 11 and not the global one. In addition, it is based on Android 9 Pie and not Android 10, so it is half good news.

If you live in any country other than China and want to receive MIUI 11 on your Note 8 Pro you will have to wait. The latest news tells us that MIUI 11 Global and stable could reach the western market at the end of December. Yes, there are still about two months to wait for this date.
