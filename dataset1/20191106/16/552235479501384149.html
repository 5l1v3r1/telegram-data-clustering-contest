Part of Kilmarnock road closed as emergency crews deal with incident

The one-way system just next to Kilmarnock Bus Station has been closed off as two ambulances and a police vehicle attend to the situation.

06 Nov 2019, 16:29 by Lochlin Highet, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/lochlin-highet/

A Kilmarnock road has been partially closed as emergency services deal with an ongoing incident.

The one-way system just next to Kilmarnock Bus Station has been closed off as two ambulances and a police vehicle attend to the situation with a bus in front of them.

Drivers heading in from London Road could be affected as buses will need to divert on to the left lane making it a single lane junction for now.

It is unclear what the incident is as of yet, we will update you on the Kilmarnock Standard Facebook page as we get more information.
