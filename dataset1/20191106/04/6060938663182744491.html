Malaika Arora feels his otherwise “perfect” Arjun Kapoor is not so good at THIS one thing!

06 Nov 2019, 04:56 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After months of speculations, Malaika Arora and Arjun Kapoor came out in public about their relationship. The two have been ever since painting the town red with their love. Recently, Malaika opened up her beau, about the kind of wedding she would like and more. During the same interaction, Malaika also revealed that her otherwise perfect partner is not so good at one thing and that is at handling his money. The fitness enthusiast also revealed that Arjun thinks her photography skills are not good.

Although Malaika and Arjun have come out in open about their relationship, they ahve made it clear that marriage is not on the cards any time soon.

Meanwhile, on the work front, Arjun will next be seen in Ashutosh Gowariker’s ‘Panipat’. The film also stars Sanjay Dutt and Kriti Sanon in lead roles.

The makers of the film unveiled the much-awaited trailer of the movie yesterday and it has been receiving mixed reviews from the audience and critics alike.

It is slated to hit the theatres on December 6, 2019.
