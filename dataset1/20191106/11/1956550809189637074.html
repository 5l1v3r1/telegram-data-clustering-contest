A massive party is coming to The Galleria Al Maryah Island in Abu Dhabi

There'll be music from DJ Jazzy Jeff at the Block Party

06 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Colin Armstrong

We’re not left wanting when it comes to parties in Abu Dhabi.

Whether it’s international artists popping up at MAD on Yas Island, DJs at Buddha Bar Beach and ANNEX or all the events over F1 weekend, there’s always loads going on.

Getting in on the action, The Galleria Al Maryah Island is hosting a 12-hour Bloc Party on November 15.

The big event will feature performances from a host of international artists including DJ Jazzy Jeff, Egyptian singer Abu, Bollywood rapper YoYo Honey Singh and pop star Nadina Lustre.

From noon on the day, as well as all the live music, you will be able to watch live entertainment, parades, take part in face painting, balloon bending, and enjoy a spot of retail therapy at the shopping destination.

Concerts will be held on the upper terraces of the shopping centre while DJ Jazzy Jeff will be keeping the party going on street level from behind the decks.

The free event will also shown the concerts live on LED screens across Al Maryah Island so you don’t miss any of the on-stage action.

We’ll see you there, we’ll be the ones demanding that DJ Jazzy Jeff plays Summertime.
Free. Nov 15, noon-midnight. The Galleria Al Maryah Island www.thegalleria.ae.
