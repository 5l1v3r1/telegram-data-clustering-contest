‘Bigil’ Box Office update: Thalapathy Vijay's film emerges as the highest-grosser in overseas territories

06 Nov 2019, 06:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After setting fresh benchmarks and pocketing a whole lot of records, Thalapathy Vijay's 'Bigil' has now become the star's top-grossing film in the overseas market. Within two weeks of release the film has collected a mammoth total of $ 11.5 million.

Bigil Movie Review : Thalapathy Vijay starrer is an engaging entertainer

'Bigil' is already the highest-grossing Tamil movie of 2019 in countries like Singapore, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, UAE/GCC, Norway and France. This sports drama is now the third consecutive film of Vijay to have crossed $10 million mark in the overseas market, after 'Mersal' and 'Sarkar'.

The film features Vijay in a double role as a father and son. He plays the coach of a women's football team. 'Bigil' is directed by Atlee and also stars Jackie Shroff, Nayanthara, Kathir, Yogi Babu and Vivek in crucial roles. The film is produced by AGS Entertainment and was made on a massive budget.
