Saudi’s Jubeir Meets Pompeo in Washington

06 Nov 2019, 06:00

Saudi State Minister for Foreign Affairs Adel al-Jubeir held talks in Washington on Tuesday with US Secretary of State Mike Pompeo.

They tackled the deep historic and strategic relations between Saudi Arabia and the United States, reported the Saudi Press Agency.

They addressed cooperation relations and ways to boost them in various fields, as well as the regional and international developments.

Jubeir later met with US Assistant Secretary of State for Near Eastern Affairs David Schenker.
