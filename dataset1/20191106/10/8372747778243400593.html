Amber Alert issued for missing Jacksonville 5-year-old

06 Nov 2019, 10:58 by WFTX Digital Team

UPDATE (11:25 AM) -- FDLE has upgraded this situation to an AMBER Alert.
----------------------------------------------
DUVAL COUNTY, Fla. -- State law enforcement has issued a Missing Child Alert for a 5-year-old girl last seen in Jacksonville.

Taylor Williams is a black female 3' tall, 50 pounds, with black hair and brown eyes.

According to FDLE, she was last seen in the 600 block of Ivy Street in Jacksonville, wearing purple and pink pajamas.

If you have any information on the whereabouts of this child, please contact the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office at 904-630-0500 or 911.
