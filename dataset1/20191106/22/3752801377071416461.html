Elderly man killed in Bloemfontein house fire

06 Nov 2019, 22:54 by Sesona Ngqakamba

An elderly Bloemfontein man died in a house fire on Wednesday afternoon, ER24 has said. 

According to ER24 spokesperson Ineke van Huyssteen, paramedics arrived at the scene in Rocklands just before 17:00 after being summoned by the police. 

Van Huyssteen said paramedics had declared the man dead at the scene. 

"The exact circumstances surrounding the incident are not known, but local authorities were on the scene."
