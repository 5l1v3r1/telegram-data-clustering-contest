Juve & Bayern Secure UCL Last 16 Spots

06 Nov 2019, 22:10 by SL International

Lokomotiv Moswcow 1-2 Juventus

Juventus struck late in the second half to secure a 2-1 victory away to Lokomotiv Moswcow on Wednesday night.

Aaron Ramsey put the visitors ahead after three minutes with his first Champions League goal for the club, while Aleksey Miranchuck restored parity nine minutes later.

With Cristiano Ronaldo taken off in the second half, it was left to Douglas Costa to grab the headlines after the Brazilian netted the winner in second-half stoppage time.

The win sees Juventus secure a spot in the knockout round.

Bayern MUnich 2-0 Olympiakos

Bayern Munich also secured their spot in the last 16 of the Champions League after cruising to a 2-0 win over Olympiakos.

After a scorless opening half, Robert Lewandowski opened the socring in the 69th minute before Ivan Perisic sealed the victory on 89 minutes.

The Bavarians sit top of Group B and hold a perfect record with four wins from four matches.
