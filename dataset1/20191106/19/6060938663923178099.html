Varun Dhawan's girlfriend Natasha Dalal reveals how they realised they are more than just good friends!

06 Nov 2019, 19:41 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Varun Dhawan and Natasha Dalal are one of the cutest couples of Bollywood. The duo often gets snapped by the paparazzi as they attend events and parties together in the city. The couple has not talked about their relationship openly, however, recently Natasha revealed that they know each other since school!

In an interaction with a magazine, she stated that they were friends until their mid-20s and started dating each other before she moved away. And soon, they realised that they share a connection and they are more than just good friends.

Natasha went on to add that a wedding will be their next step in their relationship but not right now, eventually.

On the work front, Varun will be next seen in Remo D'Souza's 'Street Dancer 3D' co-starring Shraddha Kapoor. The film will release in January 2020.

He will also feature in David Dhawan's 'Coolie No. 1' with Sara Ali Khan. The film has been slated to release on May 01, 2020.
