From previous press releases we already know that Ulefone Armor 7 is the world’s 1st 48MP triple camera with Helio 90 flagship rugged phone. Now, it finally launched on the market and many customers are curious about its performance. To give customers a first glance at the new device before they receive it, Ulefone released a hands-on video to show their versatile flagship phone. So let’s go ahead and check out the video below.

Design-wise, the Ulefone Armor 7 adopts composite material back color with matte lustre, looks decent and upscale. Of course, it is backed by IP68/IP69K/MIL-STD-810G certifications and the video proves just that since it withstood sudden drops or water bath.
