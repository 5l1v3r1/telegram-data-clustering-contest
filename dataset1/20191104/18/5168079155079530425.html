OTA announces the 3rd Monthly Junior Tennis Tournament 2019

04 Nov 2019, 18:23 by Times News Service

Muscat: Oman Tennis Association will organise the third monthly junior tennis tournament 2019 from November 7 to 9.

The association aims to give regular playing opportunity to junior players. Promising players are expected to join and confirm their status.

The categories are under-8 Unisex Mini Tennis, 10, 12 , 14, 18 under boys and girls singles. The deadline of entries will be November 5 by 12pm which will be followed by a draw.

All registration forms of the tournament are available at OTA office in Al Ghubra Oman Olympic Committee (Spors) building and Sultan Qaboos Sports Complex, Bausher Tennis Court.
