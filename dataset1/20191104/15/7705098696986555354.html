Arnold Schwarzenegger always says that he’ll be back, but this past weekend, the Terminator wasn’t alone! Linda Hamilton also returned to the franchise as Sarah Connor in Terminator: Dark Fate. Additionally, this film threw all of the other sequels out as the “official” sequel to Terminator 2. Was this the Terminator film you’ve been waiting for? Mackenzie Davis, Natalia Reyes, and Gabriel Luna had the most prominent roles among the new cast. Davis portrayed, Grace, a cybernetically enhanced soldier, while Reyes played Daniella “Dani” Ramos. Luna stepped into the franchise as the Rev-9, a fearsome new Terminator model.

James Cameron returned to the franchise to co-write the story and produce the film. However, Tim Miller directed Dark Fate from a script by David Goyer, Justin Rhodes, and Billy Ray.
