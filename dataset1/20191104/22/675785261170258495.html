Sister of terror boss al-Baghdadi captured as police find 'gold mine' of intelligence

Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi's sister, believed to be called Rasmiya Awad, was captured in a raid in Azaz, Syria

04 Nov 2019, 22:53 by Bradley Jolly, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/bradley-jolly/

The sister of slain ISIS leader Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi has been captured in Syria.

It is thought Rasmiya Awad, 65, is now in custody after Turkish officials found her living in a trailer with family members in Azaz, Syria.

Bakr al-Baghdadi was killed in a military raid in the country last week.
