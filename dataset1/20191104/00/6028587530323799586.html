Walmart’s Black Friday 2019 Shipping Policy

Despite official shipping policy news from Best Buy and from Target, Walmart has kept silent. But this silence is telling. Target has long had the best Black Friday shipping terms and it was keen to promote this. Similarly, Best Buy’s announcement was an upgrade to match Target. But, speaking to industry insiders, the bad news is Walmart’s silence is because the store will stick to the same $35 minimum spend for free shipping as in previous years. 

The good news? It looks like you’ll want to spend a lot more than $35 on Walmart’s 2019 Black Friday sales.
