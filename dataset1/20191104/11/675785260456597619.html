RuPaul's Drag Race: Werq the World Tour is coming to the UK next spring

Tickets for the UK shows scheduled for next spring will go on sale this Friday - find out where to buy yours

04 Nov 2019, 11:50 by Chantelle Symester, Jonathan Yates, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

Emmy award-winning show RuPaul Drag Race is bringing its hit world tour show to the UK next spring.

The famous faces of popular drag queens past and present will be heading to key cities across the country, including London, Manchester and Birmingham.

The full line up for the shows will include Michelle Visage and US season 11 winner Yvie Oddly, who will be joined by favourites like Plastique Tiara, Asia O'Hara and Noami Smalls.

Die-hard fans of the hit TV show and its UK spin off will be able to buy tickets for dates scheduled for next May at 10am on Friday 8 November.

Tickets will be available from major ticket outlets AXS , Ticketmaster and theticketfactory.com .

Earlier this year the tour stopped off at selected dates in Europe, including stops in London, Sunderland and Edinburgh and featured US season 10 winner Aquaria.

If you're a fan of the UK version o f RuPaul's Drag Race, ten queens from the debut season are heading out on tour with Voss Events with dates scheduled for the Troxy in London this winter.

Werq the World Tour UK show dates

  • 5 May 2020 - P&J Live, Aberdeen
  • 7 May 2020 - Manchester Arena
  • 8 May 2020 - SSE Hydro, Glasgow
  • 12 May 2020 - Arena Birmingham
  • 15 May 2020 - The SSE Arena, Wembley

RuPaul will also bring his DragCon to the UK for the first time ever for a weekend of drag performances, meet and greets and more at the Olympia in London on January 18-19.
