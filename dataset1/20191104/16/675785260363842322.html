Liverpool vs Man City referee confirmed ahead of title showdown at Anfield

Michael Oliver will take charge of the Premier League clash at Anfield between unbeaten Liverpool and champions Manchester City

04 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Liam Prenderville, http://www.facebook.com/mirrorfootball

Michael Oliver will take charge of this weekend's title showdown between Liverpool and Manchester City .

The Premier League rivals will face off at Anfield on Sunday in a potentially crucial clash in the race for the title.

Liverpool are yet to taste defeat this season while champions City are in second, six points adrift of the Reds.

A win for Jurgen Klopp's side means they could go nine points clear ahead of the festive period.

The Premier League has confirmed Oliver will take charge at Anfield, with Paul Tierney named as the VAR official.

Oliver has taken charge of Liverpool twice this season - the wins over Norwich and Chelsea, and was also in the middle for City's controversial draw with Tottenham in August.

On the back of his controversial officiating in Everton's draw with Tottenham on Sunday, Martin Atkinson will be in charge of the clash between Newcastle an Bournemouth .

Atkinson was at the centre of the encounter at Goodison Park after Son Heung-min was shown red for a challenge on Andre Gomes, that led to the midfielder fracturing his ankle.

Both sides were also denied penalties by controversial VAR calls during the 1-1 draw.

Premier League referee appointments

  • Norwich vs Watford - Andre Marriner
  • Chelsea vs Crystal Palace - Mike Dean
  • Burnley vs West Ham - Kevin Friend
  • Newcastle vs Bournemouth - Martin Atkinson
  • Southampton vs Everton - Paul Tierney
  • Tottenham vs Sheffield United - Graham Scott
  • Leicester vs Arsenal - Chris Kavanagh
  • Man Utd vs Brighton - Jonathan Moss
  • Wolves vs Aston Villa - Anthony Taylor
  • Liverpool vs Man City - Michael Oliver
