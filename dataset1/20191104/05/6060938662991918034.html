Bayern Munich sack coach Niko Kovac

04 Nov 2019, 05:26 by AFP

BERLIN: Bayern Munich on Sunday sacked coach Niko Kovac, a day after the German champions suffered a historic 5-1 loss against Eintracht Frankfurt, capping a poor run of form in recent weeks.

"FC Bayern Munich have relieved head coach Niko Kovac of his duties," the club said in a statement.
