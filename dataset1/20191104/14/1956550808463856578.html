Dubai's Shakespeare and Co. launches bargain afternoon tea

Enjoy mini sandwiches, macarons, chocolates and more for under Dhs100

04 Nov 2019, 14:48 by Reema Rahman

This much-loved family-friendly Dubai restaurant is popular for its kitsch décor, friendly atmosphere and tasty grub. And if you haven’t tried it out for yourself, we’ve got two words for you: afternoon tea.

The newly launched afternoon tea will be served daily from 3pm to 6pm at all branches across the UAE excluding The Dubai Mall and comes in at Dhs69 per person.

The pocket-friendly deal includes the choice of one tea or one coffee, a selection of bread, butter and jams, a trio of mini sandwiches (salmon toast, club sandwich and the option between a chicken or beef slider), one individual cake, four pieces of chocolate served in a box and the choice of any two macaron flavours.
