2019 MTV Europe Music Awards: The winners list

Taylor Swift has big night

04 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Lisa Respers France, CNN

(CNN) - Ariana Grande was up for seven awards, but Taylor Swift had the bigger night along with Billie Eilish and Halsey at the 2019 MTV Europe Music Awards on Sunday in Seville, Spain.
