Suspicious package sent to Liberal Democrat leader Jo Swinson

04 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Stefan Boscia

Police are investigating a suspicious package that was sent to the Westminster office of Liberal Democrat leader Jo Swinson.

Scotland Yard confirmed officers were at Portcullis House investigating the incident.
