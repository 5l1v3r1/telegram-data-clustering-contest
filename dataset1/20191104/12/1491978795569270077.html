The World Cup is like a rollercoaster ... You get down the first slope and you are not sure if you are going to throw up or hang on.Eddie Jones, England coach
We need to bring chaos into their organisation.Vasily Artemyev, Russia, before the opening match against Japan
They won it, I don't think we lost it.Rassie Erasmus, after defeat to New Zealand
Our game is about intimidation. Some people might not want to hear that, but that's a fact.Steve Hansen, New Zealand coach
I don't know where it comes from. It's inside. It's in there. Today, we had to go there to fetch it, and it came.Juan Manuel Gaminara, Uruguay captain, after defeating Fiji
Japan did not exceed my expectations. They met them.Joe Schmidt, Ireland coach, after defeat to Japan
I was behind the last man and I started laughing because the forwards were driving so well I couldn't yell.Faf de Klerk, South Africa scrum-half
If New Zealand needed four or five points against us it would not have been cancelled.Sergio Parisse, Italy
If you play it later, then that’s when we are finished for Christmas, you’d have Santa Claus giving out the World Cup.Steve Hansen
The things that happen in this tournament don't happen in other sports. You've got the Canadian blokes cleaning up (after Typhoon Hagibis).Eddie Jones
I want to set a good example and show what being a man is. Being a man isn't losing your rag and your emotions. It's about being calm.Kyle Sinckler, England prop
