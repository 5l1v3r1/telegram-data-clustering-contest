Trump committed to Asia, says US commerce secretary Ross

04 Nov 2019, 03:48

BANGKOK, Nov 4 — The administration of US President Donald Trump is “fully committed” to Asia, his commerce secretary said today at a regional forum attended by a US delegation smaller than previous years.

US Commerce Secretary Wilbur Ross spoke at a business forum, part of the annual leaders’ summit of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations.

“The Trump administration is extremely engaged and fully committed to this region,” Ross said.

“We will continue to negotiate trade deals with countries in this region.” — Reuters pic
