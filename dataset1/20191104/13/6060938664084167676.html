‘Panipat’ vs ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’: Arjun Kapoor to battle it out with Kartik Aaryan at the box office

04 Nov 2019, 13:20 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Come December 6th, fans will be left spoilt for choice as Kartik Aaryan’s romantic comedy ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ is all set to clash with Arjun Kapoor’s periodic drama ‘Panipat’.

At a time when fans are bracing themselves for some of the biggest clashes in box office history set to take place in 2020, it now looks like 2019 will be ending with a bang.

The makers of the Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday, unveiled the trailer of their film today that was well received by fans for its hilarious one-liners, interesting plot twist and plenty of quips. Not only has the trailer managed to woo fans over, but Kartik has even made it to top trends on social media handles.

Meanwhile, team ‘Panipat’ is getting ready to unveil the trailer of their historical drama on Tuesday. The team released some impressive posters of the lead stars, Arjun Kapoor, Sanjay Dutt and Kriti Sanon.
