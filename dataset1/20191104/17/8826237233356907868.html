Original Survivor Fan Favorite Rudy Boesch Dies at 91

04 Nov 2019, 17:55 by Associated Press

Rudy Boesch, a retired tough-as-nails Navy SEAL and fan favorite on the inaugural season of Survivor, has died after a long battle with Alzheimer’s disease. He was 91.

He died peacefully Friday night in hospice care in Virginia Beach, Virginia, surrounded by loved ones, said Steve Gonzalez, director of operations for the Seal Veterans Foundation.
