5.3-Magnitude Earthquake Hits Near Coast of Northern Chile

According to the website sismologue.com, the quake struck at 9:03 am local time 55.82km away from Pozo Perdido, which is the nearest city to the epicentre.

04 Nov 2019, 17:16

A 5.3-magnitude earthquake hit near the coast of Northern Chile, the United States Geological Survey (USGS) has reported.
