Time will never forgive those who remained confused: Fazlur Rehman

04 Nov 2019, 17:53 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) - Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam-Fazl (JUI-F) chief Maulana Fazlur Rehman on Monday said all national parties must not prefer minor issues over issues of public interest.

All Parties Conference (APC) of 9 opposition parties is underway under the chair of Maulana Fazlur Rehman. The meeting questioned whether to get rid from rigged rulers or to extend their rule.

Maulana said time will never forgive those parties who remained confused at crucial situations.

JUI-F head questioned whether it was decision of a single party to hold a rally against these rulers. He invited all members to devises a solid mechanism for public rule.

He said JUI-F wants to take all democratic parties with it.

On the other hand, JUI-F has asked its members to submit resignations from National Assembly to the leadership.

JUI-F leadership has decided to take resignations from its 15 members of National Assembly so that these resignations can be used at proper time.
