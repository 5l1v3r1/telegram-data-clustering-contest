Guilty Gear reveals Chip Zanuff, Potemkin and lengthy gameplay video

Break out your Flying V

04 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Chris Moyse

Arc System Works has dropped a bevy of treats for Guilty Gear fans this morning, with the reveal of two more characters, alongside all new gameplay footage, for its brand new, upcoming title in the hard-rockin' fighting game series.

The first of the new fighters is hipster ninja Chip Zanuff, a former American street gang member who has adopted a Japanese profile - as well as the art of ninjitsu - in order to better himself and redeem his prior reputation. A hugely popular character among GG players, Chip is an incredibly fast-moving fighter, capable of formidable aerial attack and practiced in teleportation arts.

Potemkin is one of Guilty Gear's most recognisable characters. An elite member of the Zepp guard, Potemkin is an intelligent and philosophical individual, a personailty somewhat juxtaposed by his enormous frame and war-ready battle armour. As one of GG's primary tanks, Potemkin has to make two trips when he wants to haul ass, but is capable of incredible damage, while commanding a huge portion of the play area.
