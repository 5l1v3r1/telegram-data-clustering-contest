'Better than Messi' - West Brom fans calling for same thing from Slaven Bilic after Stoke City win

West Brom latest | Hal Robson-Kanu came off the bench to score a second-half penalty in the 2-0 win over Stoke City - and Baggies supporters have been signing his praises

05 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Josh Murray

West Bromwich Albion fans have been celebrating their side's return to the top of the Championship table in comfortable fashion.

The Baggies triumphed 2-0 at bottom-of-the-table Stoke City on Monday night, Matt Phillips setting them on the way to victory with an eighth-minute opener.

Hal Robson-Kanu then wrapped up the points from the penalty spot after replacing Charlie Austin.

Robson-Kanu's display from the bench was one of several factors that earned praise from Albion supporters at the bet365 Stadium and on social media, as well as the quality Slaven Bilic's side showed on the ball.
