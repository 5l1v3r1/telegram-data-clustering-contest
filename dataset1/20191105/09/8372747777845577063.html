Heartbreaking video shows injured bear on Immokalee road

05 Nov 2019, 09:49 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- A video showing an injured bear being dragged off the road by other bears is going viral online.

The video was posted to Facebook on Monday night.

The woman recording the video is distraught as she sees several bears in the roadway in Immokalee. One juvenile bear appears to be seriously injured and cannot walk on its back leg.

That's when another bear grabs it by the tuft of the neck and drags the injured bear off the road and into the bushes.
