Virat Kohli celebrates 31st birthday with 'soulmate' Anushka Sharma

05 Nov 2019, 09:40 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

NEW DELHI: India cricket captain Virat Kohli celebrated his 31st birthday on Tuesday with his wife and Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma.

Kohli and Anushka are reportedly in Bhutan, as Kohli takes a well deserved break from cricket.

Kohli took to Twitter and shared a photo with Anushka and wrote: “What a blessing to be able to visit such divine places with my soulmate. Also thank you everyone for your kind wishes from the bottom of my heart.”
