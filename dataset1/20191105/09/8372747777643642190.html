Lee schools to decide on new school name Tuesday

05 Nov 2019, 09:02 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- By the end of the Tuesday we'll know the name of Lee County's newest high school.

The school board will decide between four finalists: Gateway, Thomas Edison, Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., and Gabriela Mistrial High.

Those names were among the most submitted in an open call for names . Then an online survey to vote among those finalists resulted in more than 60 percent of people voting for Gateway High .

The new school will open up in August of 2021 in the Gateway Area, on Griffin Drive south of State Road 82.

The school board meeting begins at 6 p.m.

The county will also vote on pre-construction budgets for two other new schools, both in the Estero area. One is an elementary school, the other is a middle school.

The county is set to spend about $7 million combined on those two projects.
