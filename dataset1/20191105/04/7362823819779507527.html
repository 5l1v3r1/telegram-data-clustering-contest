Tulsi Gabbard posts intense workout video on Twitter

05 Nov 2019, 04:15 by Fox News

Rep. Tulsi Gabbard, D-Hawaii, took to Twitter Monday to show her intense workout regime while on the campaign trail.

Gabbard, who has been fighting her own party, claiming that they have been working against her in her Democratic primary race, has vowed to change how the party is run if given the opportunity.
