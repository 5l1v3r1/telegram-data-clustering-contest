Intellect Design Arena plunges 19% on net loss of Rs 17 crore in Q2

The stock was trading at its lowest level since December 2017.

05 Nov 2019, 05:07 by SI Reporter

Shares of Intellect Design Arena plunged 18 per cent to Rs 153 on the BSE on Tuesday after the company reported a consolidated net loss of Rs 17 crore in September quarter (Q2FY20), due to lower revenue. The stock was trading at its lowest level since December 2017. The company, a specialist in applying true digital technologies and a global leader in financial technology across banking and insurance, had posted a net profit of Rs 31 crore in the year-ago quarter. Earlier, in December 2016 quarter, it had posted net loss of Rs 16 crore. Operational income during the quarter declined 14 per cent to Rs 328 crore against Rs 383 crore in the previous year quarter.
