Christmas Market returning to Hilton Dubai Jumeirah

There will be more than 30 festive stalls

05 Nov 2019, 12:21 by Darragh Murphy

Now that we’re out of the ghoulish Halloween celebrations, it’s time to look forward to the most wonderful time of the year. And Hilton Dubai Jumeirah on JBR has just the Christmas market to check out.

The beachside hotel on JBR is bringing back its weekend-long Christmas Market, offering more than 20 festive stalls, Santa visits, gingerbread decorations and plenty of seasonal dishes.

Expect the market to kick off on Friday December 6 until Saturday December 7 from 10am until 4pm, with the man in the big red suit dropping by from noon until 2pm.

Each of the stalls will offer Christmas goodies and gifts to fill those stockings with, along with decorations to do up your Christmas tree with.

Food-wise, expect turkey ham croquettes with cranberry dip, roasted turkey roulade and beef cheese Käsekrainer hot dog, as well as festive sweets like giant churros with hot chocolate sauce, gingerbread man cookies, popcorn and candy floss.

As for the kids, let them loose to enjoy a gingerbread decoration activity, DIY card making, garden games in the private al fresco garden, a bouncy castle and much more.
