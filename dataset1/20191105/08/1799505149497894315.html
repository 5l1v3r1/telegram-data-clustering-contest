Emotional Support Dog

05 Nov 2019, 08:05 by BRIEF

MIAMI – A federal court in Florida dismissed an emotional distress complaint against American Airlines’ brought by a woman who claims she and her dog, Simba, were mistreated by airline staff. The woman failed to show that her dog was “maliciously damaged.”
