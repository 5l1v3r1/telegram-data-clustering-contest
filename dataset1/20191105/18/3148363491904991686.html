Man Stabbed to Death at Popeyes After Alleged Fight over Sandwich

A fight over someone cutting in line over the chain’s popular chicken sandwich at a Maryland Popeyes turned fatal after a man stabbed someone in line multiple times.

05 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Katherine Rodriguez

Prince George’s County Police said it received reports of an altercation breaking out at an Oxon Hill Popeyes on Monday at around 7 p.m.
