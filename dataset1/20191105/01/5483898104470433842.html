Support Pours In For Mark Bickley Following Family Tragedy

05 Nov 2019, 01:13

The entire FIVEaa team’s thoughts are with Mark Bickley after his family was touched by tragedy over the weekend.

Opening the sports show on Monday, co-host Stephen Rowe told listeners:

“Sadly Bicks is not in today. Unexpectedly his brother David passed away Sunday morning. To Bicks, his mum Babs, dad Max, David’s family and friends -- our deepest condolences and thoughts with you in this tough time.

“The entire FIVEaa family are thinking of you. I cannot remember the last time we had so many calls and people passing on their best to Bicks and his family. Very hard to fathom, God bless.

“It’s a tough, tough time… Bicks, love ya buddy.”
