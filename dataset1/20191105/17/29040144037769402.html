Pixel 4's 90Hz display gets fewer limitations with November security patch

05 Nov 2019, 17:17

Source: Android Central

What you need to know

  • After the latest software update, the Pixel 4's "Smooth Display" switches to 60Hz when the brightness level is set at below 42%.
  • The Google Pixel 4 XL, on the other hand, now runs at 90Hz irrespective of the ambient brightness level.
  • Pixel 4 owners still have the option of forcing the display to refresh at 90Hz at all times from Developer Options.

Yesterday, Google began pushing a new software update for the Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL, fixing the screen refresh rate issue and also bringing the latest November security patch. However, as reported by XDA Developers Editor-in-Chief Mishaal Rahman, the Smooth Displays on the Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL now behave differently.
