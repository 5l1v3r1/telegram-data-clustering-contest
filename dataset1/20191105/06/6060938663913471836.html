Millennials Bonding: Ananya Panday wishes birthday girls Athiya Shetty and Khushi Kapoor with a beautiful post

05 Nov 2019, 06:27 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ananya Panday has not only established herself as one of the best actresses in the industry after giving her best in her debut film 'Student Of The Year 2' alongside Tiger Shroff and Tara Sutaria but also grabbed all the limelight with her grace and beauty as well.

The young starlet took to Instagram to share a video to wish her pals Athiya Shetty and Khushi Kapoor with a beautiful post on their birthday. While Athiya, who turns 27 today is currently busy with the promotions of her upcoming film 'Motichoor Chaknachoor' with Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Khushi, on the other hand, is busy with her education in the prestigious New York Film Academy.

The trio can be seen having a gala time with Shanaya Kapoor also joining them in this throwback video which was captioned "Happy bday babies" by Ananya. Not only that but she even asked the birthday girls if they remember who had they sent this video to as she also wrote"Do y'all remember who we sent the video to" at the bottom.

Meanwhile, on the work front, Ananya will soon be seen opposite Kartik Aaryan and Bhumi Pednekar in 'Pati, Patni Aur Woh' which is a remake of the 1978 film of the same name, starring Sanjeev Kumar, Vidya Sinha and Ranjeeta Kaur. The film is helmed by Mudassar Aziz and is slated to hit the theatres on 6th December this year.
