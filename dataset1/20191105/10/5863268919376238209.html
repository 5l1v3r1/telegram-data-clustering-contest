PM Imran convenes govt's negotiating committee meeting

05 Nov 2019, 10:26 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prime Minister Imran Khan has summoned a meeting of the government’s negotiating committee to meet today (Tuesday) in Islamabad, Dunya News reported.

According to the sources, the meeting to the government’s negotiating committee is scheduled to be held at the Prime Minister office in Islamabad at 11:00am. PM Imran will preside over the meeting.

Sources further informed that the meeting will review the current political situation in the country.

The meeting will also review the situation arise after the JUI-F anti-government Azadi March.
