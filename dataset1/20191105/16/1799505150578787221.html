Politics & Money

05 Nov 2019, 16:00 by BRIEF

ALEXANDRIA, Va. — Action Mailing sued William J. McCarthy, William J. McCarthy and Associates Inc., State Department Watch, and Americans Care in federal court, claiming it sent more than 1.1 million direct mailers for the right-wing political groups and their affiliates, which owe it $277,440 for the work.
