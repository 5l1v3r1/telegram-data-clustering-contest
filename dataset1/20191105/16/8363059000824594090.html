Your Reality May Not Be That Real in The Mandela Effect

05 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Germain Lussier

The Mandela Effect is a strange phenomenon where a lot of people remember something incorrectly. In film, a good example is “Luke, I am your father.” That’s not what Darth Vader said. And yet, we all remember it that way. (The line is “No, I am your father,” of course.)
