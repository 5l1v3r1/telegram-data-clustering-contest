Hewlett Packard

05 Nov 2019, 15:57 by BRIEF

SAN JOSE — A former audit director seeks punitive damages from Hewlett Packard Enterprise Co., claiming it retaliated against him for truthfully reporting that senior management was engaging in “revenue manipulation, kickbacks, bribery of foreign officials, and employment of ‘ghost’ employees,” in Santa Clara County Court.
