Commodities Fraud

05 Nov 2019, 15:59 by BRIEF

TUSCALOOSA, Ala. — The CFTC claims in federal court that Aaron B. Butler, of Muscle Shoals, Ala., defrauded investors of $300,000 in a commodities scam through his co-defendant company, Negus Capital.
