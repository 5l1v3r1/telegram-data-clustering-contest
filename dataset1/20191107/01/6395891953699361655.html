Person fatally struck by train in Newton

07 Nov 2019, 01:44 by Manoella Macedo

NEWTON, Mass. - A person was hit and killed by a Commuter Rail train in Newton on Wednesday night, transit police said.

According to officials, transit police received a call at around 8:15 p.m. for reports of a person struck by a train at the MBTA's West Newton Commuter Rail station.
