PM Imran, Abu Dhabi crown prince discuss bilateral cooperation, regional issues on phone

07 Nov 2019, 06:43 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) - Prime Minister Imran Khan on Wednesday called Abu Dhabi Crown Prince Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed al-Nahyan to talk about bilateral cooperation and regional issues, Dunya News reported.

Crown Prince Mohammed bin Zayed is also the deputy supreme commander of the United Arab Emirates (UAE) Armed Forces.

According to sources, both leaders discussed ways to enhance bilateral cooperation as well as the latest regional and international developments and issues of mutual interest.
