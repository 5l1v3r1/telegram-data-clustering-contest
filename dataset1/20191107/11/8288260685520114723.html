'Unleash' - Aston Villa fans are all saying the same thing about Jack Grealish

Aston Villa latest news | The claret and blue captain is in line for his first call-up to the England national team and fans of his side are all saying the same thing

07 Nov 2019, 11:47 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans are calling for Jack Grealish to be included in the latest England squad.

The Aston Villa captain is in line for his first cal-up to Gareth Southgate's senior side having featured for the Under-21s in the past.

Calls for Grealish to be included in the England team for European Championships qualifiers against Montenegro and Serbia later this month have grown after he missed out on the last couple of squads.

Grealish has watched his Villa team-mate Tyrone Mings make his debut for the Three Lions but he has remained down the pecking order below Leicester City's James Maddison and Chelsea's Mason Mount.

 

However, before Grealish picked up a calf injury against Manchester City last month - one that ruled him out of Villa's past couple of games - he had been an influential figure for his boyhood team .

He netted an equaliser against Brighton and Hove Albion and set up the winner for Matt Targett in injury time - a performance that led to Grealish being touted for the next England squad.

An official announcement is expected this afternoon and Villa fans want to see their hero included.
