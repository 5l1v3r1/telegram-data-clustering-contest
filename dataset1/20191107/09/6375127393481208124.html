Cops kill 2 armed dealers, jail 10

07 Nov 2019, 09:53 by Alfred P. Dalizon

TWO armed drug trafficking suspects were killed in shootouts while 10 others were arrested in a string of anti-narcotics operations conducted by agents of the Bulacan Police Provincial Office on Tuesday and Wednesday, a report to Police Regional Office 3 director, Brigadier General Rhodel O. Sermonia said yesterday.

The slain suspects were identified as Mark Panganoron, a man included in the unified drug watch list of the Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency and the Philippine National Police and Mark Anthony Mallares.
