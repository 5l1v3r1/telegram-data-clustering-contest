Jardine C&C's 9M earnings nearly double to US$729 mil, but underlying earnings down 9% to US$614 mil

07 Nov 2019, 10:57 by Samantha Chiew

SINGAPORE (Nov 7): Jardine Cycle & Carriage (JC&C), a holding company of Jardine Matheson, announced its earnings have nearly doubled to US$729 million (S$991 million) for the 9MFY19 ended September 2019, from US$374 million in 9M18.

However, the surge was mainly due to net non-trading gains of US$115 million from unrealised fair value gains related to non-current investments. For the same period last year (9MFY18), there were net non-trading losses of US$300 million from unrealised fair value losses on these investments.
