Stewartry Sirens beat Tennent's National One (Women's) leaders Garioch 33-7

Three tries in the first 33-7 minutes paved the way for a tremendous victory that puts the Sirens into third

07 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Galloway News, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/galloway-news/

Tennent’s National One (Women’s): Stewartry Sirens avenged a 17-31 defeat away at Garioch in September when they blew the league leaders away at Greenlaw with a six try thumping.

They scored three tries in the first 20 minutes to win 33-7. This leaves Sirens in third place behind now second placed Garioch who lost top spot with Heriots Blues winning 29-5 against Broughton.

 

Scorers for Stewartry in their third win in a row were Claire Manson (three), Chloe Johnstone (two) and Lousia Ramsey (one) and there was a last minute penalty from Lisa Ritchie.

The other results were Annan Warriors 48 Glasgow University 0, Howe of Fife 42 Oban Lorne 12.

With the World Cup taking pride of place on Saturday, most of the senior Scottish rugby leagues were postponed. However, there was Tennent’s National Two action for league leaders Dumfries Saints who beat GHK 29-10 for a seventh win out of seven.

There was a 40-0 defeat for eighth-placed Newton Stewart away at Preston Lodge.

Border Shield semi-finals: Langholm 17 Hawick Harlequins 45, Duns 62 Hawick Linden 0.
