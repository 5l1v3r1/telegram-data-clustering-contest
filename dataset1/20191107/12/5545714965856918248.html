Valkyria Chronicles 4 Steam update adds all DLC for free

Spoils of War

07 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Chris Moyse

The Steam edition of Sega's tactical RPG Valkyria Chronicles 4 has been updated and is now Valkyria Chronicles 4: Complete Edition. All previously released DLC is now included in the purchase and, best of all, everyone who has already purchased the vanilla edition now gets all of the DLC for free!

Released in 2018 for PC and consoles, Valkyria Chronicles 4 continues the story of the Second European War, telling the tale of ragtag outfit Squad E and their efforts to stave off the Imperial Invasion. As with its predecessors, Valkyria Chronicles 4 combines dramatic anime storytelling with tactical turn-based combat.

As part of the Complete Edition, players will now receive the following six DLC packs:

  • Valkyria Chronicles 4 - A United Front with Squad 7
  • Valkyria Chronicles 4 - Advance Ops
  • Valkyria Chronicles 4 - The Two Valkyria
  • Valkyria Chronicles 4 - Expert Level Skirmishes
  • Valkyria Chronicles 4 - Squad E, to the Beach!
  • Valkyria Chronicles 4 - A Captainless Squad

Valkyria Chronicles 4 is available now on PS4, PC, Xbox One and Nintendo Switch.
