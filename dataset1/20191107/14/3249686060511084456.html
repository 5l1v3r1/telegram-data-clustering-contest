ONE drops his video teaser for 'Hard to Love'

07 Nov 2019, 14:41 by germainej

ONE has dropped the teaser video for his upcoming release 'Hard to Love'.

The artsy teaser video reveals a desert setting and smooth R&B melody. "Hard to Love" is a track from ONE's upcoming album 'PRVT01', which drops on November 11 KST.
