Latest George Michael Song Rolls Out Posthumously For Comedy ‘Last Christmas’

07 Nov 2019 by Yimin Huang

George Michael passed away three years ago in a suicide attempt, upsetting fans across the world. Now, fans can at least take comfort in the latest song released from the singer-songwriter posthumously.
The song is entitled This Is How (We Want You to Get High), and will be used in the upcoming romantic comedy Last Christmas. It is his first original track material to be rolled out in seven years, according to Digital Spy.
Upon the track’s release, Michael’s sisters Melanie and Yioda, his father Jack and former collaborator David Austin expressed in a statement that they hoped fans would “rejoice” in listening to the song written with “immense passion and pride.”
