GNFC Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 1,332.35 crore, down 18.82% Y-o-Y

07 Nov 2019

Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Gujarat Narmada Valley Fertilizers & Chemicals are:

Net Sales at Rs 1,332.35 crore in September 2019 down 18.82% from Rs. 1,641.16 crore in September 2018.

Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 81.86 crore in September 2019 down 72.38% from Rs. 296.33 crore in September 2018.

Close

EBITDA stands at Rs. 155.26 crore in September 2019 down 62.39% from Rs. 412.78 crore in September 2018.

GNFC EPS has decreased to Rs. 5.27 in September 2019 from Rs. 19.06 in September 2018.

GNFC shares closed at 203.90 on November 06, 2019 (NSE) and has given -34.90% returns over the last 6 months and -45.72% over the last 12 months.
