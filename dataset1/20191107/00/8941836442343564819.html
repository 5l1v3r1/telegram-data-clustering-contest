Capital Senior Living: 3Q Earnings Snapshot

07 Nov 2019

DALLAS (AP) _ Capital Senior Living Corp. (CSU) on Thursday reported a loss of $20.7 million in its third quarter.

The Dallas-based company said it had a loss of 68 cents per share.

The operator of senior living communities posted revenue of $111.1 million in the period.

The company’s shares closed at $3.99. A year ago, they were trading at $9.34.
