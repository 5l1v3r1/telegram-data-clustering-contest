Orange County gang task force makes several arrests in Santa Ana

07 Nov 2019, 21:01

SANTA ANA, Calif. (KABC) -- The FBI's Orange County violent gang task force executed several arrest warrants in Santa Ana and other cities Thursday, according to officials.

Authorities say 11 people were indicted in an operation that uncovered guns, ammunition, money and drugs.

Some of the suspects were also arrested for violent crimes, including murder and attempted murder.

Officials were expected to announce additional details at a press conference at the Orange County district attorney's office.

An investigation is ongoing.
