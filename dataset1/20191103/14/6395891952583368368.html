Good Samaritan helping woman change flat tire in San Antonio struck by vehicle, police say

03 Nov 2019, 14:24 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

SAN ANTONIO -

A driver of a semitrailer who pulled over to help a woman change a flat tire is in critical condition after he was struck by a vehicle, authorities said.

According to San Antonio police, at 12:30 a.m. Saturday the truck driver noticed a woman pulled over on an interstate ramp, trying to change a tire after a blowout, KSAT reported.
