Man tragically dies after being hit by car on busy road in Fife

The 29-year-old was walking on the A985 between Rosyth and Limekilns around 4.15am when he was struck.

03 Nov 2019, 14:06 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

A man has tragically died after being hit by a car on a busy road in Fife.

The 29-year-old was walking on the A985 between Rosyth and Limekilns around 4.15am on Sunday November 3 when he was struck.

Emergency services rushed to the incident, but sadly he was pronounced dead at the scene.

The 23 year-old male driver of the BMW two series involved in the incident was uninjured.

Sergeant Stephen Quinn from Fife's Road Policing Unit said: “Our investigation is continuing into this road crash and I would appeal to any witnesses, or anyone with information that may assist our enquiries to contact us.

“I would also appeal to anyone driving on the A985 road around the time of the crash who has dash-cam footage to come forward, as they may hold detail that will help our investigation.

“Anyone with information should contact Fife Road Policing Unit through 101 quoting reference number 0956 of 3rd November 2019.”
