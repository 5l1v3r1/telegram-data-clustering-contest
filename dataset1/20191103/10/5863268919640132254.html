PM congratulates govt on completing Kartarpur Corridor in record time

03 Nov 2019, 10:50 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prime Minister (PM) Imran Khan on Sunday has congratulated the government for completing Kartarpur Corridor in record time for the celebrations of Baba Guru Nanak’s 550th birthday.

In a tweet, the premier shared mesmerizing pictures of the newly-constructed corridor and wrote, “I want to congratulate our govt for readying Kartarpur, in record time, for Guru Nanak jee s 550th birthday celebrations.”
