Trump Blames California Fires on Governor's 'Terrible Job' of Forest Management

Earlier this week, the Californian Department of Forestry and Fire Protection said that most of the fires in the state were contained.

03 Nov 2019, 20:53

​US President Donald Trump harshly criticised California Governor Gavin Newsom’s handling of forest management on Sunday, blaming him for the fires currently engulfing the western state.
