Deals worth considering

As you might expect, HP's ad is chock-full of deals on laptop and desktop PCs making it worth a look if you're in the market for a computer upgrade. Of course, not all deals on Black Friday are worth jumping on, so we've outlined a few choice picks you should consider as well as a some to avoid.
