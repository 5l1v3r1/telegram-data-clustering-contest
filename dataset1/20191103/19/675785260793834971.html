Strictly's first same-sex dance branded 'stunningly beautiful' by delighted fans

Professionals Johannes Radebe and Graziano Di Prima wowed Strictly Come Dancing viewers

03 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

Strictly's first ever same-sex wowed viewers this evening, who branded it "stunningly beautiful".

Professionals Johannes Radebe and Graziano Di Prima were performing to Emeli Sande's new single Shine.

And fans flocked to Twitter to gush over the dance.

One tweeted: "SAME ❤️SEX COUPLES ON STRICTLY FINALLY #Strictly @bbcstrictly #yaaaaaas."

While another commented: "Same sex dancing on strictly?! Bloody loving it! #Strictly."

A third remarked: "Graziano and Johannes. I have no words. This is just too much for me and beautiful. I can actually feel tears in my eyes. #Strictly."

"Yaaasss @jojo_radebe @GrazianoDiPrima #Strictly," another wrote.

One commented: "Two boys dancing together on strictly, finally."

Another remarked: "Johannes and Graziano are dancing and I’m so here for it. #Strictly #StrictlyComeDancing."
