McGettigan’s Souk Madinat launching musical bingo night

Musical bingo, unlimited chicken wings and prizes to be won

03 Nov 2019, 16:16 by Reema Rahman

Bonkers for bingo? Crazy for chicken wings?

Well, if you’re looking for something different to do in Dubai that involves both of your favourite things, this brand-new deal will have you in flap.

McGettigan’s in Souk Madinat Jumeirah is launching a musical bingo night that comes with three hours of all-you-can-eat chicken wings.

Kicking off tomorrow (Monday November 4), Bingo Wings will include two rounds of musical bingo, unlimited chicken wings and two house drinks for Dhs100 a head.

Prepare to sing along as the DJ plays snippets of popular songs, all while tucking into bottomless bowls of chicken wings and shouting ‘bingo’ in between mouthfuls.

It promises to be heaps of fun with loads of prizes to be won.
