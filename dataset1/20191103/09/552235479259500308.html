Concerns growing for missing teenager who vanished in Fort William

A search for 17-year-old Jason Jeffrey has been launched after he was last seen around 1:30pm on Saturday November 2.

03 Nov 2019, 09:43 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

A search has been launched after a teenager went missing in Fort William.

Jason Jeffrey, 17, was last seen leaving an address on Cameron Road around 1:30pm on Saturday November 2.

Concerns are growing for his welfare.

Police are now appealing for information from anyone who may be able to help trace him.

Jason is described as white, 5ft 8ins tall, of slim build, with brown medium length hair with a long fringe.

When last seen Jason was wearing a black baseball cap, black waterproof jacket and grey jogging bottoms.

Anyone who may have seen Jason since this time, or who has any information on his whereabouts, is urged to contact Police Scotland via 101 quoting incident number 3169 of 2nd November.
