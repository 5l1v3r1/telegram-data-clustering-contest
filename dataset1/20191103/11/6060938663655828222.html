Disha Patani to have a short role in Salman Khan’s 'Radhe'?

03 Nov 2019, 11:44 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan made a grand announcement on Friday as he revealed the cast of ‘Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai’, posting a glimpse from the film’s muhurat. Disha Patani, who had romanced Salman in ‘Bharat’, returns to playing his leading lady in this cop drama. Prabhdeva will be calling the shots for this action flick which will feature Randeep Hooda as the villain.
