Downs Remain Top, Bucs Sink Stellies

03 Nov 2019, 15:23 by Soccer Laduma

Orlando Pirates 2-0 Stellenbosch FC

Orlando Pirates got back to winning ways with a 2-0 victory over Stellenbosch FC on Saturday afternoon at Rand Stadium.

Lesiba Mahlong and Sifiso Luthuli scored in either half to propel Pirates up to fourth on the table.

SuperSport United 0-2 Highlands Park

SuperSport United lost their second match in a row after going down 2-0 to Highlands Park on Saturday morning at Lucas Moripe Stadium.

Camren Ashby and Lucky Mdingi netted for the Lions of the North.

Kaizer Chiefs 1-1 Baroka FC

Kaizer Chiefs dropped points for the second successive match after being held to a 1-1 draw by Baroka FC.

Bakgaga broke the deadlock in the 24th minute but Amakhosi levelled matters six minutes later from the penalty spot.

Mamelodi Sundowns 1-0 Maritzburg United

Mamelodi Sundowns maintained their position at the top of the table after edging Maritzburg United 1-0 on Sunday morning at Lucas Moripe Stadium.

The Brazilians broke the deadlock with eight minutes remaining of the first stanza and the goal was enough to hand them the three points.
