Pokemon Go two-day celebration features encounters from past Community Day events

20 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Stephany Nunneley

December’s Pokemon Go Community Day will span two days and feature Pokemon from previous events.

Pokemon from past Community Day events will appear more frequently in Pokemon Go this December.

To celebrate a year’s worth Community Days, Niantic is hosting a special two-day event featuring encounters and bonuses.

The two-day event will run from Saturday, December 14 to Sunday, December 15.

The details are below.

Community Day Schedule: Saturday, December 14 

From 9am – 9pm local – enjoy the following benefits:

  • Pokemon appearing more frequently in the wild: Totodile, Swinub, Treecko, Torchic, Mudkip, Ralts, Slakoth, Trapinch, Bagon, Turtwig, and Chimchar
  • Pokemon appearing in raids: Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle, Pikachu, Eevee, Dratini, Chikorita, Cyndaquil, Mareep, Larvitar, and Beldum
  • Pokemon available to hatch from Eggs: Bulbasaur, Charmander, Pikachu, Dratini, Totodile, Mareep, Swinub, Larvitar, Treecko, Torchic, Slakoth, and Bagon

From 11am – 2pm local time, experience the following bonuses and increased encounters:

  • Pokemon appearing even more frequently in the wild: Totodile, Swinub, Treecko, Torchic, Slakoth, and Bagon
  • Bonuses: 2× Catch Stardust, 2× Catch XP, and 1/2 Egg Hatch Distance when Eggs are placed into Incubators during these hours

Community Day Schedule: Sunday, December 15 

From 9am – 9pm local time enjoy the following benefits:

  • Pokemon appearing more frequently in the wild: Totodile, Swinub, Treecko, Torchic, Mudkip, Ralts, Slakoth, Trapinch, Bagon, Turtwig, and Chimchar
  • Pokemon appearing in raids: Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle, Pikachu, Eevee, Dratini, Chikorita, Cyndaquil, Mareep, Larvitar, and Beldum
  • Pokemon available to hatch from Eggs: Squirtle, Eevee, Chikorita, Cyndaquil, Mudkip, Ralts, Trapinch, Beldum, Turtwig, and Chimchar

From 11am – 2m local time, experience the following bonuses and increased encounters:

  • Pokémon appearing even more frequently in the wild: Mudkip, Ralts, Trapinch, Turtwig, and Chimchar
  • Bonuses: 2× Catch Stardust, 2× Catch XP, and 1/2 Egg Hatch Distance when Eggs are placed into Incubators during these hours

Community Day Schedule: Both December 14 and December 15 

From 9am Saturday to 9pm on Sunday local, the following bonus will be in effect:

  • Evolve for Community Day exclusive moves: Exclusive moves featured during 2018 and 2019 Community Day events will be available again.
