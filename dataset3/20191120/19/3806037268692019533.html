Nike's Epic React Flyknit 2 Gets a Galactic Midsole Design

And pops of “Sequoia Green.”

20 Nov 2019, 19:41 by Nia Groce

Following behind a Cortez-inspired colorway and renditions that pay homage to popular soccer clubs, Nike has now given the Epic React Flyknit 2 a galactic refresh.

The latest version of the sleek running shoe gets jolted with pops of “Sequoia Green,” while the Milky Way-inspired midsole evokes the star-filled solar system. Black and navy blue threads mesh together along the upper to create a shadowy aesthetic, allowing the neon green heel design, lace tips and insole to brighten up the model. Other details like a blue pull tab and lace keeper continue the dark vibes, while tonal logos round it all off.

The Epic React Flyknit 2 SE “Black/Sequoia Green/Mountain White/Black” is priced at ¥1,299 CNY (approximately $185 USD) and available now at Nike China’s webstore.

For more footwear from the brand, check out the new “Baroque Brown” colorway of its VaporMax Plus.
