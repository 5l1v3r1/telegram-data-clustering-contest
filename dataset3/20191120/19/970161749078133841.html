Celebs Go Dating line up revealed as unlucky-in-love stars join the agency

20 Nov 2019, 19:49

Celebs Go Dating is back for a new series as more single celebs look for love.

The line-up for 2020 already looks like it’s going to be great and we can’t wait to see it.

Stars include Love Island’s Amy Hart – and we all know how things went for her during her appearance on the ITV show, when she got left by Curtis Pritchard who then went on to try his luck with Maura Higgins.

She was also linked to DJ Toby Anstis, 48, but the reality star denied any romances happening.

‘I’ve joined the Agency because I’m the epitome of a dating disaster,’ Amy said in the teaser clip.

Other stars include TOWIE’s Amy Childs and James Lock, Hollyoaks’ Malique Thompson-Dwyer, EastEnders’ Dean Gaffney, and the one and only Liv Bentley from Made in Chelsea.
