Samsung is expected to launch another Galaxy S10 device before the end of 2019. The new device will come under the Galaxy S10 Lite moniker but don’t let the “Lite” on its name confuse you. The device will carry the Snapdragon 855, the same flagship SoC, that is inside the other S10 handsets. While the device’s launch is unknown, details about the cameras have been leaked.

The leak was posted yesterday on Twitter as a courtesy of Ishan Agarwal (@Ishanagarwal24). According to him, the Galaxy S10 Lite will feature a 48MP main sensor that comes paired with a 12MP ultrawide angle camera and a 5MP depth sensor. This camera is most likely the ISOCELL GM2 that was announced back in May of this year. According to the leak, the device will carry the SM-G770F serial.
