Fire guts Surelere Plaza in Lagos

20 Nov 2019, 19:58

- The popular Surelere Plaza in Lagos state has been gutted by fire

- Emerging reports claim fire service operatives are already on ground working to put out the fire

- This is coming barely five days after a building near Tejuosho market in the Yaba area of Lagos was also destroyed fire

Traders in Surelere Plaza were hit with yet another tragedy as the shopping complex was reportedly gutted by fire in the evening of Wednesday, November 20.

Footage of the fire incident was shared on Twitter by Shekpe, tweeting at @mayeecoal. In the 29-second video clip, motorists and passersby are seen scampering for safety as the fire ravages the plaza.
