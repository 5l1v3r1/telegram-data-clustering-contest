Counter for Sabarimala pilgrims at Kochi airport

20 Nov 2019, 19:18 by Special Correspondent

The Travancore Devaswom Board has started a special counter at Cochin International Airport Ltd. to help Sabarimala pilgrims. N. Vasu, Devaswom Board president, inaugurated it on Wednesday.

The counter is functioning at the arrival zone of the domestic terminal. Dhanalakshmi Bank is also functioning in the airport for the Devaswom Board. Pilgrims can get the coupons for Sabarimala offerings from the bank counter.
