Update: NoCap Released from Jail

20 Nov 2019, 19:45 by VladTV

Update 11/20/2019 7:39pm:

Alabama rapper NoCap has been released from jail after turning himself into police in connection with a shooting that took place in September. NoCap confirmed the news on his IG by reposting a headline of his release. 

Update 10/06/2019 3:16pm:

Previously, NoCap dropped a freestyle from jail after turning himself for allegedly having a part in a shooting that went down in Mobile, Alabama. The rapper allegedly fired shots in an occupied house. Now, it has been revealed that the rapper's court date for the incident is scheduled to take place on October 30, 2019. 

source: SayCheese

Original 09/28/2019 5:16pm:

Previously, rapper NoCap was wanted for shooting into an occupied dwelling as well as reckless endangerment. The rapper allegedly played a role in a shooting that took place in Mobile, Alabama on September 8. Since then, the rapper turned himself into authorities after being wanted by the police.
