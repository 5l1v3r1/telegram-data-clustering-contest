Another employee dies

Jafar was conductor in Medak depot

20 Nov 2019, 19:14 by Staff Reporter

A 32-year-old TSRTC employee who suffered brain stroke, died at a private hospital in Hyderabad on Wednesday.

The employee, Sheik Jafar, used to work as a conductor with the corporation at Medak depot.

His family member, Hussain, said that Jafar was worried whether he would get back the job and that anxiety led to brain stroke on November 13.

He was initially taken to the Telangana State Road Transport Corporation’s (TSRTC) Hospital in Tarnaka, from where he was referred to Sai Sajeevani Hospital in Saroornagar.

Jafar is survived by wife Majeeunnisa, a son and daughter. She is expecting their third child.

Jafar’s body was brought to Salabatpur in Kowdipally mandal for performing last rites.

Meanwhile, an office-bearer of the National Mazdoor Union (NMU), E Ashok, requested the State government to give government jobs to family members of RTC employees who died.

A total of 27 people have died since the strike was launched on October 5.

Mr. Ashok further requested the government to issue ₹1,000 crore for payment of pending salaries of employees. A few employees have retired in the past two months. Full and final settlement was requested to be processed.
