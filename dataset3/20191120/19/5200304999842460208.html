Priyanka beats SRK to become most searched Indian celebrity online

20 Nov 2019, 19:48 by Jeetesh Luhar

Priyanka Chopra Jonas has beaten the likes of Shah Rukh Khan and Salman Khan to become the most searched Indian celebrity online.

According to a US based-survey, people searched a lot about Priyanka between October 2018 and 2019, reports Bollywood Life. The survey, conducted by a US-based company called SEMrush, mentioned that the monthly search volume of the star was 4.2 million compared to Salman’s 2 million.

When ‘The Sky Is Pink’ (2019) star got married to Nick Jonas in December 2018, she was searched for nearly 13.6 million times. And on the male front, Shah Rukh and the late Amrish Puri took the second and third spots after Salman.

On the female front, Deepika Padukone and Sunny Leone managed to take the second and third position. Padukone’s search volume was about 3 million while Leone was searched for about 1.7 million times. Katrina Kaif, Ranveer Singh, Imran Khan, Amitabh Bachchan and Alia Bhatt also made the list. However, their search volumes were significantly less compared to the top positions.

Before becoming the most searched Indian celebrity online, Priyanka had also been named in Time Magazine’s 100 Most Influential People in the World, Variety Magazine’s Power Of Women – LA impact list, People Magazine’s 25 Women Changing The World, USA Today’s 50 Most Powerful Women in Entertainment among many more.

On the work front, she was last seen in ‘The Sky Is Pink’ with Farhan Akhtar, Zaira Wasim and Rohit Saraf. She will be seen next in ‘The White Tiger’ with Rajkummar Rao.
