PUN Vs JHA Dream11 : Second encounter of Wednesday sees Punjab and Jharkhand go at loggerheads as either open up their Super League sojourn. 

Group C and Group E second placed sides face off against each other today in the second match of the Super League of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy as either strive to maraud their way into the crucial junctures of the marquee tournamnet in aplomb. When Punjab and Jharkhand meet each other in the second clash of the day today, this is a match which has imbued in place all the ingredients of being a cracker of an encounter, a match which is bound to set he league alight.

Finishing behind Delhi in Group E, Jharkhand were unfortunate to not occupy a place in the pinnacles of the group despite finishing level on points with the side from the capital. Net run-rate played a decisive factor but it’s not something which will massively deter Jharkhand though with the outfit garnering the five wins from seven matches.

Their most recent outing saw them cruise past Gujarat in their culminating group stage encounter as the side thumped their opponents to win by 9 wickets. Stepping out in pursuit of 129, Jharkhand’s top-order was not once perturbed by the lowly target a hand as they complimented an articulate bowling effort to perfection to cruise to a win with 9 wickets to spare.

Elsewhere, things are not quite so solace filled for Punjab with the side faltering at the wrong time to go on a downward spiral right before entering this component of the tournamnet. From challenging for a spot in the echelons of the group to regressing with two defeats in three matches, the side were trounced by 45 runs in their final match as they lost their way chasing the 202 dished out infront of them by Maharashtra.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

With this being the second match of the day contested on a track which already has little in it for the bats, it is clear that the side winning the toss is undoubtedly going to come out and bat first. The wicket is going to take no time in opening up and playing fully to the capabilities of the bowlers who are ready to take pace off the ball with the spinners thriving enormously over here.
