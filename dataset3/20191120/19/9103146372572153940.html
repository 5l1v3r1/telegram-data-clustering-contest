Sports in Pictures – Wednesday, November 20

20 Nov 2019, 19:47 by Admin

Every day we bring you a selection of photo highlights from the world of sport and here is what we have for today

Kiwanuka signs up for MTN Marathon

Heavyweight boxer Shafik Kiwanuka on Wednesday received his MTN Marathon kits for the run that is set for Sunday. Kiwanuka will use the marathon to maintain his endurance ahead of his title fight against Zimbabwe's Thamsanqa Dube on November 29.

 

 

 

URA frustrates KCCA

URA's quest to cut the gap to league leaders Vipers ran into headwinds on Wednesday as they were held at home by plucky URA FC. The Tax Collectors took the lead through Ashraf Mandela in the 8th minute before Alan Okello restored parity in the 57th minutes. The draw leaves KCCA 11 points off the top of the table with four games in hand.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Kickboxing talent search launched

Kickboxer Moses Golola was on hand to launch the Unaweza Extra Kickboxing Talent Search & Exhibition fight which will be held on November 29 at Rock Gardens in Kampala.

 

 

 

Four more picked for Abu Dhabi F1 race

Four more winners from the City Tyres and Pirelli Formula One promotion draw were drawn to join James Bond Kunobere, Musa Osman Rukundo, George Mutebe and Simon Ssekankya for the all-expense paid trip.

Like has been the trend, the winners will enjoy VIP treatment from the beginning to the end. From tickets in the VIP lounge at the Yas Marina and staying at the Grand Hyatt Hotel over the four days and five night’s trip, the winners will have a memorable trip that they will wind up with a desert challenge experience.
