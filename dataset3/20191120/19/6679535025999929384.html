98% aspirants clear D.El.Ed. test

20 Nov 2019, 19:22 by Special Correspondent

Of the total 38,443 candidates who appeared for the D.El.Ed. (Diploma in Elementary Education 2017-19) 35,649 of them have passed, recording a pass percentage of 97.73 %. The examinations were held from September 27 to October 4.

A statement issued by the Director of Government Examinations on Wednesday said the dummy memorandum of marks were hosted on the website www.bseap.org for information of the candidates.

Candidates intending to apply for recounting of marks should submit their applications along with fee challan directly to this office by December 5, indicating the subject for which recounting is required.
