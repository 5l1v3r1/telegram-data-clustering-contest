Hoping for a fifth win

20 Nov 2019, 19:38 by Staff Reporter

They are keeping their fingers crossed. Six students from Holy Angels HSS, Vanchiyoor, led by Parvathy S.R., topped the Thayambaka contest at the Revenue District School Arts Festival on Wednesday, booking a place at the State School Arts Festival.

The four-time State-level winners are hoping for yet another win.

Parvathy who has been learning Thayambaka under Kalamandalam Krishnadas for the past eight years is the mainstay of the team. Training is also arranged for the students ahead of the festivals.

The team is also taking part in the Melam contest at the district event.
