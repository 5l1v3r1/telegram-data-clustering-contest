Colts Issue Injury Update For WR T.Y. Hilton

20 Nov 2019, 19:28 by Chris Rosvoglou

The Colts might receive a much-needed boost on Thursday night when they face the Texans. Despite missing the past three games with a calf injury, Pro Bowl wideout T.Y. Hilton is just about ready to return.

Indianapolis went 1-2 during Hilton’s absence. The offense struggled without its best wide receiver against the Pittsburgh Steelers and Miami Dolphins.

With the AFC South up for grabs, the Colts are ready to welcome Hilton back to the starting lineup. Even though he’s not guaranteed to play on Thursday, his status is trending in the right direction.

According to NFL Network insider Ian Rapoport, the Colts listed Hilton as a full participant for today’s practice.

Hilton is officially questionable to play tomorrow night.

Eric Ebron is also listed as questionable for Indianapolis in Week 12.
