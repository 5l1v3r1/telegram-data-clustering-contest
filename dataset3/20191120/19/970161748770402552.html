What are the net worths of the Kardashian and Jenner families?

20 Nov 2019, 19:00

Caitlyn Jenner has entered the I’m A Celebrity jungle this week, with reports alleging that she’s charging £500,000 for her stint on the reality TV show.

This proved the butt of one of Ant and Dec’s jokes during the show, with Dec saying: ‘We’ve got reality TV royalty Caitlyn Jenner. Most people come into the jungle to lose a few pounds…’ before Ant added: ‘But she’s going to be gaining 500,000 of them according to the papers.’

With that impressive sum, and the fact that Caitlyn and the rest of the Kardashian/Jenner family are pop-culture titans, here’s a quick look at what the family are worth.

What is Caitlyn Jenner’s net worth?

Caitlyn Jenner, who was a gold-medal-winning Olympic athlete before she married Kris Jenner in 1991, is worth $100 million (approximately £77 million) according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Her fortune was no-doubt been impacted by the fact that, in 2007, she began appearing (as Bruce Jenner) in hit reality show Keeping Up with the Kardashians.

The Kardashian family’s net worth

Caitlyn’s ex-wife Kris Kardashian, aka The Momager, is worth $90 million (approx. £70 million) according to the site.

Kris was Caitlyn’s third wife, and they married before Caitlyn’s transition, which was announced publically in 2015. The two divorced that same year.

When Caitlyn married Kris, she gained four future-celebrity step-children in the forms of Kim Kardashian West, who’s worth $350 million (approx. £271 million), Khloe Kardashian, who’s worth $50 million (approx. £39 million), Kourtney Kardashian, who’s worth $45 million (approx. £35 million), and Rob Kardashian, who’s worth $10 million (approx. £8 million)

Caitlyn’s daughters Kendall and Kylie, who were born after she married Kris, are worth $40 million (approx £31 million) and $1 billion (approx £774 million) respectively.

Kendall regularly works as a fashion model, and Kylie sold the majority of her share of her cosmetics company this month for $600 million (approx. £450 million).
