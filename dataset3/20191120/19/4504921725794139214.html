NamPower calls for calm

20 Nov 2019, 19:10

NamPower says the nation needs not to panic, as the company has the capacity to deal with power outage situations around the clock.
This follows last night's power outage experienced throughout many central and coastal areas, as well as most parts of the northern regions,
The power utility says the outage was caused by a technical fault on the transformer at Kokerboom-Aries's 400 kV lines in the south.
