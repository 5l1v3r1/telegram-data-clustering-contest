Santa Sleigh Bus hits road

20 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Sarita Patel

The 17th annual Santa Sleigh Bus will be hitting the road soon in Kelowna.

The bus is full of holiday spirit and is decorated by a team at Kelowna Transit. It will travel around the Central Okanagan from Dec. 8 to 14, collecting donations for those less fortunate over the Christmas season.

Collected food, toys and cash donations will be donated to the Salvation Army for its annual Christmas hamper program.

The Santa Sleigh Bus will start its route at Prospera Place in downtown Kelowna and make its way through the Central Okanagan before wrapping up at Stuart Park skating rink on Saturday.
