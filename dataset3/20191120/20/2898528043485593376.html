Sara McMann to fight Lina Lansberg at UFC Raleigh

20 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Alexander K. Lee

Sara McMann will be hunting for her first win in almost two years when she competes in North Carolina.

The one-time bantamweight title challenger has been booked to fight Lina Lansberg at UFC Raleigh on Jan. 25, officials announced Wednesday.

This will be McMannâ€™s first fight since February 2018. She is coming off of back-to-back submission losses to Marion Reneau and Ketlen Vieira. Prior to that, McMann (11-5) had won three straight, though she has not had her hand raised since defeating Gina Mazany at a Fight Night event in Halifax, Nova Scotia, on Feb, 19, 2017.

The 39-year-old was scheduled to fight Nicco Montano in July, but was forced to withdraw due to injury.

Lansberg (10-4) is on her first UFC win streak after alternating wins and losses in her first five Octagon appearances. The 37-year-old has picked up consecutive victories over Macy Chiasson and former Invicta FC champion Tonya Evinger.

UFC Raleigh takes place at PNC Arena in Raleigh, N.C. and will be headlined by a heavyweight bout between Curtis Blaydes and former UFC champion Junior dos Santos. The main card will air on the ESPN+ streaming service.
