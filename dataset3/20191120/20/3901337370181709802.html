10 Great Goalscoring Midfielders

20 Nov 2019, 20:01 by SL International

Liverpool and Netherlands midfielder Georginio Wijnaldum scored his first international hat-trick on Tuesday night to continue his incredible goalscoring form. Here are 10 great goalscoring midfielders who dominated the game.  

Click through the gallery above for images of 10 great goalscoring midfielders.

Wijnaldum has now scored eight goals in nine appearances for Netherlands in 2019, and 18 in total, to go along with 107 goals he has scored at club level for the likes of Feyenoord, PSV Eindhoven, Newcastle United and Liverpool.

When looking at the best goalscoring midfielders in the world today, however, one need look no further than Sporting Lisbon’s Bruno Fernandes. The 25-year-old has scored 75 goals in 276 club appearances, including an incredible haul of 39 since the start of last season.

Slovakia midfielder Marek Hamsik is Napoli’s all-time leading goalscorer with 121 goals, and has added a further 16 club goals for the likes of Slovan Bratislavia, Brescia and current club Dalian Yifang.

England’s former midfield trio of Steven Gerrard, Frank Lampard and Paul Scholes also rank among the greatest goalscoring midfielders to have graced the game and scored more than 600 goals for club and country between them.
