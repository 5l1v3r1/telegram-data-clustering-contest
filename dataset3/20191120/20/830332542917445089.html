2 cars stolen from Watertown street, police say

20 Nov 2019, 20:49 by Stephen Cohn

WATERTOWN, Wis. - Police are investigating two stolen car complaints in Watertown on Wednesday morning. 

According to a news release, both cars were reported stolen from the 500 block of Grey Fox Run. 

Officials said one car was found south of Watertown, while the other was involved in a police chase and last seen in Dane County. 

In the release, police reminded residents to lock their cars and not leave them in their cars. 

Anyone with information on the robberies is asking to call Watertown police at 920-261-660 or report it anonymously by texting "WTTN" and their tip to 847411.
