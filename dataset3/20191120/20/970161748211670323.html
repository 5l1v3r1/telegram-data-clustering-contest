Starving dog chewed off her own leg after being chained in yard with no food

20 Nov 2019, 20:55

A dog was so hungry she chewed off her own leg after being left chained in a garden without food.

The animal, called Zoe, was spotted by a UPS driver in Exeter, New York last week, who contacted police with his concerns.

Officers from the Susquehanna Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals responded, and deemed Zoe’s injuries so serious that a vet was called.

Zoe was rushed to the clinic, where a surgical procedure found bone fragments in her stomach believed to have come from her own leg.

The dog was kept in her spot by chain attached to a stake driven into the ground.

She is believed to have been living in a pet carrier with hay in the bottom of it.

Police did not see any food in Zoe’s vicinity, and say a nearby bowl was frozen and covered in snow.

The dog was taken to Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine for more care, WIVB reported.

She was found to have other injuries including scarred ear tips.

Her owner Carl Pritchard has been charged with overdriving, torturing and injuring animals.

He faces further charges of failure to provide proper sustenance and failure to provide appropriate shelter for a dog left outdoors.

Pritchard, 59, has since surrendered ownership of Zoe and will appear in court on December 4.

The dog is set to be put up for adoption once she has recovered from her ordeal.
