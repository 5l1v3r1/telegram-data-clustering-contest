Kristen Stewart: Charlie’s Angels reboot not trying to make overt statement

She stars in the new film alongside Naomi Scott and Ella Balinska.

20 Nov 2019, 20:45

Kristen Stewart has said the reboot of Charlie’s Angels in which she stars is not “trying overtly to make any statements”.

The Twilight star appears opposite British actresses Naomi Scott and Ella Balinska as the next generation of angels working for the mysterious Charles Townsend in the latest big-screen reboot of the classic 1970s TV show.

The film, which makes reference to ways women can be overlooked and underestimated and are not perceived as threats, has been directed by Elizabeth Banks, who also wrote the script and stars as Bosley.
