20 Nov 2019, 20:55 by Simon Asare, GNA

Accra, Nov. 20, GNA - KF Trust Point Group of Companies, one of Ghana's leading producers of beverages and quality plastic products has unveiled Kwadwo Nkansah Lilwin as their new brand ambassador.

The popular Ghanaian actor cum musician on Tuesday signed a one-year brand ambassadorial deal with SmartUp Energy and Apple Drink, a subsidiary of KF Trust Point Group of Companies.
