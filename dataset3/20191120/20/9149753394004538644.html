Teamfight Tactics 9.23 patch notes: the return of Ranked Queue

20 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Steven Messner

Teamfight Tactics patch 9.23 brings a grip of additions to the game, including the return of the Ranked Queue. There are also new balancing changes that empower Tier 2 and Tier 3 champions while pulling back on the power of Tier 1s.
