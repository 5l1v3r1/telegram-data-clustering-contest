Borderlands 3 Moxxi’s Heist of The Handsome Jackpot DLC releases next month

20 Nov 2019, 20:18 by Aran Suddi

The first expansion for Borderlands 3 has been announced by Gearbox, and it is called Moxxi’s Heist of the Handsome Jackpot. As you may guess from the title Moxxi and her crew are looking to grab some riches. In this story, players will be heading to The Handsome Jackpot, a now derelict casino space station. Derelict doesn’t mean deserted though as the space station is populated by armed Loader Bots and looters than have been stuck on the station since Handsome Jack died.
