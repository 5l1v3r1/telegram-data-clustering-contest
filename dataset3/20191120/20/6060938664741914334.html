Photos: Shahid Kapoor meets Vishal Bhardwaj at his office; we wonder if 'Kaminey 2' is on the cards

20 Nov 2019, 20:54 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shahid Kapoor is currently gearing up for his upcoming sports drama 'Jersey'. He will be playing the role of a cricketer in the film. The actor is putting all his efforts to get into the skin of the character.

Today, Shahid visited filmmaker Vishal Bhardwaj at his office. For the occasion, the actor opted for a grey sweatshirt paired with black shorts and white shoes. The director was in a black and white attire. They were all smiles as the paparazzi clicked their pictures.
