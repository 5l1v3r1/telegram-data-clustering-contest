Photos: Gym buddies Varun Dhawan and Arjun Kapoor get snapped post their workout session!

20 Nov 2019, 20:08 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood's love for fitness is not hidden from anyone. From Ranveer Singh to Ananya Panday everyone hit the gym to stay fit and maintain their tone physique. Varun Dhawan and Arjun Kapoor are no different from others.

And today, it seems the duo worked out together at the gym. The actors were snapped while leaving the venue. Varun was in a white t-shirt with a black jacket over it and track pants while Arjun was in an all-black gymwear. They were all smiles as the paparazzi clicked their pictures.
