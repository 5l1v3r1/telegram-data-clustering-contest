Who is Roman Kemp's girlfriend Anne-Sophie Flury?

20 Nov 2019, 20:00

Roman Kemp has been settling in to life in the I’m A Celebrity jungle, getting off to a good start after being one of just four celebs to win a place in the main camp straight away.

The Capital FM DJ has been opening up to his fellow campmates in recent days, talking about his famous parents, having George Michael as his godfather, and how his dad Martin battled back after being diagnosed with brain tumours in the 90s.

While Roman seems to have hit it off with the others, one person back home who’ll be supporting him from afar is his girlfriend Anne-Sophie Flury.

Here’s what you need to know about her…

Who is Roman Kemp’s girlfriend Anne-Sophie Flury and how long have they been dating?

Anne-Sophie Flury is from Bern in Switzerland and is reportedly a neuroscientist.

She recently created a product called Shu Gum, which can be used to eliminate odours and clean shoes.

The pair have been together for two years and Anne-Sophie has admitted how much she’ll miss him while he’s gone.

‘I’m so excited but I miss him so much!’ she told the Mirror. ‘It’s really difficult not being able to speak to him.

‘It’ll be easier when the show starts so I can watch what he’s up to.’

She also predicted that Roman would hit it off with Caitlyn Jenner (despite the reality star not actually knowing who he is).

‘I’m a huge fan of the Kardashians,’ she explained, ‘so it’s nice to have a different version of the show featuring my boyfriend!’

What has Roman said about his girlfriend?

Ahead of his trip down under, Roman told OK! ‘She’ll probably love a break away from me! But she’s so supportive. She lived in Hong Kong for eight months during our relationship so we’re used to long distance.’

He added: ‘My parents have instilled the value of respect and loyalty in relationships into me. I’ve seen the best husband in my dad and you can’t build something similar if you go on TV and flirt with someone else.’
