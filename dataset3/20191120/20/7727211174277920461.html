Labour Party use Liverpool boss Jurgen Klopp video clip to encourage voters to register to vote in General Election

The Liverpool boss has not expressed an opinion on the forthcoming General Election

20 Nov 2019, 20:52 by Dan Kay

Liverpool boss Jurgen Klopp features in a Labour Party tweet encouraging people to register in time to vote in next month's General Election.

Britain goes to the polls on Thursday 12th December but those eligible to vote must register by 11:59pm on Tuesday 26th November.

Labour this evening tweeted a reminder that there are only six days to go until registration closes and used a video clip which went viral in the aftermath of the Reds' Champions League final win over Spurs in June.

It shows the German-born Reds manager counting on his fingers the number of times Liverpool have lifted Europe's premier cup competition, which is six following the club's successes in 1977, 1978, 1981, 1984, 2005 and 2019.
