Machine Gun Kelly Pays Touching Tribute To Late Rapper Lil Peep

20 Nov 2019, 20:00 by AllHipHop Staff

MGK commemorated the two-year anniversary of the passing of Lil Peep, who died of a drug overdose in November of 2017.

(AllHipHop News) Machine Gun Kelly marked the two-year anniversary of Lil Peep's death with an emotional performance of his track, "Walk Away As The Door Slams."

The hip-hop star, real name Gustav Ahr, died of an accidental overdose at the age of 21 in November 2017.

Last Friday, Peep's estate commemorated the late artist by dropping the "Everybody's Everything" documentary and album, which contained an acoustic version of the 2016 tune.

And sharing a clip on Instagram MGK, real name Colson Baker honored the star with his own cover of the song.

"I just wanted to help now im goin to hell walk away as the door slams... @lilpeep thnx for taking this video @rookxx (sic)," the star captioned the video of him playing a black guitar and singing along to Peep's vocals on what looks to be the back of his tour bus.

The rapper went on to empathize with Peep's addiction issues and said he wished he could have talked to him prior to his passing.
