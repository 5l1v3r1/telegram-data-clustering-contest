Browns Player Reacts To Baker Mayfield’s Comments On Myles Garrett

20 Nov 2019, 20:26 by Chris Rosvoglou

The biggest story from last week’s showdown between the Cleveland Browns and Pittsburgh Steelers had nothing to do with football. Almost every sports fan spent the weekend talking about Myles Garrett and Mason Rudolph’s fight that took place in the final seconds.

Garrett’s actions on Thursday night put the Cleveland Browns in a tough position, but second-year quarterback Baker Mayfield tried to handle the situation with class.

When asked about the brawl between Garrett and Rudolph, Mayfield was brutally honest about it. 

“That’s inexcusable, he knows that. I don’t care rivalry or not, we can’t do that. The reality is he’s going to get suspended,” Mayfield said.

There were a few critics that didn’t like that Mayfield made those remarks. On the flip side, it appears his postgame comments sat well with his teammates.

Browns offensive guard Joel Bitonio praised Mayfield, saying he “handled it like a leader.”
