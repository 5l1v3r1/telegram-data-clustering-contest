New Spurs boss Jose Mourinho 'to earn double Mauricio Pochettino's salary'

Tottenham Hotspur have reportedly handed Jose Mourinho a salary which is worth double what Mauricio Pochettino earned at the north London outfit.

20 Nov 2019, 20:56 by Darren Plant

On Wednesday morning, Spurs confirmed that Mourinho had been brought in as the replacement for the long-serving Argentine.

The Portuguese has been given a three-and-a-half year contract by the Premier League side, who find themselves in 14th position in the Premier League standings.

According to the Daily Mail, the 56-year-old is earning the same wage packet which he received during his time at Manchester United, said to be £15m per year.

The report adds that Pochettino was receiving in the region of £7.5m each year before being sacked on Tuesday evening.

Spurs chairman Daniel Levy has allegedly had to pay £19m in compensation to the Argentine and his backroom staff.
