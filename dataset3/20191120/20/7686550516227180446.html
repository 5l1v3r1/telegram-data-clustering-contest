Adele pretended Jane McDonald was encouraging her during first eating bushtucker trial

The former Loose Women host has proved a proper inspiration so far this year

20 Nov 2019, 20:45 by Katie-Ann Gupwell

Jane McDonald appears to be the gift that keeps on giving in this year's I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here, as she somehow led radio DJ, Adele, to scoffing a camel's anus during the first eating bushtucker trial of the series.

The campmate, who has caused confusion with her luxury item this year (a framed picture of Jane McDonald), imagined the former Loose Women host was by her side to support her through the gruelling challenge.

Along with Roman, Adele took part in a trial called Just Desserts whereby the pair had to eat ten desserts on offer at the parlour.

Each star they earned was worth a meal for their camp.

The first dish was served to Roman, and was called Critter-Bocker Glory, which was made up of dead cockroaches, crickets and mealworms.

Roman had to eat three of each.

He started with the dead cockroaches saying that they were juicy and tasted a bit like eggs.

He then ate three mealworms, and finished the dish with three crickets which he described as being "quite cheesy, cheesy eggs" and earnt one star for camp.

Adele was then served Tirrama-spew, which was vomit fruit.

"It's basically like eating a piece of fruit" said Dec.

"Which tastes like vomit."

Adele gagged and retched and said: "It's like your dog's been sick and you have to eat it".

She swallowed and won her second star for camp.

Next up Roman had to eat Licky Toffee Pudding which was duck's tongues.

He had to eat three, and pretended they were calamari as he chewed and swallowed the tongues.

Then Adele had to take on the Profiter-balls which were turkey testicles.

She said that it was disgusting but Roman made her think of Christmas and they all ended up singing "The Most Wonderful Time Of The Year".

Roman was then served Mississippi Udder Pie which was a cow's teat, before Adele had Piggy Pudding which was a pig's uterus.

She chewed and said that it had popped.

Roman's next dessert was 'Eye-full' which were fish eyes.

"Don't use the word eyeball," said Roman as he swallowed making a total of seven stars.

Adele's was then asked to eat Chocolate Gat-Toe.

She made easy work of it which was a sheep's foot, taking their total to eight stars.

Roman's final dish was Cocky Road Ice Cream - or a bull's penis.

As the enormous penis was placed in front of him Ant asked if Roman could guess what animal it came from.

"James Haskell?" replied Roman.

"There's a dry bit in the middle" he complained as he chewed.

Then Jane McDonald saved the day once more as Adele came face-to-face with Creme Poo-Lee - otherwise known as a camel's anus.

She pretended Jane McDonald was there with her encouraging her and eventually swallowed the food.

The pair got a full house bagging a whole ten stars for camp.
