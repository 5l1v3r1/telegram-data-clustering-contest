Queen’s College: Alibaba blasts student and mother

20 Nov 2019, 20:13 by Sanya Agunbiade

Ace Nigerian comedian, Alibaba in his reaction to the viral video of the mother of a Queen’s College student fighting the security guards of the school for denying her daughter entrance into the school, called the woman a slay queen.

If you recall, a student of the school was in the news yesterday for fixing eye lashes to school.

She was reportedly denied entrance by the school security operatives, which made her mother lash out at one of them.

Alibaba in his reaction then wrote:

Alibaba wrote “happy Mother’s Day… slay queen… from queens’ college. Not Queens’ College ooo. Because there are rules. (Let me come use this video to weed out some senseless people from my timeline)”
