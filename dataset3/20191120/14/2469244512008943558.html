Devon residents wanted for Channel 5's The Nightmare Neighbour Next Door

Do you have problems with your neighbours?

20 Nov 2019, 14:46 by Jamie Hawkins

Devon residents are wanted to feature in a new series of Channel 5 show The Nightmare Neighbour Next Door.

They want to hear from people who have had a recent dispute with their neighbour.

From loud parties and anti-social behaviour to scary dogs, the programme shows what it's like to live next to terrible neighbours.

A spokesperson from Avalon said: "Are you having problems with your nightmare neighbours next door?

"Avalon Factual is producing a new series of the hit show The Nightmare Neighbour Next Door.

"We want to hear from people who have had a recent dispute with their neighbours.

"All information will be held in confidence and there is no pressure to take part in the programme.

"If you'd like to know more, please send an outline of your dispute and your contact details to neighbours@avalonuk or call 02075987392."
