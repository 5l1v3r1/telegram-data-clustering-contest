Pamela Anderson starts 'new chapter' as charges dropped against friend Assange

20 Nov 2019, 14:22

Pamela Anderson has announced her ‘new chapter’ just one day after charges against her friend Julian Assange were dropped.

The 52-year-old took to Instagram with a whole new lease of life following the news.

And to mark the occasion, the star shared a string of black and white glamorous photos onto her grid, because why the heck not?

In one, the mother-of-two can be seen in her iconic Baywatch attire as she held boxing gloves up to her face in front of a punch bag.

Writing in the caption, Pamela explained: ‘Back on it … body, mind, spirit.’

But while she worked up a sweat, the actress showcased a more bougie look, as she posed in front of a mirror in her lingerie.

‘Solitude is not lonely – An empty heart can be lonely while surrounded by people,’ the popular model told her 910,000 followers.

Pouting down the lens in another snap, Pamela made sure she announced her new chapter – and we’re excited for what’s to come.

She said: ‘I feel like I lost everything and gained everything all at once ..  #newchapter.’

Her pictures come after it was confirmed that charges against her friend Julian Assange had been dropped, amid the alleged rape investigation.

On Tuesday afternoon, Deputy Chief Prosecutor Eva-Marie Persson said the alleged victim’s complaint was credible.

However, she added: ‘My assessment is that all investigative measures that can be taken have been taken.

‘But… the evidence is not strong enough to file an indictment.’

Earlier on in the day, Pamela had cancelled her plans to visit the Wikileaks founder do to a ‘personal emergency’.

A statement from the Don’t Extradite Assange campaign said: ‘Pamela has a personal emergency which has meant she has had to go home and cancel everything for the next two weeks.’
