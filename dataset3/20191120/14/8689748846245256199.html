I Want A God Fearing Man Who Will Share Scriptures With Me On Daily Basis – YaaYaa Reveals

20 Nov 2019, 14:51 by vanessa nordzi

Every woman has a type of man she would date or marry and men equally have a type of women they’d like to date or marry. Some women want tall dark men, others want rich men and the list goes on s on. Ghanaian musician Yaa Yaa has  presented a checklist for the kind of man she would consider for a relationship/ marriage.

According to YaaYaa, her ideal man must be God-fearing and prayerful. She wants a man who will share scriptures with her on daily basis.

“Everyone thinks I have a boyfriend but I don’t. I want a God-fearing man who prays often and can share the scriptures with me,” YaaYaa told Andy Dosty on the Daybreak Hitz.

YaaYaa who was in the studio to promote her new song titled “E be money” , further explained that, when it comes to her ideal man, she doesn’t care about his complexion or height, as long as he is God- fearing and prayerful.

” My ideal man’s complexion or height doesn’t matter that much to me. He must be a Christian and prayerful.” She reiterated.
