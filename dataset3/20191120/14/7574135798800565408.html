Lizzo, DaBaby & more playing SNL in December

20 Nov 2019, 14:37 by Andrew Sacher

Saturday Night Live has revealed all the hosts and musical guests for the remaining episodes of 2019. This week (11/23) has musical guest King Princess and host Will Ferrell; and in December it’s musical guest DaBaby with host Jennifer Lopez on 12/7, musical guest Niall Horan (of One Direction) with host Scarlett Johansson on 12/14, and finally, musical guest Lizzo with host Eddie Murphy on the last episode of the year (12/21).

Lizzo and DaBaby both picked up Grammy nominations today. Jennifer Lopez will also be on TV when she performs at the Super Bowl Halftime Show.

Last week’s SNL had a different One Direction member, Harry Styles, as both host and musical guest. Harry is touring with Jenny Lewis in 2020, including three MSG shows.

Eddie Murphy recently revealed that he’d be starring in Beverly Hills Cop 4, which is the first Beverly Hills Cop in 25 years and coming to Netflix.
