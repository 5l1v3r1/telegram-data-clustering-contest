Alesha Dixon has shared a new photo of her baby daughter Anaya Safiya.

The Britain's Got Talent judge, 41, took to Instagram on Tuesday to post the new picture which showed the tot beaming while cuddling a white stuffed bunny.

Alesha, who gave birth to Anaya in August, captioned the post with three rabbit emojis.
