Co Galway punter wins over €6,000 from €7.50 horse racing bet

The anonymous punter placed the €0.25 each-way Lucky 15 bet in a BoyleSports shop

20 Nov 2019, 14:04 by Robert Hynes

A lucky Co Galway punter is over €6,000 richer today after seeing all four of his selections win on Tuesday.

The anonymous punter placed the €0.25 each-way Lucky 15 bet in a BoyleSports shop selecting three horses at Kempton and one at Fairyhouse, investing a total stake of €7.50.

The first winner came at Fairyhouse with The Chapel Field winning the 3.05 race at a handsome price of 10/1 to get the wager off to a flying start.

The customer then secured a fantastic treble across the pond at Kempton with All Yours (8/1), Pianissimo (20/1) and Ginger Fox (8/1) all obliging at healthy odds meaning the Galway punter could start celebrating.

When all the results where known, the client exchanged their €7.50 bet slip for the tasty amount of €6,677.26, a windfall that took just over an hour to win.

Sarah Kinsella, spokesperson for BoyleSports said: “Well done to our customer in Galway whose ambitious thinking with some big priced winners paid off in style and enabled them to walk away with €6,677.26 from a €7.50 stake.

"We wish them happy spending and hope they enjoy the festive season with their winnings.”
