Lerato Sengadi explains why HHP's grave does not have a tombstone

20 Nov 2019, 14:37

Nolusindiso Mgujulwa

Cape Town - It’s been a year since Motswako rapper Jabulani 'HHP' Tsambo died.

Earlier this week Jabba was trending on social media after two of the late rappers' friends shared a picture of his grave site. What caught fans attention the most was the non-existing tombstone on Jabba’s grave.

Tweeps even went as far as accusing HHP's wife Lerato of neglecting the late rappers grave.

The 37-year-old saw the tweets and responded – explaining that she doesn't need a crowd fund, she can afford to erect a tombstone for her husband.

"No need to crowd fund. I can afford to erect a Stone for my husband. If I was allowed to!!! Infact I would have had it done on the same day as his funeral. But Ke [sic]," she tweeted.
