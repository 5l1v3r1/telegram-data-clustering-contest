Youth held for morphing photos of girls on Instagram

20 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Special Correspondent

The Cyber crime police arrested a youngster for allegedly morphing the photos of young girls and posting them on the social media platform Instagram on Wednesday.

According to the police, the youth, who is studying engineering in a local college, used to collect the photos of the young girls through Instagram, morphed them with obscene pictures and used to create fake accounts on Instagram with those morphed photos. He also used to harass the girls to chat with him in an obscene manner. One of the victims lodged a complaint with the Matwada police as the accused posted an obscene picture in a group that belonged to the college.

Acting on the complaint, cybercrime police arrested the youth, who belonged to Dharmaram village in Warangal Rural district, on Wednesday. The police seized a cellphone, which was used for morphing the photos, from him. Police Commissioner V Ravinder appreciated the cybercrime wing Inspector Janardhan Reddy and Matwada Inspector Ganesh for arresting the accused within a short time.
