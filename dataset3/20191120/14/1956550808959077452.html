Global DJs Dimitri Vegas and Like Mike to perform in Dubai

FIVE Palm Jumeirah’s weekend-long LUVYA festival bringing top names this December

20 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Darragh Murphy

A huge three-day festival is coming to FIVE Palm Jumeirah Hotel, and it’s just revealed two of the biggest DJs duos in the world are coming to perform.

Global star DJs Dimitri Vegas and Like Mike are set to perform at the hotel’s LUVYA festival, and if it’s anything like their Tomorrowland show, partygoers of Dubai have a lot to look forward to.

Dimitri Vegas and Like Mike have iconic shows at the biggest festivals around the world, including Ibiza, Creamfields and Tomorrowland.

Set to perform on Friday December 13, the ‘Kings of Tomorrowland’ will be bringing their thumping beats to Beach by FIVE, an enormous main stage area being constructed on the hotel’s private, 150-metre beach.

They’re not the only performers set to hit the stage, as the Belgian duo will be joined by Lost Frequencies, Craig David, Eli & Fur, Disciples and Klingande. Quite the weekend we’re in for.

As for tickets, one-day pass prices for Thursday start at Dhs350 with three drinks, while Friday tickets are priced at Dhs550 with three drinks. Saturday tickets are at Dhs300, and come with three drinks and a food platter.

For those looking to party all weekend long, a weekend pass is available for Dhs1,000, and it comes with ten complimentary drinks. The choice is yours.

For more on which international star is playing when or to get yourself a ticket, be sure to visit www.luvya.live.
