Bills' Josh Allen named AFC Offensive Player of the Week

20 Nov 2019, 14:23

Bills quarterback Josh Allen has been named the AFC Offensive Player of the Week for his performance in the victory against the Miami Dolphins.

Allen threw for three touchdowns and ran for another and his passer rating of 117.7 was a career high. He was 21 of 33 passing for 256 yards and ran for 56 yards on seven carries.

It is the second time Allen has won the award; he also won in Week 17 of last season.

Allen is the first Bills players to win multiple offensive player of the week awards since Doug Flutie, who was honored in Week 9 of 1998 and Week 9 of 1999, according to the Bills.

With 20 total touchdowns, Allen ranks third in the AFC and sixth in the NFL.
