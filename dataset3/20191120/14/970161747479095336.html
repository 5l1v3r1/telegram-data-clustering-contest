Boy, 6, filmed driving 155mph Golf with no seatbelt as man eggs him on

20 Nov 2019, 14:16

This shocking video shows a six-year-old boy driving a sports car at breakneck speed while being egged on by a grown man in the passenger seat.

He can barely sit down on the seat as he takes a 155mph Golf Type R, which can reach 60mph in under five seconds, for several laps of a seemingly abandoned road.

At one point in the video the man tells the little boy to ‘show me what you got’ as he encourages him to drive faster through a narrow gate.

As the little boy turns the car around, the man asks him for the camera: ‘How old are you?’

And he replies: ‘Six.’

The boy drives up and down the potholed road at least four times as they test the sports car’s ‘launch control’.

This button turbocharges the engine in sport mode and is rarely used by most Golf drivers on the road, let alone six-year-old boys.

He is constantly being egged on by the man in the passenger seat who acts as his irresponsible instructor.

The man tells him: ‘Easy, whip her round, give it a little bit of power, go on, get up there.’

The viral footage has emerged on an Instagram page set up to show the little boy’s driving exploits.

Since the video was released, the page has posted a screengrab of a different video which claims to show the same child driving what appears to be a Land Rover when he was four-years-old.
