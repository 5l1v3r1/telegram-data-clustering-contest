Tips & Tricks For Naturally Big Plump Lips

20 Nov 2019, 14:28 by Constance Kalpakis

One of the biggest beauty trends is having huge lips. Yes, injections give you an instant desired effect, but can also be extremely costly. For instant temporary kissable voluptuous lips, fake it ’til you make it with these techniques and products that prevent you from needles, pain, and being a broke b*tch.

SkinMedica HA5 Smooth and Plump Lip System

This product is clinically proven to create a more plump appearance of lips while smoothing and hydrating. With a blend of five different hyaluronic acids and a natural molecule that can hold up to 1,000 times its weight in water, your lips are given an instant upgrade.

Burt’s Bees 100% Natural Conditioning Lip Scrub

A little goes a long way. This simple tip makes a huge difference in the appearance of your lips as it smooths the surface of your lips which allows more light to reflect, meaning an illusion of bigger and better is created. Apply every morning, or even use a toothbrush, as a way to buff your lips into perfection.

Grande Cosmetics GrandeLips Hydrating Lip Plumper Gloss

This Grande Cosmetics volumizing gloss lip treatment is formulated to give you instant and long-term hydrating benefits. It gives instant plump as well as improving your overall lip volume in just 3-5 minutes! It also features a unique cushion applicator easily wipes clean and makes it easy to use with other lips products. The best part: no stinging!

Hourglass Unreal High Shine Volumizing Lip Gloss

This gloss from Hourglass creates enhanced look to the natural shape of your lips for long-lasting volume, while using shea butter and avocado oil for maximum hydration and moisture.

Seed Peppermint Lip Oil

Lip Oils give you the same effect as a lip gloss only no annoying stickiness or weight. It’s formulated with peppermint which acts a natural irritant to your lips which makes them naturally plump up.

Kiko Milano Sicilian Notes Long-Lasting Lip Liner

Contour you lips with a high pigmented lip liner this one in a tone darker from your natural lip color gives an illusion that you are in control of shaping and altering. Its creamy consistency easily glides onto the lips giving you the freedom to draw the lines in order to achieve a full lip.

Becca Skin Love Glow Glaze Stick

This Glaze Stick acts as a highlighter, but dabbing a bit on your Cupid’s bow makes your lips look bigger thanks to the beauty of art and light reflection.

Benefit Cosmetics Hoola Matte Bronzer

Using a bronzer helps create shadows around your lips giving once again, the optical illusion of plumper lips. Easily fake a big bottom lip with some of the product blended beneath your lower lip and you’re done!

DuWop Lip Venom

This is one of the most essential products when it comes to faking a big lip. Made with a blend of nourishing essential oils that increases circulation, this gloss causes the lips to become fuller and suppler while imparting a naturally rosy hue, all while leaving lips looking gorgeously drenched with the shine supplied from Jojoba and avocado oils.
