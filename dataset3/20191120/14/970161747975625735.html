Justin Bieber looks pretty pleased with himself doing wheelies on electric bike

20 Nov 2019, 14:41

Justin Bieber must have the biggest garage on earth for all his gadgets, the latest being a fancy electric bicycle.

The Purpose star, 25, popped a wheelie on the motorised push bike while out and about in Beverly Hills – and looked pretty pleased with himself that he managed to pull off the stunt.

Justin punched the air and grinned at the waiting photographers as he pulled off the trick despite the bike’s heavy-looking wheels and bodywork.

He was keeping himself amused while his new wife, Hailey Baldwin, is in Germany for work, and it looked like his new toy was definitely curing the boredom.

An electric bike like the one Justin was riding retails around £1,000 and can get up to 25km (15.5miles) an hour.

Justin and Hailey had their second wedding ceremony in September for their friends and family after having tied the knot at a New York courthouse a year earlier.

As well as riding around on his new toy, Justin was also seen heading to the studio in Los Angeles as well as the dermatologist to deal with a breakout.

He’d shared his skin troubles with fans on his Instagram story, as well as photos of his other vehicles including motorbikes and his baby pink Lamborghini.

Justin has always been a big fan of a fancy car (or seven), and has owned Ferraris, a Batmobile-inspired Cadillac, a leopard-print Audi and even a Smart car (hey, everyone knows street parking in LA can be tight).

He’s also had some less practical vehicles in the form of Can-Am Spyder motorbike which he was once filmed riding on a pavement and narrowly missing pedestrians, and quad bike or two.

While his car insurance must be through the roof for this vast amount of high-performance supercars, at least he can afford it…
