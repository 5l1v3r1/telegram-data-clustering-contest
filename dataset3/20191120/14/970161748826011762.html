Mother let toddler drown in bath then dissolved her body in vat of acid

20 Nov 2019, 14:58

A mother has admitted letting her two year-old daughter drown in the bath before dissolving the child’s body in acid.

Monica Dominguez, 38, was jailed for 20 years Friday, nine months after Rebecka Zavala’s remains were found at a container at their apartment in Laredo, Texas.

Dominguez then admitted to a police officer that she’d left Rebecca and her other child in the bath unsupervised just before the tragedy.

She told them how she and her husband Gerardo Zavala-Loredo, 33, put Rebecka in the five gallon container before covering her corpse with corrosive fluid.

Police have been unable to determine if that story is true – and rule out foul play as a cause of Rebecka’s death – because the little girl’s remains were so badly damaged by the acid.

Dominguez later admitted tampering with evidence of a human corpse, endangering a child and abuse of a corpse.

Zavala-Loredo has struck a plea deal which will see him jailed for 14 years for tampering with evidence of a human corpse.

The couple’s four other children, aged one to 11, have been taken into care by social services, the Laredo Morning Times reported.

Dominguez was jailed for a further 10 years in February this year for violating probation over a previous child abuse charge.

Her then nine month-old son suffered six fractures – two on his right arm, and two on each leg – in September 2015.

Both Dominguez and Zavala-Loredo denied harming their son, with Dominguez handed 10 years probation over the boy’s injuries.
