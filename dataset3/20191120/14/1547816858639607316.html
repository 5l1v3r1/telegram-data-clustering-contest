Bigg Boss 13: Dolly Bindra Speaks Out About Ex-Lovers, Rashami Desai And Sidharth Shukla- EXCLUSIVE

20 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Vickey Lalwani

Dolly Bindra is in a mood to talk about Bigg Boss. After a terrific stay inside the house in Season 4, Dolly has words of advice for Rashami Desai, and by the way, she isn't impressed with Asim Riaz

Well, Sidharth Shukla-Asim Riaz fight in last night's episode of Bigg Boss 13 has become the talk of town. Twitter is divided into Sid's fans fighting Paras' lovers. Sid's rumoured ex Akanksha Puri did quite a turnaround on him, when she suddenly declared this morning on SpotboyE.com that Sidharth was getting violent and she condemned him for that behaviour.

Meanwhile, no two opinions about the fact that Sidharth's other angle with Rashami Desai is also helping the show. Sidharth is Rashami's ex- the two were passionately in love during the days of Dil Se Dil Tak--- but it was rather marred by their frequent skirmishes. Janata loves the two of them still fighting with each other. But as we told you sometime back and many celebs also agreed with us- that Rashami calling Sidharth as 'namard' was below the belt and uncalled for.

If you want to know what Bigg Boss 4's contestant Dolly Bindra thinks about the above two incidents- mind you, Dolly gave us an exhaustive interview on what she feels about the contestants that remain in the show- here she's again, frank and forthcoming as ever.

Dolly Bindra, Bigg Boss 4 contestant, has been keeping a tab on all episodes and we called her up this evening to know her take on Rashami and Sidharth.

Dolly said, "I think Rashami has killed all her chances of winning the trophy. She is almost continuously focusing on doing one-upmanship on Sidharth. Same baat ghuma firake bAar baar. Who wants to hear that so frequently."

"As for the Asim Riaz-Sidharth fight last night, I think Asim provoked Sidharth. Sidharth was not in the wrong," Dolly signed off.
