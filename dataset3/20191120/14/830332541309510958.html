Two suffer minor injuries in crash involving semi near Brooklyn, Wisconsin 92 closed, dispatch says

20 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Stephanie Fryer

BROOKLYN, Wis. - First responders are on the scene of a head-on crash, involving a semi, near Brooklyn.

Dane County Dispatchers said two people suffered minor injuries. 

The crash happened around 7:30 a.m. Wednesday at the intersection of Wisconsin 92 and Glenway Road. Highway 92 is closed in both ways because of the crash, dispatch said. 

The other vehicle involved was Kia, dispatch said.

This is a developing story. News 3 Now has a crew on the way to the scene.
