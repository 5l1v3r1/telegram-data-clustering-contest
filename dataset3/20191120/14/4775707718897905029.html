We have just picked up the Golazo! Achievement list.

There are 10 achievements worth a total of 1,000 Gamerscore, none of which are secret.

Name Description Gamerscore
Show your Talent! Score your first goal 100
We are the champions! Win the International Cup with your favorite team 100
International Star! Help your team stay on the top of the World League 100
Wild Striker Score five goals in a single match 100
Striker Deluxe! Score 10 goals in a single match 100
Rising Star Win your first match 100
Road to Glory Win 10 matches 100
Knockout! Win 20 matches 100
Wild Heart! Win 50 matches 100
All Stars! Win 100 matches 100

Golazo! is developed by Purple Tree Studio and published by Klabater.
