Two legends of the cinematic world megastar Amitabh Bachchan and Rajinikanth inaugurated the prestigious film festival named International Film Festival of India in Goa, some time ago. The legendary duo kick-started the festival by lighting the lamp, making it a memorable evening as the festival steps in its 50th year in 2019. As we write about this inaugural ceremony, the official Twitter handle of this festival, has been updating the social media users about the live happenings straight from the event. 

The inaugural ceremony also saw Minister for Information & Broadcasting, Shri Prakash Javadekar, MoS for Environment, Forest & Climate Change, Shri Babul Supriyo, Secretary, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Shri Amit Khare.  Followed by which Big B was felicitated by none other than Rajinikanth himself. The South Superstar also delivered a speech at the opening ceremony and dedicated his Icon of Golden Jubilee of IFFI recognition to all his directors, producers and crew, he has worked with so far.
