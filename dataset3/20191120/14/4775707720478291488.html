Forza Horizon 4 Introduces the Toyota Supra RZ in December

By Heidi Nicholas, 20 Nov 2019
Comments
One of the most popular fan requests has been for Toyota to return to the Forza franchise, and it looks like they've got their wish. Forza Community Manager Brian Ekberg confirmed that the return of Toyota starts in December on Forza Horizon 4, when the 1998 Toyota Supra RZ releases on December 12th.
