I'm A Celebrity's Nadine Coyle accused of 'lying' about Cheryl 'friendship'

I'm A Celebrity viewers have hit back at Nadine Coyle's claims after the Girls Aloud star previously insisted she had never been friends with Cheryl on Celebrity Juice

20 Nov 2019, 21:35 by Kyle O'Sullivan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/kyle-osullivan/

Nadine Coyle has been accused of 'lying' about her 'friendship' with former Girl Aloud bandmate Cheryl.

The Irish singer opened up about her time in the girl band during an intimate chat with Adele Roberts and Jacqueline Jossa.

When the ex-EastEnders star asked if she got on with everyone in Girls Aloud, Nadine said: "Yes."

Digging deeper, Jacqueline admitted she had heard Nadine "didn't get on with Cheryl".

Tense Nadine insisted it was "fake news" - but I'm A Celebrity viewers pointed out this went against what she had claimed before.

Nadine previously blamed the rest of the girls for splitting the band - and even insisted there was never any friendship with Cheryl.

Despite having been a bridesmaid at Cheryl's lavish 2006 wedding to footballer Ashley Cole - Nadine insisted they'd never been friends at all.

On Celebrity Juice in 2018, Nadine said: "There was no falling out, there was just no friendship to begin with."

Taking to Twitter, one concerned viewer said: "Think Nadine is a bit of a liar!"

"Just the passive way Nadine said "oh it's fake news" about her alleged feud with Cheryl," added another.

A third said: "Mmmm I don’t think I believe you Nadine"

Inquisitive Jacqueline also asked Nadine what it was really like to be in a girl band.

Nadine replied: "Really good, really good, I like being in a girl band. I am one of three sisters and went to an all-girls grammar school so I’m used to being around girls."

The former EastEnders actress then asked how the band came about - questioning whether they were put together like Little Mix.

Nadine said: "The public had voted, it was ITV’s Popstars the Rivals so a girl band and a boy band and the public put us together."

DJ Adele said she plays Girls Aloud hit 'The Promise' "all the time" on her radio show - describing the track as a "banger".

The three girls all started singing and dancing to 'The Promise' and singing the song on the way back to camp - with Adele joking if Nadine remembered all the words.

This comes just one day after Nadine gave some juicy insight into her relationship with the other members of the girl band to Kate Garraway.

She told the Good Morning Britain host she was friends with all of the girls but preferred 'partying in gay bars with ten guys'.

She told Kate: "Everyone gets on with their lives, I was friends with them all, it was all nice and lovely.

"But I was always in the gay bars with all the guys, I’d be out partying, me and ten guys, I was having a laugh, I was 18-19 years old."

Cheryl, Kimberley Walsh, Nicola Roberts and Sarah Harding split up in 2013 after their Ten: The Hits Tour and Nadine was quick to blame her fellow bandmates.

She tweeted: "You should know by now I had no part in any of this split business. I couldn't stop them. I had the best time & want to keep going."

But Cheryl vigorously denied Nadine's claims and insisted she was telling "porkie pies".

"She's full of sh**. What d'you want us to say? She's full of s***. She was the one who wanted to make a solo record. Which is why we took the hiatus," she told Attitude magazine, referencing the three year break they took from 2009.
