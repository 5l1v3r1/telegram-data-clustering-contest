Liverpool wonderkid Trent Alexander-Arnold given major upgrade on FIFA 20

EA SPORTS have released the latest database update for FIFA 20, with Liverpool's Trent Alexander-Arnold

20 Nov 2019, 21:30 by Nathan Bliss

After a short hiatus thanks to the international break, Liverpool are back in Premier League action on Saturday, as they face Crystal Palace at Selhurst Park.

The Reds have been in phenomenal form so far this season, winning 11 of their first 12 Premier League games, without a defeat so far, and find themselves eight points clear of second place side Leicester City.

Defender Trent Alexander-Arnold has played a major part in the club's form, showcasing his incredible ability on the ball.

And, in EA SPORTS' latest FIFA 20 database update, they've upgraded Alexander-Arnold's attributes to reflect his real-life form.

However, there is one notable attribute downgrade too.

Trent Alexander-Arnold - Upgrade

Overall ability: 83

Potential: 89

The 21-year-old has been given a big upgrade to his technical attributes, with his crossing (90-91), long passing (82-86), long shots (63-68), short passing (81-83) and vision (78-80) all boosted on the game.

But, somewhat harshly, his marking attribute has been downgraded from 80 to 76, which is a significant change.

Overall though, the young defender is now even better to use on the new game, especially in possession and will be a major part of many gamer's career mode saves.
