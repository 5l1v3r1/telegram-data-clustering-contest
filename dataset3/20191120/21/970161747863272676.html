Mom smothered 3 sons because she envied the attention their father gave them

20 Nov 2019, 21:50

A mother was confessed to suffocating her three young sons to death because she was jealous of the attention their father gave them, prosecutors said.

Brittany Pilkington, 27, was convicted on Tuesday of smothering her sons in three separate incidents over a 13-month period in Bellefontaine, Ohio.

Prosecutors said she killed her 3-month-old son Niall in July 2014, her four-year-old son Gavin in April 2015 and her 3-month-old son Noah in August 2015.

She pleaded guilty to involuntary manslaughter and murder in exchange for being spared the death penalty, WHIO reported.

After Noah was found dead, Pilkington admitted to killing her children because of the attention they received from their father, who was Pilkington’s ex-boyfriend.
