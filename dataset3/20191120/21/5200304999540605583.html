Manish Tiwari scoops Media Entrepreneur of the Year award at IEBF summit

20 Nov 2019, 21:16 by Lakh Baddhan

Manish Tiwari, the founder and Managing Director of UK-based ethnic media agency Here and Now 365 has been awarded ‘Media Entrepreneur of the Year’ at the Indo-European Business Forum (IEBF) summit.

The annual event took place on Tuesday 19th November at the House of Lords in London and hosted by Baroness Verma of Leicester.

The event was attended by many stalwarts such as prominent retired Indian cricketer, Kapil Dev, GP Hinduja, a major investor in India through the Hinduja Group and well-known Bollywood actor Suniel Shetty.
