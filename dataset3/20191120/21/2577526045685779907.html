Years Later, Some Can't Tell Who's on Street Fighter II Turbo's Japanese Cartridge

20 Nov 2019, 21:29 by Brian Ashcraft

You are looking at the cartridge art for Street Fighter II Turbo’s Japanese release. It’s not only different from the UK/US version but more mysterious. Can you tell who is on the cover? Ryu? Chun-Li? Which character is it?

Twitter user Tekitouaki wrote that for years, he thought that Ryu was on the cover.
