Oman weather: Aqbat Baushar opened to traffic

20 Nov 2019, 21:41 by Times New Service

Muscat: Aqbat Baushar road that was closed on Wednesday night following heavy rainfall is now opened to traffic, Royal Oman Police (ROP) has informed.
