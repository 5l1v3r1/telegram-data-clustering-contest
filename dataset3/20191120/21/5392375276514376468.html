Chinese brand ELEPHONE just launched yet another model officially and you can get the new ELEPHONE PX phone already through various usual suspects like Gearbest e-shop or Aliexpress for instance. And to help you with your potential decision the makers released today an official unboxing video so you know what to expect from the package and the phone itself. So let’s check it out for more info, but of course first let’s take a look at the summarized list of specs and selling points for the phone.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YwL-PzixKbg

ELEPHONE PX is sporting a big 6,53-inch full-screen display with 19.3:9 aspect ratio, FHD+ resolution and 93,1% screen-to-body ratio. That’s thanks to the pop-up selfie camera module with 16MP sensor, so no distracting notch is needed. Inside you can find Helio P23 processor, 4 GB RAM, 64 GB of internal storage, rear fingerprint scanner, 3300 mAh battery capacity, 16MP+2MP dual rear main camera sensors with AI support or the Android 9 Pie system. The phone is available in Breathing Crystal or Lumia Purple colors for the additional fancy design.

More information about this ELEPHONE’s latest product and of course all of the others is available on the official website of this chinese brand. And because the design if also very important part of the shopping decision nowadays we have for you also a small gallery of the phone from all the angles.
