Dunn scores 14 to lift St. John’s over Columbia 82-63

20 Nov 2019, 21:36 by Associated Press

NEW YORK (AP) — Rasheem Dunn registered 14 points as St. John’s beat Columbia 82-63 on Wednesday night.

Mustapha Heron had 15 points for St. John’s (4-1). Julian Champagnie added 14 points and seven rebounds. Marcellus Earlington had eight rebounds with eight boards for the hosts. Josh Roberts led the team with 11 rebounds.

Mike Smith had 20 points, nine rebounds and six assists for the Lions (1-4). Jake Killingsworth added 11 points. Tai Bibbs had 10 points and six boards.

St. John’s faces Arizona State on Saturday. Columbia plays Rider on Saturday.
