Brown Hill property burns in suspicious blaze

20 Nov 2019, 21:45 by Ben Hopkins

UPDATED: 9.30am

The owner of a property which went up in flames has said he is just thankful the blaze didn't spread to the neighbouring petrol station.

The man, who owns also owns the garage neighbouring the property said he received multiple calls early on Thursday morning informing him the vacant home was on fire.

He condemned the actions of the potential arsonist, saying they were "thoughtless" and weren't thinking about the consequences of their actions.

EARLIER:

Emergency services have been put on high alert heading into Thursday's total fire ban following a house fire in the early hours of the morning.

Fire crews and police were called to Water Street in Brown Hill around 12.40am to reports of a fire at a property.
