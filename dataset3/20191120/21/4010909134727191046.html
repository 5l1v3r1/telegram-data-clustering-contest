HONOR collaborating with Stoned and Co to bring you exclusive HONOR 9X merchandise

20 Nov 2019, 21:51

HONOR has officially announced that they will be collaborating with Stoned and Co to bring you limited edition HONOR 9X merchandise. Having previously worked with other high fashion labels, HONOR mentioned that the aforementioned “function fashion” merchandise will be available during the launch of the HONOR 9X which is happening on 21 November 2019.

With all that said, would you be interested in getting the HONOR 9X if it was bundled with the limited edition merchandise? Let us know on our Facebook page, and stay tuned to TechNave.com for our full coverage of the launch.
