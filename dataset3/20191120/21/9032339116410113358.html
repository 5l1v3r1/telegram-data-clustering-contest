Here’s Everything Coming to Netflix in December

20 Nov 2019, 21:08

What says "happy holidays" better than watching a romantic serial killer meet his new potential victim? The slate of Netflix's December releases has it covered. That's right, folks, the second season of You will be available during the jolliest time of the year.

Along with You, new seasons of other Netflix Original series like Fuller House and Ultraviolet will also become available for your streaming pleasure. And rejoice, Terrace House fans, because Part 2 of the Tokyo series will also go live. Of course, it's not truly December without some holiday flicks, so you can also tune in to deliciously cheesy films like A Cinderella Story: Christmas Wish, or laugh your stockings off with comedy specials like Jack Whitehall: Christmas with My Father.

Read on to see what else is in store for the last month of 2019.
