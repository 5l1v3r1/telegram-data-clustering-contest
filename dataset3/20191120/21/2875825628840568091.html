I'm a Celeb's Roman Kemp's sly dig about Jane McDonald while Adele eats camel anus

As Roman and Adele struggled through their eating trial, Roman made a sly dig about Adele's photo of Jane McDonald

20 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm a Celebrity star Roman Kemp couldn't resist making a little joke about campmate Adele Robert 's now infamous luxury item.

The radio DJs competed in a horrifying Bushtucker trial in tonight's ep, which saw them take on the first eating challenge of the series.

As Adele tried to stomach Piggy Pudding, which was pig's uterus, she gagged as it "popped" in her mouth.

Roman encouraged her: "Think how proud Jane is going to be!", referring to Adele's framed photo of Loose Women's Jane McDonald in the camp.

The two previously got into a row over the photo when Roman was confused as to why Adele loved Jane so much, so the cheeky quip shows he's still dubious over the luxury item.

But they did both give the photo a lucky rub before they headed off to the challenge.

The trial also saw Roman tuck into Critter-Bocker Glory, which was made up of dead cockroaches, crickets and mealworms.
