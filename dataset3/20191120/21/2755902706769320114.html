PICS: Chamisa Shows Compassion For Victims Of Police Brutality

20 Nov 2019, 21:37 by Andrew

MDC leader Nelson Chamisa today visited some of the victims of police brutality that took centre stage, this afternoon. Several unarmed civilians where left nursing wounds after police unleashed terror on civilians and opposition MDC supporters who had gathered at the Morgan Richard Tsvangirai (MRT) House.
