Aldi to launch Friends range for Christmas with Central Perk mugs, cushions, duvets and more

Supermarket Aldi will be the best friend of any Friends fan with its new range dedicated to the hit American comedy

20 Nov 2019, 21:19 by Simon Duke

Just as rumours swirl about a potential cast reunion being on the cards, Aldi is about to be the best friend of any self respecting Friends fan with its new range dedicated to the classic American comedy which, after lots of excitement, hits stores this week!

You might not believe it but 25 years have now passed since Friends made its debut on our screens and quickly became a big favourite with viewers across the world.

Friends is the much-loved sitcom that gave us iconic moments like The Holiday Armadillo, "pivot", "We were on a break!" and of course "I got off the plane!"

And, after Jennifer Aniston sent Instagram into overdrive by joining up and posting THAT selfie of her and her co-stars, and classic episodes of Friends are about to hit cinemas, Aldi has more excitement from lovers of all things Friends with its new range, which is flooded with bargains.

You can see the best products from the range in our Aldi Friends gallery above and some of the highlights are pyjama sets emblazoned with 'How You Doin' and 'I'd Rather Be Watching Friends' for £8.99.

And you can sleep well in the knowledge that you are the ultimate Friends fan with the choice of three different double sets, all priced at only £14.99.

The top must-have for any Friends fan has to be the Central Perk mug and you can pick up one of those for just £4.99 along with a travel cup featuring classic slogans from the show or a 'How You Doin' mug.

And if you need some Friends paraphernalia around you while you binge your favourite episodes you can furnish your sofa with a variety of Friends cushions, each priced £5.99.

*Aldi's Friends range will be available online and in store from Thursday, November 21.
