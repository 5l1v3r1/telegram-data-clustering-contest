DRAM Brings the Holiday Cheer on New Single "Litmas"

“Don’t forget the cookies for Santa (Let’s get the good kind).”

20 Nov 2019, 21:41 by Patrick Montes

DRAM is hoping to stir up some holiday cheer with his latest release. Following up his August collaboration with H.E.R., “The Lay Down,” the talented singer-songwriter is introducing a new holiday record produced by the duo of Josh Abraham & Oligee, “Litmas.” DRAM is also no stranger to festive Christmas tunes either, having released the #1EpicHoliday EP back in November of 2017.

“Love on the holidays (Let’s spread the good vibes) / Don’t forget the cookies for Santa (Let’s get the good kind) / Love on the holidays (Let’s spread the good vibes) / More love is on the way (And it’s the good kind),” DRAM sings on “Litmas.” “Cause we can deck the halls (Every day, every day, every day) / And hang the mistletoe (Every day, every day, every day) / It can be Christmas, ayy (Every day, every day, every day) / We can celebrate Christmas, ayy (Every day, every day, every day).”

You can check out DRAM’s new holiday single “Litmas” above.

For more, revisit Valee and DRAM’s ChaseTheMoney-produced track “About U,” which saw release in December of last year.
