University of Regina Cougars' Michaela Kleisinger receives U Sports honour

Michaela Kleisinger of the University of Regina Cougars had back-to-back triple-doubles in a weekend sweep of Winnipeg.

20 Nov 2019, 21:18 by Regina Leader-Post

Michaela Kleisinger of the University of Regina Cougars is the U Sports female athlete of the week.

Kleisinger, a fifth-year guard with the Cougars’ women’s basketball team, registered back-to-back triple-doubles last weekend in a sweep of the visiting University of Winnipeg Wesmen.

Until Friday, a U of R basketball player had not recorded a triple-double since the Cougars joined Canada West leading up to the 2001-02 season.

Kleisinger had 12 points, 11 rebounds and 11 assists in Friday’s 92-46 victory. She followed up with 17 points, 12 rebounds and 11 assists Saturday in an 84-51 victory over Winnipeg.

The Cougars are to oppose the University of Saskatchewan Huskies on Friday and Saturday in Saskatoon.
