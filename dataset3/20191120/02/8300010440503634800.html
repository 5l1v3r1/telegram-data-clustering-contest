Chatsworth shooting leaves man with multiple gunshot wounds, suspect outstanding

20 Nov 2019, 02:16 by ABC7.com staff

CHATSWORTH, LOS ANGELES (KABC) -- Officers found a man with multiple gunshot wounds when they responded to a shooting in Chatsworth Tuesday.

The incident happened at 4 p.m. in the 9900 block of De Soto Avenue.

The victim was unconscious and not breathing, according to the Los Angeles Police Department.

The victim in his 40s was shot inside an apartment before walking outside and collapsing in a stairwell. He was rushed to a hospital in critical condition.

The suspect is described as a female and fled the scene in a truck.

No arrests have been made.
