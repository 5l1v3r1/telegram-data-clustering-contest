Chuck Liddell doubles down on Mike Tyson street fight comments - ‘I’d take him down and he’d stay down’

20 Nov 2019, 02:57 by Adam Guillen Jr.

A few days ago, Chuck Liddell stated that if he ever found himself in a street fight against Mike Tyson during their heydays, it would be a battle he’d win. But after catching some flack for his comments on social media, the former Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) Light Heavyweight champion took the time to defend his prediction.

Taking to Twitter, “The Iceman” clarified his comments and doubled down by saying that “Iron Mike” would be unable to stop the takedown, which means he’d be like a fish out of water on the ground.
