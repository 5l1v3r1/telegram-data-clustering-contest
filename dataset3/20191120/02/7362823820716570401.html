Kelly Clarkson laughs off 'Baby, It's Cold Outside' backlash: 'We didn't kill Christmas'

20 Nov 2019, 02:32 by Nate Day

Kelly Clarkson is responding to the backlash that she and John Legend received for updating the lyrics to the popular holiday tune, "Baby, It's Cold Outside."

On Tuesday's episode of "The Kelly Clarkson Show," Legend's wife Chrissy Teigen and the "Breakaway" singer called criticism of the song "so weird."

"Did you not hear that John (Legend) and I ruined Christmas recently?" Clarkson, 37, joked. "I was like, 'how did I mess that up?'"
