Multi-award winning singer John Legend performing free concert at DTLA's Union Station

20 Nov 2019, 02:32

LOS ANGELES (KABC) -- John Legend will be holding a free concert at Union Station in Downtown Los Angeles Tuesday.

The multi-award winning singer and songwriter will perform at 7:30 p.m.

Attendance will be allowed on a first-come, first-served basis and will be limited to 500 people. Doors will open at 4 p.m.
