Google’s ‘Claw’ phone mount may damage Stadia controllers

Stadia buyers, beware

20 Nov 2019, 22:08 by Bradly Shankar

One of Google’s accessories for its recently launched Google’s Stadia game streaming service might be more trouble than it’s worth.

Raymond Wong, a tech journalist who reviewed Stadia for Inverse, noted on Twitter that Google’s ‘Power Support Claw’ Google for mounting a phone to a Stadia controller might actually damage the gamepad upon attachment or removal.
