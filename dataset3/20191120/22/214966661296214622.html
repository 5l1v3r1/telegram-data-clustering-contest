Gold Coast suburbs set for water interruptions today

20 Nov 2019, 22:02 by Monique StClair

There are a number of water interruptions to be aware of on the Gold Coast today.

Council crews will be working to install new valves in Miami, which could affect Evandale Crescent and Nundah Avenue between the hours of 9.00am and 2.00pm.

While in Southport, a water interruption has been planned on Anne Street between Lilli Pilli Drive and Susanne Street, as crews work on a water main.

Houses could be affected between the hours of 9.00am and 3.00pm.

Tomorrow, crews will return to the same area during the same hours – though Pilliga Street, and corner of Susanne Street and Alicia Street may also be affected.

Also tomorrow, various streets in Runaway Bay could be affected by the installation of new valves.

Sundance Way, Nirvana Court, Midnight Court, Lobster Pot Place and Rum Point Place may notice the interruptions between 9.00am and 2.00pm.

Overnight, residents in Helensvale may have noticed an unplanned interruption, with council reported that Ashford Road homes may have been affected by water main repairs.
