Paige and Renee Young reject idea of another controversial veteran returning on WWE Backstage

20 Nov 2019, 22:06 by Lennard Surrao

Jim Cornette is in the news again for all the wrong reasons as the pro wrestling veteran resigned from NWA on the back of the offensive comments he made about Trevor Murdoch during NWA Powerrr.

A fan tweeted out a suggestion on Cornette's Twitter handle, proposing the idea of him possibly appearing on WWE Backstage as a guest.

Paige and Renee Young commented on the fan's tweet and shot down any possibility of Cornette appearing on the FS1 show.

While Paige responded with a casual 'Nah, we're good', Renee Young laughed off the idea and reacted with a 'hard no'.
