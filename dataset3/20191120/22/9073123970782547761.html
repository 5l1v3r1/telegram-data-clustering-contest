This week, we’ll be searching for the hidden T location in the Trick Shot loading screen. Here’s a guide if you’re having trouble finding it.

Hidden ‘T’ location

The ‘T’ in the Trick Shot loading screen isn’t too difficult to spot if you look closely enough. It’s sitting under the bridge behind our two characters who are about to square off. Here’s the exact location of the hidden ‘T’.

On the map, you can find the hidden ‘T’ on the river just north of Weeping Woods. There’s a bridge heading out of the POI. You can land there or cross it and will find the ‘T’ hiding on the second layer.
