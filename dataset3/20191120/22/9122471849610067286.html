Popular Bowl Game Sponsor Not Renewing Its Contract

20 Nov 2019, 22:11 by Tzvi Machlin

For the past five years the Belk Bowl has pitted the ACC against the SEC at Bank of America Stadium in North Carolina. While the bowl will likely go on, it won’t with the same name.

After nearly a decade sponsoring the bowl game bearing its name, Belk will not be renewing its contract to sponsor it. As a result, the 2019 Belk Bowl will be the last one of that moniker.

In addition, their contract to sponsor the annual Kickoff Game at Bank of America Stadium will also not be renewed.

According to Joseph Person, Belk informed the Charlotte Sports Foundation that it would not be renewing its contract as the title sponsor for the two games.
