Jim Bob And Michelle Duggar Respond To Reports Homeland Security Raided Arkansas Home

20 Nov 2019, 22:39 by Rachelle Lewis

Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar have responded to reports Homeland Security raided their Arkansas home.

On Tuesday, news broke Homeland Security was allegedly at the Tontitown, Arkansas compound the Duggar’s call home. KNWA, a local NBC affiliate in the area, was the first to report the news.

The affiliate shared a Homeland Security spokesperson who said, “pursuant to an on-going federal criminal investigation”  was the reason. KNWA did not disclose the nature of the investigation. The U.S. District Attorney for Western Arkansas had no comment on the topic.

20-year-old Jedidiah Duggar, who is running for Arkansas State Representative, spoke to KNWA. He denied knowing anything regarding a Federal investigation into his family.
