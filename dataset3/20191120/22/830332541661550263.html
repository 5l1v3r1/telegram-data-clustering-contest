Police: 6 jailed in stolen vehicle arrests in Waunakee within 4 hours

20 Nov 2019, 22:59 by Brandon Arbuckle

WAUNAKEE, Wis. - Officials said six people have been jailed in stolen vehicle arrests in Waunakee within four hours this week.

According to a news release from the Waunakee Police Department, the first incident took place Tuesday at 11:25 p.m. 

Police were sent to the Southbridge neighborhood after a report that two male subjects ages 20-21 were attempting to enter vehicles. The men were found in a vehicle and later taken into custody.

The second incident happened Wednesday at 3:09 a.m. Authorities were dispatched to the Meadowbrook neighborhood for another report of a subject trying to enter vehicles. 

The suspect ran away when police arrived, and a K-9 track was unsuccessful. Two stolen vehicles were eventually found during the investigation. As the investigation continued, four boys ages 15-17 were found in Waunakee and taken into custody. 

One of the teens was taken to jail, while the other three were brought to a juvenile reception center. All four boys were booked on multiple charges.

Anyone with information or video surveillance footage of either incident can call the Waunakee Police Department at 608-849-4523.
