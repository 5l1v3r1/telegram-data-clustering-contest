Collier County Police led on chase after observing traffic violation

20 Nov 2019, 22:08 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. — Collier County Police has one suspect in custody after the person led police on a chase.

Deputies confirm they observed a traffic violation on Juliet Boulevard. That’s where the pursuit initiated.

The pursuit led police on and off I-75.

The pursuit came to an end on Bonita Beach Road off I-75.

Deputies confirm no other persons are involved.

This is still an ongoing investigation.
