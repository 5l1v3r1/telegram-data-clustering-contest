Doctor Who makes up for no Christmas special with amazing series 12 guest stars

20 Nov 2019, 22:01

Sir Lenny Henry and Stephen Fry will be special guest stars in Doctor Who series 12.

When Jodie Whittaker’s Time Lord travels the universe once again next year, she is set to meet the actors along the way.

Their characters are being kept under wraps for now – so will they be friend or foe? Either is fine with us!

Stephen said: ‘Short of being picked for a British space exploration programme and I readily concede that I’m past the age where I’d be considered (if I was ever the right age for such a posting) – then being in an episode of Doctor Who will certainly do as a very sweet second-place excitement.’

Sir Lenny, equally excited, added: ‘It was absolutely brilliant to be welcomed into the fantastical world of Doctor Who. The nearest I have been to the TARDIS was when I played the Caribbean Doctor in the Lenny Henry Show , so as a life-long (hiding behind the sofa type) Doctor Who fan this is a very special moment for me.’

Showrunner Chris Chibnall said he is buzzing to be working with ‘two of my absolute favourite actors’.

‘Doctor Who is coming back with a bang – with two great British icons in major roles,’ he said.
