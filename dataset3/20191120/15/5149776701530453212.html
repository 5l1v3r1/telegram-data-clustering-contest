Historic stately home throwing Christmas parties for dogs - and they're super posh

Packages start from, erm, £300

20 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Rachel Gorman

Your favourite pampered pooch need not be left out of the fast-approaching Yuletide party season, at least not at one of Nottinghamshire's grandest stately homes.

Historic 15th century Thrumpton Hall will be throwing Christmas parties for dogs as part of its festive celebrations - and in keeping with the locations, they're naturally pretty posh.

Organised by Regal Wagmore, a private portrait photography company based at the hall, packages start from £300 - and can include anything from coffee and light refreshments through to a sumptuous three course champagne luncheon rounded off with a classic afternoon tea.

Visitors can also recreate history and get the Downton Abbey treatment with a fun period photoshoot - which can include furry friends, of course.

Founder, Steven Havers, is a professional photographer and says the Regal Wagmore experience is an unforgettable one.

"Regal Wagmore offers a truly unique portrait photography experience, first class levels of service, comfort and attention,” he says.

“With stunning, authentic interiors and beautifully manicured grounds, the possibilities are endless.

“Period costumes can be hired to add a truly timeless look and feel to your portraits, Thrumpton Hall has it all.

“Dogs are very welcome to join you in your portrait photography experience. You can even choose to stay the night in a stunning four poster bed.”

To book visit regalwagmore.co.uk.
