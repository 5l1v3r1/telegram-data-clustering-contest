Rebekah Vardy thanks 'amazing sister' for surprise baby shower after Coleen Rooney drama

The heavily pregnant wag shared photos from the occasion

20 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Sophie McCoid

Rebekah Vardy has shared photos from her surprise baby shower, as she prepares to welcome her fifth child.

Rebekah's sister Esther threw the surprise shower for the 37-year-old, who has had a tough couple of months after her feud with Coleen Rooney.

Dressed in a cream jumper dress with her brown hair down Rebekah held balloons with the slogan "Oh Baby" emblazoned on them.

She then shared photos of some of the gifts she had received, along with photos of some of her friends who had attended the shower.

Rebekah captioned the photos: "Such an amazing surprise... thank you to my amazing Sister ❤️❤️ love you all."

Her husband Jamie Vardy said: "You deserve it."

And fellow WAG Nicola McLean, who was vocal in her support for Rebekah during the Coleen drama, sent a series of love heart emojis to her.

Last month Coleen caused a media storm when she accused someone operating Rebekah Vardy's Instagram account of selling stories about her to The S*n newspaper.

Rebekah categorically denied the claims and said her account had been previously hacked.

The fallout caused a series of viral memes and tweets, with the hashtag #Wagathachristie trending widely.
