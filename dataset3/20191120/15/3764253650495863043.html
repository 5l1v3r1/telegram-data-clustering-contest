The Nigerian entertainment scene is a very lovely one seeing as it always presents fans with different antics of celebrities.

One star who constantly entertains fans with his lifestyle is popular cross dresser, Idris Okuneye, popularly called Bobrisky. Bob might lead a controversial lifestyle but he has managed to garner fans to his side.

Bobrisky recently travelled out to Dubai to have a nice time and he is obviously enjoying the City of Gold. To pepper fans and haters alike, the young man has constantly kept them updated by sharing videos from his holiday abroad.

My next trip to Paris I need a sugar boy to fly with me. But d sugar boy must be cute o because am pretty too. Pls don’t hate”
