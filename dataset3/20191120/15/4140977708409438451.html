Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2019-20 Super League schedule: How many SMAT matches will be televised?

20 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Dixit Bhargav

Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2019-20 Super League schedule: The Sportsrush present before you full schedule of the Super League of SMAT 2019.

The Super League of the ongoing season of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy will begin from tomorrow in Surat. After 38 teams took part in the tournament, 10 have been shortlisted to play in the tournament.

The 38 teams that took part in the tournament were divided into five groups and now the top-two teams from each group will take part in the second round. It is worth mentioning that these teams have been divided into two groups and the top two teams from these groups will play the semi-finals from November 29.

Group A – Maharashtra, Delhi, Baroda, Rajasthan, Haryana

Group B – Punjab, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Mumbai
