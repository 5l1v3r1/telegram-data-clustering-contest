Save the Wolves

20 Nov 2019, 15:07 by BRIEF

RALEIGH, N.C. — The Center for Biological Diversity sued Interior Secretary David Bernhardt in federal court for failing to update the recovery plan for the endangered red wolf, only 14 of which are known to exist in the wild.
