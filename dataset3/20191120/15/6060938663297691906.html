Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday's upcoming film 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' has been the talk of the town. The makers today dropped the new song of the film which is a revamped version of Govinda and Raveena Tandon's 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Maare'. Soon after the song was released online, fans took to their social media accounts to pour in their love for it. However, a lot of social media users are up in arms against the reprised track.
