Piers Morgan hails ‘impressive’ Nicola Sturgeon as ‘most natural leader in British politics’

20 Nov 2019, 15:15 by Ewan Mowat

PIERS Morgan has hailed Nicola Sturgeon as "the most natural leader in British politics".

The GMB host praised the "impressive" SNP chief for her skill in dealing with the media after her ITV interview last night.

The pair often clash on Morgan's show, but he heaped compliments on Sturgeon after her telly grilling.

The First Minister was excluded from the first leaders' debate - which was a head-to-head between Boris Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn.

But she - along with Lib Dem leader Jo Swinson, Nigel Farage of the Brexit Party and Greens co-leader Sian Berry - was quizzed afterwards by host Nina Hossain.

And Morgan was clearly impressed with her interview skills - even if he didn't agree with everything she said.

He tweeted: "Without passing any judgement on the content of what she says, Nicola Sturgeon is the most natural leader in British politics.

"So impressive in media interviews."

 

His comments racked up more than 7,700 likes and around 1,000 retweets.

During her interview, Sturgeon denied a deal had already been done between the SNP and Labour, rubbishing claims by Johnson.
