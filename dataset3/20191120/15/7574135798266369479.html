Newport Folk Festival reveals 2020 ticket details

20 Nov 2019, 15:39 by Amanda Hatfield

Newport Folk Festival returns to Newport, RI’s Ft. Adams Park for its 2020 edition Friday, July 31 – Sunday, August 2, and while the lineup is still to be announced, the festival has revealed ticket information. Tickets go on sale Wednesday 12/4 at 10 AM, with single, two, and three day passes available. The festival, which is known for featuring surprise artist collaborations and appearances, sells out quickly every year, so if you want to go in 2020, you probably won’t want to wait to long to get your tickets.

The 2019 edition of Newport Folk included sets from Jeff Tweedy, Kacey Musgraves, The Highwomen, Phil Lesh and the Terrapin Family Band, Kevin Morby, Lucy Dacus, Mountain Man, surprise appearances from Dolly Parton, Kermit the Frog, and Judy Collins, and more.
