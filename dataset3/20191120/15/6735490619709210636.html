‘CMA Country Christmas’ Returns With All-Star Lineup Including Dierks Bentley, Trisha Yearwood, Lady Antebellum And More

20 Nov 2019, 15:25 by Katie Colley

Country stars are ringing in the holiday season with the annual “CMA Country Christmas” TV special.

Trisha Yearwood will serve as the host, bringing a new face to the long-running show in its 10th year.
