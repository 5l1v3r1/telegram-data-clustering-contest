Watch: Carrie Underwood gets the blues in smoldering 'Drinking Alone' video

20 Nov 2019, 15:39 by Brandy McDonnell

Checotah native Carrie Underwood has got the blues in newly released music video for her latest single, "Drinking Alone."

The mini-movie, directed by her frequent collaborator Randee St. Nicholas, as previously reported, debuted today. It features Underwood as a brokenhearted blues club chanteuse who has a significant encounter with a similarly lonely-hearted patron.
