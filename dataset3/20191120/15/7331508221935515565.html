NFL Week 12 early betting look: Cowboys will have a tough time moving the ball on the Patriots

20 Nov 2019, 15:31 by Preston Johnson

We are going to do things a little differently in Week 12. I don't have any futures that I see in the current market that are worth buying, and while I considered selling Dwayne Haskins and the Washington Redskins again, the line has already moved from -2 at open and to -3.5. I'm going to buy or sell one aspect of each of these four games and what it means from a betting perspective. There has been line movement around key numbers in all four instances as well, so I will get into the games that moved in line with my projections and the ones that did not. Let's get into it.
