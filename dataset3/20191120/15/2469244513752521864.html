Luxury watches stolen in Paignton burglary

Timepieces by Rolex, Breitling and Boss were stolen

20 Nov 2019, 15:31 by Howard Lloyd

Police are investigating a burglary in Paignton which saw a number of luxury watches stolen.

The incident happened on Brixham Road in Paignton on Sunday November, 3.

A number of watches - a Rolex, a Breitling and a Boss - were stolen from a property between 4.30pm and 6pm.
