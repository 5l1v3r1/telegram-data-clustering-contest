Put your feet up this Christmas and enjoy a five-course Indian banquet

Satisfying the tastebuds of the people of South Wales for the past decade, Mowgli's Indian Restaurant added a second restaurant in Whitchurch in 2018

20 Nov 2019, 15:03 by Jonny Dillon

Take the stress out of cooking this Christmas day with a special five-course feast at Mowgli’s Whitchurch restaurant.

Established in 2009 with a restaurant on Crwys Road, Mowgli's was created by food-loving enthusiast Mr Zaman, who has over 30 years of experience in catering business with special interest in Indian cuisine.

It has since then added a second restaurant in Whitchurch in 2018 which is open on Christmas day for the first time. Enjoy a five-course special Christmas meal - vegetarian option is available on request - from as little as £29.95 per person and can be booked from any time between 12-9pm.

During its 10 years in operation, Mowgli's has carved out a reputation of delivering high-class traditional Indian fine-dining cuisine at both of its restaurants. It has also picked up a plethora of awards along the way too.

Dining at Mowgli's on Christmas day requires advance booking notice.

Mowgli's looks forward to spreading a little Christmas cheer over the festive period and would like to thank the people of South Wales for their custom over the years.
