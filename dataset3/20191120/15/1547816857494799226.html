Grammy Nominations 2020: Jonas Brothers, John Legend, Beyonce, Shawn Mendes- Camila Cabello And Others Eyeing The Big Win

20 Nov 2019, 15:22 by Lubna Khan

The complete list of nominees for the 62ndGrammy has finally been revealed and while the entire list has more than 80 categories, we bring you some of the major categories of Grammy nominations. Check it out!

The Grammy Awards are one of the biggest music awards ever and are the second of the Big Three major music awards held every year. The award ceremony is a grand one, of course, and features power-packed performances by the most prominent artists in the industry. The 62nd Grammy Awards will take place on January 26, 2020, at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, and so far, the list of nominees had not been revealed. Now, the list is finally out and the most prominent musician from the industry are eyeing the big win!

The nominations for the 62nd Grammy Awards were announced today and ‘Good as hell’ singer Lizzo is leading on the list with eight nominations, while ‘Bad Guy’ singer Billie Eilish and Lil Nas X are close behind with six nominations each. What’s special about this is that all three- Lizzo, Billie Eilish, Lil Nas X- have been nominated in the Grammys for the first time. While the entire list has more than 80 categories, we bring you some of the major categories of Grammy nominations. Check it out!
