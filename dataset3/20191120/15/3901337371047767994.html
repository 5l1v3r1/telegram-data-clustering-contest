Billiat Brace Good Timing For Chiefs…

20 Nov 2019, 15:41 by Soccer Laduma

Having not scored for Kaizer Chiefs for almost two months, Khama Billiat’s brace for Zimbabwe this week could bode well for the Amakhosi’s trophy ambitions…

Read: Bafana Coach To Finally Get Contract After AFCON Qualifiers?

Billiat’s last goal for the Glamour Boys came back in September when he helped them to a 2-0 win over AmaZulu in the Absa Premiership, and was only his fourth goal for the club in 2019 so far.

Now though, on the back of scoring two goals in his nation’s 2021 African Cup of Nations 2-1 victory over Zambia, Ernst Middendorp will be hoping that the attacker comes back with a spring in his step ahead of Sunday’s crucial Telkom Knockout semi-final against Maritzburg United.

Both of Billiat’s goals were set up by former Chiefs frontman, Knowledge Musona, with the Zimbabwean attacker scoring a header after a corner from the ‘Smiling Assassin’ before the Anderlecht forward set up the Amakhosi man for a winner towards the end of the game.
