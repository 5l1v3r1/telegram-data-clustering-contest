You can hire your own Santa sleigh for Christmas parties in Liverpool this year

The big red sleigh even comes with its own Christmas present props

20 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Gemma Jaleel

The Christmas tree is up, the fairy lights are hung and tinsel is covering every inch of your house.

Surely there is nothing more you can add to make the setting even more festive?

Well, if you're hosting a Christmas party for all the family and want to impress, then you can hire your very own Santa sleigh.

The professionally-built, full-size red sleigh comes complete with rope lights, fur throw and even Christmas present props. Just imagine the selfies.

Owner Gerrard Aindow told the ECHO: "It’s a lovely thing. I collected it from Inverness last year and completely refurbished it - it's a proper show stopper."

The sleigh, which is £150 to hire for 24 hours, is the ideal addition to any Christmas party, display or even grotto and will be available throughout November and December.
