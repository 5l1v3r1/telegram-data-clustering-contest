Jennifer Lopez, Scarlett Johansson And Eddie Murphy To Host ‘SNL’ In December

20 Nov 2019, 15:10 by Corey Atad

It’s going to be a star-studded holiday season on Global’s “Saturday Night Live”.

On Wednesday, the official “SNL” Twitter account revealed the full host and performer lineup for the month of December.

Jennifer Lopez, Scarlett Johansson and Eddie Murphy will each host the show on Dec. 7, 14 and 21, backed up by DaBaby, Niall Horan and Lizzo respectively.
