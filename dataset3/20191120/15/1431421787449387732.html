Israel Strikes Dozens of Iranian, Syrian Targets Following Rocket Barrage

Some 20 targets hit in the attack, more than half Iranian ■ Netanyahu: 'Whoever harms us - we will strike back' ■ Iranians may have suffered fatalities, senior Israeli defense official says ■ Watchdog says 23 killed, 15 of which are non-Syrian ■ Russia calls attack a 'wrong move'

20 Nov 2019, 15:01 by Yaniv Kubovich, Amos Harel, Jack Khoury, Noa Landau and The Associated Press

UPDATE: Israel strike against Iran sends a message: We are no Saudi Arabia

The Israeli Army said Wednesday it attacked dozens of targets in Syria belonging to Iran's Quds Force and Bashar Assad's army.
