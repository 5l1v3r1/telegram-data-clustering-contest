OSU women's basketball: Natasha Mack scores 19 points to lead Cowgirls past Idaho State 70-52

20 Nov 2019, 06:05

Junior forward Natasha Mack scored a game-high 19 points and pulled down 10 rebounds to lead Oklahoma State to a 70-52 win over Idaho State on Tuesday night at Gallagher-Iba Arena.

Vivian Gray added 17 points, her 29th straight game scoring in double figures for the Cowgirls, even though the junior forward had a poor shooting night. Gray was only 3 of 16 from the field but she was a perfect 10 of 10 from the free throw line.

Gray also had six rebounds. Freshman guard Lauren Fields from Shawnee added 13 points for the Cowgirls, who improved to 4-0 on the season. Idaho State fell to 2-1 on the season.

The Cowgirls return to action on Saturday when they host Rice in a 2 p.m. contest.
