Brazil end winless run with 3-0 triumph over South Korea

20 Nov 2019, 06:00

Brazil won for the first time since lifting the Copa America trophy in July, scoring a 3-0 success over South Korea in Abu Dhabi yesterday. The Selecao had failed to win their last five matches. But goals by Lucas Paqueta, Philippe Coutinho (third from right) and Danilo gave them a comfortable win.
