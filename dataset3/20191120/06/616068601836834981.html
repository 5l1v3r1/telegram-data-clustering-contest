Late-night house fire

20 Nov 2019, 06:27 by Jon Manchester

Fire consumed the main floor of a Kelowna home overnight.

Kelowna Fire Dispatch received a 911 call at 11:45 p.m., reporting flames coming from the front door of a house on the 800 block of Morrison Road.

First arriving crews confirmed the main floor area was fully involved with flames.

A defensive fire attack was deployed, says Platoon Capt. Dennis Miller, and the fire was controlled from the outside.

No occupants were home at the time of the fire.

The house sustained extensive damage in the fire, the cause of which is unknown at this time. Fire investigators will return to the scene today.

Four engines, a command unit, rescue truck and 19 personnel responded to the blaze, along with assistance from RCMP and paramedics.
