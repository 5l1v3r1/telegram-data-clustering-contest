Aaradhya Looks Cute As Button In This Birthday Bash Picture Shared By Aishwarya Rai Bachchan

20 Nov 2019, 06:43 by Oshine Koul

Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan’s cute daughter Aaradhya Bachchan turned 8 on November 16. To make it special, her parents celebrated her birthday in the best way possible. And now, Aishwarya has shared a picture of Aaradhya from her birthday which is simply too adorable for words

Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan celebrate their cute daughter Aaradhya Bachchan’s birthday on November 16. Aaradhya turned 8 and her parents celebrated it in the presence of their family, relatives and close friends. And when it a birthday bash, our stars surely know how to make it lavish. Doing the same, Aishwarya and Abhishek hosted a lavish birthday bash for their daughter, which was attended by who’s who of Bollywood.

While the pictures from Bachchan’s birthday bash are already on the Internet, yummy mommy Aishwarya Rai shared yet another cute picture of Aaradhya. Sharing the snap, which seems to be from Aaradhaya’s birthday bash, Aishwarya captioned it, “MY WORLD I LOVE YOU INFINITELY” with lots of emojis. As seen in the picture, Aaradhya is seen sitting on the floor while holding a big 8 in front of her. With cute hairband and bangs on the faces, Aaradhya looks no less a diva as she poses for the camera. Celebs who had arrived at Aaradhya’s birthday bash were Shah Rukh Khan-Gauri Khan with their son AbRam, Karan Johar with his twins- Yash and Roohi, ace photographer Daboo Ratnani with wife and kids, Riteish Deshmukh. While Bachchan made sure to not share a glimpse of Aaradhya, shutterbugs were best at their jobs for clicking the birthday girl at their Juhu bungalow.

 
A few inside pictures from the bash saw Aaradhya getting ready to cut her unicorn-themed cake while she is surrounded by Amitabh Bachchan, Jaya Bachchan, Abhishek and Aishwarya along with her friends. The pictures also see Aaradhya enjoying a Ferris wheel ride along with her friends.
