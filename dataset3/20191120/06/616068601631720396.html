Meghan 'most powerful' - Entertainment News

20 Nov 2019, 06:58 by WENN

Meghan, Duchess of Sussex has beaten Kylie Jenner to top a list of the 10 most powerful celebrity fashion influencers of 2019.

The former Suits actress, who married Britain's Prince Harry last year, topped the list compiled by online retail shopping tool Lyst, based on data including sales, page views, global media coverage and social media mentions to generate findings on this year's biggest trends.

After releasing her debut fashion collection in association with Smart Works, an organization which helps unemployed women regain the confidence they need to succeed at job interviews, and guest editing the September issue of British Vogue, Meghan toppled make-up mogul Kylie from the top spot down to fourth place.

Call Me By Your Name star Timothee Chalamet came in second place, while Zendaya ranked in third. In fourth was teenage pop music sensation Billie Eilish, with Cardi B slipping from fourth place last year to round out the top five.
