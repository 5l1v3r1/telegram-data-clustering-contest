CM Punk says Big Dog segment on SmackDown was bad creative

20 Nov 2019, 06:04 by Karan Bedi

When Fox hired CM Punk to talk about wrestling and WWE, they knew they were signing up the Voice of the Voiceless. According to Wrestlingnews.co, CM Punk criticized the Big Dog segment on WWE SmackDown that featured Baron Corbin.

In the recent episode of WWE SmackDown, Corbin brought up a man in a dog costume to mock Roman Reigns. The segment was perhaps not very well liked by some WWE fans and was not well received in the internet wrestling community.
