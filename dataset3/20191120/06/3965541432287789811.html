Woman killed in hit-and-run crash while walking along I-5 in Whatcom County

20 Nov 2019, 06:09 by KOMO News Staff

BOW, Wash. -- A woman was struck and killed by a hit-and-run driver while she went into I-5 in Whatcom County Wednesday morning.

The woman went into the southbound lanes at Bow Hill just before 1:30 a.m. when she was hit by a car, according to Trooper Heather Axtman.

That driver sped off, but then the woman was struck again by a second vehicle. The driver of that car remained at the scene, troopers said.

Medics rushed to the crash site, but the woman was pronounced dead.

All lanes of the freeway were closed for a few hours for the crash investigation but have since reopened.

Police are still looking for the first driver. It's unclear why the woman was on the freeway.
