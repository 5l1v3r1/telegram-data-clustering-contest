Popular Wishaw shop worker is this week's Say it with Flowers winner

Maggie Dunne was nominated by her daughter Michelle for all the hard work she does for her family.

20 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Gregor Farquharson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/gregor-farquharson/

A popular Aldi worker who’s known by everyone is this week’s Say It With Flowers winner.

Maggie Dunne was nominated by her daughter Michelle for all the hard work she does for her family.

Michelle said: “My mum home-schooled me since I was 14 and helped me get into university to study mental health nursing.

“She helped support me through having my baby, Tommy, who I was 38 weeks pregnant with when I graduated.”

Maggie also helped Michelle get through uni by helping with essays and living costs.

“She read all my essays through to make sure they were okay and supported me financially. She paid for my car insurance and gave me rent-free accommodation while I was studying.”

Maggie currently works long weekends in Aldi but that doesn’t get her down and she has become a well-known face in the store.

“All her customers love her,” said Michelle.

“When I was getting scans when I was pregnant, everyone would say hello to her when walking through the hospital.”

We helped Michelle surprise her mum at their Newmains home with a beautiful bouquet, courtesy of Pixies Petals.

“She really is just a great mum, she never asks for anything and never complains about anything.

“She is 58 and has sore arms but she is always smiling and making time for people. She does everything for everyone.”
