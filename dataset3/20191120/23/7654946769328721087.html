Student slashed, robbed by group of teens near Bronx high school

20 Nov 2019, 23:17 by Larry Celona and Craig McCarthy

A 16-year-old boy was slashed and robbed outside his Bronx high school on Wednesday, cops said.

The student, who attends DeWitt Clinton High School, was confronted by a group of teens in front of campus around 4:10 p.m. when one of them slashed him in the torso multiple times, according to police.

The group then grabbed his cell phone and took off down W. Mosholu Parkway South, cops said.

The 16-year-old was taken to Jacobi Medical Center where he was expected to be OK, according to the NYPD.
