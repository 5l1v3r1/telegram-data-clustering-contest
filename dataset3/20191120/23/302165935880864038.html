Audi reveals second all-electric car at LA Auto Show (VIDEO)

20 Nov 2019, 23:10

LOS ANGELES, Nov 21 — Audi has taken the wraps off the follow-up of the E-Tron SUV: The E-Tron Sportback.

Earlier this year, Audi’s very first all-electric car — the E-Tron SUV — was made available for purchase; on Tuesday, just a few months after that release, Audi announced that a smaller rendition of the model will be available for order this month: The E-Tron Sportback.

This SUV coupe is the second member of the company’s production EV line and is using the same powertrain at its predecessor. The motor can deliver just over 400hp of power, and the car can reportedly travel up to 277 miles on a single charge — the larger, heavier E-Tron SUV can travel up to about 204 miles.
