Harry Styles fangirls over his love of Shania Twain and we have to stan

20 Nov 2019, 11:41

Harry Styles has declared himself a stan for Shania Twain and honestly, we can’t help but back it.

The Watermelon Sugar star appears on this week’s Later… with Jools Holland to talk about his new album, and revealing his not-so guilty pleasures in the process.

Speaking to the pianist, Harry spoke about his broad taste in music and what inspires him to create his own songs.

Without hesitation, the star confesses: ‘I’m like a massive Shania Twain fan that’s probably my most surprise, but it’s not surprising, she’s amazing.’

Hey, that actually does impress us much!

After all, who has created decades worth of bangers over the years? You’re Still The One? Man! I Feel Like a Woman? They’re absolute. Bangers.

Ahead of the release of his second solo album, Fine Line, Harry also admits that he’s had a lot more fun with the record, after ‘trying too hard’ to be a serious artist on his self-titled debut.

‘I kind of feel that with the first one I was maybe trying to get it wrong,’ he said.

‘I was bowling with the sides up. I feel like I’ve taken them off now.’

He added that his new material was about ‘the typical stuff’ telling Jools it was: ‘[About] being sad, and sex and stuff. It’s what everything is about! A few feelings.’

This week, Harry will be joined on the show by Noel Gallagher’s High Flying Birds, Brittany Howard, FKA Twigs and Abdullah Ibrahi.

Sir Tom Jones will be co-hosting alongside the star on Friday’s episode.

Later… with Jools Holland airs Thursday at 10pm and Friday at 11.15pm on BBC2.
