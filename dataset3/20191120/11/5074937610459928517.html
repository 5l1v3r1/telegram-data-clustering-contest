Fancy Gadam to kick start “Dream” album tour in Koforidua

20 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Simon Asare, GNA

Accra, Nov.19, GNA – Ahmed Muhajid Bello known in showbiz as Fancy Gadam, will begin his “Dream” album tour when he lights up the Culture Center Hall in Koforidua on December 15, 2019.

After the successful launch of his fifth studio album at the Alhaji Aliu Mahama Sports Stadium in Tamale back in October, the multiple award winning act would begin his tour as he looks to connect with his fan base around the country.

“The album is doing very well so far and even exceeding my expectation. So am looking to go on the tour with my fans around the country and I can’t always stay at my comfort zone.

“I’ve performed a couple of times in Koforidua and my fans out there are very amazing and I promise them something electrifying,’’ he told Ghana News Agency.

Fancy Gadam would perform alongside some budding stars including Subziro, De Donzy, John Kizzy, among others.

The Capital would be the next stop of Fancy Gadam’s tour as he set to thrill fans in January 2020.
