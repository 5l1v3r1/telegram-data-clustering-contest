Method Man Headlines Marvel's Voices Anthology One-Shot Comic

20 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Steve Russell

Marvel’s popular podcast series, Marvel’s Voices, is set to further highlight some of the comic book industry’s top storytellers with the release of Marvel’s Voices #1.

The comic will be a one-shot anthology written and drawn by a talented crop of writers and artists including Vita Ayala, Roxane Gay, Brian Stelfreeze and Wu-Tang Clan’s Method Man.

The anthology will have new stories for the X-Men, focusing on events that take place in the wake of House of X and Powers of X. It will also see the return of Moon Girl and Devil Dinosaur and stories featuring some of Marvel’s most notable characters such as the Hulk and Wolverine, Black Panther, Killmonger, She-Hulk, Black Widow and Ant-Man.

“I’m absolutely thrilled and honored to oversee this historic #1 issue celebrating the characters and creators that make up the grand mosaic that is the Marvel Universe, reflecting life as we live it! Not just the world outside our windows, but the fantastic worlds of heart and culture inside those other windows, too,”  editor Chris Robinson said in a statement.

Marvel’s Voices is a podcast series dedicated to telling the stories of diverse creators within the industry, sharing their unique perspectives and experiences. Guests and talent have come from Marvel Comics, live-action television shows, animation, video games and have even included celebrity fans talking about all things Marvel. The podcast’s new season launches in February 2020. Marvel’s Voices #1 is in stores this February.
