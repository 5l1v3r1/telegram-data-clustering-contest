Taapsee Pannu Calls Jacqueline Fernandez And Vicky Kaushal ‘Worst Co-Stars’; Here’s Why

20 Nov 2019, 11:38 by Oshine Koul

On Neha Dhupia’s chat show #NoFilterNeha4, Taapee called her Manmarziyaan co-star Vicky Kaushal and Judwaa 2 actress Jacqueline Fernandez worst actors because of these reasons

Bollywood celebrities have been gracing the chat show of Neha Dhupia’s chat show #NoFilterNeha season 4. After Malaika Arora spilling beans on her dreamy wedding to Shahid Kapoor revealing his favourite actor among Saif Ali Khan and Ranveer Singh, now it’s Taapsee Pannu who has made some interesting statements on the show. The recent actress to appear on Neha Dhupia’s chat show #NoFilterNeha4 was Taapsee Pannu, who revealed who according to her are the worst co-stars.

Replying to which, Taapee called her Manmarziyaan co-star Vicky Kaushal and Judwaa 2 actress Jacqueline Fernandez worst actors and told, “I'll say two of them - Jacqueline because she is so freaking hot and hot body, which I was struggling to match up to it in Judwaa 2 and I was like I just hope I don’t put myself to shame. And the other one which I had a difficult time, which I told also is Vicky because I was always so conscious that I should not lose out to him in the scene because he is just so good”(ALSO READ: Malaika Arora On Choosing Between Her Son And Her Dog: ‘Arhaan Is Everything But My Casper Is Also My Everything’) 

At the same show, when she was asked who according to her would be nowhere if they didn’t have famous parents. She named Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor and said, “I think Harsh because whatever I have seen of him till now, I think it would have been pretty difficult to get a second film after a not so appreciated debut.”

On the work front, basking in the success of Saand Ki Aankh the actress has a few projects lined up. Taapsee will next be seen in Anubhav Sinha’s Thappad and Anurag Kashyap’s Womaniya among others.
