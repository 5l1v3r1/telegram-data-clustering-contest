Entrepreneur Essien advises followers on the steps to follow to make dreams come true

20 Nov 2019, 11:28

- Nigerian entrepreneur Mark Essien has advised people on how to make their dreams come true

- The entrepreneur shared six steps to take for people to achieve their dreams

- Essien said his advice is for people who don't believe they can achieve their wildest dream

A Nigerian entrepreneur Mark Essien has advised people on what to do to achieve their wildest dreams. The entrepreneur shared a series of posts on the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter, teaching people on the steps to take.

Essien shared six tips for people to make their dreams come true. He noted that his post is for people who set their sight on low goals because they don't believe they can achieve their wildest dream.

The entrepreneur explained that the first step is setting the right goal without being afraid of the risk. He then shared the story of a man who bo houses for his children and one benefit more than the other because he was not afraid.
