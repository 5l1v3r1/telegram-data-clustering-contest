Mysuru: Dead body of 55-year-old woman found in drain

20 Nov 2019, 11:06 by Ranjith Kandya

The decomposed body of a 55-year-old woman was found at Lingadevarukoppal on Tuesday late.

The body of one Puttamma is found in a drain and the police suspect that it was a murder.

According to the Police, Puttamma was killed in some other place and her body was disposed in the drain. The police has arrested a person in connection with the incident.
