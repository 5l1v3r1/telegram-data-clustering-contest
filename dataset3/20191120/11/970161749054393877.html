Adele Roberts' girlfriend had awkward Kate Garraway blunder pre-I'm A Celebrity

20 Nov 2019, 11:12

Adele Roberts’ girlfriend Kate Holderness has revealed she had a pretty embarrassing moment when she thought she saw Kate Garraway before the stars were properly introduced in I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here!.

Emphasis on the thought…

Kate and the Big Brother series 3 star were fortunate enough to enjoy Hamilton where Adele’s partner got way too excited when her eyes deceived her and ‘Kate Garraway’ walked into the auditorium.

‘Fun #imaceleb fact: We sat next to @kategarraway at Hamilton a couple of weeks ago,’ Kate wrote on her Instagram. ‘I saw someone who looked like Kate across the theatre & I shouted “OH MY GOD KATE GARRAWAY’S HERE” and then it wasn’t her…and then I turned around & she was sat next to me.

‘Morto’d. Need to control my fangirling.’

Well, Kate will finally get to meet the Good Morning Britain presenter when she eventually heads out to Australia to spend two weeks at Brisbane’s Versace Hotel where the I’m A Celebrity stars’ friends and families get to stay while watching their loved ones sleep on floors with snakes and chow down on pigs’ anuses for the sake of a hot meal.

Adele has come under fire already this week after causing a huge row with Roman Kemp over Jane McDoanld.

The former Loose Women panelist and queen of the cruises has been at the centre of I’m A Celeb’s biggest blow up so far without even dipping her toe into the jungle.

The Radio One DJ’s girlfriend swiftly cleared up where Adele’s obsession with Wakefield’s answer to Celine Dion came from after the reality star bright a photograph of the singer into camp as her luxury item.

‘All the campmates have to have different luxury items, they’re not allowed to double up on anything,’ Kate began.

‘So Adele’s first choice of a family photo was already taken which she didn’t mind because she knew there’d be campmates who’d love a pic of their children in there with them.

‘Second choice, a pillow, was another goner as it’s usually the most requested item. Pretty much everything she wanted after that was already taken too.’
