Pearl barley risotto broth recipe

20 Nov 2019, 11:28 by Angela Hartnett

This hearty, warming broth is essentially a risotto made with pearl barley instead of normal rice. It's packed with vegetables and makes a great healthy dinner. 

Prep time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 30 minutes

SERVES

Four to six

INGREDIENTS

  • Olive oil, for cooking
  • Knob of butter
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery, diced
  • 2 small onions, diced
  • 1 parsnip or turnip, diced
  • 1 thyme sprig
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 150g pearl barley
  • 75ml white wine
  • 650ml chicken stock
  • 50g chopped parsley

METHOD

  1.  Heat the oil and butter in a pan then add the diced vegetables, sautéing them until they have softened. ­Add ­the thyme and bay leaf.
  2. Stir in the pearl barley and add the wine, cooking it until it disappears, deglazing the pan.
  3.  Add the chicken stock and cook for 20 minutes until the pearl barley and vegetables are cooked. The barley should be soft to the bite.
  4. Serve with the chopped parsley and a dash of olive oil.
