SOSAD seeking donations for pop up shop in the Long Walk - Talk of the Town

20 Nov 2019, 11:56 by TOTT Editor

SOSAD Dundalk are seeking donations for their pop up shop which will open in the Long Walk Shopping Centre from December 9th to 14th.

A spokesperson for the suicide prevention group said: “We are now accepting donations of clothes, jewellery, bags, household items, toys etc all in good condition to sell in the pop up shop in aid of SOSAD.

“They can be dropped off at the SOSAD Office at 42 Jocelyn Street between 10am – 6pm or they can be dropped directly to the shop in the Long Walk on Monday December 9th.”
