Seth Meyers reveals the one surprising thing Rihanna is bad at doing

20 Nov 2019, 11:32 by Judy Philander

Cape Town - Late-night television host Seth Meyers has revealed the one thing Rihanna is "bad" at doing.

During an interview on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, Seth discussed what it was like going day drinking with the superstar for a segment on his show, Late Night with Seth Meyers, Fox News reports.

"She was so much fun and she's the nicest person in the world," he said of the popstar.

"It was a blast."

The segment, called Seth & Rihanna Go Day Drinking, featured the two downing shots, beers and cocktails while making jokes and playing games.

The comedian revealed that while shooting the segment, he learnt there’s one thing Rihanna can’t do very well.

"She, at one point, took my phone and took a bunch of pictures," he explained.

"And then, when I went through them and looked at them, I'm like, 'Rihanna's bad at taking pictures'."

Jimmy then held up one of the photos taken by the star and it was indeed "bad", E! Online reports.

"I think that's actually called the Rihanna filter," Seth said of the hilarious drunk selfie. "Yeah, they just put Rihanna in a picture, and then you look happy."
