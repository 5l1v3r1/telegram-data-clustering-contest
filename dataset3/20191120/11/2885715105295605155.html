Viral video: Qawwali event in Haridwar becomes free-for-all over seating

A video from the event shows men angrily charging at each another with chairs and breaking many of the plastic chairs during the fight.

20 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Trends Desk

A qawwali event organised in Haridwar descended into chaos after a fight broke out among the people assembled for the event over the seating arrangements. Footage from the event organised in Haridwar showed people attacking and throwing plastic chairs at each other.

A video from the event shows men angrily charging at each other with chairs, and breaking a lot of the plastic furniture during the fight. Towards the end of the video, a policeman is seen breaking up the fight using a baton.
