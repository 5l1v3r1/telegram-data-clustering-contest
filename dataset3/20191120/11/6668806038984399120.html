Priyanka Chopra is the most searched Indian celebrity online

20 Nov 2019, 11:02 by Filmfare

Priyanka Chopra, an effortless actress, a phenomenal dancer, an impressive singer, ace entrepreneur and a large-hearted philanthropist. Not one, but this global diva dons several hats from time to time. PeeCee definitely has all aspects of being a top public figure in place. The glamorous B-town star has gone ahead and made a place for herself in Hollywood too and that has surely upped her star power. In a recent survey conducted by a US based agency, Priyanka Chopra topped the list of the most searched Indian celebrity online.
