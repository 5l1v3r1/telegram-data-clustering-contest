Are fans trending #ThanksMughals in reaction to Ajay Devgn's 'Surgical Strike' with 'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior' trailer?

20 Nov 2019, 11:24 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The hashtag ‘#ThanksMughals’ hit the top trend in India with netizens sharing photos of the architectural and cultural influence the empire had on the country, during their rule. While this trend seemed to have come out of the blue, some took to social media to state that it was in response to Ajay Devgn and Saif Ali Khan's ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’ trailer that dropped online on Tuesday.

According to social media tweets and reports doing the rounds, the trailer that brings to the big screen an alleged “surgical strike that shook the Mughal empire” prompted people to credit the former rulers for things ranging from the Red Fort to Taj Mahal, and even for introducing biryani.
