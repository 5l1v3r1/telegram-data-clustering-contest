Dundee Ninewells patient charged with attacking medics and threatening to rape nurse

20 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Aidan Smith

A NINEWELLS patient has been accused of attacking medics before allegedly threatening to rape a nurse.

Brian Cureton from Liverpool allegedly made violent threats towards hospital staff on December 14 last year.

The 46-year-old is also charged with ripping out an extension cord and striking a window with it in his ward.

It is claimed that two days later, Cureton made an abusive remark towards nurse Andrea Jones before allegedly punching health care assistant Caroline Foster on the head and body.

On December 17 he allegedly threatened to rape a nurse before removing plug sockets and throwing them.

A hand-held games console was also allegedly thrown at nurse Beverly Stewart.

It is further alleged that in March this year at Carseview Centre, Cureton hit Robbie Dunn over the head with a remote control.

Sheriff Robert Dickson continued Cureton's case without plea until next month.
