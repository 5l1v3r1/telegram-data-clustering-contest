Shenmue 4 is next focus for Yu Suzuki as he vows to ‘never give up’ on series

20 Nov 2019, 11:37

Shenmue 3 is finally out but series creator Yu Suzuki is already talking about making a fourth game, as he writes to thank fans for their backing.

As impossible as it may seem, Shenmue 3 not only exists but is out now and already being played by fans (there are no reviews yet because copies weren’t sent out to press early enough).

But what many may not realise is that Shenmue 3 does not end the story started on the Dreamcast two decades ago. To do that would need more games, which creator Yu Suzuki has written to fans to say he now wants to make.

You can read the entire letter below, but in it Suzuki says, ‘I sincerely hope that, together, we can continue to spin the tale of Ryo and his adventures in Shenmue 4’.
