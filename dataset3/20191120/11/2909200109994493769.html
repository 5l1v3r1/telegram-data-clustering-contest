Because I do deep songs it’s difficult to get artistes to collaborate with me – Akwaboah

20 Nov 2019, 11:28 by MyNewsGH

Highlife musician, Akwaboah says because he does extremely good songs, he finds difficulty in getting artistes with his level of thinking to collaborate with.

He made this known when he spoke on YFM in an interview.

He said “With the kind of songs I do, I struggle to get collaborations. If the song is good, how do you get someone deep like you to work on it?”

Akwaboah said he only does collaborations with deep writers and artistes whose songs move him.
