Glo sponsors Shell Cooperative expo in Lagos

20 Nov 2019, 11:49

Digital Transformation Leader, Globacom, as the official telecommunication partner, showcased its rich bouquet of products and services to visitors at the just concluded Lagos Shell Staff Cooperative Investment and Thrift Society Limited (COOPLAG) Trade Expo.

L-R: President, Shell Staff Cooperative Investment and Thrift Society Limited (Shell Cooplag), Akinrotimi Akintomide, Globacom Representatives, Adekunle Adeleke and Catherine Onotu, with GM, Shell Cooplag, Yinka Omidosu, at the formal opening of this year’s edition of the cooperative’s trade expo in Lagos on Tuesday.

Globacom was the official telecommunications partner of the trade expo, which was held at the Shell Car Park, Marina.

The company made an impression on visitors to the expo with its array of products and services, as its booth, placed in a vintage position at the expo, was the central attraction to visitors at the expo. The booth was manned by bespoke customer care representatives who provided support for Glo customers by giving responses to service inquiries. The booth also had an array of selected smartphones from some of the world’s best phone manufacturers.

In his opening remarks at the expo, Shell COOPLAG President, Mr Akinrotimi Akintomide, expressed appreciation to Globacom and other exhibitors who supported the cooperative to successfully organise the expo.

Globacom Representative, Adekunle Adeleke (left), President, Shell Staff Cooperative Investment and Thrift Society Limited, Akinrotimi Akintomide (centre), Globacom Representative, Catherine Onotu (behind) and Business Development Manager, Mary Akwuobi, Shell Cooplag (2nd right), at the opening of the cooperative’s trade expo in Lagos on Tuesday.

According to him, the idea of the exhibition was to avail Shell COOPLAG members and other categories of visitors the opportunity to get quality products and services from manufacturers and vendors at competitive rates.

Welcoming visitors to the Glo stand, the company's head of Oil and Gas, Enterprise Business, Mr Adekunle Adeleke, said the company was delighted to support the exhibition.

Adeleke reiterated that Globacom seized the occasion of the exhibition to interact with its teeming subscribers and also extended the message of its ongoing Recharge and Win Big promo (My Own Don Beta) to more Nigerians.

The trade expo was rounded off on Friday.
