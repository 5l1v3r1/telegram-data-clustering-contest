Tuna and borlotti bean salad recipe

20 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Angela Hartnett

This tuna and borlotti bean salad is a lovely starter to serve for lunch or dinner. It's very easy to prepare and the only thing that requires a little time is cooking the beans. 

Prep time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 45 minutes

SERVES

Four to six

INGREDIENTS

For the borlotti beans:

  • 200g fresh borlotti beans (podded weight)
  • 1 small onion, peeled
  • 1 stick of celery
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and smashed

For the salad:

  • Oil, for cooking
  • 400g tuna steak
  • 100g green fragolini beans, flat beans or French green beans
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped, and any celery leaves, chopped
  • Pinch of dried chilli flakes
  • 1 bunch parsley, chopped

For the vinaigrette:

  • 100ml olive oil
  • 20ml red wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Lemon zest, to taste

METHOD

  1. Place the podded beans in a pan and cover with cold water. Add the vegetables and garlic and bring to a simmer, cooking until the beans are just soft, about 30-40 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat, season and allow the beans to cool in the cooking liquid, then drain and discard the vegetables.
  3. Meanwhile cook the fragolini beans in salted boiling water, then drain.
  4. Heat a dash of oil in a griddle pan and cook the tuna until nice and pink in the middle – for a steak 4-5cm thick this should take about three minutes on each side.
  5. Whisk together the oil, vinegar and mustard for the vinaigrette and add a little grated garlic and lemon zest to taste.
  6. Toss the beans, red onion and celery stick with the vinaigrette and dried chilli.
  7. Slice the tuna and toss together with the beans. Finish with the chopped parsley and serve at room temperature.
