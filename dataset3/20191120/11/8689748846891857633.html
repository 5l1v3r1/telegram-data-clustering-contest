VIDEO: ‘I’m Only Interested In Footballers Who Play For Big Teams Because Of Their Money’- Fantana

20 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Ghbase New Writer

Fantana has carefully learnt that controversies bolster the brands of celebs in our industry and this is something we think she got from Bullet.

The kind of utterances she makes and other subtle gimmicks she tosses in our face make us know she’s really positioning herself to trend.

In a fresh interview with blogger Eugene Osafo Nkansah of Nkonkonsa news blog, Fantana stated that she’s only interested in footballers so she can squander their money.

And by the way- not just any footballer but those who play for BIG teams like Arsenal, Real Madrid, Barcelona and the other famous clubs scattered across Europe.

When Eugene asked her if money is all that matters to her and not love, the Rufftown signee said she’s definitely into the ‘kwacha’ than love.

Her new stance is a bit surprising though as she recently professed her love for music producer cum artist, Danny Beatz of ‘Medekuku’ fame.
