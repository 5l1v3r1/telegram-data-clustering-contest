Gigi Hadid ‘in touch with’ ex Zayn Malik and are we ready for this? Again?

20 Nov 2019, 11:17

Not long after she split from Tyler Cameron, Gigi Hadid has been chatting with her ex Zayn Malik, according to reports.

Word on the street is the model ‘has always had a soft spot’ for the former One Direction singer, who she began dating in 2015, and ‘they have a lot of history together.’

The pair first started lip-smacking about their potential union in November of 2015, and after a couple on and off moments, they officially announced that they were splitting via Twitter in March 2018.

Sad times for all.

While Zayn has kept his love life on the DL and Gigi was apparently romancing some dude from the Bachelorette, now sources are claiming they’re back in one another’s DMs.

‘They went through a phase where they took time apart and weren’t communicating at all, but they have been in touch recently,’ a source told E! Online.

‘She is supportive of him. They chat here and there but it’s been casual.’

Nothing wrong with a bit of friendly ex communication, right? Well, actually, probably very wrong – but for the purposes of this article we’ll persevere.

Anyway, we shouldn’t get our hopes up for a rekindling of any type so sorry for anyone who is thinking Met Gala 2020 may yet see this genetically-blessed pair join fashionable forces once again.

The source added: ‘They are definitely not getting back together but are on good terms right now.’

Last month Gigi let her shirt do the talking following her split from Tyler, as she wore a jumper emblazoned with ‘Boys lie: goodbye’ on it. Which was quite telling if you ask us (well aware you haven’t asked us, so this information is free).

Gigi was first spotted with Tyler at the beginning of August. They were later seen at the Zendeya x Tommy Hilfiger fashion show for New York Fashion Week, before the reality star accompanied the supermodel to her grandmother’s funeral.

However, a source close to Gigi said in early October that the supermodel is no longer dating Tyler.

Neither party ever confirmed the relationship although Tyler told ET: ‘I mean… that’s just where we’re at.
