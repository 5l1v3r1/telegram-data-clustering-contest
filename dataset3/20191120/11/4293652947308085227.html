Capitals’ Nicklas Backstrom to miss Rangers game with upper-body injury

20 Nov 2019, 11:06 by Sportsnet Staff

Washington Capitals centre Nicklas Backstrom will miss Wednesday game against the New York Rangers with an upper-body injury.

Additionally, the team has placed Nic Dowd (upper body) and Carl Hagelin (upper body) on injured reserve. The team is also awaiting the fate of Garnet Hathaway, who has a hearing Wednesday morning for a spitting incident in Monday’s win over the Ducks.
