Death of widely popular Glenisland man

20 Nov 2019, 11:33

THE death of Padraic (Patrick) Barrett, Glenisland, Castlebar, and late of Derreen, Keenagh, has evoked an immense sense of loss and sadness in the community.

A member of a widely known and respected family, he is deeply regretted by his wife Anne, sons Noel, Fenton, Kevin, Declan, Raymond and Damien, daughter Linda, brothers Jack (Derreen) and Michael (Glenisland), daughters-in-law Anne (Staunton), Siobhan (McManamon), Bernie (Kearns, Manulla) and Yvonne (Heneghan), grandchildren, sister-in-law Judy, nephews, nieces, relatives, neighbours and friends, to whom sympathy is tendered.

His remains wil be reposing this evening (Wednesday) at Glenisland Presbytery from 4 p.m. with removal at 7 p.m. to his residence.

Removal from his residence on Thursday morning at 10.45 a.m. to the Holy Rosary Church, Glenisland, for RequiemÂ Mass at 12 noon with burial afterwards in Glenisland Cemetery.

May his gentle soul rest in peace.
