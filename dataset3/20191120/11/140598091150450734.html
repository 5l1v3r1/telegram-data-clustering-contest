Grilled courgette and sweetcorn salad recipe

20 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Angela Hartnett

This courgette salad is quick and easy to make. It's the perfect starter to serve at dinner if you don't have a lot of time on your hands. 

Prep time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 20 minutes

SERVES

Six as a starter

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 yellow courgettes
  • 2 green courgettes
  • 1 head of sweetcorn
  • 150ml olive oil, plus a splash for frying
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 4 cured anchovies, chopped
  • 2 tsp capers, rinsed lightly
  • 2 tbsp mixture of chopped basil, parsley, marjoram and mint
  • Squeeze of lemon juice and freshly grated zest to serve
  • 200g Westcombe ricotta

METHOD

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/160C gan/Gas 4.
  2. Heat a grill pan. Wash the courgettes and slice them in half lengthways with a large knife.
  3. Depending on the size of your grill pan you may need to cut them in half again. Score the inside of the courgettes in a criss cross pattern. Season them well.
  4. Add some olive oil to the grill pan and grill the courgettes until nicely charred.
  5. When ready, drizzle with a little oil, scatter with the garlic and finish the courgettes off in then oven for about 15 minutes until soft. I always add a splash of water as this stops the courgettes sticking to the tray.
  6. Meanwhile make the salsa. Remove the corn kernels from the husk with a large knife – when they are at the peak of the season there is no need to cook them.
  7. Toss the kernels with the 150ml olive oil, chopped anchovies and capers. Check the seasoning and then add the chopped herbs. Mix well, adding a squeeze of lemon to finish.
  8. To serve, place the cooked courgettes in a dish and cover with the salsa. Scatter over the ricotta and then grate some fresh lemon zest over the whole dish.
