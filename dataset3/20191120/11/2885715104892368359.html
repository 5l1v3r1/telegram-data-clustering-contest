AYUSH Ministry clerk recruitment 2019: The Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS), an autonomous body under Ministry of AYUSH has invited applications for the post of upper and lower division clerk at the group C level. Interested candidates can apply at the official websites, http://www.ccras.nic.in, ayush.gov.in, ccrhindia.nic.in and ccryn.gov.in.

A total of 66 posts are to be filled through this recruitment process. The application process is on and will be closed on December 10, at 5:30 pm. AYUSH Ministry clerk recruitment 2019: Eligibility

Age: To be eligible for the job, candidates should be at least 18 years of age and the upper age limit is capped at 27 years.

Education: For the post of the upper-division clerk (UDC), applicants must have a graduate-level degree while for the post of lower divisional clerk, one must be class 12 pass.

AYUSH Ministry clerk recruitment 2019: Selection procedure

The selection for the post of Upper Division Clerk will be made through a written exam (OMR-based) consisting of multiple-choice questions (MCQs). For LDC, an additional type test of 35 words per minute in English or 30 words per minute in Hindi on a computer will also be held.
