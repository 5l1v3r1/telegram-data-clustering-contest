Every unmissable Black Friday 2019 deal at Beacon Electrical

The incredible sale offers shoppers savings on the latest technology including TVs, washing machines and vacuum cleaners

20 Nov 2019, 11:47 by Charlotte Gosling

Lucky bargain-hunters can pick up some amazing deals from a popular chain of Devon stores as they offer massive discounts in the lead up to Black Friday.

Despite Black Friday officially taking place this year on Friday, November 29, Beacon Electrical - which has five branches across the county, including three in Plymouth - has already started its amazing sale.

With everything from vacuum cleaners to washing machines and televisions on offer, you can kit out your entire home with the latest products for less.

Mark Higgins, director at Beacon Electrical, says: “Our Black Friday campaign is already in full swing here at Beacon Electrical. To avoid a crazy rush on “Black Friday”, which is the last Friday of the month, it is now considered normal to release the offers through the course of nearly the whole month of November.

"This year we have more offers then ever, with unmissable savings on everything from Cordless Vacuum Cleaners, to Flame Effect Electric Fires, to TV’s, and obviously a huge range of kitchen appliances and cookers.

"Several of the highest discounts are on lines which are limited in stock, so we would encourage customers to hurry to take advantage of these deals. It is likely that many of these lines will run out even before the actual “Black Friday” date."

Some of the highlights include: BOSCH Athlet Cordless Cleaner down from £189.99 to £99.99; DIMPLEX Springbourne Flame Effect Fire (RRP £250) now at £139.97; BEXEL slimline larder fridge at only £89.97; BELLING farmhouse style 60cm gas cooker from £689.99 to £499.97; and a HOOVER 7Kg washing machine at only £199.97, from £269.99.
