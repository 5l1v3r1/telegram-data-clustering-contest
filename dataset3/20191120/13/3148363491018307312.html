Former Baltimore Mayor Catherine Pugh Indicted on 11 Federal Charges

Former Baltimore Mayor Catherine Pugh (D) was federally charged Wednesday with 11 counts of fraud, tax evasion, and conspiracy as part of an alleged scheme related to her sales of a self-published children’s book series.
