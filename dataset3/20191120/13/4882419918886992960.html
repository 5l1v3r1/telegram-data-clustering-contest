Neymar in Madrid, invited by Gerard Pique

The Brazilian and Jordi Alba were present at Pique's tennis tournament

20 Nov 2019, 13:58 by David Boti

Neymar was an unexpected protagonist at the Davis Cup as Spain played Russia in Gerard Pique’s tournament. The PSG winger was invited by former Barcelona team-mate Pique to the tournament and he watched Rafa Nadal face Karen Khachanov at the Caja Magica.

The former Barcelona player watched from a VIP booth along with various friends and Pique.

Neymar is enjoying the international break as he finishes his recovery from injury, hanging out with his ‘Toiss’ buddies Gilmar and Gabriel.

Barcelona left back Jordi Alba was another present at the clash between Spain and Russia.
