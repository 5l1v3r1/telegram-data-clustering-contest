TWICE Officially Surpasses 7 Million Total Album Sales Mark, New Japanese Album Tops Oricon and Tower Records Chart

20 Nov 2019, 13:26 by HelloAnon

'Asia's One Top Girl Group' TWICE has achieved yet another milestone as their latest Japanese album recorded 80,563 sales on its first day according to Oricon.

On its first day of release, "&TWICE" ranked #1 on Oricon's Daily Album Chart and all versions of the album dominated the top 3 spots of Tower Records Daily Sales Chart.

With the addition of latest album sales data released by Oricon, TWICE has now surpassed the 7 million mark from previous 6,936,991 cumulative album sales (Korean + Japanese albums), now with a total of 7,017,554.

Congratulations, TWICE!
