David Crosby preps new album, touring in 2020, playing Lockn’

20 Nov 2019, 13:56 by Andrew Sacher

David Crosby and his Sky Trails Band (James Raymond, Mai Leisz, Steve DiStanislao, Jeff Pevar, and Michelle Willis) will hit the road for a spring 2020 tour, and as JamBase reports, they’re promising to play songs from a forthcoming album, so stay tuned for more news on that.

The tour includes two NYC shows: Pier 17 at South Street Seaport on June 5 and 6. It also hits Albany, Long Island, Santa Barbara, Sacramento, Salt Lake City, Austin, Memphis, Toronto (for Toronto Jazz Fest), and more. Most dates go on sale Friday (11/22). (Ticket info for NYC is TBA.)

His tour announcement also reveals that he’s playing Lockn’ 2020, which happens in June (instead of August) and doubles as an 80th birthday celebration for the Grateful Dead’s Phil Lesh.
