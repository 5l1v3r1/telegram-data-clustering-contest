Here Are All The 2020 Grammy Awards Nominations

20 Nov 2019, 13:33 by Chris Mench

The 2020 Grammy Award nominations are finally here, with CBS rolling out the announcements this morning during its morning broadcast. Artists like Lizzo, Lil Nas X, Billie Eilish, H.E.R., Taylor Swift, Lana Del Rey, and Bon Iver picked up multiple nominations.

The Grammys will air on CBS on Sunday, January 26, 2020 at 8 p.m. ET.
