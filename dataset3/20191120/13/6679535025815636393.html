Water level in Periyar dam

20 Nov 2019, 13:48 by Staff Reporter

MADURAI

Water level in Periyar dam stood at 128.40 feet (permissible level is 142 ft.), with an inflow of 1,478 cusecs and a discharge of 1,600 cusecs. In Vaigai dam, the level was 59.65 ft. (71 ft.), with an inflow of 1,971 cusecs and a discharge of 2,028 cusces. Combined storage in Periyar credit was 6,458 mcft.

There was no rainfall recorded in the region in the 24 hours ending at 6 a.m. on Wednesday.
