Rogue, Kagura, Sherria, Sting, and Ichiya will be playable in Fairy Tail video game

20 Nov 2019, 13:14 by My Nintendo News Admin

More additional playable characters have been announced for the Fairy Tail video game today. Those characters are Rogue Cheney, Kagura Mikazuchi, Sherria Blendy, Sting Eucliffe, and Ichiya Vandalay Kotobuki. The Fairy Tail RPG is in development by Gust and is due to be released on the Nintendo Switch platform in 2020. Here’s some gameplay perks announced in the latest edition of Japanese magazine, Famitsu:

  • Unison Raid – A combination magic attack by two characters.
  • Awakening – Drastically enhances a characters abilities for a fixed window of time.
  • Mode Change – A strengthened state that can be used by some characters.
  • Super Magic – Makarov backs you up with Super Magic. Has a fixed chance of being activated through continued magic cooperation.
