Marks and Spencer selling Percy Pig advent calendars for Christmas

M & S operates multiple stores across Birmingham - and plenty more throughout the Midlands

20 Nov 2019, 13:05 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Marks & Spencer is selling Percy Pig advent calendars this Christmas.

The retailer has also launched a Percy Pig competition where you could win a £50 prize.

Marks and Spencer took to social media to share details of the new product and contest.

M & S operates multiple stores across Birmingham - and plenty more throughout the Midlands.

 

The high street giant boasts a huge store just a couple of doors down from the world's biggest Primark here in Birmingham city centre.

On social media, the chain wrote: "Make the Christmas countdown extra sweet with our new Percy Pig Advent Calendar.

"It contains 24 festive-shaped chocolates and a full-sized bag of Percy Pig sweets to open on Christmas day.

"Get your trotters in store today – hurry, they're selling fast!"

 

M&S has also launched a festive Find Rudolf Percy Pig competition.

Shoppers are being asked to keep their eyes peeled for the red-nosed reindeer in specially parked 'Happy Christmas Percy Pig' bags.

If you find Rudolph you'll win a £50 M&S gift voucher.
