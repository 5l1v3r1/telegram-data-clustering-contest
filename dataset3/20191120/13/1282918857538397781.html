Salman Khan recently took to social media and had a hilarious response to friend and Bollywood co-star Ajay Devgn's Tanhaji trailer. Sharing the trailer, he wrote,  "Tanhaji manaji. Ajay Vijay bhava Vijay bhava Vijay bhava . . . @ajaydevgn.". His tweet left many netizens chuckling. In the film, Ajay Devgn will be essaying the role of Tanaji Malusare in the film. On the other hand, Saif Ali Khan will be playing a negative role in the film. It is produced under Ajay Devgn's home production and T-series banner.
