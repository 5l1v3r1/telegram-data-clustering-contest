Sharad Kelkar's Response To Journalist On Chattrapati Shivaji Question Applauded By Fans

20 Nov 2019, 13:12 by Chetna Kapoor

Bollywood News

"We are proud of you", "Well done", and many other fans have applauded Sharad Kelkar's response to a journalist who addressed Chhatrapati Shivaji as 'Shivaji'.

Written By Chetna Kapoor | Mumbai | Updated On: November 20, 2019 13:12 IST

Actor Sharad Kelkar who is all set to portray the role of the Maratha warrior Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in Ajay Devgn's 'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior', corrected a journalist at the trailer launch who addressed him as 'Shivaji'. Kelkar went on to tell the female journalist that it is 'Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj' and not just 'Shivaji'. The journalist immediately apologised and the audience praised and clapped seeing this.
