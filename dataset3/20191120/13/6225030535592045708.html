Kentucky Derby Radar: Three Technique reemerges a winner

20 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Jonathan Lintner

Two and a half months passed between the time Three Technique hopped on the 2020 Kentucky Derby Radar and his Wednesday return to the races. Sidelined due to a reported lung infection, the colt reappeared looking like a serious horse in allowance company at Aqueduct.

Three Technique bided his time at the break, sitting last of five but in the clear, before looping around his rivals through the turn. Jockey Irad Ortiz Jr. had the son of Mr Speaker under wraps essentially the entire furlong of a race that should put Three Technique on the Derby trail.

Off as a 1-9 shot, Three Technique chased opening splits of 23.24 seconds for the quarter mile and 47.22 for the opening half before finishing up in 1:25.65 for 7/8 of a mile. He went widest of four to grab the lead and never came close to a full drive, showing a grinding running style and distinguishable low head carriage. It was 4 1/2 lengths back to second.

NFL Hall of Famer Bill Parcells’ August Dawn Farm campaigns the $180,000 yearling purchase, who was making his fourth start in the $75,000 flight for horses who were non-winners of two races entering.
