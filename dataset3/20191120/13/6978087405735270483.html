EverQuest co-creator Brad McQuaid has passed away

The official Twitter account for Pantheon, the MMO McQuaid was working on until his passing, reported the news last night.

20 Nov 2019, 13:55 by David Craddock

EverQuest co-creator Brad McQuaid has passed away. He was 51 years old. The official Twitter account of Pantheon: Rise of the Fallen, the MMORPG McQuaid had been developing, reported the news last night.
