Neymar: I'm ready to play against Real Madrid

Ney attended Pique's Davis Cup on Tuesday and says he's fit and firing

20 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Sport EN

Neymar attended the Davis Cup tie between Rafa Nadal and Karen Khachanov (Spain v Russia), invited by his pal Gerard Pique to Madrid's Caja Magica.

The Brazilian PSG forward spoke briefly to the media and said that he is fit after his injury.

"I'm perfect, perfectly ready to play against Real Madrid in the Champions League," said Neymar after the match.

PSG face Lille in Ligue 1 at the weekend and then on Nov 26 play Real Madrid at the Santiago Bernabeu.
