Myles Stephenson's Rak-Su announced for Dublin's Academy 2 next April

20 Nov 2019, 13:50 by Ciara Walsh

Band member Myles Stephenson is currently in the I'm A Celeb jungle but will be home before the gig. 

Winners of The X Factor 2017 Rak-Su will play Dublin's Academy 2 on April, 22 2020.

Band member Myles Stephenson is currently in the I'm A Celeb jungle but will be home before the gig. 

Tickets go on sale this Friday at 9am.

The gig announcement follows the release of their highly anticipated EP Rome.

They've also just wrapped up their 2019 tour and have released their brand new single La Bomba.

The boys are best known for having won The X Factor with their own song Dimelo.

Rak-Su dropped the track titled Dimelo featuring Naughty Boy and Wyclef Jean the following year.

The boys even went on to tour with Olly Murs.

Band member Myles Stephenson has been making a name for himself on this year's I'm A Celeb.
