David Beckham on full tourist mode as he gets selfie at Berlin Wall

20 Nov 2019, 13:52

David Beckham is the king of jet-setting after he was spotted walking the streets of Berlin, before hot-footing it back to London to party with Ninja.

The former Manchester United footballer was seen taking in the sights of the German capital on Monday night, following a swanky Tudor watch party.

At one point, the 44-year-old appeared to stage his own photoshoot by the Berlin wall, before squatting down to take selfies.

Sharing a snap on his Instagram page, the dad-of-four wrote: ‘Flying visit to Berlin – Couldn’t resist a quick stop to see this iconic image… #BerlinWall.’

Victoria Beckham’s husband looked seriously smart for the evening, rocking a grey tweed suit with shiny black shoes.

But he didn’t get to spend too much time being a tourist, as he flew straight back to the UK to hit up the launch of the Adidas store in London.

David headed straight for Oxford Street to celebrate the opening of the new show, alongside gaming legend Ninja.

But, before the England ace got too comfortable, he was off again, flying to New York to headline the UN Summit on World Children’s Day, with Millie Bobby Brown.

Does the man ever rest?

The pair will be appearing at a global summit, demanding action over the protection and promotion of child rights with UNICEF.

And, in between all this jet-setting, he still found time to treat his brood to a firework display as they spent bonfire night as a family, in London.

David recently opened up about being on dad duties, admitting he’s not that fussed that eldest son Brooklyn regularly takes clothes from his wardrobe.

‘I see it as a good thing,’ he told Evening Standard. ‘I must be doing something pretty cool for a 20-year-old to be wanting to borrow my stuff.

‘Definitely. But they [the kids] are not as tidy as me. So when I walk into my closet I can see [that], “Right OK… he [Brooklyn] has had those jeans, that jumper.”

‘The other day he FaceTimed me from a restaurant. And I was like, “I have seen that suit somewhere before.” And I was like, “Mate, I have just bought that suit, I’ve not even worn it.”’

He’s not a regular dad, he’s a cool dad…
