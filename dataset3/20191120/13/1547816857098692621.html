‘Love You Eternally’ Pens Aishwarya Rai Bachchan On Her Father’s Birth Anniversary; VIEW POST

20 Nov 2019, 13:29 by Amruta Patel

After Aaradhya Bachchan’s 8th birth anniversary, today Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan remembered the actress’ father Krishnaraj Rai on his birth anniversary. The duo posted an emotional post on their respective Instagram handles, along with Late Krishnaraj Rai’s picture

Just four days ago we saw the Bachchan family celebrating Aaradhya Bachchan’s 8th birthday with much pomp and grandeur. The evening hosted by Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan, had the biggies of Bollywood attending Aaradhya’s birthday bash with their kids. While this was four days ago, today is beauty queen’s late father Krishnaraj Rai’s birth anniversary. The actress’ father passed away on Mach 18, 2017, due to severe illness and as she remembers him today on his birth anniversary, she penned a heartfelt post on her Instagram handle. 

Posing by a portrait of her father with mother Vrinda Rai and daughter Aaradhya, she wrote, “We LOVE YOU ETERNALLY” Aishwarya’s hubby- actor Abhishek Bachchan too took to his Instagram handle shared a picture of his father-in-law and wrote, “Happy Birthday Dad. Miss you.” Krishnaraj Rai was an Army biologist by profession. Like each year, the Fanney Khan actress has been snapped arriving at an event supporting a noble cause. On her 46th birthday Aishwarya had taken to her Instagram account thanking her parents. Sharing a picture of them she thanked them in her post, her caption read, “Thank YOU I LOVE YOU ETERNALLY..UNCONDITIONALLY..”
