Corrie's Andy Whyment and Eastender's Cliff Parisi revealed as new I’m A Celeb campmates on This Morning

Soap stars Andy Whyment and Cliff Parisi have been announced as the two latecomers to take part in the survival game show Down Under.

20 Nov 2019, 13:49 by Charlotte Smith

Two new celebs are set to head into the I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here jungle, it has been revealed on This Morning.

Soap stars Andy Whyment and Cliff Parisi have been announced as the two latecomers to be taking part in the survival game show Down Under.

The duo will be joining fellow campmates this week now Snake Rock has now reunited with the main camp.

Reality TV favourites and former I'm A Celeb campmates Vicki Pattison and Fern McCann appeared on the ITV daytime show to share the news with Phil and Holly earlier today.

Best known for playing the lovable mechanic Minty Peterson, Cliff Parisi, 59, played in BBC One's Eastenders between 2002 and 2010.

Meanwhile, Coronation Street fans will recognise Andy Whyment, 38, for his role as factory worker Kirk Sutherland.

Prior to their arrival in the camp, Andy and Cliff posed in a fun photoshoot with various Ozzy wildlife.

The soap actors can be see holding a koala and feeding a kangaroo with big smiles on their faces.

An ITV source said: "With Cliff and Andy we have got two big soap names joining the jungle and they’ll be a lot of fun."

"Cliff is the final piece in the puzzle and comes from two massive BBC shows.

"It will be interesting to see if he sees the funny side of things when he has been in there a while and the hunger and tiredness kicks in."

They will be making their jungle debut alongside current contestants Caitlyn Jenner, Kate Garraway, Adele Roberts, Nadine Coyle, Jacqueline Jossa, Roman Kemp, Ian Wright, James Haskall, Myles Stephenson and Andrew Maxwell.

I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here returns tonight at 9pm on ITV.
