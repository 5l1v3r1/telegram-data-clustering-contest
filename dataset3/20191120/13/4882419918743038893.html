Luis Enrique's return to Spain duty will be against Holland and Germany

The Santiago Bernabeu is likely to be the venue where Spain host Germany

20 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Ramón Fuentes

Luis Enrique’s first matches for Spain will be friendlies against Holland and Germany ahead of Euro 2020.

The first will take place in the Netherlands, likely at the Amsterdam Arena, and the second will be in Spain, with the Santiago Bernabeu the most likely place for the game.

That is usually where Spain like to host bigger matches and the clash with Germany is fitting of that arena.

The Spanish national team is living in a controversial moment with Robert Moreno’s replacement by Lucho on Tuesday.

Former coach Moreno will release a statement on Wednesday telling his side of the story.
