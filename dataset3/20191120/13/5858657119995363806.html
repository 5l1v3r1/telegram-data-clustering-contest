Bad drivers, bad habits: Canadians say not signaling worst problem on the roads

20 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Jonathan Szekeres and Lauren Boothby

Summary

Almost half of Canadians say drivers are getting worse in their communities

Not signalling tops the list of bad driving behaviours in a new survey

VANCOUVER (NEWS 1130) – Canadians are getting worse at driving – at least that’s what more than half of people are saying in a new survey.

What’s the biggest problem on the roads?

A recent poll from Research Co. suggests drivers not using their signal when turning is topping the list of regrettable behaviour behind the wheel. Three-in-five Canadians (61 per cent) say they’ve seen someone turn without using their signal over the past month, down 10 points from last year.
