Gerard Pique: I'm excited that Luis Enrique is back with Spain

The Barcelona defender says he doesn't know first hand what has happened

20 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Sport EN

Gerard Pique says he's excited by the return of Luis Enrique as Spain coach in an interview with Marca.

"I don't know what happened, I haven't spoken with my Barca's team-mates because they played Monday against Romania," said Pique. "All that I know is what I've read in the media and I suppose we'll know more in a few days.

"All I can say is that I'm really excited that Luis Enrique is back as Spain coach. All that's gone on, I suspect only a few will know."

Pique, working on his tennis tournament, confirmed they are looking at the possibility of keeping the Davis Cup in Madrid an extra year, until 2021.
