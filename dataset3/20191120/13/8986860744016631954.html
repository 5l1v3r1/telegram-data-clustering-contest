Volta Region pulls out of all future TV3’s GMB pageant after Enam was ‘cheated’ by the 2019 judges

20 Nov 2019, 13:13 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

TV3’s Ghana’s Most Beautiful beauty pageant 2019 came off some weeks ago with Ekua emerging as the winner of the competition while the fans’ favorite Enam from the Volta Region came in as the first runner up.

After the show, many people who watched the show from the beginning and have been following for a long time came out to say that Enam was robbed in broad daylight because she deserved the crown than Ekua or the other contestants.

Well, some traditional leaders from the Some Traditional Area in the Volta Region led by the Chief of the have released a statement to address the issue of the stolen verdict.

According to the report sighted by Ghpage.com, the traditional leaders stated that after deliberation with some people and stakeholders, they wish to announce that henceforth no one from their traditional area would participate in any pageant organized by TV3.
