BREAKING: Major traffic tailbacks on M1 following vehicle fire

Roads

20 Nov 2019, 13:36 by David Lynch

Emergency services are dealing with a vehicle fire on the M1 motorway southbound between Junction 5 Balbriggan South and Junction 4 Donabate.

The left lane is blocked and delays are backing up on approach.

According to reports online traffic is "bumper to bumper".
