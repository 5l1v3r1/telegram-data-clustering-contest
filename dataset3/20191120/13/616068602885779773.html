Poppy thief on camera

20 Nov 2019, 13:39 by Colin Dacre

The Penticton RCMP is distributing surveillance images of a man who stole a poppy donation box the day before Remembrance Day. 

A man walked into the Penticton Safeway on Nov. 10 at 4:30 p.m. and took off with a poppy donation box while it was temporarily unattended. 

Video surveillance captured a clear image of the suspect. 

Anyone with information on the man’s identity is asked to contact Const. Ritcey of the Penticton RCMP, 250- 492-4300, and quote file 2019-19786. Alternatively, one can call Crime Stoppers 1-800- 222-8477.
