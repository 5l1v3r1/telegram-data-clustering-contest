Winter Games at Farnell

20 Nov 2019, 09:30 by David Manners

Farnell is launching its Winter Games competition, giving customers access to weekly offers from suppliers and online games where participants can win e-vouchers as well as the Winter Games Grand Prize, a Go Pro Max 360° Camera.

The Winter Games consists of three Olympic sport disciplines including ice hockey, bobsleigh and curling.

The competition is available in 20 countries across Europe; three weekly prizes will be awarded in each country as well as the Grand Prize for all European competitors.

In addition to the prizes available in the Winter Games, Farnell is also supporting customers with exclusive offers from brands including TE Connectivity, Microchip, Renesas, Silicon Labs, Tektronix and many more.
