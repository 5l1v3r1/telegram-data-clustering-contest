WATCH: Cardi B Takes On Vogue's 73 Questions With Daughter Kulture

20 Nov 2019, 09:10 by Tara Walsh

73 questions with Cardi B

Cardi B is the latest celebrity to take on Vogue's 73 questions and she's joined by her daughter Kulture.

The rapper opens up about motherhood, her new album and her relationship with husband Offset.

The Migos rapper also makes an appearance in the video where he gushes over his wife.

Cardi opens the front door with her daughter asleep in her arms.

Instead of filming at her own home, she decided to film in one of her favourite places - her grandmother's home.

She loves that it's, "always packed with family... It's always distracting me from the chaos of the world and work."

Motherhood

The Bodak Yellow rapper opens up about family life and motherhood.

She says the biggest lesson she's learned from being a mother is that, "you're never ready on time."

"Things don't go as you plan, never."

The Hustlers star is asked what she'd like to teach her daughter, "Don't worry what people say. And to dream big and follow it."

She goes on to admit, "The most underrated part of motherhood is it's hard. You know what I'm saying?"

"People think that it's easy, but it's hard. We deserve more Mother's Day."

Offset

Cardi gets personal about her relationship with husband Offset during the interview.

She's asked what her favourite quality about him is and she responds, "Everything."

"I love that man. I wanna lick him up and down everyday."

She goes on, "I love that I feel secure with him. Protected and financially always."

"He takes care of home, he takes care of me."

Cardi takes out her phone and proceeds to Face Time her other half.

When Offset answers, the interviewer asks him, "What's the best and worst habit that Cardi has?"

He responds, "Worst habit she got is being on her phone for 35,000 hours every single day..."

She shrugs it off, "Okay, what's my best habit?" He says, "Raising Kulture. She's a good mom. She's doing a great job."

Cardi is then asked what she believes is Offset's best habit.

She whispers, "His penis is his best habit. His sex is his best habit."
