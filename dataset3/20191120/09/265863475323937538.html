Tiny Harris Writes Sweet Note For Joseline Hernandez After She Posted This Picture — Hazel-E Decided To Shade Her With This Comment

20 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Mel Walker

It seems like Joseline Hernandez was having the time of her life, being with her partner, DJ Ballistic Beats, and constantly receiving admiration from her fans.

All looks well for the former Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta star who recently posted a photo of herself and her boyfriend, posing together in Miami’s Keystone Island district.

The femcee and reality TV star expressed her joy by adding a caption to the picture, saying, “Everything on point.”

Naturally, it did not take long for the numerous fans of Hernandez to appreciate the photograph, and many of her followers were soon commenting appraisals and positive remarks.

Everything was fine until a peculiar comment by Hazel-E confused a lot of the adoring fans.

Hazel-E left a remark, saying “Mom & Dad,” an expression, which is normally used to describe two partners as the perfect couple.

However, Hernandez’s fans did not take the comment in a positive way, but the very opposite — many were angered by the statement and even took it in a totally different way.

For some, what Hazel-E had written was a sly reference to the fact that 33-year-old Hernandez and DJ Ballistic Beats look old.

As a result, Hazel-E had to endure some backlash from Hernandez’s followers, who, in a wave of comments, reminded her that she was the same age as Joseline, and some even remarked that Hazel-E even looked older.

One person told Stevie J’s ex: “Wait… Is that a wedding band on his finger?🤔 I saw Joseline with a wedding ring on too. Did y’all got married on the down-low? Love y’all as couple thou. 🔥😍💯, by the way, you look older than them frfr lbvs.🤣😣💯”

Another commenter shared: “you going to call someone way younger than you mom and dad lol wake up old lady.”

This backer stated: “Old as her how in the hell😂🤣😂 leave my princess lone 😂🤣🤣”

So far, Hernandez has not said a word about the argument but preferred to stay private, and it looks like she and her boyfriend are doing just fine.

Tiny Harris did leave a sweet comment that read: “Cute!!! 😘”

Their fans will have the opportunity to watch the couple in the next season of We TV’s Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars, as was confirmed in August.
