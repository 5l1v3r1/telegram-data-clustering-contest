SFR brings smart home offering to French customers

20 Nov 2019, 09:42

SFR has started selling a smart home automation range that comes with a dedicated app, featuring products from Legrand, Netatmo and Nest, among others. Grouped under the 'SFR Home' brand, the new offering has its own website and includes three main product bundles. The entry-level pack is priced at EUR 69, while the other two require a monthly subscription in addition to the one-off cost of the equipment (EUR 149).

Named Connected Pack ('Pack Connecte'), the entry-level product bundle combines a Wi-Fi camera, a connected colour bulb and a smart plug. SFR said that its internet customers will also have the option to purchase an 'Alert' add-on for EUR 2 per month, which saves video recording from the camera for up to 30 days and sends a message to the smartphone in case of intrusion.

As announced in July, households equipped with the operator's latest STB, SFR Box 8, will be able to control connected products using voice commands via the 'OK SFR' assistant.
