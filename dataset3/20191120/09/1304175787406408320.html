Grand Millennium Business Bay announces the appointment of Hussein Gamaleldin as Director of Sales and Marketing

20 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Tatiana Rokou

A graduate degree holder in Hotel Management and Hotel Operations, Hussein began his career in Grand Hyatt Cairo and then moved to various international brands like Starwood, Marriott, The Address Hotels & Resorts, the IHG group, Oberoi & more.

Grand Millennium Business Bay, luxurious 5-star property on Marasi Drive, announced the appointment of Hussein Gamaleldin as Director of Sales and Marketing of the hotel.

Bringing over 18 years of extensive experience in sales & marketing in the hospitality industry, Hussein will play a pivotal role in developing key sales & marketing strategies to drive commercial productivity and growth for Grand Millennium Business Bay

A graduate degree holder in Hotel Management and Hotel Operations, Hussein began his career in Grand Hyatt Cairo and then moved to various international brands like Starwood, Marriott, The Address Hotels & Resorts, the IHG group, Oberoi & more.

Commenting on his appointment, Hussein said: “I am proud to join the Grand Millennium Business Bay family and committed to achieving new heights of success for the hotel. I look forward to attracting new markets for Grand Millennium Business Bay and growing its reputation as one of the leading hotels in the region.”

Enjoying an enviable location on Marasi Drive with beautiful views of Dubai Water Canal and the city, Grand Millennium Business Bay includes 251 rooms, 11 fully equipped meeting rooms brimming with natural daylight, a 544 square metre ballroom to accommodate more than 600 guests, a spa, health club and swimming pool. The ‘Beau Rivage Bistro’ is the hotel’s European-inspired outlet which opens out onto the canal waterfront while The ‘Podium’ is the property’s international all-day-dining restaurant. The Grand Millennium Business Bay is the second Grand Millennium property to open in the vibrant city of Dubai and is the preferred choice of stay for both business & leisure guests.
