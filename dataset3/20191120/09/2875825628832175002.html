Garda helicopter deployed in manhunt for burglary suspects in Kilkenny

One man was detained at the scene before a subsequent search for the remaining men was launched

20 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Ian Mangan

A garda helicopter was used to track down two burglary suspects in Co. Kilkenny last night.

One man was detained at the scene of the burglary in Johnswell before a subsequent search for the remaining men was launched.

According to KCLR96fm, a garda helicopter was deployed to assist local authorities in the manhunt.

The two men were found a couple of hours later and all three were taken to Kilkenny Garda station for questioning.

Tools and other building equipment were also reportedly recovered.
