OMG, Elli AvrRam Rips Off Manoj Bajpayee's Moustache; Actor Cries Out In Pain – VIDEO

20 Nov 2019, 09:19 by Gretel Sequeira

Elli AvrRam recently took to her social media to treat fans with an interesting video with her having fun with Manoj Bajpayee. The actress is caught ripping off Bajpayee’s moustache, and the actor’s reaction to it is epic

Actress Elli AvrRam rose to fame after being a contestant on the most popular reality TV show Bigg Boss. AvrRam was a contestant on Bigg Boss 7 and is winning hearts since then. Elli marked her debut on the big-screen after bagging a lead role in Saurabh Varma’s comedy-thriller Mickey Virus. As the actress is well-known for her fun and jovial nature, Elli took to her social media to share a throwback video of her on-sets fun with co-star Manoj Bajpayee. In the interesting video, AvrRam is snapped ripping off Bajpayee’s moustache, and his reaction to it is epic.

The Mickey Virus actress recently took to her Instagram to share a throwback video featuring Manoj Bajpayee and herself. While Elli shared the fun and interesting video on social media, the video is amusing and entertaining as co-actors Bajpayee and AvrRam  indulge into some ‘Masti.’ In the video, Elli AvrRam is caught ripping off Manoj Bajpayee’s moustache and the actor’s reaction to it is epic. While Manoj is snapped giving Elli tips and instructions to rip off his moustache, AvrRam looks like a naughty child excited to have some fun on the sets. As Elli rips off the ‘Family Man’ actor’s moustache, the actor screams out loud crying in pain.
