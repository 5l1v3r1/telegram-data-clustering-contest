Mauricio Pochettino: Dele Alli praises Argentine after losing Tottenham job to Mourinho

20 Nov 2019, 09:15

- Mauricio Pochettino has been sacked by Premier League giants Tottenham

- The Argentine has also been replaced with former Chelsea and Man United boss Jose Mourinho

- Tottenham star Dele Alli stated that he is grateful to Mauricio Pochettino for handing him his debut

Tottenham forward Dele Alli has praised Argentine football manager Mauricio Pochettino less than 24 hours after he was sacked by the Premier League side.

Spurs chiefs took to social media on Tuesday night, November 19, to announce the departure of Mauricio Pochettino after almost six years at the club.

The club's chiefs were not happy with Tottenham's current 14th position on the Premier League standings which masterminded their decision to fire Pochettino.

Former Chelsea and Manchester United manager Jose Mourinho has been appointed as the new coach of Spurs.
