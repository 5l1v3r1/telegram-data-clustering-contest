This Christmas, give the gift of everyone's favorite Irish goodies

20 Nov 2019, 09:29 by In partnership with

If you're looking for the perfect Christmas gift for someone who's feeling homesick for Ireland, or if you're feeling a little homesick yourself, these Irish care packages offer instant comfort and joy. 

There's something about the holidays that can make you especially wistful for the comforts of home. For Irish people, that includes an array of Cadbury's chocolates, Tayto crisps, Barry's or Lyon's tea, Butlers drinking chocolate, McVitie's biscuits, Christmas pudding, and some Lucozade for when your stomach needs a little break from all the indulgence.  

Irish care package company CraicPack has launched two new baskets with all the festive items for family, friends, and loved ones who can’t make it home for the holidays. They contain all of the delicious little things that add up to make a real Irish Christmas. 

The Advent Box contains Irish goodies for the Christmas countdown. It includes a Cadbury Advent Calendar, mince pies, limited edition Cadbury chocolates, and, of course, that crucial Lucozade to get you through all the Christmas parties over the next two months.

The Irish Christmas Box, which has everything you need for Christmas Day, contains Cadbury Heroes and Roses, Bisto Gravy, hot chocolate, and Christmas pudding, as well as a handwritten Christmas card for a personal touch.

If you can't decide which one to get, you can order the Advent Box and the Irish Christmas Box as a combination deal. 

“Not being home for Christmas is incredibly difficult,” said the team at CraicPack. “We want to make it easier for Irish people to show their loved ones abroad that they are thinking of them this Christmas season.”

It's a simple but powerful gesture - a little taste of home or nostalgia at a time when it's needed most. 

CraicPack also offers a monthly subscription service if you want to give someone the gift of Irish goodies year-round.
