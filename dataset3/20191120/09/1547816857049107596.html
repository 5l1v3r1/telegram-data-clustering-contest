Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior Trailer: Salman Khan Hails Ajay Devgn’s Performance, Says ‘Tanhaji Manaji’

20 Nov 2019, 09:04 by Divya Ramnani

Salman Khan is mighty impressed by the trailer of Ajay Devgn’s Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior, check out his reaction

The recently-released trailer of Ajay Devgn’s Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior has taken the internet by storm. Right from its spectacular visuals and VFX to all the remarkable performances; netizens can’t stop raving praises over this upcoming period drama. A lot of celebrities, too, have lauded Ajay Devgn for nailing Tanaji Malusare’s role with utmost perfection. The list also includes his Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam co-star and good friend, Salman Khan, who seems to be equally blown away by the trailer.

A day after the trailer of Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior made it to the internet; the Dabangg actor took to his Twitter account and hailed Ajay Devgn’s performance in his style. Along with sharing the link, Khan wrote, “Tanhaji manaji.. Ajay vijay bhava vijay bhava vijay bhava . . . @ajaydevgn.” Well, Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior is a special film to Ajay Devgn for numerous reasons. Firstly, it’s his 100th Bollywood, and then it also reunites the actor with his wife Kajol and Omkara co-star Saif Ali Khan on the big screen.
