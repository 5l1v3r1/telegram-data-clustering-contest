Nasarawa first lady tongue-tied as she reads speech, Twitter user mocks her for it (video)

20 Nov 2019, 09:09

- First lady of Nasarawa state Hajiya Salifat has been made fun of on social media

- The first lady was mocked for being unable to read a speech at an event articulately

- A Nigerian man identified simply as Nwabulibu shared a video of Salifat reading the speech

Wife of Governor Abdullahi Sule of Nasarawa state, Hajiya Salifat, has been mocked on social media by Nigerians who were disappointed by her performance at an event that took place in the state.

In a video, the first lady of Nasarawa was seen reading a speech at the event. At the podium, it looked and sounded like she had difficulty reading the words on her written speech.

The video of the governor's wife reading her speech with difficulty has made rounds on social media.
