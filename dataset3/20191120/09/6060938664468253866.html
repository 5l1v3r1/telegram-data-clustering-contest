Before Kartik Aaryan's take on 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare'; here's a viral video of Russian dancers reprising the Govinda-Raveena Tandon hit

20 Nov 2019, 09:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The makers of 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' which stars Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday in lead roles are all set to release the film's second song 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare' which is a reprised version of Govinda-Raveena Tandon hit track from the 1998 release 'Dulhe Raja'.

The first track from the film 'Dheeme Dheeme' has already grabbed the number one spot in several music charts, fans are eager to see the millennial trio step into the shoes of Govinda and Raveena iconic song.

But what grabbed our attention was this video of a group of Russian dancers, who managed to give the hit song their own twist even before Kartik, Bhumi or Ananya could flaunt their moves. The video of the Russian group dancing on the tunes of 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare' which came out last year has grabbed everyone's attention now and is going viral on the internet.
