Memorial to be held Wednesday to honor life of Lynden police chief

20 Nov 2019, 09:28 by KOMO News Staff

BELLINGHAM, Wash. -- A memorial service was held Wednesday to honor Michael Knapp, the interim Lynden police chief who died on November 6 after being hit by a truck.

A law enforcement procession to honor Knapp began at 11:30 a.m. at the Phillip 66 Soccer Fields in Bellingham at 5238 Northwest Avenue.

Only agencies where Knapp was employed were allowed to participate in the procession. Organizers said the public could pay their respects along Meridian Street between Stuart and Westerly Road.
