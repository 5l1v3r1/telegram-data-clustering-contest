PARTYNEXTDOOR Purportedly Dropping New Music on Friday

Could ‘Club Atlantis’ finally be releasing?

20 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Torsten Ingvaldsen

It seems the return of PARTYNEXTDOOR is right around the corner. Just yesterday the OVO-signed R&B heavyweight shared an Instagram post with the caption “Friday,” alluding to his return to music after a nearly two-year hiatus following the release of 2017’s Seven Days. This past August, Drake promised that the crooning singer-songwriter had new music coming.

Rumored to be titled Club Atlantis, the followup album to 2016’s PARTNEXYDOOR 3 has been one of the most highly-anticipated released under the OVO umbrella. In 2017 he shared “I’m taking my time with this album, It’s straight party & unapologetic.” A year later he shared that his “fourth album finna drop I been yelling timber before wiggin’s.” Since then, PND has taken a break from social media, driving him into an enigmatic state that has fans craving for updates. He most recently teased snippets of a collaboration with Rihanna in March.
