Lizzie and Gordo Are Officially Reuniting for the 'Lizzie McGuire' Reboot

20 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Rachel Epstein

Please stop everything you're doing: Hilary Duff just revealed that Adam Lamberg, a.k.a. Gordo, is officially joining the cast of the Disney+ Lizzie McGuire reboot. In other words, I'm! Not! Okay!

Duff made the official announcement on Instagram with a picture of her and Lamberg. She captioned the photo, "I feel so basic saying something that’s been shouted at me almost my whole life buuuut......hey now, hey now.✨"
