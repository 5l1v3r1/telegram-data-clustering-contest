Police investigating after Virginia McDonald's cashier assaulted at drive-thru window

20 Nov 2019, 17:03

HENRICO, Va. -- Police are investigating after a Virginia McDonald's cashier was assaulted by two people earlier this month.

The Henrico Police Department said it happened on Nov. 9 in the 2300 block of Mechanicsville Turnpike.

Security footage shows the employee being assaulted by two people while working the drive-thru.

If you know who they are, call Henrico Police 804-501-5000 or Crime Stoppers 804-780-1000.
