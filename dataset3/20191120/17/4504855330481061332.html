Driver transported after crashing into Lauderhill Swap Shop

20 Nov 2019, 17:17 by 7 News WSVN

LAUDERHILL, FLA. (WSVN) - A man had to be transported to the hospital after his SUV made a crashing stop into the Swap Shop in Lauderhill.

Lauderhill Fire Rescue crews responded to the flea market located in the area of Northwest 31st Avenue and West Sunrise Boulevard at around 11 a.m., Wednesday.
