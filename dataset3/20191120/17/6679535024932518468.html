Irinjalakuda leading in Thrissur district school arts festival

Thrissur East in second position with 463 points, and Chalakudy sub-district is close behind in third position with 438 points

20 Nov 2019, 17:37 by Special Correspondent

Irinjalakuda sub district is leading in Thrissur Revenue District Arts Festival with 470 points, when results of 177 events were announced on Wednesday, the second day of the four-day festival. The fest is being conducted at various venues in Guruvayur,

While Thrissur East is in second position with 463 points, Chalakudy sub-district is in third position with 438 points.

Young artists enthralled the audience in all the six venues of the art festival.

Sivada Govind of the Thrissur Vivekodayam Girls High School, won first prize in all the three events in the UP School category she participated in. The seventh standard student won first with A grade in Katha Kadanam in Sanskrit, Gadya Parayanam and Group dance.

T.S. Hemand of St. Thomas Thope Higher Secondary School, Thrissur, too won three firsts with A grade. The young artist reached first in Pencil Drawing, Oil painting and water colour painting. He is Plus Two students in Humanities.

P.R. Niranjana won first with A grade in Kathakali music competition in the art festival. She has been winning first prize in sub-district and district level for the last four consecutive times. Her parents interest in Kathakali drew her to the world of Kathakali music. She has been practising Kathakali music under Kottakkal Santhosh for the last five years. In addition to Kathakali music, she has made her presence in Ashtapadi, Sanskrit recitation, Thiruvathirakali, Group music, and Urdu recitation. She is a Plus One student in Science.
