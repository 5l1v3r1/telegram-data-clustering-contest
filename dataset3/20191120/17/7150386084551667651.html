Jharkhand Assembly Polls 2019: BJP Releases Fifth List Comprising 8 Candidates

Other candidates who will fight the assembly polls include Laxman Nayak from Gomiya, Vikram Pandey from Tundi, Devendra Singh from Jamshedpur West and Samri Lal from Kanke.

20 Nov 2019, 17:39

New Delhi: Four days after releasing the list of 40 star campaigners, the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) on Wednesday released the fifth list of candidates for the upcoming Jharkhand Assembly polls. As per the list, Beni Prasad Gupta will contest from Pakaur, Loknath Mehto from Barkagaon, Rananjay Kumar from Ramgarh and Pradeep Sahu to contest from Dumri.

Other candidates who will fight the assembly polls include Laxman Nayak from Gomiya, Vikram Pandey from Tundi, Devendra Singh from Jamshedpur West and Samri Lal from Kanke.
