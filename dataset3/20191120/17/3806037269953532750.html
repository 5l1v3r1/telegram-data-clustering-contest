Here's Everything Coming to & Leaving Netflix in December 2019

Henry Cavill’s ‘The Witcher’ finally arrives.

20 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Isaac Rouse

Netflix has announced its programming roster for December 2019, highlighting the release of highly-anticipated Netflix Originals such as The Witcher, The Confession Killer, and Lost in Space: Season 2. There will also be some classics arriving on the platform, like Spike Lee‘s Malcolm X,  the entire Austin Powers trilogy, and Tomorrow Never Dies.

Consequently, a handful of films and TV shows are also leaving Netflix, such as both seasons of Planet Earth, Thor: Ragnarok, and the entire Rocky franchise. Take a full list at the list of movies and TV shows below.

In other entertainment news, Entertainment Weekly released special covers and new photos to celebrate Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker.
