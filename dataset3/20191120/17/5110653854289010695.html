Le'Veon Bell upset with NFL for excessive drug tests with 'dirty ass needles'

20 Nov 2019, 17:16 by Jordan Heck

Le'Veon Bell is upset with the NFL, and it may land him in some trouble.

The Jets running back expressed frustration Wednesday after claimingthe league has randomly drug tested him five times in the past 10 weeks. Bell said he's not going to cooperate if he gets tested again.
