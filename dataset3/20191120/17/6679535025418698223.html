Engineering student goes missing in lake

20 Nov 2019, 17:02 by Staff Reporter

A second-year engineering student went missing in Bommalachervu lake at Piglipur village in Abdullapurmet here on Wednesday afternoon.

Police suspect that Vishwakarma Sandeep Kumar (19) drowned. A team of National Disaster Response Force, Firefighters and local fishermen carried out a search, but could not trace the body. The rescue mission will continue on Thursday too.

Kumar was pursuing his second-year mechanical engineering from St. Mary’s College of Engineering and Technology in Deshmukh Colony. “He along with seven of his friends went to Bommalachervu. While others were sitting on the bank, Kumar entered and slipped,” inspector S Devender said, adding that the incident took place around 12.30 p.m.

Kumar is a native of Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh and was staying with his friends in Deshmukh Village.
