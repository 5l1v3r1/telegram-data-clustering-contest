Irish bartender treats pub goers to live version of “Dirty Old Town”

20 Nov 2019, 17:46 by IrishCentral Staff

"Dirty Old Town" being sung by a Dublin bartender? Nothing better.

It’s a scene familiar to plenty of pub patrons - at the end of the night (and a feed of pints) someone is bound to begin an earnest attempt at singing a tune.

Read More: Irish barman singing as he works will give you chills

Last week in Dublin, however, the bartender stepped up to bat when he broke out a guitar and offered a rousing rendition of the beloved tune “Dirty Old Town," an old song that was popularized by the likes of The Dubliners and The Pogues.

Irish man Alain Ó Donaile captured The White House Pub's singing bartender on video and later shared it with Dublin Live.

On Facebook, Ó Donaile said: “Now if this ain’t just your typical Irish pub...Ye just can’t beat it ! !”
