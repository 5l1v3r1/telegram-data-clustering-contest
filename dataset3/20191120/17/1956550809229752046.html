A paintball field is the next attraction to open at the UAE's Last Exit, Mad X

The Smash Room opened last month at the Action Park, too

20 Nov 2019, 17:31 by Kelly Johnstone

Action Paintball will be open at the UAE's Action Park by the last week of December.

Aimed at everyone over ten years old, the paintballing course, which has various zones both indoors and outdoors, can accommodate up to 60 players at any one time.

Armed with heavy guns that shoot marble-sized pellets of paint at the opposing team, players can bring out their inner soldier while tackling a range of obstacles and scenarios.

Fancy yourself as a bit of a fitness warrior? Each player is also given a health tracker, which will display their heart rate and how many calories have been burned during the game. Let the competition being.

For those less willing to get splattered with paint, can take part in some target practice (the bullets are only going one way so a lot less dangerous) or hop aboard the converted, open-top London-style bus that serves up refreshing drinks and snacks as they watch all the high-adrenalin action unfold below them on the battlefield.

The paintballing field is the second attraction to open at the brand-new adventure destination – the first was the city’s second Smash Room, which welcomed visitors in early November.

There are still plenty more attractions set to open at the massive entertainment destination in January next year, including the region’s very first upside down house and a go-karting track.

Action Park is located at The Last Exit, Mad X, Abu Dhabi-bound and will offer a while host of high-octane sports once completed.

This gives a whole new meaning to art attack!
FromDhs99 (per 100 paintballs). Sun-Wed, 10am-10pm; Thu-Sat, 11am-11pm. The Last Exit, Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai, www.actionpark.ae (04 235 8893).
