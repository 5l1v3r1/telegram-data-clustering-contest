Bale To Join Mourinho At Spurs?

20 Nov 2019, 17:44 by SL International

Tottenham Hotspur legend Gareth Bale has been linked with a return to the club after the North London side sensationally appointed Jose Mourinho as their new manager.

Former Spurs boss Harry Redknapp, who was Bale’s boss at White Hart Lane between 2008 and 2012, has backed Mourinho to make the Welshman his first signing as new club manager.

“What a signing he (Bale) would be for the club. What a signing,” Redknapp told talkSPORT.
