Lights Out Premiere: Alexander Kowalski - Inheritance [Kanzleramt]

Trippy, hypnotic, booming techno

20 Nov 2019, 17:10 by Kane

Today, Lights Out brings you another exclusive premiere. This time, from Heiko Lux's Kanzleramt imprint, which has just celebrated 25 years. Taken from his Redemption EP, Alexander Kowalski's Inheritance is a prime cut of trippy, hypnotic, booming techno. Weighty low-end drives the track forward, as the lead bell sound moves all across the audio field amidst crispy percussion, creating a sense of traveling through another dimension. This is one of those tracks that when played at the right time, can easily have you forget where you are. Alexander Kowalski's Redemption EP will be released on November 22 via Kanzleramt.
