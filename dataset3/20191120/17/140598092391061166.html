Best experience day gift ideas for couples, friends and foodies for Christmas 2019

20 Nov 2019, 17:03 by Precious Adesina

Nothing beats being gifted an experience, whether it's a cookery class or an adventure, it's a present they won't forget. And the joy of experience day Christmas gifts is that you have the luxury of also going with them if you fancy. So, it's a gift for both you and them. 

It could be that they don't need anymore physical items or that it would be nice for them to have something exciting to look forward to in the new year. From delightful afternoon teas, to exciting adventure days out - there really is something for everyone is this list.

Whatever the reason, here is a round-up of the best experience gifts for individuals, couples, families or groups of friends, and where to buy them. Hopefully these will help create some beautiful, fun, exciting moments that your loved ones cherish forever.
