Jane McDonald sends message of support to number one fan Adele Roberts

20 Nov 2019, 17:13

Jane McDonald has sent a video message of support to her number on fan Adele Roberts.

Adele raised some eyebrows on I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here! when she chose a framed picture of Jane as her luxury item.

Her camp mates were so baffled that she even clashed with Roman Kemp over it.

Advertisement Advertisement

But Adele is about to have the last laugh as Jane has recorded a supportive message during a break in filming her new series of Jane & Friends in Manchester.

The Loose Women star joked that she also carries around a framed picture of Adele to keep her company.

Stood in the audience, Jane said: ‘I would like to wish Adele all the best in the jungle. I carry a picture of you at all times my darling. Me and my friends would like to wish you the very best of luck. Good luck Adele!’.

Adele’s girlfriend Kate Holderness previously cleared up the Jane ‘obsession’, explaining that she chose the photo because other luxury items had already been chosen by other camp mates.

On Instagram she said: ‘The brief was “something to make you happy” so she chose something that never fails to cheer her up and something that reminds her of early morning Radio 1 listeners – it’s a bit of a long running feature on Adele’s show, when they’re having a bit of a slump and feeling down a listener will request “a bit of Jane” to get them through the day.

‘I do have to say that most of her decisions are made at 2am when she gets up for her show, most things make perfect sense as that time.’

However ex I’m A Celebrity star John Barrowman, who accused the DJ of using Jane for ‘votes’.

Referring to Adele’s spat with Roman about the picture, he said on Extra Camp: ‘She has been caught out a little bit because she used the word prejudice, and he was just a young man asking a question.

‘If you brought a picture of Jane McDonald on you should probably know the songs she sang.

‘She probably brought it on to get a vote from a certain community and it’s all backfired on her a little bit, I think you are seeing her true colours right now.’

I’m A Celebrity continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
