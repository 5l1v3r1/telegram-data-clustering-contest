Girl fatally knocked down

20 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Staff Reporter

D. Keerthika, 21, a college student, died here on Wednesday after a sports utility vehicle (SUV) dashed against her two-wheeler on Monday. The girl was riding her two-wheeler when the vehicle allegedly belonging to the District Rural Development Agency (DRDA), Ariyalur, hit her near Thurimangalam, sources said.

She sustained severe injuries and was rushed to a private hospital in Tiruchi where she died on Tuesday night. The police have registered a case.
