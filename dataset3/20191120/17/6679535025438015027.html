334 children rescued in Krishna

Police conduct raids under Operation Muskaan

20 Nov 2019, 17:33 by Staff Reporter

The Krishna district police on Wednesday rescued as many as 334 children engaged in various works, begging and other activities supporting their family to earn a livelihood across the Krishna district as a part of the Operation Muskaan. The police, led by Superintendent of Police M. Ravindranath Babu, ASP M. Sattibabu, conducted raids in the early hours of Wednesday, identifying the children engaged in various activities.

100 in Masula alone

“In the Krishna district headquarters of Machilipatnam alone, nearly 100 children were rescued. Many of them have been found as orphans. The operation aims at ensuring enrolment of the children in schools for their well-being,” said Mr. Ravindranth Babu. The rescued children include 62 girls.
