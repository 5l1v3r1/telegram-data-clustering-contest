Oklahoma woman all smiles after bust in husband’s stabbing death

20 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Jackie Salo

An Oklahoma woman flashed a huge grin for her mugshot — despite facing serious criminal charges for stabbing her husband to death, officials said.

Shanda Johnson-Williams, 48, was arrested Tuesday in connection with the murder of her husband Jamie Williams in Fort Towson, according to Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation.

Deputies were called to the couple’s home Sunday after the husband was found unconscious, but he was pronounced dead at the scene.

The medical examiner determined his death was the result of a stabbing wound, officials said.

Authorities didn’t release details about the circumstances surrounding his death.

Johnson-Williams was booked in to Choctaw County Jail, where she faces a charge of first-degree murder, officials said. Her bond will be set at her arraignment.
