Early Black Friday deals from NOW TV for 2019

20 Nov 2019, 17:02

Christmas might not be coming early but Black Friday sure has, with NOW TV dropping deals already.

Even though it falls on 29 November this year, the streaming service has announced that they’re offering discounts online and in-store ahead of the famous day of deals known as Black Friday.

Black Friday rolls around every year in the run-up to Christmas, promising fantastic offers on all manner of products the day after Americans celebrate Thanksgiving.

Over time, retailers began to offer deals for longer periods of time instead of just on the day, and this year, NOW TV have done just that.
