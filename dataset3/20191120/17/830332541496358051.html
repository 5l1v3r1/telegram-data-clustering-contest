Teen loses shoes while running away from robbers who put him in chokehold, police say

20 Nov 2019, 17:02 by Stephanie Fryer

MADISON, Wis. - A teenager lost his shoes while running away from two people who mugged him, Madison police said.

A 15-year-old boy told police he was walking on North Sherman Avenue Tuesday afternoon when two other teens put him in a chokehold

The 15-year-old told officers he was thrown to the ground and the other teens went through his pockets. The teen lost his shoes as he ran away from the robbers, according to a release by the Madison Police Department. The teen said the robbers laughed and yelled that they were going to kill him as he ran away.

The teen was able to provide good descriptions of the two other teens, police said.

Officers located them near a shopping center on Northport Drive. Both were taken to the Juvenile Reception Center on tentative charges of robbery with use of force, strangulation and disorderly conduct, police said.
