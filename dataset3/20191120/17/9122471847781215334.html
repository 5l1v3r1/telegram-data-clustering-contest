ESPN Insider Offers Update On Myles Garrett Suspension Appeal

20 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Tzvi Machlin

Cleveland Browns defensive end Myles Garrett is in New York today, pleading his case to have his indefinite suspension reduced. With the process moving as quickly as it has, it looks like we’ll be getting an answer soon from the league.

ESPN’s Dianna Russini reported today that the decision on Garrett’s appeal could come soon, specifically, within a day or two.

Russini’s sources explained that the league wants to “make a decision on this quickly.”

Garrett made his case to the powers that be, and the NFL will be making their case today as well.
