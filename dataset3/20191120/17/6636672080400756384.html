Picture of the Day: Spacewalk to fix the anti-matter hunter AMS-02

20 Nov 2019, 17:23 by Alun Williams

What a great picture from the International Space Station. It’s taken during the recent spacewalk to service the Alpha Magnetic Spectrometer – aka the anti-matter hunter AMS-02 – on the Mini-Research Module 2.

It’s a pic of Luca Parmitano, an astronaut for the European Space agency (and a Colonel in the Italian Air Force). He was out on the Canadarm2 robotic arm of the module.
