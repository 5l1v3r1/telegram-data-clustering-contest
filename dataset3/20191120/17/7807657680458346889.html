The SK hynix Gold S31 SSD is on sale for Black Friday 2019

20 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Cale Hunt

Source: Windows Central

It's always tempting to upgrade your laptop or desktop PC, and Black Friday is a great time to save big on quality components. For Black Friday 2019, SK hynix has dropped prices on its recently-released Gold S31 2.5-inch SATA solid-state drives (SSD), available in 250GB, 500GB, and 1TB sizes. Already priced competitively to compete with Samsung, Micron, and Western Digital, you can now save up to a further 23% on these awesome SSDs.

We recently reviewed the 500GB SK hynix Gold S31 SSD and noted its awesome five-year warranty coverage, strong performance, and quality build and endurance. The 250GB option, regularly priced at $50, can be had for $40, while the 500GB option, regularly priced at $78, can be had for $60. You can also opt for the sizeable 1TB drive, which has been slashed by $18 for a total price of $106. If you're looking for a drive that will last well into the future, the 1TB model has a 600 TBW rating that means you should be able to write up to 600TB of data before the drive fails.

The 2.5-inch form factor allows it to work in modern laptops and desktop PCs. You can expect read speeds from the SATA III connection up to about 560 MB/s and write speeds up to about 531 MB/s, both excellent results that we tested in-house.
