Crisis Showrunner Promises Even More Cameos Will Be Revealed

20 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Samuel Gelman

"Crisis on Infinite Earths" already boasts an impressive list of confirmed guest stars and cameos. However, according to showrunner Marc Guggenheim, there are few more surprises that have not been announced yet.

Responding to a tweet by a fan asking if the crossover event will feature any more cameos, Guggenheim wrote back that several more have yet to be revealed.
