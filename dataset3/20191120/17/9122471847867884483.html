NFL Insider Has Update On Chiefs Star WR Tyreek Hill

20 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Chris Rosvoglou

Fans in Kansas City held their breath when Tyreek Hill left Monday night’s game with a hamstring injury. Even though he missed all of the second half against the Los Angeles Chargers, it appears his latest injury isn’t too serious.

Earlier this season, the talented wideout missed several games because of a shoulder injury. His return to the team has helped open up the offense in ways they couldn’t before.

Kansas City struggled to push the ball down the field without him this past Monday. It would be a huge blow to the offense if he couldn’t suit up in Week 13 against the Oakland Raiders, but it’s possible he’s ready for that AFC West matchup.

According to NFL Network insider Ian Rapoport, the Chiefs believe Hill’s hamstring injury is minor.

The team is listing him as day-to-day and believes there is a chance he doesn’t miss any time.

Hill reportedly wanted to go back in the game against the Chargers, but the coaching staff wanted to be cautious with their All-Pro wide receiver.
