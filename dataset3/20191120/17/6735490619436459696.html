Twitter Is Convinced That Greta Thunberg Is A ‘Time Traveller’ After 121-Year-Old Photo Resurfaces

20 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Aynslee Darmon

Looks like Greta Thunberg has a doppelgänger.

After a photo from 1898 resurfaced on Twitter, showing three children working at a gold mine on Dominion Creek in the Yukon Territory, fans of the young environmentalist are convinced she’s a “time traveller” as one of the children looks a lot like Thunberg.

Despite their similar facial expression, the girl, who sits on the left of the photo, even wears a braid in her hair – 16-year-old Thunberg’s signature hairstyle.
