T.I. Will Address Comments He Made About His Daughter's Hymen On Red Table Talk

20 Nov 2019, 01:37 by Tara Walsh

T.I. to address hymen comments

Rapper T.I. will address the comments he made about his daughter's hymen in an upcoming episode of Red Table Talk.

He revealed earlier this month that he goes to the gynecologist with Deyjah to, "check her hymen."

His upcoming appearance comes after weeks of backlash over his comments.

Jada tells People, 'We’re about to have T.I. and his wife Tiny.'

'They came on today and he addresses the controversy that is happening right now in regards to his daughter.'

'Me and Tiny also talk about how they survived their challenges in their marriage.'

'It was an exceptional show and I’m proud of it.'

What happened

T.I.'s latest interview with the Ladies Like Us podcast has gone viral for his comments about his daughter.

During a chat about strict parenting, he reveals he goes to the gynecologist with her to, "check her hymen."

"As of her 18th birthday, her hymen is still intact."

The rapper is asked if he's had the sex talk with his 18-year-old.

"Deyjah's 18, just graduated high school, now she's attending her first year of college, figuring it out for herself."

"And, yes, not only have we had the conversation... We have yearly trips to the gynecologist to check her hymen."
