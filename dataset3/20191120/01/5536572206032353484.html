Ryan Griffin Pulled Out A Classic ‘Sticky Hands’ Celebration After TD Against Redskins (VIDEO)

20 Nov 2019, 01:12 by Darrelle Lincoln

The best thing the NFL did was relax on the players and let them be creative and entertain fans with the different types of celebrations they come up with every week.

Such was the case during the Jets-Redskins matchup when New York Jets tight end Ryan Griffin scored a touchdown and then celebrated by bringing out a classic sticky hands move that will be recognized by Madden players who remember seeing in the early 2000’s.
