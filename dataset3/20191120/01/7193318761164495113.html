Stephen F. Austin at OSU women's basketball: Tip time, starting lineups, broadcast info

20 Nov 2019, 01:08 by Abby Bitterman

Stephen F. Austin at OU

When: 11 a.m., Wednesday

Where: Lloyd Noble Center, Norman

TV: FSOK (Cox 37)

Radio: KOKC-AM 1520, KREF-FM 99.3 / 1400 AM

Three things to know

• This game is the 10th meeting all time between Oklahoma and Stephen F. Austin. The Sooners are 5-4 in the series and have won the last five matchups. This is the teams’ first meeting since 2010.

• Wednesday’s game is a Field Trip Day, and this is the sixth year in a row the Sooners have hosted such a game. More than 6,000 local elementary school students will pack Lloyd Noble Center. Oklahoma is 4-1 in Field Trip Day games.

• The Sooners are looking to rebound from their loss to Missouri State in the semifinals of the preseason WNIT.
