Tom Ford, Jennifer Hudson and More Gather to Celebrate Leonard A. Lauder

20 Nov 2019, 01:38

Last night, Hearst and Harper's BAZAAR celebrated the Lincoln Center 7th Annual Corporate Fund Fashion Gala, honoring Leonard A. Lauder with the Womenâ€™s Leadership Award. Guests included past and present Estee Lauder models such as Elizabeth Hurley, Karlie Koss, Carolyn Murphy, Hilary Rhoda and more. Elizabeth Hurley kicked off the ceremony, followed by welcome remarks by Steve Swartz. Following touching tributes from both Glenda Bailey and Tommy Hilfiger, Tom Ford and Mr. Lauder sat in conversation discussing his career and the life lessons he has learned. Then, Carolyn Murphy introduced Oscar-winning actress and singer Jennifer Hudson whom delivered a breathtaking performance. The evening raised over $2 million for the Lincoln Center Corporate Fund.
