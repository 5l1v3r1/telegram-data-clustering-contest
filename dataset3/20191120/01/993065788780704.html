Sunflower pub recognised for 'dog friendly' approach

20 Nov 2019, 01:00

A BELFAST pub has been hailed the most 'dog-friendly' in the north.

The Sunflower bar in the city centre received the most votes for its "welcoming approach, secure garden, and shaded areas" for patrons and their pets.

The awards are run by Rover.com, the world's largest network of pet sitters and dog walkers.
