YouTuber Poses as Pilot to Skip Security Check, Arrested From IGI Airport

This is not the first time that Rajan Mahbubani impersonated a pilot. He has fake IDs and uniforms which he got hold of for the purpose of making videos.

20 Nov 2019, 01:47

New Delhi: A Delhi-based man who makes YouTube, tikTok videos was arrested by CISF personnel at the Indira Gandhi International Airport as he posed as a Lufthansa pilot to escape long queues and security checks to board a Kolkata-bound Air Asia flight. According to reports, this is not the first time that Rajan Mahbubani impersonated a pilot. He has fake IDs and uniforms which he got hold of for the purpose of making videos.
