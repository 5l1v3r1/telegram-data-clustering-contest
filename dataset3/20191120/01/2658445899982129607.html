Police advise vehicle owners - The Nation Newspaper

20 Nov 2019, 01:45

THE Ogun State Police Command has advised the general public especially those who lost their vehicles/motorcycles to thieves or left abandoned to report at Ogere/Ago-Iwoye Divisions and Obantoko/Adigbe Divisions for possible identification and claims.

The vehicles and motorcycles are:

Ogere Division vehicles: One M/Benz with reg. no. BDG 571 BP; one Toyota Tundra with reg. no. AAA 381 DH and one Passat GT CAE with reg. no. EPE 437 DH.

Ogere Division motorcycles: Three unregistered Bajaj; one Lifan with reg. no. AYE 360 VR; two Jenchenge unregistered; one Honda unregistered; one Bajaj with reg. no. TTD 457 VP; one TVS with reg. no. AAB 432 VN and one Boxer with reg. no. MEK 114 QC.

Ago-Iwoye Division motorcycles: One unregistered Suzuki; three Bajaj unregistered; one Bajaj with reg. no. OY4q97 LSD; one Bajaj with reg. no. JJJ 472 QE; one Bajaj with reg. no.
