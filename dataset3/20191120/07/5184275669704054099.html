RRR Update: Makers To Announce The Lead Actress Opposite Jr.NTR In The Period Action Drama Today

The film also stars Ajay Devgn, Alia Bhatt, and Samuthirakani in crucial roles.

20 Nov 2019, 07:23 by Koimoi.com Team

Filmmaker S.S Rajamouli’s directorial venture RRR is one of the most anticipated releases and it has been in news all over from past one year. The film featuring Tollywood superstars Ram Charan and Jr NTR is one of the biggest upcoming films in 2020.

The latest news related to the magnum opus is, the makers yesterday took on the film’s twitter handle to announce an important update. The makers announced that the 70% shoot has been completed and today they will be announcing the leading actress opposite Jr.NTR and will also be revealing the names of antagonists from the film.

Their tweet read: “It’s been a phenomenal year since the shoot of #RRR began! It was a
productive year with 70% of shoot completed. Also putting an end to speculations, we’re glad to announce the lead actress for @tarak9999 & antagonists
of the film tomorrow. Stay tuned… #RRR”
