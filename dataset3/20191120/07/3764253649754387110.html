Sinach shares sweet photo with her husband, appreciates well-wishers

20 Nov 2019, 07:45

Popular Nigerian gospel singer and new mum, Sinach, has taken to social media to thank her fans and followers for their well-wishes over the arrival of her baby with her husband of 5 years.

Earlier on, Legit.ng reported the news of how the 46-year-old gospel singer welcomed her first child with her husband, Pastor Joe Egbu whom she got married to in 2014. The news was shared by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome at the just concluded LIMA awards which was held in Lagos at the Loveworld Convocation Arena.

Following the massive congratulatory messages she received, she took to her Instagram account to thank everyone. Sharing a sweet photo with her husband, she simply captioned: "Thank you for the outpouring of love!! We love you too❤️❤️❤️"

In similar news, a 42-year-old woman gave birth to five babies at Federal Medical Centre (FMC) in Lokoja, Kogi state, after 18 years of waiting.

Okeigbo, who is an indigene of Olokoro Amangbo Community, Umuahia south local government area of Abia state, gave birth to three boys and two baby girls on Wednesday, April 17, through a caesarean section.
