Crash closes Hawaii Island’s Highway 190; investigation ongoing

20 Nov 2019, 07:15 by HNN Staff

HAWAII ISLAND, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) -Another bad crash on Hawaii Island shut down a major highway Tuesday night.

Highway 190 was closed earlier in the evening in Pu’uanahulu.

Police are rerouting drivers to Waikoloa Road and Kaiminui Drive.

Officials have not yet released details on the accident.
