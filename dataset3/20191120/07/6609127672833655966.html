NEIN-CASTLE

Felix Magath not in running for Hearts job as foreigner is in frame to replace Craig Levein

20 Nov 2019, 07:28 by Derek McGregor

GERMAN Felix Magath has been given a firm ‘Nein’ for the Hearts job.

It’s believed a foreign candidate is in the frame to replace axed Craig Levein.

But it’s not former Bayern Munich boss Magath.

The 66-year-old made his interest known but was told he’s not for the Jambos.

Alan Irvine, Neil McCann, Stuart McCall and Steve Cotterill have all met Ann Budge, but there has been no approach for Motherwell boss Stephen Robinson.
