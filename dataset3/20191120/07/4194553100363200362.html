(Video) Gini Wijnaldum’s stunning Netherlands hat-trick as skipper proves he’s one of world’s best midfielders

20 Nov 2019, 07:26 by Jordan Chamberlain

Gini Wijnaldum put in a breathtaking performance last night v Estonia with the Netherlands already qualified.

Liverpool’s controlling, tactical midfielder – who Jurgen Klopp relies on implicitly – is given far more freedom by Ronald Koeman on international duty and he takes advantage of it to boot.

Gini’s hat-trick in the 5-0 win made it eight goals in qualifying for Euro 2020 – making him the highest scoring midfielder in the continent!

This term, he’s notched just once for Liverpool, but should Klopp ever feel the need to deploy him further forward – we reckon Wijnaldum will be delighted.
