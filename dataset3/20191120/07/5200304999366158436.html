Jacqueline Fernandez joins John Abraham & Rakul Preet Singh’s next

20 Nov 2019, 07:46 by Raj Baddhan

Jacqueline Fernandez has been confirmed to star in John Abraham’s untitled next, along with Rakul Preet Singh.

She was cited in The Asian Age saying, “It’s a fantastic film and script. I am coming together again with John and we have Rakul also in this film.”

Talking about Singh’s role in the film, Fernandez said, “The one Rakul is doing is brilliant”. He added, “During the story, I was wondering that Rakul’s role is really nice and I’m sure that she will do full justice to it. I am very happy that she is a part of the project.”

The film goes on the floors in January.
