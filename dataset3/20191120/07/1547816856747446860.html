Lata Mangeshkar Health Update: Madhur Bhandarkar Visits The Veteran Singer In Hospital; Informs She Is Stable

20 Nov 2019, 07:10 by Lubna Khan

Lata Mangeshkar, who had been admitted to Breach Candy Hospital on November 11, after complaining of difficulty in breathing, is now stable. Filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar visited her in the Breach Candy Hospital and informed that she is now stable

The Nightingale of India, Lata Mangeshkar had been admitted to Breach Candy Hospital in Mumbai on November 11 after she complained of difficulties in breathing. Since then, the entire nation has been worried for her health and has been praying for her speedy recovery. On November 15, a source had informed SpotboyE that the legendary singer’s condition was still delicate, and that the family had decided to keep it under wraps but Lataji was in the ICU on a ventilator and that doctors had sedated her as per protocol. Now, there is a recent update on her health and it comes from filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar, who visited Lataji in the hospital.

Filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar visited Lata Mangeshkar at Breach Candy Hospital in Mumbai and on Tuesday evening, he took to his Instagram to inform everyone that the veteran singer is now stable and that she is responding positively to the treatment. Sharing a picture of the melody queen on his Instagram, Madhur Bhandarkar wrote, “Visited the Hospital to see @lata_mangeshkar didi glad to inform that she is stable and responding positively to the treatment.
