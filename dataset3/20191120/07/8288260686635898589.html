McDonald's is giving away chips for free this Friday - how to get yours

Each customer can claim one pack each, with no purchase necessary, but rather than heading up to the counter or self-serve screens like normal, to get the freebie, you'll need to make sure you place your order via the McDonald's app

20 Nov 2019, 07:52 by James Rodger, Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

McDonald's chips will be free this Friday for one day only - and this is how to get yours.

The fast food giant operates more restaurants in Birmingham than any other UK city outside of London.

Maccy D's is running the promotion on November 22.

Each customer can claim one pack each, with no purchase necessary, but rather than heading up to the counter or self-serve screens like normal, to get the freebie, you'll need to make sure you place your order via the McDonald's app - reports Mirror Online.

The offer is available to all new and existing McDonald's app customers, in selected branches.

The full list of all the branches that aren't participating are below, which includes certain branches in Croydon, Llandudno, Dublin and a few Asda stores.

As usual, to complete registration, new users must go into the restaurant for their first order – otherwise this offer works for click and collect, through the Drive Thru and from this week, for orders placed and delivered directly to your table thanks to the nationwide introduction of Table Markers.

 

Table Markers, as the name suggests, means that customers can find a table, order through their phone and have their food delivered straight to where they’re sat.
