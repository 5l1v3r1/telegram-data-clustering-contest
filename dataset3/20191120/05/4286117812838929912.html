Supreme Court issues notice to ED on bail plea filed by P Chidambaram in INX Media case

The Supreme Court has issued a notice to ED on a petition filed by Chidambaram challenging the judgment of the Delhi HC denying him bail in the money laundering case.

20 Nov 2019, 05:44

HIGHLIGHTS

  • SC notice to ED on bail plea of Chidambaram in INX Media case
  • Supreme Court has asked the ED to file a reply by Monday
  • The next date of hearing in the case is November 26

The Supreme Court on Wednesday issued a notice to the Enforcement Directorate (ED) on the bail plea of senior Congress leader P Chidambaram in the INX Media money-laundering case.

Chidambaram had approached the Supreme Court challenging the judgment of the Delhi High Court denying him bail in the money laundering case lodged by the ED.

The probe agency has been asked to file a reply by Monday. The next date of hearing is November 26.
