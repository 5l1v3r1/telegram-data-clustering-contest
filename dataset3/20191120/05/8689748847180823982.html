Dope Nation Wins Best Group At The 2019 Ghana Music & Arts Awards Europe

20 Nov 2019, 05:12 by BB Jones

Ghanaian music duo, Dope Nation emerged as winners for the Best Group at the recently held Ghana Music & Arts Awards Europe.
The maiden edition of the award ceremony was held in Amsterdam, the Netherlands over the weekend.
The Ghana Music & Arts Awards Europe is designed to celebrate our Rich Ghanaian Culture Coupled with Rewarding Excellence in our Music and Arts Industry in Europe.
Dope Nation, known for hit songs such as Eish, Naami, Zanku and Confam were not around to pick up their award but a representative from their camp stepped forward to receive it warmly.

Stonebwoy was the biggest winner on the night, taking three awards for his hard work under the year in review.
The Bhim Nation boss won the Collaboration of the Year with his Kpo K3k3 song, Reggae/Dancehall Song of the Year as well as Reggae/Dancehall Artiste of The Year.
