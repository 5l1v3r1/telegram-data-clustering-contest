Former WWE Universal Champion calls CM Punk a "coward"

20 Nov 2019, 05:40 by Pratyay Ghosh

After his debut on last week's episode of WWE Backstage, CM Punk was back on the show last night. During the appearance, Renee Young asked Punk what he thought about Seth Rollins' Tweets after his debut, urging Punk to fight him. Rollins didn't like Punk's answer and called the former WWE Champion a coward on Twitter.

Seth Rollins calls CM Punk a "coward" after WWE Backstage segment

CM Punk made waves after his debut on WWE Backstage last week. Punk was a guest on this week's episode and during one segment, Renee Young brought up Seth Rollins' Tweets following Punk's appearance, where Rollins had hinted at his desire to wrestle CM Punk.

Renee Young asked Punk about Rollins' Tweets and Punk basically said that he needed to maintain his journalistic integrity because of which he wouldn't comment on Rollins' Tweets and also added that Rollins would do better to keep his mouth shut.

Seth Rollins replied to Punk's statements on Twitter, calling the former WWE Champion a "coward"
