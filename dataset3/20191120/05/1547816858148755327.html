Good Newwz: Before A Baby Is Born, Akshay Kumar And Kareena Kapoor Khan Give Birth To These Hilarious Memes

20 Nov 2019, 05:08 by Saheli Maity

As is the trend, after every trailer release, come a plethora of memes. Well, the meme experts this time have Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kirara Advani’s Good Newwz on target

Memes have become food and butter for the creators. From political speeches, any public figures statements, to movie trailers, the creators doesn’t spare anyone when it comes to creating memes. Earlier, Arjun Kapoor, Sanjay Dutt and Kriti Sanon’s Panipat and Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha’s Dabangg 3 became the butt of the jokes. Netizens went on a laughter riot after seeing those memes. However, this time Good Newwz becomes the amusement for the meme experts.

On Monday, the makers of Good Newwz released the first trailer starring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kirara Advani. The trailer instantly got love from the audience for its hilarious content, so much so that, some of the funny content have amused the social media people and made memes around the trailer that are not only making netizens laugh out loud but going viral in no time.
