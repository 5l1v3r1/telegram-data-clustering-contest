Bigg Boss 13 witnessed a shocking twist yesterday as best friends Asim Riaz and Sidharth Shukla fought like staunch enemies. Best friends turned rivals and things just went from bad to worse. Now, social media is divided between the two

Asim Riaz and Sidharth Shukla have been the best of friends so far. BUT, yesterday, these two had an ugly fight leaving everyone in shock. Asim Riaz and Sidharth Shukla fought as if they have been enemies for ages and threw some nasty words at each other. Now, with their fight, the house is divided between who is right and who is wrong. Social media too is divided with their fan clubs coming out in their support lashing out at the other.
