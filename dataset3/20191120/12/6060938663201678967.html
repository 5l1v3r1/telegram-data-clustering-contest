Gautam Gulati to share screen space with Salman Khan in Radhe

20 Nov 2019, 12:31 by TNN

While 'Dabangg 3' will hit the theatres soon, Salman Khan has already announced his next project, Radhe. On November 1, the actor shared a picture from the mahurat of the movie. Seen with his in the photo were Disha Patani, Jackie Shroff and Randeep Hooda, who are a part of the cast of the film. Now, there’s another addition to the already impressive cast in the form of Gautam Gulati.

Gautam, who made his beginning with the telly world, interacted with Salman Khan a lot when he participated in Bigg Boss. While Disha has shared screen space with Salman in 'Bharat' and Randeep Hooda has worked with him in 'Kick' and 'Sultan', this will be Gautam’s first acting assignment with the Bollywood superstar. Prabhu Dheva will be helming the film, which was earlier speculated to be a sequel of 'Wanted'. However, that rumour was dismissed by the film’s team.

While not much is known about Gautam’s character, the actor apparently gave his nod as soon as he was given a brief. The film is slated to release on Eid 2020. In his forthcoming release 'Dabangg 3', Salman will be seen sharing screen space with Sonakshi Sinha and Saiee Manjrekar.
