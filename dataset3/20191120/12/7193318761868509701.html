What to do in Oklahoma on Nov. 20, 2019: Hear Penny & Sparrow at the Tower Theatre

20 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Brandy McDonnell

Today's featured event:

Hear acclaimed duo Penny & Sparrow at 8 tonight at the historic Tower Theatre, 425 NW 23.

Texas natives Andy Baxter and Kyle Jahnke are touring in support of their sixth album, "Finch," released earlier this year.

Doors open at 7 tonight. For tickets and information, go to www.towertheatreokc.com.
