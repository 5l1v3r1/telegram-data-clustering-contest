Taapsee On Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor: It Would’ve Been Difficult To Get A 2nd Film After His Debut If Not Anil Kapoor's Son

20 Nov 2019, 12:56 by Saheli Maity

The next guest to grace on Neha Dhupia’s show, No Filter Neha is Taapsee Pannu who talks about Sonam Kapoor’s brother Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor’s film career

Taapsee Pannu is one of the leading ladies in Bollywood. After the success of Pink, the actress is bagging some interesting and challenging roles. Though her last released film Saand Ki Aankh failed to make an impact at the box-office, it certainly did manage to impress the critics. Taapsee has always been very vocal about certain topics. When her Mission Mangal co-star Akshay Kumar’s name was on Forbes Highest Paid actors list, Taapsee too took a hilarious dig on him. Even when Kangana Ranaut’s sister talked about her acting skills, she didn’t keep quiet, in fact, she gave it back to him. Well, a similar thing happened during No Filter Neha where Taapsee spoke about certain things without filtering it out.

No Filter Neha is a show where all the Bollywood stars spill all the juicy beans that definitely make headlines. This time the next guest to grace on Neha Dhupia’s show, No Filter Neha is Taapsee Pannu who talked about Sonam Kapoor’s brother Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor’s film career.
