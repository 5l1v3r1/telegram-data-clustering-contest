Ariana Grande Brings BFFs Liz Gillies & Matt Bennett To Perform 'Victorious' Songs at Sweetener Tour Stop!

20 Nov 2019, 12:50

Ariana Grande returned to the stage with a bang, after cancelling a show due to sickness.

The 26-year-old singer brought her former co-stars and best friends Elizabeth Gillies and Matt Bennett to perform a couple of songs from their show Victorious at her concert stop in Atlanta, Ga!

Ariana and Liz belted out their duet “Give It Up”, while Matt performed his song “I Think You’re Swell”. Liz also joined Ariana on stage during “thank u, next”.

“the most special show to me yet. truly. love u. this was too much to process and don’t know what to say yet but so grateful and thrilled. love u forever
@LizGillies @MattBennett,” Ariana wrote on Twitter after the show.

“Still can’t process. Last night blew my mind. Love you, @ArianaGrande. Love you too, @MattBennett!! We did it!!” Liz shared.

“One of the best nights of my life. Love you @ArianaGrande @LizGillies @SenSanders and everyone in Atlanta!” Matt added on his account.

Ariana even shared that they wished their co-stars could’ve been there.

“@victoriajustice @leonthomas @jogia @daniellamonet look how special this is. we love you and wish you all could have been here tonight,” she wrote on her Instagram story.

The trio even teased their appearances earlier in the day.
