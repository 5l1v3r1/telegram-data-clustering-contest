This Morning viewers in tears over defiant marriage that saw husband become partners wife

Barbara started to have suspicions that her husband of eight years was having an affair after discovering a bag of women's clothes.

20 Nov 2019, 12:56 by Thomas Lynch

ITV's This Morning left viewers in tears today when a married couple told their defiant love story.

Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield were joined by 72-year-old Barbara Hamlin and her transgender husband.

In 2014, Barbara's husband John, now 71, had gender reassignment surgery and transitioned to become Jane.

Eight years into their marriage, Barbara started to have suspicions that her husband was having an affair.

While packing to go on holiday, Barbara discovered a bag of women's clothes containing skirts, hotpants and underwear - alongside a pair of black patent stilettos.

It was then she started to fear the worst.

During a holiday to Canada, Barbara confronted John about the bag of clothes only to discover that they were for him.

Barbara was overcome "with a feeling of relief" she explained to Holly.

"I did know about cross-dressing and transgender very vaguely and this wasn't as scary as losing ones relationship in a marriage," Barbara said.

Jane went on to explain that this was the beginning of a huge weight being lifted off her shoulders.

"When it was gone I was on cloud nine," Jane explained.

They both went on to say they are the happiest they have ever been and praised their support network of friends and neighbours.
