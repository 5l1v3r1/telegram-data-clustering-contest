Forest Drive West, Felix K and DB1 are LEVL on Nullpunkt Records

A new 12-inch from the all-star trio is joined by solo records from label owner Felix K and Ena.

20 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Mark Smith

Felix K's Nullpunkt Records is releasing three new 12-inches in the coming months.

Expected to land in December or January, the new releases explore murky territory between drum & bass, techno and experimental electronics. Forest Drive West, DB1, and Felix K collaborate under their LEVL alias on LEVL #2 while Ena and Felix K supply solo efforts titled Sparse #2 and FLXK1 #2 respectively.

Upfront white label copies have been and gone at Hard Wax, but expect the releases to appear on the Nullpunkt Bandcamp over the next few weeks. All releases will be available on vinyl and digitally.

Nullpunkt has absorbed the now-closed Hidden Hawaii label, which formerly released work from Forest Drive West, Ena, DB1 and more. Ena just recently announced his debut album on Different Circles. LEVL meanwhile appeared on an Arcola 12-inch earlier this year.
