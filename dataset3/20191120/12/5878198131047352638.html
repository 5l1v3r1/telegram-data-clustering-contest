Eight celebrities, 8 moments; the last photos of these famous black people will make you miss them

20 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Novieku-Babatunde Adeola

Famous people through their dealings have grown connected to people who adore them. Despite their fame, they are humans like we all are – they feel guilt, joy, sadness, and excitement like everyone of us.

As a result of this extremely tight bond they share with followers, it becomes devastating whenever they are no more.

After their death, followers and fans hang on to the last piece of memory that connects them to their icon.

In this article, Face2Face Africa compiled a list of famous last photos taken of many celebrities before they died.
