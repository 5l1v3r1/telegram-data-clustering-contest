Ariana Grande Reacts To Her Five Grammy Nominations on Social Media

20 Nov 2019, 12:10

Ariana Grande woke up to some amazing news this morning – she got FIVE nominations for the 2020 Grammys!

The 26-year-old singer picked up nominations for Record of the Year and Best Pop Solo Performance for “7 Rings”, Best Pop Vocal Album and Album of the Year for Thank U, Next; and Best Pop Duo/Group Performance for “Boyfriend” with Social House.

Taking to Twitter later in the morning, Ariana had a cute reaction to her nominations.

“oh wow oh wow,” she first wrote, adding, “crying. moved and honored. thank u @RecordingAcad.”
