Property Officer Vacancy At Aligarh Muslim University

20 Nov 2019, 12:36 by Manav Malhotra

Aligarh Muslim University invites application for the post of Property Officer.

Name of the Post: Property Officer

No. of Post: 01 (One)

Pay Scale: Pay Level-7 (Pay Range: ₹ 44900 - 142400) Plus Allowances

Essential Qualification and Experience

  • Law Graduate with min 50% marks.
  • Minimum seven years practice in a Waqf Board/District Court/High Court/High Court/Supreme Court.
  • Experience of handling Waqfs and property Cases.
  • Knowledge of Urdu, Hindi, English

How to apply?

  • Eligible candidates are required to send in their complete application form procured in the above manner may either be delivered personally at the Reception Counter of Administrative Block or sent by post, super scribing on the top at the left side of the cover, the post applied for, advertisement number and date, to the Joint Registrar, Selection Committee Section (Non-Teaching), Registrar's Office, Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh – 202002
  • The application must reach by 09.12.2019
