The Milk Thistle and Her Majesty's Secret Service named among best cocktail bars in UK

But we already knew that...

20 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Robin Murray

There's no shortage of cracking cocktail bars in Bristol, from swanky establishments to dark and dingy speakeasies tucked away off the beaten track.

Some of the finest Bristol bars regularly scoop awards and two of the very best have just picked up a further accolade.

The Milk Thistle and Her Majesty's Secret Service have been included in a round-up of the UK's top 40 cocktail bars by hen party organiser Go Hen.

In the list of 40 bars, there are six in the South West, with No. 131 The Promenade in Cheltenham and The Hideout in Bath also making the cut.

Earlier this year Milk Thistle was named as the sixth best cocktail bar in the UK in the The Top 50 Cocktail Bars awards, making it a memorable year for the team.

The Milk Thistle

Address: Quay Head House, Colston Avenue, Bristol, BS1 1EB

Website: https://www.milkthistlebristol.com/

What Go Hen says about Milk Thistle: "From this day forward, we’re going to start every conversation we ever have with: ‘Have you heard of the Milk Thistle?’

"It’s too good not to talk about all the time (even if it is a hidden gem). The cocktails, the atmosphere, the cocktails, the décor, the cocktails, the people, the cocktails, the cocktails, THE COCKTAILS! It was love at first sip!"
