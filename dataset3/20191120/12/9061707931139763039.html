Scottish club not interested in signing former Derby County defender - report

Latest Derby County transfer news | The former Rams defender is a free agent after leaving the club in the summer

20 Nov 2019, 12:59 by Mark Wakefield

Former Derby County defender Efe Ambrose is on the lookout for a new club following the expiration of his contract at Pride Park.

Reports earlier this week linked with 31-year-old centre-back with Scottish Premiership duo St Johnstone and former club Hibernian.

However, the Daily Record claim that Hibs are not interested in bringing their former defender back to the club on a free transfer.

The experienced centre-back signed for the Rams on a short-term deal in February as a free agent, shortly after his contract at Hibs has expired.

Ambrose was signed as defensive cover to centre-backs Richard Keogh and Fikayo Tomori, but left Pride Park in the summer without a single first-team appearance to his name.
