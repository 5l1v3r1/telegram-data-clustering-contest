Matt Cornett Sings New Original Song 'A Billion Sorrys' For 'High School Musical' Series - Listen Now!

20 Nov 2019, 12:21

Matt Cornett is belting out a new tune in this week’s episode of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series!

The 21-year-old actor performs another new original song for the show, “A Billion Sorrys“.

“Guys it’s out!! Check out the next 2 stories to hear A Billion Sorrys on Spotify and Apple Music!” Matt shared on his Instagram story.

In this week’s episode, “The Wonderstudies”, After “losing” her phone and missing an important rehearsal, Nini suspects who is responsible for her bout with bad luck and surprisingly retaliates. EJ confronts Ricky about a heartfelt voicemail left for Nini. Meanwhile, Mr. Mazzara becomes suspicious of Miss Jenn’s past.

The third episode of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series hits Disney+ this Friday (November 22).
