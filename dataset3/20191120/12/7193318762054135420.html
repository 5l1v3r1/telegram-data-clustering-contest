Former Yukon High School football star Owen Olsen commits to Northeastern Oklahoma A&M College

20 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Nick Sardis

Owen Olsen will be back in Oklahoma to continue his college football career.

The former Yukon linebacker announced Tuesday on Twitter he committed to Northeastern Oklahoma A&M College.

Olsen, a freshman, said last week he was leaving the Naval Academy.

Olsen was reclassified into the 2020 recruiting class. He can play four years and is eligible for a redshirt season.

Olsen was a second-team selection on The Oklahoman’s 2018 Big All-City squad. He recorded 67 total tackles and played wide receiver.

Olsen’s brother, Perry, is a quarterback at Navy. He replaced starter Malcolm Perry in the third quarter of last week’s 52-20 loss at Notre Dame. The freshman threw a touchdown pass and rushed for a score.
