Lorry driver blocks road after getting stuck in mud

He was wheel-spinning after ending up in a hedge

20 Nov 2019, 12:25 by Beth Pridding

A farmer used his tractor to help a lorry driver out of trouble when he got stuck in the mud in a country lane.

Officers from Swadlincote Response Unit were called to the scene in Stanton-by-Bridge after the lorry got stuck and partially blocked the road.

The report came in at 9.55am this morning, Wednesday, November 20.

The lorry had ended up in a hedge at the side of the road near Swarkestone and, when trying to get out, got stuck in the mud.

A spokesman for Derbyshire Police said: "Swadlincote Response Unit went out to where it had happened on the A514, when a lorry had got stuck in the mud.

"The lorry driver was trying to get out of the mud, but it kept wheel-spinning and he was really struggling.

"A farmer was there when officers arrived and was helping to tow the lorry out of the mud. The road is now cleared."
