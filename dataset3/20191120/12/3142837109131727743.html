Quest Diagnostics Inc Plans Quarterly Dividend of $0.53 (NYSE:DGX)

20 Nov 2019, 12:02 by Jessica Moore

Quest Diagnostics Inc (NYSE:DGX) declared a quarterly dividend on Wednesday, November 20th, RTT News reports. About Quest Diagnostics

Quest Diagnostics Incorporated provides diagnostic testing, information, and services in the United States and internationally. The company develops and delivers diagnostic testing information and services, such as routine testing, non-routine and advanced clinical testing, anatomic pathology testing, and other diagnostic information services.
