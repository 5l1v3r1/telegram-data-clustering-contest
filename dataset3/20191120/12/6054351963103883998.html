A$AP Rocky's Sweden Imprisonment Included In Donald Trump Impeachment Hearings

20 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Justin Ivey

Washington, D.C. – A$AP Rocky oddly became a small part of presidential history on Wednesday (November 20). U.S. Ambassador to the European Union Gordon Sondland mentioned Rocky — who was imprisoned in Sweden this past summer — multiple times during his testimony at the impeachment hearings for Donald Trump in Washington, D.C.

In Sondland’s opening statement, the ambassador noted he “primarily discussed A$AP Rocky” with Trump during a July 26 call that’s become a major part of the impeachment proceedings.
