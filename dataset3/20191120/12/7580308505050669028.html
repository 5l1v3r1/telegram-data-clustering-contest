Tottenham unveils new coach Jose Mourinho

20 Nov 2019, 12:02 by Dennis Erezi

English Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur has unveiled Jose Mourinho as its new head coach, hours after naming the former Manchester United and Chelsea manager as the man to take over from Mauricio Pochettino.

Pochettino was sacked less than 24 hours ago.

Mourinho, who is replacing him, once famously said he will never coach the club out of respect for Chelsea, which is based in London like Tottenham.

“I am excited to be joining a club with such a great heritage and such passionate supporters,” said Mourinho. “The quality in both the squad and the academy excites me. Working with these players is what has attracted me.”
