Meghan Trainor tearing up after getting teeth pulled is best thing you’ll see

20 Nov 2019, 12:03

Meghan Trainor is absolutely not living her best life following a trip to the dentist – after she had to get all her wisdom teeth taken out.

And we’re feeling her pain.

The Lips Are Moving singer shared a snap of herself on Instagram, showing her clutching on to two pals as they made their way out of the appointment.

She could be seen dressed in all black – with the fluffiest slippers known to man – and sporting a white bandage around her head.

‘I was initially going to just take one wisdom tooth out,’ the 25-year-old told fans.

‘The dentist said they all had to go. Wasn’t emotionally or mentally prepared but sure got some great content.

‘The second vid is me talking to my amazing manager @tommymbruce who literally has my whole heart haha thank u to the amazing dentist and my family for taking such good care of me.’

In a video to rival the moment Taylor Swift broke down over a banana after getting laser eye surgery, Meghan then blessed us with a clip of her tearfully confessing her love for manager Tommy Bruce.

While her mouth stuffed was full of what we’re guessing is cotton wool balls.

She can be seen tearing up, asking: ‘Is this for Tommy? I love you so much Tommy. I can’t cry, it hurts, but I love you so much.

‘You do so much for me and I love you forever. I miss you.’

We think Tommy got the hint, hun…

But Meghan was back to her old self soon after, sharing videos of herself dancing in the car with pals.

And fans found the whole thing hilarious, with one sliding into her comments to write: ‘THE most iconic wisdom teeth pulling day ever.
