Gardaí investigating cash-in-transit robbery in Dundalk - Talk of the Town

20 Nov 2019, 12:55 by TOTT Editor

Gardaí are investigating a cash-in-transit robbery in Dundalk on Monday evening.

According to LMFM news, the incident took place in the Long Walk area at around 5.20pm.

Anyone who was in the Long Walk/Carroll Village areas between 5pm and 5.30pm is asked to contact Dundalk Garda Station on 042 9335577. Gardaí are particularly keen to obtain dash cam footage from anyone who was in the area at the time.
