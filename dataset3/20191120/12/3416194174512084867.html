Ed Sheeran Rocks Shorter Hair Than We're Used To Seeing For '& Juliet' Press Night in London

20 Nov 2019, 12:50

Ed Sheeran has super short hair now!

The 28-year-old “Perfect” singer showed off his shorter hair at the press night performance of & Juliet at Shaftesbury Theatre on Wednesday night (November 20) in London, England, where he posed for pics with Max Martin and Miriam-Teak Lee.

Ed was joined at the press event by Liam Payne and Maya Henry, Jessie J, Nicole Scherzinger, and Simon Cowell and Lauren Silverman.
