$6.93 Billion in Sales Expected for Kohl’s Co. (NYSE:KSS) This Quarter

20 Nov 2019, 12:20 by Renee Jackson

Equities analysts expect Kohl’s Co. (NYSE:KSS) to report sales of $6.93 billion for the current quarter, according to Zacks Investment Research. Three analysts have made estimates for Kohl’s’ earnings. The highest sales estimate is $6.96 billion and the lowest is $6.90 billion. Kohl’s posted sales of $6.82 billion in the same quarter last year, which indicates a positive year over year growth rate of 1.6%. The business is scheduled to announce its next quarterly earnings report on Tuesday, March 3rd.

On average, analysts expect that Kohl’s will report full-year sales of $19.83 billion for the current year, with estimates ranging from $18.97 billion to $20.14 billion. For the next fiscal year, analysts forecast that the firm will post sales of $19.93 billion, with estimates ranging from $18.78 billion to $20.48 billion. Zacks’ sales averages are an average based on a survey of research analysts that that provide coverage for Kohl’s.

Kohl’s Company Profile

Kohl's Corporation operates as an omni-channel retailer in the United States. Its stores and Website offer apparel, footwear, accessories, beauty, and home products. As of February 3, 2018, it operated 1,158 department stores; a Website Kohls.com; and 12 FILA outlets, and 4 Off-Aisle clearance centers.
