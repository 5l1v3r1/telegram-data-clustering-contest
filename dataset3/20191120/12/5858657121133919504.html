Vancouver Christmas Market returns for 10th year

20 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Tim James and Nikitha Martins

Summary

It's beginning to look a lot like Christmas at Jack Poole Plaza

The Vancouver Christmas Market opens Wednesday runs until Christmas Eve

VANCOUVER (NEWS 1130) – The Vancouver Christmas Market opens Wednesday and it’s truly beginning to look a lot like Christmas at the Jack Poole Plaza.

The market celebrates its 10 year anniversary this year, and the market just keeps growing every year according to Vancouver Christmas Markets Denise Wegener.

“Compared to the first year of the market in 2010, we’ve more than doubled our attendants over the years,” Wegener says.

“It’s been an exciting journey.”

And with the growing success, they’ve expanded.

“80 vendors, the highest’s number of vendors we’ve ever had, 24 food and beverage vendors and 56 merchandise vendors,” she says.

The market includes a variety of traditional German and European gifts, quick bites and refreshments.

Guests can get into the holiday spirit by taking walk in the picture perfect twinkling pathway or meet Santa himself.

The market is open daily from 11:30 a.m. to 9:30 p.m. and runs until Christmas Eve which is open from 11:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
