Your wife is not your blood or your relative - Kenyan man warns

20 Nov 2019, 12:51

- A Kenyan man has taken to Twitter to share his interesting take on the relationship between a man and his wife

- According to Ja Loka, a wife is the only member of a man’s family who is not related to him

- Loka added that a man should always be cautious because the woman can turn into anything

Matters of love and relationship have left many people on social media becoming experts and advisers on the issue not minding that their opinions can be controversial.

Legit.ng has sighted a now deleted tweet on social media where a Kenyan man identified as Ja Loka, explained that a wife is the only part of a man’s family who is not his relative.
