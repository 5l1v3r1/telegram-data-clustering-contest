DiData, IWG ink Azure cloud transformation deal

20 Nov 2019, 12:55 by Staff Writer

Systems integrator Dimension Data (DiData) has partnered with “flexible” workspace provider, the International Workplace Group (IWG).

In a statement, DiData describes the partnership as one of its “most strategic cloud transformation and managed services deals”.

According to the statement, IWG decided to partner with DiData and its digital business solutions arm, Britehouse, because of its Microsoft Azure skills and managed services capabilities.

Another key driver for IWG to move to Azure is risk reduction, meaning DiData could implement cost-effective disaster recovery in cloud and increase application availability, it adds.

In terms of the deal, IWG will enhance its global technology platform to create greater agility, application availability and resilience for critical applications and on Microsoft Azure.

IWG CIO Andre Sharpe says: “With our growing number of large corporate customers and new franchise partners, this was the right time to invest in improving our services even further.
