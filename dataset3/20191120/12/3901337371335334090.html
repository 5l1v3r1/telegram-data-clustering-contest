In news that completely rocked the football world, Jose Mourinho’s appointment as the new Tottenham Hotspur head coach sent social media into a frenzy. Have you seen some of the reactions?

Mourinho joining the London-based side will see him now manage his third different English club, having previously mentored both Chelsea and Manchester United.
