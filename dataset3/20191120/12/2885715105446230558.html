India lose thriller to Pakistan in ACC Emerging Cup semifinal

India lost the semifinal against Pakistan by three runs on Wednesday. They needed 7 runs off the last over to win, but could only score 4 runs.

20 Nov 2019, 12:46 by PTI

India crashed out of the Asian Cricket Council Emerging Teams Cup after losing by three runs to arch-rivals Pakistan in the first semifinal in Dhaka on Wednesday.

Pakistan dished out a strong top-order display to put up 267 for seven on the board after opting to bat.
