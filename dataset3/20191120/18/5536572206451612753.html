Dez Bryant Seemingly Takes Shots At The Cowboys, Saints On Twitter: ‘Ask Any Black Player’ (TWEETS)

20 Nov 2019, 18:47 by Darrelle Lincoln

Dez Bryant stated he would be trying to get back into the league in 2019, but ranting on Twitter is certainly not how you do it.

The free agent WR jumped on Twitter Tuesday to ask Colin Kaepernick a couple of questions after his controversial workout this past weekend, and it had social media coming after Bryant.

In the midst of his responses about Kaepernick — Bryant seemingly blasted the Saints and the Cowboys, first taking aim at the team that drafted him and their treatment of Black players.

“Ask any black player on the Cowboys how I acted.”
