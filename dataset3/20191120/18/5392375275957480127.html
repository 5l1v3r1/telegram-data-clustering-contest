20 Nov 2019, 18:24 by Marco Lancaster

Rate this post

The German Bank N26 is already supporting Google Pay, but it has now expanded to several European countries. Moreover, half of the additions are based in Denmark. The Sparekassen (savings banks) now support NFC payments, so it’s easy for them to join Google’s Payment platform.

This is the second massive Google Pay rollout in 2019.  Actually, the last one was in July when the company added support to 25 new banks. The service is currently available in 30 countries in the Globe. Google expects to increase the platform’s popularity in the coming years with more countries and more banks offering Google Pay integration.
