Is Maya returning to Emmerdale and who is the father of her baby?

20 Nov 2019, 18:00

Emmerdale viewers had a shock last night when they learned that paedophile Maya Stepney (Louisa Clein) is pregnant.

Tuesday night’s episode of the soap saw Maya released from prison after serving half her sentence, following her grooming and sexual abuse of teenager Jacob Gallagher (Joe Warren-Plant).

However the character was seen sporting a noticeable baby bump as she left prison and headed off.

So just who is the father of her baby – and could this mark a return to Emmerdale for the character?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who is the father of Maya’s baby in Emmerdale?

As it stands we don’t know who the father of Maya’s baby is.

While the character was having a sexual relationship with Jacob, she was also having a relationship with his dad David Metcalfe (Matthew Wolfenden).

So either one of them could feasibly be Maya’s babyfather.

The shock pregnancy was revealed as Jacob learned from David and Leyla (Roxi Shahidi) that Maya was due for release – and initially he seemed to brush the news off, claiming he was going out to the cinema with Leanna (Mimi Slinger).

While David and Leyla were pleased that the teenager appeared to be moving on with his life, he later disappeared as he attempted to track Maya down.

He failed to do so, but did attempt to ring her – with viewers seeing Maya deleting Jacob’s number from her phone before walking off, and making it very clear that she’s expecting.

Is Maya returning to Emmerdale?

As it stands Maya is not set to come back, with a spokesperson for the soap telling metro.co.uk: ‘There are no current plans for Maya to return.’

However Louisa Clein – who took to Twitter in the wake of the reveal – recently explained that she wasn’t ruling it out, adding that there could be more to explore with the character.

‘There’s certainly room for the story to develop,’ she said at the Inside Soap Awards.

‘We purposefully made this story go for a long long time and there will be long long repercussions with Jacob, with David, with all the people she affected in the village, so who knows?’

Emmerdale airs weeknights on ITV at 7pm.
