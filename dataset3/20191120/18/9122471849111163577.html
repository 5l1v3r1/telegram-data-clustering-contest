ESPN Analyst Releases Score Prediction For Iowa vs. Illinois

20 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Dan Lyons

Kinnick Stadium has been a nightmare for top teams in recent seasons. That continued last weekend, with Iowa’s win over previously undefeated Minnesota. The Hawkeyes need to avoid a letdown spot on Saturday, with a surprising Illinoi squad coming to Iowa City.

The Fighting Illini are just one game worse than the Hawkeyes in the record books, and have the same 4-3 mark in Big Ten play. Starting with their shocking win over Wisconsin, Lovie Smith’s team has ripped off four straight win, beating Michigan State last weekend to clinch bowl eligibility.

Even with Illinois’ hot streak, Iowa enters as a big favorite. The current line is hovering around Iowa -15.5 or -16, depending on the sportsbook.

ESPN’s Bill Connelly, creator of the SP+ ratings system, has logged its Week 4 picks and score projections. The system basically has that line right on the money.
