Cristiano Ronaldo could quit Juventus if Old Lady fail to win UCL title

20 Nov 2019, 18:15

Cristiano Ronaldo could be leaving Italian champions Juventus for a sensational return to Premier League club Manchester United if the Old Lady fail to win Champions League title.

Spanish panelist Eduardo Inda made the claim that Ronaldo will not play for the Turin based club next term if they should fail to win Champions League this season.

And Old Trafford is reported to be his likely destination as he could rejoin the club where he made his name in football.

His future at Juventus looks uncertain after his reported fallout with Old Lady manager Maurizio Sarri.
