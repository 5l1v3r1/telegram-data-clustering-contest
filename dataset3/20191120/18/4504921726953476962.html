Miss Namibia reveals national costume

20 Nov 2019, 18:38

Miss Namibia Nadja Breytenbach is leaving for Atlanta, Georgia in the USA on Thursday, she will be taking part in the 68th edition of the Miss Universe pageant.
The beauty queen on Wednesday revealed her national costume which is designed by South African designer Mzukisi Mbane.
Director of the Miss Namibia pageant Connie Maritz explained that the national costume is structured to celebrate the strength of Namibia.
"The headpiece reflects the formidable and unique collection of Africa's wildlife, the bodysuit is inspired and embraces references to the diverse and interesting many other tribes within the country," said Maritz.
Breytenbach said she is excited to wear the costume at the global event and is looking forward to representing Namibia.
