Ronnie Fieg Teases KITH x Nike Air Force 1 Collaboration

Preppy yet sporty.

20 Nov 2019, 18:29 by Ross Dwyer

KITH frontman Ronnie Fieg has taken to Instagram to unveil a new Nike Air Force 1 Low collaboration. Marking KITH’s first AF1 effort, the familiar silhouette has been equipped with a clean color scheme that’s simultaneously preppy and sporty, complete with plenty of special detailing.

The upper is dressed in a crisp white leather, accented by a dark navy blue sockliner, red tongue badge and gold detailing on the lace tips and dubrae. Special branding factors heavily into the design as well: the midfoot Swoosh shows a tonal debossed KITH box logo print; the aforementioned tongue badge equips another KITH logo; and a small forest green Swoosh appears on the lateral mudguard. An eggshell white midsole and outsole complete the look.

Although this is KITH’s first go-around with the AF1, it’s no stranger to Nike collaborations: Fieg’s brand last worked with Nike on a Nike LeBron 15 “Long Live The King” collection in 2018, and recently teamed with Converse for a Mickey Mouse-themed set of Chuck 70s as well.

A release date for the KITH x Nike Air Force 1 Low has not yet been announced, but it’ll likely make its debut in early 2020. Stay tuned for more info as it’s available.

KITH isn’t the only retailer to take on the Force as of late: A Ma Maniére recently crafted luxurious “Hand Wash Cold” iterations of both the Air Force 1 High and Low.
