Could this be the final design of the HONOR Watch Magic 2?

20 Nov 2019, 18:36

Just recently, several promotional images of what seems to be the upcoming HONOR Watch Magic 2 has been posted up on weibo.com showcasing what might be the final design of the smartwatch. Based on the image, it seems that the watch sports the familiar tachymeter scale on the outer rim, 2.5D glass as well as two physical buttons that the user would interact with.

Not only that but based on a statement by gsmarena.com, there will be a male as well as a female edition of the HONOR Watch Magic 2. The smartwatch is expected to be unveiled alongside the HONOR V30 in Beijing, China on 26 November 2019. With all that said, there is currently no official information in terms of the exact tech specs, pricing as well as the Malaysia release date for now.
