American Medical Association seeks total ban on e-cigarette, vaping products

20 Nov 2019, 18:12 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

The American Medical Association is calling for an immediate ban on all electronic cigarettes and vaping devices.

In a news release, the AMA said its House of Delegates has adopted a policy to “urgently advocate" at state and federal levels to ban all e-cigarette and vaping products.

The exception, the AMA said, are products that may be approved by the Food and Drug Administration for "tobacco-cessation purposes" and those available by prescription.

The AMA adopted the stance at its policy meeting this week in San Diego, according to The Associated Press.
