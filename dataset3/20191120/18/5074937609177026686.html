Huawei smart device provides more health functions

20 Nov 2019, 18:10

Accra, Nov 19, GNA - Huawei, a technology company has said it had included health functions in its smart devices to provide basic health check of users.

A statement copied to the Ghana News Agency said the company was keeping its focus on health to improve the health of users.

It said the Huawei Smart Scale is the smart way to keep track of all body values.

The statement noted that smart scale was Capable of reading up to nine body compositions in one detection, and would help users keep track of their weight, body fat percentage, body mass index and even more detailed readings such body water percentage, visceral fat, muscle and bone mass and protein levels.

The statement said these functions could be performed to the high precision of the Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis chip, four pressure sensors and 360Â° adjustable scale feet to achieve accurate and convenient measurement.

It said the device, which had features such as a Smart Alarm Clock, a personal fitness coach and a user-friendly app could enable users to keep track of their body.
