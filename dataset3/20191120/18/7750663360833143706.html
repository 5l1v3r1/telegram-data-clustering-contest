Real Madrid 'unhappy with Gareth Bale's bad joke'

Real Madrid have reportedly been left unhappy with Gareth Bale's actions at the end of Wales' Euro 2020 qualifier with Hungary.

20 Nov 2019, 18:43 by Darren Plant

Having helped his country qualify for next summer's Finals, Bale was given a national flag which included the words 'Wales. Golf. Madrid', which plays up to a suggestion in the press that the forward has regularly played golf during his stint in Spain.

However, according to ESPN, Real officials have been left angry with Bale, with it being claimed that they regarded the actions as 'a joke in very bad taste'.

Despite having over two-and-a-half years left on his contract, Bale has been heavily linked with an exit from the Bernabeu in one of the upcoming transfer windows.

The report suggests that Real are increasingly open to parting ways with the 30-year-old in January.
