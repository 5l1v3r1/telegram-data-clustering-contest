Open defecation-free Nigeria - Buhari signs Executive Order 009 towards

20 Nov 2019, 18:17

- President Buhari has signed an executive order on open defecation

- The order is to help make Nigeria open defecation free

- Consequently, a secretariat would be established to ensure that Nigerians have access to toilets and latrines wherever they are

Following ranking which placed Nigeria as second amongst the nations in the world with the highest number of people practising open defecation, President Muhammadu Buhari has on Wednesday, November 20, signed Executive Order 009 entitled, The Open Defecation-Free Nigeria by 2025 and Other Related Matters Order.

According to presidential aide, Femi Adesina, by the Order, Nigeria is committed to being open defecation free by 2025.

With this, a national secretariat called “Clean Nigeria Campaign Secretariat” will now be established in the Federal Ministry of Water Resources.
