Arsenal fans react as Everton star Alex Iwobi posts Tweet after excellent international break

20 Nov 2019, 18:45 by Will Butcher

Alex Iwobi helped Nigeria get their 2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualifying campaign off to a flyer with two wins from two during the international brerak – and Arsenal fans have reacted to the Everton star’s Tweet.

Nigeria took on Benin and Lesotho in a matter of days as they looked to take maximum points from their opening two games. Iwobi, 23, started and finished both matches as Nigeria did indeed win both matches – scoring six times in the process.

Gernot Rohr’s side picked up a 2-1 win over Benin, before coming from behind to secure a 4-2 win over Lesotho. Iwobi scored Nigeria’s first, with an excellent first-time finish with his right foot from an acute angle.

Iwobi, who was born in Nigeria but played for England’s youth setup until the Under-18 level before switching his allegiance to Nigeria – where the Everton star has since clocked up 40 caps for his country.
