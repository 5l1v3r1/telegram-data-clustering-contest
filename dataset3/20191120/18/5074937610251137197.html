Bibiani Anhwiaso Bekwai Assembly supports community

20 Nov 2019, 18:35 by Michael Owusu Duodu, GNA

Kwamekrom (WN/R), Nov. 19, GNA - Mr Sahib M. Abdul Rahman, Municipal Co-ordinating Director (MCD) for the Bibiani- Anhwiaso- Bekwai Municipal Assembly (BABMA), has said the Assembly is committed to ensure equitable distribution of local and national resources towards the development of all communities under its jurisdiction.

He called on the various communities in the municipality, to identify their priority areas for development and rally behind the Assembly, to mobilise the needed resources to meet their needs.

Mr Rahman said this at the commissioning of a 20-seater modern Toilet facility for the people of Kwamekrom, near Bibiani in the Western North Region.

He said Kwamekrom was strategically located in the municipality, and thus urged the people to take good care of the facility and other amenities provided them, to improve upon their lots.
