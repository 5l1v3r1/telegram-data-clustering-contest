Death of Bangor man (68) treated as suspicious

20 Nov 2019, 08:58 by David Young, PA

Police are treating the death of a man in Co Down as suspicious.

The body of the 68-year-old was found at his home in Bangor on Tuesday evening.

Officers have launched an investigation into the circumstances of the incident in Birch Drive.

A post-mortem examination is to be conducted to determine the cause of death.

Police have released no other details.
