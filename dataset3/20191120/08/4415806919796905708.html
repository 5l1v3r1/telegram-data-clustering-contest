Son of former German president stabbed to death while giving lecture in Berlin

20 Nov 2019, 08:30 by By Press Association

The son of a former German president has been stabbed to death while he was giving a lecture at a hospital in Berlin where he worked as a head doctor.

Police said they have arrested a 57-year-old man over the death of Fritz von Weizsaecker at the Schlosspark-Klinik after a man jumped up from the audience and attacked him.

Another man who tried to stop the attack on Tuesday evening was seriously wounded.

Mr von Weizsaecker died at the scene despite immediate attention from colleagues.
