Realme X2 Pro, Realme 5s with quad-cameras launched in India: Price, specs

The Realme X2 Pro has a quad rear camera setup that includes a 64-megapixel primary sensor with 20x zoom

20 Nov 2019, 08:57 by BS Web team

Chinese smartphone brand Realme on Wednesday launched in India the Realme X2 Pro and the Realme 5s. The Realme X2 Pro is the company's first premium smartphone that features Qualcomm Snapdragon 855+ system-on-chip, 6.5-inch sAMOLED screen of 90Hz refresh rate, quad-camera setup with a 64-megapixel main shooter on the back, and a 50W SuperVOOC fast charging technology.

The Realme 5s, on the other hand, boasts a 5,000mAh battery and 48MP-based quad-camera setup on the back.

The Realme X2 Pro comes in 8GB RAM + 128GB internal storage and 12GB RAM and 256GB internal storage, priced at Rs Rs 29, 999 and Rs, 33,999, respectively.

Realme X2 Pro specifications and features

The Realme X2 Pro boots Android 9 Pie operating system with ColorOS 6.1 user interface on top. It has a 6.5-inch fullHD+ (1080x2400 pixels) super AMOLED display of 90Hz refresh rate and 135Hz touch sampling rate. The phone is made of glass and metal and boasts Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection on the front and back.

The phone is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 855+ octa-core chipset, paired with up to 12GB RAM and 256GB internal storage (non-expandible).

The Realme X2 Pro has a quad rear cameras, which include a 64MP primary sensor of f/1.8 aperture, a 13MP telephoto sensor of an f/2.5 aperture, an 8MP ultra-wide sensor of 115-degree field-of-view (FoV) and an f/2.2 aperture, and a 2MP depth sensor. On the front, the phone has a 16MP selfie camera. The phone has a 4,000mAh battery and an in-display fingerprint scanner. It comes bundled with a 50W SuperVOOC Flash Charger.

Connectivity is covered by Bluetooth v5.0, NFC, USB type-C, and 3.5mm headphone jack. There are dual speakers with Dolby Atmos and Hi-Res Audio support.

Realme 5s specifications and features

The Realme5s has a 5,000mAh battery. It is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 SoC. It has a 6.5-inch waterdrop notch display. The Realme 5s is priced at Rs 9,999 and Rs 10,999 for 4GB RAM+ 64 GB storage and 4GB RAM + 128GB storage configuration, respectively.

The Realme 5s comes in three colour variants -- crystal red, crystal blue and crystal purple.
