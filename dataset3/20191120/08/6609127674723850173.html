Watch Rangers star Alfredo Morelos miss golden chance in Colombia’s win over Ecuador

20 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Ewan Paton

RANGERS star Alfredo Morelos missed a golden opportunity as Colombia defeated Ecuador.

The Light Blues striker played the first 45 minutes as Carlos Queiroz's men secured back-to-back 1-0 wins.

Mateus Uribe scored the games only goal early in the second half - but Morelos should have found the net in the 26 minute.

The hitman capitalised on a defensive error that sent him one-on-one with Ecuador goalie Johan Padilla.

The 23-year-old tried to give the keeper the eyes as he sent his effort near post, but the stopper was able to get down and turn his effort around the post.

Colombia had several opportunities to extend their advantage in the second period but were denied by the Ecuador No1.
