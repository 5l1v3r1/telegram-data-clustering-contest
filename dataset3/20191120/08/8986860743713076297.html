Medikal gives Ghana Jesus Mmebusem a bike after 4syte MVA’s

20 Nov 2019, 08:24 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

AMG rapper Medikal has dashed Kumawood actor Mmebusem a bicycle after their splendid performance over the weekend at the 4syte Music Video awards 2019 at the Kempinski Hotel in Accra.

Medikal on the night of then event mounted the stage with the newest sensation Ghana Jesus Mmebusem which made the crowd go gaga.

Well, many patrons who went for the event adjudged Medikal and Mmebusem performance as the best performance on the night.

According to reports, Medikal after their performance decided to reward Mmebusem handsomely for wowing the crowd on the night.

In a new photo, the local Jesus is seen posing with a beautiful bike which seems to be a gift from the VGMA Best Rapper of the Year.
