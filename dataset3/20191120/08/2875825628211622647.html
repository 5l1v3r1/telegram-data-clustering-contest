Man jailed for trying to smuggle drugs inside Kinder Surprise Eggs in prison

He is currently serving a five-year sentence for dangerous driving

20 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Brion Hoban

A man who was caught in prison with Kinder Surprise Eggs containing drugs has been jailed.

Patrick Morgan, 33, pleaded guilty at Dublin Circuit Criminal Court to possession of cannabis and heroin for sale or supply in Mountjoy Prison on April 6, 2016.

On Tuesday, Garda Daniel Keaney told prosecutor Fiona McGowan that on that date the accused was under the influence of an intoxicant and searched.
