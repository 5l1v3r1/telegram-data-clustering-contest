Dabangg 3: Salman Khan Could Now Be A Permanent Fixture On Your WhatsApp, Here's How

20 Nov 2019, 08:14 by Saheli Maity

To mark 30 days to the release of Dabangg 3, for the first time ever, Chulbul Pandey's animated avatar storms the internet with customized Dabangg 3 GIFs and stickers

Salman Khan is pulling out all the stops from making his the upcoming film, Dabangg 3 a full entertainer. Earlier, he first screened the trailer of the film to his fans before it had hit the internet and today, Salman is again doing something and sweet and thoughtful for his fans. Since we live in an era of social media and more than words, people usually use a lot of gifs and emojis to express their feelings on social media. Well, following on the same route, the makers have now decided to create an animated character of Chulbul Pandey that will now help you to converse in full Dabangg style.

With one month to go for the release of the film, Chulbul Pandey has a unique gift for the fans! Now the fans can express themselves the Chulbul way with Dabangg 3 GIFs. GIFs to be launched on WhatsApp on 20th of November, they will be available on the GIPHY homepage from 21st and Stickers will be live on Instagram, Snapchat and TikTok from 22nd November! Dabangayyi takes over the web and audience hooked to Dabangg 3 GIFs and stickers. Just a few minutes back, Salman’s home production shared a video of the same, tweeting, “Ab WhatsApp par, Dabangg 3 GIFs se apni dabanggayi dikhao aur chats ko superfun banao.”
