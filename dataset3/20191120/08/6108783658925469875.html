Star Wars Jedi Fallen Order patch sorts textures, slow loading time

20 Nov 2019, 08:27 by Steve Wright

Available now on PC and coming soon to Xbox and PS4.

A Star Wars Jedi Fallen Order patch has been released to PC and will soon be available on both Xbox One and PS4, developer Respawn has confirmed.

The patch does the following, as confirmed via Reddit:

  • We’ve fixed a texture flickering issue that would display on the screen while driving the AT-AT on Kashyyyk
  • Sometimes the input from the arrow keys would fail to respond within menus, this has now been fixed.
  • Dynamic resolution settings will now save properly.
  • Fixed an issued where the game could become slow and loading times longer than expected.

On Xbox One X, we’re encountering texture pop-in issues that sometimes means that we don’t have a floor to walk on. Oops.
