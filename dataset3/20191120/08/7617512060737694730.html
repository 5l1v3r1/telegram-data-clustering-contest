Crash on Trans-Canada Highway causes major traffic delays

20 Nov 2019, 08:13 by Cindy E. Harnett

A crash on the Trans-Canada Highway near the Six Mile exit has caused major traffic delays.

The City of Langford reports traffic is backed up in the following areas, primarily on the approaches to the Millstream overpass:

• Northbound on Veterans Memorial Parkway is backed up to Goldstream Avenue

• On Strandlund Avenue approaching Veterans Memorial Parkway

• Northbound on Peatt Road is also backed up to Goldstream Avenue

• Eastbound on Brock Avenue approaching Peatt Road

• Southbound on Millstream Road is backed up to Treanor Avenue

Langford’s engineering department advises drivers travelling within Langford take Treanor Avenue to Setchfield Avenue to Florence Lake Road.

Bus transportation for the Sooke School District has been notified of potential delays in these areas.

B.C. Transit has also advised its customers of potential delays.

West Shore RCMP could not be immediately contacted.
