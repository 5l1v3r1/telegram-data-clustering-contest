Rag ’n’ Bone Man and wife Beth Rouy ‘split six months after getting married’

20 Nov 2019, 08:40

Rag ‘n’ Bone Man and his wife, Beth Rouy have split up after six months of marriage, according to new reports.

The couple, who tied the knot in May, have reportedly gone their separate ways but are ‘staying amicable’ for the sake of their son.

Rag ‘n’ Bone Man, whose real name is Rory Graham, married his long-time girlfriend in Lewes Registry Office, and the bride, groom and wedding party all wore tracksuits to mark the occasion.

A source told The Sun: ‘It’s a great shame, but these things happen. Everyone is staying amicable, and they have a son together who is their top priority.

‘He likes to keep a low profile so doesn’t want to make a big deal out of it, but there are no explosive rows and he isn’t likely to say anything about it.’

The singer quit social media in October, telling fans he was ‘taking time off’ from Twitter and Instagram.

Rag ‘n’ Bone Man, 34, and Beth, 26, live together in a three-acre gated estate near Brighton with their two-year-old son Reuben, and had been together for 10 years.

The source added: ‘It looks like it’s a very sad end to what was a very loving and long-term relationship.

‘The wedding pictures show what a quirky couple they were, which makes how this has ended so soon even more heartbreaking.

‘Only they know what really happened and the truth of anything that was said.’

The Human star had spoken out about how much he was loving married life in June, telling reporters: ‘It’s great, man.

‘We’ve been together for ten years anyway so it’s exactly the same. We’ve just got the same name now.’

Rag ‘n’ Bone Man has largely tried to keep his private life out of the spotlight, so it’s unlikely he will be speaking out publicly about the reported break up.

Metro.co.uk has contacted Rag ‘n’ Bone Man’s reps for comment.
