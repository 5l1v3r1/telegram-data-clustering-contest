Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2’s Parth Samthaan DENIES Dating Erica Fernandes,'Not Aware Of These Rumours’

20 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Lubna Khan

Parth Samthaan, who plays Anurag Basu in Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2, has opened up on rumours of dating his co-actor Erica Fernandes and has denied dating her. He said that they are good friends and is not even aware of most of these rumours related to dating her

Television actor Parth Samthaan is one of the most popular TV stars and has a massive fan following, especially female. He plays the role of Anurag Basu in the television show Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 and thee have always been rumours of him dating Erica Fernandes for a long time. Recently, they were also rumoured to have broken up and while none of them had openly confessed to their relationship, the rumours refused to die down. Erica Fernandes plays the role of Prerna on the show and now, in a recent interview, Parth Samthaan has addressed these rumours and denied dating Erica.

Recently, speaking to the Times Of India, Parth Samthaan said that Erica and he are good friends and while they do have arguments and differences at work, things are smooth between them. About the rumours, he added that he is not aware of them and need not deal with it. He said,” We are good friends and friendly even today. We are in a good space. We work together daily on our TV serial and things are smooth between us. At times, there are some arguments or differences of opinions at work, but this is normal. In fact, recently, the cast came over to my new house and we all chilled together. I am not even aware of most of these so-called rumours, so we don’t need to deal with it."
