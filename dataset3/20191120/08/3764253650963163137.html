Andy Ruiz: Boxing champion throws faster jabs at nutritionist ahead of Joshua rematch

20 Nov 2019, 08:37

- Andy Ruiz has shown hunger for glory after he was spotted throwing jabs at his trainer in a more faster note

- The Mexican-American will be defending his belts when he meets Anthony Joshua in a rematch of their 2018 fight

- The heavyweight star has been tipped for glory by WBC heavyweight champion Deontay Wilder

Andy Ruiz has sent a stern warning to Anthony Joshua ahead of their much-anticipated rematch bout after the Mexican-American was spotted throwing more jabs faster than before at his nutritionist.

The boxing heavyweight champion has a huge task ahead of him when he meets Joshua to defend his belts.

The 30-year-old star stunned the world when he floored Joshua through a seventh-round knockout to cart away the Briton's WBA, IBF & WBO belts in June 1, 2018, in New York.

Ahead of their "fight of the dunes" bout, the boxing champion had promised to be "10lbs lighter" as he seeks to be a better fighter.
