Small arsenal of weapons found in Land Rover Freelander stopped in Swadlincote

Two people have now been arrested

20 Nov 2019, 08:23 by Jenny Moody

A driver was found with a small arsenal weapons in his car after being pulled over by police in South Derbyshire.

Officers stopped the man's Land Rover Freelander in Swadlincote and found the weapons, which included knives and a hammer.

Police say the driver also had no licence or insurance.

A picture shared by Derbyshire Roads Policing Unit on Twitter showed he had two knives, a claw hammer and what appears to be a small axe with a another knife on the other end.

A second picture shows the silver Land Rover which had been pulled over, with a police car in front of it.

The driver and passenger of the vehicle have now been arrested by police.

The Tweet from the police reads: "Swadlincote. Freelander driven by male with no licence or insurance. Weapons located throughout the car.

"Driver and passenger arrested, knives off the street and vehicle #Seized . Thanks for @SwadlincoteSNT support. #Crime #Traffic "
