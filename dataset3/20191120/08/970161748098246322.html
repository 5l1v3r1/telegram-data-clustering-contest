Rebekah Vardy thrown surprise baby shower as she prepares to welcome fifth child

20 Nov 2019, 08:14

Rebekah Vardy is set to welcome her fifth child very soon, with footballer husband Jamie.

And the 37-year-old celebrated the little one in style after her sister threw a surprise baby shower.

Taking to Instagram, the I’m A Celebrity star gave fans a glimpse into the sweet bash, complete with flowers, personalised balloons and cake-pops.

Basically the dream afternoon.

‘Such an amazing surprise…,’ she captioned a series of photos from the big day. ‘Thank you to my amazing Sister. Love you all xxxx.’

And husband Jamie was among the first to praise the mum-to-be, sliding in to comment: ‘You deserve it xxxx.’

While pal Nicola McLean simply posted a string of love heart emojis.

‘Looking really well, good luck with the new baby,’ a fan wrote.
