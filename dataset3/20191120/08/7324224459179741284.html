Walsall see off Shrewsbury

Walsall moved to within striking distance of the top three in Midlands 2 West (North).

20 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Nick Elwell

A 32-3 success at Shrewsbury lifted the Delves Road men to within five points of third-placed Leek.

Walsall completely dominated the opening quarter of the contest and had the win wrapped up inside the opening 15 minutes after establishing a 24-0 lead.

Jack Crowhurst was the first man on the scoresheet, charging in from 30 metres, with Roger Weston adding the conversion.
