50 Cent Lusts Over Lizzo & Taunts Drake Upon Return To Instagram

20 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Kyle Eustice

50 Cent has been absent from Instagram for the past week following a sparring session with fellow Power actor Naturi Naughton. Evidently, his hiatus is over and the infamous social media troll has made his triumphant return to the platform.

On Wednesday (November 20), Fiddy shared several new posts, but the most recent one finds him lusting over Lizzo, who was just nominated for eight Grammy Awards. He also managed to drag Drake into it.

“@lizzobeeating booty look good,” he wrote alongside a suggestive video of the singer. “I just saw @champagnepapi look at it. Lol.”

50 claimed he deactivated his account last week, albeit the timing was interesting considering he’d been accused of bullying Naughton in the days leading up to his Instagram disappearing act.

Earlier this week, he denied mistreating his co-star, tweeting, “I was sticking up for her, they just went and made some shit up.”

In addition to his recent Lizzo post, the Power executive producer also commemorated his return with a Rick James meme and another lightweight jab at Naughton.
