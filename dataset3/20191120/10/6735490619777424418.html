Dolly Parton Issues #JoleneChallenge Ahead Of ‘Heartstrings’ Premiere

20 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Katie Colley

Dolly Parton has launched her very own social media challenge.

The legendary country star issued the #JoleneChallenge ahead of her show “Heartstrings”, which launches on Netflix on November 22.

The upcoming eight-episode anthology series brings to life the stories, memories, and inspiration behind her most beloved musical hits, including the 1973 classic “Jolene”.
