Man Utd fans plead with Jose Mourinho to sign Nemanja Matic for Tottenham

20 Nov 2019, 10:48

Manchester United fans have urged Jose Mourinho to sign Nemanja Matic for Tottenham after he was appointed manager at Spurs.

Tottenham’s surprise sacking of Mauricio Pochettino was quickly followed by the announcement Mourinho would be taking charge.

Mourinho has not worked in football management since he was sacked by United last December and Red Devils fans have already sent a plea to their former boss.

Matic is struggling to get into Ole Gunnar Solskjaer’s team this season and has made just three appearances in the Premier League.

The midfielder was already being tipped with a January transfer exit, but now United fans are pleading with Mourinho to take Matic off their hands.
