Watch DJ Shadow Perform With De La Soul On Kimmel

20 Nov 2019, 10:03 by Peter Helman

Pioneering Bay Area producer DJ Shadow teamed up with De La Soul on “Rocket Fuel,” the first single from his new album Our Pathetic Age. And last night, they teamed up again, performing live together for the first time ever to do “Rocket Fuel” on Jimmy Kimmel Live joined by turntablists Babu (Dilated Peoples) and DJ Melo D (Beat Junkies) and a five-piece horn section.
