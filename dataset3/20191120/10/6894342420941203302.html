realme 5S Now Official; Essentially realme 5 With 48MP Camera

20 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Ian Chee

The realme 5 was announced alongside the realme 5 Pro, the latter having the 48MP camera. We can only assume there is demand for the realme 5 to also have a 48MP main shooter, because that’s what the company has recently announced in India. The new phone is called the realme 5S.

As mentioned, the realme 5S is mostly identical to the standard realme 5. It comes with the same Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 chipset, 5000mAh battery, and the same 13MP front facing camera. Three of the cameras on its quad-camera setup are also identical, comprising of an 8MP wide-angle camera, a 2MP protrait shooter, and a 2MP macro camera.
