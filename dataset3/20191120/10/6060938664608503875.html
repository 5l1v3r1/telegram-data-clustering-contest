Tiger Shroff's pictures from the sets of 'Baaghi 3' go viral on the internet; view here

20 Nov 2019, 10:50 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Not only is Tiger Shroff basking in the success of his last release but at the same time he is also gearing up for his next which is the third instalment of his popular 'Baaghi' franchise in Serbia.

Taking to Instagram, the 29-year-old actor shared a few glimpses of himself from the sets of the film with a caption which read as "#baaghi3 #actionday2." He can be seen sporting a sleeveless shirt and cargo pants as he poses in front of the lens.

With Tiger, all set to take the action bar set by him to the next level in the Ahmed Khan high octane action flick, fans are eagerly waiting to see their favourite star on the celluloid once again. His impressive performance alongside Hrithik Roshan has garnered him a lot of accolades and now Tiger looks adamant to entertain the audience once again by flaunting his action skills.

The film which also brings back the popular pair of Shraddha Kapoor and Tiger from the first instalment of the famous franchise will also feature Riteish Deshmukh and Vijay Varma in pivotal roles.
