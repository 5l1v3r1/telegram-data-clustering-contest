20 Nov 2019, 10:58 by Archie Blade

“Mourinho Out” trends on twitter within an hour after appointment announced. The ex-Chelsea manager has not received a warm welcome at North London.

After years of steering the ship at West London, former Chelsea manager, Jose Mourinho has now set his sights on another of their rivals; this time a much more direct one in Tottenham. While his arrival at Manchester United was greeted with much fanfare, Spurs fans have already rejected him.

Mourinho has been out of job for a year since his sacking at Old Trafford with Manchester United. However, the sacking of Mauricio Pochettino has opened the doors of yet another Premier League side for the Portuguese.

While the Portuguese is a pedigreed manager and has won several titles over the years, there is a growing fear that he is past his best and has been figured out. The argument can be made that at Tottenham he will have a better squad at his disposal than he had at United and could guide them to a trophy. However, Tottenham fans have already made their minds about their new manager.

After being treated to exciting football in the last 5 years or so, Spurs fans are now worried that the club may adopt a much more careful and defensive approach. There is also the possibility of the club enduring drama with Mourinho being such a controversial figure in comparison to the more stable Pochettino.

Fans have thus turned their back on their manager despite him only being an hour ago and took to twitter to let their feelings known.
