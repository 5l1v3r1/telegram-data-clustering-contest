Malaika Arora, Gauri Khan, Karan Johar come together for a special event

20 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Vedanshi Pathak

Ace designer duo Falguni Shane Peacock inaugurated their new store in Mumbai last night, which has been designed by Gauri Khan. B-town is buzzing with events every single day and last night too, we witnessed the same as many celebs came in to cheer for the designer duo as well as appreciate Gauri’s efforts to make the store look nothing short of regal. Our lenses snapped, Gauri Khan, designer duo Falguni Shane Peacock, Ananya Panday, Bhavna Panday, Jacqueline Fernandez, Karan Johar, Malaika Arora, Amrita Arora, Shanaya Kapoor, Maheep Kapoor, Seema Khan and other celebs at the event. 

All the celebs were dressed in ritzy attires however, the ones who caught our attention the most were Ananya Panday in a stripped shimmery dress, Malaika Arora nailing a pantsuit with a bralette and Shanaya Kapoor turning heads in an animal print mini dress. Check out all the pictures below.
