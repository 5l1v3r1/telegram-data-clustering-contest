Boo Williams launches label, Boo Moonman Records, with new Outer Limits EP

The one-track 12-inch is out December 13th.

20 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Carlos Hawthorn

Boo Williams is starting a record label.

The first release on Boo Moonman Records is Outer Limits, a one-track EP by Williams himself. Out next week on vinyl and digitally from December 13th, it'll be the Chicago artist's first outing of 2019.

Releases on the label will start at catalogue number 100 and work backwards "until it's time to start a new label," according to the press release.
