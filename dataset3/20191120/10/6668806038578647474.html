Boyfriend Rohman Shawl’s birthday surprise for Sushmita Sen is too adorable

20 Nov 2019, 10:24 by Filmfare

Sushmita Sen is one actress from B-town who has proved that one can come out victorious even while riding against the tide. The supermom of two, Renee and Alisah, turned 44 yesterday and while she took to social media to post a video of herself nailing a physical task which showed that her fitness level is max currently, the actress later shared a glimpse of her birthday celebration too. Sushmita has been in a relationship with model Rohman Shawl for a while now and on her birthday, the hottie made sure he made his ladylove jump with joy. 

Rohman planned a special surprise for Sushmita and the actress took to Instagram to share videos from the all heart celebration. 

A tent on the terrace, ferry lights, a puppy, cake and heartfelt messages from near and dear ones hanging on balloons were all part of the surprise and Sushmita was beaming with happiness as she was welcomed by her kids and Rohman there.
