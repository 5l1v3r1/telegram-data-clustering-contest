I’m A Celebrity viewers beg Holly Willoughby to return as host

She hosted the show last year with Dec Donnelly

20 Nov 2019, 10:42 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity are begging Holly Willoughby to return as host.

The This Morning presenter host the show last year alongside Dec Donnelly as Ant McPartlin took a break from the spotlight following his drink-drive arrest earlier that year.

She was an instant hit with fans and made ratings soar, but it was only a one-off stint.

Ant returned to this year's series with Dec and many viewers were over the moon.
