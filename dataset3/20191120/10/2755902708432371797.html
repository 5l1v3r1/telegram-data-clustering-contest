WATCH: Elderly Civilian Sustains Gory Head Injuries After Indiscriminate Attack By Police

20 Nov 2019, 10:57 by Andrew

An elderly civilian sustained massive head injuries on Wednesday morning after a horde of anti-riot police descended on civilians in the central business district of Harare. The police attacked civilians and opposition MDC supporters who had gathered at the Morgan Richard Tsvangirai (MRT) House, formerly Harvest House. The supporters had gathered to listen to Nelson Chamisa’s Hope of the Nation Address (HONA).
