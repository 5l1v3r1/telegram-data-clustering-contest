James Gallagher v Cal Ellenor confirmed for Bellator Dublin main event

The Strabanimal was meant to fight Ellenor in September but the Englishman was forced out of the fight with an injury

20 Nov 2019, 10:12 by Robert Hynes

James Gallagher will fight Cal Ellenor in the main event of Bellator's Dublin show in February next year.

The Strabanimal was meant to fight Ellenor in September but the Englishman was forced out of the fight with an injury.

Ellenor was stood down for the fight over a medical error, with Gallagher beating Roman Salazar with a first round choke in his absence.
