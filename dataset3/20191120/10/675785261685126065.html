Netflix to release chilling documentary about 'America's most prolific serial killer'

Netflix documentary series The Confession Killer focuses on a serial killer named Henry Lee Lucas who chillingly boasted of murdering 600 people

20 Nov 2019, 10:12 by Lewis Knight, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lewis-knight/

Netflix have released a trailer for a chilling new documentary series.

The Confession Killer tells the story of serial killer Henry Lee Lucas who was successfully convicted of murdering three people before then delivering further confessions - hundreds of them and in great detail.

However, the uncomfortable twist to this was that he may not have even done them, despite the level of detail in the description given even including sketches of his victims.

Lucas was eventually sentenced to death, but subsequent investigations soon began to show that he could not have been responsible for all of the killings he claimed were his own.

There were a great number of inaccuracies that showed he wasn't exactly being honest, but how did he know everything about the murders?

DNA test would go on to prove further contradictions to what Henry Lee Lucas was telling authorities.

So, why did the criminal justice system allow him to be convicted without true justice being done for the murders he was not guilty of?

Finally, how was Lucas able to fool so many people with his killer confessions?

Naturally, true crime fans on Netflix will be incredibly excited at the prospect of the documentary.

The official synopsis on Netflix reads: "During the early '80s, Henry Lee Lucas confessed to hundreds of murders, bringing closure to unsolved cases and grieving families.

"Even with no direct evidence linking Lucas to the crime scenes, he stunned authorities with his ability to sketch victims' portraits while citing brutal details of each attack.

"Yet journalists and attorneys found impossibilities in Lucas’ timeline, and DNA testing started to contradict his internationally-reported claims.

"THE CONFESSION KILLER, a riveting five-part docuseries, explores how the man once called America’s most prolific serial killer was really a complex figure entangled in a flawed justice system. Directed by Oscar nominee Robert Kenner (Food, Inc.) and Taki Oldham."

We can't wait to binge-watch this one!

The Confession Killer is released on December 6, 2019.
