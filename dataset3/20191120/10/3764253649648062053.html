Nigerian man weds his oyinbo lover in a beautiful ceremony

20 Nov 2019, 10:14

Currently trending on social media are photos and a video of a Nigerian man from the eastern region, who recently got married to his Asian bride in a beautiful ceremony.

Indeed, when two people find true happiness with each other, no obstacle is too big to overcome, for true love knows no colour, race, age nor distance and this couple trending online have proven this to be true.

Photos from the white wedding and a video from the traditional ceremony of the eastern Nigerian man and his Chinese bride in Guangzhou, China, have melted the hearts of many social media users.

What has left many people amazed is how stunning the bride and her family looked in the traditional Igbo outfits and how cheerfully they participated in the traditional ceremony.
