FY2020 EPS Estimates for Electronic Arts Inc. (NASDAQ:EA) Lifted by Analyst

20 Nov 2019, 10:20 by Amy Steele

Electronic Arts Inc. (NASDAQ:EA) – Analysts at Jefferies Financial Group boosted their FY2020 earnings estimates for shares of Electronic Arts in a report issued on Monday, November 18th. Jefferies Financial Group analyst A. Giaimo now expects that the game software company will earn $3.75 per share for the year, up from their previous estimate of $3.73. Jefferies Financial Group currently has a “Hold” rating and a $100.00 target price on the stock. Jefferies Financial Group also issued estimates for Electronic Arts’ Q4 2020 earnings at $0.71 EPS.
