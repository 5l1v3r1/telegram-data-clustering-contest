Omashola tells people to stop entering his DM to talk about taking their life (video)

20 Nov 2019, 10:09

- Former BBNaija housemate Omashola has asked people to stop sending him messages about taking their lives

- The ex-housemate said he has been advising them not to take their lives

- Omashola asked why they would want to die and why they keep coming to him

Former Big Brother Naija housemate Omashola has addressed his fans on social media after he continued to get messages from some of them claiming that they want to take their life.

The former reality star shared a video of himself addressing the issue on his Instagram page. He said that some depressed people have entered his direct message to reveal that they wish to take their life.

Omashola said he has tried his best to stop them and inspire them to continue to live. He noted that he does not wish to post the chats because he does not want to give the impression that taking ones' life is okay.
