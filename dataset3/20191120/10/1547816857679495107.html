Shahid Kapoor Shares What He Loves The Most About His Beloved Wife Mira Rajput

20 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Gretel Sequeira

Shahid Kapoor and Mira Rajput are one of the hottest couples in Bollywood and don’t miss any chance to prove it. Recently in an interview, the Kabir Singh actor shared what he loves the most about his beloved wife Mira, and we are amused

Shahid Kapoor and Mira Rajput have been madly in love with each other and the couple often proves they are #couplegoals. While the lovebirds have been married for quite a few years now, Shahid and Mira are considered as one of the hottest couples in B-Town for the chemistry they share with each other. After four years of marriage to wife Mira Rajput, Kabir Singh actor Shahid Kapoor shared some details about what he thinks of his wife. In a recent interview, Shahid shares what he loves the most about his beloved wife Mira and we are amused.

Kabir Singh actor, Shahid Kapoor, and Delhite Mira Rajput tied the knot in Gurgaon on July 7, 2015. Since then, Mira has been lauded by many for her easy and smooth transition from a normal girl to a star wife. Speaking of Mira Rajput, Shahid Kapoor revealed what he loves the most about his beloved wifey. In an interview with Hindustan Times, Shahid shared that wife Mira’s individuality is what drew his attention towards her. Talking about Mira Rajput and her not having any connection with the showbiz, the Kabir Singh actor said that she has never appeared out of place. Sharing a throwback memory from their wedding day, Shahid mentioned that when they got done with the marriage rituals and stepped out for some pictures, the actor shared that he remembered them both looking at each other and that moment he knew on the first day that she has her own individuality. Speaking about when the couple returned to Mumbai and hosted a get-together for friends and members of the film industry, Shahid Kapoor revealed that Mira Rajput was extremely comfortable and she doesn’t feel the need to change and adjust. Adding on further in praises of wife Mira Rajput, Shahid shared that she is who she is and people like her for that. Shahid also said that she has never tried to be someone else and that’s a great thing about her that he adores. Kapoor also shared that he always knew that Mira is going to find her own space and will definitely become her own person one day.

Meanwhile, Shahid Kapoor and Mira Rajput are blessed with two kids Misha and Zain and constantly share pictures with their little munchkins.
