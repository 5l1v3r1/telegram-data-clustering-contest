Man head butted for no apparent on Pryzm nightclub dance floor

The assailant head-butted another man in the face

20 Nov 2019, 10:53 by Jasper King

Avon and Somerset Constabulary are appealing for witnesses following an assault in a Bristol nightclub which left a man with serious face injuries.

The victim, who is in his 20s, was visiting the Pryzm nightclub on Canons Road, at about 1am on Saturday, November 9.

While on the crowded RnB room dancefloor at about 2am another man - for no apparent reason - head-butted the victim in the face.

He was shortly taken to hospital where he received treatment for a jaw which was broken in two places.

The suspect is described as a white man, in his 20s, over 6ft tall, of stocky build, with short brown/ginger hair.

On the night, he is thought to have been wearing a black Hugo Boss polo shirt with grey checked trousers.

The police would like to hear from anyone who was in the RnB room that morning who may have seen the incident.

Anyone able to help is asked to contact Avon and Somerset Constabulary quoting reference 5219259491.
