Jake Paul slams 'butt hurt' fans after hanging out with ex Erika Costell

20 Nov 2019, 10:30

Jake Paul has slammed ‘butt hurt’ fans after he was criticised for hanging out with his ex-girlfriend Erika Costell.

Ouch.

The YouTube star, who married Tana Mongeau earlier this year, caused a bit of a stir after he was spotted out in LA with his ex various times over the past few months.

With a fan asking him why they were together during an Instagram Q and A, Jake looked exasperated.

‘Exes can be friends, right?’ he asked. ‘Or just because “stans” don’t like that and get butt hurt we’re not supposed to be?’

Well, that’s that then.

Drama was stirred up again after a fan spotted the pair of them together in a car, snapping a photo.

The 22-year-old split from Erika back in November 2018, after just six months of officially dating.
