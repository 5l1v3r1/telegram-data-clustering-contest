Grilled cod with coco de paimpol beans recipe

20 Nov 2019, 10:35 by Angela Hartnett

This fish recipe makes a delicious dinner for four. Grilled cod is served with coco de paimpol beans and buttered, toasted breadcrumbs.

Prep time: 10 minutes | Cooking time: 45 minutes

SERVES

Four

INGREDIENTS

  • Olive oil, for frying and greasing
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 stick of celery, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 50g pancetta, chopped
  • 300g fresh coco beans
  • 4 x 150g cod fillets, skin on [I cut one filleted piece into 150g portions]
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 50g butter
  • 100g panko breadcrumbs
  • ½ tsp chopped thyme leaves
  • Zest of 1 lemon

METHOD

  1. Add a touch of olive oil to a pan and sweat the diced vegetables with the garlic for five minutes until they start to get soft but not coloured. Add the pancetta and cook for a further two minutes then remove from the heat and tie the contents of the pan into a muslin cloth. Leave to one side.
  2. Place the coco beans in a clean pan and cover with cold water. Add the muslin bundle and bring to a boil. Turn down to a simmer and cook until the beans are soft but not mushy, about 30 minutes.
  3. Grease a baking tray with oil and place the cod pieces skin-side down. Grill for five to eight minutes until cooked through. The best way to tell is to gently push the thin handle of a kitchen spoon through the flesh of one of the fillets; if the flesh shows no resistance, it is cooked.
  4. Open the muslin cloth and add everything to the beans in the pan. There should still be enough liquid to form a nice ragou. Keep warm and stir through the parsley.
  5. Melt the butter in a pan and stir through the breadcrumbs and thyme. Toast until golden brown. Stir in the lemon zest. 
  6. Dived the coco beans between warm dishes and place cod fillets on top. Finish with the toasted breadcrumbs.
