Borderlands 3's first DLC pulls off a casino heist at Handsome Jack's expense

This time they're hunting a different kind of vault

20 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Brett Makedonski

Poor Handsome Jack. First they took his life. Then they took his legacy. For their final trick, they're going to rob him of his riches. This is why you always get buried with all your valuables.

Gearbox has pulled back the curtain on Borderlands 3's first proper piece of DLC, and it kicks Handsome Jack while he's down (and dead). Moxxi's Heist of the Handsome Jackpot is an Ocean's Eleven-like caper where Moxxi enlists the Vault Hunters to infiltrate Handsome Jack's derelict space station casino with the intention of robbing it blind. Called "the gaudiest gambling spot in the galaxy," it's no wonder Moxxi's attracted to it.
