ROAD DRAMA

Two girls hit by car in Glasgow’s Gallowgate as emergency services race to scene

20 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Aidan Smith

TWO girls have been hit by a car in Glasgow.

Emergency services raced to the scene on Crown Point Road in the Gallowgate.

Police confirmed they received reports of two girls being by a car around 8:50am this morning.

They also confirmed that there doesn't appear to be any serious injuries.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "We were called to reports of a car hitting two girls on Crown Point Road Glasgow at around 8:50am.

"Emergency services are on the scene and there doesn't appear to be any serious injuries."
