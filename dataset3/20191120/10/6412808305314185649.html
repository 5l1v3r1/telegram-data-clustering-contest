Watch This Abang Pick Up Someone Else's Litter And Throw It Back At Them

The video was retweeted over 19,000 times in just two days.

20 Nov 2019, 10:56 by Yap Wan Xiang

Cover image via Twitter @My_CrimeWatch

A 25-second dashcam footage showing a man picking up trash thrown out of a car and sticking it on the litterbug's windscreen has gone viral

The footage, uploaded to Twitter on 18 November, has been viewed over 340,000 times and retweeted over 19,000 retweets.

The incident is believed to have taken place on Jalan Sultan Yahya Petra, Kuala Lumpur - as the Perkeso Headquarters can be seen in the background - at 5.41pm on Monday.

"Respect the way this man 'educates' this road user who doesn't have civic awareness," read the caption of the viral tweet.

The footage begins with what appears to be a child's hand lurking out of the back window and throwing an empty bag

The car was waiting at a red light at the time.﻿

After about 10 seconds, the abang alights his car and walks towards the car with a long face.

He picks up the trash from the road and slips it between the car's windscreen and wiper.

The abang's action in the video prompted thousands of netizens to thank him for serving justice

"Our hero!!" one netizen exclaimed, while another added, "Respect this guy!"

One Twitter user said, "Salute you, sir! Whoever you are."

"Get a taste of your own medicine, Waja driver! Ugh!" a netizen chastised.

Another commented, "Good idea. (I have) been thinking of what to do to these kinds of uncivilised bunch."

"Don't have to knock on the window and give eye contact (to the driver). Just slip it at the wiper. End of story. Bravo!"

One netizen even edited the video by memeing it with a Grand Theft Auto reference.
