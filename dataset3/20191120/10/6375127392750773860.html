Man wanted for rape falls

20 Nov 2019, 10:12 by Jojo C. Magsombol

BALAYAN, Batangas — A 26-year-old man wanted for rape with homicide was arrested by joint operatives during the serving of a warrant of arrest in Bgy. Magabe here the other day.

Balayan Municipal Police station chief Major Marlon C. Cabatana identified the suspect as Honorario  Seclegan Jr., 26, married, construction worker and a resident of this village.

Cabatana said, Seclegan wanted and listed as no.1 in Lambat-Sibat was nabbed around 12:10 p.m. by joint elements of Balayan MPS, RMFB and 2nd BPMFC and the Batangas Provincial Intelligence Branch (PIB).

Meanwhile, the Lipa City police along with Batangas Provincial Intelligence Branch PIB arrested Maricel Alim, Hasad Salamat, and Maria Fe Guiamelon during a drug buy-bust operation in Bgy Tambo this city at 4:20 a.m. on Tuesday.

The operating team confiscated three heat-sealed transparent plastic sachets of suspected shabu with an approximate weight of 60 grams including bogus buy-bust money amounting to P70,000.
