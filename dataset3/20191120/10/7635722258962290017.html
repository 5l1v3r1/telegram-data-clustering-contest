Spot flooding expected as status orange rainfall warning issued for two counties

20 Nov 2019, 10:10 by Stephen McNeice

A status orange rainfall warning has been issued for Tipperary and Waterford.

Forecasters say heavy rain over today and tomorrow will cause spot flooding, with accumulations of over 50mm possible in some parts of the two counties.

The warning is valid from 3pm today until 6pm tomorrow.
