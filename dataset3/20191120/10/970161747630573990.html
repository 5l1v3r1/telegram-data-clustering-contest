Were Scarlett Moffatt and Ant McPartlin ever in a relationship?

20 Nov 2019, 10:48

I’m A Celebrity 2016 winner Scarlett Moffatt has been axed as a co-host of Ant & Dec’s Saturday Night Takeaway after three years.

Scarlett had previously referred to the role as ‘an absolute dream’.

This move has led to fresh interest in old rumours concerning Scarlett Moffatt and her now-ex-co-host Ant McPartlin…

Were Scarlett Moffatt and Ant McPartlin ever an item?

Despite speculation among fans online, both Scarlett and Ant – who met on I’m A Celebrity – have firmly denied that there was ever anything romantic between them.

Rumours took flight in 2018 after Scarlett posted a selfie with a framed picture in the background of her, Ant and Dec filming an episode of Saturday Night Takeaway in which she was dressed as Mary Poppins.

She has previously been quoted in the Express as saying it was ‘A proper pinch me moment!’

Last year, Scarlett told Digital Spy on the subject of the relationship rumours: ‘Well it wasn’t true so, honestly, I always think, “Those who mind don’t matter and those who matter don’t mind”.

‘My family and friends knew, so no, it didn’t really enter my head. I do love a conspiracy theory myself, but I’m more of an alien, mermaid sort of conspiracy girl.

‘It’s a picture of me as Mary Poppins. It even beats me in the jungle, like the highlight of my working life. To do the Saturday Night Takeaway finale in Disney World, honestly, was my dream. So every morning when I wake up and see that photo, I’m like, “I’m living my dream”.’
