Alicia Keys and Bebe Rexha to announce Grammy Award nominations Wednesday

20 Nov 2019, 04:38 by Brandy McDonnell

Alicia Keys and Bebe Rexha will join "CBS This Morning" co-host Gayle King Wednesday to reveal the nominees for the top categories at the 2019-2020 Grammy Awards.

The Recording Academy will begin the nominees announcement at about 7:20 a.m., when Recording Academy President/CEO Deborah Dugan and Chair of the Board of Trustees Harvey Mason Jr. reveal nominees in select categories at Studio 43 at the CBS Broadcast Center in New York City.
