Here Are the 2020 Grammy Award Nominations

20 Nov 2019, 04:02 by Tuathla Lucey

And the Grammy goes to...

The nominations are here for the 2020 Grammy Awards.

Fans will have to wait until January 26th, 2020 to find out who will win big on the music industry’s big night.

The 62nd Annual Grammy Awards will take place at the Staples Center in Los Angeles and air on CBS, with musician Alicia Keys as host.
