Celcom offers the Huawei Mate 30 from as low as RM15/month

20 Nov 2019, 04:28 by Alexander Wong

The Huawei Mate 30 series has arrived in Malaysia and you can get one from Celcom as little as RM15/month through its EasyPhone program. In addition, the device also comes with additional freebies plus 1-year extended warranty and 1-month screen crack protection.

Huawei Mate 30

The Huawei Mate 30 with 8GB RAM + 128GB storage is officially priced at RM2,799 but you can rent it from Celcom from as low as RM15/month or buy it on instalment from RM28/month. Alternatively, it is also available on a normal contract from RM299 on the highest Platinum Plus plan but do note that you’ll have to pay an upfront payment which will be rebated back to your account.

For EasyPhone, you’ll need to enable auto-billing with a credit card and it is only applicable for cards issued by Maybank, Citibank and CIMB bank. All options are tied to a 24-month contract period.

Huawei Mate 30 Pro

For the top-of-the-line Mate 30 Pro with 8GB RAM + 256GB storage, the device is officially retailing for RM3,899. On Celcom’s EasyPhone Rent, you can get it from RM120/month or buy it on EasyPhone Buy instalment from RM139/month. The device is available on a normal contract from RM2,408.
