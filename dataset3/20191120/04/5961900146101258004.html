Are you a dynamic and young filmmaker who wants to join Australia’s biggest youth publisher? Then keep reading! Pedestrian Group are seeking a full time Junior Videographer/Editor (SYD) to assist their video team in driving amazing results for commercial clients. The ideal candidate has at least 3 years of hands-on experience in shooting, editing , sound and softwares such as Premiere Pro and Adobe Suite. The role consists of completing 4-6 videos a week, setting up camera and lights, completing storyboards and working with key stakeholders such as clients and managers. If you are an organised and process driven filmmaker looking for an exciting career opportunity, then apply now!

Pod and Parcel, a forward-thinking rapidly growing coffee start-up, are looking for a full time Social Media Manager (MELB) to produce social media content and work on content strategy and planning. Pod and Parcel ‘s purpose is to combine beautiful, specialty products with biodegradable, compostable packaging to minimise waste and create an unmatched customer experience. The successful applicant has excellent writing and communication skills, a Bachelor’s degree in marketing, media or communications and advanced knowledge of social media platforms. Apply here coffee lovers!

Food and Desire, a Melbourne based hospitality group, are on the hunt for part time Front of House Food & Beverage Attendants (MELB) to support the business through their busy season and beyond. Food and Desire’s clients include The Grand Prix, Spring Racing Carnival, Tiffany & Co, Prada, Hermes and many more! Candidates will need to be able to carry 3 plates, have a current RSA, have great personal style and outstanding communication skills. Perks of the role include free lunch meals, flexible working hours, competitive rates and training and development. Apply today!
