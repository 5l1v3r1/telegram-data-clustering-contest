Disha Patani shows off her parkour moves, gives major fitness goals

If you are looking to explore challenging workouts, Disha Patani's parkour sessions are impression-worthy.

20 Nov 2019, 03:57 by Lifestyle Desk

Disha Patani, who is known for her stellar workout videos, recently shared on Instagram a clip of her trying the “kong vault”. The movement that involves jumping over an obstacle is part of parkour, a training exercise developed by the military. Parkour practitioners aim to get from one point to another in the fastest and most efficient manner without using any equipment.

The Malang Actor captioned the video, “After ages trying my hand on kong vault, back to training with @Nadeemakhtarparkour88.”

Parkour was originally developed by the military as a tactical skill and a way to build the fitness of soldiers. While parkour involves running, freerunning, climbing, swinging, vaulting, jumping, plyometrics, rolling, quadrupedal movement (crawling) and other movements, kong vault is a type of parkour vault which is useful for jumping over long objects, such as large barriers.

It is said that the discipline was first developed in France in the late 1980s, but was popularised through Internet videos, television commercials, and feature films, including the 2006-James Bond film Casino Royale.
