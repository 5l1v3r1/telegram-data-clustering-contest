TABLE-Japan's usage of corn in animal feed rises in Sept

20 Nov 2019, 03:25

TOKYO, Nov 20 (Reuters) - Japan's usage of corn in animal feed rose to 48.8% in September, compared with 48% in the same month of 2018, preliminary data from the Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries showed on Wednesday. Below is a table based on preliminary data from the farm ministry for Japan's compound feed shipments in tonnes and each grain's percentage share in feed production: Sept-19 Aug-19 Sept-18 Total Shipments 1,863,965 1,904,516 1,852,276 Main Ingredients Corn 48.8% 48.8% 48% Sorghum 1.8% 1.8% 2.4% Wheat 1.6% 1.6% 1.8% Barley 3.4% 3.5% 3.4% Note: Data subject to revision.
