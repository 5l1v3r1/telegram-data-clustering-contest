Another fog advisory issued

Dense fog and patchy freezing drizzle are expected overnight

20 Nov 2019, 03:57 by Erin Anderson

For the second time in 24 hours, Environment Canada has issued a fog advisory for Waterloo Region and our surrounding area. 

The national weather agency says dense fog is expected overnight into Wednesday morning.

There's also a possibility we could see patchy freezing drizzle, so roadways and walkways may become hazardous.

Visibility could also be greatly reduced at times, so motorists need to use caution.
