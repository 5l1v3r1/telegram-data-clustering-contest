Tiwa Savage, Teniola, Pasuma Wonder Awilo, 9ice, others to thrill at AFRIMA

20 Nov 2019, 03:42 by Ransome Mgbeahuru and Godwin Okondo

The best of African beats will be on show at the AFRIMA Music Village themed, Wild & Wow tomorrow. Holding at the Agege Stadium, Agege, Lagos, Nigeria from 6:00pm, the show is part of events lined-up for the sixth All Africa Music Awards (AFRIMA).

Renowned music artistes who will perform at te event include, Tiwa Savage, the ‘49-99’ crooner, who recently bagged a recording deal with Universal Records Group, United States, Ghana’s dancehall artiste, Stonebwoy; South Africa’s Nasty C, Sho Madjozi and Master K.

For a first time AFRIMA appearance, the international Congolese music veteran, Awilo Longomba will bring his extraordinaire energy and sensual dance moves to play. Also performing are Sauti Sol from Kenya, Rayvanny from Tanzania, Daphne from Cameroun, Anna Jone from Angola, Toofan from Togo, Aminux from Morocco, and Kanvee Adams from Liberia.

In addition, Nigerian artistes who will be entertaining their fans are Tubaba, Teni the Entertainer, Ycee, Pasuma Wonder, Rudeboy, Saheed Osupa, Small Doctor, Skiibi, Niniola, Falz, DJ Spinall, and Mr. P. Others are Dj Cuppy, 9ice, Fireboy, Atawewe, Harrysong, Q.dot, Obesere, Slimcase, Mr. Reel, Mayorkun, Reminisce and DJ Neptune.
