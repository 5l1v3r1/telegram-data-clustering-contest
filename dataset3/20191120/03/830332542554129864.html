Marquette County deputies ask public for help in finding suspected burglar caught on camera

20 Nov 2019, 03:36 by Brandon Arbuckle

OXFORD, Wis. - The Marquette County Sheriff's Office has asked the public for help in finding a suspected burglar who was caught on camera Monday morning.

According to a post from the Sheriff's Office Facebook page, the burglar broke into a business in the village of Oxford and stole a number of items. Officials said the burglar was also seen with burglary tools.

Anyone with information on the crime or who can identify the person is encouraged to call the Marquette County Sheriff's Office at 608-297-2115.
