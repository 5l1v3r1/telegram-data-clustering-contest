Aoife Walsh too busy for Dancing With The Stars as she plans wedding

20 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Sandra Mallon

Former Miss Ireland Aoife Walsh has said she’s too busy planning her wedding to do Dancing With The Stars.

Aoife was due to be a contestant on the RTE One show in January last year but was forced to pull out after she fractured her toe during rehearsals.

But Aoife said she can’t have another crack at the show this January, as she’s spending most of her time planning her wedding to her long-term boyfriend, Gary McSharry.

She said: “Well, it wouldn’t suit me this year now. I’m getting married in a couple of months so I’m really busy with that.

“Planning a wedding is really like a full-time job.

“My wedding is in Ireland so I have an awful lot happening over the next few months so I’m focusing on that at the moment.”

The 30-year-old was replaced by model Alannah Beirne, who was booted off the show after nine weeks.

But Aoife said she wasn’t jealous when Alannah took her place on the show.

“I was absolutely thrilled to be asked to go on it and I was in it at the start and part of the show.

“I think obviously from when I broke my toe, it was so unfortunate because I never broke a bone ever in my life.

“I couldn’t believe the unfortunate timing. When the show went on, there was a part of me that was really sad — but I was really happy for Alannah at the time for her to go and reach her full potential.

“I just knew if I went on, I wouldn’t reach my full potential and I wouldn’t have done myself any service.

“I made such good friends with all the pro dancers that season so I was able to still cheer on everyone,” she added.

Aoife was speaking yesterday as she helped launch Three’s Connected Restaurant.

The pop-up restaurant, which opens from December, will connect family and friends in Dublin and New York.
