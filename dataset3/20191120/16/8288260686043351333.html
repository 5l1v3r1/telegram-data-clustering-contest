Aston Villa flop Micah Richards says this about Liverpool star - and it's bold

Latest Aston Villa news includes Richards' verdict on Liverpool midfielder and Harry Kane comparison

20 Nov 2019, 16:14 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Former Aston Villa defender Micah Richards has hailed Jordan Henderson as England’s only world-class midfielder.

Richards, whose career at Villa flopped before coming to end earlier this year, has been giving his verdict on the Premier League as part of a recent push into punditry.

Henderson captained Liverpool to Champions League glory in May but it is often claimed he is not afforded the praise he deserves outside of Anfield circles.

But there can be no doubt the 29-year-old has a big fan in Richards, who reckons he is the only Three Lions international on the same level as Tottenham striker Harry Kane.

 

Speaking to BBC Radio 5Live, Richards said: “Harry Kane is world-class. In midfield, there’s no other world, world-class midfielders.

“Henderson, you’d argue Henderson’s world-class, because of what he’s done and consistency and whatnot.

“He’s won a European Cup, he’s been consistent over the last three or four years. Yeah, it really is [hard to write off a Champions League-winning captain].

“You know what, I’m going to go with that statement, I’m going to say Henderson is world-class and a lot of the work that he does goes unnoticed because he’s not getting 10 assists a season or scoring 10 goals a season.
