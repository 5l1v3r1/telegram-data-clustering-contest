Salman Khan reacts to the trailer of Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior

20 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Filmfare

Yesterday, Ajay Devgn and Saif Ali Khan released the much awaited trailer of Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior. And minutes within the trailer going online, it was trending all over social media. Fans were e extremely excited as it will also be the first time they’ll be seeing Saif and Ajay share the screen after their 2006 release Omkara.

The trailer received a big thumbs up from netizens as they seemed to be intrigued by the grandeur and the characters that feature in the period drama. Earlier today, Ajay’s Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam co-star Salman Khan took to social media and and showered praise on the film along with congratulating Ajay for the same. The actor shared a tweet that read, “Tanhaji manaji.. Ajay vijay bhava vijay bhava vijay bhava . . .@ajaydevgn.”

Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior is also Ajay’s 100th Bollywood film which makes it all the more special for the actor and his fans.

Talking about the film, Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior is directed by Om Raut and the film will release on January 10, 2020. Apart from Ajay and Saif, the film also features Kajol in a leading role. The actress will play Ajay’s wife Savitribai Malusare.
