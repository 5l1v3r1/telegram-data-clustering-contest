Borderlands 3’s first campaign DLC is Moxxi’s Heist of the Handsome Jackpot

20 Nov 2019, 16:57 by Stephany Nunneley

Moxxi’s Heist of the Handsome Jackpot is the first DLC campaign coming to Borderlands 3.

This is the first of four DLCs for Borderlands 3 and it drops on December 19.

It is included in both the Season Pass and the Borderlands 3 Super Deluxe Edition.

The DLC was announced in the latest Borderlands show, where the development team at Gearbox also discussed the patch coming to this week, which includes the endgame challenge of Takedown at the Maliwan Blacksite launching tomorrow, Mayhem Mode 4, and some quality-of-life improvements.

All bosses will have dedicated loot pools and expanded bank spots as promised in tomorrow’s update. A target dummy will be available, and new vending machines will be deployed across all maps.

Once the patch drops tomorrow, we’ll post the notes.
