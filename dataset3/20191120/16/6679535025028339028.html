HC quashes FIR against Divya Bharathi

20 Nov 2019, 16:06 by Staff Reporter

MADURAI

Bringing relief to documentary film-maker and lawyer Divya Bharathi, the Madurai Bench of the Madras High Court on Wednesday quashed the first information report registered by Othakadai police against her in the Kakkoos documentary issue.

Justice N. Anand Venkatesh allowed her petition that sought a direction to quash the FIR registered against her under the Indian Penal Code and the Information Technology Act. The complaint was filed against her by advocate S. Baskar Maduram.

The complainant, who is associated with Puthiya Tamilagam, objected to the documentary. Ms. Divya Bharathihad shown the plight of manual scavengers in the documentary film. She had interviewed manual scavengers and documented their sufferings.
