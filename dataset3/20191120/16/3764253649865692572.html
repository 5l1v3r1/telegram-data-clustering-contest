Attention and Owo Mi Da: 3 reasons you would love Tiwa Savage's new songs

20 Nov 2019, 16:53

Talented Africa music queen Tiwatope Savage has released two songs, Attention and Owo Mi Da. The beautiful mother of one announced the release of the songs this Wednesday, November 20.

The two brand new jams will quench your thirst for bangers and you would be forced to continue to play them on repeat.

Owo Mi Da, which means where is my money, was produced by Pheelzmrproducer, and written by Tiwa Savage and Olamide. Attention is a jam talking about getting quality attention for a romantic partner.

After listening to the songs, Legit.ng has compiled a list of things that would make any listener fall in love with the songs.
