House of Fraser is selling personalised Cadbury selection boxes for Christmas

A new secret Santa workshop at House of Fraser's Oxford Street store lets chocolate lovers personalise a Cadbury selection box - just in time for Christmas

20 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Melisha Kaur, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/melisha-kaur/

If you need a Christmas gift for a sweet-toothed loved one, look no further than House of Fraser .

Just in time for the festive season, the department store has teamed up with chocolate giant Cadbury to launch a new secret Santa workshop this week.

The Cadbury workshop will be located on the fourth floor of House of Fraser's Oxford Street branch and is open from Thursday, November 21st until Christmas eve.

It gives shoppers the chance to personalise a selection box with a name of their chouse on the front. Even better, they'll also get to choose the seven chocolate treats that go inside.

 

Sadly, no other House of Fraser stores will be running the workshop - but you can find a huge selection of chocolate gifts at most branches and online at houseoffraser.com .

Entry to the festive workshop is free, but it costs £10 to personalise a selection box.

Shoppers can find a selection of Cadbury's classic favourites like the Dairy Milk Fruit & Nut, along with new festive offerings including the Dairy Milk Orange Snowman.

You can check the opening times of House of Fraser Oxford Street online .

Meanwhile, Morrisons has cut the price of various Cadbury items - including a Chocolate Selection Stocking (now £2, was £3) and a Drinking Chocolate Hot Chocolate Tub (now £2, was £2).
