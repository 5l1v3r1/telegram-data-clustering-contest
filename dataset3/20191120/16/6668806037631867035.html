Director Madhur Bhandarkar gives an update on Lata Mangeshkar’s health

20 Nov 2019, 16:25 by Filmfare

Lata Mangeshkar is a name that needs no introduction to Bollywood fans. Often referred to as the nightingale of India, Lata Mangeshkar has won over the hearts of millions with the help of her melodies and soulful voice. Naturally, when news that she’s been admitted to a hospital broke out, fans were pretty upset. However, recently the family of the veteran singer confirmed that she’s on her road to recovery.

Filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar also visited veteran singer Lata Mangeshkar at Mumbai's Breach Candy Hospital, and he took to social media to confirm that the melody queen is now “stable". He tweeted, “Visited the Hospital to see @lata_mangeshkar didi glad to inform that she is stable and responding positively to the treatment. Thanks everyone for countless blessings & prayers for her speedy recovery #latamangeshkar”
