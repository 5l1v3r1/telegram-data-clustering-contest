New documentary about how dogs can improve lives to air next week

20 Nov 2019, 16:23 by Nick Fitzgerald

The two-part series will air on TG4, showing how the lives of men, women and children who experience health challenges can vastly improve with the presence of working dogs

A bilingual two-part documentary series showing how great doggos are is set to begin next week on TG4.

Saol an Mhadra Bháin delves into how the lives of people experiencing health challenges are vastly improved by the presence of working dogs.

The heart-warming series will explore the special bond between humans living with visual impairments, autism and epilepsy, as examples, and the dogs who have transformed their lives for the better.
