Senior executive named for new Creighton Medical School in Phoenix

20 Nov 2019, 16:16 by Greg Barr, Editor in Chief, Phoenix Business Journal

PHOENIX — Catherine Todero has been named vice provost of Health Sciences Campuses for Creighton University, overseeing health science academic programs and facilities at its new Phoenix medical school campus in midtown now under construction on Central Avenue, as well as at its existing main campus in Omaha, Nebraska.

Her appointment was effective Nov. 15. Todero, who will be based in Omaha, will retain her other job as dean of the College of Nursing. She has held that title since 2015.

“We look forward to her leadership in continuing to advance Creighton University’s excellence in health sciences education both here in Omaha and in Phoenix,” said The Rev. Daniel Hendrickson, Creighton University president, in a statement.
