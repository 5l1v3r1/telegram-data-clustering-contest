MPPSC State Service applications begin: Check how to apply, eligibility, exam pattern

MPPSC State Service 2019:  A total of 330 vacancies are advertised under the same. The applications are open at mppsc.nic.in and the last date to apply is December 9.

20 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Careers Desk

MPPSC State Service application form 2019: The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC)  has released the online application form for the State Services recruitment exam 2019.  A total of 330 vacancies are advertised under the same. The application process has begun at the official website mppsc.nic.in and will remain open till December 9, 2019.

The recruitment will be based on a written test followed by a fitness eligibility test, interview and document verification. The written exam will further be in two parts – part one will test general knowledge and part two will be based on profile/interest. As per the official notification, it will be held on January 12, 2020.

MPPSC State Service 2019: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website, mppsc.nic.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘apply online’
Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page, click on ‘state services exam’ link
Step 4: Click on new registration, fill details and verify
Step 5: Log-in using registered id
Step 6: Fill form, upload documents
Step 7: Make payment and submit

MPPSC State Service 2019: Fee

Candidates will have to pay a fee of Rs 50 as application fee.

MPPSC State Service 2019: Salary

Of the total, 159 jobs are in the third category while the rest are in the second. For grade II jobs, the salary would be in the range of Rs 15,800 to Rs 39,100 with grade pay of Rs 5400. For the grade III level jobs, the salary would be in the range of Rs 9,300 to Rs 34,800 with grade pay of Rs 3600.
