Crying baby boy died after 'drunk' dad 'dropped him while trying to comfort him'

The father has reportedly confessed to his son's tragic death and been taken into custody after the baby was found with numerous bruises in Vovchyk, central Poltava, Ukraine

20 Nov 2019, 16:24 by Roksana Panashchuk

A baby boy died from skull fractures after his dad allegedly dropped him on the floor repeatedly while trying to comfort him.

The 21-year-old father, named as Artem, was believed to be drunk when he came home and heard his six-month-old son crying.

Taking little Nazar from his cot, he allegedly dropped him several times as he tried to comfort him.

The child was found with numerous bruises on his face following the alleged incident in Vovchyk, central Poltava, Ukraine.

Artem confessed to causing his son's death, was arrested and taken into custody, according to reports.

The alleged incident happened on November 6 when Artem came home after attending a birthday party.

His wife Tetiana, 20, went outside to park his car in the courtyard near their apartment block.

She came back 10 minutes later and entered the bedroom to check on Nazar who she earlier left sleeping in his cot.

But the mum reportedly saw her child lying motionless on the floor as her husband was snoring on the bed.

Terrified, she rushed to Nazar who showed no signs of life, reports said.

Tetiana told local media: ''I took him in my arms and realised that he was not breathing. I started panicking. My hands were shaking.''

The mum ran to Mykola Nadakhovsky, a paramedic living next door who immediately rushed to the scene.

Mr Nadakhovsky said: ''When I examined the boy, he was already dead.

''He had no pulse, no reflexes. I tried to bring him back to life but it was too late.

''I saw bruises on the child's face, fresh blood in his nose and called the police.''

Officers arrived on the scene and interviewed Artem. The man allegedly said "he repeatedly dropped the crying baby on the floor while trying to comfort him then went to sleep".

Police have reportedly launched a criminal case for murder against Artem.

He was arrested and taken into custody for a two-month pretrial period.

Police spokeswoman Ludmyla Ivashuk said: ''The father came home drunk and heard the baby's crying. He took the child from the crib and started comforting him.

''In the process, the man dropped the boy to the floor several times.

''According to forensic examinations, the baby died from a head trauma aggravated with multiple skull fractures.''

The suspect father faces up to 15 years in prison if found guilty.
