Borderlands 3's first campaign DLC is Moxxi's Heist of the Handsome Jackpot

What is dead may never die in Handsom Jack's case.

20 Nov 2019, 16:31 by Jennifer Locke

Source: Gearbox

What you need to know

  • Borderlands 3's first campaign DLC has us raiding the Handsome Jackpot for Moxxi.
  • It is set to release on December 19, 2019.
  • It is available for Season Pass holders or those who purchased the Super Deluxe Edition of Borderlands 3.

Because Gearbox just can't let go of Handsome Jack, Borderlands 3's first major campaign DLC focuses on the former Hyperion CEO... sort of. He's still rotting in the ground after his death, but Mad Moxxi has a job for us Vault Hunters: to raid his lavish casino and loot the riches within. Sounds fun.
