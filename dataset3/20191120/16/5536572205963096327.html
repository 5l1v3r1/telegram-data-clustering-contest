UGA Photographer Shares Picture of RB Brian Herrien Right Before She Got KO’D On Sidelines (PIC)

20 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Darrelle Lincoln

No matter what, you must always get the shot. That is the creed of the photojournalist.

Chamberlain Smith definitely lived by that motto.

On Tuesday, the UGA photographer who got knocked unconscious on the Auburn sidelines over the weekend, took to Twitter and tweeted out the last picture she took right before she got hit.

It was an extreme closeup of Georgia running back Brian Herrien.
