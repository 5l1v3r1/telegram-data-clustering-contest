Oliver Fights His Fate in New Arrow Episode 8.06 Promo

20 Nov 2019, 16:15 by Tudor Leonte

The CW has released a new promo for the upcoming Episode 6 of the eighth and final season of Arrow. Next week, Oliver will find himself trapped in a sort of alternate reality created by The Monitor. Laurel Lance and John Diggle will try to help him escape from this nightmare.  The synopsis of the next episode of Arrow reads as follows:

After being double-crossed by Lyla (guest star Audrey Marie Anderson), Oliver (Stephen Amell) finds himself facing a life or death situation that seems very familiar. Laurel (Katie Cassidy) has the opportunity to make amends with the past.

David Ramsey directed the episode written by Onalee Hunter Hughes and Maya Houston. You can watch the new Arrow Episode 8.06 promo in the player below. Then check out the new set of promotional photos from the episode.

Arrow stars Stephen Amell as Oliver Queen, as well as David Ramsey as John Diggle, and Katie Cassidy as Laurel/Black Siren. Rick Gonzalez plays Rene Ramirez/Wild Dog, with Juliana Harkavy as Dinah Drake/Black Canary. Charlie Barnett (Russian Doll) joined the cast as Diggle, Jr. — known as J.J. —  the Flashpoint-born son of John Diggle (David Ramsey) and Lyla Michaels (Audrey Marie Anderson). We’ll meet J.J. in the future as the leader of the Deathstroke Gang. There he faces off against Mia and his adopted brother, Connor.

Based on the DC characters, the series is executive produced by Greg Berlanti, Marc Guggenheim, as well as Wendy Mericle and Sarah Schechter. Additionally, Bonanza Productions Inc. produced the show in association with Berlanti Productions and Warner Bros. Television.

The next episode of Arrow Season 8 will air on The CW on November 26.
