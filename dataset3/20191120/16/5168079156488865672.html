New loyalty programme launched for Ahli Bank cardholders

20 Nov 2019, 16:36 by Times News Service

Muscat: Ahli Bank, the fastest growing bank in the Sultanate has introduced a new dynamic loyalty programme called ‘AhliRewards’ for its cardholders as part of their continuous efforts to offer the best in banking services and create more value for their customers.

The AhliRewards programme will allow the cardholders to avail discounts across various categories and enjoy a wide range of offers and discounts.

AhliRewards is available for all Ahli Bank debit and credit cardholders. Customers can now enjoy and benefit from the unlimited discounts, which include ‘Buy One Get One Free’ Offers or up to 50% discount across segments such as dining, entertainment, wellness and lifestyle. These offers and privileges are not just valid in Oman, but also in other countries that include UAE, Bahrain, etc..

Commenting on the launch of AhliRewards, Hanaa Al Hinai, Deputy General Manager, Retail Banking, Ahli Bank said, “We continuously work towards ensuring that our customers derive the maximum value with the Bank. The new AhliRewards Programme has been designed to provide exclusive privileges to our customers. The array of discounts and offers across segments are aimed to enhance the lifestyle experience of our customers and ensure they enjoy their banking experience with the bank. We have seamlessly integrated our systems, digital assets and encourage our customers to adapt to the new methods of banking.”
