Supergirl: Rama Khan Conspires with Leviathan in Midseason Finale Photos

20 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Sam Stone

Supergirl's latest foes have teamed up in new official stills from Season 5's midseason finale, spelling trouble for Kara and her friends.

The photos from "The Wrath of Rama-Khan" show Andrea Rojas -- recently unveiled as the villain Arcata, working in service to Leviathan -- speaking with Rama-Khan, who was revealed to be an immortal figure with powers rivaling Supergirl's. The photos and official episode synopsis from The CW are below:

The Wrath of Rama Khan (Ep# 508)MID-SEASON FINALE -- Supergirl's (Melissa Benoist) struggle against Leviathan reaches a boiling point as she faces off against Rama Khan (guest star Mitch Pileggi). Meanwhile, as Lena (Katie McGrath) and Hope (Andrea Brooks) work to launch Project Non Nocere, Hope proves to be an invaluable asset to Lena. Marcus Stokes directed the episode written by Lindsay Struman & Jessica Kardos (#508). Original airdate 12/1/2019.

Arcata was previously revealed to be drafted into working for Leviathan in exchange for her powers and her family business' continued success. Meanwhile, Lena Luthor has steadily plotted her revenge against Kara after feeling betrayed, determined to create a powerful device to ensure no one takes advantage of her trust again.

Airing Sundays at 9 p.m. ET/PT on The CW, Supergirl stars Melissa Benoist as Kara Danvers, David Harewood as Martian Manhunter, Mehcad Brooks as Jimmy Olsen and Chyler Leigh as Alex Danvers.
