Woman critically injured as dog killed in Bloxwich house fire

A woman was critically injured and a dog was killed in a house fire in the Black Country.

20 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Jordan Reynolds

The woman, who is in her 60s, was injured in a fire at a semi-detached house in Penderel Street, Bloxwich, at about 10.45am today and taken to Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Birmingham.

The fire was confined to one downstairs room and was out when firefighters arrived. Burnt furniture including blackened sofas were left outside the house.
