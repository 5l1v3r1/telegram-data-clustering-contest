Kara Faces Rama Khan in New Supergirl Episode 5.08 Photos

20 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Tudor Leonte

The CW has released a new batch of photos for the upcoming Supergirl midseason finale. Two weeks from now Supergirl will join forces with the Martian Manhunter to face Lena Luthor and Acrata. Additionally, Kara will have also to deal with the high-ranking Leviathan member Rama Khan. The episode will air on December 1.

The official synopsis of “The Wrath of Rama Khan” reads as follows:

Supergirl’s (Melissa Benoist) struggle against Leviathan reaches a boiling point as she faces off against Rama Khan (guest star Mitch Pileggi). Meanwhile, as Lena (Katie McGrath) and Hope (Andrea Brooks) work to launch Project Non Nocere, Hope proves to be an invaluable asset to Lena.

Marcus Stokes directed the episode, written by Lindsay Struman & Jessica Kardos.

Supergirl stars Melissa Benoist (Glee) as Kara Danvers/Supergirl. Mehcad Brooks plays James Olsen, with Chyler Leigh (Grey’s Anatomy) as Alex Danvers, as well as Jeremy Jordan as Winslow ‘Winn’ Schott, and Katie McGrath (Merlin) as Lena Luthor. The show also stars Jess Rath as Brainiac 5, and David Harewood as Hank Henshaw/J’onn J’onnz.

Based on the DC characters created by Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster, the series boasts executive producers Greg Berlanti, Sarah Schechter, as well as Robert Rovner, and Jessica Queller. Moreover, Berlanti Productions, in association with Warner Bros. Television, produces Supergirl.
