James Morrison and Will Young to co-headline Cannock Chase Forest Live summer gig - get tickets here

Make a date with love song swooners James Morrison and Will Young at the famous outdoor summer concert from Forestry England in June 2020.

20 Nov 2019, 16:54 by Alison Brinkworth

Brit Award winners James Morrison and Will Young are to co-headline the next annual Forest Live summer concert at Cannock Chase.

They will perform at the picturesque outdoor gig on Sunday June 21, 2020, that attracts thousands of music lovers.

Both famous for their soulful, romantic songs, they will perform their own individual sets featuring greatest hits and new material.

James Morrison said: “For the last 20 years, Will has been at the forefront of British Popular Culture. That’s a massive achievement.

"I think our sets will complement each other in a special way and I’m really looking forward to our shows together. It’ll be a great night out!”

Will Young added: “Both James and I have fond memories of appearing as part of Forest Live as solo artists in the past. We love the work Forestry England does, so we are glad to be back again in what promises to be a fantastic double-header of a show.”

Singer-songwriter James Morrison is famous for his husky voice and heartfelt hits including Wonderful World, You Give Me Something and Undiscovered. He has returned to the limelight with recent album You're Stronger Than You Know.

While former Strictly Come Dancing contestant Will Young has had four number 1 hits including Leave Right Now to get the crowd excited, along with tracks from his latest album, Lexicon.

Forestry England is behind the annual summer concert series that includes gigs at Cannock Chase. Previous headliners have included Paul Weller, Elbow, Jack Savoretti and Paloma Faith.
