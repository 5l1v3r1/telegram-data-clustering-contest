ESPN Analyst Releases Score Prediction For Oklahoma-TCU

20 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Matt Hladik

With its stirring come-from-behind win over Baylor last weekend, Oklahoma kept its Big 12 title hopes alive. The Sooners’ next test will be against TCU.

Oklahoma hosts the Horned Frogs in the final home game of the regular season this Saturday night. It is the second of three straight primetime contests for OU.

ESPN’s Bill Connelly released his score predictions for Week 13, using his well-regarded SP+ formula. One of the games featured is Oklahoma-TCU.

Connelly projects the Sooners will walk away with a 41-22 victory.
