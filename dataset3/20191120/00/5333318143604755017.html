Planet A needs a Menu B

20 Nov 2019 by Iain Robertson

Leading UN Global Goals advocate, Marc Buckley, is working on the new handbook, Menu B: planet-saving food solutions. The award-winning global food reformist, and member of the World Economic Forum Expert Network, is a renowned climate and innovation expert who speaks at cross sector events worldwide, sharing his insight on the most effective ways to advance the sustainability agenda. Marc is also Editor-at-large for Innovators Magazine. The 2020 handbook will be part of a new Menu B movement focussed on inspiring governments, businesses, citizens and investors to take actions that accelerate the transition to sustainable food systems. Marc, along with a number of his fellow global food reformists, will set out steps different groups can take to fast-track this goal.

“This impactful handbook will explore ways we can fix broken food systems to overcome the climate crisis and achieve zero hunger by 2030.”
