President Buhari Congratulates Former President Jonathan On His 62nd Birthday

20 Nov 2019 by Seyi Adebowale

President Muhammadu Buhari has felicitated with former President Goodluck Jonathan on his 62nd birthday, by joining the nation in praying for longer life, good health and more strength to keep serving the nation.

President Buhari said this in a press release by the Special Adviser to the President on Media and Publicity Femi Adesina noting that he rejoices with family, friends and political associates of the former president, lauding him for good counsels to leaders within and outside Nigeria since he left office, sharing his experience of serving at different levels of governance.
