Twitter Reactions Trail Mourinho’s Return To EPL With Tottenham

20 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

English Premier League side Tottenham Hotspur have confirmed the appointment of Jose Mourinho as their new manager.

Mourinho has signed a deal until the end of the 2022/23 season with Spurs and will be in charge for the Premier League clash with West Ham at the London Stadium on Saturday

Speaking on why he joined the London club, newly appointed Tottenham manager, Mourinho noted that the abundant talent in the first team, as well as the academy, convinced him to join the English Premier League side.
