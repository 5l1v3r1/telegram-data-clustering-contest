Mourinho Becomes The Second Highest-Paid Manager In The World

20 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Jose Mourinho is now the second highest-paid manager in the world after signing a massive £15m-a-year contract to become the new coach of Tottenham Hotspur. Mourinho's new job with Tottenham confirmed his 4th return as coach in England after managing Manchester United and Chelsea twice.

On Wednesday, November, 20, the Portuguese manager, 56, signed a four-year contract worth £15m-a-year and jumped straight into the list of the highest-paid managers in the World at No 2.

Jose Mourinho now comes behind Manchester City boss, Pep Guardiola, who takes home £20m-a-year.
