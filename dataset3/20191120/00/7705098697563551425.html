Rey Finds Darth Vader’s Mask in a New Rise of Skywalker TV Spot

20 Nov 2019, 00:29 by Jake Abbate

One month from tomorrow, Rey’s journey will finally come to an end in Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker. Previous footage has hinted that the character might be headed down a dark path in the final chapter of the Skywalker Saga. Now, a new TV spot for the film shows her staring darkness in the face.

The spot confirms there will be a scene in The Rise of Skywalker where Rey stumbles across the remains of Darth Vader’s helmet. You can watch it for yourself via Twitter below.
