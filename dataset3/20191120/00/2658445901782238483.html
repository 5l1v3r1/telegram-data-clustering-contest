College Alumni meets tomorrow - The Nation Newspaper

20 Nov 2019, 00:23

THE Imperial College Alumni community in Nigeria will tomorrow meet with the Senior International Relations Officer for Imperial College Alumni Clare Turner.

The meeting will hold between 7:30pm and 9:30pm at W Bar, LASMA Yard, Falomo, lkoyi, Lagos.

The Imperial College Alumni Association of Nigeria President, Olugbenga E. Adelana and Chairman Board of Trustees, Sir Edet J. Amana, stated this in a statement yesterday.

The association is a fully registered entity geared towards meeting the needs of the present and future alumnus of the prestigious Imperial College London.

According to the statement, the association’s goals include, fostering “a spirit of fellowship among the former students of the institution now known as Imperial College of Science, Technology & Medicine (‘the College’) of the University of London, United Kingdom.

“We invite all alumni to register their interest with the association on our website http://www.icaanigeria.org and register for tomorrow’s event on http://events.icaanigeria.org.”
