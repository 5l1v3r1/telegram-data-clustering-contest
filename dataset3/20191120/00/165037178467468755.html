Luis Enrique's return gets a date and time

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Next November 27 Luis Enrique will be presented as the new coach of Spain. The Asturian coach will return to the position he left less than a year ago for family reasons.

The Spanish National Team's twitter reported that next Wednesday November 27 Luis Enrique will be presented as the new coach.

The event, set in the form of a press conference at 11:30, will take place in the 'Ciudad del Futbol' in Las Rozas, and will serve as an act of presentation for the coach, who thus faces his second tint as "La Roja's" coach.Â 

Luis Enrique was put aside at the end of March 2019, prior to his resignation for family reasons shortly after. Robert Moreno, his second, was at the head of the Selection, first of interim form and then of definitive form, until this past Monday.
