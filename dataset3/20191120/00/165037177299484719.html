The best memes about Mourinho's appointment as Tottenham boss

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Tottenham announced José Mourinho as Tottenham boss after sacking Mauricio Pochettino and in a short space of time the internet was full of memes about 'The Special One's' return to the Premier League.

Twitter was once again full of memes. After the sacking of Mauricio Pochettino and the appointment of José Mourinho, the fans didn't take long in publishing tonnes of reactions and memes to have a laugh about his return to the Premier League.
