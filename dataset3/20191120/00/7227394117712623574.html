Realme teases truly wireless headphones ahead of December launch

20 Nov 2019 by Michail

In addition to bringing the Realme X2 Pro to India and announcing the new Realme 5s, Realme's New Delhi event brought another surprise. Towards the end of the big announcement, Indian CEO Madhav Sheth teased a pair of truly wireless earbuds which he said are coming next month.

Sheth explicitly mentioned that Realme is bringing “a few more exciting things” in December. We got a brief glimpse of the headphones, which appear to be in the AirPods mold with a charging case and in-ear design in the company's signature yellow color. We don’t have further details at the moment so we’ll just have to wait till next month to see what exactly Realme has in store.
