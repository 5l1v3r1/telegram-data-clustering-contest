FN Awards 2020 – entry deadline extended

20 Nov 2019 by Stephen Briers

You now have more time to enter the Fleet News Awards 2020. Due to demand from the industry, we are now offering you an extra week to get your entries to us.

The new deadline of Friday 29th November gives you more time to craft an award-winning entry.

The Fleet News Awards recognise and reward the hard work and achievements of the best fleet operators, suppliers and manufacturers operating in the UK.

As well as giving you and your team your time in the spotlight, previous winners have also told us how it helped them acquire new talent, raise their brand profile and improve client relationships.

To make the process as simple as possible for you, your entry can be completed online by answering a few short questions – details can be found here.

Winners will be announced on Wednesday 11th March at the Grosvenor House Hotel, Park Lane, London. Table bookings for the awards are now being taken, and details can be found here.
