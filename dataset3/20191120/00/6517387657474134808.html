2020 Grammys: The complete list of nominations

Female artists dominate the key categories for next year's Grammy Awards.

20 Nov 2019 by Rob Copsey

The nominations for the 2020 Grammys have been released, with female acts dominating many of the main categories.

Five of the eight albums nominated for Album Of The Year are women, including Lana Del Rey for her UK Number 1 album Norman F**king Rockwell! and Ariana's chart-topping Thank U, Next. 

Meanwhile, Lizzo and Billie Eilish are both shortlisted in the best new artist, best song, best record and best album categories.

Elsewhere, Scottish singer-songwriter Lewis Capaldi picks up a nomination for Song of The Year for Someone You Loved, and Lil Nas X has been recognised with multiple nominations for his country-rap smash Old Town Road, which spent a record-breaking 19 weeks at Number 1 in the US this year.

See the full list of the 2020 Grammy Award nominations below. The 62nd annual Grammy Award takes place on January 26, 2020.
