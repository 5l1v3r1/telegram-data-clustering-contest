Mourinho will earn almost double Pochettino

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

José Mourinho is the man of the day in the world of football. One of the most talked-about issues is the astronomical salary that Daniel Levy has given him at Tottenham, almost twice the salary that Mauricio Pochettino received.

From being unemployed to receiving an astronomical salary in a year. He is about the become the 2nd highest-paid manager in the world after Pep Guardiola. According to 'Daily Mail', this money doesn't just go to anyone.

A total of 15 million pounds a year. With the current exchange rate, this would be the equivalent to just more than 17.5 million euros. His predecessor in the Tottenham hot seat earned just less than 10 million, according to the same source.

Breaking down his wage, he earns 52,000 euros a day to manage spurs. A salary more than considerable and that surely helps him to digest the possible criticisms from Chelsea after breaking his word four years later. 

News of the Portuguese's appointment at Spurs has, therefore, cost the club quite a lot of money after sacking the Argentine and strongly backing Mourinho.
