Swarm 60 lives on at night, weekend closures end

20 Nov 2019

INLAND EMPIRE  (INT)  Motorists havent heard the last of Swarm 60.

Although the weekend closures of Freeway 60 between Riverside and Mira Loma are over, nighttime work involving specific lanes and on/off ramps continues through Friday. But, it applies only to the segment of SR-60 between the 60-91-215 interchange and Valley Way.

Lanes are open in each direction on SR-60 during the day.

Caltrans advises that the speed limit will be lowered to 55 miles per hour within the construction zone
