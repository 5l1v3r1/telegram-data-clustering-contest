Chelsea await for transfer ban verdict ahead of winter transfer market

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Chelsea's appeal hearing for a FIFA sanction banning them from signing new players until February 2020 took place on Wednesday. The hearing's outcome will not be known well into December, a few days before the winter market begins.

Chelsea will have to wait until the verdict on their appeal is decided, without knowing whether or not they will be able to make any signings in the winter transfer market. FIFA found the London club guilty of 150 offences in the contracts of 71 under-age players.
