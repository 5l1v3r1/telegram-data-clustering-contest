2023: Tinubu Fit To Be President – Guru Maharaj Ji

20 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The founder of One Love Family, Satguru Maharaj Ji, has said the National Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu, was fit to be president in 2023.

He made this known on Wednesday while speaking at a news conference held at his Maharaj Ji village

According to NAN, Maharaj Ji said anybody could become president as long as such individual has Nigeria interest at heart.

“Anybody can become the president of Nigeria as long as he or she places the interest of the country above personal interest,” he said.

“If there is anyone who is fit in the south, it is Sen. Bola Tinubu. So, I will offer him for president if the presidency would be zoned to South-West.

“This is because he has been able to play the role of a mobiliser and bridge-builder across the country. Also, Peter Obi is a fine choice from the South-East.”

The clergyman also called on the federal government to establish education bank to avail indigent Nigerians access to quality education.

“The bank can pay for an individual’s education so that the person pays back upon getting a job after graduation. This will be paid back through a stipulated percentage of salary over a period of time,” he said.

Maharaj Ji expressed dissatisfaction with the state of affairs of the country, urging Buhari to do more in salvaging the situation.
