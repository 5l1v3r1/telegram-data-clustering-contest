NDC Rebukes Bossman Asare For ‘Yes’ Vote Campaign

20 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The opposition National Democratic Congress (NDC) has chastised Dr Bossman Asare for his recent comment on the upcoming referendum in December.

The Deputy Commissioner of the Electoral Commission is campaigning for a 'YES' vote in the impending referendum.

However, the NDC feels it's unethical for Dr. Asare to wade into the Yes or No brouhaha since he's part of the EC.
