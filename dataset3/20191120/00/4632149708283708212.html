Fake News Buster: Not cool

20 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
A picture of a wall filled with air conditioner units is being shared on social media with the claim that it is of ‘Paribesh Bhawan’ in Kolkata, which is the office of the West Bengal Pollution Control Board.

SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
This is untrue. The photo was clicked in 2009 by a Reuters photographer, the caption to which says that the picture shows ‘Rows of air conditioners on the walls of a building in Singapore’s financial district.’
