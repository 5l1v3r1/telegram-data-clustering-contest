AIAIAI announces new HD edition of TMA-2 headphones

The HD update increases performance and personalization

20 Nov 2019 by Devyn White

Copenhagen-based audio brand, AIAIAI has released an HD version of its beloved TMA-2 headphones. Made for music creators and avid listeners alike, the high performance headphones have continued to evolve over the past 15 years with their exceptional modular design.

With a bio-diaphragm speaker unit and wireless headband with AptX HD, the headphones are able to provide 24-bit hi-res audio with minimal background noise, allowing for both better performance and convenience. Alcantara material, memory foam and NAC acoustic bio-cellulose speakers make the headphones stiffer, lighter and stronger, while cancelling out degrees of distortion.
