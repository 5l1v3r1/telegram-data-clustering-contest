Police abandon chase in North Canterbury

20 Nov 2019

A police pursuit was abandoned in North Canterbury on Wednesday morning.

Officers signalled a vehicle to pull over due to the manner of driving about 2.40am on Tram Rd, Ohoka, but the driver failed to stop.

A police spokesman said enquiries are ongoing.

It comes after two drivers were arrested after failing to stop for police in Christchurch this week.

A 37-year-old woman was arrested on Tuesday following a 20-minute police chase from Kaiapoi to Christchurch.

A 46-year-old man was also arrested after failing to stop for dangerous driving on Monday.
