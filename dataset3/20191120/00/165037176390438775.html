Madrid v PSG a sell out

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Santiago Bernabéu is predicted to be a sell-out for the Madrid v PSG game next week.

It is the first time that the Bernabéu will be a sell-out this season, according to 'Mundo Deportivo'. The visit of PSG causes a lot of expectations.

Keylor Navas, Di María, and of course, a fully fit Neymar all return. Neymar is somehow attached to a Madrid that has always been waiting to sign him.
