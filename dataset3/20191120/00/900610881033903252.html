Malaysia Airlines offers buy-one-get-one-half-price offer on business class fares from Malaysia

20 Nov 2019 by Michael Allen

Malaysia Airlines is running a special promotion for business class travellers from today to 1 December 2019 to enjoy increased savings on their future bookings. Travellers who purchase a business class ticket from Malaysia to any domestic destinations, Asean and China will be eligible for a 50% rebate on their next business class booking.

The special promotion is for immediate travel until 6 January 2020. Once business class tickets have been purchased, customers will receive a promo code which can be used on their next booking. The promo code will be active for bookings during the sales period from 19 November until 15 December 2019 for travel from 15 February until 30 September 2020. This offer is valid for outbound bookings from Malaysia to any domestic destinations, Asean and China.

“With the 50% rebate, you can now purchase your next business class seat at an irresistible price and enjoy our business class privileges which include our Golden Lounge access, dedicated check-in counters and 40kg checked-in baggage amongst others,” said Malaysia Airlines group chief marketing and customer experience officer Lau Yin May.

Earlier this month, Malaysia Airlines said it would boost its flights to Beijing to 10 times weekly.
