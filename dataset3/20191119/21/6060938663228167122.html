Grammys 2020 nominations leaked online a day before!

19 Nov 2019, 21:39 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The Grammy 2020 nominations were supposed to be out today on November 20. However, fans somehow managed to leak it online.

Reportedly, a day before the nominations were to be released, fans quickly noticed the change in Grammys artist page. To be precise, fans noticed how Ariana Grande's artist page had 6 nominations but now, the page says nominations: 11.

Until November 19, 2019, Ariana had only six nominations and now fans are guessing that she has received 5 more nominations in the 2020 Grammys.

Reportedly, various artist's pages have been changed and they are reflecting new numbers of nomination. Soon, the Recording Academy restored the changes which reflected new numbers.

A social media user tweeted about the total numbers for all the artists whose nomination number was altered on the Grammys site.
