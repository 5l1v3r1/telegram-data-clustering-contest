U2 Honour Australian Firefighters Battling Blazes Down Under: ‘We Salute You’

19 Nov 2019, 21:02 by Brent Furdyk

Bono, The Edge and the other members of U2 happened to run into some Australian firefighters at the Melbourne airport, and sent a big shoutout to them and the others who are risking their lives battling the wildfires that have devastated the country in recent weeks.

“An honour to run into a couple of @cfavic heroes at Melbourne airport today…” the members of the band wrote in a tweet issued on Tuesday, accompanying a photo of the Irish rockers posing in front of a plane surrounded by dozens of firefighters.

“To all the firefighters working so hard across Australia – we salute you,” the band added. “Bono, The Edge, Adam and Larry.”
