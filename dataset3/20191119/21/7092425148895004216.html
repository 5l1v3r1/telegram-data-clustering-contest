Watch: Shocking video of car crashing through Spar in Knocklyon

19 Nov 2019, 21:16 by Nick Fitzgerald

The incident occurred at approximately 2:25am this morning

A shocking video is circulating on social media, in which a car is seen crashing through the front door of a Spar in Orlagh Grove, Knocklyon.

The incident occurred at approximately 2:25am this morning, Tuesday, November 19.

The Gardai are treating the incident as a burglary.

A spokesperson for the Gardai confirmed:

“Gardaí responded to a burglary of a premises in Orlagh Grove, Knocklyon on November 19 at approximately 2.25am.

"Significant damage was caused to the premises due to a car colliding into the front of the premises. Investigation are ongoing.”
