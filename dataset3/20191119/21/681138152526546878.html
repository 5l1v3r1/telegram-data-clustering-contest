Lorry and Audi A4 crash on A14

19 Nov 2019, 21:25 by Adam Howlett

Emergency services have attended the scene of a crash between a lorry and a car on the A14 near Woolpit.

Police were called to the scene of the collision, on the eastbound carriageway, at around 6.05pm today, Tuesday, November 19.

A police spokesman said the crash had happened between junction 46 at Beyton and junction 47 at Woolpit and involved a red Audi A4 and a heavy goods vehicle.

He added there are no reports of any injuries as a result of the crash.

There was a brief closure of both lanes while the vehicles were removed from the road, however one lane was later reopened.

A fire crew from Mildenhall was also send to the scene but were soon stood down.

The road has since been cleared and traffic is flowing as normal.
