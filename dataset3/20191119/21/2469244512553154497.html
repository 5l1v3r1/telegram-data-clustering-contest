Exeter triple murder trial: WhatsApp Group launched for updates to your phone

Devon Live will endeavour to inform you as the trial progresses

19 Nov 2019, 21:38 by Alex Richards

Devon Live has launched a WhatsApp group for breaking news updates to your phone about an ongoing murder trial in Exeter.

The deaths of three pensioners rocked the city to its core in February.

Alexander Lewis-Ranwell, 28, from Croyde in North Devon, denied killing Anthony Payne, 80, and 84-year-old twin brothers, Roger and Dick Carter at a hearing on Monday, November 19.

The court case will continue at Exeter Crown Court.

If you would like to receive updates from us about the case, then all you need to do is message  NEWS  via Whatsapp to  07852 750 438.

Once you have requested to join the group, save the number to your phone contacts book as Devon Live.

We won't share your details with any other members of the group or use your number for any other purposes.

To unsubscribe from the group all you have to do is text back STOP at any time and we will remove your number.

Any questions about the service? Message us on Facebook or email newsdesk@devonlive.com
