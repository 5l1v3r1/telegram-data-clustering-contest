Kartik Aaryan to take a break from 'Dostana 2' shoot to attend 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' song launch? Read details

19 Nov 2019, 21:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar starrer 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. After releasing the trailer of the film, the makers recently dropped the song 'Dheeme Dheeme' from the movie.

And now, the makers will release the next song of the film which is none other than a revamped version of Govinda and Raveena Tandon's hit track 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Maarein'.

Kartik shared a post to announce the launch of the song in Delhi. Check out the post here:

Kartik is shooting for his next 'Dostana 2' in Patiala and we hear that the actor has asked Karan Johar to allow him to attend the launch in Delhi. Reportedly, he will be there for the song launch and quickly return to his shoot in Patiala. Ananya and Bhumi will be joining Kartik at the launch.

The film, helmed by Mudassar Aziz, will hit the theatres on December 06.
