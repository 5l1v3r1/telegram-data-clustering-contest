Kylie Jenner got Christmas shoppers excited by wrapping her naked body in a giant red bow.

The lip kit mogul promoted her upcoming festive sale on Kylie Cosmetics by turning herself into a big sexy present.

Seemingly wearing nothing but a pair of sheer red stockings, Kylie, 22, hid her naked frame behind a giant bow, ready for unwrapping.

Proving she's a businesswoman to be reckoned with, two of the first comments came from the Kylie Cosmetics account, which said: "So excited!", and her Kylie Skin account, who said: "Wow!"

It comes after reports that Kylie massively grew her fortune by selling a stake in her cosmetics line.

With her personal fortune tipping over the $1 billion mark, she has now almost doubled her worth by selling off a majority 51 per cent share in her company.

It was announced that beauty giant Coty had snapped up their stake in Kylie Cosmetics in a deal worth around $600 million.

It means Kylie could now be worth around $1.6 billion - propelling her towards the $2 billion mark.

Kylie's deal with Coty was announced this week and means she'll be able reach more customers across the world thanks to the company's international infrastructure.

Although she's no longer the majority shareholder, Kylie is still set to make an absolute fortune from the deal.

Coty produces around 77 beauty brands including Calvin Klein, Burberry Beauty, Gucci and Max Factor, and Kylie admitted the deal will turn her range into an "international beauty powerhouse".
