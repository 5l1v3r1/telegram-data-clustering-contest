Gautam Gulati joins cast of Salman Khan’s ‘Radhe’?

19 Nov 2019, 21:18 by BizAsia Correspondent

‘Bigg Boss 8’ winner, Gautam Gulati has reportedly become the latest star to join Salman Khan’s Eid 2020 release ‘Radhe’.

According to Bollywood Hungama, Gulati will be seen in a pivotal role but no details have been revealed about his character.

The film helmed by Prabhudheva, already stars Disha Patani, Zarina Wahab, Jackie Shroff and Randeep Hooda. The shoot recently commenced in Maharashtra.

Khan is currently working on promotions for his next ‘Dabangg 3’, out on 20th December 2019.
