Marvel meets The Irishman in bizarre mash-up video

19 Nov 2019, 21:59 by Nick Fitzgerald

The trailer is all thanks to the team behind the Jimmy Kimmel Live show

A bizarre mash-up of Marvel meets The Irishman was revealed on the Jimmy Kimmel Live show. It's now doing the rounds online.

The spoof, featuring heavily around Iron Man, sees Martin Scorsese's creation turned into a stereotypical view of the Irish. Did they have to include the leprechaun? Seriously?

The video starts off as normal before Marvel's usual opening theme quickly appears on the screen.

Viewers can see Robert De Niro's character using a glowing shamrock ring which transforms him into a super hero "Irish Man".
