Citizens express loyalty to His Majesty during Oman-India football match

19 Nov 2019, 21:39 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Sultan Qaboos Sports Complex in Baushar looked colourful on Tuesday evening as Omanis came together not only to support the national team but show their gratitude and loyalty to His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said on the occasion of Oman’s 49th National Day.

In a statement issued online, Oman Football Association said: “At the 49th minute of the match between Oman and India, the Omani fans made a special show to express of their celebration of the 49th National Day.”
