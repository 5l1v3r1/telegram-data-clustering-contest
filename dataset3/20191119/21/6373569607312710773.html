Plymouth Argyle will be away to Graham Coughlan's Bristol Rovers in the FA Cup second round

Rovers won 1-0 away to National League high-fliers Bromley in their first round replay

19 Nov 2019, 21:39 by Chris Errington

Plymouth Argyle will play away to Graham Coughlan’s Bristol Rovers in the FA Cup second round.

The Pilgrims will travel to the Memorial Stadium on Sunday, December 1 for a 2pm kick-off.

That follows Rovers’ 1-0 win at National League high-fliers Bromley in their first round replay at Hayes Lane on Tuesday night.
