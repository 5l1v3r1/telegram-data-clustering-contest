Ankita Lokhande to replace Kriti Kharbanda in ‘Chehre’?

19 Nov 2019, 21:06 by BizAsia Correspondent

After Kriti Kharbanda’s unceremonious exit from Anand Pandit’s thriller ‘Chehre’, it is reported that former ‘Pavitra Rishta’ actress, Ankita Lokhande is being lined up for the female lead.

The film, which stars Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi, has been in the news of late after it was rumoured that Kharbanda’s diva-like tantrums has resulted in her exit. The makers of the film confirmed that her departure from the film. It tweeted, “In the light of recent media reports, we wish to clarify that @kriti_official & @apmpictures have mutually agreed to part ways on a cordial & professional note. We wish her success in all her future endeavors!”

Lokhande, who was last seen in ‘Manikarnika: The Queen of Jhansi’, has her hands full currently with ‘Baaghi 3’, where she plays Riteish Deshmukh’s love interest.
