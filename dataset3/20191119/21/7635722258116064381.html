Winning Euromillions ticket worth almost €123m sold in the UK

19 Nov 2019, 21:54 by Jack Quann

There was one winner of Tuesday's Euromillions jackpot worth almost €123m.

The winning ticket was sold in the UK.

The numbers drawn were 8, 10, 15, 30 and 42.

The Lucky Stars were 4 and 6.

The National Lottery says over 61,000 players in Ireland won prizes.

The highest amount won by an Irish player in the draw was €2,697.
