Intoxicated school bus driver blamed her erratic driving on a 'bad doughnut'

19 Nov 2019, 21:00

A school bus driver convicted of driving while intoxicated blamed her erratic driving on a ‘bad doughnut.’

Linda Sue Godejohn, 55, was arrested and charged with driving while intoxicated after a witness saw her recklessly driving a school bus with children on board in Houston, Texas in March.

The witness told police that Godejohn took a turn so sharp that she nearly caused the bus to roll over.

Godejohn was stopped by police and ‘flunked miserably’ on a field sobriety test, but later told KHOU-TV that she seemed intoxicated because of a bad doughnut she had eaten earlier in the day.

‘I don’t drink. I have not drank in years…I had ate, got something from the school to eat that had made me sick…a doughnut,’ Godejohn told reporters.
