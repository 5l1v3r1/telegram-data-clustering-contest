If you're hunting after each of the The Outer Worlds achievements, good news: the games' first patch, Patch 1.1.1.0, addresses a range of issues, including an achievement which wasn't popping correctly. The patch is out for both the console version and The Outer Worlds (Win 10). The game's been brilliantly non-buggy, and the only issues were relatively small. The font was pretty minuscule, and some players were having an issue where the game crashed after they arrived at Tartarus. Patch 1.1.1.0 has targeted these issues, with Obsidian detailing the fixes in their patch notes.
