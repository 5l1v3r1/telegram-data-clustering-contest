Chloe Goodman is pregnant! CBB star shares baby joy after cervical cancer scare

Chloe divulged the couple decided to try for a baby sooner because she wanted to have the "best chance" of becoming a mum

19 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Irishmirror.ie

Chloe Goodman is expecting her first baby with her beau Grant Hall.

The reality star, 26, said she decided to have a baby sooner with her footballer boyfriend, 27, because her recent cervical cancer scare.

Her first smear test diagnosed her with high-grade 3 abnormal cells; if the cells were left untreated it could lead to cervical cancer in years to come.

After having a biopsy to treat the abnormal cells, the telly personality was left her fearing she would miscarry or have a premature birth.

But Chloe gushed everything had worked out perfectly with her pregnancy announcement.

The mum-to-be voiced it's been her desire to have kids for years as she remarked upon her "maternal instincts" coming from being in a family of five girls.

She made the big reveal to Closer magazine : "I've wanted to be a mum for years. I'm from a family of five girls and I've always had maternal instincts...

"When I met Grant, we agreed early on that we wanted to become parents, so it feels like everything has fallen into place."

The lovebirds have been going steady for some time and had always planned on being parents.

Chloe divulged the couple decided to try for a baby sooner because she wanted to have the "best chance" of becoming a mum.

She added: "I knew I needed to get rid of the abnormal cells and have the op but it was stressful because I worried I wouldn't be able to carry a child full-term. It sparked me into trying sooner to give myself the best chance."

Unfortunately, Chloe had suffered a miscarriage at the age of 23.

She had been with her former partner Jordan Clarke at the time.
