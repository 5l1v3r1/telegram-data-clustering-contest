Diego Maradona set to quit Gimnasia as manager two months after his appointment

19 Nov 2019, 14:35

-Diego Maradona is set part ways with Argentina top side Gimnasia as manager

- The Argentine legend took over the affairs of the club in September

- Maradona is reportedly leaving because of the club president's decision not to seek re-election

Diego Maradona, Argentine idol, is reportedly set to quit Gimnasia as manager barely two months he took over the affairs of the club.

Maradona, who has not coached any team in his homeland since 1995, was unveiled as the new manager of the club in an emotional ceremony held at the club's stadium.

But the 59-year-old is reported to have met with family members and friends on his future with the Argentine top-flight.
