O'Sullivan, Trump, Allen, Selby and more in high quality Scottish Open draw

19 Nov 2019, 14:30

The field for the Scottish Open has been confirmed, with the likes of Ronnie O’Sullivan, Judd Trump, John Higgins and defending champion Mark Allen all involved.

Highlights of the first round draw include Stephen Maguire taking on Jimmy White, Neil Robertson facing Ken Doherty and O’Sullivan looking to gain some revenge over Dominic Dale.

The Spaceman downed the Rocket in the qualifying round of the World Open earlier this season in a huge shock, and the five-time world champion will out to get a win back over the Welshman.

O’Sullivan had been non-committal over his participation in the Scottish Open, due to his like of the venue, Glasgow’s Emirates Arena.

‘If it’s a train journey for four or five hours I probably swerve it,’ O’Sullivan said at the English Open. ‘I’ll probably try and swerve Scotland, I love Glasgow but where they put it, it’s not a great place.’
