Screams ring out as boy, 13, is thrown into the air after being struck by car

19 Nov 2019, 14:29

WARNING: UPSETTING CONTENT

Distressing doorbell camera footage shows the moment a 13 year-old boy was struck by a car and thrown into the air.

Eyewitnesses screamed with shock as the youngster, named as Nomar, was hit as he crossed the street on Monday afternoon in South Whittier, California.

He had been making his daily after school visit to the home of Evelyn Panameno, whose son Nomar is friends wiht.

The youngster was filmed standing behind a parked car, waiting to cross the street.

He let two cars go past, before walking out onto the road and being struck by a black Toyota Camry.

The thud of the impact, and screams of eye-witnesses, made neighbors run out onto the street to the boy’s aid.

Eyewitnesses described the aftermath as ‘heart-wrenching.’

Nomar was rushed to hospital with major injuries.

He was later found to have broken a few bones, but is now talking again and is expected to make a full recovery.

Nomar’s friend Anthony Panamero, who he had been visiting, said: ‘It was nice to know that he was good.’

Nomar’s dad is said to have been battling leukemia.

Driver Gina Lo-Blue stopped her car after striking Nomar and stayed at the scene.

She has not been arrested.
