Blackface apologises to fans and supporters as he drags colleague 2baba to court over song theft

19 Nov 2019, 14:37

- Singer Blackface has dragged his colleague 2baba to court over alleged song theft

- The ex-Plantashun Boiz member shared the news in a lengthy post on social media

- Blackface also pointed out that the protracted issue between him and his colleague is strictly professional

Nigerian music star and ex-member of defunct music group Plantashun Boiz, Blackface, has come out to share the news of the latest development in his protracted rift with colleague 2baba Idibia.

In a lengthy post shared on his official Instagram page, Blackface apologised to his supporters and other concerned parties that have been offended by his actions and inactions in the course of the years.

"I sincerely want to apologize to close friends, relatives, well wishers who may have been affected by my actions or inactions over the years," he said.

The musician disclosed that after waiting for so long to take actions, he and his legal team have finally decided to file a case against 2baba.
