Famous Johane Masowe Prophet dies in horror crash

A famous Warren Park Prophet  known as Madzibaba Edward of the Zimbabwean Jowani Masowe eChishanu Warren Park shrine was involved in an accident yesterday and died on the spot.

Circumstances leading to the accident are still unclear as the police are yet to release an official statement.

Together with fellow Madzibabas, Mathias, Gibson, Israel, Katsiru, Edward was part of the apostolic heavyweights called the ‘big five’

Meanwhile two people died in Harare’s Mbare area yesterday afternoon when tree crushed a kombi.

This followed after a heavy storm hit the capital.
