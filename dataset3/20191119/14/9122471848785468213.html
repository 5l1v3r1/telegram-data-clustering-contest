Urban Meyer’s Daughter Sends Message To Ohio State Fans

19 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Andrew Holleran

Urban Meyer will be back on Ohio State’s campus on Saturday morning. The former Buckeyes’ head coach will be in Columbus for FOX’s Big Noon Kickoff show ahead of Ohio State’s game against Penn State.

There will be two competing pregame shows at Ohio State on Saturday morning, as ESPN’s College GameDay will also be in town.

Meyer and Co. are of course calling for fans to show up to his pregame show.

“Love when dad comes back to the Shoe! Everyone meet me there! Outside the RPAC at 10am!” Meyer’s daughter, Gigi, tweeted on Monday night.
