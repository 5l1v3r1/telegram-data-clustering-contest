Destiny 2 Season of Dawn starts on 10 December

19 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Steve Wright

You've still got time to mop up Season of the Undying, Guardians.

Destiny 2 Season of Dawn will commence on 10 December, developer Bungie has revealed.

Not much else is known of Destiny 2‘s next season, though generally we know it will bring a new seasonal artifacts alongside new Triumphs, rewards and various challenges.

The change in seasons means that Guardians will have until the weekly reset on 10 December — or 11 December here in Australia — to finish up any current season, Season of the Undying, tasks.

Bungie said it will detail more of Season of Dawn in the leadup to the big event. What do you reckon we’ll see in the meantime?

Destiny 2 is currently available on Windows PC, Xbox One, PS4 and Stadia.
