Sarah Ann Basting

MADISON-Sarah Ann Basting, age 44, of Madison, passed away on Saturday, Nov. 16, 2019.

She was born on March 1, 1975, in Madison, the daughter of Richard and Linda (Moritz) Basting. Sarah graduated from Madison East High School in 1993.

She was a very active and devoted member of St. Bernard Catholic Church and especially loved singing in the church choir and performing with the St. Bernard Performers.
