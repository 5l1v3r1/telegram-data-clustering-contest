Trial collapses of man accused of driving 60mph before Mutley Plain crash

Judge has discharged jury in the case of 32-year-old Mark Hodge

19 Nov 2019, 14:31 by Stuart Abel

The trial of a man accused of dangerous driving before an accident in Mutley Plain has collapsed.

A judge has discharged the jury in the case of Mark Hodge at Plymouth Crown Court.

Hodge, of Radford Avenue, Prince Rock, went on trial yesterday after he denied driving an Audi A3 dangerously on August 6 last year.

Prosecutors claim that the car was being driven at 60mph before hitting an oncoming vehicle.

But Judge Paul Darlow discharged the jury for legal reasons.

Hodge, aged 32, was released on bail until a new trial fixed for January 22.
