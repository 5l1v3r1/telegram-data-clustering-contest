Kaia Gerber and Pete Davidson Are Now Kissing in Public

19 Nov 2019, 14:14 by Alyssa Bailey

Kaia Gerber and Pete Davidson are fine with everyone knowing they're dating now. The model went out with Davidson to a Charlotte Lawrence concert in New York City last night at Webster Hall. They were photographed kissing inside the venue so no, they really aren't just friends anymore. E! has a photo of the kiss, although it's a little grainy.

Gerber and Davidson were also photographed outside Webster Hall. Gerber dressed fashionably in a white crop top, jeans, and a black blazer. Davidson wore a blue hoodie, grey jacket, baseball cap, and jeans, as seen above.

Their date comes after reports came out this weekend that they went out to SNL's after party and showed PDA there. "Throughout the night, they were very affectionate and flirtatious with one another," a source told Entertainment Tonight. "At one point in the evening, Pete and Kaia were alone together in the back of the restaurant, in one of the booths. They were laughing with each other and exchanging flirtatious touches."

It also comes just days after E! reported that Gerber and Davidson's relationship had become romantic. "Kaia and Pete are definitely in a romantic relationship," a source explained to the outlet. "They have been friends for a while now and run in the same friend circle, but became romantic recently. They started hanging out alone more often after meeting up in NYC in October. Kaia expressed she loved Pete's personality. She thinks he is charming and is very intrigued."
