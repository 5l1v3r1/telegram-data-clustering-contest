ESPN Computer’s Picks For Week 13’s Biggest Games

19 Nov 2019, 14:26 by Andrew Holleran

Just two weeks remain in the 2019 college football regular season. The second-to-last full Saturday of the year is coming this weekend.

Week 13 will be highlighted by Ohio State’s game against Penn State. It will serve as a de facto Big Ten Championship play-in game (and, likely, a College Football Playoff elimination contest).

ESPN’s Football Power Index has made its pick for the game between the Buckeyes and the Nittany Lions, along with the rest of Week 13’s biggest games.

No. 2 Ohio State vs. No. 9 Penn State

Winner: Ohio State, 84.3 percent

No. 20 Iowa vs. Illinois

Winner: Iowa, 86.9 percent

No. 16 Notre Dame vs. Boston College

Winner: Notre Dame, 92.9 percent

No. 4 Georgia vs. Texas A&M

Winner: Georgia, 83.3 percent

No. 13 Baylor vs. No. 19 Texas

Winner: Baylor, 62.5 percent

No. 15 Michigan at Indiana

Winner: Michigan, 77 percent

No. 10 Oklahoma vs. TCU

Winner: Oklahoma, 88.2 percent
