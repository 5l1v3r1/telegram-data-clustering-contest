Slow-Motion Crash Testing Footage Captures The Zen Of Automotive Destruction

19 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Erin Marquis and Jalopnik

We all love cars here, but there’s just something about the chaotic fibreglass origami they turn into during crash testing. It is undeniably beautiful and at times haunting. A German crash-testing company seems well aware of this particular aesthetic, as its latest set of crash testing videos were shot with the slowest of slo-mo cameras so you can revel in every tiny detail as it is flung from the body of a car.
