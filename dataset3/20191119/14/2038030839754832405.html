Blue Ivy wins her first award at 7 years old

19 Nov 2019, 14:50

Cape Town – Beyoncé and Jay-Z's eldest daughter, Blue Ivy Carter has won her first award at the tender age of seven for the song, Brown Skin Girl.

The talented artist won the Ashford & Simpson Songwriter's Award with her mom and dad and ten others, according to AP.

The trophy was handed out at the Soul Train Awards on Sunday evening in Las Vegas.

The song beat five rivals including Melissa Jefferson (aka Lizzo) according to The Daily Mail.
