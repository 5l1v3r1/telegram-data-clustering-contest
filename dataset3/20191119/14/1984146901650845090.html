Dine with Santa before the Sage Gateshead's annual The Snowman concert

Families enjoying the orchestral screening of the classic film The Snowman can join father Christmas and his elves for a festive meal before or after the show

19 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Jerry Chen

Families enjoying the orchestral screening of the classic film The Snowman can join father Christmas and his elves for a festive meal before or after the show.

Accompanied by the magical live music of the Royal Northern Sinfonia at Sage Gateshead the seasonal screening has became a staple event for Christmastime.

The film showing will also be preceded by the orchestral performance of the classic children's tale,We're Going on a Bear Hunt.

You can visit the brasserie where there is a brand new menu for visitors wanting to treat themselves.

For the adults & Overs 12s the meal price would be £10.95 while the pricing for children under 12 it would be £7.95. In the midst of enjoying the lunch children will be able to meet Santa and his helpers to share their Christmas wishes.

Adults & Overs 12s Menu

  • Roast Turkey, honey roast vegetable, confit oil roast potatoes, buttered greens, sprouts, bacon wrapped sausage, jus
  • Brie, cranberry and mushroom wellington, honey roast vegetable, roast potatoes, buttered greens, sprouts, vegetarian stuffing, jus (V)

Children’s Menu

  • Roast turkey dinner
  • Fish fingers, fries and tomato sauce
  • Chicken nuggets fries and bbq sauce
  • Pizza & Fries

Puddings (purchase on the day)

  • Ice cream – 1 scoop £2.50, 2 scoops £3, 3 scoops £3.50
  • Festive chocolate yule log, served with fresh double cream £2.95
  • Sweet mince pie, clotted cream £2.95                              

The concerts are on Saturday, December 22 at 2pm and 5pm, Monday, December 23 at 11am, 2pm and 5pm and Christmas Eve, Tuesday December 24 at 11am and 2pm. 

The “lunch with Santa and his helpers” event is happening on those days and more -  from 11:30am to 4pm on Saturday, December 7; Sunday, December 8 ; Saturday, December 14 ; Sunday, December 15; Saturday, December 21 ; Sunday, December 22; Monday, December 23 ; Tuesday, December 24.

Tickets for The Snowman are available on the Sage website from £19.50.  A family ticket is £60/£65 and consists of four tickets to include at least one adult and one child.

Those 16 and under get £2 off. Children two and under go free, sitting on a knee.

Please note that The Snowman performance on 22nd December at 2pm is a relaxed performance which provides a safe environment for autism or disabilities and their families supported by British Sign Language (BSL) interpretation.
