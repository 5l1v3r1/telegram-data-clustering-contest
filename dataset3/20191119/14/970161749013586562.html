Spoilers: Hollyoaks confirms Ste's emotional exit scenes

19 Nov 2019, 14:44

These are the emotionally charged Hollyoaks scenes tonight which see Ste Hay (Kieron Richardson) make the decision to take a step away from the village. His life was very nearly destroyed recently after he was groomed by a far right gang.

After realising the depravity of their racism, he found himself trapped in their world and it was only when he narrowly avoided death at the hands of Stuart Sumner and Jonny Baxter that he was able to find some closure. However, he has a great deal of healing still to do.

He has been growing close to Azim over the last couple of weeks as it was Azim who helped Ste see the light and start to step away from the twisted values of Stuart’s group. However, Sami is concerned that Ste is not ready to commence a relationship so soon after all of the damage that has been done to him and byhim to others.

He has a frank chat with him tonight and Ste comes to a decision and says his goodbyes – but fear not, this is not the last we will see of the character as Kieron has confirmed that he will be back in time for the show’s 25th anniversary celebrations.
