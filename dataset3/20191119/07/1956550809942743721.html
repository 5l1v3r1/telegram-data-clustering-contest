Brand-new tapas restaurant NIDO to open in Downtown Dubai

The Spanish restaurant will be at Sheraton Grand Hotel Dubai

19 Nov 2019, 07:52 by Amy Mathieson

Spanish cuisine is a little underrepresented here in Dubai, but if you’re a huge fan of tapas you’re in for a treat as a new restaurant is now open.

NIDO has launched today (Tuesday November 19) at Sheraton Grand Hotel Dubai, on Sheikh Zayed Road and Time Out Dubai’s been down for an exclusive look round.

It’s being billed as a “secret Spanish speakeasy” (although we just let the cat out of the bag on that one) and you’ll need to say something in Spanish (hola will suffice) to gain access via a secret door at Moe’s on the 5th. And the venue only has 12 tables, so expect an exclusive, yet relaxed vibe.

You can expect a whole load of tapas at the new Spanish restaurant, all with a modern twist. Dishes include empanadas, chorizo, gambas and more as well as selection of Spanish and Latino mixed drinks.

“After months of creating delicious new flavours for diners in Dubai to experience, we’re excited to finally open our doors to welcome guests on a journey from Spain to South America, to experience authentic, contemporary dishes, prepared with attention to every detail,” said NIDO’s chef Salvador Carrillo.

We’re already brushing up on our Spanish. No podemos esperar para comer las croquetas (we can’t wait to eat the croquetas).
Open daily 6pm-3am. Sheraton Grand Hotel Dubai, Sheikh Zayed Road (04 503 4444).
