Kangana Ranaut’s sister Rangoli Chandel hints at a new project announcement

19 Nov 2019, 07:18 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kangana Ranaut won heart of her fans with the portrayal of Rani Laxmibai in her period drama, ‘Manikarnika: The Queen Of Jhansi’. The actress, who has also named her production house after the warrior queen, is all set to announce a new project under her banner. Kangana Ranaut’s sister Rangoli Chandel recently took to her Twitter handle to hint at an upcoming announcement. Although it is not clear if today marks the birth anniversary of Rani Laxmibai, she is being remembered today for the same.

Mentioning the same, Rangoli wrote, ‘It is not clear that today is Queen Manikarnika’s birth anniversary, in those days there were no English calendars, but it’s nice to remember Martyrs...(contd)’
