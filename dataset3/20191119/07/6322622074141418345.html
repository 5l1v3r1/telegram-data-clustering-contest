CIMB Bank structured warrants resume trading after disruption

19 Nov 2019, 07:37 by Emir Zainul

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 19): Market-making activities of CIMB Bank Bhd-issued structured warrants on the FBM KLCI and Hang Seng Index (HSI) resumed today after a disruption due to technical problems, CIMB Bank said in statements to Bursa Malaysia today.

"We would like to inform all CIMB Bank warrant holders that we are resuming our market-making activities for all CIMB Bank-issued structured warrants on the KLCI and the HSI," CIMB Bank said.

Earlier today, CIMB Bank said its structured warrants were facing technical problems, which disrupted market-making activities for the securites on the KLCI and HSI.
