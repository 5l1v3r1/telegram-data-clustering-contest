Golden Gate teenager Jaderian Guillaume reported missing in Collier County

19 Nov 2019, 07:48 by WFTX Digital Team

GOLDEN GATE, Fla. -- Collier County deputies are looking for a missing Golden Gate teenager.

15-year-old Jaderian Guillaume was last seen Wednesday, November 13th.

He is believed to be a runaway.

He was wearing a gray shirt and blue jeans, and he has earrings. Deputies believe he ran away from home.

If you have any information on where he is, please call the Collier County Sheriff's Office at 239-252-9300.
