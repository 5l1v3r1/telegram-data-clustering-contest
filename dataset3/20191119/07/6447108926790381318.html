Half Life: Alyx announced by Valve as flagship VR title

19 Nov 2019, 07:21 by Aran Suddi

It was earlier this year when Valve properly entered the VR market itself through its own headset Valve Index, and it marked a sign of things to come. Valve confirmed it would be working on its own VR titles, and the company has made its first announcement regarding those games. This is a big one as for the first time in years a new Half Life game is coming.

Half Life: Alyx has been confirmed as one of Valve’s VR flagship titles.
