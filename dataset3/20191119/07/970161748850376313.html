Halsey denies she’s pregnant with Evan Peters' baby, just allergic to gluten

19 Nov 2019, 07:30

Halsey’s definitely not pregnant, she’s just allergic to gluten.

The singer, 25, took to Twitter to shut down claims she was pregnant with new boyfriend Evan Peters’ baby after photos of him touching her stomach got fans talking.

The couple were photographed out together over the weekend, but the shot that got everyone talking was of Halsey cradling her stomach as Evan, 32, also laid a hand on her tummy.

Fans were quick to jump to the conclusion that the pair were expecting, despite it being a pretty new relationship… but it seems like it was just an ill-advised pancake to blame for all the excitement.

Halsey took to Twitter to set the record straight, telling fans: ‘Still not pregnant. Still just allergic to gluten. Still love pancakes.’

She added: ‘Is it a boy? Is it a girl? It’s pancakes.’

Halsey and Evan have been dating since October, after she and British singer Yungblud split up after around a year of dating.

She confirmed her new relationship on the Ellen DeGeneres show, admitting: ‘I don’t know what’s scarier though … having to talk about my boyfriend on TV or [having a member of the crew jump out of a box at her]? It was a double whammy.’

The couple then went Instagram official with a photo of them in matching couples costumes for Halloween as they dressed up as Sonny and Cher.

It’s not the first time Halsey has had to deny pregnancy rumours, as fans got overexcited earlier in the year after she teased some ‘big news’.

She later posted: ‘People think my last tweet is me hinting I’m pregnant, which means I’m either: a. Gaining weight B. Acting weird than usual. Jokes on u, I’m doing BOTH! However STILL not pregnant!’

Halsey has previously spoken about wanting to have children, admitting in July 2018 that she wouldn’t say no to having a child once her tour was over.

She said: ‘Once the tour is over, I feel like anything could happen. I could decide I want to move to Italy for a year and write. I could get pregnant.
