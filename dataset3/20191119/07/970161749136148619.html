Kate Garraway's children persuaded her to join I'm A Celebrity

19 Nov 2019, 07:43

Kate Garraway’s children persuaded her to take part in the new series of I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here!

The Good Morning Britain presenter is taking part in this year’s installment alongside reality TV icon Caitlyn Jenner and pop singer Nadine Coyle.

And it turns out we have her children to thank, as they were the ones who got her on the plane to Australia in the first place.

Speaking to Hello before she headed out to the jungle, Garraway said her youngsters, Darcey and Bill, really, really wanted to see their mum eat bugs live on TV.

‘Darcey really got into the last few series,’ the 52 year-old said. ‘[She] kept saying “You should so do this mum.”‘

She also apparently had a very… unique approach to training for life in the jungle.

‘[She said] things like ‘You should eat that slug from the garden, or jump off of that wall, to practice,”‘ Garraway continued. ‘It’s been a war of attrition.’

Well, that’s one way to be prepared.

Garraway also admitted to the publication that she may be apprehensive of entering the jungle (and has a fear of snakes! We’ll sure that’ll end well) but she’s ready for all the challenges – and the benefits – that come from taking part in the reality show.

She continued: ‘It is an incredible show and also you get to meet extraordinary people. I’ve never met anyone who said they regretted doing it.

‘People say it’s absolutely life-changing and you get so much peace from being out of contact with the world.’

We don’t how much peace will be coming her way, though.

The show’s big coup this year, Caitlyn Jenner, has already broken down in tears and considered quitting on her second day in the jungle.

And Garraway herself has already had to endure another terrifying Bush Tucker trial alongside the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star.
