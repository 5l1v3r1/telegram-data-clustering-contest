Moesha Bodoung spotted in Dubai chilling with Wizkid

19 Nov 2019, 07:43 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian actress and Instagram model Moesha Boduong is really enjoying her stay in Dubai as she has been spotted in a new video chilling with Nigerian superstar, Wizkid.

The actress who loves to share photos and videos of herself shared her experience at the One Africa Music Fest in Dubai on her Instagram page where she the video of herself and the Nigerian music superstar.

Wizkid is in Dubai for One Africa Music Fest 2019. The One Africa Music Fest is considered one of the continents biggest music event with this year’s event bringing together numerous entertainers across the world.

Moesha is also seen in other videos enjoying a private party with Wizkid, Tiwa Savage and a few other people.
