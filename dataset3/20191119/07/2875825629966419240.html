Diana Ross, Cat Stevens and Christy Moore announced for final year of Cork's Live at the Marquee

Tickets are going on sale next week

19 Nov 2019, 07:58 by Cormac O'Shea

Diana Ross is heading to Cork next summer for one night before she heads to Glastonbury.

The Motown legend will play Live at the Marquee on June 25, just days before she is to make an appearance at the UK's most famous festival.

Tickets for what is sure to be a huge occasion will go on sale at 9am on Monday, November 25, CorkBeo reports.

The 75-year-old, maybe best known for Chain Reaction, previously played at the venue 14 years ago in 2005.

In what could be the last year of Live at the Marquee as we know it, Cat Stevens, Christy Moore and the RTE Concert Orchestra plus Jenny Greene will all perform in June.

Tickets for Cat Stevens will go on sale at 9am on Tuesday, November 26 - while you can get your hands on tickets for Christy Moore on Wednesday, November 27 at 9am.

And finally, Jenny Greene and the RTE Concert Orchestra tickets will go on sale on the Thursday, November 28 - again from 9am.

Ticket prices for all the gigs are yet to be released but information should be made available in the coming days.

The Live at the Marquee series will end after 2020 as the site will be designated for homes.

Developers plan to start building on the docklands site at the end of next summer which will spell the end of the annual concerts.

Live at the Marquee has been running since 2005 which acts like Elton John, Lady Gaga and Bruce Springsteen all performing at the venue.

Glenveagh Properties, who own the site, plan to build up to 1,000 homes on the plot near the River Lee.
