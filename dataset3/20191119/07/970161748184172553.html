Edinson Cavani offers to fight Lionel Messi during Argentina vs Uruguay

19 Nov 2019, 07:58

Lionel Messi and Edinson Cavani clashed during Argentina’s 2-2 draw with Uruguay with the Paris Saint-Germain striker reportedly offering to fight Barcelona’s talisman.

The flashpoint occurred towards the end the first half of Monday night’s friendly in Israel after Messi was fouled.

Cavani approached Messi after he was brought down and the two players began exchanging words.

According to Ole, Cavani offered to fight Messi and the Barcelona forward replied: ‘Whenever you want.’

The pair had to be separated by several players, while Uruguay defender Diego Godin attempted to diffuse the situation by putting his arm around Messi.
