UPDATE: Driver runs to catch dog after Western Freeway incident

19 Nov 2019, 07:05

UPDATE: Police have confirmed that a driver involved in a single car incident on the Western Freeway this evening was attempting to catch a dog which ran away from the scene.

There are small delays ongoing on the Melbourne-bound lanes this evening between Bungaree and Wallace after a car hit a stationary road works truck.

Drivers are asked to expect some delays.
