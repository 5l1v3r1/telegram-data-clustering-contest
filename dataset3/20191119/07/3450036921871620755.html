Nonpartisan Uno encourages family fun without the politics

19 Nov 2019, 07:58 by NBC 10 NEWS

Mattel is selling a new nonpartisan version of the card game Uno. It encourages families to gather at Thanksgiving and forget about politics.

Nonpartisan Uno replaces red and blue cards -- which might be seen as politically charged -- with orange and purple. It also comes in a purple box, instead of the usual red.

If someone starts talking politics while playing, anyone can grab the new "veto" card and play it to skip that person's turn.

Nonpartisan Uno is available for a limited time at Walmart stores nationwide.
