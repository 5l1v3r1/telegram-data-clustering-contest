19 Nov 2019, 07:56

- A Nigerian lady identified as Mary Isaiah Edidem has called out a woman on Facebook for chasing after her man

- The lady claimed that one Deborah Ekanem is chasing after her man, inviting him to hotels

- However, Ekanem has debunked the claims, stating that the woman is trying to tarnish her image

A young Nigerian woman identified as Mary Isaiah Edidem has taken to Facebook to call out a woman identified as Deborah Ekanem. The woman claimed that Ekanem is chasing after her man.

In the post shared by Edidem, she warned Ekanem to stay away from her man and stop disturbing him. She added that Ekanem should also stop inviting her man to hotels.

Edidem further said that Ekanem should stop disturbing since she left him for a prophet. She asked her to let them be in peace.
