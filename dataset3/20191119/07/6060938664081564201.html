Ananya Panday wishes Tara Sutaria on her birthday with an adorable throwback picture

19 Nov 2019, 07:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Today is Tara Sutaria's birthday and B-town celebs have taken to their social media handles to wish the actress on her special day. Amongst them is her 'Student of the Year 2' co-star Ananya Panday.

Read Also: Watch video: Ananya Panday enjoys her song 'Dheeme Dheeme' as she takes on a road trip

Recently, Ananya took to her Instagram handle to wish her friend on her birthday. She shared a throwback picture from their shoot days and penned a heartfelt note. She wrote, ''Happy Bday Taar!! Enjoy some mall road noodles for me''. In the picture, we can see them sitting on a bench and enjoying some noodles in Mussoorie. Besides them we can see a kid too whose face is not visible in the photo, so she called him Tiger Shroff.

Both the stars made their big Bollywood debut with Punit Malhotra's 'Student of the Year 2'. The film failed to impress the audience but the performance of Ananya and Tara were hailed by the audience.

Meanwhile, on the work front, Ananya will be seen next in 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' opposite Kartik Aaryan. She has been also roped in for 'Khaali Peeli' with Ishaan Khatter. On the other hand, Tara is gearing up for the release of 'Marjaavaan' opposite Sidharth Malhotra. The film is scheduled to release this week.
