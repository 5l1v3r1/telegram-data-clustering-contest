Jane McDonald 'thrilled' by Adele Roberts' framed photo on I'm A Celebrity

19 Nov 2019, 07:49

Adele Roberts had the nation in hysterics when she whipped out a framed photo of Jane McDonald in the I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! jungle last night and now the singer has responded in the best way possible.

The actress turned Loose Women presenter reached out publicly to Adele and admitted that she was delighted to see that the BBC Radio 1 host had brought her portrait as her luxury item.

Tweeting her 148,700 followers, Jane wrote: ‘Absolutely thrilled to see my picture in the jungle. @AdeleRoberts I flipping love you. Best of luck.’

A true queen.

During an extra task in the ITV show last night, the team at Base Camp were given the opportunity to win meaningful luxury items for each of their fellow contestants by passing them along a row on paddles above a swamp.

If the item fell in, the chance of winning the prize sank along with it.
