Vegan man sues Burger King over 'Impossible Whopper' being cooked alongside meat

19 Nov 2019, 07:52 by CBS Austin Staff

AUSTIN, Texas (KEYE) -- A vegan man is suing Burger King because its new meatless Impossible Whopper is cooked alongside other meat products, according to the lawsuit.

CNN Business reports that Phillip Williams, the plaintiff in the case, is a vegan who does not eat or drink anything that uses animal by-products.

Williams filed the class action lawsuit Monday in the Southern District of Florida. It claims that the though the Impossible Whopper is advertised as "meat-free," it isn't vegan because it's cooked on the same grill as meat products.

The suit accuses Burger King of false advertising, CNN reports, as well as "benefiting monetarily from offering a vegan option that is not in fact vegan."
