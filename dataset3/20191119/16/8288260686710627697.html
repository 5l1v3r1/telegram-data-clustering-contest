Air ambulance scrambled as man seriously hurt at Walsall industrial unit

The man has been taken to hospital via land ambulance

19 Nov 2019, 16:51 by Jordan Coussins, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/

Medics treated a man for serious injuries after a medical emergency at an industrial premises in Walsall.

Emergency crews – including the Midlands Air Ambulance – were deployed to Lichfield Road, Brownhills, after the incident at around 3pm on Tuesday, November 19.

An ambulance and a paramedic officer were also sent to the scene, a spokesman for West Midlands Ambulance Service confirmed.

The man – who has not been named – was later taken to Walsall Manor Hospital for injuries that are not believe to be life threatening.

 

A spokesman added: “We were called at 3.10pm to an industrial premises on Lichfield Road, Walsall, to reports of a medical emergency.

“One ambulance, a paramedic support officer and the Midlands Air Ambulance from Cosford was sent to the scene.

“One patient, a man, was treated for serious injuries that are not believed to be life threatening.

“He was taken to Walsall Manor Hospital for further treatment.”
