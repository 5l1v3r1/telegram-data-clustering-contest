UP board Intermediate practical exam 2020 date sheet released, check schedule

UP board Intermediate practical exams 2020: The, the practical examinations will be held in two phases, the first phase from December 15 to 29, while the second phase from December 29 to January 13, 2019

19 Nov 2019, 16:38 by Education Desk

UP board Intermediate practical exams 2020: The Uttar Pradesh Madhmik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has released the timetable for the practical examinations for Intermediate, Class 12. According to the schedule, the practical examinations will be held in two phases, the first phase from December 15 to 29, while the second phase from December 29 to January 13, 2019.

The first phase will be conducted for the students from Agra, Saharanpur, Bareilly, Lucknow, Jhansi, Chitrakoot, Faizabad, Mirzapur, Devipatan and Basti zones.

In the second phase, the intermediate students from Aligarh, Meerut, Muradabad, Kanpur, Prayagraj, Varanasi, Azamgarh and Gorakhpur will appear in the practical examination.

The marking of the practical examination will be on a 50:50 basis, 50 by the internal evaluator and external evaluator, as per the official notification.

The Class 10, 12 examinations for Class 10, 12 examinations will be held from February 18 to March 6, 2020.

The exams will be conducted in two shifts morning and evening. Morning shift will be conducted from 8 am to 11:15 am and evening shift exams will be conducted from 2 pm to 5:15 pm for both the class 10 and class 12 UP board exams.
