Lionel Messi: 5 times Barcelona star has been involved in controversy with Argentina

19 Nov 2019, 16:21

Lionel Messi is arguably that best footballer in the world considering his achievements in football since he started his career at the Camp Nou.

Messi is a one-man team as he has never played for any other club before in his career except Barcelona where he has won five Ballon d'Or awards.

He has won all major trophies at Barcelona, but Lionel Messi has failed to turn his success at club side to the national team

Recently, the 32-year-old had a bust-up with Edinson Cavani while playing for Argentina against Uruguay in which the PSG star offered the Argentine a fight which he agreed.

However, this is not the first time Lionel Messi has been involved in a controversy on the pitch as we take a look at five other incidents.

1. Telling Tite to shut up

Lionel Messi had an argument with Brazil coach during international friendly with Argentina in which the Barcelona star told Tite to shut up.
