Typhoon Ramon threatens Cagayan as Tropical Depression Sarah maintains strength

PAGASA warns of possible flooding in coastal areas under Signal Nos. 2 and 3 due to high waves caused by Typhoon Ramon (Kalmaegi). Meanwhile, Tropical Depression Sarah will also bring rain.
