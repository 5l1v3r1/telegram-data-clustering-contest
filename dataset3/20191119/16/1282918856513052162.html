Australia's David Warner is known to have a warm relationship with India's skipper Virat Kohli, as the two batting maestros have shared many light moments off the field. Recently, David Warner had posted a video of his daughter smashing some deliveries and claiming that she was Virat Kohli. Well, David Warner did not take long to publicly admire Virat Kohli and his physique. David Warner took to Instagram to comment on a abs workout video shared by Virat Kohli shortly after India beat Bangladesh in the first Test at Indore. The Australian opener replied to Virat Kohli's post and expressed his awe for the Indian batting maestro's abs.
