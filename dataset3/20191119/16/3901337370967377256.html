Brazil Stars Name Five Greatest Countrymen, Snub Neymar

19 Nov 2019, 16:29 by SL International

Two of Brazil's current stars have named the five Selecao legends they believe to be the nation's greatest of all time, but teammate Neymar was snubbed for their selection.

Click through the gallery above to see Fabinho and Roberto Firmino's five greatest Brazilian players of all time.

In the latest episode of Liverpool TV's GOAT List, Brazil stars Fabinho and Roberto Firmino were asked to name the five greatest players to have represented their nation.

The pair were given a list of 10 players to choose from, which included footballers from past and present, as well as a wildcard each to add a player not present on the list.
