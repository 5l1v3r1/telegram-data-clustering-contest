Child asks Santa for food and a home in Christmas letter

19 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Newstalk

A child has written a poignant letter to Santa Claus - asking him to provide her family with a home and food for Christmas.

The letter was found at a community centre in Liverpool, England after it was left in a Christmas post box in the cafe when they began decorating for the festive season.

The post box was left out for children who visit the centre to post their Christmas wishes.

In the letter, the seven-year-old child says: "Dear Father Christmas. Can you help?

"Can we have a home for Christmas? Mam wants us to be all together. Can you give us some food and can I have just a nice doll for Christmas?

"Thank you."
