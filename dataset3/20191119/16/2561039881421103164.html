Elago Releases AirPods Case Inspired by iPod

19 Nov 2019, 16:00 by iClarified

Elago has released a new AW6 AirPods case that is inspired by the iPod classic.

The case is compatible with first and second generation AirPods and supports wireless charging.

Features:
● RETRO DESIGN - Put the case on and enjoy the nostalgic feeling of using an older music player.
● PROTECTION - The case is made of premium silicone material to protect your AirPods case from scratches and external impacts. The LED indicator section is thinner, allowing the front LED to be visible.
● COMPATIBILITY - Compatible with Apple AirPods 2 and 1 (Wired & Wireless Charging Case). No hinge point at the back and the case supports wireless charging for the Apple AirPods Wireless Charging Case.
● ANTI-SLIP COATING - There's a special anti-slip coating inside the cap. Push it down completely and adjust the case to make it fit perfectly.
● PERFECT GIFT IDEA - Perfect gift for Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Years or any other special occasion.
● WARRANTY - Purchase with confidence! 30 day, 100% Money Back Guarantee & 1 Year Replacement Warranty. (Disclaimer - Apple AirPods not included. The scroll wheel on the case is for design and is not functional.)

You can purchase the AW6 case in white or black for $11.99 at the link below. If you like this case you may want to check out their W6 Apple Watch Stand as well.
