Robbery suspect on the run from EC police

19 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Algoa FM

A search is underway for a suspect who escaped from police custody in Humansdorp on Tuesday.

Provincial police spokesperson, Colonel Sibongile Soci, said that 20-year-old Fernando Swarts was arrested on Monday on a charge of business robbery.

She said the circumstances surrounding his escape form part of the investigation.
