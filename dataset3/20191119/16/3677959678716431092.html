Stadia Pre-Orders Reportedly Below Expectations, Might be a “Monumental Flop” For Google

19 Nov 2019, 16:52 by Nathan Birch

Google Stadia officially launches today, and, thus far, response from reviewers and players has been somewhat muted. But are people actually buying into the new platform? In order to play at launch you had to purchase the Google Stadia Founder’s Edition or Premiere Editions, which come with a Chromecast Ultra and Stadia controller for $129. Well, according to reliable Kotaku insider Jason Schreier, Stadia pre-orders were well below expectations. In fact, Schreier has gone so far as to dub the Stadia launch a “monumental flop”…
