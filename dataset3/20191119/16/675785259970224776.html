Does the Currys Price Guarantee work over Black Friday?

Under the Currys Price Guarantee shoppers won't have to hold out until 29 November to get the best price for coveted items they purchase.

Under the pledge, Currys will refund any items bought now in the 'Why Wait' deals that go down further on Black Friday .

How it works?

Currys will price match any items before you buy against the price of any UK rival retailer - whether you spot it in store or online - and the chain will even price match against discount code offers.

Shoppers can pop into store or contact Currys customer service teams online or via telephone - check details online at currys.co.uk/price-promise.

You can also price match on items you've bought up to a week afterwards and the chain will refund the difference.

What happened in 2018?

Last year Currys shoppers snapped up 14 orders a second online.

The retailer rolled out massive price cuts across all categories, but the most popular items during the 2018 sale included the Nintendo Switch and Xbox One games consoles as well as the popular Apple iPad mini.

Other highlights included the Dyson V6 vacuum cleaners which was slashed to under £200 and the Sonos One Wireless Smart Sound Speaker which was reduced to £174.

For Black Friday 2018 Currys opened 327 of its stores nationwide from 7am for earlybird shoppers to browse discounted stock before work.

Most of the deal continued over the weekend and throughout Cyber Monday online and in stores for customers who still wanted to pick up more tech bargains.
