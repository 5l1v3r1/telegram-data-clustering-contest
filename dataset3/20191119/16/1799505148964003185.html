Pot Car

19 Nov 2019, 16:36 by BRIEF

DENVER — Advantage Opco settled with a man who claimed it rented him a car with 40 lbs. of marijuana in the trunk, for which he was arrested — and the company charged him $1,724 for towing the car, in Denver County Court.
