The Clause head for Nottingham on their 2019 UK tour - here's what Pearce Macca had to say

'We're trying really hard to focus on making these gigs a spectacle'

19 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Simon Wilson

Birmingham band The Clause are coming to Nottingham on their biggest UK tour to date. You may not know the name but you may well have heard their song This Feeling, as it has been used to soundtrack Premier League coverage on BT Sport and Channel 4 reality show, The Circle. Frontman Pearce Macca does the talking...
