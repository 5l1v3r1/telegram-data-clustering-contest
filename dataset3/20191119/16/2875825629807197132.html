What time and channel is Munster v Racing 92? TV information, kick-off time, odds and more for the Heineken Cup game

Simon Zebo returns to Thomond Park

19 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Donagh Corby

It's going to be a special evening in Limerick for former Ireland star Simon Zebo when he returns with Racing 92.

The Cork native and his partner Elvira Fernandez welcomed their third child, baby Noah Anthony, earlier this week, and this will be Zebo's first game since his birth.

In terms of action on the pitch, it's the hosts that go into the game as favourites after Munster's comfortable defeat of Ospreys.

Racing beat Saracens in an impressive 30-10 win last weekend, and they look a force to be reckoned with this season.

Here's all you need to know about the game:

When is it on?  

The match is set for kick off at 5.35 pm on Saturday, 23rd November.    

Where is it on?

Thomond Park in Limerick will host the game

What channel is it on?     

The match will be shown live on BT Sport 3, with coverage beginning immediately after the Lyon v Leinster match.      

You will also be able to follow the match on the Irish Mirror Sport's live blog.    

Teams    

The teams will appear here when they're announced.     

Betting Odds    

Munster 4/9    

Draw 19/1
