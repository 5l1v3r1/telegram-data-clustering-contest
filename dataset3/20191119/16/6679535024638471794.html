Water supply to be hit today

19 Nov 2019, 16:41 by Special Correspondent

There will be no water supply in some areas in the city on Wednesday as the main water supply line from Thumbe developed a leak near Naguri on Tuesday.

Hence, water supply to the main city areas, Car Street, Mary Hill, Surathkal, Kodical, Katipalla, Sasihitlu, Mukka, Panambur, Kavoor, Kuloor, Kana and Bala will have no water supply till 8 p.m. on Wednesday.

The 18 MGD-capacity main pipeline developed the leak near Naguri pumphouse, a release from Mangaluru City Corporation said. The line supplies water to water storage sumps in Bendoor and Pumpwell.
