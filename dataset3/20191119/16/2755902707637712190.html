Trevor Dongo defends controversial semi nude pic with Jah Prayzah’s dancer

Crooner Trevor Dongo has spoken out on the controversial semi-nude picture of him with one of Jah Prayzah’s dancers known as Lissa Tanaka which has since gone viral on social media.

The picture set tounges wagging on social media with some feeling that the display was over the top and almost obscene.

Dongo scoffed at rumors that the two are in a relationship and stated it’s just pure art.

“It was just a picture appreciating art and there is a project we are working on together with Lissa.
“We are trying to portray a love so clean and without boundaries, shared with one mind joining two souls into becoming one.”
“There is nothing more to the picture and I think people should start embracing art in the new era,” said Dongo.

Lissa Tanaka declined to comment on the picture.

“I have no comment over the circulating picture,” she said.
