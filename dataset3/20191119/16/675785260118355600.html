Man who 'forced girl, 17, to watch him rape her little brother' is 'pure evil'

WARNING: DISTRESSING CONTENT - Joseph McCann is alleged to have tied the children's mother up as he sexually abused them forcing her to listen helplessly, the Old Bailey heard

19 Nov 2019, 16:52 by Joseph Wilkes, Emily Pennink, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

A teenager has described the look of 'pure evil' on the face of a man accused of sexually abusing her and raping her younger brother.

Joseph McCann, 34, allegedly tricked his way into a family home in Greater Manchester on May 5 where he is accused of carrying out sex acts on a 17-year-old girl and 11-year-old boy.

He tied up their mother with hair straighteners and molested the children, saying: "If you move, I will kill your kids," the Old Bailey heard.

The teen girl told the court how she feared becoming McCann's "sex slave" before she jumped out of a first-floor window and limped to a relative's house.

"You know when you can see inside someone's eyes and you know they are pure evil," she told the jurors.

In a video-taped police interview played in the hearing, the teenager described how her attacker had initially grabbed her, put his hand over her mouth and told her to "shut up".

She said: "I managed to wriggle out of it. He pulled me down onto the floor. That's when he must have knocked me out. His knife must have cut me in a few places.

"I was shouting 'mum, mum, mum', but at this point he had the knife.

"My mum was like 'what are you doing?', trying to stop him."

McCann allegedly tied up the mother for being "gobby" and ordered her not to move.

The teenager claims he took cocaine and swigged vodka before repeatedly sexually assaulting her and her brother.

McCann is alleged to have orally raped the 11-year-old.

Their mother listened helplessly from the room next door, as McCann also allegedly forced the brother and sister to caress his genitals.

She said: "When I did not do what he said he kept putting the knife up to me.

"In my head I was trying to think of alternative ways of trying to get out of this situation.

"I could not do anything about it. Even in the shower, he took the knife with him everywhere he went.

"He kept brushing it on my skin. That's more like an 'I'm in charge' thing."

She went on: "I mentioned my brother was 11 and he said 's*** I didn't know that, how old are you?' I said 17.

"He said me: 'you are going to Europe tomorrow, you are mine'.

"At this point my life flashed before my eyes. I thought of marriage and everything.

"I'm going to be his sex slave. I thought, I'm not having this."

She added: "We kept being held hostage, being abused at knifepoint. It's scary. It's awful."

The court has heard how the girl fractured her heel when she jumped out of a window naked to escape.

McCann, from Harrow, west London, denies 37 charges relating to 11 women and children.

The trial continues.
