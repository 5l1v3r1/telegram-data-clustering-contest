Making Headlines: Ekstein On The Billboards In Baku

19 Nov 2019, 16:00 by SL Social

It’s safe to say that life has been kind to skilful midfielder Hendrick Ekstein since leaving Kaizer Chiefs at the end of last season, not only is he lighting up the scene in Azerbaijan, he is also featuring on billboards.

Ekstein’s stats this season make for interesting reading; he has registered three assists and found the back of the net twice in ten appearances for his Sabah FC side.
