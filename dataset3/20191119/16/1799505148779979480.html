US v. California Farms

19 Nov 2019, 16:33 by BRIEF

SACRAMENTO — Labor Secretary Eugene Scalia sued California-based blueberry companies, Munger Bros. LLC, Crowne Cold Storage LLC, et al. in federal court, claiming they hired H-2A visa workers instead of U.S. citizens.
