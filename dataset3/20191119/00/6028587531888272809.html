Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Dessert Subscriptions

19 Nov 2019 by Chelsea Davis

Why not give the gift that keeps giving? For those that are looking for an unbeatable gift for the sugar-fiends in their lives, get them something that keeps the sugar flowing, non-stop. New desserts delivered to your door every month? Yes, please! From ice cream and cookies to caramels and cakes, here are a variety of impressive dessert subscriptions.

Harry & David Dessert Of The Month Club: When it comes to decadence, nothing beats Harry & David. Their dessert subscription is no exception! Each month you’re treated to one after-dinner goodie sent to your door ranging from fruity desserts such as the peach and berry pie and lemon strawberry cheesecake to the chocolaty indulgences such as the ultimate chocolate cake and cream cheese brownies. Choose from either a 3, 6 or full year subscription.

Jeni’s Ice Cream Pint Club: Smooth, rich, creamy and oh-so-delicious, Jeni’s Ice Cream is the good stuff. You won’t find any weird ingredients that don’t belong in ice cream like fake flavoring, dyes or stabilizers. It’s the kind of ice cream you find yourself devouring in minutes. Jeni’s comes in all sorts of unique flavors such as gooey butter cake, churro and brandied banana brûlée and even has fun seasonal flavors that are to die for. Hello sweet potato with torched marshmallows! Join the club and get four pints three times a year.

Norman Love Confections: For the chocoholics in your life, gift them a monthly (or 3, 6 or 12 month) gourmet chocolate box subscription filled with a variety of dessert goodies! With this subscription, you get a themed assortment of 4-5 fan favorites, including limited edition items that can’t be found else where! From artisanal truffles and premiere bars to hand crafted specialties and cookies, who ever gets this gift is in for a treat! Norman Love Confections is known for its edible master pieces and commitment to high quality ingredients, artistry and innovative flavors.

McCrea’s Caramels: This subscription delivers approximately three dozen pieces of one flavor of luxe handcrafted caramels. McCrea’s caramel connoisseurs choose a different flavor each month to send over. Special flavors range from dark roasted mocha and ginger fusion to rosemary truffle sea salt and single malt scotch. Made with real ingredients and local dairy, these caramels are the perfect combination sweet and creamy and come in luxe, biodegradable packaging.

Cheryl’s Buttercream Frosted Cookie of the Month Club: Buttery, sweet and satisfying, these buttercream frosted cut-out cookies are perfect for gift giving! With each delivery comes 24 individually wrapped cookies, baked and decorated according to the season. They’re great for sharing and bringing as party treats (or to the office!). These seasonal cookies are super festive and have been pleasing palettes for over 30 years.

Salt & Straw Pint Club: If you love one-of-kind flavors, premium ingredients and chef-driven concepts, then this ice cream is for you. Salt & Straw features seasonal flavors that keep your taste buds guessing. To give you an idea, their Thanksgiving collection includes roasted peach and sage cornbread stuffing and salted caramel thanksgiving turkey. Classic flavors depend on location, but include local ingredients known in the area whether it be SF or LA. Choose between either the Keep The Love Alive subscription to receive five new seasonal flavors each month or the Get A Taste Of The Sweet Life for five packs of our new seasonal flavors to be delivered for a period of 3, 6 or 12 months.

Caramel Caravan Co.: This sweet subscription delivers a 10 or 20-piece box of small batch, handmade tasty caramels each month. Delectable flavors to choose from include dark chocolate sea salt, cashew, dark chocolate marshmallow, sea salt and coffee. Caramel Caravan Co. is a female ran company based out of Redwood City, CA that uses local ingredients and biodegradable packaging to wrap their treats.

Cravory Cookies: Who doesn’t love freshly baked cookies? Cravory Cookies offers several types of sweet subscriptions that feature six brand new flavors each month. Seasonal flavors are constantly changing meaning you’ll never get stuck with that one flavor no one likes. Flavors include classics like birthday cake and red velvet to special ones like rosemary balsamic and pineapple upside down cake.
