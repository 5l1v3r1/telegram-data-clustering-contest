Qatar’s logistics leader GWC signs as first Official National Supporter for FIFA Club World Cup 2019™

19 Nov 2019 by FIFA.com

FIFA announced today that GWC (Q.P.S.C.), a leading provider of logistics and supply chain solutions in the Middle East and North Africa, has signed as an Official National Supporter of the upcoming FIFA Club World Cup Qatar 2019™ presented by Alibaba Cloud.

GWC has been an active logistics provider for some of the biggest international sports events Qatar held since 2006. The company is backed by a team of committed and innovative experts in the field and supported by state-of-the-art IT systems, process improvement engineers, and a logistical infrastructure covering more than 3 million square meters, including the largest self-contained logistics hub in the region, the Logistics Village Qatar. They will deliver logistics and supply chain services for the FIFA Club World Cup™, supporting the successful implementation of the event.

“We are passionate about being part of the FIFA Club World Cup Qatar 2019 as a National Supporter, a role that falls in line with our long history of supporting the nation’s greatest sporting events, youth, and helping people make success stories,” stated GWC Group CEO Ranjeev Menon. “We proudly promote the world class sporting and logistics infrastructure that we in Qatar are developing.”

“Delivering a FIFA Club World Cup™ requires a huge amount of planning – and logistics plays a key role in managing that complex operation. We are delighted to welcome GWC as a National Supporter for this event and look forward to adding their local expertise to this event,” said Jean-Francois Pathy, FIFA Director of Marketing Services.

The FIFA Club World Cup 2019 Qatar™ will be played from 11 to 21 December at three venues in Qatar. The full match schedule can be found here. Fans wishing to see the action live can apply for tickets exclusively via FIFA.com/tickets.
