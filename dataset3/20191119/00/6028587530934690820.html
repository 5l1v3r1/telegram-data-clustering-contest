Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Most Luxe Charcuterie Baskets

19 Nov 2019 by Chelsea Davis

What’s more impressive than an expertly crafted charcuterie board? Artisanal meats, cheeses, crackers, nuts and fruits, olives... sky’s the limit when it comes to crafting the perfect charcuterie platter! This holiday season keep things simple and gift your favorite savory-snack loving friend one of these ready-to-go baskets.

Ultimate Epicurean Charcuterie And Cheese Collection: For the biggest charcuterie fan in your life, impress them with this epic charcuterie and cheese gift. This basket is over flowing with a huge variety of savory goodies from all over the world. The meats include a Casalingo Italian salami; a wild boar salami; Serrano Paleta ham; and more. On the cheese side there’s an array of favorites including Alpine Swiss cheddar and Manchego. The basket is also loaded with snacks ranging from dried fruits to nuts and the charcuterie must-haves: artisanal crackers, fig spread, dolmas and more. This unbelievable collection also comes with a beautiful Acacia wood tray, perfect for hosting guests!

The Market Charcuterie Kit: This lovely gift basket features a classy selection of two hand selected cured meats; a bottle of wine; assorted accoutrements and local snacks; along with a slate tile cheese board. The curated goodies come from local craftsmen and independent farmers. The Market is an urban eatery, cocktail bar, bottle shop and specialty shop based in Columbus, Ohio.

Wine, Cheese & Charcuterie: This deluxe basket of luxe goodies is from The Epicurean Trader, a charming specialty shop based in San Francisco. Known for its small batch artisan goods and craft products, this shop has filled this basket with fine cheeses, charcuterie, crackers and condiments, along with a bottle of locally produced wine. Spiced almonds, Cowgirl Creamery cheeses, dried fruits and more will have whoever gets this gift happy as can be.

Daniel Boulud Charcuterie Selection Box: This special box is curated by renowned New York City chef Daniel Boulud’s expert Chef Charcutier, so you know its top notch. This gourmet gift box includes three types of seasonal Charcuterie including Pâté Grand-Mère, Chef Boulud’s grandmother’s recipe of pork jowl, chicken liver, parsley and shallots; house-made light and fluffy Brioche & Pain Campagne Loaves; French Cornichons; and Dijon Mustard. It’s a great gift for your friend with an affinity for traditional French cuisine.

Deluxe Charcuterie and Cheese Assortment: For a classic, well-balanced charcuterie gift, this basket from Harry and David can’t be beat. This curated selection of snacks include a cheddar Gruyère blend with black truffle and a rich, creamy Merlot cheese. These cheeses pair excellently with the two types of salami and crackers and crisps.

DeLallo Meat Lovers’ Charcuterie Collection: This indeed is a meat lovers dream. This gift basket comes filled to the brim with everything you could need on a charcuterie and cheese platter. Perfect for entertaining, this comes with a slate board for sharing and a varied collection of cheeses, meats, olives and everyone’s favorite antipasto additions. This collection also comes in a luxe blue gift box making it ideal for the holidays.

Deluxe Antipasto Assortment: This antipasto assortment brings together a delectable combination of meats that include a variety of sliced salami, several different cheeses and tasty antipasto including nuts, roasted peppers, fire roasted bruschetta, garlic-stuffed olives and artichokes with merlot. Also included is a nice bamboo cutting board ideal for entertaining and pre-dinner festivities.

Lady & Larder Large Cheese Board: This stunning charcuterie board brings together a plethora of orchard-grown nuts and dried fruits, artisanal meat, cheeses, crackers and condiments. Packaged in Williams Sonoma’s signature gift crate with a red oak serving board, it’s ideal for anyone looking to wow someone special. It even comes with a “map” to help create the most artistic arrangement possible. Lady & Larder pride themselves on supporting a 100% domestic and sustainable food culture.
