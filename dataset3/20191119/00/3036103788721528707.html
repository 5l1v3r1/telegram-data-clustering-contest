Test your broadband speed and win prizes worth R5,000

19 Nov 2019 by Staff Writer

MyBroadband’s speed test platform gives you the ability to test your Internet connection speed and rate your Internet service provider (ISP).

These consumer ratings are used to compile monthly and quarterly reports which rank South African Internet service providers.

To ensure a neutral testing environment, MyBroadband’s speed test servers are hosted in Teraco’s vendor-neutral data centres.

Through NAPAfrica, all network operators present at its peering points are provided with a free 10Gbps connection to MyBroadband’s speed test platform.

Test your speed and win

MyBroadband is giving away prizes worth R5,000 to encourage users to do a speed test.

To stand a chance to win one of these great prizes, here is what you should do:

  • Test your speed here: MyBroadband Speed Test platform or Speedtest.co.za
  • Rate your ISP after the speed test is complete
  • Post your speed test results here: Giveaway entry

The giveaway will run until 29 November 2019, and the winners will be announced shortly afterwards.
