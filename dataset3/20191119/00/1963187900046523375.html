Micho satisfied with Zamalek's last training camp

19 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

Zamalek coach Milutin Seridovic, Micho, said his team had benefited greatly during the recent international break.

"This was a very important period for us with more than 59 matches played last season without interruption, so the last period was important for us," said Micho.

"The 6th of October camp was very necessary and we were keen on the technical and physical preparation and raising the physical fitness of the players in addition to bringing the players closer to each other."

"We have increased the fitness rate of Mahmoud Hamdi" Al-Wensh "and Ahmed Sayed" Zizo "and Obama, and the result is their appearance well and Zizo joined the Egyptian team as well because of his performance.

"We played strong friendly matches against the Wadi Degla, the South African Olympic team and Haras El-Hedoud, and we benefited from them all in the best possible way," Micho said.
