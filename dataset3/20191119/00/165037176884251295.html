Pablo Hernandez signs Leeds extension

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Leeds United will have the services of Pablo Hernandez until at least 2022 after he renewed his contract.

Pablo Hernandez has signed a two-year contract extension with Championship promotion challengers Leeds United.

The winger is now tied to the Leeds until the end of the 2021-22 season. Hernandez initially arrived at Elland Road on loan from Al-Arabi in August 2016Â before signing permanently the following January. Named Leeds' Player of the Year in each of his two full seasons as a permanent member of the squad, Hernandez scored 12 goals and laid on the same number of assists last term.

His outstanding campaign was not enough to prevent Leeds from suffering agony in the play-offs, though, as they missed out on promotion. Hernandez has scored two league goals and assisted as many this term, with Leeds again challenging for a return to the Premier League.
