Oppo Reno 10x zoom Special Edition will come with 12 GB RAM and new Ocean Blue color

19 Nov 2019 by Michail

Oppo unveiled its Reno smartphone series back in May and it quickly grew in size. Now, the Oppo Reno 10x zoom is getting a refresh with 12 GB RAM and 256 GB storage alongside a new Ocean Blue color. The new Special Edition Reno 10x zoom will be launched on November 30 in Malaysia and will also make its way to more markets later.

Other than the new coat of paint and the increased RAM/storage, the phone is identical to the regular Reno 10x zoom. It still carries a 6.6-inch AMOLED with the signature shark fin pop-up camera, a Snapdragon 855 chipset, and a triple camera setup with a 13MP periscope telephoto unit. On the software front, it runs Android 9 Pie with ColorOS 6 on top.
