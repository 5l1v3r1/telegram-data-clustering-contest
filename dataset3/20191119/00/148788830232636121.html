Marvel’s ‘Runaways’ Season 3 Trailer Gives a New Look at the Show’s Final Season

19 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Ben Pearson

Runaways, the teen-centric superhero series that’s been chugging along on Hulu since 2017, is coming to an end – but there are still ten new episodes left. Hulu just dropped the trailer for the show’s upcoming third and final season, and while much of it looks pretty dour, at least Elizabeth Hurley seems to be having an absolute blast playing the evil sorceress Morgan le Fay.
