Samsung unveils Star Wars Edition Galaxy Note10+ bundled with Galaxy Buds

19 Nov 2019 by Ricky

With the holiday season coming soon, Samsung is announcing a new Star Wars Special Edition Galaxy Note10+.

In anticipation for the December 20 release of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker, the Special Edition comes with a themed smartphone case, a collectible metal badge, and a themed pair of Galaxy Buds. The Special Edition Note10+ comes with exclusively themed wallpapers, shutdown animations, icon packs, and sounds.

The S Pen is also themed, coming in a red finish while the Galaxy Buds come in a black charging case with a red interior and everything comes in themed packaging.

Starting December 13, the Star Wars Edition Samsung Galaxy Note10+ will be available in limited quantities in a single unlocked variant for $1300. You’ll be able to find it on Amazon and select Best Buy stores, select Microsoft Stores, and Samsung Experience Stores across the US. You’ll also be able to get it on Samsung.com.

Outside of the US, The Special Edition will be available in Australia, Denmark, Finland, Germany, Hong Kong, Korea, Norway, Russia, Spain, Sweden, Turkey, UAE, and the UK.

Recently, Samsung also announced a Galaxy Note10 Anniversary Premium Package which bundles the smaller Note10 with Galaxy Buds, a Galaxy Watch Active2, and a silicone case.
