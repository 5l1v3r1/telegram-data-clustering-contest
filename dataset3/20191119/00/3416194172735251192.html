Bella Hadid & Selena Gomez Spoke After Instagram Controversy (Report)

19 Nov 2019, 00:47

The drama is reportedly squashed between Selena Gomez and Bella Hadid.

According to TMZ, Bella “went out of her way to reach out to Selena this weekend to squash any potential beef.”

The site added, “Bella told Selena it was never about taking a shot, the model simply deleted because she didn’t like the pic.”

Everyone thought Bella might be shading Selena when she deleted an Instagram post that Selena had commented on.

When Selena saw that Bella had deleted the post, she wrote “that sucks” on another post. Later, Selena tried to explain that she shouldn’t have written that without knowing the full situation.
