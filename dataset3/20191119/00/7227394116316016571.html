Galaxy Watch and Watch Active get Galaxy Active 2 features with an update

19 Nov 2019 by Ro

With the launch of Galaxy Watch Active 2, Samsung introduced a number of new software features like enhanced Bixby and Samsung Health functions and plenty of cool customizations options. Now, the company is making those features available with a software update to Galaxy Watch and Galaxy Watch Active owners.

ABixby gets smarter and now supports British English, French, German, Italian and Spanish. And there's plenty to look forward to besides Bixby. New and more customizable watch faces are in tow along with re-designed UX. The most notable feature from the UI is the new Touch Bezel so users can now navigate by swiping on the bezel.

It's going to be a staged rollout so you might have to wait a couple of days before it hits your device too.
