14 years on from the Ronaldinho show at the Bernabéu

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Ronaldinho will always be remembered for his great technical details, but if there is a match that was the highlight of his career it was a match 14 years go. The Brazilian crushed Real Madrid at a Santiago Bernabéu who bowed down at his feet and ended up applauding him.

One of the most iconic gestures in the history of Spanish football happened 14 years ago. The Santiago Bernabéu surrendered to Ronaldinho's quality, one of the players that will always be remembered. Pure talent.

Barcelona crushed Real Madrid on 19th November 2005. The Brazilian fell in love at his rivals' stadium and left a few details to remember. Neither Sergio Ramos nor Casillas could stop him.

It was a unique night that saw a historical image of a father and son applauding a player from their eternal rival. But he wasn't just any player, his magic was capable of unimaginable passages of playing and making a ticket for the match worth it.

At the end of the 90 minutes, the score was 3-0 to Barca. Ronaldinho stole the show with a superb brace that made him a great in what probably was the best game of his career.

And with Messi also playing, he watched Ronaldinho and learned for the future.
