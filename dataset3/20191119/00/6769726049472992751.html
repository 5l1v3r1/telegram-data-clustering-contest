A Sephora Perth pop-up in time for Christmas – you beauty!

19 Nov 2019 by Keren Bellos

BEAUTY fans will find it easier to spread the joy this festive season following the launch of global giant Sephora’s pop-up store in Perth.

Located in David Jones Claremont Quarter, the shop has the full range of Christmas gift packs, including skincare and beauty products from Huda Beauty, Becca Cosmetics, Fenty Beauty and the Sephora Collection.

Hawaiian shopping centres general manager Scott Greenwood said it gave Perth beauty enthusiasts the chance to get their hands on leading international cosmetic brands.

“The pop-up Sephora has arrived just in time for people to start their Christmas shopping,” he said.

“It will also be warmly welcomed by those who love the Sephora brand but until now have only been able to access products online or interstate.”

The Sephora pop-up will be available until January or when stock runs out.

Sephora has stores in NSW, Victoria, Queensland and South Australia.
