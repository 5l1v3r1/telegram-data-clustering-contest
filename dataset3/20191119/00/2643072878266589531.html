President Buhari Holds Closed Door Meeting With Security Chiefs

19 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Buhari Meets Security Chiefs In Abuja

President Muhammadu Buhari on Tuesday, held a closed-door meeting with the country’s security chiefs and head of security agencies.

Naija News reports that the meeting held at the Aso Rock Villa in Abuja.

The high-level security meeting was confirmed in a tweet by the President’s personal aide on social media, Bashir Ahmad who said: “President Muhammadu Buhari is currently holding a closed door meeting with Security Chiefs and heads of other security agencies at the Presidential Villa, Abuja.”
