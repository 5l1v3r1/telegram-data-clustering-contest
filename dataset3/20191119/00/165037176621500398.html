Only 500 tickets left for Valencia v Chelsea

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Valencia informed on Tuesday that there are only 500 tickets remaining for the decisive Champions League match between Valencia v Chelsea on 27th November.

In the match that will take place at 18:55, both teams will be playing to try and get into the last 16 of the Champions League, something that Valencia would assure mathematically if they win.

Albert Celades' men will have their first opportunity to get into the last 16 in front of their fans in 7 years.

Chelsea, who Valencia beat 1-0 in the last meeting between the two, have managed to win on their last two away games. There is currently a three-way tie at the top of the group between Chelsea, Valencia, and Ajax, with 2 matchdays to play.
