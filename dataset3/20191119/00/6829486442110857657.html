This Video Of Two Men Dancing On The Dating Show ‘Flirty Dancing’ Is Ridiculously Cute

19 Nov 2019, 00:23 by Michelle Rennex

In 2019, we’ve seen our fair share of dating shows.

There’s The Bachelor, The Bachelorette, Bachelor in Paradise, Love Island, First Dates, The Farmer Wants a Wife, the list goes on. But all of these shows are nothing compared to the UK dating show Flirty Dancing.

In a premise which sounds absolutely horrifying, two strangers must learn a dance separately and perform it together when they first meet. Their blind date has no chatting, no dinner, no awkward cheese platters – literally just dancing.

While I thought this show would be a total disaster, I had to eat my words after watching a clip of Garry and Ryan. If love at first sight is real, Garry and Ryan should be the poster boys for it.
