Al Badry stresses on changing everything in the coming period

19 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

Egypt coach Hossam Al Badry said he would not be silent again against those who criticize the team without "rationality".

"I will respond to those who do not speak rationally because they do not know the technical issues in the team and I will reveal their mistakes," Al Badry told On Sport on Monday after a 0-0 draw with Comoros in the second round of the 2021 AFCON qualifiers.

"We faced difficult circumstances, represented by the delay in the appointment of the coaching staff, postponing the league for a long time and not going to many friendly matches."

"This is in addition to the injuries that hit some players, and not calling some players who have faced problems with their clubs," Al Badry added.

"I salute all the players and thank them today," he said.

"We have four games ahead and we will qualify for the African Nations," he stressed.
