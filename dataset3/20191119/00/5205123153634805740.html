I'm A Celeb fans ask for subtitles because they can't understand Nadine Coyle's Irish accent

19 Nov 2019 by Harry Brent

FANS of I'm A Celebrity: Get Me Out Of Here are apparently having trouble understanding what Derry-born Nadine Coyle is saying.

The latest series aired for the first time on Sunday evening and social media was awash with viewers complaining that they needed subtitles to decipher her strong Northern Irish accent.

One user wrote: "I legit have no idea what Nadine Coyle has said yet ... @ITV can we get subtitles for her please? #ImACeleb."
