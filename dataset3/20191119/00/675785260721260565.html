Argos slash 20% off Frozen, Star Wars and Marvel toys ahead of Black Friday 2019

As part of the Argos crazy code discounts shoppers can pick up savings on top toys for Christmas in stores and online - and it includes popular Frozen products too

19 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Chantelle Symester, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

Argos are slashing the price of Frozen, Star Wars and Marvel Avengers toys.

The popular high street chain is giving Christmas shoppers a chance to pick up coveted toys for less in the run up to the festive season as part of its ten week crazy code promotion.

Bargain hunters can use the special STAR20 promo code online at argos.co.uk or in stores nationwide from today until Tuesday 26 November.

Top items featured in the savings include this new Disney Frozen 2 Sven figure (originally £120) and singing Elsa doll (originally £20).

Comic book fans will be able to get their hands on a Marvel Avengers: Endgame Nerf Captain America Assembler (originally £24) and any kids obsessed with Star Wars will love the LEGO Star Wars Resistance A-Wing Starfighter Set (originally £25).
