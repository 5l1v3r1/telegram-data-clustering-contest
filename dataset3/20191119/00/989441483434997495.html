General Abebe Teklehaymanot interview part II

19 Nov 2019

borkena

In part II of the interview with Dereje Haile, former Ethiopian Airforce chief General Abebe Teklehaymanot talks about the war with Eritrea and the level of preparation on the part of Ethiopia, among other things.

He was a member of Tigray People’s Liberation Front (TPLF) central committee. Before joining TPLF, he was a member of Ethiopian People’s Revolutionary Party (EPRP).
