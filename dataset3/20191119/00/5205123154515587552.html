Diana Ross announces two massive concerts in Cork and Dublin

19 Nov 2019 by Rachael O'Connor

I'M COMING OUT! To Cork and Dublin to see Diana Ross!

In the latest in a series of high-profile names recently announced for Ireland -- like Lionel Richie, The Killers and Elton John to name just a few--  the legendary Diana Ross has confirmed she will play not one, but two massive concerts on the Emerald Isle next year.

The 75-year-old Motown legend will hit both Cork and Dublin in June 2020, performing at the iconic IMMA Royal Hospital Kilmainham in Dublin on Friday the 26th of June and headlining the Live At The Marquee Festival in Cork on Thursday 25th of June.
