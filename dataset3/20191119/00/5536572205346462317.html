Dean Spanos Gets Loudly Booed By Chargers Fans In Mexico City (VIDEO)

19 Nov 2019, 00:20 by Darrelle Lincoln

Dean Spanos is not a popular man among the Los Angeles Chargers’ fan base.

The Chargers owner made an appearance at an NFL event for the buildup to the Chargers’ Monday Night Football game against the Kansas City Chiefs in Mexico City. Spanos was introduced to the gathering of fans and he got loudly booed by fans there as one guy could be heard yelling, “San Diego.”
