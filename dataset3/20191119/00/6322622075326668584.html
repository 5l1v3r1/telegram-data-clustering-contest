HLIB Research raises target price for Hock Seng Lee to RM1.64

19 Nov 2019, 00:47 by theedgemarkets.com

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 19): Hong Leong IB Research has maintained its “Buy” rating on Hock Seng Lee Bhd (HSL) at RM1.31 with a higher target price (TP) of RM1.64 (from RM1.58) and said HSL’s 9MFY19 earnings of RM45 million (+7% y-o-y) were below house and consensus expectations due to slower than expected construction progress billings.

In a note today, the research house said HSL’s year-to-date (YTD) core PATAMI (+7% y-o-y) increased due to stronger contribution from both construction and property segments.

“Outstanding order book of RM2.5 billion translates into 4.8x cover ratio with YTD job wins amounting to RM605 million (FY18: RM142m).

“Cut FY19-21 earnings forecast by 7-10%. Maintain Buy with higher TP of RM1.64 (from RM1.58) despite the earnings cut as we rollover our valuation base to FY20, pegged to an unchanged 13x PE multiple,” it said.
