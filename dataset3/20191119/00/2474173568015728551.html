Stop Using The Phrase “Sex Act Gone Wrong”

19 Nov 2019 by Jenn Selby

“British backpacker died when consensual sexual activity went wrong,” screamed the headline. Not from a tabloid, not from a half-baked Men’s Rights Activism (MRA) blog, but from the BBC. After a backlash on Twitter, the headline was amended to make it clear that it was in reference to what the defence in the Grace Millane case claimed, but the damage at that point was already done.
