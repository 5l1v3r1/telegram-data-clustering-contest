Nikki Galrani reveals about her lover and marriage plans - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

19 Nov 2019

Nikki Galrani started her film career in Malayalam cinema in 2014 and has since acted in several hit movies in Telugu, Tamil and Kanada as well.  She is currently starring as the female lead opposite Arun Kumar in 'Dhamaka' directed by Omar Lulu.

During a promotional press meet of 'Dhamaka' a section of the media quizzed Nikki about her love life to which she gave the frank opinion that she is in a serious relationship with a Chennai based person but she cannot reveal who he is at this point of time.  The 'Kalakalappu 2' heroine added that she and her lover have planned to get married after three years.

Nikki Galrani's upcoming Tamil movie is 'Raja Vamsam' directed by Kathirvel in which she is costarring with Sasikumar and Yogi Babu along with an ensemble cast of Radha Ravi, Vijayakumar, Raj Kiran, Manobala, Singam Puli and Nirosha.
