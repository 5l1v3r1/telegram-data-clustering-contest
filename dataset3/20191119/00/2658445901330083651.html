Mother ‘beat’ her babies to death - The Nation Newspaper

19 Nov 2019 by https://www.facebook.com/alao.h.joshua

Damian Duruiheoma, Owerri

Police in Imo State have arrested a housewife Mrs Ukamaka Ezike for allegedly battering two of her three little children to death in Awo Idemili community in Orsu council area of the state.

Police Public Relations Officer (PPRO) Orlando Ikeokwu, in a statement, on behalf of the Commissioner of Police, Rabiu Ladodo, said the suspect’s husband, Christian Ezike reported to the command at the weekend that while he was away, his wife on November 15, allegedly beat their biological children, including a two-month old baby to a critical condition.

The police said that injuries the children received from the beating led to them being hospitalised, disclosing that the two-month old and the two-year-old died while receiving treatment in the hospital.

The suspect, the statement said, was later arrested.

The statement said, “One Christian Ezike of Awo Idemili, in Orsu LGA, reported at the Divisional Police Headquarters, Orsu, that while he was away from his wife, Ukamaka Ezike, beat up this three children, Kosarachukwu Ezike, four years old, Chinwendu Ezike, two years old and Chinecherem Ezike two months old, they were rushed to the hospital, and Chinecherem and Chinwendu died while receiving treatment. The suspect has been arrested and investigation has commenced.”
