‘Dragon Quest Builders 2’ Comes To PC This December

19 Nov 2019 by Ollie Barder

If you missed out on Dragon Quest Builders 2 on the PlayStation 4 and Switch earlier this year, you will be happy to know that it is coming to PC this December.The Dragon Quest Builders games are action role-playing games where you can build all manner of things in a manner not too dissimilar to Minecraft.

The first Dragon Quest Builders game was released a fair few years back and it was thoroughly excellent.

The added twist here is that the story of these games is currently tied into the Erdrick Trilogy of Dragon Quest games that debuted back on the NES during the 80s.

That means there are all sorts of references to parts of classic Dragon Quest lore, which adds an interesting layer to the overall narrative.

While I definitely preferred the first Dragon Quest Builders over this sequel, it is still a good game and having it available on PC is something I think is entirely worthwhile.

I only hope that the first Dragon Quest Builders is released on PC at some point in the future as well.

Dragon Quest Builders 2 is released on PC via Steam on December 11.
