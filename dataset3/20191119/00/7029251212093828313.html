Dar es Salaam roads closed due to leaking gas tanker

19 Nov 2019 by The Citizen Reporter

Dar es Salaam.Transport in Dar es Salaam has been brought to a standstill after the authorities decided to close some of the roads due to leaking of gas from a tanker at the Ubungo junction, which links Mandela-Sam Nujoma and Morogoro roads.

Kinondoni Regional Police Commander Mr Mussa Taibu confirmed the incident but did not say which roads exactly were affected and how long it would take to clear the area.

“I am aware of the incident but I can’t tell you which roads have been closed; I am currently contacting the Kinondoni Regional Traffic officer,” said Mr Taibu.
