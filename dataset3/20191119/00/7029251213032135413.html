Four opposition MPs accused of jumping bail to spend a night behind bars

19 Nov 2019 by Hadija Jumenne na Mainda Mhando

Dar es Salaam. The fate of four legislators from the main opposition Chadema, who are in remand prison for allegedly jumping bail will be known tomorrow after the Kisutu resident magistrate court postponed their case.

The Members of Parliament, who are accused of jumping bail are Tarime rural MP John Heche, Iringa Urban MP Peter Msigwa, Kawe legislator Halima Mdee and Bunda Urban MP Ester Bulaya.

On Friday, the court ordered the arrest of the four legislators for allegedly jumping bail.

On that day the accused were supposed to appear in the court at 9.00am but until 10.05am they were yet to arrive.

They are facing a sedition case number 112 of 2018. The magistrate said no explanation was given to the court by the sureties of the accused.
