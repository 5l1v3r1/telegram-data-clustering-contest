Stoke have Cosgrove on their radar

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Stoke City have got their eyes on Sam Cosgrove to strengthen their attack in the January transfer window. The player is under contract at Aberdeen.

Stoke City are going through a precarious situation. The English outfit are in the Championship relegation zone and need to turn around the situation as soon as possible.

With only 3 wins in their last 16 matches, the team's main target is a striker and for that, they have got their eyes on the Scottish Premiership, according to 'The Sun'.

They are looking at the Aberdeen striker Sam Cosgrove. In 20 matches he has scored 16 goals and has become one of the revelations of the seasons.
