Ariana Grande Is “Very Sick”

19 Nov 2019 by Olawale Adeniyi

The news is not encouraging about Ariana Grande.

Ariana Grande is “very sick” and the rest she gave herself has not improved her situation.

She made the disclosure via her Instagram handle, claiming to have woken up “ten times worse” over the past weekend, and worrying “what’s going on with her body”.

“Hello my loves. I am still very sick. I have been sick since the London concert. I do not know how, but my head and my throat are still so in pain, “she wrote first on Instagram, reports People, before adding last Sunday ( Us Weekly ) :

“I woke up ten times worse and it hurts so much (…). I’m not sure what’s going on (…) ”

Ariana Grande has cancelled her last two concerts, while her fans and loved ones are worried about her.

The singer will perform on Tuesday, November 19, on the side of Atlanta.
