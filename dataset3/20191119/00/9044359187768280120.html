WATCH: Michael V. shares the biggest lesson he learned in doing 'Family History'

19 Nov 2019 by AEDRIANNE ACAR

Multi-awarded comedian and content creator Michael V. dished out the most important thing he learned while shooting his debut film Family History.

EXCLUSIVE: Michael V., natuwa na maraming Pinoy sa Amerika ang tinamaan sa 'Family History'

During his guesting in Kapuso ArtisTambayan on Monday, November 19, Direk Bitoy stressed that aspiring filmmakers should study the whole process of doing a movie.

He added that getting a mentor would also be beneficial to help you in the learning process.

“The best lesson or best advice that I can give you is to actually study,

“Pag-aralan ninyo kasi hindi basta-basta pinapasok itong filmmaking. Marami sa atin, hindi man lang nagka-crash course sa pagpasok sa paggawa ng pelikula.

“I would suggest na magkaroon ka ng mentor para talagang maituro sa iyo ‘yung tamang paraan ng paggawa ng pelikula, hindi talaga basta-basta.”

Michael V. released his first film Family History last July a joint-production with Mic Test Entertainment and GMA Pictures.
