Apple Renames 'WWDC' App to 'Apple Developer' App, Adds Dark Mode, Dev Program Enrollment, More

19 Nov 2019, 00:22 by iClarified

Apple has updated its WWDC app, renaming it to the 'Apple Developer' app and introducing new features that include Dark Mode support, Dev Program enrollment, and more.

Welcome to Apple Developer: the primary destination for Apple developers and designers. Apple Developer is your source for developer news, educational content, the WWDC experience, and more, so you can stay up to date on the latest technical and community information.

Features:
• Browse news, developer stories, and informative videos.
• Catch up on videos from past events and download them to watch offline.
• During WWDC, access the conference schedule, sign up for labs, and watch session live streams.

What's New In This Version:
Thank you for your feedback. New features include:
• Discover tab
• Enhanced Search UI
• Dark Mode support
• Apple Developer Program enrollment (in supported countries only)
• Korean language support
• Other enhancements and bug fixes

You can download Apple Developer from the App Store for free.
