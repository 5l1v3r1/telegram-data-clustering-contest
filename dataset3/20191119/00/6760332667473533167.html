Roja serial actress Priyanka's marriage with lover cancelled - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

19 Nov 2019

Actress Priyanka Navalkar who has acted as a child artiste in Telugu cinema made her Tamil debut as Hansika's friend in 'Theeya Velai Seiyanum Kumaru' but became famous after playing the lead role in the serial 'Roja' playing on Sun Tv.  She fell in love with Telugu serial actor Rahul and got engaged in May 2018 but their marriage got delayed due to professional commitments.

It is said that Rahul got angry due to the delay and flew to Malaysia and got a job there.  Priyanka had reportedly tried her best to pacify him but Rahul did not respond to her and now the actress has revealed that the engagement is broken and the marriage with him is canceled.
