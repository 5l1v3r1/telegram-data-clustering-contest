Spain's possible Euro 2020 opponents

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Euro 2020 qualification phase is very close to ending and Spain could be handed a very favourable group.

With only a few matches to be played on Tuesday plus the playoffs, qualification for Euro 2020 is close to ending, with Spain being one of the top seeds.

This situation means that Spain could have quite a nice group. The last few results will determine the rest of the pots.

For now, the situation is as follows: Spain have been drawn in Group E, they will be a host and play their first 3 matches at the San Mamés, Bilbao.

In pot 2 there are still some top teams: Croatia, Portugal, Switzerland, and Poland are in it. The only team that's destiny is still to be found out is France. If Germany draw, France will be in pot 2.

In pot 3 everything was decided on Monday. Only Turkey, Austria, Sweden, and Czech Republic can face Spain, Lastly, in pot 4 Spain can only play the winners of the playoff involving Ireland.
