A 180 million release clause to fend off Madrid and Barca

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Napoli will not sit with their arms crossed amidst the growing interest from Barcelona and Real Madrid in signing Fabiábn Ruiz. That's why the Italians are looking at offering him a 180 million euro release clause to, with a contract until 2025.

Barcelona and Real Madrid have been after the 23-year-old midfielder Fabián Ruiz for some time, whose future at Napoli seems more than difficult.

But the Napoli board will not sit with their arms crossed and have started to work to avoid his exit. For many, he is the best Spanish midfielder at the moment.

Amidst the growing interest from clubs including Madrid in signing him, the Napoli board have already sat down to speak about the possibility of extending the ex Betis player's contract.

That is why, according to 'Corriere dell Sport', Fabián, who has scored 1 goal and got 2 assists in the 15 official matches he has played this season, will receive a contract renewal offer until 2025.

An offer that will consist of being equipped to avoid his Napoli departure, extending his contract for a couple more years. And, more importantly, his salary will be increased by two million euros, with a release clause that could reach 180 million.
