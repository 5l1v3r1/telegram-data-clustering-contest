Casting company on the hunt for Scousers for a new major action feature film

Scousers of all ages and ethnicities are being asked to apply to the roles

19 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Charlotte Hadfield

A casting company is on the hunt for Scousers to take part in a new major action feature film.

The Casting Collective have issued an urgent call for men and women of all ages and ethnicities who live in Liverpool, to star as extras in the new action film.

Filming is set to start from early to mid 2020, with Scousers aged 17-90 encouraged to come forward and apply to the roles immediately.

Those who are interested in applying, must provide three passport photos of themselves- including a recent head shot, a full-length photo and another photo of their choice.

Applicants must also provide accurate measurements of themselves and will need to have their passport details and National Insurance Number handy.

The casting call reads: "We are looking for men & women of all ethnicities age 17 - 90 for filming in Liverpool on a Major Action Feature Film shooting early to mid 2020.

"We require people of all ages and all ethnicities who live in Liverpool.

"All applicants must have a valid National Insurance number, be legally allowed to work in the UK and be over 16 years old (and if 16 completed GCSEs) Good Rates of Pay."

Liverpool is a popular place for film crews , meaning extras are regularly needed.
