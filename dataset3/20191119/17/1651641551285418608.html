Detroit: Become Human coming to PC next month

19 Nov 2019, 17:19 by Ash

The wait is almost over for those who have been aching to play Detroit: Become Human on PC, as the game is set to hit the Epic Games Store on December 12th.

Detroit: Become Human’s PC launch marks the first time that Quantic Dream’s biggest three games have all been available on PC.  The game will be priced at $39.99/£29.99 and launches alongside a demo version which contains the game’s first chapter for free.
