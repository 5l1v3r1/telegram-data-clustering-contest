There Is No Misunderstanding Between Vivian Jill And I – Tracey Boakye Discloses(+Video)

19 Nov 2019, 17:21 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian talented actress, producer and  and entrepreneur Tracey Boakye has denied allegations of a feud between her and fellow actress Vivian Jill Lawrence.

The rumor started after after the two actresses unfollowed each other on Instagram shortly after Vivian Jill launched her catering business called “Jill’s Aroma”.

Rumors had it that the “mother- daughter”  relationship between the two turned sour  mainly because Tracey also owns a pub and restaurant in Kumasi.

Reacting to this in an interview with Zionfelix  on the “uncut” show, Tracey said there was no iota of truth to the rumor.

Tracey Boakye explained that  competition in business is very common and she will never soil her great relationship with the woman she calls mother for such flimsy reasons.

She tasked her fans to forget about the rumor as there is no truth to it.
