Shenmue 3 Video Shows How the Long-Awaited Sequel Stayed True to Originals

19 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Jason Guisao

It's been almost 20 years since the last Shenmue video game. After years of radio silence, a sudden Kickstarter initiative and snippets of action-packed gameplay -- as well as beautifully rendered locales -- Shenmue 3 has finally launched worldwide today.

In addition to the game's release, publisher Deep Silver uploaded a lengthy YouTube video detailing the studio's extensive development process.

The video includes appearances from prominent YouTubers, former SEGA executives and development studio members that have been working on the series since its revolutionary beginnings back in the 1990s. But Shenmue 3 is still very much in-tune with its roots. At its core, the game is still focused on realism, top-notch martial arts combat and revenge.
