Coheed & Cambria announce cruise w/ Taking Back Sunday, Torche, Culture Abuse & more

19 Nov 2019, 17:25 by Amanda Hatfield

Emo-proggers Coheed & Cambria are throwing their own cruise festival next year. The S.S. Neverender sets sail from Miami, FL to Nassau, Bahamas from October 26 through October 30, 2020, with Taking Back Sunday, The Dear Hunter, Polyphia, Thank You Scientist, Culture Abuse, Torche, Three, Coheed side projects Fire Deuce and Weerd Science, the Emo Nite DJs, and more to be announced. Coheed are promising two unique sets featuring “never-before and never-again performances,” deep cuts, and early material, as well as “interactive experiences” from Claudio Sanchez’s comic series The Amory Wars, games, cosplay, and other activities.

“We’re always trying to find ways to improve the fan experience,” Coheed founding member Travis Stever says. “Our fans like to travel, they like to meet other fans, we’re like a big family. So the idea of a cruise seemed like a way to allow a bunch of us to just go on vacation together.”

You can sign up for the presale if you’re interested, or check out the prices for different rooms.
