New Sri Lankan president to visit India for talks with Modi

19 Nov 2019, 17:07 by AFP

COLOMBO: India’s foreign minister arrived in Sri Lanka on Tuesday and announced that newly-elected president of the country, Gotabaya Rajapaksa, had accepted Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s invitation to visit New Delhi next week.

Subrahmanyam Jaishankar said he held talks with Rajapaksa in Colombo on his first full day in office after being sworn in Monday.

“President @GotabayaR (Rajapaksa) has accepted PM @narendramodi’s invitation to visit India on 29th November,” Jaishankar said on Twitter.
