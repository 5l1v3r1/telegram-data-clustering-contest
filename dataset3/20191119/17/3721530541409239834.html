Black Friday is almost here and Sony’s ready to reveal the deals that it’s giving players on PlayStation products this year. The sales include everything from PlayStation consoles themselves, to PS VR, controllers, and games.

These deals will start on Sunday, Nov. 24 and will run for a full week until Dec. 2 — Cyber Monday. According to Sony, these deals should be available at most major retailers. Here’s all the sales you can expect from Sony when the deals go live.

  • PlayStation 4 Pro — $299.99
  • PS VR Multi-Game Bundle. Includes a PS VR Headset, PS Camera, and downloads for Astro Bot Rescue Mission, Skyrim VR, Resident Evil 7: Biohazard, Everybody’s Golf VR, and PlayStation VR Worlds — $199.99
  • PS VR Blood & Truth + Everybody’s Golf Bundle. Includes PS VR Headset, PS Camera, two PS Move controllers, and downloads for Blood & Truth and Everybody's Golf VR. — $249.99

PlayStation Accessories

  • PS Gold Headset — $69.99
  • Dualshock 4 wireless controller — $39.99
  • PlayStation Plus yearly subscription — 25 percent off
