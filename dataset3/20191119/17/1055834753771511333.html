The Game Awards 2019 – All Nominees Revealed

19 Nov 2019, 17:39 by My Nintendo News Admin

Today is the day when The Game Awards 2019 nominees have been revealed and it’s an interesting list. There’s a lot of Death Stranding nominations and a distinct lack of Fire Emblem: Three Houses. Anyway, here’s the nominees for each individual categories and the results will be published on the 12th December which is when the award ceremony takes place. Vote here.
