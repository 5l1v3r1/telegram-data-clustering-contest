'Released Chris Lynn so that we could bid for you': KKR CEO to Yuvraj Singh

19 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Gaurav Gupta

Highlights

  • Yuvraj had termed KKR's decision to release Lynn a "bad call"
  • Yuvraj also joked that he might send a message to KKR co-owner Shah Rukh Khan regarding that
  • Lynn recently smashed 91 off 30 balls while playing for the Maratha Arabians in the Abu Dhabi T10 league

MUMBAI: Kolkata Knight Riders CEO Venky Mysore on Tuesday came up with a cheeky response to Yuvraj Singh's criticism of the franchise releasing Australia's explosive opening batsman Chris Lynn ahead of IPL 2020 auctions.

Mysore said that KKR released Chris Lynn so that they could bid for him in the IPL 2020 auctions. "@YUVSTRONG12 we released @lynny50 so that we could bid for you! ?? Love and respect for both of you champions! #IPL2020 #KKR #Legends #Sixhitters @KKRiders," Mysore said on twitter.
