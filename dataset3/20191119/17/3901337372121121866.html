Cavani Comments On Messi Spat

19 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Kurt Buckerfield

Following his on-field altercation with Argentina superstar Lionel Messi, which reportedly carried on in the tunnel with Luis Suarez having to separate them, Uruguay striker Edinson Cavani has commented on his spat with the FC Barcelona talisman.

Click through the gallery above to see some images of Cavani and Messi exchanging some heated words on Monday night.

The Paris Saint-Germain striker and Blaugrana forward went at one another late in the first half of the 2-2 draw between Argentina and Uruguay on Monday, after Messi was the recipient of a harsh tackle outside the Uruguay box.

Reports that followed suggested that, in the heat of their argument, Cavani challenged Messi to a fight, to which the Albiceleste captain responded by accepting his provocation.

Suarez is believed to have separated the pair as they continued their disagreement in the tunnel at half-time, but the PSG attacker was quick to play down the incident after the match when asked about his clash with the five-time Ballon d'Or winner.
