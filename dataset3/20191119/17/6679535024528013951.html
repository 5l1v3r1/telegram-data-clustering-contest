Two police aspirants found dead in Vikarabad

Both were depressed at not having qualified in the recruitment test held recently

19 Nov 2019, 17:33 by Staff Reporter

Two youngsters who didn’t qualify in the recently held police recruitment test were found dead at their respective houses in Vikarabad district on Tuesday.

D. Rohit (24) committed suicide by hanging himself from a ceiling fan at his residence in Rasanam village in Yalal mandal of Vikarabad district on Monday. He was an engineering graduate and gave the police constable recruitment test.

“Since the results were announced, he was depressed and resorted to the extreme act on Monday around 6 a.m.,” Yalal police said, adding that the victim did not leave any note.

In another incident that took place in Bantwaram village of Vikarabad, Alipuram Kumar (25) hung himself from a ceiling fan and ended his life, as he did not clear the constable test. “He was disqualified in the exam with the difference of two marks,” Bantwaram police said.

The victim resorted to the extreme step between 2 p.m. and 6 p.m. on Monday when he was alone in the house. “His father, Narsimhulu, returned from work around 6 p.m. and saw his son in that shocking state,” police said.
