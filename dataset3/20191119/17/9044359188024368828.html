Andrea Torres has a special birthday message for Derek Ramsay's mom

19 Nov 2019, 17:34

Today is the birthday of Derek Ramsay's mother Remedios and none other than Derek's girlfriend, Andrea Torres, has a special greeting for her.

Posting a photo of Mommy Remedios from her birthday celebration, Andrea said she "will never get tired of reminding you how special you are to me. Birthday or not."

This isn't the first time Andrea shared with the public her admiration for her boyfriend's mother.

A few weeks ago, she posted a photo of the two of them and said she was "grateful for you."

In a previous interview with PEP.ph, Derek revealed that Remedios approves of Andrea.

"Mommy likes her. It's the first time na nagpakilala ako ng girl sa mom ko na my mom felt positive," he said then.

By the way, Derek also greeted his mother a very happy birthday on Instagram.

"[M]ama I love you so much! You are an amazing woman and the best mother any child could have. Thank you for taking care of our family and showering us with so much love. Happy happy birthday," he said.

Andrea and Derek were co-stars in GMA 7's "The Better Woman" series, the latter's first project as a Kapuso.
