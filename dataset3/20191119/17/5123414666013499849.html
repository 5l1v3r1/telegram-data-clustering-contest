Lights Out Premiere: Viktor Kalima - Protruder [Tripalium Corp]

Urgent, funky, and menacing blend of techno and EBM

19 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Kane

Today, Lights Out brings you an exclusive premiere from taste-making French label Tripalium Corp, in the form of Viktor Kalima's 'Protruder'. The title track of his upcoming 6-track EP as part of the labels Digital Mutant Series, Protruder is an urgent, funky, and menacing blend of techno and EBM, with a dash of acid for good measure. What really stands out about this track is the amount of space between the elements that allows each to shine brightly. Tripalium has a knack for finding talent that is hard to define by a single genre, Viktor Kalima being a perfect example. 'Prodtruder' by Viktor Kalima will be released on November 21st via Tripalium Corp.
