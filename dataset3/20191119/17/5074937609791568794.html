Minister inaugurates review Committee of PWDs Act

19 Nov 2019, 17:17

Accra, Nov 19, GNA - Madam Cynthia Mamle Morrison, Minister for Gender, Children and Social Protection has inaugurated the Technical Committee to review the Persons with Disability Act, (Act 715) and prepare an accompanying Legislative Instrument for submission to Parliament.

The Committee will also prepare the Legislative Instrument to accompany the Act, Work together with Consultants, consider the proposed amendment and draft Legislative Instrument submitted by organisations of persons with disabilities, develop a road map for the review of the Act and prepare the draft Legislative Instrument.

The Technical Committee was constituted by the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection in 2018; amidst demands from organisations of persons with disabilities and civil society organisations to amend the Act, 2006 (Act 715) to bring it in tune with international standard law.

The  Act, 2006 (Act 715) was passed into law by Parliament in 2006 and  Ghana ratified the United Nations Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disability (UN CRPD) in 2012 and submitted its initial report to the UN CRPD Committee in June 2018.

Organisations of persons with disabilities, spearheaded by Ghana Federation of Disability Organisations undertook gap analysis of  the  Persons with Disability Act, 2006 (Act 715).  

In reflection that disability is a cross-cutting issue, the Technical Committee was   constituted from representatives from the Ministry of Gender Children and Social Protection, Ministry of Local Government and Rural Development, Department of Social Welfare, Ministry of Health, Ghana Health Service, Ministry of Justice and Attorney – General, Drafting Unit and Attorney- General Department. Others were, the Special Education Division – Ghana Education Service, Commission on Human Rights and Administrative Justice, Judicial Service, Ministry of Justice and Attorney General Department, Ghana Federation Disability, the Academia – University of Ghana, Legon, Centre for Disability and Rehabilitation Studies.

The rest were; Centre for Employment of Persons with Disability, Special Mothers Project, Ghana Health, Child Health- Paediatric Neuro- Disability (Dr. Brew), Ghana Mental Health Authority, University of Ghana, Department of Audiology - Speech and language Therapy

The Chief Director of the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection Dr. Afisah Zakariah welcomed the Team and the participants for responding favourably to the call, while  Freda Prempeh, Deputy Minister of the Ministry stated that the Technical Committee were strategically and carefully selected to steer the  affairs of the committee.  

The Chairman of the Technical Committee, Dr Samuel Kaba in his remarks expressed his appreciation for the opportunity to give his time in the interest of persons with disabilities.

The inauguration and the maiden meeting of the Technical Committee was funded by the Ghana Federation of Disability Organisations, while the National Council on Persons with   Disability under the Ministry of Gender, Children  and  Social Protection coordinated and facilitated the processes towards the review.
