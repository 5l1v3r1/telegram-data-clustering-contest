Troubling Details Emerge From Arrest Of NFL RB Mark Walton

19 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Matt Hladik

Earlier today, the Miami Dolphins released running back Mark Walton due to an undisclosed “police matter.” As it turns out, the alleged matter is serious.

According to media reports, Walton was arrested on Tuesday and charged with aggravated battery of a pregnant person. The charge stems from an incident with a woman who is apparently five weeks pregnant with Walton’s child.

Walton is accused of pushing the woman into a wall and punching her in the face and head.
