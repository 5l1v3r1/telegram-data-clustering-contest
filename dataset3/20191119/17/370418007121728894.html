If Mötley Crüe Can Get Back Together, Then Anything Is Possible

Is the Mötley Crüe reunion just a sign of things to come?

19 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Luke Morton

They said it wouldn’t happen. They said it shouldn’t happen. They said it legally couldn’t happen. And yet, here we are, one day removed from the Mötley Crüe reunion and to be honest, nobody knows what to believe any more. Down is white, up is black, and Mick Mars is actually going to play again. The world of rock is in disarray and nothing makes sense.

In the space of the past three weeks, reunions have been coming thick and fast. Creeper? Check. My Chemical Romance? You betcha. Rage Against The Machine? Oh yes! But none of these bands said they wouldn’t get back together: in fact, it was always kind of assumed both Creeper and My Chem would return in some form, but Crüe actually signed a cessation of touring contract to stop any cashing in on (yet another) comeback run.

Until they blew it up. Literally. Seriously, there’s a video to prove it.
