Dubai’s Alici to host season opening soirée

Expect delicious canapés at this swish Italian restaurant

19 Nov 2019, 17:12 by Reema Rahman

If you’re looking for something to do this weekend in Dubai that combines both food and fun, look no further.

Chic Italian seafood spot, Alici is hosting a season opening soirée on Friday (November 22) and it's time to clear your diary.

Alici, which means anchovy, is the sister restaurant of Time Out Dubai's Best Restaurant 2019 Il Borro Tuscan Bistro.

It’s Dhs350 a ticket – which can be purchased here – and includes canapés featuring the best ingredients from the South of Italy and premium grape and beverages from across the country.

The dress code is “captivatingly Capri” to don’t feel shy about donning your most stylish and sophisticated attire.
