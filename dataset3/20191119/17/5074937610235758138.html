Seventy-eight companies vie for NiBS Ghana Innovation Awards

19 Nov 2019, 17:02

Accra, Nov 19, GNA - About 78 companies and individuals have been shortlisted for the maiden edition of the Nobel International Business School (NiBS) Ghana Innovation Awards.

The event dubbed "the Ultimate Industry Awards" is slated for November 22, at Kempinski Hotel, according to a statement signed by Latif Abubakar, the event Organizer, and copied to the Ghana News Agency in Accra.

The Ultimate Industry Awards is an annual awards programme that recognizes private and public sector organizations, teams, and individuals that demonstrate the core values of innovation in all its dimensions.

It provides opportunity for high-performing individuals, groups, and organizations that develop innovative products, services, business models, and implement creative organizational initiatives to be acknowledged and celebrated for their achievements.

The ultimate objective of the programme is to build a national initiative for innovation through national recognition, dialogue, and knowledge building and sharing.

The special Guest of Honour is Professor Kwaku Atuahene-Gima, scholar in Innovation and Marketing Management.

The various organizations shortlisted include: Inlaks Limited, EAI Information Systems, Nutrifoods Ghana Limited, Smocky World Limited, TCL Electronics Ghana Limited, Nationwide Medical Insurance Limited, Eusbett Hotel, GNPC Foundation, Nyonkopa Cocoa Buying Limited, Agriaccess Barclays Bank of Ghana, Kingdom Exim Ghana Limited, and Fueltrade Limited.
