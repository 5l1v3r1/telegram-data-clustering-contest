AccuWeather: Clouds break, temps turn milder

19 Nov 2019, 17:47 by WABC

NEW YORK (WABC) -- Clouds will give way to some sun on Tuesday and temperatures will turn milder in the afternoon.

This, after some precipitation moved across the region overnight. Northern parts of the viewing area, including Dutchess County, saw up to 4 inches of snow.

After dawn, traffic cameras along Interstate 84 showed snow covering grassy medians in towns including Fishkill, East fishkill, Lake Carmel and Brewster.
