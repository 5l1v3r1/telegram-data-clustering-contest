Arriva offering free bus travel to anyone taking part in Liverpool Santa Dash 2019

Red and blue Santas will descend on the city centre for the Liverpool Santa Dash

19 Nov 2019, 11:02 by Elle May Rice

Santa Dash-ers can rest easy about transport as Arriva is getting in the Christmas spirit by offering free bus travel once again.

This Sunday, December 1 , Arriva is offering free bus travel to all those taking part in the festive 5k.

The city centre will soon be taken over by red and blue Santas as the annual run takes place - but anyone travelling to and from the Santa Dash can get free bus travel.

To claim the complimentary travel service, passengers boarding the bus must have their Santa suit and race number on show - or their finisher’s medal after the event.

Lisa Pearson, head of marketing for Arriva North West and Wales, said: “The Santa Dash is a great event that really unites the people of Liverpool for a day of festive fun, which is why we are supporting the city by offering free travel to all those taking part.

“The team here at Arriva will also be getting stuck into this year’s action, so we’re looking forward to seeing lots of red and blue Santas on board our buses and throughout the city on Sunday.”

The free service, which will run across bus services throughout Merseyside, starts and ends on December 1.
