Nada Surf announce new album, share “Something I Should Do”

19 Nov 2019, 11:14 by Andrew Sacher

Indie/alt rock vets Nada Surf recently announced a 2020 tour and revealed that their ninth album was in the works, and now that album has been announced. It’s called Never Not Together and due February 7 via Barsuk (pre-order). The first single is “Something I Should Do,” a synth-fueled rocker with a spoken word section that has serious “Popular” vibes. As you’d probably expect from this consistently reliable band, it’s catchy and instantly likable. You can check out its animated video (made by Jonny Sanders) below. Artwork and tracklist below too.

As mentioned, Nada Surf’s tour includes two NYC shows: January 31 at Music Hall of Williamsburg with Aaron Lee Tasjan (tickets) and February 2 at Bowery Ballroom with Bwahaha (aka Arc Iris + Guster singer Ryan Miller) (tickets).

Their tour also includes shows with John Vanderslice, The Lees of Memory (ex-Superdrag), and more. All dates are listed below.
