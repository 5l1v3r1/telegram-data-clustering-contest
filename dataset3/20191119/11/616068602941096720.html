Hit and run caught on cam

19 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Chris Campbell / Burnaby Now

A Burnaby driver slammed into a parked SUV and didn't even stop to check the damage.

The hit and run was caught on security camera from across the street in the Duthie/Curtis area on Sunday, about 11:20 a.m.

The older compact car slams into the front of the SUV, and the driver keeps going without a moment's hesitation.

The owner of the crunched SUV shared the video with Glacier Media in hopes someone might recognize the offending vehicle.

The neighbour who captured the video ran outside to get a licence number, but the white male driving was already speeding off.
