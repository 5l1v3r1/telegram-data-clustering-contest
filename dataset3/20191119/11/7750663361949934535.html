Tuesday's La Liga transfer talk news roundup: Lionel Messi, Neymar, Fabian Ruiz

Sports Mole rounds up all of the latest transfer news in La Liga on Tuesday, November 19.

Tuesday morning's headlines:

Manchester United midfielder Paul Pogba prefers Juventus over Real Madrid?
Manchester United midfielder Paul Pogba reportedly prefers the idea of moving to Juventus next summer as opposed to joining Real Madrid. Read more.

Manchester City 'to fight Real Madrid, Barcelona for Fabian Ruiz'

Barcelona scout Dinamo Zagreb forward Bruno Petkovic?

Barcelona target Willian, Fabian Ruiz?

Barcelona to move for Fiorentina defender Nikola Milenkovic in January?

Barcelona target Neymar 'to reject new Paris Saint-Germain contract'
