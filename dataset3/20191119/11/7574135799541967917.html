Austin Terror Fest is now Oblivion Access; Swans, Converge & more on 2020 lineup

19 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Andrew Sacher

The festival formerly known as Austin Terror Fest has rebranded and is now known as Oblivion Access (named after Lil Ugly Mane’s 2015 album). The 2020 edition of the fest goes down June 5-7 at various Austin venues, including Mohawk, Barracuda, and Empire Control Room & Garage, and the initial lineup is short but already stacked.

The artist responsible for the festival’s new namesake, Lil Ugly Mane, is playing, as are Swans, Converge, Anna von Hausswolff, Despise You, True Widow, and Primitive Man.

The full lineup will be revealed in January. Three-day passes are on sale now, and single-day passes will go on sale “at a later time closer to the festival dates.” Stay tuned.
