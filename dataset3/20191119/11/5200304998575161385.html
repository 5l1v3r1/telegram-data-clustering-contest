Overnights: Star Plus shoots back to top on Monday in UK

19 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Raj Baddhan

A strong performance by ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ on Monday, gave Star Plus a huge surge in UK ratings, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Plus delivered a share of 0.15%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television and ZEE Cinema with 0.10%, PTC Punjabi with 0.08%, Star Bharat and New Vision TV with 0.05%, ZEE TV, Hum Masala, Colors and Geo TV with 0.04%, Colors Rishtey, Sony SAB TV and Dunya News with 0.03%, Hum TV, B4U Music, BritAsia TV and Sony MAX with 0.02%.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ impressed with 94,000 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 54,500 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ with 51,700 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 46,000 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 41,500 viewers, ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 32,300 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 20,600 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ at 22:00 with 11,200 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 9,700 viewers and ‘Namah’ at 18:30 with 3,600 viewers.

On Sony TV, ‘Crime Patrol’ led with 33,800 viewers at 23:00, followed by ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 with 33,500 viewers, ‘Mere Sai’ at 19:00 with 31,500 viewers and ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 28,800 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ broke its own record with 50,700 viewers at 18:30, followed by ‘Shoorveer Sisters’ at 17:00 with 26,000 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Daasi’ was watched by 16,600 viewers at 20:00.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ dropped to 11,400 viewers at 21:30.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
