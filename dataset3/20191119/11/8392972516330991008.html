Coronation Street viewers divided over Adam Barlow's new haircut

Adam has drastically changed his new hair!

19 Nov 2019, 11:40 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Sam Robertson, aka Adam Barlow, debuted his new haircut in last night's episodes of Coronation Street.

But fans have been left divided over the new do, with some comparing him to a brush, but others thrilled he's finally got rid of his longer locks.

Sam took a break from the soap earlier this year and when Adam returned to the cobbles he was sporting long hair.
