Victoria-Salt Spring ferry running late; possible busy travel day on B.C. Ferries to Vancouver

19 Nov 2019, 11:01

The ferry that runs between Swartz Bay and Fulford Harbour on Salt Spring Island is running late Tuesday.

B.C. Ferries vessel the Queen of Cumberland is operating 36 minutes behind schedule because it took extra time to load vehicles.

B.C. Ferries is also warning travellers that today could be a busy travel day on ferries going between Vancouver Island and Vancouver due to WE DAY, a popular event being held at Rogers Arena.
