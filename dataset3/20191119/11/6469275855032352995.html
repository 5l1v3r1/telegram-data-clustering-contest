Preston gym-goers work up a sweat to raise life-changing funds for Lostock Hall & Bamber Bridge Guide Dogs

19 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Laura Longworth

Staff and members of a Preston gym have cycled their way to fund-raising success in aid of Lostock Hall & Bamber Bridge Guide Dog Group.

Active Nation Gym Preston marked Guide Dog month in October with both a sponsored spin and dog walk to raise money for the charity.
