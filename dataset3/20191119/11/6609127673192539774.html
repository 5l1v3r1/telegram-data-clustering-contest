BRIEF BOOST

Celtic handed additional ticket allocation for Rangers Betfred Cup final clash

19 Nov 2019, 11:33 by David Fowler

CELTIC have been handed an additional allocation of tickets for the Betfred Cup final against Rangers at Hampden.

The Hoops have announced an extremely limited number of briefs are available to season ticket holders who meet the requirements.

A statement on the club website read: "Celtic have received an extremely limited additional allocation for the League Cup final on Sunday, December 8 at the National Stadium.

"The deadline for the original sale was 5pm yesterday (Monday, November 18).

"Those who were successful in original sale and did not purchase by the initial deadline are no longer eligible.

"Tickets for this match have been balloted amongst those STH who meet the following criteria:-

"STH on the HCTS whose payment was taken by Direct Debit for home Cup matches v Dunfermline (August 17, 2019), Partick Thistle (September 25, 2019) and who purchased a ticket under their own client ref for the League Cup semi-final v Hibs (November 2, 2019) - and who were not successful in the original sale of tickets."

"The deadline to purchase your ticket is 3pm this Saturday (November 23)."
