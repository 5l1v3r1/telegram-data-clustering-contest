Watch Kanye West’s Sunday Service With Joel Osteen

Including live performances off ‘Jesus Is King.’

19 Nov 2019, 11:14 by Mallory Chin

Over the weekend, Kanye West brought his Sunday Service to pastor Joel Osteen‘s Lakewood Church in Houston, Texas marking the multi-hyphenate’s largest audience to date.

During West’s appearance, the artist used some of his time on stage to defend the megachurch head who has come under fire in recent years for his prosperity gospel teachings and for not making his place of worship available for Hurricane Harvey victims. “There’s a lot of people in the Christian community that try to give Joel a hard time because when you turn on the radio, he just keeps telling you how good God is,” Kanye said. “That’s good, I love that. When you got Kanye defending you, you made it,” Osteen replied the endorsement.
