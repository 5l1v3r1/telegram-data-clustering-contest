Samsung Galaxy A-series leaks: A51 battery size, Galaxy A11 to run Android 10

Samsung Galaxy A51 is leaked again revealing its battery capacity and the Galaxy A11 is reported to run Android 10 out of the box.

19 Nov 2019, 11:32 by Tech Desk

Samsung is working on the new generation of Galaxy A-series devices for the year 2020 and the leaks for the upcoming devices have started to pile up already. The upcoming Galaxy A51 has been leaked on several occasions and the latest leak reveals its battery capacity. The Galaxy A11 has also been leaked to debut with model number SM-A115.

Sammobile reports that the Galaxy A11 will be a budget smartphone that will run Android 10 out-of-the-box and have 32GB of internal storage. It also says that the successor to the Galaxy A10 device will be made primarily available in Asia and Africa.
