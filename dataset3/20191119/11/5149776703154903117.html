More than 30 jobs available as CitySprint prepares for Christmas in Nottingham

CitySprint made more than 600,000 deliveries between the end of October and New Year in 2018

19 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Matthew Bunn

More than 30 temporary jobs are up for grabs in Nottingham as same-day distribution firm CitySprint gears up for the busy Christmas period.

The company says it has a "specific focus" on people in the city as it looks to boost the number of van couriers.

The peak season for deliveries normally runs from the end of October until the New Year, with CitySprint making more than 600,000 deliveries during that period last year.

Paul Gisbourne, chief operating officer at CitySprint, said: “With just 36 days to go until Christmas, businesses are gearing up for the busiest time of the year.

"Bolstering fleet numbers will allow us to continue to deliver a first-class service and ensures we stay ahead of our competitors.

"We know that the Christmas season brings increased pressure, demand and competition for our customers, which is why we are committed to going the extra mile at a time when it matters most.”

People interested in applying for more of the temporary courier jobs can find more information on CitySprint's website.
