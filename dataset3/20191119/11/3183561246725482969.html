Runaways' New Trailer Comes With The Sad News That Season 3 Will Be Its Last

19 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Cheryl Eddy

After Marvel announced the end of Freeform series Cloak & Dagger, it felt like only a matter of time before another of its shows about magical teens—Hulu’s Runaways—suffered the same fate. Today, the hammer came down, but at least the news was accompanied by a fantastic trailer for the show’s third and final season.

That much-anticipated crossover with Cloak & Dagger is front and centre in the new trailer, as well as new glimpses of the season’s big bad, Morgan le Fay, played with what looks to be great relish by Elizabeth Hurley.
