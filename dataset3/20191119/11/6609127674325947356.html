Suspect arrested over alleged firearms offences in Ayrshire after search of property

19 Nov 2019, 11:56 by Blair Meikle

A MAN has been arrested over alleged firearms offences in Ayrshire.

The 41-year-old was nicked after officers carried out a search of a property in Muirkirk last Thursday.

He was released on an undertaking to appear at Ayr Sheriff Court on Monday December 9.

Cops say there is no threat to the public.

A spokesperson said: "On Thursday, 14 November, 2019, acting on intelligence, Police Scotland officers, along with the National Crime Agency under the Organised Crime Partnership, carried out a search of a property in Muirkirk.

"Following a detailed search, a 41-year-old male was arrested on suspicion of a variety of firearms offences. He was released on an undertaking to appear at Ayr Sheriff Court on Monday, 9 December, 2019.

"There is no ongoing threat to the community or wider community impact."
