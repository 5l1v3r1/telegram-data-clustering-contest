How many devices can you use with Disney+ at once?

19 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Steve Wright

Alongside a list of supported devices.

Disney+ is now available in Australia, and we’ve got news on how many devices you can use at one time to stream content alongside a listing of compatible devices.

First, Disney has confirmed up to four different devices can stream Disney+ at the same time. A subscription can have up to seven profiles, though you obviously can’t use all seven at once.

Moreover, compatible devices include the following:

  • Amazon Fire TV
  • Android TV
  • AppleTV
  • Chromecast
  • LG TVs
  • Samsung
  • Chrome OS
  • MacOS
  • Windows PC
  • Android Phones & Tablets
  • iPhone & iPad
  • PS4
  • Xbox One

Disney+ is available in Australia from today, priced at $8.99 AUD per month or $89.99 AUD per year.
