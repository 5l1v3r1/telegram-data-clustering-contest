S Korean Fishing Boat Catches Fire, Leaving 1 Person Killed, 11 More Missing - Coast Guard

SEOUL (Sputnik) - A fire on a South Korean fishing boat near Jeju Island resulted in one person being killed, and 11 more still missing, the local Coast Guard said on Tuesday.

19 Nov 2019, 11:40

The blaze broke out on the 29-tonne vessel with 12 people aboard at about 22:09 GMT. Six of those were South Korean nationals, while the others were citizens of Vietnam, the Coast Guard said.
