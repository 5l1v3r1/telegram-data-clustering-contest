Grey Cup in Cowtown

19 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Rob Gibson

The Grey Cup has arrived in Calgary ahead of this weekend's championship game.

The Calgary Grey Cup Committee is a volunteer group dedicated to sharing the excitement surrounding the big game, each and every year with cities across Canada. This years game is in Calgary and the Grey Cup Festival presented by Shaw will feature the Winnipeg Blue Bombers and the Hamilton Tiger Cats.

The iconic emblem of Canadian football was piped into the Calgary Convention Centre Tuesday morning in preparation for Sunday's 107th Grey Cup game.
