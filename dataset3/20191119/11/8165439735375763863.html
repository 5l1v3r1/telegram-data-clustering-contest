Lizzo Sued By Delivery Person She Accused Of Stealing Her Food

19 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Tuathla Lucey

Lizzo is being sued by a delivery driver after the singer tweeted about her, saying that she stole her food.

The tweet in question included the driver's name and a picture of her face.
