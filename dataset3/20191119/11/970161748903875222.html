Kate Middleton 'to co-star with Mary Berry for Christmas cooking special'

19 Nov 2019, 11:06

Mary Berry’s Christmas special is set to get the Royal treatment as she teams up with Kate, Duchess of Cambridge, according to reports.

The princess became pals with the 84-year-old cookery legend in 2013 and is now, according to The Mirror, heading to the kitchen with her friend to serve up some festive treats.

In a one-off episode set to air next month in time for the holidays, the show will be named the Mary Berry Royal Christmas Show, and will reportedly air on the BBC.

‘Between them they will bring seasonal goodwill to their guests and to the millions of viewers watching,’ a source told the paper. ‘Expect a properly uplifting Christmas treat.’

The BBC declined to comment when approached by Metro.co.uk. 

Mary has become a beloved household name thanks to her time in the Great British Bake Off – with the Duchess reportedly becoming a fan of her cookbooks.
