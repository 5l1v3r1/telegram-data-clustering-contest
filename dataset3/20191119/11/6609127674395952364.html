'Feel at home'

Watch ex-Celtic star Tierney hold green smoke bomb before revealing Arsenal-Hoops connection that’s helped him settle

19 Nov 2019, 11:38 by Gary Taylor

KIERAN TIERNEY showed Celtic is always on his mind as he let off a green smoke bomb before an interview where he revealed an Arsenal/Hoops connection has helped him settle in London.

The Scotland international swapped the Scottish Premiership for the EPL in a £25million summer move.

KT was a fans' favourite at his boyhood heroes and was a key man in the their treble treble success.

The left-back has since traded life in the family home to living himself in London.

He admits the transition has been difficult but he's been made to feel at home by the Scottish staff on the Arsenal books - who share his love for Celtic.

And says he has created bonds with the likes of Hector Bellerin, Dino Mavropanos, Callum Chambers and Rob Holding.
