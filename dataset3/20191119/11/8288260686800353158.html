'Amazing! - The FIFA 20 news Aston Villa have been waiting for - and fans are loving it

Aston Villa latest news | Now Dean Smith's side are back in the Premier League they are a key component of EA Sports' new FIFA 20 game as the top flight takes centre stage

19 Nov 2019, 11:41 by Nathan Bliss, Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans with a love of the popular EA Sports gaming franchise FIFA will be keen to install the latest update to this year's iteration.

The game's publishers, Electronic Arts, have released another update to their latest edition today - and this one includes lifelike face scans of Aston Villa's players.

Jack Grealish is no longer a generic face - his is lifelike now along with fellow players John McGinn , Jota, Anwar El Ghazi, Wesley and Douglas Luiz.

To use these faces in the popular career mode, though, fans will need to install the update and then start a career - they won't appear in-game in careers already in progress.

 

The update is due onto the PC version today, and according to popular Twitter account @FIFACareerGems it is hoped that the update will make its way onto consoles within the week.
