After Ananya Panday, Shanaya Kapoor to make her Le Bal debut?

19 Nov 2019, 11:40 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

It looks like another star-kid is getting ready to make her debut at the prestigious Le Bal Des Debutantes' event in Paris. According to the latest reports doing the rounds, Shanaya Kapoor, who is rumoured to be making her Bollywood debut soon, is prepping up for her big appearance at the event that sees an electric mix of high society boys and girls putting their stylish foot forward for their formal introduction into society.

Over the years, star-kids like Navya Naveli Nanda, Isha Ambani, Princess Shaiyra Devi of Kapurthala, Princess Adishree Singh of Jammu and Kashmir, and even Bollywood actress Ananya Panday have all been presented at the ball.

This year, Shanaya will be making her strut down the iconic staircase at the event and waltzing the night away with one of the dashing young men at the event. Not new to the limelight, the starlet, who is currently pursuing her Bollywood dreams by assisting on the sets, has reportedly picked a stunning red creating for the big night.

Keeping in mind that Ananya and Shanaya are besties, we wonder what advise she has passed on.
