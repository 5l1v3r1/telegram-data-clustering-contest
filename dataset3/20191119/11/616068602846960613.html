Snapshot of trespasser

19 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Sarita Patel

Another day, another prowler caught on security camera.

A Castanet reader sent in this photo captured on the weekend of an unwanted person approaching their home.

The image was taken just before 7 p.m. of a male near Rose Avenue and Aberdeen Street, in the Kelowna General Hospital area.

The person came to the reader's front door and found it locked, then proceeded to the deck to check the back door.

The resident went outside to see if the prowler was still around, and ended up finding a drug baggy, a lighter and a piece of foil with burnt residue on it.
