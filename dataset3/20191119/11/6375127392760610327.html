Scam leader, fake lawyer jailed

19 Nov 2019, 11:02 by Alfred P. Dalizon

TWO con artists: one a bogus lawyer and the other the leader of an investment scam syndicate were arrested by agents of the Philippine National Police Criminal Investigation and Detection Group in separate anti-crime operations in the country last Monday, a report to PNP-CIDG director, Brigadier General Joel Napoleon M. Coronel said.

One of the suspects identified as Juvern Reyes Garcia was arrested for masquerading as a lawyer-Certified Public Accountant in Davao City, said CIDG Region 11 chief, Colonel Edilberto Leonardo.

According to the official, Garcia was arrested during an entrapment operation in Kunisawa Apartment located in Durian Street, Sitio San Roque, Daliao, Davao City conducted by members of the CIDG Davao Field Unit in coordination with the Toril Police. Station 8:45 a.m. Monday.

He said that the suspect was placed under arrest shortly after he received the marked money from a female complainant as payment for the processing of the petition for recognition of the divorce papers of the victim. Leonardo said that suspected drugs were also recovered from the suspect.

Leonardo said that it turned out that the suspect pretended to be a CPA-lawyer who can help the complainant process her petition. Recovered from the suspect were P42,000 in marked bills and a fake Professional Regulations Commission ID.

Now detained at the Toril police lock-up facility, the suspect will be facing charges for violations of Article 315 (Estafa by False Pretense), Article 172 (Falsification of Public Document), and Republic Act 9165 or the Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002.

On the other hand, Lourdes Baarde, tagged as the leader of a ‘Forex Investment Group’ operating in Western Visayas was arrested by members of the CIDG Capiz office and the Roxas City Police Station last Monday afternoon along Kilometer 1 in Roxas City, said CIDG Region 6 chief, Col. Benliner Capili.

Capili described Baarde as the ‘Queen’ of the forex investment syndicate.

The official said that the suspect was arrested on the basis of a warrant of arrest for three counts of estafa issued by Judge Manuel Barrios of the Roxas City Regional Trial Court Branch 4.
