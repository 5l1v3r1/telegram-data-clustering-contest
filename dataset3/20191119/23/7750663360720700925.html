Juventus 'to offer two players in Paul Pogba deal'

Juventus are reportedly prepared to include two players in any deal for Manchester United midfielder Paul Pogba.

19 Nov 2019, 23:47 by Darren Plant

Since the summer, Juventus and Real Madrid have allegedly been keen to secure the signature of the Frenchman, who is keen to leave Old Trafford.

United have frequently maintained that the 26-year-old, who effectively has just over two-and-a-half years left on his contract, remains not for sale.

According to Tuttosport, Juventus may attempt to make the breakthrough by including each of Emre Can and Mario Mandzukic in any proposal put forward to the Premier League giants.

After finding themselves down the pecking order under Maurizio Sarri, the fringe duo have each been linked with a switch to the north-west of England in recent months.
