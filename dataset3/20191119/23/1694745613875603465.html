When your newborn baby isn't in the in the stroller but you're out and about, it's likely you'll want to place them in a baby carrier or slings. They're practically baby essentials and a great way to ensure your precious bundle is safe and warm, plus it leaves you hands-free. So whether you want a baby carrier for back pain or a thin sling you can toss into a diaper bag and grab when you need it, here are eight of our favorites.

Shopping for other newborn essentials? Also check out our lists of the best baby gear, strollers, diaper bags, and other baby products.

Baby K'tan Original Cotton Wrap

This buckle-free, double-looped Baby K'tan Cotton Wrap ($50) will keep your little one snug as a bug no matter where you go. It comes in four colors, six sizes, and slips on easily.

Solly Baby Natural & Grey Stripe

Made from 100 percent Lenzing Modal, this Solly Baby Natural & Grey Stripe Wrap ($65) is both functional and eco-friendly. There are videos online to show you how to wrap the sling. It takes a bit of practice, but once you get the hang of it you'll love how snugly your little one fits inside.

MiaMily Hipster Plus

The MiaMily Hipster Plus ($126, originally $129) not only holds many safety awards but it has a built-in seat. This reduces the weight for the parent and maintain correct hip positioning for the baby. Plus it has pockets galore and you can use it six different ways.
