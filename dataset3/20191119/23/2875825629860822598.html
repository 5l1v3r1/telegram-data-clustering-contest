Mauricio Pochettino's assistant manager sends heartfelt message to Tottenham fans after axe

Tottenham axed Mauricio Pochettino - along with assistant Jesus Perez and coaches Miguel D'Agostino and Antoni Jimenez - on Tuesday night

19 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Irishmirror.ie

Mauricio Pochettino's assistant Jesus Perez has paid tribute to Tottenham supporters - just hours after his heartbreaking sacking.

Spurs have axed Pochettino - along with right-hand man Perez and coaches Miguel D'Agostino and Antoni Jimenez - less than six months after he took the club to the Champions League final.

The Argentine boss has ultimately paid the price for a poor run of domestic form in 2019, bringing an end to a five-and-a-half-year spell at the club.

Pochettino joined from Southampton in 2014 and delivered four top-four finishes across his five full seasons in charge - but leaves with Spurs 14th in the Premier League and having won only three of their opening league games.
