UFC News: Chris Weidman sends a message following Ben Askren's retirement

19 Nov 2019, 23:23 by Lennard Surrao

Ben Askren shocked the MMA world yesterday when he announced his retirement from the sport during his appearance on Ariel Helwani's MMA Show.

Reactions to his unforeseen retirement poured in from all quarters of the MMA fraternity and Chris Weidman was one of the many fighters who took to Twitter to show some love to Askren.

The former UFC Middleweight Champion revealed Askren had a big influence on his career as they both come from grappling backgrounds.

Askren has been an inspiration to Weidman, who has learned a lot from the former Bellator Champion's unique approach to fighting.

Weidman also reminisced his high school days when he wrestled Askren.

Here's what Weidman wrote in his tweet:

“Congrats @Benaskren on an amazing career! You were a huge inspiration to me and many more. From wrestling you in high school when I was very young to watching you throughout your wrestling and MMA careers.I learned a lot from your unique techniques as well as your mindset #funky”.
