Forward Madison giving fans chance to name new flamingo at Henry Vilas Zoo

19 Nov 2019, 23:36 by Brandon Arbuckle

MADISON, Wis. - The Forward Madison FC soccer team is giving fans the chance to name one of the new flamingos at Henry Vilas Zoo.

According to a news release from Tuesday, the name will be decided through an online contest.

The contest comes after the zoo announced it will be adding five flamingos and a bison this winter. The soccer team's Twitter page received a number of name suggestions after asking fans in a post. 

Those who would like to share their name ideas can submit suggestions to the Forward Madison website. Suggestions will be narrowed down to a final public vote.

The winner will receive a prize, and the actual flamingo will have a pink leg band tied to it with its new name.
