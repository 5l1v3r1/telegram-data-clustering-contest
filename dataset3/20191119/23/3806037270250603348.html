Casio G-SHOCK Presents New $1,100 USD Luxury MT-G Watch

Combining luxury and durability.

19 Nov 2019, 23:04 by Gabrielle Leung

Following the release of limited edition camo-print iterations of two of its most iconic models, Casio G-SHOCK has now unveiled its latest MT-G timepiece. The luxury MTGB1000XBD-1 arrives with an all-new, carbon layered bezel, along with a mirrored black case and scratch-resistant, non-reflective coating. Created with G-SHOCK’s Tough Triple G Resist technology, the new model merges sophisticated style with durability and strength.

The shock resistant MTGB1000XBD-1 boasts a sapphire crystal face and bright red dial, offering a sleek option for a day-to-night look. Bringing some of G-SHOCK’s most premium features to the stylish timepiece, the MT-G model is equipped with Bluetooth connectivity, Two-Way Time Sync technology and self-charging capabilities. Like other G-SHOCK timepieces, the watch is water resistant up to 200 meters and features a dual dial display, full auto calendar and LED light.

Retailing for $1,100 USD, the MTGB1000XBD-1 will be available to purchase on gshock.com, at G-SHOCK’s store in SoHo, New York and at G-SHOCK retailers.
