Moree Plains Shire Council to close Balo Street for installation of Christmas decorations

19 Nov 2019, 23:12

Moree will be soon be looking festive, with Moree Plains Shire Council set to install Christmas decorations in Balo Street this Sunday.

In order to safely install the decorations, council will need to temporarily close Balo Street to traffic from 6am to 6pm on Sunday, November 24.

Highway traffic to Balo Street, between the Albert Street roundabout and Gwydir Street will be temporarily closed and diverted down Frome Street.

The main street will still be accessible by pedestrians.

Council apologises for any inconvenience this may cause and thanks residents for their understanding while these decoration works are undertaken.
