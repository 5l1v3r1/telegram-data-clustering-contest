Jameela Jamil Tells CupcakKe To Put Down Her Twitter Post For Promoting Unrealistic And ‘Dangerous’ Diet Change

19 Nov 2019, 23:45 by Todd Malm

According to a report from BET.com, Jameela Jamil recently shouted out to a rapper, but not in the way that the artist would’ve liked. Jamil, who has built a reputation for herself as a warrior for social justice, shared some of her opinions regarding the unusual weight-loss tactics employed by the rapper, CupcakKe.

As a consequence of her comments on Twitter, Jamil reportedly became a target for many people online who consider her as just a buzz-kill, rather than a soldier for body positivity. On the 16th of November, Saturday, the rapper took to her account to show off her new look to fans.

In the caption of the social media post, CupcakKe shared that she had done a water fast for a month and she lost a lot of weight because of it.
