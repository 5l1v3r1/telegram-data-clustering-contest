Anastasiya Kvitko Wears Sheer Pants With The Tiniest Thong Underneath On A Yacht

Anna Harnes

Russian bombshell Anastasiya Kvitko has sent the pulses of her fans racing after posting a sizzling photo where she modeled a pair of sheer lace pants with only the tiniest of thongs worn underneath.

The picture was taken on a luxurious yacht, with the Miami skyline in the background. The location is not surprising for Anastasiya’s fans, as the stunner has called the sunny Florida city home since she moved to the United States from Russia as a teenager to pursue modeling.

In the picture, Anastasiya wore a pair of lace pants that were sheer enough to show off her tanned and toned legs. Moreover, it left almost nothing to the imagination when it came to her derriere, with only the tiniest of black thongs preserving the buxom brunette’s modesty.

The pants had a high-waist cut, emphasizing the stunner’s incredibly slim torso. Moreover, her midriff was left bare, as Anastasiya paired the sheer pants with a tight black crop top. Long-sleeved and skintight, it was the perfect complement to her daring bottoms.

Anastasiya posed with her back to the camera as she turned her head to the side, both to survey the stunning view and give the camera a view of her beautiful profile. She wore little makeup — just the simplest swipe of mascara — letting her natural beauty shine through. Her long brunette locks were styled straight, and they were windswept by the ocean breeze.

By crossing one leg in front of the other, Anastasiya expertly accentuated her hourglass figure, and fans understandably went wild over the shot.

Within less than an hour of posting, the upload got over 43,000 likes and close to 590 comments.

“Wow you are looking so perfect in anything you put on! You’re so stunning… that body is truly a masterpiece,” one awestruck fan raved, adding a heart-eye face, shocked face, and red heart emoji.

“Booty Dreams,” added a second, clearly impressed with Anastasiya’s perky posterior.

“The best body,” proclaimed a third follower, along with a fire and crown emoji.
