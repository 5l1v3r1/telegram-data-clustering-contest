I'm a Celeb viewers turn on Adele and Roman after row by voting them for trial

After a row between Adele and Roman in the celebrity jungle, the public voted them for the next eating Bushtucker trial

19 Nov 2019, 23:19 by Verity Sulway, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/verity-sulway/

I'm a Celebrity stars Adele Roberts and Roman Kemp will be the next campmates subjected to a horrifying Bushtucker trial.

They found out their fate after a public vote which revealed viewers turned on Adele and Roman following their row.

Adele was upset by Roman's questioning that she brought in a framed photo of Loose Women's Jane McDonald as her luxury item.

Roman apologised for upsetting her, and they embraced in an awkward hug.

Tomorrow night, the two radio presenters will go head to head in Just Desserts, the first eating trial of the series.

 

The pair will have to eat whatever the show bosses put in front of them, in order to win meals for the camp.

Viewers took to Twitter to voice their glee at the warring campmates being voted for the horror challenge.

"The fact Adele and Roman have been voted for this trial together already says the public are loving the tension (and...over Jane McDonald LOL)", said one.

"Everyone defo put Roman in the trial with Adele cos she didnt like how he spoke about Jane", predicted another.

"Finding it so funny adele got the trial with Roman after his 'prejudice'", laughed another.

A fourth pondered: "I’m guessing Roman was voted for the trial as he’s a favourite, and Adele was voted because she’s annoying people?"

The clash started when Roman admitted he was puzzled why Adele chose a picture of Jane McDonald as her luxury item.

When asked if she knows any of her songs, Adele said: “I know her off Loose Women, I don’t know her actual songs but I know her from the cruising show when she does covers.”

 

Giggling Roman caused offence when he claimed: "No one knows what she does."

Adele strongly insisted: "That’s prejudice, I’m allowed to like what I want."

Realising he had upset Adele, Roman said: "I wasn't saying that at all, I’m sorry, I didn’t mean to."

But the bickering did not stop there as Roman claimed he found the situation "funny".

Adele sarcastically asked: "I’m not allowed to like what I want?"

The DJ got Kate's backing and Roman apologised once again for winding Adele up.

She said: "It’s fine, you are just going on about it so much…"

* I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! airs on ITV at 9pm
