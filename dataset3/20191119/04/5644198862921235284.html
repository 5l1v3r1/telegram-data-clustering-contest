WeWork's 2025 junk bond falls to record low

19 Nov 2019, 04:55

Shared-office operator WeWork's 2025 junk bond fell to a record low on Monday, with its last yielding bid at 16.06per cent.

NEW YORK: Shared-office operator WeWork's 2025 junk bond fell to a record low on Monday, with its last yielding bid at 16.06per cent.

The We Company, owner of WeWork, said on Wednesday that net losses in the third quarter more than doubled to US$1.25 billion. The company had added a record number of desks to its global network but was unable to control rising costs.
