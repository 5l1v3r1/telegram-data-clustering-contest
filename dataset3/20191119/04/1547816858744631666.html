Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 November 18, 2019, Written Updates Of Full Episode: Anurag Helps Prerna In Decorating The Room

19 Nov 2019, 04:25 by Kristina Das

Prerna has been asked to decorate Komolika and Anurag’s room for their first night. She finds Komolika’s behaviour a little weird

Today's Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 episode begins with Mohini telling Anurag to go for honeymoon with Sonalika aka Komolika. However, Anurag wants to concentrate on his work and doesn’t want to go anywhere. Later, Prerna has been asked to decorate Komolika and Anurag’s room for their first night. She finds Komolika’s behaviour a little weird. While Prerna refuses to decorate their room but Mohini convinces her. She also asks Prerna to clean the lamps in the washroom where Anurag is also present. 

Anurag asks Prerna about the lamps and then he also asks her about her husband and baby. Prerna tells him that her husband is helpless. Meanwhile, Mohini arrives and Anurag is compelled to hide. Later, in washing the lamps Anurag helps Prerna but she gets a cut on her finger. Anurag puts bandaid on her finger and asks her why he always feels protective about her. 

Further, he wonders why Komolika has asked Prerna to decorate their room. Later, to decorate the bed, Anurag helps Prerna and they both fall on the bed with flowers.
