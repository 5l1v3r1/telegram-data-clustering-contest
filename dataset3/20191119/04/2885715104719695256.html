Ranu Mandol gets a makeover, Twitterati comes out in her support

The photos of Ranu Mandol's transformation went viral on social media. While some people criticised her look for the heavy makeup, others appreciated her for being a sport and came to her defence.

19 Nov 2019, 04:12 by Entertainment Desk

Singing sensation Ranu Mandol has undergone a makeover. She recently attended the inauguration ceremony of a salon in Kanpur where she sported a golden outfit. She completed her look with some gold jewellery. The photos of her transformation went viral on social media. While some people criticised her look for the heavy makeup, others appreciated her for being a sport and came to her defence.

Some twitter users also compared Mandol to Joaquin Phoenix’s Joker and the protagonist of horror thriller The Nun.
