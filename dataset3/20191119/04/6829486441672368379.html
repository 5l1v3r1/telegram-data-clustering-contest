Everyone Is Obsessed With This 10-Year-Old’s Truly Iconic Christmas List

19 Nov 2019, 04:14 by Michelle Rennex

There was no feeling quite like getting excited for Christmas as child.

And for Drew Johnson’s 10-year-old daughter, this couldn’t be closer to the truth.

With Christmas fast approaching, she thought she’d give her dad a 28-item long list of everything he could gift her this year. In an iconic move, she mixed in low-ticket items like toys and clothes with some bigger selections like electronics and cash.

Drew shared the list to Twitter saying “my 10-year-old daughter must be out of her mind with this Christmas list” and it caught the attention of over 100,000 people.
