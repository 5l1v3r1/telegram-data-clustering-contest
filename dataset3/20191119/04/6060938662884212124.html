Imtiaz Ali reveals the time when Shah Rukh Khan got too nervous to talk to a girl

19 Nov 2019, 04:47 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shah Rukh Khan may be the King of Romance, but according to director Imtiaz Ali, off the big screen, the superstar is just like the rest of us.

According to reports doing the rounds, the director got candid and shared a hilarious moment he shared with SRK and Anushka Sharma while shooting for their film ‘Jab Harry Met Sejal’. While calling to mind the time they shot in Portugal for their film, Ali said that Anushka and he challenged SRK to approach a girl and talk to her. He went on to add that the actor was “so nervous that he just didn’t go for it”.

He went on to add that SRK coaxed him to talk to the girl instead. Admitting he was also nervous to approach the girl, Ali said that he forgot all about it when he saw how nervous the “romantic hero” got.

Imtiaz also when to clear rumours of an alleged suicide scene in the film that didn’t make the cut. Speaking about the same, he said that there was no truth to the reports and that it was merely just one idea of the film. He also assured that SRK is a “very classy person” than to ask for a change in the script.

The director will soon be back with another romantic film, ‘Aaj Kal’, this time around with Sara Ali Khan and Kartik Aaryan playing the leads. The film is slated to release on Valentine’s Day 2020.
