Watch video: Farhan Akhtar gives us a sneak peek into his prep for 'Toofan'

19 Nov 2019, 04:48 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Farhan Akhtar is undergoing major body transformation and is also undertaking special boxing lessons for his next titled 'Toofan'. He is going to essay the role of a boxer and is doing rigorous workouts to step in the shoes of his character. Recently, he took to his Instagram handle to share a video from his boxing sessions.

Read Also: Pic: Farhan Akhtar and Shibani Dandekar strike pose for a happy picture amidst Diwali celebrations

In this video, we can see the 'Bhaag Milkha Bhaag' actor prepping hard for the film. He wrote, ''Keeping at it. #toofaninthemaking #bts #boxerlife #drillsforskills #speedball 🥊 @drewnealpt 🏋🏽 @samir_jaura 🤼‍♂️ @darrellfoster''. As soon as he shared the video, his ladylove dropped love on the most and hailed the hard work of the actor and wrote, ''beast mode''. Apart from her, Ishaan Khatter and Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor too praised the star.

Talking about his prep, the actor told Bombay Times, “Getting the character of a runner right was challenging physically, as there were a lot of races. However, when it comes to running, I had some prior practice, as I used to represent my school in athletics. A part of my fitness regime has included running, too. So it wasn’t something I felt I had to learn.” He further added and said, ''But boxing is something I have never done in my life. It is very demanding, and is not only about fitness, but also your endurance.''

Directed by Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra, the film is slated to release on 2nd October 2020.
