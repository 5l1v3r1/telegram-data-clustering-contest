This is analagous to how radios work. In a radio, a current flowing through an antenna creates electromagnetic waves. When they hit an object with the right dimensions (with the right resonant frequency), they induce an electrical current in the target which can be used to carry signals (or even energy) over a distance. It's the same here except the hammer is the current and it induces a physical wave (sound) which then hits a suitable "sound antenna" with the right resonant frequency.
