‘Don’t understand why KKR have not retained him’: Yuvraj Singh after Chris Lynn’s 30-ball 91

T10 League: Chris Lynn hit Harry Gurney for 24 runs in an over during his rampant knock of 91 runs from 30 balls for Maratha Arabians against Team Abu Dhabi

19 Nov 2019, 04:12 by Sports Desk

Chris Lynn shattered the T10 league records by hitting a 30-ball 91 against for Maratha Arabians against Team Abu Dhabi at Sheikh Zayed Stadium, Abu Dhabi on Monday. Lynn wreaked havoc on Abu Dhabi hitting nine fours and seven sixes in his innings. The Australian’s unbeaten knock of 91 is the highest ever T10 league score and the closest any batsman has come to scoring a century.

The 29-year-old helped his team post a total of 138 for two wickets in 10 overs. Maratha Arabians registered another convincing win by beating Team Abu Dhabi by 24 runs.
