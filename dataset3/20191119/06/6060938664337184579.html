Photos: Sonam Kapoor and Anand Ahuja enjoy their LA vacation with their close friends

Sonam Kapoor and Anand Ahuja are the most adorable couple of the tinsel. Whenever they post a lovey-dovey picture on social media their fans go crazy over it. From their sweet social media PDA to their cuddly photos, their Instagram profiles are all things, love!

In the photo, Sonam is sporting a skirt and top and specs while Anand has opted for simple black denim and grey shirt. Anand shared the picture and simply wrote, “LA ...”

But one thing that Anand teases her about is posting all the photos of the gifts she gets and he keeps wondering how can one do such a thing.
