Liverpool fans react as Elijah Dixon-Bonner pens a new contract

Elijah Dixon-Bonner is a boyhood Liverpool fan.

19 Nov 2019, 06:45 by Jay Williams

Elijah Dixon-Bonner has signed a new contract at Liverpool, the club’s official website has confirmed.

Dixon-Bonner has penned a new contract extension for an undisclosed period of time. Liverpool have placed their faith in the youth team midfielder and rewarded him for a string of good performances.

The 18-year-old is yet to make his senior debut for Liverpool but he is a regular in the academy teams. With 10 Premier League 2 games under his belt this season, as well as outings in the UEFA Youth League and the EFL Trophy, it is clear Liverpool value Dixon-Bonner highly.

He is regarded as a potential future first-teamer at Anfield. Operating in midfield, he is known for his goalscoring ability. Dixon-Bonner recently netted in the EFL Trophy against senior opposition in Accrington Stanley.

Since stepping up from the U18 team, Dixon-Bonner has impressed Neil Critchley enough to nail down a spot. This new contract reflects the England youth starlet’s good form this season.
