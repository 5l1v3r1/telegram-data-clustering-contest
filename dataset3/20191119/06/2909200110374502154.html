Western Togoland secession: How Ghanaians reacted with humorous comments

19 Nov 2019, 06:01 by MyNewsGH

Following the announcement of the Homeland Study Group Foundation that Western Togoland has seceded from Ghana and was now a country on its own, Ghanaians seemed to have unanimously reacted with their characteristic humour, making light of the whole issue and having a good laugh.

Of course some security analyst have called for expedited action to be taken against the secessionist given the gravity of the alleged crime thay have committed but many on social media are at their hilarious best on social media.

Separatist group that declared independence for Western Togoland last week, is set to start naming its ministers, launch its currency and as part of measures to compose an interim government.

The Homeland Study Group Foundation declared independence for the Volta, and parts of the Northern, North Eastern and Upper East Regions after its meeting held in Ho.
