DRAM shipments rise as ASPs fall

19 Nov 2019, 06:18 by David Manners

Q3 DRAM revenue rose 4% ending three quarters of QoQ decline as server and smartphone customers pulled shipments forward, says DRAMeXchange.

However profit margins were reduced as ASPs fell 20%.

Samsung increased Q3 bit shipments by 30% and quarterly revenue by 5% QoQ but its profit margin fell to 33% from 41% in Q2.

Hynix grew bit shipments by 20% and revenue by 3.5% but saw profit margin fall to 24% from 28% in Q2 which would have been worse had it not been for a production shift from server DRAM to mobile DRAM.

Micron increased bit shipments by almost 30% but saw its market share slip to under 20%.
