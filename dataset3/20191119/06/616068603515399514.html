Pam scraps Assange visit - Entertainment News

19 Nov 2019, 06:50 by WENN

Pamela Anderson has been forced to cancel a prison visit to Wikileaks founder Julian Assange, due to a personal emergency.

The actress was scheduled to visit Assange in Belmarsh Prison in London on Tuesday, ahead of a press conference, but her plans have changed.

A spokesman for the Don't Extradite Assange campaign said, "Pamela has a personal emergency which has meant she has had to go home and cancel everything for the next two weeks."

Anderson visited Assange earlier this year and recently told TV show The Talk she was "extremely distressed at the thought of his declining state."

Prior to his arrest and detention, Assange spent seven years in exile at the Ecuadorean Embassy in London to avoid extradition.

He faces 18 charges in the U.S., including allegations he conspired to break into a Pentagon computer and leaked hundreds of thousands of classified documents.
