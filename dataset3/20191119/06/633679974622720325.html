Iman Rappetti brings new SA Documentary on abuse to DSTV

19 Nov 2019, 06:32 by Algoa FM

Veteran journalist Iman Rappetti is the host of a once-off documentary focusing on high profile abusive relationships that is to air on DSTV in December.

Titled Loved Like Crazy, relationships under the spotlight will include Oscar Pistorius and Reeva Steenkamp, Sandile Mantsoe and Karabo Mokoena and Mampintsha and Babes Wodumo.

The producer of the show, Penny Peppa, told the Sunday Times that they hoped it would deepen the understanding around partner abuse.

The documentary airs on DSTV's Lifetime channel December 9.(channel 131) It will also air on the Crime and Investigation Channel on AETV.
