The weed inside your brain is too much- Allotey attacks Kwaku Skirt

19 Nov 2019, 06:02 by MyNewsGH

In what is seen as a ‘home-fight’ former Central regional Chairman of the opposition National Democratic Congress  (NDC) Bernard Allotey Jacobs has taken his  party’s US-based broadcast journalist Kwaku Skirt to the cleaners.

In a post sighted by MyNewsGh.com, ‘Uncle Allotey’ said accused Kwaku Skirt of being a wee smoker possessed by 77 demons.

It is not clear what has caused bad blood between the two, but Mr. Allotey Jacobs has been accused by his party’s rank and file of being a sellout who goes to radio and TV to defend the policies and actions of the ruling New Patriotic Party and Akufo-Addo instead of NDC and John Mahama.

The loud Kwaku Skirt also joined the attacks which did not go down well with Allotey Jacobs.

“Kweku Skirt come home I have the medicine for your paranoia come Kweku come you need a blouse for your skirt.” He wrote.

“Your psychosis as heightened you need to rest your brains for you have not used it to the glory of God the weed is too  much in your system come home and I will help you to have a good rest” he added.

“Come ooo Kweku Skirt come for you have now reach the edge of your life come my brother 77 demons in the weed you smoke has taken full control of your body. You are far away from salvation” he ended.
