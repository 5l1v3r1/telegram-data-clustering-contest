Nushrat Bharucha is riding high on the success of her last film 'Dream Girl' opposite Ayushmann Khurrana. The actress received a positive response from the audience for her performance in the film. Recently, new photos of the star have surfaced on social media. In these photos, we can see her in a brown shimmery dress which she styled it with black stilettos. Her on-point eye make-up will make you recreate this look.

Nushrat's last film 'Dream Girl' crossed 100 cr mark at the box-office. The actress played the role of a positive and loving girl. Talking about the same, the actress said, "The success of ‘Dream Girl’ has brought me in a different light. People are seeing me as a positive and loving girl. The fact that people can see me as this naive and lovable girl who was able to carry her self in a film, which was full of so many more characters is very fulfilling."

Meanwhile, on the work front, Nushrat will be seen next in Hansal Mehta's, 'Turram Khan' co-starring Rajkummar Rao.
