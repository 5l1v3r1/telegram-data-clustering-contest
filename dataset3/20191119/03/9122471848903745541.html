Injury Update For Kansas City Chiefs RB Damien Williams

19 Nov 2019, 03:17 by Matt Hladik

If you’re a fantasy football player who needed a big performance out of Kansas City Chiefs running back Damien Williams, you’re probably not very happy right now.

Williams went down late in the first half of tonight’s game against the Los Angeles Chargers when he took a big hit on a running play. Williams stayed down on the ground for a while.

It looked like the veteran running back injured his chest. Sure enough, Kansas City announced Williams is dealing with a rib injury.

He is officially questionable to return.
