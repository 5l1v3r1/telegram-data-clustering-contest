The ideal candidate has at least 5 years ticket sales experience, proven advanced skills in Microsoft Word and Excel and previous experience within a major event or festival. Experience with Tableau, an interest in the film industry and exposure to the Ferve ticketing system will be advantageous. This role requires candidates to have 2+ years social media & digital agency experience, strong client communication, social media content creation experience and Facebook Ads Manager & paid social advertising experience. Apply today to join a one of a kind agency.
