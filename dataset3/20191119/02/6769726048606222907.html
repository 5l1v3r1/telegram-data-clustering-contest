Student wins Yohfest Visual Art Prize

19 Nov 2019, 02:00 by Aaron Kirby

YEAR 12 Mandurah Baptist College student Chanae Dunstan was announced as the winner of the 2019 Yohfest Visual Art Prize.

Her oil painting Flourish is a portrait of her younger brother bathed in a wash of light.

The work comments on the beautiful stage of life during which the child transforms both physically and mentally, flourishing into a new chapter in life.
