Amazon Alexa now tells you Xbox news

Alexa can do more now!

19 Nov 2019, 02:36 by Asher Madan

What you need to know

  • Microsoft released an Xbox Skill for Amazon Alexa many months ago.
  • It now features even more features like Xbox news.
  • You can ask Alexa to tell you Xbox news and she'll share the latest stories.
  • You can purchase an Echo Dot with Alexa on Amazon.

Have you ever wanted to get Xbox news on your Amazon Echo or any other Alexa device? Well, now you can. Today, Microsoft's Harrison Hoffman said that you can now ask Alexa to tell you Xbox news. Just say, "Alexa, play news from Xbox" and it'll tell you everything that's going on today.
