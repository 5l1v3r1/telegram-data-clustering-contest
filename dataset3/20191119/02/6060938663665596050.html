Happy Birthday, Tara Sutaria: FIVE times the millennial diva's smoking hot pictures set the internet on fire

19 Nov 2019, 02:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The multi-talented millennial diva Tara Sutaria recently made her debut on the celluloid with Punit Malhotra's 'Student Of The Year 2' and left everyone bedazzled with her on-screen presence. Starring alongside the likes of Tiger Shroff and Ananya Panday, Tara managed to grab a lot of limelight as well.

The birthday girl recently featured opposite Sidharth Malhotra in Milap Zaveri's 'Marjaavaan' and impressed the audience with her stellar performance once again. The film which also stars Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh in pivotal roles has been doing exceptionally well at the box office.

For the unversed, the millennial diva is also a professional singer, who has performed across several cities across the globe since the age of seven. Not only that but Tara is also a well-trained dancer and knows multiple forms of dance forms.

With more than three million followers on social media, Tara makes sure that she keeps her fans entertained by sharing pictures from her latest photoshoots wherein she flaunts her style in traditional as well as modern outfits. Today, on her birthday we take a glimpse at the times when Tara's smoking hot pictures set the internet on fire.

Not only are her professional accolades noteworthy but her personal life and relationship with Aadar Jain also grab a lot of attention. The lovebirds have been painting the town red by making public appearances together and their PDA on social media has been giving us all some major relationship goals.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, the birthday girl is currently basking in the success of her recently released film and at the same time also gearing up for her next release 'Tadap'. The film which is being helmed by Milan Luthria also marks the debut of Suniel Shetty's son Ahan Shetty and the film is a remake of 2018 Telugu film RX 100.
