Report of cougar attack triggers alert in Tri-Cities

19 Nov 2019, 08:10 by KEPR-TV News Staff

KENNEWICK, Wash. - Law enforcement agencies are on alert after a woman reported her dog was attacked by a cougar in Kennewick.

The woman called 911 at about 11:20 p.m. Monday and said she was walking her dog when a cougar jumped out of tree, grabbed the dog and took off with it, according to the Benton County Sheriff's Office.

Deputies were unable to find the cougar or dog after the reported attack.

The attack happened on the corner of North Arthur Street and West Tucannon Avenue in Kennewick.

Sheriff's officials say they have alerted the Washington State Patrol of a potential dangerous cougar in the area.
