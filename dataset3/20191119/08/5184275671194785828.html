Housefull 4 Box Office Day 24: Records A Good 4th Weekend

Housefull 4 earned around 47 lakhs approx on 4th Friday.

19 Nov 2019, 08:38 by Koimoi.com Team

Housefull 4 Box Office: Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon & Riteish Deshmukh led Housefull 4 is raking up money even in 4th week as the film put up a good total of 2.70 crores approx in the 4th weekend.

Housefull 4 earned around 47 lakhs approx on 4th Friday, 78 lakhs approx the day after and 1.45 crores on Sunday which was a very good trend.

Housefull 4 has earned 205.18 crores (3 weeks total) + 1.70 crores i.e. 206.88 crores till the end of the 4th weekend and is all set to end the 4th week around 208 crores mark.

Also starring Pooja Hegde, Bobby Deol & Kriti Kharbanda in main roles, the film is directed by Farhad Samji. Produced by Sajid Nadiadwala, the film released on Oct 25.

Meanwhile, the hit jodi of Akshay & Kriti have been cast together again by Sajid for upcoming masala entertainer Bachchan Pandey.

Sajid’s production company Nadiadwala Grandson’s Twitter account announced: “We’re extremely happy and elated to announce the charming @kritisanon is here to make our Christmas 2020 merrier than ever! Welcome back to the #NGEFamily with #BachchanPandey!”

Sharing her excitement, Kriti said that she is beyond excited to work with Akshay and director Farhad Samji.

“Thrilled and beyond excited for this reunion! This Christmas is gonna be indeed a Merry Merry one! Can’t wait to start #BachchanPandey,” she said.
