Rev. Obofour invokes generational curse on the editor who doctored his blasphemous viral footage

19 Nov 2019, 08:20 by Adu Sarpeah

Yesterday, November 18, 2019, your favorite entertainment portal, ghbase.com reported that Kwaku Agyei Antwi popularly known as Rev. Obofour has stated categorically that never in his pastoral job will he work for God to take the glory.

In the publication, we stated that it’s not clear what triggered Obofour’s outburst against God but taking into consideration his demeanour in the viral footage, he seems serious with his weird or better still blasphemous proclamation.
