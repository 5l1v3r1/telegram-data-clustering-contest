Akanksha Puri On Celebrating The Response To Her Film, Action: ‘Not Without Paras Chhabra, Will Wait For Him To Come Out Of Bigg Boss 13 House’- EXCLUSIVE

19 Nov 2019, 08:24 by Vickey Lalwani

TV actress, Akanksha Puri’s Tamil film, Action, directed by Sundar C hit theatres, last Friday (Nov 15) and her work is being appreciated in the film. We got in touch with the actress to know how is she celebrating the moment and she had quite a romantic reply to that

TV actors Akanksha Puri is on cloud 9 as her Tamil film, Action is in theatres. The film, directed by Sundar C and also starring Vishal and Tammannah Bhatia, released last Friday (Nov 15). The actress has been getting good reviews for her work in the film and is elated about the same.  

We got in touch with Akanksha to know how she is celebrating the moment of success and the actress bowled us over with her oh-so-sweet reply, which has a mention of her boyfriend, Paras Chhabra, who is currently locked inside the Bigg Boss 13 house.
