Already dreaming about historic pink ball test: Rahane

The Mumbai batsman is scheduled to land in Kolkata.

19 Nov 2019, 08:17 by IANS

India vice-captain Ajinkya Rahane says he is already “dreaming” about the first-ever Day-Night Test in the country, which is starting from Friday against Bangladesh at Eden Gardens here.

“Already dreaming about the historic pink ball test,” Rahane said in a Twitter post along with a picture of him sleeping and a pink ball lying next to him.
