Man in his 70s dies following crash in Co Cork

19 Nov 2019, 08:36 by Newstalk

A man in his 70s has died following a crash in Co Cork in the early hours of this morning.

It happened shortly after 12:15am in the Taur area of Newmarket, when his car crashed into a ditch.

The man - who was the only person in the car - was pronounced dead at the scene.

His body has been removed to Cork University Hospital for a post mortem examination.

The scene of the crash remains closed this morning, with a forensic exam to be carried out.
