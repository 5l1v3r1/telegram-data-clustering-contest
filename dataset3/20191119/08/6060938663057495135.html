‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’ trailer launch: Rohit Shetty pays tribute to Ajay Devgn on his 100th film

19 Nov 2019, 08:27 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ajay Devgn is all geared up with his most ambitious film, ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’. The film also marks his 100th film in the industry. While the makers are all set unveil the much-awaited trailer of period drama, filmmaker Rohit Shetty pays a short tribute to the actor. Speaking about the bond he shares with Ajay, Rohit said, “My journey is incomplete without a name and that name is Ajay Devgn. People call him Ajay Devgn, I call him ‘Boss’. We have spent 20 years together. Our relationship is not about a few Fridays but about two generations.”

Elaborating further, he added, “Today, I am here because the thing you are going to experience today is a dream that Ajay Devgn had seen about 30 years ago. He dreamt of creating a visual for the audience that will become an experience of a lifetime for them. And the best part is when he is finally fulfilling his dream, it is coincidentally his 100th film.”

Rohit also stated that realisation of this dream has not been easy as hardwork of a lot of people has gone behind it. The director promised that they will have an out of the world experience with the film.

‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’ stars Ajay Devgn, Kajol and Saif Ali Khan in lead roles. The film will hit the theatres on January 10, 2019.
