Former Tiger Townsend in Essendon's sights

19 Nov 2019, 08:08 by Daniel Cherny

Richmond premiership player Jacob Townsend looks poised to join Essendon.

Industry sources have confirmed Townsend, 26, is in the sights of the Bombers, having visited the club's Tullamarine base earlier this month.

The 2017 premiership forward played just one senior game for the Tigers this year but was part of their VFL premiership.

The Dons would be Townsend's third AFL club after he spent four seasons at Greater Western Sydney.

Essendon delisted Mitch Brown last month, while Michael Hartley walked from the club and is set to join Hawthorn.

Brown will meanwhile train with Melbourne in a bid to keep his AFL career alive.
