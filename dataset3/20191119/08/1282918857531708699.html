Here's the live streaming details & schedule of the upcoming Davis Cup Finals 2019 which had a complete makeover as it is shortened into just a week this year

Written By Akhil Nambiar | Mumbai | Updated On: November 19, 2019 08:21 IST

The upcoming Davis Cup Finals 2019 has had a complete makeover with the long duration tournament shortened to just a week. Great Britain guaranteed their place with a wild card pick with defending champions Croatia and the other three semi-finalist teams in 2018. Till the previous year, it was held in one of the competing countries but this year it will be hosted by Caja Magica, home of the Madrid Open in Spain.
