Nakheel Mall to open on Dubai’s Palm Jumeirah next week

The new mall will open on Thursday November 28

19 Nov 2019, 08:18 by Amy Mathieson

If you love shopping in Dubai and have been eagerly awaiting the arrival of the city’s newest shopping mall, you’re in luck. Nakheel Mall on Palm Jumeirah has revealed it will be opening on Thursday November 28 – just in time for some Christmas shopping.

Dubai is big on shopping (after all The Dubai Mall is the world’s largest shopping mall), and now if you live near the Palm this will make hitting the shops even better. The new mall will even have its own monorail station and will be the access point for The View at The Palm – a public observatory due to open at neighbouring Palm Tower.

There’s set to be more than 300 shops and restaurants at Nakheel mall. We already know there’s going to be a Waitrose set to cover 4,200 sq m, which is sure to please British shoppers, while there will also be an H&M, Bath and Body Works and The Toy Store.

Restaurant-wise we can expect a new branch of popular Taiwanese dim sum eatery Din Tai Fung, the Mall of the Emirates version of which picked up a Highly Commended Award in our Best Chinese Restaurant category at the Time Out Dubai Restaurant Awards 2019. Frozen yoghurt shop Pinkberry is also due to open as well as milkshake company Shakeaway, burger chain Wendys and homegrown Asian restaurant The Noodle House. We’re also promised a “unique food hall concept”.

There will also be new attractions including a VOX Cinemas with 15 screens, soft play park Fabyland and a massive trampoline park Trampo Extreme. The high-octane park is almost 4,000 sq ft of bouncing fun with 16 different jump activities ideal for tots of three up to teens of 13. Also expect extreme dodgeball, foam pit, free jumping, a Western-themed shooting arena, electric-car race track, role play arena and toddlers only area.

Looking to get fit? There’s going to be a Fitness First there too.

With fitness, restaurants, shopping and movies, we can’t wait to check out Dubai’s latest shopping destination.
Opening Thu Nov 28. Palm Jumeirah, www.nakheelmall.ae.
