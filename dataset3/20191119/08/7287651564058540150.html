A Confirms new Half-Life - but only or VR? Half-Life: Alyx

19 Nov 2019, 08:32 by Hilbert Hagedoorn

Allegedly, Valve will soon announce Half-Life: Alyx. But do not be too happy, it should "just" be a VR game. According to an alleged leaked interview, Alyx would see a release in March 2020, but could only be played with a VR headset. 

The title is likely to refer to Alyx Vance, who fought alongside Gordon Freeman in Half-Life 2 and Half-Life 2: Episode One and Two against the alien Combine. Maybe the resistance fighter is now the main character of the VR-offshoot.

Much more information about the game does not provide the alleged excerpt from the conversation, a second transcript of the same conversation still adds when exactly we should expect more information on Half-Life: Alyx.
