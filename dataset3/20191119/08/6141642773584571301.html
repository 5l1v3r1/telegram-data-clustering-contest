Flight to Jersey delayed after passenger boards wrong plane

PASSENGERS were forced to disembark from a plane bound for Jersey after a passenger boarded the jet by mistake.

19 Nov 2019, 08:57 by Richard Heath

The man had been due to travel from Liverpool to Belfast with easyJet at 7.40am on Monday but instead boarded the 7.40am flight to Jersey.

The passengers were told to wait alongside the plane as the man was taken off. He was allowed to board his correct flight.

The Jersey flight was delayed for just under half an hour.

An easyjet spokesman confirmed an investigation was under way.
