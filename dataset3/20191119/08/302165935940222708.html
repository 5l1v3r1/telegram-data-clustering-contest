Lebanese parliament session expected to be postponed, say parliamentary sources

19 Nov 2019, 08:53

BEIRUT, Nov 19 —A Lebanese parliament session is expected to be postponed today because protests had prevented MPs from arriving, two parliamentary sources said, torpedoing the first session since demonstrations against the ruling elite erupted on Oct. 17.

The postponement is expected to be formally announced by Parliament Speaker Nabih Berri later today. — Reuters
