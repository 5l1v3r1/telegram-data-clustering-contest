'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior' trailer: This Ajay Devgn, Kajol and Saif Ali Khan starrer is a fitting tribute to the Maratha warrior

19 Nov 2019, 08:26 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Finally, the much-awaited trailer of Ajay Devgn, Saif Ali Khan and Kajol's upcoming period drama, 'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior' is out and it is outstanding. This is Ajay's ambitious project and has been in the news since the time of its announcement. After releasing a few stunning posters and motion pictures, Ajay has finally treated the audience with a super impressive trailer. And we are sure, this has left the fans wanting more.

This 3 minute 21 seconds trailer gracefully takes us back into history. What stands out in the trailer are the groyund breaking VFX special effects and the set decor Not to forget the stellar cast that is the main essence of the film. Saif's ferocious avatar, Kajol's promising stint and Ajay's honest attempt to play the brave Tanhaji is something to watch out for in the film. The grandeur of the film, definitely speak volumes about Maratha empire and its glory. The dialogues and the fight scenes stand out and the background score gives you the chills. Ajay's dialect and dialogues as the Maratha warrior make the trailer impactful.
