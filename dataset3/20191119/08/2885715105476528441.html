Tanhaji trailer: Ajay Devgn’s 100th film celebrates Maratha glory

Tanhaji trailer: The film starring Ajay Devgn, Saif Ali Khan, Kajol and others is set in 1670 when the Marathas fought against the Mughals to get back the fort at Kondana.

19 Nov 2019, 08:43 by Sampada Sharma

The trailer of Tanhaji, starring Ajay Devgn in the titular role, is out. The film is set in 1670 when the Marathas fought against the Mughals to get back the fort at Kondana. Here, Ajay plays the titular character who has to fight against Saif Ali Khan’s Udaybhan as he protects the Mughal control over the fort.

This movie season looks like it belongs to the Marathas. After the recently released trailer of Ashutosh Gowariker’s Panipat, Tanhaji too looks like it will celebrate Maratha courage. What’s extremely jarring here is the CGI that takes away from the experience of watching the three-and-a-half-minute trailer.
