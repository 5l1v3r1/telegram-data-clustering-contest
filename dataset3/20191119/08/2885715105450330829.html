Indian Navy SSR, AA recruitment 2019: The interested candidates can apply through the official website- joinindiannavy.gov.in. The application process will be closed on November 18, 2019

19 Nov 2019, 08:20 by Careers Desk

Indian Navy SSR, AA recruitment 2019: The application process to apply for the post of senior secondary recruit (SSR) and artificer apprentices (AA) is ending today – November 18. The interested candidates can apply through the official website- joinindiannavy.gov.in by today evening.

A total of 2700 posts of sailor will be filled through this recruitment process. To be eligible for the job, candidates will have to appear for an objective-type exam. Shortlisted candidates will be called for a physical test, medical test and final screening or medical enrollment and then training rounds.

Indian Navy SSR, AA recruitment 2019: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website, joinindiannavy.gov.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘online application for enrolment as AA, SSR August 2020 batch’ link under ‘current events’
Step 3: Click on click here next to ‘apply online’
Step 4: Register using basic details, verify
Step 5: Log-in using registration details
Step 6: Fill form, upload images
Step 7: Make payment

Indian Navy SSR, AA recruitment 2019: Salary

During the initial training period, a stipend of Rs 14,600 per month will be admissible. On successful completion of initial training, they will get a monthly salary in the range of Rs 21,700-Rs 69,100. Additionally, Rs 5300 monthly allowance and DA, ration allowances etc will also be paid.
