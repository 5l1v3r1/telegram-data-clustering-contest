Bigg Boss 13: Fans Pick Their Hero, 'Sidharth Shukla Ji Ki Personality Main Dum Hai' Say Netizens

19 Nov 2019, 08:08 by Nikita Thakkar

Being celebrity centric, there are a lot of strong personalities in Bigg Boss 13. Be it Sidharth Shukla, Asim Riaz, Rashami Desai or any other contestant; all the stars are putting their best foot forward. But who among the men is the real HERO of the house?

Bigg Boss 13 has shown us the true colors of our favourite celebrities. Be it Sidharth Shukla, Devoleena, Rashami Desai or Asim Riaz - we are getting to see a new side of all the contestants. But among the men, which one star appears to be the HERO of the house? Well, the channel, Colors raised this question on Twitter and there came a loud and clear answer - Sidharth Shukla. All his fans replied in unison and termed him the real hero of Bigg Boss 13.

A lot of Sidharth Shukla's fans commented on the post and stated that Dil Se Dil Tak star is the one who is a genuine, caring, loving and the angry young man of the house. In short, he is the hero of the house. One user wrote, "Personality mai hi dum hai bhai ki toh hero vahi hua na." Another one wrote, "It's #SidharthShuklaHe is good-looking, masculine, well-built, all girls of the house goes gaga over him, aggressive when someone targets him, has killer dance moves, on-point expressions, a loyal friend, analytical when it comes to strategies for tasks."
