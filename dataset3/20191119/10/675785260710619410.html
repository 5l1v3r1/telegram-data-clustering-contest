McDonald’s Christmas menu 2019 sees return of Big Tasty and camembert cheese dippers

McDonald's has unveiled its brand new 2019 Christmas menu - and it involves the return of the Big Tasty, camembert cheese dippers and even a new chicken burger called the Chicken Deluxe

19 Nov 2019, 10:04 by Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/mollyjaholt/

The cold weather might be here, but so is a brand new McDonald's menu - including the return of some very popular old favourites.

Loyal fans of the fast food chain will be pleased to hear that the beloved Big Tasty is back, after a year long absence from the famous restaurant.

Featuring a beef burger, Emmental cheese, tomato, lettuce, onion and its statement smoky Big Tasty sauce, it costs £4.29 on its own, or £5.89 as a medium meal. Plus, you can also add bacon to it for an extra 40p.

As well as the Big Tasty, there's also a brand new chicken burger set to appear this year, dubbed the Chicken Deluxe, for £5.19 (or £6.59 as a meal), as well as camembert cheese dippers (£1.69 for five, or £4.40 for a sharebox).

 

Originally reported by The Sun, all the new additions will arrive in McDonald's stores tomorrow, on Wednesday 20 November.

Despite being part of their festive menu, there's not much Christmassy about the new Chicken Deluxe burger, which involves two pieces of crispy chicken with a zesty tomato relish, cool mayo, cheese, lettuce and red onion rings.

As for the cheese shareboxes, you get 15 gloriously gooey cheese bites as well as three dips, making them perfect for sharing.

Once you've finished devouring all the new savoury offerings, you can wash it all down with a matchmaker McFlurry to finish, costing 99p for the mini version, or £1.39 for standard.
