BURGER RAID

Callous robbers in hi-vis who stole over £4k in cash from Glasgow BRGR restaurant hunted by police

19 Nov 2019, 10:31 by Blair Meikle

COPS are hunting for two callous robbers in workmen gear who stole over £4000 in cash from a Glasgow restaurant.

CCTV images shows the two men breaking into BRGR on Fenwick Road, Giffnock, at around 7.20am on Monday February 25.

They took a safe which had £4600 inside.

Officers are releasing the images in a bid to trace the suspects.

One of them is described as white, about 50-55 years old, approximately 5ft10in tall and was wearing black coloured bottoms and top with an orange high-vis vest and white hard hat.

He is seen to be wearing yellow gloves.

The second man is described as white, about 50-55 years old, approximately 5ft10in tall and was wearing dark blue jeans, a black hooded jacket, skip cap on with hood over it.

He was also wearing an orange high-vis vest. He is seen to be wearing yellow gloves.

Anyone with information on this incident, or who recognises any of these men, is asked to contact officers at the Community Investigation Unit (Acquisitive Crime) at Helen Street Police Station, Govan on the 101 number, quoting incident number 0919 25 February 2019.

Alternatively Crimestoppers can be contacted on 0800 555 111 where anonymity can be maintained.
