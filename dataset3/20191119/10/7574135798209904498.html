The Wonder Years announce tour w/ Free Throw, Spanish Love Songs, Pool Kids

19 Nov 2019, 10:28 by Andrew Sacher

The Wonder Years have announced a headlining 2020 US tour, which will see them playing two sets each night, one acoustic and one electric. They also lined up some pretty solid support: Free Throw, Spanish Love Songs, and Pool Kids. Head Count (“a non-partisan organization that uses the power of music to register voters and promote participation in democracy”) will be on site at most dates to accommodate voter registration signups.

The tour begins with two nights in Boston before hitting NYC on February 22 at Webster Hall, and after TWY make their way around the country, they’ll end back in the Northeast with two more tri-state area shows (NJ’s Asbury Lanes on 3/19 and New Haven’s Toad’s Place on 3/20) before wrapping up the tour in their hometown of Philly. Tickets for NYC, NJ, and CT go on sale Friday (11/22) at 10 AM, and an artist presale for the Webster Hall show starts Wednesday (11/20) at 10 AM.
