Search for 21-year-old Derbyshire man continues almost two months after he went missing

Officers have issued an appeal for help

19 Nov 2019, 10:40 by Rachel Sutherland

Police need your help to track down a missing 21-year-old from Long Eaton.

Robertas Maslenikovas, who is Lithuanian, was last seen on Tuesday, October 1.

He is described as having short, light-brown hair and a beard. He is 5ft 9ins tall, slim and has a tattoo of a tree on his right arm.

Anyone who has any information on his whereabouts should contact police on 101, quoting reference 618 of November 14.

Alternatively you can call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
