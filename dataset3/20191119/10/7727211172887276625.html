ITV This Morning's Holly Willoughby makes fans feel cold with 'stunning' short dress

Holly ditched the tights for a fresh look

19 Nov 2019, 10:53 by Rebecca Koncienzcy

Fans have said Holly Willoughby has made them 'feel cold' as she wowed in a "stunning" short dress.

The 38-year-old opted for a black, high necked ruffle hem knitted dress from Reiss, costing £185 as she posted a snap of her outfit ahead of today's edition of ITV's This Morning.

It had a fitted finish as she decided to ditch the tights and go bare-legged with black heels to finish the look.

There were dozens of comments simply saying 'stunning' or 'beautiful' as the mum-of-three rarely puts a fashion foot wrong.

One, mrscardin2012 said: "Bare legs!! You’re making me cold just looking at you.. Brrrr. Looking amazing as always Holly!!"

Another, wocko1878 said: "Wow, stunning great legs."

A third, weegran.63 said: "Need tights on."

And a fourth, chayhenderson added: "A stylish winter image. Bare legs brighten the look. Lovely!"

Yesterday, fans of the TV presenter immediately started googling where to get hold of her new winter skirt.

Her followers loved her tartan choice from Hobbs London and is priced at £99 .
