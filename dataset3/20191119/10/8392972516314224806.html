I'm A Celebrity viewers fuming as technical fault makes them miss 10 minutes of show

Dan Osborne was also affected!

19 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Nancy Brown

Viewers were left fuming after ITV Hub crashed 45 minutes into the second episode of I'm A Celebrity – Get Me Out Of Here!.

The incident happened as Roman Kemp told his campmates about the Christmas gifts his godfather, Wham! singer George Michael, had bought him.
