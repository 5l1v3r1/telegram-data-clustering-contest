Destroyer share “It Doesn’t Just Happen” from upcoming album

19 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Bill Pearis

Dan Bejar has shared a second single from the upcoming Destroyer album, Have We Met, which is out in January. Like “Crimson Tide,” there’s a distinct sleek, sultry ’80s-ness to “It Doesn’t Just Happen to Anyone,” that has this album shaping up to be very much in the mold of his much-loved 2011 album Kaputt. The song’s video is a mystery, following a snowmobile rider on a lonely trip across the tundra in middle of the night. Watch that below.
