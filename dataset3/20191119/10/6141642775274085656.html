Boeing clinches another order for its grounded 737 Max jet

The coup at the Dubai Airshow is a boost for the aircraft which remains earthbound since two fatal crashes.

19 Nov 2019, 10:52

Boeing’s embattled 737 Max jet has got another boost at the Dubai Airshow.

An order from Kazakhstan’s newly-launched budget carrier came as the Chicago-based company works to try and win US regulatory approval to get the plane back in the sky by early next year.

The plane has been grounded around the world after two crashes that killed 346 people.
