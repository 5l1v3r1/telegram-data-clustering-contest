SD Tempest: Meet The Tug Tasked With Guiding Aircraft Carriers Home

The state-of-the-art boat is entrusted with bringing the UK's aircraft carriers safely back into port.

19 Nov 2019, 10:37 by Tim Cooper

HMS Prince of Wales arriving into Portsmouth for the first time with tug SD Tempest (Picture: Royal Navy).

A state-of-the-art tug boat has once again guided the UK's newest and most advanced warship into its new home.

SD Tempest ensured the safe arrival of aircraft carrier HMS Prince of Wales into Portsmouth at the weekend.

The tug previously guided sister ship, HMS Queen Elizabeth, into the city's port for the first time in August 2017.

Her three powerful engines mean she is most capable tug in the Royal Navy and also has exceptional manoeuvrability.

She is commissioned for the job as the giant size of the carriers require an extra-powerful tug.
