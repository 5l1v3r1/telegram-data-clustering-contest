Dog spotted 'cycling' on busy Plymouth road

The pet was strapped into a bike-style dog trailer

19 Nov 2019, 10:10 by Molly Dowrick

Plymouth people were howling at the idea of a dog 'cycling' behind its owner on a busy Plymouth road after an amusing photo was shared online.

While you may be barking mad if you think pets can actually cycle, the puparazzi - a local resident - spotted the cute doggy on a wheeled bike trailer near North Cross roundabout in Plymouth last night.

Plymstock lady Becky Cooke shared the image, which Plymouth Live has received permission to post, and said: "Had to look twice...good job I wasn't driving as I thought for a minute it was Santa and his reindeer"

"But seriously? What on Earth! This poor dog strapped in and taken on a busy road. Tbf [to be fair], he looked like he was having a fab time but [I've] never seen anything like it," she added.

Local residents were divided, with some laughing at the dog and suggesting he enjoys the journey, while others were worried about the cyclist's lack of protective gear - and the impact this could have on the pet.

One resident said: "[I] see this guy everywhere! That dog does love it!"

Another agreed: "Dog was loving it!"

But another local asked: "Where's his helmet [and] hi-vis or reflective gear?"

"By not making himself more visible he puts the dog in more danger," she added.
