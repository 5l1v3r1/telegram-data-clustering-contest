Jorginho plays down Chelsea's Premier League title chances

19 Nov 2019, 10:47

Jorginho insists Chelsea are not thinking about challenging for the Premier League title despite being joint second in the table.

Liverpool are eight points clear at the top of the league, with Leicester City and Chelsea the sides closest to the high-flying Reds.

Chelsea’s young guns are being tipped as this season’s dark horses, but Jorginho claims the Blues are concentrating on securing a Champions League spot.

‘The first thing we need to think about is top four,’ Jorginho told VBET News.
