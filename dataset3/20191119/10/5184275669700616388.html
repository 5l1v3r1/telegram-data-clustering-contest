Baaghi 3: Tiger Shroff Soars The Temperature With His Hot & Intense Look

The Heropanti actor who has been shooting for the action thriller in Serbia from a past number of days shared a couple of pictures sporting a new look.

19 Nov 2019, 10:17 by Koimoi.com Team

Bollywood heartthrob Tiger Shroff never misses a chance to make heads turn. The dashing actor who is quite popular on Instagram following his images, dance, and fitness videos, today took on the photo-video sharing app to share his look from his upcoming action thriller Baaghi 3.

The Heropanti actor who has been shooting for the action thriller in Serbia from a past number of days shared a couple of pictures sporting a new look.

Talking about the pictures, the actor can be seen sporting a hot yet intense look. The actor is seen dressed in a sleeveless shirt paired with cargo pants and sunglasses flaunting his dashing looks and well-toned muscles.

Tiger starrer is the third installment to Baaghi franchise. The action-thriller is an official remake of 2012 released Tamil film Vettai.

The film has gorgeous actress Shraddha Kapoor in lead opposite Tiger. The action venture also has versatile actor Riteish Deshmukh in a pivotal role along with Ankita Lokhande, Ashutosh Rana, Nora Fatehi and Annu Kapoor.

The Tiger starrer is been helmed by choreographer turned filmmaker Ahmed Khan, and it is been produced by Sajid Nadiadwala under Nadiadwala Grandson entertainment productions.

The music for the film is been composed by Meet Bros, Tanishk Bagchi, Rochak Kohli, Amaal malik.

It is slated to release in March 2020.
