WATCH: Arthur the brave rhino calf

The true story of a four week old rhino calf who tried to save his mom from the poachers.

19 Nov 2019, 10:19 by Supplied

Arthur’s story of courage and bravery moved the hearts of people all over the world.

Now Arthur and his fellow orphaned rhino friends are helping to teach children about protecting wildlife as well as the power of bravery, courage and hope through the colorfully illustrated children’s book ‘Arthur the Brave Rhino’.
