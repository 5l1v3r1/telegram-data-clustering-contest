Lata Mangeshkar health update: Ace singer likely to return home next week

19 Nov 2019, 10:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Lata Mangeshkar's critical health has been a matter of concern in the past few days as the legendary singer was rushed to Breach Candy hospital last week after she complained of breathing problems.

Many updates from hospital authorities, her relatives, her spokespersons were making headlines. Today, we came across a post shared by a Bollywood producer, Tanuj Garg, who met the ace singer recently. Sharing the good news with the fans, Tanuj posted a health update of the singer. Accordingly, Lata didi is likely to be back home by next week and is doing fine. He wrote, "Delighted that Lata didi is recovering and is likely to return home next week. Please keep your fingers crossed and your prayers alive. A big thank you to the doctors at Breach Candy, especially Dr. Samdani, Dr. Janardan & Dr. Sharma." He also thanked the doctor, who took special care of the singer.
