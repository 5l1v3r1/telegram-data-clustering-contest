General Election 2019: Latest news Boris Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn set to go head-to-head in ITV debate tonight

19 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Natasha Clark

BORIS Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn are set to go head-to-head in the first debate of the general election tonight.

The two leaders will battle it out during an hour-long ITV debate from 8pm - with the PM expected to grill Mr Corbyn on Brexit.

Mr Johnson has said Brits have “a right to know” where the Labour boss stands on the major issues which he says his rival for No10 has “ducked".
