Bala Box Office: On Its Way To The 100 Crore Club, Topples 9 Movies In Top 100 Grossers

It has entered the list of Koimoi's All Time Grossers which consists of top 100 movies (since 2008).

19 Nov 2019, 10:08 by Koimoi.com Team

Bala Box Office: Bala is all set to become Ayushmann Khurrana’s third movie to enter 100 crore club after Badhaai Ho and Dream Girl but it’s already making huge turn around in the chart of Koimoi’s All Time Grossers.

Bala has completed its 11 days theatrical run and earned 92.99 crores at the ticket windows. It has entered the list of Koimoi’s All Time Grossers which consists of top 100 movies (since 2008). It took over 9 movies including- OMG- Oh My God! (81.50 crores), Brothers (82.47 crores), Veere Di Wedding (83 crores), Fan (85 crores), Aashiqui 2 (85.40 crores), Gabbar Is Back (86 crores), Badla (88.02 crores), Satyameva Jayate (89.05 crores) and Singh Is Bliing (90.25 crores). By tomorrow it will go past Talaash (93 crores) and Luka Chuppi (94.15 crores).
