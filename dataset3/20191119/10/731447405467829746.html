London's All Points East names Caribou, Kelly Lee Owens for 2020 festival

The AEG Presents event returns for its third year at Victoria Park in late May.

19 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Marissa Cetin

All Points East has revealed the first names for its 2020 edition.

The AEG Presents festival will return to Victoria Park in East London for the third year running. Caribou and Kelly Lee Owens feature among the first wave of artists set to play the festival, joined by bands Holy Fuck, Glass Animals, Whitney, Rolling Blackouts Coastal Fever and headliner Tame Impala. The full lineup is still TBA.

With the main event taking place over the late May bank holiday weekend, the 22nd through the 24th, All Points East will also see additional events through the 31st. That includes the free community-focused programme, In The Neighbourhood. It will highlight local Tower Hamlets businesses and organisations for four days of workshops, panels, street food and more.

As Caribou, Dan Snaith recently released the RA-recommended track "Home," the project's first new material in five years.
