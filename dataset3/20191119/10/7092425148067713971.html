Diana Ross to play massive Cork and Dublin gig next summer

Motown legend Diana Ross is set to play summer gigs in Cork and Dublin next year.

The Supremes star will perform at Live At The Marquee in Cork, on Thursday June 25 and at IMMA, Royal Hospital Kilmainham, Dublin on Friday June 26.

Tickets for the concerts go on sale on Monday November 25 at 9am.
