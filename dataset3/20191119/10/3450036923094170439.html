Wapello County has the state's highest unemployment rate again

According to Iowa Workforce Development, the October jobless rate in Wapello County was 4.2%.

That is up slightly from September. Wapello County is the only one of Iowa's 99 counties that has a unemployment rate over four percent.

The good news is that while that is highest jobless rate in the state, it still represents a fairly strong job market.

The rest of the Heartland saw unemployment rates between around three percent.
