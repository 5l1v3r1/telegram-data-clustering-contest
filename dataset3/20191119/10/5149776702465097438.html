Man believed to be rough sleeper dies after being found in alleyway next to the Hilton Hotel

A police cordon remains at the scene

19 Nov 2019, 10:17 by Joseph Locker

A man who is believed to have been rough sleeping has died after being found in an alleyway next to the Hilton Hotel in Nottingham city centre.

Police officers were called to assist paramedics from East Midlands Ambulance Service at around 8.20am on Tuesday, November 19 after a man collapsed.

The man was rushed to the Queen's Medical Centre for treatment but was later pronounced dead.

A cordon remains at the scene, just off Milton Street.
