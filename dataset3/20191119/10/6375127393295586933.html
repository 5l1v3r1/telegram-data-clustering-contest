Ex-NPA member slain

19 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Zaida Delos Reyes

A former member of  New Peoples’ Army (NPA) was shot dead by unidentified suspect inside a school compound the other day in  Roxas, Palawan.

Police Regional Office (PRO) MIMAROPA  spokesman Lt.Col. Aries Faltado identified the victim as Albert Jerome Carceler Misajon, 29, farmer, former rebel, with live-in partner and a resident of Barangay Magara, Roxas, Palawan.

Reports reaching Camp Crame showed that the incident occurred at around  7:30 am  inside Capalan Elementary School  located at Sitio Capalan, Barangay Magara in the said municipality.

Initial investigation showed that the victim was onboard his motorcycle  near a waiting shed when three unidentified suspects appeared and shot him repeatedly.

Although wounded the victim managed to run inside the campus but was chase by the suspects and continued shooting him.

The victim died on the spot due to multiple gunshot wounds in his body while the suspects fled towards unknown direction.

Over in Sinait, Ilocos Sur, unidentified armed men ambushed a barangay captain, killing his companion last night along Bgy. road in Jordan.  

Police report showed that Brgy Capt. Leonardo Inofinada and Edward Inofinada, both residence of Bgy. Calingayan, Sinait were aboard their motorcycle when they were and suddenly shot by unknown gunmen.

Police saidt the victims were onboard TMX Honda with side car when they were shot successively by unidentified suspects.

The suspects then swifly fled.

The victims sustained multiple gunshot wounds and were rushed by PNP personnel Sinait at Sto Cristo Milagroso Hospital but Edward Inofinada was declared dead upon arrival by the attending physician. Leonardo Inofinada was transferred to Metro Vigan Hospital.
