Celebs pour in to celebrate Arpita Khan and Aayush Sharma’s anniversary

19 Nov 2019, 10:40 by Vedanshi Pathak

Salman Khan’s sister Arpita Khan Sharma and Aayush Sharma celebrated their fifth wedding anniversary last night. The couple was all smiles as they hosted a big bash at their residence in Mumbai and invited several B-town celebs for the same. Arpita and Aayush have a kid, Ahil, too and the duo dated for long before tying the knot five years back. The party hosted by the duo had the who’s who of Bollywood stepping in looking fabulous and chic in their best attires. 

Our lenses snapped Ilulia Vantur, Katrina Kaif, Salman Khan, Sohail Khan, Salim Khan, Helen, Salma Khan, Zaheer Iqbal, Huma Qureshi, Jacqueline Fernandez, Daisy Shah, Sanjay Kapoor, Seema Khan, Shilpa Shetty Kundra, Raj Kundra, Karisma Kapoor, Arbaaz Khan, Georgia Andriani,  Arhaan Khan, Amrita Arora Ladakh, Alvira Agnihotri, Atul Agnihotri, and many other celebs at the bash.
