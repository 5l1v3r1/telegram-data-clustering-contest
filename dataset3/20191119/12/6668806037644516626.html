Bala continues its glorious run at the cash counters

19 Nov 2019, 12:10 by Vedanshi Pathak

Ayushmann Khurrana, Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam’s latest release is doing well at the cash counters. Bala is an out-and-out comedy film with a dash of romance. Bala (Ayushmann Khurrana) faces challenges in his love life as he starts balding prematurely. This creates a void in his life as the man feels under confident and has little or no hope about finding a life partner. The film touches on the importance of self-love and takes the audience on a fun ride. 

After raking in Rs 72.24 crore in its first week, the film continued earning well during its second week at the movies too. On its second Friday, the film earned Rs 3.76 crore, followed by Rs 6.73 crore on Saturday, Rs 8.01 crore on Sunday and finally Rs 2.25 crore on Monday, making the total a whopping Rs 92.99 crore. The film is expected to enter the Rs 100 crore club this weekend. We congratulate the entire team on their massive success.
