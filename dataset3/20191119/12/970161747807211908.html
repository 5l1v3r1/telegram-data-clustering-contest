Jeffree Star takes birthday celebrations to Bora Bora as he documents sex mishap

19 Nov 2019, 12:13

Jeffree Star is going all out for his 34th birthday celebrations, jetting off to Bora Bora for some hardcore self-care.

Why not?

The YouTube star, who has previously been embroiled in controversies, shared a snap of himself lazing in a hammock, with a stunning tropical backdrop.

‘Hi #BoraBora How Are Ya?,’ he wrote. ‘Just landed in paradise to celebrate my birthday all week and I’m so happy to be back to my favorite place on the planet.’

Unable to resist a bit of a plug, he added: ‘PS. The next episode aka the finale of my series w @shanedawson will be out this week!’

However, not everything has been smooth sailing, with the star revealing on Twitter he’d had a bit of an unconventional mishap.

‘Just broke a nail while having sex [crying emoji],’ he wrote. ‘Let’s pray this island has super glue.’

Thankfully, he managed to sort the situation pretty quickly, tweeting: ‘Nail Update: They had one bottle of superglue on the entire island, let’s pray it holds.’

The drama of it all.

Sharing other snaps from his idyllic holiday, the beauty guru enjoyed a huge breakfast, before showing his boyfriend Nate Schwandt going for a dip in the sea with a flamingo inflatable and giving us major travel envy.

Ah, the life of a YouTube millionaire.

The snaps come after Jeffree celebrated his birthday at home with his best pal Shane Dawson, decorating his home with bright pink decorations, including a huge pink marijuana leaf that took pride of place.

However, the YouTube besties recently faced some backlash over their series, with some fans branding it ‘nothing more than a huge promotion’ for Shane’s new eyeshadow palette.

Shane revealed there will be just one more episode from the project, saying: ‘Working on the last epsiode of the jeffree series now. i don’t wanna give a day cause i don’t wanna disappoint anyone if it’s not ready in time but just know me and andrew are working our hardest to make it good and to end it right.’

While it’s not clear what exactly the final episode will show, the original plan was for a nine-episode series and the trailer showed a lot more than we’ve currently seen (including Shane’s reaction to the James Charles and Tati Westbrook drama).

‘This felt like it was nothing more than a huge promotion for your palette,’ another added.
