Nintendo details Mario Kart Tour’s Winter tour

19 Nov 2019, 12:27 by My Nintendo News Admin

The Winter tour in the popular smartphone title Mario Kart Tour will be starting later today and Nintendo has rather handily produced a video staring Lakitu showing what you can expect once the event begins at 10pm PT. You’ll be able to play the DK Pass track from Mario Kart DS and unlock the following characters: Santa Mario, Pink Gold Peach and Black Shy Guy. There’s also new karts and gliders for you to unlock.
