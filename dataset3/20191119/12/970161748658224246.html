Hollyoaks star Stephanie Davis reveals high-functioning autism diagnosis

19 Nov 2019, 12:52

Hollyoaks star Stephanie Davis has revealed she’s been diagnosed with high-functioning autism.

The actress — who plays Sinead O’Connor in the Channel 4 soap — took to Twitter on Monday to inform her follows that she was going to talk about her recent diagnosis in a new video.

She uploaded the clip onto YouTube — which will be the first of a new on-going video blog.
