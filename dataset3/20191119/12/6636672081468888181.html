Schottky rectifiers up to 60V 15A for automotive

19 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Steve Bush

Taiwan Semiconductor has introduced 45V and 60V trench Schottky rectifiers in a TO-277A-compatible SMPC4.6U package, and qualified to AEC-Q101 for the automotive market.

The surface-mount package is only 1.1mm high and has wettable flanks for automated optical inspection.

The parts are TSUPxM45SH or TSUPxM60SH, depending on whther the current rating is 45A or 60A, and the ‘x’ needs to be replaced with 5 for 5A parts, 10 for 10A or 15 for 15A.

Operation is with the junction at up to 175°C – maximum leakage current is 20mA at high (unspecified) temperature.

The series is RoHS compliant and halogen free.

Applications are foreseen in switching mode power supplies, lighting, automotive chargers and for Reverse polarity protection.
