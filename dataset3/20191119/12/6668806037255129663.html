Deepika Padukone calls husband Ranveer Singh her super drug

19 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Filmfare

If you are someone who loves Bollywood romances just like us, then we have some interesting update for you on Ranveer Sigh and Deepika Padukone. Winning us with their romance yet again, this time it’s their social media PDA which has got all our attention. While usually, we see Singh flirting with his ladylove and leaving cutesy comments, this time DP is the one who has done it.

It all happened when the actress took to her social media and posted a picture of Ranveer’s t-shirt that read, “Love is a Super Power”. The snap featured only Ranveer’s back, Deepika captioned it as, “& you...my super drug!”

Meanwhile, the couple recently celebrated their first wedding anniversary in Tirupati and Amritsar. They first visited Venkateswara Temple, Tirumala, and then the Golden Temple where they were accompanied by their families including Ranveer’s parents, sister Ritika Bhavnani and Deepika's parents and sister Anisha Padukone. 

On the work front, Ranveer and Deepika will be next seen in Kabir Khan's '83. While the actor will portray the role of cricket legend Kapil Dev, Deepika will play his wife, Romi Bhatia. Other than this, Mrs Singh will also be seen in Meghna Gulzar's Chhapaak alongside Vikrant Massey.
