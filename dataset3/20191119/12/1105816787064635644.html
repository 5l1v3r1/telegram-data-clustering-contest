General Bajwa discusses regional security with Iranian commanders

19 Nov 2019, 12:14 by News Desk

Chief of the Army Staff (COAS) General Qamar Javed Bajwa met members of Iran’s top military leadership on Tuesday.

In a statement, DG ISPR Major General Asif Ghafoor said COAS Bajwa held discussions with Admiral Ali Shamkhani, the secretary of Iran’s Supreme National Security Council, and Iranian army chief Major General Abdul Rahim Mousavi.
