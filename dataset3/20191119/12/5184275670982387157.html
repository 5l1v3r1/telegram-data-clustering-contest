Allu Arjun Takes Trip Down Memory Lane, Shares A Picture With Grandpa Allu Ramalingaiah & Cousins

Talking about the picture, one gets to see Allu Arjun along with his cousins Ram Charan, Allu Sirish and Varun Tej posing for the camera.

19 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Koimoi.com Team

Tollywood heartthrob Allu Arjun is in news all over following his upcoming release Ala Vaikunthapuramloo. The superstar who is quite active on Instagram took some time out today morning to walk down the memory lane, as he shared an old picture featuring his grandfather Allu Ramalingiah and cousins.

The Arya 2 actor along with the picture wrote a caption that read: We all went to the airport to receive my grandfather when he was honoured with Padma Shri. From Palakol to Padma Shri… what a journey! #alluramalingaiah #padmashri #memories

Talking about the picture, one gets to see Allu Arjun along with his cousins Ram Charan, Allu Sirish and Varun Tej posing for the camera.

Arjun’s grandfather can also be seen with all smiles after receiving a warm welcome with a garland around his neck.

Talking about Arjun, the actor is busy filming songs of his much in talks and much-anticipated release Ala Vaikunthapuramloo.

Talking about Ala Vaikunthapuramloo the film has gorgeous actress Pooja Hegde in lead opposite Allu Arjun in lead.

So far the posters, first glimpse, and songs have been well received by the audience.

Ala Vaikunthapuramloo has been helmed by Trivikram Srinivas and it is been produced under Geetha Arta and Haarika & Haasine Creations.

The music for Allu Arjun starrer is been composed by S.Thaman.

The film will release on 12th January 2020 in Telugu and Malayalam languages.
