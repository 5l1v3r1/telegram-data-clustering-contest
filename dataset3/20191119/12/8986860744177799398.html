Angry Rev. Obofour blast and curses editor who edited his videos

19 Nov 2019, 12:26 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

The leader and founder of Anointed Palace Chapel Rev. Obofour some few hours ago was reported on some portals in the country to have said that he wouldn’t work for God to take the glory.

After the publication, some sections of social media users took to social media to bash him for making such a comment regarded as blasphemous.

Well, the rich pastor has himself reacted to the video which has since gone viral stating that none of the content is true.

According to Rev. Obofour who is also known as Iron Boy has stated that the said rival video has been edited by some of his colleagues’ pastors who want his downfall to taint his hard earn reputation.
