Vinka works with Inna on love song

19 Nov 2019, 12:22 by NATHAN ATILUK

A few months back, Vinka through her social media platforms said she would be releasing new music every month, and the former talent manager seems to mean business.

After recently signing with Sony Entertainment, Vinka released her November serving last week. The song, Bebe, features Inna, a Romanian singer-songwriter with huge following.

Bebe is a love song, a bit of stepping out of her comfort zone for the dancehall artiste. Vinka’s vocals on the song are, however, limited; the bigger percentage is dominated by Inna.

Vinka, who speaks English, Kiswahili, Luganda and French, said during the release at Swangz Avenue offices in Industrial Area that Bebe is about love for one another and love for a community. The video is so good it reminded me of Game of Thrones with some scenes shot in dark places as if they were hiding from ghosts.

Well, wait for Vinka’s December unveiling…
