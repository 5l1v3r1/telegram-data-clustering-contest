Former world champion boxer paints statue portrait of Celtic legend McNeill

19 Nov 2019, 12:55 by David Fowler

FORMER two-time world champion boxer Scott Harrison has painted a portrait of the iconic Billy McNeill statue outside Celtic Park.

The ex-WBO featherweight champ traded in the gloves for the paintbrush as he painted various sporting icons.

Celtic fans have been blown away by one of his latest pieces which captures the iconic Billy McNeill statue outside Parkhead.
