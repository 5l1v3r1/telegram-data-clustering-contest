An Original HSM Star Is Coming to 'High School Musical: The Musical: The Series' - But Which One?

19 Nov 2019, 12:54

If you missed this bit of news, it was revealed and confirmed that one of the original High School Musical stars is indeed coming to High School Musical: The Musical: The Series.

During his interview with TVLine, showrunner Tim Federle opened up about the original star popping up in a big fantasy number within the show.

And by the looks of the track listing for the soundtrack, it looks like it’s Ryan Evans! We mean, Lucas Grabeel!

The soundtrack, which is set to be out in full in January, features a song called “Role of a Lifetime” with Lucas on it, along with Miss Jenn, played by Kate Reinders.

Looks like it is Lucas after all! We’re so excited to see him again!
