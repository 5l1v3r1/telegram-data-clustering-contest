Armed police swoop on Fife house as man, 29, is arrested

19 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Aidan Smith

ARMED police swooped on a home in Lochgelly this morning as one man was arrested.

Specialist cops entered the residence on Lumphinnans Road earlier today after reports of an alleged disturbance.

Emergency services were called to the scene at 9:10am.

A 29-year-old man has been arrested following the alleged incident.

He is expected to appear in court tomorrow.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "Police Scotland are in attendance on Lumphinnans Road Lochgelly following reports of a disturbance at 9:10am this morning.

"The alleged incident is contained and there is no risk to the outer public.

"Residents are thanked for their understanding and patience.

"A 29-year-old man has been arrested and is expected to appear at Dunfermline Sheriff Court tomorrow."
