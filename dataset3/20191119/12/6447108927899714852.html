Nintendo Switch Cyber Deals promotion begins on Friday

19 Nov 2019, 12:13 by Aran Suddi

Nintendo has confirmed that on November 22nd the Cyber Deals sale will begin on the Switch eStore with over 150 games that will have price cuts. Those games include The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Super Mario Odyssey, and Xenoblade Chronicles 2 which will all be at 30% off the regular retail price.

Below are the deals that Nintendo have highlighted:

Super Mario Odyssey – 30% off
The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild – 30% off
Cadence of Hyrule – Crypt of the NecroDancer Featuring The Legend of Zelda – 30% off
New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe – 30% off
Dragon Quest Builders 2 – 30% off
Xenoblade Chronicles 2 – 30% off
EA SPORTS FIFA 20 Nintendo Switch Legacy Edition – 30% off
DARK SOULS: Remastered – 40% off
Dragon Ball FighterZ – 70% off
Wolfenstein: Youngblood Deluxe Edition – 50% off
Monster Hunter Generations Ultimate – 50% off

The sale will be running until December 1st so plenty of time to browse all of the games that will be discounted.
