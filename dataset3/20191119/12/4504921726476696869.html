Mauritius collaborates with international investigators on Fishrot case

19 Nov 2019, 12:15

Following the online publication of the 'Fishrot Files', the Mauritian Ministry of Financial Services and Good Governance has decided to shed light on the matter.
The article reports alleged cases of corruption in two African countries, where the Mauritian jurisdiction and societies of Global Business were mentioned.
According to the minister of Financial Services, Mauritius is collaborating with an international investigation for over a year now, to get to the bottom of these allegations.
