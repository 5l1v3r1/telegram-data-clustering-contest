Lauren Jauregui & Sofia Carson Set To Host AMAs Red Carpet Live This Weekend!

19 Nov 2019, 12:16

Sofia Carson and Lauren Jauregui were just announced as two of the hosts for the AMAs Red Carpet Live!

The two singers will join AJ Gibson, Jaymes Vaughan, Raja Kumari and Nick Viall on the red carpet for the official pre-show, presented by Security Benefit, at the 2019 American Music Awards this weekend,

The two-hour pre-show will stream LIVE from Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles on Sunday, November 24th @ 6PM ET/ 3PM PT on the official American Music Awards Twitter account.
