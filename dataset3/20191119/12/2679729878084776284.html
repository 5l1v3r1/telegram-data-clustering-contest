KenGen invites bids for the rehabilitation of power lines

19 Nov 2019, 12:10 by Babalwa Bungane

Deadline date: 03 December 2019

Kenya Electricity Generating Company (KenGen) hereby invites eligible bidders to tender for rehabilitation of 11kV and 415V power supply lines.

Interested bidders may obtain further information from the office of the Supply Chain Director, during normal working days.

Tel: (254) (020) 3666230

Email: tenders@kengen.co.ke

Tender documents may be collected upon payment of a non-refundable fee of KShs 1,000 ($10) paid in cash or through a banker's cheque at any KenGen finance office.

The documents can also be viewed and downloaded from the website www.kengen.co.ke and www.suppliers.treasury.go.ke.

For more tenders click here

Bidders are advised to refer to the website regularly for any additional information/clarifications/addenda.

Tenders must be accompanied by a Tender Security /Tender Securing Declaration Form where applicable in the form and amount specified and must be delivered in plain sealed envelope clearly labelled with the tender number and tender description before 03 December 2019 to the following address:

Company Secretary & Legal Affairs Director

Kenya Electricity Generating Company PLC,

10th Floor, KenGen Pension Plaza 2,

Kolobot Road, Parklands,

P.O. Box 47936, 00100

Nairobi
