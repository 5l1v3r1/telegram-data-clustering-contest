LINE FAIL

Virgin Media broadband and website down leaving customers without access to internet

19 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Levi Winchester

VIRGIN Media broadband is down this morning with frustrated customers unable to get online.

Customers are also unable to check the My Virgin website for a service update due to "essential" maintenance work being done on the page.

At 10am, outage tracker Downdetector reported almost 300 complaints from customers about their Virgin broadband.

A map on the website appears to show reports of Virgin broadband being down in several areas of the UK.

We've asked Virgin if it's aware of the issue and how many customers are affected and we'll update this story when we get a response.

Irritated customers have been taking to Twitter to complain about their lack of service.
