PICS: Harry Styles transformed into a ballerina – and we love it!

19 Nov 2019, 12:26 by Lesego Mkhize

Cape Town - Is there anything Harry Styles can’t do?

The Lights Up singer recently sent his fans into a frenzy after taking to Instagram to share snaps of himself dressed as a ballerina.

In the pics, the 25-year-old is wearing a pink tutu, white tights and executing a series of ballerina poses.

Harry’s post garnered more than two million likes on the social media platform.

"I can NOT WAIT to show my 5-year-old son! He likes his nails painted (like his big sister), and a ring his big sister made him out of elastics," one user commented. "He kept asking me if other boys do those things. I told him it doesn’t matter as long as he likes them. But he is persistent... Thank you Harry for being awesome and setting an amazing example!"

"This is the greatest thing I’ve ever seen," another follower commented.

The pics come after the hitmaker’s appearance on Saturday Night Live, where he also teased One Direction fans, Complex reports.

"I was in a band called One Direction; how crazy would it be if they were here tonight? Well, they’re not. Wouldn’t it be crazy if they were though?" he said.

"They’re not.

"I love those guys; they’re my brothers. Niall, Liam, Louis... and Ringo. Yeah, that’s it."

The 25-year-old revealed on Twitter that his new album titled Fine Line will drop on Friday 13 December.
