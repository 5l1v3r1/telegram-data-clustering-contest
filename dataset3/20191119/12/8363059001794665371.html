Black Friday at Oppo Saves You up to a Third off Flagship Handsets

19 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Shabana Arif

The Black Friday sales are coming thick and fast, and Oppo joins the fray with big discounts on its mid-range smartphones.

The star of the show is the Oppo Reno 10x Zoom that's down from £699 to £459, with a huge £190 discount. Oppo's flagship smartphone launched earlier this year and features the shark fin pop-up camera, a 6.6-inch display, and houses a Snapdragon 855 chipset. Both the black and green models are discounted.

The Reno has gotten a pretty big chunk of its price gouged out too, with a very tempting Black Friday price tag of £279, saving you £120. Oppo's RX17 Pro, geared towards snapping nighttime photos, is on sale for £249, saving you £100, while the cheaper Reno Z and RX17 Neo are reduced to £199 (£100 off) and £159 (£66 off) respectively.

If you're looking to pick up a solid mid-range handset, we'd recommend the Oppo Reno 10x Zoom, but they're are all decent enough little phones, and you really can't beat these prices. All of the deals - with the exception of the RX17 Neo are also available at the Carphone Warehouse.
