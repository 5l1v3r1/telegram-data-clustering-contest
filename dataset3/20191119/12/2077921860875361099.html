Angel Olsen Adds New Dates to All Mirrors Tour

19 Nov 2019, 12:56 by Amanda Gersten  

Each of Angel Olsen’s albums has met with its fair share of praise, but her newest effort, All Mirrors, seems slated to make her a legend. Paste, for one, called the searing, orchestral record “unparalleled” and “a late-stage contender for rock album of the decade.” Suffice it to say that Olsen’s All Mirrors tour is one not to be missed. It’s a lucky day for her fans living in the southeastern U.S., with seven new dates announced in the region for April of 2020.

Olsen has also teamed up with Spotify to bring an “immersive audio journey” to Brooklyn on Nov. 23. It’s not clear whether Olsen will make an appearance, but a press release calls the installation a “labyrinth of otherworldly spaces and sounds celebrating the visual inspirations behind the ‘All Mirrors’ video and highlighting the sonic layers of the title track.” You can RSVP for that here.

Check out all of Olsen’s tour dates below. Tickets will go on sale Friday, Nov. 22, at 10 a.m. local time.
