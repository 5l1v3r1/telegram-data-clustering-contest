Golden jubilee festival of VMA from November 22

A galaxy of artistes will perform in the 10-day festival, say organisers

19 Nov 2019, 12:39 by VISAKHAPATNAM

The golden jubilee festival of Visakha Music Academy (VMA) will be held at the VMRDA Auditorium at Siripuram here from November 22 to December 1.

Speaking to the media, VMA vice-president K.V. Rama Murthy and secretary M.S. Srinivas said that the VMA, which was established in 1969, was promoting the cause of classical music and dance in the city by organising monthly music concerts, youth festival in June and annual festival by nationally renowned artistes.

The title ‘Sangeeta Kala Sagara’ was being bestowed during the annual festival. The first-ever title was conferred on the noted singer Mangalampalli Balamurali Krishna. The title was given away to 62 artistes so far. Hyderabad Brothers – D. Seshachary and D. Raghavachary – have been chosen to receive the award this year.

VMA also presents Endowment Awards – MS Subbulakshmi, Nedunuri, Balamuralikrishna, Origala Veera Raghava Sarma and so on.

A galaxy of artistes would participate in the 10-day festival. Rajhesh Vaidhya (veena), Dr. Soma Ghosh (Hindustani vocal) and Ramakrishna Murthy and Manasi Prasad (vocal) would be showcasing their talents during the festival.

On November 25, ‘Artist of distinction’ award would be presented to violinist H. Ramcharan and awards to the artistes, who had performed in the youth festival held in June this year. Scholarships would be presented to needy students pursuing music.

Earlier, a brochure of the event was released by Mr. Ram Murthy, Mr. Srinivas, treasurer V. Chandrasekhar and joint secretary A. Bhujanga Rao.
