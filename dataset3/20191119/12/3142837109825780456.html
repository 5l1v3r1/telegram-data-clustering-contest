EATON VANCE MUN/SHS (ETX) To Go Ex-Dividend on November 21st

19 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Jessica Moore

EATON VANCE MUN/SHS (NYSE:ETX) declared a monthly dividend on Friday, November 1st, Zacks reports. Shareholders of record on Friday, November 22nd will be given a dividend of 0.071 per share by the investment management company on Friday, November 29th. This represents a $0.85 dividend on an annualized basis and a yield of 3.97%. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Thursday, November 21st.

NYSE:ETX remained flat at $$21.48 during trading hours on Tuesday. 107 shares of the company’s stock were exchanged, compared to its average volume of 15,410. EATON VANCE MUN/SHS has a 12 month low of $18.16 and a 12 month high of $22.84. The company’s 50-day moving average is $21.52 and its 200 day moving average is $21.09.

EATON VANCE MUN/SHS Company Profile

Eaton Vance Municipal Income 2028 Term Trust is a closed ended fixed income mutual fund launched and managed by Eaton Vance Management. It invests in the fixed income markets. The fund invest in stocks of companies operating across the diversified sectors. It was previously known as Eaton Vance Municipal Income Term Trust.
