‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’ trailer pits mighty battle between Ajay Devgn and Saif Ali Khan

The movie will see the husband-wife duo of Ajay and Kajol share screen space for the first time in more than a decade

19 Nov 2019, 12:41 by The Hindu Net Desk

The trailer of Ajay Devgn’s 100th film in the Hindi industry, Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior, has been released, and promises to be a treat for fans of war epics. The movie will release in 3D as well, with the makers pulling no stops to ensure it is a spectacular viewing experience.

The movie will see the husband-wife duo of Ajay and Kajol share screen space for the first time in more than a decade, since U Me Aur Hum in 2008. The movie also stars Saif Ali Khan in a prominent role, as well as others like Jagapathi Babu, Pankaj Tripathi and Sharad Kelkar.
