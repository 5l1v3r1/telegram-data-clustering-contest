Liverpool coach Klopp reveals the stadium he hates playing at most

The German coach says Sevilla's Sánchez Pizjuán is his worst nightmare

19 Nov 2019, 12:14 by SPORT.es

Jürgen Klopp usually surprises when he gives interviews or makes comments. The German coach doesn't often bite his tongue when it comes to the big subjects.

The Liverpool coach has spoken to FIFA this week about various topics. One of them was the stadium where he feels most uncomfortable as the opposition. The answer was surprising.

"I don't know if it's the best stadium to play in, but the stadium I hate playing in the most is Sevilla's because of the atmosphere it has... that's a compliment!" he said.

It's a curious comment given Klopp has coached at some of the toughest stadiums in the world. including in England and in Germany in his time with Mainz, Borussia Dortmund and Liverpool.

However, his results at Sevilla's Sanchez Pizjuan have not been good. He's played there with Mainz, Dortmund and Liverpool and has never won. With Liverpool, his side was 3-0 up, but eventually drew 3-3.
