Jane McDonald wants I'm A Celeb bosses to send her into jungle to surprise Adele Roberts

Jane enthused she "loved" the prospect of going into the jungle and she was flattered by Adele's absolute adoration of her

19 Nov 2019, 12:15 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Jane McDonald is hoping I'm A Celeb bosses will send her into the jungle as a surprise for Adele Roberts.

During Monday's jungle installment, Adele shared her luxury item - albeit an unusual choice - was a picture of Loose Woman Jane.

Adele's partner Kate was fawning over Jane saying everyone was hoping she would be sent into the jungle when she appeared on BBC 1 Breakfast with Greg Jones on Tuesday.

Kate said: "We're keeping our fingers crossed for Jane [going in] . She [Adele] won't [come back]. I'll be voting for Jane.

"She's got [book] Riding the waves by Jane McDonald [in lockdown]. It's just been released.

"She has got the audiobook already but she wasn't allowed to take that with her so she's had to take the actual paper copy as well."

Jane enthused she "loved" the idea of going in, flattered by Adele's absolute adoration of her.

Kate wrote back: "Oh Jane, she will ADOOOOOORE this! Thank you so much."

She used the hashtag team Adele as she threw her whole support behind her love.

Jane later added she was "absolutely thrilled" to see her picture in the I'm A Celeb jungle.

She tweeted: "Absolutely thrilled to see my picture in the jungle. @AdeleRoberts I flipping love you.

"Best of luck #ImACeleb ."

Adele's luxury item survived the brutal task which saw the I'm A Celeb camp mates sink many of the comfort packages at a slip of the hand.

The group were tasked with passing each of the boxes containing the celeb's luxury item across the water to each other only using oars.

Only Adele and Kate Garraway's secret parcels were successfully retrieved.

Kate was ecstatic she would be reunited with her concealed for her time in the jungle.
