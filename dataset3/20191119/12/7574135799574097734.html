Ween add more tour dates, playing New Haven before NYC

19 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Andrew Sacher

Ween continue to gradually announce tour dates, and they just added a New Haven show happening February 13 at College Street Music Hall, which is directly before their two previously announced NYC shows (2/14 & 2/15 at Terminal 5). Tickets for the New Haven show go on sale Thursday (11/21) at 10 AM. Tickets for the NYC shows are still available.

Ween also recently announced a three-night ‘March Mitzvah’ run at Las Vegas’ Brooklyn Bowl, happening 3/19-3/21 (three-day and single-day passes), and they have a previously-announced Boston/Philly run in December. Updated dates are listed below.
