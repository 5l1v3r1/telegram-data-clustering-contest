Video: Asterix & Obelix XXL3: The Crystal Menhir launches 21st November

19 Nov 2019, 12:10 by My Nintendo News Admin

The long-awaited next entry in the Asterix series of video games Asterix & Obelix XXL3: The Crystal Menhir has finally been given a release date of 21st November. Today, the team at Microids have put together a snazzy launch trailer for the game which you can view below showing Asterix and Obelix in action taking on those pesky Romans. Enjoy!
