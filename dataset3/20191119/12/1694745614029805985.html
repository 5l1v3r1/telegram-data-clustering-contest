Lady Gaga wearing a traditional, blush-tone bridesmaid dress is perhaps her most surprising style maneuver to date. The singer and actress recently attended the wedding of her friend and makeup artist Sarah Tanno in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. For the beachy wedding, Gaga went with a romantic slip dress by Jonathan Simkhai featuring a thigh-high slit with adjustable hook closures.

What helped Gaga make the look more — for lack of a better word — Gaga is her matching pink hair, which she's had since early October.
