Strictly's Saffron Barker's family home burgled while they slept

19 Nov 2019, 12:44

Strictly Come Dancing’s Saffron Barker has opened up about her family’s home being burgled while they slept.

The YouTube star said her car was stolen in the terrifying raid, with her brother Jed hearing someone coming into his room in the middle of the night.

‘It’s been a very stressful morning,’ she admitted in a now-deleted video. ‘My dad woke up this morning like he usually would, went to go outside and go to work and realised that all our cars were gone.

‘So basically our cars have been stolen. We got broken into last night and stuff inside our house was taken, like handbags and shoes as well.
