Frustrated Man Utd midfielder ready to move on amid Juventus links

19 Nov 2019, 12:17

Manchester United starlet Tahith Chong is reportedly looking for a ‘new adventure’ away from Old Trafford.

Chong has entered the final year of his contract at Old Trafford and so far there have been no confirmed noises about a new deal coming from the Red Devils, despite the player admitting the opposite.

The Daily Mail reported earlier this month that Juventus were keeping tabs on the 19-year-old, who predominantly plays as a winger at United.

Any potential move to Juve will provide United fans with flashbacks to when they let Paul Pogba move to Turin for free in 2012 before signing him back for £89million only four years later.

And now Corriere dello Sport (via Sport Witness) claims that Chong’s representatives feel the Dutchman has ‘not been used enough’ this term.

The report adds that they have ‘unsurprisingly started looking around in search of a new adventure’ and are keen to find a club that would ‘help him become a great’.

The report adds that Chong is keen on a potential switch to Juventus and that talks have already taken place between the clubs.

However, it’s claimed that Juve are no the only club keen on Chong and that despite their silence so far, United are still looking to offer the talented youngster a new contract.
