Dr. Mario World getting new stages soon

19 Nov 2019, 12:03 by My Nintendo News Admin

Dr. Mario World for mobile devices is currently experiencing maintenance, but once it is all over, new stages will be added to the smartphone game. These levels are thought to be from World 9 Area 2 and include Stages 341 through 360. No new doctors are thought to be present this time but we shall endeavour to keep you updated if this changes.
