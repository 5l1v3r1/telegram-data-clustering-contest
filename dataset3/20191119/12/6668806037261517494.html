Rani Mukerji talks about Mardaani 2 and why it makes her anxious

19 Nov 2019, 12:42 by Filmfare

Rani Mukerji is one powerful actress who is always up for experimenting with her roles. This has definitely worked in her favour as she’s delivered some power-packed performances in the last few years. Now the award winning actress is all set to be back on the big screen this December with the awaited sequel, Mardaani 2. Rani continues to play the role she played in 2014, Shivani Shivaji Roy and this time she’s out there to find a serial rapist. The trailer is dark, edgy and gripping. One look at the trailer and we know, we’re in for yet another thrilling drama.

In an interview to a portal, Rani said that she’s not nervous that the sequel might draw comparisons with her original Mardaani, which released in 2014. Here’s what she has to say, "It doesn't make me nervous, but it definitely makes me anxious. I am just happily anxious to know that the trailer has been received very well. That's the testament to the fact that the love for Shivani Shivaji Roy has been there all this while and they are liking the story, the Mardaani feel and the thriller vibe.”

Rani Mukerji surprised us last year with her act in Hitchki and this year, she’s out with Mardaani 2. Well, we can't wait to witness her magic on big screen once again.
