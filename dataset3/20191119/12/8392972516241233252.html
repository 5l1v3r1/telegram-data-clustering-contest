Coronation Street viewers upset over 'sick' robbery plot as burglar steals Daniel's laptop during Sinead's funeral

Daniel's laptop had precious video's of Sinead on it

19 Nov 2019, 12:35 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Viewers of Coronation Street have slammed the "sick" robbery plot as Daniel's laptop with videos of Sinead were stolen during her funeral.

In last night's episode of the ITV soap (November 18 2019), Sinead's family and friends, including her husband Daniel, gathered to say goodbye to their loved one.

Sinead passed away a few weeks ago from cancer whilst Daniel read their son Bertie a bedtime story.

During the funeral, a burglar broke into the flat and took Daniel's laptop along with other possessions.

Breaking into a dead woman's flat on the day of her funeral is a bit of a sick storyline.

However, Daniel's laptop had videos of Sinead on it that she filmed for Bertie before she died.

When Daniel returned from the funeral, he was devastated to see his house had been robbed.
