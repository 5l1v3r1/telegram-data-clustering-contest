Father Marries Daughter After ‘Killing Her Boyfriend’

19 Nov 2019, 09:14 by Violet Johnson

A man from Minnesota identified as Larry McClure, 55, and his daughters are accused of murdering one of their boyfriends before having sex and getting married, according to The Sun.

The murder suspect allegedly slaughtered John McGuire, 38, battered him over the head and injected him with crystal meth before dumping his body in West Virginia. He wrote a letter to the court on November 4 admitting his part in the slaying of daughter Amanda McClure’s boyfriend. The letter goes into detail about how McGuire was beaten, injected and strangled.

McClure and his daughters Amanda, 31, and Anna Marie Choudhry, 32, have been charged with murder.

Furthermore, a criminal complaint revealed that just four weeks after the slaying Larry and Amanda married each other. West Virginia State Police Trooper K.M. Saddler said additional charges could be filed.

State police also say the trio buried McGuire’s body shallow grave at McClure’s home in Skygusty of McDowell County.

All three are currently being held in South Central Regional Jail.
