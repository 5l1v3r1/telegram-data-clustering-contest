I’m A Celebrity viewers divided over Adele Roberts' luxury item

She took in a photo of actress Jane McDonald

19 Nov 2019, 09:47 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity are divided over Adele Roberts' luxury item - a photo of actress Jane McDonald - which she received during last night's show.

Kate Garraway, Nadine Coyle, Roman Kemp, Myles Stephenson and Adele competed in a late night challenge to try and win all the stars' luxury items.

The instructions read: "Your job is to get each item from the wheel onto the basket however you can only use the oars. You will win each luxury item that reaches the basket."

The stars had to pass each box - which contained the luxury item - down the row on their oars without dropping it into the swamp below.

However, they only managed to retrieve two of the boxes - Adele's picture of former Loose Women star Jane and Kate's concealer.

While many viewers found Adele's luxury item hilarious, others weren't happy that other campmates lost out on their items such as family photos.

One person said on Twitter: "What a ridiculous choice of item."

Another wrote: "So Kate and Adele get their luxury items which was concealer and a photo of Jane McDonald and all the others didn’t and they were more important gifts? How useless."
