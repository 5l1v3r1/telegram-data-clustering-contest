50 Cent Denies Bullying 'Power' Co-Star Naturi Naughton

19 Nov 2019, 09:37 by Kyle Eustice

50 Cent is denying reports he bullied his Power co-star Naturi Naughton.

The infamous Instagram troll, whose account is currently deactivated, recently found himself on the defense after he posted a meme comparing Naughton to Mortal Kombat character Goro.

But according to a recent tweet, he was actually attempting to stand up for her.

After a fan tweeted, “I thought u was sticking up for her or did I read wrong,” he replied, “I was sticking up for her, they just went and made some shit up.”
