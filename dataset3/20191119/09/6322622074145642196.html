Dr Mahathir says he is healthy, 'blood pressure is good at 120/70'

19 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Nazuin Zulaikha Kamarulzaman

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 19): Finance Minister Lim Guan Eng said today Prime Minister Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad indicated he is in good health and wants to continue working in his office.

In a tweet, Lim said he met Dr Mahathir today to discuss about Malaysia's current issues.

"Tun said not to worry, [his] blood pressure is good (at) 120/70," Lim said.

Earlier today, Bernama, quoting a message by the Prime Minister's Office, reported that Dr Mahathir suffered a slight nose bleed during a press conference here at Malaysian Palm Oil Board International Palm Oil Congress and Exhibition (PIPOC) 2019.

It was reported that he is in good health. "News that he was rushed for medical attention is not true, said a message which the Prime Minister's Office shared with the media," Bernama reported.
