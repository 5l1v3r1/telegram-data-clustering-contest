Teen boy arrested over 'rape' of 14-year-old girl in Glasgow

Enquiries into the alleged incident in the Royston area of the city are currently ongoing.

19 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Peter Davidson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/peter-davidson/

A teenage boy has been arrested over the alleged rape of a 14-year-old girl in Glasgow .

Officers from Police Scotland are probing the alleged serious sexual assault which is believed to have taken place in the Royston area of the city on Saturday , November 16.

Enquiries into the incident are currently ongoing with cops cuffing a 16-year-old male in connection.

A Police Scotland spokesman said: "Officers are carrying out enquiries after receiving a report of a 14-year-old girl having been seriously sexually assaulted in the Royston area of Glasgow on Saturday, November 16.

 

"A 16-year-old male youth has been arrested in connection with the incident and enquiries are ongoing."
