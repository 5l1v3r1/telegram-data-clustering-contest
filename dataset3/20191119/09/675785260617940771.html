Strictly's Michelle Visage hits back at Giovanni Pernice feud claims after exit

Strictly Come Dancing star Michelle Visage has addressed claims of a row with her dance partner Giovanni Pernice

19 Nov 2019, 09:43 by Charlotte Tutton

Strictly Come Dancing 2019 star Michelle Visage has shared her upset over leaving the BBC series this weekend.

She's also addressed rumours of a falling out with her professional dance partner Giovanni Pernice, and claims she "stormed out" of the Blackpool Tower after filming ended.

Viewers were gutted to see the American star leave the BBC series, losing the dance-off to AJ Pritchard and Saffron Barker.

But Michelle faced claims that following the result, she fled the venue - something she has now cleared up, revealing she in fact stayed out dancing with her co-stars until super late.

 

Speaking on Lorraine on Tuesday morning, Michelle also addressed the decision that she would not be part of the 2020 tour.

She said: "Storm out of where? They said I stormed out of Blackpool... did I go in the ocean? Where did I go exactly?

"No, I went to the party until 3am, I was dancing all night with Neil and Alex, Kevin, Oti and Gio. We were just having a really good time."

Michelle added of the rumours: "None of it's true. I’m a party girl and I love people."

On her bond with Giovanni and their vow to continue dancing, Michelle again hit back at reports of a falling out.

She explained: "We have an incredible relationship. This whole thing was so much richer cos of my partnership with Gio.

"I wouldn't want anyone else... I got the partner of my dreams and we'll continue dancing, I called him yesterday. He’s busy doing the pro stuff but I said, 'any hours you have, can we go dancing?'."

After the news she wouldn't appear on the tour next year, Michelle admitted she was gutted but suggested it wasn't as big a deal as it had been reported.

Michelle said: "I wanted to do it. I wasn’t asked to do it. I think there was a complication at the beginning with the dates.

"But at the end of the day it’s going to be a great tour, I am not going to be on it. I would love to. I’m gutted. But again it’s the way it goes."

Lorraine airs weekdays at 8:30am on ITV.
