Tanhaji Trailer Launch: Ajay Devgn Reveals Why Kajol Is Missing From The Event- Parent Duty Calls!

19 Nov 2019, 09:25 by Nikita Dalvi

A few minutes back, trailer of Ajay Devgn, Saif Ali Khan and Kajol starrer, Tanahaji hit the Internet. The stars are currently at the launch event and we are bringing to you all the LIVE updates from ground zero

Ajay Devgn and Saif Ali Khan are returning to the silver screen after a long gap, the last we saw them together was in Omkara (2003). Also, it is Ajay’s 100th film and this one is even special because it has his wife, Kajol in it. However, at the trailer launch, we saw that the beautiful actress was missing.

Of course, we asked Ajay why Kajol didn’t make it to the event. The actor replied, “Kajol had to be in Singapore as either one of the parents had to attend Nysa’s parent-teacher meet.”
