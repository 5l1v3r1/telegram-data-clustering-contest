Tamaasha's latest music video shines light on a dark world

19 Nov 2019, 09:33 by Asfa Sultan

It’s year 2545 and Tamaasha is still not coming slow, as they decide to bring an outer space performance to their latest music video. Roshni. The song is to date, the most motivational one they’ve made, however, when people heard it last year in Battle of the Bands, no one imagined living in a world without oxygen. Nor could they have guessed the five band members would appear as astronauts bringing the planet back to life – literally.
