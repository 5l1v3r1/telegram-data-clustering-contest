“I gave you my heart and put my career on hold but you cheated on me” – Princess Shyngle claps back at her Senegalese ex-boyfriend

19 Nov 2019, 09:05 by Adu Sarpeah

When your heart is broken, it can feel like the end of the world. No amount of pain has ever felt so agonising. It’s like a giant hole was pummelled into your chest, with no hope of repair.

You cry, you scream, you watch Netflix until you’ve seen every documentary your subscription has to offer and yet nothing seems to smooth your heartbreak or soothe the longing you feel.

The above-mentioned scenario has been the experience of Princess Shyngle for the past weeks as her Senegalese boyfriend has treated her fragile heart like garbage.

According to Princess Shyngle in a post on Instagram, she put her career on hold just to love Frederic Badji but she has been paid with broken heart. She, therefore vowed never to return to Frederic Badji even if he chooses to kill himself.

Simply put, the love affair between Princess Shyngle and Frederic Badji has ended on rocks over cheating issues. Interestingly, Princess Shyngle says she is keeping the engagement ring even though their relationship is over for good!
