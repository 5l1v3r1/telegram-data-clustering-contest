‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’ trailer: Fans think Ajay Devgn has met his match in Saif Ali Khan

19 Nov 2019, 09:21 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ajay Devgn has finally launched the trailer of his ambitious film ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’, and we must say, it did not disappoint. Co-starring Saif Ali Khan and Kajol in lead roles, the trailer of the upcoming period drama promises epic battles scenes, great performances, stunning visuals all in 3D.

While Ajay as the protagonist Tanaji Malusare shines through as the brave and heroic Maratha Subedar, it was Saif Ali Khan’s menacing portrayal of Uday Bhan, who stole the show, according to fans on social media.

Many took to Twitter to praise Saif’s performance as the villainous Rajput official working for Mughal emperor Aurangzeb. Some even felt that Ajay has met his match in Saif, with who he is reuniting with on the big screen after almost 12 years. They were last seen together in the 2006 hit film 'Omkara'.
