Nintendo's Tokyo Store Is Set to Open This Friday, And It's Looking Amazing

More merch than you can toss a Pikmin at!

19 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Robert Sephazon

The Parco Store in Shibuya, Tokyo has been closed for renovations since spring of this year. However, that will change this Friday, November 22nd. With its grand reopening, the public will finally have access to the Nintendo Tokyo Store.

Journalists in Japan have recently had the opportunity to go in and explore some of the merch available for consumers, and what they show is absolutely amazing. From every Yoshi colour under the sun, to Kirby bento sets to shirts featuring Pokémon sprites designed out of Mario blocks, there's something here for every Nintendo fan.

We'll be sure to cover the grand opening of the Nintendo Tokyo Store this Friday, so be sure to check back for a detailed review soon!
