Khloé Kardashian In Talks For Brand New Reality Show With Daughter True

19 Nov 2019, 09:53 by Tara Walsh

Khloé Kardashian's new reality show

Khloé Kardashian reveals she's working on her own reality show with her daughter True.

News of the upcoming series emerges during Sunday night's KUWTK.

In the episode, she discusses the new project with her momager Kris.

The pair sit with some producers to, "finalise the show that [she's] developing."

They don't reveal much in the episode but we are shown some images of concept art.

A title is suggested, 'Khloe and True Take the World.'

It's believed the show could arrive in the form of a short digital series.

The producers say they, "Want to shoot and put this on the Internet as soon as possible."

They also suggest making the episodes five to seven minutes long.

Producers think scenes should be filmed at the house and in other places.

It'll show True and Khloé, "out and about," together.

"Letting the world see just little snippets."

While nothing has been confirmed, it'll be interesting to see if Tristan makes an appearance.
