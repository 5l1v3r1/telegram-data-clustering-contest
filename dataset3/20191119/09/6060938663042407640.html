'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior' trailer: Ajay Devgn and Kajol's chemistry is unmissable

19 Nov 2019, 09:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The much-awaited trailer of Ajay Devgn starrer 'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior' is out and it is outstanding! Ajay Devgn shines out as the unsung Maratha warrior- Tanhaji Malusare and Kajol looks spectacular as his wife Savitribai Malusare.

The three minute twenty-one seconds long trailer gives us a gist of the popular battle of Sinhagad aka Kondhana. Ajay's honest attempt to play the brave Tanhaji, Kajol's promising stint and Saif's ferocious avatar is something to watch out for in the film. The grandeur of the film, definitely speak volumes about the Maratha empire and its glory.

These screenshots of Ajay and Kajol showcase their power-packed act and amazing onscreen chemistry as Tanhaji and Savitribai. Kajol's look in a traditional Marathi avatar while Ajay's avatar as a Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's 'mawala' (Maratha soldier) is just unmissable.

When one thinks of Maratha warriors, they think of Shivaji and his brave 'mawlas' (soldiers). Among the other soldiers, one who was very close to Shivaji was Tanhaji Malusare who fought like a lion and sacrificed his life for Swarajya during the battle of Sinhagad. Saif Ali Khan will be seen playing antagonist Uday Bhan, a Rajput official working for Mughal emperor Aurangzeb.

The movie will hit big screens next year on January 10.
