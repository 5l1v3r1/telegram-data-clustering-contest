Bareburger has launched a new vegan menu in the UAE

More plant-based dishes added at the popular chain

19 Nov 2019, 09:40 by Colin Armstrong

It’s a good time to be vegan here in Abu Dhabi and Dubai as more and more places offer plant-based dishes for you to tuck into.

Popular chain Bareburger is no stranger to vegan dishes and was one of the first in the UAE to offer beyond burgers on their menu to cater to vegan diners.

Now, offering you even more choice, the chain has launched a vegan menu packed full of options.

The menu features a range of burgers (obviously) including Beyond patties, a sweet potato, wild rice and kale patty and a black bean and corn patty.

A selection of sandwiches and hot dogs are also on offer featuring beyond bratwurst and other plant-based variations.

You can also chose a range of side options including fries, fries, pickles, green salad and chips and guacamole.

Those with a sweet tooth can also order vegan shakes and desserts including carrot cake, chocolate cake and a vegan sundae.

That’s a lot of options for someone following a plant based diet.

Even if you’re not a vegan, there’s still plenty to be enjoyed.
For more information, visit www.bareburger.com/uae
