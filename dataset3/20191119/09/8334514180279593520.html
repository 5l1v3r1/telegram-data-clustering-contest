Lebanese parliament session postponed: statement

19 Nov 2019, 09:14

BEIRUT (Reuters) - A Lebanese parliamentary session set for Tuesday was postponed until a time that will be decided later, a statement from the legislature said, citing that a quorum was not reached.

Protesters have sought to prevent members of parliament from attending the session and there was a heavy security presence in the streets surrounding parliament.
