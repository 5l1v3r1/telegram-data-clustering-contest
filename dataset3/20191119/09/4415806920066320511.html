From denim miniskirts to I’m A Celeb, Nadine Coyle’s style has come a long way

19 Nov 2019, 09:17

Nadine Coyle has only been in the jungle for a night, but she’s already gaining fans on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here!

She’s made friends with Caitlyn Jenner, faced one of her major fears – skydiving – and looks all set to do well on the show.

Coyle was discovered by Louis Walsh on the Irish version of the reality TV show Popstars, and in 2002 formed girl group Girls Aloud with Cheryl, Sarah Harding, Nicola Roberts and Kimberley Walsh.
