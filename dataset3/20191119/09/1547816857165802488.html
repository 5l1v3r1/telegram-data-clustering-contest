Tanhaji - The Unsung Warrior Trailer Launch: Ajay Devgn, Rohit Shetty, Saif Ali Khan Make A Powerful Entry

19 Nov 2019, 09:10 by Saheli Maity

The stars of Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior Ajay Devgn, Saif Ali Khan and Sharad Kelkar look dapper at the trailer launch of their film. Along with the star cast, we also see the producer, Bhushan Kumar and Ajay’s best friend and Singham’s director Rohit Shetty.
