King Krule shares “Hey World!,” touring in 2020, playing Kings Theatre (BrooklynVegan presale)

19 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Bill Pearis

King Krule hasn’t released an album since 2017’s The Ooz, but things are stirring in his camp. He’s just released a short film titled “Hey World!” which has Archy Marshall performing solo, playing four as-yet-unreleased tracks, titled “Perfecto Miserable,” “Alone, Omen 3,” “(Don’t Let The Dragon) Draag On,” and “Energy Fleets.” Watch that below.

Meanwhile, he’ll be heading to North America in the spring that starts April 2 in Dallas and wraps up at Brooklyn’s Kings Theatre on April 24. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday, November 22 at 10 AM but you can get them early with the BrooklynVegan presale that starts Thursday (11/21) at 10 AM. Check back Thursday morning for the presale password.

The April tour puts King Krule in the U.S. around the same time as Coachella who have not announced their 2020 lineup yet (though Rage Against the Machine are playing).
