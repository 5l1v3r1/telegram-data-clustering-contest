How Would Ofori’s R500k Beemer Fit In At Naturena?

19 Nov 2019, 09:41 by SL Social

With speculations that Kaizer Chiefs are keeping a close eye on Ghanaian number one goalkeeper Richard Ofori, would the Maritzburg United man and his R500 000 BMW fit in at Naturena?

Ofori has been the Ghanaian number one for the last couple of years, and kept a clean sheet for the Black stars when they eased past Bafana Bafana in a recent AFCON 2021 qualifier.

The lanky 26-year-old has been a mainstay for Maritzburg and has been pivotal in their recent resurgence. He was also a catalyst in seeing the Team of Choice maintain their PSL status.
