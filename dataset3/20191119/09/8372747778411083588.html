Police investigating reports of man exposing himself near school bus stop in Cape Coral

19 Nov 2019, 09:44 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Officers are monitoring a situation in a Cape Coral neighborhood where students say a man was exposing himself near a school bus stop.

According to a Cape Coral Police report, parents in an undisclosed neighborhood say several students have been complaining about a man exposing himself and making sexual comments to juveniles at the bus stop this month.

The neighbor is accused of coming outside in a robe and exposing himself on several occasions, and leaving his front door open while masturbating in view of children.

One parent reportedly approached the neighbor about the behavior, but the inappropriate behavior continued.

An officer attempted to contact the neighbor, but he refused to answer the door.

Police say a carryover will be placed at the bus stop for the next two weeks.
