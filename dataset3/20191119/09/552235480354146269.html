Person hit by gritter in East Lothian

The person's condition is not yet known.

19 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Kathleen Speirs

A pedestrian has been hit by a gritter in East Lothian .

The person was struck on Court Street in Haddington on Monday.

 

It's believed the incident took place not long after 6pm.

Gritters were out last night as sub-zero temperatures swept across the country.

Police Scotland confirmed the incident on Tuesday morning.

Further details are yet to be confirmed by officers.

A spokeswoman from the force told Record: "We can confirm the incident on Court Street, Haddington."
