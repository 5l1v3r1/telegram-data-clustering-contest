Megan Barton Hanson 'can't even look at a d***' as she rules out dating men

19 Nov 2019, 09:58

Megan Barton Hanson can’t see herself ever dating another man – or even watching porn involving guys.

The Love Island star came out as bisexual after her split from Wes Nelson in January, and has since dated Towie star Demi Sims and singer songwriter Chelcee Grimes.

But despite having dated men in the past, it seems Megan is leaning more towards women for future relationships.

Speaking on The Truth Flirts podcast, hosted by Mel B in partnership with Badoo, the 25-year-old said: ‘I can’t ever see myself getting back with a guy. Even now when I watch porn, it’s only lesbian porn, I can’t even look at a d***.’

Mel, 45, laughed: ‘Lesbian porn is more sensual, I think anyway. It’s nicer and kinder.’

Both stars have dated women in the past, and discussed how being in a same-sex relationship differs from being in a heterosexual partnership.

Spice Girl Mel, who previously dated Christine Crokos, said: ‘I had a five-year relationship with a woman and it was an intense five years but it was actually a really beautiful five years.

‘We barely kind of argued, because we both thought the same, we both wanted the same out of life. It was really interesting when I actually compare it to my other relationships.’

Megan agreed: ‘Definitely with girls, it’s so much deeper. When you go on a first date with a guy, it’s very, bit of banter, you’re just joking. With a girl, you know about the ex, what you want to do in ten years, five years, it’s a lot.’

Speaking about her first same-sex relationship, the reality star said: ‘It was only two months but it was so much deeper than I’ve ever had with any guy. I’ve had three serious relationships with guys but the two months I had with this girl, it was just so intense.

‘For me, it was really hard. I don’t see myself as a girly girl. She was like, “just tell me something romantic”. With men, all you need to say is “oh, your d*** looks big”, or just sit on their d*** and they’re happy. But with a girl, I had to be like “oh my god babe, you look so beautiful today”.
