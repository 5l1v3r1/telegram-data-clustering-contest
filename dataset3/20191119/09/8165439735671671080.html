The Number Of Young People Experiencing Severe Anxiety Is Increasing

19 Nov 2019, 09:26 by Josh Crosbie

Severe anxiety

The number of teenagers reporting severe anxiety has doubled in the past seven years.

Cases among young adults have also risen by more than two thirds.

'My World Survey 2' questioned 19,000 young people and found roughly one in four now suffer from the condition.

The study, which is the largest of its kind in Ireland, also shows around one in five report dealing with severe depression.
