'Who'd be manager?' - Nottingham Forest fans question Joe Worrall's team selection

Nottingham Forest latest | Joe Worrall selected his fantasy Forest team of the 2010s - and plenty of Reds fans have given their responses to the current centre-back

19 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Josh Murray

Nottingham Forest fans are reacting to Joe Worrall's Reds team of the decade - and the players they might have included.

Current Forest centre-back Worrall responded to our tweet asking supporters to name their fantasy City Ground XIs from the past 10 years.

His team was made up of: Brice Samba, Eric Lichaj, Jack Hobbs, Michael Dawson, Chris Cohen, David Vaughan, Ben Osborn, Henri Lansbury, Michail Antonio, Andy Reid and Britt Assombalonga.

Wes Morgan, Radoslaw Majewski and Lewis McGugan were players who just missed out, according to Worrall's tweet.
