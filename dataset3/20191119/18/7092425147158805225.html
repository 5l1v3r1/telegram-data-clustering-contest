Filming begins for new crime-thriller by Derry Girls creator

19 Nov 2019, 18:56 by Nick Fitzgerald

The Deceived will air on Virgin Media One next year

Production has begun in Belfast on behalf of a new crime-thriller by Derry Girls creator Lisa McGee.

The Deceived, co-written by McGee's husband Tobias Beer, is a "compelling, sinister narrative of lust, manipulation and betrayal".

The story centres around Ophelia (Emily Reid), a student who falls in love with her married college lecturer Dr Michael Callaghan (Emmet J Scanlan).
