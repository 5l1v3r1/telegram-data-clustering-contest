Lots going on at the Stettler Public Library

It’s not to late to get tickets for the Christmas Wine Survivor

19 Nov 2019, 18:00

Library News

With Christmas just a few weeks away, consider joining us Thursday, Nov. 21st at 6:30 p.m. for Surviving Christmas, an information session on reducing stress at Christmas so that you can enjoy this special season. Ages 18+

There are still tickets available for the second annual Friends of the Library Christmas Wine Survivor on Saturday, Dec. 7th! For just $20 and a $15 bottle of wine, you can win all the wine you need for entertaining and gifting over the holiday season.

Tickets are on sale at the library and through Friends of the Library until Dec. 5th.

Once you have a ticket, consider yourself invited to join us at our very exclusive Elimination Party with tasty snacks and entertainment.

You don’t need to attend to win. If you are not yet a member of the library, we have a remedy for that. Memberships are free to residents of Stettler and Stettler County thanks to the support of our Town and County Council and give you access to a world of resources! For more information, call the Library at 403-742-2292. The proceeds from this fundraiser will go towards establishing an equipment lending collection at the Library that everyone can enjoy.

Take advantage of the library’s volunteer resource person on Thursday afternoons between 1 and 3 p.m. She has a wealth of knowledge about community and government resources and can provide expert help with anything job related such as resumes, cover letters, work search, and interview skills.

On Thursday, Nov. 21st from 4 to 5:30 pm join us for Something Cool After School: Dinosaur Dig. We will read books about fossils and discuss how they were made, and then use tools to free plastic dinosaurs from blocks of ice! Ages 5+

Smoothies make a healthy snack on the go! We will explore fruit smoothies with a variety of different ingredients so you can make your own blend. Join other teens on Friday, Nov. 22nd from 4-6 p.m.

Ever wonder what virtual reality is all about? “Get Real with VR” runs from 1- 4 pm on Saturday, Nov. 23rd and provides an opportunity to try it out!

Interested in robots? Want to use coding to teach a robot how to navigate an obstacle course? Brain Train: Month of Mindstorms will show you how. Saturday, Nov. 23rd from 1 to 2:30 p.m. Please pre-register.

This month the Reader’s Circle Book Club will be reviewing Educated by Tara Westover. Join other readers at 6:30 pm on Tuesday, Nov. 26th to share your thoughts.

Create a festive candleholder using a stemmed wine glass. This craft makes a great gift. Join us Tuesday, Nov. 28th from 6:30 to 8 p.m. Please pre-register. 18+

Something Cool After School features Animals in the Snow on Thursday, Nov. 28th from 4 to 5:30 p.m. Learn about penguins and other cold-weather animals, play winter animal charades, then make your own “snow” with Ivory soap to play in. Ages 5+

Need some motivation to get writing? The Stettler Writers Group meets on Thursday, Nov. 28th from 6:30 to 8 p.m.

Join us for a discussion on writing and write a few pieces in class. Bring a pen and paper!

Relax after school over a few of our newest board games. We will have an assortment of snacks on hand to enjoy while you kick back. Join other teens for Teen Scene: Games Night on Friday, Nov. 29th from 4 to 6 p.m.

Parents and caregivers with young children ages 0-6 years will enjoy two programs for tots offered at the library in partnership with the Stettler Learning Centre. Magic Carpet Ride (Tuesdays) and Read and Rhyme (Wednesdays and Saturdays) are offered 10:30 to noon. Play, sing, read and have fun together! Each program provides many fun activities for the whole family OR give your partner the gift of time!

If you would like to see our other programs, check out our website at https://spl.prl.ab.ca. For more information on library services stop by for a tour anytime or call us at 403-742-2292. We post information about upcoming events and other interesting bits on Facebook, be sure to follow us there for the latest news!
