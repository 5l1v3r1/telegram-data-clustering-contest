Former Gogglebox star dropped from co-presenting Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway

19 Nov 2019, 18:14 by Nick Fitzgerald

Scarlett Moffatt landed her "dream" job back in 2017

Former Gogglebox star, Scarlett Moffatt, has been dropped from her role of co-presenting the Saturday Night Takeaway alongside Ant and Dec.

The 29-year-old, who was crowned the I'm A Celebrity winner in 2016, began her "dream" job the following year.

At the time Moffatt said:

“I’ve watched Saturday Night Takeaway with my family for as long as I can remember I'm such a huge fan, so to be part of one of the biggest entertainment shows out there, with two of my all time TV heroes Ant and Dec, is an absolute dream."

However, an ITV spokesperson has confirmed the news of Moffatt's axing.

In a statement, the ITV spokesperson said:

"Scarlett Moffatt won't feature as a contributor in the 2020 series of Saturday Night Takeaway.

"We thank Scarlett for all of her hard work on the show and will announce details of the new series nearer transmission."

Scarlett has since spoken about her discontinuation from the role, saying she has no hard feelings and the show will still be "amazing" without her.

Speaking to Matthew Wright on talkRADIO this afternoon, Moffatt said:

'They are just changing the format of the show. My bit was like challenge Anneka, and they have taken that out. It will still be amazing with Ant and Dec and Stephen, what a trio of men."
