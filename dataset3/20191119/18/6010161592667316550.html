The Game Awards 2019: Control, Death Stranding, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, more up for Game of the Year

19 Nov 2019, 18:02 by Stephany Nunneley

The Game Awards 2019 nominees have been announced and voting is open.

Control, Death Stranding, and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice have been nominated in multiple categories for The Game Awards 2019.

All three are also up for Game of the Year alongside Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, Resident Evil 2, and The Outer Worlds.

You can vote for your favorites through the official website or through Google. Like last year, you can also vote through Amazon Alexa, Facebook Messenger, The Game Awards Discord server, or Twitter.

You can look over the voting jury at the link, and you may notice VG27 is on the list.

The annual awards show will be livestreamed across 45 different services on December 12.
