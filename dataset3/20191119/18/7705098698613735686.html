Anissa Reaches Out for Help in Black Lightning Episode 3.07 Promo

19 Nov 2019, 18:22 by Tudor Leonte

The CW has dropped a new promo for the upcoming Black Lightning Episode 3.07. Next week, Anissa will ask her father Jefferson for help, before the city becomes a total warzone. The synopsis for “The Book of Resistance: Chapter Two” reads:

Anissa (Nafessa Williams) and Jefferson (Cress Williams) have a heart-to-heart and she tells him she needs Black Lightning’s help in escorting meta refugees out of the perimeter. Meanwhile, Gambi (James Remar) implores Lynn (Christine Adams) to help get him access to the Pit. Lastly, Jennifer (China Anne McClain) grows closer to Brandon (guest star Jahking Guilory). Marvin Jones III, Damon Gupton and Jordan Calloway also star.

Oz Scott directed the episode written by Lynelle White. You can check the new Black Lightning Episode 3.07 promo in the player below.
