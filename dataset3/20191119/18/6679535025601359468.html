DD employee files harassment complaint against senior

19 Nov 2019, 18:25 by Special Correspondent

An employee of Doordarshan in Bengaluru has filed a complaint against a senior colleague for allegedly sexually harassing her for the past six months. The victim, in her complaint to the RT Nagar police, said that on June 19, the accused confronted her while she was returning home from work and touched her inappropriately while seeking sexual favours.

“In her statement she said that she sought help from passersby following which the accused sped away on his bike,” said the police

The woman filed a complaint with her company soon after. A few days ago, however, the employee allegedly followed her to her neighbourhood and threatened to attack her. She ran into her house, and after consulting with her family, approached the police on Sunday.

Based on the complaint, the police registered a case against the accused who has been charged with outraging the modesty of a woman and criminal intimidation. “No arrests have been so far. We are still investigating the matter,” said the police.
