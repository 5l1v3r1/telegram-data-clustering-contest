Sheffield United midfielder John Lundstram gives his take on VAR time delay

19 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Varun Nair

Sheffield United midfielder John Lundstram believes that VAR time delay should be brought down to a single minute rather than checking it for a very long time.

VAR was once again at the forefront of controversy after Sheffield United were denied an equalising goal after John Lundstram was found millimeters offside during the build-up to the goal.

In one of the longest delays made by the VAR, John Lundstram was adjudged to have been offside after almost four minutes of delay in playing time. The Blades eventually found their equalising goal a couple of minutes later but VAR was once again the highlight of a match after everyone criticised the time delay the referees took to overturn the original decision.
