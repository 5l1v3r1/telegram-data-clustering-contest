Honor a veteran with Wreaths Across America

19 Nov 2019, 18:59

KNOXVILLE, Tenn. (WVLT-) The Knoxville Chapter of the Vietnam Veterans of America will provide wreaths to place on every headstone in Knox County's three veteran cemeteries.

This is part of the national organization, Wreaths Across America.

You can find information here if you'd like to help.

Volunteers will place the wreaths on Saturday, December 14, 2019.
