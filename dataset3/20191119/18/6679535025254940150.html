RGUHS to set up centre for excellence

19 Nov 2019, 18:47 by Staff Reporter

The Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) has decided to establish a centre for excellence, which would serve as a hub for research and invention.

Speaking at a press conference here on Tuesday, Deputy Chief Minister C.N. Ashwath Narayan said that there was a need for innovations in patient care. He also said that the centre would promote research in biotechnology, gene therapy and medical engineering. He urged practising doctors to also be involved in innovation.
