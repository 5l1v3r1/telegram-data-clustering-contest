Patrick Cutrone agent rubbishes Wolverhampton Wanderers exit rumours

The agent of Patrick Cutrone has dismissed suggestions that the forward could leave Wolverhampton Wanderers in January.

19 Nov 2019, 18:38 by Darren Plant

Since departing AC Milan for Molineux during the summer, Cutrone has contributed two goals from 19 appearances in all competitions.

While Sampdoria are allegedly keen on handing the Italian a chance to return to his homeland, the 21-year-old's representative Donato Orgnoni has claimed that no thought has been given to departing Wolves at the midway point of the campaign.

Orgnoni told SampNews: "We've never thought of leaving Wolves. I don't understand why certain news comes out. He remains in England 100%."

Cutrone has a contract at the Premier League outfit until 2023.
