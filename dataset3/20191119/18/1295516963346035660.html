Titans Photos Pit Father Vs. Daughter in Deadly Confrontation

19 Nov 2019, 18:11 by Sam Stone

It's father against daughter in the latest set of official stills from Titans Season 2, as Rose Wilson dons her Ravager costume to battle Deathstroke.

DC Universe officially released the images from Season 2's penultimate episode, titled "Faux Hawk." They include a blistering brawl between Ravager and Deathstroke, with the two crossing swords in a callback to the "death" of Slade Wilson's son Jericho earlier this season.

The episode's official synopsis also confirmed that Rose will confess a shocking secret about her background to Jason Todd over the course of the episode, putting her on a collision course with her estranged father. Previously released photos from the episode also teased an entirely different confrontation between Hawk and Dove.
