Bafana's AFCON Schedule After Second Place In Group

19 Nov 2019, 18:16 by Soccer Laduma

Bafana Bafana have moved in to second place in Group C of the 2021 Africa Cup Of Nations qualifiers after their first win on the weekend.

The South African national team secured a 1-0 win over Sudan at the Orlando Stadium on Sunday afternoon, this after suffering defeat in their opening encounter against Ghana.

Ghana are currently top of the table with two wins in two matches, with the qualifiers now only set to resume next year.

Bafana will play back-to-back clashes against Sao Tome in August and September 2020, while Ghana will lock horns with Sudan twice.

Thereafter in October 2020, South Africa will host Ghana and then travel away to Sudan in November for the last qualifier of the group stages.

The top two finishes in the group will qualify for the 2021 Africa Cup Of Nations in Cameroon.
