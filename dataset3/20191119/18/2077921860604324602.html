Live at Paste Studio - Tiny Ruins

19 Nov 2019, 18:30

New Zealand folk outfit Tiny Ruins (mainly the project of one Hollie Fullbrook) released one of the most delightful and underrated acoustic records of the year, Olympic Girls, in February. Fullbrook is now in the midst of a massive solo tour and swung by the Paste Studio in NYC in October. She performed the title track from that album, plus three more songs.
