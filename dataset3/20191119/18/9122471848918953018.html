NFL Insider Has Injury Update For Chiefs WR Tyreek Hill

19 Nov 2019, 18:57 by Chris Rosvoglou

Although the Kansas City Chiefs won last night against the Los Angeles Chargers, the offense lost All-Pro wideout Tyreek Hill to yet another injury. He left in the first half due to a hamstring issue.

Hill already missed time this season because of a shoulder injury. There’s no question that he’s the second-most important piece on Kansas City’s offense – outside of Patrick Mahomes.

Kansas City is currently on its bye week so it’ll have time to let Hill recover from his injury. But first, the Chiefs have to find out the severity of it.

According to NFL Network insider Mike Garafolo, the Chiefs will have MRI results for Hill in the next day or so since the team came back late from Mexico City.
