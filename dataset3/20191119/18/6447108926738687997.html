Rainbow Six: Siege’s Money Heist mini event kicking off soon, free weekend confirmed

19 Nov 2019, 18:52 by Aran Suddi

Ubisoft will be running a mini event in Rainbow Six: Siege as the publisher partners with Netflix to run a Money Heist inspired game mode. From November 20th until November 25th the Hostage mode will be making its return as players descend on Bank. Attackers and Defenders will be fighting over the hostage who will be dressed in a mask and red jumpsuit.
