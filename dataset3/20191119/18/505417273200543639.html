Troy Parrott Starts As Ireland U21s Name Exciting XI For Swedish Clash

19 Nov 2019, 18:34 by Oisin McQueirns

Ireland U21 manager Stephen Kenny has named his starting XI ahead of tonight’s crucial Euro 2021 qualifier against Sweden at Tallaght Stadium tonight.

Captain Jayson Molumby returns to the starting XI in centre-midfield after missing the victory over Armenia through suspension while Lee O’Connor is also back after his successful stint with the Irish senior side.

Troy Parrott starts after his role as an unused substitute on Monday night against Denmark and Kenny will be hoping he will be able to emulate his two-goal exploits from the last time the two sides met in Sweden.
