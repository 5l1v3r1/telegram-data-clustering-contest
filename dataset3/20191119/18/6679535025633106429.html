NDRF, VIT university to develop hydrogen-powered two-wheeler

The project, when realised, will replace petrol

19 Nov 2019, 18:59 by Special Correspondent

The National Design and Research Forum would extend its support to the project on hydrogen-powered two wheeler along with VIT University, said NDRF chairman Mylswamy Annadurai on Tuesday.

Addressing the gathering at the valedictory function of ‘The Future Scientist- State Science Festival’, he said that NDRF and VIT would help the team to take the invention of hydrogen powered vehicle to the next level. Such inventions may bring a break-through and provide alternative to petrol.

Lithium-Ion technology

The impact of electric vehicles operated on lithium ion are being discussed in the science forums.

There are concerns over lithium ion cell phone batteries that may have ill-effects when not used properly, he said. Such inventions may have a major impact and change the world, he added.

G.Viswanathan, VIT chancellor, K.Asokan, editor, Hindu- Tamil Thisai and S.T. Balakrishnan, State secretary, Tamil Nadu Science Forum also spoke.

The State Science Festival was jointly organised by VIT, Hindu- Tamil Thisai, NDRF and TNSF.
