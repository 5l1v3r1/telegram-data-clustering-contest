Police hunt Bristol man Kieron Morris wanted on recall to prison

He has links to Shirehampton, Lawrence Weston and Southmead

19 Nov 2019, 18:28 by Alexander Brock

Avon and Somerset Constabulary is appealing to find a wanted man.

Kieron Morris from Bristol is wanted on a recall to prison after breaching the terms of his licence.

The 27-year-old is described as white, 5ft 8ins/5ft 9ins and of medium or stocky build.

Police say he has links to Shirehampton , Lawrence Weston and Southmead.

Members of the public are encouraged to call the police if they see him.

A police spokesperson said: "We're appealing for the public's help to locate 27-year-old Bristol man Kieron Morris who is wanted on a recall to prison after breaching the terms of his licence.

"If you see him, or know where he is, please call us on 101 and give the call handler the reference number 5219254298."
