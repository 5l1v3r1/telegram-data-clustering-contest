Next Tottenham manager odds: Jose Mourinho installed as bookmakers' favourite

Spurs axed Mauricio Pochettino after a poor start to the season has left them languishing down in 14th in the Premier League table

19 Nov 2019, 20:51 by Irishmirror.ie

Jose Mourinho has been installed as the bookmakers’ favourite to become the next Tottenham manager.

Spurs axed Mauricio Pochettino on Tuesday after a poor start to the season has left them languishing down in 14th in the Premier League table.

The Argentine departs after five years in north London and having led them to the Champions League final last season.

Tottenham chief Daniel Levy said: "We were extremely reluctant to make this change.

"It is not a decision the board has taken lightly, nor in haste. Regrettably domestic results at the end of last season and beginning of this season have been extremely disappointing."

Former Manchester United boss Mourinho is in pole position to replace him at the helm, with the Sky Sports pundit 3/1 on to take over according to Betfair.

Carlo Ancelotti is second favourite at 17/2, followed by Massimiliano Allegri (9/1).

Leicester ’s Brendan Rodgers and Bournemouth ’s Eddie Howe (both 16/1) are also in the frame.

Betfair Spokesperson, Sam Rosbottom said: “All week we have been taking a large number of big bets on Jose Mourinho to become the next Tottenham manager.

“48 hours ago, odds on the former Chelsea and Manchester United boss to take over from Poch were as big as 5/1, but now they are as little as to 1/3.”
