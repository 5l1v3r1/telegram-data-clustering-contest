What is Diablo IV?

Diablo IV is an action-RPG and the long-anticipated sequel to Diablo III, which first released on PC back in 2011 before making its way to consoles a few years later. At BlizzCon 2018, Blizzard confirmed that multiple Diablo titles were in development, one of which was clearly Diablo IV. Now, at BlizzCon 2019, the game has been announced.
