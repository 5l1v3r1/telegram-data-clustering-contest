Gillingham 1-0 Sunderland: James Hunter's player ratings as Black Cats are dumped out in extra time

James Hunter's verdict on the Black Cats from their FA Cup replay against Gillingham

19 Nov 2019, 20:39 by James Hunter

Sunderland are out of the FA Cup following a miserable defeat in their first-round replay at Gillingham.

Brandon Hanlan scored with the only on-target shot of the game in extra time to send the hosts through.

The Black Cats failed to pose a genuine threat, with Will Grigg wasting their best chance when well placed on the edge of the box and firing wide.

Here's how James Hunter rated the Sunderland players following their FA Cup first-round exit:
