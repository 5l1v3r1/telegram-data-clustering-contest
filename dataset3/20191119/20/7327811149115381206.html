'General Hospital' interrupted by Trump impeachment hearings

19 Nov 2019, 20:26 by WABC

NEW YORK (WABC) -- Due to coverage of the Trump impeachment hearings on Channel 7, "General Hospital" did not air in its entirety Tuesday.

The full episode will be posted on ABC.com by 8 p.m.

To watch the full episode, visit ABC.com/shows/general-hospital.

We apologize for any inconvenience.
