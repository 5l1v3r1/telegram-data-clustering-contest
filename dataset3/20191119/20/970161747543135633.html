What's behind Jeremy Corbyn's wonky glasses?

19 Nov 2019, 20:35

As Boris Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn went head to head in the election debate tonight, many viewers have been left wondering ‘what’s wrong with Jeremy Corbyn’s wonkly glasses?’

The Labour leader appeared at the debate, wearing glasses which were bent to one side.

Many took to Twitter to ask: ‘Why are Jeremy Corbyn’s glasses crooked.’

One social media user added: ‘Jeremy Corbyn’s glasses are wonky and a little steamed up’.

Many even complained they couldn’t focus on the substance of the debate, because they were too distracted by the Labour leader’s specs.

However, Mr Corbyn has earlier said he is receiving treatment for an eye condition at Moorfields Eye Hospital.
