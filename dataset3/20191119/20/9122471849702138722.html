Colin Cowherd Unveils His College Football Playoff Top 6

19 Nov 2019, 20:51 by Andrew McCarty

In just a few hours, the newest College Football Playoff rankings will be released following a big weekend of action.

Before the poll comes out, FOX Sports host Colin Cowherd broke down what he thinks the top six should be. With the injury to star quarterback Tua Tagovailoa, Cowherd doesn’t think Alabama deserves to be in the top six.

After another dominant performance last weekend – albeit against Rutgers – Ohio State sits in the top spot.

Here’s Cowherd’s top six:

  1. Ohio State
  2. Clemson
  3. LSU
  4. Georgia
  5. Oregon
  6. Utah
