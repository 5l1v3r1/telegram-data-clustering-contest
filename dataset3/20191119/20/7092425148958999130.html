Met Eireann issues rainfall warning for five counties

19 Nov 2019, 20:43 by Nick Fitzgerald

Get out the umbrellas lads

Met Eireann has issued a status yellow rainfall warning for five counties in Ireland. Heavy rain spells will bring between 30mm and 50mm of rain at times.

The warning will take effect from the early hours of Wednesday morning and remains in place until noon on Thursday.

The affected counties will be Kilkenny, Wexford, Cork, Tipperary and Waterford.
