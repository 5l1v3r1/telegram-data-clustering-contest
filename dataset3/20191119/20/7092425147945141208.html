Betfair drop first edition of 'Serial Winners' featuring Paul Nicholls

19 Nov 2019, 20:37 by Buzz Contributor

Betfair today released the first instalment of ‘Serial Winners’, a series of short films which bid to understand and celebrating what drives their various Ambassadors to continuously strive and achieve success.

First to feature is 11 time Champion National Hunt trainer, Paul Nicholls, who, with over 3000 career winners is truly driven but what drives him to always want more?
