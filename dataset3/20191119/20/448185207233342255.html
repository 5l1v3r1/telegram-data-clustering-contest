Two Indian nationals arrested in Pakistan

19 Nov 2019, 20:30

Two men, said to be Indian nationals, have been arrested in Pakistan’s Cholistan area for illegally entering the country, media reports said. Police said that the duo didn’t have proper documents, Pakistan media reported. As per the police, the two have been identified as Prashant and Darilal, residents of Madhya Pradesh and Hyderabad, respectively. One of the arrested persons is a software engineer, said the report. Police have registered a case against them. (IANS)
