19 Nov 2019, 20:23 by Express News Service

No bids were received Tuesday during the auction of two flats belonging to gangster Iqbal Mirchi. The 1,200-sq ft flats in Juhu were being auctioned at a base price of Rs 3.45 crore.

According to sources, as the rate was higher than the market price, it did not generate much interest. The base price, an official said, will now be revised and the flats would be put up on auction again. The auction is being carried out by the Ministry of Finance under the Smugglers and Foreign Exchange Manipulators (Forfeiture of Property) Act (SAFEMA). The auctioning process is carried out online.
