19 Nov 2019, 20:30

Caitlyn Jenner is already tipped as a potential winner of I’m A Celebrity, after just a couple of days in the jungle.

The 70-year-old has got off to an impressive start in camp, from that spectacular skydive through to tackling other far from pleasant Bushtucker Trials – and opening up about transitioning following an emotional first night.

It’s all a far cry from the 70-year-old’s glamorous life back home, but of course Caitlyn’s no stranger to challenges – having previously been an Olympic champion as Bruce before she transitioned.

So just when did she win at the Olympics and in what sport?

Here’s what you need to know…

Which sport did Caitlyn Jenner compete in at the Olympics and when?

Caitlyn first took part in the Summer Olympics in Munich in 1972.

Pre-transition the former athlete competed in the decathlon, making it into the US team in spite of being a relative newcomer to the sport – and going on to finish 10th in the event in Munich.
