NYC FinTech Women’s Inspiring Fintech Females 2019 List

19 Nov 2019, 20:58 by JD Alois

NYC FinTech Women has published its list of Inspiring Fintech Females 2019. NYC FinTech Women serves over 2,000 members through mentorship, sponsorship, and networking opportunities and publishes their annual list to recognize the achievements of women in financial technology.

According to NYC FinTech Women, the leaders on the list embody the mission of their organization: to strengthen the Fintech ecosystem by empowering and sponsoring women who are transformative. These women have been selected because of their leadership and support of the community.

Luan Cox, co-founder and CEO of FinMkt, Inc. who was named to this years list for her outstanding leadership and support of the community of women in the field, shared the following statement:

“It’s an honor to be among such a talented and successful group of Fintech leaders,” said Cox. “I also hope the list serves as a reminder that inclusion is good for business and that it inspires more women to seek careers in financial technology.”
