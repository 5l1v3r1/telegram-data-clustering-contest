Water back, after pipe break

19 Nov 2019, 20:11 by Nicholas Johansen

UPDATE 8:10 p.m.

Crews have finished repairing a water main on Okanagan Avenue and water service has been restored to the area. 

As a reminder, crews will begin asphalt repairs Wednesday and anticipate the work will be complete by the end of the week. Traffic detours will be in place.

The City thank area residents for their patience Tuesday and apologizes for any inconvenience while the road is being repaired.

ORIGINAL 2:50 p.m.

A water main break in Vernon has shut off water to a number of homes in the Mission Hill area Tuesday afternoon.

The break occurred on the 4100 block of Okanagan Avenue.

Crews are currently on scene working to restore service.

It's unclear how many homes are affected, but the city says “every effort is being made to have water available as soon as possible.”

On Wednesday, city crews will be back in the area to repair the road.

Road repairs are expected to continue through the week, and traffic detours are expected.
