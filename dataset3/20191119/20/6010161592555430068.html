The Outer Worlds patch includes a toggle option to increase the font size, more

19 Nov 2019, 20:06 by Stephany Nunneley

The latest patch for The Outer Worlds is available, and one of the takeaways is the ability to increase the font size.

No one likes to squint, and thankfully, after applying The Outer Worlds patch you will no longer have to. Now, you will be able to increase fonts for conversation text, cinematic subtitles, bark subtitles, and terminal text thanks to a new toggle option.

Patch 1.1.1.0 will also stop the game from crashing at a specific point when you reach Tartarus.

Foliage on the Xbox One now has parity with that on PlayStation 4, and speaking of the latter, you will no longer experience muffled sounds on the console. For both consoles. the achievement/trophy for “It’s Not the Best Choice” will now pop correctly as well.
