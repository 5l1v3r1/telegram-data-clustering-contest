REA satisfied with power distribution work in Chato District

19 Nov 2019, 05:16 by Correspondent

RURAL Energy Agency (REA) board says it is satisfied with the implementation of its directives to Whitecity Guangdong JV – the contractor currently distributing electricity in villages in Chato District that had called for speeding up the work.

This was revealed by REA board chairman Julius Kalolo at the weekend in Chato, Geita Region soon after inspecting energy distribution work in the district. He was accompanied by REA board member Dr Andrew Komba and REA acting director general eng Amos Maganga.

He explained that the board’s directives were issued 14 days ago when the contractor was told to complete the work that was still pending in some areas including digging holes, erection of poles and fixing power cables, for all villages through the first round of REA III.

He said the contractor has also started to install transformers for power distribution to villagers hence the board stressed for speeding up of the work so that all areas earmarked to get power get it.

The board also directed the contractor to light up electricity in Kakeneno village by the end of next week so that the wananchi are connected as the infrastructures thereof have reached their houses and wiring has already been done to their houses.

After inspecting power distribution work in Mutundu Village, The board directed that power should be lit on November 18 since every infrastructure is in place.

Director of Whitecity Guangdong JV Nuhu Mringo said the company has tried its level best to implement the board’s directives issued two weeks ago and promised to speed up the work.

Whilst in Chato District the board inspected power distribution work in Mutundu, Kakindo, Kamanga, Mnekez, Kakeneno and Matofali villages.
