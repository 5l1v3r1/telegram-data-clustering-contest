Ajay Devgn teases fans with another fierce poster ahead of 'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior' trailer launch

19 Nov 2019, 05:44 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Moviegoers and film fraternity are waiting for the big trailer launch of Ajay Devgn's ambitious project, 'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior'. With impressive posters and motion pictures, the makers of the film have already heightened the excitement within the fans and Ajay who will be returning to the historical genre after a long hiatus has now unveiled a new poster of the film ahead of the trailer launch.

Ajay took to his Twitter handle and shared a new poster of the film and also expressed his excitement for the trailer launch. He wrote, "Get ready to witness the epic battle. #TanhajiTrailer out today at 1:47 PM." He also announced the timings for the trailer release. Not only the film's star cast but Ajay's fraternity friends are too excited to witness, 'Tanhaji' and Ajay's magnum opus drama. Celebs like Riteish Deshmukh, Akshay Kumar, Siddharth Malhotra praised the film's posters and are excited about it.
