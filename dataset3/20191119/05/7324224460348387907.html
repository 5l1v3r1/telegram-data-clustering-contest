Wednesbury's The New Consistent putting on Christmas party for fans

Wednesbury's The New Consistent has announced he will be hosting a Christmas party for fans in Birmingham.

19 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Leigh Sanders

The former The Star Unsigned column act will be putting on his first headline gig at the Second City's The Sunflower Lounge, in association with Birmingham promoters Qute Zoo Promotions, on December 19.

"It’s due to be a big event," he told The Star, "with support coming from Wolverhampton newcomers Pool [another former Unsigned column act], the very exciting 11 57 and new Derby dreamboats Closed Curtains.

"They’ll be a DJ set from Brum indie royalty - to be announced soon - and some very, very special guests during the headline set. Plus, I’ll be debuting the entirety of my unreleased second EP."

Having already supported the likes of The Rhythm Method and playing stages such as the BBC Introducing stage at Worcester Music Festival, The New Consistent has been making waves on the underground circuit.

"One hell of a party is promised," The New Consistent, real name Benjamin Ramsay, added.

Tickets are £7 and are available from SeeTickets.
