DJ Switch meets Chinese icons, Jet Li and Jack Ma

19 Nov 2019, 05:07 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian 11-year-old disc jockey, Erica Tandoh,popularly known as DJ Switch has shared a video on her Instagram which captured the moment she met popular Chinese film actor and producer Jet Li and business mogul and philanthropist Jack Ma at the 2019 Africa Netpreneurship Summit held at the Accra International Conference, on Saturday, 16th November 2019.

Jack Ma is the co-founder and former executive chairman of Alibaba Group, a multinational technology conglomerate.

The business mogul who was thrilled with a previous video of DJ Switch he watched upon seeing the talented kid said:

“How are you? I saw your video this morning. How did you do that? I want you to teach me how to do that. I wanna learn that,”

The two Chinese icons together with some high profile Chinese personalities including the former UN Secretary-General, Ban Ki-Moon are in Ghana to support entrepreneurs and businesses in Africa.
