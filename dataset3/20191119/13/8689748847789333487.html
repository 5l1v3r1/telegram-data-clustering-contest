Stonebwoy’s producer blasts Kelvynboy for disrespecting his former boss

19 Nov 2019, 13:23 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian record producer, Bright Kwame Appiah, popularly known as StreetBeatz has waded into the recent Stonebwoy and Kelvynboy banter.

According to Streetbeatz in a series of post on Twitter, Kelvyn Boy is too disrespectful and arrogant for throwing jab at Stonebwoy.

In the series of tweets, StreetBeatz revealed that Kelvyn Boy is now misbehaving because he can boast of a couple of hit songs.

Read his tweets below:

“I sometimes don’t wanna see some people tweet at all!!!”

“When you weak that’s when u come on here saying trash about people, if you have that balls and you call your self a man face the Don Dada.”

“I was right infront of you when u were telling someone I told u “u will never blow”. Forgetting I’m the first ever producer you recorded with and I spoke what is going on in ur life today like a prophet. Which I have witnesses!!!”

“I’m the fu•kn producer who produced the biggest song to happen to Ghana 🇬🇭, but man no Dey rush. Some one two songs nor u think say u reach. Because you dey play shows every weekend. Ahhh 🤦🏾‍♂️ this world paaaa.”

“We dey back stage, matter happen. Big man come open inside give the people for stage there nor u take for ur self. Bcoz u know say you no dey respect. So e get to u the moment.”

Their recent banter started when Stonebwoy during his performance at the 4Syte Music video awards paused to acknowledge Samini and at the same time, made a comment which was subtly directed at Kelvynboy.

Kelvynboy in a tweet replied Stonebwoy for throwing shade at him during the performance.
