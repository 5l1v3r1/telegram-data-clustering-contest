McDonald's Christmas menu arrives tomorrow with new Big Tasty

New burgers and desserts include a Matchmaker McFlurry

19 Nov 2019, 13:03 by Max Channon

The McDonald's Christmas menu lands in restaurants across the UK this week - and it features a brand new version of the Big Tasty, alongside new burgers, sides, desserts and drinks.

The full menu, which launches on Wednesday, includes a Big Tasty beef burger with Emmental cheese, tomato, lettuce, onion and smoky Big Tasty sauce, it costs £4.29 on its own, or £5.89 as a medium meal. You can also add bacon to it for an extra 40p.

There's also a brand new chicken burger, the Chicken Deluxe, for £5.19 (or £6.59 as a meal), as well as Camembert cheese dippers (£1.69 for five, or £4.40 for a sharebox).

The Chicken Deluxe has two pieces of crispy chicken with a tomato relish, mayo, cheese, lettuce and red onion rings.

According to The Sun, all the new additions will arrive in McDonald's stores tomorrow, on Wednesday, November 20.

In desserts, there's a new Matchmaker McFlurry to finish, costing 99p for the mini version, or £1.39 for standard.

The new menu additions follow Christmas drinks which have already been released - including the Millionaire Latte.
