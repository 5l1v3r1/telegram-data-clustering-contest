Sergio Aguero: John Terry stopped Chelsea from signing Argentine superstar

19 Nov 2019, 13:47

- Sergio Aguero was wanted by Chelsea before he joined Manchester City in 2011

- John Terry stopped the deal after claiming the Argentine's movement was poor

- Aguero has won four Premier League titles and other silverware since then

Argentine superstar Sergio Aguero is the current sixth all-time Premier League highest goals scorer with 173 goals at the time of this report.

The 31-year-old forward joined Manchester City in 2011 from Atletico Madrid, and since then has been superb for the Citizens - winning four EPL titles among others.

He also played a key role in helping his side become the first team in England to win domestic treble after winning all the titles available in the season.

Although, they failed to make it to the UEFA Champions League final after being bundled out by Tottenham Hotspur at the quarterfinal stage.
