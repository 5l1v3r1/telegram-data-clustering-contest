Sonam Kapoor and Rhea Kapoor are having fun on their trip in LA

19 Nov 2019, 13:48 by Filmfare

Sonam Kapoor, who was last seen on screen in The Zoya factor opposite Dulquer Salmaan, is currently vacationing in LA with her younger sister Rhea, who’s managed to build a name for herself as a successful producer and stylist.

Sonam also posted a series of candid pictures where the sisters are seen walking through the streets.
