GosT on tour with Mayhem & Gaahl, announces 2020 Tour Dates

19 Nov 2019, 13:09 by BrooklynVegan Staff

Texas-based dark synthwave project GosT is currently finishing up a European tour with Norwegian black metal legends Mayhem and Gaahls Wyrd, the solo project of Norwegian black metal legend Gaahl (of Gorgoroth, Wardruna, etc). The metal-approved and costumed GosT — who also has the ability to make you dance — has now announced a headlining North American tour, beginning in Denver in January, and hitting Knitting Factory in Brooklyn (2/12) along the way.
