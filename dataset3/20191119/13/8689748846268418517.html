‘Be Smart And Chop Your Guy’s Money Very Well’– Tracey Boakye To Young Ladies

19 Nov 2019, 13:09 by Ghbase New Writer

Actress and entrepreneur, Tracey Boakye has asked young women not to lower their guard when they are in a relationship with men.

The heavily endowed mother of one advised these young ladies to act smart and milk their guys very well since they are not yet married and therefore must exploit the relationship to their advantage.

Tracey sent out this unconventional advise to women when she spoke to blogger Zion Felix on his Uncut Show.

Tracey Boakye also confessed being in a relationship with someone older than her whiles adding that many older men are more responsible than the younger ones.

The Baby Mama movie producer said when a relationship fails, these women won’t have any sympathy or help from anyone so they should take advantage of it whiles it’s operational.
