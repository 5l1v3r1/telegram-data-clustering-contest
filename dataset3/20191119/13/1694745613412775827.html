Watch Kacey Musgraves and Willie Nelson Share the Sweetest Duet at the CMA Awards

19 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Mekishana Pierre

Willie Nelson hasn't performed on the CMA Awards stage since 2012, but his Wednesday night duet with Kacey Musgraves was well worth the wait. The two country stars teamed up for a beautiful, gentle rendition of "Rainbow Connection," the iconic Muppet Movie song originally performed by Kermit the Frog. No one will ever be able to touch Kermit's earnestly froggy performance, but Musgraves and Nelson gave it their best.
