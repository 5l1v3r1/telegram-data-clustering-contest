Another new shop set to open in Burton shopping centre

It's busy times in the town centre

19 Nov 2019, 13:29 by Ben Waldron

A new vaping shop is set to open in a major Burton shopping centre.

Signs have gone up in the window of the former Heaven Scent unit saying Electromist is set to "open soon".

The electronic cigarettes and e-liquid suppliers boasts more than 20 stores across the United Kingdom, including in Lichfield, Lancaster and Telford.

According to the firm's website, it "prides itself" on offering more than 30 lines of "juices", hardware and more for vaping.

The description reads: "Needless to say, whatever stage you're at on your vaping journey, we've got you covered."

The sign in the shop window reads: "Electromist. Store opening here. Coming soon."

The unit has sat vacant for more than a year after Heaven Scent closed in June 2018.

The cosmetics and perfume store shut after nearly 40 years of trading.

As well as its store in the shopping centre store, it had branches on the junction of High Street and New Street.

Coopers Square has been boosted by a number of stores opening in recent weeks.

Independent women's clothing store Remona opened recently, with a sweet shop set to join it in the coming weeks.

Dave Chadfield, centre manager at Coopers Square shopping centre said: "Following the openings of Remona and Grab & Go, we're pleased to now also be welcoming Electromist to the shopping centre.

"Over the last few years, it has established a strong presence across the Midlands and has proven to be a popular addition to Burton.

"Electromist has built up a good reputation for the knowledge, advice and safety tips it offers when it comes to E-Cigarettes and E-Liquid.

"We're pleased it will be opening with us at Coopers Square in the near future."

Burton Live has contacted Electromist for more information.
