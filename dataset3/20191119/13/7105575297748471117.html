Bella Hadid Reportedly "Went Out of Her Way" to Reach Out to Selena Gomez After Instagram Deletion Drama

19 Nov 2019, 13:08 by Alyssa Bailey

Selena Gomez made clear on Instagram yesterday that there really is no drama between her and Bella Hadid after Hadid deleted a post Gomez commented on and got the attention of fans and media for doing so over the weekend. But there's a backstory that led to Gomez making her final statement defending Hadid: TMZ reports the two privately talked things out.

Multiple sources connected to Hadid told the outlet that the model "actually went out of her way to reach out to Selena this weekend to squash any potential beef." Gomez and Hadid had public drama in 2017 when Gomez started dating Hadid's ex-boyfriend The Weeknd. Both women unfollowed each other on Instagram and got the attention of fans and the media for doing so. Gomez and The Weeknd broke up in October 2017.
