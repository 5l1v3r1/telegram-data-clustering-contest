Get a slap-up three-course Thanskgiving dinner from Baker & Spice

Deliveroo Dubai to bring a turkey to your doorstep

19 Nov 2019, 13:30 by Darragh Murphy

From creating gravy and cranberry sauce from scratch to stuffing the turkey, there’s a lot of effort that goes into making a hefty Thanksgiving dinner this year.

Well, Deliveroo and Baker & Spice in Dubai wantto take the stress out of all that.

From Monday November 25 until Saturday November 30, the holiday feast will come with a daily seasonal homemade soup as a starter. As for mains, choose from two options including a 250gm roast chicken with gravy or a 250gm roast turkey with gravy and cranberry sauce.

For sides, pick from red cabbage with apple and cranberry, pearl couscous and chicken sausage with cranberry and herbs, traditional sourdough bread stuffing, and roast potato with cumin and zaatar.

It wouldn’t be a Thanksgiving dinner without a sweet finishing, and celebrators can expect a changing dessert of the day throughout the week.
The special feast will be exclusively available via the Deliveroo app, all from Baker & Spice’s Souk Al Manzil, Marina Promenade and Jumeriah Town Centre locations.

Each dinner is priced at Dhs129, and all you have to do is download the Deliveroo app to get in on the dinner.
