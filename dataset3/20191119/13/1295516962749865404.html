Titans: Hawk and Dove Throw Down in a Cage Fight in New Photo Set

19 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Sam Stone

Estranged romantic partners Hawk and Dove will come to blows in an upcoming episode of Titans... or perhaps not.

DC Universe has released a set of official stills from Season 2's twelfth episode, titled "Faux Hawk," featuring Dawn Granger fighting someone wearing the costume of her ex-boyfriend Hawk. However, judging by the title, the figure in the cage match might not be Hank Hall at all.

The longtime crimefighting couple broke up in the face of a shocking revelation from Dick Grayson, feeling that their relationship put others in danger. Since then, Dawn was among the Titans recruited by Bruce Wayne to free Dick and reunite the team against a resurgent Deathstroke, with Dove hot on Hank's trail to bring him back into the fold.
