Elderly woman knocked over and injured by man fleeing security guards

She required hospital treatment

19 Nov 2019, 13:08 by Jamie Hawkins

Police are appealing for witnesses after a woman was knocked over by a man who was fleeing from security guards in Plymouth city centre.

The incident happened in Royal Parade at around 11.30am on Thursday, September 26.

Police issued the public appeal today, Tuesday, November 19.

The victim, aged in her 80s, suffered a fractured shoulder and cut to her head. SheÂ required hospital treatment.
