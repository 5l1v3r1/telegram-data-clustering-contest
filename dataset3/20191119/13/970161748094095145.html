Police mistakenly pull over disabled Emmerdale actor for drink driving

19 Nov 2019, 13:38

Emmerdale star James Moore has called for a wider knowledge and awareness of the way disability can impact motor skills after he was mistakenly pulled over by police, suspected of drink driving. The disabled star, best known for playing Ryan Stocks in the show, suffers from ataxic cerebral palsy, which impacts his movement and speech.

He took to Twitter to share a story with his followers which saw him reported by a member of the public who saw him making his way to his car with his girlfriend. He was later pulled over by police and had to undergo a breathalyser test to confirm that he wasn’t driving under the influence of alcohol.

He said: ‘So @AtaxiaUK are running a campaign called disordered not drunk, where disabled people recount their stories of mistakenly being labelled drunk. Tonight I seem to have a new one.

‘I went for a meal with my girlfriend, and as I was walking back to my car, someone apparently unbeknownst to me, called the police. I was pulled over half an hour later and was told a concerned citizen rang claiming that I was DUI.
