Is Derby County v Preston North End on TV? Channel details, kick-off time & match odds

Latest Derby County news | The Rams are back in action this weekend after a two-week hiatus following the final international break of the calendar year

19 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Mark Wakefield

Derby County resume their Sky Bet Championship campaign this weekend, as they welcome Preston North End to Pride Park Stadium.

The Rams take on the Lilywhites in their first match after the November international break, and are currently in 16th place in the league table.

Phillip Cocu and his players will be keen to improve on their last result which saw them lose 1-0 to arch rivals Nottingham Forest nearly a fortnight ago.

Preston go into Saturday’s contest in good form, winning their last three matches and are in second place in the league standings.

Ahead of the match, we’ve put together all of the key things that you need to know.

When is the game?

The game is due to kick off at 3pm on Saturday, November 23 at Pride Park Stadium.

Is it on TV?

The game is not being broadcast televised live, but you will be able to follow all the action from the contest right here at Derbyshire Live.
