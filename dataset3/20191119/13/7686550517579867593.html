Black Friday discounts on annual passes for Alton Towers, Thorpe Park and more

The discounts start from £109pp with reductions of up to £50

19 Nov 2019, 13:36 by Neil Bennett

Merlin - the entertainments company behind dozens of the UK's top attractions - have announced discounts on their Annual Passes ahead of the Black Friday sales.

With attractions like Alton Towers and Thorpe Park, the Merlin Annual Pass is already designed to save customers money.

Described as the 'ultimate passport to fun', the Pass gives it's owners access to over 30 attractions across the country. A full list of all attractions can be found at the foot of this article.

Across their Black Friday Sale, discounts have been placed across all four of their Passes. Customers who take advantage of the offer before it runs out on December 3rd 2019 will also receive 3 extra months entirely free of charge.

On a monthly basis, Merlin Memberships start at £8.99. As well as access to Merlin's parks, the Pass also grants extra benefits including 20% off food, drinks and shopping.
