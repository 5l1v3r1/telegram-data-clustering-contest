My Top Winter Skincare Picks for Acne

19 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Charlotte Cho

As the cofounder, esthetician, and curator behind Soko Glam and the skincare content site The Klog, I'm passionate about scouring Seoul for the latest and greatest innovations and bringing them to the U.S. so that your skin will see results.

Hydration and protection are at the cornerstone of any Korean skincare routine. Contrary to many Western philosophies, even acne-prone skin is in need of major hydration. By using products with moisturizing properties, you can reduce the inflammation beneath the surface of the skin that may lead to zits and breakouts. Protecting the skin from harmful UV rays is also important to protect acne-prone skin from pigmentation. These K-beauty innovations will hydrate, calm, protect, and fortify the skin barrier, leading you down the path to clear, glowing skin.

Glowy skin starts with a healthy skin barrier. The role of the skin barrier is to keep irritants out, and when it's functioning right your complexion will be supple and clear. This gentle ampoule is formulated with brazilian green propolis, which is a conditioning ingredient that protects and nourishes the skin, improves skin’s texture, and prevents moisture loss.
