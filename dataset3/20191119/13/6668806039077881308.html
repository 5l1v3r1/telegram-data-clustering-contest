15 quotes by birthday star Sushmita Sen that set her apart

19 Nov 2019, 13:35 by Vedanshi Pathak

Sushmita Sen turns 44 today and the glamorous mother of two shows no signs of dulling down. She is one actress who has been the face of women empowerment in Bollywood. Sushmita Sen has seen it all in life -- success, failure, heartbreak to new love, she has been at the peak of the ladder and also seen many lows but the one thing that has kept this diva rolling is her unwavering self-belief.

On her birthday today, we’ve listed out 15 quotes by the actress on the professional and personal life and everything in between. Scroll through and thank us for stoking you with such inspiring words later. 

“Carve your own way, choose not to drift along.”

“The only way to go beyond things is to go through them.”

“Work hard…command results, demand nothing.”

“I like winning, it’s an old habit.  But I don’t like rat races.”

“I am a very content person, but I am never satisfied. And that drives me.”

“I don’t know what’s ‘enough’,  but as long as I do everything I choose to do with everything that I’ve got, that to me is being successful.”

“Everything begins and ends with us in very many ways. And being a little selfish, it’s not a bad thing. Denying you are, is.”

“Just because a woman gives birth to a child that doesn’t make her a mother. How she raises one, makes one.”

“Either you match the size of my diamond or the size of my heart.”

“What made me win Miss Universe was my belief as an average Indian girl, that I could try and be better than average one day.”

“I think if a man wakes up next to me thinking ‘I have to be here’, that’s not good enough for me. He should want to be there. So if he has a woman on his mind while I am beside him, that doesn’t necessarily make him faithful.”

“I am so grateful that I have such wonderful people in my life, who have come and left at the right time for me to grow.”

“Things are misunderstood because they are not said properly. To make it work, you have to be very vocal about your feelings.”

“If I start doing all the whole bit of sacrificing…I’ll be miserable.”

“I have no fear of failing…I have accepted the one important thing in my life that I am not perfect. You are going to fail at some point or the other.”
