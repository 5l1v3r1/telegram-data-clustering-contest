JBL release a portable speaker with a 3D light show, the Pulse 4

19 Nov 2019, 13:21 by Scott Plowman

JBL have made some great portable speakers in recent times and today they have expanded and updated their lineup with a new Bluetooth speaker with some funky lights, the Pulse 4.

Taking over from the Pulse 3 the Pulse 4 also offers 12 hours of wireless Bluetooth playback and IPX7 waterproofing with 360 degrees of sound and light show.

The new Pulse 4 differs from the Pulse 3 with its sound. The sound is improved further with a new PartyBoost mode which allows multiple speakers to be connected to each other creating a louder, fuller sound. The new louder and better sound can be controlled via the JBL Connect+ app.

The Pulse 4 brings a full device speaker and light show and is visually stunning. For those who enjoy an outdoor party this is something that could light up the party making it a memorable night. The light show is provided by the 360 degree high-resolution LED lights underneath the surface of the device.

The new JBL Pulse 4 retails for $299.95 from leading retailers now and online at the JBL website from December 19.
