This Morning viewer breaks down in tears as she wins £500K prize giveaway

Holly and Phil broke the news to her

19 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Rebecca Carter

A woman broke down in tears as she was told she had won This Morning's £500,000 prize giveaway.

Ahead of Tuesday's show, Caroline, from Dorset, was shocked when hosts Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield told her she had bagged the cash from the competition.

A preview of the hosts breaking the news to Caroline over the phone played as Phil told her: "Caroline, you recognise our voices so you know we would never pull a stunt like this.

"Somebody always has to win, it's always a real person that wins. Caroline, you are the real person.

"You have just won £500,000."

An emotional Caroline exclaimed: "Oh my God," as she broke down in tears.

Holly said: "Oh my Goodness me," to which Caroline asked: "How much did you say it was? £5,000?"

The hosts clarified: "Five hundred thousand pounds. A half a million pounds."
