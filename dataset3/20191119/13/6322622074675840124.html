Omesti's 51%-owned unit bags RM71m contract from LHDNM for computer equipment rental

19 Nov 2019, 13:50 by Wong Ee Lin

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 19): Omesti Bhd’s 51%-owned unit has bagged a contract worth RM71.47 million to rent computer equipment to the Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia (LHDNM).

Its subsidiary, Formis Network Services Sdn Bhd (FNS), accepted the Letter of Award (LoA) from LHDNM on Monday. The contract is subject to a formal contract agreement to be entered between the two parties in not more than four months from the LoA acceptance date.

The three-year contract, scheduled to commence on Nov 15, 2019 and be completed on Nov 14, 2022, is expected to contribute positively to Omesti's earnings throughout the contract's duration.

Omesti shares closed one sen or 2.02% higher at 50.5 sen today, valuing the company at RM241.67 million. Year-to-date, the counter rose some 26% from 40 sen.
