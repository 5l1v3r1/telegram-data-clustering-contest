Detroit: Become Human arrives on the Epic Games Store December 12

19 Nov 2019, 13:48 by Stephany Nunneley

Quantic Dream’s PlayStation 4 title Detroit: Become Human will arrive on PC next month.

Detroit: Become Human will be released on the Epic Games Store on December 12. A demo featuring the game’s opening scene will be available simultaneously.

It will run you $39.99.

The PC version supports 4K resolution, 60fps and full integration of both mouse/keyboard and gamepad controls.

Previously only available on PlayStation 4, the game is the third PS4-exclusive from Quantic Dream to arrive on PC following Heavy Rain in June and Beyond: Two Souls in July. Demos are still available on the Epic Games Store for both titles.

Announced in March, all three titles are timed-exclusives on Epic’s store.
