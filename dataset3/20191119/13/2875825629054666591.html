Peter Andre slams critics for saying he's 'too strict' with Katie Price's daughter Princess

Peter remained unapologetic for his drastic actions as he set out his reasons why he's a strict dad and proud

19 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Irishmirror.ie

Peter Andre shrugged off parenting critics calling him out for being "too strict" to his children.

Instead, the Mysterious Girl hitmaker insisted he was in the right for being hard on his kids and gave an example of his parenting style.

The pop diva's daughter Princess, 12, had a detention for talking in class but Peter said this wasn't enough punishment for her.

So the singer confiscated all of her electronics for the time being.

The I'm A Celeb reality star refused to give the devices back when Princess asked for them after saying she had learnt her lesson.

Yet Peter remained unapologetic for his drastic actions as he set out his reasons why in his latest new! column .

He penned: "I had a proud dad moment last week. Bista came home from school and told me she'd been given a detention for talking during an assembly after being told to stop by a teacher.

"I asked her what she thought her punishment should be and she told me I should take her electronics away for awhile. So that's what I did. I had planned to do that anyway, but the fact that she thought it was a reasonable punishment impressed me.

"Some people think I'm too strict but she has since told me she has learned from her mistake and never wants to get a detention again."

Peter raises his two kids Princess and Junior, 14, with his first ex-wife Katie Price .

He and Katie go way back as they were married in 2005 until it crumbled in 2009.

They had first crossed paths on the 2004 series of I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here.

Peter found love again, this time with medic Emily MacDonagh.

They were married in 2015 and went onto have two children together.

The happy couple live in their family home with their kids Theodore and Amelia as well as his children from his first marriage.
