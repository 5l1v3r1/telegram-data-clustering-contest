Anxious families wait outside besieged Hong Kong campus

19 Nov 2019, 13:37

HONG KONG: Relatives of some of the young protesters holed up inside a Hong Kong university held anxious vigil on Tuesday (Nov 19) as police vowed to arrest everyone on the campus.

Around 100 protesters remain barricaded inside Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) surrounded by riot squads who have been besieging them for three days.
