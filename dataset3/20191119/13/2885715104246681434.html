The ‘richest’ woman in Pakistan: Bride ditches gold jewellery for tomatoes

In the 2.20-minute viral clip, the reporter can be seen questioning the bride as she continues to flaunt the tomatoes, prices of which have soared over Rs. 300 in Pakistan.

19 Nov 2019, 13:33 by Trends Desk

With gold and diamond jewellery being the go to choice when it comes to wedding accessories, a Pakistani bride decided to pair her wedding attire with tomatoes. As bizarre as it may sound, a video of a television channel interviewing the bride has gone viral on social media, leaving many amused.

A clip of the interview was tweeted by Pakistani journalist Naila Inayat along with a caption that read, “Tomato jewellery. In case you thought you’ve seen everything in life.”
