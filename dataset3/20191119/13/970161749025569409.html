Piers Morgan gloats at Dan Walker as ratings war continues

19 Nov 2019, 13:35

Piers Morgan has reignited his spat with BBC Breakfast’s Dan Walker over viewing figures and is showing no sign of packing it in anytime soon.

Just when thought the pair might put their differences aside for a second, the Good Morning Britain presenter waged another war against his television rival after new stats revealed that the two shows are slowly closing the gap between them.

According to Piers, yesterday’s episode of Good Morning Britain had gained 74,000 viewers and was four share points up when compared to the episode that aired on the same day a year before.

Meanwhile, BBC Breakfast, which is still in the lead, lost 201,000 viewers when compared against the 2018 episode on the same day and was 3.2 share points down.

Overjoyed by the news, Piers took to Twitter to gloat in Dan’s face, despite still coming second place, and said: ‘Hi mate, @mrdanwalker – fancy guzzling this with me to celebrate closest-ever rating gap between @GMB & @BBCBreakfast?’
