First deliveries of Christmas trees arriving in Southwest Florida

19 Nov 2019, 13:40 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- If you are looking for a Christmas tree, one Southwest Florida business has plenty of options for you.

Whited Farm Fresh Christmas Trees got their first shipment of trees in on Tuesday.

They say they are getting their trees early because they export them.

They are located on S. Cleveland Avenue south of Fowler Street in Fort Myers.
