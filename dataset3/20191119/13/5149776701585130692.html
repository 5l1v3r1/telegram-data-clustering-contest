Teenager arrested after altercation outside All Bar One in the Lace Market

He has been charged with possession of a bladed article.

19 Nov 2019, 13:46 by Gemma Toulson

A teenager has been charged with possession of a bladed article after police were called to reports of an incident outside All Bar One in the Lace Market.

Officers were called to Victoria Street in Nottingham at 2.50pm on Monday, November 18, following reports of an altercation between a teenage boy and a man.

A 16-year-old boy was arrested and has since been charged with possession of a bladed article.

A spokesman for the force said he has been bailed and will next appear at Nottingham Youth Court on December 4.
