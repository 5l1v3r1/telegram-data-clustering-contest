19 Nov 2019, 15:45 by Marie Black

We've teamed up with Honor to offer Tech Advisor readers the chance to win one of 15 Honor Band 5 fitness trackers, just in time for Christmas.

One of our top 10 fitness trackers, the Honor Band 5 is a good-looking activity band that doubles as a watch, offering you the time and basic notifications on its 0.95in AMOLED colour display.

It can do a lot more than count your steps, with 10 workout modes available. Waterproof to 50m it's a useful device for swim-tracking - or simply taking in the shower - and there's a built-in heart-rate tracker for analysing your sleep. Honor Band 5 is also the only fitness tracker at its price point to include the ability to measure your blood oxygen level (SpO2).

With a 100mAh battery inside it's incredibly lightweight at 22.7g, but should last up to two weeks on standby.
