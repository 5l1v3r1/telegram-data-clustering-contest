Lionel Messi: Mikel Obi backs Barcelona star to win 2019 Ballon d'Or award

19 Nov 2019, 15:06

- Lionel Messi is among top contenders for this year's Ballon d'Or award

- The 32-year-old has been superb all through the year under review

- Messi already clinched the FIFA The Best award earlier this year

- Nigerian star Mikel Obi joins Lionel Messi fans club

Chelsea legend John Obi Mikel has reportedly backed Barcelona superstar Lionel Messi to win this year's Ballon d'Or award.

The Argentine already won the 'FIFA The Best Player' prestigious prize - beating Cristiano Ronaldo and Virgil van Dijk to the silverware.

Messi is among top contenders for the award, with the likes of Sadio Mane, Mohamed Salah, Eden Hazard and a few more listed in the shortlist released by France Football a few weeks back.
