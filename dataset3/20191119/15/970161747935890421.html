There won't be a Love Island reunion this year, guys

19 Nov 2019, 15:28

It’s easily the most exciting bit of Christmas but this year, the Love Island reunion which sees the latest cast unleash their claws for the confrontation of confrontations, won’t be coming in 2019.

While we’ve been counting down the days for Amber Gill and Greg O’Shea’s first meeting since he dumped her by text (according to her), it’s not going to happen.

‘The cast don’t think there are any plans for a Christmas reunion like last year, but they haven’t been given a straight answer,’ a source told OK! Online.

‘A few of the islanders have seen the producers since leaving the villa and there was a bit of a reunion at the ITV Palooza! last week and the topic was brought up but quickly shut down.

‘Questions were asked about the possibility of an on-screen reunion, but they were all ignored by producers.’

So alas, we’ll never know what would have happened when Amber finally got some answers from Greg; what really went down between India Reynolds and Ovie Soko, and if Chris Taylor and Harley Brash were actually a thing. Is Anton Danyluk’s mum still shaving his bum now Belle Hassan is out of the picture?

You’re guess is as good as ours.

However, while there won’t be any catfights over the Christmas pudding, there is a second helping of Love Island instead and Caroline Flack has already touched down in Cape Town to film the first ever Winter series.

Although, speaking to Metro.co.uk, she’s already dreading the hellish commute.

‘It’s a long old flight just to do that walking,’ she told us. ‘It’s a 13 hour flight! I’ve got to go back and forth the whole time to do Aftersun over here.’

Love Island returns to ITV2 in January 2020.
