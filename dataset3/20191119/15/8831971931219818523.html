Lizzo Being Sued By Postmates Courier

19 Nov 2019, 15:37 by Constance Kalpakis

After placing an order on the food delivery app, Postmates, Lizzo had failed to receive her order, putting the delivery woman at fault. By sending a since now deleted tweet saying, “Hey @Postmates this girl Tiffany W. stole my food. She lucky I don’t fight no more,” along with a photo of Tiffany, she completely exposed her to the millions of people that follow the singer-songwriter.

The Postmates courier, Tiffany Wells, is suing Melissa Jefferson, a.k.a. Lizzo, on account of libel, intentional infliction of emotional distress, and invasion of privacy. The former Postmates worker has expressed fear of leaving the house where someone may recognize her as she was receiving “direct threats of violence” which caused her to be “feared for her physical safety.”

A lot of Lizzo’s fans took it upon themselves to attack and threaten Tiffany, but also stood up for her by criticizing Lizzo’s behavior for inappropriately putting someone who doesn’t share the same voice as her on blast.
