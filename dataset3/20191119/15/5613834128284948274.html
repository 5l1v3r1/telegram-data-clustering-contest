Laced Records Releasing Vinyl Version of ‘Gears 5’ Score

19 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Mike Wilson

Laced Records have snagged another videogame soundtrack for a vinyl release, this one being Ramin Djawadi’s score for Gears 5! The set is up for pre-order right now, and will ship in February 2020.

The two-LP set features 18 tracks specifically mastered for vinyl, and features new artwork by UK based designer, Luke Preece. And like other Laced Records releases, the set also has a limited edition variant with transparent red and orange vinyl.
