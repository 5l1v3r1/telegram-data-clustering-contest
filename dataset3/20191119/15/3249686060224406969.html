EXO's Chen faces his fiery doppelganger in 'Obsession' teasers

19 Nov 2019, 15:15 by germainej

EXO's Chen is facing his fiery doppelganger in the latest teasers for 'Obsession'.

Like his other members, Chen is face to face with his regular EXO identity and a darker persona under the tagline, "The messy world order and anomalies due to twisted orbits." As previously reported, the EXO members are facing their doppelgangers "X-EXO" for their comeback.
