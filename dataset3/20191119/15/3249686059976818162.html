Park Ji Hoon drops teaser image for 2nd mini album '360'

19 Nov 2019, 15:19 by germainej

Park Ji Hoon has dropped his first teaser image for his upcoming second mini album '360'.

The '0 Degrees' teaser image has a calm and relaxing vibe with Park Ji Hoon deep in thought as he rests his head on his hands. Fans can expect his '180 Degrees' teaser image on November 21 and his '360 Degrees' teaser image on November 22.

Park Ji Hoon's '360' mini album drops on December 4 KST. Stay tuned for more teasers until then.
