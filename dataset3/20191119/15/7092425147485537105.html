'We've hit a new low' - Ireland fans criticise Eriksen chant

19 Nov 2019, 15:30 by James Cox

Ireland fans criticised those who took part in a chant aimed at Christian Eriksen during Ireland's 1-1 Euro 2020 qualifying draw with Denmark at the Aviva Stadium last night.

Ireland put in a great performance but ultimately fell just short of qualification, with the play-offs to come.

Denmark's star man Eriksen didn't have a great game but some fans felt chants aimed at him during the game were a step too far.

They stemmed from rumours about an affair between Eriksen's partner and his Tottenham teammate Jan Vertonghen.

Eriksen subsequently rubbished the rumour but he was targeted with chants of "Jan Vertonghen, he's shagging your wife".
