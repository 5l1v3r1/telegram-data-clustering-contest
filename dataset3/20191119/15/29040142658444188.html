Blair Witch comes to PlayStation 4, set to release on December 3

Horror twists its way onto PS4.

19 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Samuel Tolbert

What you need to know

  • Blair Witch is a first-person horror game from Bloober Team.
  • Blair Witch released on Xbox One and PC earlier this year.
  • Bloober Team announced today that Blair Witch is coming to PlayStation 4 on December 3.
  • A new DLC pack will be released for all versions of the game, adding new skins for Bullet and more.

Horror title Blair Witch is making its way over to PlayStation 4, Bloober Team and Lionsgate Games have confirmed. This version of the game coincides with a DLC pack that will be available on all versions of the game called the Good Boy Pack. This DLC, appropriately enough, adds new skins for the dog Bullet, as well as new animations, cell phone games and wallpapers.
