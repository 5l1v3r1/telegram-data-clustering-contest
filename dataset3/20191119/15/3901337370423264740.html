'Messi Cannot Be Compared To Pele!'

19 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Kurt Buckerfield

Following his recent touchline argument with Argentina captain Lionel Messi, Brazil boss Tite has insisted the FC Barcelona superstar cannot be compared to Selecao legend Pele.

Algeria Boss Reacts To Benzema's Shock Plea

In Argentina's 1-0 win over Brazil in an international friendly last Friday, Tite and Messi exchanged some heated words after the Selecao manager thought the 32-year-old should have been booked for a foul.

Days later, Tite has responded to comparisons between the Argentina captain and Brazil icon Pele, insisting that not even the Catalan club forward has reached the levels the 79-year-old did.

"Messi better than Pele? Pele is incomparable," he said, according to Marca.

"Anyone who wants to compare Pele with another athlete knows nothing.

"Messi is extraordinary, but within humans. Pele is outside humans, is outside normal parameters.

"I do not say this because I am Brazilian, I say it because I saw that story, the magnitude of Pele, his plays, the individual technique, the header, ambidextrous, his strength, his impulse.

"If you want to find a defect, you will not find it."

"As if that were not enough, Pele had an extraordinary charisma," he added.
