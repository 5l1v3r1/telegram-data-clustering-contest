Australians will also be seeing Black Friday sales on the Google Store next week

19 Nov 2019, 15:15 by Scott Plowman

Sometimes we get jealous of our friends in the US who see sales of Google devices consistently. This year though Australians will also be able to join in the fun on Black Friday as Google will be having some deals on their hardware.

Although the deals are unknown at this stage the Google Store has a big advertisement on their landing page advertising the sale starting next Friday, Black Friday. At this stage we have not seen any leaks with what exactly will be on sale and how much for but we expect it to be fairly similar to the deals on the US Google Store.

The US Google store will apparently feature a cool US$200 off the Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL and a $100 discount on the Pixel 3a and Pixel 3a XL. The US store will also include sales on the Google Slate, Google Stadia, Nest Hub Max, Nest Hub, Nest Mini and Nest Wi-fi.
