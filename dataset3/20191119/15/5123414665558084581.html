Premiere: Julien Chaptal - "Fantasy" (Awesome Soundwave)

Get a taste of Julien Chaptal's upcoming full-length on Awesome Soundwave

19 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Danny Kaufman

This coming Friday marks the release of Julien Chaptal's full-length, The Way on Carl Cox's lesser known imprint, Awesome Soundwave. The L.P. may not be his first overall but it's the French producer's debut on the label and it's loaded with 11 club-ready tunes without any filler to show for, including the opener which we have the exclusive premiere for here today. 

This beautiful beast is titled "Fantasy" and it's a sparse one that stars a quirky acid line that'll reach into your mind and turn it inside out.
