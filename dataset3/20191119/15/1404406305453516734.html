Dolphins Player Mark Walton Waived After Allegedly Hitting Pregnant Woman

19 Nov 2019, 15:58 by VladTV

Running back Mark Walton was recently waived by the Miami Dolphins after he was arrested for aggravated assault of a pregnant woman. According to the police report, Walton allegedly punched a woman in her face numerous times after finding out she was pregnant with his child.

The situation reportedly went down at 4:15 am Tuesday, and along with punching the woman, Walton allegedly pushed the woman into a wall. Walton was arrested and taken to Broward County Jail.
