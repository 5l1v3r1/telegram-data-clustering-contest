Former La Liga star held in Costa del Sol drugs swoop was trafficking gang ringleader

Sergi Contreras Pardo, known as Koke, was detained at a property in Estepona, which was raided as part of a police operation stretching across southern Spain

19 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Brett Gibbons, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/brett-gibbons/

A former La Liga star has been arrested on suspicion of running a drug trafficking operation on Spain's Costa del Sol.

He was accused of being the ringleader of a drugs gang after police swooped on properties along the coast, which is a popular winter resort for British holidaymakers and a favourite destination for expats.

Sergi Contreras Pardo, known as Koke, was detained at a property in Estepona, which was raided as part of a police operation stretching across southern Spain.

About 20 people were arrested, including Koke's brother, and officers seized cash, weapons and drugs, according to local media reports.

Koke played for Malaga and Rayo Vallecano in La Liga and had spells at Marseille, a loan in Portugal, two periods at Greek premier club Aris de Salonica and Houston Dynamo in the US.

The forward also played in Germany, Bolivia and Azerbaijan in his much-travelled career.

He finished his career in the Indian Premier League.
