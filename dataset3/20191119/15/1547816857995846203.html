Bigg Boss 13: Mrunal Jain Says ‘Rashami Desai’s Emotions Are Not Drama’; Also Addresses Her Alleged Affairs With Sidharth Shukla, Arhaan Khan- EXCLUSIVE

19 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Neeki Singh

Rashami Desai is undoubtedly one of the most popular contestants of Bigg Boss 13. We spoke to her Rakhee brother and co-star Mrunal Jain and in our exclusive chat, the actor addressed her link-up stories with Sidharth Shukla, Arhaan Khan, apart from taking a big stand for the actress

Rashami Desai is one of the most talked about contestants in the ongoing season of Bigg Boss. On one side, the Uttaran actress has been receiving appreciation and love from her fans, while on the other side some are calling her gestures ‘fake’. We spoke to her Rakhee brother and co-star Mrunal Jain and here’s what he had to say:

Are you following Bigg Boss 13?
I am trying to follow the show. But at times, miss it due to my hectic schedules. However, I closely observe the clippings on social media. And when I see Rashami inside the house, I feel she was better off outside.
