Ferrari F8 Tributo video review

19 Nov 2019, 15:10 by Sergius Barretto

The F8 Tributo is the latest in Ferrari's range of mid-engine V8 supercars. Its name is an ode to its own award-winning V8 engine, which now makes 720hp, and also to other V8 Ferraris of the past like the mighty F40. We drive it on the Fiorano race track and in the mountains around Maranello to give you the verdict.
