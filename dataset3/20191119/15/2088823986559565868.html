Thurman Thomas, O.J. Simpson among running back finalists for NFL 100 All-Time Team

19 Nov 2019, 15:32

Bills legends Thurman Thomas and O.J. Simpson are among 24 finalists among running backs for the NFL 100 All-Time Team announced by the league.

The 12 running backs selected will be revealed at 8 p.m. Friday on the NFL Network in the first of six weekly episodes of the NFL 100 All-Time Team. The team also has 10 head coaches, with two being revealed Friday.

Thomas, a 2007 Pro Football Hall of Fame inductee, was the 1991 NFL Most Valuable Player and finished with 12,074 rushing yards and 16,532 all-purpose yards. He rushed for 1,000 yards in eight consecutive seasons.

Simpson, a 1985 Hall of Fame inductee, won four NFL rushing titles and ran for a then-record 2,003 yards in 1973. A six-time Pro Bowler, he finished with 11,236 rushing yards and 14,368 combined yards.
