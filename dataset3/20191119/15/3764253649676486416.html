Luis Enrique: Ex-Barcelona boss appointed Spain manager

19 Nov 2019, 15:07

- Luis Enrique left his Spain job in June to be with his daughter who was battling bone cancer

- He is set to return to management with Spain months after losing his daughter

- Spanish FA President Luis Rubiales had reportedly promised to support Enrique's decision if he decided to take charge of Spain en route to Euro 2020

Former Barcelona manager, Luis Enrique, has been offered a route back to managerial role with Spain three months after the tragic death of his daughter Xana.

The Spanish FA reportedly want Enrique back in the dugout for the Spain National team ahead of Euro 2020.

AS News reports that Spanish FA President, Luis Rubiales, has offered Enrique the chance to guide Spain to the Euros 2020.

He has replace Roberto Moreno who oversaw Spain national team’s proceedings since June.
