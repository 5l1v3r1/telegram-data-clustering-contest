Exclusive! Rapid-fire with the fiery Tara Sutaria

19 Nov 2019, 15:27 by Suman Sharma

Once you lay your eyes on Tara Sutaria, it is hard to look away without being smitten by her cute angelic face. As the actor turns 24 today, we dig in deeper to find out what this young lady likes and dislikes.

From her favourite food to what she likes to do in her free time, here’s a quick rapid-fire with Tara Sutaria.

Favourite Food:

Fortunately or unfortunately it's biryani.

Favourite Actor:

Ranbir Kapoor

Favourite Film:

Tamasha and Om Shanti Om

Favourite Designer:

Anamika Khanna

Favourite Outfit:

I love to wear chikankaari

Favourite Color:

White

Favourite past time:

Singing

Favourite Book:

Right now I am reading Love Story

Favourite song:

Chaudhavi ka chaand ho ya aftaab ho...

Favourite Make-up style:

less is more so anything in nude

Your quick make-up tip:

Concealer, blush and mascara.

Her beauty advice to fans:

Just stick to what suits you personally. Don't try to copy any looks. Don't put so much makeup on your face. If you are focusing on your eyes don't focus on your lips. If you are focusing on your lips don't focus on your eyes. 

One beauty advice  she likes

One sentence my mother keeps saying to me is beauty is as beauty does so what you do is what makes you beautiful. 

Beauty regime:

I just take a little dahi, haldi and honey, I put this on my face. I love make-up but I believe simple is effective.

Fitness regime:

I don't like to go to the gym. I just dance. I started dancing when I was 4 years old. I think the best workout you can do is Zumba and dance.

Do you believe in diets?

I don't diet. I eat everything I'm moderation. I feel lucky that I don't put on weight. So I don't need a dietitian. If I am shooting I eat healthily. I avoid white sugar and too much rice because it bloats.
