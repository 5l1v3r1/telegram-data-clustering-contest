Tropical Storm Sebastien forms in the Atlantic: U.S. NHC

19 Nov 2019, 15:21

(Reuters) - Tropical Storm Sebastien has formed in the Atlantic and is expected to strengthen slightly in next day or so, the U.S. National Hurricane Center said on Tuesday.

The eighteenth named storm was located about 275 miles (445 km) north-east of the Leeward Islands, clocking a maximum sustained winds 45 miles-per-hour (75 km/h), the Miami-based weather forecaster said.

Reporting by K. Sathya Narayanan and Brijesh Patel in Bengaluru
