BJP workers press for local body polls, stage demo

The protesters take out rally, raise slogans against govt.

19 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Special Correspondent

Around 100 workers of Bharatiya Janata Party on Tuesday courted arrest while staging a protest near the Legislative Assembly to press for the conduct of local body polls.

They took out a rally from Immaculate Conception Cathedral towards the Assembly.

But the police stopped them near St. Ange Street and S. V. Patel junction. They shouted slogans against the government for not holding the civic body polls.

Nominated legislator and BJP president V. Saminathan said local body elections were not held after the tenure of civic body ended in 2011.

The government was not serious in holding the polls out of fear of losing the elections, he charged.
