Announcer During Bills-Dolphins Says He Could “Squeeze Out A Dirty Sanchez” Mustache (VIDEO)

19 Nov 2019, 19:11 by Darrelle Lincoln

This announcer likely had no clue what ‘dirty sanchez’ actually meant when he said it.

During the final seconds of the Bills-Dolphins game — CBS play-by-play announcer Spero Dedes and color commentator Adam Archuleta got into a discussion about grooming.

Archuleta: “One has to ask themselves, a $30 tie or a $30 mustache.”

Dedes: “Can we get you to grow the stache by next week?”

That’s when Archuleta said he “could probably squeeze out maybe a Dirty Sanchez, not really a full stache.”
