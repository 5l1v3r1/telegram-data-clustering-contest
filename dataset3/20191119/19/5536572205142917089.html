Davante Adams, Stefon Diggs Clown Booger McFarland For ‘MNF’ Screwup (TWEETS)

19 Nov 2019, 19:42 by Darrelle Lincoln

A change of venue to Mexico City for “Monday Night Football” this week didn’t stop fans from throwing shots at ESPN’s Booger McFarland over his questionable commentary.

This time around was much different, because Pro Bowl Green Bay Packers wide receiver Davonte Adams couldn’t help but to join in and talk about his ramblings.
