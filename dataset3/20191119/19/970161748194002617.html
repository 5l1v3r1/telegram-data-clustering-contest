Who is I'm A Celebrity star Adele Roberts' girlfriend Kate Holderness?

19 Nov 2019, 19:00

Adele Roberts has got off to a flying start on I’m A Celebrity, winning herself an automatic place in the main camp after shrieking her way through an opening challenge alongside Nadine Coyle.

The Radio 1 DJ also raised a few eyebrows when she revealed her luxury item was a photo of singer and Loose Women host Jane McDonald – a move which left some viewers puzzled but others praising her for her offbeat choice.

One person who will be offering support from back home is Adele’s girlfriend Kate Holderness – who is also looking after her social media accounts in her absence.

Here’s what you need to know about Kate…

Who is Adele Roberts’ girlfriend Kate Holderness?

Kate is an actress and vlogger, who began her TV career back in 1997 in the CITV show Knight School.

However, more recently she’s been seen in Coronation Street, playing a character named Jamie Lee in a couple of episodes in 2013, as well as a pair of period dramas for the BBC – Henry VIII and his Six Wives, in which she played his final wife Catherine Parr, and 2017’s Elizabeth I.

Her other TV credits include Heartbeat and Emmerdale.

Kate and Adele also host their own food show, Friday Night Fakeaway – in which they make restaurant-style creations with unusually healthy food ingredients.

You can catch the pair in action on their YouTube channel.

Kate is also running Adele’s social media channels while she is in the jungle – and has given her followers some insight into the Jane McDonald photo, explaining that every campmate has to choose something different and that her first choices of a pillow and family photo had already been taken.

‘The brief was “something to make you happy” so she just chose something that never fails to cheer her up and something that reminds her of her Early Morning Radio 1 listeners,’ she explained.

‘It’s a bit of a long-running feature on Adele’s show, when they’re having a bit of a slump and feeling down, a listener will request a ‘bit of Jane’ to get them through their day. I have to say that most of her decisions are made at 2am when she gets up for her show, most things make perfect sense at that time!’

How long have Adele and Kate been dating?

It’s not known how long Adele and Kate have been together.

Adele previously came out as bisexual when she was a contestant on Big Brother 3 back in 2002.

She was seen having to let fellow housemate Lee down gently, revealing that she was bi and had a girlfriend outside of the house.

However Lee took the rejection on the chin and the pair became close friends regardless, remaining close throughout their time on the show.

I’m A Celebrity continues on ITV on Tuesday night at 9pm.
