What channel is Scotland vs Kazakhstan? Live stream, TV and kick-off details

It's the second game of Scotland's Euro 2020 double header as they look to build on the win against Cyprus.

19 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Chris Doyle, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/chris-doyle/

Another dead rubber in the context of Group I, yes, but another chance for Scotland to build momentum ahead of the play-offs.

Steve Clarke unconvincingly edged past Cyprus at the weekend now they've got revenge on their minds.

Kazakhstan humiliated Alex McLeish's Scots in Astana earlier this year and they visit Hampden looking to do it all over again.

A positive result can at least put right some of the wrongs of that horrific night.

It's also the last bit of international action before the playoff in March, and the final chance for players to impress.

Here's all the details you need to know ahead of the Euro 2020 clash.

When and where will the game take place?

The match will take place on Tuesday, November 19 at Hampden .

Kick-off is at 7.45pm.

What channel is it on?

You can watch the game live on Sky Sports Football.

Is it being streamed online?

Yes, subscribers can watch on the Sky Go app.

Where else can I follow the game?

You can follow it right here on dailyrecord.co.uk of course! We'll be running a live blog well ahead of the TV coverage, bringing you all the key moments as well as post-match reaction and analysis.

A match report and much, much more will also be available on our website following the final whistle.
