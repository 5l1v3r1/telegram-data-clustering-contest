Assassin’s Creed II’s 10th anniversary brings some new game music tracks

19 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Aran Suddi

Can you believe it has been ten years since Assassin’s Creed II was released and Ezio Auditore da Firenze arrived on the scene of the war between Assassins and Templars? Well, it has been but even now there is some new content available for the game’s fans as music composer has released 17 music tracks and demos that never made the final cut, and you can listen to them below.
