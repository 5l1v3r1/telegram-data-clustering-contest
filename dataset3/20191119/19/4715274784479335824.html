Holiday colors

19 Nov 2019, 19:03 by Norman Cruz and Roy Tomandao

​Performers dressed as Santa Claus, princesses, and mascots join the Grand Magical Christmas Parade at SM City Bacoor in Cavite to mark the mall’s countdown for Christmas. The scene was repeated at SM Santa Rosa, which opened its colorful Christmas Rainbow Experience with City Mayor Arlene Arcillas (below) together with her mother Josefina Arcillas, Sanggunian members, SM’s Angel Torreja, assistant vice president for operations, and Evelyn Verzola, mall manager.
