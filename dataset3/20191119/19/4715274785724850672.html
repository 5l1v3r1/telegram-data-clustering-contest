Blackpink’s Lisa for a local fashion label

19 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

Thai rapper and dancer Lisa Manoban of K-pop group Blackpink is the newest face of local apparel and accessories label Penshoppe. 

She joins the growing list of global endorsers of the homegrown brand. 

“We are excited to have Lisa in our growing Penshoppe family,” says Jeff Bascon, vice president for brand management of Golden ABC.

At 22 years old, Lisa is the youngest member as well as the main dancer of the group. She is currently the most followed female K-pop idol on Instagram with over 27 million followers. 

Her group has been breaking records around the world. They are the first K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella in 2019. 

In choosing her as their newest endorser, Bascon said, “She is effortlessly talented and her natural rhythm brings out the confidence in whatever she wears.”
