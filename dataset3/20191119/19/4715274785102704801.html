Seeing in high definition

19 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

Italian eyewear brand Polar, known for its polarized lenses, is now available in the country at Sarabia Optical. 

The brand promises that glare, which happens when light hits a smooth, reflective surface, will be a thing in the past with its lenses with polarized filters. The lenses offer crisp visual experience with more natural and vivid colors, with a perfect amount of contrast, accurate detail perception, and better depth perception. 

Polar lenses also have an anti-scratch coating as well as oil- and water-resistant coating to enhance each pair’s durability. They also feature a UVA, UVB, and UVC protection filter perfect for those who love to explore.

It also offers a variety of frames and lenses in different tints and colors, as well as bold frame shapes and sizes—from the famous aviators to eccentric hexagons. 

Polar is available at Sarabia Optical nationwide.
