Rainbow Six Siege limited time event Money Heist kicks off tomorrow, free weekend coming

19 Nov 2019, 19:41 by Stephany Nunneley

A new limited-time mini-event for Rainbow Six Siege based on the Netflix series Money Heist will kick off tomorrow.

The Rainbow Six Siege event will run November 20-25, and the game will also be free to play November 21-25.

This new event is set on the map Bank with the returning game mode Hostage. The hostage will be disguised in a red suit and mask worn by the robbers and their captives in Money Heist.

In the event, Attackers and Defenders will fight over the Hostage.
