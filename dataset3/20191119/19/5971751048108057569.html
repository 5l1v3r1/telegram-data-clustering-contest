Nokia 3.1 Plus, 6.1, 6.1 Plus, 7.1, 8 Sirocco and 9 PureView get the November 2019 patch

19 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Stipe

Nokia Mobile started strong this week by releasing the latest Google Security Patch for November for a number of devices. Our Nokia 7 plus received the update yesterday, but users with devices across the portfolio started reporting about the update availability.

The device that started receiving the update include the Nokia 6 and Nokia 6.1 Plus. The update for Nokia 6.1 is 97.2MB in size, while the patch for Nokia 6.1 Plus is 62MB in size. The update for Nokia 7.1, just containing the November security update, is 62.3MB in size, while Nokia 8 Sirocco got a 94.3MB update.

The November update also hit the Nokia 3.1 Plus (97.7MB) and the current flagship device, the Nokia 9 PureView (81.8MB).
