Davao Culinary Cup

19 Nov 2019, 19:12

Solane LPG recently sponsored another successful Davao Culinary Cup at SM City Davao. The DCC is the biggest, most prestigious culinary competition in Mindanao. It captures a similar value that Solane is consistently trying to advocate; that of recognizing the Filipino culinary talent and excellence that is at par with international standards, according to Solane Marketing manager Valeri Villano. As one of the Gold sponsors of the event, Solane also gave away exciting freebies and prizes to the winners and attendees.
