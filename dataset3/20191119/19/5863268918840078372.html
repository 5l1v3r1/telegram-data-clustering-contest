Nasir Chinyoti refutes reports regarding his accident

19 Nov 2019, 19:37 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – Renowned comedian Nasir Chinyoti today (Nov. 18) today released a video statement refuting all the rumors about his accident, and advised people to refrain from spreading fake news.

Nasir is currently filming the sequel of Indian Punjabi comedy film “Chal Mera Putt” in London. He made a video on Facebook and expressed, “Spreading such fake news which can hurt someone and put his family in worry is a bad act and one should refrain from hurting other’s family by such false news”.
