In Pictures: Ankush Bhardwaj & Mistu Bardhan London concert

19 Nov 2019, 19:51 by Lakh Baddhan

Indian Idol 10 finalist Ankush Bhardwaj and Bollywood singer Mistu Bardhan performed in a sell-crowd at London’s Logan Hall.

The show titled ‘Meri Awaaz Hi Meri Pechaan Hai’ took place on Sunday 17th November 2019.

The UK tour was brought to you by Rock On Music. BizAsiaLive.com was the exclusive digital partner of the event.
