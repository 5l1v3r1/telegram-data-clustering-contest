Saudi airline flynas signs order for 10 Airbus A321XLR jets

19 Nov 2019, 19:29

DUBAI (Reuters) - Flynas has signed an order for 10 long-range Airbus A321XLR jets, the Saudi budget airline said at the Dubai Airshow on Tuesday.

The Riyadh-based discount airline ordered 80 A320neo family jets in 2017, with purchase options for an additional 40 aircraft. In June, it signed a preliminary agreement to swap some of those for larger A321 versions.
