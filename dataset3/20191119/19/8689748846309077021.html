Just In: Late Aliu Mahama’s Mafara Hotel Gutted Down By Fire (+Photos)

19 Nov 2019, 19:24 by BB Jones

According to a report published by Ghgossip.com, a hotel which belonged to the late Alhaji Aliu Mahama, the Mafara Hotel has been destroyed completely by fire in Tamale.

The son of the late Alhaji Aliu Mahama identified as Farouk Mahama confirmed the accident to the website saying it happened just few minutes ago.

He revealed that there were extensive damages to properties worth thousands of cedis, but “thankfully, no life was lost”.

Detail about the cause of the fire is believed to have been as a result of a power outage.

The hotel which is a top 3.5 Star hotel is said to be located at Russian Bungalows in Tamale.
