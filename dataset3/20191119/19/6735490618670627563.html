The Game Claims To Have Slept With Both Kim And Khloe Kardashian On Leaked Migos Track ‘What You Expect’

19 Nov 2019, 19:26 by Aynslee Darmon

The Game is getting explicit about his previous relationship with both Kim and Khloe Kardashian.

The rapper appears on a leaked track from Migos and seemingly bragged about sleeping with both famous sisters. “What You Expect” surfaced on Soundcloud earlier this week.

“Kim K, that’s my old thing / Khloe K, that’s my old thing,” he rapped. “F**kin’ stars when they was no names / Everything was on the low.”

This isn’t the first time The Game has rapped about the KKW Beauty mogul when he released a preview of an unreleased song, “I held Kim Kardashian by her throat, nigga/I made her swallow my kids until she choke, nig**/I should apologize cause ’Ye is my folks, nig**.” But he told Complex that the lyrics were old.

“When that snippet came out, that wasn’t how I currently felt. That song was like 2-years-old,” he explained. “I’m not apologizing for that. I don’t think I need to apologize for doing something that I did that is done already.”

He also plans to release his ninth (and possibly final) album Born 2 Rap later this month, on Nov. 29.

The Game also claims to have had relations with Lisa Ray, Stacy Dash, Blac Chyna and Erica Mena on the single “What You Expect”.
