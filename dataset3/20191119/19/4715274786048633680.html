Sale Alert: Luxe accessories at discounted prices

19 Nov 2019, 19:10 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

Taste a luxe life at lower prices as The SM Store’s branded watches, eyewear, and jewelry are on sale on Nov. 21-24.

The sale is available at the Branded Accessories Department of The SM Store nationwide.
