Foreign delegates visit Chodavaram

They interact with locals and employees of Village Secretariat

19 Nov 2019, 19:49 by Special Correspondent

A foreign delegation comprising 23 members from 19 Afro-Asian countries on Tuesday visited Chodavaram Gram Sachivalayam on Tuesday as a part of International Training Programme of the National Institute of Rural Development, according to the Project Director, DRDA.

They interacted with locals and employees of the Village Secretariat, enquired about the job chart of various employees, objectives of the introduction of the Village Secretariat and about drinking water, streetlights, solid wealth processing centre sheds etc in the village.

They appreciated the State government for taking administration to the doorsteps of the common man, an official release said. The study team also visited horticulture and agriculture programmes taken up under Zero Budget Natural Forming (ZBNF).
