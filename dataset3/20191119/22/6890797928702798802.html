Austrac sues Westpac for money laundering

19 Nov 2019, 22:34 by Houses and Holes

Oh dear, via ABC:

AUSTRAC has applied to the Federal Court for civil penalty orders against Westpac for deficient oversight of its anti-money laundering and terrorism financing obligations.

AUSTRAC boss Nicole Rose described Westpac’s behaviour as “serious and systemic non-compliance”.
