Lewis Capaldi drops new track "Before You Go"

19 Nov 2019, 22:31 by Nick Fitzgerald

The Scottish singer has revealed it's his most personal song yet

A Christmas gift has arrived early for fans of Lewis Capaldi with a brand new single released earlier today.

The Someone You Loved singer broke the news on social media, saying "Before You Go" would be out by 5pm.

Capaldi has revealed it's his most personal track yet, describing the tune as "my favourite song I've ever written".

The release comes as the singer is due to create an extended version of his debut album, Divinely Uninspired To A Hellish Extent.

Capaldi wrote to his millions of followers:

"The past year has been absolutely wild, didn't in a million years think of all this stuff would happen to me.

"Less than two years ago I was lucky enough to get to gigs in small pubs and bars back home in Scotland and somehow I am writing this with a number one single in America and getting to play to and meet thousands of ya every night all over the world!

"Today at 5pm UK time I am releasing 'Before You Go', which is by far te most personal tune I've ever written.

"Can't wait for ya to hear it!"
