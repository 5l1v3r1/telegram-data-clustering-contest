Family-Planning Grants

19 Nov 2019, 22:59 by BRIEF

WASHINGTON – A D.C. Circuit panel dismissed as moot an appeal over changes to federal family-planning grants that prioritized abstinence over contraception, since the Trump administration dropped the changes while the case was being briefed.
