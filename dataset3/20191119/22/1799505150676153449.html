Phone Screens and Pixels

19 Nov 2019, 22:55 by BRIEF

OAKLAND, Calif. – A putative class of consumers have not shown that Apple made misleading representations about the screen sizes of iPhone X devices but they can show that they may have been misled by representations about pixel resolutions, a federal court in California ruled.
