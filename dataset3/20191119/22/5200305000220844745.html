Peace TV’s two channels disappear from Sky EPG

19 Nov 2019, 22:11 by Raj Baddhan

Peace TV’s two services, Peace TV English and Peace TV Urdu, have suddenly disappeared from the Sky EPG.

The Islamic channels have been broadcasting for a number of years on the Sky platform, hence the move comes a surprise. Peace TV English was available on EPG 740 and Peace TV Urdu on EPG 747.

Recently, Prime TV and B4U Plus were also abruptly removed from the Sky EPG.

At the time of filing this article, BizAsiaLive.com was awaiting a response from Peace TV.
