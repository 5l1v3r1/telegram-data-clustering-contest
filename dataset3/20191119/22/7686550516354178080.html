I'm a Celebrity Roman Kemp's confusion over Jane McDonald picture gets heated

Who would have thought that 90s singer Jane McDonald would be at the centre of argument?

19 Nov 2019, 22:00 by Katie-Ann Gupwell

It's the luxury item that has, perhaps, raised a few eyebrows over the past 24 hours.

On Monday night's episode of I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here, viewers were left baffled at the choice of Adele Roberts' luxury item.

The Radio 1 DJ opted for a little more unusual option than the most - a photograph of Loose Women star and cruise ship legend Jane McDonald.

It was hoped the image would bring her luck during her stint in the jungle.

But, on Tuesday night's episode, it appears a few people in the Main Camp are just as baffled as the viewers at home.

In fact, things started to get a little heated when Roman commented on the Jane McDonald picture.

Kate said: “Jane McDonald is looking over us, can you think of a song of hers?”

“I know her off Loose Women, I don’t know her actual songs but I know her from the cruising show when she does covers," replied Adele.

Myles and Roman laughed and said: “No one knows what she does.”

Kate said: “She’s a legend.”

This is where things started to take a little turn for the worse.

"She’s a legend is she?" Roman said. "Name a song?”

Kate couldn’t think of one, which led to some chuckling from Myles and Roman.

Adele was then quick to reply, saying: “She’ll cruise around the world, for example she’d go to Iceland and then sing a Bjork or would go to the USA and sing Proud Mary.”

Roman said: “It’s just random mate.”

“Why though? I don’t understand," responded Adele.

“How is it not random?” commented Roman.

“But if I had brought in a picture of Ru-Paul would you have the same reaction?” asked Adele.

Roman responded by saying he didn’t know and that he just thought it was random.

Adele then hit back, saying: “That’s prejudice, I’m allowed to like what I want.”

By this point, Roman had realised he'd started to wind her up a bit, and began to apoligise.

“I wasn’t saying that at all," he said. "I’m sorry, I didn’t mean to.”

Adele replied: “It’s like you’re saying ‘why would I like Jane McDonald?’”

“Adele it’s not," he said. "The situation is funny.”

Roman continued to apologise before Adele claimed: "It’s fine, you are just going on about it so much…”

While Kate tried to explain it was just a bit of fun around the camp, Roman tried to end the conversation claiming he liked the picture being there.

The Jane McDonald fuelled spat eventually ended with a hug.
