Tory press office rebrands Twitter account as 'factcheck UK' amid huge backlash

Royle Family star Ralf Little began tweeting about Jeremy Corbyn having won the televised ITV debate from a 'fake' Tory Party Twitter account

19 Nov 2019, 22:29 by Amardeep Bassey

The Conservative Party have been blasted for rebranding their press office Twitter account "factcheck UK" especially for tonight's election debate.

Afterwards they told how they would be fact-checking Labour throughout tonight's election debate.

The move was met with derision from the likes of comedian Ralf Little - who changed his Twitter name to "Conservative Press Office" in response.

The Royle Family star then began tweeting about Jeremy Corbyn having won the televised ITV debate that pitted the two leaders for the first time together on TV.

Even right-wing commentator Julia Hartley-Brewer tweeted to say “Ha. @CCHQPress asked for this..”
