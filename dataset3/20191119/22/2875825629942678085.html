I'm a Celeb star Ian Wright is 'scared of his own shadow' says Mark Bright

EXCLUSIVE: Mark Bright put the boot into Ian Wright's I'm a Celeb chances as he prepared to go in at the weekend

19 Nov 2019, 22:00 by Irishmirror.ie

Football pundit Mark Bright reckons his former team-mate Ian Wright has no chance in I'm a Celeb as he is “scared of his own shadow”.

Bright, below, says he knows Wright well, after they played for Crystal Palace and lived together for five years.

“I do not know why he’s gone in there,” he joked on Love Sport radio.

“He is scared of the dark. He is scared of snakes, he is scared of creepy crawlies, he is scared of his own shadow.”

The Mirror revealed yesterday that Bright, 57, is dating Wright’s sister Dionne.

This is despite a 15-year rift between the siblings, which Ian said began when Dionne disrespected his wife Nancy.

Urging viewers to vote for his pal to do any of the tasks, Bright added that Wright was “very particular about food”.

He added: “Eating these things, I know he’ll look, put them in his mouth and then spit them out. I can’t wait.”
