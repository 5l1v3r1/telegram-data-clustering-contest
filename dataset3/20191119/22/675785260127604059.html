EuroMillions results: One lucky British ticketholder scoops £105million jackpot

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

19 Nov 2019, 22:07 by Neil Murphy

A UK winner has scooped an incredible £105m in tonight's EuroMillions draw.

The win makes them one of the biggest ever British lottery winners.

Players are being urged to check their tickets to see if they have won.

Andy Carter, Senior Winners’ Advisor at The National Lottery, said, "On our 25th birthday, one very lucky ticket-holder has scooped tonight’s entire £105M EuroMillions jackpot. Players across the country are now urged to check their tickets to see if they have won big.

“Tonight’s big winner has scooped the sixth EuroMillions jackpot banked in the UK this year. As The National Lottery reaches its 25th birthday, players continue to win big, adding to the 5,500 millionaires created since launch.

"By buying a ticket for EuroMillions or any other National Lottery game, every player is helping to raise, on average, around £30M every week for projects across the UK, including funding everything from local community projects to supporting the nation’s athletes.”
