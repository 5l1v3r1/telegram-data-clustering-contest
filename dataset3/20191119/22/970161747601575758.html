I'm A Celebrity's Roman Kemp does the best impression of Ant and Dec

19 Nov 2019, 22:14

Roman Kemp did the most hilarious impression of Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly on I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here – and it’s safe to say it was just ‘great’.

As the campmates gathered around to eat, they cheered on a large lizard who was trying to climb a slope.

The celebrities then got to know each other’s weird and wonderful hidden talents which started by Myles Stephenson beatboxing, Jacqueline Jossa then joined in by making the most realistic-sounding baby crying noises.

Andrew Maxwell then made a didgeridoo sound which had the whole camp in stitches.

But, it got better because Roman Kemp decided to do his best impression of Dec.

‘Ok Ant don’t forget to dial, 09020 and for Ian add 04,’ he laughed.

The radio DJ then did his best impression of Ant when he watches the campers do a bushtucker trial.

‘You’re doing great, you’re doing great,’ Roman said as he flipped his head back and forth (which we must admit was brilliant).

Taking it in good jest, Ant and Dec show they can always laugh at themselves at Ant responded: ‘That sounded absolutely nothing like us.’

The comedy duo were on top form on Tuesday night as they also couldn’t help but make light of the debate between Boris Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn on the live TV debate.

‘Two camps are about to become one, but first there’s work to be done,’ Ant said as he introduced I’m A Celeb.

‘Which means five of them are about to face their deepest fears,’ Dec continued.

Ant joked: ‘Welcome to Australia and another TV show where two men like the sound of their own voices.’

‘Evening Prime Minister…for now,’ Dec laughed.

Ant then interrupted as he listened into his ear piece, he continued: ‘Hang on, hang on, I am just hearing that even this show must remain politically impartial.’

Dec responded: ‘You’re right, good evening Prime Minister for now, and good evening bloke who wants to be Prime Minister, although why anyone who would want that job is beyond me.’

The jokers continued as Ant said: ‘Caitlin proved there’s nothing worse than someone going on and on and on and not listening to a word you say.’

Dec interjected: ‘Again evening Prime Minister and bloke who wants to be Prime Minister.’
