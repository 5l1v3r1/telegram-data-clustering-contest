UFC News: Francis Ngannou meets Dana White, teases 'big announcement' regarding his next fight

19 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Lennard Surrao

There was a time when the Francis Ngannou hype train had well and truly been derailed, however, the Cameroonian-French Heavyweight restored his intimidating aura by putting together three consecutive finishes in the past year.

Ngannou, who hasn't fought since June this year, could end his short hiatus soon as the #2 ranked Heavyweight revealed that he had had a very productive meeting with Dana White and Hunter Campbell of the UFC.

The Predator stated that he is excited to get back to work and that the fans should be prepared for some big news that's on the way.

He tweeted:

Just had a very productive meeting with the boss man @danawhite and Hunter Campbell @ufc. Excited to get back to work. Big news coming soon #UNCROWNDEDCHAMP #thepredator #Francis2020
