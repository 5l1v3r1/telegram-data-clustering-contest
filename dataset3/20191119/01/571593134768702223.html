11 gifts for your friend who’s still a big kid at heart

19 Nov 2019, 01:08 by Mashable Brand X

We all know someone who hasn’t quite let go of their childhood. And hey, we’d be lying if we didn’t admit the holiday period can bring out the inner child in us all. 

As we are creeping closer and closer to the holiday period, it’s time to start thinking about what you want to buy for your family, friends, and that colleague you don’t really know but you pulled their name out for Secret Santa.

From nostalgic video games to intense board games, we’ve put together some items you can give to those who are young-at-heart from as little as $20 to something a bit more expensive.  

1. Oculus Quest 

Image: Amazon/oculus

Is your young-at-heart friend also quite tech savvy? The Oculus Quest provides an awesome VR experience — it is immersive, incredibly convenient with its lack of wires, and overall it’s a lot of fun. Beatsaber, anyone?

Price: From $649 on Amazon 

2.  Harry Potter Wizard Training Wand 

Image: Amazon/harry potter

If you are fortunate enough to have a Harry Potter-obsessed pal, we know the perfect gift. These Wizard Training Wands are programmed with 11 different spells, recognise movement and provide feedback. You'll be ready to duel in no time. And whether your friend is a Hermione or a secret Slytherin, there’s a wand for them. 

Price: From $22.41 on Amazon 

3. Luigi's Mansion 3 

Vacuum up ghosts with the all-new Poltergust g-00 in Luigi's Mansion 3.
Image: Nintendo

In Luigi’s Mansion 3, Luigi’s dream vacation turns into a ghostly – and gooey – nightmare. It’s charming, it’s nostalgic, we loved it, and it’s a perfect gift for the young and the young at heart. 

Price: $79.95 on Nintendo 

4. Exploding Kittens

Image: amazon/ Exploding kittens

Please don’t knock this one until you’ve tried it. Exploding Kittens is a card game unlike any other. It’s fast-paced, hilarious and incredibly enjoyable for anybody who likes kittens, explosions, tacos, or being able to see into the future. Trust me, it’s addictive. 

Price: $24.98 on Amazon  

5.  Nintendo Switch 

Image: Amazon/Nintendo

For someone special to you, a Nintendo Switch is a killer gift idea. With awesome games and incredible convenience, a Switch makes a perfect gift that’s guaranteed to make the recipient the coolest kid on the quiet carriage. 

Price: from $399 on Amazon 

6.  Novelty Statement Tee

With a vast number of original designs and ethically sourced, RedBubble is where you can find the perfect gift for that friend who likes something a bit more than your average t-shirt. Remember the last thing they raved to you about, chuck it into the search bar, and go wild.

Price: Multiple price points on RedBubble 

7.  Fujifilm instax Camera

Image: Amazon/instax

For someone who misses having physical copies of their photos, a Fujifilm instax Camera is a super thoughtful gift. The iconic little prints are also incredibly Instagram friendly, if you want to take the whole thing full circle. 

Price: From $84 on Amazon 

8.  Star Wars Remote Control Robot

Image: amazon/hasbro

For that person in your life who hasn’t shut up about how stoked they are for The Mandalorian on Disney+.  

Price: $64.37 on Amazon  

9. Chameleon

Image: Amazon/Big Potato Games

In Chameleon, everyone knows a secret word except for the chameleon. But who is it? That’s for everyone who’s playing to figure out. Cue the accusations and (friendly) arguments across the table.

Price: $23 on Amazon

10.  Wall Scrabble

Image: amazon/WS Games

Sitting down to play a game of Scrabble, while incredibly fun, can be quite time consuming. Hang this magnetic board on your wall and play with your housemates or partner, either straight-through or gradually throughout the week. Oh, and it’s always funny to spell out swear words on the board, no matter how old you are. 

Price:  $179.24 on Amazon

11.   Zoku Slush and Shake Maker 

Image: amazon/zoku

As we head into summer in Australia, what better way to pay tribute to your childhood than by giving a pal their very own slushie maker. (And this time round, why not add some booze?) 

Price: $39.95 on Amazon 

12.  Colourful, patterned socks

Nothing says “I’m not a regular adult, I’m a cool adult”  than donning a pair of crazy socks with some Appropriate Business Attire. We all have this person in our lives, and what better way to fuel their obsession than by gifting them with… more socks. 

Price: $25.99 for a four pack on Amazon
