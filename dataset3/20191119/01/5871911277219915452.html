DEVELOPING: Randy Orton And AEW President Tony Khan Trade Shots On Social Media

19 Nov 2019, 01:57 by Tom Clark

Randy Orton has never been one to shy away from confrontation or controversy. The WWE Superstar has the reputation of being very outspoken backstage and the same is true online.

Now The Viper is going to war on Twitter with AEW President Tony Khan. In a situation that began on November 18, Orton and Khan began trading shots on the social media platform and it doesn’t appear to be over yet.
