Ringgit opens lower against US dollar

19 Nov 2019, 01:42 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 19): The ringgit opened lower against the US dollar today as investors turned to US dollar as a safe haven following doubts on the progress of US-China trade talks, said a trader.

At 9.05 pm, the ringgit was trading at 4.1590/1630 against the greenback from Monday’s close of 4.1540/1570. - Bernama
