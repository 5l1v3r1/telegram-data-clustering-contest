Patrick Mahomes Threw Football 70-Yards With ease During Warmups (VIDEO)

19 Nov 2019, 01:37 by Darrelle Lincoln

Patrick Mahomes is just a few weeks removed from dislocating his kneecap and he continues to display superhuman things on the field.

Just before the Kansas City Chiefs took the field to face off against the Los Angeles Chargers — QB Patrick Mahomes was spotted during warmups casually tossing a football 70 yards like it was nothing.
