Twelve missing after fishing boat is engulfed in flames out at sea

Police and military have launched a frantic search operation for survivors in the sea. We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

19 Nov 2019, 01:12 by Matthew Dresch, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/matthew-dresch/

Twelve fishing crew members are missing after their boat caught fire off the coast of South Korea.

Police and military have launched a frantic search operation for survivors in the sea 47 miles west of South Korea's Chagwido Island.
