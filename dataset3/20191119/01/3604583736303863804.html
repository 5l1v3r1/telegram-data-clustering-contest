Ballarat businesses score big in Golden Plate Awards

19 Nov 2019, 01:15

Ballarat's Moon and Mountain restaurant has collected two major awards the the 2019 Golden Plate Awards.

The Asian fusion-style restaurant received the nod as the best cocktail bar, as well as the best value restaurant.

The awards featured finalists from throughout regional Victoria.

The recognition follows executive chef Liam Downes winning the Golden Plate Award for best chef in 2018.

Golden Point cafe Drive also received an award for the best licenced cafe for 2019.

Elsewhere in the region, Bistro Terroir Daylesford and Talbot Provedore shared the casual dining award.

Bellarine vineyard Jack Rabbit took home the main award for the night.
