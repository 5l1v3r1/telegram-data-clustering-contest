Injury Update For Kansas City Chiefs WR Tyreek Hill

19 Nov 2019, 01:39 by Matt Hladik

Earlier: Tyreek Hill has already missed time this season with a sternoclavicular injury, and the speedy wide receiver came up lame again on the Chiefs’ first possession tonight.

After the final play of Kansas City’s first series against the Los Angeles Chargers in Mexico, Hill came up limping on his way to the sideline. His right hamstring seems to be the problem.

As of now, it’s unclear if he suffered a strain of some kind or simply cramped up.
