Burna Boy Reacts As Grammys Nominates ‘African Giants’ Among Album Of The Year

21 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Burna Boy has reacted to his nomination for the 2020 Grammy Awards for Best World Music Album with his album, ‘African Giant’.

His album was nominated alongside Gece ‘Altin Gun’ Celia ‘Angelique Kidjo’

The Grammy Awards which is the 62nd edition will air on Sunday, January 26, 2020.

Since his nomination, Burna Boy has received accolades and congratulations from Nigerians and artistes all over the world.

Burna Boy, while reacting appreciated all his supporters.

He took to his Twitter page to appreciate his supporters.

He wrote: “African Giant Album is nominated for Best world music Album at the Grammys⁠ !

“Thank you @RecordingAcad and everyone supporting me and the album!”
