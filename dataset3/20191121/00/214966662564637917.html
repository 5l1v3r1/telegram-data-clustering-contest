GRIDLOCK: Major delays on M1 after truck crash

21 Nov 2019, 00:50 by Shanee Dobeson

Anyone heading to the Gold Coast Airport this morning is being urged to allow for extra travel time.

A truck crash has occurred southbound on the Pacific Motorway at Tweed Heads, approaching the Tugun Tunnel.

Live Traffic NSW has confirmed one of two southbound lanes have since been closed as a result.

Emergency crews are on scene, however it’s not yet known how many people, if any, have been injured.

Long delays are being experienced southbound, with motorists being urged to seek alternative routes, including the Gold Coast Highway.
