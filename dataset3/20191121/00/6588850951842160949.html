Late-Night Swim: Rihanna Risks Getting Another Instagram Ban

21 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Her Instagram account was disabled after she shared her topless Lui cover back in 2014.

And Rihanna risked a possible ban once again when she struggled to contain her modesty in a very tiny bikini during a late night swim on Wednesday, sharing the results on social media.
The 31-year-old captioned the images with the words: ''#trouble on FENTY.com,' perhaps foreseeing the storm her snaps could cause.
