Barca's internal debate around Ansu Fati

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Whilst Patrick Kluivert sees it best that Ansu Fati plays in the Barca B team if he is not going to have any minutes in the first team, the decision falls to Ernesto Valverde, who sees a more than valid player in him.

It was really difficult to maintain the level shown in his first few appearances with Barcelona. Injuries and a lack of game time in the last few weeks have taken their toll on him. He returned to the scene against Celta, but did not perform to the expected level. But Valverde will have patience with him.

This is not enough for Kluivert, who already stated that a young man like him must have minutes. "If all the first team players are well, I have the feeling that he has to play in the Barce B team to get game time," the director of the youth squad said.

The truth is that Valverde cannot rely on him when he so many other options in attack, (Luis Suarez, Leo Messi, Antoine Griezmann and Ousmane Dembele). For this, the coach knows that Ansu Fati adds value to the squad.

For this reason, from the club, as 'AS' states, they have also seen with good eyes that Ansu Fati did not go to the U-17 World Cup in Brazil. To have opportunities with Barca they believe that it enriches the footballer a lot more.

He was even ahead of Antoine Griezmann with all the available troops, although the striker seems to have lost steam. Not in the mind of Valverde, who will continue to count on him.

It only remains to see if he will be given more of a chance or not in the Barca first team, as Valverde needs to have confidence in him and note that he will come good again.
