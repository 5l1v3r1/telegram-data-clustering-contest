Bordeaux dismiss Sankhare

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Bordeaux confirmed the agreement reached with the midfielder Younousse Sankhare to terminate his contract. The footballer has therefore left the French club.

Younousse Sankhare has ended his ordeal at Bordeaux. The player has been separated from the team since September and he had still not made his competitive debut for the French side. 

As a result of being isolated from the rest of the team, Bordeaux have decided to end the contract linking the club and the Senegalese player.

The African midfielder is now free to negotiate a contract with any team who are interested in his services.
