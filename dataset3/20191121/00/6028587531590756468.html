Best Black Friday Deals On Xbox One, PlayStation 4 & Nintendo Switch Consoles 2019

21 Nov 2019 by Don Reisinger

If you’re a gamer, you’re probably well aware that gaming consoles aren’t necessarily cheap. On top of paying hundreds for the console itself, there are other costs too — like an extra controller or two to play with friends, and, of course, a couple of decent games.But every year, even as games remain expensive and consoles add more capabilities, Black Friday comes around. And every year, a variety of retailers offer outstanding deals on the Nintendo Switch, Sony PlayStation 4 and Microsoft Xbox One that take the sting out of buying a new game console.

When you’re buying a new console on sale, there are often some other benefits. For instance, sometimes, retailers will toss in a free game with your purchase, bringing the bundle price down and its value up. You might also get some free months of online gaming, a new controller and more.

Ultimately, if you choose the right deal at the right retailer, there’s a way to spend far less on a new game console and get much more.

Still, before you buy, make sure to keep an eye on the model you’re choosing. The Xbox One X or PlayStation 4 Pro, for instance, will have more power and better visuals than the Xbox One S or standard PlayStation 4. And before you pick your favorite bundle, be sure to check out game reviews to make sure the title that comes with your new console is really one you want to play.
