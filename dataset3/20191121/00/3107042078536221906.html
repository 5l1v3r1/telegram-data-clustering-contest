Storm damage closes Selwyn Aquatic Centre

21 Nov 2019 by Devon Bolger

The storm on Wednesday afternoon damaged the Selwyn Aquatic Centre ceiling, causing the facility to close.

The centre is currently being repaired after it was battered by hail and lightning.

ItÂ is likely to re-open on Friday, said an aquatic centre spokeswoman.

"We don't have an indication of when exactly we will be open again yet but it's looking like it will be (on Friday)."

She said the storm caused a power surge, which shut down the centre's heating and air handling systems.

When the systems restarted it caused a vibration through the air ducting, which resulted in some of the supporting bolts being pulled from the ceiling in the main pool area.

"There are maintenance workers here at the moment replacing the bolts," the spokeswoman said on Thursday.
