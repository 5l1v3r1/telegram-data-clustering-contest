Neymar responds to Tuchel with enigmatic message

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

A new mess could be brewing around Neymar. The Brazilian striker wrote an enigmatic message on his social media in what sounded like a response to the discomfort his manager had declared hours earlier about his trip to watch the Davis Cup.

Let's get the situation straight. The Paris Saint-Germain manager had made it clear on Thursday that he didn't like that Neymar travelled last Tuesday to Madrid to watch the Davis Cup, a few days before his scheduled return after more than a month out due to injury.

"As a coach, do I like that trip? No. Is it time to lose your head? No," the German manager said at the pre-match press conference before the match against on Lille on Friday.

"What can I do? I am not his dad or the police. I am his manager. And on Wednesday he trained well", he said.
