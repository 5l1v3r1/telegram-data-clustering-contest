La Liga top scorers 19-20

21 Nov 2019 by Aidan Shearer

Benzema is currently top of the table after his excellent start to the season. However, he is closely followed by Leo Messi who is 1 goal behind.

As we approach matchday 14, Benzema finds himself top with 9 goals. The Frenchman has had a fine start to the season and got 2 goals in his side's 4-0 triumph away to Eibar before the international break.

He is closely followed by Messi, Morón and Moreno who all have 8 goals. Messi has been on fire since he came back from injury. He even got a hat-trick against Celta Vigo and will be hoping to add to his tally in Saturday's match against Leganés.

Moreno hasn't scored for Villarreal since 31st October, but he has still helped his side reach 11th in the table, just 2 points behind 6th. Morón hasn't scored since 20th October as his side struggle near the foot of the table, but he will be hoping to get his ninth when Betis entertain Valencia at the weekend.
