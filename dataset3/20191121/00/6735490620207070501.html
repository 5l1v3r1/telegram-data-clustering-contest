Meghan Trainor Shares Hilarious Video After Getting Wisdom Teeth Removed

21 Nov 2019, 00:11 by Brent Furdyk

Meghan Trainor has just had some major dental work done, and she’s got the hilarious video to prove it.

On Tuesday, the “All About That Bass” singer took to Instagram to share video of herself after having all four of her wisdom teeth removed — which she admitted wasn’t originally the plan.

“I was initially going to just take one wisdom tooth out. The dentist said they all had to go. Wasn’t emotionally or mentally prepared but sure got some great content 😩😭😂 ,” she wrote, adding, “the second vid is me talking to my amazing manager @tommybruce who literally has my whole heart haha thank u to the amazing dentist and my family for taking such good care of me💖”

In the second video, Trainor is seen in a car as husband Daryl Sabara drives her home, singing her hit “Working On It” while mustering up the best post-surgery smile she can.

In a recent appearance on “A Little Later with Lilly Singh”, Trainor revealed the adorable nickname she came up with for Sabara.

“We call him ‘Serial Killer’ because he looks crazy in a lot of pictures,” she explained. “But, also, he does, like, suspicious things that we’re like, ‘Are you a serial killer and we just haven’t figured it out yet?’ Like, he’ll go to the movies by himself, but not, like, just one movie, like four back-to-back. Like, solo dolo.”
