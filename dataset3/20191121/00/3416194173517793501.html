Hailey Bieber Celebrates With Calvin Klein at the 'Night of Music' Event

21 Nov 2019, 00:08

Hailey Bieber looks amazing at Calvin Klein’s Night of Music, Discovery and Celebration event held at Musikbrauerei on Wednesday night (November 20) in Berlin, Germany.

The 22-year-old model met up with Jasna Fritzi Bauer, Elias Becker, Emilia Schuele and Noah Becker, and many others for the event.

The multi-room experience, curated around different moods, and encouraged guests to explore an installation of Fall/Winter 2019 Calvin Klein Jeans and Calvin Klein Underwear.

DJs DeadHYPE and RSS Disco provided the music, and Honey Dijon and Jayda G also performed during the evening.

The collection on display, which Hailey wore, is now available from calvinklein.com and in many of the retail stores across Europe.
