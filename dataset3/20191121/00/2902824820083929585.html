Snowbombing announces Loyle Carner, Richy Ahmed and Roni Size for 2020

It's the third wave of acts to be released for the Mayrhofen festival

21 Nov 2019 by Max Penny-Barrow

The third wave of acts has dropped for Snowbombing 2020.

In October the Snowbombing gang added Alan Fitzpatrick and Claptone to the already extensive line-up in Mayrhofen.

Now they’re back with a varied programme of 3rd wave acts to tempt you over to the slopes in April.

Loyle Carner heads the bill, bringing his socially conscious UK hip hop to Alpine Austria. Richy Ahmed’s topping the fresh wave of DJ sets, alongside Doorly, house and techno sensation Dusky and drum 'n' bass don Roni Size.
