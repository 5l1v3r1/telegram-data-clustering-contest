Only one win in Sevilla's last 10 visits to Valladolid

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Sevilla, who will play away to Valladolid in matchday 14 of La Liga on Sunday, have only won once in their last 10 visits there, drawing 5 and losing 4.

This win was in the last match played between the sides in Valladolid. It was last season when the team then managed by Joaquín Caparrís, won 2-0 thanks to goals from Roque Mesa and Munir.

Sevilla's previous 2 victories in Valladolid were last century: a 1-0 win in 1996-97 through a Onésimo goal and a 4-0 win in 94-95 thanks to a Suker double and goals from Moya and Quique Estebaranz.

Valladolid's stadium hasn't been a particularly nice place for Sevilla, since they have lost more than half of the 49 official matches played between the sides (25), winning 12 and drawing the rest.
