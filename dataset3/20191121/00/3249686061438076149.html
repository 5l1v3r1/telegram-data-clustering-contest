IU achieves her second perfect all-kill of 2019 with 'Blueming'

21 Nov 2019, 00:07 by Yanderedan

IU has officially achieved her second perfect all-kill of this year!

Back on November 3, IU earned a perfect all-kill with her pre-release single "Love, Poem". And now on November 21 as of approximately 8:30 AM KST, she has earned another with her comeback title track "Blueming"! She has become the first artist of 2019 to achieve 2 perfect all-kills.

Congratulations, IU!
