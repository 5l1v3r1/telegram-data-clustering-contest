De Ligt responds to Kluivert: "I don't regret going to Juve"

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Matthijs de Ligt, the Juventus defender, spoke to the media after Hollands 5-0 win over Estonia. The player took advantage to disagree with Patrick Kluivert, who assured that De Ligt regretted moving to the Italian side in the summer.

"I'm happy. I don't regret having signed for Juventus," he was clear. And he said he had not spoken to Kluivert about it. "I didn't speak to him about me, I understand it's a supposition," he pointed out. 

De Ligt understands that the words which Kluivert said have been magnified because he is one of the main men in Dutch football. "I guess the fact it comes from someone like Kluivert makes it more credible, but I didn't say anything to him about me," he repeated. 

The footballer always knew that it was not going to be easy for him. "I knew that, with what was paid for me, I would be looked at very closely and it all deteriorated after that match against Napoli," the former Ajax man said. 

De Ligt is still in a period of adaptation and is hoping to continue improving day after day: "I am making an additional effort. I have the fondness of my teammates and fans. I'm happy with how everything's going.
