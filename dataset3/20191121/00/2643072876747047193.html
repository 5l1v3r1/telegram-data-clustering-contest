Breaking: President Buhari Meets State Assembly Speakers In Abuja

21 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Buhari Hosts State Assembly Speakers

President Muhammadu Buhari on Thursday, held a closed-door meeting with Speakers of the state houses of assembly.

The Speakers were led on a courtesy call to the President by the chairman of the Conference of Speakers and Speaker of Lagos State house assembly, Mudashiru Obassa.

Journalists were not allowed to cover the event taking place inside the chamber of the Aso Rock Villa.
