Nigerians React To Expulsion Of Babcock Student Whose Sex Video Leaked

21 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Naija News reported earlier that the sex tape of two students, said to be students of Babcock have leaked and is being featured on a popular porn site.

The video went viral on Social media which got many reacting.

Babcock University, hours later in a statement announced that it had expelled the students who were involved in the video.

Nigerians have taken to Twitter to react to the development.
