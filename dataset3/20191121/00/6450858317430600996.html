Twitter is testing scheduled tweets on desktop

Until now the only way to schedule tweets was via TweetDeck or similar third-party apps

21 Nov 2019, 00:58 by Henry Burrell

Twitter is rolling out a new feature to select accounts worldwide that allows for scheduled tweets. Until now, the official Twitter apps did not have this functionality.

One of the most famous Twitter clients that allows for schedule tweets is TweetDeck, the independent company that Twitter acquired in 2011. Other third-party Twitter clients like Buffer also have the functionality.
