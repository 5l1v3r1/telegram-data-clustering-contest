Naicegulevu charged with a count each of Falsification of Documents and Obtaining a Financial Advantage granted bail

21 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

The former Team Leader for the Land Transport Authority’s Nausori branch Kinisalote Naicegulevu who is charged with one count each of Falsification of Documents and Obtaining a Financial Advantage has been granted bail by the Nausori Magistrates Court.

She was charged by FICAC.

She is alleged to have created fake copies of receipts in order to obtain $38.460.67 from January 2017 to December 2018 from her former employers.

The matter has been adjourned to the 9th of next month.
