Magnitude 5.9 quake strikes Laos: EMSC

21 Nov 2019, 00:09

(Reuters) - A magnitude 5.9 quake struck Laos on Thursday, the European-Mediterranean Seismological Centre (EMSC) said.

The shallow quake was initially reported as a magnitude 6.0.

Reporting by Sandra Maler in Washington; Editing by Peter Cooney
