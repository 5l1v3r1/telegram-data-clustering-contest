Ravi Teja's 'Krack' goes on floors - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

21 Nov 2019

Ravi Teja's movie with Gopichand Malineni, which is titled 'Krack', was launched recently.  The cop thriller today went on the floors at Ramoji Film City. The extant schedule sees the shooting of scenes involving the hero and Shruti Haasan.  

"The film is based on true incidents that happened in the Telugu states. It’s an intense story, whose elements are universal. The film is scheduled for release in April or May 2020," the makers say.  

Music director SS Thaman is also on board.  Produced by Tagore Madhu, its dialogues are by Sai Madhav Burra.  Tamil actress Varalaxmi Sarathkumar of 'Sarkar' fame and director-actor Samuthirakani of 'Kaala' fame are part of the cast.  Cinematography is by GK Vishnu.  Fights are by the Ram-Lakshman duo.
