Just In: Apostle Suleman To Launch Mobile Network In UK

21 Nov 2019 by Philips Sunday

The General Overseer of Omega Fire Ministries (OFM) Apostle Johnson Suleman, has revealed that he would be launching a mobile network in the United Kingdom (UK).

Naija News reports that the Apostle disclosed this on the micro-blogging site, Twitter on Thursday afternoon, November 21

“Pray along with us as we launch our our mobile network in United kingdom…coming soon,” Apostle Suleman tweeted.
