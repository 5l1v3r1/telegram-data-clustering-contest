Southampton, Nice, Leverkusen track Aribo

21 Nov 2019, 00:35 by Johnny Edward

Johnny Edward

Glasgow Rangers face risk of losing their most in-form midfielder Joe Aribo when the January transfer reopens, Sports Extra reports.

According to a report on HITC, three European clubs Southampton, Nice and Bayer Leverkusen are believed to be interested in the 23-year-old, who has been outstanding for both club and country in the last four months.

The Nigeria international has scored five times and provided three assists in 23 appearances this term for Rangers.

“Rangers fans love him (Aribo) but his form and the goals have dried up a little in recent weeks (due to injuries and national team commitments),” a report on HITC revealed.

“There was a tone of interest from English clubs and if he picks up over the coming months, Rangers would have a challenge keeping hold of him,” the report further added.

The Camberwell-born midfielder joined Rangers from Charlton Athletic last summer and is committed to Ibrox till 2023. He has been capped four times by Nigeria and has scored twice.
