EOCO grants bail to defunct Ideal Finance CEO, Kotei Dzani

21 Nov 2019

The Economic and Organized Crime Office (EOCO) on Wednesday granted bail to the Chief Executive Officer of defunct Ideal Finance Company, Nii Kotei Dzani.

According to Citi News checks, he was invited last week for questioning over some depositors’ funds which have been unaccounted for.

The suspect, a member of the Council of State appeared yesterday for a further probe by EOCO and the Receiver.

Citi News also gathers that, officials of EOCO will later today, Thursday, authenticate some documents which were part of the suspect’s bail condition.

The businessman had reported to EOCO over the ongoing investigations into the collapsed financial institutions.

On Wednesday, unofficial reports emerged was detained at the Police Station for some hours before being released on bail.

He however denied the reportage.
