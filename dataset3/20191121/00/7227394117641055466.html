OnePlus Black Friday sales include €130 discount on 7 Pro, free cases

21 Nov 2019 by Michail

OnePlus is kicking off their Black Friday sales with a broad range of discounts on accessories and several offers on the OnePlus 7 Pro and 7T series.

In Europe, the 7 Pro is getting a sweet €130/£100 discount and now starts at €599/£549. OnePlus is also throwing in a free Nylon Bumper case on the house. You’ll have to add the ‘2019BF’ discount code at the checkout. The OnePlus 7T and 7T Pro on the other hand get a free protective case.

Those in the US can scoop up the 8/256GB OnePlus 7 Pro for $549 instead of the regular $699. There’s also a $150 discount on the 8/256GB OnePlus 6T which comes in at $449 and also gets a free Nylon Bumper case. Additionally you can get up to 50% off on other OnePlus accessories. All promotions are live on OnePlus.com and are available from November 21 through December 9.
