Referendum Is A 'Very Useless Exercise' - Sammy Gyamfi Insists

21 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The National Communications Officer of the NDC, Sammy Gyamfi has described as 'useless' the upcoming referendum which will be held on December 17, 2019.

Speaking on Peace FM morning show, 'Kokrokoo' he said: "it (referendum) will not add anything to this country...it is a very useless exercise if you ask. Of what use will this be to the woman selling at Makola market"
