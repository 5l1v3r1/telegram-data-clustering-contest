Qereqeretabua refuses to make any further comments in relation to September FB post apology in parliament

21 Nov 2019 by naveel Krishant and Navitalai Naivalurua

National Federation Party MP, Lenora Qereqeretabua has refused to make any comments in relation to her apology in parliament after her facebook post in September where she had used the word curse against the government.
