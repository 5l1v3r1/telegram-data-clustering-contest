Liam Payne shows off new girlfriend Maya Henry after confusion over her age

21 Nov 2019, 00:12

Liam Payne was a proud boyfriend as he showed girlfriend Maya Henry off to the world while arriving at And Juliet’s press night.

The former One Direction singer smized as star after star arrived for the musical, which focuses on what would have happened if Juliet – from Shakespeare’s Romeo and Juliet – never killed herself.

We’re interested already.

The 26-year-old wrapped his hand around his girlfriend’s waist and made sure we all knew that they’re a ridiculously good-looking couple.

Liam went for the ‘too cool to care’ leather jacket and white shirt ensemble while Maya, 19, brought back the ’40s in a black sequin dress with even more sequins dangling off the bottom.

Their public romance comes after a savvy internet sleuth figured out there is a huge possibility his beau is a year younger than reported.

One such detective tweeted last week: ‘Every article about Liam’s girlfriend says she’s 19 when she’s actually 18.

‘This is intentional because when fabricating this ridiculous PR campaign for her they said that they’ve been together for a year, which would mean that Liam was dating a 17 y/o. She’s a senior in HS.’

However, Strip That Down singer Liam – who has two-year-old son Bear with ex Cheryl Cole – hit back at the claims, tweeting: ’19, don’t believe everything you read on the internet.’

Uh, nice save there, Liam.

In a clip from her family’s reality show Hanging With Los Henrys, from 2018, Maya said she was 17 and that she’d be 18 in December – meaning that Maya would now be 18, and turning 19 in a few weeks time.

Guess we’ll never know.

Meanwhile, Jessie J, Simon Cowell and his girlfriend Lauren Silverman, and Nicole Scherzinger were also in attendance at Shaftesbury Theatre in London last on Wednesday night.

Nicole, 41, went for full glam and was dressed to kill as she arrived in a black sequin bodycon dress.

Both Simon and Jessie J opted for a smart blazer but Jessie’s was a little bit more on the silky chic side.
