Fake News Buster: Dwayne ‘the rock’ Johnson is alive

21 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
A website using the url bbc.247updatednews, shared a post about actor Dawyne Johnson dying after a failed stunt.


SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
The website is a hoax one and has a design layout similar to BBC News. It shared a video with the headline ‘Dawyne ‘The Rock’ Johnson Dies At 47 After A Terrible Stunt Attempt Failure’.
