Robertson and Salah, out injured for Crystal Palace game!

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Bad news for Liverpool. Both Andy Robertson and Mohamed Salah are out injured for the return of the Premier League after the international break. Liverpool are to visit Selhurst Park this Saturday to play against Crystal Palace.

The 'FIFA virus' has now attacked the Premier League leaders. Andy Robertson and Mohamed Salah have returned from their respective international clubs, both with blows to the ankle.
