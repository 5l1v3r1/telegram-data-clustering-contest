Earthquake of magnitude 6.1 strikes Thailand, says USGS

21 Nov 2019, 00:28

SINGAPORE, Nov 21 — An earthquake of magnitude 6.1 struck near the district of Muang Nan in northern Thailand, the United States Geological Survey said yesterday.

There were no immediate reports of casualties or damages from the quake, which the agency said struck at a depth of 10 km about 92 km northeast of Muang Nan. — Reuters
