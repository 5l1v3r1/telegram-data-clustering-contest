Nunes on Impeachment: ‘No Conspiracy Is Too Outlandish For The Democrats’

'Time and time again they floated the possibility of some far-fetched malfeasance by Trump,' he said.

21 Nov 2019, 00:14 by Chris White

California Rep. Devin Nunes and his GOP colleagues railed Wednesday against what they believe is the Democrats’ decision to use conspiracy theories to go after President Donald Trump.

“No conspiracy is too outlandish for the Democrats,” Nunes said during the impeachment inquiry before listing what he considered conspiracy theories tying Trump to Russia. “Time and time again they floated the possibility of some far-fetched malfeasance by Trump.”
