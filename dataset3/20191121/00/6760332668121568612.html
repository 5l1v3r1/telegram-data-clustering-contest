After Verithanam Thalapathy Vijay to create a Sambavam? - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

21 Nov 2019

Thalapathy Vijay's latest release 'Bigil' is said to have become the highest-grossing Tamil film in 2019 in most territories barring a few.  One of the highlights of the film is the "Verithanam" song sung composed by A.R. Rahman and sung by the mass hero after a long time.  Now unconfirmed sources reveal that Vijay is also singing a mass song in the underproduction 'Thalapathy 64' for which Anirudh is scoring the music.

The sources say that the song that Vijay will be singing for 'Thalapathy 64' will start with the word "Sambavam".  A few reports also state that this could be the title of the film too.  Anyway the project is progressing fast and whether the song features in it or if that is the title will be known much sooner.

'Thalapathy 64' directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj has the powerful duo Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi locking horns as the hero and villain while Shantanu Bhagyaraj, Antony Varghese Pepe, Malavika Mohanan, Andrea Jeremiah, Gouri Kishan and Brigida play other important roles.
