Michigan Signs MoU With NGO Startup Nation Central

As part of the agreement, the state of Michigan and SNC will work together to provide Israel-based companies with opportunities to expand into Michigan

21 Nov 2019 by CTech

Michigan Governor Gretchen Whitmer has signed a memorandum of understanding with Tel Aviv-based non-profit organization Startup Nation Central (SNC) to collaborate on developing new technologies, the government of Michigan announced Tuesday. As part of the MoU, the state of Michigan and SNC will work together to provide Israel-based companies with opportunities to expand operations into Michigan.
