Transfer: Mourinho Set To Sign Victor Osimhen

21 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

Jeunes Footeux has revealed that Tottenham Hotspur new manager, Jose Mourinho, is set to sign Lille striker, Victor Osimhen, after bringing in two coaches from the French club to London.

Naija News had earlier reported that the Portuguese replaced Mauricio Pochettino, signing a four-year deal.

The former Manchester United coach has already added the assistant coach Joao Sacramento and goalkeeping coach Nuno Santos from Lille to his coach staff.

With the North London side looking for more attacking reinforcement, they are set to sign Osimhen who has scored seven goals for the Lille this season.

The 20-year-old Nigerian international has a contract with Lille until the end of the 2023/2024 season.
