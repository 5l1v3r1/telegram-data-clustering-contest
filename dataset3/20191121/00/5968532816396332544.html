New Galaxy Note 10 Red, Pink colors arrive just in time for the holidays

21 Nov 2019, 00:30

If the current holiday season and Black Friday sales aren’t festive enough for you, Samsung’s got your back. Just in time for the holidays, the company is dropping two new Galaxy Note 10 colors in the US: Aura Red and Aura Pink. 

These new colors are only available in limited quantities of the 256GB Note 10 variants on Samsung’s website. Unfortunately, the Galaxy Note 10 Plus and Note 10 Plus 5G will not be available in these limited edition colors.

Customers who enrolled in early access to Samsung’s upcoming pre-Black Friday deals can purchase these new Galaxy Note 10 color variants right now. If devices don’t sell out, everyone else is free to order them starting tomorrow, November 21 at 12:01 AM EST.

Samsung also announced a new starting price for the Galaxy Note 10 during this sale — $310 with an eligible trade-in. To top it off, customers will also get a free pair of Galaxy Buds which typically retail for $130.

We expect the devices will sell out pretty quickly. So don’t forget to check back tomorrow to pick one up for yourself.
