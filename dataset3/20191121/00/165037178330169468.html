Juventus study ways to sign Rakitic

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

According to both 'Mundo Deportivo' and 'Il Corriere dello Sport', Juventus are studying ways to make room for Ivan Rakitic, who Ernesto Valverde doesn't seem to rely on.

This Rakitic is not the one from other seasons. The Croatian, who before, was a key man at Barca, has hardly been given any game time this season.

The midfielder does not fit in to the Barcelona squad and has even been linked with a possible exit in winter, although this option seems difficult in face of the obstacles that Barcelona could put in the way.
