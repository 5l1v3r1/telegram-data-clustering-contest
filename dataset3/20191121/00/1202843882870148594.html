Qatar and Russia sign MoU

21 Nov 2019, 00:12

H E Ali bin Ahmed Al Kuwari (standing, second left), Minister of Commerce and Industry; and Alexander Novak (standing, second right), Minister of Energy of Russian Federation, witnessing the signing ceremony of a memorandum of understanding between the Qatar Investment Promotion Agency and the Russian Investment Agency for cooperation in the field of investment promotion on the sidelines of the Fourth Session of the Russian-Qatari Joint Committee for Economic, Commercial and Technical Cooperation, in Moscow. The MoU was signed by H E Sheikh Ali bin Al Waleed Al Thani, Chief Executive Officer of Qatar Investment Promotion Agency, and Yuri Spiridenov, Chairman of the Russian Investment Agency.
