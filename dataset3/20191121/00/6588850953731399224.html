Shatta Wale Reveals Plans If Beyoncé Wins Grammys

21 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Award-winning Ghanaian Dancehall artiste Shatta Wale has expressed excitement at the nomination of Beyoncé’s ‘The Lion King: The Gift’ album for the 2020 Grammy Awards.

Shatta Wale featured on the album in his collaboration with the American songstress on the 12th track ‘Already’.

The album gained nomination in Best Pop Vocal Album. The nominees list for the 62nd was released on Wednesday on CBS.
