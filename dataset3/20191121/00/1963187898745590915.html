New cool menu at Hilton Cairo Zamalek

21 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

CAIRO - 21 November 2019: Hold on and let your taste buds take the lead, the Hilton Cairo Zamalek has recently launched a new menu featuring delicious homemade burgers with natural healthy homemade coloured bread buns, green spinach, red beets and black dark German flour, Menu also has delicious pizzas and more.

Come and try our super cool menu over the magnificent view of the Nile, you will not believe the special dishes we came up with.
