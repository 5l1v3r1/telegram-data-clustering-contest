Samsung offers exciting Black Friday deals in Germany

21 Nov 2019 by Yordan

The week of being thankful for what we’ve got and the following Black Friday when we buy a lot of stuff, plenty of which we don’t really need, is upon us. The latest deal comes from Samsung Germany, which will give you a free Galaxy Tab S5e or Galaxy A80 as a gift if you purchase any flagship smartphone.

The promos have already begun and some of them are available until December 2, also known as Cyber Monday.

The official name of the promo is Samsung Black Weeks includes 13 days of discounts for phones, TVs, headsets and smart watches. While you can get a 55” QLED TV or a washing machine, we are clearly more interested in the smartphone deals.

With purchasing a Galaxy S10, Galaxy S10+, Galaxy S10 5G, Galaxy S10e, Galaxy Note10, Galaxy Note10+, or Galaxy Note10 5G you can get a free Galaxy A80, Galaxy Tab S5e (LTE or Wi-Fi) or Galaxy Tab A 10.1 (2019). The bundle also can include AKG Y500 headphones, 27” curved gaming monitor or a soundbar. However, the bundle deal is available only until Monday, November 25.

There is also a pretty straightforward Samsung Galaxy A80 deal - the phone is available in all three colors (Angel Gold, Ghost White, Phantom Black) for the price of €333, which is almost half of its original €649 price across Continental Europe.
