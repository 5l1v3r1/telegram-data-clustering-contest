Early Betting Lines Released For College Football Playoff Matchups

21 Nov 2019, 00:12 by Andrew Holleran

There are still two weeks remaining in the college football regular season, along with a conference championship game weekend, but it’s time to start looking ahead to the College Football Playoff.

Clemson, LSU and Ohio State are heavy favorites to reach the playoff right now. Those three teams just need to win out and they’ll be in.

The No. 4 spot will come down to Alabama, Georgia, Oklahoma, Oregon and Utah, in all likelihood.

Who’s going to be favored in these College Football Playoff matchups? The sportsbooks have released early betting lines. 

Ohio State vs. Clemson would be an EVEN spread

Ohio State would be a 3.5-point favorite vs. LSU

Ohio State would be a 7-point favorite vs. Georgia

Clemson would be a 3.5 point favorite vs. LSU
