Rupert Grint and Chris Hemsworth are all smiles as they arrive in Tokyo

21 Nov 2019, 21:10

Avengers star Chris Hemsworth and Harry Potter star Rupert Grint were spotted jetting in to the same airport.

The duo arrived separately at Narita International Airport in Tokyo, with Chris attending Comic-Con and Rupert being there for a possible film

Rupert, 31, went for an undercover look as he walked through the terminal in an all-black outfit, long black coat, and black sunglasses.

He made the look a little more easy-going by adding a dark brown baseball cap.

Chris, who is Thor in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, opted for a casual ensemble as he walked by throwing peace signs for the cameras.

The 36-year-old donned a loose white t-shirt and black sweat pants to make sure he is comfortable enough for travel.

Chris was followed by his large entourage which includes his personal trainer Luke Zocchi.

Before arriving in Japan, Chris had been pictured multiple times just enjoying the beach in his homeland Australia.

The star was spotted riding some waves and looking very cool about it all.

In an Instagram post, he revealed he was at Urban Surf in Melbourne.

Alongside the snaps, he wrote: ‘Straight off the plane and into the pool for more waves than I’ve had all year!’

However, it looks like both Chris’ and Rupert’s holidays are over as they get back to their hectic schedules of travelling around the world.
