Third Series of NECA’s “Toony Terrors” Toy Line Includes Ash, Valak and Nosferatu!

21 Nov 2019, 21:38 by John Squires

The third wave of NECA‘s Scooby-Doo-inspired “Toony Terrors” toys has been unveiled, and it brings three more icons into the line – two classics and a newcomer on the horror scene!

Standing approximately 6 inches tall, the action figure assortment includes:

  • Ash (Evil Dead 2) with shotgun accessory
  • The Nun (The Conjuring Universe) with painting accessory
  • Count Orlok (Nosferatu) with rat accessory.

Each figure comes in blister card packaging with a bonus cutout backdrop.

Look for Series 3 in March 2020.
