Video: John Terry makes baffling claim about why Liverpool & Man City stars didn’t make it at Chelsea

21 Nov 2019, 21:45 by Lucien Silverberg

Former Chelsea captain John Terry has made a slightly bizarre claim about why Liverpool and Manchester City stars Mohamed Salah and Kevin De Bruyne didn’t make it at Stamford Bridge.

Both were at Chelsea earlier in their careers but were sold before really being given much of an opportunity with the club, though they’ve since come back to haunt the Blues by becoming so successful for their rivals.
