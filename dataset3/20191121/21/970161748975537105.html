I'm A Celebrity viewers can't stop laughing as Andrew Whyment fangirls over show

21 Nov 2019, 21:56

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! viewers were left in hysterics after Andrew Whyment fangirled over the show.

The Coronation Street star couldn’t believe his luck as he got to watch his fellow campmate Cliff Parisi take part in a Bushtucker Trial.

And while he looked on from afar and cheered his newfound friend on, the 38-year-old had a pinch-me moment as he joked it was like being in front of the television.

‘I can’t help it, sorry Cliff,’ he said while trying to control his laughter: ‘It’s just making me laugh, I feel like I’m watching the TV at home.’

As Andrew was giggling away in the sidelines, the EastEnders actor had to put his hand into covered boxes in order to retrieve a number of stars for the original group.

Each box was full of either critters, creepy crawlies and reptiles with others loaded with fish guts.

Poking his hand in one, the 59-year-old squirmed: ‘What’s in here, whatever it is it’s dead.’

Ant and Dec called over: ‘Fish guts,’ which set Andrew off again.

Laughing along with the hosts, he mimicked their words as he splurted out: ‘Fish guts!’

Once the trial was over, Dec explained Andrew’s reaction to Cliff, and said: ‘He thought he was back on the sofa watching the telly!’

He then asked the pair: ‘How do you feel after your first experience in the jungle?’

And to no surprise, Andrew said with a huge grin on his face: ‘I loved it. Eight stars, I’m very happy, I think the camp will eat well, so I’m alright with that.’

Viewers at home were quick to take to Twitter where they were left in stitches as the newcomer.

‘I love Andy, he’s loving life,’ one wrote while another added: ‘Hahahaha Kirk got a front-row seat to his favourite show.’

Someone else said: ‘Andy having a fangirl moment through this trial… laughing his head off and then “I love this show!”‘
