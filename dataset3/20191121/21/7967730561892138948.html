Microsoft delays launch of its Surface Earbuds until 2020

21 Nov 2019, 21:51 by Mark Gurman

Microsoft has delayed the launch of its Surface Earbuds, missing the 2019 holiday shopping season.

The software giant was due to be the latest company to enter the increasingly crowded true wireless earbuds market, dominated by Apple 's popular AirPods, with a late 2019 release.

But the Surface Earbuds will now come out in the first half of 2020. The announcement was made by Panos Panay, the company's chief product officer, on Twitter.
