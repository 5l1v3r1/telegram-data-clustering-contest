SpaceX's First Starship Prototype Fails to Launch

It’s not a big deal for Elon Musk and team, though.

21 Nov 2019, 21:08 by Emily Engle

SpaceX recently held the launch of its first Starship prototype, but unfortunately for CEO Elon Musk and team, an accident occurred where the top section of the shuttle broke off and flew away, followed by a large cloud of smoke. To the casual onlooker, this failure appears catastrophic, but Musk and his team seem to be relatively unfazed.
