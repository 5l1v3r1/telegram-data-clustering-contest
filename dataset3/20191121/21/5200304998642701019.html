Listen: Pulkit Samrat on wedding plans with Kriti Kharbanda

21 Nov 2019, 21:23 by Lakh Baddhan

Pulkit Samrat has been on a promotional spree ahead of the release of ‘Pagalpanti’ this week.

The actor, who will be seen playing Chandu in the comedy, will be sharing screen space with real-life girlfriend, Kriti Kharbanda. The couple, who confirmed their relationship a few days ago, have been dating for over a year.

Talking about this to BizAsiaLive.com’s Raj Baddhan, Samrat said, “As far as how we met, we shot a movie together (Veerey Ki Wedding). We weren’t on talking terms at that time. It was during the promotions, we exchanged numbers. Then we started talking to each other. Then we were best friends for a year, a year and a half before we started dating. It’s important we be best friends first and then everything else comes after.”

Asked about marriage plans, Samrat added, “I don’t think anytime soon. It’s the time to work. I think for her, it’s the time to shine. That’s what both of us will be concentrating on.”

During the interview, Samrat spoke extensively about the film’s shoot in the UK and the actor’s favourite places in the UK. He also spoke about his future projects.
