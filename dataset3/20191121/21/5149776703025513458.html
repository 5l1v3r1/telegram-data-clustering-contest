Championship rumours: Nottingham Forest eye striker, Leeds star on Liverpool and Manchester United’s radar

The latest transfer rumours and gossip from across the Championship

21 Nov 2019, 21:51 by Barry Cooper, Connor O'Neill

The January transfer window may still feel like a few months away but it will soon be here as teams look to add to their squads again.

Trading will resume in the New Year with plenty of major transfers expected as Championship sides look to prepare for the business end of the season.

And below is the latest gossip from around the division.

Liverpool and United eye Leeds star

Leeds defender Ben White is attracting the interest of both Liverpool and Manchester United after impressing on loan at Elland Road.

The 19-year-old moved north from Brighton for the season in July and has become an integral part of Marcelo Bielsa's defence.

So much so that two of the Premier League big boys are weighing up a move reports the Athletic .

Shankland on Forest’s radar

Lawrence Shankland has been linked with a January move to Nottingham Forest.

The Scotland international is reportedly attracting the interest of several clubs ahead of the transfer window reopening in the new year.

The Sun reports that Sabri Lamouchi is looking to add another striker in January, but Forest will need to overcome interest from Celtic and Rangers if they are the land the forward.

No Burnley move for Phillips

Burnley aren't expected to make another bid for Leeds United midfielder Kalvin Phillips.

According to reports, Sean Dyche was keen to add Phillips to his squad last summer, along with a number of admirers.

But according to reports on Thursday morning, the Clarets are unlikely to make another move for the Whites star.

Huddersfield eye Non-League star

Huddersfield have been linked with a move for Torquay forward Jamie Reid.

The striker has scored 15-goals in the National League so far this season, and also netted 29 goals in the National League South last season.

And Sky Sports are reporting that the forward’s impressive form has alerted the attention of Hudds boss Danny Cowley.
