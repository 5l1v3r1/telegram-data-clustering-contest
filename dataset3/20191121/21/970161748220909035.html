Amy Schumer is pure squad goals as she hangs with Phoebe Waller Bridge and J-Law

21 Nov 2019, 21:05

Amy Schumer may have just won Instagram with an iconic photo of herself posing alongside Phoebe Waller Bridge, J-Law and David Byrne.

That’s right, the four legends could be seen huddled together for the picture, where they all beamed down the camera lens – and we grinned right back.

But what are they plotting?

Giving nothing away in the caption, the 38-year-old sent her 9.4million followers into a frenzy as she simply wrote: ‘I guess that this must be the place.’

And right on cue, fans flocked to the comments section where they pondered over what the gathering of A-listers might entail.

‘Omg please tell me y’all are filming??!?’ one wrote while another said: ‘Dream dinner party line up right there.’

Someone else appeared to be in agreement, as they added: ‘What! To be a fly on the wall…’

But while Amy couldn’t wait to dish up her evening on her socials, her newfound squad has remained quiet over the meeting.

Jennifer Lawrence and Amy go way back with the mother-of-one attending her fellow actress’s wedding early this year.

Sharing a picture from the big day, she wrote: ‘Chris and I had the best time #balmain.’

Jennifer wed Cooke Maroney on Saturday 20 October in a lavish ceremony in Newport, Rhode Island.

Other celebrity guests at the ceremony included Keeping Up With the Kardashians star Kris Jenner and 21 Bridges actress Sienna Miller.

Then, of course, there was also the 29-year-old’s ultimate celebrity BFF, Adele.

Jennifer, who won an Oscar for Best Actress in 2012 for her role in Silver Linings Playbook, reportedly wore a Dior gown for the wedding.

After saying their vows, the newly-weds hosted a reception at the swanky Belcourt of Newport.
