How to beat Gorgara in Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

Attack the wings.

21 Nov 2019, 21:25 by Asher Madan

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an Xbox One, PC, and PlayStation 4 game that features exploration and a lot of Dark Souls-like combat. There are a lot of challenging bosses in the game, but Gorgara on Dathomir is one of the worst. It's a massive, crazed, bat-like monster that packs quite a punch. You have to dodge again and again, as well as double jump to avoid its shockwave and echolocation attacks. This is quite possibly one of the hardest fights in the game.

Luckily, there's a great way to defeat Gorgara. Keep in mind that it's useless to attack its body, you have to keep on hitting the wings. Additionally, don't lock onto it because that limits your range of motion. Eventually, Gorgara does a stab attack where its wing gets lodged into the ground. If you keep on hitting it, then the second phase triggers where he stabs the ground with the other wing. If you do enough damage to both wings, it staggers, and you can Force Pull it to the ground and hit it in the head a few times.
