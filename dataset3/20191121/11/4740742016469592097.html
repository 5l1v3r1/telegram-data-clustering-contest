Bristol Rovers fans speculate over takeover bid as chairman departs club

He has left after three and a half years at the Gas

21 Nov 2019, 11:12 by David Byrom

Bristol Rovers fans have been reacting to the news that chairman Steve Hamer has left the club.

Hamer had been at the Gas since the al-Qadi family took charge of the club in February 2016, but has now departed the club following a brief statement on their website.
