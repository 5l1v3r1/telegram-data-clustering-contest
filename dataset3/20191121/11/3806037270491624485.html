A Better Look at the Off-White™ x Nike Dunk Low “University Gold”

1-of-3 colorways slated to release.

21 Nov 2019, 11:13 by Mallory Chin

Over the summer months, Virgil Abloh confirmed three Off-White™ x Nike Dunk Lows were on their way.

Thanks to Stadium Goods, we now have our best look yet at the forthcoming “University Gold” colorway dressing the aforementioned sneaker. The collaborative silhouette sports a Michigan-fitting “Gold/Navy” color scheme and is fashioned from various navy blue and gold panels. Off-White™’s signature additions include the zip ties dressed in full white hanging off of the red hiking-inspired lacing system, and orange accents featured on the lateral Swoosh insignias and tongue tab branding. Finally Off-White™’s boldface iconography is embellished on the medial sides.

Along with the “University Gold” colorway, a “Pine Green” and “University Red” color scheme are also slated to drop soon, retailing at $170 USD per pair. Head over to Stadium Goods to be notified when the Off-White™ x Nike Dunk Low “University Gold” drops.
