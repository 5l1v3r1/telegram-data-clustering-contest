Meizu 16T Daylight Orange Version Will Go On Sale Tomorrow

21 Nov 2019, 11:09 by Argam Artashyan

Rate this post

On November 21, Meizu Technology’s official Weibo channel announced that the Meizu 16T Daylight Orange version will be available for sale at 10:00 tomorrow.

The Meizu 16T offers a total of three color combinations: lake light green, whale blue, and daylight orange. The price of daylight orange is consistent with the other two colors: 6+128GB version is 1999 yuan ($284), the 8+128GB version costs 2299 yuan ($327), and the 8+256GB version is priced at 2499 yuan ($355).

Originally, the Meizu 16T came out on October 23. It uses a 6.5-inch full-screen, which adopts a third-generation OLED material and supports a hardware-level blue-light filtering technology. The latter can effectively reduce 58% of harmful blue light. At the same time, it has retinal-level color reproduction, the brightness of up to 600 nits, and clearly displays the content even under the direct sunlight.

It sports a Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 flagship platform. As for the native storage, it uses UFS 3.0 flash memory. The front camera has a 16MP resolution, and the rear camera consists of a 12MP main sensor + 8MP super wide-angle lens + 5MP sub-camera lens. The main camera lens is the Sony IMX362 CMOS, of which the unit pixel area is 1.4μm. The battery capacity is 4500mAh and it supports 18W fast charge.

According to the official introduction, the Meizu 16T has tank-level battery life. You can continuously play games for nearly 17 hours.

In addition, the Meizu 16T comes with dual stereo speakers. So they provide an ideal audio experience. Moreover, the linear vibration motor, 3.5mm headphone jack, are all retained on the Meizu 16T.
