Exclusive! Kartik Aaryan to have a working birthday tomorrow

21 Nov 2019, 11:17 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

National crush Kartik Aaryan will turn a year older tomorrow as he celebrates his 28 th birthday. The actor has been busy shooting for ‘Dostana 2’ in Patiala but returned to the city today. However it not much of celebrations for the actor as Kartik will be busy promoting his next release ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ tomorrow with co-stars Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar.

Kartik is going all out to promote ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’, which is a remake of a yesteryear hit with the same name. The actor took a break from the shooting of the second installment of 'Dostana' and attended the launch of 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Maare' song in Delhi. The track is a remixed version of Govinda and Raveena Tandon’s iconic song 'Ankhiyon Se Goli Maare' and received mixed reviews from fans.

‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ features Kartik in the role of Chintu Tyagi, who is married to Vedika played by Bhumi and falls in love with Tapasya Singh essayed by Anaya. Directed by Mudassar Aziz, the film will feature Rajesh Sharma and Aparshakti Khurana in supporting roles. ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ is slated for release on December 6.
