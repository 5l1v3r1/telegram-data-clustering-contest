INSIDE John Abraham's Garage: Pagalpanti Actor's Obnoxious Collection Of Motorcycles Is Worth Several Crores

21 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Amruta Patel

John Abraham taking his fans on a tour inside his garage, has got many tongues wagging. The Pagalpanti actor showcased his bike collection comprising of 6 high-end expensive bikes, that has left his fans drooling over it. But it comes with a heavy price tag!

Everyone knows Bollywood’s hottest hunk John Abraham’s love and passion for those mean machines. Fond of expensive bikes, the Pagalpanti actor gave a sneak peek of his most expensive buys and called them his babies in his recent social media post. In the video, we can see John taking us on a tour, dropping little deets about his bikes parked in his garage. The video features 6 bikes bought by the actor, and we are already drooling over his collection.

 

Looking at the actor’s collection, one can easily understand it is worth several crores. The actor captioned the video as, “My babies !! #superbikes” Along with the fans who went berserk with his mind-boggling collection, his upcoming movie Pagalpanti co-star Urvashi Rautela, too commented on his post. The actress wrote, “Omgggggggg” followed by emojis with heart eyes. While fans getting all excited was very much expected, some even went ahead to ask him if he is willing to sell. Whereas one of the social media users, commented on his post asking about bike’s mileage, he commented, "Mileage kitna deti hai?"
