WWE & The Kraken Black Spiced Rum Team Up for a Tasty Sweepstakes

21 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Steve Russell

WWE has formed a new tag team partnership with The Kraken Rum to offer fans an opportunity to attend next year's WrestleMania in Tampa, Florida.

Fans can enter the competition by writing in and sharing what finishing move would be needed for WWE’s NXT Superstars to take down the legendary Kraken sea monster

Launched ahead of Nov. 23's NXT TakeOver: WarGames, the sweepstakes runs until Dec. 13, and The Kraken Rum,  NXT Champion Adam Cole, NXT Women’s Champion Shayna Baszler and "The Bruiserweight" Pete Dunne will help determine the winning entrant. In honor of their new partnership, the "anti-hero spirit brand" has also created a range of new drinks inspired by WWE Superstars, including The Bitter End and The Kirifuda Clutch.

Described as an "incomprehensible mass," the Kraken is purported to be longer than any skyscraper known to man and has eight terrifying tentacles, each with the might to crush 80 elephants and featuring thousands of hooked suckers. And that’s before mentioning its drawbridge-like jaws and eyes that are likened to a black hole. With that in mind, the finishing move that fans have to imagine needs to be strong enough to put away such a behemoth -- clearly no easy task.

If you’re interested in entering the competition, you can share how these NXT Superstars can take down the monster on Instagram or Twitter, tagging @krakenrum with the hashtag #KrakenTakedown. The competition is open to adults aged 21 years or older. One winner will be chosen and will receive roundtrip airfare, hotel accommodation for two nights and two tickets to  WrestleMania 36.
