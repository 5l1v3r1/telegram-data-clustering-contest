Deal Confirmed, First 2019 Ke Yona Product Turns Pro…

21 Nov 2019, 11:48 by Soccer Laduma

A deal with a Premier Soccer League club has been confirmed as the first Nedbank Ke Yona Team product of 2019 turns professional.

It was reported by the Siya crew last month that AmaZulu had been sent Thulani Cele from the Ke Yona Team, after their match against the Nedbank Cup champions. 

As part of the talent identification programme’s initiative, players are given opportunities at various Absa Premiership clubs each year.

Cele was Ke Yona Team’s goalscorer in their 2-1 loss to champions TS Galaxy in the Nedbank Ke Yona Challenge match in September.  

It has since been confirmed by Nedbank Sport that Cele has signed a professional contract with Usuthu.
