Missing Qld mother, children found safe

21 Nov 2019, 11:56

A 27-year-old missing Queensland mother, her seven-year-old son and 10-month-old baby daughter have been found safe and well, police say.

The mother had left a home in Coombabah on Tuesday morning to drive to the Sunshine Coast, and was missing until Thursday night.

Police expressed their thanks to the public for their assistance.
