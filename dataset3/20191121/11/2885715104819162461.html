Bhushan Kumar: We have made Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi for the new generation

While T-Series' latest single "Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi" has taken the internet by storm, it has also received some flak.

21 Nov 2019, 11:51 by Komal RJ Panchal

T-Series head honcho Bhushan Kumar and wife, actor Divya Khosla Kumar celebrated the success of T-Series’ latest single “Yaad Piya Ki Aane Lagi” on Wednesday. The single is a recreation of Falguni Pathak’s iconic 1999 song.

While the song has taken the internet by storm, it has also received some flak.

Talking about the criticism, Bhushan Kumar said, “We launched this song three-four days ago and it has more than thirty million views (on YouTube). Today, many people criticize the trend of song recreation. Lalit Sen (music composer of the original song performed by Falguni Pathak) praised the re-composition. He is the best judge. If he is appreciating it, then it means that we have done a good job. So people who are criticizing the song should stop as we have made it for this generation. The original song released a long time ago, and people enjoyed it then as well. Now, we have revisited it, and many people including the original composer Lalit Sen and Falguni (Pathak) are happy about the recreation. Many people have criticized it, but there is a large audience that we cater to. So I am really happy with the kind of response the song has been receiving. I am very thankful to my audience.”
