World's smartest robot could turn evil - and Holly Willoughby is freaked out

21 Nov 2019, 11:53

Holly Willoughby seemed more than a little concerned on This Morning as she was informed that the world’s smartest robot could turn evil.

Holly and Phil were joined by Sophia, the self-styled world’s smartest robot, on the show and it certainly made for a very interesting interview.

Sophia was sat with one of her co-creators, scientist David Hanson, but it seemed that Holly only had one question on her mind.

Phillip Schofield had joked before the interview that the robot could end up ‘replacing’ the duo in a few years.

This seemed to have Holly rattled, and she seemed to stare at the robot almost in disbelief. Could she be looking at her This Morning replacement in a few years?

She put the question to Hanson if Sophia, theoretically, could turn against the humans and go rogue. The answer probably wasn’t what she was hoping to hear.

‘Could she…turn on us?’ Holly asked the scientist, grimacing and definitely trying not to make contact with her possible future robot overlord.

‘I don’t think so,’ Hanson replied before Holly shot the camera a very worrying look.

‘You don’t think so?’ she said. Safe to say by the look on her face, the Dancing on Ice presenter did not look convinced.

To prove her intellectual prowess even further, however, the AI machine presented the morning TV duo with their very own portraits – drawn by the robot herself.

A robot that can draw? That’s not creepy at all. She knows what you look like now Holly – run while you still can!

In other This Morning news and robots aside, Holly and Phil got another shock yesterday as radio DJ Chris Evans stripped down to his pants live on air.
