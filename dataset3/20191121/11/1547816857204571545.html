Shahid Kapoor’s Wife Mira Rajput Is Unwell And Posts A ‘Sick Days Call For Tea And Butter Toast’ Picture

21 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Oshine Koul

Shahid Kapoor’s wife Mira Rajput took to her Insta story and revealed she’s not been keeping well and reveals what she prefers having on sick days

Shahid Kapoor and Mira Rajput, who are married for over 4 years now, often make heads turn with their stunning appearances at various events and occasions. Right from hitting the gym together to rock the red carpets, Mira Rajput has built her own fan base. Every time the star wife posts a picture on social media it gets viral instantly. And when it’s a family portrait, then there are no words for it. Shahid and Mira and blessed with two kids- Misha and Zain.

But looks like, Mira Rajput has not been keeping well and to inform her fans about the same, Mira took to social media and revealed what is she eating as she’s unwell. Posting a picture of a cup of tea with a teapot beside it, Mira captioned it, “Sick days call for tea and butter toast.” Check out Mira’s Insta story below:

Mira Rajput's Insta Story
Shahid Kapoor, who enjoys an immense fan following, married Mira who is not from the industry. While speaking to a section of media about how Mira feels comfortable in her own skin. He said, “Mira doesn’t feel the need to change and adjust. She is who she is, and people like her for that. She has never tried to be someone else and that’s a great thing about her. I always knew that she is going to find her own space and will become her own person.” 

On the work front, basking in the success of his last offering Kabir Singh, Shahid Kapoor is now gearing up for his upcoming film Jersey. The actor has been paired opposite Mrunal Thakur in the Hindi remake of the film.
