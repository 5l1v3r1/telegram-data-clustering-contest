PDP To Hold Emergency Meeting Over Bayelsa, Kogi Guber Elections

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) will hold an emergency meeting today (Wednesday) to deliberate on its loss in Saturday’s governorship elections in Kogi and Bayelsa States.

The PDP National Publicity Secretary, Kola Ologbondiyan, confirmed the meeting in a statement in Abuja on Tuesday.

Ologbondiyan said: “We have not held a meeting. We are going to hold a meeting tomorrow (Wednesday).”

The party insisted that the alleged rigging of the Kogi and Bayelsa governorship elections by the All Progressives Congress (APC) could not stand. Read more here.

Police React To Death Of PDP Woman Leader In Kogi

The Kogi Police Command has said the killing of Salome Abuh, the Woman Leader of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in the state, by suspected thugs was a reprisal attack.

The spokesman of PDP Wada/Aro campaign council, Faruk Adejoh-Audu, had alleged that thugs who were chanting GYB 4+4 arrived Abuh’s house at about 2:00pm on Monday and were shooting sporadically.

According to Adejoh-Audu, the thugs surrounded the house and blocked every exit from outside. Read more here.

Kogi Election Violence: Melaye Presents 21 Video Evidence At INEC Office

Senator Dino Melaye was at the headquarters of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) on Wednesday in Abuja to demand the cancellation of the Kogi West Senatorial election.

It was learned that Melaye was received by INEC Secretary, Rose Orianran-Anthony, and National Commissioner and Chairman of its Information and Voter Education Committee, Festus Okoye.

Melaye said he came to present the pieces of evidence of violence and indiscriminate shootings by alleged agents of Kogi State government because he wants to retrieve his ‘stolen mandate’. Read more here.

APC Reacts To Death Of PDP Woman Leader In Kogi

The All Progressives Congress (APC) has condoled with the family of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) woman leader in Kogi State, Salome Abuh, who was killed during the state governorship election.

The spokesman of PDP Wada/Aro campaign council, Faruk Adejoh-Audu, had alleged that suspected APC thugs arrived at Abuh’s house at about 2:00pm on Monday and were shooting sporadically.

According to Adejoh-Audu, the thugs surrounded the house and blocked every exit from outside. Read more here.
