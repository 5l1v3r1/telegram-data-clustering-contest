Premier League injury and suspension list as Everton and Liverpool prepare for weekend matches

A full list of Premier League injuries and suspension as domestic football resumes following the international break

21 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Joe Rimmer

The international break is over and the Premier League is set to resume for what will be its busiest period of the season.

With the Christmas fixture pile-up right around the corner, the importance of having a fully-fit squad is incredibly important - but it’s something that not too many clubs will be able to enjoy.

The recent international break has come at a good time for some, giving those with knocks time to record, but others may have picked up issues while travelling across the globe to represent their nations.

Using the latest information from premierinjuries.com, we have listed all of the injuries, illnesses and suspensions ahead of this weekend’s fixtures and how many in each squad could be a doubt.
