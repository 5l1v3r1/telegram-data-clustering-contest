Atlantis The Palm’s WHITE Beach to open this weekend

The brains behind one of the city’s best nightclubs set up shop at Atlantis

21 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Tim Skinner

In September we brought you news that Dubai’s world-famous Atlantis the Palm will be opening a brand-new beach club, WHITE Beach.

The good news is that the beach club, managed by the same team behind the absolutely enormous WHITE Dubai night club at Meydan Racecourse, is opening this weekend (Saturday November 23).

So get ready for some serious chilling and partying at the weekend, with the club promising a big schedule of live entertainment, DJs and performers over the course of the winter season. It comes in the place of famed nightlife spot Nasimi Beach, and it’s promising a huge infinity pool, multiple beach areas, a restaurant with al fresco terrace, and those brilliant partially-submerged sunbeds – dreamy.

Hitting you with those proper holiday vibes, the entire club is kitted out in a pale/white/teal theme throughout. Whether its al fresco restaurant terrace smothered in palm trees and the exposed brick feel that is in keeping with the Atlantis the Palm resort, or the stunning infinity pool with views that stretch out to sea and over to Dubai Marina, the Ibiza-esque vibes are waiting.
