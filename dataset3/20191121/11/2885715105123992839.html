This Mumbai autorickshaw that has fans, a washbasin and TV is making heads turn

Satyawan Gite's autorickshaw is equipped with facilities like a washbasin, mobile phone charging points, plants and a television screen among other conveniences.

21 Nov 2019, 11:40 by Trends Desk

A Mumbai autorickshaw driver has been turning heads and making headlines, after he turned his three-wheeler into what he claims is the city’s first ‘home system auto-rickshaw’.

Satyawan Gite’s autorickshaw is equipped with facilities like a washbasin, mobile phone charging points, plants and a television screen among other conveniences.

A sticker on the back of the vehicle asks passengers to, “Take aeroplane ride experience”.
