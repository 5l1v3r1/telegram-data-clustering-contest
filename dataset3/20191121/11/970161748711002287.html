Lidl's Xbox One S for £129 is the best Black Friday 2019 deal yet

21 Nov 2019, 11:56

If you’re looking for a cheap Xbox One during Black Friday 2019 you’re not going to get a much better deal than at Lidl.

Black Friday is still more than a week away but you’re going to be lucky if there’s a better bargain then the Xbox One S All-Digital Edition for just £129.

For that you get the Xbox One S, which is the base model of the Xbox One that doesn’t have a disc drive, and three games: Minecraft, Sea Of Thieves, and Forza Horizon 3.

It’s basically the same deal as Microsoft officially revealed for £199.99 a few weeks ago, which was considered a pretty good price on its own.

The downside is that this doesn’t look like being an online offer and so you’ll actually have to go in-store on Friday, 29 November to pick one up, which will likely be… busy.

A deal like that is likely to run out fast, so if you’ve got a store nearby that stocks consoles you might want to bear that in mind.

Because there’s no disc drive that means all the games have to be digital downloads, although with Game Pass Ultimate available now for just £1 that’s not much of a hardship.

The standard Xbox offer gives you three months of access to Game Pass Ultimate for just £1, which includes over 100 games now and 50 more over the next few months – including big name titles such as The Witcher 3 and a whole raft of Final Fantasy games.
