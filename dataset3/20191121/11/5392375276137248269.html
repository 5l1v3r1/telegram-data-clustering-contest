3 (60%) 4 votes

The Realme 5 which was released back in August this year is finally making its way to Europe. Realme is the fastest-growing company in 2019, and one of the major players in India. However, with so many good offerings, there’s no reason to keep smartphones isolated to select markets.

The Realme 5 Pro is coming in Crystal Purple and Crystal Blue colors options. Worth noting that Realme is offering it only in the 4GB/128GB configuration for €169. Interestingly enough, Realme will be available for €10 less from November 27 to December 2.
