Arpita Khan and Aayush Sharma to welcome their second baby on Salman Khan’s birthday?

21 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Filmfare

Arpita Khan and Aayush Sharma recently celebrated five years of their marriage together. The couple hosted a grand bash which saw all their friends and family members in attendance. And now, it seems like Aayush and Arpita have more one reason to be excited as they’re also expecting their second child soon. During a recent event, Aayush Sharma also confirmed the same during an interaction with the media. He said, “I think the new arrival is always exciting. Arpita and I are expecting our second baby. Ours has been an amazing journey, so it starts all over again. We just can't wait for the baby to arrive.”

If a report in a leading daily is to be believed than Arpita is all set to give a birth to her second child in the next month. The couple have reportedly scheduled a C- section delivery on December 27, which also happens to be Salman Khan's birthday.

For the unversed, Arpita and Aayush got hitched at the Taj Falaknuma Palace in Hyderabad on November 18, 2014. Well, we wish the couple all the very best and hope they’re blessed with a healthy child.
