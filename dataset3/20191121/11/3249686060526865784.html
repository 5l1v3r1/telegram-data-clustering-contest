Kim Jae Hwan Preparing For Comeback

21 Nov 2019, 11:11 by BijayaMagar

On November 21, Kim Jae Hwan’s agency Swings Entertainment revealed that he is preparing for comeback. The agency said, "Kim Jae Hwan is currently preparing a new album set to be released in December."

This will be his first comeback in seven months since the release of his first solo debut album “Another.”
