Singer Davido celebrates 27th birthday with Chioma and close friends at a dinner party (videos)

21 Nov 2019, 11:27

- Davido has celebrated his 27th birthday with his close friends and wife-to-be

- The singer was spotted at a dinner party with Chioma and some of his friends

- It was gathered that the party was reportedly organised by Chioma who showered her boo with different cakes

Popular singer David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, celebrated his 27th birthday in style. The singer and father of three beautiful children turned 27 this Thursday, November 21.

The singer celebrated the special day with his wife-to-be, Chioma Avril Rowland, and some of his close friends at a dinner party.

According to reports, the birthday dinner was organised by Chioma. The mother of one took to her Instagram page to share photos and videos from the event.
