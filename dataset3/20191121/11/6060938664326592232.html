Photos of the day: From Ajay Devgn and Nysa’s temple visit to celebs snapped around the town to airport spotting, here’s what made headlines

21 Nov 2019, 11:52 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Even though there was no big Bollywood event today, celebrities knew exactly how to keep the paparazzi busy. While Ajay Devgn grabbed attention by visiting a temple with his daughter Nysa, Kareena Kapoor Khan and Sunny Leone turned heads with at the airport.

Ajay Devgn visits temple with daughter Nysa
Father-daughter Ajay Devgn and Nysa were spotted this afternoon, paying a visit to a temple in the area. Dressed in simple casuals, the two were seen offing prayers to the deity and seeking blessings, after which they quickly headed to their waiting car.

Celebs spotted around town

Varun Dhawan and Shraddha Kapoor were snapped in the city by the shutterbugs. Dressed in their casual best, both of them paused and posed for the paparazzi. While Varun looked dapper as ever in his white printed tee and blue ripped jeans, Shraddha looked pretty as a peach in her red tee and blue denims.

Airport spotting

Kareena Kapoor Khan was snapped at the airport as she turned heads with her elegant fashion sense. Dressed in a comfy cotton kurta and straight-cut pants, Kareena nailed her airport look.

Sunny Leone was also snapped at the airport along with husband Daniel Weber and her kids – Nisha, Noah and Asher. Sunny looked like an absolute chic in her neaon green top and black pants. Her cool shades and black boots completed her overall stunning look.
