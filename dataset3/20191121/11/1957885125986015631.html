Pokémon Sword and Shield becomes fastest-selling Switch game ever

Time to Celebi-rate.

21 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Imogen Beckhelling

Pokémon Sword and Shield has sold over 6m units globally in its first week, becoming the fastest-selling Switch game ever.

This figure includes both digital and physical sales, The Pokémon Company has announced, doubling that of Pokémon Let's Go's last year which sold 3m in its first week.

Previously, the record opening week for a Switch title was Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, which sits at 5m worldwide sales.
