FULL WINNERS LIST: Nadia Nakai, Youngsta CPT and Cassper Nyovest win big at SA Hip Hop Awards

21 Nov 2019, 11:49

Cape Town - The very best in local entertainment stepped out for the 8th annual South Africa Hip Hop Awards, which took place on Wednesday at The Lyric Theatre in Johanneburg. 

Ahead of the award ceremony, celebrities, who were dressed to impressed, walked the red carpet including Lungile Radu, Dimpie Dimpopo, Candice Modiselle and Shamiso Mosaka. 

The night's top winners included YoungstaCPT who walked away with Album of the Year for 3T, and  Nadia Nakai who won Best Female. Cassper Nyovest won two awards including Hustler Of The Year.

Yanga Chief won Song of The Year for his track Utatakho.
