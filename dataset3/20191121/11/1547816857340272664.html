Bigg Boss 13: Would You Like To See Arhaan Khan Back On The Show?

21 Nov 2019, 11:07 by Prajakta Ajgaonkar

Rashami Desai's rumoured lover Arhaan Khan was evicted from Bigg Boss 13 during last week's Weekend ka Vaar. Post his elimination, the actor confessed that he loves Rashami and would want to re-enter the show just to propose her for marriage. So do you think he should be brought back on the show?

When Arhaan Khan was entering Bigg Boss 13 as wild card, tongues were wagging that the actor is entering the show for his rumoured girlfriend Rashami Desai. However, in an exclusive chat with SpotboyE.com, Arhaan had vehemently denied the same stating that he’s doing the show only for his mother.

Even when inside the house, he didn’t try to get too close to Rashami, when in fact, the actress confessed to Paras Chhabra in one of the episodes that she liked Arhaan and wishes to settle down with him next year.

Before their equation could grow stronger in the house, Arhaan was evicted due to less votes. His elimination left Rashami teary-eyed and one could see the connection.

The actor who stayed mum all this while, finally confessed in a Bombay Times interview post his exit, that he is in love with Rashami and wishes to go back to the house only so that he could propose her for marriage.
