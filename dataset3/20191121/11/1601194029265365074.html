It certainly has been a chaotic road to this Sunday’s Survivor Series PPV, with WWE NXT at the center of this year’s battle for brand supremacy.

The Black & Gold brand is on the verge of the biggest weekend in its history, as one night before Survivor Series, they also go to war at NXT Takeover: WarGames.

The build to that event has revolved a lot around the women of NXT, showcasing why the division is regarded as one of the best in all of wrestling. In the midst of that, one lady has taken the chance she’s been presented and grasped it with both hands. That Superstar is Kay Lee Ray.

The NXT UK Women’s Champion made a surprise appearance at the end of last week’s NXT, revealing herself as the final member of Team Shayna at WarGames. The Scot followed that up this week with an impressive win over Dakota Kai, before getting herself in the thick of the Survivor Series warfare.
