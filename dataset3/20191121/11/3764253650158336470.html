Primary school students melt hearts as they visit their friend at the hospital (photo)

21 Nov 2019, 11:10

- A group of young school boys have melted hearts on social media

- In a post shared on Facebook, a man wrote about the touching thing the young students did

- The young boys had assembled at the hospital to visit their friend who was involved in a bike accident

Many people often say that the hearts of children are pure until society contaminates it. Well, that seems to be the case of a group of young boys whose touching gesture has melted hearts on social media.

A Nigerian man identified as Shariff Yeng on Facebook took to the social media application to share the heart-melting thing the young boys did for their friend.

According to Yeng, the young pupils had assembled at the hospital to visit their school friend who had been involved in a bike accident.

Yeng wrote: “I came across these boys discussing stuff and laughing all around at the hospital-TTH. I asked what happened and I was told the boy in Red was knocked down by a motorbike on his way to school. So his friends took permission from school to come see him and have a moment with their friend. It was just so beautiful and it showed me what friendship should be”
