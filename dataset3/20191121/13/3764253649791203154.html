Davido's birthday: Special Spesh shares video of singer in the toilet

21 Nov 2019, 13:16

A video of Nigerian singer David Adeleke aka Davido is currently making the rounds on social media, as the singer celebrates his 27th birthday ceremony.

Legit.ng sighted the hilarious video posted on the official Instagram page of Davido Worldwide Music (DMW) official hypeman, Special Spesh.

The clip captures the moment the celebrant was taking a dump in the toilet. From indications, he did not lock the door after he had settled to relieve himself.

Unfortunately for him, his colleague used the opportunity to film him on camera and share the video on social media.

In the caption that accompanied the post, Special Spesh teasingly disclosed that the post is a payback for all the times he has also shared some of his awkward recordings on social media.
