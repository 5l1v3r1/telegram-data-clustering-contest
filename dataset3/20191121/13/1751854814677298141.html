Amazon is the place to go when looking for a deal or promotion. Especially during the established promotion days like the Black Friday and Cyber Monday, you will certainly find what you need on Amazon. Black Friday is still a week away, yet Amazon Black Friday deals have just begun with some superb sales on virtually everything. During previous promotions, Amazon Black Friday deals included free shipping on more than 10 million items and this will happen this year too. XIDU is already set for the big event with some of its best models.

XIDU Tour Pro

Going below the usual 13.3″ dimension, the Tour Pro carries a 12.5″ 2560×1440 display and that makes it very portable and easy to carry as it weighs just 2.22lbs and it sports ultra-thin 4.9mm borders around the display resulting in an 85% screen-to-body ratio. It comes with Intel 3867U 8th Gen CPU inside, backed by 8GB of RAM and GB of storage. It also comes with a webcam, ergonomic keyboard design, 802.1 b/g WiFi and Bluetooth 4.2, 2 x USB 3.0 ports, 1 x mini HDMI port, TF card slot, and headphone jack. It is available here along with its coupon that you can apply directly.

XIDU PhilBook Max

The PhilBook Max features a beautiful and smart design with a 360° flexible hinge that allows you to turn the laptop into a tablet in seconds taking advantage of the touchscreen. It is powered by the Intel E3950 4-core CPU backed by 8GB of RAM and 128GB SSD. It also features a backlit keyboard as well as 1 x Mini HDMI, 2 x USB 3.0, 1 x Microphone Jack, 1 x Bluetooth 4.2, 1 x MicroSD card slot, 802.11 ac/a/b/g/n, and a webcam. It’s available on Amazon with a $30 off coupon applied on the product page.

XIDU PhilPad

The PhilPad is a business-class Tablet PC designed for those who need a powerful and mobile tool for daily work, and comes with a 2K (2560×1440) display and is powered by Intel’s Atom x7-E3950 CPU backed by 6GB of RAM and 128GB of storage that can be expanded up to 256GB more. It’s just 0.4″ thick and the rest of the specs include a 5000mAh/7.4V battery, 1 x USB Type-C, 2 x USB 3.0, 1 x Microphone Jack, 1 x MicroSD card slot, and Dual-band 2.4GHz/5.0GHz WiFi. The promotion bundles the detachable keyboard and stylus too and is available here.

The 2019 Black Friday will be the “greatest and biggest ever” and will run for 8 days, starting today at midnight offering early deals alongside the “Lightning Deals” which are accessible for a limited period of time or until out of stock. Amazon Prime members will get early access to the Black Friday 2019 Lightning Deals and you can visit XIDU’s Amazon stores on Amazon US, UK, FR, IT, and ES as well as on Aliexpress. Also, the year-end promotion on their Official store is ongoing where you can get a $50 discount directly together with free gifts. To see all available coupons, check here.
