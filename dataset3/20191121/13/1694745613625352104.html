We Almost Mistook Kylie Jenner For Posh Spice Thanks to Her New Bob Haircut

21 Nov 2019, 13:25 by Sarah Siegel

Kylie Jenner just swapped her long dark waves for a sleek bob haircut. The reality star posted a few photos of herself to her Instagram Story to promote the holiday collection for Kylie Cosmetics featuring a new chin-length hairstyle.

If the cut looks familiar to you, it might be because you're a Spice Girls fan, as Victoria Beckham had a similar style for years while performing with the group. Jenner also now even more resembles her older sister Kim Kardashian, who debuted the same haircut in September.
