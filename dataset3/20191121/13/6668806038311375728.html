Kareena Kapoor Khan’s latest airport look is a perfect blend of comfort and style

21 Nov 2019, 13:31 by Kajol Mathur

Think Kareena Kapoor Khan and you know we are talking about one of the biggest female superstars in the country. Despite not being on social media, she’s one celeb who rules our Instagram feeds the most. Truly a superwoman, Kareena knows how to maintain the right balance between her professional and personal life. The actress has been currently working on her next with Aamir Khan titled Laal Singh Chaddha.

Since the past few days, Bebo had been shooting for the film in Chandigarh with Khan and finally returned to Mumbai this afternoon. Looking effortlessly stunning, she opted for a pair of kurta and pants in the tone of olive green. She rounded off her look with white sneakers and black sunnies. If you take one look at Kareena Kapoor Khan’s fashion choices, you would know that she loves to blend style with comfort and always look elegant doing so.
