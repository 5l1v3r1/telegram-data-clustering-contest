WOW! East Kilbride bar offering up £1000 worth of prizes at next pub quiz

Having had more than a lick of paint recently, the bar has undergone a significant transformation

21 Nov 2019, 13:06 by Fraser N Wilson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/fraser-n-wilson/

A newly refurbished bar in East Kilbride is offering up a HUGE prize for the winners of its next pub quiz.

Bar 1821 at The Byre is urging would-be Masterminds and Countdown afficianados to come along and pip their wit against rival quizzers.

And the pirze? A whopping £1000 worth of vouchers to be split between the winners and runners up of its next pub quiz!

Having had more than a lick of paint recently, the bar – which sits below Tony Macaroni’s in Hairmyres – has undergone a significant transformation.

And it looks incredible. And to go with the brand new look, a brand new quiz.

So, have you got vast general knowledge skills? And do you enjoy some competitive fun?

Then you need to head along to the Big Pub Quiz on Thursday, November 28.

With £1000 worth of vouchers to be won on the night, it’s the perfect Christmas boost for the winners.

Hosted by quizmaster Bruce, the not-to-be-missed quiz kicks off at 8pm.
