Thom Evans gets flustered over Nicole Scherzinger dating rumours

21 Nov 2019, 13:36

Thom Evans got seriously flustered when he was questioned over those Nicole Scherzinger romance claims – and it was absolutely not smooth.

The pair are rumoured to have been getting closer during filming for X Factor: Celebrity, with the pair spotted out on a romantic evening together.

And the Try Star singer did nothing to discourage the rumourmill during an appearance on Loose Women, alongside bandmates Ben Goden and Levi Davis.

When asked whether there was anything going on between himself and the Pussycat Doll favourite, he instantly became flustered as he stumbled over his words.

‘Nicole is lovely and you know, I… But I’ve been concentrating on the bromance… And getting to the final,’ the 34-year-old told the panel who couldn’t contain their giggles over his denial.

Smooth, hun.

‘Who wouldn’t want to date Nicole? She’s lovely and, ermmmm…’

Saving his bandmate, Ben jumped in to reply: ‘And who wouldn’t want to date Thom? Because he’s lovely.’

While Levi added: ‘Look at him’.

An insider recently claimed the rugby player was completely smitten with the ITV judge, and plucked up the courage to ask her out this week.

‘Thom is completely Nicole’s type. And for his part, Thom was smitten early on — he’s always said Nicole is one of the most beautiful women in the world,’ an insider claimed to The Sun.

‘He fancied her long before meeting her on the show. He was initially a bit nervous about starting something up, and ruining his chances on the series, but they had great chemistry from the off.

‘And after weeks of texting, Thom finally plucked up the courage to ask her out, and took her for dinner.’

Thom was a little more sure of himself when discussing his clapback to Louis Walsh on the latest X Factor instalment, after himself and Ben were branded ‘backing singers’.

Speaking about calling the music manager Simon Cowell’s backing singer, he admitted: ‘I can’t even believe I said that, to be honest.

‘We put so much time and effort into dance choreography, to the vocals… Then we had our song changed on the Friday back to No Diggity from an Ed Sheeran song.

‘It was the combination of all of that hard work for Louis to say we’re backing singers…’

Bring on this weekend’s X Factor: Celebrity semi-final…

Loose Women continues on weekdays, at 12.30, on ITV.
