IT Writer Won't Rule Out a Chapter Three Sequel

21 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Steve Russell

Gary Dauberman, the writer behind both IT and IT: Chapter Two, believes there's enough material within Stephen King's novel to create further movies and spinoffs revolving around the demonic entity Pennywise.

"I do think it's possible," Dauberman told ComicBookMovie.com. "Anything in the Stephen King Universe interests me but there's only so much of the story we could tell in the two movies. There are definitely elements of the novel you could expand on and make its own movie. It's just a question of whether or not people want to see it but I do think IT was on this planet for a very, very, very long time and that's a lot of bloodshed and a lot of stories to tell and I think you could do that for sure."
