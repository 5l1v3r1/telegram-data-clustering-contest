Jose Mourinho praises Japhet Tanganga: Who is the 20-year old defender lauded by Mourinho for marking Ronaldo during his first training session with Tottenham.

New head coach Jose Mourinho, took charge of his first training session with Tottenham on Wednesday afternoon. The side will play their first match under the Portuguese this Saturday when they take on West Ham at the London Stadium.

Spurs have uploaded highlights from the training session on their official YouTube channel. One particular incident from the session however, has left the fans in tizzy. Mourinho, who has a reputation of not giving youngsters a chance, was seen talking to 20-year old defender Japhet Tanganga.

Jose Mourinho praises Japhet Tanganga

In a clip from the practice, posted by fans on twitter, Mourinho can be heard saying to one of his coaching staff:

“He marked Cristiano Ronaldo, make him known.”
