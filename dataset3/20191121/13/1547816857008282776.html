Anu Malik Takes A BREAK From Indian Idol 11 Post #MeToo Acquisition Uproar; Will Get Back After Clearing Name

21 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Lubna Khan

Anu Malik’s comeback on Indian Idol 11 as a judge despite several #MeToo allegations leveled against him, didn’t sit well with a lot of people and there has been a severe backlash. Now, Anu Malik has taken a ‘voluntary’ break from Indian Idol 11 and said that he will get back only after clearing his name

Anu Malik’s comeback on Indian Idol 11 has been met with severe backlash from many and the makers of the reality show must not have anticipated the backlash they would have to face after reinstating Anu Malik on the judging panel of the singing reality show. Sona Mohapatra has been miffed right from the beginning about Anu Malik’s comeback despite several #MeToo allegations leveled against him and has also slammed the channel for the same. Neha Bhasin, along with Sona also spoke up against this matter and amidst the uproar, there were several reports that suggested Anu Malik might step down as the judge.

Now, there has been an update and while talking to ETimes, Anu Malik has said that he is taking a break from Indian Idol 11 and that it is a voluntary decision. He added that he would return only after he has cleared his name.
