Christmas Day brunches in Dubai 2019

Find your perfect Dubai Christmas lunch at restaurants across the city

21 Nov 2019, 13:10 by Time Out staff

Christmas is coming, and what's better than a proper roast dinner to celebrate the big day.

Turkey, roast potatoes, Yorkshire puddings, lashings of gravy and dollops of cranberry sauce, there's no other meal that's looked forward to more.

And luckily for those who like to go all out when it comes to festive feasting, we’ve got a brilliant list of the best Christmas Day brunches across Dubai.

There are options to suit all price ranges, whether you’re looking for a budget banquet costing less than Dhs200 or something a bit more extravagant.

So why not grab yourself a glass of mulled grape and have a scroll through our list, which we’ll be updating until the big day.
