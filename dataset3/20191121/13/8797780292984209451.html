Alien: Isolation is coming to Nintendo Switch on December 05

21 Nov 2019, 13:21 by Darryn Bonthuys

What’s the best horror game on the market today that just so happens to pull from the source material of vagina monsters designed by a Swiss nightmare fuel artist? The correct answer is naturally Alien: Isolation, SEGA’s incredibly suspenseful take on the Xenomorph mythos. At least that’s what I’ve been told, because I’ve had the game on my Xbox for several months now and I’ve yet to get past the main menu because I’m a massive coward.

If you’ve been wanting to play that game on the go while you freak out your fellow morning commuters, good news! SEGA have announced that Alien: Isolation will be hitting Nintendo Switch on December 05. Here’s the reveal trailer for it:
