I'm A Celebrity: Viewers are making the same joke about Nadine's singing

Singer said she's too shy to, ahem, sing…

21 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Nancy Brown

Viewers have reacted to Nadine Coyle's shock revelation that she's "really really shy" so won't be singing in the I'm A Celebrity jungle.

During a probing chat with campmate Jacqueline Jossa, Nadine revealed that while she still gigs "all the time", she won't be warbling during her stint in Oz.

"Can you sing to us at some point?" Jac asked.

"See, I'm so shy, I am really really shy," came Nadine's reply. "How I became a singer and how I do any of this stuff is just beyond me."

"Maybe less pressured then," Jacqueline pushed, "in a different environment?"

"Yes, of course," came the former Girls Aloud star's reply. "If we're out drinking and having a good time then I will be singing."
