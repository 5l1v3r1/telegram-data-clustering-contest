EL OF A PLACE

Inside the spectacular £34m mansion owned by Tiger Woods’ ex-wife Elin Nordegren, including gym, pool and ocean views

21 Nov 2019, 13:49 by Sam Morgan

TIGER WOODS' ex-wife's incredible Florida mansion has just had its price slashed - and could be yours for £34.4MILLION.

Yes, even with £3.9m taken off, Elin Nordegren's palatial home costs an absolute fortune - although not as much as her former husband's.

And it's easy to see why.

The Swedish model and former nanny, 39, was rumoured to have got a £77m payoff when she divorced Woods, 43, in 2010 after he admitted cheating with dozens of women and checked himself into rehab for sex addiction.

Nordegren splashed some of that cash on the 25,878sq ft oceanfront home in North Palm Beach, Florida, an exclusive area loved by multi-millionaires.

Bought in 2014, the mansion comes with 11 bedrooms, 15 bathrooms, three half bathrooms and breathtaking ocean views.

The masters bedroom boasts his and hers bathrooms, walk-in wardrobe, THREE fireplaces and a balcony to look out over the Atlantic.

Got guests over? No problem. All four of the guest bedrooms have en-suites and walk-in closets.

The stunning outdoor pool - with slide and spa - sits before a lush garden that leads down to the beach.

One of the most amazing features is the three-storey Swarovski crystal chandelier that lights up the staircase.

And there is a gym, lounge area with impressive fire pits, half basketball court and a putting green set inside the 1.4 acre estate.

The wine cellar is perfect for when you get a bit thirsty and you can watch a film in the plush home cinema.

A four-car garage means there is plenty of room for visitors - who can stay in a guesthouse which boats two, two-bedroom apartments.

Woods, who will captain America in next month's Presidents Cup in Australia, and Elin have two children together, daughter Sam, 12, and son Charlie, 10.

Their marriage imploded when Woods - who now dates Erica Herman - crashed his car into a fire hydrant outside their Orlando home on Thanksgiving 2009.

Nordegren - who last month had a child with NFL star Jordan Cameron - was alleged to have smashed the back window of his SUV open with a 9-iron after she chased him out the house.

The couple - who married in a private Bahamas ceremony in 2004 - divorced around six months later when the scale of the golfing legend's cheating became clear.

Several women including porn stars, pancake house waitresses and Las Vegas party girls came forward to say they had slept with the 15-time Major champion.

Woods went public with Olympic skier Lindsey Vonn in 2013 before she announced their break-up three years later.

The 15-time Major champion would hit rock bottom in 2017 when he was arrested for driving under the influence.

But he reached the top of the golfing world back in April when he completed arguably sport's greatest ever comeback by winning The Masters.
