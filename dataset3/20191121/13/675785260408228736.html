Martin Lewis raves about super cheap £8 SIM only deal from Three Mobile for Black Friday

Three Mobile's impressive Black Friday SIM only deal gets you 8GB for just £8 a month - an absolute steal according to everyone's favourite finance expert Martin Lewis

21 Nov 2019, 13:55 by Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/mollyjaholt/

Black Friday may still be a good week away, but that hasn't stopped almost every tech retailer under the sun launching their offers early.

But with so many to pick from, it's hard to work out which ones are actually a bargain, and which are just latching onto the Black Friday name.

Cue Martin Lewis, who has tracked down one very impressive SIM only offer from Three Mobile, that gets you 8GB of data for just £8 a month.

Complete with all-you-can-eat minutes, it's available on a 12 month contract for a mere £8 a month, with no upfront cost, working out at just £96 for the entire year.

Martin Lewis originally revealed the news on Good Morning Britain earlier today, alongside a slew of other Black Friday deals, including a different Smarty SIM only deal that gets you 45GB data, unlimited minutes and texts for just £15 per month on a 12-month contract.

Boots' Black Friday beauty gift sets also earned a spot on his list of best offers, as Martin Lewis praised the brand's No7 Beauty Wardrobe gift set, which is currently just £25 instead of £50, but is worth a hefty £87.

If you're looking for an 8GB SIM only deal, we spotted an even cheaper one on Plusnet, although it's a tad more complex than Three's.

Working out at £7.92 per month, it's essentially 8GB of data for £10 a month - but with a £25 prepaid Mastercard that you can use in most places. So while it is 96p cheaper than Three's altogether, it's also slightly more faff.
