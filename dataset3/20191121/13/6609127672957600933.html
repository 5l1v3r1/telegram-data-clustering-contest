'GUESS WHO'

Rangers star Ryan Jack pictured in hilarious Aberdeen throwback with Fraser Fyvie

21 Nov 2019, 13:07 by David Fowler

RANGERS star Ryan Jack played at Aberdeen for seven years between 2010 and 2017.

The Gers ace turned out to be a great player for the Dons, as he came through the youth ranks to make his mark on the first team and eventually skipper them.

The Scotland international played with some great players such as Adam Rooney, Niall McGinn and Jonny Hayes.

However, its one other familiar name who has popped up to give fans a laugh with a baby-faced throwback picture.

Cove Rangers ace Fraser Fyvie, who played with Jack at the Dons for three years in the first team, shared the snap on social media.

The 26-year-old revisited their youth days in the north-east with a classic picture.
