Inside Chiefs: Amakhosi With Tough Decisions To Make

21 Nov 2019, 13:51 by Soccer Laduma

The doors were opened to Kaizer Chiefs’ Naturena Village today ahead of this Sunday’s Telkom Knockout semi-final, and there were some good sights for the Amakhosi faithful…

Read: Chiefs Star Gives Amakhosi TKO Boost

The Glamour Boys host Maritzburg United at Mbombela Stadium this weekend as both sides battle it out for a spot in the final of this year’s TKO competition, but Ernst Middendorp was dealt a blow earlier in the month with the suspension of Erick Mathoho, Willard Katsande and Daniel Akpeyi.

With that in mind, fans of the club will be happy to see images of the likes of Kearyn Baccus and Nkosingiphile Ngcobo in full training today ahead of their big semi-final, while Reeve Frosler – who was battling for fitness before the international break – also looks to be raring to go.

Goalkeeper, Itumeleng Khune, was booted up and in training, but he is not yet ready to take his place in the starting XI v Maritzburg, with Bruce Bvuma set to start in place of the suspended Akpeyi.

Middendorp will have some tough decisions to make regarding his starting line-up with regards to other positions on the field, especially with Baccus and Ngcobo now seemingly back in contention, and fans will be eagerly awaiting his choices come 15h00 on Sunday afternoon.
