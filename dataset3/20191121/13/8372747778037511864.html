Man gets 20 years for fatal hit and run on a boy in 2017

21 Nov 2019, 13:48 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- A judge has sentenced a man to 20 years in prison for a fatal hit and run on a 13-year-old boy in 2017.

The crime happened in November 2017. Silva-Diaz crashed his pick-up truck into a teenager on a bicycle in East Naples and fled the scene.
