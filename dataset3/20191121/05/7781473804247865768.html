John Abraham gives a tour of his garage - a closer look at his superbike collection

21 Nov 2019, 05:40 by Aditya Jadhav  

Bollywood celebrity, John Abraham posted a video on his social network platform giving his fans and motorcycle enthusiasts a sneak peek of his garage. He personally gives a tour through his collection of superbikes starting from the Kawasaki Ninja ZX-14R followed by the Aprilia RSV4 RF. He also highlights that the RSV4 is equipped with the SC-Project exhaust. Then proceeding to the newest addition to his superbike collection, the current generation Yamaha YZF-R1, which will soon be equipped with an aftermarket Akrapovic exhaust system.
