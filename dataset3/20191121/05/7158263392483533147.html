The 15 Best Couple's Retreat Locations

21 Nov 2019, 05:00

It's no secret that going on vacation can do wonders for your mental health, and the time off can also be good for your relationship if you choose to go with your partner. Maybe it's your first big trip together or you're just looking to zuzh things up... either way, getting on a plane (or in a car) and heading to a couples' focused retreat can take your love to even higher grounds.

Whether you're looking to rekindle your romance or just want to getaway for a vacay with bae, here are the 15 coolest couple's retreat packages in the world.

1 Your Own "Bachelor" Episode in Lake Forest, Illinois

Location: Lake Forest, Illinois

Price: Starting at $320/night

Cheapest months to book: January–March

Activities: Partnered yoga practice, mixology class at the inn's Couples Ginstitute (get it), rose petal and chocolate fondue turndown service, soaking in your giant tub that fills from the ceiling and the potential to add on activities like a Bachelor-style helicopter ride around town.

Book Now Couple's Retreat Package, Deer Path Inn

2 A "Rebirth" in Playa Del Carmen, Mexico

Location: Playa Del Carmen, Mexico

Price: Starting at $281

Cheapest months to book: June–September

Activities: Something fancy called a "bath of emperors" aka a Temazcal stone steam bath in a mud hut led by a local Shaman (it's supposed to symbolize rebirth), astronomy lessons, and salsa dancing classes. P.S. the whole resort is adults-only.

Book Now Hilton Playa del Carmen

3 Fireside Tarot and Wellness Getaway at an Upstate New York Inn

Location: Aurora, NY

Price: $3,500 per person

Cheapest months to book: November–April

Activities: Ayurveda consultations on how to best support your partner's energy when out of balance, restorative yoga sessions, nature walks with guided meditation, organic tea blending and cooking classes, fireside tarot card reading.

Book Now Gift of Wellness Package, Inns of Aurora

4 The Ultimate Safari Experience at Angama Mara, Kenya

Location: Maasai Mara, Kenya

Price: $5,595

Cheapest months to book: November–May

Activities: five nights of accommodations and access to the lodge's activities and experiences including bush barbecues, Maasai beading, full-day safaris with picnic lunches and balloon safari.

Book Now The Perfect Safari Package, Angama Mara

5 The Bougiest of Bouge with a Private Jet in France

Location: Paris, France

Price: from 13,545€ for complete package

Cheapest months to book: December–mid March

Activities: Holistic wellness offerings including personal nutrition counseling, fitness, meditation and breathing sessions, a private jet that brings you to a ski resort at Les Nieges Courchevel, and add ons include: hot air balloon rides and dog sledding. Bou. GIE.

Book Now Hotel Barrière Le Fouquet's

6 Swim with Whale Sharks at Puerta Cortes, Mexico

Location: Puerta Cortes, Mexico

Price: Starting at $200/night; Full activities cost $1,500 not including taxes

Cheapest months to book: June, September, and October

Activities: Sunset drinks, golf, swimming with whale sharks, sea lions, snorkelling, and a romantic lunch on a super secluded beach, couples massages and the option to add on cooking lessons, golf and tennis lessons, yoga on the beach, and mezcal tasting.

Book Now Couples Retreat Package, Puerta Cortes

7 An Old Money Weekend at a Literal Castle in Ireland

Location: County Mayo, Ireland

Price: starting at €498

Cheapest months to book: January or February

Activities: A two-night stay in the castle with rose petal turndown service, champagne, and a candlelit five-course dinner; Equestrian center, fly fishing, golf.

Book Now Sense of Occasion Package, Ashford Castle

8 THE Couples Retreat from the Movie in Tahiti

Location: St. Regis Bora Bora, the filming location of the actual movie Couple's Retreat

Price: Five nights from $4,959 per person, including roundtrip airfare from Los Angeles or San Francisco

Cheapest months to book: November–March

Activities: Snorkelling, tandem parasailing, and a romantic beach dinner. You know, plus bragging rights that you went on the same exact couples' retreat from the literal movie Couple's Retreat.

Book Now Couples Retreat, St. Regis Bora Bora

9 The Ultimate Foodie Retreat Complete with Truffle Hunting in Umbria, Italy

Location: Umbria, Italy

Price: $4,952 for three nights, all inclusive for two adults, including a transfer from Rome and anywhere in Umbria with a chauffeur

Cheapest months to book: Spring through December

Activities: Private truffle hunting tour, a day of shepherding with a picnic on the estate, pasta-making lessons with a real Nonna, cheese-making lessons, cooking lessons, and all meals and wine included.

Book Now Epicurean Experience

10 A Jam Packed Cooking, Yoga, and Crafting Experience in Tembok, Bali

Location: Tembok, Bali

Price: $280/night

Cheapest months to book: Rates are consistent year round

Activities: Spa treatments for two every night of your stay, stargazing, and daily scheduled activities including cooking classes, candle making, jamu juice making, yoga, and more. All daily meals and airport transfers are also included.

Book Now Romantic Retreat Package, Spa Village Resort Tembok Bali

11 A Jungle Trekking Malaysian Retreat in Perak, Malaysia

Location: Perak, Malaysia

Price: $230/night

Cheapest months to book: Rates are consistent year round

Activities: Speedboat transfers, a couple's massage, a sunset cruise for two, one candlelit dinner at Fisherman's Cove, and a jungle trekking session for two with the island's resident naturalist.

Book Now Romantic Retreat Package, Pangkor Laut Resort

12 THE Classic Wellness Retreat at Canyon Ranch Tucson

Location: Tucson, Arizona

Price: Starting at $1,749 per night

Cheapest months to book: Summer

Activities: If you thought Canyon Ranch was a rehab center: same. Turns out it's not, it's just been forever associated with wellness for so long that I guess it seems that way? Anyhoozles, here you can curate your own romance package and choose from communication workshops, meditation, facials, astrology programming with a special focus on gaining insight to love, career, health, wealth, travel, and spirituality. Rates are also all inclusive and include airport pickups, meals, snacks, art classes, and more.

Book Now Customized Wellness Package, Canyon Ranch

13 An Ancient Roman Weekend in Florence, Italy

Location: Florence, Italy

Price: Starting at $900/night

Cheapest months to book: November–April

Activities: You get three nights in a historic frescoed suite (art history majors come through), brekky in bed, a private rooftop dinner for two, a cocktail class, and private use of their calidarium, aka an old school Roman bath.

Book Now Ancient Romance Package, Grand Hotel Minverva

14 An Island Getaway in St. Lucia

Location: Calabash Cove, St. Lucia

Price: Starting at $295

Cheapest months to book: Rates are the same year round

Activities: The resort is all-inclusive so meals (the menu changes often, so no cruise fare here), bevvies, room service (yes including lobster when in season), are available. Oh, and here's a full-play-by-play of what you and bae can expect at Calabash Cove.

Book Now Calabash Cove

15 A Romantic Winter Escape in New Hampshire

Location: Loon Mountain, New Hampshire

Price: Starting at $783 for two people

Cheapest months to book: Fall

Activities: Stay two nights in a deluxe suite, get $75 towards their on-site restaurant, a wine tasting experience, and choice of spa treatment. They also have an on-site outdoor skating rink (rinks are available there, so no need to pack extra), and the option to add on snow shoeing, shuttles to skiing at Loon Mountain, and more.

Book Now Stay and Relax Package, Loon Mountain
