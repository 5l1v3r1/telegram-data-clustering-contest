OUI holds seventh convocation - The Nation Newspaper

21 Nov 2019, 05:21

Activities marking the seventh graduation of Oduduwa University, Ipetumodu (OUI), Osun State will begin on November 27.

A statement by the school said the two-day event will be heralded by a news conference, which will be followed later by a convocation lecture to be delivered by Prof. Tunji Olaopa, who will speak on “Banditry, kidnapping and insurgency in Nigeria: A gradual recession into a failed state.”

The event holds at Oba Okunade Sijuwade Hall, OUI.

A convocation play titled ‘Oya’ follows later at the same hall later in the day.

The two-day event will end with the convocation proper on November 28 with the award of postgraduate degrees, first degrees and award of prizes at Ramon Adedoyin Presidential Hall between noon and 3pm.
