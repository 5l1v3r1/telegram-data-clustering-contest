Jim Carrey Takes Aim at John Bolton 'Cowardice' in Latest Political Artwork

Carrey shared his latest doodle among the hearings in the impeachment inquiry into President Trump

21 Nov 2019, 05:25 by Lindsey Ellefson

Former national security adviser and ambassador to the United Nations John Bolton isn’t scheduled to testify during House hearings around the impeachment inquiry into President Donald Trump — and Jim Carrey thinks that is “just another profile in cowardice.”

The outspoken actor on Tuesday again used his doodling skills to take on a former member of Trump’s administration. This time, he wrote “BOLTON” down the side along with, “…the tough get going and the weak get silent!”
