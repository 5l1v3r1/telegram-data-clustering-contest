BTS's "Boy With Luv" becomes the 2nd most liked video of 2019.

21 Nov 2019, 05:19 by greenbaypackers

BTS has been breaking record after record in 2019 and here is a new one. BTS's Boy With Luv, which was released on April 12th, has reached 13 million likes on Youtube. The boys are just barely second to Shawn Mendes and Camilla Cabello's Senorita who currently hold the title of Number 1 .

This is also BTS's first video to hit 13 million likes. Congratulations to BTS!
