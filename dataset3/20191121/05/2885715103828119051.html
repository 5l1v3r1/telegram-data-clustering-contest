TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Applications invited for of 1508 Lab Technician Grade III posts, check details here

TNB MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: The selected candidates will be paid a sum of Rs 8000 per month and will be selected on contract basis.

21 Nov 2019, 05:52 by Careers Desk

TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Tamil Nadu Medical Services Recruitment Board (MRB), Chennai has released an official notification inviting applications for the recruitment of laboratory technician grade III posts. The total number of vacant posts is 1,508. Interested candidates must apply online on the official website-mrb.tn.gov.in.

The last date to apply for the posts is December 9 and the selection of the candidates will be done on the basis of the marks scored by the candidates in their academic and technical qualification.

TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Important dates

-Online application ends: December 9, 2019

-Last date to pay online fee: December 9, 2019

-Last date to pay offline fee: December 11, 2019

TNB MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Eligibility

Age limit: The candidates should be in the age limit of 18 to 30 years. However, reserved category candidates will get age relaxation according to the government norms.

Educational qualification: The candidates must possess a certificate in Medical Lab Technology Course (one year duration) undergone in any institution recognized by the Director of Medical Education. The candidate must also have a good physique, good vision and capacity to do outdoor work.

TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Selection Procedure

The selection of the candidates will be done on the basis of marks scored by the candidates in their academic and technical qualification and other conditions for the post of Laboratory Technician Grade-III. There will be no interview for the post.

TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Application fee

General and OBC category candidates have to pay an application fee of Rs 600. SC/SCA/ST/DAP(PH)/DW category candidates will have to pay Rs 300 as application fee.

TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website-http://www.mrb.tn.gov.in

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘Online Registration.’

Step 3: On the page that opens, click on the link for Laboratory Technician Grade-III posts

Step 4: Fill in the application form and upload the scanned picture and signature and click on submit.

Step 5: Pay the application fee and take a print out of the application form for future reference.

TN MRB Lab Technician recruitment 2019: Salary

The selected candidates will be paid a sum of Rs 8000 per month.

The candidates must have a mobile number and a working e-mail ID to fill the application form. The candidates will be selected on contract and on a consolidated pay basis.
