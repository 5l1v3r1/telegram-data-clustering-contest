Baker Mayfield Reacts To Myles Garrett’s ‘Racial Slur’ Allegation

21 Nov 2019, 19:33 by Chris Rosvoglou

We have our first report of what took place during Myles Garrett’s appeal process. According to ESPN insider Adam Schefter, the Browns defensive end accused Pittsburgh Steelers quarterback Mason Rudolph of using a racial slur prior to their brawl.

Garrett received an indefinite suspension from the NFL for his role in Thursday night’s melee.

In an effort to have his punishment reduced, Garrett met with league executives in New York on Wednesday. He’ll know his fate at some point this week.

As of right now, the latest racial slur allegations from Garrett are taking the league by storm.

It’s not too surprising that players on the Steelers are denying these claims. On the flip side, players on the Browns are also shocked by this development.

Baker Mayfield revealed that he never heard of any racial slur remarks prior to today.
