Update On T.Y. Hilton Ahead Of Tonight’s Game vs. Texans

21 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Alek Arend

T.Y. Hilton has missed the Colts’ last three contests due to a calf injury. But things are trending in the right direction for Hilton – and it looks like there’s a strong chance he plays against the Texans on Thursday Night Football.

James Palmer of NFL Network reports Hilton “believes he’s good to go” against Houston tonight. This would be a massive boost for the Indianapolis offense.

According to Palmer, the Colts “don’t have any issue with him not practicing and playing.” Houston is also expecting Hilton to play tonight.
