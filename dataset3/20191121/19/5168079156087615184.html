Times of Oman, Shabiba quizzes postponed

21 Nov 2019, 19:09 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Times of Oman Interschool Quiz and Shabiba Musabakat Bayn Madaris have been postponed by a day owing to heavy rain in Muscat and other areas of the Sultanate.

The Times quiz, which was supposed to be held on Friday, 22 November, will now be held on Saturday the 23rd, while the Shabiba Arabic quiz, which was to be held on Saturday, will has now been pushed forward to Sunday evening.

"The mega prelims and final of the English quiz will be held on Saturday, while the Arabic mega prelims and final will be held on Sunday," said Ramesh Gopalan, the CEO of Orbit Events, the company running and organising the quiz in collaboration with Times of Oman and Al Shabiba. "Everything stays the same, we have just postponed the quizzes by a day."
