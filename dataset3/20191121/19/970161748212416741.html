Who is Nadine Coyle's ex Jason Bell, do they have kids and why did they split?

21 Nov 2019, 19:45

Nadine Coyle has been getting to grips with life in the jungle in I’m A Celebrity – kicking her time on the show off with a terrifying skydive as well as spilling gossip on her fellow Girls Aloud stars while in camp.

The former girl band-er comes to the show just a couple of months after it was reported that she had split from her long-term partner Jason Bell after an 11-year relationship.

Just who is Jason and what happened between the pair of them?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who is Nadine Coyle’s ex-partner Jason Bell?

Jason is an American football star who’s played for the Dallas Cowboys, Houston Texans and New York Giants.

Since retiring in 2008 he has moved into private wealth management, specialising in the financial needs of athletes and entertainers.

He is also an NFL pundit who has appeared on BBC coverage of American football.

Nadine and Jason began dating in 2008 when they met in the US after the singer moved there for work.

They got engaged in 2010 but split up a year later – albeit briefly, before reconciling – with Nadine later revealing that she got cold feet after deciding she was too young to be married.

I got with Jason when I was 23 and [got] engaged, and I suddenly thought: ‘Hang on. I can’t get married, I’m only 25,’ she told Fabulous magazine in 2018.

Do Nadine and Jason have children?

Nadine and Jason are parents to a daughter, Anaiya, who was born in February 2014.

At the time, Nadine announced: ‘I am overjoyed to let you know our little girl is here. She is amazing! I love being a mammy & will fill you all in soon.’

It’s been reported that she and Jason will co-parent their now five-year-old daughter following their split.

Why did Nadine and Jason split?

Although no official reason has been given for the split, it was thought to have been down to the pressure put on the couple by living in different countries – with Nadine moving back to her native Northern Ireland in 2016 while Jason remained in the US.

‘Although Nadine and Jason were never married, they lived as a close family unit and remain good friends as they co-parent their young daughter,’ an insider told The Sun at the time.
