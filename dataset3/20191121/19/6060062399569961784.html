Popeyes just threw shade at Chick-fil-A on Twitter, and it reveals that the Chicken Sandwich Wars are far from over

21 Nov 2019, 19:55 by Shoshy Ciment
  • Popeyes is resurrecting its Twitter feud with Chick-fil-A.
  • "I'm gonna tell my kids this is the Original Chicken Sandwich," Popeyes tweeted on Thursday in what appears to be a jab at Chick-fil-A, which often describes itself as the home of the original chicken sandwich.
  • The two chains have been involved in a battle over which has the better chicken sandwich since August.
  • "The Chicken Sandwich was the biggest product launch in the history of our brand and we thought it would be fun to connect with our fans by jumping on the pop-culture trend we're seeing on social media that explains today's events to future generations," Popeyes said in a statement.
