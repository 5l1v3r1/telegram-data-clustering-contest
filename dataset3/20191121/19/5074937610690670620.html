GFA to set up new administrative departments

21 Nov 2019, 19:45

Accra, Nov. 21, GNA - The Executive Council of the Ghana Football Association (GFA), has said it will set up new administrative departments in line with the Council's vision for the Association.

The new departments to be created are; Competitions Department, Human Resource Department , National Teams Department, Audit and Compliance, as well as Marketing and Sponsorship Department.

The Executive Council has also decided to resource existing departments such as Communications and Technical Directorate to make them fully functional.
