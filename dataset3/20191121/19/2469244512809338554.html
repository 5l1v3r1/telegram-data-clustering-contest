Rock your socks off with Crow Dawn in Uffculme

On Saturday, November 30, they will be at The Ostler Inn

21 Nov 2019, 19:10 by Lewis Clarke

Crow Dawn are making a welcome return to Uffculme next week.

On Saturday, November 30, they will be at The Ostler Inn Playing rock covers from the 60s to the present day.

Jim Clancy from the pub said: “Expect to be rocking your socks off to Led Zeppelin, Queen, Deep Purple, Status Quo, The Police, Pink Floyd, Beatles, AC/DC, Muse, Squeeze, Rush and many more.

“Mike, Rob, Tim and Steve are real entertainers who like to break the usual boundaries between the audience and band – and boy can they play.

“As usual the music starts at 9:00pm and it’s live and free.”
