Chris Brown Teases Birth Of His First Son

21 Nov 2019, 19:14 by Shakiel Mahjouri

It seems Chris Brown is adding to the family.

The “Kiss Kiss” singer was expecting his second child with his ex Ammika Harris and that time has reportedly come. Brown, 30, published two Instagram posts teasing the arrival of his baby boy. Furthermore, sources close to the singer reportedly confirmed the news to TMZ.

Late Wednesday night, Brown posted a black-and-white photo of himself looking down off-camera. The photo was accompanied with the caption, “11-20-2019.” Soon after, he posted another picture of himself — this time wearing a hoodie adorned with the word “BORN.”

Harris also posted a telling Instagram Story reading, “I was in love when I first saw you.”

This is Brown’s second child and first with Harris. Brown is also father to daughter Royalty, 5, with model Nia Amey.
