Ghana's football season to kick start December 21

21 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Simon Asare, GNA

Accra, Nov. 21, GNA - The Ghana Football Association (GFA) has announced that the Ghana Premier League (GPL), Division One League (DOL) and the National Womens League (NWL) will kick start on December, 21, 2019.

The new calendar for the 2019/2020 season indicates that, the registration of players across various leagues which opened on start October 14 would end on December 15, 2019.

Clubs are expected to declare their match venues, strips and authorized signatories for the Club Licensing Board expected to inspect the venues.

The GFA would hold its Ordinary Congress on Thursday, December 12, 2019 as stipulated in its new statutes especially with the commencement of the new season and also review its activities and modalities.
