Rapper Snoop Dogg to perform at Seminole Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Tampa

21 Nov 2019, 19:39 by KJ Hiramoto

TAMPA, Fla. -- A hip-hop legend is making his way to the Tampa Bay area during the holiday season.

Rapper Snoop Dogg will perform at the Seminole Hard Rock Hotel and Casino Tampa on December 29 for the resort's pool party.

Guests must be 21 years or older to attend.
