ESPN Analyst Rips T.O. For What He Did On First Take

21 Nov 2019, 19:54 by Matt Hladik

If nothing else, ESPN’s First Take usually produces great theater. It certainly manufactured some of that this morning when Terrell Owens made an appearance.

In a contentious segment, Owens castigated Stephen A. Smith for his criticism of Colin Kaepernick. In his comments, Owens said First Take co-host Max Kellerman “seemed blacker” than Smith.

Naturally, Smith did not take that remark too well. Owens later claimed he didn’t mean any disrespect, but Smith clearly felt otherwise.
