Protest against disinvestment move

21 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Special Correspondent

Benny Behanan, MP, told the Lok Sabha on Thursday that the move to disinvest five major PSUs, including BPCL, would plunge the country into severe economic recession.

This was tantamount to selling off India, he said, terming it as the result of a frightening stance on the part of the Centre to place the enterprises — BPCL, Shipping Corporation of India, Container Corporation of India, THDCIL and North Eastern Electric Power Corporation — at the feet of big corporations.

Meanwhile, the CITU Ernakulam district committee will organise a human protection chain at 4 p.m. on Friday between Chithrapuzha and IRDP gate at Ambalamedu to protest the Centre’s decision to sell its stake in BPCL. CPI(M) leader M.V. Govindan will inaugurate a public meeting to be held later.
