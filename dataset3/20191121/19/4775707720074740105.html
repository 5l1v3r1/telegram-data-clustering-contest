X019 brought the announcement that around 50 games would be coming to Xbox Games Pass and Xbox Game Pass for PC, new titles have dropped today for Xbox Game Pass and Xbox Game Pass for PC.

For both Xbox Game Pass and Xbox Game Pass for PC, there is:

  • Darksiders III
  • Vambrace: Cold Soul
  • Munchkin: Quacked Quest

Just for Xbox Game Pass, there is:

  • Life is Strange 2 - Episode 4

Just on Game Pass for PC, there is:

  • The Red Strings Club
  • Subnautica

All of these titles are available now.

Darksiders III

Darksiders III is an action-adventure game about FURY, a mage and one of the Four Horsemen. Her task is to eliminate each of the Seven Deadly Sins, who are rampaging around Earth and threatening Armageddon. FURY has various forms, each of which equip her with new moves and weapons, and she'll need them; to get to the Seven Deadly Sins, she has to go through their servants.
