Grand Harmony to host Christmas concert next weekend

'The Spirit of Christmas' is set for Nov. 30th

21 Nov 2019, 23:00 by KitchenerToday Staff

NEWS RELEASE
GRAND HARMONY
**************************
Getting into the Christmas Spirit isn't hard especially when you kick off the season by attending the annual Grand Harmony Women’s Chorus Christmas concert. Over the years this unique concert has become a highlight of the season and a yearly tradition for hundreds of people.

Join the nearly 60-member Show Chorus and Quartets for an afternoon of Christmas music. They will perform traditional favourites such as Carol of the Bells, Silent Night, Winter Wonderland and O Christmas Tree as well as contemporary songs including Once Upon a December, Candles Long Ago, You're a Mean One, Mr. Grinch and A Holly Jolly Christmas.

The ‘Spirit of Christmas’ concert will take place Saturday, November 30, 2019 from 2 - 4pm at the Glencairn Mennonite Brethren Church, 725 Erinbrook Drive in Kitchener. (To accommodate a larger audience, the show moved to this new location this year).

Doors open at 1:30. Show begins at 2pm. Tickets are $20 adults, $10 students, Children 5 and under, free. Tickets are available at www.eventbrite.ca/e/the-spirit-of-christmasconcert-tickets

Complimentary coffee, tea, lemonade and snacks will be provided. Silent Auction, Raffle baskets. Delicious Christmas cookies ($5) available for purchase, and you can get a head start on your Christmas shopping, as there will also be several craft vendors.
