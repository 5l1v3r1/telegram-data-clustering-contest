Jamie Lynn Spears Reunites With Zoey 101 Cast For a Revival of Iconic All That Skit

21 Nov 2019, 23:45 by Erin Cullum

Although we still have no confirmation of a long-rumored Zoey 101 reboot, the cast of the show are getting together for another nostalgic reason: a new version of All That's sketch "Thelma Stump." Spears shared a photo on Thursday smiling alongside her former Zoey 101 cast members Paul Butcher, Sean Flynn, Kristin Herrera, Chris Massey, Erin Sanders, and Matthew Underwood on set at Nickelodeon. The all-new "Thelma Stump" episode will air in 2020, according to People.

To refresh your memory, Spears starred in the hilarious reoccurring sketch in the early 2000s as a stubborn bodyguard in her 80s named Thelma Stump who gave All That cast members a hard time. She'd often demand to see their pass if they wanted to access the set, and she was typically found in a rocking chair singing "Ain't No Sunshine When She's Gone." Who could forget the episodes with Britney Spears and her little sis? The 28-year-old singer and actress is also returning to the small screen with a role on Netflix's upcoming series Sweet Magnolias. This isn't the full Zoey 101 update we were hoping for, but it's enough to hold us over!
