Here’s Everything Coming to Netflix in December 2019

21 Nov 2019, 23:15 by Genevieve Scarano

It’s the holiday season and if you’re recovering from Thanksgiving, Black Friday, and Cyber Monday, you may want to stream Netflix’s new documentaries, movies, and TV shows premiering this month.

Many fantasy and sci-fi shows will be available to watch soon, including SYFY’s Nightflyers, vampire drama V Wars, and monster hunting series The Witcher.

If you want to binge-watch an action flick with friends or fam, Netflix’s 6 Underground brings in the dynamite. Or if a tear-jerker flick is more your thing, Adam Driver and Scarlett Johansson go through an emotional divorce in Marriage Story.

Of course, Netflix doesn’t disappoint when it comes to cute, cheesy, and hilarious holiday films and specials. Home for Christmas, Jack Whitehall: Christmas with my Father, and A Family Reunion Christmas will be added to the streaming service’s festive lineup.

Here’s everything coming to Netflix in December 2019. If you’re not a subscriber yet, start your free trial here.
