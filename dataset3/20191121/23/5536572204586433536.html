Texans Linebackers Show Up To TNF Game In Mortal Kombat Costumes (VIDEO)

21 Nov 2019, 23:41 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Houston Texans are not letting Last Sunday’s lost to the Baltimore Ravens get them down.

Just days after being blown out by Lamar Jackson in company, a few members of the Texans linebacker core came to Thursday Night Football in full Mortal Kombat costumes.
