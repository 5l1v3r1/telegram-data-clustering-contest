Best Of Linkedin Pages 2019: Announcing The Winners!

21 Nov 2019, 23:59 by Geoff Desreumaux

LinkedIn announced their 2019 Best of Pages winners, highlighting top organizations that are doing creative and innovative things to distinguish their brands on LinkedIn.

Of all platforms LinkedIn might be the one you find the most challenging. Born as professional social network, the platform has evolved drastically over the past years to become a true marketing powerhouse.

Every year, LinkedIn puts a call out for brands to submit their pages in the annual #BestOfPages contest. Thousands of people made their voices heard this year again: 12,000 submissions, 9,234 members involved and over 111 countries participating!

And now, the results have been tallied, and LinkedIn has just announced the 10 winners.

Best of Pages 2019: By the Numbers

  • Total Nominations: 12,000+
  • Unique Members Participating: 9,234
  • Companies Participating: 2,000+
  • Countries Participating: 111
  • Followers for #bestofpages Hashtag on LinkedIn: 1,398
