Sailfish X now available for Sony Xperia 10 and 10 Plus

21 Nov 2019, 23:31 by Marin

Jolla announced that SailfishOS for Xperia devices or Sailfish X is now available for Xperia 10 and 10 Plus. If you own a Sony Xperia 10 or 10 Plus, you can download SailfishOS for free to get an experience of using the gesture-based OS. If you pay €49.90 or 29.90 if you are an existing Sailfish user, you’ll be able to download and install Android apps on it.
