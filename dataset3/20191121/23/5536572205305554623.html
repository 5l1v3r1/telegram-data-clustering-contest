Tim Tebow Mourns As He Shares Tearful Goodbye To His Beloved Dog (VIDEO)

21 Nov 2019, 23:17 by Darrelle Lincoln

This is a pain that nobody wants to experience.

On Thursday, Tim Tebow shared a heartbreaking video that accompanied several pictures that showed him grieving his beloved dog, Bronco, who died after spending nine loving years with the athlete.

He shared a video crying and cradling Bronco in his final moments.

“One of the toughest goodbyes,” he wrote. “Wanted to make a special tribute to the sweetest boy ever – thank you for all the joy you brought and all the memories.”

Broncos has been around for the former Florida quarterback and SEC Network analyst when he spent three years in the NFL to his career change to playing in the Mets’ minor-league system for the past three years.
