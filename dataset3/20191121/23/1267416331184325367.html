Fido Xtra perk enters subscribers to win one of 30 Pixel 3a phones

If you're a Fido subscriber this contest is definitely worth entering

21 Nov 2019, 23:03 by Brad Bennett

If you’re a Fido subscriber, then you’ll be able to enter a contest to win one of 30 Pixel 3a phones.

You can join the contest by downloading the Fido app and navigating to the ‘Xtra’ section. From there, you can enter the contest once every day until November 27th. The terms mention that the winners will be selected on December 5th.

These Fido Xtra perks are only available to post-paid customers in good standing, according to Fido.

MobileSyrup reviewed the Pixel 3a when it launched in early May of this year. The Pixel 3A is a great device, and it’s the mid-range smartphone I recommended most to my friends.
