Amber Liu drops 'Curiosity' MV in English & Mandarin

21 Nov 2019, 03:21 by greenbaypackers

f(x)'s Amber has dropped the full MV for "Curiosity", from her upcoming album 'X'!

“Curiosity” will be featured on Amber's 'X' album alongside “Other People”, “Hands Behind My Back”, “Numb”, “Ready For The Ride”, and “Stay Calm”. The star has released the MV in "Curiosity" in 3 versions including English, traditional Mandarin, and simplified Mandarin, so check out all of the versions above and below!
