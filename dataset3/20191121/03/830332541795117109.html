Silver Alert issued for man who went missing in New Berlin

21 Nov 2019, 03:06 by Brandon Arbuckle

NEW BERLIN, Wis. - A Silver Alert has been issued for a man who went missing in New Berlin on Wednesday afternoon.

According to an alert from the Wisconsin Crime Alert Network, Stefan Diettrich, 80, was last seen at the Princeton Club at 14999 W. Beloit Road in New Berlin at 2 p.m.

Diettrich told a clerk there that he needed help and "wasn't feeling right in the head." He refused medical attention and was directed to an emergency room but instead drove away from the facility. Officials checked local hospitals but did not find him. 

Diettrich has been described as 5 feet, 9 inches tall, weighs 175 pounds and has blue eyes and short white hair. He is driving a silver 2019 Subaru Outback with the Wisconsin license plate 775-GTZ. 

Anyone with information about Diettrich's whereabouts is asked to call the city of South Milwaukee at 414-768-8060.
