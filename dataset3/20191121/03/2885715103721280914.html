RRR: SS Rajamouli casts international actors in his next movie

Director SS Rajamouli on Wednesday announced new additions to the cast of his upcoming film RRR, which stars Ram Charan and Jr NTR in the lead roles.

21 Nov 2019, 03:49 by Entertainment Desk

Director SS Rajamouli on Wednesday announced that he has handpicked British stage actor Olivia Morris to play one of the female leads in his next directorial RRR.

“Welcome aboard #OliviaMorris @OliviaMorris891! We are happy to have you play the female lead #JENNIFER. Looking forward for the shoot (sic),” read an announcement on the film’s official Twitter handle.

Olivia Morris is a graduate of Royal Welsh College of Music & Drama. According to IMDb, she has not worked in a film so far. However, she has appeared in a single episode of a mini-series called 7 Trails in 7 Days. Hence, RRR will mark her big screen debut.

After months of search, Rajamouli handpicked Olivia to replace British actor Daisy Edgar-Jones, who opted out of the project due to “unavoidable circumstances.” She was hired to share screen space with Jr NTR in the period drama.

Now, Olivia will play Jennifer, the romantic interest of Tarak’s Komaram Bheem.

The producers also introduced popular Hollywood actor Ray Stevenson. Known for his roles in television series like Rome and Dexter, Stevenson will be seen as the main antagonist, Scott, in the film. Seasoned Irish actor Alison Doody will also play a key role in the film.

“Welcome to Indian cinema, #AlisonDoody! Had a wonderful time shooting for your first schedule… We are glad to have you play lead antagonist #LADYSCOTT in #RRRMovie! #RRR (sic)” read a tweet on RRR’s handle.
