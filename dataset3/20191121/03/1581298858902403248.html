Activ Esperance score win

21 Nov 2019, 03:28 by Jake Dietsch

Activ Esperance has been awarded for an inclusive cooking competition, featuring customers and staff cooking for 'judges' from the Esperance community.

Related story: Women's team takes out kitchen competition

The team won Activ's Galaxy Award in Perth on Saturday, for being the team that best demonstrated Activ's values.

These values include leading with courage, learning and growing and sharing a passion for people.

Activ chief executive officer Danielle Newport said the Esperance 'kitchen rules-style' initiative originated from one customer expressing an interest in learning how to cook.

Ms Newport said the cooking competition was then created between a male customer team, a female customer team and a staff team.

The teams competed against each other at a series of 'instant restaurants' earlier in 2019, where community members judged the meals.

The judges included members of The Express, the council, a local chef and local businesses.

The kitchen contest was a great opportunity for customers to enhance their daily life skills in a way that was both fun and inclusive.
