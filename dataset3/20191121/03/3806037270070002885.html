1017 ALYX 9SM's Leather Low Top Sneakers Features an Aggressive Vibram Sole

Adding to its footwear options.

21 Nov 2019, 03:52 by Nicolaus Li

Aside from being known for its fashionably technical garments, 1017 ALYX 9SM has also made a name for itself with its challenging footwear options.

Moving past trail styles and contemporary classic silhouettes, Matthew M. Williams‘ label has now crafted a sporty Leather Low Top Sneaker with an aggressive Vibram sole. Centered around a sleek design, the upper of the shoe is crafted of minimal panels of shiny black premium leather. Decoration comes in the form of mesh hits, dynamic layering and debossed side logos. Rounding up the design of the shoe is a lace-up front, chunky Vibram sole unit with a ridged torsion plate done in silver.

Priced at $498 USD, the 1017 ALYX 9SM Leather Low Top Sneakers are available now at Browns.
