‘Goldilocks’ alert: Man breaks into home and sleeps in child’s bed

21 Nov 2019, 03:03 by Kenneth Garger

The third bed was just right.

A man was arrested after he broke into a home in Tulsa, Oklahoma and fell asleep in a child’s bed, a report said.

A woman inside the home first notified police of a possible burglary about 3 a.m. Tuesday after she heard somebody break in through the back patio door, according to FOX 23.

When cops showed up, they discovered Charles Shunkamolah clad in his underwear and sound asleep in one of the children’s beds, the report said, citing police.

Shunkamolah told cops he was drunk and thought he was at a family members’ home.

He was charged with breaking and entering and public intoxication.

A neighbor told the cable network that the incident left her on edge.

“Never feel too safe. Never feel too comfortable,” she said.
