Trump to host vaping meeting on Friday, White House says

21 Nov 2019, 03:45

U.S. President Donald Trump will host a meeting on vaping at the White House on Friday with a range of groups, including industry and public health representatives, a White House spokesman said on Wednesday.

WASHINGTON: U.S. President Donald Trump will host a meeting on vaping at the White House on Friday with a range of groups, including industry and public health representatives, a White House spokesman said on Wednesday.

"The policy making process is not stalled," White House spokesman Judd Deere said in a statement.
