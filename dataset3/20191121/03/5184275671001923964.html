Joaquin Phoenix Starrer Joker Sequel Is HAPPENING! Deets Inside

Since the beginning, Joaquin Phoenix and Todd Phillips had maintained that there won't be any sequel to Joker.

21 Nov 2019, 03:20 by Koimoi.com Team

Joaquin Phoenix starrer Joker is one of the best films of 2019. Directed by Todd Phillips, the film gave a new dimension and life to the DC world. The film ended on an impactful note and left the audience thinking about it after they walked out of the cinema halls.

From the time Joker released, fans are hoping that there will be a sequel to this Joaquin starrer. However, since the beginning, the actor-director clarified that there won’t be any sequel. But the latest report shows a new picture and is bound to make all the fans happy.

The Hollywood Reporter mentioned that on October 7, 2019, Todd Phillips had a meet with Warner Bros. Pictures Group chairman Toby Emmerich after the film made a collection of $96.2 on its first weekend. THR report stated that Todd asked Toby for the rights to develop a portfolio of DC Characters’ origin stories via his films. However, Emmerich was quite hesitant to give the rights as DC is very protective of their characters.

But Todd did manage to get the rights of making another DC story, reveals the sources. As Phoenix’s Joker crossed $1 billion mark worldwide, the makers are now gearing up for the sequel which will be helmed by Phillips. But the studio and director’s team have refused to comment on anything regarding the sequel.

If the sequel is indeed happening, it’s great news for all the Joker, Joaquin and DC fans. After all, who doesn’t want to watch another phenomenal craft on the big screen?
