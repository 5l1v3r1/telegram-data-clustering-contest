Fall 2020 Brides Have Some Stunning Wedding Dress Trends to Choose From

21 Nov 2019, 03:03 by Sarah Wasilak

As a fashion editor going into Bridal Week each season, I assume it'll be almost impossible for designers to reinvent the wedding dress to impress the most demanding bride, and someone who looks for something a little different. But I am always proven wrong. While the Spring 2020 bride saw an abundance of bows and bohemian touches, Fall takes us in a modern direction with fabrics styled, layered, and embroidered like never before. We see unexpected details like pleating at Oscar de la Renta and Lela Rose, and we're particularly excited about the boudoir look, which takes us back to glamorous '50s nightgowns in such a subtle, delightful way. For the more casual bride, there are plenty of minidress options here too, which allow for a major shoe moment. Jump ahead to the trend you can't wait to explore, or scroll through these categories one by one.

Bridal Trend Fall 2020: Sheer Layers

For the past few seasons, we've been seeing brides take a walk on the wild side, opting for sheer-illusion sleeves and skirts that follow suit. These skin-baring looks certainly suggest confidence, but this season's bride carries more layers when it comes to sheer fabrics like gauze and chiffon. While you'll still see plenty of sheer details on the bodice, the skirts are full of volume.

Bridal Trend Fall 2020: Boudoir Bride

Ah, the boudoir bride. This is one of our favorite trends of the season because it harks back to the '50s, when nightgowns could be as dreamy as wedding dresses. This season, the two styles go hand in hand, although these "pajama gowns" have intricate embroideries, stunning bows, and unexpected touches of lace that make them whimsical enough for a walk down the aisle. If you prefer loose, floaty, lightweight dresses, you'll find yourself an instant fan of this category.

Bridal Trend Fall 2020: Minimalist Mermaid

Perhaps emphasized most strongly in Vera Wang's Fall collection, the mermaid silhouette is definitely making the rounds. But for 2020, the mermaid gown is modern, with a small cutout here and a plunge there. The fabric is sleek and simple with clean lines, so as not to take away from your gorgeous curves.

Bridal Trend Fall 2020: Pleated Dresses

For Fall 2020, you can find subtle pleating on everything from silk to tulle, the resulting look being a gown or minidress (see Oscar de la Renta and Lela Rose) that's a bit more playful. Perhaps consider trying out the trend for your rehearsal dinner, if you're going for something more majestic for your walk down the aisle.

Bridal Trend Fall 2020: Sophisticated, Off-the-Shoulder Dress

While we're always blown away by the romantic look of an off-the-shoulder wedding dress, Fall brides who choose this silhouette must be confident and careful. We're seeing a lot of sleeves that wrap around the arm delicately rather than loosely. There's less draping involved and a tighter look to this new style.
