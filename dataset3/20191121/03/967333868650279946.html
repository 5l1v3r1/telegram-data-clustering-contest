‘Laughed So Hard I Got My Period’: Netizens Slash ACLU Tweet on ‘Men Who Get Pregnant’

On Tuesday, 19 November, the world celebrated International Men’s Day and the American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU), a nonprofit advocacy group, congratulated men in an unexpected way.

21 Nov 2019, 03:31

The ACLU has triggered argument online after its Tuesday tweets hashtagged “International Men’s Day” claiming that transgender and non-binary people should both be perceived as men.

ACLU's tweet claimed that there is more than “one way to be a man” and argued that “men who get their periods” along with those “who get pregnant and give birth” should be classified as men.
