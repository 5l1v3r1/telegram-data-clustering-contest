50 Cent Taunts Young Buck Again With Transgender Jokes

21 Nov 2019, 10:55 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Curtis Jackson is back on IG.

(AllHipHop News) Earlier this year, former G-Unit comrades 50 Cent and Young Buck were engaged in a feud on the internet. Part of 50’s Instagram attack involved claiming that Buck was attracted to transgender women.

Fiddy is back at it again. Yesterday, the Power executive producer took another shot at his ex-protégé on IG. 50 Cent posted a meme of two people embracing each other with the header “He Doesn’t Know Yet.” 50’s separate caption suggested the picture was of a man with a trans woman. It read, “👀buck type sh*t, nah he know. LOL😆#lecheminduroi #bransoncogna.”

In May, DJ Mister Cee, an NYC radio personality who was arrested several times for soliciting trans prostitution, offered advice to Young Buck about dating transgender women. He stated, “What I want to tell you, Young Buck, is whether your situation is true or not, that's not what I'm here for. I'm here to kind of give you a little bit of guidance to what I think you need to do."

50 Cent reacted to Mister Cee’s message. The 44-year-old entertainer posted on Instagram, "👀yo the funny sh*t is @djmistercee help me, after I got hit he booked the first show we did at club speed. 🤐I got nothing bad to say."
