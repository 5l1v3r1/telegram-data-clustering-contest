Rafael van der Vaart posts on Twitter as Tottenham appoint Jose Mourinho

21 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Will Butcher

Rafael van der Vaart has posted on Twitter after Tottenham Hotspur appointed the Dutchman’s former manager Jose Mourinho as Mauricio Pochettino’s replacement, agreeing a deal until the summer of 2023.

Pochettino was relieved of his duties on Tuesday night after a disappointing start to the 2019-20 campaign. Spurs are already out of the Carabao Cup and have picked up just three keague wins from 12 league games this season.

Just hours later, Spurs confirmed the appointment of former Manchester United and Chelsea boss Mourinho. The Portuguese gaffer took charge of his first training session later that day – and has his first press conference on Thursday at 2pm.

Former Spurs midfielder van der Vaart worked under Mourinho during the Dutchman’s time at Real Madrid, and while he is saddened at the departure of Pochettino – he has backed the appointment of Mourinho.
