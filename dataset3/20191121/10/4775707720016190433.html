While we expect the Black Friday 2019 season to really kick off from tomorrow until the official date (the 29th), plenty of retailers are trying to get ahead of the crowd. Xbox have just released their big Xbox Store Black Friday Sale, and though Amazon UK has yet to officially open for Black Friday business, this "Countdown to Black Friday" offering has appeared.

It looks like EA have partnered with Amazon UK to offer discounted digital codes from across their recent back catalogue. In the table below, we haven't included every alleged deal – we found some of them are no cheaper than they have been recently, and a long way off the cheapest they have been overall. Instead we've whittled it down to just those games that are discounted equal to or lower than their lowest price on Amazon UK.

Obviously it's worth bearing in mind that some of these offers may be bested by upcoming discounts in other stores, and there's also the fact that these are digital codes – no physical edition to collect or trade in when you are done. Still, there are some criminally cheap offerings in the mix, including Titanfall 2 for a preposterous £3.75. If you have been enjoying Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order, you should really play Titanfall 2's campaign – we'd bet a lot of money that its cinematic and imaginative storytelling were critical in the decision to let Respawn play with the Star Wars license.

If you happen to be looking for a new 4K TV to go along with a recent Xbox One X purchase, another early Amazon UK Black Friday deal has a decent LG UHD 4K HDR Smart LED TV range at its lowest recorded prices for the store – worth a look, but it might be worth holding out for Black Friday to properly begin too.
