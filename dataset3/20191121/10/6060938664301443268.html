Akshay Kumar's latest release 'Housefull 4' beats 'Mission Mangal' and '2.0' to create a new record at the box office

21 Nov 2019, 10:52 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Akshay Kumar's fourth instalment of 'Housefull 4' has been on a juggernaut run at the box office and even the audience has been impressed by the stellar performances. The film which had been making headlines since its inception is still managing to grab all the attention in its fourth week post its release.

According to the latest report on Boxofficeindia.com, the Fahad Samji directorial which has raked in Rs 290 crore has become the fifth highest grosser worldwide of 2019, right behind Salman Khan's 'Bharat' which collected Rs 304 crore. Shahid Kapoor's 'Kabir Singh' and Vicky Kaushal's 'Uri: The Surgical Strike' too found a place in the list with Rs 370 crore and Rs 338 crore respectively.

With Hrithik Roshan and Tiger Shroff starrer 'War' topping the list of highest-grossing film worldwide of 2019 with Rs 445 crore, 'Housefull 4' has become Akshay's highest-grossing film of the year, surpassing the collections of 'Mission Mangal', 'Kesari' and previous year's '2.0'.

The film which also features Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol and Pooja Hegde revolved around the concept of reincarnation left the audience in splits.
