BREAKING Alex Salmond in court facing 14 charges including attempted rape

The former Scotland first minister led the Scottish independence campaign, stepping down after defeat in the September 2014 vote

21 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Tom Davidson, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/tom-davidson/

Alex Salmond has appeared in court charged with carrying out a series of sexual offences against 10 women while serving as Scotland's first minister.

Mr Salmond faced a total of 14 charges at the High Court in Edinburgh.

He has previously denied all the charges.

The former SNP leader led the Scottish independence campaign, stepping down after defeat in the September 2014 vote.
