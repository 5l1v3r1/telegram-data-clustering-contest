Man dies in house fire

21 Nov 2019, 10:38

A Langley house fire claimed a man's life early Thursday.

Firefighters responded about 2:15 a.m. to the blaze on 74B Avenue near 206a Street.

Flames could be seen shooting out of the roof, CTV News reports.

"When we arrived we found this single-family dwelling with fire heavily involved in the roof and the upper floor," deputy fire chief Monty Armstrong told CTV. 

Five people were living in the house, and at least two others were taken to hospital.

There is no word yet on the fire's cause.
