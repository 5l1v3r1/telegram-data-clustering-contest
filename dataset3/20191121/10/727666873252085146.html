Lizzo, Lil Nas X, H.E.R., Meek Mill & More React To Earning Grammy Nominations

21 Nov 2019, 10:10 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Find out what celebs have to say about getting recognized by The Recording Academy.

(AllHipHop News) The nominations for the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards were revealed yesterday morning. Lizzo (8) and Lil Nas X (6) were among this year’s top nominees. 

Lizzo is up for Album Of The Year (Cuz I Love You), Record Of The Year ("Truth Hurts”), Song Of The Year ("Truth Hurts”), Best New Artist, and four other honors. The singer/rapper tweeted, “THIS HAS BEEN AN INCREDIBLE YEAR FOR MUSIC AND IM JUST SO THANKFUL TO EVEN BE PART OF IT.”

Lil Nas X earned nods for Album Of The Year (7), Record Of The Year ("Old Town Road”), Best Pop Duo/Group Performance ("Old Town Road”), and Best New Artist. The 20-year-old Georgia native told his Twitter followers, “NO F*CKING WAY.”

Other 2020 nominees also reacted on social media. H.E.R, Meek Mill, Tyler, The Creator, YBN Cordae, DJ Khaled, Rick Ross, Offset, Gucci Mane, Hit-Boy, Ella Mai, Anderson .Paak, Roddy Ricch, Mustard, J.I.D, EarthGang, BJ The Chicago Kid, and Lucky Daye posted about their respective recognitions from The Recording Academy.

The 62nd Annual Grammy Awards are scheduled to air January 26, 2020 on CBS from the Staples Center in Los Angeles. To see the full list of nominations visit grammy.com.
