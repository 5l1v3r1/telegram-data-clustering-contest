Comedian Chris D'Elia Spills The Deets Of Meeting Eminem In His Detroit Studio

21 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Kyle Eustice

Detroit, MI – Comedian Chris D’Elia could’ve never predicted what would unfold after a video of his Eminem impression went viral. D’Elia released the clip in late January and by February 5, Slim Shady was tweeting about it.
