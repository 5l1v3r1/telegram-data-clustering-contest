Is a new Crash Bandicoot game coming to PS5?

21 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Jim Hargreaves

We’re roughly a year away from the launch of Sony’s next big home console but right now we’re in the dark as to what games will actually be making an appearance on PS5. If a series of clues mined from a new PlayStation marketing campaign turn out to be legit, we may see platforming hero Crash Bandicoot among the vanguard of next generation games arriving in 2020.
