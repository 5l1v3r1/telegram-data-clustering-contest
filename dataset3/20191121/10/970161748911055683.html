Spoilers: Liam viciously attacks Jacob over sex accusation in Emmerdale

21 Nov 2019, 10:40

Jacob Gallagher (Joe-Warren Plant) is about to get a serious wake up call over his recent appalling behaviour in Emmerdale when Liam Cavanagh (Jonny McPherson) discovers that he slept with Leanna (Mimi Slinger) before walking out on her.

After Jacob took Leanna’s virginity while still having his mind messed up by the release of Maya Stepney (Louisa Clein), she was confused and hurt when he was cold and distant with her before leaving straight after the act.

Later on, she was further taken aback when he sent a blunt text asking if she is on the pill. Tonight, after Leanna implies that Jacob put pressure on her to have sex, Liam takes this as an allegation amounting to sexual assault and he is on the warpath.

He bursts into the shop and attacks Jacob, hitting him and ranting at him for his deplorable behaviour. As David Metcalfe (Matthew Wolfenden) can’t believe his ears over what has happened, the situation spirals out of control and Liam ends up being arrested.
