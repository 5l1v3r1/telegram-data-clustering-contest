India Ratings: Star Plus returns to No.1 in Urban region

21 Nov 2019, 10:47 by Raj Baddhan

After a couple of weeks facing competition from Sony SAB TV, Star Plus returned to the No.1 spot in the Urban region, according to BARC data for Week 46 of 2019.

Urban region
1 Star Plus – 448780 million Impressions
2 Sony SAB TV – 439945 million Impressions
3 Colors – 383188 million Impressions
4 Sony TV – 370290 million Impressions
5 ZEE TV – 335996 million Impressions

The top show performers in the Urban region were ‘Kundali Bhagya’ on ZEE TV with 7271 million Impressions, followed by ‘Kumkum Bhagya’ on ZEE TV with 6835 million Impressions and ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ on Star Plus with 6573 million Impressions.

Rural region
1 Dangal – 872777 million Impressions
2 Big Magic – 388500 million Impressions
3 ZEE TV – 263968 million Impressions
4 Sony SAB TV – 243874 million Impressions
5 Star Plus – 232716 million Impressions

The top show performers in the Rural region were ‘Bandini’ on Dangal with 12283 million Impressions, followed by ‘Rehna Hai Teri Palkon Ki Chhaon Mein’ on Dangal with 11821 million Impressions and ‘Dwarkadheesh Bhagwan Sri Krishna’ on Dangal with 11477 million Impressions.

Urban & Rural combined
1 Dangal – 1114546 million Impressions
2 Sony SAB TV – 683819 million Impressions
3 Star Plus – 681496 million Impressions
4 ZEE TV – 599964 million Impressions
5 Colors – 552530 million Impressions

The top show performers in the combined data were ‘Bandini’ on Dangal with 15311 million Impressions, followed by ‘Rehna Hai Teri Palkon Ki Chhaon Mein’ on Dangal with 14541 million Impressions and ‘Dwarkadheesh Bhagwan Sri Krishna’ on Dangal with 13880 million Impressions.
