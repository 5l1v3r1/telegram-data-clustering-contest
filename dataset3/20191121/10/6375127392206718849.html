6 LTO offices collect billion-peso fees

21 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Jun I. Legaspi

THE Department of Transportation–Land Transportation Office (DOTr-LTO) yesterday announced the top six regional offices which have exceeded the billion (B)-peso mark in revenue collection as of October 31, 2019.

Topping the list is National Capital Region (NCR)-East with P3.06B. It is followed by National Capital Region-West with P2.7B; Regional Office 3 with P2.68 billion; Regional Office 4-A, P2.65B; Regional Office 7, P1.6B; and Regional Office 6 with P1.2B.

“We are glad to announce that several LTO regional offices have already pegged their collection beyond the billion-peso mark this October,” LTO Assistant Secretary Edgar C. Galvante said.

The LTO Chief noted that the regional offices have been instituting reforms in their respective offices which resulted in more efficient revenue collection.

“The LTO is serious in its effort to ensure accurate reporting and remittance of revenue collections,” Galvante said.

“We will continue the adoption of technology-driven measures to make the flow of LTO transactions more systematic, user-friendly, convenient, accessible and effective, especially in terms of revenue collection,” Galvante assured.
