Save £200 on an iPhone 11 Pro Max - an ideal mobile for Apple Arcade

Plus, an iPhone XR for only £329!

21 Nov 2019, 10:53 by Colm Ahern

The iPhone 11 Pro Max - the most powerful handset in Apple's current generation - has had its price slashed by online retailer Very, even though Black Friday is still over a week away. If you order the phone using their six-month Buy Now Pay Later credit program, and then enter the code 'PEHE3' at checkout, you'll get £200 off the regular price of £1299.

There's no hidden charges or anything like that. As long as you have your new toy paid for in full within the time, then you won't be charged interest on your purchase.

So, if you're enthused by the idea of 6.5-inch OLED display, a triple-camera system that resembles Sam Fisher's goggles, and 256GB of storage, then you best get hitting that Add To Basket button as soon as possible, because the offer ends on the 28th of November.

Unveiled alongside the Pro models back in September, the vanilla iPhone 11 is a cracking phone in its own right and is also available from Very. Again, if you choose to purchase through the Buy Now Pay Later program, you'll save £120 off the regular asking price.

If you want to experience all of the great Apple Arcade games in the best possible way, then a new phone is very tempting. Bleak Sword, What the Golf, Skate City: Apple's subscription service has been throwing out gem after gem, and they would look an absolute treat on that screen. Think of that four-figure price tag as an investment in something more than a WhatsApp machine and it might make more sense.
