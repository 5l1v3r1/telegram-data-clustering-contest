EuroLeague All-Decade Nominee: Dimitris Diamantidis

21 Nov 2019, 10:13

The emblematic figure of Panathinaikos’ modern history couldn’t be missing from the All-Decade Nominees.

By Eurohoops team/ info@eurohoops.net

Dimitris Diamantidis set an example for the future generations with his leadership and basketball IQ that paced Panathinaikos to many great achievements through the 2010-2020 decade. Arguably the biggest star of the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague at the start of the 2010s, Diamantidis soon led Panathinaikos Athens to its sixth continental title in unprecedented fashion.

OUTSTANDING ACHIEVEMENTS

  • EuroLeague Champion 2007, 2009, 2011
  • Final Four MVP 2007, 2011
  • EuroLeague MVP 2010-11
  • All-EuroLeague First Team 2006-07, 2010-11, 2011-12, 2012-13
  • Best Defender 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2011
  • EuroLeague February MVP 2010-11
  • EuroLeague March/April MVP 2011-12
  • Championship Game, Single Game Record Assist (9)
  • Final Four All-Time Leader Average Assists (4.6), Steals (1.8)
  • EuroLeague All-Time Leader Steals (434)
  • Led the EuroLeague in assists 2010-11 (6.23), 2013-14 (6.21)
  • Led the EuroLeague in steals 2011-12 (1.48)
