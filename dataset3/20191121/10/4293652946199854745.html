Watch Live: Maple Leafs’ Shanahan, Dubas talk Babcock dismissal

21 Nov 2019, 10:42 by Sportsnet Staff

The post-Mike Babcock era officially begins for the Toronto Maple Leafs on Thursday night when the team takes on the Arizona Coyotes with new head coach Sheldon Keefe behind the bench.

Before the Leafs play their first game under Keefe, however, president Brendan Shanahan and general manager Kyle Dubas will address the media to discuss the dismissal of Babcock as well as Keefe’s promotion.
