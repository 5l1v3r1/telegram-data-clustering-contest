Two captains arrested for illegal fishing

21 Nov 2019, 10:05

Following three arrests over a period of two months, the Namibian Police yet again arrested two more ship captains who caught fish illegally in Namibian waters.
Captains Arngrimur Brynjolfsonn and Iurri Festison were charged with fishing illegally and made their first court appearance in the Walvis Bay Magistrate's Court on Wednesday.
