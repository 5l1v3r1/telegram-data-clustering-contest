Andaman: Indian Navy rescues woman in emergency labour

21 Nov 2019, 10:39 by PTI

In a swift medical relief operation, the Indian Navy on Thursday rescued a pregnant lady in emergency labour from a remote village in Andaman and Nicobar Islands, officials said.

A fast interceptor craft (FIC) from naval ship Kardip in Kamorta island rescued the woman from the village after getting information about the need for medical assistance.

The woman delivered a baby on the FIC and later both were admitted to a community health centre on arrival in Kamorta jetty, officials said.Â
