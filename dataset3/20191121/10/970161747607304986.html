£15,000 raised after child asked Santa for food and home this Christmas

21 Nov 2019, 10:15

Kindhearted people have raised over £15,000 after a girl wrote to Santa asking ‘Can you help? Can we have a home for Christmas?’

The child, seven, posted the letter in a special post box to Father Christmas at her community centre.

It said: ‘Mum wants us all to be together. Can you give us some food and can I have a nice doll for Christmas? Thank you.’

The heartbreaking note was left at the L6 Community centre in Everton, Liverpool.

It went viral after Labour councillor Gerard Woodhouse shared it on Twitter, saying: ‘Last week @L6Centre we started getting down Xmas decorations.

‘We placed The Santa post box in the cafe until [the] grotto is set up.
