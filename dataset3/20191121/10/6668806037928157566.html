Abhishek, Aishwarya Rai and Aaradhya Bachchan head out for a family dinner

21 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Vedanshi Pathak

Abhishek Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Aaradhya Bachchan’s trio is spotted several times heading out for dinners, parties as well as events. The adorable family always makes time for each other and whenever they step out, the paparazzi loves to go click click. Last night, Abhishek, Aishwarya, Aaradhya and Aishwarya’s mom Vrinda Rai were clicked heading out of a popular restaurant in Mumbai as they met up to celebrate Aishwarya’s dad late Krishnaraj Rai’s birthday. Aishwarya was all smiles as she stepped out post dinner with her daughter Aaradhya. 

Abhishek was papped with the fam too as he exited from the restaurant and zoomed off with them after saying bye to other guests who had come for the family dinner. The Bachchans surely know how to come together and celebrate, don’t they?
