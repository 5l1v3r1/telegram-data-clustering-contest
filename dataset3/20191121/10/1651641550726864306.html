Samsung Galaxy S11 5G get certified in China, support 25W Fast Charging

21 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Rahul

The Galaxy S11 is expected to Samsung’s first smartphone release in 2020. The S11 is rumored to be the best camera phone that Samsung has ever produced. Not only the camera but also other specs such as processor, battery, display are expected to be top-notch.

The Galaxy S11 is also expected to be one of the best smartphones when it comes to taking the battery from 0 to 100 percent, because according to the famous leaker Ice Universe, the S11 5G has recently been certified with EP- TA800 charger that has support for 25W fast charging.
