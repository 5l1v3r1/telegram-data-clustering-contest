N-Power October Stipend: Payment To Be Made Next Month?

21 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Ridwan Yusuf

It’s nine days to the end of November and beneficiaries of the Federal Government N-Power scheme are still awaiting payment of their October stipend.

Concise News understands that payment (N30, 000 monthly stipend) is yet to made for around 500,000 volunteers throughout the federation. This we can affirm as at the time of publishing this report.

Furthermore, this online news medium observed that beneficiaries are frustrated with the current situation.
