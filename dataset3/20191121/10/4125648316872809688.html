Turkish drones kill 5 Syrians in northern Al-Raqqa

21 Nov 2019, 10:08 by News Desk

Five people, including two children, were killed when Turkish drones struck the outskirts of Tell Abyad, a town in the north of Syria’s Raqqa governorate, on Wednesday, the SANA news agency reported.

According to the agency, all the victims were in a car that was struck by a projectile.

Information of casualties and destruction are also being received from other regions targeted by the air raid.
