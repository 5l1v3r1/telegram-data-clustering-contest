ESPN’s Adam Schefter Thinks NFL Team Should Move To Mexico City

21 Nov 2019, 14:31 by Andrew Holleran

There’s been a lot of talk about the Los Angeles Chargers and where they belong since the franchise decided to move from San Diego. ESPN’s Adam Schefter gave his suggestion this morning.

The ESPN NFL insider believes the Chargers should move to Mexico City. Los Angeles’ second NFL team just played the Kansas City Chiefs in Mexico on Monday night.

“The Chargers had more fans, more support in Mexico City by a landslide than they do in Los Angeles,” he said on ESPN’s Get Up! this morning.

“The Chargers should move to Mexico City where their fan base and their following is much more passionate than it is in Los Angeles.”
