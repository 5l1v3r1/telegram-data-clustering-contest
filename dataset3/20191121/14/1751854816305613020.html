Amazon just announced that they’ll be holding the longest Black Friday sale that will start today and will end next Friday and what better time to grab a smartwatch on the cheap. Whether you’re looking for a smartwatch for fitness tracking, running or even just taking calls and receiving notifications, there’s plenty of options we’d comfortably recommend but 2 of them certainly stand out. We are talking about KOSPET Classic and KOSPET Smart Watch. Let’s take a look at what they have to offer.

KOSPET Classic Smartwatch

KOSPET Classic is -as its name suggests- a round smartwatch that looks like a classic watch but offers many smart functions. It comes with genuine leather straps paired with a stainless steel case and a brilliant 1.39: AMOLED display with a resolution of 400*400.

1 of 3

The Classic offers call management as you can use it to make and answer calls coming to your smartphone. Also, you can sync SMS, SNS messages, calls, emails, etc. In addition, there is a built-in GPS tracker to use in conjunction with Google Maps while you can control the Google Assistant via the watch. Lastly, it is capable of recording steps, calories, and distance for you that want a sports assistant.

1 of 2

Of course, it has a heart rate sensor built-in to measure your heart beats and it runs on Android 5.1 OS, so it is like having a smartwatch on your wrist. Lastly, it has a SIM card slot so that you can use it as an independent device without the need to carry your smartphone everywhere. During the BF promotion, it will go down to just $111.99, that’s $30 off its official price. You can grab yours here and apply the coupon found under the description.

KOSPET Smart Watch

KOSPET Smart Watch is another full Android 5.1 smartwatch that you can use without the need of a smartphone. It comes with 3G network support and you can make calls using the cellular network. Of course, Google’s Play Store is preinstalled and you can download whichever app you like.

1 of 5

As you can see, the design here is has a sporty design and it is IP68 certified for use in water. Addressed mostly to active users, it can remind you to drink water, take medicine, do eye exercises, or be more active. Of course, there is step, calories, heart rate, and distance calculator. The Smart Watch is available at just $87.20 during the sale and you can find it here. Don’t forget to apply the $15 off coupon found on the product page!

Whether you are shopping ahead of Christmas for gifts or just want a smartwatch for yourself, these two are ideal. This Black Friday proves to be a great time to pick up a KOSPET on the cheap while it also lets you avoid the last-minute December rush.
