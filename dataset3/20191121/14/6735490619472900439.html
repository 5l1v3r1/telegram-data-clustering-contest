Michelle Obama Enlists Ellen DeGeneres For A ‘Duet’ Helping To Promote ‘Becoming: Journal’

21 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Aynslee Darmon

Michelle Obama is using her pal Ellen DeGeneres for a little Becoming: A Guided Journal for Discovering Your Voice promotion.

The former U.S. First Lady shared a sweet Twitter video of a little duet with the talk show host, singing about all the great things the new book has to offer.

The new book is based on Obama’s bestselling memoir, which features an introduction by Obama herself before the “thought-provoking questions” that help the reader discover, or rediscover, their own personal story.

“Michelle Obama has a new book,” DeGeneres sings while playing the piano very loudly.

“It’s actually a journal,” Obama cuts in. “To help you build your own story.”
