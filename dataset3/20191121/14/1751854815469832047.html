Xiaomi and Huami‘s Amazfit range of smart bands and fitness trackers pair with smartphones via the Mi Fit app. Amazfit smartwatches are paired with smartphones using their own dedicated Amazfit Watch app. The Mi Fit app also works with a bunch of other smart fitness products made by Xiaomi and its partner companies such as the Mi Smart Scales and smart shoes made by 90, Li-Ning, Kangnai, and Sprandi. The app has got a new update in China, bringing several new features and fixes.

The new version comes with the firmware version number V4.0.12 and one of the major fixes is the issue of iOS 13 OS not receiving notifications. The instruction is that after upgrading to iOS 13 and you don’t receive call notification and other notifications, go to System Settings – Bluetooth – click “(i)” on the right side of the currently bound device to open the sharing system notification and turn it on.

The firmware upgrade which is for the Chinese version also brings support for Shijiazhuang Card, Xiamen e-Tong Card for the Mi Band 3 NFC version and Mi Band 4 NFC version which also gets support for Hainan card, Shijiazhuang card, Xiamen e-card. The Amazfit Cor 2 also gets support for the three cards with the update. Further, the app is now adapted for Android 10 and also gets some bug fixes.
