Warhammer: Vermintide 2 is free to play on Steam this weekend and 75% off

21 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Stephany Nunneley

If you’ve been holding out on Warhammer: Vermintide 2. now’s your chance to try it for free.

From November 21-24, Warhammer: Vermintide 2 will be free to play on Steam.

Not only is the game free to play this weekend, but all DLC is too provided you’re invited by another player who owns the game and DLC. Be sure to hit up any friends who own it.

The most recent DLC, Winds of Magic, was released on August 13. It introduced a new enemy, the Beastmen, as well as a new game mode, weapons, and leaderboards. It also features a new city, raised level cap, and more.
