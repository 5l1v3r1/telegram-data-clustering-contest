Desert One participates in the Saudi exhibition

21 Nov 2019, 14:55

RIYADH — Azom Tech, the specialized company in information technology, invited recently esteemed members of the press to reveal the company's activities directed towards tech entrepreneurs and to unveil its mobile smartphone, Desert One, during its participation in the Saudi International Exhibition for Communications and Mobile Technology.

On the occasion, Mohammed Almonajim, managing director at Azom Tech, stressed the importance of this participation at the largest technology expo in the kingdom especially in light of the great technological advancement we currently are witnessing across the globe and with the great competition and rivalry between telecommunications, mobile phones, and information technology companies.

Almonajim also highlighted that this participation indicates that Azom Tech is keeping up with the technological transformation taking place and the Kingdom's Vision 2030.

Almonajim elaborated also that the advancement in technology and software development is reducing the challenges facing the manufacturers of smart devices, pointing out that leading and pioneering countries are working on the inclusion and adoption of advanced smart solutions.

Almonajim further stated that Desert One smartphone satisfies consumer needs in Saudi Arabia. It boasts easy browsing and high-quality specifications at a very competitive price. — SG
