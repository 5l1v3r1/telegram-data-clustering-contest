Family Fun Day scheduled in December in Punta Gorda

21 Nov 2019, 14:50 by WFTX Digital Team

PUNTA GORDA, Fla. -- The city of Punta Gorda is inviting the public to attend their Punta Gorda Family Fun Day on December 7th.

It is a free event so come with your family and enjoy the day.

There will be members of the Punta Gorda Police Department and Punta Gorda Fire Department and their equipment.

There will also be a Kids Workshop by Home Depot, face painting, the Punta Gorda Police Gaming Trailer, bounce house, dunk-a-cop, K-9 demos, and more.

It will be held at the Punta Gorda Safety Building, 1410 Tamiami Trail in Punta Gorda, from 10:00 AM until 2:00 PM.

Come see a police car and fire truck up close and personal and much more!
