PewDiePie slams transphobia claims after misgendering Dr Phil guest

21 Nov 2019, 14:10

PewDiePie has slammed claims that he’s transphobic, after misgendering a trans woman in one of his videos.

The YouTube star – real name Felix Kjellberg – reacted to clips from Dr Phil, including one featuring 19-year-old Bameron.

Describing her as a ‘Walmart Jeffree Star’, Felix, who recently claimed his videos are banned in China, used he/him pronouns while watching the clip.

The comments didn’t pass people by, with some viewers slamming the YouTuber on social media.

Speaking out in a video, Bameron also took aim at Felix, saying: ‘PewDiePie, I know you’re not out here with 100 million subscribers, with 8000 scandals, misgendering trans people.’
