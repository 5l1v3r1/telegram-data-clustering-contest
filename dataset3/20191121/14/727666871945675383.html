Migos Partner With Mike's Harder For #DudeThatsMigos Contest

21 Nov 2019, 14:55 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

“Share how you want to celebrate HARDER like Migos.”

(AllHipHop News) Rap trio Migos are offering people the chance to win a trip for two to party with Offset, Takeoff, and Quavo. The #DudeThatsMigos contest is being presented by mike's HARDER.

"Migos are wild, unexpected and a whole lot of fun. This makes for a great collaboration with HARDER's fearless and go-bolder mentality. They both work hard, and go even HARDER, setting a perfect tone for this partnership," says Sanjiv Gajiwala, Senior Vice President of Marketing, Mike's Hard Lemonade.

Through December 31, fans (21 years or older) can enter by sharing how they want to celebrate like Migos on a public Twitter account while using #DudeThatsMigos and #contest. Participants can also enter by visiting www.dudethatsmigos.com.

Winners will be announced in early 2020. The approved prize pack includes:

  • "A $20,000 stack"
  • "HARDER chain to show off your own personal drip"
  • "Dope accommodations during the stay"
  • "A ride in a luxury vehicle to box seats at a professional basketball game with the Migos crew in Atlanta" 

“Stacks, ice and drip. We get wit mike's HARDER. You get with us,” states the members of Migos. The Grammy-nominated group has released three official studio albums: Yung Rich Nation, Culture, and Culture II. The latter two projects have been certified at least Platinum by the RIAA.
