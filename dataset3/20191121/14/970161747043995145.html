I'm A Celebrity: Andy Whyment fears Corrie cast will vote him for every trial

21 Nov 2019, 14:31

Coronation Street’s Andy Whyment reckons Alan Halsall is about to stitch him right up by getting the cast to vote him in for bushtucker trials on I’m a Celebrity.

Earlier this year, Alan was heavily rumoured to be heading into the jungle, only for Andy to end up becoming a late addition to the show alongside EastEnders star, Cliff Parisi.

But now that he is going in, he reckons he could be in trouble – particularly as he’s now at the mercy of his co-stars, who all have the voting app on their phone.

Speaking ahead of heading into the jungle, Andy said: ‘I know some of my Corrie colleagues and especially Alan Halsall, who plays Tyrone, will vote for me to do the trials.

‘I know he will be telling everyone to vote for me on Twitter!’

Well…if he hadn’t thought of it before, he certainly is now!

Andy and Cliff have been thrown in the deep end with their time on I’m a Celebrity, heading straight into a bushtucker trial to win food stars for their new campmates.

In a preview clip from tonight’s episode, Andy is seen getting his hands dirty as he rifles through mountains of rank smelling offal.

Seems we’ve got a psychic in our midst, seeing as ‘putting his hands in things’ was literally the first thing he said he didn’t want to do.

‘I am not looking forward to the eating trials and I am a bit worried about what I will find when you put your hand in something,’ he said.

‘But when you are hungry, you have to do it for your campmates and try your best.’

We’ve got all our fingers crossed for him – especially considering that if he does badly, he’s got a hungry Ian Wright to deal with…
