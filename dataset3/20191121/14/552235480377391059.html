Mothercare announce Black Friday sale with further discounts to clearance stock

Stores have already commenced 'all stock must go' clearance sales but the retailer say they've now added even further reductions.

21 Nov 2019, 14:26 by Sheena Mcstravick

Retailer Mothercare has announced a huge Black Friday Sale - days after going into administration.

The popular nursery and children's retailer announced earlier this month that it had appointed administrators which will eventually result in the closure of all 75 stores across the UK.

Stores have already commenced 'all stock must go' clearance sales but the retailer say they've now added even further reductions for the Black Friday sales, Belfast Live reports.

 

A spokesperson said: "The bargains at Mothercare just keep getting bigger, just in time for Black Friday! From Thursday 21 November, a selection of nursery products will be available at Black Friday discounts.  

"With Christmas just around the corner, it’s the perfect opportunity to pick up some bargain, as prices are reduced across all ranges."

Among the reduced items are Chicco Next2Me crib, Tommee Tippee Perfect Prep machine and Mothercare chrome travel system.
