iPhone 12 renders tease industrial iPad Pro-esque design

But it looks like the notch is staying for now

21 Nov 2019, 14:39 by Carly Page

A NOTORIOUS TIPSTER has shared a render of what we can expect the so-called iPhone 12 to look like.

The render (below) which is based on leaked information, shows that the iPhone 12 will borrow some design elements from both the iPhone 4 and iPad Pro with its industrial chassis and slimmed-down bezels.
