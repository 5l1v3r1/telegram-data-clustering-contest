The best hampers for Christmas 2019

21 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Precious Adesina

It wouldn't be Christmas without a giant hamper full of delicious food and drinks. And, we believe the bigger, the hamper the better. 

The variety of hampers that you can get will leave you spoilt for choice, whether it's chocolate, wine, cheese or fruit or perhaps a mixture of lots of luxury items. Though, if you want to try making your own, you can read our guide to making the perfect hamper.

In our guide below, you'll find everything from selections of Champagne to breakfast hampers - we've got everything you heart could possibly desire for a truly unique gift. 

Here is 16 of the best Christmas hampers for 2019 - and where your can buy them in the UK.
