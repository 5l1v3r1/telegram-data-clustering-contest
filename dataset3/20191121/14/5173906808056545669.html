Black Friday anniversary at Designer Outlet Algarve

21 Nov 2019, 14:00

Designer Outlet Algarve celebrates its 2nd anniversary with the biggest campaign of the year: Black Friday

On Friday, November 29, visitors can enjoy discounts of up to -80% on over 50 fashion and lifestyle brands. To mark this day, Designer Outlet Algarve has organised various street entertainment between 18h00 and 20h00, including Latin dance performances and saxophone and violin musical performances, as well as thematic decoration that can be found in every nook and cranny.

"The celebration of the 2nd anniversary of Designer Outlet Algarve represents one of the most important dates for us and this year we decided to associate this special day with the Black Friday mega campaign. On the most anticipated day of the year we want to offer the best opportunities with exclusive discounts, in an authentic space and with a contagious energy", says the Marketing Director of Designer Outlet Algarve, Célia Meira.

At Calvin Klein visitors can count on an additional 50% off outlet price. Also on the outlet price, Guess discounts -30%, The Body Shop -25%, Adidas -20% and Puma -30% throughout the store.

Other brands will offer discounts throughout the store, such as Benetton (-70% over PVP) and Sacoor One (-80% over PVP). At Purificacion Garcia there will be exclusive items for 30 € and at Hawkers buying a pair of glasses will allow you to bring three pairs. Brands such as Tommy Hilfiger, Levi's, Bimba y Lola and Calzedonia are also participating in the campaign.
