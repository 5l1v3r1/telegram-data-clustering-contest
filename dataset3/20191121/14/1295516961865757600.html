Hunters: Jordan Peele's Nazi-Hunting Amazon Series Drops Al Pacino-Feuled Trailer

21 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Sam Stone

Brad Pitt isn't the only one who can assemble his own gang of Nazi hunters. The new teaser trailer for the upcoming Amazon original series Hunters sees Al Pacino's character form his own team to take on the last remnants of the Third Reich.

Set in 1977 New York City, the unlikely group of misfits come together when they learn that hundreds of Nazis are hiding throughout the United States plotting to start a new genocidal legacy. Instead, the Hunters vow to strike first to stop them, with the latest trailer taking full advantage of its period setting and eclectic cast.
