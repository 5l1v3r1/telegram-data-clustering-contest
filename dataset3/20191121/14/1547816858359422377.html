Bigg Boss 13: Sidharth Shukla Wins Captaincy Task But Shefali Jariwala Declares Himanshi Khurana The Captain

21 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Lubna Khan

In the upcoming episode of Bigg Boss 13, we’ll get to see Sidharth Shukla, Himanshi Khurana, Shehnaaz Gill and Hindustani Bhau battle it out for the captaincy task. While Sidharth wins the captaincy task, Sanchaalak Shefali Jariwala declares Himanshi as the winner and the next captain of BB house

Bigg Boss 13 is getting more and more interesting each passing week, with entertaining tasks, some huge fights between contestants, and endless drama with some sweet moments in between. Recently, Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz’s fight in the BB house had turned violent and housemates had to intervene to separate them. Now, looks like another captaincy task will take place and just like every other thing in Bigg Boss, this one too ends up with an argument. In the upcoming episode of Bigg Boss 13, we’ll get to see Sidharth Shukla, Himanshi Khurana, Shehnaaz Gill and Hindustani Bhau battle it out for the captaincy task.

The captaincy task for this week is going to be one where the contenders for the same will be asked to hold on to a frame. Whoever holds on to it for the longest time, will be the winner of the task and will be declared the next captain of BB house. Sidharth Shukla, Himanshi Khurana, Shehnaaz Gill and Hindustani Bhau are the contenders for being the next captain. According to a fan page Bigg Boss Tak, Sidharth Shukla manages to hold on to the frame till the end and wins. However, Himanshi raises an objection against it, after which the Sanchaalak Shefali Jariwala declares Himanshi as the winner and the captain of the BB house.
