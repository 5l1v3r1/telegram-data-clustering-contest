Woman Alleges That Dope Nation Has Defrauded Her Son Who Is An Up & Coming Artist

21 Nov 2019, 14:56 by Ghbase New Writer

A woman identified as Philomena has made a wild allegation against music duo, Dope Nation for defrauding her son called Dope Kid an amount of ¢2,000.

The woman who spoke on Zylofon FM explained that the twins, Twist and B2 refused to help her son release a song dubbed ‘Data’ sent to them after they parted away with ¢2,000 after they were initially charged ¢3,000 for four songs.

The aggrieved woman said she lodged a complaint at the police station at Achimota Mile 7 but nothing was done about the issue because of their status.

Madam Philomena said instead of arresting Dope Nation, the police rather invited them and took pictures with them which they posted on social media.

The pair is yet to answer to this allegation.
