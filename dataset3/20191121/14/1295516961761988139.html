Venom: Marvel Starts to Seed Its Next Symbiote Event in February

21 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Sam Stone

While Absolute Carnage may have just come to a thrilling end, Eddie Brock's troubles with the symbiotes are only getting deadlier in the pages of Venom.

Marvel Comics has released its solicitation information for titles going on sale in February 2020, including Venom #23, by Donny Cates and Mark Bagley. A continuation of the creative team's "Venom Island" storyline kicking off next month, the solicit teases the issue is the start of the build-up to the next major symbiote-centric event in the Marvel Universe.

VENOM #23

  • DONNY CATES (W) • MARK BAGLEY (A/C)
  • VARIANT COVER BY SKOTTIE YOUNG
  • GWEN STACY VARIANT COVER BY HUMBERTO RAMOS
  • VENOM ISLAND COMES ALIVE!
  • • They say there’s no rest for the wicked, and few creatures in the Marvel Universe are as wicked as CARNAGE.
  • • Face front, readers -- the seeds of next year’s VENOM event are already beginning to grow…
  • 32 PGS./Rated T+ …$3.99

Absolute Carnage served as the culmination of Cates' run on Venom to that point, with over a year of build-up. However, with the solicit teasing even bigger things to come, Cletus Kasady may have been the least of Eddie's worries.

Venom #23, by Donny Cates and Mark Bagley, goes on sale in February 2020 from Marvel Comics.
