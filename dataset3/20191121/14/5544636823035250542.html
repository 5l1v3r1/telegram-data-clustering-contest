Chioma Throws Birthday Party For Davido (Video)

21 Nov 2019, 14:03 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Chioma Rowland, fiancee of DMW boss and singer Davido  has organised a birthday party for him on his 27th birthday.

Concise News reports that the ’30 billion gang’ crooner added another year on Thursday, November 21.

While it is unclear if there would be a bigger celebration, Chioma threw a birthday dinner for Davido with close friends in attendance.

Some highlights of the party were shared on Chioma’s Instagram handle in the early hours of Thursday.
