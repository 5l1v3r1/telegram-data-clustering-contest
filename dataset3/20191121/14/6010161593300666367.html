GTFO’s Rundown system is how the game will keep things interesting

21 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Sherif Saed

GTFO’s latest trailer digs deep into the Rundown.

GTFO, the co-op survival horror shooter from 10 Chambers, takes place within an underground complex. Players are sent into different sections of the massive bunker to scavenge and perform specific tasks.

These orders are issued regularly by a mysterious entity called The Warden, as part of a system the developer calls Rundown. The Rundown is essentially all the missions available at any given time. The deeper inside the complex they’re set, the harder they are.

Depending on the number of missions and how deep they go, each Rundown could last days or weeks, before it’s replaced with a different one made up of entirely new missions in different environments and populated by different enemies.

The developer says each mission will be crafted to offer a specific experience, and new Rundowns will help keep the game fresh every time you log in.
