Holly Willoughby shares rare picture of her kids enjoying Winter Wonderland

21 Nov 2019, 14:24

Holly Willoughby has posted a rare picture of her children on social media after enjoying a night out at the Winter Wonderland launch, in London.

The star shares eight-year-old daughter Belle, and sons Chester, five, and Harry, 10, with her husband Daniel Baldwin.

And it looks as though the family had the best of times as they explored the capital’s festive theme park.

Taking to Instagram, the proud mum uploaded a cute snap of her bambinos looking up at the Christmas lights.

The trio could be seen all wrapped up and ready for the winter chill in their bobble hats and gloves.

In the caption, the This Morning host celebrated the evening, as she wrote to her 5.9millioin followers: ‘Thank you for a dreamy night @hydeparkwinterwonderland.’

And right on cue, Holly’s fans flooded the comments section as they cooed over the little ones.

‘I love your daughter’s coat,’ one said while another added: ‘I hope you had a lovely time xx Winter Wonderland is magical .’

Someone else pointed out how quiet it looked, and joked: ‘Wish it was like this when we go.’

The park was opened to a number of stars on Wednesday evening, where Holly was joined by the likes of Gemma Collins and Amber Gill.

Gem looked all prepared for the holiday season as she opted for a bright red scarf and matching beret.

And it seems the Towie star had a blast, as she was spotted having all the fun behind the wheel on the bumper cars.

Taking her nephew along for the ride, the 38-year-old couldn’t wipe the smile off her face as she whizzed around the track.

But it shouldn’t come as a surprise that Gemma is an expert when it comes to driving, after all, she did use to be a car salesperson before finding fame on reality TV.

She also recently swapped her £70,000 BMW for a 1999 Nissan Micra with a market value of just £275.
