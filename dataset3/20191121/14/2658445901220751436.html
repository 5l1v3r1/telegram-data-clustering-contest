Nigerian singer Calebin talks upcoming project with Zlatan Ibile

21 Nov 2019, 14:38 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

Adenike Fagbemi

Musician and instrumentalist, Caleb Godson, profoundly called Calebin, has revealed his plans of releasing a musical collaboration with street-hop singer, Zlatan Ibile.

Calebin, a certified Construction and Building engineer who was at the just held One Africa Music Fest in Dubai, made the duet announcement after posting a photo showing Zlatan and himself at the event’s back stage on social media.

The singer said his fans should await his forthcoming single featuring the Zanku crooner soonest. “2 But One Down!! #Grateful A Hit Coming, Calebin ft Zlatan Ibile,” he shared on his official facebook page.

The multiple award-winning artiste also hinted at floating a clothing line.

Stylish Calebin confirmed that the new fashion outfit would stun the whole of the Nigerian entertainment industry with its remarkable designs and his approach of business.

Calebin, in the past, had teamed up with the likes of Tekno Miles on a track titled ‘Parole’, produced by veteran music engineer DJ Coublon and Calebin himself with Fiokee on the guitar.

The energetic and thrilling performer also dropped Killer Tunes-produced mega hit, ‘Turn Up’ and ‘Morenike’, which was doctored by Doomzday recently.
