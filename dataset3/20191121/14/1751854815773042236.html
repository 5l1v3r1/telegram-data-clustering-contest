The Mi CC9 series includes the Mi CC9e, Mi CC9, Mi CC9 Pro, and the Mi CC9 Meitu Custom Edition. While all four phones are borne from Xiaomi and Meitu‘s partnership, the Mi CC9 Meitu Edition contains more of Meitu’s input. Its cameras are powered by Meitu’s imaging technology and not Xiaomi’s. It also has Meitu’s branding and comes with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage.

When it launched back in May, it was priced at ¥2599 (~$370). Xiaomi has now announced that the phone will get a price slash down to ¥1999 (~$284) at 10 AM tomorrow. So if you have set your eyes on the Mi CC9 Meitu Edition, tomorrow is an opportunity for you to get the phone at a cheaper price.

The Mi CC9 Meitu Custom Edition has a 6.39-inch FHD+ AMOLED display with a waterdrop notch. It is powered by a Snapdragon 710 processor and draws power from a 4030mAh battery.

The selfie camera is a 32MP sensor with AI low-light portrait mode. There is a 48MP Sony camera on the rear and it is paired with an 8MP ultrawide angle camera and a 2MP depth sensor. There are lots of features including a Mimoji, Xiaomi’s take on AR emojis.

The phone has NFC, an IR blaster, dual-SIM support, an audio jack, and a USB-C port. It supports 18W fast charging and runs MIUI based on Android 9 Pie out of the box.
