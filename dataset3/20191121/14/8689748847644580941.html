DopeNation in trouble for allegedly defrauding a 19 year old underground musician

21 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Nana Yaw

Lynx Entertainment signed artist, DopeNation is in trouble after a mother of an underground artist revealed that the twin brothers have defrauded her 199-year-old-son of an amount of GHC2,000.

The mother of the underground musician who goes by the name Dope Kid in a phone-in interview with Sammy Flex on Zylofon FM alleged that Dope Nation have defrauded her son.

According to Madam Philomena, the music duo, Twist and B2 (Dope Nation) have refused to release “data” of a song sent to them by his son after they had paid an amount of GHS 2000 (instead of 3000) for four songs.
