Samsung Galaxy users get free YouTube Premium for up to 4 months

21 Nov 2019, 14:06

Here’s good news for all the Samsung Galaxy users! Samsung is giving out free trials for you to access YouTube Premium for up to 4 months! However, only users who purchase and activate any Samsung Galaxy device from 20 February 2019 until 29 February 2020 are eligible.

For users who purchase the S10 series, Note 10 series, Galaxy Tab S5e and Galaxy Tab S6 are going to get 4 months of free YouTube Premium subscription. The other Galaxy devices will be receiving 2 months of free trials as well.

YouTube Premium was first released earlier this month and it is a service that offers you ads-free video viewing experience. Other features such as download the videos for offline viewing, play the video in the background and more come with the service as well.

Here is a step-by-step guide for eligible users to redeem the offer:

  • Open the YouTube app on your Samsung Galaxy device and go to Account
  • Click on Get YouTube Premium
  • Click on Try it For Free
  • Complete your purchase 
  • And finally, enjoy your YouTube videos and music without interruption
