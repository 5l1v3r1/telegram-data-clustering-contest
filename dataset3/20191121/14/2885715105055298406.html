NEET PG 2020: Registration process to end today, check exam dates

NEET PG 2020: The application process will be closed on November 21, 2019. Interested candidates can apply through the website- natboard.edu.in

21 Nov 2019, 14:10 by Education Desk

NEET PG 2020: The application process for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) for PG will be closed on Thursday, November 21, 2019. Interested candidates can apply through the website- natboard.edu.in.

The NEET-PG 2020 is scheduled to be conducted on January 5, 2020. As per the official notification, the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for PG will be conducted in the computer-based mode. The PG admit card 2020 will be released in the last week of December 2019.

Meanwhile, the examinations for NEET MDS, PDCET and FMGE will be held on December 20.

NEET PG 2020: Paper pattern

NEET PG 2020 exam pattern includes a total of 300 objective type questions for which each candidate will be allotted three hours and 30 minutes. Applicants will be able to access the demo test in the second week of December 2019.
