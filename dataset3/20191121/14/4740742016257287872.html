Clubs across the Championship have injury headaches to consider

21 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Josh Challies

The final international break of the calendar year has come to a close and opens the door for a frantic time in the Championship during the festive period.

With the Christmas fixture pile-up right around the corner, having a fully-fit squad is incredibly important - but it’s something that not too many clubs will be able to enjoy.

The recent international break has come at a good time for some, giving those with knocks time to recover, but others may have picked up issues while travelling across the globe to represent their nations.

Using the latest information from across the web, we have listed all of the injury, illness and suspension concerns ahead of this weekend’s fixtures and how many in each squad could be a doubt.
