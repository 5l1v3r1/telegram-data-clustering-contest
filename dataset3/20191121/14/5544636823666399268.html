Almost Gave Up In Early Days Of Mavin, Says Don Jazzy

21 Nov 2019, 14:18 by Oyindamola Olubajo

The road to success is usually not smooth as expected and as such, legendary music producer, Don Jazzy has recounted how he almost gave up at the genesis of the record label.

Concise News reports that the producer, in series of tweets hailed Burna Boy who bagged Grammy award nomination, while he revealed that he almost gave up on his goals.

Sharing an old photo of himself Tiwa Savage, Wande Coal, Dr, Sid and D’Prince, DOn Jazzy wrote “Everyone has their goal. I almost gave up on mine in the early days of MAVIN. As you can see in this throwback pic. I looked fed up lol . But man had to keep pushing. May you reap the rewards of all your hard work last last o.”

“I looked fed up lol ? . But man had to keep pushing. May you reap the rewards of all your hard work last last o ??” he added.
