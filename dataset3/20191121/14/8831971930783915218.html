Jennifer Lopez Is the New Face Of Coach

21 Nov 2019, 14:21 by Shivani Singh

Singer, actress, producer, and entertainer, Jennifer Lopez was just announced as the new global face of Coach! Starting in Spring 2020, J Lo will be the face in worldwide advertising campaigns for the brand’s leather goods, clothing, and footwear.

Coach chose JLo as their newest ambassador because they both have deep roots in New York.  Coach was founded in the Big Apple 78 years ago, and Lopez is a Bronx native. She said that  she’s always felt connected to the label, she even featured its signature printed purses in her 2002 music video for “All I Have.”
