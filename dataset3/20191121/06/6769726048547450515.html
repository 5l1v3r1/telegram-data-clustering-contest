Noranda Vibes holds Christmas concert

21 Nov 2019, 06:58 by Staff writer

THE vocals and sounds of Noranda Primary School’s choir and the WA Fire and Emergency Services Concert Band will be showcased at the Carols by Candlelight in Noranda.

Community group Noranda Vibes is holding its free annual event at 5pm on November 23 at Robert Thompson Reserve.

There will be performances from the choir, the band and students from the WA Academy of Performing Arts.

Community stalls, food trucks, free activities and a special visit from Santa will also be among the festivities.

Attendees can also buy LED candles and songbooks at the event.

The group urges attendees to bring picnic rugs or low backed chairs on the night.

For more information, visit Noranda Vibes’ Facebook page.
