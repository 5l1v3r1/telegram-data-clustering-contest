M1 crash causing long delays for afternoon motorists

21 Nov 2019, 06:22 by Shanee Dobeson

A multi-vehicle crash is causing long delays for motorists heading home from work this afternoon.

The accident happened southbound at Helensvale around 4.00pm.

It’s understood three vehicles collided just before the Westfield Helensvale Exit 62.

Emergency crews are on scene, however it’s not yet known how many people, if any, have been injured.

Delays are quickly building in the area, with the Department of Transport and Main Roads confirming at least one lane has been blocked.

Drivers are being urged to allow extra travel time.
