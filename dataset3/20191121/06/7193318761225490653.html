Livestock report from the Oklahoma City Stockyards

21 Nov 2019, 06:05

Livestock report from the Oklahoma City Stockyards:

Receipts: 1,982, Last week: 912; Last year: 1,063

Receipts: Monday: 13,355

Compared to last week: Slaughter cows $2-$3 higher with a majority of the test on boner and lean cows. Slaughter bulls not well tested last week but a sharply higher undertone was noted. Demand good. There was a abundance of average to attractive bred cows on offer. A week of warm temps has producers bringing cull cows in before another cold front comes through. A total of 955 cows and bulls sold with 48 percent going to packers. Supply included: 47% Slaughter Cattle (93% Cows, 7% Bulls); 53% Replacement Cattle (90% Bred Cows, 8% Bred Heifers, 2% Cow-Calf Pairs).
