Go nuts while you travel with FairPrice snacks

From snacks to wet wipes, FairPrice has items to ease all your travel worries

21 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Elaine Lee

If you travel overseas this holiday season, you are bound to feel peckish at some point - at the airport, on a plane, in between destinations or even in your hotel room.

Ready-to-eat foods that you can munch on and give you a boost of energy while on the go are the most practical solution.

So pack some healthy snacks from FairPrice Housebrand's range of cashew nuts that is not only loaded with antioxidants but is also a source of many nutrients to keep hunger pangs at bay.

For those who prefer the fuss-free original flavour, get the FairPrice Baked Cashew Nuts (150g, $4.30).

High in monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats and lower in sodium, the Healthier Choice-certified product is not only a good source of protein, it is also trans fat free and halal.

For a more premium version, check out the FairPrice Party Snacks - Natural Baked Cashews (400g, $10.95).

For those who have allergies, it is important to note that the product contains tree nuts and may contain eggs and fish.

If you prefer variety, the FairPrice Baked Walnuts with Cashews Nuts (150g, $5.25) will surely satisfy.

Also a Healthier Choice-certified product that is lower in sodium, the hypoallergenic item is naturally cholesterol free and trans fat free.

Or choose from alternatives such as the FairPrice Baked Cashew Nuts with Almonds (150g, $4.95) or FairPrice Baked Macadamia with Cashew Nuts (150g, $5.65).

Naturally cholesterol free and trans fat free, it is also a Healthier Choice-certified product that is lower in sodium.

However, do take note that the product has tree nuts and may contain gluten, crustacean, eggs, peanut and soy.

For a more fruity version, the FairPrice Snacks - Natural Fruits & Nuts (150g, $4.95) is high in dietary fibre, trans fat free and very low in sodium.

It contains natural fruits and nuts. Those with allergies should note that it has tree nuts and sulphites.

Tissue and wet wipes are also a must when travelling.

After going nutty over these tidbits, clean up with FairPrice Soft Pocket Handkerchiefs ($4.80) or FairPrice Soft Pocket Handkerchiefs - Fruity ($4.80), with 40 x 10 for each pack, made from 100 per cent pulp.

In addition, they also have the Singapore Green Label, a seal of endorsement on their environmentally-friendly claims to prevent the abuse of green-washing.

This has proved to be advantageous for Green Label-certified products, which have become more marketable and readily accepted by consumers or businesses when making a purchase.

They are also certified by non-profit organisation Forest Stewardship Council (FSC), which means that the materials came from responsible forests.

By choosing products with FSC labels, one is helping to take care of the world's forests.

For a more thorough wipe down, the alcohol-free and pH neutral FairPrice Anti-Bacterial Wet Wipes ($3.10) - which come in a 3 x 15 pack with an additional free 15 sheets for each pack - are perfect.

Not only is it softer and stronger to get rid of dirt and germs quickly, the aloe vera ingredient in the product leaves skin feeling moisturised, fresh and soft.

For a stronger scent, get the alcohol free FairPrice Wet Tissues - Green Tea ($2) or the FairPrice Wet Tissues - Sakura ($2), both of which come in a 3 x 10 per pack.

Gentle and mild with aloe vera,the wipes are not only hygienic and refreshing, but compact and handy as well.
