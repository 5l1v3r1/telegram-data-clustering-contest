Brave girl, 14, puts bus pervert in a headlock after he 'grinded on passengers'

The 14-year-old put the alleged pervert into a headlock after he rubbed his genitals against another woman on a bus in the city of Guiyang, south-western China

21 Nov 2019, 06:12 by Louise Newton, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-newton/

A brave schoolgirl put a man into a headlock after he allegedly touched a fellow bus passenger with his exposed genitals.

The assault took place on the 221 bus in the city of Guiyang, the capital of Guizhou Province in south-western China.

CCTV footage from the bus shows the suspect initially touching his crotch from the outside of his trousers.

He then stepped closer to the victim and reached into his trousers before undoing the zip in shocking footage that was captured on the bus' closed circuit system.

 

The teenage witness claims he then took his penis out of his trousers and pressed his genitals onto the unsuspecting woman's bottom.

The perpetrator tried to leave the bus but the girl was having none of it. She bravely confronted him and grabbed his arms as he tried to scarper.

The victim appeared to realise and both the women spring into action and hold onto him as he tried to escape.

 

The teenage girl can then be seen putting him into a headlock.

Police said the man is in his thirties, and he has only been identified only by his surname, Zhang.

Wang Mingjian was the bus driver and said he kept the back doors locked as other passengers helped detain the suspect.

Guiyang police arrived shortly after and arrested him.

Zhang has now been charged with harassment given a 10-day jail sentence.
