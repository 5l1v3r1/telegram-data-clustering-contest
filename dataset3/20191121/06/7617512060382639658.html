Enter to win holiday experiences with this online advent calendar

This modern advent calendar includes a month of daily prizes and the chance to win a Grand Prize trip to Vancouver.

21 Nov 2019, 06:30

There is nothing more quintessentially Christmas than a chocolate advent calendar that lets kids (and kids at heart) count down the days till December 25th. Now, Stay-Vancouver Hotels is enchanting tourists with an online advent calendar that is giving away daily prizes and the chance to win a grand prize of a luxurious, experience-packed stay in Vancouver where the winner will likely create memories to last a lifetime.

Already renowned for its natural beauty, cosmopolitan diversity, unbelievable food and shopping, Vancouver is now also sure to become known for one more thing – a perennial favourite for visitors seeking to enjoy the best of the holiday season with all that the Very Merry Vancouver contest has to offer.

Imagine walking across a breathtaking bridge suspended two hundred and thirty feet above a river gorge and adorned with a million glittering lights. Or sightseeing in an old-fashioned trolley bus and singing Karaoke carols from one unforgettable stop to another. What about strolling through a traditional German Christmas Market, sipping on hot chocolate and shopping for that perfect holiday keepsake from one of its 80 jolly merchants? These are just some of the experiences you could enjoy as a daily prize winner.

As an entrant, you also have the chance to win an evening under the Big Top of Cirque Du Soleil’s latest masterpiece, Luzia. You could also win the chance to explore four mystical worlds at the Aurora Winter Festival along with snow tubing, amusement rides in a natural setting and amidst massive light displays. Family passes to Santa’s winter wonderland at the Peak of Grouse Mountain and its awe-inspiring views of the city and the ocean below are also up for grabs. And don’t forget the heartwarming cruise, lighting up Vancouver harbour, with carolers inviting you to join in song to celebrate this festive time of year. There are these adventures, and so much more.

Over 20, must-see holiday attraction and event companies are giving away tickets and gift packages as part of the Very Merry Vancouver promotion. Not to mention there are hotel partners giving away accommodation as well.

VeryMerryVancouver.com is now welcoming online visitors to enter to win these daily holiday prizes and encouraging online users to visit Vancouver in person throughout the holidays. Don’t miss your chance to win a holiday experience that will add a little sparkle to the season. Good luck!
