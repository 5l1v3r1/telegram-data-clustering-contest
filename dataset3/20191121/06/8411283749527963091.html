Taiwan October export orders down on year

21 Nov 2019, 06:37 by Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES, Taipei

Taiwan received export orders worth US$47.28 billion in total in October 2019, increasing 3.9% sequentially but decreasing 3.5% on year, according to statistics released by the Ministry of Economic Affairs (MOEA).

IT/communication and electronic products were the top two categories in terms of export order value for October, with the former generating US$16.31 billion and the latter US$12.95 billion.

Export orders for January-October totaled US$396.24 billion in value, slipping 5.8% on year.
