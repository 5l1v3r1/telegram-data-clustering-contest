Burna Boy’s Grammy Nomination: Watch the moment his grandfather prayed for him to win a Grammy award

21 Nov 2019, 06:07 by Nana Yaw

Burna Boy’s album, African Giant was yesterday nominated in the Best World Album category at the next edition of the Grammy Awards slated for January 26, 2020.

Social media users praised him for his enormous work but a video that surfaced after the nomination is what caught our attention that the spoken prayers of the old play a major role in the lives of the young.

In the video, it was seen that Burna Boy’s grandfather was praying for him to win a Grammy award and the video we learnt is a video that surfaced a few months ago.

In the video, the singer’s grandfather prayed for him that his next award is going to be a ‘Grammy Award’ and indeed he’s on the road to winning a Grammy’s.

His grandpa’s prayer will become a reality should he win that award category.

Burna Boy’s album, African Giant got nominated in the Best World Album category alongside Altin Gün for Gece, Bokanté & Metropole Orkest Conducted By Jules Buckley for What Heat, Nathalie Joachim With Spektral Quartet for Fanm D’Ayiti, and Angelique Kidjo for Celia.
