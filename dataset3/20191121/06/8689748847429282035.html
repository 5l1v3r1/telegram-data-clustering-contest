Michy Set To Open Her Own Restaurant & Hotel

21 Nov 2019, 06:19 by BB Jones

Michelle Diamond Gbagonah, aka Michy is set to open a new eatery joint with a hotel attached to it very soon.

The former Queen of the SM empire has demonstrated that she can be on her own and be completely an independent woman since she severed ties with Shatta Wale.

Her new enterprise will add up to her already established Chopius Foods Joint and Glamorous by Michy, a firm that produces skin supplements for ladies.

Michy herself drop this new development on her Instagram to update her fans on what she’s up to.

She wrote:

“Finally a classy hideout y’all can come & wine dine. More food + drinks & good music + hotel rooms on the side if u wanna stay the night..wait on it.”
