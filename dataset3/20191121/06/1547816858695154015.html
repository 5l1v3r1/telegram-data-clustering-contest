‘Bachchan Pandey's Heroine Kriti Sanon Is A Parallel Hero In The Film,' Says Director Farhad Samji- EXCLUSIVE

21 Nov 2019, 06:16 by Vickey Lalwani

Kriti Sanon is raring to go with some interesting roles in her kitty. Now, the actress has landed a meaty part in Farhad Samji's Bachchan Pandey opposite Akshay Kumar. We have learnt that her role in the film is like a parallel hero

There are no two ways about how talented an actress Kriti Sanon is. Not only has she worked in some amazing big banner films, but the actress has also shared screen space with some superstars in Bollywood. 

Kriti Sanon has been riding high on success of films like Bareilly Ki Barfi, Luka Chuppi. Even her roles are getting meatier by the day. Now, speaking of her forthcoming film Bachchan Pandey, she will be seen playing the female lead opposite Akshay Kumar.

Yet again, Kriti has a very integral part to play. She is not just a mere prop or a glam doll in the film. But, this is endorsed by Bachchan Pandey director Farhad Samji who says, “I can say this without any qualms that Kriti’s role in the film is better than a parallel hero role. It’s a very meaty role, in fact any actress wouldn’t have gotten such a role, it is that good. “

Even Kriti is quite thrilled and beyond excited to be a part of this Sajid Nadiadwala production which is slated to release in Christmas 2020. This will be the second time Kriti will be sharing the big screen with Akshay Kumar and her fans would surely be excited to see her yet again in a pivotal role.

Atta girl!
