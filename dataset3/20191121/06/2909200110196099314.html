Only fools buy data just to insult and create rivalry – Kwaw Kese

21 Nov 2019, 06:21 by MyNewsGH

Rapper, Kwaw Kese believes only fools buy data to insult others on social media just to spark what is now known as beef or rivalry in the music scenes.

The “Dondo” hit maker made this known in a tweet addressing rowdy and disrespectful fans of his colleague artistes.

He observed that it’s prudent to rather support the work of an artiste rather than comparing artistes and pitching them against each other.

He said ” If you can buy data and come on social media just to diss people and compare other acts to others and insult them then you’re a fool You can do better than that”.
