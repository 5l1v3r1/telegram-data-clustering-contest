I can’t be a Ghanaian because some are stup*d – Shatta Wale

21 Nov 2019, 06:12 by MyNewsGH

Dancehall artiste, Shatta Wale born Charles Nii Armah Mensah says he’s not a Ghanaian considering how some persons he refused to name appear so stupid.

According to him, he belongs to Shatta Movement Nation and not Ghana because some Ghanaians do not reason as expected

He made this known while reacting to the fact that Beyonce’s album of which he was featured on was nominated for Grammys but some Ghanaians doubted him.

He said ” And who said I am a Ghanaian Shatta movement is my nation I can never stupid and poor minded like some of you here #Etsw3fuor“.

Some users on social media don’t seem to be happy for the SM boss and have openly showed that they do not like the feat he has achieved with his music.
