Prashant Kishor Opposes Amit Shah's Proposal Of NRC In The Entire Country

21 Nov 2019, 06:13 by Prakash Singh

General News

JDU vice president Prashant Kishor has opposed Union Home Minister Amit Shah's proposal that NRC would be applicable in entire India.

Written By Prakash Singh | Mumbai | Updated On: November 21, 2019 06:13 IST

JDU vice president Prashant Kishor has opposed Union Home Minister Amit Shah's proposal that NRC would be applicable in entire India. Prashant Kishor said that this is a unilateral decision and in federal structure without the consultation of the state govt, how can NRC be introduced.

Kishor in his tweet said that "15 plus states with more than 55% of India's population have non -BJP chief ministers. Wonder how many of them are consulted and are on board for NRC in their respective states." Through this tweet, Prashant Kishor has toed the line of west Bengal CM Mamta Banerjee and Bihar CM Nitish Kumar, who are opposed to NRC in their respective states.
