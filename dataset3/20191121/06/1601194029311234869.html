MJF reveals who Wardlow is

21 Nov 2019, 06:19 by Karan Bedi

While MJF may be in the finals of The Diamond Dozen when he takes on Hangman Page next week on AEW Dynamite, he did receive an assist from his new associate, Wardlow. In an interview with Bleacher Report Live, MJF revealed who Wardlow was and said that he's a freak of nature.

Wardlow made his AEW Dynamite debut last week when he almost choked out Cody Rhodes, at the behest of MJF. It was quite plausible to say that Wardlow will serve as an enforcer for MJF going forward.
