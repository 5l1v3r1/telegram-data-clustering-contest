(Video) Epic new footage shows Shakira meltdown during Liverpool 4-0 Barcelona

21 Nov 2019, 06:20 by Jordan Chamberlain

A clip is making Liverpool fans go berserk on Twitter right now that shows Gerard Pique’s wife Shakira losing the plot during our 4-0 victory over Barcelona last season.

A programme about Barcelona’s season is coming out soon, that will show inside the dressing room footage from Anfield that famous night – but the not-yet released documentary also went to film the players’ partners as they watched the game on the TV.

Shakira is sat with her two young sons, one of which is explaining that Barca will still go through with the score at 2-0…

Then Gini Wijnaldum scores his epic header and Shakira’s tantrum begins!
