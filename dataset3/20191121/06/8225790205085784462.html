Apparent shooting in Makaha leaves 39-year-old dead

21 Nov 2019, 06:43 by HNN Staff

HONOLULU, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) - Police are investigating an apparent shooting in Makaha on Wednesday night that left a 39-year-old man dead.

Authorities said the incident happened about 7:10 p.m. on Manuku Street.

Paramedics who responded pronounced the victim dead at the scene. A spokeswoman said the victim had apparently suffered several gunshot wounds.

There was no immediate information on a suspect.
