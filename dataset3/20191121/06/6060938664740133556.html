Arpita Khan Sharma to deliver her second baby on Salman Khan birthday, December 27

21 Nov 2019, 06:10 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan’s could just turn into an even more special event for the Khan family. The actor’s youngest sister, Arpita Khan Sharma is pregnant with her second baby and is due to deliver next month. According to Mumbai Mirror, Arpita and husband Aayush Sharma are planning to welcome baby number two through c-section on Salman Khan’s birthday, on December 27. The actor will turn 54 next month and will reportedly share his birthday with the newborn. Arpita and Aayush are fond of Salman Khan and hence have scheduled the good news for his birthday.

December will not just be a special month for Salman on the personal front, but professionally too, the actor has a big film lined up for release. A week before Salman’s birthday, his cop drama ‘Dabangg 3’ will hit the screens on December 20. The much awaited film will feature Salman in a younger avatar in the flashback portions wherein he will romance debutante Saiee Manjrekar, while Soanakshi Sinha will reprise in her role of Rajjo.

Salman Khan usually celebrates his birthday at his Karjat farmhouse, however with some special plans in place it remains to be seen how the actor celebrates the occasion.
