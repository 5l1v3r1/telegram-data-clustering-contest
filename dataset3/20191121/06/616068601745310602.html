Pedestrian struck by train

21 Nov 2019, 06:43 by Jon Manchester

A pedestrian was struck by a train in Surrey, Wednesday evening.

Surrey RCMP say the incident happened near the intersection of 53rd Avenue, at 184th Street in Cloverdale, about 7:20 p.m.
 
The male victim suffered serious and potentially life-threatening injuries, says Sgt. Mel Wong. 
 
Traffic in the area was shut down overnight, but roads have since reopened. 
 
Investigators are seeking witnesses to the collision, and anyone with information is asked to contact Surrey RCMP at 604-599-0502, or CrimeStoppers at 1-800-222-TIPS or www.solvecrime.ca.
