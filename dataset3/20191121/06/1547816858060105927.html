Arpita Khan To Have A C-Section Baby On Salman Khan's Birthday; Dec 27 Will Be A Double Celebration For Khans

21 Nov 2019, 06:10 by Oshine Koul

If latest reports are anything to go by, Arpita Khan Sharma and Aayush Sharma have scheduled a C-section delivery on the Salman Khan’s birthday which falls on December 27. We can’t wait for the good news!

Salman Khan’s sister Arpita Khan Sharma is all set to welcome her baby no 2 with husband Aayush Sharma. Arpita and Aayush, who tied the knot in November 2014, welcomed their first baby boy Ahil in 2016 since then the duo is enjoying family bliss. A few months back, reports of Arpita Khan carrying baby no. 2 started making headlines. The couple even dashed off with Aripta’s mother Salma Khan and son Ahil Sharma to enjoy babymoon.

Arpita’s delivery date is in next month and to make it more special, the couple has decided to welcome their 2nd baby on Salman Khan’s birthday, which is December 27. According to a report in Mumbai Mirror, Arpita and Aayush, who are fond of Salman Khan, have scheduled a C section delivery on the actor’s birthday. Yes, you heard that right! Salman Khan will now share his birthday with Arpita Khan’s 2nd baby. (ALSO READ: Arpita Khan: Yes I Am Pregnant With My Second Baby, Due In January 2020; Entire Khan-daan Is Overjoyed- EXCLUSIVE)

Arpita’s firstborn Ahil Sharma is known to be the apple of Salman’s eye. So much so that we have often seen Dabangg actor spending some quality time with Ahil and the videos have always been too cute for words.

 
 Since long rumours of Arpita Khan Sharma expecting another baby were making headlines and it was during the green carpet of IIFA 2019 when both have confirmed their pregnancy and had told media, “The new arrival is very exciting. Yes, Arpita and me are expecting a second baby. It’s been such an amazing journey. It starts all over again and we just’ can’t wait for the baby to come.”
