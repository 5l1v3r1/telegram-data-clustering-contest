Tiger Shroff poses for a picture with producer Sajid Nadiadwala and director Ahmed Khan on the sets of 'Baaghi 3'

21 Nov 2019, 06:32 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Tiger Shroff seems to have raised his social media game to the next level as he shoots for his upcoming film 'Baaghi 3' in Serbia. After sharing a picture of himself from the sets of the film, the 29-year-old actor posed for a uber-cool image with producer Sajid Nadiadwala and director Ahmed Khan.

While Tiger seems to be in his character's elements, Nadiadwala and Ahmed look happy as the trio come together for a picture. While the leading lady Shraddha Kapoor has returned to the bay, Tiger has been shooting for action sequences in Serbia along with 'Gully Boy' fame Vijay Varma, who has joined the cast of the popular 'Baaghi' franchise.

While the release date of the film isn't announced yet, these pictures from the film's set have piqued the interest of the audience. Not only that but the fans are eager to see the hit pair of Tiger-Shraddha on the silver screen once again.
