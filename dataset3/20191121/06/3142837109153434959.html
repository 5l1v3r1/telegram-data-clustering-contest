Aroundtown (ETR:AT1) PT Set at €8.50 by Jefferies Financial Group

21 Nov 2019, 06:50 by Scott Moore

Aroundtown (ETR:AT1) received a €8.50 ($9.88) price target from analysts at Jefferies Financial Group in a research note issued on Tuesday, Borsen Zeitung reports. The brokerage presently has a “buy” rating on the stock. Jefferies Financial Group’s price target points to a potential upside of 11.34% from the stock’s previous close.

AT1 has been the topic of several other research reports. Bankhaus Lampe set a €8.80 ($10.23) price objective on Aroundtown and gave the stock a “buy” rating in a research note on Wednesday, August 28th. Barclays set a €8.00 ($9.30) price objective on Aroundtown and gave the stock a “buy” rating in a research note on Wednesday, September 18th. JPMorgan Chase & Co. set a €8.25 ($9.59) price objective on Aroundtown and gave the stock a “neutral” rating in a research note on Tuesday, September 3rd. UBS Group set a €8.30 ($9.65) price objective on Aroundtown and gave the stock a “buy” rating in a research note on Wednesday, August 28th. Finally, Hauck & Aufhaeuser set a €9.40 ($10.93) price objective on Aroundtown and gave the stock a “buy” rating in a research note on Friday, August 23rd. Three research analysts have rated the stock with a hold rating and twelve have given a buy rating to the company. The company has an average rating of “Buy” and an average price target of €8.64 ($10.05).

Shares of Aroundtown stock opened at €7.63 ($8.88) on Tuesday. The company has a market capitalization of $9.50 billion and a PE ratio of 5.68. Aroundtown has a 12 month low of €6.85 ($7.97) and a 12 month high of €7.95 ($9.24). The company has a current ratio of 2.95, a quick ratio of 2.77 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 80.90. The stock’s fifty day simple moving average is €7.51 and its 200 day simple moving average is €7.42.

About Aroundtown

Aroundtown SA, together with its subsidiaries, operates as a real estate investment company in Germany, the Netherlands, the United Kingdom, and internationally. It invests in commercial and residential real estate properties, such as office, hotel, logistics, wholesale, retail, and other properties.
