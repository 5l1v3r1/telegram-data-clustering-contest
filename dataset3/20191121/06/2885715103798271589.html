SSC MTS recruitment 2019: Paper I marks uploaded, steps to check here

SSC MTS result 2019: The commission has uploaded the marks of Paper-I of all the candidates (qualified or not qualified for Paper-II) on the official website.

21 Nov 2019, 06:18 by Careers Desk

SSC MTS result 2019: Staff Selection Commission has uploaded the Paper I marks for Multi Tasking Staff posts. The candidates who have given the SSC MTS examination can check their marks from the official website-ssc.nic.in.The results for the MTS recruitment was declared on November 5 and revised results were declared on November 11, 2019. The commission has now uploaded the marks of Paper-I of all the candidates(qualified or not qualified for Paper-II) on the official website-ssc.nic.in.

The SSC MTS exam was conducted from August 2 to 22, 2019 and the candidates will be able to check their marks for a period of one month from November 20 to December 19, 2019.

SSC MTS result 2019: Steps to check Paper I marks

Step 1: Visit the official website-ssc.nic.in

Step 2: On the homepage, login using your username and password.

Step 3: On the candidate dashboard, click on the link that says, ‘Marks/Results’

Step 4: The marks of the candidate will be displayed on the screen.

Step 4: Check your marks and download them for future reference.

The result of Paper I was declared on November 5, 2019, there was a discrepancy in results and the revised results were declared on November 11, 2019. The MTS paper II is scheduled to be conducted on November 24. It will be descriptive and the candidates who will clear the examination will then be called for document verification.
