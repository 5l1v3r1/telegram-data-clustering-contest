The Spanish media front page that confirms Gareth Bale is in all sorts of trouble

Bale's post-match celebrations with Wales seem to have touched a nerve

21 Nov 2019, 06:24 by Mathew Davies

Wales will be at Euro 2020 next summer and while the country has been in raptures over the last 24 hours, it appears some in Spain aren’t overly impressed.

It all centres around Wales’ star man Gareth Bale and that “Wales, golf, Madrid” flag. Whatever your thoughts on the club-versus-country argument over the Welshman, it is impossible to ignore it.

When Ryan Giggs’ side qualified for the European tournament on Tuesday night thanks to a 2-0 win over Hungary , the post-match celebrations pushed Bale and his frosty relationship with his club Real Madrid back into the spotlight.

He was pictured dancing joyously with the “Wales, golf, Madrid” flag that has been made famous by those chants on the terraces and his club were reportedly not happy with the joke.
