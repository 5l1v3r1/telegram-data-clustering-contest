Air T, Inc. (NASDAQ:AIRT) Director Raymond E. Cabillot Purchases 4,500 Shares

21 Nov 2019, 06:51 by Doug Wharley

Air T, Inc. (NASDAQ:AIRT) Director Raymond E. Cabillot bought 4,500 shares of the business’s stock in a transaction that occurred on Friday, November 15th. The shares were bought at an average cost of $19.67 per share, for a total transaction of $88,515.00. Following the completion of the purchase, the director now owns 4,500 shares of the company’s stock, valued at $88,515. The transaction was disclosed in a legal filing with the Securities & Exchange Commission, which can be accessed through the SEC website.

Shares of NASDAQ AIRT opened at $21.50 on Thursday. The firm has a market cap of $60.33 million, a PE ratio of 19.82 and a beta of 1.26. Air T, Inc. has a 12-month low of $12.51 and a 12-month high of $24.50. The company’s fifty day simple moving average is $19.07 and its 200 day simple moving average is $19.74. The company has a quick ratio of 1.05, a current ratio of 1.77 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.90.

Separately, ValuEngine lowered shares of Air T from a “buy” rating to a “hold” rating in a research note on Tuesday.

About Air T

Air T, Inc, together with its subsidiaries, provides overnight air cargo, ground equipment sale, and ground support services in the United States and internationally. The company's Overnight Air Cargo segment offers air express delivery services. As of March 31, 2018, this segment had 79 aircrafts under the dry-lease agreements with FedEx.
