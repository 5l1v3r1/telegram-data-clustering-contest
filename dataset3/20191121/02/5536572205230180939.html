Skip Bayless Rips Ezekiel Elliott For His Terrible 2019 Season After Signing Massive Contract (VIDEO)

21 Nov 2019, 02:03 by Darrelle Lincoln

Skip Bayless is really upset.

The noted Dallas Cowboys fan has been having quite the issue with Ezekiel Elliott over the past few games, especially after he was given a massive contract just before the season began.

Elliott has yet to live up to the hype of that contract and it has been Dak Prescott that has been carrying the team throughout the first half of the season.

On Wednesday’s edition of Undisputed, Bayless went on a very lengthy rant about Elliott’s poor play, and although it was harsh at time, it was fair and honest.
