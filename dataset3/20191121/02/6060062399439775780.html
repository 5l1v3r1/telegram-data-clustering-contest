Video captures partial explosion of a SpaceX Starship rocket prototype during testing

21 Nov 2019, 02:24 by Bryan Logan
  • A SpaceX prototype rocket experienced a partial explosion during a testing procedure in Boca Chica, Texas, on Wednesday afternoon.
  • The Starship Mark 1 spacecraft was undergoing a pressure test when a portion of its top appeared to burst. Heavy plumes of smoke flowed from the vehicle immediately afterward.
  • Starship is a prototype of a vehicle SpaceX wants to employ for travel to Mars.
  • "The purpose of today's test was to pressurize systems to the max, so the outcome was not completely unexpected," a SpaceX representative told The Verge.

SpaceX's Starship Mark 1 prototype rocket partially exploded during a pressure test in Boca Chica, Texas.

The incident happened Wednesday afternoon and was captured on a livestream. During the test, the upper portion of the rocket blew apart and heavy plumes of smoke flowed from the vehicle.

"The purpose of today's test was to pressurize systems to the max, so the outcome was not completely unexpected," a SpaceX representative told The Verge, adding: "There were no injuries, nor is this a serious setback."
