Unknown Man Spotted On Roof During Cowboys’ Practice Ahead of Matchup With Patriots (PIC)

21 Nov 2019, 02:36 by Darrelle Lincoln

The mind games have already begun.

On Wednesday, there was an unknown man at Dallas Cowboys practice, but he wasn’t on the field of play, but rather on a roof just days ahead of the Cowboys Week 12 matchup against the New England Patriots.

Calvin Watkins of the Dallas Morning News shared an image of an unidentified figure overlooking the Cowboys’ session from the roof of an adjacent building.
