Stacey Solomon admits she is in no rush to marry Joe Swash following secret wedding rumours

The Loose Women panellist revealed that while she would love to tie the knot to her long-term beau Joe, a wedding is low down on her priority list

21 Nov 2019, 02:32 by Irishmirror.ie

Stacey Solomon has admitted she and Joe Swash won't be walking down the aisle any time soon.

The telly star, 30, confessed she's in no rush to marry her soap star beau, 37, during a candid chat on ITV's Loose Women on Wednesday.

The loved-up couple have been together since 2015 and despite recently welcoming their first child together Rex six months ago, Stacey insisted the smitten pair feel like "everything else comes first."

Stacey told her co-stars that a wedding isn't at the top of her priorities as the panel discussed what it would take to get them down in the aisle after recent news that a man is set to marry for the tenth time.

She said: "I've always dreamed of having a wedding but now I'm older and have children and a relationship I absolutely love, I still don't feel like marriage is at the forefront for us.

"I feel like everything else comes first and I'm not ultra religious and it's not something I feel like we need to have."

Coleen Nolan then teased Stacey's response as she cheekily questioned: "Oh, but you'd like the [wedding] day wouldn't you?"

The former X Factor star laughed and replied "yes!"

Stacey and Joe were recently forced to squash rumours that they secretly tied the knot during an idyllic family holiday to the Maldives.

The Essex-born beauty posted a sweet family snap with the boys dressed in formal summer suits, and Joe wearing a shirt, amongst a romantic sunset backdrop.

Excited fans rushed to the comment section of her Instagram to ask if the couple had married while on their sun-soaked getaway.

Eagle-eyed fans who examined the photo more closely also commented on how they could see something glimmering on Joe's left hand.

The star then addressed the speculation during an episode of Loose Women, where she mocked the rumours and sat amongst confetti with wedding bells playing in the background.

Shaking her head when asked if she had married Joe, Stacey emphatically said: "No!"

"First of all can I just clarify there's no ring," claimed Stacey as she brandished her bare finger.

"Janet, would I get married without you?"

Co-host Janet Street-Porter admitted Stacey would not have got married without her as she promised to make her a bridesmaid.

Stacey and Joe have become the nation's favourite couple since they began dating in January 2016.

The couple, who have been in a serious relationship for three years since meeting on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! in 2010 when the Essex girl was crowned Queen of the Jungle, recently welcomed their first son , Rex, together in June.

Stacey was already mother to Zachary, 11, and Leighton, seven, when she met Joe - and he also has a son, Harry, 11, from a past relationship.

Throughout her pregnancy with baby Rex and since his arrival, Stacey has been praised by fans for her honest posts about pregnancy and motherhood.
