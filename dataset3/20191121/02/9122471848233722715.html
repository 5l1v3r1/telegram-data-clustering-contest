Ohio State Fans Are Trying To “Boycott” ESPN’s College GameDay

21 Nov 2019, 02:25 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN’s College GameDay will be in Columbus, Ohio on Saturday for Ohio State’s big home game against Penn State. Not everyone is happy about this.

A group of Ohio State fans are trying to get #BoycottGameDay trending on Twitter in anticipation of Saturday’s show. Buckeyes fans have had issues with ESPN for a while, but things have boiled up thanks to the Urban Meyer/Zach Smith scandal. Some fans also believe the Worldwide Leader has an SEC bias.

FOX’s pregame show, Big Noon Kickoff, will also be in town. That show features Meyer, who’s been highly praised for his work as an analyst.

Meyer has been vocal on social media this week, pushing for Ohio State fans to attend his show and not College GameDay.
