Choristers in tune for Celebration of Life concert

The school won the Devon Schools Choir competition at Southernhay Church on October 3

21 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Lewis Clarke

Choristers at Uffculme School have won the privilege of performing at a Celebration of Life concert in Plymouth.

The school won the Devon Schools Choir competition at Southernhay Church on October 3, and are now set to perform with La La Choirs concert at Plymouth Guildhall on Friday, December 6.

Only five schools from across the county were selected for the final stage.

Samantha Abrahams, musical director of La La Choirs, said: “I am so delighted that the work of the music team at Uffculme School has been showcased today and that they have won and will be performing with us in December. 

“The staff are clearly very dedicated, and it really shone through at the final.

“This event is one of the highlights of the year for La La Choirs and will be raising money for St Luke’s Hospice, a vital and much loved Plymouth charity. For an opportunity to support their work and to see La La Choirs and the marvellous Uffculme Choral Collective in action, go to  lalachoirs.co.uk/product/celebration-of-life-for-st-lukes-hospice/  and celebrate the joy of the Christmas season with us all.”
