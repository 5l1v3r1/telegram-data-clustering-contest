It’s a hattrick! Milford Care Group scoops three accolades at the Great British Care Awards 2019

The family-run group has six care homes across Derbyshire and Nottinghamshire

21 Nov 2019, 16:51 by Dylan Wiggan

The Milford Care Group is celebrating after another night of awards success.

The group, based in Belper, Derbyshire, gathered three more accolades to add to its vast collection, courtesy of the Great British Care Awards.

The family-run group, which boasts six care homes across Derbyshire and Nottinghamshire, prides itself on offering high-quality facilities alongside high-class, personal care.

The ceremony, which was held at East Midlands Conference Centre, was a night to remember, with glittering dresses and tributes to team efforts within the care industry.

The group swept up three awards -

  • Tanya Whelan, The Dementia Carer Award – Milford House
  • Lemmy Nolan, The Dignity in Care Award – Ashbourne Lodge
  • Annie Cleare, The Palliative Care Award – Ashbourne Lodge

The awards, which included nominations for the Hucknall-based care homes, Hazelgrove and Buddleia, were greatly received by the group, and it has been invited to the finals in Birmingham in 2020.

Since opening in 1985, the Milford Care Group has remained in the hands of the same two families and has never lost sight of its aim to deliver excellent care to the local communities it serves.

It remains one of the most prestigious care groups in the country and will continue to provide fantastic service for residents and families alike.
