Cardi B Shows Fans Inside Her ‘Twerk Shop’ In New Holiday-Themed Pepsi Commercial

21 Nov 2019, 16:16 by Aynslee Darmon

Pepsi is in full Christmas mode thanks to Cardi B.

In a brand new commercial for the brand’s Holiday campaign, the “Bodak Yellow” rapper stars in “A Cardi Carol”, a hip-hop reimagining of the classic tale.

The clip starts off with sharing a story from Cardi’s childhood with her sister, but when she ends up on the naughty list Cardi decides to make her own rules and deliver money to everyone.

“I want some of that Pepsi money. I need that!” she says. “Time to make it rain… deer.”

Pepsi shared the new ad to Twitter, writing, “Cardi B doesn’t care if you’re naughty or nice… everyone has a chance to get cash this holiday.”
