All the films coming to Sky Cinema this Christmas 2019

21 Nov 2019, 16:32

Sky Cinema have got a brand new selection of movies hitting their channel in December ready for Christmas, and if you’re a fan of family-friendly films or a Star Wars fan looking to relive the saga from the start, you won’t be disappointed.

With Christmas fast-approaching, Sky have got your festive film needs covered with the brand new Grinch film and feel-good movie Mary Poppins Returns, while those counting down the days until the release of the new Star Wars movie will love re-watching both classic and contemporary installments from the saga so far.

You can also expect Hollywood blockbusters, gripping dramas and a collection of much-loved musicals to keep the whole family happy during the Christmas holidays.

So what’s going to be on Sky Cinema for festive break? Here are the new releases to look forward to from Sky in December 2019.

Sky Movies new releases – December 2019

The Grinch (2018) – December 6

Not the Jim Carrey original Grinch film but the animated remake that was released last year, Sky Cinema are bringing the green festive-hating Dr Seuss character to the screen for the build up to Christmas.

With Benedict Cumberbatch voicing The Grinch and singer Pharrell Williams lending his voice as the narrator, this is a movie sure to get everyone in the festive spirit (yes, even the Ginch-iest of family members!)
