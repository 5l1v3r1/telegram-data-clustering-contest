Rosanna Davison and hubby Wes Quirke share first photo of their baby girl

21 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Ciara Walsh

Congratulations to the happy couple. 

Rosanna Davison and her husband, Wesley Quirke have welcomed their first child together.

Taking to Instagram, Rosanna shared a picture holding her little girl that the pair had via a surrogate.

The former Miss World also revealed the name of her adorable bundle of joy too, Sophia Rose Quirke.

"Today, November 21 2019 at 11.57am, our beautiful and healthy daughter Sophia Rose Quirke arrived safely into the world weighing six pounds eight ounces. She's absolutely perfect," Rosanna wrote on Instagram.

"We're completely overwhelmed with love and gratitude for our incredible gestational surrogate, who has given us the greatest gift of all and made our family dream a reality."

Rosanna and husband Wes Quirke announced they were expecting a baby in July earlier this year.

Rosanna and Wes shared some snippets of their growing baby through scan pictures but opted to keep the location and details of their surrogate private.

Last week, Rosanna jetted off to meet her little baby girl with husband Wes Quirke.

Rosanna then posted a video of her and her husband waiting and she captioned it saying: "It's due date day!! But we think she'll keep us waiting a little bit longer."

The Irish beauty has been delighting fans as she kept them up to date with everything happening with her surrogacy.

She also revealed that she won't be sharing any pictures of her daughter when she's born to respect her privacy.

Roz told the Irish Sun: "We feel very protective of her, especially as it's been such a long journey to get to this point. So I'm not sure yet how much we'll share of her on the internet - if anything at all - and we're keen to protect her privacy until she's old enough to decide what to share online."

The pair are thrilled to be able to welcome their little baby girl into the world.

A massive congratulations to the pair.
