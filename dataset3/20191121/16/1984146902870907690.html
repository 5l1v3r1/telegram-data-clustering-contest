Who's the biggest diva? Who's the joker? Christian Atsu spills the beans on his Newcastle team-mates

Atsu took part in a new 'team-mates' feature on NUFC TV with hilarious results

21 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Sean McCormick

Ever wondered who the biggest diva in the Newcastle United dressing room is?

Or who the biggest joker is? Who picks up the most fines on the training ground?

Well wonder no more as Christian Atsu has spilled the beans on his Magpies team-mates in a hilarious YouTube feature on the club's in-house channel.

So which player reminds Atsu of legendary WWE star 'The Great Khali'? Who spends the most time in front of the mirror? And who is most likely to become a manager in the future?

Here is what Atsu had to say.

Who is the biggest diva?

"[Matt] Ritchie. Always in training he is always moaning when he loses, he is fighting, so I will give it to Ritchie."

Who spends the most time in front of the mirror?

"I have seen a lot of players doing that. Maybe Fabian [Schar]."

Who would you not like to get in a fight with?

"Jamaal [Lascelles]. He is too big for me. He is like the Great Khali!"

Who takes the most selfies?

"[DeAndre] Yedlin. Always, he is always on his phone trying to post pictures on Instagram."

Who is the joker of the group?

"Yedlin again. He is always trying to crack jokes and he is always smiling in the dressing room so I will give it to him."

Who do you think will be a future manager?

"Maybe me. I have been doing a coaching course and when I have been playing I have always tried to study the game.

"So you never know, maybe I will be a future manager."

Who is the nutmeg king?

"Allan [Saint-Maximin]. This guy is a joke in training. He is very technical, he enjoys the game and he is a fantastic player."

Who is the kitman's worst nightmare?

"We have water outside so when you finish training you can wash your shoes but I have never seen Jonjo [Shelvey] wash his shoes."

Who picks up the most fines?

"Allan. He is late in training, also in meetings. But he is a great lad. It was his first time with us but now he is getting better with it."
