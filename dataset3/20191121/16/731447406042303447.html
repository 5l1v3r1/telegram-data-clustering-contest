Croatian festival Outlook Origins reveals 2020 curators

Mala, The Bug, Calibre, Flava D and D Double E are among the first names for the week-long event beginning July 30th.

21 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Marissa Cetin

The first artists for Outlook Origins 2020 are in.

The Croatian festival has named 12 curators—spanning genres such as drum & bass, dubstep and grime—for its first edition at its new venue, The Garden Tisno. They are Calibre, Mala, Lenzman, D Double E, The Bug, Flava D, DJ Storm, Goldie, Zed Bias, Fliptrix, Iration Steppas and Mungo's Hi Fi. The full lineup is still TBA.

The week-long festival begins Thursday, July 30th, and runs through Wednesday, August 5th, and will also expand to The Garden Tisno resort's satellite locations, Barbarella's (for afterparties) and St. Michael's Fortress (for an opening concert). Dimensions festival will also take place there, as both left Fort Punta Christo in 2019.
