Tammy Abraham, Mount, Chelsea stars spotted listening to Wizkid's song while training

21 Nov 2019, 16:26

- Tammy Abraham, Mount and other Chelsea stars were spotted listening to Wizkid's music

- Chelsea stars are preparing for their next Premier League game against Manchester City

- Frank Lampard and his wards are currently occupying third position on the standings

Tammy Abraham, Mason Mount and other Chelsea stars have been spotted listening to Wizkid's music while training ahead of their match with Manchester City.

Wizkid is a popular Nigerian musician who has been signing close to 10 years and the title of his music Chelsea stars were listening to is 'Don't Dull'.

Chelsea have been impressive this season under Frank Lampard and they are currently occupying third position on the Premier League standings.

A win for the Blues on Saturday, November 23, against Manchester City will send them to second position on the Premier League standings.

Manchester City on the other hand are currently occupying fourth position on the Premier League standings and a win for them against Chelsea will do them lot of good.
