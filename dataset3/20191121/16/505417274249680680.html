McIlroy Caps Off Fast Start In Dubai With Stunning Eagle On The 18th

21 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Michael Corry

Rory McIlroy finished off a blistering first round at the DP World Tour Championship in Dubai with a stunning second shot to set-up an eagle putt on the 18th.

Mike Lorenzo-Vera leads McIlroy by one shot after finishing with an incredible nine-under-par 63. The World number two looked to be struggling midway through the round hitting bogeys on the ninth, 12th and 14th holes after a promising start.

However, he mounted a late rally with two birdies and an eagle on the final three holes to set himself up with a good opportunity to take home a fifth tournament win of 2019.
