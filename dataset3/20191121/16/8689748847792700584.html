The only musician I take inspiration from is R-Kelly – Akwaboah

21 Nov 2019, 16:08 by Nana Yaw

Sarkcess Music signee, Gladstorm Kwabena Akwaboah Jnr has revealed that the only he looks up to is American R and B singer, songwriter, R Kelly.

According to Akwaboah, he takes inspiration from R Kelly due to his singing prowess and the ability to play 8 musical instruments.

Speaking in an interview with YFM, the songwriter revealed that he also plays 4 musical instruments and is still learning to play more so he can become like his mentor.

R Kelly is undoubtedly talented, and he’s written some great songs too. Akwaboah is also a good songwriter and his breakthrough came when he wrote one of Becca’s hit songs, ‘Daa Ke Daa’. Since then, he has written songs for many musicians and this passion has driven him to become a good singer as well.
