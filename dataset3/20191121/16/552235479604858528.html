TV stars coming to Howden Park Centre for variety show

Stars of the show Ian Waite and Vincent Simone will bring their double act show The Ballroom Boys to Livingston next year.

21 Nov 2019, 16:46 by Debbie Hall, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/debbie-hall/

Strictly Come Dancing stars Ian Waite and Vincent Simone are back.

The dancers will bring their double act show The Ballroom Boys to Livingston next year.

Ian and Vincent are set to return in 2020 with a brand new show after the roaring success of their five star rated 2019 tour!

The fabulous Strictly Come Dancing stars promise another wonderful evening of old-fashioned variety - dance, comedy and song!

They will bring with them beautiful costumes, gorgeous lighting and world class routines, including the Viennese Waltz, the Foxtrot, the Rhumba and of course there has to be another incredible Argentine Tango routine by the master!

 

The boys will be joined by their stunning dance partners and a world class singer.

If you enjoyed The Ballroom Boys, then make sure you don’t miss the hilarious dancing duo when they bring you Ballroom Boys Act Two at Howden Park Centre on Saturday, April 18, 2020.

 

The show promises to be just as entertaining as this year’s offering which saw Argentine Tango legend Vincent and King of the Ballroom Ian, joined by two professional female dancers and a wonderful vocalist. The night was packed with dances, songs and loads of banter and won the boys rave reviews.

These included the show being desribed as
“world class” by the The News Letter, “Five stars, a hugely entertaining night out” by the Crawley Observer and “five stars, what a show” from Elementary Whats On.

There will also be chance to snap up a VIP Meet and Greet Package.

This includes meeting Ian and Vincent for photo and autograph opportunities, plus a signed print.

The meet and greet is pre-show, 90 minutes before the show begins.

All VIP ticket holders must arrive at least 10 minutes before the meet and greet is due to start. Anyone arriving late may not be permitted.

Get ready for a high intensity evening of glitz and glamour with Ian and Vincent

The show hits Livingston at Howden Park Centre on Saturday, April 18 at 2.30pm and 7.30pm.

Tickets are priced at £32 or £30 concession.

VIP tickets are £60.

For tickets and more information on this show and other upcoming shows call 01506 340700, e-mail hpcboxoffice@westlothianleisure.com or visit http://www.howdenparkcentre.co.uk/
