7 spectacular hotels in Africa and the Indian Ocean that won't break the bank

21 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Telegraph Travel

Picture the likes of Mauritius, and 'affordable' might not be the first place that springs to mind. With Instagram-ready white-sand beaches, stylish décor and cool pools comes a hefty price tag - right? Not necessarily so, as you'll see when you peruse these seven incredible and, yes, affordable properties selected by our global expertsand all reviewed for us.

They all have a special feature: from stellar Cape Town views to bungalows in the Namibian desert and a rooftop bar at Mauritius' first eco hotel. Because paradise needn't cost the earth.

Tintswalo at Boulders
