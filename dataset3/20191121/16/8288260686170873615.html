'Class signing' - Aston Villa fans make West Ham and Sheffield United transfer demand

Aston Villa transfer news | Dean Smith's side have been linked with a move for Argentina international Nicolas Gaitan with their Premier League rivals also said to be interested

21 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans have had their say on transfer speculation linking them with a move for Nicolas Gaitan.

Gaitan is a free agent with his contract at MLS side Chicago Fire up in December although there is an option for him to stay on for the 2020 season.

Villa will be hoping to bolster their options in the New Year when the January transfer window opens, but they could sign Gaitan sooner with the 31-year-old Boca Juniors academy product set to be unattached when his contract expires in Chicago.

West Ham United and Sheffield United are also said to be interested in the former Dalian Yifang man, with Manuel Pellegrini's side reported to have held talks with Gaitan's representatives .
