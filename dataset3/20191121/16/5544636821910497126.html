Popular Nigerian Pastor To Launch ‘Mobile Network’ In UK

21 Nov 2019, 16:21 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

Apostle Johnson Suleman of the Omega Fire Ministries (OFM) is set to launch a “mobile network” in the United Kingdom (UK), Concise News reports.

This news medium understands that Apostle Suleman announced this in a tweet on his handle on Thursday.

Apostle Suleman did not, however, provide details about the project as he called on his followers to pray for him.

“Pray along with us as we launch our mobile network in [the] United Kingdom…coming soon,” he tweeted.
