Batwoman films in city

21 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Elana Shepert/Vancouver is Awesome

After filming in New Westminster on Wednesday, the Warner Brothers TV show “Batwoman” will be filming in Downtown Vancouver Friday night.

A resident notification letter from Renraw Production Services states that filming will take place at 1000 W. Hastings Street and 300 Burrard Street from 5:30 p.m. until 7 a.m. Saturday. A minimal crew will be on location throughout the day on Friday to prepare for the shoot.

The Wednesday and earlier shoots have included crash scenes, smoke and weapons.

Friday's scenes call for more weapon play and vehicles in motion.
