Luka Doncic’s Halftime Stats vs. The Warriors Are Insane

21 Nov 2019, 01:55 by Andrew Holleran

Luka Doncic is having himself a night. The Dallas Mavericks’ young star has put up an insane stat line against the Golden State Warriors this evening.

The 20-year-old phenom is in the thick of the NBA MVP race through the first month-plus of the season. Based on his play against Golden State tonight, it’s easy to see why.

Doncic outscored the entire Warriors team in the first quarter of tonight’s game. He didn’t stop scoring in the second quarter, either.

Through one half of play, Doncic has:

  • 33 points on 10 of 11 shooting
  • 6 assists
  • 5 rebounds
  • +36 in 17 minutes of play
