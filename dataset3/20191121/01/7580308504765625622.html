Bayelsa qualify for National Division One Handball League Kaduna semifinals

21 Nov 2019, 01:45 by Saxone Akhaine, Kaduna

Bayelsa State has qualified for the semifinals of the on-going National Division One Handball League Kaduna, after drawing with Benue Buffalos 27-27 points yesterday.

The Bayelsa team had earlier beaten Ogun, Kano and Benue, but they lost to Delta, before securing a semifinal ticket after the match with the Buffalos.

In the last group match at the competition holding in Kaduna, the Bayelsa team came from behind to level with the Benue Buffaloes, who fell to make the semifinal.Speaking shortly after the match, the Coach of Bayelsa United, Opuene Robot, said the most difficult game for his team was the encounter with Delta State, adding, however, that he was sure of defeating Delta in future encounters.
