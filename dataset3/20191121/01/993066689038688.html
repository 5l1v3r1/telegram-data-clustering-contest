Death of 68-year-old man 'treated as suspicious'

21 Nov 2019, 01:00 by CLAIRE SIMPSON

THE death of a 68-year-old man in Co Down is being treated as suspicious by police.

The man's body was found at his home in Birch Drive in Bangor on Tuesday evening.

Police have launched a probe into the circumstances of the pensioner's death.

It is understood the man lived alone in the house.

Neighbours are believed to have phoned police when the 68-year-old failed to collect his bin on Tuesday.

A post-mortem examination is to be carried out to discover the cause of death.

A police spokeswoman said they are not releasing any other details at this time.
