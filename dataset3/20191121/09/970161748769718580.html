Ariana Grande confirms she's Bernie Bro after he visits her backstage on tour

21 Nov 2019, 09:08

Ariana Grande couldn’t contain her happiness as Bernie Sanders made a surprise backstage visit to her Sweetener tour.

The Thank U, Next singer shared two snaps of herself and the Democratic candidate at her latest performance, in Atlanta.

And the 26-year-old vowed she would ‘never smile this hard again’ after their meeting.

Alongside the photos, she told her Instagram followers: ‘MY GUY. Thank you Senator Sanders for coming to my show, making my whole night and for all that you stand for !!! @headcountorg and I are doing our best to make you proud.

‘I’m so proud of my fans for paying attention and getting involved. We adore you!

‘Also… I will never smile this hard again.’

Replying to her post, Bernie wrote: ‘Thank you Ariana Grande for not only being a wonderful entertainer, but also for being such an outstanding advocate for social justice.

‘We must all be prepared – like Ariana – to fight for everyone who is struggling.’

Right on cue, fans went into total meltdown over the upload, with some begging the pair to collaborate on a track.

Which is something we all now need to hear.

‘So much respect for you being a Bernie babe,’ a follower said.

‘The collab we were waiting for,’ an Instagram user commented.

‘MY FAV AND MY FAV OMG SCREAMING,’ one shouted.

While another joked: ‘Imagine Bernie just bumping it to Thank You Next and 7 Rings.’

Ariana has a lot to celebrate at the moment, after she was nominated for a whopping five Grammys ahead of next year’s awards.

The Into You singer has been tipped for best solo performance and record of the year for 7 Rings, while Thank U, Next earned her nominations for album of the year and best pop vocal album.

She was also mentioned in the best pop duo/group performance for Boyfriend, with Social House.

But Ari faces some stiff competition in the form of Lizzo, Billie Eilish, Lil Nas X and H.E.R.
