Toyota Is Ending Its Weird Holdout From Racing Games

21 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Luke Plunkett

As one of the biggest car companies in the world, it was weird when Toyota simply stopped licensing its vehicles to racing video games back in 2017. It was even weirder that the company, despite its size, never actually explained why that decision was made.
