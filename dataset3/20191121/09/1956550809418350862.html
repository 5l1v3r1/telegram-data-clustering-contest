Brand-new restaurant Argentina Grill to open at La Mer

The new licensed venue will use traditional asado cooking methods

21 Nov 2019, 09:27 by Amy Mathieson

Dubai’s La Mer is a top spot for dining, and now there’s one more restaurant to check out.

Argentina Grill – which does what it says on the tin – is due to open today (Thursday November 21) at the seaside shopping and dining destination.

Time Out Dubai’s been for a sneak peek behind the scenes, and it’s looking like an exciting new Latin American spot. Not only is it at a super beachside location, Argentina Grill also has views of Downtown Dubai and Burj Khalifa from its terrace.

There’s already one branch of Argentina Grill at The Pointe on Palm Jumeirah, but now Jumeirah residents are closer to the authentic cooking.

The restaurant uses traditional asado grill cooking methods and the kitchen boasts three Argentinean parrillas and a Josper. It also claims to have the largest meat ripening chamber in Dubai.

The interiors are bright and colourful with vibrant art, while we’re promised Latino music to get diners in a party mood.

Dishes are a Spanish-Italian combination and will include a selection of steaks, seafood, salad and burgers.

Argentina Grill is spread out over two floors with the aim of going from chilled-out beach spot in the day to lively Latino spot by night. Plus the venue is licensed so expect Latin American beverages and a Latino party every night from 8pm until 10pm.

Looking for a seaside steak? This could be one to check out.
