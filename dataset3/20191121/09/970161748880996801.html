Irina Shayk tries to hold back Lea as daughter attempts to run for it

21 Nov 2019, 09:39

Little Lea De Seine can’t be tamed, no matter how much Irina Shayk tried to hold back her two-year-old daughter with Bradley Cooper.

During a New York City jaunt Lea appeared to attempt to make a run for it down the pavement, forcing model mother Irina to place a hand on her shoulder and hold her hand.

Nice try, Lea – you can make your bid for freedom next time.

The duo were lapping up the fresh air in the city when the adorable tot – who was wearing a big, black puffer coat over a cute pink outfit – marched on ahead of her Victoria’s Secret model mum.

Irina, who was wearing a grey beanie and green coat over tracksuit bottoms with black boots, wasn’t letting go of her daughter, as they then walked along hand in hand after the prison break.

It was all a bit of fun, as Irina smiled to photographers as they kept on their way, with Irina looking ruddy chuffed with her lot in life.

Their NYC outing comes after Lea had double Halloween – dressing up as a Mrs Doubtfire double with dad Bradley, before donning some Minnie Mouse ears for costume number two.

Bradley, 44, split from his partner Irina earlier this year, following their four-year relationship, with the pair coming to a custody agreement about little Lea.

It was originally claimed the 33-year-old model was keen to stay in New York, while the actor wanted to move to LA for work.

However, according to TMZ, they managed to work things out and decided to both stay in the Big Apple, sharing custody.

The website was told by sources that Irina and Bradley agreed to joint legal and physical custody of their only child, although have decided not to opt for a formal, written custody agreement.

Meanwhile, the pair are both apparently open to dating, with a source telling Us Weekly: ‘They want to date, and won’t be shy about meeting people or being set up.

‘They’re closing a long chapter of their lives, but they’re ready to see what’s next.’
