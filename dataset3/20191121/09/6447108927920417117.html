Xbox One’s Black Friday sale now on for Xbox Live Gold subscribers

21 Nov 2019, 09:50 by Stefan L

Black Friday’s shopping bonanza comes earlier every year, and Microsoft have already kicked off their huge Black Friday sale on the Microsoft Store… so long as you’re an Xbox Live Gold subscriber.

Much like last year, Gold members can access the Black Friday deals three days early, while everyone else will be allowed to buy discounted games from 24th November. The sale runs through to 2nd December, AKA Cyber Monday.

Some of the highlights include Resident Evil 2 Remake for £14.84, Control at £32.49, Crash Team Racing at £22.74, Destiny 2: Shadowkeep for £17.99, Dirt Rally 2.0 for £16.49, Doom at a mere £4.49, Gears 5 for £24.99, Plants vs. Zombies: Battle for Neighborville for £26.24, Rage 2 for £16.49, and plenty more besides. Let us know the bargains you find.

Last week at XO19, Microsoft also detailed their wider Black Friday deals for consoles and services. Prices are being slashed on consoles, controllers, and you can grab three months of Xbox Game Pass Ultimate for just £1/$1.

We don’t have the Xbox One Store prices tabulated, but you can see the full list of Xbox One and Xbox 360 content and the discount percentage in the following tables.
