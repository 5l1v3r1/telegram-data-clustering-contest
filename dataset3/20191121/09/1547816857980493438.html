Shah Rukh Khan Is Winning Hearts With His Congratulatory Message For An Acid Attack Victim On Her Marriage

21 Nov 2019, 09:45 by Lubna Khan

Shah Rukh Khan, who is known for his philanthropic efforts through Meer Foundation, recently took to Twitter to congratulate an acid attack victim Anupama, on her marriage, and his post on Twitter is winning a lot of hearts

Shah Rukh Khan may have been missing from the big screen for a long time, but he is quite active on social media and keeps sharing something or the other with his fans on Twitter. The actor was last seen in Aanand L Rai’s film Zero co-starring Anushka Sharma and Katrina Kaif, however, the film did not perform well at the box-office. While we wait for him to return on the big screen with a hit, the superstar has been blessing us with glimpses of what he has been up to, on his social media. Shah Rukh Khan recently took to Twitter to congratulate an acid attack victim Anupama, on her marriage, and his post on Twitter is winning a lot of hearts.

 Shah Rukh Khan, who is known for his philanthropic efforts through Meer Foundation, now congratulated an acid attack victim named Anupama, who is associated with the foundation, on her marriage. Shah Rukh Khan shared pictures of the couple and also a selfie with her to wish her on this special occasion. He wrote, “Congratulations and my love to Anupama as she starts on this new journey of life. May it be filled with love light and laughter.
