Early morning shots fired

21 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Rob Gibson

The RCMP's Emergency Response Team responded to reports of a shooting at a Langley home just before 6:30 a.m. Thursday morning.

CTV News Vancouver is reporting that RCMP officers surrounded a Langley home on 48 Avenue near 272nd Street where an ambulance and ERT officers could be seen.

One man has reportedly been taken to hospital with non-life threatening gunshot injuries.

No arrests have been made so far.
