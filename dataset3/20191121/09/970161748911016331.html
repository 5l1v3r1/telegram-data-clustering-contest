Prince Andrew has been given permission by the Queen to ‘step back from public duties for the foreseeable future’ following criticism over his relationship with Jeffrey Epstein.

The move comes after the Duke Of York’s interview on Newsnight to discuss his relationship with the convicted sex offender last weekend drew widespread condemnation – leading to many charities reviewing their association with him.

He said in a statement that it was ‘clear’ that the former association had become ‘a major disruption to my family’s work and the valuable work going on the many organisations and charities that I am proud to support.

‘Therefore, I have asked Her Majesty if I may step back from public duties for the foreseeable future, and she has given her permission.’

The Duke said in the interview that his continued friendship with Epstein following his release from prison nine years ago had ‘let the side down’ for the royal family.

He also claimed that he has ‘no recollection’ of meeting Virginia Giuffre (then Roberts), who said she was forced to have a sexual encounter with him when she was 17.

How old is Prince Andrew?

Prince Andrew is 59 years old.

He was born on 19 February 1960 in the Belgian Suite of Buckingham Palace.

He is the second son of the Queen and Prince Philip, and was the third child born to them.

Their eldest, Prince Charles, was born on 14 November 1948, while only daughter Princess Anne was born on 15 August 1950.

The Queen and Prince Philip’s youngest son, Edward, was born on 10 March 1964.

Prince Andrew was the first child born to a reigning monarch since Queen Victoria’s youngest child Princess Beatrice was born in 1857.

Who is Prince Andrew’s wife?

Prince Andrew married Sarah Ferguson on 23 July 1986, with the couple being named the Duke and Duchess of York.

What is Prince Andrew’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth the Duke is said to be worth around £34m.

He owns two properties including a chalet in Switzerland ski resort Verbier as well as the Royal Lodge in Windsor Park.

The Duke was a midshipman in the Navy before being promoted to Lieutenant in 1984, and also worked as a flight commander and pilot before he he retired in 2001.

He later became the UK special representative for Trade and Investment, a position he held until 2011.

How much does Prince Andrew get paid?

Prince Andrew is reportedly given a tax-free payment from the Queen each year of around £249,000.

He’s also said to receive a £20,000 pension from the Navy.
