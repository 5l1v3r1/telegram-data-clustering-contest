Christian Pulisic injury latest ahead of Chelsea's trip to Man City

21 Nov 2019, 09:35

Christian Pulisic is a major doubt for Chelsea’s Premier League clash with Manchester City on Saturday as he continues recovery from a hip injury.

The American could not play in his country’s fixtures against Canada and Cuba over the international break, staying in London instead.

USA manager Gregg Berhalter said ahead of those matches in the CONCACAF Nations League: ‘Christian is officially at this stage ruled out.

‘We just got that information. This was a collaborative decision with the club looking after the best interest of the player

‘These decisions are always difficult because this group and the coaching staff want nothing more than to have Christian here, but it was a risk we weren’t willing to take at this time.’

Pulisic also tweeted ahead of the Canada clash: ”Good luck to the @USMNT tonight against Canada! Really sad that I’ve had to sit out these important games because of injury but I’ll be supporting from London. Let’s get the W.’
