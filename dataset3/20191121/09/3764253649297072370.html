Jose Mourinho: Tottenham's manager promises to bring passion in new role

21 Nov 2019, 09:33

- Jose Mourinho has expressed happiness about his Premier League return as Tottenham boss

- The Portuguese's appointment was confirmed on Tuesday, November 19, after Pochettino's sack

- The Special One has now promised to wheel in real passion into his new role

Newly appointed Tottenham boss, Jose Mourinho, has delightedly bared it all while expressing optimism about his appointment, saying he would wheel in "passion, real passion" into his new role.

The Portuguese manager made a surprising yet sensational Premier League return after his appointment was confirmed by Spurs on Tuesday, November 19, ending Mauricio Pochettino's five-year-spell with the club.
