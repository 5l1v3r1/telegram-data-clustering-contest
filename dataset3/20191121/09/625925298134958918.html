EPP to decide on expelling Fidesz by end of January

21 Nov 2019, 09:17 by EUobserver

The newly-elected president of the European People's Party (EPP), Donald Tusk, said on Thursday that group of 'wise men' evaluating the EPP membership of Hungarian ruling party Fidesz will present a report before the end of the year. "Then I will start an intensive consultation [and] we will have a decision at the end of January," said Tusk.
