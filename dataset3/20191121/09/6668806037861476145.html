Priyanka Chopra proves she is Jonas Brothers’ biggest cheerleader

21 Nov 2019, 09:55 by Filmfare

The official Grammys 2020 nomination list has come out and the Jonas Brothers have received a nomination in the same. Needless to say that Priyanka Chopra, who is married to Nick Jonas, celebrated the big news with a shout out to the Jo-Bros in a Tweet. The diva tweeted, "So proud of you, Jonas Brothers! I love you Nick Jonas, Joe Jonas, Kevin Jonas" and shared a montage of the Jonas Brothers from their studio.
