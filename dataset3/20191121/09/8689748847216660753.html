The f00lish broke fans are the ones who buy data to insult artistes on social media – Kwaw Kese

21 Nov 2019, 09:53 by Adu Sarpeah

Kwaw Kese’s music career is seriously bleeding, therefore, it’s no surprise that he has become an “apostle of ethics” lately on social media taking into consideration his utterances.

This time around, he has taken a swipe at music enthusiasts or better still fans of artistes who take delight in lambasting musicians on social media for no apparent reason.

To the best knowledge of Kwaw Kese in a footage he posted on Instagram, the f00lish broke fans are the ones who buy small amount of data to insults artistes on social media.

“If you can buy data and come on social media just to diss people and compare other acts to others and insult them then you’re a fool. You can do better than that”, he said.

Kwaw Kese, therefore, noted that it’s prudent to rather support the work of an artiste rather than comparing artistes and pitching them against each other.
