Cardi And Nicki Minaj Might Finally Make Up — Under This One Condition

21 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Dylan Fisher

Things between Cardi B and Nicki Minaj have been on a downhill slope for quite a while now, and there has been no indication that the situation is going to pick up anytime soon.

That is, until recently. There have been various reports indicating that both stars might be looking to put their differences aside, finally.

It is not clear what exactly might have prompted this, but it does not seem like most of their fans are too concerned with that particular detail either — for the most part, people are just happy that the two have decided to clear up the air between them.

However, it seems like there might be a few obstacles in the way of their reunion, more specifically the egos of both stars.

Cardi B recently made a post on social media in which she noted that she did not want to end the year with any feuds, prompting an unnamed person to apologize to her.

And while some saw that as an indication that she wants to put their differences in the past, others have been critical of the way Cardi B has chosen to approach this situation, pointing out that she could be the bigger person and apologize herself, because she also had a thing or two to be sorry about.

However, knowing the way Cardi B has acted in the past, and her generally massive ego, it does not seem like there is any real chance of things improving between these two.

Fans had many reactions to the post.

One person had this to say: “I’m not sorry that I always comment on your post and that I think you are amazing. I’m not sorry that I love your music and your style and Sorry, not sorry. 🤣🤣🤣”

This follower wrote: “I’m sorry for your troubles, and you are forgiven. Love u❤”️

A social media user shared: “Lol !! YUP! 🗣The power of forgiveness is great! Why is it every time cardi post someone always trying to argue with you about Nicki in the comments? 😭”

This person stated: “It’s funny to see everyone commenting on you are showing Luv n support & she just did a video before this – 2 post back talking about people commenting and arguing and so on for likes.”

Nicki seems to be in a more positive space at the moment.
