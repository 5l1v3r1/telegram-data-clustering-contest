Wendy Shay in trouble for missing a line in a popular gospel song

21 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian Afrobeat artiste, Wendy Shay is really making a name for herself since her introduction into the music scene a year ago.

She has been involved in a lot of controversies and has constantly been a victim of social media trolls. From her ‘rotten buttocks’ photo to her ‘raw face photo’ and latest to hit the media space is a line she missed in a popular Gospel song which has landed her in trouble.

In a short video, the Rufftown Record Label signee for some obvious reason missed some lines in the popular gospel song, ‘Ancient of Days’.

In the video, Wendy Shay could be heard singing “Ancient of days how old as you are,” instead of the original lyrics which reads “Ancient of days as old as you are”.

Ghanaians on social media couldn’t fathom why how the singer could miss the lyrics in such a popular song.
