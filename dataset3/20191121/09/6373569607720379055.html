Admission prices revealed for Plymouth Argyle's FA Cup clash at Bristol Rovers

Tickets have gone on sale for the second round tie at the Memorial Stadium on December 1

21 Nov 2019, 09:25 by Chris Errington

Tickets for Plymouth Argyle’s FA Cup second round tie away to Bristol Rovers have gone on sale to season ticket holders.

Ryan Lowe’s Pilgrims will travel to the Memorial Stadium on Sunday, December 1 for a 2pm kick-off.

That follows Rovers’ 1-0 first round replay win at National League high-fliers Bromley on Tuesday night.

The priority one selling period for Argyle season ticket holders began at 9.15am this morning.

Under-16s will be admitted for £1 for the FA Cup clash, which will be all-ticket for the Green Army.

Reserved seating in the away south stand at the Memorial Stadium costs £15 for adults and £7 for over-65s.

Tickets for the uncovered away terrace are priced at £10 for adults and £5 for over-65s.

The sale dates are as follows:

Priority One (Season ticket holders) 9.15am Thursday, November 21

Priority Two (Flexi-Ticket Holders, 1886 Members and Big Green Lottery Members) 9.15am Monday, November 25

General Sale: 9.15am Wednesday, November 27.

Supporters’ coaches will leave from outside the Devonport End at 9.30am on the day of the match.

The cost is £18 for 1886 adult members, £15 for 1886 junior members, and £23 for non-members.
