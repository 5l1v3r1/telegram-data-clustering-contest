Telia Finland CEO moves to head of Telia Norway as Foss leaves group for new challenges

21 Nov 2019, 09:05

Telia Company said that Telia Finland CEO Stein-Erik Vellan will take over as CEO of Telia Norway on 04 December, succeeding Abraham Foss, who will leave to seek challenges elsewhere. The role of Finnish CEO will temporarily be held by Jonas Bengtsson, group general counsel and head of corporate affairs. Heli Partanen, head of consumer business in Finland, will become acting vice-CEO with full operational responsibility, and will be leading day-to-day business in Finland.

Vellan joined Telia Company in January 2017 and has previously worked in many different management positions in telecoms globally for almost 20 years. Telia group CEO Christian Luiga said he is confident that Vellan will make Telia Norway even more competitive in the company years, after making the operator more relevant to its Finnish customers.

Luiga thanked Foss for his contribution, including integrating Tele2's Norwegian business, Phonero and Get/TDC, and doubling service revenues and increasing EBITDA by 2.5 times.

Recruitment of a new CEO for Telia Finland will begin immediately, the operator said.
