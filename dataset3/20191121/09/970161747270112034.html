Why is Rachel Riley's Jeremy Corbyn tweet so offensive?

21 Nov 2019, 09:51

Rachel Riley has angered a lot of people with a custom made t-shirt calling the Labour leader a ‘racist endeavour’.

The Countdown host tweeted a picture of her wearing the top on the night of ITV’s leadership debate as she took a stand against Jeremy Corbyn.

The leader of the opposition raised eyebrows and was accused of lying by Jewish Labour Movement national secretary Peter Mason.

Ms Riley told followers she didn’t feel comfortable knowing her workplace was ‘to be full of racists tonight’, but was met with uproar over her choice of clothing.
