Camila Cabello has announced a massive Irish gig next summer

21 Nov 2019, 09:32 by Fiona Kelly

Camila Cabello has announced a headline show at Dublin's 3Arena next summer.

The Havana hitmaker is taking her The Romance Tour around Europe and is bringing the tout to Dublin on June 8, 2020.

The three-time Grammy nominated singer's new album, ROMANCE is due for release on December 6 and features smash-hit single Señorita with Shawn Mendes (her other half, who she  went viral with this year after the pair shared the world's most disturbing kiss and thought it would be a good idea to put it online - we'd share it here but thankfully he has deleted it and also, we don't want to spoil your breakfast).

Camila  released her debut solo album, CAMILA in January 2018 to rave reviews. It debuted at #1 on the Billboard 200 chart at the same time as her single “Havana” Reached #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, making Camila the first solo artist in nearly 15 years to reach the #1 spot on the Hot 100, Billboard 200 and Artist 100 charts in the same week.

At the end of 2018, Camila was nominated for two Grammy awards, Best Pop Vocal Album and Best Pop Solo Performance for Havana.

Beginning her official solo career after four years in Fifth Harmony, Camila released Havana in August 2017. Havana became the most streamed song of all time by a female artist and was the number one song of 2018 globally.

Camila is currently the most listened to female artist in the world on Spotify and broke the record for the most monthly listeners for a female artist in Spotify history.

Tickets for her Irish gig go on sale next Friday, November 29 at 9a from Ticketmaster.
