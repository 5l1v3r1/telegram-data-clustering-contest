Two cyclists hit in separate crashes in Greater Victoria this morning

21 Nov 2019, 09:22 by Cindy E. Harnett

Two cyclists were struck by vehicles in separate crashes in Greater Victoria this morning.

One cyclist was struck in the downtown Victoria intersection of Broad and Yates streets at about 6:30 a.m. The cyclist sustained minor injuries.

The driver failed to yield at a stop sign, said Victoria Const. Cody Lapierre.
