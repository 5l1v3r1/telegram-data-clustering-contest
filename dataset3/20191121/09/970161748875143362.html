Harry Style sings while flying through the air and we can't even

21 Nov 2019, 09:47

Harry Styles has once again proved that his talents know no bounds as he was spotted flying through the sky on a zipline while singing for James Corden’s The Late Late Show.

Ever the performer, the One Direction star, who only just gave us a taste of his incredible comedic skills on Saturday Night Live, joined the infamous Gavin & Stacey co-creator in Beverly Hills to shoot scenes for an upcoming episode.

Dressed in black trousers, a cream jumper, and long black coat, Harry was seen graciously suspended mid-air with beautiful forestry in the background as James tugged at his leg.

Meanwhile, a whole host of characters were seen raising their arms in the artist’s legs as they paraded in some questionable attire in the street.

Eventually, Harry landed back on solid ground again and was photographed showcasing his vocals with his feet firmly on the road.

This time around though, he was seen wearing a pair of incredible black balloon trousers and black shirt.

James joined Harry once again but stood directly behind in a bright yellow lycra bodysuit with huge corrugated wings attached to the arms.

Yep, we have no idea either.

After the pair had wrapped the scenes, which will no doubt be entertaining, they were captured sharing a little embrace behind-the-scenes.

Honestly, these two!

Just yesterday, Watermelon Sugar singer left us seriously impressed (much) Harry confessed his love for Shania Twain.

Without hesitation, the star admitted: ‘I’m like a massive Shania Twain fan that’s probably my most surprise, but it’s not surprising, she’s amazing.’

What a legend.

The Late Late Show airs on X in the US.
