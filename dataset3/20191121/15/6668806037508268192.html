Toofan: Farhan Akhtar and Mrunal Thakur celebrate 50% shoot completion

21 Nov 2019, 15:14 by Filmfare

Farhan Akhtar, who has been making waves on the internet with his body transformation pictures and videos as the boxer in Rakyesh Omprakash Mehra’s directorial, Toofan is a happy soul these days. Apart from being appreciated for his physique, the actor is celebrating the wrap up of another schedule of his film.

Farhan took to social media to announce that 50 per cent of Toofan has been shot. He even posted pictures of the three cakes while in another picture posted by Farhan, one can see him and Mrunal, Rakyesh Omprakash Mehra, Paresh Rawal and the rest of the team cutting the cakes. Farhan wrote, "Too ho gaya. Fan bacha hai. #halfwaycelebration #cakesaysitbest."

Toofan is written by Anjum Rajabali, and jointly produced by Farhan Akhtar, Ritesh Sidhwani, Mehra, P. S. Bharathi and Rajiv Tandon. The film also stars Paresh Rawal who will portray Farhan's coach in the film. The movie is all set to hit theatres on 2nd October 2020.
