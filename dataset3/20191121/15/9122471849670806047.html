Antonio Brown Shares New Workout Video, Hints At Return

21 Nov 2019, 15:57 by Chris Rosvoglou

With only a month remaining in the regular season, it appears Antonio Brown isn’t ready to give up on a potential return.

This year has been quite a spectacle for Brown. He basically forced his way out of Pittsburgh, and then after things didn’t go well in Oakland he demanded the team to release him. Brown then signed a one-year deal with the New England Patriots. Many people thought he’d find a solid role with the defending champions, but he didn’t even last two weeks.

New England released Brown after he appeared to threaten a sexual assault accuser. The All-Pro wide receiver recently met with the NFL to discuss these claims.

Although there’s no update on Brown’s future in the NFL just yet, the outspoken wideout shared a workout video for his followers on Twitter.

Brown posted a video of him running routes with the following caption: “Staying ready excited to get back to the field and inspire others!”
