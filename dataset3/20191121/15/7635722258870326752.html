Footpad gets season off to a winning start at Thurles

21 Nov 2019, 15:23 by Will O'Callaghan

2018 Arkle champion Footpad is back to winning ways, easing to a ten-lengths success on his seasonal reappearance in the Listed Chase at Thurles.

The Willie Mullins-trained seven-year-old won four Grade Ones as a novice chaser but struggled for form last year.

Despite sitting in tenth for much of the race, the 8/11 favourite showed his pace to pull away from the field and easily see off a late rally from Mala Beach.

Mala Beach was second with 2018 Ryanair Chase winner Balko Des Flos back in fourth.
