I almost gave up in the early days of Mavin - Donjazzy shares throwback photo of his team

21 Nov 2019, 15:31

- Top Nigerian talent manager and music producer, Donjazzy, has shared a bit of his past struggles with fans

- The music mogul explained that there was a time he nearly gave up on his record label

- Donjazzy also shared a throwback photo with his Mavin crew which included Tiwa Savage, Prince, Wande Coal and Dr. Sid

Being in the limelight is easy but maintaining the shine is the hardest past. The Nigerian entertainment industry has seen the rise and fall of many talented individuals due to quite a number of reasons.

However, the successful entertainers who have maintained their relevance in the industry sometimes reveal that it has not always been a smooth journey.

Just recently, top music manager and producer in the country, Donjazzy, took to his Twitter page to speak on how he nearly gave up on his record label, Mavin. According to Jazzy, he made sure to keep pushing as he prayed that fans reap the fruits of their labour.

Donjazzy shared this piece of information alongside a throwback photo of his Mavin crew which at the time included Tiwa Savage, Wande Coal, Prince and Dr. Sid.

In his caption, the producer wrote: “Everyone has their goal. I almost gave up on mine in the early days of MAVIN. As you can see in this throwback pic. I looked fed up lol . But man had to keep pushing. May you reap the rewards of all your hard work last last o #JustLikeBurna”
