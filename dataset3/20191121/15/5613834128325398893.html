‘Lair’ Trilogy to Debunk the Supernatural

21 Nov 2019, 15:24 by Brad Miska

Planned as a trilogy, filming has begun in London on UK filmmaker Adam Ethan Crow’s low-budget horror film Lair, reports ScreenDaily.

In the film, US actor Corey Johnson plays a professional debunker of the supernatural whose beliefs are put to the test when his friend claims he was possessed by a demon. An unsuspecting LGBTQ family becomes embroiled in the drama.

UK actress Alexandra Gilbreath is co-starring with Israeli actor Oded Fehr (Resident Evil).

Crow is also developing both a prequel called The Bone Harvest and as a yet-untitled sequel.

Lair is produced by Crow’s Ditto Films with Shelley Atkin. The project was in development at Fox but was put into turnaround following Disney’s acquisition of Fox.
