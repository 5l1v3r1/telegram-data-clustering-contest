Hilarious throwback video of Okyeame Kwame rapping surfaces online

21 Nov 2019, 15:36 by Lizbeth Brown

Ghpage.com has chanced upon a hilarious video of Ghanaian rapper and songwriter Okyeame Kwame that shows that there is indeed hope for everyone.

Okyeame Kwame also known as Mr. Versatile was singing some verses from a song that featured the Late Daasebre Gyamenah.

The versatile rapper who is popularly known for his good rapping skills delivered a good performance that the crowd loved.

The video which has surfaced online has gained a lot of reactions from social media users and fans of Okyeame Kwame.

Okyeame Kwame shot to fame after he joined a hiplife group known as “Akyeame” where they recorded several hit songs but parted ways in 2004.

Okyeame Kwame also won the Artist of the year at the 2009 Ghana Music Awards after describing himself as the Best Rapper Alive (BRA).

Aside from music, Okyeame Kwame launched his “Versatile Show” in 2012 where he blends his music with drama and poetry.
