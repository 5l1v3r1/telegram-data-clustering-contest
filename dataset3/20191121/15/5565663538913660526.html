BREAKING: 6th Avenue in Bulawayo renamed Emmerson Mnangagwa Avenue

21 Nov 2019, 15:57 by Mandla Ndlovu

The Cabinet has approved new names for some roads in Bulawayo and Harare. Cabinet announced during the post cabinet briefing that 6th Avenue in Bulawayo has been renamed Emmerson Mnangagwa Avenue

Other heroes who got roads named after them are John Landa Nkomo, Queen Lozikeyi and South Africa's Oliver Tambo.
