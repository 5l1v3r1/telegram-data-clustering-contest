Unauthorised Vehicle Tried to Enter White House Complex - US Secret Service

Earlier, the federal law enforcement agency reported that it was responding to “a suspicious vehicle” jointly with the Washington DC police department.

21 Nov 2019, 15:45

An unidentified vehicle has attempted to enter the White House illegally, the US secret service revealed via Twitter.

According to the service, the vehicle attempted to enter the complex by tailing another legally entering the premises via a checkpoint. The service added that the vehicle was later pulled over and the individual driving it was immediately detained and taken into custody.
