Gardai keeping 'open mind' after discovery of man's body at house in Killarney, Co Kerry

The heartbreaking discovery was made at around 10pm last night

21 Nov 2019, 15:43 by John Patrick Kierans

A garda investigation is underway after the body of a man in his 50s was found at a house Kerry last night.

The heartbreaking discovery was made at a home in Torc Terrace, Killarney, at around 10pm.

Gardai say they are keeping an open mind, and that the results of a post mortem examination will determine the course of the investigation.

The property was sealed off and a technical examination is underway.

The man's body remains at the scene.

A spokesman said: "Gardaí are investigating the discovery of a body at a house in Torc Terrace, Killarney, Co Kerry that occurred on the 20th November, 2019.

"Shortly after 10pm, Gardaí received a report of a sudden death at a house in Killarney.

"Gardaí attended the scene and discovered the body of a man in his 50s. The scene has now been preserved for technical examination by the local Scenes of Crime Unit.

"The body of the man remains at the scene and the office of the State Pathologist and the local Coroner have been informed.

"Gardaí are keeping an open mind in relation to this matter however, the outcome of the post mortem will determine the course of the investigation."
