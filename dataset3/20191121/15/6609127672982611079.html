UP FOR GRABS

Skint Katie Price’s ‘filthy’ pink Land Rover up for sale after it’s repossessed amid bankruptcy battle

21 Nov 2019, 15:41 by Amanda Devlin

KATIE Price's “filthy” pink Land Rover is up for sale after it was reportedly repossessed.

The 41-year-old - who has been battling bankruptcy - is known for her fleet of brightly-coloured vehicles.

But this week her flashy Land Rover ended up at a car auction and sold to a a used car dealership in Essex.

The pink Urban vehicle had the sign "repo" - short for repossession - on the windscreen.

A witness at the dealership told the MailOnline: "It’s a very striking car but we didn’t know straight away that it belonged to Katie Price.

"But then one of the buyers told us that it was hers. We couldn't believe how filthy it was."

Katie was banned from driving for two years last month after refusing to tell cops who was behind the wheel when her pink Range Rover crashed last year.

She failed to appear at the Bexley Magistrates Court sentencing and was slapped with six penalty points and the ban.

She's now without her famous flashy car, but it's not yet known how much the Range Rover will go for at the dealership.

It's been a dramatic year for Katie, who narrowly avoided bankruptcy in December 2018 when the tax man accepted her plan to repay her debt.

She is being pursued by creditors and entered into a Individual Voluntary Agreement over her estimated £250,000 debts to the High Court.

The reality star could be made bankrupt before Christmas after allegedly failing to keep up with her monthly payments.

In January this year, Katie attempted to sell-off her second pink car for £90,000.

She later told Flex FM she wanted rid of her pink vehicle fleet because they "get me me into trouble".

The Sun Online has approached Katie's PR for comment.
