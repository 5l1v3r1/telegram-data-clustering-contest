Love Island's Malin tips Jacqueline to win I'm A Celeb after Myles romance

21 Nov 2019, 15:59

Myles Stephenson has become a firm favourite in the I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here camp, alongside Caitlyn Jenner and Roman Kemp.

And it turns out the Rak-Su singer, 28, has a famous ex, after it was revealed he previously dated Malin Andersson – and our minds are truly blown.

However, despite their past, the Love Island star-turned body positivity icon is rooting for EastEnders actress Jacqueline Jossa to go far in the ITV jungle.

Speaking exclusively to Metro.co.uk about her history with the X Factor winner, she told us: ‘I dated him [about] five years ago,’ she laughed.

‘That was years ago. I’m not going to say anything else.’

Fair enough.

Discussing this year’s show, she continued: ‘I like Jacqueline Jossa, I think she’s going to be cool. And the rugby player, James Haskell, he seems quite funny.

‘Everyone is on there to watch Caitlyn Jenner though, right?’

Especially now that Caitlyn has started dropping those Kardashian secrets…

Myles and Jacqueline have a bizarre connection in the jungle – as the EastEnders star’s husband, Dan Osborne, is rumoured to have been unfaithful with Gabby Allen, who previously dated the X Factor winner.

Seriously, we cannot keep up.

Dan and Gabby have fiercely denied any cheat claims, maintaining they are just friends.

However, an insider has now claimed Myles is ‘willing’ to clear anything up that Jacqueline has questions about.

‘Myles would be willing to tell Jacqueline everything – and answer any questions she might have,’ a source told The Sun.

‘Myles will tell Jacqueline everything Gabby told him. It’s a good thing Gabby’s gone to Thailand for a month because she might find it uncomfortable watching.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here continues daily, at 9pm, on ITV.
