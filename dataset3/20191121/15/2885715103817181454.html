Takeaways from India squad vs West Indies: Selectors go for experience over promise

Ins and Outs of the India squad: The selections for the upcoming ODIs and T20Is vs West Indies suggest experienced campaigners are being given a chance to reclaim or consolidate their spots. Some youngsters, who are in fine form in domestic cricket, need to bide their time further.

21 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Sports Desk

The India squads for the upcoming ODIs and T20Is against West Indies were announced on Thursday. A three-match T20I series will begin from December 6, followed by a three-match ODI series.
