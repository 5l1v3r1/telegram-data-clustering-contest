Hilary Duff confirms major character will return for Lizzie McGuire reboot

21 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Ciara Walsh

This is what dreams are made of. 

Disney+'s Lizzie McGuire has confirmed that a classic character is coming back for it.

Last August, Hilary Duff appeared at the Disney D23 Expo to announce a brand new sequel series of Lizzie McGuire, which will see her reunite with the show's creator Terri Minsky.

Now, Duff has announced Adam Lamberg will reprise his role as Gordo in the upcoming Lizzie McGuire reboot and our hearts can't take it.

The pair shared the news in a video posted to the official Disney Plus Twitter account, they captioned the post: "Reunited and it feels so Gordo!"

The actress opens the clip by introducing herself to viewers, "It's Hilary Duff here from the set of Lizzie McGuire.

"I'm so excited to be back, I'm so excited for you guys to come along on this journey with Lizzie. Obviously the show would not be the same without her family."

Hilary lists the names of those set to reprise their roles in the Disney Plus series.

Jake Thomas returns as her brother Matt, Hallie Todd is back as her mother Jo and Robert Carradine will also reprise his role as her dad Sam.

From behind the camera, we hear a voice shouting, Wait! Wait." Adam Lamberg then appears in front of the camera, "Fam's not complete without me. Gordo's back."
