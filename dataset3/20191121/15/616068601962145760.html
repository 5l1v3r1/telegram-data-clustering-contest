Gesamtkunstwerk?

21 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Lindsay William-Ross / Vancouver Is Awesome

A massive spinning chandelier is being installed under Vancouver's Granville Street Bridge.

The four-by-six-metre kinetic sculpture is the work of Abbotsford-born artist Rodney Graham and is affiliated with the Vancouver House development.

Vancouver House was designed by Bjarke Ingels, and the project overall was posited as being “Gesamtkunstwerk” or a “living sculpture.”

To that end, Graham’s public art piece will slowly rotate and rise over the course of 24 hours, then once a day release and dynamically spin back to its starting point.

Approved by Vancouver city council in December 2015, installation for the $1.2 million chandelier was expected to happen in 2017 or 2018.

However, installation finally began this month and is expected to take another couple of weeks.
