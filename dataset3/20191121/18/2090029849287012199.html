Suspected serial killer, David West makes u-turn, pleads not guilty

21 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Suspected serial killer, Gracious David West, alleged to have killed more than a dozen Women in different hotels in Rivers State and others, has made a u-turn and pleaded not guilty to the 10-count murder charge leveled against him.

Recall David West had earlier pleaded guilty to nine of the 10-count charge leveled against him.
