Iowa Football Releases Awesome Senior Day Graphic

21 Nov 2019, 18:03 by Chris Rosvoglou

This weekend, Iowa’s football program will honor the seniors on its roster when it hosts Illinois. With kickoff still a few days away, the Hawkeyes posted an awesome graphic on social media for their upperclassmen.

Iowa is coming off its biggest win of the season. Last Saturday, Kirk Ferentz led the team to a statement victory over previously undefeated Minnesota.

Next up for Iowa is a clash against Illinois on senior day.

The Hawkeyes made a graphic for their seniors which depicts them as members of The Avengers.

Since there are 19 seniors on the roster this season, it definitely took time for the program’s social media team to put this together. Fortunately for Iowa, there are a lot of Marvel characters to choose from.
