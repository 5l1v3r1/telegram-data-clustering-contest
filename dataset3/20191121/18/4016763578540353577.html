Here’s Everything We Know About Harry Styles’ New Album ‘Fine Line’

21 Nov 2019, 18:24 by Caitlin Kelley

Harry Styles’ highly anticipated sophomore album, Fine Line is on the way. Ahead of the drop, he’s already released two singles: “Watermelon Sugar” and “Lights Up.” This will be his first full-length since his self-titled debut in 2017.

The One Direction member established himself as a classic rock-indebted soloist with his last outing. His debut was well-received, with 230,000 equivalent album unit sales in its first week, and “Sign of the Times” made it all the way to No. 4 on the Hot 100. Now that Fine Line is on the way, Genius rounded up everything we know about the project ahead of its release.

The Release Date

The LP will arrive on Dec. 13.
