2017 World Series Wikipedia Page Got An Appropriate Edit To Troll The Houston Astros (PIC)

21 Nov 2019, 18:02 by Darrelle Lincoln

With every passing day, there seems to be even more uncovered cheating that the Houston Astros engaged in, especially during the 2017 season where they won the World Series.

It seems obvious at this point that the team cheated, so we should expect a punishment who draft picks taken away, fines, suspensions, etc.

Until that happens, the team and the fan base has to deal with non-stop slander and jokes, which has taken someone to the Wikipedia page for the 2017 World Series and giving it a savage update.

The World Series MVP is now listed as the “Centerfield cameraman (Houston).”

It was Astros outfielder George Springer who was awarded MVP honors after the Astros defeated the Los Angeles Dodgers in seven games.
