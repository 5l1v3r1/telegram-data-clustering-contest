Ohio State Releases Epic Hype Video For Game vs. Penn State

21 Nov 2019, 18:46 by Chris Rosvoglou

Ohio State is on the verge of clinching a spot in the Big Ten Championship with a win this weekend against Penn State. Ryan Day’s squad probably doesn’t need much motivation heading into this game, but they definitely received a boost this afternoon.

The Buckeyes will have Chase Young back for this showdown. He missed the past two games due to a suspension from the NCAA.

Penn State enters this weekend as massive underdogs against Ohio State. Nonetheless, these rivalry games usually come down to the wire.

With only two days remaining until Ohio State takes the field for its last home game of the season, the team released a hype video on Twitter.
