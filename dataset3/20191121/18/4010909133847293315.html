Snapchat's now shows you how you'd look like when you're old

21 Nov 2019, 18:56

Snapchat recently released a new filter on their app, which shows how you would look like if you were several years older. By older, I meant by at least 30 to 50 years older to the point grey hairs as well as a moustache might show up.

If you are interested in trying it out, download the Snapchat app on either the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store and select the “Time Machine” feature.
