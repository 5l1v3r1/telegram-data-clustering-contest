Seven fire crews battle large blaze at industrial unit

21 Nov 2019, 18:02 by Adam Howlett

Seven fire crews are at the scene of a large blaze at an industrial unit in Eye.

Firefighters were called to the scene, in Literator Way at around 1.20pm today, Thursday November 21.

According to a spokesman for Suffolk Fire and Rescue Service, seven crews from both Suffolk and Norfolk battled the blaze.

A spokesman for the fire service said: "This was a building fire on an industrial estate.

"The commercial building affected was 50m by 80m."

Crews extinguished the fire by 3.30pm and the scene was left in the care of Suffolk Constabulary.

No one was injured as a result of the incident.
