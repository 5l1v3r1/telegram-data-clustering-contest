Appeal over missing Afghan national Aman Brid Singh Chopra in Clare

21 Nov 2019, 18:40 by Jack Quann

Gardaí have issued an appeal for a missing person in Co Clare.

They are seeking the public's help in tracing the whereabouts of Aman Brid Singh Chopra.

He was last seen in the Kilkishen area on Thursday November 14th.

He is described as a juvenile, 5'8'' in height, of thin build with brown eyes and black hair.

Anyone with any information that can assist in locating Aman is asked to contact Gardaí in Ennis on 065-684-8100, the Garda Confidential Line oat 1800-666-111 or any Garda station.
