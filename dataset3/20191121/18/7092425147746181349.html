SuperValu launches massive gin sale just in time for Christmas

21 Nov 2019, 18:34 by Ciara Walsh

SuperValu's gin sale is the news we didn't know we needed. 

From November 28 to December 11, you'll be able to get premium gins at pocket-friendly prices just in time for the festive season.

The run-up to Christmas is always a popular time for entertaining and the sale is just the perfect time to stock up on some of your favourite tipples.

Bar carts are more popular than ever and setting yours up with balloon glasses, paper straws and fancy tonic water is a fun way to start prepping for any Christmas gatherings you might have planned.

The Irish retailer is offering discounts on a number of gin brands, some of which are available to SuperValu stores, and the deal are seriously good.

The gins on sale are available in SuperValu branches across Ireland and you can also avail of the offers online.
