Inside I'm A Celebrity's Roman Kemp's lavish romance with girlfriend Anne-Sophie

The Capital radio star shares his luxury lifestyle with girlfriend Anne-Sophie, and it includes red carpet events, parties and lavish trips

21 Nov 2019, 18:14 by Brogan-Leigh Hurst

Roman Kemp boasts a millionaire lifestyle which he's left behind for the I'm A Celebrity Jungle.

The 26-year-old Capital radio DJ is used to the showbiz world, red carpet events and a jet set lifestyle, which he shares with girlfriend Anne-Sophie Flury.

The power couple have been dating since February 2018 and prove they have a loved-up romance by their cosy Instagram snaps.

Anne-Sophie - who is reportedly a neuroscientist - was born in Bern, Switzerland.

Roman's intelligent girlfriend lived and worked in Hong Kong when their romance began and they have since spent most of it long distance.

When the pair of them have spare time on their hands, the couple enjoy travelling together and it frequently makes an appearance on their social media.

During the start of their romance, they met up around the globe including Ibiza, Mexico, Venice and Miami.

As far as their luxury breaks away go, the sweet couple recently visited Formentera in Spain and went skiing in Anne-Sophie's native place.

The hunky presenter raves about his beau on Instagram very often.

 

One post he shared said: "I get told on a daily how much I'm punching... trust me I know... so proud of you and can’t wait to have you back home".

And another one gushed: "She's gone back to Hong Kong but had the most amazing birthday weekend with my girl I love you and thank you for all the presents xxx".

As if the trips away aren't enough, Roman and Anne-Sophie also attend the BAFTAs and film premieres across the city, as well as fun nights out together.

But as Roman packed and prepared to head Down Under for I'm A Celebrity, his girlfriend shared the very romantic gift he left behind for her while he's thousands of miles away.

 

Revealing a different side to the Capital presenter, his partner explained how romantic Roman went to great lengths to ensure she won't be forgotten about while he's in Australia.

"He's left me a two minute video for every day that he can't speak to me," Anne-Sophie revealed. "I'll get to watch one of them every morning.

"He filmed them before he left and put them on a USB stick so I'll have to try and keep it to just one a day," she added.
