Valve Releases Announcement Trailer for Upcoming VR Title 'Half-Life: Alyx' (UPDATE)

A VR experience that serves as the first ‘Half-Life’ game in 15 years.

21 Nov 2019, 18:24 by Jeff Yeung

UPDATE: Valve has released an announcement trailer for its upcoming VR game Half-Life: Alyx. This new Half-Life title takes place between the events of Half-Life and Half-Life 2, and centers around the titular Alyx and her father Eli. Half-Life: Alyx is scheduled to release in March 2020.
