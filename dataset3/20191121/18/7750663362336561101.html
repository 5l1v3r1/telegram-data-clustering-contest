AC Milan, Napoli 'lining up January move for Lucas Torreira'

AC Milan and Napoli are reportedly weighing up January offers for Arsenal midfielder Lucas Torreira.

21 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Matt Law

The Uruguay international has made 65 appearances for the Gunners since arriving from Sassuolo in the summer of 2018, contributing four goals and five assists in the process.

The 23-year-old was linked with a move away from the Emirates Stadium during the summer, though, and there remains speculation surrounding his future.

According to The Sun, both Milan and Napoli want to sign the South American in the January transfer window on an 18-month loan with a view to future transfer.

The report claims that Torreira remains 'unsettled in the Premier League' and will consider any offers that arrive for his services in the early stages of 2020.

The midfielder has scored twice in 15 appearances for the Gunners during the 2019-20 campaign, including once in nine Premier League outings.
