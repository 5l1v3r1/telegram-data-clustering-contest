Microsoft delays the launch of Surface Earbuds to 2020

21 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Pradeep

During the October hardware event, Microsoft surprised everyone by announcing the new Surface Earbuds. Surface Earbuds is a truly wireless earbuds that works with any Bluetooth device and comes with dual mic array and 24 hours of battery life. It will also work with digital assistants such as Siri and Google Assistant. The responsive touch surface enables intuitive gestures like tap, touch, and swipe.

During the event, Microsoft announced that Surface Earbuds will be available for $249 later this year. Today, Microsoft’s Panos Panay tweeted that they are delaying the launch of Surface Earbuds to Spring 2020.
