‘Half-Life: Alyx’ Revealed in New Trailer, Behind-The-Scenes Video!

21 Nov 2019, 18:12 by Mike Wilson

They said they’d do it, and they finally did. Half-Life: Alyx is a thing. Valve officially revealed the Steam VR game via a new trailer and a new video where Geoff Keighley chats it up with the Valve devs on the return of Half-Life.

Alyx is also available now on Steam for pre-order with a 10% discount. On top of that, if you own Valve Index, you get the game for free! Even sweeter for those in the modding community: The Source 2 SDK will ship with the game. Half-Life: Alyx will also be making an appearance at this year’s edition of The Game Awards on December 12.

The story for Half-Life: Alyx has Alyx Vance (who made her first appearance in Half-Life 2 taking on the Combine between the events of Half-Life and Half-Life 2.
