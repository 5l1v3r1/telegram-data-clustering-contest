Anantapur team wins Under-17 Inter-District Football tourney

21 Nov 2019, 18:15 by Staff Reporter

Anantapur reigned supreme at the Andhra Pradesh U-17 Inter-District Football Tournament as they defeated Visakhapatnam to become the champions of the tournament which was held in the city from November 18 to 21.

In what was considered a nail-biting finale, both teams advanced to the penalties round as the match ended in a goalless draw at the end of the full-time. In the penalty round, Anantapur defeated Visakhapatnam by 3-2.

The districts of Krishna and Chittoor, which lost to Visakhapatnam and Anataapur respectively, in the semi-finals, competed for the third spot, wherein Chittoor team defeated Krishna by 1-0.

With the completion of the tournament, 40 members from all the district teams will be selected and sent to a coaching camp, where 20 members will be selected to represent the State at the Junior National Football championship for B.C. Roy trophy, conducted by the All Indian Football Federation at Mandi, Himachal Pradesh, from December 6 to 18.
