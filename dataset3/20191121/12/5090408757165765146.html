Robert Pattinson revealed he once gatecrashed a Harry Potter premiere “looking disgusting,” after eating pizza and drinking beer all day.

The “Twilight” star was staying at a friend’s house in Los Angeles when he read online that “Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix” was premiering at the Graumans Chinese Theater that day.

Pattinson, who rose to fame playing Cedric Diggory in the previous film, “Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire,” only featured as a flashback in “Order of the Phoenix,” but decided to email his agent and ask whether he could still check it out.

“I’m just walking down Hollywood and it was a long walk I can’t even remember, like a 45-minute walk, and it was boiling hot.

“I’d literally been just eating pizzas and drinking beers for the whole summer and looked disgusting.”

The 32-year-old told the HFPA In Conversation podcast, he thought his casual stroll to the premiere would be a relatively low-key affair, until he reached Grauman’s Chinese Theater and saw the swathes of Harry Potter fans.

“I got to the crowd and there’s thousands and thousands of Harry Potter fans, I don’t even know why I didn’t think about it, I just thought it’s cool I’ll just walk there.”

After pushing through the crowd, the main cast saw their former co-star appear out of nowhere uninvited.

“They were all kind of surprised I was there and were just like do you want to go to the red carpet and I was like err what.”

Laughing through the interview, Pattinson said his agent saw the photos online the next day and was shocked to see the star not only walking the red carpet but looking “like an unbelievable mess.”

“They were like, ‘What are you doing? Why are you being photographed like this? You look like you’re out of your mind!'”

And as pictures last a lifetime, the actor said he’ll never live down his sweaty film premiere gatecrash.

“My agent still sends them to this day … I was just pouring with sweat, you can just see the photos I look horrendous.”
