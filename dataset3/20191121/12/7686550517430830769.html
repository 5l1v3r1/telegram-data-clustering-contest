How to bag a stay at a room overlooking Wembley for the UEFA Euro 2020 final

You'll shower in the players’ changing rooms and have a breakfast at the pitch

21 Nov 2019, 12:01 by Julie Delahaye, Victoria Jones

A chance to stay in a room at Wembley Stadium overlooking the UEFA Euro 2020 final is up for grabs.

The website Booking.com is a partner of UEFA Euro 2020 and they are opening a sports suite for the occasion complete with a bed, football-inspired decor and seats right in front of the huge floor-to-ceiling windows directly overlooking the pitch.

It will be open for a stay on Sunday, July 12 for the final of the tournament so you can catch all of the action, from the comfort of your room, the Mirror report.

But first, the guests will take a VIP guided tour of Wembley Stadium followed by watching the UEFA EURO 2020 final from the suite’s balcony.

Then, there will be a "luxurious" overnight stay as the pair wake up to a shower in the players’ changing rooms and have breakfast on the pitch.

You'll be hosted by football legend David Villa for the entire match too.

The Spanish footballer said: "Wembley is one of the most iconic venues in world football, for players and fans alike, so the chance to stay in the Stadium Suite the night of the Final and experience the electric atmosphere is truly a once in a lifetime opportunity!

"I look forward to meeting the lucky winners on July 12 next year."

It's not just something you can book, unfortunately. But those aged 18 or over who book a stay via Booking.com before the March 31 (and stay before this date) can opt to be added into the prize draw to try and bag the coveted Euro 2020 Final Stay.

Other prizes include a stay for the semi-final on the 8th July 2020, as well as an array of hospitality packages.

You can find out more about the Ultimate Overnight Football Experience, including full competition terms and conditions for the competition on the the Booking.com Stadium Suite listing.

Booking.com is the Official Accommodation and Attractions Booking Partner of UEFA EURO 2020.
