Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello did the sweetest thing ever and sent Demi Lovato flowers this week!

Demi, 27, shared the gesture on her Instagram Stories with her fans.

“This is so f***ing sweet and cute.. and I also feel old now but it’s totally worth it because this is so thoughtful. Thank you guys so much!,” Demi addedon the picture. “I love you both.”

While Demi isn’t much older than Shawn and Camila (only six years), the gesture was just so sweet!
