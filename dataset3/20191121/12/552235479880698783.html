The hilarious Ryan Jack throwback as Rangers star's early days revisited in classic picture

Former Aberdeen team-mate Fraser Fyvie shared the old snap on Twitter.

21 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Record Sport Online

There's nothing like a good old throwback to get your pals blushing.

Before Ryan Jack was captaining Aberdeen or starring under Steven Gerrard at Rangers, he was a fresh-faced youngster in the Dons' youth academy.

The midfielder emerged through the ranks at Pittodrie as a kid, alongside Fraser Fyvie.

And those days were revisited by the Cove Rangers player in the form a classic picture.

It shows the pair in their early Aberdeen days, grinning for the camera.

Much has changed since then, clearly, although the pair are still identifiable despite the picture's age.

Fyvie wrote: "What about that for a #ThrowbackThursday guess who"

Fyvie became Aberdeen 's youngest-ever player when he debuted at 16 in 2009, while Jack made his league bow a year later in 2010 against, funnily enough, Rangers .

Fyvie departed for Wigan Athletic in 2012, with Jack remaining in the north east until 2017 before eventually sealing his switch to Ibrox.
