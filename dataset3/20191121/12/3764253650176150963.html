2baba speaks about state of the nation, says criminals have hijacked the country

21 Nov 2019, 12:15

- Nigerian singer 2baba has come out to speak about the state of the nation

- According to the veteran singer, the Nigerian system is a joke and the country has been hijacked by criminals

- 2baba’s take generated mixed reactions from his followers on social media

Popular Nigerian singer 2baba Idibia has recently used his social media platform to speak about the state of the nation and factors that are hindering the development of the country as a whole.

The legendary musician shared his opinion in a post on micro blogging platform, Twitter. 2baba said the Nigerian system is a total joke and the country has been hijacked by criminals.

He also mentioned that it appears men and women in the country have deliberately dedicated their time to make sure that the nation does not progress.
