In a move that Michael Scott would deem worthy of a Dundie Award, The Office fan Amy Lewis has created Inside Dunder Mifflin: The Ultimate Fan's Guide to The Office ($11), a book that acts as a tribute to every employee who's passed through the Scranton branch. Perfect for anyone who's still obsessed with The Office (aka one of the best shows of all time), the 120-page hardcover guide lets you learn about office romance with Jim and Pam, how to crush your enemies with Dwight Schrute, how to dress for success with Andy Bernard, and more!

In addition to incredibly useful office survival tips, the book also includes season overviews, episode dissections, cast profiles, and a "What's Your Management Style?" quiz. It even answers an age-old question: "Who would win in a fight: Jim and Pam or Phyllis and Bob Vance?" (My money's on Phyllis and Bob.) If you're in need of a White Elephant gift, or just a superfreaking amazing gift for your best friend, then shop the Office-inspired guidebook ahead.
