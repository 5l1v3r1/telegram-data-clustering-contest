Richa Chadha approached for another sports film

21 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Shyama Sudra

Already set to been seen sharing the screen with Kangana Ranaut in a kabaddi film titled ‘Panga’, news has it Richa Chadha has been approached for another sports film that’s on the cards.

The Asian Age, reports suggests that though the script is still in its early stages. She is yet to give her nod to the project.

Chadha has a whole host of projects in the pipeline including writing her own book and gearing up for the second season of digital show ‘Inside Edge’.
