Osinbajo, governors hold NEC meeting

21 Nov 2019, 12:56 by John Ameh

John Ameh, Abuja

A meeting of the National Economic Council has started in Abuja.

The governors of the 36 states are attending the meeting.

It is presided over by Vice-President Yemi Osinbajo.

It opened at 12.35 pm at the Council Chambers of the State House.
