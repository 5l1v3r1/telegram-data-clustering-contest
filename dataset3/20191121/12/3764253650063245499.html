Jose Mourinho vows to win trophies with Harry Kane at Tottenham

21 Nov 2019, 12:37

- Jose Mourinho has enjoined Harry Kane to stay and win trophies with him at Tottenham

- The Portuguese coach was recently appointed Tottenham manager replacing Pochettino

- Mourinho is said to have sent a text to Harry Kane telling him he is the best striker

Jose Mourinho has reportedly contacted English striker Harry Kane telling him that he is the best forward in Europe and vowed to win trophies with him at Tottenham.

The Portuguese recently replaced Mauricio Pochettino at Tottenham and he is preparing his squad for their coming Premier League game against West Ham United.

Despite reaching the Champions League final last season, Tottenham have endured a shocking start to the ongoing Premier League season sitting 14th on the standings.

And one of the big tasks given to Jose Mourinho is to ensure he convinces Harry Kane not to leave the club following the departure of Mauricio Pochettino.
