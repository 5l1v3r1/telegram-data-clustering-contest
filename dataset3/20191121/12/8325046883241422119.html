You can now book a stay at a dog-friendly mini-hotel in Scotland where every room has a beer fridge in the shower

21 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Emma Taylor
  • Craft beer brand BrewDog is launching a mini-hotel concept for city center destinations called BrewDog Kennels, with bookings now open.
  • The dog-friendly accomodation will have shower beer mini-fridges, similar to their DogHouse Hotel in Columbus, Ohio.
  • Each guest will receive a welcome beer at check-in, while the hotel can also provide dog beds and good-boy treats.

Scottish craft brewery BrewDog is now accepting reservations for its latest hotel concept that includes dog beds for your pup and shower beers in every room.

At the Aberdeen Kennels, each guest will receive a welcome beer at check-in and all rooms come with a beer fridge to dip into when showering.

If you’re travelling with a furry four-legged friend, the hotel can also provide dog beds and good-boy treats upon request.

French-press coffee, a record player, vinyl, and a guitar also feature in every room type.

Inspired by its DogHouse Hotel concept in Columbus, Ohio, the new BrewDog Kennels aim to bring the same spirit to a series of mini city-center hotels in some of the world’s busiest destinations.

The first BrewDog Kennels will launch in the brand’s home city of Aberdeen, Scotland, where bookings are now open, as well as in Columbus, Ohio.

The company also plans to open sites in London, Manchester, Paris, and Berlin.

The BrewDog Kennels Aberdeen will also host beer-themed experiences and excursions, such as an accompanied visit to its brewery in nearby Ellon or taking part in “Beer School” at its Castlegate bar below the hotel.

BrewDog cofounder James Watt commented: “Our BrewDog Kennels are a revolution in accommodation, sparked by too much time spent in immediately forgettable hotel rooms.”

“We firmly believe that where you stay in a new city should be your home from home, so have brought the spirit of our DogHouse Hotel and its reverence for great beer to a new series of mini-hotels.”

Prices for rooms start from £75.
