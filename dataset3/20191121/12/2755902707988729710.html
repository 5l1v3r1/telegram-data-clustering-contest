21 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Philip Mangwiro

‘Sanii Makhalima Vatiregerera,’ A Song About The Zimbabwe Situation

Contemporary musician Sani Makhalima has released a politically charged song on the state of affairs in Zimbabwe. The song ‘Vatiregerera’ speaks on the plight of ordinary citizens.

Makhalima leaves nothing out from poverty to the illicit things Zimbabweans have resorted to out of desperation to survive.
