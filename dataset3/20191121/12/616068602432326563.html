Eastside Rd. to be closed

21 Nov 2019, 12:35 by Colin Dacre

Motorists are being advised Eastside Road near OK Falls will be closed for most of next week for maintenance. 

DriveBC says Eastside Road will be closed between McLean Creek Road and Maple Street between Monday Nov. 25  at 7 a.m. and Thursday Nov. 28 at 4 p.m.

Motorists will have to detour around the closure using McLean Creek Road or Highway 97.
