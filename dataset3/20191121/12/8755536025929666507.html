Ian Wright snapped at Andrew Maxwell on I’m A Celeb – and people are NOT happy about it

The stars came to blows during last night's show

21 Nov 2019, 12:15 by Clodagh Meaney

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! stars, Ian Wright and Andrew Maxwell, came to blows during last night’s show.

The ex-football player lost his cool with the Irish comedian when he offered him more dinner.

Ian complained he was hungry, and when Andrew pointed out there was a crocodiles foot left over for him to eat, the ex-Arsenal player said: “I really honestly don’t want no more, [James] needs it more than I do, so what he can have another one if he wants.”

Andrew insisted he should eat more, to which Ian snapped backL “I said I don’t want no more f***ing crocodile bro, I don’t want no more.”

Taking to Twitter, fans of the show branded Ian a “diva” and a “proper p***k.”
