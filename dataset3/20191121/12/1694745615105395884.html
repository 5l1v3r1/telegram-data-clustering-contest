Sofia Richie Used Her Baby Photos to Inspire Her New Blond Hair Color, and It's Perfect

21 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Jesa Marie Calaor

Sofia Richie's Instagram grid is a photo journal of her travel adventures, fashion looks (especially when it comes to bikinis), and covetable hairstyles. Regarding the latter, the 21-year-old changed up her hair yet again with a natural blond color that is perfect for Winter.

She visited hairstylist Tauni Dawson at the LA-based Nine Zero One salon to refresh her hair color for the cooler months with a surprising source of inspiration in hand. "We referred back to her photos of her as a child to help pull inspo of what we wanted to work toward because her natural base color was so amazing," Dawson said. She added lowlights "to add dimension to [Richie's] ends," blended in her old highlights, and toned her hair to a more neutral hue. Dawson also trimmed one inch off of Richie's ends.

Prior to her new color, she was a warmer shade of blond, with her natural brown hair color peeking from the roots. See Richie's new hair look ahead.
