Janhvi Kapoor and Kartik Aaryan wrap up the Punjab schedule of Dostana 2

21 Nov 2019, 12:29 by Filmfare

Coming together for the first time on the big screen are Kartik Aaryan and Janhvi Kapoor in Karan Johar’s Dostana 2. The film also stars debutant Lakshya who already is gaining a lot of attention. Since the last couple of days, Kartik, Janhvi and Lakshya have been shooting in Chandigarh and Patiala for the rom-com. Thanks to social media, we have been getting several pictures of the star cast from the sets of the film and it has got us really excited.

After spending all these weeks there, the trio has finally wrapped up the Punjab schedule of the film and celebrated it with a cake and special bash. Check out their pictures from the wrap-up party below.

Dostana 2 is a sequel to Priyanka Chopra Jonas, John Abraham and Abhishek Bachchan starrer Dostana. Produced by Karan Johar, the project is helmed by Collin D'Cunha and slated to hit the screens in 2020. It was recently when the makers announced that the Stree actor Abhishek Banerjee has joined the lead stars to play an important part in the film.
