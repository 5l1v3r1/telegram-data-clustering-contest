Video: Shahid Kapoor is going all out to prep for Jersey

21 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Filmfare

Shahid Kapoor has proved his mettle as an actor with several power-packed performances. His last outing at the movies created a massive stir at the box-office and now all eyes are set on his upcoming projects. Shahid will be seen in the official Hindi remake of Telugu film Jersey.  The sports-drama will have Shahid essaying the part of an aspiring cricketer who decides to step into the sport at a later stage in his life in comparison to usual newbies in the sport.  The actor is busy prepping hard for the film and this morning he gave his fans a glimpse of it through his social media account. 

Shahid posted a video of hitting the ball out of the park as he practiced the game. Well, looks like this dapper dude is set to impress one and all yet again with a killer performance. 

Jersey will star Super 30 actress Mrunal Thakur alongside Shahid, we are kicked for this one, aren’t you too?
