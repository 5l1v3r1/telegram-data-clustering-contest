Cristiano Ronaldo marriage: Has the Juventus star secretly wedded his partner?

21 Nov 2019, 12:21 by Archie Blade

Cristiano Ronaldo marriage: Has the Juventus star secretly wedded his partner? The Italian media have had their say and Ronaldo has responded.

Italian gossip magazine Novella 2000 has claimed that Cristiano Ronaldo has married his long-time partner Georgina in a secret Moroccan wedding. The magazine has promised to reveal more details of the wedding. They have also quoted an unnamed source, described as close to the couple saying:

“I understood everything on August 29.

“That day Cristiano went to Madeira with his lawyers and changed his will to protect Georgina as well

“Before that his assets were administered by his mum Dolores.”

However, representatives of the Juventus star have categorically denied the news of Cristiano Ronaldo and his marriage to Georgina Rodriguez per TMZ. Sources close to the footballer on Wednesday night have also described the secret wedding reports as “fake news”.

We’ll be married one day, for sure!

Earlier in February, Georgina had admitted that she would love to become Mrs Cristiano Ronaldo in the future. However, she denied being already engaged to the star. While speaking to Spanish magazine Hola! she said:

‘Every couple wants to get married and I would love to personally.

‘Right how we have priorities and a lot of responsibilities. Although in the future it would be marvellous to become Cristiano’s wife.’

Cristiano Ronaldo echoed her views in an interview with Piers Morgan a few weeks ago. He said:

“She helped me so much. Of course, I’m in love with her.

“We’ll be (married) one day, for sure. It’s my mum’s dream as well.”

The couple, who have been dating since 2015, have a daughter Alana Martina, who turned two earlier this month. Georgina also helps raise Ronaldo’s other three children.
