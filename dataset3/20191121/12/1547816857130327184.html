21 Nov 2019, 12:44 by Lubna Khan

Brad Pitt was recently spotted at an art gallery with Alia Shawkat, who plays the role of Maeby Funke in Arrested Development. While they were at the same gallery, they both chatted with other people present at the art gallery

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, once Hollywood’s most loved couple, left their fans shocked with the news of their divorce three years ago back in 2016. The two had been married for two years and Angelina shares six children with Brad Pitt namely, Zahara, Shiloh, Vivienne, Maddox, Pax, and Knox. Brad and Angelina’s divorce is always in the headlines for some reason or other, since the couple is still sorting out their differences over the divorce settlement. However, looks like Brad might be moving on and was spotted hanging out with Arrested Development star Alia Shawkat.

Instead, they can be seen chatting with other people present at the art gallery.
