Kim Kardashian West reveals KKW Beauty sales have DOUBLED

The entrepreneur has had a busy year of beauty launches

21 Nov 2019, 12:16 by Clodagh Meaney

Beauty entrepreneur and reality TV star Kim Kardashian West has revealed that sales for her brand KKW Beauty have doubled in the last year.

It comes after her younger sister Kylie Jenner sold a majority stake in her beauty company, Kylie Cosmetics, for $600,000 million to COTY.

The sale of 51% of the company, values the business as a whole at $1.2 billion.

In an interview with WWD.com to promote her The Artist & Muse collection with her longtime make-up artist, Mario Dedivanovic, Kim revealed she’s very proud of her brand.

“I’m very active during sales and launches, we’ve doubled in a year,” she explained.

“It just makes me proud of the brand and makes me want to push to do exactly what I want to do and continue to make the products that I love and stay true to the authentic collaboration.”

The busy law intern also recently launched her own shapewear brand, Skims, and a new perfume with her sisters Khloe and Kourtney.

The Artist & Muse collection launches on the KKW website tomorrow.
