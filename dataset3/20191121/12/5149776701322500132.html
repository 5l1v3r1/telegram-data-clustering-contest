East Midlands Airport passengers evacuated from terminal

A video on social media shows a large number of passengers standing outside

21 Nov 2019, 12:34 by Ben Reid

Passengers inside East Midlands Airport were evacuated after a fire alarm was triggered.

A video on social media shows a large amount of passengers standing outside the terminal at around 12pm on Thursday, November 21.

A spokesman for the airport confirmed to Nottinghamshire Live that the fire alarm was a false alarm and was set off by accident.

All passengers have since been allowed back into the terminal, they confirmed.
