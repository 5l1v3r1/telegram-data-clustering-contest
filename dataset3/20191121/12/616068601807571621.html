Crews put out hot wheels

21 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Darren Handschuh

Vernon Fire Rescue were called to action Wednesday night following a report of a car fire.

At 10:52 p.m., fire crews responded to a report of an SUV on fire on Commonage Road.

Firefighters put out the fire quickly and were done on the scene by 11:30 pm.

There was no threat to any structure.

RCMP also attended the scene and are now investigating the circumstances.
