Here are details of Nagarjuna’s role in Ayan Mukerji’s Brahmastra

21 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Filmfare

Brahmastra has been the talk of the town ever since the movie was announced. Helmed by Ayan Mukerji, the film is currently in post-production and will release in the summer of 2020. The movie stars Ranbir Kapoor, Alia Bhatt, Amitabh Bachchan, Mouni Roy and Shah Rukh Khan in a cameo. The movie that was earlier supposed to hit the screens on December 19, 2020 was pushed to an undisclosed date in summer 2020 during to some extensive VFX works.

The film also stars South superstar Nagarjuna who extensively shot for his part at the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, the Chet Singh Fort and on the banks of Ganga for the film. A soruce close to the filmmakers revealed, “Nagarjuna’s character, along with his students, undertakes an expedition to restore an ancient temple along the banks of the Ganga.”

The sources further added that an important event leads Shiva (Ranbir Kapoor) and Isha (Alia Bhatt) to Nagarjuna's character and the mystical events of the films roll. He said, "The adventure kicks-off after all of them come face-to-face in Varanasi. A high-octane action sequence featuring them was shot earlier in the year.”

While the makers are trying their best to keep things under wrap there was a speculation that Amitabh Bachchan will be essaying the role of Lord Brahma and Nagararjuna who probably returns to Bollywood after a decade with be seen potraying the role of Lord Vishnu. But a new recent report suggested that he will be instead playing an Archaelogist in the film. The makers however haven’t confirmed on any of these speculations.
