Mekaal Hasan Band re-records ‘Chal Bulleya’ with 19-year-old vocalist

21 Nov 2019, 12:37 by Asfa Sultan

KARACHI: In a recent video, Mekaal Hasan revealed his new band member, Rassab Amir. The vocalist is unsurprisingly, a classically trained 19-year-old boy who has filled in the shoes of Javed Bashir, Humaira Channa, and Sharmista Chatterjee.

The video showed a re-recording of the band’s 2009 song, Chal Bulleya, based on the poetry of Baba Bulleh Shah. It was earlier released in the voice of Javed Bashir.
