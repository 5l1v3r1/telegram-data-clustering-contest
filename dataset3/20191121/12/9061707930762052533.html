Derby city centre building closed after man's sudden death

The centre is closed until further notice

21 Nov 2019, 12:29 by Ben Lyons

A city centre building will be closed until further notice after a man died suddenly in the early hours of this morning.

Police were called to the Padley Homeless Centre, in Becket Street, just before 5am, after getting a call from East Midlands Ambulance Service.

The charity's building was closed this morning, with a sign on the door reading: "Centre closed until further notice. Sorry for any inconvenience."

A spokesperson for Derbyshire police said: "Police were called to reports of a sudden death of a man at The Padley Centre.

"Officers got the call from East Midlands Ambulance Service at 4.48am."
