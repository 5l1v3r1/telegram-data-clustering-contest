The notchless HONOR 9X is RM999 and comes with pop-up camera, Google Mobile Services and more

21 Nov 2019, 12:43

As per tradition, HONOR Malaysia would always launch a new X-series at the end of the year and here we have finally witnessed the HONOR 9X's official launch. Priced at just RM999, the most interesting thing about this phone is not really the tech specs but rather the fact that it's also installed with Google Mobile Services. Anyway, here's everything you need to know about the phone and pre-order. 

The pre-order of the HONOR 9X will begin tomorrow (22 November) on hihonor.com.my and Lazada platform. Depending on which platform you're planning to get from, there is a special deal for each starting from 22 until 27 November 2019 for every HONOR 9X purchase. For hihonor, you get an "add-on RM0.99" and a free HONOR Sports Pro Headphone, whereas for Lazada, you can get a free HONOR Band 5 Sport and gift box. After the pre-order period, the phone will officially go on sale on 28 November across the nation at their experience stores as well as Shopee with exclusive gift packs. 

The remaining tech specs of the HONOR 9X are 6GB of RAM, 128GB ROM (expandable up to 512GB), 6.59-inch FHD+ IPS display, AIS Super Night Mode, GPU Turbo 3.0, and more.
