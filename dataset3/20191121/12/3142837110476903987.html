Science in Sport (LON:SIS) Hits New 1-Year Low at $48.00

21 Nov 2019, 12:04 by Teresa Graham

Shares of Science in Sport PLC (LON:SIS) reached a new 52-week low on Tuesday . The company traded as low as GBX 48 ($0.63) and last traded at GBX 49 ($0.64), with a volume of 14673 shares traded. The stock had previously closed at GBX 49 ($0.64).

Separately, Liberum Capital reiterated a “buy” rating and set a GBX 115 ($1.50) target price on shares of Science in Sport in a report on Tuesday, October 29th.

The firm has a market capitalization of $58.83 million and a P/E ratio of -7.98. The company has a debt-to-equity ratio of 3.10, a quick ratio of 1.43 and a current ratio of 2.16. The firm has a 50-day moving average of GBX 50.34 and a 200-day moving average of GBX 55.05.

Science in Sport Company Profile (LON:SIS)

Science in Sport plc, together with its subsidiaries, develops, manufactures, and markets sports nutrition products for professional athletes and sports enthusiasts under the SiS brand name in the United Kingdom, rest of the European Union, Australia, and internationally. Its principal product range SiS GO energy powders, isotonic gels, energy bars and hydration tablets; and SiS REGO, a range of spectrum recovery products; and WHEY20, a protein product.
