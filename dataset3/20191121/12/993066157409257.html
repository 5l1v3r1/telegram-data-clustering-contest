Shots fired at Ballywalter house

21 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Digital Staff

SHOTS have been fired at a house in Ballywalter, Co Down.

It was reported to police yesterday but the house in Meadow Park is believed to have been targeted at around 1am on Sunday November 17.

No-one was in the house at the time, according to PSNI Detective Inspector Nigel Snoddy who said an upstairs bedroom window and downstairs living room window were damaged.
