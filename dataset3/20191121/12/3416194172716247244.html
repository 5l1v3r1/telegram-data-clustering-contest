Olivia Holt & 'I Didn't Do It' Cast Reunite In New Instagram Photos!

21 Nov 2019, 12:50

The cast of I Didn’t Do It are giving us all the feels!

Olivia Holt, Austin North, Sarah Gilman, Peyton Clark and Piper Curda reunited in Los Angeles this week and were sure to share it with fans.

“Got the whole gang together,” Austin captioned a pic, in all caps.

“ya know we’re doing just fine, cause this is the time of our lives,” Olivia commented on his post.

“ok fine I guess i’ll do it too,” Piper shared on her Instagram account.

“all the emotion from this reunion is in austin’s eyes meanwhile peyton is still hitting us with the smolder all these years later,” Sarah wrote with a post.

Peyton commented on Sarah‘s post with, “What can I say? I give the people what they want. 😏”

I Didn’t Do It first premiered on Disney Channel in January 2014, and ended in October 2015 after two seasons.
