'Madhuswamy should apologise for insulting Kurubas'

21 Nov 2019, 12:46 by Ranjith Kandya

CLP leader Siddaramaiah said Law Minister J C Madhuswamy should apologise for insulting the Kuruba community.

Speaking to reporters, here, he asked why Chief Minister B S Yediyurappa apologised for Madhuswamy's statements. "Madhuswamy should apologise and close the matter. The
issue should not be politicised," Siddaramaiah said.
