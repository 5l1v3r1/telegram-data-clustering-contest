Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 November 21, 2019, Written Updates Of Full Episode: Shivani Gets To Know The Truth

21 Nov 2019, 17:49 by Kristina Das

Ronit says he is there at the party not for Shivani but for a friend. Shivani sees Komolika with Ronit while Komolika wishes to kill Prerna

Today's Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 episode begins with Prerna thinking about similarities between Komolika and Sonalika. Later, while Komolika leaves she hides behind a pillar. Anurag asks Prerna about Sonalika and also about her presence near the pillar. Prerna decides to follow Sonalika just to see how she reacts.

While Komolika decides to burn the piece of clothing on which the servant’s feet touched and thus, she goes in private. Prerna notices the same and gets shocked to see that in the same way, Komolika has also burned the cloth.

Later, Komolika gets shocked to see Ronit and decides to send him away. But Mohini and Anurag stop her when she is about to go out. In the meantime, Anurag wishes to dance with Prerna. They start dancing while Shivani notices that Sonalika does not like their closeness. Later, Komolika gets mad at Ronit and she moves out of the party.

Ronit says he is there at the party not for Shivani but for a friend. Shivani sees Komolika with Ronit while Komolika wishes to kill Prerna.

Shivani starts searching for Prerna to tell her the truth and she hides behind the car. Meanwhile, Anurag wants to meet Prerna’s husband while she tells him that he is out of Kolkata. Ronit tells Komolika that he will kill Prerna and she asks him to do it on the same day. Later, Shivani tells Prerna about Sonalika talking with Ronit.
