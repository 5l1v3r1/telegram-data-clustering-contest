Three fire crews tackle wood-burning stove fire in Lytham

Firefighters were called to a fire involving a wood-burning stove in Lytham today.

21 Nov 2019, 17:29 by Wes Holmes

Fire engines from Blackpool, St Annes and Penwortham were attended the fire on Park View Road at 12.35pm.

Firefighters used a ladder, a nimbus nozzle and thermal imaging camera to extinguish the fire. They remained at the scene for several hours.
