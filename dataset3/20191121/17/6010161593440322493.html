World of Warships: Legends cross-play coming to Xbox One and PlayStation 4

21 Nov 2019, 17:32 by Stephany Nunneley

You’ll soon be able to match up with other World of Warships: Legends players on PS4 and Xbox One through cross-play.

If you play World of Warships: Legends, you will soon be able to enjoy a bit of cross-play between other Xbox One and PlayStation 4 players.

At the moment, it will only work in matchmaker, so you won’t be able to interact between the consoles in regards to voice-chat or playing with friends who are on a different console. Still, it’s a start.
