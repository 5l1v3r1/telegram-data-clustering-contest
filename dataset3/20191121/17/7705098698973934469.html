The CW Teases Barry Allen’s Last Temptation in New The Flash Episode 6.08 Stills

21 Nov 2019, 17:41 by Tudor Leonte

The CW has released a new batch of promotional photos for the upcoming second part of The Flash midseason finale. A couple of weeks from now, Barry Allen will face one of the toughest challenges in his life. The monstrous Bloodwork has infected the beloved superhero himself, and now he seems almost invincible.

The synopsis of “The Last Temptation of Barry Allen, Part 2” reads as follows. “With The Flash (Grant Gustin) freshly infected by the monstrous Bloodwork (guest star Sendhil Ramamurthy), Iris (Candice Patton) and Cisco (Carlos Valdes) battle to help Barry take control of himself before he’s lost forever to Ramsey’s influence. Meanwhile, the rest of Team Flash fight to reclaim control of Central City from Bloodwork’s growing army.

Michael Nankin directed the episode written by Kristen Kim & Joshua V. Gilbert. You can check out the promotional photos in the gallery below.

Grant Gustin stars in the series as Barry Allen/The Flash. Candice Patton co-stars as Iris West-Allen, with Danielle Panabaker as Caitlin Snow/Killer Frost, Carlos Valdes as Cisco Ramon/Vibe, Hartley Sawyer as Ralph Dibny/Elongated Man, as well as Tom Cavanagh as Harrison Wells, and Jesse L. Martin as Detective Joe West.

Greg Berlanti executive produces the series, along with Sarah Schechter and Eric Wallace. Bonanza Productions Inc. produces The Flash together with Berlanti Productions and Warner Bros. Television.
