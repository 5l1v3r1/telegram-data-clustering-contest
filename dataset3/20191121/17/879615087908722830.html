Wednesday, November 20

Waris Ahluwalia Unveils New Beverage Brand at Top of The Standard
Actor/designer/entrepreneur Waris Ahluwalia invited VIPs to the Top of The Standard to celebrate the launch of House of Waris Botanicals, a new line of organic teas and beverages. Guests enjoyed an exclusive tasting of the brand’s debut blends. Three distinct bars were set-up throughout the space, featuring the teas, zero-proof beverages, and cocktails made with premium Tanqueray No. TEN.

Guests — including Katie Holmes, Chloe Sevigny, Leigh Lezark, and Young Paris — danced to DJ sets by Paul Sevigny, Justin Miller, and Isaac Likes. Meanwhile, Caroline Vreeland serenaded the crowd, singing a belated happy birthday to Waris.

Frame Gets In the Thanksgiving Spirit
Frame founder Erik Torstensson hosted an intimate Thanksgiving Feast to celebrate friends and family at Morandi in New York. Guests included Nina Agdal, Halima Aden, and Sean O’Pry. As a way to give back this holiday season FRAME will be providing 1000 Thanksgiving meals for families in need with FeedingNYC.

Dree Hemingway Fêtes the Launch of Emilia Wickstead Nightwear
Dree Hemingway and MATCHESFASHION celebrated Emilia Wickstead’s nightwear collection at San Vicente Bungalows in West Hollywood. Guests enjoyed grilled vegetable salad, grilled salmon, cauliflower steak, french fries, orzo pilaf, and flourless chocolate fudge cake.
