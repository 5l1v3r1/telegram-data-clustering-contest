PM Imran believes in across the board accountability: Firdous

21 Nov 2019, 17:35 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Special Assistant to the Prime Minister on Information and Broadcasting Dr Firdous Ashiq Awan on Thursday said that Prime Minister Imran Khan firmly believes in supremacy of constitution, rule of law and across the board accountability.

The special assistant took to the twitter and said that the entire nation is witness to the fact as to who was declared  Sadiq & Ameen  by the country s top court and who was declared dishonest.
