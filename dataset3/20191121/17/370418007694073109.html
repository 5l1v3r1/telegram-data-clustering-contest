Creeper Release New Video For Born Cold

Watch the video for Creeper’s comeback single, Born Cold

21 Nov 2019, 17:00

Southampton goth-punks Creeper have dropped a new video for Born Cold, the first new music since their surprise comeback last month.

It’s the first new music from the enigmatic six-piece since 2017’s debut album Eternity, In Your Arms. And as you might expect, the storyline and mythology surrounding the band is expanding even further.

“The video reveals the world and themes of the new album for the first time,” says frontman Will Gould. “It also introduces fans to the first of the new characters in the Creeper universe; Roe, a man from another world.”

Speaking previously about the song, Will said: “This song is the introduction of one of the more villainous characters from our new story. It was inspired by what the Thin White Duke was for David Bowie – a more selfish, narcissistic character.”
