I'm A Celebrity: Kate Garraway's husband Derek struggling without her as he's 'never cooked a proper meal'

Trouble in the kitchen

21 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Richard Bell

Presenter Kate Garraway left her husband Derek Draper to look after their two kids when she jetted off to take part in this year's I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!

The Good Morning Britain host, 52, has already had to contend with swarms of cockroaches and other nasties crawling all over her - including up her "noo noo" - but it appears her husband has faced struggles of his own.

Derek Draper, who shares daughter Darcey, 13, and son William, 10, with Kate, took to social media to pose a cookery question to his followers as he had "never cooked a proper meal" in his life.
