Angry Birds Splash Water World opens in Setia Ecohill in Semenyih tomorrow

21 Nov 2019, 17:48 by Bernama

SEMENYIH (Nov 21): This school break get ready for a one-of-a-kind holiday experience with your family by visiting the world’s largest mobile-themed water inflatable park, Angry Birds Splash Water World at Setia Ecohill, here.

Angry Birds started as a popular video game developed by Rovio Entertainment Corp and has since evolved from games to various entertainment and consumer products.

The one-hectare inflatable water park will be officially opened to the public tomorrow (Nov 22) for two months and will operate on specific dates such as weekends and public holidays.

Organiser, One Universal Production Sdn Bhd’s chief executive officer, Alvin Lee said only 2,000 tickets will be available to the public per day to ensure smooth operation and a quality experience for all.

He said lovable characters in the Angry Birds franchise including Red, Chuck, Bomb, Terrence, and Mighty Eagle will be featured across the Angry Birds Splash Water World.

The highlight would be the inflatable Angry Birds water slides and obstacles featuring Red, Chuck, Bomb, Mighty Eagle, Terence and also the tallest water slide standing at 12 meters in height, he added.

For more information, tickets details and purchases, please visit Ticket2U website (ticket2u.com.my).
