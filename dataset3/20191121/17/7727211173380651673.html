Christmas light switch on events across Merseyside including Liverpool, Knowsley and Sefton

You can also enjoy fireworks, grottos, carols, mulled wine, markets, live music and more

21 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Mark Johnson

Merseyside is getting into the festive spirit and a great way to celebrate the start of the season of goodwill is to attend a Christmas lights switch on.

There are plenty of them happening across the region and many of the events are offering lots more attractions for you and your family and friends to enjoy.

Some events will be offering fairs, a chance to meet Santa Claus, grottos, live music and more.

There will also be food and drink available at some of the planned events.

Here are some of the Christmas light switch on events that are happening across Merseyside ...

Liverpool

Woolton

Woolton Village Father Christmas event.

Every November for the last 22 years, Woolton Village Residents Association has funded and organised a Father Christmas tour of the village in his sleigh.

It has provided the huge Christmas tree with LED flashing lights - and these stay on until the new year - and the kiddies Grotto and Lodes Pond Allerton Road.

The switch on of the main Christmas tree lights and Lodes Pond.

When:  Wednesday, November 30 2019 at 4.15pm.

Where: Woolton Village Residents Association, Allerton Road, Woolton,

 

Knowsley

Huyton

Huyton Village's Christmas light switch-on will coincide with November's first Huyton Live event.

There will also be a firework display for the finale.

The fun will begin at 12 midday and there will be food, drink, live music and a reindeer visiting.

There will be live entertainment from 4.30pm until 8.30pm - and the Christmas lights will be switched on at 6.30pm.

When: Friday, November 22 at 6.30pm - but the fun will begin at 12 midday

Where: Huyton Village

Kirkby

There will be a Christmas lights event at Kirkby town centre on Thursday, November 21 from 4pm until 7pm.

It will take place in the Newtown Gardens.

There will be music and festive fun, with performances from Andy Bennett of Ocean Colour Scene and an entertainment line-up including Ben from Phats and Small, Katy Ashworth from Cbeebies and 2014 X-Factor finalist, Jack Walton.

A spectacular firework display with also conclude the evening.

When: Thursday, November 21 from 4pm until 7pm

Where: Newton Gardens, Kirkby town centre

Halewood

This Christmas, you can take your family to New Hutte Neighbourhood Centre in Halewood.

There, you can watch the Christmas lights switch on, visit Santa’s Grotto and receive a gift.

You can even create a letter for Santa, too, and more.

There will be face painting, a raffle, arts and craft activities.

The event takes place Friday December 6 between 3.30pm and 6pm - with the lights being switched on at 5.45pm.

This year, Halewood Town Council  and partners at Hurricane Productions are presenting Jack & The Beanstalk, with tickets costing £2 per child, which includes a selection box and visit by Santa Claus.

Accompanying adults are free and tickets are available from the Hollies and Arncliffe Centres.

When: The event takes place Friday, December 6 between 3.30pm and 6pm - the lights being switched on at 5.45pm.

Where: New Hutte Neighbourhood Centre, Lichfield Road, Halewood, Liverpool, L26 1TT

Whiston

Willowbrook Hospice will hold two services, "Light Up A Life," to mark the turning on the lights on the Christmas trees.

The services will be held at Prescot Parish Church on December 9 at 7pm and St Marys Lowe House Church on December 11 at 7pm.

When: December 9 at 7pm ( Prescot Parish Church), and December 11 at 7pm (St Marys Lowe House Church)

Where:  Prescot Parish Church and St Marys Lowe House Church.

Cronton

Cronton Tree Lighting and Ecumenical Carol Service will take place on Tuesday, December 17.

The tree lighting will be at 7pm at Coronation Gardens.

Following that will be the Carol Service at 7.30pm at Holy Family Church on Hall Lane and a reception at Holy Family Social Club at 8pm.

When:  Tuesday, December 17.

Where: Coronation Gardens, Cronton

 

Sefton

Crosby  

The Crosby Christmas Lights Switch On will be held from 3pm with the lights switched on at 6pm by the Mayor of Sefton, Cllr June Burns.

Both Labour Party Candidates, MP Bill Esterson and Bootle MP Peter Dowd are expected to attend.

There will be a funfair for the kids.

The Christmas tree lights will be switched on at 6pm.

The tree has been planted in the Lady Diana Bed and Gardens, so will not be in it's usual location.

When: Friday, November 22 at 6pm, but the fun starts at 3pm

Where: Crosby Village

Thornton

There will be carols, choirs, a visit from Santa courtesy of Rotary Club Crosby, a Christmas horse from Shy Lowen and more.

Celebrate the start of the Christmas period.

When: Friday, November 22 - Thornton Christmas Lights Switch On starts at 6pm, lights switch on at 6.45pm

Where: On the Village Green opposite the Nags Head Pub

Bootle

The Christmas Lights Switch On in Bootle will be held by Bootle Action Group.

The lights will be switched on by the Mayor of Sefton, Councillor June Burns.

There will be free face-painting, raffles, games, free refreshments, live music from 'Singing Our Socks Off' and local celebrity singer George Gallagher.

The entertainment will begin at 2pm and the light switch on at 5pm.

There will be a box for food bank donations, which will be next to the raffle table. Or you can pass to any steward in orange jackets.

When: Saturday, November 23 - the fun starts from 2pm with the lights switch on at 5pm

Where: Bootle Library, 220 Stanley Road, Bootle, L20 3EN

Formby  

Formby will see the return of Frosty the Snowman again this year which will be at Formby Pool.

There will be Christmas market stalls, children's fairground rides and entertainment from 2pm.

All the fun will culminate in the lights being switched on at 5pm.

The event is free entry as always.

When: Sunday, November 24 at 5pm - the lights are switched on at 5pm, but the event starts at 2pm, and the festivities will continue on through until 7.30pm.

Where: Formby
