The Patriots’ “Mystery” Wide Receiver Has Been Identified

21 Nov 2019, 17:17 by Andrew Holleran

The New England Patriots had a “mystery” wide receiver at practice on Wednesday afternoon. He was wearing No. 10.

No, it was not Antonio Brown.

The Patriots are in need of some depth at the wide receivers position, as New England is dealing with injuries, including one to standout Phillip Dorsett. So, the team brought in some help this week.

Reporters noticed a “mystery” wide receiver wearing No. 10 at practice on Wednesday.
