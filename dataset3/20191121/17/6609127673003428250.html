Man arrested after woman ‘raped in Dundee flat by stranger’ in broad daylight

21 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Aidan Smith

A MAN has been arrested after a woman was allegedly raped in a Dundee flat.

Tayside cops confirmed that they had arrested a 31-year-old man in connection with the alleged incident.

The alleged rape took place in Balunie Crescent in Dundee.

A Police Scotland statement read: "Police Scotland can confirm that a 31 year old man has been arrested in connection with serious sexual assault which happened in Balunie Crescent in Dundee on Saturday 19 October.

"Enquiries are continuing."

Cops had previously called on the public to come forward with any information as they launched a manhunt to find the culprit.

A dedicated team of both uniformed and detective officers were called in for the investigation.
