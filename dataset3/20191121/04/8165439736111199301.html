Netflix Restored After Having Trouble Streaming Earlier

21 Nov 2019, 04:22 by Josh Crosbie

Netflix down

Netflix is now back up and running.

There was a problem with streaming on all devices earlier.

The company has apologised for any inconvenience.

Netflix is thanking users for their patience after people experienced issues streaming on all devices today.

The company's technicians worked to fix the problem as soon as possible.
