86-year-old comedy sensation Lynn Ruth Miller to go wild in Bangkok

21 Nov 2019, 04:01 by Coconuts Bangkok

While most people are upset by thoughts of aging and death, 86-year-old Lynn Ruth Miller has been killing audiences by laughing about it. 

Possibly the oldest woman doing stand-up comedy, the octogenarian entertainer will grace a Bangkok stage next week and hand out some good ol’ fashioned LOLs.

Stagetime Comedy Club is rolling out its fourth International Showcase Special, this time featuring American-born Miller, whose style doesn’t only talk about failed marriages and getting old, but also singing and performing burlesque.
