Chiwenga’s profile says he died on 18 November 2019

21 Nov 2019, 04:23

Vice President Guvheya Constantino Chiwenga’s Wikipedia profile claimed yesterday that he died on the 18th of November at a Chinese military hospital.

The profile has since been updated to show that Chiwenga  is not dead. His academic qualifications(Ph.D in Genocide) have also been updated to Political Science.

This latest mishap will fuel speculation on the former general’s future following rumours that he is planning to takeover from Mnangagwa when he returns from Beijing.
