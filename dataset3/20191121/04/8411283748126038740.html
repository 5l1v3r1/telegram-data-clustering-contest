Open AI Fab provides subscription-based AI services

21 Nov 2019, 04:18 by Mark Tsai, Taipei; Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES

Open AI Fab provides AI services, mainly consulting in AI technology and AI solutions, on a subscription basis for small- to medium-size enterprises to help them transform or upgrade operations, according to company CEO Jon Huang.

The services come in three stages: Diagnosis of clients' business areas and needs, followed by development of AI-based models to meet the needs, and then by implementing and improving the models, Huang noted.

Founded in May 2018, Open AI Fab develops AI-based models using technologies in graphic recognition and natural language processing as well as open source algorithms for the time being, and aims at clients from retail, finance, manufacturing and agriculture, Huang indicated.
