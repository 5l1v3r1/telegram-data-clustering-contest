Andrea Bocelli, Barbra Streisand, John Legend, Michael Buble And Elvis Costello Compete For Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album Grammy

21 Nov 2019, 04:03 by Charisse Van Horn

The 62nd Grammy award nominations have been released and the nominees for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album have been announced. The nominees have been going viral on social media as topping the list is the opera singer and tenor Andrea Bocelli. Andrea has been nominated for five Grammys but hasn’t won yet. He has been nominated for his album Si and his fans are sounding off online letting him know that they want him to win.

Barbra Streisand has 46 Grammy nominations and 8 wins and is nominated for her album Walls. It is her only nomination this year and she has plenty of support from those who want to see her earn her ninth Grammy.

Elvis Costello as a solo artist has 1 win and 15 nominations. He is nominated with his band Elvis Costello and the Imposters for their album Look Now.

John Legend has been nominated for his album A Legendary Christmas. John Legend has 31 nominations and 10 wins.

Michael Buble is also nominated for his album Love (Deluxe Edition). Michael Buble has 11 nominations and 4 wins.
