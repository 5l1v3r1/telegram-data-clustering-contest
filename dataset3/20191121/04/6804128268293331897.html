Chelsea news: Jose Mourinho's Spurs move, Jadon Sancho's hint, transfer ban latest

The latest news from Stamford Bridge

21 Nov 2019, 04:00 by Joe Doyle

While their rivals have decided to use the international break to chop and change managers, it has been a pretty quiet one for Chelsea.

The Blues went into the two week hiatus in third place in the Premier League table, joint on points with second placed Leicester City, meaning it has been so far a successful return to Stamford Bridge for Frank Lampard.

The team's 2-0 win over Crystal Palace last weekend means that they have now won six league games in a row, which is perfect preparation for the match coming up after the international break.

Chelsea travel to the Etihad Stadium to take on reigning Premier League Champions, Manchester City, in a mouthwatering fixture. Pep Guardiola's men are currently one point and one place behind the Blues in the table, in the fourth and last Champions League spot.

As preparations for the match start to take shape, check out the latest news from Stamford Bridge over the last 24 hours.

Jose Mourinho arrives at Spurs

Jose Mourinho was officially confirmed as the new Spurs manager on Wednesday morning.

The former Chelsea and Manchester United boss stated in the past that he would never join Spurs. But the world constantly throws up surprises.

Fans react to Mourinho's move

It's safe to say that not all of Chelsea's fans were happy about Mourinho's new job.

Some Blues supporters have reacted angrily to Mourinho's appointment, with others setting their sights set on the first clash with the Portuguese manager as Tottenham boss.

Chelsea and Spurs are yet to meet in the Premier League this season, with the first clash at Tottenham's new home on Sunday, December 22, before Mourinho is set to return to Stamford Bridge on February 22.
