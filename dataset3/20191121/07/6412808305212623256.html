KFC Just Rolled Out An Affordable Soy Garlic Chicken Set And New Sweet Potato Fries

Yum!

21 Nov 2019, 07:23 by Arisha Rozaidee

Cover image via KFC Malaysia SAYS Makan

Calling all Korean fried chicken fans, KFC has a new menu addition that you'll love!

KFC = Korean Fried Chicken?

You can now get Soy Garlic Glaze Drummets at selected KFC outlets across Malaysia

This brand new menu item is now selling at only RM9.90 per set which includes two pieces of the Soy Garlic Glaze Drummets, one piece of fried chicken, one large whipped potato, and a drink.

The Soy Garlic Glaze Drummets are not available at all outlets just yet, but you can try them at these locations:

- KFC Kota Warisan, Selangor
- KFC Shah Alam 2, Selangor
- KFC Setiawangsa, Kuala Lumpur
- KFC Danau Desa, Kuala Lumpur
- KFC Batu Lanchang, Penang
- KFC Mersing, Johor
- KFC Taman Perling, Johor
- KFC Taman Tas, Pahang 
- KFC AEON Manjung, Perak, and
- KFC Jerteh, Terengganu.

To celebrate the festive season, KFC is also introducing Frosted Sweet Potato Fries

The waffle fries are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside and they're frosted with some icing sugar which really brings out the taste of the sweet potato!

You can purchase it on its own for RM5.50 or get it with the Holiday Bucket set which is priced at RM34.90.

We tried it out for ourselves and we would 10/10 recommend.

And if you're feeling reminiscent, I've got good news for you.
The Hot & Cheezy chicken is back!

Spicy fried chicken blanketed with a warm layer of cheese sauce. *chef's kiss*
