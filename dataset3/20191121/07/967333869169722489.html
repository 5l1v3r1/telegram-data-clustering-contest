Fact Check of X-rated Pictures of JNU Protest Reveals Dirty War of Divided Indians

21 Nov 2019, 07:07

New Delhi (Sputnik): The student union at India’s premier university, Jawaharlal Nehru University, has been protesting against structural reforms within the school for about a month. The protest turned violent on Monday after clashes erupted between students and Delhi Police.

Obscene and x-rated pictures, allegedly of JNU students, have popped up on social media. Pictures of a gay couple kissing, a student partying with liquor bottles in their hands are going viral on the internet with the hashtag #JNUFacts.

A simple fact check into the pictures through a reverse Google image search have revealed that the pictures are old and unrelated to JNU.
