Star-studded Oprah tour - Entertainment News

21 Nov 2019, 07:02 by WENN

Oprah Winfrey has assembled a lineup of leading ladies, including Lady Gaga, Jennifer Lopez and Michelle Obama, for her upcoming wellness arena tour.

The actress and media mogul is teaming with bosses at diet brand WeightWatchers for her Oprah's 2020 Vision: Your Life in Focus event, which is set to kick off on Jan. 4.

Also making appearances at events spread out across nine U.S. cities will be Dwayne Johnson, Amy Schumer, Tina Fey, Tracee Ellis Ross, Kate Hudson, and Oprah's longtime friend Gayle King.

In addition to interviews, the tour stops will include full-day wellness activities including a dance exercise session from choreographer and actress Julianne Hough.
