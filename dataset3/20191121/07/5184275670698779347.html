Shraddha Kapoor Joined By Rumored Beau Rohan Shrestha On The Sets Of Baaghi 3?

Rohan has reached the shoot location to give his lady love some company.

21 Nov 2019, 07:46 by Koimoi.com Team

A lot has been spoken about Shraddha Kapoor’s love life. Right from her alleged link-ups to her co-actors like Aditya Roy Kapoor to Farhan Akhtar and even Ranbir Kapoor, Shraddha has often found herself centred with dating rumours. And now, Shraddha has yet again been linked to celebrity photographer Rohan Shrestha.

The industry grapevine also has it that the couple is head over heels in love with one another and will soon tie the knot too! Meanwhile, a certain source has revealed that while Shraddha is busy shooting for the third instalment of Baaghi alongside Tiger Shroff, Rohan has reached the shoot location to give his lady love some company.

Quoting the source, Bollywood Bubble has reported, “Shraddha is admittedly in love with her photographer boyfriend Rohan Shrestha. What many might have thought of as a fling – even her closest friends thought it won’t last – but it did and how. Shraddha is madly in love with Rohan and they are planning to get married sometime next year as well. Shraddha comes from a very conservative family and her parents love Rohan. They have already given their nod and want the marriage to happen sooner than ever.”

The source has further said, “Shraddha will be in for a 45-day schedule for ‘Baaghi 3’ in Serbia. Tiger Shroff and she have several important scenes that need to be shot. The gruelling action schedule also begins there. To give girlfriend Shraddha company, Rohan has also taken a few days off from work and flown off to Serbia to spend some quality time with his beau. They didn’t want it to become news hence they flew off on different days to avoid the paparazzi. But now, they are happily enjoying in Serbia and when Shraddha gets break from work, she’s off to the arms of her beloved.”

On the professional front, apart from Baaghi 3, Shraddha also has Remo D’Souza’s 3D dance film, Street dancer 3 alongside Varun Dhawan in the making.
