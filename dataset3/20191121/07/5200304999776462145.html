‘Indian Idol’ contestant Jannabi Das signs debut with Ajay-Atul

21 Nov 2019, 07:09 by Sahar Junejo

‘Indian Idol 11’ contestant Jannabi Das has been signed to sing for music composers Ajay-Atul after a memorable performance on the show.

Jannabi performed the classic song ‘Mera Naam Chin Chin Chu’ from ‘Howrah Bridge’ (1958) on the latest episode, which featured Ajay-Atul as guest judges, Bollywood Life reported. After receiving praise from the judges, the composing duo promised to work with Das in the future. It is unclear which project Das will be a part of, but Ajay-Atul have a promising list of films releasing next year, including ‘Shamshera’ and ‘Sardar Udham Singh’.

The latest episode of ‘Indian Idol 11’ featuring Ajay-Atul will air this weekend on Sony TV.
