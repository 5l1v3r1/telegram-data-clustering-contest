Sharks don't like Kiss - Entertainment News

21 Nov 2019, 07:07 by WENN

Big fish don't like Kiss.

The rockers were left all at sea this week when a boat gig for sharks off the coast of Australia was a bust.

The promotion for Airbnb's new Animal Experiences travel program failed to attract any great whites, after experts insisted the huge creatures would swarm the boat at the sound of "low-frequency sounds of rock and roll."

Kiss stars Gene Simmons, Eric Singer and Tommy Thayer joined fans and reporters for the odd sea gig, which was broadcast via underwater speakers in the hope of attracting sharks.

Poor health forced singer and guitarist Paul Stanley to miss the event.
