3 CeraVe Resurfacing Retinol Serum

target.com $19.99 SHOP NOW

The CeraVe team came to visit me at the Cosmo offices last week to show me their newest serum—the Resurfacing Retinol Serum—and you guys, I was IMPRESSED. It’s designed to fade post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation from old acne marks, minimize the look of pores, and smooth fine lines (so, all of my skin concerns) with a combo of encapsulated retinol, niacinamide, licorice root extract, and ceramides. 

I’ve been using it in the morning under my sunscreen (and yes, you can use this retinol in the a.m. since the encapsulation prevents it from degrading in sunlight) and my skin already looks brighter and a little bit smoother. Pretty awesome considering it’s only $20.

✨ Lauren Balsamo, senior beauty editor

4 Missha Time Revolution Artemisia Treatment Essence

misshaus.com $33.00 SHOP NOW

Okay, setting aside the fact that this packaging is hella gorgeous—like, prepare to be all over my Instagram, TY—the formula of this essence is amazing for my sensitive skin. It has exactly one ingredient: 100 percent artemisia annua extract, which is double-fermented sweet wormwood that’s incredibly soothing and anti-inflammatory by nature. I just pat it over my clean, dry skin morning and night, then immediately apply my moisturizer to lock in the hydration. Love, love, love.

✨ Chloe Metzger, senior beauty editor

5 Kate McLeod Atelier Stone I

katemcleod.com $65.00 SHOP NOW

I’ve ONLY been using Kate McLeod’s body stone moisturizers on my skin since having my daughter—specifically the Mama Stone, which is safe for breastfeeding—but I’ve recently become obsessed with her limited-edition crystal collab with healer Rashia Bell. First of all, Kate’s formula (a blend of cocoa butter, sweet almond oil, apricot-kernel oil, avocado oil, and fractionated coconut oil) feels like no other body lotion on your skin and will instantly turn a dry patch around (one stone lasts around four months with daily use, BTW!). 

Fun fact: She used to be a pastry chef and treats her ingredients as she would a chocolate ganache (I mean, so chic), so the stone holds its shape like a sweet treat but melts on (and absorbs into) your skin in the most nongreasy way possible. But this collab takes the meditative experience of rubbing a body stone all over your body to another level thanks to the relaxing vetiver and balancing bergamot it contains—not to mention the crystal heliotrope you walk away with when your stone is done.

✨ Carly Cardellino Vaccaro, beauty director
