Thursday's weather: Cloudy and dry for most

21 Nov 2019, 07:30

Much of the UK will have a cloudy but mainly dry day on Thursday.

The best of any brightness will be across northwestern areas, especially across western Scotland.

Through the day, outbreaks of rain will spread into south west England and south Wales.

It will remain breezy, making it feel cold for most.

Top temperature 10 Celsius (50F).
