The Apprentice's Ryan-Mark reveals he 'would never' date Lottie Lion

21 Nov 2019, 07:01

Ryan-Mark Parsons has finally opened up about the rumours that he’s dating Lottie Lion – and unfortunately they’re not true.

The 19-year-old, who was the latest candidate to be fired from the BBC show has previously shared with his followers that the pair were dating.

Although he has now come clean and revealed that he was actually joking.

Following his exit the womenswear consultant told Metro.co.uk: ‘We were just messing around, it was just a little joke.

‘I would never ever date Lottie she is the complete opposite of what I am interested in.

‘We will stay close friends though as she is in London sometimes.’

It all began when a fan of The Apprentice tweeted Ryan-Mark after last Wednesday show to comment on their friendship.

They tweeted: ‘Just watched last night’s episode of the apprentice, another brilliant episode. My favourites so far are
@LottieLion1 @ryanmarkparsons and @ImLewisEllis.

‘I’ve noticed Lottie and Ryan-Mark have quite the friendship.’

Ryan-Mark then added fuel to the fire by responding: ‘We’re actually dating. EXCLUSIVE.’
