‘Awakening Bangkok’ lights up the night (Photos)

21 Nov 2019, 07:01 by Coconuts Bangkok

The night is aglow with a wash of spectacular lighting now through Sunday in Bangkok’s Old Town. Stroll through the Charoen Krung area to find the 40 lighting installations comprising Awakening Bangkok at venues such as the Grand Postal Office, Prince Theatre Heritage Stay and Haroon Mosque.
