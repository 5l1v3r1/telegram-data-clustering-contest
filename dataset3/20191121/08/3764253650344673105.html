Actress Halima Abubakar receives celebrity personality of the year award, says 'thank you' (photo)

21 Nov 2019, 08:56

- Nigerian actress Halima Abubakar has received the award for celebrity personality of the year

- The actress was presented the award by Nigerian Gold Face Awards

- Abubakar took to social media to show off the award and thank the organisation

Popular Nigerian actress Halima Abubakar has bagged a 2019 celebrity award. The beautiful actress had received the award for the celebrity personality of the year.

The actress took to her Instagram page to show off the framed award. She celebrated it and thanked the organisation.

According to her, the award was presented to her by the Nigerian Gold Face Awards. She expressed her gratitude for being honoured with the award.
