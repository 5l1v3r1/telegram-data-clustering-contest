Christian Pulisic, Antonio Rudiger, Ross Barkley - Latest Chelsea injury news ahead of Man City

The latest injury news for Chelsea as they travel to face champions Manchester City after the international break

21 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Oliver Harbord, Joe Doyle

Chelsea are back in action this weekend with a trip to the Etihad Stadium to face Premier League champions Manchester City.

Fourth plays third in the table on Saturday evening as the battle to chase down Liverpool at the top of the league intensifies.

A win for Frank Lampard's side could send them second in the table should Leicester fail to beat Brighton in the 3pm kick-off, while Liverpool will be looking to extend their lead at the top of the table with a trip to Crystal Palace.

Having as many players available as possible could be key to getting a positive result, but the Blues do have some injury concerns ahead of the trip, with as many as five players potentially unavailable.

With that said, here's the latest Chelsea injury news and expected return dates.

Christian Pulisic

Chelsea face an anxious wait to discover the full extent of Christian Pulisic's hip injury after USA head coach Gregg Berhalter confirmed the winger would miss their international matches.
