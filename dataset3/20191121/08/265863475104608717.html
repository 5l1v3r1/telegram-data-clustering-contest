Kim Kardashian Teases What Fans Can Expect From Annual Family Christmas Card

21 Nov 2019, 08:37 by Rachelle Lewis

Kim Kardashian West is teasing what fans can expect from the famous family’s annual Christmas.

The Kardashian and Jenner Christmas card has become a holiday staple over the years. Thanks to the success of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, the family catapulted to stardom. Each year the glamorous clan gathers for an iconic card. There is always a theme and it is always elaborate.

Over the years the family has grown, making getting them all together for a photoshoot, challenging. Some members of the Kardashian and Jenner clan have even decided to forgo the family tradition in recent years.
