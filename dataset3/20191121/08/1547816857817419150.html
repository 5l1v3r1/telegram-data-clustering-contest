The Game Changers: The Docu That Got Virat Kohli, Anushka Sharma And Others Profess A Plant-Based Lifestyle

21 Nov 2019, 08:42 by Lubna Khan

Everyone has been talking about The Game Changers on Netflix. It is one documentary that left many inspired and willing to give veganism a try, including Anushka Sharma and Virat Kohli. What is the hype all about? Read on to find out!

There has been a lot of hype about one Netflix documentary, in particular, titled The Game Changers which has been described as “a quest to find the optimal diet for human performance and health” by Netflix. The documentary released at a time when veganism is on the rise and people all over are starting to acknowledge and accept its benefits and environmental advantages. Everyone has been talking about The Game Changers on Netflix. It is one documentary that left many inspired and willing to give veganism a try, and even Anushka Sharma and Virat Kohli lauded it, calling it an eye-opener. Which brings us to the question, what is the hype all about?

For starters, this Netflix documentary stars several world-famous athletes and bodybuilders who talk about their plant-based diets and their success stories. The film begins with a message that states, “The statements expressed in this film are not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice.” The documentary revolves around a UFC fighter who is on a quest to find the best diet and meets top scientists and athletes for the same. It also stars Arnold Schwarzenegger, Lewis Hamilton and mixed-martial artists James Wilks, and many others. This documentary smashes myths about meat and protein and strength, and elaborated on the advantages of vegetarianism. It’s not just about maximizing performance and benefit to self, it also sheds light on the benefits to our planet and flora-fauna.
