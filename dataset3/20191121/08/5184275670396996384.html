Baaghi 3: Tiger Shroff Gives A Sneak Peek Of An Action Scene He Shot With Director Ahmed Khan & Producer Sajid Nadiadwala

A few days ago, the actor even shared his look from the shoot in Serbia, in which he is sporting a sleeveless jacket and a rugged look.

21 Nov 2019, 08:09 by Koimoi.com Team

Post the grand success of War, Tiger Shroff’s next Baaghi 3 is one of the highly-anticipated action movies of Bollywood. He will be seen with Shraddha Kapoor once again after Baaghi. While the shoot is going in full swing, the cast and crew is making sure of maintaining the buzz by teasing with glimpses from the high octane shoot.

Yesterday, Tiger Shroff shared an Instagram story in which he posed with Baaghi 3 director Ahmed Khan and producer Sajid Nadiadwala. One can spot an injury mark on Tiger’s face, suggesting that he is gearing up to shoot an action sequence. He is holding a clapperboard with his director.

Baaghi 3 also features Vijay Varma, Ashutosh Rana, Annu Kapoor and others in key roles and is slated to release on 6th March 2020.

A few days ago, the actor even shared his look from the shoot in Serbia, in which he is sporting a sleeveless jacket and a rugged look.

Meanwhile, Vijay Varma, who is also part of the movie shoot along with Tiger Shroff in Serbia, says he is having a gala time with the latter on the sets.

“I am really having a great time during the shoot of ‘Baaghi 3‘ in Serbia, the shoot is coming out really well. Tiger and I share a great bond and I’m sure that it will be reflected on the big screen,” he said.

Vijay also shared his admiration for Tiger’s father and veteran actor Jackie Shroff, who is fondly known as Jaggu dada.
