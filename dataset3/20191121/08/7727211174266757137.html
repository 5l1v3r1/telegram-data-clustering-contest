Liverpool's interest in rumoured target 'confirmed' by agent ahead of potential summer transfer

Liverpool have regularly been linked with Trabzonspor goalkeeper Ugurcan Cakir by reports in Turkey

21 Nov 2019, 08:51 by Caoimhe O'Neill

Liverpool continue to be linked to Turkish shot-stopper Ugurcan Cakir.

Reports in Turkey have regularly suggested Liverpool have sent scouts went to watch the 23-year-old in action for Turkish Super Lig outfit Trabzonspor in recent months.

And now the goalkeeper's agent Engin Kirkpinar has claimed the Champions League winners are one of the clubs interested in acquiring Cakir.

Kirkpinar told Turkish-football.com as cited by The Mirror: "Liverpool are one of four English sides keeping close tabs on Ugurcan.

"Liverpool are among the sides interested in Cakir, and they have been monitoring his progress.

"Trabzonspor value Cakir as being worth a minimum £18m, and will not sell him in January.

"An end-of-season move could however be on the cards."
