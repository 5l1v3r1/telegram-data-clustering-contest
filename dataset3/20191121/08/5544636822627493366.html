House Help Steals Davido’s Daughter, Imade’s International Passport

21 Nov 2019, 08:46 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Brand Influencer and Davido’s first baby mama, Sophia Momodu, has revealed how her trust issues increased since her former house help stole their daughter, Imade’s international passport.

According to her, the house help also carted away with her money and other valuables.

Sophia stated that she was able to recover the valuables two days after the house help had left.

Taking to her Instagram handle to narrate the incident, the brand influencer said ” earlier this year my former assistant stole my money and my daughters yankee passport.

“I didn’t even know it was missing till my oldest nanny told me to please check my room for missing valuables 2 whole days after the girl had left us.

“I thank God for his grace we were able to retrieve most of the things she stole. Since then my trust issues heightened when it comes to workers so I literally just let my mom and auntie do all the talking cos me I don’t even have that strength walahi” she added.
