This fixer-upper cottage for sale in Co Roscommon is less than $30k

21 Nov 2019, 08:58 by IrishCentral Staff

The Beehive Cottage is an "ideal renovation project"  

Looking for a fixer-upper Irish cottage? The Beehive Cottage in Elphin, Co Roscommon is surely in need of intense renovations, but with an asking price of only €25k ($28k), it’s a hard offer to pass up.

Connaughton Auctioneers is managing the sale of this bungalow in Co Roscommon which has the potential to become a cozy homestead or dreamy vacation spot.

The “attractive” two-bedroom cottage is situated on just over an acre of land and is nearby to Elphin, Strokestown, Rooskey, and Carrick-on-Shannon. The home is also located close to many lakes and the River Shannon.

While the bungalow is in need of work, it does currently have mains water, electricity, and a septic tank, which means you wouldn't exactly be starting from scratch if you want to revamp this quaint Irish cottage.

The sellers promise this is “an ideal renovation project” and note that the existing accommodation is “deceptively spacious” and consists of a reception hallway, sitting room, living room / kitchen, and two bedrooms.
