Infinix launches the S5 with an Infinity-O Display in Uganda – Techjaja

21 Nov 2019, 08:26 by pages/Techjaja/291142381017322

Infinix Mobile has announced the launch of the S5, as rumored with an Infinity-O Display in Uganda for under UGX 599,000. The S5 boasts not only of the biggest Infinity-O Display but a 32MP In-Display AI selfie camera as well and 4 AI rear cameras.
The device will come in two options, the 4GB RAM+64G ROM and 6GB RAM+128GB ROM configurations and other specs are as below;

An Infinity-O Display!

One of the key highlights of S5 is its 6.6-inch HD+ Infinity-O Display, which makes it the biggest Infinity-O Display smartphone under RRP UGX 599,000 in the Uganda market. The Infinity-O Display adopts a slimmer, bezel-less design, which allows S5 to achieve a 90.5% screen-to-body ratio. With a 20:9 screen aspect ratio that follows the current cinema screen trends, S5 will provide users with a brilliant viewing experience. Immerse yourself in video streaming, gaming and image capturing!

A 32MP In-Display AI selfie camera!

The S5 sports a 32MP In-Display AI selfie camera with 1.6 μm larger pixels (after 4-in-1), F2.0 aperture and 4-in-1 super pixel technology. When users are taking photos, the In-Display AI selfie camera automatically implements a 4-in-1 effect based on the ISO value, at which point the size of a single-pixel reaches 1.6 μm, enabling consumers to capture sharp, vivid selfies, especially in low-light and backlight environments. HDR+ is the new technology on S5. It’s the combination of Face recognition, intelligent scene recognition and AI HDR technology, which enable users to capture impressive and artistic portraits. S5 is no doubt a backlit portrait master. What’s more, S5’s selfie camera supports an upgraded 3D Face Beauty function and provides 4 exclusive options to let consumers customize their beauty style.

4 AI rear cameras for artistic backlit portraits!

The S5 is also equipped with 4 AI rear cameras and quad-smart flash. The rear camera consists of a 16MP AF high-resolution camera, an ultra-wide-angle lens & macro lens camera, a depth field camera, and an AI lens camera. With the macro lens, users will get closer to the subjects they want to capture and see every vivid detail. In addition, with the ultra-wide-angle lens and macro lens, users can create wide-angle and macro videos in video capturing mode, which is a great improvement in comparison with the last generation.

Crystal feather pattern design!

Inspired by nature and Quetzal bird, S5 adopts a crystal feather pattern design. To bring inspiration to life and ensure that the gradient colors and patterns are perfectly presented on the devices, Infinix teams have invested tremendous efforts and conquered a lot of difficulties. The design of S5 is a manifesto of how Infinix strikes to combine stylish design with the latest technologies and the device is available in three colors – Quetzal Cyan, Nebula Black and Violet.

S5 is equipped with a 4000mAh big battery with Xcharge function. It adopts a MediaTek Helio P22 processor and runs on the AndroidTM 9 Pie system. Together with so many “bests” in the under RRP UGX 599,000 price range, from being the device with the biggest Infinity-O Display, one of the few devices with a 32MP In-Display AI selfie camera, the only device with 4 AI rear cameras, to offering the best memory options – 4GB RAM+64G ROM and 6GB RAM+128GB ROM, Infinix is confident that the S5 will bring an outstanding smartphone experience to young consumers and make them become trendsetters!

Price and where to buy from

The Infinix S5 as articulated earlier will retail for around UGX 599,000 and Infinix will also provide a light version – the S5 Lite. Both versions will be available in all Infinix branded shops countrywide however S5 Lite will arrive later on the market.
