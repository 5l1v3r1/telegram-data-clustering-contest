Abhay Deol gets internet laughing with his latest confession, “I slept with my director”

21 Nov 2019, 08:03 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Abhay Deol whipped up a hilarious storm on social media with his latest post. The actor was snapped dozing on the sets with director Mahesh Majrekar and gave the picture a quirky caption which read, “I finally did it. I slept with my director. On set with Mahesh Manjrekar.” Dressed in the garbs of a police officer, Abhay was snapped sleeping on a wooden chair while actor turned filmmaker Mahesh Majrekar took a nap while it seemed like he was listening to music.

From one user stating, “The most peaceful casting couch ever!”, to another comment, “Sleeping beauties, in between duties!” – internet came up with some hilarious responses to the actor’s funny caption.

Abhay’s last Bollywood outing was ‘Zero’ and he is all set to debut in South with an interesting venture. The actor will mark his Tamil debut with ‘Hero’, wherein he plays the antagonist. Directed by PS Mithran, the film stars Sivakarthikeyan as the lead and will hit the screens on December 20.
