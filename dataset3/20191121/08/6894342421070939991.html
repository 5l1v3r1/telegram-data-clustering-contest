Sony Interactive Entertainment Announces One More Mega Pack For The PS4

21 Nov 2019, 08:01 by Ian Chee

The PlayStation 4 (PS4) is near the end of its life cycle, now that its successor is officially scheduled to be announced next year. But that’s not stopping Sony Interactive Entertainment from releasing a new PS4 Mega Pack. The company is also updating its Party Bundle for the year.

The Mega Pack bundles the PS4 with three of the best games on the platform. They are Grand Theft Auto V Premium Edition, God of War, and Horizon Zero Dawn Complete Edition. All three games come in their physical disks. It also comes a PS4 Fortnite Neo Versa Bundle voucher, as well as a PS Plus 3-month membership subscription.

As for the Party Bundle, the two titles that come with it this year are Fifa 20 and Crash team Racing Nitro-Fueled. There are actually two variants of the bundle, both featuring the same games as well as two DualShock 4 controllers. One comes with a PS4 Pro, while another has the standard PS4 bundled.

The PS4 Mega Pack comes with a RM1349 price tag. As for the Party Bundles, they are priced at RM1499 and RM1899 for the PS4 and PS4 Pro versions, respectively.

Incidentally, if you decide to get a 12-month PS Plus membership subscription card, you shoul do so between 22 November and 2 December. If you get one from selected stores and redeem them within said window, you’ll get two extra months for your subscription at no extra cost.
