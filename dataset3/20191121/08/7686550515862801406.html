Asda slashes prices on TVs, Nintendo Switch, tablets and Fitbit in massive Black Friday sale

These are the best deals we found

21 Nov 2019, 08:57 by Rachel Pugh, Jillian MacMath

Following a three-year gap, Asda has one again decided to take part in Black Friday.

And it has launched a series of offers which certainly won't disappoint shoppers.

The supermarket has slashed prices on some of the most in demand items this Christmas, including Nintendo Switch Lite, Fitbit, Polaroid TVs and Now TV sticks.

However, there's just one catch - most of the offers aren't available online, so you'll have to head to a store near you to find them, the MEN reports.

Here are some of the best bargains we've found.

Nintendo Switch Lite (turquoise, grey and yellow) - £169.99 instead of £199.99

Polaroid Smart Tv 49" - £229 instead of £299

RCA Gemini 2in1 10" Tablet £89 instead of £129

Original Crock Pot - £20 instead of £40

Now TV Smart Stick - £15 instead of £30

Fitbit Inspire - £49 instead of £69

Tefal 9-Piece Cookware sets - £40 instead of £70

If you're planning on heading to Asda to bag some Black Friday deals, you're advised to call ahead to check stock with the store to avoid disappointment.

You can find your nearest supermarket by using Asda's branch finder.
