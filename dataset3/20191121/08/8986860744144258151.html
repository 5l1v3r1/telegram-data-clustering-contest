Forget about your locked up monies at Menzgold – Ibrah One

21 Nov 2019, 08:23 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

One of Ghana’s big boy Ibrah One seems not to be done with Nana Appiah Mensah and his Menzgold Investment company as he has launched another attack on them.

According to Ibrah One in his new post sighted, customers of Menzgold should forget about their monies which have been locked up for over a year because there won’t be any better outcome.

He added though the matter is in court, he knows for sure that nothing better can come out.

He posted: “My dear Menzgold customers, it’s better you forget about your locked up money and channel your energy in making some new monies for yourself!

NAM 1’s court case will be dragged in court till 2022. Just another trick to escape paying you your monies.”
