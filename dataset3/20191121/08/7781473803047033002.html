CFMoto's first dealership inaugurated in Mumbai

21 Nov 2019, 08:35 by Team OD  

CFMoto launched four motorcycles in the month of July and this time around the manufacturer has inaugurated its first dealership in the country for its potential customer's to have a more hands-on experience. The dealership situated in Mumbai, in Flower Valley Complex Z5-Wing, Eastern Express Highway, Runwal Nagar, Thane West will offer sales, service and spare parts support. Customers can visit the showrooms for a test ride should they wish. The showroom will retail all the four CFMoto motorcycles - 300NK, 650NK, 650MT and the 650GT.

Kenneth Chen, Assistant General Manager - Global Sales CFmoto said, "We are happy to unveil our first individual outlet in Mumbai. The company has seen a tremendous acceptance for our launched models and we look forward to exceeding our customer's expectation by providing them the best value with our product range. The confidence placed by CFMoto's aficionados and our dealer partners are truly helping us in building the brand's confidence which will help us offering an un-paralleled customer experience with best in-class sales and service.

The following are the prices for the CFMoto's portfolio in India:

CFMoto 300NK - Rs 2.29 lakh

CFMoto 650NK - Rs 3.99 lakh

CFMoto 650MT - Rs 4.99 lakh

CFMoto 650GT - Rs 5.49 lakh

All prices are ex-showroom pan India.

The 300NK gets a single-cylinder engine that displaces 292cc and produces 33.99PS of power and 20.5Nm of torque. The engine mated to a six-speed gearbox.

The 650 range of motorcycles gets a parallel-twin, 649cc engine that puts out 71PS and 62Nm of torque. And come mated to a six-speed transmission.
