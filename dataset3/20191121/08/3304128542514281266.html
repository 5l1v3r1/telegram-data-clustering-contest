Star Newspaper Journalist Found Dead In Police Woman’s House

21 Nov 2019, 08:03 by Wycliffe

Radio Africa Group journalist Eric Oloo is dead.

Oloo, a Star Newspaper correspondent based in Siaya County, was found dead with a stab wound in the house of a female chief inspector of police in Ugunja on Thursday morning.

The officer said to have been in a romantic relationship with the deceased has since been arrested and is aiding police with investigations.

Siaya County Police Commander Francis Kooli confirmed the incident saying the body had bloodstains on the head.

The body of the deceased was moved to Ambira Sub-county hospital mortuary awaiting post-mortem.
