Emily Atack steals Holly Willoughby's jungle style for I'm A Celeb spin-off show

The comedian confessed she was blown away by Holly's I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here wardrobe last year

21 Nov 2019, 08:42 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Emily Atack has stolen Holly Willoughby's jungle style.

After being blown away by the presenter's style last year, it's no wonder the actress enlisted the same stylist for herself this year.

The Inbetweeners star, 29, hired Australian-based Angie Smith who was the fashionably trained eye behind Holly's jungle looks last year.

Extra Camp's Emily showed the similarities didn't bother her while she worked all her best angles for her snaps to document each of her different looks on Instagram .

Just days into the show, the presenter has already dressed in a similar plaid button-down minidress for the day.

Holly had nailed a plaid look last year in a button-down minidress but she pulled off the flirty number in black and red.

Emily also accessorised her look with Doc Marten-style boots like Holly did as she wanted to achieve the same chic vibe.

So it appears fans will expect to see some zebra print and floral dresses in the weeks to come.

Last year, Holly jetted off to the I'm A Celeb jungle for three weeks to present alongside Dec Donnelly.

The blonde bombshell had been filling in for Ant McPartlin who stepped down from his TV duties following his drink-drive arrest.

Holly had cemented her style status on This Morning so it's no surprise there was a lot of attention on what clothes she was wearing.

Last year's jungle princess Emily wanted to ensure she was the queen of style when she assigned the help of Holly's former jungle stylist.

The former I'm A Celeb star has made her own name in the fashion spheres with her own In The Style collection.

In March, the comedian confessed she was blown away by Holly's I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here wardrobe last year.

The stand up act admitted the style icon never puts a fashionable foot wrong as she gushed about how much she loved her jungle looks.

She told Hello! magazine : "[Holly] is amazing - she can't do any wrong. I LOVED her outfits in the jungle. It's hilarious really; when we are in there - you know, we didn't really look our best - we had these mad jungle clothes on.

"So when Holly comes in with her beautiful outfits, it was so refreshing on the eyes! Every time she and Dec came in, we were like 'Woah!' They smelled amazing too."
