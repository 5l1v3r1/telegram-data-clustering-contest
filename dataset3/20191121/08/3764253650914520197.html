Mohamed Salah, Andrew Robertson, doubtful for Premier League clash against Crystal Palace

21 Nov 2019, 08:43

- Mohamed Salah could miss Liverpool's Premier League clash against Crystal Palace this weekend

- The Egyptian alongside Andrew Robertson was rested from the FIFA break due to the ankle injury they suffered earlier this month

- Although, there is a slim chance they might be back for the game in one of Saturday's encounter

- Joe Gomez is also unfit yet but they are hoping they can get him ready for the encounter

Liverpool duo of Mohamed Salah and Andrew Robertson are reportedly doubtful for their Premier League clash against Crystal Palace this weekend.

The two players are said to have failed fitness tests having suffered ankle injuries before the international break.

They were also excluded from their respective national team games in a bid to rest well ahead of their trip to Selhurst Park but it doesn't look like they would be available for selection in the game.
