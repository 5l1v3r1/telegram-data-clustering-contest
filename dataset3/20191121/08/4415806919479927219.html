Former Scotland captain Tom Smith fighting colorectal cancer

21 Nov 2019, 08:10

Former Scotland captain Tom Smith is determined to tackle his fight against cancer head on.

The 48-year-old prop forward – who won 61 international caps between 1997 and 2005 as well as touring wit the British and Irish Lions – has been diagnosed with stage four colorectal cancer.

Smith has received intensive rounds of chemotherapy and is also undergoing radiotherapy after the cancer spread to his liver and brain.
