Kim's spiteful revenge, wedding and stripper drama and 8 more Emmerdale spoilers

21 Nov 2019, 08:00

There is revenge in mind over in Emmerdale as Kim Tate (Claire King) is determined to make Graham Foster (Andrew Scarborough) in the wake of his romance with Rhona Goskirk (Zoe Henry) – and her party leads to chaos as she makes her move.

Meanwhile, Cain Dingle (Jeff Hordley) is out to make Moira (Natalie J Robb) pay over her recent affair as he stops her from seeing Kyle. In other dramatic developments, Bernice Thomas (Samantha Giles) is fearful as her wedding plans start to fall apart – and there is some shocking news ahead.
