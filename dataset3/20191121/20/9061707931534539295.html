'It's not good' - Derby County star admits frustrations since arrival

Derby County latest - Evans joined the Rams from Reading in the summer 2018, and things haven't quite gone to plan for the midfielder

21 Nov 2019, 20:54 by Jamie Kemble

Derby County midfielder George Evans has opened up on his injury frustrations since joining the club.

Evans' playtime has been limited since arriving from Reading in 2018 due to a number of injury issues.

The 24-year-old has made just 11 league appearances since arriving at Pride Park, six of those coming this season.

And Evans himself has admitted just how frustrating his time so far at Derby has been due to those setbacks.

"Similar to last year, I felt like I just started to get going and then I picked up another injury," he told RamsTV.

"Since I’ve been here, I’ve been very frustrated. It’s been very stop-start and it’s not good.

"Hopefully it’s all behind me now and I can kick on."

Evans is under contract with the Rams until 2021, joining on an undisclosed fee from the Royals in 2018.

He began his career at Manchester City, before joining Reading in 2016.
