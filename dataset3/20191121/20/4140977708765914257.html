KAR Vs JHA Dream11 Team : Rampant Karnataka look to emulate their resplendent display from yesterday and make it two wins on the bounce.

Coming into the revered land of the Super League stage of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2019 having won five out of their six encounters in Group A, Karnataka had ended up earning a spot in the echelons of the top contenders. It was one which came not only due to their exceptional form in this tournament but the fact that this was a side which had also ended up winning the foregone Vijay Hazare Trophy.

And the team ensured that they continued their rapturous form when they played their first Super League contest against Tamil Nadu yesterday as well with the side marauding their way to a sublime and pristine 9 wicket win. In what was a terrific display in all the departments of the game, the team ended up walking all over their opponents as they chased down the 159 run target at hand with 9 wickets and over 3 overs in hand.

When they take on Jharkhand who were defeated yesterday, Karnataka will be making an advent into the encounter quietly confident of cruising to yet another win and further consolidating their first spot in Group B of the Super League. This is a Jharkhand side which was ripped into and blown apart by 109 runs by Punjab as they faltered and fell apart in their pursuit of 200 to register a mere and sorry 90 runs on the scoreboard.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

We perhaps couldn’t have augured for a more august and sanctum wicket for this encounter with this being a track where the ball bounces at just the right height with the batsmen being accorded all the room in the world to cream the ball to the fence. With over 400 runs being scored here yesterday, this promises to be another behemoth scoring affair with the team winning the toss all set to come out and bat first.
