Does Myles Stephenson have a girlfriend and when did he date Gabby Allen?

21 Nov 2019, 20:00

X-Factor co-winner Myles Stephenson is in the middle of his first week on I’m A Celebrity but a psychic predicted that he’ll be the winner before he even entered the jungle.

Psychic Chris Riley said he feels ‘very drawn’ to the Rak-Su member, who has been appearing on the reality TV show since Sunday night.

While we wait to see whether or not this psychic’s prediction is correct, here’s what we know about whether anyone’s been, erm, drawn to him in a different way lately.

Does Myles Stephenson have a girlfriend?

Rak-Su singer Myles does not seem to be dating anyone at the moment, which is understandable given that he’s newly single and currently in the middle of the Australian jungle.

Myles, 28, previously dated Love Island 2017 alum Gabby Allen, 27, from September 2018 until August 2019.

They’d moved in together at the start of 2019, however it seems their relationship was not meant to be, as their split came amid claims that Myles had been unfaithful.

Gabby previously announced their split by telling her 1.1 million Instagram followers: ‘The newspaper articles are true. I am no longer in a relationship. I hope youse (sic) have all had a better week than me! If anyone else has anything they need to tell me, please do.’

In April 2018, Dan Osborne was seen on holiday with Gabby. Dan has been married to Myles’ fellow I’m A Celebrity contestant Jacqueline Jossa since 2017 but they briefly split after Dan was spotted with the former-islander.

A source has now told The Sun that Myles will be up for telling Jacqueline ‘everything’ she might want to know.

The source said: ‘Myles would be willing to tell Jacqueline everything – and answer any questions she might have.

‘Myles will tell Jacqueline everything Gabby told him. It’s a good thing Gabby’s gone to Thailand for a month because she might find it uncomfortable watching.’

Representatives for Dan declined to comment further.

I’m A Celebrity continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
