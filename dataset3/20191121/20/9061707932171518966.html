Latest Preston North End injury news ahead of Derby County clash

Derby County latest - The Rams are back in action following the international break on Saturday when they welcome Preston to Pride Park

21 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Jamie Kemble

Preston North End are fit and ready for their trip to Pride Park Stadium this weekend.

The Lilywhites head to Derby in fine form, sitting second in the table and only two points behind leaders West Bromwich Albion.

On Saturday, they will be looking to continue their fine start to the campaign against a Rams side sitting 16th in the table after a bumpy start.

And they will be helped by a clean bill of health.

An update on Preston's official website today confirmed captain Tom Clarke and Josh Harrop have recovered from knee and hamstring injuries respectively, both coming through a behind-closed-doors game this week.

North End will also be boosted by the return from suspension of Ben Pearson, and only one player will definitely miss out.

Louis Moult is recovering from an ACL injury suffered in August.

Derby are expecting to benefit from the return of Scott Malone, while Tom Lawrence will recover from illness.

The Rams will, however, likely be without Tom Huddlestone, who continues to battle hamstring issues.
