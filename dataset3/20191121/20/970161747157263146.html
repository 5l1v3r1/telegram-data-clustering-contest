21 Nov 2019, 20:30

I’m A Celebrity‘s Ian Wright hasn’t had the easiest few days since arriving in the I’m A Celebrity jungle.

Having roughed it in Snake Rock and faced a terrifying helmet full of scorpions in a Bushtucker Trial, things also got tense between him and comedian Andrew Maxwell over their crocodile foot dinner, in scenes aired on Wednesday night.

All of which has led to speculation that he could walk from the show before he is eliminated – with Ladbrokes offering odds of just 4-1 on him quitting the series of his own accord.

The former footballer was one of the first celebs tipped for the jungle this year – and rumour has it he’s also one of the best paid.

But just how much is he earning for his time on the show?

Here’s what you need to know…

How much is Ian Wright getting paid for I’m A Celebrity?

It’s been reported that Ian Wright is the second-highest paid star of the 2019 series behind Caitlyn Jenner.

According to the reports the ex-Arsenal and England striker is pocketing £400,000 for his appearance on the show – although that’s a little less than 2018 winner Harry Redknapp claimed he was getting recently.

Last year’s king of the jungle accidentally let the secret of Ian’s appearance on the show slip last month – before the line-up had been confirmed – during an appearance in Nottingham, where he also dropped hints about his salary.

An audience member promptly tweeted what he had said, saying: ‘Interestingly, he let slip that Ian Wright has been paid £1m to appear on this years #ImACeleb.’

What is Ian Wright’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth Ian has a fortune of around £15m.

The 56-year-old played for Crystal Palace and West Ham United during his career as well as Arsenal, where he spent seven years from 1991 to 1998, and the England team.

He was also a part-time coach for Milton Keynes Dons from 2012-2013.

Since retiring from the game he has become a football pundit, firstly for Match Of The Day, and more recently for BT Sport, covering the 2014 World Cup and Euro 2016 for them.

He’s also been a regular fixture on TV, appearing on such shows as They Think It’s All Over, Gladiators, Live From Studio Five, Friends Like These and his own talk show, Friday Night’s All Wright.

I’m A Celebrity continues on ITV on Thursday night at 9pm.
