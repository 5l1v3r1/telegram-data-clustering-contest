Every Premier League game BT Sport will show live on New Year's Day including Burnley v Aston Villa

Aston Villa latest news | Dean Smith's side make the trip north to Burnley on New Year's Day and they will be on TV and here's how you can watch the match and every other

21 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Elliott Jackson

BT Sport will be the only place to watch Premier League football on New Year's Day.

For those nursing a hangover from the night before and wanting to curl up in front of the TV, you will need a BT Sport subscription to catch all the Premier League football.

The broadcasters have once again thrown a spanner in the works for Sky Sports after they won the rights to show all ten Premier League fixtures exclusively on January 1 - including Aston Villa 's trip to Burnley (12.30pm).

BT Sport will kick off its New Year schedule with live coverage of Chelsea's trip to Brighton, with the game kicking off at 12.30pm at the Amex Stadium.
