21 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Gautam Kapoor

RJS Vs HAR Dream11 : Haryana begin their Super League sojourn today as they look to dish out a second straight win to Rajasthan. 

Playing their first match of the Super League stage yesterday, for Rajasthan at the end of the day, the runs they ended up leaking were too much to get around. Paired with that, the side kept losing wickets at regular intervals, a predicament which saw them fall to a 15 run defeat despite a late surge bat their middle-order.

Needing 202 as they took on Baroda in what was an edge of the seat thriller, for all the clean hitting sorties in the death overs, the side ended up suffering the aberrations of a shambolic top-order collapse. It not only saw them totter towards the target but see the net run-rate climb to a colossal 11 per over in the culminating overs to see the side fold out their effort at 186.

And when they face off against Haryana come their second match within the space of day, the side know they can’t afford to repeat the mistakes of yesterday. Playing their first match of this stage, Haryana will be looking to come hot out of the blocks against a hurting Rajasthan unit, one which is there for the taking as things stand.

Haryana themselves are an extremely formidable unit with their second place in Group D being a testament to the pristine brand of cricket the same have engaged their ardent supporters in. The side come into today’s encounter carrying with them one of the longest winning sprees with the side having registered clinical wins in all their previous five outings, a form they’ll be looking to reverberate come today as well.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

A tricky wicket to bat on, this surface will relatively play out in favour of the bats in the second part of the encounter with the pitch settling in the under sun. The side winning the toss is bound to come out and field first today and allow their spinners to restrict the opposition to a low score and give their batters ample time to chase down the target.
