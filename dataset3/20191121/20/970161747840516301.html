Who did Cliff Parisi play in EastEnders as he joins I'm A Celebrity 2019 cast?

21 Nov 2019, 20:00

EastEnders star Cliff Parisi has joined Coronation Street’s Andrew Whyment to become the two brand new camp-mates to enter the jungle and join the cast of I’m A Celebrity 2019.

Ant and Dec revealed last night that Cliff and Andy would be joining the other stars, including Caitlyn Jenner, Nadine Coyle, Roman Kemp and Jacqueline Jossa in the Australian jungle camp – but not until they had taken part in their first Bushtucker Trial.

Cliff has made appearances in both Hollyoaks and Casualty over the years as well as playing Fred Buckle the handyman in Call the Midwife since 2012.

The actor is probably best-known for his time starring in EastEnders – but who did he play?

Who did Cliff Parisi play in EastEnders?

Cliff Parisi played trouble-making Ricky ‘Minty’ Peterson the mechanic in EastEnders for eight years from 2002 to 2010.
