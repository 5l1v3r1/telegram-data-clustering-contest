Punjabi film ‘Doorbeen’ premieres on Amazon Prime Video

21 Nov 2019, 20:40 by Raj Baddhan

Amazon Prime Video has premiered the Punjabi film ‘Doorbeen’ (2019) on its platform.

The film starring Ninja, Wamiqa Gabbi and Jass Bajwa, is about Shinda, a small time crook, who lives in Punjab and invents new ways to smuggle liquor inside the territory while trying to win the love of his life.

‘Doorbeen’ is directed by Ishan Chopra.
