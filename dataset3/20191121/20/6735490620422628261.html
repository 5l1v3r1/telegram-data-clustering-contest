Harry Styles And James Corden Take Over L.A Traffic To Film Skits

21 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Jamie Samhan

Harry Styles and James Corden have taken over the streets of Los Angeles.

With the upcoming release of Styles’ new album Fine Line on Dec. 13 and his Love on Tour, the former One Direction member is busy on his press stint.

For those who are big fans of Styles, they will remember the 2017 episode of “Carpool Karaoke” where two famously switch shirts multiple times.

The two are back up to their mischief and from pictures and clips that have been emerging online, a new skit is coming but perhaps not another episode of “Carpool Karaoke”.

Pictures were taken of the two that show Styles and Corden on a sidewalk as they hug it out.

Other clips on Twitter show the two running into traffic with Corden in a yellow unitard before quickly moving before the light changes to green or the “Sign Of The Times” singer hanging from a zipline as Corden dressed as cloud pulls him along.
