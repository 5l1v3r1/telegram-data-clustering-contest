Phoenix Rising FC midfielder Solomon Asante named USL Championship MVP

21 Nov 2019, 20:18 by Cydeni Carter

PHOENIX — Phoenix Rising Football Club had a record breaking season and team captain, Solomon Asante led the team, earning himself the USL Championship Most Valuable Player.

During the 2019 season, Asante was named the regular-season USL Championship Golden Boot Winner with 22 goals and 16 assists, breaking the club and league record for combined goals and assists. He also lead the team in goals, assists, shots, and chances created.
