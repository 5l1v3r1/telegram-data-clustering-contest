'Toxic' - This is how Aston Villa fans will welcome back Steve Bruce

Aston Villa v Newcastle United | Former claret and blue manager will be bringing his Newcastle United side to his old stomping ground in the Premier League on Monday

21 Nov 2019, 20:25 by Richard Cusack

Some Aston Villa fans have been discussing how they will react when Steve Bruce returns to Villa Park on Monday night.

Bruce brings his Newcastle United side to B6 in a crucial Premier League clash in front of the television cameras (8pm) hoping to inflict a defeat on the club that sacked him just over a year ago.

The former Birmingham City manager was relieved of his duties in October last year after a 3-3 draw to Preston North End at Villa Park.

He spent two years at the club, leading Villa to the 2018 Championship play-off final where his side suffered a 1-0 loss to Fulham.

Dean Smith took over Bruce and led Villa to a second successive Wembley trip, beating Derby County in the Championship's showpiece occasion.

It is widely acknowledged that Bruce steadied an uneasy ship he inherited from the brief reign of Roberto Di Matteo in 2016 and averted what would have been a disastrous relegation in 2016-17.

And Villa fans have had their say on social media as to how they will greet Bruce.
