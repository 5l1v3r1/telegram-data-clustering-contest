Sasha's Last Night On Earth Launching New Limited Edition Everpress Long Sleeve Tee

This will launch a new line of clothing for LNOE.

21 Nov 2019, 20:54 by Magnetic

Sasha’s Last Night On Earth label is launching a new limited collaboration with Everpress on a batch of long sleeve tees.

In addition to the limited shirt with Everpress, LNOE is launching a line titled, LNOE: Dance Like There’s No Tomorrow. It will be the first collection of a new series of clothing lines for the brand, and consists of a tee, jumper and tote, all gender neutral. It is made from 100% organic cotton and is waste-free. 

The shirt will be released on December 5 and can be pre-ordered now.
