Houses evacuated and road closed as police deal with man refusing to leave house

A 36-year-old man has since been arrested for threats to cause damage by arson

21 Nov 2019, 20:09 by Katie-Ann Gupwell

A man has been arrested after residents were evacuated because an occupant in the street refused to leave a property.

South Wales Police confirmed officers attended a property in Gower View Road, Gorseinon, on Thursday at around 3.15pm after concerns were raised about the welfare of the occupant, who refused to leave.

The fire service also attended and, due to concerns for their safety, a number of neighbours were evacuated and the road was closed.

At around 7pm a 36-year-old man was arrested for threats to cause damage by arson.

Police have now confirmed the road has re-opened. Officers have also thanked residents for the patience when they dealt with the incident.
