Who does Andrew Whyment play in Coronation Street and is he leaving?

21 Nov 2019, 20:30

Coronation Street’s Andrew Whyment left viewers confused after I’m A Celebrity 2019 launched last week without the star entering the jungle as the leaked line-up had initially promised.

However, the line-up mystery was solved last night after it was revealed that Andrew would indeed be joining the likes of Caitlyn Jenner, Nadine Coyle, Roman Kemp and Jacqueline Jossa in the Australian jungle camp – along with another new campmate, EastEnders’ Cliff Parisi.

Andy and Chris will make their Bushtucker Trial debut tonight on the show, with a hunt for stars among guts and worms proving to be a far cry from The Cobbles or Albert Square.

Who does Andrew Whyment play in Coronation Street?

Andrew Whyment plays Kirk Sutherland in Coronation Street, the older brother of Maria Connor (Samia Longchambon) and Beth Tinker’s (Lisa George) husband.

Andrew joined the soap back in 2000 and has been a regular on the ITV show ever since.
