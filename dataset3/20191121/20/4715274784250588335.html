Quarantine first

21 Nov 2019, 20:13 by Norman Cruz

Thousands of Filipinos who work abroad line up Thursday for international Certificate of Vaccination at the Bureau of Quarantine in Port Area Manila after several countries required OFWs to have polio vaccine.

Meanwhile, McDonald’s Philippines and the Department of Health announce their partnership for the “Sabayang Patak Kontra Polio,” with McDonald’s Philippines President Kenneth Yang and Health Secretary Francisco Duque III attending the ceremonial opening.
