Man stabbed ‘multiple times’ during fight at Gold Coast pub

21 Nov 2019, 20:16 by Shanee Dobeson

A MAN has been rushed to hospital after allegedly being stabbed multiple times at a pub on the Gold Coast overnight.

It’s understood the 21-year-old victim and a 30-year-old man, who were not known to each other, got into an argument at the licensed premises on the corner of Tooloona Street and Golden Four Drive around 6.30pm.

The older man is then accused of pulling out a knife and stabbing the other man multiple times in the chest and abdomen.

The younger man was treated at the scene before being taken to Gold Coast University Hospital, where he remains in a serious condition.

Police say the 30-year-old man later surrendered himself to officers and was transported to Gold Coast University Hospital for treatment to his finger.

He has since been charged with one count of grievous bodily harm and will appear in Southport Magistrates Court on December 3.
