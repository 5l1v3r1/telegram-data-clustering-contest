Oman weather: PACDA issues statement on missing child

21 Nov 2019, 20:05 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Public Authority for Civil Defence and Ambulances (PACDA) has denied reports about finding the 15-year-old child who went missing in Al Hoqain area in Al Rustaq on Thursday.

In a statement issued online, PACDA said the search for the child will resume on Friday morning. PACDA, meanwhile, managed to rescue a person who was stranded inside his vehicle in a wadi in Al Qurum.
