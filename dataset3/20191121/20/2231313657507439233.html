Best Way to Invest Money: Value Investing vs Growth Investing

Is one approach actually better?

21 Nov 2019, 20:25 by Motley Fool Staff, Chris Hill, Ron Gross, and Emily Flippen

As long as people have been buying stocks, there have been strategies to recognize and profit from buying business before the market realizes what they're really worth. Among investors, two strategies predominate: value and growth. Which one is better? That's a debate nearly as old as investing itself.  

In this live broadcast from our YouTube channel, we break down the differences between value and growth investing and the ways investors can use bits of both to their advantage!
