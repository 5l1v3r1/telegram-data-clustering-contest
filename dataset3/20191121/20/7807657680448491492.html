MechWarrior 5 drops new video on customization and destruction

Take a look at some of the metallic carnage that awaits.

21 Nov 2019, 20:46 by Dan Thorp-Lancaster

Source: Piranha Games

What you need to know

  • A new video walkthrough offers a closer look at MechWarrior 5's single-player gameplay.
  • The video shows off some of the customization options for your mechs, how you'll choose missions, and more.
  • MechWarrior 5 Mercenaries is due to launch on December 10 and is available to preorder on the Epic Games Store.

The next entry in the MechWarrior series, MechWarrior 5 Mercenaries, is almost upon us, and the team at Piranha Games has offered a new peek at some of the single-player gameplay. In a new walkthrough video, you can check out some of the destruction that awaits, along with a look at customization options for your mech, how you'll choose your missions, and more.
