SA's breaking news blog, The Pulse: 18yo man dies in crash on Stuart Hwy | 13yo arrested for starting fires in Adelaide's south

21 Nov 2019, 20:01

An 18-year-old Roxby Downs man has died in a crash on Stuart Hwy, bringing the state’s road toll to 99. Meanwhile, a 13-year-old has been arrested for lighting several fires in the southern suburbs. Follow all the morning's breaking news in The Pulse.

About 6:50pm on Thursday police arrived at the scene in Kootaberra - about 50km north of Port Augusta - where the car left the road.

The teen was the only occupant in the car.

The Stuart Hwy was closed in both directions for several hours last night but has since reopened.

At the same time last year there were 68 deaths on our roads.

Meanwhile a 13-year-old girl has been arrested for lighting several fires in the southern suburbs as South Australia faces one of its worst start to the bushfire season.

The fires were lit on Burgess Dr at Noarlunga Downs, Goldsmith Dr and Grey Box Ave at Noarlunga Centre just before 11pm last night.

A suspect was seen running from the scene before the police helicopter and police dog Axe searched the area.

Another blaze was reported on Oslo Cres, Hackham West an hour later.

A Hackham West teenager, 13, will face Christies Beach Magistrates Court charged with causing a bushfire today.

These fires burnt vegetation, no properties were damaged.
