Chris Jericho hits out after NXT tops AEW in the 'Wednesday Night Wars' ratings

21 Nov 2019, 22:56 by Gary Cassidy

With WWE NXT beating AEW Dynamite in the 'Wednesday Night Wars' on the measurement of television ratings for the first time, a few feathers have certainly been ruffled - and one man who's spoken out is current AEW World Champion Chris Jericho.

This week, NXT hit a whopping 916,000 viewers on the go-home show for NXT TakeOver: WarGames and WWE Survivor Series, while AEW finished up just 23,000 viewers below on 893,000.

NXT's increase was substantial, having only reached 750,000 the week before, while AEW was very slightly down on last week. Chris Jericho, though spoke out saying it was a marathon, not a sprint.
