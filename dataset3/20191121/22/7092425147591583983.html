Details released following incident at Rosslare Harbour

21 Nov 2019, 22:18 by Ciara Walsh

Gardai have provided an update after 16 people were found in a sealed trailer on the vehicle deck during a routine inspection.

Gardai announced earlier on Thursday that they were investigating potential breaches of immigration legislation which may have occurred onboard a Stena Line Ferry that arrived in Rosslare.

They have since offered an update on the situation, saying that each of the 16 people on board were males, and that one of them may be a juvenile.

It is also believed that all of the men found are of Middle Eastern origin. The owners are co-operating fully with the ongoing investigation.

The driver is a male in his 50's, who's been a resident of Ireland for over 25 years and he is also co-operating fully with the ongoing investigation. The articulated lorry was photographed and examined on the ferry and has now been removed to a secure location for further technical examination.

An Garda Siochana have said that they will continue to investigate all the circumstances of this incident.
