Alabama To Honor Redshirt Junior On Senior Day

21 Nov 2019, 22:18 by Alek Arend

Alabama star linebacker Terrell Lewis has been a critical piece for the Crimson Tide’s defense over the past few years. Many expect this to be his last season with Alabama before he moves on to the NFL.

That speculation has seemingly been confirmed. Lewis, a redshirt junior, will take part in Alabama’s Senior Day this Saturday.

It’s clear that this will be the linebacker’s final home game with the Crimson Tide.
