Caitlyn Jenner claims to have 20 grandkids but viewers say 'maths doesn't add up'

I'm A Celebrity star Caitlyn Jenner explained what her grandkids call her while opening up on family life - but some viewers struggled to work out who they all are

21 Nov 2019, 22:53 by Irishmirror.ie

Caitlyn Jenner claimed to have 20 grandkids on tonight's I'm A Celebrity - but viewers are insisting the 'maths doesn't add up'.

The reality TV star, 70, opened up about family life in an intimate jungle chat with some of her fellow campmates.

When Kate Garraway questioned how many grandchildren she had, Caitlyn said: "20 in December."

Asked what the grand children called her, Caitlyn said: "They’ve come up with ‘Boom Boom’, it kinda stuck, it was easy for them to say."

But some viewers struggled to work out who all of the grandkis are - with many admitting they could only count 16.

Taking to Twitter , one baffled viewer said: "Pretty sure Caitlyn don’t have 20 grandkids...? Who do the last 4 belong too"

Another asked: "Caitlyn Jenner saying she’ll have 20 grandchildren by December on but my maths isn’t adding up... Who had the last 4? What’s going on?"

A third said: "Did Caitlyn says she has 20 grandkids?... Name them..."

While a fourth agreed: "Did Caitlin just say 20 grandkids? Someone do the maths for me"
