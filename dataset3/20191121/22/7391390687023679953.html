Your Complete Shawn Mendes & Camila Cabello Relationship Timeline, From PDA To Tattoos

21 Nov 2019, 22:05

Pop singers and now-confirmed lovers Camila Cabello and Shawn Mendes's relationship timeline has been a hot topic as of late, and it all but reached fever pitch back in August, when they got just close enough onstage to kiss at the 2019 MTV Video Music Awards...before pulling away. A month later, they finally locked lips at one of Mendes' concerts in Toronto (more on that later), but there was still no confirmation that they were together — just a whole lot of paparazzi pics and online fodder.

It took a whole three-ish months (and lots more kissing) for Mendes to finally use the word "dating" in reference to Cabello. During a Fan Q&A in Adelaide, Australia, he revealed they'd been official since July 4th. So at this point, they've been together for just under five months.

That might not be a long time, but the the two musicians knew each other for several years before getting romantically involved — since 2014, to be exact — which could be why they took things slow in the beginning (you know, aside from all that PDA). Now that they are together, though, let's reminisce on how it all came to be, shall we?

2014: They Met

Cabello and Mendes first crossed paths in 2014 when they were both opening for Austin Mahone. Mendes had not yet released his debut album; Cabello was still a part of Fifth Harmony. Five years may not seem that long ago on paper, but in the pop music world? It might as well be a full century. Needless to say, a great deal has changed since they met on Austin Mahone: Live on Tour.

In their recent joint interview with V magazine, Mendes and Cabello waxed nostalgic about the moment they first met. Cabello said, “I remember thinking we met on the Austin Mahone tour, and I remember I wanted to hang out with you, but you were always on the tour bus, just learning guitar.” Mendes admitted that he “didn’t talk to anybody,” and then went on to add,

“You were the only person that talked to me. Like, you were the only one of everybody on that tour who would say words to me. Actually, I think if we go into our DM’s or something, there’s a photo from the day we met, I think.”
