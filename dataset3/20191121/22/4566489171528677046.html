Exclusive premiere: 'Jingle Bells' acoustic video by JT Hodges 

Country artist JT Hodges exclusively premieres his new acoustic video for "Jingle Bells." Hodges chatted with Digital Journal about his latest endeavors.

21 Nov 2019, 22:58 by Markos Papadatos

Regarding his new acoustic video for "Jingle Bells," Hodges said, "I wanted to release content that was different from everybody else. I want to celebrate the holidays and the new Christmas movie. It is a little too early to start decorating my house for Christmas but it was great that we got to shoot this acoustic video. This video gets you in the Christmas spirit."
