Katie McGlynn's incredible transformation since leaving Coronation Street leaves viewers gobsmacked

Recently, Corrie viewers have tragically seen Sinead Tinker exit the long-running ITV1 soap after being diagnosed with terminal cancer

18 Nov 2019, 06:52 by James Rodger, Charlotte Hadfield, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

A recently departed Coronation Street star has stunned fans with a radical transformation.

Recently, Corrie viewers have tragically seen Sinead Tinker exit the long-running ITV1 soap.

Sinead, who was married to Daniel Osborne, tragically died after being diagnosed with terminal cancer.

Her death has sparked an outpouring of grief on the cobbles, as actress Katie McGlynn moves on to pastures new.

This weekend, the 26-year-old actress flaunted her new look as she donned a golden dress while partying.

Katie attended Loose Women panelist Denise Welch's annual charity ball last night and called the gown the "dress of dreams", reports the Liverpool Echo.

 

She posed for pictures with her friends Lee Bennett and former Corrie co-star Alexandra Mardell who plays Emma Brooker in the soap.

Since leaving the soap, Katie has been hitting the gym and has been sharing constant updates about the progress of her fitness regime with her Instagram followers.

After posting pictures at the charity event last night, Katie's followers were quick to tell her how beautiful she looked.
