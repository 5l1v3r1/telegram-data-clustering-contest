INEC declares Kogi West Senatorial rerun inconclusive

18 Nov 2019, 06:39

By James Azania, Lokoja

 

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) is to announce the date for the supplementary in Saturday’s Kogi West Senatorial District rerun.

While Senator Smart Adeyemi of the All Progressives Congress (APC) has 80,118, Senator Dino Melaye of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) polled 59,548, a winner has yet to be declared.

The Returning Officer (RO), Prof. Olayide Lawal declared the Kogi West Senatorial election as inconclusive and that supplementary election will be conducted.

Affected are 53 polling units and 46,127 registered voters, as INEC decide on the new date for the supplementary poll.
