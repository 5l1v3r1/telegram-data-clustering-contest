Monday's Liverpool transfer talk news roundup: Kylian Mbappe, Adam Lallana, Joe Gelhardt

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Liverpool eye possible January reinforcements.

18 Nov 2019, 06:21 by Andy Brent

Jurgen Klopp's title contenders were not particularly busy during the summer and are unlikely to make any major moves in January either, but that has not stopped the rumour mill from linking them with additions.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest news on possible comings and goings from Anfield.

Monday morning's headlines:

Liverpool 'rule out signing Kylian Mbappe next summer'
Liverpool reportedly rule out the possibility of making an approach to sign Kylian Mbappe from Paris Saint-Germain next summer. Read more.

Manchester United, Liverpool 'still interested in Joe Gelhardt'
Manchester United and Liverpool are still interested in Wigan Athletic teenager Joe Gelhardt despite the forward's new deal, according to a report. Read more.

Paris Saint-Germain 'line up Adam Lallana as Edinson Cavani replacement'
Liverpool attacker Adam Lallana emerges as a transfer target for Paris Saint-Germain, according to a report. Read more.
