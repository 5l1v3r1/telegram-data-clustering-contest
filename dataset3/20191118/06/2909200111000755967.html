Claims of boxer shorts treating kidney diseases, others FAKE – FDA warns

18 Nov 2019, 06:09 by MyNewsGH

The Food and Drugs Authority (FDA) have warned the public against patronising a male boxer shorts called “male quantum magnetic boxer shorts” being advertised on social media with the potency of treating various illness

The boxer shorts is purported to be able to treat a wide range of diseases including kidney diseases, prostate conditions, erectile dysfunction among several others.

But the FDA has, in a public alert dated 15th November, 2019 and signed by Delese A. A. Darko, the Chief Executive Officer, cautioned people to not buy the product since it has not approved it for sale.

It further warned that the diseases the product is purported to be able to treat require proper medical diagnoses from trained professionals, adding that persons must seek medical care and prescription.

The authority has also appealed to the public to provide information on the product if it is found anywhere in Ghana
