The Book of Mormon is coming to the Liverpool Empire Theatre

The musical announced it was coming to the city in 2020 but now a venue has been confirmed

18 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Catherine Murphy

The Book of Mormon musical is coming to Liverpool's Empire theatre next year.

Fans will be able to watch the Broadway smash hit, which is written by Trey Parker, Matt Stone and Robert Lopez, when it comes to the city on Tuesday, July 14 2020.

The musical confirmed it was heading to Liverpool last week with billboards and signs placed around the city including Lime Street Station but until now its date and venue were kept under wraps.

The Book Of Mormon is hugely popular receiving huge praise wherever it goes. The world tour includes Australia, New Zealand and America.

Its story follows a pair of Mormon boys sent on a mission to a place that’s a long way from their home in Salt Lake City.

Since making its world premiere in March 2011 at New York’s Eugene O’Neill Theatre, where it won nine Tony Awards, including Best Musical, The Book of Mormon has been performed on three continents and won over thirty international awards.

The musical has smashed long-standing box office records in New York, London, Melbourne, Sydney and cities across the US.

ATG theatre Card pre-sale opens at 10am today and general sale will take place from 10am on Wednesday, November 20.
