Here's where Nottinghamshire's mobile speed cameras will be from Monday, November 17

The list shows where mobile speed cameras and their crews will be working

18 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Jamie Barlow

Mobile speed cameras and their crews will be out again this week on Nottinghamshire's roads.

Nottinghamshire Police have published a list which shows where they will be working to tackle speeding motorists and reduce collisions.

The locations from Monday, November 18 are:

  • A60 Nottingham Road / Mansfield Road, Ravenshead
  • B6018 Sutton Road, Kirkby-in-Ashfield
  • B6023 Mansfield Road, Sutton-in-Ashfield
  • A60 Leeming Lane North, Mansfield Woodhouse
  • Eakring Road, Mansfield
  • B6040 Retford Road, Worksop (40 limit section)
  • A631 Gringley to Beckingham (near Mutton Lane)
  • B6041 Kilton Hill, Worksop
  • A612 Main Road, Upton
  • Spital Hill / Leverton Road, Retford
  • A609 Ilkeston Road / Wollaton Road / Russell Drive/ Trowell Road, Nottingham
  • A6200 / A52 Derby Road, Nottingham
  • B682 Sherwood Rise through to Hucknall Lane, Nottingham
  • Bestwood Park Drive, Nottingham
  • A6002, Bilborough Road, Nottingham
  • Glaisedale Drive, Bilborough
  • B600 Nottingham Road / Kimberley Road, Nuthall
  • Shelford Road, Radcliffe-on-Trent
  • A612 Burton Joyce, Notts
  • Clifton Road, Ruddington

Mobile speed cameras may also be in operation at additional sites on an ad hoc basis during the week, in response to recent complaints about speeding or sites that have been identified as emerging locations for road incidents.
