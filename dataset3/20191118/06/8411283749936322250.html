URE, Motech, TSEC report 3Q19 operating losses

18 Nov 2019, 06:27 by Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES, Taipei

Solar cell and PV module makers United Renewable Energy (URE), Motech Industries and TSEC suffered net operating losses of NT$943.6 million (US$30.9 million), NT$205.6 million and NT$37.2 million respectively for third-quarter 2019.

URE has reported consolidated revenues of NT$1.132 billion for October, decreasing 17.08% sequentially and 41.50% on year, and those of NT$15.391 billion for January-October increased 53.41% on year.

Motech had consolidated revenues of NT$403.1 million for October, slipping 16.17% sequentially and 23.01% on year and those of NT$4.642 billion for January-October dipped 62.46% on year.

TSEC reported October consolidated revenues of NT$323 million, dropping 8.72% sequentially but growing 1.39% on year, and January-October's NT$3.774 billion rose 15.04% on year.
