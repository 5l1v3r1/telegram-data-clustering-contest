Manish Malhotra Bereaved; Bollywood's Favourite Fashion Designer's Father Passes Away

18 Nov 2019, 06:55 by Lubna Khan

Famous designer Manish Malhotra’s father passed away today. He had been unwell for a while and was in his early 90’s. Karan Johar, Shabana Azmi, Sophie Choudry were spotted outside Manish Malhotra’s residence in Bandra to pay condolences

Bollywood's favourite fashion designer Manish Malhotra’s father passed away today. He had been unwell for quite some time now and was in his early 90s. Karan Malhotra, a very close friend of Manish Malhotra’s was among the first ones to reach Manish Malhotra’s residence. Other friends from the industry, Shabana Azmi and Sophie Choudry also arrived to pay condolences and were snapped by the paparazzi outside Manish Malhotra’s home.
