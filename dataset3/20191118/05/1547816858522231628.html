Dabangg 3: Salman Khan Steadies Saiee Manjrekar's Nerves As She Looks Very Uncomfortable Amid Fan Frenzy - Watch Video

18 Nov 2019, 05:34 by Oshine Koul

A latest video, that has been doing the rounds of social media sees Salman Khan comforting Saiee Manjrekar as she gets uncomfortable seeing massive crowds throng them ahead of the release of Dabangg 3. Saiee is all set to make her Bollywood debut with Salman and Sonakshi Sinha in the third installment of the light-hearted action drama

Salman Khan is back to treat you this Christmas with the 3rd instalment of his hit Franchise, Dabangg. Playing the role of a police officer Chulbul Pandey, the actor will once again romance Sonakshi Sinha aka Rajjo in the film. But looks like, Dabangg 3 is going to be special for Salman Khan as it all set to launch a new face. Actor and filmmaker Mahesh Manjrekar’s daughter Saiee Manjrekar is Salman’s new discovery who’s  all set to make her Bollywood debut with Dabangg 3.

It was on IIFA 2019’s red carpet when Saiee made her first official appearance with Salman Khan and since then the actress has is often being spotted with the actor, like recently. Salman Khan recently made his presence felt at a fitness event. The actor was accompanied by his Dabangg 3 leading lady Saiee Manjrekar. During the event, Salman Khan was seen addressing his fans with Saiee alongside. But seeing the huge fan following of Dabangg actor, Saiee can be seen getting uncomfortable in the video.
