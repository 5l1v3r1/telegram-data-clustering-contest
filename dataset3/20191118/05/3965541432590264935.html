Man struck by alleged distracted driver while crossing Bellevue street

18 Nov 2019, 05:42 by KOMO News Staff

BELLEVUE, Wash. -- A man crossing a Bellevue street was critically injured when he was stuck by an alleged distracted driver Sunday evening.

It was just after 7 p.m. when the victim was crossing 145th Place SE in a crosswalk at SE 18th Street when the crash occurred.

Medics took the man to Harborview Medical Center in Seattle where he was being treated for life-threatening injuries, Bellevue Police said.

The driver stayed on scene and was cooperating with police, officials said. Investigators believe he was being distracted when the crash occurred.
