Laal Singh Chaddha first poster: Aamir Khan is all set to win hearts again

Aamir Khan on Monday shared the first poster of his upcoming film Laal Singh Chaddha. The Advait Chauhan directorial will release on Christmas 2020.

18 Nov 2019, 05:24 by Entertainment Desk

Bollywood superstar Aamir Khan on Monday shared the first poster of his upcoming film Laal Singh Chaddha on Twitter. In the poster, we see Aamir as a Sardar. This is the first time the actor is sporting a turban in a film.

The beard, handlebar moustache and graceful smile also draw your attention to the poster.

Well, it seems like Aamir Khan is set to wow fans with his transformation yet again.

Laal Singh Chaddha is the Hindi remake of award-winning Tom Hanks film Forrest Gump. The announcement of the Hindi remake was made by Aamir on his 54th birthday on March 14 this year.

The film also stars Kareena Kapoor Khan. The project marks the third collaboration of the actors after 3 Idiots and Talaash.
