Taco Bell recommends blending its tacos into soup to serve on Thanksgiving

18 Nov 2019, 05:41 by Ida Domingo

LYNCHBURG, Va. (WSET) -- If you're hosting a Thanksgiving dinner, Taco Bell wants to help.

Taco Bell has released its own recipe on how to turn a simple fast food dish into a conversation-worthy Thanksgiving meal on its blog.

The chain wants you to serve blended Taco Bell Rolled Chicken Tacos at your dinner.

The blog says the Rolled Chicken Tacos Bisque will be the star of your Friendsgiving.

If you're interested in making this dish, you'll need to buy the company's Rolled Chicken Taco party pack featuring six Rolled Chicken Tacos and six Crunchy Tacos.

After a few other ingredients, they recommend using an iron cast stockpot to put it all together.
