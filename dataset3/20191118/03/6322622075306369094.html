GDB rises 6.89% on bagging RM67.9m Autohaus job

18 Nov 2019, 03:32 by Surin Murugiah

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 18): Shares in GDB Holdings Bhd rose 6.89% at mid-morning today after it secured a contract worth RM67.9 million to undertake construction works as the main contractor for the Hap Seng Star Mercedes-Benz 3S (sales, service and spare parts) Autohaus at Setia Alam, Selangor.

At 10.53am, GDB rose 3 sen to 46.5 sen, valuing it at RM290.63 million.
