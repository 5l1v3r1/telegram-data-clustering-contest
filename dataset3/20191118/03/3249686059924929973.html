Sungmim basks in the warm sunlight for the first 'Orgel' MV teaser

18 Nov 2019, 03:19 by jennywill

Sungmin has released the MV teaser to his first solo mini-album's title song "Orgel".

In the mini-album 'Orgel', fans can look forward to 5 all-new tracks including title song "Orgel" as well as "Stay", "Zzz...", "Rest", and "I Pray". According to SM Entertainment, Sungmin also took part in composing and producing tracks "Zzz..." and "I Pray".

Look out for Sungmin's 1st mini album set to drop this November 22 at 6 PM KST, and in the meantime, check out the teaser below.
