Alan Sugar criticises The Apprentice contestant Lewis Ellis over 'horrific' tattoos

But Lewis has hit back and now viewers are convinced he is not the winner of this year's show

18 Nov 2019, 09:09 by Jessica Sansome, Jasper King

Lord Sugar has entered into a social media Twitter spat with one of The Apprentice's candidates over the choice of his tattoo's.

It all started last Wednesday (November 13) during the show when he called Lewis Ellis' chest tattoos 'horrific' on Twitter as he watched the BBC show live, the Manchester Evening News reports.

Last weeks' episode featured The Apprentice contestants trying to sell a summer holiday to Finland by creating an advertising campaign.

Lewis, who is a digital marketer from Manchester, featured in his group's ad campaign, which saw him strip his shirt off, showcasing his tattooed arms and chest.

However, Lord Sugar expressed his extreme distaste to his potential business partners body art tweeting: "Lewis your tattoos are horrific. Tell me where I am missing the point. What does he look like?"
