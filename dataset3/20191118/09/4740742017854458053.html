Salmon on song as Bristol City claim point in six-goal WSL thriller against Reading FC

The Robins came back from 3-1 down

18 Nov 2019, 09:20 by Jake Russell

Ebony Salmon fired in a dramatic late leveller as the points where shared in a six-goal thriller at the Madjeski Stadium.

Reading made the early inroads in this crunch WSL clash, Remi Allen and Brooke Chaplen scoring inside the opening 20 minutes.

But they were pegged back before the break by Charlie Wellings’ strike before restoring a two-goal lead when Angharad James netted at the start of the second period.

But Yana Daniels summoned an immediate response before Salmon’s late leveller.

Key moments

Chaplen was at her creative best in the early exchanges for the home side, and Fara Williams twice came close to opening the scoring.

But it was Allen who got on the scoresheet first, getting on the end of James’ flick-on and slotting home.

One became two soon after, Chaplen capitalising on a poor clearance to round the keeper and double the lead.

Bristol needed a response before the break and Wellings provided it with a fine turn and finish into the bottom corner.
