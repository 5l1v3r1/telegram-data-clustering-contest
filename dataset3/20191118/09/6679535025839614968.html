Malayalam actor Sreenivasan hospitalised

The veteran actor’s most recent film was ‘Love Action Drama’ that released earlier this year

18 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Special Correspondent

Senior Malayalam actor Sreenivasan was admitted at a private hospital here on Sunday evening after he experienced breathing difficulty at the Cochin International Airport at Nedumbassery here.

Hospital sources said that his condition was stable and he remains under observation at the intensive care unit. The actor, who was on his way to Chennai, had completed check-in procedures when he complained of breathing difficulty and was taken to a hospital near the airport.

He was later shifted to Aster Medcity hospital here for detailed check-up and investigations.
