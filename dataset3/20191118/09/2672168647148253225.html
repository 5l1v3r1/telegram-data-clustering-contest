Regular flights resume in Lashio

18 Nov 2019, 09:45 by Tun Nay Hlaing (Naungpain)

 

Regular flights are resumed in Lashio on November 17 after some wards in Lashio was under attacked by rocket-propelled grenades on November 16, sources said.

The attacks were targeted on residential areas and Lashio airport.

“The flights are canceled as the blast made a big hole on runway. Now it is already repaired and flights are resumed,” said Arkar Lin, administrator of Lashio Township.

A local was injured in the blast and sent to Lashio general hospital.
