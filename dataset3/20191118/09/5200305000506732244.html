Hum TV confirms telecast of Hum Awards 2019

18 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Raj Baddhan

Hum TV has confirmed the telecast of the 7th annual Hum Awards.

The event, which was held in October, took place in Houston, US. ‘Dar Si Jati Hai Sila’ and ‘Suno Chanda’ were named the biggest winners of the night.

As a part of its special programming, Hum TV has lined up a series of special shows as a build-up to the main event.

Starting on Saturday 23rd November, there will a ‘Making of Hum Awards’ special at 19:30. This will be followed by the ‘Grand Rehearsal’ on Sunday 24th November at 19:30.

The Red Carpet Event will be telecast on Saturday 30th November at 19:30, followed by the Hum Awards – Main Event on Saturday 30th November at 20:00.
