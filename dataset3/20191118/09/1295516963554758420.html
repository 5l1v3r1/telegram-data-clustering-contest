EXCLUSIVE: Batman/Superman Preview Proves How Powerful the Infected Are

18 Nov 2019, 09:11 by L.D. Nolan

Picking up from the events of The Batman Who Laughs, writer Joshua Williamson and artist David Marquez's Batman/Superman has dealt with the title two heroes trying to figure out who the villainous Bruce Wayne from the Dark Multiverse infected and turned into his allies. Those infected heroes, called the Secret Six, were eventually revealed as Donna Troy, Jim Gordon, Shazam, Hawkman, Supergirl and Blue Beetle (Jaime Reyes).

DC Comics has provided CBR with an exclusive preview of Batman/Superman #4, which sees Donna Troy, Jim Gordon, Blue Beetle and Hawkman teaming up to do major damage to the title superheroes. Although he's imprisoned, The Batman Who Laughs takes advantage of Superman's exceptional hearing to taunt the Man of Steel from afar.

You can see CBR's exclusive preview of Batman/Superman #4 below along with the solicitation for the issue.

Batman/Superman #4

  • Written by Joshua Williamson
  • Art by David Marquez
  • Colored by Alejandro Sanchez
  • Cover by Rex Lokus, Emanuela Lupacchino
  • Even from prison, the Batman Who Laughs is staying two steps ahead of the Caped Crusader and the Man of Steel. His plan to infect heroes and turn them into the Dark Multiverse versions of themselves is starting to build steam, with Shazam and others already succumbing to his evil toxin. Batman and Superman are racing the clock to prevent the other three members of the Secret Six from being poisoned, but they realize they are too late when those three come looking for them instead-one of whom is a lot closer to Superman than expected and itching for a fight!

Batman/Superman #4 releases November 20 from DC Comics.
