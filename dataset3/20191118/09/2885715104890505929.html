Indian Navy MR recruitment notification: 12th pass can apply for 400 sailors post

Indian Navy MR notification 2019: Selected candidates will get monthly salary up to Rs Rs 69,100 with allowances extra. Candidates can apply at the official website, joinindiannavy.gov.in till November 28.

18 Nov 2019, 09:53 by Careers Desk

Indian Navy MR recruitment notification 2019: The Indian Navy has released an official notification inviting applications for unmarried male candidates as sailors for its Matric Recruitment (MR). Interested candidates can apply from November 23 on the official website-joinindiannavy.gov.in. The total number of vacant posts is 400 and the candidates can apply online till November 28, 2019.

The selection of the candidates will be done on the basis of the computer-based exam, followed by a physical fitness test and medical examinations. The computer-based exam will consist of 50 objective type questions and will comprise of two sections – science and mathematics and general knowledge. The duration of the exam will be 30 minutes.
