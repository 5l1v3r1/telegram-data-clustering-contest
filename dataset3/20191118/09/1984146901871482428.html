Urgent search for 17-year-old last seen outside Liam Gallagher gig at Newcastle arena

Family members have said that Joe Hogg was last seen without a top on and does not have his phone or any money

18 Nov 2019, 09:24 by Katie Collings

Police are searching for a 17-year-old boy who has gone missing after Liam Gallagher's Newcastle concert.

Joe Hogg was reported missing late on Sunday night by frantic family members after last being seen at the Utilita Arena following the gig.

Officers are trying to locate Joe, believed to be from the Sunderland area, and are asking for anyone with information to get in touch.

Worried family members have said that Joe was last seen in the car park without a top on and does not have his phone or any money with him.

A spokesperson for Northumbria Police said: "We can confirm we are searching for missing 17-year-old Joseph Hogg who was reported missing from the Utilita Arena shortly after 11.30pm on November 17.

"Enquiries to locate him are ongoing and anyone with information about his whereabouts is asked to contact police on 101 quoting log 1128 171119."

The former Oasis frontman has also shared the appeal for information on the teen's whereabouts.
