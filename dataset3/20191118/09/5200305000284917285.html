‘Prassthanam’ premieres on Amazon Prime Video

18 Nov 2019, 09:31 by Raj Baddhan

The Sanjay Dutt political drama ‘Prassthanam’ has premiered on Amazon Prime Video.

The film revolves around a politician Baldev Pratap Singh (Sanjay Dutt) and his family. When it comes to choosing his heir, Baldev chooses his stepson because he’s a thinking man, eventually angering his own son and in the process triggering violence.

It also stars Jackie Shroff and Manisha Koirala.
