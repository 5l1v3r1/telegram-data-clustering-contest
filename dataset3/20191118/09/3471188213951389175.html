Google Stadia game pricing revealed along with the 22 games available at launch

18 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Scott Plowman

Last week Google announced the game titles that would be available and it was a bit disappointing to only see 12 titles on the list. That list has now expanded to 22 games just one day before the launch of the new gaming service.

The new games that have been announced are:

  • The Attacking Giant 2: The Final Battle
  • Mock Farm 2019
  • Final Fantasy XV
  • Football Manager 2020
  • Super Touring Car 2019
  • Metro: Escape
  • NBA2K20
  • Furious 2
  • German Headquarters: New Blood
  • Stunt Motorcycle: Awakening
