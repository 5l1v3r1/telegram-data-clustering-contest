Chemical Brothers to play Irish Independent Park in Cork in June 2020

Tickets go on sale this Friday

18 Nov 2019, 09:09 by Brynmor Pattison

The Chemical Brothers will return to Ireland next year for a huge gig.

The electronic band announced their Irish return with an open-air show set for Irish Independent Park in Cork on Tuesday, June 30 2020.

The concert will feature special guests KETTAMA and James Holroyd.

Tickets are €49.90 and go on sale at 9am this Friday, November 22 via Ticketmaster outlets and www.ticketmaster.ie .

The band’s most recent album Born In the Echoes became their sixth consecutive number one album in the UK.

The Chemical Brothers have since released a new single Free Yourself from their forthcoming ninth album.
