Celtic star Scott Sinclair trolled by John Terry as he's sent leg day advice

The Hoops winger turned heads on Instagram with a post showing off his incredible physique.

18 Nov 2019, 09:03 by Record Sport Online

John Terry has told Scott Sinclair "don't forget the legs" after the Celtic star turned heads with a viral Instagram post.

The winger racked up over 20,000 likes on Sunday night as he showed off his incredible physique on social media.

Sinclair has become the forgotten man under Neil Lennon but sent a timely reminder that he's fot and available for selection.

Alongside the picture, Sinclair commented: "No matter what the situation, I'm focused and ready!"

But his incredible conditioning didn't stop former Chelsea captain Terry chiming in with a playful dig.

He replied: "Don't forget the legs geez"

Sinclair has been restricted to just six appearances this season, despite extending his contract by a further year during the summer.

The arrival of Mohamed Elyounoussi on-loan from Southampton and the rise of Mikey Johnston have ensured the Englishman's opportunities have been severely limited.

Sinclair won Player of the Year following his first campaign at the club and has won every domestic trophy he's competed for since arriving in 2016.

He has scored 62 goals and racked up 34 assists in 166 games for the club.
