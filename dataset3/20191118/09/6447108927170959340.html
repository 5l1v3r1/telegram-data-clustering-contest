UK Charts 16/11/19: Pokémon Sword leads the charge, Jedi Fallen Order in second

18 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Aran Suddi

There’s been a lot of changes in the chart this week as Nintendo dominates the top ten through

Pokémon, Mario, and Luigi all taking prominent spaces. Pokemon Sword was the top-selling game in the physical chart while Respawn’s Star Wars: Jedi Fallen Order came in second place. Pokemon Shield was in third while Pokemon Sword & Shield: Dual Edition in seventh. The other new entry is Terminator Resistance which debuted in 38th.

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare fell from first to fourth as a result of the Pokemon entry. The full Top 40 can be found below.
