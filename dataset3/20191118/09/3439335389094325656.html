Man, 28, allegedly uses box cutter in Kapolei threat | Honolulu Star-Advertiser

18 Nov 2019, 09:28

A 28-year-old man allegedly threatened a 35-year-old man with a box cutter knife in Kapolei Saturday night.

The threat allegedly occurred at 8:11 p.m. and the suspect was arrested at 8:30 p.m.

He was in custody Saturday night on suspicion of first-degree terroristic threatening.
