Travel Counsellors celebrate 80th franchisee

18 Nov 2019, 09:20 by Vicky Karantzavelou

Travel Counsellors is an independent travel company whose global network of travel professionals deliver bespoke travel experiences to both the leisure and corporate markets.

Meath travel expert Katrina McMullan has officially launched her own business as part of Travel Counsellors. She is now the 80th Travel Counsellor operating across the country, and one of five in Meath.

Navan-based Katrina comes to Travel Counsellors with over 20 years in the travel industry and is a previous winner of the Best ITAA Leisure Travel Professional in 2012 and the Best ITAA Overall Travel Professional in 2013.

Speaking on her new business, Katrina said: “I am delighted to be part of Travel Counsellors and love their approach to building personal relationships. I always strive to provide customers with an exceptional service that exceeds expectations, an ethos that is completely in-line with Travel Counsellors. My passion for travel began when I worked for a camping tour operator in France, and I became a travel consultant in 1998. I’m now so excited about my new career path, owning my own business and creating perfect bespoke holidays for my customers.”

Welcoming Katrina to Travel Counsellors, General Manager, Cathy Burke said: “We are delighted to see Katrina become the country’s 80th Travel Counsellor, as she brings over 20 years of passion and expertise to her new business."

“It is completely evident that travel is an industry that Katrina adores, and with Travel Counsellors she will build her flourishing leisure and corporate travel business, with her clients at the heart of everything she does.” 

Katrina is now the fifth Travel Counsellor spread across Meath, joining fellow Travel Counsellors Mandy Walsh, Karen Morgan-Murphy, Sharon Tiernan-Murphy and Sinead Daly.

Travel Counsellors is an independent travel company whose global network of travel professionals deliver bespoke travel experiences to both the leisure and corporate markets. Katrina joins a large team of Travel Counsellors in the Republic of Ireland supported by the national head office in Cork. The company is licensed and bonded by the Commission for Aviation Regulation in Ireland.
