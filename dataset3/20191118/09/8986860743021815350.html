Ghanaians don’t see anything good in everything that I do – Efia Odo cries

18 Nov 2019, 09:42 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Slay queen cum actress and television presenter Efia Odo has bemoaned why some Ghanaians find it difficult to see the good in anything that she does.

Her comments come after some section of Ghanaians took to social media to blast her for her choice of dress to the just-ended 4syte TV’s Music video awards.

She posted:

”I don’t disrespect anyone , I stay in my own lane doing things that makes ME happy. Sad how my own people don’t see the good in me. Hurts because I love y’all sooo much and you guys just throw stones at me each time. It’s all good tho, in the end I still have nothing but love”.

She continued;

”Only religion I practice is love. Being religious is too hard. I don’t wanna be a hypocrite neither do I want to discriminate another due to our differences In “sin”. My God teaches me to love and accept each one as they are, whether I agree with their life or not. “
