Frozen 2: Sunny Leone’s Aww-Dorable Daughter Nisha Weber Steals The Show At The Film's India Premiere

18 Nov 2019, 09:14 by Gretel Sequeira

As the Disney film Frozen 2 was premiered in India yesterday, Sunny Leone and Daniel Weber’s eldest daughter Nisha Weber attended it looking adorbs as she enjoyed her time with characters Anna and Elsa

Sunny Leone and husband Daniel Weber have a beautiful family and often try to make the best for their children. From hosting grandeur birthday celebrations for the kids to picking them up from playschool, Sunny and Daniel do it all. As the second instalment of the most-anticipated Disney animated film Frozen has premiered in the country yesterday, Sunny Leone and husband Daniel Weber accompanied the eldest daughter Nisha Weber at the event. The adorable munchkin enjoying the premier with the Disney characters is unmissable.

Daniel Webber and Sunny Leone are blessed with kids Nisha Weber, Noah Weber, and Asher Weber. As the Disney film Frozen 2 was premiered in India yesterday, Sunny and hubby Daniel were snapped at the event with their eldest daughter Nisha. In a video shared on Instagram, the little munchkin looked endearing as she enjoyed some fun-time with Disney Frozen 2 characters Anna and Elsa. The video features Nisha enjoying playing with Anna and Elsa at the premier, later clicking pictures and having some adorable moments with the Disney sister characters. The premier also saw Sunny Leone and husband Daniel Weber posing for pictures along with Nisha and the Disney sister characters. Dressed in a beautiful peach coloured tulle dress, Nisha Weber hugs the Disney princesses.

Directed by Chris Buck and Jennifer Lee, the Disney film Frozen 2 is based on the life of princesses Anna and Elsa. Frozen 2 is the second instalment of the 2013 film ‘Frozen’.
