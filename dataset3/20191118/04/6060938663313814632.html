Abhishek Bachchan has the perfect Monday motivation for those battling with online bullying

18 Nov 2019, 04:16 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Abhishek Bachchan, who was last seen in Anurag Kashyap's 'Manmarziyaan' often shares motivational posts on Monday mornings. Recently, he took to his Twitter profile to share another inspiring post and it is something you shouldn't miss!

He shared a quote, ''When you build in silence, people don't know what to attack''. He captioned the post with hashtags Monday Motivation and Believe. A few days back, the 'Guru' actor was trolled for his Monday motivation post. A social media user wrote, ''What do you call a person who is happy on a Monday? Unemployed!' Abhishek who is known for his savage and spontaneous replies said,'Nah! Disagree. Somebody who loves doing whatever they are doing.''
