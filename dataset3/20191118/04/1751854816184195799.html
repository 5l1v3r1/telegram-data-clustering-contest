Oppo has already revealed that the company will be hosting a press event on 20th November at the Minsheng Modern Art Museum in Beijing where it will launch the next-generation ColorOS 7 custom operating system.

Ahead of the official announcement, the company has released a couple of teaser videos which reveals some of the features of the upcoming ColorOS 7 Android skin.

The company will be introducing a Focus Mode which will help the user better their sleep pattern and make sure they are not disturbed during the sleep time. Previously, the company had hinted at video editing feature named “Soloop Recording” with an AI-based one-button video editing.

Further, the ColorOS 7 operating system will be better optimized for gaming as the company is all set to introduce new game acceleration features. It is said to offer great gaming experience without degrading the picture quality, frame rate and resolution.

The upcoming custom user interface from Oppo will also be designed in such a way that the UI will be more optimized and will offer comfort and efficiency to the users. To more about the features coming with the ColorOS 7, we’ll have to wait for the official launch event in China on 20th November.

We also expect the company reveal the roadmap for this new update as well as the list of compatible smartphones at the launch event. There will also be an exclusive customized edition of the ColorOS 7 for Realme smartphones, as confirmed by the Realme CEO Madhav Sheth. However, nothing much is known about it at this time, but it is expected to be closer to stock Android UI, similar to OxygenOS from OnePlus.
