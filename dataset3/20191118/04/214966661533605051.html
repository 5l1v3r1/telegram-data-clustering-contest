Truck and car crash halts M1 traffic, Gold Coast

18 Nov 2019, 04:22 by Monique StClair

There are already massive delays on the M1 southbound this afternoon, after a truck and car crash.

It happened in the left lane between exit 89 and exit 92 (through Burleigh Heads/Palm Beach) just before 2.00pm.

Witnesses report that a car cut in front of a truck too soon, and was quickly rear-ended by the large vehicle.

The Department of Transport and Main Roads has warned of delays throughout the afternoon.

At the time of writing, the traffic was banked back up to Robina.

Northbound traffic through that area is also experiencing delays.

It’s understood paramedics haven’t been called to the scene.
