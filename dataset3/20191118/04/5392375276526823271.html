The stable version of Xiaomi MIUI 11 started rolling out in mid-October. While Xiaomi is preparing this update for more devices, it is also adding some new MIUI 11 features. As of now, 27 Xiaomi phones are receiving either the stable or beta version of MIUI 11 on top of Android 10. However, recent information shows that not all MIUI 11 versions are based on Android 10. Older Xiaomi devices will most likely receive this update but just not based on Android 10 for now. A few days ago, MIUI 11 arrived two years old Redmi 4. However, this update is on top of Android 7.1.2 which the company claim is classic and rare. Now, we have a list of older devices that will get the Xiaomi MIUI 11 update in the third batch

The reported devices for the third batch of MIUI stable update include

  • Xiaomi Mi MIX
  • Xiaomi Mi 5s
  • Redmi 5
  • Xiaomi Mi 5s Plus
  • Xiaomi Mi 5X
  • Redmi 5 Plus
  • Xiaomi Mi 5C
  • Xiaomi Mi Note 2
  • Redmi 5A
  • Xiaomi Mi Play
  • Xiaomi Mi Max 2
  • Redmi 4X
  • Redmi Note 5A etc

The devices on this list were released between 2016 – 2018. Like we said earlier, there is no guarantee that the MIUI 11 update will be on top of Android 10. However, it may come with many MIUI 11 classic features such as notifications’ sound effect changes with time, dark mode and so on.
