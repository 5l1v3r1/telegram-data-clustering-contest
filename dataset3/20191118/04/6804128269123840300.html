Arsenal news: Raul Sanllehi ready to sanction January deal, player's father 'contacted'

All the latest Arsenal news

18 Nov 2019, 04:00 by Andy Ha

Arsenal return to Premier League action against Southampton after the international break.

The 2-0 defeat to Leicester last week left the Gunners eight points adrift of the top four, but Unai Emery's side have the chance to bounce back against a struggling Saints team that have shipped 14 goals in their last three league games.

Emery's future has been under scrutiny but the Arsenal head coach was recently given a vote of confidence by the Gunners hierarchy.

Can he improve results to save his job? Or will the north Londoners be looking for a new coach by Christmas?

Here's the latest stories doing the rounds from the last 24 hours.

Arsenal Women take spoils in historic north London derby

Arsenal beat Tottenham 2-0 at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium to win the first ever derby at Spurs' new ground.

Second half goals from Kim Little and Vivianne Miedema were enough for the three points in front of a league record crowd.

What Reiner Jesus said about playing in England

Arsenal are reported to have Reiner Jesus' father over a potential move to north London.

17-year-old has impressed since making his debut for Flamengo in July and is already to be thought to be on the radar of some of Europe's biggest clubs.

Agent provides update on Lucas Torreira's Arsenal future

The agent of Arsenal midfielder Lucas Torreira has revealed that his client is not happy with his role at the Emirates Stadium this season.

The midfielder has mostly been used as a substitute during the 2019/20 campaign, although he has been a regular pick in the Europa League, starting three of the four games so far.

Torreira has however been named in the starting 11 for Arsenal's last two Premier League games, starting in the middle of the park for the matches against Wolves and Leicester, where the Gunners picked up one point from the 180 minutes.

Before securing a move to the Emirates in summer 2018, Torreira was in negotiations with Italian side Napoli, and his agent Raffaele Bentancur has revealed just how close the Uruguayan came to moving to Napoli.

Arsenal willing to sanction Xhaka sale in order to seal January transfer

According to The Mirror, Arsenal could offer Granit Xhaka to former club Borussia Monchengladbach in January, with Denis Zakaria potentially moving in the other direction.

Zakaria often partners Xhaka for Switzerland, but the pair could be set to swap clubs in January.
