I'm A Celeb camp-mates' furious reactions behind the scenes to first challenge

Ant and Dec were chatting about the stars' reaction to what was coming next - and it's fair to say it didn't go down at all well with the celebs after the news dropped

18 Nov 2019, 04:03 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin has revealed I'm A Celebrity contestants turned the air blue after discovering a swimming challenge was up first.

He revealed all on Extra Camp - and said the news couldn't have gone down any worse.

The presenter didn't name names but said there was plenty of off camera swearing as the stars grumbled about being chucked in at the deep end.

Appearing on the the ITV2 spin-off show alongside Ant, Declan Donnelly says he is glad he ducked telling the camp-mates about the watery blow coming their way.

Ant said: "Nobody liked the news. There were quite a few swear words!

I just gave the news and walked off - there was quite a lot of bitching, believe me!

"But to be fair, they eventually just got in and got on with it."

Dec joked: "I'm glad Ant revealed that... I would have given in and said 'don't worry, you don't have to do it'."

Meanwhile John Barrowman worried I'm A Celebrity fans after his appearance on Extra Camp.

The 52-year-old looked almost unrecognisable as he sported shaved gray hair on the show.
