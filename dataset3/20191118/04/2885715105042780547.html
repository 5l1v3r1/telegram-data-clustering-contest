Indian Coast Guard Navik admit card 2019: The Indian Coast Guard has released the admit card for the recruitment exam to be conducted for the post of Navik. Candidates can download their admit card from the official website, joinindiancoastguard.gov.in. The admit card will only be available till November 22.

The written examination will be of objective type, which will generally cover subjects – quantitative aptitude, mathematics, general sciences, general English, general awareness (current affairs and general knowledge) and reasoning (verbal and non-verbal). Those who qualify written examination will undergo Physical Fitness Test (PFT) and Initial Medical Examination (Preliminary).

Indian Coast Guard Navik admit card 2019: Documents required

— Three copies of admit card
— Original class 10 certificate and mark sheet
— Caste certificate, if required
— Reservation certificates, wherever required
— Government-approved identity card
— 10 recent passport-size photograph

Original educational certificates will be returned after verification. If any variations found in the application at the time of verification, the candidature of the applicant will be cancelled, as per the official notification.

Selected candidates will have to undergo training beginning from April 2020 at the INS Chilka followed by sea training and professional training. After which they will be hired in their respective batches.

Indian Coast Guard Navik 2019: Salary

Candidates will be hired at basic pay of Rs 21,700 with allowances, LTC, ration etc additional.
