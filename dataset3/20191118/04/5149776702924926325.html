THE SECRET TO SABRI'S RELATIONSHIP

Last month Sabri Lamouchi called for Lewis Grabban to be more like Luis Suarez.

Not necessarily the kind of aspirations you have for your player in all areas given Suarez's checkered record, but when it comes to scoring goals there are few better reference points.

Grabban is Nottingham Forest's best striker by some margin unless Rafa Mir has more to his game than he has had the chance to show yet, but Lamouchi wants more from his star man - he wants him to be 'a killer' in front of goal, just like Barcelona's controversial scoring master Suarez.
