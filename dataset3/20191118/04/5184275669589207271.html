Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior: Kajol’s Fierce Eyes Intrigues Us Further To Witness The Big War

The highlight point of this poster has to be Kajol’s intense expression and those fierce eyes that speak volume.

18 Nov 2019, 04:43 by Koimoi.com Team

Ajay Devgn and Saif Ali Khan starrer Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior is one of the most talked-about and awaited films in recent time. After impressing the audience with the posters and the teaser, the makers have revealed Kajol’s first look from the film and it is intense. The actress is playing Ajay’s onscreen wife and below is all you need to know about it.

The trailer of the Ajay Devgn starrer will be launched tomorrow, and in the meantime, it is Kajol as Savitribai Malusare who is here to impress her with the first character look. The actress can be seen dressed in a Maharashtrian traditional saree. With traditional jewellery, a bindi and kohl eyes, Kajol looks stunning in the poster.

The highlight point of this poster has to be Kajol’s intense expression and those fierce eyes that speak volume. While sharing her first look. Kajol took to Twitter and wrote, “Main aapko harne nahi doongi” #TanhajiTheUnsungWarrior, in cinemas 10th January 2020. TANHAJI TRAILER TOMORROW.”
