Everyone Is Loving Pokémon ‘Sword And Shield’ But They Love Wooloo Even More

18 Nov 2019, 04:22 by Michelle Rennex

The reception of Pokémon’s newest game, ‘Sword and Shield’ has been overwhelming positive from critics.

Pokémon’s general appeal lives in its ability to let you “tap into a sense of wonder that becomes more and more difficult to access as an adult,” according to Kotaku. “‘Sword and Shield’ does that more successfully than any Pokémon release has in years.”

Casey DeFreitas from IGN called the game “such a complete joy to play” but still recognised that ‘Sword and Shield’ could be improved on. While “better cutscenes, companion Pokemon, [and] the complete Pokédex” could’ve catapulted the game into “true legendary status,” ‘Sword and Shield’ is still more than impressive.

Even with all the drama surrounding the National Dex removal ahead of its release, ‘Sword And Shield’ has still been mostly received positively by the public too. Beyond the “superfans” angered over Game Freak’s game design and graphics, others online are enjoying the game.
