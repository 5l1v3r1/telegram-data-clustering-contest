American preachers, RMD for Lagos youth conference

18 Nov 2019, 04:40

American preachers Stephen and Zai Chandler, actor Richard Mofe Damijo (RMD), celebrity pastor Tony Rapu as well as singers Bankole Wellington (Banky W) and Sinach would be speaking to young people on self discovery at a conference in Lagos later this month.

Organised by the Waterbrook, the three-day conference known as Living Your Best Life is designed to help youths find their purpose and identity.

According to the organisers, the speakers including life coach Lanre Olusola, Founder, Real Talk Women Initiative Tolu Ijogun, Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Aspire Coronation Trust Foundation Osayi Alile and CEO Enyo Rerltail Yomi Awobokun would be expected to share insights on how they are living their best lives at 5ye event slated for November 22 to 24.

While there will be panel discussions and interactions, participants will also be thrilled with musical performances.
