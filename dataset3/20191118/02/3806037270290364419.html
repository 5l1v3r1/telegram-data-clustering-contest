Images of an adidas YEEZY BOOST 700 "Carbon Blue" Surface

No word yet regarding a release date.

18 Nov 2019, 02:56 by Sophie Caraan

While the adidas YEEZY 500 High “Slate” preps for its debut this December, Kanye West has returned to the adidas YEEZY BOOST 700 drawing board for a new “Carbon Blue” offering. Utilizing a combination of materials from the silhouette’s most iconic iterations, the rumored model features the gray, light blue and orange sole unit of the “Inertia” colorway, the grey mesh panel and leather overlays of the “Wave Runner,” and the assorted shades of blue and grey of “Teal Blue.” Cool pastel tones can be found on the tongue, laces and various areas of the upper, with the pair finally resting on a subtle gum outsole.

Take a look at the adidas YEEZY BOOST 700 “Carbon Blue” below and stay tuned for a release date and pricing details.
