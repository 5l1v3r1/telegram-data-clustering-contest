Shooting at Elmira business

18 Nov 2019, 02:18 by Chelsi Byrnes

ELMIRA (WBNG) — Sunday night at around 9:30 p.m. the Elmira Police Department responded to reports of gun shots at a local business on the 400 block of Balsam Street.

When they got to the scene, police found that the business was shot up and police believe the people inside were targets. Police say there are no injuries at this time and there are no witnesses.

The Elmira Police Department says if anyone has any information regarding the shooting to contact them at (607)737-5626, or the tip line at (607)271-HALT.
