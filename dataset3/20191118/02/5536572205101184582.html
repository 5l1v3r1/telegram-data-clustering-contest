UGA Photographer Speaks Out After Being KO’d During Auburn-Georgia Game (TWEET)

18 Nov 2019, 02:25 by Darrelle Lincoln

Chamberlain Smith, who was the Georgia photographer injured on the sideline at Auburn, has released a statement following her injury that scared everybody watching when she was ran over by Georgia RB Brian Herrien.

Smith was knocked unconscious as Herrien was pushed out of bounds near the goal line.
