Dream11 Team Bangladesh Emerging Team vs Nepal Emerging Team Prediction Emerging Asia Cup 2019 – Cricket Tips For Today’s Match 12 Group B BD-ET vs NEP-ET: The 2019 ACC Emerging Teams Asia Cup is the fourth edition of a cricket tournament currently being held in Bangladesh between 14 and 23 November 2019. Eight teams are participating in the tournament including five under-23 age level teams of Test nations and top three teams from the 2018 Asia Cup Qualifier. The tournament is organised by the Asian Cricket Council (ACC). Nepal replaced the United Arab Emirates, after they pulled out due to an unknown reason.
