MOT sets up taskforce to review CAAM downgade and 33 issues flagged by FAA

18 Nov 2019, 07:01 by Syahirah Syed Jaafar

PUTRAJAYA (Nov 18): The Ministry of Transport has set up a special taskforce dedicated to addressing the 33 issues highlighted by the the US' Federal Administration (FAA) with an aim to raise back Civil Aviation Authority of Malaysia's (CAAM) status to category one in the next twelve months.
