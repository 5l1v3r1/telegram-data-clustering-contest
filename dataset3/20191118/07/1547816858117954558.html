Just now Akshay Kumar posted a video featuring himself, Kiara Advani and Diljit Dosanjh as they wait for Kareena Kapoor Khan to arrive for Good Newwz trailer launch event. They are practicing their version of ‘Ankhiyon se goli maarein’ meanwhile

Akshay Kumar, Kiara Advani, and Diljit Dosanjh are all set for the trailer launch of Good Newws, but where is Kareena Kapoor Khan? As the excitement level is at its peak, Akshay Kumar posted a cute video featuring Kiara and Diljit as they make their fans wait for the trailer to be dropped. As seen in the clip, Akshay Kumar along with Kiara us seen making a fish face while winking on the sound of gunshots. Further, Diljit is seen saying, “They are ready for the trailer” Well, the stars say, ‘they are practising our version of ‘Ankhiyon se goli maarein’’.
