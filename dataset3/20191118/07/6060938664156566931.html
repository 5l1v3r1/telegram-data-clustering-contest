'Good Newwz': Akshay Kumar-Kareena Kapoor Khan Vs Diljit Dosanjh-Kiara Advani's goofy trailer is a joy ride; fans call it a 'blockbuster'

18 Nov 2019, 07:40 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After a long wait, the trailer of Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani starrer 'Good Newwz' is finally out. With several posters piquing the audience's attention, the wait was worth it.

The three minutes twenty-two-second trailer brings back the popular pair of Akshay and Kareena on the celluloid while being joined by the likes of Diljit and Kiara, who add the much-needed punch to the film.
