Yes, Right There! Doggy Gets Massage from its Feline Friend

In our crazy world, a good massage can be an effective means of shaking off stress - and even cats and dogs understand that.

18 Nov 2019, 07:30

Cats and dogs are known for their incessant feuds with each other but even they can sometimes bury the hatchet and reconcile - and sometimes in a peculiar way, like this white pooch and ginger kitty.

The two were caught on camera as the cat was giving a massage to the dog. The kitty is seen working its paws on the dog's shoulders, while the dog is sitting still like the good boy he is.
