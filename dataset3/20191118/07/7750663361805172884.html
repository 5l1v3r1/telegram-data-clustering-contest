Monday's Arsenal transfer talk news roundup: Reinier, Luis Enrique, Granit Xhaka

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Arsenal eye possible January reinforcements.

18 Nov 2019, 07:13 by Andy Brent

The Gunners spent more than many expected during the summer - most notable their club-record purchase of Nicolas Pepe - and could look to further strengthen their squad when the window reopens.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest news on possible comings and goings at the Emirates Stadium.

Monday morning's headlines:

Arsenal sound out Luis Enrique over replacing Unai Emery?
Arsenal are reportedly sounding the possibility of installing Luis Enrique as manager following the club's recent poor run of form under Unai Emery. Read more.

Manchester United, Arsenal 'eye teenage goalkeeper Brad Young'
Manchester United and Arsenal are reportedly both monitoring Hartlepool United goalkeeper Brad Young. Read more.

Arsenal 'open talks with in-demand Reinier'
Arsenal contact the father of Flamengo attacker Reinier to discuss a move for the teenager, according to a report. Read more.

Arsenal to offer Granit Xhaka in Denis Zakaria deal?
Arsenal are reportedly preparing to offer Granit Xhaka to Borussia Monchengladbach in a bid to bring Denis Zakaria to the Emirates Stadium during the January transfer window. Read more.
