Eastern Wash. man burns face, arms while fueling his lawn mower

18 Nov 2019, 07:57 by Taylor Fritz | KEPR-TV News

BENTON COUNTY, Wash. — A Benton County man is in the hospital after firefighters say he burned himself while fueling his lawnmower.

The man received third-degree burns on his face and arms, according to Benton Country Fire District 2.

Firefighters responded to the call around 1:30 p.m. on Sunday at West Rainy Lane in Benton County after family members called 911.

It wasn't immediately known what caused the fuel to catch fire and flare up.

The third-degree burns are not life-threatening, according to Benton County firefighters.
