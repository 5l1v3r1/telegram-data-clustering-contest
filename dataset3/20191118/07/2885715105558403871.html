18 Nov 2019, 07:31 by Trends Desk

The latest influencer on social media is B, reportedly the world first insect influencer. The bee influencer is taking the internet by storm with unique photographs and is aiming to spread a very important message- help save the world’s bees.

The Instagram page of B is run by a French philanthropic network, Fondation de France, and presently has over two lakh followers.

The foundation works on the issue of protecting bees and several of the foundation’s posts urge people to save the bee population. Many of B’s posts also carry fun facts and information about the insect.

Bees, who are active pollinators, play a pivotal role in our food supply. However, the bee population has been reducing and factors like with the use of pesticides and intensive farming techniques are blamed for it.

The page aims to raise funds for preserving bees.
