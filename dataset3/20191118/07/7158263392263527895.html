The 10 Best Shampoo Bars for Every Single Hair Type

18 Nov 2019, 07:26

The amount of times I’ve insisted on packing my favorite shampoo in my suitcase only to have it pop open and ruin my clothes is, like, actually embarrassing. (Four times. It has happened four times. Why.) So when I was introduced to shampoo bars—aka shampoo packed down in the form of a bar—I decided it was a much more ~civilized~ way to bring my shampoo on the go.

They may sound weird if you’ve never used one before, but shampoo bars are actually quite simple: Just rub the bar over your roots and hair, massaging and lathering as you work. And not only are they great traveling companions, but shampoo bars are also a super eco-friendly approach to beauty—they can last up to 80 washes, which equates to three-ish bottles of shampoo. Convinced? Good. Because ahead are the 10 best shampoo bars money can buy.
