Indian Navy Entrance Test 2019: Notification released, check details here

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: Notification has been released for Indian Navy Entrance Test (INET) for January 2021 course. The registration link to open on November 29 at the official website-joinindiannavy.gov.in.

18 Nov 2019, 07:28 by Education Desk

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: The Indian Navy has released a notification inviting applications for the recruitment of Short Service Commission Officer through Indian Navy Entrance Test (INET) for January 2021 course. The interested candidates should apply online from November 29 at the official website-joinindiannavy.gov.in

The total number of vacant posts is 144 and the last date to submit online applications is December 19, 2019.

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: Vacancy Details

-SSC Naval Armament Inspection Cadre: 6 posts

-SSC ATC: 4 posts

-SSC Observer: 6 posts

-SSC Pilot (MR): 3 posts

-SSC Pilot (Other than MR): 6 posts

-SSC Logistics: 11 posts

-SSC X (IT): 10 posts

-SSC General Service (GS/X)/Hydro Cadre: 30 posts

-SSC Engineering Branch [General Service (GS)]: 26 posts

-SSC Electrical Branch [General Service (GS)]: 27 posts

-SSC Education: 15 posts

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: Application fee

-General/OBC category candidates: Rs 215

-SC/ST/Women candidates are exempted from paying the application fee.

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: Selection Procedure

-The candidates will be shortlisted on the basis of INET. The time duration of INET will be of two hours.

-It will consist of 100 multiple choice questions on English, Reasoning & Numerical Ability, General Science, Mathematical Aptitude and General Knowledge.

-Each section will comprise of 100 marks and there will be negative marking of one mark for every incorrect answer.

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: Important documents

-The candidates should have a relevant e-mail ID and a working mobile number.

-The candidates should have marksheet up to fifth and seventh semester for regular B.E./B.Tech courses and other dregree exams, date of birth proof (according to the 10 and 12 certificates) and recent passport size coloured photograph.

Indian Navy recruitment 2019: How to apply

-All the interested candidates will be able to apply online on the official website-joinindiannavy.gov.in from November 29 to December 19, 2019.
