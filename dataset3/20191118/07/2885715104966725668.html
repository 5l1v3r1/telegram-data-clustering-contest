Good Newwz trailer: The Akshay-Kareena-Diljit-Kiara film is a comedy of errors

Good Newwz trailer: This Akshay Kumar and Kareena Kapoor starrer is about in vitro fertilisation (IVF). More specifically, it deals with the confusion (and comedy) that arises from a mix-up suffered by two couples at an IVF hospital.

18 Nov 2019, 07:43 by Entertainment Desk

The trailer of Good Newwz, starring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani in the lead roles, is out. The posters of Good Newwz promised the biggest goof-up of the year, and it seems the comedy-drama is set to ‘deliver’ precisely that.

The film is about in vitro fertilisation (IVF). More specifically, it deals with the confusion (and comedy) that arises from a mix-up suffered by two couples at an IVF hospital.

While Akshay Kumar is paired with Kareena Kapoor, Diljit Dosanjh will share screen space with Kiara Advani in Good Newwz.
