I'm A Celeb viewers spot Dec Donnelly get very distracted by glam female rangers

Declan Donnelly had viewers in hysterics as his head was turned during the launch of 'm A Celebrity: Extra Camp

18 Nov 2019, 07:09 by Irishmirror.ie

Declan Donnelly had I'm A Celebrity viewers in hysterics on Sunday night as they spotted his hilarious reaction to two female rangers who had popped briefly into the studio.

It came as Dec and his co-presenter Ant McPartlin appeared on spin off show Extra Camp after wrapping up the main launch show.

The team were playing a game to celebrate Ant's birthday and two glamorous female rangers walked on set to deliver props.

The IAC crew are usually men and the Geordie duo noticed the difference straight away.

"They are not the normal rangers!" cried Ant, before waving after them.

Dec, however, jumped out of his seat and asked: "Where did you get those rangers?" before a laughing Ryan Thomas had to pull him back down.

Eagle eyed viewers at home immediately clocked the blunder.

"If my future husband doesn’t react to me the way dec reacted to the new rangers what’s the point #ImACeleb " said one.

"Dec eyeing up the rangers, actually can’t cope," laughed a third.

"I don't know what’s funnier @antanddec ’s (especially Dec’s) reaction to the pretty rangers on extra camp or @adamthomas21 laughing his a*** off about it," added a fourth

The presenters were in great spirits after an action-packed opener to the 2019 series of I'm A Celeb.

The launch saw the celebs plank walking, sky-diving and facing bugs within hours of the show starting.

Tomorrow night looks set to be just as thrilling with Caitlyn Jenner and Kate Garraway faced to take on the first Bushtucker Trial.

Caitlyn, who is taking home the largest fee this year, impressed viewers with her courageous attitude and support of her fellow campmates.

The US star was unphased at the prospect of jumping out of a helicopter and was the only one not to jump out of a jeep screaming when the celebs realised they'd been joined by a huge spider.

Find out how Caitlyn and Kate cope with their joint trial when I'm A Celebrity returns tonight at 9pm on ITV
