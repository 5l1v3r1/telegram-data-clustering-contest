UK Ratings: ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ tops Pakistani genre in weekly chart

18 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Raj Baddhan

Hum TV continued to dominate the Pakistani TV genre with its ‘Ehd-e-Wafa’ proving the biggest draw of the week, according to official BARB data for week ending Sunday 10th November 2019.

1. Ehd-E-Wafa (Hum TV)
Audience: 61,900 viewers

2. Ishq Zah-e-Naseeb (Hum TV)
Audience: 61,500 viewers

3. Yeh Dil Mera (Hum TV)
Audience: 58,800 viewers

4. Main Na Janu (Hum TV)
Audience: 49,200 viewers

5. Daasi (Hum TV)
Audience: 47,300 viewers
