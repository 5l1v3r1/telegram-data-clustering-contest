Odd-even will not be extended: CM

18 Nov 2019, 20:12 by Staff Reporter

Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Monday said there is no need for the odd-even scheme to be extended as pollution levels in the city have come down.

“The sky is clear now, there is no need of it [scheme],” he said at a press conference.

The government had decided to implement the scheme starting November 4 after the city’s air quality plunged into the ‘severe’ category. The scheme concluded on November 15 with Mr. Kejriwal saying it could be extended if required.
