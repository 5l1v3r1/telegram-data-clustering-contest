Lisa Armstrong and Tess Daly hit up Strictly's Blackpool after-party

18 Nov 2019, 20:22

Former Towie star Lucy Mecklenburgh showed off her baby bump at the Strictly Come Dancing after party in Blackpool on Saturday night.

Strictly stars let their hair down and partied until the early hours of Sunday morning… some reportedly leaving the venue at 5am.

Lucy, whose baby is due around January next year, waltzed into the venue wearing a stunning black jumpsuit and showing off her baby bump.

She linked arms with fiance Ryan Thomas, looking cute in a simple black tee, jacket, ripped jeans and white trainer combo.

Lisa Armstrong didn’t look like she was letting the claims of a bitter divorce between her and Ant McPartlin get to her, arriving at the after party looking ready to party.

Sporting leather trousers, high heel boots and a beautiful green flowing top, the star completed the look by wrapping a sparkly black scarf around her neck.

Gorgeous.

Meanwhile, Strictly host Tess Daly went for a silver look, with oversized grey trousers and matching jacket, paired with grey and black leopard print flats.

She was clearly ready to break it down with the Strictly crew on the dance floor and judging by their Instagram accounts, that’s exactly what they did.

Fellow host Claudia Winkleman took to Instagram to show her enthusiasm for the evening.

She posted a series of photos with Tess and some pretty hilarious captions.

One simply read: ‘Sorry Blackpool’, while another read: ‘Leopard print in Blackpool is mandatory.’

Meanwhile Tess kept it more professional with the comment: ‘What a weekend that was! Hope you all enjoyed Blackpool as much as we did.’

Other celebrities present include judge host Motsi Mabuse, who looked stunning in a pair of silver strappy heels, sparkly slit dress and coat.

We wish we were there to see her whip that coat off and run to the dance floor.

The after party comes about after the shock elimination of RuPaul’s Drag Race host Michelle Visage from the latest episode of Strictly Come Dancing.

She shared a heartfelt message to Instagram after Sunday’s results show and said that she was ‘proud’ to leave the show on their couple’s choice routine.

The 51-year-old explained: ‘We are SO proud of the dance we did, but for me, it wasn’t about judges scores. It was about shedding light on a group of people who deserve recognition and would otherwise not get it.

‘I owe my life to the LGBTQIA+ community so as an ally, I try to bring attention to them, their love, their struggles, in any way that I am able to.

‘In this instance it happened to be through dance and hear this: Voguing IS dancing.’

Michelle and Giovanni found themselves in the dance off with Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard.

Strictly continues on Saturday 23 November, from 7pm.
