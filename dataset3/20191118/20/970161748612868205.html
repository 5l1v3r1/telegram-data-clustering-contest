Is I'm A Celebrity star Roman Kemp single or does he have a girlfriend?

18 Nov 2019, 20:46

Roman Kemp has got off to a flying start in the I’m A Celebrity jungle, winning himself a place in the main camp – complete with decent food and immunity from Bushtucker trials – after the first challenge of the series.

The Capital FM DJ is one of 10 famous faces taking part in this year’s series (even if Caitlyn Jenner wasn’t sure who he was) – meaning that he’s had to leave his loved ones behind as he gets to grips with life in the Australian jungle.

But while we know all about his famous parents, does the 26-year-old have a significant other?

Here’s what you need to know about who Roman’s dating…

Is I’m A Celebrity star Roman Kemp single or does he have a girlfriend?

Roman Kemp is indeed in a relationship, with Swiss-born girlfriend Anne-Sophie Flury.

The pair have been together for two years – and although Anne-Sophie spent eight months of their relationship living in Hong Kong she has admitted this will be the longest time that they’ve ever gone without contact.

Speaking to the Mirror she explained: ‘He’s left a two-minute video for every day that he can’t speak to me. I’ll get to watch one of them every morning.

Anne-Sophie – who recently launched Shu Gum, a product which can be used to eliminate odours and clean shoes – added: ‘I’m so excited but I miss him so much! It’s really difficult  not being able to speak to him.

‘It’ll be easier when the show starts so I can watch what he’s up to.’

Meanwhile Roman told OK, ahead of his trip to the jungle: ‘She’ll probably love a break away from me. But she’s so supportive. She lived in Hong Kong for eight months during our relationship so we’re used to long distance.
