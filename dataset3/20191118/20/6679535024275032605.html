Lata Mangeshkar’s health improving

18 Nov 2019, 20:08 by Jyoti Shelar

Legendary singer Lata Mangeshkar’s health is improving, sources from the Breach Candy Hospital said on Monday. Her chest infection has been clearing and she is in a stable condition. The singer was admitted on November 11 and has been in the intensive care unit since then. Doctors had said she was diagnosed with pneumonia and left ventricular failure.
