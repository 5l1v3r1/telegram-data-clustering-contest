Coronation Street fans notice something strange in Daniel and Sinead's flat

Viewers couldn't believe what they were seeing

18 Nov 2019, 20:08 by Sophie McCoid

Coronation Street fans watched the street gather to say goodbye to Sinead in tonight's episode, but before the funeral they noticed something strange in her and Daniel's flat.

Daniel finally returned to the street with son Bertie, after running away after struggling to cope with Sinead's loss.

Daniel walked into the flat for the first time in over a week and his eyes were drawn to his laptop on the table, that displayed a photo of him, Sinead and Bertie on their wedding day.

The touching photo was taken shortly before Sinead's death, after she lost her battle with cervical cancer.

But viewers were more confused by the fact that the laptop still had power, after being left on for days without being plugged in.

And lots of fans took to twitter following the blunder.

Andrew said: "Christ where did Daniel get that laptop battery"

@emma1293 said: "Unrealistic battery life on that laptop #Corrie "

Adam Saunders said: "BLUNDER ALERT: I’m right thinking nobody has been in Daniel’s flat since he went away. Now he’s back, his laptop has been charged up all this time?"

Despite Daniel saying he wasn't going to the funeral he was eventually persuaded by Beth to go and to take Bertie with him as well.

But as they were at the funeral the episode ended with a burglar in their flat stealing the laptop - sure to leave Daniel heartbroken.
