Man found 'lying face down' on busy Exeter road

A man with a pet dog was found in a "very, very intoxicated" state

18 Nov 2019, 20:11 by Alex Richards

Police and paramedics were called to reports of somebody laying face first in one of Exeter's busiest roads.

The incident happened shortly after 7pm at Topsham Road, where it joins Holloway Road.

A concerned driver had phoned in the report to Devon & Cornwall Police.

On arrival, officers found a "very, very intoxicated man" with a dog and a rucksack in the layby area of a bus stop.

South West Ambulance Service also attended.

Police believe the man is a street drinker and possibly homeless, and say "nothing untoward" was involved.

The man is being treated by paramedics.

A drone was seen flying over the scene, however police say the device was not involved in the call-out and likely belonged to a member of the public.
