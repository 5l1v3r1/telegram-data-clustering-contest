Special train to Santragachi

18 Nov 2019, 20:05 by Special Correspondent

A train with special fare will be operated between Coimbatore and Santragachi on November 22 and 29.

This train will have halts at Duvvada and Kottavalasa near Visakhapatnam.

Train no. 02844 Coimbatore - Santragachi special train will leave Coimbatore at 9.45 p.m. on Friday and will reach Duvvada on Saturday at 6.03 p.m. and depart at 6.05 p.m. to reach Santragachi at 8.45 a.m. on Sunday, a statement issued by Senior Divisional Commercial Manager G. Suneel Kumar said here on Monday.

Halts

These train will halt at Tiruppur, Erode, Salem, Katpadi, Perambur, Sullurupeta, Gudur, Nellore, Ongole, Tenali, Vijayawada, Eluru, Rajahmundry, Samalkot, Tuni, Duvvada, Kottavalasa, Vizianagaram, Srikakulam Road, Palasa, Sompeta, Brahmapur, Khurda Road, Bhubaneswar, Naraj Marthapur, Bhadrak, Balasore and Kharagpur stations en route.
