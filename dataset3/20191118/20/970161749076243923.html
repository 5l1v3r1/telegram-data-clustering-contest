Where is Nadine Coyle’s accent from?

18 Nov 2019, 20:15

Nadine Coyle has already had a tough time on I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! after she was forced to face her fears and jump out of a helicopter.

The former Girls Aloud band member, who has a five year old daughter called Anaiya with her former partner, American football star Jason Bell, is one of 10 celebrities currently holed up in the Australian jungle for the ITV reality show.

Fans are already loving Nadine on the show, but some have been struggling to understand her accent, so where is Nadine from?

Where is Nadine Coyle from?

An Irish singer and performer, Nadine is originally from from Londonderry, also known as Derry, in Northern Ireland.

The 34-year-old star moved to the UK after she found fame in Girls Aloud, the girl group that was formed after the final of Popstars: The Rivals in November 2002.

Nadine moved over to America in 2008 to work after Girls Aloud split and lived there for eight years. As a result, despite the fact that she has an Irish accent, the star does also have a slight American-twang when she speaks.

Nadine moved back to her native Northern Ireland from the US with her daughter in 2016.

Talking to Lorraine Kelly in January 2018 about her accent, Nadine said: ‘With my accent, whenever I slowdown, which I try to do on TV, otherwise the tweets will be saying ‘what is she talking about’…and when I slow it down I think it does get a bit of an American twang going on.’
