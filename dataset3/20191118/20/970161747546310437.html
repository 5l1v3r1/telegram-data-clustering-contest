Is Jacqueline Jossa still with Dan Osborne and how many children do they have?

18 Nov 2019, 20:25

Jacqueline Jossa might be a long way from her family while she’s in the I’m A Celebrity jungle but they’ve been quick to take to social media to show their support for her.

The actress – best known as EastEnders’ Lauren Branning – was left terrified on Sunday night’s season opener as she faced the show’s notorious ‘walk the plank’ challenge alongside teammate Ian Wright.

And back home Dan Osborne and their children have been donning their ‘Team Jac’ T-shirts in her honour as Dan said on Instagram: ‘Where’s that phone, we need to get voting.’ Which is all too cute for words.

Jacqueline and former Towie star Dan have had their ups and downs in recent years, briefly splitting last year – but are they still together?

Here’s what you need to know…

Are Jacqueline Jossa and Dan Osborne still married?

Despite all the rumours, Jacqueline and Dan are indeed still married.

The actress confirmed they are still together while answering fan questions on Instagram back in May, sharing a video of Dan and answering the question of whether they were still married with a simple ‘yep’.

Dan had sparked speculation earlier this year after suggesting he and Jacqueline were ‘losing their friendship’, and addressed his marriage in an interview with new! magazine.

When asked if they had talked about ending things he said: ‘No, but there is a real negative energy and life’s too short to live like that. I just want to be happy and I want Jac to be happy. Whether that’s with me or not.

‘It’s more like a really bad tension in the house and it’s getting worse. We’re losing our friendship. When I feel it’s getting too much, I’ll get out of the house and go to the gym or something.’

The couple – who have been together since 2013 – have been married for two years, and have previously struggled with rumours of infidelity.

Their relationship came under scrutiny earlier this year after they faced rumours that Dan had been unfaithful with Love Island’s Gabby Allen – whom he later appeared with on Celebrity Big Brother – and also Alexandra Cane.

He also hit back after he was pictured partying with a female friend in Los Angeles in April, saying on Instagram Stories: ‘So much bulls**t gets posted and printed about me. Seriously just want them to leave me alone now. It’s getting too much, I can’t even hang out with friends or even work without them talking s**t about me. Am I being targeted? Cause I swear I don’t see others getting it like I do.’

Jacqueline and Dan did break up briefly in early 2018, reportedly due to ‘a series of rows’ but reunited after Dan’s spell in the Celebrity Big Brother house in August, when he admitted how much he had missed her.

How many children do Jacqueline Jossa and Dan Osborne have?

The couple are parents to two daughters, Ella and Mia.

Ella was born in February 2015, while Mia was born in June 2018.

Dan also has a son, six-year-old Teddy, from his previous relationship with ex-girlfriend Megan Tomlin.

I’m A Celebrity continues on ITV on Monday night at 9pm.
