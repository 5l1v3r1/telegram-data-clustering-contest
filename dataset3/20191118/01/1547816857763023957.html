Tanhaji -The Unsung Warrior: The Latest Promo Of Ajay Devgn’s Magnum Opus Is Intriguing; Trailer Out Tomorrow

18 Nov 2019, 01:30 by Divya Ramnani

The latest promo of Ajay Devgn’s Tanhaji -The Unsung Warrior is simple yet impactful, fans wonder if it also has a glimpse of Kajol

The makers of Tanhaji -The Unsung Warrior are leaving no stone unturned in adding to the film’s hype, as with each passing day, Ajay Devgn has been unveiling new promos. Other than Ajay Devgn as a fierce and brave Tanaji Malusare, the film boasts of a highly-ensemble cast and the back-to-back convincing character posters serve as enough proof. Today, the actor took to his social media and shared a new promo, which doesn’t show anyone’s face but has still sent fans into a tizzy. (ALSO READ - SAME-TO-SAME: Saif Ali Khan’s First Look Poster From Tanhaji Is A Complete Rip-Off Of Jon Snow From GoT; We Have Proof)

The clip shows two hands, all drenched, exchanging golden bangles. It made fans wonder if the two hands are that of Ajay and Kajol, as the latter will be playing the role of Tanaji Malusare's wife Savitri Malusare. Other than the visuals, it’s the promos super-intense background score that calls for attention. And by the looks of it, we are assuming that the makers have sure done justice to this one of the most important milestones in the history of India.

Tanhaji -The Unsung Warrior is a special one for Ajay Devgn for numerous reasons. Well, it reunites the actor with his wife Kajol on the big screen and also with Saif Ali Khan after Omkara. Other than that, Tanhaji will be Ajay Devgn’s 100th film. It has been helmed by Om Raut and also stars Sharad Kelkar and Luke Kenny in pivotal roles. The film is slated to release on January 10, 2020, alongside Deepika Padukone’s Chhapaak.
