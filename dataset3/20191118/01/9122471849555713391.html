Urban Meyer Tweets Message Ahead Of Ohio State-Penn State

18 Nov 2019, 01:03 by Matt Hladik

Ohio State will host Penn State in a battle for the Big Ten East this Saturday. Urban Meyer is certainly ready for the game.

Meyer will be on hand as part of FOX’s pregame show and if you were wondering if he was going to remain neutral, his message to Ohio State fans tonight ended any possibility of that.

“See you Saturday for the @CFBONFOX Big Noon Kickoff Show, Buckeye Nation! #GoBucks,” Meyer wrote on Twitter.
