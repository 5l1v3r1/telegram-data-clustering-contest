Michelle Visage slams 'deplorable' James Jordan after Strictly 'fake crying' claims

The pair have been taking pot shots at each other online after Michelle, 51, and Giovanni Pernice landed in the bottom two and were sent home - but she isn't going quietly

18 Nov 2019, 01:49 by Irishmirror.ie

War has broken out between axed Michelle Visage and former Strictly Come Dancing star James Jordan.

The pair have gone in on each other on twitter after she became the the eighth celebrity to leave the show.

James alleges the Drag Race star only performed to iconic Madonna hit Vogue to win 'the LGBT vote'.

He then called out her 'fake tears' before the 51-year-old hit back.

She tweeted: "That's deplorable. Gross" after being directed to his post - before telling him: "Also James, you should @ people when you talk about them! That's all."

He then claimed she was trolling him after saying the American was "boring!!!".

James said her choice backfired, adding: "Not for me sorry... not dancing, just posing. Obviously needs the LGBTQIA community's vote".

The dancer then put it was an "EASY EASY decision" to get rid of the her.
