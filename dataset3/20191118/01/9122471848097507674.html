Patriots Star Taunts Eagles TE Zach Ertz Following The Win

18 Nov 2019, 01:22 by Andrew Holleran

The New England Patriots notched a win over the Philadelphia Eagles on Sunday evening, 17-10. They’re winning the postgame, too.

Patriots cornerback Stephon Gilmore took a shot at Eagles tight end Zach Ertz while speaking to reporters following the win.

The All-Pro defensive back referenced Ertz crying after a game earlier in the season.

“Stephon Gilmore said he knew he could get Zach Ertz a little rattled because he had seen the Eagles tight end crying on film,” Doug Kyed tweeted.
