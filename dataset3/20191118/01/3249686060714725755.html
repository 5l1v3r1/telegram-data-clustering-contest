VICTON bring on the 'nostalgic night' in the performance version of their MV

18 Nov 2019, 01:10 by jennywill

VICTON has dropped the performance version of their "nostalgic night" MV.

The performance version of the MV takes only the clips from the night streets in the original MV, where they're dancing their choreography. The performance version of the MV is a surprise gift to their fans for all they've helped VICTON achieve with their most recent comeback.
