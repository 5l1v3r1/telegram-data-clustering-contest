Elderly man missing from Gold Coast medical facility

18 Nov 2019, 01:21 by Shanee Dobeson

POLICE are appealing for help to find an elderly man who went missing from a medical facility in Robina this morning.

Vaskrsija Slavnic is believed to have left the Bayberry Lane establishment between 4am and 6am.

Police say he walked out without telling staff.

Concerns are being held for his welfare due to his age and medical background.

Anyone who sees Mr Slavnic or has any information to offer police about his whereabout is urged to contact Policelink on 131 444.
