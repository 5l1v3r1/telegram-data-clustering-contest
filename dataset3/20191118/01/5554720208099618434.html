Disha Patani buying coriander, Janhvi Kapoor’s chuski, Taimur Ali Khan and Tiger Shroff’s facial six-pack feature prominently as Richa Chadha makes debut as stand-up comedienne

18 Nov 2019, 01:51 by JKR Staff

Disha Patani buying coriander leaves, Jahnvi Kapoor’s pet bottle chuski having an Instagram fan page and the paparazzi’s obsession with Taimur Ali Khan were the highlights of the Richa Chadha’s debut performance as a stand-up comedienne. Richa left her audience in splits as she took to stage to perform for Amazon Prime’s new series One Mic Stand.

Richa brought some funny anecdotes from her personal life and how her close relatives had reacted when they learnt that she was dating a Muslim, actor Ali Fazal. She also mentioned that she will proudly announce to the world whenever she’s pregnant with Ali Fazal’s child to make sure that the couple’s child fully benefits from the phenomenon of nepotism. In a veiled dig at Karan Johar, she said that the name of her child’s debut film will be Foetus Of The Year.

Her swipe at Karan did not stop here. She said that actors from commercially hit films made appearances on shows like Koffee With Karan, whereas actors from critically acclaimed films such as herself were invited to perform as a stand-up comedian on a platform such as One Mic Stand for free. As expected her impromptu improvisations left her audience in splits.
