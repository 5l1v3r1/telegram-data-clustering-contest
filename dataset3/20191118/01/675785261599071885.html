Stacey Solomon 'in tears' and says she misses boyfriend Joe Swash on Extra Camp

Loose Women star Stacey, 30, was left feeling emotional tonight as she tuned in to I'm A Celebrity's spin-off show

18 Nov 2019, 01:23 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

Stacey Solomon was in tears this evening as she watched Extra Camp this evening - which, for the first time in ten years, didn't feature Joe Swash.

The Loose Women star, 30, was left feeling emotional tonight as she tuned in to I'm A Celebrity's spin-off show.

Instead of seeing her boyfriend's smiling face, Joel Dommett was joined by new presenters Emily Atack and Adam Thomas.

Taking to Twitter during the episode, she wrote with crying emojis: "Soooo wierd watching #ExtraCamp without you @realjoeswash."

 

And viewers agreed, insisting it wasn't the same without him.

One tweeted: "I don’t like it!! it’s not the same."

While another commented: "It’s so not the same. Bring him back."

A third remarked: "Missing @realjoeswash already."

"Miss him already. Our loss is your gain," another wrote.

One wrote: "It’s not gonna be the same without him."

Joe is set to take part in this year's Dancing On Ice, which he is already training for ahead of its launch in January.

Ever since he won I'm A Celebrity in 2008, Joe has presented the ITV2 spin-off show for the main programme.

Over the years he has hosted the show alongside a number of other presenters, including Caroline Flack, Russell Kane, Laura Whimore, Rob Beckett and even girlfriend Stacey Solomon.

*I'm A Celebrity Extra Camp returns tomorrow at 10pm on ITV2
