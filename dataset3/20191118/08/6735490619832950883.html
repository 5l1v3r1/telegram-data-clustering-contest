John Mayer Offers Up His Own Take On The ‘Christmas Lights’ Lyric In Taylor Swift’s ‘Lover’

18 Nov 2019, 08:28 by Corey Atad

John Mayer has no problem with leaving Christmas lights up ’til January.

The singer talked about his love of Taylor Swift’s recent hit “Lover” on his Instagram Live show “Current Mood” with special guest Shawn Mendes, who appears on a new remix of the song.

“Love the song, I really like the song,” Mayer commented, before offering up a slight criticism. “I get a little chuckle because she says, ‘We can keep the Christmas lights up ‘til January.’ And I go, ‘You’re insane. Everyone keeps their Christmas lights up until January.'”
