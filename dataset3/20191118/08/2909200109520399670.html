Jet Li to train NPP militia karate: Brogya Genfi denies story

18 Nov 2019, 08:24 by MyNewsGH

Former Regional Youth Organizer of the opposition National Democratic Congress in the Ashanti Region, Brogya Genfi has denied making any statement regarding the visit of Chinese martial actor Jet Li to Ghana.

According to a supposed screenshot of a news story going round, Brogya is alleged to have said President Akufo-Addo brought Jet Li to Ghana to train NPP militia.

But reacting to the issue, Mr Genfi said it is a malicious story that must betreated with disdain.

See his full reaction

Good morning friends. I have seen a screenshot of a purported published story on ghanaweb.com with headline “Akufo-Addo brings Jet Li to train NPP Militia in karate – Brogya Gyamfi”.

I have not spoken about the visit of Jet Li to Ghana anywhere; not to any media house. I therefore entreat all to ignore this obvious malicious non-existing story and treat it with disdain.

Brogya GENFI
