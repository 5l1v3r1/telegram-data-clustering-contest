Two pedestrians struck in crosswalk at Sidney Avenue and Third Street in Sidney

18 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Cindy E. Harnett

Two pedestrians in their 80s were hit by a vehicle at a Sidney intersection Sunday night.

The pedestrians, ages 88 and 87, suffered non-life threatening injuries when they were struck in the crosswalk at Sidney Avenue and Third Street near the Star Cinema.

One received treatment at the scene and the other was taken to hospital for assessment and was released, said Sidney-North Saanich spokeswoman Meighan de Pass.

The driver received a ticket for failure to yield to pedestrians at a crosswalk and for a windshield or window that impairs vision, de Pass said.

The fire department was at the scene by 10 p.m. and the intersection remained closed for a time as Sidney RCMP investigated.
