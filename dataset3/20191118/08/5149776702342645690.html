Man charged after robbery at William Hill betting shop

He has been remanded in custody

18 Nov 2019, 08:08 by Jamie Barlow

A 42-year-old man has been charged after a robbery at a William Hill betting shop.

Karl Ward, of no fixed address, has been charged with robbery.

It relates to an incident at a William Hill shop on Outram Street, Sutton-in-Ashfield, on November 13.

Ward has also been charged with domestic assault by beating and malicious communications.

He has been remanded into custody and will appear at Mansfield Magistrates' Court on Monday, November 18.
