Kerala PSC invites applications for LDC posts, 10th pass can apply

Kerala PSC recruitment 2019: The application process will be closed on December 18, 2019. Interested candidates can apply through the website- keralapsc.gov.in

18 Nov 2019, 08:33 by Careers Desk

Kerala PSC recruitment 2019: Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) has invited applications for the posts of Lower Division Clerk (LDC). The online application process for the posts of Lower Division Clerk (LDC) has started, and the candidates can apply online through the website- keralapsc.gov.in.

The minimum qualifications to apply for the posts is class 10. The candidates can apply online through the official website on or before December 18, 2019.

The commission will soon release the district wise vacancy details for the Lower Division Clerk (LDC) posts. The age of the candidates should be between 18 to 36 years.

Kerala PSC recruitment 2019: Eligibility criteria

Educational qualifications: The candidates need to possess a class 10 or SSLC certificate from any institution recognised by the state/ central boards.

Age limit: The age of the candidates should be between 18 to 36 years. For the age-wise relaxation of various reserved categories, please check the official notification.

Pay scale: The selected candidates will be on the pay scale between Rs 10,000 to 43,600 per month.

Selection process: The candidates will be selected on the basis of written examination and interview.

KPSC recruitment 2019: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website, keralapsc.gov.in

Step 2: On the home page, scroll down to click on ‘register’ link for new users

Step 3: Create a user id and password to log-in

Step 4: Create one-time profile by adding information

Step 5: Submit the application form

Step 6: Make payment.

The candidates can apply online through the website- keralapsc.gov.in on or before December 18, 2019.
