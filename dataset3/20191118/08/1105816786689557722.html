New AirPods Pro that cost $67,000 are sold out

18 Nov 2019, 08:46 by Tech Desk

If Apple devices and accessories were not expensive enough, Caviar, a Russian luxury gadget company has recently introduced its Airpods covered in 18-karat gold costing a whopping $67,000.

 

Sold out on their website, extravagant designs are not new to the company.

They previously, launched a  $70,000 diamond-embellished iPhone 11 Pro and this time around they have introduced a pair of Airpods Pro made of “750-content gold” which means it is 75 per cent pure gold.

You could also purchase a gold-covered wireless charging case to go with the Airpods.

Catering to a specific niche market, Caviar designs are well-suited for those who like purchasing collector items.
