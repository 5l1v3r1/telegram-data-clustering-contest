Body located, Okarito river mouth, West Coast

18 Nov 2019, 08:33 by New Zealand Police

The body of a man was located at the Okarito river mouth, West Coast, around 10.30am yesterday.

The body has been recovered from the lagoon mouth and a formal identification process is underway.

Police are unable to comment further at this time.

The matter will be referred to the Coroner.
