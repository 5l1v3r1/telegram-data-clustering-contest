GlobalStar Travel Management and Nina & Pinta launch global SME Research programme

18 Nov 2019, 08:55 by Stavros Andriopoulos

GlobalStar Travel Management has partnered with managed travel consultancy Nina & Pinta to conduct a survey collecting research on small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and their future travel programme requirements. The results, along with expert advice on this topic, will be shared in a white paper which will be distributed in the first quarter of 2020.
