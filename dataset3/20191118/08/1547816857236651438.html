Karan Johar Says 'Yash Is Camera Shy Like Me,' But Goes On To Flirt With Cams At Good Newwz Trailer Launch

18 Nov 2019, 08:40 by Lubna Khan

Karan Johar recently took to Instagram to share that his son Yash is camera shy, just like him. However, it is quite unbelievable, and given how confidently he poses in front of the cam, especially at Good Newwz trailer launch, we don’t quite buy it! Check out the pictures

Karan Johar has a huge following on social media and the ace filmmaker is also quite active on it, frequently posting his pictures and sometimes, with his kids. His pictures are quite aesthetic, and he oozes so much confidence and elegance in every picture of his. Which is why, it comes as a surprise as Karan Johar took to his Instagram recently and shared a picture with his kids and captioned it, “My son is camera shy just like me!!!” Now, given how confidently he poses in front of the cameras, especially at Good Newwz trailer launch, we don’t quite buy it!

A little while ago, the cast and crew of Good Newwz starring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosnjh and Kiara Advani launched the trailer of the film. Karan Johar was also present at the event and photos from the event just prove that he is a natural in front of the camera. Karan Johar very confidently addressed the media and posed with the stars quite confidently. We had many laughs doing it. The fact that he is expressing himself so well in the advertisement is really a treat to watch. He is great fit for this commercial”
