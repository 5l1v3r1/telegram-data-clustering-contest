Scottish duo set to battle for transfer of former Derby County defender - reports

Latest Derby County transfer news | The 31-year-old had a sixth-month stint with the Rams but failed to make a single first-team appearance

18 Nov 2019, 08:38 by Mark Wakefield

Former Derby County defender Efe Ambrose is reportedly the subject of two clubs in Scotland

The experienced centre-back signed for the Rams on a short-term deal in February as a free agent, shortly after his contract at Hibernian has expired.

Ambrose was signed as defensive cover to centre-backs Richard Keogh and Fikayo Tomori, but left Pride Park in the summer without a single first-team appearance to his name.

Now fresh reports are linking Ambrose with a move to two clubs north of the border, one of which is his former club Hibs.

The Daily Mail claim that Hibs are set to battle it out with fellow Scottish Premiership outfit St Johnstone for the 31-year-old’s signature.

Ambrose made 81 appearances for Hibs before leaving the club in January of this year, and could return less than a year after his departure.
