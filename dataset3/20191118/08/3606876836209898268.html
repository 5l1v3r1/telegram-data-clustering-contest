Jubilee MP bereaved

18 Nov 2019, 08:19 by brian oruta

Condolences to the family

Embakasi West Member of Parliament George Theuri is mourning the death of his father.

In a post on Facebook, the Jubilee elected MP announced that his father had passed on, Monday morning.

He went on to say that he is broken by the passing of his dad and can’t believe that his hero is no more.

“My family mourns......"I can't believe Dad you are gone" R.I.P Dad. I celebrate u my hero. I may try to look strong on the outside but I'm so broken,” wrote Theuri.

Yusuf Hassan mourns Khadija

The passing of George Theuri’s father comes a few days after another Jubilee MP, Yusuf Hassan announced the death of his aunt, Khadija, who was the last remaining matriarch of their family.

The Kamukunji Member of Parliament (MP) in a tweet revealed that the relative had passed on in Garissa County.

"Lost my aunty Khadija today. She passed on in Garissa. Also an end of era for my family, she was the last surviving child of my grandfather Sultan Osman ‘Gelle’ Yusuf Magan," the MP disclosed.
