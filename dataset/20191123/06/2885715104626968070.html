Ambati Rayudu alleges corruption in Hyderabad cricket, requests state govt to intervene

Ambati Rayudu cited personal reasons for the break on Friday. However, his tweet on Saturday morning tells a different story.

23 Nov 2019, 06:50 by Sports Desk

Ambati Rayudu appealed to Telangana’s Minister for Industry and Municipal Administration, KT Rama Rao to look into the ‘rampant corruption prevailing in HCA (Hyderabad Cricket Association)” in a tweet on Saturday. Rayudu alleged corruption in HCA one day after taking a break for a few weeks from playing first-class cricket for Hyderabad. Rayudu cited personal reasons for the break on Friday. However, his tweet on Saturday morning tells a different story.
