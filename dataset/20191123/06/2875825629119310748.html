Young Mason Mount shows how he learned to score free-kicks like Cristiano Ronaldo

Chelsea star prepares to face Man City by sharing video of himself as a youngster firing home a set-piece that would make CR7 proud

23 Nov 2019, 06:25 by Irishmirror.ie

Mason Mount fired a warning of his talents ahead of Chelsea's showdown against Manchester City - by sharing a video of himself as a youngster firing home a Cristiano Ronaldo-style free-kick.

Chelsea midfielder Mount is in thrilling form ahead of Saturday's mouthwatering clash against City at the Etihad Stadium, where third plays fourth as the rivals aim to catch Premier League leaders Liverpool .

Mount has scored four goals in 12 Premier League games so far this season, and notched his first for England in the Three Lions' 4-0 win over Kosovo last weekend.

And now he has shown what else he has in his locker, tweeting a video of himself as an aspiring young footballer showing how he had learned to take direct free-kicks from set-piece master Ronaldo.

In the video, Mount is seen confidently explaining how he has adopted Ronaldo's technique - before confidently tucking away a kick that would have made the former Manchester United man proud.

The young Mount says: "I try to base my free-kick on Ronaldo.

"Hit the ball by the valve and it moves. That's all you do - top corner, top bins."

He is then seen confidently stepping up and bending a free-kick over the wall and past the helpless keeper into the back of the net.
