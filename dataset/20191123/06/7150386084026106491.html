Bala Box Office Day 15: Film Reaches Rs 100 cr, Another Super-Hit For Ayushmann Khurrana

Here are the latest Box Office figures of Bala featuring Ayushmann Khurrana, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar. The film, directed by Amar Kaushik, has been declared a super-hit and has collected Rs 100.15 crore in 15 days. Check out the detailed report.

23 Nov 2019, 06:05

Actor Ayushmann Khurrana has got another super-hit in his kitty as his latest film Bala has crossed the benchmark of Rs 100 crore at the Box Office. The film collected Rs 1.35 crore on Friday that took the total to Rs 100.15 crore after 15 days of its release. The second week total of the film remained Rs 26.56 crore. The Amar Kaushik directorial has emerged as another solid entertainer featuring Ayushmann bringing up an important subject for discussion among the audience.
