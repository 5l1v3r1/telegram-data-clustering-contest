October Transient Occupancy Tax Results

23 Nov 2019, 06:52

Source: City of Santa Barbara

The City of Santa Barbara collected approximately $1.76 million in transient occupancy taxes (TOT) for October 2019, which is 8.1% above September of last year.

Through October 31, the City has collected approximately $8.5 million in TOT revenues for fiscal year 2020, which runs from July 1 through June 30.  The City’s adopted TOT budget is $19,989,179.
