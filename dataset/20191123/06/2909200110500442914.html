God will punish you – Allotey Jacobs to Eku Juice naysayers

23 Nov 2019, 06:02 by MyNewsGH

Former Central Regional Chairman of the National Democratic Congress (NDC), Bernard Kojo Allotey Jacobs in an interview on Peace FM has said God will punish all those speaking ill about the Ekumfi Pineapple factory.

According to Mr. Allotey in the interview monitored by MyNewsGh.com, some people have tagged the newly Eku Pineapple juice as poisonous and therefore treating the life of people.

“I have heard news and I have even read some on social media especially Facebook that the Eku Pineapple fruit juice is poisonous, now this goes to all saying and spreading the false news God will punish you all”, he said.

MyNewsGh.com published earlier that he factory, the first to be constructed under the government’s One District One Factory (1D1F) policy project is expected to employ over 5000 people and most of the slots will be going to Ekumfi residents.

Mr. Allotey concluded that we should all support the course and stop the ill talk.
