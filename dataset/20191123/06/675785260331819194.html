What channel is West Ham vs Tottenham on? TV and live stream information

Jose Mourinho takes charge of Tottenham for the first time against West Ham United on Saturday with both teams desperately needing a London derby win

23 Nov 2019, 06:30 by Gareth Bicknell

Jose Mourinho leads his Tottenham side into battle for the first time against West Ham United on Saturday, in a mouthwatering London derby between two sides who desperately need to reverse their form.

Tottenham head to the London Stadium today hoping for the boost a new manager normally brings, after Mourinho took over the reins from Mauricio Pochettino, who was sacked on Tuesday.

Last season's Champions League finalists are floundering in 14th place in the Premier League, and they go in search of their first win in six league games against a West Ham side two places below them and in even worse form.

After Pochettino's sacking, Hammers boss Manuel Pellegrini is one of the favourites to be the next Premier League manager for the chop.

His side have suffered four defeats in their last five matches - and the Argentine boss desperately needs to spoil Mourinho's welcome party.

When is the match?

West Ham vs Tottenham is the Saturday lunchtime game, with a 12.30 kick-off UK time.

Where is the match being played?

The game is being played at London Stadium, where West Ham have lost three and won just two of their six home league games so far this season.

What TV channel is the game on?

West Ham vs Tottenham will be shown on BT Sport 1 and BT Sport Ultimate, with coverage starting at noon UK time.

Can you live stream the match?

BT Sport subscribers can live stream the match via BT Sport's video player and the BT Sport app.
