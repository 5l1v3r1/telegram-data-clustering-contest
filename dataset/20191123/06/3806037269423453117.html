The adidas YEEZY BOOST 350 V2 Is Set to Receive an "Earth" Makeover

Another take on the popular model.

23 Nov 2019, 06:21 by Nicolaus Li

As we approach the new year, we are learning about new colorways YEEZY and adidas are making for the YEEZY BOOST 350 V2.

According to YEEZY MAFIA, an “Earth” take on the popular model is set to release sometime early 2020. This time around, the adidas YEEZY BOOST 350 V2 is given a medium brown Primeknit upper paired with a dark brown sockliner. Elevating the shoe is a full-length BOOST midsole encased in a matching brown rubber cover and contrasting gum rubber outsole with a neon yellow heel hit at the rear.

Reportedly priced at $220 USD, the adidas YEEZY BOOST 350 V2 “Earth” is rumored to be releasing sometime early 2020
