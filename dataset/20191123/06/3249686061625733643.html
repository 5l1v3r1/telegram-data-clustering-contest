Kim Chung Ha enjoys luxury in Bali for 'Nylon'

23 Nov 2019, 06:11 by beansss

Solo artist Kim Chung Ha flew to the luxurious island of Bali for a stylish getaway, in 'Nylon' magazine's December issue!

Despite the heat of the Bali sun, Kim Chung Ha showcased nothing but professionalism during her photoshoot in various knitted sweaters, matched with skirts, knee-high boots, and more.
