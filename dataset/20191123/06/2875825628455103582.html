What channel is Arsenal vs Southampton? TV information and live stream

Arsenal return to Premier League action after the international break looking to get their season back on track at home to Southampton

23 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Irishmirror.ie

Arsenal will look to end a run of four Premier League games without a win when they host Southampton at the Emirates.

The Gunners have experienced a miserable run leaving manager Unai Emery under pressure.

But they will hope to get back on track after the international break to make up ground on the top four.

They are eight points off fourth placed Manchester City , already facing a tough task in their bid to secure a return to Champions League football.

A struggling Southampton side travel to north London after four defeats in their last five games leaving them 19th in the Premier League table, just one point clear of Norwich at the bottom.

Arsenal are set to be without Spaniard Dani Ceballos due to a hamstring injury, with Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang set to captain the side after taking over the armband from Granit Xhaka.

When is the match?

Arsenal will host Southampton at 15:00 (GMT) on Saturday 23rd November.

Where is the match being played?

The match takes place at Arsenal's 60,260-capacity Emirates Stadium in North London.

What TV channel is it on?

Unfortunately, the game is not being shown on live TV.

Can you live stream the match?

Due to a lack of live television coverage in the UK, it cannot be live streamed.
