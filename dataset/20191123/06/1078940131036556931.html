First Vice-President of Azerbaijan Mehriban Aliyeva graced with Order of Friendship of the Russian Federation

23 Nov 2019, 06:11 by admin2

Monitoring Desk: Russian President Vladimir Putin awarded Order of Friendship of the Russian Federation to First Vice-President of Azerbaijan Mehriban Aliyeva, reports State Run News Agency AZERTAC.

First Vice-President of Azerbaijan Mehriban Aliyeva was on an official visit to Russia and held a meeting with President of the Russian Federation Vladimir Putin who awarded her one of the highest award of the Russian Federation.

She was honored for her contributions to the development and strengthening of the Azerbaijan-Russia relations.

First Vice-President of Azerbaijan Mehriban Aliyeva is internationally acclaimed personality for her contribution for philanthropist activities and receiver of several international awards.

Her international Awards

Some of her international titles and awards include Legion of Honour (France), Hilal-e-Pakistan (Pakistan), “The Symbol of Humanism – The Person of 2012 (Pakistan), Martyr Benazir Bhutto Woman Perfection Prize  (Pakistan), Order of Merit of the Republic of Poland, Sretenjski Orden (Serbia), Ruby Cross’ Order of Philanthropists of the Century International Charity Foundation of Russia, Golden Heart International Award (Russia), Order of “For Services to Astrakhan Region” (Russia), Elpida Award Greece, Commander’s Cross Order of Merit of the Republic of Hungary, Diploma of an honorary citizen of Veliko Tyrnovo (Bulgaria, Saint Princess Olga Order of 2nd degree of the Russian Orthodox Church, Knight Grand Cross of the Order of Merit of the Italian Republic, Order of Friendship (Russia),

She had been honoured by UNESCO Goodwill Ambassador, Goodwill Ambassador of ISESCO, World Health Organization prize, UNESCO Mozart Medal and High Order of the European Olympic Committee.
