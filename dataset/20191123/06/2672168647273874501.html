Army clashes with ARSA terrorists in Maungdaw

23 Nov 2019, 06:00 by EMG

 

An army column encountered with eight ARSA terrorists near Kamaungseik Village in Maungdaw, Rakhine State on November 21 and the army found two bodies and caught three ARSA terrorists and weapons and ammunitions, according to the announcement from the office of the Commander-in-Chief of the Defense Services on November 22.

The army is providing security in villages to find the ARSA terrorists and the detained ARSA terrorists are kept at Aungzu police station to take action in line with rules and regulations, announced the office of the Commander-in-Chief of the Defense Services.
