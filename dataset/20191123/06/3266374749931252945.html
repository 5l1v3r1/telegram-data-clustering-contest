Predicted Real Madrid lineup against Real Sociedad | La Liga

23 Nov 2019, 06:47 by Keshav Awasty

Marcelo and Rodrygo are in line to start when Real Madrid welcome Real Sociedad to the Santiago Bernabeu on Saturday.

Real Madrid will be hoping to remain in the upper echelons of La Liga when they play hosts to high flying Real Sociedad at the Bernabeu on Saturday night. And, Zinedine Zidane has seen his squad’s injury troubles ease up considerably heading into this weekend.

Marcelo is the latest player to return from injury, having made a swift recovery from an issue with his leg. The Brazilian had sustained the injury in the 6-0 rout of Galatasaray in the Champions League, but is now fit and available.

Marcelo should replace Ferland Mendy on the left side of the Real Madrid defence, with Dani Carvajal a certain starter on the right flank. Raphael Varane and Sergio Ramos will form the central defensive pairing once again, with Thibaut Courtois taking his place in goal.
