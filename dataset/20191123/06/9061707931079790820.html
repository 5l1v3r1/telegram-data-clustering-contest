Amazing hack for de-icing the car you have to try

and you won't get cold hands doing it either

23 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Nathan Standley

Freezing cold hands and damaged bank cards could be a distant memory after one driver shared his amazing life hack for dealing with a frozen windscreen.

As the weather gets colder, motorists are having to scrap their windscreens in a morning - and it can prove to be a huge pain.

But one driver has banished frozen hands and scraping until the cows come home, with his ingenious hack that went viral.

No longer will people be late for work due because they just could not get the car de-iced.

But if you take on board what this man has come up with your mornings might go a whole lot smoother as he can de-ice his car in an instant.

His tutorial on just what to do, which he posted on Facebook, went viral.

Instead of using a trusty can of de-icer and a scraper, Daniel Harris simply fills a sandwich bag with warm water.

He then drags it over the car windows and watches the ice melt immediately.

On Facebook, he wrote: "Hot water in a food bag and bam you've got a de-icer and a hand warmer. Works a treat, and it's fast!"

He added: "I used warm tap water so I could safely hold it and lower the risk of cracking my screen.

"The de-icer cans are so cold in the morning and this way keeps you nice and toasty.

"Oh and yes it was ice not condensation. I know it looks it but try it and you won't be disappointed."

The tip is sure to come in handy this winter.
