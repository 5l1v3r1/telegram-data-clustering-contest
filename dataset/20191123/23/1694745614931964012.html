Finding the ideal liquid eye shadow can be tricky. You want the formula to be pigmented, be comfortable to wear, and provide the punch of color or shimmer you crave without creasing. Fortunately, POPSUGAR editors have tried hundreds of varieties over the years and can recommend the most high-quality choices out there.

From glitter-packed formulas from brands like Stila and NYX Professionals to metallics from PYT Beauty and Kosas, you're sure to find an option you'll adore on our list.

Check out the best liquid eye shadow choices that make a major impact ahead.

Make Up For Ever Star Lit Liquid

The Make Up For Ever Star Lit Liquid ($24) contains a light-reflective, buildable shimmer that can be worn on both eyes and cheeks.

Touch In Sol Metallist Liquid Foil & Glitter Eye Shadow Duo

Once you try the Touch In Sol Metallist Liquid Foil & Glitter Eye Shadow Duo ($25), you'll be a convert for life. One side contains a pigmented liquid shadow, while the other contains a foiled hue for maximum metallic shine.

Cover FX Shimmer Veil

If you've been a fan of the brand's liquid highlighters, you're going to love the Cover FX Shimmer Veil ($28). The hues are completely crease-proof and dry down for a high-impact sparkle.

Giorgio Armani Beauty Eye Tint Liquid Eyeshadow

For the longest-lasting color, try Giorgio Armani Beauty Eye Tint Liquid Eyeshadow ($28). It promises to stay on for 16 full hours.

Kosas 10-Second Eyeshadow

The Kosas 10-Second Eyeshadow ($28) glides across the lid effortlessly and looks gorgeous in one swipe.

PYT Beauty Glam Glitter Gel in Gold

The PYT Beauty Glam Glitter Gel in Gold ($18) uses environmentally friendly glitter so you'll never have to choose between marine life or shimmering makeup again.

Beauty by POPSUGAR Make Waves Liquid Metallic Eye Shadow

Shake up a tube of the Beauty by POPSUGAR Make Waves Liquid Metallic Eye Shadow ($23), and swipe on shimmery color with a clean formula.

Huda Beauty Matte and Metal Melted Shadow

The Huda Beauty Matte and Metal Melted Shadow ($25) applies opaque in a single swipe and lasts all day.

Stila Shimmer & Glow Liquid Eye Shadow

For anyone whose life motto is "the more glitter, the better," the Stila Shimmer & Glow Liquid Eye Shadow ($24) will be a dream come true. The long-wearing, shimmer-packed liquid formula has a translucent base, allowing you to apply pure glittery pigment to your eyes.

Maybelline Eye Studio Color Tattoo Liquid Chrome Shadow

The intensely metallic Maybelline Eye Studio Color Tattoo Liquid Chrome Shadow ($6) gives you major color on a budget.

CoverGirl Exhibitionist Liquid Glitter Eyeshadow

The CoverGirl Exhibitionist Liquid Glitter Eyeshadow ($10) delivers heavy on the shine without any of the fallout.

Wet n Wild Megalast Liquid Catsuit Eyeshadow

The shimmery Wet n Wild Megalast Liquid Catsuit Eyeshadow ($5) lasts all day without flaking.

Pixi Liquid Fairy Lights

Get a high-impact and reflective shine with the Pixi Liquid Fairy Lights ($15).

Milani Hypnotic Eyeshadow Lights Glitter Eye Topper

The iridescent Milani Hypnotic Eyeshadow Lights Glitter Eye Topper ($6) gives the prettiest finishing touch to your makeup look.

NYX Professional Glitter Goals Liquid Eyeshadow

Pack on the shimmer with the vegan NYX Professional Glitter Goals Liquid Eyeshadow ($8), which features a lightweight formula and won't crease.
