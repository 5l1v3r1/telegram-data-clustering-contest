NFL disciplines 33 players after wild brawl

23 Nov 2019, 23:13 by NY Post, with staff writers

The Pittsburgh Steelers-Cleveland Browns brawl didn’t just prove costly for Myles Garrett and Mason Rudolph.

The NFL handed down 35 fines for $US732,219 ($AU1.1 million) total – including $250,000 each to the Browns and Steelers franchises — in response to last week’s fight, the league announced Sunday (AEDT).
