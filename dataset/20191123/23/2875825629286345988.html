Simon Cowell's girlfriend appears to 'ask who Mark Wright is' live on X Factor

Some X Factor viewers were convinced that Simon Cowell's girlfriend Lauren Silverman did not know who the former TOWIE star was while he spoke to Dermott O'Leary

23 Nov 2019, 23:51 by Irishmirror.ie

X Factor viewers were surprised to see Mark Wright sat in the audience tonight - and it seemed to confuse Simon Cowell's girlfriend.

Lauren Silverman was sat on the table next to the former The Only Way Is Essex star during the semi-final.

Radio host Mark spoke to X Factor presenter Dermot O'Leary about his favourite performers, including his pal Thom Evans and fellow Essex resident Megan McKenna.

However, some viewers tried to lip read Lauren as she spoke to her friend and were convinced she had no idea who mark was.

One viewer asked: "Did Simons partner just ask who @MarkWright_ was live on tv directly in front on the camera?!?!?  Love the lady answering clarified he was on Towie"

Lauren started listening to Mark as he spoke to Dermot but had a baffled look on her face.

Simon's girlfriend, who is mum to their son Eric, then leaned across the table to seemingly ask her friend a question.

After getting a response, Lauren then sat back in her chair and clapped Mark while looking towards the camera.

When Dermot asked who Mark was cheering for, he said: "It's a tough one. Thom Evans is my boy so I obviously want him to win.

"Megan is an Essex girl who's repping the Essex.

"And Vinnie is one of my all time heroes. It's tough."

Dermot pointed out that he had chosen "pretty much the whole semi-final line-up" to support.

Giggling Mark admitted he was a big fan of the show before finally choosing his favourite to win.

He added: "I think with what just happened it's got to be Megan. She was phenomenal. [Louis] Walshy's got to win."

X Factor viewers took to Twitter after spotting Mark in the audience.

One viewer asked: "Was that Mark Wright cheering Meghan?"

"Wtf is @MarkWright_ doing there?," added another.

A third said: "Mark Wright’s teeth are keeping the whole audience lit up in the background"
