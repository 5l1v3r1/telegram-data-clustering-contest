Great Britain beaten 2-1 by Spain in Davis Cup semi-final

23 Nov 2019, 23:48 by Ben Coley

Rafael Nadal hauled Spain to a 2-1 victory over Great Britain in the Davis Cup semi-finals in Madrid.

First, the Spanish talisman beat Dan Evans to force a decider, and then he starred alongside Feliciano Lopez as the pair beat Jamie Murray and Neil Skupski in a close doubles rubber.

Murray and Skupski performed brilliantly, forcing two deciding sets, but on the shoulders of Nadal's serve the Spanish duo took both, latterly four saving set points in a 7-6 (3) 7-6 (8) win.
