We love... Fashion fixes for the week ahead – in pictures

23 Nov 2019, 23:50 by Jo Jones, Bemi Shaw

Dodo Bar Or and Annabel’s collaborate on a winter collection; Peter Lindbergh’s final project on Dior before his death; and Canada Goose and Concepts release limited-edition outerwear

Last look

This two-volume book was photographer Peter Lindbergh’s final project before his death this year, and includes never-before-seen images from 70 years of work with the house of Dior. £150, taschen.com

Party people

Get into the festive spirit with H&M’s holiday collection and dance the night away. Dress, £34.99, jumper, £19.99, boots, £69.99, all from hm.com, available from 4 December

Flight jackets

Canada Goose and Concepts have taken inspiration from the 1990s for their new limited-edition collection. Jacket, £750, canadagoose.com

Wall flowers

Annabel’s night club and fashion brand Dodo Bar Or have created a limited-edition winter getaway collection inspired by Annabel’s Jungle Bar exotic wall coverings. Animal prints, jungle foliage and rare birds are reimagined into women’s bathing suits and maxi dresses. From £265, net-a-porter.com

‘Like a rich jewel...’

Tada & Toy and Amelia Windsor have launched a Romeo & Juliet-inspired collection. From £35, Reformation, 186 Westbourne Grove, London W11 ( tadaandtoy.com)

Stella Stan Smiths

Stella McCartney and Adidas are back again with a new take on fashion’s favourite trainers, seen on tennis courts since the 1970s. The first ever vegan Stan Smith has been updated with rainbow laces for a pop of colour. £235, stellamccartney.com
