Lucky punter wins nearly €150k in Saturday's lotto draw

23 Nov 2019, 23:03 by James Cox

There was no winner of Saturday's €5 million Lotto jackpot but one lucky Irish punter won nearly €150,000.

The €5,147,131 jackpot will now rollover to next Wednesday.

Over 100,000 players won something in tonight's draw.

Almost 17,000 won €10 while somebody is €148,259 richer. Hey, it's not €5 million but we surely wouldn't turn it down!

The jackpot numbers were 4, 15, 20, 21, 34, 39 and the bonus number was 19.

There was no winner of the  €1,000,000 Lotto Plus One draw either but the winning numbers in that were 1, 8, 11, 15, 17, 29, and the bonus number was 33.
