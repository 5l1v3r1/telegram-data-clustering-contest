Ohio State Fans Will Love Ryan Day’s Quote About Michigan

23 Nov 2019, 23:28 by Alek Arend

The Buckeyes’ celebration after beating No. 8 Penn State on Saturday was short lived. Ryan Day and his players immediately had to shift their focus to the Michigan Wolverines. The massive rivalry will be played next Saturday.

But make no mistake – Day and the Buckeyes have been thinking about this game for a long time. In fact, the Ohio State head coach revealed the Buckeyes talk about Michigan (The Team Up North to those within the program) every day.

“The Team Up North is something we talk about every day,” Day said following Ohio State’s win over Penn State on Saturday, via Colin Hass-Hill.
