Winter Weather Advisory issued November 23 at 11:07PM EST until November 24 at 1:00PM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

23 Nov 2019, 23:07

Winter Weather Advisory: Luzerne, Southern Wayne, Pike and 1 more area until 1:00 PM

* WHAT…Mixed precipitation expected. Total snow accumulations
of a coating in the valleys with 1 to 2 inches expected over
the Poconos. Ice accumulations of up to one tenth of an inch
over the Poconos with no ice accumulation expected in the
valleys.

* WHERE…Lackawanna, Luzerne, Pike and Southern Wayne counties.

* WHEN…Until 1 PM EST Sunday.

* IMPACTS…Plan on slippery road conditions especially over the
Poconos.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS…A mix of rain and freezing rain will
continue into the overnight hours and change to all rain. Most
of the freezing rain will be found in the Poconos. Expect the
rain to turn over to snow Sunday morning before ending by
afternoon.
Slow down and use caution while traveling through the Poconos.

The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can
be obtained by calling 5 1 1.

The Pennsylvania Department of Transportation and Pennsylvania
Turnpike Commission remind motorist to adjust speeds based on
driving conditions as winter weather impacts Pennsylvania
roadways. Visit www.511pa.com for the latest travel, roadways,
and traffic conditions.
