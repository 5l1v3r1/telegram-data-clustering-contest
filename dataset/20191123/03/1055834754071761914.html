Video: Nintendo Minute play The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild “Ultimate Feast or Fail Challenge”

23 Nov 2019, 03:58 by ssf1991

Thanksgiving is less than a week away from occurring in the United States, so Nintendo Minute’s Kit and Krysta is celebrating the occasion with a special episode for this week. The pair said in the video description that “it’s almost time for the wonderful day of eating – Thanksgiving. We’re celebrating by cooking some delicious and definitely not dubious recipes in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. We hope you all have a very happy and yummy Thanksgiving”.
