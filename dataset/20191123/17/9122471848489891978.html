Video: Chase Young Ties Ohio State Sack Record In Return vs. Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 17:35 by Andrew Holleran

Chase Young is back for Ohio State today after serving a two-game suspension for taking an improper loan at the end of the 2018 season. This is not good news for Penn State.

The best defensive player in college football is already making a big impact. Young pressured Penn State quarterback Sean Clifford on the Nittany Lions’ first drive. On PSU’s second drive of the game, he made history.

Young has tied Ohio State’s single-season sack record of 14 with a sack in the first quarter.

The future top NFL Draft pick has tied Vernon Gholston’s record. He’ll probably break it later in the game.
