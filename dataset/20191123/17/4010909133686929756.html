iPhone 12 Pro could come with up to 6GB of RAM next year

23 Nov 2019, 17:54

Based on a statement by Barclays analyst Blayne Curtis, next year’s iPhone 12 Pro as well as the Pro Max variant will most likely come with 6GB of RAM. This is quite an increase, compared to the iPhone 11 Pro and Pro Max which only comes with 4GB of RAM. Not only that, but it the iPhone 12 Pro variants might have a rear-facing 3D sensor, 5G connectivity with support for mmWave and more. On a side note, it seems that the iPhone SE 2 might start production on February 2020 as well.

With all that said, do take every bit of information with a grain of salt, due to the fact that Apple has yet to give an official statement in regards to the smartphones mentioned.
