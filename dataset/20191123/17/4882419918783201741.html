Valencia suffers defeat against Real Betis with a 2-1 at Benito Villamarin

After this win, Rubi's squad has 16 points from 14 games

23 Nov 2019, 17:14 by SPORT.es

Betis went pass Valencia on a hard-earned victory of 2-1 on Saturday at Benito Villamarin. Real Betis wanted to improve their position in the tournament after losing against Sevilla 1-2 while Valencia won in their last two matches of the competition against Granada at home stadium, and Espanyol away, by 2-0 and 1-2 respectively. Following today's result, Real Betis is 14th in the LaLiga table with 16 points from 14 matches, while Valencia sits 10th with 20 points after 14 matches.

An impressive opening from Valencia, thanks to a goal from Maxi Gómez in the 32nd minute. However, Real Betis in the 37th minute gave an equalizer thanks to a goal from Joaquín, which saw the first half to a 1-1.

Real Betis took the lead in the second half, with a goal from Sergio Canales just before the end of the second half, and the game ended 2-1.

At the end of the match, both teams made 3 substitutions each. For Real Betis Borja Iglesias, Barragán and Alfonso Pedraza came on for Edgar González, Sidnei and Loren Morón, while Valencia brought on Kevin Gameiro, Carlos Soler and Kang-in Lee to replace Manu Vallejo, Rodrigo Moreno and Ferrán Torres.

The referee booked Edgar González and Álex Moreno from Betis, while for Valencia Daniel Wass and José Luis Gayá saw a yellow card as well.

Real Betis with their next fixture against Mallorca at away stadium, while Valencia will face Villarreal at home stadium.
