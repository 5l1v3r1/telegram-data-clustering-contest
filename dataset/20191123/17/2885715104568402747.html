India stamp authority on Day 2 in Kolkata, Bangladesh produce late fightback

Bangladesh survived a major scare owing to Mushfiqur Rahim’s valiant 59-run knock against India on Day 2 of the second Test at Eden Gardens in Kolkata, as the visitors were in threat of being bundled up on the second day itself. The Tigers finished the day on 152/6, still training the hosts by 89 runs.
