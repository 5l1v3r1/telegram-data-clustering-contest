Ayushmann Khurrana's latest post with wifey Tahira Kashyap is all about love!

23 Nov 2019, 17:43 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushmann Khurrana - Tahira Kashyap is one of the most adorable couples of Bollywood. The actor often shares pictures with his wifey on his social media accounts. Ayushmann wore a black loose t-shirt with a striped white jacket over it and matching pants while Tahira opted for an all-black look. And once again, his short caption stole our hearts. He wrote, "With her" with a heart emoji.
