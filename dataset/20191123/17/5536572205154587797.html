Russell Westbrook Calls Patrick Beverley “Trash” After Both Players Foul Out (VIDEO)

23 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Darrelle Lincoln

What a great last night in Los Angeles as the Clippers and Rockets did battle with each other for the second time during the season.

During the 4th quarter, foul trouble would plague both teams as Patrick Beverley saw his night end with six fouls and eventually Russell Westbrook would catch his sixth foul.

Both players have a long history with disliking each other and it kept up when Pat Bev mocked Westbrook and his shot while Russ called him ‘trash’ several times.

The Clippers would go on to win the game 122-119.
