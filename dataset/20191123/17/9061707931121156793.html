'Fortress' - Derby County fans are all saying the same thing after Preston win

Derby County 1-0 Preston North End | A Martyn Waghorn goal secured all three points for the Rams at Pride Park Stadium

23 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Mark Wakefield

Derby County got back to winning ways on Saturday as they claimed a 1-0 win over Preston North End in the Sky Bet Championship.

A first half goal from substitute Martyn Waghorn was enough for the Rams to secure all three points at Pride Park Stadium.

The victory means that Derby have now won their last five league matches at home, and Phillip Cocu’s side have now moved up to 11th place in the Championship table.

After the final whistle, Rams fans took to social media to express their thoughts and emotions on the result and their side’s performance.

Here is a selection of what they had to say.
