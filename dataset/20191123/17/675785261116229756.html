Arsenal player ratings v Southampton as the Gunners' winless run continues

The Frenchman's 96th minute strike rescued a point for Arsenal but it is now six games since the Gunners have recorded a win in the Premier League

23 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Alex Milne

Alexandre Lacazette scored at the death to earn Arsenal a point against Southampton but they were still booed off by a frustrated Emirates crowd.

Ryan Bertrand's quick free-kick caught out the Gunners defence on eight minutes and Danny Ings fired Southampton in front.

Lacazette levelled in the 18th minute, though, after strike partner Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang's shot was blocked by Bertrand.

Aubameyang and Nicolas Pepe had good chances at the start of the second half for Arsenal but were wasteful, and James Ward-Prowse made them pay with a 71st minute strike after his penalty was saved.

But Lacazette had the final say when he met Gabriel Martinelli's deep cross.
