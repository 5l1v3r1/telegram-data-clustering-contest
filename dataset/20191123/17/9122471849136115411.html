Urban Meyer Reveals His Score Prediction For Ohio State-Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, the biggest game of the college football weekend was set to kick off from Columbus. No. 2 Ohio State hosts No. 8 Penn state in a match-up of College Football Playoff contenders.

Just a few hours before they were scheduled to kick off, Ohio State and Penn State players decided to get the party started a little early. Over an hour before kickoff, players from both teams met a mid-field to share their distaste for one another.

Former Ohio State head coach Urban Meyer issued an ominous message to Penn State’s players before the game. “Be careful what you wish for,” he said.

Meyer later revealed his score prediction for the game. He thinks Ohio State is going to blow the doors off of Penn State this afternoon.

He likes Ohio State to win 42-20.
