Fairway felines: Bobcat kittens steal golf ball off Florida course

23 Nov 2019, 17:50 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

A pair of young bobcats decided to disrupt a golfer's game Monday morning at a Florida course.

The kittens batted around a golf ball on the fairway at Bonita Fairways Golf Course in Bonita Springs before taking off with the sphere, the Naples Daily News reported.
