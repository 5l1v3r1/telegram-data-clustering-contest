Miley Cyrus birthday: Singer’s most memorable fashion moments, in pictures

Fashionable star celebrates her 27th birthday on 23 November

23 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Sabrina Barr

Since achieving stardom on the Disney channel as Hannah Montana, to forging a trailblazing music career, Miley Cyrus’ sense of style has continually developed over the years.

The musician has become known for making daring, unforgettable sartorial choices, from the silver, strappy, custom Versace ensemble she wore to the 2015 MTV Video Music Awards to the netted, punk-inspired Marc Jacobs gown she showcased at the 2013 Met Gala.

Earlier this year, prior to her split from actor Liam Hemsworth, Cyrus represented her ex at the Isn’t It Romantic? premiere wearing an ethereal, ruffled Valentino gown.

In celebration of the star’s 27th birthday on Saturday 23 November, we have taken a look back at some of her most memorable fashion moments.
