India vs Bangladesh, Day/Night Test: Virat Kohli, Ishant Sharma put rampant India on verge of series sweep

23 Nov 2019, 15:36 by Reuters

Highlights

  • Indian quick bowlers reduced Bangladesh to 152/6 in second innings as the hosts closed on a 2-0 series sweep on day two
  • Ishant, who had taken 5-22 in the first innings, wrecked the Bangladesh top order to return figures of 4-39
  • Earlier, Kohli smashed a magnificent 136 and added 99 runs with Ajinkya Rahane who fell for a fluent 51

KOLKATA: India captain Virat Kohli embellished the pink-ball Test with a sparkling century and his quick bowlers reduced Bangladesh to 152/6 as the hosts closed on a 2-0 series sweep on day two of the second and final Test on Saturday.

SCORECARD

After India declared their first innings on 347/9 in their maiden day-night Test, Bangladesh risked losing the match inside two days following a spectacular top-order collapse.
