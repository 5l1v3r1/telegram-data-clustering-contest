Wolves dealt major blow ahead of Sheffield United clash

Latest Wolverhampton Wanderers news | Nuno's side returned to Premier League action on Saturday against Bournemouth at the Vitality Stadium

23 Nov 2019, 15:12 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Romain Saiss will miss Wolverhampton Wanderers ' clash with Sheffield United after picking up his fifth booking of the season.

The Moroccan international came into the game with Bournemouth having already picked up four cautions and referee Simon Hooper showed him a yellow only minutes into the game.

Saiss has stepped in at centre back since the long term injury to Willy Boly, but Wolves boss Nuno will have to shuffle his deck when they meet the Blades next week.
