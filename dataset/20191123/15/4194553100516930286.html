(Video) ‘If that was Salah or Mane…’ Some Reds claim Harry Kane’s ludicrous Spurs dive proves media hypocrisy

23 Nov 2019, 15:16 by Matt Addison

Harry Kane dived as Jose Mourinho got his first win as the new Tottenham boss this afternoon, as Spurs beat West Ham United 2-3 at the London Stadium.

The England forward has a history of throwing himself to the ground easily, though many Reds supporters were quick to point out that had it been one of Sadio Mane or Mo Salah, the reaction from the media and the wider public might have been a little different.

The clip below shows an example of Kane jumping up in the air to exaggerate the contact, courtesy of BT Sport’s footage.
