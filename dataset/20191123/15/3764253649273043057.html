Leganes vs Barcelona: Arturo Vidal's goal gives Catalans 2-1 win

23 Nov 2019, 15:56

- Leganes vs Barcelona's Spanish La Liga match ended 2-1 in favor of the Catalans

- Luis Suarez and Arturo Vidal scored the two goals for the Spanish champions

- Barcelona are still topping the Spanish La Liga standings

Reigning Spanish champions Barcelona on Saturday, November 23, came back from a goal down to beat Leganes 2-1 in what was a tough La Liga encounter for the Catalans.

Leganes who are at the bottom of the Spanish La Liga started the match with the determination of getting the maximum points against Barcelona.

And they had the chance to score the first goal in the 3rd minute when Oscar set up Ruben Perez, but he was unable to beat Barcelona goalie Marc Stegen.

Morocco international Youssef En-Nesyri scored the goal for Leganes in the 12th minute after an incredible assist from Roque Mesa.
