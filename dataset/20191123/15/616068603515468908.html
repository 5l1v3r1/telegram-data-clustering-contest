Handbags for Hope returns

23 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Chelsea Powrie

The annual Handbags for Hope project is back in Keremeos, looking for donations from the community to help women in need. 

Organized by the ladies of the Keremeos Royal Purple, the campaign is looking for new or gently used large purses and/or toiletries, all of which will be donated to needy women in Keremeos, Ollala, Cawston and Hedley. 

Last year, they packed 43 handbags full of items for distribution. 

Donations can be dropped off at the Valley First Credit Union in Keremeos until Nov. 29.
