Seaside, Florida's Cottage Rental Agency Offering Black Friday and Cyber Monday Deals on Florida Beach Vacation Rentals

23 Nov 2019, 15:00

SEASIDE, Fla., Nov. 23, 2019 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- Cottage Rental Agency (http://www.cottagerentalagency.com), the longest established property management company in Seaside, Fla., will offer special promotions for beach rentals booked on Black Friday, November 29 for 25 percent off the full stay booked and Cyber Monday, Dec. 2 for 30 percent off visits during the remainder of 2019 and 2020.

This Gulf Coast region of Northwest Florida, often referred to as the Emerald Coast, is internationally recognized as a luxury vacation destination featuring sugar-white sand beaches and sparkling gulf waters, award-winning restaurants, family-focused leisure activities and luxury accommodations.

"As families and friends come together to catch up this holiday season, it's a great time to start planning beach vacations," said Robert Kallmeyer, general manager for Cottage Rental Agency. "We're pulling out all the stops to offer our guests exclusive deals to book holiday travel, a quick winter getaway or get a jump start on those 2020 beach trips."

Each year, CRA offers tens of thousands of Northwest Florida visitors the largest selection of Seaside vacation rentals. The agency is the most tenured vacation rental management company with over 33 years of experience and boasting the largest inventory in Seaside. Each rental is individually owned, reflecting an upscale but diverse selection of architecture and decor. Rental sizes vary from quaint and eclectic beachfront honeymoon cottages to spacious, fully appointed homes that sleep up to 20 guests.

Rentals are located throughout Seaside in areas that range from cozy wooded alcoves to breezy beachside dunes to the charming but sophisticated town center, giving guests even more accommodation options for their vacation experience. Seaside is an idyllic beach vacation town, located on scenic Highway 30A, nestled between Destin and Panama City along the beaches of South Walton.

Those wishing to book any of the discounts should visit http://www.cottagerentalagency.com, or speak to one of our professional vacation planners by calling (844) 825-4809. The Cyber Monday offer will only be redeemable through online bookings using the promo code CYBER for 24 hours on Monday, Dec. 2. Black Friday's deal on Friday, Nov. 29 will be available through the CRA website and by phone with the promo code BLK19.

The Cottage Rental Agency Black Friday/Cyber Monday promotions are available for participating cottages only and applies to stays booked in the 2019 and 2020 calendar year. Limited date and holiday restrictions apply.
