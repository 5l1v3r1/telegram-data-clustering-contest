Dan Osborne treats kids to festive day as Jacqueline Jossa takes on I'm A Celeb

23 Nov 2019, 15:00

Dan Osborne is making the most of his time without wife Jacqueline Jossa while she takes part in I’m A Celebrity, as he took his children to the launch of the Christmas Grotto at Shrek’s Adventure in London.

The 28-year-old wrapped up warm for the weather in a tracksuit ensemble, as he was seen hand in hand with their little ones, Ella, five, and Mia, 17 months, and six-year-old Teddy, who he shares with ex Megan Tomlin.

And the family seemed to be having the best day together as they prepared for the festive season, posing alongside Santa Claus, while Dan even wore some Shrek ears.

We are also totally not jealous of Ella’s amazing rainbow face paint.

Dan recently revealed he has plans to fly out to Australia to support his wife during her stint on the ITV reality series.

‘I’ll be going out there though, not to go into the jungle but to support Jac,’ he told The Sun.

And when asked about the prospect of facing a possible bushtucker trial, he replied: ‘Yeah, yeah, but I’d do it. I guess it depends on what it is.’

Jacqueline also previously revealed that she did not tell her children that she was going to be entering the jungle, and it was going to be ‘really hard’ leaving them behind.

‘This is definitely the longest I have ever been away from them and that is going to be really hard,’ she admitted.

‘I didn’t know how to face even telling them. In the end, I thought the best idea was to say, “Mummy is going away for work”, and I would see them soon.

‘My family is so close-knit and I will miss them all terribly.’

The former EastEnders star also said that one of the main reasons for signing up for the show was to dispel a misconception about her relationship with Dan – and she wants us all to know that she is not a ‘pushover’, as some may think.

‘I think people think I am a pushover or a doormat,’ she continued.

‘I also think some people pity me. I want to show I am fine, I am up for a laugh and that I will for it and try my best.’
