Special Weather Statement issued November 23 at 3:08PM EST by NWS State College PA

23 Nov 2019, 15:08

Special Weather Statement: Sullivan; Northern Lycoming; Tioga until 8:00 PM

Areas of freezing rain may bring a light glaze of ice across the
highest elevations tonight.

The icy mix will likely transition to a period of snow late
tonight into the predawn hours Sunday, with accumulations up to 3
inches possible.

Ice and snow accumulation will primarily be confined to the higher
terrain north of Interstate 80 and east of U.S. Route 15.

Be alert for slick spots on untreated roads and elevated surfaces.
