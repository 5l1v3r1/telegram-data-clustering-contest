Argos is selling half Christmas trees to keep naughty pets at bay

It comes in two different designs

23 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Entertainment Daily

Argos 'half' Christmas trees are an absolute must for anyone with a naughty cat or dog who loves nothing more than to play with your brand new festive tree as soon as you get it through the front door.

The high street chain's genius faux trees are on sale for less than £35, and you can choose from two designs.

The trees are 6ft but the branches only start at 93cm, which means baubles, tinsel and fairy lights will be well out of the reach of pets and curious toddlers.

The stand is included and the product weighs 6kg in total.
