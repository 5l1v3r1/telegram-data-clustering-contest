Suzanne Jackson names and shames Instagram account after receiving cruel message

The vile troll told her she "should be shot"

23 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Kendra Becker

Suzanne Jackson has named and shamed an Instagram account, after receiving a cruel message from them.

Last night, the beauty influencer received a private message which said: “You should be shot you pig advertising and selling makeup you pig.”

Suzanne then shared a screenshot of the message on her Instagram Story, revealing she received the DM from @daniel.kenna21 – which turned out to be a fake account.

The SOSU by SJ founder was flooded with supportive messages after sharing the troll’s nasty DM.

Taking to her Instagram Story today, Sue told her 276k followers: “Lots of people are messaging me about that message last night, but it really doesn’t bother me.”

“That’s just light compared to what you would receive some days.”

“Obviously sometimes comments can be really hurtful, but that one last night was just… I was like, ok, little piggy went to the market, oink oink,” she joked.

Suzanne added: “Yeah, whatevs, fake page so don’t really give a s***e.”
