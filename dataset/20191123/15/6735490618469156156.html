Dolly Parton Explains Her Recent Focus On Christian Music: ‘I’ve Just Felt Like God Was Calling Me’

23 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Brent Furdyk

Dolly Parton has been nominated for dozens of Grammys over the years, but her nomination for the 2020 awards is indicative of a recent shift in her music toward faith-based music.

Her collaboration with Australian duo For King & Country, “God Only Knows”, received a nomination for Best Contemporary Christian Performance, and she opened up to People about why she’s focusing on Christianity in her new music.

“I’ve done so many things,” Parton tells the magazine. “And I see that I am in a position to help. People look at me like someone they’ve always known, like a mother or sister. If I say something good, people might listen.”
