Star-Studded Barca Scrape Past Leganes

23 Nov 2019, 15:55 by SL International

LaLiga

Result: Leganes 1-2 FC Barcelona

Date: 23 November 2019

Venue: Estadio Municipal de Butarque

A star-studded FC Barcelona side produced a less than convincing display after rallying from behind to defeat struggling Leganes on Saturday afternoon.

Ernesto Valverde went for a very attacking line-up as Messi was joined in the attack by Luis Suarez, Antoine Griezmann and Ousmane Dembele, while there was a first start for Senegal international Moussa Wague.

However, it was the home side who got the game's first goal thanks to Youssef En-Nesyri, who collected a pass from Roque Mesa before rifling the ball into the top corner, leaving Marc-Andre ter Stegen with no chance.

The Catalan giants upped the tempo in search of an equaliser, but there met by Leganes shot-stopper Ivan Cuellar, who produced a couple superb saves to keep out Suarez on two occasions after the 25-minute mark.

Leganes, meanwhile, did not sit back though as they went in search of another goal, although En-Nesyri and Mesa went close in the final seven minutes of the first half.

Barca came out firing in the second half and were rewarded with the equaliser when Messi's free-kick found Suarez, who nodded past Cuellar from six-yards out.

With the visitors chasing the game, Ernesto Valverde surprisingly took of Griezmann for Arturo Vidal, while Sergio Busquets made way for Ivan Rakitic with 30 minutes remaining in the contest.

Leganes managed to carve out a couple openings against the run of play, with Silva going closest with a header that sailed inches wide of the upright while Perez produced a vital block at the other end to deny Rakitic on 71 minutes.

The comeback was completed with 10 minutes remaining in the second half when Vidal found the back of the net with a neat finish from close-range, with the Video Assistant Referee approving the goal.

Barcelona will hope that Real Madrid fail to beat Real Sociedad by three goals if they want to maintain top spot in LaLiga this evening.
