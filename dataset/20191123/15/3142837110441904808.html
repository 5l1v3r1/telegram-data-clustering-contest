Aggreko plc (LON:AGK) Receives Average Recommendation of “Hold” from Brokerages

23 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Teresa Graham

Shares of Aggreko plc (LON:AGK) have been given a consensus rating of “Hold” by the eight research firms that are currently covering the company, MarketBeat reports. Four analysts have rated the stock with a sell rating, one has given a hold rating and three have given a buy rating to the company. The average 12 month price objective among brokerages that have covered the stock in the last year is GBX 816.67 ($10.67).

A number of brokerages have recently issued reports on AGK. UBS Group restated a “sell” rating on shares of Aggreko in a research report on Monday, July 29th. Barclays raised their price objective on Aggreko from GBX 760 ($9.93) to GBX 820 ($10.71) and gave the stock an “equal weight” rating in a research report on Tuesday, August 6th. Royal Bank of Canada restated an “outperform” rating on shares of Aggreko in a research report on Wednesday, November 13th. Peel Hunt restated a “reduce” rating and set a GBX 700 ($9.15) price objective on shares of Aggreko in a research report on Friday. Finally, Credit Suisse Group upgraded Aggreko to an “outperform” rating and raised their price objective for the stock from GBX 750 ($9.80) to GBX 905 ($11.83) in a research report on Tuesday, November 5th.

Aggreko stock traded up GBX 8.60 ($0.11) during trading hours on Monday, hitting GBX 800.40 ($10.46). The stock had a trading volume of 353,435 shares, compared to its average volume of 1,200,000. The company has a quick ratio of 1.39, a current ratio of 1.94 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 62.68. The stock has a market cap of $2.05 billion and a price-to-earnings ratio of 16.57. Aggreko has a one year low of GBX 690.40 ($9.02) and a one year high of GBX 868.60 ($11.35). The business’s 50 day simple moving average is GBX 795.49 and its 200-day simple moving average is GBX 801.96.

About Aggreko

Aggreko Plc supplies modular, mobile power, heating, cooling, and related services worldwide. The company operates in three segments: Rental Solutions, Power Solutions Industrial, and Power Solutions Utility. It offers power generation products, including diesel generators, G3+ generators, and gas generators, as well as Solar-diesel hybrid power plants; cooling systems, such as cooling towers, chillers, heat exchangers, and air handlers and conditioners; and desiccant and refrigerated dehumidifiers to prevent metal corrosion, aid processes, preserve equipment on cold stacked offshore rigs, reduce mold and mildew growth during production processes, and dry out water-damaged buildings, as well as to reduce drying times for concrete, insulation, and fireproofing.
