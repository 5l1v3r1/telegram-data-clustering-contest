Wall destroyed in Plymouth car crash

A passer by filmed the wreckage

23 Nov 2019, 15:24 by Paul Greaves

A car was wrecked after smashing into a garden wall in Plymouth.

It happened in Pomphlett Road at about 8pm on Friday.

The wreckage was filmed by a passing motorist shortly after police arrived at the scene.

The footage shows one police car at the scene. As the vehicle passes by a black car is clearly visible having collided with a stone wall at the front of a house.

The damaged car has come to rest at the entrance to the driveway, with large garden stones scattered underneath. It has clearly mounted the pavement before impact.

Police say they are unable to give any details of what happened or whether anyone was injured.

The weekend has seen a number of police call-outs for police, many caused by the heavy rain.

Weather warnings are in place for the next five days and there have been several crashes on the major roads of Devon and Cornwall.
