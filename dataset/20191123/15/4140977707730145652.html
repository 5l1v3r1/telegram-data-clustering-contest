RM Vs RS Dream11 Team And Match Report: Tricky test awaits Real Madrid today as they look to keep snapping a Barcelona’s heels.

With both Barcelona and Real Madrid being in topsy-turvy form in the ongoing edition of La Liga with inconsistency riddling both the sides, this season of the league looks to be wide open one. What it also does is make each encounter extremely pivotal for both the sides with the slightest of slip ups proving to be calamitous given the extremely close contested title fight both find themselves embroiled in at the moment.

Level on 25 points after 13 gameweeks, Real Madrid ended up capitalising on Barcelona’s defeat to Levante with a 4-0 routing of Eibar to go into the break just short of Barcelona by the barest of margins with only goal difference separating the two outfits. Although the side have won only three of their previous five encounters, what works in their favour is the fact that they have managed to squirm their way to four draws, managing to scrape and scram the bare minimum from those matches.

When they go out in pursuit of top spot today, Real Madrid know that things will be far from easy as they strive to register their second win on the bounce. They are up against a ruthless, exceptional and relentless attacking unit in the form of Real Sociedad, a side which will come hot out of the blocks in this one.

Currently in fifth place, Real Sociedad missed a pristine chance to move level on points with Real Madrid the last time around as they drew 1-1 with Leganes in a performance which the side haven’t been associated with in this edition of the league. They’ll be looking to overturn it come today and try to obliterated a vulnerable Real Madrid side which has fallen down infront of succulent attacking displays.
