Guedes could be sidelined until 2020

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Goncalo Guedes' injury looks a long term one and that is bad news for Valencia. He has not played for 'Los Che' since October and according to 'AS', he may not return until 2020.

There is no time frame on his recovery and Valencia are not too optimistic about him. Guedes' injury situation really concerns Valencia. 

Guedes' injury problems have made him miss more than a month of the season. According to 'AS'. he could be out for up to three months.

At Valencia they prefer to put a time frame on him. Celades, the Valencia coach, spoke about Guedes' injury. "He still needs a few weeks. I got his return date wrong once and I will not say again when he will return. His return is not going to be imminent," he said in a press conference. 

Valencia's aim, 'AS' says, is for Guedes to come back into things gradually and reappear on 8th January against Real Madrid in the semi-final of the Spanish Super Cup.
