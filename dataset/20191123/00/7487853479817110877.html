Twitter Reacts as Sloppy Barça Scrape to Victory Over Basement-Dwelling Leganes

23 Nov 2019 by Ed Alexander

​Barcelona may have come from a goal down to beat Leganés 2-1 on Saturday, but manager Ernesto Valverde seems to be on the brink of losing his job.

Luis Suarez and Arturo Vidal got the goals as the visitors maintained their status as La Liga's number one side. However, the fans did not hold back in their merciless criticism of the coach.
