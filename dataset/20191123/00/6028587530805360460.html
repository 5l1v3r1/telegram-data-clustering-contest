13 Good AirPod Deals Available This Black Friday 2019

23 Nov 2019 by Jay McGregor

Apple’s AirPods will undoubtedly be some of the most sought-after purchases this Black Friday, but which retailer has the best deals?Most of the deals are fairly similar, so if you have a preference for a particular store - perhaps because you have a gift card or loyalty card discount - then shop there. But Walmart appears to have the cheapest pair of second generation AirPods I’ve found so far (without the wireless charging case). 

If you’re in the market for a new smartphone, however, then you could potentially pick up a pair of AirPods for free with one of the gift card deals Target and Walmart are offering - with some money to spare for extra tech.  

Here’s a run-down of the best second generation AirPod deals and additional buying options. 

Gift cards

Let’s start with the big retailers. Target is selling AirPods for $144.99 ($15 off) and the wireless charging case AirPods for $169.99 ($30 off). 

If you’re in the market for a new phone, then you can pick up a free pair of AirPods with the gift card you’ll receive from buying the following phones. The iPhone 11 Pro Max, iPhone 11 Pro and iPhone 11 come with a free $200 gift alongside a contract with verizon or or AT&T. Beware, this likely means tying yourself to a lengthy contract deal. 

Google’s Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL come with a slightly bigger $300 gift card. But there’s an even bigger gift card deal on Samsung’s latest flagship line. The Note 10+, S10 and S10+ come with a $400 gift card, whilst the S10e comes with a $200 gift card. All come with a contract plan. 

Walmart has an even better AirPods deal at $139.99 with the wired charging case. There will be a further ($10 off) on Black Friday bringing them down to $129. For the wireless charging case bundle they’re down to $164.99 from $199.99. 

Walmart is also offering a $300 gift card that comes with every iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro and 1Phone 11 Pro Max purchase (if you sign up to a contract). 

The rest

Best Buy is selling the AirPods for $139.99 ($20 off) and $164.99 for Airpods 2 with the wireless charging case ($35 off).

There’s $34 off the latest AirPods at Amazon with with the wireless charging case and $20 for the same pair with a standard case. Act fast these Amazon deals sell out quickly, but they may also reappear at another date. 

Staples is selling AirPods for $139 ($20 off) - with the wireless charging case they’re $169 ($30 off).

Fred Myer is slightly more expensive, without the wireless charging case AirPods are selling for 149.99 ($10 off), with the wireless charging case they’re $179.00 ($20 off).

Finally Costco matches Walmart’s deal for the wireless charging case AirPods, which have been discounted to $164.99 ($15 off).
