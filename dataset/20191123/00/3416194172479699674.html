Shawn Mendes Will Perform at AMAs 2019 with Camila Cabello, Flies Back to L.A. for Show!

23 Nov 2019, 00:28

Shawn Mendes will be performing during the 2019 American Music Awards this weekend with his girlfriend Camila Cabello!

The couple will be performing their smash hit single “Senorita” during the awards show and he just flew back in time to be there. Shawn was spotted catching a flight out of Toronto, Canada on Friday (November 22).

Shawn is also up for two awards at the show this year. “Senorita” is nominated for Collaboration of the Year and he is also up for Favorite Social Artist.
