Man curses fraudsters with eggs for robbing him of his phone, money

23 Nov 2019

Fear gripped passers by who watched as a man who was duped of his money and mobile phone by fraudsters cursed them with eggs for luring him to give away his pocket money and mobile phone.

A video circulating on social media suggests the incident happened in Kumasi in the Ashanti region.

To depict the pain caused to him, the male victim who is yet to be identified called on river gods to kill the scammers together with their households.

The man was attacked by some passerby who observed him as he cursed the fraudsters with some hurling stones at him.

Reports say an eyewitness even went to the extent of giving the man a slap.
