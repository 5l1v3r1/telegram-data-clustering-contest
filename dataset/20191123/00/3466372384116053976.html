It's Ajit's decision, not of NCP: Sharad Pawar

23 Nov 2019

IMAGE: NCP chief Sharad Pawar leaves after a meeting with Shiv Sena and Congress leaders at Nehru Centre in Mumbai on Friday. Photograph: PTI Photo

Nationalist Congress Party chief Sharad Pawar on Saturday said Ajit Pawar's decision to support the Bhartiya Janata Party was his own and not that of the party.

"Ajit Pawar's decision to support the BJP to form the Maharashtra Government is his personal decision and not that of the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP). We place on record that we do not support or endorse this decision of his," Pawar tweeted.
