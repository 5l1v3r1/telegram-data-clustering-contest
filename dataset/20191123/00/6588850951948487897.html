Strip Club Invites Kanye West To Come Host His Sunday Service At Their Venue

23 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

This past Sunday (Nov. 17), Kanye West touched down in Houston to join famed pastor and televangelist Joel Osteen at his Lakewood Church for his latest Sunday Service experience. The Jesus Is King rapper joined Osteen in the pulpit, where he spoke on re-dedicating his life to Christ.
As Ye was in the pulpit espousing how his Christian-led reformation has changed his way of thinking, he controversially said that he considers strip clubs to be akin to a form of sex trafficking.
