Set-Piece Supremacy Leads Liverpool to Historic Premier League Start After Palace Win

23 Nov 2019 by Adam Aladay

​A sub-par Liverpool edged past Crystal Palace 2-1 in the Premier League on Saturday, with Roberto Firmino scoring a late winner.

Reds forward Sadio Mane made it 1-0 early in the second half, but Wilfried Zaha levelled things up with eight minutes remaining. Jurgen Klopp's men pushed for a winner and found it only three minutes later, as Firmino poked home following a corner to ensure his side left with all three points.
