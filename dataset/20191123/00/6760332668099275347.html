Manchu Vishnu's grey character in 'Mosagallu' - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

23 Nov 2019

Manchu Vishnu plays a grey character in 'Mosagallu', a Telugu-English crime thriller running against the backdrop of an IT scam.  The film's title and first look are out.  

"Vishnu has got an author-backed role in this intense thriller," a source says.  This will hopefully help the actor make a bounceback in movies.  "Am I good or Am I Bad? You be the judge," says Vishnu, referring to his character.  

This one also stars Kajal Aggarwal, Navdeep, Naveen Chandra, and Suneil Shetty.  

Directed by Jeffrey Gee Chin, the film is scheduled to hit the screens in April or May 2020.
