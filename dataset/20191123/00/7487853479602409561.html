Twitter Reacts as VAR Takes Centre Stage...Again & Liverpool Leave it Late...Again

23 Nov 2019 by Adam Aladay

​Oh, Liverpool really did do it the hard way against Crystal Palace, but it is another three points.

Sadio Mane gave the away side the lead four minutes into the second half, but Palace drew level with eight minutes remaining through Wilfried Zaha. However, Jurgen Klopp's men retook the lead only three minutes later, as Roberto Firmino bundled home to give his side all three points.
