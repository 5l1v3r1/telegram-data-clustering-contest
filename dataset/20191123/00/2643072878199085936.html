Again, Fani-Kayode Urges VP Osinbajo To Resign

23 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

Former Minister of Aviation Femi Fani-Kayode has called on Vice President Yemi Osinbajo to resign from his position as the nation’s number two citizen.

The chieftain of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) was reacting to Osinbajo allegedly flagging off the Use The Toilet campaign.

The campaign is aimed at putting a stop to open defecation across the country.

This follows President Muhammadu Buhari’s signing of the Executive Order 009 entitled, ‘The Open Defecation-Free Nigeria by 2025 and Other Related Matters Order’ into law.

Reacting in a post on Twitter, Fani-Kayode said Oisnbajo will soon be used to “wash toilets.”

Fani-Kayode wrote: “VP Yemi Osinbajo launches and flags off the Use The Toilet Campaign, a campaign to stop open defecation in Nigeria while Buhari is presiding over a security council meeting!

“A few days ago I wrote: “If Osinbajo doesn’t resign they will send him to wash toilets”. Almost there!”

Recall that Fani-Kayode had warned Yemi Osinbajo to resign before he is fully disgraced.

Fani-Kayode noted that the Vice President had already seen his roles reduced to the barest minimum since the second term of the Buhari administration came on stream.

He, therefore, warned that Osinbajo might be disgraced and ‘thrown out’ of the Presidential Villa anytime soon should he fail to take initiative and voluntarily resign from office.
