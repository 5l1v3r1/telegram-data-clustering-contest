Bigil actress takes up Nayanthara's role! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

23 Nov 2019

Malayalam actress Reba Monica John had made her debut in Tamil last year, with the movie Jarugandi co-starring Jai, and had recently got a big break in Kollywood with the Vijay starrer Bigil directed by Atlee. Reba Monica John played a football player and acid attack survivor Anitha in Bigil.

After her performance in Bigil that got her appreciation, Reba is acting in a number of movies in Tamil like Dhanusu Raasi Neyargalae with Harish Kalyan and the movie releases on December 6, and also has FIR Faisal Ibrahim Raiz which has Vishnu Vishal in lead role.

While she acted with Lady Superstar Nayanthara in Bigil, the actress has revealed in an interview that she is set to debut in Kannada, with the movie Sakalakala Vallabha, and the interesting fact about this movie is that it is the remake of the 2015 hit Naanum Rowdydhaan and she will play Nayanthara's role in the original.
