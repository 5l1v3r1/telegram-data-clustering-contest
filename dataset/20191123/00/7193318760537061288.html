'Baby Shark Live!' tour coming to OKC in 2020

23 Nov 2019, 00:34 by Brandy McDonnell

Pinkfong and Round Room Live have announced that "Baby Shark Live!," the new, fully immersive concert experience, will continue to make a splash across North America and play more than 70 cities in spring 2020.

The live tour of the viral sensation will continue its run starting March 1 in Independence, Missouri. The hit show will swim to new markets including Los Angeles, Seattle and Toronto, according to a news release.

Based on the kid-friendly viral video smash, the tour will include an April 17 stop at Oklahoma City’s Chesapeake Energy Arena, 100 W Reno.

Tickets for many upcoming tour dates, including Oklahoma City, are on sale starting today. For tickets and additional information, visit babysharklive.com.
