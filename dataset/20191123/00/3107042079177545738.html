Seven injured in Northland crash

23 Nov 2019

Seven people have been injured, four seriously, in a single-vehicle crash in Northland.

The crash occurred on State Highway 1 near the Piano Hill intersection south of Hikurangi, 13km north of Whāngārei, about 1.20pm.

"Initial indications are that a number of people have sustained injuries, with four reported to be in a serious condition and three in a moderate condition," police said.

The Serious Crash Unit was attending, a police spokeswoman said.

The highway was closed for a time.
