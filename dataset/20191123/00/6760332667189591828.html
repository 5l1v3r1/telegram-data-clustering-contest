Mysskin to do it again for Vishal? - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

23 Nov 2019

Director Mysskin has been known for directing interesting movies like Anjathe, Chithiram Pesuthadi, Yudham Sei, Onayum Aatukutiyum, Mugamoodi and Thuppparivalan, and has completed his next movie Psycho starring Udhayanidhi Stalin, Aditi Rao Hydari and Nithya Menen.

Now, Mysskin is directing his next movie Thupparivalan 2, the sequel to his 2017 hit thriller, and this movie is being shot in London. The movie stars debutant Ashya as the female lead and also stars Prasanna, Rahman, Gauthami and has music by Isaignani Ilaiyaraja.

The latest speculation making rounds is that, Mysskin who has acted in movies like Super Deluxe, Nandalala, Onayum Aatukuttiyum and Savarakathi, will be playing a role in Thupparivalan 2 as well.An official clarification on this speculation is expected soon.
