When a certain Messi dazzled Aguirre at just 15 years old

23 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Barcelona and Leganés face each other on Saturday in Butarque. Javier Aguirre will be in charge of the hosts, and back in 2002, he witnessed some of the first flashes of Messi as a Barca player.

Aguirre is a legend of La Liga and that's why he knows all about and has suffered the phenomenon of Leo Messi.

Aguirre has had to face the Argentine several times who he saw for the first time in 2002.

"I had just become Osasuna manager and we played in Seville. We were training at the opposition's training ground and I was told that Sevilla youth were playing against Barça", the manager told 'AS'.

This match left an unexpected mark on Aguirre who witnessed the Barcelona superstar being made first hand.

"I was told 'come here, Javier. There's a youngster that you're going to watch play'. I went and saw him for the first time. He was a rat!" the Mexican said in a kind tone as he recalled the past to the aforementioned source.

"He was a machine (Messi), with long hair. He took the ball and scored. Then again and goal. Almost 20 years later and he's the same or better," Aguirre said about a Messi who continues to beat records with inhuman voracity.
