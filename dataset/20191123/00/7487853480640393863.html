Leganés 1-2 Barcelona: Report, Ratings & Reaction as Sub-Par Visitors Secure Comeback Victory

23 Nov 2019 by Ed Alexander

Barcelona remain top of La Liga after coming from behind to defeat Leganés 2-1, despite a disjointed performance from La Blaugrana at Estadio Municipal de Butarque.

Youssef En-Nesyri broke the deadlock on 11 minutes when he whipped a devilish effort beyond a statuesque Marc-André ter Stegen, the German helplessly watching on as the forward cut inside onto his left foot and arced a mesmerising shot into the top corner.
