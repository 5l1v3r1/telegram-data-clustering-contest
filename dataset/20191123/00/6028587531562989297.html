Deontay Wilder Vs. Luis Ortiz Live Results

23 Nov 2019 by Josh Katzowitz

Deontay Wilder is ultra-confident he’s going to knock out Luis Ortiz again to regain his heavyweight title on Saturday night on Fox PPV. Ortiz feels sure he’s going to get his revenge from their first fight 20 months ago and score his own stoppage victory. The Wilder vs. Ortiz odds favor Wilder—as do the boxers who were making predictions—but as Tyson Fury said, “Don’t go to sleep on Luis Ortiz, because he’s a monster. He’s definitely a bad old man, that’s for sure.”

Ortiz would rather put Wilder to sleep, for sure. But Ortiz is also 40 years old, and oftentimes, when a boxer gets knocked out by an opponent, they get knocked out even quicker in the rematch.

Either way, it should be a fascinating bout, and Forbes will be here for the night, giving our round-by-round thoughts, explanations and unofficial scoring on the Fox card.
