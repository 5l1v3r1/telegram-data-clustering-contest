Crash closes Terrace Tunnel for an hour

23 Nov 2019

Wellington.Scoop
A crash in the Terrace Tunnel this afternoon closed the road in both directions and caused what the police described as significant delays.

The crash was reported at 1.30.

The Transport Agency said there were delays throughout the CBD.

The tunnel was reopened in both directions an hour later.

There were no serious injuries.
