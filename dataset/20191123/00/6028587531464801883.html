‘Destiny 2’ Is Still Down, But A Fix Is Incoming

23 Nov 2019 by Erik Kain

Destiny and Destiny 2 remain down Friday evening after an entire day of server issues and outages.The servers went down early this morning a little after 7 am ET and players have been reporting issues logging into the game ever since. Players across all platforms, from PC to Stadia to consoles have faced issues with the game Friday.

There is good news, however!

Bungie has identified the problem and taken the games offline completely in an effort to implement a fix. They have not shared what the problem is, however–it could be a DDOS attack or something else entirely.

Bungie says that it will slowly add players into queues in order to not overload the servers once the game is back online. They’re targeting 7 pm PT.
