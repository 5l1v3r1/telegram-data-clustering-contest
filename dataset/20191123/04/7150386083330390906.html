Rohit Sharma's Breathtaking Catch During Pink Test Has Become a Hit Meme | POSTS

Sharma's brilliant catch brought an end to Bangladesh captain Mominul Haque's short stay.

23 Nov 2019, 04:22

India opener Rohit Sharma took a breathtaking one-handed catch at second slip on day one of the ongoing Pink-Ball Test in Eden Gardens, Kolkata on Friday. Sharma’s catch brought an end to Bangladesh captain Mominul Haque’s short stay.

Umesh Yadav was the bowler, who bowled the ball at a good length to the left-hander and it tailed in slightly. Haque tried to defend it, but it found the outside edge. Rohit at second slip had to dive across to pull off the catch in front of the first slip where India captain Virat Kohli was present.
