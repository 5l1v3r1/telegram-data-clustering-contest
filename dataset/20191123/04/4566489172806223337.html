Clint Lowery of Sevendust talks new solo album, success and fans 

Clint Lowery of the acclaimed heavy metal band Sevendust chatted with Digital Journal about his upcoming breakthrough solo album, "God Bless the Renegades."

23 Nov 2019, 04:24 by Markos Papadatos

Lowery is known for his musical work as a co-founder, vocalist, and guitarist of the Grammy-nominated Sevendust.

God Bless the Renegades will be released on January 31, 2020, via the record label, Rise Records. This album was produced and co-written by Michael "Elvis" Baskette, who has worked extensively with Alter Bridge. "I had 30 or 40 ideas and frameworks for songs. I brought a lot of those to the table to 'Elvis,' my producer. We went through all of those songs and we tried to figure out which songs best represented the material that I wanted to put out as my first solo release," he said.

"It was very important to pick the right songs, and still connect with the Sevendust fan-base and at the same time, try to expand a little bit, and make new fans," he said.
