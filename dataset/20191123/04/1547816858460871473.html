Shabana Azmi's Mother Shaukat Kaifi Passes Away At 93: Tabu, Boney Kapoor, Urmilla Matondkar Pay Their Respects

23 Nov 2019, 04:25 by Saheli Maity

Veteran actor and Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi breathed her last on Friday evening due to age-related illness. To pay their last respects, Bollywood celebs like Tabu, Boney Kapoor, Urmilla Matondkar and Sayami Kher, were spotted outside Shabana’s residence

Bollywood is hearing some sad news back-to-back. Earlier this week, we heard Bollywood’s ace designer and filmmaker Karan Johar’s best friend, Manish Malhotra’s father passing away due to illness. Last night we learned that veteran actor and Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi breathed her last on Friday evening due to age-related illness. As the Akhtar family were mourning, they found support from the industry colleagues, who stood by them in this weakest phase. 

 

To pay their last respects, we saw quite a few Bollywood celebs arriving like Tabu, Boney Kapoor, Urmilla Matondkar, and Sayami Kher,ace designer Manish Malhotra, to name a few, who were spotted outside Shabana’s residence. According to the Times Of India report, she was admitted to Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani hospital, for certain days and was in the ICU. The reports further said that they had brought her home late last night. The last rites will be held on Saturday noon.
