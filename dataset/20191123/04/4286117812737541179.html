Sanjay Raut should shut up, with Ajit Pawar's support we will prove majority with 170 MLAs: BJP

Maharashtra BJP president Chandrakant Patil said Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut should now keep silent; he has ruined the Shiv Sena.

23 Nov 2019, 04:56

Moments after BJP's Devendra Fadnavis took oath as Maharashtra's chief minister Saturday morning, the BJP came out all guns blazing against its former ally - Shiv Sena - and said the party should now keep quiet.

Maharashtra BJP president Chandrakant Patil said Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut should "now at least keep silent". "He has ruined the Shiv Sena," Patil said.

Sanjay Raut, a key Shiv Sena leader and Rajya Sabha MP, had been very vocal criticising the BJP in the past two weeks and took personal digs at Devendra Fadnavis, saying he is hungry for power.
