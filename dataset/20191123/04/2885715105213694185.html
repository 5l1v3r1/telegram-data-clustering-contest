Kartik Aaryan birthday bash: Kiara Advani, Ananya Panday, Janhvi Kapoor and others attend

Kartik Aaryan had a star-studded birthday bash in Mumbai which was attended by his Pati Patni Aur Woh co-stars Ananya Panday, Bhumi Pednekar and Dostana 2 co-stars Janhvi Kapoor and Lakshya among others.
