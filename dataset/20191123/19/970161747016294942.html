Does I'm A Celebrity: Extra Camp's Adam Thomas have a girlfriend?

23 Nov 2019, 19:40

Former Emmerdale actor Adam Thomas is reaching the end of his first week as a co-host on I’m A Celebrity: Extra Camp.

Adam previously competed on I’m A Celebrity in 2016, and came third to winner Scarlett Moffatt and his Extra Camp co-host Joel Dommett.

While his professional life in soapland is well-known, let’s take a closer look at his private life ahead of his next appearance on our screens.

Does Adam Thomas have a girlfriend?

Adam Thomas does not have a girlfriend, but he does have a wife called Caroline whom he married in 2017.

The ex-Waterloo Road star has two children with Caroline called Teddy, born in 2014, and Elsie-Rose, born in 2017.
