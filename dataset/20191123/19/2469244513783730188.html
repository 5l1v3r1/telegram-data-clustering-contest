Injury ruled out Alex Fisher for win at Crawley, Matt Taylor explains

Taylor said that Fisher has picked up a groin strain and the club chose not to risk his fitness.

23 Nov 2019, 19:04 by Daniel Clark

Alex Fisher missed out of Exeter City’s trip to Crawley Town with a groin injury, boss Matt Taylor revealed after the match.

The ex-Yeovil Town man had grabbed the winner in midweek against Cambridge United, his second goal in three games, but eyebrows were raised when he was not part of the 18 for Saturday’s clash.

Taylor though said that Fisher has picked up a groin strain and the club chose not to risk his fitness.

Dean Moxey’s 77th minute winner was enough for the Grecians to pick up a 1-0 win and see them rise to 4th in the table.
