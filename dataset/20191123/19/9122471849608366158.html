Video: Huge Protest Breaks Out On Field Of Harvard-Yale Game

23 Nov 2019, 19:22 by Andrew Holleran

The second half of the Harvard-Yale game is currently being delayed. A massive student protest has broken out on the field at the Yale Bowl.

What appears to be hundreds of students are sitting at midfield, protesting climate change.

“This is an emergency,” one sign reads.

The game is under delay and based on the size of this protest, it’s unclear if it will resume anytime soon.
