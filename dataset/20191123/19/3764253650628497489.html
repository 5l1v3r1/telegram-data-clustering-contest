APC leaders give Buhari mandate to constitute national reconciliation committee

23 Nov 2019, 19:57

- President Buhari has been asked to constitute a national reconciliation committee of APC

- The decision was agreed upon by the party's NEC on Friday, November 22

- Crisis has been rocking the party since 2018 over the conduct of its primaries

Apparently determined to reconcile its aggrieved members, the National Executive Committee (NEC) of the All Progressives Congress (APC) has adopted the recommendation of the national caucus that President Muhammadu Buhari should constitute a national reconciliation committee.

Legit.ng reports that the party in a thread of tweets on its twitter page on Friday, November 22, said the standing committee would be empowered to address grievances of party members and resolve existing conflicts with a view to fostering unity and oneness within the party.

It was gathered that the decision was one of the highlights of the party's NEC meeting held on Friday, November 22, at the party's national secretariat, Abuja.
