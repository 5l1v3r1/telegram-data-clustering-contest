WWE Superstar Seth Rollins has predicted that Pep Guardiola's Manchester City will crush Frank Lampard's Chelsea 18-0 when the teams meet on Saturday in EPL

Written By Sreehari Menon | Mumbai | Updated On: November 23, 2019 19:29 IST

WWE superstar Seth Rollins has predicted an 18-0 win for Manchester City against Chelsea on Premier League Matchday 13 on Saturday. The two teams square off at the Etihad Stadium on November 23. Chelsea are 3rd in the Premier League table and lead Manchester City by a point, who are placed 4th.
