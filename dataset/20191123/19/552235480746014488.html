Celtic fan beats the bookies as he wins thousands of pounds with free bet

The Hoops diehard couldn't believe it when he checked his coupon at full time to see he had won an incredible sum of money.

23 Nov 2019, 19:50 by Mark McDougall, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/mark-mcdougall/

A Celtic fan cleaned out the bookies by claiming an incredible £4000 from a free bet.

Kieran Caw picked nine teams to win during the Saturday 3pm kick-offs and astonishingly all of them were winners.

It was a double celebration after watching his own team batter Livingston at Celtic Park.

Late goals were needed to seal the huge win with Rotherham even coming back from two goals down to win 3-2 in the final 20 minutes.

The incredible bet was made with McBookie and the Celtic season ticket holder admitted he didn't even realise the offer was in his account.

And now Kieran admits his daughter will have an even better Christmas than normal after his incredible win.

Kieran said: "I noticed I had a £20 free bet with McBookie this morning that I had no idea about.

"Decided to fire it on nine teams just to see what happened. I looked at half time and saw Rotherham were 2-0 down so thought it was done and didn't bother checking it again.

"In the end it was three or four late goals to win it and it was absolute madness.

"That will be Christmas sorted for my daughter Layla now and I'll pick up a couple of things for myself as well."

Kieran picked Fleetwood, Rotherham, Peterborough, Oxford, Forest Green Rovers, Swindon, Crewe, Leeds and West Brom as winners in his coupon.

Peterborough scored a 91st minute winner, Leeds needed an own goal in the 90th minute and West Brom scored with an 88th minute penalty to secure the incredible win.

McBookie were quick to respond to Kieran's initial tweet about the win, saying: "With two 90min winners with one of them being an OG. Send me the lottery numbers please."
