Strictly's Tess dazzles in gold glitzy jumpsuit while Claudia sparkles in black

The Strictly Come Dancing hosts wore glittering outfits to present week 10's live show on Saturday night

23 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Irishmirror.ie

Strictly Come Dancing has always loved a bit of glitz and glam, and presenters Tess and Claudia are no exception.

Tonight the presenting duo both stepped onto the ballroom in glitzy fashion ensembles, ahead of hosting week 10's live show.

BBC dancing competition host Tess Daly looked phenomenal as she sizzled onto the Strictly Ballroom in a shimmering champagne-coloured jumpsuit.

Her co-host Claudia Winkleman was also unmissable as she sparkled onto the 10's show of the series in a black one-shouldered gown with sequin embellishments.

Blonde bombshell Tess, 50, looked sensational as she shimmered onto the dancefloor in the glittering bardot-style, one-shouldered jumpsuit which featured a flattering tie-in waist detail.

Meanwhile, brunette beauty Claudia, 47, changed up her look from her classic and plain outfits to a glitzy dress.

Claudia sticked to her trademark all-black fashion but opted for a dazzling one-shouldered dress with statement shimmering fabric, a sleek design and a subtle asymmetrical hemline.

The mum-of-three added glamour to her Strictly look with dark eyeshadow, thick mascara and added a slick of nude gloss to her lips.

While Tess wore her glossy blonde locks in a straight style and accentuated her natural features with gold-tinted make-up and a glowing complexion.

Both presenters added height to their fashion ensembles with stiletto heels.

Despite presenting as a united front, both presenters have their own personal stylists to ensure their looks suit their individual personalities.
