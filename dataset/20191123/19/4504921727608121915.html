ACC searching for Shanghala, Hatuikulipis

23 Nov 2019, 19:17

The Anti Corruption Commission (ACC) Director General Paulus Noa has confirmed that warrants of arrest have been issued for the former Minister of Justice Sacky Shanghala, former Fishcor board chairman James Hatuikulipi and Tamson Hatuikulipi.
Noa says they are currently looking for the trio.
Former Fisheries Minister Bernhardt Esau and Ricardo Gustavo were arrested earlier on Saturday.
They have been accused of receiving kickbacks for fishing rights from Icelandic company Samherji.
