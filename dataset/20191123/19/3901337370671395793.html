Unbeaten Liverpool Net Late Winner At Palace

23 Nov 2019, 19:02 by SL International

English Premier League

Result: Crystal Palace 1-2 Liverpool

Date: 23 November 2019

Venue: Selhurst Park

Premier League leaders Liverpool maintained their unbeaten run this season following a 2-1 win over Crystal Palace on Saturday evening.

Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain got rewarded with a spot alongside Roberto Firmino and Sadio Mane up front in place of Mohamed Salah, who started on the bench.

Liverpool received the first scare when a Gary Cahill header deflected off the bar after looping over Alisson Becker following a free-kick from Luka Milivojevic.

Palace seemed to have gone ahead three minutes to halftime when James Tomkins nodded home from the far post, but the Video Assistant Referee (VAR) ruled out the strike following a foul in the build-up.

With no changes from either during halftime, the visitors were off to a threatening start with Mane in particular flashing attempts at the goal of Vicente Guiata.

Mane went and broke the deadlock four minutes later when he turned in the area to volley in off the left hand post despite the best efforts of Guaita.

Christian Benteke looked to haunt his former club in the final 12 minutes, but his acrobatic bicycle kick in the area flashed across the far post.

Palace were back on level term on 83 minutes when Wilfred Zaha collected a square ball from the left to slot beyond Alisson after hesitant defending from Dejan Lovren.

However, Liverpool regained control five minutes later when sloppy clearances off a corner invited Firmino to fire home from a close range to seal three points.
