Chase Young Heisman Trophy Hype Increasing vs. Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 19:59 by Alek Arend

A defensive player winning the Heisman Trophy? It’s a rare occurrence in college football. Michigan’s Charles Woodson is the last to do so, and that was over 20 years ago in 1997.

But due to Chase Young’s stupendous performance against Penn State today, there’s legitimate discussion regarding Young’s chance of winning the Heisman Trophy. The Ohio State defensive end is proving he’s not just the best player at his position, but the best player in college football, regardless of position.

Folks are starting to voice their support for Young to win the Heisman Trophy. Should he be the clear front-runner after his performance against the Nittany Lions?
