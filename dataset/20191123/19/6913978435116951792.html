HSBC Saudi Arabia voted ‘Best Asset Manager’ for 2019

23 Nov 2019, 19:53

RIYADH — HSBC Saudi Arabia has recently won the Global Investor MENA Award for "Best Fund Manager in Saudi Arabia". The award demonstrates HSBC's long-standing excellence in the financial markets and reaffirms its position as the leading Asset Manager in the region.

On this occasion, Waleed AlRashed AlHumaid, General Manager of Asset Management for HSBC Saudi Arabia, said: “We are proud to be granted this prestigious recognition on top of the many other awards we have received, and we would like to ensure our steadfast commitment in providing the best and most diversified investment products and services covering Asset Management.”

He confirmed HSBC Saudi Arabia’s assurance to continuously endeavor to maintain its position as a leading pioneer in the Investment sector across the Kingdom, through its team of dedicated experts with high levels of qualification. — SG
