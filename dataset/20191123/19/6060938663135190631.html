Photos: Sonakshi Sinha treats her fans with her saree avatars from 'Yu Karke' song from 'Dabangg 3'

23 Nov 2019, 19:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sonakshi Sinha will be next seen in Salman Khan starrer 'Dabangg 3' helmed by Prabhu Deva. The trailer received a lot of love from the fans of the franchise. Recently, the makers of the film dropped a new song 'Yu Karke' featuring Salman and Sonakshi.

In the song, the actress can be seen donning different coloured sarees and now, she is treating her fans with her looks from the song. She captioned the post, "The colors of #YuKarke! #hotpink @ashley_rebello". Her fans have been pouring in love for the actress in the comment section of her posts. The actress will be her reprising role as Rajjo in the film and her posts have us excited for the film.
