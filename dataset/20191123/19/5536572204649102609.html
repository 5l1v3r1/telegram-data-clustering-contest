Yale-Harvard Game In Delay Because of Students Protesting Climate Change On Field (VIDEO)

23 Nov 2019, 19:35 by Darrelle Lincoln

Mark this under things you never see. Usually when people decided to protest at sporting events, they bring signs and hang them from the stands, but that wasn’t the case on Saturday.

During the Yale-Harvard game, hundreds of students came out on the field and sat down as they protested climate change.
