Vaani Kapoor and Priyanka Chopra groove to the tunes of 'Ghungroo'; watch video

23 Nov 2019, 19:47 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Priyanka Chopra, Katrina Kaif, Kriti Sanon, Vaani Kapoor, Nushrat Bharucha and other celebs recently attended a Bollywood bash. Pictures and videos of the same are all over the internet.

Vaani Kapoor also took to her Instagram to share some glimpse from the mad night. In one of the videos shared by the actress, she can be seen dancing with Priyanka on the song 'Ghungroo' from her recently released film. For the night, Vaani opted for a satin brown ensemble and looked stunning it while PeeCee decided to go with an all-white outfit.

She captioned the post, "Ain't nobody like you PC!! @priyankachopra"
