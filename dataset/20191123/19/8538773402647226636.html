Man City earn win over Chelsea with De Bruyne, Mahrez goals

23 Nov 2019, 19:16 by ESPN

Manchester City fought back from a goal down to beat Chelsea 2-1 at the Etihad Stadium on Saturday.

Kevin De Bruyne, Riyad Mahrez and Raheem Sterling scored for the champions, who leapfrogged Frank Lampard's side into third place in the league table.

Chelsea were the best team in the opening stages of the match with Willian and Fikayo Tomori missing good chances.

The visitors took a deserved the lead just after 20 minutes through an N'Golo Kante goal after a fine assist from Mateo Kovacic.

De Bruyne equalised for City just eight minutes later with a deflected effort off Kurt Zouma.

Mahrez then put the hosts in the lead after 37 minutes after he skipped past Emerson and produced a curling strike into the far corner.

On 43 minutes, Sergio Aguero pounced on a Kepa Arrizabalaga mistake but hit the crossbar.

City dominated possession throughout the second half but failed to get a third after a number of missed chances, with VAR ruling out a Raheem Sterling goal in stoppage time.

City boss Pep Guardiola will be concerned that Rodri, David Silva and Aguero were all taken off due to injury.
