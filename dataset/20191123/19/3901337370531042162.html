Arsenal Booed Off, Despite Lacazette's Heroics

23 Nov 2019, 19:51 by SL International

Arsenal 2-2 Southampton

Arsenal were booed off by their own supporters following a 2-2 with Southampton at the Emirates Stadium on Saturday evening.

The Saints went ahead as early as the eighth minute through Danny Ings but Alexandre Lacazette restored parity 10 minutes later.

James Ward-Prowse seemingly secured the winner when he scored from the rebound in the 71st minute after his penalty was saved, but Lacazette struck in the sixth minute of time added on to rescue a point for Arsenal.

Brighton and Hove Albion 0-2 Leicester City

Leicester City cemented second spot in the Premier League with a 2-0 victory over Brighton at the The American Express Community Stadium.

Ayoze Perez opened the scoring on 64 minutes, before Jamie Vardy sealed the win from the penalty spot with eight minutes remaining.

Bournemouth 1-2 Wolverhampton Wanderers

Wolverhampton Wanderers claimed their second away win of the season after defeating Bournemouth 2-1 at Vitality Stadium.

Joao Moutinho and Raul Jimenez got the goals for the visitors, while the hosts had Simon Francis sent off before Sam Cook netted a consolation.

Everton 0-2 Norwich City

Norwich City piled more misery on Everton with a 2-0 victory at Goodison Park on Saturday evening.

After a scoreless first half, Todd Cantwell and Dennis Srbeny secured the vital goals for the visitors.

Watford 0-3 Burnley

It was a good outing in London for Burnley, who registered a 3-0 win over Watford at Vicarage Road.

Chris Wood got the scoring underway on 53 minutes before Ashley Barnes and James Tarkowski sealed the win with strikes in the final 10 minutes.
