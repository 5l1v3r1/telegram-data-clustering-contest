Munster snatch late draw in thrilling Racing encounter

23 Nov 2019, 19:42 by James Cox

Munster 21-21 Racing 92

Munster and Racing 92 played out a stunning draw at Thomond Park in the Champions Cup this evening.

A try from Andrew Conway late on gave Munster the chance to equalise but JJ Hanrahan still had to notch a conversion from the touchline.

It could have been even better as Hanrahan attempted a drop goal right at the death but it flew wide of the left post.

Finn Russell's try gave Racing the advantage before Teddy Thomas touched down after half an hour.

Munster struck back through Keith Earls just before half-time but Hanrahan missed the conversion leaving the score at 14-11 in favour of the visitors at the break.

A Hanrahan penalty in the 48th minute made it 14-14 before disaster struck for Munster when Juan Imhoff scored his side's third try.

Munster were 21-14 down going into the final quarter but they launched a brilliant comeback, culminating in Hanrahan's brilliant conversion.
