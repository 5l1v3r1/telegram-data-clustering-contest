Video: Ohio State Marching Band Trolls Michigan During Halftime Show

23 Nov 2019, 19:01 by Alek Arend

The Ohio State marching band is making the most of its final home game of the season. While the Buckeyes are currently engaged in a contest against No. 8 Penn State, the marching band took it upon itself to troll another team – the Michigan Wolverines.

Ohio State and Michigan will face off next week in Ann Arbor. Rivalry tensions are already high as the Buckeyes have so much to play for including national championship aspirations. The Wolverines would love nothing more than to spoil those championship hopes.

During the halftime show of the OSU-PSU contest, the Buckeyes’ marching band put together one of its best performances yet. The band arranged itself into scissors, and proceeded to “cut” a University of Michigan flag.
