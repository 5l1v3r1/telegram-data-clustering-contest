‘Conspiracy To Capture Financial Capital Through Backdoor’: Ravi Shankar Prasad On Failed Sena-Cong-NCP Bid For Govt

23 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Swarajya Staff

Addressing a press conference at the BJP headquarters in New Delhi today (23 November), veteran BJP leader and union minister Ravi Shankar Prasad spoke about the latest political developments in Maharashtra where his party today returned to power.

He firstly talked about how the moral, as well as electoral mandate in the state was for the BJP-Shiv Sena alliance under the leadership of Devendra Fadnavis. He also said that the BJP’s vote bank and the image of Fadnavis played a major role for the Shiv Sena candidates to perform as they did.
