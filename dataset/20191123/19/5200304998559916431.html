Alia Bhatt stunned by ‘Gangubai Kathiawadi’ sets

23 Nov 2019, 19:19 by Shyama Sudra

With her success growing faster than anyone had expected, Alia Bhatt is about to make her move with her first Sanjay Leela Bhansali film titled ‘Gangubai Kathiawadi’, and it seems she is already overwhelmed.

“Alia was passing by the set at Film City and was awestruck by the magnitude of the set. The movie set for Gangubai is not just larger than life but has a lot of intricate details despite the Herculean size. There is a lot of fine detailing that has gone into the making of the set,” Asian Age reports a source stating. 

Further news suggests that this set will be even larger than Bhansali’s previous films, where it’s been kept tightly under wraps.
