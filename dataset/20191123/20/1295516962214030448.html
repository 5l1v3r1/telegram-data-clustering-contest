Lupin III: The First's Opening Title Sequence Revealed

23 Nov 2019, 20:11 by Adreon Patterson

With the release date for the movie approaching, Toho Company released the opening sequence for the 3D animated film Lupin III: The First.

The film's opening draws directly from the original series' opening sequence. Lupin can be seen dodging a series of spotlights before running towards the screen with a cheeky smile. Other essential characters - Daisuke Jigen, Goemon Ishikawa XIII, Fujiko Mine and Inspector Koichi Zenigata - received their moments in the opening.
