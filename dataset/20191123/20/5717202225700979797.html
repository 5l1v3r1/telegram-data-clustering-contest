Everton fans are furious after Marco Silva’s post-game comments

Everton fans were not happy about Marco Silva blaming a lack of pace for the 2-0 defeat to Norwich.

23 Nov 2019, 20:15 by Mathew Nash

It is fair to say that Everton fans were not happy with manager Marco Silva after watching their side lose to Norwich City earlier today. 

The Toffees were poor throughout, losing 2-0 after goals from Todd Cantwell and Dennis Srebeny. It was the manner of the defeat that most frustrated Everton fans and the mood in Goodison Park after the game told the story. 

Everton lacked creativity, pace, mobility and that was not a surprise considering the starting XI. Cenk Tosun and Gylfi Sigurdsson are hardly going to be beating any opponents in a foot race. 

Indeed, Richarlison also looked leggy after his international antics whilst Morgan Schneiderlin has never exuded dynamo qualities in midfield. 

But remarkably, after the game, Marco Silva made a comment which incensed Everton fans on Twitter even more. 

The Portuguese commented on the lack of speed in the side, as shared by Phil Kirkbride of the Liverpool Echo.
