Tom Herman Almost Gave Himself A Concussion While Banging His Head On Player’s Helmet Pregame (VIDEO)

23 Nov 2019, 20:43 by Darrelle Lincoln

Tom Herman is a mad man.

The Texas Longhorns are in the midst of yet another disappointing season as they are coming off a 23-21 loss at the hands of Iowa State last week.

Just before their big matchup against the Baylor Bears — Texas head coach Tom Herman was spotted getting rowdy with his players before the Longhorns went back into the locker room by head-butting senior captain Malcolm Roach in the team huddle.
