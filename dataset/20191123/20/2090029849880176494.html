We never loved each other – Nina opens up on relationship with Miracle

23 Nov 2019, 20:57 by Sanya Agunbiade

Reality TV star and actress Nina has just opened up on her relationship with Miracle both while they were on the show and outside of the show.

According to the star, who was responding to a question by a follower on twitter during a question and answer session, their love for each other wasn’t real

The follower had wanted to know if the breakup with Miracle after the show really hurt her, but she responded saying they never loved each other.

   ‘neither of us loved each other’. she wrote
