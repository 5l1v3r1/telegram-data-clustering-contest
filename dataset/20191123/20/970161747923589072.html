What is vomit fruit as seen on I'm a Celebrity?

23 Nov 2019, 20:46

When it comes to I’m a Celebrity, there are certain things that can be counted on.

A ‘sexy’ shower scene? 2019 has that covered thanks to, er, Andrew Maxwell’s budgie smugglers. Hilarious fails from well-meaning stars? Caitlyn taking a tumble might do the trick.

The great-and-gross Bushtucker trials are also integral to making the show so great – and that includes the annual tradition of force-feeding a famous face something known as vomit fruit.

What is vomit fruit?

The fruit’s proper name is Morinda citrifolia. Other names for the fruit include the Indian mulberry, noni, starvation fruit and the dog dumpling.
