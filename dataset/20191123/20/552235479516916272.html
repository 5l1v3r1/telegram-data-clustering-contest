Celtic WAG Helen Flanagan hits back at fan who dug her up for moaning about her kids

The 29-year-old, who has two daughters with Celtic winger Scott Sinclair, found herself under attack after posting about her children on Instagram.

23 Nov 2019, 20:09 by Record Reporter

Helen Flanagan has hit back at one of her fans after the Celtic WAG complained about the stresses of coping with her children on social media.

The Coronation Street actress blasted one follower after they grew tried of her "blaming her stresses" on her two kids.

The 29-year-old, who has two daughters – Matilda and Delilah – with Celtic winger Scott Sinclair , found herself under attack after posting on Instagram .

The follower said: "Stop ranting about ur kids!" in a direct message that Helen later shared.

"Buckle up and get on with it!!! There are some people who are unable to conceive then u have people like urself moaning!

"It's part of the process get on with it and stop RANTING! Which is what your doing," the follower angrily concluded.

But Helen did not take kindly to the message and hit back at the follower - before sharing the exchange for all to see, the Mirror reports.

The Corrie star replied: "Actually. I am honest on my instagram so other mums can identity with me and feel less on their own.

"I am supportive of other women," she added - while adding to the screen grab of the exchange: "Direct messages like this. I have friends very close to me who have struggled to conceive."

The fiery exchange appeared to have been ignited after Helen shared two updates about her daughters testing her patience and causing her to stress eat.

"To get an peace at the moment I have to lock myself in the toilet... any other mums do the same?" the TV star mused as she shared a photo of herself online.

"Delilah screamed so much in the car today I was so stressed I had to stop at Costa drive thru and inhale this," she wrote in a second story post alongside a photo of a muffin.

However, the argument with the fan may have hit a nerve as she later shared a snap of her eldest daughter with the caption: "I'm not perfect but I love you so darling."
