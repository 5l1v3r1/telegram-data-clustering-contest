Local treasures await at Kitchener Collectibles Expo

From 10 a.m. to 3 p.m., residents can find a large selection of new and vintage items

23 Nov 2019, 20:00 by KitchenerToday Staff

A trove of treasures await residents at the Kitchener Collectibles Expo.

The event, run by the Ontario Collector Shows, will be held at Bingemans on Sunday, Nov. 24th. 

From 10 a.m. to 3 p.m., the expo will feature a mix of over 175 vendor tables packed full of new and vintage items for both serious and casual collectors.

The Kitchener Collectibles Expo has been held at Bingemans for the past 21 years, gathering buyers and sellers from across Ontario.

Organizers from the Kitchener Collectibles Expo say the show is 'an experience the entire family will enjoy.'

Residents with collectible items at home, are welcomed to bring an item or two to show to the vendors as many vendors are also buying as well as selling.

According to show organizers, participants customers who bring items to sell to the vendors cannot sell to other customers. 

Admission for this event is five dollars, which is payable at the door, and children under 12 will be given free entry.

For more information about the event, go to collectorsshow.ca.
