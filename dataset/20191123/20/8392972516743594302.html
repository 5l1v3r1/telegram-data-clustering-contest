Strictly Come Dancing: Kelvin Fletcher and Karim Zeroual top the leaderboard

A lot of 10s flying about...

23 Nov 2019, 20:44 by Karen Hyland

Karim Zeroual and Kelvin Fletcher wowed the judges to find themselves at the top of the Strictly Come Dancing leaderboard.

The CBBC presenter and his professional partner Amy Dowden impressed with their couple's choice dance to Drops of Jupiter, earning them 38 points.

Bruno Tonioli told them: "Brilliant! What I loved is the change in the dynamics how you mixed the movements. It had that freedom of spirit that I loved."

Motsi Mabuse added: "I stand tonight, as a dancer, I'm amazed at what you bring to the show.

"Your technique is unbelievable. I love that you always give 100%. To watch you is a gift."

Meanwhile, Kelvin also danced a couple's choice performance to Do I Love You, with Shirley Ballas branding it "absolutely impeccable".
