X Factor: Celebrity's fans fuming after Dermot reveals no wild card twist

23 Nov 2019, 20:55

There was another shock twist on Saturday night’s X Factor: Celebrity after host Dermot O’Leary revealed there would be no wildcard returning to the show.

The ITV presenter announced the unexpected news during Saturday’s episode of the show and revealed there will only be four acts competing in the final.

He explained: ‘There is no longer a wildcard for the acts who have been eliminated.’

It was only two weeks ago that music mogul Simon made the announcement that he would bring back on of the eliminated acts.

Viewers took to Twitter to share their thoughts on the shock twist.

One fan tweeted: ‘Simon stop changing things, u should have had everything sorted before show started. Why did you get rid of wildcard
“This has been very disappointing series, celebrities is a good idea, and I hope it comes back next year with the original as well.’
