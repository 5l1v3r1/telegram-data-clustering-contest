LeBron James Not Happy With The Scene Outside His Hotel In Memphis

23 Nov 2019, 20:42 by Chris Rosvoglou

Teams haven’t had much success this season trying to stop LeBron James, but it sounds like residents in Memphis have cracked the code.

James is having an incredible start to the season with the Los Angeles Lakers. He’s averaging  24.9 points, 7.7 rebounds and 11.3 assists per contest.

Los Angeles currently owns the best record in the NBA at 13-2. Next up on the schedule are the Memphis Grizzlies.

The four-time MVP is trying to get some rest before tonight’s game, however, the scene outside of his hotel in Memphis is apparently disruptive.

James went on social media to complain about the ruckus by his hotel.

“Literally trying to sleep/rest up for the game this evening and there’s a full out concert going on outside our hotel since around 10am,” James wrote on Twitter. “OK I see what y’all trying to do.”
