Country Singer Sam Hunt Breaks Silence Following DUI Arrest

23 Nov 2019, 20:10 by Rachelle Lewis

Country singer Sam Hunt has broken his silence following his DUI arrest on Thursday. He is taking full responsibility for his not so smart actions.

Hunt has issued an apology to fans, as well as authorities for “selfish actions” that caused him to drive the wrong way on a Nashville street. He used social media to express his deep regret over what led to his arrest.

“Wednesday night I decided to drive myself home after drinking at a friend’s show in downtown Nashville. It was a poor and selfish decision and I apologize to everyone who was unknowingly put at risk and let down by it. It won’t happen again,” Hunt shared via Twitter.
