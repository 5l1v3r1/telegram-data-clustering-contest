Video: Tom Herman Is Head-Butting Players Before Kickoff

23 Nov 2019, 20:49 by Dan Lyons

The Texas Longhorns will attempt to play spoiler today, on the road at Baylor. The Bears are 6-1 in league play after last weekend’s devastating blown game against Oklahoma, but can still get revenge if they make the Big 12 Championship. Tom Herman’s team has a chance to take advantage of a Baylor squad that could be reeling from that game.

Texas sits at 4-3 in league play and 6-4 overall, at third place in the Big 12. Under Herman, the Longhorns have a reputation for playing up and down to opponents, which would be good news today.

Baylor is a six-point home favorite entering the game. Things kicked off in Waco a few minutes ago.

Before the game, Herman had a unique way of getting his ‘Horns fired up. In a scrum of his players, he took one by the facemask and headbutt him pretty hard.

There’s no way that felt good, but whatever works to get the team ready, I guess.
