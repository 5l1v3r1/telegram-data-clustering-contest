Medicom Toy and Karimoku Introduce 400% 'HALF & HALF' BE@RBRICK

Utilizing contrasting shades of wood.

23 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Isaac Rouse

Following the reveal of its chessboard-inspired figurine, Medicom Toy has introduced yet another Karimoku collaboration BE@RBRICK. This 400% version piece utilizes contrasting shades of wood for its aptly named “HALF & HALF” appearance.

This 280mm figure was painstakingly made by Karimoku Corporation craftsmen, whom handcrafted each piece one by one. As such, figurings from the toymaker and furniture manufacturer will only be available in limited quantities.

Medicom Toy and Karimoku’s “HALF & HALF” 400% BE@RBRICK will sell for ¥89,800 JPY (approximately $827 USD) on November 30. These can only be purchased exclusively from Medicom Toy stores and its official website.
