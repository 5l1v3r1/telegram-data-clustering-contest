Ajit Pawar blackmailed, Saamana will expose culprits: Sanjay Raut

Shiv Sena leader Sanjay Raut has been a busy man this Saturday morning. His party, like the rest of the Opposition in Maharashtra, is reeling from the news that the BJP is suddenly back in power thanks to an NCP leader who switched alliances.

23 Nov 2019, 08:36

Does Surprise Saturday need more drama? Yes, apparently. Would you believe Sanjay Raut now claims Ajit Pawar -- the rebel NCP leader who stunned nearly everyone by unexpectedly throwing his weight behind a BJP government in Maharashtra -- was blackmailed?

And there's more. The Shiv Sena leader, who happens to be the executive editor of party mouthpiece Saamana, has promised to reveal the culpits in the newspaper.

Raut even says there's a possibility that Ajit Pawar will come back.

Five out of "8 MLAs" who went with Ajit Pawar returned, Raut said, adding that these lawmakers had been lied to, put in a car and "sort of kidnapped".
