Young girl allegedly sexually assaulted by two men in a Perth park

23 Nov 2019, 08:49 by Lauren Pilat

A 12-year-old girl is believed to have been sexually assaulted in a southern suburb of Perth on Thursday night.

A police spokeswoman said the alleged sexual assault happened in a park on McKay Street in Bentley between 6pm and 11pm.

She said two men, with their faces covered, grabbed the girl and sexually assaulted.

Child Abuse Squad detectives are investigating and would like to speak to anyone who was in the area who may have seen the men.

Anyone with information relating to this incident should call police on 131444.
