Mike Shipman: Fighting is like being in a unhealthy relationship with opponent

23 Nov 2019, 08:30

Mike Shipman obsesses over each and every one of his opponents, to the point where the Bellator middleweight can think of nothing else.

‘It’s like a very unhealthy relationship,’ he quips, perched on a chair at Bellator’s media day in London. Shipman fights domestic rival Fabian Edwards on Saturday night in the co-main event of Bellator’s last London event this year.

Tension has been building between Edwards (8-0) and Shipman (12-2) for the last two years.
