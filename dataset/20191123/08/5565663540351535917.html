WATCH: General Chiwenga lands in Harare; welcomed by China officials

23 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Mandla Ndlovu

Vice President Constantino Chiwenga arrived in Harare on Saturday morning where he was received by the Chinese Deputy Ambassador to Zimbabwe and close members of his family.

The former Zimbabwe strongman has been on a medical pilgrimage to China for the past three months after suffering multiple organ failure.

There have been fears that Chiwenga is plotting a coup against President Emmerson Mnangagwa with China.

There was no government official of ZANU PF official to receive the former Army boss.
