AIBE result 2019 released, steps to check merit list

AIBE result 2019: The AIBE results for several centres including six in Allahabad and one each in Visakhapatnam, Bhopal and Jabalpur has bee withheld. Result can check the result at allindianbarexamination.com.

23 Nov 2019, 08:16 by Education Desk

AIBE result 2019: The Bar Council of India has released the result of the All India Bar Examination (AIBE) at its official website, allindiabarexamination.com. The exam was conducted on September 15 and a tentative answer key was released on September 16. The result is based on the final answer key.

The AIBE results for several centres including six in Allahabad and one each in Visakhapatnam, Bhopal and Jabalpur has bee withheld. As per AIBE, several complaints were received from the concerned centres and the inquiry by the monitoring committee is still pending.

AIBE result 2019: How to check

Step 1: Visit the official website, allindiabarexamination.com
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘result’ in the left panel
Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page
Step 4: Log-in using credentials
Step 5: Result will appear, download

The Bar Council of India is an open book eligibility examination for students and aspirants of law. The exam will be a multiple-choice (MCQ) based test. The exam is conducted in 11 languages. Those who clear the exam will be allowed to practice law in India and can work as lawyers.
