Pete Davidson had crush on Leonardo DiCaprio

23 Nov 2019, 08:59

Cape Town - Pete Davidson had a "huge crush" on Leonardo DiCaprio when he was younger, because he was a huge fan of the iconic star's work.

He even met the star a couple of times.

Despite his childhood crush, Pete identifies as straight, but is known for being an ally of the LGBT community.
