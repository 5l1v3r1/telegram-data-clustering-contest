Man found dead after 'incident' between two inmates at Cloverhill Prison

Gardai are following a definite line of enquiry

23 Nov 2019, 08:38 by Darragh Berry

A man in his 30s has been found dead in his cell after an incident involving two male inmates in custody at Cloverhill Prison.

Gardai say they are following a definite line of enquiry after they and Emergency Services were called by prison authorities shortly after midnight.

The man was treated at the scene by emergency services but was pronounced dead a short time later.

The body of the deceased remains at the scene which is currently being preserved and a full forensic and technical examination is due to take place later today, Dublin Live reports.

A garda spokesman said: "The Coroner has been notified and the services of the State Pathologist and Garda Technical Bureau have been requested.
