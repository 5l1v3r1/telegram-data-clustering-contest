8 of the best Christmas gifts for boys, handpicked by musician and children's author Tom Fletcher

23 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Telegraph Writers
Guitarist Tom Fletcher has been cultivating the hearts of young people across the world since his McFly days. He is now a big name in the children's book world. Here he chooses his favourite 2019 gifts for kids.

Most of us remember Fletcher as the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of the hit band McFly, who skyrocketed to fame after supporting Busted on their 2004 tour. But, Fletcher's creative expertise isn't reserved for singing and guitars alone. Not only did he write many of the number one hits for his band (and a few of Busted's too), he has written multiple children's books, too.

Fletchers's story, The Dinosaur That Pooped Christmas, co-written with bandmate Dougie Poynter, became the most popular debut children's book of 2012. His Christmas-themed books didn't stop there — in 2016, his first solo novel The Christmasaurus, about a young boy's magical adventure on Christmas Eve, was also well received. 

It's fair to say that Fletcher knows what young people want, especially having three small kids himself, and this year, he says Christmas is all about lightsabers. "I got my two eldest a lightsaber each last year and it turned into quite the duel. The force is strong with them — and my shins too, luckily."
