The inaugural Wedding of the Year presented by the Sunday Mail and Beresford House

23 Nov 2019, 08:30

Did you tie the knot this year? The Sunday Mail is opening the floor to crown the Wedding of the Year. And if you win? A mind-blowing luxury retreat at Beresford House awaits. Enter now … or forever hold your peace.

The venue. The dress. The cake. And of course, the tale of love. But was yours the best wedding in 2019?

That honour will be decided by you, South Australia.

Couples who have tied the knot this year can join the Sunday Mail as South Australia votes to crown the inaugural Wedding of the Year, presented by Beresford House.

The chosen couple will win a luxury retreat worth $2500 at Beresford House in McLaren Vale, featuring a two-night stay at the winery’s McLaren Vale estate, a private chef and activities including a scenic helicopter flight.

Couples must have been married in South Australia in 2019. Entries close at midday on Thursday, November 28.

A shortlist will be announced by the Sunday Mail on December 1. And then it’s over to you, South Australia.

Voting on Wedding of the Year presented by the Sunday Mail and Beresford House will be open from December 1 until December 6.

The winners will be revealed in the Sunday Mail on December 8.
