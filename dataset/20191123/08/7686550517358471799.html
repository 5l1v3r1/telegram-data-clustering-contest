Amazon is restocking its sell-out beauty Advent calendar

£40 limited-edition calendar contains a plumping mist, a face mask, a FOREO Luna Play, an Olay brightening cream and TIGI hair care products

23 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Nisha Mal

Amazon will be restocking its out 2019 Beauty Advent calendar, giving shoppers another chance to snap it up.

The sought after beauty must-have first went on sale in November and sold out quickly.

Now, the retail giant has revealed that its Christmas countdown will be restocked online on December 6.

The Amazon Beauty calendar is one of the most affordable ones on the market, costing £40 it contains £220 worth of products.

The bumper Advent calendar contains a range of skincare, hair care and make up products hidden behind 24 doors.

It includes luxury brands like FOREO, Elemis and Percy & Reed, as well as drug store favourites like Sleek, L'Oreal and Rimmel.

Amazon's offering comes in a striking red and white box, with a picture of a Christmas tree on the front, made from non descript make products.
