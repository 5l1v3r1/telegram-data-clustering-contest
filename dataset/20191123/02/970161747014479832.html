I'm A Celebrity: Myles Stephenson sets record straight on cheating rumours

23 Nov 2019, 02:15

I’m A Celebrity star Myles Stephenson has had his say on claims he cheated on his ex-girlfriend Gabby Allen.

Myles first got together with Love Island alum Gabby, 27, in September 2018. They dated for nearly a year, and had been living together, before she confirmed their split to her followers on Instagram.

At the time, it was claimed Myles had been unfaithful but speaking ahead of his jungle debut, the singer has had his say on the rumours.

He told The Sun that he and Gabby had been on a ‘break’ and he had messaged a previous ex girlfriend of his. He claims he and the ex never actually met up, but she had messaged Gabby to tell her the two had been talking.

‘We were on a break at the time when I messaged this girl,’ Myles told the paper.

‘I shouldn’t have done it, even though me and Gabby weren’t speaking at the time and I moved home, but it was a general conversation.

‘It was a stupid mistake and I stopped speaking to her when we started getting back together, but by that time she’s messaged Gabby and said we’d been speaking.’

‘Nothing happened,’ Myles, who is now starring on I’m A Celeb alongside Jacqueline Jossa and Caitlyn Jenner, said. ‘I didn’t even meet up with her.’

Viewers recently saw Myles discussing relationships with his campmates and revealed he is worried about finding a ‘meaningful’ relationship.

While Gabby said she hasn’t been watching her ex in the jungle, she was made aware of his comments and was quick to hit back on social media.

She wrote: ‘Apparently he was on about “meaningful relationships”. I’m not sure the guy knows what he’s on about tbh.

‘I didn’t know cheating, lying and a whole load of other [poo emoji] came under “meaningful”. Who knows? Each to their own?’

Ouch.

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
