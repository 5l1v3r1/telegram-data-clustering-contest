BJP’s Devendra Fadnavis takes oath as Maharashtra CM, NCP’s Ajit Pawar his deputy

23 Nov 2019, 02:45 by JKR Staff

In a surprising development on Saturday morning, the BJP’s Devendra Fadnavis took oath as the chief minister of Maharashtra once again. Ajit Pawar of the Nationalist Congress Party was sworn in as the deputy chief minister of the state.

This has effectively brought an end to the month-long political stalemate in the second largest state of India after last month’s assembly results were inconclusive.

Prime Minister Narendra tweeted congratulating Fadnavis on becoming the chief minister once again. He wrote, “Congratulations to Devendra Fadnavis ji and Ajit Pawar ji on taking oath as the CM and Deputy CM of Maharashtra respectively. I am confident they will work diligently for the bright future of Maharashtra.”
