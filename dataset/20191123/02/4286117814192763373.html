Rajasthan: 11 dead, several injured after 2 mini buses lose balance

As many as 11 people died and several injured after two mini buses they were travelling in lost balance in Kuchaman city of Nagaur in Rajasthan on Saturday morning, newsagency ANI reported.

23 Nov 2019, 02:32

As many as 11 people died and several injured after two mini buses they were travelling in lost balance in Kuchaman city of Nagaur in Rajasthan on Saturday morning, newsagency ANI reported.

The accident happened around 3 am in the morning.

Those injured have been admitted to hospital.

More details are awaited.
