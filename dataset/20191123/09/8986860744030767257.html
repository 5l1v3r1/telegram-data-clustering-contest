Bro Sammy speaks on his alleged sexual relationship with Akuapem Poloo

23 Nov 2019, 09:11 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Popular Ghanaian Gospel musician, Bro Sammy has finally spoken about his alleged sexual relationship with Akuapem Poloo exposed by Xandy Kamel.

According to the Gospel musician, all the allegations leveled against him about having an intimate relationship with Akuapem Poloo are false.

Bro Sammy explained that all these people peddling these lies are bent on tarnishing his image.

He further disclosed that Akuapem Poloo has never been to his house and they only met on set while shooting a movie.

The gospel musician added that Akuapem Poloo is just his friend and nothing else.

Bro Sammy added that Akuapem Poloo only shot an advertisement for him, explaining that nothing ever happened between them.

According to Bro Sammy he always gets saddened when he is accused of such immoral act because he had never cheated on his wife since he got married.
