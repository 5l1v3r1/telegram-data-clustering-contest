Shatta Wale goes after popular Ghanaian blogger for discrediting his Grammy award nomination

23 Nov 2019, 09:24 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian dancehall King, Shatta Wale has descended heavily on Ghanaian blogger, Ameyaw Debrah for personally discrediting his Grammy award nomination.

Wale who is known for his outburst went vulgar on the celebrity blogger for attempting to discredit him over his technical Grammy award nomination.

Following the release of the nominations list for the 62nd Grammy Awards, a lot of media personalities have tried to discredit the ‘My Level’ hitmaker as a nominee after Beyonce’s Lion King: the Gift album which he was featured on was nominated.

With this, many have argued that Shatta Wale cannot be regarded as a nominee since the ‘Already’ track was not nominated; whilst others are of the view that since the album which contains the track has been nominated, Wale automatically qualifies as a nominee.

In a response to all the ongoing debate, a fumed Shatta Wale in a self-recorded video asked media houses and personalities in Ghana to stop the ‘foolery’ as they are always interested in painting a certain picture of how artists should look like making a particular reference to how Ameyaw Debrah.

According to Wale, the blogger asked UK based Ghanaian Grammy winner Fuse ODG how he felt for winning a Grammy’s with Ed-Sheeran without been given a plaque whiles the former was given a plague; a disrespect to the entertainment industry and a backbiting attitude.
