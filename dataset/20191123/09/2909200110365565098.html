Shatta Wale’s camp has no better Management – Kelvyn Boy

23 Nov 2019, 09:35 by MyNewsGH

Kelvyn Boy says there’s no better management in Shatta Wale’s camp that can handle his affairs and therefore does not opt to be part of them.

According to him, Shatta Wale cannot sign him neither can the Dancehall artiste manage him.

“I don’t see any good management that can manage me from Shatta’s camp. Yes he can endorse me and support me and provide me with a feature that will help push my brand. For Ghanaians we love to say it than to do it so i need to do it my own small way till I get people who have the energy to support me then I will work with them.”

He made this known when he spoke to Vibes In Five.

He mentioned that from past experience, he will not agree to be managed by Shatta Wale because although they will give you the platform, you’ve to put in the work to become who you want to become.

Kelvyn Boy noted that he’s appreciative of his few followers because it shows they have love for him and he will work hard to make them proud of him.
