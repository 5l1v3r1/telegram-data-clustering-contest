Anil Ravipudi Tasted the Power of Star Director!

23 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Akhil

It was an eventful day for director Anil Ravipudi on 23rd November for two things. Birthday wishes came pouring in for him from all quarters, the four-film old director tasted the power of being the director of a star hero.

Birthday wishes poured for him starting from celebs, technicians, production houses etc. and especially Mahesh Babu’s fans’ birthday wishes came along throughout the day showing this is how it would be to be directing a superstar like Mahesh Babu.

The teaser of ‘Sarileru Neekevvaru’ came out yesterday and has been receiving a positive response as it has been quite a while that we saw the superstar in a full-blown mass role, and this teaser promised that as the Sankranthi treat.

If Anil Ravipudi delivers a hit, he is sure to be for the top league of directors in the coming days. Anything different means he has to start over himself again or settle for non-stars. So, SLN would be a deciding film for him.
