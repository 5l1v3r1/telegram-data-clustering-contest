Prince Narula is quite sure that his buddy Arhaan Khan will propose to Rashami Desai, soon

Prince Narula adds that Arhaan is a very nice boy. We quickly asked him his take on a starlet Amrita's claims that Arhaan is fake and his real name is Mazhar Shaikh. Amrita Dhanoa had also told us in that BREAKING STORY of our's that Rashami is being fooled by Arhaan. Prince Narula said, "I know nothing like that. To me, he has always been Arhaan."
