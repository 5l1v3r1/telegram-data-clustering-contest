Davido’s Latest Album ‘A Good Time’ Sets Record

23 Nov 2019, 09:52 by Oyindamola Olubajo

 

Davido‘s newly released album, ‘A Good Time’ has become the first Nigerian effort to reach number one on US pop Itunes chart.

This feat by the album sets record as it also the first to enter into the top 10 US album chart on the same platform.

‘A Good Time’ released in the early hours of Friday, November 22 is Davido’s third studio album coming after ‘Omo Baba Olowo’ and ‘Baddest’.

The album comprises seventeen tracks, including collaborations with Peruzzi, Zlatan, Naira Marley, WurlD, Chris Brown, Yonda, among others.

Prominent music producers on the album include Shizzi, Kiddominant, Speroach Beatz, Tekno, etc.

This news is another indication that Afrobeats music is appreciated in other parts of the world.
