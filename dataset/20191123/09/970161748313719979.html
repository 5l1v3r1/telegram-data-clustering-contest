Liam Gallagher brands brother Noel ‘bully’ as he warns feud isn’t 'banter'

23 Nov 2019, 09:40

Liam Gallagher has piled on to his feud with brother Noel, branding him a ‘big bully’.

So that Oasis reunion isn’t on the way anytime soon…

It all kicked off between the Wonderwall singers when Noel, 52, appeared on Radio X with Zane Lowe, where he was asked about the praise Liam had heaped on him.

Not mincing his words, he told the host: ‘That doesn’t mean s**t to me. Because he’s on Twitter probably right now saying the exact opposite so it doesn’t mean anything.

‘In here on camera, playing to the gallery, fine, good. But when it f**king matters, he’s abusing my wife and my kids.

‘It’s like no, no, no, you don’t get to f**king do both. Either be a c*** and own it or don’t be a c***.’

‘When I eventually see him, he’ll realise it’s not f**king banter.’

Unsurprisingly, Liam, 47, wasn’t too impressed with his brother’s rant, and promptly let rip.

Responding to a fan’s post of the video on Twitter, the Beady Eye star hit back: ‘Noel Kevin Keegan Gallagher is the funniest thing I’ve seen or heard in over a decade the gift that keeps on giving LG x.

‘I’m scared I’m seriously upping my security so many people out there wanting to cause me harm HELP ME

‘There all BIG BULLYS (sic).’

Noel previously lifted the lid on his relationship with his former bandmate, admitting that every message he shares on social media is a ‘nail in the coffin’ of a potential revival of the Lyla singers.

In a candid chat with the Big Issue, he admitted: ‘It’s strange behaviour for someone who is gagging for me to pick up the phone and say let’s do it.

‘He’d put his whole life on hold to get Oasis back together. But every tweet he sends out it’s another nail in the coffin of that idea.’

Addressing his brother directly, he added: ‘If you think for one minute I am going to share a stage with you after what you’ve said you are f**king more of a moron than you look.’

Can’t we all just get along?
