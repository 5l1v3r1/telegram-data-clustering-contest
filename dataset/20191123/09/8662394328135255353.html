Have you seen Jennifer Robertson?

23 Nov 2019, 09:05

ACT Policing has appealed to the public for help finding a missing 17-year-old girl.

Jennifer Robertson was last seen at her home in Kambah on Saturday morning.

Police believe she may now be in the city, or making her way there.

She is described as Caucasian, of medium build and about 160 centimetres tall, with long brown hair and blue eyes.

When she was last seen, Jennifer was wearing a black polo shirt, black board shorts and black Converse shoes.

Police said they and Jennifer's family were concerned for her welfare.

Anyone with information on her whereabouts was urged to contact ACT Policing Operations on 131 444, quoting reference number 6464020.
