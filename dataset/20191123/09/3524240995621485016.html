23-year-old model battles stage-3 melanoma cancer

23 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Agency Report

American model Slick Woods has revealed via an Instagram post that she’s currently undergoing chemotherapy for stage three melanoma cancer.

Melanoma, also known as malignant melanoma, is a type of cancer that develops from the pigment-containing cells known as melanocytes.

The cancer typically occurs in the skin, but may rarely occur in the mouth, intestines, or eye, experts say.
