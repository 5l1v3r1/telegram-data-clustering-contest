(Video) Trent rakes a straight 60-yard cross-fielder that sums up jaw-dropping passing ability

23 Nov 2019, 16:39 by Matt Addison

Trent Alexander-Arnold has made swinging long-range passes across the pitch to Andy Robertson his speciality this season – and he’s just produced another from the top drawer against Crystal Palace.

He nonchalantly got hold of possession at full-back before he effortlessly fed his opposite full-back with a perfect pass.
