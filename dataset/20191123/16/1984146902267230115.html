Aston Villa vs Newcastle United: Lee Ryder's Premier League prediction

The Magpies face a Villa side who have lost their last two matches but will the break work in Newcastle's favour or not?

23 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Lee Ryder

Newcastle United travel to Aston Villa in excellent fettle as they find themselves in the top six of the Premier League's form table.

It could be argued that the Magpies had the momentum going into the international break and they have also had to wait over the weekend as other teams get back into the swing of things.

Will the break work in their favour or not?

But Newcastle will feel confident after two successive wins with Steve Bruce's return to Villa Park setting up an interesting clash.

Lee Ryder's prediction

United are 13/5 to get the victory at Villa Park and go in against a newly promoted outfit who have lost their last two games against Liverpool and Wolves.

Newcastle have been relying on defenders to get the goals for them but Villa could be in trouble if this is the game when it clicks for Allan Saint-Maximin, Joelinton and Miguel Almiron.

If the extra two days rest works in United's favour it could be a long night for Villa.
