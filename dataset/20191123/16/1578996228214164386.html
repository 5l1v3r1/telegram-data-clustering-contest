Here's Why Kim Kardashian Cried 'the Whole Way Home' from the 2013 Met Gala

23 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Hilary Weaver

In recent years, Kim Kardashian has become a Met Gala staple. On the first Monday in May, she dresses to impress all the cameras waiting to flash at the best looks on the carpet. This year, her tight-fitting gold dress was no exception, even if it did create a Met Gala eve rift between Kardashian and her husband, Kanye West.

But six years ago, Kardashian was not yet the belle of the Met Ball. In 2013, she was invited as West's guest. At the time, she was pregnant with the pair's first child, North, and wore a long floral print Givenchy by Riccardo Tisci dress. In a “Life In Looks” video for Vogue, Kardashian recounts the look—and why it ended up being infamous in her mind.

“Kanye was performing so I wasn’t actually invited; I was just Kanye’s plus one. And that was okay with me because I never really dreamed I would be at the Met Ball,” she said. “I know no one really probably wanted me there at the time.”

Despite feeling insecure, Kardashian said she gave into Tisci's suggestion for a stretchy floral print. But then the memes started rolling in.
