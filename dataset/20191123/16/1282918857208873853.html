Amala Paul Announces Release Date Of New Film 'Adho Andha Paravai Pola'

23 Nov 2019, 16:31 by Danish Ansari

Others

Amala Paul is all set to entertain the fans with her upcoming Tamil film 'Adho Andha Paravai Pola'. The fast-paced action drama is finally has a release date.

Written By Danish Ansari | Mumbai | Updated On: November 23, 2019 16:31 IST

Amala Paul is one of the most popular actors in the southern film industry who has impressed her fans with her outstanding performances in her latest outings. Amala Paul was last seen in the controversial film Aadai where she portrayed one of the most nerve-wracking characters on-screen and received a lot of mixed reactions from the viewers. The actress will be next seen in the upcoming thriller film Adho Andha Paravai Pola.
