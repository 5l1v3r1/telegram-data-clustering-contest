Peyton Manning launches long pass to Nuggets' mascot during NBA timeout

23 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

DENVER -

Peyton Manning still cannot resist throwing a pass.

>> Read more trending news

The retired NFL quarterback was sitting in the stands when the Denver Nuggets hosted the Boston Celtics on Friday night, WBIR reported.

During a timeout in the fourth quarter, the Nuggets' mascot, Rocky, tossed a pass to Manning, who caught it.

Manning, who had a career completion rate of 65.3% during his 17-year NFL career, then launched a pass toward the mascot, who was streaking on a "long" pass pattern on the court.

The spiral was true, but the ball went through the mascot's paws.
