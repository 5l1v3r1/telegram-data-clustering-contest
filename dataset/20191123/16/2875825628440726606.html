Marnie Simpson and Casey Johnson unveil baby son’s name with adorable family snap

The Geordie Shore star has finally announced the name of her baby son after welcoming him last month

23 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Irishmirror.ie

Marnie Simpson has finally announced the name of her baby boy.

The former Geordie Shore star, 27, took to Instagram on Saturday afternoon to share an adorable family snap and reveal the name of her tiny tot.

The reality TV babe told her 3.6 million followers: "Rox Johnson. 29.10.19. 8.1.

"We adore you so much our beautiful boy."

Marnie looked blissfully happy in the photo as she lovingly gazed at her bundle of joy while Casey looked unbelievably proud as he cosied up to his girlfriend and newborn.

The new family-of-three looked complete as they posed in matching neutral-coloured outfits for the professional snap.

The Geordie Shore OGs star, who was 12 days overdue, previously took to social media to share that she and beau Casey registered their son's name on Friday morning.

Speaking to the camera, as she sat in the passenger seat whilst Casey drove them to their destination, Marnie told: "So it's a very early morning this morning, but we are going to register the baby’s, well we are going to register the baby, so we have actually officially decided on the name.

“We had to chose it by today and we are going to register it. Can’t wait to tell you all, I hope you are going to like it."

She also shared that she and Casey were unable to come to an agreement on a middle name for their tot.

In an interview with OK! , Marnie divulged that she came up with the 'unusual name' but also liked other names like "Columbus, Diego, and Emmett."

Casey, 24, also revealed to the publication that he put his foot down when Marnie came up with a middle name he thought their child would be mocked for at school.

He said: "Marnie wanted his middle name to be Star but I had to put my foot down! I thought it was silly. He would have had the p**s taken out of him at school."

Marnie also opened up about her 'horrendous' birth experience and '28-hour labour' that she endured to welcome baby Rox.

The new parents welcomed the adorable baby on October 29 at 4:18pm weighing 8lbs 1oz.

She called the process 'traumatic' and revealed she suffered a postpartum haemorrhage and lost two pints of blood.

She told OK!: "I wouldn’t change it for the world and that feeling when they put him on my chest was like no other."

The delighted dad penned a touching post dedicated to Marnie and their baby with a photo of their son's tiny hand.

He wrote alongside it: "Where do I start... after 28 hours of labour my strong amazing princess @marns delivered our gorgeous little man on the 29th October at 4:20pm 8.1!!!

"My beautiful baby boy was born, I can’t even describe my emotions right now, it’s been a long journey (10 months).

Watching the person you adore in pain is not nice but the gift we both have right now just wipes away all the bad moments through labour.

"I literally sit and stair at him non stop with Marnie, he’s the start of our family, he’s so cute!!!"

Casey continued: "Again I wanna say thank you to everybody who has messaged me it means a lot the support has been absolutely amazing."

Marnie you have done so well you grew this human inside you and boy did you do it perfect and the right way.

"I love you Marnie thank you for being my baby mumma, you KILLED IT".
