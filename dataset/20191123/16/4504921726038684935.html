Namibians urged to use solar cookers

23 Nov 2019, 16:13

Environmental Educator at the Namib Desert Environmental Education Trust (NaDEET) training centre, Michael Kanyangya, has urged Namibian citizens to use renewable energy, especially solar energy, as a source of electricity as it is durable, cheap and clean.
He called on individuals to use solar cookers and solar ovens.
