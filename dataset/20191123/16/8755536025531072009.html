An Irish soap star just made his debut on Grey’s Anatomy

Fans are swooning over the Irish actor

23 Nov 2019, 16:05 by Goss Team

Grey’s Anatomy fans are swooning over the latest cast member – and he’s Irish.

Dr. Cormac Hayes made his debut on the ABC drama this week and you may already recognise him.

Actor Richard Flood came to fame in Ireland thanks to his role on Red Rock.

So far Dr. Hayes has already had a run-in with Meredith where he revealed his wife died, with Cristina Yang already giving him the nickname “McWidow”.
