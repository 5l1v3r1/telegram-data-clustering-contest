(Video) Sadio Mane’s perfect finish puts the Reds in front at Selhurst Park

23 Nov 2019, 16:20 by Matt Addison

Sadio Mane put the Reds in front at Selhurst Park with a superb finish having been found inside the box by Andy Robertson today.

It was the first shot on target from Jurgen Klopp’s men in the game and it was a scruffy goal, which nicked both posts before trickling over the line, but it’s another assist for the left-back and another goal for the Senegalese winger.
