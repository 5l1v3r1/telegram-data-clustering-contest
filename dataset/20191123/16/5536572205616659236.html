Rajon Rondo Hits Dennis Schroder In The Groin, Gets Ejected For Calling Ref A “F–king Pu–y” (VIDEO)

23 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Darrelle Lincoln

Rajon Rondo has always been a fiery player. That fire of his got him to an early shower on Friday night against the OKC Thunder.

With about ten minutes late in the 4th quarter, the NBA veteran could be seen hitting Thunder guard Dennis Schroder in the groin and receiving a technical foul.

He kept arguing with a ref to the point that you could see him call him a “fucking pussy.”

That’s when he got ejected.
