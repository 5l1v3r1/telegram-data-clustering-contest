Pentagon's Hui and Kino reveal M/V for 'La Di Da'

23 Nov 2019, 16:23 by olmal

Pentagon's Hui and Kino reveal M/V for 'La Di Da' which was composed by Kino and Nathan. Kino also wrote lyrics himself.
