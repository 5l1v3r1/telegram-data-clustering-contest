Chelsea Confirmed lineup vs Manchester City: Azpilicueta returns; Jorginho in for Mount

23 Nov 2019, 16:41 by Keshav Awasty

Cesar Azpilicueta returns to the starting lineup, with Jorginho replacing Mason Mount as Chelsea get set to take on Manchester City at the Etihad.

Frank Lampard has made two changes to the side that eased past Crystal Palace last time out as Chelsea prepare to lock horns with Premier League champions Manchester City at the Etihad Stadium.

The first change comes in defence, with Cesar Azpilicueta replacing Reece James as the Blues’ right-back. Azpilicueta dons the armband for Chelsea, joining Kurt Zouma, Fikayo Tomori and Emerson Palmieri in the back four, with Kepa Arrizabalaga assuming his place in goal.
