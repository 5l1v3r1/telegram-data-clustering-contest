PM Imran lauds economic team for ending current account deficit

23 Nov 2019, 16:59 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prime Minister Imran Khan has heaped praises on his economic team for ending the current account deficit, Dunya News reported on Saturday.

Is his statement on social media the premier said, “I am proud of my team which ended the deficit during the last four months and the value of rupee increased as a result.

“Economic progress has been achieved due to measures taken by the government to stabilize economy. Pakistan’s stock market has surged by 30 percent or almost 10,000 points during the last three months.”

It is pertinent here to mention that Imran Khan has also convened Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) core committee meeting tomorrow (Sunday) in Islamabad.
