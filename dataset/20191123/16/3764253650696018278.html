Actress Etinosa Idemudia replies a troll who asked her to lose weight

23 Nov 2019, 16:36

Nollywood actress and producer, Etinosa Idemudia, left social media users impressed after she replied an Instagram troll who tried to body shame her by asking her to lose weight.

Living in the spotlight comes with many advantages. However, there are also the downsides to it. When it comes to celebrities, many people tend to believe their opinions about how they should live their lives.

Etinosa recently had to deal with a follower who tried to dictate how she should live her life. She had taken to her Instagram page to share a photo of herself with singer, Kcee on an airplane.
