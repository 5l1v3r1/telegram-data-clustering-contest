Jose Mourinho reveals what pleased him most about 'amazing' Dele Alli skill

23 Nov 2019, 16:29

Tottenham boss Jose Mourinho was full of praise for Dele Alli after the midfielder’s Man of the Match display in Spurs’ 3-2 win against West Ham United.

Goals from Heung-Min Son, Lucas Moura and Harry Kane ensured Spurs kicked off the Mourinho era with three points and their first victory on the road in the Premier League since last January.

While goals from Angelo Ogbonna and Michail Antonio meant it was a nervous finish, there were still plenty of positives for Spurs and none more so than the performance of Alli.

The 23-year-old has been out of form in the last 18 months but he was reinstated to an attacking midfield position at the London Stadium and he provided the assist for Son to score the opener.

But Alli’s afternoon got even better when he produced a stunning piece of skill to keep the ball in play for Spurs’ second goal.
