India vs Bangladesh: Batting at twilight was most difficult, says Cheteshwar Pujara

23 Nov 2019, 16:55 by PTI

Highlights

  1. Pujara said facing the pink ball under lights, especially at twilight, was the most difficult task during D/N Test
  2. Pujara also became the first Indian to score a fifty in a D/N Test
  3. Pujara said the ideal time to bat with the pink ball is at the start of the innings and late into the final session

KOLKATA: India batsman Cheteshwar Pujara on Saturday said facing the pink ball under lights, especially at twilight, was the most difficult task during their maiden Day/Night Test against Bangladesh.

Pujara, who boasts of a double century with the pink ball at the domestic level, became the first Indian to score a fifty in a Day/Night Test but failed to convert his 55 into a bigger score on Friday.
