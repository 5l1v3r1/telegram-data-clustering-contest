Sprawling £1.4million mansion with zipwire in the back garden on the market

It's also got a library and a cinema room, if you prefer indoor activities

23 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Emilia Bona

A sprawling family home has come on the market in Merseyside - and it's got a very exciting feature tucked away in the back garden.

The six-bedroom property is nestled down a private drive on Caldy Road in Caldy , in one of the most affluent areas of the Wirral peninsula.

On the market with Bradshaw Farnham & Lea, this plush property carries a hefty price tag of £1,395,000 - but photos taken inside show just how much you get for your money.

A listing uploaded to Rightmove shows the high-end features and impressive interior design scheme that make this property so spectacular.

Approached by a huge private driveway and set within stunning landscaped gardens, the house enjoys considerable privacy from the road outside.

Named 'Beechwood', the property has undergone extensive renovation work and despite the classical frontage the interior is decidedly modern.

Stepping through the archway at the front of the property, the home opens out onto an entrance hallway with a separate cloakroom.

A bespoke kitchen fitted with underfloor heating has a central island with the sink and kettle tap, breakfast bar and two Miele ovens, with an integrated Miele fridge and freezer.

Spread across three floors, the first floor has four double bedrooms - three of which have their own en suite facilities.

The master suite has a dressing room and massive en suite bathroom, complete with a stand alone bath and wet room shower.

While the entire property is dripping in elegance, some of the most impressive features are the cinema room, bar, games room and library tucked away inside.

A cinema room is fitted with an Epsom projector and screen, letting prospective owners take their Netflix and chill nights to another level.

The games room and bar has a pair of bright red bar stools, a drinks fridge and a fully stocked bar area, as well as a huge silver bucket for champagne.

The study and library area has a welcoming log burner and old fashioned ladder to reach the very highest shelf, leading on to bedroom three.

Perhaps the most unusual and exciting feature of the entire property is tucked away in the back garden, which is immaculately presented with landscaped lawns and a patio area.

As well as goal posts and a decent sized private pitch, the property also has its own zipwire - making it a dream home for kids and big kids alike.

With the zipwire running down the side of the house, the garden also has a tyre swing and barbecue area.
