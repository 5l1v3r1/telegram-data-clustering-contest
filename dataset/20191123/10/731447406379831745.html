New UK warehouse venue, District 3, to open in Stoke-on-Trent

Pete Tong, Darius Syrossian and more will play the launch party next Saturday, November 30th.

23 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Marissa Cetin

A new venue is opening in the UK next weekend.

Midlands club District 3 will open in Stoke-on-Trent on Saturday, November 30th, with a launch party. The 1000-capacity warehouse venue, run by local crew The Move, is located on the same site as larger sister space China Hall.

The opening party lineup features Pete Tong, Darius Syrossian, Manu Gonzalez, Pete Bromley and Move resident Matt Erst.

Future programming is still TBA, but a New Year's Eve party is already in the works. (There's no lineup for that one yet).
