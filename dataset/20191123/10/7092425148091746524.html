Sasha Baron Cohen uses example of Hitler in dig at Facebook

23 Nov 2019, 10:35 by Nick Fitzgerald

Some serious shots were fired

Sasha Baron Cohen recently attended an Anti-Defamation League summit on anti-Semitism and hate in New York.

During the summit, the British comedian behind Borat and Ali G, slammed social media platforms, in particular Facebook and its founder Mark Zuckerberg.

No stranger to joking around, Baron Cohen was definitely not joking this time. He hit out at social media giants for facilitating the spread of misinformation around political campaigns.

Addressing the summit, Baron Cohen took a dig at Zuckerberg, who in October, defended his company's position not to ban political adverts that contain falsehoods.
