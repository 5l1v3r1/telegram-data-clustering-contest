PMCO Prelims Day 1 Live Standings : MEGA on 1st, Synergy top Indian Team at 8th

PUBG Mobile Club Open Champions is back after 5 months with its 2nd edition in Kuala Lampur. PMCO Fall Championship that hapened in July saw only Indian Team SouL participationg from South Asia. This Time it has been 2 Teams which directly qualified for the Grand Finals and 3 Teams which qualified for the Prelims which makes total of 20 Players from India.

PMCO Fall Split 2019 Prelims South Asia Teams Include SynerGE, Team INS and Team IND. This is Team INS and Team IND Second PMCO Prelims as they also qualified in July. Due to Visa issues in berlin, they had to play the prelims from India only,which saw them fall short. Meanwhile, SynerGE has been one of the best teams in India in this season and a huge performance would be expected from them.
