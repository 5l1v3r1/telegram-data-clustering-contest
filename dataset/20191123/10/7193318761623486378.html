The Oklahoman's Listing of the Week for Nov. 23, 2019

23 Nov 2019, 10:13

The Listing of the Week is an updated home on a 0.21-acre, wooded, corner lot across from a greenbelt in the heart of Quail Creek.

The 3,106-square-foot home at 3212 Brookhollow Road has four bedrooms, two baths, one half-bath, three living rooms, two dining areas and a two-car, side-entry garage. There is a formal dining room. The den has a fireplace and built-in bookcase. The kitchen has a breakfast bar, granite counters and pantry. The master bedroom has a ceiling fan, walk-in closet and full bath with granite counters. Upstairs is a game room.

Indoor updates include new textures and paint, ceiling fans, carpet, spray foam insulation in the walk-in attic, and Pella front door, back French doors and picture window in the breakfast room. Exterior updates include new paint, shutters, gutters, lighting, sprinkler system and landscaping.

The home, built in 1967, is listed for $329,900 with Ryan Litz, of Keller Williams Central Oklahoma. Open house will be from 2 to 4 p.m. Sunday. For more information, call 245-7812 or 330-2626.
