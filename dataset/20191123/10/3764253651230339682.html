Pep Guardiola rubbishes media reports suggesting he will dump Man City this season

23 Nov 2019, 10:40

- Pep Guardiola is not planning to walk out of his deal with Manchester City

- The Spaniard has a deal with the Citizens until the summer of 2021

- Guardiola won back to back Premier League titles for City but looks like he will concede the title to Liverpool at the end of this season

Manchester City boss pep Guardiola has rubbished media reports linking him away from the Etihad Stadium at the end of this season.

The Spanish born tactician has a deal with the current Premier League champions until the summer of 2021 having joined them in 2016.

Guardiola's men are currently struggling to catch up with table toppers Liverpool on the log as they are eight points shy of the Reds after 12 matches.
