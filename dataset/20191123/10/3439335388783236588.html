Kauai outage to affect Kokee residents | Honolulu Star-Advertiser

23 Nov 2019, 10:24

Power has been restored after a brief emergency outage in Kokee this afternoon to allow crews to cut trees threatening power lines along Kokee Road.

The emergency outage lasted from about 2:30 to 3:45 p.m. and affected about 200 customers in the area, a KIUC spokeswoman said.

Crews took preemptive measures because the trees looked like they could pose a hazard due to the strong winds today, the spokeswoman said.

Trimming of the trees also helps prevent an outage during the Kauai Old Time Gathering, a festival on Sunday in Kokee.
