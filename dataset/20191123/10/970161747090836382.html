Jeffree Star apologises after Shane Dawson Conspiracy palette backlash

23 Nov 2019, 10:15

Jeffree Star has apologised after fans claimed they found ‘hairs’ in their Shane Dawson Conspiracy palettes.

The YouTube beauty guru, who’s currently enjoying a birthday trip to Bora Bora, took to social media to explain the situation.

‘Hey everyone, it’s come to our attention that a few dozen people out of 1.1 million palettes produced, have a few ribbon fibers embedded in their products,’ he said.

‘The lab has done a full investigation & we found the issue. I pride myself on quality and fully apologize for this error.’

The 34-year-old attached a screenshot of an email, explaining that a few palettes contained ribbon fibres, and detailing the measures that will be taken in future, including eliminating the use of scissor-cut ribbon and using a black light to identify fibres.

Jeffree has been praised for handling the situation quickly, with one fan saying: ‘Thank you for proving that it is NOT HARD to take part in quality control,’ and another adding: ‘THIS is how you handle an issue, take notes gurus.’
