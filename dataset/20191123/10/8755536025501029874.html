Kim Kardashian breaks silence on Caitlyn Jenner’s stint on I’m A Celeb

It looks like the KarJenner clan HAVE been keeping up with Caitlyn

23 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Kendra Becker

Kim Kardashian has finally addressed Caitlyn Jenner’s stint on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here!

The transgender star has been in the jungle for a week now, and many found it odd that her kids weren’t showing their support for her on social media.

However, the 70-year-old’s former stepdaughter Kim has since shared a video of Caitlyn telling her campmates to “Rise and Shine” – poking fun at Kylie Jenner’s viral phrase.

Kim tweeted the video from the show’s official ITV account, and wrote: “I literally recorded this and set this as my alarm a few weeks ago.”
