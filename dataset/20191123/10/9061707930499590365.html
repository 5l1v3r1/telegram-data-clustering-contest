These are the shop jobs that are available at Intu Derby

Want to work in retail?

23 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Hritika Sharma, Oliver Astley

With people starting to think about starting on their Christmas shopping, stores are taking on extra staff to cope with demand.

As a result, there are a number of jobs available at shops at Intu Derby .

From management to sales, there are vacancies at numerous top stores including Judith Hart, Timberland, and Hugo Boss.

Here are the seasonal and permanent roles available:

Judith Hart
Sales Assistant (part-time)

A sales assistant is required at Judith Hart to cover maternity leave. A minimum of one year of previous experience in sales is required. Bring your CV to the store to apply.

Footasylum
Store Supervisor (full-time)

Footasylum is looking for an efficient, highly organised candidate with previous retail management experience, ideally in fashion retail. Apply in-store or email 078derby@footasylum.com .

Footasylum
Sales Assistant (temporary – Christmas)

There is a vacancy for a sales assistant to work over the Christmas period, with previous experience essential. Apply in-store or email recruitment@footasylum.com , or alternatively contact 0844 209 0803 for more information.

Timberland
Assistant Store Manager (full-time)

Timberland is looking for an enthusiastic commercial manager with an eye for detail and a proven track record of delivering results. Previous experience in customer service or retail management is required. Bring your CV to the store to apply.

Timberland
Sales Assistant (temporary – Christmas)

Another vacancy for the Christmas period. The selected candidate will be required to work on both Boxing Day and New Year’s Day. Previous experience as a sales assistant or in customer service is preferred but not essential.

Muffin Break
Assistant Manager (full-time)

Muffin Break is recruiting for an assistant manager who will be available for early (starting 6am) as well as late (ending 9pm) shifts for a hands-on role. Previous supervisory experience is preferred but not essential. Email derbymuffinbreak@gmail.com for an application form or collect it in-store.

Hugo Boss
Sales Associate (part-time)

Hugo Boss is looking for a sales associate who can demonstrate commercial acumen and brand knowledge and is willing to learn and develop, with the ability to be flexible and adaptable. No previous experience is required and training will be provided. Send your CV and cover letter to BOSS_Derby@hugoboss.com.

These were all correct at the time of writing.
