'Won the internet' - Aston Villa favourite replies to teammate's tweet and fans love it

Aston Villa latest news | Villa's wait for a return to Premier League action goes on with Dean Smith's men not in action following the international break until Monday night

23 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Jonny Bonell

John McGinn is already adored by Aston Villa fans.

His talent on the pitch, goals, assists and performances in the Premier League have all endeared himself to the Villa Park faithful.

The Scot clearly has a sense of humour, as well, with a number of clips from social media bringing a smile to fans’ faces.

And he’s done it again with a witty response to team-mate Ahmed Elmohamady after the Egyptian tweeted a post in his native language.

While the message wouldn’t have made much sense to most UK readers – McGinn “won the internet” with his reply.

“Completely agree with you mate,” he said.

Villa fans were quick to react to his comment on Twitter.
