Mingle at the Mansion set for Dec. 14

23 Nov 2019, 10:00

Preservation Oklahoma Inc. will kick off the holiday season at the second annual Mingle at the Mansion, presented by Sage Sotheby’s International Realty, from 6 to 9 p.m. Dec. 14 at the Overholser Mansion, 405 NW 15 in historic Heritage Hills.

The velvet ropes of the Overholser Mansion will come down and partygoers are invited to experience the house in all of its splendor, just as the Overholser family would have in its golden age of parties.

Guests can tour the house with all the halls decked for the holidays while enjoying light bites and festive libations provided by COOP Ale Works, Artisan Fine Wines and Oklahoma Distilling. Holiday music by The Savoy Trio will play as guests mingle and explore this 1903 Oklahoma treasure. Holiday attire is encouraged.

Tickets for Mingle at the Mansion are $60, and can be purchased at www.preservationok.org/mingle-at-the-mansion. For more information or to learn about sponsorship opportunities, contact Cayla Lewis at 525-5325 or director@preservationok.org.
