Police say missing Banchory man David Strachan could be in Dundee

Police say a Banchory man reported missing could be in the Dundee area.

23 Nov 2019, 10:35 by Jake Keith

David Murray Strachan, 51, has been missing from the Wilson Road area of the town since 9pm on Tuesday November 19.

David is described as being 5 feet 10 inches tall and having short, fair/blonde hair.

When last seen, he was wearing black jeans, a blue fleece, black v-neck jumper and a red shirt.

Huntly Police Station sergeant Graeme Forbes, said: “We are keen to hear from anyone who may have seen David, or maybe knows where he is, to get in touch. His family are concerned for him.

“We would also ask that David call us or his family to let everyone know he is OK.”

Anyone with information should call 1o1 quoting reference number 2400 of 21 November 2019.
