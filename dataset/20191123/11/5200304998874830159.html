Overnights: ‘KBC 11’ & ‘Crime Patrol’ put Sony TV back at No.1 on Friday in UK

23 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Raj Baddhan

Sony Entertainment Television returned to the No.1 position on Friday after big ratings for ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati’ and ‘Crime Patrol’, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Sony TV attracted a share of 0.15%, followed closely behind by Star Plus with 0.14%, PTC Punjabi with 0.13%, Star Gold with 0.06%, Colors and B4U Movies with 0.05%, Hum TV, ZEE Cinema, New Vision TV and Colors Rishtey with 0.04%, ZEE TV with 0.03%, Geo Kahani, Geo News, Dunya News, Sony SAB TV and Hum Masala with 0.02% and Geo TV with 0.01%.

Sony TV’s biggest attractions on Friday were ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 66,300 viewers, followed by ‘Crime Patrol’ at 23:00 with 52,700 viewers, ‘Mere Sai’ at 19:00 with 35,800 viewers and ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 with 22,400 viewers.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ topped ratings with 65,800 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 64,900 viewers, ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 60,300 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 49,700 viewers, ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 47,500 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 40,500 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 30,000 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ at 22:00 with 13,600 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 10,600 viewers and ‘Namah’ at 18:30 with 2,900 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ was watched by 45,200 viewers at 21:30, followed by repeat of ‘Bigg Boss 13’ at 16:30 with 6,600 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Mehboob Aapke Qadmon Mein’ led with 25,000 viewers, followed by ‘Ishq ZeheNaseeb’ at 20:00 with 24,500 viewers and ‘Soya Mera Naseeb’ at 19:30 with 10,400 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ topped ratings with 25,900 viewers at 18:30, followed by ‘Happu Ki Ultan Paltan’ at 14:30 with 17,700 viewers and ‘Bhabhi Ji Ghar Par Hain’ at 14:00 with 16,600 viewers.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
