Two dead and woman fighting for her life after Subaru ploughs into pedestrians

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

23 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

A driver and a pedestrian were killed when a car ploughed into two people on a road close to a showground.

The horror collision happened on the A1074 Dereham Road, near Norfolk Showground, at 5pm on Friday.

A blue Subaru Legacy car struck a man and a woman who were walking on the road, according to police.

The Subaru's driver - a man in his 30s - and the male pedestrian, aged in his 40s, both died at the scene.

The woman, aged in her 50s, suffered life-changing injuries and is in a serious condition in hospital.
