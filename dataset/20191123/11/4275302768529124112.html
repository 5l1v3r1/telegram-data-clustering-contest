Toy crossbow sparks armed police response in Dunfermline

A large armed police response descended on a Dunfermline street in the early hours of Saturday morning – only to discover it had been sparked by an innocent children’s toy.

23 Nov 2019, 11:17 by Craig Smith

The alarm was raised at around 12.35am when police received reports about a man being in possession of some sort of weapon – described as a crossbow – in Broomhead Drive.

Armed officers and an ambulance attended, and surrounding roads were cordoned off in a bid to minimise any potential danger.

However, it soon emerged that the ‘weapon’ in question had been a toy crossbow which had been given to an 18-year-old man as a gift earlier that evening.

A Police Scotland spokeswoman confirmed: “Around 12.35 am on Saturday November 23 2019, police received a report of a male in possession of a cross bow in Broomhead Drive, Dunfermline.

“Police attended and it was established that the item in question was actually a toy crossbow.

“Advice was given and no further police action taken.”
