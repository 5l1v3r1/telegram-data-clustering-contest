Controversies Hurt Only Mega Brothers, Not Others?

23 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Sridhar Raavi

Mega Brother, Naga Babu finally ended his successful journey as the host of the one of the most popular (or unpopular) comedy shows on television, ‘Jabardast’. To put an end to all the speculations on his exit, the actor released a video explaining his concern.

He said that he didn’t want to invite controversies anymore and that’s why he was quitting the show because of the ideological differences with the producers of the show, Mallemala Productions. This statement seems to be strange coming from Naga Babu.

When it comes to him, he wants no controversies despite the fact that he was seen laughing at indecent jokes even, but he can make all sorts of controversies and fun on others, targeting a particular and a party, though. Remember?

Coming to Naga Babu’s latest activities, he recently posted a photoshoot with younger looks, sporting cool T-shirt and looking slimmer than before. That was said to be his best appearance in recent times. Is he planning something new for himself, a web series or something else? Let’s wait and see.
