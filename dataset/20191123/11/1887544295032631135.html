OPP investigating after body of missing male is pulled from Lake Erie

23 Nov 2019, 11:33 by The Canadian Press

NORFOLK, Ont. - Ontario Provincial Police in Norfolk County have launched an investigation after the body of a male who was reported missing was found in Lake Erie.

Police say friends of the male had reported him missing Friday morning after spending a night at a cottage on Millionaires Island.

They say an OPP Marine Unit along with various other emergency personnel were called in and that the body was pulled from the lake Friday evening.

Police say a post-mortem will be conducted at a Hamilton hospital to determine the cause of death.

In the meantime, they say the name of deceased is being withheld pending notification of next of kin.

Police say the investigation is in its early stages and that more information will be released as it becomes available.

This report by The Canadian Press was first published on Nov. 23, 2019.
