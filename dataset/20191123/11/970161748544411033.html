Inside Kimberley Walsh's wild birthday party with Cheryl and Nicola Roberts

23 Nov 2019, 11:19

Nadine Coyle might be roughing it in the I’m A Celebrity jungle, but the same can’t be said for her Girls Aloud bandmates.

In fact, Cheryl and Nicola Roberts were on hand to celebrate Kimberley Walsh’s 38th birthday with a wild night at Bagatelle, in London.

Thanks for the invite…

The Fight For This Love singer, 36, was spotted arriving at the swanky restaurant, rocking a black leather top, coupled with dark orange trousers.

She completed the look with a patterned scarf and a pair of giant triangle earrings we never knew we needed.

While birthday girl Kimberley was a walking glitterball in a sparkly mini-dress, and strappy silver heels.

And Nicola, 34, stunned in a black and white checked blazer and matching skirt, on top of a black lace bralet.

We are absolutely not worthy of the Love Machine singers.

Sarah Harding didn’t appear to join the trio – who had a wild night out at the bash.

In a clip shared on Cheryl’s Instagram story, Kimberley could be seen looking a little delicate as she appeared to take a shot.

And another video showed the Strictly Come Dancing star being presented with a huge cake and sparklers, before they all headed straight to the dancefloor.

And we’re not at all jealous of their night out.

However, it wasn’t all fun and games for the Sound Of The Underground singers, who were forced to hit back at claims they had been voting for Nadine to undertake all of the Bush Tucker trials on I’m A Celeb.

In fact, Chezza insisted there was nothing but ‘love’ for the ITV star.

‘We only want to see Nadine do well, and regardless of any differences we might have had, we will always all have love for each other,’ she told MailOnline.

‘We did not enjoy watching her distressed when jumping out of the plane. And we don’t have time for group chats regarding the trials on the show!’

‘I felt really emotional watching Nadine jump out of the plane and texted Cheryl saying how hard it was to watch,’ Kimberley added.

‘I think she’s doing great in there.’

So that’s that, then.
