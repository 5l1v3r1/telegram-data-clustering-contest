U.S. Central Command says anti-IS operations to pick up in coming days, weeks

23 Nov 2019, 11:09

MANAMA (Reuters) - U.S. Central Command chief General Kenneth McKenzie said on Saturday there are 500 U.S. personnel in east Syria, adding he expected anti-Islamic State operations to pick up in coming days and weeks.

McKenzie was speaking on the sidelines of the Manama Dialogue Conference in Bahrain.
