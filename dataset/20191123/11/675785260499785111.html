Katie Price shares old pic of her kids as Emily Atack attack backlash rages on

Katie has come under fire after making an incredibly rude comment about Emily's alleged lack of personal hygiene

23 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Mirror .co.uk

Katie Price looks to be recycling old photos of her daughters as she attempts to distance herself from a furore following ghastly comments she made about I’m A Celeb star Emily Atack.

The 41-year-old former glamour model shared a photo on Instagram of eldest daughter Princess Tiaamii Crystal Esther Andre, 12, and five-year-old Bunny entertaining a pair of dogs.

However it appeared the image was from a photo shoot that the ex-Loose Women star had previously shared via Instagram three days earlier.

Posting the snap, Katie showed her two girls sat on a wooden fence while wearing matching parkas and hats - available to buy from the brand Katie set up in her children’s name.

“Saturdays with my girls,” Katie wrote, alongside a pink heart emoji - and adding: “wearing their own range @princessandbunny.co.uk coats & hats.”

She added a Christmas tree emoji and a gift box emoji while adding an “Ad” hashtag to signify that the post was an advert for the Princess and Bunny brand.

However, estranged husband, Kieran Hayler, 32, had previously shared a video of himself with his two children from Katie, Bunny and six-year-old son Jett, at the cinema together.

Katie’s innocent looking post came less than 24-hours after she viciously lashed out at I’m A Celeb star Emily Atack, 29.

In a video shared on Instagram stories which has been widely panned by fans and critics, Katie alluded to Emily’s sex life and made a vile comment about her personal hygiene.

Katie’s outburst came after Emily had alluded to Katie’s own sexual health after she caught I’m A Celebrity: Extra Camp co-hosts Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas splashing around in a jungle pool where Katie had first embarked on a romance with Peter Andre 15 years earlier.

"I hope you boys went to the clinic after being in that pool," Emily joked on camera as she pulled a face.

But the quip didn't land well back at home, as Katie was reportedly fuming when she heard about Emily's comments from friends.

"She was telling people that she can't believe Emily would say something so snide and if she 'wants a problem, I'll give her a problem'," a source close to Katie told The Sun following the incident.

The insider added: "At one point Katie was saying she'd fly out to Australia and confront her. She's not going to, obviously, but she was asking around to see if anyone had Emily's number."

Indeed, some fans went as far as to accuse Katie of posting her old photo of Princess and Bunny in an effort to divert attention from the ongoing argument with Emily.

One fan slammed Katie on Instagram, typing: "U lost the minute you bit back! Have some maturity and class. Dont rise to it. But for someone who has campaigned for online bullying to be a crime wtf are u doing!"
