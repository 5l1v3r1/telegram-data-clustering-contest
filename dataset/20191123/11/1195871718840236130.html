If you’re looking for a top smartwatch this Black Friday then look no further than this UK deal on the Samsung Galaxy Watch.

Slashes from its regular £279.99 price, you can bag it for £179.99. It’s the most significant price drop we’ve seen.

Of course, it’s been out a long time – so the most surprising thing is that it’s taken Samsung this long to offer big discounts. That shows the enduring success of the Galaxy Watch, which was first released in 2018.

The Samsung Galaxy Watch entering the coveted sub-£200 bracket is something of a watershed moment – and is a direct response to Apple which will now sell you an Apple Watch Series 3 for £199.

That device will only work with iPhone, which leaves more than half the population looking elsewhere for the best smartwatch.
