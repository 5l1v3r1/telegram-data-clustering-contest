DC Kills Batman, Wonder Woman and Superman in Animated DCeased Promos

23 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Dan Conner

New animated promos for DCeased kill Batman, Wonder Woman and Superman.

Posted to the Twitters for each of the three characters, the short animations transform these Justice League members from their usual appearances into their DCeased versions. "There’s a monster inside all of us," reads the Batman tweet, which features a closeup of Batman that blends into his DCeased version with a sinister smile.
