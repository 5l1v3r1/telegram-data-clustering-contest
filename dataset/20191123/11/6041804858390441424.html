The World of #NC19: Showcasing Naga Chaitanya’s Middle-Class World

23 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Manaswini K

The makers of Naga Chaitanya – Sekhar Kammula film have released a small video titled The World of #NC19 on the occasion of the actor’s Birthday. The video essentially introduces Naga Chaitanya’s character in the film. It looks like Naga Chaitanya will be seen as a lower-middle-class man and his looks similar to that of his matured look in his recent hit film, Majili.

The actor is seen wearing worn-out clothes, sweating while sweeping the floor, saving money and having fun with the neighboring kids. The premise looks promising and Akkineni fans would only hope Kammula will not get into his routine formula of A-Phase and B-Phase story. The movie shooting is progressing at a brisk pace.

The makers are planning February 2020 release for the movie. Sai Pallavi is teaming up with Sekhar Kammula for the second time after Fidaa. This is her first time with the Akkineni hero. Buzz is that the movie is titled ‘Love Story’ but the makers have not confirmed it yet.

Naga Chaitanya is currently waiting for the release of his Venky Mama. The movie is likely to release on December 13th. The movie is his first Multistarer film with his uncle, Venkatesh.
