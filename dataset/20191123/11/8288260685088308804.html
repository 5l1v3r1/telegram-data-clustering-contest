Euromillions £105 million prize - ticket holder comes forward

The prize coincides with the 25th anniversary of the first National Lottery draw

23 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Ben Hurst, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-hurst/

A ticket-holder has come forward to claim the £105 million EuroMillions jackpot

prize won on Tuesday, National Lottery operator Camelot has announced.

The winning numbers were 8, 10, 15, 30 and 42, with 4 and 6 selected for the Lucky Star numbers.

It is the sixth Euromillions jackpot that has been banked this year.

It also coincides with the 25th anniversary of the first National Lottery draw.

Another player also became a millionaire by matching the "Millionaire Maker" code ZMSF62402.

Andy Carter, senior winners' advisor at The National Lottery, said earlier this week: "On our 25th birthday, one very lucky ticket-holder has scooped tonight's entire £105m EuroMillions jackpot.

"Players across the country are now urged to check their tickets to see if they have won big."

He added that last night's "big winner has scooped the sixth EuroMillionsjackpot banked in the UK this year."
