Watch: Burna Boy, Ed Sheeran And Stormzy In “Own It” Music Video

23 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Michael Bamidele

Hours after releasing the his new single “Own It”, British rapper Stormzy has followed up with a video.

“Own It” is a track off Stormzy’s forthcoming second album “Heavy Is The Head”.

The Nathan James Tettey directed music video follows Stormzy, Burna Boy and Ed Sheeran as they perform and party across different sceneries in London.

The album is set for release on the 13th of December and features the singles “Wiley Flow,” “Vossi Bop,” and “Crown”.

The album is the U.K. rapper’s follow-up to his 2017 debut “Gang Signs & Prayer”.
