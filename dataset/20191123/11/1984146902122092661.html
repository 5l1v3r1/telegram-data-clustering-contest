Gateshead's By The River Brew announce huge party for NYE with Gin Palace and midnight fireworks

By The River Brew on the Gateshead side of the River Tyne are planning a huge party for New Year's Eve

23 Nov 2019, 11:41 by Simon Duke

It was the place to be as 2018 became 2019 and By The River Brew is planning on more of the same this year.

The popular shipping container development on the Gateshead side of the River Tyne is once again proving it is definitely not just for summer as, hot on the heels of the launch of its WNTRMRKT and Alpine Lodge , it has put tickets on sale for its massive New Year's Eve party.

Come Together II A New Year's Eve Celebration will, as you'd expect, take place on Tuesday, December 31 and revealing all on Facebook, By The River Brew posted: "After an incredible first NYE at By The River Brew Co. last year we are stoked to announce details of our this years New Years Eve event!

"COME TOGETHER is a FULL SITE PRODUCTION incorporating our Tap Room, Trakol, Backyard Bikeshop, HWKRMRKT, Alpine Lodge plus a huge heated Marquee and of course unparalleled views of the City."

The end-of-year spectacle also promises midnight fireworks, street food traders, cracking cocktails, beers, a host of DJS, a Gin Palace and special guests yet to be announced.
