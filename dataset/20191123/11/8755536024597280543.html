Elizabeth Banks shocks by making controversial joke about Prince Andrew on The Graham Norton Show

Viewers were left in STITCHES

23 Nov 2019, 11:37 by Kendra Becker

Elizabeth Banks shocked viewers last night after she made a controversial joke about Prince Andrew on The Graham Norton Show.

The actress appeared on the show on Friday alongside Ricky Gervais, Kylie Minogue and Lewis Hamilton, and during her interview, she took a pop at the Duke of York.

Graham tried to embarrass Elizabeth by showing an old photo of her from when she was a teenager, and then pointed out a stray hand touching her shoulder in the picture.

Graham said: “I’ve only just noticed this hand, is it someone saying, ‘You’ll regret this photograph?'”

Causing jaws to drop in the audience, Elizabeth replied, “That’s Prince Andrew’s hand.”

A shocked Ricky Gervais said, “Oh God, Jesus,” before Graham joked that it was just photoshop.
