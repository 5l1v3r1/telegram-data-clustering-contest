Khurshid Shah's judicial remand extended for 14 days

23 Nov 2019, 11:01 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

SUKKUR (Dunya News) – Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP) senior leader Khurshid Shah’s judicial remand has been extended for 14 days in assets beyond income case, Dunya News reported on Saturday.

The PPP leader was taken to Sukkur’s Accountability Court (AC) from hospital via ambulance. Khurshid Shah’s counsel requested the court to direct the National Accountability Bureau (NAB) to return his client’s mobile phone.

The NAB prosecutor told the court that Khurshid Shah’s cell phone has been sent for forensic audit and will be returned after completion of required procedure. The AC ordered to produce the PPP leader on December 7 again.
