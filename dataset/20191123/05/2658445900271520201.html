Water Gate parish holds 6th adult harvest - The Nation Newspaper

23 Nov 2019, 05:27

The 6th adult harvest thanksgiving of Celestial Church of Christ (CCC) Water Gate Parish, Ekundayo, Ifo , Ogun State, will take place on Sunday, December 8, 2019.

Activities marking the weeklong anniversary tagged ’’Harvest of Promotion’’, according to a release issued by Prophetess Olubunmi Mohammed would begin on Monday December 2 with a special vigil for sidemen and women.

The statement reads: ‘’ It’s been six years since God led our father in the Lord and shepherd in charge, Superior Evangelist Mathew Odunjo to establish the parish. There will be special blessing and needy service on Wednesday December 4, and special prophets and prophetesses intercession for people on Thursday December 5, while the new moon service will be held same day.

‘’Friday December 6 is a special ‘night of promotion’ featuring Prophet Seun Ale of True Anointing Parish as guest preacher. The programme will also feature song ministrations by choirs from other celestial church parishes and guests artistes including Evangelists Shina Harmony; Niyi Best aka Okiki Ogo Jesu; Shola Hephzibah among others.’’

‘’ The harvest thanksgiving will be held at the church’s auditorium on Sunday December 8 by 10 am.’’
