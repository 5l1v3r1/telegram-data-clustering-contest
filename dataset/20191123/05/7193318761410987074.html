Thunder Buddies Podcast: Lakers sequel even more suspenseful

23 Nov 2019, 05:19 by Joe Mussatto, Maddie Lee

Welcome to the home of our Thunder Buddies podcast, named, in part, from Universal Studios' comedy 'Ted,' but inspired by our continued hope for wall-to-wall coverage of the Thunder.

Below is our list of episodes (click the link to listen). If you prefer Apple Podcasts for your podcast listening experience, click here and subscribe. Questions? Comments? Join the conversation via Twitter.

Thanks for listening. Share with your loved ones, pets and neighbors:

Thunder Buddies – Lakers sequel even more suspenseful (Game 15 of 82) – The Thunder fell to the Lakers 130-127 Friday night in OKC, three days after a five-point loss to the Lakers in L.A. There were superstar highlights, third-quarter runs, an ejection and a crucial crunchtime decision in this one. Maddie Lee and Joe Mussatto discuss.
