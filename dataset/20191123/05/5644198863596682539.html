FDA approves Aquestive's ALS treatment

23 Nov 2019, 05:50

REUTERS: The U.S. Food and Drug Administration on Friday approved Aquestive Therapeutics' treatment for neurological disorder amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS).

Aquestive has developed riluzole oral film (ROF), and will market the film under the brand name Exservan. (http://bit.ly/2pIFzi5)
