Photos: Priyanka Chopra, Katrina Kaif, Bhumi Pednekar, Ayushmann Khurrana party it up together with their B-town friends

23 Nov 2019, 05:40 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

B-town was buzzing Friday night with some of the biggest stars in the business, zooming around town to attend glitzy parties. Actresses Priyanka Chopra, Katrina Kaif, Bhumi Pednekar were spotted bringing on their fashion A-game as they attended a house party that also saw a host of other celebs in attendance.

While Peecee added a pop of colour in a bright yellow crop top and matching pants, Katrina gave off major boss lady vibes in a coat dress that with a sexy lace frill. Bhumi opted for a shimmery suit that she paired with a bralet.

Ayushmann Khurrana and wife Tahera added some wow factor with their stylish appearance at the do. The actor, who was at the success bash of his latest film ‘Bala’, seemed to be in no mood to slow down. Joined by Rajkummar Rao, Patralekha, Aditi Rao Hydari, brother Aparshakti Khurana, the bunch posed together for the shutterbugs.

Also spotted at the big bash was Diana Penty, Athiya Shetty, Huma qureshi, Arpita Khan Sharma, Dino Morea and others.
