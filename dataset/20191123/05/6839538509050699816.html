Fremantle axe attack lands man in hospital

23 Nov 2019, 05:47

A 29-year-old East Perth man has fronted court over an alleged horror axe attack which left one man needing surgery on Friday night.

Dain Alan Wainwright Boogaard was in Perth Magistrates Court yesterday, charged with an act intended to cause grievous bodily harm and aggravated burglary after he allegedly slashed another man’s arm at a block of flats in Fremantle on Friday night.

Police allege Mr Wainwright Boogaard and the victim were arguing outside the block of flats on Hampton Road in Fremantle around 7.40pm.

According to police, the argument ended but Wainwright Boogaard returned to the victim’s flat armed with a tomahawk and attacked the victim.

The victim was taken to Royal Perth Hospital for surgery on his arm, where he remains in a stable condition.

Mr Wainwright Boogaard’s will be remanded in custody until December 20 when he will appear in Fremantle Magistrates Court.
