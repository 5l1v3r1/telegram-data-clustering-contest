Justin Bieber 'wants babies' with Hailey Baldwin as she 'turns him on in every way'

Justin Bieber has posted a gushing tribute to his wife Hailey Baldwin, confessing that he wants children with her as soon as possible

23 Nov 2019, 05:16 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Justin Bieber has paid tribute to his wife, Hailey Baldwin, in a gushing post for her birthday.

The 25-year-old singer has celebrated the 23rd birthday of Hailey by telling her they she 'turns him on' and that he can't wait to 'have babies' with her.

Biebs shared a split photo on Instagram of the pair at the their second wedding ceremony on September 30.

In one of the images, the pair are locked lips in a passionate kiss, while Hailey wears her spectacular wedding gown and Justin dons his white tux.

In the second photo, Hailey is seen looking over her shoulder while in her wedding dress, with the look being captured through a dramatic black and white filter.

Justin captioned the pics with his tribute to Hailey and his request to start a family.

He wrote: "Happy birthday babes. You make me want to be better everyday! The way you live you life is so attractive.. ps you turn me on in every way."

Justin concluded the post with: "next season BABIES"

The pair were legally married in a courthouse ceremony in September 2018.

They then went all out with a spectacular bash this year so that they could celebrate their coupling with family and friends.

A source close to the pair told Hollywood Life last week: "Hailey loves that she and Justin went all out for their wedding which really made it feel even more official being surrounded by their friends and families.

"It’s also been really nice enjoying this next phase of their relationship and lives together. They have started settling into what it really feels like to be together as a married couple.

"All the distractions of wedding planning, the early phases of dating, moving in together etc. have all been accomplished and it’s a more regular, day-to-day routine now which has been really nice."
