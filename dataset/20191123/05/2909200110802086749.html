2020:You will lose disgracefully in MP race – John Dumelo told

23 Nov 2019, 05:59 by MyNewsGH

Controversial Radio owner, DJ Oxygen has advised celebrity turn politician, John Dumelo to abort the dream of becoming a Member of Parliament(MP) as he is set to lose disgracefully in the 2020 polls.

According to him, the actor will not poll more than three votes on December 7, 2020 so to avoid the humiliation, he should focus on his acting career and forget about becoming an MP

He made this known when he spoke on Accra-based Rainbow Radio.

He noted that John Dumelo has started making big promises on social media which should not have been the case because that will be his down fall.

Dj Oxygen advised that John Dumelo gives up the dream of becoming an MP and focus on making gains from his acting career.
