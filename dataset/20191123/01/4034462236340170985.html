2020 Infiniti Edition 30 Model Pricing Announced

Infiniti is celebrating its 30th anniversary at the LA Auto Show with the Edition 30 collection.

23 Nov 2019, 01:15 by Martin Bigg

Back in August at Monterey Car Week, Infiniti unveiled its new Edition 30 range to celebrate the company's 30th anniversary. Now, the commemorative models are making their motor show debut at the 2019 LA Auto Show.

The Edition 30 lineup comprises of Infiniti's best-selling models: the Q50, Q60, QX50, QX60, and QX80. Each model includes new standard safety tech that's usually offered as part of the company's ProAssist package, including intelligent cruise control, distance control assist, around view monitor, backup collision intervention and predictive forward collision warning.

In addition, each Edition 30 model features distinctive design touches unique to the collection. Three exterior colors are available: Black Obsidian, Graphite Shadow and Majestic White, combined with a minimalist interior featuring handcrafted materials. Each model also boasts special "Edition 30" exterior badging and stylish new 19-inch alloy wheels for the Q50 and Q60 and larger 20- or 22-inch alloys for the QX50, QX60 and QX80 finished in dark chrome.

"For our 30th Anniversary, we've been focused on looking 30 Years Forward while celebrating the momentous technological and design innovations of our past and present," said Jeff Pope, Group Vice President at Infiniti Americas. "This includes our Edition 30 line of vehicles, which we are honored to showcase at the Los Angeles Auto Show this year."

To mark the Edition 30's auto show debut, Infiniti has also announced pricing details for the commemorative models. The Q50 Edition 30 starts at $43,700, while the Q60 starts at $49,700. The QX50, on the other hand, starts at $45,300, while the QX60 starts at $50,850 and the QX80 starts at $66,750. Each model includes a complimentary ProAssist package.

The Infiniti Edition 30 models will be on display at the 2019 Los Angeles Auto Show from November 22 to December 1. Also on display at the Infiniti stand will be the Project Black S and the Qs Inspiration concept.
