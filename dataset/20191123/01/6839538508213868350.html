Police investigate armed robbery at Bunnings Warehouse in Launceston

23 Nov 2019, 01:32

Tasmania Police is calling for potential witnesses to an alleged armed robbery at a Bunnings Warehouse, in the state’s North.

Tasmania Police allege a man driving a white Mitsubishi Triton utility stole items from the Bunnings store in Lindsay St, Invermay, about 6pm Friday.

As the man was leaving the store, he was asked for a receipt by a male staff member.

It was then the man is alleged to have produced a small knife, threatening the staff member, before then leaving the scene.

No one was injured in the incident.

Police would like to speak with anyone with information, particularly anyone who saw a man driving a white Mitsubishi Triton utility in the area at the time of the incident.

Anyone with information should contact Launceston Police on 131444 or report

anonymously to Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000 or crimestopperstas.com.au
