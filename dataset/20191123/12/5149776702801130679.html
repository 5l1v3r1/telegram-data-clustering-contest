Police want to speak to this man after iPad was stolen

The incident happened on October 26 at Carter Gate in Newark at around 11am.

23 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Ben Reid

Police have released a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to in connection with the theft of an iPad.

The theft happened on October 26 at Carter Gate in Newark at around 11am.

Officers are now appealing to the public to get in touch if they have any information.

If you recognise him or think you can help, please call Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident number 450 of 26 October 2019.
