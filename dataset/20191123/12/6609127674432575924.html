Worried family issue desperate Facebook appeal to find missing Fife man George Brownlie

23 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Kirsty Feerick

A WORRIED family have issued a desperate Facebook appeal to find a missing Fife man.

George Brownlie vanished from the Townhill area leaving his loved ones distraught.

One concerned family member took to social media in frantic bid to find him, which has been shared over a thousand times.

They begged the public to keep an eye out for George as his disappearance is very out of character.

She said: "He is from the Townhill area and this is very out of character from him.

"He is usually seen in the Townhill area regularly near the library, the park, cost cutters and so on.

"Urging all people to please and I beg please keep an eye out for him as we are all worried sick.

"He sometimes goes out walking in the area but has not been seen in a few days now.
"Can people please share this and if anyone sees him or thinks they have seen him get in Contact with myself or Dunfermline police station right away.

"I pray your safe and are found soon uncle George all my love."

A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: "We can confirm George Brownlie is a missing person."
