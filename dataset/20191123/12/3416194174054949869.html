Sydney Sierota's Husband Cameron Quiseng Guest Stars in Echosmith's 'Shut Up & Kiss Me' Video - Watch!

23 Nov 2019, 12:30

Sydney Sierota recruited her real-life husband Cameron Quiseng for the new Echosmith music video!

The duo, who tied the knot earlier this year, star in the “Shut Up and Kiss Me” video and it’s too cute!

In the vid, Sydney tries to catch the attention of Cameron, who isn’t picking up on the hints that she’s into him!

“This song is so me. I fully believe in being forward with how you feel and not playing games. The beginning of a relationship is so fun and romantic but sometimes all you wanna say is ‘Shut up and kiss me already!’” Sydney said in a statement.

The video also features some hilarious clips with Sydney’s brothers and band mates Graham and Noah!
