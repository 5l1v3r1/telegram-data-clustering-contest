Why England management were 'unhappy' with Jofra Archer during New Zealand Test

23 Nov 2019, 12:33

The England management were ‘unhappy’ with Jofra Archer and ‘rebuked’ the fast bowler after he jumped on a segway on the outfield before the start of the first Test against New Zealand.

Archer was seen using a broadcaster’s segway as the teams prepared for the opening day’s play in Tauranga on Wednesday evening.

England fans have become accustomed to the Ashes and World Cup star’s somewhat casual warm-ups, with Archer often bowling left-arm spin moments before the start of matches.
