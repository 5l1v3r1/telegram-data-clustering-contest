Barcelona coach Valverde switches to 4-2-3-1 formation at Leganes!

Messi, Dembélé, Griezmann and Suárez all start for Barça at Butarque

23 Nov 2019, 12:17 by SPORT.es

Ernesto Valverde has named an attacking side for Barcelona's game at Leganes on Saturday, with four forwards included in a 4-2-3-1 formation.

Lionel Messi, Antoine Griezmann and Ousmane Dembélé all start behind Luis Suárez at Butarque as the Barça coach mixes things up.

There is also a start for Moussa Wague at right-back with Nelson Semedo (injury) and Sergi Roberto (suspended) missing. Junior Firpo continues on the left side with Jordi Alba injured.

Sergio Busquets and Frenkie de Jong are tasked with controlling the midfield, while Gerard Pique starts at the back despite a week occupied by the Davis Cup.

Full team: Ter Stegen, Wague, Pique, Umtiti, Junior, Busquets, De Jong, Messi, Griezmann, Dembele, Suarez
