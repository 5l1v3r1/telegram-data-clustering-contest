Watch Crystal Palace vs. Liverpool online – Live Streams and Worldwide TV Info

23 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Joanna Durkan

Liverpool return to Premier League action with a trip to Crystal Palace this afternoon. Here’s how to watch on TV around the world.

The Reds return to Premier League action at Selhurst Park looking to maintain their stranglehold atop of the table.

Jurgen Klopp‘s side are on the hunt for their 12th win in 13 games, and a further three points would place pressure on a Man City side who host Chelsea in the late kickoff on Saturday – ensuring at least one team in the chasing pack is set to drop points.

There are concerns over a number of Liverpool players, however, with Mohamed Salah and Andy Robertson headlining the list of injuries ahead of the Reds’ trip to London, with a late decision expected to be made over the duo’s ability to feature.

Roy Hodgson’s Crystal Palace, meanwhile, head into the encounter off the back of three defeats in their last four league games.

And while Selhurst Park was once a destination which saw Liverpool stumble, the Reds have won their last five games at the stadium.
