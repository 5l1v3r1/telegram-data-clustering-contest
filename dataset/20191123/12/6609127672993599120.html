Aiden Lochrie missing – East Kilbride teen who vanished after leaving school found safe and well

23 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Kirsty Feerick

A MISSING schoolboy who vanished from East Kilbride yesterday has been found safe and well.

A desperate search was launched for Aiden Lochrie after he failed to return home from school.

He had last been seen at 2.45pm heading towards an underpass at Telford Road.

Concerns were growing for the 14-year-old.

But cops confirmed he has now been traced.

Lanarkshire Police Division wrote: "Aiden Lochrie, 14, who had been missing from East Kilbride since Friday, 22 November, 2019 has now been traced safe and well.

"Thanks to everyone who shared our post."

Inspector Mark McLachlan of East Kilbride Office previously said: “Aiden’s current whereabouts is unknown and our concern is growing due to his age and the passage of time as he has not been in contact with family.

"I would appeal to anyone who may have seen Aiden to contact us.

"Any small piece of information could help us find him.”
