Whitechapel stabbing: Man dies and three injured in horror knife attack

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

23 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

A man has died and three other men have been injured after a stabbing in east London.

A man in his 20s died at the scene in Buckle Street, Whitechapel, on Saturday morning, the Metropolitan Police said.

Two men have been arrested on suspicion of GBH.

Three other men with stab injuries were treated at the scene and then taken to hospital.
