Southbound US 41 Caloosahatchee Bridge is reopened to traffic

23 Nov 2019, 12:23 by WFTX Digital Team

The Fort Myers Police Department says all lanes on the Caloosahatchee Bridge have reopened after a rollover crash on Saturday caused all lanes to close.

The Fort Myers Police Department was on the scene of a rollover crash on Southbound US 41. The crash caused all lanes to close on the Caloosahatchee bridge leading into downtown Fort Myers. The southbound lanes are completely closed to traffic, and drivers were advised to use the Edison Bridge as an alternate route.

Police say there were minor injuries. No further information is available at this time.
