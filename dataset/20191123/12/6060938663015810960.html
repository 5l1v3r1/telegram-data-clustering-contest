Watch video: Madhuri Dixit’s family jam session will set the tone for your weekend

23 Nov 2019, 12:50 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The ‘Dhak Dhak Girl’ Madhuri Dixit is one of the most active celebs on social media. She is known to keep her audience hooked with her interesting and stunning pictures and clips, and today is no different. Today also Madhuri Dixit took to her social media handle to share an entertaining and engaging post. She shared a video of her trying her hand at guitar and jamming with family.

In the video in question, Madhuri with her husband Dr. Nene is jamming on the song ‘All Of Me’ and the jam session is sure to set the tone for your weekend. Sharing the video Madhuri wrote, “Family jam session! It was fun trying my hand at playing the guitar with @drneneofficial 'cuz "All of me, loves all of you..." ❤ #WeekendJam #AllOfMe @zacdsouzaa”.
