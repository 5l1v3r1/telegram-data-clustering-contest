Shabana Azmi’s Mother Shaukat Kaifi’s Funeral: Amitabh Bachchan, Rekha, Farhan Akhtar, Shibani At Last Rites

23 Nov 2019, 12:55 by Saheli Maity

Shabana Azmi's mother Shaukat Kaifi breathed her last yesterday. Her funeral, which took place today, was attended by Amitabh Bachchan, Rekha, Farhan Akhtar and Shibani Dandekar and others
