Rev Natasha Treated Like a Queen in Zimbabwe [PHOTOS]

23 Nov 2019, 12:13 by Samuel Mbataru

Popular Nairobi pastor Reverend Lucy Natasha took the gospel to Zimbabwe’s capital, Harare, in an event graced by First Lady Auxillia Mnangagwa.

A Facebook post on November 22, 2019, by the flamboyant pastor showed multitudes in attendance, including the high and the mighty in the Rhodesian land.

The reverend appreciated the presence of the first lady in the event, referring to her as a dedicated nationalist who brings inspiration.

“Delightful moments with the Her Excellency Auxillia Mnangagwa, the first lady of Zimbabwe. I celebrate her commitment to positively inspire the nation. God's grace abounds upon your life.

“You will blossom and experience fruitfulness. May God's favour make you a living testimony of God's goodness,” her post read.

In the gospel meeting, Reverend Natasha took to the podium with a message of embracing a billionaire mindset, a message she intends to preach in different nations during her world tour.

“Impacting generations through the word of God. May you walk in divine favour, wisdom, and breakthroughs. I see you testifying of the wonders of God upon your life,” read another of her post.

The Zimbabwe event, dubbed Oracle in Zimbabwe started on November 21 when the preacher arrived in a private jet.

Her convoy of high-end vehicles waded the streets with residents watching in amazement. 

“Such a blessing to have such a wonderful humble woman of God in Zimbabwe!!! God bless you Oracle, God bless Zimbabwe as well,” Patience Sephai remarked. 

"Thanks you Jesus! The church is marching on, Zimbabwe will remember the Lord their God. Shine for Jesus Mighty Woman of God! The Oracle anointed one," stated a fan.

“Wow, more grace to you Rev Natasha. He who honours a prophet receives a prophet's reward. Zimbabwe will receive immeasurable rewards,” wrote Thokozani Eunice Kunkeyani.

The minister of the word also informed of her oncoming Oracle Global Tour slated for 2020, with an ambition to reach different cities in the world.

Reverend Natasha is the founder and overseer of Prophetic Latter Glory Ministries International.
