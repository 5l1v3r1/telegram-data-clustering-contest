Avengers' Josh Brolin shares PDA snap with wife after Zoe Saldana comment

23 Nov 2019, 12:34

Avengers’ Josh Brolin has shared a sweet snap with his wife Kathryn after leaving a slightly awkward comment on his co-star Zoe Saldana’s picture.

He’s shrugging off the judgement from fans.

The Thanos star, who recently celebrated six years of sobriety, snapped a photo of his wife biting his mouth, as he held up her hair.

‘For reals. @kathrynbrolin,’ the actor wrote.

Fans and friends are loving the cute photo, with one writing: ‘That’s forever. Eternal,’ and another telling him: ‘Beautiful.’

Josh and his model beau tied the knot back in 2016, with the pair sharing their son Westlyn.

The photo comes after Josh left a slightly awkward comment on his on-screen daughter Zoe Saldana’s Instagram.

Zoe, who plays Gamora in the MCU, had us all in awe as she posted a stunning topless photo, writing in the caption: ‘Pre-everything….’

However, Josh, who stars as the Mad Titan, came in with the awkwardness as he commented: ‘Dang @zoesaldana.’ Fans weren’t about to let the comment pass them by, with one writing: ‘That’s your daughter bro lol.’

‘Stay away Thanos!’ another urged, with one more quipping: ‘Smells like alabama in here.’

However, other fans made sure to point out that Thanos and Gamora aren’t actually related by blood since he’s her adoptive father.

Meanwhile, bits of trivia are still being released about the final movie of the Infinity Saga, with it now being revealed that a battle involving Thanos’ army was cut.

In the new artbook for the movie, concept illustrator Josh Nizzi spoke about the prospect of squeezing in another fight. Alongside a string of drawings, he explained how it was hoped the purple one would be seen going up against a military alien race, reports Comicbook.com.

He said: ‘Marvel wanted to show how powerful Thanos was even before he had any of the Stones.

‘There was going to be a huge battle sequence where he fights a military alien race. The aliens actually did show up in the film but only for a few seconds before being killed by Gamora and Nebula.’
