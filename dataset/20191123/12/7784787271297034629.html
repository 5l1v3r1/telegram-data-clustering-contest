Perth Glory forward Bruno Fornaroli becomes the fastest player to reach 50 A-League goals

23 Nov 2019, 12:30

History is made.

Perth Glory forward Bruno Fornaroli has become the fastest player to reach 50 A-League goals thanks to his first half strike against Sydney FC.
