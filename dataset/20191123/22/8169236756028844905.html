Video: Gareth Bale jeered by Real Madrid fans after making return from Wales duty

23 Nov 2019, 22:13 by Tom McNeil

It will be interesting to see how long the Real Madrid fans decide to stay in the huff with Gareth Bale. Yes he did celebrate Wales qualifying with a flag that read “Wales-golf-Madrid – in that order” on it, but it was clearly a bit of fun.

It was also his chance to take a shot back at the Spanish press who have been so keen to put him down because of his love of golf.
