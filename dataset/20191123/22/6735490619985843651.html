Justin Timberlake And Alisha Wainwright​ Are ‘Just Friends’ After Photos Of The Two Emerge

23 Nov 2019, 22:39 by Jamie Samhan

On Thursday night, Justin Timberlake, Alisha Wainwright and fellow “Palmer” co-stars headed out to a bar in New Orleans where the two were spotted holding hands at one point.

In pictures obtained by The Sun, Timberlake and Wainwright were touching each other but a source has confirmed to ET Canada that it was all “innocent.”

“There was a group of people hanging out together,” the insider said. “Justin and Alisha are just friends, the evening was innocent.”

“She was just concerned for his well-being,” they added.

Video obtained by the tabloid shows Timberlake, who appears to be inebriated, being told that he should go home.

“At the end of night, he went home by himself,” the insider confirmed.

Timberlake is in the Big Easy to film “Palmer” about a college football star who returns to his hometown after serving time in prison.

The singer has been married to Jessica Biel since 2012, they share son Silas, 4.
