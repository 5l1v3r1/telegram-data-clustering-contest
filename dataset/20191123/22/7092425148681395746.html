Dublin bar posts job advert for 'experienced snowflake'

23 Nov 2019, 22:38 by James Cox

A Dublin bar owner has posted a hilarious job advert for an 'experienced snowflake'.

Are you self righteous, over confident and over sensitive? Then maybe it's the role for you!

Oscars Cafe Bar in Smithfield posted the job advert on Facebook, adding that if it offends you then you have the job already.

Some of the key points include;

• Extensive experience in using a smartphone required, in fact we expect our candidate to have a smartphone attached to their hand at all times and be able to get through the working day without looking up from phone.

• Be adept at taking selfies throughout the working day. Ability to disregard one’s own safety or that of colleague in the quest of a perfect “selfie” would be desirable. Additionally, being completely insensitive will help, for example taking selfies at funerals, car accidents and any type of personal tragedy

• A thorough knowledge of all current and emerging social media platforms is essential. Your social media “friends” will ideally be like minded individuals who can support your outrage when leaving comments.
