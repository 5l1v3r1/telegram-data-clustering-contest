Police searching for elderly man missing from Logan

23 Nov 2019, 22:48 by Matt McDonald

Police are appealing for help from the public to track down an elderly man missing from the Logan area.

73-year-old Heikki Nieminen has been missing since around 2pm on Saturday afternoon.

He was last seen leaving a home on Turill St in Shailer Park and hasn’t been heard from since.

Police believe he may have travelled to Brisbane on public transport.

Concerns are held for his welfare as he has a medical condition and may be disorientated.

Heikki is described as Caucasian, around 175cm tall with grey hair and blue eyes.

He was last seen wearing a blue and white polo shirt.

Police are urging anyone who sees him to contact them.

If you have information for Police, please contact Policelink on 131 444 or provide information using their online form 24 hours per day.

You can also report information about crime anonymously to Crime Stoppers by calling 1800 333 000 or via crimestoppersqld.com.au 24 hours per day. Crime Stoppers is a registered charity and community volunteer organisation.
