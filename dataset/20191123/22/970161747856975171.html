Two dead after car crashes into pedestrians in Norwich

23 Nov 2019, 22:44

Two men have been killed and another left with life-changing injuries after a car crashed into pedestrians in Norwich.

The incident occurred on Friday evening at around 5pm on Dereham Road, near Norfolk Showground.

The driver of the Subaru Legacy, a man in his 30s, and a male pedestrian in his 40s were pronounced dead at the scene.

Another pedestrian, a woman in her 50s, was taken to Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital with life-changing injuries.

She is in a serious condition.

Emergency services attended the scene, along with an air ambulance, as the road was closed for more than eight hours.

Norfolk Police are keen to hear from anybody who was in the area at the time and may have dashcam footage.
