I'm A Celebrity's Jacqueline Jossa in tears over trial and says she doesn't want to do show anymore

I'm A Celebrity contestant Jacqueline Jossa was left in tears by hosts Ant & Dec over news of the latest Bushtucker Trial

23 Nov 2019, 22:39 by Simon Duke

There was drama during Saturday night's I'm A Celebrity when Jacqueline Jossa broke down live on air.

It would be fair to say that the ex-Eastenders actress has struggled a bit with the trials on this year's show and it was news of the latest one that reduced her to tears in front of millions of viewers.

While the majority of each episode of I'm A Celebrity is recorded footage, Ant & Dec's links are live as is the bit each day when they go into camp to reveal who the public have voted to take on the Bushtucker Trials.

And it was the reveal that she would be facing Sunday's trial alongside Ian Wright that prompted Jacqueline cry, as you can see in the footage above taken from ITV, with her campmates rushing to console her as she could be heard saying "I don't want to do it anymore."

The latest trial is called Crevis of Cruelty and will see Ian and Jacqueline search a series of underground tunnels and chambers for vital stars, as water rises around them.

As always, they will be joined by all sorts of creepy crawlies with spiders, snakes and baby crocodiles among the critters they will encounter.

"Two celebrities are about to get down and dirty in an underground cavern where a world of woes await," Ant & Dec said in the teaser video, adding "The stars are in reach, but who can keep their head while the crawl through the Crevice of Cruelty?"
