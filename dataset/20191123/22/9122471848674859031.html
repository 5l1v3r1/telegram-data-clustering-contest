Micah Parsons Issues Promise After Loss To Ohio State

23 Nov 2019, 22:17 by Andrew McCarty

The Penn State Nittany Lion’ hopes of making the College Football Playoff were dashed on Saturday afternoon. No. 2 Ohio State was just too tough on defense for the Penn State offense as the Buckeyes walked out with a 28-17 win.

It’s the third-straight win for Ohio State over Penn State.

After the game, star linebacker Micah Parsons had a message for the Nittany Lions faithful.

“Don’t give up on us I promise we gonna go harder! Ima try harder to bring it back for us!” he said on Twitter.
