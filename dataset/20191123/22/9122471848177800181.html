Justin Fields Posts Message After Win vs. Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 22:58 by Andrew McCarty

Ohio State’s undefeated season rolled on this weekend with a huge win over Penn State.

The Buckeyes defense dominated for much of the day and escaped a late Nittany Lions comeback attempt to walk out with a 28-17 win. With the win, Ohio State clinches the Big Ten East and a spot in the Big Ten title game.

Despite the win, there was a worrisome moment in the final minutes of the fourth quarter. Star quarterback Justin Fields was tackled awkwardly and appeared to be hurt on the play.

However, he bounced right back up and hobbled off the field. After the game, head coach Ryan Day said Fields is “good” and shouldn’t miss any time with an injury.

A few hours after the game ended, Fields sent a message for the Ohio State faithful.

“Love this team and Buckeye Nation. Gotta keep getting better each and everyday! #GoBucks,” Fields said on Twitter.
