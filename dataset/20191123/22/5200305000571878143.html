‘Bigg Boss 13’: Khesari Lal Yadav latest to leave reality show

23 Nov 2019, 22:15 by BizAsia Correspondent

Khesari Lal Yadav has become the latest contestant to exit the ‘Bigg Boss 13’ house.

In Saturday night’s episode, ‘Bigg Boss’ requested housemates to gather in the living area to name a contestant proving the least entertainment. Yadav received the most votes, leading to his eviction.

Upon learning about his departure, a number of contestants expressed their emotions including Siddharth Shukla, Shehnaz Gill and Vishal Aditya Singh.

Incidentally, Yadav is the third wild card contestant to be evicted after Arhaan Khan and Tehseen Poonawalla.
