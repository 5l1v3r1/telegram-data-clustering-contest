Brandi Passante Puts Curvaceous Derriere On Display In Snug Jeans With Crossed Legs

Trisha Faulkner

All of her Instagram followers’ eyes were on Brandi Passante’s curvaceous derriere as the Storage Wars queen sat in a Victorian-style chair with her legs crossed while rocking a snug pair of blue jeans.

The photo featured the blonde bombshell in a Gypsi Tearoom as she appeared to be having her fortune read. Brandi tagged several of her friends who were presumably hanging out with her at the time before noting that she saw a “hangover” in everyone’s future.

Passante sat with one leg crossed over the top of her other as she had her back toward one of the chair arms with the actual back of the chair off to the side of her. The combination of the odd position she sat in and her snug jeans allowed her to flaunt her curvy form for the camera.

In the fashion department, Brandi paired her snug blue jeans with a black t-shirt and a white jacket that appeared to be sliding off of her shoulders. Passante had her eyes squeezed shut in the snapshot as she held hands with another blonde who was sitting in the same strange position.

The other woman in the photo, however, had her knees pressed together instead of crossing one leg over the other.

The snapshot oozed Victorian Era vibes as the chairs the two woman sat on as well as the small stool under the table looked as though they’d been plucked from that Era.

In less than 24 hours, her followers showered the photo with over 3,000 likes and just shy of 50 comments.

As The Inquisitr reported back in September, rumors swirled on social media that Brandi and her Storage Wars king Jarrod Schulz were no longer an item. While the end of their relationship was never official confirmed. Passante has been steadily having a wonderful time surrounded by those she cares about on Instagram. Notably, however, Jarrod hasn’t been anywhere in sight.
