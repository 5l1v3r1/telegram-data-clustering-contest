Alex Scott and Neil Jones stuck together after Strictly wardrobe ‘malfunction’

The couple could not untangle themselves after their fiery Argentine tango to the aptly-picked song Never Tear Us Apart.

23 Nov 2019, 22:00

Neil Jones had to carry Alex Scott across the dancefloor when their clothes became attached after their routine on Strictly Come Dancing on Saturday night.

The pair performed a fiery Argentine tango to Bishop Briggs’s version of Never Tear Us Apart, ending with a tight embrace and her leg around his waist.

However, instead of walking separately to the judges after the dance, Jones lifted the former footballer into his arms and carried her over to the panel.
