Iggy Azalea & Playboi Carti’s Home Robbed While She Was Home Alone

23 Nov 2019, 22:55 by Suzy Kerr

A burglar hit Iggy Azalea and Playboi Carti’s 6,500-square foot rental mansion in the exclusive Atlanta suburb of Buckhead last weekend, and they successfully got away with approximately $366,000 in jewelry, according to a new report. Azalea – AKA Amethyst Kelly – was home alone on Sunday night, November 14th, when the thief showed up. However, she did not report the robbery until three days later.

The Atlanta Journal Constitution reports that the burglar stole 19 pieces of jewelry, including diamond rings, luxury watches, and platinum bracelets. In the incident report, Azalea says she unlocked a door for Carti at around 9:50 p.m. because it was raining outside, and she believes that’s how the burglar got into the home.

The Australian rapper says she was on the first floor of the house when she heard footsteps on the second floor, but she thought it was Playboi Carti, AKA Jordan Carter. The couple eventually noticed that a designer bag that they keep their jewelry in was missing from the second floor dining room.

Luckily, the home does have outdoor security cameras, and Azalea gave police surveillance footage of the suspect walking into the house. The video, which has not yet been released to the public, shows a man wearing gloves and a dark mask – and also carrying a gun – walking into the home through an unlocked door in the back.
