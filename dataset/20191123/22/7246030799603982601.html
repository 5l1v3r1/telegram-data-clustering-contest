60 Terror Suspects Captured, Illegal Weapons & Terrorist Funding Seized

23 Nov 2019, 22:05 by Hana Levi Julian

Israeli security forces carried out a massive joint counter terrorism raid throughout Judea and Samaria this weekend.

As part of the effort to keep residents safe, IDF fighters and Shin Bet security agents, Border Guard Police and Israeli Police officers combined forces in an operation to capture 60 wanted terror suspects this weekend.

The suspects are accused of involvement in terrorist operations, terrorist activities and violence against civilians and Israeli security forces. The detainees were transferred to security personnel for interrogation.

In addition, nine weapons and ammunition were seized during searches to track down illegal weapons in the Ephraim, Etzion, Binyamin and Menashe brigades, according to the IDF. The findings were passed on via Shin Bet intelligence agents.
