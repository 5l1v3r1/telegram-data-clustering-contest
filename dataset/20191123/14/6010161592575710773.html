Hellbound is a ’90s throwback inspired by shooters such as Doom and you can play the demo

23 Nov 2019, 14:51 by Stephany Nunneley

If you’re looking for a ’90s shooter throwback, maybe you should give Hellbound a try.

Hellbound, the fps inspired by shooter classics such as Doom, Quake, Duke Nukem 3D, Blood, and others, has a demo available.

According to the developers, Argentina-based Saibot Studios, you play as a “huge-ass mother-f*cker” who is killing demons in Hell.

Going by the trailer, it definitely has a Doom vibe to it, and you’ll notice a tongue-in-cheek comment regarding Doom Eternal’s delay.
