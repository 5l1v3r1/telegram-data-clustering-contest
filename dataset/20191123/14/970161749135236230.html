Klopp explains why Salah is benched for Liverpool's clash vs Crystal Palace

23 Nov 2019, 14:48

Jurgen Klopp admits it was not a difficult decision to bench Mohamed Salah for Liverpool’s clash with Crystal Palace on Saturday.

Salah has been struggling with an ankle injury for several weeks and the forward did not link up with Egypt during the international break.

Klopp is confident that Salah will not require surgery to fix his ankle problem but the Liverpool manager admits he is carefully managing the player’s recovery.

And for Liverpool’s trip to Crystal Palace on Saturday afternoon, Salah dropped to the bench and was replaced by Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain in the starting line-up.

Asked if it took a lot of thinking to play Oxlade Chamberlain ahead of Salah, Klopp told Sky Sports: ‘No, during the week a little bit because we didn’t know exactly how Mo would come back or how especially Oxlade would come back.
