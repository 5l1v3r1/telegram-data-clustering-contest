BTS’ Jungkook's videography skills get highly applauded by UK’s Independent Film Festival, Raindance

23 Nov 2019, 14:37 by dose-nel

BTS’ Jungkook has caught the eye of professional film makers as they posted a congratulatory tweet under a chart account for his spotify record. Raindance Film Fest went on to praise him as a "filmmaker with great skills" and also revealed that they've watched his GCFs.They then shared their favourite GCF made by him which was the "3J 2018 MMA Practice". Raindance Film Festival is a prestigious international independent film festival launched in 1993, attended by thousands annually. Its impressive to note that Jungkook is recognised widely by professionals even for his hobbies.
