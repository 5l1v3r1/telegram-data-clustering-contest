L'Art de l'Automobile Releases Reversible Crewneck for the Streetwear-Petrolhead

Subtle, bold to bold, subtle.

23 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Eddie Eng

Following up on its Dover Street Market NY exclusive capsule, lifestyle brand L’Art de l’Automobile releases imagery for its next drop — a reversible logo crewneck.

Clad in black, the reversible sweater arrives in a boxy cut crewneck style that proudly displays the brand’s circular emblem on the rear. The first way to wear it utilizes the brand’s font and branding at the breast and the large circular emblem — boldly displaying Arthur Kar’s favored MKI Volkswagen Golf GTI — at the back. The inside version is subtler, with just a breast-location “KAR” logo. Both iterations create a staple piece for all street wear-automotive aficionados. Ribbed cuffs at the wrists and waist give a snug fit, while oversized elastic at the neck will provide a longer lasting durability while putting-on and taking-off.

The L’Art de l’Automobile reversible crewneck sweater will release on L’Art de l’Automobile’s online shop on November 23, while official stockists will receive the sweater the following week.

Elsewhere in automotive, Tesla has revealed its Cybertruck design, one reminiscent of a Mars rover vehicle.
