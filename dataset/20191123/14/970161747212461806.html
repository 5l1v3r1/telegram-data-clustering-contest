Liam Payne totally smitten as he cosies up to girlfriend Maya Henry

23 Nov 2019, 14:11

Liam Payne looked happier than ever as he cosied up to girlfriend Maya Henry after shutting down rumours over her age.

And the Strip That Down singer couldn’t wipe the smile from his face while cuddling the 19-year-old model, during an appearance at the Business of Fashion Presents VOICES 2019 Gala Dinner, in Oxfordshire.

The 26-year-old rocked a pinstripe suit, complete with a white shirt and black tie, at the swanky bash – in honour of Vogue editor Edward Enninful.

While the model looked every inch the proud girlfriend, in a stunning a lime green, strapless floor-length gown.

Their appearance comes after the former One Direction star was forced to hit out at speculation Maya had lied about her age, with many claiming she was actually a year younger.

Super sleuths on the internet have other ideas dug up old Instagram pictures and an article written about her lavish quinceanera (15th birthday) celebrations back in 2016 – with a fan suggesting she is actually 18-years-old.

‘Every article about Liam’s girlfriend says she’s 19 when she’s actually 18,’ the viral Twitter thread read.

‘This is intentional because when fabricating this ridiculous PR campaign for her they said that they’ve been together for a year, which would mean that Liam was dating a 17 y/o. She’s a senior in [High School].’

Hitting back, the dad-of-one swiftly poured ice-cold water on the claims, replying: ‘19 don’t believe everything you read on the internet.’

Noted.

Liam recently lifted the lid on their relationship – and melted our hearts at the same time – when he praised his girlfriend for always keeping a huge smile on his face.

Alongside an Instagram snap of the pair cuddling on the sofa, he said: ‘Sometimes I don’t recognise this happy guy… sure glad you brought him back though.’

After thanking his team for their help, he added: ‘Then last but not least this one @maya_henry for always keeping the biggest smile on my face through all the stress and making me realise how perfect my life really is.

‘Love you all let’s stack this s**t up.’
