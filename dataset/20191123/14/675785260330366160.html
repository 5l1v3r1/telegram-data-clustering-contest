Helen Flanagan claps back at troll who criticised her 'honest mum' moaning

The Coronation Street star was sent abuse by an angry Instagram follower - but she didn't take the criticism lying down

23 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Mirror .co.uk

Helen Flanagan hit back at one of her fans after they criticised her for complaining about her own children.

The 29-year-old Coronation Street star lashed back after one follower grew tired of her repeatedly blaming her stresses on her two children.

Helen has two daughters with her 30-year-old footballer boyfriend, Celtic winger Scott Sinclair - with four-year-old daughter Matilda and second daughter Delilah, born in June 2018.

But the soap star found herself under attack after complaining about her offspring on Instagram Stories.

“Stop ranting about ur kids!” the disgruntled follower wrote in a direct message that Helen later shared for all to see.

“Buckle up and get on with it!!! There are some people who are unable to conceive then u have people like urself moaning!” the angry follower continued.

“It’s part of the process get on with it and stop RANTING! Which is what your doing,” the follower angrily concluded.

But Helen did not take kindly to the message and hit back at the follower - before sharing the exchange for all to see.

“Actually. I am honest on my instagram so other mums can identity with me and feel less on their own,” she replied.

“I am supportive of other women,” she added - while adding to the screen grab of the exchange: “Direct messages like this. I have friends very close to me who have struggled to conceive.”

The fiery exchange appeared to have been ignited after Helen shared two updates about her daughters testing her patience and causing her to stress eat.

“To get an peace at the moment I have to lock myself in the toilet… any other mums do the same?” the TV star mused as she shared a photo of herself online.

“Delilah screamed so much in the car today I was so stressed I had to stop at Costa drive thru and inhale this,” she wrote in a second story post alongside a photo of a muffin.

However, the argument with the fan may have hit a nerve as she later shared a snap of her eldest daughter with the caption: “I’m not perfect but I love you so darling.”
