Man stabbed near Perry Barr train station

A man was taken to hospital after being stabbed near Perry Barr train station.

23 Nov 2019, 14:25 by Jordan Reynolds

The victim, aged in his 20s, was attacked just before 6.40pm yesterday.

His injuries are not thought to be life-threatening, police said.

Officers are investigating and appealing for anyone with information to come forward by calling 101.

A spokesman for West Midlands Police said: "Police were called to Perry Barr train station just before 6.40pm yesterday to reports that a man had been stabbed nearby.

"The victim, in his 20s, has been taken to hospital. His condition is not believed to be life-threatening or life-changing.

"Anyone with information has been asked to contact officers via Live Chat at west-midlands.police.uk, via 101, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
