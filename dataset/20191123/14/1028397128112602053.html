Cyprus: Has CyBC internally chosen Sandro Nicolas for Rotterdam?

23 Nov 2019, 14:24 by Stratos Agadellis

Who’s gonna be the one? Who will fly our flag onstage? Probably this question has been of concern to the managers of the Cypriot broadcaster CyBC for a long time now, as it all seems that we are soon to find out who will succeed Tamta on the stage of our favorite competition. Or do we already know?

The story behind

According to reports from both Cypriot and Greek media, after two powerful female performances, with successful placings on the final scoreboard (Eleni Foureira and Tamta), CyBC was rather desirous of a male artist to represent them in Rotterdam. Despite the initial proposal to rising singer Ian Stratis, things didn’t work out and thus, other names fell on the table.

Greek TV channel Star also suggests that CyBC had also approached Ivi Adamou, who represented the island of Aphrodite in 2012, with the song La la love and finished in 16th place. However, the famous artist politely rejected the proposal, citing personal and family reasons.
