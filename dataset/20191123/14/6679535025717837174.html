Six persons to get Amma award

23 Nov 2019, 14:32 by Staff ReporterKALABURAGI

Matoshree Mahadevamma Nagappa Munnur Trust, Sedam, has released the list of people to be honoured with the Amma award for the year.

In a release, Ratnakala Mahipalreddy Munnur, convener of the trust, said that Jogi, Sudha Adukala, Prabhakar Satakhed, G.N. Nagaraj, Bhuvana Hiremath, and Channappa Katti, who have made significant contributions to art, literature and theatre, will be honoured with the award in Sedam on Tuesday.
