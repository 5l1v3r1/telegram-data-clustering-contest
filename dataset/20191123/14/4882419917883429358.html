Vidal scores fourth goal of the season to earn Barça win against Leganes

Javier Aguirre's squad let the match slip from their hands against Barça at Estadio Municipal de Butarque on Saturday

23 Nov 2019, 14:22 by SPORT.es

Leganés on home loss to Barça at Estadio Municipal de Butarque on Saturday. Leganés arrived with the intention of increasing their league score after a draw against Real Sociedad (1-1) while Barcelona came from winning 4-1 against Celta in the last match played. Following today's result, Leganés is 20th in the LaLiga, having garnered 6 points from 14 matches, while Barcelona sits at 1st with 28 points after playing a total of 14 matches.

An impressive opening from Leganés, thanks to a goal from Youssef En-Nesyri 12 minutes in the game, finalising the first half with a 1-0.

Barcelona started strong in the second half of the game soon, with a goal from Luis Suárez 53 minutes in the game. Later, Barcelona scored again and turned the match 1-2 thanks to a goal from Arturo Vidal in the 79th minute, and the game ended 1-2.

At the end of the match, both teams made 3 substitutions each. For Leganés Javier Eraso, Rodrigo Tarín and Guido Carrillo came on for Óscar, Kenneth Omeruo and Roque Mesa, while Barça brought on Ivan Rakitic, Arturo Vidal and Ansu Fati to replace Sergio Busquets, Antoine Griezmann and Frenkie de Jong.

The referee booked Roque Mesa from Leganés, while for Barcelona Sergio Busquets and Arturo Vidal saw a yellow card as well.

Barcelona and Leganés will play at away stadium against Atlético de Madrid and Sevilla respectively.
