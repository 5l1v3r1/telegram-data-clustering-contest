Centre Notifies The Appointment of Three Addl. Judges In Gauhati HC

23 Nov 2019, 14:53 by LIVELAW NEWS NETWORK

The President of India has appointed one Lawyer and Two Judicial Officers as Additional Judges of Gauhati High Court.

The appointees are

1. Shri Soumitra Saikia, Advocate;

2. Shri Parthivjyoti Saikia, Judicial Officer; and

3. Shri S. Hukato Swu, Judicial Officer.

The Supreme Court Collegium had recommended the names in a meeting held on October 15, 2019
