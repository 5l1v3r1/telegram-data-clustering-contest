Khloe Kardashian 'worried' after ex French Montana's rushed to hospital

French Montana has been admitted to an LA hospital for reportedly suffering stomach pains, nausea and an elevated heart rate after police called him an ambulance

23 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Lucy Domachowski

Khloe Kardashian is ‘’worried'' but ‘’in touch'' with her ex French Montana after he was rushed to hospital yesterday.

The 35-year-old rapper was rushed to the hospital after experiencing nausea, stomach pains and an elevated heart rate .

Officers in the Calabasas area, where French lives, are said to have responded to a call about a possible robbery at the rapper's home.

It was a false alarm - but it's reported the officers noticed something wasn't right.

French - real name Karim Kharbouch - is said to have been acting abnormally, with sources close to French telling TMZ that he'd been experiencing stomach pains, nausea and an elevated heart rate.

A source also told the website that he was admitted to San Fernando Valley Hospital, and that close pals believe he may have been exposed to contaminated food.

He was expected to be released early Friday, but he reportedly continues to experience stomach pains and remains in the hospital where doctors can continue to monitor his heart.

A source has revealed to E! that with the rapper still in hospital Keeping Up With the Kardashians star Khloe Kardashian felt inclined to “make sure he is going to be ok and see if he needs anything".

“She is still worried about him and hoping he gets some good news and they figure out what’s going on,” the insider revealed.

This likely means a lot to French Montana, who just this year said he and Khloe shared a “real” love.

In an August interview, the star said, “When the love was like that, it’s always going to be like that. Friendship after a relationship is something that’s real hard to do, and I’m glad we’ve been able to do it.”

The rapper's internationally renowned and respected, and his latest bout of travelling saw him jetting all over Europe and the Middle East.

French is known for a huge range of songs including Famous and No Stylist, in which he raps alongside Drake.
