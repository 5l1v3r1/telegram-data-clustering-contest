Man arrested in connection with robbery at Dundee newsagents

A 24-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a robbery at a Dundee shop.

23 Nov 2019, 13:16 by Jake Keith

The incident occurred at Keystore Newsagents on Dunholm Road on Friday at around midday.

The man has been kept in police custody and is due to appear at Dundee Sheriff Court on Monday.
