Christmas opening times and late night shopping dates in Burton town centre

Make the most of extended hours this festive period

23 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Ben Waldron

With the festival period only just round the corner, many people will be busily preparing for the special day.

One of the biggest things that can cause a big strain on time, and our wallets, is the all-important Christmas shopping.

Making sure you get that perfect present for your loved ones is an obsession in itself.

Bosses at Burton's three shopping centres have made it a little bit easier this Christmas by opening their doors for longer hours on select dates.

Make sure you know when you can access Burton Place, Coopers Square and The Octagon shopping centres this December.

Take note of the times and ensure you make the most of the shopping availabilities across Burton this Christmas period.

Burton Place shopping centre

Late-night opening will run up to 8pm on every Thursday from November 28 up to and including December 19.

It will also be open until 8pm on Friday, December 20 and Monday, December 23.

Christmas Eve: 7am to 5pm;

Christmas Day: Closed;

New Year's Eve: 8am to 5pm;

New Year's Day: Closed;

On all other days the centre will be open as normal.

These hours are simply for the actual centre, but each store will manage their own individual opening hours.

Coopers Square shopping centre

Late-night shopping will be in place at Coopers Square shopping centre from Thursday, November 28 until Thursday, November 19.

The extended hours will be from 8am to 8pm.

Friday, December 20: 8am - 8pm;

Monday, December 23: 8am - 8pm;

Christmas Eve: 7am - 5pm;

Christmas Day: Closed;

Boxing Day: 8am - 6pm;

New Year's Eve: 8am - 5pm;

New Year's Day: Closed.

The Octagon shopping centre

Thursday, November 28: 9am - 8pm;

Thursday, December 5: 9am - 8pm;

Thursday, December 12: 9am - 8pm;

Thursday, December 19: 9am - 8pm;

Friday, December 20: 9am - 8pm;

Saturday, December 21: 9am - 7pm;

Sunday, December 22: Normal Sunday hours;

Monday, December 23: 9am - 8pm;

Christmas Eve: 9am - 5pm;

Boxing Day: 9am - 5.30pm;

New Year's Eve: 9am - 5pm.
