What channel is Ohio State vs. Penn State on today? Time, schedule for Big Ten showdown

23 Nov 2019, 13:20 by James Crabtree-Hannigan

Some of the luster has been knocked off the Week 13 matchup between No. 2 Ohio State and No. 9 Penn State due to the Nittany Lions losing to Minnesota, but there's still a lot on the line in one of the biggest games on Saturday's schedule.

The clash in Columbus serves as a de facto Big Ten West title game. A Buckeyes victory would clinch the division, and if Penn State wins, all it would need is a win over lowly Rutgers next week to secure a spot in the Big Ten championship.

A one-loss Ohio State team that misses out on the conference championship game would be an interesting case for the Playoff committee, and a two-loss Nittany Lions team would be out of contention.

The Buckeyes have been universally dominant this season, their closest win being a 34-10 triumph over Michigan State. But a Penn State team with its back against the wall may post the biggest challenge yet.

Below is all the information you need to watch Ohio State vs. Penn State on Saturday, including kickoff time, TV channel and a full Week 13 college football schedule.
