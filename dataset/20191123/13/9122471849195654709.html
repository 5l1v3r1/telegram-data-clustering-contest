ESPN’s Kirk Herbstreit Names College Football’s Best 1-Loss Team

23 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Andrew Holleran

Three undefeated teams are the frontrunners for the College Football Playoff right now – LSU, Ohio State and Clemson. If all of those teams win out, they will be the top three seeds in the postseason.

The No. 4 spot is probably going to go to a one-loss team. There are several of them competing for that final spot.

ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit was asked to name his top one-loss team right now. He’s going with a team out of the SEC. It’s not Alabama, though.

“I think you’ve got to give Georgia the nod right now,” Herbstreit said.

Georgia is ranked No. 4 in the latest College Football Playoff rankings. Some, though, would give the nod to Alabama or Oregon.

Herbstreit added that if Georgia loses to LSU in the SEC Championship Game, it’ll open the door for someone else, namely the Pac-12 champion.

“If they end up losing the game, it opens up the discussion for the Big 12 champ, the Pac-12 champ and Alabama,” he said.

The ESPN college football analyst said he would probably give the nod to the Pac-12 champion in that case. Oregon and Utah both have one loss heading into the regular season’s final weeks.
