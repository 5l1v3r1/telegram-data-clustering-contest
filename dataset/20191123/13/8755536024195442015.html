Dublin footballer Cian O’Sullivan expecting first child with wife Danielle

Amazing news!

23 Nov 2019, 13:33 by Kendra Becker

Dublin footballer Cian O’Sullivan is expecting his first child with his wife Danielle.

The couple announced the news on Instagram by sharing a photo of Danielle posing alongside Cian, showing off her growing bump.

She simply captioned the post: “Mom and Dad.”

Cian and Danielle tied the knot in December 2018, in St Mary and Laurence Church in Athy.

The pair then celebrated their nuptials at the stunning Rathsallagh House in Dunlavin, surrounding by friends, family, and a host of well-known faces.
