Photo: Urban Meyer Reveals Special Ring He’s Wearing At Ohio State Today

23 Nov 2019, 13:22 by Andrew Holleran

Former Ohio State head coach Urban Meyer has made a habit of wearing some of his football rings on set of FOX’s Big Noon Kickoff. He’s wearing a special one today.

Meyer and the FOX college football pregame show are in Columbus this afternoon, ahead of today’s Ohio State-Penn State game.

The former Ohio State head coach is wearing a ring that celebrates his first Buckeyes team.

‘Wearing the 2012 (Ohio State) undefeated team ring today for (FOX) Big Noon Kickoff,” Meyer tweeted.
