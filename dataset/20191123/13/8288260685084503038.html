New role 'working a trick' for Aston Villa magician John McGinn, says Sky Sports pundit

Aston Villa news | McGinn has been in great form for Villa in the Premier League this season, scoring three goals in the top flight

23 Nov 2019, 13:00 by James Pallat

Dean Smith's decision to play John McGinn in a more advanced role for Aston Villa "is working a trick", says Sky Sports pundit Charlie Nicholas.

McGinn has been in great form for Villa in the Premier League this season, scoring three goals in the top flight.

His form has prompted speculation about a move to Manchester United.

And speaking ahead of Villa's Premier League clash with Steve Bruce's Newcastle United at Villa Park on Monday night (8pm), Nicholas said: "John McGinn is playing further forward and it is working a trick."

Nicholas is also predicting a Villa win over their former manager.

"Aston Villa need to nurture and control leads much better, and at times they leave themselves vulnerable," he added.

"That being said, I have to admire the way Dean Smith goes about it.

"Villa is also Bruce's old club and will sit in once again, but Villa will sneak it. There is enough about them to create enough chances."
