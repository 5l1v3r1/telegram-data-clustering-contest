Two minutes of madness dumps A-League classic between Adelaide United and Melbourne Victory on its head

23 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Staff Writers

The A-League rivalry between Adelaide United and Melbourne Victory suddenly burst into life with two minutes of absolute madness around the hour mark.

Two quickfire goals were scored at opposite ends of the pitch after Reds defender Michael Maria cancelled out Ola Toivonen’s equaliser — the score officially being level for less than a minute.
