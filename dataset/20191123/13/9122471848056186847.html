Tua Tagovailoa Has Message For Fans Before Today’s Alabama Game

23 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Chris Rosvoglou

Last weekend, the Alabama Crimson Tide were dealt a crucial blow to their team as Tua Tagovailoa went down with a dislocated hip. We’re only a week removed from that injury, but the southpaw signal-caller is already back in Tuscaloosa.

Alabama has a non-conference matchup today with Western Carolina. It should be a fairly easy test for Nick Saban’s squad.

Before the Crimson Tide take the field against the Catamounts this afternoon, Tagovailoa went on social media to address the fans that will be present for the school’s senior day.

Tagovailoa shared the following message on his Twitter account:

“Back in ttown! Thank you all for the prayers and support! Join us tomorrow in support of our seniors and our last home game of the season!!! Roll Tide”

Clearly, the superstar quarterback will not let his injury affect his mindset this season.

Even though Alabama will have to battle the rest of the year without him, Tagovailoa plans on supporting his teammates the rest of the way.
