Delta flight from Boston to L.A. made emergency landing in Syracuse

23 Nov 2019, 13:56 by EndPlay

BOSTON - A Delta flight from Boston to Los Angeles made an emergency landing in Syracuse on Friday night.

Flight 2531, a Boeing 757, landed safely at Syracuse Hancock International Airport just after 7:30 p.m. after declaring an emergency because of an engine problem.

The plane taxied to the gate.

The FAA is now investigating.
