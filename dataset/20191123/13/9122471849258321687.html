ESPN Computer Has 1 Top 25 Team On ‘Upset Alert’ Today

23 Nov 2019, 13:09 by Andrew Holleran

Week 13 of the 2019 college football regular season features one true notable matchup, with Ohio State hosting Penn State in Columbus this afternoon. Does this mean we’re going to get some upsets?

Perhaps. ESPN’s Football Power Index has one top 25 team on legitimate “upset alert” heading into today’s game.

The ESPN computer has the No. 14 team in the country on upset watch before kickoff.

No. 14 Baylor vs. Texas

Upset chance: 37.2 percent

No. 14 Baylor is coming off a disappointing home loss to Oklahoma last weekend, in which the Bears blew a 28-3 first half lead.

The Bears and the Longhorns are set to kick off at 3:30 p.m. E.T.

The game will be on FS1.
