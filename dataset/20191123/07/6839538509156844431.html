One dead, four hurt in Parry Creek Road Kununurra crash

23 Nov 2019, 07:58

One person has died and four others have received minor injuries after a crash in Kununurra.

It is believed the car was travelling along Parry Creek Road near Valentine Spring Road when it hit a tree about 1.42pm.

Four patients have been transferred by St John Ambulance to Kununurra Hospital.

It is not known what caused the crash.
