Kim Kardashian finally breaks silence on Caitlyn Jenner's I'm A Celebrity stint

23 Nov 2019, 07:25

Kim Kardashian has finally addressed Caitlyn Jenner’s stint in the I’m A Celebrity jungle, and it turns out she’s loving her on it just as much as we are.

Taking to Twitter, the 39-year-old quote-tweeted a video showing the moment the Olympian – who was previously married to momager Kris Jenner – gave a motivational speech to the campmates.

The 70-year-old could be seen telling her co-stars: ‘Let’s go team. Not just another day, another day to excel, another day to be great.

‘Rise and shine!’

And Kim revealed she had gone the extra mile and set the audio as her alarm.

‘I literally recorded this and set this as my alarm a few weeks ago,’ she told her 62.3million followers.

Not sure it’s going to catch on quite as much as Kylie Jenner’s Rise and Shine bop, but you do you, hun.

Kimmy’s tweet marks the first time any member of the famous family has spoken publicly about Caitlyn’s time in Australia, with fans wondering why they were staying so silent.

But Cait has been spilling a fair few nuggets of information about them all to her new best friends.

In fact, she lifted the lid on her relationship with Kanye West, and took a second to praise Kim’s business sense.

Lifting the lid on how the mum-of-four got the business bug, she recalled: ‘Kimberly never went to college. The minute she left high school she started businesses. She had her first business out of the closet.’

‘Kimberly would go into the ladies’ closets. Some of these are like 2,000 square feet – they’re huge. Beverly Hills ladies. ‘They figure out what they want to get rid of and Kimberly has an eBay site… Kimberly sells them for them and gets 30% commission.’

She went on to reveal her thoughts about the Jesus Walks rapper and added: ‘I like Kanye. Kanye was very, very good to me.’

However, Caitlyn might want to watch just how much tea she spills as she could face ‘huge fines’ for exposing all the Kar-Jenner secrets.

I’m A Celebrity continues daily, at 9pm, on ITV.
