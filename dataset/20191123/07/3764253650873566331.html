Portugal vs Nigeria: Abu Azeez scores as Super Sand Eagles suffer 10-1 defeat

23 Nov 2019, 07:46

- Portugal vs Nigeria's Beach Soccer World Cup game ended in 10-1 defeat for Sand Eagles

- Abu Azeez scored the only goal for Nigeria's Beach Soccer team in an embarrassing defeat

- Sand Eagles will face Oman in their next game on Sunday, November 24

Nigeria's Super Sand Eagles on Friday night, November 22, began their campaign at the 2019 World Cup with an embarrassing 10-1 defeat against Portugal.

Audu Adamu's side started the match impressively with the former Warri Wolves and Kwara United star Abu Azeez scoring the first goal for Nigeria.

But the Super Sand Eagles lost concentration after scoring the first goal and they went on to concede fives goals in the second period.

Nigeria came out aggressively in the third period looking for goals, but they were unable to stop high flying Portuguese side who added another five goals.
