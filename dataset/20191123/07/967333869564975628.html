Tread Softly: Little Golden Retriever Tries to Sneak Up on Big Doggo

23 Nov 2019, 07:30

Every canine is a natural-born hunter, but even the most talented dogs need a lot of training to become a dangerous predator. It is important to be fast, strong, and smart – and, of course, to be secretive and cunning.

This little Golden Retriever is learning how to be a ninja, so it tries to sneak up on a big doggo without being detected. And the attempt was almost successful – but the little guy totally forgot that the big retriever is watching its every step.
