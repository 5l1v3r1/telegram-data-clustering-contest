‘Pawarji tussi great ho’: Congress’s Singhvi after Fadnavis, Ajit Pawar take oath in Maharashtra

Senior Congress leader Abhishek Manu Singhvi blamed the duration of negotiations between his party, NCP and Shiv Sena for the developments in Maharashtra.

23 Nov 2019, 07:03 by Express Web Desk

Reacting to the sudden twist in government formation in Maharashtra, senior Congress leader Abhishek Manu Singhvi Saturday said he thought the developments were “fake news”, but blamed the duration of negotiations between his party, NCP and Shiv Sena.

“Candidly and personally speaking, our tripartite negotiations should not have gone on for more than 3 days… took too long. Window given was grabbed by fast movers. Pawarji tussi grt ho!” he tweeted this morning.
