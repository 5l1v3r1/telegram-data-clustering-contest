UPPRPB announces UP Police Constable document verification, admit card release dates

UP Police Constable recruitment 2019: A total of 31,360 vacancies for the post of reserved civil police and 18,208 for PAC posts are to be filled through this recruitment exam.

23 Nov 2019, 07:50 by Careers Desk

UP Police Constable recruitment 2019: The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB), Lucknow has announced to conduct the document verification (DV) for the post of reserve civil police and reserve PAC recruitment on November 28.

The admit cards for the first stage of the exam for D-01 to D-05 candidates will be uploaded on November 25 at the official website, uppbpb.gov.in. The information regarding the admit card of the rest of the candidates will be released later.

A total of 31,360 vacancies for the post of reserved civil police and 18,208 for PAC posts are to be filled through this recruitment exam. As per the official notification, the number of candidates shortlisted for document verification will be 2.5 times the number of vacancies.

Those who clear the document verification round will have to appear for a physical efficiency test (PET). Selected candidates will get a salary in the pay grade of Rs 5200 to Rs 20,200 an additional grade pay of Rs 2000 will also be granted.
