Priyanka Chopra Jonas And Vaani Kapoor Grooving To The Beats Of Song Ghungroo Is All Things LIT- Watch Video

23 Nov 2019, 07:20 by Gretel Sequeira

Priyanka Chopra Jonas has been in town lately and the actress is surely not missing out on the shimmery parties of B-Town. Friday night was all LIT for Vaani Kapoor and PeeCee as the stars grooved to the beats of song Ghungroo from the film WAR

Priyanka Chopra has been in town lately owing to her work commitments and the actress is making sure she makes the best of it. The actress was last in the country during the promotions of her film ‘The Sky Is Pink’ which was well acclaimed by the audiences but did not make great numbers at the Box-Office. While PeeCee is town once again, many Bollywood celebrities had a LIT Friday night as they partied all night long. As Priyanka was present at the party, the actress grooving to the beats of song Ghungroo from the film WAR along with Vaani Kapoor is unmissable.

Yesterday, Vaani Kapoor took to her Instagram stories to share a glimpse with fans from the Friday night party she attended. While the actress shared pictures and videos from the party, in a video shared by Vaani, the actress was spotted shaking a leg with Priyanka Chopra Jonas. The WAR actress captioned the video, “ Ain’t nobody like you PC!!,” and tagged the actress in it. As Vaani looked excited and thrilled to groove to the beats of song Ghungroo with PeeCee, the actress mentioned how dancing with PeeCee was ‘all love’.
