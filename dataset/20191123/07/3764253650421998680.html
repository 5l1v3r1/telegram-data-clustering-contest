Nigerians react to news of Air Peace boss Allen Onyema accused of fraud

23 Nov 2019, 07:26

- Air Peace boss, Allen Onyema, has been indicted by the US government for bank fraud and money laundering

- According to report, he has been charged with 27 counts of money laundering and 3 counts of bank fraud amongst other charges

- Many Nigerians have taken to social media to react to the news

Earlier on, Legit.ng reported the news of how the chairman, CEO, and founder of Air Peace, Allen Onyema was been indicted by the US government for bank fraud and money laundering by the US Department of Justice.

According to the reports, he has been charged with bank fraud and money laundering for moving more than $20 million from Nigeria through United States bank accounts in a scheme involving false documents based on the purchase of airplanes.

Following the report which has since gone viral, many Nigerians have taken to social media to react to the news. While there are people who have expressed doubt over the reports, others have expressed their disappointment.
