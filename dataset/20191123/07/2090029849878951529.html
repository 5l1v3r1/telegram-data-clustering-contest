Three children stolen in Anambra state rescued at the point of sale (Photo)

23 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Damilola Ismail

Three children stolen and trafficked from Enugu-Otu village, Anambra state have been rescued at the point of sale.

Following intelligence report Police detectives attached to Ogidi Division arrested two female suspects in Nkpor, Anambra State while they were attempting to sell three children they had stolen.

They were arrested on 22/11/2019 at about 1:30pm with three kids,(two boys and a girl ) whose age ranges between 1 and 5 years respectively while negotiating on how to sell the children.
