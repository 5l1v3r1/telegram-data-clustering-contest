Marjaavaan Box Office Day 8: Has A Drop On 2nd Friday, Expected To Go Up Again During The Weekend

Directed by Milap Zaveri, Marjaavaan stars Sidharth Malhotra and Tara Sutaria in the lead roles.

23 Nov 2019, 07:41 by Koimoi.com Team

After holding well during the weekdays, Marjaavaan had a drop in numbers on the second Friday as 1 crore* came in. On Thursday, the collections were 2.53 crores so even after a reduction in screens due to the arrival of Pagalpanti, it was expected that at least 1.25 crores would come in.

Nonetheless, the film now stands at 38.87 crores* and while 40 crores would be crossed today itself, it has to be seen how big is the jump that the Milap Zaveri directorial takes. Of course, at one point in time, it had seemed that the film would manage to come close to the 50 crores mark by end of the second weekend itself. However, that would now happen by end of the week, provided there is a good jump today and tomorrow.

The job is still done for the Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh, Tara Sutaria and Rakul Preet Singh starrer to emerge as a plus film at least. Of course, a bigger box office verdict would have been an even more heartening sign. Still, the fact that Marjaavaan has managed to reach this far is fair enough as well and all associated with the film would now be hearing up for their next one in the making.

*Estimates. Final numbers awaited

Note: All collections as per production and distribution sources
