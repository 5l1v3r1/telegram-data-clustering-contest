Samantha wishes husband Naga Chaitanya on his birthday: Soar higher and higher my love

On Naga Chaitanya's birthday, the makers of his upcoming film, tentatively titled NC19, shared a teaser giving a glimpse of his character in the film.

23 Nov 2019, 07:42 by Gabbeta Ranjith Kumar

On Naga Chaitanya’s birthday, his wife and actor Samantha conveyed her heartfelt birthday wishes through Instagram. In a beautiful message, she wrote, “Happy birthday my @chayakkineni ❤️ .. Every single day I pray for your happiness and I am so proud that with every passing day you have grown to be the best version of yourself .. and I believe with all my heart that we are stronger together ..I love you darling husband ❤️”

Samantha also wished him to “soar higher and higher” while congratulating him on the release of the first teaser of Chay’s next, tentatively titled NC19. Conveying her happiness on teaser glimpse, Samantha wrote, “So happy for you.. this looks beautiful and what an amazing team.. soar higher and higher my love. Happy birthday.”
