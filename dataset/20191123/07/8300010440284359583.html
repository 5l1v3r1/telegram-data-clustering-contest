LAFD crews encounter heavy smoke when responding to blaze at medical clinic in East Hollywood

23 Nov 2019, 07:56

EAST HOLLYWOOD, Calif. (KABC) -- Fire crews encountered heave smoke when they responded to a structure fire in East Hollywood Friday evening.

Over 100 firefighters were assigned to the blaze in the 1200 block of N. Vermont Avenue at a single story medical center. The location was the site of a previous burn and arson investigators were called in to investigate.

No other structures were threatened and there were no reports of injuries.
