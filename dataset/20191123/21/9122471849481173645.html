Nick Saban Reacts To Mac Jones’ Performance vs. Western Carolina

23 Nov 2019, 21:21 by Alek Arend

Tua Tagovailoa’s unfortunate season-ending injury last week against Mississippi State has opened the door for Alabama QB Mac Jones. The quarterback got his first start under center on Saturday against Western Carolina.

The Crimson Tide QB was nearly perfect, going 10-of-12 for 275 yards and three touchdowns. It was certainly an impressive start for the young quarterback.

Alabama head coach Nick Saban discussed Jones’ performance after the Crimson Tide’s 66-3 victory on Saturday. Saban kept his comments brief.

“Mac was efficient.” Saban said, via Mike Rodak of AL.com. “Made a lot of explosive plays. Maybe didn’t throw it as much.”
