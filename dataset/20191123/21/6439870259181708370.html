Truck runs through home in Gaston County

23 Nov 2019, 21:35 by WBTV Web Staff
Link to the video.
Car into Cramerton house

CHARLOTTE, N.C. (WBTV) - Gaston County emergency officials responded to a house in Cramerton Saturday afternoon.

After arriving to the scene on Main Street, firefighters saw that a truck had driven directly into the side of a home.

Gaston County EMS believes the driver had a medical episode - leading him to crash his vehicle.

The driver has been transported to CaroMont Regional Medical Center.

Dylan Windham lives in the house with his dad and brother.

Dylan said his dad was home and had just gotten up to switch on the lights in his aquarium on his way to the kitchen. Dylan said as his dad stopped at the aquarium, he heard a loud noise - like a gunshot - and hit the floor.

That’s when he noticed an F-150 in the room he’d just been sitting in.

Dylan said the driver seemed to be having a seizure.

Dylan had got off work 30 minutes early on Saturday, but said The Lord told him to stay and keep working, anyway.

Thankfully, no one in the house was injured.
