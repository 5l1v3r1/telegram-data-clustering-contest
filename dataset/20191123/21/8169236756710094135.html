Raheem Sterling tries to make light of another controversial VAR decision during Man City vs Chelsea

23 Nov 2019, 21:01 by Tom McNeil

It really has got to the point where there’s not much else you can say about VAR. Every weekend it’s become a talking point due to overturning some very marginal decisions.

It’s not even just the decisions that annoys the fans, it’s the length of time it takes to come up with any kind of ruling as well. A goal used to be pure joy, now it’s usually reduced to spending minutes watching a man with his finger to his ear and just waiting to be put out of your misery.
