Star Trek: Picard Teases Romulan Swordsman As Countdown To Release Begins

23 Nov 2019, 21:11 by Math Erao

A new motion poster for Star Trek: Picard teases a Romulan swordsman as the countdown to the show's premiere continues.

The Star Trek Twitter account posted a teaser featuring a Romulan in action, leaping high and brandishing a blade. The motion poster comes two months ahead of the show's debut on CBS All Access.
