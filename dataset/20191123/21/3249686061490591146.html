Golden Child looks golden in Hanbok in the choreography video for 'Wannabe'

23 Nov 2019, 21:38 by olmal

Golden Child drops a choreography video for their new single 'Wannabe', and the group shows sleek dance moves in a graceful looking Hanbok. Hanbok is a Korean traditional garment. 

The song is the title track off their first full-length album 'RE:BOOT,' which was released on November 18 at 6 PM KST.
