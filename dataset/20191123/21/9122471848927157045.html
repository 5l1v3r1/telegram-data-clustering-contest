JK Dobbins Has Message For Ohio State Fans After Win vs. Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Andrew Holleran

J.K. Dobbins had quite a final home game for the 2019 Ohio State football regular season today. The Buckeyes’ running back was dominant against Penn State.

The junior star totaled 157 yards on 36 carries, scoring two touchdowns.

Dobbins has been sensational all season long and might be in the Heisman Trophy race if not for the dominant performances of Joe Burrow and his teammate, Justin Fields.

Ohio State fans will certainly take what they’ve gotten from the junior running back so far this season, though.
