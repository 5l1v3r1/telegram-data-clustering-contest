Kaia Gerber and Pete Davidson Were Kissing by the Pool

23 Nov 2019, 21:10 by Hilary Weaver

Pete Davidson is not new to getting caught in acts of PDA. Now, more than a year after his relationship with Ariana Grande came to a halt, Davidson has been spotted sharing some romantic moments with Kaia Gerber.

On Saturday, the two were seen smooching poolside in Miami. Davidson, 26, wearing black trunks and a graphic tee, leaned over a pool chair to give Gerber, 18, a kiss. Gerber, wearing a black bikini and blue shirt, seemed to laugh along with Davidson as they kicked up their affection. In another image, they seemed pretty aware of someone nearby snapping a camera as they touched tongues.

This was not the first time Davidson and Gerber have been seen spending quality time together. On Monday, the two were seen out together for a casual evening out in New York City. Per People, they left Davidson's New York City apartment to grab dinner and then were seen out at Webster Hall sharing a kiss.

Davidson also reportedly celebrated his 26th birthday last weekend with Gerber at the Saturday Night Live after-party.

“Pete and Kaia, they showed up together, but did not hold hands,” a source told Us Weekly. “However, they were very flirty throughout the night as they became more relaxed inside of the restaurant. Again, there were a lot of people nearby and were very aware that people were watching them and their every move."

A source also told Us that Gerber was seen taking a Magnolia Bakery confetti cake with pink frosting that said, "Happy birthday, Pete" into the SNL studios.
