Pioneers: Godfrey Lukongwe Binaisa - first Attorney General

23 Nov 2019, 21:25 by Admin

This new constitution was promulgated on 15 April 1966; it was named “pigeonhole constitution"

He was the first post-independence Attorney General of Uganda.

 

In this position, he made great contribution to constitutionalism in Uganda and he is credited for single-handedly drafting the 1966 Pigeon Hole Constitution. 

 

This new constitution was promulgated on 15 April 1966; it was named “pigeonhole constitution" because copies of it were posted in the pigeonholes of Members of Parliament.

 

He also played a significant role in the drafting of the 1967 Constitution that made Uganda a republic.
