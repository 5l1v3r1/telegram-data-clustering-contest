NFL Releases Full List Of Fines, Suspensions For Browns-Steelers

23 Nov 2019, 21:18 by Chris Rosvoglou

Earlier today, Pittsburgh Steelers quarterback Mason Rudolph was fined $50,000 for his role in his team’s brawl with the Cleveland Browns. Roughly a few hours later, the NFL released its full list of fines and suspensions from the fight.

The biggest punishment was handed down to Myles Garrett, who is suspended indefinitely by the NFL. He’ll need to apply for reinstatement in order to play next season.

Garrett wasn’t the only player suspended by the NFL, as Maurkice Pouncey will miss two games and Larry Ogunjobi will miss one game due to their involvement in the fight.

As for all the fines, NFL Network insider Tom Pelissero shared the list on Twitter.

In total there were 33 players that received a fine from the league.
