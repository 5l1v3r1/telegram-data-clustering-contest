Big result for Grecians, says match-winner Dean Moxey, after win at Crawley

“I felt we were good value for the win and had we not won we would have been very disappointed. They had a few chances at the end but didn’t trouble us really."

23 Nov 2019, 21:09 by Daniel Clark

City skipper Dean Moxey felt that the Grecians were good value for the three points after their 1-0 win away to Crawley Town.

Moxey, 33, was wearing the armband as club captain Jake Taylor, although back from his suspension, only started on the bench.

The defender popped up with the winning goal 13 minutes from time to give Matt Taylor’s men the win, as he turned in a Randell Williams corner.

Speaking after the game, Moxey, said that given the chances created and how they defended, they would have been disappointed not to have won.
