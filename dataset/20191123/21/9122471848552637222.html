Kirk Herbstreit Reacts To Ohio State’s Win vs. Penn State

23 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, Ohio State showed it is the best team in the Big Ten by handling Penn State at home.

The Buckeyes dominated on the ground in the first half. Despite a furious comeback attempt from Penn State, Ohio State held on to walk out with a 28-17 win and take control of the Big Ten East.

After the game, ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit sent a message to Ohio State and Penn State players.

Check it out.

“Great game. Ohio State beats a very motivated and determined Penn State team. Buckeyes clinch a trip to Indy w/ the win & now turn their attention towards Michigan week!! And the Lions finish with a lay up vs Rutgers & a 10-2 year AND a well deserved NY6 bowl bid!” Herbstreit said.
