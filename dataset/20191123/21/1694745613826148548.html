Dance to Dollface's Soundtrack in All of Its Eclectic, Pop-Filled Glory

23 Nov 2019, 21:35 by Stacey Nguyen

Together, Kat Dennings, Shay Mitchell, and Brenda Song make up a holy pop trinity in Hulu's Dollface. As the protagonist Jules (Dennings) processes her breakup, we follow her surreal journey which features, among many things, a literal cat lady. Whether or not the Instagram pink feminist fantasy works for you, the quirky soundtrack gets an A+ for mixing dreamy current songs with reliable oldies. We hear everything from Kacey Musgraves's "Rainbow" in a cutesy Wizard of Oz-themed episode to Billy Squier's "The Stroke" in a racy striptease scene. Need an anthem for dancing it out at the club or crying with a glass of fine wine? You'll find it in Dollface's eclectic season one soundtrack.
