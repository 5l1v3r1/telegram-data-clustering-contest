Rani Mukerji says she along with Deepika Padukone and Katrina Kaif can feature in 'Charlie's Angels' remake

23 Nov 2019, 21:07 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Deepika Padukone and Katrina Kaif are the A-list actresses of Bollywood. They have come a long way in the industry and made a place for themselves in everyone hearts.

Fans have been waiting for them to collaborate for a project and Rani Mukerjee has suggested a film for them. In a recent interview, Rani said she found Katrina's action sequences in 'Tiger Zinda Hai' on point. Talking about Deepika she said the 'Piku' star will make for a great action star.

Rani went on to say how the three of them together could do a film like Charlie's Angels.
