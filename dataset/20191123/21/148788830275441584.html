‘Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker’ TV Spot: The Knights of Ren and Sith Troopers Finally Appear

23 Nov 2019, 21:08 by Ethan Anderton

With less than a month until Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker hits theaters, Disney and Lucasfilm will be releasing new snippets of footage here and there leading up to the release. If you’ve already seen everything you want to, then it might be time to start staying away from the internet for a bit. This new Rise of Skywalker TV spot uses a lot of footage from the final trailer, but it also has new shots, such as a sweeping shot of the Knights of Ren, a Sith Trooper in battle, and plenty of Rey and Kylo Ren.
