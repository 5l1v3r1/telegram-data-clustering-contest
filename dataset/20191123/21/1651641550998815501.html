WhatsApp is rolling out the Group Stickers and Albums feature to WhatsApp Web

23 Nov 2019, 21:00 by Rahul

Grouped Stickers and Albums is a feature that group photos, videos, and stickers when you send them in succession. This feature saves a lot of space in the chat window and was available in both the WhatsApp Android app and WhatsApp iOS app.

Aside from the Android and iOS app, WhatsApp Web too, can now group photos, videos, and stickers when you send them in succession, according to WABetainfo.

The feature not only saves space in chat windows but also lets you delete all the images or stickers at once, meaning that you don’t have to go through the pain of deleting each and every image, which in turn, saves a lot of time.

Unless you have been living under a rock, you’d know that WhatsApp will stop working on Microsoft’s mobile platform after 31st of December. So, the arrival of Grouped Stickers and Albums feature in WhatsApp Web is good news especially for those who are still using Windows Phone and aren’t planning to switch to Android or iOS anytime soon. You can access the web app at web.whatsapp.com.

WhatsApp is also working on new important new features — Blocked Contact Notice and Grouped Blocked Contact. These new features will provide you better control over blocked contacts.

If you’re using WhatsApp on an Android smartphone that has a fingerprint sensor, you can now use your fingerprint to unlock your WhatsApp.
