Former Buckeyes Star Taunts Penn State After Win

23 Nov 2019, 21:56 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, the Ohio State Buckeyes used an impressive defensive effort to take down the Penn State Nittany Lions by a final score of 28-17.

The win secures Ohio State’s spot in the Big Ten title game and makes the Buckeyes a virtual lock for the College Football Playoff. Ohio State didn’t quite live up to the insane betting line set by Las Vegas (19.5 points), but a win is a win.

It’s the third-straight win over the Nittany Lions for the Buckeyes. Following the game, a former Ohio State star decided to taunt Penn State.

“Penn State our lil homie,” former Buckeyes defensive lineman Jalyn Holmes said.
