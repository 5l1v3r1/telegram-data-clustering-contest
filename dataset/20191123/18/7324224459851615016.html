Bournemouth 1 Wolves 2: Joe Edwards and Nathan Judah analysis - WATCH

Joe Edwards spoke to Nathan Judah following Wolves' 2-1 win over Bournemouth at the Vitality Stadium.

23 Nov 2019, 18:07 by Nathan Judah

Joao Moutinho and Raul Jimenez fired Wolves to a 2-1 victory over 10-man Bournemouth.

Portuguese ace Moutinho's sublime set-piece opened the scoring in the 21st minute with Jimenez netting his 14th of the term just after the half-hour mark.
