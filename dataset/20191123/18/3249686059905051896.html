Sneakers Store in Switzerland Flooded with People as They Trying to Get a Hand on G-Dragon's Shoes

23 Nov 2019, 18:51 by k81

Korean star and fashion icon G-Dragon’s label PEACEMINUSONE has teamed up with Nike for the Nike Air Force 1 PEACEMINUSONE Para-noise, and it launched today November 23th worldwide, and it was sold-out completely all around the world.

In Zurich, Switzerland, people flooded the Titolo store, trying to get their hands on the Para-Noise shoes, as it was sold-out online in few moments after release. The shop thanked fans on their Instagram account and tagged all G-Dragon accounts as well Peaceminusone accounts with caption "The PEACEMINUSONE X NIKE AIR FORCE 1'07,, PARA-NOISE rocking Switzerland! Thank you all!"
