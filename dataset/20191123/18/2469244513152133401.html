All the faces we met on the streets of Wonford during the noughties

It's time to shine a light on the characters who have made the red-brick estate what it is through the years

23 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Miles O'Leary

Remember what Wonford looked and felt like way back in the early to mid noughties? Those were the days.

It doesn't seem like yesterday the old post office was buzzing with life, Wonford Inn was the place to go and Choy's was still doing a roaring trade.

We met you at fun days, fetes and jubilee celebrations - ensuring the spirit of Wonford was brought to a wider audience.

There's so many characters in our archives. So it seems silly to keep them all locked away with nowhere to shine.

Which is why we've rustled up an epic, rare gallery of the very finest moments from the east red-brick Exeter estate.
