Funko, Amazon Announce Special Marvel's Avengers Pop! Line

23 Nov 2019, 18:11 by Vernon Miles

If you're still in recovery from Avengers: Endgame and feeling nostalgic for the team's first outing, Amazon and Funko have announced a new series of Pop! figures celebrating the 2012 iterations of the core members of Earth's Mightiest Heroes.

Funko revealed the first of the six figures today, Iron Man. Amazon will unveil another hero in the set every other month. The display of the box on Amazon sports shadowy silhouettes of the Hulk, Hawkeye, Thor, Black Widow and Captain America figures. Eventually, all of the pieces will connect at their base for a 12-inch diameter recreation of the scene when the six heroes circle up in 2012's The Avengers.

The new Iron Man Pop! sports his red and gold Mark VII armor from the first Avengers film. The armor conceals his face, unlike the Quantum Realm figure from earlier this year. The figure hovers slightly off the ground, with dust from his boosters splayed out across the base of the figure. The Iron Man Pop! stands at 5.5 inches tall with a five-inch wide base.

The Iron Man figure is scheduled to ship in January and is currently available for pre-order for $19.99.
