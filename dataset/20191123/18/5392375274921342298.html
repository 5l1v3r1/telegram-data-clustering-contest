ColorOS 7 beta will reach Realme X2 Pro and XT in India soon

23 Nov 2019, 18:56 by Marco Lancaster

Rate this post

The first reports about ColorOS 7 arrival on the Realme X2 Pro and Realme XT emerged yesterday. According to these reports, both smartphones will get the new version of Oppo’s traditional skin on December 18 in China. Now, new reports indicate that users of these two devices will also receive the beta update pretty soon in India.

The information is as official as it could be since it was shared by no one else than Realme’s India CEO Madhav Sheth. He made the announcement through a twitter post where he also will reveal the update timeline for ColorOS 7 (Realme Edition) on November 25. Worth noting that the Realme smartphones will reveal a custom version of ColorOS 7. According to the CEO, this version will be closer to vanilla Android.
