Bala crosses the 100 crore mark

23 Nov 2019, 18:58 by Akash Nihalani

Ayushmann Khurrana is currently flying high on the success of his last release Bala. The actor has been on a great run at the box-office ever since Andhadhun last year which was followed by Badhaai Ho, Article 15, Dream Girl and now Bala. Bala also marks the seventh consecutive hit at the box-office for Ayushmann. 

Yesterday, the film’s collection amounted to Rs 1.35 crore making the total to Rs 100.15 crore at the domestic box office. This news came a day after Bala crossing the Rs.150 crore make worldwide. Bhumi Pednekar shared her excitement over the same with a tweet that read, “#Bala crosses another milestone. And it's a big one! So tonight's the time to celebrate the success with the team! Soda, pani nimbu ke saath kya piyenge aaj? #DineshVijan @amarkaushik @ayushmannk @yamigautam @MaddockFilms @JioCinema @jiostudios.”
