The I'm A Celebrity moment which made Kate Garraway husband's heart 'break' - and you probably didn't notice

23 Nov 2019, 18:52 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

 I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here is continuing on ITV1 with a star line-up.

Ant McPartlin was absent for the last series, with Holly Willoughby taking his place, but Declan Donnelly will be reunited in Australia with his long-time co-host as I'm A Celebrity continues.

Caitlyn Jenner is among the stars taking part in this year's series, the 19th in the ITV show's history, alongside Kate Garraway.

The pair are among the 12 celebrities who have headed Down Under for daring challenges and unappetising trials.

Garraway's husband Derek Draper has opened up about the moment his heart broke while watching her on I'm A Celebrity.

Appearing with their two children Bill and Darcey, Derek heaped praise on Kate, 52.

He told hosts "The only bit where I felt really upset for her was something people might not have noticed when there was some good news delivered about the food - Wagon Wheels I think it was.

“Everyone’s jumping in and hugging and she turned to hug someone but everyone was already hugging someone.

“I just wanted to dive through the television and give her a massive hug.”

“It is hard watching her struggle with things but equally I know how much she likes a challenge so in a way I know deep down she’s having the time of her life,” he said.

I'm A Celebrity airs tonight at 9pm on ITV.
