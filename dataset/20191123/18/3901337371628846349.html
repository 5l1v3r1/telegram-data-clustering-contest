Starting XI: Man City v Chelsea

23 Nov 2019, 18:32 by SL International

Manchester City will be hoping to get their Premier League campaign back on track when they host Frank Lampard's in-form Chelsea this afternoon - Here's how they line up.

English Premier League

Teams: Manchester City v Chelsea

Date: 23 November 2019

Venue: Etihad

Pep Guardiola's troops will be licking their wounds following a 3-1 loss to league leaders Liverpool before the international break.

The defeat saw the defending champions drop nine points behind Jurgen Klopp's men after only 12 games played this season.

One team who took advantage of City's slip up is Chelsea, who moved up to third spot with a 2-0 triumph over Crystal Palace at Stamford Bridge.

Since the defeat to Liverpool a Stamford Bridge back in September, Lampard's have gone on to win six straight league encounters.

City will be a man less as Bernardo Silva serves his one-game suspension for a controversial tweet about teammate Benjamin Mendy.

The Citizens will have fond memories of this fixture last season as they ran out 6-0 winners, with Sergio Aguero starring with a hat-trick.

Here's how they line up:

Manchester City: Ederson, Cancelo, Stones, Fernandinho, Mendy, Rodrigo, David Silva, Mahrez, De Bruyne, Sterling, Aguero.

Chelsea: Kepa, Azpilicueta, Zouma, Tomori, Emerson, Kante, Jorginho, Kovacic, William, Pulisic, Abraham.
