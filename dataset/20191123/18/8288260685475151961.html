Kim Kardashian breaks silence on Caitlyn Jenner's I'm A Celebrity stint

Caitlyn is one of 12 campmates on the ITV1 reality show, which also features Ian Wright, James Haskell and Jacqueline Jossa

23 Nov 2019, 18:48 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Kim Kardashian has finally spoken out over Caitlyn Jenner's stint in I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!

Jenner’s daughters Kylie and Kendall are still ignoring I’m A Celeb as they fail to show support.

Caitlyn is one of 12 campmates on the ITV1 reality show, which also features Ian Wright, James Haskell and Jacqueline Jossa.

The star is being paid £500,000 to appear on the series, which is hosted by Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly.

None of Caitlyn's family have publicly discussed or supported her during her time on the ITV show - until now.

Kim Kardashian has shared a clip of the Olympic gold medal-winning decathlete, telling her 62.4m followers on Twitter: "I literally recorded this and set this as my alarm a few weeks ago!" in response to a tweet from the official I'm A Celebirty...account.

Caitlyn is captured waking up the rest of the camp and impersonates her daughter Kylie's viral 'Rise and Shine' video.

One follower replied to Kim's post: "At last the whole of the uk were wondering if poor Caitlyn was going to have any family support. The uk love her in I’m a celeb" while another said: "We have all been waiting for a Kardashian tweet about #ImACelebrity2019".

Another Twitter user replied with: "Finally you have tweeted about #ImACelebrity2019 we all have been waiting."
