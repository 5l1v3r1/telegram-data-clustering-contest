Who is Chris Ramsey's wife and when is the Strictly star going on tour?

23 Nov 2019, 18:46

Strictly Come Dancing 2019 has been full of shocks since before the show even made it to air.

From Jamie Laing’s injury to Michelle Visage’s gutting exit or Will Bayley having to quit, it’s hard to pick the biggest ‘omg’ moment – but Chris Ramsey could pull off a big surprise.

The comedian has been impressing fans and judges, outstaying some early favourites like Michelle Visage.

Ahead of his next appearance on Strictly, here’s a look at Chris’ personal and professional life off the dance floor.

Who is Chris Ramsey’s wife Rosie?

Chris is married to Rosie Ramsey nee Winter.

Actress and presenter Rosie married Chris back in July 2014 and the pair welcomed son, Robin, in 2015.

Together Chris and Rosie co-host the Shagged, Married, Annoyed Podcast.
