Their red hot dance floor chemistry has seen them shoot to the top of the Strictly leaderboard.

But despite their sizzling moves, Karim Zeroual and Amy Dowden have both rubbished rumours they have fallen victim to the Strictly 'curse'.

The CBBC star has claimed her is far "too busy" to even think about romancing Amy.

He said: "I don't really know about any rumours. I'm too busy learning to do this waltz than to worry about stuff like that."

He is coupled up with university student, Poppy Birtwhistle, and both are keen to keep their relationship out of the public eye.

But Amy and Karim have developed such a close bond she's already invited him to her wedding to Ben Jones next year.

Amy said: "Karim is coming to my wedding next July. I'm so excited."

He said: "I'm doing a speech but she doesn't know that."

He said: "We tease each other constantly and it goes a bit far and she nags me a bit - bit it's all out of love."
