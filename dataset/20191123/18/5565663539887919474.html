Bulawayo City promoted to PSL

23 Nov 2019, 18:50 by Staff reporter

WhaWha have been promoted back to the Castle Larger Premiership after beating Shabanie Mine 2-0 in the final match of the Central Region Division One League on Saturday.

Sheasham, who went into the final day two points clear on top of the standings, lost 1-0 at Dulibadzimu United.

In the Southern Region, Bulawayo City also won the ticket back to the top-flight with a 3-0 over Arenel while their rivals Talen Vision lost 1-0 away to Makomo.

WhaWha and City join Cranborne Bullets who won the Northern Region ticket.
