Crystal Palace vs Liverpool: Roberto Firmino scores the winner in Reds 2-1 win

23 Nov 2019, 18:02

- Crystal Palace vs Liverpool's Premier League game ended 2-1 in favor of the Reds

- Sadio Mane and Roberto Firmino scored the two goals for Liverpool in this tie

- Liverpool are still topping the Premier League standings with 37 points

Liverpool on Saturday, November 23, continued with their impressive performance this season in the Premier League beating Crystal Palace 2-1.

The hosts wanted to impress their fans in this Premier League encounter, but they were unable to get a win despite their efforts.

But Crystal Palace ensured they prevented Liverpool from scoring in the first half of this Premier League tie.

Senegal international Sadio Mane scored the first goal for Liverpool in the 49th minute after an assist from Andy Robertson.
