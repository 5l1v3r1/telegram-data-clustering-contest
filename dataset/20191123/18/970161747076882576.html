The Walking Dead star rushed to hospital after being thrown off a horse

23 Nov 2019, 18:46

The Walking Dead’s former hero Chandler Riggs has been rushed to hospital after he was thrown off the back of a horse.

Don’t worry – he’s doing okay.

Taking to Instagram, the 20-year-old’s mum, Gina-Ann Riggs, shared a picture of her bambino looking slightly worse for wear as he threw his thumb up towards the camera from a hospital bed.

In the caption, the concerned parent updated fans, and wrote: ‘The call every mom hopes she never gets… Thankfully, he’s going to be ok and thankfully I’m here with him!

‘He was thrown off of his horse yesterday afternoon. Thank GOODNESS he was wearing a helmet. He has a pretty bad concussion and is in a lot of pain but no broken bones!’

She added: ‘I took the first flight out to LA and got here at around 9:30 last night.  Keep him in your prayers!!

‘Oh, and I had missed his episode so I stayed off of social media until I finally watched it this morning. Thank you for all of the kind words!!’

Fans were quick to comment and sent their well wishes to the Carl Grimes star.

‘Omg I hope he is okay now,’ one wrote while another added: ‘Praying for him. Glad all is okay.’

Someone else shared a string of heart emojis and said: ‘Feel better soon Chandler sending so much prayers and love.’

Chandler played a huge role in The Walking Dead, as viewers watched him grow up on screen in the character Carl Grimes.

But despite his tragic death, it seems the young actor is hoping to return to the role in the upcoming Rick Grimes movies.

Earlier this year, he was asked by TVGuide if he’d want to make a comeback in the highly anticipated trilogy.

And pleasing us no end, he confessed: ‘I hope so. That would be amazing. It’d be so much fun to get to work with Andy again. That’d be great.’

The Walking Dead’s midseason finale airs Sunday at 9pm on AMC and follows a day later on Fox UK.
