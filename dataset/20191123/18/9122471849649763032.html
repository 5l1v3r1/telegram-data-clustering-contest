Photo: Ohio State Fan’s Sign For Joe Burrow Is Going Viral

23 Nov 2019, 18:25 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday morning, ESPN’s College GameDay broadcast from Columbus ahead of a huge match-up between Ohio State and Penn State.

Each and every Saturday, fans bring their best signs to College GameDay. Every now and then one sign takes the cake and goes viral on social media.

Today, that sign came from an Ohio State fan who had a message for former Buckeyes quarterback Joe Burrow. Ohio State fans are looking forward to seeing Burrow in the College Football Playoff – assuming both teams make it there.

Except the fan used the wrong stadium – they pictured the Atlanta Falcons stadium instead of the New Orleans Saints stadium.

Oops.
