Somebody Dug Up Old Joe Burrow Tweets That The LSU QB May Wanna Delete From His Account (PIC)

23 Nov 2019, 18:15 by Darrelle Lincoln

Once again, a high profiled athlete forgot to do a clean sweep of his Twitter account and he is certainly going to pay for it.

LSU Tigers QB Joe Burrow has likely become the most-coveted QB on most draft boards after Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa suffered a dislocated hip in his last start.

Before we get to that point, however, the 22-year-old will need to address his tweets that were unearthed by social media users that show him not only ripping commissioner Roger Goodell, but also taking a shot at the late Stuart Scott, as well as telling NBA teams to stop choosing foreigners in the Draft.

Yea, this is not a good look for Burrow. Address it as soon as possible and it goes away.
