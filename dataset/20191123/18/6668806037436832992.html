Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon launch Mann mein Shiva from Panipat

23 Nov 2019, 18:40 by Akash Nihalani

There has been massive hype surrounding Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon starrer Panipat ever since the trailer went online. Today, the makers of the film held a special event in the city for the launch of the song Mann mein Shiva. The lead star of the film along with their director Ashutosh Gowariker arrived looking truly royal in a chariot. 

Arjun was seen wearing green Indian attire, while Kriti opted for a long black dress with floral designs.
