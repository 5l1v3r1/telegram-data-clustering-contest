Seven teenage boys arrested on drug charges at Schoolies

16 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Jocelyn Garcia

Seven teenage boys were arrested overnight on 15 drug-related offences during Schoolies celebrations on the Gold Coast.

A police spokesman said the possession and consumption of illicit drugs was illegal and could have fatal consequences.

"Police will not hesitate to continue to take enforcement action against anyone found in possession of, supplying, trafficking or producing dangerous drugs," he said.

Paramedics also treated 50 patients, including 27 females and 23 males, in the Emergency Treatment Centre. Only three required further assessment in hospital.

Police said that in the same time frame last year, six schoolies and 11 non-schoolies had been arrested on 11 charges.

The majority of the non-schoolies' charges related to drug and public nuisance offences.
