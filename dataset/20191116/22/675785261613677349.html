Prince Andrew slammed for saying paedo friend acted in a 'manner unbecoming'

The Labour MP Jess Phillips was among viewers who reacted with horror to the Duke of York's choice of words in his interview with BBC Newsnight interview Emily Maitlis

16 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Talia Shadwell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/talia-shadwell/

BBC viewers have reacted with disbelief to Prince Andrew's description of his paedophile friend Jeffrey Epstein's behaviour as "unbecoming."

Many who watched the broadcast reacted with shock to the Duke of York's choice of words in an exchange with BBC Newsnight interviewer Emily Maitlis tonight.

Prince Andrew has faced questions over his judgement in maintaining a friendship with the disgraced billionaire, who was found dead by apparent suicide in his cell in New York in August.

Epstein was awaiting trial over sex trafficking charges.

In 2010, the prince was photographed walking with Epstein in New York's Central Park - two years after Epstein's earlier first conviction for soliciting a minor for prostitution. 

 

The exchange arose as Prince Andrew described his relationship with Epstein and the financier's sometimes-girlfriend Ghislaine Maxwell, who has been accused of recruiting young women to have sex with the billionaire financier.

Prince Andrew can be heard to reflect to Maitlis during the interview that aired tonight on BBC Two: "On balance, could I have avoided ever meeting him?

"Probably not and that’s because of my friendship with Ghislaine, it was…it was…it was inevitable that we would have come across each other.

"Do I regret the fact that he has quite obviously conducted himself in a manner unbecoming? Yes."

 

Maitlis replies replies: "Unbecoming? He was a sex offender."

The Prince responds: "Yeah, I’m sorry, I’m being polite, I mean in the sense that he was a sex offender."

Labour MP Jess Phillips was among those shocked by the Duke's comments, tweeting: "A manner unbecoming, he raped children!!!!"

The Duke of York has come under additional scrutiny in accusations by Virginia Giuffre- formerly Virginia Roberts- that she had sex with him on three occasions when she was aged 17.

In tonight's wide-ranging interview, the prince claimed he had never had sex with Ms Giuffre, and did not recall ever meeting her.

He has questioned the validity of a photograph showing the pair pictured together with his arm looped around the teenager.

 

He claimed he had been eating at Pizza Express with daughter Princess Beatrice on one night that Ms Giuffre claims she had sex with the prince when she was 17.

He said he recalled the dinner at the pizza branch in Woking for a party "weirdly distinctly" as it was such a rare occurrence.

When Maitlis asked the Prince about Giuffre’s allegations that he was sweating when they danced in Tramp nightclub, the Prince said it was impossible as he had  "peculiar medical condition.

“I didn’t sweat at the time because I had suffered what I would describe as an overdose of adrenaline in the Falklands War when I was shot at and I simply... it was almost impossible for me to sweat.”

The Prince also told Maitlis he and Epstein were not as close friends as some had suggested, and told Maitlis his New York home was a "convenient place to stay."
