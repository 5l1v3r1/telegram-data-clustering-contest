Photo: Minnesota Players Show Great Class vs. Iowa

16 Nov 2019, 22:10 by Chris Rosvoglou

Not only is P.J. Fleck building something special at Minnesota, he’s teaching his players how to conduct themselves on and off the field. Despite trailing by double digits at the end of the first quarter, the Golden Gophers joined in on the Hawkeyes’ tradition called ‘The Wave.’

After the first quarter comes to an end during every home game, Iowa fans wave to children in the University of Iowa Stead Family Children’s Hospital.

It’s truly one of the most heartwarming tradition in all of sports.

This time around, the Hawkeyes weren’t the only team that waved toward the hospital.

The Golden Gophers went to midfield so they could get a clear shot of the University of Iowa Stead Family Children’s Hospital.
