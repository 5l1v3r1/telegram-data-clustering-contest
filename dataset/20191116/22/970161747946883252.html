I’m A Celebrity’s Jacqueline Jossa terrified as she takes on plank in first task

16 Nov 2019, 22:30

Jacqueline Jossa isn’t adjusting to life in the I’m A Celebrity jungle very well, and the series hasn’t even started yet.

In fact, the EastEnders actress can be seen with her head in her hands as she attempts to take on the first challenge, walking on a shaky-looking plank on top of The Focus Building, in Australia.

Well, to be fair to her, it is 334ft above the ground.

It seems this year’s task is harder than ever, as the famous faces will have to walk the plank – suspended off the edge of the skyscraper, before having to climb down a ladder.

And, even if they complete that part, they then have to hit a switch with their foot as quickly as possible.

To be fair, if we were Jacqueline, we would’ve been on the flight home before Ant and Dec completed the instructions.

The mum-of-two has been joined by a string of celebrities in this year’s series, including Caitlyn Jenner, Ian Wright, Nadine Coyle and Roman Kemp.

However, a psychic has stated that Jac will be the first one to go home – and could walk from the jungle before the first half of the season is complete.

‘Jacqueline is going to find it really difficult being away from home, she’ll feel incredibly homesick,’ Chris Riley told Metro.co.uk.

‘I’m seeing that she may try to walk out of the jungle in the first half of the series, hopefully the team’s morale and friendships will convince her to stay.’

He also explained that he feels the mum-of-two will be an easy target for the bushtucker challenges, adding: ‘The public will vote both her and another female into many of the early trials, a result which will help Jacqueline’s personality to shine through.

‘I’m seeing that Jacqueline has fears, as we all do, although some are quite strong, possibly leading her to experience a panic attack and subsequently pull out of her first or second trial.’

Well, we won’t have long to find out…

I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here! returns on 17 November, at 9pm, on ITV.
