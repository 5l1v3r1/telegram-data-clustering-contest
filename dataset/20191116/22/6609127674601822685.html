Radio One star Arielle Free reveals Lewis Capaldi interview went down the pan because pair started joking about poo

16 Nov 2019, 22:35 by Beverley Lyons

RADIO One star Arielle Free revealed 15 minutes of an interview she did with Lewis Capaldi went down the pan — as the pair started joking about poo.

The Stirling-born DJ grilled Someone You Loved singer Lewis, 23, for her Weekend Early Breakfast Show.

But the Scots pair’s toilet banter proved too mucky for station bosses and the segment had to be censored.

Arielle, 31, said: “We did this interview and they had to cut out 15 minutes of it because it was pure filth.

“We talked about poos and jobbies. I introduced Lewis to the term ‘thunderjobby’ and he couldn’t believe it.

“I feel I’ve done something really good in my life because I’ve educated Lewis Capaldi on what a thunderjobby is.

“My mum’s going to be like, ‘I can’t believe you talked about jobbies’.”

London-based Arielle regularly returns to Scotland for DJ dates and will perform at Edinburgh’s Hogmanay street party.

She’s also working on a secret BBC Scotland show about romance, hot on the heels of her role co-hosting the Love Island: The Morning After podcast with former winner Kem Cetinay, 23.

Arielle also told how her Scots roots are going down a treat with her new colleagues at Radio One — as she’s turned them on to the joys of tattie scones.

She said: “I fill my suitcase with them every time I’m home — it’s become a thing at the station and I have orders from people to bring them back.

And she added: “Aside from tattie scones I miss my family. Every time I come back my grandpa picks me up from the airport.

“We go for a wee scran and I get to catch up with my niece.”

Trained ballerina Arielle said she still can’t believe her luck after landing the Radio One gig in September.

She said: “I pinch myself every time I walk into that building. Every day I’m waiting for it all to go Pete Tong.”
