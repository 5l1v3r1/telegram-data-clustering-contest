ATP Finals: Dominic Thiem beats Alexander Zverev to reach final in London

16 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Press Association

Dominic Thiem will face Stefanos Tsitsipas in the ATP Finals final after the Austrian beat defending champion Alexander Zverev.

A break of serve in each set saw Thiem, 26, through 7-5 6-3 in an hour and 34 minutes.
