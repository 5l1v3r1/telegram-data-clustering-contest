Jim Harbaugh Reacts To Michigan’s Blowout Of Michigan State

16 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Dan Lyons

Entering the season, one of the major issues plaguing Jim Harbaugh was his inability to beat rivals and top teams at Michigan. The season didn’t get off to the start that fans had hoped, with two losses early including a blowout defeat against Wisconsin.

Since the season’s second loss, a tight game at Penn State, Michigan has figured some things out. That includes those coveted rivalry victories that have eluded Harbaugh to this point.

A few weeks ago, Michigan stomped No. 8 Notre Dame at home, 45-14. Today, Michigan State faced a similar fate, falling 44-10.

The Spartans have really struggled this year, and hasn’t won since September. Still, any win against the Spartans is one that the Wolverines will take. Harbaugh wasn’t worried about rubbing it in either. He kept his starters in well into the fourth quarter, and had Shea Patterson throwing during Michigan’s last full drive.
