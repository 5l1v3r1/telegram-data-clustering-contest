There’s A Live Feed Of The Colin Kaepernick Workout In Atlanta

16 Nov 2019, 21:53 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, ESPN NFL insider Adam Schefter revealed the location of Colin Kaepernick’s workout for NFL scouts changed last minute.

The NFL set up a workout for Kaepernick to showcase his skills at the Atlanta Falcons training facility. However, just over an hour before the workout was scheduled to begin, Kaepernick’s camp decided to move the location.

They released a statement revealing the decision for the change. The NFL did not want media to attend and film the workout, so Kaepernick changed the location to allow media and the fans to see the workout for themselves.

A report suggested he asked for cameras to be present for the workout initially – but his request was denied by the NFL.

The workout moved to a local Georgia high school, where media could attend.

In fact, everyone can watch from the comfort of their own home. There’s a live-stream available so anyone not in attendance can watch the workout as well.
