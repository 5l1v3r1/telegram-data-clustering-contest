16 Nov 2019, 21:18 by Ver Noveno

House Majority Leader and Leyte Rep. Martin Romuladez, in partnership with Tingog Party List Rep. Yedda Marie K. Romualdez, distributes P3,000 each as well as mosquito net, water container, pail, and dipper to more than 1,000 families affected by the recent earthquake at three barangays in Makilala, North Cotabato. Romualdez recently donated P500,000 to the victims during his birthday celebration last Thursday.
