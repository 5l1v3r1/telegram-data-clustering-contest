Beyoncé Went to a Gala in a Nude Sequin Gown and Carried a $1 Billion Bill Stack Clutch

16 Nov 2019, 21:31 by Alyssa Bailey

Brace yourself: Beyoncé posted quite possibly one of her greatest outfit of the day shots ever on her Instagram this afternoon. The singer shared close-up details of the shimmering nude sequin gown she wore to the Shawn Carter Foundation Gala last night. Come for the dress, stay for the spot-on accessory: Queen Bey carried a clutch designed to be a stack of $1 billion bills. Beyoncé has had a quieter couple months following the release of The Lion King and her press tour for it. But Emilia Clarke made headlines this month when she recounted her experience meeting Beyoncé at Bey's Oscars party this year.

"I did not expect to see them, you know, in the flesh, I wasn’t expecting that at all,” Clarke said of Beyoncé and her husband Jay-Z on the Graham North Show, via People. “I was just like, ‘Oh my God, I’m invited!'”

“And Queen Bey came up to me, and obviously, looked at me like, ‘Oh…I’d like to talk to you!’ and I just did not respond in the way that I was thinking she thought that I might respond,” Clarke said. “As in, she obviously came up and was like, this’ll be cool, and I was like, ‘Oh my God,'" Clarke then made a crying noise, saying she was "too many drinks in, [I] started crying, it didn’t go well. I mean, literally, eyes welling up like, ‘I can’t handle this, it’s too intense.'”
