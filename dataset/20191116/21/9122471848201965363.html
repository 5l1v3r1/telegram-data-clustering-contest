Michigan Player Taunts ‘Little Brother’ Michigan State After Win

16 Nov 2019, 21:30 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, No. 15 Michigan hosted Michigan State in a rivalry game that has produced some of the most-memorable moments in recent college football history.

The Spartans are in the midst of a down season. Mark Dantonio’s squad lost four-straight including an abysmal performance against Illinois last weekend heading into today’s contest.

Make it five straight.

Michigan State found the back of the endzone first, but from then on it was all Wolverines. Jim Harbaugh’s program dominated over the final three quarters en route to a 44-10 win.

After the game, Michigan defensive lineman Michael Dwumfour called out the team’s little brother.

“Our little brothers didn’t learn they lesson last year had to remind them again,” he said on Twitter.
