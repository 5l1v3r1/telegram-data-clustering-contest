Noah Cyrus Gets Treated By EMTs After Running Off Stage In The Middle Of Her Performance

16 Nov 2019, 21:48 by Jamie Samhan

Noah Cyrus is in good hands after a health scare at her concert on Saturday.

She took to Instagram to update her fans after running off stage before her last song at the 2019 Corona Capital Festival.

“I had to run off and throw up before my last song. 😫😫😫 sorry I couldn’t finish my set but thanks so much @coronacapital. you guys were so loud,” she captioned a photo of an EMT providing her with an oxygen mask.

Her fans quickly took to her comments section to send their well-wishes.

Cyrus has previously spoken about her struggles with depression and anxiety.

“I’ve struggled with anxiety and depression since I was 10 or 11 years old,” she said while on “The Late Late Show”. “So, I think it’s a huge topic. One of the things that I’ve always wanted to use this platform [for] was to talk about my mental health and help young adults all around America and everywhere in the world know that they’re not alone.”
