Brazilian GP Practice Three: Lewis Hamilton leads Max Verstappen

16 Nov 2019, 16:03 by James Galloway at Interlagos

Lewis Hamilton just beat Max Verstappen to the fastest time in Practice Three as Mercedes, Red Bull and Ferrari all showed strong speed ahead of Brazilian GP qualifying.

In an exciting hour-long session in which the fastest time repeatedly changed hands, new six-time champion Hamilton finished just 0.026s ahead of Verastappen as he goes in search of his first pole position in over three months later on Saturday.

Ferrari, Friday's pacesetters, were third and fourth although are expected to close up for qualifying after finishing 0.3s behind Hamilton in P3.

Alex Albon and Valtteri Bottas endured difficult sessions and will hopr for better in qualifying.
