Mrs. Gokongwei passes away  

16 Nov 2019, 16:36

Mrs. Elizabeth Yu Gokongwei passed away peacefully on Saturday, Nov. 16, 2019, a week after the death of her husband the late industrialist John Gokongwei Jr.,

Mrs. Gokongwei, 85 years old, was married to John Jr. for 61 long years.

The couple had six children Robina, Lance, Lisa, Faith, Hope and Maria.

An official family statement from Elizabeth Yu Gokongwei said private wake details will be shared with friends and family.
