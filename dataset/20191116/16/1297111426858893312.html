66-year-old pedestrian dies after being struck by vehicle on Dewdney Ave.

16 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Regina Leader-Post

Regina police and the Office of the Chief Coroner are investigating after a 66-year-old man died after he was hit by a vehicle at the intersection of Dewdney Avenue and Robinson Street.

According to a police news release, officers were dispatched to the scene of the collision shortly after 9:30 p.m. on Friday.

The initial investigation indicated that the man was struck by an eastbound vehicle while he was crossing the street, walking north, in the west crosswalk.

Paramedics also attended the scene, transporting the man to hospital where he was confirmed to have died.

The investigation remains ongoing and police are asking anyone who may have information relating to this incident to call the RPS at 306-777-6500.
