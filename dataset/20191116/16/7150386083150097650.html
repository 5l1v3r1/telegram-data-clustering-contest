'Will Quit Politics Once...' Says Giriraj Singh on Retiring From Politics

In 2016, the leader kicked up a controversy as he said that if the country's population policy is not changed to mandate a two-child norm across religions, then "our daughters" will not be safe and might have to be kept under the veil like Pakistan.

16 Nov 2019, 16:15

New Delhi: Political leaders like him can retire now from politics peacefully as the Supreme Court has passed its verdict on the Ayodhya land, said Union minister Giriraj Singh, known for his inflammatory speeches, often aimed at a specific community.
