Jharkhand Polls: BJP Releases List of 40 Star Campaigners, Names Include PM Modi, Amit Shah

As per the EC notification, Jharkhand is all set to go to polls in five phases, with the first one on November 30 and the last on December 20.

16 Nov 2019, 16:57

Ranchi: Ahead of Jharkhand Assembly election 2019, Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) on Saturday issued a list of 40-star campaigners for the 1st phase of the upcoming polls.

Some of the prominent leaders among start campaigners include Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Party President Amit Shah, Party Working President JP Nadda and BJP MP from Gurdaspur Sunny Deol.
