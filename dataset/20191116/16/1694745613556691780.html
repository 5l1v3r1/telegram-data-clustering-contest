Lili Reinhart Makes the Time 100 List Wearing a Rodarte Gown That Betty Cooper Would Stan

16 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Kara Kia

Actor and soon-to-be-author Lili Reinhart was listed as one of Time magazine's 100 Next most influential young people. To celebrate the expansion of the magazine's illustrious list, Lili attended the gala on Thursday night at Pier 17 in New York City, wearing a Rodarte gown and Harry Winston jewels — we think Betty Cooper would approve of this sweet look.

Lili's black-and-white peplum gown covered in black satin bows was straight from Rodarte's Fall/Winter 2019 collection. The choice isn't surprising because she is a Rodarte ambassador, since being featured in the brand's dreamlike, star-studded Spring/Summer 2020 lookbook, which also included Kiernan Shipka and Alexa Demie.

The simple, monochromatic palette of Lili's F/W 2019 gown paired with the excessive bows is giving us major inspiration for our party season looks. It's perfectly festive with the present-like ribbon details, while the minimal color choice keeps the dress from looking over the top. We're also loving that the ruching on the bust looks like an elegantly wrapped gift. Keep reading ahead for every angle of Lili's gorgeous gown.
