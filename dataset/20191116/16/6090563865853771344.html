Zimbabwe vs Zambia Afcon match: List of Zim Warriors players dropped

16 Nov 2019, 16:44

Zimbabwe Warriors coach Joey Antipas has dropped three players for the trip to Zambia tomorrow.

The Warriors will face their northern neighbours in the second Group H encounter of the 2021 Afcon Qualifiers. The match will be played at Heroes National Stadium in Lusaka on Tuesday. Kick-off is at 6 pm.

Antipas dropped Terrence Dzukamanja, Thabani Kamusoko and goalkeekper Talbert Shumba from the travelling squad.
