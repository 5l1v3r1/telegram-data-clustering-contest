Corrie star dies after telling friends she wasn't feeling well at party

The actress was best known for appearing in the soap and for her long standing role in Last of the Summer Wine

16 Nov 2019, 16:43 by Charlotte Hadfield

A Coronation Street star has died suddenly just a few days after telling her friends that she wasn't feeling well at a party.

The death of actress Jean Fergusson was confirmed on Friday by her manager.

Jean is best known for playing the part of Dorothy Hoyle in Coronation Street until 2011 and for her long standing role as Marina in Last of the Summer Wine from 1985 until it ended in 2010.

Just a few days ago, on Sunday, Jean had been with friends celebrating Su Pollard's birthday, when she posed with 'Allo 'Allo star Vicki Michelle for what could have been her final picture, reports the Mirror Online .
