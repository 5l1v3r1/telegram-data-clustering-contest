Funko Debuts The Dragon Prince's First Pop! Line

16 Nov 2019, 16:11 by Adreon Patterson

Netflix's animated series The Dragon Prince is the next big property to get the Funko Pop! treatment, as the company unveiled figurines based on human Callum, elf assassin Rayla, crown prince Ezran and the Dragon Prince, Zym.

On its website, Funko wrote, "Go on an epic adventure to the continent of Xadia where magic is very real and elves, humans and dragons struggle for power." The description reads, "Bring home your favorite The Dragon Prince characters including Pop! Callum who is the first human to wield primal magic, Pop! Rayla who is the Moonshadow elf assassin, Pop! Ezran the crown prince of Katolis who can speak with animals and Pop! Zym the infant sky dragon and titular Dragon Prince." Each figurine highlights the characters and their place within the animated series.

The Dragon Prince Funko Pop! figurines have not been given a release date, though Funko's website assures they will be available soon.

Featuring the voices of Jack DeSena as Callum, Sasha Rojen as Ezran, Paula Burrows as Rayla, Jesse Inocalla as Soren, Racquel Belmonte as Claudia, Luc Roderique as King Harrow of Katolis and Jason Simpson as Viren, The Dragon Prince Seasons 1 and 2 are available now on Netflix. Season 3 arrives Nov. 22.
