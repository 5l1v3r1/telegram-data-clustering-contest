UNLV honors 105-year-old alumna as honorary homecoming queen

16 Nov 2019, 16:31 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

LAS VEGAS -

The oldest Runnin' Rebel was the toast of homecoming ceremonies Friday night.

Audrey James, who is 105, was crowned 2019's honorary homecoming queen at her alma mater, the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, KLAS reported.
