Kirk Herbstreit Doesn’t Think Michigan-Michigan State Will Be Close

16 Nov 2019, 16:27 by Andrew Holleran

The Michigan-Michigan State rivalry has provided the college football world with some pretty crazy games over the years. Will we get another one today?

Kirk Herbstreit doesn’t think so. The ESPN college football analyst has unveiled his prediction for this afternoon’s rivalry game in Ann Arbor.

The College GameDay analyst likes Michigan – big.

“Michigan kills Michigan State today,” he said this morning.

ESPN’s Football Power Index projections seem to agree with Herbstreit, too. The computer model doesn’t think Michigan State has much of a chance.

ESPN’s Football Power Index is giving Michigan an 84.2 percent chance of winning today’s game. It gives Michigan State just a 15.8 percent chance of pulling off the upset.

Few head coaches need a win today more than Mark Dantonio, though it doesn’t look like anyone is expecting him to get one.

Michigan and Michigan State are set to kick off at noon E.T.

The game will be on FOX.
