Le Vignoble bar with wine on tap opening in Bristol this weekend

Up to 64 wines will be available at Le Vignoble

16 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Geoffrey Bennett

A new wine lounge and shop is opening in Bristol.

At 6pm today (November 16) the doors open at Le Vignoble in Finzel's Reach - which is being marketed as "wine heaven".

Set in the restored heritage brewery building, Le Vignoble will be offering up to 64 wines in a sophisticated bistro-style environment.

A changing monthly selection of wines will be available from self-service dispensers which keep the wine at the perfect temperature.

Le Vignoble is opening on the ground floor of the historic Fermentation Buildings and the interior has been decorated with reclaimed wood tables, vintage lights and aged leather Chesterfield sofas.

This will be first Le Vignoble in Bristol and the largest of their three sites, the others being in Bath and Plymouth.

Le Vignoble founder Yannick Loué is on a mission to take the snobbery out of the wine tasting tradition and help everyone to sample new wines and flavours.

Every wine comes with tasting notes and all staff are highly trained experts able to guide novices towards new grapes, regions and varieties.

An authentic French tapas-style food has been created to perfectly compliment the wine.

The menu includes meat and cheese platters, much of it imported direct from Paris, alongside several traditional hot dishes. 

There are also craft beers and ciders all sourced from the South West.

Mr Loué said: "I really want to democratise the wine tasting process and make it accessible to everyone.

"So many people stick to well-known wine brands because they don't have an easy way to find out more about the huge variety that's available.

'People are encouraged to become more adventurous'

"By enabling people to help themselves and sample wines in smaller quantities, people are encouraged to become more adventurous and really find out what they like."

Gavin Bridge, executive director at Cubex, the developer behind Finzels Reach, said: "Le Vignoble brings something completely new to the city's eating and drinking scene and I, for one, can't wait to try it out."

Another bar with wine taps opened in south Bristol last month.
