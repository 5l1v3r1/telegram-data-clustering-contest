Inside pictures and video from Aaradhya's birthday bash: Aishwarya, Abhishek and birthday girl Aaradhya Bachchan enjoy the 'Ferris-Wheel' ride

16 Nov 2019, 15:26 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Today, Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya's daughter Aaradhya turned a year older and the Bachchan family has organised for a lavish themed birthday party at Juhu to celebrate their dear members' special day. Celebs along with their kids have already marked their attendance at the birthday bash and the party is all set for the night.

Celebs like Shah Rukh Khan, Gauri with Abram, Karan Johar along with kids Roohi and Yash, Riteish and Genelia with their two sons and other biggies from the B-town have attended the birthday bash. While the party is on, we got our hands on some inside pictures from the bash.

In the pictures, we can see birthday girl, Aaradhya enjoying a Ferris-wheel round along with her friends. Not only the birthday girl but her parents, Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya too are enjoying the fun-ride.
