We’ll get our next look at Gears Tactics at The Game Awards in December

16 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Stephany Nunneley

We’ll finally get another look at Gears Tactics in December.

Our first and last glimpse of Gears Tactics occurred when it was announced at E3 2018, but that’s about to change in December.

According to a tweet from The Game Awards, The Coalition will be at the show to give us another look at the title.
