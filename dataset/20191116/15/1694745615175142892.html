Chrissy Teigen Wasted No Time Trolling John Legend About His Sexiest Man Alive Title

16 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Kelsie Gibson

John Legend is People's Sexiest Man Alive, and no one is prouder than his wife Chrissy Teigen. Shortly after the news was announced on Tuesday afternoon, the 33-year-old TV personality and author went on a hilarious Twitter spree. In addition to sharing their kids' reactions — spoiler: they weren't impressed — Chrissy wasted no time trolling her husband.

"My secret is out," she wrote alongside a photo of John's People magazine cover. "I have fulfilled my dream of having boned @people's sexiest man alive!! an honor!!!!!" She even changed her Twitter bio to "currently sleeping with people's sexiest man alive." John may be the sexiest man alive, but Chrissy is clearly the funniest person on Twitter.
