Pokemon Sword and Shield’s first Gigantamax Event is live with Butterfree

16 Nov 2019, 15:03 by Stephany Nunneley

Pokemon Sword and Shield players can now participate in the first Gigantamax Event featuring the Pokemon Butterfree.

Now through January 2020, you are more likely to encounter Gigantamax Butterfree in Pokemon Sword and Shield Max Raid Battles.

During this same period in Pokemon Sword, you are also more likely to encounter Gigantamax Drednaw. If you play Pokemon Shield you’re also more likely to encounter Gigantamax Corviknight.

Other Gigantamax Pokemon could appear more often than usual, so be sure to participate in Max Raid Battles.
