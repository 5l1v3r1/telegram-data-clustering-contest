Rare look into Russian military movements in Syria: video

16 Nov 2019, 11:20 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (1:10 P.M.M.) – The ANNA News Agency released a new video on Friday showing the Russian military carrying out patrols and movements across northern Syria.

In the four-minute-long video, the Russian military is seen entering different parts of northern Syria after they secured a deal with their Turkish counterparts.
