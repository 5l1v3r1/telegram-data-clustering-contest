WWE: The Fiend Unveils First Look at Universal Title Redesign

16 Nov 2019, 11:11 by L.D. Nolan

After overcoming Seth Rollins at WWE's Crown Jewel event in Saudi Arabia to become the new Universal Champion, Bray Wyatt/The Fiend has revealed a new look at the redesigned version of his belt.

The video, which appeared on Smackdown before later being uploaded to YouTube, features Bray hosting "Firefly Fun House." In the video, he transforms into a magician and changes the WWE Universal Championship belt, making the belt much more blue. The segment then transitions into Bray talking about an upcoming episode of "Miz TV" featuring Daniel Bryan.
