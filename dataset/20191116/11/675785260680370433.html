Ricky Hatton criticised for explicit fancy dress at family festival in Benidorm

Boxing legend Ricky Hatton has travelled to Benidorm for the resort's fancy dress season and had an interesting selection of clothing

16 Nov 2019, 11:36 by Liam Prenderville, http://www.facebook.com/mirrorfootball

Boxing legend Ricky Hatton has been criticised for his choice of fancy dress outfit.

Hatton has made a trip to Benidorm for the resort's fancy dress bash as part of its fiesta season.

The 41-year-old was pictured arriving as a jockey, with what appeared to be a giant penis hanging in front of his silks.

Images have emerged of Hatton laughing and joking with his fan as he held a pint.

The post was shared on a Benidorm Facebook page with many criticising the 'bad taste' outfit.

One person wrote: "It’s a bit much when there’s kids around.''

Thousands of Brits have descended on Benidorm once again to take part in the celebrations.

Hatton was joined by a group of pals in fancy dress, with others wearing costumes of priests, robbers and Snow White.

Meanwhile, Hatton is working with Love Island star Tommy Fury ahead of his return to the ring.

And Fury, brother of heavyweight superstar Tyson, has shot down rumours Hatton has prevented him from having sex with girlfriend Molly-Mae Hague.

Asked if Ricky had asked them to cool off their bedroom antics, Tommy gave a firm 'no.'

"No, Ricky trains me, he does everything perfect and at the end of the day my private life is my private life," he said.

Fury is set to make his long-awaited return to boxing on December 12.
