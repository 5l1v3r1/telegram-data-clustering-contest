Stats: Virat Kohli becomes 1st Indian captain with 10 innings wins

India extended their winning streak in Tests under Virat Kohli with a dominating victory over Bangladesh in the 1st Test. This was Kohli's 10th innings win as captain, making him the most successful Indian captain in this regard.

16 Nov 2019, 11:56 by Sports Desk

Virat Kohli’s Team India registered yet another dominating Test win over Bangladesh in Indore, wrapping up the game on Day 3 on Saturday, crushing the visitors by an innings and 130 runs.
