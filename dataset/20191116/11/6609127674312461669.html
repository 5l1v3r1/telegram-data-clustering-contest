STREET DRAMA

Two men rushed to hospital after cops cordon off Kirkcaldy street

16 Nov 2019, 11:50 by Rachel Macpherson

TWO men have been rushed to hospital this morning following a "disturbance" on a Kirkcaldy street.

Police rushed to the scene on Links Street - which has been cordoned off following the incident.

Emergency services arrived at the incident at around 10.50am following reports of a "disturbance" on Links Street.

It is understood that the two men do have not "life-threatening" injuries at this stage.

A Police Scotland spokeswoman said: "We were called at ten to eleven this morning following a report that two men had been injured during a disturbance on links street in Kirkcaldy.

"Both men have been taken to Victoria Hospital in Kirkcaldy.

"At this time we don't have details on their injuries but medics do not believe they are life-threatening at this stage.

"Enquires on going."
