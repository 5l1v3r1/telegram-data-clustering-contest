Titans: Curran Walters Celebrates Season 3 with BTS Robin Photo

16 Nov 2019, 11:11 by John Ridlehoover

To celebrate the recent announcement of Titans Season 3, Curran Walters shared a behind-the-scenes image of himself in full Robin gear.

In the image Walters, who stars as Jason Todd/Robin on the series, is sitting in a chair with his legs up. "Straight chillin. Mood going into season 3," reads the caption provided with the image.
