Thiago Silva slams Lionel Messi after Barca star tells Brazil coach to 'shut it'

16 Nov 2019, 11:44

Thiago Silva has slammed Lionel Messi for his conduct during Argentina’s victory over Brazil.

The Barcelona superstar scored the only goal of the prestige friendly in Saudi Arabia, reacting fastest in the 14th minute, converting the rebound after Liverpool goalkeeper Alisson Becker had saved Messi’s penalty.

Messi, making his return from a three-month international ban having accused tournament organisers of fixing the Copa America in Brazil’s favour, was also involved in the game’s most controversial moment when he told Selecao coach, Tite, to ‘shut it’.

PSG defender Silva accused Messi of showing signs of disrespect and believes the 32-year-old is guilty of trying to referee matches on his own and place too much pressure on the officials.

‘He wanted to rule over the game,’ said Silva. ‘He kicked two people and the referee did nothing.
