Aston Villa legend issues injury update and fans will love it

Aston Villa latest news | European cup hero Peter Withe has undergone surgery on both knees and he has issued an update on his recovery as he battles back to full fitness

16 Nov 2019, 11:37 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa legend Peter Withe has posted an update on his knees.

In August he confirmed that he had undergone surgery on both knees as well as having a hip replacement.

Withe is a legend at Villa Park , netting 90 goals in over 200 games for the club including the only goal in the 1982 European Cup success against Bayern Munich.

And the claret and blue faithful will be glad to hear that he is on the mend after surgery.

In the message, Withe has updated fans on his condition with the 68-year-old well on the road to recovery.

Earlier this year he revealed that he was in remission following a cancer scare , confirming in March that he was diagnosed with a melanoma that had been removed through surgery.

In Withe's most recent health update, posted on Facebook, he wrote: "Operation both knees 10/06/2019 healing knees today coming along nicely."
