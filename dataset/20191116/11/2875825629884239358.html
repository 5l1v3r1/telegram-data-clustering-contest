Irish weather: Met Eireann forecast fog to descend during frosty weekend in Ireland

Temperatures will plummet to minus two degrees

16 Nov 2019, 11:46 by Colin Brennan

Ireland will have to wrap up warm as Met Eireann say there's a chilly weekend in store for Ireland

Saturday will be a cold day with frost, icy patches and mist.

Met Eireann forecast most places will be dry, there'll be a few showers on the northwest coast and there'll be hazy sunshine for a time.

Tonight will have lowest temperatures minus 1 to plus 3 °C with temperatures rising a little around dawn.

Sunday will start mostly cloudy with isolated showers, but the afternoon is expected to be dry and sunny.
