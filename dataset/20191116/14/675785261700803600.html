BREAKING Body of woman, 25, found near Worcester fire station as man arrested

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

16 Nov 2019, 14:26 by Tom Davidson, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/tom-davidson/

The body of a 25-year-old woman was found near a fire station this morning.

Police were called to the scene ear to Upton on Severn in Worcester at roughly 8am.

A 44-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is currently in police custody.

Detective Inspector Jim Bayliss said: “Our enquiries are very much in their early stages and at this stage the woman’s death is being treated as suspicious. A man has been arrested and is currently in police custody.

“Extra officers will be in the area, both to carry out enquiries and to offer reassurance.”
