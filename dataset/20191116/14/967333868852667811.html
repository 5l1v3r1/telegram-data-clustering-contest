Bird Attack! Crow Teases Dachshund in a Vienna Park

Crows are known to be very protective and territorial birds. They don't actively hunt dogs, but they do see dogs as predators and can attack them.

16 Nov 2019, 14:00

An adorable dachshund called Herbert was walking with his owner, in a park in Vienna, Austria, when a crow started to tease the little dog. The bird slowly neared the dog and bit its tail, then it flew away.

"We are frequently at this park, and these crows often bully our 7-year-old dachshund, Herbert", the owner said.
