HDFC Bank Crosses Rs 7 Trillion Market-cap

16 Nov 2019, 14:00

Mumbai: India’s largest private sector bank HDFC Bank on Friday surpassed Rs 7 trillion in market capitalisation for the first time. HDFC is only the third Indian company after TCS and Reliance Industries to reach this milestone. The private lender on Friday touched Rs 7,00,252.30 crore market capitalisation, a metric used to evaluate the size of a company.

Currently Reliance Industries with a market cap of Rs 9,39,463.36 crore, is the country’s largest company followed by Tata Consultancy Services at Rs 8,25,168.16 crore. At 11.25 am, HDFC Bank was trading at Rs 1,278.90 a share, up by 0.41 per cent. (IANS)
