Malden PD find missing man who suffers from dementia and Alzheimer's

16 Nov 2019, 14:42 by EndPlay

MALDEN, Mass. - Malden Police announced that Stephen Lafrance and his dog have been found safe and reunited with family on Saturday.

The 56-year-old Malden resident and his beagle dog Ellie were reported missing on Friday. Police asked for the public's help in finding him, as he suffers from dementia and Alzheimer's disease.

At the time, he was last seen walking his dog along the Granville Avenue and Salem Street area of the city on Friday afternoon.
