The social media activity that might cause a transfer stir with Aston Villa fans

Aston Villa transfer news | A potential transfer hint has been dropped with the window re-opening in the New Year as Dean Smith looks to improve his Premier League side

16 Nov 2019, 14:37 by Richard Cusack

Jarrod Bowen has fuelled speculation of a move to Aston Villa by following the club on Instagram.

The Hull City forward also follows Villa stars Jack Grealish and Tyrone Mings on the photo-sharing platform.

Bowen burst onto the scene when he netted in the 1-1 draw with Villa in August 2017 - he has since gone on to score 47 goals for Hull in 116 appearances from the wing.

He scored 15 that season, 22 last campaign and has nine goals in 16 Championship games.
