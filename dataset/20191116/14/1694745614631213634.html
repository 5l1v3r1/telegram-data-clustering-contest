When you boil down the best of Fall fashion, cute sweaters, cozy loungewear sets, and coats are often at the crux, right? Think about it: when it's chilly, what's better than throwing on a sweater that feels like a warm hug, lounging around the house in supersoft joggers (that you can totally style with heels if that's your thing), or layering a plush coat over jeans, leggings, dresses, and skirts?

While we live for great boots, layerable dresses, and cool jeans this time of year, these cozy style staples are some of our absolute favorites. So we naturally had to design new-season versions of all of them in the latest POPSUGAR collection at Kohl's, and let me tell you, every piece is C-U-T-E!

From stylish sweaters with trendy puffed sleeves to the coolest puffer jackets and a supercute "Love" sweatshirt (which Kerry Washington has already worn!), the new-for-November arrivals are what Fall fashion dreams are made of. Also involved? A versatile printed top (we're already planning layered looks and date-night options for this!) and the perfect pair of black jeans you can dress up or down. In other words, your cold-weather evening essentials.

What's more, each cozy piece makes for a great holiday gift for friends, family, or your most fashionable coworkers, especially when said gifts are affordable to the tune of $40 or $50 (which most pieces in the collection are!).
