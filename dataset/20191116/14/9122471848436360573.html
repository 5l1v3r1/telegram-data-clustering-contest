ESPN’s Paul Finebaum Predicts The Winner Of Auburn-Georgia

16 Nov 2019, 14:04 by Andrew Holleran

One of the biggest games on college football’s Week 12 slate is taking place in Auburn this afternoon. The Tigers are set to host Georgia in a major SEC showdown at Jordan-Hare Stadium.

The game has serious College Football Playoff implications. The Bulldogs remain in the thick of the race. There are even some who believe Auburn could get into contention if it wins out.

ESPN’s Paul Finebaum has made his pick for the game.

The college football analyst likes the ‘Dawgs.

“I like the Dawgs. I think they’ve been a sneaky good team all year,” Finebaum said of Georgia.
