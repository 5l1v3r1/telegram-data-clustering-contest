Little Mix searching for Scots talent at open mic auditions in Edinburgh

Producers from a brand new talent show Little Mix The Search will host the event on Monday night.

16 Nov 2019, 14:31 by Daniel Morrow, Neil Pooran, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

Pop sensations Little Mix are looking for Scots singers to get involved in open mic auditions for their new BBC talent show.

Producers from Little Mix The Search will be holding an event in Edinburgh on Monday night.

Those successful in the auditions will be given the opportunity to be mentored by Perrie Edwards, Jesy Nelson, Jade Thirlwall and Leigh-Anne Pinnock as part of the new BBC One programme.

Winners of the show will form an all-female, all-male or mixed group who will have the chance to go on tour with Little Mix in 2020, Edinburgh Live reports

 

The singers, who make it into new bands, will live together and gain access to Little Mix’s inner circle who have contributed to their phenomenal success, including vocal coaches, song writers, producers and stylists.

Those interested in auditioning can go to the Footlights bar on Spittal Street on Monday, November 18 from 6pm onwards.

Audition slots can be booked by emailing john.ferrier1956@gmail.com. Under-18s will need permission from parents or guardians.

Little Mix The Search will be on BBC One and iPlayer next year.

Little Mix said: "We want to create lots of incredible groups who really gel. As we have been there and done it ourselves, we know what it takes to make a group successful, and will be there to mentor them every step of the way.

"We want solo artists to audition as well as groups and every type of genre is welcome too. It's going to be really exciting and people can apply right now!"

Andrea Hamilton, executive producer at ModestTV, said: "It’s a real privilege to be building a new show around such a successful and loved British band. Creating bands means relationships matter, and there’s no one better to share that experience than Little Mix, who are living it right now."
