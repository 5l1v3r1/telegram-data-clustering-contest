Stonebwoy Spotted On A Giant Quad Bike Wearing A Bandana Instead Of Helmet- Photo

16 Nov 2019, 14:07 by Ghbase New Writer

Stonebwoy has been spotted on a giant quad bike with a beautiful bandana worn around his head instead of a helmet.

We don’t know where exactly he was going or whether he posed on the bike to take a shot from the lenses of the camera.

Whatever the motive is, he should never compromise on his safety.

In a related development, The BET award winning artist confirmed to Abeiku Santana on Okay FM in an interview that he’s dropping three songs this December.

Stonebwoy also confirmed featuring Kuame Eugene and Kidi on one of the songs (we guess it is the ‘Sobolo’). He also revealed he has one song produced by Beatz Dakay titled ‘More’ with the third song unknown.
