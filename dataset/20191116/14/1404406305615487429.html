Donald Trump Was Told to Let A$AP Rocky Get Sentenced in Sweden

16 Nov 2019, 14:16 by VladTV

According to the testimony of David Holmes—an official of the US Embassy in Kiev, Donald Trump was advised to let A$AP Rocky get sentenced to “play the racism card.”

In Holmes’ testimony, he said Trump spoke on the phone with Gordon Sondland, an American ambassador to the European Union, and said Sondland told Trump that A$AP Rocky “should have pled guilty.”

Holmes said Sondland told Trump, “let [Rocky] get sentenced, play the racism card, give him a ticker-tape when he comes home." Sondland also reportedly said that if Trump couldn’t get Rocky out of the jam, he could “tell the Kardashians you tried.”

source: New York Times
