Iranians Protest Over Hike In Gas Prices

16 Nov 2019, 14:23 by AP

Protesters angered by Iran raising subsidized fuel prices blocked traffic in at least a dozen cities and clashed occasionally with police on November 16, the second day of sometimes violent street protests. Mobile-phone footage shows a large crowd of protesters in the city of Kermanshah, which later drew tear-gas fire from police. Protesters were heard chanting: "Police, support us!"
