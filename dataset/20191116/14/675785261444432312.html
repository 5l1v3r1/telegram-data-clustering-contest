Moise Kean 'offered to AC Milan' by Mino Raiola after nightmare Everton transfer

Everton signed Moise Kean in a £27m deal from Juventus in the summer but he has so far failed to adapt to life in the Premier League

16 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Liam Prenderville, http://www.facebook.com/mirrorfootball

Moise Kean has reportedly been offered to AC Milan after a nightmare spell at Everton .

Kean, 19, joined Everton in a £27m deal from Juventus in the summer but has struggled to adapt to the Premier League.

He hasn't found the net for Everton and was left out of the squad for the clash with Southampton after turning up late for a team meeting.

Everton are not thought to be considering letting him go but reports in Italy suggest he could head back to his homeland.

Corriere dello Sport claims Kean's agent Mino Raiola has offered the striker to Milan as they look to add to their ranks in January.

But the Serie A giants are not thought to be convinced by a deal for the teenager.

A more experienced option is on their radar and Milan could rival Manchester United for Mario Mandzukic.

Roma are also interested in Kean, whose father has claimed he made a mistake by moving to Goodison Park.

"Sending my son to England was a mistake, because he's still too young, he's not feeling good at Everton, I didn't like this transfer," Jean said.

"At Everton, where he is playing now, he is not settling in well. I think he was supposed to wait a few more years before he had an experience abroad.

"If there is a possibility for him to come back to Italy, I hope he can do that, so as not to ruin him."
