Everton U21s find out next EFL Trophy opponents after winning group

Everton will play their Round Two fixture at home

16 Nov 2019, 14:21 by Adam Jones

Everton will play Fleetwood Town at home in the next round of the EFL Trophy after progressing through the group stage as winners.

David Unsworth's made it to Round Two of the Leasing.com Trophy for the first time thanks to a win over Burton Albion and draws with Crewe Alexandra and Mansfield - winning a penalty shootout to take an extra point following the latter.

The young Blues are taking part in the tournament for the fourth time, but will be playing their first knockout match this year.

The tie is set for the week commencing December 2nd, the same week the senior side will be facing Liverpool in the first Merseyside derby of the season.

Everton finished one point clear at the top of thier group, with League Two side Mansfield Town finishing in second place.

"To be unbeaten against three Football League sides, to win one and draw two, that makes me very proud," Unsworth told the ECHO after watching his side draw with Crewe.

"To get through to the latter stages is great for the players. We haven't managed to do it in the last couple of years, so that's been a bit of a disappointment.

"But tonight we got the result and I'm looking forward to these latter stages."
