[Bayelsa election] PHOTOS: Voters wade through mud in Southern Ijaw

16 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Oluwakemi Abimbola

Some electorate whose polling units fell in Ayama Ijaw Community Secondary School, Southern Ijaw Local Government Area,  Bayelsa State, had to wade through the mud to cast their votes on Saturday.

In photos captured by our correspondent, some of the voters waded through the mud barefoot, while a few had on some footwear.
