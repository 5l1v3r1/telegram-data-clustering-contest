Path of Exile 2 in development, beta planned for “very late 2020”

16 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Stephany Nunneley

Path of Exile 2 was announced during ExileCon 2019, and it will be available alongside the original game’s campaign.

Grinding Gear Games has announced Path of Exile 2 is in the works. It will include a seven-act storyline available alongside the original game’s campaign.

According to the studio, both the old and new storylines will lead to the same, shared Atlas endgame. It will also retain all of the expansion content created over the last six years and engine improvements.

“Over the years there have been many changes that we would have loved to make to but couldn’t because they would break existing characters,” said the studio. “This is an opportunity to make all of these changes in one large update.”
