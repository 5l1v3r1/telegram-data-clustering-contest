Off-duty NYPD cop killed, 2 critically injured, in horror crash after car wraps around median: reports

16 Nov 2019, 14:40 by Paulina Dedaj

An off-duty New York City cop is dead and two other people were critically injured when their car crashed into a median on the highway early Saturday morning, reports say.

A 25-year-old, believed to be an off-duty NYPD officer, was driving northbound on the FDR near 23rd St. at around 2:30 a.m. when he reportedly lost control of his 2016 black Lexus, crashing into a median beneath a pedestrian footbridge, the New York Post reported citing police.
