Kahaani Ghar Ghar Ki Actress Raped By Junior Artist, Left Pregnant - Reports

16 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Nikita Thakkar

As per reports, a TV actress has come forward and lodged a complaint against a Junior artist who allegedly raped her in a hotel room and subsequently made her pregnant

A TV actress who has worked in many hit serials like Kahaani Ghar Ghar Ki, Des Mein Nikla Hoga Chand and Nach Baliye has come forward to speak about an unwanted incident. She has filed a complaint with police that a Junior artist raped her in a hotel room and subsequently got her pregnant. The accused who hails from Yamunanagar in Haryana is absconding now.

As per a report in timesnownews.com, TV actress and the junior artist became friends in the month of October this year. He then took her to a hotel room, drugged her and raped her. Upon learning that she has become pregnant, the accused has gone missing. The actress claims that his family based in Yamunanagar knows about his actions and are rather supporting him. They are unwilling to support the victim in any way as claimed by the actress.

Reportedly, the TV actress proposed marriage as she learnt that she is pregnant. However, the man refused went missing and she had to lodge a complaint in Yamunanagar police station, state reports. The unnamed actress and the junior artist first met in Mumbai and have worked together in a few shows. They became good friends until this unfortunate incident.
