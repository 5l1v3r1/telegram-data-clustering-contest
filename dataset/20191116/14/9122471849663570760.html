ESPN’s Kirk Herbstreit Warns LSU’s Players Before Today’s Game

16 Nov 2019, 14:14 by Andrew Holleran

No college football team was riding higher this week than LSU. The Tigers finally took down Alabama, knocking off the Crimson Tide in Bryant-Denny Stadium, and rose to the No. 1 overall spot in the College Football Playoff rankings as a result.

The season is far from over, though. LSU still has a couple of regular season games left and a potential SEC Championship Game before getting to the College Football Playoff.

Today, LSU is taking on Ole Miss.

ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit thinks the Tigers need to be careful.

“I just hope the players are locked in,” Herbstreit said on College GameDay this morning. “Ole Miss can run the football…they’ll challenge LSU’s defense today.”
