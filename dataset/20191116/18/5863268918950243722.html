Preparations for Nawaz Sharif's treatment in London completed

16 Nov 2019, 18:29 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – After the order of the Lahore High Court (LHC) to remove former premier Nawaz Sharif’s name from the Exit Control List (ECL), preparations of his travel to London has started.

According to Dunya News, Nawaz’s sons, Hassan and Hussain Nawaz, called Maryam Nawaz and apprised her about the arrangements they are making with medical experts. Maryam Nawaz expressed that all the arrangements has been completed here while she is just waiting for the clearance certificate.

According to sources, an air ambulance reservation is also underway to take Nawaz Sharif to London. Former premier will be treated at Harley Street Clinic in London. The Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) supremo will be shifted to hospital as soon as he arrives in London.

Currently, the Sharif family is waiting for the Interior Ministry to exclude Nawaz’s name from the ECL.  

Earlier, while rejecting the objection by federal government on its proposed draft, the Lahore High Court on Saturday ordered to remove name of former premier from Exit Control List (ECL) and allowed him to travel abroad to get health treatment for four weeks.
