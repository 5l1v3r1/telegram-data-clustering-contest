Aston Villa tipped to sign Rangers striker in January transfer window

Latest Aston Villa transfer news | The January transfer window is just around the corner, and plenty of players are being linked with a move to the Premier League and to Villa Park next year

16 Nov 2019, 18:55 by Richard Cusack, Mark Wakefield

Aston Villa continue to be linked with Glasgow Rangers striker Alfredo Morelos .

The Colombian international has been frequently tipped to sign for Dean Smith’s side in the upcoming January transfer window.

Villa currently have summer recruit Wesley as the club’s first-choice striker, after the Brazilian signed for a fee of £22m from Club Brugge.

It is no surprise to see Morelos linked with a move to England, given his recent comments about being interested in signing for a club south of the border.

 

The former HJK Helsinki man netted his 22nd goal in 26 matches on Sunday, the second in a 2-0 win at Livingston.

He has nine goals in 12 matches for Steven Gerrard's side, who are level with Scottish Premiership League leaders Celtic on 31 points.

Premier League rivals Leicester City were rumoured to be interested in Morelos, as are Crystal Palace.
