Compensation will be given to affected farmers: Minister

16 Nov 2019, 18:23 by Staff Reporter

Minister for School Education, Sports and Youth Welfare K.A. Sengottaiyan has said that compensation of ₹ 5,400 per acre would be given to farmers whose crops were affected due to breach in the left bank of Lower Bhavani Project in Mallanaickanoor recently.

The Minister along with Minister for Environment K.C. Karuppannan in the presence of Collector C. Kathiravan and MLAs distributed assistance to the tune of Rs. 2.93 crore to 3,035 beneficiaries in Gobichettipalayam and Nambiyur blocks who submitted petitions during the Chief Minister Special Grievances Redressal Programme held in August.

Mr. Sengottaiyan said that 57,000 petitions were received from the public of which 32,000 were accepted.

So far, officials had assessed the damage to crops on 110 acre in three villages that were affected by flood. He added that new houses would be constructed for people whose houses were damaged beyond repairs.

The Minister said work on four lane had began on Chithode – Gobichettipalayam – Nilgiris road and Bhavani – Appakudal – Athani – Sathyamangalam road.

Check dams would be constructed across River Bhavani at ₹ 200 crore and a survey was being done for the work, he added.

Superintendent of Police S. Sakthi Ganesan, and District Revenue Officer S. Kavitha were present.
