Bettor Drops Nearly $14K On Rutgers To Defeat The Overwhelmingly Favored Ohio State Buckeyes

16 Nov 2019, 18:09 by Darrelle Lincoln

Do you want to lose thousands of dollars for nothing, because this is how you lose thousands of dollars for nothing.

The Ohio State Buckeyes are such heavy favorites for their game on Saturday that it borderlines on being quite ridiculous.

Per Bovada, Ohio State is favored to beat Rutgers by 53 points.

That didn’t seem to matter t one bettor because Ben Fawkes of ESPN reported a person laid down $13,750 on Rutgers to beat the 53-point spread.
