Video: Nebraska Defense Gives Up Embarrassing TD vs. Wisconsin

16 Nov 2019, 18:20 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, the Nebraska Cornhuskers hosted the Wisconsin Badgers in a pivotal game for both programs.

Nebraska, which sits at 4-5 on the season, is in desperate need of a win to keep its bowl hopes alive. Meanwhile, Wisconsin is hoping to keep its momentum going after a lull in the middle of the season with losses to Illinois and Ohio State.

The early action favored the Huskers, who jumped out to a 7-0 lead.

However, the Badgers have battled back and actually took the lead on a touchdown pass from Jack Coan to wide receiver A.J. Taylor.

Taylor caught a short slant pass and took it 55 yards to the house, thanks to some very poor tackling from the Nebraska secondary.
