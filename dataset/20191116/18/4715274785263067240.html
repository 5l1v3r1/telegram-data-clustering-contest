'Bayong,' not plastic

16 Nov 2019, 18:23 by Manny Palmero

Environmental groups belonging to the Global Break Free from Plastic Movement hold up ‘bayong’ or woven leaf bags as they stage a protest in front of the head office of Department of Environment and Natural Resources in Quezon City, criticizing the agency’s ‘hypocritical and pro-industry stance’ on plastic pollution.
