You cannot miss these pictures of Shah Rukh Khan with Dua Lipa

16 Nov 2019, 18:51 by Filmfare

Popular singer, songwriter and model Dua Lipa is in Mumbai currently for her India concert and that sure has got all music lovers excited. Ahead of her concert, Dua met Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan and their pictures have already gone viral on social media. Wishing her luck and love for her show tonight, King Khan shared pictures with the pop sensation.

SRK captioned the post as, “Have decided to live by New Rules’ and who better to learn them from but @dualipa herself!! What a charming and beautiful young lady....& her voice!! Wish her all my love for the concert Tonite. Dua if you can, try the steps I taught u on stage.”

Check it out here.

For the uninitiated, this isn't Dua's first visit to India. She had holidayed here last year in October with boyfriend Isaac Crew and visited places including Ranthambore, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kerala and Goa. She will be performing with Katy Perry at the concert. Stay tuned for more updates on the same.
