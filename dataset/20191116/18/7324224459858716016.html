Molineux sleepout raises incredible £30,000 for homeless in Wolverhampton - with VIDEO and PICTURES

A sleepout held at Molineux has raised £30,000 to help homeless people in Wolverhampton.

16 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Jamie Brassington

About two hundred Wolves fans slept in the stands on Friday night. There were special guests amongst the crowd including former players Carl Ikeme, Karl Henry and Matt Murray, and music producer S-X.

The sleepout, which lasted from 7pm to 7am, raised money for Good Shepherd Charity and the Wolves Foundation.
