maharishi Drops Seasonal DPM: "CHACRUNA FOREST STRIPE"

Inspired by South American medicine, Ayahuasca.

16 Nov 2019, 12:42 by Ambrose Leung

maharishi kicks-off a new Fall/Winter DPM offering with the introduction of disruptive pattern “Chacruna Forest.” The pattern utilizes parts of Belgian and American Tigerstripe camouflage patterns along with the use of Cuba’s “Elm Leaf” camo. The background of the pattern is then filled by Ayahuasca shaped vines, all the while reshaping the elm leaf to look closer to the “Chacruna leaf.”

Accompanying the seasonal DPM is maharishi’s lookbook featuring the DPM pattern across a series of jackets, sweats, camo barboutas, and T-shirts. Take a look at the set of styled images above and head over to maharishi’s web store to shop the collection.
