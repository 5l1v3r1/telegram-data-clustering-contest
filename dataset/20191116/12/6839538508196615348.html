Cyclist rushed to Royal Perth Hospital with life-threatening injuries after being struck by car in Girrawheen

16 Nov 2019, 12:45

A male cyclist has been rushed to Royal Perth Hospital with life threatening injuries after being hit by a car in Girrawheen.

The 20-year-old was riding along Marangaroo Drive around 7.30pm when the accident happened.

Police have taken one person into custody and are continuing their investigation.
