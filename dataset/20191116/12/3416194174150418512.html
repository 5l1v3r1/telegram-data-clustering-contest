Dove Cameron Is Not Okay After Meeting Paul Rudd

16 Nov 2019, 12:21

Dove Cameron had quite the meeting at the HFPA & THR Celebration of the 2020 Golden Globe Awards Season event on Thursday (November 14).

The 23-year-old actress and singer met one of her favorite actors Paul Rudd!

“here’s the thing , here’s the thing . here it is. !! there is nothing that i could say to go along with this photo because it is perfect. thank you for listening. please respect my privacy and leave me be for a few days while i recover,” she captioned a photo of the two.

A year ago, Dove freaked out over Paul talking about her in the Clueless musical on Broadway. She also met the original Cher, Alicia Silverstone, during her run in the show.
