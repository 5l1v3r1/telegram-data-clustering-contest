Photos: Shah Rukh Khan wishes Dua Lipa for her concert, asks to try the steps he taught her

16 Nov 2019, 12:34 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

On a dull Saturday afternoon, it was Shah Rukh Khan's Instagram post with the famous singer Dua Lipa that lit up the mood, literally! . Shah Rukh Khan shared a few amazing pictures of him posing with the pop star and trust us the actor looked fresh like never before.

It was his new and fresh look that attracted millions of eye-balls. As today, the very famous Katy Perry and Dua Lipa concert will take place in Navi Mumbai tonight and the king khan has a special wish for the singer. SRK took to his Instagram handle and shared a few pictures with Dua as the actor meets her ahead of the concert. Not only did he shared the dazzling pictures, but he also wished the singer. He wrote, "Have decided to live by ‘New Rules’ and who better to learn them from but @dualipa herself!! What a charming and beautiful young lady....& her voice!! Wish her all my love for the concert Tonite. Dua if you can try the steps I taught u on stage."

Shah Rukh also asked Dua to try the steps on the stage which he taught her. Well, this is something to watch out for.

SRK looked charming as ever in a blue checkered shirt, paired with denim. His hairstyle and those stylish sun-glasses are giving the actor the needed 'cool' look. Dua, on the other hand, looked gorgeous in a beige-colored crop top and black denim. Her wacky hair-style is adding drama to her look.
