Disney Channel Is Holding a Digital Open Casting Call, Submit Your Audition!

16 Nov 2019, 12:06

Do you think you have what it takes to be on the Disney Channel?!

Now’s your chance, because the network is holding a digital open casting call for everyone, now through December 31.

If you are able to play a role from ages 8-16, you can submit your audition, as long as you meet all the guidelines, and have parent’s permission.

“In our ongoing efforts to reach a broader pool of talent across the U.S., our digital open casting call allows parents whose kids aspire to be actors the opportunity to submit their child’s audition right from where they live. This also gives Disney Channel a chance to potentially discover fresh, young and diverse talent who may not yet have agency representation,” Disney Channel Senior VP in Casting and Talent Relations, Judy Taylor, said in a statement.

Raven’s Home‘s Issac Ryan Brown and Gabby Duran & The Unsittables‘ Kylie Cantrall even put together a video with some tips and tricks to help. Check it out!
