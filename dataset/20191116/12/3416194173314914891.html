JoJo Siwa Totally Transforms Into a VSCO Girl

16 Nov 2019, 12:58

JoJo Siwa is trying out a new look in her new video!

The 16-year-old entertainer transformed from her usual ponytail, big bows and sparkly outfits to an almost full on VSCO girl.

If you didn’t know, a VSCO girl is “basically the most basic girl you’ll find out there. Common interests include scrunchies, hydro flasks, seashell chokers, and Birkenstock’s,” according to an Urban Dictionary definition.

In the video, JoJo even gave fans a good look at her with her hair down, out of a ponytail, and without a bow.

In case you missed the exciting news, JoJo just announced that she is extending her D.R.E.A.M. The Tour for 50 more dates in 2020!
