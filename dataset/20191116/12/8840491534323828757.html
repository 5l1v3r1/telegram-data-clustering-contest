Get ready for Christmas jumper walk, run and stroll

16 Nov 2019, 12:00

MAYO footballers Paddy Durcan and Diarmuid O’Connor are encouraging walkers, runners and strollers to come out in support of the Christmas jumper walk, run and stroll on December 8 around Lough Lannagh, Castlebar.

This fun, family friendly event, now in its fourth year, appeals to all ages and abilities, raising funds for the Irish Cancer Society on a festive jaunt around Lough Lannagh.

There is a trophy for best Christmas themed group, prizes for the best Christmas jumper, first finisher and last finisher.

Paddy and Diarmuid, at the launch, said the event was a great way to get everyone active, no matter what level of fitness they have.

Diarmuid commented that raising funds that support life-saving cancer research and support for patients and their loved ones all through the holidays is something we should all try to be a part of.

Paddy acknowledged the dedication of the night nursing team and spoke about the important and caring service they provide not only to homes in Mayo but throughout the country. The nurses work 365 days a year and we should all do our best to support the Irish Cancer Society sustain this service.

The event, on December 8, is at 1 p.m., from Castlebar Tennis Club. Registration is open from 12 midday.

If you would like to take a sponsorship card for the event just drop a line to ltoner@irishcancer.ie. If you raise €50 sponsorship there's no need to pay on the day.

Registration on the day is €10 for grown-ups, €5 for students and smallies, or €20 per family.

Check out https://www.facebook.com/ChristmasJumperRunCastlebar/ for photos and updates.

A special thanks to sponsors such as House of Plates, Sharon Ralph Hair Salon and Scalp Clinic, Duggan & Barry Solicitors, Blue Thunder, Westport, and many more for making this event possible this year.
