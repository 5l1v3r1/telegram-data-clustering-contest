Tonto Dikeh Reveals How Bobrisky Spent 47k Dollars On One Shoe In Dubai(+Screenshot)

16 Nov 2019, 12:06 by vanessa nordzi

When you have money spending it becomes easy. Whiles some of us have been crying over the hardship of the economy, Nigerian cross dresser Bobrisky doesn’t seem to feel the heat at all.

According to Nollywood actress Tonto Dikeh who is currently in Dubai shopping, she was shocked to the core when she went shopping with Bobrisky and saw him spend 47k Dollars on one shoe, two bags and a necklace.

Explaining what happened in a post on her Instagram page, Tonto Dikeh said that instead of spending that huge amount of money on a single shoe and bag, Bobrisky should rather donate it to her charity organization to put smiles on the faces of the less privileged this Christmas.

She wrote ; Bob spent 47k dollars on 1 shoe and 2bags and necklace in my front..ooo I am holding myself back from crying…. Can you all tell Bobrisky to pls Donate for #10,000 SmilesforChristmas by the Tonto Dikeh Foundation.
#PS- I miss my single girls days when I was wild, money careless and free egennnn(pikin cry lol)”.

Well, whatever Bobrisky is doing is definitely putting huge sums of money in his pocket for him to be able for afford such luxury.
