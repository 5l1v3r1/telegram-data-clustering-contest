Serious Crash, Tuakau Bridge-Port Waikato Road

16 Nov 2019, 12:00 by New Zealand Police

"Serious Crash, Tuakau Bridge-Port Waikato Road"

Police are attending a serious crash on the Tuakau Bridge-Port Waikato Road.

Police were advised of the two-vehicle collision at around 9.50am.

One person is reported to have received serious injuries.

While a diversion is in place, motorists are asked to avoid the area.
