Date revealed for West Bridgford Christmas Lights Switch-On - and Santa's coming to visit

There will be fireworks and a Christmas market for people to enjoy

16 Nov 2019, 12:41 by Matthew Bunn

Street performers, fireworks and a festive market will all be on offer in West Bridgford next weekend as the town's Christmas Lights Switch-On takes place.

Councillor Christine Jeffreys, Mayor of West Bridgford , will turn on the lights during the event on Saturday, November 23.

Bridgford Road, Central Avenue and Gordon Road will all be illuminated by the lights until January.

Councillor Jeffreys said: "Such events are a great opportunity to showcase everything our local economy has to offer and we as a council are keen to promote the successful businesses across Rushcliffe at this busy time of year.

â€œI would encourage everyone to come along and support their local businesses and traders for this fabulous start to the festive season."

The switch-on itself will take place at 5pm but there will be lots of festivities taking place throughout the day.

Seasonal entertainment will be on offer from 10am with performers, local singers and a Christmas market on the croquet lawn. Stalls will also be set up by young entrepreneurs in Gordon Road from 12noon.

Elsewhere, children and their parents can meet Santa Claus at his Gordon Road grotto from 12.30-2pm and 3-4pm, while a Frozen-themed snowflake trail will also happen at 4pm.

A fireworks display will follow the switch-on.
