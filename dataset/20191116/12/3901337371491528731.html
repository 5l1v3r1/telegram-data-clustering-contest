'England Star Would Be Crazy To Join Man Utd'

16 Nov 2019, 12:32 by SL International

Borussia Dortmund star Jadon Sancho would be crazy to join 'struggling' Manchester United, according to a former Liverpool star.

Read: Expected January Signing To Start Training With Man Utd?

Sancho rose to prominence in the Bundesliga last season and has reportedly attracted interest from the likes of the Red Devils and Liverpool.

The English winger left Manchester City in search of regular game time, but former Reds defender Steve Nicol feels Sancho would be making a mistake by joining United.

“I think he’s crazy if he goes to Man Utd.

“We’re talking about he’s struggling in a team that’s struggling (Dortmund) - Man United are going to be struggling for a couple of seasons, so I would stay away from there.”

“Well, if I was advising him, let me put it that way, I would like to see him at Liverpool.

“I think he would be fantastic. If he could get his head round playing second fiddle to Mane and Salah, then that would be huge for Liverpool in my opinion.

“They could save the legs of both of those two and kind of swap the three of them in and out.

“But he’s not going to do that, he’s going to go somewhere to play. So I think Liverpool’s out.”
