Gaten Matarazzo Feels Like He's In An Episode of 'Black Mirror' Sometimes

16 Nov 2019, 12:37

Gaten Matarazzo is opening up about getting recognized more often.

The 17-year-old actor has two shows on Netflix right now – Stranger Things and Prank Encounters.

Gaten recently shared a story of the length fans go to get a photo and how would prefer they go about it.

“I was at Starbucks today and someone was sneaking a photo through the window and videotaping me. It almost feels like that Black Mirror episode,” he told MTV.

“I get why people do it. But I’d much rather people come up and talk to me,” Gaten added. “It feels creepy when I look around and people are taking pictures [of me]. I feel uncomfortable doing what I want and being myself.”
