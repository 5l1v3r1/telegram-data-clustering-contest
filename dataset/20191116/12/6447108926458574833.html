Gears Tactics will be at this year’s Game Awards

16 Nov 2019, 12:01 by Aran Suddi

At E3 2018 The Coalition took to the Microsoft conference stage to announce a new spin-off set in the Gears of War universe. That game is Gears Tactics which is a strategy game similar to the recent XCOM titles in which players control units and move them around battlegrounds. The game is being developed with Splash Damage. Since its reveal and teaser, however, there has been very little news on what is going on with the title.
