Bigg Boss 13: Ex-Contestant Gauahar Khan Raises Voice Against Body Shaming In The House, Lauds Devoleena For Supporting Mahira Sharma

16 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Nikita Thakkar

Gauahar Khan is an ardent follower of Bigg Boss 13 and quite frequently she shares her thoughts about the show on social media. This time around, she has raised her voice against the body-shaming happening inside the house

Bigg Boss 13 is calling for everyone's attention. We like how ex-contestants of the reality show are taking a keen interest in the latest season. Gauahar Khan who appeared as a Quality Controller on one of the Weekend Ka Vaar's is quite active in sharing her thoughts through social media.
