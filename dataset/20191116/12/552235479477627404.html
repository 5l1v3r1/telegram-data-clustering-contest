Police tape off Fife street after two men injured in 'disturbance'

Officers rushed to close Links Street, Kirkcaldy, after reports of the incident on Saturday morning.

16 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

Two men have been rushed to hospital after a disturbance on a Fife street.

Officers swooped on Links Street, Kirkcaldy at around 11am this morning.

A section of the road was taped off while police officers investigated the incident.

Both men were taken to the Victoria Hospital after suffering injuries.

A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: "We were called to attend an incident at 10.50am on Links Street, Kirkcaldy, after reports of a disturbance.

"Two men were taken to Victoria Hospital. The extent of their injuries remains unclear but they are not thought to life-threatening at this stage.

"Enquiries are ongoing."
