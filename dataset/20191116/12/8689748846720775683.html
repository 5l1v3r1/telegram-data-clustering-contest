Awal Suleman, the pitch invader, has been granted bail 

16 Nov 2019, 12:26 by Adu Sarpeah

Awal Suleman, the pitch invader who got on to the Cape Coast pitch while the Black Stars of Ghana were playing against South Africa has been granted bail by the Cape Coast Police.

The bail conditions remains unclear but the Central Regional RFA boss Mr Robert Duncan was instrumental in acquiring the bail for the accused. Nonetheless,the Police are making efforts to process him before the law court.

As a reminder, Awal Suleman stepped onto the pitch around the 84th minute of Ghana’s game with the Bafana Bafana of South Africa. It’s however worth knowing that Awal Suleman is a student nurse and a final year student of the Nalerigu Nursing Training School.
