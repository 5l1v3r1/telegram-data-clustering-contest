Take a look inside Newcastle Christmas Market's Apres Ski bar with cosy VIP cabins

The Apres Ski bar which surrounds Newcastle's Monument has a very special attraction and here are all the details

16 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Lesley Oldfield

The massive Christmas Market which has opened in Newcastle city centre has a large bar at it's heart called Apres Ski Bar - and hidden inside are the cutest little cabins.

The wooden bar, draped in Christmas lights and full of Christmas trees filling the air with their pine scent, has a proper log-cabin feel, with wooden bench-style tables, log stools, and rustic wooden furniture.

There will be live music every night with bands and singers doing Christmas numbers, soul classics and swing tunes too. And there are portaloos for customers too.

But the highlight is tucked away inside the bar area, in front of Berrys Jewellers  - a line of private cabins garlanded with holly and berries. Inside are heaters, fairy lights, cushions with reindeer of Christmassy designs, tartan blankets, furs, brass lamps and bowls of spicy pot pourri, where you and your friends and family can take a breather and enjoy a mulled wine or hot chocolate.

The VIP snugs, as Apres Ski Bar call them, hold up to eight people and can be booked ahead.

Prices and how to book

The cabins can be occupied for up to 90 minutes and all you need to do is spend a minimum of £25 for the group - this is paid up front and is then taken off your bill.

You can book ahead from tomorrow via the bar's Facebook page, which will be appearing shortly - or pop in when you are in town.
