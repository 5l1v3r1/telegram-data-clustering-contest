India declare on 493-6 in Indore test against Bangladesh

16 Nov 2019, 12:30

NEW DELHI: India have declared their first innings with a score of 493 for six, a lead of 343 runs, on day three of the opening test against Bangladesh in Indore on Saturday.

Mayank Agarwal topscored for the hosts with a career-best 243, while Ajinkya Rahane (86), Cheteshwar Pujara (54) and Ravindra Jadeja (60 not out) also helped themselves to easy fifties.
