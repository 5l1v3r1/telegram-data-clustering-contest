Pink Brought Her Cute Kids to the CMA Awards, and I Can't Get Enough of Their Giddyup Getups

16 Nov 2019, 01:25 by Karenna Meredith

Is there a trophy for cutest coordinated outfits at the 2019 CMAs? If not, there should be, and it should go to Pink, Carey Hart, and their kids, Willow and Jameson. Just a few days after the crew showed up to celebrate at the People's Choice Awards, they walked the red carpet again for another award show on Wednesday night. The family of four was completely on-theme for the Nashville event, down to Jameson's tiny cowboy hat and boots, and Willow's darling horse-print dress. A-freakin'-dorable.
