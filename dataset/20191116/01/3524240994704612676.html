70 NECO workers sacked over certificate forgery

16 Nov 2019, 01:17 by Olaleye Aluko

Olaleye Aluko, Abuja

The National Examinations Council has approved the dismissal of 70 of its workers over certificate forgery.

The NECO Governing Board carried out the dismissal, according to a statement  by the body’s  Head of Information and Public Relations Division, Azeez Sani, on Friday,  after a report on certificate verification was carried out by the management committee.

The committee had invited the workers with allegedly questionable credentials to appear before them, after which some of them reportedly confessed that their credentials  were fake.

The council noted that the dismissal was the first phase in the series of the ongoing staff certificate verification.

According to the statement, the committee also contacted the institutions the affected workers claimed to have attended only to  be  told  that they didn’t know them.

Sani said, “The  Governing Board vetted the report and approved the dismissal of the affected staff.”
