Ahem, Bella Hadid Chopped Her Hair Into a Supershort Bob and Dyed It a Sleek, Raven Black

16 Nov 2019, 01:23 by Chanel Vargas

Winter beauty typically involves incorporating darker, warmer colors into our everyday looks, and Bella Hadid took that trend and ran with it. While attending Vogue's Fashion Festival Photocall in Paris on Nov. 15, the model debuted her supershort bob haircut that's basically the exact opposite of the long, dirty blond look she had before. Hadid showed off her new haircut in a hot chocolate brown blouse and dark brown makeup that perfectly complimented her new Winter look, and we can't wait to see what she pairs the gorgeous hairstyle with next.
