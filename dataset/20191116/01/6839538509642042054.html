Home-made pipe bomb seized during raid on Rockingham house

16 Nov 2019, 01:39

A man will face court today after a pipe bomb was uncovered during a police raid south of Perth earlier this week.

Detectives were searching the home on Harrison Street in Rockingham on Tuesday when the homemade explosive device was allegedly found on a shelf in the living room.

Police bomb technicians were called to x-ray the device, which contained gas cylinders, nuts and bolts.

It will be alleged police found a flask containing ammonium nitrate - which is commonly used in the manufacturing of explosives - and two detonators.

Police also allegedly found cannabis, methylamphetamine and a homemade taser.
