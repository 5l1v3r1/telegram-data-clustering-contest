Video: Nintendo Minute do the “Be The Pokemon” challenge to celebrate Pokemon Sword & Shield’s launch

16 Nov 2019, 01:04 by ssf1991

It’s Friday, so Kit and Krysta are back with a brand new episode of Nintendo Minute. The pair are celebrating the release of Pokemon Sword and Shield in an interesting way. The duo said in the video description that “we hope you’ve all started your adventure in the Galar Region. We’re celebrating by BECOMING Pokémon. How do you think we did”?
