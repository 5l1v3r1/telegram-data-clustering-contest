Jet Li, Jack Ma and former UN Secretary General arrive in Ghana for Africa Netpreneurship Summit

16 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Nana Yaw

Popular Chinese film actor and producer Jet Li and a number of high profile Chinese personalities including business mogul and philanthropist Jack Ma and former UN Secretary-General, Ban Ki-Moon are in Ghana.

According to what we were told, the above personalities and a Chinese entourage are in the country for the 2019 Africa Netpreneurship Summit which will be held at the Accra International Conference, on Saturday, 16th November 2019.

The personalities who arrived in Ghana on Friday, November 15, 2019, were first captured in photos at the Kempinski Hotel before they made their way to the Jubilee House to call on the President of the Republic of Ghana, H.E. Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo – Addo.

Jack Ma is the co-founder and former executive chairman of Alibaba Group, a multinational technology conglomerate.
