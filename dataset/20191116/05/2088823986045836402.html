Smiles at NYBP 4th anniversary celebration

New York Beer Project marked its fourth birthday with a full weekend of festivities, beginning with a beer collaboration with Perry's Ice Cream and continuing with a show by Dueling Pianos, both on Friday, Nov. 15, 2019, in Lockport. The party continues all day Saturday and with a champagne brunch and Bills game gathering on Sunday.
