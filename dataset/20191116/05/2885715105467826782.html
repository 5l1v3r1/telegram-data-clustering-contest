Hrithik Roshan, Anand Kumar reminisce Super 30 journey

Hrithik Roshan hosted mathematician Anand Kumar, the man he portrayed in the critically-acclaimed film Super 30, for a special dinner to celebrate the movie's success.

16 Nov 2019, 05:20 by PTI

Hrithik Roshan hosted mathematician Anand Kumar, the man he portrayed in the critically-acclaimed film Super 30, for a special dinner to celebrate the movie’s success.

The film, directed by Vikas Bahl, featured Hrithik as Anand, the Patna-based scholar who trains 30 deserving, economically-backward students for IIT-JEE entrance exams every year with commendable success rate.

“It was a delightful evening. My mother has watched Super 30 nine times in the theatres but never got the chance to meet Anand sir and his brother Pranav personally.

“Yesterday, we all sat and reminisced about the tough journey of super 30 with smiles and laughter. Incidents and interactions that were devastating while shooting today seemed to make us laugh in victory,” Hrithik said in a statement.
