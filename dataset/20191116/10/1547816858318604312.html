Bigg Boss 13: Shehnaaz Gill's Father Santokh Singh Sukh Says, 'Himanshi Khurana Will Soon Be Out If She Avoids Talking About Her Controversy With My Daughter'

16 Nov 2019, 10:17 by Neeki Singh

Shehnaaz Gill's father thinks that his daughter's arch-rival Himanshi Khurana should play on the front foot and discuss the ugly spat the two girls had in the past

Bigg Boss 13 fans have been waiting for a lot of fireworks between rivals Shehnaaz Gill and Himanshi Khurana. The two, who made headlines for their ugly spat over social media, were brought to the controversial house essentially so that the viewers get to know what went wrong between the two and how they behave inside in each other's presence. Shehnaaz entered the House in the premiere episode whereas Himanshi came in as a wild card entry. Seeing Himanshi, Shehnaaz did lose her temper--- but nothing much about their friction has snowballed since then, at least not yet. 

We spoke with Shehnaz's father Santokh Singh on how the Shehnaaz-Himanshi spat has seemingly gone cold. "When Himanshi entered the house, Shehnaaz welcomed her but Himanshi ignored Shehnaaz completely and went away. I think that disturbed my daughter a lot and she took this to heart. Also, Shehnaaz seems to have got insecure seeing Himanshi inside because she felt that whatever love and positivity she was being praised for, might now be marred.
