Lewis Capaldi announces first UAE concert

The Scottish singer-songwriter will perform in Sharjah in 2020

16 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Time Out staff

Scottish singer and Instagram sensation Lewis Capaldi is coming to the UAE for the first time. 

The pop star, who has hit number one in the UK and US charts will perform at Shajah's Al Majaz Amphitheatre in 2020. 

Best known for his hit Someone You Love, as well as public spats with former Oasis star Noel Gallagher, Capaldi is due to play on January 17.

He announced the gig on his Instagram account. 

Capaldi is probably as well known for his cheeky chappie persona as well as his music, gaining fans through his funny Instagram stories and on-stage antics.

It goes against his music, which is earnest singer-songwriter fare, full of yearning lyrics and soaring vocals.

Tickets are on sale through platinumlist.net and start at Dhs156. 

From Dhs156. Jan 17, 2020. Al Majaz Amphitheatre, Sharjah, www.platinumlist.net.
