Female drug dealer jailed in QC mall sting

16 Nov 2019, 10:25 by Alfred P. Dalizon

A 23-year-old woman believed to be a top drug dealer in Metro Manila was arrested by agents of the Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency shortly after she sold 500 grams of shabu worth P3.4 million to an undercover officer in a parking lot of a Quezon City Mall last Wednesday, PDEA chair, Director General Aaron N. Aquino announced yesterday.

Suspect Roselle Bolivar alias ‘Carol’ yielded five knot-tied plastic bags each containing 100 grams of the so-called ‘poor man’s cocaine’ valued at P3.400,000, one mobile phone she used in making the drug transaction and the marked money during the entrapment operation conducted by members of the PDEA National Capital Region headed by Director Joel Plaza inside the Novaliches mall’s parking lot.
