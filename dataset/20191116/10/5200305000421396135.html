Overnights: Star Plus takes back lead on Friday in UK

16 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Raj Baddhan

Star Plus clinched back victory in UK ratings on Friday, with ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ proving the biggest draw, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Plus attracted a share of 0.14%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television with 0.11%, Hum TV with 0.08%, PTC Punjabi with 0.07%, Colors with 0.06%, New Vision TV and Geo News with 0.05%, Star Bharat, Geo TV, Colors Cineplex, Colors Rishtey, B4U Music, ZEE TV and ZEE Cinema with 0.03%.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ topped ratings with 82,800 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 51,800 viewers, ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 45,300 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 42,200 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 39,900 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 36,600 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 32,700 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka’ at 22:00 with 21,300 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 16,900 viewers and ‘Namah’ at 18:30 with 2,100 viewers.

On Sony TV, ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 led with 36,100 viewers, followed by ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 32,600 viewers, ‘Crime Patrol’ at 23:00 with 23,800 viewers and ‘Mere Sai’ at 19:00 with 16,300 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Ishq Zehe-e-Naseeb’ delivered 36,500 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘Mehboob Apke Qadmon Mein’ at 21:00 with 31,000 viewers and ‘Deewar-e-Shab’ repeat with 13,700 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ attracted 36,000 viewers at 21:30, followed by ‘Bahu Begum’ at 22:45 with 28,300 viewers and ‘Kitchen Champion’ at 20:30 with 16,900 viewers.

On Geo TV, ‘Kahin Deep Jalay’ repeat led with 7,600 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ topped ratings with 24,100 viewers at 18:30.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
