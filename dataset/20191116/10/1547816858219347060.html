Naagin 4: Gathbandhan Actress Shruti Sharma REJECTS An Anti-Heroine Role In Ekta Kapoor's Drama

16 Nov 2019, 10:58 by Saheli Maity

After bidding adieu to Gathbandhan, Shruti Sharma approached for the anti-heroine role in Ekta Kapoor's much-awaited Naagin 4

Ekta Kapoor’s Naagin 4 is no less than a big-budget Bollywood movie. The show has been grabbing attention since season 1 and it is pretty much expected that the fourth season will follow the same route. So far, Ekta Kapoor has announced her two sexy ladies who will be starring in Naagin 4 and they are Jamai Raja actress Nia Sharma and Dil Se Dil Tak actress Jasmine Bhasin. However, the producer is hunting for her third Naagin, for which she even approached Gathbandhan actress Shruti Sharma.

Colors' popular cop romance drama, Gathbandhan starring actors Shruti Sharma and Abrar Qazi in the lead roles is all set to bid adieu to the audience and fans are already feeling sad about it. There is a question, that is revolving everywhere as to where will the audience see their favourite Dhanak next? And from what our sources have informed us, Shruti has signed a couple of projects and has been approached for Ekta Kapoor's much-awaited Naagin 4.

Our sources told us, "Shruti was approached for Naagin 4 and seeing her capabilities as an actor she could have been seen in a negative role. Shruti was approached for the anti-heroine but she hasn't given a nod yet." When contacted Shruti, she said, "Yes, I was approached for Naagin 4 but due to my prior commitments I can't do it. I wish Ekta ma'am and the entire team, good luck. Looking forward to associating with them in the near future."
