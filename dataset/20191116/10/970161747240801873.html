Jennifer Lopez and Owen Wilson dressed up to the nines as they film for Marry Me

16 Nov 2019, 10:29

Jennifer Lopez and Owen Wilson have been spotted looking like the most adorable couple as they film for rom-com Marry Me.

As Owen would say, ‘Wow!’

The pair, who will be starring in the flick alongside Sarah Silverman and Game Of Thrones’ John Bradley, were dressed up to the nines.

Jen rocked a pale pink coat and dress, with Owen fixing a corsage to her wrist at one point during filming in New York City, with the on-screen couple giving us all the date night goals.

Meanwhile, the Marley And Me star looked dapper in a suit, with JLo laughing as she played with the flower in his pocket.

The movie, which will be released in 2020, is set to follow a pop superstar who’s jilted by her rock-star fiancé right before their wedding in Madison Square Garden, instead marrying a random attendee in the crowd.

We’re already sold.

Jen is also preparing for her big day in real life after getting engaged to her fiancé Alex Rodriguez during a dreamy holiday.

The singer and actor revealed she has yet to do much planning for the big day, telling Entertainment Tonight: ‘We’re talking about it, but we don’t have any firm plans… and we’re talking about a lot of places [to get married in], but I don’t know yet.’

And it seems ARod has had a huge impact on the singer, with her explaining that she ‘understands marriage’ after meeting him.

So cute!

Speaking to The Sun, she said: ‘I think I’ve understood that marriage takes a lot more than just falling in love and sharing a home.

‘Alex and I had a connection from the very beginning and maybe could see a future for ourselves.

‘We talked about it a few times. But when he proposed it was a ­surprise for me. We love each other, love being around each other and have a lot of respect for each other. That’s a big part of it.’

We’ll be waiting on our invite then, guys.
