Gary Oldman to play the lead in Apple’s Slow Horses adaptation

Based on Mick Herron's spy novel series of the same name, Slow Horses’ story is about Jackson Lamb, a brilliant but irascible leader of a group of spies. The series, starring Gary Oldman, will stream on Apple TV.

16 Nov 2019, 10:42 by PTI

Oscar winner Gary Oldman will headline Apple’s drama series Slow Horses.

Based on Mick Herron’s spy novel series of the same name, the story is about Jackson Lamb, a brilliant but irascible leader of a group of spies, who end up in MI5’s Slough House, having been exiled from the mainstream for their mistakes.

Graham Yost, the producer of Jane Campion’s Top of the Lake”, will executive produce the show, reported Variety.

Former Veep writer Will Smith will write and also executive produce.

Jamie Laurenson, Hakan Kousetta, Iain Canning, Emile Sherman, Gail Matrux, and Douglas Urbanski are other executive producers on the series.

See-Saw films is attached to produce.
