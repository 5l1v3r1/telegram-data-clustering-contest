'The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City' arriving in 2020

16 Nov 2019, 10:07 by Hunter Geisel, KUTV

(KUTV) — It's official: "The Real Housewives" is coming to Utah.

Andy Cohen announced on Saturday morning during his Ask Andy panel at BravoCon - Bravo's fan convention - in New York City that the channel's franchise is coming to Salt Lake City.
