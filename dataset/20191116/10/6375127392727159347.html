7 Chinese held in kidnap try

16 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Itchie G. Cabayan

SEVEN Chinese nationals who allegedly attempted to kidnap a couple — a male Chinese and a female Taiwanese — were arrested by the Manila Police District-Plaza Miranda Poliçe.Community Precinct.  

Already turned over to the MPD-General Assignment and Investigation Section (MPD-GAIS) for proper disposition were suspects Fen  Xiu  TIan, 24; Kang  Xin  Hang, 30;  Li Dong, 22; Yao  Shu, 23; Li You Bang, 28; Li Bing,  20  and Chan  Dang, 28, all Chinese nationals and residents of  20th Floor, Medical Plaza located at Amorsolo corner Dela Rosa Streets, Legaspi Village, Makati City.

A probe by GAIS showed that the alleged attempted kidnapping took place at around 6:20 p.m. in front of the Sun Star Grand Hotel on Quezon Boulevard in Quiapo, Manila the other night. The victims were identified as Xu Jun, 30 and Chloe Yuan, 35, a Chinese and a Taiwanese, respectively.

It was learned that the two were about to check in at the said hotel but it was fully-booked.

Owing to this, they asked a hotel employee to book them a cab service so they can proceed to a hotel in Las Pinas City.
