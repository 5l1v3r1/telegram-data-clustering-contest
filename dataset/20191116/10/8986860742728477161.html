Shatta Wale responds to Nana Boroo’s threat after attacking Kweku Smoke

16 Nov 2019, 10:46 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Dancehall artiste Charles Nii Armah Mensah known in showbiz as Shatta Wale has reacted to the ongoing story that he sent some thugs from his SM camp to attack and beat upcoming musician Kweku Smoke and his manager.

According to Kweku Smoke, Shatta Wale sent some thugs of his to attack him when he together with his manager paid a visit to his house to settle the issue of song theft.

Hiplife artiste Nana Boroo after a video of the attack went viral also took to social media to tell Shatta Wale that he has crossed the line with his gangster attitude and threatened to deal with him.

But Shatta Wale in his response has urged his fans not to be bothered with the threat from the two people.

He continued and added that people would henceforth not go around peddling lies about him again especially to his fans.

He wrote:

“From today they will learn how to keep their mouths shut ,so they will stop lying to Ghanaians and especially my fans… Pussy!!”
