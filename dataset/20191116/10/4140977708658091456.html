16 Nov 2019, 10:37 by Gautam Kapoor

KAT vs DEB Dream11 : New entrants Delhi Bulls look to set a statement today as they push for their second straight win in the league.

Entering the dramatic land f the T10 league for the first time in three seasons, for newly conceptualised Delhi Bulls, the league couldn’t have transpired in a more flourishing style. The side ended up winning their league opener against Deccan Gladiators in comprehensive style to lay down an early marker for their credentials this season.

Needing more than 100 to win, Delhi’s chase was as comfortable as they come with the side easing their way past the opponents by 7 wickets to chase down the 103 run target with three balls to spare. While the side getting acclimatised to 10 over cricket immediately was something to write home about what saw the side really win us over was the electric performance they doled out despite being devoid of a slew of outstanding players for the opening set of matches.

And when they take on Karnataka Tuskers today, the side will be looking to reverberate the same display as they look to make it two wins out of two in as many days and maraud their way to the top of Group A. For the Tuskers meanwhile, today’s clash will be a major determinant in how the league will transpire for the side, one they’ll be looking to transcend in tranquil manner come the imminent encounter.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

The T10 format means that irrespective of the pitch doled out, the batsmen are going to go after the bowlers. And the wicket today will assist them in their sorties as well with the ball lying nicely onto the bat, allowing the batters to peel away the boundaries at will.

The side winning the toss will be looking to come out and field first and get a clear idea of how to subject themselves to their run chase.
