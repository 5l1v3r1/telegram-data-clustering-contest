BREAKING: The Fiend's First Universal Title Challenger Revealed For WWE 'Survivor Series'

16 Nov 2019, 03:23 by Tom Clark

The November 15 edition of WWE Friday Night SmackDown saw a new challenger to the recently crowned Universal champion, Bray Wyatt. Wyatt won the tittle from Seth Rollins at Crown Jewel on November 2.

That challenger is none other than former WWE champion Daniel Bryan. Longtime fans will remember that Bryan was part of Bray’s Wyatt Family at one time and the two feuded for several months leading to their partnership and after their eventual split.
