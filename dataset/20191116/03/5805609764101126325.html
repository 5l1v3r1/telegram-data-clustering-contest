Top 10 Best YouTube Video Editor Apps For Android

Still, if you are a YouTuber and looking for ways to edit your videos on Android, then you can consider some of the best YouTube video editor apps listed in this article. All of these Youtube video editor apps for Android are available on the Google Play Store and can be downloaded for free.

#1 FilmoraGo

FilmoraGo is one of the best free Youtube video editor app that you can use on your Android smartphone. The great thing about FilmoraGo is that it allows users to trim, cut, add filter, music to the video. Not only that, but users can also create 16:9 video (YouTube), slow-motion videos as well with FilmoraGo.

#2 Adobe Premiere Clip

Well, if you are searching for the best free and most advanced YouTube video editor for your Android smartphone, then Adobe Premiere Clip might be the best pick for you. The great thing about Adobe Premiere Clip is that it got the automatic video creation capability. That means, it can create videos for you with any photos or clips that you choose. Apart from that, you can expect other video editing features like Trimming, transitions, filters, cutting, merging, etc with Adobe Premiere Clip.

#3 PowerDirector Video Editor App

PowerDirector Video Editor is for those who have been searching for a full-fledged video editing suite with a timeline interface. In fact, the computer type timeline interface is the most noticeable thing of PowerDirector Video Editor. The user interface of PowerDirector Video Editor might look complicated due to its timeline interface, but with the time, you can create professional videos in seconds.

#4 KineMaster

Well, if you are searching for a full-fledged video editing app for your Android smartphone that has a well-designed user interface and lots of powerful features, then you need to give KineMaster a try. When it comes to the video editing features, the Youtube video editor has features like multiple layer support, blending modes, chroma-key, speed control, transitions, special effects, etc.

#5 Magisto

If you are searching for ways to convert your regular videos into movies quickly, then you need to use Magisto. Magisto is a free video editing software available on the Google Play Store which can be used to cut, merge, trim video clips. To create attractive YouTube videos, Magisto also provides lots of music and special effects. So, Magisto is another best YouTube video editor that you can use right now.

#6 VideoShow

If you are searching for an easy to use video editing app for Android that’s ideal for editing YouTube videos, then VideoShow might be the best pick for you. The video editing app has a clean user interface and you can add text, filters, emojis, music to the video. So, VideoShow is another best video editing app for Android that can be used to create YouTube videos on the go.

#7 Movie Maker for YouTube & Instagram

Movie Maker for YouTube & Instagram is among the best free Youtube video editor apps currently available. With Movie Maker for YouTube & Instagram, you can easily trim, crop, merge your video content. Not only that but to give your videos a new touch, the app also provides a wide range of video effects. Despite all of its offerings, Movie Maker for YouTube & Instagram has always struggled with bugs and glitches. The app is completely free, but it’s ad-supported.

#8 VivaVideo

Well, if you are searching for an easy to use video editing app for Android that could allow you to edit, cut, trim and merge video clips, then VivaVideo might be the best pick for you. The app is heavily used by Tech YouTubers to edit their how-to videos, gameplay videos, etc. What’s interesting is that the app includes over 200 video filters and text effects. Not only that, but the app also got fast and slow-motion support as well.

#9 VideoShow

VideoShow is another popular video editing app on the list which can be used to edit your YouTube videos on the go. Just like every other video editing apps, VideoShow also lets you do basic video editing stuffs like video trim, merge, organize, add music, etc. Apart from that, there are few additional features like you can add emojis, text in the video, hundreds of filters, etc. So, the video editing app is perfect for creating videos for Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, etc.

#10 Vlogit

Vlogit is one of the best and a new YouTube video editing app available on the Google Play Store. The great thing about Vlogit is that it’s entirely free with no trial and watermark. Apart from that, Vlogit has almost every feature that users looks for in a video editing app. What’s more interesting is that Vlogit can be used to create custom YouTube Thumbnail as well.

So, these are the 10 best YouTube video editor apps that you can use on your Android smartphone. If you know any other such apps, let us know in the comment box below.
