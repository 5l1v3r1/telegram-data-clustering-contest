Prince Andrew Opens Up About His Relationship With Jeffrey Epstein and Says He Let Down the Royal Family

16 Nov 2019, 03:47
  • Prince Andrew talks about his relationship with Jeffrey Epstein for the first time in a new interview.
  • Andrew also addresses the allegations of sexual misconduct against him.

Ever since Jeffrey Epstein's highly publicized sexual trafficking case and suicide, Prince Andrew has been criticized for his friendship with the billionaire. The palace released a few short statements about their relationship, trying to distance the royal from the scandal, but now for the first time, Andrew himself is answering questions about his involvement with Epstein.

In an interview with BBC, which is set to air in full tonight, Andrew says that he regrets remaining friends with Epstein and staying at his house after Epstein served time for soliciting a minor for prostitution and was registered as a sex offender.

"The problem was the fact that once he had been convicted I stayed with him and that’s…that’s the bit that…that, as it were, I kick myself for on a daily basis," Andrew says. "It was not something that was becoming of a member of the royal family and we try and uphold the highest standards and practices. I let the side down, simple as that."
