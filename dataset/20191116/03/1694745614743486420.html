26 Christmas Movies Streaming on Hulu That Will Fill You With Holiday Cheer

16 Nov 2019, 03:30 by Kelsie Gibson

The weather is getting colder, and suddenly all we want to do on the weekend is curl up on the couch with a good Christmas movie. Sure, there are plenty of movie marathons happening on TV — like Freeform's Kickoff to Christmas — but who wants to sit through countless commercials? If you're looking for the perfect flick to watch, Hulu has you covered. There are plenty of cheesy rom-coms and quite a few comedies for the whole entire family to enjoy. And if you still can't find what you're looking forward, see what Christmas movies are on Amazon Prime Video.
