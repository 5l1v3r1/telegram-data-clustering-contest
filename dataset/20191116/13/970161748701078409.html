Emmerdale spoilers: Megan Macey set for return after shock exit?

16 Nov 2019, 13:11

Gaynor Faye has hinted she could be returning to Emmerdale sooner than we think after her sudden exit earlier this year.

After seven years in the soap, Gaynor’s character Megan Macey said goodbye to Emmerdale in August 2019 as she decided to move away following Frank Clayton’s funeral, after he died in the factory blaze.

Although, the actress has now hinted to Graham Norton that a return could be on the cards as ‘Emmerdale is around the corner’.

Gaynor has been cast in Band of Gold, the stage adaptation of Kay Mellor’s multi award-winning ITV series, which she discussed with the Radio 2 host on Saturday.

However the comedian couldn’t help but ask her about her character returning to the ITV soap.

‘Leaving Emmerdale, yeah, it’s heart-wrenching,’ she began.

‘[I left] to do the stage show. I just wanted to do something different. That’s what an actor does, I do different roles.’

Addressing a potential return to Emmerdale she added: ‘I left in a taxi, so who knows? I didn’t leave, I still see loads of people and Emmerdale’s around the corner, but I’m absolutely loving doing this show.’
