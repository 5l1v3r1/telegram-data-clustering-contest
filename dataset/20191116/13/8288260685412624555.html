Turkish giants linked with January transfer for Aston Villa star - report

Aston Villa transfer news | With the January transfer window nearly upon us speculation is already happening with Dean Smith set to further evaluate his squad in the New Year

16 Nov 2019, 13:36 by Richard Cusack

Besiktas have been linked with a move for out-of-favour Aston Villa striker Jonathan Kodjia.

The forward has seen his game-time limited upon Villa 's return to the Premier League, the 30-year-old playing just 20 minutes of League football this season.

He's spent most of 2019-20 out injured with a cheekbone problem, but he returned to full fitness last month and managed just four minutes in two games.

Kodjia was linked with a move to Qatari side Al-Gharafa before the transfer window shut for Qatar Stars League clubs.
