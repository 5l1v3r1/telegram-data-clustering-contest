Birds of Prey: DC Film Running Art Competition for Fans

16 Nov 2019, 13:11 by John Ridlehoover

Warner Bros. Pictures is giving artists the opportunity to create work for the upcoming DC filmÂ Birds of Prey (And the Fantabulous Emancipation of One Harley Quinn).

Talenthouse's official description for the contest reads, "Create one-of-a-kind static artwork for Birds of Prey inspired by the latest imagery and trailer from the film. Celebrate the diverse group of Birds coming together and create artwork that showcases all of the dynamic, powerful and complex women that team up as a collective force."

The contest is open to art of many different mediums, including posters, illustrations and digital paintings. No purchase is required to enter the contest and everyone over 18 across the globe is eligible. Five pieces will be chosen.

The artists' whose work is chosen by Warner Bros. will receive several prizes, including the possibility of having their art featured in the film, possible merchandising opportunities and $2,000 for each art piece. Merchandising for the artwork could include t-shirts and printed materials.

Directed by Cathy Yan from a script by Christina Hodson, Birds of Prey (And the Fantabulous Emancipation of One Harley Quinn) stars Margot Robbie, Jurnee Smollett-Bell, Mary Elizabeth Winstead, Rosie Perez, Ewan McGregor, Steven Williams, Derek Wilson, Dana Lee, Francois Chau, Charlene Amoia, Chris Messina and Matthew Willig. The film is scheduled for release on Feb. 7, 2020.
