Two gold medals for Manu Bhaker

Lakshay Sheoran wins trap and double trap

16 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Special Correspondent

Manu Bhaker won the women’s air pistol and sports pistol gold medals for Delhi University in the all India inter-University shooting championship organised by the Manav Rachna University at the Dr. Karni Singh Shooting Range.

Manu, a student of Lady Shriram College, was bang on target in winning the 10-metre event with a score of 583, three points ahead of Yashaswini Singh Deswal.

Lakshay Sheoran, the Asian Games silver medallist, won the trap and double trap gold medals for Guru Nanak Dev University (GNDU), Amritsar.

Nivetha Nenthirasigamani won the women’s trap gold for Bharathiar University, Tamil Nadu, two points ahead of Kirti Gupta of University of Delhi.
