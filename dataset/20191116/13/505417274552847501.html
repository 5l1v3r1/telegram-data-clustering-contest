John Cooney Atones For Error With Great Individual Try Against Bath

16 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Michael Corry

After a tough opening Ulster lead Bath at The Recreation Ground, thanks to a great individual try from John Cooney who atoned for an error just a minute earlier.

Ulster found themselves under pressure early on as Bath utilised the strong running of Jonathan Joseph and Jamie Roberts but the Ulster defence held strong and managed to turn the home side over right underneath their own posts after the first ten minutes.
