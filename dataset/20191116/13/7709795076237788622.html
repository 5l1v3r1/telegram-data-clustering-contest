Allmankind lands Triumph trial plunge at Cheltenham

16 Nov 2019, 13:29 by Sporting Life

Allmankind was a well-backed winner of the JCB Triumph Trial Juvenile Hurdle at Cheltenham on Saturday for Dan and Harry Skelton.

The three-year-old was very free when winning on his hurdles debut at Warwick and he was keen again at Cheltenham, but he still managed to make all despite that tendency.

Available at 13/2 on Friday, he returned the 11/4 winner with uneasy favourite Mick Pastor disappointing.

Sky Bet cut him to 20/1 from 33/1 for the Triumph Hurdle back at Cheltenham at the Festival in March.

"I thought those tactics were possible but not probable here," said Dan Skelton.
