FLAT TRAGEDY

Man’s body found in Glasgow flat as police launch unexplained death probe

16 Nov 2019, 13:02 by Kirsty Feerick

A MAN'S body has been found in a Glasgow flat as police launch a probe into the unexplained death.

The man, who was in his 50's, was discovered at in a block of flats on Linkwood Crescent.

Mercy crews raced to the scene at around 1am on Friday but he was pronounced dead at the scene.

The'unexplained death has sparked a major investigation from detectives.

A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: "At around 1.05am on Friday 15th November police where called to a flat on Linkwood Crescent were a man in his 50's was pronounced dead the death is being treated as unexplained and inquires are ongoing.

"A report will be submitted to the fiscal."

An eyewitness told the Daily Record that officers remained in the area more than 24 hours.
