I Didn’t BLeach My Skin, I Took After My Father – Xandy Kamel Debunks Bleaching Rumours

16 Nov 2019, 13:56 by vanessa nordzi

Controversial Ghanaian actress Xandy Kamel has debunked reports that she has bleached her skin in a photo of her that went viral.

Xandy Kamel subjected herself to ridicule when she posted a picture of herself in a darker skin tone.

The huge difference in the skin tone caused people to accuse her of bleaching her skin.

But Xandy Kamel in an interview with Zionfelix On his “uncut” show stated emphatically that she never bleached.

According to Xandy Kamel,  she took after her late father who was very light skinned. Xandy in a bid to prove her point showed Zionfelix pictures of two younger sisters who are all light skinned.

Meanwhile Xandy Kamel will be gracing the maiden edition of the Ghana Music and Arts Awards Europe which will be held in Amsterdam on the 16th of November, 2019.
