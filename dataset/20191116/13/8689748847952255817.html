Naa Ashorkor goes head-to-head with Obrafour on his ‘Ako’ song

16 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Nana Yaw

If you thought Naa Ashorkor was only good behind the console, presenting and hosting shows, then have a second thought because the video you’re about to watch below will blow your mind.

The radio presenter has been sighted in a video rapping Obrafour’s ‘Ako’ song word for word. Naa Ashorkor in the self-recorded video was seen in her car rapping to the song with so much enthusiasm it got Obraofur to post the video on his Instagram page and congratulate her.
