High Tourism Season Starts at the Brisas Cobarruvias Hotel

16 Nov 2019, 13:10 by Rafael Quiroga Álvarez

The Brisas Covarrubias hotel, on the north coast of Las Tunas, opens its doors today after executing a repair and maintenance process, confirmed Elizabeth Llorente León, commercial director of that facility.

Puerto Padre, Las Tunas.- The official added that among the improvements are the replacement of refrigerators, air conditioners and solar heaters for the 180 rooms of the tourist resort.

In addition, the floor of the ranchón was changed, the kitchen was conditioned, and all the buildings and exterior areas were painted.

In this way, the workers of the "Brisas Covarrubias" are able to receive the first guests, who can make their reservations in the lobby of the hotel, said Llorente León.
