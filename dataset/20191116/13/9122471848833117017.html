Tom Brady Sends Message To LeBron James About Retirement

16 Nov 2019, 13:16 by Andrew Holleran

Two of the greatest athletes professional sports has ever seen continue to dominate their respective leagues well into their twilight years.

LeBron James is continuing to thrive in the NBA at the age of 34. Tom Brady, meanwhile, is going for another Super Bowl championship at the age of 42.

The Los Angeles Lakers star revealed in a recent interview that he and the New England Patriots quarterback are one and the same.

“We’re going to play until we can’t walk no more,” he said.
