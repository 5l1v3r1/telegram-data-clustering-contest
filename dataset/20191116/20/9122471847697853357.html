Jim Harbaugh Didn’t Hold Back Late vs. Michigan State

16 Nov 2019, 20:59 by Dan Lyons

We all know about the Michigan-Ohio State rivalry, and how the current losing streak has weighed on the program. There is no love lost with in-state rival Michigan State either.

Michigan State has had UM’s number for much of the last decade. Last season, the Wolverines won a chippy game, 21-7.

MSU has struggled this season, without a win since September. That continued today, and Jim Harbaugh was happy to rub it in.

Late in the game, Michigan kept throwing, putting up a touchdown on a 39-yard Shea Patterson pass to Cornelius Johnson to make it 44-10.

He finished with 384 yards and four touchdowns, a record performance for a Wolverines quarterback vs. Michigan State.
