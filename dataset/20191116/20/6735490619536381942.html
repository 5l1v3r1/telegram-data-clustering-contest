Sia Jokingly Tries To Steal John Legend’s Grammy Awards, Chrissy Teigen Lets Her

16 Nov 2019, 20:17 by Jamie Samhan

Sia was visiting John Legend and Chrissy Teigen when she teasingly decided to steal a few of Legend’s Grammy awards.

“Oh dear @johnlegend and @chrissyteigen I super dooper love you, your children, your politics and…um… Your grammys…byeeeeee,” she captioned the cute post.

Teigen wasn’t too fussed that the singer was stealing her husband’s awards.

“Take them all! You deserve 10 more!!” she replied.
