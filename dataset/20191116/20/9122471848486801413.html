Nick Saban Offers Injury Update For Tua Tagovailoa After Mississippi State Game

16 Nov 2019, 20:33 by Andrew McCarty

Earlier Saturday afternoon, Alabama star quarterback Tua Tagovailoa left the game against Mississippi State with an apparent injury.

Tagovailoa scrambled to his left and was hit by two Mississippi State defenders. His helmet popped off and his face could be seen covered in blood.

However, the more serious injury appears to be to Tua’s hip.

According to multiple reports, Tua is being transported by helicopter to a hospital in Birmingham. That by itself shows the severity of the injury.

After the game, head coach Nick Saban revealed Tua suffered what could be a “serious” injury.
