Shah Rukh Khan, Karan Johar and more attend Aaradhya Bachchan’s birthday bash

16 Nov 2019, 20:42 by Filmfare

Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan’s adorable daughter Aaradhya Bachchan turns a year older today and fans of her star parents are already started sending out special wishes to the little one. Aaradhya, who turns 8 today was joined by various Bollywood celebs and their star kids at her birthday bash.

Marking their presence were stars like Shah Rukh Khan, Gauri Khan along with their son AbRam Khan, Sonali Bendre with husband Goldie Behl and son Ranveer Behl, Karan Johar with kids Yash Johar and Roohi Johar,  Adar and Natasha Poonawalla with their kids, Riteish Deshmukh and Genelia D’Souza with their son, Aaradhya’s grandma Brindya Rai and various others.
