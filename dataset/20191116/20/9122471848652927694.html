Paul Finebaum Reacts To Tua Tagovailoa’s Injury vs. Mississippi State

16 Nov 2019, 20:47 by Andrew McCarty

Earlier Saturday afternoon, Alabama star quarterback Tua Tagovailoa left the game against Mississippi State with an apparent injury. Tagovailoa scrambled to his left and was hit by two Mississippi State defenders.

Head coach Nick Saban revealed Tua is dealing with a hip injury, though further details are unknown at this time. SEC reporter Cecil Hurt reported Tua is being transported by helicopter to a hospital in Birmingham.

After the game, ESPN college football analyst Paul Finebaum weighed in on the loss of Tua, despite the win for Alabama.

“This may be the most heartbreaking 31-point victory in Alabama football history,” Finebaum said.
