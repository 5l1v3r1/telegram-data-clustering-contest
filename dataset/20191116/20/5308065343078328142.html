Song Hye Kyo and Lee Jin shares long lasting friendship

16 Nov 2019, 20:30

- Advertisement -

South Korean actress Song Hye Kyo and Fin.K.L.’s Lee Jin recently shared a heartfelt moment on Instagram with Song uploading a beautiful video of the ocean on Nov 13.

Lee Jin, who is a good friend of Song Hye Kyo responded with a “Miss you” and accompanied that with a heart emoji.

Song Hye Kyo then responded with a “Me too” and a heart emoji.

The Full House actress is good friends with all the Fin.K.L. members but is especially close to Lee Jin.

- Advertisement -

Both of them attended the same high school and have been friends for 20 years.

Fans were happy to see them showing their closeness on social media.

Song Hye Kyo is now thinking of joining the cast for the film ‘Anna’ as her next project.

In February 2016, Lee Jin tied the knot in Hawaii and have been living in New York ever since then.

Born on November 22, 1981, Song Hye Kyo is a South Korean actress who rose to stardom in Asia with roles in TV dramas Autumn in My Heart, All In, Full House, That Winter, the Wind Blows, Descendants of the Sun and Encounter.

She also starred in films like Hwang Jin Yi, The Grandmaster, My Brilliant Life and The Queens.

Song Hye Kyo ranked 7th in Forbes magazine’s Korea Power Celebrity list in 2017 and 6th in 2018. Her acting in TV dramas made her famous worldwide.

Born on March 21, 1980, Lee Jin is a South Korean actress and singer. She debuted as a member of K-pop girl group Fin.K.L. together with group members Lee Hyori, Ock Joo-hyun and Sung Yu-ri. When the group ended in 2002, Lee debuted her acting career.

Lee Jin’s friend Ock Joo Hyun (who is the first member to be selected for the group) introduced Lee Jin to Fin.K.L. Lee Jin sang Eco’s Heng Bok Han Na Reul during casting and was immediately selected to be in the group.
