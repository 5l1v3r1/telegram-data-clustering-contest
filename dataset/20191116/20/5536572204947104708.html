Trevor Lawrence Met His TikTok Doppelgänger Before Wake Forest Game (PIC)

16 Nov 2019, 20:14 by Darrelle Lincoln

There aren’t too many people in the country who can play the quarterback position quite like Trevor Lawrence, but there is at least one person who looks like him.

That led to one young lady sharing a TikTok video highlighting the fact that she looks exactly like the Tigers star QB.

Lawrence certainly thought it was funny, saying, “She does look like me.”
