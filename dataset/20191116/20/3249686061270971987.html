Jessica hints that she is releasing a book in recent Instagram post

16 Nov 2019, 20:30 by Yanderedan

On November 16th, Jessica posted on her Instagram account that she is releasing a book.

The only confirmed information so far is that is it titled 'Shine'.

Although in an unofficial Amazon listing for the kindle version it is stated it's going to be around 350 pages long, with an unconfirmed release date of October 6th, 2020. 

Stay tuned for more information in the future!
