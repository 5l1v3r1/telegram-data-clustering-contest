16 Nov 2019, 20:48 by Canadian Press

TORONTO — Sudbury police say an 18-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a murder in that city on Friday morning.

Investigators say the man, who they did not identify, was detained today without incident.

Officers were called to a weapons complaint around 10:30 a.m. Friday and found an injured 17-year-old.

The teen, who police have also not identified, was taken to hospital but later died from his injuries.

Investigators allege a dispute between the two men resulted in the death of the 17-year-old.

This report from The Canadian Press was first published Nov. 16, 2019.
