#KogiDecides: Two shot dead in in my polling unit — Melaye

16 Nov 2019, 20:13 by The Eagle Online

Senator Dino Melaye has stated that two people have been shot dead in his polling unit.

Melaye accused one Sunday Faleke and another Taofik Isah of involvement in the shooting of the two people.

According to him: “Sadly the 2 people shot in my pooling unit just died. May their souls rest in peace. Sunday Faleke, Taofik Isah and Co will explain.”
