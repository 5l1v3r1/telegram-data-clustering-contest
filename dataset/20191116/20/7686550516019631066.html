Anton just got his first 10 on Strictly and people are going mad

The professional was so stunned his kissed partner Emma Barton

16 Nov 2019, 20:20 by Anna Lewis

Strictly's Anton made history on the show tonight after receiving his first ever 10 from the judges.

The professional dancer received glowing praise across the board after his American smooth foxtrot with partner Emma Barton.

Dancing to Frank Sinatra's Let's Face the Music and Dance, the couple certainly made an impression in Blackpool - bringing judges Bruno and Shirley to their feet.

And to top it all off, he was awarded his very first 10 from the judges after 17 series, much to his fans' delight.

Praising the pair, Motsi Mabuse said: "That dance was amazing, it was all about music.

"You were a partner, that's what I love . When you guys dance together I felt so close to you guys.

"I think that was the best you did best since you started Strictly."

And Shirley added: "You covered everything there.

"It was just beautifully executed, you had some lovely footwork there.

"It was just lovely, I just felt it was your best dance by far."

Despite facing criticism by Craig Revel Horwood about her expression, Eastenders star Emma and Anton received a perfect 10 from both Bruno and Shirley - causing Anon to kiss Emma before falling to his knees in amazement.

And he wasn't the only one to react to the news.
