Katy Perry performs first-ever gig in Mumbai

The singer crooned hits like “Dark horse”, “I kissed a girl”, “Bon appetite” and “Roar” among others at the concert.

16 Nov 2019, 20:42 by Express News Service

American pop star Katy Perry on Saturday performed at Navi Mumbai’s DY Patil Stadium at the OnePlus Music Festival. The singer crooned hits like “Dark horse”, “I kissed a girl”, “Bon appetite” and “Roar” among others at the concert.

This is her first concert in Mumbai. She had previously performed at the opening ceremony of 2012 Indian Premier League in Chennai.

“It’s a dream come true to watch Katy Perry live!” Priya Batish, a fan of the 35-year-old star, said. “It is great that so many international artists are coming to India and I am really looking forward to the U2 concert next month,” she added.

Before Katy Perry hit the stage, British pop star Dua Lipa, Amit Trivedi, Ritviz, rock bands ‘The Local Train’ and ‘Aswekeepsearching’ entertained the audience at the music festival.
