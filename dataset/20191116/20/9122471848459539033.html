Colin Kaepernick’s Girlfriend Tweets Message Before Workout

16 Nov 2019, 20:13 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, ESPN NFL insider Adam Schefter revealed the location of Colin Kaepernick’s workout for NFL scouts has changed.

The NFL set up a workout for Kaepernick to showcase his skills at the Atlanta Falcons training facility. However, just over an hour before the workout was scheduled to begin, Kaepernick’s camp decided to move the location.

Kaepernick released a statement revealing the decision for the change. The NFL did not want media to attend and film the workout, so Kaepernick changed the location to allow media and the fans to see the workout for themselves.

His girlfriend, Nessa Diab, released a statement about the move.

“#Transparency We are ready!!! For teams and media wanting to attend today please contact his agent now,” she said on Twitter.
