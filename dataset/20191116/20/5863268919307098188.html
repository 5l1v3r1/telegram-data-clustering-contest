Govt must challenge LHC's decision regarding Nawaz Sharif: Fawad Chaudhry

16 Nov 2019, 20:59 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Federal Minister for science and technology Fawad Chaudhry on Saturday said government should challenge the decision of LHC. He said he never heard about verdicts where convicted persons were allowed to go abroad.

He said examples are there where Sharif family left the country but never returned, adding that Ishaq Dar is one of them.

He questioned whether Nawaz would convince his sons to return to Pakistan.

Chaudhry said it is not suitable to comment on court decision but law would be followed.

He said Supreme Court must be knocked in this regard.
