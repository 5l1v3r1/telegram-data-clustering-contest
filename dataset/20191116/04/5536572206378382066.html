Maurkice Pouncey Responds To Primanti Bros Offering Him a Job As A Bouncer (TWEETS)

16 Nov 2019, 04:28 by Darrelle Lincoln

Along with Myles Garrett receiving a 6-game suspension plus the playoffs, Maurkice Pouncey also received a 3-game ban after he came in with fists and legs swinging on the Cleveland Browns defensive end after he watched him hit his quarterback with a helmet in the head.

Pouncey won’t be getting paid for those games, but there is no reason to not get any money during that time period.

Primanti’s Bros in Pittsburgh took to Twitter and offered a three-week bouncer gig to Pittsburgh Steelers Maurkice Pouncey following the Steelers-Browns brawl. The Pittsburgh-based restaurant said it’s reaching out to Pouncey to see if he’d be interested in working the door at Primanti’s original location in the Strip, starting today.
