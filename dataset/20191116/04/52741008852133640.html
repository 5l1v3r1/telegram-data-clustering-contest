Regional Police looking for missing man

Police say there is concern for the well-being of the 42-year old man

16 Nov 2019, 04:50 by Mark Pare

Regional Police are seeking the public's help in locating a missing man.

42-year old Brock Huffman was last seen in Kitchener Friday, driving a grey Ford Escape and wearing a brown jacket, grey pants and a Denver Broncos hat.

He stands 5'8" and weighs 175 lbs.

Police say there is concern for his well-being.

If you have any information on Huffman, you're asked to call police at 519-570-9777 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477.
