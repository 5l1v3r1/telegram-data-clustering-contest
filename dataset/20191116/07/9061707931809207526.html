Wayne Rooney in, but who goes out? - Decide who Derby County keep or sell

Latest Derby County transfer news | You decide which players the Rams should keep or sell during the January transfer window

16 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Mark Wakefield

Derby County, like the rest of the teams in the Sky Bet Championship, are enjoying a two-week hiatus from action due to the international break.

The Rams are in 16th place in the league table following their latest outing which saw them lose 1-0 to arch rivals Nottingham Forest last weekend.

Phillip Cocu and his players will rest up before preparing to take on Preston North End on Saturday, November 23 when their league campaign resumes.

The summer transfer window saw Derby recruit six new players, with four of those coming in on loan deals for the campaign.

Derby will also welcome Wayne Rooney to Pride Park in January in a player-coach role, after he recently left DC United.

And in terms of outgoings, Cocu allowed nine players to depart while a number of others leave the club after the expiration of their contracts or, in the case of Luke Thomas to Barnsley, via a sale.

So what will the upcoming January transfer window have in store for the Rams?

Cocu could look to bring in fresh faces in the New Year in order to galvanise his squad which could enhance the club’s chances of success this season.
