Pulisic, Rudiger, Barkley - The latest Chelsea injury news ahead of Manchester City trip

The Blues have several players currently out injured as Frank Lampard prepares his Chelsea side to face Manchester City

16 Nov 2019, 07:35 by Oliver Harbord, Robert Warlow, Tashan Deniran-Alleyne

While it may have come at a bad time for Chelsea, after picking up their sixth league win in a row, there is one good aspect of the international break coming around.

The two-week hiatus gives players struggling with their fitness a chance to step up their recovery.

Chelsea currently have one of the bigger injury lists in the Premier League, and head coach Frank Lampard will be relishing the return of the injured players.

The Blues are next in Premier League action when they take on current Premier League Champions Manchester City at the Etihad Stadium, and the Chelsea boss will be looking to have as many players as possible available for selection.

With that said, here's the latest Chelsea injury news and expected return dates.

Christian Pulisic

Chelsea face an anxious wait to discover the full extent of Christian Pulisic's hip injury after USA head coach Gregg Berhalter confirmed the winger will miss the upcoming international matches.

"Christian is officially at this stage ruled out," he said.

"We just got that information. This was a collaborative decision with the club looking after the best interest of the player.

"These decisions are always difficult because this group and the coaching staff want nothing more than to have Christian here, but it was a risk we weren’t willing to take at this time."

Pulisic confirmed via his Twitter account that he is London, with PremierInjuries rating the American's chance of being fit for the match against Manchester City at 25%.
