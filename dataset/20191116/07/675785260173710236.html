Coronation Street Christmas spoilers: Pub siege, heartache and death horror

Coronation Street will face tragedy this Christmas, with spoilers and storylines slowly being teased

16 Nov 2019, 07:55 by Charlotte Tutton

Coronation Street Christmas spoilers have been revealed, while no 2019 festive storylines have actually been confirmed by the ITV soap.

With exits looming on the soap there's talks of a shock death or two, while one cast member has promised "something very dramatic" happens on the Cobbles.

Sarah Platt actress Tina O'Brien told Lorraine something was happening this Christmas, but she remained coy over the details.

It comes ahead of heartache for some amid the joy, as well as reports of a shooting potentially leaving one character dead.

Here's what's lined up... 

 

Robert dies in The Rovers siege?

It's heavily rumoured that Robert Preston is being killed off this Christmas.

A siege is reportedly set to take place in The Rovers Return pub, with newcomer Derek taking residents hostage.

The gun will go off with Robert and another character shot and left fighting for their lives, if the claims are true.

It's said Robert will lose his life off-screen with wife-to-be Michelle Connor breaking the news to everyone.

A source told The Sun Online: "Derek gets drunk and starts waving a gun around the pub.

"There’s a tense stand-off then a massive fight between the two men and two people are shot, including Robert, who dies on Christmas Day.

“He makes it to hospital but suffers a heart attack and dies off-screen in a bit of snub to Tristan. It’s left to Michelle to tell everyone he’s dead.”

Gary Windass exposed?

The siege is set to come about as Gary Windass' business partner, who he recently threatened, comes looking for revenge.

A drunk Derek will apparently wave a gun around in the pub, with he and Gary embroiled in a vicious fight.

But will this be the moment Gary Windass is exposed ahead of drama involving Adam Barlow and Ali Neeson?

He killed Rana Habeeb when he caused the factory roof collapse earlier this year, before blaming it on loan shark Rick Neelan.

Gary also murdered Rick, before taking on his business and harassing several Corrie residents.

Rita Sullivan fights for life

Coronation Street is said to be lining up a heartbreaking new Christmas storyline, that will see Rita Sullivan "left for dead".

The plot will apparently focus on lonely OAPs, and will see Rita, played by actress Barbara Knox, suffer an accident during he festive episodes.

It's claimed Rita will take a fall at home while attempting to decorate her house for Christmas, putting up some fairy lights on her Christmas tree.

But Rita will end up falling with her Christmas tree landing on top of her, and it's said she will be left trapped beneath it for several days waiting for help.

Reflecting on loneliness and the importance of checking up on elderly loved ones and neighbours, the plot will see Rita waiting for help with no one aware of what has happened to her.

Michelle exit?

Michelle Connor is leaving the ITV soap, with actress Kym Marsh bowing out of the role.

With Robert potentially murdered, will it lead to lover Michelle vacating the Street in grief?

 

Daniel and Bertie's first Christmas without Sinead

Daniel Osbourne is grief-stricken following the death of his wife Sinead, who lost her battle with cancer in October.

He's struggling to raise their baby son Bertie alone, and this Christmas will be their first without the love of his life.

Coronation Street airs Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays at 7:30pm and 8:30pm on ITV.
