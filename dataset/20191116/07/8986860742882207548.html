I need a baby boy – Sarkodie tells pregnant wife Tracy

16 Nov 2019, 07:20 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Ghana’s finest rapper Sarkodie has taken to social media to ask his wife Tracy Sarkcess to please give him a baby boy since he already has a girl.

It was reported on major news portals around the country that Tracy Sarkcess was pregnant with their second child but neither the rapper or his wife has come out to deny or confirm this.

In the post on Instagram, BET Award winner shared a random photo of a baby boy playing on a sofa with the caption: “Need a baby boy.”
