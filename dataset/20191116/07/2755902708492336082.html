16 Nov 2019, 07:35 by Sharon Chirisa

First Lady visits selfless Mbare Gogo who delivered 100 babies

The First Lady Auxillia Mnangagwa yesterday visited the Mbare midwife Mrs Gwena, who is assisting women to deliver following the strike by council nurses.

She donated an assortment of foodstuffs and toiletries to the midwife .

Mbuya Gwena grabbed the headlines after she assisted expecting mothers and  delivered about 100 babies last week alone.

Her efforts come at a time when the medical sector is in chaos with medical staff striking over paltry salaries and poor working conditions.

Some of the women who can not afford the costly services of private doctors have turned to Gogo Esther Gwena.

Gogo Esther Gwena is a midwife with St Johns Apostolic Church.
