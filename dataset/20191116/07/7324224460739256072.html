Missing Wolverhampton man, 38, is found

A Wolverhampton man who was reported missing has been found, police said today.

16 Nov 2019, 07:42 by Ian Harvey

Ryan Kavanagh, aged 38, had last been seen just before 10am on Friday and Wolverhampton police said they were concerned for his wellbeing.

Today they tweeted that Mr Kavanagh has been found and thanked people for sharing their appeal.
