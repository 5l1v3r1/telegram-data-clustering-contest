Boxing tonight: Schedule, TV channels, live streams, fight times, undercards and odds

Lee McGregor takes on Kash Farooq in all-Scottish clash for the British and Commonwealth bantamweight titles

16 Nov 2019, 07:30 by Martin Domin, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

Boxing takes something of a back seat this weekend amid a hectic spell for the sport.

But there is a potential barnstormer in Glasgow as Lee McGregor and Kash Farooq clash for the British and Commonwealth titles.

The unbeaten Scots have only 20 fights between them but are expected to steal the show.

Elsewhere, Alex Dilmaghani takes on Daniel Fonseca at York hall in their rearranged clash for a 'world title' at super-featherweight.

There is no boxing in America this weekend as Las Vegas builds up to Deontay Wilder vs Luis Ortiz next weekend.

Here is everything you need to know about tonight's fights...
