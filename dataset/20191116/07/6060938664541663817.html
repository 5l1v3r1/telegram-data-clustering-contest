Vidya Balan recreates Dina Pathak's hilarious scene from 1979 film 'Golmaal'

16 Nov 2019, 07:57 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood actress Vidya Balan never fails to disappoint her fans with her magic on the big screen. Beyond her great acting skills, Vidya is also a fun person who often entertains with her fun antics.

Recently the actress decided to entertain her fans and shared a hilarious video on Instagram. Vidya recreated veteran actress Dina Pathak's funny scene from Hrishikesh Mukherjee's 1979 hit film, 'Golmaal'. In the video, Vidya is seen entering her hotel room through a small window and it is the best thing of the internet today.

On the work front, Vidya recently began shooting for a Shakuntala Devi biopic. The actress has perfectly adopted to look of the Shakuntala Devi, who was popularly known as a human computer. The film is directed by Anu Menon and is scheduled to hit the screens in May 2020.
