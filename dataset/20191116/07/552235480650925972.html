Larkhall man hit his partner with an ashtray

The accused appeared at Hamilton Sheriff Court.

16 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Hamilton Advertiser, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/hamilton-advertiser/

A Larkhall man hit his partner on the head with an ashtray.

Scott Wilson, 45, of Auldton Terrace, appeared at Hamilton Sheriff Court and admitted assaulting Tracy Reston at his home on September 22.

Wilson was put under supervision for a year and ordered to attend alcohol counselling.
