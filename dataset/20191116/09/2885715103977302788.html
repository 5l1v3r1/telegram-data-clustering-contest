Thambi teaser: Karthi-Jyotika promise an intense film

Thambi marks the first film where Jyotika will be working with her brother-in-law, Karthi. Sathyaraj will also be seen playing a pivotal role in the film. 

16 Nov 2019, 09:22 by Antara Chakraborthy

The teaser of Thambi starring Karthi and Jyotika is out. The film has been directed by Jeethu Joseph. In the trailer, Jyotika’s character is seen waiting for the return of her younger brother, played by Karthi. Karthi gets in trouble with the cops and we are told that he has committed various crimes.

There are car chases, fight scenes and even a gunshot is heard in the background. The premise of the film looks very interesting.

Karthi is sporting various looks in the teaser. Thambi marks the first film where Jyotika will be working with her brother-in-law, Karthi. Sathyaraj will also be seen playing a pivotal role in the film.
