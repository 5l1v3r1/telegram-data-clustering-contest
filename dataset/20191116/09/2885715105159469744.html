16 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Careers Desk

UPPSC computer assistant 2019: The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) invited applications for the post of computer assistant at its official website, uppsc.up.nic.in. The online applications will close on December 16, while the fee submission window will be closed by December 11.

A total of 14 vacancies are to be filled through this recruitment exam.  Candidates will also have to send hard copy of their online application form along with an enclose self-attested copies of all certificates.

UPPSC computer assistant 2019: Eligibility

Age: Candidates should be at least 18 years of age to be eligible to apply. The upper age is capped at 40 years. the age will be calculated as on July 1, 2019. There is relaxation in the upper age limit for reserved category candidates.

Education: Candidates who have passed the intermediate examination with a diploma in Computer Science from a recognized institute or university are eligible to apply.

UPPSC computer assistant 2019: How to apply

Step 1: Visit the official website, uppsc.up.nic.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘click here to apply online’ next to ‘computer assistant examination 2019’ link
Step 3: Click on ‘apply’, in the new page, click on ‘registration’
Step 4: Answer yes or no in the drop-down menu
Step 5: Fill details, click on proceed
Step 6: Fill form, upload images
Step 7: Make payment and submit

UPPSC computer assistant 2019: Fee

Candidates will have to pay a fee of Rs 125 for SC/ST and ex-serviceman category the fee is Rs 65 and for handicapped category candidates, it is Rs 25.

UPPSC computer assistant 2019: Salary

Candidates will be hired at the pay scale of Rs 5,200 to Rs 20,200 with additional grade pay of Rs 2,400.
