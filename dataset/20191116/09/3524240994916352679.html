Kogi election: Large turnout of voters in Okene

16 Nov 2019, 09:37 by Gbenga Odogun

Gbenga Odogun

There is a large turnout of voters in Okene, the headquarters of Kogi Central, at the on-going Kogi State Governorship election.

Our correspondent observed that voters started trooping out as early as 6 am and there are orderliness in other polling stations visited.
At St Andrew polling units 004 and 005, Lafia Obessa ward, Okene, security men were on ground to ensure peaceful elections.
