Free entry for Omanis at UAE's Public Parks

16 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Times News Service

Muscat: Dubai Municipality has announced on its official account that it will allow Omani visitors and Omani residents in the United Arab Emirates to enter Public Parks for free across the UAE on Monday, 18 November 2019 on occasion of the 49th National Day of Oman.

A statement issued online by Dubai Municipality said, “On the occasion of the 49th National Day of Oman, Dubai Municipality will allow Omani visitors and residents to enter the public parks for free on 18 November 2019.”
