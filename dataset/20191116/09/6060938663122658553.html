Watch: Ayushmann Khurrana takes the internet by storm as he dances to Kartik Aaryan's 'Dheeme Dheeme' with Bhumi Pednekar

16 Nov 2019, 09:53 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The recently released song, 'Dheeme Dheeme' from Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar, and Ananya Panday starrer film, 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' has already impressed the audience with its quirky signature step. Adding more to the song, B-town celebs are taking the 'DancelikeChintutyagi' challenge and Ayushmann Khurrana is the one to join in.

Ayushmann Khurrana, who is currently basking in the glory of his recently released film, 'Bala', which also stars the 'PPW' actress Bhumi Pednekar took up the challenge and grooved on 'Dheeme Dheeme'. Bhumi shared the video on her Instagram handle and fans just can't get enough of it. In the video, we can see Bhumi dancing with her Bala co-star Ayushmann Khurrana on this song, 'Dheeme Dheeme'. Alongside the video, Bhumi wrote, “Chandani Raat main..Hero @ayushmannk ke saath main. #DheemeDheeme Thank you AK for always just being the best.Love Love Love you #PatiPatniAurWoh.”

Ayushmann and Bhumi were together seen in 'Bala', which has reportedly collected Rs. 73 crore in just 9 days. Talking about, 'Pati Patni Aur Woh', it is a remake of an old classic film with the same title. The makers of the film, of late dropped the hilarious trailer post which they unveiled this quirky dance number. 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' is slated to release on 6th of December.
