No stopping on this road in Oman: ROP

16 Nov 2019, 09:01 by Times News Service

Muscat: Vehicles and trucks will be prohibited from stopping on both sides of the road from Bait Al Barakah to Saeed bin Sultan Naval Base on Monday November 18, 2019.

In a statement online, the Royal Oman Police (ROP) said: "Royal Oman Police would like to draw the attention of citizens and residents that vehicles and trucks are prohibited from stopping on both sides of the road from Bait Al Barakah to Saeed bin Sultan Naval Base in the Wilayat of Al Masn'ah on Monday 18 November from 8:00 am to 8:00 pm."
