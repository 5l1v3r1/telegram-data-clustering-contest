First look at I'm A Celebrity stars as they hop into helicopter and canoes to enter camp

Kate Garraway, Caitlyn Jenner and James Haskell can all be seen doing their first I'm A Celebrity challenge as they arrive in camp

16 Nov 2019, 09:48 by Emmeline Saunders

The cast of I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! 2019 have started landing in the jungle camp, ready to be shut away for two weeks with rats and cockroaches for company.

The stars were spotted looking a little nervous this morning as they took up their first task: making their way into the rainforest camp.

Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly were seen presenting together for the first time, concentrating as they watched the famous faces getting to grips with their challenge.

At one point, Dec raised his arm as though to point something out to his best mate Dec.

 

Wearing red shirts to denote the team they're on, Caitlyn Jenner and James Haskell were seen chatting together next to a helicopter before getting into a canoe and paddling their way over to the other shore.

James took up the front seat in the rickety vessel and tucked a white flower behind his ear before seizing an oar and directing him and Caitlyn to safety.

Caitlyn looked a little hesitant as she gazed down at the murky water, no doubt hoping not to spot one of the alligators that populate the area.

 

Good Morning Britain host Kate Garraway was also there, standing with the group as producers told them what they'd be doing.

She was wearing a bright blue shirt, as was the comedian Andrew Maxwell, who could be seen in the back of a mud-splattered 4x4 with a huge grin on his face.

Radio presenter Roman Kemp and Rak-Su singer Myles Stephenson are also on a team together, both wearing purple shirts.

 

And Nadine Coyle joined up with Adele Roberts for the yellow team.

Ian Wright and Adele shared a hug before the task, with Ian wearing the colour green alongside a nervous Jacqueline Jossa.

It means all 10 of the contestants are still in the programme, with no last-minute pull-outs.

 

However, there may still be one late arrival left to come, as ITV bosses love to spring a surprise on the camp.

Emily Atack could be seen laughing with the camera crew as she filmed for the Extra Camp spin-off, which she'll be hosting with Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas.

*I'm A Celebrity launches Sunday November 17 at 9pm on ITV, followed by Extra Camp
