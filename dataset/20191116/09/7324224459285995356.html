West Midlands Trains services disrupted as rail workers go on strike

A strike by West Midlands Trains workers in the long-running dispute over guards on trains was being “solidly supported” on Saturday, disrupting services.

16 Nov 2019, 09:29

Members of the Rail, Maritime and Transport union on West Midlands Trains walked out for the first in a series of 24-hour strikes on the next few Saturdays.

Trains to Shrewsbury, Wolverhampton, Stafford, Dudley, Kidderminster, Walsall, Smethwick and Birmingham are affected – some customers were even emailed last week with the option of a refund instead of travelling.
