Spoilers: Daniel broken in Corrie after losing final memories of Sinead

16 Nov 2019, 09:01

Daniel Osbourne (Rob Mallard) has been put through the wringer in Coronation Street over the last couple of months after wife Sinead Tinker (Katie McGlynn) died as a result of her cancer diagnosis. The young man left the cobbles shortly after her death, but he returns in time for her funeral — however, there’s more heartache ahead for him.

After Beth’s (Lisa George) hurtful words, Daniel took it upon himself to get out of dodge with Bertie, and thus he left the cobbles several weeks back. However, he makes a return in the coming episodes, as he comes back for Sinead’s funeral.

The service is an incredibly emotional one and, afterwards, everyone gathers in the Rovers.

While there, Daniel informs Peter (Chris Gascoyne) that he’s going to head back to the flat so that he can watch the videos Sinead made for him and Bertie — which are on his laptop.
