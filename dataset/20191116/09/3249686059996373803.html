EXO's Sehun focuses in black in bonus 'Obsession' teaser image

16 Nov 2019, 09:29 by germainej

EXO have revealed a bonus teaser image for 'Obsession' featuring Sehun.

After his previous teasers and bonus teaser clips, Sehun is in all black as he looks focused on something for his EXO persona. As previously reported, the EXO members are facing their doppelgangers "X-EXO" for their comeback.
