The Kapil Sharma Show preview: A crazy weekend with the cast of Pagalpanti

Kapil Sharma and his team will pay a tribute to Anil Kapoor by performing on his popular songs, depicting his journey in the industry.

16 Nov 2019, 09:25 by Sana Farzeen

This weekend will be all about craziness as the team of Pagalpanti will grace The Kapil Sharma Show. The cast of the film – Anil Kapoor, John Abraham, Arshad Warsi, Pulkit Samrat, Kriti Kharbanda, Urvashi Rautela, and director Anees Bazmee will be seen joining the fun.

The actors will talk about the fun they had while working together and also share anecdotes from their personal and professional lives. As a surprise, Kapil Sharma and his team will pay a tribute to Anil Kapoor by performing on his popular songs, depicting his journey in the industry.

Anees Bazmee on working with Raj Kapoor

Director and writer Anees Baazmee will talk about working with legendary actor-filmmaker Raj Kapoor. He will share that working under him was like taking a course in a university where one could learn so much. He will add that Kapoor used to shoot only three-four sequences in a day but with utmost perfection. On the other hand, today, every filmmaker takes about 40-50 shots.

Kriti Kharbanda envious of Anil Kapoor’s youthfulness

Kriti Kharbanda will take the opportunity to unravel the fun side of Anil Kapoor. She will share that not just the male actors but even females feel insecure around Kapoor. She will say that Anil has a fun habit of teasing other actors how they can never match up to his looks. Kriti will also say that working with Anil Kapoor is a blessing for any actor as he keeps everyone on their toes.

How Bazmee manages to convince Salman, Akshay and Ajay for doing his films

Kapil Sharma will reveal to the audience that Bazmee has managed to convince stars like Salman Khan, Akshay Kumar and Ajay Devgn in just five minutes of narration. Kapil will quiz the filmmaker on how he manages to do that. Bazmee will share that it’s not his talent but the actor’s ability to gauge a film script. He will share that with the kind of experience they have, these stars manage to know how the film will turn out in just hearing a few minutes of the narration.

When Anil shot John with a real gun

John Abraham will talk about his experience of working with Anil Kapoor. He will share an incident from the filming of Shootout at Wadala when Kapoor shot him with a real gun. It so happened that the climax was a dramatic one and in the heat of the moment, Kapoor picked up a real gun and shot John on his ears. The actor had to take a break and recover from the injury.

Pagalpanti actors on their indulgence

When Kapil Sharma will quiz the actors on their ‘pagalpanti’ indulgence, Kriti will share that she loves buying shoes. She will share that she has more than 100 pairs and never repeats her footwear. John Abraham, on the other hand, will share that he loves buying speakers and has close to 58 of them at his home.
