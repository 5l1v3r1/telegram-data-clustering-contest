Supergirl: Lena Luthor Reveals Her Plan for Kara in Exclusive Clip

16 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Meagan Damore

Lena Luthor has an endgame for her friendship with Kara Danvers.

In "Tremors," the next episode of Supergirl, Lena will confront Leviathan head-on in an attempt to fall under Kara's protection. Then, she hopes to end up at the Fortress of Solitude to get her hands on something for Project Non Nocere, her plan "to rid humanity of its instincts to hurt each other."

The CBR-exclusive clip opens in Lena's apartment, while she sat on the couch reading. When her alarm chirped, she looked up and said, "You can come out now." At that, a Leviathan agent stepped into her living room, cocking a loaded gun and demanding, "Give me the medallion."
