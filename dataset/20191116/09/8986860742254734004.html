I didn’t collapse after Ekua was declared the winner of GMB 2019-Enam

16 Nov 2019, 09:01 by ‌ RASHAD

The management of Enam, the first runner up at the just ended Ghana Most Beautiful 2019 has finally cleared the air on stories making rounds that Enam collapsed and was rushed to the hospital after Ekua was pronounced the winner of GMB 2019.

According to the management, Enam never collapsed on stage as being alleged by many media portals. She was at the venue throughout the crowning ceremony.

SEE HERE: Video of when Enam allegedly collapsed and was rushed to the hospital after Ekua was declared the winner of GMB 2019 pops up

Explaining the issue of the ambulance, the management revealed Enam had been sick for days prior to the finals and was taken to the finals from the hospital.

So the doctor taking care of Enam suggested an ambulance is placed at the venue just in case it was needed.

Read the full statement below

The management of BIE CONCEPTS (Believe In ENAM) has received with shock a news item making rounds on social media and other websites after the finals of GMB’19 last Sunday at the Accra International Conference Center on the above subject matter.

We would like to state unequivocally clear that our beautiful queen ENAM, never collapsed and rushed to any hospital in an ambulance after EKUA was announced the GMB’19 WINNER.

SEE ALSO: TV3 robbed Enam by selecting Ekua as the winner of Ghana’ Most Beautiful 2019 – Netizens laments (Social Media)

THE TRUTH OF THE MATTER:

The eloquent ENAM was hospitalized on Wednesday prior to the finals until Sunday evening of that faithful day. Doctors of the health facility had arranged for a standby ambulance and paramedics for ENAM because her health was not excellent which they can’t leave anything to chance. It may be observed that ENAM was not of herself on stage and had to receive medical attention intermittently during the live show.

After the show, ENAM had to be sent back to the hospital to continue her medication and not because she collapsed. We would like to appeal to all and Sundry to disregard the said news item and treat it as false.

ENAM has since been discharged from the hospital and has recorded her first public video to thank ALL GHANAIANS who supported and prayed for her to reach that far.
