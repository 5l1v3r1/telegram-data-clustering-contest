Incredible Grade II flat offering the best views of the Liver Birds

The apartment offers direct views over the Three Graces

16 Nov 2019, 09:07 by Lottie Gibbons

A modern Grade II flat has come on the market with unparallelled views of the Royal Liver Building.

Situated on Water Street, the property has got one of the best aspects in Merseyside as it looks over The Three Graces.

The two-bedroom flat has been listed by Entwistle Green for £235,000.

And as a Grade II Listed Tower Building Development, the property exudes period charm and character.

Located right on the waterfront, the flat is just a short walk from the city's business district, as well as the Albert Dock and  Liverpool ONE.

Photos on Zoopla show the apartment is made up of an entrance hall, master bedroom with en-suite shower room, second double bedroom and family bathroom.

And for homeowners that want space, a large living room is combined with an open-plan kitchen.

The property also benefits from a secure car parking space and concierge service. 

But without a doubt, it's the uninterrupted view looking directly on to the city's best known building that makes this property so special.
