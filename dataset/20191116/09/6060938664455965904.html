Then and now: Meenakshi Sheshadri has aged like fine wine

16 Nov 2019, 09:15 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Former Miss India and Bollywood actress, Meenakshi Sheshadri, who featured in many noteworthy Bollywood films is celebrating her 55th birthday today. The actress after ruling the B-town with her talent took a back seat from Bollywood films post marriage. Now, the actress has settled abroad and runs her dance classes.

Today on account of her birthday, while browsing the internet, we stumbled upon a few rare pictures of the actress. And looking at the picture all we can say is the actress is aging like a fine wine. Many may not recognize the actress at the first glance but she has definitely preserved her beauty.

In the year 2015, Rishi Kapoor had put up a picture with the actress and with in these four years, Meenakshi has changed drastically, we must say!

Seshadri made her film debut with a Bollywood film, 'Painter Babu' in 1983 and shot to fame with the film 'Hero', which was followed by a number of films in the 1980s and 1990s.
