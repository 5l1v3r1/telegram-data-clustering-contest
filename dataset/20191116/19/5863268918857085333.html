PML-N president Shehbaz Sharif became guarantor for his elder brother Nawaz Sharif and sated that Nawaz will return to the country after 4 weeks if doctors said he is medically fit.

The affidavit submitted by Shehbaz Sharif said that the medical reports attested by foreign embassy would be submitted in the court. He said government can question doctors of Nawaz Sharif if it is needed.

Nawaz Sharif also affirmed that he would return in 4 weeks after his treatment and would face the cases against him.

He said the return would be made if doctors allowed me to go to Pakistan. Nawaz also said he would abide by the affidavit submitted by his younger brother Shehbaz Sharif.
