Video: Jim Harbaugh Is Fired Up On The Sideline vs. Michigan State

16 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Andrew Holleran

Michigan is having its way with rival Michigan State in Ann Arbor this afternoon. Wolverines head coach Jim Harbaugh appears to be enjoying it.

The Wolverines expanded their lead to 24-7 early in the third quarter after taking a 10-point lead into halftime.

Michigan scored on a nice pass from quarterback Shea Patterson to wide receiver Donovan Peoples-Jones.

Harbaugh was extremely fired up on the sideline.
