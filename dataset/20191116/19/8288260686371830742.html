'It’s normal' - Aston Villa midfielder no longer surprised by ability of team mate

Latest Aston Villa news | The pair both joined AVFC in the summer and have been regular figures for manager Dean Smith in the Premier League this season

16 Nov 2019, 19:54 by Mark Wakefield

Aston Villa midfielder Marvelous Nakamba says he is not surprised by the ability of one of his team-mates.

Nakamba moved to Villa Park in the summer from Belgian outfit Club Brugge, and has had a good start to life in the claret and blue.

The Zimbabwe international was joined at B6 in the summer by midfielder Douglas Luiz, who was signed from fellow Premier League side Manchester City.

 

The Brazilian has netted two goals for Villa since joining the club in the summer, long-range efforts which came against Bournemouth and Norwich City.

And Nakamba says he is no longer surprised to see the ball find the back of the net from Luiz’s long-range efforts, as it is something the Brazilian frequently practices in training.
