Tua Tagovailoa carted off the field, out for remainder of the season

16 Nov 2019, 19:46 by HNN Staff

STARKVILLE, Miss. (AP) - Tua Tagovailoa injured his hip while being dragged down by tacklers late in the first half on what was likely his last series and No. 4 Alabama went on to bear Mississippi State 38-7 Saturday in a game that might have cost the Crimson Tide its star quarterback.

Tagovailoa was injured after a scramble when he was taken down hard by Bulldogs linebacker Leo Lewis. Tagovailoa needed help getting to his feet and was carted off the field with 3:01 left in the second quarter.

According to multiple reports, a dislocated hip and fracture will keep Tagovailoa out for the remainder of the season.
