Tua Tagovailoa Reportedly Leaves Mississippi State Game In Ambulance

16 Nov 2019, 19:37 by Andrew Holleran

Tua Tagovailoa left this afternoon’s Alabama-Mississippi State game with an apparent injury. The Crimson Tide’s standout quarterback has now reportedly left the stadium in an ambulance.

ESPN showed footage of Tagovailoa getting placed into an ambulance late in the third quarter of this afternoon’s game.

A large Mississippi State banner was placed in front of the ambulance’s doors to give Tagovailoa some privacy.

Tagovailoa is believed to have suffered a hip injury.
