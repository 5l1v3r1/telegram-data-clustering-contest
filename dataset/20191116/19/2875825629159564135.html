Strictly's Anton kisses stunned Emma as he receives first ever 10s after 17 years

The Strictly Come Dancing pro was stunned after head judge Shirley Ballas and Bruno Tonioli awarded him and EastEnders star Emma 10s

16 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Irishmirror.ie

Anton Du Beke kissed partner Emma Barton after receiving his first ever 10s after 17 years on Strictly.

The pro was stunned after head judge Shirley Ballas and Bruno Tonioli awarded him and EastEnders star Emma 10s.

Emma and Anton received a total of 37 after performing an American Smooth to Ella Fitzgerald's Let's Face The Music.

Both Shirley and Bruno were on their feet afterwards, hailing the dance "elegant".

Anton couldn't believe it when they were awarded the scores, with Anton almost collapsing as Emma stared on in shock.

Later, Anton curled an arm around Emma and kissed her cheek.

Tonight, the remaining seven couples took to the floor to perform at the famous Blackpool Tower Ballroom.

This year's celebs packed up their fake tan, glitter and dancing shoes to head to the home of ballroom.

The grand final is now in sight, so the celebs will know what's at stake as they do their best to impress all of the judges.

Tonight's dances included a Beyonce-themed Paso to a Salsa to Uptown Funk.

Fans will have to wait until tomorrow to discover the remaining celebs' fate, as well as watch a huge Westlife performance.

Unfortunately, BBC presenter Mike Bushell missed out on Blackpool as he became the latest celeb to leave last weekend.

His partner Katya Jones told him after they were voted out: "Honestly I’m so proud of him and I think after even this dance which I think was your best dance you can leave with your head held up honestly; and in my eyes you’re the winner. The only thing he has lost is weight.

"He came in every single day giving his heart, soul, everything, 200% no matter what after every single dance off and thank you so much. I’m so sorry I couldn’t take to Blackpool."

*Strictly results air tomorrow at 7.15pm on BBC One
