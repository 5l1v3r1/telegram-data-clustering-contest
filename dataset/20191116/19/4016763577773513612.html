DaBaby Claims He Spent Half A Million On Music Videos This Year

16 Nov 2019, 19:22 by Caitlin Kelley

Out of all of DaBaby’s calling cards, his run of music videos might be his most prominent. The Charlotte MC knows how to put an absurd twist on his outsize persona, and his visuals span comedic sketches and cinematic vignettes. Most recently, he turned “BOP” into a “hip-hop musical” with a little help from Jabbawockeez. As it turns out, this eye-catching video production carries a hefty price tag.

“I paid for every video I ever shot,” he told Rolling Stone. “$100,000 budget, $70,000, $60,000, I’ve probably spent like half a million dollars on videos this year, by myself, just me. I ain’t willing to settle for less… If you’re capable of being this caliber of an artist, do I want to settle for doing shit like this just to keep some money in my pocket and spend it on bullshit or something?”
