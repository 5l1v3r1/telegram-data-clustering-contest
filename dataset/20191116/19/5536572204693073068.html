Penn State Fans Go Crazy After ‘Condom Balloon’ Scores Touchdown (VIDEO)

16 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Darrelle Lincoln

On Saturday, the Penn State Nittany Lions took the field against the Indiana Hoosiers to a frenzy crowd who were having a great time all game long.

During one part of the game, a fan decided they were going to bring a condom inside the stadium, not for protection for what it’s created for, but to blow it up like a balloon and throw it down to the field.

That condom balloon not only made it, but scored a TD as the fans went wild.
