The Top-Rated Moisturizing Products From Sephora For When the Weather Has No Chill

16 Nov 2019, 19:10 by Morgan Ashley Parker

You may (really, really) want to curl up under a weighted blanket and binge eat Christmas cookies, but a pesky thing like life has likely gotten in the way of your seasonal hermit dreams. Even if the weather isn't as wintry and bleak as it could be (yet), what if your skin has already begun its revolt? After all, even small changes in the air's moisture level, or subtle degree drops, can be a shock to your system.

Maybe your skin is flaking, your hands and feet are looking rough, or your face just seems dull, no matter how much highlighter you apply. Or perhaps you don't want to get to that place ... again. Listen, we're here to help. We combed through moisturizing products at Sephora, and read through the reviews, to see which products were solving these same problems for other people.

There are moisturizers for your face, all packing a slightly different powerful punch, and ones specifically for problem spots on your body. There's an almond mask for your feet with cute penguins on it, and a honey mask for your hair that comes out of a bear. And, at the very least, there's a strong case for changing up a routine for the winter in some form or fashion.

Dare we say these top-rated Sephora products can bring a smile to your dry, chapped lips this season? Check them out for yourself ahead.

First Aid Beauty Hydration Stars

If your skin is simply parched this season, the First Aid Beauty Hydration Stars ($12) is the equivalent of a tall glass of water. The Ultra Repair Cream offers long-term hydration for face and body, and the Ultra Repair Lip Therapy offers the same to your lips. The tube of cream is small enough to toss in a bag — but there are also two larger versions available ($32-$40). The 6,000 reviews and 150,000 loves at Sephora for it must be onto something.

Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Honey Moisture Deep Conditioning Mask

Not only does the Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Honey Moisture Deep Conditioning Mask ($36) have top ratings, the teddy bear container is just too cute. It's not just for show though — this baby bear comes bearing a hair mask with organic, unpasteurized honey, a natural humectant that strengthens hair and helps retain moisture. Also inside is rosehip oil for controlling frizz and maintaining the hydration added by the honey, and algae extract with protective minerals, amino acids, antioxidants, and vitamins, too.

Here's a sheet mask you'll want to stock up on in winter. The top-rated Dr. Jart+ Dermask Water Jet Vital Hydra Solution ($6) boasts moisturizing hyaluronic acid and algae extract that supports your body's natural collagen creation and leaves skin looking completely refreshed. The serum in the mask works to strengthen the skin's barrier and help to prevent water loss. Plus, Sephora shoppers noted it's good for even sensitive skin and reducing redness, too.

Drunk Elephant B-Hydra Intensive Hydration Serum
