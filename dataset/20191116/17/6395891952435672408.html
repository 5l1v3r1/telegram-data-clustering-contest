Gloucester Police locate young man missing for five days

16 Nov 2019, 17:36 by EndPlay

GLOUCESTER, Mass. - Gloucester Police are asking for help in finding 24-year-old Caleb McCarthy.

McCarthy, from Gloucester, was last seen on Nov. 11, and officials fear that his health and safety may be in danger.

He mentioned wanting to travel to California, police said, and he may have been in the Boston area earlier this week.

McCarthy has an outstanding arrest warrant out of Gloucester District Court, but police said he isn't considered a danger to the public.

Anyone with information pertaining to McCarthy's whereabouts can call the Gloucester Police Department at (978) 283-1212.
