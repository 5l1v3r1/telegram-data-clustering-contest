Aviation Nation comes to Nellis Air Force Base

16 Nov 2019, 17:23 by Damon Seiters

Officials expect more than 200,000 people to attend the Aviation Nation 2019 air show Saturday and Sunday at Nellis Air Force Base. The show is free and open to the public.

Because of crowds, guests are encouraged to arrive early at Las Vegas Motor Speedway, where buses will transport them to the base.
