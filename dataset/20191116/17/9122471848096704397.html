Protestor Outside Falcons Facility Has Message For Colin Kaepernick

16 Nov 2019, 17:06 by Andrew Holleran

Colin Kaepernick is set to work out in front of 20-plus NFL teams in Atlanta this afternoon. The quarterback, who hasn’t played in the league since the 2016 season, is getting a chance to impress today.

ESPN reports that more than 24 teams will be in attendance for today’s workout, which is taking place at the Falcons’ facility.

Kaepernick, his receivers and NFL officials conducting Saturday’s workout will not be allowed into the facility until about 2 p.m. ET, when the Falcons are scheduled to depart for Carolina for their Sunday matchup with the Panthers, a source told Schefter. The Falcons are supplying an equipment person, a trainer and a videographer to help the NFL conduct Kaepernick’s workout.

Not everyone appears to be happy with today’s workout, though.
