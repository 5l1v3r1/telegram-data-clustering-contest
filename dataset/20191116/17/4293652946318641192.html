Canucks’ Olli Juolevi has lower-body ailment, no timeline for return

16 Nov 2019, 17:34 by Sportsnet Staff

Vancouver Canucks defence prospect Olli Juolevi has a lower-body ailment and there is no timeline for his return to play, the team announced Saturday.

The Canucks said that Juolevi has been removed from the roster of their AHL affiliate, the Utica Comets, and will be evaluated by the NHL club’s medical staff in Vancouver.
