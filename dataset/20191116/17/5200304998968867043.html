AVTA 2019: Winners List

16 Nov 2019, 17:09 by Raj Baddhan

The Asian Viewers Television Awards (AVTA) took place on Saturday night at Hilton London Heathrow Airport Terminal 5.

This year’s event was the biggest to date with the likes of Harshad Chopda, Erica Fernandes, Kunal Jaisingh, Nakuul Mehta and Bharati Kumar all in attendance. Karan Tacker returned as host for the sixth year running.

As well as sponsoring AVTA 2019, BizAsiaLive.com is the exclusive digital media partner of the Asian Viewers Television Awards.
