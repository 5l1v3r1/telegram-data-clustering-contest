Walsall draw Chelsea U21's in the EFL Trophy

Walsall will welcome Chelsea's youngsters in the next round of the EFL Trophy.

16 Nov 2019, 17:03 by Liam Keen

The Saddlers topped their group after a 6-0 thrashing over Forest Green Rovers on Tuesday and were guaranteed a home tie.

The Saddlers are playing in the southern section of the draw and will now welcome Chelsea U21's in the next round.

The ties are due to be played on the first week of December.
