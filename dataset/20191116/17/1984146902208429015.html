Newcastle haunt Colonel Porters makes top 40 best cocktail bars in the UK list

Leading hen party organiser GoHen has named the Newcastle haunt one of the best cocktail bars in the UK

16 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Simon Duke

There's thirst quenching news for a popular Newcastle bar this week with the news that it has been named one of the best cocktail bars in the UK.

Colonel Porters Emporium, on the bottom of Dean Street near the Quayside , has only been open for two years but has established itself as a big favourite among locals and visitors to the city.

And now it has had the seal of approval on a national scale as a leading hen party company has named it as one of the best cocktail bars in the UK.

GoHen has compiled a top 40 list of its favourite places to go for a cocktail all over the country and Colonel Porters is the sole representative from Newcastle, with the other 'North East' picks (though we're not sure on the geography!) being in Leeds and Sheffield.

The glowing review of bar and restaurant Colonel Porters reads "Newcastle is bold, bright and brilliant, and the Colonel Porters Emporium is a shining jewel in the crown of its vibrant bar scene. As well as its fabulous array of cocktails, the venue itself is superb and the décor is resplendent and retro.

"No stranger to a GoHen list, they return as one of the best cocktail bars in the UK and they are certainly a standout entry."

Colonel Porters will be the place to be this weekend as they are bringing some Ibiza sunshine to Newcastle this Saturday in the shape of Melon Bomb.

In the five short years of its existence, Melon Bomb has become one of the most well known and respected party brands in Ibiza and truly encapsulates Ibiza’s magic and the essence of the island’s hedonistic vibes.

For one night only party goers and lovers of good music can dance the night away at Colonel Porters for free! Saturday, November 16, Paul Reynolds & Scott Gray will be playing till late, with resident Pete Wharrier warming up from pm- get down early to ensure entry.
