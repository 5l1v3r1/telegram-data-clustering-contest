Holiday Merryland at SM City Sta. Mesa

16 Nov 2019, 17:48

Finally, the doors of Holiday Merryland at SM City Sta. Mesa is now open. It was a fun-filled launch on November 9, 2019 and even made extra merry by special guests, Kelley Day as host, Alwyn & Jenica Uytingco with their lovely little ones, and rising star Krystal Brimner. Also bringing some gladness and cheer are San Sebastian Chamber Singers and Party with Jive Theatre Group. Splashed in red & gold, the Holiday Merryland truly is an #AweSM showing of what #SparklingSMallidays is all about!

Also present at SM City Sta. Mesa’s Holiday Merryland inauguration was Quezon City District IV Councilor, Hon. Hero Bautista, as he played a part in opening the IG-worthy spectacle to the shopping public. The Holiday Merryland is SM City Sta. Mesa’s Christmas centerpiece for the year and this bright and festive display is open to all shoppers who wish to have their pictures taken with it from November 9, 2019 up to January 5, 2020.
