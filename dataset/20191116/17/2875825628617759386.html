Molly-Mae Hague uploads telling snap from abroad amid I'm A Celeb rumours

The Love Island beauty ignited rumours she is set to enter the jungle after she shared a snap from Gatwick airport

16 Nov 2019, 17:49 by Irishmirror.ie

Molly-Mae Hague has revealed her whereabouts - after she sparked rumours that she is a secretly on the I'm A Celeb 2019 line-up - as she shared a holiday image onto social media on Saturday.

The Love Island star, 20, took to social media to share a holiday photo that gave away the latest destination she is visiting.

Looking every inch the glamorous social media influencer, Molly posed on a seat dressed in a white cropped-top teamed with Gucci tracksuit bottoms.

She shared her location by captioning the offering with a Barbadian flag emoji.

The reality TV personality also tagged herself at the exotic location.

It comes after Molly was quizzed by fans if she was heading into the jungle after uploading a pre-flight photo taken at London Gatwick airport on Friday.

The star, who came second on this year's Love Island with boyfriend Tommy Fury, wrote: "Flight life. Can't even contain my excitement for this trip. Full outfit @prettylittlething."

She was rocking an outfit from fashion brand Pretty Little Thing - who she has an extremely lucrative partnership with.

The snap caused fans to speculate that she might be jetting to Oz.

"Oh my God..." one wrote, "...Are you going on I'm A Celeb? Would love to see you in the jungle xx."

Another demanded: "Where you going why you going on the plain [sic]."

A third needed to know: "Why did u have your suitcase x."

Others poured praise on Molly, who since leaving the villa has become a fashion icon.

One wrote: "Such a babe."

Another called her a "real life angel", while a third gushed that Molly has "natural beauty."

Molly and hunky boxer Tommy are one of the three couples from the Love Island cast of 2019 to have stayed together.

They're joined by Joe Garratt and Lucie Donlan, and fellow finalists Curtis Pritchard and Maura Higgins.

Meanwhile, in Australia, the I'm A Celeb stars have been arriving at Brisbane Airport.

Co-hosts and national treasures Ant and Dec are also kicking about in the Aussie sun - it's Ant's first year back after sitting out the 2018 series to focus on his rehab for alcohol and prescription medication addictions.

Molly might not be among them, but Ian Wright, Caitlyn Jenner, Andrew Maxwell, Jacqueline Jossa, James Haskell, Adele Roberts, Myles Stephenson, Kate Garraway, Nadine Coyle and Roman Kemp are all confirmed for the jungle.

*I'm A Celebrity: Get Me Out Of Here! is back on Sunday 17th November at 9pm on ITV1.
