Urban Meyer Owns Michigan On FOX’s College Football Pregame Show

16 Nov 2019, 17:25 by Chris Rosvoglou

We’re still a few weeks away from another Ohio State-Michigan showdown, but Urban Meyer is already throwing shade at the program from Ann Arbor.

Meyer is no longer the head coach of the Buckeyes, however, it’s obvious that he still represents his former program well.

During his time at Ohio State, Meyer never lost to Michigan. He made sure to remind fans in Ann Arbor of his perfect record.

Before FOX’s Big Noon Kickoff show came to an end, Meyer was asked if he knew any of the lyrics to Michigan’s fight song. His response was about as good as it gets.

“I know 7-0,” Meyer said.

That’s the equivalent of a punch to the gut for the Wolverines.
