Homicide unit investigating after body found in burned SUV in Abbotsford

16 Nov 2019, 17:37

ABBOTSFORD, B.C. - Homicide detectives have been handed the case after police say a body was discovered in a burnt-out SUV in Abbotsford, B.C.

Firefighters responded just after 10:30 p.m. Friday to a report of a vehicle on fire on Bates Road.

Police say the vehicle was fully engulfed when the fire crew arrived.

They say the body was located after the flames were doused.
