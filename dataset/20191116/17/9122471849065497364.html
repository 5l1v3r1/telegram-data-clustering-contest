College GameDay Staff Makes Week 12 Picks For 2019 Season

16 Nov 2019, 17:01 by Matt Lombardi

College GameDay is in Waco, Texas ahead of tonight’s big showdown between the Baylor Bears and the Oklahoma Sooners. Per usual, the show’s staff made its picks for the biggest games of the day.

Chip and Joanna Gaines of ‘Fixer Upper’ were the celebrity guest pickers. The HGTV stars live and work in Waco, hence the selection.

Lee Corso made his headgear selection and went with Baylor to score the upset at home.
