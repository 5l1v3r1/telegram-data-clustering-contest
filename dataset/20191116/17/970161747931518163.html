India Reynolds hits out as she's trolled for 'spelling Dubai wrong'

16 Nov 2019, 17:45

Love Island’s India Reynolds has been met with a string of trolls over her spelling of Dubai.

The model – who recently split from Ovie Soko – has been living her best life out in Dubai, and took a second to bless us with a snap of herself on the beach.

Alongside the stunning upload – of the 28-year-old posing in a black bikini with skyscrapers behind her – she told her 1.1million followers: ‘In love with Dubae @fivepalmjumeirah.’

And, while we giggled at her pun, some didn’t quite get the joke, sliding in to claim she had spelled the country wrong.

Yes, seriously.

‘It’s Dubai learn how to spell,’ one helpful person commented, while another wrote: ‘Imagine flying all the way there and can’t even spell it kmt.’

‘Should probably learn how to spell it then…..,’ a follower suggested.

Having absolutely none of it, the ITV2 star then took to her Instagram story, insisting that she actually knows how to spell correctly.

‘Shall we all calm down…,’ India began. ‘I know how to spell “Dubai”.

‘It clearly wasn’t all that punny… but it gets like that sometimes.’

Well we thought she showed great pun-tential…

Yeah, we’ll leave the puns to the expert.

India recently made headlines after she confirmed her split from Ovie Soko, just months after they met in the Love Island villa.

The pair ended the show in third place, but went their separate ways at the end of October.

And she appeared to hit back at negative comments she received since their break-up, urging people to think before they comment on things.

Alluding to the romance, she posted: ‘It’s free to be kind… Think before you type… Thank you x.’

It seems some of her followers didn’t quite get the memo…
