Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez: Republicans 'Beclowning Themselves' Defending Trump | Breitbart

16 Nov 2019, 02:15 by Pam Key

On Friday’s broadcast of MSNBC’s “All In,” Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez (D-NY) said after President Donald Trump’s tweet about former U.S. ambassador to the Ukraine Marie Yovanovitch, Republicans were “beclowning themselves” trying to defend the president.
