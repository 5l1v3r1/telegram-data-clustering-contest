Jennifer Hawkins sells luxury home to move north

16 Nov 2019, 02:39 by Vanessa Stubbs

The $20 million dollar home is currently on the market.

Model and new mum Jennifer Hawkins has quietly listed her $20 million waterfront northern beaches property the Manly Dailyreports.

Jen and hubby Jake Wall welcomed their baby girl, Frankie into the world in October and it's understood the couple plan to move north to be closer to her family.

Hands-on house builders

The couple spent three years building their four-level home, Casa Paloma on Pittwater at Newport.

The home features an indoor elevator, home theatre, private gym, pool, five bedrooms and a walk-in dressing room.

Its sale is expected to smash local records with an estimated $20m price tag.

Acreage near the coast

The couple are reportedly wanting to buy on acreage in the Central Coast area, close to Jennifer's family who are based in Newcastle.

The pair have already designed and built two houses, one in Curl Curl as well as the Newport property, so it's possible they might take up tools and build another incredible home.
