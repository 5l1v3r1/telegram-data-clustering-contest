Brent Clapp named Hemphill County Sheriff

16 Nov 2019, 02:04 by Cassie Stafford

CANADIAN, Texas (KFDA) - Hemphill County now has a new sheriff.

Former chief deputy Brent Clapp was named the new Hemphill County Sheriff Friday.

The Hemphill County Commissioners Court appointed him.

Former sheriff Nathan Lewis resigned Tuesday, after he was reprimanded by the Texas Commission on Law Enforcement.
