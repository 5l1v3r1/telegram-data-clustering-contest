Michael Bloomberg’s gal pal re-registered as Democrat last year

16 Nov 2019, 02:19 by Carl Campanile

Mike Bloomberg’s longtime girlfriend, Diana Taylor, re-registered as a Democrat last year as he first considered a 2020 White House bid, The Post has learned.

Taylor, a longtime Republican who toyed with a 2010 Senate run on the GOP line, filed the paperwork with the Board of Elections in October 2018.

That’s roughly the same time the billionaire media mogul and former mayor changed his registration from independent to Democrat as he first explored a possible challenge to President Trump.

Bloomberg has yet to launch his campaign, but that didn’t stop one longtime admirer from announcing an endorsement on Friday.

“If Mike Bloomberg runs for president, he will command the support of current and former mayors across America. I would be one of them,” said former Miami Beach, Florida, Mayor Philip Levine, who is running for governor as a Democrat in Florida and considers Bloomberg a mentor.

“As a fellow mayor, Mike was and is considered one of the greatest mayors in history,” he added. “If he throws his hat in the ring, I believe he would present the president with the most formidable challenge in the field.”
