Fire engulfs Red Carpet TV office

16 Nov 2019, 23:12 by The Eagle Online

The office of Red Carpet Communications Limited, owners of Red Carpet TV, caught fire on Saturday.

The inferno, which began around 2pm, caught the attention of residents within the area who then alerted the Lagos State Fire Service in Alausa, Ikeja.

With swift response from the fire fighters, the fire was doused.

But this was not before engulfing the entire studio compartment of the building.
