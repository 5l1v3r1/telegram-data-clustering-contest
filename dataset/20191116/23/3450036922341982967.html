15th annual Nights N' Lights returns to Ottumwa

16 Nov 2019, 23:12 by Asia Mitchell-Tabb

OTTUMWA, Iowa — It’s starting to look a lot like Christmas in the City of Bridges.

After months of preparations, the annual Nights 'N Lights has made it’s return to Ottumwa.

Visitors don’t have to get out of their vehicles to enjoy the hundreds of lights and display.

They can cruise around the park in their car while gaining excitement for the holiday season.

Volunteers were even passing out popcorn to those who were visiting the annual attraction.

Many visitors were very excited about Nights 'N Lights returning to the Heartland.

“They’re so excited. My kids are. We come every year, usually on my birthday which is the 15th of December but we’re doing it early this year because they cannot wait, "said Sheila Alexander.

You can purchase tickets in advance for $10 at any BP Elliot Oil, or pay $12 at the gate per car.

The Ottumwa Nighs 'N Lights ends on Dec. 27.
