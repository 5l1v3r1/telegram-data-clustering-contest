Explore: your new favourite travel destination

16 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Joanne Crawford

Explore exciting adventures and inspiring travel ideas in your new weekly travel lift-out - Explore - published every Saturday in 14 daily newspapers across Australia.

With destinations, deals and advice, plus travel writing to transport you around Australia and around the globe, Explore has the details on places to stay and the things to see and do.

In the first edition, appearing on Saturday, November 23, you'll discover how to spend 24 hours in Singapore and catch all the must-see sights, sounds and tastes that this exciting city has to offer, while Hollywood actor and now Byron Bay resident Chris Hemsworth (of Thor fame) reveals his favourite Australian holiday destinations.

Join us on board an Asian cruise to sample an amazing feast of coastal resorts from Thailand to Hong Kong and don't miss our guide to Alaska, to learn how you can sled with huskies, cruise the glaciers and eat the most delicious seafood.

Let Explore help you dream and plan. From cruising to tours, resorts and family getaways, there's a world of possibilities for your next holiday.

Look for the first edtion on Saturday, November 23 in the Canberra Times, Newcastle Herald, Border Mail, Ballarat Courier, Bendigo Advertiser, Warrnambool Standard, Launceston Examiner, Burnie Advocate, Illawarra Mercury, Wagga Daily Advertiser, the Northern Daily Leader, Central Western Daily, Western Advocate and Dubbo Daily Liberal.

Explore will quickly become your new favourite travel destination.
