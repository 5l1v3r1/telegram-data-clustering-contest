College football scores: Results, updates, highlights from Week 12's top-25 games

16 Nov 2019, 23:30 by Zac Al-Khateeb

Week 12 of the college football season might not have quite as a big game as a Game of the Century, but it has what may be the most top-25 matchups of the college football regular season.

Five games take place between teams ranked in the AP Top 25, including No. 24 Indiana at No. 9 Penn State, No. 21 Navy at No. 16 Notre Dame, No. 7 Minnesota at No. 23 Iowa and the headliners of No. 5 Georgia at No. 13 Auburn and No. 10 Oklahoma at No. 12 Baylor
