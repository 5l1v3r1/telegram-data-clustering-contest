UNDERCOVER Delivers Hoodies, Graphic Tees & More for New "UNDERCOVER NOISE LAB" Line

A lean run of black and white streetwear staples.

16 Nov 2019, 23:27 by Patrick Montes

Directly following the debut of a new BEA@BRICKS collaboration with Medicom Toy, UNDERCOVER is revealing plans for a graphic-heavy Fall collection.

Set to line the shelves of a special pop-up space in Tokyo, UNDERCOVER’s upcoming “UNDERCOVER NOISE LAB” range revolves around a new array of hoodies, short- and long-sleeve t-shirts, sweaters and coach jackets. The items will be released in black and white colorways, with a handful of different “UNDERCOVER NOISE LAB” motifs utilized for the line’s offerings. Additionally, each piece featured in UNDERCOVER’s latest drop is also adorned with a a more understated, tasteful style of branding.

You can take a look at UNDERCOVER’s new “UNDERCOVER NOISE LAB” collection above. Each selection from the “UNDERCOVER NOISE LAB” lineup will be available for purchase at UNDERCOVER’s forthcoming Shibuya Parco (15-1 Udagawacho , Shibuya-ku, Tokyo Shibuya Parco 2F) pop-up shop, which will open its doors on Friday, November 22.

Earlier this month, UNDERCOVER teamed up with Converse to introduce two camo-clad Chuck 70s as part of their jointly-designed “THE NEW WARRIORS” collection.
