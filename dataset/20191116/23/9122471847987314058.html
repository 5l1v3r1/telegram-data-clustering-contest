Urban Meyer Reacts To Tua Tagovailoa’s Devastating Injury

16 Nov 2019, 23:23 by Andrew Holleran

Urban Meyer knows what it’s like to see his star quarterback go down with a devastating injury. Both Braxton Miller and J.T. Barrett suffered serious injuries during his time at Ohio State.

The FOX Sports college football analyst has thus shared his reaction to the devastating injury to Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa, who suffered a season-ending injury against Mississippi State today.

“I was just staring at Coach Saban’s face and he was hurting,” Meyer said on FOX. “We’re all hurting for a kid like that. That’s the worst thing about this sport is injury and having to deal with it.”

Meyer then addressed Saban’s somewhat controversial decision to keep Tagovailoa in the game with Alabama up big. The Crimson Tide’s head coach said he wanted to get Tagovailoa one more drive and some practice in a two-minute drill.

“He made the comment about the two-minute drill, I certainly understand that,” Meyer said. “Is it the right thing or the wrong thing, that’s his decision to make with the player involved.”
