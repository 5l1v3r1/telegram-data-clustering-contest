Kaepernick To NFL Scouts After Workout: ‘Tell Your Owners To Stop Being Scared’ (VIDEOS)

16 Nov 2019, 23:27 by Darrelle Lincoln

After a lot of adjustments, Colin Kaepernick showed up and showed out on saturday in front of NFL scouts.

Earlier this week, the NFL sent out a memo to all 32 teams saying the free agent quarterback would essentially have a Pro Day in Atlanta at the Falcons practice facility, but that fell apart about half an hour before the event and it was subsequently moved to Charles Drew High School.
