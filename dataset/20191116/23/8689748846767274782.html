Stonebwoy’s Kpoo K3k3 Wins Best Collaboration At 2019 MTN 4Syte Music Video Awards

16 Nov 2019, 23:51 by Ghbase Staff

Stonebwoy popular song ‘Kpoo K3k3’ has won the Best Collaboration category at the 2019 MTN 4Syte Music Video Awards.

Stonebwoy featured the likes of Kwesi Arthur, Kelvynboy, Medikal, and Darko Vibes on the song and has over 4 million views on Youtube.

The song was in the same category with these songs below:

King Promise ft Wizkid – Tokyo
Ponobiom ft Stonebwoy – Obiaa Wone Master
Medikal ft Shatta Wale – Omo Ada remix
Article Wan ft Patapaa – That thing
Sarkodie ft King Promise – Can’t Let You Go
Adina ft Kuami Eugene – Killing Me Softly
Kwaw Kese ft Sarkodie, Medikal, Skonti – Dondo
MzVee ft Yemi Alade – Come and see my Mother
Captain Planet ft Kidi – I Miss You Die
Samini ft Kidi – Master key
R2bees ft King Promise – Picture
13 Kidi ft kwesi arthur – Mr Badman
