Patrick Mahomes Sends Message To Alabama QB Tua Tagovailoa

16 Nov 2019, 23:21 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, Alabama’s star quarterback suffered a season-ending injury.

Tua Tagovailoa, who entered the game with a questionable designation, was carted off the field after dislocating his hip.

According to a report from Aaron Suttles, Tua also suffered a fracture in the hip. “Tua Tagovailoa is out for the season with a dislocated hip with a posterior wall fracture, a person with knowledge of the situation tells me,” Suttles reported.

With just two games left in the regular season, Tagovailoa’s season is over.

After the news broke that Tua suffered a season-ending injury, college football fans, players and others wished the best for the star quarterback.

Now a star NFL quarterback has weighed in. Kansas City Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes sent a message to the injured Tua.

“Prayers up Tua, hope this is just a minor setback in a bright future!” Mahomes said on Twitter.
