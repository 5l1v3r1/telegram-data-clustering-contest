Colin Kaepernick Reportedly Got “Very Positive Feedback” From His Workout

16 Nov 2019, 23:39 by Matt Lombardi

Colin Kaepernick ticked off the NFL on Saturday, but it’s possible he may have landed himself a job in the league regardless. Kaepernick reportedly looked good in front of scouts and displayed “elite” arm talent.

Kaepernick was slated to appear for an NFL-sanctioned workout at the Atlanta Falcons indoor facility in front of 25 teams at 3:00 PM ET. Less than an hour before the event, however, Kaepernick’s team announced to the public that they’d be holding his actual workout elsewhere – at a school about an hour from Atlanta.

The media was also invited. The NFL’s scheduled workout was not to include media.

ESPN’s Adam Schefter says an NFL executive told him that Kaepernick still had “elite” arm talent. Jason La Canfora also said that Kaepernick got “very positive feedback” from the event.

Both reports suggest Kaepernick impressed with his ability to throw the deep ball.
