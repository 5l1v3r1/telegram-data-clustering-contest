No one injured in Vernon house fire

16 Nov 2019, 23:46 by Parker Crook

No one was home when a fire sparked in a Vernon residence Saturday.

Vernon Fire Department Chief David Lind confirmed that crews responded to a blaze at 7521 Brooks Lane shortly after 1:30 p.m. Saturday, Nov. 16.

There is no word on the extent of the damages.
