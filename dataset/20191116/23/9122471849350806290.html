Colin Kaepernick Tells NFL Owners To Stop Being Scared

16 Nov 2019, 23:44 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, former NFL quarterback Colin Kaepernick was scheduled to work out for NFL scouts at the Atlanta Falcons facility. ESPN NFL insider Adam Schefter revealed the location of his workout changed last minute.

However, that didn’t stop the workout.

Kaepernick and his team re-located the workout to a local Georgia high school instead. Once there, he warmed up, threw to receivers and met with a few scouts after the workout.

According to Carolina Panthers reporter Jourdan Rodrigue, Kaepernick spoke with scouts from the Washington Redskins, New York Jets and Kansas City Chiefs after the workout was complete.

He told those scouts to sent a message to their team’s respective owner.

“When you go back, tell your owners to stop being scared,” Kaepernick said.
