Luis Suarez’s story is that of redemption. How many times have we seen an exceptionally talented footballer break into the scene only to fall on the wayside because of a lack of discipline? The Uruguayan was on the verge of the same.

He has bitten three players in his career, with 2 of them coming for Ajax and Liverpool, while the last of them was at the World Cup! He was also involved in the racism saga with Patrice Evra apart from being called out for diving and play acting on several occasions.

However, that side of him is now but a blur. The striker has changed his ways since joining Barcelona in 2014 and has since come good on his potential. A two-time champions league winner with Barcelona, he is the only man in the last 10 years apart from Cristiano Ronaldo or Lionel Messi to win the European Golden shoe and the man has done it twice in his career! One at Liverpool and one with Barcelona.

In the years since, he has become an integral side of the Blaugrana and has become close friends with his teammates. However, with all friendships come pranks. Unfortunately for Gerrard Pique, it seems Suarez’s pranks can hurt just as much as his bites.

Watch: Luis Suarez plays brutal prank on Gerrard Pique

A video from Barcelona’s upcoming documentary shows Luis Suarez planting a toothpick onto Pique’s seat while the defender stood up. After passing bread to a teammate Pique sat on the seat only to have the toothpick pierce his behind.
