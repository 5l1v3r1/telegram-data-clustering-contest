SSC JHT, SHT admit card 2019 released: Check how to download, exam pattern

SSC JHT admit card 2019: The examination will consist of two papers. Paper-I will be held on November 26 and paper-II will be conducted later. Check the admit card at ssc-cr.org.

16 Nov 2019, 08:24 by Careers Desk

SSC JHT admit card 2019: The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) released the admit card for the post of Junior Hindi Translator (JHT), junior translator, senior Hindi Translator (SHT). Candidates who have registered for the exam can check their admit card at the regional websites – ssc-cr.org. The exam will be held on November 26, 2019.

The examination will consist of two papers. Paper-I will be held on November 26 and paper-II will be conducted later. The paper I will be a computer-based test of 100 marks. It will be conducted on two hours and assess Hindi and English of candidates. Paper-II will be a descriptive test of 200 marks.

SSC JHT admit card 2019: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website, ssc-cr.org
Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link ‘download admit card for JHT..’
Step 3: Click on ‘proceed now’
Step 4: Fill the credentials, click ‘search’
Step 5: Admit card will appear, download

SSC JHT admit card 2019: Salary

Those selected for the pots of JHY will be paid in the range of Rs 35,400 to Rs 1,12,400. Candidates selected for the post of SHT will be paid in the range of Rs 44,900 to Rs 14,2400 and for CHTI the salary is Rs 47600 to Rd 1,51,100.
