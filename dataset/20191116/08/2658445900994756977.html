BREAKING: Hoodlums attack Dino Melaye's polling unit

16 Nov 2019, 08:35 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

By Tony Akowe

Thugs just attacked the polling unit where Dino Melaye voted.

The hoodlums who came in black hillux van and Honda Civic saloon car broke one of the boxes and carted away the other, shooting into the air.

Those who were yet to vote scamper for safety as the thugs arrived the unit.
