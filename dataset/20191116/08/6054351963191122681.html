Kodak Black Reportedly Hit With 2 New Felony Gun Charges

16 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Kyle Eustice

Miami, FL – Miami-Dade prosecutors have reportedly charged the incarcerated Kodak Black — real name Bill K. Kapri — with two new felony gun charges. Both charges carry a maximum sentence of 30 years each.

According to Miami New Times, Miami-Dade State Attorney Katherine Fernandez Rundle slapped the chart-topping rapper with two felony counts of possession of a firearm by a felon.
