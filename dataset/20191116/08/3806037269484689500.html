Billy's & Vans Link for Exclusive OG Classic Slip-On LX

Featuring eco-fur lining.

16 Nov 2019, 08:31 by Ambrose Leung

Billy’s Tokyo and Vans link once more for an exclusive version of the OG Classic Slip-On LX. This iteration sees the Slip-On clad in a wheat-colored suede with white piping that frames the uppers along with the white rubber foxing.

In keeping with the Fall/Winter season, Vans has incorporated a shearling lining in light pink that can be seen spilling out from the shoe’s opening. The warm detailing is enhanced with 3M reflective panels at the heel of the foot; readying these Slip-Ons for both cold and dark conditions.

Those interested can find these Billy’s Tokyo exclusives over at their online shop for a price of ￥13,000 JPY (approximately $120 USD).
