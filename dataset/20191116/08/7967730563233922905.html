Child critical after near-drowning at council pool in Brisbane's south

16 Nov 2019, 08:59 by Toby Crockford

A young boy is fighting for life after a near-drowning at a council pool on Brisbane's southside.

Emergency services, including critical-care paramedics, were called to Runcorn Pool on Bonemill Road at Runcorn about 3.20pm on Saturday.

A Queensland Ambulance Service spokesman said a "male school-aged child" was pulled from the water before being taken to the Queensland Children's Hospital in a critical condition.
