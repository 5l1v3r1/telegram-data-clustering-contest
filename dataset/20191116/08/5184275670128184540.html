Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior: Ajay Devgn Glimpses Us With Fierce Maratha Army & It’s Just 3 Days Away

Ajay Devgn is making sure to keep the buzz intact by treating fans with some new looks and posters at regular intervals.

16 Nov 2019, 08:45 by Koimoi.com Team

Ajay Devgn’s Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior is making all the right noises and being amongst the most ambitious projects, Ajay is making sure to keep the buzz intact by treating fans with some new looks and posters at regular intervals.

Just a while ago, Ajay teased with another poster of Tanhaji as a reminder of trailer release on 19th November. In the poster, we can see the backshot of Ajay and him holding the saffron flag which is considered as a symbol of Maratha Swaraj (empire). The poster has “Swaraj Se Badhkar Kya?” and it just lifts your spirit up.

Ajay took to Twitter to share the poster and he wrote, “Swaraj se badhkar kya? #TanhajiTheUnsungWarrior, in cinemas 10th January 2020. 4 DAYS TO TANHAJI TRAILER.”
