Walla Walla From Pagalpanti On ‘How’s The Hype?’: BLOCKBUSTER Or Lacklustre? VOTE NOW!

Pagalpanti stars John Abraham, Arshad Warsi, Illean D'Cruz, Anil Kapoor and promises to be a laughter riot.

16 Nov 2019, 08:43 by Koimoi.com Team

John Abraham, Arshad Warsi, Anil Kapoor, Pulkit Samrat, Illeana D’Cruz, Urvashi Rautela and Kriti Kharbanda starrer Pagalpanti is successfully spreading their magic all over the Town. The trailer and the first two songs from the film got a massive response from the audience and now the makers have released another peppy track titled Walla Walla for you to groove on.

Crooned by Nakash Aziz, Neeti Mohan and Nayeem Shah, Walla Walla has now made it to Koimoi’s ‘How’s The Hype?’ meter. The song has a very Arabic vibe and the lyrics are penned down by Arafat Mehmood. Nayeem Shah has composed the peppy music of the song. The actors can be seen dressed in afghani attires as they groove to this song from the Anees Bazmee film.
