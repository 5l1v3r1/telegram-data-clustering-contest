Floods hit Buhera… Houses and property destroyed

In an unfortunate case, houses and property were destroyed after a series of floods hit Buhera district early last night (Friday).

The rains were accompanied by strong winds resulting in roofs being blown away, homes immersed in water and trees uprooted.

Said the Children of Zimbabwe War Veterans Association Platform on Twitter

Please check with all ur relatives in the following areas, chirumhanzu, Chikomba & Buhera district. There was heavy rain with serious wind & thunder. Most houses trees & roads were destroyed today btwn 1730 hrs to 1830.
