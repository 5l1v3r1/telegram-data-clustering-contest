I’m now a Shatta Wale fan because Sarkodie snubbed me – Comedia OB Amponsah

16 Nov 2019, 06:43 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian Comedian, OB Amponsah has revealed that he has now thrown his weight behind dancehall artiste, Shatta Wale after he was snubbed by his favourite Ghanaian musician, Sarkodie.

According to the media on why he is taking this step, he revealed that the BET award winner he once loved, cherished and fought for snubbed him on social media.

In his revelation, he revealed that he tagged Sarkodie in a post on Twitter after his exciting performance in Nigeria, but the Team-based rapper ignored his tags and mentions.

In an interview with Ghanaweekend TV, he noted that Sarkodie used to be his favourite artiste in Ghana but he has now changed him for Shatta Wale.

“My favourite musician in Ghana, earlier it was Sarkodie but after he ignored me it’s Shatta Wale now,” he said when he was asked who his favourite Ghanaian artiste is.

he continued that: “Shatta has not even heard about me he tweeted about me…I love people who love me back so it’s Shatta Wale now…Paaah paaah paaah”
