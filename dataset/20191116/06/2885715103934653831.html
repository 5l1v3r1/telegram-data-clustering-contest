Delhi DSSSB various posts admit card released: Check exam date, how to download

DSSSB admit card 2019: Those who have registered for the exam can download their admit card or hall ticket from the official websites - dssb.delhi.gov.in, dssbonline.nic.in.

16 Nov 2019, 06:13 by Careers Desk

DSSSB admit card 2019: The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Commission (DSSSB) released the admit card for the post of craft instructor, welfare officer, staff car driver, dhobi and district staff officer. Those who have registered for the exam can download their admit card or hall ticket from the official websites – dsssb.delhi.gov.in, dsssbonline.nic.in.

The exam is scheduled to be conducted on November 24. Candidates will have to take the copy of their e-admit card along with them to the exam hall. No candidates will be allowed to enter the exam hall without a valid admit card or verification. Candidates need to take their government-approved photo identity cards along as well.

DSSSB admit card 2019: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website, dssb.delhi.gov.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘admit card for offline exam dates November 24..’
Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page (if a dialogue box appeared, click ok)
Step 4: Click on the job you have applied for
Step 5: Log-in using credentials
Step 6: Admit card will be available in the dashboard, download
