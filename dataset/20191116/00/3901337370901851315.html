Spain Fire Seven Goals Past Malta

16 Nov 2019, 00:09 by SL International

Spain 7-0 Malta

Spain, who have already qualified, maintained their unbeaten record at the top of Group F after hammering Malta 7-0 at Estadio Ramon de Carranza.

Alvaro Morata and Santi Cazorla got first-half goals, before La Roja ran riot in the second stanza through goals from Pau Torres, Pablo Sarabia, Daniel Olmo, Gerard Moreno and Jesus Navas.

Bosnia and Herzegovina 0-3 Italy

Italy, with their ticket to next summer's tournament already secured, kept their perfect record at the top of Group J after beating Bosnia and Herzegovina 3-0 at Stadion Bilino Polje.

Francesco Acerbi and Lorenzo Insigne gave Roberto Mancini's men a 2-0 lead going into the break, while Andrea Belotti sealed the three points in the second stanza.

Denmark 6-0 Gibraltar

Denmark are on the cusp of qualifying after thrashing Gibraltar 6-0 at Telia Parken on Friday evening.

Christian Eriksen and Robert Skov both bagged two goals each, while Christian Gytkjaer and Martin Braithwaite were also on the score-sheet for the Danes, who moved to the top of the Group D

Norway 4-0 Faroe Islands

Norway's chances of finishing among the top two sides in Group F came to an end, despite thumping Faroe Islands 4-0 at Ullevaal Stadion.

Tore Reginiussen and Iver Fossum got two early goals for the hosts, before Alexander Sorloth got a quick brace just after the hour-mark.

However Norway were made to settle for fourth spot, with 14 points - four points behind second-placed Sweden, with only one match left to play.

Switzerland 1-0 Georgia

Switzerland kept their hopes of qualifying alive with a 1-0 victory over Georgia in Group D at Kybunpark.

Cedric Itten got the crucial goal for the hosts with 13 minutes remaining to move up to second in the pool, just a point behind leaders Denmark.
