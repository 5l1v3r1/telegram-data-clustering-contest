Binder unbeatable in Valencia FP3

Friday pacesetter continued to stay at the summit as almost everyone improves on Saturday morning in the Moto2™ class

16 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Brad Binder (Red Bull KTM Ajo) continued to set a precedent in the Moto2™ class at the Gran Premio Motul de la Comunitat Valenciana, the South African going even quicker in FP3 to lead Fabio Di Giannantonio (MB Conveyors Speed Up) and Augusto Fernandez (FlexBox HP 40) by two tenths.
