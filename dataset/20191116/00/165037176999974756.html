Bruno Fernandes will not force through exit from Sporting CP

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Sporting Lisbon player Bruno Fernandes is clear that he is at the team he wants to be at. Despite rumours linking him with Real Madrid and Tottenham, the midfielder assures that he is happy in Portugal.

"I'm not worried, I've been at the club since 2016 and they say that I'm going to leave, but I'm still here", Bruno Fernandes said in comments reported by "TMW".

Bruno Fernandes admits that he would like to achieve certain goals, but he has all the time in the world. "I have dreams to fulfill but I' not worried", he signaled.

The midfielder is focussed on Sporting CP, and above all, happy. "I have no reason to force through my exit, I am good in the club that I like". he continued.

"If I have to leave one day, I hope to leave good memories", Bruno Fernandes concluded who, for now, prefers not to think about his possible exit.
