Olympiacos threaten to withdraw from Greek league

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Olympiacos are top of the table, but they are threatening to withdraw from it because of the situation of the Greek Federation and because they consider there to be manipulations in VAR.

In matchday 10, Olympiacos are top with 26 points, 2 points above PAOK in the eternal fight of the two biggest teams in Greece.
