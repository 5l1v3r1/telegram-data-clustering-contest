Video: Thugs Snatch Ballot Boxes, Shoot Sporadically In Kogi State

16 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

There has been a report that some suspected political thugs on Saturday, stormed one of the polling units in Kogi State.

The incident is said to have happened at ward 002, in Iluofa.

The people of the community were seen scampering to safety as the thugs escaped with the boxes and shot into the air as they left.
