You can’t miss activated charcoal in the beauty and wellness aisles these days. Not only because of its distinct jet-black color, but because the ingredient is seemingly everywhere: bottled water, haircare, and even toothpaste. One category activated charcoal is appearing in more and more? Soap. Cleansing with charcoal-colored soap or body wash takes some getting used to, but you may not mind once you see the results.
