Daesh leader arrested in Ukraine

16 Nov 2019 by MENA

KIEV, Nov 16 (MENA) - A leader of Daesh terrorist group was arrested in the Ukrainian capital Kiev, Sputnik news agency quoted the Ukrainian Security Service as saying.

The leader was arrested in a joint special operation of the Security Service of Ukraine, the Ministry of Interior of Georgia and the US Central Intelligence Agency, added the security service.
