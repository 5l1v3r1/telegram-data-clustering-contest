Biafra: Nnamdi Kanu To Hold Live Broadcast Today, See Time

16 Nov 2019 by Philips Sunday

The leader of the Indigenous People of Biafra (IPOB), Nnamdi Kanu, will hold a live broadcast today, Saturday, November 16.

Naija News reports that this was revealed by the IPOB leader in a tweet on the micro-blogging site, Twitter.

Kanu said he would address Biafrans by 7 pm Nigerian time, which he refers to as Biafran time.

In the tweet, Kanu maintained that his group (IPOB) “will continue the International diplomatic onslaught.”

Naija News reports that Kanu has been making diplomatic connections recently in a bid to actualize the Republic of Biafra, a struggle he claimed, took the life of his mother who died in Germany recently.

The IPOB leader tweeted: “IPOB will continue the International diplomatic onslaught.
