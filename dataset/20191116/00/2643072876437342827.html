Kogi Decides: Governor Yahaya Bello Cast His Vote

16 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

The incumbent Governor of Kogi State, Mr. Yahaya Bello has casted his vote at Agaza Okene-Eba/Agassa/Ahache Ward, Unit 11, Okene Local Government.

The governor who is also the candidate All progressive party was the first key stakeholder to cast his vote.

Naija News understands that the voting has kicked off in many polling units. Everything has been peaceful so far and card readers are functioning in all polling units so far visited.
