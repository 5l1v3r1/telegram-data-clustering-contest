WATCH: Smriti Irani performs 'Talwar Raas'

16 Nov 2019 by ANI

Union minister Smriti Irani performed 'Talwar Raas', a traditional dance form using swords, at a cultural programme in Bhavnagar on Friday.

Irani, who could be seen wielding two swords on the stage, tried her best to match steps with the girls performing there.

The Union minister for textiles and women and child development was in Bhavnagar for a cultural event hosted by the Shri Swaminarayan Gurukul.
