Hazard's brace for Belgium

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Belgium went up 3-0 against Russia in the 1st half. A scoreline where Hazard was the protagonist: Thorgan got the first and Eden got a brace just 6 minutes later.

Eden Hazard retrurned to lead Belgium against Russia in a stratospheric first half. Roberto Martinez's men went in at the break 3-0 up with the Madrid star, Eden, and Borussia Dortmund player, Thorgan, stealing the show.

Thorgan Hazard opened the scoring in the 19th minute from a Thorgan Hazard pass. A great goal from just outside the box that was proceeded by an Eden Hazard brace.

The attacker scored a nice half-volley in the dee after Romelu Lukaku's knockdown to make the 2-0 in the 33rd minute. Russia goalkeeper Guilherme failed to keep out Hazard's powerful shot, which was his second in 6 minutes.
