Dacoit shoots himself dead by accident during robbery attempt in Karachi | Pakistan - Geo.tv

16 Nov 2019 by Kashif Mushtaq

KARACHI: A dacoit shot himself dead by accident during a robbery attempt on Friday here near a petrol pump in the metropolis' Quaidabad area.

The armed dacoit attempted to rob a fruit-seller at Manzil Pump but both ended up throwing hands at each other. As they fought, the robber's gun went off and he was hit by his own bullet, dying shortly afterwards.
