Kogi: APC Deputy Governorship Candidate Wins Polling Unit

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The Deputy Governorship candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC) in Kogi State, Edward Onoja, has won his polling unit.

Onoja, who was the former Chief of Staff to Governor Yahaya Bello, voted on Saturday at unit 13, ward 3, Omonyoku-Odidoko, Ogugu-Olamaboro Local Government Area of Kogi.

PU13, Ward 3 Omonyoku-Odidoko, Ogugu LGA

APC: 934

PDP: 0

Naija News reports that Yahaya Bello also defeated Natasha Akpoti, the candidate of the Social Democratic Party (SDP) at her polling unit by two votes.
