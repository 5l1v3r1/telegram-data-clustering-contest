Kogi: Gov. Yahaya Bello Massively Wins Polling Unit

16 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Governor Yahaya Bello delivered his polling unit totally for the All Progressives Congress in the Kogi State governorship election which held on Saturday.

According to results obtained by Naija News, Bello cleared all the votes in his polling unit, defeating all other candidates.

See the figures below from polling unit 11, Okene-Eba/Agasa/Ahache Ward, Okene Local Government Area of the state.

Result of first count

APC: 546

SDP: 0

PDP: 0

Invalid vote: 1

Second count

APC: 716

SDP: 0

PDP: 0

It should, however, be noted that only INEC has the constitutional powers to declare the final results.
