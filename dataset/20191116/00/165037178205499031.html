De Jong looks for the ticket to the Euro's

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Netherlands are very close to sealing their ticket to the Euro 2020 and De Jong wants to ensure that they get it this Saturday night.

There are Barcelona players who have already qualified for the Euro 2020, as is the case for the Spaniard, Busquets, who continues to be a very important player in the Spanish national team.

This Saturday could see another Barcelona player qualify, De Jong. The player will once again lead the midfield in the difficult game against Northern Ireland.

Holland only need a draw to qualify for the Euro's, but a win would take them to first place in their group, after four wins in a row. 

Germany seem like they are not going to fail, so it is up to the Dutch to look for this victory to round off the performance in the standings and confirm that the new squad of young players has a lot to say.
