Edward Onoja Votes In #KogiDecides, Reveals Why Yahaya Bello Will Be Re-elected

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The Deputy Governorship candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Edward Onoja, has voted in the Kogi State Guber election.

Naija News reports that Onoja, who was the former Chief of Staff to Governor Yahaya Bello, voted on Saturday at unit 13, ward 3, Omonyoku-Odidoko, Ogugu-Olamaboro Local Government Area of Kogi.

Speaking to newsmen after voting, Edward Onoja said voters were eager to return Yahaya Bello to office.

He said residents want Yahaya Bello to return as Governor because they want him to consolidate on his achievements.

Onoja said: “You can see the enthusiasm of the voters who want to usher in a government that will continue to consolidate on its gains in service delivery.

”God brought a divine leader who in spite of coming from another senatorial district saw the need to meet the infrastructural gap in the Eastern district that has always produced the governor of the state.

”Because of this, our people who are people of gratitude appreciate that gesture and then made up their mind to repay good for good.

”I think today at the end of the day, we will all be happy.”

Naija News had reported that Bello voted at Agaza Okene-Eba/Agassa/Ahache Ward, Unit 11, Okene Local Government.

The governor who is also the candidate All progressive party (APC) was the first key stakeholder to cast his vote.
