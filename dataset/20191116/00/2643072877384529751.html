Bayelsa Guber: Fmr. President Goodluck Jonathan Expresses Disappointment AS He Casts His Vote

16 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Former President Goodluck Jonathan has voted alongside his wife Dame Patience Jonathan, in their hometown, Otueke, Bayelsa State.

He called for the calm of good people of Balyesa and Kogi states respectively as the election continues.

He expressed his disappointment in the late arrival of election materials, adding that as a reult the election in his polling unit commenced late.

He noted that he arrived at his polling unit at about 8:19am.
