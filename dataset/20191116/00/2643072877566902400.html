Live Updates: 2019 Bayelsa State Governorship Election

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

Electorates will today, November 16, 2019, head to polling units to participate in the Bayelsa State Governorship election.

Naija News reports that the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) had fixed today for the Bayelsa and Kogi States Guber polls.

Today’s election will determine who would take over from Governor Seriake Dickson and govern Bayelsa for the next four years.

The governorship election will be keenly contested between David Lyon of the All Progressives Congress (APC) and Senator Douye Diri of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).

The High Court sitting in Yenagoa had dismissed the candidate of the APC, but the Court of Appeal, Port Harcourt, dismissed the ruling and asked the APC to participate in today’s election.

Other candidates include: Accord Party, Ebizimo Diriyai; African Action Congress (AAC), Kalango Stanley Davies; Alliance for Democracy, Owei-Tongu Woniwei; Alliance National Party (ANP), Kunde Noah.
