Bandec Las Tunas Celebrates Anniversary at the Top Nationwide

16 Nov 2019, 00:55 by István Ojeda Bello

The Directorate of the Bank of Credit and Commerce (Bandec) in this province of eastern Cuba celebrated the 22nd anniversary of the financial institution with the satisfaction of occupying the first place among its counterparts in the rest of the country.

Las Tunas, Cuba.- During this year, this bank continues to participate in the main working directions of the entity: the expansion of e-payment channels, e-commerce, subsidies for housing construction and financial services to companies and agricultural producers of the territory; while maintaining all the efficiency indicators in positive figures, said Xiomara Leyva Pérez, its director.

Entities such as Acinox Stainless Steel and the Metal Structures Factory, as well as sugarcane growers,  are among the most common clients of the bank. Something that distinguishes them among their peers in other regions of the Archipelago is the stability in their workgroup and the efficiency in services, the directive added.

During the celebration here of the 22nd anniversary, the workers who have remained for five, 10, 15 and more than 30 years of uninterrupted work in the banking sector were awarded, as well as the branch 6461, the most prominent among the 11 that Bandec has in Las Tunas.
