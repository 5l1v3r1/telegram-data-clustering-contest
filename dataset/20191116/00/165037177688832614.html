The historical fact in Spain's victory over Malta

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The win against Malta was not expected to be such a trivial result. The 7-0 win was a milestone for Spain. It is the first time the Spanish national team have a won a game with seven different goalscorers.

Nobosy scored twice in the thrashing over Malta. From the 13 players that Robert Moreno used in the game, seven of them scored a goal. This is to say, more than half of the players with real possibilities of scoring (we know that goalkeepers can also score, but this is less likely) did so.

It started with Morata, then Cazorla, Pau, Sarabia, Dani Olmo and Gerard Moreno and then finished with Jesus Navas. Seven goals from seven different goalscorers. An unprecedented event in the national team's football.

A fact that did not go unnoticed by Alexis Tamayi, who did not take long to point out the historic event that had just occurred on the pitch at the Ramon de Carranza stadium on Friday night.
