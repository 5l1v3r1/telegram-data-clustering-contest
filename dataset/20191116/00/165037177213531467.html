This was the tribute to Sergio Ramos

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

When the players of the Spanish national team came on to the pitch to face Malta, the stands at the Ramon de Carranza stadium welcomed Sergio Ramos with a tribute. The photograph above was taken previously and the Federation revealed the 'making of' it.

He is loved by many and also hated by some, but what is unquestionable is that he is vital for the Spanish national team, as well as Real Madrid, where he has spent the most part of his career and where he has reaped almost all of his triumphs.

In the last game that Robert Moreno's team had to play, a tribute was organized to his past and present. The stands received him with a big roar of applause and a big photo of him, along with a mosaic. In this one the defender could be seen with several of his European titles.

When the centre-back left the pitch after being substituted, a clear tactic from his coach so that he could receive a standing ovation, the fans got up to thank him for his good work. The Sevillian has his home very close to Cadiz and has already boasted a lot about Andalucia.

But all great things are made with time and effort and the Federation wanted to bring to light how the image, that went around the stadium, was actually made. He had to stand in front of the photographer and do several different poses.
