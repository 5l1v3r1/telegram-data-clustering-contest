Egyptian football team fly to Comoros for 2nd leg of AFCON qualifiers

16 Nov 2019 by MENA

CAIRO, Nov 15 (MENA) - The Egyptian national football team left here on Friday, heading to Comoros for the second round of the Cameroon 2021 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) qualifiers, slated for Monday.

On Thursday, the Pharaohs were held to a 1-1 draw against Kenya in their first match in the group.
