Here Is A Triumph List And Guide For Destiny 2’s Harbinger Seal Title

16 Nov 2019 by Paul Tassi

While I am just a few days away from getting the Undying title, you technically can already get Harbinger, now that the dungeon has arrived, as everything you need from the moon is present and accounted for. The problem? When you Google what the Harbinger triumphs are, most of the top results are ads for paid “Seal carriers” where someone will do it for you for the low, low price of hundreds of dollars, which seems wild.In an effort to knock those down the Google front page, I am going to write my own Harbinger Title triumph list and guide here real quick, as hopefully this will help you get the Seal the legit way instead of paying some rando and/or scam site to do it for you.

1. Lunar Rover (Badge)

So, this one is sort of complicated because it actually contains some of the other triumphs on this list, as the items required for the “Moon Gear badge” are rewards for many of them.

You need the Moonrider One sparrow from doing all of Eris’s lost fireteam quests. You need the Pulse Rifle which only drops from the dungeon. The Bane of Tyrants ship is a random dungeon drop with a very low chance. I got it my first try, don’t be mad. The other random ones we will get to over the course of this guide.

2. Nightmare Hunt Time Trial Master

This is probably one of the hardest triumphs on the list. You need to beat every Nightmare Hunt on Master in a set amount of time, except the game doesn’t tell you how much time. Enter my friend Niris, master of infographics, who has all the times listed below which he’s gathered after community testing:

This will get you the A Sibyl’s Dreams emblem that you need for the Lunar Rover badge. This is going to take a lot of trial and error, and beware the boss phases and their repeated immunity that makes them very hard to burst down in most instances.

3. Flawless Master Hunter Nightmare

All you have to do is beat one Master Nightmare Hunt without dying here, which is not too bad. Pick your favorite and go for it. This gets you the Lunar Halcyon Gilt shader you need for the collection badge.

4. Symphony of Death

You need to complete the Deathbringer quest for the exotic launcher that you get after the main moon questline wraps. Here’s a guide from Rick Kackis walking you through the entire thing:

5. Altar’s of Sorrow: Sorrow’s Bane

This gets you the ship The Third Tale for collections which is given for a “perfect” Altars of Sorrow run where no Nightmares get sacrificed, aka they do not make it to the glowing green thing from the other glowing green thing. Hard, but doable. You want a good team.

6. Eternal Heretic

You have to do a flawless run of the new dungeon to get this one. Not solo, but flawless, no deaths. I hear transition glitch deaths do not count against this.
