Kogi Guber: INEC Distributes 2015 Ballot Papers, Not 2019

16 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

As voting continues in Kogi and Bayelsa States, Naija News has learnt that some of the ballot papers distributed by the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) to be used for voting by the voters in Kogi State, were that of 2015, not 2019.

This is as carried by SituationRoomng on its Twitter page this morning.
