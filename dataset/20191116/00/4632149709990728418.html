Fake News Buster: India condemns fake letter from PM Modi to Gogoi

16 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
A letter supposedly written by Prime Minister Narendra Modi to Chief Justice of India Ranjan Gogoi, thanking him for the Supreme Court’s verdict about the Ram Janmabhoomi-Babri Masjid title case, has been doing the rounds on social media.

SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
The Indian government on Wednesday denounced the letter as fake and condemned Bangladesh’s local media for circulating it.
