Racing's Howard Abrahams in hospital after collapsing at the track

16 Nov 2019

KINGSTON, Jamaica – One of the voices of local racing for over 40 years, Howard Abrahams is now in hospital after collapsing at the race track today (November 16).

The reason (s) why Abrahams was rushed from the race track to the hospital are still unknown despite checks by this medium.
