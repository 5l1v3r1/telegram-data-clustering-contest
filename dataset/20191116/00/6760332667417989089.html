Does TDP have a plan against RGV? - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

16 Nov 2019

'Kamma Rajyamlo Kadapa Reddlu' is gearing up for a release on November 29.  So far, the TDP is not making much noise against the movie that allegedly is against Chandrababu Naidu and Nara Lokesh.  They haven't reacted to the 'Pappu lanti abbayi' song that portrays a Lokesh look-alike as an inept joker.  

As per film activist Mahesh Kathi, who has played the role of a CBI officer in the film, the TDP is not going all guns blazing against 'KRKR' as it thinks that its opposition will only give "free publicity" for the movie.  

But, if some folks are to be believed, the party may be having a plan against the film.  The TDP is biding its time and is waiting for the political fiction to hit the screens before reacting to its content.  If there is something offensive, devious and even ill-intentioned, legal action can be initiated against the makers and they can get the film banned through judicial intervention.  That way, the party will have the support of sections of civil society as well, and also achieve the aim of successfully screwing up RGV and his producers.
