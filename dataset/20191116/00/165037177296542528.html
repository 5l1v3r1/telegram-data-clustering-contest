Barcelona, Atletico and PSG now know Reinier's price

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Reinier has got onto the agendas of various European sides. Barca, Atletico and PSG have asked Flamengo about him. The club have not taken long to reply: if they want him, they must pay at least 35 million euros.

Everyone wants Reinier, but only one team can have him. Either that or he will stay at Flamengo... because the young promise recently signed for the Brazilian side. 

In the meantime, Barca, Atletico Madrid and PSG are following him closely. According to 'Mundo Deportivo', Barcelona have got in contact with Flamengo to ask about Reinier.

Flamengo have now put a price tag on the player amid the interest from clubs all over Europe. Reinier costs 35 million euros. They are not thinking of letting him leave for less than that. 

At the Brazilian outfit, Reinier is considered to be the 'other' Vinicius. The striker, now at Real Madrid, moved to 'Los Blancos' for 45 million euros. 

Now that Flamengo have another gem, they want to cash in on his sale. The latest reports suggest PSG could offer up to 60 million euros for the Brazil youngster.
