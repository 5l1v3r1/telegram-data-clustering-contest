Hickman man sentenced to prison for sex abuse of girl

16 Nov 2019

LINCOLN, Neb. (AP) - A 42-year-old Hickman man has been sentenced to up to 25 years in prison for sexually abusing a young girl over a four-year period.

The Lincoln Journal Star reports that Jeremy Marteney was sentenced Wednesday in Lancaster County District Court to 8- to 25-years. He was convicted following a bench trial of two counts of first-degree sexual assault, child abuse and another sex crime.

Prosecutors alleged Marteney had abused the girl from the time she was 12 until she was 16.
