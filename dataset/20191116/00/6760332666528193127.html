The price of Priyanka Chopra's new home will shock you! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

16 Nov 2019

Bollywood actress Priyanka Chopra had last year got married to singer and Hollywood actor Nick Jonas, and the couple have been one of the most talked about star couples  of the world. Priyanka Chopra had recently acted in the movie The Sky Is Pink.

The latest update on the couple is that,  Priyanka Chopra  and Nick Jonas have shelled about around $20 million which is  Rs 144 crores Indian rupees to buy a  20,000-square-foot property in Los Angeles.As per the reports, their new home will have  seven bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, high ceilings and a vast outdoor space. 

Previously, Nick's brother Joe Jonas and his wife Sophie Turner had spent $14.1 million to buy a 15,000-square-foot house with 10 bedrooms and 14 bathrooms.Nick Jonas who is occupied  with his musical assignments, will be seen next in the Jumanji sequel and Priyanka Chopra is now shooting for The White Tiger.
