Gov Fayemi Attends Fayose’s Son Wedding In Lagos (Photos)

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

Governor Kayode Fayemi of Ekiti State on Saturday attended the wedding of Tomiwa, one of the sons of Ayodele Fayose, his predecessor.

Naija News reports that the event, which had many dignitaries in attendance, held at Haven Event Center in Ikeja.

Fayemi and Fayose had made the headlines over their political differences but they set aside that on Saturday to celebrate together.
