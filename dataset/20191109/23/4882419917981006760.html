Eibar 0-4 Real Madrid: Benzema and Hazard shine in Basque Country

After this win, Zinedine Zidane's squad has 25 points from 12 games

09 Nov 2019, 23:04 by Sport EN

Real Madrid romped to victory over Eibar in La Liga on Saturday, sitting joint top of the table with Barca on 25 points.

Karim Benzema struck twice, Sergio Ramos bagged a penalty and Fede Valverde hit the fourth in a comfortable victory.

Benzema sent Madrid ahead with a low finish after the ball broke to him on the left. He was combining well with Eden Hazard, who won a penalty, dispatched by Sergio Ramos.

Then Lucas Vazquez earned a second penalty which Benzema dispatched to become the top scorer in La Liga on nine.

In the second half Valverde fired Madrid further ahead with a low drive from Modric's pass. A comfortable victory.
