Torrential rain forecast brings UK further weather warnings

09 Nov 2019, 17:16 by Nadeem Badshah

Met Office issues yellow warning for showers across southern England

Parts of the UK are bracing for further torrential rain on Saturday evening following flooding in northern England.

The Met Office issued a yellow warning until 11.59pm for showers in Brighton, London, Oxford and Portsmouth, expected to cause disruption on bus and train services.

There is also a yellow warning for Belfast and Newry until 8pm for heavy downpours and the risk of homes and businesses being damaged by flooding.
