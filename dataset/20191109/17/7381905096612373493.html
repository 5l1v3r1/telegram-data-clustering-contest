IPL: Shahr Khodro moves up to second, Machine Sazi defeats Zob Ahan [VIDEO]

09 Nov 2019, 17:09 by Mir Farhad Ali Khan

Tasnim – TEHRAN, Shahr Khodro football team edged past Pars Jonoobi and moved up to the Iran Professional League (IPL) second place.

Hossein Mehrban scored the only goal of the match in the 87th minute.

In Tabriz, Machine Sazi rallied back from a late 1-0 deficit to take down Zob Ahan 2-1.

In Masjed Soleyman, Naft was held to a goalless draw against Nassaji.

Sepahan sits top of the table with 22 points, followed by Shahr Khodro (20) and Persepolis (19).
