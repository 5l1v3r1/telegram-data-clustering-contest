Logan Paul trainer Shannon Briggs could be BANNED from corner for KSI fight after bust-up with Viddal Riley at weight-in

09 Nov 2019, 22:39 by https://www.facebook.com/BoxingSunSport/

LOGAN PAUL'S trainer Shannon Briggs could be BANNED from his corner for the KSI fight tonight after a sensational bust-up at their weight-in.

The ex-heavyweight star clashed with his YouTube's rivals coach Viddal Riley in a heated confrontation in Los Angles, US.

According to reports, KSI's team are trying to get the California Commission to stop Briggs from being with Paul for their bout at the Staples Center.
