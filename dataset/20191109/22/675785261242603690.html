Coronation Street's Bernie confronts paedo ex Kel, as abuse storyline heats up

The latest scenes in the gripping storyline see Bernie try to take a photo of Kel on her phone for evidence, after he sexually abused Bernie's son Paul

09 Nov 2019, 22:50 by Karen Rockett

It all kicks off as Bernie confronts her ex-partner, paedophile Kel, in Coronation Street next week.

Kel, played by Joe Alesso, is caught stalking woodland and struggles with her as she tries to take his picture on her phone for evidence.

Son Paul (Peter Ash), sexually abused by him, had been hiding with her in bushes and helped her pounce. And his twin Gemma (Dolly-Rose Campbell) turned up too as things got heated.

Bernie (Jane Hazlegrove) is shocked to learn that a plastic bag Kel was carrying contained booze and condoms.

Appearing on Lorraine, actor Joe Alesso admitted that he had "qualms" about playing the character.

The dark sex abuse storyline has seen Paul Foreman (Peter Ash) come face to face with his childhood groomer Kel, who is back dating his mum Bernice (Jane Hazelgrove).

Paul was only 14 when Kel sexually abused the teenager and manipulated him into thinking that they were in love.

Speaking to Lorraine Kelly about the storyline, he said: "I have no idea [how long I have left], it is open-ended.

"The character I’m playing is finite, I’ll never be a regular on Corrie that’s sure. My role will come to an end."

Asked whether he had any qualms about playing the character, he said: "Yes of course. Talk about being thrown into the deep end. It's not just another acting job."

Revealing he felt a "lot of pressure" to get it right, he said he researched the subject matter and found that 12,000 men were raped last year.

He also said that it was important to not make Kel a 'caricature': "A lot of these people seem to be normal people; they’re not monsters – they’re not the child catcher from Chitty Chitty Bang Bang."

Coronation Street airs Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays at 7:30pm and 8:30pm on ITV.
