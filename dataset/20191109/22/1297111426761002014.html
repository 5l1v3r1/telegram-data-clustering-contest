LeBoldus Golden Suns win provincial 6A football title

The LeBoldus Golden Suns defeated the Saskatoon Holy Cross Crusaders 28-15 on Saturday in the SHSAA's 6A football final at Mosaic Stadium.

09 Nov 2019, 22:59 by Regina Leader-Post

The LeBoldus Golden Suns defeated the Saskatoon Holy Cross Crusaders 28-15 on Saturday at Mosaic Stadium to win the Saskatchewan High Schools Athletic Association’s 6A football title.

LeBoldus capped an undefeated season by capturing its first SHSAA championship since 2016, when the Golden Suns won for the fifth year in a row.

Golden Suns tailback Ryker Frank rushed 25 times for 138 yards. He also caught six passes for 102 yards, including a 19-yard touchdown.

LeBoldus quarterback Christian Fischer completed 16 of 26 passes for 262 yards and two touchdowns — the aforementioned toss to Frank, plus a 20-yarder to Ethan Marshall. Fischer also rushed for a one-yard major.

Kaeden Brennan added two field goals for LeBoldus, which got 5 1/2 tackles, one pass deflection and one blocked kick from Joe Camplin.

• The Yorkton Regional Raiders won the SHSAA’s 5A title, defeating the visiting Saskatoon Bishop Mahoney Saints 31-23 on Saturday.

In the provincial 4A nine-man final, the Melfort Comets got 24 points from Mason Gordon-Danku in a 55-15 victory over the visiting Martensville Royals.
