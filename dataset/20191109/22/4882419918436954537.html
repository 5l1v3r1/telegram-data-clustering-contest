Right-back problems? Sergi Roberto suspended against Leganes

Sergi Roberto is banned after picking up his fifth booking of the season

09 Nov 2019, 22:26 by SPORT.es

Sergi Roberto collected his fifth yellow card of the season on Saturday when he was booked in Barcelona's 4-1 win over Celta Vigo at Camp Nou.

The right-back was booked in the 52nd minute of the match after a foul on Junca and will be banned for the game at Butarque against Leganes after the international break.
