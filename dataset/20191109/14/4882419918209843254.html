Depleted Real Valladolid stunned by Alavés 3-0 at Estadio de Mendizorroza

After this result, Asier Garitano's squad has 15 points from 13 games

09 Nov 2019, 14:14 by SPORT.es

Alavés ease to comfortable win over Valladolid 3-0 on Saturday at Estadio de Mendizorroza. Alavés faced the match with a desire to make up their league after losing against Osasuna 4-2 while Real Valladolid came from winning 3-0 against Mallorca in the last match played. As of today, Alavés is 15th in the LaLiga table with 15 points from 13 matches, while Real Valladolid sits 13th with 17 points after 13 matches.

Good start of the game for Alavés, with a goal from Joselu in the 26th minute. Later, Alavés scored once more and increased their lead 2-0 thanks to a goal from Tomás Pina 32 minutes in the game, thereby seeing the first half to a 2-0.

Alavés boosted the game in the second half, thanks to a goal from Lucas Pérez 75 minutes in the game, which saw the second half to a 3-0.

At the end of the match, both teams made 3 substitutions each. For Alavés Oliver Burke, Pere Pons and Burgui came on for Luis Rioja, Joselu and Tomás Pina, while Valladolid brought on Sandro Ramírez, Anuar and Pedro Porro to replace Sergi Guardiola, Toni Villa and Javi Moyano.

The referee booked Víctor Laguardia, Manu García and Luis Rioja from Alavés, while for Real Valladolid Federico Barba, Rubén Alcaraz and Pedro Porro saw a yellow card as well.

Alavés with their next fixture against Eibar at away stadium, while Real Valladolid will face Sevilla at home stadium.
