'Dostana 2': Kartik Aaryan shares a picture with clapboard from the sets as he kick-starts shooting for the film

09 Nov 2019, 14:19 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan, Janhvi Kapoor and Lakshya starrer 'Dostana 2' has been in the limelight since its inception. Directed by Collin D'Cunha the film is the sequel to Priyanka Chopra, Abhishek Bachchan and John Abraham's 'Dostana'. The cast was announced a while back and on Diwali, the entire cast of the film got clicked together at Karan Johar's office.

Yesterday the cast of the film along with the man of the ship Collin jetted off to Amritsar for the first schedule of the film. Today, Kartik took to his Instagram to share a BTS picture from the sets. In the picture, the actor can be seen hiding his face behind the clapboard.

Kartik shared the pic and captioned it as, " #Dostana2 Begins !!! 👫🚶🏻‍♂️🌈 @karanjohar @janhvikapoor @itslakshya @collindcunha ❤️"

Before flying out of Mumbai, Kartik made sure to stop by at producer Karan Johar’s residence to take his blessings and even shared a comical post about it on his Instagram handle. “Maa da laadla #Chandigarh nikal gaya #Dostana2!! The Dharma rivaaz that all actors need to follow before starting a Dharma film @karanjohar,” he captioned the photo.

Meanwhile, reportedly, 'Dostana 2' will see Janhvi and Kartik as siblings who fall for the same guy played by Lakshya. The film is slated to release in 2020.
