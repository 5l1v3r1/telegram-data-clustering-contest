Wagon rams river’s wall

09 Nov 2019, 11:15 by Edd Reyes

A 31-year-old man was killed when the Mitsubishi Adventure wagon he was driving crashed into the concrete wall of Tullahan river Friday evening along North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) in Valenzuela City.

Aris Gascon of VMN Compound, Bgy. Potrero, Malabon City died on the spot from severe head and body injuries when he was pinned and dragged by his vehicle at the southbound lane of Km. 12 NLEX within the vicinity of Bgy. Gen. T. De Leon.

NLEX investigator Noel Joseph Dela Cruz said the victim was driving his wagon (ZAM-665) when he accidentally hit the wall of Tullahan River Bridge at around 8:40 p.m.
