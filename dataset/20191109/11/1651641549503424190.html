RUINER and Nuclear Throne are currently free on the Epic Games Store

09 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Ash

This week’s free offerings on the Epic Games Store consist of brutal action shooter RUINER and post-apocalyptic roguelike Nuclear Throne.

All you need to claim the two games is an Epic Games account and the Epic Games launcher, both of which are also free. You also don’t need to give Epic any of your payment information, in case you’re worried about that.
