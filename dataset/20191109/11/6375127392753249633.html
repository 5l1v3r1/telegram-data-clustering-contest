Girl, 12, survives croc attack

09 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Zaida Delos Reyes

A 12-YEAR-OLD girl on Friday night survived a crocodile attack in Balabac, Palawan.

Police Regional Office - MIMAROPA spokesman Lt. Col. Aries FaltadoÂ  identified the victim as Haina Lisa Jose Habi, a student and a resident of Bgy. Catagupan, Balabac town.

The attack happened when the girl was on her way home, with her brother Hashim, at 6:30 p.m. on Friday.

The siblings were crossing a creek in Bgy. Poblacion VI when Haina was snatched by the crocodile.

Hashim immediately jumped to the water and huggled and forcefully pulled Haina until the girl escaped from the crocodile bite.

The victim sustained a wound in the leg. She was brought to the Balabac Rural Health Unit for treatment.
