'Get me up top' - Tyrone Mings shows off his expert finishing in Aston Villa training

Aston Villa latest | The England defender posted a training video on social media showing him taking part in shooting practice with the club's strikers at Villa's Bodymoor Heath Training Centre.

09 Nov 2019, 11:02 by Tom Leach, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/tom-leach/

We've all seen managers throw the biggest defender they have into the thick of attack in the dying moments of a game - but Tyrone Mings ' talent in front of goal may well be enough to earn him the number nine shirt for Dean Smith 's side next season from the get go.

The England defender posted a training video on social media showing him taking part in shooting practice with the club's strikers at Aston Villa 's Bodymoor Heath Training Centre and his expert finishing has not gone unnoticed.

And he has even asked his manager to throw him up front with Brazilian teammate Wesley in what would make one of the Premier League 's most powerful strike partnerships.

 

Mings has only scored twice in a Villa shirt, but you would not have been able to tell him apart from some of Smith's most talented strikers were you at Bodymoor Heath on Friday.

The video was captured as Villa continued their preparations ahead of their trip to face Wolves at Molineux on Sunday.

Victory for Villa could see them extend their buffer to the bottom three to six points, results dependant, and could even climb as high as 10th - leapfrogging Manchester United.
