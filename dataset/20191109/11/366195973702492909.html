Rockie Fresh has returned with visuals for his new single "Round Here." The Rostrum Records artist and his crew stomp around his city of Chicago with purpose in the music video. Produced by Mathius Young, "Round Here" is a bouncy banger that walks the line between radio single and album cut. Rockie employs a plucky autotune filter to croon over the instrumental. As the name of the single implies, Rockie reps his city and his accomplishments hard on "Round Here."
