'Panipat': Arjun Kapoor introduces Zeenat Aman aka Sakina Begum's royal avatar

09 Nov 2019, 11:28 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ashutosh Gowarikar directorial historical film 'Panipat' starring Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon released its much-awaited trailer a few days back and it is receiving rave reviews from the audiences. Recently, the makers unveiled the first look posters of the leading star cast and they are nothing but menacing.

After unveiling Arjun Kapoor as Sadashivrao, Sanjay Dutt as Ahmed Shah Abdali, Kriti Sanon as Parvati bai, Padmini Kolhapure's look as Gopika Bai and Mohnish Behl's look as Nana Saheb Peshwa, today, Arjun Kapoor shared yet another poster of Zeenat Aman as Sakina Begum. Padmini looks royal in the poster and her face portrays that she certainly essays a quintessential role in the film.

Arjun took to his Instagram shared the poster and wrote, "Sakina Begum - Friends can be found in the darkest of places. #PanipatLook #Panipat #Dec6"

Helmed by Ashutosh Gowariker and produced by Sunita Gowariker and Rohit Shelatkar, 'Panipat' will hit the screens on December 6 this year. Veteran actress Padmini Kolhapure will also play a pivotal role in the film.
