Unai Emery slaughtered by Arsenal fans as Kieran Tierney snub in Leicester defeat leads to sacking calls

The former Celtic star was dropped to the bench and it's fair to say Arsenal fans aren't happy.

09 Nov 2019, 19:23 by Mark McDougall, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/mark-mcdougall/

Arsenal fans have reiterated their calls for Unai Emery to be sacked as manager after defeat to Leicester.

The Gunners were beaten 2-0 at the King Power after goals from Jamie Vardy and James Maddison gave Brendan Rodgers side a deserved win.

Emery was already under pressure but the decision to leave Kieran Tierney and Nicholas Pepe on the bench angered the Arsenal support.

The resulting defeat left them even more annoyed and fans thought the decision to switch to 3-5-2 would have been the perfect time to play Tierney ahead of Kolasinac.

And fans were quick to take to Twitter to call for the Spaniard to be axed as Tierney was left on the bench for the full 90 against his old side.
