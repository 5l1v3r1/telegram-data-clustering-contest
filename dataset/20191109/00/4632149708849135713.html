Damaging vehicles land seven in jail

09 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

They wanted to strike fear among residents of Vijayanagar

Seven people have been arrested for reportedly damaging 21 vehicles, including 17 cars that were parked on roads and outside the houses of residents in Vijayanagar area.

The police managed to arrest the accused based on CCTV footage.

The incident occured on Wednesday night at Sampige Layout in Pattegarapalya.

The accused used stones to break the windshields of the cars and lethal weapons to damage them.

The arrested have been identified as Yeshwanth alias Pengga, 20, Darshan alias Ashray, 19, Nithin alias Bonda, 18, Balaji, 45, all residents of Panchasheelanagar, Kiran Reddy alias Kiran, 26, Muthu, 22, residents of Kanakanagar and Charan Raj, 20, a resident of Nagarbhavi.

During questioning, the accused said that they wanted to rob passersby and wanted to establish their supremacy in the area.

Efforts are on to arrest the remaining accused.

The police had initially picked up Yeshwanth, Darshan, Nithin and based on their interrogation the remaining accused were arrested.
