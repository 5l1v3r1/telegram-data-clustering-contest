How to Watch Bellator 233

09 Nov 2019 by Cole Shelton

Dazn is the exclusive streaming partner of Bellator MMA. You can sign up here and live stream 100+ fight nights a year.

Bellator MMA on Friday returns to the WinStar World Casino and Resort in Thackerville, Oklahoma, for Bellator 233.

The main event will see a middleweight contest between John Salter and Gegard Mousasi protégé Costello van Steenis. The co-main event, meanwhile, will pit former Strikeforce champion Muhammed Lawal against Andrew Kapel in a 195-pound catchweight bout.

Bellator 233’s prelims start at 6:15 p.m. ET and will be broadcast on Bellator.com, the Bellator mobile app as well as Dazn. The main card begins at 9:00 p.m. ET and can be watched on Dazn as well as Paramount Network.

Bellator 233 Main Card:

185: John Salter vs. Costello van Steenis
195: Muhammed Lawal vs. Andrew Kapel
145: Leslie Smith vs. Arlene Blencowe
265: Tyrell Fortune vs. Azunna Anyanwu
