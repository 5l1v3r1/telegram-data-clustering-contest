Kylie Jenner kept 'fling' with Drake secret as she didn't want family to find out

Reality TV star Kylie Jenner reportedly had a brief romance with Drake but was determined to keep it from her famous family because of his beef with Kanye West

09 Nov 2019, 07:58 by Louise Randell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-randell/

Kylie Jenner kept her rumoured fling with Drake secret because she reportedly didn't want family to find out.

The reality TV star, 22, is said to have romanced the music star earlier this year, despite both of them denying they were ever an item.

However, a new report suggests they got involved months ago and decided to keep it under the radar because of Drake's previous feud with her brother-in-law Kanye West .

The Sun reports Kylie "wanted to keep the news from her sister Kim Kardashian " who is married to West.

The publication goes on to state that Kylie wanted a more serious relationship, but Drake is more of a "free spirit" and didn't want to be tied down.

However, there's no suggestion that there was any overlap with Kylie's ex-boyfriend Travis Scott, the father of her daughter Stormi, who she split from this year.

Rumours of the couple first ignited when Kylie recently attended the pop icon's 33rd birthday bash in Hollywood and it was reported that the pair "had an attraction".

Insiders claimed the pair regularly see each other and it sparked speculation that they are dating.
