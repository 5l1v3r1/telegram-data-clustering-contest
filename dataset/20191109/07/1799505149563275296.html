Street Performance

09 Nov 2019, 07:40 by BRIEF

LAS VEGAS – A federal court in Nevada granted a disabled man a temporary restraining order to keep the Las Vegas police from enforcing against him a city code that prohibits him from using a table during his street performance, in which he draws pictures using his mouth.
