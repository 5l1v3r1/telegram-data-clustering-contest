Robbie Savage has made this Aston Villa v Wolves prediction

Aston Villa v Wolves latest | The former Birmingham City star was asked to make his prediction ahead of this weekend's West Midlands derby.

09 Nov 2019, 07:58 by Tom Leach, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/tom-leach/

Former Leicester City and Derby County midfielder Robbie Savage has been casting his eye over this weekend's Premier League fixtures and has made this Aston Villa v Wolves prediction.

The two sides lock horns in the Premier League for the first time this season on Sunday afternoon as Wolves play host.

There is just two points currently separating Nuno Espirito Santo's side and Dean Smith's Villa and the winner will drastically boost their survival hopes with a the points haul.

 

Villa could climb up to back above Manchester United with the win, should results go their way, advancing to 10th in the standings.

While Wolves, after their slow start to the league campaign, could find themselves sixth.

But Savage has predicted a share of the spoils at Molineux with a 1-1 draw

Here are Sav's predictions in full...

 

Norwich 1-2 Watford

Chelsea 3-1 Palace

Burnley 2-1 West Ham

Newcastle 1-0 Bournemouth

Southampton 1-1 Everton

Tottenham 2-2 Sheffield United

Leicester 2-1 Arsenal

Man Utd 2-0 Brighton

Wolves 1-1 Aston Villa

Liverpool 2-1 Man City
