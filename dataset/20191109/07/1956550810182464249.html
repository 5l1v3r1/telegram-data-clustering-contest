Dubai’s Azure Beach launches outdoor cinema

Watch blockbusters on the beach this winter

09 Nov 2019, 07:11 by Reema Rahman

Looking for something alternative to do in Dubai this week?

How does catching a multiple award-winning film on the beach sound to you?

Azure Beach in Jumeirah Beach Residence has launched a new seaside cinema night, otherwise known as "Sea-nema".

There’s a special menu of snacks including pizzas, hot dogs, loaded fries and popcorn – because it’s not really a trip to the cinema without popcorn, right?

The sea-nema nights will take place every Wednesday at 9.30pm with tickets coming in at Dhs50.

Catch blockbuster hits like The Fate of the Furious and Suicide Squad this November, while the December line-up will feature Logan, Avengers: Endgame, A Star Is Born and a special screening of The Grinch for Christmas.

Head down early to the new open-air cinema at the beach club and grab yourself a beanbag or a cosy sofa – perfect for couples.
