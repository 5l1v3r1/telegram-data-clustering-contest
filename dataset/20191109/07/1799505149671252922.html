Road Rage Tragedy

09 Nov 2019, 07:59 by BRIEF

LITTLE ROCK, Ark. – A man was properly convicted of murder and committing a terroristic act for a road range incident in which he shot into another car, killing a 3-year-old boy, an Arkansas appeals court ruled.
