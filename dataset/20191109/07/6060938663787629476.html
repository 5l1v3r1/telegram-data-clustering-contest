This bride's shimmery sari will be the hottest trend this wedding season!

09 Nov 2019, 07:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Who says saris have to look traditional? The new-age saris are modern, stylish and just so trendy. From pre-draped versions to tropical prints, the humble sari has a got a stylish makeover. From everyday wear to parties, it can be worn on any occasion! That was the case for bride Silpa Goriparthi, who wore a stunning sari for her wedding in California.

The bride picked a shimmery sari from Sabyasachi. The rose gold sari featured a heavily sequined pallu. She paired it with a full-sleeved blouse, which was heavily embellished. The pleats of the sari featured delicate floral embroidery.

Her look was accessorised with a golden choker, jhumka earrings, a nose ring and matha-patti. For her make-up, she sported a soft glam look with kohled eyes, highlighted cheeks and soft pink lips. And, when it came to her hairdo, she sported a half-tied look with soft curls.
