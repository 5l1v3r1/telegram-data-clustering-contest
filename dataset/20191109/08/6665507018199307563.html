Uma Bharti Credits LK Advani For Ayodhya Verdict, Compares Him To The Himalayas

"We are here because of him," Uma Bharti said, reacting to the Supreme Court's verdict on the Ram Janmabhoomi-Babri Masjid title case.

09 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Rohini Chatterji

BJP leader Uma Bharti reacted to the Supreme Court’s Ayodhya verdict on the Ram Janmabhoomi-Babri Masjid title case saying, “we are here today because of Advaniji.” 

Bharti made the remarks when she went to visit BJP veteran LK Advani at his residence in New Delhi.

“It was Advaniji who brought the debate of pseudo secularism vs nationalism to the table of Indian politics. The Ayodhya movement came out of that debate,” she tweeted.

Bharti also praised Prime Minister Narendra Modi and said he was taking India to new heights. She compared Advani to the Himalayas and gave him all the credit for the verdict today.
