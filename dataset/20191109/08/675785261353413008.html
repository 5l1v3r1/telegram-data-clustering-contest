What UK time is KSI vs Logan Paul? Fight start time, TV channel, YouTube and live stream

The rivalry between KSI and Logan Paul will finally be settled as they fight for the second time - and this time it is in Los Angeles

09 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Aaron Flanagan, Martin Domin, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

KSI and Logan Paul will finally settle their rivalry tonight when they clash in their eagerly-awaited rematch.

The YouTube stars fought to a controversial draw in Manchester last August before finally coming to terms for the sequel earlier this year.

The rivals have turned professional for the second fight and will dispense with the head guards and pull on 10oz gloves at the Staples Center in Los Angeles.

KSI - real name Olajide William Olatunji - has made the trip from London for the fight but will start as the underdog against his American foe.

Unlike their first fight, the undercard will not feature any other social media stars but will showcase two world champions in super-middleweight ruler Billy Joe Saunders and recently anointed lightweight king Devin Haney.

Here is everything you need to know about the fights...

When is the fight?

All of the action will take place through the night of Saturday November 9 and into the early hours of the morning on Sunday November 10.

What UK time is the fight likely to start?

It is predicted that the action will begin between 4am and 5am for those watching in the UK.

It will be approximately 9pm or 10pm local time in Los Angeles, where the fight is taking place.

What TV channel is the fight being shown on?

The KSI and Logan Paul rematch will be shown for those in the UK on Sky Sports Box Office, at a price of Â£9.95 - half the price of their normal boxing events.

If you are in the United States or around the world, the DAZN streaming service will be your port of call. It costs $19.99 per month to subscribe, or $99 for the year.

Can you live stream the fight?

If you purchase the Sky Sports Box Office coverage online, you will be able to log on and stream it on the Sky Sports Box Office website.

Will the fight be shown on YouTube?

On this occasion, the fight will not be shown on YouTube.

A pre-show, Countdown to KSI vs Logan Paul 2, will be streamed on YouTube in the build-up to the fight. It will be shown on the DAZN channel, as well as KSI and Logan Paul's personal channels.

Is there a way to watch the fight for free?

Unfortunately not. This is a completely pay-per-view event and you will have to purchase the action to be able to watch it.
