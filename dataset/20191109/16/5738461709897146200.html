MÉT EIREANN: Heavy rainfall and flooding for Louth

Warning

09 Nov 2019, 16:27 by David Lynch

According to Met Eireann, Louth is to be hit with heavy persistent rainfall today with 20mm possible.

They add that after recent heavy rainfall and with water levels elevated, some flooding may occur.

A rainfall warning is in place from today at 12:00 noon to 6:00pm.
