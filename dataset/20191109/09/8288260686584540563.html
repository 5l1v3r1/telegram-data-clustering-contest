'Pivotal' - The best and worst Premier League scenarios for Aston Villa this weekend

Aston Villa will look to return to winning ways when they make the short trip to face Wolves on Sunday

09 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

After facing the two title front runners, Aston Villa are looking to return to winning ways when they travel to Molineux to face Wolverhampton Wanderers on Sunday.

Dean Smith's men sit on 11 points from their opening 11 games and sit in 16th place ahead of the main batch of the weekend's fixtures.

Ahead of what could prove to be a pivotal weekend at the top and bottom of the Premier League table, data analysts @Possible_tables have outlined where every team could finish up by Monday morning.

After Watford's win on Friday night, Villa sit three points outside of the relegation zone and although the table does show they could drop into the bottom three, they have a 15 better goal difference than Southampton so realistically, can only finish as low as 17th.

 

In order for that to happen, Everton will need to beat Southampton and Dean Smith's men lose out to Wolves.

However, for those of a glass half full persuasion, Villa can make a big leap up the table with a win in the Black Country.

Should results go their way, they could end the weekend in the top half and in 10th position. The team directly above them, Newcastle United face Bournemouth at home, whilst the two teams above the Magpies, Burnley and West Ham square off at Turf Moor.

 

Should that end in a draw and Villa beat Wolves, they will leapfrog their claret and blue rivals.

Finally, they could overtake both Tottenham and Manchester United, should they lose to Sheffield United and Brighton respectively.
