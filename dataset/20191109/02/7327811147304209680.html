2 students stabbed while walking home from Long Island high school

09 Nov 2019, 02:54 by WABC

FREEPORT, Long Island (WABC) -- Two students were stabbed while walking home from their Long Island high school Friday afternoon.

Police say a large disturbance took place after dismissal on Lexington and Park avenues.

The victims, 15 and 18 years old, attend Freeport High School.

They were rushed to the hospital after suffering superficial stab wounds.

The circumstances of the attack are unknown at this time.

So far, there are no arrests.

The investigation is ongoing, and anyone with information is urged to contact police.
