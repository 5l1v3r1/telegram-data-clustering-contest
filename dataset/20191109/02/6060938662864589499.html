Anushka Sharma and Virat Kohli holidays in Bhutan

Indian cricket team captain Virat Kohli recently celebrated his birthday and the skipper and his wife Anushka Sharma jetted off to Bhutan for a much-needed getaway. Pictures from their trek in the beautiful mountains of Bhutan went viral on the internet. The duo also shared pictures and videos of them having a good time together. Virat and Anushka were seen having a gala time mingling and mixing with the locals and even visiting the local markets. Both of them are travel enthusiasts and zeroed in on one of their favourite destinations to ring in Virat's birthday.

Marriage not in the cards for Arjun Kapoor-Malaika Arora soon

Arjun Kapoor and Malaika Arora have been in the news for quite a while now for their relationship. The couple had recently come out about their relationship in open but remained tight-lipped about their plans to tie the knot. However, much to the disappointment of their fans, in a recent interview, Arjun clarified that they don't want to get married and shall invite everyone whenever it happens. The reason behind the same is that his family has big fat weddings and he won't be allowed to get married in a hush-hush wedding.

Kartik Aaryan steals the limelight in 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' trailer

The trailer of the much-anticipated comedy-drama 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' starring Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday, and Bhumi Pednekar was dropped this week. The trailer became an instant hit and the social media couldn't stop piling praises on the lead cast. Kartik will be seen playing the role of Chintu Tyagi, who gets into a difficult spot as he tries to impress his wife Bhumi as well as his crush Ananya. The trailer soon started a meme fest on social media as well and was also criticized for a dialogue delivered by Kartik. The film is slated to hit the screens on 6th December this year.

Alia Bhatt's exotic vacation in LA

Alia Bhatt is currently enjoying some time off the grid as the actress headed to Los Angeles with her friends. The diva had recently completed the shoot of her upcoming film 'Sadak 2' and returned to the bay before heading off to LA. The 26-year old actress has been posting pictures from her exotic holiday on social media and her fans just cannot have enough of her. Alia was also reported to looking out for an international celebrity manager as she aims to take the Hollywood route walked by several B-town queens.

Gauri Khan's viral family picture

Shah Rukh Khan and Gauri Khan have recently completed twenty-eight years of marriage but the couple still manages to give some serious couple goals. Recently, Gauri took to social media to share an adorable family picture. The family photo from a vacation which featured SRK-Gauri alongside their kids Aryan, Suhana and AbRam was all over the internet. SRK too reacted to it with a sweet message saying, "Over years I made a good house...Gauri made a good Home but I really believe we are the best at making some really good kids!"
