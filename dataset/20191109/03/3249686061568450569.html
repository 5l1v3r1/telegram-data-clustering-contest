IZ*ONE's movie release confirmed to be postponed

09 Nov 2019, 03:39 by honeycider

The film 'Eyes On Me: The Movie' that was said to be a collection of concert videos from various IZ*ONE stages has been officially postponed.

On November 8, the distributor said "'Eyes On Me: The Movie' is set to be premiered on November 15, however, we are so sorry to announce that the company has to delayed reluctantly the movie's premiere.”

This news follows the postponement of IZ*ONE's album release and comeback showcase.

Read the distributor's full official statement below:

"This is the distributor of the movie "Eyes On Me: The Movie".

​

"Eyes On Me: The Movie" is a movie depicting the actual situation of IZone's concert, which set to be premiered on November 15 (Friday), however, we are so sorry to announce that the company has to delayed reluctantly the movie's premiere.

​

Once again, we apologize to announce the news of unexpectedly delaying the movie’s premiere to the fans as well as the audience who are looking forward to the release of “Eyes On Me: The Movie“.

​

We sincerely hope to get the audience’s sympathy.

​

Thank you."
