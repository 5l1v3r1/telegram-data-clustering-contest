Sharon Stone Repeats ‘Basic Instinct’ Pose at GQ Awards 2019

Award-winning actress Sharon Stone repeated her famous pose from 1992 ‘Basic Instinct’ film at the GQ Awards 2019 on Thursday.

09 Nov 2019, 05:30

Stone, who received the Woman of the Year Award, took a chair while onstage and had a slow sit before crossing her legs and gently asking the audience to repeat after her. The actress thanked GQ for choosing her for this year’s award and during her acceptance speech inspired the audience with her story.
