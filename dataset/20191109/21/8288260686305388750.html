Wolves 'complete' defender transfer ahead of January window - reports

Wolves transfer news | WWFC have made it a transfer priority to sign talented youngsters from across the continent

09 Nov 2019, 21:02 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Wolverhampton Wanderers have reportedly completed the signing of highly-rated PSV defender Nigel Lonwijk.

According to reports in the Netherlands, Wolves are set to pay a nominal fee of around €200,000 for the 17-year-old.

ED have claimed that he will join Wolves next week after agreeing a fee with the Dutch giants.

Lonwijk is described as ‘a strong and athletic defender’ and has been playing for the club’s U19 side and has also represented the Netherlands at age group level.

The defender has reportedly already told teammates that he has completed his departure and will sign a 'multi-year' contract at Molineux when the window opens in January.

 

Wolves have made it a priority in recent years to sign talent from across the continent to compliment their development squads.

Meritan Shabani and Raphael Nya arrived this summer from Bayern Munich and PSG respectively, whilst Pedro Neto and Bruno Jordao signed from Lazio.
