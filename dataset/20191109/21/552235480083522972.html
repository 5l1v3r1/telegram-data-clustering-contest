Lewis Capaldi poses as High Street Musical star Troy Bolton thanks to NRJ Awards jacket

The Scots singer joked on Instagram about his new look while wearing a snazzy varsity jacket.

09 Nov 2019, 21:48 by Stephen Stewart, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/stephen-stewart/

Lewis Capaldi joked he was Troy Bolton from High School Musical as he posed in a snappy red and white varsity jacket given to him by the NRJ Awards.

The Scots singer took to Instagram to reveal he is in Cannes for the posh ceremony on Saturday night. After arriving at his hotel he was given a number of gifts by the French radio station.

His fave present was a bright red and white jacket – which Capaldi said made him look like high school basketball player Troy – played by Zac Efron.

Capaldi said: “See that? Is that the captain of the basketball team?”

“Is that the jock going out with the head cheerleader? He’s the coolest guy in school.

“Thank you NRJ Awards for this lovely jacket. It makes me feel like I’m Troy Bolton.

“I’m Troy Bolton – I’m the prom king, captain of the school basketball team, star quarterback.”

Lewis said he had been wanting to “spice up” his look for a while – making the memorable varsity jacket a perfect gift for him.

The Whitburn singer also revealed that the jacket had his name on the back. He joked: “It says my f***ing name on the back of it. You misspelled s**gger.”
