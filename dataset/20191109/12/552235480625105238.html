Gerard Butler snapped at light show at Glasgow's Botanic Gardens

The Hollywood megastar struck a pose while exploring the GlasgGlow event last night.

09 Nov 2019, 12:19 by Lynn Love

Scottish actor Gerard Butler was spotted out in Glasgow's west end last night.

The Hollywood megastar struck a pose while exploring the  GlasGLOW  event at the  Botanic Gardens.

The 47-year-old, who is originally from Paisley, was one of the thousands of visitors to experience the light show spectacle before it ends on Sunday (November 10), reports Glasgow Live.

And he dropped a massive hint that he might return to the city at Christmas.

Gerard was snapped grinning in front of a giant poster for  Elfingrove, the winter wonderland festival that's due to  Kelvingrove Art Gallery and Museum next month.

The 300 star is no stranger to the city - he was spotted in Glasgow earlier in the week having a coffee at a Bearsden cafe. Could it be that he's ditched the Hollywood hills for Dear Green Place?

The Grace and Favour team uploaded a snap of the PS I Love You heartthrob to their Facebook on Thursday afternoon, and of course they entitled it "dish of the day".

We hope you stick around in Glasgow, Gerard! And if you'd like to drop by Glasgow Live HQ, we really could do with a Butler of our own.
