Strictly's Emma Barton's very famous ex-boyfriends as she keeps love life under wraps

EastEnders star Emma reveals little about her romances but is believed to have had relationships with some big names

09 Nov 2019, 18:50 by Jane Lavender, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/, Tiffany Wallis

EastEnders' star Emma Barton's adorable Strictly partnership with Anton Du Beke is one of the most endearing things on the dance floor.

While the couple have struggled to reach the top of the leaderboard on the hit BBC One show, it hasn't stopped them winning over millions of new fans.

And Emma's heartbreaking reaction to Sharley Ballas' harsh comments following her Rumba performance last Saturday prompted Anton to step in to defend her.

Shirley said: "First of all I want to say that you're tall and elegant and you have everything it takes to do a Rumba before I say what I'm about to say."

But desperate to protect his partner, Anton jumped in to say "there's no need to say any more", which left Shirley "fuming".

 

The judge added: ""The only timing that I could read was the basic - the alemana and the sliding doors... the rest of it I have absolutely no idea on what count you were dancing for this dance."

Emma was close to tears as the judges tore her and Anton's Rumba apart and the couple ended up at the bottom of the score board.

The actress, who first appeared in EastEnders as Honey in 2005, has always made sure she keeps her love life close to her chest.

But the 42-year-old has had some very famous ex-boyfriends.

Currently believed to be single, she's had no shortage of admirers from among her co-stars.

 

She married lawyer Nigel Stoat in 2002 but the couple separated in 2005.

Emma then dated hunky cop Harry Waddingham, who she met through his sister, who is also an actress.

The EastEnders star had appeared in the West End musical Grease with Harry's sister and she is believed to have introduced the pair.

A two year love affair with fellow EastEnders actor, Joel Beckett, followed in 2007, according the reports.

The actor played Jake Moon from 2004 to 2006.

Emma was linked to presenter Stephen Mulhern after they met in 2008.

Emma and Stephen appeared in several pantos together although they're believed to have split for good in 2011.

Then, in 2016, Jamie Borthwick, who plays Jay Brown in the soap, admitted he had a huge crush on Emma.

The 24-year-old claimed he had had a soft spot for Emma since he was 14 but she soon shut down rumours of a possible romance, insisting he was "more like a brother".
