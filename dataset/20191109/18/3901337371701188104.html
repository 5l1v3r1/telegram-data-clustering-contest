Starting XI: Dortmund v Bayern

09 Nov 2019, 18:41 by SL International

Bundesliga

Teams: Borussia Dortmund v Bayern Munich

Date: 09 November 2019

Venue: Signal-Iduna Park

Bayern Munich will be out restore some pride after last week's humiliating loss when they travel to Borussia Dortmund on Saturday evening - Here's how they line up.

The match is scheduled to get underway at 19h30.

The Bavarians were left embarassed seven days ago after suffering a 5-1 loss to Eintracht Frankfurt, which ultimately resulted in the sacking of manager Niko Kovac a day later.

The Bundesliga champions switched their focus to the UEFA Champions League this week after defeating Olympiakos 2-0 to advance to the round of 16.

Dortmund, meanwhile, looked to have got their season back on track with three straight victroies in all competitions, the last of which was a come-from-behind 3-2 win over Inter Milan.

Thomas Tuchel's troops will have the chance move top for at least a day should they secure three points, while Bayern can only move as high as second.

Bayern will be looking to Robert Lewandowski to lead them to victory, as the Polish strike has already bagged 21 goals across all competitions this season.

Lewandowski does have an immpressive record against Dortmund since joining Bayern, having netted 16 goals in 18 matches against his former employers.

Here's how they line up:

Borussia Dortmund: Bürki; Hakimi, Akanji, Hummels, Schulz; Witsel, Weigl; Sancho, Brandt, Hazard; Götze.

Bayern Munich: Neuer; Pavard, Javi Martínez, Alaba, Davies; Goretzka, Kimmich; Coman, Müller, Gnabry; Lewandowski.
