Khabib Nurmagomedov says ‘95 percent chance’ his next title defense will be against Tony Ferguson

09 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Lewis Mckeever

It might finally be happening.

Speaking to reporters in Moscow on Friday, undefeated UFC lightweight champion Khabib Nurmagomedov said there’s a ‘95 percent chance’ his next title defense will be against top-ranked contender Tony Ferguson, per Twitter user @MMARusContext.
