Strictly star Michelle Visage's secret health battle 'caused by her breast implants'

The RugPaul's Drag Race judge has wowed judges and her fans on the dance floor but has battled a hidden disease for years

09 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Jane Lavender, Will Twigger, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

Strictly star Michelle Visage is very much the woman of the moment with her stunning dance floor moves.

The judge on RuPaul's Drag Race was the first contestant to reach a lofty score of 39 with partner Giovanni Pernice when they performed the foxtrot on Halloween.

And in last weekend's BBC show the couple once again flew close to the top of the leaderboard when they netted 34 for their stunning Paso Doble.

But the 51-year-old has battled secret a health problem for decades, which led to her hair falling out in clumps and her suffering with crippling panic attacks.

Michelle was diagnosed with Hashimoto's disease, which is a thyroid problem that can cause serious health problems.

Symptoms can include fatigue, weight gain, depression and joint pain.

And Michelle is convinced it was caused by the breast implants she had when she was just 21.

She said: "My skin was crackly dry and my hair was falling out in clumps. I was also having hardcore panic attacks.

"My journey goes back to me getting breast implants at the age of 21. My body couldn't fight off the attacker because it was there 24 hours a day, seven days a week."

Her fears her implants had caused her disease were never confirmed by a doctor but Michelle carried out her own research.

She has now had her breast implants removed and says she feels better than ever.

Michelle said: "After 30 years of having breast implants and 20 years of suffering with Hashimoto's, not one doctor told me what it could be.

"So I found a Facebook group where over 50,000 women who had the same symptoms from their breast implants and started a documentary on my journey with Breast Implant Illness, before getting them removed.

"Since having them removed, the thyroid nodule is going down, which is amazing."

Michelle has also been open about her daughter's battles with mental health.

Heartbrekingly, 19-year-old Lillie, was struggling so much she had even contemplated taking her own life in the past.

Michelle said: "She had to deal with depression for so long that she lost a lot of her teenage years. I've told her I want her to date everything so she knows what she likes. she's really flourishing now."

"Lillie was suicidal for a while. But she's been three years on the other side now. "

The TV star, who also has daughter, Lola, 17, tried a hollistic approach to helping her daughter before turning to medication and therapy.

She said: "With mental health, children are always forgotten, people tell them to man up and quit complaining.

"Instagram feeds into that. Lillie was log out for a month and Lola's now come to a place where she realises her self worth. I've taught her to love herself."
