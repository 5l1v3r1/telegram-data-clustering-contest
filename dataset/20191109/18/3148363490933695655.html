Warren Veers from Sanders, Says Billionaires Should Exist

Sen. Elizabeth Warren (D-MA) deviated from Sen. Bernie Sanders’ (I-VT) hardline position that billionaires “should not exist,” articulating her view at an Environmental Justice forum in South Carolina on Friday evening.

09 Nov 2019, 18:27 by Hannah Bleau

In rare form, Warren, who has embraced and tweaked many of the positions long held by Vermont’s socialist senator, expressed a vastly different position than her ideological similar, telling Democracy Now!’s Amy Goodman that she does not agree with Sanders’ view that billionaires “should not exist.”
