Miami Heat player Dion Waiters suffers ‘medical emergency’ aboard NBA team’s plane: reports

09 Nov 2019, 10:12 by Dom Calicchio

Miami Heat guard Dion Waiters was scratched from the team’s game against the Lakers in Los Angeles on Friday night, reportedly because he ate too many “gummies” candies.

Waiters was unconscious when the plane landed, and suffered a seizure when he awakened, according to Andy Slater of South Florida’s FOX Sports 640 radio.
