Arjun Kapoor, Janhvi Kapoor, Khushi Kapoor and Boney Kapoor's Whatsapp group chat is like every family chat group ever

09 Nov 2019, 10:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

When it comes to family, be it us or celebrities, everyone's the same. The proof of it is the Kapoor family Whatsapp group, in which Arjun Kapoor, Janhvi Kapoor, Khushi Kapoor, Boney Kapoor and Anshula Kapoor keep one another updated about their whereabouts.

In a recent screenshot shared by Anshula Kapoor of the family Whatsapp group, one can read Janhvi Kapoor informing all about landing in Amritsar, while Boney Kapoor confirms his safe landing in Chennai, from where he will board the flight for Mumbai. Anshula also shared her update on boarding from Delhi, and this chat is like every family group chat ever.
