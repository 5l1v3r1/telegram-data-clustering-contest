Lana Del Rey brought out Best Coast & Lucy Dacus in Chicago (watch)

09 Nov 2019, 10:48 by Andrew Sacher

The surprise guests on this Lana Del Rey tour just keep coming. At her show at Chicago’s Aragon Ballroom last night (11/8), she brought out Best Coast’s Bethany Cosentino to perform Best Coast’s classic “When I’m With You” with Bethany, and then turned the stage over to Bethany to do “Up All Night” on her own. Later in the night, she brought out Lucy Dacus (who opened the show) to sing Lucy’s song “Night Shift” with her. Watch videos of both collabs and check out the full setlist from the show below.

The tour continues in Des Moines on Sunday (11/10) and includes more US shows through late November. What other surprises will Lana have in store?

Lucy Dacus had just released her new holiday EP the day of the show, and she has a tour with Haley Heynderickx coming up, including a show at NYC’s Webster Hall in December.

Best Coast just announced a new album and tour with Mannequin Pussy for 2020 earlier this week.
