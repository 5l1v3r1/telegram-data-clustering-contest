Have an adult-only Christmas in Kilkenny

ENJOY the Christmas magic in the medieval city of Kilkenny and a chance to relax in a peaceful adults-only environment at the four-star Hotel Kilkenny. Arrive on Christmas Eve to a warm welcome reception and indulge in the elegant Taste Restaurant, followed by live entertainment in Pure Bar with a complimentary festive drink. Christmas Day starts with a delicious Prosecco breakfast. A full traditional Christmas lunch will be served, accompanied by a visit from magician Andy James. Later in the evening, guests can enjoy a light bite or evening meal and live music. Two-night festive breaks are from €405pp. Hotelkilkenny.ie

Love your hotel in Fuerteventura

One of the fastest-growing ATOL Protected online travel agents, LoveHolidays are offering seven nights in Fuerteventura from £527 per person based on two adults sharing. The luxurious break involves staying at the five-star Gran Hotel Atlantis Bahia Real on a bed and breakfast basis, flying from Belfast International on December 7. Located opposite the sea, next to the Natural Park of the Corralejo Dunes and close to the beautiful fishing village of Corralejo, the hotel also boasts the Bahia Vital Spa. Loveholidays.com

Girly getaway in Limavady

Get away from it all with a 'girls' retreat' at the Lodge at Roe Park Resort. The offer includes sparkling wine and chocolate-dipped strawberries on arrival, an Elemis Spa Gift Bag each, a variety of chick flick DVDs, a three-course dinner, full use of the thermal areas and topped off with a locally sourced full Irish buffet breakfast. The break is priced from £59pp, on selected dates until December 31. Minimum five adults, maximum 11 adults. To book call 028 7772 2222.
