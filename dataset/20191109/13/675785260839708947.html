What US time is KSI vs Logan Paul? Start time plus USA TV, YouTube and live stream information

KSI and Logan Paul are heading to LA for their highly-anticipated rematch - and this time the stakes are much higher

09 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Aaron Flanagan, Martin Domin, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

KSI and Logan Paul clash for a second time tonight - and this time it's professional.

The YouTube stars will bid to settle their rivalry once and for all and have come to Los Angeles for their eagerly-anticipated rematch.

Having fought to a controversial draw in Manchester last year in a white-collar bout, this time the social media stars have dispensed with the head guards and pulled on 10oz gloves.

They will star at the Staples Center in front of a 20,000 crowd after Eddie Hearn agreed to promote the sequel.

And such is their pulling power that world champions Billy Joe Saunders and Devin Hane, as well as a host of up-and-coming stars, have agreed to box on the undercard.

Here is everything you need to know about tonight's fights...

When is the fight?

The fight is taking place on Saturday November 9.

It is the first time they have fought off in the United States.

What US time is the fight likely to start?

The main event is expected to begin between 9pm and 10pm for those on west coast time. This is also the local start time, with the fight taking place in Los Angeles.

For those in central time in the USA, the action will begin between 11pm and midnight.

On the east coast, it will be between midnight and 1am.

For UK viewing information, follow this link.

What TV channel is the fight being shown on?

For those in the USA, it can solely be streamed on the DAZN service.

DAZN is only available for subscribers at a cost of $19.99 per month or $99 for the year.

Can you live stream the fight?

DAZN is an online streaming service and subscribers will be able to stream the fight onto their relevant devices.

Will the fight be shown on YouTube?

The fight will not be streamed on YouTube, unlike their first clash.

However, a pre-show, Countdown to KSI vs Logan Paul 2, will be shown on DAZN, KSI and Logan Paul's channel ahead of the event.

Is there a way to watch the fight for free?

No. Unfortunately, this is solely a pay-per-view event.

However, you can follow the action as it happens on the Mirror Fighting live blog.
