Tesla Cybertruck Already Has Nearly 150,000 Preorders!

24 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Mike Sanders

I think it’s pretty safe to say that the recently revealed Tesla Cybertruck is a somewhat polarising design. And that may just be the biggest understatement I’ve made in 2019. Like it or not though, it seems that the car is proving to be more than a little popular with consumers.

Just how popular? Well, in a post on his official Twitter account, Elon Musk has confirmed that the Tesla Cybertruck already has nearly 150,000 preorders placed upon it.
