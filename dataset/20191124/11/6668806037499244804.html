B-town celebs and kids attend the birthday bash of Riteish Deshmukh and Genelia D'Souza's son Riaan

24 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Kajol Mathur

Riteish Deshmukh and Genelia D'Souza's son Riaan Deshmukh turned 8 yesterday and celebrating the special day, the two hosted a special bash at a popular venue in the city. Truly a star-studded affair, Riaan was joined by various B-town stars and their little ones for the celebrations.

Present for the birthday bash were Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan with their daughter Aaradhya Bachchan, Misha and Zain Kapoor with mamma Mira Kapoor, Vivek Oberoi with his kids, Esha Deol along with daughter Radhya Takhtani, Rani Mukerji with daughter Adira, Tusshar Kapoor with son Laksshya Kapoor, Arpita Khan Sharma with son Ahil Sharma, Akshay Kumar’s daughter Nitara and various others.
