Mark Wright looks overwhelmed with emotion as he gazes at Megan McKenna singing

The former The Only Way Is Essex star was mesmerised as he watched Megan performing on stage on Saturday night

24 Nov 2019, 11:05 by Mirror .co.uk

Mark Wright looked close to tears as he gazed upon Megan McKenna as she warbled away on The X Factor : Celebrity on Saturday night.

The 32-year-old reality star was a surprise guest in the audience of the long running ITV singing contest where he was reunited with his former TOWIE co-star Megs, 27.

Sitting in the audience, Mark looked close to tears as he watched Megan sing a rendition of Roxette’s 1987 classic power ballad, It Must Have Been Love.

Teary eyed Mark gazed upon reality beauty Megan as she sang like an angel while dressed like a vision in red, wearing a strapless red dress with a revealing slit running up the front of her left leg.

As she neared the chorus of the iconic song, cameras cut to show Mark absolutely lost in the performance as he gazed at her before breaking into a smile.

The performance won the full support of Mark, who gushed about her to X Factor host Dermot O’Leary after she performed.

“Megan’s an Essex girl. She’s repping Essex,” the star - who appeared on the show without soap star wife Michelle Keegan, 32.

Mark even backed Megan to win, adding: “I think, with what just happened, it’s got to be Megan. She is phenomenal.”

It’s not the first time Mark has crossed paths with Megan, having made a cringe attempt at flirting with the dark haired beauty way back in 2010 when they both featured on The Only Way Is Essex.

Mark desperately asked her: “Do you have a boyfriend?” long before he began dating Coronation Street star Michelle - with whom he has been blissfully married since 2015.

Meanwhile, there was embarrassment for Mark following his appearance in the X Factor audience as Simon Cowell’s girlfriend Lauren Silverman appeared to ask who he was.

As Dermot sat beside Mark to ask him for opinions, Lauren could be seen shooting him quizzical looks.

"Did Simons partner just ask who @MarkWright_ was live on tv directly in front on the camera?!?!? Love the lady answering clarified he was on Towie,” one fan asked on Twitter .
