Controlled burn near Myra

24 Nov 2019, 11:17 by Miriam Halpenny

UPDATE: 11:17 a.m.

The Kelowna Fire Department has confirmed that the smoke spotted near Myra Canyon is the result of a controlled burn being conducted.

ORIGINAL: 10:28 a.m.

At approximately 10 a.m. two large clouds of smoke were spotted near the Myra Canyon area and have been growing larger in size.

Castanet received a news tip from a witness living in West Kelowna who says he spotted one cloud of smoke across the lake just after 9 a.m., which has now grown into two larger plumes of smoke.

It is not known at this time what caused the smoke but Castanet will update when more information becomes available.
