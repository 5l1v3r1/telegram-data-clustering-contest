John Abraham talks about 'Dostana 2'; says Karan Johar will add glamour, entertainment, and commercial value to the movie

24 Nov 2019, 11:59 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

John Abraham recently featured in Anees Bazmee's Pagalpanti with Ileana D'Cruz, Anil Kapoor, Arshad Warsi and others. The star is currently prepping for his next flick titled 'Attack'. Recently, reports are doing the rounds on the Internet that Karan Johar's 'Dostana 2' will be seeing a cameo of the original 2008 film Dostana's lead actor. In a recent interview with a daily, John opened up about the second instalment. The film stars Janhvi Kapoor, Kartik Aaryan and Lakshya in important roles.

The actor reportedly said that that he wasn’t feeling bad about not being a part of the KJo's new film. He further added and said that Karan will add glamour, entertainment, and commercial value to the film. He must be having something interesting and entertaining things in mind.

The star also talked about his character in the film, he said that he prefers to detach himself from his characters after the film was released because he doesn’t see a point in holding on to them. He feels that there’s no point in getting emotionally attached to anything, especially films.

'Pagalpanti' also featured Pulkit Samrat, Kriti Kharbanda, Saurabh Shukla and Urvashi Rautela in pivotal roles.
