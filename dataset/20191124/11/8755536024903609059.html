Myles Stephenson’s ex warns I’m A Celeb camp mates ‘he’s after anything with a pulse’

"He is a massive f*ckboy, charmer and narcissist," she claimed

24 Nov 2019, 11:49 by Sarah Magliocco

An ex- girlfriend of I’m A Celebrity star Myles Stephenson has alleged that the X-Factor singer is a “womanizer.”

Rebecca Bignell, who previously dated the Rak-Su singer, told The Sunday Mirror that the female jungle campmates should be wary if he makes romantic advances.

“Watch out. He’s after anything with a pulse,” Rebecca claimed to the paper.

She said: “I thought he was my soulmate, but fame and money just turned him into somebody I don’t know.”

Rebecca and Myles reportedly got together in 2016 before his 2017 appearance on the X Factor show.

“Myles chased after me for years, but I didn’t want to know as I was getting over an ex,” she claimed. However, she said she soon fell for him, and alleged that they even discussed marriage.

Her claims come days after Myles was accused of “cheating” by Love Islander Gabby Allen.

Rebecca claimed that seeing Gabby’s allegations has put her off Myles entirely.

“I came to my senses last Thursday. I’m done with Myles Stephenson. He is a massive f*ckboy, charmer and narcissist,” she alleged.

Myles has insisted that he never cheated on Gabby.
