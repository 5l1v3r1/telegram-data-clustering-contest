Daniel O'Donnell wants an affair on Coronation Street

24 Nov 2019, 11:31 by Ian Brennan

National heart-throb Daniel O'Donnell has claimed he is ready to have an affair...on Coronation Street.

The Donegal native spoke of his love for the British soap opera, his favourite show, and that he would love a cameo appearance.

However, a simple walk-on part or showing up in the background wouldn't be enough for the singer.

"I'd love an affair," he joked. "I could have had an affair with Mavis (Rita’s famously dithery sidekick), but Mavis has been gone for ages.

"Mary might be the only one left for me now, so maybe me and Mary in her campervan!

"I could be the mechanic! I’d love a cameo – I’d sweep the streets, anything. It’s a great programme."

O'Donnell also gave an insight to the Irish Daily Mirror into what his life is like outside of work, saying that he enjoys playing cards in his local town hall.

"Everybody knows me, this is where I was born and grew up. I’ve gone for the past 50 years.

"I love playing cards and we have great fun – we don’t take it seriously and we’re always laughing.

"Sometimes you can win a fiver or a tenner. We pay 5 Euros to go in and then the top prize is 15 Euros."
