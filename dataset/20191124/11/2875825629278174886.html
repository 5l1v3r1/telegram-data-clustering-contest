Essex lorry: Armagh man, 23, charged with human trafficking offences

The 31 men and eight women were found dead in a lorry trailer found in Grays, Essex on Wednesday, October 23

24 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Jilly Beattie

A Co Armagh man has been charged with human trafficking offences.

Christopher Kennedy, 23, was arrested last Friday, November 22, by detectives in Essex in connection to the deaths of 39 Vietnamese nationals.

The 31 men and eight women were found dead in a lorry trailer found in Grays, Essex on Wednesday, October 23.
