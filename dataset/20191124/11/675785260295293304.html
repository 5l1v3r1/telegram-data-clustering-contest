Hunt for driver after female pedestrian killed in hit-and-run near hospital

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

24 Nov 2019, 11:51 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

A 53-year-old woman has died after she was struck by a car in a horror hit-and-run outside a hospital.

Police were called to the scene outside the Royal Albert Edward Infirmary in Wigan, Greater Manchester, just before 9pm on Saturday.

The woman was pronounced dead at the scene.
