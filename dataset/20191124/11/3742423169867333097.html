You Can Stream A Ton Of NFL Games For Free This Weekend Via Yahoo Sports

24 Nov 2019, 11:17 by Emily Price

If you’d like to watch the Seahawks vs. Eagles game tomorrow and don’t have a TV, you can tune in no matter where you are using the Yahoo Sports app for free.

Yahoo is streaming both tomorrow’s Seahawks vs. Eagles game and Monday’s game between the Ravens and Rams via Yahoo Sports for free. The app is free to download, as is the streaming itself.

It’s also offering some localised games this weekend that you can stream depending on your location.

Sunday, Nov. 24 at 1:00pm ET:

○ Miami Dolphins at Cleveland Browns on CBS

○ Detroit Lions at Washington Redskins on FOX

○ Oakland Raiders at New York Jets on CBS

○ New York Giants at Chicago Bears on FOX

○ Carolina Panthers at New Orleans Saints on FOX

○ Tampa Bay Buccaneers at Atlanta Falcons on FOX

○ Denver Broncos at Buffalo Bills on CBS

○ Pittsburgh Steelers at Cincinnati Bengals on CBS 

Sunday, Nov. 24 at 4:05pm ET:

○ Jacksonville Jaguars at Tennessee Titans on CBS

Sunday Nov. 24 at 4:25pm ET:

○ Green Bay Packers at San Francisco 49ers on FOX

○ Dallas Cowboys at New England Patriots on FOX

And Yahoo’s free games are in addition to the free Thursday night games being streamed by Amazon this year for Prime members.

There are still a few of those left to catch this season. All of the games kick off at 8:20pm ET/5:20 PT and can be played on any device that’s capable of streaming from Prime Video.
