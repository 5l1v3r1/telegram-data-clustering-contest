Migrant Declares War on Booze Destroying Liquor Bottles at Supermarket in Central Germany - Video

Police in the city of Nordhausen, where the incident took place, have launched an investigation into the matter. The disgruntled man's motives remain unclear.

24 Nov 2019, 11:50

A 24-year-old asylum seeker from Afghanistan ravaged a supermarket in Nordhausen, Thuringia in central Germany, on Thursday afternoon destroying hundreds of alcohol bottles at a supermarket.

In a video from security cameras, the man was seen walking along an aisle with liquor bottles before he started violently knocking the bottles on the floor.
