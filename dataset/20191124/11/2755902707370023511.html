24 Nov 2019, 11:46 by Timothy Ndoro

Fuel Shoots Up Again

The Zimbabwe Energy Regulatory Authority (ZERA) has announced yet another increase in the price of fuel effective tomorrow, Monday, 25 November 2019.

Diesel has gone up by 16 cents per litre to cost ZWL$17.90 per litre while Blended Petrol has gone up by 37 cents per litre to cost ZWL$17.44 per litre.

This week’s margin of increase is slightly bigger than last week’s increase which was of 21 cents and 30 cents per litre of diesel and petrol respectively.

In other news, President Emmerson Mnangagwa’s spokesperson George Charamba has clarified on the welcome that was received by Vice President Constantino Chiwenga who returned home in the early hours of Saturday morning. Chiwenga spent more than 4 months away from the office after suffering from an undisclosed ailment, although some reports have said that he was poisoned. However, the Vice President seems to have recovered well after he went for treatment in China where specialists from Zimbabwe, South Africa, India and China worked on his health.

Following the return of the Vice President,  social media has been awash with speculation and conspiracy theories. The speculation and the theories were generated after the Vice President was welcomed by a delegation of officials from the local Chinese Embassy. The lack of high-level government officials at the airport also roused interest in some who speculated that this was a clear indication that Chiwenga was not in good books with his principal, President Emmerson Mnangagwa.
