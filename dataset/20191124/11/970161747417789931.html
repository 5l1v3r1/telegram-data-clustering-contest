K-Pop star and former Kara member Goo Hara 'found dead' at 28

24 Nov 2019, 11:14

K-Pop star Goo Hara has reportedly been found dead, according to Korean media.

Police say the singer, formerly a member of the girl group Kara, was found in her home in Cheongdam on Sunday evening.

Her cause of death is being investigated.

Kara was originally made up of seven members, but the current line-up consisted of Hara, Gyuri, Seungyeon and Youngji.

The group debuted in 2007 under DSP Media and released hits such as Cupid and Step.

They disbanded in 2016.

Japanese agency Production Ogi released a statement which requested privacy for the star’s family.

It read: ‘Hello. We are here to deliver tragic and unfortunate news.

‘Family members and friends of Goo Hara are deeply shocked and anxious at the moment.
