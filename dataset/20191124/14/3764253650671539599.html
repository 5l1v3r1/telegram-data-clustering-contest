Man City vs Chelsea: Blues' fans berate Emerson Palmieri's performance

24 Nov 2019, 14:16

- Man City vs Chelsea's Premier League game ended 2-1 in favor of the Citizens

- Kevin De Bruyne and Mahrez scored for Manchester City while N'Golo Kante scored for the Blues

- Chelsea fans however blamed Emerson Palmieri for their defeat against Manchester City

Chelsea fans have berated the performance of defender Emerson Palmieri after allowing Riyad Mahrez to score the winner for Manchester City on Saturday, November 23.

Frank Lampard and his wards were beaten 2-1 by the reigning Premier League champions who have now moved to third position on the standings.

Emerson Palmieri has now been blamed by Chelsea fans for their defeat against Manchester City with many telling the Blues chiefs to replace him.

But Chelsea fans will have to pray that the club's win their appeal against the two-year ban currently slapped on them over registering young players.

Chelsea have taken the case to the Court of Arbitration for sports and they are awaiting judgement.
