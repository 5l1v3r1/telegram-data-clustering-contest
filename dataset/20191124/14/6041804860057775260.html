Lokesh Busts Paytm Batch Propaganda of YSRCP?

24 Nov 2019, 14:52 by Sridhar Raavi

Rajya Sabha MPs – Sujana Chowdary and CM Ramesh quit TDP and joined BJP after the elections. YSR Congress suspects that Chandrababu himself sent them to BJP to save his skin. And they are trying their level best to propagate that.

CM Ramesh’s son’s wedding is happening in Dubai today. A total of 15 flights have been booked for Dubai. Many political leaders were invited to the event. YSR Congress Media Social Media Wing has been spreading the news that Lokesh also attended the event.

They posted some pictures of Lokesh’s US Trip from 2015 and claiming them to be the photos of Dubai. Lokesh took to Twitter and lambasted the news as Fake. He termed the news as the had work of ‘Five Rupees Paytm Batch’.

He further ridiculed that the Paytm batch should demand more money per post since the Chief Minister is making crores with J-Tax.
