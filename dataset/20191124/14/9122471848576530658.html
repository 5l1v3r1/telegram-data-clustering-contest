Oklahoma Fans Will Love What Lincoln Riley Said After Win vs. TCU

24 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Andrew Holleran

Oklahoma barely escaped TCU on Saturday night to remain in the thick of the College Football Playoff hunt. The Sooners got some help, too.

Oregon suffered a surprising upset loss on Saturday night, losing at Arizona State. The Ducks are now eliminated from the College Football Playoff race.

This helps Oklahoma, which is ranked below the Pac-12’s two contenders in Utah and Oregon (though not for long).

Lincoln Riley isn’t worrying about any of that right now, though.

Oklahoma’s head coach is strictly focused on his rival.

“Getting ready for Oklahoma State,” Riley said when asked about Oregon’s loss.
