Song Hye Kyo thank fans for birthday wishes and gifts

24 Nov 2019, 14:00

- Advertisement -

It was Song Hye Kyo’s birthday recently and Yoo Ah In celebrated with her by taking photos with her and gifting her a beautiful bouquet. He wrote in an Instagram post with the caption, “Long live the queen.”

The South Korean actress thanked her fans all over the world who celebrated her birthday with her.

On 22 November, she wrote on Instagram Story about her gratitude towards her Korean and international fans who have wished her happy birthday. She also said she will always remember the love that they shower her with.

Song Hye Kyo also shared photos of how the fans are celebrating her special occasion with along with the message.

Billboard advertisements of the actress can be found at Times Square in New York City while others sent in certificates of proof of charitable donations and volunteer work done in her name.

There is also a Song Hye Kyo Baidu Bar working to hold a “Song Hye Kyo’s Drawings of Love class” for children with hearing disabilities.

The starlet celebrated her birthday on 22 November. Song Hye Kyo is said to be looking over offers for her next drama role as she returns to acting.

Born on November 22, 1981, Song Hye Kyo is a South Korean actress who rose to stardom in Asia with roles in TV dramas Autumn in My Heart, All In, Full House, That Winter, the Wind Blows, Descendants of the Sun and Encounter.

She also starred in films like Hwang Jin Yi, The Grandmaster, My Brilliant Life and The Queens.

Song Hye Kyo ranked 7th in Forbes magazine’s Korea Power Celebrity list in 2017 and 6th in 2018. Her acting in TV dramas made her famous worldwide.

In other news, Song Hye Kyo and Fin.K.L.’s Lee Jin recently shared a heartfelt moment on Instagram with Song uploading a beautiful video of the ocean on 13 Nov.

Lee Jin, who is a good friend of Song Hye Kyo responded with a “Miss you” and accompanied that with a heart emoji.

Song Hye Kyo then responded with a “Me too” and a heart emoji.

The Full House actress is good friends with all the Fin.K.L. members but is especially close to Lee Jin.
