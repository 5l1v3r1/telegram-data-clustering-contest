Who should I vote for in the 2019 general election?  

24 Nov 2019, 14:23 by Telegraph Reporters

The starting gun has been fired finally in the general election, with less than a month to go. The candidate lists have been confirmed and televised debates have begun. A lucky few voters might even have a candidate knock at their door. 

Although some policy lines have already emerged, this week will see the big manifesto launches. First out of the blocks were the Green Party, followed by the Liberal Democrats. Labour and the Conservatives will round off this week. The Telegraph is keeping a close eye on all announcements, and you can keep up to date through our manifesto watch article here. 

With Brexit battle lines drawn, tactical voting could be a big issue. But who to vote for? Well, the Telegraph has an interactive tool to help you decide. All you need is a postcode.
