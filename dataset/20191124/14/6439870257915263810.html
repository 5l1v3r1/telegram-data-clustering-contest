CHARLOTTE, N.C. (WBTV) - One person has been arrested in connection to a police pursuit on Saturday night in Gastonia. Police say 20-year-old Harrison Hillman is the man who led them on the chase.

According to Gaston County Communications it started at North Poplar Street and the chase ended along Cox Road.

Hillman has been arrested and is in Gaston County jail. His forst appearance is scheduled for Tuesday morning.

Gastonia Police are currently investigating three vehicle-related to the chase.

The first incident happened at 10:28 p.m. when Gastonia police officers encountered a suspect in a stolen motor vehicle from Charlotte.

Gaston County Communications confirmed that the car was found along North Poplar Street and East Park Avenue.

Reportedly, the Hillman took off and tried to hit the patrol cars in an attempt to evade police.

A vehicle stop was conducted and the suspect was taken into custody without incident and without injury, according to police.
