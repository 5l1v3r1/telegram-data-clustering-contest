Tower Bridge shut down after ‘police incident’

24 Nov 2019, 14:18

Tower Bridge has shut down to traffic following a ‘police incident’, officers have confirmed.

City of London Police urged drivers to stay away and use alternative routes while officers deal with the ‘accident’.

The London Fire Brigade tweeted: ‘Firefighters are currently assisting @Ldn_Ambulance to rescue an injured member of the public from Tower Bridge.

‘Road closures are in place in both directions of the bridge.’
