Michigan Fans Had Message For Ohio State After Win vs. Indiana

24 Nov 2019, 14:03 by Andrew Holleran

Rivalry Week has arrived. It should be a good one.

The biggest week of the college football regular season is always highlighted by Ohio State-Michigan. The Buckeyes and the Wolverines are set to meet in Ann Arbor on Saturday.

Jim Harbaugh has yet to defeat his rival, and Ohio State will be the favorite in this year’s matchup once again, but Michigan is coming on strong.

The Wolverines topped Indiana on Saturday in blowout fashion. They’re riding a solid winning streak into The Game this year.

Michigan fans appear to have some optimism, too.

A “Beat Ohio” chant was started late in the Wolverines’ win over Indiana. Michigan’s fan base appears to be ready to go.
