Tiny Shares Sneak Peek At T.I. + Jada Pinkett’s Sit-Down Over Daughter Virginity Drama: “One Of The Realest Interviews”

24 Nov 2019, 14:02 by Angie Dare

Grammy-winning rapper T.I. didn’t ghost Jada Pinkett-Smith. The King of the South’s wife Tiny has shared a sneak peek at their roundtable sit-down reportedly centered on his decision to monitor their daughter’s virginity.

Tiny went to Instagram this weekend to share a snapshot from the Q&A and also encourage people to check out the upcoming broadcast.

Earlier in the week, Jada revealed T.I. would pull through to her talk show to discuss his recent hymen and doctor visit revelation.

“My next big guest is Tip. T.I. is coming to the table. Yes indeed,” Pinket Smith revealed. “And of course, he’s going to address the controversy that has been swirling.” (ET Online)
