15-year-old boy builds wooden fan – Nigerians blast him

24 Nov 2019, 14:58 by Sanya Agunbiade

Some Nigerians on Twitter have praised a 15-year-old boy who made a wooden standing fan all by himself, while some didn’t waste time to blast him over his invention.

According to a social media report, the boy made the fan in a space of two days, but most users are  urging him to do something better than a wooden standing fan.
