French hooker's disgusting act on England World Cup star sees him sent off in Champions Cup clash

There's no place for this on a rugby pitch

24 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Matthew Southcombe

Sale Sharks and La Rochelle were locked in a tight European Champions Cup battle in the north west when another French moment of madness unfolded.

A bit of pushing a shoving broke out as the match ticked towards half time with the scores 10-7 in favour of Sale.

England World Cup star Tom Curry was in the middle of it, though nothing particularly sinister was unfolding.

That was until La Rochelle hooker Pierre Bourgarit approached Curry, among a plethora of other scuffles, and grabbed his face, appearing to gouge him.
