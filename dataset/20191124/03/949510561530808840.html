Winter Weather Advisory issued November 24 at 3:42AM EST until November 24 at 3:00PM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

24 Nov 2019, 03:42

Winter Weather Advisory: Otsego; Delaware until 3:00 PM

* WHAT…Mixed precipitation changing to snow expected. Additional
snow accumulations of 3 to 6 inches expected, with the higher
end totals across the hilltops. Ice accumulations around a tenth
of an inch expected.

* WHERE…Delaware and Otsego counties.

* WHEN…Until 3 PM EST this afternoon. Steadiest snow expected
mid morning through early afternoon.

* IMPACTS…Plan on slippery road conditions.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS…A mix of rain, sleet and higher elevation
freezing rain will transition to mainly wet snow for a time mid
morning into the early afternoon. The snow ends from west to
east during the afternoon. Snow rates could occasionally be up
to 1.5 inches per hour at times over the higher terrain.
Temperatures will be hovering between 30 to 35 degrees through
the event.
Slow down and use caution while traveling.

The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can
be obtained by calling 5 1 1.
