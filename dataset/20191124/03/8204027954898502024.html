UD Public Safety asking for public’s help in locating SUV that hit 2 DECA students

24 Nov 2019, 03:12 by Breaking News Staff

DAYTON — An SUV struck two Dayton Early College Academy students Friday afternoon and University of Dayton Public Safety is asking the public for help in locating the vehicle, which left the scene.

The students were hit about 4:30 p.m. at Brown and K streets by the SUV that was southbound on Brown, UD officials said. 

Medics checked and cleared the students at the scene, UD officials said. 

UD Public Safety is asking anyone who has information about the incident to call them at 937-229-2121.
