DraftKings Picks Week 12: NFL DFS lineup advice for daily fantasy football cash games

24 Nov 2019, 03:36 by Thomas Emerick

Our Week 12 DraftKings cash lineup willcapitalize on some offenses that have become quietly competent, or at least ones that have flown under the radar enough to avoid aggressive price adjustments on NFL DFS sites.

Last Sunday's main slate attack focused on mid-tier receivers with target floor and receiving upside, which got us across the finish line thanks to John Brown and Calvin Ridley. We also ran with the pack on D.J.Moore and made out fine there.

DraftKings Week 12 Picks: NFL DFS cash lineup

Playing under a $50K salary cap for Sunday afternoon ET contests.

UPDATE: QB Carson Wentz, Eagles vs. Seahawks ($5,600). We'll capitalize on Wentz going dirt cheap after three straight games against top-10 defenses, including the top two units in New England and Buffalo. Wentz is primed to rebound at home against a bottom-10pass yards defense with the expected return of his top receiver in Alshon Jeffery.

INITIAL QB Sam Darnold, Jets vs. Raiders ($5,800). The Raiders defense fared better recently against Philip Rivers and Ryan Finley. However, this unit still allows the sixth-most DK points to quarterbacks and visits Sam Darnold, who's coming off consecutive 20-plus-point weeks.
