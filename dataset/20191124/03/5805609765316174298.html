Top 5 Best Camera Apps For OnePlus 7 Pro

24 Nov 2019, 03:00 by Amarnath Chakraborty

If we look around, we will find that OnePlus 7 Pro is one of the best flagship smartphones available in the marketplace. Just like every other OnePlus model, OnePlus 7 Pro also comes at an affordable price. At the cost of just $669, the smartphone offers top-end specifications.

The most noticeable feature of OnePlus 7 Pro is its triple camera setup. The primary camera sensor of OnePlus 7 Pro is of 48 megapixels which on paper looks unbeatable. Still, when it comes to the actual camera performance, the smartphone lags behind the Samsung Galaxy S10 Plus.

Some of the OnePlus 7 Pro users have claimed that they are getting a warm tint on photos while using the stock camera app. Actually, OnePlus 7 Pro has capable camera hardware, but its processing needs more optimization. So, in that case, users either need to wait for the update to fix camera bugs or use a third-party camera app.

Top 5 Best Camera Apps For OnePlus 7 Pro

In this article, we are going to share five best camera apps for OnePlus 7 Pro that would help you click stunning photos in no-time. So, let’s check out the five best camera apps for the OnePlus 7 Pro 2019.

#1 GCam Mod

Well, GCam Mod is undoubtedly the best camera app that one can get on any supported device. For those who don’t know, the GCam Mod is basically a port of the camera app from the Google Pixel smartphones. The camera app is optimized by Google to provide the best processing results. Apart from that, GCam Mod also introduces lots of Pixel exclusive features like Night Sight, Photobooth, etc. So, GCam Mod could be the best option to replace the stock camera app of OnePlus 7 Pro.

#2 Adobe Lightroom

Adobe Lightroom is basically an image editing app, but it has a powerful camera built-in. Although the camera interface of Adobe Lightroom is not known for its manual picture processing features, it does offer lots of useful features. With Adobe Lightroom, you can lock exposure, apply live filters, etc. So, Adobe Lightroom is definitely the best camera app that you can use on your OnePlus 7 Pro smartphone.

#3 HedgeCam 2

HedgeCam 2 is one of the best and most advanced manual camera control apps that you can have on any Android smartphone. The app offers lots of powerful features like subject locking, exposure control, shutter speed control, etc. Apart from that, HedgeCam 2 also offers lots of photo filter modes as well. So, HedgeCam 2 is another best camera apps for the OnePlus 7 Pro to click stunning photos.

#4 Footej Camera

This is another best and robust camera app on the list that can help you capture amazing shots from your OnePlus 7 pro device. The camera app is known for its bloat-free and straightforward user-interface. Apart from that, it uses the Camera 2 API to provide more controls. From slow-motion video recording to RAW format support, Footej Camera has everything.

#5 Open Camera

Open Camera is one of the best and most advanced camera apps that you can use on your OnePlus 7 Pro device. It provides users a fully manual camera control to control ISO, exposure, colors, shutter speed, etc. Apart from that, there’s also support for focus modes, scene modes, and color effects as well. So, Open Camera is another best camera app that you can use on your OnePlus 7 Pro smartphone.

So, these are the five best camera apps for your OnePlus 7 Pro. If you are using any other camera app on your OnePlus 7 Pro, let us know in the comment box below.
