Kirk Herbstreit Singles Out 1 Player From Ohio State-Penn State

24 Nov 2019, 03:56 by Alek Arend

Ohio State controlled the first half of today’s game against No. 8 Penn State. But the Nittany Lions didn’t go down quietly, capitalizing off a few Buckeyes’ turnovers to make it a 21-17 game heading into the fourth quarter.

But Ohio State responded in quick fashion early in the fourth quarter. QB Justin Fields threw a deep shot to WR Chris Olave, prompting the Buckeyes’ WR to adjust and make a terrific touchdown grab. The score propelled OSU to a 28-17 lead which ended up being the dagger to put the game away.

The play prompted plenty of praise from fans around the country. Even Kirk Herbstreit chimed in on Olave’s impressive touchdown catch.
