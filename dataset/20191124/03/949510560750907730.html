Winter Weather Advisory issued November 24 at 3:42AM EST until November 24 at 1:00PM EST by NWS Binghamton NY

24 Nov 2019, 03:42

Winter Weather Advisory: Broome; Susquehanna; Chenango until 1:00 PM

* WHAT…Mixed precipitation changing to snow expected. Additional
snow accumulations of 2 to 5 inches. The highest amounts are
forecast across the higher elevations in the eastern portions
of these counties.

* WHERE…In Pennsylvania, Susquehanna county. In New York,
Broome and Chenango counties.

* WHEN…Until 1 PM EST this afternoon. Steadiest snow expected
through noon.

* IMPACTS…Plan on slippery road conditions.

* ADDITIONAL DETAILS…A mix of rain, sleet and higher elevation
freezing rain will transition to mainly wet snow for a time this
morning. The snow ends from west to east by early afternoon.
Snow rates could occasionally be up to 1 inch per hour at times.
Temperatures will be hovering between 30 to 35 degrees through
the event.
Slow down and use caution while traveling.

The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can
be obtained by calling 5 1 1.

The Pennsylvania Department of Transportation and Pennsylvania
Turnpike Commission remind motorist to adjust speeds based on
driving conditions as winter weather impacts Pennsylvania
roadways. Visit www.511pa.com for the latest travel, roadways,
and traffic conditions.
