AFRIMA 2019: Burna Boy wins African artiste of the year (Video)

24 Nov 2019, 05:37 by Damilola Ismail

Burna Boy has won the African Artiste of the year at the 6th AFRIMA which held at Eko Hotel and Suites in Lagos on Saturday.

Burna Boy is indeed the rave of the moment with his Grammy nomination announced earlier this week, he has now been crowned the AFRIMA African artiste of the year and Best Male artiste in West Africa

Although the 2019 All Africa Music Awards (AFRIMA) was held in Lagos, Nigeria on Saturday, Burna Boy and several other Nigerian artistes who won different categories were absent but represented by their managers unlike award winners from other regions of Africa who were in attendance.
