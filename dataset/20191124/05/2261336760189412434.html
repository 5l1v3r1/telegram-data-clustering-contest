They said what?! Pros react to Deontay Wilder’s one punch KO over Luis Ortiz

24 Nov 2019, 05:53 by Anton Tabuena

Deontay Wilder and Luis Ortiz fought again on Saturday night, after the pair originally went 10 rounds back in 2018, with “The Bronze Bomber” winning the back-and-forth affair. Like the first fight, Ortiz had his moments again, winning numerous rounds early, until the great equalizer finished the fight.

One punch from Wilder and the fight was all over. Here’s how pro boxers and MMA fighters reacted to the heavyweight title bout.
