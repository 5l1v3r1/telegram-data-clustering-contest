2 heel and 2 face turns we could see at WWE Survivor Series 2019

24 Nov 2019, 05:37 by Nishant Jayaram

We are just hours away from Survivor Series, which is set to be one of the most exciting editions of the PPV in recent times. The addition of NXT to the show has spiced things up, and if NXT TakeOver: WarGames - which happened on Saturday night - is any indication, we are in for a treat!

Eight matches have been announced for Survivor Series 2019, five of those inter-brand matches, while there are three intra-brand matches as well. Brock Lesnar will defend his WWE Championship against Rey Mysterio, The Fiend Bray Wyatt will put his Universal title on the line against Daniel Bryan, while Adam Cole puts his NXT Championship on the line against Pete Dunne.

At a huge PPV like Survivor Series, there are bound to be some shocks and surprises that WWE have in store for us. We could possibly see a few face and heel turns on the show to keep the viewer on the edge of their seats.

Let's take a look at 2 heel and 2 face turns we could see at Survivor Series 2019.
