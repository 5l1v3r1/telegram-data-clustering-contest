Kartik Aaryan kick starts his Sunday morning with a ride on empty roads and Bollywood songs on high volume

24 Nov 2019, 05:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

While you are enjoying a laid back Sunday morning, waiting in your bed for the perfect brunch, Bollywood actor Kartik Aryan had already given his morning a kick start with a ride on the empty roads. Not just this, he made music his ride buddy, and he enjoyed a number of his own movie songs on a high volume.

Three songs that topped his list are ‘Dheeme Dheeme’ from his soon to release ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’, ‘Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare’ from the same movie and ‘Bom Diggy’ from ‘Sonu Ke Titu Ki Sweety’. Kartik shared the video of him enjoying all these songs on his social media handle and was seen even singing the last one while handling the steering wheel.

On the work front. Kartik Aaryan is geared up for the release of his one of the much awaited movies ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’. Starring him, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday, the movie is slated to release on 6th December 2019. Thereafter, will be seen in three sequels in 2020 - ‘Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2’ with Kiara Advani scheduled to release on 31 July 2020 , ‘Dostana 2’ with Janhvi Kapoor and Lakshya (release date yet to be out) and ‘Aaj Kal’ with Sara Ali Khan which will release on 14 February 2020.
