Sarkodie goes in hard for Shatta Wale, disses him for always talking ill about him in new song

24 Nov 2019, 05:05 by Adu Sarpeah

As many peace makers in the industry are calling on Stonebwoy and Kelvyn Boy to cease fire, it looks like another fearsome battle is about to erupt like volcano between Sarkodie and Shatta Wale.

This is because Ghana’s rapper of the decade has dropped another advice aimed at dimming the arrogance and ill behaviour of his arch-rival, Shatta Wale. Sarkodie strategically employed the services of Prince Bright of defunct music group (Buk Bak) on his new project.

Per the dictates of the lyrical content of the song, Sarkodie subtly labeled Shatta Wale as a pure envious person aiming at his downfall. Sarkodie noted that Shatta Wale thinks of him anytime he wakes up and throughout his whole day looking for ways and means to cause havoc in his camp.
