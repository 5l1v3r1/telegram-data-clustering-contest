Custom truck and tools worth $15,000 stolen

24 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Vanessa Schmitt

MANDURAH Detectives are investigating the theft of a truck and $150,000 worth of tools from Meadow Springs this morning .

The custom truck containing Snap On tools, was stolen from a residence on Kiawah Link between 1am and 4am.

The truck was found later this morning in Dawesville but the tools were not in the truck.

Detectives would like to speak to anyone who may have seen or has dash-cam vision of the distinctive truck, possibly in convoy with a mid-sized sedan.

Anyone with CCTV or any information relating to this incident should call Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000 or to make a report online at Crime Stoppers.
