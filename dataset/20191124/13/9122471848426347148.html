ESPN Releases New Top 25 Poll After Week 13

24 Nov 2019, 13:39 by Andrew Holleran

Week 13 of the 2019 college football regular season is in the books. We’ve got just one more full weekend of the regular season.

ESPN’s Football Power Index has updated its top 25 following all of the action on Saturday. There’s been somewhat of a shakeup, with Oregon being upset by Arizona State and Ohio State beating Penn State at home.

The Buckeyes are No. 1 in ESPN’s latest computer rankings.

Here’s the full top 25:

  2. Clemson
  3. Alabama
  4. LSU
  5. Georgia
  6. Penn State
  7. Michigan
  8. Florida
  9. Auburn
  10. Oregon
  11. Oklahoma
  12. Utah
  13. Wisconsin
  14. Notre Dame
  15. Texas A&M
  16. Iowa
  17. Minnesota
  18. Iowa State
  19. Baylor
  20. Washington
  21. USC
  22. UCF
  23. Texas
  24. Memphis
  25. Boise State

The official new top 25 polls, the Coaches’ Poll and the AP Poll, will be out later this afternoon.

The new College Football Playoff rankings will be out on Tuesday night.
