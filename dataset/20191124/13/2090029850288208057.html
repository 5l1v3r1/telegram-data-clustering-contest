Femi Otedola reveals why he donated N5 billion to his daughter’s foundation (Video)

24 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Damilola Ismail

Nigerian billionaire businessman, Femi Otedola has revealed why he donated N5 billion to his daughter’s Cuppy Foundation.
