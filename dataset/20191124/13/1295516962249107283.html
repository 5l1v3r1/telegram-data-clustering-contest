RUMOR: Disney May Be Working on Series Featuring Star Wars' Doctor Aphra

24 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Tom Chapman

The latest rumors from the Star Wars universe suggest that Disney could be working on a new live-action series based on Doctor Aphra.

According to Star Wars Unity, Disney+ is sketching out plans for a Doctor Aphra series. While the news should be taken with a pinch of salt for now, the anti-hero's fan-favorite legacy would undoubtedly make her a popular choice for a spin-off. For those who need an introduction, Doctor Aphra first appeared as part of the new canon in the 2015 comic, Star Wars: Darth Vader.

Effectively a villainous space archeologist, Aphra also has the honor of becoming the first character outside the movies to get their own Marvel comic. Originally working with the Dark Lord of the Sith, Aphra later went on the run from Vader. Elsewhere, she's usually found with murderbots Triple-Zero and Beetee-One -- dark versions of R2-D2 and C-3PO.
