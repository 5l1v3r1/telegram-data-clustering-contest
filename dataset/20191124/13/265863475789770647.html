Joseline Hernandez Made This Comment About Stevie J’s Wife, Faith Evans, In New Video And Drops A Bombshell About Her Relationship With DJ Ballistic

24 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Mel Walker

Joseline Hernandez opened up and talked to Nessa of Hot 97 in a recent interview about her struggles with co-parenting daughter Bonnie Bella with Stevie J.

The former pair had hit some rough patches while trying to find the right balance, and there have been a lot of rumors saying that the two celebrities were battling legally for the custody of the little girl.

According to recent gossip, Stevie J reportedly even managed to obtain sole custody of his daughter for one whole month, because Hernandez’s schedule had been too problematic.

In the meantime, it seems that Hernandez and Stevie J have been trying to prove the rumors wrong as they regularly post photos of themselves, spending peaceful family time together.

According to Hernandez, the current situation of co-parenting her daughter was okay, as things always got better even if they were ugly before that.

She also stated to Nessa that Stevie J was married to a gorgeous woman, referring to Faith Evans.

Hernandez also added that she and Evans were on excellent terms, and they spoke regularly on the phone as well, as Stevie J had met her fiancé DJ Ballistic — yes, the former Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta star casually announced that she is engaged.
