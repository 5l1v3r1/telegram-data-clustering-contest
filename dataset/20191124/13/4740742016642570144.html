K-Pop star found dead aged 28 after previous suicide attempt

Goo Hara was found in her home on Saturday evening

24 Nov 2019, 13:43 by Lucy Buckland, Alexander Brock

A K-Pop star has been found dead at her home just six months after a previous suicide attempt.

Goo Hara, 28, was found on Saturday evening (November 23) at her home in Gangnam's Cheongdam neighborhood.

She was a member of the band Kara before she made her solo debut in 2015 with EP Alohara (Can You Feel It?).

It was reported she tried to take her own life back in May, Mirror Online reports .

As she was recovering in hospital, she apologised to her fans, saying: “I am sorry for causing concerns and a commotion.

"In terms of health, I am recovering … I had been in agony over a number of overlapping issues. But from now on, I will steel my heart and try to show up healthy.”

Goo was close friends with tragic star Sulli who died in October after being targeted by online trolls.

Police said in a statement that the 25-year-old had been suffering from "severe depression". The exact cause of her death was still under investigation.
