Outrage as Love Island's Molly-Mae Hague uses dark shade of foundation

24 Nov 2019, 13:01

Love Island’s Molly-Mae Hague has come under fire yet again after using a foundation that is way too dark for her natural skin tone.

The influencer has disappointed fans after her latest make-up tutorial had her using a foundation shade that a lot of black and Asian people use, bringing up the question of why she feels the need to be darker than she actually is.

Describing her everday routine, the 20-year-old said: ‘I’ve really been enjoying Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk but this is too dark for me so I’m going to be mixing it with my studio fix fluid from MAC which is a holy grail of mine I use it all the time and this is in the shade NC42 which is still very dark.

‘I often get lots of comments on how my face is too dark.’
