Big Red Barn finalists in SFA National Small Business Awards

24 Nov 2019, 13:56

THE finalists in the SFA National Small Business Awards 2020 have been officially announced.

Some 41 companies have been selected as finalists, from 12 different counties, across nine categories. Mayo company Big Red Barn has been shortlisted in three categories - Innovator of the Year, Manufacturing, and Exporter of the Year.

The awards celebrate the achievements of small business in Ireland, and recognise the vital contribution of the small business sector to Irish industry.

The SFA National Small Business Awards are now entering their 16th year and are a celebration of small business owners who in the past five years created over 75,000 jobs.
