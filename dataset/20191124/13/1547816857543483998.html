From Dwayne Johnson AKA The Rock’s bed to the cast of Jumanji, Nick Jonas’ good-looking cardboard is everywhere!

Nick Jonas is everywhere these days, and we aren’t complaining! Only a few days to go until his upcoming film Jumanji makes it to the big screens and the cast of the film are neck-deep into the promotions. But hey, they are minus Nick Jonas himself! And the pop star seems to have no issues with that because his good-looking cardboard is compensating all the loss. A while ago, we spotted him on Dwayne Johnson AKA The Rock’s bed and now, it has reached to the remaining cast of Jumanji. (ALSO READ - Nick Jonas Caught Sleeping With Dwayne Johnson But The Rock Calls Kevin Hart An A***E)

And, for a second, we thought that it’s the singer himself. Thank you to everyone who continues to makes this story so much sweeter every day. May the happiness continue. #happinessbeginstour,” read his post.
