Huawei Y9s Officially Confirmed For Malaysia: Features 48MP Main Camera and Kirin 710F Processor

24 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Chief Chapree

Around one year ago, Huawei has released a mid-range phone called Y9 2019 into Malaysia. Soon, its successor which is called the Huawei Y9s will be making its way into our market very soon as per what Huawei Malaysia has revealed through its Facebook page and Twitter recently.

Interestingly, Huawei didn’t label this phone as a 2020 model as per what it has done for Y9 Prime 2019, Y9 2019, and Y9 2018. Nevertheless, the Y9s does come with several features that made it so much different from the Y9 2019.
