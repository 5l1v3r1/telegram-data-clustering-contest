Floyd Mayweather target 2 fights as he comes out of retirement in 2020

24 Nov 2019, 13:12

- Floyd Mayweather will come out of retirement in the year 2020

- And the American is already targeting fighting two opponents next year

- The 42-year-old has not fought any opponent since his last fight two years ago

American boxing legend Floyd Mayweather has a plan in place to face two big opponents when he returns to professional fight in the year 2020.

Since he defeated Conor McGregor two years ago, Floyd Mayweather has not fought with anyone until Friday, November 22, when he announced his decision to come out of retirement.

Floyd Mayweather revealed news of the comeback beneath a photo of him wearing boxing trunks and hand wraps on his official social media page.

He has been challenged to fight by many professional boxers since he retired from boxing which Floyd has turned down.
