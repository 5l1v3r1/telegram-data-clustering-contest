Bigg Boss 13: Hindustani Bhau’s Wife Files A Complaint At Khar Police Station Against Fake People Posing As Bhau’s Family Members

24 Nov 2019, 13:06 by Amruta Patel

Hindustani Bhau’s wife filed a police complaint against people posing to be his family members and tarnishing his image on social media. Check out her letter in the copy below

Entertainment sites will have enough fodder till the ongoing reality TV show Bigg Boss 13 continues to air and entertains the audience on a daily basis. Latest news, comes in not from the house but from outside this controversial house. Now, the wild card entrant Hindustan Bhau’s wife Ashwini Fhatak penned a letter and filed a complaint at the Khar Police station.

Hindustani Bhau’s (Vikas Fhatak) wife Ashwini has filed a complaint against all those people who are pretending to be his relatives in the society. In the letter, she has warned the media and publications to not fall prey to them and not indulge into any type of conversation with them. Also, she mentioned in her letter of these fake people trying to tarnish her husband’s image by posting videos carrying false info about him on the social media platform.
