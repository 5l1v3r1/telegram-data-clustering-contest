Bullring tennis and racing brides: the weekend's best photos

24 Nov 2019, 13:19 by Arnel Hecimovic

The Guardian’s picture editors select photo highlights from around the world, including the pope in Nagasaki and Coldplay in Amman

Maryland, US

A Washington Redskins cheerleader performs during the first half of an NFL football game against the Detroit Lions, in Landover, Md.

Photograph: Alex Brandon/AP

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

A coach carrying the players of Flamengo football club makes it’s way through a sea of supporters during a parade to celebrate winning the Copa Libertadores final against River Plate of Argentina.

Photograph: Fabio Motta/EPA

Telford, UK

Prime Minister Boris Johnson holds up his party’s manifesto during its launch at Telford International Centre

Photograph: Stefan Rousseau/PA

Hong Kong, China

People line up to vote outside of a polling place. Long lines formed outside Hong Kong polling stations Sunday in elections that have become a barometer of public support for anti-government protests now in their sixth month

Photograph: Ng Han Guan/AP

Newcastle, Australia

A baby seems to struggle with the noise of the racing trackside during the Newcastle 500’s Supercars Race 23 at the Newcastle Street Circuit

Photograph: Darren Pateman/AAP

Bogotá, Colombia

Protesters shout slogans during a demonstration in front of the private residence of Colombian president Iván Duque

Photograph: Mauricio Dueñas Castañeda/EPA

Nagasaki, Japan

Pope Francis conducts a mass at Baseball Stadium. The pontiff is on a four-day visit to Japan, which will be the first of a pope in 38 years and only the second in history

Photograph: Ciro Fusco/EPA

Venice, Italy

People walk in the flooded St Mark’s Square during a period of seasonal high water

Photograph: Manuel Silvestri/Reuters

Bangkok, Thailand

Thai brides-to-be wearing bridal gowns compete in the Running of the Brides event. 150 couples compete in a 3km race and a seven-task challenge to win wedding package prizes.

Photograph: Rungroj Yongrit/EPA

Sittwe, Myanmar

A spider is seen hanging from a web on a fence at Rakhine State

Photograph: Nyunt Win/EPA

Venice, Italy

Visitors and security walk across a flooded arcade during an acqua alta, a meteorological phenomenon of high flood waters.

Photograph: Miguel Medina/AFP via Getty Images

Las Vegas, US

Luis Ortiz (R) throws a right at Deontay Wilder in the second round of the WBC heavyweight championship at MGM Grand Garden Arena. Wilder retained his title by seventh-round knockout

Photograph: Ethan Miller/Getty Images

Ecatepec, Mexico

Activist Diana Ceballos, second left, takes part in a procession for felled women like her 14-year old cousin. In 2014 Ceballos’s cousin was raped and stabbed to death by an ex-boyfriend of the teenager’s mother. It took three years to secure a jail sentence for the perpetrator even though he had confessed via a text message to the mother immediately after the murder

Photograph: Alicia Fernandez/AP

Johannesburg, South Africa

The Bank of Lisbon building implodes during a controlled explosion. The iconic building was deemed structurally unfit after a 2018 fire that lasted for three days, claiming the lives of three firefighters

Photograph: Michele Spatari/AFP via Getty Images

Amman, Jordan

Coldplay frontman Chris Martin (C), drummer Will Champion (R) and guitarist Jonny Buckland (L) perform at the Citadel, launching their new album, Everyday Life

Photograph: Amel Pain/EPA

Mount Fuji, Japan

Japan’s highest peak at 3,776-meters tall (12,385 feet), is seen from an airplane window

Photograph: Gregorio Borgia/AP

Nevşehir, Turkey

A drone photo shows a view of hot air balloons gliding over the Göreme district at the Cappadocia region, a Unesco world heritage site

Photograph: Anadolu Agency/Anadolu Agency via Getty Images

Mexico City, Mexico

A drone photo shows an aerial view of the Mexico City Bullring during a tennis match between Swiss player Roger Federer and German player Alexander Zverev

Photograph: Madla Hartz/EPA

Utrecht, Netherlands

A cosplayer attends the Heroes Dutch Comic Con at Jaarbeurs

Photograph: Robin Utrecht/REX/Shutterstock

Hong Kong, China

Protesters (in red) speak to the media during a press conference on the campus of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University where they remain barricaded.

Photograph: Nicolas Asfouri/AFP via Getty Images
