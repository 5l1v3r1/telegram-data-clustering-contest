Mike Denver: I'm excited to meet Corrie favourites Samia Longchambon and Mikey North

"I will have to put my nerves and any thoughts of being starstruck aside"

24 Nov 2019, 13:25 by Mike Denver

We were all big soap fans when I was growing up simply because we didn’t have as much choice.

There were no Netflix, streaming or on-demand TV back then so I grew up watching the likes of Emmerdale and Fair City and it’s great credit to them that they are still as popular as ever today despite the stiff competition.

So you’ll understand my excitement at the prospect of meeting Coronation Street’s Mikey North and Samia Longchambon at the Guy Clothing Fashion Show in Tullamore on Wednesday.

I will have to put my nerves and any thoughts of being starstruck aside because the night is all in aid of two great charities Jigsaw Offaly - providing a free and confidential support service for young people and Offaly and Search and Recovery.

All these years later I am still watching the soaps because Liz always has them on, well that’s my story anyway.

I’m also looking forward to catching with Dancing with the Stars champion, and former Kerry footballer Aidan O’Mahony as well as getting a few Christmas style tips from rugby pundit Brent Pope at the event.

Sad welcome to the jungle

There have been a lot of nasty comments directed at Caitlyn Jenner on I’m a Celebrity... Get me out of Here!

It’s a shame to have such negativity attached to one of ITV’s biggest shows consistently with a massive fan base across Ireland and the UK.

It’s not fair that Caitlyn has been subjected to cruel transphobic abuse online- this social media bullying has to stop once and for all before something serious occurs! Perhaps the broadcaster needs to step in and protect the cast.

They do have a responsibility to those taking part, it can’t all be just about the ratings.

Once people are watching it, ITV are going to continue airing it and paying the stars huge money to appear.

We have two Irish stars taking part this year Nadine Coyle and Andrew Maxwell.

I hadn’t heard of Andrew before he went into the jungle but he’s coming across really well and he has my support to become King of the Jungle next month.

Sort out this mess

The news that former prisoners are suing the government over conditions in Mountjoy has raised a few eyebrows.

I know prison is supposed to be a form of punishment, however, it is a basic right for people to have proper toilet facilities in this day and age.

The Government must sort this out.

Friendly hairdressers are a cut above

For me, a trip to the hairdressers or barbers is as much about the chat as the grooming, so I don’t get why people are calling for silent hair appointments these days.

I go to the salon once or twice a month to keep trim and tidy and I look forward to switching off and having a light conversation.

Edel at Empire in Portumna has been doing my hair for the past 20 years, she is part raconteur, part problem-solver and hair stylist all rolled into one.

It’s a great place to hear the news and get to know what is happening around town.
