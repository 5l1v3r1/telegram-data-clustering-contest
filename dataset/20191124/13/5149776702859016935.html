Here's where Nottinghamshire's mobile speed cameras will be from Monday, November 25

The list shows where mobile speed cameras and their crews will be working

24 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Oliver Pridmore

Nottinghamshire Police has revealed the latest list of locations where its mobile speed cameras will be patrolling over the next week.

The force sends out the cameras to sites across the county in a bid to tackle speeding motorists and reduce collisions.
