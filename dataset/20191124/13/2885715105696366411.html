Al Hilal win AFC Champions League as late goals down Urawa Red Diamonds

Al Hilal won their first AFC Champions League title since the competition was launched in 2002 and it was their third in the competition after victories in the Asian Club Championship in 1991 and 2000.

24 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Reuters

Al Hilal won the Asian Champions League for the first time on Sunday as a 2-0 win against Urawa Red Diamonds in the final’s second leg gave the Saudi Arabian side a 3-0 aggregate victory over the twice champions from Japan.
