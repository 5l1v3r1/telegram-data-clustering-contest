Spurs fans will love Lucas Moura's tweet to a supporter after Jose Mourinho's first win

Tottenham beat West Ham 3-2 at the London Stadium

24 Nov 2019, 09:01 by Andy Ha

Jose Mourinho won his first game as Tottenham Hotspur head coach after a 3-2 win against West Ham United.

Goals from Son Heung-min, Lucas Moura and Harry Kane sealed Spurs' first three points away from home since January and lifts the north London side to ninth in the table.

Moura was handed a start by Mourinho, after being benched by Mauricio Pochettino for the 1-1 draw against Sheffield United before the international break.

And the former Paris Saint-Germain winger rewarded his new head coach with a goal in the first half to give Tottenham a 2-0 lead.

After the game, Moura posted a tweet regarding a chance he missed during the game. A fan wrote: 'Can we take a minute to discuss your miss please Lucas?'

The Brazilian responded with a grinning emoji. Another Spurs supporter replied with: 'That night in Amsterdam more than makes up for the miss!!'

And the 27-year-old said: 'Hahahahha thank you.'
