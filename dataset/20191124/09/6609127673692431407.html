'YOU GOT ME'

Rangers fan pranks Celtic ace Bolingoli with bum pic after star slid into girlfriend’s DMs

24 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Aidan Scott

A RANGERS fan has pranked Boli Bolingoli with a picture of his BUM - after the Celtic star slid into his girlfriend's DMs.

The Belgian defender contacted fitness fanatic Emma Lonsdale, 23, in the early hours of Friday morning when he sent her a loveheart emoji on Instagram.

He complimented her on her physique but the Hoops ace got more than he bargained for when Emma's boyfriend Richard Lind, 29, replied later that day.

During the hilarious conversation, Richard - posing as Emma - told Bolingoli that she'd just finished off a session at the gym.

When Bolingoli said she looked like she was working hard on her bum, Richard asked if he wanted to see a better picture of it.

He then sent the defender an image of his OWN bum, but with Bolingoli then questioning whether it was Emma, Richard sent another full-body image to reveal it was actually him.

Speaking exclusively to SunSport, the couple, from Glasgow, lifted the lid on the funny interaction and highlighted that the Belgian even shared a laugh with them about it.

Richard said: "I was at work and Emma sent me a screenshot and we just kind of played it from there.

"We just carried it on as we got home and then just sent the photos!

"We didn't want to put it on anything but then I started getting random messages from a few boys saying it was in their work group chat, and that it was going wild."

Emma added: "Richard had sent it to his small group chat and they've obviously sent it on and it's been spread about.

"I've been getting messages off people that I don't even know like, 'this is so funny, it's in my group chat and I didn't realise it's your boyfriend'.

"I've been like, 'what?'"

Richard, a big Rangers fan, couldn't resist the chance to have a laugh at a Celtic player's expense, but praised Bolingoli for being a good sport.

He said: "I'm a Rangers fan so I thought right, we'll have a laugh with this.

"The majority of my pals are Rangers fans so it was just about having a laugh, and it's just went wild since then.

"He was a good sport with it. He'll get pounded for it for a laugh and that'll be it. If it got done to me I'd be absolutely howling!"

Images of the conversation have been liked nearly 3,000 times on Twitter while Emma has gained around 200 more followers on her Instagram account.

And while it has mostly been Rangers fans reacting to it, they aren't the only ones.

Richard added: "People have been saying it's brilliant. A few Rangers pages have shared it and hundreds of Rangers fans have been liking it.

"A Bolingoli page actually liked it as well!"

SunSport have contacted Celtic and Bolingoli for comment.
