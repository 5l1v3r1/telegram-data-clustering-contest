Don’t be ashamed of what you do for a living – Edem

24 Nov 2019, 09:16 by MyNewsGH

Rapper, Edem has asked the Youth never to be ashamed of what feeds them.

Most people feel ashamed of the jobs they do and that is breeding laziness among the youth of Ghana.

But in a tweet, Edem has advised that young people should always be proud of what they do for a living because no one will feed them when they go hungry.

He said ” DONT BE ASHAME OF WHAT YOU DO FOR A LIVING…NOBODY WOULD FEED YOU IF U GO BROKE”.
