Bellator London: Patrice Evra's hilarious commentary of Michael Venom Page's KO

24 Nov 2019, 09:36

Patrice Evra was a captivated spectator when Michael Venom Page took less than two minutes to knock out Giovanni Melillo at Bellator London on Saturday night.

The Manchester United legend met MVP for the first time before the fight and watched his new friend in action in the main event at the SSE Wembley Arena.

Page dominated Melillo from the first bell and stopped his opponent with a quick overhand right in the first round.

Evra was watching the action unfold on TV and released a hilarious blow-by-blow account of the fight on his Instagram story.

‘Couldn’t come to the fight but I’m watching it on TV. Make it quick, I’m tired, I need to go to sleep,’ Evra said as MVP made his entrance.

After the British welterweight made short work of Melillo, Evra celebrated MVP’s latest victory .
