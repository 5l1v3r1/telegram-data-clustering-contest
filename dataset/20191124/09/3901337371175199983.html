Mourinho Apologises To Spurs Fans After Winning Start

24 Nov 2019, 09:00 by SL International

Despite getting a win in his first Tottenham Hotspur game, manager Jose Mourinho sent an apology to the club's supporters after the game.

Read: Mourinho Off To Flying Start At Spurs

Mourinho began his Spurs tenure with a 3-2 victory over London rivals West Ham United on Saturday afternoon.

The win ended a barren of poor results on the road in the Premier League season, but Mourinho felt the need to apologise for not going to the away stand at the end of the game.

"At the end of the game I didn’t want to go to our fans because I felt I shouldn’t,’ Mourinho told Spurs TV.

"It’s about the players, not about me.

"Apologies for that, I stayed away.

"But I appreciate the few thousand who were there behind the team and I think really enjoying and understanding the difficulties and supporting, and probably now as happy as we are."

Mourinho will shift focus to Wednesday's UEFA Champions League encounter against Olympiakos at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium.
