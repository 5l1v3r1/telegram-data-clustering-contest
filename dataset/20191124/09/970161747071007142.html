Michael van Gerwen 9-darter matches Phil Taylor world record

24 Nov 2019, 09:00

Michael van Gerwen’s 9-darter against Adrian Lewis at the Players Championship Finals was the 22nd of his career, going level with Phil Taylor at the top of the list of perfect legs.

The world number one fired in 180, 177, 144 in the eighth leg of his second round clash in Minehead, going on to beat Jackpot 6-5.

The few seconds of magic came out of nowhere, with the Dutchman not playing at his best beforehand, ending the match with an average of just 96.40.

It was the first televised 9-darter of 2019, with the last coming at last year’s Grand Slam of Darts when Dimitri van den Bergh managed perfection against Stephen Bunting.

There have been plenty away from the cameras this year, though, with 33 perfect legs being thrown on the main tour in non-televised events.
