Woman, 75, dead and man, 79, fighting for life after hit-and-run

24 Nov 2019, 09:44

Police are hunting for a driver who killed a 75-year-old woman and left a 79-year-old man fighting for his life in a hit-and-run.

They were hit by a dark-coloured car near the Punch Bowl pub on Jesmond Road, Newcastle, just after 7.20pm on Saturday.

The woman was pronounced dead at the scene while the man was rushed to hospital with potentially life-threatening injuries.

Officers are looking for the vehicle and its driver and have urged witnesses to come forward.

Sergeant Matt Sykes said: ‘This has been a terrible collision where a woman has sadly lost her life and a man is in hospital with very serious injuries.

‘Our thoughts are with their family and friends at this time.

‘Enquiries are now being carried out by our officers to establish exactly what has happened – but we still need to speak to people who witnessed the collision or have any information about this collision.

‘If anybody saw what happened, or thinks they may have dashcam footage that could assist with us, I would ask them to contact police as soon as possible.’

The victims’ family are being supported by specialist officers.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Northumbria Police on 101 quoting reference 883 23/11/19 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
