Larkhall woman electronically tagged for brandishing a knife

Kayleigh Patrick admitted shouting abuse, making threats and brandishing a blade.

24 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Hamilton Advertiser, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/hamilton-advertiser/
