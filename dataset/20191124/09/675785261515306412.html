Paul Hollywood strikes again with new barmaid lover and holidays in same resort he got married

Paul Hollywood whisked Melissa Spalding off to the very same Greek resort he married ex Alex

24 Nov 2019, 09:03 by Lucy Buckland, http://www.facebook.com/MirrorCeleb

Paul Hollywood is certainly a creature of habit - after landing a new younger barmaid lover he decided to whisk her off to his favourite holiday spot - where he got married 20 years ago.

Blue-eyed baker Paul is now reportedly dating barmaid Melissa Spalding - who at 36 is 12 years older than his ex Summer Monteys-Fullam.

The dewy eyed pair were spotted holding hands at the Paphos resort Annabelle in Cyprus after booking an exclsuive sea view suite.

Melissa is in charge of the Chequers Inn in Smarden, Kent, near Paul's former home he shared with Summer.

It is believed Summer confronted Paul about his friendship with Melissa before they split.

Paul was head chef at the resort 23 years ago when he met ex wife Alex - they wed two years later in 1998.

She described their time in Cyprus as "incredibly happy" back in 2013.

A source told The Sun: “It is a bit odd that he brought his new girlfriend to a place he had so many memories with his ex, including where their actual wedding took place."

“They went for a Japanese meal one evening and came back holding hands. I think they want to stay low key so they haven’t been talking to guests.

“They will be in the honeymoon stage. They certainly don’t seem to have eyes for anyone else.”

Paul split from girlfriend Summer in August. Mirror Online has contacted Paul for a comment.
