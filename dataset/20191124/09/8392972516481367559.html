Dunelm is selling a hot water bottle that wraps around your neck for £7

Comes with a 'Teddy Fleece' covering

24 Nov 2019, 09:09 by Paul Hirons

As the weather gets wetter and colder, the struggle to keep warm is real.

Now Dunelm is selling a 'teddy fleece' hot water bottle that wraps around your neck for £7 that looks as though it will do the job.

Members of the Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains were the first to spot this winter warmer, with some recommending the product to others.

"Bought this teddy bear neck water bottle today," one member wrote.

It's lovely, fluffy and so warm.

Another said: "It's lovely, fluffy and so warm. For someone who suffers bad neck pain this is great and a bargain at £7 from Dunelm."

"Also comes in another three colours. They have teddy bear fleece throws as well for £10," a third wrote.
