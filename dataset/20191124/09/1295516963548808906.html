Suicide Squad's David Ayer Releases New Photo of Joker and Harley Quinn

24 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Math Erao

David Ayer posted a never-before-seen photo of the Joker and Harley Quinn from his film Suicide Squad.

While there's a new Suicide Squad film on the horizon, Ayer wanted to throw back to his stab at the franchise. In the photo, posted to his Instagram, we see Margot Robbie and Jared Leto during a makeup and wardrobe test for the film. Check it out:

Ayer won't be back for the new movie, as James Gunn will work with Robbie for The Suicide Squad, the follow-up to/reboot of the 2016 film. As for Leto, he won't be involved in the project, as Warner Bros. seems to have firmly moved on from his taken on the Joker. And with Joaquin Phoenix's Clown Prince of Crown breaking records left and right, it's no surprise that DC doesn't want to juggle two versions of the character.

But while this Joker won't be getting the last laugh, the new film is said to be even funnier than the first, according to Robbie at least. Luckily, fans of her take on Harley Quinn will get their fill. Along with The Suicide Squad, Robbie will reprise the role in next year's Birds of Prey.

Written and directed by James Gunn, The Suicide Squad is set to hit theaters Aug. 6, 2021. Several cast members from 2016's Suicide Squad will reprise their roles, including Margot Robbie as Harley Quinn, Viola Davis as Amanda Waller, Joel Kinnaman as Rick Flagg and Jai Courtney as Captain Boomerang. Also joining the cast are David Dastmalchian, John Cena, Joaquin Cosio, Nathan Fillion, Mayling Ng, Flula Borg, Sean Gunn, Juan Diego Botto, Storm Reid, Pete Davidson, Taika Waititi, Alice Braga, Steve Agee, Tinashe Kajese, Daniela Melchior, Peter Capaldi, Julio Ruiz, Jennifer Holland, Idris Elba and Michael Rooker.
