Ruth Jones switches on Christmas lights as Gavin And Stacey's Nessa

24 Nov 2019, 09:39

Gavin And Stacey co-creator Ruth Jones switched on the Barry Christmas lights in Wales in the character of Nessa and obviously, it was everything.

Wearing her iconic black cropped hair and dressed in typical Nessa gear, the 53-year-old actress filled her speech with classic lines from the BBC comedy much to the delight of the excited audience.

Taking to the stage, the star asked the packed crowd ‘what’s occurin’?’ before adding that it was ‘crackin’ to see everyone gathered around for the special occasion.

‘There are some lookers out here tonight,’ Nessa continued. ‘A few exes in the crowd there I can see.’

Honestly, this has got us buzzing tenfold for the hotly-anticipated Gavin And Stacey Christmas special that’s due to land on our television screens on 25 December.

Ruth and fellow showrunner James Corden confirmed that the hit series would be making a dramatic comeback for a festive treat after a nine-year hiatus.

‘Over the last 10 years we’ve talked a lot about Gavin And Stacey – where they might be today and what their lives might look like,’ they said in a joint statement.

‘And so in secret we took the plunge and wrote this one-hour special.

‘We’ve loved revisiting Barry and Essex again and bringing the characters back together has been a joy.’

Earlier this month the cast of Gavin And Stacey were all smiles as they arrived for the press screening for the hotly-anticipated Christmas special in London.

Actress Alison Steadman, who plays meme queen Pam Shipman, said that returning for the one-off episode was like a ‘family reunion’.

‘[Filming] was brilliant,’ she recalled. ‘It was so good to be back on Barry Island! I really was quite nervous about it because expectations are so high.

She continued to OK!: ‘I thought, “Can I do it again? Can I get the character back properly?” But as soon as we were all back together it was just like a family reunion. At the end we were all really sad to say goodbye.’

Not long to go now, folks!

Gavin And Stacey returns on Christmas Day on BBC One.
