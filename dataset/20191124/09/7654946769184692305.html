Rudy Giuliani says his safe is stuffed with ‘files’ on the Bidens

24 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Laura Italiano

Rudy Giuliani said in a tweet Saturday that he has a safe stuffed with “files” on the Bidens.

“The statement I’ve made several times of having an insurance policy, if thrown under the bus, is sarcastic & relates to the files in my safe about the Biden Family’s 4 decades monetizing of his office,” President Trump’s personal lawyer tweeted Saturday.
