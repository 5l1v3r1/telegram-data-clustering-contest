Lucky Irish punter bags €148,259 after matching five numbers and bonus in Saturday's Lotto draw

They'll be looking forward to Christmas

24 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Edel Hughes

A lucky punter will be looking forward to Christmas after bagging a cool €148,259 in Saturday night's Lotto draw.

The player netted the windfall after matching five numbers and the bonus last night.

The numbers drawn were: 4, 15, 20, 21, 34, 39 and bonus number 19.

National Lottery bosses have yet to reveal where the winning ticket was sold.

There was no winner of the €5.1m jackpot, however, a total of over 101,000 players picked up prizes.

There was no winner of the Lotto Plus 1 top prize of €1,000,000 but one player did pick up the next highest win of €5,000.

The numbers drawn were: 1, 8, 11, 15, 17, 29 and bonus number 33.

There was also no winner of the Lotto Plus 2 top prize, the numbers drawn were 5, 29, 31, 32, 36, 47 and bonus number 23.

In the Raffle draw, 92 players won €500 each. The winning Raffle number was 0223.
