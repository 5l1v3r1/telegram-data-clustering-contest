Bigg Boss 13: 'Sidharth Shukla Was A Torture Machine, Made Me Suffer In Pain' Reveals Evicted Contestant Khesari Yadav

24 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Amruta Patel

Bigg Boss 13 evicted contestant, Khesari Lal Yadav, revealed that Sidharth Shukla was mean to him and literally tortured him in the first two weeks. The Bhojpuri star had entered the house as a wild card entrant; he was nominated by the housemates for his least contribution in the house on Weekend Ka Vaar

Bigg Boss 13’s latest eviction of Khesari Yadav came as a shock to all the viewers. The Bhojpuri star was elected by the housemates for contributing the least in the house. Along with him actress Rashami Desai too was elected with maximum votes, but seems like destiny favoured Rashami, resulting into Khesari’s eviction from the house. On being out from the Bigg Boss 13 house, the actor interacted with leading publication and shelled out some shocking deets about the housemate Sidharth Shukla.

The Bhojuri actor who entered the house as a wild card entrant, revealed of being tortured by Sidharth and held him responsible for suffering in the house. In conversation with Times of India, he said, “For two weeks he tortured me a lot. It affected me. He created a lot of problems. He used to make faces and became a torturing machine. In the third week, he tried to understand me and we were bonding.
