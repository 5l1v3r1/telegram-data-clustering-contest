Wolves make January transfer enquiry over Arsenal target - reports

Latest Wolves transfer news sees WWFC linked with Southampton player

24 Nov 2019, 15:39 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Wolverhampton Wanderers are interested in signing Southampton defender Cedric Soares, according to reports.

The 28-year-old has made only five Premier League appearances for the Saints this season and is expected to be available when the transfer window re-opens in January.

Cedric is out of contract at the end of this campaign and has already informed the club of his desire to move on.

According to TeamTalk, Southampton are willing to cash in to avoid losing him for nothing and Wolves have made an enquiry over the possibility of doing a deal.

Sporting director Kevin Thelwell recently told the club’s website. “We’re always going to try and do more to take us that little bit further, and hopefully we can address that in the next window.

“I don’t think we need a revolution at all, we’ve got a very strong foundation and a very strong base.

“We’re working across a load of leagues, continually reviewing where we are and evaluating what we need.

“And then talking to the coaching staff about what positions they need before making some hard and fast decisions as we get closer to the window.”

Arsenal and West Ham United are also said to be tracking Cedric and the latter are ready to make a formal bid for the full-back in January, the report adds.

Cedric – who has played 33 times for Portugal – arrived on the south coast from Sporting four years ago and has made 126 appearances.
