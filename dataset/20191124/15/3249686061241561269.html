Rapper DinDin shares his longing for Goo Hara on Instagram

24 Nov 2019, 15:20 by honeycider

On the evening of November 24, singer Goo Hara was found deceased in her private residence.

Since then, many artists have adjusted their schedules in respect and mourning for the young singer's passing.

DinDin, a rapper and close friend with Hara took to his Instagram to share his longing and mourning for her passing. He posted a photo of her bright smile and said,

"Hara, when I started getting noticed, you worried about me and told me to get in touch with you if I struggled. You are so beautiful and brilliant, but I'm sorry I didn't do anything and help you. I am so sorry. I'm so sorry that I should have helped you a bit. I'm so angry and I hate the real world so I hope you are happy where you are. Sorry. Thanks."
