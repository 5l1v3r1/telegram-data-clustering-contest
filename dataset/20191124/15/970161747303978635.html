Spoilers: Keanu's EastEnders Christmas exit confirmed in new pictures?

24 Nov 2019, 15:00

EastEnders cast have been filming what look set to be Keanu Taylor’s (Danny Walters) exit from the soap this Christmas. It’s no secret that Danny has called time on his tenure in the soap – but is he fleeing after Phil Mitchell (Steve McFadden) prepares to kill him?

It has already been confirmed that Phil will discover the truth about Keanu’s affair with Sharon (Letitia Dean) in a dramatic festive showdown. Danny has promised ‘drama and stunts’ for the big day and pictures recently showed him caught up in an armed hostage situation with Martin Fowler (James Bye).

A desperate Martin is seen brandishing a gun and holding Linda Carter (Kellie Bright) captive. Keanu becomes involved in the showdown and given that Ben (Max Bowden) previously had a hold over Martin – and Phil still does – it could be that he has been instructed to kill Keanu.

With a shot ringing out, it looked to all that Keanu was destined to be killed off. But these brand new pictures show another twist to the tail as Keanu, under the guise of a baseball cap (subtle) says a furtive goodbye to his mum Karen (Lorraine Stanley).

The stars were filming the scenes at Stanstead Airport and it appears to suggest that Keanu will somehow manage to survive the wrath of a seething Phil. A source has suggested that Martin has set up Keanu’s getaway – is he set to lie about his mission being accomplished?

Martin might have done some dark things recently but murder might be beyond even his conscience.

Whatever happens, the dramatic storyline will see the end of Keanu – and his mum Karen sees him off. Whether these are his final scenes or another twist follows remains to be seen.

But there are other storyline developments to take place with Louise (Tilly Keeper) set to give birth to Keanu’s baby and a pregnant Sharon still carrying his offspring.
