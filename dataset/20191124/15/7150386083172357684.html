NCP MLA Dhananjay Munde Rejects Rumours, Says ‘I’m With NCP, Sharad Pawar’

The speculation of Dhananjay Munde supporting Ajit Pawar went high as he stayed away from media glare on Saturday.

24 Nov 2019, 15:34

New Delhi: Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) MLA Dhananjay Munde, who was believed to have sided with Ajit Pawar to back him for government formation in Maharashtra, on Sunday broke silence on the issue and said he is with the NCP and party chief Sharad Pawar. He also urged all not to spread rumours in his name. “I am with the party, I am with Pawar saheb. Please don’t spread rumours,” he said in a statement.
