And among the celebrities hoping to take home the ballroom crown is Saffron Barker.

Saffron and pro partner AJ Pritchard had established themselves as one of the frontrunning couples for Strictly 2019, earning some terrific scores for their dancing, particularly their ballroom routines, so it came as a huge shock when they landed in the bottom two last week.

Other couples still in the running include Kelvin & Oti, Alex & Neil and Chris & Karen and this weekend's results will air on BBC1 on Sunday night.

Who is Saffron Barker?

Saffron is a Youtuber from Brighton. The now 20 year old first started uploading videos to the platform while she was still at school and things took off in a big way with her video Types of Boyfriends.

Uploaded in 2016, the jokey video has now amassed over 10 million views.

Saffron now has over three million social media followers, with many fans also following her on Instagram, where she posts sponsored content, as well as updates on her daily life and of course, life as a Strictly contestant.
