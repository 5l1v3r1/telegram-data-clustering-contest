24 Nov 2019, 15:19 by Miles O'Leary

Torpoint - it's the patch on the west Tamar side of Cornwall that arguably gets forgotten about.

Loads of us moan about the ferry crossing - but perhaps we think less about the face it has a really strong community bursting with pride.

Way back in the early to mid 2000s, we went out capturing the moments that defined the parish neighbourhood.

So many lovely faces feature in our archives and so it made sense to rustle up a gallery honouring them all.
