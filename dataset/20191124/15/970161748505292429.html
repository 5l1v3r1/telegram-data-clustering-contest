Fans pay tribute after K-pop star Goo Hara is found dead aged 28

24 Nov 2019, 15:12

Tributes have poured in for K-pop star Goo Hara, after she was found dead at the age of 28.

Police say that the singer, who was a former member of the girl group Kara, was found unresponsive in her home in Cheongsam on Sunday evening, with the cause of her death currently being investigated.

Since the news of Hara’s death broke, fans have taken to social media to share tributes for the South Korean singer.

One fan shared on Twitter: ‘Sending our prayers and deepest condolences to Goo Hara’s family, friends and fans. It’s still hard to believe.

‘Rest In Peace Hara, you will always be remembered #RIPHara.’

Another tweeted: ‘A woman full of talent with a big heart. She was such a strong person and the world didn’t deserve her.

‘May you rest in peace Goo Hara, we will never forget you.’
