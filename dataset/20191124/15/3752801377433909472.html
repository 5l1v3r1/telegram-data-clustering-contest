An end of an era: Bank of Lisbon – a site of tragedy – destroyed

24 Nov 2019, 15:23 by Azarrah Karrim

It was an emotional moment for onlookers as they watched the demolition of the Bank of Lisbon building in Johannesburg on Sunday. Last year firefighters Simphiwe Moropane, Mduduzi Ndlovu and Khathutshelo Muedi died as they battled to put out a fire that had  engulfed the building in September 2018.

As the building came down in a loud rumble, the three were in the thoughts of those watching. 

"It was tragic what happened to those men," one onlooker remarked as she wiped a tear from her eye and shook her head.

The Gauteng provincial government made the decision to implode the building after a structural assessment was conducted, following the fatal fire.
