The Witcher's Episode Titles, Cryptic Synopses, Revealed

24 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Evangelia Galanis

Netflix's highly-anticipated adaptation of The Witcher features a fresh take on Polish author Andrzej Sapowski's hit novel series. As the release date nears closer, more news hits the forefront, letting fans know exactly what to expect.

The Witcher's official Twitter account released the names for all eight episode titles. The tweet reads, "Did someone, somewhere, summon a djinn to then wish for all 8 chapter titles? Here you go. Now does anyone know a doctor to check Jaskier's vitals? A monster slain, a butcher named."
