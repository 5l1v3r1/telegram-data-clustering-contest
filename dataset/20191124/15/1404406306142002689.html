Tay-K Speaks From Prison, Says Media is Spreading "Complete Lies" About Him

24 Nov 2019, 15:28 by VladTV

Tay-K has been silent on the issues he’s been facing, especially after being hit with a 55-year prison sentence. Now, the rapper has taken to Twitter to express his thoughts on the entire situation after being silent for so long.

Tay-K said “I wanna apologize to all my fans and supporters for letting my management and label convince me that it wouldn’t be a good decision to speak out against the way media makes me look and the complete lies they spread..”

He continued on saying “but ‘I’m gonna make sure the truth about my life and my character gets out with or without management or a label. And whoever don’t like it f**k you..” The rapper went on to say the media is portraying him as a monster and also went on to apologize for the way he’s presented himself publicly. Take a look above.
