Gardai investigating alleged assault on woman in Howth, Dublin as pavement closed off

A pavement was cordoned off for several hours

24 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Darragh Berry

Gardai are investigating an alleged serious assault on a woman in Dublin.

The alleged incident took place in the Howth area late on Saturday night.

A pavement in the area was cordoned off from 1am until around 12pm on Sunday with garda presence there for the duration.

A Garda spokesman confirmed that no arrests had been made in relation to the attack and investigations are ongoing, Dublin Live reports.
