Coach O Has Brutally Honest Comment About Arkansas Following Win

24 Nov 2019, 04:10 by Andrew Holleran

LSU notched its 11th win of the regular season tonight, as the Tigers blew out Arkansas, 56-20, at Tiger Stadium in Baton Rouge.

There were no big celebrations for LSU following tonight’s win. It was business as usual for the Tigers, who have bigger things to accomplish than a home win over the Razorbacks.

LSU head coach Ed Orgeron went further into detail about the lack of a celebration.

He did not hold back.

“There wasn’t going to be celebration for beating Arkansas, they haven’t beaten anyone in a long time,” Coach O told reporters.
