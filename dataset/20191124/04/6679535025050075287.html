The man behind the mission in A.P.

24 Nov 2019, 04:18 by Staff ReporterGUNTUR

Adviser to Government of Andhra Pradesh, AP Rythu Samstha, T. Vijaya Kumar is the prime mover behind the implementation of Zero budget Natural Farming (ZBNF) in the State.

As the man who has driven the Society for Elimination for Rural Poverty (SERP) as the CEO for 10 years, Mr. Vijaya Kumar had launched a state-wide programme for empowerment of rural women through self help groups. After his superannuation, Mr. Vijaya Kumar has joined the Government of Andhra Pradesh as Advisor in the implementation of ZBNF.

12-year warning

"The impact of climate change is being felt everywhere and the latest analysis shows that if we do not act now, we cannot reduce carbon emissions. Moreover, within 12 years, we cannot reduce carbon emissions which have an adverse impact on food security," Mr. Vijaya Kumar told The Hindu.

He began by holding a series of awareness meetings on natural farming in which Subhash Palekar, often referred as the father of ZBNF, was present. The idea of natural farming soon caught the attention of farmers. So much so, the number of farmers practising the natural farming rose to 5.8 lakh farmers from 40,000 in two years.

"We have registered an increase of 8 per cent of farmers practising natural farming, while all over the world, there is just 0.5 per cent increase," said Mr. Vijaya Kumar.
