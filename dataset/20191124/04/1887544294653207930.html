Toronto Fire captain injured in downtown blaze out of hospital

24 Nov 2019, 04:11 by Bryann Aguilar, CP24.com

A Toronto firefighter who was injured while battling a downtown blaze earlier this month has been released from the hospital.

“Now this is great news! This is @Toronto_Fire Captain Warren (seated) and Firefighter Leimonis (crutches) leaving the Hospital together,” tweeted Toronto Fire Chief Matthew Pegg on Saturday evening. “As they explained, ‘we came in here together and we will go out together too’. On the road to recovery!”
