Priyanka Chopra says that she will never dare to sing a duet with hubby Nick Jonas; here's why

24 Nov 2019, 04:07 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

In spite of singing various popular singles in the past, the international icon Priyanka Chopra Jonas confesses that she would never want to sing a duet with hubby Nick Jonas who is a well-known American singer.

Although she has already featured in his music video, crooning along with him is not something she imagines to do. Recently while talking on a chat show the actress said that she loves music but she doesn't have that kind of education in the field. Therefore she won't dare to sing in front of Nick.

Furthermore, she added, Nick is a child prodigy, he makes songs, writes lyrics and composes music. While she just likes music; hence she will never try a duet with him.

She also said that the pop-singer does a special performance for her 'every morning'. PeeCee mentioned that every morning as she wakes up, she doesn't have to turn on the radio because Nick is playing the piano and singing for her live.

On the work front, Meanwhile, on the work front, Priyanka was last seen in Shonali Bose's 'The Sky is Pink' opposite Farhan Akhtar, Zaira Wasim and Rohit Saraf.
