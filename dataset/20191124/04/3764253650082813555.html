Real Madrid vs Real Sociedad: Los Blancos cruise to 3-1 at Santiago Bernabeu

24 Nov 2019, 04:15

- Real Madrid vs Real Sociedad La Liga clash ended in favour of Los Blancos

- A goal each from Benzema, Modric and Federico Valverde saw the hosts emerge victorious

- Real are still joint league leaders with Barcelona as the chase for the title hots up

Real Madrid survived Real Sociedad's scare after coming from a goal down to beat their visitors 3-1 at the Santiago Bernabeu in late La Liga fixture.

Los Blancos conceded just after two minutes of action through a Willian Jose's stunning strike to hand the away team the lead.

The visitors tried as much as possible to double their lead but all efforts failed to materialise until the home tea gained momentum.
