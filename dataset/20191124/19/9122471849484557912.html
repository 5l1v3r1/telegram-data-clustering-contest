The Week 14 AP Poll Top 25 Has Been Released

24 Nov 2019, 19:06 by Matt Hladik

With the release of the latest AP Poll Top 25 moments ago, both major polls have been updated heading into Week 14.

Several teams benefited from Oregon’s upset loss at Arizona State, moving up within the top 10 as a result.

Additionally, Michigan is back in the top 10 after thrashing Indiana, setting up a showdown between top 10 teams when the Wolverines host Ohio State on Saturday.

The full Coaches’ Poll top 25 can be seen here. You can view the AP poll on its website here.

This week’s College Football Playoff rankings will be revealed Tuesday night. The only real drama in the top four will be seeing if Ohio State’s win over Penn State was enough to move them past LSU and into the top spot.

Outside of that, we’ll see how the committee sorts out the 5-through-7 spots, as those teams are the ones knocking on the door heading into championship weekend.

Next weekend, we’ll finish off the 2019 regular season with several marquee rivalry matchups, including Ohio State-Michigan, Alabama-Auburn and Oklahoma-Oklahoma State.
