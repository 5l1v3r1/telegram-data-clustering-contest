Homeowners urged to check mortgages by Martin Lewis

Failing to check your mortgage could see you losing thousands

24 Nov 2019, 19:38 by Rachel Pugh

Money Saving Expert's Martin Lewis has urged homeowners to check their mortgages or risk losing out on thousands of pounds.

Speaking on ITV's Good Morning Britain, he explained how mortgage rates are currently the best they've been.

According to Manchester Evening News , he said mortgages should be checked to ensure homeowners are getting the best possible deal.

He said: "Longterm prediction of interest rates have dropped, and those are the ones that mortgage rates are set on.

"Now I'll give you some of the mortgage rates - please see this as a totem and not recommendations, because they've all got specific terms.

"We've got two-year fixes at 1.05% with Halifax, five-year fixes at 1.44% with TSB, and a two-year tracker with Halifax under 1%. It's the first time we've seen rates like that since 2017."

"My clarion call to everybody out there is that you need to check your mortgage now to see if you could get a cheaper deal and possibly save thousands."
