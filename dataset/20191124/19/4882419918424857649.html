Celta earned hard-fought win over Villarreal 1-3 at Estadio de La Ceramica

Javier Calleja's squad let the match slip from their hands against Celta at Estadio de La Ceramica

24 Nov 2019, 19:44 by SPORT.es

Villarreal on Sunday lost to Celta on a home defeat at Estadio de La Ceramica. Villarreal and Celta arrived at this game after suffering a defeat in their previous league game. Villarreal faced the match with a desire to make up their league after losing against Mallorca 3-1 while Celta lost by a result of 4-1 in the previous match against Barcelona. Following today's result, Villarreal and Celta occupy position 12th (18 points) and 18th (12 points) respectively after playing a total of 14 matches.

The first half ended with little further action at either end and goalless.

Celta started the second half on an intensified spirit, thanks to a goal from Pione Sisto 54 minutes in the game. However, Villarreal found an equaliser 1-1 thanks to a goal from Samuel Chukwueze in the 59th minute. Celta got the go-ahead goal thanks to a goal from Iago Aspas in the 80th minute to establish the 1-2. Later, Celta scored again and increased their advantage 1-3 thanks to another goal from Iago Aspas, scoring his second goal in the 90th minute just before the end of the second half, which saw the second half to a 1-3.

As for substitutions, both coaches used full substitutions. For Villarreal Moi Gomez, Carlos Bacca and Javier Ontiveros came on for Vicente Iborra, Karl Toko Ekambi and Xavier Quintilla, while Celta brought on Santi Mina, Fran Beltran and David Costas to replace Brais Mendez, Pape Diop and Denis Suarez.

The referee booked Vicente Iborra, Ramiro Funes Mori and Pau Torres from Villarreal, while for Celta Sergio Alvarez and Santi Mina saw a yellow card as well.

Villarreal will play against Valencia at away stadium, while Celta will face Real Valladolid at home stadium.
