Video: Texas A&M Should Be Very Afraid Of Coach O

24 Nov 2019, 19:22 by Andrew Holleran

One team is standing in the way of a perfect regular season for Ed Orgeron and the LSU Tigers. It just so happens to be the team that beat them in the wildest game of the 2018 season.

LSU is set to play Texas A&M on Saturday evening. The Tigers lost to the Aggies, 74-72, in seven overtimes last season.

Coach O seems determined to not let that happen again.

“There’s no question. There’s no question about that,” Orgeron said after LSU’s win over Arkansas. “It’s going to be on.”

“I’ll never forget that game last year. We’re going to be ready.”
