No injuries after garage fire in Kitchener (5 photos)

It happened Sunday afternoon on Frederick Street

24 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Ariel Deutschmann

Four Kitchener residents are safe after a fire broke out Sunday afternoon. 

An estimated 25 firefighters responded to the scene on Frederick Street. 

Assistant Platoon Chief Jeff Noble says the fire started in the garage, where propane tanks and kerosene were kept.

He says because of the accelerants, the fire spread to the home's exterior.

The cause of the fire is believed to be smoking related.

After the fire was contained, officials say it has caused an estimated $50,000 worth of property damage. 

No injuries have been reported.

The family will be displaced until the damage is fully assessed and repaired.
