The Betting Line For The Alabama-Auburn Game Is Set

24 Nov 2019, 19:37 by Tzvi Machlin

The Iron Bowl is next weekend but the stakes of the annual Alabama-Auburn game are far lower than usual. Both teams are out of the SEC title race, but Alabama still needs a win to potentially get into the College Football Playoff.

The two teams are coming off dominating wins over FCS foes. They beat Western Carolina and Samford respectively by a combined score of 118-3.

But the bettors seem to think that we’re in for a very close game.

According to CircaSports via Michael Bratton, Alabama will open as a three point favorite on the road against Auburn.
