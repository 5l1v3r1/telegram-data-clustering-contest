Liam Payne shows off cooking skills as he treats Maya Henry to Sunday roast

24 Nov 2019, 19:55

Liam Payne is clearly a man of many talents, as he revealed his surprise cooking skills this weekend while making Sunday lunch for his girlfriend, Maya Henry, and a group of friends.

The Strip That Down singer invited his pals over to his mansion this afternoon for a pool party, and while he has the means to hire an expensive chef and catering company, Liam decided to DIY the Sunday roast.

And we have to admit, it looked pretty tasty.

In an Instagram video, Liam is seen chopping carrots and boiling a large pot of potatoes while explaining his cooking process.

‘We are having a little cheeky pool party at the house,’ he revealed.

While chopping carrots, he offered some pretty savvy advice, warning: ‘Mind your fingers, don’t try this at home children unless you’re an expert chopper like me and you end up with a nice chopped carrot.’

With the carrots and potatoes cooking nicely on the hob, it was time to crack open the champagne and it’s safe to say, Liam doesn’t do things by halves.

In his glass wine store, the popstar brings out a magnum-sized champagne bottle and joked to his friend: ‘It’s medium sized.’

If that’s what he drinks for a casual Sunday roast, we’d love to see what Liam brings out for a real party.

‘I reckon I can open it without it going anywhere actually,’ he boasted before impressively managing only a little spill after popping the cork.

Offering more pearls of wisdom, he then quipped: ‘You’re only as good as your last meal in cooking, I think.’

Watch out Gordon Ramsay, ‘Chef P’ is in the house.

In photos from the meal, Maya, 19, is seen sweetly cupping Liam’s face at the dinner table before the group pose for a snap.

Our Sunday lunch wasn’t nearly as fun.

Liam and Maya are pretty much inseparable these days, and he recently praised the model for putting a smile on his face.

The former One Direction singer wrote in a post: ‘Sometimes I don’t recognise this happy guy… sure glad you brought him back though…
