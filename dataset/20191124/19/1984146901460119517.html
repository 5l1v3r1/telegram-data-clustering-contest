Strictly Come Dancing result sees Saffron & AJ go home and fans are fuming Karim was in bottom two

Strictly Come Dancing's results show aired on Sunday night with another couple leaving the competition

24 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Simon Duke

The shock Strictly Come Dancing results just keep on coming and fans of the show can't get their heads around this week's bottom two.

This year is definitely the year of the surprise results on Strictly , with viewers still scratching their hands over Dev Griffin's premature exit from the BBC programme and expressing similar shock at frontrunner Michelle Visage's departure from the ballroom last week.

The latest dancefloor casualty is social media star Saffron Barker, who has been voted off the show alongside her professional partner AJ Pritchard.

But it is the fact that they were in the dance-off with Karim & Amy that has angered Strictly fans the most, with many furious about it as soon as the Strictly spoiler leaked late on on Saturday.

Karim is one of the strongest contenders and highest scorers of the series so far, with the CBBC presenter consistently near the top of the leaderboard.

One fan tweeted "Karim in the dance off! I am shook!!!!," and, echoing those sentiments, another reacted "Can’t believe Karim in the dance off. Why???" with a third fan puzzled at the outcome of the public vote, posting "Kinda shocked about the results tbf - was thinking Karim would never be in the bottom."

Other viewers think voters are wrongly backing the 'underdogs' this year, with one ranting "People in this country need to get over their obsession with the “underdog”. It’s a sickness. If both Kelvin and Karim don’t make the final and one of them win, strictly will officially be a joke," and another writing "KARIM of all people was in the dance off! This is why voting matters and the favourites are most definitely not safe."

On Saturday's live show, Karim was lambasted by judge Craig Revel-Horwood for lacking emotion not just in his dance this week, but throughout the whole of the competition, something else viewers have taken exception to.
