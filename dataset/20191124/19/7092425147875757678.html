Man Utd player ratings as Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's side draw 3-3 with Sheffield United

24 Nov 2019, 19:27 by Buzz Contributor

Manchester United were held by Sheffield United at Bramall Lane, piling more pressure on an under-fire Ole Gunnar Solskjaer

Manchester United were minutes away from coming back from two goals behind to beat Sheffield United at Bramall Lane.

But in the 90th minute, Oli McBurnie popped up to level proceedings, ending a dramatic afternoon of football.

The Red Devils were two goals down after John Fleck and Lys Mousset found the net, as they struggled to lay a glove on the newly-promoted side.

But while Solskjaer's men were lacklustre for the first 70 minutes, they came alive in the closing stages, scoring three in quick succession to turn the tide completely.

But in the dying seconds, Sheffield United scored from close range to shatter the hearts of the travelling fans.

Solskjaer's side deserve credit for their initial comeback - but who impressed and who flopped in the epic clash?
