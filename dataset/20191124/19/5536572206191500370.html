DJ Moore Looks To Have Broken His Arm During Panthers 2-Point Conversion (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 19:42 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Carolina Panthers aggressiveness may have cost them big time after DJ Moore went down with an injury.

It happened shortly after the team scored a touchdown before half where they decided to go for 2 in an attempt to tie the game against the Saints.

Moore immediately grabbed for his arm after an awkward fall to the ground.
