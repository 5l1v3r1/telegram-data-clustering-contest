Is Katie Price pretending to be out with twelve year old daughter Princess?

Katie claimed she and Princess were enjoying a day out together with a photo that bears a striking similarity to one from a fashion shoot that Princess posed for

24 Nov 2019, 19:04 by Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

Katie Price has fans scratching their heads with her Instagram story.

The former glamour model, 41, shared a snap of 12 year old daughter Princess during what she claims was a day out together.

"Day out with my Princess," mum of five Katie penned on the photo of her eldest daughter, one of two kids she shares with first husband Peter Andre.

It might seem a perfectly ordinary photo to post - the only thing out of the ordinary is that it bears a great similarity to a fashion shoot Princess did.

Princess, along with Katie's other daughter, five year old Bunny, is the face of online clothing boutique Princess & Bunny.

One snap on the website shows Princess in a faux fur parka jacket and matching beanie.

The one on Katie's story shows her in those same clothes, in the same spot.

This comes after her estranged husband - and Bunny's dad - Kieran Hayler hinted he was upset with Katie for posting a picture of Bunny and brother Jett, six, that he'd already shared.

"Never be intimidated by someone who copies you," a quote he shared read, "The original is always better than the copy."

Katie also baffled fans with a post of Jett in the garden with big brother and Katie's eldest Harvey, writing: "Love Sunday mornings with my boys."

Jett also appeared on Kieran's Instagram , prompting one fan to mock: "Those kids are remarkable; two places at once."

A source recently told Mirror Online that 14 year old son Junior - her eldest with Peter - is "so embarrassed" by the mess of his mum's house that he prefers to stay at his dad's.

The source said: "He was so embarrassed by the mess of his mum's house and preferred Peter's cleaner house.

"He is like any teenage boy and embarrasses easily so he just didn't visit the house. He still saw his mum but wouldn't stay overnight because he just couldn't stand the mess."

They added that Katie's driving ban hasn't helped the situation, continuing: "Junior also needed to get to school and Katie not driving meant it was more difficult to get around."

Mirror Online has contacted representatives of Katie for comment.
