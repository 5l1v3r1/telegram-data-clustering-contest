Young Scots family with newborn baby 'lose everything' in devastating house fire

Sophie Duffy and Daniel Taylor and their baby daughter, Ruby are left with nothing following the blaze at their home in last night.

24 Nov 2019, 19:59 by Kathleen Speirs

A young family with a newborn baby have lost everything weeks before Christmas after a fire ripped through their home.

Sophie Duffy and Daniel Taylor and their baby daughter , Ruby, have been left with nothing following the blaze at their home in Whitburn, West Lothian, last night.

In a desperate bid for help, Sophie took to social media to share their heartbreaking story.

On Facebook she wrote: "Due to Daniel Taylor and I's house going on fire we have totally lost everything.

"All our clothes and all our baby daughter's belongings.

"If anyone known anyone who has anything that could help us out, we would be forever grateful."

Daniel's sister-in-law, Kelly Taylor set up a Go Fund Me page to raise money for the young family.

In a statement on the page Kelly wrote: "My brother-in-law, Daniel, his partner, Sophie and my baby niece, Ruby lost their home and all their belongings after their home went on fire on Saturday, November 23. 

"They have literally lost everything. 

"They have no clothes, no baby stuff or anything left at all. 

"I am fundraising to support them to rebuild and replace all their belongings. 

"We would appreciate any donations that you can spare to help. 

"Thankyou."

In just one day over £1,800 has been raised.

One fundraiser commented on the page: "What an awful tragic thing to happen, I am so sorry."

A second posted: "Terrible thing to happen, especially at this time of the year.

"Glad you’re all safe, hope you're back on your feet soon."

A third added: "So sad that this family lost their home.

"As we have a baby girl ourselves I know how expensive things can be.

"So close to Christmas too.

"I wish this family all the best and hope they can still enjoy Christmas with their beautiful baby."
