Tragic Glasgow mum dies after giving birth as devastated friends pay tribute to 'beautiful girl'

Young mum Stacy Louise Joyce, 26, from Glasgow, tragically died during childbirth.

24 Nov 2019, 21:00 by Samantha Croal, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/samantha-croal/, Kathleen Speirs

Heartbreaking tributes have poured in for a tragic young mum who died after giving birth .

Stacy Louise Joyce, 26, from Drumchapel in Glasgow , passed away after her baby was born on Saturday.

Her distraught father, John Joyce, shared the devastating news on social media and paid tribute to his 'princess'.

On Facebook John wrote: "How the f**k can this world be so f*****g cruel with such sad devastating news.

"Our world has come crashing down like we as a whole family could never imagine.

"Our daughter, sister, granddaughter, niece, cousin, mummy, Stacey Louise Joyce was sadly taken from us while giving birth to a perfect wee, over eight pounds, healthy baby boy.

"We promise you one thing, Stacy, that wee stunner will be looked after like you'll never know.

"I promise you that with every breath I'll ever take.

"Fly high my princess.

"Words will never describe how we're all feeling or will ever feel again.

"Love you more than you'll ever know."

John continued: "How can someone so special give me this perfect grandson and leave us with so much devastation Stacy?

"You'll never believe how much I miss you already and love this wee guy so much.

"Things will never be the same.

"Please give your mum, son, brother and sister the strength to get through this nightmare."

Hundreds of grieving friends paid tribute to the young mum who was 'beautiful inside and out'.

Nicolle Fullerton said: "A healthy, young, beautiful girl, full life ahead of her starting a new chapter in her life as a mummy to be taken away in the most devastating way possible.

"You were such a lovely, kind girl and would have been an amazing mum to your little boy.

"My heart breaks for you and your family."

Sarah Jane Smith said: "Stacy Louise Joyce thinking of your baby boy and family at this heartbreaking time.

"RIP you were the most beautiful girl inside and out."

Kimberley Hall said: "My thoughts, love and prayers to John Joyce and the whole family on a day which should be the best day, ever becoming grandparents and gaining a new family member, to losing their child.

"I cannot imagine what you are going through. Just know we are all here with you and Stacy's beautiful baby."

Gayle Mains commented: "Still doesn't feel real.

"She was a beautiful girl inside and out and will be truly missed."

Eryn Mullen said: "Devastating. It was supposed to be the happiest day of her life and it all was taken from her such a shame."
