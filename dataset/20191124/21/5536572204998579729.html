Chicago Bears Fans Fight Each Other In The Cheap Seats of Soldier Field During Game (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 21:23 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Chicago Bears went out in front of their fans and actually secured a win over the New York Giants that keeps their slim playoff chances alive.

During that game, however, a few members of the fan base got into a disagreement that turned to violence as they fought in the cheap seats at Soldier field.
