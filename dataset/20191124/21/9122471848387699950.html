Video: Dwayne Haskins Gets Emotional After First NFL Win

24 Nov 2019, 21:34 by Matt Hladik

Dwayne Haskins is a winner at the NFL level for the first time in his career. The rookie quarterback helped the Washington Redskins beat the Detroit Lions 19-16 this afternoon.

After kicker Dustin Hopkins booted the game-winning field goal with just seconds remaining, Haskins could be seen excitedly congratulating Hopkins and his teammates. You could tell the win meant a lot to him, given the amount of struggles he’s had early on in his career.
