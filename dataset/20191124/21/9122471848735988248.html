Urban Meyer Reacts To Ohio State’s 11-0 Start

24 Nov 2019, 21:11 by Matt Hladik

With a win over Michigan this coming Saturday, Ohio State would finish the regular season 12-0. They haven’t done that since 2012, Urban Meyer’s first year as head coach.

Meyer has no doubt been enjoying watching his protege Ryan Day lead the Buckeyes to an 11-0 start. He got to see yesterday’s win over Penn State up close as a member of FOX’s pregame coverage.

Still, he doesn’t want to see his old program get its head too swollen before “The Game” against Michigan on Saturday. That’s why Meyer tweeted one of his favorite mantras this afternoon.

“Best thing about 11-0….”he wrote.
