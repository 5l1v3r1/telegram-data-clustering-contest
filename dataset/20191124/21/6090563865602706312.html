Zimbabwe fuel prices increased again

24 Nov 2019, 21:02

The Zimbabwe Energy Regulatory Authority (ZERA) has announced yet another increase in the price of fuel effective tomorrow, Monday, 25 November 2019.

Diesel has gone up by 16 cents per litre to cost ZWL$17.90 per litre while Blended Petrol has gone up by 37 cents per litre to cost ZWL$17.44 per litre.
