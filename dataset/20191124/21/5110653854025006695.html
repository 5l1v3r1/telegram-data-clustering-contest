Toronto Maple Leafs' Alex Kerfoot to have hearing for hit on Erik Johnson

24 Nov 2019, 21:21 by Sammi Silber

A boarding minor for Alex Kerfoot on Saturday could turn into a suspension. The 25-year-old will have a hearing Monday for his hit on defenseman Erik Johnson in the Leafs' 5-3 victory over the Colorado Avalanche.

On the backcheck, Johnson raced for a puck behind the Avs net when Kerfoot came from behind and checked the blueliner in the lower back, sending him head-first into the boards.
