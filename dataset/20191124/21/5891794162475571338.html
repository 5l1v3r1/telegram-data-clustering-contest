Footage Shows Crackdown On Iranian Protests

24 Nov 2019, 21:17 by AP

AP released undated footage from protests in Iran on November 24. The videos were shot by anonymous Internet users. Protesters in Shiraz were seen running away from heavy gunfire. In Kerman, security forces on motorbikes were seen chasing protesters. In Kermanshah, men in plainclothes -- some armed with batons -- were seen dragging a man away.
