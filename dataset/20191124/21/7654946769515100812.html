Daniel Jones’ girlfriend, Ella Bonafede, couldn’t bring Giants good luck

24 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Jaclyn Hendricks

So much for Daniel Jones’ No. 1 fan bringing the Giants some much-needed luck.

On Sunday, Ella Bonafede, the girlfriend of the rookie Giants quarterback, shared a snap from Soldier Field, where New York was hoping to score its third win of the season but ended up falling to the Bears, 19-7.

Bonafede, who caught Jones’ first win as the starter in September, has shown the quarterback and Big Blue plenty of love this season.

Before the Giants battled the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in Week 3, Bonafede posted a sweet message in support of Jones, who helped lead the Giants to a 32-31 road win.

Unfortunately for Bonafede, she’s seen Jones win only once more since then as the Giants have lost seven straight.
