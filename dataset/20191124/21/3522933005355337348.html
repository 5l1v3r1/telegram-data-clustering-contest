World’s Only Brown Panda Who Was Bullied as a Cub Finally Gets Adopted

24 Nov 2019, 21:20 by Carl Samson

Qizai, the world’s only known brown panda in captivity, has been adopted by a U.S.-based charity last week.

The male bear, aged 10 — equivalent to 30 human years — is said to possess his unique brown-and-white coat as a result of genetic anomaly.
