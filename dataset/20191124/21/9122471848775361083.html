ESPN’s New Year’s Six Bowl Predictions After Week 13

24 Nov 2019, 21:54 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN’s college football analysts have updated their bowl predictions following Week 13 of the 2019 college football season. There’s been a bit of a shakeup.

Week 13 was highlighted by Oregon’s stunning upset loss to Arizona State. The Ducks’ defeat has taken them out of the College Football Playoff race.

Ohio State notched the best win of the week, meanwhile. The Buckeyes took down Penn State at Ohio Stadium to lock up a spot in the Big Ten Championship Game.

ESPN’s bowl predictions have shifted a bit following Week 13’s games.

Here are the latest picks:

College Football Playoff Semifinal at the PlayStation Fiesta Bowl: Ohio State vs. Clemson

College Football Playoff Semifinal at the Chick-fil-A Peach Bowl: LSU vs. Oklahoma

Allstate Sugar Bowl: Alabama vs. Baylor

Rose Bowl Game presented by Northwestern Mutual: Utah vs. Wisconsin

Capital One Orange Bowl: Georgia vs. Virginia Tech

Goodyear Cotton Bowl Classic: Florida vs. Memphis
