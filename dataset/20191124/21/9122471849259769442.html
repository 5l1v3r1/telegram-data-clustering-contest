Tom Herman Has Brutally Honest Admission After Loss To Baylor

24 Nov 2019, 21:07 by Andrew Holleran

Texas’ 2019 regular season could not have been much more disappointing. The Longhorns dropped to 6-5 on the season with Saturday’s loss to Baylor. Tom Herman’s team needs to beat Texas Tech in Week 14 to avoid a .500 season.

Herman was in a somber mood following Saturday night’s loss to Baylor.

The Texas head coach took the blame for what’s transpired this season.

“I’ve done a poor job and the buck stops with me. We’ll evaluate everything,” Herman told reporters.
