Mauro Ranallo deletes Twitter account after harsh comments from Corey Graves

24 Nov 2019, 23:45 by Greg Bush

When it comes to NXT, one of the main reasons that the brand is so beloved is the commentary team. Mauro Ranallo, Nigel McGuinness, and Beth Phoenix are considered the best announce team in the company, and it's easy to see why.

With the insights from two legendary performers and a third man who many have compared to Good Ol' Jim Ross, the trio is able to both inform and entertain viewers throughout the night.

Last night was no different, with the NXT commentary desk delivering some great lines at War Games. However, Ranallo himself received some criticism from colleague Corey Graves. It seemed that Graves was hinting that Ranallo may have been overdoing it, not giving McGuinness or Phoenix a chance to get a word in.
