The Betting Line For Florida-Florida State Has Been Set

24 Nov 2019, 23:09 by Tzvi Machlin

The final matchup of the Florida Cup in 2019 will be Florida vs. Florida State at Ben Hill Griffin Stadium in Gainesville.

Florida is angling for a New Year’s Six bowl game at 9-2 on the season. If not for a close loss to Georgia, they’d be competing in the SEC Championship Game the week after.

Florida State, on the other hand, has been an unmitigated mess for most of the season. But after firing head coach Willie Taggart, the Seminoles won back-to-back games to secure bowl eligibility at 6-5.

Despite FSU’s current hot streak, though, the bettors are pretty confident Florida has this game wrapped up.

According to CircaSports, the hosting Florida Gators are 18 point favorites against the Seminoles.
