NYPD steps up patrols in Times Square following social media threat

24 Nov 2019, 23:34 by Naveen Dhaliwal

TIMES SQUARE, Manhattan (WABC) -- As forecasters track the skies for the Thanksgiving holiday week, police are keeping a close eye on the ground.

The NYPD is beginning to ramp up its forces ahead of Thanksgiving - that included checking out a threat to Times Square posted on social media.

Investigators say a man posted a message on Reddit claiming he was coming to New York City with two pounds of explosives.
