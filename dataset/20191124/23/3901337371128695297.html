On-Loan Man Utd Star Impresses In Serie A

24 Nov 2019, 23:05 by SL International

AS Roma 3-0 Brescia

AS Roma proved too strong for Brescia after cruising to a 3-0 victory over Brescia at the Stadio Olimpico.

Chris Smalling opened the scoring for the hosts on 49 minutes and turned provider to set up Gianluca Mancini for the second goal in the 57th minute.

Edin Dzeko rounded off the scoring in the 66th minute as Roma moved up to fourth spot, while Brescia remain rooted to the foot of the table.

Hellas Verona 1-0 Fiorenina

Hellas Verona got back to winning ways in Serie A with a slender 1-0 victory Fiorentina on Sunday afternoon.

Samuel Di Carmine was the hero on the day after netting against his former side in the 66th minute.

Sassuolo 1-2 Lazio

Lazio maintained third spot in Serie A with a 2-1 triumph away to Sassuolo on Sunday afternoon.

Ciro Immobile gifted the visitors the lead on 34 minutes before Francesco Caputo restored for the hosts on the stroke halftime.

It was Lazio who had the final say, with Felipe Caicedo netting the winning goal in the second minute of time added on.
