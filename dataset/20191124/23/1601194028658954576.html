Dolph Ziggler and Robert Roode win the Tag Team Battle Royal to get the first point for WWE SmackDown

24 Nov 2019, 23:32 by Shubham Roy

Tonight's WWE Survivor Series emanates from the Allstate Arena in Chicago, Illinois. The pre-show kicked off with a Tag Team Battle Royal between 10 teams from WWE RAW, SmackDown and NXT competing for brand supremacy.

The team of Dolph Ziggler & Robert Roode from SmackDown won the battle royal to score the first point for the Blue Brand.
