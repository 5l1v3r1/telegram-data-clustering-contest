N-Power Latest News Today As October Stipend Payment ‘Hangs’

24 Nov 2019, 23:12 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

Hello Nigerians! This is today’s version of the latest N-Power news online updates as beneficiaries await the payment of their 2019 October stipends.

1. N-Power Beneficiaries To Be Sacked After October Stipend Payment?

There have been rumours that beneficiaries of the N-Power scheme will be sacked after the payment of the October 2019 stipend, Concise News has learned.

This news medium understands that beneficiaries of the N-Power scheme have not been paid their October 2019 stipend by the federal government.

This has caused groaning among the beneficiaries as well as speculations that the scheme is coming to an end. More details here.

2. N-Power October Stipend Payment To Begin On Monday

Beneficiaries of the N-Power scheme are still expectant that the October 2019 stipend payment “alert” will start rolling in on Monday, Concise News reports.

This news medium understands that some of the beneficiaries are expecting the October 2019 stipend to be paid alongside that of November. More!
