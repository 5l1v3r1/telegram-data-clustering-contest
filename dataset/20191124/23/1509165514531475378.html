Watch: Did Cowboys QB Dak Prescott snub Jason Garrett on sideline?

24 Nov 2019, 23:30 by Steve DelVecchio, Larry Brown Sports

The Dallas Cowboys got off to a rough start against the New England Patriots on Sunday, and a video that surfaced made it seem like Cowboys QB Dak Prescott may have taken out some of his frustrations on coach Jason Garrett.

Prescott was jogging to the sideline in the first half just before the Cowboys punted the ball, and a clapping Garrett stuck his hand out to give the quarterback a high-five. 

Prescott ran right past him.
