Scott Frost Has A Graphic Description About Illness The Huskers Dealt With

24 Nov 2019, 02:51 by Dan Lyons

A trip to Maryland was just what the doctor ordered for Nebraska football. That is more literal than you’d assume, this week, as Scott Frost made very clear after the game.

The Huskers entered today’s game riding a four-game losing streak. They took out frustrations on the struggling Terrapins, winning 54-7.

That type of win against a conference foe is always pretty impressive. For Nebraska, it was even more so with the bug going around the locker room this week.

After the game, Scott Frost didn’t downplay how sick some of his guys were, and made sure to drive home how impressive today’s result was because of it.
