Mentioned by countless fashion outlets and more, BTS Jimin takes the world of fashion by storm with his sophisticated fashion and style!

24 Nov 2019, 02:59 by parkhaneul1013

BTS Jimin, live up to his title as a fashionable idol as he was mentioned in several huge fashion media outlets such as Allure Magazine,Harper's Bazaar, STATUS Magazine and Flooxernow.com for his fashion and style.

Allure Magazine is an American women's magazine focused on beauty, published monthly by Conde Nast in New York City. This award winning magazine focuses on topic such as beauty, fashion, and women's health. Recently Allure Magazine reported about BTS Jimin's latest hair color.

In Harper's Bazaar, BTS Jimin along with Harry Styles and Nick Jonas break the stereotype for mens' fashion and style for wearing jewelleries such as earring thus making it considered as the modern trend.

STATUS is a monthly youth culture digital magazine, focusing on the future of fashion, music, art, and urban lifestyles–from Manila to Singapore, Seoul to Paris, LA to New York, and everywhere in between. STATUS features today’s taste-makers and trendsetters from musicians, actors, directors, designers, photographers, and artists. Recently STATUS shared an article about BTS Jimin and the times he inspired others with his eye makeup.

Flooxernow.com is a Spanish website where they shared various topic with their readers and one of the topic is K-Pop. In one of their recent topic, they shared about BTS Jimin's iconic style and they described BTS Jimin's as the real "IT Boy". They also quoted him with "Every time he goes out, he makes everything as a fashion runaway"

On November 12, BTS Jimin also snatched the world attention with his airport style during BTS departure to Helsinki, Finland.BTS Jimin who appeared in all-black sophisticated style become a hot topic and immediately climbed to #1 real time trend in Daum.

BTS Jimin looks absolutely stunning in his Saint Laurent coat, Chanel necklace, Universal Chemistry hat, army boot and ID convertible bag. In addition to that, Universal Chemistry shared a photo ofBTS Jimin at airport for wearing one of their head wear on their official Instagram account. Universal Chemistry is famous for their head wear which is heavily influenced by underground music and old-school.

BTS Jimin also made headlines and trended around the world as he appeared with a whole different style during BTS arrival from Helsinki, Finland. The combination of his fluffy white hat, black mask and sweater makes netizens calling him 'Onigiri'. Netizens are amazed with BTS Jimin's duality from before and after going to Finland. 

On November 22, BTS Jimin were spotted with a whole new outfit in BTS Magic Shop Fan Meeting in Chiba, Japan. BTS Jimin wear a Breaded Velvet Robe Jacket by PATBO and was mentioned by the founder and creative director for the brand, Patricia Bonaldi. She uploaded a few pictures of BTS Jimin on her official Instagram account.

BTS Jimin also received several custom-made sunglasses from GENTLE MONSTER as gift after he sold out one of their sunglasses which he wore during BTS performance in the 2019 Billboard Music Awards. 

BTS Jimin really take the fashion world by storm in 2019 as he was included in the Business of Fashion's Class of 2019 (#BoF500), included among GQ Magazine's best dressed men of the week, voted #1 in King Choice's 2019 Most Fashionable Idol poll and recently he was mentioned by SJ Group for his impact in fashion industry. 

Aside from that, famous act such as Edward Barsamian, the head editorial for Victoria Beckham and style editor for Vogue.com mentioned BTS Jimin a couple of time and Gus Van Sant, a famous film director, screenwriter, painter, photographer, musician and author expressed his wishes to work with BTS Jimin recently. Other than that several other fashion brands founder or employer also shared about BTS Jimin wearing their products 

BTS Jimin prove to everyone that his massive popularity not only can be seen within the music industry but also in fashion industry.
