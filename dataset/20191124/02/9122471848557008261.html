Miami Fans Are Freaking Out About The Halftime Score vs. FIU

24 Nov 2019, 02:03 by Matt Lombardi

Miami isn’t the dominant program it once was, but the Hurricanes still have the talent year-in and year-out to make some noise in the ACC. The effort they’re putting forth against FIU on Saturday, however, is making some lose faith in the program.

Miami, which has a 6-4 record on the year, is currently getting shut out by FIU, a 5-5 team from Conference USA. At halftime, the score is 13-0. The Hurricanes are getting mocked mercilessly on social media as a result.

Miami quarterback Jarren Williams is just 5-of-10 for 78 yards with an interception so far. FIU signal-caller James Morgan, meanwhile, is 13-of-20 for 125 yards with a touchdown.

The Hurricanes have been thoroughly dominated in every phase of the game so far. Fans of the program are melting down.
