Tennessee ranks second "nicest" state in America

24 Nov 2019, 02:35 by WVLT News

KNOXVILLE, Tenn (WVLT) -- Big Seven Travel has released a new list of the top 50 nicest states.

The website describes The Volunteer State as, "That classic Southern charm is in full swing in Tennessee, where locals have a buzzing attitude and an eagerness to show off their city to out-of-towners. Add in the lively music scene and it’s clear why it’s one of the friendliest states in America."

Minnesota was ranked number 1.
