(Video) LFC fan creates hilarious meme-filled flick of Reds’ 3-1 win over Man City

24 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Ste Carson

If you’re active on social media, you’ve probably heard of a Liverpool fan called When I’m Elmo’ – or, simply, just Elmo. This person creates videos, originally shared on Reddit, but no shared on their Twitter profile too.

Like similar creations by people like HUGE_HOG, these videos are often hilarious and filled with memes and references to pop culture.

Ever time there’s a big win, supporters on social media eagerly anticipate their next creation, and Elmo’s latest is a doozy, with references to the new Star Wars film and Pokemon games.
