Actress Princess Nnenna Orji says she can't marry an older man like Regina Daniels

24 Nov 2019, 12:03

- Actress Princess Nnenna Orji has stated that she has no desire to marry an older man like her colleague Regina Daniels

- The actress, however, noted that she respects Daniels' decision to marry Ned Nwoko

- Orji also addressed the rumour that Daniels' ex-boyfriend, Somadina Adinma, dated her because Daniels dumped him

Nigerian actress Princess Nnenna Orji has assured her fans that she would never marry an older man like her colleague, Regina Daniels. Daniels is married to billionaire and former politician, Ned Nwoko.

In an interview with The Sun, the actress was asked if she would marry an older man like Daniels and she replied, saying she would not got that far.

However, she stated that she believes that Daniels did it for reasons best known to her and she respects her decision.
