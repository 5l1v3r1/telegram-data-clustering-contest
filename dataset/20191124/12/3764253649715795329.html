What about its hardware?

Design

The phone has a sleek design. It is easy to operate with one hand. Its weight is 179 grams. The width of the handset is 76.3 mm. It comes in a length of 165 mm. Its thickness is 8.7 mm. Infinix Hot 8 all colours are Midnight Black, Shark Grey, Quetzal Cyan, and Cosmic Purple.

Display

The handset comes with a 6.52-inch HD + display that houses a mini-drop notch. It has a resolution of 720 x 1600 pixels, a screen-to-body ratio of 90.3%, and an aspect ratio of 20:9.

Processor, storage & performance

A 12nm Helio P22 2.0 GHz Octa-core processor ensures the smooth and high-speed performance of the phone. The MediaTek MT6762 Helio P22 chipset controls data flow to/from the processor. Users can enjoy 4 GB of RAM and 64 GB of inbuilt storage. Upon their wish, the user can expand it up to 256 GB via a microSD card.

Infinix Hot 8 battery life

The Infinix Hot 8 comes with a 5,000mAh battery. It is suitable for launching power-consuming applications, shooting pictures, downloading videos, and playing games.

Speaker & call quality

The smartphone features Dirac Stereo Widening technology. It is in charge of the frequency and transient optimization, enhancing the bass fidelity and sound clarity. It also features a 3.5 mm jack to plug in headphones and headsets. The quality of both call and audio files is clear.

Camera, video, and image quality

The smartphone features a triple rear camera setup, and an 8 MP front camera (f/2.0 aperture) with a dedicated LED flash.

The rear camera module consists of:

  • A 13 MP primary lens (f1.8 aperture)
  • A 2MP depth sensor
  • A low light sensor

The phone takes incredible pictures of landscapes and selfies.

Thanks to the availability of AI HDR, the photos have more natural and precise details.

You can also use the Customizable Bokeh feature to control the amount of blur of your photos and the AI Beauty mode to ensure that your selfies are beautiful.
