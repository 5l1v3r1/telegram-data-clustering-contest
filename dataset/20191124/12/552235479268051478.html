Ally McCoist roasts Rangers star Ryan Kent as he leaves Chris Sutton in stitches over dodgy footwear combo

The Ibrox legend was baffled by the winger's socks and flip flops and wasn't shy in pointing it out.

24 Nov 2019, 12:26 by Liam Bryce, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/liam-bryce/

Ally McCoist left Chris Sutton in stitches as he roasted Ryan Kent's dodgy socks and flip flops combo.

The Rangers star sat down for a BT Sport interview ahead of scoring a stunner during Sunday's 3-1 win over Hamilton and his choice of footwear left McCoist baffled.

Kent wore white socks over said flip flops and McCoist 's lighthearted quip prompted Celtic legend Sutton to burst out laughing.

"Listen, he's an excellent player, no doubt about it.

"The only mistake he's made so far is those white socks with the flip flops.

"I don't know what was going on there, frankly, to be honest with you.

"He's been Rangers key player, when he plays. I spoke to Gary McAllister about him and he's loving his football.

"He loves the club, the fans and he just feels loved. I think it's evident in his performances.

"On his game, he can twist, turn and create chances - and he can finish.

"We speak about Morelos and clearly he's the key player for Rangers but not for behind him, for me, would be Ryan Kent ."
