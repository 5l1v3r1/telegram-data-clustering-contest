Rihanna Shares A Few Steamy Photos Of Normani For This Reason — They Are Taking Things To The Next Level

24 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Mel Walker

Rihanna’s fashion brand, Fenty, is developing fast, as the lingerie line has found a global ambassador in the face of singer Normani.

The news first appeared on the brand’s official Instagram page, where a message was posted that introduced Normani in her new role along with a couple of photos of the 23-year-old wearing a red piece from the holiday 2019 campaign.

Rihanna said: “As if she needs an introduction! We’re SO Xcited to announce that @normani is officially a #SavageXAmbassador, and she’s bringing ALLLL of her baddie self to this holiday SZN. Need some “Motivation” to stay hawt during the holidays? Peep the link in bio! ♥️ #SavageXNormani #TisTheSavage”

The first official representative for Savage X Fenty also took it to Instagram to express her gratitude for the given opportunity.

Furthermore, she expressed her wish for everyone to feel empowered and embrace who they truly are and explain she did not have to wear makeup to feel confident and powerful but wearing good lingerie like Savage Fenty helped a lot.

She stated: “I’m the first-ever brand ambassador for @savagexfenty !!! I love you, @badgalrir. I want all individuals to feel empowered and to embrace who they truly are. I don’t always wake up feeling the most confident and powerful, but trust and believe having my Savage X on always helps!!”

Normani is also stated to appear in Fenty’s fashion campaign for the upcoming Spring collection in 2020.

The star was perfect for the company’s Savage style, which claims that no matter the size, forms, and color, a woman should be able to be confident.

Rihanna expressed her belief in the qualities of her new ambassador as well, saying that Normani was such a good choice because she always exuded power and belief in herself, and that was one of the reasons why people gravitated towards her.

Normani is no newbie when it comes to promoting the Savage X Fenty lingerie line, as she modeled for the brand in its September show.

When asked about the show back then, the model stated that she loved how inclusive the brand was and how it gave the opportunity to everybody to feel sexy, regardless of shape and size.

She is now taking the collaboration to the next level.
