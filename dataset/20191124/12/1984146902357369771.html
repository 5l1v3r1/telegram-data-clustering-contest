Inside the Gateshead home which will go under the hammer for just £20,000

The property on Tweed Street in Chopwell, Gateshead has been described as a great investment

24 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Kali Lindsay

This property could be yours for as little as £20,000 when it goes under the hammer.

The two-bedroom terraced house in Chopwell, Gateshead will be up for sale on Wednesday, November 27.

Based on Tweed Street, the property has a lounge, open plan kitchen/diner, bathroom with separate shower cubicle and two large bedrooms.

Outside, there is a rear yard with secured fencing.

The property is decorated in neutral colours throughout and is described as being in "great condition".

The auction will be held at Kingston Park Rugby Club at 5pm.
