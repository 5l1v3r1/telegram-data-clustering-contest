UFC News: Montana De La Rosa vs Mara Romero reportedly in the works for February 15th event

24 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Soumik Datta

According to a report from MMA Junkie, a potential UFC Women's Flyweight bout between Montana De La Rosa and Mara Romero Borella is reportedly in the works for early 2020 as part of the UFC's February 15th card that the promotion is yet to confirm.

Montana De La Rosa and Mara Romero Borella's run in the UFC

Having made her UFC in 2017, Montana De La Rosa had finished off most of her opponents in the Octagon via submission, prior to the recent defeat that she suffered in June at the hands of Andrea Lee. The loss at UFC on ESPN+ 12 ended De La Rosa's four-fight winning streak, however, she has secured vital wins in the UFC against the likes of Cristina Marks, Rachael Ostovich and Nadia Kassem.

On the other hand, Mara Romero Borella also signed with the UFC in 2017 and in her first fight she defeated Kalindra Faria via submission but suffered a loss to Kaitlyn Chookagian. In February, Borella defeated Taila Santos via submission, however, she lost her last Octagon outing to Lauren Murphy in the third round via strikes.

Montana De La Rosa vs Mara Romero Borella set for UFC's February 15th event

As of now, an official announcement is yet to be made by the UFC in terms of a potential Flyweight bout between De La Rosa and Romero Borella, however, following a recent Instagram post from the former, it looks like the two women could be set for a showdown in the Octagon on the 15th of February.
