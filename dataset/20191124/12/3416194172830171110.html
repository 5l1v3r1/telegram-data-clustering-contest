Watch Shawn Mendes & Camila Cabello Perform 'Senorita' Live at AMAs 2019 (Video)

24 Nov 2019, 12:14

We all knew it was going to be the hottest performance of the night and Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello surely delivered on our expectations!

The real-life couple and collaborators performed their smash hit song “Senorita” at the 2019 American Music Awards on Sunday (November 24) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

While everyone was waiting for them to kiss by the end of the performance, they only did a little Eskimo kiss. The couple won the award for Favorite Collaboration later in the night!

Camila is also going to perform solo during the event.
