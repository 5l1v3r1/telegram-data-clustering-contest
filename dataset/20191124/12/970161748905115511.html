Body found in woods in search for missing boy, 15

24 Nov 2019, 12:45

A body has been discovered in woodland during a search for a 15-year-old boy who disappeared three days ago.

Scott Smith was last seen in the village of Skirlaugh, East Yorkshire, on Thursday afternoon at 5.30pm.

Humberside Police confirmed they had been called to a remote location on the outskirts of the village following an ‘incident’ at around 1pm on Saturday.

A spokesperson said the circumstances of the death are not thought to be suspicious.

In a statement, the force said: ‘We can confirm that sadly a body has been found in woods on Benningholme Lane in Skirlaugh.

‘Enquiries are in their very early stages and the body has yet to be formally identified.
