CAF 2019 award: Gernot Rohr nominated for the best coach category

24 Nov 2019, 12:29

- CAF 2019 award's nominees for various categories has been announced by the body

- Gernot Rohr will go in battle with Algeria and Senegal coaches for the men's category

- Nigeria won bronze medal at the 2019 Nations Cup in Egypt beating Tunisia in third place tie

Super Eagles gaffer Gernot Rohr has been nominated among the 10 coaches who will fight for the Confederation of African Football (CAF) 2019 best coach of the year award.

Gernot Rohr led the Super Eagles to win bronze medal at the 2019 African Cup of Nations in Egypt where Nigeria defeated Tunisia in the third place match.

Nigeria had the target of reaching the final of the 2019 Nations Cup having missed the last two editions, but they were defeated in the semifinal by Algeria.

Algeria went on to win the championship beating Senegal in the final of the 2019 Nations Cup.

Also, Gernot Rohr has started the qualification race for the 2021 Nations Cup on a good note with the Super Eagles beating Benin and Lesotho in their first two games.

Meanwhile, Gernot Rohr will face serious competition from nine other coaches for the 2019 CAF award who is Algeria coach Djamel Belmadi.

Djamel Belmadi has even been tipped to win this award by fans following his impressive show in Egypt helping Algeria to win the 2019 AFCON.

Aliou Cisse who took Senegal to the final of the 2019 Nations Cup is also among the coaches who will fight for this award.
