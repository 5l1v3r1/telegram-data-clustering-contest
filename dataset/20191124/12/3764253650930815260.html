Important facts about the social media bill proposed by Senate

24 Nov 2019, 12:29

In the past few days, Nigerians on social media have been enraged over a controversial bill being considered for passage by the Nigerian Senate: Social Media Bill.

The bill has provoked outrage by Nigerians on Twitter, and other social media platforms, who have shared the contact details of all the 109 senators in the upper legislative chamber, encouraging everyone to write lawmakers representing their districts about the need to reject it.
