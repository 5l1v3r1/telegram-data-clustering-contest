Mariano Diaz re-joined Los Blancos from Olympique Lyon at the start of the 2018-2019 season and took the No.7 jersey which was vacated by all-time top scorer Cristiano Ronaldo.

However, it has not been a dream move for the 26-year-old, who only 13 LaLiga appearances last season while he is yet to play a minute of first team football this term.

The player's agent, David Aranda, confirmed that his client will leave leave the Santiago Bernabeu on loan for the next two seasons.
