The best Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals on Pixel 4, Nest Wifi, and other Google products

Pixel deals are live now through Cyber Monday

24 Nov 2019, 12:34 by Cameron Faulkner

Black Friday deals on Google products are happening all over the web, including at Google’s online storefront. Regardless of where you shop, be it Best Buy, Amazon, B&H Photo, or Walmart, the discounts cover almost its entire range of products, including the Pixel 4, Stadia, Nest Wifi, Nest Mini, other Nest products, and more.

While the start and end dates for most of these sales haven’t been shared, we’ll be keeping this post updated with the latest info.

Pixel 4 deals are now live

Google’s Pixel 4 is $200 off, priced at $599 for the 64GB version and $699 for the 128GB version. Applying this discount to the Pixel 4 XL makes it $699 for the base 64GB version and $799 for the model with 128GB of storage. This deal concludes at the end of Cyber Monday, which takes place on December 2nd.

  • Pixel 4 is $599 at Amazon, Best Buy, Google Store and B&H Photo
  • Pixel 4 XL is $699 at Amazon, Best Buy, Google Store and B&H Photo

Best Buy also announced that it will discount the Pixel 4 by $400 if you activate it with a carrier, but that deal won’t begin until November 28th.

The Pixel 3 is cheaper than usual, too

Google’s 2018 Pixel 3 is cheaper than we’ve ever seen at Amazon. The 64GB version is $349 (usually around $449). If performance matters to you, and it’s between this and the slightly cheaper Pixel 3a, definitely pick the Pixel 3. It has a more premium glass build, a faster processor, and dual front-facing speakers. (We’ve been seeing this deal come in and out of stock, so try again a little later if you’re not seeing the right price reflected. Same goes for the Pixel 3 XL below.)

The Pixel 3 XL is discounted, too, down to $449 for the 64GB model. It’s usually around $549, so this is the best price we’ve seen, as well.

Pixel 3A deals start on 11/28

Starting on Thanksgiving Day, November 28th (and ending as Cyber Monday concludes on Monday, December 2nd), Google’s Pixel 3A and 3A XL will each be $100 off their usual prices at Best Buy, Google Store, Amazon, and B&H Photo.

This sale brings the starting price for the Pixel 3A down to $299.99. For the Pixel 3A XL, with its bigger screen and battery, you’re looking at a starting price of $379. These phones are commonly $50 off their retail prices, and the best deal before this was when they launched in May; several retailers included $100 gift cards with purchase, but the actual price of the phones didn’t change.

  • Pixel 3A will be $299.99 at Amazon, Best Buy, Google Store and B&H Photo
  • Pixel 3A XL will be $379.99 at Amazon, Best Buy, Google Store and B&H Photo

Deals starting on 11/28

Unless otherwise indicated, all of the deals below will go live on the Google Store starting on Thanksgiving Day. The new Stadia Premiere Edition will come bundled with Nest Wifi (three-pack; one router, two points) for $398. Normally, the Nest Wifi three-pack costs $349, so adding on this Stadia package that includes a controller and a Chromecast Ultra is a good deal if you’re in for the investment.

If you’re only looking for a single Google Nest Wifi router, those will be $20 off, down to $149. Another Nest Wifi deal will be a $40 discount on the Nest Wifi router and point bundle. It usually costs $269, but you’ll be able to pick it up for $229.

The Nest Hub smart display will be $79, which is a $50 discount off of its retail price. Granted, it doesn’t usually cost that much; $99 is a normal price to pay for this product. If you’re looking for the bigger Nest Hub Max that has a camera, Google is knocking $30 off of that, bringing it down to $199. This is the first official price cut we’ve seen for the Nest Hub Max.

The new Google Nest Mini smart speaker will be almost half-off of its usual $49 price. That makes it $29, which is a nice price for this wall-mountable speaker. Other smart speaker discounts include the Google Home going down to $49, its lowest price.
