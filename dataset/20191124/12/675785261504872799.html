Made In Chelsea star slams Man Utd’s Brandon Williams and Patrice Evra for girlfriend DMs

Sam Thompson called out the Red Devils pair after contacting Love Island's Zara McDermott through social media site Instagram

24 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Ned Keating

Manchester United legend Patrice Evra and current full-back Brandon Williams has incurred the wrath of reality star Sam Thompson after being caught contacting the Made in Chelsea cast member's girlfriend.

Thompson is currently dating Zara McDermott who herself shot to fame on Love Island in 2018 and was most recently seen on this year's series of The X Factor as part of the band 'No Love Lost'.

The pair got together earlier this year and have not been shy in sharing a glimpse into their romance with both regularly uploading pictures of the other to their social media accounts.

Despite having a boyfriend, it seems McDermott does not remain short of admirers with Evra and Williams apparently contacting her on Instagram .

And Thompson decided to take the pair to task over their actions, though did do it with good humour.

"I've always dreamed of talking to footballers, but no one ever replies to me. The only way I can talk to them is through my girlfriend's phone on the DMs," said Thompson in a clip on his own Instagram account on Saturday night.

"We've got Brandon Williams today who is actually sick and got his debut, congratulations Brandon. Little eye emoji, yes boy. Love that, classic stuff.

"We've got Patrice Evra on there who's like a hero of mine and who I absolutely love. He's sent one of those things where he says 'I love this game'. Why don't they reply to me, but slide into her DMs? I'm just as good."

Made in Chelsea star Thompson then went on to finish the clip by making a joking plea to players of a club far closing to his hometown to get in contact.

"It's always Man United, but where are the Chelsea boys? Mason Mount, slide in. Or Tammy Abraham, you're my hero, come on," he added.

Former Utd full-back Evra married wife Sandra in 2007 and the pair have two children together.
