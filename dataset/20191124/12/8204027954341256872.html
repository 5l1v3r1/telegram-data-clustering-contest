Car crashes into tree near West Alexandria

24 Nov 2019, 12:56 by Breaking News Staff

PREBLE COUNTY — One person was flown to an area hospital Friday night after a car crashed into a tree near West Alexandria.

The crash happened just before 11 p.m. in the 2000 block of New Market Banta Road, according to the Preble County Sheriff’s Office.

MedFlight took one person to a hospital with unknown injuries.
