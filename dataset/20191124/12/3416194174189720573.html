Maya Hawke & Katherine Langford Show Off Their Stylish Sides at AMAs 2019!

24 Nov 2019, 12:44

Maya Hawke is all smiles as she arrives at the 2019 American Music Awards on Sunday (November 24) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.
