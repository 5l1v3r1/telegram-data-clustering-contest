Kelsea Ballerini Is a Sequin Queen at American Music Awards 2019

24 Nov 2019, 12:08

Kelsea Ballerini wears a sequin suit while walking the red carpet at the 2019 American Music Awards held at Microsoft Theater on Sunday (November 24) in Los Angeles.

The 26-year-old singer was nominated at the award show for Favorite Female Country Artist, but unfortunately lost out to Carrie Underwood.

“Honestly part of my brand has now become always looking like halseys proud mom,” Kelsea shared on Twitter with a retweet of her applauding during Halsey’s performance.

Also in attendance was fellow nominee Ella Mai.

FYI: Kelsea is wearing a Gucci suit with Christian Louboutin shoes. Ella is wearing an Alexandre Vauthier dress with Roger Vivier shoes.
