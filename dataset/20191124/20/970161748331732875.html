How old is Caitlyn Jenner’s girlfriend Sophia Hutchins and how did they meet?

24 Nov 2019, 20:46

Caitlyn Jenner is currently winning an army of new fans on I’m a Celebrity.

From a comical pratfall out of a window, knuckling down and bossing the Bushtucker trials to letting some tidbits out about the Kardashians, she’s more than earned her alleged astronomical fee.

But who is Sophia Hutchins, Caitlyn’s rumoured girlfriend, who’s running her social media while she’s in the jungle and how did the two meet?

How old is Sophia Hutchins?

Sophia is 23 years old.

She is a budding businesswoman, having recently come up with an odourless SPF 50+ sunscreen mist that can be applied after make-up.

When did Caitlyn and Sophia meet?

The pair first met in 2015 through their make-up artist and hairstylist.

Sophia – who is also a transgendered woman – was just starting her transition at the time.

She revealed her decision to transition in her school newspaper in 2016, and, according to reports in The Mirror, said Caitlyn had inspired her to do it.

Speaking with podcast The Hidden Truth in October 2018, Sophia opened up about her relationship with Caitlyn, saying she ‘wouldn’t describe it as a romantic relationship.’

But she did elaborate to say: ‘We’re partners in everything we do, we’re inseparable, we’re business partners, we live together, we share a dog, we share family, we share a life together.’

Sounds pretty close, and she’s close enough to be in charge of Caitlyn’s social media.

Sharing a sweet snap of them together on Instagram, Sophia posted: ‘Hey guys it’s @hutchins_sophia While @caitlynjenner is in the jungle for @imacelebrity we are all rooting her on here in the USA!! Go CAITLYN!!’
