'He picked the wrong house': New York bodybuilder, 82, fights back against break-in suspect

24 Nov 2019, 20:25 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -

A man attempting to break into a New York home Thursday was in for a rude surprise.

Willie Murphy, an 82-year-old bodybuilder in Rochester, threw her weight around, along with some nimble moves, to subdue the would-be thief, WHAM-TV reported.

"He picked the wrong house to break into," Murphy told the television station.
