Josh Norman Gave Zero Effort After Being Demoted to Special Teams (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Darrelle Lincoln

Josh Norman has fallen out of grace in Washington.

The Redskins cornerback was said to be a healthy scratch before the game, but instead, the team decided to demote him to special teams play.

He clearly didn’t like the decision as he gave zero effort on a field goal as he watched and then walked off the field.
