Is James Haskell married and does the rugby player have any children?

24 Nov 2019, 20:01

I’m a Celebrity 2019 is well under way – and rugby hunk James Haskell has got right into the swing of things in the jungle.

The rugby ace has been tackling freezing showers and bonding with Caitlyn Jenner. – as well as having to survive those measly jungle rations.

But who has he got watching back home?

Here’s what you need to know about James.

Is James Haskell married?

James Haskell is married to Chloe Madeley.

Chloe, 32, revealed to Hello magazine that James, 34, got down on one knee and proposed to her in Paris.

She said: ‘He kissed me on the forehead and said, “You do know how much I love you, don’t you? The last three and a half years have been the best years of my life.”’

The couple got married in a festive ceremony on 15 December, 2018.

Who is James Haskell’s wife Chloe Madeley?

If the name is ringing some bells, it’s because Chloe is a well-known figure in her own right.

The daughter of iconic TV-duo Richard Madeley and Judy Finnigan, Chloe is a television presenter, freelance journalist and fitness model.

Haskell’s father-in-law has flown down under, causing a bit of mayhem on I’m a Celebrity Extra Camp.

She has written three health and fitness books, appeared on Channel 4’s The Jump and also reached the final three of 2011’s Dancing on Ice.

Does James Haskell have any children?

The pair don’t have any children yet, but Chloe has hinted that they’d both be ready to start a family.

Speaking with Hello, she said: ‘We’ve gone from one end of the spectrum, where we didn’t want children, to the other end where we thought, “yes, let’s try it”.’

She added: ‘James is made to be a father. He’ll be a brilliant dad.’
