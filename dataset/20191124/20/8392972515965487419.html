Strictly Come Dancing Week 10 elimination: Saffron Barker's out

Viewers had a very mixed reaction to the announcement

24 Nov 2019, 20:01 by Karen Hyland

Week 10 of Strictly Come Dancing and the pressure was really on for all the celebs and their pro partners.

There were six couples left, and none of them were ready to go home.

But one of them had to miss out on a place in next week's Quarter Final.

Claudia and Tess were on hand to break someone's dreams tonight...

Karim Zeroual and Amy Dowden, and Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard faced the dance-off.

Saffron, who was first to discover she was at risk, became tearful ahead of tackling her Samba again.

Karim, who was in the dance-off for the first time, took the news better, saying he was happy to share his couples' choice dance again.

Karim had been joint first with Kelvin Fletcher on the leaderboard last night, with 38 points, while Saffron had been bottom with 26.

After both performances the judges made their decision... and all chose to say goodbye to Saffron.
