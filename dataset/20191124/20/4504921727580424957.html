Venaani says PDM is the only vehicle to shared prosperity and better Namibia

24 Nov 2019, 20:46

Leader of the Popular Democratic Movement (PDM), McHenry Venaani has pleaded with all Namibian to vote for him and his party on Wednesday saying PDM is the only vehicle to shared prosperity and better Namibia.
Venaani was speaking at his party's final rally in Windhoek.
