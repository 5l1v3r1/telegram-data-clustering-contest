ACC wants Fishrot accused's assets confiscated

24 Nov 2019, 20:28

The Anti Corruption Commission (ACC) has asked the Prosecutor General to consider initiating civil proceedings to confiscate some of the assets of the accused in the Fishrot scandal in terms of the Prevention of Organised Crime Act.
This is contained in a media statement, in which the workings of the alleged corruption scandal into the offshore conspiracy to commit corrupt practices, including bribery and money laundering.
