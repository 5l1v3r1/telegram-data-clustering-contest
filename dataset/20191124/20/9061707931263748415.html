Burton drivers warned of roadworks on major routes

Take note and do not get caught out on your commute

24 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Ben Waldron

A number of roadworks are planned to roll out across Burton roads in the coming weeks.

Motorists are being warned to take note of all the works coming up soon.

Using roadworks collaboration website one.network, we have collated a list of works coming up in the next two weeks across the town.

Issues will soon start appearing on major routes both in and out of the town, including in Wellington Road, Shobnall Road, Derby Street and Evershed Way.

Nothing ruins a good mood in the morning like the sudden appearance of roadworks when you are not expecting it.

We have done the hard work for you and collected a list of all the roadworks coming up from now until Friday, December 6.

Newton Road

Where: Near Burton Canoe Club.

When: Until Thursday, November 28.

For: Works on the road system.

Parkway

Where: On one exit of the roundabout near McDonald's, in the left-hand filter lane.

When: Until Friday, December 6.

For: The installation of a rising main sewer section in the footpath.

Wellington Road

Where: On the entrance to the roundabout near Morrison's.

When: Until Tuesday, December 3.

For: The laying of a new cable.

Branston Road

When: Until New Year's Eve.

Where: On the approach to the roundabout with Orchard Street.

For: The laying of cables in the footpath and road.

There are further works in Branston Road, with one set of traffic lights near the junction with All Saints Road until Tuesday, December 3, and a second set near Queen Street, also until December 3.

Watson Street

When: Until Wednesday, July 1, 2020.

Where: Towards the end of Watson Street, near the entrance for the Washlands.

For: Flood defence improvement works.

Evershed Way

When: Until Thursday, December 5.

Where: On the roundabout with Anglesey Road.

For: The laying of cables in the footpath and road.

Shobnall Road

When: Monday, November 25 to Wednesday, November 27.

Where: Near The Albion pub.

For: The maintenance of flood monitoring equipment in the water system.

Burton Road

When: Thursday, November 28 and Friday, November 29.

Where: Near the junction with Jephson Road.

For: Highway works.

Stanton Road

When: Sunday, November 24 until Wednesday, November 27.

Where: Near Saxon Street.

For: The completion of road maintenance.

Derby Street

When: Tuesday, November 26 to Thursday, November 28.

Where: From the junction with Derby Road to the junction with Little Burton West.

For: road maintenance.

Borough Road

When: Until Sunday, December 1.

Where: Near Burton Railway Station.

For: A safety crossing point.

Rosliston Road South

When: Until Friday, November 29.

Where: Near the junction with Walton Road.

For: Gas mains reinforcement.
