DLC Season Pass Content For ‘Code Vein’ Arrives Next Year

24 Nov 2019, 20:31 by Mike Wilson

Bandai Namco have announced that Season Pass content for their anime-inspired Souls-like game Code Vein will arrive in 2020.

The downloadable content will include new bosses, new story, new stages, new NPC costumes, and new customization parts including weapons, Blood Codes, and Gifts.

Additionally, a new free update is planned for release in December, which will include Christmas-themed accessories and emotes such as dances and new poses. Code Vein is available now for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC. The Season Pass is available for $24.99.
