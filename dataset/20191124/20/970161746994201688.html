Little Mix confirm BST Hyde Park performance

24 Nov 2019, 20:42

Little Mix have announced the exciting news that they’ll be performing at BST Hyde Park next summer, while also confirming their 2020 tour.

The Woman Like Me singers gathered together to make the announcement in a video on Sunday, with Jesy Nelson doing the honours of sharing the news of their BST Hyde Park show.

‘We have some huge news that we are so excited about, that we want to share with you guys,’ the popstar teased.

‘We are heading back on the road next year for our summer 2020 tour. This will include a date playing the legendary American Express Presents BST Hyde Park in London.’

Leigh-Anne Pinnock chimed in: ‘Tickets go on sale on Thursday the 28th – get your tickets guys!’ as Jade Thirlwell and Perrie Edwards blew kisses.

See you there, girls.

The Black Magic hitmakers didn’t reveal the other 2020 tour dates but they’re expected to be announced on Monday.
