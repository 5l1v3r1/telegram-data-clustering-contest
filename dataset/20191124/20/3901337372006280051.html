24 Nov 2019, 20:27 by SL International

English Premier League

Result: Sheffield United 3-3 Manchester United

Date: 24 November 2019

Venue: Bramall Lane

Manchester United were denied three points thanks to a late eqauliser in their entertaining 3-3 draw with Sheffield United on Sunday evening.

De Gea almost saved Man United again in the 19th minute after saving Lundstram's low shot inside the box from a through ball but John Fleck reacted quickly to tuck home from the rebound for the lead.

The Blades kept on pressurising the visitors and that did not give Solskjaer's men time to breathe and settle, as they hardly created any chance going forward for the remainder of the half.
