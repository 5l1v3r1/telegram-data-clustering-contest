FULL LIST: Ighalo, Ndidi, Osimhen shortlisted for CAF 2019 awards

24 Nov 2019, 20:39 by Remi Sulola

Odion Ighalo, Wilfred Ndidi and Victor Osimhen, three Super Eagles stars, have been shortlisted among thirty for the 2019 Confederation of African Football (CAF) player of the year award.

The nominees for the different categories were announced by the confederation on Sunday.
