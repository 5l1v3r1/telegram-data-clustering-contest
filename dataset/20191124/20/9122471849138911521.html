The Betting Line For Nebraska-Iowa Is Set

24 Nov 2019, 20:46 by Tzvi Machlin

Iowa and Nebraska face off next Friday with both teams eyeing very different goals. The Hawkeyes will be hoping for a place in a major bowl game, while the Huskers will be fortunate to play any bowl game.

Both teams are coming off huge wins, but the visiting Hawkeyes have been the better program all year. The bettors seem to think Iowa has the edge too.

According to CircaSports, the Hawkeyes will head into Memorial Stadium as 3.5-point favorites over the Huskers.
