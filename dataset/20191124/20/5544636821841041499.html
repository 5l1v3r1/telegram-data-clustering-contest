RCCG November 2019 Fasting Prayer Points For Day 25 (Healing)

24 Nov 2019, 20:58 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) November 2019 fasting prayer points for Day 25, will be focused on healings for the nations

Concise News understands that the RCCG November 2019 fasting prayer points is part of the preparations for the Holy Ghost Convention billed for December.

Day 25 prayer point for the RCCG fasting (November 2019) will have its text from Matthew 24: 7; 13 and are 14 in number.

RCCG November 2019 Fasting Prayer Points Day 25, Monday

“We are in a time when nations are rising against nation, troubles and killings in all nations and continents of the word,” the RCCG said.

“This will continue until the return of our Lord Jesus, notwithstanding, there is salvation for the nation who call on God and anyone that endures.”

Below are the prayer points for the November 2019 RCCG fasting with Bible verses to go with them:

DAY 25: HEALINGS FOR THE NATIONS

TEXT: Mat 24:7; 13

  1. Bless the Lord O my soul and all that is within me. Bless His Holy Name. Psa 103: 1
  2. Thank God for a new day and for the salvation of your soul
  3. Thank God for His goodness and mercy over your life and family members,
  4. Father; please raise peace-loving people and nations to work against conflicts, aggressions in the world, in Jesus name.
  5. Father; please raise peace labourers for the soul harvest in countries where the gospel is forbidden, turn the heart of the people all over the world to accept the gospel in Jesus name.
  6. Father; arise and break all the strongholds against evangelism in nations that are resisting your gospel in their domain in Jesus name. Luk 8:37
  7. Father, let your word have its rightful place in the hearts of the world leaders that will foster global peace and unity in Jesus name. Prov 21:1.
  8. Oh Lord, silent violent groups all over the world by sending confusion among them to cease from war, in Jesus name. Gen 11:1-9
  9. Father, by your mighty power, save humanity from increasing weapons of mass destruction; let there be peace movements all over our nations and continents towards the elimination of nuclear weapons in Jesus name.
  10. We thank you, Lord, for you are the desire of nations. Come shake all nations of their evil plans and fill the whole world with your glory. Hag 2:7
  11. Lord Jesus, you are the Prince of peace, Kings of kings, come and reign all over the whole universe in Jesus name. Isa 9:6
  12. Father; break the power of idolatry and let everything that represents Dagon in our nation’s collapse and break to pieces in Jesus name. I sam 5:3-4
  13. I declare and decree, let all the idols of the nations be confounded and let their images be broken into pieces, in Jesus name. Jer 50:2
  14. Lord according to your word in Prov 21:1 make all world leaders and decision-makers to make a regulation that will allow gospel in their lands, in Jesus name.
