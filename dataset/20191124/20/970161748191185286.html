Pink Taco founder and Lindsay Lohan's ex Harry Morton dies aged 38

24 Nov 2019, 20:50

The founder of Pink Taco has died after being found unresponsive at home, TMZ reports.

Harry Morton, who previously dated Lindsay Lohan, is understood to have been found by a relative yesterday.

The businessman was the son of Hard Rock Cafe co-founder Peter Morton.

An autopsy is pending, and foul play is not said to be suspected.

Mr Morton was pronounced dead at the scene in Beverly Hills after he was found by a relative.

As well as dating Ms Lohan in the 2000s, he was also since linked to Demi Moore, Britney Spears and Paris Hilton.

He founded LA-based Pink Taco in 1999 aged just 18.

In 2008, he also bought Hollywood nightclub the Viper Room.

Mr Morton survived by both of his parents, Peter Morton and Paulene Stone.

A Pink Taco spokesman said: ‘We are saddened by the passing of Harry Morton, the founder and former owner of Pink Taco.
