Police investigating after man is dropped off at hospital with gunshot wound

24 Nov 2019, 10:39 by Breaking News Staff

DAYTON — Dayton police are investigating after a man was dropped off at Miami Valley Hospital with a potentially life-threatening gunshot wound to his left side, according to dispatch records. 

The vehicle that dropped the man off left the hospital.

Records indicated that police were investigating an area near the intersection of Germantown Street and Broadway Street in relation to this shooting. 

We are working to learn more, and will update this story with any new information.
