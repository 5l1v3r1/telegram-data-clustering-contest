Mane Equals Liverpool Legends' Records

24 Nov 2019, 10:59 by SL International

After finding the back of the net against Crystal Palace, African superstar Sadio Mane matched a record previously held by Michael Owen and Luis Suarez.

Read: Unbeaten Liverpool Net Late Winner At Palace

The Reds maintained their unbeaten record in the Premier League this season with a 2-1 victory over Palace at Selhurst Park on Saturday evening.

The Senegal international opened the scoring with a deflected effort shortly after the halftime break to take his tally to eight league goals this season.

In doing so, the 27-year-old became the third player in Liverpool history to score in five successive league games against the same opponent (Palace).
