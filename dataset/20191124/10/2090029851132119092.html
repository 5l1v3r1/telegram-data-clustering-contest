Heavily pregnant woman mixing “organic” skincare products goes viral (Video)

24 Nov 2019, 10:31 by Damilola Ismail

A video showing a pregnant woman mixing organic skincare products for sale has quickly gone viral.

In the video, the pregnant woman sitting on a stool was dragging herself on it while pouring some of the ingredients into over 6 big bowls filled with the products to be used.

The video went viral for different reason as some wonder how she is able to sell this large amount of products she is making, some others are shocked she wasn’t measuring the amount of ingredients she was pouring into each of the bowls with some ingredients already mixed and a few others were commending her strength and ability to still do this swiftly while heavily pregnant.
