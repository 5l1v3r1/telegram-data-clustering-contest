Justin Timberlake holds hands with co-star and ditches wedding ring

24 Nov 2019, 10:44

Justin Timberlake has been seeing holding hands with co-star Alisha Wainwright having ditched his wedding ring.

The star, who is married to Jessica Biel and shares son, Silas, four with the actor, looked cosy with his colleague as they were pictured enjoying a boozy night out recently.

In images obtained by The Sun, Alisha, 30, can be seen with her hand on 38-year-old Justin’s leg, while in another photo the pair hold hands under the table.

Wearing a cap, Justin looks up at Alisha as she, at one stage, got up from her seat holding a bottle as she draped herself over a railing.

The pair were sitting on a balcony, surrounded by other partygoers who all appeared to be having a wow of a time they joked with Justin, who was in great spirits.

Justin, who married Jessica in 2012, was seen without his wedding band in the New Orleans bar, as he took a break from filming latest project, Palmer.

In footage he appeared unsteady on his feet as he was helped up by another friend, leaving the area without Alisha, who remained and chatted with friends.

According to the publication, the pair were on the balcony for around 40 minutes before leaving at 12.30am.

This may be nothing more than a friendly interaction, with the pair surrounded by a larger group.

However, neither Justin nor Alisha, or Jessica for that matter, have publicly addressed the images so far.

Justin often shares gushing tributes to his wife, earlier this year posting a sweet message to celebrate her 37th birthday.

The singer shared a number of cute throwback pictures of the couple to celebrate the day, including selfies and posed backstage shots.

In the post caption, he called his wife his ‘partner in this thang called life’ and described her as the ‘god of all smoke shows’.

Justin wrote: ‘My partner in this thang called life… you are the most wonderful human I have ever met. You make me smile, laugh, and love life more than I knew I could. I cherish every moment with you and can’t wait to spend so many more years doing the same… but, new. Happy Birthday, you GOD OF ALL SMOKE SHOWS!! Love, Your Huz.’
