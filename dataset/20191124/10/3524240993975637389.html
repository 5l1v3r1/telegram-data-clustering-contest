Three arrested as Hausa protest over installation of Seriki Ife

24 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Bola Bamigbola

Bola Bamigbola

Three people said to be sponsors of those that blocked Sabo Road, Ile Ife on Saturday during a protest have been arrested by the police.

Osun State Police Commissioner, Abiodun Ige, confirmed their arrest in a chat.

Some northerners had blocked the busy Sabo Road, while protesting against plans to install a candidate as Seriki Hausawa.

The development caused panic in the area before the situation was brought under control by the police.

According to findings, three people were eyeing the stool of Seriki after the demise of the last occupant of the seat.

However, protesters that blocked the road were said to be protesting against plan to install a candidate said to be unpopular.

According to residents of Sabo, during the period that the road was barricaded, commuters passing through the area very early on Saturday had hectic time.

Policemen deployed to the scene, however, dismantled the barricade and arrested those suspected to be masterminds of the incident.
