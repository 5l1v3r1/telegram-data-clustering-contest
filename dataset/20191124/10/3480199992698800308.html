£5,000 tools stolen from van in Gobowen

24 Nov 2019, 10:52 by Nick Humphreys

Power tools worth £5,000 have been stolen from a van in Gobowen.

The Makita tools were taken from a Ford Transit van in the Almond Avenue area either late on Friday night or in the early hours of yesterday morning.

Oswestry SNT tweeted: "Theft of £5,000 worth of Makita tools from a Ford Transit in the Almond Ave area of Gobowen overnight 22/23 Nov. Any info: 431S 231119
