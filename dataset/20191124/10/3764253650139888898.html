Gareth Bale, Real Madrid star, linked with a January move to Man United

24 Nov 2019, 10:16

- Gareth Bale has been linked with a January 2020 move to Manchester United

- Real Madrid could ask the Red Devils' chiefs to give them Paul Pogba in return

- Italian side Juventus are also interested in resigning Paul Pogba next year

Manchester United are reportedly interested in Real Madrid winger Gareth Bale and he could be on his way to Old Trafford when the January 2020 transfer window opens.

Before the close of the last transfer window, Gareth Bale was close to a one-year deal with China’s Jiangsu Suning which could have made him the highest paid players on earth.

But Real Madrid chiefs refused to sell the former Tottenham star to the Chinese Super League.

According to the report by UK Sun, Real Madrid chiefs knew that Manchester United were interested which made them to block the move.
