PMCO Fall Split 2019, Prelims Day 2 Match 9: INS finishes #3, IND finishes #6, Mega Esports wins Game 9

24 Nov 2019, 10:42 by Kuldeep

PMCO Fall Split 2019 Prelims for Global Finals are being held in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The 9th match of Prelims Day 2 took place in the map Miramar, from a third-person perspective. The flight path for this match started from the north-east part of the map and went towards the south part of the map. The first play-zone formed near the south side of the map, and it circled hot-priority locations such as Los Leones, Pecado, and Chumacera.

Asterion Myth and Orange Esports clashed early in the game after both teams hot dropped in Los Leones. Orange Esports's Stalker eliminated Asterion's Lunatic to secure the first kill of the game. SynerGE was underwhelming in the first two games of the day but the team came alive in Miramar as AustinX single-handedly eliminated three members of Orange Esports. However, the tempo was short-lived as INS rushed on SynerGE to wipe their entire squad.

It was a cluster at the end with IND, GoSkilla and Temp holding the same compound. The competition was cut-throat but it Mega Esports who ultimately won the Chicken Dinner as they eliminated three teams at a single go. The ending was very tense where Satsura from GoSkilla tried to out heal the entire Mega Esports team but that was not enough to stop the juggernauts from Thailand. Mega Esports ended the game with 23 kills securing their first Chicken Dinner of Day 2 PMCO Prelims.

Match 9- Miramar TPP

  1. Mega Esports: 23 kills and 43 points
  2. GoSkilla: 4 kills and 18 points
  3. INS: 7 kills and 12 points
