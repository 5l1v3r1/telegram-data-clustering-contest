Fans fear for Andrew Maxwell’s mental health as he considers QUITTING I’m A Celeb

The comedian was feeling "overwhelmed"

24 Nov 2019, 10:57 by Sarah Magliocco

Irish comedian Andrew Maxwell revealed that he considered walking away from the I’m a Celeb show last night.

The funny man broke down from exhaustion, before being comforted by his fellow campers.

“I was up in the Leaders Cabin, I was chatting to James (Haskell) and Jacqueline (Jossa) and I was just so tired, I just hit a wall,” he told the Bushtucker Telegraph.

Andrew told Eastenders actress Jacqueline: “I honestly thought that I was gonna walk out, but I haven’t, when you’re tired, you just say ‘have I done the right thing’.”

Jacqueline replied: “You’d never leave, I can tell that you’re just tired.”

James also offered words of comfort to Andrew, saying: “Everyone has a bad day.”

“You’re an important part of the group and we need you.”
