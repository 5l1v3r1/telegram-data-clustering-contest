Congress Gives Fadnavis Imperative, Ajit Pawar A 'chance', Before SC Maharashtra Showdown

24 Nov 2019, 10:38 by Prachi Mankani

General News

After NCP sacked Ajit Pawar as the legislative party leader, Ashok Chavan stated the Devendra Fadnavis should resign promptly without waiting for the floor test

Written By Prachi Mankani | Mumbai | Updated On: November 24, 2019 10:38 IST

After the NCP sacked Ajit Pawar as the legislative party leader, Former Maharashtra Chief Minister and Congress leader Ashok Chavan stated the Devendra Fadnavis should resign promptly without waiting for a credible resolution they will not be able to prove majority in the floor test. While the BJP claims that the entire NCP legislative party has consented to be a part of the government, NCP supremo Sharad Pawar made it clear that he did not endorse the decision of Ajit Pawar. Chavan also claimed that Fadnavis has tricked the Governor to prove the majority. Furthermore, he asserted that Ajit Pawar should also forget his 'mistake' and resign.
