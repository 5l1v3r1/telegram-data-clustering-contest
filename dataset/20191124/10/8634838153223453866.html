Citizen TV's Lilian Muli Mourns Nanny With Moving Message

24 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Derrick Okubasu

Citizen TV news anchor Lilian Muli, on Saturday, November 23, mourned her nanny with a very moving message.

The anchor described her deceased nanny, identified as Ndeta, as one of the most important women in her life and that she was her rock.

The journalist went on to add that Ndeta encouraged her in life and career and inspired her to become a strong woman.

"Today, I lost the woman who raised me. My nanny Ndeta, the woman who has wiped my tears so many times and taught me to be strong.

"Heartbroken and humbled at having shared my life with such an amazing human being. RIP Ndeta," stated Muli.

It was not immediately clear what caused the deceased's death since the anchor declined to comment on the matter.

In April, another Citizen TV staffer, Rauka host Kambua Manundu, mourned the tragic death of her grandmother.

Her grandmother's death came in the same month as her father professor Manundu anniversary. He passed away on April 9, 2014, after battling illness for a long time.

"People I love seem to find their angel wings in April. Koma nesa sûsû wakwa (Sleep well, grandma). 15.04.2019,” she posted on Twitter at the time.

Professor Manundu was known for his proprietorship at Manundu Herbal clinics.
