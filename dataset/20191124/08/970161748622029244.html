Luiz Ortiz 'upset' by referee decision to stop Deontay Wilder fight

24 Nov 2019, 08:31

Luis Ortiz slammed the decision of the referee to stop his fight with Deontay Wilder during the seventh round of their rematch in Las Vegas.

Wilder knocked Ortiz down with an explosive right hand and the Cuban was visibly struggling to get back to his feet and beat the count.

Kenny Bayless was the referee for the fight and waved off the contest during the count, clearly fearing for Ortiz’s safety after a painful knockdown.

Speaking through a translator, Ortiz expressed his anger at the stoppage in the post-fight press conference: ‘Yes he was in great shape both physically and mentally. He was prepared to take this fight wherever it went – knockout or decision.
