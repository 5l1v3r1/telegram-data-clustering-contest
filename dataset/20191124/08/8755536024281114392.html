Ellie Brown responds to rumours that she is dating Love Island’s Michael Griffiths

"When your man's giving you a migraine."

24 Nov 2019, 08:59 by Sarah Magliocco

Ex on the Beach star Ellie Brown has responded to rumours that she is dating Love Islander Michale Griffiths.

Ellie was linked to the firefighter after she took to social media to share a pretty selfie, writing in the caption:

“When your man’s giving you a migraine.”

Fans thought that the star was referring to Michael, after the pair reportedly hit it off while both starring on Ex On The Beach.

A source told The Sun: “Ellie and Michael hit it off during filming on Ex On The Beach and now they’ve started dating.”

“They were supposed to keep the relationship under wraps until the show had aired of course, but it seems they just couldn’t keep quiet.”

However, Ellie claims that she is still very much single.

In the comments section of the photo, her fellow Ex On The Beach star Sophie Kasaei commented, asking her who her “man” was.

Ellie replied, “I couldn’t be anymore single just thought it was a funny caption.”
