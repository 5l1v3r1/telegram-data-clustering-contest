TRNSMT fans can find out 2020 line-up early and win free tickets with online treasure hunt

Punters will need to solve clues to reveal who will be coming to Glasgow Green next year and put themselves in with a chance of seeing them for free.

24 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

TRNSMT are announcing their 2020 festival line-up a little differently this year - and you could win free tickets.

Punters will be able to find out which megastars will be taking to Glasgow Green in 2020 through a digital treasure hunt across the UK.

Music fans fancying themselves as a bit of a Sherlock Holmes must solve clues on different venues and locations to reveal the artists.

Once you've answered the questions, share your findings on Facebook and Twitter to be in with a chance of winning briefs to next year's three-day event kicking off on July 10.

The treasure hunt will go live at 4pm on Tuesday, November 26.

To get involved head here just before 4pm, follow the clues and work out the correct destinations before anyone else, type in that guess, and search for the mysterious X and the artist found at that address before sharing your findings on Twitter or Facebook to be in with a chance of winning a pair of tickets to TRNSMT 2020.

Last year's star-studded event was headlined by Stormzy, Catfish and the Bottlemen and George Ezra, with previous editions welcoming the likes of The Arctic Monkeys, Liam Gallagher, The 1975 and Radiohead.

Fans will also be treated to a Black Friday Deal where those signed up to TRNSMT's database receiving a discount on weekend tickets for one week only before the next tier of ticket pricing kicks in on December 7th.

It will be applied on both pre-sale and full general release.

Tickets are on sale from 9am on Friday, November 29 here.
