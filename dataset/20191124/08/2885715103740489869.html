Ananya Panday’s recent outings have been a mixed bag; check pics

Ananya Panday was recently spotted in three different looks and while she disappointed in one, she impressed in the other two. Click here to see the pictures.

24 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Lifestyle Desk

Ananya Panday, who is busy with the promotions of her upcoming film Pati Patni Aur Woh, was recently spotted in three different looks. While she disappointed in one, she impressed in the other two.

The actor had recently stepped out donning an ensemble from Nisse consisting of a white shirt that was paired with an asymmetrical skirt. The outfit stood out for the feather detailing. The intention was perhaps to re-create a retro-look evident in the way the look was rounded out with curls and the shades.

While it is great to see her experimenting, the look just did not work for not really coming together.

In another look, she was spotted in an all-black ensemble from Flor et.al. The bralette top was teamed with a mesh skirt and it made for a great look. It was Styled by celebrity stylist Ami Patel, the look was rounded out with dewy make-up and a nude shade of lipstick. Messy hairdo completed the look.

The actor was also spotted at the airport giving us some major denim fashion goals. Taking the denim game further, she stepped out wearing two different tones. This was paired with a matching jacket.
