GUESS WHO'S BACK

TRNSMT returns to Glasgow as music fest announces 2020 line-up with Scottish treasure hunt

24 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Chelsea Rocks

TRNSMT is making its much anticipated return to Glasgow next summer - and organisers are already promising another "memorable weekend".

Work is already under way on next year's line-up - and now the event has announced it's ready to reveal its first confirmed acts.

We previously told how the 2019 line-up was announced in a huge live reveal where fans had to solve cryptic song lyrics to piece together the headliners.

And this year, bosses have announced they're taking the puzzle to another level - with a giant digital treasure hunt on the streets of Scotland.

Hopeful festival punters will be given tip-offs about venues and locations, with correct answers taking them to find interactive badges and artist names.

Once they have identified the artists the next step is to share them on your social media for the chance to win free tickets to next year's fest.

The first clues will be announced at 4pm on November 26.

Organisers will then reveal the first headliners for the festival at 5pm.
