Dull and cloudy day but mostly dry and mild

24 Nov 2019, 08:11

Heavy rain will clear northeastwards from Scotland this morning, though some drizzly rain will linger here.

Otherwise it will be a dull and rather cloudy day with some murk and hill fog but it should be mostly dry and some brightness may break through.

Southwestern parts are likely to turn wet and windy later.

Despite the cloud it should be relatively mild with highs of 12 or 13 Celsius.
