I'm A Celeb bosses 'increase jungle security after man's body discovered'

24 Nov 2019, 08:46

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! bosses have ramped up security after a man was found dead in the surrounding area.

Earlier this week, police in New South Wales launched a manhunt to find the killer after a 50-year-old was discovered in Jack Evans Boat Harbour, Tweed Heads, an hour from where the ITV reality show is filmed in Murwillumbah.

‘The celebs are being watched 24 hours a day, seven days a week by a team of staff and our security measures are incredibly tight,’ an insider said.

They continued to The Sun: ‘So there is zero chance of anyone sneaking into camp.

‘But of course, it is very unsettling to know that there is someone out there, running from the police, who has committed such a horrible crime.’

The police investigation was launched on Friday after a couple stumbled across the body of a man in a sleeping bag around 11am local time.

Detective Chief Inspector Brendon Cullen revealed that the victim was found with ‘physical wound’.

‘The movements and the history of the victim is something that we are in the early stages of establishing,’ he told ABC.

‘We are very keen to speak to any person that was in Jack Evans Boat Harbour yesterday evening and this morning who might have seen something or know anything about this.

‘We do want to establish where this man is from and how he came to be in the state he is in now.’

The current series of I’m A Celebrity kicked off last week with a number of impressive names on the line-up, including Caitlyn Jenner and Nadine Coyle.

Last night’s episode saw all 12 contestants undertake a gruesome Bushtucker Trial with one group having to endure live critters being dropped on their heads while the other group had to hold live bugs in their mouths for 60 seconds.

During the trial, Caitlyn gagged on a cow’s eye but managed to pull through and Adele Roberts and Nadine had to chomp down on cow anus.

Elsewhere, the campmates were finally given their luxury items and it was revealed that former EastEnders star Cliff Parisi had brought with him a Christmas tree for a little bit of festive cheer.
