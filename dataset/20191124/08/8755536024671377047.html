Justin Timberlake pictures holding hands with new co-star are “innocent,” says source

"They are just working together."

24 Nov 2019, 08:27 by Sarah Magliocco

Sources close to Justin Timberlake have denied rumours that the star is involved with his co-star Alisha Wainwright.

On Saturday, photos circulated online that appeared to show the pair looking flirty.

In photos obtained by the UK Sun, Justin, who is married to Jessical Biel, is seen holding Alisha’s hand as they enjoy some drinks. The pair were photographed in New Orleans, where they are filming a movie together.
