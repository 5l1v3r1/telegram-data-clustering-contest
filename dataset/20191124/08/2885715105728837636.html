Gautam Gulati on Salman Khan’s Radhe: Can’t thank God enough for this opportunity

Ex-Bigg Boss contestant Gautam Gulati will be sharing screen space with Salman Khan in the actor's upcoming film Radhe.

24 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Entertainment Desk

Ex-Bigg Boss contestant and actor Gautam Gulati is all set to take over the screen with Salman Khan’s upcoming film Radhe. The actor has joined the cast of the Prabhudheva directorial, which also stars Disha Patani.

Gautam Gulati took to Twitter and thanked God for the opportunity.

“Great feeling to be part of sir Salman Khan Films. What a genuine and kind human being. It is like a dream working and seeing him around almost everyday and I cannot thank God enough for this great opportunity to be working with the top megastar of our country. Love you Salman sir,” Gautam wrote in a small note he shared on Twitter.

He continued, “I appreciate your determination in showing me I can do it. Thank you from the bottom of my heart for your unconditional support and love.”
