Ed Sheeran makes surprise visit to Exeter

The rumours turned out to be true

24 Nov 2019, 08:02 by Paul Greaves

Pop superstar Ed Sheeran made a surprise visit to Devon yesterday - to watch his wife play hockey at the University of Exeter.

Rumours started to spread the singer was at the Streatham campus on Saturday to support Cherry Seaborn. Later a number of students took to social media to confirm they had seen him.

Beth, a second year student, bumped into Ed as they left the gym this afternoon, according to The Exeter Tab.

She said: "He was watching the girls hockey match as his wife was playing for the team 'Pies' I think."

She added: "I didn't speak to him really, just asked for a pic as he was walking away."
