Saina Nehwal withdraws from PBL 5 due to injury-prone year

Saina Nehwal blamed her injury-prone season and pancreatitis for not participating in Pro Badminton League Season 5.

24 Nov 2019, 08:17 by Sports Desk

Ace Indian shuttler Saina Nehwal has withdrawn from Pro Badminton League Season 5, blaming her injury-prone season and pancreatitis for not participating in the tournament.

Admitting that she has not stayed fit for the most part of the year, Saina announced her withdrawal in a tweet, saying, “Hey everyone, I won’t be part of the PBL Season 5. I haven’t been well most part of the year due to pancreatitis and injuries and I would like to take time during the PBL to prepare better.”

“I want to say sorry to all my fans and I hope to be part of the next season of PBL,” she added.
