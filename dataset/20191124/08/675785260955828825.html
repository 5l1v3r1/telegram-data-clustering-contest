Man Utd predicted line-up vs Sheffield United as Ole Gunnar Solskjaer considers midfield

Man Utd will be without Paul Pogba, Scott McTominay Eric Bailly, Timothy Fosu-Mensah and Diogo Dalot but Nemanja Matic should make his return against Sheffield United

24 Nov 2019, 08:36 by Alex Smith

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer is considering his midfield options with first-choice stars Paul Pogba and Scott McTominay absent for Manchester United's Premier League fixture with Sheffield United.

The Red Devils travel to Bramell Lane on Sunday afternoon aiming to extend their winning run to three matches - having beaten Partizan Belgrade and Brighton before the international break.

They will have to do so without long-term lay-off Pogba and McTominay - who has been one of Solskjaer's most consistent performers this season.

France international Pogba has been injured since the end of September, after damaging his ankle, and could now be out until the New Year.

While Scot McTominay pulled out of international duty with an ankle problem and could be away for a few weeks.

Matic is expected to face the Blades for just his fourth appearances of the season, and his first in the Premier League since he was replaced against West Ham on September 22.

He will likely line-up alongside Fred and Andreas Pereira - who have both seen plenty of action already this season, with the latter netting the opener in the victory over Brighton.

At the back, Solskjaer is set to name an unchanged back-five - with David de Gea in goal protected by Aaron Wan-Bissaka, Harry Maguire, Victor Lindelof and youngster Brandon Williams.

Eric Bailly, Timothy Fosu-Mensah and Diogo Dalot are stepping up their rehabilitation from injury, but all will miss out.

Luke Shaw and Axel Tuanzebe are available but lacking match fitness, while club captain Ashley Young is available after suspension.

Up front Marcus Rashford will be hoping to continue his purple patch - he has scored 10 goals in his last 11 outings for club and country.

Daniel James and Anthony Martial will probably link up with him.

Manchester United predicted XI: De Gea; Wan-Bissaka, Maguire, Lindelof, Williams; Fred, Matic, Pereira; James, Martial, Rashford
