Premier League standings: Liverpool topping the table after win over Crystal Palace

24 Nov 2019, 08:56

- Premier League standings see Liverpool still topping the table after 13 games played

- Jurgen Klopp's men are still the only side who have not lost any game this season

- Tottenham have moved to to ninth position on the standings after win against West Ham United

Liverpool are still topping the Premier League standings after their 2-1 away win against Crystal Palace on Saturday, November 23 with Roberto Firmino scoring the winner for the Reds.

Leicester City have been impressive this season under Brendan Rodgers and they are still occupying second position on the standings after beating Brighton.

Chelsea dropped from third position to fourth on the Premier League standings after their 2-1 defeat against Manchester City in which the Etihad club moved to the other direction.

Arsenal had the chance to move to fifth position on the Premier League standings if they had beaten Southampton, but the Gunners settled for a draw against the Saints.
