Joe Biden Forced To Defend His Son, Hunter Biden, After DNA Test Results Confirmed He Is The Father Of Lunden Alexis Roberts’ Baby

24 Nov 2019, 08:57 by Dylan Fisher

Recent reports indicate that Hunter Biden has one more child than he knew of. As DNA test results confirmed, Hunter is now the father of four children, as he has three daughters from his first marriage to Kathleen Buhle.

In May 2018, the second son of former Vice President Joe Biden agreed to a DNA test to prove that he had not had sexual relationships with 28-year-old Lunden Alexis Roberts, and he was not the father of her child.

49-year-old Hunter decided to challenge the DNA test results when Roberts filed a petition for paternity and claimed she had the right to child support just two weeks after his marriage to Melissa Cohen.

The unidentified child was born in August 2018, when at the time, Biden was still romantically involved with the widow of his late brother Beau, Hallie Biden.

Biden started dating his former sister-in-law in 2016, and the relationship continued until April 2019. Just months later, Hunter married the South African filmmaker, Melissa Cohen. So apparently, Biden cheated on his brother’s widow with Roberts.

In the meantime, Roberts was determined to show once and for all who the biological father of her kid is, and to ensure that her child, who is now 14 months old, received some kind of financial support and health insurance.

According to Roberts’ motion, she has requested that information regarding her baby remain secluded from the public and wanted for the child to receive the protection by the United States Secret Service that is granted to the other members of the Biden family due to Joe Biden’s political status.
