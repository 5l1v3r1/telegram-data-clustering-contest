The Kite Runner is coming to Dubai Opera in February

The tragic story has been adapted for the stage

24 Nov 2019, 08:35 by Kelly Johnstone

The Kite Runner is coming to Dubai Opera – and we can’t wait.

Khaled Hossein’s best-selling novel has been adapted for the stage  by Matthew Spangler and is set to run at Dubai Opera in February next year.

The theatrical production has already enjoyed a successful stints in London’s West End where it received glowing reviews.

And in 2020 the play will be embarking on an international tour, which includes three days in Dubai on Thursday February 27, Friday February 28 and Saturday February 29.

The emotional story set in Afghanistan, a divided country on the verge of war, tells a haunting tale of two little boys from very different backgrounds who form a strong bond.

But their friendship is tested and the story covers the multiple betrayals that take place over a number of years, ultimately showing one man’s journey to find redemption from atrocities in his past.

Tickets for the show start at Dhs250, a special offer on VIP and Platinum seats means 20 percent off booking before Wednesday December 4.

This is a real tear-jerker so don't forget the tissues.
From Dhs350. Thu Feb 27-Sat Feb 29. Dubai Opera, Downtown Dubai, www.dubaiopera.com.
