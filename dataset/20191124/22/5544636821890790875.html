N-Power October Stipend Payment To Begin On Monday

24 Nov 2019, 22:26 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

Beneficiaries of the N-Power scheme are still expectant that the October 2019 stipend payment “alert” will start rolling in on Monday, Concise News reports.

This news medium understands that some of the beneficiaries are expecting the October 2019 stipend to be paid alongside that of November.

No official statement has been made about the delay in the payment of the October 2019 stipend.

Already, the beneficiaries are worried about the development and are threatening to go on a protest to demand the payment of the N30, 000 stipends.

There are even fears in some quarters that the N-Power scheme would be scrapped with beneficiaries sacked.
