'My dress had nipples on it. Kanye wanted them taken off'. 3 things we learnt from Kim Kardashian's The Project interview.

24 Nov 2019, 22:04 by Brielle Burns

Whether you love her or not, Kim Kardashian never fails to surprise… and tonight was no different.

The businesswoman, law student and reality star extraordinaire sat down with Lisa Wilkinson on The Sunday Project to chat about her family life and her relationship with Kanye West.

During the interview, Kim – who wore an incredibly comfy looking bathrobe – also spoke about her emerging legal career and discussed how she “empathises” with Prince Harry and Meghan, Duchess of Sussex.

The whole interview is filled with interesting tidbits, but these are the three talking points that stood out most for us – from her controversial Met Gala dress to her parenting group chat.
