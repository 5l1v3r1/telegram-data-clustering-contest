I'm A Celeb fans in hysterics after Ian Wright screams for his mum during chaotic trial

Social media went into meltdown after Ian Wright's hilarious commentary and reaction while attempting the Crevice of Cruelty Bushtucker trial

24 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Irishmirror.ie

Ian Wright sent social media into meltdown tonight after his comical reaction to the Crevice of Cruelty Bushtucker Trial left viewers in hysterics.

Arsenal legend Ian, 56, screamed for his 'mummy' and dramatically cried out as he took on the trial alongside campmate Jacqueline Jossa during tonight's episode of I'm A Celebrity .

Thousands of fans instantly flocked to Twitter to share their laughter at Ian's frantic shrieking and scared reaction.

The football star competed for stars by dodging water dragons and other creepy-crawlies while totally submerged in water, only freeing the stars by shifting them through a wire mesh through to Jacqueline.

The terrified sports pundit screamed for his mum as he desperately tries to fend off water dragons as he moved stars into a spider and snake-filled cave where mum-of-two Jacqueline tried to untie knots.

Show hosts Ant and Dec laughed as Ian and Jac screamed in terror as they desperately tried to win stars for the camp.

The Geordie boys gleefully laughed as Ian screamed “Mummy! Mum!!” as he tried to slide stars past the dragons in his cave.

Viewers took to social media to comment on Ian's hilarious reaction and called his dramatic screaming "the best thing on TV."

One user wrote: "Ian Wright shouting “MUMMY” whilst in water surrounded by lizards is everything."

While another posted: "Ian screaming for his mummy is the best thing on tv tonight."

Another viewer commented: "Swear nothing has made me happier today than Jac and Ian exuding totally chaotic energy to get each other through that trial."

One viewer called Ian's screaming "iconic", while another said it was the "funniest thing I've heard in a long time."

Another fan joked that they'd had their fair share of "Ian's screaming for the next decade."

Despite the chaotic and frantic trial with both celebrities unable to control their shrieking, Jac and Ian managed to secure all 12 stars.

After the Trial, Jacqueline said: "I almost enjoyed some parts, it’s so surreal, you’re in a tunnel with Ian Wright and he’s going ‘arghhh’.”

Ian hilariously brushed off his extravagant reaction as he simply stated: "I touched a couple of things that really frightened me in there."

He later told the Bush Telegraph: "What you feed off is how happy people are to know you got twelve stars.

"I’ll sleep really well now because I know I brought it home, I done it, my Jackie helped me through that, she’s one of the strongest women I know."

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! continues on ITV tonight at 9pm.
