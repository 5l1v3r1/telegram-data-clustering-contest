Auckland train network grinds to a halt

24 Nov 2019, 22:01

Trains across Auckland were temporarily delayed or cancelled on Monday morning.

Just before 11am, Auckland Transport said a "network control matter" knocked the network offline.

At 11:34am, the agency said "most" services had resumed.

The outage followed cancellations and delays on the city's western line after a truck hit a rail overbridge in New Lynn.

The bridge was later cleared by engineers, but traffic in both directions was backed up until the truck could be removed.
