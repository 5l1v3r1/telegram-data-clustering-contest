Dak Prescott Ignores Jason Garrett’s Attempt At High-Five During Game vs. Patriots (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 22:44 by Darrelle Lincoln

Jason Garrett can’t seem to catch a break sometimes.

Following a trend with this team, the Dallas Cowboys offense were making their way off the field in the first quarter to get ready for a special teams pump. Dak Prescott was seen jogging to the sidelines while head coach Jason Garrett clapped and extended his hand for a high five.

Dak ran right past him without acknowledging his existence.
