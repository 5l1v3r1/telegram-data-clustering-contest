Kim Kardashian says her A-lister struggles match Prince Harry and Meghan Markle

24 Nov 2019, 22:47 by Buzz Contributor

Kim Kardashian agreed her family are on par with the Royal family in terms of fame and says she understands their need for a break

Kim Kardashian has compared her A-lister reality star life to the British Royal family in a brutally honest interview.

The 39-year-old mum-of-five compared her overexposed life to Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, the Duchess of Sussex.

Kim rose to fame rapidly after her sex tape went viral in 2007, with the families reality show Keeping Up With the Kardashians launching later that year.

Now Kim, who has said her business Skims will make her a billionaire, is a name that is arguably known all over the world and she recently gaining more respect as a businesswoman and intellect as she trains to be a lawyer.

Speaking on her fame, during an interview with Australian entertainment show The Project, Kim compared her life to the Royals.

She drew parallels between her own life and those of Prince Harry, 35, and 38-year-old Meghan.

Kim told the publication: "I think everyone has their own journey and I think their journey is extremely personal with what his mother experienced and how her life was so affected by the spotlight and by paparazzi.

"I don’t think anyone can know what that is like except for them."

She continued: "I think, as I’m older and I have kids, and what I would want for them, is just the safest place.

"I can definitely empathise with their need for having a secure safe place and taking time for themselves and having privacy when they need it."

The business mogul then wanted to make it clear that she wasn't complaining about her very public life.

She added: "I’m not one of those people that will complain and be like ‘I don’t have any privacy’ or 'my life is so different'."

Kim continued to say her biggest issue with being famous and having a busy life is keeping up with pals.

She said: "I think the hardest part is keeping up with my friends. I have the best friends I could ever have wished for and they’re all girls I grew up with.

"But you do have to put in work to maintain those relationships."
