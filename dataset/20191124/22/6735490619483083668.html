Carrie Underwood Tears Up During AMAs Acceptance Speech

24 Nov 2019, 22:05 by Katie Colley

Carrie Underwood did not expect to receive two awards at once during the 2019 American Music Awards.

As the 36-year-old country star took the stage to accept her award for Favourite Country Album for her 2018 record Cry Pretty, presenter Cobie Smulders revealed there was a “surprise” for her.

A shocked Underwood looked relieved once told she was getting another award for Favourite Female Country Artist.
