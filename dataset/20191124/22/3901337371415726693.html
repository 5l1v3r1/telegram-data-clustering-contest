Ex-Chiefs Star Shines Abroad

24 Nov 2019, 22:29 by Soccer Laduma

Azerbaijan

Hendrick Ekstein played a starring role as Saba registered a 3-1 victory over Sebail on Saturday afternoon.

The former Kaizer Chiefs midfielder grabbed an assist and a goal in the second half got back to winning in the Azerbaijan Premier League.

France

Lebogang Mothiba has rediscovered his form after inspiring Strasbourg to a 4-0 thumping of Bongani Zungu's Amiens on Saturday night.

Mothiba got the assist for Strasbourg's opening goal in the first half, before he got on the score-sheet in the 72nd minute to make it 3-0 on the night.

England

Kgosi Ntlhe was the hero for Scunthorpe United, who claimed a 2-1 win over Port Vale in League Two on Saturday afternoon.

The South African full-back popped up with the winning goal, which was also his first for the club.

In the Championship, Kamohelo Moktojo played the full 90 minutes as Brentford claimed a 1-0 victory over Reading on Saturday afternoon.

Belgium

Percy Tau played the final 30 minutes in Club Brugge's 2-0 win over KV Oostende on Friday evening.

Brugge face Galatasaray in the UEFA Champions League on Tuesday evening.

Scotland

Keaghan Jacobs played the final 22 minutes for Livingston, who suffered a 4-0 loss to champions Celtic on Saturday afternoon.
