I'm a Celebrity: Caitlyn Jenner compares Jacqueline Jossa to daughter Kylie

24 Nov 2019, 22:03

Jacqueline Jossa had her life made on tonight’s episode of I’m a Celebrity when Caitlyn Jenner compared her to her idol, Kylie Jenner.

The mum-of-two has made no secret of her love of the Kardashian clan, which has given her lots of ammunition now she’s stuck in a jungle with Caitlyn, the parental figure of the famous fam.

Having a deep and meaningful chat with Cait and Nadine Coyle about overcoming her fears for the bushtucker challenge, Jacqueline admitted that she struggled with her self-esteem, and looked for things that made her feel back.

Giving her encouragement, especially after she got 12 stars for the camp, Caitlyn told her: ‘After you came back and you were all happy and that good feeling you should be and you reminded me of Kylie.

‘Enthusiastic, like wow, here we go. We’re doing this, wow that was fun, you know.  It’s very much that way.’

Obviously it was the compliment to end all compliments for Jacqueline, who couldn’t hide how chuffed she was in the bush telegraph.

‘How cool is that?!,’ she declared with her mouth open. ‘Caitlyn Jenner just casually said “oh you remind me of Kylie.” Winning. Winning!’

‘She always looks out for me Caitlyn,’ the soap star then added. ‘She always notices when I’m down, she notices when I need picking up, and she’s just really, really an amazing person.’

During the blunt chat, Caitlyn also spoke openly about her transition, including the surgery she’s undergone over the years.

I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here continues weeknights at 9pm on ITV.
