'ABSOLUTELY DEVASTATING'

Scots couple with newborn baby ‘lose everything’ in tragic house fire weeks before Christmas

24 Nov 2019, 22:53 by Alice Walker

A FAMILY with a newborn baby have "lost everything" in a tragic house fire just weeks before Christmas

Sophie Duffy, partner Daniel Taylor and baby daughter Ruby have been left with nothing after a blaze ripped through their Whitburn home last night.

A fundraising page was set up by sister-in-law Kelly Taylor who told how she was hoping to help the West Lothian couple replace what they had lost.

In a touching statement, she wrote: "My Brother in Law Daniel, his partner Sophie & my baby Niece Ruby lost their home and all their belongings after their home went on fire on Saturday 23rd November.

"They have literally lost everything. They have no clothes, no baby stuff or anything left at all.

"I am fundraising to support them to rebuild and replace all their belongings.

"We would appreciate any donations that you can spare to help. Thank you."

Since being shared the page has already managed to exceed it's target of £1,000 - raising over £2,200 for the family ahead of Christmas.

And today, the family thanked punters for their support.

Kelly wrote: "We would like to thank everyone who has made a donation or turned up to offer clothes, furniture & other things.

"We don’t require any further offers for clothing, furniture etc as we have been overwhelmed with what we have been given so far.

"Thank you everyone for your true generosity."

People had flocked to the page after it was created this morning - with donations still rolling in for the couple.

One person said: "So sad that this Family lost their home.. As we have a Baby Girl ourself I know how expensive things can be. So close to Christmas too. I wish this Family all the best and hope they can still enjoy Christmas with their beautiful Baby."

While another added: "Terrible thing to happen, especially at this time of the year. Glad you’re all safe, hope your back on your feet soon."

And one supporter wrote: "We went through the exact same in March. It is absolutely devastating but the main thing is you are all safe and I promise you will get through it."
