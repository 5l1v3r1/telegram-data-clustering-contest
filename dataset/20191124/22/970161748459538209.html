Tributes paid to mother and daughter killed by falling tree

24 Nov 2019, 22:13

A mother and daughter killed by a falling tree have been described as ‘amazing’.

Elaine Reason, 56, and 16-year-old Grace were both killed on Thursday while driving through the Cotswolds.

In a statement released by Gloucestershire police today, their family said: ‘It is with great sadness that we have lost two amazing family members.

‘Elaine and Grace have been tragically taken away from us far too early.

‘They have touched many hearts and will remain with us and others forever.

‘We are still struggling to come to terms with the tragedy and would ask that our family are given the time and space we need to grieve.’

The mother and daughter were travelling on the B4077 at Stanway Hill, near the town of Winchcombe, when the tree fell just before 10.15pm.

Rain and high tides had hit the county in the previous couple of days, causing the closure of 10 roads in the area.

A spokesperson for Gloucestershire County Council said: ‘We are shocked and saddened by this awful accident.

‘We send our sincerest condolences to the those connected to the people who were tragically killed.’
