Joe Burrow Deletes Terrible Tweets Clowning Foreign NBA Players, Roger Goodell (PICS)

24 Nov 2019, 22:33 by Darrelle Lincoln

Heisman front runner Joe Burrow has some explaining to do.

Over the weekend, we here at TPS uncovered various tweets from the LSU Tigers quarterback where he took aim at commissioner Roger Goodell, the late great Stuart Scott, as welll as some tweets where he seemed to clown NBA teams for choosing foreign players.

He also had a tweet about ‘monkeys’ going to town that I’m sure many folks would like to know what or who he was referring to.

Burrow has since deleted all tweets but has not addressed it, not has LSU stated anything as of yet.
