Michael van Gerwen blasts Mervyn King as 'bad for darts' as feud continues

24 Nov 2019, 16:02

Michael van Gerwen has labelled Mervyn King ‘bad for darts’ after beating the Englishman in a bad-tempered contest at the Players Championship Finals.

Neither were at their best in the quarter-final match, but MVG was able to get over the line with a 10-6 win, averaging just 92.15.

King has been playing through serious back pain throughout the event, and it was clearly bothering him in the contest with the world champion, although MVG felt he was milking it somewhat.

In fact, Van Gerwen thinks that King complains so much during games that he has become bad for the sport.

‘Mervyn didn’t want to make it a game, he was complaining about everything, the wind, the dartboard, but I had the same,’ Van Gerwen told ITV. ‘Merv was the old Merv.

‘People like him are bad for darts. Normally I don’t say that, but if you are that injured on your back, why would you play? Simple.

‘Of course, if people keep complaining…we’re all here to do a job, try to win the game with a good performance.
