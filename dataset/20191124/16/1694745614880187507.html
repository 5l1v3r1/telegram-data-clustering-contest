A Star Has Been Born: Watch AGT Winner Kodi Lee and Lea Michele Perform "Shallow"

24 Nov 2019, 16:25 by Chanel Vargas

Kodi Lee has kept busy since winning season 14 of America's Got Talent, and his recent duet with Lea Michele has me keeping an eye out for the nearest golden buzzer.

Lee, who is blind and has autism, showed up to perform at the Autism Society Gala in Washington DC with Michele on Nov. 13, where they sang a beautiful cover of the Oscar-winning megahit "Shallow" from 2018's A Star Is Born. From Michele's incredible high notes to Lee's tearjerking piano skills, every second of the performance would make Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper proud. Watch their full performance above, and then treat yourself to more covers of the iconic song.
