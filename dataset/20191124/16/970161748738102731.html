The Greatest Dancer series 2: Todrick Hall joins cast for epic dance-off

24 Nov 2019, 16:33

The Greatest Dancer dropped a first look teaser of the new season – with Cheryl leading the show’s cast ready for action for another round.

Airing just after Strictly Come Dancing ended on Saturday night, the singer was joined on screen by Oti Mabuse and Glee’s Matthew Morrison as they start showing off their moves.

Dancing to Solo Dance by Martin Jensen (classic) gang just wanted to dance, dance, dance through a series of brightly coloured room.

Joining them this time round is the newest addition to the dance captain panel, YouTuber, musician and frequent Drag Race guest judge, Todrick Hall.

It looks like this season is very much going to be a batter of the styles with Todrick showing off more contemporary street dance moves and Oti showing off her ballroom abilities.

Matthew was performing in the style of theatrical Broadway routines, while Cheryl showed off some killer choreography.

Presenters Jordan Banjo and Alesha Dixon also joined in on the dance-a-thon, proving that the gang have got their dancing shoes on ready for another round of performances in the new year.

While he wasn’t in the ad, Love Island’s Curtis Pritchard will also be joining the team as the new receptionist lining up the hopefuls who are fighting to be crowned champion.

It appears that the new team are already getting on like a house on fire, considering Cheryl joined Todrick on stage in London earlier this year as a dancer.

Announcing he was joining the squad, Todrick said: ‘Thrilled does not even begin to explain how excited I am to be joining the cast and crew of The Greatest Dancer.

‘As a performer who has danced my whole life and been a contestant on a reality competition show, it’s wild to see my career come full circle this way.

‘I’m looking forward to working with the upcoming talent and to join the BBC family for the second season of a show that has proven to be a huge, smashing success.’

Oti has a title to uphold this time round after winning last year’s series with dancer Ellie Ferguson in the 2019 series.

She won the opportunity to perform to Strictly, as well as a £50,000 prize.

The Greatest Dancer returns early 2020 on BBC One.
