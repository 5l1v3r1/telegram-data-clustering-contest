The 2019 American Music Awards Winners List

24 Nov 2019, 16:41 by Jamie Samhan

The 2019 American Music Awards are upon us.

Despite the controversy between Taylor Swift, Scooter Braun and Scott Borchettta leading up to the big day, all has been working out and the party begins.

It has already been announced that Swift will be honoured with the Artist of the Decade Award but check out the other winners below which will be updated as the show goes on.
