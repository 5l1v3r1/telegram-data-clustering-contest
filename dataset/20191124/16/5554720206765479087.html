Bigg Boss host Salman Khan extends extraordinary support to Asim Riaz after fight with Siddharth Shukla, says he had warned Siddharth about his aggression

24 Nov 2019, 16:24 by JKR Staff

In an extraordinary development, Bollywood megastar Salman Khan on Sunday extended his support to Asim Riaz after his fight with Siddharth Shukla dominated the proceedings inside the Bigg Boss house for nearly a week. Speaking to friends and relatives of some of the Bigg Boss contestants, Salman said that Siddharth would have been thrashed if there was someone else in Asim’s place.

Defending his brother, Umar Riaz told Salman that Siddharth had been frustrating Asim and had resorted to shouting and pushing only when it became unbearable for him. Salman agreed with him as he said, “Yes agree, Asim’s frustration level had reached a tipping point.”

Salman also said that he had warned Siddharth about his aggression and arrogance all through the show. “I had been telling Siddharth from the beginning that arrogance won’t work. Asim hadn’t pushed him that hard. If there was someone else in place of Asim, Siddharth would have been thrashed,” Salman added.

Salman said that both Paras and Siddharth were wrong.
