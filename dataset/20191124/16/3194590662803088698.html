Bambam announces pregnancy — a week after white wedding

24 Nov 2019, 16:08 by James Ojo

Bamike ‘Bambam’ Olawunmi, former housemate of the 2018 season of the Big Brother Naija (BBNaija) reality television, has announced that she is expecting her first child.

The announcement comes barely a week after she had her white wedding with Tope ‘Teddy A’ Adenibuyan, her fellow housemate, in Dubai.

The talented reality star took to her Instagram page to share the good news to her over one million fans.

Clad in a dazzling white rope and flaunting a stunning pose, Bambam, who seems to be developing a protruded tummy, which is suggestive she could actually be pregnant, seems to be addressing her unborn baby in the post.

“The little one decided to photobomb my hot shot, seems baby would be as vain as me,” she wrote.

The couple’s star-studded white wedding, held last week, comes over two months after they had their traditional marriage ceremony at Ibese, Ilaro in Ogun state — following their engagement.

Their long-standing relationship started during the third edition of the reality TV show and ever since they left the house, the duo have not shown signs of breaking that emotional bond.

In a previous chat with TheCable Lifestyle, Teddy A had revealed why it was easy for him to connect with Bambam back then in the house.
