Real Madrid team news: Injury, suspension list vs. Paris Saint-Germain

Real Madrid will be looking to keep their hopes of finishing top of Group A alive when they welcome Paris Saint-Germain to the Bernabeu in the Champions League on Tuesday night.

24 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Matt Law

Los Blancos are currently second in the section on seven points, five behind leaders PSG, who recorded a 3-0 win in the reverse clash in Paris earlier this season.

Madrid would book their spot in the knockout round with a victory, although they could lose and still advance providing that third-placed Club Brugge do not overcome Galatasaray in the group's other match.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up Madrid's latest injury and suspension news ahead of Tuesday's European tie in the Spanish capital.

Marco Asensio

Status: Out
Type of injury: Knee
Possible return date: Unknown

Marco Asensio faces a battle to play again this season after suffering a serious knee injury during the team's preparations for the 2019-20 campaign. A timeframe on his return has not yet been set by the club.

Nacho

Status: Out
Type of injury: Knee
Possible return date: Unknown

Nacho trained with Madrid on Sunday as the defender steps up his recovery from a knee injury, although the Spaniard will not be considered for Tuesday's European affair at the Bernabeu.

James Rodriguez

Status: Out
Type of injury: Knee
Possible return date: Unknown

James Rodriguez could miss the remainder of 2019 after suffering a knee injury during a training session with Colombia. The attacker was allowed to join up with his country despite his injury problems in recent weeks but is now facing an extended period on the sidelines.

Lucas Vazquez

Status: Out
Type of injury: Toe
Possible return date: Unknown

Lucas Vazquez was ruled out of Saturday's clash with Real Sociedad after suffering a fractured toe in his left foot during a session in the gym. As a result, the Spaniard is unlikely to play again until 2020.

REAL MADRID'S SUSPENSION LIST

Madrid have no players suspended for the European fixture.
