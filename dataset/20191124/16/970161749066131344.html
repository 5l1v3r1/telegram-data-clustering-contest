Fans flood Goo Hara's final Instagram post with emotional tributes

24 Nov 2019, 16:58

Goo Hara has been found dead, according to South Korean media, prompting fans to head to the K-pop star’s final Instagram picture in their thousands to post emotional tributes.

The singer, who was better known as Hara, was 28 years old.

Her final post to her 1.2 million followers on the photo-sharing site was a selfie, along with the caption ‘goodnight’, which was shared yesterday (23 November 2019).

It had amassed almost 75,000 comments at the time of writing. Among them were heart-breaking notes such as ‘rest in peace’ and ‘you will be dearly missed and we love you.’

According to police, Hara was found in her home of Seoul, South Korea, on Sunday evening. The cause of death is being investigated.

She was a member of the massive-selling girl group Kara, who were active from 2007 to 2015.

She then forged a successful solo career, releasing hit singles such as Secret Love, Magic of Love and Choco Chip Cookies.

Meanwhile her debut EP Alohara (Can You Feel It?) went top five on the South Korean charts back in 2015.
