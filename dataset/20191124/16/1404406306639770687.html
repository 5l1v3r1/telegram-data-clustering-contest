Drake's Baby Mother Sophie Gifts Her Art to Pope Francis

24 Nov 2019, 16:21 by VladTV

Recently, the mother of Drake’s child—Sophie Brussaux, made a trip to Vatican City with her friends, and she was able to be in the same room with Pope Francis. From there, she gifted her artwork to Pope Francis that features his likeness. Via Instagram, she said, “Green is the color of hope, and red is the color of the fight against poverty. Such an honor to meet Pope Francis and gift him the portrait I painted for him in honor of his concert on November 9, 2019. Thank you, @etihadmodernart, for giving me this amazing opportunity.”

For those unaware, Sophie Brussaux is an artist who lives in Paris and has become known for creating celebrity portraits of Michelle Obama, Beyoncé, Amy Winehouse, Leonardo DiCaprio, Angelina Jolie, and more. Take a look above.
