21 Savage Drops Gems On Atlanta Students During "Guns Down, Heads Up!" Talk

24 Nov 2019, 16:11 by AllHipHop Staff

Atlanta rap star 21 Savage paid it forward by talking to Atlanta students about avoiding the street life.

(AllHipHop News) Rapper 21 Savage proved his dedication to the Atlanta community during an event to inspire students.

21 Savage hosted his "Guns Down, Heads Up!" event earlier this week for hundreds of Fulton County students.

During his talk, the rapper drew from his own life experiences, to warn the students about the pitfalls of gun violence and the street life.

"Lot of struggles in my life, I've dealt with a lot of the same things you are going through today," 21 Savage told the students. "Stay out of the way, do the right thing. It's a sense of calm and it can change your life."
