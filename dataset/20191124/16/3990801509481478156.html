Bitcoin whale moves $310 million cryptocurrency fortune — for just $0.32

Take that, TransferWise!

24 Nov 2019, 16:51 by David Canellis

A cryptocurrency whale has just moved a ball-busting 44,000 BTC (worth more than $310 million) — and they paid a minuscule $0.32 in Bitcoin BTC transaction fees to do so.

The hefty movement was detected by Twitter-based transaction monitor @whale_alert this afternoon. Bitcoin’s miners included it in block #605230.
