Michael Irvin’s Message For The Cowboys Is Going Viral

24 Nov 2019, 16:24 by Andrew Holleran

You’ll be hard pressed to find a more-passionate television analyst than Michael Irvin these days. The former Dallas Cowboys wide receiver loves football and he really loves his former team.

Irvin had a special message for the Cowboys ahead of this afternoon’s game against the Patriots.

“I want my young Dallas Cowboys to know, those young Davids to know this giant that sits before you today, he is not for your destruction, it is for your construction,” Irvin said.

“SLAY. THIS. GIANT.”
