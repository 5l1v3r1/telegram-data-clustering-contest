Breaking: Eagles, Seahawks Are Both Without Star Players Today

24 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Andrew Holleran

The Philadelphia Eagles and the Seattle Seahawks are a couple of hours away from their pivotal NFC matchup this afternoon. Both teams have lost key players to injuries ahead of kickoff.

Philadelphia will be without wide receiver Alshon Jeffery this afternoon.

Jeffery was expected to play as of Saturday night, but he’s been trending in the wrong direction this morning.

The wide receiver has officially been listed as inactive.
