Amarillo police working crash at Westgate Drive and Soncy

24 Nov 2019, 16:28 by Kaitlin Johnson

AMARILLO, Texas (KFDA) - The Amarillo Police Department is working a two-vehicle crash at Westgate Drive and Soncy.

The southbound lanes of traffic will be blocked for at least the next hour.

Police ask drivers to avoid the area or expect delays.

Details on the crash are not available at this time. We will update this story as we learn more.
