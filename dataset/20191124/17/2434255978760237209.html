K-pop star Goo Hara found dead at her home aged 28

The day before, she posted a harrowing one-word message on her social media

24 Nov 2019, 17:18 by Clara Kelly

Ex Kara band member and popular k-pop star, Goo Hara has been found dead at home aged 28.

Local news stations reported the star was found on Sunday evening at her home in Gangnam's Cheongdam neighborhood.

A grieving fan wrote online, "Oh my this is a.... terrible news. Koo Hara found dead - she was one of the closest friends of Sulli, who have also passed away recently."

At the time of her death, the pop sensation was also in a public argument with ex-boyfriend Choi Jong-Bum, who she claimed was blackmailing her with sex tapes the pair had made.

Meanwhile, In May this year, Goo, 28, was found unconscious by her manager at her home in Seoul’s Gangnam district.

The day before the incident, she posted a one-word message on her Instagram,“Goodbye".

Later when she was recovering in hospital she wrote, “I am sorry for causing concerns and a commotion.

"In terms of health, I am recovering … I had been in agony over a number of overlapping issues. But from now on, I will steel my heart and try to show up healthy.”

Goo was last performing four days ago.
