Urban Meyer Has Already Made His Pick For Ohio State-Michigan

24 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Andrew Holleran

Former Ohio State head coach Urban Meyer went 7-0 against Michigan during his tenure in Columbus. The Buckeyes will attempt to keep the streak going on Saturday in Ann Arbor.

Meyer will be on hand for the game at Michigan Stadium, as FOX’s Big Noon Kickoff will broadcast live from Ann Arbor.

The former Ohio State head coach will make his official pick for the game on Saturday, but he’s already revealed it.

There is no surprise.

“No chance I’m picking Michigan,” Meyer said on FOX following Ohio State’s win over Penn State.

The former Buckeyes’ head coach does have respect for the Wolverines.

“I will tell you this — do I respect good football teams? Always. That’s a good football team up there. That is a good team,” Meyer said.
