Dec is taller than Ant for the first time as he jokes about first kiss on I'm A Celeb

The legendary TV duo sent viewers into hysterics as Ant mocked Dec's height and hilariously recreated his first kiss by giving him a boost

24 Nov 2019, 17:19 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin left I’m A Celebrity viewers in stitches last night after he joked about Dec Donnelly 's height and picked him up to recreate his first kiss.

The legendary TV duo have made jokes about their heights repeatedly throughout their career, especially while filming down under in the Australian Outback.

During last night's episode of I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! Dec's height was once again the subject of the joke as the Brit presenting favourites reminisced about Dec's first kiss in a lift.

Earlier in the show, Girls Aloud star Nadine opened up about her first kiss to an Irish lad from school, Arsenal legend Ian confessed he felt like he was "in a trance" after his first lock of lips, while Cliff Parisi said his first kiss was in a lift.

Ant, 44, then mocked Dec's first kiss after he cheekily divulged juicy details about his best friend's first kiss and admitted he was witness to the encounter.

He revealed: "There is a coincidence there though because your first kiss was in a lift wasn't it Dec?

"I remember because I nearly broke my back lifting you up to reach that girl, but I did it!"

Dec, 44, continued the joke by confessing: "You only had to do it once then I bought a step ladder and we didn't need you and we could kiss in peace."

After announcing the next trial, Dec joked: "Everybody needs a lift sometimes."

Ant finished by hilariously saying: "Want another one for old times sake?!"

Dec smirked as Ant lifted up his co-star in a hilarious embrace and squeezed him tightly.

Their sweet embrace was captured with a fun Instagram snap and showed Dec as slightly taller than Ant for the first time ever.

They posted the photo with the caption: "We all need someone that lifts us up," and fans instantly rushed to the comment section to comment on their friendship.

One wrote: "Ant picking up Dec!!!" followed by a series of laughing face emojis.

While another shared: "Ant lifting Dec is the cutest thing I’ve ever seen in my life."

A third commented: "Ant lifting Dec is so precious"

Another viewer added: "The sad thing is: I can really picture Ant lifting Dec so he's able to kiss his girlfriend who's like two heads taller than him."

Dec's height is thought to be approximately 5ft 5 ½in, while Ant's height is around 5ft 9in.

Gags about height have never been far from I’m A Celebrity, with Ant and Dec describing 2017 contestant Dennis Wise as ‘a very small man’, even though he is actually 5ft 6.

All of which was ultimately settled when Dennis was voted out of the jungle – and was revealed to actually be taller than Dec.
