Eddie George Reacts To J.K. Dobbins Passing Him On All-Time List

24 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Tzvi Machlin

It was a historic day for Ohio State running back J.K. Dobbins. On top of leading a rushing attack that mowed down the Penn State defense, he surpassed Buckeyes legend Eddie George on the school’s all-time rushing list.

The former Heisman Trophy winner just so happened to be in attendance for the historic event. So naturally, George was asked what his thoughts were on being surpassed by Dobbins.

In a very classy response, Georgie asserted that records were made to be broken. He said he was excited for Dobbins to pass him, much like he was when Ezekiel Elliott passed him a few years prior.

George also said that he was excited about Dobbins’ future in football and beyond.
