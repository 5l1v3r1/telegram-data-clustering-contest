Trump retweets award for Doral resort: 'Too bad we didn't have the G-7 here'

24 Nov 2019, 17:06 by Justine Coleman

President Trump retweeted an announcement that his Doral resort received an award, saying it was “Too bad we didn’t have” the Group of Seven (G-7) summit at the Florida location.

The Trump Golf account posted that the resort received the Resort of the Week award from GOLF.com. The president called Doral the “Best location” for the summit and pushed back on accusations he would have gained financially from hosting the gathering of world leaders.

“Too bad we didn’t have the G-7 here,” Trump tweeted. “I offered to pick up the entire cost, would have saved at least $35,000,000 for the USA. Best location. Very stupid people thought I would gain. Wrong! Looking at Camp David. Will announce soon.”
