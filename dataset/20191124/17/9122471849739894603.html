Photo: Cardale Jones Is Ready For Ohio State-Michigan

Former Ohio State quarterback Cardale Jones knows what it takes to beat Michigan. The former Buckeyes star went 4-0 against the Wolverines during his time in Columbus.

Rivalry Week has arrived for the Buckeyes and the Wolverines. Jones appears to be getting Columbus ready for The Game.
