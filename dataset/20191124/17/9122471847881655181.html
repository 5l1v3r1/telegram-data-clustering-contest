Brutal Weather Is Expected For The Cowboys-Patriots Game Today

24 Nov 2019, 17:31 by Tzvi Machlin

Lambeau Field has been the sight of some of the coldest, snowiest games in NFL history. But Gillette Stadium in Foxborough, Massachusetts has been pretty brutal in the colder months, too.

Unfortunately, some of that brutal weather just so happens to be making its way to the stadium one of the most anticipated regular season games in years.

The 6-4 Dallas Cowboys head to Foxborough today for a showdown with a 9-1 New England Patriots. But there’s trouble on the horizon.

According to ESPN’s Todd Archer, rainy, windy weather is expected for this afternoon’s game. The forecast currently projects rain to last for the entire duration of the game.
