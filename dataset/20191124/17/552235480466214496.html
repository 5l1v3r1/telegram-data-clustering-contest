Missing woman from Barrhead sparks major police search

Natalie Horner was last seen at around 6.05am on Saturday.

24 Nov 2019, 17:55 by Kathleen Speirs

Police are appealing for information to trace a missing woman from East Renfrewshire.

Natalie Horner from Barrhead , was last seen at around 6.05am on Saturday.

The 29-year-old, who lives in Henry Street, was reported missing at around 12.50pm the same day.

Natalie is described as around five foot, five inches with long, dark hair.

She was last seen wearing a blue jacket and dark leggings.

Natalie has links to Glasgow, Paisley and Ayrshire.

She also has access to a Black Vauxhall Corsa, registration number BL11 AJX.

Police say this is not normal behaviour from Natalie and they are appealing to anyone with information to come forward.

Inspector Michelle Grant said: “It’s unusual for Natalie to not be in contact with her family for this length of time.

"We are appealing for anyone who has any information as to her whereabouts to get in touch.”

Anyone with any information should contact police via 101, quoting incident number 1881 of Saturday, 23 November, 2019.
