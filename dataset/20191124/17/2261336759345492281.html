Conor McGregor claims he’ll fight any top fighter, Masvidal says ‘put your money where your mouth is’

24 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Anton Tabuena

ESPN recently put out an infographic detailing possible options for big money fights in 2020 involving fighters such as Conor McGregor, Jorge Masvidal, Khabib Nurmagomedov, Tony Ferguson, Kamaru Usman and others. The former two-division champion responded on social media to claim that he is willing to fight anyone on that list.
