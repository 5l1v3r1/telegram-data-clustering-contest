The Week 14 Coaches’ Poll Top 25 Has Been Released

24 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Andrew Holleran

The Week 14 Coaches’ Poll top 25 has been released following Saturday’s Week 13 games. There’s been a bit of a shakeup inside the top 10.

Week 13 was highlighted by Oregon’s stunning upset loss to Arizona State. The Ducks are now out of the College Football Playoff race.

Alabama and Oklahoma were arguably the biggest winners of Week 13. If Oregon can beat Utah in the Pac-12 Championship Game, one of the Crimson Tide/Sooners will likely get the fourth spot in the College Football Playoff.

There’s still one week left in the regular season, of course.
