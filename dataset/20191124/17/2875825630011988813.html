Young people have their say on climate change at Dail na nOg sitting

A total of 68 young people from 31 Comhairle na nOg groups debated the chosen theme of climate action as part of the Dail 100 commemorations

24 Nov 2019, 17:16 by irishmirror.ie

Ireland's young people have had their say on climate change at a sitting of Dail na nOg.

Amy White from Wexford is shown taking part in one of the voting sessions at the youth parliament with her fellow members looking on.

A total of 68 young people from 31 Comhairle na nOg groups debated the chosen theme of climate action as part of the Dail 100 commemorations.

They agreed a two-year programme of work focusing on how young people in Ireland can take action to tackle climate change.
