FIRST LOOK: Nando’s opens new flagship store at The Dubai Mall

Check out the cheeky new store in one of the world’s biggest mall

24 Nov 2019, 17:49 by Tim Skinner

Sometimes absolutely nothing beats a cheeky Nando’s (just ask literally anyone from London), and the world famous chicken-grilling restaurant is opening its brand-new flagship store in Dubai.

The South African chain is swinging open the doors of its newest store, its 22nd in the UAE, in The Dubai Mall, and Time Out Dubai got a cheeky first look inside before anyone else.

Sat on the Waterfront Promenade in the world’s most famous mall, you can now enjoy your hot, medium or mild chicken with views of the Burj Khalifa, the world’s tallest tower (among other records), and The Dubai Fountain, the world’s largest synchronised fountain.

“We are very proud of this milestone, opening our 22nd Casa in the UAE, and in this grand location at The Dubai Mall, home to some of the most prestigious brands and iconic attractions in the country,” said Suhail Gidwani, Chairman and CEO of Nando’s UAE. “We couldn’t be more grateful to our UAE customers for their continued loyalty, which has led to our expansion here.”

The new restaurant features a series of specially-commissioned pieces of art from renowned South African artist Dion Cupido. It’s also home to the classic dishes we’ve all come to love from Nando’s, plus this branch will be the first in the UAE to offer Nando’s breakfast – including classic South African breakfasts like doughnuts, and roast almond and peanut butter oats.

You’ll find it open daily from 11am until 1am. Check out the pictures above to get a cheeky glimpse inside.
Open daily 11am-1am. The Dubai Mall, Downtown Dubai (04 362 7500).
