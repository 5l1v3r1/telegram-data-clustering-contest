A Shimmering Air Jordan 11 Is Dropping Just in Time for Your Holiday Parties

A women’s exclusive however.

24 Nov 2019, 17:28 by Robert Marshall Jr.

Just in time for your holiday parties and New Year celebrations, Jordan Brand is less than a week away from dropping the shimmering Air Jordan 11 “Vast Grey/Silver.”

The women’s exclusive sneaker features a glittery looping mudguard, rather than the shinny patent leather version we’re use to seeing on the AJ11. Its theme is a nod to one of Michael Jordan‘s initial thoughts when he first saw Tinker Hatfield’s prototype of this iconic shoe; he imagined grooms and brides, ball-goers, young prom dates and the like sporting the statement sneaker on their feet.

What’s more is that this sneaker features a white base, a cloudy translucent outsole, and an exposed metallic Jumpman at the ankle.

The Air Jordan 11 “Vast Grey/Silver” will hit retailers, like Nike.com, on November 30 for $220 USD.

In other AJ11 news, Nike SB is dropping a Dunk High inspired by the Space Jam iteration.
