Sheffield Utd 3-3 Man Utd player ratings: Phil Jones a "car crash" but Marcus Rashford shines

Manchester United continued their upturn in form at Sheffield United as goals from Marcus Rashford, Mason Greenwood and Brandon Williams turned the game around

24 Nov 2019, 18:24 by John Cross, https://www.facebook.com/johncrossmirror/

Manchester United's problems were laid bare against Sheffield United - and even showing the spirit of yesteryear couldn't save them.

Sheffield United were dominant at Bramall Lane, with John Fleck and Lys Mousset's strikes looking like they were going to seal the a memorable and famous three points.

But United's young guns had other ideas in the latter stages. First, Brandon Williams hammered home his first professional goal.

Minutes later, Mason Greenwood hopped off the bench to tap home a pinpoint Marcus Rashford cross and level the game.

And Rashford got in on the act himself, completing the seven minute salvo, handing United what they thought would be all three points.

That was until Oli McBurnie survived a stoppage time VAR check to ensure the spoils were shared.

Here is how John Cross, our man at the ground, rated the performances...
