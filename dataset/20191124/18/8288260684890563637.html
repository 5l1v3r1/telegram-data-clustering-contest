West Ham set to complete transfer after Aston Villa trial - report

Latest Aston Villa news | The goalkeeper was training with AVFC in October

24 Nov 2019, 18:24 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Jordan Archer was training with Aston Villa last month after his release from Millwall in the summer.

The goalkeeper spent time at Bodymoor Heath as part of his search for a new club following his four-year spell at the Den.

Nothing materialised and Archer remains a free agent – for now at least.

West Ham United are weighing up signing him to solve their goalkeeping crisis, according to reports.

First-choice Lukasz Fabianski is injured and Roberto Jimenez, a summer signing from Espanyol, has struggled since coming into the side.

The Hammers are considering their January options and could snap up Archer, according to the Guardian.

Archer spent time in Charlton Athletic's academy before joining Spurs in 2009.

He graduated through the North London side's academy in 2011 but didn't make a single senior appearance for Spurs.
