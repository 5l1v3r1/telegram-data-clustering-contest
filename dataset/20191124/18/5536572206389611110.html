Rob Gronkowski Makes Final Decision On His Playing Career & Whether He’s Coming Back In 2019 (VIDEO)

24 Nov 2019, 18:02 by Darrelle Lincoln

Former New England Patriots tight end Rob Gronkowski has been rumored for months that he might once again suit up and play for his former team, even teasing a big announcement last week that had nothing to do with him being on the field.

That turned out to be him planning a huge Super Bowl party in South Beach.

On Sunday, the future hall of famer confirmed to his co-workers that he won’t be returning in 2019.
