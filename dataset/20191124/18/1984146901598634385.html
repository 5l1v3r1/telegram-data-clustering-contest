37 reasons why you should marry a Geordie - and why you won't regret it

Thinking it is time to pop the question, well these are our reasons why you should ask a Geordie to marry you

24 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Lisa Hutchinson

We all know it - Geordies really are the best people in the country.

We're funny, caring, love a laugh and we have great dress sense.

With Christmas fast approaching, maybe you are thinking of popping the question.

So, with this in mind, here are our reasons why you should definitely marry a Geordie!

And if you have any others then don't hesitate to leave them in the comments.

1. You will always be guaranteed great food

Let’s be honest, here in Newcastle, we really do serve up the best scran in the country - and we do like our bait.

If you find a keeper get them to teach you the family recipes. It’s amazing what you can do with a stottie!

2. You’ll get the chance to try another language

And that’s ‘cos we have a language of our own.

A canny gadgie will want to find a canny lass. And at the wedding ya get maartal drunk.

3. We have a wicked sense of humour

We just see the funny side of things. We’re just plain funny and enjoy a laugh.

4. You are always guaranteed a Sunday dinner

Because it just wouldn’t be Sunday without your mum’s Sunday dinner.

5. We enjoy tea

Which is really dinner, but we have dinner for lunch.

6. You’ll always have a roof over your head

Housing is cheap round here so whatever your budget you’ll find somewhere.

7. You’ll be spoilt for choice for date nights...

With so much our city has to offer - nightlife, restaurants, cinemas and theatres - there is always somewhere to go.

8.... or date days

There’s so much to do in the daytime too.

We have the most stunning coastline and a walk along our beautiful beaches is a perfect day.

Or a day date at our gorgeous gardens (Alnwick Garden and Cragside at Rothbury) is prefect.

9. We are sport mad

You’ll learn to love football and the importance of being a black and white.

If you marry a fan then match days are taken care of.

10. We are generous

Yes, we really are.

No matter where you go in the world, there will always be a Geordie at the bar offering to buy a round.

11. We make great holiday mates

We really are a friendly bunch.

Imagine being thousands of miles away on holiday and hearing another Geordie accent.

You’ve just got to stop and chat.

12. You’ll get the chance to live in the best city - with the best surroundings - in the world - Obviously

This goes without saying.

16. You won’t need to worry about the in-laws because you’ll already be considered family

Loving your family is a big deal.

17. Your partner will always be the best dressed

And that’s because we always like to look our best - particularly on a night out.

18. We are loyal

We always have each others backs.

19. We are annoyingly, always so clever

Ever watched an episode of The Chase with a Geordie? We really do know the most random stuff.

20. You will never, ever, be bored being married to a Geordie

Absolutely never. Ever.

21. Everyone in Newcastle seems to know each other

It’s true. We all know the cousin’s husband’s best friend’s gran.

22. We like to go ‘over the top’ when it comes to special occasions

Whatever the occasion we always like to celebrate birthdays and Christmas in style.

23. And that’s why the kids ALWAYS get spoiled at Christmas

Always.

24. Newcastle has so many hidden gems

You’ll never be bored because there is always so much to do.

From restaurants and secret heritage sites, to some of the UK’s best shopping, we really do have it all here.

25. We are very proud people

Well, come on, we DO have a lot to be proud of here on Tyneside.

26. We have the best mams and nanas

No one will ever beat a Geordie mam or nana - they're simply the best.

27. You’ll learn words to football songs without even realising

You’ll hear them that much, that you’ll find yourself knowing all the words.

28. There’s no shortage of wedding venues

Whatever your budget or taste there are plenty of options right here in Newcastle and the surrounding area.

29. We have a heritage

And of that we are rightly proud.

30. You’ll never, ever run out of places to go for a pint

The price of a pint here is cheaper than most other places in the UK. Cheers!

31. Our local Wetherspoons are always packed

Cheap food, cheap beer - what more could we ask for?

32. We will call you hinny or bonny

We use the words hinny or bonny and they are used as terms of endearment.

33. We can be very sarcastic

But funny - and we mean you no harm.

34. And that means you’ll spend your whole relationship taking the mickey out of each other - because who wants a boring relationship anyway?

35. We are straight talking

If we have something to say, then we’ll say it. End of.

36. We all have a story about Alan Shearer

‘Tis true.

37. And, you’ll learn, that despite all the stereotypes - we really are the best people

And that’s because we are friendly, chatty, funny, clever, smart, handsome, pretty.... need we go on?
