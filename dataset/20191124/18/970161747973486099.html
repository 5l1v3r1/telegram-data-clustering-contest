Girl stabbed pet husky to stop it mauling babysitter to death

24 Nov 2019, 18:16

A child was forced to stab her pet dog after it started mauling the babysitter.

Rachel Anderson, 39, was looking after her friend Adel Johnson’s two young children last Boxing Day at their home in Rednal, Birmingham, when Siberian husky Storm attacked.

The large animal locked its jaws on her face, before pulling her to the floor and dragging her into the kitchen at around midnight.

It savaged her arm for 45 minutes while she screamed in agony, fearing she’d never make it out alive.

It was only when one of the children stabbed the hound with a knife she was able to get free and call police.

Mum-of-two Ms Anderson, from Skegness, said: ‘I was screaming for the kids to help me. In the end it got to the point where I shouted “do you want me to die here?”

‘I told them “you need to come in and stab this dog”.’

She added: ‘It was sinking its teeth in, pulling me around, releasing it a bit to get a full grip again, letting me go to bite again.’

Ms Anderson, who initially tried to get a knife herself, also said the thought of her little boy had kept her going.

Police eventually shot the dog dead after one of the children had put it in the backyard.

Ms Anderson needed nearly a dozen operations at Queen Elizabeth Hospital Birmingham to repair her wounds, and surgeons initially feared they’d have to amputate her arm after she got a skin infection and grafts refused to take.

They managed to save her limb in the end after sewing it inside her stomach for four weeks to get new skin to grow back.

In total, she spent two months in hospital and had ten operations.

Police have now dropped the case but Ms Anderson is currently off work because she can’t use her right hand properly and is still receiving treatment.

She said: ‘There has to be some kind of recognition. If you have a guard dog, have a guard dog. This was supposed to be a pet.

Ms Anderson said when she’d visited Ms Johnson’s home before, the dog would be shut in the garden, but this time Storm was in the kitchen.

It launched its attack when she went to get one of the children a drink.
