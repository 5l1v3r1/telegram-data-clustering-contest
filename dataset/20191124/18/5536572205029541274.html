FedEx Field Has Empty Parking Lots & Hundreds of Empty Seats During Lions-Redskins Game (PICS)

24 Nov 2019, 18:42 by Darrelle Lincoln

Earlier this week, there were multiple reports of game tickets going for as low as $5 for Redskins fans to purchase and come out and watch their terrible football team play football against the Detroit Lions.

Not even that was enough for fans to show up, because images on social media show a mostly empty FedEx Field parking lot as well as seats.
