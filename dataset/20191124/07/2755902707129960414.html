24 Nov 2019, 07:51 by Timothy Ndoro

Mnangagwa Expresses Relief Over Chiwenga’s Recovery

President Emmerson Mnangagwa has expressed what Vice President Constantino Chiwenga’s recovery and return home means to him personally. Mnangagwa said that he had been personally affected by Chiwenga’s recovery and was mightily relieved by the recovery. Mnangagwa expressed his relief over Chiwenga’s recovery after he had visited his deputy at his home.

Mnangagwa was accompanied by his wife, First Lady Auxillia Mnangagwa.
