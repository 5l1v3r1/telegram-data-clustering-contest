The Kabuki Warriors debut new theme at a WWE live show

24 Nov 2019, 07:08 by Shubham Roy

The WWE Women's Tag Team Champions, The Kabuki Warriors debuted a new entrance theme at a WWE live show in Grand Rapids, Michigan. One-half of the tag team champions, Kairi Sane retweeted a fan's post on Twitter which shows video footage of the team walking out to their new entrance.
