Cody Simpson shares sweet birthday tribute to Miley Cyrus as she turns 27

24 Nov 2019, 07:39

Cody Simpson has shared a sweet tribute to Miley Cyrus on her birthday, days after Elsa Pataky said ex-husband Liam Hemsworth ‘deserved better’.

Miley’s boyfriend Cody posted a message to his lady on her 27th birthday over the weekend, thanking her for ‘being herself’.

The pair have been dating for a short while, following Miley’s September split with Hunger Games actor Liam.

On Saturday, Cody, 22, had a sweet message for the Mother’s Daughter singer when, alongside a cute video, he wrote: ‘Happy birthday baby. Thanks for being you (sic)’.

Cody wasn’t the only one sharing birthday shout outs to Miley as her father Billy Ray Cyrus also had a sweet message for the star, writing: ‘Happy Birthday @mileycyrus!!! Love you so much (sic)’.

While her mother Tish shared: ‘Always and forever my angel … Happy Birthday MCzzyyyy! I love you (sic).’

‘They are very happy together. She has a new house in LA and when she’s in town, she has been spending most nights there. She was out of town and they definitely missed each other. They reunited and you could see how happy they were to be back together again. I

‘t’s clear they are very into each other. She has a busy schedule and a lot of work commitments that have kept them apart. But when they are together there’s no doubt they are crazy about each other.’

The Wrecking Ball hitmaker apparently values the ‘freedom’ she gets in her relationship with Cody.

It comes after her former sister-in-law Elsa – who is married to Chris Hemsworth – spoke about her support for Liam in the wake of his marriage split.

She said at a press conference in Madrid this week: ‘My brother-in-law, well… after a relationship that you’ve dedicated 10 years to, he’s a little bit down, but he’s coping well.
