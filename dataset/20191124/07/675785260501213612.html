Decorate your tree with these pretty ornaments, packed with make-up, skin care and pampering treats

24 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Lynne Hyland, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lynne-hyland/

Who needs boring regular decorations when you can festoon your Christmas tree with fabulous beauty baubles?

Beauty crackers and advent calendars have become festive staples for beauty brands over the past couple of years, so it's not surprising we're seeing more of them doing tree decorations too.

Whether you fancy hanging luxury skin care from your branches, or soaps, make-up, fragrance or pampering treats, there's a beauty bauble for you this year.

From high-end luxury brands like Molton Brown and L'Occitane to high street faves like No7 and Sanctuary Spa, you'll find a bauble in your budget too.
