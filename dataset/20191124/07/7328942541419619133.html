Elon Musk Tweets There Are 146k Orders for the Tesla Cybertruck

The orders were achieved without any advertising or paid endorsements.

24 Nov 2019, 07:06 by Loukia Papadopoulos

Tesla CEO Elon Musk took to Twitter on Saturday to announce that there have been about 146k orders for the carmaker’s Cybertruck, just two days after its unveiling.
