The first look of Kangana Ranaut starrer Thailavi released yesterday. The film, that will tell the story of Indian politician J Jayalalithaa, who served as the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu for fourteen years and was a mega movie star before that has Kangana Ranaut playing the lead role.

After the first look poster and the teaser of the film released yesterday, it did not take a lot of time for Twitterati to draw comparisons between the two ladies.
