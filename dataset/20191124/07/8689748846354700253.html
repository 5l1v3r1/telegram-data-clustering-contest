Stonebwoy wins AFRIMA 2019 Best African Reggae Dancehall Artist for the 3rd time

24 Nov 2019, 07:42 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian dancehall artiste, Livingstone Etse Satekla, aka Stonebwoy is arguably the most successful musicians the country has witnessed in the past decade and there’s no doubt about it.

His numerous awards speak volume for him. The latest to add to his cabinet is the Best Artist, Duo or Group in African Reggae, Ragga or Dancehall award at the 6th All Africa Music Awards, also known as AFRIMA 2019.

Stonebwoy with this has won the award for best Dancehall artiste of the year at the 2019 AFRIMA for the 3rd  time proving his worth on the continent and why he’s the only African dancehall artist to win a BET award.

He won it for the first time in 2015, then in 2018 and 2019 is his the third time he’s winning it.

For this year’s own, he was in the same category with the likes of Daphe (Cameroon), Navy Kenzo (Tanzania) Patoranking( Nigeria) Paris (Nigeria) Rosa Ree (Tanzania) Rudeboy (Nigeria), Shatta Wale (Ghana) Timaya (Nigeria) Winky D (Zimbabwe)

He performed on the night with his Bhim band and got the crowd entertained.
