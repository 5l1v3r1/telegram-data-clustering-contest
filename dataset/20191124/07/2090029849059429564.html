The full list of winners at the 2019 AFRIMA

24 Nov 2019, 07:19 by Damilola Ismail

The winners of the 6th All Africa Music Awards, AFRIMA held on Saturday, November 23, 2019, at the Eko Convention Centre of Eko Hotels and Suites, Victoria Island, Lagos, Nigeria.

There were 36 categories of the 2019 AFRIMA awards ceremony, which comprise the Continental and Regional categories and we have a full list of all the winners.

See the full list of winners below:
