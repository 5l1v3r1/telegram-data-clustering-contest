Little Paws Are Cold: Golden Retriever Pup Wants to Go Home

24 Nov 2019, 07:30

Every doggo likes to eat and play, but their favourite thing is to go outside and walk for a few hours… or for a few days. But sometimes it is too cold even for those that have a thick coat of fur, so they have to hurry and rush back to warm and cozy houses.

This little Golden Retriever knows how to walk properly and even holds a lead in its mouth. But the snow is too cold for the pup – so it needs to wrap it up as fast as possible.
