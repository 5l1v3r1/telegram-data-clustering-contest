Billie Eilish Says She Absolutely Didn’t Snub Her Fans After ‘Jimmy Kimmel’ In Instagram Rant

24 Nov 2019, 01:20 by Hannah Story

Pop superstar Billie Eilish has clapped back at paparazzi and so-called “eBayers” who she says booed her after her appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on Friday.

Responding to a video that’s been circulating since the incident, in which Eilish appears to be ignoring her screaming fans, she posted a series of Instagram Stories on Saturday.

She described how there was a group of fans to her left, and who she thought were fans and paparazzi on her right. She went to speak to the people on her left: “I walked all the way over to them and I hugged them all, I did pictures, I talked to them for a second…”

Then she went over to the right side, to people who were cheering and shouting her name. “As I got closer, I realised that it was pretty much all paparazzi and eBayers, which is what we call people that just take my signature and sell it and couldn’t give less of a fuck about me at all.

“I didn’t want to be disrespectful at all so I went up to them all, and I tried to get hugs or I tried to touch all their hands, go down the line, you know, whatever. I started to do that and nobody put their hands out and nobody tried to hug me back. I hugged one girl at the end, she was really sweet.”
