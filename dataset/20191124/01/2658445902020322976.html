'Access The Stars' officially kicks off - The Nation Newspaper

24 Nov 2019, 01:42

 Afolabi Idowu

 

ASPIRING Music Stars gathered at the Abeokuta edition of the much-awaited music reality TV show; ‘Access The Stars’. The auditions, which kicked off this week, saw talented musicians gather in Abeokuta to impress the judges at the auditions. At the end of the Abeokuta auditions, five contestants were selected to perform alongside A-list Nigerian stars. The show will continue to Lagos for the next auditions while the selected talents from Abeokuta will prepare for a special bootcamp in Lagos.

‘Access The Stars’ is an initiative of two of Nigeria’s biggest brands who have teamed up to discover new music talents across the country. ‘Access The Stars’, sponsored by Star Lager Beer and Access Bank, has begun the journey of giving exceptional Nigerians the chance to be developed and empowered to reach superstar status in the music industry.
