Ivy League climate activists break up Harvard-Yale football game

24 Nov 2019 by Mary Kay Linge

Scores of student activists from Harvard and Yale universities stormed the field at the Ivy League’s premier football match up in a climate-change protest Saturday.

The demonstrators, hoisting a banner reading “Nobody Wins — Yale & Harvard are complicit in climate injustice,” demanded that both schools divest from fossil-fuel investments, The Hill reported.

“Activism disrupts the present to change the future,” Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez tweeted approvingly.

Police had to remove the protesters from the field.
