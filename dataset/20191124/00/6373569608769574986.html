Faces and names that defined Southway in the 90s and early 00s

Take a trip down memory lane and enjoy all of the Plymouth estate's best moments from yesteryear

24 Nov 2019 by Miles O'Leary

Remember when the whole of Southway came together to fight the closure of its beloved community college? We do.

Our sister print title The Herald joined locals at marches, public meetings and on the streets in support of their impassioned cause.

No-one wanted the legendary comprehensive taken away. Sadly, it was consigned to history, way back in 2001.

But all those cherished memories of the classroom and school grounds still remain to this day. The site itself is now home to Beechwood Primary Academy.

There were many other things that defined life in Southway way back when too - as our wonderful nostalgic gallery of photos from the late 90s and early 00s prove.

Fetes, fun days and carnivals, our photographers captured all sorts of occasions. And we spoke to many on the ground about their concerns with anti-social behaviour and the like.
