Aggie Vakaloloma expected to release new album

24 Nov 2019 by Navitalai Naivalurua

Songwriter and singer, Aggie Vakaloloma is expected to release her new album very soon.

Vakaloloma says her new album will be a neat one as she is trying out a new genre of music.

She was one of nine artists that performed at Fiji's first-ever VitiFM Live Mai Rooftop concert with Digicel last night. 

Vakaloloma rocked the stage with songs such as Rutayabe, Duibana, Tulua ni Vuki, Cauravou Nikua, and Isa Mama.
