Scott Frost Reacts To Nebraska’s Blowout Win vs. Maryland

24 Nov 2019, 00:29 by Andrew Holleran

Nebraska’s bowl hopes were on the line against Maryland this afternoon. The Huskers needed a win to keep their eligibility alive heading into the regular season finale against Iowa.

Scott Frost’s team delivered in a big way.

Sure, it was just Maryland, but Nebraska was in desperate need of a blowout, comfortable win. It had been a while since the Huskers got one.

Frost and Co. got just that in College Park, Md. today.

Nebraska shellacked Maryland, 54-7, to improve to 5-6 on the season. Frost was encouraged with the result following the game.

“It gives me great enthusiasm for things to come,” Frost told reporters.
