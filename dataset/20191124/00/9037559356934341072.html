Sindh govt lifts ban on issuance of arm licenses | Pakistan - Geo.tv

24 Nov 2019 by APP

KARACHI: The ban on issuance of arm licenses in Sindh has been lifted by the provincial government, according to a notification.

The notification was issued in this regard by the Provincial Home Department.

As per the notification, the monthly quota of the approving authorities as prescribed under Rule-4.1 of the Sindh Arms Rules-2018 was restored.

It further added that only computerised arms licenses of Non-Prohibited Bore (NPB) would be issued as per the prescribed monthly quota and subject to the fulfillment of all requirements as laid down in the Sindh Arms Act-2013 and the Sindh Arms Rules-2018.
