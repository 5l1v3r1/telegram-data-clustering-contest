Kamyab Jawan Program: PM Imran constitutes 13-member steering committee | Pakistan - Geo.tv

24 Nov 2019 by Web Desk

ISLAMABAD: A thirteen-member steering committee has been constituted by Prime Minister Imran Khan for Kamyab Jawan Program.

The committee will put forward the suggestions of the new projects to prime minister regarding the youth.

Besides, it has been delegated the authority of planning strategy and program monitoring. It will also assist projects starting under Kamyab Jawan Program.

The prime minister himself will chair the committee, reported Radio Pakistan.

Other members include Special Assistant to the Prime Minister on Youth Affairs Usman Dar and federal ministers from education, economic affairs and IT Board.

Advisors on Finance and Commerce as well as the federal minister for planning have also been nominated as committee members.
