Video: Billie Eilish hosts Q&A for Just Dance 2020

24 Nov 2019, 00:55 by alba

Ubisoft recently teamed up with Billie Eilish to promote Just Dance 2020. In a new promo, the publisher asked the American singer to host a Q&A session with some of her biggest fans, particularly in regard to the Bad Guy track featured in the recently-released dance rhythm game. You can check out the Q&A by watching the embedded video below. Just Dance 2020 is available now across multiple platforms, including the original Wii.
