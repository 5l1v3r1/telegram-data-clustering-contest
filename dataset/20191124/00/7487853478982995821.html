Twitter Reacts to Phil Jones' Latest Nightmare, Calamity Man Utd, Good Man Utd & More

24 Nov 2019 by Gary Stringer

​Manchester United almost completed a completely undeserved comeback at Bramall Lane on Sunday evening, scoring three goals in ten second-half minutes after falling 2-0 behind to Sheffield United. 

But they didn't as the Blades grabbed a deserved equaliser thanks to Oli McBurnie, confirmed after VAR determined that he'd controlled the ball with his shoulder in the build up, rather than with his arm.

Before the game, Phil Jones' inclusion in the starting XI had ​Manchester United fans breaking into a nervous sweat, particularly as it wasn't immediately clear whether he'd be featuring in defence or midfield.

Naturally, after just 18 minutes, Jones proved why fans were right to be apprehensive - as abysmal positioning on two separate occasions forced David de Gea into action to deny John Lundstram from long-range, followed by David McGoldrick's close range header.

While United got away with those two mistakes, they didn't escape a third time. A simple long ball over the top proved far too much for Jones to handle as Lys Mousset shrugged him off with ease, before he teed up Lundstram - whose shot was palmed by De Gea into the path of the onrushing John Fleck to give the Blades the lead.

It went down well on Twitter, as you can imagine.
