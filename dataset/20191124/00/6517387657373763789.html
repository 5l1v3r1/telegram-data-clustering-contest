Lewis Capaldi, Stormzy and Ed Sheeran take on Tones and I for Number 1

Plus new entries for Digdat x D-Block Europe and Partynextdoor, and climbs for Arizona Zervas, Jax Jones and Endor.

24 Nov 2019 by Lauren Kreisler

Could Lewis Capaldi be within grasp of a second UK Number 1 single before the year is out?

The 2019 Scottish breakthrough star goes head-to-head with reigning chart topper Tones & I and her global chart smash Dance Monkey in this week’s Official Singles Chart race. Before You Go is up 17 places to Number 2 and trails by just 1000 chart sales after the first 48 hours, can the self-styled "America's Sweetheart" overtake Tones & I before Friday? It’s one of two Capaldi tracks heading for this week’s Top 5; Bruises (5) is up one place since last week.
