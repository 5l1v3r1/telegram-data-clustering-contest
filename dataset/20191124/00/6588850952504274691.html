Ashanti Regional Executives Celebrate Kingsley Agyemang On His Birthday

24 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The Ashanti regional Chairman of the New Patriotic Party together with his executives, today sent birthday wishes to the Registrar for the Scholarship Secretariat.

In a short message to celebrate Mr. Kingsley Agyemang, Mr. Bernard Antwi-Boasiako's popularly known as Chairman Wontumi noted that :

"Hoping this wondrful day will bring you lots of happiness and love. Wherever your feet may take, whatever endeavor you lay hands on. It will always be successful. Happy birthday."

Chairman Wontumi also praised Mr. Kingsley Agyemang for exhibiting quality leadership skills since he was appointed by President Akufo-Addo to head the Scholarship Secretariat.
He urged him to continue to give his best and serve needy student just as he has done since he was given the appointment. Featured Video
