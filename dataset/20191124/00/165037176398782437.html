Alderweireld seems ready to negotiate a renewal

24 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Things are changing at Tottenham. Belgian centre-back, Toby Alderweireld, is in the last year of his contract and it seemed inevitable that he would leave the club at the end of the season, but now he is ready to listen to renewal offers from Spurs.

Perhaps it is the arrival of Mourinho, or perhaps just a meer coincidence, but now, according to 'The Telegraph', Alderweireld is ready to at least listen to contract renewal offers from Tottenham.
