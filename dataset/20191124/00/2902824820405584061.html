Eglo Records, Vol. 2 is out next month

The 30-track compilation features releases from the last five years, alongside new exclusives and previously unreleased material

24 Nov 2019 by James Ball

Alexander Nut will celebrate ten years of his Eglo Records imprint with a new compilation, titled 'Eglo Records, Vol. 2'.

The 30-tracker features previous Eglo releases from the likes of Henry Wu and Byron the Aquarius, in addition to exclusives and previously unreleased tracks from Floating Points, Dirg Gerner and other artists.
