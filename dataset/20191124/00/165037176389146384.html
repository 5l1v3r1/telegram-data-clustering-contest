Legendary coaches looking for a job!

24 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Almost halfway through the 2019/20 season there are several top-level coaches that are looking for a coaching job. Pochettino being the latest in this.

Pochettino's sacking allowed another coaching legend to return, Jose Mourinho. The Premier League has a series of coaches looking for a job, such as Arsene Wenger.

Italy also has several legends waiting for an opportunity. Here we are speaking about Massimiliano Allegri (who has had a full year out after winning almost everything with Juventus), Gennaro Gattuso, Luciano Spalletti and Marco Giampaolo.
