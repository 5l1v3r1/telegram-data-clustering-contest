‘I Know My Husband Is A Womanizer And Its None Of Your Business’ – Lady Publicly Warns Friends Reporting To Her

24 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

A Nigerian lady identified as Joie Apera who seems to have no problem with her husband’s infidelity, has issued a stern warning to those always reporting her husband to her.

According to Jolie, she acknowledges the fact that neither her nor her spouse was perfect and will never be, in her words:

I’m not perfect and I will never be perfect in this life, likewise him….
While thanking them, the unconcerned lady stated that she is aware that her husband is a womanizer. The lady also stated that she doesn’t care to know who her husband is having an affair with, all she wants is peace of mind because nobody is perfect.
“Thanks to everyone who calls me to report my husand Jenkis Shande to me, I really appreciate you all. But that have to stop, I’m not perfect and I will never be perfect in this life. Likewise him and I know that he is a womanizer which I’m okay with it because is none of your fucking business. I’m not interested in knowing if ladies, women or girls are following him or he is following them. Peace of mind is all I need now. So keep your opinion and thoughs to yourself.
