New Apple MacBook Pro – South African pricing

24 Nov 2019 by Staff Writer

iStore has released pricing for the new Apple MacBook Pro as part of a pre-order campaign.

The 16-inch MacBook Pro was launched by Apple earlier this month and features a range of hardware improvements compared to its predecessors.

The new laptops are powered by up to an Intel octa-core processor and 64GB of RAM. They also include a new keyboard design, a 16-inch Retina Display, and more powerful GPUs.

Apple stated that the display boasts a resolution of 3,072 x 1,920 and a pixel density of 226ppi.

A six-speaker sound system, longer battery life, Touch Bar, Touch ID, a Force Touch trackpad, and Apple’s T2 Security Chip are additional features included with the new device.

South African pricing

The local pricing for the two 16-inch MacBook Pro models available at the time of writing from the iStore are below.

Colour options are Space Grey or Silver.
