Kwesi Pratt Backs NDC After U-Turn On Referendum

24 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The Managing Editor of the Insight newspaper, Kwesi Pratt Jnr does not understand why the ruling New Patriotic Party (NPP) is making a big deal out of NDC's decision to vote NO in the upcoming referendum on December 17, 2019.

Contributing to a panel discussion on Friday's edition of Peace FM morning show 'Kokrokoo', Kwesi Pratt said it is normal for one to change position especially 'in the light of new facts'.

According to him changing one's position "happens all the time otherwise why would you (NPP) change your mind on the Ameri deal after approval? I can continue citing other examples where you have also changed your mind; it's allowed and there is nothing wrong with it. you might not have the full facts...you can say something and change your position in the light of new facts; there is nothing wrong with it..."
