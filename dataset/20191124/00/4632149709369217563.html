Man on the run after murdering wife nabbed

24 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

A 29-year-old man, who had killed his wife and was on the run, was arrested by the Mahalakshmi Layout police on Saturday.


The accused has been identified as Rakesh Kumar Gupta, a native of Gonda district in Uttar Pradesh. The couple got married in 2016 and were residing in JC Nagar. Rakesh worked as medical representative while Radha was a home-maker. Police said that he had killed his wife Radha and lied to them saying that she fell off the stairs and succumbed to injuries.

According to the police, Radha had doubts that he was having an extramarital affair and the couple often fought over this. He told the police that she would always check his mobile phone and fight with him. On November 17, around 11 pm, the two had fought over the issue. In a fit of anger, Rakesh hit Radha with a rolling pin and the lid of a cooker. He then slammed her head against the wall, which killed her on the spot. The next morning, he called her parents and informed them that their daughter had slipped from the stairs and sustained injuries.


Her parents, who came down to the city, found his behaviour suspcious and approached the Mahalakshmi Layout police.
