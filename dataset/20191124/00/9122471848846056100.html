Mark Stoops Comments On The Florida State Job Rumors

24 Nov 2019, 00:34 by Alek Arend

Florida State is still going through the process of trying to find its next head coach. Rumors continue to swirl regarding coaching candidates. Top names have emerged during the search – Mark Stoops being one of the names associated.

The Kentucky head coach had little time to celebrate the Wildcats’ 50-7 blowout victory over the Tennesseee-Martin Skyhawks on Saturday. Stoops had to offer a response to a question regarding the Florida State opening after the game.

He’s clearly committed to Kentucky football.

“This is where my heart is, this is where I’m going to be,” Stoops said, via Jon Hale of the Courier Journal. “I’m going to be at Kentucky.”
