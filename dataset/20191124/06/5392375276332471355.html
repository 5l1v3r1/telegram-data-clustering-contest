In December, Xiaomi will host a presentation that will mark the arrival of the Redmi K30 (aka Xiaomi Mi 10T). The company already boasts that it will be the first smartphone with support for 5G networks from the sub-brand Redmi, and it also seems that the new product will receive a special design that is not typical for other devices of the manufacturer.

Redmi K30 will bet on a round camera

With its front panel, the Redmi K30 will look like the Samsung Galaxy S10+, the designers have opted for a dual punch-hole display in order to minimize the frames. Now, the latest news is that the main camera will be round. The solution is already adopted in some smartphones like the Huawei Mate 30 and OnePlus 7T.
