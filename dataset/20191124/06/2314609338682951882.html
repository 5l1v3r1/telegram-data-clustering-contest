Fire alarm disrupts preview screening of Frozen 2 at Rivoli Cinemas

24 Nov 2019, 06:52 by Tate Papworth

Young Disney fans have been forced out onto the street after an emergency evacuation tone sounded during a preview screening of Frozen 2 at the Rivoli Cinemas in Hawthorn East on Sunday afternoon.

Movie goers say the emergency tone sounded midway through the highly anticipated film, forcing the evacuation.

Two Metropolitan Fire Brigade trucks attended the scene and the incident has since been confirmed as a false alarm.
