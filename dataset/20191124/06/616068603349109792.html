Harrison Ford eyes TV role - Entertainment News

24 Nov 2019, 06:59 by WENN

Harrison Ford is considering his first small screen venture for an adaptation of chilling docu-series The Staircase.

The Indiana Jones star has been circling the project, which follows the story of a novelist accused of murdering his wife in 2001, after she died following a fall down a staircase in the couple's home.

According to Deadline, Ford is also attached to executive produce the seres alongside screenwriter Antonio Campos.

The docu-series was released in 2004, with director Jean-Xavier de Lestrade updating it with new information in 2018.
