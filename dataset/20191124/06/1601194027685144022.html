Bayley shows off new look ahead of Survivor Series 2019

24 Nov 2019, 06:29 by Abhilash Mendhe

BayleyBayley's new look

WWE SmackDown Women's Champion Bayley was recently spotted sporting a new look during the Survivor Series Meet and Greet session. Bayley's appearance seems to be inspired by her best friend Sasha Banks.

A short while ago, Bayley shocked the WWE Universe by attacking WWE RAW Women's Champion Becky Lynch. Soon after, Bayley debuted a completely new look right before a match where she was scheduled to compete against Charlotte Flair for the blue brand's Women's Title. Bayley's new look consisted of short black hair and a new outfit. To add to it, she destroyed the Bayley Buddies while making her entrance for the match, completing the heel turn.
