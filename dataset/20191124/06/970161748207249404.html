Deontay Wilder sends message to Tyson Fury over rematch after Luis Ortiz KO

24 Nov 2019, 06:16

Deontay Wilder has assured fans the rematch with Tyson Fury will go ahead after his demolition of Luis Ortiz in Las Vegas.

The WBC champion successfully defended his belt with a seventh-round stoppage, adding yet another one-punch knockout to his impressive resume.

Ortiz was ahead on the scorecards when Wilder landed the telling blow and he is set to fight Fury next, although the American appeared reluctant to commit to the 22 February date.

‘Next, of course we have Tyson Fury, the rematch,’ Wilder said in the ring after his victory.
