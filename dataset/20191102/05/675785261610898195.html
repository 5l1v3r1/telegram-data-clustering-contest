What channel is England vs South Africa on? Rugby World Cup Final TV and live stream information

England are looking to win their second-ever World Cup and are the big favourites heading into the clash with South Africa

02 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Aaron Flanagan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/

Eddie Jones faces his day with destiny as England take on South Africa in the Rugby World Cup Final.

Jones had always claimed that he was building England for success at this year's competition and he has guided them to the final.

But after a sensational performance against New Zealand in the semi-finals, have England peaked one game too soon?

The Red Rose are unchanged for the final - the first time Jones has named an identical starting line-up for around three years.

Here is all you need to know ahead of the match...

When is the match?

England vs South Africa takes place on the morning of Saturday November 2.

Kick-off, for those in the UK, will be at 9am.

What TV channel is the match on?

For those in the UK, the Rugby World Cup Final will again be shown on ITV, just like the rest of the competition has been.

Coverage of the match begins two hours before kick-off, with punditry starting from 7am.

Can you live stream the match?

If you are in the UK, you will be able to live stream the match completely free of charge.

ITV can be streamed on their website if you click on the 'watch live' tab on their homepage.
