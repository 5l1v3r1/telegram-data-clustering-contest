In pictures: England agony as South Africa win Rugby World Cup

02 Nov 2019, 12:15

England’s dreams ended in tears today, with South Africa winning the Rugby World Cup final.

The Springboks beat England 32-12 at the International Stadium Yokohama, in a thrilling finale.

Fans flocked to pubs across England to enjoy the game – but ultimately were left drowning their sorrows.
