Two-car smash at Loch Lomond as emergency services rush to scene

Police and ambulance are still on the scene of the crash on the A82 at Luss with the road partially blocked.

02 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

Police are at the scene of a two car crash on the road around Loch Lomond .

Emergency services, including ambulance, rushed to the scene of the smash on the A82 at Luss around 11.20am today.

Motorists are advised to slown down on approach as the road is partially blocked. Delays are expected.

Officers are still at the scene.

A Police Scotland Spokesperson said on Twitter : "Police and ambulance currently on scene and dealing with a two car road traffic collision on the A82 at Luss with the road partially blocked.

"Please slow down on approach."
