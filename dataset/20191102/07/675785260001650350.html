What channel is Arsenal vs Wolves on? TV and live stream information

The pressure is growing on Unai Emery and the Arsenal squad and they face another tough test as Wolves visit the Emirates

02 Nov 2019, 07:26 by Aaron Flanagan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/

Arsenal return to Premier League action for the first time since Granit Xhaka’s extraordinary outburst.

But one week on from his expletive-ridden rant, Xhaka will not be in the squad to take on Wolves at the Emirates.

Xhaka sat out of the Carabao Cup thriller with Liverpool on Wednesday night and again will not play a part as Arsenal return to league duties.

Standing in Arsenal’s way are Wolves, who have had an upturn in form following a difficult start to the season.

Here is all you need to know ahead of the game…

When is the game?

The match will be played on Saturday 2 November at 15:00 GMT.

Where is the game being played?

The game is being played at Arsenal ’s home ground, the Emirates Stadium.

What TV channel is the game on?

Due to the Saturday 3pm blackout, the match will not be shown in the UK.

It did not get selected to be moved for TV coverage.

Can you live stream the game?

Due to the lack of TV coverage, there is no available live stream for this match.
