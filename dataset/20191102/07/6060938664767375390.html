Rohit Sharma shares 'Zlatan moment' as Ranveer Singh finds it 'cool'

02 Nov 2019, 07:26 by IANS

NEW DELHI: India's stand-in skipper Rohit Sharma on Friday posted a throwback picture with Ravindra Jadeja and Ishant Sharma and that has gone viral with Rohit comparing Ishant to Zlatan Ibrahimovic due to the similarity in hairstyle.

The caption in the photo read: "We got zlatan amongst us. Great fun chatting."

In fact, it also got Bollywood star Ranveer Singh excited and he wrote: "Cool."

India will play the opening T20I of the three-match series against Bangladesh on Sunday.
