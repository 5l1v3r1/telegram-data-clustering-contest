Trump Says de Blasio and Cuomo Ruined New York for Him

US President Donald Trump on Friday tweeted that he loved New York yet the elected officials running the city and state had undermined his love for the Big Apple.

02 Nov 2019, 04:53

In a series of tweets about making Florida his primary residence, the president ripped Gov. Andrew Cuomo, Mayor Bill de Blasio and New York prosecutors investigating tax returns for his decision to resettle at his Mar-a-Lago resort.
