Scuffle Between Police And Advocates At Tis Hazari Court, Police Vehicle Set Ablaze

The cause of the incident is yet to be ascertained.

02 Nov 2019, 11:01 by Press Trust of India

A scuffle broke out between police personnel and advocates at Tis Hazari Court on Saturday afternoon during which a police vehicle was set afire, officials said.

The cause of the incident is yet to be ascertained, they said.

A vehicle was set on fire during the fracas, the officials said, adding that further details are awaited.Â
