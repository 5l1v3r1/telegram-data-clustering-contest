NU'EST win #1 on November 2nd 'Show! Music Core'!

02 Nov 2019, 10:06 by germainej

NU'EST took #1 with their track "Love Me" on the November 2nd installment of 'Show! Music Core'!

NU'EST's "Love Me" was up against Song Ha Ye's "New Love" and AKMU's "How Can I Love the Heartbreak, You're the One I Love", and NU'EST took their fourth music show win with "Love Me" and their first win on 'Show! Music Core' ever this week. Congrats to NU'EST!
