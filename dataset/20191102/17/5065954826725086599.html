Cleanfuel to roll back gasoline, diesel prices

02 Nov 2019, 17:31 by https://www.facebook.com/BusinessMirror/

Facebook Twitter Pinterest WhatsApp Linkedin ReddIt Email Print Tumblr Telegram Mix VK Digg LINE Viber

Local independent fuel company Cleanfuel announced a pump price rollback in all Cleanfuel Stations effective this Sunday, November 3 at 4:01PM.

Cleanfuel will reduce its diesel prices by P0.25 per liter while gasoline prices will be reduced by P0.10 per liter.
