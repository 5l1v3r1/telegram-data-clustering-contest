Car wrecked after driver lost control and flipped on M8 in shocking crash

Shocking pictures posted by police show the smashed-up vehicle lying on its back after the crash near Livingston in West Lothian.

02 Nov 2019, 17:37 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

This is the horrific wreckage of a car which flipped after the driver lost control due to the wet weather on the M8.

Emergency services rushed to the scene of the smash at J3 eastbound near Livingston in West Lothian just after midnight on Saturday November 2.

Three people were taken to hospital as a precaution, but thankfully no-one was seriously injured.

Police Scotland issued a picture of the vehicle on Twitter showing its mangled back-end as the car lies on its roof.
