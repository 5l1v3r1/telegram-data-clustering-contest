England vs South Africa free live stream: Watch Rugby World Cup Final for free

England are taking on South Africa in the Rugby World Cup Final and the match can be live streamed free of charge

02 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Aaron Flanagan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/

England's day with destiny has arrived as they take on South Africa in the Rugby World Cup Final.

Eddie Jones' men produced a spectacular performance to see themselves past New Zealand in the semi-finals, but can they find that intensity again?

The Red Rose are unchanged for the final - the first time Jones has named an identical starting line-up for around three years.

But will it be enough to see themselves past South Africa and right the wrongs of the 2007 final?

Here is all you need to know ahead of the match...

When is the match?

Saturday November 2.

Kick-off, for those in the UK, will be at 9am.

What TV channel is the match on?

For those in the UK, the Rugby World Cup Final will again be shown on ITV.

Coverage of the match will begin from 7am, with full build-up.

How to live stream the match for free

The match will be free to be live streamed, providing that you are based in the UK.

ITV can be streamed on their website if you click on the 'watch live' tab on their homepage.

The full coverage will be available on there.
