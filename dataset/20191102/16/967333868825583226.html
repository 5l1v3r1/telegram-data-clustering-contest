At Least 10 Killed by Explosion in Syria's Tel Abyad - Report

The explosion reportedly occurred in a market, claiming the lives of civilians.

02 Nov 2019, 16:37

At least 10 people were killed by an explosion in the Syrian town of Tel Abyad, Turkish Anadolu agency reports.

It added that the incident took place in a market, resulting in civilian deaths. 

According to Hürriyet, the injured were taken to a hospital in Akçakale. The Turkish armed forces have strengthened security on the border with Syria following the explosion.

Meanwhile, the Syrian Observatory says that there was a car explosion in Tel Abyad, in which 10 people were killed and about 20 injured.
