Opposition parties to hold APC in Islamabad today

21 Nov 2019, 10:09 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Opposition s All Parties Conference (APC) will be held in Islamabad today (Thursday) to discuss the future course of action against the government including anti-government rallies, Dunya News reported.

According to sources, the All Parties Conference will be held at the residence of Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) leader Tariq Fazal Chaudhry in Islamabad.

Sources further said that Pakistan People’s Party (PPP), Jamiat Ulema-i-Islam-Fazl (JUI-F) and other opposition parties will attend the meeting.
