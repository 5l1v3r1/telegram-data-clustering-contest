Save 50% on Massive X in Native Instruments’ “biggest ever” Black Friday sale

Native Instruments has kicked off what it’s calling its “biggest ever Thanksgiving sale.” These discounts will be available throughout the Black Friday weekend and on into December.

If you own a previous version of the Komplete bundle, you can upgrade to the latest one at a 50% discount, and there are also half price offers on a wide range of instruments and effects. There are some great Black Friday plugin deals to be had, including Massive X, NI’s latest supersynth, and a whole host of other titles. Traktor Pro, the flagship four-decking DJing software, also has a 50% reduction.

There are also big savings to be had when you buy one of NI’s hardware/software bundles. So, if you’ve been waiting to pick up a Maschine, Komplete Kontrol or Komplete Audio interface, wait no longer.

Check out all the offers on the Native Instruments website. The sale runs until 9 December.
