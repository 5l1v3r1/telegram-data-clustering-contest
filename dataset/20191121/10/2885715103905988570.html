Viral video: Children skip using a dead snake as rope

The three children are seen playing with the snake, with two of them holding the head and tail of the reptile, like a rope. The third girl is seen laughing as she jumps over it.

21 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Trends Desk

A video of some children skipping in Vietnam has gone viral after it was revealed that ‘the rope’ they were jumping over was actually a dead snake. The video shows two children holding the head and tail of the snake to use it as a rope, while another girl skips over it, laughing.

A woman’s voice can be heard from behind the camera encouraging the children to play, even as they sing.
