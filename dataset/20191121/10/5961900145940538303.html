Vegemite Launches Personalised Care Packages To Gift Yr Overseas Mates This Chrissy

21 Nov 2019, 10:28 by By PEDESTRIAN.TV

Vegemite has announced their new ‘Mitey Aussie Care Packages’ which can be personalised and gifted to your overseas mates this Christmas. This is surely the greatest way to earn some brownie points with your fellow expats.

“We know that our VEGE-Mates living overseas are missing the taste of Australia, especially around Christmas time, and we’re delighted to now ship Vegemite internationally,” Senior Vegemite Marketing Manager Matt Gray said in a press release.

The ‘Mitey Aussie Care Package’ – pictured below –  includes a personalised 380g Vegemite Jar, socks, a mug, 220g Vegemite Jar and Arnott’s Shapes Vegemite & Cheese.

The package itself is AU $49.95, plus between $15-30 for postage if you’re shipping to a pal over New Zealand, Canada, US, UK.

The ‘Mitey Aussie Care Package’ is available for purchase via their online store. You can grab your own package for a mate now until midnight on Friday 13 December (ominous day but, whatever, don’t mind my superstitions) or until stocks last.
