NO MARRIAGE PLANS SOON

Sunshine Cruz plans to enjoy single life and with kids...for now

21 Nov 2019, 10:18 by JANNIELYN ANN BIGTAS, GMA News

Sunshine Cruz admitted she has no plans to tie the knot with her partner Macky Mathay yet, saying she wants to enjoy her single life with her children first.

"Wala, kaka-annul ko lang. Ine-enjoy ko muna 'yong pagiging single, hindi pa nga nalilipat ng Cruz, magma-Mathay na," Sunshine told the press in an interview after her contract signing with Em-Core Dot Net, her first endorsement deal with her daughters.

The actress said she is currently happy with her partner Macky, who she's been in a relationship with for over three years.

"'Yong set-up ko kay Macky, OK naman kami, we inspire each other. OK naman kami nagkakaintindihan kami so 'yon na muna. Gusto ko muna i-enjoy muna 'to," Sunshine said.

Sunshine continued and said she wants to focus on saving for her dream travel destinations with her three daughters Angelina, Sam and Chesca.

"Gusto ko muna i-enjoy muna 'to and mag-focus muna ako sa pag-iipon para ma-travel ko 'yong mga anak ko," she said.

"Gusto nilang pumunta pa sa Europe, gusto nila mag-Italy, gusto ko muna pag-igihan ang pagtatrabaho para madala ko sila sa mga dream destinations nila," she added.

It's been a little over a year since Sunshine announced that a court granted her petition for the annulment of her marriage to Cesar Montano, the father of her three children.
