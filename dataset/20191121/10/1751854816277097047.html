Huawei’s sub-brand Honor is all set to launch its new Honor V30 series of smartphones in its home market next week. Ahead of the official announcement, the company has been teasing some of the features of the smartphone.

In a similar teasing of the features, the company has revealed that the Honor V30 series will come with powerful video shooing features. The phone is said to come equipped with a “movie” lens for better video capturing.

On the back side, the phone has a rectangular camera module placed at the upper-left corner which looks similar to that of the Samsung Galaxy M30s and the upcoming Galaxy A51 phone. The module has three cameras and an LED flash.

As per the rumors, the smartphone could be equipped with a primary lens of 60-megapixel. A recent report has also revealed that Honor V30 series will come with the new Camera Matrix algorithm that will optimize the photography experience.

The Honor V30 renders reveal that it will be arriving in Orange and Blue colors. Both color variants have gradient finish that gives it a premium appeal. Further, the display of the phone has a pill-shaped cutout for housing the two front-facing cameras.

On the right side, there’s a volume rocker and a side-mounted fingerprint reader is placed. There is a USB-C port, a microphone, a SIM tray and an external speaker on the bottom side of the Honor V30 while on the topside, there’s an IR blaster and an additional microphone but the phone lacks 3.5mm audio jack on the device.

Under the hood, the V30 Pro smartphone will be powered by Kirin 990 5G SoC and will be backed by a 4,200mAh battery with support for 22.5W fast charging support. It remains to be seen how the company prices the V30 and V30 Pro smartphones.
