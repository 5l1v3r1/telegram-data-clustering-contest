Jersey Shore’s Angelina Pivarnick Marries Chris Larangeira With JWoww As Bridesmaid

21 Nov 2019, 10:12 by Rachelle Lewis

Jersey Shore: Family Vacation star Angelina Pivarnick married Chris Larangeira today. The brunette beauty said “I Do” alongside three of her famous costars.

Entertainment Tonight was first to break the news Pivarnick is a married lady. The couple wed at Park Château Estate & Gardens in East Brunswick, New Jersey. Pivarnick and Larangeira were surrounded by nothing but love from family and friends, as they exchanged vows.

The blushing bride chose a white strapless Castle Couture dress. She wore a matching veil that included a silver tiara and matching jewelry. There is no question Pivarnick was a stunning bride. Her new husband, Larangeira went with a classic black tuxedo custom made by Karako Suits.

Once the ceremony was over it was time to party. The newlyweds and their guests attended a reception filled with a massive martini bar, as well as delicious eats and a giant elegant cake.

In a surprising turn of events for fans, Pivarnick had JWoww as a bridesmaid. The two ladies had a bitter blowout following Jennifer “JWoww” Farley’s younger boyfriend, Zack Carpinello, hitting on Pivarnick last month.

Whatever bad blood was between JWoww and Pivarnick is now squashed. JWoww, along with Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi and Deena Cortese, all stood by their costars side on her wedding day. Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino and his wife Lauren, as well as Pauly “DJ Pauly D” DelVecchio, Vinny Guadagnino and Ronnie-Ortiz-Magro were also in attendance.

Larangeira asked Pivarnick to be his wife last January, after dating nearly a year. in an elegant Italian dinner that took place in their home. She shared her happy news with a collage of photos from the special night.
