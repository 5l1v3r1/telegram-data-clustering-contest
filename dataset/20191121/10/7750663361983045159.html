Jose Mourinho 'planning talks with Tottenham Hotspur contract rebels'

Jose Mourinho will reportedly hold talks with Tottenham Hotspur contract rebels Christian Eriksen, Jan Vertonghen and Toby Alderweireld over the next few weeks.

21 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Leo Nieboer

The 56-year-old was announced as the new Spurs boss on Wednesday less than 24 hours after Mauricio Pochettino received his marching orders from the club.

Mourinho faces the prospect of losing the aforementioned trio for free over the summer due to their contracts expiring in June.

According to The Mirror, the Portuguese coach will hold talks with the trio over the next few weeks.

Chairman Daniel Levy is concerned by the prospect of each player being free to speak with other clubs and sign a pre-contract agreement in January.

It is claimed that Eriksen, Vertonghen and Alderweireld are all open to the prospect of working with Mourinho, who they view as a serial winner.
