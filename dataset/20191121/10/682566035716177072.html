WOW! Fan designs epic Kaizer Chiefs kit for 2020/21 season

21 Nov 2019, 10:45

Cape Town - A football mad fan designed a beautiful Kaizer Chiefs home, away and alternative kit for the 2020/21 season.

Jay Chez has shared three diverse looking kits on social media and received raving reviews from Amakhosi fans. 

"This is my Kaizer Chiefs jersey designs idea for next season. Please rate out 10 and help," Chez said via a Twitter post.

However, Chez has reassured followers that the designs are not the official kits for next season but a mere appreciation for the Soweto giants.

"This are not official kaizer Chiefs jerseys they are just design ideas from a KCFC fan."

Amakhosi have been beaming in the Absa Premiership with a 10-point gap at the top of the log standings and are one match away from a Telkom Knockout (TKO) final.

The Soweto giants have thus far accumulated 28-points from 11 games - 9 wins, 1 loss and 1 draw - while current champions Mamelodi Sundowns are in second place (18-points) with a match in hand.

On Sunday, the Glamour Boys take on a plucky Maritzburg United at the Mbombela Stadium while the Brazilians host Steve Komphela's Golden Arrows in the TKO semi-final.

Kick-off for both matches are at 15:00.
