I'm A Celebrity's Andy Whyment digs through piles of raw meat and fish guts in first Bushtucker trial

Absolutely disgusting!

21 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Rebecca Carter

Andy Whyment digs through piles of raw meat and fish guts in his first Bushtucker trial on I'm A Celebrity.

The Coronation Street actor was revealed as a new campmate at the end of Wednesday night's episode along with Call The Midwife star Cliff Parisi.

In a first look at tonight's trial, Andy is left disgusted as he tries and finds stars buried in the raw guts in the gruesome Frontier of Fear task.

Andy is locked in a meat shed and has to hunt for the stars to bag meals for his new campmates.

The star is seen looking through shelves of meat while the entire room is covered with the guts.

In a clip released by ITV, Andy tells hosts Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly: "There's just loads of guts and worms and stuff."

Ant asks: "How does it smell?" to which Andy says: "Absolutely disgusting! Uh this absolutely stinks."

Dec says: "Everything is covered in offal and fish guts," as Ant adds: "There's 25 pigeons in there with you."
