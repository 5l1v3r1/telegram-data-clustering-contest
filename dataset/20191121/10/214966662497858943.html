Woman and two kids missing from Gold Coast found safe

21 Nov 2019, 10:55 by Shanee Dobeson

UPDATE @ 8.55 PM | A Gold Coast woman and two children missing from the Gold Coast have been located.

Police confirmed that the 27-year-old and her two children, aged 7 and 10-months, were located safe and well a short time ago.

“Well Done Team! The 27yo woman and her two children have been located safe and well THANKS TO YOU.”

“Someone saw our social media post and was able to direct police to their location.”

EARLIER @ 7.00 PM | Police are appealing for urgent help to locate a Gold Coast woman and her two children, who were last seen in Coombabah on Tuesday.

The 27-year-old and the children, a boy aged 7 and a 10-month-old baby girl left a home on Angela Avenue some time Tuesday morning intending to drive to the Sunshine Coast for a few days.

Family and friends have not heard from the woman since, which police say is out of character, with serious concerns now being held for their safety and well-being.

Anyone with information is urged to immediately contact police.

If you have information for Police, please contact Policelink on 131 444 or provide information using their online form 24 hours per day.

You can also report information about crime anonymously to Crime Stoppers by calling 1800 333 000 or via crimestoppersqld.com.au 24 hours per day. Crime Stoppers is a registered charity and community volunteer organisation.
