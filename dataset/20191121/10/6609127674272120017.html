Football Manager 2020 wonderkids: Celtic, Rangers and rest of Scotland’s highly-rated stars

21 Nov 2019, 10:41 by Ewan Paton

EVERY year gamers across the world are keen to take a look at Football Manager wonderkids.

With FM20 released earlier this week, this time around will be no different.

Both Celtic and Rangers have an array of young talent either on the fringes of the first team or in the academy.

Elsewhere, other Premiership clubs are littered with starlets ready for action, with some already making their mark.

We've already highlighted who is worth more in the computer game - Alfredo Morelos or Odsonne Edouard?

But now SunSport takes a look at the best wonderkids and young talent that Scotland has to offer...
