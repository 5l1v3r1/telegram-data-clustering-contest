Xiaomi’s 100W Super Charge Turbo wired fast charge technology to arrive in devices next year

In the last two years, we have witnessed great strides in fast charging technology – both wired and wireless. At the moment, OPPO’s 65W SuperVOOC 2.0 fast charging is the fastest on a commercially available smartphone – the Reno Ace.

Prior to the launch of the OPPO Reno Ace, Xiaomi showed off its 100W Super Charge Turbo fast charge technology. Earlier this week, it confirmed at its Xiaomi Developer Conference that the technology will be available in devices starting next year.

The technology was expected to show up in phones this year following statements by Xiaomi executives Lu Weibing and Lin Bin that the technology was going to being mass-produced. However, the tech didn’t show up in any of Xiaomi and Redmi’s flagship phones announced afterward such as the Mi 9 Pro 5G and the Redmi K20 Pro Premium Edition.

At the developer conference, Xiaomi disclosed more details about the fast charging technology. The technology will use two batteries just like the OPPO Find X Lamborghini Edition. It will also have software and hardware protection to keep your device safe. Xiaomi says the charger will have an ultra-high efficiency of 98% and will be built with high-quality materials.
