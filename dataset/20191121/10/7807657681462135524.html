Should you buy the Lenovo Yoga C940 or Surface Pro 7?

21 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Rich Edmonds

Traditional Performer

Lenovo Yoga C940

Latest Surface

Microsoft Surface Pro 7

Lenovo Yoga C940 comes in two sizes: 14 and 15 inches. Depending on the size you choose, you'll be able to kit it out with some powerful internal components, making it a great 2-in-1.

From $1,150 at Lenovo

Pros

  • 10th Gen Intel CPUs available
  • Beautiful 4K touch display option
  • Soundbar hinge with full audio
  • NVIDIA GTX 1650 GPU available
  • Comfortable keyboard

Cons

  • Heavier
  • More pricey

The Surface Pro 7 is one of the more powerful Windows tablets, rocking the latest 10th Gen Intel CPUs. You can't choose a dedicated GPU and you must buy the keyboard cover separately, but it's a stunning form factor.

From $749 at Microsoft

Pros

  • More affordable
  • Great tablet form factor
  • Works with Wi-Fi 6
  • Up to 1TB storage
  • Still a solid design

Cons

  • No dedicated GPU
  • Less capable configurations
  • No included pen or keyboard

Lenovo's Yoga C940 is a very capable 2-in-1, depending on which size you go for. The larger 15-inch model can be kitted out with a dedicated GPU from NVIDIA too, but you'll be paying a premium. If you're more into owning a powerful tablet, the Surface Pro 7 is a great option.
