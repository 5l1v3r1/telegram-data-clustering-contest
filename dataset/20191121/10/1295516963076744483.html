Martin Scorsese's The Irishman Gets a Marvel Makeover in Parody Trailer

21 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Steve Russell

Not one to miss an opportunity, Jimmy Kimmel has poked fun at the recent controversy created after filmmaker Martin Scorsese stated that Marvel Studios movies don't count as "cinema," referring to them instead as "theme parks."

On Jimmy Kimmel Live!, Kimmel introduced a parody trailer for the Scorsese's new movie The Irishman entitled The Irish Man. The tongue-in-cheek take turns Robert De Niro's character into an Irish-themed version of Iron Man and gifts him a special ring that glows with the power of the cloverleaf. Then, after offering his services and promising that "Whatever you need to do, I'm available," an Iron Man helmet shuts over De Niro's face, its eyes glowing green before cutting to inside where we see De Niro surrounded by Iron Man's HUD display.
