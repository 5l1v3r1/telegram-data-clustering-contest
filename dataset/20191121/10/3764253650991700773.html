Jose Mourinho holds first training session with Tottenham Hotspur players

21 Nov 2019, 10:35

- Jose Mourinho had his first training session with Tottenham players ahead of a London derby against Westham

- The tactician seems to have already blended with the players barely 36 hours into his new job

- Video highlights of his first session with Spurs have since gone viral

- Mourinho was picked to manage the team after Pochettino was fired over poor performance.

Jose Mourinho has already established himself as new Tottenham manager barely a day into his appointment if training videos doing rounds online are anything to go by.

The Portuguese had less than 72 hours to instill some of his tactics on his new set of players as they are set to travel to Westham for a London derby on Saturday, November 23.

A video released by Tottenham highlighted the key aspects of his first training session with players seemingly in high spirit.
