Festive concert to raise money for south Shropshire village hall

21 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Charlotte Bentley

A festive concert will take place in south Shropshire to raise money for village hall funds.

The Annual Festive Concert will take place in Bucknell, near Knighton, to raise money for their local hall by getting into the festive spirit with entertainment and Christmas food and drink.

Bucknell Memorial Hall will host the Orthopaedic Male Voice Choir from Oswestry, who will entertain guests with traditional songs and music.

The event will take place on Saturday, November 30 at 7.30pm when the choir will perform.
