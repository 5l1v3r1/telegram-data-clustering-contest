Oyo: Gunmen Attack Makinde’s Commissioner In Ibadan

21 Nov 2019, 10:12 by Olugbenga Ige

The Oyo State Commissioner for Lands, Abdulraheem Abiodun, has been attacked by unknown gunmen in Ibadan, the state capital.

Concise News understands the attack took place on Wednesday around 8:00 p.m. after the commissioner was dropped at his residence.

It was also learned that the gunmen killed his driver in the process and also injured his orderly who is currently hospitalised after sustaining bullet wounds.

As at the time of filing the report, the state Police Command said there will be a briefing with journalists later in the day to give further details on the incident.
