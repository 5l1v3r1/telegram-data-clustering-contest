Ole Gunnar Solskjaer 'determined to sign Erling Braut Haaland'

Manchester United manager Ole Gunnar Solskjaer has reportedly made completing the signing of Red Bull Salzburg striker Erling Braut Haaland a priority ahead of next summer's transfer window.

21 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Leo Nieboer

The Norwegian has shot to prominence this season after accumulating a tally of 26 goals from 19 games in all competitions since August.

It has been suggested that United consider the 19-year-old as a top target ahead of the summer transfer window.

According to the Daily Mail, Solskjaer has expressed a determination to secure Haaland, who is viewed as somebody capable of developing a partnership with Marcus Rashford.

United are desperate for reinforcements up front having dispensed with and then failed to replace attacking duo Romelu Lukaku and Alexis Sanchez over the summer.
