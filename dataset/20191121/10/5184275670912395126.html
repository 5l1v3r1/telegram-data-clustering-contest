Ranu Mondal’s Makeup Photos Are FAKE; Her Artist Posts The Original Photos To Shut The Trolls

The sensational singer was trolled for her makeup which was much more whiter than her original shade.

21 Nov 2019, 10:19 by Koimoi.com Team

Ranu Mondal is a proud example of rags to riches and the singer is making the best use of it. The internet sensation, who got her break for singing through Himesh Reshammiya, is always in the news for one or the other thing. Recently, the sensational singer was trolled for her makeup which was much more whiter than her original shade.

The makeup artist who did Ranu’s makeup was also bashed for experimenting with her face and not keeping the makeup natural. Seeing the negative remarks, the makeup artist finally broke her silence on the news and claimed that the picture that went viral online were fake.

Sandhya’s Makeover, the artist who helped Ranu Mondal get ready for the event, took to social media to reveal what Ranu’s makeup actually looked like. Sandhya wrote, “As you can see, this is the difference between the work that we have done and the ‘Fake’ picture that has been edited to an extent. All the jokes and trolls are fine and they make us laugh too but to hurt someone sentiments, that’s not a very good thing to do.”

She added, “We truly hope that you all will understand the truth and realise the difference between the fake one and the one that is genuine. That’s all we ask for.” The picture shows a beautiful Ranu in the makeup done by Sandhya compared to the picture that went viral.

Ever since her talent’s discovery, Ranu Mondal has been in the limelight for something or the other. Recently, she came in the news for misbehaving with a fan who asked for a selfie. The netizens were quite upset with her behaviour and backlashed the sensational singer with harsh comments. Himesh Reshammiya had to intervene and defend Ranu for what she did.

On the work front, Himesh just released the first song from his film Happy Hardy And Heer titled Aashiqui Mein Teri 2.0 which is sung by Ranu and him.
