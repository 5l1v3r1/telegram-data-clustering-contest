Bodex Social Media Hangout by Florence Bodex Hungbo

21 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Laila Ijeoma

Bodex Social Media Hangout 2019 convened by renowned media professional and entrepreneur, Florence Bodex Hungbo holds November 30, 2019 at Raddision Blu, Ikeja, Lagos.

According to Bodex:

“The aim is to bring under one umbrella the producers, who are publishers of news and the consumers, who are the fans either as followers and readers and other stakeholders to co-exist.

“This initiative will enable all stakeholders connect, network, share and have fun.

More of this kind of interaction can bring sanity to our youths by creating more awareness with constant reminder of the benefit of using social strengths in nations building.”

Florence Bodex Hungbo added that the event will feature different speakers from all fields to speak on how social media can be used to stop fake news and fallacious comments.

There will be performances by music stars, drinks and foods, comedians and actors to grace the hangout, including products exhibition, games and competitions

The renowned media professional and entrepreneur, while speaking to newsmen about the maiden edition of Bodex Social Media Hangout, BSMH, said the event will bring governors, representatives of ministries and parastatals, business executives, journalists from across platforms and media, social media influencers and fans under one roof.

Producers and consumers, both in the traditional and new media sub-sectors, should interact more, as they need each other to co-exist as such interaction is a tool to positively shape the society because without publishers there won’t be readers and vice versa.
