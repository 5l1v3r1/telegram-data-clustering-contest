Forget Immortal - Marvel Introduces The Living Hulk

21 Nov 2019, 10:11 by L.D. Nolan

Bruce Banner will have some competition in Al Ewing and Joe Bennett's Immortal Hulk #31, which will see the Living Hulk join the fray.

Living Hulk, who is teased in Marvel's February solicitations for Immortal Hulk, is one of the aliases of Xemnu, who was created by Jack Kirby and introduced in 1960's Journey into Mystery #62. Xemnu has previously tangled with both Hulk and Red Hulk, and he appears on several billboards on Alex Ross' cover for this issue, indicating he's indeed the one being referenced in here. Although the plot summary for the issue teases Xemnu acting heroically, things may not pan out that way.
