FACT CHECK: Did Gambhir say commentary is more important than meetings?

21 Nov 2019, 10:56 by Times Fact Check

Highlights

  1. Trimmed version of Gambhir's video is being shared to claim that he said “Doing commentary is more important than attending meetings to discuss pollution crisis”.
  2. In the original video, he is talking about the work he has done as MP to tackle pollution.
  3. Among those making the false claim were AAP leaders and media coordinator.

CLAIM

A short video clip of East Delhi MP and former Indian opener Gautam Gambhir is being shared to claim that he said “Doing commentary is more important than attending meetings to discuss pollution crisis”.

A Twitter user by the name of Aarti shared the clip from her verified handle with the same claim and cautioned people against voting for celebrities in elections.
