15-year-old boy stabbed at Bronx subway stop

21 Nov 2019, 20:52 by Craig McCarthy

A group of men stabbed teen on a Bronx subway platform Thursday morning, cops said.

The 15-year-old boy was stabbed multiple times in the shoulder while he was on the southbound No. 6 platform at the Westchester Square-East Tremont Avenue station around 11 a.m., according to cops.

Witnesses told police they saw seven men running from the subway stop, leaving the boy bleeding on the platform, cops said.

The 15-year-old was taken to Jacobi Medical Center where he was expected to recover.

It was unclear what caused the brawl to break out, and no arrests have been made, officials said.
