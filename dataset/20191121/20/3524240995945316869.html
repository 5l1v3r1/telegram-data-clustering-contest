Hillary Clinton hails Michelle Obama over Grammys nomination

21 Nov 2019, 20:19 by Oluwakemi Abimbola

Former First Lady of United States, Michelle Obama, has been nominated for the Grammys.

The audio version of her memoir, Becoming, secured her a spot in the ‘Spoken Word’ category at next year’s 61st Annual Grammy Awards.
