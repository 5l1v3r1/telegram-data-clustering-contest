Nigerians react to Mompha’s ₦5 million lawsuit against EFCC

21 Nov 2019, 20:38 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Following Instagram bigboy, Ismaila Mustapha popularly known as Mompha, lawsuit against antigraft agency, EFCC, Nigerians have taken to Twitter to bare their thoughts.

Earlier today Mompha filed a lawsuit against the EFCC, challenging his continued detention. He has asked the court to mandate the EFCC to pay him N5 million for unlawful detention.

Recall, Mompha was arrested in October on allegations of Cyber crimes and Money laundering in a joint operation by the EFCC and INTERPOL, in Abuja.
