Fans Chooses Sides In Leanne Locken And Kary Brittangham Feud After Episode Airs Where Leanne Mocks Kary’s Ethnicity

21 Nov 2019, 20:58 by Ricki Mathers

The first of a string of problematic episodes of Real Housewives of Dallas aired Wednesday night. Leanne Locken has come under fire for making racial comments about her co-star Kary Brittingham.

During the latest installment of RHOD, the ladies travel to Thailand where Kary and Leanne bump heads.

First, the group visits a few temples where Locken tells Kary to take off her hat multiple times. She then says to a co-star Kary should respond to her in ‘Mexican words’ after they get into a mini spat.

Then, at a dinner where D’andra Simmons wore Leanne’s infinity dress as a joke, Locken was offended by Brittingham who took it as an opportunity to make fun of her creation.
