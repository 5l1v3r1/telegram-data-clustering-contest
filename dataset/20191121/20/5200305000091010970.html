Freida Pinto engaged to boyfriend Cory Tran

21 Nov 2019, 20:33 by Raj Baddhan

‘Slumdog Millionaire’ (2008) actress, Freida Pinto has announced her engagement to photographer boyfriend, Cory Tran.

The news was announced on social media, which coincided with Tran’s birthday. The actress had previously been linked to Dev Patel, who she dated for six years until 2014.

Pinto shared an image on social media, along with the caption, “It all makes sense now. Life makes sense, the world makes sense, the past tears and trials make sense, what wise old lovers said about love makes sense, where I am makes sense and where I want to go completely makes sense.”

“You my love are just the most beautiful creation to have ever walked into my life,” she continued. “And you are here to stay. Well, I am making you stay. Ha! All my love with all my heart. Oh and Happiest Birthday sweet Fiancé!”
