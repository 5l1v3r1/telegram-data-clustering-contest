B&M releases its Black Friday 2019 deals with £100 off TVs, cordless vacuums and more

Amazon Alexa Speaker was £79 but will be on sale for £39

21 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Catherine Murphy

Bargain retailer B&M has announced its Black Friday deals with customers able to save up to 60% off branded electrical products.

This year B&M is offering its customers 10 days of deals which will be able available from Friday, November 22 until Monday, December 2.

Shoppers will even be able to pick up items for much less than in other high street stores. For example, the Hoover H-free Cordless Vacuum will go on sale tomorrow for £60 with the same item on sale at Argos for £149.99.

Other deals include a Samsung 50" Smart 4K Ultra HD TV for £350 reduced from £450 and Beat earphones for £29 which are currently on sale at Currys for £54.95.

For anyone wanting to take a look at the full list of offers, a full list will be available on B&M's website here.

Deals will also be available on toy brands including LOL surprise, LEGO, Peppa Pig and more.

It seems many retailers have decided to extend its Black Friday deals this year, with Asda, Smyths and Amazon have already started its offers.
