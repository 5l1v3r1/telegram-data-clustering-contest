The Voice: Gwen Stefani Looked "Good as Hell" Performing a Lizzo Cover With Her Team

21 Nov 2019, 20:15 by Karenna Meredith

Gwen Stefani feels "Good as Hell," and she wants the world to know it. On Tuesday night's episode of The Voice, Stefani joined her team members — Joana Martinez, Myracle Holloway, and Rose Short — on stage to perform Lizzo's hit for a live audience. Their rendition was so energetic, the team had Stefani's fellow judges bopping in their seats — except Kelly Clarkson, who stood and danced along the whole time!

It's a rare treat for The Voice's judges to perform with their team throughout the course of a season. Stefani and Clarkson both had the chance to belt it out with their crew on Nov. 19, and it's likely John Legend and Blake Shelton will follow suit. Maybe we'll get a coaches quartet before the season finale, too! We'd love a "More Than Words" reprise. Watch Stefani's awesome Lizzo cover above.
