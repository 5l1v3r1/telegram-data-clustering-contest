Daddy Yankee to open reggaeton museum in Puerto Rico

The Daddy Yankee El Jefe Museum opens on Friday in Plaza de Las Americas in San Juan and will be free of charge.

21 Nov 2019, 20:54

Puerto Rico reggaeton star Daddy Yankee is opening a museum in the US Caribbean territory that will be dedicated to his life and the music that made him famous.

The Daddy Yankee El Jefe Museum opens on Friday in Plaza de Las Americas in San Juan and will be free of charge.
