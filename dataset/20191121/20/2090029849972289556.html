He wants to marry me not for love but loyalty – Lady cries out

21 Nov 2019, 20:10 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

A lady has written to relationship expert and adviser Joro about a discovery she recently made regarding her fiance.

According to the lady, she met her fiance in the UK and they dated for 3 years and will be tying the knot with him next year but unfortunately discovered that the young man does not love her but wants to marry her regardless because she is loyal to him. This discovery she made when she visited his mom.

She on the one hand loves the guy and would really want to spend the rest of her life with him. But she seeks the advise of the relationship expert on what next to do owing to the fact that the feeling is not mutual between both of them.
