Lanark shop launches £30 Buckfast hamper for tonic lovers just in time for Christmas

21 Nov 2019, 20:23 by Katy Pagan

A LANARKSHIRE shop has launched a Buckfast hamper for tonic lovers this Christmas.

Londis, based at Carstairs Junction in Lanark, is selling the specially wrapped bucky treat in store now.

The hamper comes complete with two full-sized 75cl bottles, one 35cl bottle and two miniatures.

It also features the newly launched BBQ Buckfast sauce - which almost sold out before even hitting shelves.

And it will only set you back £30.99.

But a £10 deposit is required when you order.

Shop bosses said: "Bucky hampers are ready.

"Pop in and pick what you want and arrange a date for collection"

In other Buckfast news, fans are convinced the tonic firm are launching a 2L bottle after teasing a "big announcement" was coming soon.
