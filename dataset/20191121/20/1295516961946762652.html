Titans: Warner Bros. Reveals First Official Look at Nightwing Suit

21 Nov 2019, 20:11 by John Ridlehoover

Warner Bros. Studios hosted a Super Suit Workshop Open House event where guests were treated to a look at Dick Grayson's Nightwing costume for Titans.

Photos of the costume depict a comics-accurate suit, featuring Nightwing's signature black padding and bright blue symbol across the chest. The costume is designed by specialty costume designer Laura Jean Shannon with the support of her team and built by Creative Character Engineering. Official photos released by Warner Bros. feature the Nightwing costume alongside suits for Jason Todd's Robin, Wonder Girl, Aqualad, Hawk, Dove, Ravager and Deathstroke.

Dick Grayson will make his debut as Nightwing in the Season 2 finale of Titans. The finale will be Episode 13 of Season 2 and is set to release on Nov. 29, with the finale fittingly titled, "Nightwing."

Grayson took up the identity of Nightwing in the Tales of the Teen Titans comic series by Marv Wolfman and George Perez. The character has appeared in many animated DC series, including Young Justice and Teen Titans.
