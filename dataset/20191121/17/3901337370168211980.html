Inside Sundowns: 10 Images Of Smiles & Laughs

21 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Soccer Laduma

Mamelodi Sundowns are underway with preparations for an important cup clash this weekend, with a spot in the final of the competition up for grabs. 

Click through the gallery to view the Brazilians with smiles and laughs in their training session this week

The Brazilians will lock horns with Golden Arrows in the semi-finals of the Telkom Knockout competition on Saturday afternoon, this off the back of a 3-2 league loss to the same side in their most recent Absa Premiership encounter. 

Read more: How Middendorp Should Approach Semi-Final

In what is deemed as a game of vengeance, Sundowns are aware of the importance in adding more domestic silverware to their cabinet. 

While the club has won back-to-back league titles, it has been three seasons since they last won a South African cup competition.

A spot in the final means that they will either come up against their nemesis, Kaizer Chiefs, or Maritzburg United, with the two sides set to lock horns the day after. 

Arrows will play hosts to Sundowns at the Sugar Ray Xulu Stadium on Saturday, November 23 and it remains to be seen whether Head Coach Pitso Mosimane’s side will send Steve Komphela and his troops out of the competition in their own backyard.
