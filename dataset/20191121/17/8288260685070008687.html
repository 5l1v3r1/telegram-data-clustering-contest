The Birmingham International Tattoo is returning this weekend - here's everything you need to know

The spectacular display will feature more than 1,200 performers

21 Nov 2019, 17:25 by Becky Weaver, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/becky-weaver/

Marching bands, exciting displays and an incredible closing ceremony - those are just a few of the proceedings that will be held at the Birmingham International Tattoo when it returns to Arena Birmingham on Saturday 23 - Sunday 24 November, 2019.

Set to welcome more than 1,200 performers, The International Tattoo will see international marching bands join forces with our very own British military bands in a three-hour jam-packed performance that is sure to entertain all the family.

Featuring displays from RAF Cosford Queen's Colour, the RNRMC Field Gun Competition and the Parade of Massed Standards, the event will come to a close with a spectacular Grand Finale.

Tickets for The Birmingham International Air Tattoo are on sale now from The Ticket Factory.

They're priced between £15 - £30, plus a £2.55 fulfilment fee.
