Victim's credit card stolen during home burglary and used in numerous shops

It happened in New Basford

21 Nov 2019, 17:06 by Joseph Locker

Police are looking for two men in connection with a burglary where the victim's credit card was stolen and used to buy items from a number of shops.

The burglary happened in Herbert Road, New Basford, and among the items stolen was a credit card.

It was used to purchase items from a number of local stores in the Basford and Sherwood area, police say.

The burglary happened between September 13 and 16 earlier this year.

A Nottinghamshire Police spokesman said: "If you recognise the men or think you can help please call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 357 of 16 September 2019, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
