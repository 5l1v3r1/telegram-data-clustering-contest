Taapsee Pannu says if she would have been in Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor place, she wouldn't have gotten the second film

21 Nov 2019, 17:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Apart from her classic performances in the films, Taapsee Pannu is also known for her unspoken nature. She's badass, unabashedly honest and never shies away from speaking her mind! And her recent conversation on a chat show was proof of the same.

The host of the show asked about the newcomer of Bollywood who wouldn't have survived in the industry. Taapsee was quick to name Anil Kapoor's son Harsh Varrdhan Kapoor. He marked his debut in Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra's 'Mirzya' which failed to pull the audience to the theatres. He also featured in Vikramaditya Motwane's vigilante drama 'Bhavesh Joshi Superhero'. The actor is currently working on Abhinav Bindra's biopic.

Taapsee went on to say that after whatever she has seen of him till now, it would have been a task due to his not so appreciated debut. She added that if she would have been at his place, she probably wouldn't have got any film after that.

Meanwhile, on the work front, Taapsee was last seen in 'Saand Ki Aankh' co-starring Bhumi Pednekar. The film was released in October this year.
