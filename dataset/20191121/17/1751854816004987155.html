Remember the cool Solar Red color on the HTC U11? Meizu has tried to bring a similar color back in trend with the Meizu 16T Daylight Orange. Nearly a month after its release, the Meizu 16T Daylight Orange color model will go on sale tomorrow in China. This color is unlike the Tropical Orange version of the Meizu HD60 headphones and is a lot more glassy and reflective, looking closer to the Solar Red variant of the HTC U11.

The Daylight Orange variant of the Meizu 16T will be available in all three storage variants. The 6GB+128GB variant will set you back by 1999 yuan ($284), while the 8GB+128GB and 8GB+256GB models will cost 2299 yuan ($327) and 2499 yuan ($356) respectively. It will be an open sale and kicks off at 10:00 AM sharp on November 22 i.e. tomorrow.

The Meizu 16T is a gaming-centric device that features a 6.5-inch 1080p FHD+ display and Snapdragon 855. The phone comes with a linear vibration motor and Game Mode 4.0 for an immersive gaming experience. The handset also features dual stereo speakers and a 3.5mm audio jack. Meizu has also teamed up with Tencent for better game optimizations. The Meizu 16T has a vertical triple camera system with Sony IMX362 12-megapixel with f/1.9 aperture and PDAF as the primary lens, an f/2.2 aperture 8-megapixel ultrawide lens with 118-degree field-of-view and another f/1.9 aperture 5-megapixel depth sensor.
