Man accused of kidnapping woman on Desiard Street, raping her

21 Nov 2019, 17:25 by KNOE

MONROE, La. (KNOE) - A Monroe man is accused of kidnapping and raping a woman on Desiard Street.

According to arrest records, the kidnapping happened on Nov. 19 near the Walmart Neighborhood.

The victim told Monroe police that she was walking along the street when the suspect, identified as Chad Morehead, pulled up and asked her to get into his vehicle several times. She said she refused because she did not know the suspect.

The victim says that Morehead then jumped out of his vehicle, physically forced her inside, and drove off.

She says she demanded to be let go and he refused. She says Morehead then took her to a residence and raped her.

She says she was able to get away after the rape, leaving her shoes behind. She then contacted the police.

When police questioned Morehead, he said he had used drugs with the victim in the past and had prior sexual encounters with her, but that he had not seen her on that day.

Police say they found the victim's shoes at his residence.

According to the report, the victim told police repeatedly that she did not know Morehead.

Morehead was arrested and charged with second-degree rape and second-degree kidnapping. Bond is listed at $400,000.
