Multiple police cars attend rush hour incident in Kilmarnock town centre

One lane was blocked while officers carried out their duties.

21 Nov 2019, 17:58 by Ross Dunn, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/ross-dunn/

Police have attended the scene of an incident in Kilmarnock town centre tonight.

Two police cars were spotted blocking one lane on West George Street shortly after 5pm this evening.

The nature of the incident is unclear but officers were spotted speaking to four individuals outside Sinforiani Bros at the time.

Traffic was sluggish as the officers carried out their duties on the one-way road.
