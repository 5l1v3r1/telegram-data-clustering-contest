Urban Meyer Sends Video Message To Ohio State Fans

21 Nov 2019, 17:32 by Tzvi Machlin

The Ohio State Buckeyes take on the Penn State Nittany Lions this weekend in what is essentially a Big Ten Championship Game play-in. Ahead of the game, former Buckeyes head coach Urban Meyer had a message for the fans.

In a message directed at Block O – the student section – he called on the students to show up nice and early to the stadium for FOX Sports’ Big Noon Kickoff Show.

“See you Saturday 10 AM sharp at the RPAC. (Big Noon Kickoff) needs your support,” he tweeted.
