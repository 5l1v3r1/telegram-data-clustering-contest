Guns N’ Roses’ Welcome To The Jungle, In The Style Of Rammstein

Ever wondered what would happen if Rammstein covered Welcome To The Jungle? Well wonder no longer!

21 Nov 2019, 17:06

Welcome To The Jungle is a classic. One of the greatest opening tracks of all time, Guns N’ Roses’ 1987 smash cannot be improved and will go down in history as a bona fide rager.

But that doesn’t stop people on the internet messing with its formula.

Ten Second Song Guy (aka Anthony Vincent) makes his living by reinventing rock’n’roll staples in the style of over artists, and this month he’s decided to cover Welcome To The Jungle in the guise of German industrialists Rammstein.

READ THIS: A deep dive into Rammstein’s video for Du Hast

And to be fair to him, he goes the whole hog. Rather than doing a dodgy Till Lindemann impression and barking out Axl’s lyrics, he translates every line into German before adopting a dodgy Till impersonation – complete with trouser suspenders and a dog collar. He even has a flashing light in his mouth for good measure.

Sure, the translations and pronunciations aren’t entirely accurate, but it kinda works – Anthony even replaces Slash’s signature guitar with a menacing synth.
