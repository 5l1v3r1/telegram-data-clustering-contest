St. Bonaventure to wear Final Four throwbacks vs. Canisius

21 Nov 2019, 17:18

St. Bonaventure's men's basketball team will wear throwback uniforms honoring the Final Four team from 1970 for Saturday's game against Canisius in KeyBank Center.

The uniforms were worn during the late 1960s and early 1970s.

St. Bonaventure said it partnered with Adidas to re-create the look of the Final Four team with brown, white and gold.

Saturday's game is the 168th meeting between Canisius and Bona and the 73rd time the rivalry has taken place in KeyBank Center or the Aud.

The Bonnies also will wear the throwbacks Dec. 7 against Hofstra for the reunion of the Final Four team at the Reilly Center.
