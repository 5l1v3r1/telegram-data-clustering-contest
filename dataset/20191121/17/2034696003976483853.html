Canadian fun-punks PUP: “Every band has a responsibility to call out bad behaviour at shows”

21 Nov 2019, 17:09

Over the past decade the Canadian punks have been outspoken about creating a safe environment at their gigs

A few weeks ago, PUP frontman Stefan Babcock shared a clear message for punters who come to cause trouble at his band’s shows: “If you’re gonna act like a piece of shit in the pit, don’t come to PUP shows.”
