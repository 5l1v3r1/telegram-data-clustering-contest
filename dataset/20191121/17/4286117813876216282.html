Day-night Test: India pacers in focus as Kolkata gears up for festival of pink

India vs Bangladesh: India start as overwhelming favourites against Bangladesh in their 1st ever Pink ball Test, thanks to their well-oiled pace battery.

21 Nov 2019, 17:41 by Akshay Ramesh

HIGHLIGHTS

  • India take on Bangladesh in their 1st ever Pink ball Test in Kolkata
  • Kolkata has turned pink in the lead up to the historic Test
  • India's pace attack likely to dominate headlines in Day-night Test

There are hardly many cities across the globe that celebrate festivals better than Kolkata. As expected, the City of Joy is gearing up for India's 1st ever Pink ball Test with a festive mood.

In the part of the country where sweets are a touchy subject, pink-coloured sweets are being sold. Famous landmarks in the city have been lit up in pink. The scenic Eden Gardens is only looking better after wearing pink.

A giant-sized pink ball hangs over the Eden Gardens letting people know that the iconic venue is going to become a part of Indian cricket's history, once again! The stands at the Eden Gardens have been equipped with pink lights.

There has not been this much hype in the lead up to a Test match in India in a long, long time. BCCI president and local hero Sourav Ganguly apparently was brimming with pride when he announced that tickets for the 1st 4 days of the Test were sold out. Expect nothing less than a carnival-like atmosphere over the next 5 days in Kolkata.
