Wearing Panties And Bra Makes Me Uncomfortable So I Don’t Wear Them – Singer Nikki (+Video)

21 Nov 2019, 17:25 by vanessa nordzi

Fast rising Afropop dancehall artist and Ebony’s lookalike, Nikki Banks has explained why she hates wearing bra and panties.

Speaking to Abeiku Santana on his “Efie Kwanso” show, Nikki explained that she is always uneasy anytime she puts on a bra.

She added that; ” my boobs are small so I don’t see any reason why I should put on a bra. Anytime I put it on, I feel so uncomfortable that I am forced to take it off that instant”.

Abeiku Santana who was obviously taken aback by her response asked her if she wears panties. Nikki replied saying that she doesn’t wear panties often to allow fresh air pass her private part.

“I don’t always wear panties because sometimes you have to allow fresh air pass there to make it feel happy”. She reiterated.
