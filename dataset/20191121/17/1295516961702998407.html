Mulan is a Warrior in the Live-Action Disney Film's Latest International Poster

21 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Colin Hickson

The international poster for Disney's upcoming live-action remake of Mulan has been released, showcasing the two sides of the titular character as she heads off into battle.

Tweeted by Regal Cinemas Vice President of Media Chris Sylvia, the poster sees Hua Mulan (Liu Yifei) holding a sword while dressed in a red robe and standing in front of mountains while light hits her face and wind blows through her hair. In the background, an image of Mulan leading the Chinese army on horseback appears while a bird flies over the mountains.
