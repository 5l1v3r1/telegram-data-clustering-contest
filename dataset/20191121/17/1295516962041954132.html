Star Wars: Who Is Willrow Hood - and Why Did He Get An Emoji?

21 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Bea Caicoya

As part of the ongoing promotional campaign for The Rise of Skywalker, the Star Wars Twitter account has released custom emojis for 42 of the franchise's legendary characters.

Although fans will recognize most of them and have no trouble finding the corresponding hasthtag, newer or more casual fans might be surprised to encounter a strange hashtag, "#WillrowHood," which includes detailed picture of a mustached man in an orange jumpsuit holding a white object that looks suspiciously like an ice cream maker. Now, we'll be taking a closer look at the mysterious Willrow Hood, and why did this minor Star Wars character became notable enough to be immortalized as an emoji.
