Policeman, five-year-old girl allegedly killed in Ekiti bank robbery

21 Nov 2019, 17:33 by Abiodun Nejo

Abiodun Nejo, Ado Ekiti

Armed robbers on Thursday afternoon attacked a UBA branch in Oye-Ekiti, leaving two persons dead.

Sources said the victims were a young girl of about five years old and a man suspected to be the policeman attached to the bank.

A source said the armed robbers, who shot endlessly into the air on arrival and during the robbery, gained entrance into the banking hall after blowing up the door with dynamite.

According to the eyewitnesses, the robbers escaped with huge amount of money.

The sources added that a van, suspected to belong to the Police, was also burnt in the process.
Police Public Relations Officer for Ekiti State Command, Caleb Ikechukwu, confirmed the incident, noting that the Police were already on the trail of the armed robbers, adding that “preliminary investigation is ongoing concerning the number of casualties.”
