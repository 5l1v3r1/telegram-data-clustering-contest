Mompha: Alleged fraudster sues EFCC for N5m over unlawful detention

21 Nov 2019, 17:49

- Alleged fraudster, Mompha has slammed the EFCC with a N5m lawsuit for unlawful detention

- The popular big boy was arrested by the anti-graft agency on allegations of cybercrime and money laundering

- Mompha has accused the EFCC of detaining him beyond the constitutionally-allowed period without being charged to court

Disgraced social media big boy, Mompha, has slammed the Economic and Financial crimes commission (EFCC), with a N5m lawsuit. He filed the fundamental rights enforcement suit alleging unlawful detention and a violation of his rights.

Recall Legit.ng had earlier published the news of how the social media celebrity and big money spender, caused quite a stir on social media after he was arrested by the Economic Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC), on allegations bordering on internet fraud.

In his suit filed before the Lagos state High Court Igbosere by his counsel, Gboyega Oyewole SAN, Mompha accused the EFCC of detaining him beyond the constitutionally-allowed period without being charged to court.
