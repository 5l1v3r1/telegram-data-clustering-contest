Kim Junsu greets viewers for the first time in 10 years on Korean terrestrial broadcasting in preview for 'House of Sharing’'

21 Nov 2019, 21:00 by soum

Singer/musical actor Kim Junsu is slated to return to Korean terrestrial broadcasting for the first time in ten years. The former TVXQ member is slated to appear on the pilot episode of MBC's reality show 'House of Sharing'.

Junsu mentions in the preview of 'House of Sharing', how it feels to be on TV after so many years and how it's like to live alone in a big house. Also that he doesn’t really care too much how he looks to other people and that he is a lot more easy-going than people think and more.

'House of Sharing' is scheduled to broadcast at the beginning of December.
