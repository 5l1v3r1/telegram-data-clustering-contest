Daniel Bryan set to confront Bray Wyatt on Friday Night SmackDown before WWE Survivor Series

21 Nov 2019, 21:55 by Arunava Ghoshal

Just a few days before Survivor Series, Daniel Bryan and Bray Wyatt will probably have an in-ring confrontation on the upcoming episode of SmackDown. WWE.com has revealed that Bryan wants to summon the WWE Universal Champion before their matchup and it will be interesting to find out who shows up on the show, Bray Wyatt or The Fiend.

Will Bryan send a message before the Survivor Series showdown? Can Wyatt’s mind games get his adversary back into a “Yes!” state of mind? Catch the confrontation on SmackDown at 8/7 C on FOX.
