U.S. stock funds lose $3.6 billion in week - Lipper

21 Nov 2019, 21:24 by Reuters

(Nov 22): U.S. equity funds saw $3.6 billion in outflows in the week ended on Wednesday, according to data released on Thursday by Lipper.

U.S. taxable bond funds attracted $12.4 billion, the 15th straight inflow, while U.S. money market funds shed $25.3 billion during the period, the largest outflow since mid April, Lipper data showed. - Reuters
