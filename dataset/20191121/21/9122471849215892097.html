Updated Betting Line Released For Ohio State-Michigan

21 Nov 2019, 21:19 by Alek Arend

The No. 2 Ohio State Buckeyes are solely focusing on eighth-ranked Penn State this upcoming Saturday. But that hasn’t stopped Vegas from looking ahead to the Buckeyes’ big-time rivalry game against No. 13 Michigan on Nov. 30.

Ohio State is among the top four of the latest edition of the College Football Playoff rankings. The Wolverines would love nothing more than to spoil the Buckeyes’ playoff hopes.

Michigan has steadily improved over the course of the season en route to an 8-2 record through 10 games. The Wolverines may be improving, but Vegas’ oddsmakers think Ohio State will win by at least two scores.

The Buckeyes are currently 13-point favorites according to the latest spread. That’s a rather comfortable margin for most teams. But 13 points is a bit closer than most are expecting. 

Michigan’s home field crowd will be huge factor. And as happens in most rivalry games, tensions will be higher than normal. Will Jim Harbaugh’s squad give Ryan Day and the Buckeyes a scare in the regular season finale?

One thing’s for sure – the Wolverines must contain OSU QB Justin Fields. The dual-threat quarterback is as dangerous as they come. If Michigan is going to have any shot at an upset, they must slow him down.

Michigan and Ohio State play a week from Saturday on Nov. 30 in Ann Arbor.
