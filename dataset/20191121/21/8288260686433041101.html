‘Worried’ - This is what Aston Villa fans are saying about the January transfer window

Aston Villa transfer news | Reports claim that Dean Smith's side will not be spending big in January and that they are not anticipating a busy window in the New Year

21 Nov 2019, 21:58 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans have been reacting to a report that claims the club will not be spending big money in January.

The transfer window re-opens in the New Year and offers Dean Smith's side the perfect opportunity to strengthen in their quest for Premier League survival.

Villa currently sit in 17th place in the League table, three points ahead of Watford in the final relegation spot.

With the re-opening of the market creeping up, speculation has started to ramp up with Argentinian international Nico Gaitan and Austrian hot-shot Christoph Monschein linked with moves to Villa .
