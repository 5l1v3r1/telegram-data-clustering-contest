REPORT: NFL Spokesperson Says League ‘Found No Evidence’ of Racial Slur By Mason Rudolph

21 Nov 2019, 21:23 by Darrelle Lincoln

On Thursday, Browns defensive end Myles Garrett was said to have told NFL officials during his appeal that Steelers quarterback Mason Rudolph used a racial slur towards him that caused him to take his helmet off and hit him with it.

Hours later, he got a huge shot to that story when ESPN’s Adam Schefter stated an NFL spokesperson relayed to him that the league had found nothing to corroborate that allegation.
