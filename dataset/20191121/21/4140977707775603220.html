BRD Vs DEL Dream11 Team : Top spot decider in Group A of the Super League promises to engulf us in a riveting affair.

Although it’s still just the early exchanges of the Super League of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2019-20, today’s contest between Baroda and Delhi comes bearing with it gargantuan ramifications for both the sides. After registering wins in their round openers yesterday, both the sides know another win for them today will massively bolster their chances of making it to proceeding round of the marquee tournamnet.

Currently on top of Group A of the Super League stage after trouncing Maharashtra yesterday, Delhi come into today’s encounter with immense momentum in their kitty. It was not the just the win but the manner it came about and the identity of the side it came against which saw Delhi earn immense laurels as they ended up winning by 77 runs,

Batting first to score 167, Delhi returned with an even better bowling display as they curtailed their opponents to a mere 90 runs. Elsewhere, Baroda also tasted consummate success in yesterday’s clash to come into the docket of today’s clash having thumped Rajasthan by 15 runs on the same wicket as the one which will be laid out today.

The 202run target they had dished out infront of Rajasthan was one which was never going to be peeled off by Rajasthan and despite cameos from their lower middle-order, Baroda kept snagging wickets at regular intervals to ease through to a win. With both the sides having doled out such resplendent displays yesterday, this is a clash which is an intoxicating brew, a match which will grip us from the first to the last ball.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

With almost 400 runs being scored in the final contest of the day played over here yesterday, the number is one which clearly alludes to the kind of conditions we are going to have in place for this encounter as well. An extremely exuberant track for batting, the batsmen are going to be fruitful over here with the side winning the toss bound to step out and choose to bat first.
