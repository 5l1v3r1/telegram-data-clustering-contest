Jamie Lynn Spears will make a return to Nickelodeon and reunite with Zoey 101 cast

21 Nov 2019, 21:37 by Ciara Walsh

Jamie Lynn Spears is returning to Nickelodeon. 

Before you think a Zoey 101 reunion is in the works, it's not like that. However, her former co-stars will be involved.

As well as the beloved Zoey, Jamie also used to play the character of Thelma Stump on the original All That. The bacon-loving bodyguard is coming back for a sketch in the revival of the show where she will be joined by her Pacific Coast Academy gang.

It's not uncommon for the Zoey 101 cast to have reunions, and over the years we've seen many on social media - Jamie Lynn has always been missing, though.

Recently, Spears linked up with Paul Butcher, Sean Flynn, Kristin Herrera, Chris Massey, Erin Sanders and Matthew Underwood, and it gave us total nostalgia.

The creator of Zoey 101, Dan Schneider, gave us goosebumps when he shared a picture of them all back together again.

He wrote: "Some Zoey 101 buzz got me talking with my good friend Sean Flynn (Chase) and within about 72 hours, this happened! I'm not sure how I feel about my new glasses, but that was one of the most fun nights ever. A wonderful cast of kids then, and even better all grown up! Much love/respect for all of you! Jamie Lynn is busy shooting her new show in Atlanta. We missed you, congrats.

According to Entertainment Weekly, the sketch will be airing in early 2020 and we can't wait to see the gang all back together again.
