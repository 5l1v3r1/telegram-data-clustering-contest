Exclusive Trailer: An App Connects the Living and the Dead in Mena Suvari-Starring ‘Apparition’

21 Nov 2019, 21:28 by John Squires

Vertical Entertainment has acquired the supernatural horror film Apparition for release, unleashing it in theaters here in North America and on VOD later this year, on December 27.

Mena Suvari and Kevin Pollack star in the Waymon Boone-directed film, and we’ve got the official trailer and poster art for you today. Find both down below, exclusively here on BD.

The film is said to be “based on true events about former correctional facility the Preston School of Industry.”

“Apparition follows a group of millennials at an abandoned castle, experimenting with a spiritually guided app that connects the living with the dead. This ominous, historic site of murder and torture is linked to each of them in ways they’ll soon discover.”
