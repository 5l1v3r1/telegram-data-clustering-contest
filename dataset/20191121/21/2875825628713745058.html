Basement Jaxx and The Divine Comedy announced as headliners for Kaleidoscope 2020

The three day event will take place next June

21 Nov 2019, 21:21 by William Dunne

Basemen Jaxx and The Divine Comedy have been announced as the headline acts of a festival in Co Wicklow next summer.

The Where's Your Head At hitmakers and the Northern Irish band will take to the main stage for Kaleidoscope 2020.

The three day event will take place between June 26-28 at Russborough, Blessington.

The festival has also announced the return of all of the much-loved entertainment areas that debuted in June - Rumpledumpus, The Front Lawn Stage, The Hippodrome Theatre, Cinema Under The Stars, Wonderland Junior, Wonderland Teen, the Scrumdiddlyumptious Festival of Food and The Big Hooley.

The Sports and Wellness programme, Kaleidoscope Carnival, and the World’s Largest Bouncing Castle are set to return next summer.

And if you fancy kitting the family out in Kaleidoscope gear, check out the website for t-shirts and hoodies, alongside the festival's limited edition Irish handmade Kaleidoscope soft by Irish toymakers Red Rufus costing €14.95 and Kaleidoscope Christmas mug priced at €15.00 all available from the Kaleidoscope online shop  here.
