Sydney Blender User Group: Blender for Beginners, November 27

21 Nov 2019, 05:37

Chris Lee writes:

If you are in Sydney and you are interested in Blender, even if you know nothing about it, this event is for you.

Blender for beginners will be a bi-monthly meet up, dedicated to those who wish to learn the basics of Blender 2.8. This will alternate with meet ups for general to advanced users, in between the beginner meet ups.

Click on the event link below to indicate you are coming and join our Discord server, link on Meetup, for live chat and questions.

Bring your own laptop and work through some basic scene modelling and animation. We may have a couple of laptops spare, just in case. Otherwise two people can share and learn together.

6:00 PM to 9:00 PM
