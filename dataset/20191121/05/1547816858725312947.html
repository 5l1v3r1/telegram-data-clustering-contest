Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 November 20, 2019, Written Updates Of Full Episode: Anurag Dances With Komolika

21 Nov 2019, 05:01 by Kristina Das

At the sangeet party Komolika and Anurag dance in a sensual way. With Komolika’s moves, Anurag doesn’t feel comfortable

Today's Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 begins with Anurag getting upset at Prerna for not taking care of herself. Later, Prerna realizes that Sonalika is similar to Komolika and is two-faced. Meanwhile, for not taking good care of Prerna, Anurag gets upset with Sonalika. Next morning, he calls Prerna and apologizes for being rude with her. 

Further, Anurag tells Prerna that he feels responsible to take care of her child while being with her. While Prerna wonders how she fell and tells Shivani about the incidents with Anurag. And suddenly, she realises that because of Sonalika she was about to fall. She also tells Shivani that she feels Sonalika and Komolika are almost similar.

Shivani is invited at a party while Nivedita asks Komolika if she is coming with them at the same party. Later, to sangeet function, Nivedita arrives with her family. Prerna along with Shivani arrive there and both Prerna, Anurag come face to face. Anurag sends a chair for Prerna and even keeps staring at her.

Later, at the sangeet party Komolika and Anurag dance in a sensual way. With Komolika’s moves, Anurag doesn’t feel comfortable. Prerna feels strongly that there is a lot of similarity between Sonalika and Komolika.
