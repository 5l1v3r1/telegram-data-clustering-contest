66 Things to Be Grateful For, Big and Small

21 Nov 2019, 05:05 by Sierra Horton

When was the last time you practiced gratitude for the simple things? With the hustle, bustle, and stress of everyday life, it's easy to become fixated on big-picture worries and take for granted all the little facets of life that deserve appreciation and can be a major boost to your overall happiness. If you're in need of a mindset transformation, read through these 66 things to be grateful for all year long.
