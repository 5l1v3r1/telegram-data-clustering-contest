Cape Coast Pitch Invader Awal Suleman Finally Meets Thomas Partey

21 Nov 2019, 05:06 by BB Jones

The 25 year old nursing student who made history by becoming the first Ghanaian pitch invader, Awal Suleman has finally met his idol Thomas Partey.

Awal who later said he only wanted a selfie with midfielder Thomas Partey, leapt onto the pitch and started running helter skelter during Thursday’s 2021 Africa Cup of Nation’s Qualifier match between the Black Stars and Bafana Bafana of South Africa.

The match took place at the Cape Coast Sports Stadium in Central Region of Ghana.

He was later arrested and later charged for disturbing public peace at the University of Cape Coast police station.

Now his dream has come true as Partey has met him in person. He also gifted him a signed Black Stars jersey.
