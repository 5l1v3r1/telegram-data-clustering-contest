FEATURE JOBS: Love to Dream, Pedestrian Group, Theory + More

21 Nov 2019, 05:53 by By Yasmin Adhami

Do you want to work for a brand that is loved by parents (and babies) all around the world? Love to Dream have a new and exciting opportunity for a Social Media Specialist (SYD) to join their team on a full time basis! The successful candidate will ensure that the brand’s social advertising meets best practice through creating, developing and optimising paid social content. The focus of the role is to build awareness, drive conversions and retain loyal customers and fans. To be considered for this role, candidates must have 4-5 years experience in paid social media advertising, excellent verbal communication and writing skills and the ability to connect with the target audience. If you a social media expert and want to help bring sleep back to exhausted new parents in Australia and beyond, then apply now!

Pedestrian Group, Australia’s fastest growing publishing brand, are seeking a full time Campaign Executive (SYD) to ensure the delivery of written native and video content campaigns and assist the Campaign Managers with display campaign management. The successful applicant will join the Client Solutions department in an Operations role, a role that is pivotal to the success of Pedestrian Group’s display and native campaigns. To excel in this role, candidates must have 1-2 years experience at a digital publisher or media agency, an incredible attention to detail and experience using Google Ad Manager (DFP), Facebook Ad Manager or Google Analytics. Apply today to join a fun and dynamic workplace!

Theory, a pioneer in the contemporary fashion market, are on the hunt for a Casual Sales Associate (MELB) to work in their Flagship concession stores in David Jones. The ideal candidate has a genuine interest in fashion, can deliver a premium customer experience and assist in maximising store sales. Requirements for this position include previous experience as a retail sales professional, exceptional personal presentation & communication and a proven track record in meeting sales targets. Theory offer competitive pay, the chance to be a part of an international brand with growth in Australia and direct support from the team at Head Office. Apply here if you appreciate fashion and want to be part of growing an international brand.
