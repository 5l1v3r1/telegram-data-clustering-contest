Mauricio Pochettino's emotional message to Tottenham players following sacking

Maurico Pochettino's assistant manager, Jesus Perez, has posted an emotional picture of the former boss leaving a heartfelt message for his players

21 Nov 2019, 22:06 by Irishmirror.ie

Maurico Pochettino left an emotional message to his Tottenham players on a tactics board in the changing room after his shock sacking.

Pochettino's stint at the club came to an abrupt end on Tuesday night when he was removed from his post and replaced by Jose Mourinho the very next morning.

Tributes for the Argentinian, who guided the club to the Champions League final last season, poured in from players.

And his assistant, Jesus Perez, has now posted pictures of him writing a heartfelt message on the tactics board for his players after not getting the chance to say his farewells in person.

"Big thanks to you all!" Pochettino wrote.

"We can't say goodbye...you will always be in our hearts."

He then signed the board, with Perez posting it on social media and simply stating "COYS" with a love heart emoji.

Several Spurs players paid tribute to Pochettino after his departure from the club was confirmed.

Skipper Harry Kane tweeted: "Gaffer. I’ll be forever thankful to you for helping me achieve my dreams.

"We’ve had some amazing moments in the last 5 and a half years that I will never forget. You were my manager but my friend as well and I thank you for that relationship.
