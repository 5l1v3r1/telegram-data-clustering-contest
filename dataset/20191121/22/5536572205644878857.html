REPORT: Patriots Have ‘Kicked The Tires’ On Bringing Back Antonio Brown

21 Nov 2019, 22:46 by Darrelle Lincoln

Just a little over 2 months ago, the New England Patriots parted ways with Antonio Brown due to his behavior of sending text messages to a sexual assault accuser, but things have seemingly changed since that time period.

WEEI in Boston is reporting the Patriots have ‘kicked the tires’ on the idea of bringing the controversial wide receiver back on the team.
