Report: French Montana In Hospital With Cardiac Issues After Cops Visit His Home

21 Nov 2019, 22:32 by Shakiel Mahjouri

French Montana is reportedly in the hospital after suffering a health scare.

The “Unforgettable” rapper was admitted to a San Fernando Valley hospital in Los Angeles on Thursday afternoon, according to TMZ. L.A. County Sheriff’s deputies were allegedly called to Montana’s Calabasas home due to a possible robbery.

A source told ET that robbery is said to have been a false alarm. But when the cops arrived they were concerned with Montana’s odd behaviour. Sources reportedly told TMZ that Montana has been “suffering severe stomach pains, nausea and perhaps most alarmingly, an elevated heart rate.”

Another rep for the LA County Sheriff’s Office adds that there was a medical call made on site and that a person was transported to a hospital.

Montana, 35, is said to now be awake and alert. He is receiving IV fluids and rumours suggest contaminated food may be responsible for his worrying health.

ET Canada has reached out to Montana’s rep for a comment.
