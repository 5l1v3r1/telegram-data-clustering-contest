I'm A Celebrity's Caitlyn Jenner apologises as she throws shade soap stars

21 Nov 2019, 22:48

I’m A Celebrity’s Caitlyn Jenner previously revealed that she didn’t know who her fellow campmates were before she entered the jungle – but now she’s done it again.

As actors Andy Whyment and Cliff Parisi made their debut on the show with their first bushtucker trial, the 70-year-old admitted she didn’t who they were – while throwing shade at soap stars at the same time.

She asked as she pointed at the poster of the duo: ‘Does anyone know who they are?’

Roman responded: ‘You know the one on the left it’s a guy from Coronation Street which is another big soap in the UK.’

‘What’s the deal with all these f***ing soap people?’

Jacqueline Jossa then piped up with ‘Oi’.

Caitlyn was quick to apologise as she added: ‘Oh sorry.’
