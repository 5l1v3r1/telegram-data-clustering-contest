BREAKING Chris Brown 'welcomes second child' with ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris

The singer is reported to have welcomed a baby boy with his ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris, as she seemingly announced the news on Instagram

Chris Brown has reportedly welcomed his second child with his ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris.

Chris, 30, is already dad to daughter Royalty, five.

Chris shared a snap of himself wearing a hoodie with the word "Born" printed around the hood, and she posted on her Instagram story: "I was in love, when I first saw you."
