UFC News: Rafael dos Anjos set to take on Michael Chiesa at Fight Night 166

21 Nov 2019, 22:41 by Anurag Mitra

According to reports, former UFC lightweight champion, Rafael dos Anjos is all set to return to the Octagon against Michael Chiesa in a welterweight bout at the upcoming UFC Fight Night 166 set to take place on 25th January next year at the PNC Arena in Raleigh, North Carolina.

ESPN journalist Bret Okamoto confirmed the news in a tweet, saying,

"Rafael dos Anjos vs. Michael Chiesa agreed to for UFC Fight Night on Jan.25 in Raleigh, North Carolina, per sources."
