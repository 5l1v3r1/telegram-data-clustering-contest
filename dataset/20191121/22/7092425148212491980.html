Andrea Corr confirms The Corrs are talking about a reunion tour

21 Nov 2019, 22:34 by Ciara Walsh

Leave me breathless. 

The Corrs might go on and leave us breathless again if they reunite in the New Year as Andrea revealed the siblings are talking about touring again.

Dundalk natives Andrea, Sharon, Caroline and their brother Jim made up the band that delivered 90s classics like Summer Sunshine and Only When I Sleep.

The band split in 2006 but Andrea announced they were making music again in 2015 and in 2017 The Corrs released their seventh studio album in, Jupiter Calling.

So, could 2020 be the year The Corrs go back on tour?

Speaking to Evoke.ie at the An Post Irish Book Awards Andrea said: "We are talking about it, there are no definitive dates as yet.

"It takes quite a while for us to get organised," she joked.

When asked whether or not her return to music might lead Andrea to make a wider return to media she didn't fancy it.

The Irish beauty also said she wouldn't appear on Dancing with the Stars.

Andrea added: "No, I think they are great to watch but I would not like to do that."
