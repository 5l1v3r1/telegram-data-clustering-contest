Bad Bunny Interpolates A Reggaeton Classic On “Vete”

21 Nov 2019, 22:06 by Eddie Fu

Fresh off a Latin Grammy win for X 100PRE, Bad Bunny dropped a new single titled “Vete.” It is produced by his previous collaborator Subelo NEO, who is also known for working with Lunay, Farruko, and Eladio Carrión.

The intro contains an interpolation of Noriega and Kartier’s classic song, “Si Te Vas,” as Genius contributor Javy Bell breaks down in an annotation:

On the track, Bad Bunny tells off an ex:

En mi corazón ya tú no ere' la que manda
Se acabó, por ti ya no siento nada
De nuestra serie ya no salen temporada'
Así que vete lejo', dile al diablo que te envíe el pin
Hace tiempo que no somo' un team
Pa'l carajo nuestro aniversario y San Valentín
