World's First Penta 108MP cam phones, Xiaomi Mi Note 10 series will launch in Malaysia on 3 December!

21 Nov 2019, 09:31

Xiaomi Malaysia has teased us about the world's first 108MP penta camera smartphone for awhile now. This time, they made the launching teaser on a big screen at Pavilion KL now instead of announcing it through their own official social media platforms. The launching date will be on 3 December 2019 and it's not just the Mi Note 10 but the Mi Note 10 Pro as well! 

So let’s have a quick recap on the tech specs. It comes with a 6.47-inch FHD+ display and equipped with the Qualcomm Snapdragon 730G processor, up to 6GB RAM and a 128GB storage capacity for the non-pro variant and 8GB RAM + 256GB ROM for the pro variant. Also, it has a large 5260 mAh battery capacity that is supported with a 30W fast charging technology.
