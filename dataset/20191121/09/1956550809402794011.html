Five top nights out this weekend in Dubai

A famous rapper, an award-winning singer, a big name DJ, concerts and more

21 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Reema Rahman

Guess what time it is? It’s time to start planning the weekend.

If you’re on the hunt for different things to do in Dubai this weekend, we’ve rounded up some of the best events taking place across the city below.

A mega rapper? Check. Award-winning singer? Check. Big name DJ? Check. Or how about a free gig or a stellar concert? Read on to find out what's big in Dubai's nightlife scene this weekend.

ibis One Central
A free gig with top local talent? Where do we sign up? Get all the details here.
Free. Fri Nov 22, 8pm-midnight. ibis One Central, Sheikh Zayed Road (04 519 5555).

Ronnie Scott’s
London’s legendary jazz club Ronnie Scott’s is taking over the opera house for one night only.
From Dhs175. Fri Nov 22, 9pm. Dubai Opera, Downtown Dubai, www.dubaiopera.com.

Shaan and KK
Popular Indian singers Shaan and KK are set to rock Dubai.
From Dhs50. Fri Nov 22, 7pm. Dubai Duty Free Tennis Stadium, Garhoud, www.dubai.platinumlist.net.

The Bootleg Beatles
All You Need is Love… And maybe tickets to this gig, if you’re big fan of The Beatles.
From Dhs195. Thu Nov 21, 8pm. Dubai Opera, Downtown Dubai, www.dubaiopera.com.

Zero Gravity
UK garage star, DJ EZ plays at the beach club’s sixth birthday.
Free (ladies), Dhs150 (gents). Fri Nov 22, 5pm-3am. Skydive Dubai Drop Zone, Dubai Marina (04 399 0009).
