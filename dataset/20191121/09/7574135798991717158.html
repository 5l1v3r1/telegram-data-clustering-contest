Black Mountain & Ryley Walker played Bowery Ballroom (pics, setlist)

21 Nov 2019, 09:11 by BrooklynVegan Staff

Black Mountain and Ryley Walker are on tour together now, and they recently played NYC shows at Bowery Ballroom (11/15) and Knitting Factory Brooklyn (11/16). We caught the former. This is a pretty amazing double bill, as both Black Mountain and Ryley Walker combine the sounds of modern indie rock and classic psychedelic rock in different ways. Ryley started out as a ’60s-style folk singer, but he’s evolved into someone who basically straddles the line between indie and jam band, especially live. And Black Mountain continue to be a genuinely heavy band in a Sabbath/Zeppelin kind of way, but they really know how to make it their own, and that was on full display at Bowery Ballroom.
