Nabisco cheese nips recalled due to possible plastic pieces

21 Nov 2019, 09:45 by WFTS Digital Staff

Mondelēz Global LLC is recalling a limited amount of Cheese Nips because there might be plastic pieces inside the product.

RECALL ALERTS | The latest recalls that could affect you and your family

According to the FDA, small food-grade yellow plastic pieces from a dough scraper may have gotten into the product during production.

There have been no injuries or illness reported in relation to this recall, the FDA says.

If you bought this product, do not eat it and contact the company at 1-844-366-1171, 24 hours a day to get more information.
