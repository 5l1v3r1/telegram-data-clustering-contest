Here’s how Amitabh Bachchan’s fans came to his rescue

21 Nov 2019, 09:52 by Filmfare

Amitabh Bachchan usually shares fascinating videos and pictures on social media with his fans and followers. The actor is immensely active on social media and therefore all his posts are closely watched by all. Recently, Amitabh shared a video of a few girls dancing in absolute sync and the megastar captioned the post as, “wow .. even their locks of hair move in sync ..”
