Rolex, Breitling and Tag Heuer watches stolen in burglary

An engagement ring was also taken

21 Nov 2019, 09:09 by Beth Pridding

Burglars have stolen several Rolex, Breitling and Tag Heuer watches from an East Staffordshire home.

They gained entry to a property in Balance Street, Uttoxeter, between 1am and 5.30pm on Monday, November 18.

Thousands of pounds' worth of designer watches and an engagement ring were stolen.

They include three Rolex "Datejust" watches, three Tag Heuer watches, two women's Breitling watches, a Rolex "Submariner" watch, an Omega "Seamaster" watch and an art deco emerald-cut platinum engagement ring with a "baguette surround".

Police are now appealing to anyone who might have seen something suspicious on Monday evening.

DC Emma Francis said: "The investigation is ongoing and we are appealing for anyone in the Uttoxeter area who saw anything suspicious on Monday afternoon or anyone with dash-cam footage from Balance Street between 5pm and 5.30pm to come forward and speak to officers.

"If you know where the stolen property is, or have been offered these items for sale, please get in touch."

Anyone with any information is asked to call 101, quoting incident number 638 of November 18.

Alternatively, information can be provided anonymously by calling Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
