WATCH: “When Bad MakeUp Happens To Good People”

21 Nov 2019, 09:04 by Timothy Ndoro

A video has gone viral on social media which shows the pitfalls of applying too much make-up without the aid of feedback.

In the video, the woman’s makeup is drastically different from her skin one and seems to have been gratuitously applied, perhaps without the aid of a mirror.

Unfortunately, for the lady in question, she does not seem to appreciate how bad her makeup is and how horrible she looks.

The video does not appear to have been shot in Zimbabwe though.
