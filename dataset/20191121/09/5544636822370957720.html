EPL: Tottenham New Boss Mourinho Tackles Chelsea, Others

21 Nov 2019, 09:43 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

Tottenham boss Jose Mourinho has said the clubs he coached including Chelsea, and Real Madrid did not have structures to develop young talents.

Concise News understands that Mourinho was on Wednesday named as the new coach of Tottenham after the club sacked Mauricio Pochettino.

Mourinho has been known for not developing young players and going for already-established stars.

The Portuguese, has, however, dismissed the notion saying the clubs he coached in the past, unlike Tottenham, do not have strong youth systems.

“I really like this squad and looking to the young players – there is not one manager in the world that doesn’t like to play young players and to help young players to develop – not one,” Mourinho told Tottenham’s website.
