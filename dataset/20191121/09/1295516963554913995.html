The Last Airbender Celebrates 15 Years with Steelbook Collection

21 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Math Erao

To celebrate 15 years since the show's debut, all three seasons of Nickelodeon's hit series Avatar: The Last Airbender will be coming to Blu-ray in the form of a new steelbook collection.

Next February, the three books of the animated series will debut on Blu-ray in a limited steelbook featuring artwork by Caleb Thomas. According to the press release, Avatar co-creator Bryan Konietzko personally chose Thomas for the task after seeing his work online. Only 20,000 units of the steelbook will be released.

The collection will have a price tag of $97.99, but it will offer plenty of bonus features alongside the episodes. There are 11 featurettes (including an interview with M. Night Shyamalan), commentary from the actors and creators, animatics of several episodes, and Escape From the Spirit World, an animated graphic novel. The Avatar Super Deformed Shorts will also be included.

Avatar launched a franchise after becoming a hit, leading to a maligned live-action film and a follow-up series, Legend of Korra, which ran for four seasons. Recently, it was announced that the show's original creators would return for a live-action series from Netflix, which looks to retell the beats of the first show.

Avatar: The Last Airbender – The Complete Series Blu-ray 15th Anniversary Limited Edition Steelbook Collection arrives on February 18, 2020.
