These African notorious groups are giving legitimate governments and their securities sleepless nights

21 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Novieku-Babatunde Adeola

Terrorism has taken shape in Africa causing a lot of instability and security concerns in some parts of the continent.

Over the years, the continent has seen the establishment of notorious groups with vested interest of making governments unpopular or pushing for a particular cause against the establishment.

The world is not new to the ever-popular Al-Qaeda, which has a great deal of network spreading into Africa.

In this article, we shed light on some of the major terrorist groups operating on the African continent.
