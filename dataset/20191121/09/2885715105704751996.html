Kamya Punjabi to tie the knot on February 10

Kamya Punjabi's wedding will happen in Mumbai, and the Shakti: Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki actor plans to make the occasion a fun-filled one for both the families.

21 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Sana Farzeen

Television actor Kamya Punjabi is set to tie the knot with Shalabh Dang on February 10, 2020. The wedding will happen in Mumbai, and Kamya plans to make the occasion a fun-filled one for both the families. While the pre-wedding festivities will be held in Mumbai, the couple plan to have a grand reception in Shalabh’s hometown Delhi.

Sharing a romantic click of herself and Shalabh, Kamya wrote, “So here i m with my fav picture with my fav man @iamshalabhdang announcing my fav date #10thFeb2020 ❤️ Bless Us for this New Journey New Beginnings.”
