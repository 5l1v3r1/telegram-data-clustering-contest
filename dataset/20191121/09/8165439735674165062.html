Camila Cabello Announced For Dublin's 3Arena Next Summer

21 Nov 2019, 09:27 by Tara Walsh

Camila Cabello's coming to Dublin

Pop star Camila Cabello has announced she's taking her 'The Romance Tour' to the 3Arena on June 8th next year.

It'll follow the release of her brand new album Romance which is out December 6th.

Tickets go on sale Friday November 29th at 9am.

The singer announced today that she's taking 'The Romance Tour' around Europe starting from May 2020.

Fans can pre-order her upcoming album out December 6thvia SYCO/Epic Records now.

Camila has continued to tease the release of her new album by releasing the songs Shameless, Liar, Cry For Me, and Easy.

Her latest release came out last Thursday and it's called Living Proof.
