Grammy 2020: Stefflon Don helped Burna boy get international recognition -Lady

21 Nov 2019, 09:02

- A social media user recently got dragged after reacting to Burna Boy’s Grammy nomination

- The lady had tweeted a post saying Stefflon Don helped the singer gain international recognition

- Reacting, many music lovers clamped down on the lady and dismissed her submission

Social media was recently thrown in a fit of excitement after news broke that Nigerian singer, Burna Boy, had been nominated in the 2020 Grammy Awards, in the World Best Album category.

Several fans and supporters of the music star had celebrated the self-acclaimed African Giant and showered accolades on him.

However, a Twitter user identified as @rebecca_spero, got more than she bargained for after congratulating Burna, and saying that his UK based rapper girlfriend, Stefflon Don, helped him gain international recognition.
