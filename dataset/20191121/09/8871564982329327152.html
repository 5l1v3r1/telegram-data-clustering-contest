Rostarr Exhibition | Mural Fest | Beach Clean Up | Cool Things To Do in Bali This Weekend (Nov 22-24)

21 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Coconuts Bali

Vino + Vinyl 

Da Maria Bali, the Osteria-style restaurant in Seminyak, is set to bring good wine, tasty snacks and a conversation about the former with Australian winemaker Taras Ochota of Ochota Barrels as a special guest. For the love of wine and good food, this might just be a wonderful way to kickstart your weekend. 

Friday, Nov. 22 at Da Maria, from 6pm to 1am. IDR 140-150k per glass of wine. 

Rostarr Opening Exhibition at The Slow

Romon Kimin Yang, who is more commonly known as Rostarr, will be taking over The Slow’s ROOM13 this Saturday with his latest array of creative pieces. 

Born in South Korea in 1971, Yang was raised in the United States since he was one year old. This childhood raised across Asia and America has been a key subject of exploration for the artist, who reflects on his heritage throughout, including his painting, abstract calligraphy, large-scale murals and experimental filmmaking. This weekend, you can see his work in the flesh. 

Saturday, Nov 23 at The Slow, Canggu from 7pm to 12am. Free entry. For more information on the event, check out The Slow’s Instagram. 

Mural Fest

There’s going to be a mural competition right by Kuta beach this weekend, where the winner will get a chance to carve their art on the iconic surfboard. Hosted by Hard Rock Hotel Bali, the event also promises music spins from a live DJ, cocktail bar and beer specials – all the right ingredients for a pretty festive weekend. 

Saturday, Nov 23 at Hard Rock Hotel Bali from 4pm to 9pm. For more information, email entertainment@hardrockhotels.net . 

Old Man’s Beach Clean Up 

There’s not much to do but show up and bring yourself, as you join Old Man’s, Ocean Mimic and Eco Living Bali for this week’s beach clean up. Volunteers will get free beer, sangria or juice, and a small goodie bag as a show of appreciation. On top of doing your small part for the environment, you’ll get to mingle and sip on tasty drinks. 

Saturday, Nov 23 at Old Man’s Canggu from 3pm to 4pm. 

Fetch 1st Anniversary

Fetch Surf Concept, the first and only surf training center in Bali, is celebrating their one-year anniversary this Saturday, and you can join them on their surfing workshop. 

This is where participants can learn some theories and experience carver surf simulation, and there’s even a lucky draw you can join. For y’all looking to get some surf training while living on the island, why not start here? 

Saturday, Nov 23 at Tamora Gallery, from 4.30pm to 6.30pm. Free entry.
