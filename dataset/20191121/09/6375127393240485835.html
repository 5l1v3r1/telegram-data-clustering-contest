Top Cavite drug dealer caught

21 Nov 2019, 09:25 by Alfred P. Dalizon

A most wanted drug trafficker in Cavite province was arrested by the local police in a manhunt operation in Tagaytay City late afternoon Wednesday, Cavite Police Provincial Office Officer-in-Charge, Colonel Marlon Santos said yesterday.

In a report to Police Regional Office 4-A director, Brigadier General Vicente Danao Jr., the official identified the suspect asÂ  Jess del Mundo, a 38-year old resident of Sitio Bitin, Barangay Sambong in Tagaytay City who is wanted for murder and illegal drug trafficking.

Santos said that the suspect was arrested in Bgy. Maharlika West by operatives from the Tagaytay City Police Station and the PRO4-A Regional Special Operations Unit 4-A led by Major Juan Oruga Jr. on the strength of a warrant of arrest for murder issued by Judge Eleuterio Bathan of the Tanauan City Regional Trial Court Branch 6 in Batangas on December 13 last year.

No bail was recommended for the temporary liberty of the accused.

Santos said that during his arrest, the suspect was also found in possession of a plastic packet containing some 12 grams of suspected shabu worth P72,000, cash worth P25,200, an improvised tooter and two mobile phones.
