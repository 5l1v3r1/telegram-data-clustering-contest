Ukrainian officer says two enemy troops killed in Donbas on Nov 20

He monitors reports on losses in enemy troops' social media accounts.

21 Nov 2019, 09:58

Officer of the 53rd Separate Mechanized Brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Anatoliy Shtefan has disclosed the enemy death toll in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, over the past day.

"On November 20, 2019, the losses of Russian occupying forces were two deaths: one served in the 9th separate assault motorized rifle regiment and the other in the 4th separate motorized rifle brigade," he wrote on Telegram.

Shtefan monitors reports on enemy losses on social media.

The 9th separate assault motorized rifle regiment is part of the 1st Army Corps, Donetsk, while the 4th separate motorized rifle brigade belongs to the 2nd Army Corps, Luhansk.

As UNIAN reported earlier, Russia's hybrid military forces on November 20 mounted seven attacks on Ukrainian army positions in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, with seven Ukrainian soldiers reported as wounded in action.
