Lidl unveil bargain Black Friday deals - including insanely cheap Xbox One S

Lidl is selling a bargain Xbox One S with three digital games - and we can't find it for anywhere near as cheap elsewhere

21 Nov 2019, 09:53 by Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/mollyjaholt/

Already famous for their impressive price points, Lidl has just announced that it's about to get even cheaper, as the affordable supermarket will officially be taking part in Black Friday 2019.

But rather than offering price cuts on its already market-leading food, it seems that most of Lidl's Black Friday discounts will be on tech and electricals - and decent ones at that.

The best deal of the lot is surely their Xbox bundle, which nets you the Xbox One S Digital 1TB, along with three digital games for £129, down from £199.99.

Elsewhere online, the same Xbox One S console - minus any additional games - will still set you back a hefty £189.99 at Amazon, £199 at John Lewis and £160 on eBay.

But being Lidl, there's likely to be limited stock, with customers warned that they'll be limited to one per person, so we recommend getting to the story very early on the day it goes live, on Black Friday itself (Friday 29 November).

It's also worth noting that as the Xbox One S console in question is the all digital edition, it doesn't have a disk drive, as £129 is still a lot of money to spend on something if you'll just be left disappointed.

 

Unveiled in the retailer's weekly magazine and spotted by bargain hunters on deals forum HotUKDeals , Lidl has revealed that it will be offering price cuts on everything from Xbox consoles, to coffee machines, to electric toothbrushes, hoovers and even Prosecco. We've picked out some more of our favourite deals from Lidl's Black Friday sale event below, along with how much they cost elsewhere.

Top Lidl Black Friday deals for 2019

 

  • Bosch Tassimo Happy Single Serve Coffee Machine, £34.99 at Lidl - same price on Amazon
  • Russell Hobbs 6l Slow Cooker, £29.99 at Lidl - £44.99 on eBay
  • George Foreman 10 person grill, £29.99 at Lidl - £35 on AO.com
  • Fitbit Inspire, £49.99 at Lidl - £59.49 on Amazon
  • Oral-B Pro 650 3D electric toothbrush, £19.99 at Lidl - £23.89 on Amazon

But Lidl isn't the only supermarket getting involved in the Black Friday 2019 action, with Asda and Morrisons also both having announced that they'll be taking part in Black Friday this time round.
