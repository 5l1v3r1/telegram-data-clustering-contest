Kendall Jenner cuddles fan’s baby after shooting down pregnancy questions

21 Nov 2019, 09:53

Kendall Jenner has 10 nieces and nephews running around, so it’s no surprise she’s a dab hand with a baby.

And the model showed off her skills as she posed with a fan and their child for a photo in New York.

The 24-year-old was spotted smiling as she cuddled the little one on the streets of Manhattan on Wednesday.

Scooping the newborn up, she couldn’t resist holding the baby close to her during the impromptu photoshoot.

Kenny is the only one of her sisters to not have a child, with Kylie Jenner and Khloe Kardashian both welcoming their first children, Stormi and True, last year.

While Kourtney shares Mason, Penelope and Reign with ex Scott Disick, and Kim and Kanye West have North, Saint, Chicago and Psalm together.

So basically she is total aunt goals.

And the model revealed she has thought about starting a family in the future.

During a recent episode of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, she told Kim: ‘I can’t wait to have a kid.

‘I’m going to make him do the coolest things, so when he’s older, he’s just a stud.’

Good to know…

But, in a chat with People, she insisted she’s in no rush to get pregnant, explaining: ‘I still have years that I want to spend for myself for a little while, but more power to [Kylie].

‘She is built for this, she is an incredible mum.

‘There are parts of me that go in and out of [wanting a baby], but I am so okay right now just to live my life. Maybe I’ll have a kid in a couple of years…’
