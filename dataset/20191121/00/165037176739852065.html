Bale's agent about the trouble with the flag: "It's ridiculous"

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Gareth Bale's representative, Jonathan Barnett, referred to the controversy that has arose regarding the famous flag, that reads "Wales. Golf. Madrid. In that order."

"It's more than ridiculous". Barnett bluntly said to 'Talk Sport' about the trouble with the flag that Welsh players, including Bale, wore after winning a ticket to Euro 2020.

His words, short but clear, had some continuity in 'ESPN'. In this way, the Madrid player's agent clarified who was the receiver of the message and that image.

"The message was dedicated to the media," Barnett stated, before speaking about how all this has made the number '11' feel: "He is not worried about the reactions".
