Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite appears in FCC listing

21 Nov 2019 by Michail

The rumored fifth addition to the Galaxy S10 line - the S10 Lite is indeed coming soon with the name itself finally getting an official confirmation. The phone passed through the FCC and one of the documents shows a screenshot of the Settings and About phone sections.

There we clearly see the Galaxy S10 Lite and it also reveals its SM-G770F model number which is in line with earlier rumors.

Previously, the Galaxy S10 Lite was spotted on Geekbench which revealed its packing a Snapdragon 855 chipset alongside 8GB RAM and Android 10. Another certification from Brazil revealed the battery capacity will be 4,370 mAh.

Rumors have it the phone will come with 45W fast charging support, a triple camera at the back a 48MP main shooter and a 6.7-inch FHD+ SuperAMOLED display. Lastly, we also got a hint at the launch colors of the S10 Lite which will include White, Black and Blue. Seeing how the listings keep piling up, the S10 Lite should be arriving soon enough.
