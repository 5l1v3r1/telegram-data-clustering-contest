United ready to pay 60 million for this Bundesliga talent

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Manchester United have their wallets ready to hit the table in the up-coming winter market. One of the names that is on their agenda is Denis Zakaria, a Borussia Monchengladbach midfielder. And, according to 'Bild', the 'Red Devils' would be willing to pay up to 60 million euros for him.

The season has not started off great for Manchester United. Ole-Gunnar Solskjaer's team can't get out of the middle of the table, so the 'Red Devils' will turn to the winter market to sign footballers that will push them into the European positions.

One of the names sounding strong for the next market is Borussia Monchengladbach midfielder, Denis Zakaria, who has sounded more than once to strengthen United's midfield.

23-year-old Zakaria is shining brightly in his team and is a precious asset. According to the English media, the footballer has been valued at around 50 million euros, but in the German team, according to 'Bild', they are not ready to let him go in the winter.

As a consequence, the German media states that Manchester Unite would be willing to pay 60 million euros to convince Monchengladbach to release the footballer as soon as possible to help Solskjaer's side go back to winning ways.
