PNC HAS NOT TAKEN ANY DECISION ON THE 17TH DECEMBER, REFERENDUM.

21 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The National Executive Committee(NEC) of the People's National Convention wishes to inform the general public that it has not taken any stance on the impending referendum.

The National Chairman of the Party, Mr. Bernard Mornah has no powers to singlehandedly decide for the party on such a matter. It is only the NEC that can take such a conclusive decision.

It is important to note that the PNC is bigger than any individual and we shall prioritize the interest of the party above that of any individual or individuals.

The PNC at the moment is focused on the reorganization of the party from the grassroots level to National and would not wish to jeopardise any opportunity that has the potency to revamp the party at the grassroots level especially where we have a larger following.

The public is, hereby, advised to ignore any position from any quarters other than that of NEC of the party which is scheduled to meet soon and deal with the issue.


Thank you.

Signed

Dr.Edward Nasigrie MahamaLeader, People's National Convention Featured Video
