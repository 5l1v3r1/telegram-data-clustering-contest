Bale would repsond with a resounding "no" to Tottenham offer

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Real Madrid have a great unknown in their dressing room, Gareth Bale. His exit was in the air, but the flag incident has placed him one step closer to the exit door. It was speculated that Tottenham, now with Mourinho in charge, would like to welcome him, but the Welshman would not agree with the deal.

At least, that is what was said on 'El Chiringuito'. According to José Luis Sánchez, Bale would say 'no' to a hypothetical offer from his ex-team, Tottenham.

Both the Welshman and Spurs are experiencing turbulent times. Bale's situation in Madrid is delicate, like Tottenham's, especially now that Pochettino has been sacked and replaced by Mourinho.

The aforementioned source does not add more explanations to the reasons that would lead Bale to reject an offer that is still just a rumour.
