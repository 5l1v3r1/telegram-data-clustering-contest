Jazz in the Vineyards at Hope Estate postponed to October 2020

21 Nov 2019, 00:07

Punters planning on attending Jazz in the Vineyards at Hope Estate in December will have to wait almost 11 months after the event was postponed to October 2020.

A scheduling clash has been blamed for the postponement which was to feature Kate Ceberano and band, John Morrison's Swing City Band featuring Jacki Cooper, The Aston Martinis and The Djangologists. A new date has been announced for Saturday, October 31.

"We hate to change the date of any event, however the clash has left us no other option. We had hoped to find a date a bit sooner, but on the back of Ms Ceberano's recent success on 'The Masked Singer' her touring schedule for the early months in 2020 is understandably full," Michael Hope said.

"For those who hold a ticket for the December 2019 show, tickets will automatically transfer to the new date with an upgrade or gift on us. For those who prefer a refund, they can contact us via email info@hopeestate.com.au."
