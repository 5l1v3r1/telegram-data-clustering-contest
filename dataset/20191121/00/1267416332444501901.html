Here’s what’s coming to DAZN Canada in December 2019

21 Nov 2019, 00:09 by Dean Daley

Each month, DAZN features live sporting events and original content on its platform in Canada.

This December, ‘The New Home of Football’ will feature every game and every match of the National Football League (NFL), Premier League, Champions League and Europa League.

Boxing will be highlighted this month by the highly-anticipated rematch of the biggest heavyweight upset in decades between Andy Ruiz and Anthony Joshua on December 7th, as well as Daniel Jacobs vs. Julio Cesar Chavez Jr. on December 20th. Bellator MMA action includes Josh Barnett vs. Ronny Markes on December 20th, Ilima-lei Macfarlane vs. Kate Jackson on December 21st, and Fedor Emelianenko vs. Rampage Jackson on December 28th – all on DAZN.
