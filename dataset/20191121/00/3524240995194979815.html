Unsworth impressed with Adeniran’s progression

21 Nov 2019, 00:39 by Solape Lawal-Solarin

Solape Lawal-Solarin

Toffees’ U-23 coach, David Unsworth, believes Dennis Adeniran has improved tremendously following his promotion to first-team training at Everton. Adeniran has worked with Marco Silva’s senior side for the entire campaign and despite not being included in a match-day squad, the U-23 manager is pleased with his development.

“Without a doubt, certainly from a tactical point of view,” Unsworth replied when asked if he had noticed a marked progress in the 20-year-old.

“He’s a great athlete and has got great power and pace but what we’ve tried to do is teach him the game defensively as a central midfielder.

“That’s something with young players that takes a little bit of time.”

“We’ve looked at areas to get him and he loves running with the ball,” Unsworth told Liverpoolecho.

“There’s loads of development left in Dennis and loads of opportunities for him to get better. He’s another player that is a work in progress to get him in the first-team.”

Prior to his promotion to the first team, Adeniran was a key player in the young Toffees’ double-winning campaign last term.
