Thanksgiving gasoline prices no bargain, but improving

21 Nov 2019

SOUTHLAND  (INT)  Beleaguered Southern California motorists are getting some relief at the gas pump.

While some retailers have been reluctant to lower their pump prices, the Auto Club reports some rapid price drops.

Theres been an overall 10-cent-a-gallon decline the past week to $3.86 for 87 octane regular in Riverside and $3.91 in San Bernardino.

While the averages are down nearly 25-cents from last month, its still 28-cents higher than last Thanksgiving.

Octobers peak above $4 a gallon was the highest in four years.
