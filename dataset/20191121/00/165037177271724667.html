Tottenham close to getting one of the best scouts in Europe

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

According to 'Sky Sports', Tottenham are close to getting Luis Campos, who until now has been the author of success in may European clubs.

Pépé, Fabinho, Lemar, Falcao, Bernardo Silva... there is a long list of names that make Luis Campos one of the best sporting directors in Europe.

Milan's, Lille's and Monaco's, amongst others, successes are due to his work. According to 'Sky Sports', the director could be about to join Tottenham.
