The Club World Cup trophy is now in Qatar

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Club World Cup is just around the corner and FIFA have now started to prepare everything for the tournament. The cup is now in the country where the tournament will be played.

The Club World Cup will be held in Qatar between 11th and 22nd December and the marketing campaign for the event is now underway. 

The first thing done was the presentation of the trophy in Doha, the Qatari capital. The cup is expected to be taken round a few cities in the nation so that the public can see it at the tournament venues.
