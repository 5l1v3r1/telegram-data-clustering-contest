BREAKING NEWS: Fire at Voyager building in Castries

21 Nov 2019 by SNO Staff

(SNO) – Sections of Castries was affected by smoke due to a fire on Thursday afternoon.

Reports are that the fire is at the Voyager building in the city but this has not been officially confirmed.

The cause of the fire is unknown at this time.
