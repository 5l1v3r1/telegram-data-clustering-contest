Ex First Lady, Mrs Aguiyi-Ironsi Celebrates Her 96th Birthday In Grand Style

21 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

Nigeria’s former First Lady, Victoria Aguiyi Ironsi turns 96, and has displayed great dance steps even at that age.

Victoria’s husband was the former Head of State, JTU Aguiyi-Ironsi who was the first military head of state of Nigeria. He was assassinated on 29 July 1966.
