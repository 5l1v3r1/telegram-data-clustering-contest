"VAR? Six or seven points that Barca and Madrid have lost"

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Jose Manuel Llaneza spoke about the arrival of VAR to La Liga and made it clear that it is something that directly harms Real Madrid and Barcelona.

VAR is currently in its second season in Spain. Now fully in place, although constantly trying to improve, VAR should be that tool so that referees can decide more safely.

Llaneza also wanted to talk about this, who directly suggested that without it, Real Madrid and Barcelona would have more points due to the referee's mistakes. A statement that will not go unnoticed.

"There is equality, the six or seven points that Madrid and Barça would have are gone. Now they are wrong with them and against them," said the vice president of Villarreal to 'AS'.

"Nor do they agree with VAR because those six or seven points are gone. Before, a play at the edge of the doubtful area was a penalty for them. That possibility soon went away..." he closed.
