Rodrigues, Verma advance in Women's T20I Rankings

21 Nov 2019

India batters Jemimah Rodrigues and Shafali Verma have made significant progress in the MRF Tyres ICC Women’s T20I Player Rankings, after playing stellar roles in a stunning series sweep over the West Indies three months ahead of the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2020.

⦁ Matthews advances for the West Indies, Pakistan’s Javeria and Bangladesh’s Jahanara among others to gain in latest rankings update

Rodrigues has moved up three places to fourth position and teenage sensation Verma has gained 57 slots to reach 30th position as India leapfrogged the West Indies to fourth position in the MRF Tyres ICC Women’s T20I Team Rankings after the 5-0 victory over the former T20 World champions.
