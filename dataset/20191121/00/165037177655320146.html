Maradona withdraws and will lead just one more match

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Matias Morla, Diego Maradona's lawyer, revealed that Maradona will manage the game for Gimnasias y Esgrima La Plata in the next game in the Superliga Argentina.

Diego Armando Maradona will sit on Gimnasia y Esgrima La Plata's bench for the match against Arsenal de Sarandí, as Matías Morla, Maradona's lawyer announced.

This is a reverse of the '10' that, at least, will last until the match. Recently the possibility of his return was opened, although at the moment there is nothing confirmed.

This option has been reactivated by the unity generated on club presidential nominations. The absence of it was the main reason for Maradona's farewell from the club.

Gabriel Pellegrino, current president of Gimnasia, was the main supporter of the arrival of Diego. But his figure, at first, was out of the electoral race, meaning they were convinced to let him leave.

However, there has also been a change of plans here. Now, his name could be included for some position, which again activates the possible continuity of the technician.
