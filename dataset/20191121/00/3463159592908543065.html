FAN ZONE

21 Nov 2019 by FIFA.com

HOURS

  • OPENS: 1 hour and 15 minutes before the first match kicks off (15:00 Paraguay time)
  • CLOSES: 1 hour after the final whistle of the last match

ACCESS

  • You need to have a ticket for the four games in order to access the Fan Zone, which is located on your way to the stadium

PUBLIC VIEWING OF THE MATCHES

  • Matches will be shown in the Fan Zone only if the stadium has reached its maximum capacity
