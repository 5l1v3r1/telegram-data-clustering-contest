‘Love Island’ Star Justin Lacko Has Given More Details About That Surprise Bebe Announcement

21 Nov 2019, 00:01 by By Melissa Mason

Anyone who followed season 1 of Love Island Australia was shooketh to their core yesterday, when Justin Lacko announced out of the bloody blue that he’s expected une bebe next year.

In case you missed it, Justin uploaded this Instagram:

The second pic if you slide across is him and a girl we’d never seen before – whose account is (probably understandably tbh) set to private.

Now To Love got in touch with Justin off the back of the mysterious announcement, and he’s given a bit more background to the whole thing.

The woman is Anita Barone Scott, and it turns out she and Justin were dating – although they aren’t anymore.

“We aren’t together anymore but we are very supportive of one another and will be loving and caring for our child. Best friends for life,” he said.

He also reiterated how exciting the news is as a closure on his 20s.

“This is such a great feeling and a perfect way to celebrate the last year of my 20s,” he told the publication, adding that he will be making a “little Zoolander” out of the kid, which is mildly alarming because I don’t think he’s joking.

Look, congrats Justin and Anita, and thank you for the wonderful tea you poured into Instagram yesterday because all I care about in this world is juicy mysterious life announcements from ex reality TV stars.
