"A beauty that's soulful!" - Gautham Menon showers praises! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

21 Nov 2019

Director Gautham Vasudev Menon has been known for giving movies with great music and has worked with Harris Jayaraj in most of his movies, and AR Rahman, and has worked with Ilaiyaraja in Neethaane En Ponvasantham and Darbuka Siva in Enai Nokki Paauym Thotta.

Being a great fan of Isaignani Ilaiyaraja, Gautham Menon has enjoyed the recently released song "Unna Nenachu from Mysskin's Psycho, which was composed by the Maestro, and has tweeted praising the song. He tweeted "Just heard this song& had to like share& embrace it. Raja sir delivers a beauty that’s soulful & connects deep within. Reminds you of why he’s the master. Listen to it with your eyes closed unless you are driving or walking.@sidsriram Bravo. Transporter!".

He also tweeted "Thank you #mysskin for this beauty of a song that’s going to linger forever now. And sweetest lyrics by Kabilan. Unna nenachu Urugi ponen mezhuga, nenje odhachu parandhu pona Azhaga(wow)". Psycho stars Udhayanidhi Stalin, Aditi Rao Hydari, Nithya Menen and Ram, and releases on December 27.
