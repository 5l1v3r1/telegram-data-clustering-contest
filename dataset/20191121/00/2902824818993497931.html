Beat Horizon festival is back for 2020

The festival takes place across events in ​London, Manchester and Bristol

21 Nov 2019 by Rebecca Maguire

Beat Horizon Festival is returning bigger than ever, going from an all-day festival in London to two weekends across London, Manchester and Bristol.

The festival features a wide range of genres from a variety of acts, including the legendary bars of GZA of Wu-Tang Clan fame and the pulsing low-end of Goldie and Mala.

Each festival bill in each city will also feature a range of local underground talent, featuring Bristol Dub Club, Real Roots and Jamie Groovement.

The festival will take place in three popular venues in the cities: O2 Academy Brixton, O2 Victoria Warehouse and Bristol nightclub Motion. London will host Beats Horizon on January 18, Bristol on January 24 and Manchester on January 25.
