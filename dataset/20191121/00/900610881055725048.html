Delhi Metro introduces flight check-in facility for Air Asia, Spicejet and Go Air

21 Nov 2019 by Nisha Shroff

Further enhancing passenger experience, Delhi Metro Rail Corporation in collaboration with GMR led-Delhi International Airport has announced a new check-in facility for passengers travelling on Air Asia, Go Air and Spicejet via metro to Terminal 3 of Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi.

Air Asia, Go Air and Spicejet travellers can avail check-in facilities at New Delhi metro station and are also allowed to check-in their bags and collect their boarding passes. The check-in counters have been set up at the entrance of the New Delhi metro station of the Airport Express Line.

While this facility caters to both domestic and international travellers of Air Asia and Spicejet, however, for Go Air, this facility is only available for passengers travelling on international flights.

The service is available 12 hours before the flight and closes two hours before the scheduled departure. Earlier, this facility was only available to Air India and Vistara passengers.
