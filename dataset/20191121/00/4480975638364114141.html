PBOC to set Yuan reference rate at 7.0209 per US Dollar - Reuters estimate

21 Nov 2019, 00:56 by Omkar Godbole

The People's Bank of China (PBOC) is expected to announce the daily Yuan fix at 7.0209 per US Dollar on Thursday, having set the reference rate at 7.0118 on Wednesday. 

The USD/CNY pair closed at 7.0343 on Wednesday, representing a 0.12% rise on the day.
