Thala Ajith's new get up is double 'Valimai' and sets internet on fire - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

21 Nov 2019

Thala Ajith has given two massive blockbuster hits this year 'Viswasam' and 'Nerkonda Paarvai' which not only entertained but also delivered strong messages of family values and women empowerment respectively.  Now the publicity-shy megastar is all set to start shooting his next 'Thala 60' in mid December and fans have been eagerly waiting for updates from the team.

Thala Ajith's new getup photo has gone viral in which he is seen sporting a handlebar mustache, short receding hairline, and a killer cooling glass.  There is a strong resemblance of the 'Vedalam' character and fans have started speculating if the cop in 'Thala 60' will also have a ruthless side to him.

Meanwhile Shalini Ajith celebrated her fortieth birthday last evening and the photo of Ajith was obviously taken during the family dinner outing.  However his macho look suggests that H. Vinod and team are brewing something big for the mass Thala fans.
