One killed in Invercargill crash

21 Nov 2019

One person has died following an early morning crash in Invercargill.

Emergency services were alerted to the two-vehicle crash at the intersection of Oteramika Rd and Mill Rd South in Seaward Bush  about 6.35am.

One person died at the scene, police confirmed this afternoon.

Inquiries into the incident were continuing.

Police have appealed for anyone who saw the crash, or who has information about it, to call them on 105.
