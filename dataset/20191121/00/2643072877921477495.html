Four Men Rob, Gang Rape Lady, And Blackmail Her With Video Made From The Rape

21 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

A four-man gang, who allegedly robbed a 21-year-old girl of her belonging, kidnaped and raped her, while also using video made from the sex to blackmail her, have been arrested by Police in Anambra.

This was disclosed by the Spokesman of the Police Command, SP Haruna Mohammed.

He said the four-man gang was arrested yesterday by men of the Special Anti-robbery Squad (SARS) in conjunction with men of the recently launched Operation Kpochakpu.

Haruna said, “On the 20/11/2019 at about 03:30am, Police operatives attached to the Command Special Anti robbery Squad (SARS) in conjunction with the recently launched Operations Kpochakpu by Governor of Anambra State, Chief Willie Obiano arrested members of the syndicate at Ozubulu.”

He disclosed the identity of the suspects to include; “Collins Okpala ‘m’ aged 25 years, Nwachukwu Chimuanya ‘m’ aged 20years, Kenechukwu Anyasi ‘m’ aged 23 years and, Pascal Ogbuagu ‘m’ aged 22 years.

Haruna said that the incidence occured 14/2/2019.

He said after the suspects robbed her of he belongings, they made a pornographic video clip of the rape incident and used the video to blackmail the victim to the tune of one million naira (N1,000,000).

Haruna Mohammed said the suspects have confessed to the crime, while the sex tape was also recovered from their phones and registered as exhibit.

“They will soon be charged to court for prosecution as soon as investigation is concluded,” he added
