Camila Cabello announces Romance UK Tour arena shows for June 2020

The chart-topping Senorita has announced five arena shows.

21 Nov 2019 by Jack White

Camila Cabello is bringing her Romance tour to the UK.

The Cuban-American singer will play five arenas in June 2020, stopping off at Birmingham, Leeds, Manchester, Glasgow and London, where the tour will culminate in a show at The O2 Arena.

Tickets for the show go on general sale on Friday November 28 at 10am.

"I've never lived as much as I did when writing this album," Camila said of the album. "It was messy and beautiful, unforgettable and at times so painful I wish I could forget, it was excruciatingly consuming and impossible not to get lost in."

The tour is in support of Camila's second studio album Romance, which features the Number 1 single Senorita, Top 40 hit Liar and promotional singles Shameless, Easy, Cry For Me and Living Proof.

Released December 6, the album will be in the midst of a chart battle with new releases from The Who, Liam Payne and Kylie Minogue.
