'Ruler' Teaser: A template Balakrishna movie - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

21 Nov 2019

Nandamuri Balakrishna sports not one but two-three looks in 'Ruler'.  Why he is called a ruler is not known but he sure comes off as a shadow of Boyapati Srinu's 'legend' when he shouts as he warns.

Director KS Ravikumar packs the teaser with loads of action.  It's safe to assume that the film, too, is in the same mould.  Whether it's as a cop or as a suited-and-booted Van Dyke, Balayya is at his loudest and action-hero self.  

With music by Chirantan Bhatt, this one has Sonal Chauhan in a glamorous role and the few times she is seen in the teaser, the actress is full of oomph.

Bhumika Chawla, Prakash Raj and Jayasudha surely have got heavy rules.  Ram Prasad is taking care of cinematography.  The story is by Paruchuri Murali.  

The big-ticket film will hit the screens on December 20.
