Israeli attorney general formally charges PM Benjamin Netanyahu

Attorney General Avichai Mandelblit issued an indictment on November 21 charging Netanyahu with fraud, breach of trust and bribery

21 Nov 2019 by PTI

Israel's attorney general has formally charged Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu in a series of corruption scandals.

Attorney General Avichai Mandelblit issued an indictment on November 21 charging Netanyahu with fraud, breach of trust and bribery. He was scheduled to address reporters later on November 21.

Netanyahu has denied any wrongdoing and said he is a victim of a witch hunt.
