21 Nov 2019 by Curtis Silver

Pornhub, the reason for the continued existence of this grand human social experiment we call the internet, is joining Walmart, Target, Best Buy and the rest of the pillars of consumerism by offering its own Black Friday deal. It's a lifetime Pornhub Premium subscription, dubbed "LifePlan". Starting on Black Friday, Pornhub users can purchase LifePlan (www.pornhub.com/lifeplan), a lifetime subscription to Pornhub's HD on-demand premium service — Pornhub Premium — for a one-time fee of $299. Pornhub is also offering subscribers the option to get four free months tacked on to a 12 month subscription, but why would you want to bother with that? The math doesn't work in your favor here.
