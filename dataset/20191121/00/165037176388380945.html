Pochettino's hefty compensation after being sacked by Spurs

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Mauricio Pochettino has now become part of Tottenham's past, not future. The club fired the Argentine coach this Tuesday, with José Mourinho taking over, and the move will cost them almost 15 million euros

Tottenham's poor performance has forced the entity to unilaterally terminate Pochettino's contract, but the Argentine will not leave empty-handed. 

The manager, who consistently delivered good results and even took Spurs to a Champions League final, should receive a compensation of around 14.6 million euros, as reported by 'The Independent'.
