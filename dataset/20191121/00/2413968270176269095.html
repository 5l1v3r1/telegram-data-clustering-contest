IN PICTURES: 2019 tt100 Business Awards Programme

21 Nov 2019

The 2019 tt100 Business Awards took place on Thursday, 14 November at the Country Club in Johannesburg. Under the theme, “Leadership Lessons from Nature”, this year tt100 saw a total of 112 category finalists selected and from this, 28 category winners emerged. There was an increased number of small and emerging enterprises that took part this year as well as a number of entries from the informal sector.

There were eight categories namely the Management of Technology, the Management of Innovation, the Management of People and the Management of Systems. There was also the Sustainability as well as the Innovation Concepts category. Other categories include the Department of Science and Innovation Director-General Award for overall Excellence recognizing emerging and small enterprises as well as the Ministers award for Overall Excellence for medium and large enterprises.
