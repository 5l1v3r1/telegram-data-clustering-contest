Haverhill Police searching for armed man who robbed 2 tellers at same bank

21 Nov 2019, 00:33 by Mike Saccone

HAVERHILL, Mass. - Haverhill Police have released surveillance pictures of a man they say robbed a bank at gunpoint last week. 

Police said the man was dressed in all black and wearing a full face mask when he walked into the Bradford branch of the TD Bank on South Main Street just before 6 p.m. November 12. 

He pulled out a handgun and pointed it at a teller, while passing the teller a bag and demanding money, according to police.

Police said the man robbed a second teller before running from the bank with an undisclosed amount of cash.

Anyone with information on the man’s identity is urged to contact Haverhill Police Detective Kyle McCann at 978-373-1212.
