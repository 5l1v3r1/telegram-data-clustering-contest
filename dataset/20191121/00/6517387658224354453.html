Who is Arizona Zervas? The Roxanne rapper climbing the charts after TikTok popularity

Arizona's 'overnight success' has actually been years in the making.

21 Nov 2019 by Jack White

Arizona Zervas has achieved the rare feat of scoring a UK Top 40 single without being signed to any kind of record label.

Now based in Los Angeles, the 24-year-old singer/rapper from Maryland, US, debuted at Number 42 on the Official Singles Chart in November with his song Roxanne, which seemingly came out of nowhere. The following week, the track entered the Top 40 at Number 24 - and it's expected to continue climbing in the coming weeks.

Roxanne's popularity has been boosted significantly by TikTok. On the video sharing site, the song has been used in over 300,000 videos. That's led to huge success on charts across the world, including a Number 1 placing in New Zealand, a strong start on the Billboard 100 at 34, Number 13 in Ireland and Top 5 placings in Australia and Norway.
