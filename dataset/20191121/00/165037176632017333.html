Ter Stegen, Arthur and De Jong back in training

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Barcelona's manager Ernesto Valverde had his squad in full for the first time since the international break this Tuesday, after the last few players playing in the EURO 2020 qualifiers came back to the Spanish city.

Leo Messi and Luis Suarez had already joined the group, with Ter Stegen, Arthur and Frenkie de Jong joining up as the day progressed.

Also completing the training other players from the academy also joined up, with Iñaki Peña, Morer, Mingueza, Akieme, Carles Pérez and Ansu Fati, as reported by FC Barcelona.

Barcelona's first game after the international break will be against Leganés (13:00), where the team will be looking to get the win to confirm their spot as firsts of LaLiga and face a game as visitors after the disaster in Valencia against Levante (3-1), in Ernesto Valverde's team last LaLiga outing.
