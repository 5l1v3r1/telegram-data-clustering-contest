Chelsea rule out Ampadu return

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Ethan Ampadu is not enjoying the minutes he was hoping to have with his loan to RB Leipzig. Chelsea could cancel it and bring him back for the second half of the season, but this does not seem likely.

At least, this is what the 'London Evening Standard' states. Ampadu, one of last season's revelations at Chelsea, went out on loan to RB Leipzig in the summer, but his situation leaves a lot to be desired.
