Prince Andrew steps down from public duties

21 Nov 2019

Britain's Prince Andrew is stepping down from public duties for the foreseeable future after being engulfed in a scandal involving convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein.

Andrew, the Queen's second son, denies an allegation that he had sex with a 17-year-old girl Virginia Roberts (now Giuffre)  procured for him by his friend Epstein, the American financier who killed himself in prison earlier this year while awaiting trial on sex trafficking charges.
