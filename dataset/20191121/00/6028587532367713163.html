Holiday Gift Guide 2019: Best Salty Snack Baskets

21 Nov 2019 by Chelsea Davis

Salty snacks are universally loved, so give the gift that can be enjoyed by anyone and everyone, on any occasion or average day. From crunchy popcorn and smoked nuts to jerky and pickles, here’s a drool-worthy selection of salty, delicious snack baskets to gift this holiday season.

Let It Snow Popcorn Basket: This is a snack lover’s dream come true. This gorgeous gift basket is filled to the brim with a huge selection of popcorn flavors including butter, cheese, caramel, chocolate, cinnamon sugar, cornfusion, cranberry kettle, cracked pepper & sea salt, holiday cookie dough, mint chocolate chip, ranch, s'mores, salted caramel bourbon, and white cheddar. Other treats featured are chocolate truffles, chocolate covered pretzels and an assortment of snacks to keep you going all holiday season.

Deluxe Almond Favorites: This is for the almond aficionados who crave the crunch of these wholesome nuts! This gift box comes loaded with both sweet and savory almonds including milk chocolate, yogurt and butter toffee-covered almonds, in addition to the salty gourmet flavors such as rosemary, black truffle and garlic, olive oil and herb. These savory almonds are roasted, seasoned and cured with herbs and spices to create a rich taste. Sharable and delicious? Yes, please!

Caveman Crate: This “Mancrate” is jam packed with all the tasty, meaty and salty treats any snack enthusiast would go crazy for. This crate celebrates our foreheaded forefather’s most awesome food contributions: meats and nuts. Once you get this crate open, and trust me, it’s somewhat of a challenge, inside you’re rewarded with bison, beef, turkey and venison jerky, along with two types of flavored nuts. If you want to go even further, opt for the exotic meats cane which is filled with, you guessed it, more types of jerky!

Popcornopolis Santa Gift Sack: This festive Christmas sack is filled with the ultimate snack food: popcorn! This perfectly sharable gift basket comes filled with 24 individual snack-sized mini cones featuring caramel corn, kettle corn, cheddar cheese and zebra popcorn. Salty, sweet, crunchy and addictive, it’s ideal for hosting and entertaining during the holidays.

Bloody Mary Kit and Garnishes: For your friend who loves a tasty bloody mary, gift this kit that comes with everything you need to create an over-the-top cocktail. Included is a Bloody Mary drink mix, chili lime rim salt, dill pickle hot sauce, garlic dill cornichons and pitted grilled green olives. If you want to get extra fancy, top it off with some crunchy bacon strips (not included). Add your favorite vodka and you’re ready to party.

Paleo Yes You Can Have A Cheeseboard: From Miyoko’s Creamery, the maker of dairy products made from 100% plants, this snack basket is for all your snack-loving paleo friends. This special basket comes with a variety of “cheese” wheels in favors like garlic herb, chive, black ash and herbes de provence. Other fun salty snacks inside are mushroom jerky, olive spread, crackers and more.

The Bacon Crate: Everyone is obsessed with bacon, so get the carnivores in your life a crate of all-bacon-everything! This “Mancrate,” while tough to crack open even with the mini crowbar provided, is worth every ounce of sweat. Inside is a selection of salty treats such as carnivore candy jalapeno bacon jerky, carnivore candy old fashioned maple bacon jerky, along with bacon peanut brittle, seasoning and jam.

Kind of a Big Dill Deluxe Kit: Crunchy, briny and oh-so-dilly this gift basket is filled with a variety of pickled vegetables including cauliflower, red onion and carrots; two jars of fine pickles; and five jars of assorted pickles including extra spicy, garlic dill, bread & butter and more. Anyone with a refined pallet will appreciate these artisan pickled vegetables coming from the top pickle producers in the country. Epicurean Trader is a specialty shop based in San Francisco.
