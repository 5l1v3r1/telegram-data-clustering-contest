Guardiola, closer to an indefinite holiday than to Germany

21 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Pep Guardiola has once again been linked to Bayern Munich in the last few days. According to 'Kicker', the Spanish coach would opt for a year out rather than returning to German football.

When Guardiola cuts ties, it's usually forever. Already based in Manchester City, Pep has the most difficult task yet in trying to surpass a very comfortable first placed Liverpool. Beyond sporting performance, many are wondering what will become of his future.

With the recent departure of Niko Kovac from Bayern Munich, Germany has come link Guardiola with Bayern Munich once again, raising the possibility of a return to the Allianz Arena.
