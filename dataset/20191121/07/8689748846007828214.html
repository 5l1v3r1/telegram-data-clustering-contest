Burna Boy’s girlfriend, Steflon Don and Nigerian musicians reacts to his Grammy award nomination

21 Nov 2019, 07:06 by Nana Yaw

Yesterday, Burna Boy’s album, African Giant got nominated in the Best World Album category.

He was nominated in the category alongside Altin Gün for Gece, Bokanté & Metropole Orkest Conducted By Jules Buckley for What Heat, Nathalie Joachim With Spektral Quartet for Fanm D’Ayiti, and Angelique Kidjo for Celia.

The news was well-received by his numerous fans and social media users. His colleague musicians and his British girlfriend, Steflon Don took to Twitter to show love and support and congratulate him on his achievement.
