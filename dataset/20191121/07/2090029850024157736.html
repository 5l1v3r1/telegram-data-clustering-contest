Residential building on fire in Alausa, Ikeja (Video)

21 Nov 2019, 07:46 by Damilola Ismail

A residential building in Alausa area of Ikeja, close to Fela Shrine Lagos is on fire.

According to an eye witness on his way from work around the Alausa area of Lagos, the fire started some minutes ago and the numbers for Fire fighters in the was not going through.

He said it looked like no one was around in the compound as the fire continue to burn the building.
