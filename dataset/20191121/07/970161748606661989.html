The Apprentice's Ryan-Mark reveals mystery man lying on floor wasn't him

21 Nov 2019, 07:00

The Apprentice’s Ryan-Mark Parsons has revealed that the mystery man lying on the floor of the boardroom was not him.

The womenswear consultant, who was the latest candidate to be fired from the show, has finally put the record straight – but it means we may never find out who the mystery man really was.

Ryan-Mark’s fellow candidate Thomas Skinner took to social media to joke that the man lying on the floor of the boardroom was the 19-year-old.

He originally tweeted: ‘People keep asking me who was the geeza laying on the floor under the table in last tasks boardroom! Well I can actually reveal it was @ryanmarkparsons.

‘Foot masseuse, the #Pig flys him in from Hamburg every Wednesday to keep his feet relaxed during the boardroom! #TheApprentice.’
