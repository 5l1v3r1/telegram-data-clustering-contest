What it's like to be in a Bristol City team talk given by Lee Johnson

Dressing room insight given by the Robins players.

21 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Gregor MacGregor

Bristol City this week published some very revealing insight into the club and how the playing squad perceive the senior coaching team.

The Robins players were tasked with giving impersonations of the coaching team delivering talks to the group. The results were highly amusing, with the club's Twitter feed showing senior members pretending to be Lee Johnson, Dean Holden and Jamie McAllister. See the videos below.

If there was any worry that the impressions - with Ashley Williams' rendition of Holden, Bailey Wright's McAllister and Sam Szmodics' Johnson also proving very funny - could lead to repercussions, it was allayed in this week's press conference.

"The gaffer came up with it on the way up to Barnsley it was, and what a great idea. It shows from a supporter's point of view a little bit behind the scenes, and there's a lot of time spent in hotels and it shows the team spirit we've got as well," explained Holden earlier this week.
