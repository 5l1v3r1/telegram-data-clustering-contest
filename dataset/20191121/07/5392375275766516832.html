Chinese manufacturer, Oppo, held a conference in Beijing yesterday where it released the ColorOS 7 system. In addition, the company announced that Oppo Reno 3 series will be the first ColorOS 7 device. At the press conference, Oppo also announced that ColorOS now has more than 300 million users worldwide. Its users cover more than 40 countries/regions. In addition, Oppo ColorOS has been translated into more than 80 languages.

Oppo ColorOS 7 improvements

The internal code of ColorOS 7 is “killer whale”. This system evolves with a borderless concept and is lightweight. It comes with a softer color scheme, redrawn icons, and Art + wallpapers. This in association with the new quantum animation engine provides more visual interaction. It also has an all-weather dark mode, global theme, rich personalization.

The UI can fully optimize the system while also improving the application startup speed. It also improves the system resource utilization efficiency. The official said that the overall startup performance increased by 25%; RAM utilization increased by 40%; system fluency increased by 30%; game frame rate increased by 19%; touch response speed increased by 15%. This is what this system will offer the Oppo Reno 3 series.

ColorOS image algorithm comes with Soloop recording video editor. This ensures that from shooting to editing to sharing gives users a convenient experience beyond imagination. In addition, there is also the Breeno sleep mode for a relaxing sleep. It also comes with the flashback button for scene switching and the automatic filling of the input codebook.
