I'm A Celebrity fans worry as 'accident prone' Dec Donnelly sports second injury

I'm A Celebrity's Dec Donnelly joked he was 'accident prone' after appearing to cut his finger, with fans noticing his plaster

21 Nov 2019, 07:59 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity viewers noticed Dec Donnelly sporting a slight injury in the latest episode of the ITV jungle series.

Some fans even aired their "worry" for Ant McPartlin's co-host on Twitter , with him confessing he was a little "accident prone" at the moment.

As Ant and Dec returned for the latest episode, eagle-eyed fans quickly spotted a plaster around one of Dec's fingers.

While the tiny injury wasn't addressed, fans assumed he'd suffered a cut and wondered what had happened.

Just days ago Dec addressed a separate injury after fans spotted marks on his arm.

This time around, Dec did respond to concern with a light-hearted tweet but didn't explain the need for the plaster.

A fan tweeted to the duo's account: "Dec mate stop with the injuries please, first the burnt arm now you’ve cut your finger?? We worry!!!!"

Several other fans liked the comment and shared their own concern.

Dec responded: "So accident prone atm! D x."

So there's no need to worry Dec fans, he's all good.

Earlier on in the week Dec explained the mystery marks on his arm were in fact burns after a separate incident.

Viewers took to Twitter throughout the particular episode to ask if he was okay, wondering what had happend.

Dec wrote: “Nothing exciting unfortunately. I scalded myself while sterilising Isla’s dummies!”

Fans then responded to the news to warn Dec of the hazards of being a father.

“Oh Dec, life of a dad,” one follower replied - along with a couple of crying-with-laughter emojis.

“Bless ya Dec you’re a good dad for Isla,” commented another.

I'm A Celebrity airs nightly at 9pm on ITV.
