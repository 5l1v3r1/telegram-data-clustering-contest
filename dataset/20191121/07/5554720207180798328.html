UPPRPB Police 2018 Constable Recruitment Results: Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) declares Police 2018 Constable Recruitment Results @ uppbpb.gov.in

21 Nov 2019, 07:23 by JKR Staff

UPPRPB Police 2018 Constable Recruitment Results: The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has declared Police 2018 Constable Recruitment Results on its official website uppbpb.gov.in. The results announced on 20 November are for the written exams for the 2018 Reserve PAC (Constable).

123,921 candidates have made the cut. The successful candidates will not undergo what’s known as the Document Verification and Physical Standard Test (DVPST) round. The UPPRPB will announce the details on the next round soon.

Follow these steps to get your results

  • Visit the UPPRPB official website uppbpb.gov.in
  • Click the link to check the result for the relevant exam available on the home page
  • A PDF with details of all the successful candidates will open
