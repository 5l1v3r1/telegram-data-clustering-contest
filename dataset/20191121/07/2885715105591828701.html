UPPRPB UP Police Constable Result 2019 declared, check direct link

UP Police Constable result date 2019: The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board conducted the written exam on January 27 and 28, 2019 for 49,568 posts. The result is available at the official website - uppbpb.gov.in.

21 Nov 2019, 07:16 by Careers Desk

UP Police Constable results 2019: The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has released the UP Police Constable results on November 22. The candidates who have given the constable recruitment exam will be able to check their results from the official website – uppbpb.gov.in.

The UPPRPB conducted the written exam on January 27 and January 28, 2019. All those who have cleared the written exam, these applicants will be called for physical efficiency test and document verification process.

UP Police Constable results 2019: Steps to check results (once declared)

Step 1: Visit the official website-uppbpb.gov.in. Here is the direct link

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘UP Police Constable results 2019’

Step 3: A pdf file will open carrying names of the selected applicants

Step 4: Check your results and download for future reference.

The recruitment examination was conducted to fill a total of 49,568 posts.

Meanwhile, UPPRPB has declared the cut-off for the constable recruitment held in 2013 for the remaining posts. The results for the 3,295 vacancies was declared in 2015 and after the order from the Supreme Court of India, cut off for the remaining posts has been declared on the website.
