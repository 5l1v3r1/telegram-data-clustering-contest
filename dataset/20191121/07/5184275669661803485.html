VIDEO: Ananya Panday Witnesses Fan Frenzy In Delhi During Pati Patni Aur Woh Promotions

Ananya Panday was spotted in Delhi while promoting her new song from her upcoming film ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’.

21 Nov 2019, 07:34 by Koimoi.com Team

Ever since the debut, Ananya Panday has become the most loved star of the young generation thanks to her magnetic charisma.

Testimony of the same is the recent video where Ananya Panday was spotted in Delhi while promoting her new song from her upcoming film ‘Pati Patni Aur Woh’ and well, the screams and the cheers with “Ananya! Ananya!” were totally unreal!

Thousands of people were cheering and hooting for the young actress as she arrived in style along with her co-stars for the launch of ‘Akhiyon Se Goli Maare’ from her upcoming film.

Ananya Panday is receiving a lot of love from the audience ever since her first look from the film came out.

Ananya who has been receiving a lot of appreciation for the trailer of her upcoming film Pati, Patni Aur Woh is on a new high. She’s not just a loved actress but also, the youth influencer which makes her truly one star of this generation. Also, on the work front, the actress will be seen alongside Ishaan Khatter in Khaali Peeli.

Ananya’s upcoming film Pati Patni Aur Woh is the official remake of the 1978 film of the same name. The film stars Kartik Aaryan as Chintu Tyagi, Bhumi Pednekar as his wife and Ananya Panday as the ‘Woh’ between them. The film is all slated to release on December 6 this year.
