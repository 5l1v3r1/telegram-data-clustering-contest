Viral video: People fall, run as a ‘T Rex’ charges at them in this prank

Prankster Goubran Bahou's latest video documents what people do when confronted by what seems like a small Tyrannosaurus Rex, that emerges from around a bend.

21 Nov 2019, 07:10 by Trends Desk

A new prank video, featuring what looks like a dinosaur, has taken the internet by storm. Prankster Goubran Bahou’s latest video documents what people do when confronted by what looks like a small Tyrannosaurus Rex, that emerges from around a bend.

In the video, Bahou is first rounding a corner in panic. Curious passersby watch him run down the market street, but continue on their way. However, moments later, the big head of what looks like a dinosaur emerges from around the corner.

Some of the people turn and run, others fall and general hilarity ensues as the person wearing the inflatable dinosaur costume emerges. In one case, a man falls to the ground after spilling his lunch, and as the ‘dinosaur’ inches closer, he tries to fight it off with an umbrella.
