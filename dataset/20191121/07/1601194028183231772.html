Adam Cole reacts to winning the Ladder Match on WWE NXT

21 Nov 2019, 07:05 by Arunava Ghoshal

NXT Champion Adam Cole and Dominik Dijakovic faced each other in a Ladder match on tonight's episode of the Black-and-Gold brand. Both the Superstars gave their all to win the battle and eventually, it was the leader of The Undisputed Era who emerged as the victor. Owing to his win, Adam Cole and his team will have a significant advantage during their match against Team Ciampa at NXT TakeOver: WarGames this weekend.

Adam Cole's reaction
