India vs Bangladesh, Pink Ball Test: India clear favourites in maiden Day-Night Test at Eden Gardens

21 Nov 2019, 07:19 by PTI

Highlights

  • Sourav Ganguly convinced the Bangladesh Cricket Board to agree to a Day/Night Test just a few days before their team was to land in India
  • Already at the top of the World Test Championship standings, India would look to consolidate their position after another series victory

KOLKATA: Indian cricket will finally embrace the pink revolution after initial reluctance when Virat Kohli's seemingly infallible galacticos lock horns with a deflated Bangladesh in their maiden Day/Night Test starting on Friday.

It took India a new BCCI regime under maverick former captain Sourav Ganguly to take the pink ball plunge, a good seven years after the International Cricket Council approved the format to revive interest in Tests.
