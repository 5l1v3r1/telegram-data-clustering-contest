Borderlands 3’s first story expansion Moxxi’s Heist of The Handsome Jackpot arrives on December 19

21 Nov 2019, 07:30 by Darryn Bonthuys

If you want colour, absurd guns and plenty of cannon fodder on which to test a rifle that spews forth a beam of fiery death while adding rockets for good overkill measure, you’d be hard-pressed to find any game that does that ridiculous requirement better than Borderlands 3. Gearbox’s return to Pandora was an absolute hoot this year, and much like the games before it, a quartet of meaty DLC expansions plan to flesh out the experience further. For the right price of course.

The first DLC is coming soon! Called Moxxi’s Heist of The Handsome Jackpot, it’ll feature your favourite Vault Hunter joining Moxxi for a trip into space where you’ll be raiding a seemingly abandoned space station that was once owned by that Handsome Jack fella.
