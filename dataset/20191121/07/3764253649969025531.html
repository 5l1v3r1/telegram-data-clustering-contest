Burna Boy's Grammy nomination: AKA congratulates him after Nigerian man asked for his thoughts

21 Nov 2019, 07:13

Hard work truly pays as Nigerian singer Burna Boy is receiving payment for his hard work throughout the year. The singer was recently announced as one of the African artiste who made it to the Grammy nomination list for 2020.

Legit.ng had earlier reported that the singer made the nomination list for Best World Music Album for his Africa Giant album.

Ever since it was announced, celebrities and Nigerians have been celebrating the singer for making the country proud.

In a turn of events, South African rapper AKA, who is not on good terms with Burna Boy, has also congratulated him. The rapper congratulated Burna Boy in a post on the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter.
