10 EastEnders spoilers: Linda's car crash, wife swap shock and Mel aftermath

21 Nov 2019, 07:01

Marriages are feeling the strain in EastEnders – so what better time than now for a wife swap event? Yeah, that’s sure do go down well. One couple facing almighty troubles is Mick and Linda Carter (Danny Dyer and Kellie Bright), especially as she is hiding the fact that her struggle with alcohol dependancy is becoming worse.

Meanwhile, with Martin Fowler (James Bye) having split from Stacey (Lacey Turner), he finds himself becoming close to Ruby Allen (Louisa Lytton) and he soon makes a move on his wife’s best mate. But how will Max Branning (Jake Wood) respond to this turn of events?

10 EastEnders spoilers

  • Linda is arrested after backing into a car.
  • Louise fears that Lisa is becoming ill again.
  • Ian’s campaign to become a councillor hits a pretty big snag.
  • Ben promises to change his ways and offers support to Sonia.
  • Mick launches a wife swap event – with varying results for couples.
  • Chantelle and Jags reminisce about old times.
  • Billy gives Karen a trial at E20.
  • A stressed out Martin makes a move on Ruby but how will she respond?
  • Honey uses the kids as an excuse to get out of a holiday with Adam.
  • Mick realises Linda’s drinking is out of control.

Linda is hungover but proceeds to take Ollie to school so that Mick can attend his medical appointment. Later, she hopes she has gotten away with it when she backs into a car but later the police turn up and breathalyse her before arresting her. Meanwhile, Mick gets the plans for the wife swap underway.

As Lisa reels from what happened, Louise starts to think that her mum is unwell and Keanu makes Sharon feel guilty when he relays this to her. Ian’s attempt to plug his councillor campaign goes awry so he takes drastic measures.

Ben is in high spirits after helpinh Sonia, Billy gives Karen a trial at E20 and Max feels Ruby is avoiding him.

Tuesday 26th November

The wife swap gets underway and everyone at the Vic gathers excitedly to find out who they have been paired with. But an unhappy Gray soon takes steps to saboatge the results. Meanwhile, Chantelle and Jags reminisce about old times.

Honey tries to avoid the topic of a holiday with Adam and uses the kids as an excuse but when Billy agrees to babysit, Honey is forced to think again. Dotty’s moneymaking scheme leaves her on the wrong side of Martin while Ian seeks help from the Panesar brothers.

Thursday 28th November

Iqra and Adam are both unimpressed to be paired off together so Adam books a hotel so that he doesn’t have to stay with her. However, as they start to reminisce, there is hope that they can start repairing their relationship.

Ruby arrives to stay with Mick and Ollie as the Vic while Gray is watchful as Chantelle bumps into Jags and Kheerat. Bex is flustered when Martin continues to clash with Dotty and Sonia while Ruby hears that Martin and Stacey are over.

Friday 29th November

Ruby unwillingly helps Ian with his campaign and Martin is coerced into getting involved but when Ian makes a dig about Stacey, Martin loses his cool with her. As Mick, Linda, Max and Ruby catch up, drama erupts with Martin and Ruby follows him only for him to lean in for a kiss.

Ash encourages Honey to have more confidence in herself and helps her pick an outfit – but she gets a lukewarm response from Adam. Chantelle has a go at Mitch about his parenting so he sets out to make things up with Bailey. When he and Chantelle spot Gray comforting Bailey, Chantelle passionately kisses her husband.

Kat orders Kush to put an end to their part in the wife swap competition while Sheree opens up to Kush about struggling to get on with Denise so Kush offers some advice. Meanwhile, Mick realises that Linda’s drinking is out of control.
