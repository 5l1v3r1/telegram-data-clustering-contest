‘Donations to Rotary are well-managed’ - The Nation Newspaper

21 Nov 2019, 04:27

Joseph Jibueze

 

A former District 9110 Director-General, Dr Mathias Soga, has assured the Rotary Club’s donors that the money they commit to worthy causes will be well-utilised.

He spoke during a lecture organised by the Rotary Club of Onigbongbo at Ikeja GRA, Lagos.

The theme was: The obligations and benefits of Rotary Foundation.

He advised Rotarians to accept the foundation as theirs and to be committed to its ideals, which can be realised through regular donations.

Soga, who was Director-General between 2010 and 2011, said Rotary Foundation was founded with the objective of “doing good to the world”.

He described the foundation as the largest in the world and the fifth among the world’s best charities.

Allaying fears of fund mismanagement, he said: “The fund is efficiently managed by a committee, who devote over 95 per cent of its money to charity.

“If you have people who are looking for where to put their money, tell them to come to the foundation.

“Tell them that it has been at the forefront of eradicating diseases.”
