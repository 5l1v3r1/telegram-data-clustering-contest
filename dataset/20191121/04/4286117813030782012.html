Nicobar: Woman gives birth on Navy vessel after Daring rescue

The woman was rescued while in labour from a remote island. She gave birth on a Fast Interceptor Craft that had left from INS Kardip.

21 Nov 2019, 04:34

A woman gave birth on an Indian Navy interceptor boat on Wednesday after she was rescued while in labour in Daring, a village in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

Both mother and child are safe; they were taken to the Community Health Centre in Kamora Jetty.

The boat that rescued the woman was a Fast Interceptor Craft that had left from INS Kardip in Kamorta island.
