Thursday's Liverpool transfer talk news roundup: Kylian Mbappe, Sander Berge, Ugurcan Cakir

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Liverpool eye possible January reinforcements.

21 Nov 2019, 06:57 by Andy Brent

Jurgen Klopp's title contenders were not particularly busy during the summer and are unlikely to make any major moves in January either, but that has not stopped the rumour mill from linking them with additions.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest news on possible comings and goings from Anfield.

Thursday morning's headlines:

Liverpool 'step up interest in Ugurcan Cakir'
The agent of Trabzonspor goalkeeper Ugurcan Cakir reveals that Liverpool are "keeping close tabs" on his client. Read more.

Kylian Mbappe agent 'tells Real Madrid he won't pen Paris Saint-Germain deal'
Kylian Mbappe's mother and representative allegedly tells Real Madrid that the forward will not sign a new contract at Paris Saint-Germain. Read more.

Chelsea 'to battle Liverpool for Sander Berge'
Chelsea prepare to battle Liverpool for the signature of Genk midfielder Sander Berge, according to a report. Read more.

Chelsea join Liverpool, Manchester United in Joe Gelhardt race?
Chelsea are reportedly considering an offer for Wigan Athletic youngster Joe Gelhardt, who is attracting widespread interest from Premier League clubs. Read more.
