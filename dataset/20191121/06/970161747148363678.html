Demi Lovato shows off baby bump on Instagram after going public with new man

21 Nov 2019, 06:48

Demi Lovato made fans take a double take as she showed off a sizeable baby bump on Instagram… but it’s all for her guest appearance in Will & Grace.

The singer, 27, posted a photo of her cradling the bump in a polka dot dress as she told her followers: ‘Real or fake? #WillandGrace #MeetJenny’.

Despite the caption giving the game away that the bump was in fact fake, her followers shared their shock at the photo in the comments.

They posted: ‘Literally just had a mini heart attack’, ‘Before I read the caption I was hyped af. Mama demi’ and ‘oh lawddd my heart dropped for a sec’.

Others added: ‘DEMI DONT PLAY W ME LIKE THIS’ and ‘Ooh gurl unless you got 7 months pregnant in like 3 months than that’s the Lords baby’.

Demi had recently gone public with her new boyfriend Austin Wilson, so fans were quick to assume she had some more big news to spill before they read the caption.

It’s not known how long she and the tattooed 25-year-old model have been together, but the pair both announced their relationship to the public on the same day, sharing a loved up photo of them together.

She also shared a comment from one fan on her Instagram story, hinting that a new album might be on the way before a baby.

The fan wrote ‘Demi is pregnant with D7’, to which the singer replied: ‘This is true’.

The singer and actress will be starring in three episodes of the final season of Will & Grace, where her character Jenny is described as ‘a guarded gal who comes into the life of Will (Eric McCormack) in an unexpected way.’

There may not be a baby on the way, but we can’t wait to see Jenny and a new album from Demi on the horizon.
