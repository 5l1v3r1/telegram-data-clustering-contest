What happened after NXT went off-air

21 Nov 2019, 06:10 by Abhilash Mendhe

Tonight's WWE NXT saw several RAW and SmackDown Live Superstars invading the show, mere days before the upcoming Survivor Series PPV, where all the three brands will collide in a series of Triple Threat matches.

RAW Women's Champion Becky Lynch competed against Rhea Ripley in a match that ended in a No Contest after interference by Shayna Baszler and others. NXT Tag Team Champions Bobby Fish and Kyle O'Reilly defeated SmackDown Live's The Revival. The episode came to an end with a full-fledged brawl involving Superstars from all three brands. The Full Sail audience greeted Seth Rollins with a "Seth's not cool!" chant. The brawl continued as the show went off-air.
