Mindhunter season 3 might not arrive on Netflix for a really long time

21 Nov 2019, 06:44

Mindhunter season three is currently on pause so we could be waiting quite a while for the next chapter to land on Netflix.

Fans have been eagerly anticipating the crime thriller’s renewal following series two’s launch earlier this year, but showrunner David Fincher is currently preoccupied with another major project according to The Hollywood Reporter.

The publication reported: ‘Talk of a third season of Mindhunter is on hold while Fincher focuses on his next feature.’

Fincher’s next film has been commissioned by the streaming giant and is a black and white movie called Mark, which tells the story about Citizen Kane screenwriter Herman J Mankiewicz.

Netflix has yet to actually confirm whether there will be a third season of Mindhunter, but that doesn’t mean that there won’t be one just yet.

Speaking to PopCulture.com about a third season, actor Holt McCallany said: ‘I don’t think anybody knows right now, in August of 2019, exactly what lies in store for Holden and Bill, and Wendy.

‘We are hopeful that we will get to do the whole five seasons because the audience seems to have really responded to the show, people really like the show.

‘And we’re very proud of the show, and very excited to keep making the show.’

McCallany previously revealed that Fincher is keen to roll out five seasons of Mindhunter, much to everyone’s delight.

‘Because you know I believe in his talent, you know, and I know that you know, he [Fincher] wants to do five years of this show,’ he said to Screen Rant.

‘Five seasons of these characters and so I’m hopeful, you know I don’t take anything for granted.’

Mindhunter is available to stream on Netflix.
