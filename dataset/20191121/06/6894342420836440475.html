Forget Dark Souls, Someone Made A Game Where You Play Tetris And Snake Simultaneously

21 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Heirul Kamel

There are a token number of people who find enjoyment in playing ‘rogue-like’ or Dark Souls-like video games. While classic games such as Tetris and Snake does offer a good challenge, it’s unlikely that these two games would fall in the category of ‘sadisticly difficult’. Until now.

French freelance developer Grégoire Divaret-Chauveau created a web game that pits you to play both Tetris and Snake simultaneously. Focusing on one of the games is hard enough but in the interest of crushing our fragile souls, Divaret-Chauveau have coded both games to share the same controls and it doesn’t just stop there. If you lose in one game, you’ve basically lost both games immediately.
