Kriti Sanon to play a pivotal role in 'Bachchan Pandey'

21 Nov 2019, 06:25 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kriti Sanon is one of the most sought-after stars in Bollywood. After her big Bollywood debut opposite Tiger Shroff, there was no looking back for the actress. She worked in some amazing big banner films. The star has also shared screen space with some superstars in Bollywood.

Read Also: Photo: Kriti Sanon looks pretty as a peach as she gets papped at the airport

And like all her other roles, Kriti is also going to shine bright with her lead role in the film 'Bachchan Pandey'. Recently, Bachchan Pandey’s director Farhad Samjhi spoke about Kriti’s role in the film. He said, “I can say this without any qualms that Kriti’s role in the film is better than a parallel hero role. It’s a very meaty role, in fact, any actress wouldn’t have gotten such a role, it is that good.''

Even Kriti is quite thrilled and beyond excited to be a part of this Sajid Nadiadwala production which is slated to release in Christmas 2020. This will be the second time Kriti will be sharing the big screen with Akshay Kumar and her fans would surely be excited to see her yet again in a pivotal role.
