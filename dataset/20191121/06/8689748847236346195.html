Akwaboah to announce his exit from SarkCess Music at this year’s Rapperholic Concert?

21 Nov 2019, 06:28 by Nana Yaw

There’s a cloud still hanging over SarkCess Music over the reported exit of Akwaboah.

A few days ago, it was reported of the exit of Ghanaian Highlife singer, Gladstorm Kwabena Akwaboah Jnr, popularly known as Akwaboah from. Sarkcess Music.

According to the report, the ‘Hye Me Bo’ singer has left Sarkodie’s record label and signed a contract with a multi-million record label.

The name of the record we were told is OB Empire Music, a multi-million new record label owned by one Oheneba.

Well, speaking on the said issue on YFm, Akwaboah stated that there’s nothing like that but his demeanour showed he had something to hide.

The interviewer who had information about his exit asked that he has heard the big announcement will be made on stage at the upcoming Rapperholic Concert.

Akwaboah who looked reluctant to answer nodded that maybe that will be the case on the night of the Rapperholic Concert because it wouldn’t be a bad idea.

Akwaboah also noted that his 3-year contract with the label will soon expire and that there have been advance talks over the contract but a decision will soon be made.
