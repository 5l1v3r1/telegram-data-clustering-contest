For how long can you push your music with “beefs”? – Joey B quizzes

21 Nov 2019, 06:58 by MyNewsGH

Rapper, Joey B says he will not stoop so low to project his brand with a “BEEF” with other artistes.

According to him, “Beefs” have a short lifespan and therefore not a recipe to recognition in the music industry.

He noted that it’s imperative for musicians like himself to build solid brands that has lasted impressions on the masses.

Joey B who was speaking to ghanaweekend.com mentioned that “I want to leave this thing with a clean sheet. With a very solid brand. With a very cool wave or music”.

He added “I just wanna make music and just make all my people happy and try and give back to the needy and do cool stuff. That’s me”.
