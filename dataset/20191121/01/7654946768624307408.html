Jacoby Ellsbury’s sad Yankees career by the numbers

21 Nov 2019, 01:54 by Post Staff Report

Jacoby Ellsbury never lived up to the $153 million contract he received from the Yankees, and will end up having not played more than half the potential games in his seven-year deal after being released Wednesday night.

By the numbers

$294,230: Money per game played (520)

$299,412: Money per hit (511)

$3,923,076: Money per home run (39)

$772,727: Money per RBI (198)

2015: 111 Games – 116 hits – 7 home runs – 33 RBIs – .257 – $21M
