Momoland's JooE selected as endorsement model of Korean burger franchise 'Mom's Touch'

21 Nov 2019, 01:56 by beansss

On November 21, South Korean burger and chicken franchise 'Mom's Touch' announced Momoland's JooE as their newest endorsement model!

JooE will be working alongside YouTuber Yoon Jjoo Kkoo to promote delicious 'Mom's Touch' menu items with her signature, bright energy. Fans can look forward to new 'Mom's Touch' CFs featuring JooE beginning this November!
