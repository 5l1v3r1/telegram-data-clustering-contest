Louis Vuitton Introduces New Sneaker Trunk

The latest addition to the luxury label’s Fall/Winter 2019 lineup.

21 Nov 2019, 01:43 by Patrick Montes

Last week, Louis Vuitton revealed plans for a special pop-up residency to commemorate their 2054 collection. Now, the popular luxury brand is introducing a new Sneaker Trunk.

Specifically, the Sneaker Trunk will be debuted at Miami’s forthcoming Savoir-Faire showcase on December 3. It will be able to hold at least six pairs of low-top shoes — up to size 13 — and eight pairs of high-top sneakers — up to size 12. Louis Vuitton will also offer a wide array of customization options for each consumer who is interested.

“Combining Louis Vuitton’s classic art of trunk craftmanship with contemporary design, Louis Vuitton’s Sneaker Trunk is the newest addition to the House’s iconic hard-sided collection,” reads an official description. “This one-of-a-kind Sneaker Trunk is an emblem of Louis Vuitton’s visionary spirit and heritage craftsmanship.”
