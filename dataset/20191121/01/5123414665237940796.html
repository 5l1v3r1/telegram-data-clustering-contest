Squarepusher Announces New Album 'Be Up A Hello,' Shares Single "Vortrack"

This will be Squarepusher's first album in five years when it is released in January.

21 Nov 2019, 01:34 by Ryan Middleton

Squarepusher, real name Tom Jenkinson, has announced his new album Be Up A Hello. The first since 2015’s Damogen Furies, Squarepusher returns to the moniker after spending the past few years working on various different projects.

According to a press release, the album will features moody tracks, floor fillers and breakbeat records. On this album, he used an array of vintage analogue and digital hardware, similar to what he used to make his music in the 1990s. These are in contrast to the types of tools that were used on Damogen Furies.

To support the new album, Jenkinson will also tour around North America, the UK and Europe in the spring.  

Be Up A Hello will be on January 31, 2020 via Warp. To help push the album, he has released the first single “Vortrack,” which also comes with a Fracture remix. Pre-order the album now.
