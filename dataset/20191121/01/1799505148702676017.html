Shipwreck

21 Nov 2019, 01:37 by BRIEF

NEW YORK – A federal court in New York ruled that a shipwreck salvage company should be awarded silk cloth recovered from a British ship that was torpedoed and sunk by a German U-boat during World War I, but the company is not awarded 526 silver bars that were also part of the ship’s cargo.
