India allows telecoms firms to defer spectrum payments

21 Nov 2019, 01:46

India will allow mobile operators to defer payments due for airwave capacity bought via auction until the end of March 2022, finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman said late on Wednesday.

NEW DELHI: India will allow mobile operators to defer payments due for airwave capacity bought via auction until the end of March 2022, finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman said late on Wednesday.

The decision of Prime Minister Narendra Modi's cabinet provides some respite to wireless carriers which are due to pay the country's telecoms department at least 920 billion rupees (US$12.82 billion) in overdue levies and interest following a court ruling.
