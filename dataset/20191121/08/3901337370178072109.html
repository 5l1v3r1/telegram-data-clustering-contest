How Ronaldo Once 'Settled' Messi Debate Revealed?

21 Nov 2019, 08:29 by SL International

The debate as to who the best player in the world is between Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi will seemingly rage on forever, but the Portugal superstar once reportedly settled the question based on his appearance.

In an interview with Sportsmail's Piers Morgan, former England international Peter Crouch revealed something of his national team teammates, who played for Manchester United at the time, reveaed to him about Ronaldo. 

The former Tottenham Hotspur and Liverpool striker explained how the United players would tease the Portugal star about his rivalry with Messi, to which he once made a witty comeback.

"I spoke to some of the United players and said 'what’s Ronaldo like?'" Crouch started. 

"... And they said they used to wind him up by saying Messi was better than him and he said: 'Yeah, but Messi doesn't look like this (him)'.

Crouch later went on to choose which one of the two global superstars he believes is the greatest of all time, naming Messi ahead of Ronaldo. 

"Ronaldo's record is ridiculous. He averages over a goal a game in like 500 games," Crouch added.

"... I admire his determination and mentality and his single-mindedness, but if you’re talking gifted footballers, there is no comparison to Messi."
