Deontay Wilder: WBC champion once criticized Tyson's boxing record in IG rant

21 Nov 2019, 08:48

Deontay Wilder, WBC heavyweight champion, has been caught in an explosive Instagram live rant where he criticized Mike Tyson's boxing record after his ability in the ring was questioned by fans.

Mike "Iron Mike" Tyson is considered as one of the boxing legends who brought class and aggression to the game but during a live IG rant dated back to 2017, the 53-year-old's quality was called out by his fellow American.
