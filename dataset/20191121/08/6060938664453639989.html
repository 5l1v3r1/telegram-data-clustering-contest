Waterrelief! Mom-to-be fan gets Bengaluru FC box seat

21 Nov 2019, 08:07 by Manuja Veerappa

BENGALURU: Meghana Nair, eight months into pregnancy, had one wish before motherhood — to watch her team Bengaluru Football Club play at their fortress, the Sree Kanteerava stadium. The 27-year-old BFC fan found the going difficult during the last match since water bottles and eatables from outside are not allowed.

On Wednesday morning, referring to the team’s Saturday night game against Kerala Blasters, Meghana tweeted, “Hello @bengalurufc! Is there any possibility that I could get permission to carry some hot water from home in a reusable bottle?? I'm currently 33 weeks pregnant & I need to stay hydrated, also take my medication. It was terribly inconvenient when I came to watch previous matches."

The Sunil Chhetri-led side have won over fans with their thoughtful gestures and this time it wasn’t any different.

Within a couple of hours, BFC responded,“Hey, Meghana. We love the fact that you're introducing your little one to the Fortress already! We would be more than happy to accommodate you in the Owner's Box for Saturday's game, where there's hot water and more. This one's on us. #FansFirst”
