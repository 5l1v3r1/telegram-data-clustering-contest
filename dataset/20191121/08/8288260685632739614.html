British Airways flights chaos at Heathrow and Gatwick amid 'technical issue'

Flights to and from the UK's two busiest airports, Heathrow and Gatwick, appear to be among those affected

21 Nov 2019, 08:55 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

British Airways flights are disrupted on Thursday morning - due to a "technical issue", the airline said.

Flights to and from the UK's two busiest airports, Heathrow and Gatwick, appear to be among those affected.

BA operates flights out of both London transport hubs to a variety of far-flung destinations.

London Heathrow and London Gatwick are widely used by Brummies aiming to head to the likes of North America and the Far East.

A spokeswoman for the airline said: "Our teams are working hard to resolve a technical issue which is affecting some of our flights, and we have re-booked customers onto alternative flights and offered hotel accommodation where they have been unable to continue their journeys last night."

The British Airways spokeswoman then added: "We are very sorry for the disruption to their travel plans."

Customers were urged to check ba.com for the latest flight information.

Passengers are also being urged to ensure their contact details are up to date on their booking.
