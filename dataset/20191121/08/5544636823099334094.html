Grammy: What Stefflon Don Said About Burna Boy’s Nomination

21 Nov 2019, 08:05 by Oyindamola Olubajo

 

Stefflon Don, a British cum Jamaican rapper and girlfriend of Nigerian afro-fusion star Burna Boy has reacted to his new feat of bagging Grammy 2020 award nomination.

Concise News reports that Burna Boy was nominated in the World Music category for his album ‘African Giant‘ which was released on Friday, July 26, featuring acts like YG, Darmian Marley, Jorja Smith, Manifest, Angelique Kidjo among others.

The announcement was made by the Recording Academy, popularly known as the Grammy Awards.

The World Music category has African music legend like Angelique Kidjo who is also a Grammy winner as a nominee.

Since the news broke on the internet, celebrities have taken turns to congratulate Burna Boy and Stefflon has also joined the league.

In a Twitter post Stefflon wrote “Issa Grammy for the boy!!!!!!!!! 🦍🙏🏽🏆😁 We claim it!!!!! ❤️ #AfricanGiant”

Meanwhile, earlier this year, there were speculations that Burna Boy and Stefflon parted ways and the Nigerian star was dating actress, Princess Shyngle.

According to a report, Burna Boy and Shyngle were seen at an outlet in Lagos.

But later debunking the claims, the duo was spotted sharing a passionate kiss at a popular club in Lagos.

The video which became available to the public was shared on social media by the “Hurtin’ Me” crooner.
