A young Nigerian man based in the United States identified as @ValObikwere has shared his thoughts on dating women from different countries compared to Nigerian women.

The man went further to compare Nigerian women to Americans and Caribbean. He said Nigerian women are ungrateful while others are grateful for the littlest things. He also shared his experience with a Nigerian woman he tried to impress.
