Dominican Convent raises school fees to $15 000 per term

21 Nov 2019, 08:03 by Mandla Ndlovu

The Dominican Convent High School in Bulawayo have made a shocking announcement of the 2020 fees structure for pupils learning there or intending to enrol for next year.

In a letter to the parents the school said the SDC EGM had adopted the following fees structure:

Form 1-4: $12 500
Form 5-6: $15 000

The school said the parents must deposit an amount of $3 600 by 29 November 2019.

Parents who cannot afford to pay the school fees are advised to inform the school by 6 December 2019.
