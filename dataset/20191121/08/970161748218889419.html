Ovie Soko continues to be GOAT as he returns to basketball after Love Island

21 Nov 2019, 08:10

Ovie Soko is officially returning to basketball, following his stint in the Love Island villa.

The 28-year-old – who ended the ITV2 series in third place with ex India Reynolds – has joined the London Lions, and will be making his court comeback next month.

And we are absolutely living for it.

Confirming the news, he revealed his excitement at playing basketball in his hometown.

‘I’m excited to be back home and help contribute to basketball back home where my love for the sport started,’ the MailOnline reports he said.

‘To be able to play in front of friends and family and supporters in my hometown is huge for me and look forward to pushing the basketball culture in the right direction.’

While his new head coach, Vince Macaulay, added: ‘Soko is a highly experienced and talented player who is in demand across Europe.

‘We are thrilled that he has chosen to play for the London Lions, UK’s current Premier League Champions, and inject increased passion into British basketball.

‘He will add power and creativity to the team – everyone is buzzing!’

Ovie will be appearing for the London Lions in their game against Glasgow Rocks on 8 December, at the Copper Box Arena in Stratford.

The basketball pro has played for teams around the world, including a five-year stint in the US, as well as France, Greece, Spain and Italy.

Basically, he’s smashed it.

Ovie’s news comes after he split from India Reynolds, just months after they left the Love Island villa together.

According to reports, the pair found it hard to co-ordinate their conflicting schedules, and ended things amicably.

But, rather than throw himself a pity party with seven gallons of Ben And Jerry’s, the athlete remained as upbeat as ever, quoting Rihanna and reminding fans to ‘shine like diamonds’.

Sharing a video on his Instagram page soon after their break-up, he said: ‘On this beautiful Sunday you should all remember: “Shine bright like a diamond.” Because Rihanna said so’.

We are absolutely not worthy of Ovie Soko.
