‘Viswasam’ versus ‘Bigil’ box office collection: Distributors get into a heated debate

21 Nov 2019, 08:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The fight between Ajith and Vijay fans is something that will never end, currently there is a competition between the fanclubs as to which actor is leading the Twitter trends and has highest box office collections. But now this argument has boiled down to the distributors as well, who are having a heated conversation over the box office collection of 'Bigil' and 'Viswasam' on Twitter. KJR Studios, who distributed Thala Ajith's 'Viswasam' criticised BOFTA Dhananjayan for his statement about 'Bigil' box office report by sharing a video on social media.
