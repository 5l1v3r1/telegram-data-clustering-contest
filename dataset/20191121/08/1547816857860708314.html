The Call Of The Wild Trailer: Harrison Ford And A Big-Hearted Dog Promise The Adventure Of A Lifetime

21 Nov 2019, 08:51 by Gretel Sequeira

The much anticipated trailer of the film, The Call Of The Wild, has been dropped online, today. Based on the journey of a friendly and a big-hearted dog ‘Buck’, the trailer is gripping and takes you on an adventure of a lifetime.

The most anticipated trailer of the 2020 release ‘The Call Of The Wild’ is finally out and looks gripping and shows potential. ‘The Call Of The Wild’ is a live-action CGI-animated adventure film and is a remake of the Twentieth Century Pictures' 1935 film with the same title. The film ‘ The Call Of The Wild’ is an adaptation of the famous 1903 literary piece of Jack London of the same name. Starring Harrison Ford in a pivotal role, the trailer of the film is looks promising from the audience's perspective and is set to take you on an adventure to a lifetime.

The trailer of the film starts with Harrison Ford on his adventurous journey to the Alaskan Yukon. Based on the dangerous place ‘Yukon’, the trailer resumes with the voice of Harrison Ford narrating his journey to the magical and risky Alaskan Yukon where he wanders to find peace. Moving on further, Ford explains how he meets the big-hearted dog ‘Buck’ who had been spoiled in a Californian home and landed up suffering in the exotic wilds of the Yukon during the Gold Rush of 1890s but could not be broken at all.
