Famous birthdays for Nov. 21: Ken Griffey Jr., Carly Rae Jepsen

21 Nov 2019, 08:00 by UPI Staff

-- Pope Benedict XV in 1854

-- Belgian painter Rene Magritte in 1898

-- Wrestler Brie Bella in 1983 (age 36)
