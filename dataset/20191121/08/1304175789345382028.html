Residence Inn hotel opens in Natick, Massachusetts

21 Nov 2019, 08:47 by Tatiana Rokou

Located across from the Natick Mall and a short drive to downtown Boston, the Residence Inn Boston Natick offers its guests convenient access to Wellesley College, Framingham State University and Babson College.
 

NATICK, MA – The 136-suite Residence Inn by Marriott in Natick, Massachusetts is now open for business with its upscale, stylish decor that offers flexibility, while creating a unique environment. Located at 1 Superior Drive, the all-suite Residence Inn Boston Natick will operate as a Marriott franchise, jointly owned by XSS Hotels of Hooksett, New Hampshire and National Development of Newton Lower Falls, Massachusetts, and managed by Colwen Management, Inc. of Portsmouth, New Hampshire.

This is the first extended-stay hotel to open in Natick. Located across from the Natick Mall and a short drive to downtown Boston, the Residence Inn Boston Natick offers its guests convenient access to Wellesley College, Framingham State University and Babson College.

“We are pleased to introduce Residence Inn to the Natick area,” said Diane Mayer, Vice President and Global Brand Manager, Residence Inn. “When on the road for an extended period, our guests need space to spread out, maintain their life’s pace and restore their energy to help maintain a healthy balance and routine while traveling. This new hotel offers a seamless blend of modern style and functionality that allows them to settle in and thrive.”

Residence Inn Natick is designed as an all-suite hotel that offers studio and one-bedroom suites. Guests looking for a revitalizing sleep experience will enjoy the hotel’s plush mattresses and crisp linens, while guests focused on being productive will value their suite’s large, well-lit work desk, ergonomic chair and complimentary high-speed Internet access. Designed for stays of five nights or more, each suite also has a fully-equipped kitchen with a coffeemaker, microwave oven and residential-sized appliances.

With the maximizer in mind, Residence Inn has created a decor with a “home plus” feel that is warm, optimistic and flexible. The Residence Inn Boston Natick rotates a wide selection of healthy choices for its free hot breakfast buffet every day. Extending the feeling of comfortable living on the road, the hotel features grocery delivery service, complimentary enhanced Wi-Fi, 24-hour onsite food and beverage market, dry cleaning services and onsite guest laundry room. The hotel is pet-friendly and provides guests with a business library, a fitness center, a full-service bar in the lobby, and complimentary parking.

The Residence Inn Boston Natick features a 3,800 square foot Event Center offering sophisticated, flexible space accommodating groups up to 160 people. The Event Center features the divisible Broadmoor Ballroom, spacious pre-function space, and the Cochituate and Hunnewell Boardrooms.
