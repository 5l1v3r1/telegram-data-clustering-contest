Xiaomi has been launching innovative products but now the company has launched an older device, which this generation of people may not have even seen. The company has now launched the old-fashioned DIY retro projector.

EDITOR’S PICK: Qualcomm reveals expected global 5G rollout roadmap for 2020 and beyond

The DIY retro projector from Xiaomi is priced at 229 Yuan, which roughly converts to $33.  The company says that all the parts of the projector are equipped with German laser cutting technology, which ensures accurate precision when assembled.

Xiaomi has said that the entire assembly process is divided into 16 parts, where each part has a corresponding number. The user can start assembling the product as per the manual which will guide them step by step and the company says that each step is clearly written. After assembly, the product turns out to be 22cm long and 24.5cm high.

The company has also revealed that the once the assembly is done, it can display real life film with 58 frames. Also, there’s no need to install a battery or a cable and the device can be directly projected through hand crank.
