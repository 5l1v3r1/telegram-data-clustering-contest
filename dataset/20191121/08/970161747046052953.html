Australia legends baffled by controversial no-ball call in opening Pakistan Test

21 Nov 2019, 08:28

The decision not to reverse a Pat Cummins wicket for an apparent no-ball has baffled a host of Australia legends during the first Pakistan Test.

Ashes star Cummins had Pakistan wicketkeeper-batsman Muhammad Rizwan caught behind shortly after tea at the Gabba but Australia’s celebrations were muted as the third umpire was told to check for a front-foot no-ball.

The replays appeared to show Cummins’ heel landing on rather than behind the line but, much to the surprise of the star-studded commentary team, third umpire Michael Gough upheld the on-field decision.
