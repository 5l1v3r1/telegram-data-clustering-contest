Become a Jew: Residents, strangers, and other aliens

Rav Yitzchak and William address the concept of Ger Toshav (Resident Alien) from the weekly Torah portion of Chayei Sarah.

There has been so much controversy over this term and it is time for people to just learn what it means, who it is speaking about, and if it is something that applies today?
