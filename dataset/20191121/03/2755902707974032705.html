Shocker As Police Detain 10-Month-Old Baby & Mother After Brutal Crackdown On Civilians

21 Nov 2019, 03:00 by Andrew

In another damning indictment on the Mnangagwa administration which has come under fire for not upholding human rights, a 10 month-old baby was detained at Harare Central Police Station together with the mother, yesterday. The mother is among 8 people who were reportedly arrested on yesterday after a brutal crackdown on citizens in Harare’s CBD.

According to online publication, 263chat, the detained civilians who were arrested after the orgy of violence unleashed by the ZRP  have since been charged with disorderly conduct in public place as defined in section 41 of the Criminal Law (Codification and Reform) Act.
