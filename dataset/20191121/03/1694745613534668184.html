Ashley Graham Is Still Twerking at 7 and a Half Months Pregnant, and Her Energy Is Goals

21 Nov 2019, 03:15 by Chanel Vargas

We all know that keeping active while pregnant is important, but Ashley Graham's mid-week dance moves during her third trimester have me truly impressed. In an Instagram video posted to her page on Wednesday, the almost first-time mom shared a clip of herself twerking (well, while sitting in a chair) at seven and a half months pregnant, and, uh, where does she get all this energy from?!

"Wrapped a new project and I can't wait to tell you!!" the model and Pretty Big Deal podcast host captioned the post, and her secret project gives us twice the reason to get up and dance. Ashley will soon welcome a baby boy with her husband, Justin Ervin, and they recently went all out for a baby shower. Watch the mood-boosting video above, and prepare to smile.
