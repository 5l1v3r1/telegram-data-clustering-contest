Computer Society seeks structure for digital economy

21 Nov 2019, 03:52

By Lucas Ajanaku

 

The Nigerian Computer Society (NCS) on Wednesday urged the Federal Government to come out with a structure for digital economy.

Its President, Prof Adesina Sodiya who spoke with ICT reporters yesterday in Lagos, commended the Federal Government for renaming the Communications Ministry to Communications and Digital Economy but said the government must move beyond this and take concrete steps to harness the potential of a true digital economy.

He advised the Federal Government to automate its processes, saying it is curious that a document such as asset declaration form should not be done electronically.

He said for the country to take full advantage of the fourth industrial revlution, there is need for change of strategy.

According to him, there has been a series of roadmaps which have become outdated.

He said the NCS will unveil a new roadmap next month to give direction to members of the National Assembly, adding that the new roadmap will take of the requisite skills being developed in the era of Artificial Intelligence (AI).
