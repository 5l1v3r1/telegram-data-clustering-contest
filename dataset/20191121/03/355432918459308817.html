Kamala Harris: "Donald Trump got punked" by North Korea

21 Nov 2019, 03:43 by Marina Pitofsky

Sen. Kamala Harris mocked President Trump over his administration’s policy towards North Korea during the fifth Democratic primary debate.

“Donald Trump got punked,” Harris joked when asked whether she would make concessions to North Korean leader Kim Jong Un in order to keep the talks started under the Trump administration going with the country.
