Selena Gomez Reveals Release Date of New Album - Find Out Here!

21 Nov 2019, 12:10

Selena Gomez just revealed the release date of her upcoming album!!!

The 27-year-old singer, who just released “Lose You To Love Me” and “Look at Her Now” last month, revealed the release date on her official website this morning.

The album, filled with 13 tracks, will be out on January 10th, 2020 and is available for pre-order on Apple Music.

Just the night before, Selena confirmed something huge was coming last night on her Instagram, writing, “Something exciting is coming tomorrow. ✨”

The post came with a montage of black and white photos of her.

Fan accounts have noted that the album artwork and title are temporary.
