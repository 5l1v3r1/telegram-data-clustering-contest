SS Rajamouli ropes in three International actors for his next film RRR

21 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Filmfare

SS Rajamouli is one of the biggest names in Indian cinema right now. After the success of his directorial ventures Baahubali and Baahubali 2 that made over Rs. 2000 crores worldwide, cine-goers are waiting for his next big project. The filmmaker will be directing stars like Ajay Devgn, Alia Bhatt, Jr. NTR and Ram Charan in RRR. A periodic action flick, RRR is based on India’s freedom struggle against the British.

After roping in big names from both the Hindi and South film industry, RRR will also see international actors Olivia Morris, Alison Doody, and Ray Stevenson in pivotal roles. Welcoming these eminent Hollywood stars to the film, the official social media handle gave out details of their characters.

Olivia Morris will play one of the female leads in the film while Alison Doody who starred in Indiana Jones and Ray Stevenson from the Thor franchise will be playing antagonists in this periodic drama.

According to the series of tweets on the film's official handle, Doody has already shot the first schedule of the film while the other two actors will soon join the cast for the next schedule. RRR will release in 10 languages worldwide on July 30 next year. It has been confirmed that the budget of the film is  around Rs. 300 crores.
