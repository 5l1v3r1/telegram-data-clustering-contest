TWICE Tops Billboard Japan with Impressively Huge Margin

21 Nov 2019, 12:06 by HelloAnon

TWICE dominates Billboard Japan once again as their new Japanese album "&TWICE" was released a day ago.

According to Billboard Japan, the Asia's One Top Girl Group currently ranks #1 in album sales with a total of 101,810 copies sold in 2 days leading the chart with an impressively wide margin.

1. TWICE - &TWICE (101,810 copies)
2. Generations from Exile Tribe - Shonen Chronicle (27,855)
3. Sheena Ringo - Newton no Ringo / Newton's Apple (13,167)
4. Swordsman Masters Formation of 300 Years - Musical Touken Ranbu 300 Years Lullaby (12,481)
5. Tokyo Ska Paradise Orchestra - Tsugihagi Colorful (6,567)

Congratulations, TWICE!
