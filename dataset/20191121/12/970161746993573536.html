Where is Jeffery Epstein's ex-girlfriend Ghislaine Maxwell now?

21 Nov 2019, 12:03

Following the statement from Buckingham Palace that Prince Andrew is to withdraw from all Royal duties over his association with Jeffrey Epstein and the ongoing unanswered questions surrounding the case, one question very much on the minds of everyone following the scandal is where is Jeffery Epstein’s ex-girlfriend Ghislaine Maxwell?

Ghislaine Maxwell was an associate and alleged former girlfriend of Epstein’s and is also the woman accused of recruiting girls to be exploited and abused by the convicted sex offender.

Maxwell has been accused of acting as Epstein’s ‘madam’, but she has denied those allegations.

Prince Andrew and Ghislaine Maxwell became friends after the Prince split from wife, Sarah Ferguson, in 1996.

During the Duke of York’s highly-criticised interview with the BBC at the weekend, the royal revealed to Emily Maitlis that he had seen Maxwell as recently as earlier this year admitting that he had last seen his long-term friend: ‘in the spring, summer. She was here doing some rally.’

Andrew denied that the meeting had taken place after Epstein’s arrest and told Maitlis that he and Maxwell hadn’t discussed Epstein at all.

‘There wasn’t anything to discuss about him because he wasn’t in the news,’ Andrew said. ‘You know, it was just… we had moved on.’

Where is Jeffery Epstein’s ex-girlfriend Ghislaine Maxwell now?

While Prince Andrew has confirmed that Ghislaine was definitely in the UK earlier this year before taking part in a charity motoring rally from London to Monte Carlo, the exact whereabouts of Maxwell now is not known.

Maxwell has withdrawn from public life and has not been seen out in public since finding herself at the center of an FBI inquiry.

The Daily Mail reports that Maxwell has been: ‘laying low in a New England beach town’ while her relationship with Epstein has been under interrogation.

The 57-year-old is allegedly reluctant to venture out into the public and it is not known if she is still living in the $3million (£2.3m) mansion in Manchester-by-the-Sea, Massachusetts, where neighbours say she had previously lived.
