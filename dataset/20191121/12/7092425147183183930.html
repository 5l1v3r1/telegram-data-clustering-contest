BREAKING: 16 people found alive in truck on ferry bound for Ireland

21 Nov 2019, 12:42 by Fiona Kelly

Up to 16 people have been found alive in the back of a truck on a ferry which is en route to Rosslare Europort in Wexford.

It is understood the discovery was made about 9.20am by a staff member who heard banging from inside the truck on a Stena ferry from France.

All of the people involved are men aged between 20 and 40 years old, and it is believed they said they had travelled from Belgium up to five days ago.

It is not known what nationality the people are but it is believed they may be from Asia.

Gardai and paramedics are now waiting at Rosslare Europort for the ferry to dock at 3pm.
