IGNOU December TEE hall ticket 2019 released: How to download

IGNOU December TEE hall ticket 2019: The hall tickets have been released on the official website and the candidates can download the admit cards using their enrollment number.

21 Nov 2019, 12:55 by Education Desk

IGNOU December TEE hall ticket 2019: Indira Gandhi National Open University has released the IGNOU Term End Examination hall ticket 2019. The candidates who will sit for the examination must download their admit cards from the official website of IGNOU-ignou.ac.in. The term end examination will be conducted from December 31, 2019 across the country.

The term end examinations were rescheduled and were earlier supposed to be conducted from December 7 and will now be conducted from December 31, 2019.

IGNOU TEE admit cards: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website-ignou.ac.in

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘Hall ticket for December Term end examination’

Step 3: You will be directed to a new page.

Step 4: Enter the 9 digit enrollment number and select the program and click on submit.

Step 4: The admit cards will be displayed on the screen.

Step 5: Check the details on the admit card, download and keep them safe for future reference.

The students will have to bring their admit cards along with them to the exam hall. They will not be allowed to enter the examination hall without valid admit card.
