Anand Mahindra takes a trip down memory lane with 'Bijlee', Mahindra's EV from 1999

21 Nov 2019, 12:32 by BusinessToday.In

Back in the day, Mahindra group had partnered with Delhi government in 1998-99 to conceptualise and launch the 10-seater electric three-wheeler Bijlee

As India hopes to see electric mobility become the norm in coming decades, Anand Mahindra reminisced about an electric vehicle from the last millennium. The Chairman of Mahindra group posted a tweet on Thursday about Bijlee, an electric three-wheeler developed by Mahindra back in 1999.

The tweet read: "Mahesh Babu of @MahindraElctrc fished this out from the archives. Very few know we launched an electric 3wheeler called 'Bijlee' back in 1999. Conceptualised by S.V.Nagarkar,here in the pic. Didn't work out financially but we learned a lot & renewed our commitment to promote EVs!"
