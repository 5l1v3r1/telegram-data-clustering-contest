Young boys who vanished after being dropped at school in Midlothian found safe

Michael and James O’Brien have been traced after they went missing from Carnethie Street in Rosewell around 8am this morning.

21 Nov 2019, 12:23 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

Two young boys who went missing after being dropped at school in Midlothian have been traced.

Michael and James (Jamie) O’Brien , aged 9 and 6 respectively, vanished from Carnethie Street in Rosewell around 8am this morning.

Police search teams, including dog units, were looking for the youngsters after they failed to enter the school .

But now officers have confirmed they have been found safe and well.

A Police Scotland statement read: "We would like to thank members of the public and our partners who shared information and assisted us with inquiries."
