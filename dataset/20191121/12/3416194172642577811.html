Derek Hough Jokes About Getting His Appendix Removed

21 Nov 2019, 12:11

Derek Hough heads out after some shopping at Barneys New York on Wednesday (November 20) in Beverly Hills, Calif.

The 34-year-old dancer was joined by his girlfriend Hayley Erbert.

That day, Derek shared a joke on his Instagram about getting his appendix taken out.

“Dang girl, you’re like my appendix… Cause i don’t understand how you work, but this feeling in my stomach makes me want to take you out,” he posted.

The weekend before, Derek had emergency surgery to get his appendix removed after waking up early in the morning to severe pain.
