The World Is Your Oyster With New Google Earth Creation Tools

21 Nov 2019, 12:32 by Stephanie Mlot

Over my 33 years, I’ve lived in nine different cities in six different states/regions on two different continents.

Each home comes with its own sights, sounds, smells, and tastes—anecdotes that can’t be contained to a growing photo album.

Now, with the help of new creation tools in Google Earth, I can rekindle those memories.

“For nearly 15 years, people have turned to Google Earth for a comprehensive view of our planet,” according to product manager Gopal Shah. “But our mission has never been to just show you a static picture of the planet; we want to bring the whole world to life.”
