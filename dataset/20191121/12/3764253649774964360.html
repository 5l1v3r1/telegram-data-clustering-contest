Audio is the new trend now - BBNaija Nina says

21 Nov 2019, 12:25

- Former BBNaija housemates Nina Ivy is out here throwing shades

- The former reality star says audio is now the new trend in Nigeria

- It is unclear who the shade is directed at but some Nigerians have reacted with their assumptions

Former Big Brother Naija 2018 Double Wahala edition housemates, Nina Ivy, has been caught thrown shade on the streets of Twitter. The young lady has shared a tweet that got Nigerians talking about her intention.

In her tweet, Nina Ivy stated that audio is now the new trend. The term audio became a popular slang after Rudeboy released a song titled Audio Money, the song talks about people living fake life and audio has become something Nigerians say to refer to anything fake.

Nina's tweet got many people thinking she is aiming her words at someone in particular. Nigerians took to her post to comment on their thoughts.
