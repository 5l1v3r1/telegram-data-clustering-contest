Importers of rice blacklist CITI FM; cancel all advertising contracts – Sammens reveals

21 Nov 2019, 12:07 by MyNewsGH

Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Citi FM/TV, Samuel Atta Mensah better known as Sammens has revealed that his establishment has been blacklisted as companies that import rice have withdrawn all their adverts from the media house as well as cancelled existing contracts.

Citi TV/ Radio have started a campaign to help in the purchase of rice grown by Ghanaian farmers in view of a bumper harvest and poor market

The initiative has had the support of numerous people in positions of influence and has been trending on various social media platforms.

Some Agric experts have said that there will be an improvement in the rice farming industry only when decision makers make it a point to regulate the importation of rice and other foodstuff into the country.

In a tweet updating his followers and how the initiative is doing, he mentioned that his media house has lost clients that import rice into the country.

He said ” And all the companies that advertise imported Rice on Citi FM withdrew their ads and cancelled the contracts. Big deal? Ghana wins!”.
