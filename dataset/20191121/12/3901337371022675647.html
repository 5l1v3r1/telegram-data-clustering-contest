How Middendorp Should Approach Semi-Final

21 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Soccer Laduma

In an exclusive feature in this week’s edition of Soccer Laduma, former Kaizer Chiefs goalkeeper, William Shongwe explained how he thinks Ernst Middendorp should take on Maritzburg United.

Amakhosi will lock horns with Maritzburg on Sunday in the semi-finals of the Telkom Knockout competition and with a spot in the final at stake en-route to their first piece of silverware in years, Chiefs are expected to throw everything at the Team Of Choice.

Speaking to Soccer Laduma this week, former Chiefs shot-stopper, Shongwe shared his opinion on how the German tactician should approach the encounter.

“The exact same way he’s been approaching all these other games. He shouldn’t change anything about his team. One thing I believe Ernst has got right is the structure and organisation. Look at the number of goals they’ve conceded, and you can clearly see that there’s a plan and tactical structure in his team. I think that’s what has worked a great deal. Chiefs are playing in a more structured form. They might not be playing that well, but they have good structure and organisation. That’s why they’re playing with a lot of purpose,” he said.
