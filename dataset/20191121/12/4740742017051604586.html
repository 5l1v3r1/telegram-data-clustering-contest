The Killers at Ashton Gate: What you need to know about getting tickets for 2020 tour date

Demand for tickets for the 2020 tour is expected to be high

21 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Robin Murray

This year's concert series at Ashton Gate was a roaring success with the likes of Muse, Spice Girls and Rod Stewart all putting on spectacular shows for packed out crowds.

Music lovers were delighted when the stadium's bosses confirmed the series will return next year - and even more so when The Killers were announced as the first act last week.

The Las Vegas band are playing just nine stadium gigs in the UK and Ireland next year as part of their biggest-ever stadium tour, one of which will be at the home of Bristol City FC.

Fronted by Brandon Flowers, the three-piece took the Pyramid Stage at Glastonbury Festival by storm this year when they headlined Saturday night, after performing a secret set on the John Peel Stage in 2017.

And June 9 will see the hugely popular outfit return to the West Country, supported by Welsh rockers Manic Street Preachers.

Ashton Gate will be increasing the capacity to 32,000 for the gig, and stadium managing director Mark Kelly said it was important to select the right gig for next summer’s series.

"I am delighted that we have secured such a renowned act to perform here for the first time in Bristol," said Mr Kelly, speaking after the booking was announced.

"The Killers have had five chart-topping albums since 2001 and are one of the most iconic rock bands of the 21st century.

"With the Manic Street Preachers as their support act, it’s set to be another memorable night for the stadium with a 32,000 crowd capacity."

How to get tickets

General tickets go on sale tomorrow (Friday, November 22) at 9am.

Given the huge worldwide appeal of the band, demand for tickets is expected to be high.

Tickets will be available on Ticketmaster and Gigs and Tours.

Gig-goers who want to indulge in a more luxurious experience go try for hospitality packages, which will also go on sale at 9am tomorrow on Bristol Sport's website.
