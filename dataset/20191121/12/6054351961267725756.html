Not Over It: Summer Walker Laughs At Becoming A Rigid Meme

21 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Brian Alvarado

A photo of Over It singer Summer Walker at the Soul Train Awards has made its way around social media. And once again, the internet proved why it’s undefeated, transforming the quirky shot into a collection of memes.

The Best New Artist Award winner took to Instagram to have a good laugh at the internet’s clever creations. The memes inserted Walker into random situations, including the singer blasting off into space as a rocket and decked out in Popeye’s gear behind a cash register.

“I’m really tired of this,” she wrote in the caption alongside a laughing emoji.

Walker has had her battles with anxiety. Earlier this month, she had to cancel a number of tour dates due to her anxiety issues, which she addressed on Instagram.

“I wasn’t even gonna go today cause I was to scared,” Walker explained. “When I got there I dodged the red carpet and tried to get escorted through the back…BUT I pushed through my #socialanxiety today and I’m really glad I did.”

Walker’s new collaboration with Chris Brown and London On Da Track, “Something Real,” premieres on Thursday (November 21).
