JUST IN: Osinbajo presides over NEC meeting in Aso Rock - The Nation Newspaper

21 Nov 2019, 12:30 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

Augustine Ehikioya, Abuja

Vice President Yemi Osinbajo on Thursday presided over the National Economic Council (NEC) meeting.

The meeting was held at the Council Chamber, Presidential Villa, Abuja.

At the meeting are State Governors who are members of NEC and some Deputy Governors.

Ministers in charge of the economy and the Governor of Central Bank of Nigeria, CBN, Godwin Emefiele were also in attendance.

The meeting was still in progress as at the time of filing in this report.
