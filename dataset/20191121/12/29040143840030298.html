Protect the trade-in value of your Galaxy S9+

The Galaxy S9+ is a big and beautiful phone that's still great to use or buy in 2019. Whether you're looking to give your phone a new look or simply want a case for protection, we're sure you'll find one of our recommended cases a winner. There's a case out there for everybody, whether you want something that doubles as a wallet, something luxurious, or something that's barely there at all.

Our top recommendation is the Samsung Alcantara case, which is a very unique and fancy case that was admittedly a hard sell to make at the full price, but harder to pass up at these discounted prices.

All of these cases would be great for your Galaxy S9+, but my personal favorite would have to be the Spigen Tough Armor. I've enjoyed used Spigen cases for years, and you can be confident that each Spigen case will fit your phone perfectly.
