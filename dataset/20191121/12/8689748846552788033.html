Never in my life will I invest in a Ghanaian artiste – Popular artiste manager vows

21 Nov 2019, 12:13 by Adu Sarpeah

It seems the negative virtue called ungratefulness can be found in the genes of 8 out of 10 Africans assembled especially Ghanaian musicians.

This is because there have been many instances where budding artistes have had a scuffle with their managers after getting the needed recognition, the story of Kelvyn Boy and Stonebwoy as the two have dominated headlines lately for the wrong reasons.

Back to the subject matter, Ayisha Modi who has worked with several artistes including Ofori Amponsah, Kwesi Slay, Obrafour, Castro and Co. has added her voice to artistes being ingrate to their helpers.

She vowed never in her life to invest her money in any artiste (both uprising and A-listed artistes) going forward perhaps because of past experience and the recent happenings.

“And I am one of those ppl, I will never invest my money into any Artist again in my life. Never,” Ayisha commented under a post on Facebook.

She vowed never in her life to invest her money in any artiste (both uprising and A listed artistes) again.
