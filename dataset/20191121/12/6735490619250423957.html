Selena Gomez Reveals The Release Date For Her Next Album

21 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Corey Atad

Mark your calendars, Selena Gomez fans!

On Thursday, the 27-year-old singer revealed on her official website that her next album will be out on Jan. 10, 2020.

Gomez teased the announcement the night before in a post on Instagram that promised “something exciting.”

The album, tentatively titled SG2, will feature 13 tracks, and is now available for pre-order.

Gomez has released two singles from the album, “Lose You To Love Me” and “Look at Her Now”.
