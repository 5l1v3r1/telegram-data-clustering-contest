(Video) First glimpse of Liverpool’s new training ground looks potentially stunning

21 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Biscan's Boots

Liverpool have a released a video showing what’s been done so far at the new Kirkby training base, which is set to be completed at some point next year.

Work is going along very nicely on the facility which will see us combine the first-team and the Academy instead of having them separate which is what happens currently with Melwood restricted to senior pros.

In the video, you’ll see the ground being initially broken by Jurgen Klopp and co. and the three full-sized pitches which have been carved out already!

This club really is going places and as fans, it’s wonderful to be part of the journey.
