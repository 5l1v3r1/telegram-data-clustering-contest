Here's where you can watch A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving this year

21 Nov 2019, 12:35 by WFTS Digital Staff

With "It's the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown" in the rear view, it's time to get ready for the next classic Peanuts holiday episode, "A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving."

In this beloved holiday special, Charlie Brown finds himself in a bit of a bind when Peppermint Patty invites herself and a few friends over for Thanksgiving dinner. With Snoopy and Woodstock pulling kitchen duty, a traditional turkey and stuffing meal is simply not in the cards. Will good old Charlie Brown survive the holiday and the wrath of his hungry dinner guests?
