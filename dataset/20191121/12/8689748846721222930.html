Manifest Gets His First Ever Credit On Grammys For His Contribution On Burna Boy’s Album

21 Nov 2019, 12:25 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian rapper, singer and songwriter, Manifest has received his first ever Grammy’s credit.

Manifest who won Best Rapper and Hip-Hop song of the year at the 2017 Ghana Music Awards, has been credited for his appearance on Nigeria’s Burna Boy’s “African Giant” album.

Burna Boy’s 19- track album was listed in the “Best World Music Album” category alongside “Celia” by sensational singer Angelique Kidjo, amongst others.

This clearly indicates that Manifest automatically earns a credit for his contribution to the album.

This is also a big win for Ghana as Shatta Wale and Manifest were the only two artists to be credited for the Grammys.

Maifest has worked with Damon Albarn, Flea, Tony Allen, Erykah Badu, and is featured on five songs on the Rocket Juice and The Moon album.
