Holly Willoughby baffled as Kem Cetinay says he thought reindeers were 'made up'

Love Island star Kem Cetinay was left stunned when he met the animals - which he previously thought were just in fairy tales

21 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Irishmirror.ie

Holly Willoughby was left very confused on This Morning today after former Love Island star Kem Cetinay said that he thought reindeers weren't real.

Today, Kem ventured to the places in Norway that inspired Disney's Frozen ahead of the second film's release at the weekend.

But the former Islander was stunned when he came face to face with a reindeer - which he had been convinced didn't exist.

Meeting a woman called Ava, who looked after reindeers like Sven in Frozen, he went to see some in real life.

But as he entered the pen, Kem quipped: "I didn't think reindeers were real, I'll be honest with you. I thought they were made up!"

He also feared that the reindeers would bite him.

After the VT, Phil said: "He is so funny."

Holly added in disbelief: "He didn't think reindeers were real. Kem!"

Earlier, viewers were left thoroughly creeped out by Sophia the Robot's sinister smile over terrified Holly Willoughby's remark.

Sophia the Robot, hailed as the world's most intelligent robot, appeared on the sofa alongside her creator David Hanson.

However hosts Holly and Phil were clearly unsettled by the robot's appearance, Phil pointed out that there is a "potential sinister side", to which the creator agreed but said that there was potential for good as well.

Sophia rattled off her accomplishments, which included visiting 38 countries, and even drew a picture of Holly and Phil.

But viewers noticed Sophia's creepy smile after Holly asked whether Sophia could turn on them if she got smarter.

One tweeted: "She's f***ing creepy that's what she is #notorobots #ThisMorning."

Another wrote: "THAT IS SO CREEPY #ThisMorning."
