PM Imran felicitates Sri Lankan counterpart over appointment

21 Nov 2019, 12:57 by News Desk

Prime Minister Imran Khan felicitated on Thursday his Sri Lankan counterpart Mahinda Rajapaksa on his appointment as prime minister of the democratic socialist republic.

The prime minister lauded the progress made by Sri Lanka during Rajapaksa’s two terms as president, said a statement by Pakistan’s Foreign Office.

Earlier this week, newly-elected Sri Lankan President Gotabaya Rajapaksa named his brother, Mahinda, as prime minister, cementing the grip on power of a clan credited with crushing the Tamil Tigers 10 years ago.

PM Imran expressed the desire to work closely with the Sri Lankan counterpart to “further strengthen Pakistan-Sri Lanka bilateral relations in all dimensions”.
