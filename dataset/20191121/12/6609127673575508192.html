KID PANIC

Frantic search launched after two young brothers, 9 and 6, vanish from Midlothian school

21 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Blair Meikle

A FRANTIC police search was launched this morning after two young brothers vanished after being dropped off at school in Midlothian.

Michael O'Brien, 9, and brother Jamie, 6, have been traced safe and well after going missing from school in Rosewell at around 8am.

They failed to enter the school and cops were alerted.

Officers have launched a frantic search to locate them, and deployed sniffer dog units.

But they have now confirmed Jamie and Michael were found safe and well.

A spokesperson for the force said: "We are pleased to report Michael and Jamie O'Brien, missing from Rosewell this morning, have been traced safe and well.

"We would like to thank members of the public and our partners who shared information and assisted us with inquiries."

 

We told earlier how a pensioner has been missing overnight in Dumfries and Galloway as fears grow for his safety.

Thomas McKee, 71, was last seen at about 2.30pm yesterday on Main Street, Newton Stewart.

He has not been heard from since and his whereabouts are unknown.

Thomas' family and friends are becoming increasingly concerned for his safety.
