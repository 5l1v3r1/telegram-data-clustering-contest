Jessie J wraps up for London winter as Channing Tatum's divorce is finalised

21 Nov 2019, 12:09

Jessie J has been spotted wrapping up warm for the winter as her boyfriend Channing Tatum’s divorce from Jenna Dewan is finalised.

The Price Tag singer, who has been dating the actor since October, was seen smiling away as she attended the press night for the & Juliet musical.

Battling the freezing London temperatures (we feel you, girl), she looked stunning in a huge black coat and navy suit, rocking her hair up in a bun.

The 31-year-old looked in high spirits as she snapped photos with fans, before being escorted by security.

Meanwhile, Channing and Jenna’s divorce has been finalised almost two years after their split.

The former couple, who married in 2009 three years after meeting on the set of Step Up, announced their split in April 2018.

It’s now reported that a judge has granted their request to legally separate while they discuss custody agreements and a property settlement.

Channing and Jenna filed papers asking for the divorce so that they have the option to settle down with their new partners.

According to TMZ, documents filed by Jenna’s lawyer Samantha Spector indicate the divorce judgement was announced on Tuesday.

Although Jessie and Channing kept the romance lowkey at first, they’ve since been enjoying a number of adorable dates, cosying up to each other in Santa Monica, California.

Oh, you guys.

However, Jenna previously admitted she was ‘blindsided’ by her ex’s new relationship.

According to Radar Online, the 38-year-old wrote in her new memoir: ‘There I was, on a plane, alone, finding out about his new relationship. I felt blindsided.’

The mother-of-one later revealed how she was going to react to the news via Twitter, but decided not to share her string of messages.

‘Instead of reacting the way I wanted… I asked myself this: How do I choose grace in this moment?’ she explained.

Yet, there is no bad blood between both Jessie and Jenna, as the Step Up star threw her support behind the singer when fans compared the two women, pitting them against each other.

It’s happy endings all round.
