Spoilers: Fiz and Tyrone lose their kids as evil Jade strikes in Corrie

21 Nov 2019, 12:45

There is a devastating start to 2020 for Coronation Street couple Fiz Stape (Jennie McAlpine) and Tyrone Dobbs (Alan Halsall) when sinister nanny and tutor Jade Rowan (Lottie Henshall) executes an evil plot to have their children Hope and Ruby taken away from them.

Ever since Jade arrived to support troubled Hope’s education, viewers have been wary that there is something not right with her and it has become clear that she has a vendetta against the family as she secretly plots againt them.

And in an upsetting turn of events, she will manage to paint the loving parents as unsuitable to take care of the kids, leading to social services becoming involved. As Fiz and Tyrone face losing their kids, they have a fight on their hands as wicked Jade steps up her campaign against her bosses. But just what is her grudge and how far is she prepared to go in order to destroy them?
