Home sales report: 73% of costliest US zip codes are in California

21 Nov 2019, 23:48

A newly published report shows that 73% of the costliest U.S. neighborhoods in which to own a home are in California.

Property Shark analyzed residential sales this year for condominiums, co-ops, single-family and two-family homes.

Out of 125 ranked zip codes, 91 were in California.

The Bay Area town of Atherton, near Palo Alto, is first overall with a $7 million median sales price.

In Los Angeles County, Santa Monica's 90402 zip code is the third overall priciest place to buy a home in the U.S.

Beverly Hills, 90210, comes in at No. 4. And Pacific Palisades, 90272, is No. 29.
