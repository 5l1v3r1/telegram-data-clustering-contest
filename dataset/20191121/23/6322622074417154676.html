CGS-CIMB Research raises target price for Kossan to RM5.21

21 Nov 2019, 23:59 by theedgemarkets.com

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 22): CGS-CIMB Research has maintained its “Add” rating on Kossan Rubber Industries Bhd at RM4.19 with a higher target price (TP) of RM5.21 (from RM5.07) and said Kossan’s 9M19 core net profit of RM163.8 million came in broadly in-line at 72% of house fullyear estimate.

In a note Nov 21, the research house said it expects a stronger 4Q19 result from higher glove sales.

“3Q19 net profit waned 9.2% year-on-year and 12% quarter-on-quarter from start-up losses of its newly-commissioned Plant 18 and impact of labour shortages in the quarter.

“Maintain Add, with a higher TP of RM5.21 (25x CY21F P/E),” it said.
