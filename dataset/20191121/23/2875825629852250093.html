I'm A Celeb's Caitlyn Jenner learns iconic Girls Aloud dance as she performs with Nadine Coyle

Caitlyn Jenner gave the UK exactly what they wanted as she danced to a classic Girls Aloud track with other stars on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! tonight

21 Nov 2019, 23:25 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity star Caitlyn Jenner was taught all she needs to know about former girl band Girls Aloud in tonight's episode.

During another day in the camp, Jacqueline started to dance to The Promise by the iconic former pop group.

Nadine taught her some more dance moves and Adele joined in.

Watching the girls have fun, Caitlyn asked if Nadine had danced on stage and Nadine said: "Yes," before Caitlyn smiled and said: "I love that."

Caitlyn, Nadine, Adele and Jacqueline all stood up to form their own girl band and Nadine showed the girls some epic moves.

Talking in the VT, Nadine said: "I taught the girls a little bit of The Promise routine, Jacqueline and Adele knew most of it already."

Caitlyn was having the time of her life and enjoyed dancing to The Promise as Nadine sang the lyrics.

Jacqueline said: "I just love Caitlyn Jenner, she just throws herself into everything, she doesn't even know the songs."

All the camp cheered as the girls finished the dance routine, while Nadine joked: "We're ready for the stage, send us in."

An over excited Adele said: "I've just danced to The Promise with Nadine Coyle, Caitlyn Jenner and Jacqueline Jossa!"

Nadine proudly announced: "Caitlyn just threw herself into it, we'll teach her Sound of the Underground next."
