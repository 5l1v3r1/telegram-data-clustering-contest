Listen: Tasmina Ahmed-Sheikh on ‘Sacch’ being first Pakistani film to be shot in Pakistan

21 Nov 2019, 23:05 by Lakh Baddhan

Scottish politician and former UK Parliamentarian, turned film producer Tasmina Ahmed-Sheikh OBE, spoke to BizAsia’s Raj Baddhan about her upcoming production ‘Sacch’, billed as the first Pakistani film to be entirely shot in Scotland.

In the interaction, Ahmed-Sheikh opened up about the shoot of the film in Scotland, its story and how the project came together after over two years of planning.
