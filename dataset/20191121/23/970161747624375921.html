Freida Pinto is engaged to her photographer boyfriend after two years of dating

21 Nov 2019, 23:26

Indian actress Freida Pinto is engaged to her photographer boyfriend Cory Tran after two years of dating.

The photogenic couple shared the news on Instagram with professional shots of the two, with Freida showing off the luxurious ring (we are not jealous at all).

Posting a carousel of snaps announcing their engagement, the 35-year-old star wrote: ‘It all makes sense now. Life makes sense, the world makes sense, the past tears and trials make sense, what wise old lovers said about love makes sense, where I am makes sense and where I want to go completely makes sense.

‘You my love are just the most beautiful creation to have ever walked into my life. And you are here to stay. Well, I am making you stay. Ha!

‘All my love with all my heart. Oh and Happiest Birthday sweet Fiancé!’

What a way to announce your engagement.

Meanwhile, Cory kept his post short and sweet, writing: ‘The best birthday present I could’ve asked for. Fiancé.’

The Slumdog Millionaire actress was previously dating her co-star Dev Patel, 28, after the release of the movie in 2009 to 2014.

Freida and Cory weren’t spotted together until October 2017 and went public with their romance at the US Open a year later.

The star took her beau to India and Nepal earlier this year, showing him around the local areas.

She showed him where she grew up in Mumbai and even met her parents for the first time ever.
