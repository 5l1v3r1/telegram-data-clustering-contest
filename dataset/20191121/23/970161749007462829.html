RuPaul's Drag Race star Michelle Visage insists 'feud' with Cheryl was 'banter'

21 Nov 2019, 23:59 by Cydney Yeates

RuPaul’s Drag Race star Michelle Visage has shut down speculations that her and Cheryl ‘hate’ each other.

No judge is as honest as Michelle, but when Cheryl was invited as a guest, all we could see was smoke coming out of her ears.

However the Strictly Come Dancing contestant set the record straight, revealing that they were just ‘giving them some telly’.

The 51-year-old spilled: ‘I love Cheryl. It’s called – I know this is going to be really far fetched – banter.

‘We’re doing a TV show about drag queens, so we got to give them some telly. Cheryl and I’ve known each other for years, we love each other.

‘She gave it back and when you give it back, you’re gonna get it.’

Well, there you have it, folks. They weren’t going head-to-head on the judges’ panel and they actually really love each other.

Maybe they should try acting instead because they certainly fooled us.

The pair had a disagreement on the panel over Baga Chipz’s efforts in the main challenge from episode six, where the queens were asked to brand their own water bottle and make an ad for it.

Sticking with the ol’ ‘down the chippy’ thought process, Baga opted for a ‘Chippy Tea Water’, which was meant to taste and fill you up like a fish and chip supper would’ve done.

After watching the ad, which saw Baga sprawled across two underwear-clad models on a park bench while shoving battered sausages in her mouth (we’ve all been there), Michelle admitted that she didn’t particularly buy what Baga was selling.

‘Really not sure about the water concept and what you were selling, I think we had more fun watching you put stuff in your mouth!’ Michelle said.

When Baga tried to explain her concept, she added: ‘Hear from the other judges, maybe it’s just me.’

Cheryl then spoke up in defence of fellow northerner Baga and said: ‘I think it’s just you.’
