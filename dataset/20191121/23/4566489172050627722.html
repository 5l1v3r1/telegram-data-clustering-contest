Review: Adam Lambert delivers magical performance of 'Closer to You' 

International pop star Adam Lambert performed "Closer to You" on "The Late Late Show with James Corden" on CBS and it was simply magical.

21 Nov 2019, 23:51 by Markos Papadatos

Lambert was able to dazzle the Stage 56 audience with a superb and expression live version of "Closer to You" on The Late Late Show with James Corden. He embodies the song's lyrics and was able to convey a wide spectrum of emotions.
