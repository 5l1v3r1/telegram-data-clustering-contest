Ant and Dec joke Stephen Mulhern will 'present anything' after BGT spin-off taken off air

The cheeky I'm A Celebrity presenters joked that Stephen Mulhern should front a 'cheap, one dimensional and very low rent' gameshow based on the campmates' game 'Quick Draw'

21 Nov 2019, 23:46 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin and Decland Donnelly want Stephen Mulhern to present a new gameshow based on the I'm A Celebrity Campmates new game.

At the start of Thursday night's show, James Haskell invented 'Quick Draw', which entertained the camp for a good few minutes.

He explained: "Where Caitlyn or an independent adjudicator would drop a hat, once the hat touches the floor, you reach for your water bottle, get it and drink it.”

After watching Ian Wright become the Sheriff and win the competition, which gave Ant and Dec a good idea.

The cheeky Geordie presenters joked that Stephen should front a "cheap, one dimensional and very low rent" gameshow based on the campmates' game.

Dec joked: "Don’t miss Stephen Mulhern’s quick draw next Saturday only on ITV."

Giggling by his side, Ant added: "He’ll present anything."

Turning to face the camera, Dec cheekily said: "Hi Stevie."

Later on in the show, Caitlyn Jenner started talking about life with her famous family.

Dec joked that the Kardashians should get their own reality TV show.

Ant said: "We've got Stephen Mulhern's Quick Draw and a reality show for the Kardashians."

Praising themselves, Dec added: "We're on fire tonight."

Could they have found the perfect new job for Stephen after Britain's Got More Talent was taken off air?

* I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! airs on ITV at 9pm
