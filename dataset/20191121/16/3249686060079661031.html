ATEEZ Amazes Fans with Their Natural Look on Naver's NOW

21 Nov 2019, 16:38 by stigmata

On November 20, ATEEZ appeared as hosts on Naver's live studio 'NOW's 'School Road' segment. The live broadcast which started at 8 A.M. (KST). In addition to sincere conversations, members sang songs of their choice.

Meanwhile members' photos that were taken during the live streaming broadcast drew a lot of attention. The biggest reason was because they were all makeup free and still had stunning visuals! The group has proved that their visuals are above the standards.
