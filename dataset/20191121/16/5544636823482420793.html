Criminals Have Hijacked Nigeria- Tuface Claims

21 Nov 2019, 16:02 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Legendary singer, Innocent Idibia better known as Tuface has bemoaned the leadership structure in Nigeria, Concise News understands.

According to Tuface, the state of the country has become worst in the hands of the present administration.

In a tweet, the singer wrote “Naija system is a total joke, criminals have hijacked Nigeria.

“Men and women that have dedicated their time to make sure that Nigeria will carry last. No shame, no dignity, no honour.

He further expressed hope that younger generation will put in their best to foster the growth of the country

“I pray the young men that have entered the system will do their best to influence positive change,”  Tuface said.
