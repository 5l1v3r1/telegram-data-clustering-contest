‘Days Of Our Lives’ Renewed For 56th Season After Entire Cast Was Released From Contracts

21 Nov 2019, 16:41 by Aynslee Darmon

Fans were worried about the fate of the beloved soap opera “Days Of Our Lives” after a report from TV Line suggested producers of the long-running series had released the entire cast from their contracts.

Yet no worries were needed as Deadline confirmed that NBC has renewed the series for its 56th season.

The cost-cutting move by releasing the cast from their contracts came as NBC and “Days” distributor Sony Pictures TV were in negotiations to renew the show which typically happens at the start of the year. However, with the backlash on Twitter, negotiations were likely sped up.

There is no report on the renegotiation of cast contracts but one actor previously told The Wrap that if the show does resume production in March, the entire cast will receive a sizeable pay cut.
