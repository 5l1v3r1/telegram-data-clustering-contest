Lakeview advisory ends

21 Nov 2019, 16:49 by Sarita Patel

The City of West Kelowna has cancelled a water quality advisory for the Lakeview water system.

Working in conjunction with Interior Health, the city has also concluded water main maintenance activities for the year.

No water quality advisories are in effect in West Kelowna’s systems at this time.

The city has stopped free access at the bulk filling station at Shannon Lake and Asquith roads.
