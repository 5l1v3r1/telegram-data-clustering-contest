If your wardrobe is lacking some cold weather essentials or could benefit with a few trend pieces, there’s no better time to update your wardrobe than Black Friday. Whether you’re in the market for timeless classic chunky knitwear or want to tap into current trends from cord workwear jackets to bum bags, you’re sure to bag a bargain in the ASOS sale, with prices last year slashed to less than half price in some cases.

While you may not be hanging up your stocking just yet, it might be worth considering your Christmas party options too, as occasion wear tends to be reduced in the ASOS Black Friday sale.

Winter warmers such as coats and jumpers are also Black Friday staples, so there’s no better time to stock up on clothes to keep you feeling cosy and looking great. With the money you save, you’ll be able to add a woolly hat to your basket too.
