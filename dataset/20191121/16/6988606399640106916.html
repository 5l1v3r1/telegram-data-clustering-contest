Go ahead given to demolish Newry Road creche in order to build new house - Talk of the Town

21 Nov 2019, 16:49 by TOTT Editor

The go ahead has been given to demolish a creche on the Newry Road and replace it with a new two bed house.

Christine Gallagher was given conditional planning permission in September 2018 to change the use, alterations and associated site development works at 1, 2 & 3 Seaview Terrace from a creche into a two bedroom single storey dwelling house with attic store.

Now she has been given similar conditional planning permission to demolish the creche and in its place construct a new two bedroom, single storey dwelling house with attic store and associated site development works.
