Bluetooth speakers sold at Costco, Best Buy and Amazon recalled over impact hazard

21 Nov 2019, 16:53 by WFTS Digital Staff

Grace Digital is recalling thousands of ECOXGEAR EcoBoulder Bluetooth Speakers over an impact hazard because the battery can overcharge and burst.

According to the recall, there have been five reports of speaker batteries bursting. That includes two reports of property damage to the surrounding area. There have not been any injuries reported.
