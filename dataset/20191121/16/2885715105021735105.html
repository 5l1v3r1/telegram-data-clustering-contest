Punjabi singer Elly Mangat booked as video of him firing bullets in air goes viral

In a seven-second video posted on social media network Instagram, shared by another guest at the party, Mangat is seen firing twice with a weapon and again with a rifle.

21 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Express News Service

The Ludhiana Police Thursday lodged an FIR against Punjabi singer Elly Mangat for allegedly firing celebratory bullets in the air at an event in Dharaur village.

The incident took place at a birthday party of one Bhupinder Singh on the night of November 19, where Mangat opened fire in the air twice, using a 12 bore licensed rifle of Gurbant Singh, father of Bhupinder Singh, said the police. The police claimed that Bhupinder, who arranged the party, made an attempt to destroy the rifle but failed to do so.
