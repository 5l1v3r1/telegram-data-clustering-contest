Now, scroll down to the bottom of the page where you can choose to pay for your device in full. From the new list of tariffs that appears, choose the 1GB on for £21 per month.

Now that’s out of the way, you just have to speak to O2 to cancel your contract within the 14-day cooling-off period and the iPhone XR will be yours for just £329. From there, you can choose to pick up another better SIM-only deal or stick with your existing contract. Easy!
