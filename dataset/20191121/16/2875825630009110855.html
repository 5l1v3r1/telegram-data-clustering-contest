Rosanna Davison baby birth: First pictures as model and husband Wes Quirke welcome 'perfect' daughter Sophia Rose

In July the Dublin-born model announced she’s expecting her first child via gestational surrogate

21 Nov 2019, 16:03 by Katie Gallagher

Rosanna Davison and husband Wes Quirke told of their love and gratitude as they welcomed their "perfect" daughter Sophia today.

In July the Dublin-born model announced she was expecting her first child via gestational surrogate and bravely opened up about suffering several miscarriages in the past.

The delighted couple were flooded with messages of congratulations after Rosanna announced their daughter's birth earlier today.

Sharing images on her Instagram account, she told her 176,000 followers: "And the dreams that you dare to dream really do come true!

"Today, November 21st 2019 at 11.57am, our beautiful and healthy daughter Sophia Rose Quirke arrived safely into the world weighing 6lbs8oz...She’s absolutely perfect.

"We’re completely overwhelmed with love and gratitude for our incredible gestational surrogate, who has given us the greatest gift of all and made our family dream a reality."
