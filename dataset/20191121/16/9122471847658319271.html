Injury Update For Patriots WR Phillip Dorsett

21 Nov 2019, 16:13 by Tzvi Machlin

The New England Patriots are preparing for a big showdown with the Dallas Cowboys this weekend. Unfortunately, it looks like there’s a chance they’ll be without one of their best receivers: WR Phillip Dorsett.

According to Jeff Howe of The Athletic, Dorsett missed practice on Wednesday due to a concussion. Howe noted that despite missing practice there is still optimism that he will play on Sunday.

However, Howe also noted that there is no “straight line to recovery.”

Dorsett suffered the injury in New England’s win over the Philadelphia Eagles this past week. He had caught three passes for 33 yards and a touchdown prior to the injury.
