Some Of Davido’s Outstanding Achievements In 2019

21 Nov 2019, 16:55 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Year 2019 has obviously been a good year for Nigerian singer Davido as he keeps earning several feats for himself and his career.

Davido has over time carved a niche for himself in the showbiz and has been the favourite of many afrobeat lovers.

As the singer clocks 27 on November 21, this platform takes time to examine some of his achievements.

  • Davido bagged award in Crossing Boundaries Music category of African Muzik Magazine Awards (AFRIMMA) im October.
  • The 30 Billion gang crooner has attained an impressive position of being the seventh on the list of most influential African people in the world.
  • He made a record on YouTube, as his popular single, ‘Fall’ hits 150 million views on YouTube. This feat is said to be a milestone for afrobeats as Davido’s ‘Fall’ becomes the first afrobeat record to achieve such number of views.
  • Davido set another record for himself as he becomes the first African celebrity to hit one million likes on a post on Instagram after he shared a photo of himself and his newborn, Adedeji David, whom he welcomed in the early hours of Sunday, October 20.
  • He was ranked the richest Nigerian on Instagram, with his single post valued at over N26 million. The Instagram Rich List 2019 by Hopper HQ 2019 also reveals that Davido stands at second position in Africa and occupies 38 positions on global rating.
  • He also earned some accolades for himself as he became the most viewed Nigerian artist on streaming platform, YouTube. Davido had over 500 million views across all his uploads on the platform.
  • The ‘Aye’ crooner also made history by becoming the first Nigerian artiste to hit 13 million followers on social media network, Instagram.
  • Davido finally proposed to his longtime girlfriend, ChiomaRowland in September.
  • The DMW boss welcomed his first male child, David junior on October 20.
