Half-Life: Alyx as Long as Half-Life 2, “Couldn’t Possibly” Work with Mouse and Keyboard

21 Nov 2019, 16:56 by Nathan Birch

Earlier today Valve revealed Half-Life: Alyx, their long-awaited VR-exclusive revival of the beloved series. The trailer looked fairly impressive, but what kind of scope can we expect from the game? Is this basically another full Half-Life or more of a glorified tech demo? Well, according to The Game Awards head honcho Geoff Keighley, we can expect a full 15-hour adventure from Half-Life: Alyx.
