Superstar André Rieu returns to Liverpool in 2020

'The King of the Waltz' will be bringing his acclaimed Johann Stauss Orchestra back to the M&S Bank Arena on May 8, 2020

21 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Tom Bramwell

Dubbed the Maestro of the Masses by the New York Times and The King of Crossover by the Wall Street Journal, André Rieu returns to Liverpool's M&S Bank Arena on May 8, 2020 for a magical evening of music and dance with his 70-piece Johann Strauss Orchestra. It’s the perfect event for the whole family!

With over 40 million album sales worldwide, André Rieu is one of the biggest music stars on the planet. His extraordinary performances include toe-tapping waltzes as well as some of the best-loved melodies from the worlds of film, opera and musicals.

André Rieu firmly believes that classical music is made for everybody to enjoy, not just for an elite. He makes beautiful classical music so much fun. At his concerts you can see his devoted audiences of all generations laughing, weeping, singing and dancing in the aisles.

Rieu established his Johann Strauss Orchestra in 1987 with just 12 members. Today it is the biggest privately-owned orchestra in the world. Each year they play for over 600,000 people around the world.

André Rieu also performed at the 1994, 2009 and 2019 Champions League Final soccer matches. His violin dates back to 1732. Rieu has reached 30 number 1 chart positions and won 500 platinum awards.

In May, he will bring his arena-sized show to Liverpool for one night only. Don’t miss your chance to see André Rieu live. Bring your family and get ready for an unforgettable evening full of laughter, dance and romance!

Tickets and travel packages are available now at www.andrerieu.com.
