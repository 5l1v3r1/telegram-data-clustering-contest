KBR awarded HCl purification contract by Italmatch

21 Nov 2019, 16:39 by Mary Page Bailey

KBR (Houston; www.kbr.com) has been awarded a contract for its PLINKE Hydrochloric Acid (HCl) Purification technologies by Italmatch Deutschland GmbH for its phosphorous acid production plant in Frankfurt, Germany.

Under the terms of the contract, KBR will provide technology license, basic and detailed engineering, proprietary equipment and related advisory services to Italmatch to help with the production of phosphorous acid as well as absorption and recovery of hydrochloric acid.

“We are proud to be selected as Italmatch’s technology partner for its new plant and contribute to Italmatch’s continued growth,” said Doug Kelly, KBR President, Technology Solutions. “KBR has designed, built, supplied and supported tailored plants and facilities for purifying and concentrating strong inorganic acids and metal salts for customers all over the world.”
