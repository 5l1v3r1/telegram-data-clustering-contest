Football transfer news: Erling Haaland release clause revealed

21 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Archie Blade

Football transfer news: Following his form in the Austrian and Champions League, Erling Haaland has become a target for many top clubs in Europe.

Earling Haaland was a name that not many had heard a few months before. However, come November, he has been linked with almost every major club across Europe. Such has been his dominance this season, that clubs have now lined up for his signatures.

Under normal circumstances, this would result in the price of the player skyrocketing. Just as it did for the likes of Joao Felix, Ousmane Demebele, Kylian Mbappe and several youngsters who were sold for blindingly high prices. However, with Erling Haaland, the clubs can breathe a sigh of relief. That is because his contract with Red Bull Salzburg contains a buyout clause; One that many top clubs would consider quite affordable.

Erling Haaland release clause revealed

German newspaper Sport Bild have reported that Erling Haaland can be bought out from his contract at Red Bull Salzburg for 30 million Euros. While some may consider the price a steal, German Transfermarkt believes that this is the appropriate amount one should dish out on him.

Haaland started the season with a market value of 12 million Euros. However, he has risen to 30 million by November. The list is normally updated on a semi-annual basis but a first-time exception was made for the Norwegian.

His 7 goals in the Champions League so far and 26 goals in 18 matches in all competitions this season may have something to do with it.
