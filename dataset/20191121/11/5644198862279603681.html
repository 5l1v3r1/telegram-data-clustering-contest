Micron relationship with Huawei 'very good', chief executive says

21 Nov 2019, 11:30

REUTERS: American memory chipmaker Micron's relationship with China's tech giant Huawei 'continues to be very good', its chief executive, Sanjay Mehrotra, said on Thursday.

He was speaking to broadcaster CNN on the sidelines of the Bloomberg New Economy Forum in Beijing.
