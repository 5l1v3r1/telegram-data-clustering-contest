EXCLUSIVE: Michael Oeming's Folklords #2 Variant Revealed By BOOM! Studios

21 Nov 2019, 11:11 by L.D. Nolan

BOOM! Studios has given CBR an exclusive first look at Folklords #2, by Matt Kindt and Matt Smith. The unlettered preview shows Ansel entering a forest on his quest to learn more about the titular Folklords, though doing so is punishable by death in his village. The preview also seems to give some indication that the issue will further flesh out the backstories of its main characters.

You can see CBR's exclusive preview of Folklords #2 below alongside Matt Smith and Michael Avon Oeming's covers below.

FOLKLORDS #2

  • Written by Matt Kindt
  • Art by Matt Smith
  • Cover by Matt Smith
  • Variant Cover by Michael Avon Oeming
  • Against the orders of his village, Ansel strikes out to find the Folklords, the only beings that can explain his visions of another world. But the forest is even more dark and terrible than he was told, and Ansel meets two very different women who will change his life—one way or another—forever.
  • On sale Dec. 18

Folklords #2, b Matt Kindt and Matt Smith, releases December 18 from BOOM! Studios.
