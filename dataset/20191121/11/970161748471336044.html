Scarlett Moffatt shares I’m A Celeb’s Adele Roberts’ love for Jane McDonald

21 Nov 2019, 11:39

I’m A Celebrity star Adele Roberts isn’t alone in her love for Jane McDonald, as Scarlett Moffatt admits she’s also fascinated by the Loose Women star.

Former Big Brother star Adele has kept us entertained this week when she revealed herself to be Jane’s number one fan, after choosing a photo of the TV presenter as her luxury item in the Australian jungle.

While the likes of Roman Kemp have struggled to understand Adele’s fandom for the Cruising star, Scarlett is right there with her.

Speaking to Metro.co.uk in celebration of the National Lottery’s 25th anniversary, Scarlett explained: ‘I have said always for the past three years that my ideal candidate would be Jane McDonald.

‘I feel like subconsciously I’ve made that happen.’

Scarlett, 29, won I’m A Celebrity in 2016 and would have loved to have Jane as her campmate.

She added: ‘Joel [Dommett] in my year, he took a framed photo of Ant & Dec in but most people take in something they can actually use.

‘But if Jane McDonald gets her through the hard times then who are we to judge?’

Rightly so, Scarlett.

The former I’m A Celebrity Extra Camp host has been absolutely gripped by this year’s series and is already making big predictions for the final.

‘I honestly feel like it’s the best line-up yet. Caitlyn is amazing. I could watch her all day,’ she said.

‘Jacqueline I love, I feel like she’s going to win. I know that’s a bold statement because it’s very early on. Roman, I feel like he’s definitely in the final.’

This year’s campmates seem to be mostly getting along fine in the jungle and Scarlett doubts anyone will seriously lose their cool.

She predicted: ‘I feel like it’s going to be a harmonious camp this year. I feel like we’re never going to have a Lady C moment.

‘Even though it’s really entertaining, it’s nice watching people get on as well because the world is a bit crap but, maybe there’s never going to be a Lady C season. Maybe everyone’s just going to get on.’

I’m A Celebrity airs nightly on ITV at 9pm.
