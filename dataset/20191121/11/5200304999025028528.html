ZEE5 premieres its latest Telugu Original ‘Hawala’

21 Nov 2019, 11:59 by Raj Baddhan

With a series of memorable Telugu Originals like ‘Mrs. Subbalakshmi’ and ‘Kailasapuram’ having been launched in recent months, ZEE5 is all set to add another masterpiece to the list with the global premiere of Telugu Original, ‘Hawala’. The six-episode suspense thriller web series releases today on ZEE5 globally, along with the Hindi dubbed version of the series.

Starring Gourish Yeleti, Anusha, Tarun Rohith and Jayasree Kshatriya, ‘Hawala’ combines gambling and cricket in an unsavoury tale of deceit, revolving around the ‘hawala’, a note commonly used during a betting exchange. The story follows two couples whose chase for fast money, constantly lands them into unexpected situations.
