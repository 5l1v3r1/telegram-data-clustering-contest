Legacies: Landon and Josie Confront Professor Vardemus in New Photos

21 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Sam Stone

Professor Vardemus and his less-than-altruistic agenda begin to surface in new official stills from Legacies Season 2.

The CW has released photos from the season's seventh episode, ominously titled "It Will All Be Painfully Clear Soon Enough," that show Landon and his girlfriend Josie confronting the Salvatore Boarding School for the Young & Gifted's new headmaster. Meanwhile, Josie and her fraternal twin sister Lizzie commiserate with Hope as she contemplates leaving town.

It Will All Be Painfully Clear Soon Enough (Ep# 207)THE RACE TO CLOSE THE MALIVORE PORTAL -- After deciding that leaving town would be for the best, Hope (Danielle Rose Russell) gets pulled back in after Josie (Kaylee Bryant) comes up with a risky plan to close the Malivore portal. Elsewhere, Alaric (Matthew Davis) and Dorian (guest star Demetrius Bridges) make an alarming discovery about Professor Vardemus (guest star Alexis Denisof), while Rafael (Peyton Alex Smith) learns some upsetting news about Landon's (Aria Shahghasemi) future. Jenny Boyd also stars. Lauren Petzke directed the episode written by Jimmy Mosqueda & Cynthia Adarkwa (#207). Original airdate 12/5/2019.
