Vodafone are giving away Apple Airpods and PlayStation 4s this Black Friday

The network are offering free gifts as part of its bundle deals

21 Nov 2019, 11:28 by Jonathan Yates

Vodafone are offering free gifts this Black Friday.

Customers who purchase a monthly contract with Vodafone can get their hands on a PlayStation 4, Apple AirPods or a Hauwei watch for free.

When purchasing the iPhone 11 with Vodafone for £60 a month you'll receive free AirPods which are typically priced at £159.

One of the best deals which is likely to be popular this Christmas is a free FIFA 20 PS4 bundle when purchasing the Sony Xperia 5 phone for £56 a month.

Ahead of the day of deals on November 29 a number of phone companies have started early with discounts on contracts and SIM-only phones.

Competitors including Three Mobile have launched deals with Apple iPhone 11 contracts for less than £50 a month and a 24 months unlimited SIM from £20 a month.

VOXI are offering SIM-only contracts with data used on apps like Whatsapp and Instagram not counting towards monthly allowances as part of their 'endless' social media usage offer.
