I'm A Celeb's Myles 'willing to tell Jacqueline everything' about Dan and Gabby

The singer will reportedly speak to Jacqueline about what happened between her husband Dan Osborne and his ex Gabby Allen after they were alleged to have had an affair

21 Nov 2019, 11:40 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity camp mates Jacqueline Jossa and Myles Stephenson will reportedly sit down for a chat about what really happened between her husband and his ex.

Myles dated Love Island star Gabby Allen for just under a year, calling it quits in August.

Soap star Jacqueline tied the knot with Dan Osborne back in 2017 - but in April the year after, he was spotted enjoying a getaway in Marbella with his CelebrityBig Brother co-star Gabby.

Both parties denied it, with Jacqueline even bluntly texting Gabby: "Did you sleep with my husband?"

Now it's thought that Myles and Jacqueline will have a talk in camp - and he'll be willing to spill the beans on what Gabby told him.

A source told The Sun : "Gabby and Myles discussed the whole Dan situation during their relationship, and even though both Dan and Gabby always denied anything went on between them, there are a lot of things that Jacqueline might not know.

"Myles would be willing to tell Jacqueline everything - and answer any questions she might have.

"Now the two camps are all back together, there will be a lot of chats around the fire about everyone's lives. And Myles will tell Jacqueline everything Gabby told him.

"It's a good thing Gabby's gone to Thailand for a month because she might find it uncomfortable watching."

Jacqueline and Dan have had some ups and downs in their relationship, with Dan telling new! magazine earlier this year that their marriage was plagued by a "negative energy."

"I just want to be happy and I want Jac to be happy," the TOWIE hunk said, "Whether that's with me or not.

"It's more like a really bad tension in the house and it's getting worse. We're losing our friendship. When I feel it's getting too much, I'll get out of the house and go to the gym or something."

He also struck out when snapped partying with a female pal in LA, writing: "So much bulls**t gets posted and printed about me.

"Seriously just want them to leave me alone now. It's getting too much, I can't even hang out with friends or even work without them talking s**t about me.

"Am I being targeted? Cause I swear I don't see others getting it like I do."

Jacqueline meanwhile has won the affection of I'm A Celeb fans for her no nonsense and down to earth approach to the challenges of life in the jungle.

She's also been candid about her struggles with weight, with former Queen of the Jungle Scarlett Moffatt gushing: "Jacqueline will easily be in the top three and she'll have a really good chance of being Queen of the Jungle.

"I've met her a few times and we really hit it off and I think she has real star quality."
