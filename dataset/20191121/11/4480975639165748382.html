UK election: Ipsos MORI poll shows support for PM Johnson's Conservatives rose to 44%

21 Nov 2019, 11:43 by Eren Sengezer

Support for the British Prime Minister Boris Johnson's Conservative Party in the upcoming election increased by three points to 44%, an opinion poll conducted by Ipsos MORI showed on Thursday.

Additionally, Ipsos MORI said the support for the main opposition Labour Party rose to 28% from 24% while Liberal Democrats and Brexit Party shares both lost four points to 16% and 3%, respectively.Â
