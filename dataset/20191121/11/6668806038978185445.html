Arjun Rampal and Mehr Jesia end 20-year-long marriage

21 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Filmfare

After 21 years of a blissful marriage, Arjun Rampal and Mehr Jesia have officially parted ways. The couple had signed up for a divorce six months ago and today their plea was officially granted by the court. Principal judge Shailaja Sawant granted the duo divorce under the Special marriage act. Reportedly, Arjun and Mehr’s marriage hit a rough patch around 2011 and it was seven years later in 2018 the two chose to separate.

Although the terms of the divorce have not been revealed yet, it is expected that their daughters will continue living with Mehr in her Bandra home.

Meanwhile, Arjun has begun a new chapter in his life with South African model, Gabriella Demetriades. The couple was recently blessed with a baby boy and have gone ahead with the name Arik.
