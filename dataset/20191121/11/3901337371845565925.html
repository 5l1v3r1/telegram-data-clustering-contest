Reports: Zim Coach Hands In Shock Resignation

21 Nov 2019, 11:26 by SL International

Just three months after taking charge of Zimbabwe, Joey Antipas has reportedly stepped down from his role as national team coach.

On Wednesday, reports emerged that Antipas had stepped down from his role as the Warriors' interim coach, just a day after he had masterminded a 2-1 win over Zambia. 

According to Zimbabwean publication The Chronicle, Antipas confirmed that he had resigned from his position with the national team. 

"I take a bow," were Antipas' reported words.
