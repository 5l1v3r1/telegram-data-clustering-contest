Emmerdale viewers predict Leanna Cavanagh will be pregnant with Jacob Gallagher's baby

Leanna and Jacob slept together

21 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Viewers of Emmerdale have predicted Leanna Cavanagh will fall pregnant after sleeping with Jacob Gallagher.

This week Jacob was shocked to find out paedophile Maya Stepney was being released from prison.

Fans of the show know school teacher Maya groomed her boyfriend's son Jacob.

She made the student believe they were in love, meanwhile she was in a relationship with Jacob's dad, David.

Eventually the truth came out about Maya and she was sentenced to 12 months in prison, but was released earlier this week after serving five.

Finding out Maya had been released, Jacob tried to see her but David convinced him not to.

However, it's clear Jacob isn't giving up on Maya.

As he left her a voicemail telling her how much he loved her, it was revealed to viewers that Maya is pregnant, but neither Jacob nor David know this.

In last night's episode of the soap (November 20 2019) Leanna went to see Jacob and tried to help him.

Well that's Leanna pregnant now as well as Maya.

Leanna, who is also Maya's former stepdaughter, tried to comfort her friend, but one thing led to another and the pair ended up sleeping together.

Immediately afterwards Jacob made his excuses and left, but he was shocked when Leanna revealed that that had been her first time.
