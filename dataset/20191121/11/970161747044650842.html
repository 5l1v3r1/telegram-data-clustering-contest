Will I'm a Celeb's Myles tell Jacqueline 'truth' of Dan Osbourne & Gabby Allen?

21 Nov 2019, 11:37

I’m a Celebrity’s Myles Stephenson is set to tell Jacqueline Jossa what happened between his ex, Gabby Allen and her husband, Dan Osborne, new reports claim.

Dan and Jacqueline have been married since 2017 but briefly split in April 2018 after Dan was seen on holiday with former Love Islander Gabby.

Gabby and Rak-Su singer Myles started dating that September, and were in a relationship for 11 months before splitting amid claims Myles cheated on her.

Dan and Gabby have always denied that anything happened between them, stating that they were just friends and were with a collective of mates during the holiday.

But The Sun is now claiming that that Myles will tell Jacqueline ‘everything’ she wants to know during those long days bored by the camp fire.

‘Myles would be willing to tell Jacqueline everything – and answer any questions she might have,’ a source told the paper.

‘Myles will tell Jacqueline everything Gabby told him. It’s a good thing Gabby’s gone to Thailand for a month because she might find it uncomfortable watching.’

Representatives for Dan declined to comment further.

Jacqueline and Dan have two children together – four-year-old Ella and Mia, 15 months – with Dan also having a son from a former relationship, Teddy.

While in the jungle, Dan has been hosting Instagram lives to watch his wife take on challenges, which have included being covered in spiders as she’s submerged in an orb.

I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here airs weeknights at 9pm on ITV.
