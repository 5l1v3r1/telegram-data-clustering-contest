Jeremy Corbyn promises to scrap tuition fees and universal credit

21 Nov 2019, 11:43

Jeremy Corbyn has today announced that if Labour are to be successful in the General Election next month, university tuition fees and universal credit will be abolished.

 

Mr Corbyn is announcing his official plans for government this morning by targeting ‘the rich and powerful’ to fund ‘world class public services’.
