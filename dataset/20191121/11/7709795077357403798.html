Davis Cup: Great Britain one win away from quarter-finals

21 Nov 2019, 11:51 by Press Association

Kyle Edmund more than justified his Davis Cup place with a superb performance to beat Mikhail Kukushkin 6-3 6-3.

Edmund was called up to replace Andy Murray on Thursday, with the latter admitting he was not in peak physical condition following a three-set battle less than 24 hours earlier.

Kukushkin is ranked two places higher than Edmund, who has had a poor season and only clinched his spot in the Davis Cup team at the last minute by virtue of a decent run at the Paris Masters.

But here he struck his big groundstrokes with authority, pounced on any short balls and hung tough under pressure, saving all the break points he faced.
