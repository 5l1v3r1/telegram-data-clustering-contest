Overnights: ‘YHM’ & ‘KHKT’ boost Star Plus at No.1 on Wednesday in UK

21 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Raj Baddhan

Strong performances by ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ and ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ proved big winners in UK ratings, giving Star Plus a surge at No.1 on Wednesday, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Plus delivered a share of 0.16%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television with 0.10%, PTC Punjabi and Sony SAB TV with 0.09%, New Vision TV with 0.08%, Hum TV with 0.07%, ZEE TV, Colors and Geo News with 0.04%, Star Bharat, Colors Rishtey, Dunya News with 0.03% and Geo TV with 0.02%.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ stayed top with 71,700 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 71,000 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 64,200 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 49,700 viewers, ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 46,100 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 33,600 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ at 22:00 with 19,600 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 16,300 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 12,800 viewers and ‘Namah’ at 18:30 with 6,000 viewers.

On Sony TV, ‘Patiala Babes’ topped ratings with 50,700 viewers at 20:30, followed by ‘Mere Sai’ at 19:00 with 43,500 viewers, ‘Mere Dad Ki Dulhan’ at 22:00 with 36,400 viewers and ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 34,200 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Ye Dil Mera’ ruled with 36,300 viewers at 20:00, followed by repeat of ‘Naqab Zun’ at 11:00 with 19,900 viewers and ‘Soya Mera Naseeb’ at 19:30 with 19,700 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ was watched by 31,000 viewers at 21:30, followed by ‘Kitchen Champion’ at 20:30 with 8,400 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ pulled in 31,200 viewers at 18:30, followed by ‘Happu Ki Ultan Paltan’ at 14:30 with 15,700 viewers and ‘Bhabhi Ji Ghar Par Hain’ at 14:00 with 13,400 viewers.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
