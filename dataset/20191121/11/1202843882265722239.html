Safari opens hypermarket in Al Khor with many special offers

21 Nov 2019, 11:54

Doha: Leading hypermarket chain Safari Group opened its 4th hypermarket in Qatar yesterday as thousands thronged the Al Khor outlet for the special offers. The one of a kind ‘Visit and Win’ promotion has become a big hit among the visitors.

The hypermarket in Al Khor was inaugurated by Sheikh Mohammed bin Ahmed bin Jassim Al Thani yesterday.

Safari Group Chairman Hamad Dafar A A Al Ahbabi, Managing Director Aboobacker Madappatt, Safari Group Director and General Manager Zainul Abideen, Executive Directors Shaheen Backer and Shameem Backer, and other officials were present during the opening cereony.

For the inauguration, there are many exciting offers and promotions for customers.

The occasion saw large crowds turn up at the hypermarket to make the most of the special promotions offered to mark the opening. The hypermarket was giving away 32-inch LED TV for QR189 and laptop for QR 599.

Safari ‘Visit and Win’ promotion kicked off yesterday. Customers visiting the new Safari in Al Khor can win a Lexus LX 570SUV by just visiting the hypermarket. The highlight of this promotion is that one need not purchase anything from Safari to get lucky. Visitors receives coupon from customer service and can try their luck in the draw.

The new Hypermarket is operational on four floors covering 193,000 square feet. The basement also has spacious car parking for customers. The ground floor is a supermarket and the first floor is department store. Second floor is for food court.

“This is the first of its kind lucky draw in the country. Offer, which started yesterday is on till January 19, 2020. The winner will be announced at the draw on January 20 at Safari in Al Khor,” a statement from the group said.

Another mega promotion started from November 20, where as many as 20 Toyota Corolla cars will be rewarded with a coupon offer from Safari branches for every purchase of QR50. Unlike usual practice, three Toyota Corolla cars are being gifted every 15 days.

The first three draws will take place at Safari’s Al Khor branch and the next four draws will be at the Safari Mall in Abu Hamour.

Safari, with many years of experience in retail, offers a range of products to suit the needs of its customers. Safari’s success story is that providing all world class products under one roof with affordable price which suits every one’s budget. Safari has a dedicated team of experienced professionals to market a variety of products to meet the needs, tastes and budgets of customers. The advantage is that consumer can by quality products within the limit of their budget.
