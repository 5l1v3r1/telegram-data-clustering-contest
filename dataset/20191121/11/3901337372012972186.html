Chiefs Star Gives Amakhosi TKO Boost

21 Nov 2019, 11:18 by Soccer Laduma

While Ernst Middendorp has three suspensions to deal with for Kaizer Chiefs on Sunday afternoon, one player seems to have given him a boost for their Telkom Knockout semi-final.

Kearyn Baccus has played for the Glamour Boys since having to be taken off in the TKO quarter-final win over Cape Town City over a month ago back in October, and while Middendorp’s side have won all four games since then, his return will come as a bonus for the German coach.

Chiefs go up against their coach’s former employers, Maritzburg United, this weekend as the two teams lock horns for a place in the final of this year’s tournament, and the midfield man is thought to be back in full training and available for selection ahead of Sunday’s game.

The Siya crew recently reported that Baccus could make a return for Chiefs this weekend, and with the 28-year-old back with the team as they prepare to face the Team of Choice, he’s in contention to return to the matchday squad when they travel to Nelspruit.

It remains to be seen whether Middendorp will look to bring the midfielder straight into the XI given Willard Katsande’s suspension, or whether he needs more time before getting a full game under his belt, but fans will be pleased to see their new signing drawing close to a return regardless.
