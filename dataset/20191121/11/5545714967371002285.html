Nintendo Download: The Touryst

Also, Black Future '88

21 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Chris Carter

Nintendo kind of went all-out last week with Pokemon Sword and Shield, which are already selling incredibly well.

As a result a little bit of a lull: a pre-holiday cooldown period, if you will, is in order. You see pretty much every major release (including Shenmue III) already came out, and there's only a few really big games coming in December like Darksiders: Genesis and MechWarrior 5.

Until then, there's The Touryst and Black Future '88 on the Switch eShop, as well as all of the below games.

  • Castle of no Escape 2 
  • Chameleon

  • Fractured Minds
  • Green Video Game
  • Groove Coaster Wai Wai Party
  • Lots of Slots
  • Marblelous Animals

  • Narcos: Rise of the Cartels

  • Story of a Gladiator

  • The Unicorn Princess

No 3DS or Wii U offerings this week! You expect it from the Wii U, but the 3DS still has life in it according to Nintendo. As much as I love the system, I wish they'd just shift gears and move on since the 3DS has no games in active first-party development. Maybe they can finally update My Nintendo to fully support the Switch!
