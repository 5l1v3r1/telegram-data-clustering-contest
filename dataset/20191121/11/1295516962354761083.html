Arrow: The Monitor Rewrites Oliver's History in 'Reset' Promo

21 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Sam Stone

Realties and timelines converge in a new promo for Arrow's final season as Oliver Queen finds himself in an altered version of his past.

The episode title "Reset" appears to be quite literal based on the footage, which sees Oliver awaken to an incarnation of Star City where his longtime friend and deputy mayor Quentin Lance is still alive and well. With Oliver visibly conflicted by his fragmented memories, his time-displaced daughter Mia Smoak also appears in the altered timeline.
