Chinese manufacturing giant, Xiaomi, has a couple of projectors in the market. These projectors have different prices and qualities with its ANSI lumens reaching 9000. The recent projector from Xiaomi is the Mijia 2400 ANSI lumens laser projector. However, the company now has something different. It is the Xiaomi manual DIY projector which is currently available for 229 yuan ($33). You can check Xiaomi YouPin for this projector.

For starters, this projector is an analog old-fashioned hand-held projector. In addition, it uses a mechanical gear transmission and can achieve a real display. Furthermore, this old-fashioned projector needs to be assembled by hand. The assembly process also requires the coupling of 16 parts. Each part has a corresponding number. Before starting to assemble, first carefully read the graphic manual, which will give you a step by step guide on how to complete the assembly.

The Xiaomi manual DIY projector comes with a 58-frame film. It does not need a battery or a connecting cable. It can directly project the 58-frame film by turning the projector’s hand crank. The 28mm focal length lens makes it easy to control the size and brightness of the image. All parts of the projector use laser-cutting technology in Germany. In addition, accurate cutting ensures the precision of the assembly.
