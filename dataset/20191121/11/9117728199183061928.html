Racing Point confirm driver line-up for post-season testing

21 Nov 2019, 11:34 by Tom Mason

Racing Point has confirmed that Sergio Perez and Lance Stroll will drive for the time at the end of season test in Abu Dhabi. This comes as no surprise after both drivers were confirmed for the 2020 season by the team.

Perez will drive on the first day which is Tuesday, while his teammate Stroll will be in position on the second day which is Wednesday.

The test days take place after the climax of the season next weekend in Abu Dhabi.

With one race remaining, Racing Point is seventh in the constructors' standings on 67 points, 16 behind Toro Rosso.

The Mexican Perez is comfortably leading the battle between the two teammates, with 46 points to his name compared to Stroll's 21.
