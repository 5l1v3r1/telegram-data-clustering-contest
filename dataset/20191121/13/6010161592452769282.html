Pokemon Sword and Shield has sold over 6 million copies in a week

21 Nov 2019, 13:13 by Sherif Saed

Pokemon Sword and Shield is already one of the most successful games in the series.

The Pokemon Company has announced [PDF] that Pokemon Sword and Pokemon Shield have together sold over 6 million units globally, 2 million of which just in Japan.

Not only does this beat Super Smash Bros. Ultimate’s 5 million, it’s over double that of Pokemon Let’s Go Pikachu and Eevee’s 3 million in the first week, and well above Pokemon X and Y’s 4 million.

As the press release notes, this is a new record for Nintendo Switch.
