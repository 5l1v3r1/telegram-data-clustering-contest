Desperate for You to Install Its Cameras in Your House, Facebook's Portal Is £50 off This Black Friday

21 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Shabana Arif

Facebook is offering up to £50 off is Portal and Oculus devices this Black Friday.

The £50 discount applies to Facebook's Portal TV, Portal Mini, and Portal which brings the most expensive of the bunch - the Portal - down to £119 from £169, while the cheapest - the Portal Mini - drops down from £129 to just £79. The new Portal+ gets £30 off, bringing the price down from £269 to £239.

As well as its various Portal products, Facebook is also knocking fifty quid off the Oculus Rift S and the Oculus Go. That brings the Rift S down to £349 from £399, while the Oculus Go 32GB drops down to £149, and the 64GB model down to £199.

The discounts go live on Tuesday, November 25, and run up until Monday, December 2.

Meanwhile, the Oculus Quest, which is having no discounts applied, will come with all three episodes of the Emmy-nominated Vader Immortal: A Star Wars VR Series, which is definitely enough of a motivator to make you shell out a few hundred quid for the hardware. This particular promo goes live today and extends all the way up until January 31.
