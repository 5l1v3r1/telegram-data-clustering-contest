PICS: Paraffin’s Amai Sorobhi APPEALS For Help After Crippling Stroke

21 Nov 2019, 13:32 by Andrew

Amai Sorobhi Appeals For Help

iHarare today visited veteran television actress Rhoda Mtembe, popularly known as Amai Sorobhi in the yesteryear hit drama series “Paraffin”. We visited Gogo Mtembe at her house in Kuwadzana Extension, after receiving reports that she was unwell and in need of aid for medical bills, food and crutches as she is unable to walk.

Gogo Mtembe suffered a stroke early this year and is now handicapped. She is barely able to walk on her own or to perform day to day tasks. She is currently living with her daughter Charity who is taking care of her every need, including moving her around the house when she needs to.

After yesterday’s article on Gogo Mtembi’s plight, iHarare has been receiving numerous calls and inquiries from well-wishers from all over the world, who want to assist the veteran actress and wanted to confirm the channels through which to send their aid.

Anyone willing to assist Gogo Mtembi can contact her daughter Charity Mtembe on +263 771 713 693. For those who want to help in person, their home Address is House Number 14370/1 Kuwadzana Extension.
