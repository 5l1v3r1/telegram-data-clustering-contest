EXCLUSIVE: The New Mutants Get a Brutal Prison Sentence

21 Nov 2019, 13:11 by L.D. Nolan

Roberto Da Costa/Sunspot's quest with the New Mutants to bring home a missing team member from Shi'ar space saw the entire team getting captured after the Starjammers got sick of the group. Marvel Comics has given CBR an exclusive preview of Jonathan Hickman and Rod Reis' New Mutants #2, which details their stint in prison before their trial and features Sunspot narraings the events so far as they've occurred from his perspective. Due to an incompetent lawyer, things don't go well for the team, and each of them ends up receiving a life sentence.

You can see CBR's exclusive preview of the issue alongside the solicitation information below.

NEW MUTANTS #

  • JONATHAN HICKMAN (W) • ROD REIS (A/C)
  • VARIANT COVER BY ARTHUR ADAMS
  • THE NEW GENERATION CLAIMS THE DAWN!
  • The classic New Mutants (Sunspot, Wolfsbane, Mirage, Karma, Magik, and Cypher) get together with a few new friends (Chamber, Mondo) to seek out their missing member and share the good news… a mission that takes them into space alongside the Starjammers!
  • 32 PGS./Rated T+ …$3.99

New Mutants #2 releases November 27 from Marvel Comics.
