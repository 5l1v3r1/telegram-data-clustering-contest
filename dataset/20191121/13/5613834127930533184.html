‘Alien Isolation’ Arrives December 5 on The Nintendo Switch

21 Nov 2019, 13:47 by Mike Wilson

Nintendo Switch users: What are you doing on December 5? If you’re keen on getting your Alien fix, then that’s the day that Sega’s Alien Isolation will be landing on the Switch, courtesy of Feral Interactive.

The trailer below seems to indicate that the game will be available from the eShop only, with no retail version available at the moment. Pre-purchases are also available today, so get on it.

The Switch version of the game will come packed with the base game and all 7 of the previously launched DLCs. It will also introduce gyroscopic aiming and HD rumble for added immersion.

Alien Isolation is also available for PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.
