Black Friday 2019: SuperValu announce massive gin sale just in time for Christmas

The retailer has slashed their prices on a selection of gins

21 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Ian Mangan

Irish supermarket chain SuperValu have announced they are slashing prices on their gin selection ahead of the Black Friday sales.

Shoppers can get their hands on the discounted booze in stores nationwide or online via the SuperValu website here.

The massive gin sale will begin on Thursday November 28 and run until Wednesday December 11 so punters can stock up for Christmas.

From dry gin to international style the retailer boasts a range of over 30 special gins which will be available at reduced prices for a limited run for the duration of the sale.

Eglington Irish Pink Gin 70cl. (Sale price: €27)
