Killer of Helen McCourt, 22, released from jail despite never telling family where body is

EXCLUSIVE: Insurance clerk Helen McCourt, 22, was murdered by pub landlord Ian Simms, now 63, in 1988

21 Nov 2019, 13:33 by Louie Smith, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

Parole board judges have freed Helen McCourt's killer from his life sentence.

Ian Simms, 63, murdered 22-year-old insurance clerk Helen in 1988.

Her mum Marie McCourt, 76, has spent three decades campaigning for Simms to remain in prison until he revealed the location of her body.

But following a parole hearing earlier this month he will now be released.

Marie has successfully campaigned for the introduction of Helen's Law, to make it more difficult for 'No Body' killers to gain freedom.

A spokeswoman for the Parole Board said:  “The Parole Board must make its decisions solely focused on whether a prisoner would represent a significant risk to the public after release.

"The panel will have carefully looked at a whole range of evidence, including details of the original evidence and any evidence of behaviour change.

“This is done with great care and public safety is the number one priority.”
