BREAKING: Oshiomhole to meet House of Reps APC caucus

21 Nov 2019, 13:46 by Leke Baiyewu

Leke Baiyewu, Abuja

The National Chairman of the ruling All Progressives Congress, Adams Oshiomhole, is to meet with members of the party’s caucus in the House of Representatives.

The Deputy Speaker, Ahmed Wase, while presiding over the plenary on Thursday, announced the meeting scheduled for 3.00 p.m.
