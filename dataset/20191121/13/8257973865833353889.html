Becky Lynch invades WWE NXT, Adam Cole competes in ladder match

21 Nov 2019, 13:26 by Wade Sheridan

Nov. 21 (UPI) -- Raw Women's Champion Becky Lynch invaded NXT ahead of Survivor Series on Sunday where all three of WWE's brands will be battling each other to determine which show is the best.

Lynch called out NXT Women's Champion Shayna Baszler who The Man will be facing at Survivor Series along with Smackdown Women's Champion Bayley in a Triple Threat match.

Rhea Ripley instead arrived onto the scene and challenged Lynch to a match which the champ accepted.
