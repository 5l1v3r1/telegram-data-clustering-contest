Kendall Jenner and Gigi Hadid Set the Tone For 2020 With Their Matching Street Style

21 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Sarah Wasilak

Gigi Hadid and Kendall Jenner joined Joan Smalls for dinner at Carbone in NYC. It's not very often we get to see Kendall and Gigi (or "KenGi," as they like to call themselves) hanging out in the flesh, but naturally, we take advantage when we can. If it's not a selfie on Instagram we're checking out but a paparazzi shot, we're fully immersed in what they're wearing. This time, the supermodels stuck to a similar color palette, and we feel like that means we should be taking notes.

Kendall opted for high-waist leather pants, a ribbed mock neck, and an olive leather shirt left undone. She accessorized with a Louis Vuitton graffiti purse from What Goes Around Comes Around, square-toe boots, and gold chain-link drop earrings. Meanwhile, you could say Gigi was her hippie-grunge counterpart, rocking loose, two-tone charcoal EB Denim, combat boots, and a tie-dye Diesel Red Tag x A-Cold-Wall* t-shirt and vest, finished with a small, gray fold-over pouch.

In our minds, Kendall and Gigi were just setting the tone for us fashion folk out there: in 2020, why not try an outfit grounded with dark pants that gets topped with neutral shades like army green, coffee, navy, and khaki? Ahead, get all the details on KenGi's street style moment, then shop their essentials.
