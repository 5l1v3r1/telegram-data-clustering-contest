NOT KEEPING UP

Caitlyn Jenner’s daughters Kylie and Kendall STILL ignoring I’m A Celebrity as they fail to show support

21 Nov 2019, 13:06 by Rebecca Lewis

I'M A Celebrity viewers have been left shocked at the lack of support Caitlyn Jenner has received from her family.

None of Caitlyn Jenner's family - from former wife Kris Jenner to daughters Kendall and Kylie Jenner - have publicly discussed or shared love and support to their father during her time on the ITV show.

The 70-year-old is currently taking part in the ITV show but has not had any mentions from her six biological kids and four step-children so far.

One disgruntled fan shared: "I’m not saying that you have to put anything on social media to prove you care etc but not one of @Caitlyn_Jenner’s family members have said anything about her stint on #ImACeleb and its bothering the hell out of me."

Another added: "Its literally breaking my heart that not a single member of Caitlyns family appear to be supporting her (sic). You're doing great Caitlyn! I am rooting for you!!

Caitlyn Jenner also has four other children from her previous marriage but son Brody Jenner admitted he would not be watching as he didn't know anything about the show.
