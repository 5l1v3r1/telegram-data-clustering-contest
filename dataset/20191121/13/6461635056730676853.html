Premier League star desperately wants Barcelona move

21 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Shiva

Chelsea Star Willian is keen on completing his rumoured switch to Barcelona in the coming January Transfer Window. As per Fox Sports, the Brazilian is now desperate to join the Camp Nou.

It is no secret that Barcelona have had Willian on their radar for quite some time now. Moreover, it has only been a few weeks since the Midfielder admitted that the Catalans did indeed make an offer for him post the 2018 World Cup. However, it was rejected by the blues.

This is not the first time that Chelsea have offers for Willian from the Blaugranes. A few months back, the Brazil international shocked the world when he refused to pen down a contract extension with the Blues. As per reports, he has been stalling down the deal in a bid to attract a deal that would see him head to the Camp Nou.

Ernesto Valverde’s side have not been consistent in the midfield in recent times and it is quite visible. Since the team is undergoing a transitional change, it is quite natural that inconsistency will creep in. However, Willian has still got a few years left and his presence could help the youngsters for the better.

La Liga’s defending champions should go for Willian as they will have a quality and experienced midfielder in the ranks. William is someone who can lead the attacking midfield and guide the potential star in Frenkie de Jong.
