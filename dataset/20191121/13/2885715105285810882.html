Nicholas Cage or Phil Collins? People divided over who a Mexican church’s Jesus statue looks like

The nearly 22-foot-tall, 2,000-pound giant sculpture built for the La EpifanÍa del Señor church in Zacatecas could set a world record for being the biggest statue in the world of baby Jesus.

21 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Trends Desk

A huge statue of baby Jesus in Mexico has taken social media by storm. The nearly 22-foot-tall, 2,000-pound giant sculpture that was built for the La EpifanÍa del Señor church in Zacatecas has got everyone talking online. The debate is about who it resembles more: actor Nicholas Cage or pop star Phil Collins?

According to Mexican channel Info 7 News, artist Román Salvador Barrueto and his team created the statue after being commissioned by the church. However, with its big blue eyes, full cheeks and a receding hairline, the statue reminded some of the Hollywood star and others of the former Genesis frontman.
