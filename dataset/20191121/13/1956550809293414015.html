Dubai’s Rodeo Drive launches new country music night

Get ready for Wild West Wednesdays at this all-American bar

21 Nov 2019, 13:10 by Reema Rahman

Looking for alternative things to do in Dubai?

Well, dig out that Stetson – it’s time to head to the rodeo…

Popular Sheikh Zayed Road haunt, Rodeo Drive, has launched a new night and it’s one you’ll want to don your cowboy boots for.

Head to the all-American themed bar every Wednesday as county music duo Mike Ulanski and Adam Showalter, otherwise known as Down Home, will be crooning the crowd from 9pm until midnight.

It promises to be a cracking night with a load of drinks deals including buy one, get one free on select drinks, a special menu with discounted rates and special combo offers, as well as games, prizes to be won and themed outfits to try on.

Gather your mates and you’ll be yelling yee-haw in no time, cowboy.

Wed 9pm-midnight. Ongoing. Saaed Tower One, Sheikh Zayed Road (054 417 7028).
