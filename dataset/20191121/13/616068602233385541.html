Saturday parking free in Dec

21 Nov 2019, 13:30 by Colin Dacre

Motorists who want to do their Christmas shopping downtown Penticton next month will enjoy free parking on Saturdays.

City council this week approved no-charge street parking downtown on all four Saturdays in December. The municipality has run a similar program for the past several years, at request of the Downtown Penticton Association. 

The city says waiving the parking fees will cost them about $5,000 in lost revenue. 

A motion by Mayor John Vassilaki at Tuesday’s council meeting to have free transit on the same days failed, after a majority of councillors decided they wanted more information on the idea and will consider it next year. 

Chief financial officer Jim Bauer said free transit would have cost the city an estimated less than $1,800 per day.
