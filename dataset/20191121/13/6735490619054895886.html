Rebecca Ferguson Takes Swipe At Interviewer After He Asks ‘Sexiest’ Question About All The ‘Handsome Men’ She’s Worked With

21 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Becca Longmire

Rebecca Ferguson was quick to shut down an interviewer after he asked her about all the “handsome men” she’d worked with.

A clip of Ferguson promoting “Doctor Sleep” has been doing the rounds online, with the actress seeming less than impressed as the journalist in question listed off numerous actors she’d starred in films with – but he left out all the amazing women.

Ferguson fired back, “How about Meryl Streep? Emily Blunt?”
