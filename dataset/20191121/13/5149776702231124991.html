Man to appear in court over 'threatening message' about Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn

He is due to appear at Nottingham Magistrates’ Court in January

21 Nov 2019, 13:09 by Ben Reid

A 37-year-old man is due to appear in court charged in connection with a report of malicious communication regarding Jeremy Corbyn.

Samuel Naylor, of Nottingham Road, Eastwood, is charged with "sending a letter, communication or article conveying a threatening message" regarding the Labour Party leader.

He is due to appear at Nottingham Magistrates’ Court on Tuesday, January 3, 2020.

The incident was reported to Nottinghamshire Police on Tuesday, November 19, 2019.
