Ben Foden says ex-wife Una Healy has always been supportive of his music career

21 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Ciara Walsh

The former rugby player is proving to be a hit on X Factor: Celebrity. 

Ben Foden says ex-wife Una Healy has always been supportive of his music career.

People were shocked to learn that the former England player would be taking part in X Factor: Celebrity alongside fellow rugby stars Thom Evans and Levi Davis, but boyband Try Star have proven to be a hit with viewers of the show.

Having been married to Una, who was a member of girl group The Saturdays, for six years, it came as a bit of a shock when Ben revealed that he would be trying his hand at singing.

However, the dad-of-two, says Una is hugely supportive of his career change despite their split.

"Una always used to encourage me to do the music thing. When I told her that I was doing The X Factor she thought it was a great idea. She loves it," Ben told MailOnline.

Despite divorcing each other after Ben's infidelity came to light in 2018, he insists that he and Una have put their differences aside and that she loves his band.

He continued: "Una and I have a good relationship for the sake of the kids. I always wish her the very, very best. And the kids are loving The X Factor.

"Their mum is obviously mega talented at singing. She always says when she catches the show, sometimes she's busy, but she thinks Try Star are doing great."

Ben has since remarried following the pair's divorce after finding love with Jackie Smith earlier this year.

The rugby pro-turned singer said that Jackie has been a huge supporter of his, however he joked that his bandmate Thom was her favourite member.

X Factor: Celebrity continues Saturday's on ITV at 8.30pm.
