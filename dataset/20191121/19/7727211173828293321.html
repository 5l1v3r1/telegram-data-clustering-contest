ITV Emmerdale fans compelling theory over what will happen to Leanna

Viewers think they know what's next for the teenager

21 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Sophie McCoid

Emmerdale fans have an interesting theory about Leanna's future after her tryst with Jacob.

Jacob and Leanna had sex in last night's episode, after Leanna had had a crush on him for weeks.

Viewers were stunned last night as Jacob left her just minutes after their afternoon of passion, despite it being her first time.

Jacob is struggling to come to terms with Maya being released from prison, with fans seeing her walking out of the prison heavily pregnant, presumably with his baby.

And now eagle-eyed fans think that Leanna might also become pregnant with Jacob's baby, and many of them took to social media to voice their theory.

Lizzy said: "So wait is Leanna also gonna get pregnant with Jacobs kid."

Sarah Freer said: "It wouldn’t surprise me if Leanna gets pregnant too, then that’s two women he’s got pregnant."

And Elaine said: "I bet it's #davidmetcalfes #emmerdale odds leanna is pregnant as well."

Tonight's episode saw Leanna try and persuade Bernice to get the morning after pill for her, after revealing her and Jacob hadn't used any form of protection.

Bernice handed over the pill and told her it was totally normal if she felt sick after it, and sure enough she was shortly after all over her dad.

She ended up revealing all to her dad, who went ballastic at Jacob for "using" his daughter.
