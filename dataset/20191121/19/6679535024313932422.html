One arrested in Mulanur murder

21 Nov 2019, 19:05 by Staff Reporter

The police arrested a man on Thursday for the murder of a woman, whose body was found at Perumal Gounden Valasu in Mulanur on Monday.

T. Dhanapal (24) was working in a shop in Namakkal near the restaurant where the deceased, Thirumangai (33) was working. Police said that the woman allegedly had an extramarital affair with Dhanapal. He killed her following an argument in Namakkal and threw the body in Mulanur, according to the police.

He was remanded in judicial custody at Coimbatore Central Prison on Thursday.
