Hill: 'Often when women show anger it's not fully appreciated'

21 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Rebecca Klar

Fiona Hill, the former National Security Council senior director for Europe and Russia, said she was “angry” with U.S. Ambassador to the European Union Gordon Sondland and had “a few testy encounters” with the ambassador. 

"I was actually, to be honest, angry with him,” Hill said during her public testimony on Thursday. 

“And I hate to say it, but often when women show anger it's not fully appreciated, it's often pushed off onto emotional issues, perhaps, or deflected on other people,” she added.
