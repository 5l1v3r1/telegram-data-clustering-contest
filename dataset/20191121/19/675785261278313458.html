Argos Black Friday sale officially starts tomorrow - and these deals are included

Mark your calendar - the Argos Black Friday 2019 sale starts tomorrow, and we've got a sneak preview of what deals will be included

21 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Chantelle Symester, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

Argos Black Friday deals start tomorrow and the chain has revealed some of the incredible discounts on offers.

The deals, which will be available in stores and online at argos.co.uk , include plenty of top tech and electrical brands including Apple, Samsung, Beats by Dre and Dyson.

The biggest price cuts include £120 off 50-inch Hitachi 50HK25T74U Smart 4K LED TVs, now £249.99 and £100 off the Dyson V7 Motorhead Extra Cordless Vacuum Cleaner, now £199.

But it's not just tech and electricals bargain-hunters can pick up in the Black Friday discounts. Argos is also rolling out discounts on toys as part of its crazy codes promotion.

Shoppers can currently pick up 20% savings on selected Frozen, Star Wars and Marvel Avengers toys until 26 November using the STAR20 discount code online and in stores - ideal for parents doing their Christmas shopping.
