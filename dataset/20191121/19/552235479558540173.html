Police probing assault and robbery in Aberdeen release CCTV images of two men

Police Scotland officers believe the men – who are though to be from the Liverpool area – can help them with their inquiries.

21 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Kathleen Speirs

Police have released images of two men they believe can help them with their investigation into an assault and robbery in Aberdeen.

On Saturday a man and woman were assaulted inside a property in Marchburn Drive area in the Northfield area of the city at around 7pm.

A number of items were taken during the incident.

Investigating Officer Detective Constable Tom Camerson said: "This has been a very upsetting incident for the man and woman involved.

"We are carrying out an extensive investigation and are now looking for the public to help us trace the men pictured as we believe they may have information which could assist us.

"It is understood that the men may be from the Liverpool area.

"They are described as aged between mid-twenties and early thirties, of average build, and around five foot, eight inches tall.

"It is believed they were in the company of a third male also understood to be from the Liverpool area, of a similar age and build, and around five foot ten inches tall."

"Anyone who recognises the men in the images released or who has any information is asked to contact police on 101  quoting reference number 3699 of November 17, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 to remain anonymous."
