Hat-trick at his last chance in festival

21 Nov 2019, 19:22 by R.K. Roshni

Goutham M. scored a hat-trick in his last appearance at the Revenue District School Arts Festival on Thursday.

A Plus Two student of St. Mary’s Higher Secondary School, Pattom, Gautham secured first A grade in Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, and Folk Dance.

Goutham has been securing the first position in all three events for the past four years.

He has also done well in the State School Arts Festival in the past. At last year’s festival, he got second A grade in Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi and A grade in Folk Dance. The year before that, he secured first A grade in folk dance, second A grade in Kuchipudi, and third A grade in Bharatanatyam.

Goutham has been learning dance under Attingal Joshi. It is Bharatanatyam that he is learning in-depth. Mr. Joshi has been teaching dance to Goutham and some other students for free taking into consideration the financial situation of his family, says Goutham.

A Bio-Maths student, he wants to take a crack at the entrance examinations but feels that studying for those along with Plus Two and pursuing his passion for dance together may be difficult.
