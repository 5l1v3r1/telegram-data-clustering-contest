Half-Life: Alyx trailer drops – it’s coming out in March 2020

21 Nov 2019, 19:13 by Stefan L

Half-Life: Alyx has just been revealed in a sensational new trailer, with the first Half-Life game in well over a decade (and Valve’s first flagship VR exclusive) set to release in March 2020 for Valve Index, HTC Vive, Oculus Rift and Windows Mixed Reality.
