Lady tells parents their children don’t belong to them

21 Nov 2019, 19:12

- A Twitter user has taken to the platform to state that children don't belong to their parents

- According to the lady identified as @neauxna, she stated that parents only have borrowed time with them

- The Twitter user advised parents to help kids become the best version of themselves

A Twitter user recently gave a lot of social media users some food for thought about parenting. The lady who goes under the Twitter handle, @neauxna, stated that parents need to understand that their kids don't belong to them.

According to her, rather than focus on living through their kids, parents should focus on helping them become the best version of themselves.

She wrote: "Your children don’t belong to you. You’re on borrowed time with them. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to build a better human being; not to build a mini version of you and all your deflated hopes and dreams. Help them become the best them not the best you."
