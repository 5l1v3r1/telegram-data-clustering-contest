Nigerians react to video of Bayelsa gov-elect, David Lyon speaking wrong English

21 Nov 2019, 19:38 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

A video of Bayelsa State Governor-elect, David Lyon has gone viral for the wrong reason. In the video, the Governor-elect was seen addressing journalists shortly after his election and Mr. Lyon committed serious grammatical blunders.

The video went viral and has attracted several reactions from Nigerians. the Bayelsa State Goveror-elect, David Lyon while addressing newsmen said:

”Today is one of my great day in my life. The great people of Bayelsans have spoken their mind by taking time to vote for our great party APC. It’s indeed a welcome development because in our own campaigns, we give to them, when being elected and swear in, we give to them security and development; it is our key agendas.”
