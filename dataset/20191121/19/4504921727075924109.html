SWAPO members, supporters invited to attend last rally in Windhoek this weekend

21 Nov 2019, 19:23

SWAPO presidential candidate Hage Geingob has called on the party's rank and file as well as supporters to attend the last rally in Windhoek to be held over the weekend.
Geingob made the call during a drive through campaigns at Groot Aub settlement and Rehoboth on Wednesday.
