Rest in Peace, ookyo.

21 Nov 2019, 19:20

Earlier today, ookyo recently updated their website stating that they will be "turning RED" on 21 January 2020. With that said, all existing ookyo subscribers will be ported over to Hotlink RED on the aforementioned date and they will also be given 8GB of free internet data with non-Stop Social worth RM45.

Furthermore, ookyo has mentioned that they will no longer be accepting new customers starting today. For those who have already ordered a new ookyo plan, they will still send the SIM over to you and it will be ported over to Hotlink RED when the time comes.
