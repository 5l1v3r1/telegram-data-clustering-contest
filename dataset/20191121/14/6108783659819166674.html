WolfEye is the new studio from Arkane founder Raf Colantonio

21 Nov 2019, 14:04 by Steve Wright

We'll get a look at the studio's first game during The Game Awards.

WolfEye is the name of the new studio from Arkane founder Raf Colantonio.

Colantonio will act as Creative Director and President of the new studio, working alongside Executive Producer and CEO Julien Roby. WolfEye’s first game will be revealed at The Game Awards 2019, held on 13 December here in Australia.

Raf started his career at EA, followed by a short stint at Infogrames before opening up Arkane, where he oversaw development of titles including Dishonored, Dishonored 2 and Prey.
