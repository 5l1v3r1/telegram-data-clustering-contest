Adopt a family for Xmas

21 Nov 2019, 14:36 by John Lawless

While some of us won't have to worry if there will be enough presents under the tree this year, there are many families that do.

The Okanagan Boys and Girls Clubs' Adopt-A-Family program pairs families with organizations and individuals to help ensure that families have a happy holiday.

For the past 12 years, sponsors have put together gift and food hampers for families that may not be able to do it themselves for the holiday season.

"We hope you will consider adopting a family this holiday season and help make a family in our community have a little brighter holiday," says Richelle Leckey from Okanagan Boys and Girls Clubs. "Thank you for bringing joy in a time of need."

To learn more about the program and how you can be a part of it, you can visit their website.
