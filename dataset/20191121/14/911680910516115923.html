Lara Trump brings President Trump's re-election campaign to Tucson Thursday

21 Nov 2019, 14:51 by KGUN 9 On Your Side

TUCSON, Ariz. -- President Donald Trump's 2020 Presidential campaign is making a stop in Tucson Thursday.

According to the the re-election campaign's website , President Trump's daughter-in-law and 2020 campaign manager will be in attendance.

Lara Trump and Brad Parscale will be at Hotel Tucson City Center for the event at 11 a.m..
