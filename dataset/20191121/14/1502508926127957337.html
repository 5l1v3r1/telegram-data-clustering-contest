Tata Power issues 15,000 non-convertible debentures, raises Rs 1,500 cr

The NCDs would be listed on WDM segment of NSE

21 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Press Trust of India

Tata Power on Thursday raised Rs 1,500 crore by issuing unsecured, non-cumulative, redeemable, taxable, listed, rated, non-convertible debentures (NCDs) on private placement basis.

The company has issued 15,000 NCDs worth Rs 1,500 crore on November 21, 2019, Tata Power said in a BSE filing.

The NCDs would be listed on WDM (wholesale debt market) segment of NSE, it added.
