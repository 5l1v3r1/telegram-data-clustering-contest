Princess Love Says Ray J Blocked Her On Instagram After She Alleged He Abandoned Her & Baby In Vegas

21 Nov 2019, 14:48 by Jenna Lemoncelli

The war between estranged couple, Ray J and Princess Love is getting uglier by the day. After she accused him of leaving her and their 18-month-old daughter ‘stranded’ in Las Vegas, the pregnant model now claims the rapper blocked her on social media.

Princess Love may be ready to sever ties with her husband, Ray J. The model, 35, who is pregnant with their second child, revealed that the rapper, 38, blocked her on Instagram, according to a screenshot captured by The Shade Room. The latter comes after Princess accused Ray of leaving her and their 18-month-old daughter Melody, “stranded” in Las Vegas on November 17, following BET’s Soul Train Awards.

“Somebody tell Scooty boy he’s only hurting himself 😂😂😂,” Princess allegedly wrote next to a screenshot of Ray J’s account, which she can no longer see. “This is so funny to me.” In response to a fan, that wrote, “My future husband would never,”  Princess  replied, “Mine either, sis.”

As the estranged couple’s relationship woes continue to play out on social media, Princess tweeted a cryptic message, hinting at the end of their relationship. “Time to let go,” she tweeted on November 20. Before that, Princess hadn’t tweeted since later October when she retweeted Love & Hip Hope’s video of her with Ray and their daughter during happier times.
