Fortnite: Chapter 2 – Dance at the Pipeman, the Hayman and the Timber Tent

21 Nov 2019, 14:55 by Lauren Aitken

For this Fortnite challenge, you’ll need to visit and dance at three points of interest around the map.

You can complete this challenge across three matches or in one if you’re fast enough. Once you’ve found the Pipeman, Hayman and timber tent, do a little dance at them for a few moments until the step registers as complete.

Keep an eye out for opponents in these locations, so grab a weapon and build a few walls around you before you get started.

Dance at the Pipeman, the Hayman and the Timber Tent

To find the pipeman, you’ll need to head South of Misty Meadows. You’ll find him chilling on a hill, so use your favourite emote for a few seconds before moving on.

Next, it’s off to Frenzy Farm to find the Hayman. He’s in the Southwest of the area giving you a big wave. Again, dance in front of him for a moment before setting off to find the timber tent.

The timber tent can be found Northeast of Holly Hedges and looks like a teepee without any cover on it. There’s a chest inside it, so open it up to grab a weapon then do a wee dance to finish off the challenge.
