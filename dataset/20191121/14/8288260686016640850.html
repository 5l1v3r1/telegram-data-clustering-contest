Baileys Red Velvet is being sold in the UK - at just one store

It comes in at £17.99 for a 700ml bottle and has the same alcohol by volume (ABV) content as the original Baileys at 17%

21 Nov 2019, 14:29 by James Rodger, Melisha Kaur, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Baileys Red Velvet has hit the UK for first time ever - but only from one retailer.

Sweet-toothed shoppers will be pleased to hear that Baileys has released a new liqueur that tastes like red velvet cupcakes.

But you can only buy it online.

It comes in at £17.99 for a 700ml bottle and has the same alcohol by volume (ABV) content as the original Baileys at 17%.

Sadly it isn't available to buy in UK supermarkets just yet, but sweet-toothed shoppers can pre-order a bottle of the sweet liqueur at online alcohol store  The Bottle Club  .

Orders of the Baileys Red Velvet bottles will then be shipped on Friday, December 27th 2019 - just in time for New Year's Eve, according to the Mirror.

The limited-edition tipple has been created in collaboration with Georgetown Bakery, which say red velvet cupcakes are their best-seller.
