New Goodwill store now open in Punta Gorda

21 Nov 2019, 14:19 by WFTX Digital Team

PUNTA GORDA, Fla. -- There is now a new place to shop while helping people in the community.

A brand new Goodwill store and donation center opened Thursday in Punta Gorda. This new location offers an expanded retail space and has been customized for the community.

It is located on Tamiami Trail just south of Aqui Esta Drive, in the former Laishley Marine store.

A grand opening ribbon-cutting was held at 8:45 a.m. Thursday.
