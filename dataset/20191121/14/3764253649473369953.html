Harry Redknapp makes £100 bet with Morgan that Spurs will finish top 4

21 Nov 2019, 14:16

- Harry Redknapp has made £100 bet with Piers Morgan on Tottenham making the top four

- Redknapp is confident that his former side will qualify for next season Champions League

- But Piers Morgan says Tottenham can never finish among the top four teams this season

Former Tottenham manager Harry Redknapp has expressed confidence that his former club will finish among the top four teams at the end of the ongoing Premier League season.

His confidence came after Tottenham chiefs sacked Mauricio Pochettino and appointed Jose Mourinho as the new manager.

Tottenham have only won three games this term after 12 encounters and they currently occupy 14th position on the standings.

Mourinho and his new players will face West Ham United in their next game on Saturday, November 23, and a win will be good for Spurs.
