Fortnite: Chapter 2 – Consume foraged apples at The Orchard

21 Nov 2019, 14:01 by Lauren Aitken

Grab a weapon and a bucket because you’re going scrumping for apples for this week’s Fortnite challenge.

To complete this challenge, you’ll need to consume foraged apples from The Orchard landmark. You’ve probably wandered into this area a few times before and spotted some red apples lying around, which will replenish some of your health when eaten.

If you haven’t found The Orchard yet, you’ll be able to find it just Northeast of Frenzy Farm, which you can see on the map below.

Consume foraged apples at The Orchard

Before going hunting for apples, it would be a good idea to make sure you’ve got a weapon or two to hand and to build a few walls around yourself for protection. Better yet, you can activate Party Assist and have a friend watch your back while you gobble up apples.

If your health bar is full, you might not be able to consume them, so now might be the time to risk a few hits. That way, you’ll have a chance at completing the elimination challenge where you must defeat an opponent while your health is at 50 or less, then quickly regain health by chomping on some fruit.

Season 1 has been extended to February 2020, so you’ve got plenty of time to complete these challenges.
