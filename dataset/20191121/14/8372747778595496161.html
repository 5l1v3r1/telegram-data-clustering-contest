Crash with injuries reported on Hancock Bridge Parkway Thursday

21 Nov 2019, 14:27 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Police say a serious crash is affecting traffic at the intersection of Hancock Bridge Parkway and Cultural Park Boulevard.

According to Cape Coral Police, it happened around 2 p.m. Thursday.

Injuries are reported in the crash.

Drivers are asked to avoid the area for the next several hours if possible.
