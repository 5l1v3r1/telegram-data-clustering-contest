Pregnant Lydia Bright was hospitalised after split from baby's dad

Former TOWIE star Lydia Bright, who was left by her boyfriend in the eleventh week of her pregnancy, has shared that she was hospitalised from stress at the time

21 Nov 2019, 14:42 by Irishmirror.ie

Lydia Bright has told that she was admitted into hospital in the first trimester of her pregnancy after she split from boyfriend Lee Cronin.

The 29-year-old former TOWIE star discussed how the breakdown of her relationship that left her "heartbroken and lost" resulted in her suffering from severe sickness - which she believes was down to stress - that meant she had to be hospitalised.

The Essex native, who is expecting a little girl, revealed the ordeal occurred in the eleventh week of her pregnancy during a Q and A session with her Instagram followers on Thursday.

Lydia was quizzed by a follower: "What has been the toughest part of your pregnancy so far?"

In response, the single star unveiled how the early stages of her pregnancy were the hardest

The beauty shared: "The beginning was very hard. I'm so strong now but at 11 weeks I was completely heartbroken and lost. I was so worried that people would judge my situation...

"Before my announcement shoot hit the shelves I was in hospital with sickness (probably due to the stress of it all)...

"People are always quick to talk about the negative side of social media and the press, But I truly believe that my strength came from the support I received from strangers."

Lydia and Lee's love story has been turbulent from the moment sparks flew when they first met at a nightclub in Ibiza in July 2017.

They split four months after the pair had first started dating but unable to be apart from one another, they rekindled the flames of their romance.

The exes moved in together in January 2018 and they broke up briefly for three months in January this year.

They confirmed things were back on track in April but Lydia recently confirmed her pregnancy and their split again.

In June, just months before their split, Lydia dished she was "dangerously obsessed in love" with Lee.

Lydia told MailOnline: "With Lee, I was straight away obsessed in love with him. We always say we have a dangerous love.

"We'd be together the whole time and that's made my mind up [he's The One]!

"I’d love to get married! It’s not really for me to decide, that’s all down to him. I would love to get married. I think most girls dream of being married. We dream of our wedding day."
