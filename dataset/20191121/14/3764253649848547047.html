Kylian Mbappe tops players' market value list with ₤200m

21 Nov 2019, 14:07

Kylian Mbappe, French sensation, has once again dominated the players' market value list with a staggering value worth of ₤200 million above fellow Paris Saint German star Neymar Jr.

The forward announced his presence into the global stage with a consistent stellar form that made him switch side to Paris-Saint-German in a big money transfer worth £180 million.

The 22-year-old star, who is often tipped as football posterboy after Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo, is impressively justifying his value with over 33 goals in 29 games for the French giants this year alone.
