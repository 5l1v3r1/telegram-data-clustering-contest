All set for Oman Al Khair festival

21 Nov 2019, 14:07 by Times News Service

Muscat: Oman Al Khair festival will kick off on Friday in Amirat public park.

Muscat Municipality said in a statement: "Within the celebrations of the 49th National Day, H.E Eng. Issam al Zidjali, Chairman of Muscat Municipality will preside over the opening ceremony of our Festivity Oman al Khair, to be held at al Amirat public park and will last for 8 consecutive days."
