Kogi elections: INEC presents certificate of return to Yahaya Bello

21 Nov 2019, 14:20

- The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has issued a certificate of return to Kogi governor-elect, Yahaya Bello

- Edward Onoja, deputy governor-elect of the state, also received his own certificate

- The certificate was presented by INEC’s national commissioner in charge of Nasarawa, Kogi, and Kwara states, Mohammed Haruna

Following his victory at the polls, the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has presented the certificate of return to Kogi state governor, Yahaya Bello.

The governor received the certificate on Thursday, November 21, approximately, three days after he was declared the winner of the governorship election in the state.

Bello was presented with the certificate at a ceremony, which was held at the INEC office in Lokoja, the state capital, by INEC’s national commissioner in charge of Nasarawa, Kogi, and Kwara states, Mohammed Haruna.

The ceremony was also attended by the commissioner of police in Kogi.

Bello was accompanied to the ceremony by his deputy, Edward Onoja, and top government officials and chieftains of the All Progressives Congress (APC) in the state.
