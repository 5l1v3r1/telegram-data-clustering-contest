CONFIRMED- Kamal Haasan To Undergo An Implant Removal Surgery

21 Nov 2019, 14:10 by Amruta Patel

Legend Kamal Haasan will be off the public eye for some time now; the actor has been confirmed to undergo an implant removal surgery in the upcoming days. The news was confirmed by his political party’s vice president Dr. R Mahendran

Legendary actor Kamal Haasan will be slowing down for a while now. The actor is confirmed to undergo surgery in the upcoming days, which had been delayed due to the actor’s choc-o-block schedule. As per the report in PTI, the actor will be undergoing surgery to remove an implant in his legs. Due to an injury in 2016, Kamal Haasan had suffered a fracture following which he placed an implant in his leg. The news was confirmed by his political party’s vice president.

Vice president of Makkal Needhi Maiam, a political party founded by Kamal Haasan, Dr. R Mahendran confirming the news said, “Upon his doctor's advice Kamal Haasan will be getting admitted in the hospital for the surgical procedure on November 22 to remove the implant. Furthermore, he has been compelled to take a break for the next few weeks to physically recoup.” Therefore a lot of his political as well as his film commitments shall be pushed ahead.
