IT Writer Admits a Supercut Would Be Cool, But Unnecessary

21 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Sundi Rose

IT and IT: Chapter Two were extremely popular contributions to the horror genre, but writer Gary Dauberman thinks a supercut of the latter, while interesting in theory, wouldn't be all that necessary.

"I know Andy [Muschietti] has an idea about a 'supercut' which I would love to see but we cut things for a reason so if there was something I desperately wanted to see or wanted put in there, we could have found a way to put it in," he told ComicBookMovie.com. "That's not to say that the stuff that's not in there isn't great but we put the stuff that's in the movie there because that's what makes it better."
