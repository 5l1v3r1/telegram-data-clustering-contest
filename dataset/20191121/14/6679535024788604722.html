M.A.M.R. Muthiah elected Madras Race Club chairman

21 Nov 2019, 14:51

M.A.M.R Muthiah was elected chairman and senior steward of the Madras Race Club (MRC)on Thursday by the 12-member committee of management that was elected at the annual general meeting of the club held here on Wednesday. Ramesh Rangarajan was elected chairman of the Board of Appeal.

Committee of Management: M.A.M.R. Muthiah (chairman), Arun Alagappan, Chaduranga Kanthraj Urs, T. Dhevanathan Yadav, K. Kaliyaperumal, SP Lakshmanan, A.C. Muthiah, Paul Antony, Rm. Ramaswamy, Ramesh Rangarajan, M. Ravi and K. Shashabindu Das.

Stewards: M.A.M.R. Muthiah (senior steward), Arun Alagappan, Chaduranga Kanthraj Urs, T. Dhevanathan Yadav, Paul Antony and K. Shashabindu Das.

Board of Appeal: Ramesh Rangarajan (chairman), K. Kaliyaperumal, F.R.A. Nathan, Rajendra Mootha and Rm. Ramaswamy.
