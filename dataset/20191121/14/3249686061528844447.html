AKB48's General Manager shows her support for IZ*ONE

21 Nov 2019, 14:34 by honeycider

During the November 19 broadcast of 'Saku no Ki' on "bayfm", which Sakura Miyawaki regularly hosts, AKB48's general manager, Mukaichi Mion, filled in as the hoston Sakura's behalf. Bayfm stated that Sakura was unable to participate in this broadcast due to other IZ*ONE activities.

During the broadcast, Mion read supportive letters from fans all over the world for the members of IZ*ONE.

After the broadcast, Mion posted a tweet stating "Thank you to everyone who listened to yesterday's broadcast. May everyone's feelings reach the 12 members." Then ended the tweet with the hashtags #Saku no Ki #WeTogetherWithIZONE.
