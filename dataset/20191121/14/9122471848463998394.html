Tom Brady’s New Tweet Goes Viral Before Cowboys Game

21 Nov 2019, 14:52 by Andrew Holleran

One of the most-hyped games of the NFL’s regular season is taking place on Sunday afternoon. The New England Patriots are hosting the Dallas Cowboys in Foxborough.

Tom Brady appears to be ready to go.

The Patriots quarterback, who’s struggled in the passing games at times this season, is channeling is inner Yoda ahead of Sunday’s game.

“Do or do not. There is no try,” Brady tweeted.

The tweet has been retweeted more than 11,000 times and liked more than 83,000 times. Decent numbers, for sure.
