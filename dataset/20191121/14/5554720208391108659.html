Akshay Kumar’s fans detect hidden motive in ‘died laughing’ appreciation by Aamir Khan for Good Newwz also starring Kareena Kapoor Khan

21 Nov 2019, 14:23 by JKR Staff

Bollywood megastar Aamir Khan may not be prolific social media user anymore, but the Dangal star on Wednesday made a confession saying that he ‘died laughing’ after watching the trailer of Good Newwz. The film stars Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Daljit Dosanj and Kiara Advani in lead roles.

Aamir wrote on Twitter, “What a great trailer! I died laughing! Loved it!”
