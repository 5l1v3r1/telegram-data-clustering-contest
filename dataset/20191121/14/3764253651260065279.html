Floyd Mayweather coughs out $37K (N13m) to buy Louis Vuitton Flags Fur Scarf

21 Nov 2019, 14:51

- Floyd Mayweather recently flaunted a Ghana flag in a photo on social media

- The photo made some Ghanaians excited as it sparked talk of Mayweather coming to Ghana

- It turns out it was no ordinary flag but an expensive Louis Vuitton fashion accessory

- Dubbed the LV Flags Fur Scarf, it costs $13, 900 and $37,000 for the mini and maxi versions respectively

Former undisputed world welterweight boxing champion, Floyd Mayweather, has been showing his fondness for Ghana in recent times.

On Wednesday, November 20, Mayweather shared a new photo in which he had a scarf partly made of a Ghana flag and the United States flag.

The photo of Mayweather flaunting the Ghana flag went viral on social media as it sparked talks of the American boxing great visiting the country.

Sharing the photo, Mayweather had urged fans to "zoom in" which Legit.ng has done and realized that he was drawing attention to the flag being a designer made.

Mayweather's flag was no ordinary scarf but an LV Flags Fur Scarf, a brightly coloured piece of fashion accessory from Louis Vuitton.

It has been gathered that the LV Flags Fur Scarf sells for $13,900 (N5m) for a mini version which is 35 x 165 cm (Length x Height). A maxi version which measures 55 x 270 cm (Length x Height) costs $37,000 (N13m).

Looking at Mayweather's photo, his scarf is big and will likely be a maxi version but even if we are to assume it is a mini, it means he paid N5m to get.

Earlier, Legit.ng reported that Mayweather has stated that he is done with boxing and will not be returning to professional fight.

This comes after the American was tipped for a rematch with Manny Packquiao in the ring.

But the 42-year-old explained that he is making more money in exhibitions than professional fight.
