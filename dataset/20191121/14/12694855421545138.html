Square Enix hopes Final Fantasy XIV will “one day” come to PS5

21 Nov 2019, 14:16 by Jade King

Update: Square Enix has helped clarify some of the claims made during last night’s fan gathering, providing us with the following statement: “While not a confirmation, Yoshida-san expressed a hope that FINAL FANTASY XIV Online may one day appear on the next generation of Sony console.”

Judging by the above statement, we’ll still be waiting a little longer for a formal reveal of Final Fantasy XIV on Sony’s next generation platform, but it certainly isn’t completely out of the question. For now, let’s dive into Eorzea and grind through Shadowbringers.

Original story follows:

Speaking at the Final Fantasy XIV Fan Gathering event in London (via WCCFTech), game director Naoki Yoshida has confirmed a PS5 version of the MMORPG is in development. 

One of the masterminds behind the hugely popular Final Fantasy title descended on London this week to partake in media interviews and surprise fans at a recent event, which seemed to go down a treat. 

Trusted Reviews was in attendance and witnessed a raffle which saw a variety of prizes given away to fans lucky enough to receive the right ticket. This concluded with a rather snazzy, signed PS4 console being given away by the director.
