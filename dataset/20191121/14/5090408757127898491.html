Netflix is down for people and 'experiencing issues streaming on all devices', Business Insider - Business Insider Singapore

21 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Paige Leskin, Business Insider US
  • Netflix is down for thousands around the world as of Thursday morning.
  • The video-streaming platform is “currently experiencing issues streaming on all devices,” according to Netflix’s website.
  • Outage tracking site Down Detector is showing users experiencing issues in Europe, eastern Asia, and the US.
  • Visit Business Insider’s homepage for more stories.

Netflix is down Thursday morning for many users around the world, including in the US and Europe.

People have been reporting issues playing TV shows and movies on the video-streaming platform, according the outage tracking site Down Detector. The site shows reported outages from thousands in Japan, China, the Netherlands, and more.

According to Netflix’s website, the platform is “currently experiencing issues streaming on all devices.” Business Insider has reached out to Netflix for further comment.

Netflix’s support account on Twitter has also been responding to individual users complaining the platform is down, and acknowledged its service was experiencing issues.

“At the moment we are experiencing technical problems, we are busy rectifying this as quickly as possible,” Netflix tweeted, according to a Google Translate translation. “I recommend trying Netflix again at a later date. Sorry for the inconvenience.”
