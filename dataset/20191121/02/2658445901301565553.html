President, Wike greet ex-President Jonathan at 62 - The Nation Newspaper

21 Nov 2019, 02:24

Augustine Ehikioya, Abuja

 

President Muhammadu Buhari, Rivers State Governor Nyesom Wike have felicitated with former President Goodluck Jonathan on his 62th birthday.

The President joined the nation in praying for Dr Jonathan to have longer life, good health and more strength to keep serving the nation.

In a statement by his Special Adviser on Media and Publicity, Chief Femi Adesina, the President also rejoiced with family, friends and political associates of the former president.

Buhari lauded him for good counsels to leaders within and outside Nigeria since he left office and sharing his experience of serving at different levels of governance.

President Buhari believed the former President’s legacy of humility and patriotism will continue to resonate, inspiring generations to come on the sacrifices made for the stability of democracy and promotion of sustainable development.

The President wished the former President and his family more years of joyful celebrations.

On behalf of his wife, Suzette, the government and people of Rivers State, Wike felicitated with former President Goodluck Ebele Jonathan, GCFR, on his 62nd birthday.

The governor described Dr Jonathan as a selfless leader and a beacon of light for the growth of democracy in Nigeria.

As a statesman, he said, the former President has continued to nurture the ideals of unity and peaceful co-existence, according to a statement by the Permanent Secretary in the Ministry Information and Communications, Paulinus Nsirim.

The governor prayed that God will grant him good health and the divine enablement to continue to serve the nation and humanity.
