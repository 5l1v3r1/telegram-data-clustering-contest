Throwback Thursday! Saif Ali Khan’s childhood picture is an exact copy of Taimur Ali Khan

21 Nov 2019, 02:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Taimur Ali Khan is a celebrity in his own right. He enjoys maximum attention on social media amongst all Bollywood star kids and is a favourite with the paparazzi too. The adorable toddler has the exact nuances of his Nawab daddy, Saif Ali Khan. This childhood picture of Saif sitting on his mother, actress Sharmila Tagore’s lap, is a reminder of how Taimur currently looks. Taimur is a Xerox copy of his father and this picture is proof.

Earlier, Saif had expressed his displeasure against the constant paparazzi attention and told IANS, “I can't say I feel kindly about their presence, because 10 people lounging about outside your house waiting for a shot of your kid is disturbing to say the least.” Saif had also requested the photographers, "Please don't stalk Taimur. He is not a star. He is just a child."

Speaking about the media attention surrounding her toddler, Kareena Kapoor Khan had told IANS, "Looking at the media, looking at those lights..wondering what media is doing. Sometimes they cross the line..especially when comes to Taimur. What he is eating ? where he is going ?...media is constantly following him. Once in a while it is fine, but everyday? He is just a two-year-old kid. One should allow that person to live his life."
