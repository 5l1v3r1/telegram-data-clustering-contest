Zac Efron Is Starring In A New Series Called ‘Killing Zac Efron’, Which Sounds Nice

21 Nov 2019, 02:54 by By Steffanie Tan

Zac Efron, having dabbled in a range of different genres since his High School days, has a new series coming out. This time it’s a survival adventure series and it’s – I shit you not – called Killing Zac Efron. 

Efron will star and executive produce the show, which will stream on Quibi – a relatively new American short-form video platform for phones.

I am very confused by the title but the premise makes it sound like some intense Bear Grylls, Man Vs Wild shit.

The official synopsis is as follows, according to the presser: “In Killing Zac Efron, Zac heads deep into the jungle of a remote island to carve his own name in expedition history. Zac is going all-in, falling off the grid for 21 days with nothing but basic gear, a guide partner and a will to survive.”

Oof.

I mean there was that time Efron went on a very normal and not at all dangerous 48-hour adventure with Bear Grylls himself on Running Wild, so this isn’t exactly a surprise.

Efron literally jumped out of a helicopter with Grylls for fun. Normal.
