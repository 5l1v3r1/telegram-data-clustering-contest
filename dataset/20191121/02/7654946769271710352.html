Reputed ‘Albany-Drizzy’ gangbanger busted with semi-automatic weapon in Brooklyn

21 Nov 2019, 02:32 by Ben Feuerherd

A reputed Brooklyn gangbanger was busted with a semiautomatic pistol and an extended magazine by field intelligence officers who executed a search warrant at his apartment Wednesday morning, cops said.

The suspect, Earl Haile, 25, was arrested at the Albany Houses in Crown Heights when cops found the loaded 9mm pistol at about 11:25 a.m., authorities said.

Haile, a reputed member of the Albany-Drizzy crew in the public housing complex, was hit with criminal possession of a loaded firearm and criminal possession of an ammo clip, a police source said.

“This is the result of our ongoing strategy to disrupt gang activity,” Deputy Inspector John Buttacavoli, the commanding officer of the 77 precinct, wrote on Twitter after the bust.

Haile has a number of other previous arrests, a police source said, including busts for grand larceny and possession of a forged instrument.
