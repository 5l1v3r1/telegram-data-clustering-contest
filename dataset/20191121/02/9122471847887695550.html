Urban Meyer: The Penn State Game Isn’t A ‘Rivalry’ For Ohio State

21 Nov 2019, 02:45 by Andrew Holleran

Saturday’s Ohio State-Penn State game is a big one for the Buckeyes, but don’t call it a rivalry game. That’s what Urban Meyer is saying, anyway.

The former Ohio State head coach previewed Saturday’s game on Big Ten Network this evening. He said it might be a rivalry game for Penn State, but it’s not for his Buckeyes.

“Certainly over the last few years, just because of the quality of team that Penn State is, that’s become a war…my goodness, two very good teams, two very good programs,” Meyer said tonight. “Great players all over the field. It’s become a rivalry in a sense, but not in Ohio State’s – we know which one that is coming up next week.”

Ohio State has one rival, according to Meyer, and we don’t need to tell you who that is.
