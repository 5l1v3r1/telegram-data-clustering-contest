NASA & Timex Celebrate 50th Anniversary of Lunar Landing With Navi XL Timepiece

Available in blue and white colorways.

21 Nov 2019, 18:49 by Gabrielle Leung

This year has seen brands across the board celebrating the 50th anniversary of the moon landing. PUMA revamped its CELL Alien and RS-X models with a “Space Explorer” pack, while LEGO built a life-sized astronaut sporting the same spacesuit worn during the July 16, 1969 moon mission. Joining other watch brands that celebrated Apollo 11, Timex has now launched two new timepieces in collaboration with NASA.

Available in blue and white colorways, the vintage-inspired diver-style watch sports a co-branded face with geometric shapes adorning the edges and a 41mm fabric strap. Water-resistant up to 100 meters, the practical lightweight design features a stainless steel watch case, offering a sleek option for wearing day or night. The Navi XL incorporates the patriotism that arose from the 1969 milestone that shaped American history into a classic Timex timepiece.

Take a closer look at the collaborative Navi XLs in the gallery above. Priced at $129 USD, the NASA x Timex watch is available to purchase now on Timex’s website.

In related news, Timex is reissuing yet another iconic style from the ’70s with the the Q Timex Falcon Eye.
