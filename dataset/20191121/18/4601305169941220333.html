HILL ON SONDLAND 'He was involved in a domestic political errand'

21 Nov 2019, 18:41 by Xeni Jardin

She is going right for the jugular.

On Capitol Hill today, America's former top diplomat in Ukraine is testifying, and the bus is running over heads, backing up, and running right over them again and again.

"He was involved in a domestic political errand," Dr. Fiona Hill testified Thursday, speaking of Gordon Sondland, and of Rudy Giuliani and the other errand-boys of Putin and Trump in Ukraine.
