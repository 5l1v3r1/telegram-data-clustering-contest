RCMP seek help locating wanted Vernon man

21 Nov 2019, 18:59 by Ben Bulmer

The RCMP is requesting the public's assistance in locating a wanted Vernon man.

According to a Vernon-North Okanagan RCMP media release, Allan Clayton Gabriel Jules is wanted on six outstanding warrants for breaching conditions. The RCMP have made numerous attempts to locate Jules but have not been successful.

Jules is a 25-year-old Aboriginal male, 6' tall, weighing 190 Ibs., with brown hair and brown eyes.

Anyone with any information of Jules whereabouts is urged to contact the Vernon North Okanagan RCMP on 250-545-7171, or remain anonymous and call Crime Stoppers on 1-800-222-8477, or leave a tip online at www.nokscrimestoppers.com.
