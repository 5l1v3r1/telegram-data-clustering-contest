Arkhon endorser

21 Nov 2019, 18:26

Retired Philippine Basketball Association player and businessman Chris Tiu (right) reunites with Rain or Shine co-team owner Raymond Yu after being named endorser of Arkhon Premium Building Solutions. Asian Coatings Phil. Inc., which owns Rain or Shine Elastomeric Paints,  also manufactures the new product line of premium construction chemicals that includes Arkhon ready-to-use skimcoat.
