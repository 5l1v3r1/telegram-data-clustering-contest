Leaky QPR are 17/4 to keep a clean sheet against Championship rival's Fulham

Can Mark Warburton's side grab that elusive clean sheet at Craven Cottage this weekend?

21 Nov 2019, 18:08 by Irishmirror.ie

Championship rivals QPR and Fulham meet for the 38th time in their history this weekend.

The Pools are running a special on QPR to keep a clean sheet at 17/4. Something the side has failed to achieve all season in the league.

QPR, managed by Mark Warburton, are 10th in the Championship and two points behind their rivals.

However, they have a far inferior goal difference of -4 and have not kept a clean sheet against their west London neighbours since February 2000 in a 0-0 draw.

But as we all know in football, stranger things have happened - I mean, Brighton are in the Premier League!

So QPR's defence hold out for a full 90 minutes and get that elusive clean sheet?
