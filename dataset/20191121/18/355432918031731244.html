Bloomberg files paperwork to run for president

21 Nov 2019, 18:19 by Max Greenwood

Michael Bloomberg filed paperwork with the Federal Election Committee on Thursday declaring his candidacy for president.

An aide to Bloomberg told The Hill that the former New York City mayor has not made a final decision on launching a presidential bid, but noted that the filing was “another step towards running.”

Bloomberg ruled out a bid for the Democratic presidential nomination earlier this year but has renewed speculation of a presidential run in recent weeks by taking steps to get on the ballot in a handful of states, including Alabama, Arkansas and Tennessee.
