EPL: Mourinho Eyeing One Nigeria’s Super Eagles Striker

21 Nov 2019, 18:03 by Ridwan Yusuf

English Premier League (EPL) club, Tottenham Hotspur Head Coach, Jose Mourinho is eyeing in-form young Super Eagles of Nigeria striker, Victor Osimhen after poaching two members of Lille coaching staff, according to reports in France, Concise News reports.
