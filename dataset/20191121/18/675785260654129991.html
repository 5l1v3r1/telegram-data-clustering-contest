Lewis Bloor snorts suspicious white powder and urinates in street on night out

Lewis Bloor has been seen holding and snorting a suspicious white powder on a night out with friends after urinating in public outside a homeless charity

21 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Verity Sulway, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/verity-sulway/

Former TOWIE and Celebrity Big Brother star Lewis Bloor has been pictured snorting a suspicious white substance in public before urinating outside a homeless charity.

During a night out with pals in Central London, Bloor, 29, was spotted buying the substance from two men in a car, briefly sitting in the back seat.

He immediately snorted the substance from a small white plastic bag and rubbed some into his gums.

Bloor and his friends then stood on the street divided the substance up on their phones, with one rubbing his nose and putting his fist up to his face, in pictures acquired by The Sun .

Another friend even waved the bag around, before the four of them left and headed towards Old Compton Street.

Earlier on in the evening, Bloor was also pictured urinating outside a homeless charity in public at around 10:30pm.

MirrorOnline has reached out to a rep for Bloor for comment.

Bloor joined The Only Way is Essex in 2013, appearing on the ITV show for three years, before he starred in CBB in 2016.
