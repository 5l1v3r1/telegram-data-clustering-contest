OSU basketball: Montreal Pena signs with Cowboys, joins AAU teammates

21 Nov 2019, 18:34 by Jacob Unruh

STILLWATER — Oklahoma State has a trio of Titans.

The Cowboys made one final splash in the early signing period, landing three-star power forward Montreal Pena, an AAU teammate of other signees Cade Cunningham and Rondel Walker.

All three played over the summer for the Texas Titans, which announced Pena’s decision on Thursday. His National Letter of Intent was signed before the deadline.

The Arlington Martin (Texas) product is 6 feet 9 and 200 pounds. Pena chose the Cowboys over offers from Baylor, TCU, Wake Forest, North Texas, Oral Roberts, Texas-Arlington and Texas-El Paso.
