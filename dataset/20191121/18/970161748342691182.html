Katie Price's bright pink Range Rover gets repossessed after bankruptcy rumours

21 Nov 2019, 18:12

Katie Price had her iconic pink Range Rover repossessed a month after getting a two-year driving ban.

The former glamour model’s car was seen at Saxton 4X4 in Chelmsford after it got purchased at a car auction last week.

Katie had been hit with a series of financial problems over the last year, which almost led to her declaring bankruptcy and entering into an Individual Voluntary Agreement.

The word ‘repo’ was marked on the car’s windscreen but there has not been any further information released on what is happening with Katie.

Her £75,000 Range Rover is possible the same one she was in when she was arrested for driving under the influence in 2018.

Katie’s luck with cars just seems to be getting worse and worse.

A witness told MailOnline: ‘It’s a very striking car but we didn’t know straight away that it belonged to Katie Price.

‘But then one of the buyers told us that it was hers. We couldn’t believe how filthy it was.’

The star – who started her own YouTube channel – was spotted getting a couple lines on the Euromillions lottery last month after news broke of her apparent bankruptcy last month.

She entered into an ongoing Individual Voluntary Arrangement with debtors in 2018.

It reportedly allowed her to pay £12,000 a month over the course of four years.

But a bankruptcy petition was placed on the IVA, and at a hearing at the Insolvency and Companies Court on Monday 18 October, Judge Sally Barber ordered the 41-year-old to be served with a draft petition notice within seven days.

Metro.co.uk have contacted reps for comment.
