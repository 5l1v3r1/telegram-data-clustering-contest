TBT: Verbauwhede Downs’ Funniest Goalie In The World

21 Nov 2019, 18:00 by SL Social

Remember former Mamelodi Sundowns and Maritzburg United Keeper Glenn Verbauwhede? The Belgian was rated as one of the funniest goalkeepers in the world.

Verbauwhede was brought in by the Brazilians in the 2012/13 season to solve their goalkeeping woes, but was released a year later after failing to live up to his billing.

He then enjoyed spells with Free State Stars and Maritzburg United respectively, before hanging up his boots in 2016.
