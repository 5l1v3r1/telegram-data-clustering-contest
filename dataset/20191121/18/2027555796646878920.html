BJP expels rebels Sharath Bachegowda, Kaviraj Urs

21 Nov 2019, 18:45 by PTI

Ruling BJP in Karnataka on Thursday expelled from its primary membership rebels Sharath Bachegowda and Kaviraj Urs, contesting the coming assembly polls as independent candidates, for anti-party activity.

In separate letters to both Bachegowda fighting from Hoskote assembly constituency and Urs from Vijayanagara, BJP said, their contest against the party's official candidates was being considered as an anti-party activity.

BJP on Wednesday had asked party rebels to withdraw their candidature by Thursday evening, the last day to do so for the by-polls to 15 assembly constituencies in the state to be held on December 5.Â
