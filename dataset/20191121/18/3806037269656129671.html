Cozy Up in LANDLORD's Latest Colorful Faux-Fur Outerwear

Featuring collared jackets and kangaroo pouch hoodies.

21 Nov 2019, 18:42 by Nia Groce

LANDLORD has released another batch of its signature faux-fur outerwear, opting for a colorful series of collared jackets and oversized hoodies for its latest Fall/Winter 2019 delivery. The Brooklyn-based brand hits on two ends of the color spectrum with rich navy and vibrant “Yellow Matcha” colorways, offering both subdued and bright options for a range of preferences.

On the sportier side of things, LANDLORD’s kangaroo pouch hoodie provides an easy go-to piece. The label’s interpretation sees the exterior livened up with a colorblocked design, while turquoise and orange drawstrings pop against the yellow and navy styles respectively. Meanwhile, the jacket is constructed with a classic, collared silhouette and is coated in one color from front to back, while contrast lining provides subtle variation.

LANDLORD’s faux-fur jacket is priced at $680 USD, while the hoodie is priced at $600 USD. Both styles are available to shop now at SSENSE.com.

Many brands are embracing the faux-fur movement as a means to move toward eco-friendly practices. Last month alone, California became the first state to ban fur, while Macy’s and Bloomingdale’s joined a growing list of retailers that have announced plans to stop selling fur in the future.
