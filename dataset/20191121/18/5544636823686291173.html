Armed Robbers Raid Commercial Bank In Ekiti

21 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Donatus Anichukwueze

Armed Robbers have attacked a commercial bank in Oye Ekiti, the capital city of Ekiti State.

The robbers who launched their operation around the closing hour of the bank blew the entrance door with a dynamite, reports Channels Television.

The TV station said that the Police Public Relations Officer in the state, Caleb Ikechukwu, confirmed the incident but could not ascertain if there were casualties.

It also said that eyewitness pictures showed a police van on the road that had allegedly been set ablaze by the criminals as well as two yet to be identified persons, lying in a pool of blood.
