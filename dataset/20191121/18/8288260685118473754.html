Huddersfield Town face injury concerns ahead of Birmingham City clash

Birmingham City latest - Blues return to action this weekend with a trip to face Huddersfield Town in their first game back after the international break.

21 Nov 2019, 18:34 by Jamie Kemble

Huddersfield Town have a number of injury concerns ahead of their clash with Birmingham City this weekend.

The Terriers , who are slowly pulling away from the relegation zone under Danny Cowley, welcome Blues on Saturday after suffering their first defeat in eight games, against high-flying Preston.

They will be targeting a return winning ways against a Birmingham side currently in 13th place.

But they may not be helped by injury concerns.

Defender Tommy Elphick is out after suffering a serious injury last time out, requiring surgery, and either of Terence Kongolo or Jon Gorenc Stankovic are his likely replacement.

Jaden Brown is also a doubt having suffered an ankle injury and not travelled with the Huddersfield squad for a warm-weather training camp in Dubai over the international break.

Full-back Danny Simpson is also a doubt, suffering a hip problem in the defeat to Preston, though he did make the trip to Dubai, according to the Huddersfield Examiner .

And striker Fraizer Campbell is also hoping to make a full recovery having missing the Terriers' last outing with a hamstring issue.

On the plus side for Danny Cowley, midfielder Jonathan Hogg has returned from his one-match suspension.
