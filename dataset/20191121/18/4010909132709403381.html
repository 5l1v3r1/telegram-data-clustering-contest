Promo video of the Huawei MatePad Pro spotted on Youtube

21 Nov 2019, 18:04

Image courtesy of gsmarena.com

It seems that a promo video for the Huawei MatePad Pro has been leaked on Youtube, showcasing the final design of the tablet. Based on the video, it aforementioned tablet will come equipped with a dual-camera setup in the rear and it is compatible seems to be compatible with a case that features a magnetic keyboard as well as a M-Pen Stylus.

With all that said, gsmarena.com mentioned that it is expected to come with a HiSilicon Kirin 990 chipset, up to 6GB of RAM as well as Android 10 instead of the HarmonyOS. With all that said, there is currently no official news on the Malaysia release date and local pricing of the Huawei MatePad Pro.
