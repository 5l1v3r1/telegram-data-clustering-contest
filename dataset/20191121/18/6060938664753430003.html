Photos: Kartik Aaryan turns Mr chivalrous as he opens the door for Janhvi kapoor and sees her off

21 Nov 2019, 18:43 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan and Janhvi Kapoor recently wrapped the first schedule of their upcoming film 'Dostana 2' in Punjab. The duo announced the same on their respective social media accounts.

And now, they are back in Mumbai and were snapped at the airport by the paparazzi. Kartik was in an all-black attire while Janhvi looked pretty in orange t-shirt with a denim jacket over it and denim shorts. They were all smiles for the cameras. However, what caught our attention Kartik's sweet gesture for his co-star Janhvi. As the duo walked towards their vehicle, Kartik opened the door for the actress before seeing her off.
