Half-Life: Alyx trailer released; free for Valve Index owners

21 Nov 2019, 18:26 by Lewis White

It’s almost unbelievable: a new Half-Life game is real. The Half-Life Alyx trailer is here. 

Gamers with any form of PC headset will be able to play the game. Even though Valve is creating the title for their own VR hardware, those with an Oculus Rift or Windows Mixed Reality headset will still be able to enjoy the game.
