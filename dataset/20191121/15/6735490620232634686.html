Robert Pattinson, Jennifer Lopez, Adam Sandler Land Independent Spirit Award Nominations

21 Nov 2019, 15:14 by Rachel West

The nominations for the 35th annual Independent Spirit Awards are in with “The Lighthouse” and “Uncut Gems” leading the pack with five nods each.

Known for highlighting both small films and high-profile Oscar contenders, the awards are handed out the day before the Academy Awards. Movies made in the U.S. with a maximum budget of $22.5 million are considered eligible for the award. The Spirit Awards have a history of crossover with the Oscars with movies like “Moonlight”, “Spotlight”, “Birdman” and “12 Years A Slave” all winning top honours at the ceremony before taking home the Best Picture trophy at the Oscars the following night.

This year’s nominations include Robert Pattinson and Willem Dafoe for their eerie black-and-white film “The Lighthouse” and Adam Sandler’s dramatic turn in the gritty drama “Uncut Gems”. Also earning a nomination for Best Supporting Actress is Jennifer Lopez for “Hustlers”, who officially enters the awards conversation as does Best Female Lead nominee Renee Zellweger for her turn in the Judy Garland biopic “Judy”.

Netflix’s “Marriage Story” earned Best Feature and Screenplay nods as well as the Robert Altman which goes to the film’s director Noah Baumbach, ensemble cast including Adam Driver, Scarlett Johansson and Laura Dern, and the casting directors.
