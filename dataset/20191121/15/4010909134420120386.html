Apple Smart Battery Case for iPhone 11 series will be selling for RM539

21 Nov 2019, 15:35

Have you been waiting around for Apple to release its charging case for your iPhone 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max? Finally, Apple announced the all-new Smart Battery Case for its iPhone 11 series. All variants of the charging case will be sold at the price of RM539 in Malaysia soon.

The charging cases now support wireless charging with Qi-certified chargers. They also offer up to 50% longer battery life so that you don’t have to worry about running out of battery while you’re on-the-go. The percentage of the battery case will be shown on the iPhone lock screen and in Notification Center as well. 

Design-wise, Apple remained using soft microfiber lining as the inner material for the case. It is claimed to offer your iPhone a great protection. Silicon is used for the exterior design and there is an elastomer hinge design as well for you to slide in your phone without any difficulty. There is a new dedicated camera button added this time for you to launch the Camera app when the phone is either locked or unlocked.

The colours available are white, black, and pink sand for iPhone 11 Pro/ iPhone 11 Pro Max. For the iPhone 11, there are only two colours which are black and white. The Apple Smart Battery Cases are coming soon to Apple’s official website, Apple Store App, Apple Stores and selected Apple Authorized Resellers and carriers.
