Lily Allen sobs over Labour manifesto as she declares it 'the best I've ever seen'

Lily Allen filmed herself gasping for air as she dramatically cried over the promises made by Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn

21 Nov 2019, 15:46 by Emmeline Saunders

In an election campaign marked by the strange and dramatic, Lily Allen might just have tipped everybody else to the post with her bizarre reaction to the Labour manifesto.

The chart-topping songstress, who has been an ardent supporter of the party for years, filmed herself on TikTok bursting into tears over the promises made by Jeremy Corbyn .

"Guys I've just watched the Labour manifesto and I think it's the best... manifesto I've ever seen," she gasped, as tears streamed down her face.

Wearing a black and white stripy jumper, Lily lay back on her sofa as she spoke into the camera.

She wiped tears from her face with her long-nailed hands, dramatically choking back the sobs that wracked her body.

However, as TikTok is a video-editing platform, followers suspected she might have used a filter to create the effect of tears rolling down her cheeks.

Earlier today, Lily shared a picture of her polling card with the caption: "Tories OUT."

She also used the hashtags " #RealChange#LabourManifesto " to spread her message far and wide.
