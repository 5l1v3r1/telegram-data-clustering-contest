Win tickets for Nottingham Forest's home clash with Cardiff City

We're giving away 10 pairs of tickets to the Reds' home date with the Bluebirds

21 Nov 2019, 15:48 by Barry Cooper

Carabao Energy Drink has teamed up with Nottinghamshire Live to give 10 lucky Nottingham Forest fans the chance to win a pair of tickets to Forest v Cardiff City on Saturday, November 30.

Sabri Lamouchi’s Reds will hope to build on their last City Ground win over arch-rivals Derby County, courtesy of Lewis Grabban’s second half winner.

Currently in the third year of its partnership with the EFL, which includes the title sponsorship of the Carabao Cup, South-East Asian energy drink Carabao is proud to support all 72 football league clubs for the 2019/20 season.

Carabao energy drinks, which also come in low sugar versions, are available in a range of flavours such as Green Apple, Mandarin Orange and Original, and are stocked at Asda, BP, Morrisons, Sainsbury’s, Spar, WHSmith and a significant number of independent retailers.

To be in with a chance of winning this amazing prize, simply answer the following question and email your name, address and contact phone number to competitions@wcommunications.co.uk before 12pm on Friday November 22.

Cardiff manager Neil Harris scored how many goals during his three-year spell at Forest as a player?

A: One

B: Five

C: 13

Good Luck and Get Game Ready!
