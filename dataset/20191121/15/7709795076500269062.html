Sporting Life Racing Podcast: Ben Linfoot and Matt Brocklebank are joined by some star guests including Daryl Jacob ahead of the Betfair Chase

21 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Sporting Life

Ben Linfoot and Matt Brocklebank are joined by Ed Chamberlin, Oli Bell and Fran Berry to discuss the weekend’s action, while Daryl Jacob is on the line to talk Bristol De Mai.

In part one, Ben and Matt discuss Slate House’s 6lb rise for falling in the BetVictor Gold Cup at Cheltenham before talking Newbury Winter Festival following a press day in the week.

Onto part two where Ben is joined by Oli Bell, Fran Berry and Ed Chamberlin to look ahead to Altior v Cyrname and the Betfair Chase ahead of what promises to be a thrilling weekend.

And finally, Daryl Jacob talks all things Bristol De Mai and more ahead of his horse’s mission to win his third consecutive Betfair Chase at Haydock on Saturday.
