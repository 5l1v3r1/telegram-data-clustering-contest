We have just picked up the G.R.E.E.N. THE LIFE ALG0R1THM Achievement list.

There are 10 achievements worth a total of 1,000 Gamerscore, none of which are secret.

Name Description Gamerscore
Leave OA515 Start a new game 100
Find Father Cross the Desert 100
Global Worming Defeat Big Maggie 100
Human no more Defeat Dr. Ranish 100
No gardener? Find the exit in the Gargantuan Garden 100
Balance restored Beat the game 100
Cliffhanger Obtain the Wall Grab Power-Up 150
The perfect pitch Obtain the Power Throw Power-Up 50
Walk on air Obtain the Artificial Platform Power-Up 100
Fly me to the Moon Obtain the Glide Power-Up 100
G.R.E.E.N. THE LIFE ALG0R1THM is a 2D action-adventure video game based in a world invaded by genetically altered vegetation.

G.R.E.E.N. THE LIFE ALG0R1THM is developed and published by Estacion Pi.
