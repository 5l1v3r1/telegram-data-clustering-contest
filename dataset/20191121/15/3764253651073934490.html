Young lady rejoices as she survives 2019 without getting pregnant

21 Nov 2019, 15:24

- A Nigerian lady has celebrated an unusual achievement on social media

- The excited lady revealed that she managed to scale through the most of 2019 without getting pregnant

- The post was received with mixed reactions from her followers

With just about a few weeks to the end of 2019, a lot of people are already taking out time to reflect on how the year has been for them, and how they have managed to stick to the resolutions they made at the start of the year.

Many have shared stories of how they achieved some of their goals as it concerns their careers and other aspects of their lives.

For a social media user identified as @Miss_Okikiola on micro blogging platform, Twitter, her goal was fully realised and she was filled with excitement as she shared the news with members of the internet community.
