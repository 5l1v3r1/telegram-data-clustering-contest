Cardi B opens up about her unique vision for upcoming 2020 album

21 Nov 2019, 15:32

- Cardi B allowed Vogue to film her at her grandmother's home in Washington Heights

- In the video, Cardi holds her sleeping baby, Kulture

- The American singer also facetimed Offset and spilled some deets on her sophomore album

The Bodak Yellow hit-maker said she is constantly thinking about her second album. She will be releasing the much-anticipated record in 2020.

Cardi said it will be spicy and controversial. The 27-year-old insisted it wouldn't follow any current rap trends.

She said: "I still gotta go with what I want to do."

Cardi has also evolved into a fashion icon in the industry. She often serves flawless looks on the red carpet and even at her numerous court appearances for the 2018 assault on two bartenders who she claimed were sleeping with her husband, Offset.
