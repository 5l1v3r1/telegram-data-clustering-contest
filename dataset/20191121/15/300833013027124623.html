Attractive Deals on Ultrabooks Can Help You Save about $300

21 Nov 2019, 15:48 by Review Team

Ultrabooks are designed to offer the functions of a laptop or a notebook computer but sport smaller screens like 12 inches and 14 inches. They are easy to tuck into your briefcase or office bag and carry wherever you go. These ultrabooks are quite thin as well and occupy very little space. You should go through the key specifications and select the model that comes close to what you have been looking for.

XIDU 12.5-Inch Tour PRO Touchscreen Ultrabook 2K

This XIDU 12.5-inch Tour PRO touchscreen Ultrabook 2K, as the name suggests, has a 12.5-inch IPS Full HD+ display offering a screen resolution of 2560 x 1440 pixels. The bezels have been kept to the bare minimum leaving a screen-to-body ratio of 85%. It’s a touchscreen display allowing you to freely move around the screen without a hassle. There is a 180° hinge that facilitates rotating to an angle that suits your viewing and working preferences. The processor fitted in this ultrabook is an 8th generation 3867U and it is supported by 8 GB LPDDR4 RAM along with 128 GB storage. Windows 10 Home edition OS has been pre-installed in this ultrabook.

This XIDU 12.5-inch Tour PRO touchscreen Ultrabook 2K is on offer at $382.49, $117.50 off on the earlier price of $499.99. You also get a mouse free with this ultrabook.
