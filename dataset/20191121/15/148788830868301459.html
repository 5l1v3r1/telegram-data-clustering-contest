‘Without Remorse’ Teaser Trailer: Michael B. Jordan Becomes a Tom Clancy Character

21 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Ethan Anderton

Michael B. Jordan has upheld the legacy of Apollo Creed and taken on the king of Wakanda, and now he’ll dive into the world of Tom Clancy in Without Remorse, an adaptation of the book of the same name that focuses on one of the author’s recurring characters. The first Without Remorse teaser trailer has arrived, and while it doesn’t really feature much footage, it does promise an intriguing mystery set in the heart of the military. Watch below.
