Roger Federer: Tennis star cries after Maradona calls him all-time greatest

21 Nov 2019, 15:29

- Roger Federer has been hailed as the greatest of all time by Argentina football idol Diego Maradona

- Maradona's emotional compliment to the Swiss tennis star reduced him to tears

- Federer is set to play an exhibition match against German star Alexander Zverev

Tennis superstar, Roger Federer, was humbly reduced to tears after being branded greatest of all time by Argentina football idol Diego Maradona.

The Swiss international star will be playing his exhibition match against German star Alexander Zverev in Argentina as part of his South America tour.

The Grand Slam champion had earlier invited Maradona to the match of which 59-year-old Argentine great humbly rejected for personal reasons.
