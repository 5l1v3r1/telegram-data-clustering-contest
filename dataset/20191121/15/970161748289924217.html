Myleene Klass's baby is already a trendsetter in Vans and he joins mum at work

21 Nov 2019, 15:25

Myleene Klass only gave birth to her baby son Apollo three months ago, but she’s already making her way back to work.

The little one had the pleasure of joining his mum to the office, as she was pictured making her way through London to the Smooth Radio studios.

Snuggled up in a leopard print sling, the tot could be seen peering over the wrap as he looked up at the singer.

And he is already a little fashionista, as he sported a pair of black and white Vans that were dangling on his feet.

Our hearts are well and truly melted.

Myleene was just as stylish as the tot, as she paired a black fedora and oversized sunglasses with a long duffle coat.

After smiling at the cameras, the 41-year-old later revealed she had met up with Robbie Williams for a catch-up.

Taking to Instagram, the star shared a selfie with the Angels hitmaker to her 305,000 followers on Instagram.

Alongside the snap, she wrote: ‘Just played this lovely ones new Xmas song on @smoothradio. (Interview on the way) @robbiewilliams.’

Her bambino wasn’t in sight, but we’re sure he enjoyed listening to the song.

Myleene gave birth to Apollo just three months ago and confirmed the news on her Instagram.

Sharing an adorable snap of her holding his hand, she simply wrote: ‘1.08.19’.

The star already has two daughters Ava and Hero from her previous relationship, however, this is her first baby with partner Simon Motson.

She recently spoke about her new man, who she jokingly dubbed her ‘best friend with benefits’.

‘He makes me laugh, he’s funny, he’s kind, he’s super-intelligent, he’s super-gorgeous. We’re best friends who f**k!’ she told Fabulous magazine.
