WIN a family pass to The Three Musketeers at The Helix

21 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Buzz Contributor

We have a family pass to The Three Musketeers panto to give away!
TheatreworX Productions return to The Helix this Christmas with all the wit, magic and fun celebrating their 12th anniversary show with the first ever professional production of The Three Musketeers.

A superb cast which includes, Ireland’s premier tenor, Paul Byrom will take to the stage for this hilarious and magical production which runs from Friday, November 22, 2019 to Sunday, January  19, 2020.

Tickets, priced from €17.50 (booking fees apply) and are on sale now from www.thehelix.ie and at The Helix Box Office.

To celebrate we have a family pass of four to giveaway for their Wednesday 8th of January performance at The Helix.

To enter, fill out our online form before midnight on Sunday, November 24.
