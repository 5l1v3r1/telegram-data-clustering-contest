[Trailer] From the Producers of ‘Get Out’ and ‘Us’ Comes Lionsgate’s New Horror Film ‘Antebellum’

21 Nov 2019, 15:14 by Brad Miska

It took me a beat to dig up all the info on Antebellum, a brand new horror film from Lionsgate that will arrive in theaters on April 24, 2020.

This morning a trailer quietly dropped alongside a trio of posters that all tease:

“If It chooses you, nothing can save you.“

Antebellum stars Janelle Monáe as successful author Veronica Henley, who finds herself trapped in a horrifying reality and must uncover the mind-bending mystery before it’s too late.

Oh, and it’s being hyped as being from the producers of Get Out and Us.

Gerard Bush and Christopher Renz directed the film, which also stars Marque Richardson II, Eric Lange , Jack Huston, Kiersey Clemons, Tongayi Chirisa, Gabourey Sidibe, Rob Aramayo, Lily Cowles, and Jena Malone.

Here’s the first trailer that looks to bring horror to the past and present.

The biggest question is, what is “It”?
