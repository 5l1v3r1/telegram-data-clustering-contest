The Crown boss teases 'paradigm shift' arrival of Margaret Thatcher in season 4

21 Nov 2019, 15:20

The Crown’s third season may have just dropped on Netflix – but fans are already looking forward to the fourth installment.

Among the new arrivals expected for the new series – which is filming now – are Princess Diana herself and Gillian Anderson’s much-touted turn as former PM Margaret Thatcher.

Star Olivia Colman has already raved about The X-Files actress’ ‘brilliant’ transformation into The Iron Lady and now the creator and writer of the show, Peter Morgan has spoken about what we can expect when she turns up.

Speaking on The Crown podcast, Morgan made an interesting point about how the Queen’s relationship to each successive Prime Minsister can be seen through a familial prism.

‘I’ve always found it useful when thinking about her relationship with those men [prime ministers] to think of them in family terms,’ the writer said.

‘Churchill was a grandfather as a paradigm. And then Macmillan very much a father. And then you move through all the prime ministers and you get to someone like Blair, who would have been the first son.’

And of course, the Oscar-nominated scribe teased that another ‘paradigm’ shift will occur when the Queen meets the first female PM.

‘[Thatcher] is a twin. They were born just six months apart. They’ve got an awful lot in common, not least their gender.’

He continued: ‘Sometimes the things that you think will bring two people together actually drives them apart.’

Sounds very dramatic, are we sure he’s not talking about House of Cards instead of The Crown?

There’s already a look to forward to in the fourth season – not least the now-mythical arrival of the future Princess Diana into the royal circle.

The icon will be played by actress Emma Corrin – who has already been praised by co-star Josh O’Connor, who plays Prince Charles.

Corrin has already been snapped in action and she looks eerily like the late, beloved princess during the couple’s 1983 tour of Australia.

The Crown season 3 is available to stream now on Netflix.
