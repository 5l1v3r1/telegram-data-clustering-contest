GTA Online Has Triple Rewards in Motor Wars This Week

By Heidi Nicholas, 21 Nov 2019
Comments
This week, Rockstar are rolling out a wave of bonuses and discounts for GTA Online. A number of Adversary Modes are dishing out double or triple rewards, or 2x GTA$ and RP, there's a big Pegassi sale, and a new top prize at the Lucky Wheel in the Diamond Casino & Resort.

Adversary Mode

First up, the bonuses for Adversary Modes. There's Triple Rewards for Motor Wars; the Last Team Standing-style match, where weapons and armoured vehicles litter a map that gradually shrinks as time wears on. Next, there's 2X GTA$ and RP for Hardest Target and Power Play. Buisness Battles and VIP/CEO Work and Challenges are handing out Double Rewards, whilst Associates will be paid a Doubled Salary.

Sales

There's discounts across Pegassi's collection, with 35% off:

  • Oppressor
  • Tezeract (Super)
  • Zentorno (Super)
  • Infernus (Super)
  • Infernus Classic (Sports Classic)
  • Monroe (Sports Classic)
  • Osiris (Super)
  • Reaper (Super)
  • Tempesta (Super)
  • Torero (Sports Classic)
  • Vacca (Super)
  • Toros (SUV)
  • Bati 801 Motorcycles (both variants)
  • FCR1000 Motorcycle (both variants)

Whilst on other items, such as Properties, there's 40% off:

  • Executive Offices
  • Executive Office Renovations
  • Executive Office Garages, Add-Ons & Renovations
  • All Liveries

And 50% off:

  • Special Cargo Warehouses

The Lucky Wheel

The Lucky Wheel, found in the Diamond Casino and Resort, offers a chance at winning GTA$, RP, and clothing. This week, it also has a new top prize: German hypercar, the Benefactor Krieger.

These bonuses and deals look like they'll only last a week, so if there's something on that list you've got your eye on, or you want to take advantage of the double or triple rewards, be sure to pick up GTA Online this week.
