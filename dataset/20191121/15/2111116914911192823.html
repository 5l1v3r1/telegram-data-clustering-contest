It's official: the John Lewis Black Friday deals kick off on November 22, at exactly 7AM, so if you were waiting for one of the UK's biggest retailers to start its sales, the wait is nearly over.

While many other retailers like Argos, Currys and Amazon have already started posting Black Friday deals, John Lewis was being left behind, but that's not the case any more. Now, fans of the shop's guarantee policy are in luck, as this tends to be the clincher that makes John Lewis preferable to its competitors.

At the moment we don't know exactly what deals John Lewis will be posting (although you can be sure we'll cover them all in detail), but we can gather a little from its Black Friday landing page.

It seems the top deals will be on electronics, with images of a TV, a laptop and a smartwatch all present, and home and furniture, with a chair, a lamp and a cushion shown.

There are also set to be deals on beauty items, clothes, toys, and sports and leisure items, although the appropriate icons are smaller than seen in the electrical and home and furniture sections, suggesting the deals won't be as prolific.

We'll know for sure what deals John Lewis posts tomorrow morning, when they're all put live. We'll make sure to bring you the best deals, so bookmark our John Lewis Black Friday homepage to easily find the biggest discounts.
