Hot Gossip: Salma Mumin BLasts Her Celeb “Friends” For Accusing Her Of Doing Shady Things For Money (+Screenshot)

21 Nov 2019, 15:20 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian actress and entrepreneur, Salma Mumin in a recent post on her Instagram page has rained curses on her colleague celebrities for badmouthing her.

According to the Salma Mumin in the post, she is praying hard so that  God doesn’t forgive her colleagues in the showbiz industry who have ever said anything negative about her, without knowing her personally.

It is unclear what triggered these comments from the actress but a few minutes after sharing the post, she deleted it.

Salma Mumin is obviously very swift but we at GhBase.com were swifter to secure a screenshot of the post minutes before she deleted it.

Her post read; “May God never forgive you for opening your mouth to say anything negative about me that you don’t know,”

Salma Mumin also added to her post that unlike the others, she is a very hardworking person who invests her money.

Salma Mumin is currently working on her upcoming clothing line “Lure by Salma”.
