Jo Muir named 2019 Stewartry Sports Personality of the Year

The Haugh of Urr pentathlete took the award for a second time at the ceremony at Ernespie House Hotel in Castle Douglas

21 Nov 2019, 15:40 by Stuart Gillespie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/stuart-gillespie/

Jo Muir was named Stewartry Sports Personality of the Year on Friday night for the second time.

The Haugh of Urr athlete has had a fantastic 2019 representing Pentathlon GB, achieving career best finishes in both the European and World championships.

The full list of winners at Ernespie House Hotel in Castle Douglas was as follows:

■ Sports Personality of the Year, sponsored by the Galloway News: Jo Muir (pentathlon)

 

■ Junior Sports Personality of the Year, sponsored by the The Co-operative: Rosie Porter (athletics)

■ Team of the year, sponsored by BSW Timber, Dalbeattie: Dalbeattie High School’s gymnastics team

■ Club of the year, sponsored by Sulwarth Brewery. Stewartry Sharks (rugby)

■ Coach/Administrator of the Year, sponsored by Drew Low, Castle Douglas: Mark Geddes (football)

■ Outstanding Achievement at club level (senior), sponsored by H Smith and Sons: David Houston (mountainbiking)

■ Outstanding Achievement at club level (junior), sponsored by James Smith Fencing: Ailsa Brannock (golf)

 

■ Newcomer to Sport award, sponsored by Helen Bell: Rebecca Boardman (ball hockey)

■ Service to Sport award, sponsored by the Scottish Association of Local Sports Councils: Kirsty and Michael Riddick (rallying)
