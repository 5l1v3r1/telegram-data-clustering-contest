Netflix DOWN – app goes offline as thousands of users around world moan about mystery outage

21 Nov 2019, 15:09 by , Digital Technology and Science Editor

NETFLIX has stopped working for thousands of users around the world.

The surprise outage began on Thursday afternoon, leaving users unable to watch their favourite TV shows and movies.

A Netflix spokesperson told The Sun: "We're sorry for the inconvenience and we're working to fix this as quickly as possible."
