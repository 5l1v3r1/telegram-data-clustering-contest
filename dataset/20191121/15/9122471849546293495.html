Coach Cal’s Message For Kentucky Fans Amid Early-Season Struggles

21 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Tzvi Machlin

It’s been a rough couple of weeks for Kentucky to say the least. After suffering a stunning home upset to Evansville last Tuesday, the Wildcats were taken to the limit by Utah Valley this past Monday.

As a result, fans are understandably nervous to see their team struggling against the mid-majors this early in the season.

But Coach Cal has a message for the fans: This is what we do every year.

Taking to Twitter on Wednesday, Calipari outlined the process that the team goes through to become better as the season progresses. He explained that it’s the same method he has been using for years.
