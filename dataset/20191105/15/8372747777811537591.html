Harlem Globetrotters coming to Southwest Florida

05 Nov 2019, 15:37 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. — Get ready for a memorable night the world-famous Harlem Globetrotters are coming back to Southwest Florida.

From a live world record attempt to a glow in the dark performance. The Globetrotters "pushing the limits" tour will create even more significant moments and memories.

The show happens on December 12th at Hertz Arena.
