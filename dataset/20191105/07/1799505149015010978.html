Chocolate Kiss Dispute

05 Nov 2019, 07:45 by BRIEF

HERSHEY, Pa. – A federal court in Pennsylvania ruled against a Ukranian business in a trademark lawsuit brought by Hershey’s relating to its “Kiss” chocolates. The defendant has been using the internet domain kisschocolaterie.com to sell its own chocolate confections.
