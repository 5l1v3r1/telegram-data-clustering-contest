New Meyiwa Update: ‘The Family Deserves Justice’

05 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Soccer Laduma

Advocate Gerrie Nel, the head of AfriForum’s Private Prosecution Unit, has been appointed as the Meyiwa family’s advocate in the murder investigation into the death of former Orlando Pirates and Bafana Bafana goalkeeper Senzo Meyiwa.

AfriForum made the announcement in a press briefing on Tuesday. Since the death of the former shot-stopper in a fatal shooting incident in Vosloorus in 2014, there has been no progress in the investigation.

To date, no conviction nor sentencing has taken place since incident.
