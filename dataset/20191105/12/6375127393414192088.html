NPA member gunned down in front of wife

05 Nov 2019, 12:19 by Gil Aman

A MEMBER of the February Six Movement (FSM) on Monday was shot dead in front of his wife in Cabuyao City, Laguna.

Slain was NPA member Reynaldo Malaborbor, 61,  of Blk 1, Lot 31 and 32, Masinop St., Bgy. Banay-Banay, Cabuyao City, Laguna Police Provincial Director Col. Eleazar Matta said.

Malaborbor died on the spot from  gunshot wounds in the head.

Cabuyao police chief Lt. Col. Raycon Garduque said the victim was with his wife walking  home at  Katapatan Homes, Bgy. Banay-Banay when approached from behind by unidentified armed suspects and shot at 9:30 p.m. on Monday.

Malaborbor, a labor union coordinator, was known to his comrades as Kumander Ace.

The gunmen fled on a motorcycle driven by a cohort.

A source said the victim was a friend of Laguna 4th district Rep.  Benjie Agarao and Pakil, Laguna Mayor Vince Soriano.

Cabuyao police are investigating the killing.

3 MORE REBELS DIE IN QUEZON CLASH

Three suspected NPA members yesterday were killed in an encounter with government forces in  Mauban, Quezon.

The identities of the slain rebels were not yet available.

According to Col. Alex Rillera, commander of the 202nd Infantry Brigade, elements of the Philippine Army’s 1st Infantry Battalion were patrolling in Bgy. Abu Abu in the town when they encountered the rebels.
