Pirates Fear The Worst For Mntambo

05 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Soccer Laduma

Orlando Pirates have revealed that Linda Mntambo could miss the team’s match tonight, against Maritzburg United at Orlando Stadium.

Following his Man of the Match performance against Highlands Park last Tuesday, Mntambo was a notable absentee from the team’s line-up against Kaizer Chiefs in the Telkom Knockout Soweto Derby at the weekend.

The Buccaneers have confirmed that the 30-year-old central midfielder is set to undergo a late fitness test for the injury he picked against the Lions of the North.

“Meanwhile, Linda Mntambo looks set to miss this encounter as he struggles to shake off an injury he sustained in his Man of the Match performance over Highlands Park last week. The enterprising midfielder is due to undergo a late fitness test this morning to determine his readiness,” Pirates confirmed on the club’s official website.
