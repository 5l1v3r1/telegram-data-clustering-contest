Photos: Ranbir Kapoor's dinner outing with Rishi Kapoor, Neetu Kapoor and Riddhima

05 Nov 2019, 19:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ranbir Kapoor has several films lined up ahead and on Tuesday night the actor time off from his busy schedule to be with his family.

The actor was snapped as he stepped out with his father Rishi Kapoor, mother Neetu Kapoor, sister Riddhima Kapoor Sahni with her daughter. It was the first time that the whole family was snapped together.

For the night, Ranbir and Rishi were twinning in blue while Neetu opted for a black and Riddhima was in pink. The family was all smiles for the pictures. The 'Jagga Jassoos' actor looked cool as he was sporting glasses.
