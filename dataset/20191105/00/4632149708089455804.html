NASSCOM product conclave in Bengaluru from today

05 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

The National Association of Software and Services Companies (NASSCOM) is hosting its flagship event NASSCOM Product Conclave (NPC) in the city from Tuesday.

The 16th edition of the annual event, will highlight the theme “10X Challenge: Scale@Speed” and focus on creatively enhancing businesses.

The 10X Challenge entails the vision held by the product sector to drive its growth.

NPC 2019 will hold a myriad of roundtables, plenaries and curated sessions.

“This means that companies would have to expand rapidly and grow their products to capitalise on emerging opportunities ,” NASSCOM stated.
