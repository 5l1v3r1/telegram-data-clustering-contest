Free Entry For Harambee Starlets, Zambia Olympics Qualifier

05 Nov 2019, 08:59 by Bonface Osano

Football Kenya Federation has moved to waive gate charges for Friday’s Olympics Qualifier pitting the Harambee Starlets against Zambia.

Starlets will be looking to gain an advantage in the first leg as they seek qualification for the 2020 Olympics in Tokyo.

The match has been slated for Friday, November 8, 2019, with the second leg set to be played on November 11, 2019 in Lusaka.
