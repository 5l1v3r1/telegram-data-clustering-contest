David Moyes has revealed how close he came to landing the Aston Villa job.

Moyes was understood to have been on the shortlist to take over at Villa Park following the club's relegation to the Championship in 2016.

The Scot eventually linked up with Sunderland and oversaw a relegation campaign that took them out of the Premier League following several near-misses.
