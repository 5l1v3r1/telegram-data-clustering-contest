Saracens docked 35 points and fined £5m for breaching salary caps

05 Nov 2019, 11:05 by Joe Curtis

Premiership and European rugby champions Saracens have been docked 35 points for breaking salary caps.

Saracens must also pay a £5m fine after a robe into investment partnerships between its chairman, Nigel Wray, and some players, according to multiple reports.
