Technical error for flight information display screens at klia2 still being fixed, says MAHB

05 Nov 2019, 06:03 by Justin Lim

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 5): The recovery process of the flight information display screen error is still ongoing at klia2, said Malaysia Airports Holdings Bhd (MAHB) in its Twitter post today.

MAHB said its IT team is working hard to fix the technical error for the flight information display screens at klia2. Of the screens affected, some of them have been backed up.

The airport operator urged passengers to approach its ground staff for assistance for the time being.

In a previous post, MAHB said the team was rectifying the technical error that has impacted several flight information display system screens at klia2.

At the noon break, shares in MAHB were 10 sen or 1.23% lower at RM8.04, giving it a market capitalisation of RM13.32 billion. The counter saw some 268,800 shares traded.
