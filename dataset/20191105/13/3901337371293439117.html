Incredible Messi Stat Makes Mockery Of All-Time Greats

05 Nov 2019, 13:27 by SL International

After Lionel Messi scored his 608th FC Barcelona goal and overtook rival Cristiano Ronaldo's all-time club goalscoring tally, an incredible statistic has emerged about the Argentina forward's record numbers that makes a mockery of some of the game's great players.

Towards the end of last month, Messi netted a brace in Barcelona's 5-1 win over Real Valladolid, and in doing so, surpassed Ronaldo's tally of 606 club goals despite having played 118 fewer matches.

The 32-year-old scored again this past weekend, during the Blaugrana's shock defeat to Levante, to take his tally to 609, and after that match, an incredible statistic emerged surrounding the nature of his goals.

According to ESPN, who cited Gracenote, the Argentina international's penalty goal against Levante was the 500th time he had scored with his left foot for Barca, which amounts to 82.1% of all of his strikes for the club.
