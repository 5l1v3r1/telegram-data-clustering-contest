Military Mail holiday deadline for ground service is this week

05 Nov 2019, 13:48 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- You need to get a move on holiday shipping if you're sending anything to a military or diplomatic post office.

Some United States Post Office holiday deadlines are rapidly approaching.

The deadline is Wednesday.

For regular ground service, it's December 14th.

For priority mail its December 21st.

Keep in mind the busiest time for the post office starts about two weeks before Christmas.
