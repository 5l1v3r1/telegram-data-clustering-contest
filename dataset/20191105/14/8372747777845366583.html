Collier County middle school student charged with making threat at school

05 Nov 2019, 14:12 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- A Golden Gate Middle School student is facing charges for making a violent threat toward the school.

Collier County deputies say the 13-year-old girl is responsible for writing a threat on the school wall last week.

She is charged with written threats to kill or do bodily injury.

Deputies want to remind everyone that if you see something concerning, report it to law enforcement.
