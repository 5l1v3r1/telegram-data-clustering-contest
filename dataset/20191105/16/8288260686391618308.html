'He will be off' - Rangers fans divided on latest striker update amid Aston Villa interest

Aston Villa transfer latest | Villa continued to be linked with Rangers striker Alfredo Morelos, who is in stunning goal-scoring form for the Ibrox club after a League Cup double at the weekend

05 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Josh Murray

Rangers fans are having their say on the future of in-form striker Alfredo Morelos, as speculation continues to link him with Aston Villa.

The Colombian took his eye-catching tally for 2019-20 up to 20 goals from 24 matches on Saturday, netting twice in a 5-2 Scottish League Cup win over Hearts.

Gers boss Steven Gerrard responded to talk of a potential move away from Ibrox for Morelos on BT Sport recently, saying: "I'm not confident, I'm sure. I'm 100 per cent sure he's not going anywhere."

Former Rangers and Wolverhampton Wanderers midfielder Alex Rae took to Twitter after Gerrard's comments to celebrate the hopes of Morelos staying in the Scottish Premiership, amid the Villa rumours.
