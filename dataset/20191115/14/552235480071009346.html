Two cars cordoned off in Kilmarnock with police tape after crash on busy road

There appears to have been a collision between the vehicles near the Ayrshire Athletics Arena, heading towards the Bellfield Interchange.

15 Nov 2019, 14:02 by Lochlin Highet, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/lochlin-highet/

Two cars have been cordoned off with police tape on Kilmarnock's Queen's Drive this afternoon.

There appears to have been a collision between the vehicles near the Ayrshire Athletics Arena, heading towards the Bellfield Interchange.

Both cars are sealed off with police tape and an ambulance car is on the scene.

The road is not shut as it stands as they are both pulled into the side of the road, but traffic is slower than normal around the incident.

Police Scotland have been contacted for comment.
