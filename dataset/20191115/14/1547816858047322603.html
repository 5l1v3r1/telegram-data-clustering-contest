Bigg Boss 13: Mahira Sharma Is ‘Naagin’ And Paras Chhabra The ‘Naag’ Says BB 3 Winner Vindu Dara Singh

15 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Lubna Khan

Ex-Bigg Boss contestant Vindu Dara Singh has expressed his opinion on Bigg Boss 13 contestants in a series of tweets and has supported Sidharth Shukla and also explained his fight with Asim Riaz. Not just this, he also called Mahira Sharma a Naagin, while he thinks Paras Chhabra is the Naag

Bigg Boss 13 has now become synonymous with drama and fights. This season saw a lot of ex-contestants of Bigg Boss give their views and express their opinions on Big Boss 13 contestants. Among those are Gauahar Khan, Dolly Bindra, Kamya Panjabi and others. Now, Bigg Boss 3 winner Vindu Dara Singh took to Twitter and has expressed his opinion on Bigg Boss 13 contestants in a series of tweets. He has supported Sidharth Shukla and also explained his fight with Asim Riaz. Not just this, he also called Mahira Sharma a Naagin, while he thinks Paras Chhabra is the Naag.

Recently, Sidharth Shukla had become a hot topic of conversation, especially after his aggression with Mahira Sharma, and his constant fights with Rashami Desai. Host Salman Khan had supported Sidharth Shukla and now Vindu Dara Singh has supported him too.
