'Better deal' - Update on Luciano Acosta's transfer link with Derby County

Derby County transfer news | Wayne Rooney has reportedly told his former DC United team-mate to join him at Pride Park in January

15 Nov 2019, 14:13 by Mark Wakefield

There has been an update on the reported interest from Derby County in DC United midfielder Luciano Acosta .

In an interview in Argentina, Acosta claimed his former DC United team-mate, Derby-bound Wayne Rooney, had told him to join him at Pride Park.

Rooney joins the Rams as a player-coach in January after spending nearly two years in the United States with the MLS outfit.

Acosta has also called time on his DC United career, and is looking for a new club.

Fresh reports in The Athletic claim that Acosta is “waiting for the call” from Derby over a potential move, but there has been no sign from the Rams that deal could take place.

The report also adds that Acosta could be “leveraging his situation in order to get a better deal”, with Boca Juniors also reportedly in the running for his signature.
