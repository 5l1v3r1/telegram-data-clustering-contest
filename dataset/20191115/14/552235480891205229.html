Get four months of Amazon Music Unlimited for just 99p

For less than £1 you can enjoy non-stop, ad-free music until March!

15 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Linda Howard

Amazon is offering new customers the chance to enjoy four months of non-stop, ad-free streaming on its Amazon Music Unlimited service for just 99 pence.

Music-lovers usually have to shell out £9.99 for the service (or £7.99 for Amazon Prime members) - so this offer will save you a whopping £38.97 over the four-month period. 

Amazon Music Unlimited gives listeners unlimited access to over 50 million songs, all on-demand and ad-free. You can also listen to music offline with unlimited skips and enjoy hands-free listening with Alexa-enabled devices.

So, whether it’s the daily commute or a road trip over the festive season, Amazon Music Unlimited can go with you.

The 99p offer is available to all new Amazon Music Unlimited customers and if you’re already signed up to another streaming service such as Apple Music, Spotify or YouTube Music  - this is the perfect opportunity to try it out.

So, why not enjoy four months of unlimited tunes for just 99p and set a reminder to cancel your subscription before the trial period ends and you won’t be charged a penny. 

The offer is available until January 6, 2020.
