Donald Trump is coming to the UK again in two weeks' time

15 Nov 2019, 14:14

The White House has announced that US President Donald Trump will travel to the UK again on December 2.

He will be here for a two-day Nato Summit in London which starts on December 3.

Melania Trump will accompany him during the visit, which will include a reception at Buckingham Palace with the Queen.

It will come just ten days before the General Election on December 12.

The last time Mr Trump came to the UK was in June, when he was honoured with a full state visit.

He was given a ceremonial welcome at Buckingham Palace and shown around Westminister Abbey.

There was a state banquet in the palace ballroom, during which the president praised the ‘eternal friendship’ of the US and Britain.
