Hallmark Movies job: Watch 24 movies before Christmas Day, get $1,000

15 Nov 2019, 14:56 by Natalie Dreier, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

Many people already do it for free, but how would you like to snuggle up with a glass of wine (or your favorite beverage) and binge-watch 24 of the 40 new Hallmark movies in the 12 days before Christmas Day and earn $1,000?

It's really a job, and Century Link Quote is looking for the perfect person for the gig.

You have to be 18 or older and know how to use Instagram, Twitter, Facebook and use them to document your viewing habits, sharing your honest opinions on each of the 24 movies to your followers with the hashtag #CountdowntoChristmas and #HallmarkDreamJob in each post.

You also should submit a 2- to 3-minute video showing all of your Christmas cheer.

If chosen, Century Link says you'll get a subscription to Hallmark Movies Channel streaming service, cocoa, Christmas cookies, twinkling lights, a mini Christmas tree and other Hallmark-branded gifts.
