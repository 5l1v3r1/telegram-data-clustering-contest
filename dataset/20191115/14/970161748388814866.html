Donald Trump cracked joke about beauty queens with Ukrainian president

15 Nov 2019, 14:55

Donald Trump told the president of Ukraine that his country was ‘very well represented’ at the Miss Universe pageant.

The President of the United States cracked the joke with Volodymyr Zelensky during a phone call in April, a memo from which was released on Friday.

Trump responded to Zelensky’s positive comments about Ukraine by saying: ‘Well, I agree with you about our country, and I look forward to it.

‘When I owned Miss Universe, they always had great people.

‘Ukraine was always very well represented.’

Trump owned the pageant between 1996 and 2015, while he was a property developer and reality TV star.

The White House released the memo of a call between the men as the second day of public impeachment proceedings which could end Trump’s presidency kicked off.

Former US Ambassador to the Ukraine Marie Yovanovitch is appearing before the House Intelligence Committee at the US Capitol Building in Washington DC on Friday.

Trump is accused of suggesting Ukraine would only receive $400million in military aid if Zelensky instructed his country’s prosecutors to dig dirt on his political rivals Joe Biden and Hillary Clinton.

Democrats say that alleged behavior constitutes Trump abusing his office to advance his own political campaign, although the president denies any wrongdoing.

The call which prompted the impeachment campaign took place in July, with the transcript released Friday an innocuous call of congratulations from Trump to Zelensky after he was elected president.

Trump congratulated Zelensky on ‘a fantastic election’ and told his Ukrainian counterpart ‘I think you will do a great job.’

Zelensky returned the compliment by hailing Trump as ‘a great example’ to his new administration, and asked him to visit the Ukraine.

He also stressed that Ukraine was ‘an independent country.’

Russian President Vladimir Putin had his country’s military annex the Crimean Peninsula in 2014, a territory previously held by Ukraine.

Russia has since been accused of aggression towards Ukraine, which was previously part of the Soviet Union.

And Trump has repeatedly been accused of being too soft on Putin.

Critics say he knew full well how important the US aid package was to Ukraine to help protect its borders when he made the call to Zelensky.
