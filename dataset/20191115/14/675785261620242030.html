Woman shares the tactics that got her £900 worth of freebies in under a year

Alina Andreea only started applying for things in March, but in far less than a year she's bagged freebies worth £900 - including designer makeup that would have set her back hundreds on its own

15 Nov 2019, 14:53 by James Andrews, http://www.facebook.com/jameswritesmoney/

Earlier this year Alina Andreea started looking to see if there were any freebies she could pick up on the internet.

Working as a duty manager at a guesthouse in Bath, she was keen to see what she could get from a few minutes looking a dat.

It's fair to say it worked with the 25-year-old picking up and incredible £900 worth of goodies in a a few months.

And now she's shared her tactics as well as some of her biggest success stories to give other bargain hunters a helping hand.

“I apply daily and receive freebies three to four times a week - I’m always following the postman!” Alina told members of the freebie site LatestFreeStuff.co.uk Facebook group.

“Usually, I get the freebies from the Free Stuff Facebook group or from active ads on Facebook.

“I am also registered with Home Tester Club, TryIt and Alba Science. I have saved around £900 since March this year when I discovered the freebie group on Facebook.

"I always feel happy when I get a freebie, especially when it's make-up, as working as a duty manager I always need to look good so a make-up freebie is always a joy - also, the make-up samples are so handy for holidays.”

Alina added that she checks Facebook groups daily to stay up to date.

"To make sure I don't miss a freebie, I have joined Latest Free Stuff’s Telegram group, and I like the Facebook pages of cosmetic brands (for example MAC, Estee Lauder, Clarins and YSL) as there are active ads from where I get free samples,” she added.

"For example, I have around 60 foundation samples, so this means around 10 full size bottles that would have cost me probably £280!”

And here top advice for people looking to get involved is simple.

"Do not give up!” Alina said.

"I always see people saying they are applying but they don’t receive anything or the lady from the counter was rude and didn’t give them any samples - this takes time and if someone says no just try again tomorrow when is a different person at the counter!”

LatestFreeStuff.co.uk founder Deepak Tailor said: “Success stories like Alina’s ... prove that five minutes of work and research every day can add up to thousands in savings."
