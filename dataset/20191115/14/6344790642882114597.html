New TROLLS WORLD TOUR Trailer Is Sure to Bring a Smile to Your Face, We Pinky Promise

15 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Jessica Fisher

Trolls World Tour has rolled out a new trailer for fans to enjoy. The sequel is loaded with new songs, new musical genres, and new talented voices to fill out the cast. The film will feature the return of Anna Kendrick, Justin Timberlake, James Corden, Caroline Hjelt, Aino Jawo, Ron Funches, and Kunal Nayyar. Newcomers to the sequel feature Kelly Clarkson, Jamie Dornan, Anthony Ramos, Karan Soni, Flula Borg, Chance the Rapper, and Sam Rockwell.

Poppy and Branch discover that there are six different troll tribes scattered over six different lands. Each tribe is also devoted to six different kinds of music -- funk, country, techno, classical, pop and rock. When rockers Queen Barb and King Thrash set out to destroy the other music, Poppy and Branch embark on a daring mission to unite the trolls and save the diverse melodies from becoming extinct.

My kids are stoked for this sequel, and I’m not mad about it either. The first one is cute and funny, and I haven’t minded rewatching it over the past few years. Enjoy the trailer below, and let us know what you think. Trolls World Tour is set to hit theaters on April 17, 2020.
