Buckingham Palace incident: Police 'scream at people to move' and clear The Mall

Witnesses have reported seeing police chasing people down the street and telling them to clear the area. Officers are said to be 'assessing a suspicious vehicle'

15 Nov 2019, 14:33 by Joseph Wilkes, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

Shocked tourists have been cleared from The Mall after police spotted a suspicious vehicle outside Buckingham Palace.

Dramatic footage posted on social media showed police clearing an area in front of the home of the Queen in central London.

A section of the Mall also appeared to have been cleared.

The City of Westminster Police have confirmed cordons are in place and "officers are assessing a suspicious vehicle".

They tweeted: "Officers, including specialist officers, are assessing a suspicious vehicle in Constitution Hill, SW1.

"Cordons are in place while emergency services deal. Motorists are advised to avoid the area."

Passersby told how officers screamed at people to move as they quickly cleared the area to make it safe. 

Witnesses have reported seeing police chase people down Marlborough Road.

Another witness has reported seeing "50 policeman rocked up with blues on. They are evacuating that strand."

Twitter user @kayjaysnark tweeted: "Something is going down around Buckingham. Way more than normal police presence and the police are screaming at people to move to 'the end of the mall.'

"They are chasing individuals down by police van and telling them to go. Just saw a police van with 8+ officers cruise down Marlborough Road."

Twitter user @JoeSLeeUK said: "50 policeman rocked up with blues on. They are evacuating that strand... What's going on in Buckingham Palace?"

Rob Richards tweeted: "Somethings happening at Buckingham Palace. Someone's said a cars been dumped outside and legged it." (sic.)
