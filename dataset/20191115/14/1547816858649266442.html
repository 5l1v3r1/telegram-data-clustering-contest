Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah: After Dayaben, Rita Reporter Aka Priya Ahuja To Go On Maternity Leave

15 Nov 2019, 14:07 by Amruta Patel

After Disha Vakani, actress Priya Ahuja is all set to welcome her first kid with husband Malav Rajda. The actress who plays the role of Rita Reporter onscreen in the TV show Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashma will be taking a break from the small screen for some time now

TV show Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashma doesn’t fall short of entertainment, despite being one of the longest-running shows on the small screen platform. Hats off to the makers who have successful kept the show relevant, touching upon the topics in a common man’s day to day life. But of late the show has been in news for pivotal character Daya’s absence for almost two years now. Actress Disha Vakani, who played Dayaben went on her maternity leave 2 years back and she didn’t make her comeback on the show to date, following many speculations and rumours hinting towards her return as well as bidding goodbye to the show.

Now yet another actress from the show is set to go on her maternity leave. Actress Priya Ahuja who plays the role of Rita Reporter on the show is set to take her maternity leave, to welcome her first kid. The actress has been regular with her social media posts, documenting her pregnancy phase beautifully; giving a glimpse of her, flaunting her baby bump to her fans.  One of her latest Instagram posts read, “And the final month begins..
