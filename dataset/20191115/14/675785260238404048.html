Lisa Armstrong grins ready for Blackpool as she refuses to give in to ex Ant

Lisa Armstrong beamed as she arrived for Strictly rehearsals amid her tough divorce battle with ex Ant McPartlin

15 Nov 2019, 14:26 by Verity Sulway, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/verity-sulway/

Lisa Armstrong was all smiles as she was spotted at Blackpool Tower, where she is working on Strictly Come Dancing .

The make up artist, 43, sported a trendy camo coat and chic sunnies as she waved at photographers.

Her happy demeanor comes amid reports she is standing up to her ex husband, I'm a Celeb presenter Ant McPartlin .

The warring former couple are currently embroiled in £62 million divorce battle, with legal letters between lawyers already mounting costs of £1.5 million.

But Lisa is putting her brave face on as she throws herself into work preparing for the Strictly Christmas special and this weekend's Blackpool show.

 

The stars have been giving sneak glimpses into their gruelling rehearsals as the competition heats up, with seven couples left in the competition.

Lisa claimed she was "the victim of a narcissist" as she liked a venom-laden tweet about narcissism and the victims who are accused of being "crazy mentally ill delusional".

Lisa favourited a tweet which read: "A narcissist is always going to paint their victim as "crazy" mentally ill "delusional" imagining the abuse "psycho" emotional.

"In fact, these are the top 3 projectory words that every narcissist uses A victim of a narcissist however never refers to a narcissist this way."

The Mirror have approached Lisa Armstrong's representatives for further comment.

Time is moving on as Ant has jetted out to Australia to host I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here after his year hiatus from his TV duties.

 

It was claimed the comedian was hopeful to have settled his divorce before he made his trip Down Under.

At the crux of their complaint is Lisa's unwillingness to sign a non-disclosure agreement - as Ant is said to be desperate to avoid a public washing of his laundry in open court.

Lisa, however, is relishing the opportunity to finish her marriage in the High Court, and is telling pals she will never sign the gagging order designed to protect Ant's reputation from further damage, according to The Sun
