City of Naples considering allowing off-leash dogs at beach on a limited basis

15 Nov 2019, 14:05 by WFTX Digital Team

NAPLES, Fla. -- The City of Naples has announced plans for a public meeting to discuss allowing dogs on a Naples beach during certain times.

The public meeting will be held on Tuesday, November 19th at 2:30 p.m. at the Naples City Council (735 8th Street South).

The discussion will address requests to allow off-leash dogs on a portion of Lowdermilk Park beach on Monday through Friday from sunrise to 8:30 a.m.

The Community Services Advisory Board will take public comment at the meeting.
