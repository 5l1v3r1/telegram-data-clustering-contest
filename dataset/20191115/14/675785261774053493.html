Corrie fans spot hilarious blunder as ITV announces Sinead won't attend her own funeral

The video on Coronation Street's official YouTube channel was meant to say that Daniel wouldn't be at the funeral

15 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Coronation Street fans were left in stitches after ITV made an embarrassing blunder on the soap's official YouTube channel.

The account featured a video announcing that the late Sinead Tinker wouldn't be able to attend her own funeral in an upcoming episode.

It was captioned: "Ken breaks the news that Sinead won't be attending Sinead's funeral."

It was obviously meant to say that Daniel Osbourne wouldn't be attending the service and it was quickly changed - but not before the mistake was noticed by eagled eyed fans on Reddit.

One Redditor jokingly commented: "Imagine not attending your own funeral! Talk about ungrateful."

"They're taking the mickey now," another posted.

A third said: "At least they've fixed this one, usually they just leave them wrong forever."

The video was advertising an episode where Daniel said he couldn't go to his late wife's funeral and decided to leave Weatherfield.

But he will turn up just in time to pay his respects.

He reads a eulogy and has the mourners in tears, except her auntie Beth.

Beth is still furious with Daniel for betraying Sinead with Bethany Platt while his wife lay dying.

Corrie fans were left in tears last month when Sinead lost her battle with cervical cancer.

She was diagnosed while she was pregnant but had been given the all clear, before she was told it had returned and spead.

Coronation Street airs Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays at 7:30pm and 8:30pm on ITV.
