About 200,000 Households Facing Blackout in Southeastern France Over Snowfall - Reports

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - Large snowfall in southeastern France led to a disruption of electricity supply to about 200,000 households, media reported early on Friday.

15 Nov 2019, 04:24

The power cuts were registered in the departments of Drome, Ardeche, Isere and Rhone, the BFMTV broadcaster reported, citing electricity supplier Enedis.
