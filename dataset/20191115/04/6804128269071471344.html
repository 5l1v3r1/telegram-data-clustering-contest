Where do Spurs turn next?

Once the international break is over, Spurs face a run of seven games in 23 days - almost a game every three days.

It could be a run of fixtures that defines the rest of the season, given how poorly the campaign has started.

Pick up a healthy number of points in the five Premier League games in that spell and the top four chase could be back on. Continue to stutter and the season may be ended before it's begun.

Mauricio Pochettino has work to do on the training ground and here we round-up the biggest stories from around Tottenham in the past 24 hours.

Harry Kane verdict

Harry Kane has not exactly been at his best so far this season.

But then again, nor have many of the Tottenham players.

The England captain has six goals in 12 Premier League games this season, not quite up to his usual goalscoring standards.

But he would walk into Liverpool's team, a team top of the table without defeat so far this season, according to one former Liverpool and Spurs man.
