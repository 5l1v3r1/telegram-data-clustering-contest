Foxconn to invest NT$10 billion in Asia Pacific Telecom

15 Nov 2019, 01:17 by Ninelu Tu, Taipei; Steve Shen, DIGITIMES

Foxconn Electronics has announced that it will invest NT$10 billion (US$327.23 million) for new shares to be issued by Asia Pacific Telecom (APT) through private placement for development of 5G networks and related applications.

After the completion of the deal, Foxconn and its subsidiaries will hold a 40.74% stake in APT, up from 19.7% currently.

Foxconn also said that it will join forces with APT to bid for Taiwan's 5G spectrums slated for release in early 2020, and the two firms will continue to make commitments for mobile broadband business development.

Leveraging APT's 5G networks, Foxconn will also continue to deepen its deployments in 8K+5G, AI, smart manufacturing and smart medical care, the company said.
