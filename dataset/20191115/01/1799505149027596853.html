Super Bowl Commercial

15 Nov 2019, 01:47 by BRIEF

NEW YORK – A federal court in New York ruled in favor of PepsiCo in a dispute with an advertising agency that claimed the beverage company stole its idea for a 2016 Super Bowl halftime commercial.
