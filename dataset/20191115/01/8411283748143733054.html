GSEO sees record 3Q19 profit

15 Nov 2019, 01:18 by Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES, Taipei

Optical lens module maker Genius Electronic Optical (GSEO) has reported third-quarter 2019 net profit reached NT$1.301 billion (US$42.6 million), hitting a record level and soaring 496.54% sequentially and 377.76% on year.

GSEO posted consolidated revenues of NT$5.057 billion, gross margin of 49.27%, net operating profit of NT$1.829 billion and net EPS of NT$12.91 for third-quarter 2019, all hitting quarterly records.

For the first three quarters of 2019, GSEO recorded consolidated revenues of NT$8.265 billion, gross margin of 45.30%, net operating profit of NT$2.169 billion, net profit of NT$1.524 billion and net EPS of NT$15.13.

GSEO's October consolidated revenues reached NT$1.468 billion, slipping 23.88% sequentially but hiking 79.32% on year, and those of NT$9.726 billion for January-October grew 47.24% on year.
