Immigration Detention

15 Nov 2019, 01:32 by BRIEF

TACOMA, Wash. – A federal court in Washington ruled that a Tacoma, Washington, city ordinance preventing an immigration detention facility from expanding or modifying its facilities does not conflict with the company’s federal contract.
