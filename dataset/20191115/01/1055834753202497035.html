Mega Man Zero/ZX Legacy Collection has been delayed to February 25th, 2020

15 Nov 2019, 01:32 by ssf1991

Not too long ago, Capcom announced that Mega Man Zero/ZX Legacy Collection would be coming to PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch and PC on January 21st, 2020. Unfortunately, that is no longer the case. Capcom has decided to delay Mega Man Zero/ZX Legacy Collection to February 25th, 2020. According to Mega Man series producer Kazuhiro Tsuchiya, “it wasn’t a decision we made lightly”. Nevertheless, Tsuchiya also said that “the team behind Mega Man Zero/ZX Legacy Collection is working hard to deliver the best experience possible, and wants to bring you a product that will live up to your expectations”.
