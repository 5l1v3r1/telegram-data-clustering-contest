Econpile gains 1.33% on bagging RM26m sub-structure job in KL

15 Nov 2019, 01:45 by Surin Murugiah

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 15): Shares in Econpile Holdings Bhd rose 1.33% this morning after it was awarded an 11-month contract worth RM26.1 million from WCT OUG Development Sdn Bhd to undertake sub-structure and basement works for a property development project on Jalan Awan Besar here.

At 9.26am, Econpile added 1 sen to 76.5 sen, valuing it at RM1.02 billion.
