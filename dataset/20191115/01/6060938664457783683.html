Exclusive! Sidharth Malhotra on Captain Vikram Batra's biopic ‘Shershaah’: It's a special story

15 Nov 2019, 01:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sidharth Malhotra will soon don the uniform to play Captain Vikram Batra and celebrate the Kargil war hero’s contribution. Excited about the film, Sidharth exclusively told ETimes, “My next release is ‘Shershaah’ which will come out next year. It's a biopic on Captain Vikram Batra who was a soldier who gave his life for our country during the Kargil war and I'm giving out his life story. For the first time I am playing a real life character. We have pretty much finished shooting and have only a few more days left.”
