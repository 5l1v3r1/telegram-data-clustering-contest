Tencent 3Q19 revenues up

15 Nov 2019, 01:18 by Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES, Taipei

China-based Internet value-added service provider Tencent Holdings has reported third-quarter 2019 consolidated revenues reached CNY97.236 billion (US$13.84 billion), growing 9.47% sequentially and 20.65% on year.

Of the revenues, CNY28.604 billion (29.42%) came from online game services, CNY22.025 billion (22.65%) from social networking services, CNY26.758 billion (27.52%) from FinTech and business services, CNY18.366 billion (18.89%) from online advertising, and CNY1.483 billion (1.53%) from others, Tencent said.

As of the end of September 2019, there were 731 million active users of its QQ platform for instant messenger, online game, music, movie and shopping services; 1.151 billion active users of Weixin/WeChat mobile instant messenger, social communication and e-payment app, and 170.6 million registered subscribers of chargeable value-added services,Tencent indicated.

Tencent recorded capital expenditure of CNY6.632 billion in the third quarter.
