Laura Anderson reveals herself as Rangers fan as she begs for tickets for Betfred Cup final against Celtic

15 Nov 2019, 09:51 by Blair Meikle

LAURA Anderson has revealed herself as a Rangers fan - and begged for tickets to next month's Old Firm cup final.

The Scots Love Island star put out an appeal for tickets for the Betfred Cup clash with Celtic at Hampden, because she's "Simply The Best".

She said: "I'm back in Glasgow start of December, it's a long shot but can anyone help me out with a ticket for the cup final?

"For the good end obviously. Because I'm Simply The Best".

Laura's Gers will face their arch rivals in the final after beating Hearts 3-0 in the semi-final.

Celtic won 5-2 against Hibs.

We told this morning how HMRC hit back at claims they were responsible for Rangers going bust - after a bombshell report stated up to £50million could be wiped from the club's tax bill.

In a tweet last night they insisted they "didn't miscalculate anything" and won the case in the Supreme Court.

The shock report in The Times claimed the final EBT bill could be as low as £20m.

If this figure was known back in 2011, the Ibrox club may not have been plunged into financial turmoil leading to liquidation.

But Thomas Wallace, Head of Tax at London-based firm WTT, claimed in a Q&A that HMRC did NOT make any serious mistakes in the case.

Meanwhile, the agent of Light Blues' star striker Alfredo Morelos has fired a warning to potential clubs interested in the hitman to act soon.

The Gers ace has been on red hot form for Steven Gerrard's side this season so far and finished the league's top goalscorer last campaign too.

He has scored 22 goals already this term in all competitions, including 11 in the Europa League.

SunSport revealed that Crystal Palace are among keen parties to swoop for the Colombian.

And now one of the 23-year-old's representatives has told potential suitors to come in for him sooner rather than later - because the longer they wait, the more money it'll take to snap him up.
