ASTRO are draped in blue in 'Blue Flame' concept trailer

15 Nov 2019, 09:03 by germainej

ASTRO have revealed their concept trailer for "Blue Flame".

In the trailer, ASTRO are draped in sheer blue, and a book burns up in flames. As previously reported, the group's sixth mini album 'Blue Flame' drops on November 20 KST, and they're returning with a chic, sexy concept.
