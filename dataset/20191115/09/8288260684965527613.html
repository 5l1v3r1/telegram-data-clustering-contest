Dean Smith given 'green light' to complete this Aston Villa transfer - reports

Aston Villa transfer news | With just over six weeks until the January window opens, rumours are continuing to emerge

15 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Aston Villa boss Dean Smith has been give the green light to sell Lovre Kalinic this summer, according to reports.

It is less than a year since the Croatia international made the move to B6, but he has endured a nightmare spell at Villa Park, with both parties now willing to cut their losses.

Football Insider  have reported that Smith is planning to shift the 29-year-old, who Villa signed for £7m last January.

Villa accept that they will struggle to recoup anything like the fee they paid for him, but are still keen to take him off the wage bill.

The news comes as Villa’s goalkeeping department has been ravaged by injuries. Tom Heaton missed the trip to Wolverhampton Wanderers last weekend, whilst his replacement Jed Steer was stretchered off minutes into the game at Molineux.
