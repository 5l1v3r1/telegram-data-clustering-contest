Man Utd To Sign Two Juve Stars?

15 Nov 2019, 09:27 by Kurt Buckerfield

With Ole Gunner Solskjaer looking to strengthen his side in the January transfer window, Manchester United could reportedly be in talks to sign two Juventus stars.

It has been reported Bianconeri sporting director Fabio Paratici has flown to England, where he is set to have transfer discussions with the Red Devils.

According to La Stampa, Paratici will hold talks with United over the potential sale of striker Mario Mandzukic, who has been tipped to join the Premier League club for a fee of about £13 million (R249 million).

The Croatian has not played a single minute under manager Maurizio Sarri in Italy this season, and is keen to leave the Serie A giants in search of regular game time.

United's chiefs could also reportedly discuss a transfer for 21-year-old centre-back Merih Demiral, who has played just once for the Italian giants since his arrival at the start of the campaign.

The Red Devils reportedly had scouts watch the defender when he played for Turkey against Iceland in a 2020 European Championship qualifier on Thursday night.

Demiral has been capped 11 times for his country since his debut a year ago, and is highly rated, but has been down the pecking order at Juve, with Matthijs de Ligt joining later in the recent transfer window.
