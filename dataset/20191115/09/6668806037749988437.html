Here’s Taking You Inside Karan Johar’s Party For Katy Perry

15 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Vedanshi Pathak

Pop singer Katy Perry is currently in Mumbai as she will be performing at the D.Y Patil Stadium on Saturday. The Hot N Cold singer has managed to keep the paparazzi on their toes ever since she landed in the city. Last night, Dharma Productions’ spearhead Karan Johar hosted a starry bash for the singer to welcome her in Mumbai and make her meet b town stars.

A lot of celebs came in for the Party, from Anushka Sharma, Alia Bhatt, Arjun Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Abhishek Bachchan to Kiara Advani, Shahid Kapoor,  Malaika Arora, Karisma Kapoor, Kajol,Jacqueline Fernandez and others. With so many popular names in attendance, the party surely was a massive hit. Posing for group pictures, selfies or catching up for a conversation, B-towners were seen gelling well with the pop sensation.
