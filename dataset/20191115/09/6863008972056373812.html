Ukrainian soldier killed amid 15 enemy attacks in Donbas on Nov 14

The enemy opened fire from 82mm mortars, cannons installed on infantry fighting vehicles, grenade launchers, heavy machine guns, and rifles.

15 Nov 2019, 09:27

Russia's hybrid military forces on November 14 mounted 15 attacks on Ukrainian army positions in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, with one Ukrainian soldier reported as killed in action.

"The armed forces of the Russian Federation and its mercenaries violated the ceasefire 15 times on November 14. One Ukrainian soldier was killed in enemy shelling," the press center of the Joint Forces Operation Headquarters said on Facebook in a morning update on November 15.

The enemy opened fire from 82mm mortars, cannons installed on infantry fighting vehicles, grenade launchers of various types, heavy machine guns, and small arms.

Under attack were Ukrainian positions near the town of Krasnohorivka, and the villages of Kamianka, Pisky, Novomykhailivka, Shyrokyne, Hnutove, Zholobok, Novoluhanske, Luhanske, Mayorsk, Novotoshkivske, and Orikhove.

"Since midnight, Russia-led forces have opened fire on Ukrainian positions once near Novoluhanske, using hand-held anti-tank grenade launchers and rifles," the report said.
