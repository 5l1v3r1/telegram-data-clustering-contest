WATCH: First Official Trailer For The Brand New SpongeBob Movie

15 Nov 2019, 09:05 by Tara Walsh

Sponge on the Run

Paramount Pictures have released the first official trailer for The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge on the Run.

SpongeBob and Patrick embark on a hilarious adventure to save Gary.

It's due out in cinemas in May 2020.

The trailer begins with the title character opening up The Krusty Krab.

He walks into the burger restaurant and shouts, "I love my life," as his customers applaud him.

We then see him at home with his snail friend Gary, "I love you so much Gary."

SpongeBob then recalls the day he first met Gary.

We're then shown a baby version of SpongeBob finding Gary behind a log.

From that day on, they became best friends.

We're then brought back to present day where Sponge returns home to greet Gary only to find he's nowhere to be seen.

He searches the whole town but he can't find his pal anywhere.

So he turns to his other best friend Patrick who exclaims, "Friends don't let friends go on dangerous quests alone!"

Possibly the strangest part of the trailer however, is when Keanu Reeves shows up as the face of a tumbleweed named Sage.

"I'm made out of sage and I am a sage, so it works out pretty well."
