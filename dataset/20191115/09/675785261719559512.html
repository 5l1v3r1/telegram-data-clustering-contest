Steven Gerrard speaks out about replacing Jurgen Klopp at Liverpool

Steven Gerrard is the frontrunner to replace Jurgen Klopp at Liverpool when his contract expires

15 Nov 2019, 09:41 by Liam Prenderville, http://www.facebook.com/mirrorfootball

Steven Gerrard is refusing to lose sight of his targets at Rangers amid speculation of a return to Liverpool .

Gerrard continues to be tipped to move back to Anfield once Jurgen Klopp departs the club.

Klopp's current deal expires in 2022 and he has so far failed to discuss his intentions after that.

Kop legend Gerrard appears the most logical replacement and is continuing to impress north of the border with Rangers.

But on talk of returning to Merseyside, the former Liverpool captain said: “I am very driven and ambitious and I haven't gone into this management lightly.

“We feel like we have got a really good team and we are enjoying it.

“I get this question a lot about where I want to end up and what is the ambition and the dream - the dream for me is to just win the next football match.

“That is all I need to focus on. I don't need to put time-frames on this or that.

“I am really enjoying what I am doing and I am privileged to be in the position and the focus is the next three points or the next challenge. That is all I worry about.

“It will take me wherever it takes me, if that is to the top, fantastic, but I already feel like I am working for a top club now.”

Gerrard has Rangers level with Celtic at the top of the Scottish Premiership after 12 matches.

The Gers have managed 10 wins so far this season and are on course to mount a title challenge.

Rangers are also sat second in their Europa League group with two matches remaining.
