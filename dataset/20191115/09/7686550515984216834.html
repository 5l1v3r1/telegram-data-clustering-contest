You can buy a doormat sensor that'll blast out Christmas songs when stepped on

Visitors will hear songs like 'We Wish You a Merry Christmas' and 'Jingle Bells' as soon as they step on it

15 Nov 2019, 09:05 by Melisha Kaur, Victoria Jones

If you're looking for a way to spread the festive cheer, B&M might have the answer.

They are selling a doormat sensor that will play Christmas songs like "We Wish You a Merry Christmas" and "Jingle Bells" as soon as you step on it.

And it only costs £4.99.

It is battery-operated, reports the Mirror, and can be stuck under any doormat to play classic festive tunes.

The £4.99 product was revealed on Facebook in a post by the Extreme Couponing and Bargains group, which showed a picture of the sensor captioned "This is amazing for all you Christmas lovers... B&M, £4.99."

B&M is selling a big selection of doormats, like this Printed Coir design (£5) featuring a 'hello' greeting.
