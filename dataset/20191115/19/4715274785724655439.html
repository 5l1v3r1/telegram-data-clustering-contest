‘Itzy? Itzy!’ showcase tour in PH

15 Nov 2019, 19:30 by Manila Standard Showbiz

Globe continues to bring the best of Korean entertainment to the country by partnering with JYP Entertainment and PULP Live World for Itzy Premiere Showcase Tour Itzy? Itzy! in Manila

 on Dec. 8 at the New Frontier Theater in Quezon City.

Rookie girl group, ITZY is a new Korean girl group under JYP Entertainment. The group debuted in early 2019 and are composed of Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna

. They gained global recognition with their single “Dalla Dalla” and “Icy.”

ITZY

 is unstoppable—with two successful album releases and recognitions such as the 2019 Genie Music Awards’ Female New Artist, 2019 Soribada Best K-Music Awards’ Rookie Award, and 2019 Brand of the Year Awards’ Female Rookie Idol of the Year, it is set to end 2019 with a bang through its showcase tour.
