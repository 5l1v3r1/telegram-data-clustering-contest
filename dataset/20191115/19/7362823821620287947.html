Hidden Valley releasing Ranch-filled stocking for holidays

15 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Alexandra Deabler

You ever dream of waking up Christmas Day and instead of a stocking stuffed with trinkets and treats, it was just filled to the brim with lukewarm, viscous ranch dressing? Because you don’t have to dream anymore – Hidden Valley has made it a reality! A slightly tangy, slightly sweet reality.

The brand, which has released Ranch-themed apparel and accessories before, has launched a new limited-edition stocking that is filled with 52 ounces of Hidden Valley Original Ranch Dressing just in time for the holidays.
