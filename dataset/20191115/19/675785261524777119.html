Kelly Brook left flustered after strip tease by Magic Mike crew for her naughty 40 party

Swooning Kelly got very giddy when she was greeted by many topless dancers for her "naughty 40 party"

15 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Kelly Brook got hot under the collar when she was paid a visit by the Magic Mike crew on her birthday.

The radio presenter couldn't believe the naughty surprise the Heart team had prepared for her as she turned 40 on Friday.

Swooning Kelly got very giddy when she was greeted by many topless dancers for her "naughty 40 party".

The personality looked on with awe as the shirtless men danced around her while she sat surrounded by the hunks.

Clearly delighted, Kelly shared her big 40th birthday with her legion of fans on social media.

In a piece to camera, she said: "Hello, it’s Kelly here. This is my naughty 40 party here.

"I’d like to join you in the celebrations, we’ve got balloons, we’ve got food, cake, fun people and we’re gonna have the best time!"

The model gave the men a round of applause for their steamy performance in the London-based studios.

Kelly sparkled in a glittering purple minidress and she wore a glittering hat to ensure she was twinkling in each of the eyes of the Magic Mike crew.

Ahead of her 40th birthday, the bombshell reflected on her dating disasters and her life now.

Having been engaged four times, Kelly confessed she has made a "few reckless decisions" in the romance department.

She added: "I probably made a few reckless decisions in terms of my personal life, too, but they were quite fun at the time – dating crazy people who didn't have my best interests at heart, but… whatever. I don't regret things."

Kelly has had a turbulent love life and has enjoyed a number of headline-grabbing romances including seven years with Jason Statham and four years with Titanic actor Billy Zane.

But now she has settled down and found happiness for the last four years with Jeremy Parisi, 33.

She told The Sun : "I’ve found a new kind of happiness."
