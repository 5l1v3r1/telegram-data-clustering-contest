Notre Dame vs. Navy: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

15 Nov 2019, 19:01 by Chris Rosvoglou

One of the most storied rivalries in college football will have its 93rd meeting this weekend when Notre Dame hosts Navy in a battle of ranked opponents.

Notre Dame has already lost two games this season, but is coming off of back-to-back wins against ACC programs. Last weekend, the Fighting Irish dismantled the Duke Blue Devils on the road.

Navy is riding an impressive five-game winning streak that includes victories over Air Force and Tulane. The offense is averaging 357.9 rushing yards per game.

Ian Book has to be the marquee player to watch in this matchup, but dual-threat quarterback Malcolm Perry is also dynamic in his own right. He currently has 722 passing yards, 1,042 rushing yards and 21 total touchdowns on the year.

The Midshipmen are underdogs heading into this game, but they should keep things close against the Fighting Irish.
