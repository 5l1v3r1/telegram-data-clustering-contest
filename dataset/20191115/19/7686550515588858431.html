You can buy a gift set containing three mini jars of Nutella for Christmas

These are so adorable

15 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Nisha Mal

This Christmas shoppers can buy a gift set of novelty mini jars of Nutella.

The little pots are oozing with the chocolate hazelnut spread, making them perfect for fans of the sweet treat.

The festive gift set is illustrated with a snowman, Rudolph and a penguin, all of which are wearing red stripy hat and mittens.

Nutella says: "Containing two 15g portions in each jar, the trio will make a fun addition to family breakfasts – whether used as a tasty topping on toast, as a partner to pancakes, or an accompaniment to fresh fruit, Nutella can spread some extra happiness this Christmas."

The chocolate maker is also bringing pack its popular super-sized jars (£4-£7).

Shoppers can personalise the label to include a name or a message for someone special.

You can choose from a 400g jar all the way to a 1kg one.

The mini jar gift set is available to buy in Asda, Sainsburys, Tesco and Debenhams for £3.20.
