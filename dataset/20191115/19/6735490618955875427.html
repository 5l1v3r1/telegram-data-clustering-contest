Kate Beckinsale Denies Romance With Jamie Foxx: ‘Standing Next To Someone Does Not Mean I Am Dating Them’

15 Nov 2019, 19:30 by Jamie Samhan

Kate Beckinsale and Jamie Foxx recently took a photo together which sent the rumour mill into overdrive with speculation that they were now a couple.

But before things get too out of hand, Beckinsale is setting the record straight.

“I would like to point out that standing next to someone does not mean I am dating them. Currently in line at the dry cleaners and if that’s true I’ve got a hell of a problem,” she captioned photos from the night.

Although if the “Underworld” actress is interested, Foxx did just end his relationship with Katie Holmes. And conveniently Beckinsale has also just finalized her divorce from Len Wiseman.

Then again, Beckinsale is a strong independent woman who can find a man on her own.
