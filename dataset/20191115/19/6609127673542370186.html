SICK BEAST

Depraved Fife paedo sexually abused three children and violently attacked two women over 28-year period

15 Nov 2019, 19:32 by Justin Bowie

A DEPRAVED Dunfermline paedo abused three children aged between eight and 16-years-old and also violently assaulted two women.

Sick James Whitton Kerr, 41, is facing jail after being convicted at Glasgow’s High Court for his vile crimes which were carried out over a 28-year period.

The beast carried out sex attacks on his child victims between 1991 and 2015 at various addresses in the town.

And he also battered two women in Fife between 1998 and 2019.

Police Scotland Detectives began a probe into dangerous Kerr after a woman reported non-recent sexual abuse he had subjected her to.

He was then arrested and charged on June 6 this year.

An investigating officer hailed the victims who came forward to help police, and called Kerr a “predatory” and “dangerous” man.

Detective Constable Karen Nairn from Fife’s Public Protection Unit said: “James Kerr is a dangerous and predatory individual who subjected these children to horrific levels of sexual abuse and also subjected two women to serious physical violence.

“I would like to commend the victims who bravely came forward and assisted the police.

“His guilty plea is testament to the evidence they provided and the investigative work carried out by officers in the Public Protection Unit.

“We treat all reports of sexual crime – no matter when the offence happened – with the utmost professionalism and sensitivity to bring perpetrators of such crimes to justice.

“Anyone who has been subjected to abuse should feel confident in coming forward, that we will believe you and will thoroughly investigate.”
