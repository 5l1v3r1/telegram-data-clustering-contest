Philadelphia Eagles Reportedly Plan To Sign Veteran RB Jay Ajayi

15 Nov 2019, 19:05 by Chris Rosvoglou

Injuries have plagued the Philadelphia Eagles on offense this year, and the hits keep coming as the team will now be without Darren Sproles for the rest of the season. In an effort to bolster their depth chart, the front office has signed Jay Ajayi.

Ajayi was an impact player for a few years when he was with the Miami Dolphins. He was then traded to the Eagles in 2017 and helped the franchise win its first Super Bowl.

The Eagles thought Ajayi would have a breakout season in 2018, but he unfortunately tore his ACL after just four games. Several teams decided to pass on the veteran tailback in free agency this year because there were concerns about his health.
