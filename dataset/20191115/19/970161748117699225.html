Spoilers: Mandy discovers that Lydia's mum is alive in Emmerdale

15 Nov 2019, 19:25

Lydia Hart (Karen Blick) is in for a huge shock as Mandy Dingle (Lisa Riley) has discovered that her supposedly dead mum Agatha is actually alive and well in Emmerdale. After persuading Lydia to reach out to her sister Beth, Mandy regretted her actions as Lydia just felt worse after Beth treated her with disdain.

As Beth was cold with Lydia and made it clear she had no interest in building a relationship, Mandy was fuming and stormed over to the house to confront her. But when Beth was alerted to the noise Mandy made, Mandy hid and heard Beth explain to her mum that it must have just been kids.

Hearing Beth address her mum made Mandy realise what is truly going on – and she will waste no time in telling Lydia that her mum is alive. As Lydia reels from this, she braces herself to meet her mum but there are more shocks on the way.
