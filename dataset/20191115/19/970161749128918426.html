What was George Michael's cause of death and how old was he when he died?

15 Nov 2019, 19:00

It’s been nearly 3 years since George Michael died on Christmas Day in 2016, but he’s still in the hearts and minds of many fans.

So Emilia Clarke’s latest film Last Christmas is going to be a welcome treat for fans of the late singer.

The movie doesn’t just borrow the title of Wham!’s iconic Christmas song – the soundtrack is comprised of George Michael’s solo hits, with songs from his Wham! Days and a previously-unreleased new track.

As the film lands in cinemas across the UK, let’s take a look back at how we lost the pop icon 3 years ago…
