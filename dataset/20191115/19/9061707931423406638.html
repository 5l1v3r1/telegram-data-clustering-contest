Man dies after becoming trapped under car he was working on

He was pronounced dead at the scene, police have said

15 Nov 2019, 19:13 by Beth Pridding, Ciaran Fagan

A man has died after he became trapped under a car he was working on.

Emergency services descended on Brittany Avenue, in Ashby, at around 3.30pm today.

The man, who was working on the car on the driveway of a property in the street, was pronounced dead at the scene, a spokesman for Leicestershire Police has said.

Nobody else was injured in the incident.

Police put up cordons at the scene, and they remain in place this evening, Leicestershire Live has reported.

People who live close to the scene of the tragedy were reportedly being escorted by officers to and from their homes as they passed the location of the incident earlier today.

A green tent was erected in front of one of the houses in the street this afternoon, and officers were seen going in and out of it.

Police cars were spotted in various locations across the estate, including in Rouen Way and Toulouse Place.

Residents have been making cups of tea for the officers to help keep them warm.

The estate itself is the biggest in Ashby, and is a vast and largely quiet area.

The police spokesman said a file on the death was being prepared for the coroner.
