Chris Evans' Double-Breasted Winter-White Suit Is Giving Me So Many Conflicting Feelings

15 Nov 2019, 19:01 by R. Eric Thomas

Oh, beloveds, I'm vexed. Last night, Chris Evans—who is not so much a snack as he is an entire buffet, like a buffet in one those restaurants that is 100 percent buffet; there is no menu, just a buffet with its own mailing address and a carving station and a Hibachi grill and fresh biscuits, and froyo—wore a white, double-breasted suit to the West Hollywood premiere of his new film, Knives Out.

Beloveds, what in the Sam Hill is this cream-colored Kentucky Fried couture? And why do I love it so much?

Here's the facts: A double-breasted suit is so much suit. How many breasts does this suit have? Double what you'd think. It's the man equivalent of a wrap dress but not as cute. It is nigh on impossible to wear a double-breasted suit without looking like a very wealthy box. Like a box that was given a small million-dollar loan from its father, Mr. Box. Double-breasted suits literally look like the year 1983. Every double-breasted suit has a gigantic pocket for you to store your equally gigantic carphone in.

As if the surplus of fabric wasn't enough, double-breasted suits also have six buttons! What are these, Harry Styles' pants? Outrageous.
