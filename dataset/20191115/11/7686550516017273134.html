Prince Andrew gives a 'no questions vetted' interview about his relationship with paedophile Jeffrey Epstein

It will be the first time the Duke of York has answered questions on his relationship with Epstein since 2015

15 Nov 2019, 11:32 by Emma Bowden PA, Victoria Jones

Prince Andrew has given a "no questions vetted" interview about his relationship with paedophile Jeffrey Epstein.

It will be the first time the Duke of York has answered questions on his relationship with Epstein since 2015, when he used a public appearance at Davos in Switzerland to deny the claims.

The interview with Newsnight presenter Emily Maitlis is set to be shown on BBC Two this Saturday.
