Bajaj Chetak - a closer look at the new electric scooter from Bajaj Auto

15 Nov 2019, 11:29 by Team OD  

The Bajaj Chetak Scooter has been brought back to life but this time in an electric form. While many could miss the two-stroke and four-stroke engines that Bajaj Auto offered in its heyday, the new Chetak has been equipped with an electric motor for its drivetrain. The scooter sources its power from a lithium-ion battery.

Bajaj Auto plans to retail the new Chetak from KTM outlets pitching the product in a premium market space. That said, the ex-showroom cost for the new Chetak is likely to be between Rs 1 lakh and 1.5 lakh.
