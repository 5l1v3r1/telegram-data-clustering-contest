Kalekye Mumo Speaks Out On Illness After Being Discharged From Hospital

15 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Mercy Auma

Media personality Kalekye Mumo has recently been discharged from hospital after a short illness, and if her posts are anything to go by, the ex-K24 TV host is elated to be back home.

On Saturday, November 9, the former radio queen shared a short video of herself in her hospital room clad in a hospital gown, revealing that she had been ailing for the past few days and was longing to be home.

“Super homesick aki…. is they just let me go home?” she posted.
