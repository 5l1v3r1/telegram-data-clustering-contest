Daryl Jacob: Fusil Raffles deserves credit for Elite win

15 Nov 2019, 11:31 by Daryl Jacob

Top jockey Daryl Jacob reflects on Fusil Raffles' Wincanton win while looking ahead to his potential rides at Cheltenham this weekend.

Fusil Raffles will come on a bundle

I was delighted with Fusil Raffles at Wincanton in the Unibet Elite Hurdle last weekend.

I know a lot of people were crabbing his performance afterwards but I don’t know how you can, really, as it was only his third run over the sticks in England and Ireland.

It was his first run back of the year, too, racing against a hardened type from Paul Nicholls’, and you’ve got to remember Grand Sancy is a very good horse, he beat Sceau Royal in the Kingwell Hurdle last year, so I know how good he is.

For my horse to beat him first time out, knowing that he’d need the run as much as he did, I was very impressed with him and I know he’ll come on bundles for the run.

To get that first run out of the way is always difficult, but for him to win like he did, I was delighted.

I’d say the Christmas Hurdle is a strong possibility for his next start. Obviously he won the Adonis around Kempton last year so it’s the obvious race and we’ll go there with high hopes.
