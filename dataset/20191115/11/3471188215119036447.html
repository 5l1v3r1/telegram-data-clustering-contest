Australian Alexa Speakers Can Now Control Dyson and Roomba

15 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Neerav Bhatt

Amazon Australia has announced that they have officially enabled the local Skills so that Echo and Alexa powered speakers can control compatible Dyson and iRobot Roomba devices.

At an event earlier this week in Sydney, Amazon demonstrated controlling settings on a Dyson fan and iRobot Roomba vacuum.

To be able to do the same at your home, you need to enable the Alexa skills for
Dyson and iRobot Roomba and set them up.

Amazon Alexa is compatible with these Dyson connected machines: Pure Cool Link purifier fan, Pure Hot+Cool Link purifier fan heater and 360 Eye robot.
