Surprise! Call of Duty: Modern Warfare was the best-selling game for October and the whole of 2019

15 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Geoffrey Tim

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare was released last month, and has unsurprisingly sold a boatload of copies. According to NPD video game guy Mat Piscatella, Modern Warfare was the best-selling game in the US for October, and the year in general. It’s the 12th year in a row that Call of Duty has been the top-selling game in the month of its release, and that’s not likely to change. As a result, Call of Duty is the best-selling franchise in the US overall since tracking began.

The incredibly well-received (but slightly underdone, in my opinion) Outer Worlds is the second best-selling game in the US for October. That, like with Call of Duty, includes both physical and digital sales. In number 3 comes the best-performing Switch game in the US last month, Luigi’s Mansion 3. That only includes physical sales though, so it’s possible it’d end up higher if digital sales were included.
