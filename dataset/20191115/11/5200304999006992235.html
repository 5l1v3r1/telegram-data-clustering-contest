Overnights: Sony TV leads on Thursday; Star Plus & PTC Punjabi at No.2

15 Nov 2019, 11:59 by Raj Baddhan

Sony Entertainment Television clinched back lead in UK ratings on Thursday, while Star Plus and PTC Punjabi took the No.2 spot, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Sony TV delivered a share of 0.10%, followed by Star Plus and PTC Punjabi with 0.09%, New Vision TV with 0.06%, Geo TV with 0.05%, Hum TV, Star Gold and Colors with 0.04%, Geo News, Dunya News and Colors Rishtey with 0.03%, Star Bharat, Hum Masala, Samaa TV, Colors Cineplex, Sony SAB TV, B4U Movies, Sony MAX and ZEE TV with 0.02%.

On Sony TV, ‘Mere Dad Ki Dulhan’ was watched by 41,900 viewers at 22:00, followed by ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 with 38,500 viewers, ‘Mere Sai’ at 19:00 with 31,000 viewers, ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 22,600 viewers and ‘Ishaaron Ishaaron Mein’ at 20:00 with 20,900 viewers.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ led with 56,100 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 46,000 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 37,000 viewers, ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 35,200 viewers, ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 32,900 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 19,100 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 18,800 viewers, ‘Namah’ at 18:30 with 13,900 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka’ at 22:00 with 6,300 viewers and ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 5,400 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Shakti’ was watched by 26,700 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘Bigg Boss 13’ at 21:00 with 14,400 viewers and ‘Kitchen Champion’ at 20:30 with 7,600 viewers.

On Geo TV, ‘Kahin Deep Jalay’ at 20:00 topped ratings with 29,200 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Kun Faya Kun’ at 20:00 led with 27,300 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ delivered 22,300 viewers at 18:30.
