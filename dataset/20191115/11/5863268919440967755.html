Court verdict exposes Khawaja Asif's lie in NA: Fawad Chaudhry

15 Nov 2019, 11:43 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Federal Minister for Science and Technology Fawad Chaudhry has on Friday said paragraph number two of the court order exposes Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) leader Khawaja Asif’s lie that he told in the National Assembly.

Fawad Chaudhry took to Twitter and maintained that Khawaja Asif Thursday said on the floor of the House that law officer remarked let him (Nawaz Sharif) die. The federal minister was surprised that how people blatantly lie in the NA.

The Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) leader also shared paragraph number two of the court order which states:

“We are also satisfied after hearing the nominated representatives of the Chief Minister, Punjab and the Prime Minister of Pakistan that they also have no objection if interim bail till 29-10-2019, is granted.”
