Box Office: Anurag Basu’s untitled action comedy to release in March 2020

15 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Raj Baddhan

Anurag Basu’s untitled next action-comedy has been confirmed to release in March 2020.

Directed by Anurag Basu, the film stars Abhishek Bachchan, Aditya Roy Kapur, Rajkumar Rao, Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sanya Malhotra, and Pankaj Tripathi. It is produced by Bhushan Kumar, Divya Khosla Kumar, Anurag Basu, Tani Somarita Basu, and Krishan Kumar.

The film releases on 13th March 2020.
