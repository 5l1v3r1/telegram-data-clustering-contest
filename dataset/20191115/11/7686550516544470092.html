How to order a Buddy the Elf frappuccino from Starbuck's secret menu

It's made with peppermint sauce, whipped cream and Christmas sprinkles

15 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Danny Thompson

There any many tell tale signs Christmas is on the horizon.

One of them is Starbucks releases its festive drinks menu and another is Will Ferrell's Elf comes on the telly.

Now you have the chance to bring these two Christmas delights together, with a secret festive drink nicknamed the Buddy the Elf frappuccino.

But if you want to try the Buddy the Elf frap you can't just outright ask for it.

Website Totally The Bomb claims you have to ask for a particular drink with specific extras to get the desired look.

You need to ask for a Matcha Frappuccino, specifically with peppermint syrup instead of the normal one it comes with.

The request it be topped with whipped cream and red and white Christmas sprinkles.

The resulting drink has beautiful festive colours, and has a striking resemblance to Buddy the Elf.
