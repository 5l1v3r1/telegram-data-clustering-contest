Finn links up with DJ Q on smooth 2-step anthem, ‘Baby I Don’t Know’

15 Nov 2019, 11:53 by Henry Bruce-Jones

Photo by: Press

Manchester’s finest reunites with a Huddersfield legend for Swing Ting.

Finn has joined forces with DJ Q on ‘Baby I Don’t Know’, a smooth 2-step garage anthem that marries tough beats with melodic vocal chops and piano licks.
