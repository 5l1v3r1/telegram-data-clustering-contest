Races cancelled

15 Nov 2019, 11:59

After the dramatic incident in the first race, which caused the fall of three jockeys Suraj, Srinath and Kiran Rai, while Srinath had an hand injured, Suraj and Kiran Rai escaped unhurt. The Stewards of BTC decided to cancel the remaining races of the day due to administrative reasons. The Rajyotsava Trophy has been postponed, and the date for the same will be announce later.
