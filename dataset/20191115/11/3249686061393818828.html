BTS’s Jungkook breaks yet another global record: he's the youngest artist on Billboard's Top Artists of the current decade

15 Nov 2019, 11:35 by dose-nel

Recently, Billboard released Top Touring arists of the 2010s. BTS has ranked at #45. This as Jeon Jungkook is the youngest artist on the chart. Jungkook, 22, is above well-known celebrities such as Ed Sheeran and Justin Bieber.

Jeon Jungkook is also the youngest recipient of South Korea's Cultural Merit Medal (received it at the age of 21) a Unicef ambassador, Grammy nominated and the member of the Recording Academy.
