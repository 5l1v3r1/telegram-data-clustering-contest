IPL: Shahr Khodro moves to first place after win against Gol Gohar [VIDEO]

15 Nov 2019, 08:10 by Mir Farhad Ali Khan

Tasnim – MASHHAD, Shahr Khodro football team edged past Gol Gohar 1-0 to move top of the Iran Professional League (IPL) on Friday.

Amin Ghaseminejhad scored the sole goal of the match in the 65th minute in Mashhad’s Emam Reza Stadium.

Shahr Khodro sits top of the table with 23 points, one point ahead of second-placed Sepahan.

Persepolis is third with 19 points.
