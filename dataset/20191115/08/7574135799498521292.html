The Killers announce new album ‘Imploding the Mirage’

15 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Andrew Sacher

Brace yourselves, The Killers will have a new album out in spring 2020. It’s called Imploding the Mirage, and it’s the followup to 2017’s Wonderful Wonderful. No music is out from it yet, but the band says “future musical teasing imminent.”

They also announced a UK/Ireland tour, with support varying by date and coming from Blossoms, Sam Fender, and Manic Street Preachers. All dates are listed below.
