Police investigating man approaching children

15 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Vanessa Schmitt

MANDURAH Detectives are investigating reports of a male approaching children in Lakelands.

Police said the suspicious behaviour occurred at 7.20pm on Monday, November 11 on Yindanna Boulevard and 6.30pm yesterday on Albina Chase.

Police believe the male was driving a blue utility.

Detectives request anyone with dashcam footage or CCTV within the area of the park on the corner of Yindanna Boulevard and Nullewa Parkway and also the vicinity of Albina Chase, Lakelands to contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000 or report online at Crime Stoppers.
