STUNNER OR BUMMER: Priyanka Chopra, Sunny Leone, Nora Fatehi, Sonakshi Sinha Or Fatima Sana Shaikh?

15 Nov 2019, 08:07 by Aditi Singh

Meet the ladies who kept it quite fashionable in the city and those who doused the style flames

Priyanka Chopra returned to Mumbai, last evening. She was spotted at the airport as she arrived from Delhi. Sunny Leone was busy with mommy duties and was snapped with her kids. Fatima Sana Shaikh, Nora Fatehi and Sonakshi Sinha attended Marjaavaan screening, last night.

So, let’s see who were the stunners and bummers of the day:

PRIYANKA CHOPRA- BUMMER- PeeCee’s airport vogue is not the best we have seen from the actress. The black jeans and the heels don’t impress much.

SUNNY LEONE- STUNNER- Sunny’s blush pink jumper is an extremely cute pick for a casual outing.

SONAKSHI SINHA- BUMMER- Something is not right about Sonakshi’s LBD and printed long cape look.

FATIMA SANA SHAIKH- STUNNER- Fatima keeps it simple and elegant in an all-black ethnic wear.

NORA FATEHI- STUNNER- Nora’s fitted black maxi is super hot and the actress looks extremely sexy in it.
