Remnant: From The Ashes, Witcher 3, Darksiders 3 Are Coming To Xbox Game Pass

15 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Alex Walker

Microsoft's X019 conference is basically like a mini E3, so it's no surprise that Xbox Game Pass would get a ton of new stuff to announce. From today, Xbox Game Pass will have RAGE 2, Remnant: From The Ashes, The Talos Principle and Hearts of Iron 4, and later this year it'll get The Witcher 3, Darksiders 3, the Final Fantasy series, and more.

If you're just playing on Xbox, later today you'll be able to download Age of Wonders: Planetfall, LEGO: The Ninjago Movie, RAGE 2, Remnant From the Ashes, The Talos Principle and Tracks. PC subscribers will get Age of Empires 2 and Hearts of Iron 4 exclusively as well, with PC versions of Age of Wonders: Planetfall and RAGE 2 to come later.

And before the end of the year, Game Pass will get Geralt's magnus opus, Darksiders 3, Life is Strange 2, Halo Reach and more:

  • The Witcher 3 (Xbox only)
  • Halo Reach (Xbox + PC)
  • My Friend Pedro (Xbox + PC)
  • Life is Strange 2 (Xbox only)
  • Darksiders 3 (Xbox + PC)
  • The Escapists 2 (Xbox + PC)
  • The Red Strings Club (PC)

Of course, it wouldn't be a subscription service if games weren't pulled from the platform: ABZU, GRID 2, Below, Football Manager 2019 and Strange Brigade are all leaving Game Pass from today.
