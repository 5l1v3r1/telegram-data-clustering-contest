Brighten your desktop with this free 4K Vintage Motorcycles Windows 10 wallpaper pack

15 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Surur

Microsoft has released another set of vibrant 4K wallpaper packs, this time for chopper heads, available to download for free from the  Microsoft Store.

Titled Vintage Motorcycles, the description of the high-quality wallpaper selection reads as follows:

Imagine hitting the road on one of these rare, classic motorbikes. These 15 premium 4k images are free for Windows 10 Themes.

After downloading the collection, go to Start > Settings > Personalization > Themes and click on the theme you prefer.

To apply and change to the most appropriate colour, based on the current wallpaper, go to Customize > Colors > Automatically select a primary colour from the background.
