Work starts on Mayo social services building project

15 Nov 2019, 08:14

WORK has commenced on renovating and extending the headquarters of Castlebar Social Services.

MVS Contractors have moved onto the site at Castle Street car park in the county town.

The project, which will take a number of months to complete, will help the organisation and its volunteers to further enhance the level of assistance provided to people in Castlebar and surrounding areas.

Councillor Ger Deere welcomed a start being made to the project and praised Castlebar Social Services for its outstanding work in the community.
