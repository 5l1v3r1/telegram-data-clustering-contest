PHOTOS: Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh return to the bay after their spiritual first wedding anniversary celebration

15 Nov 2019, 08:02 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh are back to the bay after spiritually celebrating their first wedding anniversary. The couple completed their one year of togetherness on November 14.

Just a few minutes ago the couple was papped at the Mumbai airport along with their families. In the photos both the actors are seen wearing in the same outfits which they had donned at the Golden Temple. Deepika looked stunning in a maroon, heavily embroidered dress while Ranveer looked dapper in a floral ethnic suit with Nehru jacket. The power couple happily posed for the shutterbugs along with their families as they walked out of the airport.

On Wednesday the duo had jetted off to Andra Pradesh along with respective families. Yesterday, DeepVeer visited the Tirupati Balaji Temple and Padmavati temple. Later in the evening, they headed to Amritsar and today in the wee hours of the morning they offered their prayers at Golden Temple.
