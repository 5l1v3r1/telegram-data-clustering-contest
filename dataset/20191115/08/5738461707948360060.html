Lennon Cup final

Three sent-off as Bush PP win Lennon Cup for the first time

Lennon Cup final

15 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Dan Bannon, at Darver Centre of Excellence

Lennon Cup final

Bush PP 0-11 St. Mary’s Drogheda 0-3

Bush Post Primary etched their name on the Lennon Cup for the first time in the school’s history after grinding out victory over St. Mary’s Drogheda in Darver on Thursday night.

Cian Connor guided the peninsula men across the line, with seven points. However, the final will be mostly remembered for a triple sending off by referee David J McArdle. 

Three St. Mary’s players saw straight red cards for dissent towards the official. David Bell was first to go and in the same break of play substitute Cian Flynn followed. Full-forward James McWeeney didn’t learn his lesson and saw the line minutes later.
