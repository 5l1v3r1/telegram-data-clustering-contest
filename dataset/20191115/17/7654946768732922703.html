Myles Garrett suspended indefinitely for Browns-Steelers fight

15 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Post Sports Desk

Myles Garrett was suspended indefinitely by the NFL on Friday following his helmet attack on Steelers quarterback Mason Rudolph.

Maurkice Pouncey of the Steelers was suspended three games for going after Garrett in the aftermath, and Browns tackle Larry Ogunjobi was suspended one game.

Garrett will at least miss the rest of the season with further punishment possible.
