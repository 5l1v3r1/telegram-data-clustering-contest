Dixon, Omar-S, Skrillex remix Kelsey Lu on new EP

Blood Transfusion reworks tracks from Lu's 2019 LP, Blood.

15 Nov 2019, 17:55 by Andrew Ryce

Kelsey Lu's new remix EP is out now.

Blood Transfusions features six remixes of tracks taken from Lu's Blood album, released earlier this year.

Dixon, Omar-S, Skrillex, Lafawndah, Lil M and NAR x ADR all contribute remixes. The "Dixon Retouch" of "Why Knock For You" is only the second remix from the Innervisions head this year.
