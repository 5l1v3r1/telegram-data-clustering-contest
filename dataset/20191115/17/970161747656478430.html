What is the EastEnders Children In Need sketch for 2019?

15 Nov 2019, 17:00

EastEnders is taking a break from the screen tonight in order to clear the schedule for Children in Need.

In recent scenes, viewers were left stunned after a dramatic episode last night which saw Mel Owen die in an car crash,

And if that wasn’t enough, Lisa told Phil (Steve McFadden) that Mel’s untimely death was a result of her finding out that Sharon’s baby isn’t his.

How will it all unfold? You’ll have to wait until Monday to find out.

In cheerier news, some of the cast will take a break from Walford tonight to take part in the BBC’s Children in Need telethon.

But who will be taking part and what will they do? Keep on reading to find out.
