SSC CGL tier-II 2017 revised results declared for Assistant Audit Officer, Accounts Officer

SSC CGL tier-II revised results 2017: A total of 4015 candidates qualified for the posts of Assistant Audit Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer

15 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Careers Desk

SSC CGL tier-II revised results 2017: The Staff Selection Commission declared the revised results of CGL Tier II examinations 2017 for the posts of Assistant Audit Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer. A total of 4015 candidates qualified for the posts which was earlier 3,675 for the posts of Assistant Audit Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer.

“Due to revision in some of the answer keys of paper IV of Tier II exam, 4015 candidates are getting qualified for the posts of Assistant Audit Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer,” released the official notification.

“Further, out of these 4015 candidates, 3675 candidates have already been declared as qualified for the posts of Assistant Audit Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer (as per the earlier result declared on 06.06.2018) and 340 candidates are additionally getting qualified now for these posts,” mentioned the official notification.

A total of 340 candidates who were declared not qualified in the earlier results, have now qualified for the posts of Assistant Audit Officer and Assistant Accounts Officer, while 44 qualified candidates did not qualify in the revised result.
