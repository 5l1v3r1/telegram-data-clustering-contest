Florida at Missouri Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

15 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Tzvi Machlin

The Florida Gators take on the Missouri Tigers this week, needing a win and a lot of help to get a spot in the SEC Championship Game.

Florida is 8-2 on the season and 5-2 in the SEC, and are currently No. 11 in the College Football Playoff rankings. With a loss to SEC East leader Georgia, the Gators would need Georgia to lose against both Auburn and Texas A&M in order to take their spot in the SEC title game. But they’ll have to take care of business against Missouri for that to even be a factor. Fortunately for Florida, they have done a great job of taking care of business against just about everyone below them in the standings.

Though not bowl eligible due to NCAA sanctions, Missouri is still hoping to finish with a third-straight winning season. The Tigers started off impressively enough, going 5-1 to start the season. But three straight road losses to Vanderbilt, Kentucky and Georgia have them at risk for a major meltdown in the final stretch of the season. They’ll have to hope senior quarterback Kelly Bryant can work his magic against one of the better teams in the country.

Between Florida’s dominance and Missouri’s recent slump, it should come as no surprise that the Gators are favored in this one.
