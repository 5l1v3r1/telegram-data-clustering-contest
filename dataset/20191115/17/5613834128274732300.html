Dive Into The Battle Against Skynet With The ‘Terminator: Resistance’ Launch Trailer

15 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Mike Wilson

Despite the release date push to December for consoles here in North America, the Steam version of Terminator: Resistance is now available. As is the norm nowadays, publisher Reef Entertainment and developer Teyon have released a launch trailer for the first-person shooter.

Terminator: Resistance will be available on PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in North America on December 10, while European console gamers can download it now.
