The Seabound Soul is the next free update to Sea of Thieves

15 Nov 2019, 17:28 by Stephany Nunneley

The Seabound Soul is the next update coming to Sea of Thieves.

Sea of Thieves players can enjoy the next free update to the game, The Seabound Soul, starting November 20.

The update features an all-new, lore-focused Tall Tale quest. In it, you will join Captain Pendragon to “uncover the mystery of the feared ship, the Ashen Dragon.”

You will also find out the secrets of a new threat.

With the update comes firebombs which can be thrown or loaded into cannons. The firebombs can be used in both Adventure and in The Arena.
