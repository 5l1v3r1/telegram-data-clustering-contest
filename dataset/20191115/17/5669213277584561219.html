USS IWO JIMA fire, Mayport

15 Nov 2019, 17:12 by NIKOLAY TORKIN

Fire erupted in cargo hold of amphibious assault ship USS IWO JIMA, docked at Naval Station Mayport, at night Nov 14. Fire was reported at 2345 LT, The ship’s crew, assisted by sailors from USS The Sullivans, federal firefighters and the Jacksonville Fire and Rescue Department, worked together to fight fire, which was reported extinguished at 0435 LT Nov 15, fire didn’t spread beyond cargo hold. 11 sailors reported to suffer minor injures, extent of ship’s damages unknown.
US Navy amphibious assault ship USS IWO JIMA, MMSI 338813000, GT 41150, commissioned 2001, 1687 troops, complement 1208.
