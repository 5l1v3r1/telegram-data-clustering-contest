Clemson vs. Wake Forest: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

15 Nov 2019, 17:33 by Tzvi Machlin

The Clemson Tigers look to extend their winning streak to 26 games as they take on the Wake Forest Demon Deacons in their final in-conference game of the year.

Clemson clinched a spot in the ACC Championship Game last week after beating NC State and watching Wake Forest lose to Virginia Tech. The Tigers seemed on the verge of losing after a rough September. But since then they’ve reeled off five wins where they’ve scored at least 45 points while allowing 14 or less.

If not for Wake Forest’s loss to Virginia Tech last week, this game would have had far greater implications. The Demon Deacons would have been in a position to steal a spot in the ACC Championship Game out from under Clemson if they had won out. But now, even if Wake Forest upsets the heavily-favored Tigers, they’ll have to settle for second place in the division.

Given that Clemson has outscored their last five foes by 31 to 52 points, it’s no shock that the Tigers are favored by five touchdowns at home.
