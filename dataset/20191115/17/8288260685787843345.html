Boy, 11, suffers 'serious leg injuries' after collision with car

Emergency services were called at 4.18pm to reports of a collision between a car and a pedestrian

15 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Charlotte Regen, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/

An 11-year-old boy has been rushed to hospital after he was hit by a car in Wolverhampton.

Emergency services swooped to the scene on Lichfield Street at around 4.18pm.

The boy has sustained serious leg injuries and has been taken to hospital.

One ambulance attended and police were at the scene for traffic management.

A spokesman for West Midlands Ambulance Service said: "We were called at 4.18pm to reports of a collision between a car and a pedestrian on Lichfield Street. One ambulance is in attendance and we are treating one male, who is the pedestrain. "

A spokeswoman for West Midlands Police said: "An 11-year-old child has been taken to hospital with serious leg injuries."
