Luigi's Mansion 3 Sets A New Launch Month Sales Record In The United States

It's outdone the original game

15 Nov 2019, 02:25 by Liam Doolan

Luigi's Mansion 3 was unable to top the Japanese and UK charts, being outperformed by Persona 5 Royal, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, Death Stranding and FIFA 20. Despite this, it still managed to become the fastest-selling physical Switch game of 2019 in the UK, surpassing The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening.

Over in the United States, the Nintendo exclusive has performed much the same. According to NPD Video Game Industry Analyst Mat Piscatella, Luigi's Mansion 3 was the third best-selling game during the month of October within this region, behind Call of Duty: Modern Warfare and The Outer Worlds.
