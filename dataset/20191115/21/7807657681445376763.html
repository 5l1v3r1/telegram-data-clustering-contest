Gears Tactics and Ori and the Will of the Wisps will be at The Game Awards

There are going to be some surprises.

15 Nov 2019, 21:02 by Asher Madan

What you need to know

  • The Game Awards 2019 take place in December.
  • Microsoft will showcase even more games at the event.
  • Gears Tactics and Ori and the Will of the Wisps are confirmed to be in attendance.
  • Xbox's Aaron Greenberg and Phil Spencer said they'll talk about more games before 2019 concludes.

The Game Awards 2019 take place in December, and host Geoff Keighley is hyping them up by teasing a number of amazing Microsoft Exclusives. Not only will Ori and the Will of the Wisps be there, but Gears Tactics — an XCOM-like title set in the Gears of War Universe — will also showcase new gameplay or possibly get a release date.
