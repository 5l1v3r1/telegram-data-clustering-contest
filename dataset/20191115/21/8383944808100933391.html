Harvard law students walk out of lecture held by Israeli Consul General

15 Nov 2019, 21:50 by DAILY SABAH

Harvard University law students on Friday walked out on a lecture held by the Israeli Consul General in New York, Dani Dayan.

Dayan was due to talk on "The Legal Strategy of Israeli Settlements" in Israeli-blockaded Palestine when the students stood up, lifted placards reading "Settlements are a war crime" and walked out of the room.
