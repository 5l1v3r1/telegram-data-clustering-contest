SIN CITY TV Show Moves to LEGENDARY, Calls in ROBERT RODRIGUEZ

15 Nov 2019, 21:05 by George Marston, Newsarama Contributor

Legendary Television has signed a deal to produce a TV series based on Frank Miller's Sin City, according to Deadline.

Frank Miller will produce the series, with director Robert Rodriguez, who helmed 2005's Sin City film, in talks to join him as producer. As part of the deal, which Deadline reports is contingent on finding a network or streaming service to air the series, Miller will also produce an R-rated animated Sin City feature.

A previous deal was struck to bring Sin City to TV with The Walking Dead showrunner Glen Mazzara, though that project never materialized.

No timeframe was given for the Sin City TV show's release.
