Anti-Government Protesters Take to the Streets of Iraqi Capital - Video

15 Nov 2019, 21:14

The demonstrations have been rocking Iraq for over a month, with the protesters demanding that the government is dissolved and the crucial reforms, including the economic one, are implemented. The rallies have been marked with clashes with police and the deaths of over 300 people.

On Friday, demonstrators took to the streets of Baghdad to protest against corruption, the lack of healthcare, education, and job opportunities as part of continuing rallies in the Middle Eastern state.
At least 320 people have been killed and thousands more wounded since the unrest began in early October. Since then, the rallies have grown to demand a total reform of the political system.
