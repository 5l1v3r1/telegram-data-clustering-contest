Chuck Liddell Claims He’d Beat Mike Tyson In A Street Fight (VIDEO)

15 Nov 2019, 21:57 by Darrelle Lincoln

Chuck Liddell thinks he could come out on top in a hypothetical street fight against Mike Tyson.

During an interview with business personality Ed Mylett — Liddell stated while Mike would beat him in a boxing match, he would definitely win in MMA and if they were to meet in an alley.

“Street fight, I win,” Liddell said. “I mean, he’s got a puncher’s chance. He’s got a chance to catch me coming in. But other than that, it’s over.”
