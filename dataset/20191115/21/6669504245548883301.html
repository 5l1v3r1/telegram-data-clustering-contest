Canadian funny man better known across the pond heading to Vernon

15 Nov 2019, 21:45 by Ben Bulmer

He's appeared in some of the most popular TV comedy shows in the U.K. and now the Canadian stand-up comedian, who you may never have heard of, is performing in Vernon.

Comedian Glenn Wool is set to take to the stage at the Green Pub at the Village Green Hotel on Friday, Nov. 29.

Originally from Vancouver, Wool started his career in comedy in 1995 and moved to London in 1998. Over the last two decades, Wool has performed what one critic described as "cleverly subversive" stand-up comedy all over the world winning awards and receiving nominations at various international comedy festivals.

While lesser known in his native Canada, he's received strong praise from critics across the U.K. getting rave reviews from the country's major daily newspapers including the Guardian, the Daily Telegraph and the Independent.
