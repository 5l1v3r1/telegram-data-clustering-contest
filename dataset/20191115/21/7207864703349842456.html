Pelosi: Trump's Actions 'Makes What Nixon Did Look Almost Small'

It's a strong comparison for sure.

15 Nov 2019, 21:39 by Matt M. Miller

Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi compared President Donald Trump to former President Richard Nixon and the 1970s Watergate scandal.

“The cover-up makes what Nixon did look almost small,” Pelosi stated.
