Bristol-Myers Squibb wins antitrust approval to buy Celgene on condition it sell psoriasis drug

15 Nov 2019, 21:57 by Reuters

WASHINGTON — Bristol-Myers Squibb Co

and Celgene Corp have won U.S. antitrust

approval for their merger on condition that they sell Celgene’s

psoriasis drug Otezla, the Federal Trade Commission said in a

statement on Friday.
