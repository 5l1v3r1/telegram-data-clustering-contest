Lil Reese Released From Hospital After Being Shot and Put in Critical Condition

15 Nov 2019, 21:56 by Peter A. Berry

Lil Reese is out of the hospital after reportedly being shot in the neck.

On Friday (Nov. 15), Reese appeared on his Instagram Live. In the clip, which has gone viral on social media, the Chicago rapper is covered in a black hood while looking at the comment section while bopping his head to Jon B.'s 1997 song, "They Don't Know."

The rapper was shot on Country Club Hills, Ill.'s 167th Street and someone reportedly took him to the hospital before police arrived at the scene. He had been in South Suburban Hospital's emergency room before being transferred to Advocate Christ Medical Center where he was said to be in critical condition.
