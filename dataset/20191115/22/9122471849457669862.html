Penn State vs. Indiana Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

15 Nov 2019, 22:15 by Andrew McCarty

Penn State had an opportunity to enter its game against Ohio State as one of the top-three teams in the country. However, with their loss to Minnesota on Saturday afternoon, the Nittany Lions will likely fall short once more of a Big Ten title.

Penn State would have to win-out, which means a win over Ohio State in Columbus. The Nittany Lions have a chance to bounce back this weekend with a home game against a surprising Indiana team.

The Hoosiers sit at 7-2 on the season and have won four-straight games heading into Saturday afternoon’s game.

Tim Allen’s Hoosiers boast a solid defense that gives up just 20 points per game. Can they slow down a Penn State offense that scores over 37 points per contest?

More on tomorrow’s huge Big Ten game:

Game Time: Saturday, 12:00 p.m. ET

Channel: ABC

Spread: Penn State -14.5

Over/Under: 55.5

Announcers:Jason Bennetti, Rod Gilmore, Quint Kessenich

Indiana’s only two losses have come from Ohio State and Michigan State. The Hoosiers are riding a nice win-streak and have the momentum heading into Beaver Stadium this weekend.

Meanwhile, James Franklin needs to right the ship with a win on Saturday to have a shot at a conference title and potential College Football Playoff berth.
