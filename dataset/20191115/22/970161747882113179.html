EastEnders' Maisie Smith wins Children in Need's Strictly Come Dancing special

15 Nov 2019, 22:01

Maisie Smith emerged as the winner tonight of EastEnders’ very own Strictly Come Dancing special for Children in Need.

The actress – who portrays Tiffany Butcher in the soap – was competing against fellow castmates Louisa Litton, Rudolph Walker and Ricky Champ.

The 18-year-old was partnered with Strictly pro Kevin Clifton for a special group routine to the classic bop Dancing in the Street, which has been performed by everyone from Van Halen to David Bowie and Mick Jagger.

But in the battle of Walford, it was the youngster who came out on top in the end.

Judges Shirley Ballas, Craig Revel Horwood, Bruno Tonioli and Motsi Mabuse all conferred on the decision – and awarded Maisie with the very special Pudsey edition of the famous Glitterball trophy.

Speaking after her win, Maisie said: ‘I honestly had the most amazing week and I couldn’t have asked for a more wonderful team by my side.
