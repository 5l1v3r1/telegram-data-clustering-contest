Spain 7-0 Malta: Seven different scorers in rampant La Roja romp

They pounded Malta as seven different players got in on the action

15 Nov 2019, 22:34 by Jordi Gil

Spain thrashed Malta 7-0 on Friday to secure top place in their Euro 2020 qualifying group, with seven different goalscorers on the scoresheet.

Alvaro Morata continue his goalscoring form when he broke the deadlock from close range, with Santi Cazorla netting the second, in off the post.

Spain really opened Malta up in the second half with Pau Torres bundling home on his debut after coming on.

Pablo Sarabia made it four with debutant Dani Olmo netting the fifth and Gerard Moreno notching the sixth.

The best goal was saved for last when Jesus Navas lashed home from distance to seal top spot in Group F.
