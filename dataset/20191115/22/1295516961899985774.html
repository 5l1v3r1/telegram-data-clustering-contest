Crisis on Infinite Earths Gets New Show-Specific Teasers

15 Nov 2019, 22:11 by L.D. Nolan

The CW has released several new teasers specific to the various Arrowverse shows  for the upcoming "Crisis on Infinite Earths" crossover event

Posted on YouTube by TV Promos, the first trailer is for The Flash and sees Harbinger recruiting Barry Allen. His cosmic treadmill also appears in the clip. The second next teaser is for Batwoman and shows her getting recruited for the event. The third brings Black Lightning into the mix, as he remarks to various heroes, "You just told me my world is over, and you need my help?" The next two shows that appear are Legends of Tomorrow and Supergirl. The final clip is the first teaser that debuted earlier this week.
