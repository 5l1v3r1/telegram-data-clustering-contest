James Charles launching YouTube beauty influencer competition with $50,000 prize

15 Nov 2019, 22:50

James Charles is launching his very own YouTube series, in a bid to find the next biggest make-up artist influencer – on what happens to be on-off pal Jeffree Star’s birthday…

The influencer will be laying down his skills, expertise and all the sass, to uncover the next big thing in the beauty world – who will take home a $50,000 (£38k) prize.

Think Project Runway meets America’s Next Top Model with a dash of beauty.

And we are living for it.

The 20-year-old’s four-part show will be coming to his YouTube channel next year, and he branded the new project a ‘dream come true’.

In a chat with Variety, James said: ‘Ever since I entered the beauty space three years ago, I’ve dreamed of producing my own beauty competition show.

‘It’s a dream come true… It’s such an amazing opportunity for [YouTube] to take it above and beyond what we expected.

‘The whole point is to pick a person I think will bring something positive to the community.

‘It was important for me to make it really true to how the beauty influencer community works.’

Aspiring contestants will have to apply through his website, with a YouTube link to a clip showing off their skills.

Six contestants will be chosen and flown to LA to compete for the money – with James joined by a string of famous faces on the judging panel.

But, before you start preparing your make-up brushes, only US residents are able to compete.

Fans went wild over the news, flocking to Twitter to congratulate James on the new venture.

‘I’m so f**king proud of u and everything you have done! You deserve this success, I’m so so excited for this,’ one commented.

‘Honestly this is such a good idea lol,’ another replied, while a Twitter user said: ‘Such an insanely incredible opportunity! i always hoped someone would create a show or series like this. How perfect!’

However, others were questioning why James decided to confirm his big news on Jeffree Star’s 34th birthday.

‘Do y’all know how p**sed I am James Charles chose @JeffreeStar birthday to f**king do a launch. WHAT A F**KING LOSER,’ a Twitter user fumed.
