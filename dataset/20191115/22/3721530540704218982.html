Xbox Black Friday deals: Xbox One X bundles for $350, Xbox One S for $150

Follow Polygon online:

15 Nov 2019, 22:12 by Cass Marshall

During the X019 presentation in London, Xbox announced their of Black Friday deals, ranging from individual games, to bundles, to the Xbox Game Pass Ultimate. The deals begin Thursday, Nov. 21 for Xbox Game Pass Ultimate and Xbox Live Gold members, and Nov. 24 for all users. These sales will last until Dec. 2; we’ve listed all of them below.

Xbox One X and Xbox One S

  • Xbox One X bundles will be available for $349.99. Normally, they go for $499.99.
  • Xbox One S bundles, including the Xbox One S All-Digital Edition, will be on sale for $149.99. They usually retail for $249.99. The Digital Edition comes with Minecraft, Sea of Thieves, and Fortnite in a bundle.

Peripherals

Select Xbox Wireless Controllers are up to $20 off, including the following: Night Ops Camo Special Edition, Sport Blue Special Edition and Kait Diaz Gears 5 Limited Edition. The standard price of these controllers is $69.99.

Xbox Game Pass Ultimate

Signing up for Xbox Game Pass Ultimate (as a new subscriber) will grant you the first three months for $1; this is usually $14.99 a month.

Games

These game sales run from Nov. 24 through Dec. 2.

  • Gears 5 is 33% off.
  • Forza Horizon 4 is 50% off.
  • Sea of Thieves: Anniversary Edition is 50% off.
  • State of Decay 2 is 50% off.
  • Hellblade: Senua’s Sacrifice is 50% off.
