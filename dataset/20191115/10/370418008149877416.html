Suicide Silence Release Two New Songs

Listen to two new Suicide Silence songs, taken from their upcoming album Become The Hunter

15 Nov 2019, 10:25

Just a few weeks after announcing their new album Become The Hunter, Suicide Silence have released two brand new tracks.

The follow-up to 2017’s opinion-splitting self-titled album will be released on February 14 2020, but you can listen to opening track Meltdown and Love Me To Death now on streaming services.

Both tracks hark back to Suicide Silence’s original deathcore sound, with hefty doses of brutality for those of us who love a chugggggg.

Produced by Steve Evetts (The Dillinger Escape Plan, Sepultura, Hatebreed), Become The Hunter was also mixed by Josh Wilbur (Trivium, Lamb Of God, Gojira) and mastered by Ted Jensen (Pantera, Deftones, Slipknot). Big names, huh?
