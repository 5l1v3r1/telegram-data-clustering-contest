Kate Middleton Delivers Heartfelt Speech As She Attends Children’s Hospice Launch

15 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Becca Longmire

Kate Middleton donned one of her favourite outfits to attend the launch of the Nook children’s hospice Friday.

The Duchess of Cambridge helped initiate a public appeal to acquire funds for the centre, which supports children with life-threatening illnesses, five years ago.

Middleton wore a pink Oscar de la Renta skirt suit, which she’s been snapped wearing on numerous occasions, for the Norfolk engagement.

Middleton said in a speech, “You have created here at the Nook a nurturing, caring environment that allows families who are going through the unimaginable the ability to spend precious quality time with each other, comforted in the knowledge that their children are being looked after in the best possible way.”
