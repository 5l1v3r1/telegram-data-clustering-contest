Photos: Ileana D'Cruz slays in a tiger-printed pantsuit; set the internet on fire

15 Nov 2019, 10:27 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood actress Ileana D'Cruz has aced her social media game and always manages to grab everyone's attention with each post nowadays. From sizzling bikini pictures to flaunting mesmerising outfits, Ileana's post always manages to become the talk of the town.

Taking to Instagram recently, the 'Rustom' actress shared a series of pictures of herself dressed in a tiger-printed pantsuit with witty and humorous captions. She completed her look with light makeup and tresses all set behind in a half ponytail with rectangular earrings.

Being an avid social media user, Ileana is one of the actresses in Bollywood who enjoys a huge fan following on social media. The diva might have gone through a rough patch in her life post parting ways with her longtime boyfriend Andrew Kneebone but she has bounced back gracefully.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Ileana will soon be seen in Anees Bazmee's 'Pagalpanti' along with the likes of John Abraham, Anil Kapoor, Kriti Kharbanda, Pulkit Samrat, Arshad Warsi, Saurabh Shukla and others. The film is slated to release on 22nd November.
