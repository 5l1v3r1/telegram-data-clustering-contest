Oppo A9 2020 comes in a new colour with free Baskin-Robbins

15 Nov 2019, 10:36 by Alexander Wong

Oppo Malaysia is introducing a new Vanilla Mint colour option for the Oppo A9 2020. The device will be available for pre-order starting 21st to 27th November 2019 and they are giving away a Baskin-Robbins voucher for free.

On top of that, Oppo Malaysia is also bundling the device with 1-year screen warranty worth RM99. The Baskin-Robbins voucher will be offered for early bird customers while stocks last and you can use to redeem a junior scoop of chocolate mint ice cream.

The device is priced at RM1,199 and it comes with 8GB RAM + 128GB of storage. The device comes with a 6.5″ HD+ LCD display and it runs on a Snapdragon 665 processor.

In the camera department, it features a quad-camera setup which consists of a 48MP main camera, an 8MP ultra-wide-angle lens, 2MP portrait lens and a 2MP mono lens. For selfies, there’s a 16MP selfie camera that’s placed on the tiny notch on the display.

The device also features dual-stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos and it also retains a 3.5mm headphone jack. Powering the A9 2020 is a large 5,000mAh battery and it charges via USB-C.
