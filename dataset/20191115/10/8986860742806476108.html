Stonebwoy set to drop another banger next week

15 Nov 2019, 10:36 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Dancehall artiste and Bhim Nation President Stonebwoy is set to drop another banger next week a few months after the release of his ‘Ololo’ song which features Nigerian artiste Teni.

From what we gather, the song is set to be the song to be used to celebrate this year Christmas just like his ‘Kpoo k3k3’ song did last.

The new song which features Lynx Entertainment signees Kuami Eugene and Kidi and it’s titled ‘Sobolo’.

The “Sobolo” song has this highlife and afrobeat feel; one cool song to dance to.

Just like the success with ‘Kpoo K3k3’, Stonebwoy is hoping to do same with Sobolo.
