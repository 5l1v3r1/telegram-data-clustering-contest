India's Ashalata Devi nominated for AFC Player of the Year

15 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Sourav Modak

KOLKATA: Indian women's football captain Loitongbam Ashalata Devi added another feather to her cap on Friday when she was nominated for the AFC Player of the Year at AFC Annual Awards to be held at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre on December 2. The 26-year-old from Imphal has been enjoying quite a successful year by leading India to the Olympic 2020 Qualifiers Round 2 and winning the 2019 SAFF Championship.

The defender also played a pivotal role in helping her club Sethu FC to the Indian Women's League crown. She was subsequently voted as Women's Player of the Year for the 2018-2019 season by the All India Football Federation (AIFF) in July this year.
