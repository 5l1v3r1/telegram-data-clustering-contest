Ole Gunnar Solskjaer 'sends personal scout' to watch Man Utd transfer target Erling Haaland

Man Utd are eyeing a transfer move for Erling Braut Haaland with Ole Gunnar Solskjaer stepping up their interest in the RB Salzburg striker

15 Nov 2019, 07:29 by Rich Jones, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rich-jones/

Manchester United boss Ole Gunnar Solskjaer has reportedly stepped up his interest in Erling Braut Haaland - by sending his most trusted scout to monitor the Norwegian.

Haaland has emerged as one of Europe's hottest properties after his stunning goalscoring form for RB Salzburg so far this season.

He has bagged 22 goals in just 16 games in all competitions to put top clubs on red-alert.

According to The Athletic , Solskjaer has sent Simon Wells to specifically watch the teenager in Champions League action as the club ponder a move.

Solskjaer is no stranger to Haaland having handed him his senior debut a Molde, where he scored three goals in Solskjaer's last five games in charge before departing for Manchester United .

But Wells has been charged with running the rule over Haaland with the club preparing to pursue a move for the forward.

United are keen to bolster their attacking options after struggling for goals so far this season.

Romelu Lukaku and Alexis Sanchez both departed for Inter Milan over the summer leaving United thin on the ground without replacements.

They have been boosted by Anthony Martial's return from injury but will still look to address their forward line in January.

Mario Mandzukic has been linked with a move as a short-term fix with Haaland unlikely to leave Salzburg until the summer.

But United could face serious competition to land Haaland with the likes of Juventus and other European giants linked.

He has already bagged seven Champions League goals during his breakthrough and could be keen to stay in the competition with a return for United currently looking unlikely.
