McDonald's breakfast hours to be extended in every single restaurant next week

Students and hungover people rejoice, you will soon be able to pick up a McMuffin a lot later into the the day after a successful trial led McDonald's to raise the cut-off time for its breakfast menu

15 Nov 2019, 07:39 by James Andrews, http://www.facebook.com/jameswritesmoney/

Earlier this year McDonald's decided to see what would happen if it extended the breakfast hours at some of its restaurants .

Instead of rotating the menu boards on the dot of 10:30, cutting hungry and hungover customers off from hash browns, McMuffins and pancakes, it would let them keep ordering until 11am.

And - in fantastic news for fans of breakfasts wraps with hash browns in them - the company has now said it will roll out the trial everywhere.

Michelle Graham-Clare, McDonald’s UK cice president food & marketing, said: "We all understand the pain of missing out on a McMuffin by a matter of minutes.

"We have listened to our customers’ pleas and are delighted to extend breakfast serving time until 11am, particularly as we head into the festive season when we see a rush of latecomers searching for those all-important hash browns."

The new system kicks off on 20 November, 2019 - that's Wednesday next week.

And if you're thinking of ordering from home, the extra 30 minutes means you have until 10:45am to order through Uber Eats.

Of course, it also means that if you're a fan of a breakfast double-cheeseburger and fries, you'll also have to wait an extra half-hour.
