Project xCloud preview adds more than 50 new games

15 Nov 2019, 07:39 by Steve Wright

A host of third-party titles have been added, including Tekken 7, Madden NFL 20 and Devil May Cry 5.

More than 50 new games have today been added to the Project xCloud preview, Microsoft today confirmed as part of its X019 Inside Xbox livestream.

Microsoft’s Kareem Choudhry confirmed that “more than 50 new titles from over 25 of our valued partners” alongside additional first-party titles have been added to the service, including EA’s Madden NFL 20, Capcom’s Devil May Cry 5 and Bandai Namco’s Tekken 7.
