Vaani Kapoor Tagged 'Shameless' For Wearing A Deep Neck Top With ‘Ram’ Printed On It; Actress Deletes The Picture

15 Nov 2019, 07:47 by Oshine Koul

Vaani Kapoor posted a sexy picture of her wearing a pink frilled crop top. What didn’t go down well with netizens was the name of Lord Ram printed all over it. The actress received massive backlash following which she deleted the image from her social media handles

Bollywood celebs are always under netizens' radar. One wrong move and they receive backlash. The latest target of trolls is Vaani Kapoor. Riding high on the success of War, co-starring Hrithik Roshan, and Tiger Shroff, Vaani is making headlines for all the wrong reasons. A few days back, the Befikre actress took to social media to treat her fans with a sexy and glamours picture. The actress looked hot in a deep neck pink crop top with a sexy knot on the front. Vaani paired it with Khaki pants. The actress flaunted her infectious smile in a now-deleted picture. Poor girl didn’t know she’ll be slammed for wearing such a top.

With frills on the border, what caught netizens’ attention was the name of Lord Ram printed on it, which obviously didn’t go down well with them. As soon as the actress posted the picture, she received massive backlash for hurting Hindu sentiments. Not only this, netizens even demanded a ban on the actress.
