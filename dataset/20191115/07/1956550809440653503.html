Three UAE cinemas perfect for taking kids

Enjoy a family movie with all the gang

15 Nov 2019, 07:11

NoVO kidz
NOVO Cinemas
If kids want their movie served up with a warm hug then the colourful furry ‘friendly monster’ seats at the recently-launched NOVO Kidz cinema in Festival City will make their popcorn taste even sweeter – or saltier if that’s their thing.
Dubai Festival City Mall, Dubai, www.novocinemas.com (04 232 8328).

REEL JUNIOR
Reel Cinemas
Mini movie-goers can watch films in comfort on squishy beanbag seats in this cinema. Plus, the kiddie combo meals will keep little tummies satisfied from start to finish – so it’s just toilet breaks you’ll have to contend with.
The Springs Souk, The Dubai Mall and Al Ghurair Centre, www.reelcinemas.ae.

KIDS
VOX Cinemas
Because little ones can’t sit still for long, there’s Kids at VOX Cinemas where they will be glued to their luxurious primary coloured cinema seat throughout whatever family blockbuster is showing.
Various locations in Dubai and Abu Dhabi including Mercato and Yas Mall, ww.uae.voxcinemas.com.
