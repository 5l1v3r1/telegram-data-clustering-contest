Eldon Square Christmas opening hours including late night shopping

intu Eldon Square in Newcastle is sure to be very busy as people get cracking with their Christmas shopping

15 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Simon Duke

If you've been in the vicinity of intu Eldon Square recently you'll have noticed that things are starting to get busier and busier as we hurtle towards Christmas.

Things are set to get more and more and more congested in the city centre as we edge closer and closer to Santa coming, with the biggest ever Newcastle Christmas Market, taking over Monument, Blackett Street and Northumberland Street, with a whole host of different stalls and rides.

And then of course there's always huge queues of people lining up to see Fenwick's Window, as well as big crowds for festive tipples at STACK and many surrounding bars.

But a place that is set to be jam packed as November runs into December is Eldon Square and the shopping centre has already revealed its festive opening hours.
