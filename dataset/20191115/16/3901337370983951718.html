Dikgacoi & Social Co. Weigh In On Makola Saga

15 Nov 2019, 16:00 by SL Social

Following the news of Mpho Makola’s six-month ban for assaulting a referee, former Bafana Bafana star Kagisho Dikgacoi and other prominent figures in the football freaternity such as Ace Ncobo and Matthew Rusike have weighed in on the matter.

The Premier Soccer League revealed on Friday that Makola was given the minimum ban that could be handed down for an offence of this nature.

The incident took place during the Telkom Knockout match between Cape Town City and Kaizer Chiefs, with a visibly upset Makola physically attacking referee Abongile Tom.
