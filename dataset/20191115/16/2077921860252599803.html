Live at Paste Studio - La Santa Cecilia

15 Nov 2019, 16:30

L.A. rock band La Santa Cecilia aren't just a rock band. The four-piece group combines rock with the Latin music inspirations from their childhoods, ultimately delivering a mix of soul, bossa nova, tango, ska, rumba and much more. The band stopped by the Paste Studio in September to play some tunes from their new self-titled album: "A Thousand Times," "I've Been Thinking" and "Nobody Knows When You're Down And Out," which features California Feetwarmers on the album version.
