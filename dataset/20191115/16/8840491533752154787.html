Mayo garda issue fresh appeal over spate of burglaries

15 Nov 2019, 16:49

GARDAÍ in Mayo are conducting investigating into two new burglary cases which have occurred in the south of the county.

Cash and a number of valuable items were stolen from properties in the Ballindine district last night.

It follows a series of similar raids in the Claremorris, Charlestown and Knock areas within the past number of days, incident which have struck fear into communities.

Gardai have confirmed thay gained entry to the properties by forcing windows or patio doors open.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Claremorris Gardai on 094 93 72080.
