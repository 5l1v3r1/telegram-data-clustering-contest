Good deed of the day - local charity in need of new trustees

Could you be the next trustee for the United Charities of Abel Collin?

15 Nov 2019, 16:31 by Sarah Brazendale

Here's a chance to use your talents to help others, the United Charities of Abel Collin are in need of a new trustee for its board.

The charity, originally founded in 1709, provides quality housing for up to 100 independent, older people of modest means in homes in Beeston in Nottingham.

The board currently has 10 trustees, these are unpaid volunteers that are hands-on in the role, ensuring proper governance, developing new policies and making decisions across a wide range of issues. The board also manages the finances, grounds, properties and two members of staff.
