Salman Khan’s Hud Hud Dabangg has taken the internet by a storm

15 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Poonam Jain

Salman Khan is all set to entertain the audience as Chulbul Pandey in Dabangg 3. Starring Sonakshi Sinha alongside Salman and marking the debut of Mahesh Majrekar’s daughter Saiee Manjrekar, the film has already created a mega buzz around it. Directed by Prabhudeva this is the third movie from the Dabangg franchise. Dabangg 3 has Saiee Manjrekar playing Khan’s love interest before he met Rajjo (Sonakshi Sinha). The makers of the movie dropped the title track Hud Hud Dabangg yesterday which has taken the internet by a storm. 

The song has Salman doing the hook step and impressing the audience with his entertaining antics. The song has sparked off a meme fest and Netizens are going all out to showcase their creativity. The film is set to come as a treat for the audience on December 20 this year after which Salman will be starting work in full force on his Eid 2020 release Radhe.
