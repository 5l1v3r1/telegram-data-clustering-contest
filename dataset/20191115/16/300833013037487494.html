Get the Best Out of the Deals on Chromebooks

15 Nov 2019, 16:05 by Review Team

Chromebooks bear that name since they run on the Google Chrome OS instead of the usual Windows OS. They can handle practically all the computing tasks that a regular laptop or notebook can do and they cost much less. Some of the known brands like Acer and Lenovo have adopted this Chromebook model in their range of offerings. Just check out the other specs like the processor etc. before deciding on the right Chromebook for your use. There are two-in-ones available too in the same range.

Lenovo Chromebook S330 Laptop

The Lenovo Chromebook S330 comes equipped with a 14-inch full HD display. A MediaTek MTK8173C processor supported by 4GB LPDDR3 RAM and 64GB eMMC 5 storage offer processing support. You can make use of an additional 100GB from the cloud via Google Drive. Lenovo has taken great care in designing this Chromebook with just one inch in thickness and 330lbs in weight. The Chrome OS takes care of most functions of this laptop making computing activity a breeze. The connectivity options include Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.

This Lenovo Chromebook S330 laptop can be picked up now for $219.99, $68.19 off the regular price.
