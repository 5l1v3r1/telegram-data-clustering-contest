One Punch Man: A Hero Nobody Knows releases on February 28th

15 Nov 2019, 16:07 by John Papadopoulos

Bandai Namco has announced that One Punch Man: A Hero Nobody Knows will release on February 28th, 2020. In order to celebrate this announcement, the publisher released a brand new trailer that you can find below.

In this new fighting game, players will learn hero skills and complete missions as a pro hero in the Hero Association and climb the ranks to become an S-Class hero in the main story, based on the first season of the ONE PUNCH MAN anime.

Players can also battle with powerful characters such as Metal Knight, Stinger, and Melzargard in over-the-top clashes true to the ONE PUNCH MAN world. Powerful villains may also show up during battle and disrupt the fight in events; these villains include King, a character so intimidating that he causes everyone but Saitama to freeze in battle.
