Metrocentre Christmas opening times including extended hours for late night shopping

intu Metrocentre is set to be very busy in the run up to Christmas as people look for presents

15 Nov 2019, 16:36 by Simon Duke

Whether you want to admit it or not, it's beginning to look a lot like Christmas out there, which means that many people's thoughts, if they haven't already, have turned to present shopping.

And a place which is expected to see plenty of people through its doors is intu Metrocentre.

If you've paid the Gateshead shopping mall a visit over the past few weekends you'll know it's steadily getting busier in the run up to Santa coming.

Last year, the Metrocentre put plans in place to extend its opening hours over the festive season so that shoppers can pace themselves, factor in some buy-time after work and hit the shops at quieter times late into evening.

And it's the same last year, with the reveal of the centre's opening hours for Christmas 2019, with Eldon Square's Christmas opening times also confirmed.
