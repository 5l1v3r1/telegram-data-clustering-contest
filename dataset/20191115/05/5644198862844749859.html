FDA panel unanimously backs expanding use of Amarin's heart drug Vascepa

15 Nov 2019, 05:25

REUTERS: A panel of experts to the U.S. FDA on Thursday recommended allowing Amarin Corp Plc's fish-oil derived drug to be used as add-on therapy for reducing the chance of heart attacks and strokes in high-risk patients with cardiovascular disease.

The panel voted 16-0 in favor of expanding approval, potentially opening up a multi-billion dollar opportunity for the company which has only one drug, Vascepa, in the market.
