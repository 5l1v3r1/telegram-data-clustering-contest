Read All The Lyrics To Disney’s ‘Frozen II’ Soundtrack

15 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Caitlin Kelley

Ahead of the Frozen sequel’s theatrical release, the accompanying soundtrack just arrived. The album includes the previously released track, “Into the Unknown.” The film franchise’s original soundtrack was so commercially successful, it reached No. 4 on the Billboard 200, marking the highest position for an animated film’s soundtrack since 1995’s Pocahontas.

A handful of big names contributed to the tracklist: Panic! at the Disco, Kacey Musgraves, Weezer, and AURORA. The album also features cast members, including Josh Gad, Kristen Bell, Idina Menzel, Jonathan Groff, and Evan Rachel Wood. Kristen Anderson-Lopez and Robert Lopez—who won an Oscar for the first film’s “Let It Go”—return as songwriters

“The songs and score of ‘Frozen 2’ reflect the growth of the characters and the deepening of their story,” said director Jennifer Lee in a press release. “The music is fun but emotional, personal yet powerful, intimate but also epic. Kristen, Bobby and Christophe [Beck] have definitely outdone themselves and taken the music to brave new heights.”
