Man found dead at Wyndham Work Camp, investigations underway

15 Nov 2019, 05:21

A 29-year-old man has been found dead at the Wyndham Work Camp.

The Department of Justice said the death did not appear to be suspicious.

“While the circumstances of his death are the subject of a police investigation, there does not appear to be any suspicious circumstances,” a spokesman said.

The man was found unresponsive yesterday morning, with attempts to revive him unsuccessful, according to a spokesperson from the Department of Justice.

WA Police are investigating the death, which will be subject to a Coronial Inquest.

An internal review has also been launched into the man’s management during his time in custody.
