Read All The Lyrics To Lil Peep’s New Posthumous Album ‘Everybody’s Everything’

15 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Cameron ひ

It has been two years since rising emo rap star Lil Peep tragically passed away from an accidental drug overdose. Today, a documentary titled Everybody’s Everything was released, following his life from childhood to the start of his music career. It’s directed by Ramez Silyan and Sebastian Jones, and comes accompanied by a new posthumous Lil Peep album of the same name.

The album has 19 songs overall ranging from unreleased tracks and songs only available through leaks and snippets to tracks like “Witchblades,” one of the rapper’s biggest hits. It also includes the entirety of his recent EP, Goth Angel Sinner, and boasts features from Lil Tracy, Rich The Kid, Gab3, and more.

In a recent interview with Genius, co-director Ramez Silyan spoke about what he hopes fans will take away from the documentary. “It’s sometimes such a simple message, but he did even teach me, don’t just judge a book by its cover,” he said. “It’s important that this is a portrait of him as a human being behind that cover that he created himself.”
