Read All The Lyrics To Tory Lanez’ New Album ‘Chixtape 5’

15 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Caitlin Kelley

Tory Lanez is back with Chixtape 5. The album includes the previously released track, “Jerry Sprunger.” This is his first release since last year’s LoVE mE NOw? and the latest Chixtape installment since 2017’s Chixtape 4.

The Canadian hitmaker enlisted the help of Jagged Edge, Jermaine Dupri, T-Pain, Snoop Dogg, Chris Brown, Ludacris, The-Dream, Mýa, Mario, Trey Songz, Ashanti (who can be found on the album cover), Lloyd, Lil Wayne, Fabolous, Slim, and Nyce. Coop Chardonnay, Papiyerr, Play Picasso contributed production.

“THIS IS THE BEST CHIXTAPE I HAVE EVER MADE,” Lanez declared on Instagram. “And when u hear it you will understand the work , passion and love that I put into every vocal , every beat , every SURPRISE.”
