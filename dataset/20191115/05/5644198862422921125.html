BeiGene drug wins US FDA approval for rare form of lymphoma

15 Nov 2019, 05:02

REUTERS: The U.S. Food and Drug Administration on Thursday approved BeiGene Ltd's lymphoma treatment, validating the China-based drugmaker's strategy of largely using data from trials held outside the United States to file for approval.

The company tested the treatment, Brukinsa, in 118 patients with mantle cell lymphoma enrolled in two studies. About three-quarters were Asian, 21per cent Caucasian, and just between 10per cent to 15per cent were from the United States, BeiGene said.
