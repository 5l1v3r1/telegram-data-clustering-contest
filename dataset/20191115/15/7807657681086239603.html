Abzu, Below, and more leave Xbox Game Pass soon

They'll be missed.

15 Nov 2019, 15:13 by Asher Madan

What you need to know

  • Xbox Game Pass is a monthly game subscription service from Microsoft.
  • A lot of amazing games from the Yakuza franchise are joining the service in 2020.
  • Abzu, Below, and other smaller games are also leaving Xbox Game Pass at the end of November.
  • You can purchase Xbox Game Pass from Amazon.

Every month, a number of amazing games are added to Xbox Game Pass, but just like Netflix or any other subscription service, select titles leave. While select Yakuza games are coming to the service in 2020, some smaller experiences are leaving today. You can read about them below.

The following titles will be removed from Xbox Game Pass on November 30.

  • Abzu for Xbox One and PC
  • Below for Xbox One
  • Football Manager 2019 for PC
  • Grid 2 for Xbox One
  • Kingdom Two Crowns for Xbox One
  • Strange Brigade for Xbox One

Out of all of these, we'll miss Below the most. It's an interesting top-down roguelike that features an incredible atmosphere. If you haven't played it, you should do so. Additionally, you can buy it for a discount if you want to buy it and have it in your catalog.
