Former U.S. Ambassador To Ukraine Says She Was 'Kneecapped' By Smears

15 Nov 2019, 15:36 by AP

Former U.S. Ambassador To Ukraine Marie Yovanovitch said President Donald Trump was persuaded to fire her after hearing false allegations from suspect sources. Speaking at impeachment hearings in the U.S. House of Representatives in Washington on November 15, she said she was "kneecapped" and that U.S. policy on Ukraine had been "thrown into disarray."
