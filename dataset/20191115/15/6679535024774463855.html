Cell counting machines in PHCs

15 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Staff Reporter

“Everyday, 80 fever cases are reported in Theni district but only six people have been diagnosed with dengue so far this year and they are receiving treatment,” said Dr. Krishnaraj, Joint Director, Vector-borne Diseases Control, while visiting an anti-dengue camp held at a Primary Health Centre in Vadapudhupatti village near here on Friday.

He said that 20 workers have been appointed in every panchayat to monitor anti-dengue activities. “In town panchayats and municipalities, one monitoring mazdoor has been assigned for every 300 households,” he said.

He added that cell counting machines are available in PHCs with over 30 beds, government hospitals and the Theni Medical College Hospital.
