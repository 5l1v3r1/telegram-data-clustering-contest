Facts about the young man who invaded the pitch during Ghana – South Africa match

15 Nov 2019, 15:25 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Yesterday’s pitch invader during Ghana vrs South Africa football match has become the man of the moment after his act.

After some diggings, we have been able to unravel some things about the invader whose name we now know to be Awal Suleman London.

Awal’s speed on the pitch whiles been chased by the security men on duty really surprised many with questions being asked on social media on how he managed to get unto the pitch.

Well, below are some of the things we have been able to gather about Ghana’s first pitch invaded.

1. He is a student nurse from the Nalerigu College of Nursing.

2. Awal attended Apam Senior High School

3. He was one of the best 400m runners during his time in SHS.

4. He is also the first person to invade a professional football match in the history of Ghana.

And Finally,

5. Awal, is likely to be the first person to be prosecuted for invading a pitch.
