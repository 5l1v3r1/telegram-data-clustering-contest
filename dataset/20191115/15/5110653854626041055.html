Baker Mayfield, Mike Tomlin react to Myles Garrett helmet swing; Browns QB calls it 'inexcusable'

15 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Bill Bender

Browns quarterback Baker Mayfield did not mince words when asked about teammate Myles Garrett’s actions in the final seconds of his team's 21-7 victory Thursday night over the Steelers. Pittsburgh coach Mike Tomlin, on the other hand, refused to comment.

Garrett ripped Pittsburgh quarterback Mason Rudolph’s helmet off with eight seconds remaining in the game, then swung the helmet at Rudolph and hit Rudolph in the head. A fight ensued in the end zone, with Pittsburgh center Maurkice Pouncey punching and kicking Garrett.

The incident marred the Browns'victory over their biggest AFC North rival.Mayfield addressed it directly in a postgame interview.
