Home Bargains has a giant 4.5kg Toblerone on sale - for £50

The giant festive Toblerone is the equivalent of 45 100g small Toblerones and is 80cm long

15 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Simon Meechan

Home Bargains has mammoth 4.5kg Toblerones on sale.

The jumbo sized chocolate treat is the equivalent of 45 standard-sized 100g Toblerone bars, which cost between £1 and £1.50 each at most supermarkets. Or a dozen-plus 360g large bars, which usually cost £4.25.

Home Bargains has the huge Toblerone on sale for £49, down from an RRP of £73.99. The bar is a giant version of a Toblerone, complete with huge triangular chunks of chocolate, which you might need help to cut through.

As Liverpool Echo reports , the price of the 80cm long bar has startled some shoppers, who would never part with almost £50 for a chocolate bar. If you bought 45 standard Toblerones from ASDA today, it would cost you £45 for the same weight of Toblerone, based on a 100g bar being on sale for £1.

Posting on Facebook, Home Bargains wrote: We’re gonna need a bigger bag!”

"GIANT Toblerone bars are available in stores now."

But customers are not all convinced, even though Home Bargains is selling the huge Toblerone for less than its recommended retail price.

"I love these, but seriously £49 for chocolate," wrote one.

"Who would pay £50 for this? A Christmas dinner to feed the whole family wouldn't cost this much," replied another. The bars are also being sold by Cadburys online at £75.
