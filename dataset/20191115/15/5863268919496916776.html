Lahore: NAB chairman briefed on mega corruption cases

15 Nov 2019, 15:33 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – National Accountability Bureau (NAB) Chairman Justice (r) Javed Iqbal chaired an important session in which DG NAB Lahore Shehzad Saleem briefed him on mega corruption cases.

The meeting – held in the provincial capital of Punjab – deliberated on nine mega corruption cases including the Chaudhry Sugar Mills case.

Sources privy to the anti-corruption watchdog told Dunya News that a detailed discussion was held to file the Chaudhry Sugar Mills case reference. The NAB chief reiterated to bring all cases to their logical conclusion on priority basis.
