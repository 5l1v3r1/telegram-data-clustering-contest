Katie Price’s son Junior hits back at troll who slated her parenting skills

Teenager gives keyboard warrior a well-deserved telling off

15 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Nancy Brown

After Katie Price's eldest son Harvey made an emotional plea to trolls to stop bullying him, the model's son Junior has also hit back at the keyboard warriors.

It all started when Junior's dad Peter Andre posted a cute family video of himself playing an egg catching game with daughter Princess, as Junior danced in the background.

Someone commented on the post: "Junior is always bopping around, so funny!"

However, they added: "Nice to see what real families do and not what Katie does, which is use her kids to make the headlines! Sorry but had to be said."

Clearly keen to stick up for his mum, Junior – who is just 14 years old – replied to the troll who criticised his mum's parenting skills.

Nice to see what real families do and not what Katie does, which is use her kids to make the headlines! Sorry but had to be said.

He said: "You don't need to post negative stuff about my mum."

Peter was clearly keen to stop things escalating and turned off the comments on his post.

Letting his eldest kids have social media accounts has long been a worry for Pete.

Recently someone impersonated Junior on Instagram and asked their followers to send him inappropriate pictures.
