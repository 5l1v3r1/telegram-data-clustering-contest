Latest Strictly Come Dancing odds aren't good news for Chris Ramsey

Bookies have revealed who they think will be the next celebrity to be eliminated from Strictly Come Dancing

15 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Simon Duke

We're now well into the latest series of Strictly Come Dancing, with just a month to go now until this year's winners take home the Gliiterball trophy and bookies reckon they know who will be eliminated next.

This year's Strictly has sprung a number of surprises, with shocking exits for Dev Griffin and Catherine Tyldesley and the fact that Michelle Visage landed in the bottom two last weekend, despite the fact she was second on the leaderboard with a score of 36.

This week, Strictly stars have headed to Blackpool, hoping to impress in the seaside town's legendary Tower Ballroom.

On Thursday, South Shields comedian Chris Ramsey revealed to ChronicleLive that he and pro partner Karen Hauer would be dancing a Salsa to Uptown Funk but, while Chris is massively excited for the routine which he also teased includes a dream dance break, bookies reckon it will be his last turn on the dancefloor.

Chris has so far avoided the dreaded dance off, despite finishing bottom, or near the bottom, of the leaderboard on numerous occasions.

But William Hill think his dancing days are finally numbered, putting him at 8/13 to leave Strictly this weekend. He is the outsider to win the show on odds of 40/1.
