CCTV appeal after man steals booze from River Escapes boat on Newcastle Quayside

Police believe the man in the image could help them with their investigation into the incident

15 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Katie Collings

A man broke into a boat on the Newcastle Quayside and stole alcohol from the bar.

An investigation is under way following the burglary and police have now released a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to.

Between 6.30am and 7.45am on Saturday, October 19, an intruder gained entry by smashing a window on the River Escapes boat and stole a quantity of alcohol from the bar before fleeing the scene.

Enquiries to locate those responsible are ongoing but today officers have released an image of a man they need to trace.

A spokesperson for Northumbria Police said: "He was in the area at the time of the offence and may be able to assist police with their investigation.

"If anyone knows the man, or can help police with their enquiries, then call 101 quoting crime number 132883X/19.

"You can also report online at the Northumbria Police website or call independent charity Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
