Laster to construct factory in Taiwan

15 Nov 2019, 06:32 by Siu Han, Taipei; Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES

Laster Tech will invest NT$450 million (US$14.8 million) to construct a factory for making smart LED automotive lighting modules in northern Taiwan with trial production scheduled for 2020.

Laster posted consolidated revenues of NT$1.002 billion, gross margin of 17.84%, net operating profit of NT$6.0 million, net loss of NT$8 million and net loss per share of NT$0.11 for third-quarter 2019, leading to consolidated revenues of NT$3.306 billion, gross margin of 16.61%, net operating profit of NT$17.3 million, net loss of NT$4.5 million and net loss per share of NT$0.06 for January-September.

Laster has reported consolidated revenues of NT$293.5 million for October, slipping 2.39% sequentially and 27.48% on year, and those of NT$3.324 billion for January-October fell 6.83% on year.
