Friday to feel less cold but rain will continue throughout the weekend

There will be scattered showers across the region from Friday morning but temperatures won't feel as low

15 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Katie Collings

After waking up to bitterly cold temperatures on Thursday, the good news is things won't be quite as bad today.

There will even be some sunny spells, but we're not completely in the clear.

Scattered showers will begin from early Friday morning and continue throughout the weekend.

From Monday, weather will become drier but will be chillier as temperatures drop.

Here is what the Met Office predicts:

Friday

"Rain at first on Friday, easing through the morning as brighter spells develop.

"Some sunny spells and isolated showers by the afternoon.

"Windy at times but a little less cold. Maximum temperature 9 °C."

Outlook for Saturday to Monday:

"Cloudy with outbreaks of rain Saturday.

Further rain or showers Sunday but some brighter spells later.

"Drier but cold Monday, with isolated showers but breezy on the coast."
