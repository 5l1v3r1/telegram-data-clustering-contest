50 Former Disney Stars in Their *Least* Disney Roles Ever

15 Nov 2019, 06:17

There comes a point in every Disney Channel star's career when they go a little ~wild~. Some focus that energy into partying and tabloid drama (not naming names, we all know who they are). Others show the world they've grown up by taking on the LEAST Disney-like role they can find. From Keke Palmer playing a stripper in Hustlers to Zac Efron portraying serial killer Ted Bundy in Extremely Wicked, Shockingly Evil and Vile, here are 50 former Disney stars in their most ~shocking~ roles.
