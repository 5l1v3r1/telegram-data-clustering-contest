PSL Teams In Action!  

15 Nov 2019, 13:49 by Soccer Laduma

While waiting for the restart of the Absa Premiership season after the FIFA international break, Baroka FC and Highlands Park have decided to play a competitive friendly match today.  

The match is scheduled to take place at Balfour Park in Johannesburg.

Bakgaga will host in-form Golden Arrows at Peter Mokaba Stadium in their next match after the break on November 27, while the Lions of the North will welcome Bidvest Wits to the Makhulong Stadium on November 26.

Baroka received a morale booster of a 1-0 win over Polokwane City in their last match before the break.

The team has moved up to 12th on the log. The Lions of the North are 6th on the standings, having drawn 0-0 with Stellenbosch in their last match before the break.
