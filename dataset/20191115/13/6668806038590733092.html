10 pictures of Ranveer Singh & Deepika Padukone from the sets of Ram Leela

15 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Vedanshi Pathak

Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone are adored by millions of fans separately and as a couple too. The duo got married last year on November 14 and 15 and it is rather interesting that the film on the sets of which the couple's love blossomed also released on November 15 in 2013. Goliyon Ki Raasleela: Ram Leela, directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali hit the theatres six years back and gave the audience an on-screen pair which captivated them with their chemistry in no time. A lovestory, the film had Ranveer and Deepika setting the screen on fire with their romance and it went on to be a big hit at the box-office.

Today, as the film's release completes six years we thought about treating our readers with pictures of Ranveer and Deepika from the sets of the film that show the sparks were not just flying on the big screen but off it too. These 10 pictures take you behind the scenes where Deepika and Ranveer are seen rehearing for various romantic sequences from the film as well as chilling between the shots.
