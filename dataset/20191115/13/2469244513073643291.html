Rich Kids Go Skint star from Exeter hits back at trolls who branded her 'thick'

Last night's episode of Rich Kids Go Skint followed Devon, as she went to stay with the Gibbins family, who are a lot less well off than herself

15 Nov 2019, 13:16 by Jess Morcom

A loaded trainee solicitor from Exeter has hit back at online trolls who branded her 'thick as sh**' after her appearance on Rich Kids Go Skint.

Last night's episode followed Devon, as she swapped her pampered lifestyle and parents mega-mansion for life with a debt-ridden family of five in Lincoln.

Devon is used to receiving a £500 monthly allowance from her parents, who also pay her bills - and they bail her out if she is every short of cash.

Not only does she spend a lot on designer items, from Gucci and Louis Vuitton bags to £800 on a designer coat, but she also pays out £100 a week on takeaways.

She lives in a mansion the size of three small houses which has an incredible 12 bathrooms - three of which are just for her.

And last night, she received the shock of her life when she went to stay with the Gibbins family.

Paul and Sammie have three young kids and survive on a combined salary and benefits of £1,730 a month.

After bills, childcare and food, they have a little over £50 for the rest of the month.

They are also in £3,000 worth of debt.

Despite Devon's efforts to adapt to the Gibbins' lifestyle, by helping fix a bathroom door, going hunting for clothes and gifts in a charity shop and even attempting to cook a meal for the family, viewers were left in shock by the contrast between the two lifestyles.

Some took to Twitter, to challenge Devon on her efforts.

"How do you turn the hob on?!," one wrote.

"Honestly, her parents have got a lot to answer for, they should be embarassed #RichKidsGoSkint.

Another said: "100k on her "education" and still tick as pig s**t #RichKidsGoSkint"
