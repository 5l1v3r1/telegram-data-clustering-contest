Indian Community Rejoices as Government Pulls Bill to Ban Crypto from Winter Parliament

15 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Ashwath Balakrishnan

The Indian government released the list of bills they plan on presenting in the parliament’s winter session, and to the surprise of the Indian cryptocurrency ecosystem, the bill to ban virtual currencies was not on the list. While this is good news for the domestic industry in the short term, it is unknown if they plan to present the bill at a later date or drop their plans altogether, November 15, 2019.

Current Outlook for Indian Cryptosphere

The draft bill to ban cryptocurrencies was the only hindrance that the country’s domestic industry was facing. Fundamental developments are as robust as they have ever been, with several startups from InstaDapp to Matic Network going live in 2019.
