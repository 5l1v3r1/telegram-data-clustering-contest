Loose Women's Saira Khan admits she's stopped wearing her wedding ring

Saira Khan, 49, explained that she doesn't feel like she has to wear a ring to prove that she has a happy marriage

15 Nov 2019, 13:12 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Saira Khan has admitted that she's stopped wearing her wedding and engagement rings.

Speaking on ITV's Loose Women, the panelist, 49, explained that she doesn't feel like she has to wear a ring to prove that she has a happy marriage.

She's also waiting for her engagement ring to be repaired after she lost the diamond while she was working out at the gym.

But Saira did confess that it upset her when her husband Steve Hyde stopped wearing his rings.

Saira, who married Steve in 2004 and shares two children with him, explained: "I haven't worn them for quite a while for many reasons.

"I work out quite a bit, I did used to wear my wedding and engagement rings but I was doing burpees and I got home and realised my diamond had fallen out."

Saira then removed her rings but it took a few weeks for her husband to realise they were gone.

She continued: "I took it off and didn't think anything more of it and I genuinely didn't think Steve would think anything of it.

"He was really, really upset about it."

A few weeks later Steve stopped wearing his wedding ring and Saira was hurt.

She added: "I thought I'd never notice something like that but it did actually really bother me.

"It pained me a little bit and it did make me upset."

Her co-star Denise Welch then jokingly rung Steve up on the phone and said he was angry with Saira.

Denise said: "I've got Steve on the phone and he's spitting feathers."
