Prince tribute @ Town Hall w/ Spike Lee, New Power Generation, more

15 Nov 2019, 13:34 by Bill Pearis

Prince memoir The Beautiful Ones was published in October, and to celebrate there’s a special tribute show happening at NYC’s Town Hall on Monday, November 18. The evening will feature performance by New Power Generation and special guests, and speakers include Spike Lee, Prince’s editor Chris Jackson and memoir co-author Dan Piepenbring. Tickets are on sale; they come with a copy of The Beautiful Ones, and proceeds from the event go to Harlem Children’s Zone.
