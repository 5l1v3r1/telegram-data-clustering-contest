Sun: Tottenham want Philippe Coutinho

Pochettino's side may try to bring in the Barca man, currently at Bayern on loan

15 Nov 2019, 13:03 by sport.es

Mauricio Pochettino's Tottenham want Barcelona's Brazilian mdifielder Philippe Coutinho. The playmaker is currently on loan at Bayern Munich.

Per The Sun, Spurs - who once wanted to sign Coutinho - are interested in him again, with the idea that he would join next summer.

Poch knows Coutinho well having coached him at Espanyol, where he played on loan for a season from Inter Milan.

Spurs were interested last summer but Bayern eventually sealed a loan deal. Cou has five goals in 16 matches.

However he is not adapting well at Bayern and has come in for criticism. The Sun say Poch has asked Spurs' board to sign him.
