Social Media Reacts To Bafana’s Ghana Catastrophe

15 Nov 2019, 13:00 by SL Social

It’s safe to say that fans were left less than pleased after Bafana Bafana succumbed to a 2-0 defeat at the hands of Ghana on Thursday evening. 

Check out some of the social media reactions after the match.

Goals from Thomas Partey and Mohammed Kudus ensured Bafana suffered their first loss under Molefi Ntseki. 

There was widespread displeasure at the fact that the match was not broadcast on South African television and fans had to make do with live streaming.

With many fans catching a glimpse of the match highlights on social media the following day, fans took to Twitter to show their displeasure.

There were some hilarious responses from fans, with some saying that Bafana dampened the spirit after the Boks’ incredible Rugby World Cup victory.
