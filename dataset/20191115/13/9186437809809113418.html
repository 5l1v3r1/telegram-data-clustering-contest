Which Sony products will receive Android 10 upgrades?

15 Nov 2019, 13:07

Google has announced Android 10, so you’re probably wondering when your trusty Xperia device will get the shiny new upgrade. Well, look no further than this blog – as we have all the news you’re waiting for!

Owners of Sony’s premium flagship Xperia 1 and recently announced Xperia 5 will be among the first to receive the new tech, which will be available this December.

Owners of Xperia 10, 10 Plus, XZ2, XZ2 Compact, XZ2 Premium and XZ3 devices can expect to access the upgrade early 2020 onwards*.

*Software rollouts are a phased process – timing and availability will vary by market and/or operator. Most models supported but some operator/market exceptions will apply.
