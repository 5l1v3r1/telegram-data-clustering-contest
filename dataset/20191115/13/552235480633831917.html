Tennent's Lager offering free pints to Liam Gallagher fans ahead of Glasgow gig

The brewer has asked people to come up with the best Oasis/Tennent's related puns in exchange for a pint before Liam Gallagher's Hydro show on Friday night.

15 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Dailyrecord.co.uk

Tennent’s Lager is offering free pints to Liam Gallagher fans ahead of his headline show in Glasgow .

The Scots brewer is calling for fans to come up with their best Oasis/Tennents puns which can be exchanged for a pre-gig pint at the Dirty Duchess in Finnieston area of the city.

It comes as the former Oasis rockstar has touched down in Glasgow for his show at the SSE Hydro on Friday night.

The promotion is in memory of the Manchester band’s special connection to the Scottish city and Tennent’s Lager, reports Glasgow Live .

 

Oasis were famously signed by Alan McGee after playing a set in Glasgow’s iconic King Tut’s Wah Wah Hut in 1993. And, instead of being paid in cold hard cash, they were paid with cold cans of Tennent’s for their thirsty work. 

In a fitting tribute to the Gallagher brother’s return, Tennent’s are giving punters the chance to win a pint themselves – provided their pun game is nailed down to a T.

If you'd like to take part Tennent’s Lager Twitter channel here from 10am today and get ready to show off you best Oasis/Tennent’s puns. The top submissions will be selected at 2pm and the first pint will be on the house at the Dirty Duchess on the Finnieston strip. 

And who knows, maybe Liam will join you?

The star has already tweeted his excitement about the up coming gig.

Taking to social media, he wrote: "Glasgow Glasgow Glasgow c’mon you know LG x”

James Hughes, Tennent’s brand manager, said: “Liam Gallagher returning to Glasgow is massively exciting for the city - he’s an incredible talent who has contributed a lot to Scottish culture through his music.

"We know a lot of Tennent’s fans are also fans of his work, so what better way to celebrate his visit than to offer those fans a chance to win a pre-gig pint?  Of course, if Liam fancies a pint himself, we’re sure we could arrange one. After all, he’s One of Us.”
