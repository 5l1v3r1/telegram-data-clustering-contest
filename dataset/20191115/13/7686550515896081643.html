9 reasons to come Holm for Christmas

Make your Christmas one to remember as luxury Penarth hotel Holm House transforms into a winter wonderland this Christmas

15 Nov 2019, 13:47 by Jonny Dillon

Whatever type of Christmas you’re looking for, Holm House Hotel can turn your Christmas wishes into reality. The Penarth-based property can cater for everything from those intimate gatherings to big Christmas parties.

Here are nine reasons why you should spend Christmas with Holm House.

1. Relax at Holm

Enjoy a pampered journey of relaxation and escape at Holm House Spa. Take a dip in the hydrotherapy pool, indulge with a luxurious Elemis treatment and then retire to the relaxation room with its terrace that boasts views across Windsor gardens and the Bristol channels.

Take advantage of Holm House’s festive spa package which includes a 60-minute treatment, full use of spa facilities, a two-course lunch in the restaurant and a glass of prosecco from only £89 this Christmas.

2. Sing your heart out at Holm

Pencil Tuesday December 10 into your diary and enjoy a night of Christmas carols from the Cardiff Bach Choir. Tickets include a delicious hot turkey, stuffing and cranberry baguette with a glass of mulled wine. Bring along family and friends for the ultimate festive cheer.

3. Feel at Holm

Holm House scooped the Best Boutique Wedding Venue in Wales at the Welsh National Wedding Awards this November. See what the hype is about for yourself and book a consultation with Holm House's wedding coordinators.

They’ll run through your vision and tell you more about the fabulous packages, menus and facilities. Imagine exclusive use of the picturesque Grade-II listed house which you can transform as you wish to realise your big day vision.

4. Shop ‘til you drop at Holm

Vouchers might seem an afterthought but a Holm House gift voucher is always received with delight. Treat a loved one to a specific package or monetary vouchers.

It doesn’t stop there either, Holm House also stocks luxurious gifts from Molton Brown, Elemis and La Sultane de Saba.

5. Stay at Holm

Christmas can be a frenzied time thanks to juggling Christmas parties and shopping on top of the pressures of everyday work and life. Escape to Holm House to discover a world of calm.

Enjoy a relaxing night’s sleep in one of the stylish suites, soak in your roll top bath, order room service, head to the spa or to the bar, it’s all up to you!

6. Eat at Holm

The best cuisine always begins with the finest ingredients and at Holm House you can expect to enjoy dishes focused on the very best of local and seasonal produce. Its festive menus have been lovingly created to ensure you have an unforgettable culinary experience when you indulge in lunch, dinner or festive afternoon tea at the AA Rosette restaurant. Don’t forget to treat yourself to one of South Wales’ best Sunday roasts too - they’re the stuff of legend. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointment.

7. Drink at Holm

Curl up by the open fire with a glass of mulled wine in hand and enjoy an intimate catch up or date night in the cosy Snug Room. The main cocktail bar offers a more buzzing atmosphere and boasts one of the best cocktail menus in the city, as well as an extensive wine and champagne list.

8. Party at Holm

Head to Holm House on Saturday December 20 for the festive party night where you can expect delicious food, DJ and live entertainment. You can also explore the exclusive hire options if you have a large party or corporate do to organise. Tables of two to 16 available.

9. Ring in the New Year at Holm

Start the new year as you mean to go on - in style! Holm House's New Years Eve gala dinner offers a lavish five-course menu from the AA rosette kitchen, a festive cocktail on arrival, table wine and live entertainment, from only £120 per person.

Suitable for just the two of you or a big group, the team will make the drinks and you can make the memories! There’s always the option to stay over too and truly immerse yourself in the Holm House experience.
