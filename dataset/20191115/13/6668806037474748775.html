Sidharth Malhotra’s talks about his upcoming film Shershaah

15 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Poonam Jain

Sidharth Malhotra who made his debut with Karan Johar’s Student of the year is quite excited for the release of his next Shershaah. The movie will have Sidharth playing the role of a soldier. The actor’s latest release Marjaavaan might have received mixed reviews from the critics as well as the audience but that isn’t stopping the actor from being motivated about his next venture.

The actor in his interview to a leading news portal spoke about his upcoming film Shershaah. Directed by Vishnuvardhan this Dharma Productions’ venture will have Sidharth playing a real life character for the first time. Talking about the same he said,  “It's a special story, I've been trying to make this story for about two and a half-three years. It's a biopic on Captain Vikram Batra who was a soldier who gave his life for our country during the Kargil war and I'm giving out his life story. For the first time I am playing a real life character. We have pretty much finished shooting and have only a few more days left.”

Having played varied roles in the past, we can’t wait to watch the actor take on the big screen in this avatar.
