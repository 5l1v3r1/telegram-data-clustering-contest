Wizkid promises Ugandans an epic comeback show

15 Nov 2019, 18:39 by Reagan Ssempijja

This follows Wizkid’s successful worldwide tour that has seen him perform in countries like Australia, United Kingdom, Portugal, France and most recently, Tanzania.

ENTERTAINMENT

KAMPALA - The last time Ugandan partiers heard of a Wizkid show, anticipations rose higher than ever before and the excitement was indeed felt everywhere. The star put up an energetic show and did it in full blast in 2017.

The performance was epic but quite frankly, the crowd was left yearning for more. 

Fortunately, the Nigerian born Singer Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun, widely known as Wizkid, will return with a bang to offer yet another performance in Kampala on December 5 at Kololo Airstrip.  

This follows Wizkid’s successful worldwide tour that has seen him perform in countries like Australia, United Kingdom, Portugal, France and most recently, Tanzania, with Bongo flavor star, Diamond Platnumz at the Wasafi Festival. 

The show will also have performances from some of the top artistes in Uganda’s Music Industry.

“Wizkid is set to jet into the country a day before his concert and he will be live on stage on December 5,” said the organizers of the show, ExW 

“I can’t wait to come to Uganda. It’s one of my favorite countries in the world because it shows me so much, love. I can’t wait to turn up and give my fans an electrifying performance on December 5,” Wizkid said. 

He went on to say, “I am excited to give my fans an amazing surprise concert. It’s going to be epic,” he concluded.

Wizkid has so far registered hit songs and won international accolades. He continues to be an inspiration to the artistes of his time.
