An absolute bargain at this price, the Huawei M6 Turbo Edition is loaded with exciting features.

First off, the tablet features an 8.4-inches IPS Capacitive screen with a high resolution of 2560 x 1600 pixels for vivid visual experience and enhanced contrast. Furthermore, it is powered by a HiSilicon Kirin 980 processor and packs GPU Turbo 3.0, along with Mali G76 720MHz graphics processing unit under the hood.
