Disney+'s Loki Series In Talks With Yesterday Actor to Co-Star

15 Nov 2019, 18:11 by L.D. Nolan

Disney+'s Loki series may be getting a little bigger.

According to Variety, Sophia Di Martino is in talks with Marvel Studios to join Loki. It's unknown at this time who Di Martino would play if she does indeed sign on for the project. Marvel did not comment on the potential casting, so at this time it's possible negotiations will breakdown and the actor will not appear in Loki.

Di Martino has appeared in a variety of films and TV series. She recently played Carol in this year's Yesterday, which explores a world in which the Beatles never existed. Di Martino also played Lily on Into the Badlands.

Little is known about Loki at this time. However, the Disney+ series will tie into Doctor Strange and the Multiverse of Madness, much like WandaVision. The show will seemingly involve time travel and follow the 2012 version of Loki, who escaped with the Tesseract during Avengers: Endgame, as he alters historical events.

Loki, starring Tom Hiddleston, is set to hit Disney+ sometime in Spring 2021. Rick & Morty writer Michael Waldron will write the series and be billed as creator, with Sex Education's Kate Herron directing all the episodes.
