Russian airstrike destroys militant base in southern Idlib: video

15 Nov 2019, 18:10 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (8:00 P.M.) – The Russian Air Force carried out a powerful attack over the Idlib Governorate, as their warplanes targeted one of the bases belonging to the Turkish-backed National Liberation Front (NLF) in the town of Hass.
