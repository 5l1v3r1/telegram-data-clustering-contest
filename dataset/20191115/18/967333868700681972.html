Trump in First Zelensky Call Praised Ukraine's Representation at 'Miss Universe' - Memo

15 Nov 2019, 18:57

WASHINGTON (Sputnik) - US President Donald Trump told his Ukrainian counterpart Volodymyr Zelensky that Ukraine had always been "very well represented" at the "Miss Universe" beauty pageant he formerly managed, according to a rough transcript of their first phone call released by the White House on Friday.

"When I owned Miss Universe, they always had great people. Ukraine was always very well represented", Trump is quoted as saying.
