‘Sin City’ In The Works To Become A TV Series

15 Nov 2019, 18:44 by Jamie Samhan

Sin City author Frank Miller has sold the rights to his comic books to Legendary TV so they can make them into a TV series.

The noir-film influenced comics were previously made into the “Sin City” movies and Robert Rodriguez, who directed the films, is set to executive produce. Stephen L’Heureux, who produced the sequel “Sin City: A Dame to Kill”, is also in talks to produce.

Legendary TV promised a first season as long as they can find distribution with a network or streaming platform.

The indie studio’s deal with Miller also includes an animated prequel series.

Legendary has an impressive collection of shows including “The Expanse”, “Carnival Row”, “Lost in Space” and “Dune: The Sisterhood”.
