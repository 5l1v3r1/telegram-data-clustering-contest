Ideal way of living

15 Nov 2019, 18:35 by Manila Standard

P.A.-Hankyu One, the joint venture between homegrown P.A. Properties and Osaka-based Hankyu Hanshin Properties Corp., recently welcomed its first batch of proud homeowners to their new homes at Idesia Dasmariñas. 

“The hand-over ceremony signifies the start of our homeowners’ new life in a community where they will experience the serenity of countryside living while enjoying the convenience of urban lifestyle,” said P.A. Properties president Jonathan G. Lu. Idesia Dasmariñas is the flagship project of the joint venture. The 37-hectare township is a masterplanned community that will cater to urbanites; startup, growing or middle-income families; and those who seek to upgrade their total community living. Apart from affordable yet attractive dwellings, they will get to live in a property that preserves the original greenery of the site, plus the addition of more trees and flowering plants. Construction of the housing units is ongoing, as well as amenities which include reading, picnic and leisure areas; jogging path; indoor and outdoor gyms; basketball and badminton courts; and a provision for an outdoor cinema.
