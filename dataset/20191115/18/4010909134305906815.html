You can now buy the Mate 30 series without any referral code, get free gifts worth up to RM537

15 Nov 2019, 18:04

If you’re a fan of Huawei, you should know that Huawei’s latest Mate 30 flagship series has officially started selling in Malaysia from today onwards (15 November 2019)! This time, you can choose to purchase either Huawei Mate 30 for RM2799 or Huawei Mate 30 Pro for RM3899 without any referral code. 

Other than that, for those who purchase the device can also get exclusive FREE gifts worth up to RM537 on a first come first serve basis! The free gifts include a Huawei SuperCharge Wireless Car Charger and 1 year extended warranty from Huawei Care. Other than the Mate 30 series, there are also other Huawei products you can buy and get free gifts as well.

Huawei Mate 30's specs:

  • 6.62-inch FHD+OLED display (2340 x 1080)
  • Kirin 990 chipset
  • 8GB RAM
  • 128GB storage (expandable up to 256GB)
  • 4200mAh battery
  • Huawei SuperCharge (40W), Wireless Huawei SuperCharge (27W)
  • USB Type-C, 3.5mm audio jack
  • Back cameras: 40 MP ( SuperSensing Camera, f/1.8 aperture) + 16 MP (Ultra Wide Angle Camera, f/2.2 aperture) + 8 MP (Telephoto Camera, f/2.4 aperture, OIS)
  • 24 MP front camera (f/2.0 aperture)
  • running EMUI10 based on HMS

Huawei Mate 30 Pro's specs:

  • 6.53-inch FHD+OLED display (2400 x 1176), 88° ultra-curved screen
  • Kirin 990 chipset
  • 8GB RAM
  • 256GB storage 
  • 4500mAh battery
  • Huawei SuperCharge (40W), Wireless Huawei SuperCharge (27W)
  • USB Type-C
  • Back cameras: 40 MP ( Cine Camera, f/1.8 aperture) + 40 MP (SuperSensing Camera, f/1.6 aperture, OIS) + 8 MP (Telephoto Camera, f/2.4 aperture, OIS) + 3D Depth Sensing Camera supports Autofocus (phase focus, contrast focus), supports AIS (Huawei AI Image Stabilization).
  • 32 MP front camera (f/2.0 aperture)
  • supports NFC
  • IP68 rating for dust and water resistant
  • Virtual Volume Turner
  • running EMUI10 based on HMS
