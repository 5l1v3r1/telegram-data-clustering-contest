When is the next episode of The Morning Show and how can you watch it?

15 Nov 2019, 18:30

The Morning Show, starring Jennifer Aniston and Reese Witherspoon, has defied critics to win praise from fans, with some even going as far as to say that Jen should win a Golden Globe for her performance.

For those who have been enjoying the show, the good news is that a second season of the series has already been confirmed and, if that wasn’t enough of a reason to catch up on all the episodes so far, there’s also a new episode on the horizon.

When is the next episode of The Morning Show on?

There have been four episodes of The Morning Show available to watch on Apple TV Plus so far.

In last wee’s episode, titled That Woman, the network experiences growing pains while Alex (Aniston) and Bradley (Witherspoon) must adjust to their new roles as onscreen co-workers.

Fans of the show will probably already know that the next instalment of the show, episode five, is out today, Friday 15 November, following Apple’s pattern of releasing a new one every Friday.

The new episode, called No One’s Gonna Harm You, Not While I’m Around, is available to stream now on Apple TV Plus.

How can you watch The Morning Show?

The Morning Show was one of the first shows to be available on Apple’s new streaming service Apple TV Plus.

You can access Apple TV Plus through Apple’s TV app and a monthly subscription to the service costs £4.99 a month (but you can also sign up for a free seven-day trial).

If you buy a new iPad, iPhone, iPod touch, Mac or Apple TV2, you’ll also get free access to Apple TV Plus for a year.
