Changanassery tops in arts fete

Kuravilangadu wins both HS, UP categories in Kottayam district fete

15 Nov 2019, 18:13 by Staff Reporter

As curtain came down on the 32nd Kottayam revenue district school arts festival here on Friday, the Changanassery sub-district with 406 points emerged overall champions in the Higher Secondary School category while Kuravilangadu emerged winners in both the High School and UP School categories.

Spirited race

Changanassery staged a spirited race to win a clear majority while Kottayam East, which emerged the runners-up, scored just 353 points. Kuravilangadu stood at the third position with 307 points.

Neck-and-neck race

On the contrary, the High School category witnessed a neck-and-neck race among the first three teams.

While Kuravilangadu scored 280 points to win the title, Kottayam East and Kanjirappally sub-district scored 277 and 274 points respectively to reach the second and third positions.

Kuravilangadu won the title in the UP section with 129 points, while Erattupetta and Kanjirappally shared the second position by scoring 126 points each.

Sanskrit, Arabic fete

Kottayam West emerged winners in the Sanskrit festival in both UP and High School categories while Erattupetta won the Arabic festival in both the categories.

MGM HSS, Lakkattoor, retained the overall championship in the individual school category of the Higher Secondary School section for the 20th consecutive year. The school which arrived for the competition with 77 students returned with 157 points. Of the 33 events it participated, its students secured ‘A’ grade in 30 events and won first in Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, classical music, patriotic songs and instrumental music.

Crossroads School, Pampady, continued its dominance in the High School section by winning the overall title for the 13th consecutive year.

The school participated in 51 events and won 46 ‘A’ grades.
