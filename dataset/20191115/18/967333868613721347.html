UK Police Rush to Buckingham Palace as ‘Suspicious Car Abandoned Outside the Gates' - Report

On 1 November, Vauxhall Bridge in London was shut down amid a police investigation of an area where an unidentified vehicle had been abandoned.

15 Nov 2019, 18:38

British police were earlier reported to have rushed to Buckingham Palace in order to investigate a "suspicious" car abandoned outside the gates. Police were said to be closing nearby roads after receiving unconfirmed reports of a bomb.
