Rumour: New Mass Effect Game in Early Development at BioWare

Massive effect

15 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Robert Ramsey

We know for sure that BioWare is currently working on the next Dragon Age -- commonly referred to as Dragon Age 4 -- but according to a lengthy report by Jason Schreier on Kotaku, that's not all BioWare has in the oven. Alongside what sounds like it could be a significant overhaul of the ill-fated ANTHEM, the Canadian developer apparently has a new Mass Effect title in the early stages of development at its Edmonton base.

The article doesn't go into any real detail on the subject, but it does state that the project is supposedly being headed by studio veteran Mike Gamble. Gamble was a lead producer on both ANTHEM and Mass Effect: Andromeda. Before that, he was a producer on Mass Effect 3 and its DLC, and a project manager on Mass Effect 2.

If accurate, this leak comes as no huge surprise. Back at E3 last year, BioWare said that it would like return to Mass Effect at some point, and it then teased "secret" Mass Effect projects a month later. In the meantime, why not release a remastered Mass Effect Trilogy? It's not like we've spent the entire generation asking for it or anything...
