Kieran Hayler bonds with Bunny and Jett after news Katie Price will spend Xmas alone

Kieran is making the most of the lead up to Xmas with his kids following the news they will spend Christmas with him

15 Nov 2019, 18:20 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Kieran Hayler enthused about bonding time with his kids Bunny and Jett as it's emerged Katie Price will spend Christmas on her own.

It come to light earlier on Friday, the dad-of-two will have the delight of waking up with his two children on Christmas day this year.

The proud dad has been making the most of his time with the kids in the lead up to the Xmas period as the happy trio enjoyed a winter stroll over the evening.

Kieran snapped a picture of Bunny, five, and Jett, six, wrapped up warm in their winter coats as they watched the sun set together.

He wrote: "I take the picture, but nature paints it."

Kieran added the hashtags: Blessed, proud dad, nature, afternoon walks.

It comes as Katie is not looking forward to the festive period.

The former model, 41, is said to be "dreading" Christmas as four of her five kids are spending the holidays with their dads and is planning to to head to warmer climes with her on and off again beau Kris Boyson.

Her children Junior, 14, and Princess, 12, who she shares with ex Peter Andre will be with him and her two little ones Jett and Bunny are going to be waking up on the big day at their dad Kieran's home.

Katie's first son Harvey, 17, who is partially blind and autistic and has a genetic disorder named Prader-Willi syndrome, is thought to be remaining in residential care over the period.

A source told The Sun Online : "Katie is dreading this Christmas, as she only has her eldest son Harvey at home with her.

"She says she has nothing to stay in England for as Harvey is likely to remain in residential care, and hates the cold so is looking at going abroad instead.

"Katie has told her friends that she won't be able to afford decent presents anyway and is leaving it to the children's dads to splash the cash instead."

The Mirror reached out to Katie Price for further comment at the time.
