8 Holiday Makeup Palettes to Get You in the Spirit

15 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Hana Hong and Taylore Glynn

Shopping for a beauty aficionado can be tricky because there are so many factors to consider. You want to snag an item that’s budget-friendly yet substantial enough to hold its own. A run-of-the-mill lipstick might not be the right shade, and a tub of face cream might not be compatible with his or her skin type.

Well, take it from a beauty maven herself: One can never have too many palettes. And with the multitude of pigment options enclosed in these festive covers, there’s bound to be a shade they’ll use. So if you are shopping for a verified beauty lover this holiday season, find some gifting inspiration in our favorite picks, below.
