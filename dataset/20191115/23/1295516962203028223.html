Did Frank Miller Seriously Write the 'Ninja Rap' From Ninja Turtles 2?

15 Nov 2019, 23:11 by Brian Cronin

MOVIE URBAN LEGEND: Frank Miller wrote the "Ninja Rap" for Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles 2.

The second Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles film is probably not the most memorable sequel in comic book film history, but it is still well remembered for the bizarre "Ninja Rap" by Vanilla Ice, which included the Turtles fighting some bad guys inside of a night club and then the rapper (played by Vanilla Ice) coming up with a rap that the Turtls then dance to during the rap performance, with Vanilla Ice infamously rapping "Go Ninja, Go Ninja, Go!"
