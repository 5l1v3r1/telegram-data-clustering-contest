BD-ET vs IND-ET Dream11 : Both sides face their first real challenge when familiar faces square off against each other today.

Well versed and aware of each other capabilities, the junior teams of Bangladesh and India are two outfits who each other quietly nicely. The two have gone at loggerheads on a haord of occasions across the course of 2019, navigating their way through different countries and tournamnets with India rejoicing on the majority of occasions.

It’s a gripping narrative the overwhelming favourites for the ACC Emerging Teams Asia Cup will be looking to emanate come today as well when they take on Bangladesh in one of the four clashes scheduled for the day. Today’s match is pivotal owing not only due to the two points at stake from this clash but with the winner of the tie set to put forward their bid for first place in the group standings.

Both the sides are making an advent into today’s face off against each other having tasted consummate success the last time around with astounding wins seeing the sides set a statement. Playing against Nepal, India eased their way to a three wicket win as they chased down the meagre 194 run target set infront of them with a full 8 overs to spare.

Elsewhere, playing against Hong Kong in their tournamnet opener, Bangladesh cantered their way to a colossal 9 wicket win, one which saw them chase down the target of 165 without even requiring half of the overs at their disposal. Given their previous displays and the staunch stature of both of our opponents of the day, this tie is poised to be one of the most enticing and riveting affairs 

Pitch Conditions And Toss

An extremely exquisite batting surface, the batting conducive conditions prevelant over here are going to make this is a high scoring encounter.
