Children in Need host apologises for technical issues as child violinist cut off

15 Nov 2019, 20:28

Ade Adepitan apologised for technical difficulties that plagued Children in Need live on TV.

The presenter, who is co-hosting the event with Tess Daly, issued the apology after a live segment in London went awry.

Viewers watching from the capital saw the live feed, direct from the Royal Mail sorting office, cut short as the show cut to an advert and then back to the studio.

Many were upset that a child playing the violin live on the segment was cut away from and his time in the spotlight ruined.

‘We are experiencing technical problems in London,’ Ade confirmed. ‘So apologies for that.’

He then quipped: ‘Probably some wet leaves in the wi-fi or something.’

Every single Tube user just had a shiver down their spine. Don’t mention wet leaves!

Fans watching the show live from home certainly had some thoughts about the on-air problems.
