Baghdad bomb kills two, injures 12: security sources

15 Nov 2019, 20:20

BAGHDAD (Reuters) - An improvised explosive device went off in Baghdad’s Tayaran Square, killing two people and injuring 12, Iraqi security sources said late on Friday.

It was not immediately clear if the incident was related to anti-government protests going on in the capital’s nearby Tahrir Square.
