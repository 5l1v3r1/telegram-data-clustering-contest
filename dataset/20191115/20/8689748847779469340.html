Did Fuse ODG Plead With The Police To Stop Arresting Prostitutes? –Watch This Video

15 Nov 2019, 20:20 by BB Jones

Fuse ODG has pleaded with government to stop arresting prostitutes and instead create jobs for them.

The singer did not make this passionate appeal directly but through his new song titled “Osu”, which features an Osu based rapper called Toyboi.

Shedding the spotlight on the song and factors that contribute to the high rate of prostitution, Fuse ODG said most of them move from their rural areas with the aim of making a living for themselves and their families.

He suggested that solid measures should be put in place to improve the economy, create good jobs and protect them rather than arresting them.
