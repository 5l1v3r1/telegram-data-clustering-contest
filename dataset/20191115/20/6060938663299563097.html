'Marjaavaan': Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh and Tara Sutaria starrer leaked online

15 Nov 2019, 20:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh and Tara Sutaria's 'Marjaavaan' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. The film team left no stones unturned to promote their flick on various platforms.

And on Friday, the film has hit the theatres, however, unfortunately, it became the latest victim of piracy as it got leaked online on the same day.

According to reports making rounds on the internet, the infamous TamilRockers are responsible for leaking the film online. Several films like 'Dream Girl' 'Super 30', 'Bharat', 'Kabir Singh', 'Avengers Endgame' and 'MIB: International' were made available on the internet by the piracy site.

Reportedly, the web portal keeps changing the domain extension in order to keep running the site illegally.

Meanwhile, 'Marjaavaan' is helmed by Milap Zaveri and also stars Rakul Preet Singh.
