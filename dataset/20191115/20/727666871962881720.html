Sean "Diddy" Combs Mourns Kim Porter In Touching Post

15 Nov 2019, 20:00 by AllHipHop Staff

The business mogul took to Instagram to pay some respect to his ex Kim Porter, who died unexpectedly last year.

(AllHipHop News) Rap mogul Diddy is honoring the memory of his late ex Kim Porter a year after her tragic death.

The Bad Boy Records founder was left devastated when the model and mother to three of his children, passed away unexpectedly of pneumonia at her Los Angeles home in November 2018.

On Thursday, he took to Instagram to share a broken heart emoji with the caption "KP."

The former couple dated on and off from 1994 to 2007, but Diddy and Kim remained close until her death.

The rap mogul vowed to prioritize his family after Kim's untimely death of the 47-year-old, who was the mother to three of Diddy's six kids.

"Before this, I was a part-time father, you know?" he told Essence magazine. "My family was always first, but there are countless times when I chose work over everything else."
