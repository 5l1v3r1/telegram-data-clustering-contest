Last chance to get tickets to the 2019 Hurling Classic, competition winners revealed

15 Nov 2019, 20:54 by IrishCentral Staff

The 2019 Hurling Classic in Citi Field is TOMORROW!

Today is your last chance to get tickets for the GAA event of the year.

This weekend, the popular Irish sport of hurling is coming to Citi Field for the “New York Hurling Classic,” a four-team, three-game tournament, and to accompany the hurling action, a lively Irish festival will take place leading up to the first game of the tournament, complete with Irish music, dancing and more.

During the week, we ran a competition for free tickets and we're glad to announce the winners, below. If you were not lucky this time, you still have a few hours to pick up tickets yourself here.

Gates will open at 11:00 a.m. with the three-game tournament beginning at 12:30 p.m. ET. For more information about the New York Hurling Classic and to purchase tickets, please visit www.mets.com/hurling.
