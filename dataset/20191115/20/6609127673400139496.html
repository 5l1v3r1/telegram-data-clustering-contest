COP 'CHASE'

Two rushed to hospital as car crashes after ‘speeding away from police’ in Glasgow as two arrested

15 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Ewan Mowat

TWO people have been arrested after a car crashed in Glasgow after reportedly speeding away from police.

Cops were trying to trace the motor in Castlemilk this afternoon when the alleged smash occurred - with two people rushed to hospital.

The car was spotted by officers on Carmunnock Road but reportedly drove off and wouldn't stop.

Soon after, it crashed into another motor on Holmlea Road.

Cops arrested two people from the car.

Ambulance crews also attended the scene of the crash.

Two people were raced to hospital and treated for their injuries.

Police have launched a probe into the alleged incident.

A spokesperson for the force said: "Around 1.50pm on Friday 15 November 2019, enquiries were ongoing to trace a car in the Castlemilk area of Glasgow.

“The car was located in the Carmunnock Road area of Croftfoot, however, it failed to stop.

“Officers stood down and the car collided with another vehicle a short time later on Holmlea Road at Ruel Street and two occupants of the car were arrested.

"Two people were taken to hospital where they were treated for minor injuries.

“Enquires into the circumstances surrounding this incident are continuing.”
