Steelers Fans Starts GoFundMe To Pay Maurkice Pouncey’s Fine For Defending Mason Rudolph

15 Nov 2019, 20:40 by Darrelle Lincoln

Pittsburgh Steelers fans want to put their money where their mouth is. The fan base wants to help cover the fine levied to Maurkice Pouncey for his role in the Thursday night brawl against the Cleveland Browns.

It was Myles Garrett who grabbed at Maosn Rudolph’s helmet and took it off when he unexpectedly swung it and hit him over the head with it. Rudolph’s teammates were pissed, but none more than Maurkice Pouncey who came in swinging multiple punches and kicking Garrett in the head for his actions.
