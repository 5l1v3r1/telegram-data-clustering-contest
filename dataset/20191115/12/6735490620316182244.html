Eva Amurri Reveals She Has Split From Husband Kyle Martino In The Middle Of Pregnancy

15 Nov 2019, 12:03 by Corey Atad

Eva Amurri and her husband have parted ways.

On Friday morning, Amurri and husband Kyle Martino both shared identical posts to Instagram featuring a photo of them together with their two young children, along with a message announcing their split.

“Our family is starting down a new path,” they wrote. “After a lot of thoughtful consideration, and work on our relationship, we have made the difficult decision to lovingly part ways as a couple.”

In September, Amurri revealed that she is pregnant with the couple’s third child.

“One of the biggest things Kyle and I have had to work on in our marriage is our different views of gender roles when it pertains to parenting,” Amurri wrote on her blog in announcing the pregnancy news. “For many years, he was involved the bare minimum in helping me with the kids, even though I was working full time.”
