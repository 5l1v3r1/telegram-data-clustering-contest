Marcus Rashford's Man Utd goals outlined as his mum digs out childhood keepsake

Marcus Rashford posted a throwback to something he wrote in school which shows how much he is living the dream as a Man Utd player

15 Nov 2019, 12:43 by Rich Jones, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rich-jones/

Manchester United striker Marcus Rashford has shared a heartwarming throwback which shows he is living the dream.

Local lad Rashford has risen through the ranks to become on of United's star players.

And he took to social media to share a picture of a document he wrote as a child that was dug out by his mother.

In the piece, he discusses his future ambitions and highlights his dream to succeed as a professional footballer with the Red Devils.

A section entitled "future and aims" said: "I hope that my future is very bright in and out of school, especially in my career as a footballer.

"I want to have a different lifestyle and make my family and others proud of me.

"I only have one aim in life and that is to be a professional football, and hopefully at Manchester United ."

Rashford then wrote about his "journey so far", stating: "My journey so far has been amazing.

"I have experienced a lot of things associated with Manchester United and with school. I have learned a lot since my birth and I am very proud of myself because I have got to where I am today.

"Many kids would love to do what I have done therefore I will always work hard and never take anything for granted.

"Thank you everyone and especially to those who have helped me on this breathtaking journey. I promise that I WILL work my hardest and I WILL try my best to become a professional footballer."

Rashford joined United aged just seven, bursting on the scene under Louis Van Gaal as a teenager in 2015.

The England international, 22, is now United's leading attacker and has scored six Premier League goals so far this term.

He also got on the scoresheet in England's 7-0 thrasing of Montenegro at Wembley on Thursday night.
