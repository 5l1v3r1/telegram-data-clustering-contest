Man convicted of murdering Steven Donaldson found dead in his prison cell

Steven Dickie was sentenced to a minimum of 23 years behind bars for his part in the murder of the 27-year-old last year.

15 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

A man who was convicted of killing Steven Donaldson has been found dead in his cell, according to reports.

Steven Dickie was sentenced to a minimum of 23 years behind bars for his part in the murder of the 27-year-old last year.

Prison officers discovered Dickie’s body at HMP Perth on Friday morning, according to the Evening Telegraph.

The Scottish Prison Service have been approached for comment.

 

Donaldson’s body was found battered and burned beside the charred shell of his BMW at Kirriemuir’s Loch of Kinnordy Nature Reserve in June 2018.

An autopsy revealed that he had been stabbed, struck with a baseball bat and had his spine severed by a sword or machete.

The Daily Record previously reported that Dickie, along with Callum Davidson and Tasmin Glass would be appealing their sentences for their part in the death of Donaldson.
