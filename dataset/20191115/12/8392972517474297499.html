Aldi selling gin selection box for Christmas but you’ll have to be quick to get one

We're upgrading our usual chocolate selection box!

15 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Nancy Brown

While the Cadbury's Selection Box will always hold a special place in our hearts, it's the new Aldi offering that we'd like to unwrap this festive season!

The humble selection box has had an over-18s makeover, with the bargain supermarket releasing a gin selection box.

Yes, you read that correctly.

Instead of the traditional bars of chocolate in your Christmas selection box, this year you can go boozy and unbox a selection of gins.

If that doesn't put you on the naughty list, we don't know what will!

The 12 Gins Of Christmas Selection Box costs £24.99 and it's on sale now.

Be quick though gin fans, this limited-edition boxset is only on sale while stocks last.

Inside the box you'll find 12 different gins and tonics by Scottish gin makers Eden Mill.

Flavours include Salted Caramel & Apple Gin with cola, Passion Fruit & Coconut Gin with pineapple juice, Blueberry & Vanilla Gin with American cream soda and Chilli & Ginger Gin with ginger ale.

The collection offers something for every gin lover this Christmas.

There are also a couple of cocktails – the Candy Cane promises to be particularly festive – and an alcohol-free Citrus Gin and tonic.

An Eden Mill spokesperson said: "Perfect for Christmas gifting or sharing with friends and family over the festive period, the collection offers something for every gin lover this Christmas."
