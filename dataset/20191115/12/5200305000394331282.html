Box Office: ‘Dil Bechara’ confirmed for cinema release in May 2020

15 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Raj Baddhan

After rumours earlier of a digital-exclusive release of Sushant Singh Rajput’s next ‘Dil Bechara’, makers have today denied the speculation, instead announcing a new cinema release date.

The film, which is based on the Hollywood film ‘The Fault in Our Stars’, also stars Sanjana Sanghi. It is directed by Mukesh Chhabra, with music by AR Rahman. The Fox Star Studios production, releases on 8th May 2020 at cinemas.
