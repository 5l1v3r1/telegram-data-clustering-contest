EastEnders fans pay tribute to 'iconic' Mel Owen after she dies in horrific accident

RIP Mel

15 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Helen Fear

Fans of EastEnders have paid tribute to "iconic" and "legendary" character Mel Owen after her shock death last night.

On Thursday night (November 14), Mel died a violent death after walking into the path of a lorry believing she about to be reunited with her deceased son Hunter.

And those watching from home were blown away by the dramatic scenes, which marked the end of an era for the Owen family.

Lamenting Mel's demise on Twitter, one fan wrote: "Mel Owen really was one of the most iconic characters in EastEnders history and I can't believe she's gone.

"The Square won't be the same without her causing chaos and looking good doing it."
