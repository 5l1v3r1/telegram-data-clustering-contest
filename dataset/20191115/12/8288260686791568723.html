Fans have been sent this warning ahead of Aston Villa's Carabao Cup clash with Liverpool

Aston Villa latest news | AVFC will face Jurgen Klopp's men in the last eight of the Carabao Cup next month

15 Nov 2019, 12:41 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Aston Villa have warned that any supporters that purchase tickets for Liverpool fans in the home areas of Villa Park, will have their right to purchase further tickets removed.

It promises to be another bumper crowd on December 17, as Villa and Liverpool both look to make it to the final four of the first domestic cup competition of the season.

Wolves brought over 5,000 fans for the round of 16 tie, with an attendance of more than 30,000 greeting Villa's 2-1 win.

Liverpool are expected to name an inexperienced side when they make the trip to B6, with the squad split in two.

Jurgen Klopp and his established regulars will be in Qatar competing in the FIFA Club World Cup, with Liverpool already confirming two separate squads will be used.

Liverpool have taken the bigger allocation, meaning, as Wolves did, they will be in the North Stand, with season ticket holders needing to relocate.

 

The visitors are using the game as a chance for fans who do not normally get the chance to see Klopp's men play on the road.

But Villa have confirmed that any home fans purchasing tickets for Liverpool fans will have their right to purchase further seats removed.

Tickets will go on sale to season ticket holders from 17.00 on November 15, with a tiered release from then onwards.
