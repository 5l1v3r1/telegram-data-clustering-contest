‘Halo: Reach’ Launches For PC And Xbox One December 3rd

15 Nov 2019, 12:53 by Mike Wilson

It’s not Halo Infinite, but Microsoft’s X019 conference gave Halo fans something to cheer about. Halo: Reach will launch for Xbox One and PC via the Halo: The Master Chief Collection on December 3.

As was reported back in March, rather than release the six games in the collection all at once, Microsoft is opting for a staggered release. Halo: Reach comes to PC as the first installment. Optimized for PC, Halo: Reach will feature mouse and keyboard support, native PC features, up to 4K UHD and at least 60 FPS.
