Wanna Watch ‘Euphoria: The Movie’? Too Bad, But ‘Waves’ Is the Closest Thing to It

15 Nov 2019, 03:12

I don’t know if I’ve ever had a movie rip me open emotionally quite as much as Waves. I’m not exaggerating when I say that I left a showing and openly sobbed on the street on the way to my train. The film, which hits theaters in limited release today, follows a Florida high school brother and sister as they deal with a loss that throws their lives into a total tailspin, which might sound familiar, because it’s really, REALLY similar to the plot of a little show you might have seen called Euphoria.

Let me preface by saying that Euphoria is incredible, and so is Waves. This is a positive comparison. Like, if you loved Euphoria, you WILL love Waves. I would bet my hard-earned dollars on it. Lemme explain why. FYI, there are light spoilers for Waves ahead, but they’ll make you wanna go see the movie, so...it’s your call.
