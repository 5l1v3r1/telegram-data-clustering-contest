Anushka Sharma is a vision in white as she decks up for Karan Johar's house party

15 Nov 2019, 03:58 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Karan Johar hosted a welcome party for international pop singer Katy Perry at his residence last night. Several Bollywood celebs like Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Abhishek Bachchan, Anushka Sharma, Alia Bhatt, Shahid Kapoor, Ananya Panday, Kunal Kemmu, Sanjay Kapoor, Dinesh Vijan, Kajol, Karisma Kapoor, Arjun Kapoor, Malaika Arora and more attended the bash.

Actress Anushka Sharma was also present at the star-studded bash. Before arriving for the celebration, the actress shot for a photoshoot donning her last night's OOTD. The actress was looking a vision in white as she posed for the lenses.

The 'Zero' actress was seen wearing a simple yet shiny white short dress with long puffed sleeves. She accessorised her look with watch and bracelet set and opted for smokey eye make-up. Her beautiful open hair jazzed up her overall look.

On the work front, she was last seen in 'Zero' alongside Shah Rukh Khan and Katrina Kaif. She hasn't announced any project yet.
