Impeachment hearing roundup, the first public hearing

15 Nov 2019, 03:21 by John Aravosis

In today’s episode, Cliff and I do a major review of yesterday’s first public impeachment hearing with State Department officials Ambassador William Taylor and George Kent.

We talk about the latest evidence, the latest Republican disinformation talking points, Nancy Pelosi’s delicious swipe at Donald Trump today, and we also talk about the elections, including the upcoming tight governor’s race in Louisiana and Deval Patrick entering the Democratic presidential race.
