Police on hunt for thieves who stole World War II medals from Dianella home

15 Nov 2019, 03:16

Police are hunting for thieves who stole war medals from a Dianella home this week.

The burglary occurred at a home on Homer Street on Tuesday between 7.45am-4.15pm.
