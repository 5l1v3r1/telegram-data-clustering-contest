#IStandWithTaylor: Fans pledge support to Taylor Swift after she says Scooter Braun won't let her play her own songs

15 Nov 2019, 03:49 by AP

Taylor Swift said Thursday that she may not perform at the American Music Awards and may have to put other projects including a forthcoming documentary on hold because the men who own her old recordings won't allow her to play her songs.

"Right now my performance at the AMAs, the documentary and any other recorded events I am planning to play until November 2020 are a question mark," Swift said on Twitter and Instagram.
