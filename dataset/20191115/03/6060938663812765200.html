DeepVeer first Anniversary: After Tirupati, Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone seek blessings at the Golden Temple

15 Nov 2019, 03:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After offering prayers at Tirupati Balaji, the power couple Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone jetted off to Punjab to seek blessings at the Golden Temple in Amritsar on Thursday.

DeepVeer celebrated their first wedding anniversary on November 14. The duo decided to go divine way to mark the special occasion. Yesterday, they visited the Tirumala temple in Andhra Pradesh followed by the Padmavati temple in the same area.

Today, in the wee hours of the morning, Deepika and Ranveer were snapped at the Golden Temple along with their families.

In the photos, DeepVeer looks like a newly wedded couple as they pose with their families at the Golden Temple. From Deepika’s side, her parents Prakash Padukone, Ujjala Padukone, her sister Anisha Padukone while from Ranveer’s side, his parents Jagjit Singh Bhavnani, Anju Bhavnani and his sister Ritika Bhavnani has travelled along.

Deepika was seen wearing a heavily embellished Punjabi salwar suit which she paired with a beautiful traditional set of necklace and bangles. She had covered her head with her dupatta and had adorned sindoor on the forehead. Deepika looked gorgeous as ever. On the other hand, Ranveer was seen in a flower-printed kurta pyjama paired with and a bandhgala jacket.
