Christmas Dubai 2019: Festive family fun at Tania's Teahouse

And dogs are welcome too

17 Nov 2019, 08:42 by Kelly Johnstone

Tania's Teahouse is ringing in Christmas with some fun festive treats – and kids and fur babies are invited too.

Now that the cooler weather is upon us, restaurants and cafés around the city are opening their terraces and gardens so we can all enjoy eating al fresco.

But Tania's Teahouse is going one step further and inviting customers of the four-legged variety to come along as well.

Pampered pooches can chill in the sun while human mums, dads and siblings can tuck into the fun sweet treats that are being laid on for Christmas.

The pretty pink café has thrown open the doors on its recently renovated – and more Instagrammable than ever – terrace where new wall art and foliage will provide the perfect backdrop to those family selfies.

Social media taken care of, it's time to tuck into the cookie selection – themed for the holidays with snowmen, gingerbread houses and Christmas trees that are almost too cute to eat... almost.

And if you can't get enough of the cookies while you're there, you can load up a box to take home.

Like the biscuits, the hot drinks – all with the usual play on words names we've come to expect at the Jumeirah eatery – are festively themed too.

For the smalls, there is every child's dreamy winter drink, hot chocolate – luckily the snow doesn't need to be coming down outside to enjoy it.

There are more grown-up options available too – although to be honest, the S'mores the Merrier hot chocolate with marshmallows wins it for us even though we are (slightly) above 15.

'Teas the Season is everything you want to make you feel warm and cosy – yes, even though it's still 25 degrees outside – with cinnamon, cloves, jasmine petals and xmas-themed candy sprinkles, while a homemade gingerbread latte sounds truly tempting.

And if you really want to get the in the mood for Christmas - dress your doggie in a festive outfit... cute Santa hat anyone?

Grab the fam – and the pooch – and we'll see you there.
Daily 10am-6pm. Jumeirah Beach Road, Dubai, www.taniasteahouse.com (04 324 0021).
