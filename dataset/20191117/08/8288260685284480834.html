Strictly spoiler leaves fans livid as 'potential finalist' eliminated in Blackpool

Viewers of the BBC One talent contest were left seething after some Twitter users spoiled the results from Blackpool

17 Nov 2019, 08:50 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Strictly fans have been left gutted after the result for Sunday evening's show was leaked on social media - AGAIN.

Viewers of the BBC One talent contest were left seething after some Twitter users spoiled the results from Blackpool.

Strictly Come Dancing films its Sunday night results show on Saturday evenings, meaning leaks often occur.

But this week's has left fans particularly annoyed - after a crowd favourite was axed.

The contestant, widely seen as a potential finalist, was voted off the show after the judges opted to save a rival contestant.

BirminghamLive knows who has left the show but won't spoil the result for viewers keen to watch it unfold tomorrow.

One tweeted: "Absolutely gutted #StrictlySpoiler."

While another commented: "Noooooooo #StrictlySpoiler."

Social media was awash with other angry comments, too, with one branding the contestant a "finalist" - and another saying: "Totally wrong."

 

"I shouldn't have searched #StrictlySpoiler. My life is over", one tweeted.

And another remarked: "Nah you’re joking me #StrictlySpoiler."

Another raged: "I AM F***ING FUMING #StrictlySpoiler."

Fans will have to wait until tonight to discover the remaining celebs' fate, as well as watch a huge Westlife performance.

*Strictly Come Dancing's results show airs tomorrow on BBC One
