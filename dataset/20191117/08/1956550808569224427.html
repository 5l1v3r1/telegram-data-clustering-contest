Roxy Cinemas' popular ladies’ night is back

It's Dhs99 for a ticket, starter, main course and more

17 Nov 2019, 08:15 by Reema Rahman

Looking for different things to do in Dubai on ladies’ night?

How does a Hollywood blockbuster, mini massages and a slap-up meal sound to you?

Well, mark your calendars as Roxy Cinemas popular ladies’ night is back.

Head to the City Walk branch on Tuesday November 19 from 6.30pm onwards for an evening of pampering and a top movie.

Tickets cost Dhs99 and include free express nail polish changes and mini massages from 6.30pm onwards before the movie begins at 7.30pm.

This month, ladies can pick between Charlie's Angels, The Good Liar and Poms and the screenings will be taking place in the in the Platinum Plus experience so get ready to get comfy with a blanket, pillow and fully reclining leather chair.

While watching the movie you’ll be a served a starter, main course and mocktail.

Sounds like our kind of ladies’ night.
