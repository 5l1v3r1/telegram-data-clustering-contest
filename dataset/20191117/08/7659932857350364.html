Saudi Arabia's Relief Arm Distributes Food Aid in Yemen's Governorates

17 Nov 2019, 08:45

King Salman Humanitarian Aid and Relief Center (KSrelief) distributed food aid in Yemen through Al-Khair Humanitarian Coalition.

The Riyadh-based center distributed up to 200 food baskets in Taiz governorate.

A total of 600 people benefited from this distribution, the Saudi Press Agency reported.

Meanwhile, 477 food baskets were also distributed to needy people in Al-Khawkhah district, Hodeidah governorate.

The aid reached a total of 2,862 displaced people in Hodeidah.

This comes within the framework of humanitarian and relief projects being provided by KSrelief, Saudi Arabia's relief arm, to the Yemeni people.
