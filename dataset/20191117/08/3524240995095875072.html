#KogiDecide2019: My nephew shot at polling unit on Saturday is dead —Melaye

17 Nov 2019, 08:21 by Agency Report

Candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party in Saturday’s Kogi West Senatorial election, Mr. Dino Melaye, says his nephew who was allegedly shot at a polling unit on Saturday, has died.

Melaye said the man, Olorunjuwon, died on Sunday morning.
