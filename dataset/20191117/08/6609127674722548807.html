ANOTHER LEAK

Strictly spoiler leaks AGAIN leaving viewers outraged over the celebrity booted off the show after Blackpool special

17 Nov 2019, 08:52 by Jill Robinson

Strictly Come Dancing fans are outraged after the results of the latest dance off in Blackpool were leaked.

A spoiler website repeatedly ruins the suspense for fans every week, revealing which celebrity has been voted off by the judges after the dance off.

This weekend, Saturday's show broadcast live from the Tower Ballroom in Blackpool, but within hours of the show finishing, the name of the latest celebrity to leave was leaked.

The Sun Online has seen the result but has chosen not to reveal it, but fans who saw the news on Twitter were left outraged.

One wrote: "Omg actually very shocked in that result #StrictlySpoiler #Strictly."

Another raged: "strictly’s voting system is acc the biggest pile of s*** ive ever seen like #StrictlySpoiler."

A third was equally furious, tweeting: "the #StrictlySpoiler this evening is the final straw. this series is an absolute f***ing fix and I refuse to watch any more episodes.

"I used to enjoy Strictly so much but decent people have gone out and this is taking it way too far. f*** this I'm going to bed."

Meanwhile a fourth wrote: "honestly feel more let down by the british public about this than brexit #strictlyspoiler."

Another viewer demanded the BBC start airing the results show on a Saturday to avoid the spoiler situation.

They tweeted: "BBC it’s time you start showing the results on a Saturday night at 9pm. We all know it’s filmed straight after the show. #StrictlySpoiler @BBC."

The Blackpool spectacular saw Kelvin Fletcher and Karim Zeroual share the top spot on the leaderboard with 39 points for the Jive and Charleston, respectively.

However, viewers were furious that neither star received a perfect score from the judges, after Craig Revel Horwood kept his 10 paddle locked away all evening.

The Strictly Come Dancing Results Show airs on Sunday at 7.15pm on BBC One.
