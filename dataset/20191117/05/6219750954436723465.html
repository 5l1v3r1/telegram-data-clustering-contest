Text description provided by the architects. The project is situated in the rural part of Zürich in Switzerland. Together with the listed farm house from the 18th century it forms an ensemble around a yard.

Similar to local barns the building is conceived as a single big volume, although the rowhouse contains five vertically organised single-family houses.
