Ozigbo speaks on 2019 Unusual Praise - The Nation Newspaper

17 Nov 2019, 05:31 by https://www.facebook.com/alao.h.joshua

Olushola Victor

Valentine Ozigbo is popularly known as the President and Chief Executive Officer of Transcorp Plc. But he is also the Chairman of the Unusual Entrepreneur, an extension of Unusual Praise.

The 2019 edition of the annual mega gospel event will be held at the Periwinkle Estate, Lekki, Lagos, on Friday, November 29.

Tagged ‘Declare the Glory of the Lord among Nations,’ the event targeted at Nigerians across all tribes and faiths will feature gospel singers such as Nathaniel Bassey, Panam Percy Paul, Chioma Jesus, Tope Alabi, Big Bolaji and BJ Sax.

However, Ozigbo said they had introduced an empowerment scheme to this year’s edition, which will empower 250 smart men and women members of the Church from the entire Archdiocese of Lagos. He stated: “To be eligible for the programme, the business must be a Nigerian or the applicant must be a legal resident in Nigeria (Lagos), the business must be for-profit, the applicant must be willing to attend all training and mentoring exercises and the applicant must be at least 18 years old.”

It was gathered the entrepreneurs would be awarded with prizes ranging from N50,000 to N1,000,000 per person. Apart from the funding, entrepreneurs will also be provided with mentorship, business training and other critical support they require to succeed long term.
