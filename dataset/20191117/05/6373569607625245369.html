Almost 80 death notices have been published in our sister print title The Herald this week

17 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Edd Moore

A former Herald and Western Morning News journalist and a recipient of the Imperial Service Medal are among those to whom heartfelt tributes have been paid this week.

There have been almost 80 death announcements and funeral notices printed in our sister title The Herald this week.

Messages of thanks, acknowledgements and birthday memoriams have also been submitted by relatives and loved ones.

Every week, dozens of family announcements are published in our print-based sister title The Herald.

They range from funeral details to births, graduations, birthdays and messages of thanks.

All of them - whether sombre or uplifting - are placed to provide comfort to the nearest and dearest of those either lost of being celebrated.
