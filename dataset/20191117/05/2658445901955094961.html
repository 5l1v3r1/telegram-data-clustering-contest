Charly Boy steps out for Opera News Hub launch - The Nation Newspaper

17 Nov 2019, 05:43 by https://www.facebook.com/alao.h.joshua

Olushola Victor

There has never been a dull moment for Charles Oputa, popularly known as Charly Boy. Even the loss of his mother, Mrs Margaret Oputa, in October, has not stopped him from being his lively self.

The entertainer was one of the personalities sighted at Radisson Blu Hotel, Ikeja GRA, Lagos, for the unveiling of Opera News Hub, a new online media platform that helps authors and bloggers produce original online content. Social Circuit gathered that he has also signed up with the company as an influencer and writer.

Charly Boy’s presence caught the attention of admirers who took turns to take photos with him. He also seemed to enjoy the moment as he was warm with everyone and didn’t leave in a hurry.
