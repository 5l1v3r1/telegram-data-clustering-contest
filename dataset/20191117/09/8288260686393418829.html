Coronation Street Christmas spoilers 2019 in full - death, drama and an iconic character in peril

Robert Preston, who is currently in a relationship with both Vicki and Michelle Connor (Kym Marsh), will see his luck expire

17 Nov 2019, 09:34 by James Rodger, Katie Fitzpatrick, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Coronation Street's Christmas spoilers have been revealed for 2019 - and the cobbles are set to be pretty dramatic.

Corrie will see scores of Brits tune in to each episode as it airs its iconic festive instalments.

Weatherfield will be the scene of plenty of drama, with death and shocks, romance and festivities, and twists and turns galore.

Robert Preston, who is currently in a relationship with both Vicki and Michelle Connor (Kym Marsh), will see his luck expire.

The owner of the bistro will end up being shot and killed in a siege at the Rovers Return on Christmas Day, reports the M.E.N.

This time hitting the booze will be Derek Milligan, loan shark Gary Windass' stooge. According to reports alcoholic Derek will burst into the pub, waving a gun around and threatening Gary's life, and Robert is caught up in their feud with fatal consequences.

"So apparently Robert Preston will be shot and killed on Christmas Day! Bring on #Corrie," tweeted one fan.

Michelle is now plotting revenge against Robert after she found out that he had a secret other fiancee, Vicky Jefferies who is pregnant with his baby.

 

Fans previously assumed that Robert and Michelle would leave together, but talk of his sudden death has dispelled this theory.

Sally Ann Matthews, who plays Rovers Return landlady Jenny Bradley, previously warned that this Christmas on Coronation Street is 'beyond dramatic', reports the Daily Star .

She said: “I’ve just received scripts for Christmas. What I can say is it’s beyond dramatic.

"It’s big and edge-of-your-seat stuff. It’s not just one story either. There are several big stories happening.”

And Jennie McAlpine, who plays Fiz Stape, said: “I’ve read the script for Christmas Eve. It’s quite dramatic. I’m not sure if I’ll be getting any turkey this year.

"Fiz is going to be quite busy!”

 

Jennie also teased that there might a death.

She added: “I can’t say if anyone dies. If I told someone that, I’d have to kill them!”

Coronation Street is also said to be lining up a heartbreaking Christmas storyline for Rita Tanner.

 

The Weatherfield legend will reportedly have another brush with death after she suffers an accident involving a Christmas tree.

It's claimed that the plot, which is set to highlight the plight of the lonely and elderly over the festive period, will see Rita suffer a fall while attempting to put up some fairy lights.

It's said that Rita will be left trapped beneath the tree for several days waiting for someone to help after it falls on top of her.
