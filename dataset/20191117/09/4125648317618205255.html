Syrian Army seizes large cache of weapons near Israeli border: photos

17 Nov 2019, 09:49 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (11:30 A.M.) – The Syrian Arab Army (SAA) seized a large cache of weapons this week that were left behind by the jihadist rebels near the Israeli border.

According to the Syrian Arab News Agency (SANA), the cache had a wide-variety of weapons that were left behind inside the town of Beit Jinn near the Israeli border in the occupied Golan Heights region.
