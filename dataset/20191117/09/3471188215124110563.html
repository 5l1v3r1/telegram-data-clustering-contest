Lenovo’s Smart Display 7 makes its way to Australia; on sale now

17 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Duncan Jaffrey

Back in September Lenovo announced their next Google Assistant powered smart speaker, the Smart Display 7 with Google Assistant. The device has quietly made its way to our shores, listed for sale in Australia for $129.

You’ll find listings for the Smart Display 7 at Harvey Norman, JB Hi-fi and The Good Guys list the device on their sites, with The Good Guys even marking is as in stock in some locations.

For those looking for a little more, the latest Harvey Norman catalogue has the 7-inch Smart Display along with a Lenovo Smart Plug and Colour Changing Bulb (both of which work with Google Assistant) for just $147 – that’s not a bad deal!

If you’ve been looking for a smart display, this new Lenovo sounds like a good deal – it’s much cheaper than Google’s own 7-inch offering (Nest Hub) and includes a camera for Duo calling.
