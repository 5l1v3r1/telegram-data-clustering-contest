Watch New Sons of Apollo Video for ‘Goodbye Divinity’

17 Nov 2019, 09:12 by Martin Kielty

Sons of Apollo have released a new video for their track “Goodbye Divinity,” which appears on their upcoming album MMXX.

The band – which features former Guns N’ Roses guitarist Ron “Bumblefoot” Thal, ex-Dream Theater members Mike Portnoy and Derek Sherinian, onetime Journey singer Jeff Scott Soto and Mr. Big bassist Billy Sheehan – launch the follow-up to debut LP Psychotic Symphony on Jan. 17.
