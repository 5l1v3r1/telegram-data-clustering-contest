Spoilers: Billy and Paul are official in Corrie as Kel's abuse is revealed

17 Nov 2019, 09:01

Billy Mayhew (Daniel Brocklebank) and Paul Foreman (Peter Ash) have quite the history in Coronation Street, and it appears as if that spark between them is still very much there, as in the coming episodes, the pair rekindle their romance — just prior to Bernie and Gemma Winter (Jane Hazelgrove and Dolly-Rose Campbell) learning the truth about sex abuser Kel (Joe Alessi) is revealed.

Bernie (Jane Hazelgrove) prepares for her first day on the job as cleaner at the church hall, and she informs to Paul that Billy is the one who gave her a job — and Paul’s left confused as a result.

With this playing over in his mind, Paul later quizzes the vicar about why he helped his mum out, and Billy reveals that he just wanted to help because he loves him.

Paul’s taken aback by what Billy’s said. However, he later takes it upon himself to whip up a romantic meal for both him and Billy, and — when Billy returns home — Paul informs him he very much wants him in his life.
