BREAKING: Dino Melaye loses council to Give Bello

17 Nov 2019, 09:27 by https://www.facebook.com/alao.h.joshua

Senator Dino Melaye has lost his local government of Ijumu to the All Progressives Congress governorship candidate, Yahaya Bello.

Bello scored a total vote of 11,425 to defeat the PDP candidate Musa Wada in the local government.
