Strictly viewers slam judges' scoring as they claim Karim Zeroual was 'robbed' of perfect 40

They received an incredible 39 points out of 40

17 Nov 2019, 09:46 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of Strictly Come Dancing have slammed the judges' scoring as they claim Karim Zeroual was 'robbed' of 40 out of 40.

Fans were also left fuming as Michelle Visage ended up at the bottom of the leaderboard with her professional partner Giovanni Pernice.

As the celebrities and their pro partners took to the dance floor at the iconic Blackpool Tower ballroom, judges were giving out 8s, 9s and 10s throughout the show.

Karim and his partner Amy Dowden received 39 points out of 40 for their Charleston.

But viewers thought they should have got the perfect 40 and shared their thoughts on Twitter.

Karim and Amy were robbed he deserved 40.

One person said: "#Strictly can't believe the judges have been overmarking all night just because it's Blackpool and then when we see a dance that was a clear 40, it doesn't get a 40! Karim was robbed."

Another wrote: "Karim and Amy were robbed he deserved 40 #Strictly."

A third tweeted: "Karim and Amy were robbed should of been 40!!!!!!!!! #Strictly."
