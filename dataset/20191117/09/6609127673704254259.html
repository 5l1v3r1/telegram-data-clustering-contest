Scotland return?

Hibs and St Johnstone ‘could be set to battle it out’ for ex-Celtic star Ambrose

17 Nov 2019, 09:35 by Alan Galindo

EFE AMBROSE could be set for a return to Scottish football, according to reports.

The Daily Mail claim his former employers Hibs could be set to battle it out with St Johnstone for his signature.

Ambrose has been without a club since departing Derby County at the end of last season.

The former Celtic star failed to make an appearance for the Rams' top team after leaving the Hibees to join them.

Ambrose, 31, has previously admitted he would never rule out a return to Scotland.

Speaking earlier this month, he said: “I am back in Nigeria now and I am just waiting to see what happens.

“I am training with my first club, Kaduna, and keeping myself fit. Hopefully in a few weeks I will know about my future.

“I watch a lot of Scottish football and  keep updated on Celtic and Hibs.

“I love Scotland and Scottish football, but right now I don’t know what the future holds.”

Tommy Wright is believed to be keen on adding an experienced centre-back to his ranks while a return to Easter Road would make Ambrose the first signing of the Jack Ross era.
