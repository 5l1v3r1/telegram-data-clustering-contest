Bleary Dan Osborne stumbles out of Butterfly Ball after partying with Christine McGuinness

Dan took the night off from daddy duties to party at the charity bash in London while wife Jacqueline Jossa is in Australia for I'm A Celebrity

17 Nov 2019, 09:12 by Natalie Corner

Dan Osborne was spotted enjoying a night out while his wife Jacqueline Jossa is down under preparing to enter the I'm A Celeb jungle.

The former TOWIE star took a night off from daddy duties to attend the Butterfly Ball at the Grosvenor Hotel in London.

Arriving at the swanky do via the red carpet, the dad-of-three, looked smart in a black suit and skinny tie.

But by the end of the evening he looked worse for wear, with his shirt unbuttoned and clinging on to his tie - and the support of a pal - as he stumbled out of the fancy venue.

Celebs gathered for the annual charity bash in aid of the Caudwell Children charity that supports and helps the lives of children with disabilities.

Other stars partying at the charity ball also included Christine McGuinness , who Dan was spotted chatting to in the lobby of the hotel.

The pair had a particularly animated conversation as Paddy McGuinness' wife repeatedly pulled funny faces.

It appeared as though Dan, who was fired from TOWIE in 2015 over threats to stab his ex-girlfriend , let his hair down while partying at the do.

A dishevelled Dan was all smiles and giggles as he attempted to navigate the pavement with the help of his pals at the end of the night.

He spent time trying to take selfies while the rest of the guestlist mingled around the exit, waiting to be taken home.

Dan partied alongside the like of other reality stars like Amy Childs, Imogen Thomas, Love Island's Tyne-Lexy Clarson and Geordie Shore's Holly Hagan.

Elsewhere Lizzy Cundy, Nell McAndrew, and host Kirsty Gallacher, all made impressive entrances on the red carpet.

Dan's attendance at the Float Like A Butterfly Ball, which also hosted a charity boxing match, comes after he made a show of unity for his wife alongside his kids.

He has his hands full while Jac appears on I'm A Celeb, and showed what he's been up to while she's in Australia.

Posing with his three children eldest son Teddy, from his previous relationship, and his daughters Ella and Mia in a cute snap as they got used to life without the ex-EastEnders star.

"Morninggg not the best picture but this is us first thing in the morning, cuddles with daddy always first, then breakfast time," Dan captioned the shot.
