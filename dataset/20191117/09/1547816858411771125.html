Saina Nehwal Biopic: Parineeti Chopra Begins With Physiotherapy Sessions Post Nape Injury

17 Nov 2019, 09:19 by Amruta Patel

On Friday evening, we came across an unfortunate news of actress Parineeti Chopra slowing down due to a nape injury while shooting for Saina Nehwal biopic. The actress taking to her Instagram informed about the same, and now has shared about beginning her recovery journey. We wish the actress a speedy recovery

On Friday Parineeti Chopra taking to her Instagram account, informed of an unfortunate injury, she suffered while shooting for Saina Nehwal biopic. Accompanying this post was an image of the actress seated facing her back to the camera, with a bandage on her nape. Now the actress has once again taken to her Insta, to share how she has begun her recovery journey. Just like her previous post, her fans and industry colleagues, prayed for her speedy recovery. For example, after singer Amaal Malik, singer Guru Randhawa too commented on her post.  

In the video shared by Parineeti, we can see her lying, while her physiotherapist makes her do some light leg exercises. In the caption, she mentioned about beginning her recovery journey, and thanked her physiotherapist. Her caption read, “Recovery Day 1. My whole body is stiff and tight Thanks my physiotherapist Apoorva for taking care of me” In the actress’ previous post, along with her injury, she informed of how despite several measures taken by the crew, she ended up with this unfortunate injury, ultimately slowing down the process of the shoot.
