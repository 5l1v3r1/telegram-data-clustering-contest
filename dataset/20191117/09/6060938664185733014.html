‘Bala’ box-office collection second Saturday: The Ayushmann Khurrana, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar starrer raked in Rs 6.50 crore

17 Nov 2019, 09:17 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Like all of his previous films of late, Ayushmann Khurrana’s latest release, ‘Bala’ has been making waves at the box-office ever since its release last week. The film that opened to Rs 9.50 crore on its first day, has been growing with each passing day. Now, according to a report in BoxofficeIndia.com, the film grossed Rs 6.50 crore on its second Saturday. The total box-office collection of the Ayushmann starrer so far is Rs 79.50 crore.

Meanwhile, 'Bala' is Ayushmann Khurrana's biggest release to date. The film released across 3000 screens in India and more than 550 screens in the overseas markets.

Directed by Amar Kaushik, 'Bala' is a film set in Kanpur that revolves around a man suffering from premature balding and the lack of confidence and societal pressure which runs with it. The movie received great reviews from both the audience and the critics alike.

The film also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles. It hit the theatres on November 7, 2019.
