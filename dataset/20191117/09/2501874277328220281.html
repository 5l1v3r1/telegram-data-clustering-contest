Dog of the Week: Frances

17 Nov 2019, 09:10

Source: Spark Rescue

Meet Frances " Fritz" for short, this adorable 3 month old male cattle dog puppy ( possibly Basenji, Kelpie?) was recently rescued from an over crowded shelter in southern CA.

Fritz is incredibly smart and sweet with all animals but should be fed separate around other animals! He is a high energy pup that would thrive in a ranch or working type of environment!

Fritz is going to his foster home today and will be available for adoption soon! www.sparkrescue.org
