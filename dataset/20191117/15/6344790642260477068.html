You Can Stream the LADY AND THE TRAMP Soundtrack Now

17 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Tommy Williams

Disney+ launched the other day and with it, the live-action Lady and the Tramp remake. Now, Walt Disney Records has released the soundtrack digitally which features the score from Joseph Trapanese, plus two songs performed by Janelle Monáe (“He’s a Tramp” and “That’s Enough”). Trapanese’s score is said to be “a marriage of American classical and traditional New Orleans sound.” That sounds pretty fantastic to me. You can stream the soundtrack now on Apple Music and Spotify.
