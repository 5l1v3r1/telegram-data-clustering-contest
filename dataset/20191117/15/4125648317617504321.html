Russian military convoy filmed heading to Assyrian town in northeast Syria: video

17 Nov 2019, 15:52 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (5:30 P.M.) – A Russian military convoy was recently filmed heading to the Assyrian town of Tal Tamr in northeastern Syria.

According to the reports, the Russian military convoy traveled from the Al-Raqqa Governorate town of ‘Ayn ‘Issa to Tal Tamr in the Al-Hasakah Governorate.
