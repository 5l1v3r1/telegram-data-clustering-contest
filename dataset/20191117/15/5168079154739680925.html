All set for Oman’s 49th National Day

17 Nov 2019, 15:44 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Supreme Committee for the celebration of National Day announced that it has completed all arrangements for the 49th National Day, Oman News Agency reported on Sunday.

“A number of development projects will be opened in the fields of oil and gas, electric power, water desalination and other development projects,” the statement said.
