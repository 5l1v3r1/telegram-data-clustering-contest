Straphanger kicks rider in face during Queens subway spat

17 Nov 2019, 15:09 by Craig McCarthy

A rider kicked another straphanger in the face during a fight on the subway in Queens, cops said.

The encounter occurred around 9 p.m. Saturday when the two men started arguing over the unknown man holding the train doors open on the northbound E train at the 74th Street and Roosevelt Avenue subway stop, according to police.

The fight spilled out onto the platform and, at one point, the unknown man kicked the 41-year-old man in the face while he was picking up his phone, police said.

Police released a photo of the unknown attacker Saturday.
