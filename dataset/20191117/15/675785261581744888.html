Girl's Christmas wishlist for Santa leaves dad speechless - for wrong reasons

This 10-year-old is not holding back with her extravagant requests - including Gucci sliders, a Chanel bag and thousands of pounds in cash

17 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Amber Hicks, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

A little girl's Christmas wishlist for Santa has left her dad speechless - but for all the wrong reasons.

The 10-year-old from the States has requested 26 items she would like to find under the tree next month.

But instead of the usual Barbie or LEGO, the youngster from LA clearly already has a taste for the finer things in life.

Among the gifts on her list are Gucci sliders, a Chanel purse, an iPhone 11, as well as a 'real bunny' and clothes for the bunny.

She is also hoping for $4,000, a MacBook Air, a GoPro, perfume, makeup, clothes and 'asenchal [essential] oil'.

Amusingly, the youngster has asked Santa to bring her an alarm clock, new sheets and cover, laundry detergent and pink duct tape too.

Her dad shared the list on Twitter , where it has gone viral, and joked she "must be out of her mind."

One person quipped: "Sneaking in that 4K at the bottom is a rockstar move."

Another said: "Get her that alarm clock sis ! Lol tell her it’ll wake her up from that dream where she think shes getting everything on there."

A third mused: "You raised a boujee princess and now you wanna complain. Get mama her Gucci slides and stop playing."

A fourth said: "She ten! She doesn’t need perfume! She needs to go out to play."

Another added: "Looks like she's planning on moving out."
