BRUTAL ASSAULT

Elgin man left with serious facial injuries after horror attack by ‘group of men’

17 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Justin Bowie

AN ELGIN man was left with serious facial injuries after he was attacked by a “group of men” in the early hours of this morning.

The 56-year-old was taken to hospital after the brutal assault on High Street at 2am.

Cops have now made an appeal for any witnesses who can help to come forward.

They say a number of people would have in the area at the time.

And they are particularly interested in speaking to a woman wearing a red dress or jumpsuit who may have seen the horror attack.

Sergeant Phil Wu of the Divisional Alcohol and Violence Reduction Unit, Elgin, said: "From our enquiries so far, we believe that there may have been a group of men in their 20s involved in the assault.

“A number of people would have been in the area at the time and we are particularly keen on speaking to a woman wearing a red dress or jumpsuit who may have witnessed this assault.

“If you have any information relating to this incident please contact police on 101 quoting incident number 0504 of 17 November 2019.

“Or alternatively, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 where information can be passed on anonymously.”

The 15-year-old boy knifed his 13-year-old victim during a school lunch break in Glasgow’s southside.

The attacker fled the busy street leaving the injured boy stricken on the ground in March this year.
