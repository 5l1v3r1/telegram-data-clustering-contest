Harmeet downs Amalraj for title

17 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Sports BureauBATAM (INDONESIA)

Harmeet Desai, seeded fourth, defeated seventh seed A. Amalraj 11-9, 9-11, 11-9, 11-9, 10-12, 11-9 in the final of the ITTF Challenge Indonesia Open table tennis championship here on Sunday.

This is Harmeet’s second international title of the year after the Commonwealth championship in July at Cuttack.

Harmeet said it felt great to win his first title on foreign soil.

“It was a very different feeling. It wasn’t a easy tournament for me as I had tough matches from the first round and I beat quite a few good Asian players,” said the 26-year-old.
