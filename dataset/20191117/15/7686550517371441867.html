Cadbury, Argos, Lidl and Costa are urgently recalling these products

Shoppers are being warned not to use them and to return them to stores

17 Nov 2019, 15:19 by Anna Lewis

Products are being urgently recalled over safety fears.

Customers are being warned about these items so it's important to check whether you have any in the cupboards.

Cadbury is recalling one product over undeclared nut allergens, while others risk salmonella.

Here's the full list - as well as older recalls that are still relevant.

Cadbury Dairy Milk Little Robins

Mondelez UK is recalling Cadbury Dairy Milk Little Robins because some packs contain almonds which are not mentioned on the label.

The product has been incorrectly packed with Cadbury Dairy Milk Little Robins Daim Chocolate.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy to nuts.

Mondelez UK is now recalling this product from customers. Those who have bought the chocolate are advised to return to to the story they bought it from full a full refund or contact Mondelez UK on 0800 818181.

Pack size: 93g

Batch code: CIS3092812 & CIS3092822

Best before : March 31, 2020

Allergens : Nuts (almond)

Harvey Nichols

Harvey Nichols is recalling its own brand Dark Chocolate Gingers because the product contains pistachio which is not mentioned on the label.

The product has been incorrectly packed with Dark Chocolate Pistachios. This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy to nuts.

Pack size: 125g

Batch code: 190916

Best before: August 20, 2019

Allergens: Nuts (pistachio nut)

No other Harvey Nichols products are known to be affected.

Welsh cottage cakes

Welsh Cottage Cakes is recalling its Spelt & Fruit Cake because it contains wheat which is not mentioned on the label.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to wheat or gluten.

Pack size: 550g and 360g

Batch code: 190920, 191004, 191017, 191025, 191108

Best before: November 23, 2019; December 7, 2019; December 20, 2019; December 28, 2019; January 11, 2020.

For more information call 01558 824213.

Pots & Co

Pots & Co is recalling its lighter range of Colombian Chocolate Pot, Mango & Passion Fruit Pot and Summer Berry Pot because some jars may contain pieces of glass.

Products: Pots & Co Colombian Chocolate Pot, Pots & Co Mango & Passionfruit Pot, Pots & Co Summer Berry Pot

Pack size: 65g

Batch codes: Barcode 5060253701920, barcode 5060253701906, barcode 5060253702224, barcode 5060253701913

Use by date: All date codes up to and including 23 November 2019

If you have bought any of these products, do not eat them. Instead, return them to the store from where they were bought for a full refund.

For more information call 0208965117.

Lidl

Zorba Delicacies Limited is recalling houmous from Lidl because the products may contain salmonella.

Products: Lidl Meadow Fresh Red Pepper Houmous, Lidl Meadow Fresh Caramelised Onion Houmous, Lidl Meadow Fresh Moroccan Style Houmous, Lidl Meadow Fresh Classic Houmous.

Pack size: 170g and 200g

Use by date: November 3 to November 17 2019

La Boulangere

La Boulangere is recalling its brioche hot-dog buns because they contain milk and wheat (gluten) which are not mentioned on the label in English.

This means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to milk or milk constituents or an allergy or intolerance to wheat or gluten.

Pack size : 270g

Best before: November 9, 2019; November 23, 2019 and November 30, 2019

Costa Coffee

Costa Coffee is recalling Millionaire Mallow Bites because some of the products may not have the correct allergen labelling information.

Some of this product has been mistakenly labelled as Cornflake Cluster, therefore does not mention that they contain egg and wheat which means the product is a possible health risk for anyone with an allergy or intolerance to egg, wheat or gluten.

Batch code: 19289

Best before: January 7 and 8 2020

Allergens: Egg, Gluten (wheat)
