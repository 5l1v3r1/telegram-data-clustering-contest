Cool Fan-Made STAR WARS Trailer Covers the Entire Skywalker Saga

17 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Joey Paur

With the Skywalker saga coming to an end with Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker, a Star Wars fan by the name of Alec Siegel has cut together an extremely cool trailer that covers all nine films that make up the Skywalker saga. It’s actually a really good fan trailer that might just tug at your heartstrings. It’s actually going to be really sad when the Skywalker saga comes to a close. I just hope that J.J. Abrams hits a grand slam with it because if he screws this up… well, he should stay off social media forever.
