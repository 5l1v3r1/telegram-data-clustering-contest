Infinix S5 Lite Announced In India, Coming On November 22

17 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Argam Artashyan

Rate this post

The Indian smartphone market is very attractive to all manufacturers. If previously, mainly, the low-end to mid-range handsets were launched in this region, now everything has changed. Almost all companies are bringing mid-to-high-end smartphones to India. And though this is a fact, not all of the companies want to fight in higher categories. Say, today, following the schedule, the Infinix S5 Lite was officially announced for this market. It costs 7999 Indian rupees ($112). Not that much, agree? It will go on sale on November 22.

When mentioning a low-price, many think there won’t be any worthy feature to talk about. But the Infinix S5 Lite can boast of a few features. Say, it sports a 6.6-inch punch-hole screen. And though its resolution is not that high, only 1600×720 pixels, not all smartphone makers can afford themselves to come in with such a screen. At the same time, the screen ratio is 90.5% and the aspect ratio is 20:9.

In terms of photography, the Infinix S5 Lite is packed with a triple-camera on the back. It consists of a 16MP lens, a 2MP lens, and a dedicated low-light sensor. There is a quad-flash as well. Thus, in other words, this phone should be good for taking pictures in low light conditions. By the way, the front-facing camera has a 16MP resolution.

As for the rest of the features, the phone has dimensions of 164 x 76 x 7.9 mm and weighs 178 g. Under the hood, we can find a MediaTek Helio P22 chipset. It is paired with a 4GB of memory and 64GB of internal storage space. But the latter supports up to 128GB microSD card expansion. The battery capacity is 4000mAh.
