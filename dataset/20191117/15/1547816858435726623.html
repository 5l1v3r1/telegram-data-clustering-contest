Alli Simpson Gives A Thumb Up To Cody Simpson And Miley Cyrus’ Relationship

17 Nov 2019, 15:07 by Amruta Patel

Cody Simpson and Miley Cyrus are dating and it is no hidden fact. Now, the singer’s sister Alli Simpson has revealed of being all cool and happy with their relationship and justified how they are made for each other

Cody Simpson and Miley Cyrus painting the town red in love is not an unknown fact. Now comes his sister Alli Simpson’s take rather we can say an approval on their relationship. The singer’s sister as per reports in who.com.au, has expressed how the much-in-love couple has many things similar in between them, pertaining to their likes and dislikes and so on. Adding further, Alli also mentioned that the two are made for each other; thus if Cody is happy with Miley, the family too shall be happy for them.

Alli was quoted saying, “They’re so similar and they love all the same things. We’re (the Simpson family) used to have our relationships scrutinised having grown up in the spotlight. If Cody is happy, then we are happy.” So it won’t be wrong to say that the duo might escalate their relationship to next level, by taking a plunge.  From sharing several selfies and quirky videos on their respective social media handles, the duo has time and again, upped their PDA game, warming our hearts.
