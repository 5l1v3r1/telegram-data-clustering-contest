'Dumb and unfair': Trump attacks 'nasty & obnoxious' Chris Wallace after Scalise interview

17 Nov 2019, 20:05 by Mike Brest

President Trump attacked Fox News anchor Chris Wallace and defended Republican Rep. Steve Scalise, whom Wallace interviewed earlier Sunday.

Scalise, 54, faced a contentious interview with Wallace, who repeatedly pushed back on his defense of the president regarding the House's impeachment investigation. In one particular moment of the interview, Wallace criticized the Louisiana congressman for labeling the government officials who are testifying as "Schiff's witnesses."

".@SteveScalise blew the nasty & obnoxious Chris Wallace (will never be his father, Mike!) away on Chris’s lowest rated (unless I’m on) morning show," Trump tweeted. "This kind of dumb and unfair interview would never have happened in the @FoxNews past. Great job Steve!"
