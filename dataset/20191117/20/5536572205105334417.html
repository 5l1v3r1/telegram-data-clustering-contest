Ezekiel Elliott Does The Dak Prescott Hip-Thrust Dance After TD (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019, 20:44 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Dak Prescott dance continues to be a thing.

Following a screen pass from Dak Prescott to Ezekiel Elliott who took it in for the score — the Cowboys running back already had something in mind on how to celebrate.

Zeke broke out the Dak dance in the back of the end zone.
