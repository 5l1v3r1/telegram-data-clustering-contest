HIM vs RAI Dream11 Team Prediction : Himachal Pradesh Vs Railways Best Dream 11 Fantasy Picks for Group C Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2019-20 Match

17 Nov 2019, 20:40 by Gautam Kapoor

HIM Vs RAI Dream11 Team : Struggling in the trenches of Group C, anything less than a win for Himachal Pradesh all but wraps up their 2019 sortie.

Two sides, one match and completely different agendas. Where Railways have aspirations of moving to the echelons of Group C in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy, Himachal Pradesh on the other hand are trying to keep themselves alive in their bid of challenging for a spot in the next round, a position which is slowly slipping away from them.

Barring from a late surge, Himachal Pradesh’s resurgence in the marquee tournamnet looks improbable. The team have been embroiled in an ordeal, one which sees them slumped into seventh place in the group with the side clearly bereft of ideas to wrench out of their impasse. They have managed to muster up a mere two wins in six matches to see the deficit between themselves and Railways stand at eight points as things stand.

The side enter the flay of today’s encounter frayed in their last tie, an outing which saw them defeated by 8 wickets by Maharashtra. It was an encounter in which they were comprehensively dismantled and outplayed in all the departments of the game with their mere 135 never enough o play a real challenge infront of Maharashtra. 

Elsewhere, Railway pulled off an incredible win over Chandigarh, a result which impressed not only owing to the manner it came about in but the identity of the side they managed to put down as well. It was a humdinger of a match, an edge of the seat thriller which saw them manage to win by the barest of margins with the team managing to hang onto the 196 runs they had piled up by the solitary run to make it three wins in succession.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

With the sun set to bake the track and with this being the second encounter of the day to be contested over here, the side winning the toss is going to be a regulation decision. The pitch will be a good track for batting early on with strokeplay being rewarded once you have settled into your groove but eventually as the encounter traverses, spinners will come into full effect to leave the bats perplexed.
