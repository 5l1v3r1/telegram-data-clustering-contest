'Get the deal done': Trump tweets at North Korea's Kim, tells him to 'act quickly'

17 Nov 2019, 20:30

US President Donald Trump on Sunday told North Korean leader Kim Jong-un to "act quickly" to reach a deal with the United States, in a tweet weighing in on North Korea's criticism of his political rival former Vice President Joe Biden.

Trump, who has met Kim three times since 2018 over ending the North's missile and nuclear programs, addressed Kim directly, referring to the one-party state's ruler as "Mr Chairman".

In his tweet, Trump told Kim, "You should act quickly, get the deal done," and hinted at a further meeting, signing off "See you soon!"
