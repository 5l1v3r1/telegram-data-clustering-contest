Ayushmann Khurrana shares an adorable childhood picture of brother Aparshakti Khurana to wish him on his birthday!

17 Nov 2019, 20:28 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Aparshakti Khurana marked his Bollywood debut with blockbuster hit 'Dangal' in the year 2016. The actor went on to impress the audience with his performances in various films.

And now, as the actor turned a year older today, his elder brother Ayushmann Khurrana took to his Instagram to share a childhood picture of Aparshakti. In the photo, the 'Stree' actor can be seen wearing a sweater and pants with a bat in his hands which had some stuff hanging to it. Also, don't miss the hat! In the caption, Ayushmann revealed that his younger brother was trying to mimic legendary actor Raj Kapoor's look from 'Shree 420'.

The caption read, "Happy bday @aparshakti_khurana. 🤗💛👏You’re the world’s best brother. And I wanted to show this picture of yours to the world, where you are trying to mimic Raj Kapoor saab of Shri 420. (Mera joota hai jaapaani) 🤓"

Meanwhile, on the work front, the Khurana brothers shared the screen space in recently released 'Bala'.

Aparshakti will be next seen in Mudassar Aziz's 'Pati Patni Aur Woh'. On the other hand, Ayushmann will feature in films like 'Gulabo Sitabo' and 'Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan'.
