What happened between I’m a Celebrity’s Nadine Coyle and Girls Aloud?

17 Nov 2019, 20:30

For one girl band, friendship never ends. For others… it’s not always so smooth.

Girls Aloud – made up of Cheryl, Sarah Harding, Nadine Coyle, Nicola Roberts and Kimberly Walsh – were created through the ITV talent show Popstars: The Rivals in 2002.

After winning the show, the band became one of Britain’s biggest pop bands, earning 20 consecutive top 10 singles in the United Kingdom, including four number ones.

But the band have since gone their separate ways, with a reunion ‘highly unlikely.’ What went wrong with the Biology hit makers?
