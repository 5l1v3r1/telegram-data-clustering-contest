PE police search for missing man

17 Nov 2019, 10:10 by Algoa FM

Update: Kevin Crouse was found safe and unharmed.

Humewood Detectives are seeking the community's assistance in tracing a 50-year-old man who went missing on Friday, 15/11.

According to police information, Kevin Crouse is residing at Dickens Street in Central, Port Elizabeth with his mother, and his brother reported him missing. 

Mr Crouse left his residence on Friday at approximately 07:00 and never returned home. 

He was reported missing at Humewood police station on Friday evening.

Police visited hospitals in the area and also places he frequently visited in an attempt to trace him.

Police are appealing to anyone who can assist in locating Mr Crouse to urgently contact Humewood SAPS on 041 5045019/18 or Crime Stop 08600 10111 or their nearest police station.
