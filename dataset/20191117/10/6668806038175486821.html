Karisma Kapoor, Amrita Arora and Malaika Arora party together

17 Nov 2019, 10:27 by Kajol Mathur

Time and again we have said that the Kapoor and Arora sisters are our favourite women in Bollywood. The lovely ladies have been tight for years now and it’s only amazing to see their friendship grow so strong. Having yet another fun night in the city we spotted Karisma Kapoor, Amrita Arora and Malaika Arora sans Kareena Kapoor Khan in the city last.

Looking their usual best, the trio was seen exiting a popular eatery and posing for our shutterbugs present there. While Karisma opted for a shimmer gold top which she paired with a black blazer, matching pants and heels, Amrita kept it casual yet chic in a black crop top and printed pants with boots. Malaika, on the other hand, went for comfort as she was seen in all-black athleisure which she rounded off with a pair of white sneakers.
