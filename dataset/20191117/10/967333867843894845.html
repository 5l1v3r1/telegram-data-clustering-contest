Watch Awesome Nine-Year-Old Girl Drum Cover of Nirvana

The nine-year-old drummer Nandi Bushell conquered users online following a YouTube video of her performing a cover version of the song In Bloom (1991) by the band Nirvana.

17 Nov 2019, 10:03

The video showed the young drummer getting into the 'teen spirit' of the 1990's Seattle grunge band. It is also noted that the girl, in addition to drums, plays guitar and sings.

In several days, the video of the young drummer gained almost 360 thousand views.
