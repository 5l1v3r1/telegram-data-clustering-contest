Egypt says 9 killed in road accident in southern province

Road accidents are common in Egypt, often the result of badly maintained roads and poor enforcement of traffic laws.

17 Nov 2019, 10:10

CAIRO: Egyptian police said nine people were killed when a minibus collided with a small truck on a highway in the southern province of Sohag.A police statement said the accident took place late on Saturday and that along with the nine killed, nine others were injured.
