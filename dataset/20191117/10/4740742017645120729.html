Newly promoted Luton Town beating the like of Leeds United, West Brom and Bristol City to the title of best Championship attendances

We've calculated the best capacity percentages after 16 Championship matches

17 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Max Baker, Elliott Jackson

I think we can all agree passionate fans are what make football so special

The Championship is regarded by many as 'the most competitive league in the world', supported by the most loyal fan bases. 

Each week, thousands of fans spend their hard-earned money travelling up and down the country to support their team in their droves. 

It's, at times, a thankless task, which makes the good times all the more rewarding.

Bristol City fans are one of the best clubs across the league at filling their stadium, with the 27,000 seater Ashton Gate in the top half of biggest stadiums in the Championship. 

Thanks to Transfermarkt, we've created a league table based on attendance capacity this season. 

This calculates the maximum capacity of each stadium, compared against the average attendance of each Championship team after eight home games apiece.
