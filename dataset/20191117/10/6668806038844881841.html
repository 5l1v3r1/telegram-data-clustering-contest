Karan Johar bonds with Sidharth Malhotra and his family

17 Nov 2019, 10:36 by Kajol Mathur

Just when Sidharth Malhotra’s latest release – Marjaavaan has opened to decent numbers at the box-office, the actor decided to celebrate it with his family, film’s director Milap Zaveri and good friend Karan Johar. For those who don’t know, the film stars Tara Sutaria opposite Sidharth and minted Rs. 7.03 crores on Friday and Rs. 7.21 crores on Saturday. Enjoying its impressive start, the actor was snapped at a posh Mumbai restaurant where he bonded with KJo and his parents.

They were all later seen happily posing for the shutterbugs. Looking all things hot, Sidharth opted for a white t-shirt which he layered with a denim shirt and paired it with black tracks and eye-catching red sneakers. Karan meanwhile was seen dropping some serious swag in a black tee, ripped denim which he paired with a bright yellow bomber jacket. He completed his look with rimmed glasses and white sneakers.
