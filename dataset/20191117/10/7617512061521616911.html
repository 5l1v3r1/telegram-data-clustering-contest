Traffic moving on Trans-Canada Highway following slide near Goldstream Provincial Park

17 Nov 2019, 10:26

Northbound traffic on the Trans-Canada Highway is moving again following a rockslide south of Goldstream Provincial Park on Sunday morning.

A slide blocked the lane near the entrance to Ice Cream Mountain at about 10 a.m. Flaggers were sent to the scene to direct single-lane alternating traffic from Finlayson Arm Road to Westshore Parkway.

Emcon Services, the road and bridge contractor for southern Vancouver Island, said a loader and dump trucks are clearing the lane.
