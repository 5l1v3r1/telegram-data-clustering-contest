Disha Patani and Aditya Roy Kapur enjoy a beach party in the first look of Malang

17 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Filmfare

Ever since Aditya Roy Kapur and Disha Patani starrer Malang has been announced, there’s a lot of excitement among fans to watch these two stars come together for the first time on the big screen. Helmed by Mohit Suri, the film also stars Kunal Kemmu and Anil Kapoor in interesting roles. Touted to be a revenge drama, Malang is scheduled to release on February 14, 2020.

Finally after a long wait, makers decided to unveil the first official look of Aditya and Disha from the film. Looking like a beach party, Kapur can be seen shirtless with green trousers and a t-shirt tied to his waist. Disha on the other hand, looks cute in a white top and a blue skirt. In the still, both the stars can be seen smiling and looking up in the sky. Aditya shared the look on his Instagram handle saying, “MALANG”

Check it out here.

Malang is jointly produced by Bhushan Kumar, Luv Ranjan, Ankur Garg, and Jay Shewakramani. It’s after super hit Aashiqui 2 that Mohit Suri and Bhushan Kumar are reuniting for a project. The film features Kunal Kemmu playing the role of a cop alongside Anil Kapoor.
