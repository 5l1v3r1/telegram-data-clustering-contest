What time is I’m A Celebrity on tonight and is it on every night this week?

17 Nov 2019, 10:21

The 19th season of I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! is due to kick off in a matter of hours.

At long last, the wait is almost over, as the celebrity contestants head for the jungle and prepare themselves to face the dreaded Bushtucker trials.

The line-up for 2019 is an absolute corker, with Caitlyn Jenner joining the likes of Kate Garraway, Nadine Coyle and Ian Wright in the Australian jungle.

Ahead of what is sure to be a first-rate season, here’s what you need to know about how and when you’ll be able to tune in to I’m A Celeb.

What time is I’m A Celebrity on tonight?

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! starts tonight, 17 November, at 9pm on ITV.

This season will see Ant McPartlin returning to host alongside Declan Donnelly after he was absent from the show in 2018.

Holly Willoughby filled in for him as co-presenter as Ant took time away from being on TV in 2018 to spend time in rehab after he faced drink-driving charges.

At the time, he issued a statement saying: ‘My recovery is going very well and for that to continue having spoken to Dec and ITV, I have made the decision to take the rest of the year off.

‘I’d like to thank both Dec and ITV for their continued support and I look forward to getting back to work in the new year.’

Is I’m A Celebrity on every night?

Yes, once I’m A Celebrity starts, it will be on seven nights a week.

The very first episode will be a special one hour and 45-minute long launch show to get the new season rolling.

It will run from 9pm to 10.45pm on Sunday night, with the rest of the episodes being an hour-long.
