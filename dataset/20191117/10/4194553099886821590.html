(Video) Senegalese artist paints Sadio Mane upside down using a football

17 Nov 2019, 10:22 by Ste Carson

Senegalese artist Boubou, known for going viral when some videos of him painting celebrities hit the internet by storm, has now tried his hand with Sadio Mane.

As an extra twist, as if this skill wasn’t already hard enough to pull off, Boubou decided to use a football as a makeshift paint brush.

He even does this upside down – I mean, he’s not upside down, but he paints Mane the wrong way around. It’s an incredible watch.
