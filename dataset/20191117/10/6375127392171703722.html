2 die, 1 injured in MC accident

17 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Zaida Delos Reyes

TWO persons were killed while one was wounded in a motorcycle accident the other day in San Narciso, Quezon.

The fatalities were identified as Jerwin Carabot and his back rider Mark Jovel Abejuela.

Taken to the nearest hospital was Blademer Culla, of legal.age and also resident of the province.

Reports reaching Camp Crame showed that the accident occurred at around 1:50 am in Bgy. Calwit, San Narciso, Quezon.

Initial investigation said Carabot and Abejuela were onboard a motorcycle on their way home when they accidentally sideswiped another motorcycle driven by Culla.

Carabot and Abejuela were thrown out of the motorcycle and sustained severe injuries.

The three victims were rushed to San Narciso Municipal Hospital but Carabot and Abejuela were pronounced dead on arrival by the attending physician.

Over in Gumaca, Quezon, nine persons were wounded when the passenger bus they were riding crashed into a residential house before dawn.
