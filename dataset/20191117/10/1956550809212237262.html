Be in with a chance to win a staycation for four with every McDonald’s Happy Meal

Sponsored: Celebrate the Global 40th Anniversary of the iconic Happy Meal to win family stay-cations and more

17 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Time Out staff

We don’t need much of an excuse to pick up a meal that always puts a smile on our face, but McDonald’s restaurants around the UAE is giving us just another reason dig in.

In celebration of the Global 40th anniversary of McDonald’s famed Happy Meal, families who get the iconic red box will be in for a chance to win an impressive staycation for four in its huge raffle draw. Count us in.

Taking place from Wednesday November 13 until Monday December 9 with every Happy Meal, families will get the chance to enter the draw to win one of ten weekend stays for a family of four at Lapita Hotel, Dubai Parks and Resorts.

Better yet, it also includes four tickets to all the thrilling theme parks at Dubai Parks and Resorts. That will put a bigger smile on your face when heading to McDonald’s next.

The anniversary celebrations don’t stop there, as other surprises will be taking place across all McDonald’s restaurants in the UAE, including face-painting, balloon modelling and plenty more.

Since the Happy Meal was introduced on the menu, now at more than 38,000 restaurants in more than 100 countries around the globe, billions of families have enjoyed the iconic treat. Oh, and the toy that comes with it, of course.

Over the years, the meal has served up favourites like Chicken McNuggets and Beefburger, and has also given families more balanced options – offering 6.4 billion fruit, vegetable, low-fat dairy and water options in Happy Meals since 2013 across the world.

Picked up your Happy Meal? The raffle draw will be taking place between Wednesday December 11 and Thursday December 12. To learn about how to get involved and where your nearest McDonald’s is, be sure to visit www.mcdonalds.com.

It’s off to McDonald’s we go…
Wed Nov 13-Mon Dec 9 (entry to raffle draw with every Happy Meal), Wed Dec 11-Thu Dec 12 (raffle draw and winners announcement). Various locations across the UAE, www.mcdonalds.com.
