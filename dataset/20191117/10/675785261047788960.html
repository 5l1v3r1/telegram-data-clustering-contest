17 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Lewis Knight, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lewis-knight/

It's almost time for a whole new host of celebrities to be put through their paces.

The 2019 series of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here is almost here and will see Ant and Dec back to present the show in the Australian bush.

The celebrities facing the Bushtucker trials this year are: former Olympian and reality star and transgender icon Caitlyn Jenner, former Girls Aloud star Nadine Coyle, former EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa, Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway, Radio presenter Roman Kemp, former Big Brother star Adele Roberts, comedian Andrew Maxwell, Rak-Su singer Myles Stephenson, former rugby player James Haskell, and former footballer Ian Wright.

However, we are sure there is the potential for some late additions too.

So, just when does the new series start on ITV tonight?

Here is what you need to know about the I'm a Celebrity 2019 start time.

What time does I’m a Celebrity 2019 start tonight on ITV?

I'm a Celebrity 2019 starts on ITV at 9pm on Sunday, November 17, 2019.

The series usually run for about three weeks, showing on every evening on ITV, occasionally missing some Saturday evenings.

The finale is expected to fall on Sunday, December 8, 2019, but this is yet to be confirmed.
