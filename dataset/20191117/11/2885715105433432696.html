Watch: Virat Kohli signs an autograph for fan after Bangladesh Test in Indore

Virat Kohli took some time out to greet and offer an autograph to a fan after wrapping up the first Test against Bangladesh with an innings win inside three days in Indore on Saturday.

17 Nov 2019, 11:27 by Sports Desk

Virat Kohli became the first Indian captain with ten innings wins in Test cricket after defeating Bangladesh by an innings and 130 runs at Holkar International Stadium on Saturday. Instead of revelling in his glory, the skipper took some time out after the match to interact with a fan in Indore.

Along with his leadership skills on the field, the 32-year-old’s heartwarming gesture awed fans which showed how down-to-earth he is. In the video, Kohli stopped by a fan with a disability, named Pooja Sharma, greeted her and signed an autograph.
