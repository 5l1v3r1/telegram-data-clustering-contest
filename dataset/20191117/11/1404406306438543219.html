Lil Reese Says He'll Do VladTV Interview for $1 Million, Vlad Responds

17 Nov 2019, 11:26 by VladTV

Recently, news surfaced that Lil Reese was shot in Chicago, and reportedly suffered a gunshot wound to the neck. Authorities said a gunman allegedly shot the rapper with a small rifle. Since then, the rapper has hopped on IG Live to show that he is good. While on Twitter, a fan suggested to Reese that DJ Vlad was going to try and get the first interview of the rapper since being shot “by any means.”

Reese saw the tweet and responded, saying, “Tell him I need a million for this story this s**t ain’t fake or free s**t bigger [than] 50 Rick Ross and whoever [else].”
