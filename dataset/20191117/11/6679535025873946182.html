NDA convener should be appointed for better coordination: Chirag Paswan

At an NDA meeting ahead of the winter session of Parliament, the newly appointed Lok Jan Shakti party chief said the Shiv Sena’s absence was felt

17 Nov 2019, 11:36 by PTI

An National Democratic Alliance convener should be appointed for better coordination among alliance partners, newly appointed Lok Jan Shakti party chief Chirag Paswan said on Sunday.

Mr. Paswan, speaking to reporters after an NDA meeting ahead of the winter session of Parliament, said the Shiv Sena’s absence was felt at the meet as it was one of the oldest NDA member.

“There should be better coordination among allies and an NDA convener should be appointed,” he said.

“It’s a matter of concern that the Telugu Desam Party left the alliance first and then the Rashtriya Lok Samata Party did.

“But all of us (allies) will work together in the upcoming session and more such meetings should happen,” said Mr. Paswan.
