Ukrainian hryvnia strengthens against U.S. dollar again

The central bank set the official forex rate for the hryvnia against the U.S. dollar for Monday, November 18, at UAH 24.2201 per U.S. dollar.

17 Nov 2019, 11:51

Trade on Ukraine's interbank forex market on Friday, November 15, closed at UAH 24.14/24.17 per U.S. dollar, which was nine kopiykas higher than on the previous day.

Hryvnia quotations to the euro were at UAH 26.66/26.69, which was one kopiyka lower than the closing rate on the previous day.

The National Bank of Ukraine (NBU) set the official forex rate of the hryvnia against the U.S. dollar for Monday, November 18, at UAH 24.2201 per U.S. dollar.

The official forex rate against the euro for Monday is UAH 26.7245 per euro.

As UNIAN reported earlier, trade on Ukraine's interbank forex market on Thursday, November 14, closed at UAH 24.23/24.25 per U.S. dollar, which was seven kopiykas higher than the closing rate on the previous day.
