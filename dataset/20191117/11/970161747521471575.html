Nicole Scherzinger and X Factor: Celebrity's Thom Evans 'secretly dating'

17 Nov 2019, 11:01

Nicole Scherzinger is secretly dating X Factor: Celebrity contestant Thom Evans after splitting from tennis star Grigor Dimitrov earlier this year.

The 41-year-old Pussycat Dolls singer and the 34-year-old rugby star spent a romantic evening together according to reports, with insiders claiming that the pair flirted for weeks before the Try Star member plucked up the courage to ask her out.

‘Thom is completely Nicole’s type,’ a source revealed. ‘And for his part, Thom was smitten early on — he’s always said Nicole is one of the most beautiful women in the world.

‘He fancied her long before meeting her on the show. He was initially a bit nervous about starting something up and ruining his chances on the series, but they had great chemistry from the off.’

The insider continued to The Sun: ‘And after weeks of texting, Thom finally plucked up the courage to ask her out, and took her for dinner.’

According to the source, Nicole and Thom initially met up for drinks as friends but want to see whether they could take their relationship to the next level once X Factor: Celebrity has wrapped.

Thom is currently a contestant on the spin-off series and is a member of the band Try Star, alongside rugby internationals Ben Foden and Levi Davis.

At the beginning of the series, Nicole was seriously excited to see the three heartthrobs with their torses out as they entered the stage with their shirts unbuttoned.

‘Hello, who do we have here?’ the panelist purred.

‘I’m really excited about this,’ Simon Cowell grinned, but that was nothing on his co-judge.

‘Me too, I’m also excited about this shirt right now. How it just hangs, freely and openly… and your chesticles,’ she gasped.

But after Nicole’s on-off romance with Lewis Hamilton and relationship with tennis hero Grigor, Simon jumped in: ’Not another sport’s star…’

Metro.co.uk has contacted a rep for both Nicole and Thom for comment. 

The X Factor: Celebrity continues on Saturday at XX on ITV.
