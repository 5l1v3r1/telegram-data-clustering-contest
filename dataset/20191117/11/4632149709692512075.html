Watch: Drunk, female techie attacks cops at police station in Hyderabad

17 Nov 2019, 11:20 by P Pavan

Hyderabad: A young woman techie attacked a female sub-inspector and constables at Banjara Hills police station in Hyderabad late on Saturday night.

Police said they brought her to the station after she was found lying in an inebriated condition on the road at Jaheeranagar area in upscale Banjara Hills on Saturday night.

Upon gaining senses, she tried escaping from the police station. A woman SI and two constables tried to hold her. However, she overpowered them and bite one of them.

She tried to run but fell down after a constable slapped and pushed her. She got up and threatened to kill the cop and also threatened the male cops present there.


The police suspect that she was under the influence of alcohol or drugs. They sent her blood samples for test.

The woman is said to be from Nagaland who works in a software company at Madhapur in Hyderabad.
