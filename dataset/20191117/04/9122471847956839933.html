NFL Insider Names 7 Teams In Attendance At Kaepernick Workout

17 Nov 2019, 04:01 by Andrew Holleran

Colin Kaepernick ended up moving his workout for the NFL from the Atlanta Falcons facility to a high school about 60 miles away in Georgia. The quarterback opened up his workout to the media and a couple of hundred fans.

There were reportedly more than 24 NFL teams set to attend the workout, though that number likely decreased once the QB changed locations last minute.

Still, at least seven teams were in attendance on Saturday evening.

CBS Sports NFL insider Jason La Canfora reports the following seven teams all had scouts on hand: Eagles, Chiefs, Skins, Jets, Titans, 49ers and Lions.
