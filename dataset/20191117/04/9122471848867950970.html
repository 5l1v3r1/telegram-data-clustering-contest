CeeDee Lamb Tweets Message For Oklahoma Fans After Crazy Win

17 Nov 2019, 04:36 by Andrew Holleran

Oklahoma was without star wide receiver CeeDee Lamb on Saturday night, but the Sooners’ offense was able to pull off a crazy comeback anyway. Lincoln Riley’s team rallied from a 21-point halftime deficit to take down Baylor in Waco.

Lamb, who didn’t play after missing practice with an unspecified injury, was in street clothes on the sideline during OU’s 34-31 win.

The star wide receiver took to Twitter following the game.

“This team I’m grateful for! SOONER NATION THANK YOU! Now let’s work sum more!!!!” he wrote.
