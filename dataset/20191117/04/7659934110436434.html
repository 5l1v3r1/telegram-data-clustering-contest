UAE, Bahrain Discuss Regional Developments

17 Nov 2019, 04:45

Prospects for further strengthening the long-standing relations between the UAE and the Kingdom of Bahrain were reviewed on Saturday by Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of the UAE Armed Forces, and Bahraini Foreign Minister Sheikh Khalid bin Ahmed bin Mohamed Al Khalifa.

Sheikh Mohammed received the Bahraini top diplomat in Abu Dhabi, with the two sides echoing their two countries' determination to grow bilateral relations for the common good of their peoples.

During the meeting their reviewed regional developments and exchanged viewpoints on a number of issues of mutual concern.
