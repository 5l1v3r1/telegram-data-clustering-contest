‘Bala’: The Ayushmann Khurrana starrer shows good growth at the box office on second Saturday

17 Nov 2019, 04:29 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

There is just no stopping the Ayushmann Khurrana starrer 'Bala' at the box office! In just a week of its release, the film has surpassed the Rs 70 crore mark in India and is well on its way to becoming the lead actor’s third 100-crore film.

The news comes a day after the star shared that the film has crossed the Rs 100 crore mark worldwide.

According to the early estimates report on Boxofficeindia.com, the film is maintaining its hold over the ticket windows and has registered a good second Saturday collection of Rs 6.50 crore nett. The 75% boost in the business ensures that the film will make it past the 80-crore nett mark and will cover the lifetime numbers of ‘Luka Chuppi’ in just 11 days.

‘Bala’ had a decent opening day and registered a total approximating to Rs 73 crore till Friday. It also happens to be Ayushmann's biggest release till date as it opened in 3000 screens across India and more than 550 screens in the international markets.

Earlier, clearing the standards of the censor board, 'Bala' also became the 35-year-old actor's first to be released in Saudi Arabia on Thursday, November 14.

The film also starring Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in the lead role is based on premature balding. Ayushmann is seen playing the role of a guy who suffers from the condition and revolves around his love life struggles and how he gets demotivated because of his looks.

The film is helmed by 'Stree' director Amar Kaushik and produced by Dinesh Vijan. It also stars Javed Jaffery and Saurabh Shukla in supporting roles.
