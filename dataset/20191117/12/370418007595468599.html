Sharon Osbourne: “Ozzy Would Dress Up For Me Like A Waiter With No Trousers…”

On a recent episode of The Talk, Sharon revealed an adorable if eye-opening side of her and Ozzy’s private life…

17 Nov 2019, 12:31

Part of what really made The Osbournes an entertaining piece of television was that it showed just how much Ozzy Osbourne really loved his wife, Sharon, and their kids. Even when times were insane and everyone was screaming at one another, there was a sense that the Osbournes had a sweet if unorthodox home life. Case in point, in a recent episode of her show The Talk, Sharon Osbourne revealed a little bit about Ozzy’s boudoir routine that’s pretty adorable — if a little eye-opening.

Sharon’s story comes up when she and co-host Eve discuss withholding sex from their partners. Eve talks about how she doesn’t like to be controlling, but enjoys bartering one service with another. Sharon chimes in: “Do you like to dress up? My husband used to dress up for me!”

This gets the collective co-hosts’ attention, so Sharon explains: “He would dress up in evening wear. He would be like a waiter, and he’d have like a beautiful white tie on, with no trousers, holding a tray! And he’d say, ‘Can I help you, madam?’ And I’m, like, ‘Yeees!’”
