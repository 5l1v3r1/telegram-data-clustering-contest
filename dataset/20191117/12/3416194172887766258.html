Liana Liberato & Jacob Elordi Are Rising Stars at Napa Valley Film Festival 2019

17 Nov 2019, 12:05

Liana Liberato dons a yellow dress to the Rising Star Showcase during the 2019 Napa Valley Film Festival held at Materra | Cunat Family Vineyards on Saturday (November 16) in Napa, Calif.

The 24-year-old Light as a Feather actress was honored as a rising star, alongside Jacob Elordi, Kelsey Asbille, Mena Massoud, Jessica Barden, and Dean-Charles Chapman.

Jessica‘s BFF Hayley Law was also in attendance to show her support.

“Best friend in the world @hayleaulaw,” she captioned one photo of the two together, adding with another, “Have you ever seen two people more in love ❤️”

FYI: Jessica is wearing a Zimmermann dress. Kelsey is wearing Louis Vuitton.
