Cristiano claims Brazil would have won 5 more World Cups with him

17 Nov 2019, 12:25 by Archie Blade

Cristiano claims Brazil would have won 5 more World Cups with him. The revelation was made by his Juventus teammate Danilo.

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the best players if not the very best to have ever played the game of football. He has achieved almost everything possible when it comes to club football. However international football is a whole different ball game.

The Portuguese has fared extremely well individually with 98 goals in 163 games. These goals have helped his side to a European Championship glory in 2016 and a UEFA Nations League triumph in 2019. A World Cup still eludes him though. However, Juventus teammate Danilo says that Cristiano Ronaldo has joked that he could have ended with not one but 5 had he been born a Brazilian.

Cristiano claims Brazil would have won 5 more World Cups with him

In a recent interview, Danilo told the DAZN series Saudade:

“I have known Cris since Real Madrid and now it’s very nice reuniting with him at Juventus.

“He has an eye for detail and that, along with his natural quality, makes it impossible that he wouldn’t be reaping the results that he has been.

“If he played for Brazil, we would have five more World Cups by now, he jokes.”
