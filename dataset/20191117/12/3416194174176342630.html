Skai Jackson & JoJo Step Out For Soul Train Awards 2019

17 Nov 2019, 12:38

Skai Jackson wears a short pink dress with black accents to the 2019 Soul Train Awards presented by BET held at the Orleans Arena on Sunday night (November 17) in Las Vegas.

The 17-year-old actress wore her hair up in a pony, and donned black nail polish.

Also in attendance at the award show was singer JoJo, who is nominated for Best Collaboration with PJ Morton for their song “Say So”.

JoJo actually drove straight from Los Angeles to Las Vegas earlier in the day to attend the event. She shared a video on Instagram story of herself with a face mask on riding in the back seat, going down the highway.
