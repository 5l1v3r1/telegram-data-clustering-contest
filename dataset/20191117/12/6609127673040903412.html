Man struck by motor on Bellshill road as forensic teams lock down scene

17 Nov 2019, 12:35 by Katy Pagan

A MAN was struck by a motor in Lanarkshire last night.

Emergency teams raced to Hope Street, Bellshill, near Glasgow, after the alarm was raised at 8.50pm.

An eyewitness told the Scottish Sun Online there was still a "large police presence" including a cordon and forensic teams at the scene this morning.

The condition of the victim remains unknown at this time.

Cops said enquiries are ongoing.

But it is yet to be confirmed whether the incident will be treated as a hit-and-run.

A police spokesman said: "Police are investigating following reports of a male being hit by a motor vehicle at around 8.50pm on Saturday, November 16, on Hope Street, Glasgow.

"Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 3869 of November 16."
