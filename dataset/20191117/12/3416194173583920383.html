Ross Lynch Has a Night Out With 'Sabrina' Co-Stars Kiernan Shipka & Jaz Sinclair

17 Nov 2019, 12:30

The cast of Chilling Adventures of Sabrina reunited for a fun night out!

Star Kiernan Shipka joined Ross Lynch and Jaz Sinclair for a hockey game outing on Saturday night (November 16) at Rogers Arena in Vancouver, Canada.
