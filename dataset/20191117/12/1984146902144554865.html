The former Oasis frontman is playing at Newcastle's Utilita Arena on Sunday night

17 Nov 2019, 12:52 by Katie Collings

The drummer of North East band Lindisfarne had a cheeky response to Liam Gallagher's tweet ahead of his Newcastle gig.

Ray, who played with the band on and off between 1968 and 2003, is also heavily involved in events such as Sunday for Sammy and Christmas at the Cathedral for the Sir Bobby Robson Foundation.

Liam has yet to respond to Ray's Tweet.

The star is touring his second solo album Why Me? Why Not which follows 2017's As You Were, which led to sell-out shows.

Liam Gallagher and support acts will kick off his 2020 tour at Newcastle Utilita Area from 7pm on Sunday November 17.
