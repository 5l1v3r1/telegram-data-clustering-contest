7 top things to do in Dubai this week

Unlimited pizza, cinema on the beach and new ladies’ nights

17 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Time Out Dubai staff

It’s a fresh new week and it’s time to get planning. We bring you seven fun things to do in Dubai this week, from a new cinema ladies’ night, to money-saving dining deals. Enjoy.

Watch The Bodyguard on the beach
Dubai’s Zero Gravity has just revealed the next Hollywood blockbuster to hit the sandy shores of Dubai Marina as part of its Cinema on the Sand series and it’s probably favourite one yet – The Bodyguard. And no we’re not talking about the British thriller series that everyone was going on about last year – we’re talking about the 1992 romantic thriller film starring Kevin Costner and Whitney Houston. The movie boasts the best-selling soundtrack of all time including Whitney Houston’s ‘I Will Always Love You’. And we know you’ll be singing along on the beach.
Dhs50. Sun Nov 17, 8pm. Skydive Dubai Drop Zone, Dubai Marina (04 399 0009).

Eat, Sip, Save
Time Out Dubai’s city-wide Eat, Sip, Save campaign is now underway and runs all the way through until Sunday November 30. We’ve scoured the city and partnered up with some of Dubai’s best restaurants, cafés and bars to come up with some exceptional dining promotions – just for Time Out Dubai readers. Bon appetit. For a full list of deals, click here.
Until Nov 30. Various locations. Quote Time Out Dubai when booking. For full list of venues visit timeoutdubai.com/eatsipsave.

Check out a new gents’ night
El Sur is offering señors a night of Spanish bites and bevvies. Every Wednesday from 8pm until midnight, gents can tuck into a generous platter of Spanish tapas and portion of barbecued meat served with patatas bravas which can all be washed done with four drinks including red grape, red grape-based mixed drinks and Spanish hops. Olé!
Dhs195. Wed 8pm-midnight. Ongoing. The Westin Dubai Mina Seyahi Beach Resort & Marina, Al Sufouh (04 511 7434).

Have a bargain afternoon tea Shakespeare and Co.
Tea on a shoestring? Enjoy mini sandwiches, macarons, chocolates and more for under Dhs100 at this cute tea spot. The newly launched afternoon tea will be served daily from 3pm to 6pm at all branches across the UAE excluding The Dubai Mall and comes in at Dhs69 per person. The pocket-friendly deal includes the choice of one tea or one coffee, a selection of bread, butter and jams, a trio of mini sandwiches (salmon toast, club sandwich and the option between a chicken or beef slider), one individual cake, four pieces of chocolate served in a box and the choice of any two macaron flavours.
Dhs69. Daily 3pm-6pm. Locations include Arabian Ranches, Dubai Marina Mall, Palm Jumeirah, www.shakespeare-and-co.com.

Try a new ladies’ night at Roxy Cinemas
Looking for different things to do in Dubai on ladies’ night? How does a Hollywood blockbuster, mini massages and a slap-up meal sound to you? Well, mark your calendars as Roxy Cinemas popular ladies’ night is back. Head to the City Walk branch on Tuesday November 19 from 6.30pm onwards for an evening of pampering and a top movie. Tickets cost Dhs99 and include free express nail polish changes and mini massages from 6.30pm onwards before the movie begins at 7.30pm. Ladies can pick between Charlie's Angels, The Good Liar and Poms and the screenings will be taking place in the in the Platinum Plus experience so get ready to get comfy with a blanket, pillow and fully reclining leather chair. While watching the movie you’ll be a served a starter, main course and mocktail.
Dhs99. Tue Nov 19, 6.30pm. City Walk, Jumeirah, www.theroxycinemas.com.

Buy tickets to Iron Maiden
Iron Maiden will be coming to Dubai to perform a huge gig as part of their Legacy of the Beast World Tour, on May 27, 2020. Now, tickets are on sale. Prices start at Dhs250 for seated tickets, and from Dhs495 for regular standing tickets. For those that want to be right next to Bruce Dickinson and co on stage, premium standing tickets are priced at Dhs995. Whatever your ticket choice, Iron Maiden are known for their absolutely electric, phenomenal live shows.
From Dhs250. Wed May 27, 2020.. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, 800tickets.com

Get unlimited pizza at Carluccio’s
Every Tuesday at Carluccio’s you can enjoy unlimited pasta and pizza as part of the new Giro menu. Head along to any branch of the Italian restaurant from 6pm and you can feast on a range of pasta dishes and pizza. The offer costs Dhs89 per person, better still, children under the age of 11 eat for free so it’s a great chance for a mid-week family night out or a feast with a few friends.
Dhs89. Various locations Dubai Marina and The Dubai Mall www.carlucciosme.com
