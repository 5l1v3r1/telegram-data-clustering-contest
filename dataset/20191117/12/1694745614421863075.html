15 Healthy Meal-Prep Recipes That Will Help You Fight Lunchtime Cravings All Week Long

17 Nov 2019, 12:07 by Tarah Chieffi

Some days it seems like temptation is everywhere . . . especially when lunchtime rolls around. Saying no to takeout or a drive-through is much easier when you've devoted some time to meal prep and have a healthy meal waiting for you in the fridge, so we've pulled together 15 healthy recipes that are just as craveable as whatever your coworkers try to tempt you with on any given day.

While these meals run the gamut from soups and salads to lettuce wraps, lentil bakes, and burrito bowls, they all have a few things in common. They use fresh, natural ingredients, they're made with lean or plant-based proteins, and they're all easy to portion out and pack up for lunch. It'll be tough to choose a favorite, but read on to give it your best shot.

1 Avocado Chicken Salad

While this recipe only makes enough for one serving, you can easily add a few portions to kick-start your week. Just store the chicken salad separate from the other sandwich ingredients until ready to serve.

Get the recipe: avocado chicken salad

2 Cucumber, Corn, Black Bean, Tomato, and Avocado Salad

What's not to love about this fresh and flavorful salad? There are beans for protein, avocado for healthy fats, and corn, peppers, and cucumbers for that all-important crunch.

Get the recipe: cucumber, corn, black bean, tomato, and avocado salad

3 Red Pepper and Lentil Bake

This combo of red peppers and lentils bakes in one dish and is a good alternative to salad when you want something warm for lunch. It's the perfect meal-prep dish for vegetarians.

Get the recipe: red pepper and lentil bake

4 Roasted Sweet Potato and Quinoa Salad

Mason jar salads were made for meal prep. This one is made with quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, and a mango balsamic vinaigrette that'll leave you feeling satisfied until dinner.

Get the recipe: roasted sweet potato and quinoa salad

5 Dandan Noodles Cups

Instant noodles can't compete with this healthier version, made with zucchini noodles and a flavorful tahini-based sauce. The recipe includes instructions for prepping and storing enough food for four meals.

Get the recipe: dandan noodles cups

6 Black Bean Burrito Bowl Salad

To keep this (or any) salad crisp and fresh, store it in a mason jar with the dressing and other wet ingredients, like tomatoes, at the bottom and the lighter ingredients, like lettuce, on top.

Get the recipe: black bean burrito bowl salad

7 Red Pepper and Tomato Meatball Soup

Soups work well for meal prep because most recipes make at least six servings, and leftovers can easily be refrigerated or frozen for later. We especially love the turkey meatballs and parmesan cheese in this hearty soup.

Get the recipe: red pepper and tomato meatball soup

8 Mexican Cobb Salad

Some salads just don't have enough staying power to get you through to dinner, but this is not one of them. To keep the ingredients fresh, try storing them in a compartmentalized container, where you can keep the veggies, lettuce, and protein separate, then add the avocado just before serving.

Get the recipe: Mexican Cobb salad

9 Italian Stuffed Peppers

Many dishes don't work well for lunch because they aren't easy to pack, but these Italian stuffed peppers are healthy, portable, and delicious. You can make as many as you need and reheat them in the microwave when hunger calls.

Get the recipe: Italian stuffed peppers

10 Harissa Roasted Sweet Potato Power Salad

We cannot get over the bright, fresh colors and flavors in this sweet potato, kale, and avocado salad. Kale is heartier than other greens, so things won't get soggy even if you prep the whole thing ahead of time.

Get the recipe: harissa roasted sweet potato power salad

11 Vegan Burrito Bowls

It's hard to believe, but the "meat" used in these burrito bowls is actually made with walnuts and cauliflower. Along with roasted veggies and rice, you've got yourself one tasty lunch.

Get the recipe: vegan burrito bowls

12 Mexican Avocado Egg Salad

We guarantee this will not be like any other egg salad you've eaten before. It's spicy, nutritious, and gets all wrapped up in lightly steamed collard green leaves.

Get the recipe: Mexican avocado egg salad

13 Moroccan Carrot Soup With Chickpeas

This soup is thick, creamy, and loaded with warm Moroccan spices. The recipe makes six servings, but it's pretty low in calories, so we suggest topping with yogurt or crunchy chickpeas (or both).

Get the recipe: Moroccan carrot soup with chickpeas

14 Veggie Burger Taco Salad

If you're looking for a creative way to use frozen veggie burgers, look no further than this taco salad. Store prepped ingredients separately until ready to serve or layer in mason jars, pouring in the chipotle tahini dressing first.

Get the recipe: veggie burger taco salad

15 Beef Chili

This veggie-filled chili is made with ground beef, but you could just as easily swap in turkey or chicken, and with five servings, you'll have lunches all week.

Get the recipe: beef chili
