Two killed, four hurt in a collision near Hunsur

17 Nov 2019, 12:34 by Gayathri V Raj

Two people from Chennai, were killed and four others sustained injuries in an early morning mishap near Hunsur, Mysuru district, on Sunday.

The mishap occurred near Chilkunda village when a tipper rammed into the car they were travelling. 

The four passengers who were seriously injured have been admitted to a private hospital in Mysuru.
