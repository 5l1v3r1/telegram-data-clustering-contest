Serious crash, Mount Eden

17 Nov 2019, 12:06 by New Zealand Police

"Serious crash, Mount Eden"

Emergency services are attending a serious crash at the intersection of Dominion Road and Wiremu Street, Mount Eden, Auckland.

Police were called to the crash, involving a car and motorcycle, around 7pm.

One person is reported to have received serious injuries.

Motorists are asked to avoid the area.
