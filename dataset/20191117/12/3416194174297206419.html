Ally Brooke Has Joined a New Band!

17 Nov 2019, 12:30

Ally Brooke joined a rock band…sort of!

The 25-year-old singer took to her Instagram to jokingly reveal that she joined a new band, but it’s all a part of her gig on Dancing With the Stars!
