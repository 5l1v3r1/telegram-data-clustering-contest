Guwahati Body Building meet on November 20 at District Library

17 Nov 2019, 12:02

Our Sports Reporter

GUWAHATI: Chidananda Das Memorial 7th All Guwahati Body Building and Physique Sports Championship’2019 will be held on November 20 at District Library, Guwahati. There will be several weight categories in the competition for Mr. Guwahati, Senior, Junior, Men Model Physique and Miss Guwahati  Fitness and Miss Guwahati. Guwahati District Body Building Team for Mr.Assam 2019 will be selected from the competition. Sarupathar will host the event on December 29-30.
