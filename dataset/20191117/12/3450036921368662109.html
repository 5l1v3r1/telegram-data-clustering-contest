Body of deceased female found in rural Mahaska County

17 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Ashley Hoak

MAHASKA COUNTY, Iowa â€” Law enforcement in southeast Iowa are investigating after a body was located over the weekend.

According to the Mahaska County Sheriff's Office, deputies received a report of a body lying on the shoulder of the road in the 2500 block of Highway 163, in rural Mahaska County.

Authorities say the deceased body was that of an elderly female.

The body was transported to the Iowa State Medical Examiner's Office in Ankeny for an autopsy.

The identity of the woman is not being released, and the cause of death is unknown at this time.

A preliminary investigation indicates there is no danger to the public in relation to this death.
