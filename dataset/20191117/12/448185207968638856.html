Assam Cyclists Win Medals in National Road Cycling Championship

17 Nov 2019, 12:25

Our Sports Reporter

GUWAHATI: Assam Cyclists won two medals in the National Road Cycling Championship which was held in Bikaner. Chayanika Gogoi bagged gold and her teammate Gongutri Bordoloi won a silver in the meet.
