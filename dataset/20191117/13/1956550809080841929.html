See local talent at a free gig at Dubai’s ibis One Central

Catch some of Dubai’s best local talent for free

17 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Reema Rahman

A free gig with top local talent? Where do we sign up?

ibis Music, a new initiative that aims to bring together established musicians, up-and-coming artists and live music fans together in intimate concerts, is launching in Dubai this weekend.

The first gig will be taking place on Friday November 22 at ibis One Central at Dubai World Trade Centre and in better news, the four-hour gig is completely free to attend. Winning.

The diverse line-up of homegrown talent consists of some of Dubai’s most popular local artists – we’re talking Moh Flow, Freek, Seki SuperVillain and Jaymie Deville, while DJ 1Takennando will be hitting the decks throughout the night.

The gig will be taking place on the outdoor terrace at the hotel’s pan-Asian eatery Wok & Co and concertgoers can register here (www.ibis-music.com/uae/events) to attend the free gig.
