Mohammed Shami best bowler in the world on current form: Dale Steyn

17 Nov 2019, 13:55 by IANS

Highlights

  • Steyn said so while answering fans' questions on Twitter in a Q&A session that he held on his handle
  • Shami has been turning heads with his form this year
  • Shami jumped eight spots to the seventh position in the latest ICC Test rankings

NEW DELHI: Former South Africa pacer Dale Steyn feels that Mohammed Shami is the best bowler in the world on current form. Steyn said so while answering fans' questions on Twitter in a Q&A session that he held on his handle.

When one user asked him who he felt was currently the best bowler in the world, the Proteas great answered, "Shami on current form."
