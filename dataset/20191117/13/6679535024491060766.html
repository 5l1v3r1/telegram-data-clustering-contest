Governor General claims the Royal Calcutta Turf Club Trophy

17 Nov 2019, 13:24

Governor General, ridden by apprentice K. Pranil, claimed the Royal Calcutta Turf Club Trophy, the main event of the opening day’s races of Mumbai racing season held here on Sunday (Nov. 17).

The winner is owned by Mr. & Mrs. I. Nathani, M/s. Murad I. Nathani & Zain I. Nathani rep. Alpha Stud and Farms Pvt Ltd.

M.K. Jadhav trains the winner.
