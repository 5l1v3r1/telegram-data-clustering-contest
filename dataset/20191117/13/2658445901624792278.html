Gunmen kill herbalist in Ondo - The Nation Newspaper

17 Nov 2019, 13:32

Damisi Ojo, Akure

Tragedy struck in the ancient town of Ifira, Akoko Southeast local government, yesterday evening when a 36-year-old herbalist called Omotayo Omoskin was gunned down by yet-to-be-known assassins.

A source from the neighbouring house of the slain herbalist revealed Â that the late Omo Akin was called on phone to come out by his assailants and he came out and he was shot dead immediately.

The deceased, a polygamist with two wives who resided at Isowo Quarters, Ifira Akoko, was described as very humble and hospitable with moderate lifestyle.

The police from Isua-Akoko led by the Divisional Police Officer (DPO), Superintendent of Police(SP) Olowoyo Akinbode, led his men to the scene of the incident and carried the corpse to the mortuary of the General Hospital Ipe-Akoko.

According to Akinbode, the case will be transferred to the state Criminal Investigation Department (CID) Akure for more investigation.

The Asiwaju of Ifira Akoko, Chief Boboye Ojomo, described the incident as shocking and another period of mourning for the entire Ifira community.
