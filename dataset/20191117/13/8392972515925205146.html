Prosecco bath bombs are now available to buy from Firebox for £7.99

They come in packs of 10

17 Nov 2019, 13:51 by Paul Hirons

If you're looking for something a little bit more luxurious for relax in your bath than the last squirts of the bubble bath bought for you five Christmases ago, Firebox is selling Prosecco bath bombs.

The gift website is pushing out the wine-scented fizz capsules in the run up to Christmas.

Potential customers are encouraged: "Imagine a tub of shimmering golden fizz, rich in glitter and nourishing skin-friendly naturals like coconut oil.

"The scent of prosecco luxuriates in the air around you. You're basted in hydrating, bubbly glitter - leaving a healthy glow when you finally dare to part with your dream bath. Bliss."

Each pack contains 10 bombs, and costs £7.99 from the site.

With an average review score of 4.5, it seems the bath bombs have gone down a treat with customers.

One of them said in a review: "Exactly what I expected!"

Another wrote that they were "really nice".

Firebox isn't the only outlet selling booze-inspired bathroom products this festive season.

Soapmaker Baylis & Harding has teamed up with boutique Cotswolds coaching inn The Fuzzy Duck to produce a range of hand soaps inspired by some of the pub's favourite cocktails.

And now that same range, including gin and tonic and espresso martini, is on sale at Sainsbury's.

The Fuzzy Duck range also extends to male grooming kits, bubble bath and fragranced candles.

The hand soap range, which includes fragrances inspired by cocktails gin and tonic, espresso martini, ginger and lime and cosmopolitan, is also on sale in Tesco, priced £3 per 500ml bottle.
