US military convoy passes through Qamishli city en route to oil fields: video

17 Nov 2019, 13:44 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (3:30 P.M.) – A U.S. military convoy entered the northeastern city of Qamishli on Sunday and was seen heading east for a regular patrol.

The U.S. military convoy was reportedly heading towards the oil fields that are under their control in the eastern part of the Al-Hasakah Governorate.
