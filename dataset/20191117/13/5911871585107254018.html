Watch Julia Jacklin Cover Avril Lavigne’s “I’m With You”

17 Nov 2019, 13:15 by James Rettig

For the last few weeks, Australian musician Julia Jacklin has been on a North American tour in support of her latest album Crushing. While she’s been stateside, Jacklin was even brought out by Lana Del Rey at a recent Norman Fucking Rockwell! show.

At her own concerts, Jacklin has taken to performing a great cover of Avril Lavigne’s “I’m With You,” from the Canadian’s 2002 album Let Go. She did it a couple nights ago with opener Christian Lee Hutson, and she also did it when she rolled through Brooklyn a couple weeks ago at Warsaw. You can watch video of her performing it below.
