Marjaavaan box office collection Day 2: Sidharth film mints Rs 14.24 crore

The Riteish Deshmukh and Sidharth Malhotra starrer earned Rs 7.21 crore on the second day. Its total collection now stands at Rs 14.24 crore. The film also stars Tara Sutaria and Rakul Preet Singh.

17 Nov 2019, 13:49 by Entertainment Desk

Riteish Deshmukh and Sidharth Malhotra starrer Marjaavaan has collected Rs 14.24 crore after two days of its box office run. The film had opened with Rs 7.03 crore. It earned Rs 7.21 crore on the second day. The Milap Zaveri directorial also stars Tara Sutaria and Rakul Preet Singh.

Trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted the film box office’s figures. He wrote, “#Marjaavaan maintains strong occupancy in mass belt/single screens on Day 2… Metros/multiplexes – average on Day 1 – remain in the same range… Biz should grow on Day 3… Eyes ₹ 22 cr [+/-] weekend… Fri 7.03 cr, Sat 7.21 cr. Total: ₹ 14.24 cr. #India biz.”

Trade analyst Girish Johar told indianexpress.com earlier, “Marjaavan is looking like a commercial masala entertainer. Milap Zaveri is coming with this film after Satyameva Jayate, which was again a mass entertainer. It is after a long time a film is coming which will cater to the B and C towns and the audience of hardcore action drama. Riteish Deshmukh is the key element of this film. He has played a dwarf villain, and he is looking fabulous. The songs have also been appreciated.”
