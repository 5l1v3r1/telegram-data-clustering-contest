Ruthvika, Siddharth Clinch Senior Ranking Badminton Titles

Ruthvika, a South Asian Games gold medallist, triumphed over 13th seed Shruti Mundada to win the final 21-10, 21-17 in just 35 minutes.

17 Nov 2019, 13:55

Ruthvika Shivani Gadde bounced back to form in style as she cliched the women’s singles title while 10th seed Siddharth Pratap Singh pocketed the men’s section at the Yonex Sunrise All India Senior Ranking badminton tournament in Pune on Sunday.

Ruthvika, a South Asian Games gold medallist, triumphed over 13th seed Shruti Mundada to win the final 21-10, 21-17 in just 35 minutes.
