Busta Rhymes Celebrates Rodney Reed’s Powerful Stay Of Execution: “When The People Speak + The Truth Is Undisputed”

17 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Biz Jones

New York rapper Busta Rhymes is celebrating Rodney Reed getting closer to freedom. The hip-hop artist has reacted to the Texas prisoner receiving a stay of execution.

Last night, Busta went to Instagram to celebrate the huge achievement.

Heading into the weekend, reality TV star Kim Kardashian revealed she met Reed the same day he received the stay of execution.
