Mariah Carey Shares Adorable Photo Of Son Wearing Deadpool Mask When Meeting Ryan Reynolds

17 Nov 2019, 13:49 by Brent Furdyk

Mariah Carey has been in full-on mom mode on Instagram, and on Sunday she shared an adorable photo of herself, twins Moroccan and and Monroe (a.k.a. Roc and Roe), and a pair of celebrity guest stars.

In the photo, the “Glitter” star and her kids are posing with Ryan Reynolds and wife Blake Lively, while Roc strikes a heroic pose while wearing a Deadpool mask.

“Rocky snuck up on an unsuspecting couple wearing a Deadpool mask. Will they ever recover? 😉💖,” she wrote in the caption.

This isn’t the only celebrity encounter that Roc and Roe have experienced in recent days. On Thursday, their mom posted a photo of the kids with “Stranger Things” star Millie Bobby Brown, describing it as the “best day of their 8 year old lives!”

 

In a 2016 interview with Clash, Carey revealed that her kids — whom she shares with ex Nick Cannon — are definitely not homebodies.

“But they love traveling. They love flying,” she explained. “If I keep them home for like five days, they’re like, ‘When are we going on an airplane?’ They really love it.”

She added: “They love doing all these things and seeing places — they love the Eiffel Tower. It’s amazing, you know what I mean? I just have the best time with them.”
