Updated: Many Nokia smartphones getting Google Assistant’s Ambient mode now

17 Nov 2019, 06:30 by Nayan

Nokia 6.2 and 7.2 launched with an exclusive feature, the Google Assistant’s Ambient mode and it was surprisingly unavailable on even these two devices in markets like India. But now many of our readers report that they are now able to use “Ambient mode” on their Nokia smartphones in what looks like a sever-side roll-out of the feature by Google.

Update: So far we have received tips about Nokia 6.1, 6.1 Plus, 7.1, 8.1, 9 PureView and even Nokia 3.2, 4.2, 3 users receiving the feature in some markets. You can check for the feature availability by going to Settings–>Google–>Account services–>Search, Assistant & Voice–>Google Assistant–>Assistant–>Scroll down to Assitant devices–>Phone.

Google describes Ambient Mode as a new visual overview that gets triggered when devices are plugged in. The Ambient Mode makes it easier to see notifications and reminders, start a playlist and control smart home devices on the lock screen of your Nokia smartphone.

While your mobile device is charging on a nightstand, living room table, or kitchen counter, it can still help you stay on top of your day.
