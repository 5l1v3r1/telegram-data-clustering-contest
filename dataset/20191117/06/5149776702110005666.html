The most bizarre reviews people have left for McDonald's in Nottingham

We've searched through some of the most rib-tickling comments from customers

17 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Hayley Watson

There's got to be very few people who have never tried McDonald's in Nottingham.

So surely, there's no reason to check the online reviews for the fast food chain.

Or is there?

While most of us already know what we're getting with a 'Maccies', there's one good thing review sites like TripAdvisor and Google exist for - our entertainment.

From savage comments on the food to bizarre encounters, review sites are filled with rib-tickling comments from customers.

Here are some of the weirdest ones we've spotted for Maccies branches in Nottingham:

McDonald's Angel Row

Google user Barry French said: "Was off my trolley. McDonald's is always good when you're drunk."

McDonald's Clumber Street

Google user Steve Burn said: "I have a McDonald's every other Thursday, but shh, don't tell anyone, it is a secret."

TripAdvisor user Ormstein said: "Wipe clean, utilitarian laminate everywhere. Not a Ronald McDonald anywhere."

McDonald's Exchange Walk

Google user Joe de Max said: "The Golden Arches. What can I say. The one thing in my life that will never let me down."

McDonald's intu Victoria Centre

Google user Alexander Place said: "The mcwaiter was great and I loved the mcchairs."

Google user Marc Grylls said: "Nice after 10 pints."
