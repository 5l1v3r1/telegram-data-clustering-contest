Why do women like to bring colleagues down? – Juliet Ibrahim quizzes

17 Nov 2019, 06:50 by MyNewsGH

Actress, Juliet Ibrahim is wondering women take delight in bringing their colleagues down in a challenging world when they should be polling resources to make progress

According to her,women do not want to see their colleague women flourish at what they do; something she sees as not right.

This comes on the back of reports that Yvonne Nelson sabotaged Nana Akua Addo who was supposed to have styled the MC for Obrafour’s PAE MU KA.

In the post, Juliet said ” Why do we, as women bring down other women, knowing how hard life can be for a woman? Never mock or laugh at another woman when she’s in her predicament, because Sister one day you or someone close to you might be a victim of something similar or worse”.
