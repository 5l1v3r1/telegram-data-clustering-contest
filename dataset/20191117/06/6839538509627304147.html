37-year-old man charged with setting his own house alight in Kalgoorlie causing up to $300,000 damage

17 Nov 2019, 06:05

A 37-year-old man has been charged with criminal damage by fire after allegedly setting his own house alight in regional WA, causing up to $300,000 damage.

Fire brigades were called to the Kalgoorlie home on Thursday to extinguish the blaze, which is thought to have started around 10.30pm.

The accused man lived alone at the address.

He is due to appear in court on November 25.
