Apart from Kojo Cue other albums released this year are wack – Kwaw Kese

17 Nov 2019, 06:59 by MyNewsGH

Rapper, Kwaw Kese says apart from Kojo Cue’s album, albums released by other artistes this year are wack.

He made this known in a tweet.

He said ” Like joke like joke no better album drop this year oo”.

But Kwaw was quick to add that “@KOJO_Cue Album be fire, not a scam”

Some tweeps, however, did not agree to his claims because to them other artistes such as Patapaa and Shatta Wale released albums which are so good.
