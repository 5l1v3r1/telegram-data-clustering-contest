Looking for a forever home; Penticton SPCA pet of the week

17 Nov 2019, 18:28 by Parker Crook

Zeke is a one-year-old dog looking for a new home.

"This sweet boy is quite excitable and is always ready to play," said Holly with the South Okanagan-Similkameen SPCA. "Zeke would love an active lifestyle and a home where he can spend most of ihs time with his people."

For more information about Zeke and other animals available for adoption, visit the South Okanagan-Similkameen SPCA.
