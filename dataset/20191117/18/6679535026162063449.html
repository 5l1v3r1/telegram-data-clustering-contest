Commendable gesture

17 Nov 2019, 18:35

The air traffic controller from Pakistan’s civil aviation authority deserves accolades for coming to the rescue of 150 passengers flying from Jaipur to Muscat (“Pak. Air traffic controller saves Jaipur-Muscat plane,” Nov. 17). His prompt response to the pilot’s “Mayday” broadcast and directing the flight through the remaining journey speaks volumes about the ATC’s presence of mind.

P.G. Menon,

Chennai
