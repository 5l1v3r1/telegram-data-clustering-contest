The Handmaid’s Tale author Margaret Atwood to visit Perth on In Conversation with Margaret Atwood tour

17 Nov 2019, 18:00

From Gilead to Australia — famed author Margaret Atwood is headed Down Under.

The Handmaid’s Tale author will appear live on stage early next year as part of her In Conversation with Margaret Atwood tour, including a date at Perth’s Riverside Theatre on March 1.

The visit comes after the publication of The Testaments, the much-hyped sequel to her seminal novel.

It comes 34 years after The Handmaid’s Tale, which remains a symbol of women’s rights and inspired the Emmy-winning TV series.

Atwood said the tour would include the stories behind her diverse works and her latest project.

“I am so happy to be returning to Australia ... where readers have always been so warm and welcoming,” she said.

Ticket pre-sales begin a week today before the general release on November 29. See margaretatwoodlive.com.au.
