Nebraska’s Wan’Dale Robinson Has Message For Huskers Fans

17 Nov 2019, 18:45 by Andrew Holleran

Nebraska played better on Saturday, but the Huskers were still incapable of pulling out a victory at home against Wisconsin. Scott Frost’s team fell, 37-21, to drop to 4-6 on the season.

The Huskers were without star freshman Wan’Dale Robinson, who missed the game with an injury.

Robinson has been one of the team’s lone bright spots this season. He posted a message for Huskers fans following the game, asking them to be patient.

“We’ll get this thing turned around, stay patient,” he tweeted.
