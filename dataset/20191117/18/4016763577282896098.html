OneRepublic’s Ryan Tedder Says There’s “Misinformation” On Both Sides Of Taylor Swift & Scooter Braun’s Dispute

17 Nov 2019, 18:31 by Caitlin Kelley

Taylor Swift’s conflict with Scooter Braun and Big Machine Records just escalated over a licensing dispute. However, OneRepublic’s Ryan Tedder just told Rolling Stone that “there’s a lot of misinformation” on both sides.

The pop superstar claims that Braun and Big Machine’s Scott Borchetta are blocking her upcoming performances and Netflix documentary while strongarming her out of re-recording her pre-Lover material.
