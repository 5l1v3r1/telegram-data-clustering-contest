Holly Willoughby skips I'm A Celebrity launch to attend the Frozen 2 premiere

Holly Willoughby, 38, is more than just a fan of I'm A Celebrity as she was brought in to replace Ant McPartlin last year

17 Nov 2019, 18:29 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

There's just hours to go until the start of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!, but it doesn't look like Holly Willoughby will be tuning in.

She skipped a night in front of the TV with the rest of the nation to attend the premiere of Frozen 2 in London on Sunday night.

Holly, 38, is more than just a fan of the ITV reality show as she was brought in to replace Ant McPartlin when he took a year out of I'm A Celebrity.

A self-confessed Disney fan, Holly made sure she didn't miss out on being one of the first to see the highly anticipated sequel.

She wrapped up warm in a monochrome outfit, with a chic gingham coat thrown over her shoulders.

Underneath she donned a black midi-dress with a high neck and matched it with a pair of leather gloves.

Holly kept her legs covered in a pair of knee high boots and black tights.

Her trademark blonde locks with worn straight and loose around her shoulders.

The mum-of-three sported a slick of bright red lipstick and a coat heavy black eyeliner.

Holly posed for pictures with actors dressed up as Kristoff, Anna, Elsa and Olaf, the characters from Frozen.

I'm A Celebrity kicks off tonight with a host of famous faces heading to the jungle for three weeks of gruelling challenges.

On the line-up is Olympian and reality star and transgender icon Caitlyn Jenner, former Girls Aloud star Nadine Coyle, former EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa, Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway, Radio presenter Roman Kemp, former Big Brother star Adele Roberts, comedian Andrew Maxwell, Rak-Su singer Myles Stephenson, former rugby player James Haskell, and former footballer Ian Wright.

There are also a few rumoured late additions.
