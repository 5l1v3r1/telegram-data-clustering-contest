Tyga spotted riding with mystery woman who looks very similar to ex Kylie Jenner

17 Nov 2019, 18:34

Tyga painted the town red in his Ferrari after a hot dinner date last night, but it’s his mysterious lady friend who’s got everyone talking.

The pair were spotted sheepishly leaving Delilah restaurant in West Hollywood.

Dressed in a Louis Vuitton jumper and green headband, Tyga drove his date out of the restaurant’s parking lot as she kept her head down.

She appears to be wearing a nude-coloured top with blue jeans, but nothing else is yet known about the mystery woman… besides the fact that she bears a striking resemblance to Tyga’s ex, Kylie Jenner.

Don’t you think?

The 29-year-old American rapper sparked rumours he was seeing Kylie again after the two were spotted together in a late night rendezvous straight after she announced her split with Travis Scott.

In photos obtained by the Mail Online, the mother-of-one is seen outside the Sunset Marquis where Tyga was reportedly laying down tracks in the hotel’s recording studio.

Kylie was accompanied by her friends Anastasia ‘Stassi’ Karanikolaou and Kelsey Calemine, whom she had earlier partied with at the West Hollywood club Delilah until 2am.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star is reported to have left at around 3am – Stassi and Kelsey left via the same exit as Tyga but Kylie was able to avoid the cameras when she made her way home.

It’s not the first time Kylie and Tyga, 29, have bumped into each other since their split two years ago.

They were spotted in the same club together last month and partied together in Las Vegas.
