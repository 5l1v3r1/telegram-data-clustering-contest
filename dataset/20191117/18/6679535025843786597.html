Fire breaks out at vada pav shop

17 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Special Correspondent

An accidental fire that broke out at a vada pav shop near Shani Mandir in Belagavi caused panic among residents here on Sunday.

Onlookers complained to the police that cooking gas appeared to have leaked from a cylinder that the shop owner was using, thereby causing the fire.

One person suffered minor burns in the incident. A case has been registered.
