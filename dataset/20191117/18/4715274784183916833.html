Hong Kong MX returns to PH

17 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

After a short visit here in the country during the mid-autumn festival, Hong Kong MX returns to offer its well-loved pastries. 

The brand’s bestselling Eggrolls and Pastries Trio Deluxe that consists of Butter Cookies, Macadamia Nut Cookies, Hazelnut Cookies, Hearty Butter Pastry Original and Maple Syrup, Almond Mille-feuille, and Hazelnut Mille-feuille will be available at its pop-up stores in SM Mall of Asia in Pasay and Lucky Chinatown in Binondo, Manila. 

Both pop-ups will open on Nov. 23 until Jan. 26, 2020.
