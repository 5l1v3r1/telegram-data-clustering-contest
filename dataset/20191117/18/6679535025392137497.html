Farmers asked to insure paddy crops

17 Nov 2019, 18:54 by Staff Reporter

Since crop loss due to unforeseen circumstances is compensated, farmers have been asked to apply for crop insurance for the samba paddy crop under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (Prime Minister’s Crop Insurance Scheme) in the district.

Successful implementation

In a press release, K. Premalatha, Joint Director of Agriculture, said the scheme introduced in 2016 by the Centre had been successfully implemented in the district, encouraging more farmers to pay premium.

She said that currently paddy was being cultivated in about 63,000 acre in the district that were irrigated by Lower Bhavani Project canal, Thadapalli and Arakankottai canals, Mettur East Bank Canal and Kalingarayan Canal.

Information related to insurance had been disseminated to farmers in 192 revenue villages in the district and farmers can contact Assistant Directors of Agriculture at the following numbers: Ammapettai 70100-09141, Anthiyur 99526-97911, Bhavani 97885-19522, Bhavani Sagar 99408-1830, Chennimalai 94438-65485, Erode 99449-10101, Gobichettipalayam 94438-52710, Kodumudi 94885-76435, Modakurichi 99764-80379, Nambiyur 79048-93500, Perundurai 99764-95153, Sathyamangalam 94430-62463 and Thuckanaickenpalayam 80725-77227.

Premium

She said that Oriental Insurance Company had been permitted to collect a premium of ₹ 478.50 per acre from the farmers and the coverage was ₹ 31,800.

The last date for submitting application was November 30.
