Three held, drugs seized

17 Nov 2019, 18:39 by Staff Reporter

Sleuths from the Narcotics Wing of the CCB have busted a gang of inter-State drug peddlers and seized 1.2 kg hashish oil, 2.2 kg ganja, 12 g MDMA, and a weighing scale, together worth ₹10 lakh from them.

The gang members, who were hailing from Kerala, were sourcing narcotics from Visakhapatnam and selling them to college students in the city, the police said. The police identified the arrested as Shakeer V.V., 32, Nazeer T.C., 30, and M. Mohammed Ziad, 26, all residents of Mangammanapalya.
