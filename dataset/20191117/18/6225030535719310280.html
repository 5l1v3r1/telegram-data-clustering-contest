Queen Bee to You wins Betty Grable to cap emotional week for Lerner

17 Nov 2019, 18:19 by Victor Ryan

Trainer Andrew Lerner enjoyed an emotional win Sunday when Queen Bee to You drew off to win the $100,000 Betty Grable Stakes for California-bred distaffers at Del Mar.

The second career stakes win for Queen Bee to You comes exactly one week after Lerner endured the lows of the sport when stablemate Princess Dorian suffered a serious leg injury at Del Mar.

"Just happy she made it around the track safely more than anything with everything that's going on," Lerner told TVG immediately after the race. "What a great win here. Flavien [Prat] rode her perfectly. Team did a great job. Just a lot of emotion with everything that happened."

Queen Bee to You stalked the pace while racing three-wide behind fractions of :22.82 and :45.33 set by Creative Instinct with odds-on favorite Mo See Cal racing between those horses in second.

Prat was full of horse entering the stretch as Queen Bee to You surged to the front and then drew off to win by 4 1/2 lengths. Show it N Moe It was second and was followed by Mo See Cal, who came up empty in her first start since the Breeders' Cup Distaff.

Queen Bee to You completed seven furlongs in 1:22.90 and paid $8.00 while providing Lerner with his first Del Mar stakes win.

"When I asked her on the turn, she really went," Prat said. "She’s won at a mile before, but I think seven eighths is just right for her.”
