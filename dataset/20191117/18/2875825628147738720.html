Charlotte Crosby bragged about relationship with Josh Ritchie just days before split

Charlotte Crosby confirmed she's broken up with Josh Ritchie in a rather surprising statement on Instagram

17 Nov 2019, 18:49 by Irishmirror.ie

Charlotte Crosby has confirmed she's broken up with Josh Ritchie in a surprising statement she shared on social media.

The news will come as a shock to fans as the former Geordie Shore star, 29, had bragged about how happy they were just days earlier.

They moved in together in August, with Charlotte leaving the Toon to be with Josh in Bolton - and she'd only just said how good the big step in their two-year relationship had been for them.

Charlotte told Mirror Online that the move had brought them closer together and they hadn't been rowing at all.

She said: "We have learnt a lot more [since moving in together] and it's been really, really good. We're getting on so well, I think it's brought us closer together.

"We've had no arguments about cleaning and stuff, he's so tidy, always doing the washing, it's really been perfect."

Charlotte was speaking to us to plug the new series of Just Tattoo of Us.

Awkwardly, Josh will be making several appearances in the MTV show with her.

Charlotte announced the breakup on her Instagram stories on Sunday evening.

She said their relationship had "broken down" but hoped they could "remain civil" after calling it a day.

Charlotte wrote: "Me and Josh have decided to part ways. Sadly the relationship seems to have broken drown.

"Hoping we can still remain civil and wish him the best for the future."

Josh is yet to comment on their breakup on his own Instagram account.
