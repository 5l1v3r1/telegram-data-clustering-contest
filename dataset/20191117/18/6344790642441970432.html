JUMP FORCE Releases Final Trailer for the Latest DLC Launching Next Week

17 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Tommy Williams

Next week, the new DLC for Jump Force will release, and we’ve got a cool new trailer that briefly shows off Toshiro Hitsugaya and Madara Uchiha. In addition to two new fighters, a new stage is coming to the game: Whole Cake Island! That’s three anime adding parts to the game on one day. The DLC will be available starting on November 19, or some of you may have gotten the early access which gives you access to the new content today.

The next batch of DLC for Jump Force will feature Grimmjow Jaegerjaquez and Trafalgar Law.
