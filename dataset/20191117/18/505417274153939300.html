Finn Russell Creates & Finishes Amazing Try In Win Over Champions

17 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Michael Corry

Racing 92 rounded off the opening round of the Heineken Champions Cup in style with a 30-10 victory over Saracens in Paris.

It was all one-way traffic from the get-go as the French outfit dominated a largely second-string Saracens from start to finish.

Virimi Vakatawa got Racing off the mark with their first try after nine minutes before Maxime Machenaud added to his earlier penalty to make it 10-0 after 10 minutes.
