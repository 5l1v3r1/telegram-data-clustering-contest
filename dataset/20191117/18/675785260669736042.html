Best ps4 games of 2019 so far

Game, set, match. Unleash your inner gamer with these top rated picks of the best Playstation 4 games of the year, so far

17 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Brijiena Lovelace, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/brijiena-lovelace/

As it's officially 'stay at home unless absolutely necessary' weather, we hear all of your inner gamers rejoicing at longer time in front of your consoles.

The Playstation 4 is a powerful machine and one of the most advanced consoles to date, other than the slightly newer model the PS4 pro.

Like many gaming fans, the PS5 in making a much anticipated arrival next year; but for now, most are quite contented with the performance of the PS4.

If you're ready to stock up your winter game collection, we've rounded up 10 of the best games of 2019 so far, that you can buy now.

Best PS4 games of 2019  

1. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare

There's no wonder one of the nations favourite console games, Call of Duty made it to our list.

Enjoy exclusive bonus content, this edition includes a themed in-game emblem, calling card and weapon charm.

If you like to enjoy the game with someone else, you can take part in white-knuckle 2v2 matches, 6v6 combat and epic 32v32 ground war matches - sure to be an epic battle. You can also play as a group in a collection of elite operations.

Price: £49.99, Amazon - buy here now

2. FIFA 20

If you're more of an off the pitch player, the new FIFA brings to life the magical game of football in stunning realism.

Experience the feeling of professional football, from the comfort of your own home. Choose from an array of club modes and competitions - build your characters and pick your gear, all you have to do is win the game.

Price: £49.99, Very - buy here now

3. Death Stranding

Maybe sports games aren't your thing, if you're more intrigued by a gameplay experience that involves 'killing the bad guys' - you'll love death stranding.

With supernatural creatures plaguing the earth and humanity on the verge of a mass extinction, it's up to Sam (you) to journey across through the danger lands to save mankind from perishing. You'll uncover a world of secrets along the way, but you'll need to play first to find them out.

Price: £49.99, Argos - buy here now

4. Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

Star Wars fans rejoice, this single player game is narrative-driven and puts you in the role of a Jedi Padawan.

During the game you'll complete your Jedi training, develop powerful force abilities and of course, master the art of the iconic lightsabre.

Enjoy iconic characters, locations, weapons and enemies in addition to a roster of new ones to become familiar with. 

Price: £49.99, Amazon - buy here now

5. Detroit Become Human

One of the cheaper options on the gaming market at the moment, but not without an amazing narrative and stunning gameplay experience you're bound to love.

Explore the near-future metropolis of Detroit, which is now bustling with technological developments; robots to be clear. You'll take on the role as Kara - a female bot trying to navigate her place in a turbulent landscape.

Discover what it means to be human from the perspective of a machine.

Price: £19.99, Selfridges - buy here now

 

6. Need For Speed Heat

Bringing back a much love classic, with a modern twsit. If your need for speed never left you - you'll love the heat.

With even more options to customise and personalise your rides, you can dare to be expressive and build your racing reputation.

Drift, drag and race your way to success.

Price: £45.98, Amazon - buy here now

7. Spyro Trilogy Reignited

One the kids can get involved with, if you'll let them. Another classic, indulge your childhood nostalgia with everyone's favourite purple dragon, Spyro.

Rekindle the fire with remastered versions of the original three games, Spyro the Dragon, Spyro 2 Ripto's Rage! and Spyro Year of the Dragon.   

Price: £22.99, Amazon - buy here now

8. The Outer Worlds

Sci-fi fans, this one's for you, enjoy this first person journey through a world unknown. 

You awaken decades after becoming lost in transit on a colonist ship flying through the galaxy, you’ll encounter various factions all vying for power. Take sides, find a ship, engage and build your crew as you explore a vast range of environments.

The story is completely driven by your actions so the stories development is completely down to your choices.

Price: £42.99, Very - buy here now

9. NBA 2K20

If you're more of a basketball fan, bring the NBA to your home and experience the excitement of the game in perfect quality.

The game scores best in class for the stunning graphics and hyper realistic gameplay. Enjoy ground breaking game modes and unparalleled player control and full customisation. 

Price: £44.99, Amazon - buy here now

10. MXGP 2019

Experience motocross like never before. The new track editor allows you to bring to life the track of your dreams and share it with your friends and the motocross community!

Train your riding skills and earn special points for difficulties, air ability and speed. With over 10,000 customisable options for your bike and rider you can rally make this game your own.

A brand-new Waypoint mode will bring the competition to another level. 

Price: £29.99, Amazon - buy here now
