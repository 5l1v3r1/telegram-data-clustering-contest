Horrified family receive Iceland pizza 'covered in black mould'

17 Nov 2019, 18:01

A family has been ‘put off’ shopping at Iceland again after receiving a pizza covered in black mould.

Kim Bann says her niece ordered the Iceland own-brand Deep Pan Double Pepperoni pizza from the Selsdon branch last Friday.

However, Kim got a shock when she found ‘disgusting’ black mould ‘all the way round it’ when she opened it for her daughter’s lunch.

Kim said: ‘This is meant to be a double pepperoni pizza but it’s all black mould all the way round it

‘Absolutely disgusting.’

The family claim to have been offered a refund for the pizza which normally costs £1.

However, they say this is ‘appalling’ customer service, given the shock they experienced.

Kim said: ‘I would have thought that they would least give her a voucher to coax her back to shopping there.

‘It’s actually put her off shopping there now as well as me.’

Iceland confirmed to The Sun they had been contacted on social media by Kim and her niece.

However, they said they were waiting for the account holder to speak to them directly so they can investigate further.

A spokesperson said: ‘At Iceland, we pride ourselves on both the quality of our food and the quality of our service. On this occasion, our customer has clearly not received this usual high standard of experience.

‘We are very keen to look into this matter further and to ensure that this customer feels valued and understands that she is important she is to Iceland.’

Metro.co.uk has contacted Iceland for further comment.
