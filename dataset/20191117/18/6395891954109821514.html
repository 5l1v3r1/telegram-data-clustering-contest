Teen caught speeding 126 miles per hour in New Hampshire

17 Nov 2019, 18:28 by EndPlay

GREENLAND, N.H. - A 19-year-old was clocked driving 126 miles per hour on Sunday morning in Greenland, New Hampshire.

He told an officer he was late for work.

Related Headlines

Alexey Kidney, of York, Maine, was driving at 7:30 a.m. on 95 south when a New Hampshire State Police officer patrolling from the air noticed the speeding car.

The trooper radioed to a trooper on the ground, who stopped Kidney in the 65 mile per hour speed zone. 

Kidney was arrested and charged with reckless operation. He's scheduled to appear in the 10th Circuit Portsmouth District Court on Jan. 27, 2020.
