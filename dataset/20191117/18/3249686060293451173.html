Kim Jaejoong will sing the ending theme song for the anime ´Smile Down the Runway´

17 Nov 2019, 18:10 by soum

Kim Jaejoong succesfully completed the second day of ‘’J-JUN LIVE 2019 ~ Love Covers ~” concert which was aired today live on MTV Japan. Kim Jaejoong announced during the concert that he will be singing the ending theme song < Ray of Light > for the anime ‘Smile down the Runway’. The anime is scheduled to air in January 10, 2020 on the Animeism programming block on MBS, TBS, and BS-TBS.

Translation: TV Anime “#ランウェイで笑って” (Smile Down the Runway) announcement

#ジェジュン (Jaejoong) an artist who’s popular in all Asian countries, will sing the ending theme!
Jaejoong: ''This is a ballad which gives hope to people around the world who strive to fulfill their dreams. Please listen!'
