Don’t be deceived; Wee does not cause MADNESS – Ras Kuuku

17 Nov 2019, 07:51 by MyNewsGH

Reggae artiste, Ras Kuuku says cannabis do not cause madness as some persons have been deceived to believe.

He said while speaking to Dj Adviser on Accra-based Happy FM that “the European’s wisdom is equal to that of the African. But how come they’ve legalised weed and mental illness has not increased because of the use of ‘wee’.

This is a misconception we need to clear. ‘Wee’ doesn’t cause mental illness.”

Ras Kuuku appealed to government to desist from arresting the youth who smoke the herb but rather find a way to regulate its use.
