What channel is Kosovo vs England on? TV and live stream information

England have already made it to Euro 2020 so Sunday's clash with Kosovo is something of a dead rubber, given the hosts cannot make it

17 Nov 2019, 07:20 by Irishmirror.ie

Gareth Southgate will likely not rotate his squad too significantly as England travel to Kosovo.

The Three Lions have booked their place at Euro 2020 on the back of their 7-0 win over Montenegro on Thursday night.

It opens up the chance for England to give opportunities to the likes of James Maddison and Tammy Abraham, but Southgate is expected to stick with one of his strongest possible teams.

England have almost definitely topped the group, as they hold a three point lead over the Czech Republic, with the better head-to-head and also a remarkably bigger goal difference.

Here is the key information ahead of the game...

When is the match?

The match takes place on Sunday November 17.

Kick-off is scheduled for 5pm GMT for those in the UK.

Where is the match being played?

The Fadil Vokrri Stadium in Pristina is hosting the match.

Around 900 England fans will be in attendance.

What TV channel is it on?

If you are in the UK, the match is being shown live on ITV 1.

Can you live stream the match?

Yes, providing you are in the UK.

The match is available on the ITV website, on the ITV Hub.
