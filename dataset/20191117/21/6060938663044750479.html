Shraddha Kapoor explores Serbia while shooting her next 'Baaghi 3'

17 Nov 2019, 21:32 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After delivering two back to back hits, Shraddha Kapoor is currently shooting for the third installment of 'Baaghi' with Tiger Shroff in Serbia. The actress has been sharing pictures and videos from the sets on her social media accounts.

And now, she took to her Instagram to posts some pictures and videos as she explored the capital of Serbia, Belgrade. She captioned her post, "Never. Stop. Exploring. ~ Anonymous". From scenic views to historical monuments, her post had it all.

With 'Baaghi 3', Shraddha will be reuniting Tiger Shroff after 'Baaghi'. Helmed by Ahmed Khan, the released date of the action flick is not yet announced.

Shraddha will be next seen in Remo D'Souza's 'Street Dancer 3D' co-starring Varun Dhawan. The film has been slated for Summer 2020 release. She was last seen in Prabhas starrer 'Saaho' and Nitesh Tiwari's 'Chhichhore' alongside Sushant Singh Rajput.
