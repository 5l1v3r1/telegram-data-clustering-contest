Video: Redskins Fans Send Message To Owner Daniel Snyder

17 Nov 2019, 21:31 by Tzvi Machlin

The Washington Redskins are in the waning minutes of a blowout loss to the New York Jets. At 1-8 on the season – soon to be 1-9 – the team has long-since been knocked out of any realistic playoff contention.

But after firing head coach Jay Gruden earlier this year, the Washington Redskins fans have turned their anger towards the man at the top: Owner Daniel Snyder.

In the middle of the game, with Washington struggling mightily on offense, fans began chanting “Sell the team!”
