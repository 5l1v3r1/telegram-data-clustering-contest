Ant and Dec address I'm a Celebrity reunion with outrageous joke

The pair had not been in the jungle together since 2017

17 Nov 2019, 21:15 by Anna Lewis

Ant and Dec broke the ice in signature style for their I'm a Celebrity reunion tonight by making a dig at Prince Andrew's Newsnight interview.

The launch of the series marked the first time the duo have been in the jungle together since 2017.

Last year, Dec was forced to team up with Holly Willoughby to present the popular series after Ant took time away to deal with a drink and prescription-drug addiction.

But he made his return Down Under in a rather casual fashion.

During Sunday's episode Ant opened the show by acknowledging: "We're back, I'm back, he's back, we're all back."

Seconds later the duo also went on to make a dig at Prince Andrew's Newsnight interview by suggesting the series will make the celebrities sweat - if they can.

He said: "And what a line up we've got in store for you this year.

"Ten celebrities all of them very nervous, all of them sweating."

Before Dec added: "Presuming they can sweat, not everyone can sweat apparently."

The reference comes after the Duke of York contradicted claims he had been sweating while dancing with teenager Virginia Giuffre in Tramp nightclub - by saying a medical condition means he can't sweat.

Ant's return to I'm a Celebrity comes nearly a year after viewers at home saw his emotional return to TV during the audition stage of Britain's Got Talent.

In January, stepping onto the stage, he said: "Hello everyone. Oh god, I’m really really emotional now. I’ve missed you all. As for you (pointing at Dec) I’ve missed you. Thank you very much, what a warm welcome. What a great show to come back to, I’m thrilled, I’m over the moon. Shall we move on before I cry?"

In April this year, Ant was fined £86,000 and banned from driving after being caught more than double the legal drink driving limit.

The 44-year-old had crashed into two others cars on the other side of the road in Richmond, London, causing one child to be taken to hospital to be checked for injury.

Back in November, last year Dec addressed Ant's absence with a gentle ice breaker.

He said: "Good morning, good evening, welcome to Australia, where the more eagle-eyed viewers among you will have already noticed a big change to this year's show.

"Yes, one of the most gorgeous presenters on British television ... has been joined by Holly Willoughby."

The gentle ice-breaker was greeted with cheers from the production crew, applause, along with a curtsy and wave from Holly.

This year, the full celebrity line up includes the likes of Caitlyn Jenner and former footballer Ian Wright.

But for the first time, contestants will be banned from eating live bugs in Bushtucker trials, the Mirror reports.
