Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Athiya Shetty's 'Motichoor Chaknachoor' leaked online

17 Nov 2019, 21:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Athiya Shetty's 'Motichoor Chaknachoor' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. The film team left no stones unturned to promote their flick on various platforms.

And on Friday, the film has hit the theatres, however, unfortunately, it became the latest victim of piracy as it got leaked online.

According to reports making rounds on the internet, the infamous TamilRockers are responsible for leaking the film online. Several films like 'Marjaavaan', 'Dream Girl', 'Bharat', 'Kabir Singh', 'Avengers Endgame' and 'MIB: International' were made available on the internet by the piracy site.

Reportedly, the web portal keeps changing the domain extension in order to keep running the site illegally. Meanwhile, 'Motichoor Chaknachoor' is helmed by Debamitra Biswal the film was released on November 15.
