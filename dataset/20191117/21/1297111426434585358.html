Police in Regina say a 66-year-old man is dead after he was struck by a vehicle Friday night in Regina.

17 Nov 2019, 21:02 by The Canadian Press

Police in Regina say a 66-year-old man is dead after he was struck by a vehicle Friday night in Regina.

Investigators say the man was crossing at the intersection of Dewdney Avenue and Robinson Street just after 9:30 p.m. when he was hit.

They say he was rushed to hospital where his death was confirmed.
