I'm A Celeb's Ant jokes about 'year off' as he's reunited with Dec after drink-drive shame

Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly were finally back together on I'm A Celeb this evening after two years

17 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin joked about his "year off" as he was reunited with Declan Donnelly on I'm A Celebrity tonight.

The double act was back and stronger than ever this evening as a whole new host of scared celebrities ventured into the jungle.

However the pair couldn't resist a joke at Ant's drink-drive shame last year.

Introducing the programme tonight, Dec announced: "We're back, he's back, we're all back, back back!"

After a series of hilarious gags at Prince Andrew's expense, they said it was down to the public to decide who wins and is crowned queen or king of the jungle.

Ant said: “I'm A Celebrity 2019 can only have one winner.”

Dec said: “Caitlyn Jenner's agent by the sound of it.”

Ant added: “OK so two winners. Time to meet our first celeb, someone who always does the right thing, is a right laugh and is made of the right stuff. It's...”

Dec interupted by joking: “It's Kate Garraway.”

Ant said: “It's Ian Wright. I have had a year off, what is your excuse?"

Ant was forced to pull out of the series in 2018 and was replaced by Holly Willoughby following his drink-drive shame earlier that year.

The 43-year-old decided to take a break from his TV presenting duties to focus on his recovery.

In a statement, he said: "My recovery is going very well and for that to continue having spoken to Dec and ITV, I have made the decision to take the rest of the year off."

Ant returned to his presenting duties in January 2019, just after Holly filled in for him in Australia.

This year's I'm A Celebrity line-up includes the likes of reality TV Caitlyn Jenner , footballer Ian Wright , Girls Aloud star Nadine Coyle , and former EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa .

However former host Holly won't be tuning in for this evening's episode.

Instead, she will be skipping a night in front of the TV with the rest of the nation to attend the premiere of Frozen 2 in London on Sunday night.

Holly, 38, is more than just a fan of the ITV reality show as she was brought in to replace Ant McPartlin when he took a year out of I'm A Celebrity.

A self-confessed Disney fan, Holly made sure she didn't miss out on being one of the first to see the highly anticipated sequel.
