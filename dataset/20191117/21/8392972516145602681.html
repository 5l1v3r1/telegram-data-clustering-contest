I'm A Celebrity fans 'teary' as Ant McPartlin makes his return

He's back!

17 Nov 2019, 21:31 by Carena Crawford

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! were thrilled as Ant and Dec were reunited on this year's show.

Ant took a year off after being arrested for drink driving and going to rehab, so he missed presenting last year's series.

Holly Willoughby made the trip to Australia alongside Dec Donnelly to present in Ant's absence.

But depsite the fact she was hit with fans, it was made very clear it was just temporary and Ant would be returning.

And return he has.
