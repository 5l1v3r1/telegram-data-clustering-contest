IU reveals M/V teaser for 'Blueming'

17 Nov 2019, 21:19 by olmal

IU has dropped a M/V teaser for her upcoming release 'Blueming', just a day before the full release of her 5th mini album 'Love Poem'. The long-awaited comeback album is set for release on November 18 at 6 PM KST.
