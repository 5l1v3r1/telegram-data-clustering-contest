Video: Charles Oliveira knocks out Jared Gordon in first round at UFC Sao Paulo

17 Nov 2019, 02:40 by Steven Marrocco

Charles Oliveira, known as the most successful submission artist in UFC history, has now knocked out his past two opponents.

Oliveira starched Jared Gordon in the co-headliner of UFC Sao Paulo on Saturday, extending his current winning streak to six fights and adding another piece of highlight-reel footage to his decorated career.

Gordon walked into a right hand one minute after the featured lightweight bout, and Oliveira finished the American off with more punches to force referee intervention at the 1:26 mark of the opening frame.
