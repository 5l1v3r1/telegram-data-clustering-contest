M Partners Weighs in on Home Capital Group Inc’s FY2019 Earnings (TSE:HCG)

17 Nov 2019, 01:43 by Amy Steele

Home Capital Group Inc (TSE:HCG) – Equities research analysts at M Partners increased their FY2019 earnings per share estimates for shares of Home Capital Group in a research report issued to clients and investors on Wednesday, November 13th. M Partners analyst A. Hood now forecasts that the company will earn $2.63 per share for the year, up from their prior estimate of $2.25. M Partners also issued estimates for Home Capital Group’s FY2020 earnings at $3.55 EPS.

A number of other analysts have also commented on the company. BMO Capital Markets increased their price objective on Home Capital Group from C$23.00 to C$25.00 in a research note on Thursday, August 8th. National Bank Financial increased their price objective on Home Capital Group from C$29.00 to C$35.00 and gave the company a “sector perform” rating in a research note on Thursday. Royal Bank of Canada increased their price objective on Home Capital Group from C$31.00 to C$37.00 and gave the company an “outperform” rating in a research note on Thursday. TD Securities increased their price objective on Home Capital Group from C$28.00 to C$35.00 and gave the company a “hold” rating in a research note on Thursday. Finally, CIBC increased their price objective on Home Capital Group from C$23.00 to C$25.00 in a research note on Thursday, August 8th. Four investment analysts have rated the stock with a hold rating and two have issued a buy rating to the company. The stock presently has a consensus rating of “Hold” and a consensus price target of C$31.83.

HCG opened at C$35.20 on Friday. The firm has a market cap of $2.00 billion and a P/E ratio of 17.00. Home Capital Group has a 52 week low of C$13.34 and a 52 week high of C$35.49. The business’s fifty day simple moving average is C$27.10 and its 200 day simple moving average is C$22.85.

About Home Capital Group

Home Capital Group Inc, through its subsidiary, Home Trust Company, provides deposit, mortgage lending, retail credit, and credit card issuing services in Canada. It offers various deposit products, such as savings accounts; and single-family residential and insured residential lending, as well as residential and non-residential commercial mortgage lending services.
