Kirby Smart Reveals What He Told His Players After Win vs. Auburn

17 Nov 2019, 01:25 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, No. 12 Auburn hosted No. 4 Georgia in a huge SEC showdown with College Football Playoff implications.

After a slow start to the game, Georgia’s offense finally got the ball rolling. The Bulldogs took a 21-0 lead into the fourth quarter and looked to be running away with the game.

However, the Tigers stormed back in the fourth quarter and cut the Bulldogs’ lead to just seven points late in the fourth quarter. Their comeback attempt fell just short and Georgia survived with a 21-14 win.

After the game, Georgia head coach Kirby Smart delivered a message to his team.

“I told them: How bout those f***in’ Dawgs,” Smart said.

Immediately after the win, Smart tried to celebrate with one of his players, but it didn’t go too well. Smart played college ball back in the day, but he’s no match for defensive lineman Travon Walker.
