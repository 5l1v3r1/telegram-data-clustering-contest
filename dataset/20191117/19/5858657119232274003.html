Family dog dies in Chilliwack house fire

17 Nov 2019, 19:01 by Jonathan Szekeres

Summary

Chilliwack house fire breaks out Saturday night

Family dog dies in fire due to heavy smoke

The cause of the fire is under investigation, but the Fire Department says it looks like this was accidental

CHILLIWACK (NEWS 1130) – A house fire in Chilliwack Saturday night has taken the life of a family dog.

The fire broke our near Ashwell Road and Jimmie Road around 7:15 p.m. while the family wasn’t home.

Unfortunately the family dog did not survive the heavy smoke.

Once fire crews arrived on the scene, crews saw flames and heavy smoke coming from the roof of the house.

No one was hurt in the fire.

The cause of the fire is under investigation, but the Fire Department says it looks like this was accidental.
