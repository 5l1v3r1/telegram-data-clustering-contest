Roy Keane says Marcus Rashford is 'back to his best' as fine form continues

17 Nov 2019, 19:30

Roy Keane believes Marcus Rashford is back to his best as his superb run of goal-scoring form continued in England’s 4-0 win over Kosovo.

The Manchester United forward produced a fantastic finish to net England’s third goal on Sunday night, having also scored in the 7-0 win over Montenegro on Thursday.

The 22-year-old has now scored in each of his last four games for club and country, and nine in his last 10 matches.

The former Red Devils captain was full of praise for the forward after his cool finish in Pristina for the Three Lions.

‘Brilliant, brilliant goal. What a finish,’ Keane said on ITV. ‘He is back to his best. Lovely play, nice and simple.
