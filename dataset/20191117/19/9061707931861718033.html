These are the Burton roads set to be hit by roadworks

Make sure you take note of where they are

17 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Ben Waldron

Burton roads are set to be hit by a number of sets of roadworks within the coming weeks.

Many well-used roads in the town will be impacted, including Derby Road, Branston Road, Evershed Way and Shobnall Road as work is carried out and the temporary lights will be in place.

Drivers are being warned to make a note of where they will be as they are about to be rolled out on roads in their area as they will impact their commute to and from work.

Nothing can quite ruin a good mood in the morning like the sudden appearance of roadworks when you are not expecting it.

All of the following cases of roadworks will happen within the next two weeks, according to monitoring website one.network .

We have done the hard work for you and collected a list of all the roadworks coming up from now until Friday, November 29.

Upcoming roadworks in Burton

  1. Newton Road

    Where: Near Burton Canoe Club.

    When: From Friday, November 22 until Thursday, November 28.

    For: Works on the road system.

  2. Derby Road

    Where: Near Tile Choice.

    When: Monday, November 18 until Friday, November 22.

    For: Lay new pipes.

  3. Moor Street

    Where: Near the junction of Moor Street and Mosley Street

    When: Friday, November 15 to Tuesday, November 19.

    For: Works on the road system.

  4. Parkway

    Where: On one exit of the roundabout near McDonald's.

    When: Monday, November 4 to Friday, December 6.

    For: Left hand filter lane up to the main roundabout by McDonald's. This is for the installation of a rising main sewer section in the footpath.

  5. Wellington Road

    Where: On the entrance to the roundabout near Morrison's.

    When: Until Tuesday, December 3.

    For: To lay a new cable.

  6. Branston Road

    When: Until New Year's Eve.

    Where: On the approach to the roundabout with Orchard Street.

    For: Laying cables in the footpath and road.

    There are further works in Branston Road, with one set of traffic lights near the junction with All Saints Road until Tuesday, December 3, and a second set near Queen Street, also until December 3.

  7. Derby Street

    When: Tuesday, November 19.

    Where: Near The Alfred pub.

    For: To replace a sunken manhole cover.

  8. Watson Street

    When: Until Wednesday, July 1, 2020.

    Where: Towards the end of Watson Street, near the entrance for the Washlands.

    For: Flood defence improvement works.

  9. Evershed Way

    When: Until Thursday, December 5.

    Where: On the roundabout with Anglesey Road.

    For: Laying cables in the footpath and road.

  10. Ashby Road

    When: From Friday, November 15 to Thursday, November 21.

    Where: Near the junction with Mervyn Road.

    For: To lay a new gas service.

  11. Shobnall Road

    When: Monday, November 25 to Wednesday, November 27.

    Where: Near The Albion pub.

    For: Maintaining flood monitoring equipment in the water system.

  12. Burton Road

    When: Thursday, November 28 and Friday, November 29.

    Where: Near junction with Jephson Road.

    For: For highway works.

  13. Stanton Road

    When: Sunday, November 24 until Wednesday, November 27.

    Where: Near Saxon Street.

    For: To complete road maintenance.
