What happened between I’m a Celebrity’s Myles Stephenson and Gabby Allen?

17 Nov 2019, 19:40

When reality TV worlds collide, the results aren’t always pretty.

Just ask Love Island’s Gabby Allen and The X Factor’s (soon to be I’m a Celebrity’s) Myles Stephenson.

The two went from love struck to love sucks after a year of dating.

How did the two meet, how long were they together for and when did the relationship end?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who is Myles Stephenson?

Myles is one of the stars appearing in I’m a Celebrity 2019.

He first found fame on The X Factor, where he and his band Rak-Su won the competition in 2017.

However, the band were dropped by their label in August 2019 due to disappointing sales.

Who is Gabby Allen?

Fitness enthusiast with a massive social media following, Gabby Allen appeared on Love Island 2017 as well as finishing sixth in Celebrity Big Brother 2018.

When did Gabby and Myles meet?

The couple first started dating back in September 2018 but ended their romance in mid-August 2019.

Myles, 28, had moved into Gabby’s North London flat at the start of 2019, however, things became complicated when she became suspicious of what he was up to.

Why did Gabby and Myles break up?

Gabby, 27, posted a message to her 1.1m Instagram followers confirming her split with the Rak-Su singer.

Her post read: ‘The newspaper articles are true. I am no longer in a relationship. I hope youse (sic) have all had a better week than me! If anyone else has anything they need to tell me, please do.’

Although no official reason was given for the break-up it was reported that Gabby had ended things after she caught Myles messaging other girls on Instagram.

It’s not all bad news for Myles though after a psychic has predicted him being crowned King of the Jungle.

Myles seems to be focusing on his next career moves and not mourning his relationship too much.

Speaking ahead of the I’m a Celebrity 2019 launch, he revealed that he’d take career chances that might mean leaving his band Rak-Su if they came his way.

He said: ‘They [Rak-Su bandmates] are really supportive and they totally understand. I would love to stay in the band but if other opportunities come along my way as well after this, you have to take it by the scruff of the neck.’
