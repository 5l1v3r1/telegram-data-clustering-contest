Banggood offers the best bang for your buck. To bring products across various categories at unbeatable prices, the Chinese e-tailer comes up with amazing promotions, events, and activities from time to time.

With Black Friday sales around the corner, online stores gearing up to offer their highly promoted sales, but Banggood has launched a new promotion sales before the Black Friday sale kicks off. As part of its latest activity, Banggood is giving its loyal users a chance to earn big bonuses and win amazing prizes.

How to participate

To take part in the activity, you need to either create your own team or join an existing team. Your next goal is to earn stars, which requires you to do tasks. By earning enough stars, your team can qualify for lucky draw rounds and join the Black Friday $1,000,000 Carve Up. To stand a chance of getting more prizes and bonuses, your team should earn more stars.

On the event page, click “Create Team” and invite your friends to join it. Alternatively, you can join a friend’s team by telling them to send you their invitation link, and then following it. The  DS/WS users are not allowed to join this activity.

Tasks

The tasks start from November 12th 16:00 and end on November 26th at 14:00 (UTC+8). All tasks will be updated at 0 o’clock (UTC+8). The New Shipped Order tasks, which gives you 50+ stars, on the other hand, will be available once per day.

Between November 12th 16:00 to November 26th 14:00 (UTC+8), there will be Daily Mystery Tasks, which will be available for 24 hours. You can complete the task within 24 hours to get the star reward. Aside from that, you can earn stars by inviting your friends to like your team every day.

For getting 5 Likes, you get +50 stars, for 10 Likes you get +100 stars, 15 Likes will get you +150 stars. You can get up to 30 LIkes and win +300 stars every day. Note that while you can like other teams, you can like your own team. Each user has three likes every day.

Prizes

There are multiple prizes including coupons, stars, and even physical prizes up for the grabs during the activity period (November 21st, 16:00 – Nov 22nd, 16:00 UTC+8).

Carve Up Bonuses

Aside from that, you can win shopping allowances, which can be reused with coupons, FD, Pro, etc,*excludes Snap Up & Group Buy items). Before carve-up, users must confirm their email address. As an individual user, you can double your bonus up to 11 times. Note that Individual base bonus will be carved up Individual base bonus.

Timed Gift Box Draws

To join Timed Gift Box Draws, your individual Stars should reach 50. The task will be valid on November 12, November 15, November 18, November 21, November 23, and November 25 (UTC+8).

Receiving Rewards

Within 15 working days, Banggood will send Physical prizes. Check the “My coupons” section for coupons. Teams and individuals will automatically receive the stars. Shopping allowance will be added to users’ accounts directly and they can be applied while placing an order. The discount will be immediately, and you can further combine it with a coupon, Flash Deal, Unbeatable Deal or other promotional offers.

The promotion page features a slew of amazing products carrying lowered prices. These products have been added to the Best-Selling to ensure you do not waste a lot of time searching for them.

Among other products, the recently unveiled Xiaomi Mi Band 4 fitness band has gone up for sale bearing a reduced price tag of $63.99 under the Best-Selling category. This is an 18 percent reduction in the device’s original asking price.

Aside from getting the discount, new users can take advantage of an $18 OFF New User Coupon and save more money. Moreover, there’s free shipping on orders over $19.99. You can follow this link to participate in the event and stand a chance to win amazing prizes.
