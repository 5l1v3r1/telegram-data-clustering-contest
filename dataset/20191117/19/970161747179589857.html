Who is I'm A Celebrity's Adele Roberts and what has she done since Big Brother?

17 Nov 2019, 19:26

I’m A Celebrity 2019 contestant Adele Roberts is no stranger to reality TV, having appeared in one of the earliest incarnations of the format when she took part in Big Brother 3 in 2002, finishing seventh.

While most participants fade into relative obscurity following their brief television experiences, Roberts has persevered to stay in the limelight, earning her a place as one of ten contestants in the latest series of the ITV show.

Originally from Southport, on the Merseyside coast, Roberts was a DJ and personal assistant before entering the house almost 20 years ago, lasting 43 days of the series in an era when Big Brother reigned supreme and shocked viewers with its largely unprecedented fly-on-the-wall approach.

Like many evicted contestants, Roberts exited the famous house to a hostile atmosphere, having earned more than 60 percent of the public vote to decide whether she should have been ousted from the complex.

Her enduring public profile, though, is testimony to the way Roberts, who was 23 at the time, approached life after the at-times brutal experience of being judged by the nation.

As she prepares to be scrutinised in the more challenging setting of the Australian jungle, let’s take a look at some of her story…

Who is Adele Roberts and when was she on Big Brother?

Although Roberts’ stint as a housemate on Big Brother ended in 2002, she returned to the show briefly in 2007, taking part in a task alongside fellow Big Brother 3 resident Alex Sibley.

She is in a minority of participants to have combined their pre-fame careers with a life in the spotlight after being on reality TV, building on her previous taste for broadcasting to become a long-term DJ on BBC Radio 1.
