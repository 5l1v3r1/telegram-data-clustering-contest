'Cannot deny it' - Aston Villa flop wins first cap after Champions League revival

Aston Villa latest | Pierluigi Gollini was a Roberto Di Matteo signing in 2016 - but he was overtaken at Villa Park by Sam Johnstone later in the 2016-17 season

17 Nov 2019, 19:09 by Josh Murray

Former Aston Villa goalkeeper Pierluigi Gollini has made his senior international debut for Italy.

The 24-year-old - who was signed at Villa by Roberto Di Matteo in 2016 - made a late substitute appearance in his country's 3-0 win over Bosnia & Herzegovina in European Championship qualifying, replacing AC Milan stopper Gianluigi Donnarumma.

The Italians had already booked their place at next summer's finals.

“It was really a fantastic feeling and I cannot deny it reminded me of my Serie A debut,” Gollini told Rai Sport in Zenica.

“I am so happy, even to be there only for a few minutes, for this debut.”
