Broncos’ Andy Janovich Gets Carted To Locker Room After Gruesome Arm/Elbow Injury (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019, 19:33 by Darrelle Lincoln

You know an injury is too bad if CBS decides not to show a replay of it.

During the Broncos-Vikings game on Sunday, FB Andy Janovich caught a pass, got tackled, but his arm seemed to snap in two as he tried to brace for the fall.

He got up instantly and grabbed for that arm. He would eventually get carted off to the locker room.
