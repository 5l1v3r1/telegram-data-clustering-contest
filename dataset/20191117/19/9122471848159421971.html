Photo: Alabama Players Visit Tua Tagovailoa In The Hospital

17 Nov 2019, 19:14 by Tzvi Machlin

Tua Tagovailoa suffered one of the scariest injuries a player can suffer yesterday. He dislocated his hip in a manner that ended the football career of the great Bo Jackson.

But as heartbreaking as it was to see their leader go down, the Alabama players have made it clear how much they care about him.

On Sunday morning, Alabama defensive lineman Phidarian Mathis shared a photo of himself and teammate Raekwon Davis visiting Tagovailoa in the hospital.

Tagovailoa, from his hospital bed, looked to be in good spirits and happy to see his teammates.
