Bayelsa election result: APC floors PDP in Jonathan’s LGA

17 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Chukwudi Akasike

Chukwudi Akasike, Yenagoa

The All Progressives Congress floored the Peoples Democratic Party in Ogbia, the local government area of former President Goodluck Jonathan, in Saturday’s Bayelsa governorship election.

In the results collated so far at the LG, the governorship candidate of the APC, David Lyon, got 58,016 votes to beat his closest rival in PDP, Douye Diri, who scored 13,763 votes.

Before the election on Saturday, Ogbia had always been PDP’s stronghold.
