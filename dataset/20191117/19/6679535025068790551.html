Two killed in different accidents

17 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Special Correspondent

A 28-year-old woman was killed after a mini-van hit a two-wheeler near the toll plaza at Chengalpattu on Sunday morning. The woman, identified as Kanchana, was killed on the spot.

A police official of Chengalpattu Taluk Police station said Kanchana was riding a two-wheeeler when a speeding mini van hit her vehicle near the Paranur toll plaza.

She suffered serious head injuries and was rushed to the Chengalpattu General Hospital where she was pronounced brought dead. The police have filed a case and are investigating.
