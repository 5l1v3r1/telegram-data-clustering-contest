[BREAKING] AFCON 2021 qualifier: Super Eagles beat Lesotho 4-2

17 Nov 2019, 19:53 by Agency Report

Super Eagles came from a goal down on Sunday to demolish Lesotho 4-2 in the African Cup of Nation’s 2021 qualifier played in Maseru, Lesotho.

Lesotho took a lead at 11 minutes through Nkoto, but the Eagles battled back through Alex Iwobi, who equalised for Nigeria in the 26th minute.
