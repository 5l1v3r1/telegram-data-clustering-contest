Harry Styles Debuts “Watermelon Sugar” On ‘Saturday Night Live’

17 Nov 2019, 19:38 by Caitlin Kelley

The rollout for Harry Styles’ sophomore album Fine Line just ramped up as the former boybander debuted “Watermelon Sugar” while pulling double duty as the host on Saturday Night Live.

Produced by Tyler Johnson and Kid Harpoon, Styles spins a sensual tale about romance:

Tastes like strawberries on a summer evenin'
And it sounds just like a song
I want your belly and that summer feelin'
I don’t know if I could ever go without

As Genius user @tirelessmess points out, the pop star’s ex-girlfriend Camille Rowe previously told Elle that Richard Brautigan’s In Watermelon Sugar is one of her favorite books.

Earlier this year, Styles spoke to Rolling Stone’s Rob Sheffield about becoming an active reader. “Reading didn’t really used to be my thing. I had such a short attention span,” he said. “But I was dating someone who gave me some books; I felt like I had to read them because she’d think I was a dummy if I didn’t read them.”

He dropped the studio version of the track less than an hour ahead of his SNL performance. Naturally, the noted aficionado of high-waisted pants color-coordinated his outfit with the song title.
