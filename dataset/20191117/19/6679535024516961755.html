Vizag zoo nets ₹6.61 lakh in a day

It registered 14,292 visitors on Sunday, say officials

17 Nov 2019, 19:46 by Staff Reporter

The Indira Gandhi Gandhi Zoological Park (IGZP) received a record number of footfalls on the third Sunday of ‘Karthika Masam’.

“The zoo recorded as many as 14,292 visitors on Sunday, the highest during the 2019-20 financial year. The total revenue earned on Sunday was around ₹6.61 lakh,” IGZP curator Yesoda Bai said.

The IGZP has been a major hotspot for picnics during the Karthika Masam. Not only from Visakhapatnam, a good number visitors from the neighbouring Vizianagaram and Srikakulam districts, especially students, visit the zoo.

The zoo officials are expecting more crowd next Sunday.

Amenities for picnickers

Recently, the zoo has added additional attractions including two Bengal tigers, a pair of wolf, Sarus cranes, new born Hyena cubs in view of the picnic season. The authorities have also facilitated space for picnickers, apart from other amenities such as RO drinking water, rest shelters and eight-seater battery operated vehicles among others.

Around 10.73 lakh people visited the zoo during the 2018-19 financial year, while the count was put at 9.29 lakh during the 2017-18 . The zoo generated a revenue of ₹4.20 crore in the 2018-19 financial year as of December 31.
