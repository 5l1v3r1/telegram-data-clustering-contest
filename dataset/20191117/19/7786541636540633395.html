'God has been calling me': Kanye West speaks to large crowd at Houston church

17 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

HOUSTON -

Rapper Kanye West told a packed congregation at a Houston church he is now "in service to God" and no longer beholden to fame and money.

"God has been calling me for a long time," West said after Sunday's service at Joel Osteen's Lakewood Church, KTRK reported. "The devil has been distracting me for a long time."
