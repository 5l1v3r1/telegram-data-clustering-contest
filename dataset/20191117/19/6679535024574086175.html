‘Give households prior notice on YSR Navasakam survey’

Final list of beneficiaries will be ready by Dec. 8, says Collector

17 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Special Correspondent

Collector V. Vinay Chand on Sunday asked the resource persons to give a prior intimation to the householders ahead of the door-to-door YSR Navasakam Survey scheduled to begin from November 20 to identify the genuine beneficiaries of government welfare schemes.

Addressing the resource persons, MPDOs and municipal officials at the inaugural of a training programme at the VMRDA Children’s Arena here, the Collector said that the survey data collected bu =y the village and ward volunteers should be entered to the system on a daily basis.

The survey will be done to identify the beneficiaries of rice, pension, Aarogyasri, Jagannana Vidya Deevena, Jagannana Vasathi Deevena and seven other schemes such as YSR Matsyakara Bharosa, Netanna Nestham, Amma Vodi among others.

“The survey will be completed by November 30 and the list of ineligible beneficiaries will be prepared by December 2. The final list of beneficiaries will be displayed at the respective village and ward secretariats by December 8,” Mr. Vinay Chand said.

Grama Sabha

Grama Sabhas will be organised from December 10 to 14 to receive pleas from people. The beneficiary identity cards will be printed on December 18 and 19 and they will be distributed on December 20.

The officials and volunteers should not be granted any leave till the completion of the survey, the Collector said.

The Collector asked the MPDOs and Municipal Commissioners to foloow the guidelines pertaining to various government schemes and monitor the progress of the survey. He also asked the officials and volunteers to ensure that there were no errors in the entry of data.

Municipal Commissioner G. Srijana, ITDA PO DK Balaji, Sub-Collector Venkatesh, Trainee Collector Pratishta, DRDO PD Visweswara Rao and others were present.
