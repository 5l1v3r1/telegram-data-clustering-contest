Furious Strictly fans rage show is 'ruined' as 'winner' Michelle Visage is axed from competition

Strictly Come Dancing fans were furious after Michelle's performance to Vogue was hailed as 'iconic' by fans but was criticised by the judges and left her at the bottom of the leaderboard

17 Nov 2019, 19:58 by Irishmirror.ie

Furious Strictly Come Dancing fans raged that the show was "ruined" after Michelle Visage became the eighth contestant to leave the competition this evening.

Raging viewers flocked to Twitter and lashed out at the show after the judges chose to send her and Giovanni Pernice home - despite the pair receiving consistently high scores in the previous weeks.

One viewer raged: "um @michellevisage deserved to be in the final because of her incredible dancing talent, and she got kicked out half way just because she wanted to create something meaningful for a community that has supported her. and she executed it flawlessly. absolute MESS #strictlyspoiler."

While another commented: "Michelle’s dance had a more meaningful story. She’s a confident woman, and an amazing dancer. She proved to be consistent throughout the whole of the competition, from the start. Saffron is only good at ballroom, not latin. She should’ve been kicked off. #strictlyspoiler."

A third remarked: "How the absolute F**k is the flop of a social media star like Safron still in Strictly over an amazing woman like Michelle??! I don't get it. Social media stars shouldn't be on telly. Their platform is online, let them stay there!! #StrictlySpoiler."

"#StrictlySpoiler I am so, so upset with the result. I feel like Michelle leaving really shows how small our community is and how people still have such a flippant attitude towards us. @michellevisage we love you and you're the best ally to us ️," another wrote.

One fumed: "Such a shame as Michelle really was top 3 and a finalist in my eyes. This years strictly has been so unpredictable and it’s a real shame that so much genuine potential has left too soon whilst more average dancers (imo) are cruising through #StrictlySpoiler."
