"Valverde will decide whether this is his last season at Barca"

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Barcelona sporting director, Eric Abidal, said that the Barca coach himself, Ernesto Valverde, will decide whether he stays or not.

In an interview which 'Sport' publish this Sunday, Eric Abidal says that "Ernesto Valverde will decide whether this is his last season at Barca." He signed a contract renewal until 30th June 2020 with the option of one more campaign. 

In addition, he admitted that the Barca president, Josep Maria Bartomeu, "is talking to Messi's agents to seal his contract renewal." This comes a few months after it was revealed that the player had the possibility of leaving the club at the end of any season.
