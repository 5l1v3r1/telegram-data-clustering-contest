Man arrested for allegedly assaulting man and spitting at police

17 Nov 2019, 00:16 by Jocelyn Garcia

A man has been charged after allegedly putting another man in hospital with serious injuries, and for spitting at and kicking police south of Brisbane.

Police were called to a violent fight between two men, who are known to each other, about 4.30pm on Saturday at a Turner Court address at Marsden in Logan.

A 30-year-old Boyne Island man was taken to Princess Alexandra Hospital with serious facial and head injuries. He remains in a serious condition.

Police arrested a 32-year-old Marsden man at the scene, with police alleging he spat and kicked out at officers.

He was charged with one count of grievous bodily harm, assault occasioning bodily harm, seriously assaulting police, and obstructing police.

He was denied police bail and is due to appear in the Beenleigh Magistrates Court on Monday.
