Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Backpacks For Every Kind Of Traveler

17 Nov 2019 by Lois Alter Mark

Remember when backpacks used to be worn only by hikers? Now, they’re the bag of choice for businesspeople, students, fitness enthusiasts and, especially, travelers.

Here are the ones that are elevating – and, in some cases redefining – the category. With these backpacks, stylish, easy travel is in the bag.  

There’s a reason Away has such a loyal following: their bags are pretty much perfect. This compact Daypack has a streamlined profile that holds everything you need neatly and easy to access. Its straps are cushy enough to spend the day on your shoulders, and its sleeve slides smoothly on to your favorite Away luggage. It’s just another reason to go Away. Price: $145

This handcrafted leather backpack is so luxurious, you’ll want to show it off everywhere. Ghurka is a favorite of A-listers like Tom Hanks and LeBron James, and you’ll feel like one yourself, especially since every piece comes with its own unique registration tag. The bag is equipped with a padded laptop holder and a nine-inch interior zipper pouch to keep your belongings safe and secure. In Vintage Chestnut Leather, it just gets better with age. Price: $1,295

This beauty’s good looks belie its practicality. Lightweight and durable, it’s available in Olive, Deep Lake and Black nylon, and features a padded laptop compartment and tablet pocket as well as an organizational panel with dedicated pockets for your essentials. Simple and stylish, this backpack means business. Price: $179

If you want a backpack that truly stands out, Cotopaxi has created an entire collection made from repurposed fabric, ensuring that every brightly-colored piece is one-of-a-kind. You can choose the exact bag you want or let Cotopaxi surprise you. Either way, you will treasure this rugged, thoughtfully-designed, feature-filled backpack meant for adventure. Price: $180

Made specifically for creatives by creatives, Osuza takes the idea of a backpack and totally twists it and turns it into a whole new bag. This premium black canvas bag is expandable, weatherproof and customizable. Best of all, it opens flat so you can access all your well-organized tools. If only your creativity and inspiration could last as long as Osuza’s lifetime warranty. Price: $349

Pretty in Mauve (or Buttermilk or Black), this light as air backpack will just make you happy. Everything you need stays safely cuddled in cloud-like puffiness while the waterproof nylon keeps it all dry. Surprisingly tough, the bag has an antimicrobial lining, secure passport sleeve and luggage slip, making it a great travel companion. Built-in phone and battery pockets keep you powered up, Price: $225

Sleek and sophisticated, this backpack from beloved luggage maker Briggs & Riley has three sections to keep you organized. There are separate pockets for tablets, cables and chargers, cords and USB drives and there’s even an RFID blocking pocket with two hidden credit card slip pockets to protect your personal data. The whole bag perfectly represents the band’s philosophy: engineered for reality. Price: $319

This super-light ripstop nylon backpack may weigh a mere 14 ounces but it is actually a powerhouse that can hold 100 pounds of your most important stuff. It’s got lots of pockets, including an internal stow pocket you can smush it into for travel and two side ones with new loop locks to keep water bottles secure. It’s also got a “Blinky” loop for attaching a light to help keep you visible because you’re likely to want to stay out late, now that you can comfortably carry around everything you need. Price: $49.95

Celebrate the coolest mom with this beautiful backpack, which also happens to be a diaper bag and changing station. Made from 100% recycled plastics, it features cushioned straps, easy access to everything inside and out plus a padded laptop sleeve for mama multitasking. It looks so good, you may want to use it even if you don’t have babies. Price: $180

Incase may be driven by design but their ICON backpack is an organizational wonder. Made of lightweight yet durable Woolenex to repel moisture, mildew and chemicals, it has a designated place for everything from papers to tech accessories to your laptop. Padded shoulder straps adjust for optimal comfort and ergonomic support, and there’s a cable port – just Incase. Price: $199.95

CamelBak is all about hands-free hydration, and the Rim Runner has long been a bestseller for day hikers. It offers a clean aesthetic and cargo pockets that let you grab what you need without removing your pack. The new Crux reservoir holds 2.5 liters, delivers 20% more water per sip, along with an ergonomic handle for easy refilling and an on/off lever to prevent leaks. This is definitely the bag to quench your thirst. Price: $100

It’s literally the only bag you need for a long weekend away. Price: $269.99
