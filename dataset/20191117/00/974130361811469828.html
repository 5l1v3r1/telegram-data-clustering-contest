Watch: Ireland U19s Fill Their Boots In 13-0 Win Over Gibraltar

17 Nov 2019 by Gary Connaughton

While the senior squad have struggled to find the back of the net in their Euro 2020 qualifying campaign, the U19 side seem to have no such issues.

Ireland have performed well at this level in recent times, reaching the semi-final of the European Championships during the summer. While some of the key members of that team have moved on to feature for Stephen Kenny's U21 side, the current crop are now making their mark.

Tom Mohan's side took on Gibraltar in a Euro qualifier yesterday, and managed to bag a hatful of goals. The game would finish 13-0, with Aston Villa's Tyreik Wright bagging four.
