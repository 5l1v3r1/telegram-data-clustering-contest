Real Madrid's players performing during international break

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Real Madrid's footballers on international duty are shining. Some of the senior players will return to Valdebebas after quite a positive week for their national teams.

There were things up for grabs for many of them and they have done their jobs. Sergio Ramos, Varane, Courtois, Hazard, Kroos, Modric and Fede Valverde put in great performances during the international break. 

Toni Kroos and Varane were decisive in the triumphs against Belarus and Moldova. The German scored twice was the the player of the match in Germany's win over Belarus which saw them qualify for Euro 2020 while Varane scored an important goal in France's win over Moldova.

Just like Toni Kroos, Eden Hazard also scored a double. He scored two goals against Russia in the 4-1 win in which Courtois also performed well. Modric assisted from a corner for Croatia in their win over Slovakia. 

Sergio Ramos became the most capped player for Spain with 169 caps, showing how important he is both for Spain and Real Madrid. 

Lastly, Fede Valverde and Rodrygo will also arrive in high spirits since the former started for Uruguay while the latter made his Brazil debut.
