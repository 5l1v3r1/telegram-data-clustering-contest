Billie Eilish heading for highest new entry on the Official Singles Chart with Everything I Wanted

Maroon 5 are heading for the Top 5 with Memories.

17 Nov 2019 by Jack White

The new Official Chart: First Look, on BBC Radio 1 today between 6pm – 7pm, offers a first glance of the Top 20 ahead of Friday’s Official Singles Chart Top 100. The chart is based on preliminary sales and early streaming reports.

Here's how the week is shaping up so far.

Billie Eilish is on course for this week’s highest new entry with Everything I Wanted at Number 3, what could be her third Top 10 single.

Tones and I is heading for an eighth week at Number 1 with Dance Monkey.

Maroon 5 are on track for a new peak of Number 5 with Memories, potentially becoming their eighth UK Top 5 hit.

Viral rap song Roxanne by Arizona Zervas is charging to a Top 20 finish, currently up 10 places to Number 14.

Find out Billie's final chart position on The Official Chart with Scott Mills every Friday from 4pm on BBC Radio 1.
