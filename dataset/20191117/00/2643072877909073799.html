Just In: Tonto Dike Denies Deportation Rumors

17 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

Nigerian Actress and a controversial social media celebrity Tonto Dike has debunked rumors of her being deported from the United Arab Emirates following an altercation with a security guard.

Naija News had earlier reported that controversial social media celebrity was deported from Dubai after she got into a fight with a security guard because she was denied entry at an event center in Dubai.

speaking In a video shared via the Instagram page of her friend, Idris Okuneye aka Bobrisky, Tonto said that she had not been deported from the UAE contrary to reports floating around social media.

Naija News recalls that earlier this weekend, the actress was arrested for assaulting a security guard who refused her entry at a private event and was scheduled for deportation.
