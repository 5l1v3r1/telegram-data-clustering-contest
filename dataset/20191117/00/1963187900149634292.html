Egyptian killed, 4 injured in Kuwait highway accident

17 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today staff

CAIRO â€“ 17 November 2019: An Egyptian was killed and four others were injured in an accident on a highway in Kuwait Sunday.

A pickup truck collided with a four-wheel drive on King Fahd road in the capital, where the 4x4 was transporting Egyptian workers. One of the workers died and four others were injured.

As the police was inspecting the accident, a large truck hit the police car from behind. The police car was smashed, but none was injured.
