Sowore: Stop Insulting The Collective Intelligence Of Nigerians, Falana Tells DSS

The DSS, after failing to release Sowore after he had met his bail condition twice, has given various reasons for not releasing the rights activist.

17 Nov 2019 by SaharaReporters, New York

 

Rights lawyer and counsel to Omoyele Sowore, Femi Falana (SAN), has urged the Department of State Services to stop insulting the collective intelligence of Nigerians and immediately release Sowore.

The DSS, after failing to release Sowore after he had met his bail condition twice, has given various reasons for not releasing the rights activist.

The DSS had initially claimed that nobody came to receive Sowore, and on another occasion claimed that his sureties had yet to appear before it.

In another development, the security agency said it is yet to release Sowore because he asked and preferred to be in their custody.

Reacting to the claims by the DSS, Falana asked the agency to put the record straight and stop lying to the general public.

The Senior Advocate of Nigeria urged the agency to release all those it had detained illegally in their custody and “stop insulting the collective intelligence of the Nigerian people by attempting to justify the subversion of the rule of law.”
