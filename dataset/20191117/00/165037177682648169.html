Cristiano asks Juve to sign Pogba

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Paul Pogba hasn't been removed from Juventus' radar, much the opposite. As time goes by there is more interest in the Manchester United player. According to 'Tuttosport', Cristiano Ronaldo has asked the Juve board to sign Pogba.

The aforementioned source made the supposed request from Ronaldo their headline. "CR7 wants Pogba".

"Cristiano has expressed to the Juve board his enthusiasm for Paul Pogba's return", they explain.

Juventus continue working to make the dream of Pogba returning come true. They are trying to make a move so that he changes the Premier League for the Serie A.
