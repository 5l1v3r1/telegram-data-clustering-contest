Ruben Neves is declared non-transferable

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

John Marshall stated in declarations with 'Express and Star' that Ruben Neves is totally off the market. The head of the coaching body at the club is a very authoritative figure and his words could stop any future negotiations from taking place.

The midfielder has become a key part of the recently promoted team and has already received interest from other clubs in England. The latest being Liverpool, who once again showed their interest in signing him, but there was no offer in the summer.

For three seasons now he has been essential for his club. He nearly always appears in the starting XI and has been highly revalued. Recently, he scored a goal that showed his quality with the ball in his feet despite his defensive cut.
