I Don’t Need Money For Heart Surgery But For Medicine And Upkeep… - Actor Psalm Adjetefio

17 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Veteran Ghanaian actor, Psalm Adjetefio has denied calling on Ghanaians to help him raise money for a heart surgery.

In an interview with Kwesi Aboagye on Peace FM Entertainment Review programme, 'TT', as he is popularly called explained how reports emerged that he was soliciting for funds and further went on to disclose his current health condition and what his Doctors have told him so far.
