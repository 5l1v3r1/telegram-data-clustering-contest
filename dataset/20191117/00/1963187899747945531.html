EgyptAir participates in Dubai Airshow 2019

17 Nov 2019 by MENA

CAIRO, Nov 17 (MENA) - Civil Aviation Minister Younis el Masri said that EgyptAir is participating in the 16th edition of Dubai Airshow, which runs from November 17 to 21, to showcase the latest and greatest in military, general and commercial aviation.

In statements on Sunday, Masri asserted the importance of Egypt's participation in such international gatherings not only to make deals but also to exchange expertise in the field of air transport and study the international market.

The Dubai Airshow 2019 attracts the global aerospace industry to DWC, Dubai Airshow Site for the five day event. Some 87,000 aerospace professionals are expected to attend to interact with 1,300 exhibitors and see up to 165 aircraft in the static and flying displays during the event which features sector specific conferences including Cargo Connect, Global Air Traffic Management (GATM) and free to attend conferences in the Space Pavilion: Women in Space and Tech Talks.
