Actress Damilola Shares VIDEO Of Her Car With The Message 'I Hate You' Scribbled On It By An Unknown Person

17 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Nollywood actress Damilola Adegbite has addressed her haters after someone mysteriously left a spiteful message on her car.

The unknown individual had left a message stating that he/she hates the actress by scribbling the words 'I hate you' on her car using a sharp object.

Sharing a video of her car on Instagram, Damilola wrote, 'so apparently, someone somewhere hates me so much they had to leave a message on my car. I must be doing something right'.
