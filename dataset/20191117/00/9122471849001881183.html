Texas’ Disastrous Mistake Leads To Heartbreaking Loss vs. Iowa State

17 Nov 2019, 00:15 by Andrew Holleran

Losses don’t get much tougher than the one just suffered by Tom Herman and the Texas Longhorns, who fell on the road to Iowa State, 23-21, on a last-second field goal.

Texas took the lead late and appeared to be in good position when a go-ahead field goal by Iowa State was missed with a little more than 2 minutes to go. The Longhorns were offsides on the missed kick, though, so the drive continued.

Fast forward about two minutes and Iowa State was attempting a shorter game-winning field goal. This one did not miss.

Iowa State wins.
