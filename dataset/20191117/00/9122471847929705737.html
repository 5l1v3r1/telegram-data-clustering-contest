Michigan Linebacker’s Harsh Message For Michigan State After Win

17 Nov 2019, 00:03 by Andrew Holleran

Michigan continued to roll today, as the Wolverines took down Michigan State with relative ease in Ann Arbor, winning 44-10.

Jim Harbaugh and his team were extremely pumped with the win, as the postgame locker room celebration video shows.

One Wolverines linebacker had his own message for the Spartans following the win, too.

Michigan senior Josh Uche told Michigan State to “never” come back to Ann Arbor following the victory.

“We kicked them out the building, they should never come back here,” he said on FOX.
