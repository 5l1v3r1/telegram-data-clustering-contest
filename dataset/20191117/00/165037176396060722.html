Did Cristiano get his 99th goal?

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Portugal beat Luxembourg 0-2 to book their place at Euro 2020, but we do not know who scored the second goal. Was it Cristiano Ronaldo's or Diogo Jota's?

In the 86th minute, Portugal sealed their victory over Luxembourg and at the same time, their place at Euro 2020. The question is did Cristiano Ronaldo or Diogo Jota score it? 

The Wolverhampton man beat Moris with a shot which the goalkeeper got a hand to. The ball trickled towards the goal and then Ronaldo appeared to make sure no Luxembourg player could clear it at the last minute. 

The doubt is over whether the Juventus player touched the ball once it was fully over the line or not. The ex-Real Madrid player celebrated the goal as if it were his.

The UEFA graphics credited Cristiano with the goal although Spanish referee Gil Manzano will have the final say in his match report. 

If it goes down as a CR7 goal, it will be his 99th for his country in 153 appearances.
