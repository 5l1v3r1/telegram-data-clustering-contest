Dungog Commonwealth Bank collecting bags for Share the Dignity campaign

17 Nov 2019, 22:19

Dungog Commonwealth Bank branch has again joined the "#inthebag" campaign, setting up a collection point for handbags in new or near new condition filled with women's personal care and sanitary items.

The initiative, run by Australian not-for-profit organisation Share the Dignity, has been providing homeless and at risk women with sanitary products since early 2015.

Karen Hancock, Branch Manager of Commonwealth Bank Dungog said staff were thrilled to take part in the initiative,which helps make life a bit better for women experiencing homelessness or poverty.

You can support Share the Dignity by donating a handbag in good condition filled with essential items to a participating Commonwealth Bank branch before December 7.
