The Latest MF DOOM Imposter Turned Out To Be Hannibal Buress

17 Nov 2019, 22:59 by Caitlin Kelley

MF DOOM is still as elusive as ever. The eccentric rapper seemed to make a guest appearance during Flying Lotus’ set at the Adult Swim Festival in LA’s Banc of California Stadium. But the true identity behind the mask turned out to be none other than Hannibal Buress.

Before the big reveal, Buress performed “Accordion.” When FlyLo unmasked the Doctor Doom-style villain, he asked, “Hannibal, what are you doing here?” The comic had this to say: “I wanted to help out.”
