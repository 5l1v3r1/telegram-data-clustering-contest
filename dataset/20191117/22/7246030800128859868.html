Dozens of Firebombs Found Hidden in Galilee Cemetery

17 Nov 2019, 22:14 by Hana Levi Julian

Dozens of ready-to-use firebombs (Molotov cocktails) were found this weekend hidden in a Christian cemetery in the Galilee Arab village of Tur’an.

A total of 34 of the firebombs were discovered, all of them primed and ready for use.

It’s not clear who stockpiled them in the cemetery. An investigation is underway.
