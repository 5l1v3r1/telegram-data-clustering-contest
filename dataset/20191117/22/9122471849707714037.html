ESPN’s Updated Prediction For Alabama-Auburn Game

17 Nov 2019, 22:07 by Andrew Holleran

Alabama and Auburn both have effective bye weeks in Week 13, with the Crimson Tide taking on Western Carolina and the Tigers playing Samford.

So, it’s time to start looking ahead to the Iron Bowl.

The 2019 edition of the rivalry game will look a lot different than initially expected due to Tua Tagovailoa’s unfortunate season-ending injury. The Crimson Tide remain the favorite, however.

ESPN’s Football Power Index has updated its prediction for the game. It’s predicted to be closer than originally projected, though.

ESPN’s Football Power Index is giving Alabama a 68.3 percent chance of winning this game, giving Auburn a 31.7 percent chance of pulling off the upset.

Alabama needs a win, perhaps in blowout fashion, to remain in the hunt for the College Football Playoff.

The Crimson Tide and the Tigers are set to meet on Saturday, Nov. 30. A kickoff time and TV channel have yet to be announced.
