Gold Coast cyclist fighting for life after suspected medical episode

17 Nov 2019, 22:23 by Shanee Dobeson

A man’s fighting for life after suffering a suspected medical episode while riding a bike on the Gold Coast this morning.

The cyclist is understood to have fallen off his bike at the intersection of Napper Road and Arundel Drive at Arundel just before 6.00am.

Paramedics rushed to the scene and treated the man for critical injuries.

He has since been taken to the Gold Coast University Hospital in a critical condition.

A Queensland Ambulance spokesperson said the High Acuity Response Unit was on board for transport.
