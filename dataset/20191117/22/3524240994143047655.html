#Kogi/BayelsaDecide: We remain strong, united –PDP

17 Nov 2019, 22:17 by Adesola Ayo-Aderele

The Peoples Democratic Party has said that it remained strong and united “in the face of all its experience” in the Bayelsa and Kogi governorship elections conducted on Saturday.

The party stated this in a statement issued by its National Publicity Secretary, Mr. Kola Ologbondiyan, on Sunday in Abuja while reacting to the outcomes of the two states’ governorship elections.

He said that the PDP would continue to “fully exert itself in upholding the wishes and aspirations of Nigerians.”
