In pictures: Spectacular scenes as Edinburgh and Glasgow hold Christmas lights switch-ons

In Glasgow, some 13,000 ticket holders filled George Square for the occasion.

17 Nov 2019, 22:17 by Tim McNulty

The skylines of Edinburgh and Glasgow were lit up this evening as both cities held their annual Christmas lights switch on events.

In Glasgow , some 13,000 ticket holders filled George Square for the occasion.

The festive crowd was treated to entertainment from Michelle McManus and members of the Soundsational Choir and the cast of The Steamie.
