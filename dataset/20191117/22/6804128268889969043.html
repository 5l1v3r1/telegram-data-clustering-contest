Jody Morris has an amazing message for Chelsea trio Tammy Abraham, Mason Mount and Fikayo Tomori

Chelsea's assistant manager has taken to social media to send a message to several Blues stars after England's latest win

17 Nov 2019, 22:00 by Robert Warlow

Chelsea's England contingent have had a pretty good week on international duty.

From Tammy Abraham scoring his first England goal on Thursday night against Montenegro to Mason Mount celebrating his first goal in a 4-0 win against Kosovo this evening (Sunday), the Chelsea stars playing for the Three Lions have enjoyed some good moments.

Add to that an England debut for Fikayo Tomori, who came off the bench in Pristina tonight - and a third cap for Callum Hudson-Odoi - and the young Blues have plenty to cheer, with the Three Lions celebrating qualification for next summer's European Championships in style.

The Chelsea quartet will now return to the Blues' training ground at Cobham ahead of the Premier League clash with Manchester City next weekend.

And ahead of their return, Chelsea assistant manager Jody Morris has sent a message to three of the Blues stars after their exploits for England this week.

Taking to Twitter following England's 4-0 win against Kosovo, Morris has posted the following message, together with a blue heart.

"What a week for the boys @tammyabraham @masonmount_10 @fikayotomori_ very proud of all 3...you deserve it lads."

The post has already been liked by more than 4,000 people.
