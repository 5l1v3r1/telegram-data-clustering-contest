Gordon Ramsay 'paid Ed Sheeran £500,000' to perform at daughter Tilly’s 18th birthday

The TV chef parted with a large amount of money to ensure his daughter had a party to remember

17 Nov 2019, 22:14 by Irishmirror.ie

Gordon Ramsay reportedly 'spent £500,000' on the entertainment for daughter Tilly's 18th birthday bash last week.

The TV chef, 53, paid the massive amount to Ed Sheeran so that he would perform for Tilly's milestone birthday as the Shape of You hitmaker is her favourite artist.

“Tilly’s bash was next level. It was clear Gordon had spared no expense in making sure it really was a night to remember,' a party insider told The Sun .

“Ed is Tilly’s favourite singer so it meant the world to her that he was there, even though his set didn’t come cheap.

“The whole party had a James Bond theme, with drinks topped with her face or the 007 logo and guests encouraged to come in black tie. There was even a 007 ice sculpture which you could drink shots out of.”

The publication also claim that it cost Gordon a whopping £500,000 to get the red-haired musician on board for the intimate gig.

Tilly's birthday party came after she risked the wrath of the sweary chef when she went public with her new boyfriend on Instagram earlier this month.

She shared two sweet black and white photos with an unnamed boy as they did some sightseeing in South Africa.

In one photo they pose with their arms around each other, and in the next Tilly, a budding chef herself, looked giggly as she nestled in close.

Her post shocked her famous dad, who wrote "WTF" in the Instagram comments.

Tilly's brother Jack had anticipated their father's reaction, writing: "@gordongram interrogation time."

Their sister Megan joined in, saying: "We have all been there Tills - gotta face up to it."

One person commented asking if the boy was Tilly's boyfriend and she said "maybe".

No wonder it comes as a surprise to Gordon, who just weeks ago revealed that Tilly is dating Gino D'Acampo's son Luciano.

He said Tilly, who has her own CBBC show Matilda and the Ramsay Bunch, grew close to Gino's boy, 17, while their dads were filming new TV show Men Behaving Badly.

Gordon shared news of his daughter romance's with the offspring of his pal Gino, 43, during an appearance on The Jonathan Ross Show.

When he was asked about rumours the teenagers had grown close, famously foul-mouthed Gordon described the situation as a "f***ing nightmare".

Gordon said: "We were driving through San Francisco and FaceTiming Luciano, and all of a sudden Tilly pops up behind them, and they are out having lunch.

"I think they are friends."
