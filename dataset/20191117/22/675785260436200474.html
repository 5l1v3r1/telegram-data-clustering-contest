Kate and Cait become the first I'm A Celeb contestants to face bushtucker trial

Kate Garraway and Caitlyn Jenner will face a skin crawling trial in an effort to win food for the camp on I'm A Celeb

17 Nov 2019, 22:51 by Mirror .co.uk

Two I'm A Celebrity contestants were picked to face the first Bushtucker trial of the 2019 I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! competition.

The public whittled down the contestants to Kate Garraway and Caitlyn Jenner to be the first to face a challenge called Bugged Off.

As the launch episode of the show reached a conclusion, show hosts Ant and Dec broke news to the camp that someone would be facing a trial.

And after revealing Caitlyn could face a trail, Kate gasped when she realised she was the last to be named - meaning she could also be in the line of bad luck.
