DeAndre Hopkins Calls Out NFL Refs After Game Against Ravens (TWEET)

17 Nov 2019, 22:41 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Baltimore Ravens absolutely dominated the Houston Texans on Sunday afternoon, winning 41-7, in what was billed as one of the games of the season before the teams took the field.

That game also included a terribly missed call where the refs hurt the Texans early on with a missed defensive pass interception.

Deandre Watson went deep to DeAndre Hopkins early in the game on a 4th down attempt, but it fell incomplete, largely due to Hopkins being grabbed before the ball got there.

Houston challenged it, and they still upheld the call.
