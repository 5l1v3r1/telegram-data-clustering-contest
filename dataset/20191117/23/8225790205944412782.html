Brush fire on Maui prompts temporary closure of Pulehu Road

17 Nov 2019, 23:11 by HNN Staff

MAUI, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) -Firefighters on Maui worked to put out a brush fire Sunday afternoon.

Maui police said the fire was burning near the central Maui landfill. Early reports indicate about 20 acres were burned.

The fire forced the closure of Pulehu Road from Hansen Road to just above the landfill.
