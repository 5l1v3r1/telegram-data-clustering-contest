Man dies, teen trapped and left fighting for life after two-car crash

17 Nov 2019, 23:24 by Rachel Eddie

An elderly man has been killed and an 18-year-old driver is fighting for his life after a two-car crash in the state's east.

Emergency services were called to the South Gippsland Highway in Leongatha, near Steele Street, at 3.15am on Monday.

A 79-year-old man from Meeniyan died at the scene.

A 74-year-old woman travelling in the same car, also from Meeniyan, was flown to the Royal Melbourne Hospital in a stable condition with chest injuries.

An 18-year-old Leongatha man, who had to be freed after becoming trapped in the second car, has been airlifted to The Alfred hospital in a critical condition.

Police are investigating the exact cause of the crash.
