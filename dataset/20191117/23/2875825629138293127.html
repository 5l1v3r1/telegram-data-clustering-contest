Strictly's Chris Ramsey jokes Blackpool was better than any of his comedy gigs

The comedian made a joke that dancing in Blackpool for Strictly Come Dancing was better than any of his previous comedy gigs

17 Nov 2019, 23:49 by Irishmirror.ie

Chris Ramsey joked his Strictly Come Dancing performance at Blackpool Tower was better than all of his comedy gigs combined.

He opened the start of the incredible Blackpool show last night and gushed that he had been honoured to perform there.

Ahead of Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard's dance-off against Michelle Visage and Giovanni Pernice, host Claudia Winkleman sat down with the comedian to ask his thoughts.

Praising him for kicking off the start of yesterday's show, Claudia told him: "You opened the show in Blackpool.

"You just loved every second!"

A very overwhelmed Chris laughed: "That was better than all of my previous Blackpool gigs ever combined.

"That was the biggest attended... that was the best gig I've ever had in Blackpool."

His professional dance partner Karen Hauer giggled beside him as the star got emotional with Blackpool being so close to home.

Furious Strictly Come Dancing fans raged that the show was "ruined" after Michelle Visage became the eighth contestant to leave the competition this evening.

Raging viewers flocked to Twitter and lashed out at the show after the judges chose to send her and Giovanni Pernice home - despite the pair receiving consistently high scores in the previous weeks.

One viewer raged: "um @michellevisage deserved to be in the final because of her incredible dancing talent, and she got kicked out half way just because she wanted to create something meaningful for a community that has supported her. and she executed it flawlessly. absolute MESS #strictlyspoiler."

A distraught Michelle said: "At 51 years old and never danced in my life, I never thought I'd be able to do what I have done and to have such an incredible partner.

"To be here for my husband and my kids and the entire community, that's why I did what I did. I am so honoured to be welcomed by my new friends here."
