Eric Reid Campaigns For Panthers To Sign Colin Kaepernick After Kyle Allen Throws 4 INTs (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019, 23:09 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Carolina Panthers were one of the hottest teams in the entire league after they lost QB Cam Newton to a foot injury, as they won four games in a row after his departure.

Then they faced the 49ers and got smacked to reality and that has led to one win over their past four games.

That also includes on Sunday where the team lost to the Atlanta Falcons 29-3 at home.

QB Kyle Allen completes 31 of 50 passes for 325 yards and 4 interceptions.

After the game, Panthers Pro Bowl safety Eric Reid campaigned for the team to sign Colin Kaepernick.
