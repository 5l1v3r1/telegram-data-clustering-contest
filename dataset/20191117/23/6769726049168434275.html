Woman taken to hospital with neck wound after fight

17 Nov 2019, 23:22

A WOMAN sustained a wound to her neck after a physical altercation in Bertram last night.

Police said there was a fight between a man and a woman known to each other at a house in Parkfield Boulevard at 8.20pm.

A woman in her 30s was injured and sought assistance from staff at a nearby restaurant. She was taken by ambulance to Fiona Stanley Hospital for treatment.

A man in his 60s is currently assisting police with their inquiries.
