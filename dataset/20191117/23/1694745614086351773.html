11 Thanksgiving Movies and TV Episodes on Netflix Right Now

17 Nov 2019, 23:25 by Andrea Reiher

Gobble, gobble! It's almost time to loosen your belt, carve the turkey, and commence stuffing your face on Thanksgiving Day. But after the last bite of pumpkin pie is gone (or you've returned from going out to eat), what better way to pass the time than watching classic Thanksgiving fare on Netflix?

We've rounded up 11 possibilities for families to enjoy together (including one horror movie that should be saved for after the kids have gone to bed). So settle in with your mashed potatoes and gravy and get ready for some delicious Thanksgiving offerings.

1 Cheers

Viewers get their first and only view of Norm's wife, Vera (played by George Wendt's real-life wife Bernadette Birkett), though the unseen-character gag remains intact as she takes a pumpkin pie to the face before anyone can see her.

Watch Cheers on Netflix.

2 Fuller House

Almost the entire Tanner clan gets together, including Jesse and Becky's grown twin sons and Joey's four kids, who annoy everybody. Who isn't in attendance? Michelle, of course.

Watch Fuller House on Netflix.

3 Friends

Friends set a high bar with its annual Thanksgiving episodes, featuring everything from amazing flashbacks to a hilarious touch football game to some famous guest stars (Brad Pitt, Christina Applegate). Can any show ever top Friends in sheer volume of legendary Turkey Day episodes? Highly unlikely.

Watch Friends on Netflix.

4 Gilmore Girls

Who doesn't love four insane Thanksgivings where the turkeys run the gamut from deep-fried to "tofurkey"? And, Lane and Dave kiss for the first time!

Watch Gilmore Girls on Netflix.

5 Gossip Girl

This WB teen drama had a memorable Thanksgiving episode with "Blair Waldorf Must Pie," where the Van der Woodsens crash the Humphreys' Thanksgiving and Blair has to come to terms with her father being MIA.

Watch Gossip Girl on Netflix.

6 Kristy

If you love a good old-fashioned holiday horrorfest, check out this slasher movie about a gang of killers terrorizing a college student who stays on campus during Thanksgiving weekend.

Watch Kristy on Netflix.

7 That '70s Show

Laurie comes home for the holidays, bringing a friend with her. Eric ends up kissing said friend, much to Donna's irritation.

Watch That '70s Show on Netflix.
