Solskjaer handed boost as Man Utd star Dalot posts injury update on Instagram

17 Nov 2019, 23:22

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer and Manchester United have been handed an injury boost after defender Diogo Dalot revealed he was closing in on a return from the treatment room.

Premier League giants Manchester United spent £19million to sign Dalot from Porto last year but the Portuguese full-back has endured a mixed start to his career at Old Trafford.

The 20-year-old has shown glimpses of his potential over the past 17 months but his progress this season was halted by an injury he sustained during the defeat to Newcastle in October.

As a result of the injury setback, Dalot has missed Manchester United’s last seven matches in all competitions.

But the highly-rated defender is confident a return to first-team action is imminent and posted on Instagram: ‘Getting closer…’

Following the international break, Manchester United – currently seventh in the Premier League table – travel to fifth-placed Sheffield United.
