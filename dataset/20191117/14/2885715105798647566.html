Pankaj Tripathi shares Mirzapur Season 2 teaser, watch video

Mirzapur 2 teaser: The clip didn't reveal anything major about the second season, except maybe hinting at more violence as we see a host of people attend a funeral in the video. 

17 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Entertainment Desk

Actor Pankaj Tripathi made his Instagram debut on November 16. The actor shared a video that left everyone excited. The versatile actor shared a clip from the upcoming season of Amazon Prime Original Mirzapur.

The clip didn’t reveal anything major about the second season, except maybe hinting at more violence as we see a host of people attend a funeral in the video.

In the video, we hear Pankaj Tripathi’s voice saying ‘whosoever has come on the earth, will leave too but everything will happen in accordance to his wish.’ So, what is going to happen?

By the end of the video, the makers announce that the second season will stream in 2020. However, the fans will have to wait a bit longer for exact details on the same.

Mirzapur is an Indian crime thriller web series on Amazon Prime Video produced by Excel Entertainment. The series revolves around drugs, guns and lawlessness. It depicts the governance and rule of mafia dons and the rivalry and crime prevailing in the Purvanchal region of Uttar Pradesh.

The series was Amazon Prime Video’s third fiction Indian Original after Inside Edge and Breathe. Apart from Pankaj Tripathi, the series starred an ensemble of actors including the likes of Ali Fazal, Vikrant Massey, Shweta Tripathi, Shriya Pilgaonkar, Rasika Dugal, Harshita Gaur, Divyendu Sharma and Kulbhushan Kharbanda.
