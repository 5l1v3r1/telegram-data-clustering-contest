Michigan Star Reveals What He Told Michigan State’s Players After Win

17 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Andrew Holleran

Michigan got another needed victory on Saturday, as the Wolverines took care of business at home against Michigan State. Jim Harbaugh’s team entered the rivalry game as a sizable favorite, though you never know what could happen when these two teams meet on the field.

Harbaugh’s team ended up dominating Mark Dantonio’s squad, winning 44-10.

The Wolverines were an extremely happy group following the game. One Wolverines player had some harsh words for the Spartans, too.

Michigan star defensive back Josh Metellus said he told Michigan State’s players to get off the field and go home following the game. Metellus said that the Wolverines are a better and classier program.
