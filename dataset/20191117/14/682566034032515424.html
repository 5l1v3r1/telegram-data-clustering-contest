Bafana lineup named for AFCON clash against Sudan

17 Nov 2019, 14:00

Cape Town - Bafana Bafana coach Molefi Ntseki has named his starting XI for South Africa's crucial 2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualifer against Sudan at Orlando Stadium.

After losing to Ghana 2-0 away from home on Wednesday, this match is a must-win for South Africa, especially as Sudan easily beat São Tomé and Principe 4-0.

Ntseki has made a number of changes to his starting lineup with a win in mind.

The most important see Lebo Mothiba starting up front and Thulani Serero also starting in midfield.

Kick-off for this fixture is at 15:00.

Bafana Bafana XI

Ronwen Williams (goal-keeper), Thapelo Morena , Sfiso Hlanti , Erick Mathoho, Thulani Hlatshwayo (captain), Kamohelo Mokotjo , Dean Furman , Lebohang Phiri , Percy Tau , Thulani Serero, Lebo Mothiba

Subs: Keet, Lebusa , Mkhwanazi , Lorch , Manyama, Mvala , Modiba , Maela , Grobler, Mokeke, Erasmus.
