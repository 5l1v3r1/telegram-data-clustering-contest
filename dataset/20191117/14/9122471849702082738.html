ESPN Releases Its New Top 25 After Week 12

17 Nov 2019, 14:07 by Andrew Holleran

Week 12 of the 2019 college football season is in the books. It was an eventful one, with a couple of undefeated teams losing their first games of the season.

Minnesota lost on the road to Iowa and Baylor lost at home to Oklahoma. The College Football Playoff picture is starting to get a little clearer, though that No. 4 spot is still mostly up for grabs, with LSU, Ohio State and Clemson likely getting the first three spots.

The new top 25 polls will be out later today.

Until then, ESPN’s Football Power Index has updated its top 25.

The new Coaches’ Poll will be out at noon E.T. and the new AP Poll will be out at 2 p.m. E.T.

The College Football Playoff rankings will update on Tuesday night.
