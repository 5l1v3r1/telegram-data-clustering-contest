Birmingham woman transforms empty cupboard under stairs into incredible 'bedroom’ for her dog

The woman revealed what was once a bare box room with grey carpet and white walls has been given a modern revamp with marble-style tiles

17 Nov 2019, 14:49 by James Rodger, Amber Hicks, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

A Birmingham woman has revealed how she transformed an empty cupboard under her stairs into a bedroom - for her dog.

The woman, from the Second City, revealed the incredible transformation which has seen her create an adorable bedroom for her Labrador.

The Lab, named Luther, is enjoying his new home - with plenty of blankets to keep him snug, as well as a cosy bed.

The images have been posted on social media, with home lovers flocking to laud the Birmingham woman after she shared her transformation.

She created the heavenly environment for her pup and has subsequently been branded the "best human ever".

Facebook users who are members of a popular DIY group have taken to the social networking platform to give feedback.

The woman revealed what was once a bare box room with grey carpet and white walls has been given a modern revamp with marble-style tiles.

The walls have decorated using a combination of pale green tiles and wallpaper, while a number of shelves house Luther's treats, reports the Mirror.

 

The thrifty decorator captioned the post: "Wasn’t using my under stairs cupboard so made my Labrador a bedroom."

One person replied: "This is a great idea."

Another wrote: "Absolutely LOVE. Your pet is treated better than some people probably has a nicer pad too! Fantastic." 

One social media user quipped it was "better than Harry Potter’s under stair bedroom".

 

Another gushed: "This is the cutest thing! You are the best human.'
