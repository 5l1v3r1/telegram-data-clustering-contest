Former Bangladesh captain Aminul Islam says India pace attack like Windies in 70s

Former Bangladesh skipper Aminul Islam said India will be huge favourites in the upcoming Day/Night Test, which will be played in Kolkata from November 22, because of the potency of the home team's pace attack.

17 Nov 2019, 14:42 by Sports Desk

Former Bangladesh captain Aminul Islam said India will be huge favourites in the upcoming Day/Night Test, which will be played in Kolkata from November 22, because of the potency of the Indian pace attack.

The Kolkata Test, the second match of the series, will be the first Test to be played with the pink ball on Indian soil.
