Donald Trump Watched ‘Joker’ At White House Screening And Reportedly Liked It

17 Nov 2019, 14:54 by Brent Furdyk

While “Joker” has been laughing all the way to the bank thanks to a worldwide box-office tally in excess of $1 billion, the Joaquin Phoenix-starring feature received a special VIP screening on Saturday night.

In fact, a tweet from CNN’s Shimon Prokupecz reveals that a White House official told him that President Donald Trump hosted a special screening of the film.
