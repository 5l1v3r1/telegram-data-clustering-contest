ESPN’s Paul Finebaum Releases His New Top 6

17 Nov 2019, 14:57 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN college football analyst Paul Finebaum has updated his top six poll following Week 12 of the 2019 college football regular season.

Week 12 saw a couple of undefeated teams go down, with Minnesota losing at Iowa and Baylor blowing a huge lead to Oklahoma.

Georgia also notched a big-time win, staying in the College Football Playoff race with a win against Auburn.

Finebaum has since updated his top six:

  1. LSU
  2. Ohio State
  3. Clemson
  4. Georgia
  5. Oregon
  6. Alabama
