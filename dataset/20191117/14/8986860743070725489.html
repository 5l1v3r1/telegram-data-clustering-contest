Shatta Wale and Stonebwoy would soon become enemies – Pope Skinny prophesy

17 Nov 2019, 14:55 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Asuoden music boss and former close buddy of Dancehall artiste Shatta Wale, Pope Skinny, has prophecied that the newfound friendship between Shatta Wale and Stonebwoy won’t last long.

His comment comes after Shatta Wale yesterday awarded Stonebwoy with the ‘Most Popular Song’ during the MTN 4Syte TV Music Videos Awards 2019.

But according to Pope Skinny who was the former right-hand man to Shatta Wale, he believes his former friend has something up his sleeves and Stonebwoy must be very careful.

Taking to his social media handle, he wrote:

“Success delayed is not success denied. Blade and Bone, I wish you guys well. Very soon Good over Evil and Stone go join you guys. May God grant us long life Itz a movie. The world go watch am live.”
