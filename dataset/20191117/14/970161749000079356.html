Kosovo vs England kick-off time, TV channel, live stream, odds, team news

17 Nov 2019, 14:11

England have already booked their spot at Euro 2020, but will complete their qualification process away to Kosovo on Sunday evening.

The Three Lions are coming off a resounding 7-0 walloping of Montenegro on Thursday at Wembley and will be full of confidence as they head to Pristina.

Kosovo have had an impressive campaign, but cannot make it into the tournament, with Czech Republic already guaranteeing second place in Group A.

However, they will be looking to finish off their campaign in style and produce another exciting performance after their thrilling 5-3 defeat to England in Southampton earlier in the process.

Gareth Southgate will be making a number of changes to his team with qualification already secured, but will be looking to score a hatful of goals again after his side have netted 33 times in just seven matches so far.

Kosovo vs England team news

Raheem Sterling will return to the starting line-up after being left out against Montenegro on Thursday.

Joe Gomez and Jordan Henderson are out with a knee injury and a viral infection respectively, while James Maddison is struggling with a calf strain.

There will be numerous changes for England, including in goal, with Nick Pope likely to start instead of Jordan Pickford.
