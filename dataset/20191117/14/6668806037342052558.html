Salman Khan flaunts his love for fitness at an event in the city

17 Nov 2019, 14:33 by Kajol Mathur

Salman Khan’s love for fitness is known by all. The superstar is known to have trained several Bollywood actors and been inspiring several men to look their absolute best. Snapped in the city today at an event, Bhaijaan was seen looking dapper at a workout studio where he was present to review the equipment. 

The 53-year-old actor donned a grey t-shirt which he paired with blue denims and black shoes. He rounded off his look with black sunnies and smiled for the paps present there. He was accompanied by brother Sohail Khan and the two had a good time together.
