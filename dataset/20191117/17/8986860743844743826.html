Shatta Wale gifts his award to Stonebwoy at 4Syte TV Awards

17 Nov 2019, 17:42 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Award-winning dancehall artiste Charles Nii Armah Mensah Jnr. popularly known as Shatta Wale gifted his award to Stonebwoy at the just ended 4Syte TV Awards.

Shatta Wale after receiving the award for the ‘Most Popular Video’ decided to give it to the “Bawasaba” hitmaker.

The dancehall musician stated that he decided to gift his award to Stonebwoy to show how much he loves him.

The “My Level” hitmaker further stated that his beef with Stonebwoy is over adding that he is at peace with him.

“I know there is a whole lot of controversy about Shatta Wale and awards but I just want to present this award to Stonebwoy. I just want him to know that I love him so much. The beef is over and today I am giving my award to him”, Shatta Wale stated.
