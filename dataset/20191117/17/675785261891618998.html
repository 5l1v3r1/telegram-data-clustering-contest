Gwyneth Paltrow puts £33 toilet paper on her 'essential' Christmas list

Gwyneth Paltrow's website Goop has put together a list of gifts guides in time for Christmas, and some of them are bizarre

17 Nov 2019, 17:21 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Gwyneth Paltrow has put posh toilet paper on her "essential" Christmas list — and it'll set you back £33 a roll.

Her lifestyle website Goop has put together a list of wellness gifts for their readers to buy their loved ones for for the holidays.

Included on the list is No.2 Toilet Paper, which is described as bog roll "with a conscience" because it's made out of "100% sustainable bamboo".

The website praises the "chic dark floral packaging" that the toilet paper arrives in and says it has a "silky, smooth texture that's gentle on skin".

It is also "Clog-safe and septic safe" and plastic free.

"Need we say more?" Goop asks its readers.

The website has put together several lists of presents to buy all the different people in your life, including a "lovers" list.

On that one is a £329 pair of gold handcuffs which are described as being "exquisite and (almost) escape-proof".

A 'Ridiculous but Awesome' gift guide has a a £193 gold fire extinguisher.

You can also buy an £85,000 tree house and a £620 herb garden.

Gwyneth, whose Goop empire is worth around £200million, is known for kickstarting unusual trends.

The Hollywood movie star has shared her love of vaginal steaming, as well as advocating the use of crystal sex toys costing hundreds of pounds.

She keeps her skin fresh with vampire blood facials, and once revealed she had been 'stung by bees on purpose'.

He nighttime skincare regime costs £600.
