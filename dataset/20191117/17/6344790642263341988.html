There Are a Ton of Cool Easter Eggs in The CREEPSHOW Series and This Video Points Them All Out

17 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Joey Paur

Greg Nicotero’s horror anthology series Creepshow was a lot of fun to watch and it was loaded with a ton of Easter Eggs, some of which you might have missed while watching the show. There are a lot of Stephen King Easter eggs along with Day of the Dead, Halloween, A Nightmare on Elm Street, Evil Dead, The Shining, Texas Chainsaw Massacre, and more.

Thanks to a video that was released by AMC’s streaming service Shudder, you can see every Easter egg that was packed into this show.

If you haven’t watched the first season of Creepshow yet, you should check it out! The series was so well received that Shudder has renewed it for a second season.
