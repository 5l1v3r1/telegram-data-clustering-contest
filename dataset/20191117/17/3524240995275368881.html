No ad hoc staff missing in Kogi —INEC

17 Nov 2019, 17:01 by Agency Report

The Independent National Electoral Commission has said that none of its ad hoc staff is missing in Kogi, where governorship and senatorial elections had held on Saturday.

There had been claims that some ad hoc staff of the commission were abducted to enable politicians rig the election and falsify results; but INEC said the reports were untrue.
