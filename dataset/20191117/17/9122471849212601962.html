Paul Finebaum’s Message For Alabama QB Tua Tagovailoa

17 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Matt Hladik

Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa suffered a dislocated hip in yesterday’s win over Mississippi State, ending his season and presumably his college career.

The devastating injury broke the hearts of Crimson Tide fans and immediately brought concern for Tagovailoa’s football future. Hopefully, he will still be able to achieve his NFL goals.

This morning, ESPN radio host and SEC media personality Paul Finebaum shared a message of support for Tagovailoa on Twitter.

“As dawn has broken across the nation this Sunday morning, a desperate feeling of devastation for @Tuaamann has turned to one of hope for his health and recovery. #PrayersforTua,” Finebaum tweeted.
