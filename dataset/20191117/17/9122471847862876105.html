Penn State QB Sean Clifford Has A Message For Ohio State

17 Nov 2019, 17:16 by Tzvi Machlin

Penn State recovered from last week’s road loss to Minnesota with a solid home win over Indiana. That win sets up a high-stakes showdown with Ohio State next week with a spot in the Big Ten Championship Game potentially on the line.

Sean Clifford is ready for Ohio State. Following the 34-27 win over the Hoosiers, the Nittany Lions signal caller had a message for the Buckeyes.

Speaking to a reporter after the win, Clifford called his team “very, very hungry” to beat Ohio State next week.

“We are not done, because we have one loss, and we are very, very hungry.”
