What channel is Cowboys vs. Lions on today? Schedule, time for Week 11 NFL game

17 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Eli Boettger

The Cowboys are back on the road in Week 11, looking to pick up their first NFC North victory of the season after dropping earlier games this season to the Packers and Vikings. Detroit is up next, hoping to gain some traction after dropping five of its last six games.

The Lions will have to do it without quarterback Matthew Stafford, who missed the team's loss to the Bears last week with a back injury. Jeff Driskel, who went for 269 yards with a touchdown and an interception in Week 10, will start in Stafford's absence.

Given the upper hand at the quarterback position, Dallas (5-4) has a golden opportunity to pick up a win over Detroit (3-5-1) and maintain pressure on the Eagles in the NFC East division race.

Below is all the info you need to watchCowboys vs. Lions on Sunday, including the TV channeland kickoff time.

What channel is Cowboys vs. Lions on today?

  • TV channel (national): Fox
  • TV channel (Dallas): KDFW
  • TV channel (Detroit): WJBK

Kevin Burkhardt (play-by-play), Charles Davis (analyst) and Pam Oliver (reporter) will be on the call for Cowboys vs. Lions.

For those who can't get to a TV and wish to find Cowboys vs. Lions on the radio, the Dallascall can be heard on Sirius XM channel 385, and the Detroit call can be heard on Sirius XM channel 226.

Cowboys vs. Lions: What time is kickoff?

  • Date: Sunday, Nov. 17
  • Start time: 1 p.m. ET

The 1 p.m. ET kickoff will be the Cowboys' third such kickoff and the Lions' seventh so far this season.

As for nationally-televised matchups, Dallas will take on the Bills on Thanksgiving (4:30 p.m. on CBS) and the Bears on Thursday night on Dec. 4 (8:20 p.m. on Fox/NFL Network).

Detroit, meanwhile, also plays on Thanksgiving against the Bears (12:30 p.m. on Fox).
