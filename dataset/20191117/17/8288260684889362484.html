'Correction' - Wolves fans all saying the same thing about Cristiano Ronaldo

Wolverhampton Wanderers latest | There were four Wolves players involved as Portugal secured their Euro 2020 place - and Diogo Jota came so close to his first international goal

17 Nov 2019, 17:07 by Josh Murray

Wolverhampton Wanderers fans are having their say after Cristiano Ronaldo's 99th international goal helped Portugal secure their qualification for next summer's European Championship finals.

The reigning European champions won 2-0 away to Luxembourg in group B to make sure they will be in the running to defend their title in 2020 - and there were a quartet of Wolves stars on the field by the end of the game.

Rui Patricio played the full 90 minutes in goal, while Ruben Neves, Joao Moutinho and Diogo Jota all came off the bench.

It was Jota's involvement that got Wolves supporters talking in the immediate aftermath, as the forward came close to registering his first goal for Portugal's senior side with a close-range chance, only for captain Ronaldo to tap the loose ball home from barely a yard out in the 86th minute.

 

That came after Bruno Fernandes' 39th-minute opener.

Here is what has been said on social media as Jota came agonisingly close to his debut international strike...
