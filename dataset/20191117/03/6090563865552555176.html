Pictures: Moyo, Kasukuwere fleeing Chiwenga coup, In Mozambique

17 Nov 2019, 03:49

Exiled former Zimbabwe government minister and G40 kingpin Jonathan Moyo has shared some photos of his great escape during the November 2017 coup.

Sharing the pictures on Twitter Moyo shared the sentiments below:

Sight after crossing Ruya River into Mozambique. At this time #TrevorNcube was lying on & to readers of his newspapers that we were under arrest at KGVI barracks. The other gentleman is a Mozambican. Details are in a coup story, yet to be told!

Moyo and Kasuwere were targeted by security forces after General Chiwenga’s soldiers overthrew Mugabe’s government in a bloodless coup.
