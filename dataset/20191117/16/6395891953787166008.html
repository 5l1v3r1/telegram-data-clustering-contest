Injured bald eagle rescued when driver finds it in ditch by south Georgia road

17 Nov 2019, 16:21 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

VALDOSTA, Ga. - An alert motorist helped rescue an injured bald eagle that was lying in a roadside ditch in south Georgia, authorities said.

Lowndes County Animal Services Officer Jeff Atkison said he was sent to an area near the Florida state line after a motorist reported the disabled bird, the Valdosta Daily Times reported.
