Arkle Trophy Trial Novices' Chase report: Put The Kettle On causes surprise

17 Nov 2019, 16:43 by Matt Brocklebank

The Henry De Bromhead-trained mare Put The Kettle On won the Arkle Trophy Trial Novices' Chase at Cheltenham under a fine, front-running ride from Aidan Coleman.

The outsider of the four runners (6/1) took them along in the testing conditions at Prestbury Park and she ground out success from the Nigel Twiston-Davies-trained 7/4 market leader Al Dancer by two and a half lengths.

The strong-travelling Getaway Trump, representing champion trainer Paul Nicholls, made a late mistake which knocked the stuffing out of him and he once again failed to fully convince with his finishing effort up the hill.

Harry Cobden's mount finished a couple of lengths ahead of Harry Whittington's Rouge Vif in last.
