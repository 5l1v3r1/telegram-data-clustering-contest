Media centre at school fest

17 Nov 2019, 16:38 by Staff Reporter

Poet P.K. Gopi will open the media centre set up as part of the Kozhikode Revenue District School Festival at BEM Higher Secondary School here on Monday.

Over 12,000 students are expected to take part in the festival beginning on Tuesday. The official proclamation rally heralding the event will begin from the BEM school at 4 p.m. on Monday.

A flash mob by students will be part of the rally.
