Glow in the dark Christmas bedding is available to buy for less than £13

This would make a lovely festive gift

17 Nov 2019, 16:01 by Entertainment Daily

A Christmas-themed duvet set that glows in the dark is now available to those who like to curl up in the festive snug.

And it can be yours for less than £13. The adorable bedding is available from Terrys Fabrics.

A single set is available for just £12.99 and a double can be purchased for £18.99.

The design show's a cosy Christmas village covered in snow, which is lit up as soon as the lights are switched off.

Glow in the dark seems to be a very hot trend, especially when it comes to bedding.

Budget friendly store B&M are also selling glow in the dark bedding - but this time it is a fleecy unicorn themed version.

Their bright pink set goes for just £15 and is also available covered in stars.

Only available in single size it is described as: "Wool-like, sherpa fabric offers a luxuriously soft feel while the duvet's reverse features fleece material, keeping kids warm and snuggly on chilly nights."

Meanwhile even Nutella is getting in on the glow in the dark action!

The beloved hazelnut chocolate spread is offering a limited collection of jars you can find even when the lights are switched off.

Each jar depicts a different Christmas scene and also impress as an ornament.
