Abington Police searching for missing 51-year-old man 'at risk'

17 Nov 2019, 16:54 by EndPlay

ABINGTON, Mass. - Abington police are searching for a man who's been missing since Friday.

Herbert Jones, 51, was last seen at 893 Brockton Ave at 10:30 p.m. on Friday. It's unknown what he was wearing.

He's 5-foot-6 and roughly 216 pounds. Police said he's an "at risk" person, and he goes by the name Herbie.

They also said he doesn't have any family in the area. He is known to struggle with mental health and police said he can't take care of himself without help.

If you have information that can assist in helping safely locate Herbert "Herbie" Jones, call 911 or contact the Abington Police Department at (781) 878-3232.
