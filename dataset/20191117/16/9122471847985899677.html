ESPN’s Kirk Herbstreit Releases His New Top 6 Poll

17 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit has updated his personal rankings following Week 12 of the 2019 regular season.

There hasn’t been too much of a shuffling in Herbstreit’s rankings, as most of the country’s top teams won on Saturday.

Two undefeated teams – Baylor and Minnesota – both lost in Week 12, though, and the Golden Gophers have since fallen out of Herbstreit’s top six.

Here’s his latest poll:

  1. LSU
  2. Ohio State
  3. Clemson
  4. Georgia
  5. Alabama
  6. Oregon
