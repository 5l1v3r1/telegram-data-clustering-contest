Luke Combs Breaks Records While Landing His First No.1 Album On Billboard 200

17 Nov 2019, 16:47 by Jamie Samhan

Luke Combs has just gained his first No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 chart.

The country star released What You See Is What You Get on Nov. 8 and by the week ending on Nov. 14, the album had 172,000 equivalent album units in the United States.
