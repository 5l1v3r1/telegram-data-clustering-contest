Hamilton not bothered if he never gets knighted!

17 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Adam Newton

Six time F1 world champion Lewis Hamilton says that he is not bothered if he never receives a knighthood for his career, saying that he doesn't think it is ever going to happen. Hamilton is by far the most successful British F1 driver, and won his sixth title last week.

The 34-year old has an MBE but believes that he will never receive the ultimate honour of a knighthood. F1 drivers such as Jackie Stewart, Jack Brabham and Stirling Moss, but Hamilton is not confident of becoming the next addition to that list, despite his incredible achievements.
