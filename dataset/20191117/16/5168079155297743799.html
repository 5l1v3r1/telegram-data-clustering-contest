Ministry of Housing to construct six new mosques, five schools in Oman

17 Nov 2019, 16:20 by Times News Service

Muscat: Sheikh Saif bin Mohammed Al Shabibi, Minister of Housing has signed a number of housing agreements in Liwa and Dima Wa Al Tayeen which include mosques, schools and housing units.

A statement published online by Oman News Agency (ONA) said: “The Minister of Housing signed four agreements, including the construction of two huge mosques, six mosques and five schools in the new residential area of Liwa in North Al Batinah Governorate and the construction of 74 housing units in Dima Wa Al Tayeen in North Al Sharqiyah Governorate.”
