I'm a Celeb's Adele Roberts's Big Brother journey including 'Am I minging?' row

17 Nov 2019, 16:21

Radio One’s Adele Roberts is heading into the I’m a Celebrity jungle tonight but this is far from her first rodeo in reality TV.

The star actually got her breakthrough thanks to her appearance in the third season of Big Brother, which by all accounts might be the most legendary season the show ever created, back in 2002.

And she actually proved to be behind some of the most notable moments of the series history.

Moving into the Elstree compound with a bunch of unknown names at the time, Adele and a whole heap of her castmates are now TV royalty.

Among the bunch? Jade Goody, Alison Hammond and Kate Lawler.

The Southport native proved to be a winner within the house to begin with, as one of the OG castmates that were fighting for the prize money.

In particular, the boys of the house found her attractive, and she even engaged in a flirtation with Lee Davey for a while.

However, that came to an abrupt end with major moment number one – coming out as bisexual on national telly.

While that might not be a big deal now, it definitely was 16 years ago, as Adele had to let Lee down gently, revealing that she was bi and had a girlfriend on the outside of the house.

Lee took the rejection on the chin, and the pair became tight friends regardless once she’d come out to him, remaining close throughout their time on the show.

However her relationships weren’t always smooth sailing, which comes to major moment number two: her massive fallout with Jade.
