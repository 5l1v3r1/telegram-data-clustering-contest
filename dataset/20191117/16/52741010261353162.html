Man arrested after robbery in Waterloo

Police say he tried to take keys from a man early Sunday morning

17 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Ben Eppel

A quick police response and a citizen standing up for themselves has led to an arrest.

Regional Police say a 74-year-old man was leaving his car around 1:00 a.m. Sunday morning in the area of Erb Street East and Moore Avenue when a 27-year-old man tried to take his keys.

The 74-year-old resisted and called for help.

Police say the suspect was quickly arrested.
