Tarsha Whitmore is showing off both her fashion sense and her killer figure in a red hot new snapshot. As those who follow her on social media know, Tarsha has a huge fan base, boasting over 640,000 fans on Instagram alone. The model travels all over the world and posts photos from her trips, but in a sizzling new snapshot, the model found herself back home in Australia.

In the caption of her double-photo update, Tarsha told fans that she was starting to shop early for Christmas gifts, but she actually ended up buying a few things for herself. The model could be seen striking a pose outside against a blue-colored building. Whitmore looked nothing short of stunning, wearing her long, brunette locks down and slightly waved for the occasion. The bombshell also rocked a gorgeous application of makeup that included eyeliner, mascara, blush, highlighter, and lipgloss.

Tarsha’s figure was on full display in a pair of tiny Daisy Dukes that showed off her toned and tanned stems. The model paired the look with a low-cut white tank top that she wore tucked into her shorts. Her top offered generous views of cleavage, and Tarsha completed the look with a white clutch and a pair of matching white sneakers. In the second image in the series, the model wore the same outfit, but the picture was taken at a closer angle.

The post has only been live on her page for a short time, but it’s already earning the brunette beauty a ton of attention from her fans, racking up over 15,000 likes in addition to 150-plus comments. Some followers took to the comments section to let Tarsha know that she looks stunning, while countless others raved over her killer figure. A few more fans chimed in to let the model know that they were also starting their Christmas shopping early.

“You are perfect from head to toe,” one of Whitmore’s fans wrote, adding a purple heart emoji to the end of the comment.
