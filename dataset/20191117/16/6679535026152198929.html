Survey for highway

17 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Staff Reporter

Revenue and Housing Minister E. Chandrasekharan has said all work on the Kanhangad-Panathur highway, including the survey, will be completed by December.

Addressing a meeting convened by District Collector D. Sajith Babu here on Sunday, the Minister said the Deputy Director of Survey was entrusted with the task of identifying the Poramboke and encroached land and remeasuring the land boundary required for the road.

The panchayat presidents, who attended the meeting, assured the cooperation of the public for the land survey. The Minister sought the completion of the survey without delay.
