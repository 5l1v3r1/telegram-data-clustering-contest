C9 Entertainment's new girl group 'C9 GIRLZ' reveals all 7 members

17 Nov 2019, 16:37 by olmal

C9 Entertainment has revealed all 7 members of their upcoming girl group, 'C9 GIRLZ'. The group name is tentative until the official name is revealed.

Seven members are Jeewon, Semi, Chaesol, Sunn, Belle, YeAh, and Seline.
