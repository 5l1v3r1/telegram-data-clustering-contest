Florida deputies recover 15 kilos of cocaine that washed up on beach in Palm Coast

17 Nov 2019, 16:04 by Lindsey Nolen, ActionNewsJax.com

PALM COAST, Fla. - Deputies in a northeast Florida county recovered 15 kilograms of cocaine that washed up on a beach Friday, authorities said.

The Flagler County Sheriff's Office received an anonymous call Friday at 7 p.m. about a duffel bag that washed up on the beach in Palm Coast, Action News Jax reported.

The duffel bag was covered in barnacles and seaweed and contained 15 individually wrapped packages, the Sheriff's Office said in a Facebook post. The bag also contained a large amount of sand and shells, indicating that it had been in the ocean for some time.
