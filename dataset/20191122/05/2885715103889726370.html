Sanju Samson reacts to T20Is snub on Twitter, fans criticise selectors

Sanju Samson scored a double-century in Vijay Hazare Trophy 2019 and played a match-winning innings against South Africa A earlier in the year which led to his selection in the T20I team.

22 Nov 2019, 05:49 by Sports Desk

Sanju Samson was excluded from India’s T20I squad announced on Thursday for upcoming home series against West Indies starting December 6. Samson took to Twitter on Friday and posted a ‘smiley face’ instead of any text reacting to his exclusion. The 25-year-old did not get to play a single game in the recently-concluded three-T20I series against Bangladesh.
