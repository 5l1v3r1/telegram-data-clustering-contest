Media: New Zealand jury convicts man of murdering British backpacker

22 Nov 2019, 05:09

SYDNEY, Nov 22 — A jury in New Zealand convicted a 27-year old man today of murdering a British woman backpacker, media reported, ending a case that has shocked the Pacific nation.

Grace Millane, 22, who was travelling after finishing university, went missing in Auckland on December 1 last year. Police found her body on December 9 in bushland just a few metres from a scenic drive in Auckland’s Waitakere Ranges.

The man, whose name has been suppressed by the court, met Millane on the evening of December 1 through dating app Tinder, the jury heard. He pleaded not guilty to murder, arguing she died accidentally during consensual sex.

The jury rejected the argument, convicting the man just hours after beginning deliberations, Television New Zealand reported. — Reuters
