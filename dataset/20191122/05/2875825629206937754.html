Ant and Dec say I'm A Celebrity stars 'stink and one is nicknamed cheese fruit'

The I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! hosts have revealed that the current crop of campmates are among the smelliest that have had on the ITV show

22 Nov 2019, 05:38 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant and Dec have admitted that the current crop of I'm A Celebrity campmates are among the smelliest they have had in the jungle.

Show hosts Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly were speaking on I'm A Celebrity spin-off show Extra Camp with former campmates Emily Atack, Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas.

The Geordie duo revealed that with a gas stove replacing the real fire in the camp, there was no disguising the smell of body odour that was the backdrop to the camp.

Ant, 44, started off by saying that it wasn't so bad this year before changing his mind after listening to comedian Joel Dommett having his say.

Ant said: "The difference this year is that we have the gas stoves in there, no smoke. They don’t stink as much."

Joel interjected: "They smell of B.O. instead because they are all smelling themselves and they stink but you don’t normally smell yourself because you have the fire on the whole time."

Ant agreed with this and went on to say that a Bushtucker Trial with Roman Kemp and Adele Roberts was particularly notable for the campmates odour.

He revealed: “At the trial with Roman and Adele, whoo, I am not saying what one but one of them honked."

Dec then chimed in, saying: “One of them has got the nickname cheese fruit."

While a cheese fruit is not something that many in the UK get to smell, reports online suggest that the taste is like "rotten cheese."

It changes as the fruit ripens, going from a almost palatable "spicy and grassy, with hints of horseradish and parmesan," to its ripened state, when it takes on the taste of "sharp cheese, lemon and vomit."

After discussing the smell in camp, talk turned to the new arrivals, actors Andy Whyment and Cliff Parisi.

Ant caught his TV partner out by referencing that he took time away from the spotlight to get sober, leaving Dec momentarily speechless.

The Extra Camp hosts were discussing with Ant and Dec what impact the new arrivals will have and Emily suggested that Coronation Street star Andy was "like all of my mates."

She went on to suggest that she would "like to have a pint with him," to which Ant quickly replied in: 'Or a coke!'

Dec was halted mid-sentence at Ant's canny nod to his drink driving arrest and the stint in rehab that followed.

After laughing at Ant's comment, Dec was able to regather his thoughts and carry on with his thread.
