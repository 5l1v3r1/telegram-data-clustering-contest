AEW Rumors: Marty Scurll not WWE bound; headed to All Elite Wrestling or NJPW

22 Nov 2019, 05:54 by Karan Bedi

A member of The Elite could be coming home to AEW. According to WrestleTalk, Dave Meltzer of WON is reporting that Marty Scurll is deciding between two promotions and neither of them is WWE. AEW or NJPW will be the likely destinations with All Elite Wrestling being the most likely choice.

For a long time, Marty Scurll made frequent appearances on Being The Elite and still featured in the opening credits of the show on YouTube. When AEW started, Scurll was on a different contract than the rest of his Elite brethren, who all went to AEW.

They even made a sketch on Being The Elite about it, when neither Cody or The Young Bucks came to Scurll's new years party and he got intoxicated by himself.
