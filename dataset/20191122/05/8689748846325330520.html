‘Deadly Spirits Are Controlling The Entertainment Industry’ – Patapaa

22 Nov 2019, 05:29 by BB Jones

Following his near death experience, Patapaa has boldly claim the music industry is controlled by evil spirits.

According to him, artists in the industry need to be watchful because there are spirits that run the affairs of the industry who are bent on causing havoc to them.

He indicated that he has come to the realization that there is the need to be spiritually fortified as an entertainer because the industry is no joke considering the kind of things that go in there in the spiritual realm.

Patapaa made this comment after sharing his experience on how he nearly got poisoned by some around him.

On October 28, news went viral that ‘One Corner’ hitmaker, Patapaa has been hospitalised after being in a critical condition just some minutes before a show.

According to the report, Patapaa was supposed to perform at a show the previous day but was quickly sent to St. Martins de Porres hospital just some few hours to his performance at the Kundum Show at Atuabo.
