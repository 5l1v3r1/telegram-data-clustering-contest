China approves imports of wheat flour for feed from Kazakhstan

22 Nov 2019, 05:24

BEIJING, Nov 22 (Reuters) -

* China's customs office said on Friday that it has approved imports of wheat flour for feed from Kazakhstan

* The flour eligible for imports must be free from harmful pests, including Tilletia indica Mitra and Alternaria triticina, the General Administration of Customs said in a statement on its website

* Beijing has already allowed imports of corn, barley and rapeseed meal, also feed ingredients, from its central Asian neighbour (Reporting by Hallie Gu and Dominique Patton; editing by Uttaresh.V)
