Today is 25 Years Since the Sega Saturn Released in Japan

22 Nov 2019, 13:30 by Brian Ashcraft

Today is the 25th anniversary of the Sega Saturn. The console first went on sale on November 22, 1994 in Japan, but didn't come to Europe until 8th July 1995. The console wasn’t a smash hit, but did produce classics like Panzer Dragoon. However, a Sonic game was not released on the console.
