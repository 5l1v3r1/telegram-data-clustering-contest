The Killers 2020 tour: Fans furious after tickets for sell-out Falkirk show appear online for FIVE times original price

22 Nov 2019, 13:38 by Paul Smith

FANS of The Killers are furious after tickets for their Falkirk performance sold out and have appeared online for FIVE times the original price.

The American rockers are coming to Scotland next summer to kick off a UK stadium tour.

Those who were lucky enough to get their hands on tickets are starting to sell them on sites like Viagogo.

And some are going for almost £600.

Tickets officially went on sale this morning at 9am, priced £60.50 - but are being re-sold for much more.

Posting on Twitter, Mrs Broony Said: "Can someone tell me why this is allowed? Blatantly ripping off fans.

"So many genuine fans like myself desperate to get tickets to see the killers in Falkirk stadium only for it to be sold out in seconds & reselling for triple the price on sites like

viagogo worse than touts".

Some people have also expressed outrage at being held in queues for more then 15 minutes only to find tickets had sold out.

Another said: "Gutted not to get tickets for Killers for Falkirk or Bristol - was on ticket master before 9am - how did it end up like this!

"Will have to console myself with an armchair disco tonight blastin Killer’s tunes".

We've contacted Ticketmaster and Viagogo for comment.
