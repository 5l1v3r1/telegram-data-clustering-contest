Bigg Boss 13 Weekend Ka Vaar: Salman Khan Set To Interact With Umar Riaz, Akansha Puri, Parag Tyagi, Rannvijay Singh

22 Nov 2019, 13:36 by Amruta Patel

Salman Khan has started shooting for the Weekend Ka Vaar episode in Film City now, as we write about it. But the fun is going to double up in the house as Salman Khan will interact with Asim Riaz’s brother Umar Riaz, Akansha Puri -Paras Chhabra’s GF, Parag Tyagi- Shefali Jariwala’s husband and Rannvijay Singh

This picture shared on the Twitter handle of BiggBoss_Tak assures that the weekend episode of Bigg Boss 13 will be high on entertainment. But this time there will be equal amount of entertainment inside as well as outside of the house on the sets of Weekend Ka Vaar. Well, not keeping it suspense anymore, Salman Khan will be seen interacting with near and dear ones of the Bigg Boss contestants in weekend episode currently being shot in Film City, Mumbai.

The Twitter handle tweeted a picture of vanity vans with the nameplates of Umar Riaz, brother of Asim Riaz, Akansha Puri- Paras Chhabra’s girlfriend, Parag Tyagi- husband of Shefali Jariwala and host-actor Rannvijay Singh, pasted on the doors of the vanity vans. Wooh! Their tweet read, “Lo Bhai Tumhse na hopayga.....Bhaiyo or Behno ye dekho with Proof Vanity Van k Bhar se Exclusive Pics” Well guys we hope this enough for you to catch up the upcoming episode of Weekend Ka Vaar.
