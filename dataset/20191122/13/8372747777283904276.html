Lee County 17-year-old girl reported missing from Gateway area

22 Nov 2019, 13:32 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- Police are looking for a Gateway teenager who has been reported missing and may be in the company of two homeless men.

According to Fort Myers Police Department, 17-year-old Danielle Jacob was last seen in the Gateway area, headed to Lehigh Acres.

Danielle is 5'2" tall and 150 pounds, with brown hair and green eyes.

She is known to be in the company of two men believed to be homeless, named Gerald and Raymond. Their last names are unknown.

Gerald is a black male, approximately 20-30 years old, over 6' tall and lean.

Raymond is a white male with dark hair, approximately 5'5" tall.

Anybody with information on Danielle's whereabouts is asked to call Fort Myers Police at 239-321-7700.
