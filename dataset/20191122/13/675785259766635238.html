French Montana in hospital after cops visited house and saw him acting strange

French has been admitted to an LA hospital for reportedly suffering stomach pains, nausea and an elevated heart rate after police called him an ambulance

22 Nov 2019, 13:06 by Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

French Montana is reportedly is hospital after cops called him an ambulance.

Officers in the Calabasas area, where French lives, are said to have responded to a call about a possible robbery at the rapper's home.

It was a false alarm - but it's reported the officers noticed something wasn't right.

French - real name Karim Kharbouch - is said to have been acting abnormally, with sources close to French telling TMZ that he'd been experiencing stomach pains, nausea and an elevated heart rate.

A source also told the website that he was admitted to San Fernando Valley Hospital, and that close pals reckon he may have been exposed to contaminated food.

The rapper's internationally renowned and respected, and his latest bout of travelling saw him jetting all over Europe and the Middle East.

French is known for a huge range of songs including Famous and No Stylist, in which he raps alongside Drake.

He's also noted for his dating past, which includes none other than Khloe Kardashian .

It was thought that earlier this year, Khloe turned to her ex following her split from NBA player and father of her child, Tristan Thompson.

A source told Hollywood Life: "Khloe has been leaning on her ex French in the last few months.

"French is someone that Khloe has always been able to trust and talk about things."

They added: "Khloe always felt that French had her best interests at heart and will forever respect him and what he has to say.

"It's too soon to see if sparks will ever fly again between the two as it isn't out of the realm of possibility, but Khloe needs him more as a friend right now and they are both OK with that."

The rapper made some serious waves when he claimed he could have saved the life of Mac Miller, a fellow rapper who was found dead at his home last year from a drug overdose.

He said: "I feel like I have people that if I do something like that, like how I was to him, like a big brother, more like, 'You bugging me out', he didn't have that around him.

"So if I did it that night, if I'd been around him a couple more nights, I would've made him stop, but he didn't have nobody who was doing that."
