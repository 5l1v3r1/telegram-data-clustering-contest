Ihop's Elf on the Shelf Menu For the Holiday Season Is Basically a Kids’ Dream

22 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Alessia Santoro

Your Elf on the Shelf may not have made its first appearance yet this year, but tell your kids they'll still want to make sure they're on the nice list — IHOP is going to have an Elf on the Shelf-themed menu filled with delicious items for your whole crew. From fluffy green Jolly Cakes topped with cream-cheese icing to a delectable hot chocolate topped with marshmallow syrup, whipped cream, and "elf sprinkles," everything on this menu is a serious treat. A bonus? Kids under 12 eat free through the holiday season with the purchase of an adult entrée between the hours of 4 and 10 p.m.

Ahead, get a peek at the menu, which is available now through Jan. 1, 2020, at participating IHOP locations.

Elf on the Shelf Jolly Cakes

Order the Jolly Cakes and get four green pancakes topped with sweet cream-cheese icing, whipped cream, and shimmery elf sprinkles, which resemble elf-size ornaments. The Jolly Cakes are also available as a side dish.

Elf on the Shelf Holiday Ham & Sausage Omelette

Order the Elf on the Shelf Holiday Ham & Sausage Omelette and get a fluffy omelette filled with ham, sausage, peppers, onions, hash browns, jack cheese, and cheddar cheese. It's topped with more diced ham and sausage, cheddar cheese, and white cheddar cheese sauce and served with your choice of side or three pancakes.

Elf on the Shelf Oh What Funnel Cakes

Order the Oh What Funnel Cakes and get two golden, crispy funnel cakes lightly dusted with powdered sugar and topped with glazed strawberries, whipped cream, and elf sprinkles.

Elf on the Shelf Little Elves Combo

The littlest elves can order the Little Elves Combo, which comes with the choice of one Jolly Cake or one Oh What Funnel Cake with one scrambled egg, one bacon strip, and one pork sausage link.

The Entire Elf on the Shelf Menu

The menu also includes the Merry Marshmallow Hot Chocolate with marshmallow topping and elf sprinkles!
