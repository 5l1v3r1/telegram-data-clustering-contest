Mahesh Babu Inspires Chay-Sam?

22 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Manaswini K

Superstar Mahesh Babu is one actor who always dedicates time to the family. He takes his family on a holiday on a regular basis despite his busy schedule. It looks like he inspired Naga Chaitanya and Samantha as well in this aspect. The couple is also going on frequent holidays to celebrate every moment in their lives.

Now, they flew to Europe for celebrating Naga Chaitanya’s Birthday (November 23rd) and will be back on December 3rd. Naga Chaitanya has completed his part in Venky Mama and is shooting for his next with Sekhar Kammula which is tentatively titled ‘Love Story’.

Venky Mama is likely to release on December 13th. The makers of Love Story are planning to release the First Look of their film on 23rd. On the other side, Samantha is yet to make public her next project after Oh Baby. She is making her digital debut with Amazon Prime Video’s The Family Man.

She will appear in the second season of the Web Series and is shooting for it. Rumors are that she will be seen as a Terrorist in the film.
