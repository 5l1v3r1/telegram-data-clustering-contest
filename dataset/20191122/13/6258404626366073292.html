Microsoft's Surface Laptop 3 Enjoys $300 Discount In Early Black Friday Deal

22 Nov 2019, 13:14 by Brandon Hill

The ink is barely dry on the release announcement for the Surface Laptop 3, but Microsoft is already piling on the discounts for the ultra-portable. In this case, Microsoft is providing a big discount on the 256GB SKU of the 13.5-inch Surface Laptop 3.

The Surface Laptop 3 typically starts at $999 for 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage. The next step up includes a 256GB SSD and is priced at $1,299. However, with this latest discount, Microsoft is knocking $300 off the price, making the 256GB version the same price as the entry-level model. The discount applies to all five available colors: Sandstone, Black, Cobalt Blue (w/Alcantara palm rest), Platinum, and Platinum (w/Alcantara palm rest).

Microsoft's 13.5-inch Surface Laptop 3 in this configuration comes with an Intel Core i5-1035G7 processor, which is based on 10nm Ice Lake architecture. It also uses Gen11 Iris Plus integrated graphics.

The Surface Laptop 3 has a 13.5-inch 2256x1504 PixelSense Display, support for the Surface Pen, and weighs just 2.84 pounds.
