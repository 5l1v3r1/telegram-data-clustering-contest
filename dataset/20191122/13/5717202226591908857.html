Ole Gunnar Solskjaer comments on Nemanja Matic liking post suggesting Tottenham move

22 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Will Butcher

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer has been quoted by Sky Sports as saying that Nemanja Matic has a sense of humour after the Manchester United man was caught commenting on a post that suggested he could join Tottenham Hotspur.

Matic, 31, is in the final year of his contract at Old Trafford after being signed by Mourinho at both Chelsea and then at Manchester United. The Serbia international cost United a whopping £40million (Telegraph) in 2017.

With Mourinho joining Tottenham, fans immediately suggested that the Portuguese gaffer would turn to Matic for a third time – especially with the ex-Benfica star’s £120,000-a-week (Spotrac) contract set to expire at the end of the season.

Renowned football account Benchwarmers uploaded a jokey post suggesting that Mourinho is waiting to target Matic in January, and the United midfielder responded – immediately suggesting that their is a chance of a reunion at Spurs.
