Toke Makinwa accuses BBnaija fans of causing more harm than good for ex housemates

22 Nov 2019, 13:06 by Damilola Ismail

OAP Toke Makinwa has called the attention of fans of BBNaija to stop causing harm to the former housemates and putting pressure on them.

Toke Makinwa took to her social media page to address the issue saying the BBNaija fans are causing the former housemates to constantly be in competition which could cause depression and is in no way good for the people they call their favorite housemates.

She wrote;

Been so long since I aired my thoughts on social issues cos I’m busy building my empire but you BBrothernaija fans really need to stop tripping and putting so much pressure on your favourite house mates…

You guys are causing more harm than good, stop putting them against each other, the show is over let them focus on building their lives , this type of constant competition causes depression, just quit already

You can support your favourite housemate outside the house without fighting and insulting yourselves or other housemates, get your own lives on and leave them to figure this new phase, we are all tired

The industry is hard as it is, they are not yet even in the industry, all the noise does not bring success, it doesn’t help anyone. Ask the other housemates, some of them had to start from scratch, let people focus and do what they need to do to really succeed
