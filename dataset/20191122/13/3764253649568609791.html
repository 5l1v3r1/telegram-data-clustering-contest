Kirill Tereshin: Russian 'Popoye' shows off reduced biceps after surgery

22 Nov 2019, 13:47

Kirill Tereshin, known as Russian 'Popoye', who recently underwent surgery is recovering well after the procedure.

The 23-year-old MMA fighter known for his huge biceps was said to have taken out about six liters of Vaseline during operation.

Pictures of the MMA fighter went viral across most social media around the world after injecting the substance into his veins as reported by Sport Bible.

Afterward, his biceps became too big that people likened him to one of the old popular cartoon characters 'Popoye'.

The effect of the Vaseline caused his arms to deteriorate and eventually got paralyzed. It was then he decided to listen to the doctors.
