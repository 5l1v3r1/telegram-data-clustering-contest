'BJP will win 15 Assembly seats in Karnataka bypolls'

22 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Gururaja B R

Large and Medium Industries Minister Jagadish Shettar exuded confidence that the BJP will win all the 15 Assembly segments in the bypolls. 

Speaking to reporters here on Friday, he said that by winning all the seats, the BJP government led by B S Yediyurappa will become further stable and will complete its full term. 

"Rebellion is common in all parties. But, it is temporary in the BJP," he told.
