Bogotá clashes and a Brexit manifesto: Friday's best photos

22 Nov 2019, 13:18 by Compiled by Preeti Bhambri

The Guardian’s picture editors select photo highlights from around the world

London, UK

Nigel Farage at the launch of the Brexit party’s ‘Contract with the People’ election manifesto

Photograph: James Veysey/Rex/Shutterstock

Nakhon Pathom province, Thailand

Pope Francis greets Catholics in St Peter’s church. The pontiff is in Thailand for an apostolic visit for the 350th anniversary of the founding of Mission de Siam. He is the first pope to visit the country in nearly four decades

Photograph: Diego Azubel/EPA

Christchurch, New Zealand

The Prince of Wales greets members of the public during a visit to Christchurch Cathedral, on the sixth day of a royal visit to the country

Photograph: Chris Jackson/PA

Mexico City, Mexico

Men ride horses down Paseo de la Reforma during a parade marking the 109th anniversary of the Mexican revolution. More than 1,000 participants dressed in period clothing rode through the streets of the capital after re-enacting scenes from the revolution in the city’s main square

Photograph: Rebecca Blackwell/AP

Surif, West Bank

Palestinian demonstrators pray as Israeli troops stand guard during a protest against Jewish settlements in the Israeli-occupied West Bank

Photograph: Mussa Issa Qawasma/Reuters

Bogotá, Colombia

Protesters clash with the Mobile Anti-Disturbances Squad. President Iván Duque’s government had ramped up security before the marches amid fears Colombia could join the wave of violent protests sweeping other Latin American countries

Photograph: Juancho Torres/Anadolu Agency via Getty Images

Johannesburg, South Africa

A bodybuilder does his early morning workout at Hillbrow boxing gym

Photograph: Kim Ludbrook/EPA

Mediterranean Sea

People fish on the NGO Proactiva Open Arms migrant rescue boat

Photograph: Juan Medina/Reuters

Caracas, Venezuela

A university student holds a chain with his body painted during a protest against the country’s president, Nicolás Maduro

Photograph: Yuri Cortéz/AFP via Getty Images

Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir

A Kashmiri man rides a boat in the waters of Dal Lake as it rains in Srinagar

Photograph: Tauseef Mustafa/AFP via Getty Images

Hong Kong

A stuffed toy depicting Pepe the Frog, a character used by pro-democracy activists as a symbol of their struggle, next to a helmet and charred glass bottles at the Polytechnic University in Hung Hom. Protesters held their ground in a campus besieged for days by police as the US passed a bill lauding the city’s pro-democracy movement, setting the scene for a clash between Washington and Beijing

Photograph: Dale de la Rey/AFP via Getty Images

Yarzeh, Lebanon

A special forces officer salutes the Lebanese flag during a military parade to mark the 76th anniversary of Lebanon’s independence from France at the defence ministry, near Beirut. Lebanon’s top politicians attended the parade, appearing for the first time since the government resigned amid nationwide protests

Photograph: Hassan Ammar/AP

Naypyidaw, Myanmar

Aung San Suu Kyi attends a political conference

Photograph: Thet Aung/AFP via Getty
