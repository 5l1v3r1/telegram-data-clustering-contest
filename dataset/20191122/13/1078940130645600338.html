President Arif Alvi & PM Imran Khan felicitate Lebanon on Independence Day

22 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Mati

ISLAMABAD, Pakistan: President Dr Arif Alvi and Prime Minister Imran Khan have felicitated the President of Lebanon Michel Aoun and Prime Minister Saad Hariri respectively on the auspicious occasion of Independence Day of Lebanon.

In their separate congratulatory messages on Friday, the president and the prime minister stressed that Pakistan-Lebanon bilateral relations were based on common faith and shared traditions and values.

The president and the prime minister expressed their resolve to further deepen these cordial ties in all spheres.

The Lebanese Independence Day is the national day of Lebanon celebrated annually on November 22 in commemoration of the end of the French Mandate over Lebanon in 1943 after 23 years of Mandate rule.
