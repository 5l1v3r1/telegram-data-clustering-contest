Sanju Samson dropped: Kerala wicket-keeper Sanju Samson was left out of India’s T20I squad announced on Thursday for upcoming home series against West Indies.

Written By Jatin Malu | Mumbai | Updated On: November 22, 2019 13:02 IST

Kerala wicket-keeper Sanju Samson was left out of India’s T20I squad announced on Thursday for upcoming home series against West Indies starting December 6.
