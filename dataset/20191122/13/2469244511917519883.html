Pensioner disturbs three burglars in own home stealing 'sentimental' jewellery

Police now want to speak to the men in these pictures

22 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Colleen Smith

The elderly occupant of a house challenged three daylight burglars who had smashed open a front door and were helping themselves to jewellery 'of significant sentimental value'.

Detectives have now released photographs of three men who they want to speak to in connection with their investigation into the burglary.

It happened in Kingsdown Crescent in Dawlish between 12.30pm and 1pm on July 17.

Police have now released CCTV images of three men.

A spokesman for Devon and Cornwall Police said: "Police would like to speak with the men pictured in connection with a burglary which occurred on 17 July 2019 between 12.30pm and 1pm at an address in Kingsdown Crescent, Dawlish.

"Three males have gained entry to a property in Kingsdown Crescent causing damage to the front door.

"Once inside they were disturbed by the elderly occupant who challenged them.

"The three males have made off with items of jewellery which were of significant sentimental value.

"If you have information in regards to these persons please email   16862@devonandcornwall.pnn.police.uk  and quote crime reference CR/063969/19."
