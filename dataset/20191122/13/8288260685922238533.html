'I've said it' - The injury update Aston Villa fans have been waiting for

Aston Villa v Newcastle United | Jack Grealish has missed his side's past three matches in all competitions with Monday's night's clash with Newcastle United looming

22 Nov 2019, 13:47 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa head coach Dean Smith has offered an injury update on Jack Grealish ahead of Monday night's Premier League clash with Newcastle United (8pm).

Grealish has missed the past three matches in all competitions for Villa after suffering a calf injury in the 3-0 defeat to Manchester City in October.

He sat out a Carabao Cup win over Wolverhampton Wanderers and Premier League losses to Wolves and Liverpool.

Before he picked up his injury he was in fine form, netting in back-to-back wins over Norwich City and Brighton and Hove Albion.

He has been a big miss for Smith and his Villa side, who have lacked his creative flair in midfield.

It's no coincidence that his side have lost two straight games without him.

"Jack's been training with us, but I am a bit ill-at-ease to say he's been available because I've said it the last couple of weeks and he's not played, such has been his calf injury," Smith said.

"But he's trained with us for the last week so I have no doubts he will be there and in his happy place at Villa Park playing in front of the fans."
