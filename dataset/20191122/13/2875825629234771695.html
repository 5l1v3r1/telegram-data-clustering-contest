Joe Swash feared he'd sexually harassed Stacey Solomon with awkward first kiss

Joe Swash admitted he'd lunged at Stacey Solomon during her time in Australia - and worried his boss would punish him for harassing her

22 Nov 2019, 13:42 by Irishmirror.ie

Joe Swash was so worried about the way he first kissed Stacey Solomon that he thought he might get fired.

The former EastEnders star made an ill-advised lunge at his now-girlfriend while they were both out in Australia for the 2015 series of I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!, where Joe was presenting the spin-off show.

And while the pair had known each other for a few years, Joe wasn't sure his flirting attempts were going down well with her.

Speaking to Kate Thornton on the Yahoo podcast White Wine Question Time, Joe confessed how badly he'd misjudged their first kiss.

"Stacey was only there (in Australia) for four days. So I was thinking: 'I've got four days. I've only got four days'. And we got to the last day, nothing, she hadn't given me the scent or anything…" he recalled.

"I thought, 'Right!' It was her last night in the apartment before she went, so I thought I'd knock on the door and I thought, 'I've seen it on films before, I'm just going to go in and kiss her'. It weren't like that, it were nothing like that."

Stacey, laughing, said: "It was nothing like in the films, Kate. I almost felt like I was being attacked. He said bye, and I said bye, then I shut the door and he knocked again, so I opened the door and he literally went…" [Mimes sudden head movement forward.]"

Joe went on: "So basically I was just kissing teeth, as I was doing this I was just thinking, 'I've really misread this situation'.

"Then she went home and I got a text from her. I can't remember what the text said, but I think it was like: 'Sorry I didn't kiss you back. It was really nice.'

"I was expecting a call from my boss saying, 'Have you been sexually harassing Stacey Solomon?' And I thought, 'Aw, I've got a problem here, Joe.'"

Once they'd established Stacey was happy to be kissed, the pair's friendship quickly flourished into romance - after she'd spelled out exactly what she wanted.

"I didn't want to be too available!" Stacey went on.

"When you've got kids, you feel the need to set it out so like, I have two kids, a cat, a dog and a really big mortgage, do you still want to go out with me.

"You've got to be upfront. 'Here's my Ts&Cs - let me know if you fancy it'."

The couple moved in together last year, bringing their blended family - Joe's 12-year-old son and Stacey's boys Leighton and Zachary - under one roof.

And they welcomed their baby son Rex into the world earlier this summer.

Cheeky Joe even asked for a puff of Stacey's gas and air while she was busy pushing.

"Every time I got it she growled, 'don't you put your germs on there'," he said sheepishly.
