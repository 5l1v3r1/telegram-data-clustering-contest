Shatta Wale angry replies enemies who asked about his Grammy nomination

22 Nov 2019, 13:09 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Dancehall artiste Shatta Wale has sent an epic response to some people on social media believed to Sarkodie fans over his rant over the 2020 Grammy nominations.

But some fans of his enemies are finding it difficult to accept the fact that the ‘Gringo’ hitmaker was able to chop that success.

Shatta Wale tweeted stating the finally his name has made it to the Grammy’s thanks to the Beehive team.

His tweets generated a lot of comments on his timeline with some mocking him as to how his name made it unto the list but his reply to the comments was epic.
