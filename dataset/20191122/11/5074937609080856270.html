Kumi adjudged YEA Youth Photographer of the Year

22 Nov 2019, 11:07 by Simon Asare, GNA

Accra, Nov. 21, GNA - Samuel Kumi has retained the Photographer of the Year accolade at the Youth Excellence Awards (YEA).

The third edition of the awards seeks to recognize hardworking future leaders for their dedication and achievements in their respective fields.

According to the 24 year-old, known as "Ace Photographer", he was delighted to win the award for the second successive time and would be a great motivation for him.

''To win the award for the second time shows how far I have come and I want to thank the organizers for recognizing my efforts.

''I'm grateful to the management of Ace Photography for their support of the past years and we shall certainly move to greater heights,'' he said.

The Ace Photography brand under Martcarl Entertainment, promotes works of Ghanaian acts and well known top photography brand.
