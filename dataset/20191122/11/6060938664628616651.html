Pink Ball Test: Legends relive glorious Eden Gardens moments

22 Nov 2019, 11:44 by PTI

Highlights

  1. Tendulkar, Kumble, Harbhajan and Laxman relived some of the iconic moments at the famous venue
  2. All of them thanked their captain and BCCI president Ganguly for bringing them together
  3. The Kolkata crowd showed all their love for Tendulkar by chanting 'Sachin Sachin'

KOLKATA: Indian cricket legends including Sachin Tendulkar and Anil Kumble on Friday revisited their glorious on-field moments at the Eden Gardens, the venue for the country's first ever day-night Test.

In a special programme during the lunch break on the opening day of the India-Bangladesh pink-ball Test, Tendulkar, Kumble, Harbhajan Singh and VVS Laxman relived some of the iconic moments at the famous venue, including the 1993 Hero Cup final versus West Indies and the 2001 Test versus Australia.
