Stacey Solomon unveils glitzy tree as she prepares for baby Rex's first Christmas

The Loose Women panellist couldn't contain her excitement as she decorated her home in Christmas spirit ahead of her first year as a family-of-five

22 Nov 2019, 11:50 by Tiffany Wallis

Stacey Solomon has revealed her glitzy Christmas tree after she decked her home with festive decorations ahead of the season.

The Loose Women star, 30, shared a snap of her towering Christmas tree dressed with shimmering silver baubles, pine cones and fairy lights to her 2.5 million Instagram followers on Thursday.

Showing the magic of the festive season had reached every corner of her swanky abode, the TV icon told her fans that she "couldn't wait any longer" as it is "Rex's first Christmas."

The star shared a series of clips to her Instagram story showing how her gorgeous home has been transformed into a winter wonderland.

She wrote: "The tree is up. I couldn’t wait any longer. Sorry to anyone who thinks it’s far too early but I just had to put the tree up today.

"It’s Rex’s first Christmas and I want it to last as long as possible! Rex thinks it’s wonderful, he’s had a sensory OVERLOAD today and keeps chuckling at it."

The star transformed her home to exude extravagant Christmas spirit and played an array of iconic Christmas songs in the background as she decorated with the help of her sister.

She added: "I love this time of year sooooo much and I am so glad I get to stare at this for the next 6 weeks."

Stacey filmed the whole process as she assembled the tree before decorating it with a sea of sparkling decorations and pine cones.

She also showed off a special, customised bauble that she had purchased to make baby Rex's first Christmas memorable.

The former X Factor star shared a glimpse of the moment Rex saw the dazzling tree.

The adorable little one can be seen giggling and attempting to touch the tree in one clip as Stacey wrote "the pickle loves it".

Baby Rex looked excited as he admired the glamorous tree, while Stacey also joked she'd been arguing with her sister over what to put on the tree.

Stacey also unveiled a small wooden tree hung on the wall so the boys could get creative and pick out their favourite decorations when they arrived home after school.

This year marks Stacey and her long-term beau Joe Swash's first Christmas as a family-of-five.

The loved-up couple have been together since 2015 and recently welcomed their first child together Rex six months ago.

Stacey was already mother to Zachary, 11, and Leighton, seven, when she met Joe - and he also has a son, Harry, 11, from a past relationship.

Despite being excited to experience their first Christmas with their new family set-up, Stacey recently insisted they won't be walking down the aisle any time soon.

During a chat on Loose Women this week, the telly star revealed she was in no rush to marry Joe and said they feel like "everything else comes first."

Stacey and Joe were also recently forced to squash rumours that they secretly tied the knot during an idyllic family holiday to the Maldives.
