Lionel Messi's gift to German football's promising young talent

Youssoufa Moukoko turned 15 and Leo has sent him a present

22 Nov 2019, 11:58 by Sport EN

Lionel Messi has given Borussia Dortmund's young talented star Youssoufa Moukoko a nice present.

The German - from Cameroon background - is a big fan of the Blaugrana star and Messi sent him a gift for his 15th birthday.

Moukoko showed it off via his official Instagram account.

"Oh my god! Got this as a gift from my idol. Many thanks, Leo Messi. Thanks for all your birthday wishes," he wrote on the social network site.

Moukoko will be in Barcelona on Wednesday to face Juvenil with Borussia Dortmund in the Youth League match before the first teams meet later on.
