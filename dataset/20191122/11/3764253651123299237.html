CAF best XI: Uzoho, Ndidi, Iwobi and Ighalo shortlisted for 2019 award

22 Nov 2019, 11:10

- CAF best XI nominees have been shortlisted with four Super Eagles stars making the list

- Francis Uzoho, Wilfred Ndidi, Alex Iwobi and Odion Ighalo are the four Nigerian players

- The CAF best XI will be announced in a ceremony on January 7, 2020

Four Super Eagles players who played for Nigeria at the 2019 African Cup of Nations in Egypt have been shortlisted for the 2019 Confederation of African football award best XI.

They are goalkeeper Francis Uzoho, Wilfred Ndidi, Alex Iwobi and striker Odion Ighalo who won the Golden Boot award at the championship.

Odion Ighalo scored five goals at the 2019 African Cup of Nations where he helped Nigeria to win bronze medal at the tournament.

Sadly, the former Watford striker announced his retirement from international football after the Nations Cup in Egypt.
