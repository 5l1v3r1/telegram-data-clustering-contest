Surrey RCMP asking for help finding teen sisters missing for over 2 weeks

22 Nov 2019, 11:37 by Elana Shepert

Surrey RCMP is asking the public for help locating two missing girls who haven’t been seen for over two weeks.

According to RCMP, Elizabeth and Denisa Stana, who are sisters, were last seen at noon on Nov. 7 in the 8600 block of 192nd Street in Surrey. Since the, they haven’t been seen or heard from.

Elizabeth STANA is described as a 15 year old Romanian female, 5’5″, 88 pounds, with black hair and brown eyes, last seen wearing a black jacket.

As it stands, both police and family are concerned for these youths’ health and well-being.

Densia STANA is described as a 12 year old Romanian female, 5’3″, 99 pounds, with blond hair and brown eyes, last scene wearing a white jumper and white t-shirt.

Anyone with information about the whereabouts of these youths is asked to contact the Surrey RCMP as 604-599-0502, or Crime Stoppers, if they wish to remain anonymous, at 1-800-222-8477 or www.solvecrime.ca, quoting file number 2019-180851.
