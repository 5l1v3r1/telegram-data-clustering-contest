Listen To Marilyn Manson’s New Cover Of The End

Marilyn Manson has taken on The Doors’ The End, and revealed an accompanying music video.

22 Nov 2019, 11:31

Not for the first time in the past couple of months, Marilyn Manson has unveiled a new cover song – a brilliant take on The Doors’ 1967 classic The End.

The rendition follows The God Of Fuck’s cover of American folk song God’s Gonna Cut You Down, and comes with an accompanying music video based on one of his own original watercolour paintings.

Fans who attending Manson’s tour with Rob Zombie in the U.S. over the summer will have already heard The End being played over the PA, but now it’s not only available to hear in all its glory – it’ll also be available as a limited-edition picture disc from March 6 next year.
