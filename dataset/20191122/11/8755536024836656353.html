The line-up for The Graham Norton Show is AMAZING

We're definitely tuning in tonight!

22 Nov 2019, 11:22 by Kendra Becker

The line-up for The Graham Norton Show tonight is seriously star-studded.

First up, Ricky Gervais will be on the show to chat about the success of his Netflix series After Life.

The comedian and actor will also be joined by Australian pop legend Kylie Minogue.

The singer will have plenty to talk about as she performed at Glastonbury for the first time over the summer, 14 years after she was forced to cancel her 2005 performance after being diagnosed with breast cancer.

Actress Elizabeth Banks will be on the red couch too, following the release of her Charlie’s Angels reboot.

And finally, British F1 driver Lewis Hamilton will also be in studio.

The 34-year-old will be on the show before heading to Abu Dhabi for his final race of the season on December 1.
