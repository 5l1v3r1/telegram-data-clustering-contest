A body of a 47-year-old man was found in DLF Mall of India in Noida on Friday. The body was found on the terrace of the Sector 18 mall. The deceased, identified as Bhuvan, was an employee of the PVR Cinemas.

The police and the forensic teams have reached the incident site. A probe has been initiated in the case.

Speaking with India Today TV, Noida SP Vineet Jaiswal said there was an injury at the back of the deceased's head.

"The man has been identified as Bhuvan, a PVR cinemas employee. There is an injury at the back of his head. The forensic and the police teams are present, probe on," he said.
