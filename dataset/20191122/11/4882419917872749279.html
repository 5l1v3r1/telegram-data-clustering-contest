Advance: Luis Suarez - The numbers speak for themselves

The Barcelona forward spoke to Sport in an interview coming out on Friday

22 Nov 2019, 11:58 by sport.es

Barcelona forward Luis Suarez spoke to Sport in an exclusive interview that will go out on Friday. The Uruguayan, a football legend, analysed the current situation of the club, speaking about Gerard Pique, Lionel Messi and Antoine Griezmann, as well as other topics related to Barcelona.

He was at a presentation of new Puma boots when he conducted his interview with Sport.
