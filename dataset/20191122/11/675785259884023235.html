Jane Austen's EMMA movie 2020 release date, cast, trailer, plot for new adaptation

Anya Taylor-Joy will take on the iconic titular role of Emma in the latest adaptation of the classic Jane Austen novel - but who else stars in the film?

22 Nov 2019, 11:07 by Lewis Knight, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lewis-knight/

Oh we do love a fresh revamp of a classic piece of literature.

The latest adaptation of Jane Austen's classic romantic comedy Emma is on the way, with Peaky Blinders actress Anya Taylor-Joy taking on the role in the new film from director Autumn de Wilde in her debut feature film.

Naturally, the new film has an all-star British cast to act in the new period piece, with the characters coming together to orbit the handsome, clever, and very rich Emma Woodhouse.

But just when can we catch this latest adaptation of Emma?

Here is what you need to know about the 2020 release of EMMA.

Jane Austen's EMMA movie 2020 release date

EMMA is released in UK cinemas on February 14, 2020.

A perfect romantic-comedy for Valentine's Day, then!

Jane Austen's EMMA movie cast

Anya Taylor-Joy as Emma Woodhouse

The Peaky Blinders star plays the beautiful, intelligent, and meddling Emma, who loves to matchmake and interfere in the affairs of others.

Jane Austen described her in the opening lines as "Emma Woodhouse, handsome, clever, and rich."

Johnny Flynn as George Knightley

The star of Beast portrays Emma's considerate older neighbour and chief critic, who also owns the estate of Donwell Abbey.

Bill Nighy as Mr Woodhouse

The veteran British actor plays Emma's hypochondriac father who gets on well with his daughters.

Mia Goth as Harriet Smith

The Suspiria actress portrays Emma's beautiful but unsophisticated younger friend.

Miranda Hart as Miss Bates

The comedian plays the part of spinster who is friends with Mr Woodhouse and is mocked by Emma.

Josh O'Connor as Mr Elton

Currently playing Prince Charles in The Crown, O'Connor plays the well-mannered and ambitious vicar who begins to pursue Emma despite her plans to matchmake him.

Callum Turner as Frank Churchill

A wealthy and immature young man, portrayed by Glue star Callum Turner.

Rupert Graves as Mr Weston

The Sherlock star plays the wealthy and optimistic businessman who is the father of Frank.

Gemma Whelan as Mrs Weston

The Game of Thrones actress plays Emma's former governess and confidante who married Mr Weston.

Amber Anderson as Jane Fairfax

The star of The Riot Club plays the talented and well-educated orphan who is the one person that Emma is jealous of.

Tanya Reynolds as Mrs Elton

The Sex Education actress plays the well-bred but pretentious wife of Mr Elton.

Connor Swindells

The Sex Education star also has a role in the film.

Jane Austen's EMMA movie plot

The official plot synopsis of the adaptation is below.

Jane Austen’s beloved comedy about finding your equal and earning your happy ending, is reimagined in this delicious new film adaptation of EMMA. Handsome, clever, and rich, Emma Woodhouse is a restless queen bee without rivals in her sleepy little town. In this glittering satire of social class and the pain of growing up, Emma must adventure through misguided matches and romantic missteps to find the love that has been there all along.

EMMA is released in UK cinemas on February 14, 2020.
