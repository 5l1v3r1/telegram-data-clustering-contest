Aima Baig refused an offer to feature on 'Bigg Boss 13'

22 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Entertainment Desk

In a recent turn of events, singer Aima Baig has revealed that she received a call from India to participate in Bigg Boss 13. However, she refused the offer due to the controversial nature of the show.

Aima appeared on a recent online web show and made some confessions about her personal and professional life. She started off by talking about rumours surrounding her pictures with her friend Shahbaz Shigri to being invited to participate in Bigg Boss 13.
