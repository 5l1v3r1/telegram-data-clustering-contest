EU complains to WTO over Indonesia export curbs on raw materials

22 Nov 2019, 11:44

BRUSSELS, Nov 22 (Reuters) - The European Union has launched a complaint at the World Trade Organization against Indonesian export restrictions on raw materials used in the production of stainless steel, the European Commision said on Friday.

EU Trade Commissioner Cecilia Malmstrom said in a statement that Indonesia had maintained its measures, despite the EU's efforts, and had even announced a new export ban for January 2020. (Reporting by Philip Blenkinsop; editing by Francesco Guarascio)
