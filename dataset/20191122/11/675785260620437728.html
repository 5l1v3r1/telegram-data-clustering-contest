Chilling tape and violent threats almost destroyed Jacqueline Jossa's romance with Dan Osborne

Dan Osborne's life crumbled after the release of a sickening tape in which he was heard threatening to 'stick a knife in' the mother of his son

22 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Louise Randell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-randell/

Jacqueline Jossa and Dan Osborne 's relationship was almost destroyed by a bombshell tape in which he threatened to "stick a knife in" his ex-girlfriend.

The pair got together in 2013 after Dan left his partner, Megan Tomlin, when she was four months pregnant with their first baby.

Megan later gave birth to a son named Teddy, and by 2014, Jacqueline was pregnant with Dan's next child, a daughter named Ella.

However, Jac and Dan's romance almost came crashing down when tape recordings of Dan threatening to stab Megan were leaked.

The shocking audio recordings had Dan telling Megan, "it won't be funny when you're in hospital" and calling her a "dirty little c***" and a "slag".

He also threated to "stick a knife in" her if he found out she'd had sex with another man.

When Megan pleaded that she'd not bedded anyone else, Dan raged back - telling her to "shut your f*****g mouth".

He also referenced his budding romance with Jacqueline in the tape, calling it a "mistake" and insisting he wasn't still with her.

It was this clanger that particularly stung the EastEnders star when she later listened to the recording.

A police investigation into Dan's threats was launched, but was later dropped.

The tape is believed to date from November 2014, around the same time Dan was arrested on suspicion of assault following a blazing row with Megan at her Essex home.

He was released without charge.

The fall out from the tape led to Dan being fired from his job on Towie, and prompted him to issue

In a statement, Dan said: "It has taken me a while to find the right words but I would like to say that I am truly sorry to anyone who was offended by what they heard.

"I am unable to talk about the difficulties with my current family situation but I am taking steps to resolve it to achieve the best outcome for my son.

"I am ashamed and embarrassed that I allowed myself to reach such a dark place that I behaved in a way that even I didn’t recognise."

In March 2015, months after the recording was leaked, Megan branded Dan "controlling" and said his behaviour had destroyed her self esteem.

"If you haven't experienced it, it's hard to explain how much a person can get inside your mind and control how you feel," Megan said.

"Of course, I was in love with Daniel and I was embarrassed to speak out about what was happening. I didn’t want other people to criticise him and part of me hoped he would change."

She went on: "He tried to make me believe that the situation between us was my fault.

"You can hear this on the tape, when Daniel tells me I've broken up the family, despite the fact he moved on when I was four months pregnant – causing a considerable amount of strain and stress during such a precious time – only to then return once our son was born and leave again when he was four months old."

Jacqueline leapt to Dan's defence after Megan's interview , tweeting a long ode to her boyfriend.

"People selling stories on him, show one side to a story, but people around Dan know the whole truth, and you would all be shocked," she wrote on March 31 2015.

"I can assure you I wouldn't stand by a man capable of everything dan has been made out to be.

"He is my best friend, boyfriend… and he is a brilliant father, above all else, I just want to clear up, he has never once been violent or even shown signs towards me at all."

She added: "I am 100 per cent not saying what Daniel has said was right or okay

"But people make mistakes when on dark places, and it's something that we sorted out privately not in front of the whole world.

"It's something he has apologised for and is embarrassed about."

Jacqueline also told OK! magazine that she thought Dan's threats were "disgusting" - and admitted she was really hurt when she heard him call their relationship "a mistake" on the recordings.

"I listened to them and I was completely shocked by what I heard - I thought it was disgusting but it's something that we've worked through as a couple," she said.

"Daniel has lost out on countless work opportunities because of it and he's had to deal with everyone passing judgement on him, so I think he's been punished enough."

She added at the time: "It caused a lot of arguments between us but he explained that he was only saying what he thought he had to in order to see Teddy.

"I don't think I'll ever forgive him for what he said but at least I understand his reasons why."

They couple managed to get their relationship back on track after the bombshell tape, marrying in 2017 and welcoming a second daughter.

However, the romance was later rocked by a series of scandals, including 2018 allegations linking him to reality stat Gabby Allen .

Dan denied cheating on his wife after growing close to Gabby while they filmed a TV show in Spain.

After getting back together again, the couple were rocked by more cheating allegations earlier this year when Dan was accused of snogging Love Island's Alexandra Cane on a night out in Manchester.

Dan denied the claims and managed to patch up his relationship with Jacqueline once again.
