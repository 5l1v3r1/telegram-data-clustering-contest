Check out the first picture of Neha Dhupia and Angad Bedi’s daughter Mehr

22 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Filmfare

The first pictures of Neha Dhupia and Angad Bedi’s daughter, Mehr, are finally here. The duo recently celebrated Mehr’s first birthday by paying a visit to the Golden temple. They later visited Angad’s ancestral home in Putlighar in Amritsar. Neha Dhupia and Angad Bedi had earlier shared pictures of their daughter but they never revealed the little one’s face. But Angad’s father, ex-cricketer Bishan Bedi,  took to the internet to share pictures of the cute little baby on the couple’s visit to his haveli.

He posted a picture of the family where Angad is seen carrying Mehr in a baby carrier and mommy Neha is in an all-black attire smiling for the camera. He captioned the picture as ‘’MEHR seems to approve of her presence in the dilapidated ‘Haveli’ of her great grandparents. Maybe the renovation of the old relic is around the corner..!! Thanks fellas for visiting my birth place. Guru MEHR Kareh”.
