Sending deadlines for Christmas presents

22 Nov 2019, 11:43 by New Zealand Post

Please be advised that NZ Post has released the sending deadlines if NZers want to ensure their Christmas gifts are under the tree in time.

For domestic parcels the deadline is in just under one month.

You can view the full deadlines at this link, with permission to publish: https://cloud.youcan.nzpost.co.nz/key-sending-dates
