A third season of The Young Offenders has been confirmed

They've already shot scenes for the next season

22 Nov 2019, 11:54 by Kendra Becker

A third season of The Young Offenders has been confirmed.

Fans of the hit series will be delighted to know that Alex Murphy, Chris Walley and Hilary Rose have signed up for yet another season.

Writer and director Peter Foott told Chortle: “We are so grateful to BBC Three and thrilled that they had such faith in the stories and characters of The Young Offenders that they commissioned a third series from us.”

“We can’t wait for everyone to see more of Conor, Jock, Mairéad and all the characters next year in our third series.”

“We’ve been really amazed at the huge reaction to series two so far, and we’re so happy that we can officially announce that there’s more on the way for fans of the show.”

The second season of the hit series, which is currently airing on RTE2 and BBC One, has down a hit with viewers in the UK and Ireland.
