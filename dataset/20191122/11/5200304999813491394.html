Overnights: Star Plus stays ahead on Thursday in UK

22 Nov 2019, 11:09 by Raj Baddhan

Star Plus remained top in UK ratings with ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ keeping the channel high, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Plus delivered a share of 0.12%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television with 0.10%, New Vision TV with 0.06%, Star Bharat and ZEE Cinema with 0.05%, Hum TV, PTC Punjabi and Colors Rishtey with 0.04%, Colors and Dunya News with 0.03%, Sony MAX 2, Geo News, Colors Cineplex, B4U Movies and Hum Masala with 0.02%, Geo TV with 0.01%.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohabbatein’ was watched by 71,000 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 44,600 viewers, ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 39,100 viewers, ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 39,000 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 29,600 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 21,500 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 21,400 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ at 22:00 with 14,000 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 9,700 viewers and ‘Namah’ below 1,000 viewers.

On Sony TV, ‘Mere Sai’ was watched by 37,500 viewers at 19:00, followed by ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 33,700 viewers, ‘Mere Dad Ki Dulhan’ at 22:00 with 32,700 viewers, ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 with 31,300 viewers and ‘Tara From Satara’ at 22:30 with 20,400 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Kun Faya Kun’ led with 23,500 viewers at 20:00, followed by ‘Tum Mere Hi Rehna’ at 21:00 with 18,300 viewers and ‘Malaal-e-Yaar’ at 22:00 with 12,600 viewers.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ dropped to 18,100 viewers at 21:30, followed by ‘Choti Sarrdaarni’ at 19:30 with 9,100 viewers and ‘Shakti’ at 20:00 with 8,700 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ topped ratings with 24,900 viewers at 18:30, followed by ‘Bhabhi Ji Ghar Par Hain’ at 14:00 with 15,300 viewers and ‘Happu Ki Ultan Paltan’ at 14:30 with 14,600 viewers.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
