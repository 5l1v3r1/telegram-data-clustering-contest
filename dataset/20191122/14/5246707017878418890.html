Gallery: Here's A Wee Look At Wargroove's Delightful Deluxe Edition

Getting physical with Chucklefish's strategy classic

22 Nov 2019, 14:45 by Damien McFerran

Until a new Advance Wars appears, Chucklefish's superb Wargroove is here to scratch your tactical itch on Switch – and it does that brilliantly.

Previously a digital release, a physical 'deluxe' version is now available – courtesy of Sold Out – which packs in some neat bonus items, and Chucklefish was kind enough to send us a sample to show off you lovely people. It's the first in-house game the company has made to get the physical release treatment, so you can forgive them for feeling a little bit chuffed.

The package includes:

  • Cartridge copy of the game
  • Downloadable version of the Wargroove Soundtrack by Phonetic Hero and Boss Battle Records
  • Commander sprite sticker sheet
  • Poster map of Aurania
  • Mini Strategy Manual with stats & tips on all units (available in English, French, Italian, German & Spanish)
  • A reversible coversheet
