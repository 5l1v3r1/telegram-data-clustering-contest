Ant McPartlin reminds Emily Atack of sobriety after pub remark on Extra Camp

22 Nov 2019, 14:13

Ant McPartlin poked fun at his sobriety during last night’s I’m A Celebrity: Extra Camp after Emily Atack cracked a joke about going for a drink with newcomer Andy Whyment.

Andy was joined by fellow soap star and former EastEnders actor Cliff Parisi last night and won us all over as a die hard superfan of the show.

The presenter was famously absent from last year’s I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! and replaced by Holly Willoughby after he was charged with drink-driving in March 2018.

Ant subsequently pulled out of all TV duties for the remainder of the year.

However, the 44-year-old is now even making light of the disaster live on air during the ITV2 spin-off show.

When The Inbetweeners actress explained she’d love ‘to go for a pint’ with Andy, Ant reminded the 29-year-old that he can’t.

‘Or a coke,’ he chipped in, before Declan Donnelly burst out in laughter.

Fans have been delighted to have Ant and Dec back together in the jungle, although ideally they’d love to see two become three and have Holly back on the series too after she not only made the perfect stand-in, the This Morning host actually boosted I’m A Celebrity viewing figures to record heights.

But the real star of the series has to be Caitlyn Jenner, who has been leaning all the Girls Aloud dance moves, spilling the Kardashians tea and even having a pop at soap stars when she’s currently living with three of them.
