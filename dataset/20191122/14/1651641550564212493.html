Samsung Galaxy S11+ may still feature a radical design

22 Nov 2019, 14:40 by Surur

We have heard recently from reliable leaker Ice Universe that the Samsung Galaxy S11 will feature a Note 10-like near borderless design, but be otherwise relatively conventional.

This news is rather disappointing when other Asian OEMs are pushing the envelope with waterfall screens and under-screen cameras.

Now Ice Universe has thrown us a bit of a rope, as he revealed the Samsung Galaxy S11+ will, in fact, have a different design to the S11.
