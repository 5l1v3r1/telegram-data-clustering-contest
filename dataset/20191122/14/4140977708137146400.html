His job entails not just forming the tactics required to win but also keeping his players in top condition, both physically and mentally to get the best out of them and it appears that perhaps the Italian has taken his job description a little too far.

Antonio Conte reveals that he gives instructions to his players on how to have s*x

One of the most heavily debated issue in sports is s*x. There are many, who believe, that indulging in the physical action of love causes before a sporting event causes athletes to underperform. On the other side of the fence, there are also quite a number that making love actually helps them perform better.

Inter Milan manager Antonio Conte is the man who sits between the two. While he hasn’t asked his players to practice celibacy, he does give them advice on how to go on about it,

‘During the season, I advise my players to have sex for short periods and with the minimum of effort, and better use positions where they are under their partners,’ Conte said in an interview with L’Equipe. ‘And preferably, with their wives, because if with others, well, that needs extra action.’

The Former Chelsea manager has won the Serie A thrice. However, he won all of his titles during his time with Juventus. He was appointed manager of Inter Milan earlier in the summer and will hope to break Juventus’ monopoly on the league and win his 4th Serie A.
