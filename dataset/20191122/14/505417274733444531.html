Harding Cup Heads To UCD After Powerful Performance Against Maynooth

22 Nov 2019, 14:59 by The PA Team

UCD  3-0   MAYNOOTH UNIVERSITY –  (November 22nd, Queens University)

By John Hudson

Two set-piece first-half goals from central defenders plus a late strike helped capture the Irish Universities Harding Cup, the first year’s main cup, for UCD with a hard-earned 3-0 win over Maynooth University in the final at Queen’s University Belfast.
