CCC vs JAM Dream11 Match Prediction : Combined Campuses and Colleges Vs Jamaica Best Dream 11 Team Group A Super50 Cup

22 Nov 2019, 14:28 by Gautam Kapoor

CCC Vs JAM Dream11 : Jamaica look to challenge for a spot in the annuls of the points tally with a convincing win against Combined Colleges and Campuses today.

Combined Colleges and Campuses will just be wishing that the ongoing edition of the Super50 Cup comes to an end and with it helps them wrench out of their ordeal, something the side have failed to do on their own. Match after match, it’s been the same sordid story for the outfit with the team languishing and failing to string together even the two meaningful results till now to find themselves firmly planted in the deaths of Group A.

Having won just the lone match in the six matches they have played in till now, the side come into today’s contest having lost four matches in succession. The 9 wicket win against Leeward Islands the last time around perfectly epitomised the peril of the side as they were not only ripped into but saw their gameplan being torn apart and thrown out of the window as they were comprehensively decimated and destroyed in that match.

With the side being curbed for a mere 190 while batting first, their bowling performance was equally dire as Leeward Islands cantered their way to the score at hand with 9 overs in hand. And when they meet Jamaica today, their stinker of a form refuse to seize with the side up against an imperious and formidable opponent.

Where CCC have lost four successive matches, Jamaica on the other hand come into the clash having not lost a match in their last four outings. The side have done extremely well for themselves to recover from what looked to be a fallout after losing their opening couple of matches with the team defeating Barbados by 26 runs as they defended their 331 to lay down a real marker. 

Pitch Conditions And Toss

Chasing under the lights is a tricky task on this wicket with the wicket set to unravel as the match scrimmages, leading the team winning the toss opting to step out and bat first over here. The conditions in the first part of the clash are completely conducive for the bats, something the team winning the toss will be looking to capitalise on.
