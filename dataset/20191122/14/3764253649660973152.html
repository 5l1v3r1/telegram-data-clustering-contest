Lionel Messi sends surprise birthday gift to 15-year-old Borussia Dortmund star Moukoko

22 Nov 2019, 14:26

- Lionel Messi sends a surprise birthday gift to Borrusia Dortmund youngster Moukoko

- The forward who turned 15 took to Instagram to express his happiness after receiving the gift

- Messi has been the teenager's idol but recently copied Ronaldo's celebration after scoring a goal

Barcelona superstar Lionel Messi surprised Borussia Dortmund youngster Youssoufa Moukoko after sending him a gift on for his 15th birthday.

The 32-year-old forward sent a couple of things to the teenage sensation which includes a postal of the five-time Ballon d'Or winner.

Moukoko was surprised after he received the gift from his idol and he took to social media to express his excitement.
