Watch MGMT Debut New Song “In The Afternoon”

22 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Peter Helman

MGMT had a late-career highlight last year with their hooky synth-pop album Little Dark Age. And it seems they have some more new material on the way. Last night, during their show at the Pearl Concert Theater at the Palms Casino Resort in Las Vegas, they debuted a brand new song called “In The Afternoon.” It sounds pretty good!
