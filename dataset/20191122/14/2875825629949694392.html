Jacqueline Jossa's husband Dan Osborne flying to Australia to support her on I'm A Celeb

Former Towie star Dan Osborne has declared he's planning a trip Down Under to see his wife when she gets out of the jungle

22 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Irishmirror.ie

Dan Osborne has declared he's planning a trip to Australia to support his wife Jacqueline Jossa on I'm A Celebrity .

The former EastEnders star, 27, is currently battling it out in the jungle as a contestant on the ITV show and Dan, 28, has been at home watching his wife on the telly every night.

Now he's confirmed he's going to be flying Down Under to support Jacqueline and so he can see her as soon as she gets out.

He told The Sun Online : "I'll be going out there though, not to go into the jungle but to support Jac."

As well as looking after the couple's two daughters, Ella and Mia, while the actress is away, Dan has also been doing some DIY.

He's been busy turning the couple's garage into a gym where he can work out and hopes to have it finished by the time she gets home.

Earlier this week, Dan revealed he's been surviving on takeaways since his wife left for Australia.

He confessed he's not been eating well since Jacqueline left and has been making do with takeaway dinners.

During a video posted on Wednesday night, he said: "Tonight's meal is an Indian so as you can see from the last few days my diet is terrible.

"We're getting a curry."

Dan has been watching the show every night and posting his reactions on social media.

However, this could leave him red-faced if his own antics get brought up on TV.

There's growing speculation that Dan's alleged affair with Love Island star Gabby Allen is going to be mentioned in camp.

Jacqueline is in there with Myles Stephenson, who dated Gabby after she was rumoured to have grown close to Dan backin 2018.

Reports suggest the campmates are going to end up chatting about their love lives - with Dan and Gabby likely to be brought up.

Dan and Jacqueline married in 2017 - but in April the year after, he was spotted getting close to Gabby while they were filming a TV show in Spain.

Both parties denied anything happened, but Jacqueline was furious and even texted Gabby: "Did you sleep with my husband?"

Now it's thought that Myles and Jacqueline will have a talk in camp - and he'll be willing to spill the beans on what Gabby told him.
