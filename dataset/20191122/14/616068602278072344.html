Why Bill Murray's in town

22 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Elana Shepert/Vancouver is Awesome

Curious why Bill Murray was recently spotted in Vancouver with Oscar-winning screenwriter of the Greenbook, director Peter Farelly?

According to Vulture, the comedy legend recently joined the cast of Quibi’s "The Now," which also stars Dave Franco, O’Shea Jackson Jr., Alyssa Milano and Daryl Hannah.

The show, which is set to launch in 2020, has been filming in Metro Vancouver.

Locals have snapped photos from the set, and @lightboxgallery tweeted that, “filming is set to appear The Bill Murray / Dave Franco project #TheNow will be at Westminster Pier Park at 1 6th st in New West this Friday. Night shoot.” They add that crew call is for 3:30 p.m., so the shooting should commence a couple of hours after that. 

The Warner Brothers TV “Batwoman” is also filming tonight in Downtown Vancouver.
