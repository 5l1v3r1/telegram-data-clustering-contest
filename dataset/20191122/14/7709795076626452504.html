Our Weekend Best Bets podcast returns with selections and analysis covering horse racing, football, golf and snooker.

First up it's a weekend of head-to-heads in racing, as Altior and Cyrname clash at Ascot while Bristol De Mai takes on Lostintranslation at Haydock.

Ben Linfoot and Matt Brocklebank sit on opposite sides of both fences, but it's reputations rather than readies on the line with both advising listeners to sit back and watch a pair of mouthwatering races.

Worry not, however, as LinBank do expand the search to reveal their best racing bets before George Pitts takes you through the pick of Saturday's action in the Premier League.

Finally, Ben Coley finds one golfer hard to overlook in Dubai, while Richard Mann can barely hide his excitement ahead of snooker's UK Championship.
