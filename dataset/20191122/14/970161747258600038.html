Chelsea manager Frank Lampard provides Christian Pulisic injury update

22 Nov 2019, 14:47

Frank Lampard is confident Christian Pulisic will feature in Saturday’s heavyweight Premier League showdown between Chelsea and Manchester City.

The America international injured his hip during Chelsea’s win over Crystal Palace prior to the international break and missed USA’s two wins against Canada and Cuba during the international break, as they reached the CONCACAF Nations League finals without him.

‘He didn’t go and we had full on communication with the States, manager Gregg Berhalter and their medical team,’ Lampard said at his pre-match press conference..

‘They want their players for top games. He [Pulisic] had a problem going into Palace. He has trained again this midweek. He is in contention to play.’
