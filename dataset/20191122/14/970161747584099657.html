Does Kim Kardashian have six toes on each foot? Fans obsessed with extra digit

22 Nov 2019, 14:22

Kim Kardashian has become the talk of the internet once more, but this time it’s for having six toes on each foot.

The reality star, 39, has got fans everywhere freaking out over the fact it appears she’s got an extra digit on each of her feet after fans spotted it in recent photos.

While Kim has never addressed the rumours, fans can’t work out whether the toe they’re seeing is actually there or whether it’s a trick of the light.

The sixth toe debate comes up every now and again, as the mysterious digit pops up in her red carpet photos as well as adverts for her product launches.

The latest being this week’s red carpet photos from the American Influencer Awards, where the pesky toe was visible in her red and Perspex heels.

It gained so much attention that nobody was looking at her stunning high-necked red dress with a thigh split and sleek, pulled-back hairdo.
