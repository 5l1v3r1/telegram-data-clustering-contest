First look inside new Sainsbury's Local supermarket in Lyde Green

The store has created 24 jobs

22 Nov 2019, 14:31 by Robin Murray

A new supermarket has opened its doors in South Gloucestershire.

Sainsbury's opened to the public in Lyde Green this morning and will offer residents a range of grocery products and fresh foods.

The 4,024 sq ft convenience store has created 24 new jobs and will play an active role in the local community, according to a spokesperson.

It offers a wide range of fresh fruit and vegetables, dairy products, an in-store bakery and other convenience products.

It also has its own car park for customers to use.

Local residents only had a small bakery in the village to buy their food items from before the launch, however nearby Emerson's Green is home to a huge Sainsbury's superstore.

Jamie Ahearne, Sainsbury’s Lyde Green local store manager, said: "We are delighted to be opening a brand new Local store in Bristol, providing residents and shoppers with a convenient place to pick up their favourite grocery products at great value.

"The new store looks fantastic and we have a great team of colleagues who are really excited to welcome and serve customers."
