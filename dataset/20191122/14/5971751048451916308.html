New Nokia phone coming on 5 December 2019

22 Nov 2019, 14:27 by Marin

Nokia Mobile just teased that the new member of Nokia phones is coming on December 5th.

They shared a video that could suggest a device that will be intended for joggers, or folks that like trail races. The video could also suggest that the device will be able to endure a lot or have the energy to last for more than two days.
