China's Xi says wants to work out 'phase one' trade deal with US

22 Nov 2019, 14:10

BEIJING: China wants to work out an initial trade agreement with the United States and has been trying to avoid a trade war, but it is not afraid to retaliate when necessary, President Xi Jinping said on Friday (Nov 22).

"We want to work for a 'phase one' agreement on the basis of mutual respect and equality," Xi told representatives of an international forum, according to a pool report.
