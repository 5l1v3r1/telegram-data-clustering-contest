Tacha: It’s Time To Cause Another Trouble, Says Zlatan

22 Nov 2019, 14:06 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Controversial rapper Raphael Omolayo better known as Zlatan has revealed that reality star, Tacha blocked him on social media platform, Twitter.

Zlatan made this known while he stated that the last trouble he caused was ‘Kilorun’ audio he released in which he stylishly shaded Tacha.

He however stated that it was time to cause another with the video he would release soon.

The ‘Zanku’ crooner wrote” That Kilorun was the last trouble I caused, time to cause another one with a video I’m about to drop”

In another tweet, Zlatan revealed that Tacha blocked her on the platform as the announcement of the video did not go well with her.

“Seems Tacha blocked me, na only her I sabi for the house sha before she comot” the singer added.

Recall that shortly after Tacha’s disqualification from the pepper dem edition of the show, Zlatan released a diss track for mocking the Instagram queen over ‘body odour’ claims.

The rapper shared a footage on his Instagram handle, where he is seen spraying perfumes and saying “ki lo n run, Tacha ti wole” meaning “what is smelling, is Tacha in”

Meanwhile, Tacha had reacted to the claims, saying she loves Zlatan Ibile despite the singer mocking her with a diss track about ‘body odour.

In an interview with Cool FM, Tacha said “I didn’t see it as anything, I like #Zlatan and I love his music, it’s all love,” she said.
