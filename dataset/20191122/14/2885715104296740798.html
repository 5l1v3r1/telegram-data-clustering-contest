AIIMS PG January 2020 result declared, check merit list

AIIMS PG January 2020 result: Those who clear the exam have to appear for the counselling process on December 2, 2019. Approximately 499 MD/MS seats in Basic Clinical and Clinical Sciences.

22 Nov 2019, 14:37 by Education Desk

AIIMS PG January 2020 result: The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) released the result for the entrance exam conducted to enroll candidates in the postgraduate courses MD, MS, MCh, DM, MDS. The merit list is available at the official website, aiimsexams.org. The AIIMS PG entrance exam was conducted on November 17.

Those who clear the exam will have to appear for the counselling process on December 2, 2019. After which candidates will be allotted seats in postgraduate (PG) courses across AIIMS including AIIMS, New Delhi and six other AIIMS – Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh for January 2020 session.

AIIMS PG January 2020 result: How to check marks online

Step 1: Visit the official website, aiimsexams.org
Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link ‘AIIMS PG result’ under ‘important notice’ (link to be activated by evening)
Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page
Step 4: Log-in using credentials
Step 5: Marks will appear online, download

The printout of the online result will act as a provisional mark sheet. Approximately 499 MD/MS seats in Basic Clinical and Clinical Sciences.

Candidates who are selected through the counselling will have to appear for a medical examination. The medical exam will be conducted by the board. The course will begin on January 1. In case, seats are left vacant, a spot counselling will also be conducted, as per the official notice.
