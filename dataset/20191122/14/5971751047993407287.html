November security patch available for Nokia 7.2

22 Nov 2019, 14:06 by Marin

Nokia 7.2 is receiving the November security patch from Google. The update is also available for devices with the WW_1_200 build number. The size of the update is not so big, just 68.5 MB, and it just brings the security patch.

If you are near your most elegant midrange device, check for the update availability. Do share with the rest of us if you got it.
