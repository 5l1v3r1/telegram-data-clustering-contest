John Lewis Has Discounted Lego, But it's the Bundles That Really Interest Us

22 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Tom Pritchard

Black Friday season is in full swing right now, with deals popping up all over the place. Those deals also include Lego, though so far most of the discounts have been pretty samey and not all that interesting. That said, John Lewis has bundled some stuff together, and that has got our attention.
