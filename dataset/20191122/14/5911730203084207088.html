Polk County Sheriff's Office hosting second annual free turkey giveaway

22 Nov 2019, 14:50 by WFTS Digital Staff

POLK COUNTY, Fla. — The Polk County Sheriff's Office is hosting their second turkey giveaway on Saturday.

For the second year in a row, Polk Sheriff's Charities is sponsoring a free turkey giveaway on Saturday, November 23 at three locations.

East Polk: 9 a.m. to 10:30 a.m. at the Church on the Hill.
