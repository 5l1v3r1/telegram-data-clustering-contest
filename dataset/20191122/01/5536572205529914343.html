DeAndre Hopkins Pulled Off A Crazy Toe-Drag On The Sidelines To Complete Catch (VIDEO)

22 Nov 2019, 01:58 by Darrelle Lincoln

Faced with a 3rd-down, the Houston Texans were looking to be on their way to punt the ball away to the Indianapolis Colts until DeAndre Hopkins had his number called.

While scrambling in the pocket, the shifty WR caught a pass near the sidelines that was ruled out, but a ref came in to change his mind, because Hopkins pulled off an unreal toe-drag to complete that catch.
