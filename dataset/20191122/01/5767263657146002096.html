The British Royal Family Is Looking for a Janitor, and the Salary Is $19,000

22 Nov 2019, 01:30

Searching for a job isn’t that fun unless you come across a job posting from the Royal Family themselves. Yes, that’s absolutely right — you have the chance of working for the Queen and living in one of her castles! This job post from Windsor Castle is definitely worth taking a look at.

We at Bright Side would like to inform you about a very interesting job opportunity. Read below if you would like to know more about the position.

If you’ve ever wanted to become a member of the British royal family, this won’t do it, but it might get you close to them! A few days ago, on their website, a job offer was posted about an opening for the position of Daily Cleaner (and a few others).

The starting salary you could be earning is £14,742 pounds a year (which is around $19,000) including a 15% employer contribution pension scheme. The pay is good and you get to live there for free, but it’s no cakewalk... To be considered, you have to be willing to work 30 hours per week from Monday to Sunday, which could get tough, especially when it gets very busy in the summer. And of course, it’s important to point out that the position is permanent.

If you’re considering applying for the position, you should know what your duties will be. Your responsibilities would include upkeeping, cleaning, and caring for a wide range of interiors and items because they have to be presented at their very best — it’s the Windsor Castle after all! Sometimes you will have to support functions and events as well. You’d be learning from your more experienced colleagues and will acquire all the professional skills needed.

According to the hiring professional, it’s all about working as a team to achieve exceptional results. The job is definitely not easy and will require a lot of dedication and teamwork and there will be no shortage of responsibilities or service to your employers, the Royal Family.

According to former employees who worked at various jobs at Windsor Castle, it helped them improve their interpersonal skills, it led them to meet new people, and some even had access to the Royals, yes you can most definitely meet the Queen!

Free lunch seems to be a theme and we can absolutely understand why. They must make the best sandwiches at the Castle!

You know, these former employees could mention anything under the Pros section, but they choose to mention food. So just imagine how good it must be, seriously.

Any job deserves respect and is important, but it’s hard to beat working for the Royal Family at Windsor Castle...where you also live. We assume the lucky person who gets hired won’t be able to stop bragging to their friends for ages!

Aside from the “royal janitor” position, there a few other job openings that you could check out on their official website here, so if being a janitor isn’t your thing, maybe you can find something else there that is.
