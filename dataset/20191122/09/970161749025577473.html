Bear Grylls's pornstache has got people going wild

22 Nov 2019, 09:40

Bear Grylls unveiled an interesting new look on Good Morning Britain – the adventurer is rocking a serious moustache while doing his bit for Movember.

Viewers couldn’t deal with the ‘70s porn star’ ‘tache, which the survival expert has been growing for the whole of November in honour of Men’s Health Awareness Week.

The money raised by Movember goes to help tackle prostate cancer, testicular cancer, mental health and suicide prevention – so it’s worth Bear looking a bit silly for a few weeks.
