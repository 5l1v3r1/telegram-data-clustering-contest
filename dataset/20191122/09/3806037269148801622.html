The YEEZY 700 V3 “Azazel” Sports Glow-In-The-Dark Details (UPDATE)

The new model is expected to arrive next month.

22 Nov 2019, 09:29 by Mallory Chin

UPDATE (November 22, 2019): After getting a few sneak peeks at the forthcoming YEEZY 700 V3 “Azazel,” it now looks like the anticipated model will sport glow-in-the-dark and reflective 3M detailing. Check out the newly surfaced image below.

So far an official release date and photos have yet to surface, but the YEEZY 700 V3 “Azazel” is expected to land at select adidas retailers on December 23. For more, check out the potential adidas YEEZY December release dates.

Original Story(July 12, 2019): Kanye West has been shining a light on his expansive YEEZY collection as of late, having been spotted earlier this week in what many believe to be the YEEZY BOOST 350 V3 as well as sitting down with Forbes and showcasing his massive range of over 1000 adidas YEEZY prototypes.

This time around West stepped out with his wife Kim Kardashian in Los Angeles sporting a new BOOST iteration. Taking on similar design cues as the YEEZY BOOST 700 and 700 V2, many believe this to be the forthcoming 700 V3. The updated look incorporates an inner bootie system placed within a semi-translucent cage. This all then sits atop a more streamlined midsole compared to the silhouette’s prior chunky look. Additionally, a new graphic print takes over the upper, here seen in a cream and grey colorway.
