tickets for KISS’ 2020 North American tour on sale

22 Nov 2019, 09:28 by Andrew Sacher

Tickets for all dates of KISS‘ 2020 North American tour go on sale today (11/22).

That includes shows in Atlantic City, NJ; Canandaigua, NY; Hartford, CT; Bangor, ME; Mansfield, MA; Allentown, PA; Buffalo, NY; Peoria, IL; Fort Wayne, IN; Los Angeles, CA; El Paso, TX; Atlanta, GA; Raleigh, NC; Tinley Park, IL; Dayton, OH; Milwaukee, WI; Phoenix, AZ; Austin, TX; and many other cities.
