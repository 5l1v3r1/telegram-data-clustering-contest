Team Marjaavaan comes together to celebrate the success of the film

22 Nov 2019, 09:49 by Diya Bachchani

Marjaavaan starring Sidharth Malhotra, Ritiesh Deshmukh, Tara Sutaria and Rakul Preet Singh released last Friday and in a week, the film has done good business at the box-office. Directed by Milap Zaveri the film has equal amounts of romance, action and drama which have connected with the audience even though the critics gave it a mixed response. 

Last night, the makers held a success bash for the film at hotspot in the city. The shutterbugs clicked Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh, Genelia Deshmukh, Nora Fatehi, Milap Zaveri, Nushrat Bharucha attending the success bash of the film.
