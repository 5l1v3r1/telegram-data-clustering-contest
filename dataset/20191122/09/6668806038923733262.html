Kartik Aaryan & Janhvi Kapoor back after Dostana 2's first schedule wrap

22 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Diya Bachchani

Kartik Aaryan and Janhvi Kapoor have collaborated for the first time on the sequel of the 2009 release, Dostana, which starred Priyanka Chopra, John Abraham, and Abhishek Bachchan. Dostana 2 is being directed by Collin D'Cunha and is penned by Navjot Gulati, Sumit Aroraa and Rishabh Sharma. The film will also launch a fresh face in the industry. The debutant's name is Lakshya. The team of Dostana 2 had been shooting in the different cities of Punjab since the past few weeks  Now they have wrapped  up the first schedule of the film. After shooting at various locations in Chandigarh, Patiala and Amritsar, the team is back in Mumbai.

We spotted Kartik and Janhvi at the airport. Kartik opted for an all-black look in a crisp shirt and a pair of pants while Janhvi wore a pair of denim shorts, an orange T-shirt and a denim jacket with oversized sliders. The duo looked at ease in each other’s company. A glimpse of their fresh pairing also made us excited too.
