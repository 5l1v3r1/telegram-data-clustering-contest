Reno Omokri advises followers against leading flashy lifestyle to impress others

22 Nov 2019, 09:55

- Author and social commentator Reno Omokri has shared a piece of advice for people on social media

- Omokri advised against living to impress people by leading a flashy lifestyle

- The post generated mixed reactions from his followers

Popular Nigerian author and social commentator Reno Omokri recently used his social media platform to share a piece of advice for people in the internet community.

According to Omokri, it is not wise to lead a flashy lifestyle with an intention to impress other people; as such a person may end up dying broke.

"If you live to impress people with a flashy lifestyle, you will die broke and none of those you impressed will contribute to your funeral," his post read.
