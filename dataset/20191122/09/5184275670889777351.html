Bala Box Office: Ayushmann Khurrana Starrer Topples One Film Each Of Shah Rukh Khan & John Abraham!

If we compare this Ayushmann Khurrana starrer with the huge scale of the aforementioned movies, it’s a commendable feat

22 Nov 2019, 09:56 by Koimoi.com Team

Bala Box Office: Despite being a moderately budgeted, this Ayushmann Khurrana flick is competing with major biggies at the ticket windows. Since it’s entry in Koimoi’s All Time Grossers, there’s been a huge turn around in the list. On day 14, it further surpassed Shah Rukh Khan’s Zero and John Abraham’s Batla House.

Bala with its total of 98.80 crores, crossed Batla House (97.18 crores) and Zero (97.50 crores). If we compare this Ayushmann Khurrana starrer with the huge scale of the aforementioned movies, it’s a commendable feat in the kitty of the actor and the makers.

By today, Bala is set to beat Ajay Devgn’s Shivaay (100.35 crores) in the list.

Further mentioning about the dream run, Bala is already 8th most profitable film of 2019 with returns of 182.28%. It is also 3rd back to back superhit for Ayushmann Khurrana in a single year after Article 15 and Dream Girl.

Meanwhile, speaking about his winning streak at box office, Ayushmann says, “I have a lot of hunger to do the best films. It is something that drives me, motivates me to seek out good, new, disruptive cinema and better myself as an actor.

They say you learn a lot through your successes and this current phase has told me that I should always push the content envelope and pick and back edgy stories because somehow my brand of cinema has become synonymous with something that’s a little left of centre,” reports IANS.
