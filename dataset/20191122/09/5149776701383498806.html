Police use stinger to arrest man after van failed to stop in Mansfield

The van was found to contain plant machinery

22 Nov 2019, 09:44 by Phoebe Ram

A man has been arrested after a police pursuit where a van failed to stop in Mansfield.

The 32-year-old was arrested on suspicion of theft, dangerous driving, failure to stop for police and criminal damage after a police pursuit.

An officer spotted a transit van pulling out of a building site on Atkin Lane, Mansfield just after 8.20pm yesterday (Thursday, November 21).

After the van failed to stop, a pursuit was authorised with stinger and tactical pursuit units travelling to support the officer.

The van stopped on a side street and was found to contain plant machinery.

If you have any information which could help this investigation call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 782 0f November, 21 2019.
