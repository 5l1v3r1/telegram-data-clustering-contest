Emmerdale's Jacob kicked out of village by dad after vile sex comments

Teen Jacob has descended into a downward spiral ever since he began being groomed by teacher Maya Stepney

22 Nov 2019, 09:55 by Irishmirror.ie

Jacob Gallagher has finally gone too far for his dad on Emmerdale, which results in him being him out of the house in an explosive twist tonight.

The teen, who has descended into a downward spiral ever since he began being groomed by teacher Maya Stepney.

And things got even worst when Maya was jailed and Jacob struggled to deal with the fact that he was groomed.

After hearing that Maya had been released from prison, Jacob's bad behaviour began escalating again and led him to sleep with Leanna.

Following this, Jacob acted coldly towards her and left her upset. Her dad Dr Liam Cavanagh discovered what had happened and punched Jacob in a fit of anger.

Tonight, Jacob is confronted by his dad, granddad and mum, but he doesn't take it well.

"We can't go on like this," David tells his son in a clip from tonight's episode.

Jacob snaps: "I don't need another lecture."

However his granddad shouts: "Just shut up for once, will you?!"

"You've hurt too many people," David tells him. "And we love you but we just can't cope with you.

"I want you to leave."

This comes just after revelation that Maya is pregnant, which no one in the village currently knows about.

Will Jacob and David find out about the baby when their relationship is at its lowest point?
