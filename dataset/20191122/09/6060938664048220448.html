Did you know that Ileana D’Cruz rejected THESE two films with Salman Khan?

22 Nov 2019, 09:42 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ileana D’Cruz has been on a roll in her career of late. Apart from some giving some entertaining performances, she has also been treating her fans with some stunning pictures of herself on Instagram and her fans just cannot have enough of her. While Ileana has worked with some very good actors like Ajay Devgn, Ranbir Kapoor and Varun Dhawan in the industry, her fans were eager to know if she has any plans of working with Salman Khan.

In an interview recently, she replied to the question asked by one of her fans on twitter. Ileana revealed that she was approached for two Salman Khan starrers namely ‘Kick’ and ‘Wanted’ and she couldn’t do both the films due to some circumstances.

According to the actress when she was offered the role of Ayesha Takia in the movie, she had her exams going on owing to which she couldn’t take up the offer. As far as ‘Kick’ is concerned, she had already given her dates to some other film she was doing at the time and so she couldn’t manage dates for the film. Later, Jacqueline was got on board for the Salman Khan starrer.

Meanwhile, on the work front, she is seen in ‘Pagalpanti’ which hit the theatres today, November 22, 2019.

The Anees Bazmee directorial boasts of an ensemble star cast that includes John Abraham, Arshad Warsi, Pulkit Samrat, Kriti Kharbanda, Urvashi Rautela, Anil Kapoor and Saurabh Shukla. The movie has received mixed reviews from the audience and critics alike.
