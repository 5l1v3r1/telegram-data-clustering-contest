Spoilers: David kicks Jacob out in Emmerdale

22 Nov 2019, 09:59

He has been through a lot due to the grooming and abuse from Maya Stepney (Louisa Clein) but Jacob Gallagher’s (Joe-Warren Plant) recent behaviour is set to push David Metcalfe (Matthew Wolfended) and Leyla Harding (Roxy Shahidi) to take drastic action in Emmerdale tonight.

In the wake of Jacob’s aggressive behaviour and his heartless and cruel treatment of Leanna Cavanagh (Mimi Slinger), everyone is turning their backs on him including mates like Liv Flaherty (Isobel Steele).

Jacob disgusted everyone, including fans, by expressing a callous attitude to sex and claiming that he didn’t want Leanna but she was asking for it. Liam (Jonny McPherson) was infuriated by the way that Jacob was talking about his daughter and snapped before punching him.

And after his actions got Liam arrested and put his job at risk, Leanna can’t bear to look at Jacob. When Jacob arrives back at the shop, David and Leyla are cold with him and an angry Eric Pollard (Chris Chittell) gives him a piece of his mind, telling him he can’t keep behaving this way to people.
