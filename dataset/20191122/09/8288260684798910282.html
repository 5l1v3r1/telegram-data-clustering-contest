Discover the 80s will play in Cheltenham - here’s how to get tickets

The throwback show will welcome classic music icons to the stage on Saturday 23 May 2020

22 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Scott Dickinson, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/scott-dickinson/

Cheltenham’s Johnny-Rocks Stadium will host a one of a kind show next year that guarantees to be nostalgic thrill.

Discover the 80s will celebrate some of the finest music from the decade with an astonishing line-up featuring Marc Almond, Heather Small, The South, China Crisis and Johnny Hates Jazz.

The show will play at the Johnny-Rocks Stadium in Cheltenham on Saturday 23 May 2020 and you can purchase tickets here.

Marc Almond has over 30 million record sales under his belt and has just finished a huge tour of America with dates in Dallas, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Chicago Detroit and Brooklyn.

Equally, Heather Small has remained a relevant force on the music scene since her smash hits 'Moving on Up' and 'One Night in Heaven', having released a slew of incredible remixes this month.

The South are best known for their number one single 'A Little Time' and other classics such as 'Perfect 10', 'Rotterdam', 'Old Red Eyes' and 'Good as Gold.' The nine piece band will be performing at the gig in Cheltenham in the midst of their UK tour.

China Crisis will also feature in the show fresh from a variety of gigs accross the UK. The band spent 92 weeks in the charts with hits such as 'Christian', 'Black Man Ray' and 'King in a Catholic Style.'

The chart topping Johnny Hates Jazz, responsible for timeless classics such as 'Shattered Dreams', 'I Don’t Want to be a Hero' and 'Turn Back the clock', will also be taking to the stage and we can look forward to an album from them later in 2020.  

The combination of such talented icons of 80s culture promises an unforgettable night.

Discover the 80s will play at the Johnny-Rocks Stadium in Cheltenham on Saturday 23 May 2020.

Tickets will be released on Friday 22 November 2019 at 9am and can be purchased from the Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.
