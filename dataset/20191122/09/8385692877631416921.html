This doorbell from Ring is designed for those that live in apartments and can't install a video doorbell outside of their door. Instead, this one attaches to the peephole outside your door. And will still do everything that a regular Ring Doorbell is able to do.

This includes showing you video of who is at your door, on the Ring app or on an Echo Show device. It will notify you if someone presses the button on the Ring Peephole Cam, or if they don't. So if someone is attempting to steal a package from in front of your door, you'll know. And you'll also see exactly who it is.
