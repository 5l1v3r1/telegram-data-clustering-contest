Tesla’s new Cybertruck reveal didn’t go quite as smoothly as expected after its “armour glass” windows got smashed during a live test.

CEO Elon Musk unveiled the $US39,900 truck in Los Angeles on Thursday, and he got Tesla’s lead designer, Franz von Holzhausen, to test the left-side windows by throwing a silver metal ball at them.

After von Holzhausen smashed the front-left window with ease, Musk exclaimed: “Oh my f—ng God. Well, maybe that was a little too hard.”
