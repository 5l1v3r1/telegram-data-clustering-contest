Tesla unveils futuristic CyberTruck electric pickup truck

22 Nov 2019, 09:20

Tesla has unveiled its first electric pickup truck, known as CyberTruck.

The futuristic-looking vehicle will be released at the end of 2021, and with a range of up to 500 miles.

Tesla says the vehicle has “more utility than a truck, with more performance than a sports car”, while company boss Elon Musk attempted to show its ruggedness during a live demonstration at the launch event in Los Angeles.
