Ensure your loved one is well looked after with respite care breaks in Derby

Sherwood Forest provides dementia, nursing and palliative care in addition to residential and respite care

22 Nov 2019, 09:24 by Leena Sidat

When caring for a loved one at home, full time, it’s important to take the opportunity to have a rest and recharge your batteries.

Part of Sanctuary Care, Sherwood Forest Residential and Nursing Home in Normanton provides short-term respite care breaks to give you this deserved time, whilst assuring you that your loved one will be well cared for.

Based on Village Street, the home offers respite care for anything from one to six weeks, enabling carers to take a holiday or simply a break of their own, safe in the knowledge that their loved one is being cared for by the team.

One carer, Elsie looks after her husband Jack, who has Parkinson’s disease, and books regular respite breaks with Sanctuary Care for Jack, so that she can have a couple of weeks to herself, with the peace of mind that her husband is safe and happy.

Elsie said, "Respite care is a blessing and the freedom it gives me is remarkable. It is a wonderful care home, the team are lovely and Jack is happy there."

Respite care breaks are also available for people who live independently - perhaps your family are going on holiday and you would like some company.

With every respite break, Sherwood Forest ensures you have your own bedroom, and its dedicated team is on hand around the clock to provide care tailored to your individual needs.

There are delicious home-cooked meals, including freshly baked cakes for afternoon tea, fantastic activities, and of course, the company of other residents and the team.

Sherwood Forest Residential and Nursing Home is located at 29 Village Street, Normanton, Derby, DE23 8TA.
