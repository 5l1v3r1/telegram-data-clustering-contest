CTM Festival adds Sherelle, Squarepusher for 2020

A second wave of names has been announced for the Berlin festival.

22 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Kiana Mickles

CTM Festival has confirmed more acts for its 2020 edition.

Centered on a "Liminal" theme, the 21st edition of the experimental-leaning Berlin festival will run from January 24th through Febuary 2nd. New additions include Sherelle, Afrodeutsche, Valesuchi and Squarepusher, who will perform in Berghain.

The event will also feature collaborative sets from the likes of Cera Khin and Lokier, Nene H and Ensemble Basiani, and Gabber Modus Operandi, Wahono and Nakibembe Xylophone Troupe. They join previously confirmed acts such as Giant Swan, AYA (FKA LOFT) and Deathprod. More names will be added in mid-December.

CTM 2020 will take place across various Berlin venues, including HAU Hebbel am Ufer, Schwuz and Kunstquartier Bethanien.
