CHYM goes all Christmas music

'It's the Most Wonderful Time of the Year' was the first tune

22 Nov 2019, 16:10 by KitchenerToday Staff

You can now get in the Christmas spirit by tuning into CHYM 96.7.

The Kitchener radio station switched to all Christmas music on Friday at 8 a.m.

"It's the Most Wonderful Time of the Year" by Andy Williams, was the first song that was played.

The station will spin Christmas tunes up until Boxing Day.
