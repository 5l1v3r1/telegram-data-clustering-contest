Here’s how Shilpa Shetty and Raj Kundra are celebrating their 10th wedding anniversary

22 Nov 2019, 16:36 by Filmfare

Bollywood beauty Shilpa Shetty continues to inspire us in several ways. The actress is known to strike the perfect balance between her personal and professional life. Despite her hectic work schedule, she makes sure to spend a good amount of time with her family. Speaking of her personal life, the diva is celebrating her 10th wedding anniversary with husband Raj Kundra today. The couple tied the knot on 22nd November 2009 and ever since then, they continue to give us major couple goals.

Speaking of their wedding anniversary celebrations, the duo has decided to keep it a low-key affair and have headed to Japan for a three-day-trip. A source close to the actress informs that she had been working continuously to keep her schedules free later. Opening up about her anniversary, Shilpa said, “Our journey so far has been beautiful and I have loved every part of it. These 10 years have definitely been very memorable and while a decade is a milestone for any marriage, I wanted a break from work and keep the celebrations modest. A trip to Japan was Raj's idea.”

Here’s wishing Shilpa and Raj a very happy wedding anniversary. Meanwhile, on the work front,  she will be next seen in Nikamma which is currently on floors. We are quite excited to see Shilpa on the big screen once again, what about you?
