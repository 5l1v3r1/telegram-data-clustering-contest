Looks Like Pokémon Go Developer Niantic is Involved with Settlers of Catan AR

22 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Alistair Jones

Last month the makers of Settlers of Catan announced Catan: World Explorers, an AR game that promises to turn the entire world into a massive game of Catan. While the similarities to games like Pokémon Go are immediately apparent, fans have discovered that that game’s developer, Niantic, might have a closer involvement than first suspected.

Last week a post on the subreddit (via Techcrunch) for Niantic’s first game, Ingress, discovered that the Catan: World Explorers website’s privacy policy leads users directly to Niantic’s own privacy policy. On top of that, another user discovered that a section of the website’s code made repeated reference to the developer.

This doesn't mean Niantic is directly involved in development; our money would be on Catan: World Explorers using the developer’s Waypoint tech to plot out its world. We’ve reached out to Niantic for comment, and will update if any is forthcoming.
