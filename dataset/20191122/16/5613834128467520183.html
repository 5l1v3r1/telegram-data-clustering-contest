[Gift Guide] ‘Jaws’ and ‘Gremlins’ Highlight Amblinesque Poster Series

22 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Brad Miska

Don’t feed this art after midnight.

Get a head start on your holiday shopping with a series of four posters hailing out of Matt Ferguson‘s Amblinesque exhibition.

Ferguson has created stunning new art for the Steven Spielberg-directed Jaws, E.T., and Close Encounters of the Third Kind, as well as Joe Dante‘s Amblin-produced Gremlins. All four would go phenomenally with art he created for the Back to the Future trilogy, which are still available as signed APs.

As for the Amblinesque pieces, all four will be available at 6pm UK time at Ferguson’s website.
