PlayStation’s Black Friday sale cuts prices on tons of great PS4 games

22 Nov 2019, 16:52 by Colm Ahern

PlayStation has kicked off its Black Friday sale, which runs until 2nd December, and there are some pretty decent deals in there for all you PlayStation 4 owners. As well as a couple of cracking PlayStation exclusives from the past couple of years getting discounts, like Marvel’s Spider-Man and Horizon Zero Dawn, there are plenty of savings on some great games from this year too.

The PSVR hasn’t had an avalanche of hits since its launch back in 2016, but there was definitely one this year that caught the attention of many: Blood & Truth. The virtual reality shooter is available on the PS Store right now for only £15.99, and well worth your money if you’ve got the PlayStation goggles. Plus Colin Salmon is in it, and who doesn’t love a bit of Salmon from time to time?

The Bandicoot-fronted kart racer from 20 years ago reminded us just how good it was with a beautiful remake back in June. Crash Team Racing: Nitro-Fueled is currently available on the PlayStation Store for only £22.74, and you would be really silly to not grab it. This isn’t nostalgia talking: CTR is a quality racing game.

One of 2019’s biggest surprises, Dragon Quest Builders 2, is a third off its regular price at the minute. You can play the game Kirk called a “masterpiece” when he reviewed it back in July for a mere £33.49. Get on it. Get on all of them, frankly.
