COLD SNAP

Ex-Celtic star Tierney shocks Arsenal team-mates by braving cold in short sleeves

22 Nov 2019, 16:13 by David Fowler

EX-CELTIC ace Kieran Tierney shocked his Arsenal team-mates as he braved the cold in a short sleeved shirt and shorts.

The former Hoops full-back trained in London without extra layers as temperatures plummet across the UK.

And team-mates Alexander Lacazette and Pierre Emerick-Aubameyang couldn't believe the Scot as Auba trolled Tierney on Instagram.

Posting a snap of himself and Lacazette laughing and pointing at training Auba wrote: "@kierantierney : with short sleeves and Shorts

"Us: Daaaammmmnnnnn @Lacazettealex"

Tierney notoriously joined Celtic skipper Scott Brown in exclusively training in shorts and short sleeve shirts at Lennoxtown.

And they caught the eye of the press in Russia as the pair strolled around St Petersburg in short sleeve shirts before a Europa League clash.

But Lacazette and Aubameyang were fully kitted out in jumpers, trackies and gloves as they were clearly feeling the cold.

Tierney laughed off the jokes from his team-mates as he re-posted the picture and adding some laughing and an snowflake emoji.

Earlier this week we told how Tierney held a green smoke bomb before revealing the Arsenal-Hoops connection that's helped him settle.

The left-back traded life in the family home to living himself in London.

He admits the transition has been difficult but he's been made to feel at home by the Scottish staff on the Arsenal books - who share his love for Celtic.

And says he has created bonds with the likes of Hector Bellerin, Dino Mavropanos, Callum Chambers and Rob Holding.

Speaking to Pro Direct Soccer, he said: "It was a hard transition. I lived with my family right until I had to move.

"It's been in the house myself, doing the cooking and washing.

"Everyone has been great, even the staff.

"There’s a few Scottish staff as well – Celtic fans – so they’ve helped me settle in and make me feel at home.”
