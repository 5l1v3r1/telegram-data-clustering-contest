Auburn vs. Samford: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

22 Nov 2019, 16:48 by Chris Rosvoglou

Before the No. 15 Auburn Tigers take the field for the annual Iron Bowl against the Alabama Crimson Tide, they’ll have to take care of business this weekend as they’re set to host the Samford Bulldogs.

Despite making a comeback late in the fourth quarter last weekend, Auburn still fell short to Georgia. Outside of Bo Nix making plays with his arm or legs, the offense didn’t have much life.

The Tigers should easily defeat the Bulldogs this Saturday at home as they own a 27-0-1 all-time record against the Southern Conference program.

Samford snapped its losing streak with a victory over Western Carolina in Week 12. Nonetheless, the odds are greatly stacked against the Bulldogs this weekend.
