New Fortnite box fighting map promises ‘zero bugs’

22 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Jimmy Russo

One Fortnite creator has developed an innovative Fortnite box fighting map that promises “zero bugs.”

Box fighting has become the meta in Fortnite. Build-battles still happen, but not as frequently as they did in past seasons. There aren’t any items that save you from fall damage this chapter, which makes “cranking” a risky proposition.

Practicing your high-ground retakes and build battle strategy is still useful, but it’s not as practical as box fighting. You can guarantee a few box fights in a given competitive match, where the build battles have become a bit rarer.

If someone cranks 90’s on top of you, challenging them probably isn’t the best decision. Most players box-up and wait for their opponent to come to them.

Since this is the case, box fighting maps have become one of the best ways to practice in Fortnite. There are several maps out there, but all of them seem to suffer from bugs that force you to double-tap your keybinds in order to build, edit, or reload (among other issues).

Percy, a Fortnite Creative map builder, has come out with his first box fighting map of Fortnite Chapter 2. He promises “zero bugs” in his map, which remains to be seen.
