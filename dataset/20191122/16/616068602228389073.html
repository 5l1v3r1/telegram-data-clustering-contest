Shop local, handmade gifts

22 Nov 2019, 16:35 by Sarita Patel

Holiday shopping can get stressful and sometimes feel more like a chore, that's why Craft Culture is looking to change the way you shop. They teamed up with local wineries to host a handmade holiday shopping festivities.

The Sandhill Wines Holiday Market takes place on Saturday and Sunday at the Sandhill Urban Tasting Room on Richter Street in Kelowna. 

The market will host 28 local vendors, and live music in a fun and festive atmosphere. Admission is free with a donation to Friends of the Okanagan Rail Trail. 

Craft Culture will be bringing a market to West Kelowna's Grizzli Winery Nov. 30-Dec. 1.

Customers will be treated to live music, wine tastings, and 32 local vendors, featuring handcrafted wares. Admission to this event is also free, with a donation to Kelowna Community Resources to assist with the Christmas Sponsor a Family Program.

Craft Culture’s largest and final market of the season will take place at Prospera Place Dec. 7-8.
