Thistle do nicely as Perth and Kinross winners scoop awards

The Old Manse of Blair in Blair Atholl was awarded the best hotel experience

22 Nov 2019, 16:48 by Douglas Dickie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/douglas-dickie/

Businesses from across Perth and Kinross struck gold at the Scottish Thistle Awards regional finals.

The event for those in the tourism industry for Central, Fife and Tayside proved to be a fruitful for six nominees from the region.

The Old Manse of Blair in Blair Atholl was awarded the best hotel experience, sponsored by Fishers.

Judges praised the hotel for offering personal touches to guests and working in collaboration with the local community.

Straloch Highland Retreats in Blairgowrie received the best self catering accommodation experience award, sponsored by Bruce Stevenson Insurance Brokers.

Its emphasis on customer service and sustainability struck a chord with the judges.

Northlands B&B in Pitlochry was another accommodation winner, being named the most hospitable B&B/guest house, sponsored by Abbey UK.

The four-star accommodation was commended by judges for demonstrating real passion and purpose in its offering, with a customer at heart ethos.

Land Rover Experience Scotland, based in Dunkeld, received the best outdoor adventure experience award.

Judges commended the use of low emission vehicles and the steps to minimise the environmental impact. Staff empowerment was also praised by the judging panel.

WILDHOOD festival received the award for best cultural event or festival. The festival for children aged 12 and under took place in a 17th-centure castle near Kinross and included arts, crafts, theatre, circus, nature, outdoors and music. Finally, the Library at Innerpeffray received the best heritage tourism experience award, sponsored by Historic Environment Scotland.

The authentic and engaging experience was praised by judges, as well as the ambitious but sustainable plans for the future.

The awards, which were presented by TV star Jennifer Reoch, took place in Edinburgh.

The regional winners have earned their place at the prestigious national final in March.
