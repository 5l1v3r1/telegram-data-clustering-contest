I'm A Celeb confirms when the next King or Queen of jungle will be crowned

22 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Ciara Walsh

ITV confirmed the reality show's final date. 

I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here 2019 has been underway for less than a week, but we already know when the big finale will air.

ITV have confirmed that the next King or Queen of the jungle will be crowned on Sunday, December 8 - just over two weeks from now.

Hopefully, the campers won't be too smelly by that point - after all, hosts Ant and Dec have said that switching the camp's traditional fire for a gas stove has made things easier on the nose.

"The difference this year is that we have the gas stoves in there, no smoke," the host's explained on last night's I'm A Celeb: Extra Camp. "They don't stink as much."

However, presenter Joel Dommett mused that it has had the opposite effect, saying: "They smell of BO instead, because they are all smelling themselves and they stink whereas usually you just smell fire the whole time."

Ant then joked: "At the trial with Roman and Adele, woo, I am not saying what one, but one of them honked!"

Dec added: "One of them has got the nickname cheese fruit."

I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here continues tonight from 9pm on Virgin Media One and ITV.
