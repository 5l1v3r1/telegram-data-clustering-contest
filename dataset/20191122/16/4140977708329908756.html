Mohammad Kaif takes sneaky dig at Pakistan’s 16-year-old sensation Naseem Shah

22 Nov 2019, 16:27 by Tanish Chachra

Mohammad Kaif takes a sneaky dig at the 16-year-old sensation from Pakistan Naseem Shah, who shocked the world with his lethal bowling.

Mohammad Kaif takes a sly dig at the 16-year-old bowling sensation from Pakistan over where he highlights an old tweet mentioning the young bowler to be 17-year-old.

The tweet which was claiming Naseem 17, posted by Pakistan Journalist Saj Sadik one year ago. Mohammad Kaif hilariously reacted over the tweet, he wrote, “Looks a terrific prospect. But is 16 now, ageing backwards I think”.
