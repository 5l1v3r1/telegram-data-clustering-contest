Maame Yeboah Asiedu finally details why she left Despite Media Group

22 Nov 2019, 16:45 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Popular marriage counselor and media personality Maame Yeboah Asiedu has refuted claims that she was sacked from Despite Media Group.

The media personality in an interview with Delay revealed that she resigned from the company for reasons best known to herself.

Maame Yeboah Asiedu explained that during her six-year stay at the Despite Media Group, she never had any issue with anyone.

She further revealed that she actually resigned from Despite Media Group and was never sacked.

“I am too good to be sacked. Do I look like someone who can be sacked? If you are a visionary and you know where you are going, you don’t get distracted by hearsay.

I wasn’t sacked, I resigned. I don’t regret my decision and I am very alright with where I am now”, Maame Yeboah Asiedu revealed.
