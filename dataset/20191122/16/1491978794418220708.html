Arsenal v Southampton: match preview

22 Nov 2019, 16:43 by Guardian Staff

Unai Emery has his board’s public backing but that will surely wear exceedingly thin if Arsenal slip up against Southampton or, next weekend, Norwich. He may spring a surprise by recalling Granit Xhaka – a temptation given the thin midfield options at his disposal – but is more likely to use the bulk of the side that put in an improved performance at Leicester before the international break, perhaps reverting to a back four. A dominant showing is needed, for the first time this season really, against opponents who should be there for the taking. Nick Ames
