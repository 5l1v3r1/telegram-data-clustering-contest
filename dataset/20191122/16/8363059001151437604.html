Three's Half Price Unlimited Data Offer is Back

22 Nov 2019, 16:15 by Tom Pritchard

Earlier this week we mentioned that Three's Unlimited everything contract had been discounted down to £18 a month - which is a very good deal. That said if you weren't fond of that, the network has brought back the contract that knocks 50% off the first six months.

That means you get unlimited data, calls, texts, and tethering (plus the usual Three bonuses) for £10 a month for the first six months. Then you pay £20 a month for the final 18 months, bringing your total up to £420 over the course of the two year contract.

For reference the £18 a month deal would cost you £432 over the same length of time. Not such a huge difference, I know, but at least it means you won't wind up having to handle a price increase after a year. It'd cost you £456 if you discover the £18 a month plan isn't being repeated next year.

You can head over to Three to enjoy this deal right here, or you can head over to the main storefront and see what other Black Friday bargains are available.
