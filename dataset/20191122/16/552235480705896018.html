Kieran Tierney leaves Arsenal mates Aubameyang and Lacazette stunned with training ground get-up

The full back made a £25million move in the summer but the look on his team-mates face was priceless.

22 Nov 2019, 16:57 by Mark McDougall, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/mark-mcdougall/

It's not often a Scotsman leaves two of the Premier League's best striker's shellshocked in training.

But that's exactly what Kieran Tierney did to Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang and Alexandre Lacazette on Friday.

The left back made the summer switch to Arsenal from Celtic and the 9 degrees celcius London weather clearly didn't bother him.

The harsh winters of Wishaw - where he grew up - and Lennoxtown have clearly toughened him up for life in London.

The same can't be said for Aubameyang and Lacazette who both grew up in France where the temperatures are generally a bit higher. 

And Tierney's decision to not cover himself in hats, gloves, snoods or wear a jumper or trousers during training left the pair a bit baffled.

Aubameyang teased the Scotland left back on social media after training finished with a brilliant Instagram post of the strike pair that captioned: "@KieranTierney: With short sleeves and shorts.

"Us: Daaaaammmmmmnnnn!!!"

Tierney was regularly spotted during the coldest months of winter with short sleeves and shorts on during training with Celtic alongside skipper Scott Brown.

The pair would even refuse to stick a jacket on when travelling to freezing conditions in countries such as Kazakhstan or Russia for European competition.

It might take Lacazette and Aubameyang a little while longer to get used to that one.
