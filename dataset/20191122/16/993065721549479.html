Police investigating illegal lottery arrest man (42) in Derry

22 Nov 2019, 16:19

POLICE investigating an illegal lottery have arrested a 42-year-old man in Derry.

Detectives from PSNI's Terrorism Investigation Unit detained the man in the Creggan area of the city today.

A PSNI spokesman said items were seized during the search and arrest operation "as part of an ongoing investigation into the illegal use of a lottery in Derry".
