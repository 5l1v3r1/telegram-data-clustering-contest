CCTV image released of man following burglary in Mansfield Woodhouse

Do you recognise the man?

22 Nov 2019, 16:07 by Ben Reid

Police have released a CCTV image of a man they want to speak to in connection with a burglary.

The incident happened overnight on October 24/25 in Sherwood Rise, Mansfield Woodhouse.

Officers are now appealing for anyone with information to get in touch.

A spokesman for Nottinghamshire Police said: "If you recognise him or think you can help please call us on 101, quoting incident number 122 of 25 October 2019."
