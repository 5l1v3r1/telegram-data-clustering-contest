Preschool worker slaps toddler hard across the face for spitting his food out

22 Nov 2019, 16:37

A preschool worker was filmed slapping a three year-old boy hard across the face for spitting his spaghetti onto the floor.

Heather Reese, 41, pleaded no contest to assaulting the youngster at a court hearing Friday, a month after the attack at Bright Beginnings Childcare in Toledo, Ohio.

That means she has been convicted of the crime without admitting to the allegations she faces.

Disturbing surveillance footage shows Reese, dressed in an orange T-Shirt, grabbing the arm of the boy while he stands next to her at a table.

She can be seen spoon-feeding the boy the pasta, before striking him across the face with her right hand after he regurgitates it.

Reese slapped the boy so hard he stumbled backwards, but she was holding onto him, and was able to stop him falling.

The youngster’s mother Cassie Sullivan arrived at the daycare shortly after her son was attacked, and said he was so upset he could barely talk.

She told abc13: ‘It’s sickening, and I’m mad.

‘It’s horrible. She struck him very, very, very hard. He almost fell.

‘I walked in there… He was screaming, crying, hard to catch his breath.

‘He held on to me and didn’t let go.

‘I noticed the red mark with welts and a scratch on the left side of his face.’

Sullivan was able to establish what had happened to her son, and called police.

Probation officers are now set to investigate further before Reese is sentenced on January 10, the Toledo Blade reported.

She was fired from the daycare after attacking the boy.

Bright Beginning owner Kristen Hill apologized to Sullivan, and said her facility has a zero-tolerance policy on any kind of abuse.
