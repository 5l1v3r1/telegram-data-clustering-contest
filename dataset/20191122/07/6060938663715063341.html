IFFI makes an IFFY ‘goof-up’: Credits lyricist Gulzar as filmmaker Satyajit Ray

22 Nov 2019, 07:22 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

International Film Festival of India is currently going on in Goa and while they celebrate cinema and laud artists, here’s an iffy goof-up made by the team. A social media user pointed out that the website of the festival, who is in its 50th year, has a picture of Bollywood lyricist Gulzar showcasing in the credit space for celebrated filmmaker Satyajit Ray.
