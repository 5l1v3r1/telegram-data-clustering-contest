Davido’s album: Naira Marley sounded like he recorded his verse on suit and tie - Fans

22 Nov 2019, 07:41

- Nigerian singer Davido recently released his much anticipated album A Good Time

- Just a few hours after the release, the album became a trending topic on social media

- Rapper Naira Marley’s verse on a particular track off the album got music lovers talking

Popular Nigerian singer David Adeleke aka Davido is currently the topic of discussion on social media, following the release of his long awaited album, A Good Time.

The music star released the 17-album in the early hours of Friday, November 22, and despite the time of release, many fans stayed up late to be among those who experienced the beauty of the project fresh out of the oven.

Fans of OBO as he is often called showered accolades on him for making the album worth the wait. Although Davido had some interesting collaborations on the project, the track with controversial rapper Naira Marley, is already getting a lot of buzz.
