Tesla Cybertruck unveiled: Price, motor, range, features, other details explained

New Tesla Cybertruck will be launched in three versions -- single motor RWD, dual-motor AWD and tri-motor AWD -- and priced between USD 39,900 and USD 69,900.

22 Nov 2019, 07:24 by Varun Singh

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Tesla Cybertruck comes with an ultra-hard 30X cold-rolled stainless-steel body and armour glass.
  • The Cybertruck gets a 17-inch touchscreen with an all-new customised user interface.
  • Tesla Cybertruck features an adaptive air suspension that can be raised and lowered four inches in either direction.

Tesla has unveiled its new Cybertruck, which the company claims has a better utility than a truck with more performance than a sports car. To be available in three versions -- single motor RWD, dual-motor AWD and tri-motor AWD, the Cybertruck's production will reportedly start in late 2021.

Tesla Cybertruck Price

The single motor RWD variant of the Cybertruck has been priced at USD 39,900, while the dual-motor AWD and tri-motor AWD trims are available for USD 49,900 and USD 69,900, respectively. The self-driving option comes at a premium of USD 7,000.

Tesla Cybertruck Motor

Like we have already mentioned, the new Cybertruck has three motor options -- single motor RWD, dual-motor AWD and tri-motor AWD. The single motor RWD can make 0-60 mph in less than 6.5 seconds and has a towing capacity of over 7,500 pounds. The dual-motor AWD is good for 0-60 mph in under 4.5 seconds and boasts of a towing capacity of more than 10,000 pounds. The tri-motor AWD will go from 0 to 60 mph in less than 2.9 seconds and gets a towing capacity of 14,000 pounds.

Tesla Cybertruck Range

The new Cybertruck single motor RWD has a range of over 250 miles, while the dual-motor AWD gets a range of more than 300 miles. Now hold on. The Cybertruck tri-motor AWD boasts of a massive 500+ mile range.

Tesla Cybertruck Features

Tesla Cybertruck comes with an ultra-hard 30X cold-rolled stainless-steel body and armour glass. The vehicle has up to 3,500 pounds of payload capacity and features an adaptive air suspension that can be raised and lowered four inches in either direction. The Cybertruck gets vault-like lockable storage. The cabin can accommodate six adults. There is a 17-inch touchscreen with an all-new customised user interface. Also, the second row has additional storage.
