Here are your latest rugby headlines on the morning of Friday, November 22.

Joe Marler goes global

England prop Joe Marler has become something of a global star after his brilliantly bizarre interview went viral.

Marler's antics during the hilarious interview with Harlequins' in-house media team, which included him imitating a horse, have proved a huge hit on social media.

And the story has even reached Australia, New Zealand and America, with NBC and the Washington Post reporting on the video. The Post's headline reads: "A rugby player talks about horses in hilarious interview. We don't know what it means, either."

Marler's interview starts off in seemingly normal fashion, initially using the old sporting cliche of "getting back on the horse" after a heavy defeat against Clermont last weekend.
