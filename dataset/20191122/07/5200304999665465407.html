Hrithik Roshan boosts his fees for upcoming films?

22 Nov 2019, 07:29 by Sahar Junejo

2019 has been a successful year for Hrithik Roshan. After proving that he is an irreplaceable part of Bollywood, Roshan is now receiving offers from some of the top filmmakers. In response to the high demand, the actor has decided to raise his asking price.

Roshan has not revealed what he charges for a film, but according to a source the increase is substantial, Deccan Chronicle reported. The change applies to all filmmakers including Yash Raj Films, who will now have to pay Roshan the higher fee if they decide to bring him back for a sequel to ‘War’ (2019).

Currently, Roshan’s only upcoming film is ‘Krrish 4’. He has been rumoured to be in talks for Farah Khan’s ‘Satte Pe Satta’ and Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s ‘Inshallah’.
