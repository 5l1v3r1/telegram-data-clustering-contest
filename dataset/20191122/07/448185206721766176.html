Snatchers loot cash worth thousands in Noonmati area in Guwahati

22 Nov 2019, 07:35

STAFF REPORTER

GUWAHATI: Ashthami Bharali, while withdrawing Rs 50,000 from Noonmati SBI branch on Tuesday, was loot by two people in a Pulser bike. Cases of loot by snatchers in bikes have recently been increasing in a city causing much distress among the residents.
