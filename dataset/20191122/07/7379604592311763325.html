I’m A Celebrity 2019 catch up: What happened in the jungle last night?

22 Nov 2019, 07:30 by Victoria Garo-Falides

I'M A Celebrity last night saw two new faces in camp - Coronation Street favourite Andrew Whyment and former EastEnders star Cliff Parisi.

Read our I'm A Celebrity 2019 blog to find out what happened on episode five.

  • Nicola Agius9 hours ago21st November 2019WHEEL-Y GOOD
    Tonight was Wheel-y good. In fact it was Wagon Wheel-y good.
    Cliff Parisi and Andy Whyment (can you not have less tricky names to spell, gents?) brought some much needed silliness onto our screens.
    They took part in the Bushtucker Trial – and Andy couldn't stop laughing as he watched from his sofa (or behind the window).
    After getting 8 stars for camp, it was off to jail for them both.
    Another spat in Main Camp erupted and Kate Garraway and Andrew found themselves trotting off to jail with their tail between their legs.
    Twitter went mad as Jacqueline and Nadine threw their toys out the pram and said they didn't even want to offer to go to prison.
    They didn't even bother to pretend. Which is why Jacqueline then got voted to do tomorrow's Bushtucker Trial by viewers. 
    But who's worse? Jac for telling the truth and saying she didn't want to go – or Kate for lying to be nice and saying she 'didn't mind'.
    Oh, the British stiff upper lip.
    “It's OK. I'll take the bullet. As long as I look like a nice person.”
    Nadine taught Caitlyn, Jac and Adele the routine to The Promise from Girls Aloud. It was hard to tell the difference. At one point I thought I was watching The Brits.
    Meanwhile, the camp was treated to a thimble of Wagon Wheel after a successful Dingo Dollar challenge which left the team in tears.
    They were besides themselves. Maybe it was stale.
