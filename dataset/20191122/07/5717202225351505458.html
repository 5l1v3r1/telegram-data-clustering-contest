Liverpool fans discuss who should replace Mo Salah if he is ruled out of Crystal Palace clash

Andrew Robertson is also out.

22 Nov 2019, 07:15 by Jay Williams

Liverpool winger Mo Salah is likely to miss the game against Crystal Palace this weekend.

According to Sky Sports News, Andrew Robertson and Salah have both been ruled out. The pair aggravated ankle injuries against Manchester City before the international break.

Robertson and Salah sat out of international duty in a bid to get fit for this weekend’s game against Palace. But it looks like neither player will pass the fitness test.

Who should replace Salah?

Salah’s absence means that Jurgen Klopp needs to find a replacement on the wing. Roberto Firmino, Sadio Mane and Salah form a devastating front three but this could be broken up.

There are four main candidates to take Salah’s spot. Divock Origi is one of the options and he can play anywhere across the front three. Xherdan Shaqiri is another but he is recovering from an injury.

Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain could play in an advanced role and he has been scoring recently. Alternatively, Adam Lallana might fancy Salah’s spot on the right.
