Chris Brown becomes a dad again as ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris gives birth

22 Nov 2019, 07:17

Chris Brown has become a father again, after his ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris gave birth to their child on Wednesday.

The singer, 30, appeared to confirm the news on Instagram, posting a photo of him looking down adoringly at something just out of shot, with the caption: ’11-20-2019’.

He also hinted at the arrival by posting a photo of him wearing a hoody, with the word ‘born’ clearly visible in large letters on the hood right after.

Ammika also posted about the new baby on her Instagram story, telling her followers: ‘I was in love, when I first saw you’.

Neither have spoken out with any more details about the baby as of yet, but TMZ reported back in August that the former couple were expecting a baby boy.

Chris already has a daughter, five-year-old Royalty, with his ex Nia Guzman, and he and Ammika are reportedly on very good terms despite not being together.

The pair split mid-way through the pregnancy, but Chris was said to be giving Ammika financial, medical and emotional support throughout.

He had started seeing new girlfriend Indyamarie during the pregnancy, but reportedly split because of it.

Although Chris and Ammika never officially confirmed the pregnancy on social media, rumours they were expecting started back in May, when he commented on a photo of her, saying ‘BM [baby mama] BAD’.

Chris and Ammika were first romantically linked in 2015, and the pair confirmed their relationship when she supported him through the rape allegations earlier this year.

In February, the R&B singer was accused of raping a woman in a hotel room in Paris but, after being arrested, was released without charge days later.

While Ammika stood by Chris during the ordeal, it’s claimed they split shortly after.

There’s no news yet of the baby’s name, weight or official confirmation of whether it’s a boy or girl, but all eyes are on Chris and Ammika’s upcoming posts.
