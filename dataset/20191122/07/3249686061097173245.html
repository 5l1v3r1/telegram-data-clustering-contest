The legendary fancam by BTS Jimin surpassed 82 million views and still remain as the most viewed fancam in history!

22 Nov 2019, 07:40 by parkhaneul1013

BTS Jimin's legendary M Countdown "Fake Love" fancam has surpassed 82 million views on YouTube thus remain as the most viewed K-pop individual fancam in history. Previously this fancam broke record when it surpassed 30 million views in just four months after released. Full with emotion with outstanding vocal and powerful dancing skills, this legendary fancam was seen trended in YouTube more than 72 hours proving BTS Jimin's potential as BTS main dancer and front-man. In addition to that this fancam also ranked first in MNET'S "2018 M2 Year End Award".
