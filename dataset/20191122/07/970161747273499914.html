Spoilers: Jags in danger from Gray in EastEnders as he gets close to Chantelle

22 Nov 2019, 07:00

The Panesar clan arrived in EastEnders several weeks back, and — upon their arrival — they were determined to track down their sister, Ash (Guraline Kaur Garcha). However, Jags (Amar Adatia) soon found himself distracted from his mission when he set his sights upon Chantelle (Jessica Plummer) in a scene which implied that he recognised the young woman.

A conversation with Kheerat (Jaz Deol) later clarified matters for the viewers, as it was revealed that Jags had previously went to school with Chantelle — and that he’d once went out on a date with her.

In the coming episodes, Jags decides it’s time that he speak to Chantelle, and thus he approaches her and strikes up a conversation.

It doesn’t take long for Chantelle to remember who he is, and they begin to fondly reminisce about old times.

Later in the week, Chantelle bumps into Jags once more — only this time he’s with Kheerat, and she’s with Gray (Toby-Alexander Smith). Of course, Gray wastes little time in making his presence known.
