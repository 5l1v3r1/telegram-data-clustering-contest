Another Pirates Defender On Suspension Tightrope...  

22 Nov 2019, 07:41 by Soccer Laduma

With Orlando Pirates still to be without their captain Happy Jele for their upcoming match, another defender will be going into the fixture walking a suspension tightrope.  

Jele, who missed Pirates’ match against Kaizer Chiefs before the international break, will sit out the second of his two-match suspension for the red card he received against Maritzburg United.

Ntsikelelo Nyauza wore the captain’s armband against Chiefs, as was the case against Stellenbosch FC earlier in an earlier fixture, when Jele had also been suspended. 

However, Nyauza himself is now on a suspension tightrope, after picking up his third yellow card of the campaign, against Chiefs.

Should he play against Polokwane City in Pirates’ next match on Tuesday and recieve one more yellow card, the centre-back will be suspended for the next match against Baroka FC.      

In their last three matches of the year, the Buccaneers will face all three Limpopo top-flight teams in Rise and Shine, Bakgaga and Black Leopards.
