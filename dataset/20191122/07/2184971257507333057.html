Heavy rain expected to sweep across much of the country during the day

22 Nov 2019, 07:25

Most areas will have a cloudy day on Friday.

Patchy rain will spread northwards through the day, with scattered showers elsewhere.

Skies will be brightest across Western Scotland. Through the afternoon, heavy and more persistent rain will spread across many central and southern parts of the UK.
