Kilwinning Christmas Lights – Girl seriously hurt after being hit by car at Ayrshire lights switch on

22 Nov 2019, 07:21 by Katy Pagan

A SCHOOLGIRL has been seriously hurt after being knocked down by a car at a Christmas lights switch on in Ayrshire.

The little girl, understood to be 11, was struck by the motor in Lauchlan Way, Kilwinning at around 6.55pm yesterday evening.

Mercy crews raced to the scene of the festive event after the alarm was raised and the girl was rushed to Crosshouse Hospital, Kilmarnock.

Clyde reports that the girl is in hospital with serious but non-life threatening injuries.

The road was locke down after emergency services were scrambled to the scene.

A spokesman for the force said last night: "We received reports of a female child pedestrian being struck by a vehicle on Lauchlan Way , Kilwinning, at around 6.55pm tonight".

The girl was taken to Crosshouse Hospital in Kilmarnock for treatment.
