'One stumble will not deter him': Anand Mahindra backs Subhash Chandra

22 Nov 2019, 07:59 by BusinessToday.In

Anand Mahindra lauded Chandra's accomplishments and stated that for someone of his stature it is a stumble that will not deter him or his entrepreneurial courage

Anand Mahindra took to Twitter to back Zee Entertainment promoter Subhash Chandra as the company navigates through a change of hands. Mahindra lauded Chandra's accomplishments and stated that for someone of his stature it is a stumble that will not deter him or his entrepreneurial courage.

"Subhash Chandra saw the future of Indian Television & single-handedly created the country's first media & entertainment empire. One stumble will not deter him & his entrepreneurial courage has always been admirable. May he steer his ship to safe harbour," stated Anand Mahindra.
