Rajinikanth on IFFI 2019 award: I thought ‘Do I deserve this honour?’

Rajinikanth was recently honoured with the Icon of Golden Jubilee award at the 50th edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa.

22 Nov 2019, 07:11 by Entertainment Desk

Rajinikanth was honoured with the Icon of Golden Jubilee award at the ongoing edition of International Film Festival of India in Goa. The actor in a chat with Doordarshan shared his surprise when he learned about the honour.

“First when they announced this award for me, it was pleasant.. the shock. I really thought, ‘Do I deserve this honour?’ My heartfelt thanks to the Indian government,” Thalaivar shared. He reminisced about his early days in the film business and said, “My entire film journey, the turning point was meeting K Balachander. He changed my life.”
