Reactions Trail Davido’s ‘A Good Time’ Album

22 Nov 2019, 07:13 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Positive reactions have continued to roll in since Award-winning singer, Davido dropped his much anticipated album ‘A Good Time’.

Concise News reports that ‘A Good Time’ is Davido’s third studio album coming after ‘Omo Baba Olowo’ and ‘Baddest’.

Since the album was released by 12 am on Friday, November 22, music lovers have taken to Twitter to share how they feel about it, with majority showering praises on the DMW boss for a job well done.
