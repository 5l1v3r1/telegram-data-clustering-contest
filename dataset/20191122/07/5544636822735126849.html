EPL Transfer: Liverpool, Arsenal Battle For Nigerian Player

22 Nov 2019, 07:58 by Emmanuel Egobiambu

Premier League clubs Liverpool and Arsenal are battling for the signature of German-born Nigerian star Karim Adeyemi, Concise News reports.

This news medium understands that Karim who was born on 18 January 2002 has been in fine form as a striker for FC Liefering in the Austrian division two.

His showing for the club has drawn Arsenal and Liverpool’s attention and the clubs are now ready to grab him during the January transfer window.

The player started out his club career with Bayern München in 2012 before heading to SpVgg Unterhaching.

And in the start of the 2018/19 season, Adeyemi teamed up with FC Red Bull Salzburg where he is contracted till May 2021.

The player was born to a Nigerian father and Romanian mother in Munich, Germany.
