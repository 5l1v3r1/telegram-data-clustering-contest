Friday's Tottenham Hotspur transfer talk news roundup: Jose Mourinho, Mauricio Pochettino, Bruno Fernandes

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Tottenham Hotspur eye possible January reinforcements.

22 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Andy Brent

The Champions League runners-up broke their transfer record during the summer, but it is possible outgoings which have made most of the headlines with a number of key players reportedly considering exits.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest news on possible comings and goings at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium.

Friday morning's headlines:

Jose Mourinho interested in bringing Zlatan Ibrahimovic to Tottenham Hotspur?
New Tottenham Hotspur manager Jose Mourinho wants Zlatan Ibrahimovic as a backup for leading striker Harry Kane, according to a report. Read more.

Nemanja Matic, Bruno Fernandes, Ruben Dias 'all of Jose Mourinho shortlist'
Nemanja Matic, Bruno Fernandes and Ruben Dias are among the names on Jose Mourinho's shortlist of targets at Tottenham Hotspur, according to a report. Read more.

Jose Mourinho 'wants Bruno Fernandes at Tottenham Hotspur'
Jose Mourinho wants Tottenham Hotspur to sign Sporting Lisbon attacker Bruno Fernandes in January, according to a report. Read more.

Jose Mourinho 'planning talks with Tottenham Hotspur contract rebels'
Jose Mourinho is reportedly set to hold talks with Tottenham Hotspur contract rebels Christian Eriksen, Jan Vertonghen and Toby Alderweireld over the next few weeks. Read more.

Jose Mourinho tells Daniel Levy that Harry Kane must not leave Tottenham Hotspur
Jose Mourinho reportedly tells Tottenham Hotspur chairman Daniel Levy that Harry Kane must not be allowed to leave the club next summer. Read more.

Bayern Munich to appoint Mauricio Pochettino in January?
