Photos Of Stonebwoy As Guest Judge On MTN Hitmaker Season 8 Trend On Social Media

22 Nov 2019, 18:34 by BB Jones

Stonebwoy was made guest judge on Season 8 of the MTN Hitmaker this week, the photos of which have started trending on social media.

Stonebwoy joined regular judges, Richie Mensah, Kaywa and Eazzy as he used his expertise to advise the contestants on their performances to help them make the cut in the industry in future.

The Hitmaker reality show is aimed at giving talented and authentic people who have the ability to sing and rap a platform to build their talents and prepare them for the music industry.
