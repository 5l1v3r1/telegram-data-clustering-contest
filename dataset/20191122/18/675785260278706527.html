Best heart rate monitors for 2019

If you want to keep track of how your heart is doing during physical activity or just throughout the day, these are the best heart rate monitors you can buy now

22 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Brijiena Lovelace, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/brijiena-lovelace/

If you're looking to maximise your health and get the most out of your workouts, a good indicator into your overall fitness is to track your heart rate.

Sure, step counters are great and not a fitness measurement to be sneered at by any means; however, if you want to track how intense you're hitting your workout - or likewise want to be sure you aren't overdoing it - you ticker will let you know.

Nowadays there are a range of different devices that can easily and accurately monitor your heart rate. Although the most accurate readings are said to be found in chest monitors, they aren't always convenient for every type of workout. These days, you can get great readings from your wrist, head or even your ears.

Whatever your preferred workout method - we've rounded up some of the best heart rate monitors to try out now.
