Fort Myers Police Narcotics Unit conducts drug bust in Fort Myers

22 Nov 2019, 18:12 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. — The Fort Myers Police Department Narcotics Unit seized many drugs, cash, and weapons from home in Fort Myers.

FMPD obtained a search warrant for the home of Jerrel Bowman, 24 and Michael Cortez, 25.

When police began their search, they uncovered heroin, cocaine, controlled substances, and drug paraphernalia.

Both men were charged with multiple counts of trafficking and as well as resisting arrest and obstruction of justice.
