RAW Superstar Humberto Carrillo recalls backstage conversation with Vince McMahon

22 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Soumik Datta

WWE Monday Night RAW Superstar Humberto Carrillo was recently in conversation with Super Luchas and during the interview, the former 205 Live star revealed a certain backstage conversation he had with WWE Chairman Vince McMahon while the former was still a part of the Cruiserweight Division.

Humberto Carrillo on what Vince McMahon told him backstage

While speaking with Super Luchas,Carrillo recalled an interesting conversation he had with company Chairman Vince McMahon while backstage on a certain night during his time with WWE 205 Live.

Carrillo stated that during a backstage interaction with Vince McMahon, the latter congratulated him for the work he was putting in and also told him that he was doing things pretty well. McMahon further praised Carrillo for his work and was happy with the eventual outcome, as well. (H/T: Wrestling Inc for the translation)

"On one occasion, when I was in 205 Live. I had the opportunity to talk with him. He congratulated me for my work, told me that I am doing things very well and not despair. He said he likes the way I work and is happy with the results. Those comments, coming from someone like him, are very encouraging."

What's next for Humberto Carrillo?

Earlier this week, Carrillo picked up a vital win over Karl Anderson on RAW and the former is now set to challenge AJ Styles for the WWE United States Championship once again.
