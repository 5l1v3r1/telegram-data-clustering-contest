Insulin Price-Fixing

22 Nov 2019, 18:03 by BRIEF

HOUSTON — Harris County, Texas accuses Eli Lilly & Co., Novo Nordisk, Sanofi-Aventis, Express Scripts, et al. of conspiring to fix insulin prices, creating a “massive slush fund derived from the difference between the reported and net prices,” in Harris County Court.
