Manchester United monitoring Everton forward Richarlison?

Manchester United will reportedly keep a close eye on the situation of Everton forward Richarlison ahead of the January transfer window.

22 Nov 2019, 18:38 by Leo Nieboer

Ole Gunnar Solskjaer has placed a strong emphasis on signing a striker in January following the club's failure to replace Romelu Lukaku and Alexis Sanchez last summer.

Reports have suggested that Mario Mandzukic, Erling Braut Haaland and Callum Wilson are among the club's targets.

According to the Evening Standard, Solskjaer is a big fan of the player and has urged the club to monitor his situation ahead of the upcoming window.

Richarlison has impressed with his performances at Goodison Park, scoring 13 goals in the Premier League - including one against United - last season.

Despite the recent resurgence in front of goal spearheaded by Anthony Martial, United's total of 16 goals from 12 league games has attracted criticism.
