Met Eireann issue status yellow rainfall warning for five counties

22 Nov 2019, 18:07 by Ciara Walsh

A rainfall warning has been issued for five counties in Ireland and it will stay in place until 3pm tomorrow.

Met Eireann says that there will be "thundery" downpours throughout the night, with the Status Yellow warning in place for Carlow, Kilkenny, Wexford, Wicklow and Waterford.

It came into action at 3pm today, so you might want to be careful making your way home tonight.
