Securities Fraud

22 Nov 2019, 18:05 by BRIEF

MANHATTAN — The SEC accused the International Investment Group LLC of “a string of frauds … to cover up tens of millions of dollars in losses on bad bets and keep its investment advisory business afloat,” in federal court.
