Political Asylum

22 Nov 2019, 18:11 by BRIEF

WASHINGTON — A federal lawsuit accuses Attorney General William Barr and top-ranking immigration officials of issuing two “biased, error-filled set(s) of instructions designed to turn back as many asylum seekers as possible,” in violation of federal and international laws and the Constitution.
