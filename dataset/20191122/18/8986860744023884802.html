Shatta Wale insults Ameyaw Debrah over Grammy nominations brouhaha; Fuse ODG joins in

22 Nov 2019, 18:36 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Popular reggae-dancehall musician Shatta Wale has rained insults on popular blogger Ameyaw Debrah over comments he made about his Grammy nominations.

In his usual rants on social media, the “Melissa” hitmaker, used unprintable words the blogger and other media personnel who claimed he is not a Grammy nominee but a participant.

Shatta Wale stated that he cannot comprehend why some media people feel he doesn’t deserve the nominations but will have reacted differently if it was Sarkodie.
