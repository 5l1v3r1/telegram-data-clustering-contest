News Anchor Nick Vasos Accidentally Calls In Sick To The Entire Company, Entire Company Responds

22 Nov 2019, 18:54 by Jamie Samhan

On Thursday night, news anchor Nick Vasos was trying to let his boss know that he would have to call in sick but made a mistake that has since gone viral.

#PrayersForNick and #NickStrong were trending after Vasos accidentally sent the email to the entire company, which includes nearly 200 TV stations.

Vasos’s inbox was flooded with well-wishes from the stations across the country.
