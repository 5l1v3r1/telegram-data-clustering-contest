Review: MSI GeForce GTX 1650 SUPER GAMING X

22 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Hilbert Hagedoorn

MSI released their GTX 1650 SUPER GAMING X, a product with a more than capable cooler as well being factory tweaked. The SUPER edition card comes with 12 Gbps GDDR6 graphics memory, however, it has only got 4GB of it. The card performs well but can be tweaked even better.
