Where Bills stand in AFC playoff picture

22 Nov 2019, 18:00

Here is what we know at the end of Week 11 in the AFC playoff picture following the Kansas City Chiefs' win against the Los Angeles Chargers on Monday night.

AFC East

The New England Patriots (9-1) beat the Philadelphia Eagles on Sunday, so the Buffalo Bills (7-3) remain two games behind for the division lead.

AFC wild card

The Bills have the third-best record in the AFC, but are in position as the first wild card in the AFC because of the division winners.

Currently, New England is the No. 1 seed, followed by No. 2 Baltimore (8-2), No. 3 Kansas City (7-4) and No. 4 Houston (7-4) after their win Thursday against Indianapolis.

The Bills would be the fifth seed and Oakland is the sixth seed (6-4).
