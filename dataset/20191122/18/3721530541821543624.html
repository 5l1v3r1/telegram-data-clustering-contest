PlayStation Plus is on sale at the PlayStation Store for Black Friday

Follow Polygon online:

22 Nov 2019, 18:57 by Michael McWhertor

Sony is getting a head start on Black Friday deals with a sale on the PlayStation Store that starts today, Nov. 22 and runs all the way through Dec. 2. It’s a good opportunity to re-up your PlayStation Plus subscription; a 12-month membership to the service is currently $44.99, down from the standard $59.99.

You can also get a deal on a PlayStation Plus annual membership from Amazon, GameStop, and Green Man Gaming (where it’s actually $42.74).
