James Gallagher confirms Conor McGregor's return to training for UFC return

22 Nov 2019, 10:11

James Gallagher has confirmed Conor McGregor is back in training for his UFC return and backed his SBG team-mate to return to his best.

McGregor announced last month he would fight in January, with UFC lightweights Donald Cerrone and Justin Gaethje leading the race to welcome the Irishman back to the octagon.

Bellator star Gallagher, 23, has trained alongside McGregor since he was a teenager and explained why the gym had been closed to non-members during the day.

Head coach John Kavanagh announced the decision last week, leading to speculation McGregor was officially back in camp.

‘We’ve had real issues with bus loads of people coming into the gym when we’re training,’ Gallagher explained to Metro.co.uk.

‘That’s the reason. You can’t have people wandering in when you’re on the mat and asking for a picture.

‘Now if you’re asking me is Conor back training, yes he is. It doesn’t matter if he’s there or not, people are coming in every day.

‘We had one bus full of Asian tourists pull up. About 50 of them! All over the gym and taking pictures. It’s become a tourist attraction and we can’t have that as fighters.’

McGregor has not fought at all in 2019, with his last bout in October 2018 when he lost to lightweight champion and bitter rival Khabib Nurmagomedov.

UFC president Dana White has refused to be drawn into speculation about McGregor’s official comeback, but the former featherweight and lightweight champion’s manager confirmed talks were ongoing about a January return.

McGregor has not fought regularly in MMA since 2016, fighting just once since defeating Eddie Alvarez to become two-weight champion in November of that year.

But Gallagher has no concerns about McGregor’s insistence he will avenge his loss to Nurmagomedov and believes his team-mate can rediscover his best form.

‘Of course he can get back to his best, and better,’ Gallagher insisted.

‘He can do anything he wants to do when he puts his mind to it. There’s no limits with him. You only limit yourself.’
