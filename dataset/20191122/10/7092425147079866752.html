Cat missing for five years reunited with ecstatic owner

22 Nov 2019, 10:23 by Nick Fitzgerald

Sasha the cat somehow ended up 1,200 miles from his home

A black cat found wandering the streets by the Santa Fe Animal Shelter has been reunited with his owner after five years.

Sasha the cat somehow ended up 1,200 miles from his Portland home in Oregon and was spotted on the streets of Santa Fe in New Mexico.

It remains unknown just how the cat ended up so far away from home. Only Sasha knows these details but miraculously, staff from the Santa Fe Animal Shelter scanned his microchip and discovered 31-year-old Viktor Usov was the pet's owner.
