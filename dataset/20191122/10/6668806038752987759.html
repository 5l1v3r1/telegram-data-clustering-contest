Yu Karke from Dabangg 3 is all things naughty and nice

22 Nov 2019, 10:41 by Analita Seth

Christmas is only a month away and our excitement is double-fold this year because superstar Salman Khan is coming back to the big screen as Chulbul Pandey with Dabangg 3. A blockbuster franchise, Dabangg 3 takes you back into the life of a young Chulbul and narrates the story of how he became Robin Hood Pandey. We are also introduced to his first love, played by debutante Saiee Manjrekar.

While the film will have a lot of flashbacks, we are super stoked to see Salman and Sonakshi bring back the Chulbul -Rajjo romance once again on the screen. After serenading Rajjo with Tere Mast Mast Do Nain in the first instalment, we will be seeing a naughty side of this onscreen couple in Dabangg 3. Giving us a glimpse of the same, Salman Khan just dropped the new song, Yu Karke, and its super fiery.

Sung by the superstar himself, Yu Karke is a break from the storyline of the film and has Sonakshi and Salman grooving in exotic locations. Also keeping up with the tradition, the song has a difficult hook step that Salman doesÂ  effortlessly.
