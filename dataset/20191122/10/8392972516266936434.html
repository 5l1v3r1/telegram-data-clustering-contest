I'm A Celebrity's Jacqueline Jossa screams in terror in first look at tonight's snake-infested trial

She's joined by campmates Myles Stephenson

22 Nov 2019, 10:58 by Rebecca Carter

Jacqueline Jossa screams as she takes on tonight's Bushtucker trial on I'm A Celebrity.

The former EastEnders actress was picked to do the trial by the public last night and she wasn't too pleased.

ITV has released a preview clip of tonight's trial which sees Jacqueline trying to find stars in a fake hotel room while Myles Stephenson is strapped down in a box outside with snakes all over him.

In the clip, Jacqueline walks into the room and begins searching for stars.

She pulls out one drawer which is filled with millworms.

Four minutes gone, need to get a move on now.

Ant asks: "You okay?" as Dec says: "What is it?"

Jacqueline replies: "Yep, yep. Creature worm things. I don't think there's anything in there."

Dec says: "Four minutes gone, need to get a move on now," to which Myles adds: "Come on Jac, you've got this my darling."

She moves on to the bed and lifts up the pillow, which is covering two huge snakes.

She screams again and says: "No! Why would you do that?!"

Myles says: "You've got this Jac. Ooh I can feel [the snakes] in my hair. It's in the afro," as he's seen holding a star.

Last night, Jacqueline - who played Lauren Branning in EastEnders - admitted she was worried about facing the trial.

After it was announced she will be doing the trial, Jacqueline shouted: "No! No!"
