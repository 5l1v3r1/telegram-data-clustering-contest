Rajan Shahi welcomes Helly Shah on sets of ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’

22 Nov 2019, 10:06 by Raj Baddhan

Trust producer Rajan Shahi to make his actors comfortable on the sets of his show. His actors have always spoken highly of him and have expressed how he often makes sure they are happy while shooting the show.

Recently, actor Helly Shah joined the cast of his show ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ for a special appearance and producer Shahi wanted to welcome her warmly. However, he was travelling on her first day of the shoot. So he specially arranged for flowers and a good luck gift to be delivered to her on the set.

Shah will be playing an integral part in the show’s current storyline.
