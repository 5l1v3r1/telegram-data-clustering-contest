On Kartik Aaryan’s 29th birthday, here’s a throwback to his epic monologues in Pyaar Ka Punchnama 1-2 that helped him garner a crazy fan-following

Kartik Aaryan is the national crush of every girl out there, thanks to his charming personality and an even charming screen presence. Over the years, his fan-following has crazily increased, and why not? The actor has managed to carve a niche for himself and has some of the biggest films lined-up in his kitty. And one can’t deny the fact that other than his remarkable script selection, looks and talent, it is Kartik Aaryan’s impressive dialogue delivery that has helped garner a huge fan base. Well, monologues to be precise. And on Kartik Aaryan’s 29th birthday, here’s a rundown of the actor’s whistle-worthy monologues that made him a household name. It was his stint in Pyaar Ka Punchnama that made people aware of Kartik Aaryan’s existence; however, his five-minute-long rant as a frustrated boyfriend made him an overnight sensation. The romantic comedy chronicles the life of three friends with messed-up love lives. However, things get worse when the boys reach their saturation point. Well, then comes Rajat, who starts venting out his exasperation and there’s no end to it. His monologue that begins from “Problem yeh hai ki woh ladki hai, aur kya problem hai,” managed to strike a chord among the viewers, especially guys.
