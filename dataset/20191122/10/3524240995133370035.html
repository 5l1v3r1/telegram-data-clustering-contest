JUST IN: Nigeria’s GDP grows by 2.28 per cent —NBS

22 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Oluwakemi Abimbola

Nigeria’s Gross Domestic Product has grown by 2.28 per cent in the third quarter of 2019, the National Bureau of Statistics has said.

In its quarterly report, NBS said that the figure was better than 2.12% (revised from 1.94%) in the second quarter of 2019 and the third quarter of 2018, in the year-on-year analysis.
