Watch Mac DeMarco Play New Song “I Like Her”

22 Nov 2019, 10:24 by Peter Helman

Bafflingly successful indie-rock star Mac DeMarco has been busy touring Europe in support of his recent album Here Comes The Cowboy. And at his last few shows — in France, Amsterdam, and England — he’s been trotting out a brand new song called “I Like Her.” It sounds like a pretty good Mac DeMarco song, an easygoing and tuneful love song, and you can watch him perform it at London’s Alexandra Palace last night.
