These parts of Oman to get rain today

22 Nov 2019, 10:09 by Times News Service

Muscat: Rainfall is expected today in Muscat, North and South Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta and Dhofar governorates, the Public Authority fo Civil Aviation (PACA) has announced.

The authority said: "Aerial photos show the spread of clouds over the governorates of Muscat , North and South Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta and Dhofar."

PACA said in its weather forecast for Friday, "Clear to partly cloudy skies over parts of Muscat , North and South Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta and Dhofar with chance of isolated rain, and mainly clear skies over the rest of the Sultanate's governorates."

In last two days, most parts of the Sultanate witnessed rainfall ranging between medium and heavy that caused the flow of wadis and some dams to overflow."
