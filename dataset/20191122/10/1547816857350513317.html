Bigg Boss 13: Gauahar Khan SLAMS Sidharth Shukla; Says ‘Baap Pe Jaana Shows What Value You Have For Parents’

22 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Lubna Khan

Bigg Boss 7 winner Gauahar Khan, in her recent Tweet, has slammed Sidharth Shukla for his recent fight with Asim Riaz on Bigg Boss 13. She also appreciated Asim Riaz for understanding the kind of person Sidharth is

Bigg Boss 7 winner Gauahar Khan has been watching and following Bigg Boss 13 closely and has often expressed her opinions on several contestants before. Now, after the physical brawl between Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz, looks like everyone is talking about it. What started as a verbal argument soon turned into a violent fight and the two were seen shoving each other. Now, after Dolly Bindra and Vindu Dara Singh, Gauahar Khan has commented on the fight, but contrary to others’ opinions, she has slammed Sidharth Shukla.

Gauahar Khan took to Twitter and in her tweet, she slammed Sidharth, while also mentioning that Asim Riaz has understood the kind of person Sidharth is. Her tweet read, “How the hell is Siddharth Shukla allowed to push people like that ?Instigating is a part of the show ! If it makes u hit someone then mission accomplished! Hats off to Asim to have understood someone enough ! Baap pe jaana , har baat mein , shows what value u have for parents!” ALSO READ: Bigg Boss 13: Fans Trend ‘Throw Asim Riaz Out’, Slam Him For Instigating Sidharth Shukla To Lose Temper
