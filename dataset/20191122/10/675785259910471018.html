Glossybox is launching a Christmas beauty box worth £130 - and it costs only £35

If you missed out on the Glossybox beauty advent calendar, then you'll be pleased to hear that the brand is releasing a limited-edition Christmas box - and it contains £130 worth of products

22 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Melisha Kaur, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/melisha-kaur/

'Tis the season to spoil your loved ones (or yourself) and Glossybox has the perfect beauty gift on its way.

Just in time for the festive period, the beauty giant is launching a limited-edition Christmas box filled with over £130 worth of prettifying and pampering goodies.

Inside the gloriously designed box are 11 must-have products from the likes of Huda Beauty, NARS, Laura Mercier and bareMinerals.

The best part? Shoppers can get their hands on one for a bargain price of £35 - unless you're a Glossybox subscriber, in which case you only pay £30.

It'll be available at glossybox.co.uk from 7am on Monday December 2nd, with subscribers able to buy the box from 12pm on the same day.

Eleanore Nash, Editor at Glossybox UK, said: "To celebrate the end of 2019, we’ve curated our best Limited Edition yet. The iconic HUDA palette allows you to create endless shimmery party looks, while the award-winning NARS blush will give you the healthy, rosy glow you need this time of year.

"We’re also really excited to be introducing beauty lovers to bareMinerals’ first-of-its kind vegan moisturiser infused with a mineral SPF. This box really does feature some of the hottest beauty brands out there!"

The beautiful box boasts the same rose gold packaging as the 2019 Glossybox advent calendar, which was an instant sell-out.

If you missed out on the brand's annual countdown, then this new festive box provides another great way to enjoy some of the best beauty brands around.

However beauty fans will need to be fast, because some of the brand's previous limited-edition boxes have sold in less than half an hour.

The collection includes a Huda Beauty eyeshadow palette worth £25 and a Luxie eyeshadow brush worth £14, which will certainly come in handy for those upcoming Christmas parties.

We've listed all of the products below.

  • bareMinerals Complexion Rescue Defense SPF 30 (deluxe mini), worth £9.10
  • Luxie Precision Shader Eyeshadow Brush (full size), worth £14
  • Rituals Nuit a Marrakech Perfume (travel size), worth £9.90
  • Sprectrum Collections Dark Matter Mascara (full size), worth £12.99
