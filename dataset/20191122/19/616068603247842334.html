Charity auction opens

22 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Colin Dacre

What does lunch with the Mayor, a track day at Area 27, bagpipe lessons and a two-night stay in Whitehorse have in common?

They are all up for grabs in this year’s South Okanagan Similkameen Volunteer Centre’s online auction. 

The annual event raises funds to support volunteers and encourage volunteerism in the region, acting as one of the organization’s largest fundraisers of the year. 

Bidding for items opened Friday at 5 p.m. and runs through to Dec. 1.
