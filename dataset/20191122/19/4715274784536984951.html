CREBA, La Salle opens property valuation course.

22 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Manila Standard

The Chamber of Real Estate & Builders’ Associations Inc. (CREBA), in coordination with the De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde School of Professional and Continuing Education (DLS-CSB SPACE), recently opened it certificate course, “Highest and Best Uses for Real Estate.” The course is the fourth of six modules under the Executive Diploma Program in Real Estate Management (EDPREM) offered by the two leading institutions since 2002.

Classes are ongoing at the ninth floor of the CSB School of Design and Arts Campus at Pablo Ocampo Street, Malate, Manila. CREBA, headed by national president Noel Toti M. Cariño, is the umbrella organization of the real estate and housing industry composed of property developers, builders, contractors, suppliers and manufacturers of construction materials. The course  tackles effective tools for real estate marketing, land laws and property titling, planning and development, financial analysis and management and project feasibility studies. For more information, please contact the following email addresses:   creba_national@yahoo.com or space@benilde.edu.ph.
