Emily Atack has heart to heart with Adam Thomas amid Katie Price feud

22 Nov 2019, 19:52

Emily Atack had a heart to heart with Adam Thomas as the two took some time away from their presenting duties for I’m A Celebrity: Extra Camp.

And the 29-year-old didn’t seem to have a care in the world as the duo took a break from the jungle – and it seems like her feud with Katie Price was the last thing on her mind as she enjoyed some down time.

Emily donned a jungle-appropriate camouflage dress, complete with sunglasses, while Adam opted for a cool but casual grey sleeveless t-shirt.

As she sipped on her drink, she and Adam appeared to be very busy in conversation.

The two are new presenters for the spin-off series to I’m A Celebrity, with both replacing Scarlett Moffatt and Joe Swash respectively, after they previously quit their roles earlier in the year.

Earlier in the week, Emily threw some shade towards former I’m A Celeb contestants Katie Price and Peter Andre, who famously fell in love with each other when they competed on the show together in 2004.

During Tuesday night’s edition of Extra Camp, while watching Adam and fellow co-host Joel Dommett take a dip in the jungle’s iconic lake, they were all reminded of when Katie and Peter famously smooched in the water.

‘This is where Katie Price and Peter Andre had fondles,’ Joel boasted.

‘I hope you boys went to the clinic after being in that pool,’ Emily replied.

And Katie has not been left very impressed by what the former Inbetweeners star had to say.

‘Katie says she’s not watching the show, but when Emily Atack mentioned her on Extra Camp all her friends told her what she said,’ a source shared to The Sun.

‘She was telling people that she can’t believe Emily would say something so snide and if she “wants a problem, I’ll give her a problem.”’

The former glamour model has since made a personal attack on Emily in a video shared to her Instagram story, as she claimed she was heading to the jungle to confront her in person.

‘So Emily Atack you think it’s clever do you to slag me of? Who are you anyway?’ she said. ‘Looks like I’m coming for you.

‘Because if you’ve got something to say don’t say it behind my back on telly, say it to me.’
