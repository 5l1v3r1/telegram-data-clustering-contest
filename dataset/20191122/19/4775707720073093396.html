Sid Meier's Civilization VI Achievement List Revealed

By Rich Stone, 22 Nov 2019

We have just picked up the Sid Meier's Civilization VI Achievement list.

There are 57 achievements worth a total of 1,345 Gamerscore, none of which are secret.

Name Description Gamerscore
Escort Service Put two units into a formation 10
Game, Settler, Match Win a game at Settler difficulty or harder 15
Machiavelli's Great Work Win a game at Prince difficulty or harder 30
God-Like Win a game on Deity 95
Mission to Mars Win a regular game with a Science victory 30

Blue Jeans and Pop Music Win a regular game with a Culture victory 30

Nobody Expects the Spanish Inquisition Launch an Inquisition as Spain 15
