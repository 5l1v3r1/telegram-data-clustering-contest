Who is I'm A Celebrity's Cliff Parisi married to?

22 Nov 2019, 19:00

Cliff Parisi has entered the I’m A Celebrity jungle as a late arrival to the show with new fan-favourite and fellow soap actor Andrew Whyment.

Cliff is best known for appearing as Minty Peterson in EastEnders for eight years from 2002 until 2010 as well as appearing on Casualty, Hollyoaks and Call the Midwife.

While his professional life in soap-land is well-documented, let’s take a closer look at Cliff’s personal life ahead of his next appearance in the jungle tonight.

Who is Cliff Parisi married to?

Cliff, 59, has been married to BBC producer Tara Wyer since 2010.

They met while he was working on EastEnders.

The newest I’m A Celeb campmate has a child, Arthur, with his Tara and three children from previous relationships – Mandy, Dean and Jack, who are all grown now.
