Hompa Mundjembwe's coronation stopped after an interdict from Mukwahepo clan

22 Nov 2019, 19:52

The Shambyu chieftainship dispute is far from over, despite the Government having recognised Sophia Mundjembwe-Kanyetu as the new Hompa of the tribe.
The High Court has stopped the coronation of Mundjembwe scheduled for today, after the Mukwahepo clan succeeded in its urgent application, to oppose the recognition.
