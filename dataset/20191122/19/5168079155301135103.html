Child falls from shopping mall in Oman

22 Nov 2019, 19:37 by Times News Service

Muscat: A child fell from the third floor of a shopping mall in Muscat governorate, Public Authority of Civil Defence and Ambulances ( PACDA) has confirmed.

According to a top official, the incident happened at 3:23pm on Friday and the condition of the child is in critical stage and is admitted in intensive care unit.
