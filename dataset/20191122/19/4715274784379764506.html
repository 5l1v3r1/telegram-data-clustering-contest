UP CFA mounts ArtGifts 2019

22 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

The University of the Philippines College of Fine Arts stages ArtGifts 2019 beginning Nov. 22 at the UP CFA gallery as a way of sharing art with the UP community this Christmas.

The event features an exhibit-sale of works such as drawings, paintings, prints, photographs, illustrations, digital art, sculptures, ceramics, pottery, and other small works.

Participating artists include Dean Mitzi Reyes, Benjie Cabangis, Neil Doloricon, Bim Bacaltos, Ernie Enrique, Romalito Austria, Rita Godinez, Mark San Valentin, Manolo Sicat, Doi Rosete, Joey Tanedo, Dem Bitantes, and Ninel Constantino, among others.Â
