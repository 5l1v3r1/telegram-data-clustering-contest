Donald Trump’s Childish Handwriting Is Now A Free Font

22 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Geoff Desreumaux

Donald Trump’s infamous childish handwriting is now a free font anyone can download, thanks to Mark Davis, a graphic designer at BuzzFeed and Font Bureau.

There are three interesting facts about Donald Trump: his incessant tweeting, he does not use computers (outside of his iPhone) and has the worse/funniest handwriting you would have ever seen.

Now thanks to the success of a recent satirical article on BuzzFeed, the company decided to task Mark Davis, one of their graphic designers who also work for Font Bureau, to recreate Donald’s handwriting as a custom font. It is called Tiny Hand (!).

Tiny Hand is available for free download here.
