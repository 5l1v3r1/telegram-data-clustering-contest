First Disappointment from ‘Ala Vaikuntapuramlo’

22 Nov 2019, 20:02 by Jhansi

The third single ‘OMG Daddy’ from ‘Ala Vaikuntapuramlo‘ came out, and it’s not as catchy as the previous two songs that came out creating a sensation, immediately.

Unless there are visuals that may bring some funny vibes it’s a bit disappointment from ‘Ala Vaikuntapuramlo’, in fact, its the first disappointment after SS Thaman delivered two catchy and well-written songs aka ‘Samajavaragamana’ and ‘Ramulo Ramula’.

Coming to the song, it’s definitely different from the other two songs. After a class and a mass treat, this third song is a hip hop song by Bigg Boss star Roll Rida. It might look very impressive with the visuals. Anyway, the movie has got enough buzz because of the previous two songs.

Allu Arjun will be coming with this classy mass treat for the upcoming Sankranthi in January 2020. Though this song was a bit disappointing, the other two songs have already made it up for the rest of the album, haven’t they?
