The Highest-Paid Managers in The World Right Now

22 Nov 2019, 20:01 by David Kappel

Jose Mourinho has jumped straight back among the highest-paid coaches in England, but where does he feature in comparison to the likes of Zinedine Zidane and the rest of world football's top-earning managers?

Click through the gallery above to see the highest-paid managers in football, and how much they earn annually, according to Tuttomercato.

The new Tottenham Hotspur boss has reportedly put pen to paper on a deal worth a whopping £15 million (R284 million) a year, which makes him the second highest-paid coach in England.

Only Manchester City boss Pep Guardiola earns more, with a whopping annual salary of £20 million (R378 million).

The abovementioned pair are also the top-earning managers in world football right now, with Atletico Madrid's Diego Simeone, who takes home £13 million (R246 million) annually, completing the top three in a list recently published by Tuttomercato.

He shares that spot with Los Blancos boss Zinedine Zidane, who also pockets £10 million a year since returning to the Santiago Bernabeu.
