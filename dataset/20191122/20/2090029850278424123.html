BBNaija’s Thelma receives car gift from fans during meet and greet (video & photos)

22 Nov 2019, 20:29 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Former housemate and reality star of the recently concluded reality TV show Big Brother Naija season 4, Thelma has just been gifted a Mercedes car gift by fans during her meet and greet.

According to reports, fans of the reality star presented her the Benz gift during her meet and greet session with well wishers.

In the video shared online, the reality star was utterly speechless as she became emotional and enjoyed the paparazzi as light, camera and cheering adorned the moment.

She took to her Instagram page some moments ago to share photos and videos of her new car gift.
