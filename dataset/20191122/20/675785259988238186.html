Question Time audience boos and jeers Boris Johnson as he walks onto set

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

22 Nov 2019, 20:33 by Neil Murphy

Boris Johnson was booed as he appeared on tonight's BBC Question Time election special.

The Prime Minister made the appearance at the Sheffield studio alongside leaders of UK's political parties.

He is the last of the leaders to answer questions ahead of the upcoming General Election on December 12.
