Chelsea hero explains why Fikayo Tomori is already better than Man United's Harry Maguire

Fikayo Tomori has made an impressive ascent at Chelsea during the 2019/20 campaign

22 Nov 2019, 20:15 by Paul Clarke

Former Chelsea defender Frank Sinclair has provided a prediction about Fikayo Tomori that fans will love.

Tomori has come on leaps and bounds this season after spending last term at Derby County, while he also featured for the likes of Hull City and Brighton.

But the 2019/20 campaign has seen a significant ascent from the centre-back, who is now not only starting for Chelsea, he has also broken his duck for England.

The 21-year-old is well respected in the Chelsea dressing room and the same can be said about England, with manager Gareth Southgate quick to offer the young gun a chance to shine in the Euro 2020 qualifier against Kosovo.

But Sinclair feels that Tomori can go way beyond simply making the odd appearance for England.

He feels that the pacey defender has what it takes to be a regular, while he insists that Tomori is already better than fellow centre-back Harry Maguire of Manchester United.

"Tomori has been great this season. He had a terrific experience at Derby. He made a big impact but that doesn't always tell the correct story about whether he can step up," Sinclair, who was speaking on behalf of GentingBet, told football.london.

"He has been a massive part of how well Chelsea have been defensively in recent times. He has a lot of pace, he's good on the ball and he's great in one vs one situations. He is a player who has been talked about for a long time and it's no surprise that he's been talked about for England.

"I think he should be in the England squad and playing regularly. I think Tomori is a better player than all of them and that includes Harry Maguire.
