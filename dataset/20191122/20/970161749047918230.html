Road rage driver spits food into woman's mouth during traffic fight

22 Nov 2019, 20:24

A disgusting driver spat food into a woman’s mouth during a road rage dispute, police say.

David Paul Wipperman, 61, was arrested on Thursday in connection with the vile attack on the female driver in Pinellas County, Florida.

Police said Wipperman got into a road rage incident with the woman on on October 9, although it is unclear how the fight began.

Wipperman reportedly got out of his Chevy S-10 truck and approached the woman in her 2014 Kia sedan.

The woman rolled down her window and apologized to Wipperman, who spat food that he had been chewing into her face.

‘The food struck the victim in the face and entered (her) mouth,’ Police wrote in the arrest report.

Wipperman then opened the driver’s door, shouted at her and stuck his finger in her face, police said.

He reportedly left after ‘a passerby interrupted the incident,’ according to the Smoking Gun.

Police did not say why it took several weeks for Wipperman to be arrested. He has since been charged with felony battery and burglary of an occupied vehicle.

Wipperman was booked at the Pinellas County Jail Friday in lieu of $12,5000.

According to public records, Wipperman has an extensive criminal past that includes arrests for battery ,grand theft, battery with a deadly weapon and disorderly conduct.
