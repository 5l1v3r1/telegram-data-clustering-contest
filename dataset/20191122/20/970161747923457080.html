Are Kendall and Kylie Jenner and the Kardashians watching Caitlyn on I’m A Celeb

22 Nov 2019, 20:00

Caitlyn Jenner has quite literally thrown herself into her I’m A Celebrity stint as, after skydiving into the jungle and getting off to something of a rocky start in the camp, she’s now bonding with the other women through the medium of dance.

In scenes last night, Nadine Coyle showed Caitlyn how to dance to the Girls Aloud number The Promise.

She said later in the hut: ‘I taught the girls a little bit of The Promise routine, Jacqueline and Adele knew most of it already.

‘Caitlyn just threw herself into it, we’ll teach her Sound of the Underground next.’

As Caitlyn settles into the jungle after that emotional first night, many fans have been left wondering whether or not the Kardashian/Jenner clan are tuning in.

Are Kendall and Kylie Jenner watching I’m A Celebrity?

At the time of writing, none of the Kardashian clan have spoken publically about Caitlyn’s appearance on the show.

The same goes for Caitlyn’s biological daughters Kendall and Kylie – so we don’t know whether they’re tuning in or not.

Caitlyn’s relationship with that side of her family has seen some strain in the past.

As seen on the family’s hit reality TV show, Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Caitlyn and members of the Kardashian clan went through a rough patch after Caitlyn released her memoir as it was perceived that Caitlyn was making digs at her ex-wife Kris and the Kardashian lifestyle.

In the book, Caitlyn wrote ‘that anytime a Kardashian is on television, many in the public tend to think it is a publicity stunt to make money’.

These days, Caitlyn’s relationship with her ex, Kylie, Kendall and step-children has largely improved.

She’s still close with Kendall and Kylie, and Kim, Kris, and Kourtney have all hung out with her recently either at Christmas or her 70th birthday bash last month.

However her relationship with Khloe still appears strained, as she was not seen at Caitlyn’s 70th.

On the Lorraine Show, Ross King previously asked the reality star: ‘Will having a child affect anything between you and Caitlyn?’

‘I don’t think it affects anything with Caitlyn. Things are just as they are,’ Khloe responded.

There’s a good chance that at least one member of the Kardashian clan is keeping up with Caitlyn on I’m A Celebrity as a source has said that Kris is secretly worried that Caitlyn will spill some secrets about the family.

They told The Sun: ‘Kris doesn’t care what Caitlyn says about her or their marriage – after all, she had her book and her TV show – but she’s scared and horrified about Caitlyn damaging Kim or Kylie’s brands by telling campmates about their off-screen private lives.’

This isn’t the first time that someone connected to the Kardashians has been on a reality TV show on this side of the pond, as Kim’s friend Jonathan Cheban was seen on Gemma Collins’ Diva Forever in August.

Meanwhile Khloe’s longtime friend Malika Haqq appeared on Celebrity Big Brother in 2017, with Khloe reaching out to fans at the time for help with finding a way to watch the show from the US.

She took to Twitter to say: ‘I am so happy for @ForeverMalika being on CBB but I hate that I can’t watch. I have to know what’s going on in there!! Is she ok? I’m trying to find links! Can anyone help me?’

I’m A Celebrity continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
