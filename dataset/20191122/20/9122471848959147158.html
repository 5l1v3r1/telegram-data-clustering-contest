Injury Update For Steelers WR JuJu Smith-Schuster

22 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Alek Arend

The Steelers are looking to bounce back after their disastrous defeat and subsequent brawl against the Browns two Thursdays ago. But Pittsburgh will be without one of its offensive stars against the Bengals on Sunday: wide receiver JuJu Smith-Schuster. This could spell trouble for QB Mason Rudolph and the Steelers.

Smith-Schuster has officially been ruled out for Sunday’s AFC North Division contest. ESPN’s Adam Schefter was first to report the news.

As listed in the report, running back James Conner – who’s dealt with multiple injuries this season – will miss Sunday’s game as well. The Steelers’ offense will have to find some answers to avoid an upset on Sunday.
