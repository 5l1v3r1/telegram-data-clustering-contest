Boris Johnson sneaks into Question Time studio while Jeremy Corbyn greets crowds

The Tory party leader made a low-key entrance, shortly after Jeremy Corbyn greeted large crowds gathered outside the studio

22 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Dave Burke

Boris Johnson has been pictured sneaking into the studio of tonight's BBC Question Time by a back door - not long after Jeremy Corbyn greeted crowds outside.

The Tory party leader made a low key entrance ahead of his questioning on the flagship politics show.
