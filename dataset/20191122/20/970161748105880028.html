Who ate the strawberries in I'm A Celebrity after the Dingo Dollar challenge?

22 Nov 2019, 20:06

Who can forget the infamous Strawberry Gate situation in I’m A Celebrity back in 2017?

In I’m A Celebrity last night Kate Garraway expressed her dismay that after winning Wagon Wheels during their Dingo Dollar challenge, Nadine Coyle and Ian Wright chose to take their winnings back to the main camp and give the treats to the celebs in jungle jail.

But, how would Kate have felt had she been a campmate back in 2017, when the celebrities learnt that the two stars who had been sent to complete the Dingo Dollar challenge decided to eat the reward for their success – a basket of strawberries and cream – between the two of them, instead of returning to share the treat with everyone?

The shock decision was an absolute scandal at the time and is still the only time in I’m A Celebrity history that two celebrities have decided to eat the Dingo Dollar treat reward themselves instead of taking the food back to the camp to share.
