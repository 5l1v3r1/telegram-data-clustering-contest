'Brave' child rose from sickbed to give evidence that jailed pedophile for life

22 Nov 2019, 20:17

A little girl was hailed for her bravery after she rose from her sickbed to testify against a pedophile later jailed for life.

The unnamed youngster insisted on coming to court to share the abuse she suffered at the hands of Kristopher Tetreault, 36, despite running a 102F fever.

Tetreault, of North Port in Florida, was convicted of two counts of sexual battery on Thursday, and handed two life sentences.

Prosecutor Ryan Felix said: ‘I am so proud of this child.

‘On the morning of her testimony, she had a 102-degree fever, yet she was able to bravely testify against her abuser while he was seated in the courtroom.

‘Because of her courage, Tetreault will never harm a child again.’

He molested his victim, whose relationship to him was not outlined, while they watched a movie together on May 1 this year.

The girl told an adult, with her mother immediately calling police.

During questioning, Tetreault admitted abusing the same girl on previous occasions.

The youngster was less than 12 years-old throughout the period of time she was abused.

DNA tests were performed which discovered the pervert’s skin cells inside his victim’s underwear.

The little girl told police that Tetreault had told her that people would blame her if she told them what he was doing to her.

Tetreault’s sentences will run consecutively, meaning he will die behind bars for his crimes.
