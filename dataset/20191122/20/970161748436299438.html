Marina Joyce returns to YouTube after she was previously reported missing

22 Nov 2019, 20:49

YouTuber Marina Joyce has made a return to her YouTube channel with her first video in five months, after she was previously reported missing.

The vlogger returned to the video sharing website this week to upload a video of a clothing haul for her merchandise, as she can be seen showing off a series of different clothing items from her merchandise line in a park.

Fans were overjoyed to see Marina back and active again on her channel, and later took to the comments to welcome her home.

‘SHE’S BACK!!!’ one fan commented.

‘Finally she posted something,’ another chimed in.

‘Yay you’re back, I’m so happy to see you again,’ another added.

‘OMG THANK GOD YOU ARE OK!!!!’ another fan wrote.

It comes after the 22-year-old had previously been reported missing in August, with the charity Missing People releasing an appeal for her to get in touch.
