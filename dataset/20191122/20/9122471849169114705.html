Ed Orgeron Has A Message For Critics Of LSU’s Defense

22 Nov 2019, 20:31 by Andrew McCarty

The LSU Tigers are the No. 1 team in the nation thanks to their revamped offense. Star quarterback Joe Burrow is the Heisman Trophy front-runner for the second-highest scoring offense in the country.

However, there remain serious questions about LSU’s defense.

Just last weekend, the Tigers gave up over 600 yards – 400 on the ground – and 37 points to Ole Miss. Six of LSU’s 10 opponents have scored at least 20 points, while four teams have scored at least 37 points against the Tigers.

However, head coach Ed Orgeron remains confident in his defense.

“Hell no! I love our defense. We’re 10-0. When you talk about that other stuff, let’s block out the noise. All that stuff on TV don’t matter. We gotta play football games. We gotta focus in on the task at hand, and right now, it’s beating Arkansas, and that’s all we’re focusing on.”
