PICTURES: Soldiers in police gear attacked MDC supporters

22 Nov 2019, 04:30

Jonathan Moyo has claimed that MDC supporters were on Wednesday violently dispersed by a combined police, army and CIO team.

Posting on Twitter, Moyo said:

The Police Support Unit is currently conducting #CrowdControl training at 2 Brigade, Cranbone, bringing together the Army, Police & CIO. The vicious “cops” that unleashed yesterday’s gratuitous violence on MDC supporters, bystanders & passersbys came from the 2 Brigade Unit!

Moyo’s theory concurrs with that of Team Pachedu who suggested that the sjamboks which were used to beat protesters are used by soldiers and not the police.
