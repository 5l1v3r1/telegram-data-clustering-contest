Best respect quotes for every occasion

22 Nov 2019, 04:19

Considering other people's wishes and desires is the foundation of healthy communication in our society. If someone wants to be valued highly, they need to give this attitude back to other people. These inspirational respect quotes will help you to learn how to show regard for other people and yourself.

Respect is not only a feeling of admiration for someone but also regard for other people's wishes and rights. There are many various understandings of this term, and the following quotes about respect will help you see what it truly means to have this feeling towards others and yourself.

Respect is earned quotes

Through lessons in life, we learn that you should earn consideration instead of demanding it just because of your status. Treating people right will get you the same in return. Many respect others quotes mention that this treatment should be earned by being a good and caring person.

Age is not a defining factor in caring about a person's opinions and feelings.

  • "Give to every other human being every right that you claim for yourself." - Thomas Paine

If you think about your most basic rights, you will realise that every single human wants the same for themselves.

  • "Treat people the way you want to be treated. Talk to people the way you want to be talked to. Respect is earned, not given." - Hussein Nishah

Respect women quotes

Women have been through a lot of mistreating and discrimination over the years. Even now, they have to face harmful stereotypes and disregard on a daily basis. These respect woman quotes are important for understanding that ladies play a valuable role in our lives and deserve the best treatment.

  • "If you want to be treated like a king, then treat your woman like a queen." - Anonymous

The rule "treat others the same way you want to be treated" also applies in romantic relationships.

  • “When a woman is talking to you, listen to what she says with her eyes.” - Victor Hugo

Listening and being attentive to her emotions is the best way to show a woman that you take her feelings into consideration.

  • “It’s her right to be treated with love and dignity.” - Anonymous

  • “The measure of any society is how it treats its women and girls.” - Michelle Obama

  • "Stop teaching your daughters how to defend themselves from the unwanted advances of men. Instead, teach your sons how to respect and value a woman." - Anonymous

Respect your parents quotes

Our parents are the ones who gave us life, and it is always important to remember and acknowledge their contribution to our existence.

  • "When you look into your mother’s eyes, you know that is the purest love you can find on this earth." - Mitch Albom

A mother's love has no equivalent, and this alone should be a valid reason to value your parents.

  • “Children begin by loving their parents; as they grow older they judge them; sometimes they forgive them.” - Oscar Wilde

This quote is important because our parents are human, and sometimes it can be hard to forgive their faults. However, treating them with dignity makes you a bigger person.

  • “Appreciate your parents. You never know what sacrifices they went through for you.” - Anonymous

Self respect quotes

One person that you should always remember to respect is yourself. Treating yourself right is just as important as doing it to everyone else. If you need a reminder about it right now, here are some respect yourself quotes.

  • “You yourself, as much as anybody in the entire universe, deserve your love and affection.” - Buddha

Some people seek approval from others but forget about taking care of themselves.

  • “It's a funny thing about life, once you begin to take note of the things you are grateful for, you begin to lose sight of the things that you lack.” - Germany Kent

Focusing on the positives in life can free you of destructive mindsets.

  • “Love yourself first and everything else falls into line. You really have to love yourself to get anything done in this world.” - Lucille Ball

Once you love yourself unconditionally, it becomes so much easier to get through life.
