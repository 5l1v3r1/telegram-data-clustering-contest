22 Nov 2019, 04:54 by Careers Desk

CG Vayapam CPEB assistant teacher result 2019: The Chhattisgarh Professional Examination Board (CPEB) declared the result for the online exam conducted to hire at the post of lecturer, assistant teacher. The exam was conducted on August 25. Candidates can check their result at the official websites, cgvyapam.choice.gov.in or vyapam.cgstate.gov.in.

A total of 14276 posts were advertised and will be filled through this recruitment exam including both E and T cadre. Of the total, 5,745 posts were for teachers and 3,177 for lecturer. The result for SEDT19 exam has been declared, under which recruitment result for the post of teachers of English, life science, mathematics, agriculture and PE subjects has been announced.

CG Vayapam assistant teacher result 2019: How to check

Step 1: Visit the official website, cgvyapam.choice.gov.in
Step 2: Click on the tab ‘result’ in the pink box on the homepage
Step 3: Click on ‘final result’ next to SEDT exam
Step 4: You will be redirected to a new page
Step 5: Fill in roll number, click on ‘show’
Step 6: Result will appear, download

The answer key for these posts was released on September 25 at the official website for which objections were to be declared by September 30, 5 pm. A final answer key was released on November 21 and the result is based on the final answer key.
