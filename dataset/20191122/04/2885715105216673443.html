We can’t say goodbye: Mauricio Pochettino leaves emotional message for Tottenham players

Mauricio Pochettino parted ways with the club on Wednesday and in the span of eleven hours the North London club announced Jose Mourinho as his replacement.

22 Nov 2019, 04:29 by Sports Desk

Two days after ending his stint at Tottenham Hotspur, Mauricio Pochettino left an emotional message for his players. The Argentine left a note on the tactics board, which read: “you will always be in our hearts.”

The manager, who helped Tottenham reach the finals of the UEFA Champions League in the previous edition, parted ways with the club on Wednesday and in the span of eleven hours the North London club announced Jose Mourinho as his replacement. The Argentine spent over five and a half years at the club.

Pictures of Pochettino writing an emotional message was shared by his assistant Jesus Perez on Twitter.
