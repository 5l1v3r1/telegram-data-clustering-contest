Huawei Mate X 5G foldable phone sells out for the third time
5 (100%) 2 votes

A couple of weeks ago, the Huawei Mate X 5G foldable phone officially hit the market. Since then, it has been on sale three times. The third official sale took place today and this 16,999 ($2416) machine sold out swiftly again. Since we don’t have the official number of units that hit the store, we cant not be sure about the market acceptance of this device. Certainly, 500 units will not be a challenge to sell. Nevertheless, the user rating of this device on Huawei’s official mall exceeds 200 for now. Giving that only a small fraction of buyers come back to give ratings, it seems that this device is quite popular. The fourth round of sales will take place at 10:08 on November 29.

Recently, Huawei officially announced the maintenance price of the Huawei Mate X. This includes 278 yuan ($40) for battery replacement and 7080 yuan ($1006) for replacement/ screen repair. Repair or maintenance of the motherboard costs 3579 yuan ($509) while 361 yuan ($51) will handle replacement/repair of the battery cover. For the camera, replacement or repair will cost 698 yuan ($99).

Huawei Mate X details

Huawei Mate X uses a flexible foldable display that can expand up to 8 inches. It can transform into a 6.6-inch screen as well as a 6.38 display screen for one-hand operation. The thickness of the body is only 5.4mm. Huawei Mate X comes with Huawei’s first 7nm 5G multimode chip, Balong 5000+ Kirin 980. Other batches upgrades to the new Kirin 990 5G. This phone also comes with a 4500 mAh battery which supports 55W fast charge which delivers 85% in 30 minutes. It is equipped with dual card design and supports 4G and 5G networks.

In the camera configuration, this device comes with a triple camera setup. It uses a 40MP super-sensing camera (27mm) f/1.8, an 8MP telephoto lens (80mm) f/2.4 which supports OIS optical anti-shake and a 16MP super wide-angle lens (17mm) f/2.2. Its 3D depth sensor supports professional-grade blur effects. Mate X’s lens supports ISO 204800 ultra-high sensitivity, 45x zoom range (ultra wide-angle -3x optical – 30x digital), 2.5cm super macro shooting, and OIS + AIS super anti-shake.
