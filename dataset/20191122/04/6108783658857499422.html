Black Future 88 available now on PC, Switch

22 Nov 2019, 04:00 by Steve Wright

A "synth-punk Contra with tons of explosive twists."

Black Future 88 is now available on Windows PC and Nintendo Switch, Good Shepherd Entertainment and indie developer SuperScarySnakes have confirmed.

“Black Future 88 is synth-punk Contra with tons of explosive twists. In a grim alternate history where it’s always 1988, you’ll ascend a procedurally generated tower through a merciless onslaught of bullets, lasers and steel on a mission to exact revenge on the maniac who destroyed the world with nuclear fire,” Good Shepherd said of the game. “Battle homicidal robots, cutthroats, bosses and more in a death trap that’s constantly evolving to kill you.”

Available on both Windows PC and Switch, Black Future 88 is priced at $28.95 AUD or $19.99 USD alongside a 10% launch discount that will be avaliable until 28 November.
