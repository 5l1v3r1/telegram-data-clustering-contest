Mompha: Nigerians reacts to N5m lawsuit against EFCC

22 Nov 2019, 03:42

Social media is currently buzzing with reactions following the news of detained alleged fraudster and social media big boy, Mompha's intention to sue the EFCC for N5m over unlawful detention.

Earlier on, Legit.ng reported the news of how Mompha, slammed the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC), with a N5m lawsuit. He filed the fundamental rights enforcement suit alleging unlawful detention and a violation of his rights.

Following this, many Nigerians have taken to social media to react to the news. Some have tagged the big money spender a cunning man who is trying to flee from the grasp of the anti-graft agency.
