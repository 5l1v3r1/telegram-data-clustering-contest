Tip: Nokia 9.1 PureView dummy images (claimed) with case emerge

22 Nov 2019, 03:56 by Nayan

Nokia 9.1 PureView, the successor to Nokia 9 PureView may be the next big smartphone offering from HMD. Now a known tipster has sent us some images of what looks like the Nokia 9.1 PureView dummy wearing a case.

The images reveal a wider smartphone than Nokia 9 PureView but with the similar Penta-camera module. Design wise also the Nokia 9.1 looks a bit different from Nokia 9 PureView if one notices the back and side profiles.

While we have earlier reported about HMD targetting Q4 2019 for Nokia 9.1 PureView launch, HMD has moved very slowly in last few months and hence the launch in Q4 2019 seems a bit difficult now. But we hope that HMD surprises us this time.
