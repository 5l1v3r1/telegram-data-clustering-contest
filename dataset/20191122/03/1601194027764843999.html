Stone Cold Steve Austin reacts to CM Punk's return

22 Nov 2019, 03:07 by Abhilash Mendhe

WWE Hall of Famer Stone Cold Steve Austin appeared on the State of Combat podcast earlier this week and discussed a bunch of topics. Austin opened up on former WWE Champion CM Punk working for WWE Backstage.

After he made his shocking appearance on WWE Backstage last week, CM Punk appeared on the show as a special contributor and analyst on this week's edition. The Straight Edge Superstar gave his opinion on Seth Rollins' challenge, and the blue Universal title, among other things.
