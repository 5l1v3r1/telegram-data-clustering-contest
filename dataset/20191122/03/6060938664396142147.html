Amazing at 80: Inside photos of Helen Khan celebrating her birthday with Salman Khan and the family are too cute to miss

22 Nov 2019, 03:12 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Helen Khan is was feeling all things amazing as she rang in her 80th birthday with her family. Everyone from Salman Khan, Sohail Khan, Alvira and Arpita joined in the celebrations as the birthday girl cut into her elegant two-tiered cake with a message that read, ‘Amazing At 80’.

While Helen looked gorgeous in a blue and gold salwar suit, her guests all turned up in shades of black for the wonderful evening together.

Atul Agnihotri shared a cute picture of Salman, Alvira, Arpita and Sohail posing together for a photo with the birthday girl and her cake. Helen also put on her best smiles while posing with her lady friends Asha Parekh and Waheeda Rehman at the do.

The happy bunch later even posed for one happy family picture for the shutterbugs before calling it a night.

Some of the other guests at the bash included Iulia Vantur, Arpita’s hubby Aayush Sharma and son Ahil, Alizeh Agnihotri among other close friends.
