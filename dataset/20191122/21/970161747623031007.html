Justin Bieber and wife Hailey Baldwin want babies 'next season'

22 Nov 2019, 21:10

Justin Bieber is getting broody as he revealed his baby plans with Hailey Baldwin while wishing her a happy birthday.

The 25-year-old took to Instagram on his wife’s special day with a celebratory post – and it’s too cute.

Sharing a picture of them on their wedding, the Sorry hitmaker gushed over his other in the caption.

Writing to the birthday girl, the proud hubby said: ‘Happy birthday babes! You make me want to be better everyday!

‘The way you live you life is so attractive.. ps you turn me on in every way. Next season BABIES.’

Right on cue, the singer’s fans flooded the comments section and completely lost it over the thought of mini Biebers.

‘BABIES WHUT?’ one wrote while another added: ‘B-babies???????????????’

Someone else commented: ‘Happy Birthday!! And just remember when she gets pregnant take good care of her, it’s hard! I love you both.’

This isn’t the first time Biebs has mentioned his wishes to start a family as he recently revealed he wants a daughter.

Under a picture of himself enjoying a day out in Disneyland with Hailey this summer, the star wrote: ‘Love dates with you baby…

‘One day I’ll be doing daddy-daughter dates….. not hinting at anything soon I’m not in a rush. I just wanna enjoy you by yourself for a while!’

And it seems his wifey is on the same page as she previously confessed she can’t wait to have a mini-me.

‘I love kids and I can’t wait to have my own. I would say now that’s a closer reality,’ she told Vogue Arabia.

The 23-year-old also caught baby fever from Kylie Jenner this month as she commented on a picture of the beauty mogul’s daughter, Stormi.

‘Please stop giving me the most baby fever,’ the model said: ‘She’s the sweetest.’
