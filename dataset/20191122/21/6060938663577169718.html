Photos: Kriti Sanon, Rajkummar Rao, Vaani Kapoor, Diana Penty and more attend 'Bala' success bash along with the lead pair of the film

22 Nov 2019, 21:13 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushmann Khurrana, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar's 'Bala' had been the talk of the town since the announcement. The film was released on November 07 and it has managed to score huge at the box office.

And on Friday, the makers of the film hosted a success bash for their friends of the fraternity. It was attended by the lead pair of the film along with celebs like Kriti Sanon, Rajkummar Rao with girlfriend Patralekhaa, Aparshakti Khurana, Amar Kaushik, Sriram Raghavan, Vaani Kapoor, Milap Zaveri, Luv Ranjan, Amyra Dastur, Diana Penty, Boney Kapoor, Dinesh Vijan and more.

Meanwhile, 'Bala' revolves around a man living in Kanpur who suffers from premature baldness. The film's story is about his lack of confidence and the societal pressure that comes with balding. Directed by Amar Kaushik, who earlier made 'Stree', the film also stars Jaaved Jaaferi and Saurabh Shukla in key roles.
