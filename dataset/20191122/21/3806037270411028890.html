NEIGHBORHOOD Puts a Stark Spin On Two adidas UltraBOOSTs

The UltraBOOST ATR and UltraBOOST 19 get re-worked.

22 Nov 2019, 21:08 by Ross Dwyer

NEIGHBORHOOD and adidas Consortium have joined forces for collaborative iterations of the UltraBOOST ATR and the UltraBOOST 19. Dressed in NEIGHBORHOOD’s signature black-and-white color scheme, each pair presents a sleek and striking look — adding a little high-fashion flair to its performance-geared construction.

Crafted to combat adverse conditions, the UltraBOOST ATR features a thickly woven black Primeknit upper. This is accented with sharp white triangles that creep up from the midsole and run from the toebox to the midfoot, with the two closest to the collar offering a digital fade detail as well. Further accents arrive via a grey tongue tag and debossed UltraBOOST/NEIGHBORHOOD branding on the black leather heel counter. A full-foot black BOOST midsole and hardy Continental rubber outsole with an enhanced traction pattern provide comfort and grip, completing a surprisingly sleek weatherized design.

Geared towards speed, the UltraBOOST 19 offers a slim and low-slung black Primeknit upper. Its signature detail is provided by the jet black plastic midfoot cage, which is coated in an all-over NEIGHBORHOOD text logo print. Touches of grey appear on the heel clip and tongue badge, with a black BOOST midsole and black Continental rubber outsole finalizing the look.

This marks the third co-branded effort for the collaborative partners this year: after teaming up with BAPE for a three-way collaboration that included the NMD STLT and POD S 3.1, they also dropped a striking UltraBOOST OG with bold lightning accents. Both styles will release at retailers like Sneakersnstuff on November 29 — Black Friday. The retail price for each is set at $200 USD.

Elsewhere in the world of adidas, we were recently given another look at Kanye West‘s highly anticipated YEEZY BOOST 700 v3 “Azazel.”
