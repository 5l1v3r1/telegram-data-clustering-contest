Former church volunteer sentenced to 12 years in prison for sexual abuse of young boy

YORK COUNTY, S.C. (WBTV) - A Rock Hill man and former church volunteer was sentenced to 12 years in prison Friday for the sexual abuse of a young boy more than a decade ago.

According to court officials, Julio “Andy” Castillo was found guilty on all counts, which include two counts of criminal sexual conduct with a minor second-degree and four counts of lewd act.
