Tour the best in Kelowna

22 Nov 2019, 21:29 by Miriam Halpenny

The second annual Christmas Lights Tour for Charity is returning to Kelowna in December to provide support to Mamas for Mamas.

Cheers Okanagan Tours will be hosting the event on Dec. 5th, 11th, 12th, 18th and 19th, where attendees can tour some of the best Christmas light displays in the city.

The coaches will be travelling through areas including Kettle Valley, Candy Cane Lane and the Tree of Hope.

Admittance to the tour is through donation and all proceeds will go towards Mamas for Mamas, which is a non-profit organization that aims to bring poverty relief to all.

"Mamas for Mamas covers all things needed for both moms, dads, children or single parent families in Kelowna," said Lou Burke with Cheers Okanagan Tours.

"We just like to make sure that they have enough resources to be able to serve their clients for Christmas."

A list of preferred donations from the Mamas wish-list include spring clothing, rubber boots, infant cutlery, sippy cups and underwear - the full list is available here and cash donations in a sealed envelope are also accepted.

Pick-up for the tour is at Prospera Place parking lot at 6 p.m. and will conclude at 8 p.m., pre-registration is required by calling Cheers Okanagan Tours at 250-717-8452.
