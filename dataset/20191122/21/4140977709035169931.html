HB-W vs AS-W Dream11 Match Prediction : Hobart Hurricanes vs Adelaide Strikers Best Dream 11 Team for Women’s Big Bash League 2019 Match 45

22 Nov 2019, 21:55 by Arya Sekhar Chakraborty

HB-W vs AS-W Dream11 : It will be Adelaide Strikers who will lock horns against the Hobart Hurricanes in the 45th match of Women’s Big Bash League.

While Adelaide Strikers have played some exceptional cricket in the tournament which has helped them surge to the 2nd position in the points table, Hobart Hurricanes had an indifferent tournament so far. They managed to win their previous clash against the Sydney Sixers but are still languishing at the bottom of the points table with 7 points from 11 games.

Hobart Hurricanes need to win majority of their remaining match to harbour hopes of going through to the last 4. On the flip side, Adelaide Strikers have an opportunity to surge to the top position if they manage to beat Hobart. They will start as favourites but in T20 cricket nothing can be predicted as Hobart will look to replicate their performance against Sydney.

Match Conditions and Toss

This will be only the 2nd match of the tournament to be played at the Bellerive Oval in Hobart. In the earlier match of the tournament between Hurricane and Brisbane Heat, it was the latter who managed to defend a competitive score of 157 set up by Hobart. It is expected to be much of the same. The team winning the toss might look to defend given the anonymity of this surface.

HB-W vs AS-W Dream11 Wicket-keeper

The first pick in this segment will be Tegan McPharlin from the Adelaide Strikers. She hasn’t been required much for the majority of the tournament due to her franchise’s consistent run in the competition. She will be a sensible pick in the Dream XI team.

HB-W vs AS-W Dream11 Batswoman

Suzie Bates will be our first pick in this segment. Over the years she has been a consistent performer New Zealand and even in this tournament, she has done a good job with both bat and the ball. She has looked in good touch all through the tournament and has also chipped in with some important wickets.

Chloe Tryon will be our next pick in this segment give her power-hitting abilities and her string of good scores in the tournament. She scored a quickfire 29 of just 12 deliveries in the previous clash against Hobart Hurricanes which included four and 3 sixes.

Corinne Hall, the stand-in skipper for the Hobart Hurricanes will be the next pick in this segment. She has le well in the previous clash. She scored a brilliant 50 against Melbourne Renegades and has also got off to good starts in the previous clashes.

Bridget Patterson will be the next pick in this segment. She has been pretty consistent in the tournament and scored a brisk 41 of just 29 deliveries against Melbourne Stars.

HB-W vs AS-W Dream11 All-rounders

Sophie Devine will be our first pick from the all-rounder’s segment. She has lent the ideal balance to the Adelaide Strikers. She scored a brilliant 62 in the previous clash against the Melbourne Stars. She has also been among wickets and has been a consistent performer for them.

Nicola Carey will be our next pick in this segment. She has been in good touch with the bat and scored a fluent 55 of just 46 deliveries against the Sydney Sixers. She has also been among wickets in the tournament.

HB-W vs AS-W Dream11 Bowlers

Megan Schutt will be the first pick in this segment. She has been an impressive performer for Australia over the years. She hasn’t been in the best of form but given her class, a big performance will be just around the corner.

Tayla Vlaeminck will be the next pick given her good performance in the tournament. She bagged a couple of wickets in the previous clash against Brisbane Heat. She has also scored some useful runs coming late in the batting order.

Amanda Wellington will be the next pick in this segment given her good form in the competition. She bagged a wicket each in the last two clashes against Sydney Thunder and Melbourne Stars. She also bagged 3 wickets in the earlier clash against the Stars.

Belinda Vakarewa will be the last pick in this segment. She has been in sensational form bagging 7 wickets in the last two matches. Vakarewa bagged 3 wickets against the Brisbane Heat and a 4 wicket haul against Sydney Sixers.
