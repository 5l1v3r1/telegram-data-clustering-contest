Jon Jones vs. Dominick Reyes headlines Feb. 8 UFC PPV in Houston

22 Nov 2019, 21:40 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Sorry Corey Anderson, but Jon Jones vs. Dominick Reyes is officially happening.

Jones and Reyes are set to fight for the UFC light-heavyweight title in the Feb. 8 pay-per-view main event. The news was first reported by BJPenn.com’s Chris Taylor and later confirmed to ESPN’s Brett Okamoto by UFC President Dana White. Jones had said earlier today that the contract was sitting in his email inbox and simply needed a signature.
