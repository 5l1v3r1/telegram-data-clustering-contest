Daily Paper Delivers Range of Matching Sets for Holiday 2019

To keep you warm during the coldest season.

22 Nov 2019, 21:15 by Gabrielle Leung

After celebrating its heritage in its Fall/Winter 2019 collection and campaign, Amsterdam-based label Daily Paper returns with a selection of styles for Holiday 2019. The latest collection includes layering pieces, cozy knits and warm accessories for those ready to brave the cold in the coming months.

Available in navy, black and grey colorways, unisex pullovers and hoodies are paired with matching sweatpants for a sleek and cohesive look. Another seasonal set features corduroy jackets and pants adorned with multiple pockets and bold branding. Other highlights of the collection include a vintage-inspired sweater, wide-legged pants and a modern black dress and shirt with eye-catching red “Daily Paper” branding adorning the necklines. To complete the looks, ribbed beanies and soft, Merino wool scarves round out the collection.

Take a look at Daily Paper’s Holiday 2019 lookbook in the gallery above. Starting November 23, you can shop the looks on Daily Paper’s website, at the Netherlands store location and at select retailers across Europe.

For more seasonal collections, KITH Treats will drop a collegiate-inspired selection of heavyweight crewneck sweatshirts for the holidays this weekend.
