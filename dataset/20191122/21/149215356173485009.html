Barbara Eden is down for an I Dream of Jeannie reboot

22 Nov 2019, 21:53 by Scott Meslow

In Hollywood's reboot-happy climate, one classic sitcom has somehow been overlooked: I Dream of Jeannie, which ran for five seasons on NBC back in the 1960s.

But for the record, Barbara Eden — who played Jeannie in the original series — thinks a reboot is long overdue. "It's a good idea. They should do it," said Eden.
