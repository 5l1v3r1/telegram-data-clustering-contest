'Loan watch: Paderborn' - Chelsea fans send Jadon Sancho transfer message after Dortmund heroics

Chelsea supporters have been talking about Jadon Sancho after the winger scored for Dortmund

22 Nov 2019, 21:19 by Paul Clarke

Chelsea fans are back on Twitter declaring their love for Jadon Sancho once again.

The former Manchester City young gun has continued his strong start to the season by bagging another goal for Borussia Dortmund in their match at home to Paderborn, which at one point saw the basement dwellers lead 3-0.

The England international broke onto the scene last term for the German outfit, scoring 12 goals and assisting 14 in the Bundesliga.

He has kept up the good form this term and despite signing a new Dortmund contract, rumours continue to persist about a potential return to the Premier League.

Manchester United, Arsenal and Chelsea are just some of the clubs who have fans hoping that the 19-year-old becomes their next big signing.

And it's Chelsea supporters who were the latest to lose themselves in the moment after Sancho's goal for Dortmund.
