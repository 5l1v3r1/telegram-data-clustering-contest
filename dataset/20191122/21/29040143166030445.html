Best Galaxy Note 10 Cases: Black Friday 2019 - Deals & Buyer's Guide

Best Galaxy Note 10 Cases Android Central 2019

The Galaxy Note 10 is here, and it's a gorgeous sight to behold. Samsung's latest entry in the Note series is a lot more manageable than past Notes, offering a large, bezel-less display in a form factor that's easy to hold. As striking as it is it may be, however, the large screen, glass back, and thin metal frame mean it likely won't hold up that well in the event of a fall. Want to ensure the Note 10 stays looking good for years to come? Here are a few cases we recommend checking out.

There's not a right or wrong time to pick up a case for your Galaxy Note 10, but with Black Friday right around the corner, we're about to enter a season of discounts that are bound to slash prices on some of those cases you've been eyeing for your phone. The only case you need: Spigen Neo Hybrid

Staff pick

No matter what phone we're talking about, one of our all-time favorite cases is the Spigen Neo Hybrid. The Neo Hybrid shines on the Note 10, keeping the phone safe and secure while also looking quite nice (especially in the Burgundy colorway shown here). It's also thin, lightweight, and is easy on the wallet.

From $13 at Amazon

Can't go wrong: Caseology Vault

In the market for a Note 10 case that does the basics of keeping your phone safe, look great, and come at a low price? Caseology nails all of those points on the head. The Vault case features a fantastic textured design, shock absorption, and precise cutouts for all of the Note 10's buttons and ports.

$10 at Amazon

Lovely hybrid: Ringke Fusion X

Ringke consistently makes great cases for just about every phone that comes out, and for the Note 10, its Fusion X stands out as something special. The clear case + rugged bumper hybrid is as great as ever, allowing the Note 10's design to shine through while simultaneously getting great protection.

$13 at Amazon

Colorful silicone: Samsung Silicone Cover

The most affordable first-party case that Samsung sells, the Silicone Cover gives your Galaxy Note 10 all-around coverage in a slim form factor that's easy to hold thanks to the grippy silicone material. There are a lot of available colors, with red and blue being among our favorites.

$30 at Samsung

Leather wallet: Snakehive Leather Wallet Case

Should you be after a leather wallet case for the Note 10, you can't do better than the Snakehive Leather Wallet Case. Made out of genuine European full grain cowhide nubuck, this premium leather case is bound to get the attention of anyone that sees it. It can hold up to three credit cards and comes in six colors.

$37 at Snakehive

Clearly great: Anccer Clear Case

If you know you need a case but aren't ready to hide the Note 10's incredible design, another great option is the Anccer Clear Case. Measuring in at just 0.33mm thick, this super-thin case fits the Note 10 like a glove, has a completely clear design, and comes with a money-back guarantee.

$11 at Amazon

Two cases in one: AMOVO Galaxy Note 10 Wallet Case

One of the most unique cases you can get for the Galaxy S10 is the AMOVO Galaxy Note 10 Wallet Case. Why? It's effectively two cases in one sleek package. You can use this as a regular leather case, offering good daily protection for the Note 10. However, you can also attach the optional wallet portion to store your cards and cash!

$26 at Amazon

360-degree protection: SUPCASE Unicorn Beetle Pro

The Unicorn Beetle Pro from SUPCASE is a little expensive, but you're getting a lot for your money. Perhaps one of the most rugged Note 10 cases out there, the Beetle Pro has a trusted dual-layer impact-resistant backside, front cover with a screen protector, and a kickstand that can prop the phone up vertically and horizontally.

From $16 at Amazon

Protective pattern: Caseology Parallax

Caseology's Parallax case is as rugged as it is stunning. The case first pulls you in with its geometric design and subtle accent colors, and from there, you end up staying thanks to the non-slip material, shock-absorbent frame, and relatively slim design.

From $12 at Amazon

Information at a glance: Samsung S-View Flip Cover

Without a doubt, the S-View Flip Cover is one of the most unique cases on this list. The folio style isn't anything new, but the S-View stands out thanks to its interactive display on the front. You can use this to accept phone calls, silence alarms, and more.

$50 at Samsung

Glitter glitter everywhere: Spigen Liquid Crystal Glitter

There's no denying that the Note 10 is a good-looking phone, but there's also room for a little extra pizzaz. That's where the Spigen Liquid Crystal Glitter comes into play. This slim and lightweight clear case shows off the Note 10 while also adding a healthy dose of sparkles.

$12 at Amazon

Clear and durable: SUPCASE Unicorn Beetle Style

If you want to retain the Note 10's excellent design while still getting the enhanced protection of a rugged case, look no further than the SUPCASE Unicorn Beetle Style. The clear back allows the Note 10's stunning aesthetic to shine through, while a TPU bumper enables drop protection. You also get scratch resistance and a raised bezel over the display.

From $13 at Amazon

Durability beast: ArmadilloTek Vanguard Series

The ArmadilloTek Vanguard Series is one of the most durable Note 10 cases you'll find. Available in a few different colors, this case offers 360-degree full body protection, exceeds military-grade drop tests, and there's even a kickstand on the back that works great in vertical or landscape modes!

From $15 at Amazon

Handy dandy kickstand: Spigen Tough Armor

The Tough Armor is one of Spigen's most popular cases, and for good reason. The Tough Armor gives the Note 10 serious protection in a seriously lightweight and slim profile. It comes in two colors (Gunmetal and Black) and features a kickstand on the back that's perfect for impromptu movie sessions.

From $16 at Amazon

Lucious leather: Samsung Leather Back Cover

Samsung's Leather Back Cover is a premium case designed for a premium phone. Made out of genuine leather, this is one of the best-looking Note 10 cases you'll find. It's slim, looks and feels tremendously good, and is also offered in a wide array of colors.

$55 at Samsung

These are the best Note 10 cases.

Among all of the cases here, we have to give our top recommendation to the Spigen Neo Hybrid. The Neo Hybrid wins in just about every category, featuring a great design, copious durability, and coming in at a price that's tough to argue with. If you're feeling a little overwhelmed with all of the choices that are available, you can just get this one and rest assured it'll serve you well.

Another great case is the Anccer Clear Case. It sacrifices some durability in place of an ultra-thin design, and thanks to the clear nature of the case, you can keep the Note 10 safe from daily wear and tear while showing off its sleek design to the whole world.

Finally, if you want something a touch classier, the official Samsung Leather Back Cover is a phenomenal choice. While it is one of the most expensive cases on this list, the genuine leather looks and feels outstanding, it isn't too bulky, and you can trust it'll fit the Note 10 perfectly since it's made by Samsung itself.

How to find the best Black Friday deals on Galaxy Note 10 cases

Alright, so you're ready to buy a case for your Galaxy Note 10. Before diving right in and picking up the first case that catches your fancy, there are a couple pointers to keep in mind that'll ensure you get a case that fits your lifestyle as best as can be. First, ask yourself what you're looking to get out of your case. Do you want something that's super rugged, or would you rather have a case that's slim and lightweight? Perhaps you'd like your case to double as a wallet, or maybe you want it to be clear. Every case for the Galaxy Note 10 is unique, with each one bringing its own set of pros and cons. Of course, there's also the matter of price. You can get a lot of excellent third-party cases for the Note 10 without spending a heap of money, but if you want to go for ones made by Samsung itself, expect to dig deep in your wallet.

Looking ahead at Black Friday, we're expecting to see solid savings on Note 10 cases. Samsung will definitely have a Black Friday sale to promote, and we'd be surprised if some of those discounts didn't include its own Note 10 cases. If you'd prefer to get something from a company like Spigen, SUPCASE, Caseology, or other third-party case makers, there's a good chance they'll get a Black Friday discount, too.

More details on Galaxy Note 10 case deals will roll in as we get closer to actual Black Friday, so keep checking back for all of the latest deals.
