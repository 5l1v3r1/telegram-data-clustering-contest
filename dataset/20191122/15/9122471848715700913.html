4-Star DL Announces Decommitment From LSU

22 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Andrew McCarty

One of the top defensive lineman recruits in the country announced his decommitment on Thursday night.

Four-star defensive tackle Jalen Lee revealed he’s re-opening his recruitment. Lee committed to LSU earlier this summer, but decided to open his recruitment back up heading into the final few months of recruitment.

Lee issued a simple statement on Twitter.

“I will be decommitting from LSU and re-opening my recruitment,” the top recruit said on his social media account.
