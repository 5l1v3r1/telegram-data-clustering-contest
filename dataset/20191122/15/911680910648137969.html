St. Luke's trying to find jobs for displaced workers at other facilities

22 Nov 2019, 15:36 by Angela Gonzales, Phoenix Business Journal

As Steward Health Care winds down operations of St. Luke's Medical Center in Phoenix, the Dallas-based health system is trying to find jobs for the hundreds of employees who will be out of work when the hospital closes Nov. 24.

The hospital will stop taking ambulances and admissions in the emergency department on Nov. 23 at 1:01 am.

Any patients remaining in the hospital at that time will be transferred to Tempe St. Luke's or to Mountain Vista Medical Center.
