X-Force Is About to Make A Major Change to a Character's Mutant Power

22 Nov 2019, 15:11 by L.D. Nolan

The luck of X-Force's most fortunate mutant, Domino, is about to change.

Created by Fabian Nicieza and Rob Liefeld, Domino's abilities allow her to swing the odds in her favor. However, the solicitation text for the issue indicates that her luck will change for issues #7 and #8 of Benjamin Percy and Joshua Cassara's X-Force, and that she'll become unlucky. The summary also teases Domino looking for a solution to her predicament with help from Colossus, but the text does not reveal why or when this change might have come about in the first place.

You can see the covers for the issues below as well as the solicitation.

X-FORCE #7 & #8

  • BENJAMIN PERCY (W) • JOSHUA CASSARA (A)
  • Covers by DUSTIN WEAVER
  • MISS FORTUNE STRIKES!
  • Domino’s luck seems to be changing… Can she find the source of her misfortune before it costs lives all over the world? And has Colossus healed enough to help her… or even himself?
  • 32 PGS. (EACH)/Parental Advisory …$3.99 (EACH)

Dominio, also known as Neena Thurman, has served on a variety of different mutant-oriented teams and played a key role in a number of different storylines over the years. The popular character made her debut on the big-screen in 2018's Deadpool 2, in which she was played by Zazie Beetz.

X-Force #7 and #8 release February of 2020.
