'You can do' - John McGinn's brilliant 10-word vow to Aston Villa boss Dean Smith

Aston Villa v Newcastle United | The Scottish midfielder has now scored 10 goals from his last 20 games for club and country, meaning he's in the goal-scoring form of his career

22 Nov 2019, 15:53 by Ashley Preece, https://www.facebook.com/PreeceBirminghamLive/

Aston Villa head coach Dean Smith could have his goal-scoring prayers answered - by super John McGinn.

The Scot notched three in games against Cyprus and Kazakhstan during the international week, taking his goals tally to 10 from 20 matches for club and country.

The all-action midfielder is in a red-hot streak in front of goal and "bounced into Bodymoor Heath" after being in fine form away with Scotland.

McGinn, 25, has three Premier League strikers from Villa's 12 games and pulled Smith aside during training this week.

"All the internationals have come back in a buoyant mood," the Villa boss explained. "We've got John McGinn who's been to Scotland and scored, what is it? Six goals his last three games for Scotland. He's bounced into the camp!

"We're hoping (he continues scoring), yeah. Hopefully he'll get a few now, he's hit the goal trail. He said to me if you need to throw me upfront, you can do!"
