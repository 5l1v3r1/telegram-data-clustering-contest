Man Utd squad vs Sheff Utd as Ole Gunnar Solskjaer faces central midfield crisis

Manchester United are short of senior options in central midfield for the trip to Bramall Lane where Chris Wilder's Blades lie in wait

22 Nov 2019, 15:33 by Alex Richards, https://www.facebook.com/AARichards/

Manchester United boss Ole Gunnar Solskjaer is lacking options in the centre of midfield for Sunday's meeting with Sheffield United.

Solskjaer confirmed on Friday that the Red Devils are keeping tabs on a number of midfield targets ahead of the January transfer window, amid continued injury issues ahead of the trip to Bramall Lane.

Paul Pogba remains absent due to injury and is currently undergoing rehab in Miami.

The World Cup winner could be missing until the new year, and has been joined on the sidelines by Scott McTominay, who has his own ankle issue.
