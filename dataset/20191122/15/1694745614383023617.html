Marsai Martin Spiced Up Her French Manicure in the Most Luxurious Way

22 Nov 2019, 15:10 by Danielle Jackson

Image Source: Getty / Raymond Hall

We can always count on Marsai Martin to provide us with some high-quality beauty inspiration, and now she's taking on one of our favorite new nail trends of the season. The 15-year-old Black-ish star took to her Instagram Story on Wednesday to give fans a look at her new nail art, which appears to be a pointy french manicure created with orange paint and gold foil pieces at the tips. It's basically a fun, slightly luxurious twist on the popular manicure, and the look was created by Michelle Won, her go-to nail artist who's also worked with beauty gurus Jaclyn Hill and Nikita Dragun.
