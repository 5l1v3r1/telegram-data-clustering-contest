Benny The Butcher, A$AP Twelvyy, Smoke DZA, others playing NYC holiday show

22 Nov 2019, 15:03 by Jeremy Nifras

The Smoker’s Club is throwing a big hip hop holiday show the Saturday before Christmas at Queen’s Knockdown Center (12/21) with Benny The Butcher, A$AP Twelvyy, Smoke DZA, Flipp Dinero, Xanman, Slayter, and more unannounced guests. Tickets are on sale now.

Meanwhile, Benny the Butcher is on the new Paul Wall & Statik Selektah single “Overcame” off their upcoming album Give Thanks, which comes out on Thanksgiving (when else?). Listen below.

And speaking of Thanksgiving and A$AP Mob members and hip hop holiday shows in NYC, A$AP Rocky is playing Thanksgiving Eve at Avant Gardner.

Benny and Smoke DZA recently released a collab EP, and DZA has a collab EP with Curren$y called Prestige Worldwide due the day after Thanksgiving (11/29).
