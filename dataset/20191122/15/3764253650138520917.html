Henry Onyekuru, Super Eagles striker, emerges as transfer target for Celta Vigo

22 Nov 2019, 15:22

- Henry Onyekuru who plays for Monaco has been linked with a move to Celta Vigo

- Celta Vigo are currently not doing well this season in the Spanish La Liga as they languish in 18th position on the table

- They, however, want Onyekuru to help their quest of escaping relegation this season

Spanish La Liga side Celta Vigo are reportedly interested in signing Super Eagles striker Henry Onyekuru who currently plays for French club Monaco.

Celta Vigo have not been impressive this season in all competitions and they are currently occupying 18th position on the standings with 9 points after 13 games.

According to the report on Hurriyet, Celta Vigo chiefs are ready to go for the signing of the Nigerian forward when the January transfer window opens.

They see Henry Onyekuru as a player who can help them get goals to avoid relegation this season.
