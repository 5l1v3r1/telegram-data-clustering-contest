Watch Rick Dalton Dance, Sing and Smoke in Extended Once Upon a Time in Hollywood Scene

22 Nov 2019, 15:14 by Scott Russell  

Quentin Tarantino’s acclaimed latest Once Upon a Time in Hollywood has hit digital platforms a few days early, and to celebrate, Sony Pictures has put out a new extended scene from the film’s bonus features.

The scene gives us some more of Leonardo DiCaprio’s washed-up actor character, Rick (fuckin’) Dalton, as he cuts a rug on the 1960s musical variety show Hullabaloo. DiCaprio nails Dalton’s smug sense of superiority, exaggerating his expressions and movements, because surely singing Jim Lowe’s “Green Door” (in a hilariously joyless monotone) on television is beneath the beloved star of Bounty Law ... right? Right?!

Assuming Sony doesn’t have any more surprises up their sleeves, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood hits Blu-ray and DVD on Dec. 10. Watch Dalton dance below and revisit a recently released deleted scene from the film here.
