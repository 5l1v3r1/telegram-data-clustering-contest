Rangers trade Rupoli to Windsor

Kitchener gets draft picks in return

22 Nov 2019, 15:39 by KitchenerToday Staff

The Kitchener Rangers have swung a trade with Windsor.

Heading to the Spitfires is defenceman Joseph Rupoli.

In exchange, the Rangers will receive Guelph's 12th round pick in next year's OHL draft.

The native of Oakville played in 13 games for Kitchener this season.

"We would like to thank Joseph for his contributions to our team," general manager Mike McKenzie said in a release. "His personality and work ethic will be missed but we wish him the best of luck in Windsor and life beyond the OHL."
