The best PlayStation 4 Black Friday bundles of 2019

Follow Polygon online:

22 Nov 2019, 15:53 by Julia Lee

If you’re in the market for a PlayStation 4, there are some pretty good deals this Black Friday, including a bundle that comes with three PlayStation exclusive games.

The “Only on PlayStation” bundle feature features a 1TB PS4, as well as three games: The Last of Us: Remastered, God of War, and Horizon Zero Dawn: Complete Edition. It’s $199.99 and available at Target, Best Buy, Walmart, Amazon, and GameStop. That’s a savings of around $160.

If you’re looking for a PlayStation VR kit, there’s two bundles going around for $100 off. One of them is $199.99 (usually $299.99), and includes a PS VR Headset, PS Camera, and vouchers to download Astro Bot Rescue Mission, Skyrim VR, Resident Evil 7: Biohazard, Everybody’s Golf VR, and PlayStation VR Worlds. The other bundle is $249.99 (usually $349.99) and it comes with a PS VR Headset, PS Camera, two PS Move controllers, and Blood & Truth and Everybody’s Golf VR.

Though not a bundle, PlayStation 4 Pro consoles will also be marked down $100 to $299.99.

Related

According to Sony, all of these deals will start running on Sunday, Nov. 24 and keep going until Dec. 2 — or until they sell out — from most major retailers. Last year’s Spider-Man bundle sold out within a few days, and we expect the “Only on PlayStation” bundle to be equally popular.
