'Mark my words' - Newcastle United fans make Aston Villa claim

Aston Villa v Newcastle United | Magpies fans have been discussing the Premier League meeting between the two sides on Monday night as they prepare to do battle in B6

22 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Richard Cusack

Newcastle United fans have revealed who they want to see in defence for Monday night's Premier League clash with Aston Villa.

Fabian Schar and Florian Lejeune are both back available for selection and could start the match, with Jamaal Lascelles and former Villan Ciaran Clark both out injured.

Matt Ritchie will miss the game as he continues his recovery from an injury picked up in the Carabao Cup clash with Leicester City earlier this season.

French defender Lejeune is on the comeback trail from a knee injury suffered in April 2019 and he could make his first appearance of the season at Villa Park while Schar has missed the last three League games.
