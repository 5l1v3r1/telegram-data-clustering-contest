Emmerdale SPOILERS: David Metcalfe throws Jacob Gallagher out?

Jacob's family have had enough

22 Nov 2019, 15:01 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Things get worse for Jacob when his dad David throws him out in tonight's Emmerdale (November 22).

After Jacob's cruel treatment of Leanna, he finds his friends are soon turning their backs on him, including Liv Flaherty.

Jacob disgusted everyone, including viewers when he slept with Leanna and afterwards claimed he didn't want to but did it because she came looking for him.

After a confrontation with the teenager, Leanna's dad Liam ended up punching Jacob in the face for being disrespectful to Leanna.

But Jacob wanted the last word and went to the police station and reported Liam for assault, meaning the doctor could lose his job.

Tonight Jacob arrives at the shop but his dad David and biological mum Leyla are still cold with him over what happened and it doesn't take long till Eric gives him a piece of his mind.

His grandfather tells him he can't keep behaving this way and treating people badly.

Jacob hits back but is shocked by what's coming next - they want him out!

As the consequences of his actions hit, Jacob begins to realise he is being kicked out.

Will this be the wake-up call that he needs?

Viewers know Jacob hasn't been the same since he was groomed by school teacher Maya Stepney.

The paedophile, who was in a relationship with David, began grooming Jacob when he was 15 and began sleeping with him just days after his 16th birthday.
