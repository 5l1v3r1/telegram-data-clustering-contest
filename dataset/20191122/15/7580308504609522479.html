Appeal court upholds Ganduje’s election

22 Nov 2019, 15:40 by Abdulganiyu Alabi, Kaduna

The Court of Appeal sitting in Kaduna on Friday upheld the election of Governor Abdullahi Umar Ganduje of Kano State.

The court ruled that the appeal filed by Abba Kabiru Yusuf and his party, the People’s Democratic Party (PDP) lacks merit.

The five-man panel of Appeal Court Judges led by Justice Tijjani Abubakar unanimously dismissed the appeal in its entirety and affirmed that Governor Ganduje was elected by the majority of Kano voters.

Meanwhile, counsel to the appellants, the PDP and Abba Kabiru Yusuf, Bashir Yusuf Muhammad said, they will appeal the judgement.

He said, the reasons for the decisions arrived in the judgement had not been given, but they will get the full judgement to know the basis of the judgement before proceeding to the Supreme Court.
