Drag Race UK's The Vivienne and Baga Chipz land Trump and Thatcher show

22 Nov 2019, 15:32

Have you ever tuned into Good Morning Britain and thought, ‘this is great, but what it needs is more Margaret Thatcher, Donald Trump and drag’?

Well, it’s your lucky day, as RuPaul’s Drag Race UK’s The Vivienne and Baga Chipz have landed their own show thanks to their Snatch Game characters.

The pair will appear as Thatcher and Trump on Morning T&T, new web series for World of Wonder, and we’re already cackling at the thought.

Speaking to Metro.co.uk after winning RuPaul’s Drag Race UK, The Vivienne said: ‘It’s called Morning T&T, and basically it’s a feature length Snatch Game. It’s like a spoof of a morning

TV show like Good Morning Britain or Good Morning America, Donald Trump and Margaret Thatcher are the hosts, so as you can imagine, it doesn’t go well.’
