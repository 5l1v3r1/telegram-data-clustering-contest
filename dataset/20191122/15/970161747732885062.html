Towie’s Lewis Bloor snorts suspicious white powder and urinates on street

22 Nov 2019, 15:00

Towie’s Lewis Bloor has been pictured snorting a suspicious white powder during a night out in London.

The Celebrity Big Brother star was enjoying an evening out with pals in Soho when he was spotted joining two men in a car.

The 29-year-old reportedly spent one minute in the back seat, before collecting something from the men in question and returning to his friends.

He was then seen holding a bag containing suspicious white powder and snorting something on the street while a pal looked on.

The Sun states the reality star turned DJ reportedly coated his finger in the substance before rubbing it into his gums.

Other pictures appear to show his friends dividing the substance up, with one later holding his palm to his nose.

Things all got too much for Bloor at one point, as he was photographed urinating in the street outside a homeless charity.

Lewis has yet to address the pictures publicly.

The ITVBe star came to fame back in 2013, after joining Towie as an ex of Sam Faiers – before going on to date Chloe Lewis and Lauren Pope.

He turned his back on the show in 2016, before appearing in Celebrity Big Brother in the same year.

During his time on the series – where he came 10th – he sparked a romance with Marnie Simpson, but it wasn’t to last and they split soon after, when he admitted to cheating on her.

Lewis recently made headlines after appearing in court on charges of conspiracy to defraud in an alleged £3 million diamond scam.

He has been accused of duping 50 victims with coloured diamonds along with five other men, something he has fiercely denied.

Lewis Bloor’s reps declined to comment.
