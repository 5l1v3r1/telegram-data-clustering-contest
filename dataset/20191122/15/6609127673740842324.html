Alloa man seriously hurt after three yobs stormed his house during brutal attack

22 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Paul Smith

COPS in Alloa are on the hunt for three men after a man was left with significant facial injuries on Tuesday night.

It's believed they entered the 47-year-old man's house on Argyll Street at around 8:25pm and carried out the attack.

He was rushed to Glasgow Royal Infirmary to be treated for his injuries.

Medics describe his condition as stable.

Officers say the first suspect is of slim build and between 5ft 6 and 5ft 9 inches in height.

He was wearing a dark coloured long sleeved hooded top with the hood up, trousers and dark trainers.

The second is of slim build and between 5ft 6 - 5ft 9 inches in height and thought to have been wearing a two toned long sleeved top with the hood up.

The top is a darker colour on the arms, shoulders and hood and lighter on the chest and torso.

He had dark-coloured trousers and trainers, and walks with a distinguished gait.

The third suspect is of medium build and between 5ft 6 - 5ft 9 inches in height.

He was wearing a dark coloured hooded top with a lighter stripe down the arms, jogging bottoms and trainers.

Detectives are looking at CCTV footage to find all three men.
