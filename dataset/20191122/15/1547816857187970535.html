Bhumi Pednekar Vs Taapsee Pannu: Who Wore The Sequin Top Better?

22 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Prajakta Ajgaonkar

The Saand Ki Aankh actresses Bhumi Pednekar and Taapsee Pannu were papped in sequin tops and black pants at different events. Let’s see which diva scored more points on the style meter

Some days back, Taapsee Pannu was papped at the success bash of Saand Ki Aankh wearing a glitter top. Taapsee wore a one-shouldered rainbow sequin top with high-waist black pants. The actress let her curls loose and rounded off the look with a choker neckpiece and a watch.

Now talking about Bhumi Pednekar’s look, the actress was spotted during promotions of her film Pati Patni Aur Woh. The actress was dressed in a shimmery multi-coloured crop-top that she paired with high-waist black pants.

Well, both the actresses chose to add glitter to their outfits and you got to tell us who pulled off the sequin top look better?
