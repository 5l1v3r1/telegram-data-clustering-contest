Fake News Buster: Photo of CPI leader shared as image from JNU protest

22 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
A photo of police personnel taking a woman into a van is doing the rounds on social media. The image is being circulated with a claim that it is a photo captured during the protest by Jawaharlal Nehru University students against the varsity’s decision to increase the fees.

SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
However, the photograph being circulated had been published by several news publications, which identified the woman as Communist Party of India leader Annie Raja, during a protest against the clean chit given to former Chief Justice of India Ranjan Gogoi over allegations of sexual harassment.
