"If I see Pique focused and in good condition, he'll play"

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Ernesto Valverde spoke in a press conference, this time to analyse this weekend's game between Leganes and Barcelona, a game in which Pique and Rakitic are protagonists, by what the coach says: "I have to play the best".

The Barcelona coach made it clear with Pique, whom the Davis Cup organisation may be throwing away: "If I see him focused and in good condition, he will play against Leganes".

"I have spoken with him and I see that he is focused. He has things to do outside of football. When all the players are accused of not knowing how to do anything other than play football, the one who does it is criticised," he added.

About Rakitic, Valverde recognised that "the team is changing a few things". "There are a lot of players in some positions. I don't plan anything as we will see what the moment brings. I have to play the best," he explained.

He was positive about the return of the internationals, as he sees those as "the main ones". "There have been long trips, but not as long as on other occasions," Valverde said. 

A little fed up with being questioned on some subjects, such as Griezmann. "He is in November, like everyone else. Sometimes I don't know what to say. Everyone else is valued on the basis of their success. I see him well and we hope he is better," he said.
