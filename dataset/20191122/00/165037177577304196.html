Arsenal and Liverpool also want Barca's desired star

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Red Bull Salzburg have a very talented youngster in their team. Karim Adeyemi is a player who Barcelona have been following for several months and now Arsenal and Liverpool have also shown their interest.

17-years-old is enough for Barcelona to follow you for the past few months. As if this is not enough, Karim Adeyemi is also receiving interest from Arsenal and Liverpool for his signing.

But what does this young, promising footballer have? The young, German striker of the moment has scored a whopping 11 goals in just 31 games with Liefering, as well as getting six assists.

In Red Bull Salzburg's youth team, he has already scored two goals and got an assist in only 3 games played. Figures that have led Barca to offer 15 million euros for him.

Adeyemi is Barcelona's most desired young star. Patrick Kluivert, the director of Barcelona's youth squad, has been following him for months, but his possible incorporation into the squad could become difficult due to the interest from two English clubs.
