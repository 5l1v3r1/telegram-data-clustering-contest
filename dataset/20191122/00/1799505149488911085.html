Campus Sexual Assault

22 Nov 2019, 00:57 by BRIEF

BOSTON – The First Circuit ruled that the lower court improperly granted a Boston College student’s motion for a preliminary injunction against his one-year suspension following sexual assault allegations, finding that state law does not control the actions that a private college takes in addressing assault claims.
