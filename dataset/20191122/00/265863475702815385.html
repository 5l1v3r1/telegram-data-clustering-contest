Lucky Daye Responds To His Four Grammy Award Nominations For Debut Album Painted

22 Nov 2019, 00:31 by Charisse Van Horn

R & B singer Lucky Daye is speaking out about his four Grammy award nominations for his debut album Painted. Lucky Daye has scored nominations for Best R&B Performance for the song “Roll Some Mo,” Best Traditional R&B Performance for the song “Real Games,” Best R&B Song for “Roll Some Mo,” and Best R&B Album for Painted. Born David Debrandon Brown, the 34-year-old New Orleans native shared his excitement on his social media platforms such as Instagram and Twitter.

Lucky Daye shared the following message on Twitter where he thanked the Recording Academy for recognizing his work with his debut album.

I’m on the other side of the world and I find out I’ve been GRAMMY nominated 4x’s for Roll Some Mo, and Real Games, and my debut album PAINTED !! THANK YOU
@RecordingAcad #DAYEONES You love me and I love you billion back🍀 🤯🤯🤯🤯
