Rajamouli promotes a comedy entertainer - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

22 Nov 2019

SS Rajamouli has plugged a trailer after a long gap.  This time, he has done so to promote a comedy entertainer.  It's 'Bhagyanagara Veedullo Gammathu', which will release on December 6.

"Srinivas Reddy is one of the finest comedians I know since the beginning of my career... Wishing him the best on his debut as a director and producer," the 'RRR' director said.  

The parody has Vennela Kishore playing a cop, who is after the idiosyncratic characters played by Reddy, Shakalaka Shankar and Satya.  With music by Saketh Komanduri, this one has Dollysha as a female lead.  Raghu Babu, 'Hyper' Aadhi, Suman Shetty, Praveen, Satyam Rajesh, Viva Harsha are part of the cast.
