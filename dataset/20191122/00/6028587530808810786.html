Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Diffusers For Plant-Based Essential Oils

22 Nov 2019 by Tiffany Leigh

Diffusers are becoming a regular household - and even office space necessity. This isn’t surprising considering the wealth of healthy benefits they offer. According to natural wellness company Saje and its founders Kate and Jean-Pierre LeBlanc, diffusers aid in the release of negative ions to elevate your wellbeing, revitalizes the air in your space using 100% plant-based essential oils, and provides an atmosphere similar to that of standing near a calming waterfall.With many of us heading into the hectic holiday season, keep your loved one’s health in mind and give them the gift of these natural diffusers:

Electrical Diffusion

Saje x Poosh - Positively Poosh Ultrasonic Diffuser Kit

With an aim to welcoming more positivity into your space, this diffuser kit features a limited-edition Poosh Diffuser Blend and Aroma Lune Diffuser. Appearing as a sculptural stone sphere, this item was designed in collaboration with Poosh by Kourtney Kardashian and with the Saje x Poosh experiences in mind. Benefits of use include the ability to humidify while infusing the air with the healing power of plant extracts/natural oils. The unit itself diffuses the oil via a heat-free system, thereby preserving the integrity of ingredients; it offers the ability to mist for 7 continuous hours or 14 hours intermittently (30 sec on/30 sec off); and lastly, a whisper-quiet operation. The diffuser also comes with a Euphoric Oil blend of rose, lavender, grapefruit - which is exclusive to this Positively Poosh Kit.

Puressentiel Ultrasonic Diffuser Humidifier

Puressentiel is a family and independently-run laboratory based in France which was started by Isabelle and Marco Pacchioni. In 2005, they founded their company with a passion for aromatherapy and asserting “its effectiveness in treating and improving their everyday health.” Their diffuser and respective products utilize 100% organic and pure essential oils. The Ultrasonic Diffuser features an ultrasound system that disperses a light mist of water along with your chosen essential oil, whose integrity and formulation is preserved because of their cold ionisation system. Diffusion lasts up to 12 hours and promotes wellbeing which includes neutralizing indoor air, taking care of joints and muscles, managing respiratory system, and sound sleep. Puressentiel’s research is supported by botanists, engineers, aromatherapy and toxicology specialists, doctors and pharmacists. Essential oils are sold separately, with the most popular within their range being Positive Energy, Meditation, Relax, Air Purifying, and Respiratory.

Vitruvi Terracotta Stone Diffuser

Siblings Sean and Sara Panton founded Vitruvi whose brand not is not only crafted for beauty, body and home rituals, but that these wellness-based creations are also works of art and enhance the ambiance of a space. Described as sophisticated natural beauty, their Stone Diffuser comes in 5 shades (white, black, charcoal, blush, terracotta). Units can run for 3 continuous hours or at interval settings of 7 hours; they feature a porcelain lid; and a BPA-free plastic water reservoir. Essential oils are sold separately; and similar to how each personality is wholly unique, the brand offers a Diffuser Cocktails section to blend to your mood - such as their calming and grounding Goddess Love Recipe. Otherwise, you can opt for your usual single origin essential oil, or try their bespoke line of pre-made blends, with the most popular being their Retreat (tropical and restorative; uses eucalyptus, grapefruit, palma rosa) and Ceremony Blend (earthy and smoky; uses clary sage, cedarwood, palo santo).

Asakuki 200ml Essential Oil Diffuser

The wellness company Asakuki - roughly translated from Japanese to English as - “air in the morning”, originated in Japan. Their core mission is to offer people a better life through its designs and creations that encompass innovation, comfort and technology. For their diffuser line, of their many popular styles, this feathered-out aesthetic is particularly striking. The stylish orb, which cycles through 7 LED Mood lights (a feature that can be switched on and off), is portable for home, yoga studio, and the office. Natural/Essential oils (sold separately) are emitted through their humidifier - which helps to prevent dry air, lift one’s mood, alleviate stress and fatigue.

Reed Diffusion

LAFCO’s Reed Diffusers 

In 1992, John Bresler founded LAFCO in New York, and to this day - his company’s philosophy is about honoring the traditional craftsmanship of luxury home fragrances and personal care products. Their reed diffusers provide sensory and emotional wellbeing, due to their use and sourcing of 100% natural essential oils. Moreover, their diffusers can last an impressive one year, versus the usual 3-4 months. The stylish hand-blown art glass diffuser vessels add beauty and richness to home or office décor; and best of all, they are designed to be reusable. The most popular scents include Rosemary Eucalyptus (a clean, fresh aroma to clear the mind), Duchess Peony (provides a calm sanctuary), and Amber Black Vanilla (sensual and serene).

Dr. Vranjes Firenze Diffuser Decanter and 750mL Bordeaux Bottle

Dr.Vranjes is a luxury Florence-based brand that recently celebrated its 36th anniversary and prides itself upon “olfactory recollections that are linked to the scent of an environment, a place, a moment.” For this diffuser, it’s foodie meets fragrance as Dr.Vranjes crafts an artistically-endowed piece for wine enthusiasts. Made of crystal and shaped like a decanter, this exclusive fragrance playfully diffuses its Rosso Nobile scent via natural reed sticks. Natural oils such as orange, strawberry, ripe berries and earthy birch are utilized to disperse the aromatic nuances of what would be perceived as a Tuscan red wine into a room’s atmosphere.

The Merchant of Venice Vase Snake Yellow and Orange

The Vidal family founded The Merchant of Venice, whose brand mandate is honoring the ancient artistry of Perfumery and its influences through their line of Eau de Parfum, body products, Eau de Toilette, and home fragrances. For their Murano Art Collection, these home diffuser vessels are handmade by NasonMoretti master glass blowers. There are 5 vibrant forms available and all are ideal for reed and stick diffusers (sold separately). Their home fragrances utilize exotic ingredients and natural oils; for instance, Cachemire Wood uses an oily resin sourced from Boswellia plants - lending woody and earthy tones to an environment. Other popular choices are their Egyptian Linen and Amber Intense scents.

Oojra Thai Jasmine Bamboo Essential Oil Reed Diffuser

Founder and CEO CJ Lemsky explains that her company’s name Oojra, is a creative combination of the Hindi word for energy “urja” and the feel-good sound people make when in tasting/touching/smelling something delightful...”oooou”. Much of her “scentspiration” stems from her love for travel, design and commitment to natural health. In fact, one of her earliest diffuser creations stemmed from a trip to Thailand - the Thai Jasmine Bamboo Essential Oil Reed Diffuser. The aroma transports you to Thai spa in Hua Hin and you’re engulfed in uplifting jasmine flowers and the verdant retreat of a rainforest. As one of their most popular sellers, this aromatherapy of jasmine and bamboo creates an atmosphere of happy relaxation. The company prides itself upon use of 100% natural fragrance oils that are derived from extracts and botanicals. There are no synthetics, no parabens and no phthalates in their oil blends; and as a result, scents offer greater fragrance depth compared with the artificial variety. Diffusers also use natural rattan reeds and the glass vessels and gift boxes can be reused and/or recycled.
