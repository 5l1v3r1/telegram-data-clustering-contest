Amazon Black Friday deals live as Echo, Kindle and Alexa gadgets get discounts

22 Nov 2019, 00:02

Amazon has officially launched its Black Friday sale on the Friday before Black Friday.

The gigantic online retailer is putting out eight days of sales to cash in on the shopping frenzy, leading with its own gadgetry.

The likes of the Kindle e-reader and the Echo smart speakers have been given hefty discounts to get the ball rolling.

The Amazon Black Friday sale began on Friday 22 November at 00.01 and runs until 23:59 on Friday 29 November.

As well as discounting thousands of products, the website will also feature a Deals of the Day section that will round up the best bargains customers can score every day.

There are also thousands of ‘Lightning Deals’ – products available at a discount, in limited quantities, for a short period of time.

Doug Gurr, VP, UK Country Manager at Amazon.co.uk said: ‘We know how important it is to help our customers save money where they can at this time of year, so we’re excited to be launching our tenth Black Friday Sale to UK customers in the lead-up to the festive season.’
