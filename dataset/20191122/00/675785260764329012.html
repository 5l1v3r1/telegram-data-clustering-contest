Argos Black Friday 2019 delivery options

Argos’s same-day delivery service, Fast Track, will still be available throughout the Black Friday sale period.

For speedy delivery customers pay one flat fee of £3.95 regardless of address, and shoppers have the added bonus of knowing the service covers more than 90 per cent of UK postcodes and is available seven days a week.

Black Friday is set to be a hectic day for retailers, if you are planning to hit the high street and pay Argos a visit you can opt to use the click and collect services to beat the queues.

Shopper using the option just need to look out for the Fast Track logo on the page of their chosen items, and then pre-pay on Argos.co.uk or via the app, using the click and collect option at checkout.

Once a notification is sent your shopping will be ready to collect at your chosen store. All you have to do is go to the dedicated Fast Track counter in store and quick as a flash you’ll have them in your hands.

The best bit is there’s NO extra charge for the service.
