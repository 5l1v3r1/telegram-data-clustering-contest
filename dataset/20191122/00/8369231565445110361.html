Video Captures Dramatic Explosion of SpaceX's Starship Prototype During a Test

22 Nov 2019 by BRYAN LOGAN, BUSINESS INSIDER

SpaceX's Starship Mark 1 prototype rocket partially exploded during a pressure test in Boca Chica, Texas.

The incident happened Wednesday afternoon and was captured on a livestream. During the test, the upper portion of the rocket blew apart and heavy plumes of smoke flowed from the vehicle.

"The purpose of today's test was to pressurize systems to the max, so the outcome was not completely unexpected," a SpaceX representative told The Verge, adding: "There were no injuries, nor is this a serious setback."
