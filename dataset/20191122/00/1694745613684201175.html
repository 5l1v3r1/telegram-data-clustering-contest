The Best Bob Haircuts of the 2010s, From Taylor Swift to Dua Lipa

22 Nov 2019, 00:55 by Sarah Siegel

Over the last decade, hair trends have come and gone, but one has remained popular throughout the 2010s: the bob. While the styles have varied from sleek to curly, blunt to layered, and with or without bangs, this is the one look that is always in fashion.

Some of the most iconic bobs are still as memorable as the day we first saw them. From Taylor Swift's edgy textured bob at the iHeartRadio Music Awards in 2016 to Nicki Minaj's sleek black bob in 2011, many of these daring haircuts still inspire us today.

Ahead, check out the best bob haircuts of the last decade. But be warned, these photos might tempt you to get a haircut.

Hayley Williams's Orange Hair and Baby Bangs in 2010

We're totally obsessed with Hayley Williams's orange hair, baby bangs, and bob haircut from the Grammys in 2010.

Carrie Underwood's Layered Bob in 2010

We're tempted to use Carrie Underwood's layered bob haircut at the Grammys as inspiration for our next cut.

Nicki Minaj's 1920s-Style Bob in 2011

At the MTV Movie Awards in 2011, Nicki Minaj stunned with a chic black bob and thick bangs in a 1920s style.

Rihanna's Bob Haircut in 2011

It's hard to forget the vivid red hue of Rihanna's bob in 2011.

Katy Perry's Inverted Bob in 2012

Katy Perry's blue inverted bob from 2012 might inspire your next haircut.

Rose Byrne's Sleek Bob in 2012

Rose Byrne's sleek and shiny bob with bangs at the Oscars has been on our Pinterest inspiration board since 2012.

January Jones's Edgy Bob in 2013

January Jones's textured bob with a deep side part was an edgy change from her Mad Men character, Betty Draper.

Beyoncé's Bob Haircut in 2014
