Zidane about Mourinho's return: "I don't mind"

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Real Madrid coach, Zinedine Zidane, spoke in a press conference before the game against Real Sociedad, in which he assured that the signing of Portuguese José Mourinho by Tottenhm, who has been related on several occasions with a possible return to the white team, "does not matter" and does not make things "calmer".

"Quieter no, I don't mind. i think the relationship that he has with our President is good, if that's the case. He (Florentino) has brought me here, as a player I've lived thanks to him in this great club and I've already completed almost 18 years in this great club and I will always be grateful to him," said the Frenchman.

In addition, Zidane responded categorically when asked if he would only coach Real Madrid in the League: "Yes. I'm white (he laughs), you either have this or you don't":

Jose Mourinho was presented as the new Tottenham manager, a club in which he has taken the baton of a Mauricio Pochettino who took the team to the 2019 Champions League final.

In the Premier League, Spurs are 14th and are eleven points from the fourth place occupied by Manchester City. The Portuguese will get a good plus if he manages to qualify for the Champions League again.
