Student murdered over cricket fight

22 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

A 20-year-old degree student was stabbed to death over a fight that happened while playing cricket. The victim had slapped a player of the rival team. This player teamed up with three others and stabbed the student to death and are absconding. The Nandini Layout police has registered a case.

The victim has been identified as Uma Maheshwar, a resident of Ganesha Block in Nandini Layout. He was pursuing a BBA degree from at a private college in Rajajinagar. Two days back, he is alleged to have slapped a person while playing cricket. The other players intervened and separated both of them and sent them away.

This person, who is said to be the friend of the victim, got frustrated after being humiliated in public. He then ganged up with three others and approached the victim on Wednesday around 7 pm and picked a fight. “In the heated argument, the accused stabbed Maheshwar and fled the scene. Locals shifted the victim to a nearby hospital, where he succumbed to injuries on Thursday morning at around 7 am,” said an officer on part of investigations.

The Nandini Layout police have registered a case.
