Bale ready to face the Bernabeu music

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Real Madrid complete their final training session this Friday prior to hosting Real Sociedad at the Santiago Bernabeu on Saturday (21:00). Gareth Bale was in the training session once again for the second day running and he could play for Real Madrid for the first time in 49 days.

The winger got injured with his national team during the October international break and, after not participating in the following six games for his club, he reappeared for Wales against Azerbaijan and Hungary. In those two games, he helped his country qualify for the Euros. He celebrated it together with the rest of his teammates, with his country's flag and the phrase "Wales, Golf, Madrid, in that order," words which have generated quite a lot of controversy. 

So, the Frenchman Zinedine Zidane had the same footballers on Thursday. Only Lucas Vazquez, James Rodriguez, Nacho Fernandez and Marco Asensio were missing. They are carrying out specific work to recover from their respective injuries.

Real Madrid, level on 25 points with Barcelona at the top of the league, will return in La Liga from the international break against Real Sociedad in matchday 14. 

Zizou's men thrashed Eibar 0-4 in their last league outing on 9th November just a few days after thumping Galatasaray 6-0 in the Champions League. 'Los Blancos' next CL outing is on 26th November against Paris Saint-Germain at the Santiago Bernabeu, their penultimate one of the group stage.
