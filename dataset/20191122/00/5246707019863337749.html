Reminder: A Hat In Time Nyakuza Metro DLC Is Out Now On Nintendo Switch

The adventures of Hat Kid continue

22 Nov 2019, 00:50 by Liam Doolan

Update - Fri 22nd Nov, 2019 00:50 GMT: Humble Bundle and Gears for Breakfast have now officially released the Nyakuza Metro DLC for A Hat In Time on the Nintendo Switch. This $6.99 / £3.99 DLC includes a new chapter, 10 new Time Pieces, a new sticker system, new flairs, new dyes, a new weapon, and a new purple Time Piece.

Original Story - Thu 17th Oct, 2019 03:01 BST: A Hat In Time is out this week on the Nintendo Switch and to celebrate, Gears for Breakfast has revealed the base game and Seal the Deal will eventually be joined by the Nyakuza Metro downloadable content.
