How driver ‘raped’ his boss’ daughter - The Nation Newspaper

22 Nov 2019, 00:42

Adebisi Onanuga

 

AN Ikeja Domestic Violence and Sexual Offences Court heard on Thursday how a driver, Francis Apai, allegedly raped an 18- year-old daughter of his boss.

A witness, Dr.  Ogunsegun Bankole, in the Department of Family Medicine, Alimosho General Hospital, Igando, Lagos gave the narration while giving evidence before Justice Serifat Solebo.

Led in evidence by State  prosecutor, M.I Oshodi, the witness informed the court that some of the emergency cases he attends to  included traffic accident, assault, domestic and rape cases.

In his evidence,  the witness revealed that he was on call duty in the Emergency Department in the evening on October 19, 2015, when a young  girl came in.

He said the victim who was accompanied by other persons was 18 years old at the time of the incident and claimed to have been forcefully raped.

He said he identified her violator as her parents’ driver.

Bankole, narrating the victim’s story, alleged that Francis Apai dragged her  into a room, held her down by the neck, pulled up her dress and while she was struggling,  forcefully raped her.

The witness said  after taking the statement of the victim, they conducted a  physical examination on her.

He said they discovered that the young girl had multiple bruises around her neck and two hands.
