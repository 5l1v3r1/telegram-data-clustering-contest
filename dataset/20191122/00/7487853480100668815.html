José Mourinho Offers to Be Spurs Players' Girlfriends in Bizarre Opening Speech to New Squad

22 Nov 2019 by Tom Gott

​José Mourinho attempted to win over his new Tottenham Hotspur squad this week by giving them a passionate speech, in which he offered to be each player's father and girlfriend, in an attempt to turn around their poor form.

Former boss Mauricio Pochettino was sacked after seeing Spurs fall to 14th in the Premier League, but the club are hoping for an immediate recovery when they travel to face bitter rivals West Ham on Saturday.
