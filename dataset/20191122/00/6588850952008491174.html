Chris Brown And Ex-Girlfriend Welcome Their First Child, A Baby Boy

22 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

It’s a boy!

Chris Brown and his ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris welcomed a son on Wednesday (Nov. 20), according to reports.

The former couple did not publicly confirm the baby’s arrival, but they have been dropping cryptic teasers on social media. Chris posted a black-and-white photo of himself smiling and looking down off camera. He captioned it with the baby’s presumed birth date: 11-20-2019.
