The world's biggest Toblerone is the sweetest deal in Amazon's Black Friday sale

The 4.5kg chocolate bar has been reduced on the first day of the Amazon Black Friday sale

22 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Stephen Hurrell

Amazon has announced the full details of its Black Friday sale and one discount it sure to be a hit with people with a sweet tooth.

The online retail giant has announced it is cutting the price of the huge 4.5kg bar of Toblerone - the biggest Toblerone money can buy.

The giant treat was previously listed at £75 bit Amazon has cut the price of the chunky chocolate to just £45, which represents a saving of £29.

The bar is approximately 80cm long and was previously spotted on shelves of budget store Home Bargains. However, Amazon's deal undercuts the retailer's original price of £50.

It still may be a little steep even for the biggest chocolate fans. After the bars first appeared on shelves one customer said: ""Who in their right mind would pay that amount for a chocolate bar, especially a cheap one like that."

 

Another added: "You want your head looking at if you're gonna spend £50 on chocolate".

It seems Amazon's cost-cutting chocolate deal may not be too popular with bargain hunters. However, the retailer has also announced a host of other deals for the first day of the Amazon Black Friday sale on 22 November.

Amazon has cut the price of its Echo Dot , Firestick and Kindle devices to new lower prices on the first of the eight days of deals.

Other discounts include a 55-inch Phillips Smart TV for just £509, a saving of £240 on the original price of £749.

The sale is live now and will take place for the next eight days before Black Friday itself on 29 November. There will be further discounts on Cyber Monday on 2 December.
