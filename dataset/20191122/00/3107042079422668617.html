Bushfires rage as political heat intensifies

22 Nov 2019

Firefighters continue to battle hundreds of bushfires across Australia as blazes spring up in new locations, stepping up pressure on Prime Minister Scott Morrison to tackle climate change.

In Victoria, dozens of fires burned across the southeastern on Thursday and temperatures of 40.9 degrees Celsius in the capital Melbourne matched the hottest day on record in 1894, Australia's weather bureau said.
