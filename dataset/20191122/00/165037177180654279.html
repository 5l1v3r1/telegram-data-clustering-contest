Robert Moreno confirms he has offers!

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The former Spain coach, Robert Moreno could shortly go back to coaching. The coach confirmed in an interview with 'Teledeporte' that there is already another team interested in his services.

"I have already had some calls from teams. It's incredible how people value your work," Robert Moreno said.

However, it is so that, at least for the moment, the former Spain coach needs a rest. "Now I want to rest for a while with my family," he said.

Robert Moreno is grateful for all the proposals he has received in the last few days. "The amount of projects that have been offered to me in just 48 hours is incredible. I am glad because it means that we are doing our job well," he said.

When time passes, Robert Moreno will decide what to do. At the moment, he stated that he is "full of energy" and that he will do "whatever it takes", including coaching abroad.

Luis Enrique became the new Spain coach, A lot of people have spoken about their relationship and, according to recent information, the problems between Robert and Luis were only professional.
