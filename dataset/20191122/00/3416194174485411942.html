Little Mix Drop Their First Original Christmas Song 'One I've Been Missing' - Listen Here!

22 Nov 2019, 00:51

Little Mix just made our holiday season a little bit better with their first original Christmas song, “One I’ve Been Missing”.

The new track was written by Leigh-Anne Pinnock, with Tre Jean-Marie, Jez Ashurst, Sinead Harnett and Rachel Furner.

“We absolutely love Christmas, it’s our favourite time of the year. We’ve always wanted to write and release a Christmas song, we were just waiting for the right moment!” Leigh-Anne shared about the track. “The minute I finished writing ‘One I’ve Been Missing’ I couldn’t wait to play it to the other three girls.”

You can pick up Little Mix‘s “One I’ve Been Missing” on Apple Music and these digital retailers.
