Young Mother Narrates How Her 10-Year-Old Nephew Poisoned Her Baby

22 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

A Nigerian woman has taken to social media to rant about her little nephew who she believes may be mentally sick as he has been displaying behaviours that suggest he is psychologically not okay.

The woman who narrated that the most recent behaviour her 10-year-old nephew demonstrated was intentionally hurting her baby with a poisonous insect.
