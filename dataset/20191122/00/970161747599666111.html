I'm A Celebrity: Richard Madeley threatens to boycott show by shouting

22 Nov 2019, 00:03

Richard Madeley threatened to boycott I’m A Celebrity Extra Camp by shouting into the jungle – and Joel Dommett looked genuinely worried about it.

The ITV host appeared on Extra Camp with Fleur East to discuss the antics on the show so far as his son-in-law James Haskell is part of this year’s line-up.

As he sat on the sofa he asked Joel and Emily Atack: ‘Are they in ear shot?

‘Because my daughter Chloe asked me to do something.’

Richard then put his hands to his face motioning as if he was going to shout something – and it did not go down well as the hosts’ panicked a bit as they responded ‘no no no no’.

Laughing Richard continued: ‘My daughter wanted me to say “James I love you”.

Joel added: ‘You not allowed it’s against the rules.’

What happened on Thursday night’s show?

When we thought it couldn’t get any better – TV bosses have thrown another two celebs in – and they have won the nation’s hearts already (we love you Cliff and Andy).

Thursday night’s episode was probably one of the best ever and it’s safe to say Caitlyn Jenner was on fire.

Not only did she learn the dance to Girls Aloud’s The Promise (which was pure comedy gold) but she accidentally insulted newcomers Andy Whyment and Cliff Parisi for not knowing who they were as she questioned ‘Who are these ‘f***ing soap people?’

And just in case you were wondering, Ian Wright and Andrew Maxwell have made up after they had a clash over crocodile feet (phew).

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! airs weekdays at 9pm on ITV.
