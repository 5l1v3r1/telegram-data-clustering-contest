vivo S5 is now available for purchase

22 Nov 2019 by Sagar

The vivo S5 unveiled last week is now available for purchase in China through the company's official site.

It comes in two memory versions - 8GB/128GB priced at CNY2,698 ($385/€345) and 8GB/256GB that costs CNY2,998 ($425/€385). However, only the 128GB version is currently available for purchase.

The vivo S5 is powered by the Snapdragon 712 SoC and runs Android Pie with Funtouch OS 9.2 layered on top. It sports a 6.44" FHD+ Super AMOLED display with a fingerprint underneath and a punch hole in the top-right corner for the 32MP selfie camera.

The back of the phone is home to a 48MP main camera joined by an 8MP ultrawide, 5MP depth, and 2MP macro units arranged in a diamond formation.

The S5 packs a 4,100 mAh battery with 22.5W fast charging support and comes in three colors - Icelandic Blue, Phantom Blue, and Star Black. But the Phantom Blue version is currently unavailable.
