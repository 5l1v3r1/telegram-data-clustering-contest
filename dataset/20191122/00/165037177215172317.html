Getting the team to the Champions League is Mourinho's aim

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Aside from the great salary he will receive, Jose Mourinho can also get a succulent prize if he manages to get Tottenham to finish in the top 4.

Once the initial shock has passed, little by little, more details about Jose Mourinho's contract with Tottenham are emerging.

The Portuguese will receive a big salary to manage the London club, but it does not stop there for Jose.

Despite his salary, the former Manchester United coach's contract also contains numerous clauses (aims) for him.
