Injury Update For Cowboys LB Leighton Vander Esch

22 Nov 2019 by Matt Hladik

The Dallas Cowboys may head into this weekend’s showdown with the New England Patriots without the services of key linebacker Leighton Vander Esch.

Vander Esch missed the Week 9 game against the New York Giants with a neck injury but returned to play in Dallas’ next two games. However, it looks like the issue has reared its ugly head again.

According to the Dallas Morning News’ Michael Gehlken, Vander Esch had an MRI today on his neck. Ian Rapoport of NFL Network said Vander Esch would miss tomorrow’s practice because of a back problem, but we’re assuming both are talking about the same thing.

Both reporters said Vander Esch is not expected to play this weekend.
