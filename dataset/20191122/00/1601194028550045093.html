ROH Fires Women's Champion Kelly Klein

22 Nov 2019, 00:19 by Rick Ucchino

According to her husband, BJ Whitmer, three-time and current Women of Honor Champion Kelly Klein has been fired by the company. The 33-year-old, who is currently out of action after suffering a concussion, was apparently informed of the news via email. Whitmer took to Twitter on Thursday to confirm the news.
