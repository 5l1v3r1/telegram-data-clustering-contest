Amazon drop prices across Kindles in the Black Friday sales

Deals are available now on the Kindle, Kindle Paperwhite and Kindle Oasis

22 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Neil Bennett, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/neil-bennett/

Amazon have kicked off their Black Friday sale a week before the day itself.

The Amazon Kindle is among dozens of digital products that have been reduced in price, with offers on the Kindle, the Kindle Paperwhite and the Kindle Oasis.

There are a total of three Kindles available on the market: the Kindle, the Kindle Paperwhite and the Kindle Oasis.

The cheapest of the three options is the Kindle; a device that is 'purpose-built' for reading. It's front light has 4 LEDs, which is 21 LEDs less than the Oasis, but still enough to read indoors and outdoors, day or night.

The 167 ppi glare-free display 'reads like printed paper' and has been designed to withstand even direct sunlight.

As well as digitising your book collection, the Kindle also allows you to interact with your favourite texts by highlighting passages, looking up definitions, translating words and adjusting text size.

  • Kindle (2019) - £54.99 - Saving of £15
  • Kindle Paperwhite (2018) - £84.99 - Saving of £35
  • Kindle Oasis (2019) - £179.99 - Saving of £50

Whereas an 'entry-level' Kindle has an indented screen, the Paperwhite has an all-glass front that digitaltrends.com describes as giving "a slightly more expensive feel and look".

The Paperwhite is also said to have greater durability, an improved frontlight and greater features surrounding Audible, Bluetooth and water-resistance.

The all-new Oasis, meanwhile, has further additional components including adjustable light sensors, an ergonomic design and an auto-rotating page orientation as seen on modern smart phones.

What is 'better' comes down to personal preference. Despite the added features on the Paperwhite and Oasis, many still opt for the original Kindles smaller and lighter design.

Elsewhere in Amazon's Kindle Store, deals have also been placed on a series of refurbished devices and accessories including leather covers have also been discounted by at least 20%
