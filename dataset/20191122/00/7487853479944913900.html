Premier League Fantasy Football: Who's Hot and Who's Not in Gameweek 13

22 Nov 2019 by James Cormack

​We made it.

After three international breaks in three months, English football fans are treated to non-stop domestic action until March.

In the end, the recent break wasn't all so bad. England and Wales booked their places at Euro 2020 while the two Irish teams were forced to settle for the playoffs. Not only that, but we were also blessed with the huge news of Mauricio Pochettino's sacking from Spurs and the hiring of ze 'Special One'. So, the last few days have been fairly interesting.
