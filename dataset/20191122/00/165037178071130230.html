Pochettino could give Barca a hand with Coutinho

22 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Mauricio Pochettino's goodbye from Tottenham has immediately started the rumours about his arrival at Bayern Munich. His possible hiring by the Bavarian team would be good news for Barcelona, as it could be key to the definitive purchase of Coutinho by the German team.

Pochettino has been the talking point (as well as Mourinho) in the last few days. His sacking from Tottenham has caused a big shake up in the football world!

Since he said goodbye to the London club, the rumours have started about his possible spell at Bayern Munich, something that would also affect Barcelona.

If the German team signed the Argentine it would have a direct affect on Barcelona in regards to Philippe Coutinho and a potential definite stay at Bayern.
