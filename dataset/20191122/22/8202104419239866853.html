Police investigating after shots fired through wall into occupied apartment

22 Nov 2019, 22:23 by Saskatoon StarPhoenix

During the investigation, officers executed a search warrant at a home in the 100 block of Avenue N South where they seized several firearms — though no arrests were made.

Saskatoon police are investigating after receiving reports of gunfire in the 100 block of Avenue N South Monday morning.

According to a police news release, when officers arrived at the scene just before 11:30 a.m. they found evidence of gunshots — including one that had torn through a wall into an occupied apartment. Police believe the shots occurred shortly after 9 a.m.

During the investigation, officers executed a search warrant at a home in the 100 block of Avenue N South where they seized several firearms — though no arrests were made.

Police said no one was injured during the incident but they believe the shooting was not random.

The incident remains under investigation with the guns and gangs unit. Anyone with information is asked to contact police at 306-975-8300 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477.
