Are gel nail extensions less damaging than acrylics?

22 Nov 2019, 22:52 by Ally Giannini, Kyle Kerchaert and Taylor Davis
  •  Acrylic extensions can be very damaging to the natural nail due to its stiffness and strength.
  • Celebrities like Ariana Grande have rocked soft gel extensions instead, which are supposedly less damaging.
  • Insider producer Herrine Ro visited Paintbucket in NYC to try soft gel extensions and to see if they caused any harm to her nails after wearing them for a few weeks.
