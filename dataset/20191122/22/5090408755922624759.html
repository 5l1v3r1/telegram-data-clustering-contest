Does Macy’s have free shipping and returns on Black Friday purchases?

Macy’s often has free shipping with low order minimums; right now, you’ll need to hit at least $25 to get free shipping. That’s super easy considering how many great deals you’ll find during Black Friday.

Macy’s has free returns in-store or by mail within 90 days. There are exceptions around certain items purchased around the holiday season. Jewelry, watches, small electronics, occasion dresses, and Backstage items bought between November 1, 2019 and January 31, 2020 can only be returned until January 31, 2020; tech accessories and Apple products purchased between December 1, 2019 and January 8, 2020 can only be returned until January 8, 2020.
