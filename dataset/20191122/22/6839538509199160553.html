Fears for 12-year-old girl missing from Bundaberg

22 Nov 2019, 22:35

Police are appealing for public assistance after a 12-year-old Queensland girl disappeared.

The girl was last seen about 4.30am on Friday and has not been in contact with family or friends since then.

Police hold concerns for her welfare as this is out of character.

She is described as Caucasian, about 150cm tall, with a proportionate build and brown eyes and hair.

She was last seen wearing black clothing and a Chicago Bulls shirt.
