I'm A Celebrity campmates 'disappointed' over Cliff Parisi's arrival into jungle

22 Nov 2019, 22:47

Some of the celebs in the I’m A Celebrity main camp were a tad ‘disappointed’ at Cliff Parisi’s arrival – as they were hoping for a treat instead.

Even Declan Donnelly noticed the ‘lukewarm welcome’.

Cliff entered the jungle as a late arrival to the show with new fan-favourite and fellow soap actor Andrew Whyment.

The actor is best known for appearing as Minty Peterson in EastEnders for eight years from 2002 until 2010 as well as appearing on Casualty, Hollyoaks and Call the Midwife.

It all began when James Haskell and Caitlyn Jenner went to see Kiosk Kev – and as they did they were greeted by a scroll asking them which outlaw they’d like to free for $100 from the Jungle Jailhouse – Andy or Cliff.

James turned to Caitlyn and joked: ‘I tell you what, I’d rather a bag of crisps or chocolate bar if I’m completely honest with you.’

‘I don’t know these Brits, how can I make a decision’ added Caitlyn.

James and Caitlyn told Kiosk Kev they’d like to free Cliff.

Although not everyone was happy with their decision, especially Nadine Coyle as she was hoping for chocolate instead (ouch poor Cliff).

Caitlyn and James returned to the main camp to explain their news – and there were a lot of disappointed faces.

Cliff arrived to the main camp and Nadine exclaimed: ‘I felt sorry for Cliff as there was high hopes for a phone call and come chocolate and then Cliff came which was great but I think there was a “oh, they’re back and they’ve not got any chocolate”.’

Viewers felt sorry for poor Cliff as they took to social media to share their thoughts about it.
