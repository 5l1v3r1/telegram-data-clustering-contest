Oregon at Arizona State: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

22 Nov 2019, 22:05 by Alek Arend

No. 6 Oregon carries the weight of the Pac-12’s College Football Playoff hopes on its shoulders. The Ducks have won nine straight since losing to Auburn in Week 1. Now, they’re in a prime position to win the conference and potentially earn the No. 4 seed in the CFP.

Arizona State is one of two teams currently standing in Oregon’s way. ASU head coach Herm Edwards would love nothing more than to knock off the Ducks on Saturday afternoon.

The Sun Devils had a fantastic start to the season, going 5-1 with a notable win over Michigan State in Week 2. But Arizona State has collapsed, losing its last four games including a loss to Oregon State last weekend. The Sun Devils look to end the four-game losing streak on Saturday against the Ducks.
