Banggood doesn’t disappoint when it comes to offering the best products at unbeatable prices. The Chinese e-tailer continues to live up to its reputation for offering the best bang for your buck by coming up with amazing promotion sales.

With the year’s biggest shopping event, the Black Friday Sale just around the corner, Banggood is making sure it is giving its loyal users a chance to avail of the biggest discounts it has to offer on a wide range of products.

Paving the way for the Black Friday Sale 2019, Banggood has launched a new promotion called the Home Appliances & Outdoor Sale. As the name suggests, the promo features products that come in handy in day to day life, as well as gadgets that are designed for adventure junkies and sports lovers at lowered prices.

The promotion sale is divided into three phases including:

  • 1/2 Price Daily (from November 12th to November 27th)
  • $0.01 Snap Up (from November 20th to November 22nd)
  • 373 Free Gift & Free Order (from November 22nd to November 30th)

The first category on the promotion page is called Free Gifts & Free Orders. Starting November 12th, Banggood users will stand a chance of getting free gifts with their orders.

From November 27th to November 30th, orders over $0.01 can win free orders. Winners will receive their free gifts within seven working days after November 30th. The top 5 spenders will be eligible for free gifts worth $199.99.

To qualify, you must place one of the top 5 single payment orders (which can’t be paid in installments). It is worth mentioning here that once the gift has been issued, you cannot cancel the order.

The 1/2 Price Daily category will feature a product carrying half price during the activity period. The Top Sellers category, on the other hand, comprises products that have received 99% positive feedback. More importantly, you can get these items for considerably lowered prices.

A must check-out for coupon chasers, the Coupon Deals section is teeming with high-end products that have been assigned specific coupon codes for you to use at the time of checking out. These coupons help you save more money before you place your order.

For example, the Xiaomi Mijia LCD Writing Tablet usually retails for $48.84, but thanks to a 35 percent off on Banggood, you can buy it for a lowered price of $31.99. Furthermore, you can get an extra $6.40 off and bring the already lowered retail price further down to $25.59 by applying coupon code BGBMV20V.

The next category called New Products features heavily discounted items. The Home & Garden Must category includes items that have been split into Hot Picks, Kitchen, Dining & Bar, and various other sub-categories to make the search process easier.

Likewise, the products available under the Home Appliances Must, Outdoor Must, as well as the Lights & Lighting Must categories have also been divided into specific categories so that users do not end up spending a lot of time finding the product they want to purchase. Note that each sub-category includes products carrying noteworthy discounts.

Brand conscious buyers can head straight to the Top Brand section, which is graced with products from leading manufacturers. You can avail of up to 63 percent off on products from Digoo, 54 percent off on products from BlitzWolf, use a 20% OFF Brand Coupon for a discount on XMUND branded products and 20% OFF Brand Coupon on products from Xiaomi.

We have reached the concluding section on the activity page called More To Love. To ensure you can check out products that fit inside your budget, this section has been into under $9.99, $9.99-$49.99, $49.99-$99.99 and From $99.99 sub-categories.

You can visit a specific sub-category based on the amount you are willing to shell out on a product and save a lot of time you’d otherwise waste looking for those items that carry steep price tags. To check out more details and to start placing your orders, simply visit the promotion page by following this link.
