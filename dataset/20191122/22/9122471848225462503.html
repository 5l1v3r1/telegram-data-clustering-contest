Miami at FIU: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

22 Nov 2019, 22:25 by Dan Lyons

Miami doesn’t need to go too far for its road game this weekend for the second-to-last game of the season. The Hurricanes are heading across today for a date with the FIU Panthers.

The ‘Canes have been a bit up-and-down this year, but the team has responded very well to a rough home loss to a rebuilding Georgia Tech squad earlier in the year. After that game, Miami has won three straight, including blowout wins over rival Florida State and a surprisingly decent Louisville squad.

At 6-4 (4-3), Miami is one game out of the ACC Coastal, though they are one of four teams that are somewhere between 5-2 and 4-3 in league play. The Coastal, as usual, is a bit of a mess, though Virginia has a half-game on any other team at 5-2 in ACC play.

Butch Davis’ FIU team is 5-5 on the year, but has won four of its last six. When the Panthers lose, they really lose though. FIU’s only single-score loss came to Western Kentucky, and the last two losses to Middle Tennessee and FAU came by 33 and 30 points, respectively.
