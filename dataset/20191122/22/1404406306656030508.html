Playboi Carti and Iggy Azalea Rental Home Burglarized for $366K in Jewelry

22 Nov 2019, 22:00 by VladTV

According to TMZ, Playboi Carti and Iggy Azalea had their Atlanta rental home burglarized and with the burglars making off with $366,000 in jewelry. Among the items stolen include "custom rings, chains, bracelets, pendants, watches, earrings and even a diamond engagement ring. The missing jewelry is a mix of gold, diamonds, silver, platinum, pink sapphire and rose gold." 

According to law enforcement, Iggy said that she was in the home at the time of the burglary and heard footsteps in the home and believed the burglar was armed. Authorities have home surveillance footage showing the thief walking out with a bag in hand.
