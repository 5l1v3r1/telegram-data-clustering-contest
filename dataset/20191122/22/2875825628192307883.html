EastEnders confirms Stacey's link to newcomer Jerome leaving Martin distraught

EastEnders' Stacey Fowler was confirmed to have a secret new fella in a twist, leaving husband Martin devastated

22 Nov 2019, 22:25 by Irishmirror.ie

EastEnders confirmed it was over for good for Martin Fowler and wife Stacey, with a devastating bombshell in the latest episode of the BBC soap.

Stacey was teased to be returning on Friday night to collect her and her kids' things, after her split from Martin.

She'd been on the run with her husband ever since she left Phil Mitchell for dead in September, with his son Ben blackmailing Martin to work for him.

Martin hoped Stacey would finally be able to return but Ben threatened to kill her if she did - leading to Martin pretending to cheat on her with ex Sonia to keep her away.

Stacey was distraught by the split, and this week Martin decided to tell her the truth and bring her home.

But on Friday he still couldn't get through to her on the phone, and was left reeling by a discovery.

As he headed to Stacey's mum Jean's house, he found her packing together her daughter's things.

It's then that mystery newcomer Jerome, played by actor Chris Charles, arrived and made a shocking announcement.

He came to collect Stacey's belongings without her, before saying he was her "friend".

Martin quickly realised that Stacey had moved on though and had a new fella, with him trying to attack him in the Street.

Jerome told him: "Me and Stacey are only early days.

"I met Stacey on the school run. I saw how wrecked she was after your visit. Think what you like. I care about her, the kids are great."
