J.P. Nadda and Nitin Gadkari to campaign for BJP in Jharkhand

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will address a rally on November 25 for the first phase of the Assembly election in which 13 constituencies will go to the polls on November 30

22 Nov 2019, 12:37 by IANS

Bharatiya Janata Party working President J.P. Nadda and Union minister Nitin Gadkari on Friday held rallies in Jharkhand and sought votes for the party.
