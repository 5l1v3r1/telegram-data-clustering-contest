Bella Thorne & Jacob Elordi Support Cara Delevingne at PUMA x Balmain Launch Party

22 Nov 2019, 12:14

Cara Delevingne gets support from Jacob Elordi and Bella Thorne at the PUMA x Balmain created with Cara Delevingne LA Launch Event!

The model, the Euphoria actor, and the Midnight Sun actress joined forces at the event held at Milk Studios on Thuesday (November 21) in Los Angeles.

They were joined by Balmain Creative Director Olivier Rousteing (whom Cara shared a kiss with!), Jacob‘s Euphoria co-star, Angus Cloud, Aquaman‘s Amber Heard, Brooklyn Nine‑Nine‘s Eva Longoria, The Bachelorette‘s Hannah Brown, Villains‘ Maika Monroe, Star‘s Matthew Noszka, rappers G-Eazy and Machine Gun Kelly, and models Alessandra Ambrosio, Sasha Luss, and Slick Woods.

Also in attendance were DJ Chantel Jeffries, media personality Draya Michele, fashion stylist and blogger Tina Leung, and Lionel Richie‘s son Miles Richie.

“Best night with my best friend celebrating the launch of our collaboration #PUMAxBALMAIN. ❤️,” Cara wrote on Instagram. “You can shop the collection in stores & online now!”
