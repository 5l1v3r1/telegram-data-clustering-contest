Bangla Tigers vs Maratha Arabians Live Cricket Score: Ball by ball scorecard and match updates

22 Nov 2019, 12:57 by Vinay Chhabaria

Two in-form sides of the Abu Dhabi T10 League 2019, Bangla Tigers and Maratha Arabians will square off against each other in the second match of the league stage's final day. Both the teams have all but sealed their place in the Qualifier 1, however, the winner of this match will finish at the top of the points table.

This match will start at 7:15 PM IST at the Sheikh Zayed Stadium of Abu Dhabi.

You can follow ball-by-ball scorecard updates of the match right here.

Bangla Tigers have played some quality cricket in this tournament and the three all-rounders David Wiese, Thisara Perera and Robbie Frylinck have been instrumental in the team's success. While the Tigers are reliant on their multi-skilled players, the Maratha Arabians rely a lot on their batsmen.

With the presence of Chris Lynn, Yuvraj Singh, Adam Lyth and Hazratullah Zazai in their batting line-up, the Arabians have trounced almost every opposition in this tournament.

Though the Bangla Tigers have been phenomenal in this tourney, the Arabians will start as the favorites to win this match because of their in-form batting unit which has been backed by the fast bowling duo of Lasith Malinga and Mitchell McClenaghan.
