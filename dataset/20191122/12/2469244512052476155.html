Two arrested after boy, 15, in hospital after taking MDMA

It follows a series of raids in the town

22 Nov 2019, 12:36 by Joel Cooper

Two people have been arrested after a 15-year-old boy was left in hospital after taking MDMA, police have revealed.

Officers in Ilfracombe say they conducted drug raids at a property in the St Brannocks area of the town yesterday evening (Thursday, November 21).

And the local force's superintendent has said the raid was prompted by a teenager being hospitalised after taking ecstasy.

The details of the incident involving the 15-year-old boy are not clear at present, but Devon Live has contacted the police for more information.

However, officers have revealed that two people have been arrested on suspicion of drug dealing.

Superintendent Toby Davies, commander of North and West Devon Police, took to Twitter to say: "Following a 15 yr old from Ilfracombe being hospitalised after taking MDMA, local officers have conducted drug warrants in the area and two persons have been arrested on suspicion of drug supply #DrugsRuinLife "

Ilfracombe Police also shared the news on social media.

On their Facebook page, they said: "On the evening of Thursday 21st November Ilfracombe Police along with colleagues from specialist units executed a drugs warrant in the St Brannocks area of Ilfracombe.

"Two persons have been arrested on drugs offences and enquiries are ongoing."

Earlier this month we reported how class A drugs, thousands of pounds in cash and two stun guns had been seized by police during a series of drug raids in Ilfracombe.

Ilfracombe Police took to Facebook to share the results of the warrants, which were issued under the misuse of drugs act.

In their post on November 8 they said: "Officers from Ilfracombe neighbourhood supported by colleagues from specialist units executed a number of drug search warrants yesterday.

"Bongs & Biscuits, Ilfracombe High Street. Cannabis was found at the property.  These offences will be processed and appropriate referrals made to trading standards.

"It is noted that some sections of the community are already aware of this and are not happy with the positive action taken by the police.

"These people should be assured that we will continue to take a zero tolerance to all forms of drugs.

"Simultaneous warrants were executed at two connected addresses in Ilfracombe during the afternoon leading to the arrest of a male and a female on suspicion of drug supply offences.

"Class A drugs, Several thousand pounds in cash and two stun guns have been recovered from the addresses.

"To those dealing and supplying drugs in Ilfracombe the message is simple, don’t. We will catch up with you eventually."
