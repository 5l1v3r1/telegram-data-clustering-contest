Cheese star biscuits recipe

22 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Xanthe Clay

These classic buttery cheddar biscuits, made with a hint of cayenne and mustard powder, are great with drinks.

Prep time: 25 minutes, plus 1 hour chilling | Cooking time: 18-20 minutes

MAKES

About 30

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g mature cheddar, grated 
  • 100g plain flour
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 100g chilled butter, grated 

METHOD

  1.  In a food processor, pulse together the cheese, flour, cornflour, salt, cayenne, and mustard powder to make a nubbly sand. Add the butter and pulse again until evenly combined.
  2.  Tip the mixture out on to the work surface and bring together with your hands to make a dough, sprinkling over a little water if it is dry.  On a sheet of baking parchment, roll out to ½cm thick. Cover and chill for at least a couple of hours, or up to two days.
  3.  Preheat the oven to 180C/160C fan/Gas 4.
  4.  Cut the dough into star shapes and lay on two baking sheets lined with baking parchment, at least 1cm apart.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes, then turn the baking sheets.
  6.  Bake for another eight to 10 minutes until pale gold.
  7.  Cool on the baking sheets.
