Rocket League drops loot boxes in early December

22 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Steve Wright

Blueprints and Credits will take the place of Loot Boxes and Keys.

Rocket League will remove loot boxes and keys in early December, developer Psyonix has advised.

From 4 December, the items will be removed, replaced with Blueprints and Credits instead. Keys in your inventory on the day of swapover will be converted into 100 Credits. They can be used to purchase in-game items from the Item Shop or can be converted to Rocket Pass Premium. Credits can also be used to build items from Blueprints.
