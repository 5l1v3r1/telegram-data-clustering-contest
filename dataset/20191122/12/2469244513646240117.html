Mince pie and custard pasties top Warrens Bakery's quirky Christmas menu

There's an array of delicious new treats to enjoy on Warren Bakery's Christmas menu

22 Nov 2019, 12:11 by Charlotte Gosling

Following its huge success over the last three years, Warrens Bakery has only gone and done it again and announced the return of the extraordinary mince pie and custard pasty.

Back by popular demand, for a limited time only, the quirky handmade sweet pasty is filled with mincemeat and custard, encased in a cocktail pasty case and sprinkled with sugar.

It always gets tongues wagging as it's perfect for satisfying both your sweet and savoury festive cravings as well as being an innovative twist on a traditional favourite.

Jason Jobling, master baker at Warrens Bakery, comments: “The Cornish twist to the traditional mince pie has proven to be a success with our customers in previous years so it makes sense to bring it back for a limited time this year.

"This pasty is handmade using rough puff pastry and hand filled with specially selected mincemeat and a vanilla custard.

"It’s crimped by hand in the same way we would a savoury pasty then glazed and topped with a cinnamon and sugar blend for extra crunch.”

Alongside this, the bakery is introducing a range of tasty food items, starting from just £1.30.

For Christmas dinner lovers, the Turkey Feast is a must. It's everything you want from a Christmas Pasty - delicious turkey, sage and onion stuffing, potato and swede as well as a dash of cranberry sauce.

There is something for all the family too with an offering of colourful and themed kids Christmas biscuits. They can choose from a Santa Biscuit, Melted Frozen Snowman, Mallow Christmas Pudding, Shortbread Christmas Tree or Gingerbread Snowman.

The bakery has also introduced a twist on a popular sweet customer favourite, the Festive Fruit Danish. This is a combination of sweet glazed bread which is layered with raisins, sultanas, glacé cherries and mixed peel, and topped with nibbed sugar.

The Luxury Star Mince Pie is also tempting - made up of crumbly shortcrust pastry filled with matured mincemeat, topped with an exquisite pastry star and dusted with icing sugar.

Or grab the bakery's three-for-two hot meal deal

Warrens Bakery is also offering the perfect family lunch deal for less than £5.

Shoppers have been flocking to Warrens to make the most of the bakery's three-for-two offer.

This means the whole family can be treated and tuck-in to everything from sausage rolls to chicken and mushroom slices, as well as delicious strawberry sprinkle doughnuts - if you've saved enough room for afters!

With customers spending an average of just £3.70 on three tasty treats, families can enjoy a hot, fresh meal deal each and every day.

Choose from Bacon and Cheese Turnovers to Chicken and Mushroom Slices or even a Meat Pattie as your main, as well as many more.

Warrens boasts an incredible range of colourful doughnuts as part of the deal with tantalising flavours including White Chocolate and Custard, Chocolate and Hazelnut, Creme Brûlée, Coconut and Lovely Lemon to name just a few.

And vegetarians are certainly catered for with options such as delicious Cheese and Onion Slices or Rolls, and Bean and Cheese Savouries - not to mention delicious doughnuts including the Triple Chocolate and the unbeatable Glazed Ring. Vegans can also grab the famous Meat-Free Sausage Roll within the deal.

For more information, visit the Warrens Bakery website at warrensbakery.co.uk or head into store at one of Plymouth's three branches on Old Town Street, Derriford Road or Plumer Road.
