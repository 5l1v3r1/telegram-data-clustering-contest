Dylan Sprouse Gave Barbara Palvin Something Really Special She Carries With Her Whenever She Travels

22 Nov 2019, 12:18

We 100% positive that Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin are actually the best couple in the world.

During a new interview with British Glamour, the 26-year-old model opened up about her relationship with Dylan and how he’s helped her learn to put herself first.

“We brought the best out of each other,” she shared. “As cheesy as that sounds it’s true. I wore a lot of baggy t-shirts before and because I feel more confident, I wear tighter, sexier clothes.”

Barbara adds that “we do respect each other and that’s the key to a healthy relationship. We trust each other and the fact I can be transparent with him and he loves me for who I truly am empowers me to be myself, to go out there and not worry about what anyone else thinks because I am great the way I am!”

“He thinks the same thing, too. I feel like I am learning every day from him.”

Barbara also shared the sweet thing that Dylan gives to her every time she travels: “I do have a huge coin in my bag which is sentimental, as Dylan gives it to me every time I leave him.”

How sweet is that?! Couple goals!
