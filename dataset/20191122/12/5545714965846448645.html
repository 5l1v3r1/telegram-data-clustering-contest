Ace Combat 7 video details upcoming DLC Ten Million Relief Plan

Because I was... inverted

22 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Chris Moyse

Bandai Namco has released a lengthy gameplay video of Ace Combat 7's upcoming DLC Ten Million Relief Plan. The near ten-minute video features a mission briefing and some high-flying, dog-fighting action, set against a golden sunset.

Ten Million Relief Plan will be the final piece of the DLC "trilogy" that began in September with Unexpected Visitor, and was then followed up in October with Anchorhead Raid. Ten Million Relief Plan will bring an end to the arc of The Alicorn, an immensely powerful submarine that is posing a devastating nuclear threat to the world.

It's your outfit's duty to plant sonar receivers to ascertain the location of the Alicorn, before pinpointing its exact coordinates, relay this information to base, and then prepare for a strike against this dangerous vessel. It's all hands on deck for this mission, as the consequences of failure would be absolutely devastating.
