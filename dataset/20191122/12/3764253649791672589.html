Ramon Azeez reveals threats from Barcelona fans in Nigeria after scoring against club

22 Nov 2019, 12:18

Ramon Azeez, Super Eagles and Granada midfielder, has explained how he was threatened by Barcelona fans in Nigeria after scoring against the Spanish champions in September this year.

Barcelona have lost three games this season in the Spanish La Liga, and one of those came from Ramon Azeez's club Granada.

The Super Eagles midfielder scored the first goal for Granada against Barcelona in his side's shocking 2-0 win over Valverde and his wards.

Many Nigerian football lovers were happy and congratulated the former youth international for scoring against Barcelona, but some were livid with him.
