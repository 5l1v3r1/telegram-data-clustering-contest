Raptors unveil all new 2019-20 OVO City Edition jerseys

22 Nov 2019, 12:44 by Sportsnet Staff

The Toronto Raptors officially unveiled their new 2019-20 OVO City Edition jerseys on Friday.

Design-wise, the jersey keeps with past OVO styles, incorporating the black-and-gold that is now readily associated with Drake and his accompanying OVO branding.

This year’s version, however, also incorporates throwback elements from Toronto’s inaugural season.
