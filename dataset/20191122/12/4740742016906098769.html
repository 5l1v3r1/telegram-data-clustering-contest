TSB bank problems: Money Saving Expert Martin Lewis responds to payment delays

"Many customers will be struggling not to think that TSB stands for 'The Screwed Bank"

22 Nov 2019, 12:11 by Jasper King

Payments into TSB accounts up and down the UK have been delayed overnight, leaving some customers without wages.

The issues are currently ongoing and have left some customers overdrawn and struggling to buy everyday essential items.

TSB has apologised to their customers in a tweet, but there is still no sign when customers money will arrive in their bank accounts. They have said however that no customers will be left out of pocket.

But founder of Money Saving Expert.com Martin Lewis has hit back at the bank.

He said: "Many customers will be struggling not to think that TSB stands for 'The Screwed Bank.'
