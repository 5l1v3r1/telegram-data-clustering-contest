Colts vs. Texans final score: Houston improves playoff stock with win over rival

22 Nov 2019, 12:20 by Dan Bernstein

The Texans took an importantstep toward claiming an AFC South crown with a 20-17 win over the Colts on Thursday night.

Their offensive lineprotected quarterback Deshaun Watson well and allowed him to pick apart a shaky Indianapolis secondary. Watson threw for 298 yards, two touchdowns and an interception.

Wideouts Will Fuller (140 receiving yards) and DeAndre Hopkins (94 receiving yards) repeatedly beat single coverage, particularly in the second half when the Colts (6-5) were forced to be aggressive on defense against the Texans (7-4).
