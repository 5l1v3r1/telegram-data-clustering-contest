Report: Resident Evil 3 remake in production

22 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Tuffcub

YouTuber SpawnWave has reported on a rumour the somewhat obvious news that a remake of Resident Evil 3 is currently in development and will be released next year. While we wouldn’t usually report on such things Eurogamer say they have also heard of the project. I too may have heard something as well but couldn’t possible comment.

Capcom have said they are open to the possibility of more remakes following the huge success of Resident Evil 2. They are currently working on the spin off game Project Resistance and recently released a video showing a full match, splitting the screen four ways so you can see the three survivors teaming up while the Mastermind sets traps.We also get to see the Mastermind taking control of some of the zombies and directly attacking the other players.
