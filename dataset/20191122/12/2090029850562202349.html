Lady calls out Babcock University for their slow reaction to her harassment report in school

22 Nov 2019, 12:21 by Damilola Ismail

A lady has taken to her social media page to call out Babcock University for allegedly reacting slowly to her report on being harassed in the school.

According to her, Babcock University management were quick to expel the 300 level accounting student whose video went viral but slow to react to her report of being harassed in school.

The social media user, @the crusader__ on Twitter wrote;

You reacted to this faster than when I reported that I was harassed in school. Aren’t you ashamed of yourselves?

Recall that within 24-hours after the video of the student got leaked and went viral on social media, the University released a statement revealing that although the incident didn’t happen in the school premises, she has been expelled.

The statement released by the school resulted in a public outrage as Nigerians aired their disappointment and disapproval in the decision made by the school authority.
