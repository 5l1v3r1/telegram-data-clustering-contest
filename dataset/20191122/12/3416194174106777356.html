Jason Bell, Olivia Holt's 'Bad Girlfriend' Co-Star, Passes Away In Motorcycle Accident

22 Nov 2019, 12:06

We are so sad to hear that Jason Bell, who starred with Olivia Holt in her “Bad Girlfriend” music video, has died.

Olivia shared the disheartening news on her Facebook, opening up about working with Jason for the video.

“my heart is real heavy this week. my handsome leading lad in my music video for bad girlfriend has sadly passed away,” she wrote. “my heart hurts so much. he was a true professional. one of the kindest humans i’ve ever had the pleasure of working with. if everyone could please pray for healing for his family and friends and send them some love that would be above and beyond x rest in paradise jason.”

Olivia added, “we worked on this shoot from sunrise to sundown on probably the hottest day this year in california. he never complained once. he was grateful to be there and to help bring my vision for this song to life. and i am forever grateful for him.”

Jason sadly passed away after being involved in a motorcycle accident this week. You can donate to funds for his funeral here.
