Punk Rock Bowling 2020 lineup: Madness, Circle Jerks, Cock Sparrer, Lunachicks, more

22 Nov 2019, 12:59 by BrooklynVegan Staff

Las Vegas festival Punk Rock Bowling has announced the lineup for its 2020 edition which happens May 21-25 at Downtown Las Vegas Event Center. Headliners are UK ska icons Madness, a reunited Circle Jerks, and Cock Sparrer, plus Mighty Mighty Bosstones, Streetlight Manifesto, Propagandhi, the reformed Lunachicks, Adicts, AVAIL, Rose Tattoo, Stiff Little Fingers, Ruts DC, Bishops Green, G.B.H., Municipal Waste, Anti-Flag, The Lawrence Arms, The Bronx, Roy Ellis, Good Riddaance, Ho99o9, Plague Vendor, Jaya the Cat, Single Mothers, and more.
