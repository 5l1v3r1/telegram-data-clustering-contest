New York Islanders OT win breaks franchise record, surpassing longest point streak since '82 Stanley Cup year

22 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Paulina Dedaj

The New York Islanders' overtime win against the Pittsburgh Penguins Thursday night earned them their longest point streak in franchise history.

Brock Nelson scored his second goal of the game at the 4:16 mark of the extra period, not only helping the Islanders win their fifth consecutive game but also breaking the franchise record of the longest point streak with a 15-0-1 record in their last 16 games.
