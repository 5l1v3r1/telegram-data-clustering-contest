Kartik Aaryan Rings In His Birthday With His Family

22 Nov 2019, 12:02 by Filmfare

In the past year, Kartik Aaryan has seen a steep rise in his popularity and his success at the box-office with films like Sonu Ki Titu Ki Sweety and Luka Chuppi being the biggest factors behind it. Today as the young actor Kartik Aaryan celebrates his 29th birthday, fans have taken to social media to share their wishes and shower some love. However, the best surprise Kartik could have got on his birthday was at the stroke of midnight, given by his parents.

The actor shared some pictures on Instagram today morning where he’s seen cutting a birthday cake with his parents at home. The caption of these series of adorable pictures reads, ‘Jab Mummy Papa ne bday pe surprise Kiya…’ followed by a heart and an emotional-teary-eyed emoji. It was adorable seeing Kartik Aaryan celebrate his special day with his special people.  Check out the pictures below.

On the work front, Kartik Aaryan has just finished the first schedule of Dostana 2 in Patiala and has returned back to the bay. Now the actor will be busy promoting his upcoming flick, Pati Patni Aur Woh, which is making waves on the internet with its songs Dheeme Dheeme and Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare.
