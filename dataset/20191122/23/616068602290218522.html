Hamaliuk feasts on T-birds

22 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Wayne Moore

Dillon Hamaliuk continued to remind the Seattle Thunderbirds, and their fans, what they lost when he was dealt to the Kelowna Rockets in the summer.

Hamaliuk, who came to Kelowna in a four-player deal during the WHL bantam draft, scored twice Friday, including the game winner, as the Rockets edged Seattle 3-2 in the Washington city.

In two games against the T-Birds, both in Seattle, Hamaliuk has scored three times and added an assist.

But, the T-Birds battled back.

The other former T-Bird involved in the deal to Kelowna, Jake Lee, was involved in a late third period fight with rookie Lucas Ciona.
