Manny Jacinto Of ‘The Good Place’ Protested Climate Inaction While Looking Extremely Hot

22 Nov 2019, 23:30 by Joseph Earp

Over the last few months, actress and activist Jane Fonda has used her public profile to protest the U.S. government’s inaction on climate change.

Fonda, who has been an active participant in leftist movements since her days campaigning against the Vietnam War, has even been arrested for her actions. She has refused to budge in the face of police and has even rallied her famous friends around her for support, drawing more and more attention to an extremely pressing issue.

A few weeks ago, Fonda brought along Ted Danson for the ride. Now, she’s brought along another The Good Place star — Manny Jacinto.

Jacinto is best known for three things — portraying the sweet and bumbling Jason Mendoza on the show, being kind and respectful in interviews, and for being extremely hot.
