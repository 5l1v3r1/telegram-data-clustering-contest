Gogglebox stars tear Jo Swinson's Liberal Democrat election video apart

22 Nov 2019, 23:46

Gogglebox viewers were treated to a slice of politics on Friday night as the on-screen families watched a sponsored party election broadcast from the Liberal Democrats.

With party leader Jo Swinson fronting the campaign, the Gogglebox clan were not impressed by the trailer, which tried to play on Jo’s reliability.

With the opening shot showing Jo going out for a run (in a rather fluorescent orange top), the families were having none of the political leader’s antics, asking how she was managing to ‘smile and run’ at the same time.

In the next scene, Jo is seen tidying her home while telling the audience that they can create a brighter future: ‘For my children, for your children and for the country we love.’

However, the families were skeptical, with Siddiqui sibling Baasit asking: ‘Has the fact that’s in her house and that setting made her more relatable, though?’

‘Not really, I always knew Jo Swinson had a house,’ brother Umar hilariously replied, ‘And that she wore shoes… and did things. It’s no surprise to me.’

With a following shot seeing Jo travel on a train, there was a considerable amount of cynicism from the eye rolling Michael family, with son Louis sarcastically saying: ‘Oh, she gets the train, just like everyone else.’

As Jo went on to mention ‘saving the planet’, Louis continued: ‘They’re going to cure all diseases, they’re going to end poverty, they’re going to discover alien life.’

However, it was Jo’s closing words of ‘join us’ that seemed to rub the TV personalities up the wrong way, with zombie-style impersonations cries of ‘joooiiin us’ echoing across the show.

‘She’s not even saying vote, she’s saying join the Liberal Democrats,’ Umar repeated. ‘Can you join a party and then vote for another party?’

Ah well Jo, better luck next time.

Gogglebox returns next Friday at 9pm on BBC One.
