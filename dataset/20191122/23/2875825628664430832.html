I'm A Celeb Extra Camp's Adam Thomas and Emily Atack enjoy boozy lunch during downtime

Extra Camp co-host's Adam Thomas and Emily Atack enjoyed a bottle of rosé wine together as they soaked up the sun away from set

22 Nov 2019, 23:04 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity Extra Camp presenters Emily Atack and Adam Thomas appear to be getting on well.

The pair, who host the jungle spin-off show, alongside longtime host Joel Dommett, were spotted soaking up the Australian sunshine together as they sipped on rosé and ate food at a rooftop bar in photos taken during their downtime on Friday.

Married man Adam, 31, appeared engrossed in his conversation with single Emily, 29, as he placed his hand on his colleagues shoulder during their chat.

The bubbly Inbetweeners star and former soap actor shared a few laughs before finishing their beverages and heading off.

Emily looked like she was right at home in the heat as she dressed her curves in a satin camouflage printed dress and shaded her eyes with sunglasses.

Cheeky chappy Adam kept it casual in a vest and shorts.

It comes after Adam was accused by viewers of being 'inappropriate' with a crew member on-screen.

The first-time host came under fire for his on-screen 'flirting' with an I'm A Celeb production staff member on Extra Camp.

The former Emmerdale actor was giving TV watchers a tour of the set of the upcoming Bush Tucker trial named Just Desserts when he was joined on-screen with a crew member named Molly.

Introducing the behind-the-scenes worker, Adam exclaimed: "I'm here with the one, the only, the beautiful, the gorgeous - sorry what's your name again? - It's Molly! I knew that!"

He continued: "Molly just told me how nervous she is and I'm really nervous so if we get through this without messing up it's gonna be a miracle. Let's do it! We're a team ok?".

The Manchester native then caused a stir by asking Molly: "Right, first things first. What's your number?".

He then blurted out: "Joking!"

Adam's behaviour did not go unnoticed by fans who called it 'creepy' and 'inappropriate' who took to Twitter to hit out at him.

A viewer watching blogged: "Adam Thomas hitting on the Trial woman and his whole encounter with her was just creepy #ImACelebExtraCamp #ImACeleb."

"Omg I didnt even know he was married! And he just openly and creepily hit on a woman on live TV! Not okay #extracamp," typed another.

An uncomfortable TV watched added: "Adam Thomas! Bit inappropriate at the trial thing."

Some berated him, as they asked: "How’s adam Thomas pure flirting on live tv with his wife and kids sat at home," and "Why was Adam Thomas flirting with a member of @ImACelebrity's production team when he has a wife and child? That's a big no-no."

Adam hosts Extra Camp alongside Emily Atack and Joel Dommett.

While Joel's been on I'm A Celeb's sister show for two years now, this year is Emily and Adam's debut after the departure of Scarlett Moffatt and Joe Swash.
