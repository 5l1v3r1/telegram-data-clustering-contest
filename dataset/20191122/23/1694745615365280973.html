International Delight Has a New Birthday Cake Coffee Creamer (Sprinkles Sold Separately)

22 Nov 2019, 23:10 by Danielle Jackson

International Delight wants to help you satisfy your sweet tooth as soon as you wake up in the morning. The brand recently announced that it'll be releasing two brand-new coffee creamer flavors that might give those Cinnamon Toast Crunch and Funfetti creamers from Coffee-Mate a run for their money.

The two new flavors, Birthday Cake and Vanilla Cannoli Crème Latte, will officially launch in January 2020, joining a growing lineup of over 20 flavors from International Delight. The Birthday Cake flavor is made to taste like a classic yellow birthday cake with sprinkles and vanilla frosting, while Vanilla Cannoli Crème Latte is meant to taste like coffee and your favorite dessert blended into one. Both products will be available at retailers nationwide for around $4 a bottle. Read ahead to get a look at the product imagery.
