FIND HIM

Connor McGinley missing: Loved ones issue desperate plea to trace Dundee dad after shock disappearance

22 Nov 2019, 23:33 by Ewan Mowat

LOVED ones of a missing Dundee man have issued a desperate social media appeal to find him.

Connor McGinley, 23, disappeared from the north of the city this morning, with police launching a hunt to trace him.

He is believed to have left his dad's house in the Ardler area around 11.45am.

Connor was then caught on camera around 1pm at a Morrisons store near Pitkerro Road in the north-east of Dundee.

He is described as 5ft6in tall, with a stocky build and short, dark hair.

Connor - who has a one-year-old son - was wearing his work clothes when he went missing: a grey hoodie, black trousers and a pair of beige boots.

His brother Jordan launched a social media plea to try and trace his younger sibling following his disappearance.

He shared pictures of Connor and asked for anyone with information to get in touch.

His mum Laura also took to Facebook to appeal for help from locals.

She also said she was "extremely worried" about her son.

Anyone with any information about Connor is urged to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 1698 or speak to an officer in the area.
