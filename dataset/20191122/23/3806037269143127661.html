OMEGA to Launch Limited Edition Seamaster Diver 300M James Bond Set

Celebrating the 50th anniversary of ‘On Her Majesty’s Secret Service.’

22 Nov 2019, 23:07 by Gabrielle Leung

After the success that followed OMEGA’s limited edition James Bond timepiece, the Swiss luxury watchmaker returns with a special Seamaster Diver 300M James Bond Limited Edition Set. The set, which will only release in a quantity of 257, is comprised of two unique watches inside a Globe-Trotter suitcase.

Celebrating the 50th anniversary of On Her Majesty’s Secret Service, the first timepiece carries a sleek black bezel, 42mm stainless steel case and PVD-coated black ceramic dial. An 18k yellow gold plate engraved with the limited edition number each unit is given is found on the side of the case. The second model comes in the luxe 18k yellow gold, complete with a complementary buckle and spiral-brushed black ceramic dial.

Both watches feature a laser-engraved gun-barrel design, gold hands and indexes, and a marker at the 12 o’clock mark which features a design inspired by the Bond family’s coat-of-arms. A hidden number 50 is set within the Super-LumiNova at 10 o’clock — a tribute to On Her Majesty’s Secret Service’s 1969 release. Each owner will also receive an additional stainless steel bracelet and a NATO strap for each model.

Take a look at the limited edition set above and stay tuned for more information, as exact pricing and release details have yet to be announced.
