State Road 82 shut down due to double fatal crash Friday morning

22 Nov 2019, 08:13 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- Troopers say a fatal hit-and-run crash caused roadblock on State Road 82 in Lehigh Acres Friday morning.

According to Florida Highway Patrol, the crash happened a little after 7 a.m. on S.R. 82 at Gregory Avenue.

The Lee County Sheriff's Office is also on scene, reporting that all westbound lanes of S.R. 82 are closed, and that drivers should seek alternate routes if possible.

FHP tells Fox 4 that two fatalities are involved. They say that two vehicles were stopped on the roadside to repair a flat tire when a pickup truck crashed into both vehicles and left the scene.

The road is expected to remain closed until approximately 11 a.m.
