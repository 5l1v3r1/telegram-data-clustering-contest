Kazakhstan to host Syria talks on December 10-11

22 Nov 2019, 08:32

NUR-SULTAN (Reuters) - Kazakhstan will host a fresh round of Syria peace talks sponsored by Russia, Turkey and Iran on December 10-11, Kazakh foreign minister Mukhtar Tleuberdi told reporters on Friday.

Tleuberdi provided no details about the agenda or participants in the talks.
