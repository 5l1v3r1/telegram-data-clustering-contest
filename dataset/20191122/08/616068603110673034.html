Icy roads cause headaches

22 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Rob Gibson

Emergency crews were kept busy Friday morning after early-morning frost left some roadways, particularly bridges and overpasses slippery.

The overpass at John Hindle Dr. and Highway 97 was icy and was the scene of a four-vehicle collision at 8:40 a.m.

Emergency crews responded to the crash and everyone is reported to be out of their vehicles and there doesn't appear to be any serious injuries.
