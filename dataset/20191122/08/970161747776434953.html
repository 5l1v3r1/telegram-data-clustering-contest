EastEnders arrest, Corrie hostage situation and 23 more soap spoilers

22 Nov 2019, 08:00

It’s another incredibly dramatic week in soapland, as Linda Carter (Kellie Bright) finds herself in quite the predicament when she’s arrested after an incident at the school gates in EastEnders, while Michelle Connor (Kym Marsh) reveals the extent of her plans to get revenge on Robert Preston (Tristan Gemmill) in Coronation Street.

Meanwhile, Graham Foster (Andrew Scarborough) is left devastated when Kim Tate (Claire King) makes a shocking revelation in Emmerdale, while Breda McQueen (Moya Brady) makes Sylver (David Tag) choose in Hollyoaks.

EastEnders

1. With Mick having to attend a doctor’s appointment, Linda agrees to to leave Ollie to school — in spite of the fact that she’s still a bit hungover. While at the school, she accidentally reverses her car into another, and refrains from telling anyone. However, unbeknown to her, someone was watching. Later, Mieko and Shelley wait on her — with two police officer’s in tow — and, after a breathalyser test, Linda is arrested.

2. Mick is determined to make his Wife Swap competition a success, and — as the day of said competition arrives — everyone awaits the outcome at The Queen Vic. However, Gray decides to take it upon himself to sabotage the results.

3. Jags speaks to Chantelle, and reminds her of who he is. However, it doesn’t take long for her to remember him, and the pair fondly reminisce about days gone by. Later in the week, she bumps into him again — only this time Gray and Kheerat are there.

4. Adam suggests a family holiday, but Honey’s not convinced. She uses her kids as an excuse to stay put. However, she’s not sure how to proceed when Billy offers to babysit in her absence.

5. Louise grows concerned about Lisa and quizzes her about her medication. Later, Keanu informs Sharon about Louise being convinced that Lisa’s unwell, and she’s left feeling guilty as a result.

6. With a new attitude to boot, Ben gives Sonia a hand — and he’s in high spirits as a result.

7. Martin’s problems continue, and when drama ensues in The Queen Vic, he makes a quick exit. However, Ruby is quick to follow him, and tells him that he can stay at her flat if he wishes. Martin’s emotional as a result, and her thanks her for being so supportive, and leans in to kiss her — however, he stops himself from doing so.

Coronation Street

8. Paul’s fuming with Bernie when he discovers that she’s reported Kel for sexual abuse. Kel’s arrested for burglary, but — during the interview — he’s shown the photos that were at his flat. As a result, and angry Kel later takes Bernie hostage at No. 5. However, Gemma and Paul come to the rescue, and Bernie promises to be a better mum.

9. Hope isn’t pleased when Fiz refuses to throw a fireworks party. However, with the young girl exhibiting worrying behaviour once more, Tyrone suggests that maybe they’d be better off doing as she wishes. Later, during the party, Kevin and Tyrone come to the realisation that the back yard is on fire — and Hope is evidently amused as she watches the flames.

10.  Richard and Nina pay a visit to Weatherfield to see where Roy works. However, Richard’s tired and frail afterwards, and Nina blames Roy for the setback. Later, Roy looks after Richard — and realises just how hard it must be for Nina to do this on a regular basis.

11. Nick reveals his new plans for the factory, but the workers aren’t too pleased — and neither is Sarah. Later, she overhears Carla discussing Sinead’s beard oil business with Adam, and it gives her an idea, and thus she informs Nick and the girls that she has a better plan — making and selling bespoke products directly to the public.

12. With revenge on her mind, Michelle reveals to Robert that she wants to go ahead with the wedding. As a result, Robert suggests they should gather all their friends and family together in order to break the news.

13. Steve’s heartbroken when Emma thinks about spending Christmas in Australia after mum Fiona bought her tickets to do so. However, Amy shows Emma the stocking that Steve made for her, and thus she decides to spend the festive season in Weatherfield.

14. Michael offers to financially support daughter Tianna by making regular payments, and Grace promises she’ll give it some thought.

Emmerdale

15. Determined to get revenge on Graham, Kim implies to Marlon that her ex-lover shouldn’t be around Leo. Rhona berates her for interfering, but little does she know Kim’s planning something big. Later, Graham receives a text from Kim saying she needs to speak to her employees in the Woolpack. However, when Graham arrives, he knows what’s about to happen. Kim reveals a shocking secret about Graham — and Rhona’s floored as a result.

16. The week of Bernice and Liam’s wedding arrives, and Dee Dee informs her mum that she’s decided to start school in Emmerdale as a wedding gift. At the hen do, Bernice’s kids are mortified when a stripper shows up, but — during the celebrations — Bernice misses a call. Meanwhile Dee Dee takes a call — and she makes a shocking revelation.

17. Kyle wants Moira to attend his birthday party, but — given everything that’s happened — Cain isn’t too keen on such a thing.

18. Dawn uncovers a secret of Al’s.

19. When Laurel’s in need of a babysitter, Jimmy offers to step in.

20. Jacob and David agree to see a counsellor — and Jacob’s hopeful as a result.

Hollyoaks

21. After Luke disappears without telling her where he’s off to, Cindy becomes suspicious and decides to check out his laptop. However, Joel spies an opportunity and thus hides the device, promising to return it to her if she helps him out in the soup kitchen.

22. Sylver and Grace spend quite a bit of time together, and Breda is disgusted when Grace enters the living room in nothing more than her dressing gown — with Sylver’s tool belt as an accompaniment. Worried about how Sylver’s actions will look to the police, Breda asks her son to choose between his family and Grace.

23. Damon returns to the village — as does Maxine — and the pair set their sights upon one another for the first time since his sentencing. However, with Liberty flirting with Damon, Maxine begins to believe that they’re dating.

24. After speaking to Sid, Juliet informs Sienna that the young man is sleeping rough, and thus Sienna meets with Alice — Sid’s mum — to try and sort something out. She manages to convince Alice that she’s capable of being a mum to Sid, but when the pair arrive back at Sienna’s, Liberty and Brody make a worrying discovery about Sid.

25. Mitchell and Scott aren’t sure how to act around one another in public, so Scott suggests that he could perhaps move in — and Mitchell’s not pleased, to say the least. It takes some persuasion, but Mitchell finally agrees, but problems ensue when Martine later calls round — and Scott is forced to hide in the bedroom.
