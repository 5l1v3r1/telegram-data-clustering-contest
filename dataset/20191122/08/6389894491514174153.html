TSB apologises to customers after payment delays

22 Nov 2019, 08:37 by Sebastian McCarthy

TSB has been rocked by a fresh technical issue this morning as customers complained about an overnight delay of payments into accounts.

The embattled bank has apologised to customers this morning, promising to ensure that people will not be left out-of-pocket after some payments into TSB accounts were delayed last night.

TSB customers expressed their frustration with the bank on social media.
