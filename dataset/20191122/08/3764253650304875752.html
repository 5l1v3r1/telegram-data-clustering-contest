Jose Mourinho claims Tottenham can win PL next season but not this term

22 Nov 2019, 08:17

- Jose Mourinho spoke on a number of issues in an interesting first presser as Spurs manager

- The tactician admitted Spurs were not title contenders this season but expect to mount some competition next year

- The Special One also admitted he made mistakes in his past jobs and would try to “make new mistakes”

New Tottenham manager Jose Mourinho has ruled out his own club from much success this season in his very first media interaction since his appointment.

Mourinho was in attendance at a press conference on Thursday, November 21, ahead of Tottenham’s trip to the London Stadium to take on Westham on Saturday, November 23.
