Pagalpanti FIRST Reviews: John Abraham, Anil Kapoor & Team Receive A Thumbs Up From The Audience

Twitterati have flooded social media sites with their views.

22 Nov 2019, 08:18 by Koimoi.com Team

Pagalpanti starring John Abraham, Arshad Warsi, Anil Kapoor, Urvashi Rautela amongst others, hit the theatre screens today. The movie which is a family drama has already praises pouring in for itself, and fans can’t stop hailing their favourite actors and their collab!

The movie directed by Anees Bazmee also stars Pulkit Samrat, Ileana D’Cruz and Kriti Kharbanda in leads. Twitterati have flooded social media sites with their views.
