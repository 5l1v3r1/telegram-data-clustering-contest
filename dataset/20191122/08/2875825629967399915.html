Kate Garraway's husband desperate to jet to I'm A Celeb after noticing heartbreaking sign

Derek Draper appeared on Good Morning Britain today with their two children Bill and Darcey as they spoke about Kate Garraway on I'm A Celeb

22 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Irishmirror.ie

Kate Garraway's husband Derek Draper has admitted that he was desperate to jet to I'm A Celebrity after noticing a heartbreaking moment that many may have missed.

Appearing on Good Morning Britain today with their two children Bill and Darcey, Derek said that he was incredibly proud of Kate.

After suggesting that she could win the show, he joked about Kate saying that she missed her children but hadn't said anything about him.

But Derek also said that Kate is struggling more than she had let on.

"The bit that I thought really upset for her was something people might not have noticed," he said. "There was some good news delivered about the food or wagon wheels.

"Everyone was jumping and hugging and she turned to hug someone but everyone was hugging someone. And I wanted to dive into the television and give her a big hug.

"So it is hard seeing her struggle with things but at the same time I know she likes a challenge."

Questioned about the rumours she has smuggled make-up in, Derek said: "I think it’s probably harder to smuggle gold out of Fort Knox than make-up into I’m A Celeb. For sure she’s not. But I said, she never looks better than first thing in the morning.”

He added: "We want her back but it is a bit tidier without her."

Asked whether she would wear a bikini in the jungle shower, he said: "She’s got some bikinis that cover her up a little bit. At the bottom of the case, she’s got a few skimpier ones.

"She said ‘I don’t think I’ll wear these’. I of course think she looks amazing and said, ‘Wear them if you want to’. We’ll see, we’ll see which bikini comes out."

*I'm A Celebrity airs tonight at 9pm on ITV
