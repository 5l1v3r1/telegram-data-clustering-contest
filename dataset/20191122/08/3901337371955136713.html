Rulani Set To Visit Liverpool’s New R950m Training Facility?

22 Nov 2019, 08:55 by SL Social

Could Orlando Pirates head coach Rulani Mokwena be visiting Liverpool’s brand new R950 million training facility in December? Well his Instagram story suggests he is. Check it out.

Mokwena has a penchant for traveling as seen in his trips to Europe earlier this year, where he spent some time with Bongani Zungu in France.

Now it appears that the 34-year-old could be jetting out to England to visit Liverpool’s state-of-the-art Kirkby training facility.

“Counting down the days to December,” Mokwena gushed along with a video of the training facility.

The center which cost the defending UEFA Champions League champions around £50 million (R950 million) will comprise of two gyms, a full-size indoor sports hall, pool, hydrotherapy complex and specialist sports rehabilitation suites.
