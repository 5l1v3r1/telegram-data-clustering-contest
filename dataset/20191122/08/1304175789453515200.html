Giant iTab targets event agencies with launch of lunch and learn tech events

22 Nov 2019, 08:41 by Stavros Andriopoulos

Event technology and digital solutions company, Giant iTab has launched a series of free lunch and learn events for event agencies keen to learn more about using technology to drive engagement at events and more face-to-face time with colleagues and clients.

The series of events held in the run to Christmas will take place at Giant iTab’s new London customer experience centre for event professionals. The centre has been designed as a learning and collaboration zone and features a range of giant touchscreens, tablets and smartphones for agencies to interact and engage with and to understand how the technology can be used to enhance their events.
