Norway's Telenor sold remaining VEON stake in $362 mln deal

22 Nov 2019, 08:04 by Reuters

OSLO — Norway’s Telenor said on Friday it had sold its remaining 157 million shares in telecoms group VEON for $362 million.

“This transaction is in line with the previously communicated strategy and concludes the final sell-down in VEON,” Telenor said. “Citi has been Telenor counterpart for the sale.”
