Davido drops much anticipated album A Good Time

22 Nov 2019, 08:51

- Nigerian singer Davido recently released his much anticipated second studio album

- The body of work titled A Good Time features 17 tracks which include 7 collaborations

- Since its release, the project is already the topic of discussion on social media

The past few months for popular Nigerian singer Davido Adeleke aka Davido, has been riddled with the music star gearing up his fans and supporters for the coming of his second studio album, A Good Time.

From occasionally dropping teasers on his social media pages, to reiterating the amount of effort and dedication he put into the project, Davido has been speaking and indeed, people were listening.

The moment of glory finally came at the early hours of Friday, November 22, when the album dropped on multiple music streaming platforms in the world, and received an incredible reception from music lovers that have had their appetite aroused time and again.

A Good Life featured 17 tracks, with 10 of the songs being solo efforts from Davido, while the other seven were collaborative efforts with a rich selection of local and international talents.

The album kicks off with an intro that sees Davido smoothly ushering his listeners into a socially conscious, yet feel good kind of music.

Hugely successful singles like Fall, If, Assurance, Blow My Mind and fairly recent Risky, make a return on the album, with the singer making a statement with other fresh tracks like Green Light Riddim, Milli and Get To You.
