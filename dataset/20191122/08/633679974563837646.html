Mossel Bay police hunting rape suspect

22 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Algoa FM

Police in Mossel Bay are searching for a man who allegedly raped a woman at a complex in Hartenbos.

A spokesperson said that the woman, who is in her thirties, was busy hanging up washing on Thursday morning when she was overpowered by the suspect.

Police said he was wearing a dark blue T-shirt, denim pants, a dark blue cap and black safety boots.

Anyone who may have information that could assist should contact Mossel Bay police.
