The bargain retailer B&M have announced its Black Friday deals which sees customers being able to get 60 per cent off branded electrical items, reports the Liverpool Echo.

And there are plenty of bargains to be had ahead of Christmas with the Hoover H-free Cordless Vacuum on sale for £60, Samsung 50" Smart 4K Ultra HD TV for £350 reduced from £450 and Beat earphones for £29.
