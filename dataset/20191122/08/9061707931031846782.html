Heavy rain causes closure of Derby school

'The decision to remain closed has not been an easy one'

22 Nov 2019, 08:43 by Anna Whittaker

A Derby school is closed today due to drainage issues caused by heavy rain.

Grampian Primary Academy, in Grampian Way, Sinfin, closed yesterday afternoon and will remain shut today.

The school posted on Facebook yesterday asking parents to pick their children up "as quickly as possible" due to "great difficulty" with drainage after the heavy rainfall.

They said: "Despite our efforts, we have a significant issue with the drains which means that we have had to close the main toilet block.

"This difficulty is now extending throughout the other toilets in school."

They later announced that the school and nursery would also be closed today , as they had been "unable to resolve the problem to a satisfactory standard".

They said: "We apologise for the inconvenience we know this causes for our families. The decision to remain closed has not been an easy one."
