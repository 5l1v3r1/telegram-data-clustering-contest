Chris Hemsworth's Wife Elsa Says Liam Hemsworth Deserves Much Better Than Miley Cyrus

22 Nov 2019, 08:48 by Tara Walsh

Liam Hemsworth's sister in law supports him

Elsa Pataky has come out to say her brother in law Liam Hemsworth deserves much better than Miley Cyrus.

She made the comments this week at a press conference in Madrid.

'After a relationship that you've dedicated ten years to, he's a little bit down.'

Liam and Miley called off their relationship after eight months of marriage earlier this year.

She was spotted kissing Kaitlynn Carter during a break away in Italy just as her split from Liam was announced.

Miley and Kaitlynn split in September and she's since moved on with Cody Simpson.

As per Hola! Magazine, Liam's sister in law Elsa has spoken out about the split.

She says, 'My brother-in-law, well… After a relationship that you've dedicated ten years to, he's a little bit down.'

'He's coping well, he’s a strong boy and he deserves the best, I think he deserves much better.'

After the shocking split, Liam flew to Australia to spend some time with family.

Elsa says, 'You always find support in your family, and he's joined at the hip with his brother, who has been there to give all the strength he needed.'
