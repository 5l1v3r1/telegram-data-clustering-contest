Charlie Brooker teases Black Mirror-themed guest appearance on The Simpsons

22 Nov 2019, 08:12

The Simpsons may have waned in quality over the past 20 years or so, but let’s face it – you still wouldn’t turn down the chance to have your voice heard in Springfield.

Charlie Brooker certainly didn’t want to pass on that kind of opportunity, and the Black Mirror creator has now announced that he’ll be making a special guest appearance in this weekend’s Thanksgiving-themed episode.

Simpsons executive producer Matt Selman first invited Brooker to provide a guest voice on ‘a Black Mirror-ish episode’ back in August, owing to the fact that the long-running cartoon’s production team were ‘super fans’ of the dystopian Netflix series.
