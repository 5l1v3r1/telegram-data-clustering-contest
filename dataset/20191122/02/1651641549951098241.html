Tesla will be launching the Cybertruck today, here’s everything you need to know about it

22 Nov 2019, 02:03 by Anmol

Just a couple of hours back, we reported that Tesla is finally taking the wraps off its much anticipated Cybertruck. Now, Tesla’s official account has confirmed that the launch is scheduled for 8 PM PT today.

As usual, the launch of the Cybertruck will be live-streamed online. Unfortunately, neither Tesla nor Elon Musk has shared the live stream details but the official Tesla account confirmed a live stream of the launch. We will be updating the article as soon as the details of the live stream are available.
