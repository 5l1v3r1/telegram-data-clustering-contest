Eagles midfielder kicking burger goals

22 Nov 2019, 02:48 by Staff Writer

WEST Coast Eagles midfielder Dom Sheed is bringing a beloved Collingwood burger chain to suburban Perth.

Huxtaburger’s second outlet in WA will open at 515 Beaufort Street, Highgate on November 28.

The store will be operated by 2018 AFL Grand Final star Dom Sheed and family friend Zoe Tsagalis.

Over the coming weeks there will be many offers to launch the Highgate store, including a $1 burger day on Saturday, November 30.

Owner Zoe Tsagalis said Beaufort Street has had its share of negativity lately but they strongly believed the location and history of the strip would always retain its appeal.

“We love the neighbourhood and are excited to share the block with Mary Street Bakery, The Queens, El Publico and The Must Wine Bar,” she said.

“The Huxtaburger brand is synonymous with the Melbourne food culture. Each store has its own personality and becomes part of the local neighbourhood.

“I have loved the product for a long time and I know that when Dom travels to Melbourne he often drops into the Collingwood store for a burger, so we jumped at the opportunity to join the business.”

Huxtaburger started in Melbourne by three food lovers, including Master Chef guest Daniel Wilson and Dante Ruaine.

The brand has expanded with stores across Melbourne, Sydney and Perth.

Its first WA location opened at Hibernian Place, Perth in April 2018.
