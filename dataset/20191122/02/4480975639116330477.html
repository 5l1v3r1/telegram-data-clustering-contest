BIS: Fiscal policy must be wise

22 Nov 2019, 02:36 by Omkar Godbole

Bank for International Settlements (BIS) on Friday said it is not a question of fiscal policy per se, but of wise fiscal policy and added further that amalgamating monetary and fiscal policy to bolster the economy isn’t the right solution to the world’s slow-growth, low-inflation challenge.

Key quote

"Well-chosen and executed investment, for example, is generally preferable to current expenditures"
