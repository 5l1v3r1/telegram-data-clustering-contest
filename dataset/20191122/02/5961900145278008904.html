FEATURE JOBS: Happy Media, Proper Goods, Modern Currency + More

22 Nov 2019, 02:10 by By Yasmin Adhami

Happy Media, an Australian based online youth culture network focused primarily on independent news, reviews and feature articles, are looking for a full time Graphic Designer/Digital Producer (SYD) to join their Happy Mag team! Happy Mag is a progressive publisher in the emerging music, art and culture space with tens of thousands of weekly readers. The successful candidate will be working on a range of internal Happy Mag campaign collateral pieces, developing branded concepts for new events and taking ideas from concept to creation. Happy Mag are looking for a mid-weight to senior graphic designer with at least 5 years commercial design experience, excellent written and verbal communication skills and a proven track record of elevating brands through graphics. Sound like you? Then apply now!

Do you have previous experience in business administration, accounts, supply chain coordination or logistics operation? Then keep reading! Proper Goods, a leading eyewear and sunglass supplier to the Australia and NZ market, are on the hunt for a full time Operations Coordinator (SYD) to work on their procurement, shipping and delivery process. Duties include communicating with suppliers in relation to the processing of purchase orders, creating commercial shipping documents and overseeing the day-to-day operations of the shipping team. The ideal candidate has basic business, accounts or admin understanding, good to high level of proficiency in Excel and the ability to work to deadlines. Apply here.

Modern Currency, an integrated, disruptive and creative communications agency, are seeking a hungry and proactive PR Account Manager (MELB) to work with them on a full time basis. Modern Currency’s past and current clients include DFO, Cadbury, The Body Shop, Chemist Warehouse, Coco Vodka, Tourism Malaysia and many more! A typical day in this role is overseeing and implementing PR and Marketing projects for clients, producing media materials such as media releases, news stories and blog posts, assisting with events and taking client briefs. To excel in this role, candidates must have 3-5 years public relations or relevant combined industry experience, strong computer literacy and the ability to build positive relationships. If you want to thrive in a flexible, fun and modern (no pun intended) company, then apply today!
