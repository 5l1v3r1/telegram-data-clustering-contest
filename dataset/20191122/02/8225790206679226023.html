Maui brush fire that prompted evacuations now 100% contained

22 Nov 2019, 02:37 by HNN Staff

HONOLULU, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) - A brush fire on Maui that triggered evacuations Thursday is now 100% contained.

The fire, first reported about 1:40 p.m. Thursday, was threatening Kahikinui Homesteads on the Southern flank of Haleakala, near Ulupalakua Ranch.

At least a dozen homes were evacuated, but all evacuation orders have been called off and no roads are closed.

Meanwhile, a portion of Piilani Highway that was closed in the area has since reopened.

The flames scorched approximately 300 acres.

On Friday, officials said while the blaze is contained, firefighters continue to monitor it.
