Beaufort Town Market hosts internationally renowned dog trainer

22 Nov 2019, 02:00 by Rochelle Kirkham

An internationally renowned dog trainer will share his tips and tricks on how to get your dog to behave during a free presentation on Saturday.

Tony Knight travels around the world helping desperate dog owners overcome everyday problems like aggression, anxiety and walk chaos and is bringing his entertaining approach to teaching to the Beaufort Town Market.

He will take the stage with Beaufort resident Haley Wright from "Talking the lead" to share dog discipline techniques that help dog lovers with naughty pooches from 10.30am. Visitors can bring along their dogs on a lead.

Beaufort Progress Association president Liza Robinson said the dog training demonstration was in line with the aim to incorporate entertainment, food and gardening tips, and all sorts of workshops into the traditional market.

"It is shopping but also an entertainment experience. Visitors can get tips on dog training with their pooch, have a look at the stalls and then sit down and enjoy Devonshire Tea," she said.

"Because it is a small area in a small country town the market has a very personal feel. There is a lot of interaction and people chatting."

The new look Beaufort Town Market also offers old fashioned board games, a pop-up Vegemite museum, music, food and handcrafted products.

The Beaufort High School Choir will also perform from 9.30am to 10.30am.

The market runs on the fourth Saturday of every month from 8am to 1pm at the Beaufort Memorial Garden.
