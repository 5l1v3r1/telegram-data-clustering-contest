Sophie Molineux latest Aussie cricketer to step away from sport due to mental health concerns

Cricket Australia has ensured that they'll provide Molineux with all the support from their end to help the all-rounder get back to the game.

22 Nov 2019, 06:24 by Sports Desk

Melbourne Renegades all-rounder Sophie Molineux is the latest Australian cricketer to take a break from the sport due to mental health concerns. The all-rounder will not participate in Renegades’ Women’s Big Bash League (WBBL) clash against Melbourne Stars on Saturday. She will be replaced by Makinley Blows for the upcoming clash against Stars.

This development comes days after Glenn Maxwell, Will Pucovski and Nic Maddinson stepped away from the sport citing the same reason. Cricket Australia has ensured that they’ll provide Molineux with all the support from their end to help the all-rounder get back to the game.
