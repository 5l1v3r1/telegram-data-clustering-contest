Philippine Airlines 777 makes emergency landing at Los Angeles airport

22 Nov 2019, 06:28

LOS ANGELES: A Boeing 777 bound for Manila suffered an apparent engine failure on Thursday (Nov 21) shortly after takeoff and made an emergency landing in Los Angeles.

Soon after Philippine Airlines Flight 113 departed from Los Angeles International Airport, pilots reported a possible engine failure and landed without incident, the Federal Aviation Administration said.
