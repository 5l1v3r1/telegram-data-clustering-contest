Davido Drops ‘A Good Time’ Album

22 Nov 2019, 06:50 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Award-winning singer, David Adeleke better known as Davido has finally dropped his much anticipated album ‘A Good Time’.

Concise News reports that ‘A Good Time’ is Davido’s third studio album coming after ‘Omo Baba Olowo’ and ‘Baddest’.

‘A Good Time’ comprises seventeen tracks, including collaborations with Peruzzi, Zlatan, Naira Marley, WurlD, Chris Brown, Yonda, among others.

Prominent music producers on the album include Shizzi, Kiddominant, Speroach Beatz, Tekno, etc.

The ‘Fall’ crooner is obviously living a good time as he earlier shared his experience in the past seven years.

He wrote “A Good Time, It’s been a long 7 years. A lot has transpired in my life since my debut album ‘Genesis’, and I, David Adeleke (aka Davido) have [sic] gone through a process of immense changes and growth.
