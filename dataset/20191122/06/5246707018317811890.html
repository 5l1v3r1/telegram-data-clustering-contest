Gallery: This Zelda: Breath Of The Wild Bedroom Mural Is Absolutely Stunning

Wildly artistic

22 Nov 2019, 06:30 by Liam Doolan

Would you like to fall asleep in the kingdom of Hyrule every night? Perhaps you've already done this during a prolonged session of The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild on Switch or Wii U. If so, you'll likely appreciate this absolutely stunning bedroom mural based on the land of Hyrule in Breath of the Wild.

It's been created by a Zelda fan and freelance artist named Hannah Baker – who runs Platypus Murals out of High Point, North Carolina. When her little sisters asked her to paint their room (who also happen to be fans of Link's latest 3D adventure), Hannah decided to transform it into the beautiful open-world scenery featured in the game.

Hannah says her sisters helped her prepare the walls and ceiling, and then she spent "the next few weeks" after that, painting it all. She's even added some glow in the dark elements, apparently. The room itself is also filled with plenty of Zelda-themed items and even the beds match the surrounding environment.

Get a better look in the images below and visit Hannah's website to see more of her work.
