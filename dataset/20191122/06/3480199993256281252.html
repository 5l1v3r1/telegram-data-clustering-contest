Woman who proposed to Charles finally meets her prince

22 Nov 2019, 06:44

Her second request of the prince was met with a positive response.

A woman who sent the Prince of Wales a marriage proposal when she was seven finally got a positive answer – to a request for him to visit the university she works at in New Zealand.

Shona Mardle, now an education administrator, wrote to Charles suggesting marriage when she was younger but her dreams of getting hitched were dashed.

However, her request for Charles to visit Lincoln University in Christchurch had a more positive response as he spoke on environmental issues on Friday.

“We share the same birthday, but he’s five years older than me,” Mrs Mardle said.

“When I was seven I thought it would be a very good idea that we should get married because then we would have only one birthday party each year and that would save money.

“I thought it was really good.

“Whoever answered the letters sent back a very nice response.”
