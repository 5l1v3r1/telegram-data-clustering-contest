India vs Bangladesh, Pink Ball Test: Full list of activities planned on historic Day 1

22 Nov 2019, 06:49 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Highlights

  • A number of Indian cricketing heroes will be present at the occasion on day one, which will also witness cultural and musical programmes
  • West Bengal CM Mamata Bannerjee and Bangladesh PM Sheikh Hasina will also be present at the Eden Gardens
  • City landmarks are being illuminated in pink to mark the occasion

India may be the last major Test playing nation to embrace pink-ball cricket but the BCCI, under new President Sourav Ganguly have left no stone unturned to make the historic first day-night Test a memorable affair.

The City of Joy is firmly in the grip of a pink fever and tickets for the first four days of the match at the iconic Eden Gardens have already been sold out.

City landmarks are being illuminated in pink to mark the occasion, and a ball-shaped pink blimp hovers over the 67,000-capacity stadium.

A number of Indian cricketing heroes will be present at the occasion on day one, which will also witness cultural and musical programmes. West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Bannerjee and Bangladesh Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina will also be present at the Eden Gardens.

The match is scheduled to begin at 1 PM and will have a 40 minute lunch break and a 20 minute tea break.

Here’s a look at the full list of activities to mark the historic occasion:

TIME ACTIVITY
12:15 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister of Bangladesh and the Hon’ble Chief Minister of West Bengal arrives at the Club House of the Eden Garden stadium. The Chief Minister is expected to arrive first, followed by the Hon’ble Prime Minister.
The dignitaries will be received by the following inside the Club House driveway - Mr. Sourav Ganguly, President BCCI • Mr. Nazmul Hassan, President BCB • Deputy High Commissioner, Bangladesh • Mr. Safiful Imam, Counsellor Sports, Deputy High Commissioner, Bangladesh • Mr. Naresh Ojha, Vice President, CAB • Mr. Avishek Dalmiya, Secretary, CAB • Mr. Debabrata Das, Joint Secretary, CAB • Mr. Debasish Ganguly, Treasurer, CAB
The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister is escorted to the ground through the passage between the players dressing room. All the people mentioned above accompany the Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister to the ground.
Both the teams line up on the ground and the Captains introduce their team members to the Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister.
12:28 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister is escorted to their hospitality box in the second floor/lounge on the first floor of the Club House.
12:30 PM The captains of both the teams are on the ground for coin toss.
12:53 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister is escorted to the Lower Tier of the Club House for the ceremonial bell ringing.
Following dignitaries will be present at the Lower Tier. • BCCI, President • BCB, President • Mr. Sachin Tendulkar • Mr. Sunil Gavaskar • Mr. Kapil Dev • Mr. Anil Kumble • Mr. Rahul Dravid • Ex-Bangladesh team captain • BCCI, Office Bearers (4 people) • CAB, Office Bearers (4 people)
Ex-players from India and Bangladesh stand in the passage just opposite to the bell.
12:54 PM Both the current team’s line-up for the national anthems on the ground.
A Border Security Force band team plays the national anthems of India & Bangladesh from the ground.
12:58 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister rings the ceremonial bell, indicating the start of play.
12:59 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister is escorted to their respective hospitality box in the Club House.
1:00 PM The game starts. The Hon’ble Prime Minister’s box will have 10 seats and 2 reserved seats. 5 seats allocated to Bangladesh and India each. Indians will include – Chief Minister, Sourav Ganguly, Sachin Tendulkar, Jay Shah & Avishek Dalmiya.
3:00 PM End of first sessions of play. The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister leaves the ground.
3:10 PM A chat show involving the six legends of Indian cricket on the ground. The panel involves the following • Mr. Sachin Tendulkar • Mr. Rahul Dravid • Mr. VVS Laxman • Mr. Anil Kumble • Mr. Kapil Dev • Mr. Sunil Gavaskar
3:40 PM Play resumes for second session
5:40 PM End of second session of play
5:43 PM India’s ex-cricket captains and some other invited Indian athletes take a lap of honour in a golf cart on the playground. The Hon’ble Governor of West Bengal is also on a golf cart. An instrumental musical performance is delivered by Sorendru and group from a small stage outside the BCR and adjacent to B block, towards the field of play.
5:55 PM The lap of honour ends.
6:00 PM The last session of the play starts
7:30 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister arrives at the Club House of the Eden Gardens stadium. She is received by the Office Bearers of CAB and escorted to her hospitality box in the Club House.
8:00 PM The last session of play is over. Immediately after the end of play, a team of 200 odd people rush in to set up the stage on the field of play.
8:10 PM Runa Laila perform with her group of musicians. 250 dancers perform.
8:23 PM The Hon’ble Prime Minister & Chief Minister is escorted to the play-ground for the felicitation program.
8:25 PM Cultural program ends. The dignitaries go on the stage. The felicitation program is hosted by Harsha Bhogle/Boria Majumdar.
Hon’ble Prime Minister– Felicitates the current Indian team
Chief Minister– Felicitates the current Bangladesh team
Hon’ble Prime Minister – Felicitates the ex-Indian team, which played the inaugural test match against Bangladesh
Chief Minister- Felicitates the ex-Bangladesh team, which played the inaugural test match against India
Hon’ble Prime Minister - Felicitates the ex-Indian captains and Indian athletes
BCB President – Felicitates CM, Mr. Sourav Ganguly & Mr. Jay Shah, Hony Secretary, BCCI.
The following people deliver a speech of approximately 2 mins each. • Mr. Sourav Ganguly, BCCI President • Mr. Nazmul Hassan, BCB President • Mr. Sachin Tendulkar • Hon’ble Chief Minister of West Bengal • Hon’ble Prime Minister of Bangladesh.
9:15 PM Felicitation program ends. This is followed by a small musical program by Mr. Jeet Ganguly.
