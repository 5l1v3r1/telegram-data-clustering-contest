Friends props up for auction - Entertainment News

22 Nov 2019, 06:42 by WENN

Over 80 items from the set of hit '90s sitcom Friends are going up for auction.

Classic pieces including a wedding invite from Monica Gellar (Courteney Cox) and Chandler Bing's (Matthew Perry) ceremony, the infamous turkey with sunglasses and a fez, and the bright gold frame that sat on the back of Monica and Rachel Green's (Jennifer Aniston) apartment door are being sold by memorabilia site, Prop Store.

Other items including the canoe and paddles that Chandler and Joey Tribbiani (Matt LeBlanc) traded their entertainment center for are also up for sale, along with Ross Geller's (David Schwimmer) Holiday Armadillo costume and Joey's Hugsy Plush Toy.

Meanwhile, the famous orange Central Perk couch is perhaps the most coveted item going under the hammer, and is estimated to sell for between $6,000 and $8,000.

All proceeds from the sale will be donated to The Trevor Project, the world's largest suicide prevention and crisis intervention organization for LGBTQ young people.

The popular NBC comedy series aired from 1994 to 2004.
