Olympic boxing medallist Paddy Barnes retires

22 Nov 2019, 06:32

Double Olympic bronze medallist Paddy Barnes last night announced his retirement from boxing.

The 32-year-old north Belfast man, who fought for the Holy Family Golden Gloves club as a top Irish amateur, battled to Olympic bronze in Beijing in 2008 and again in London four years later as well as winning European and Commonwealth gold medals at the 48kilos, light-flyweight division.
