China's Xi says long-term improving trend for economy will not change: Xinhua

22 Nov 2019, 06:03 by Reuters

BEIJING (Nov 22): The long-term improving trend for China’s economy will not change, and the economy has great resilience, potential and room for maneuver, China’s President Xi Jinping said on Friday, as reported by official Xinhua News Agency.

Xi, speaking at a meeting with International Monetary Fund Chief Kristalina Georgieva, said he has full confidence in China’s development, according to Xinhua.
