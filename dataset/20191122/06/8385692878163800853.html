With the Bose Home Speaker 300 you're getting Amazon Alexa built-in. So you are able to stream music, from your voice. Alexa has support for a number of different streaming services including Spotify, Amazon Music Unlimited, Apple Music and much more. And if Alexa doesn't support your favorite music service, there's also Bluetooth available.

The audio quality on this speaker is exactly what you'd expect from Bose. It's full sounding and also offers some really good mids and highs. The bass isn't super deep though, which might be a good thing for many people.

It comes in two colors: black and silver. The silver color is silver for the speaker grates and then white for the rest of the speaker. Depending on your preference, the black might be the better looking speaker. It's more of a murdered-out black color.
