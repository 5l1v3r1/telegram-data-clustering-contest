Friday's Barcelona transfer talk news roundup: Patrick Kluivert, Dani Olmo, Erling Braut Haaland

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Barcelona eye possible January reinforcements.

22 Nov 2019, 06:57 by Andy Brent

The Spanish champions spent big on Antoine Griezmann during the summer and have been tipped to open their wallet again with a host of big names linked to the Camp Nou.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest transfer news from the Camp Nou.

Friday morning's headlines:

Juventus 'join Barcelona in Dani Olmo race'
Juventus are ready to battle Barcelona for the signing of Dinamo Zagreb attacker Dani Olmo, according to a report. Read more.

RB Leipzig 'able to sign Erling Braut Haaland for reduced fee'
RB Leipzig will reportedly be able to sign Red Bull Salzburg forward Erling Braut Haaland for a reduced fee if they wish to add the Norwegian to their squad. Read more.

Patrick Kluivert claims Matthijs de Ligt regrets Juventus move
Netherlands international Matthijs de Ligt regrets leaving Ajax for Juventus, according to Barcelona academy director Patrick Kluivert. Read more.

Agent confirms Real Madrid leading Barcelona in Milan Skriniar race
A representative of Inter Milan defender Milan Skriniar suggests that Real Madrid are well placed to sign the Slovakia international. Read more.
