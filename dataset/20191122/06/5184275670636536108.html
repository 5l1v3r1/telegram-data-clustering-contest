Ankhiyon Se Goli Mare From Pati Patni Aur Woh On ‘How’s The Hype?’: BLOCKBUSTER Or Lacklustre? VOTE NOW

The song features the three leads and is a party number.

22 Nov 2019, 06:46 by Koimoi.com Team

Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar starrer Patni Patni Aur Woh has managed to create a decent buzz around its release. After releasing the recreated version of Dheeme Dheeme as the first song, the makers released the second which is yet another remake of Govinda’s hit number Ankhiyon Se Goli Maare.

The number again features all three leads and is a peppy one. Flashy clothes, synchronized hook steps and fun lyrics compile for a usual Bollywood number. The recreated number is sung by Mika Singh and Tulsi Kumar. Composed by Tanishk Bagchi and penned by Shabbir Ahmed the song might make it to your playlists.

The song originally features Govinda and Raveena Tondon and was sung by Sonu Nigam and Jaspinder Narula.
