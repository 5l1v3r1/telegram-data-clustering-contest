Video: Mega Man is officially coming to Dragalia Lost

22 Nov 2019, 06:43 by alba

The development team behind Dragalia Lost has released the below intro video for Mega Man, who appears in the Mega Man: Chaos Protocol event scheduled to start on November 28. Mega Man is an adventurer you can add to your team during this event. If you participate in it and meet certain conditions, you’ll be able to recruit him in the mobile game.
