'The Mandalorian' Baby Yoda Merch Coming This Holiday Season

Some are already available.

22 Nov 2019, 17:42 by Isaac Rouse

UPDATE (November 22, 2019 12:57 EST) It looks like Amazon has taken down the two shirts on their site. Stay tuned as we get more word on availability.

After going viral on Twitter, merchandise for the breakout Baby Yoda from Disney+‘s The Mandalorian will arrive this holiday season. Official apparel and accessories will feature the unnamed alien and be available through Amazon, Hot Topic, Target, and more as soon as today. Meanwhile, Disney Store, Shop Disney, and Disney Parks will have apparel available before the holidays, with presale availability approaching in the coming weeks.

Although it doesn’t officially have a name, fans have come to call The Child Baby Yoda because of its resemblance to the actual Jedi master. Showrunners Jon Favreau and Dave Filoni stated they initially exempted the creature from appearing in initial merch and ads to keep its appearance a surprise. There are already two tees available on Amazon for $27.50 USD.

In other Star Wars news, adidas is offering a Star Wars Starship-inspired capsule collection pack.
