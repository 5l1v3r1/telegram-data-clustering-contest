Electoral Bonds: India's Opposition and Governing Parties Lock Horns

22 Nov 2019, 17:29

New Delhi (Sputnik): The Indian government headed by Prime Minister Narendra Modi has introduced “Electoral Bonds” – a banking instrument similar to promissory notes, to make political funding by citizens or companies incorporated in the country. The bonds were touted as a move to make political funding more transparent.

After a series of media exposures, India’s opposition parties, mainly Congress, have alleged that Electoral Bonds are in fact bribes or illegal commissions paid to political parties.
