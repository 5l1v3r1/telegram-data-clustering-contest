Man arrested after police seize cocaine and cash

It followed a traffic stop in Kitchener early on Friday morning

22 Nov 2019, 17:07 by KitchenerToday Staff

NEWS RELEASE
WATERLOO REGIONAL POLICE
*************************
On November 22, 2019, at approximately 1:20 a.m., Waterloo Regional Police patrol officers conducted a traffic stop at Highway 7/8, near Courtland Avenue in Kitchener. 

As a result of the stop, one male, 28, was arrested and a quantity of suspected cocaine and cash were seized. 

The male was charged with Possession for the Purpose of Trafficking.
