Vivo will be launching the Vivo X30 series 5G phones in December. The company has already confirmed that it will be powered by the Exynos 980 5G chipset. A new V1938T Vivo phone that has a model number has been approved by 3C authority of China. The fast charger that has been spotted in its certification reveals it could be the next high-end phone from the company. Probably, the V1938T could be the forthcoming Vivo X30 phone. New information surrounding the X30 reveals that it could be coming with 60x zoom capabilities.

Vivo V1938T Could be Vivo X30 or X30 Pro

The Vivo V1938t 3C certification suggests that it could be shipped with a charger that has a model number of V3330LOAO-CN. It is the very same charger that ships with the newly introduced iQOO Neo SD855 gaming phone.

The 3C certification also revealed that the Vivo V1938T is a 5G phone. Since the Vivo X30 series is the next 5G phone from the company, there is a possibility that the Vivo V1938T could be the model number of the X30 or X30 Pro phone. Vivo has recently confirmed that it the Vivo X30 will be the first phone from the company to support dual-mode 5G. Nothing else is known on the specifications of the Vivo V1938T smartphone. Now that the phone has appeared on 3C, it is likely to get spotted on TENAA with its full specifications.

Vivo X30 Cameras to Support 60x Zoom?

The Vivo X30 was recently spotted during the telecast of the “追我吧” reality show that includes popular Chinese artists on the Zhejiang Satellite TV. While the design of the phone was not clearly revealed, it appears that it could be featuring a full screen display with a notch or punch-hole. Also, the rear cameras of the phone to deliver 60x zoom.

Vivo X30, X30 Pro Specs and Pricing (Rumored)

The Vivo X30 could be coming with a 6.5-inch AMOLED whereas the X30 Pro could be featuring a 6.89-inch AMOLED display. Both phones are expected to deliver 90Hz refresh rate. The Exynos 980 5G SoC will be fueling both phones. The X30 may come with 8 GB of RAM and 128 GB / 256 GB UFS 2.1 storage. The X30 Pro could be coming with 12 GB of RAM and 256 GB of UFS 3.0 storage.

The Vivo X30 may feature 64-megapixel + 8-megapixel + 13-megapixel + 2-megapixel quad camera system and a front-facing camera of 32-megapixel. It could be housing a 4,500mAh battery. The X30 Pro could be coming with 60-megapixel + 13-megapixel + 13-megapixel + 12-megapixel quad camera setup and a frontal shooter of 32-megapixel. It could be housing a 4,500mAh battery along with 44W fast charging support. The Vivo X30 may have a starting price of 3,198 Yuan whereas the X30 Pro may begin at 3,998 Yuan. As always, readers are advised to digest Vivo X30 and X30 Pro specs and pricing rumors with a pinch of salt.
