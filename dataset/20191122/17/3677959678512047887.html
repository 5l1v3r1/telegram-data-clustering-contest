Red Dead Redemption 2 Actually Has Lower Control Latency on Stadia Than Xbox One X

22 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Nathan Birch

Google Stadia is here and the experience isn’t quite what was promised. As we’ve reported, the “4K” version of Destiny 2 you get with Stadia Pro is actually 1080p upscaled, while the Pro stream of Red Dead Redemption 2 is native 1440p at 30fps. Well, now the tech heads at Digital Foundry have delved even deeper to find out what kind of visual quality you can expect from the Stadia Pro stream of Red Dead Redemption 2, and it’s a pretty mixed bag.
