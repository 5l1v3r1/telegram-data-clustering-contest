I'm A Celebrity's Jacqueline Jossa 'to do every Bushtucker Trial' this series

Jacqueline Jossa was left in tears on I'm A Celebrity as she was voted to do the latest Bushtucker Trial by viewers

22 Nov 2019, 17:38 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity viewers think former EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa will be up for plenty of Bushtucker Trials this series.

A usual format on the ITV jungle show, hosted by Ant and Dec, could mean the Lauren Branning actress does several challenges.

After spotting a pattern in previous series, and given her reaction to the news she'd be doing the next trial, fans now think she will be the next serial trial doer.

Jacqueline is super squeamish, and in both challenges she has done so far she's been left in tears by even the thought of doing them.

On Thursday night, she was left visibly distressed when Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly told her the news.

Fans immediately took to Twitter , and tipped her to be voted again and again for the rest of the series.

In the past viewers have seen those campmates who visibly struggle with jungle life forced to do task after task by the public.

Katie Price , Natalie Appleton and Helen Flanagan have all been jungle stars who have done numerous challenges, often back-to-back.

There's always one person facing the most amount of trials, and if fans are correct this year it will be Jacqueline.

Taking to Twitter, fans shared their theory as others admitted they hoped it wasn't going to be the case.

One fan said: "I kinda get the feeling Jacqueline will face most (if not all) bushtucker trials!"
