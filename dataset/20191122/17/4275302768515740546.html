Buses diverted after two vehicle crash in Dundee

Police are in attendance at a two vehicle crash in Dundee.

22 Nov 2019, 17:05 by Jake Keith

The collision, on Pitkerro Road, took place at 4.15pm and is reportedly causing delays.

Xplore Dundee services 15 and 17 are currently being diverted via Kingsway to Longtown Road.

The services will not serve Pitkerro Road, Happyhillock Road, Longtown Street, until further notice.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said an ambulance is in attendance but added there are no serious injuries.
