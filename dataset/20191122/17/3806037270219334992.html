Microsoft Reveals Xbox One Black Friday 2019 Deals

Even offering a Project Scarlett upgrade for everyone buying a newer Xbox One console.

22 Nov 2019, 17:38 by Isaac Rouse

Following Sony’s PlayStation 4 Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals, Microsoft has now announced its own Black Friday specials for the Xbox this year. There are several deals for consoles where customers can receive up to $150 USD off any Xbox One/Xbox One X system with bundles, including the Xbox One S All-Digital Edition that comes with Minecraft, Sea of Thieves, and Fortnite Battle Royale.

United States customers can also grab an upgrade option for Xbox’s upcoming Project Scarlett console next year when purchasing an Xbox One X, Xbox One S or Xbox One S All-Digital Edition. Each also comes with 24-months of Xbox Game Pass Ultimate for as little as $19.99 USD per month. There are also deals for titles like Gears 5, Forza Horizon 4, and Sea of Thieves.
