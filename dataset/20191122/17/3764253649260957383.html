Leave me alone - Chioma says to people trying to tell her how to feed her baby

22 Nov 2019, 17:55

- New mom, Chioma, has lashed out at people trying to tell her how to feed her baby

- The young lady recently shared a video of herself bottle-feeding her baby boy

- Some Nigerians took to the post to comment about her decision to bottle-feed her baby at an early stage

- Chioma replied to the comment, telling them to leave her alone

Nigerian singer Davido's wife-to-be, Chioma Avril Rowland, has put people trying to advise her on how to feed her baby in their place.

Legit.ng had earlier reported that Chioma had shared a video of herself bottle-feeding her newborn baby boy, Ifeanyi David Adeleke Junior. In the video, the mother and son were listening to track from Davido's recently released album, A Good Time.

After Chioma shared the video, Nigerians commented under the post, some congratulated her and gushed about the mother and son while others took it upon themselves to share advice.
