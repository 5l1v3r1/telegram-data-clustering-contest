PlayStation Kicks off Its Black Friday Sale Early

22 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Shabana Arif

The sale starts today with money off games and hardware, but no consoles just yet.

PlayStation's first round of Black Friday deals includes discounted headsets, games - including PlayStation hits - and PS Plus membership.

The Gold Wireless Headset (Black, Rose Gold, and Fortnite Neo Versa) is £49.99 down from the usual £69.99, while the Platinum Wireless Headset is £99.99 down from £139.99 - although some of the selected retailers are flogging them for even less that the officially discounted price, so keep an eye out for that.

The library of PlayStation Hits titles has had a few quid knocked off, down from the usual £15.99 to £12.99, while there are other savings to be had on games on the PlayStation Store, including the following:

  • Grand Theft Auto V Premium Online Edition - £7.99
  • FIFA 20 - £39.99
  • Borderlands 3 - £34.99
  • Days Gone - £34.99
  • NBA 2K20 - £29.99
  • Marvel’s Spider-Man - £15.99
  • Blood & Truth - £15.99
  • Resident Evil 2 - £15.99
  • Battlefield V - £15.99
  • Crash Team Racing Nitro-Fueled - £22.74
  • Devil May Cry 5 - £15.99
  • Monster Hunter: World - £12.99
  • Red Dead Redemption 2: Ultimate Edition – £29.99
  • Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice: £38.99

And if your PS Plus membership is up for renewal in the next week or so, you can pick up 12 months for 25 per cent off from either the PS Store or an online retailer. That'll bring the cost down from £49.99 to £34.99, saving you £15.

PlayStation's Black Friday sale runs up until midnight on December 2, so there's plenty of time to peruse, with potentially more deals dropping next week. Keep up to date with all of the sales going on everywhere over on our Black Friday hub.
