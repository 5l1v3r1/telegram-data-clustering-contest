Weekend events

22 Nov 2019, 17:28 by Ben Wilson

There’s no shortage of events this weekend to get you in the holiday spirit!

The Vancouver Christmas Market is on until Christmas Eve at Jack Poole Plaza.

The Olde Farmhouse Vintage Market is on this weekend at the Tradex in Abbotsford.

Visit Heritage Christmas at the Burnaby Village Museum on now until January 3rd.

The 37th Annual Dunbar Holiday Craft Fair happens Saturday at the Dunbar Community Centre.

Stop by the Surrey Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony Saturday.

The Aurora Winter Festival at Hastings Park is on until January 5th.

The Riley Park Winter Farmers Market is on now.

Air Supply play the River Rock Show Theatre Saturday.

Check out Barney Bentall & the Cariboo Express Saturday at The Vogue.

The VSO presents: Mozart Requiem And Schubert Unfinished Saturday at The Orpheum.

The Black Keys “Let’s Rock” tour hit Rogers Arena Sunday.

The BC High School Football Championships is on now until the end of the month at BC Place.

The Chutzpah! Festival wraps up this weekend.

Cirque Du Soleil Luzia is on now until December 29th Under the Big Top at Concord Pacific Place.

Out of Concealment – Female Supernatural Beings of Haida Gwaii – is on now at the Bill Reid Gallery until April.

Shake Up: Preserving What We Value explores the convergence of earthquake science and technology. It’s on at the Museum of Anthropology until the fall.

The Museum of Vancouver presents Haida Now, on now until next February.
