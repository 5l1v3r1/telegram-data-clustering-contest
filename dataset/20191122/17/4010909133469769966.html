More vivo S1 Pro info "leaked". It's expected to come with 32MP front camera and 48MP main camera

22 Nov 2019, 17:47

Apparently, there was a “leak” stating that the upcoming vivo S1 Pro is expected to come with a 32MP front-facing selfie camera as well as a 48MP main camera. Not only that, but they are expected to be accompanied by an AI Face Beauty feature which allows them to improve how they look when taking selfies.

The leak also mentioned that the cameras in the rear are able to capture images with up to a 120-degree field of view, making it suitable for taking pictures of the landscape. Furthermore, it will come with an additional 2MP Super Macro Lens and a 2MP Bokeh Camera as well.

That being said, we’ll find out more information as we slowly progress to the launch of the aforementioned smartphone.
