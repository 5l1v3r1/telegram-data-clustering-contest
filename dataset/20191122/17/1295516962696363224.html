Matt Reeves' The Batman Taps Big Lebowski Star to Play Carmine Falcone

22 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Jon Arvedon

In a familiar play, The Batman director Matt Reeves has once again employed social media to reveal the newest addition to the cast of his DC film.

"I said... #CarmineFalcone," Reeves tweeted with a GIF of John Turturro from The Big Lebowski, confirming the actor's role in the film.
