Penn State Dealt Unfortunate Injury News Before Ohio State Game

22 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Chris Rosvoglou

When Penn State takes the field this Saturday against Ohio State, the offense will be missing a key starter. Redshirt junior offensive lineman Michal Menet will not be available to play this weekend.

Menet has been the starting center for the Nittany Lions every game this season. Before his junior campaign even began he was named to the preseason Rimington Trophy Watch List

Penn State will replace Menet with redshirt sophomore Mike Miranda, who typically plays offensive guard. Nonetheless, he’s second on the depth chart at center.

FOX43 Sports first broke the news about Menet being inactive for this weekend with an undisclosed injury.
