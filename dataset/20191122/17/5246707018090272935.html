Nintendo Opens Official Merchandise Store On Amazon And We've Picked Out The Best Stuff

Apparel, toys and accessories galore

22 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Anthony Dickens

In recent years, we've noticed quite a surge in officially licensed merchandise. Nintendo appears to be more relaxed with its own IP signing off on many more products than ever before. Its latest move in the US is to highlight many of those products in a new official Amazon US storefront.

The "Nintendo Official Merchandise Amazon Storefront" has a huge number of products, mainly clothing and apparel but also plenty of toys, figures, accessories and costumes. Why now? Well, probably because it's Black Friday next week.
