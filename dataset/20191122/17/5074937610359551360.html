Man, 25 remanded for defilement of ten-year old mentally retarded girl

22 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Gifty Amofa, GNA

Accra, Nov. 22, GNA - An Accra Circuit Court has remanded a 25-year-old man for allegedly defiling a ten-year old mentally retarded girl.

The plea of Mugis Karim was not taken and he will make his next appearance on December 15.

Police Detective Sergeant Opoku Aniagyei told the Court presided over by Mrs Rita Abrokwa Doko that the complainant in the case is the head teacher of the victim’s school.

The prosecution said Karim is unemployed and a resident of Agbogbloshie in Accra.

 He said on November 19, this year, at about 1200 hours whilst the school was on break, the girl went to a refused dump, ten metres away from her school, close to the big Odorna gutter to ease herself.

Prosecution said she met the accused also collecting things from the dumping site where he lured her into a nearby wooden structure.

The prosecution said he made her removed her panties, bent her down and had sex with her from behind.

He said after the act, Karim gave the girl GH¢2.00 and left the place, unknown to him some artisans opposite the gutter were watching them.

The prosecution said they then caused the arrest of Karim, beat him up and later handed him over to the school’s authorities together with the victim.

Prosecution said a formal complaint was lodged with the Domestic Violence and Victim’s Support Unit of the Ghana Police Service.

He said the victim was issued with a medical report form for the school authorities to send her to the hospital for examination, treatment and report.
