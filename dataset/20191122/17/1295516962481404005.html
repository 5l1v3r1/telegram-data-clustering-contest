Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker Poster Showcases the Light and Dark Sides

22 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Dan Conner

The new Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker international poster from Germany sees both the light and dark sides of the Force on display, with Rey in the center to divide the image while blue and red tints either side.

With droids to her left and the First Order to her right, Rey stands confidently as she hoists the former Skywalker lightsaber high above her head. To her left is Chewbacca, with Finn, still with a blue tint, to her right, both of whom are firing crimson-red bolts -- Finn from his blaster and Chewie from his iconic bowcaster.

Meanwhile, an unmasked Kylo Ren appears just beneath Rey, with his reforged helmet above. Notably, he's abutted by both the red and the blue tints, possibly signaling hope for those banking on a Ben Solo redemption.

Directed and co-written by J.J. Abrams, Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker stars Daisy Ridley, Adam Driver, John Boyega, Oscar Isaac, Lupita Nyong’o, Domhnall Gleeson, Kelly Marie Tran, Joonas Suotamo, Billie Lourd, Keri Russell, Anthony Daniels, Mark Hamill, Billy Dee Williams, and Carrie Fisher, with Naomi Ackie and Richard E. Grant. The film arrives on Dec. 20.
