To qualify for the promotion, all you have to do is bring along a ticket to any National Lottery game - such as Lotto, Euromillions, Thunderball, Hot Picks, Set for Life, Instant Win Games and Scratchcards.

This unique opportunity is available to anyone who brings along a valid National Lottery ticket but be quick as spaces are limited.
