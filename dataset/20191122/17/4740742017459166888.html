Bristol singer Yola nominated for FOUR Grammy Awards including Best New Artist

She's one of the most nominated UK artists and goes up against the likes of Lizzo and Billie Eilish

22 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Robin Murray

A Bristol musician has been nominated for four gongs at one of the music industry's most prestigious award ceremonies.

The Grammys was launched 60 years ago and honours the very best in music across the world, recognising the artists who have had standout years.

Nominees for the 2020 Grammys were revealed on Wednesday (November 20) and include the likes of Lizzo, Billy Eilish, The 1975 and Ariana Grande.

Also among the potential winners at the coveted award ceremony is Bristol-born singer Yola - who is in the running to walk away with a staggering four awards.

About Yola

Aged 35, Yola (real name Yola Carter) has had an on-off career in music for over a decade.

Even if you’ve never heard the name, you may well have heard the voice, as Yola’s powerhouse vocals - and songwriting talent - have featured on tracks from the likes of Iggy Azalea, Massive Attack, Katy Perry, Will Young and the Chemical Brothers, among others.

After years in London Yola moved back to Bristol following an eviction, which left her temporarily living on the streets of the capital.

Incredibly, it was a house fire in 2015 that put her solo career back on track, she told Bristol Live's Matthew Jenkins.

It led her to pick up the guitar again and her debut album, Walk Through Fire, was then recorded in Nashville with acclaimed producer Dan Auerbach of The Black Keys.

The LP, which features break-up songs and sun-soaked tales of new opportunities which already sound like they’ve been around for years, was released in February and widely praised.

What has she been nominated for?

Yola has been shortlisted in the Best New Artist category alongside the likes of Billie Eislish, Lizzo, Maggie Rogers and Rosalia.

She has also been nominated for Best American Roots Performance for Faraway Look, which is awarded for performances in the style of any of the subgenres encompassed in the American Roots Music field including Americana, bluegrass, blues, folk or regional roots.

Her other nominations include Best Americana Album for the aforementioned Walk Through Fire and Best Americana Roots Song.

Speaking about the nominations, Yola said: "To be nominated for four awards in the 10 month of my debut year is beyond a dream come true. 

"Thank you to my incredible team and everyone who has supported and believed in me along the way. 

"Great to see such incredible talent nominated alongside me.  I’m truly honoured to be in the category with every artist nominated - see you in January!"

The 62nd annual Grammy Awards will take place in Los Angeles on Sunday, January 26, 2020, at the Staples Center in Los Angeles.
