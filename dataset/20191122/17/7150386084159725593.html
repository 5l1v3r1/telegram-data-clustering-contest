Uddhav Unanimous Choice For Maharashtra CM, Says Pawar; Final Deal to be Sealed Today

The proposed 'Maha Vikas Aghadi' comprising the Sena-NCP-Congress is likely to hold a press conference on Saturday where the final details will be shared with the media. 

22 Nov 2019, 17:56

New Delhi: ‘Uddhav Thackeray has agreed to be the chief minister of Maharashtra’, said Rajya Sabha MP Sanjay Raut, hours after Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) supremo Sharad Pawar asserted that the Sena president was unanimous choice of ‘Maha Vikas Aghadi’ (Sena-NCP-Congress) for the Chief Minister’s post.

“A consensus has evolved on Uddhav Thackeray’s name for the Chief Minister of a Shiv Sena-Nationalist Congress Party-Congress alliance government in Maharashtra”,  Pawar said after the two-hour long joint meeting ‘Maha Vikas Aghadi’ (Sena-NCP-Congress) at the National Science Centre in Mumbai on Friday.
