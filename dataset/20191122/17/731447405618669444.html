Nicola Cruz announces 2020 US tour, releases remix album

The Ecuadorian artist will hit the road in the states next May.

22 Nov 2019, 17:36 by Andrew Ryce

Nicola Cruz is going on tour in the US next year.

The Ecuadorian artist start the tour off at Avant Gardner in New York on May 1st, hitting the Southwest US, Denver, Boston, DC and Chicago after that. (See the full list of dates below.)

Today marks the release of Siku Reworks, an album of remixes of tracks taken from Siku, his second album, which came out earlier this year on ZZK Records.

Listen to the Hermetics remix of "Obsidiana."
