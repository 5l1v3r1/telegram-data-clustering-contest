Pedestrian seriously injured in Exeter crash

Seven police and ambulance vehicles were at the scene in Cowley Bridge Road

22 Nov 2019, 17:28 by Colleen Smith

A pedestrian has been seriously injured in a collision with a car on Cowley Bridge Road in Exeter on Friday afternoon.

Police have now confirmed that the pedestrian is seriously injured . Seven police and ambulance vehicles were called to the scene of the crash near the Esso petrol station forecourt at 3.20pm on Friday afternoon.

The road was initially closed in both directions causing long tailbacks between Exeter and the Crediton road.

Police remained at the scene on the petrol station forecourt after the road reopened.

A police spokesman said: "Police were notified at around 3.20pm to reports of a collision between a pedestrian and a vehicle on Cowley Bridge Road in Exeter.

"A road closure was put in place.

"The pedestrian has received serious injuries."
