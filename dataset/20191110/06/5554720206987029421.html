Priyanka Gandhi Vadra’s special Eid Milad-Un-Nabi greetings for Kashmiri ‘brothers’ and ‘sisters’

10 Nov 2019, 06:47 by JKR Staff

Congress General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi Vadra on Sunday wished Muslims on the occasion of Eid Milad-Un-Nabi -the birth anniversary of Prophet Mohammad (peace be upon him). While wishing Muslims on the birth anniversary of their Prophet, Priyanka also made a special mention of Kashmiris, who’ve been forced to live under a complete lockdown since 5 August this year.

Priyanka wrote, “Congratulations on the occasion of Eid Milad-Un-Nabi to all of you, especially to our brothers and sisters in Jammu and Kashmir, who’ve been facing difficulties and hardship for the last four months.”
