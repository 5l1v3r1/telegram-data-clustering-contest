Three protesters shot dead in southern Iraq by security forces: police, medical sources

10 Nov 2019, 21:05

BAGHDAD (Reuters) - Security forces opened fire on protesters in the southern Iraqi city of Nassiriya on Sunday, killing at least three people, police and medics said.

Protesters had gathered on a bridge in the city, and security forces used live ammunition to disperse them, the sources said. More than 100 others were wounded in clashes in the city, they added.
