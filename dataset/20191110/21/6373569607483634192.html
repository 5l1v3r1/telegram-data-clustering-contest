Primark is selling matching Christmas pyjamas for the family and the dog

People are raving about them

10 Nov 2019, 21:17 by Charlotte Becquart

People are going crazy for Primark's new range of Christmas matching pyjamas.

The popular retailer has launched sets for the whole family... including the dog.

The PJs are grey and white with a polar bear on it. They read 'Mama Bear’, ‘Papa Bear’ or ‘Little Bear’.

There is also a dog coat with fuzzy ears.

The adult PJs cost £11, the child set costs £8 and the dog coat costs £7.

People have been raving about them on social media.

There have been many posts on Facebook about them including one on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, which got more than 2,000 comments and likes but has now been deleted.
