10 Nov 2019, 21:04

RIYADH: King Salman received Sheikh Sabah Khalid Al-Hamad Al-Sabah, Kuwait’s deputy prime minister and foreign minister, on Sunday, who conveyed the greetings of the Kuwaiti emir.

During the meeting in Riyadh, the king also conveyed his greetings to Kuwaiti Emir Sheikh Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah.

The meeting was attended by Saudi State Minister Prince Turki bin Mohammed bin Fahd, Foreign Minister Prince Faisal bin Farhan, State Minister Dr. Musaed bin Mohammed Al-Aiban, Kuwaiti Ambassador to Saudi Arabia Sheikh Ali Al-Khaled Al-Jaber Al-Sabah and a number of officials.
