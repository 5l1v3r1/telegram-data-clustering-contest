Atatiana Jefferson shooting: Victim's father dies of heart attack, family says

10 Nov 2019, 21:23 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

DALLAS - The father of a woman fatally shot in her Texas home last month by a Fort Worth police officer died Saturday night, family members said.

Marquis Jefferson died around 6:30 p.m. Saturday at a Dallas hospital, family spokesman Bruce Carter told The Dallas Morning News. He was 59.
