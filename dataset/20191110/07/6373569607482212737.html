Plymouth Argyle: FA Cup second round draw details, ball numbers, TV coverage and Ryan Lowe comment

The Pilgrims won 1-0 away to Bolton Wanderers in the first round on Saturday

10 Nov 2019, 07:47 by Chris Errington

Plymouth Argyle will find out their FA Cup second round opponents when the draw takes place on Monday night.

It will be televised live on BBC2 between 7.00-7.30pm and the Pilgrims will be in it after their 1-0 win at Bolton Wanderers on Saturday.

The draw will be conducted at Oaklands Park, the home of Chichester City, the lowest-ranked club left in the competition by Dion Dublin and Mark Bright.

The second round is scheduled for the weekend of November 30, December 1, with winning clubs receiving £54,000 from The FA prize fund.

Argyle manager Ryan Lowe said: "You would always like a home draw because you think it's an advantage, but sometimes it's not.

"Sometimes it's a disadvantage. Whatever comes our way we will take it. We are just happy to be in the hat for the next round."

There will be 20 ties in the second round, which is the stage of the competition which Argyle have lost in for the last two seasons.

The Pilgrims have been beaten by Bradford City (away, 3-1) in 2017 and Oxford United (home, 2-1) in 2018.

The last time Argyle reached round three was in 2016 when they lost 1-0 to Liverpool in a replay at Home Park after holding out for a 0-0 draw at Anfield.
