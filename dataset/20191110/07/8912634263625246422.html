Al-Rajhi improvises to seal dramatic victory in AlUla-Neom Cross-Country Rally

10 Nov 2019, 07:17

DUBAI: Saturday night saw the UK’s Olajide William Olatunji, known as KSI, win out over American entertainer Logan Paul in a much-anticipated boxing match in Los Angeles’ Staples Center.

After months of trash talk and media hype, England’s KSI won the battle to see who is the king of social media with a split decision six-round victory over Paul.

The re-match — last year’s bout ended in a draw — ended with the famously antagonistic pair declaring respect for each other, before KSI quashed hopes that he would go for a trilogy of matches, saying “it is done. I’m on to the next thing.”
