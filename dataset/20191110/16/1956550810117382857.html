Christmas in Dubai 2019: Madinat Jumeirah massive Christmas market is back

Get ready for 16 days of festive fun

10 Nov 2019, 16:18 by Reema Rahman

With Christmas just 45 days away – not that we’re counting – we're planning our Christmas shopping. And in good news, Madinat Jumeirah's popular festive market is back for 2019, and needless to say, we’re excited.

The market will run from Friday December 13 until Saturday December 28 – that’s 16 jam-packed days of festive fun.

While the little ones are having fun taking part in a snow fight zone or bungee jumping, big kids can spend the afternoon chomping away on festive food and washing it all back with festive drinks.

There’s also going to be a live band, daily visits form Santa between 5pm and 7pm, gingerbread house decorating, abra tours with the big man himself and cookie decorating sessions.
