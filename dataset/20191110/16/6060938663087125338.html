Ajay Devgn's 'Maidaan' to release on 27th November, 2020

10 Nov 2019, 16:49 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ajay Devgn's hugely awaited film 'Maidaan' which is based on the Golden years of Indian Football is all set to release on 27th November 2020.

The film also stars National award winner Keerthy Suresh opposite Ajay Devgn. Ajay plays the role of Syed Abdul Rahim, under whose leadership, the Indian football team won the Asian games in 1951 and 1962. He is also regarded as the architect of modern Indian football.

The shooting of the film is underway and will finish by March 2020. The actor is currently shooting for the third schedule of the film in Kolkata. As the film is VFX heavy, the makers want to ensure that the film has top quality VFX. The VFX supervisor has been flown in from LA for this purpose.

Produced by Production company, Boney Kapoor, Akash Chawla and Arunava Joy Sengupta, the film's screenplay and dialogues are written by Saiwyn Quadros and Ritesh Shah respectively.
