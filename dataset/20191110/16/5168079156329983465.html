Several parts of Oman receive rainfall

10 Nov 2019, 16:36 by Times News Service

Muscat: Several parts of the Sultanate are witnessing rainfall, the Oman News Agency has said. In a statement published online, ONA said, “Rainfall in a number of the governorates of Muscat, Musandam, Buraimi, Dakhiliyah, South Al Batinah and North Al Batinah ranging from light to medium and heavy rains which led to flowing water in a number of valleys.”
