'Costing us now' - Aston Villa fans' message to Dean Smith after defeat to Wolves

Aston Villa latest | Villa supporters give their thoughts on the 2-1 defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers at Molineux, as both sides head into the third international break of the season

10 Nov 2019, 16:41 by Josh Murray

Aston Villa remained three points above the Premier League relegation zone after losing 2-1 to Midlands rivals Wolverhampton Wanderers.

Ruben Neves fired the hosts into a first-half lead, after Villa goalkeeper Jed Steer - who replaced the injured Tom Heaton in the starting XI - was

forced off early on.

Raul Jimenez then tucked home a low ball from ex-Villan Adama Traore late on to all but seal the points, despite Trezeguet's stoppage-time response.

The results sends Dean Smith's men into the international break on 11 points from 12 games, three ahead of 18th-placed Watford and Southampton in 19th.

 

Wolves, meanwhile, move up to eighth - although their points tally is only five better than Villa's at this stage.
