UAE residents can go to Motiongate Dubai for Dhs49 this weekend

Celebrate UAE’s Year of Tolerance at the massive theme park

10 Nov 2019, 16:28 by Darragh Murphy

If there was ever a reason to head to Motiongate Dubai, the huge movie-themed park is giving UAE residents of Dubai and good one this weekend.

To celebrate the UAE’s Year of Tolerance, Motiongate Dubai is offering all UAE residents a very special price of entry – all for Dhs49.

Taking place from Thursday November 14 until Saturday November 16, UAE residents will get full access to the movie-themed rides, live shows and much more at an affordable price.

What kind of attractions? Motiongate has more than 20 attractions inside the indoor, air-conditioned park including Madagascar Mad Pursuit and Dragon Gliders.

That’s not all, as the park is spread across five zones, with each boasting thrilling rollercoasters, interactive zones, threatre shows, roaming entertainment and live performances. Not to shabby for Dhs49.

The special initiative aims to "bring people from all walks of life and diverse cultures under one roof", says the team.
