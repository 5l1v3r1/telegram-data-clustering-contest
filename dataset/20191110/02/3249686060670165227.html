(G)I-DLE's 'Lion' hits 10 million views on YouTube

10 Nov 2019, 02:40 by jennywill

(G)I-DLE's "Lion" has hit 10 million views!

The song was revealed at midnight KST on the 4th as the group's 'Queendom' final stage song. The MV hit over 10 million views before the week was over, at around noon on the 9th. When the MV hits 20 million views, they'll have a special video out.
