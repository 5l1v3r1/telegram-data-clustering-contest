Samsung Black Friday 2019 TV Deals: Full Range Of Massive Savings Revealed

10 Nov 2019 by John Archer

A few days ago Forbes colleague Gordon Kelly introduced us to the first four of Samsung’s Black Friday TV deals. Now, though, Samsung has unveiled its full roster of Black Friday TV price reductions - and there are some truly massive savings to be had on sets right across the brand’s latest range. Including its excellent 8K and 4K QLED (Quantum Dot) models, and its ‘high design’ Frame sets.What’s more, you don’t have to wait until Black Friday itself (November 29) to take advantage of Samsung’s Black Friday deals; they’ve all just gone live right now via Samsung’s US website.

Here’s a full break down of every set included in the sale, including the size of the price cut each model is getting.

This brightness is partnered, though, with nearly 480 dimming zones, to ensure that the screen can also deliver outstanding black levels for LCD screen technology. These designer 4K TVs are intended to look like works of art when you’re not using them as TVs.
