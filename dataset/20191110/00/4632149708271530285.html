FIVESIXZERO music summit: Know these artists

10 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

So you can’t get enough of commercial music? Then block your calendar for the ‘Main Stage’ of the upcoming FIVESIXZERO music summit and fest

Nucleya

If you are inclined towards Indian indie music, you would have heard of him or his work. Udyan Sagar, known as Nucleya on stage, is a bass heavy electronic artist from New Delhi. Having started his career by performing at various music venues across the country, including at the Bacardi Weekender in 2011, he subsequently performed around the world – at Glastonbury, Edinburgh Fringe Festival, Lille 3000 and the Electron Festival, to name a few.
After leaving Bandish Projekt in 1998, which he had cofounded, Nuclyea ventured on his own. Since then, he’s opened for David Guetta at the Eristoff Invasion Festival in 2012 and forayed into films with the 2016 Bollywood hit Kapoor & Sons. Thereafter, he made his acting debut in a documentary titled Ride to the Roots – Nucleya (2017), where he played himself and chronicled his rise to success. And so, this music heavyweight is ready to floor you at the ‘Main Stage’ of FiveSixZero concert as a headliner.

Justin Oh

K-pop fans, you have a reason to rejoice. One of South Korea’s top EDM artists and the maker of bass-fueled hit Jekyll & Hyde, Justin Oh is on the roster too. He has performed at major festivals worldwide including Ultra Miami, Ultra Korea, Ultra Japan, Tomorrowland, Ultra Europe, and DWP, and has been hailed by Mixmag and Beatport as well. Since then, he has been on a phenomenal rise with multiple releases on Monstercat, amassing millions of streams and views. His recent ‘City of Oz’ performance at the Timeout Festival was recognised as the best performance and visual show.


Tank

Tank has worked with some of the world-renowned DJs and is fast making a name with his mixes. He is the resident DJ of World Top 100 Club MYST.


Attend the fest at The Lalit Ashok, Kumarakrupa Road, from November 22-24, tickets on bookmyshow.com, get details on fivesixzero.in
