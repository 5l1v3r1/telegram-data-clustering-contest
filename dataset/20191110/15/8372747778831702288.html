Missing Clark Atlanta University student found dead, 2 arrested

10 Nov 2019, 15:39 by WFTX Digital Team

Two people have been arrested in the murder of a Clark Atlanta University student in Atlanta. Twenty-one year old Alexis Crawford disappeared days ago. Her roommate, Jordyn Jones, and Jordyn's boyfriend Barron Brantley are facing charges.

The exact motive remains under investigation. Police are considering the sexual assault allegations she made against her roommate's boyfriend.
