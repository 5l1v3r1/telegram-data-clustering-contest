Firefighters knock out 2-alarm blaze at Cambridge duplex

10 Nov 2019, 15:16 by EndPlay

CAMBRIDGE, Mass. - Cambridge firefighters responded to a two-alarm fire at 151 Cambridge Street on Sunday morning.

Officials tweeted reports of smoke at the three-story, wooden-framed duplex.

Related Headlines

Cambridge Police tweeted that the fire has been knocked out, but Cambridge Street remains closed from First to Third Street until units clear the scene.

They said there are no known injuries.
