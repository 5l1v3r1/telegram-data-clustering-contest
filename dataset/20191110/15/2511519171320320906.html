Manchester United vs Brighton, player ratings: Dan James, Anthony Martial and Marcus Rashford star in victory

10 Nov 2019, 15:57 by Lawrence Ostlere

Manchester United proved too good for Brighton as they overcame the visitors with a 3-1 win at Old Trafford.

Andreas Pereira, a Davy Propper own goal and a Marcus Rashford finish ensured United's victory.

Brighton did momentarily hit back through a Lewis Dunk header, but Rashford's reply moments later snuffed out any hope of a late comeback.

Brighton had positive moments but ultimately couldn't cope with United's pace of Rashford, Anthony Martial and Dan James up front.
