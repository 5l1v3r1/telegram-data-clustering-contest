Athletic cruise to a 2-1 victory over Levante at San Mamés

Following this result, Athletic secured their 4th win in this campaign

10 Nov 2019, 15:14 by SPORT.es

Levante on Sunday lost to Athletic on an away defeat at San Mamés. Athletic faced the encounter with a desire to add more points to their locker after a 0-0 draw against Villarreal while Levante won in their last two matches of the competition against Barcelona at home stadium, and Real Sociedad away, by 3-1 and 1-2 respectively. At the moment, Athletic and Levante occupy position 6th (20 points) and 12th (17 points) respectively after playing a total of 13 matches.

An impressive opening from Levante, with a goal from Sergio Postigo just before the end of the 45 minutes, thereby seeing the first half to a 0-1.

Athletic took the lead in the second half, thanks to a goal from Iker Muniain 57 minutes in the game. Later, Athletic scored once more and turned the match 2-1 thanks to a goal from Ander Capa in the 88th minute just before the end of the second half, finalising the game with a 2-1.

At the end of the match, both teams made 3 substitutions each. For Athletic Ibai Gómez, Mikel San José and Asier Villalibre came on for Iñigo Córdoba, Unai López and Inaki Williams, while Levante brought on Hernani, Nikola Vukcevic and Roger Martí to replace Borja Mayoral, Gonzalo Melero and José Luis Morales.

The referee booked Dani García, Raúl García and Ander Capa from Athletic, while for Levante Enis Bardhi and Rubén Vezo saw a yellow card as well.

Athletic will be playing against Osasuna at away stadium, while Levante will face Mallorca at home stadium.
