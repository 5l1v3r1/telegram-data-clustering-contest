'Fearing the worst' - Aston Villa fans in meltdown after nightmare start to Wolves match

Wolverhampton Wanderers v Aston Villa live coverage | The claret and blue faithful have been having their say on social media after a half to forget at Molineux

10 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans were fearing the worst after a dismal first half against Wolverhampton Wanderers this afternoon.

Villa trailed 1-0 at half-time, Ruben Neves' stunning strike four minutes before the interval the difference after an opening 45 to forget for Dean Smith's side.

Goalkeeper Jed Steer, starting his first Premier League game of the season , had to come off injured after eight minutes and Matt Targett also left the match in injury time for the same reason.

Villa's injury worries have mounted with Jack Grealish, first choice 'keeper Tom Heaton and Bjorn Engels all missing the trip to Molineux.

 

So it's no surprise to see Villa fans are not holding out much hope for their side making a comeback at their West Midlands rivals.
