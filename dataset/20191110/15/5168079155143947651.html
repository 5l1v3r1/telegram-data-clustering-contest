Oman rains: Family stuck in car

10 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Times News Service

Muscat: In the middle of heavy rainfall currently being experienced at Al Buraimi, a family is reported to be stuck in their vehicle in Wadi Madha while another person is stuck in a vehicle at north Al Batinah.

In a statement, the Royal Oman Police said, "A family was reported to be stuck in a car at Wadi Madha, the situation is still being dealt with."

The Public Authority for Civil Defence and Ambulance (PACDA) said in a statement, "The Authority's Operations Center received a report on the detention of a person inside a vehicle in a wadi stream in the wilayat of Shinas, North Al Batinah Governorate."
