Boston-themed sports bar O’Learys serving up Dhs15 pints

There’s a top gents’ night deal too at this Dubai Creek bar

10 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Amy Mathieson

O’Learys Dubai Creek threw open its doors last month, bringing with it a wealth of Boston-themed memorabilia to Dubai.

And it also brought some top deals. And by top, we’re talking Dhs15 pints. What a bargain.

The huge Sippy Sundays deal is every Sunday from 6pm until 9pm, so you can round off the start of the working week in style.

There’s also a daily happy hour from 5pm until 7pm where you’ll get 40 percent off drinks, while on a Monday it’s gents’ night from 5.30pm until 9pm where men get three drinks and a bar bites platter for Dhs90.

There are also plenty of TV screens to watch all the latest live sport at this new bar at Hilton Dubai Creek. Décor is also sports related, showing Boston’s sporting history. The bar chain was founded in Gothenburg, Sweden in 1988 by Jonas Reinholdsson, who was inspired after working in Massachusetts in the 1980s.

He decided to open a Boston-themed bar and O’Learys is now global, with four in the Middle East.
