Starting XI: Leopards v CT City

10 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Soccer Laduma

Absa Premiership

Teams: Black Leopards v Cape Town City

Date: 10 November 2019

Venue: Thohoyandou Stadium

Cape Town City travel to Black Leopards on Sunday afternoon, looking to build on their big win over fellow Limpopo outfit Polokwane City.

The Citizens put an end to a nine-game winless run with a 5-3 triumph over Rise and Shine in front of their new head coach Â Jan Olde Riekerink, who watched from the stands on Wednesday night.

Kermit Erasmus will be looking to continue his form in front of goal after the striker bagged a brace on Wednesday to take his tally to seven goals in 10 matches.

City come up against a Leopards outfit who have not featured since 25 October when they drew 2-2 with SuperSport United on home soil.

Leopards have only registered two wins this season, while Luc Eymael is yet taste victory since taking charge in the dug-out as the Belgian was in the stands for the win over Bloemfonein Celtic.

It was Leopards who claimed the spoils in the fixture last season, with Khomotso Masia netting the game's only goal.
