Check out a new truffle menu in the capital this month

Head to Verso as part of the Tastemasters series

10 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Time Out staff

With Abu Dhabi Culinary Season now in full flow, there's no better excuse needed to head to the capital to check out a new restaurant.

There are so many amazing events taking place and delicious dishes to try, and one of the best things happening is The Tastemasters Series.

This programme is bringing a line-up of Michelin-recognised chefs to Abu Dhabi. Next to set up residence is Italian chef Andrea Monesi.

He is serving a special truffle menu at Verso in the Grand Hyatt from Saturday November 23 to Friday November 29, so if you’re a fan of truffle, then check it out.

The culinary master comes from the gourmet trattoria Locanda di Orta, which is located in the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Sacro Monte d'Orta in Italy and won a Michelin star in 2017.

“I am excited to come to Verso and allow diners to experience the way I like truffles to be served. I hope it will be a pleasant revelation for their taste buds," he said.

We're already planning our road trip.

For more on culinary season you can visit the official website here (www.adculinaryseason.ae) and follow it on Facebook and Instagram @ADculinaryseason or follow the hashtag #ADculinaryseason.
Nov 23-29 noon-11pm. Grand Hyatt Abu Dhabi Hotel & Residences Emirates Pearl, West Corniche (02 510 1234).
