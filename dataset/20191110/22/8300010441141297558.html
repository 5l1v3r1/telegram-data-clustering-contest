3.5 earthquake east of Palm Springs rattles desert communities

10 Nov 2019, 22:55 by ABC7.com staff

PALM SPRINGS, Calif. (KABC) -- A 3.5 magnitude earthquake rattled desert communities east of Palm Springs on Sunday.

The quake hit at 2:07 p.m. about 10 miles north of Indio at a depth of 5 miles, according to the USGS.

Shaking could be felt near Palm Springs, Desert Hot Springs and Joshua Tree National Park.

No significant damage was reported.
