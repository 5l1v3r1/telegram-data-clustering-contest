Spanish voter turnout at 56.8% at 6 p.m., down from April election

10 Nov 2019, 17:17

MADRID (Reuters) - Turnout in Spain’s repeat parliamentary election stood at 56.8% at 6 p.m. (1700 GMT) on Sunday, according to the Interior Ministry, a drop from the 60.7% recorded at the same time during the previous election in April.
