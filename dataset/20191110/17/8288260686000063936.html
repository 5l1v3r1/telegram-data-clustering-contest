'Only he can' - Nuno heaps praise on two Wolves stars after Aston Villa win

Wolverhampton Wanderers 2-1 Aston Villa reaction | Nuno Espirito Santo has his say on the Premier League win at Molineux on Sunday afternoon as his players eased to three points

10 Nov 2019, 17:12 by Alex Dicken, https://www.facebook.com/alexdickenjournalist/?fref=ts

Ruben Neves and Adama Traore have been praised for their decisive displays against Aston Villa.

Midfielder Neves gave Wolves the lead at Molineux with a wonderfully taken strike from the edge of the box, while Adama Traore had a hand in both goals.

Traore, who has been called up to Spain’s senior team for the first time, won the free-kick which led to Neves’ goal before carrying the ball 70 yards to tee up Raul Jimenez for the clincher .

And Wolves boss Nuno Espirito Santo heaped praise on the pair following the 2-1 victory over Villa .

On Neves, he said: “He played really well, really well. Defensively he managed the ball with good passing between Joao and him - really, really good.”

 

Joao Moutinho cut the ball back perfectly for Neves’ goal in a routine straight off the training ground. “We work on a lot of things,” added Nuno. “But they pass it good and finish it in the right way. The credit is for them. It’s the decision of Joao - he has the knowledge to do it.”

Traore, playing against his former club, was excellent on the right of Wolves’ front three and completed a staggering 10 of the 11 dribbles he attempted.

The winger’s assist for Jimenez takes his tally to 10 goal contributions (two goals, eight assists) for the season.

“He’s playing very well,” Nuno said. “He’s sticking to his tasks and helping the team. He’s unique, he gives you these moments that only he can produce.”
