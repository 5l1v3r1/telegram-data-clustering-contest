'Quality' - Aston Villa fans pick out one positive from Wolves defeat

Wolverhampton Wanderers 2-1 Aston Villa reaction | Claret and blue faithful have picked out this positive on a poor day overall for Dean Smith's side at Molineux

10 Nov 2019, 17:56 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans have been reacting to Ezri Konsa's tweet after his debut today.

The £12 million summer signing from Brentford made his first Premier League start in the 2-1 defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers at Molineux, Villa 's third straight loss in the Premier League.

He partnered Tyrone Mings at centre-back, but wasn't able to help his side get a positive result in the West Midlands derby.

Villa's cause wasn't helped by injuries , with first choice goalkeeper Tom Heaton and captain Jack Grealish also out of the clash.

 

So Konsa was handed a debut in tricky circumstances and that told with Ruben Neves and Raul Jimenez on the scoresheet for Nuno Espirito Santo's side, with Trezeguet netting a consolation in injury time.

Konsa took to social media after the match, tweeting: "Disappointed with the defeat this afternoon but proud to make my full Premier League debut.

"Thanks for all the support today... we go again after the international break."
