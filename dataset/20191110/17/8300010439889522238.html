Bear cub transported for medical care after being struck by vehicle in Claremont

10 Nov 2019, 17:39 by ABC7.com staff

CLAREMONT, Calif. (KABC) -- A bear cub was injured after being struck by a car in Claremont.

Officials said they took the little one in overnight for medical attention.

The cub was spotted wandering around in a neighborhood about two miles away from the mountains.

The car had major front end damage but the driver was not injured.
