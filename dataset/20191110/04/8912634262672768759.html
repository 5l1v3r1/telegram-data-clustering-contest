SABB posts net profit of SR1,913m for 9 months

10 Nov 2019, 04:04

LuLu Hypermarket has signed an agreement with the National Center for Palms and Dates (NCPD) to promote Saudi-graded dates across its stores in the Kingdom.

The MoU was signed by Dr. Muhammed Al-Nowiran, CEO of NCPD, and Shehim Muhammed, director of LuLu Hypermarket Saudi Arabia, during the 38th Saudi Agricultural Exhibition.
