Chiefs' Big Win, Sundowns Loss - 12 Pictures Of Absa Prem Action

10 Nov 2019, 20:16 by Soccer Laduma

With the Soweto derby headlining this weekend’s fixtures, Soccer Laduma takes a look at 12 pictures from all the Absa Premiership action.

Bidvest Wits opened the league action on Friday with a 1-0 loss to SuperSport United, with Head Coach Gavin Hunt’s side left with four games to catch up.

On the other Side, Maritzburg United extended their unbeaten run with 2-2 draw against Bloemfontein Celtic.

On Saturday, Kaizer Chiefs secured a 3-2 win over Orlando Pirates at the FNB Stadium, while Stellenbosch FC played to a 0-0 draw against Highlands Park, Baroka beat Polokwane City 1-0 and Chippa United securing their first win of the season over AmaZulu FC.
