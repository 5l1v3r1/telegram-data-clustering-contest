Cheap terrace house in Selly Oak up for sale for just £5,000

This three bedroomed terrace is on the market from just £5,000 at an auction taking place at Aston Villa football club

10 Nov 2019, 05:30 by Cathrina Hulse-BM

If you're a house-hunter looking for a great doer-upper for your cash then take a look at this three-bed mid terrace located in the popular student area of Selly Oak.

The guide price is £5,000 for the bargain home in Dunstall Grove which is being advertised on property website Rightmove and is set to be sold in the next Bond Wolf Auction at Aston Villa Football Club on December 11.

Other terraced houses in the road have sold for up to £112,000.

For your money, you'll get two reception rooms, WC and wet room and kitchen all on the ground floor.

 

Upstairs there are three bedrooms while the property benefits from a gas fired central heating system and partial double glazing.

The house stands behind a driveway and garden and is offered with vacant possession.

Reception room one

In the reception room one, you will find a rather room complete with patterned carpet, net curtains and armchair, the room is ripe for redecoration.

There are also wooden built-in cupboards.

There's plenty of potential in a second reception room which boasts two recessed alcoves with uplighting.

 

Overlooking the garden, the kitchen at Dunstall Drive is a decent size and offers plenty of opportunity to update.

It presently has a cooker and small table with cream work surfaces. The floor is covered with a terracotta lino.

Bedroom one

If buyers can see past the clutter in this bedroom, there's lots of potential to modernise.

Bedroom two

Best ignore the gaudy carpet though!

Garden

At first glance the garden appears desperately wild and overgrown but the groundwork has been laid to produce a really good sized garden - perfect for a family.

There is plenty of foliage and mature trees with a sturdy fence separating neighbours.
