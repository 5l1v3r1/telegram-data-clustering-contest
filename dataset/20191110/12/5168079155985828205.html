Oman's State Council set to hold first session this month

10 Nov 2019, 12:03 by Times News Service

Muscat: The State Council will hold its first annual session on November 14, following a directive from His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said.

A statement issued by Oman News Agency said the State Council will hold its first session of seventh term (2019-2023).

“ Upon His Majesty the Sultan's orders, the State Council will start its seventh term (2019-2023) and will hold its first session for its first annual session on Thursday, 14 November 2019.”
