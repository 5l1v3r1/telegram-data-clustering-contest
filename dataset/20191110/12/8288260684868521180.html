Watch brazen robber attempt to wrestle cash box off guard outside Aldi in Great Barr

The suspect - who is wearing a hi-vis jacket and a mask to hide his identity - was caught on camera as he tried to steal a cash box outside the supermarket in Birmingham Road, Great Barr

10 Nov 2019, 12:58 by Kelly Ashmore, https://www.facebook.com/dudleybclive/

A bungling robber is being hunted following a brazen attempt to snatch a cash box outside Aldi.

The botched robbery took place outside the busy budget supermarket in Birmingham Road, Great Barr, on Monday, November 4.

CCTV footage shows the suspect - who is wearing a hi-vis jacket and a mask to hide his identity - running up to the security driver from behind. The would-be crook can then be seen making a grab for the container as a struggle ensues.

Luckily the guard was unhurt as he fought off his assailant, who fled the scene empty handed in a silver Ford Mondeo.

  West Midlands Police has now released the footage of the incident - which can be viewed above - and appealed for anyone with information to come forward.

A spokesman for the force said: "Do you recognise this man?

"We want to speak to him after an attempted cash in transit robbery outside an Aldi store in Birmingham Road, Great Barr, at 10am on Monday (November 4).

 

"The security van driver was walking to his van when a man tried to grab the cashbox out of this hand.

"The driver managed to fight him off and the man left empty-handed in a silver Ford Mondeo."

If you recognise him, or have any information that may assist the investigation, contact police via Live Chat at  west-midlands.police.uk  between 8am and midnight, quoting crime reference number 20SW/260740F/19.

 

Alternatively people can call 101 anytime or contact Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
