Kelly Brook loves her new curvy body after being 'skinny and miserable' for years

The telly personality, 39, admitted 'my body likes being curvy' after she spent her younger years being her thinnest ever due to stressful relationships and work

10 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Brogan-Leigh Hurst

Kelly Brook has admitted she loves her new curves as she's finally happy with her body following years of being "skinny and miserable".

The presenter, 39, has finally found confidence in loving herself after a huge "wake-up call" two years ago at a red carpet event.

The telly personality had spent some time out of the spotlight and turned to a rather quiet life in the countryside.

She made her debut appearance when she stepped in front of the cameras again but was left shocked as photographers were shouting at her to "breathe in".

And although it was a rude gesture, Kelly wasn't phased and took it as her motivation to shed some weight.

She told The Sun : "Well, I'm glad they told me! They actually thought they were doing me a favour, but yeah, it was a bit of a shock.

"But they knew me as skinny, and all of a sudden I'd turned up with bigger boobs, a bigger bum, bigger arms - just a lot bigger, so I think it was a shock for them as well!"

 

The model said she was aware she had crept up to a size 16 as she enjoyed regular hearty pub lunches and fulfillment in her personal life while living in Kent.

Brook said it "dawned on me" and thought she had do something about her weight even though she felt the same.

And since then, she has made several lifestyle changes, lost two stone and she is going to slim down to a size 12.

The gradual process has seen Kelly running up to three times a week and following a SlimFast eating plan to achieve her "Size Sexy" as she approaches her 40th birthday later this month.

 

The star admitted she would never want to go back to her tiniest waistline because they were some of her "saddest" years.

Now Kelly is in love with her body.

She added: "My body likes being curvy. It would be really difficult for me to be a skinny girl cos it’s not my shape or who I am and I’m not going to aspire to be something that’s unattainable."

Kelly confessed relationships were hard because she was constantly travelling, saying she kept the weight off due to the anxiety and stress of always being on the go.

As time has moved on, the beauty is happy with her beau Jeremy Parisi.
