Cole Sprouse and Lili Reinhart Share Make Out Shot to Instagram

10 Nov 2019, 18:08 by Erika Harwood

Cole Sprouse and Lili Reinhart are definitely still together, and they posted the make-out photobooth shot to prove it. Reinhart posted some photobooth photos to Instagram showing her cuddling up with her boyfriend and Riverdale co-star writing, “The sequel.”

After a rumored split this summer, the couple is very much back on and appears to be better than ever.

“Cole and Lili are in a great place right now,” a source told E! News last month. “The two of them are very happy together. They’re getting along so well both on and off the set. They’re often openly affectionate and loving with one another.”

Sprouse and Reinhart took to Instagram to deny the rumors of a split, posting their joint W Magazine cover.

“BREAKING: A reliable source has confirmed that none of you know shit,” Reinhart wrote.

Sprouse echoed the sentiment writing, “UNPRECEDENTED: Cole Sprouse and Lili Reinhart consume the flesh of ‘reliable sources’ to fuel their bacchanalian sex cult.”

Despite this, a source told E! News that the couple briefly split as filming picked back up on Riverdale.

“Lili and Cole broke-up earlier this summer. The two are not living together this season,” a source shared this summer, adding that they were “intentionally keeping their distance from each other.”

“Right now, it's unclear where things stand,” the insider added. “But it seems [like] they could be heading back in the direction of getting together.”

Apparently that was very much correct.
