Here's how much it rained across Oman on Sunday

10 Nov 2019, 18:55 by Times News Service

Muscat: Most wilayats and governorates of the Sultanate were exposed to rainfall ranging between moderate and heavy on Sunday. With Oman experiencing the effects of Al Haya, a deep depression from Friday, the weather condition will continue till Monday, the Public Authority for Civil Aviation (PACA) had forecast last Thursday.

On Sunday, PACA said in a statement, “The amount of rainfall (in mm) recorded at some monitoring stations of wilayats and governorates around Oman is as follows:

Mhada 46.8, Nakhal 35.0, Bidbid 27.0, Seeq 21.4, Saham 19.4, Dibba 18.2, Liwa 15.2, Al Buraimi 14.0, Sohar 13.0, Bukha 12.0, Dhank 10.2 and Bawshar 9.4.”

Mhada in Al Buraimi Governorate recorded the highest amount of rainfall.
