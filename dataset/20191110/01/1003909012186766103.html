City seeking opinions on a dual park and cemetery in northeast Calgary

10 Nov 2019, 01:16 by Stephanie Babych

The city of Calgary is seeking public input on the development of a cemetery in the north that could include sports courts, wading pools and ziplines.

An undeveloped plot of land in the north, south of Highway 566 and east of 6th Street N.E., has been selected as the prime location for a cemetery but, in researching modern trends, the city became interested in the development of a dual-purpose facility, which would blend elements of a regional park with a cemetery.

The city launched an online survey to ask about design, usage, and opinion on the regional park and cemetery to determine how comfortable Calgarians are with the idea.
