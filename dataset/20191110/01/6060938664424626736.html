THIS video of Sara Ali Khan waving back to little fans calling her 'Sara didi' is way too adorable

10 Nov 2019, 01:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sara Ali Khan is the most loved millennial star. She has always been the centre of attraction, especially among the young audiences. Whenever she makes a public appearance her fans go frenzy and start calling her out to get clicked. She also with utmost poise greets them with her radiant smile and her signature 'namaste'.

Yesterday, also when the actress stepped out of her pilates classes along with her trainer, her little fans started calling her 'Sara didi'. Sara too in the sweetest manner waved back to her fans. Later, she stopped for a few seconds, posed for the shutterbugs and walked towards her car. Sara was seen sporting cool athleisure teamed up with a pair of white sports shoes. She had tied her hair in a single braid and also adorned a hairband.

On the work front, Sara will be next seen in 'Aaj Kal' opposite Kartik Aaryan. The film is helmed by Imtiaz Ali and is slated to release on Valentine's Day 2020. Later she will be seen in 'Coolie No.1' opposite Varun Dhawan.
