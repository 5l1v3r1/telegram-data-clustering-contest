'Housefull 4' box office collection Day 16: Akshay Kumar and Kriti Sanon multi-starrer manages to rake in another Rs 2.75 crore

10 Nov 2019, 10:22 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Farhad Samji directorial 'Housefull 4' is still managing to make an impact the at the domestic box office as the multi-starrer comic caper gives Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Bala' some competition in its third week as well.

The film which stars Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon, Kriti Kharbanda, Riteish Deshmukh, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde in the lead roles has managed to mint another Rs 2.75 crore on its third Saturday at the box office as per a report on Boxofficeindia.com.

With Rs 135.86 crores in the first week itself, the fourth instalment of the popular 'Housefull' franchise has gone on to collect Rs 48.75 crores in the second week, followed by Rs 2 crore and Rs 2.75 crore in the third week taking its total collection to a staggering figure of Rs 189.36 crores.

The film which had released on October 25 had clashed with Rajkummar Rao's 'Made In China' and Taapsee Pannu-Bhumi Pednekar's 'Saand Ki Aankh' at the box office and the reincarnation film managed to surpass their collection with ease.
