FIR lodged against ex-PM's son Ali Haider Gilani

10 Nov 2019, 19:29 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

MUZAFFARGARH (Dunya News) – A First Information Report (FIR) has been lodged against former prime minister and Pakistan Peoples’ Party (PPP) leader Yousaf Raza Gillani’s son Ali Haider Gilani.

The FIR was registered for interfering in government matters.

It is pertinent here to mention that Member Provincial Assembly from Multan Ali Haider Gilani and District Police Officer (DPO) had exchanged hot words on November 8 during PPP chairman Bilawal Bhutto Zardari’s public rally in Muzaffargarh.

Following a heated debate, the local administration registered a case against the PPP leader.
