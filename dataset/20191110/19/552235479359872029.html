'Absolutely bowfin!' Scotland confirm leaked kit as fans react with horror

The mood music around the national team makes it a challenging time to release a new strip.

10 Nov 2019, 19:44 by Jonathan McFarlane

The SFA have confirmed that the Scotland strip leaked last week IS the genuine article.

They have announced on social media that the top, shorts and socks will be available from JD Sports from tomorrow morning.

Social media Pictures emerged on Wednesday of a display of shirts in a US shop with the tops priced at $90.

The major changes unveiled by kit manufacturer Adidas include blue shorts (rather than white), a new red and white sleeve trim and a sure to be polarising electricity style jagged pattern across the top.
