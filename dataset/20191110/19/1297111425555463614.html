Campbell Tartans win sixth consecutive city title

The Campbell Tartans and LeBoldus Golden Suns celebrated RHSAA senior tier 1 volleyball championships Friday night.

10 Nov 2019, 19:19 by Regina Leader-Post

The Campbell Tartans won the Regina High Schools Athletic Association’s senior girls tier 1 volleyball title for the sixth consecutive year on Friday.

Campbell defeated the Winston Knoll Wolverines 25-6, 25-13 in the city final at Luther College High School’s Semple Gymnasium.

“I am so proud to be part of this team and can’t believe how amazing it is to be part of a senior team that has won three years in a row,” said Campbell’s Jordy McEachern, who has won city titles with the senior girls team in Grades 9, 10 and 11.

“We have a real shot at winning back-to-back provincial championships and that is so exciting.”

The Saskatchewan High Schools Athletic Association’s 5A senior girls championship tournament is set for Friday and Saturday in Weyburn.

In Friday’s senior boys tier 1 final, also at Luther, the LeBoldus Golden Suns defeated Campbell 23-25, 25-19, 16-14.

The provincial 5A boys championship tournament is slated for Friday and Saturday at Winston Knoll and Riffel.
