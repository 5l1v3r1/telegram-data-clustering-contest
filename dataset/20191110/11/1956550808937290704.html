Rove Hotels Dubai to launch huge flash sale for UAE National Day 2019

Expect 48% off rooms

10 Nov 2019, 11:09 by Darragh Murphy

Looking for plans for the 48th UAE National Day 2019? A staycation in Dubai is always a good shout.

With the public holidays revealed for the 48th UAE National Day 2019, Rove Hotels is offering a fittingly numbered discount on rooms, brunch and dining for its extra special flash sale.

From midnight on Monday December 2 until Tuesday December 3, Rove will have a 48-hour flash sale, offering 48 percent off bookings for stays between December 7 and 24.

The hotel chain’s The Daily restaurants will also be offering 48 percent off à la carte menu dishes across Dubai from Monday December 2 until Tuesday December 3. Get eating.

All set for a discounted staycation? The sale is available for Rove Hotel’s around Dubai, including Rove City Centre, Rove Healthcare City, Rove Trade Centre, Rove at the Park and Rove Dubai Marina.
