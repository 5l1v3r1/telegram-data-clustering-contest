Pic: Check out Tiger Shroff flaunting off his perfect abs

10 Nov 2019, 11:44 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Tiger Shroff is currently busy with the shooting of ‘Baaghi 3’, and amid the shoot he has treated his fans with a stunning selfie, wherein he can be seen flaunting his chiseled abs. Though his face has been hidden by a sticker, his perfectly carved abs are like a treat to the sore eyes.

Sharing the picture on his social media story, Tiger wrote, "Day 2-Baaghi 3."

Talking of ‘Baaghi 3’, the movie will once again feature Tiger Shroff and Shraddha Kapoor in the lead. Further, in this threequel of the ‘Baaghi’ franchise, Riteish Deshmukh will be seen playing the antagonist. The movie is slated to release on March 6 2020.
