Two Ex-PSL Players Reunite Abroad!  

10 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Soccer Laduma

Two players who previously plied their trades in the Premier Soccer League have reunited at a club abroad. 

After some time in the football wilderness, following a stint with Tshakhuma Tsha Madzivhandila FC in the National First Division last season, Malawian striker Atusaye Nyondo has been given an opportunity to revive his career in Ethiopia.

The 28-year-old has signed a one-year deal with Ethiopia Premier Soccer League side, Saint George SC.

Speaking to the Siya crew, the player’s representative Kenneth Yamongbe confirmed the deal for the former SuperSport United and University of Pretoria player. 

“Atusaye Nyondo has signed in Ethiopia. He has joined Ethiopia giants Saint George,” confirmed the representative.

“The club has won the Ethiopian League 29 times. Nyondo is currently in Addis Ababa. The team’s new Serbian coach (Srdan Zivojhov) likes him,” Yamongbe added.

In his new club, Nyondo has reunited with Getaneh Kebede, whom he played with at Tuks, during the Ethiopian striker’s time in Tshwane on loan from Bidvest Wits in the 2015/16 season.
