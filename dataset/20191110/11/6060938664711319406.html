'Pagalpanti' new teaser: John Abraham, Ileana D'Cruz and others offer another dose of laughter

10 Nov 2019, 11:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The makers of the upcoming comedy-drama 'Pagalpanti' starring John Abraham in the lead role have dropped yet another teaser from the film. The film has been creating a lot of anticipation and the makers are doing every bit to keep the audience engaged.

The new teaser is fully packed with laughter and gives us a glimpse into the craziness yet to be witnessed. The teaser features Saurabh Shukla, John Abraham among others giving us a good dose of laughter. Sharing the teaser, director Anees Bazmee wrote, "Beti se lekar behen tak sabko kar denge yeh pagal ;) #Pagalpanti"
