Ant McPartlin thought the public had had enough of him after his stint in rehab

Ant opened up about his struggles with alcohol and prescription medication during his new show with presenting partner Declan Donnelly

10 Nov 2019, 23:48 by Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

Ant & Dec's DNA Journey was an emotional roller coaster for fans of the Geordie duo.

One particularly tear-jerking moment saw Ant - who has battled with alcohol and prescription medication addictions - reveal his anxiety around his career path following a stint in rehab.

The presenter checked in to rehab for his addictions back in 2017 - but the 41 year old reckoned this could have been the end of the road for his telly career.

He was also fined Â£86,000 for a drink driving offence, having caused a three car collision in south London - Ant had been walking his dog with his mum, who was also in the car at the time.

"I thought the public had had enough," he said bluntly, "I didn't think I'd be allowed to do what I love anymore.

"But coming back and being on screen and love and support shown to us in overwhelming.

"I can't thank people enough."

Dec also got emotional about the past few years, saying: "It's been a tough couple of years. It's really tested the bond that we've shared since we were like 13 years old.

"I was incredibly angry at the start. So angry. Disappointed in him that he didn't ever come to me and say, 'I'm struggling, I need you.'

"I thought that if the shoe was on the other foot I would have gone to him and said, 'I'm in trouble and I can't handle this, I need your help.'

"He never came to me - and that hurt me a lot."

Later he added: "There was a point that I thought...that that was it."

Ant was very honest about his feelings of guilt, saying: "My actions affect nor only me but they affect him and his family and his loved ones."

The duo are back together once more - Ant returned to presenting duties for Britain's Got Talent: The Champions earlier this year, and the pair have both landed in Australia for the newest series of I'm A Celebrity.

Ant sat out last year's show to focus on his rehab, meaning Holly Willoughby went Down Under in his place.

While many fans wanted DNA Journey to show Ant and Dec are actually related, they were left shocked by the revelation that Dec's related to US wrestling star Dixie Carter.

Dixie flew out to Ireland to meet Dec and his pal, with Dec joking that Ant could get the boot in favour of his newfound American cousin.
