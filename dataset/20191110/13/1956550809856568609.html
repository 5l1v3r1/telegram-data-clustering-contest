You can now get unlimited pizza and pasta at Carluccio’s in the UAE

Get your fill of Italian food for less than Dhs100

10 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Colin Armstrong

Midweek can sometimes be tiring, you’re halfway through the week but the weekend still feels far away.

Luckily we keep you up to date with all the events, deals and offers so that you can make every week interesting and we’ve found another one you might like.

Every Tuesday at Carluccio’s you can enjoy unlimited pasta and pizza as part of the new Giro menu.

We know, unlimited pizza, we’re not sure a better combination of words exists in the English language.

Head along to any branch of the Italian restaurant from 6pm and you can feast on a range of pasta dishes and pizza.

The offer costs Dhs89 per person or Dhs99 per person if you’re planning on visiting the location at Eastern Mangroves.

Better still, children under the age of 11 eat for free so it’s a great chance for a mid-week family night out or a feast with a few friends.

The venue also recently launched a brand new vegan menu so if you’re following a plant-based diet you can still feast, too.

Plus who doesn’t like a good deal? We’ll see you there.
From Dhs89. Various locations including Eastern Mangroves and Dubai Marina www.carlucciosme.com
