El Salvador's President Warns Citizens of Potential Tsunami, Urges to Leave Coastal Areas

MEXICO CITY (Sputnik) -  The president of El Salvador, Nayib Bukele, on Monday urged the country's population to leave coastal areas and move to higher ground over a threat of tsunami.

11 Nov 2019, 23:57
"The entire population of the coastal zones needs to move to higher ground. The US Department of Defence has discovered a possible tsunami", Bukele wrote on Twitter.
