Woman hospitalized following rollover crash in Chelsea

11 Nov 2019, 23:33 by Manoella Macedo

CHELSEA, Mass. - A woman has been hospitalized following a two-car rollover crash in Chelsea on Monday evening.

According to police, the crash happened on Route 1 North beyond the Sixth Street ramp when one car crashed into another.

That caused the car to flip over the median wall, falling 25 feet on its roof to the southbound side of Route 1.

The victim, identified as a 43-year-old woman, was transported to the Massachusetts General Hospital, but the extent of her injuries is unknown at this time. She was conscious and alert when she was transported.

No further details have been released.

This is a developing story. Boston 25 News has a crew on the way and will report back with the latest information as soon as it becomes available.
