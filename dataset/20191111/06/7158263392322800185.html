12 Best Eyeshadow Palette Sets You Need in Your Life

11 Nov 2019, 06:55

I hate when I spend money on an eyeshadow palette and only use, like, two colors in the whole palette. It’s my ultimate makeup pet peeve. And because I know I’m not alone in this struggle, I decided to round up the 12 best eyeshadow palettes that are full of colors you’ll actually want to use—yes, even the ones with 30+ shades. And not only are the colors gorgeous, but the formulas are long lasting, ultra pigmented, and non-creasing too. Whether you’re a makeup beginner who doesn’t know how to do a smokey eye or a beauty guru who could do a cut crease in your sleep, you’re going to fall in love with at least one of the eyeshadow palettes below.
