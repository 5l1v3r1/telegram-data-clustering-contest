Jennifer Aniston calls Friends 'the gift of a lifetime' as honoured as icon

Jen was accepting her Icon Award at the People's Choice Award ceremony when she thanked the 'fantastic show and fantastic cast' for exploding her career

11 Nov 2019, 06:04 by Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

Jennifer Aniston has gushed over hit sitcom Friends and credited it as being just the thing she needed to launch her acting career.

Jen, 50, starred in all ten seasons of the wildly popular comedy, portraying spoiled rich girl turned fashion titan Rachel Green.

Now, she's been honoured at the People's Choice Awards with the Icon Award, presented to her by close friend and frequent collaborator Adam Sandler.

Introducing his pal, Adam said: "I truly love this girl and love making movies with her and hope we can do a lot more."

Stunning in a black dress, Jen took to the stage, hugged Adam and spoke to the star-studded crowd holding her award.

"This town puts out a lot of awards," she said, "But People's Choice has always been special to me.

"As actors, we don't do this for the critics or for each other or to make our families proud...we do this for the money! I'm kidding, we do it for you guys, we really do.

"If I have a claim to this word 'icon', it was only because I was able to be on an iconic show with an iconic cast and an iconic haircut.

"I mean, Friends was truly the gift of a lifetime and I would not be standing up here without that fantastic show and that fantastic cast. It's paved the way for everything I've had the chance to do since."

She's also gearing up for the launch of her new comedy-drama The Morning Show, in which she stars as a journalist in a sexist newsroom, alongside close friend Reese Witherspoon.

Last month, Jen shattered fans' minds when she not only joined Instagram , but marked the occasion with a selfie of the Friends cast - Jen was joined by Courtney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, David Schwimmer, Matt Le Blanc and Matthew Perry.
