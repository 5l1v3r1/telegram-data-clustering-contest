Imran Khan takes notice of inaction on complaints lodged on PM Citizen Portal

11 Nov 2019, 06:16 by News Desk

Prime Minister Imran Khan has taken notice of officials for their inaction regarding complaints lodged on the Citizen Portal.

According to the Prime Minister Office, a written letter has been sent to all ministries and departments who have been directed to constitute five-member committees to conduct performance evaluation and submit their reports within 30 days.

The committee will be headed by a 20-grade officer or a joint secretary and it will submit its report after the evaluation of deployed officers.

The committee will also point out misconduct in all reports on citizen portal including resolved and pending complaints.

The premier was informed that complaints were disposed of without proper inquiry, satisfactory reply, and documentary evidence.

It was also observed that departments concerned took unnecessary time in addressing complaints and decisions of resolving complaints were taken by unconcerned officials.
