Cricket fraternity hails T20I hat-trick man Deepak Chahar

11 Nov 2019, 06:23 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

NEW DELHI: The cricket fraternity hailed Deepak Chahar after the medium pacer became the first Indian man to take a hat-trick in Twenty20 Internationals. Chahar ended the match with figures of 6-7, beating the earlier record held by Sri Lanka's Ajantha Mendis.

Chahar also recorded the best bowling figures in a T20I match.
