Tania’s Tea House

A thoroughly Instagrammable coffee shop

11 Nov 2019

Time Out Says

The whimsical interiors, endless Instagram selfie opportunities and brand-new kids’ menu make Tania’s Teahouse a dreamy destination for the whole family.

The pink door, framed by blush-coloured roses, is a clear indicator of what’s to come before we even step inside (which we do after a few doorstep pictures for the gram).

Step into a space that wouldn’t look out of place in the pages of a fairy tale picture book. It’s as though a unicorn has waved her horn and created a magical café in a two-storey villa in Jumeirah.

But Tania’s Teahouse isn’t just pleasing on the eye, the food is an assault on the taste buds – in a good way too – and the staff clearly feed off the happy vibes of the eatery. The beautifully illustrated menu offers a selection of breakfast and lunch dishes as well as two afternoon tea options.

With fun names like “Save The Pesto For Lasto” and “Let’s Taco ‘Bout It” there’s plenty of fun involved in choosing what to eat.
The presentation of the food is as Instagrammable as the décor, and so pretty are the dishes that there is even a warning on the menu advising social media-lovers not to take too long over their snaps before the food gets cold and is no longer so tasty.
This little teahouse is worth a visit, it’s cute pretty and to quote Mary Poppins, “practically perfect in every way” (if pink is your vibe).
