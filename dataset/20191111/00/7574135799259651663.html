What’s going on Monday?

11 Nov 2019, 00:01 by BrooklynVegan Staff

You can browse our full NYC show calendar for all of tonight’s shows, but here are some highlights…

Black Midi, Onyx Collective @ Warsaw
Despite having only one album under their belt (this year’s great Schlagenheim), UK mathy experimental band Black Midi have gained a reputation for being one of the most exciting live bands around. Tonight’s Brooklyn show features support from NYC-based jazz trio Onyx Collective, making for an eclectic pairing.

Shoreline Mafia @ Webster Hall
LA rap group Shoreline Mafia have been on the rise all year, and having just dropped Party Pack, Vol. 2, they’ll bring the party to NYC’s Webster Hall tonight.

Asian Doll, $toney, Miss Money, Natalie @ Knitting Factory
Dallas rapper Asian Doll (also known as Asian Da Brat) headlines this night of hip hop at Knitting Factory, where she’ll perform alongside $toney, Miss Money, and Natalie.

Butterboy Comedy @ Littlefield
Jo Firestone, Aparna Nancherla and Maeve Higgins celebrate the 2nd anniversary of their weekly Butterboy Comedy show, with special guests Todd Barry, Joyelle Nicole Johnson, James Fritz, and more.

For all of tonight’s shows, and tomorrow’s, check out our NYC concert calendar.
