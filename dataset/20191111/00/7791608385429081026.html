Rihanna Teases Holiday Lingerie Collection Looking Like A Sexy Christmas Gift

11 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Rihanna will be releasing a new red-hot lingerie collection for the holiday season, and has teased it with some fitting pictuers.
The songstress modeled some of the new pieces from her Savage x Fenty collection on Instagram. The new additions will be available during a special VIP box giveaway for Christmas.
In a series of sultry snaps, Rihanna smouldered in striking red leggings and a backless long-sleeved top that showed off her midriff. She also wore matching crimson-hued lips, while lounging on a rug full of silver confetti and balloons.
A second picture shows her laying on a suitcase trolley and holding some balloons. “This holiday season, Bad Gal’s just want to have fun,” she captioned one of her photos.
Another caption reads, “Who said the party had to end?!”
Other pieces from various VIP boxes include sultry lingerie modeled by various sized models. Rihanna herself wore a black two-piece set with black tights that displayed her brand name over her right thigh.
