The pop queen, 27, stripped down to lingerie for the very raunchy shoot, displaying her eye-popping toned bod for the Agent Provocateur Christmas campaign

Pop star Charli XCX has set pulses racing in incredibly saucy snaps posing in sheer lingerie for Agent Provocateur's Christmas campaign.

The songstress, 27, showed off her tiny display and toned tum in revealing pictures for the lingerie brand.

She was saddled up on a horse, leaving almost nothing to the imagination.

Charli - real name Charlotte - was pictured in several different lingerie sets in the jaw-dropping shoot.

In one snap, she's seen grabbing onto a cowboy hat while sitting on the horse, showing off her assets in a lace pink underwear set.

While in another she's spotted in a sexy red set paired with stockings.

She's not one to shy away from the camera - last night the star shared a sizzling swimsuit selfie to her Instagram page.

 

Charli is known to show off her stunning physique and she was back to her old tricks once again.

She rose to fame in 2013 with her chart-topping hit I Love It and the singer hasn't been afraid to express her larger-than-life persona through both her music and style.

 

Amid her busy work schedule, the star revealed she and Australian songwriter Troye Sivan used to enjoy some downtime by throwing huge house parties in LA.

On her wild days, the pop star told Dazed: "We went through this phase of just throwing like a f**k-load of parties.

"We would just leave the doors open, send out the biggest group texts, and so many people came. Like, the police would come, it was a real nightmare. Our neighbours hated us. All our cars got keyed."
