KKR May Take Lead In Walgreens Buyout

11 Nov 2019 by Bruce Japsen

Reports emerged Monday that KKR & Co has “formally approached” Walgreens Boots Alliance to take the global drugstore chain private.Bloomberg News said “it could be the biggest-ever leveraged buyout” given the size of the company, which has , according to media reports and sources close to the company.

Talk of a potential leveraged buyout of the giant drugstore chain comes as Walgreens looks for ways to compete with rival CVS Health, which last year spent $69 billion to buy Aetna, the nation’s third largest health insurance company.
