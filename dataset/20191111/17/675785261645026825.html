EastEnders' spoilers: Mel's humiliating plan to expose Sharon in front of whole Square

Unhinged Mel Owen, played by Tamzin Outhwaite, continues to spiral out of control as she plans a nasty surprise for Louise and the rest of her family

11 Nov 2019, 17:33 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

Mel Owen's twisted attempt at revenge begins to spiral out of control as she looks set to humiliate Sharon Mitchell in front of her friends and family on EastEnders tonight.

The grieving mum, played by Tamzin Outhwaite, has been using Sharon's affair with Keanu (and that she is pregnant with his baby) as her ace in a bid to get what she wants.

Yet a new villa in Spain and forcing Sharon to sell her half of E20 isn't enough for Mel as she begins to plan a party for Sharon and Louise.

Insisting that friends and family of the pair keep it all a secret, she elaborately decorates the club with photos.

Eventually, Sharon gets wind of what Mel is planning and implores Keanu to get Louise out of Walford, uncertain of what Mel's objective is.

Unfortunately, Louise has already found out about the bash and is busy getting ready for it.

Sharon tentatively arrives for the party with Louise and Mel's plan begins to unfold...

What is she up to?

In an explosive trailer for this month, Sharon and Keanu are seen panicking as they make their way across the Square.

Keanu demands to know what they're going to do, but soon the party is underway and Mel appears unhinged.

In scenes later, the guests look horrified over an announcement made at the bash - including Louise and Lisa, who cover their mouths in shock.

Will Mel tell all of their friends and family about who the real dad of Sharon's baby is?

*EastEnders airs tonight at 8pm on BBC One
