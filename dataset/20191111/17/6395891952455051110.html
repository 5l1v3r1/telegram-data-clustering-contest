Motorcycle crash claims life of operator

11 Nov 2019, 17:23 by EndPlay

SOUTH KINGSTOWN, R.I.Â (AP) - Police are investigating the death of a motorcycle rider in Rhode Island.

Police say the motorcycle operator crashed on U.S. Route 1 in South Kingstown at about 7 p.m. Sunday.

The crash occurred in the area of the Post Road-Camp Fuller Road off-ramp.

No other vehicles were involved.

The operator was pronounced dead at the scene.

The victim's name was not immediately made public.
