Tyler, The Creator reacts to Drake being booed off stage at Camp Flog Gnaw festival

11 Nov 2019, 17:45

It's thought fans were disappointed that Frank Ocean wasn't a surprise guest.

Tyler, The Creator has responded after Drake was forced to cut short his surprise headline set at Tyler’s Camp Flog Gnaw Festival, after the rapper faced unrelenting booing from fans.

The Canadian rapper made a surprise appearance at the event on Sunday evening in a headline slot which was also split between Lil Uzi Vert and A$AP Rocky.

However, footage on social media seemingly shows Drake choosing to bring a premature end to his slot, after facing relentless criticism from fans.
