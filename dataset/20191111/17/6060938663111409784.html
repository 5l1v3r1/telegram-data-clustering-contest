Bihar civil court stays the the release of Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Athiya Shetty's 'Motichoor Chaknachoor'

11 Nov 2019, 17:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Athiya Shetty's 'Motichoor Chaknachoor' has been the talk of the town. The film has been slated for the release on 15th November. However, according to the latest report, a civil court in Bihar has stayed the release of the film.

According to the reports, one of the local distributors has claimed the rights to the film with relevant documents to support the claim. Reportedly, there have been a lot of complaints against the producer of the film, Rajesh Bhatia for not clearing the dues. If the reports are to be believed, the producer had sacked the director Debamitra of the film earlier.

Also, Debamitra had filed a petition on Bombay High Court which had ordered a stay on the release of its trailer, over non-payment of dues by the makers of the film, however, the stay was later lifted by the court. There were reports that Athiya has not yet been paid fully!
