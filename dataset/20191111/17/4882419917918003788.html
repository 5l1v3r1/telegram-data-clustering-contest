Indignation in Madrid as Gareth Bale trains with Wales

The Welshman has been missing for his club but seems fine to play for his country

11 Nov 2019, 17:16 by Sport EN

Gareth Bale is back on the grass. However not as a Real Madrid player, but for his country, Wales. Bale had no problem training with his international team-mates after missing four weeks of Madrid sessions with injury.

Bale was injured during the last Wales game and since then he has not played.

Now he's back with his national team to face Azerbaijan and Hungary in the Euro 2020 qualifiers.

Ryan Giggs counts on him for the games and everything indicates Bale will play for the first time since he suffered pain against Croatia on October 13.

In Spain, Bale's attitude has been heavily criticised.
