Bolivia Armed Forces say it will protect 'essential public services'

11 Nov 2019, 19:02

LA PAZ (Reuters) - Bolivia’s Armed Forces said in a statement on Monday that it would launch plans to protect essential public services amid escalating violence in the South American nation after leftist leader Evo Morales resigned as President on Sunday.
