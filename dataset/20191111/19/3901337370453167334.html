The Top Goalscorers This Season So Far

11 Nov 2019, 19:16 by David Kappel

Despite missing five league games already this season, Lionel Messi has closed in on the top goalscorers in Europe, while Cristiano Ronaldo remains off the pace. Here are Europe's top marksman in the 2019/20 campaign so far!

The Argentine netted a hat-trick in FC Barcelona's 4-1 win over Celta Vigo on Saturday, with two of his goals coming from direct free-kicks while the third was a converted penalty.

As a consequence, Messi has now scored eight times in just seven LaLiga games this season, three more strikes than Ronaldo, who has three more league appearances to his name, has.

However, the Blaugrana superstar remains just one goal shy of the top 10, which is led by Robert Lewandowski. The Bayern Munich forward extended his superb run of scoring in every Bundesliga game this campaign with a brace against his former club Borussia Dortmund on Saturday.

He has now struck 16 goals in 11 Bundesliga games in 2019/20, while second-placed Lazio striker Ciro Immobile also found the back of the net on the weekend, taking his tally to 14 strikes.

The top two are followed by Leicester City's Jamie Vardy and Red Bull Leipzig star Timo Werner, who have both scored 11 times so far.
