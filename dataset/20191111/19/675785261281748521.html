BREAKING FA Cup second round draw in full as fixtures are decided

The draw for the second round of the FA Cup was completed on Monday afternoon with all sides just 90 minutes off a potential Premier League opponent

11 Nov 2019, 19:23 by Aaron Flanagan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/

The draw for the second round of the FA Cup has been completed.

Those left in the competition are potentially just 90 minutes away from securing a dream money-spinning tie against Premier League opposition.

A host of non-league clubs remain in the competition at this stage and they now know who they will face for a place in the exclusive third round.

Among those still in the competition are Chichester City and Maldon & Tiptree, who both play in the eighth tier of English football.

Maldon remarkably knocked out League Two side Leyton Orient, beating them 2-1 at Brisbane Road.
