Georgia deputy charged with man's murder during domestic incident

11 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Robert Gearty

A Georgia deputy was arrested Monday and charged with murder in the deadly off-duty shooting of another man during a domestic incident, police said.

Athens-Clarke County officers responded to an address in Athens on Sunday evening and found 26-year-old Benjamin Cloer dead of multiple gunshot wounds, police said.

Winford “Trey” Adams III, a 32-year-old deputy with the Madison County Sheriff’s Office, was taken into custody and arrested early Monday, police spokesman Geoffrey Gilland said in a news release.
