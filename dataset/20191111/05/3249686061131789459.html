Mnet says X1 and IZ*ONE's future is being discussed

11 Nov 2019, 05:39 by jennywill

Mnet denied that X1 and IZ*ONE were certain to disband.

Mnet said, "As the situation is serious, X1 and IZ*ONE's future is being discussed in various ways. However, we have not made any detailed plans on disbanding."

The police will be making a debriefing on their investigations on the 'Produce 101' series later today.
