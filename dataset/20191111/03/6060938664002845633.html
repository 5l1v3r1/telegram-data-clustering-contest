PHOTOS: Alia Bhatt is back to the bay after vacaying in Los Angeles

11 Nov 2019, 03:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Actress Alia Bhatt who was holidaying with friends in Los Angles has returned to the bay today during the wee hours. The actress was papped at the airport in an uber-cool avatar as she walked out of the Mumbai airport. The actress was seen sporting a black tee paired with same colour leggings, a lavender-pink flared overcoat and white shoes.

She had also shared an Insta story from the flight of feeling jet lagged, she shared a boomerang video and wrote, "Hello jet lag..pls go away..."

While Alia was in LA, she kept treating us with her stunning pictures from her exotic vacay. She shared a few pictures on her Instagram profile giving us major holiday goals.

Meanwhile, reportedly the young talented actress looking out for an international celebrity manager as she aims to enter Hollywood just like her Bollywood contemporaries Priyanka Chopra, Deepika Padukone and others have done in their careers.

On the work front, the 'Raazi' actress will be collaborating with her father Mahesh Bhatt and sister Pooja Bhatt for the first time in 'Sadak 2'. The film will also star Sanjay Dutt, Aditya Roy Kapur and Gulshan Grover in pivotal roles. Apart from 'Sadak 2', Alia has her kitty full with some interesting projects like Ayan Mukerji’s ‘Brahmastra’, Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s ‘Gangubai Kathiawadi’ and Karan Johar’s ‘Takht’.
