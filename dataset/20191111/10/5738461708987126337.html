DUNDALK CRIME

Investigation ongoing after Dundalk taxi driver attacked by passenger

11 Nov 2019, 10:57 by Tia Clarke

Gardaí in Dundalk are investigating an assault on a male taxi driver aged 50 who was assaulted by a passenger on November 9 at 2.20am at Ecco Road, Dundalk.  

The suspect was charged with assault and appeared before the Criminal Courts of Justice in Dublin on November 9 and has since been bailed. 

A Garda spokesperson added: "This matter is under investigation and no further information is available at this time."
