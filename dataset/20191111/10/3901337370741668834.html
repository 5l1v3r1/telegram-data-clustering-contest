Former Barca Star Makes Africa U-Turn

11 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Kurt Buckerfield

After he was believed to have chosen to represent an African country over Spain, a former FC Barcelona starlet has now been called up to La Furia Roja's latest squad to replace a player who had to be withdrawn due to injury.

Last week, it was reported that Wolverhampton Wanderers winger Adama Traore had decided he was going to play for Mali, having previously only represented Spain at age-group level.

The 23-year-old, who qualifies to play for the African country through his family, was called up to play for Mali back in 2014, but chose not to switch his allegiance at that stage.
