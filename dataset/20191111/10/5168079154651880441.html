Oman weather: Missing man found dead

11 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Times News Service

Muscat: A citizen who was missing from Al Batinah governorate has been found, Royal Oman Police announced.

Rescue teams received a report on Sunday, after he was swept away in a flooded wadi in Rustaq.

ROP said in an online statement: "The citizen who was missing yesterday as a result of drifting his vehicle in the course of the wadi in wilayat Al Rustaq was found dead."
