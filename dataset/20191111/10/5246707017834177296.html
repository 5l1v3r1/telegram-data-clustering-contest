UK Charts: Luigi's Mansion 3 Falls To Fourth As Death Stranding And Call Of Duty Take Command

Mario & Sonic grabs eighth

11 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Ryan Craddock

UK chart data is now in for the week ending 9th November, revealing that Luigi's Mansion 3 has slipped down to fourth place in its second week on sale.

After becoming the fastest-selling physical Switch game of 2019 in the UK last week, the game couldn't manage to overturn Call of Duty: Modern Warfare's dominance. Activision's shooter stays at number one, with new PS4 exclusive Death Stranding earning a second place debut.

Elsewhere, Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020 sprinted into eighth place in its opening week; other newcomer Just Dance 2020 managed 15th.
