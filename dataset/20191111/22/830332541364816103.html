Deposition transcript for deputy assistant defense secretary released

Only Pentagon witness to testify so far

11 Nov 2019, 22:45 by Jeremy Herb, CNN

WASHINGTON (CNN) - House impeachment investigators on Monday released the deposition transcript of Laura Cooper, the deputy assistant defense secretary for Russia, Ukraine and Eurasia.

Cooper has been the only Pentagon witness to testify so far as part of the House Democratic impeachment inquiry into President Donald Trump and Ukraine. According to lawmakers, she provided a technical readout of how foreign aid was disbursed, and her closed-door testimony helped show how the freezing of US aid to Ukraine deviated from that normal process.

Cooper, who was issued a subpoena for her testimony, did not give an opening statement, lawmakers said.

She testified the day that a group of House Republican lawmakers barged into the House Intelligence Committee secure spaces to protest the Democratic-run impeachment inquiry. The stunt, which was led by GOP Rep. Matt Gaetz of Florida, delayed her deposition for five hours.
