The You Season Two Teaser Offers a Creepy Conclusion to 2019

11 Nov 2019, 22:20 by Jordan Crucchiola

Once all the gifts are unwrapped and your Christmas festivities come to an end, you can combat those postholiday blues with Netflix’s most compelling stalker. Happy holidays to all! The first teaser dropped today for season two of You, and even though it only gives us quick looks at Joe (Penn Badgley), his new “special friend” Love Quinn (Victoria Pedretti), and a few other supporting characters, it also gives us a date: December 26. That’s when you can start watching Joe have new adventures in Los Angeles, a place that really embodies everything he didn’t like about Beck in season one, but we’re sure to find some things about the city that he enjoys. (Badgley previously told ET that “there are some things I’ve done with prosthetic bodies in this season that were kind of nauseating as I did them, I will say that,” about season two, and we already know he still loves murder.) We miss you already, Peach Salinger.
