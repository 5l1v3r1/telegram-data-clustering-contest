Charles Rogers dead: Former Detroit Lions NFL star passes away aged 38

Rogers was picked second overall in the 2003 NFL draft by the Detroit Lions before injuries and off-field issues brought a premature end to his career

11 Nov 2019, 14:11 by James Whaling, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/james-whaling/

Former Detroit Lions NFL star Charles Rogers has died aged 38.

The wide receiver penned a £30.7million contract with the Lions in 2003 which included a signing bonus of over £11million after being picked seventh overall in the NFL draft.

But his career was brought to a premature end by injuries and off-field issues.
