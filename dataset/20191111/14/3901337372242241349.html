Chiefs Defender Among Latest Arrivals In SA U23 Camp

11 Nov 2019, 14:59 by Soccer Laduma

A Kaizer Chiefs defender and two overseas-based stars are among the latest players to join the camp South Africa’s Under-23 camp. 

As confirmed by the South African Football Association, Siyabonga Ngezana, Lyle Foster and Luther Singh are the latest players to join coach David Notoane’s team in Egypt for the U23 Africa Cup of Nations.

At the weekend, SuperSport United midfielders Sipho Mbule and Teboho Mokoena joined the team in Cairo. 

Notoane’s side opened their AFCON campaign with a 0-0 draw against Zambia. The team’s second Group B match will be against Ivory Coast on Tuesday at the Al Salam Stadium.
