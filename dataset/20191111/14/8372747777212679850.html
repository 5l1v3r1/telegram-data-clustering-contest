Suspect sought for two thefts at North Naples Walmart

11 Nov 2019, 14:29 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- Deputies are looking for a man they say was involved in two separate thefts from a Walmart store in recent months.

According to the Collier County Sheriff's Office, the thefts happened at the North Naples Walmart store in September and October.

They believe the man seen here is involved.

If you have any information regarding his identity please contact CCSO at 239-252-9300. If you wish to remain anonymous you can call our tips line at 1-800-780-TIPS (8477) or SWFL Crime Stoppers 1-800-780-TIPS (8477).
