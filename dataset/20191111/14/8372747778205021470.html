Surveillance images released in grand theft investigation at Cape Coral store

11 Nov 2019, 14:18 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Police hope these surveillance images lead to tips in a grand theft investigation at a Southwest Florida home improvement store.

According to Cape Coral Police, it happened at the Lowe's store on NE Pine Island Road.

Police say the man seen here left the store without paying for merchandise.

If you have any information on the identity of the suspect, please contact the Cape Coral Police Department at 239-574-3223. You can also submit an anonymous tip. Go to www.capecops.com/tips or text a tip- text CCPD+"Your Message" to CRIMES (274637).

You can also call Southwest Florida Crime Stoppers at 1-800-780-TIPS. Please use Case Report #19-019098.
