Farhan Akhtar-Shibani Dandekar planning a wedding in February-March 2020?

11 Nov 2019, 14:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Farhan Akhtar and Shibani Dandekar have been painting the town red with their romance. From exotic holidays to Holi party and Bollywood events, this couple has been doling out massive relationship goals. And if latest buzz is to be believed then Farhan and Shibani are all set to take their relationship to the next level.

Farhan and Shibani are reportedly serious about their relationship and plan to get married in the first half of 2020, probably in February or March. Shibani is reportedly bonding well with Farhan’s kids and marriage seems to be the next step in their relationship now. Farhan and Shibani sparked rumours of their relationship in October last year, but the couple remained tight lipped about their romance. However now that the cat is out of the bag, Farhan and Shibani don’t shy away from posting loved-up clicks of each other on Instagram.

Farhan and Shibani’s nuptials will be Bollywood’s first wedding of 2020 and could well be followed by Varun Dhawan and Natasha Dalal’s destination wedding in April.
