Tamara Ecclestone puts up her Christmas decorations six weeks before the big day

The Formula 1 heiress put her decorations up last night with help from her husband Jay Rutland and daughter Sophia

11 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Tamara Ecclestone is clearly already in the festive spirit - six weeks before Christmas Day is even here.

The Formula 1 heiress, 35, put up her Christmas decorations last night alongside her husband Jay Rutland, 38, and their daughter Sophia, five.

Their London mansion was transformed into a winter wonderland as they decorated a giant Christmas tree with white, gold and silver ornaments.

Dressed in festive red pyjamas, little Sophia had to stand on a step ladder to reach the tree so she could hang up the decorations.

She twinned with her proud mum, who showed off her natural beauty with no makeup on in the snaps.

Both Tamara and James, who have been married for six years, posted pictures of their decorating session on Instagram .

Tamara shared one of Sophia putting the first decoration on the tree, writing: "Feeling festive."

She then posted a cute photo of her family sitting at the foot of the tree once it was all done.

"The most wonderful time of the year," she said.

Jay shared one of Sophia, who they call Fifi, hanging up baubles, and another of the trio posing by their tree together.
