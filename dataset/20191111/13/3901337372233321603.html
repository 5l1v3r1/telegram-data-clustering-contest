Brazilian Star Sent Off After Reacting To Racist Abuse

11 Nov 2019, 13:27 by Kurt Buckerfield

After pulling the middle finger and kicking a ball at racist supporters, a Brazilian star playing in Europe was given his marching orders for his reaction to some disgusting chants from fans.

On Sunday night, Shakhtar Donetsk midfielder Taison was reportedly subjected to racist abuse in his team's home encounter against Dynamo Kiev.

The midfielder, who was clearly triggered by chants from a certain section of the crowd, stuck his middle finger up before taking aim at them with a ball. Taison and his teammate Dentinho, who was also targeted, left the field in tears, while several Kiev players tried to console them.
