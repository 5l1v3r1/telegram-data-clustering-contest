Hong Kong's Monday of rage: Protester shot, man set on fire, hundreds arrested

11 Nov 2019, 13:46

A 21-year old protester was shot by police with a live round and a pro-China man was set on fire as Hong Kong woke up Monday to chaos on its streets. Over 260 people have been arrested, Hong Kong police said on the agency's Twitter account.
