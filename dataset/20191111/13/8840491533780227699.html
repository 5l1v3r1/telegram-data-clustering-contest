Heroin discovered by Mayo drugs squad in recent raid

11 Nov 2019, 13:16

HEROIN was discovered by gardaí during a recent raid in Mayo, it has been revealed.

Councillor Christy Hyland said it is a very dangerous sign as the drug is crime related.

The Westport-based representative called for more gardaí on the beat to deal with the problem.

He highlighted how, in Westport, the population quadruples on a Saturday night and is as busy as Temple Bar.

Yet there were two guards in a patrol car policing an area from the border of Leenane down to Achill Island.
