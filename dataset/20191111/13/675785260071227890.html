FA Cup second round ball numbers confirmed for Monday night's draw

After the weekend's FA Cup first round action, 56 teams are in the hat for the second round draw, one step away from potential Premier League opposition

11 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Alex Richards, https://www.facebook.com/AARichards/

The FA Cup first round produced the goods over the weekend, with thrills, spills, heartbreak and much much more as Football League sides entered this year's competition.

Now 56 teams take their place in the hat for the second round draw, knowing that they are one step away from a potential clash with one of the Premier League big boys.

The lowest ranked side left are Chichester City, the non-league outfit who were handed a bye into round two following Bury's expulsion from the Football League.

The side from step four of the Non-League period - who drew 2-2 with Sevenoaks Town over the weekend - are joined by another huge underdog in round two, Maldon & Tiptree.

The Essex outfit caused quite the stir with the biggest shock of round one, knocking out League Two side Leyton Orient.

Another giant-killer were Kingstonian, who produced a remarkable result at Macclesfield, running out 4-0 winners on the road.

They are all joined in Monday night's draw by League One leaders Ipswich Town and Sunderland - both of whom have replays to navigate - while 1987 FA Cup winners Coventry City are there too.
