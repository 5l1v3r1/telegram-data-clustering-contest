Amazon is selling chocolate flavoured wine that 'tastes like Christmas in a cup'

Amazon is selling the ultimate festive tipple and it comes in a handy presentation box

11 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Linda Howard

We’re entering the season of high spirits and good cheer and nobody is embracing that more than Rubis who have launched a 50cl bottle of chocolate and wine that ‘tastes like Christmas in a cup’.

Made from Spanish Tempranillo grapes, the festive tipple is 'infused with the luxurious essence of dark chocolate and boasts subtle hints of spice, cherries and vanilla'.

Rubis Chocolate Wine comes in a presentation gift box and costs £15.20 at Amazon.co.uk

The seasonal wine is also on sale at Morrisons for £9.50 - without the presentation box.

Buying Manager at Morrisons, Anastasia Duncanson, said: "This wine is Christmas in a cup.

"We hope that customers looking to indulge their sweet tooth enjoy this tipple whilst it’s available at a market-leading price."

It’s not the only Christmas-themed offering to hit the supermarket shelves this month, Morrisons are also selling bottles of Chocolate Orange Shimmer Gin Liqueur for £10.

The festive drink features notes of both zesty oranges and velvety chocolate and looks Christmassy too - shimmering with bronze and gold glitter when you shake the bottle.
