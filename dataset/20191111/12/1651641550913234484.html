Windows 10 Theme: Download Microsoft’s new Fractal Art PREMIUM theme pack from Microsft Store

11 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Raka

Microsoft has added a new theme pack to the Microsoft Store for Windows 10 users. Titled Fractal Art PREMIUM, the new theme pack contains 12 high-quality 4K wallpapers, the description of which reads as follows:

Algorithms are art in these 12 kaleidoscopic premium 4k images. These images are free for Windows 10 Themes and are to be used as desktop wallpaper only.

After downloading the collection, go to Start > Settings > Personalization > Themes and click on the theme you prefer.

To apply and change to the most appropriate color, based on the current wallpaper, go to Customize > Colors > Automatically select a primary color from the background.
