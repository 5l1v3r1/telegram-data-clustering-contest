Premier League team of the week: Star players from Liverpool, Manchester United, Leicester and more

Two Liverpool stars make our team after their huge victory over Manchester City

11 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Harry Latham-Coyle

Liverpool FC extended their lead at the top of the Premier League table with a huge 3-1 victory over Manchester City this weekend.

Two Reds feature in our selection of the best eleven players of the weekend, while Manchester United and Leicester City also contribute two players apiece.

United were impressive victors over Brighton while the Foxes moved into second with victory over Arsenal.
