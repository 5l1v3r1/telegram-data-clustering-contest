Govt not giving Asif Zardari access to specialist doctors, personal physician: Sherry Rehman

11 Nov 2019, 12:16 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP) parliamentary leader in the Senate Sherry Rehman said government is not giving former president Asif Ali Zadrari access to specialist doctors and his personal physician, Dunya News reported on Monday.

While expressing concerns, the PPP leader maintained that Asif Zardari’s health is deteriorating with every passing day. No private medical board is being formed for the erstwhile president’s health, she added.

Sherry Rehman further said that the incumbent government is playing with Asif Zardari’s health. The medical reports of the former president and PPP co-chairman are also not being shared with anyone, she told.
