Kym Marsh says goodbye to Corrie cast as 'second family' throw star leaving party

The actress, who has played Michelle Connor in the soap since 2006, ensured she celebrated in style as she wrapped up her time on the cobbles

11 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Sara-Aisha Kent, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sara-aisha-kent/

Kym Marsh said goodbye to her Coronation Street castmates who are like her 'second family' with a lavish party on Friday night.

The 43-year-old star, who has played Michelle Connor on the ITV soap since 2006, enjoyed a night of partying with her fellow Corrie co-stars at the Great John Street Hotel in Manchester after wrapping up her scenes.

Upon her departure, the former Hear'Say singer reflected on her time on the cobbles and how the show "was a safety net during tough times."

Speaking to OK! magazine , who were at the bash, Kym told: "When you go through bad times, you still have to go into work as life goes on."

Sharing on how the cast and crew are like a 'second-family' to her, she added: "When I lost Archie [Kym’s son who died in 2009 following a premature birth at 21 weeks], I took some time off, but when I went back, the cast and crew were the people I relied on. They still know to hug me on Archie’s birthday."

For the party Kym ensured she put on glamorous display in a black wrap dress embellished with sequins which she teamed with platform boots.

Joining her were Lucy Fallon, who plays Bethany Platt, and Tina O'Brien, who portrays Sarah-Louise Platt.

Also on the guestlist were Jane Danson (Leanne Battersby), Alison King (Carla Connor), Rob Mallard (Daniel Osbourne) and Jack P Shepherd (David Platt).

Kim's beau Scott Ratcliff could not join the celebrations as he is in the middle-east on an Army tour.

Making his presence known at the party, Scott filmed a heartfelt video message for the actress.

He said: "I love you from the bottom of my heart, I miss you every second of every day and I can’t wait to be back home with you."

Kim later revealed that the special message "made her cry."

The Corrie favourite first announced her exit from the soap in February, after 13 years of playing Michelle.

Recently speaking on This Morning, Kym revealed that pal Alison has been left in tears over her decision to leave the ITV soap.

The pair, who are best friends and part of the Connor family on-screen, share a dressing room - but not for much longer.

On Alison's reaction to her exit, Kym said: "She's kind of fallen out with me about it. She keeps having a little cry. She's going to miss me and I'm going to miss her incredibly."
