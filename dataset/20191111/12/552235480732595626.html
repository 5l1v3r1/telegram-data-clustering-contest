Man critical after being hit by taxi outside Edinburgh's Balmoral Hotel

The 27-year-old is critical in Edinburgh Royal Infirmary.

11 Nov 2019, 12:35 by Dailyrecord.co.uk

A man is in a critical condition in hospital after he was hit by a taxi on a city street.

The incident happened on North Bridge near a bus stop opposite the Balmoral Hotel in Edinburgh at around 2am on Monday.

Police Scotland said the pedestrian, a 27-year-old man, was taken by ambulance to Edinburgh Royal Infirmary , where he remains in a critical condition.

Officers are appealing for information about the incident.

Sergeant Jennifer Forbes, of Edinburgh road policing, said: "We are carrying out enquiries to establish the full circumstances that led to this crash. In particular, we are keen to speak to anyone who may have dash-cam footage or who was walking in the area at the time.

"We would urge anyone with information who has not yet spoken to police to come forward to help with our investigation.

"If you can help please contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident 0238 of November 11, or make a call anonymously to Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111."
