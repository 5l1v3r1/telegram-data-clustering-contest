GOT7 top physical album charts for the week

11 Nov 2019, 04:52 by jennywill

GOT7's 'Call My Name' has topped album charts.

The boys released their mini-album 'Call My Name' on the 4th, and they ranked #1 on Hanteo's weekly album chart for November 4th-November 10th. They also ranked #1 on Gaon's Retail Album Chart for the week of November 3rd to November 9th. 

Congratulations to GOT7.
