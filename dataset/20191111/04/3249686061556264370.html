Police to debrief on the 'Produce' series' manipulation investigations later today

11 Nov 2019, 04:25 by jennywill

The police will be debriefing about their 'Produce 101' series investigations.

The Seoul Police District's Cyber Security Team revealed that they would be holding a debriefing related to the investigations on the 'Produce 101' series. During the course of their investigations, their area was broadened to include 'Idol School' as well.
