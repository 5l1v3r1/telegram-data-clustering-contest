In Pics: Shah Rukh Khan, Abhishek Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and others attend Ambani's house party

11 Nov 2019, 04:25 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood celebrities were looking all decked up as they attended a house party at Ambani's residence in Mumbai yesterday. Mukesh Ambani's residence Antilla was decorated for his niece Nayantara Kothari’s pre-wedding bash. Nayantara is the daughter of Nina Kothari, Mukesh’s sister.

The star-studded event was attended by Aishwarya Rai Bachchan with husband Abhishek Bachchan, Anil Kapoor and his wife Sunita Kapoor, Shahid and Mira Kapoor, and Shah Rukh Khan among others

The event was marked by eminent personalities including Bollywood celebs who posed in style, as they were clicked by the paparazzi.

Among those in attendance were Aishwarya Rai Bachchan with husband Abhishek Bachchan, Shahid and Mira Kapoor, and Shah Rukh Khan among others.

SRK looked handsome in an all-black suit teamed up with a white shirt and black shoes. Abhishek also wore a black suit while Aishwarya looked enchanting in a red salwar suit.
