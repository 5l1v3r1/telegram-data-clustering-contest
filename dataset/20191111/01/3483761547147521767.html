Works begin on Jesmond bridge

11 Nov 2019, 01:57

Work will start this week on the new shared path bridge at Jesmond as part of early work for the Newcastle Inner City Bypass.

Work was to start from November 11 to be carried out between 7am and 6pm from Monday to Friday and between 8am and 5pm on Saturday. Some night and weekend work will be required to ensure the safety of workers and road users.
