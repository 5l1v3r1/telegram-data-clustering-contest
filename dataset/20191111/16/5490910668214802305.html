Short Suits Are the Coolest Spring 2020 Fashion Trend

Contemporary brands are offering cool, modern takes on tailoring with short suits that easily transition from the office to a night out.

11 Nov 2019, 16:12 by Andrew Shang

Across the contemporary market, brands are tapping into a cool factor to entice younger customers to dress up, namely through short suits with a modern and edgy attitude.
