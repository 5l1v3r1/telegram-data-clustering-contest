Bolivia's legislative assembly receives Morales resignation letter: Vice Presidential office

11 Nov 2019, 16:34

LA PAZ (Reuters) - Bolivia’s legislative assembly has received the resignation letter from Evo Morales after the leftist leader announced that he would step down on Sunday, an official at the Vice Presidential office confirmed on Monday.
