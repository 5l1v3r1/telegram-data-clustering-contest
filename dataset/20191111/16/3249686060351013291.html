Stray Kids release 'Astronaut' individual teaser images for Changbin, Seungmin, and Hyunjin

11 Nov 2019, 16:36 by sl278

JYP Entertainment has released the first individual teasers for Stray Kids' upcoming release titled 'Astronaut'. The first teaser images feature members Changbin, Seungmin, and Hyunjin.

The boys are looking particularly in preppy outfits that show off their handsome looks.

Stay tuned for more teaser images until the full release on November 14.
