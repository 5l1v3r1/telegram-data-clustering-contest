Vince Staples shares “Yo Love” ft. 6LACK & Mereba from ‘Queen & Slim’ soundtrack

11 Nov 2019, 16:56 by Andrew Sacher

As previously mentioned, there’s a new Vince Staples song on the Queen & Slim soundtrack, and that song, “Yo Love,” is out now. It features 6LACK and Mereba, and it sees Vince going in a chilled-out, soulful direction, which suits him well. Listen to that below.

The Queen & Slim soundtrack is out this Friday and also features Ms. Lauryn Hill, Megan Thee Stallion x VickeeLo, Lil Baby, Tiana Major9 & EARTHGANG, Coast Contra Ft. BJ The Chicago Kid, Blood Orange And Syd. Blood Orange also did the score, and the film was written by Lena Waithe (Master of None, The Chi) and directed by Melina Matsoukas (Insecure, Master of None, Beyonce videos). The film’s out November 27. Watch the trailer below.
