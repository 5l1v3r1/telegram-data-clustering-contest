Rescued kidnapped Durban businesswoman 'in good keeping', 4 accused appear in court

11 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Kaveel Singh

Durban businesswoman Sandra Munsamy, who was recently found after being held captive for six months by kidnappers, is safe, authorities said.

"For security reasons, all I will say [is] she is in good keeping. It will be wrong to say where she is for security reasons," investigating officer Brigadier Gops Govender told journalists on Monday.
