Photos: Hrithik Roshan goes on evening bicycle ride on the streets of Mumbai

11 Nov 2019, 16:34 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Hrithik Roshan is currently enjoying the success of his two back to back hits at the box office. Along with being a fantastic actor, Hrithik has also been a dance and fitness icon for a lot of people out there.

He often shares pictures and videos from his workout session on his social media accounts. And today, the actor was snapped by the paparazzi while enjoying a cycle ride in the city. For his night cycling session, the actor was in a black hoodie teamed with black and blue joggers and shoes. He was also sporting spectacles during the ride.
