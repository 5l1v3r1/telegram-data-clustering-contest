Bar doorman shot employee dead

11 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Itchie G. Cabayan

A DOORMAN of a KTV bar in Malate, Manila is now behind bars after shooting to death the other night an employee for still unknown reason.

Ceasar Caballero, of 2193 Barangay 705, Zone 77, San Andres, Manila, died instantly due to multiple gunshot wounds while the suspect who surrendered to the police was identified as Roman Rey Alvarado, 24, doorman of Jerr KTV Bar, at 511 Alonzo Street, Malate.

A report from the Ermita Police Station 5 of the Manila Police District (MPD) said that the incident took place at around 9 p.m. at the basement of Jerr building located at No. 511 Alonzo Street.

Witness Mario Cruz Micolub, 26, a delivery man and resident also ofÂ  Alonzo Street, was said to be sleeping when he was startled by gunshots.

Immediately, he checked where the shots came from and saw the victim lying on a pool of his own blood, while the suspect fled on foot.

Lights and sound operator Ronnie Coratcho meantime reported the incident to the police who conducted a follow-up operation.

The suspect surrendered at aroud 1 a.m.
