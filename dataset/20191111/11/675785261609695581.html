Loose Women's Saira Khan poses completely nude and vows 'she's not ashamed'

The Loose Women host has found a new lease of confidence ahead of her 50th birthday

11 Nov 2019, 11:42 by Lucy Needham, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

Loose Women's Saira Khan stripped off for a completely nude photoshoot as she revealed she's 'no longer ashamed' of her body.

The 49-year-old mum said that she made the decision to bare all in attempt to breakdown body image taboos that she was brought up believing.

"All my life I've felt bad about my body," admitted Saira, who spoke about not feeing 'tall, slim or pretty enough' in a candid interview with OK! magazine.

Ahead of her 50th birthday, Saira shed her inhibitions and proudly admitted she was finally confident in her own skin.

"My body no longer makes me feel guilty, it gives me pride and strength," said Saira, who wore nothing but a strategically placed white sheet for the shoot.

Saira - who first shot to fame on the Apprentice - grew up in the Midlands with Pakistani immigrant parents.

She said she felt pressure to keep to strict rules regarding her regarding behaviour and appearance.

"I had to wear loose clothes that covered my body. It gave me the message that men can’t control their sexual urges," she explained.

Saira said she still feels like she has disappointed her mother.

"She won’t like me wearing lingerie," she admitted. "She really cares about what other people think, but I'm a modern woman and I want to celebrate a time when I finally feel great about myself, it's taken a long time to get here so I'm really proud of this shoot."

Saira also revealed that she'd kept her nude shoot a secret from her Loose Women co-stars, but said she felt certain they'd be proud of her decision.

The Loose women have been public advocates of body-confidence in the past.

In 2017 Saira, along with her pals including Coleen Nolan and Stacey Solomon stripped down to her underwear and posed without any filters or airbrushing for a body positive campaign.
