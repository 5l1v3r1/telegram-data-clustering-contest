Did you know that Tara Sutaria was body shamed after she starred in ‘Student Of The Year 2’?

11 Nov 2019, 11:28 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Tara Sutaria made her big Bollywood debut with Punit Malhotra’s ‘Student Of The Year 2’ along with Ananya Panday and Tiger Shroff. The actress received mixed reviews for her performance in the movie. However, did you know that Tara was also body shamed after she starred in ‘SOTY 2’? Yes, you read it right! Speaking about the most obnoxious criticism she had faced, Tara reportedly said that people do say hurtful things every now and then. She revealed that some people called her anorexic when she did ‘SOTY 2’. She also added that when she put on 2-3 kilos, they called her overweight.

Elaborating further, she reportedly stated that people are never going to be satisfied and they pull you down. She also confessed that it really bothered her earlier.

Tara is all ready for her second film. She will next be seen in Milap Zaveri’s ‘Marjaavaan’. The film also stars ‘Ek Villain’ duo Sidhath Malhotra and Riteish Deshmukh in lead roles along with Rakul Preet Singh.

The film will hit the theatres on November 22, 2019.
