Italian restaurant and bar Bottega opens in Dubai International Airport

Tuck into contemporary Italian dishes before your flight at DXB

11 Nov 2019, 11:00

As the world’s busiest airport by international passenger traffic, Dubai International Airport (DXB) certainly has a lot of mouths to feed. In fact, almost 90 million of them in 2018 alone.

Thankfully, DXB isn’t short of stellar restaurants, cafés and bars, and now Italian restaurant and bar Bottega joins the list. From a family renowned for its production of Italian bubbly, the restaurant promises a whole range of eats and drinks.

Open from today (Friday November 11), pull up a seat at Bottega and try some of the most sophisticated bubbly in the world, among 21 unique beverage options that use Bottega produce.

Dishes meanwhile include creamy risotto balls filled with spinach, ricotta and provola cheese, deep-fried crumbed green olives stuffed with mozzarella (be still, our beating hearts), and fresh sourdough topped with sweet cherry tomatoes, garlic and basil.

If you’re in search of something heartier, you’ll find refuge in the restaurant’s bowls of linguine and freshly fired pizzas. Dreamy.

Get ready to say ‘bonjourno’ to great food this winter.

And if you’re looking for a little light reading material while you enjoy a drink or meal, don’t forget you can pick up a copy of Time Out Dubai’s free sister title, Time Out DXB, at locations throughout Dubai International Airport. The 80-page monthly magazine is a guide to making the most of your time in the airport, plus all the best things to do in Dubai.

(And then use the pages to wipe those delicious juices off your chin. We’ve all been there.)
Open now. Concourse A, Terminal 3, Dubai International Airport.
