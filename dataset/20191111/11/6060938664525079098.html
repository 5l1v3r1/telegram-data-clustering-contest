Bigil Box Office collection update: Vijay's sports drama collects Rs 300 crore in 17 days!

11 Nov 2019, 11:45 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Vijay’s 'Bigil', that released on October 25 on the festive occasion of Diwali, has earned Rs 300 crore worldwide in 17 days. The film took box office by storm and Vijay has emerged as the most bankable and successful star in the Tamil cinema industry.

Fans are busy celebrating this massive victory with #BIGILHits300CrsInWW trending on social media. Initially, it was believed that the film might face some loss for distributors in Tamil Nadu since they invested a huge amount in it. However, as the film started to collect big numbers at the box office, opinion changed and ‘Bigil’ has turned out to be a profitable venture for everyone.

'Bigil' is written and directed by Atlee and features Vijay, Nayanthara, Jackie Shroff, Vivek, Kathir. The music of the film is composed by AR Rahman and produced by AGS Entertainment.
