2 students, trike driver nabbed in drug busts

11 Nov 2019, 11:15 by Alfred P. Dalizon

TWO students and a tricycle driver on Sunday were arrested in separate anti-narcotics operations in Cagayan province, Police Regional Office 2 director Brig. Gen. Angelito Casimiro said yesterday.

One of the suspects, Nicole Ace Battung, a 21-year-old college student from Bgy. Centro North East in Solana town, was arrested shortly after he soldÂ  illegal drugs to an undercover officer in Bgy. Calamagui at 6:30 p.m. on Sunday.

Recovered from the suspect were one heat-sealed plastic sachet containing shabu, a marked P1,000 bill, a cellphone he used in making contact with the undercover officer, and a motorcycle he used to transport the drugs.

The second suspect, Eugene Contillo, 24, a Grade 11 student from Centro Norte in Gattaran municipality, was arrested after he sold marijuana leaves to a poseur.
Contillo yielded seven plastic sachets containing dried marijuana leaves and a marked P500 bill.

The third suspect identified as Vergel Marcos, 30, a tricycle driver from Bgy. Aguiguican in Gattaran town, yielded two plastic sachets containing suspected shabu, a marked P500 bill, and a tricycle during the buy-bust.

All suspects are facing non-bailable charges for sale and possession of dangerous drugs under Republic Act 9165 or the Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002.
