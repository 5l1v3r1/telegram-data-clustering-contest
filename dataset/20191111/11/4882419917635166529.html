Messi vs. Lewandowski: the fight to be the top goal scorer in 2019

The Barcelona and Bayern stars are leading the way as we near the end of the year

11 Nov 2019, 11:52 by sport.es

Lionel Messi's hat-trick against Celta Vigo at the weekend reaffirmed his place among the top three goal scorers in 2019 as we near the end of the calendar year.

Messi, who missed several games earlier this season due to injury, has now scored 39 goals in 2019, two fewer than Bayern Munich frontman Robert Lewandowski, who leads the way with 41.
