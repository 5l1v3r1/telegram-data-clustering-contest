Cops solve CdO pawnshop heist

11 Nov 2019, 11:18 by Alfred P. Dalizon

POLICE Regional Office 10 director Brig. Gen. Rolando Anduyan yesterday announced the solution of a a multi-million peso theft at a Palawan Pawnshop branch in Cagayan de Oro City following the arrest of a former manager of the establishment who owned up to the crime.

Anduyan said that suspect John Paul Cruz, a 26-year-old former branch manager of the Palawan Pawnshop in Carmen, Cagayan de Oro City, yielded P319,000 in cash and assorted jewelry worth nearly P8 million.

Members of the Cagayan de Oro City Police Station 4 led by Maj. Ian Borinaga arrested the suspect in a follow-up operation in Westgate Subdivision in Bgy. Lumbia at 7 p.m. on Sunday.

Cagayan de Oro City Police Office director Col. Henry Dampal said that the suspect will be facing a theft case.
