Wattam releases December 17, 2019 (for real this time!)

Hands Across America

11 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Jordan Devore

We love Katamari Damacy, and I'm confident we'll also love Keita Takahashi's latest oddball, Wattam.

It's been such a long time coming, but the friend-making puzzle-adventure game is about to launch. Look for it to land on PlayStation 4 and PC (via the Epic Games Store) on December 17, 2019.

"Six years constipation has been relieved finally," the creator said.
