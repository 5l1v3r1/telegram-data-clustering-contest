Two PMC Bank auditors held for flouting norms

11 Nov 2019, 21:54 by Mateen Hafeez

Highlights

  • The Economic Offences Wing (EOW) of Mumbai Police is probing the Punjab and Maharashtra Cooperative Bank’s Rs 6,500- crore loan fraud
  • 'We have arrested them and a probe is on,' EOW chief Rajvardhan Sinha said

MUMBAI: The Economic Offences Wing (EOW) of Mumbai Police, probing the Punjab and Maharashtra Cooperative Bank’s Rs 6,500- crore loan fraud, arrested two auditors of the bank on Monday for negligence and flouting norms in the audit process.

“We have arrested them and a probe is on,” EOW chief Rajvardhan Sinha said. He said the two chartered accountants, Jayesh Shah and Ketan Lakdawala, would be produced before the Esplanade court for remand on Wednesday.

The number of people arrested in the scam has now gone up to seven.
