Origin Access subscribers won’t get to play Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order early

11 Nov 2019, 21:24 by Ash

Ahead of the game’s launch on November 15th, Origin has revealed that Origin Access members won’t get early access to Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order in order to “reduce the risk of spoilers.”

That’s not to say that Origin Access members won’t get anything, though. Origin Access Basic subscribers can get 10% off the game while Origin Access Premier subscribers will have the Deluxe Edition included in their membership. All Origin Access members will also get some exclusive in-game cosmetics.
