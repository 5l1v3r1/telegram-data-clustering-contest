Khalid bin Salman Discusses Counter-Terrorism Efforts with Omani Officials

11 Nov 2019, 18:00

Saudi Deputy Defense Minister Prince Khalid bin Salman held talks in Muscat on Monday with Oman’s minister in charge of defense affairs Badr bin Saud bin Harib Al-Busaeedi.

Discussions focused on bilateral relations and efforts to protect their common border.

They also tackled efforts to counter terrorism and extremism and ways to bolster the current military cooperation between their countries.

Prince Khalid also met with Minister for Sultan's Bureau Affairs General Sultan bin Mohammed Al-Numani.

The Saudi minister had arrived in Oman earlier on Monday on an official visit.
