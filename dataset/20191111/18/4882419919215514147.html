Busquets: Playing against Atletico is always difficult

The Barca midfielder spoke after the Spanish Super Cup draw

11 Nov 2019, 18:55 by Sport EN

Sergio Busquets made a small analysis of the Spanish Super Cup draw that pitted Barca against Atletico Madrid on Jan 9 in Saudi Arabia, at the King Abdullah Yeda stadium.

Busquets, one of the Barca captains, said: "Playing against Atletico is always difficult because they have great players and a great coach. They're a really competitive team that always give you problems."

He was sad that Barca play in the second semi-final. "We have a day lest of rest before the final, but that's how the tournament is. We hope to compete to the max to win the tournament."
