Take stock of your investments in less than 10 minutes with new show from MarketWatch and Barron’s

11 Nov 2019, 18:22 by MarketWatch

Barron’s and MarketWatch have teamed up to break down the most important business and financial news for investors everywhere.

Every Monday before the opening bell our “MarketBrief” will give investors the information they need to gain an edge in their financial lives.

Our editors and reporters tackle the big financial and economic stories of the week, in order to help viewers make sense of what the news means for their money.

The series, which is produced at Nasdaq’s MarketSite in New York, cuts through the noise and breaks down the most important developments for investors of all levels — from beginners to billionaires.
