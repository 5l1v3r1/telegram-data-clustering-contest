Huawei EMUI 10 stable update hits Huawei P30, and P30 Pro
4.8 (95%) 4 votes

Earlier this month, Huawei revealed an updated list of  Huawei smartphones that will receive its EMUI 10 stable update. Per the updated list, a number of Huawei flagships are due to get the update by the end of this month with the Huawei P30 and P30 Pro being the first on the list.

Huawei appears right on target, as the firm is at the moment now rolling out the EMUI 0 stable update to the Huawei P30, and P30 Pro flagship duo. The update is currently available only in China and is available to both regular, and beta testers. For regular users, the update takes your device from version 9.1.0.26 to 10.0.0.166, while the beta version gets updated from 10.0.0.159 to 10.0.0.166. It’s about 1GB for those on Beta, while it weighs as much as 5GB for regular users.

EMUI 10 stable update for Global variants?

Global units of the P30 and P30 Pro are yet to start getting the EMUI10 stable update, though we expect that to happen pretty soon. If you own a unit of the Huawei P30 or P30 Pro, you can head to HiCare App>>Services>>[Upgrade]  to check for the update.

What’s new?

The Huawei P30 and P30 Pro EMUI 10 stable update brings a number of new features,  including the dark mode, magazine-style UI layout, Morandi UI color system, and lots more.
