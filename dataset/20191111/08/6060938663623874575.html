Priyanka Chopra Jonas’s pretty selfie gets a cute response from Nick Jonas

11 Nov 2019, 08:56 by TNN

Their first wedding anniversary is just around the corner and Priyanka Chopra Jonas and Nick Jonas continue to give us couple goals. The couple, who tied the knot on December 1 last year, are active on social media and regularly post pictures. PeeCee, who was last seen on the big screen in Shonali Bose’s ‘The Sky Is Pink’ also starring Farhan Akhtar, Rohit Saraf and Zaira Wasim, is currently shooting in India. And with her better half all the way back in USA, it’s natural for the couple to miss each other. Priyanka, who posted a pretty selfie of herself on Instagram, was quickly inundated with comments and the one, which stood out, was from Nick, so wrote, ‘So beautiful’.

In the picture, PeeCee is wearing an aquamarine top and simple hoop earrings with minimal make-up. She captioned the picture, ‘The wound is the place where the Light enters you.” – Rumi #setlife #musings.’

Apart from her work commitments, the actress is also spending quality time with family. She also shared a picture of some of her relatives where she is seen wearing a black shirt and skirt with knee-high boots. In a recent interview, Nick was asked what special plans he had for their first anniversary, but refused to divulge any details because he wanted to surprise his wife.
