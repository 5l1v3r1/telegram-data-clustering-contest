Chiefs Midfielder Receives Bafana Call-Up

11 Nov 2019, 08:50 by Soccer Laduma

The South African Football Association has confirmed that Themba Zwane has been withdrawn from the Bafana Bafana set-up with a Kaizer Chiefs midfielder being called up in his place.

The South African national team assembled for camp on Sunday evening as they prepare for their upcoming Africa Cup Of Nations qualifiers against Ghana and Sudan.

SAFA confirmed that one player has been withdrawn from the set-up, with Lebogang Manyama being called up in his place.

“Kaizer Chiefs midfielder Lebogang Manyama has been called up to Bafana Bafana for the two crucial Cameroon 2021 Africa Cup of Nations clashes against Ghana and Sudan.”

“Manyama, who has already reported for national team duty, replaces the injured Themba Zwane of Mamelodi Sundowns,” SAFA confirmed.
