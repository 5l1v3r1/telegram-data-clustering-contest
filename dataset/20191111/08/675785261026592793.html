Kim Kardashian stuns on People's Choice Awards red carpet with momager and sisters

Keeping Up With The Kardashians star Kim was joined by Khloe, Kourtney and Kris Jenner at the People's Choice Awards, where Khloe was named Reality Star of the year

11 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Vicki Newman, Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

The People's Choice Awards was all about the Kardashians.

The famous family stole the limelight - as always - as they posed up together on the red carpet.

Kim Kardashian stunned as she was joined by her sisters Khloe and Kourtney and their momager Kris Jenner.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians stars grabbed all the attention as they posed up together at the awards bash, where Khloe was named Reality Star of the year.

Kim looked sensational in a figure-hugging snakeprint dress that accentuated her hourglass figure.

Khloe showed off her enviable figure in a sheer black dress with a dramatic thigh-split and Kourtney rocked a glitzy black bejewelled trouser suit with an oversized blazer on top of a bralet.

Kris oozed sophistication in a black trouser suit with a camisole underneath and rhinestone detailing on her jacket.

The family also won Reality Show of the year for their series.

A host of other celebs stood out on the red carpet too.

Singer Gwen Stefani, on the arm of her man Blake Shelton, was receiving a Fashion Icon Award - she was determined to prove she's the right choice, opting for a statement Vera Wang dress with suede knee highs to complete her ensemble.

Zendaya, meanwhile, went for a more understated and classic, but no less fabulous, look of a black cropped gown with silver straps.

Modern Family star Sarah Hyland added a splash of colour with an orange, black and gold Fausto Puglisi dress with a high thigh slit.

Singer Pink walked the carpet with the carpet with husband Carey Hart and their two kids, Jameson and Willow.

The So What hitmaker posed in a long coat with a see-through bodice underneath.

Channing Tatum's ex Jenna Dewan showed off her baby bump in a red Monique Lhuillier gown, and paired it with classic silver jewellery.

Riverdale heart throbs KJ Apa and Cole Sprouse also went big on colour, with the hunky New Zealander going for a flashy pink suit while Cole went for a more traditional blue.

Friends legend Jennifer Aniston looked the picture of sophistication in a black gown, while the event marked a return to the public eye for comedian Kevin Hart.

It's the comedians first official appearance following a near fatal car crash two months ago that fractured his spine in three spots and hospitalised him for ten days.

While Kevin's look was relaxed and casual, his wife Eniko wore a fabulous black, white and gold dress, pairing it with some black heels.

It was a glam night for social media star Tana Mongeau, who's sparkly red dress turned a few heads.

Scottish actress Karen Gillan chose a ruffled white dress, while the Stranger Things kids carried over their show's eighties style with some seriously retro outfits.
