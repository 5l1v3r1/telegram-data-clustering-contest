Amazon is selling a heated blanket for your car to help motorists stay warm this winter

The handy electrically-heated throw plugs into your car's cigarette lighter and provides three levels of warmth

11 Nov 2019, 08:55 by Linda Howard

Winter is coming.

We don’t need weather forecasters to tell us that, the sharp drop in temperatures, wet and windy conditions and the extra layers are enough of a clue for everyone.

And it’s also the time for motorists to start planning ahead to worsening conditions and the potential for delayed journeys.

Despite the best efforts of gritters up and down the land, Scottish weather is unpredictable and every year we read about delayed journeys and motorists getting stranded.

So it comes as no surprise to find that Amazon is selling heated blankets made specifically for your car  to make winter travel a bit more comfortable.

 

The blanket is designed specifically for your car - the cord plugs directly into the cigarette lighter instead of a traditional outlet.

The Sojoy Auto's electric car blanket costs between £29.99 and £39.99, includes free delivery and comes with an eight-foot cord making it comfortable and easy for backseat passengers to snuggle up on long road trips.

Get the Sojoy Auto's electric car blanket here

Amazon states the blanket works in any car, truck or RV that has a cigarette lighter and starts warming up as soon as you plug it in.

And the fleece material keeps the blanket warm long after you unplug it, making it perfect for outdoor events in the colder months.
