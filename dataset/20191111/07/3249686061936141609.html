KBS moves 'Battle Trip' time slot

11 Nov 2019, 07:10 by jennywill

'Battle Trip' will be changing its time slot.

Currently, the KBS 2TV variety show airs on Saturday nights. However, starting on December 6th, the show will air instead at 8:30 PM KST on Friday nights. This is part of KBS's overhaul of their variety show timing as they revamp the new season of '1 Night 2 Days'.
