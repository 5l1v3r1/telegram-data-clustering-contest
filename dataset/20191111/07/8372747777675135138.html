Missing endangered man in North Fort Myers located

11 Nov 2019, 07:12 by WFTX Digital Team

UPDATE (7:45am): The Lee County Sheriff's Office reports that Getts has been located, but have not confirmed his condition with Fox 4.

However, members of Getts' family have posted to social media saying that he has passed away.
--------------------------
LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- Lee County deputies need your help finding a missing endangered man.

70-year-old Johnny Getts was last seen leaving his home on Littleton Road in North Fort Myers on foot.

Investigators say he has medical conditions and has made recent threats to harm himself.

His family is concerned about his safety, so if you have any information on where he is, please call the Lee County Sheriff's Office at 239-477-1000.
