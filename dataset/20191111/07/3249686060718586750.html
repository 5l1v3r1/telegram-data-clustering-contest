GOT7's Jinyoung, Heo Kyung Hwan, Choi Ri, and Seo Eun Soo to be on 'Running Man'

11 Nov 2019, 07:51 by jennywill

GOT7's Jinyoung, comedian Heo Kyung Hwan, and actresses Choi Ri and Seo Eun Soo will be on 'Running Man'.

The four of them are filming the episode right now on the 11th, and they'll be filming the ghost story episode of the variety show.

The episode will air on the 17th.
