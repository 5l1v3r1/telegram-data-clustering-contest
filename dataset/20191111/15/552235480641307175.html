Big-hearted Creetown residents organising party to make sure no one spends Christmas alone

Volunteers are sacrificing their big day to host a party in Creetown Youth Club Hall

11 Nov 2019, 15:41 by Stuart Gillespie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/stuart-gillespie/

Big-hearted Creetown residents have pledged that no-one living locally will spend Christmas on their own.

And to make that happen volunteers are sacrificing their big day to host a party in the village.

An unofficial committee has been formed to run the event – which will be free to everyone attending.

Founders Eddie McGaw, Val Nichol, Evelene McClymont and Donna Alexander have already been inundated with cash and gifts.

Evelene said: “This is for everybody that’s on our own – no matter what their age.

“We want to help because you don’t want anybody to be lonely.

“We dont yet know what kind of numbers we will get but even if we get five or 10 people at least we will have made a difference for some folk.”

Evelene, 61 and friend Val, 59, will be cooking turkey and all the trimmings at home.

 

The festive fare will then be taken piping hot up to Creetown Youth Club Hall.

And from noon to 3pm drinks, a sing-a-long, bingo and music will also be laid on.

Evelene reckons putting off family celebrations is a small price to pay.

“We can have dinner at night when we get home,” she said.

“This is something for folk to look forward to instead of sitting on their own at Christmas.

“Transport will be laid on for folk if anyone needs it – they don’t have to worry about going.”

 

The party committee has already met twice and will gather again on December 5 – the deadline for names to be submitted.

Several other volunteers have come on board since the group was formed.

Val, 59, said: “We are asking local businesses for help. Some and have already given us raffle prizes and donations.

“People have just been handing us money in the street. Everybody has been absolutely wonderful.”

She added: “If this benefits other folk that’s what matter most. We know we all have families to come home to.”

Anyone from Creetown or Carsluith who would like to attend the party – or knows someone who would – can contact Eddie on 07967 267850, Val on 07552 560429 or Evelene 07975 798801 by December 5.

Donations can be made via the same numbers.
