Two Clubs Interested In Leopards’ Gyimah  

11 Nov 2019, 15:33 by Soccer Laduma

Following his impressive start with Black Leopards, Edwin Gyimah is attracting interest from other PSL clubs. 

The former Orlando Pirates player has at least two clubs keen on his services, according to sources.

The Ghanaian was in the starting line-up in both matches against SuperSport United and Cape Town City and did well. 

Polokwane City and Baroka FC are believed to be keen on the 28-year-old, who joined Lidoda Duvha on a short-term contract last month.

“Polokwane City and Baroka FC are interested in Edwin Gyimah. Both clubs are following the player’s progress at Black Leopards and are keen on him,” said the source.

Gyimah’s contract with Leopards will expire in June.
