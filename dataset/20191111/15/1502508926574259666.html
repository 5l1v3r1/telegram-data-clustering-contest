Uranium particles detected at an undeclared site in Iran, says IAEA

Uranium particles were detected at an undeclared site in Iran

11 Nov 2019, 15:50 by AFP | PTI

The United Nations' nuclear watchdog said it had detected uranium particles at an undeclared site in Iran in its latest report on the country's nuclear programme issued on Monday.

The report from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), seen by AFP, says: "The agency detected natural uranium particles of anthropogenic origin at a location in Iran not declared to the agency.
