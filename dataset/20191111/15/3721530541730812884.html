Every movie and show arriving to Disney Plus on day 1

Share this story

11 Nov 2019, 15:13 by Matt Patches

Classic animated films from the Disney renaissance. Every Star Wars movie since the 1977 original. A smattering of Marvel’s MCU collection, with a promise for more. A dizzying amount of Disney Channel Original Movies. James Cameron’s Avatar, a prelude to James Cameron’s Avatar 2, Avatar 3, and Avatar 4. Loads of National Geographic documentaries. The movie Fuzzbucket.

Disney Plus is here, sporting a $6.99-a-month price tag and more than 600-some titles, old and new. For fans of the company’s animated short films and live-action adventures, long locked in the “vault,” and for anyone looking for more Forky from Toy Story and a movie where Anna Kendrick plays Santa, Disney’s Netflix competitor has something for almost everyone. (If you’re looking for Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice, you’ll have to sign up for HBO Max next year, sorry.)

To get your journey into Disney nostalgia started, Polygon compiled and collated everything coming to the platform. Here’s what’s lurking in the depths of the service.
