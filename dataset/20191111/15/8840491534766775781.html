Greater garda input sought for Mayo CCTV system

11 Nov 2019, 15:47

A MEMBER of Mayo County Council has highlighted the value of CCTV in Ballinrobe since it was installed in 2011.

Councillor Michael Burke said it has proved very successful in tackling anti-social behaviour and other issues.

Such has been its success, the community invested about €40,000 in maintaining and upgrading it over the past eight years.

However, it is time for the garda authorities to take a bigger responsibility and not ask a community to sustain it, added Councillor Burke.
