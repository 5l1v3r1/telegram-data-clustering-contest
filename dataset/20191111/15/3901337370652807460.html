Khune Takes Free Kicks Like CR7- Nkosi

11 Nov 2019, 15:42 by SL Social

A former Kaizer Chiefs teammate has heaped high praise on Itumeleng Khune’s free kick-taking ability, likening it to that of Portuguese superstar Cristiano Ronaldo.

Siyabonga Nkosi did not hold back when it came to showering Khune with praise while working as a pundit in the Mamelodi Sundowns Vs Golden Arrows match.

The former Chiefs star spoke highly of Khune’s free-kick prowess, likening him to the great CR7.

“He takes the best free kicks you know. I always encouraged him to take them. Out of ten he would score seven in the top corner, the Cristiano Ronaldo type of shots,” the 38-year-old can be heard saying.
