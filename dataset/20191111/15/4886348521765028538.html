OceanEx Launches the Second Selective IEO Project – SNKr

11 Nov 2019, 15:01

OceanEx, an AI powered digital asset trading platform with a large number of global users, unveiled their second selective IEO project, SNKr, today, along with their token ‘SNK’. 

The launch of “SNKr” comes two months after the announcement of OceanEx’s first selected IEO project, “JUR”. OceanEx GO!, the selective IEO platform of OceanEx, has a rigorous project selection process with strict requirements and principles such as project maturity and preparation, community and team background review. It aims to explore and discover first-class technical background and real-world application. OceanEx focuses on projects that are currently undiscovered but have high potential in the near future.

OceanEx’s second selected IEO project ‘SNKr’ is a digital asset targeting young people worldwide. By connecting real fans with real products, the project helping brands, consumers, and commerce platforms recognize fake goods through the use of IoT and blockchain technology. With their blockchain-powered applications, the project allows brands to reward contributions from consumers through SNKr’s token, ‘SNK’, which allows buyers to shop with confidence. This creates a system that rewards creativity, originality, authenticity and active participation.

There is a total supply of 2 billion SNK, including allocations for contribution from mining, development and operation, marketing, investors, founding teams and foundations.

Founding Team and Superior Strategic Cooperation Partners

“It is a great honor to work with the OceanEx team because of the professionalism and safety of OceanEx”, said George Yang, co-founder and CEO of SWELL

Yang confirms that SneakerCoin Foundation was initiated by SWELL and it helped launch the SNKr street culture ecosystem project. Starting with KPMG, George has been working with Private Equity and Venture Capital firms for over 10 years. His deep understanding of finance and his unique background in the streetwear industry gives SneakerCoin a tremendous advantage in their mission to disrupt the future of fashion.

Theresa Zheng, the co-founder and CMO of SWELL, has held positions at major fashion companies including Louis Vuitton China which she spent over 10 years and is now an in-demand marketing strategy consultant and oversees a number of international brands and social media platforms.

VeChain is a strategic partner of SNKr and provides the underlying blockchain technology development and product service support for the project with application scenarios and plans for future development. Their common goal is to build a trusted distributed business ecosystem with information transparency, efficient collaboration, and high-speed transmission of value, and to facilitate application of blockchain technology in the mass market. 

SNKr’s public token sale officially starts on November 20th, 2019. For more information, visit OceanEx’s official website.
