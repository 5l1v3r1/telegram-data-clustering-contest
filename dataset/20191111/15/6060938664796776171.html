Ajay Devgn will be next seen in 'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior' which will be his 100th film. A lot of Bollywood actor congratulated him for this achieving this feat in the industry.

And now, the actor in an exclusive conversation with Filmfare announced that he is in plans to make the remake of his first film, 'Phool Aur Kaante'. He said, "I want to remake my first film, Phool Aur Kaante (1991). I’ve thought of a new way of presenting it in keeping with the changing times. I will co-produce it with someone. I’m looking for a fresh face for that. The approach will be different but the emotional texture will be retained."
