Juve Star Comments On Ronaldo's Angry Reaction

11 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Kurt Buckerfield

After an angry Cristiano Ronaldo stormed down the tunnel after being substituted during Juventus' win over AC Milan on Sunday night, one of his teammates has since addressed his furious reaction.

Click through the gallery above to see some images of Ronaldo's response to being taken off on the weekend.

The Portuguese superstar was substituted off in the 55th minute of the Bianconeri's 1-0 Serie A victory over Milan on Sunday, a decision from manager Maurizio Sarri that prompted an enraged response from the 34-year-old, who walked straight down the tunnel after leaving the pitch.

After the match, goalkeeper Wojciech Szczesny seemed to show his support for the former Real Madrid striker, before adding that Ronaldo is currently not at his physical best.

"When a player of this level is replaced after 50 minutes, it is normal that he gets angry," the Poland goalkeeper told Sky Sports.

"He is a great player, but now he is not well physically. His quality must not be doubted at all because, when the decisive moments of the season arrive, he will surely give us a big hand as always."

Juventus manager Sarri also discussed the issue after the game, and also appeared to hint at Ronaldo not being fully fit.
