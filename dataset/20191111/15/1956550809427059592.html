Drai’s Dubai reveals mega line-up this weekend

Get ready for two nights of star-studded performances

11 Nov 2019, 15:57 by Reema Rahman

Is it ever too early to start planning the weekend? No, is the correct answer to that.

Time Out Dubai's Club of the Year from the Time Out Dubai Music & Nightlife Awards 2019 has just revealed a star-studded line-up for this coming weekend.

On Thursday November 14, Drai’s Dubai will be hosting Quavo & Takeoff from hip-hop group Migos for a special edition of its ladies’ night.

Don’t tire yourself out from dancing the night away as the following night (Friday November 15) will see Davido take to the stage. The Nigerian singer-songwriter most popular hit Fall already has over 150 million views on YouTube and counting.

For both nights, ladies get free entry as well as free-flowing drinks until 1am and gents can get in on the action for Dhs200 which includes two drinks with tickets available via Virgin Megastore.
