Photos: Shahid Kapoor sharpens his batting skills as he preps for his next 'Jersey'

11 Nov 2019, 20:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shahid Kapoor recently shared the first look of his upcoming film on his social media accounts. He will be seen playing the role of a cricketer in the film. The look received a lot of appreciation from all the quarters.

The actor has started his prep for the film already and today he was snapped while he was honing his cricketing skills. He was sporting a full cricket gear while paparazzi clicked his pictures practising at the ground.

Talking about the film, Shahid said in a statement to IANS, "It took me some time to decide what to do next after 'Kabir Singh'. But the minute I saw 'Jersey', I knew I wanted it to be my next. It is a wonderfully inspiring and personal human journey that I connected with deeply."

Meanwhile, 'Jersey' will be directed by Gowtam Tinnanauri who helmed the original Telugu film as well. Produced by Allu Aravind, Aman Gill, Dil Raju, the film is scheduled to release on August 28, 2020.

He was last seen in Sandeep Vanga's 'Kabir Singh' co-starring Kiara Advani. The film scored a huge at the box office.
