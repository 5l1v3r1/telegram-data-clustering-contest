'The Nightmare Before Christmas' & Vans Unveil New Sk8-Hi "Christmas Town"

Getting in the holiday spirit early.

20 Nov 2019, 17:53 by Ross Dwyer

The Nightmare Before Christmas and Vans have teamed up for the Sk8-Hi “Christmas Town,” following up the extensive collaborative capsule that released in October. A special nod to one of the film’s most memorable moments, the “Christmas Town” features a highly-detailed design, replete with prints, patterns and embellishments.

Both shoes nod to main character Jack Skellington’s discovery of the Holiday Doors while exploring the Holiday Woods. The left foot’s lateral side shows Jack’s wonderment with Christmas lights, while the right foot’s lateral side depicts his memorable moment with the snowman. Both of these printed details sit on top of a blue canvas base. A large Christmas tree graphic is also present on the black suede heels.

The detailing doesn’t end there. Each shoe features a red suede toebox, black suede eyestays and overlays, gold metal eyelets, and a red and black checkered print on the laces. Down below, a white midsole with red foxing and a classic vulcanized gum outsole complete the look.

Look for this The Nightmare Before Christmas x Vans Sk8-Hi to release on November 22 at Vans.com and Vans retail outposts in both men’s and toddler sizes.

In true Vans fashion, this isn’t the only collaboration the brand has on the way — it recently linked with frequent partner BILLY’s Tokyo for an exclusive OG Classic Slip-On LX with a fur liner.
