Borderlands 3's first DLC Sends You to Handsome Jack's Space Casino

20 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Alistair Jones

Borderlands 3's first DLC arrives next month. Unveiled earlier today on The Borderlands Show, Moxxi's Heist of the Handsome Jackpot takes the Vault Hunters to a space casino formerly run by Borderlands 2 baddie Handsome Jack in an attempt to reclaim both the station and the untold riches within.

As is to be expected for Borderlands DLC, the new pack comes with its own mini-campaign, as well as new baddies, boss fights, and loot. The casino is mostly filled with Hyperion bots and crazed bandits, but I did spot something that looked a lot like an angry slot machine at one point in the trailer, which you can check out below.
