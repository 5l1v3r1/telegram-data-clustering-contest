NYPD investigating death of Harlem woman found unconscious on street

20 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Anabel Sosa

A woman died eight days after she was found unconscious on a Harlem street just blocks away from her home –– and police are now investigating it as a homicide, authorities said on Wednesday.

Renee Campbell, 31, was found unresponsive on the corner of 127th Street between Malcolm X Boulevard and Adam Clayton Powell on Wednesday, Oct. 2 around 9:30 p.m., police said.

Campbell was taken to NYC Health and Hospitals/Harlem where she died on October 10, police said.

Cops have responded to domestic incidents at her home about eight blocks away before, according to police sources.
