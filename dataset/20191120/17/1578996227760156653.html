Gordo Will Return in the Lizzie McGuire Reboot

20 Nov 2019, 17:10 by Savannah Walsh

Consider our Disney+ subscriptions purchased. The new streaming service, which launched November 12, confirmed today that Adam Lamberg will return to the reboot of Lizzie McGuire as Gordo. In the original series, he played Lizzie's best friend and low-key love interest, a relationship explored further in 2003's The Lizzie McGuire Movie.

"Gordo was an essential piece of the puzzle to what made the original Lizzie McGuire so authentic and beloved," Hilary Duff, who's starring in and executive producing the show, said in a statement. "I couldn't imagine the series without him. I can't wait for fans to see what he's up to 15 years later and how he fits into Lizzie's adult world."

McGuire and Lamberg also confirmed the news with a video on the official Disney+ Twitter account. Lamberg joked, "This is what dreams are made of."
