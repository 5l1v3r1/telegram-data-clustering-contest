WATCH: Police bash an elderly man at MDC office

20 Nov 2019, 17:31 by Mandla Ndlovu

Police assaulted an elderly man in Harare during the violent dispersing of MDC supporters at Harvest House in Harare on Wednesday.

A video of the elderly man has surfaced online. The man with white hair is seen with blood on his head after the assault.

MDC and other institutions have condemned the violent act.
