Prosecutor who impeached Bill Clinton says Donald Trump committed bribery

20 Nov 2019, 17:33

The prosecutor who led the impeachment effort against Bill Clinton has sensationally claimed that Donald Trump committed bribery.

Ken Starr, a former US circuit judge and US solicitor general, told Fox News: ‘We now know that the president, in fact, committed the crime of bribery.’

‘I think the articles of impeachment are being drawn up if they haven’t already been drawn up.’

Starr’s comments were the first time the famed former prosecutor indicated that there were legitimate grounds for Trump to be impeached. He had previously said there were insufficient grounds to impeach the president.

His statement came as US Ambassador to the European Union, Gordon Sondland, testified Wednesday that there was ‘quid pro quo’ when Trump called the Ukrainian president ‘for a favor.’
