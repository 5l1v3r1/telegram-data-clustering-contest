The Galleria Al Maryah Island launches F1 activities for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix

There’s lots to do before the big race

20 Nov 2019, 17:12 by Colin Armstrong

The Abu Dhabi Grand Prix is just around the corner and the city is gearing up for the busiest weekend of the year.

This weekend The Galleria Al Maryah Island is hosting a range of events and attractions to get F1 fever building ahead of the big race.

From Thursday November 21 to Saturday November 23 the shopping destination will host a kids driving school, a racing simulator and give you the chance to take a picture with a F1 racing car.

Giving little ones the chance to have some fun the kids driving school will offer children from four to ten the chance to whiz around the pop-up track on the ground floor of the mall.

Mini drivers will be given a few quick tips and safety basics before taking on the fun track in push-bike race cars.

Grown-ups can enjoy a thrill with the racing simulator where you can choose your favourite car, colour and driving method before taking to the track to set your best lap time.

A fun, active racing game will also be on hand where you can challenge your friends by racing mini cars around a track by pedalling a bicycle.

Finally a full-sized F1 racing car will also be on show at the mall for you to see what the amazing vehicles are like and snap a few pictures too.

We’re already feeling competitive about the simulator.
Free. Nov 21-23. 4pm-10pm. The Galleria Al Maryah Island www.thegalleria.ae.
