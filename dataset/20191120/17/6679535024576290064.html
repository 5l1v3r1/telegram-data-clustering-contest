Prajavani journalist killed in road accident

20 Nov 2019, 17:48 by Special Correspondent

The Haveri district correspondent of Kannada daily Prajavani, Manjunath M.C., was killed in an accident in Davangere district on Wednesday night.

Manjunath, 30, was on his way to his sister’s house at Holalkere on his motorcycle, when he was hit by a tractor near Kodaganuru on P.B. Road. According to the police, he died on the spot. A native of Shivamogga district, Manjunath had recently been transferred to Haveri from Bengaluru. He is survived by parents, brother and sister.
