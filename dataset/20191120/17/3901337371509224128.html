As far as the ex-Chelsea man’s car collection is concerned, Mourinho has taste for the exquisite and was often spotted in his Jaguar F-Pace while in England. It was also reported that Mourinho’s first car he purchased himself was a Honda Civic and while working under the tutelage of Bobby Robson at Barcelona, drove a Suzuki Vitara.
