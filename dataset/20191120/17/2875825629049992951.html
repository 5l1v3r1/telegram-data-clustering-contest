Fixtures released for the 2020 Lidl Ladies National Football League

The season begins on January 26th

20 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Donagh Corby

Reigning champions Cork will host Westmeath on the opening day of next year's Lidl Ladies National Football League.

Today, the LGFA released the fixtures, while also confirming that the top two finishers will progress straight to the Finals in each of the divisions.

The season will begin on January 26th next year, and will finish by the week ending April 19th with the final.
