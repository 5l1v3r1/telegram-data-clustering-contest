The Flash: Does Panabaker's Pregnancy Mean Killer Frost Will Have a Baby?

20 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Sam Stone

Earlier this month, news broke that longtime star of The Flash Danielle Panabaker was pregnant with her first child. With Panabaker's character Caitlin Snow, AKA the ice-powered hero Killer Frost, such a central character on the Arrowverse series, speculation has arisen how the pregnancy will impact Caitlin moving forward.

Panabaker revealed that the current plan for her character moving forward through Season 6 will be to simply hide her growing baby bump rather than make her character pregnant as well.

"I don't think Caitlin is going to have a baby. I think the intention is sort of to ignore my rapidly growing size," admitted Panabaker in an interview with Entertainment Tonight. "It's been done before and I have complete faith that it will be done again, so hopefully it won't be too obvious."

In addition to starring on The CW series, Panabaker also directed an episode from Season 5 along with Season 6's sixth episode "License to Elongate." Despite her increased role behind-the-scenes, Panabaker noted the decision where to take her character after she revealed her pregnancy was out of her hands.

"It's not up to me. It's up to the writers. I told them when I was pregnant and sort of left it up to them to make a plan of however they wanted to [handle it], and I think they have a great vision for the rest of the season," explained Panabaker. "It is nice though that Killer Frost's clothes are a little bit looser than Caitlin's were. That's for sure."

Airing Tuesdays at 8 p.m. on The CW, The Flash stars Grant Gustin, Candice Patton, Carlos Valdes, Danielle Panabaker, Tom Cavanagh, Jesse L. Martin, Danielle Nicolet and Hartley Sawyer.
