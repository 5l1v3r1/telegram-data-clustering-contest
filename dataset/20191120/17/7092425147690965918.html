Nominees for 62nd Annual Grammy Awards revealed

20 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Nick Fitzgerald

The announcements were streamed live today on The Grammy Awards social media channels

In a press conference streamed live today, the nominees for the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards have been revealed.

Esteemed singer Alicia Keys will return to host the ceremony next year.
