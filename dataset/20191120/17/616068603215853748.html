Pedestrian hit by truck

20 Nov 2019, 17:00 by John Lawless

A pedestrian was hit by truck at about 2 p.m. this afternoon at the intersection of 32 Avenue and 34 Street.

"The ambulances were on scene and the victim was conscious," said Captain Parsons. "I don't know the extent of her injuries but they weren't massive injuries or anything."

Vernon Fire and Rescue wasn't sure about the cause of the incident, but Parsons speculates visibility may have played a factor since the sun was going down and may have shone in the driver's eyes.
