The best Black Friday apps on iPhone for shopping and deals

20 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Sandy Writtenhouse

Black Friday is one of the best shopping days of the year for deals. And nowadays, you don’t have to wake up at 2AM to stand in line and fight the crowds. You can do your Black Friday shopping right from your device.

The nice thing about Black Friday apps for iPhone is that you’re already ahead of the game. You can start looking now at terrific deals and even grab some early sales.

Stay home and munch on Thanksgiving leftovers this year while you shop from your couch with these awesome Black Friday apps for iPhone and iPad.

Black Friday shopping apps

Check out these excellent apps for one-stop shopping this Black Friday.

  • Black Friday 2019 Ads
  • BFAds: Black Friday 2019 Sales
  • Black Friday 2019 Ads & Deals
  • Black Friday 2019

Black Friday 2019 Ads

Black Friday 2019 Ads gives you ads, deals, coupons, and news as handy tabs for quick browsing. When you find a deal that you want to hold onto but not purchase yet, tap to save it. Then head to the Saved tab when you’re ready to buy.

Black Friday 2019 Ads sections and features:

  • Ads: Browse the stores and how many ads they have available. Tap to view and head right to the ad on the retailer’s site.
  • Deals: Check out specials on all sorts of items from electronics to apparel to household goods. Tap to Shop Now or Save for later.
  • Coupons: View coupons available from your favorite stores like Macy’s, JCPenney, Lands End, Bealls, Target, and more.
  • News: Take a look at news regarding everything Black Friday.
  • Saved: All of your saved deals are in one place.

With the Black Friday 2019 Ads app on your device, you can find amazing deals at the stores you love to shop at, all in one spot.

  • Availability: iPhone, iPad, Android, and the web
  • Cost: Free

BFAds: Black Friday 2019 Sales

BFAds: Black Friday 2019 Sales lets you check out all ads and deals or narrow it down by just the new ones. Sign up for a free account and save Favorites that you can access with a tap.

BFAds: Black Friday 2019 Sales sections and features:

  • Ads: Select All or New and Ads or Deals at the top. Then sort or filter to narrow down the options. When you select an ad, you can jump to the page you want.
  • Stores: Shop by store and see how many ads they have available for you to browse. You’ll find every store from Apple to Walmart.
  • News: Get the latest news and featured stories that you can share right from the app.
  • Today’s Hot Deals: Browse through awesome deals each day and tap to head right to the store’s online shop to make your purchase.
  • My BFAds Account: Once you sign up for a free account, you can check out Favorites you save and receive alerts when new ads arrive.

If you prefer to head straight to the stores you love to view their ads and deals, BFAds: Black Friday 2019 Sales is the right app for you.

  • Availability: iPhone, iPad, Android, and the web
  • Cost: Free

Black Friday 2019 Ads & Deals

Black Friday 2019 Ads & Deals from BuyVia is another excellent Black Friday shopping option for iPhone. Popular stores and categories are listed across the top for quick access and you can find sales at your local shops too.

Black Friday 2019 Ads & Deals sections and features:

  • Deals: Find deals at popular stores or by category, see the Hottest Daily Deals, Top 10 Daily Deals, and much more.
  • Search: Pop in a search word or phrase to find a certain store or deal.
  • Scan: Scan a barcode or QR code to find a deal on a particular product.
  • Stores: Shop deals at your favorite stores, local or online. From 1-800-PetMeds to Zales, you have all the stores you love at your fingertips.
  • More: Sign up for a free account and save a shopping list or create alerts.

For one of the best shopping apps for Black Friday this year, with tons of deals and more, be sure to check out Black Friday 2019 Ads & Deals.

  • Availability: iPhone, iPad, Android, and the web
  • Cost: Free

Black Friday 2019

Black Friday 2019 may not have as many ads as the other apps on this list, but when it comes to finding the lowest prices on Black Friday, you can never check too many places.

Black Friday 2019 sections and features:

  • Latest Deals: Browse through sales from stores like Best Buy, Home Depot, Walmart, eBay, JCPenney, and others.
  • Black Friday Ads: Search for a store or pick one from the collection to view their ads page by page.
  • Saved Deals: Head to the Saved Deals section for all of the terrific finds you save.

You can filter the deals you see by category like computers, clothing, or home and garden. Plus you can search for something specific. So for one more app to check this year, take a look at Black Friday 2019

  • Availability: iPhone, iPad, and the web
  • Cost: Free
