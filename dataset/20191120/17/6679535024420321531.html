Tanveer Sait stable

20 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Special Correspondent

Former Minister Tanveer Sait may be shifted to the ward from ICU on Thursday as his condition has improved significantly and his vital health parameters are stable. Upendra Shenoy, Medical Superintendent, Columbia Asia Hospital, Mysuru, told The Hindu that Mr. Sait may to be shifted after a complete update on his health condition. He is taking normal diet and has started walking. Physiotherapy sessions are underway, he added. “He will remain in the ward for few more days for the wound to heal,” Dr. Shenoy said.
