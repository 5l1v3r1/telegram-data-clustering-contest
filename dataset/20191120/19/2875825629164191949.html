Dean Gaffney, Amy Hart, James Lock and Amy Childs among Celebs Go Dating line up

A look at the confirmed line up of the next series of Celebs Go Dating, and their troubled love lives

20 Nov 2019, 19:13 by Irishmirror.ie

The official line up for the next series of Celebs Go Dating has been confirmed.

The stars looking for love on E4's expert dating agency are Love Islands Amy Hart , TOWIE star Amy Childs, and EastEnders actor Dean Gaffney.

They will be joined by Hollyoaks and I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! star Malique Thomson–Dwyer, TOWIE's James Lock, who is newly single, and Made in Chelsea's Olivia Bentley.

The hopefuls will throw themselves into the dating scene, receiving advice from life coach Anna Williamson and matchmaker Paul C Brunson.

As always, junior Client Coordinator and celeb confidante Tom Read Wilson will be ready and waiting for the celebs to divulge all, offering a comforting shoulder to cry on when things go wrong and a warm, lingering embrace to help celebrate a dating triumph when things go right.

Amy Hart, 26, revealed she can't wait for the dates to begin.

"I can’t wait to walk through those famous agency doors and find out who Anna and Paul have lined up for me!", she gushed.

"I haven’t been on a proper date in ages, so I’m excited - and nervous! - to see what happens.

"I’ve been a huge fan of Celebs Go Dating for years - so to join the agency really is a dream come true, and hopefully it’ll help me find The One!”

Amy dated Curtis Pritchard in the Love Island villa, but was left broken hearted when he admitted he had feelings for another islander just as she realised she was in love with him.

Sobbing Amy quit the villa and has been single since, while Curtis moved on with Maura Higgins.

Dean Gaffney added that he was looking for true love, after a string of failed relationships.

“I’m looking for that ‘Wellard’ kind of love, you know that unbreakable force", he said.

"It’s hard to find someone that you can do nothing with, that’s what I want!"

Dean, 41, played Robbie Jackson in EastEnders on and off for 30 years, but was axed last month amid a string of embarrassing incidents for the troubled star.

It emerged he had pestered a woman ‘young enough to be his daughter’ with questions about her breasts and buttocks.

And before that, he had turned to dating apps after being dumped by his girlfriend of three years, who was 16 years younger than him.

Before Rebekah, Dean was married to childhood sweetheart Sarah Burge, who is the mother of his children.

During an appearance on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! in 2006, Dean confessed that he had cheated on Sarah.

Meanwhile James Lock, 32, recently split from Yazmin Oukhellou amid cheating rumours after she caught three girls leaving his hotel room in Turkey - one of them pulling her knickers up - on a holiday that Yazmin had actually paid for.

He then had a brief fling with Ellie Mackenzie, but it didn't last long.

Amy Childs, 29, split with boyfriend Ritchie at the start of this year.

They share a son together, and Amy also has daughter Polly, two, with whom she shares with her ex partner Bradley Wright.

Malique Thompson-Dwyer, 20, parted ways with his girlfriend Molly Redknapp, granddaughter of Harry Rednknapp, in September.

He was seen flirting with Love Island winner Amber Gill not long after.

Olivia Bentley, 24, recently split with her MIC boyfriend Digby Edgley.

Their tumultuous relationship came to an end as Digby travelled to South Africa to work for three months.
