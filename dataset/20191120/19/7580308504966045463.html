Watch: Mr Eazi Teams Up With Tyga For Latest Single “Tony Montana”

20 Nov 2019, 19:21 by Violet Johnson

Leg Over crooner, Mr Eazi collaborates with American Hip-Hop artist, Tyga on this infectious tune titled ‘Tony Montana‘.

Following the official audio release, the duo has dropped its album with is just as interesting as the song itself. 

Titled after the fictional Scarface character, the joint effort from Eazi and Tyga show the two artists channeling their inner Tony Montana to create a vibe in the 1980s Miami themed video; with Eazi kicking things off with his smooth melodies before Tyga joins in to drop some bars on his verse.

However, ‘Tony Montana‘ serves as Mr Eazi‘s fifth output for 2019, it comes after the release of his previous record ‘Call Waiting‘ featuring King Promise and Joey B.
