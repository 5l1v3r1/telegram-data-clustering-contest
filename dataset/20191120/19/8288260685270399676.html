Woman seriously hurt after severe Walsall house fire

Medics and firefighters dashed to the Penderel Street, Walsall, after the blaze broke out

20 Nov 2019, 19:22 by Jordan Coussins, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/jordan-coussins/

Medics treated a woman for serious injuries after a severe fire ripped through a Walsall home.

Emergency crews – including West Midlands Ambulance Service – dashed to Penderel Street, Bloxwich, after the blaze at around 10.45am on Wednesday, November 20.

The victim, a woman in her 60s, was treated for serious injuries before being taken Queen Elizabeth Hospital for further treatment.

 

It is no known how the blaze started but West Midlands Fire and Rescue Service has been contacted for more information.

A spokesman for West Midlands Ambulance Service said: "At 10.29am we were called to reports of a fire at a property in Penderel Street.

"We sent one ambulance and a paramedic officer to the scene.

"We have treated one patient, a woman in her 60s, for serious injuries.

"She was taken to the QE Hospital for further treatment."
