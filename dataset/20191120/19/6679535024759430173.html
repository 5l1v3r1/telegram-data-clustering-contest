New Paediatric Hematology-Oncology division at Kasturba Hospital

20 Nov 2019, 19:13 by Special Correspondent

Siddhartha Laskar, Deputy Director, Academics, Tata Memorial Hospital Mumbai, inaugurated the Division of Paediatric Hematology-Oncology OPD and Ward at Kasturba Medical College and Hospital on November 18.

According to a press release, H. Vinod Bhat, V-C, MAHE, said, “The demand for different sub specialties in a single specialty is the need of the hour.”

H.S. Ballal, Pro Chancellor, MAHE, said that cancer did not mean death sentence for patients now. Medical science was now much more advanced when compared to earlier decades. “Now advanced medication is available for cancer treatment. If the cancer is detected at an early stage it is possible to cure it,” he said.
