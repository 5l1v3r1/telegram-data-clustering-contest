Some People May Post Bible Quotes And Dress Decently But They Are Worse Than Prostitutes – Moesha Buduong

20 Nov 2019, 19:20 by vanessa nordzi

Curvaceous Ghanaian actress and Instagram model Moesha Buduong has a piece of advice for those who judge other ladies by their appearance.

According to Moesha in an Instagram post, appearances are deceptive. She explained that, women who dress skimpily are not always looking for someone to have sex with neither does it mean she is cheap.

Moesha added she has friends who dress decently and post Bible Quotes on daily basis but behind close doors they engage in all kinds of sexual immorality.

She wrote” A woman posting sexy pictures doesn’t mean she is easy or looking for sex. I have friends who post Bible Quotes and are decently dressed but are dick eating monsters”.

As the saying goes, never judge a book by its cover.

Moesha was recently on a vacation in Dubai to chill with some starts including Wizkid and Tiwa Savage.
