Huge Disappointment For Pirates Duo, Tough Clash Awaits…

20 Nov 2019, 19:30 by Soccer Laduma

Orlando Pirates duo expected to return to help the team in their upcoming Absa Premiership match, after their disappointment on international duty. 

Following their 5-0 loss against Algeria last Thursday, Justin Shonga and Augustine Mulenga and their teammates from the Zambian national team suffered a 2-1 loss to Zimbabwe on Tuesday.
