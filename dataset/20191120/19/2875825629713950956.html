Emmerdale's Jacob in two pregnancy scandals with paedo Maya and teen Leanna

Emmerdale fans were stunned by the Maya Stepney pregnancy bombshell - but Jacob may have another baby on the way after sleeping with teen Leanna without protection

20 Nov 2019, 19:27 by Irishmirror.ie

Emmerdale looks set to be rocked by another pregnancy scandal involving Jacob Gallagher.

Stunned viewers were horrified to discover that Maya Stepney is pregnant as she left prison on Tuesday night's episode.

The paedophile teacher, who groomed schoolboy Jacob for months, walked free while cradling a big baby bump.

It is unknown whether Jacob or dad David Metcalfe is the father of the unborn child - but if it is the teen then he could become a second time dad very quickly.

Jacob may have another baby on the way after sleeping with Leanna Cavanagh without using protection.

Still unable to contact Maya, dejected Jacob confided in Leanna about his feelings and apologised for his behaviour towards her.

The pair leaned in for a passionate kiss before having sex together for the first time - with Leanna losing her virginity.

Afterwards, Jacob started acting very distant and shocked Leanna with his cold attitude as he zipped up his trousers and left.

Leanna looked worried when she got a text from Jacob, which read: "Just checking you're on the pill."

Coming up in the next episode, Leanna is left devastated over Jacob's harsh rejection.

Leanna tells step mum Bernice that she slept with Jacob - but begs her not to tell her dad.

But she can't keep the truth from Liam and he furiously marches over to confront Jacob at the shop.

Angry Liam accuses Jacob of forcing Leanna to sleep with him during a heated row.

As David tries to get the doctor away from his son - Liam punches the teenager.

At the end of the week, the rest of the villagers are left stunned when Liam is arrested under suspicion of assault.

Some viewers are confused by the latest twist as there have been no confirmed plans for Maya to return to the soap.

However, actress Louisa Clein hinted that a return to the Dales was on the cards for Maya while speaking to Mirror Online last month.

She told us: "I think the storyline will almost certainly continue. There are repercussions, no doubt, for years for those characters."
