PPP protest for not providing personal physician to Zardari

20 Nov 2019, 19:19 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

KARACHI (Dunya News) – Pakistan People’s Party (PPP) on Wednesday protested outside Karachi press club for not providing personal physician to former president Asif Zardari in jail and against the bad policies of federal government.

Provincial minister Saeed Ghani on the occasion said PPP is being subjected to political victimization. He said for the first time in the history a case on one place is being run from other place.

He said Asif Zardari is not demanding relief but his right.

A large number of workers including advisors, Special assistants and members of National and Provincial Assemblies participated in the protest.

The protestors said purpose of this protest is to open the eyes of government against its ill policies.
