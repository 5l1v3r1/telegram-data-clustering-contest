Dame Dash Reportedly Arrested for Unpaid Child Support

20 Nov 2019, 19:22 by VladTV

According to TMZ, hip-hop mogul Damon Dash has been arrested as a result of unpaid child support. Fortunate for Dash, he was quickly released after being apprehended. He was picked up by authorities in New York City after trying to clear two of the warrants associated with his child support. 

Dame says he was able to take care of one warrant in Manhattan but while en route to the other courthouse to take care of the second warrant, 7 deputies arrested Dash. TMZ reports Dash had to fork over a million dollars to clear the debt after being taken into police custody.
