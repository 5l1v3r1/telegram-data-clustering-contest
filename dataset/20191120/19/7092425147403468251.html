Watch: Woman risks her own life to save koala from Australian bushfire

20 Nov 2019, 19:08 by Nick Fitzgerald

The woman, called Toni, literally took the shirt off her back to save the animal

A woman has gone viral after risking her own life to save a poor koala from burning in an Australian bushfire.

Identified as Toni, the woman literally took the shirt off of her own back to help carry the animal to safety.

The video shows the koala letting out squeals of pain as Toni douses it with cold water.

The incident happened near Long Flat in New south Wales. Speaking to Nine News, the heroic woman said she would transport the inured animal to Port Macquarie Koala Hospital.

Bushfires have ravaged areas across South Australia in recent weeks. Six people have died since last month, with a further 500 houses being destroyed.

Australia's koala population has also been majorly impacted by the flames. More than 350 of the marsupials are feared dead as a result of the blazes.
