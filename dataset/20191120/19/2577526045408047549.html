Snack World: The Dungeon Crawl - Gold releases on the Nintendo Switch on February 14, 2020.

20 Nov 2019, 19:34 by Heather Alexandra

Snack World: The Dungeon Crawl - Gold releases on the Nintendo Switch on February 14, 2020. The colourful action RPG is made by Level-5, makers of Dark Cloud and the Ni no Kuni games. It is an expanded port of the previously Japanese only 3DS game The Snack World: Treasurers.
