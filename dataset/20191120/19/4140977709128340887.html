MAH Vs DEL Dream11 : The enthralling Super League stage of the marquee tournamnet gets underway today as two stalwarts go at loggerheads.

After exulting a pristine and sublime brand of cricket in their group stage encounters to top their respective groups, both Maharastra’s and Delhi enter the land of today’s enthralling encounter in ravishing form. These are two staunch names in Inca’s domestic cricketing fraternity, sides who have fully lived up to the enormous expectations accorded on their head by doling out pinch perfect performances.

Topping Group C of the ongoing edition of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy with the five wins from seven matches, Maharashtra look unstoppable, unforgiving and unsparing at the moment with the side upbeat with the three wins in succession. The most reason was a thumping 45 run win over Punjab, one which saw them maraud their way to 201 while batting first before curbing their opponents at just 156, a pulsating effort which emphasised the gargantuan nature of their side.

And when they make an advent into the Super League stage of the showpiece T20 tournamnet, the side will be looking to emanate the same rampant display as they and Delhi get the newest round of the tournamnet underway. They know things won’t be easy for them though with Delhi themselves in flawless touch at the moment having topped Group E with the five wins as well from seven matches with the side emulating Maharastra’s three wins in succession.

The 20 run win over Odisha in the previous match ensured that the margin the win came by saw the side edge Jharkhand into first place despite both ending with 22 points by the finest margin of NRR. This is a clash befitting to throw open the doors of the Super League of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy, a match which promises to regale us in an edge of the seat thriller.
