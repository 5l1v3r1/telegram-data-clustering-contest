Transit workers promise 'complete system shutdown' of buses if no deal reached

20 Nov 2019, 19:13 by Martin MacMahon and Espe Currie

VANCOUVER (NEWS 1130) – The union representing striking Metro Vancouver transit workers says there will be a complete shutdown of the bus system three days next week if Unifor and Coast Mountain Bus Company are not able to reach a deal.

Unifor spokesperson Gavin McGarrigle said at a news conference on Wednesday that if the two parties don’t come to a deal there will be a total shutdown of the bus system on Wednesday, Thursday and Friday of next week.
