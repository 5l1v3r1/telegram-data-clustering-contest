The Best Disney+ Movies You Actually Have To Search For

20 Nov 2019, 19:30 by Josh Ocampo

Disney+ is finally available in Australia. The Netflix competitor comes with over 600 Disney movies, which can be a lot to sift through. With that in mind, here are some highlights to get you started that you won't find on the front page.

By now, you probably know that Disney+ is home to a bunch of Pixar, Marvel and Star Wars titles, including the Toy Story trilogy, every Avengers movie and The Last Jedi. But judging by the response on social media, the most exciting thing about the service is the huge back catalogue of Disney shows and movies you once loved and cherished.

While our friends at Gizmodo have compiled the absurdly long list of movies and TV shows as part of the announcement, who is actually looking forward to streaming the very mediocre sequel to Twitches?

Instead, defer to our list of the best — and we mean best — film and TV coming to Disney+, beginning with some classics (and then followed by some Lifehacker staff favourites.) Enjoy!
