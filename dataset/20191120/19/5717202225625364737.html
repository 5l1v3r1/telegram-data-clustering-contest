Everton fans react as humble Anthony Gordon remains patient

Anthony Gordon has impressed in the U23 team.

20 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Jay Williams

Everton fans believe Anthony Gordon deserves his chance in the first-team.

But Gordon has told evertontv that he will continue to improve and wait for his chance. He is not taking anything for granted and wants to get into a position where Marco Silva calls him up.

Gordon has been the standout performer in the Everton academy team this season. Offering goals, assists and excitement, the 18-year-old is also making a name for himself at youth level for England. He now wants to add to his one first-team outing.

Gordon on his first-team chances

Gordon told evertontv: “I just try to enjoy myself and get better every day. You never sit down and think, ‘I have a chance’. That is for other people to say. I am just trying to keep getting better and progressing.

“To be around it shows I am progressing well. But I am not there yet. I am not starting for the first team and I still have so much to do.”
