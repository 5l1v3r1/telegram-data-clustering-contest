ECN says EVMs can produce a paper trail to show total votes cast

20 Nov 2019, 19:18

The ECN says electronic voter machines (EVMs) can produce a paper trail, to show how votes were cast to both Presidential and National Assembly elections.
Its chief elections and referenda officer Theo Mujoro said this when he addressed the SADC Election Observer Mission on the transparency of the EVMs, in the absence of the voter verifiable paper audit trail (VVPAT).
