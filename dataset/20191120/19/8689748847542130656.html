The Kind Of Songs I Do Makes It Difficult For Me To Get Collaborations – Akwaboah Laments

20 Nov 2019, 19:10 by vanessa nordzi

Ghanaian songwriter, producer and singer Gladstorm Kwabena Akwaboah Jnr., known in showbiz circles as Akwaboah says the genre of music he does makes it difficult for him to find artistes to collaborate with.

In a recent interview with Rev. Erskine on Yfm’s  Morning Radio Show, Akwaboah said,  “With the kind of songs I do, I struggle to get collaborations. If the song is good, how do you get someone deep like you to work on it?”

According to Akwaboah,  he puts his heart and soul in making his music and expects anyone he will collaborate with to do same.

He finally stated that he only does collaborations with deep writers and artistes whose songs move him.

Akwaboah is currently promoting his new song titled ‘Gangsta lovin’.
