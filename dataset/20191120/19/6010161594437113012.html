Red Dead Online players will earn bonus items for taking down this week’s Legendary Bounty

20 Nov 2019, 19:41 by Stephany Nunneley

Red Dead Online players who take out the latest bounty will be handed bonus items.

The latest Legendary Bounty in Red Dead Online is “Yukon” Nikoli Borodin, and if you decide to take the man on, he was seen in the area south of Strawberry, near Fort Riggs.

Along with the monetary reward for the bounty, you will also receive three Special Snake Oil and 10 Big Game Meat within 24 hours of completion.

Madam Nazar this week is looking to complete the Collectors the Bowman’s Collection. You will need to find the Flint Arrowhead, Raw Arrowhead, and Agate Arrowhead.

Also, this week’s Featured Free Aim Series pits two squads against each other a Team Shootout.

This week, you can also earn a 50% bonus on Goods delivery using Small or Medium wagons. You can also enjoy 25% off Trading and Hunter Wagons and a 50% discount on Trader Resupply orders. You can also take 40% off everything sold by Wilderness Outfitters.

The Wheeler, Rawson & Co. has new clothing available in the form of the Chumley Flat Cap, Salcedo Shirt, Crutchfield Suspenders, a pair of Dress Shoes and the Cardenas Poncho. For the women range wrangler, there’s the Ballard Jacket, Aguirre Boots and Goodale Shirtwaist. Fellas can pick up the Fulbright Coat.
