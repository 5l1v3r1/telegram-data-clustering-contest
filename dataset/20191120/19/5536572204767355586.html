Social Media Finds Old ‘Racist’ Tweets From Reporter Who Accused Charles Barkley of Threatening Her (PICS)

20 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Darrelle Lincoln

Social media is truly undefeated.

On Tuesday night, Charles Barkley started to receive a wave of backlash after an Axios reporter posted on Twitter that the NBA Hall of Famer told her, “I don’t hit women but if I did I would hit you.”

Alexi McCammond said Barkley made that comment after she asked for clarification over which Democratic presidential candidate he supports ahead of the 2020 race.
