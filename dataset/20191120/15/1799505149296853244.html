Uber Settlement

20 Nov 2019, 15:09 by BRIEF

SAN FRANCISCO — A federal judge on Tuesday gave final approval to a $395,000 settlement for Uber drivers, and $100,000 in attorney fees and expenses, with $40,000 of it coming from the settlement.
