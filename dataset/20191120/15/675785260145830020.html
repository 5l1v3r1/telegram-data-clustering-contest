I'm A Celebrity 2019 odds slashed as Roman Kemp and Jacqueline Jossa become joint-favourites

The campmates have now been reunited after a few nights in Snake Rock

20 Nov 2019, 15:40 by Tom Phillips, https://www.facebook.com/JournoTom/

Roman Kemp and Jacqueline Jossa are now joint-favourites to win I'm A Celebrity 2019 , according to The Pools .

The radio DJ won the camp over and UK viewers with his impression of TV hosts Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly while the actress faced her worst fear in last night's bushtucker trial - being covered in spiders.

Her campmates comforted her afterwards as she managed to battle through her fear for spiders by getting both stars.

Early favourite Ian Wright continues to drift from 2/1 to 6/1 after managing just one star in the trial - although his reaction to Caitlyn Jenner having to deal with snakes was VERY amusing - straight to former rugby player James Haskell!

Although, we are still scratching our heads over Adele Roberts' luxury item of a Jane McDonald photo...
