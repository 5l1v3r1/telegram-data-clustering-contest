Victoria Beckham says she and husband David are equal partners in parenting

It's no wonder David and Victoria have got it right as they have been married for nearly two decades

20 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Irishmirror.ie

Victoria Beckham reckons she and husband David have perfected parenting.

The fashion designer said the parents take it in turns when it comes to looking after their four kids during her Tuesday appearance on Jimmy Kimmel.

Posh Spice and David raise their children Brooklyn, 20, Romeo, 17, Cruz, 14, and Harper, seven, together.

In a loving moment, Victoria celebrated her husband's successes as she praised him for being a "very good dad".

She said: "I've been married to him for over 20 years, so I'm kinda fond of him. He's a good dad, he's a very good dad...

"He's really good, I mean. David is watching the kids and we swap. When I'm at home, he's away, so one of us is always there looking after the kids."

It's no wonder David and Victoria have got it right as they have been married for nearly two decades.

Recently, the Beckham brood spent Halloween in Los Angeles with David while Victoria was busy with work.

The mother-of-four discussed the famous family's wide connections in the US.

Close to Justin Bieber and his new wife Hailey, David took Harper for a magical trick or treating session at the singer's home.

Victoria explained how the special evening come about.

She said: "He texted David, this was on Halloween, he asked what David was doing, he was out trick or treating with the kids.

"[Justin] said “Come over, I’m giving out snow cones”. He's such a nice man."

Victoria is in Los Angeles this week to launch her Cell Rejuvenating Prime Moisturizer.

She has been focusing on the launch of her beauty brand as she continues to expand her image in the fashionable spheres.
