Erling Haaland 'visits Arsenal as interest grows'

Red Bull Salzburg striker Erling Haaland has reportedly visited Arsenal with the Gunners firmly in the hunt to sign the teenager.

20 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Matt Law

Manchester United are widely considered as the favourites to sign the 19-year-old, who has scored 26 times in 18 appearances for Salzburg during the 2019-20 campaign.

Haaland's father, Alf-Inge Haaland, has reportedly recently visited United's training complex to discuss a switch to the Red Devils.

However, according to Bild, a visit to Arsenal has also been made as the London club are just as keen as the Red Devils to sign the Norway international.

Haaland has scored seven times in just four Champions League outings this season, while he has managed 27 goals in 23 appearances for Salzburg since arriving in January 2019.

Real Madrid, Barcelona and Juventus are also all said to be considering a swoop for the Norwegian with Salzburg seemingly facing a losing battle to keep him.
