Let Burna Boy go first - Teni says after SA fan asked when she's visiting

20 Nov 2019, 15:45

- Teni has got people laughing after she replied a South African fan on Twitter

- The fan asked Teni when she would visit South Africa

- In a sarcastic reply, Teni told the singer to let Burna Boy go and come back home first

Nigerian singer Teniola Apata, popularly known as TeniEntertainer, has got people laughing on the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter, after she replied a South African fan on the platform.

The fan identified as @Awesome_Gugu tweeted at Teni asking her when she plans to visit South Africa.

Teni replied to the fan with a sarcastic and playful comment. The singer told the fan to wait until Burna Boy goes to the country and comes back.
