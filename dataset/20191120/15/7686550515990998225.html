New Apple Air Pods deal undercuts Amazon's £129 discount

The deal means you can get your hands on Apple Air Pods for just £111 - cheaper than Amazon's deal.

20 Nov 2019, 15:51 by Stephen Hurrell

Amazon's Black Friday sale has Apple Air Pods listed for £129 but there is a way to get them for even less.

Amazon surprised many when it cut the price of the expensive Air Pods from their retail price of £159 to £129 and it proved to be a popular deal for those who did not want to shell out an eye-watering £249 for the noise-cancelling Air Pods Pro.

Fans of Apple devices should be used to paying for pricey accessories by now but one Black Friday deal could cut the price of the wireless headphones to just £111 - the lowest ever price for the Airpods.

Laptops Direct has price-matched Amazon with the second generation headphones and charging case priced at £129. That is a £30 saving that matches Amazon for the lowest price around.

However, members of deals website Topcashback can use the service to claim back 2.2% on any purchase at Laptops Direct. The deal represents a saving of £2.80 once the cashback is taken into account.

The discount may not be enough to entice buyers but new users of Topcashback can get an extra £15 cashback when they sign up to Topcashback here and then order the headphones via Laptops Direct.

The £15 cashback, 2.2.% offered to existing members and Laptops Direct's £30 discount means the Apple Air Pods are now priced at just £111.20 once cashback is returned.

The saving of around £50 means the Air Pods are the cheapest on the market - and undercuts Amazon's Black Friday countdown deal.

Of course, if £111 is still too expensive for a pair of headphones there are other wireless Apple-compatible pods on the market for a fraction of the price. Wowcher is currently selling Apple and Siri compatible ear buds and a charging case for just £20 , down from £89.99.
