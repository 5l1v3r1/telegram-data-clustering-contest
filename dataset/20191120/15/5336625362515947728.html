Gordon Sondland's explosive statement at impeachment hearings sets internet on fire

20 Nov 2019, 15:50 by Nicole Gallucci

Gordon Sondland testified in the official impeachment probe and boy did he bring receipts.

On Wednesday, the U.S. Ambassador to the European Union testified in a public hearing on Capitol Hill. He brought a bunch of emails and WhatsApp screenshots and laid everything out in plain, and very damning, language. 

In his opening statement, Sondland said that essentially everyone from Trump on down was aware of quid pro quo in Ukraine. He even specifically admitted it in the iconic line, "Everyone was in the loop. It was no secret."
