Lidl unveil Black Friday sale with up to 50% off TVs, laptops, hoovers and fitbits

Lidl are offering up to 50% off TVs, laptops, hoovers and more.

20 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Blathin De Paor

With Black Friday set to arrive at the end of the month, Lidl have announced they are slashing prices ahead of the big day.

Shoppers across the country will be looking to nab a bargain on November 29 and Lidl is the latest retailer to jump on the band wagon.

The German discounter has unveiled details of their amazing Black Friday Weekend sale with up to 50% off TVs, laptops, hoovers and more, RSVP Live reports.

Punters also don't have to worry about being restricted to one day of deals as the sale runs over three days, with discounts running across November 29 to December 1.

Some of the deals on offer include the retailer's cordless hoover, which you can bag for 50% off at just €99, as well as a FitBit inspire for just €54.99.

A range of Smart TVs including a Samsung 50inch 4K SMART TV - Reduced from €549 to €399.99.

Each item is only available as long as stock lasts and may not be available in each store.
