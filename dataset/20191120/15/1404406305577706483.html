Nipsey Hussle Nominated for 3 Grammys, Full Nominee List Revealed

20 Nov 2019, 15:19 by VladTV

Nipsey Hussle's final release before his death, "Racks in the Middle" featuring Roddy Richh and Hit-Boy, was nominated for Best Rap Song and Best Rap Performance at the 2020 Grammy Awards. DJ Khaled and John Legend's collaboration with Nipsey, "Higher," also landed a nomination for Best Rap/Sung Performance. Nipsey's Victory Lap was nominated for a Best Rap Album in 2019, but lost out to Cardi B's Invasion of Privacy.

Lizzo led the pack with 8 nominations, including for Album of the Year, Song of the Year, and Record of the Year. She was followed by Billie Eilish and Lil Nas X, who grabbed six nominations each. You can check out the full list of nominations below.
