Banthia in doubles quarterfinals

20 Nov 2019, 15:33 by Sports BureauEast Lansing

Siddhant Banthia and James Story of Great Britain defeated John Carlin and Ozan Colak of the USA 3-6, 6-4, [10-5] in the doubles pre-quarterfinals of the $15,000 ITF men’s Futures tennis tournament here.

Banthia was defeated in the third and final qualifying round of singles.

Other results:

$15,000 ITF, Sharm El Sheikh: Doubles (pre-quarterfinals): Men: Sagadat Ayap (Kaz) & Giorgio Ricca (Ita) bt Miguel Damas (Esp) & Sahil Gaware 6-2, 6-0; Women: Wrona Mdlulwa (RSA) & Catalina Pella (Arg) bt Andreea Scurtu (Rou) & Sri Vaishnavi Peddi Reddy 6-0, 6-1.

$15,000 ITF women, Monastir, Tunisia: First round: Clara Vlasselaer (Bel) bt Kaaviya Balasubramanian 6-0, 6-0; Doubles (pre-quarterfinals): Kaaviya & Vasanti Shinde bt Francesca Falleni & Cristina Elena Tiglea (Ita) 4-6, 6-3, [10-4].
