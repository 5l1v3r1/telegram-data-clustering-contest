It's The Last Temptation of Barry Allen in The Flash Mid-Season Finale Pics

20 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Sam Stone

The Flash Season 6 has revolved around Barry Allen and his friends and family bracing for his long-predicted death during the upcoming "Crisis on Infinite Earths," and the Crisis has nearly fallen upon Central City.

The CW has released official stills from Season 6's mid-season finale, with the eighth episode titled, "The Last Temptation of Barry Allen, Part 2." The photos reveal Team Flash contemplating Barry's last days on Earth before he is whisked away to help save the DCTV Multiverse. The photos and official episode synopsis from the network are below.
