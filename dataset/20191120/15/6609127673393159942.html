TRAGIC FIND

Man found dead in Fife village as police seal off hotel

20 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Justin Bowie

A MAN was found dead in a Fife village hotel today.

Cops sealed off Drummond’s Hotel in Markinch after they made the grim find.

And police confirmed they are currently treating the death as unexplained.

Inspector Rachael Burns, from Glenrothes Police Station, said: “Officers were called to an address on Balbirnie Street, Markinch, on Wednesday November 20, following the sudden death of a man.

“The death is being treated as unexplained.

"Enquiries are ongoing.”

We told earlier today how a body was found in the search for missing Oban man Alex Carter.

The 49-year-old had vanished without a trace after leaving for work yesterday morning.

And yesterday a body was found in the search for a pensioner who had vanished amid freezing conditions.

An appeal was launched to help trace Helen Maider, 89, from the Bishopbriggs area after she disappeared around 6am.

But cops later confirmed a body had been found as part of their efforts.
