Officers 'extremely concerned' after man, 27, reported missing from Newark

Michal suffered 30% burns and this had led to distinctive scarring to his face, ears, and hands.

20 Nov 2019, 15:44 by Phoebe Ram

An appeal has been launched for a missing 27-year-old man who may not have been seen for more than 10 days.

Michal Krysztofczyk, 27, has been reported missing from Cross Street in Newark on Monday, November 18.

It is not known exactly when Michal was last seen but it is likely to have been around Saturday, November 9.

Officers are now "extremely concerned for his welfare".

Michal is described as a white Polish man of slim build with a bald head.

Michael suffered 30 per cent burns and this had led to distinctive scarring to his face, ears, and hands.

It is not known what Michal was wearing when he was last seen.

If you have seen Michal since November 9, please call Nottinghamshire Police on 101 quoting incident 350 of November 18 2019.

Alternatively if you see Michal please call 999 immediately.
