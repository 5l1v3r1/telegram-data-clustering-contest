Public meeting over proposed landfill near Mayo's holy village

20 Nov 2019, 15:00

A PUBLIC meeting to be held in Knock on Wednesday, November 27, at 8 p.m. over plans to build a landfill site in the vicinity.

The venue is Knock Community Centre.

Aontú general election candidate Paul Lawless has confirmed that an application has been lodged with Mayo County Council for the proposed landfill at an existing quarry.

He said: “Many locals are extremely concerned about the proposed landfill in such close proximity to the village, just 500 meters from the centre of the village.

“We had an initial meeting when this news originally broke and we had over 100 people there. This meeting is expected to be bigger.

“All elected repesentatives have been invited and we hope they will attend.”
