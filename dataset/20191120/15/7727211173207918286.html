How to create the festive cartoon family pictures that are appearing on Facebook

Get your own personalised Christmas family cartoon image

20 Nov 2019, 15:22 by Gemma Jaleel

If you been scrolling through Facebook then no doubt heart-warming family cartoon Christmas scenes have been popping up in your feed.

This is the latest festive fad on social media which allows you to turn your family into a cartoon complete with a log burning fireplace and decked out Christmas tree in the background.

The clever idea actually comes from a website offering personalised gifts including home decor, jewellery, photo socks and even underwear, the MEN reports.

There's also an option to create your own personalised Christmas digital image which can then be printed on to mugs.

The MadeMine site will let you choose the number of people and pets you have in your family, then you can customise, skin colour, hair colour and style and add everyone's names on the image for a truly personalised design.

You can also choose your own wintry backdrop, whether outside in the snow

You can buy a stand alone digital image which is currently reduced to £2.31 on the website which is sent to your email within two days or pay extra to have it printed on to a mug.
