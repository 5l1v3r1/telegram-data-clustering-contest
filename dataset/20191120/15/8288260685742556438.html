How much each I'm A Celebrity 2019 star is being paid - from Caitlyn Jenner to Ian Wright

Jenner is pocketing a huge £500,000 to star - and she's not the only well-paid contestant on this year's series

20 Nov 2019, 15:17 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Caitlyn Jenner has become one of the most high-paid stars to appear in I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!

The celebrity is starring alongside nine others in the ITV1 reality show, which returned on Sunday evening (November 17).

Jenner is pocketing a huge £500,000 to star - and she's not the only well-paid contestant on this year's series.

Ian Wright, a former Arsenal star turned broadcaster, who regularly appears as a pundit on BBC One's Match of the Day, is earning £400,000.

Girls Aloud star Nadine Coyle is taking home £250,000, according to reports.

Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway and former EastEnders actress Jacqueline Jossa are set to make £75,000 each.

At the other end of the table, £50,000 is being paid to former Midlands rugby star James Haskell.

The former Wasps and England star will be paid the same amount as BBC Radio DJ Adele Roberts.

 

Capital FM DJ Roman Kemp, the son of Spandau Ballet star Martin, is being paid £40,000.

Rak Su boyband member Myles Stephenson will take back £25k.

 

I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! will return to our screens on ITV1 from 9pm this evening.

Jenner is among the favourites with bookmakers to win the show, with Ian Wright, Jacqueline Jossa and Roman Kemp also among the front-runners.
