Liverpool fans make transfer demand after noticing Fabinho and Son shirt swap

Liverpool fans are all saying the same thing after a video of Son, Fabinho and Roberto Firmino has emerged on social media

20 Nov 2019, 15:08 by Caoimhe O'Neill

Liverpool fans have made a transfer request to Jurgen Klopp in the last 24 hours.

Supporters are urging the Reds boss to sign Tottenham Hotspur forward Son Heung-min.

This comes after Brazil international Fabinho was spotted swapping shirts with the South Korean forward after the two sides met on Tuesday afternoon.

Liverpool goalkeeper Alisson was also involved as Tite's side ran out 3-0 winners in Abu Dhabi.

And following the international friendly, Premier League rivals Fabinho and Son were filmed exchanging shirts.

A video of the moment was shared to social media, and has sent Reds fans into a frenzy.

Particularly as the clip also showed Roberto Firmino, who came on as a late substitute, having a friendly chat with Son.
