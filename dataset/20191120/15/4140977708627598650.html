AUS vs PAK Dream11 Match Prediction : Australia Vs Pakistan Best Dream 11 Team for First Test Match

20 Nov 2019, 15:45 by Gautam Kapoor

Aus Vs PAK Dream11 : Australia look to keep their unblemished record at the ground intact when they square off against Pakistan in the series opener.  

Fresh off having tasted consummate success in the longest format of the game as they retained the Ashes test series trophy after the series ended level on 2-2, Australia make an advent into the two match test series against Pakistan today looking to resonate the same rampant showings. The side enter the land of the upcoming series as overwhelming favourites, a side which is expected to trounce and rout the visiting side on familiar home conditions.

For Australia, this series comes bearing with it immense ramifications owing not just the fact that it is part of the World Test Championship but also the shot at redemption it offers Australia. The side were defeated 1-0 by Pakistan in the last test series the two sides partook in back in 2018, a result they’ll be looking to overhaul come this two match series which comes offering with it 120 points in the test championship as well.

And with Australia having turned a new page from their disastrous time in the cricketing fraternity in 2018, the side will be quietly confident of sweeping this series as well. What adds to the intrigue of today’s series opener is the 30 year long unbeaten spree Australia come bearing at the Gabba, one the side will be looking to keep intact with a comprehensive and clinical result at a track which has traditionally always favoured them.

 “It’s important that we start to play more consistently, regardless of the surface. We can’t wait to come to the Gabba, knowing that Australian sides play well and win a lot of Test matches here. We have to be able to do it on any surface and any venue, and that’s something we are building towards,” said Australia’s captain Tim Paine prior to this encounter.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

The wicket laid out over here has been traditionally a one which immensely plays out in favour of the quicks for the first two days and the pictures emerging preceding to the match promise us to be in for reminiscent conditions. The new ball pacers are going to ruffle the batsmen and leave them in remorse with the side seeing the coin turn its way bound to step out and field first.
