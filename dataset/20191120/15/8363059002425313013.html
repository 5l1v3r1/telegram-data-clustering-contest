Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite Will Give the S10 a Run for Its Money Based on Leaked Specs

20 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Shabana Arif

Weirdly, the specs for the S10 Lite are significantly better than we'd expect.

Twitter leaker Ishan Agarwal is at it again, this time sharing details of the upcoming Galaxy S10 Lite and S11, with specs for the former garnering some measure of surprise because they're not just a bit on the shit side, like they should be.

He tweeted that the S10 Lite will house the Snapdragon 855 processor, and will sport a 48MP main camera, 5MP depth, and 12MP ultra-wide, as well as a 32MP front-facing camera.
