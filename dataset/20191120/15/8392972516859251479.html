Shoppers clamouring to get Asda's light-up singing unicorn Christmas stocking

Unicorns are real – and there's one on our mantelpiece!

20 Nov 2019, 15:03 by Nancy Brown

Shoppers are loving Asda's new Christmas launch – a light-up singing unicorn stocking.

The pink stocking plays Christmas songs and shakes it ears when one of its hooves is pressed.

Its central pink horn also lights up.

With The Magic Of Christmas written across the stocking, it certainly does what it says on the tin.
