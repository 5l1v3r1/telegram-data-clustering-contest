Taylor Swift fans fuming at Lover's Album of the Year Grammys snub

20 Nov 2019, 15:24

Taylor Swift fans have been left fuming at Taylor Swift being snubbed for album of the year at the Grammys – despite three other nominations.

It’s a shock for Swifties.

The Lover hitmaker has been nominated for song of the year, best pop vocal of the year (both for Lover), and best pop solo performance for You Need To Calm Down.

Meanwhile, the likes of Bon Iver, Lana Del Rey, Ariana Grande, and Lizzo were nominated for AOTY, leaving Taylor fans less than impressed.

‘First RED album, second reputation album and now #Lover album?’ one fumed. ‘Really @RecordingAcad? Seriously? She don’t deserve this!! She deserves better fyi. GRAMMY ISSA TRASH!! #GRAMMYs.’

Another added: ‘Fun Fact: Despite being snubbed, Taylor Swift’s ‘Lover’ album outsold (pure) all of the 2020 #Grammys ‘Album Of The Year’ nominated albums COMBINED. Lover deserved a nomination at least.’

‘I’m really happy for everyone but Lover was really the best album and deserved more,’ one more said. ‘But it’s alright. Taylor‘s gonna go home after #Grammys and we’re gonna get 1989 2.1.’

Nevertheless, many fans are classing the three nominations as a win for Taylor following her row with Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta.

After claiming that she was being blocked from performing her own songs (claims which Big Machine Records denied), she had stars including Halsey, Lily Allen, and Gigi Hadid standing with her.

Meanwhile, it’s now been confirmed that she’s able to perform her tunes at the AMAs – a performance which she previously described as having a ‘question mark’ over it.

Last year, Taylor skipped the awards to support her boyfriend Joe Alwyn at the Baftas for his performance in The Favorite, with her album Reputation missing out in all the major categories.

According to Billboard, Reputation was the biggest selling album of 2018 and the third biggest album of 2017, despite only being released that November.
