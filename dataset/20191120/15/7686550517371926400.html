Great British Bake Off is looking for contestants for its 2020 series

The popular show will return to screens next year

20 Nov 2019, 15:14 by Danny Thompson

Series 10 has only just finished, but The Great British Bake Off is already on the hunt for participants for series 11.

David Atherton was crowned this year's winner, and you could be following in his footsteps.

The 36-year-old from London triumphed in the final, beating fellow finalists Alice Fevronia and bookies' favourite Steph Blackwell.

After two months of baking, 13 amateur bakers were whittled down to just three and David, Alice and Steph took on their final challenge.

The popular show will return to screens next year and Channel 4 need a whole new host of enthusiastic bakers to take part.

So if you, or someone you know, can separate your ramekins from your bread tins, you could join Paul, Prue, Noel and Sue.

How to apply

On the website says the show is looking for "smart cookies in the kitchen".

You will also need to be 16 or over by the January 1, 2020.

If you are interested, you need to apply online here by January 5, 2020.

Alternatively you can ask for an application form to be posted to you by calling 0207 067 4837 or by email: applyforbakeoff@loveproductions.co.uk
