Blood-stained dress of Congress MLA in Kerala Assembly

20 Nov 2019, 15:01 by Arjun Raghunath

The Opposition, Congress-led United Democratic Front, on Wednesday disrupted proceedings of the Kerala Assembly by staging a strong protest against the police action against Congress MLA Shafi Parambil on Tuesday.

Blood-stained dress of the legislator was even brought to the house, apart from placards and banners, some Congress MLAs entered the Speaker's dais.
