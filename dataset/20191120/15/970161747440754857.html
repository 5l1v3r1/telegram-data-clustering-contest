Married couple reveal why they stayed together after husband transitioned

20 Nov 2019, 15:35

This Morning viewers were heaping praise on a heartwarming interview from the show about a married couple with a very remarkable story.

Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield met Barbara and Jane. who have stayed married despite Jane transitioning from male to female.

Barbara initially thought Jane was having an affair when she found women’s clothes hidden away in their wardrobe, before her then-husband Jane actually surprised her by coming out to her as transgender.

‘It certainly wasn’t [what I was expecting],’ Barbara said of Jane’s confession. ‘I was a bit relieved. This wasn’t as scary as losing [our] relationship.’

In another tender moment that captured viewers hearts, Jane explained her fears prior to her transition about her secret being discovered.

‘Trans people have two great fears,’ she said. ‘Fear of rejection by those they love, their family and friends, and fear of humiliation by other people.

‘It’s only when you get the courage to come out…and when you do, life is suddenly in colour instead of in black and white.’

The couple proved to be a hit on social media too, with viewers taking to Twitter to rave about the heartwarming story and how sensitively it was portrayed on screen.

It’s fair to say, a lot of people loved Barabra and Jane.
