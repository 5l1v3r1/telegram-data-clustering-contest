I’ll only date a rich footballer who plays active football with a big team – Fantana

20 Nov 2019, 11:50 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

RuffTown records signee Fantana who was rumored to be in a relationship with beatmaker Danny Beatz has revealed that she is still single and would only date a footballer.

According to Fantana, her fans and people should ignore the rumours going around between herself and Danny Beatz because she is very well single and ready to mingle.

She continued that, even though she is ready to mingle, she is would only date a footballer who is rich and plays actively for a very big club in the world.
