Jose Mourinho to earn £15m-a-year at Tottenham, double of Pochettino's salary

20 Nov 2019, 11:58

- Jose Mourinho salary at Tottenham Hotspur has been revealed

- The Portuguese manager was appointed new boss of the Premier League giant after the sack of Pochettino

- Mourinho will now earn £15m-a-year at the club - double of the former manager's wages at the club

Tottenham Hotspur confirmed Jose Mourinho as their new manager after sacking their former boss Mauricio Pochettino.

The Argentine tactician was shown the exit door barely five years after leading them to their first-ever Champions League final last season.

Although they lost 2-0 to Liverpool after regulation period - his start to the new season has been awful as they currently rank 14th on the Premier League table.

Pochettino's struggles, however, led Spurs' chiefs to relieve him of his duty and hire Mourinho as his replacement.
