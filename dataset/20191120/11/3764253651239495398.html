20 Nov 2019, 11:17

- Obanye Chizzy Frances, an acid attack survivor, is celebrating her 24th birthday

- The young lady who became an internet sensation after the story of how her uncle's wife doused her with acid 15 years ago, went viral

- Frances recently took to social media to share beautiful new photos and an old one, to celebrate her new age

Acid attack survivor, Obanye Chizzy Frances, turned 24 on November 20 and has taken to the social media platform to celebrate with her friends and followers, with stunning photos.

Recall a while ago, Legit.ng reported the news of a Nigerian lady who became an internet sensation after she took to social media to share the heartbreaking story of how she was attacked with acid by her uncle's wife.

The incident which occurred 15 years ago, took the lady over a year of hospital care to get back on her feet. Frances who now lives with permanent scars of the horrific incident is not letting her past define her.
