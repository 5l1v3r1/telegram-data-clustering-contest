Nike Celebrates Ekiden Season With Updated Zoom Pack

Dropping in a bullet train-inspired colorway.

20 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Jack Stanley

Nike has returned to its Zoom family of silhouettes for a new pack celebrating Ekiden season in Japan. Beginning in October and continuing until the end of the year, Ekiden season sees thousands of teams from high schools and universities, as well as professional groups, compete in intranational road-racing relays across the country.

The silhouettes included are the Zoom Fly 3, the ZoomX Vaporfly NEXT%, the Zoom Pegasus Turbo 2, the Air Zoom Pegasus 36 and the Zoom Rival Fly.
