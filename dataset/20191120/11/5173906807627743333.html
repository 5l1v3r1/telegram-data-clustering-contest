José Mourinho succeeds Mauricio Pochettino at Tottenham

20 Nov 2019, 11:08 by TPN/Lusa

Portuguese team manager José Mourinho, who had been unemployed for almost a year, is the successor of the Argentine Maurício Pochettino in charge of Tottenham, the team in 14th place in the Premier League the club said on Wednesday.

Mourinho, 56, returns to the Premier League after two appearances for Chelsea (2004/05 to 2007/08 and 2013/14 to 2015/16) and one for Manchester United (2016/17 to 2018/19), which fired him on 17 December 2018.

In his career, the Portuguese manager, who has won two Champions Leagues, a European League and a UEFA Cup, in addition to eight Portuguese championships, also coached Benfica, União de Leiria, FC Porto, Inter Milano and Real Madrid.
