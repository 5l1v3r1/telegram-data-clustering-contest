Ukrainian officer reveals enemy death toll in Donbas on Nov 19

One invader was killed on Tuesday.

20 Nov 2019, 11:55

Officer of the 53rd Separate Mechanized Brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Anatoliy Shtefan has disclosed the enemy death toll in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, over the past day.

"On November 19, 2019, the losses of Russian occupying forces totaled four people, namely: one person from the 2nd separate motorized rifle brigade was killed, and three people from the 11th separate motorized rifle regiment were wounded," he wrote on Telegram.

As UNIAN reported earlier, Russia's hybrid military forces on Tuesday mounted 11 attacks on Ukrainian positions in Donbas, eastern Ukraine.
