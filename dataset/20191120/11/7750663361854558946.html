Manchester City 'join the race for Reinier'

Manchester City have reportedly joined the hunt for Flamengo attacker Reinier.

20 Nov 2019, 11:06 by Matt Law

The 17-year-old, who has scored four times and contributed two assists in 12 appearances for his Brazilian club this season, is believed to be on the radar of a number of European clubs.

Indeed, Barcelona, Chelsea, Real Madrid, Atletico Madrid and Paris Saint-Germain have all recently been credited with an interest in the attacker.

Meanwhile, Arsenal are said to have made contact with the in-demand teenager's father in the hope of stealing a march on their rivals.

According to Mundo Deportivo, City are also now weighing up a swoop for Reinier with the Premier League champions being alerted to his progress since breaking into the Flamengo first team.
