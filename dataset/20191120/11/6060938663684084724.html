RRR: SS Rajamouli confirms Olivia Morris opposite Jr NTR

20 Nov 2019, 11:16 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The replacement for Daisy Edgar Jones in RRR is finally announced. Olivia Morris has been roped in as to play the lead opposite Jr NTR in the movie. From Irish villains to Emma Roberts, several names had cropped up in the topic. However, Olivia Morris has finally been confirmed for the role named Jennnifer.

On Wednesday (November 20) afternoon, the official handle of RRR tweeted the news.
