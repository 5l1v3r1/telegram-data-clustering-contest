Body found in search for missing Oban man Alex Carter

20 Nov 2019, 11:04 by Justin Bowie

A BODY has been found in the search for a missing Oban man.

Alex Carter, 49, disappeared without a trace yesterday morning.

He was last seen in Dalintart Drive leaving for work at around 7am on Tuesday morning before vanishing.

Cops launched a frantic hunt to find him.

But  they confirmed a body has now been found in their search.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "Around 10am on Wednesday, November 20, the body of a man was found in a wooded area near to Stevenson Street, Oban.

"The man has not yet been formally identified however it is believed to be Alex Carter who had been reported missing in the early hours of today.

"His family have been informed.

"The death is not being treated as suspicious and a report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal."

Police described Alex as a “loving family man” who loved the outdoors and cycling.

And they said his loved ones were left “distraught” by his disappearance.

His family had reported him missing after he failed to return home last night.
