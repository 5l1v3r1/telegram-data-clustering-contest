(Video) LFC’s stunning rondo under new coach Vitor Matos

20 Nov 2019, 11:38 by Biscan's Boots

There were plenty of youngsters on display at training today – with the majority of the first-team still absent due to their international break commitments.

The likes of Yasser Larouci, Elijah Dixon-Bonner, Neco Williams and Curtis Jones all featured heavily under Jurgen Klopp, Pep Lijnders and newly appointed coach Vitor Matos.

You can watch the whole training session from ‘Inside Anfield’ by signing up to LFCTVGO, here.

We were most impressed by this rondo drill, under the close watch of Matos, which displayed the immense close-control of the various Academy lads who got the Melwood call-up.

If you cut to two minutes into the clip, courtesy of LFCTV, you’ll see what we’re talking about!

The rest of the first-team should be returning tomorrow to being preparations for Crystal Palace on Sunday.
