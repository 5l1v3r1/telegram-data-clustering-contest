Twilight Zone filming continues in downtown Victoria Wednesday; traffic delays expected

20 Nov 2019, 11:21

Filming of the latest Twilight Zone series continues Wednesday at Bastion Square, with traffic disruptions expected in the area.

Hundreds of extras were on set Tuesday for the filming of the CBS All Access show, shutting down a portion of Government Street.

Actor Jordan Peele, who is the show’s executive producer and narrator, wrote the second-season episode that will air next year. It’s part of the fourth incarnation of the original 1959-64 anthology sci-fi series created by Rod Serling

The new version premièred in April.
