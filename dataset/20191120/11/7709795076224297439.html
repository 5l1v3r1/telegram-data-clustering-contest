Jose Mourinho to Tottenham: Sporting Life Podcast extra on the appointment

20 Nov 2019, 11:15 by Sporting Life

Jose Mourinho is the new manager of Tottenham - Dom Newton-Collinge, Tom Carnduff and Paul Higham sit down to discuss the appointment.

Mauricio Pochettino may have guided Spurs to the Champions League final but they have struggled in the early stages of the 2019/20 campaign which has forced Daniel Levy and the board into making the switch.

Can Mourinho win something with Tottenham? Either this season or next? The team discuss the chances.
