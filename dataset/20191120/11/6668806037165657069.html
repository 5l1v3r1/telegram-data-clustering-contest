Bala is just a step away from entering the Rs 100 crore club

20 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Vedanshi Pathak

Ayushmann Khurrana has been rightly deemed as the King of content in Bollywood. The actor has since day one picked scripts that are socially relevant and at the same time are woven in a way which is entertaining for the audience. The stars has a string of hits attached to his name and another blockbuster is surely on the cards as his latest release Bala is all set to enter the Rs 100 crore club.

After earning Rs 72.24 crore during its first week, the Ayushman, Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam starrer earned Rs 3.74 crore on its second Friday, Rs 6.73 crore on Saturday, Rs 8.01 crore on Sunday, Rs 2.25 crore on Monday and finally Rs 2.05 crore on Tuesday making the total an enormous Rs 95.04 crore. With the film being inches away from the Rs 100 crore club, we say team Bala, take a bow!
