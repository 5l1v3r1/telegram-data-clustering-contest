'beautiful angel'

Falkirk mum dies suddenly leaving behind daughter, seven, as gran heartbroken at losing second child

20 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Katy Pagan

A FALKIRK mum's sudden death has sent shockwaves through her family, who have already had to cope her younger brother dying in recent years.

April Cassidy, 27, passed away suddenly at home in Tamfourhill, Falkirk, on Thursday, leaving a daughter of seven years old.

Her death is being treated as unexplained.

Paying tribute to April on Facebook, her grieving mum Tracey Aikman wrote: "Absolutely breaking my heart. My beautiful angel.

"Sleep tight beautiful girl."

Tracey told the Scottish Sun she is still struggling to come to terms with April's tragic death - as is her granddaughter Aleise.

The mum died suddenly at her Falkirk homeShe said: "I'm in total shock, I just didn't expect that at all. I still just can't take it in.

"Aleise has been really upset. Her mum got her a doll and she takes it to bed with her every night and won't let anyone touch it."

It is the second tragedy to hit the family in recent years, after her little brother Jordan passed away following a battle with Duchenne muscular dystrophy.

Tracey said: "It's really hard because that's me lost my son and my daughter. I have three other kids but it's still so hard.

"A parent should never had to bury a child - and I've had to do it twice."

Close pal Martina Rooney, who shares a child with April's brother, said April was an "amazing mum" to Aleise, aged seven.

Paying tribute to the tragic 27-year-old, she added: "RIP April. Had a few good memories and will laugh about them forever."

"Sleep tight beautiful girl."Tracey Aikman

Martina has now launched a fundraising page to support the family with any funeral costs.

The GoFundMe page has already raised £250 of its £1,000 target.

Posting on the site, she said: "April was taken far before her time, she still had her happiest years ahead of her.

"She leaves behind one young daughter at the age of seven who has now been left without her mum.

"We kindly ask that you spare a small amount of money to help her family financially at their deepest time of need to help make the funeral a proper celebration of April's life.

"Any donation of will go a long way."
