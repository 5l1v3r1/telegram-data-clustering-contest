Floyd Mayweather says he's not returning to boxing for Manny Pacquiao rematch

20 Nov 2019, 11:50

- Floyd Mayweather has stated that he is done with boxing and will not be returning to professional fight

- This comes after the American was tipped for a rematch with Manny Packquiao in the ring

- But the 42-year-old explained that he is making more money in exhibitions than professional fight

Retired American boxer Floyd Mayweather has made it clear that he is not returning to the ring for a rematch with Manny Pacquiao.

Since he stopped Conor McGregor in the 10th round in 2017, Floyd Mayweather has not fought any professional fight.

There have been speculation that Floyd Mayweather could return to professional boxing two years after quitting.

But the 42-year-old has rubbished the claims saying that he is making more money than those who fights now in the ring.
