It’s not a shame If you can’t write your own songs- Akwaboah tells colleague artistes

20 Nov 2019, 11:55 by MyNewsGH

Akwaboah has told his colleagues that they should not be worried if they cannot write their own songs.

According to him, there is a reason why there are songwriters so they should not be ashamed.

Akwaboah who was speaking on YFM in an interview “there is nothing wrong with you not being able to write a song”.

According to him he did not know he could write until he wrote a song for Becca

“I didn’t do it for the money initially but now, I know the business part of music.”
