Soap SPOILERS: Here's what's happening in Emmerdale and Coronation Street tonight

Daniel's grief is overwhelming

20 Nov 2019, 11:24 by Carena Crawford

There's one episode of Emmerdale and two of Coronation Street on ITV tonight.

In the Dales, with the spectre of Maya looming large, Jacob and Leanna sleep together, while Lydia still finds herself confused over recent revelations.

In Corrie, Daniel is arrested, and Bernie is accused.

Here are the seven biggest spoilers from tonight's soaps.

Emmerdale, ITV, 7pm

1. Jacob sleeps with Leanna

After David persuaded Jacob not to meet Maya by telling him she'd be sent straight back to prison if they had any contact, Jacob is still trying to deal with everything.

He left Maya a message declaring his love for her, but viewers saw she deleted his number.

Back in the village Jacob agrees to speak with Leanna, and as they chat, she leans in for a passionate kiss, nervously unbuttoning her shirt.

As they sleep together, Jacob's reaction following the act is set to leave Leanna devastated.

2. Lydia has a big decision to make

Lydia is still trying to come to terms with the news she may have Huntington's disease.

As she talks to Sam about it, she doesn't know whether or not she should take the test.

Will he offer her any advice?

3. Chas is concerned

New mum Chas has had a lot going on lately.

As she tries to find a way to cope, she finally opens up about her worries.

Coronation Street, ITV, 7.30pm and 8.30pm

1. Daniel is arrested

Distraught Daniel is heartbroken to discover his laptop was taken in the robbery, so he decides to take matters into his own hands and track it down.

He visits local pawn shops in the area and when he spots a laptop that looks like his in a glass cabinet, he accuses the shopkeeper of handling stolen goods and grabbing a golf club, he smashes the cabinet.

With the police called, Daniel is arrested, but can Craig and Peter get the shop owner to drop the charges after explaining the circumstances?

And how will Daniel feel when he discovers the laptop wasn't actually his?

2. Bernie is accused

Billy tells Paul on the day Bernie started working for him, his diary was stolen with funeral details in and then the spate of burglaries followed.

Paul is immediately suspicious of his mum and along with Gemma they are horrified to find a bag of stolen goods under the stairs at No.5.

Gemma confronts her mum in the pub about the robberies and tells her she's gone too far, before throwing her out.

But did Bernie really do it?

3. Michael sees his daughter

James doesn't come home after a night out and later confides in brother Michael he spent the night with a guy called Dan and has lost his phone.

As the brothers head off to collect it, meeting the guy in Freshco's car park, Michael is shocked when he sees his ex, Grace, and his DAUGHTER.

Grace blanks him and drives off, leaving Michael upset.

4. David and Shona make plans

David and Shona reveal their plans to have Shona adopt Max and Lily formally.

Max is only concerned that he'll still get to see Marion, leaving David put out.
