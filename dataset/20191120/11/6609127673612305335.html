Swede strike

Watch as Celtic kid Lee O’Connor scores for Ireland U-21s in win over Sweden

20 Nov 2019, 11:43 by David Fowler

CELTIC starlet Lee O'Connor scored a brilliant goal as Ireland U-21s came from behind to beat Sweden.

The Hoops' summer signing is yet to appear for Neil Lennon's top team - but has shown his credentials on the international stage.

The 19-year-old made his full international debut last week and grabbed an assist in the 3-1 win over New Zealand.

He was back on Under-21 duty last night and his goal sparked a stunning comeback against the Swedes in Euro qualifying.

Stephen Kenny's men currently occupy top spot in their section from Italy - who have played two games less.

The visitors opened the scoring after 18 minutes through Viktor Gyoekeres.
