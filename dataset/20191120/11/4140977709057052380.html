Harry Kane drops heartfelt message for outgoing manager Mauricio Pochettino

20 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Tanish Chachra

Harry Kane drops a heartfelt message for the outgoing manager Mauricio Pochettino after the latter received the sacking last night.

Mauricio Pochettino was sacked by Tottenham Hotspurs in a surprising revelation by the club through their official website last night. The messages from his players and admirers poured in on social media.

One of the most heartfelt messages was from none other than Harry Kane, who was brought to fame by the Argentine manager. In a tweet posted by the English striker, he has expressed his gratitude towards Pochettino.

“Gaffer. I’ll be forever thankful to you for helping me achieve my dreams. We’ve had some amazing moments in the last 5 and a half years that I will never forget. You were my manager but my friend as well and I thank you for that relationship. Good luck with your next chapter!” wrote Harry Kane on Twitter.
