This is when Santa will be visiting your street

He'll be making a list and checking it twice

20 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Beth Pridding

Father Christmas will be visiting Uttoxeter on his sleigh to see what youngsters want in their stockings.

Santa's sleigh will descend on town in the coming weeks as Rotary Club volunteers spread Christmas cheer and to help raise money for worthy causes.

This year's route includes door-to-door visits to Uttoxeter estates and a trip to the neighbouring village of Bramshall.

Santa's travels will start at 5pm, unless otherwise stated.

His route around Uttoxeter will start on the Birdland estate on Wednesday, December 11.

On Thursday, December 12, he will venture to Byrds Lane before, the big man heads to Tesco on Saturday, December 14, Waitrose on Friday, December 20 and the Maker's Market the following day.
