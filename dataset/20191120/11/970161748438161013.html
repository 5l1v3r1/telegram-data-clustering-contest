I'm A Celebrity: Extra Camp host Adam Thomas branded 'creepy' by fans

20 Nov 2019, 11:47

I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here! Extra Camp co-host Adam Thomas creeped viewers out last night as he appeared to chat someone up on-camera in a segment for the show.

The former Emmerdale actor was filming a segment for the spin-off show, showing viewers a sneak peek at an upcoming trial, Just Desserts.

The married presenter seemed to get distracted, however, by the crew member, Molly, who was showing him around behind-the-scenes.

‘Right, first things first,’ Adam said. ‘What’s your number?’

It was all fun and games, of course, and the segment quickly got back on track – giving viewers a glimpse of what’s to come for the first eating challenge of the new series.

It was announced that this year’s contestants won’t be chowing down on live bugs in any eating trials, and this will be a permanent change for the show after an animal rights backlash.

Some viewers took to social media after the programme to blast the new presenter for his on-camera flirting techniques, however, with one fan branding the actor ‘creepy.’
