Johnny Vegas has paid tribute to his mother Patricia who passed away aged 82 last Sunday.

The comedian and actor took to Twitter on Tuesday to share the sad news, he wrote: "With a kind of deep regret that I'm still incapable of processing, I have to confirm that Ma Vegas, Patricia, passed away last Sunday, peacefully in her sleep.

"To say she was a bit of a character would be too huge an underestimation. As a mum I dare to argue I had the best x."
