Overnights: Star Plus continues to lead in UK on Tuesday

20 Nov 2019, 11:53 by Raj Baddhan

Star Plus continued to dominate in UK ratings on Tuesday, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

Star Plus delivered a share 0.13%, followed by Sony Entertainment Television with 0.11%, PTC Punjabi with 0.10%, New Vision TV with 0.08%, ZEE Cinema with 0.06%, Sony SAB TV and Sony MAX with 0.05%, Colors, Hum Masala, Dunya News, B4U Movies and Star Bharat with 0.04%, ZEE TV, Geo News and Hum TV with 0.03%, Geo TV and Colors Rishtey with 0.02%.

On Star Plus, ‘Yeh Hai Mohababtein’ stayed ahead with 71,800 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Kullfi Kumarr Bajewala’ at 20:30 with 53,100 viewers, ‘Kahaan Hum Kahaan Tum’ at 20:00 with 51,900 viewers, ‘Sanjivani’ at 19:00 with 49,600 viewers, ‘Kasautii Zindagii Kay’ at 21:00 with 44,000 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ at 18:00 with 30,400 viewers, ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’ at 21:30 with 20,000 viewers, ‘Nazar’ at 22:30 with 10,600 viewers, ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ at 22:00 with 3,500 viewers and ‘Namah’ at 18:30 with 2,000 viewers.

On Sony TV, ‘Mere Sai’ led with 46,800 viewers at 19:30, followed by ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati 11’ at 21:00 with 42,900 viewers and ‘Patiala Babes’ at 20:30 with 39,400 viewers.

On ZEE Cinema, ‘Satyam Shivam Sundaram’ topped ratings with 15,500 viewers at 10:30.

On Colors, ‘Bigg Boss 13’ led with 25,900 viewers at 21:30, followed by ‘Kitchen Champion’ at 20:30 with 14,400 viewers.

On Hum TV, ‘Main Na Janu’ topped ratings at 20:00 with 10,700 viewers, followed by ‘Tum Mere He Rehna’ at 21:00 with 9,800 viewers.

On ZEE TV, ‘Guddan’ was watched by 29,200 viewers at 18:00.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
