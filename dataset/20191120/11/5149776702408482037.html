Nottinghamshire's first 'super gully' installed as part of flood prevention

It is much more efficient at removing water

20 Nov 2019, 11:34 by Phoebe Ram

A new high-capacity gully is being installed in an area prone to flooding in Nottinghamshire.

The 'super gully' will be a first for the county and is being added to Halam Road in Southwell by Via East Midlands.

This type of gully - or channel - is described as being many times more efficient to remove water from flooding hotspots.
