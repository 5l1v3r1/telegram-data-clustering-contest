LSU Releases Chapter 2 Of Its “That’s Joe” Series

20 Nov 2019, 23:24 by Tzvi Machlin

As part of their campaign to get star quarterback Joe Burrow Heisman Trophy votes, LSU has been putting together a mini-series outlining how he got to where he is now.

Last week, the Tigers released Chapter 1 of their “That’s Joe” series. And today they’ve released Chapter 2.
