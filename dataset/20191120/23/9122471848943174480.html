Analyst Unveils His 2 Upset Picks For Week 13

20 Nov 2019, 23:48 by Andrew Holleran

A college football analyst for the Associated Press has unveiled his game picks for Week 13 and he’s predicting two notable upsets this weekend.

Week 13 doesn’t feature a ton of notable contests – outside of Ohio State-Penn State – but there could be a few top 25 upsets.

Ralph D. Russo of the AP is predicting two of them.

Texas over No. 13 Baylor

Score prediction: Texas 28, Baylor 24

Utah State over No. 20 Boise State

Score prediction: Utah State 28, Boise State 23
