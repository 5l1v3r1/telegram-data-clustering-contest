Nadine Coyle breaks silence on Cheryl relationship on I'm A Celebrity after being branded 'full of s***'

The group split in 2013 amid rumours of feuds and in-fighting, and the Irish singer recently admitted that she had not really spoken with her bandmates since then, with the exception of Sarah Harding

20 Nov 2019, 23:07 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Nadine Coyle has rubbished claims she did not get on with her Girls Aloud bandmate Cheryl, calling it "fake news".

The group split in 2013 amid rumours of feuds and in-fighting, and the Irish singer recently admitted that she had not really spoken with her bandmates since then, with the exception of Sarah Harding.

However, opening up to fellow campmate Jacqueline Jossa on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!, Coyle said rumours about her relationship with Cheryl were not accurate.

As they were sat by the creek, Jossa asked Coyle if she got on with everyone in the group, and she replied: "Yes."

The actress said she had heard that Coyle did not get on with Cheryl, and she responded: "Fake news."

Girls Aloud - made up of Coyle, Cheryl, Harding, Nicola Roberts and Kimberley Walsh - was created during TV talent show Popstars: The Rivals in 2002.

Coyle said being in the group had been "really good".
