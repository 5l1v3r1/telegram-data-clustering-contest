Rays the roof: Real estate magnate Louise Sunshine selling glam Miami penthouse

20 Nov 2019, 23:46 by Jennifer Gould Keil

Real estate titan Louise Sunshine is in contract to sell her duplex penthouse at 10 Venetian Way in Miami.

The final sale price is not yet available.

Sunshine is now a strategic advisor for Fort Partners, where she has tapped Douglas Elliman’s Fredrik Eklund and John Gomes to rep a soon-to-be-completed $35 million penthouse at the Four Seasons Hotel and Residences at the Surf Club in Surfside, Florida.

We hear Sunshine herself is gearing up for a move to that same oceanfront development.

Sunshine’s soon-to-sell home — a 6,422-square-foot, six-bedroom, six-bathroom pad — was last asking $6.49 million.

The residence is a combo of two separate penthouses, featuring a dramatic glass staircase, an open kitchen, 22-foot ceilings and stunning views of Biscayne Bay and the Miami skyline.

The unit also features three sprawling terraces and comes with five parking spaces.

Building amenities include a “resort-style” pool, a gym, tennis courts and a concierge.

The listing broker of her 10 Venetian Way home is Jill Hertzberg, of Coldwell Banker Residential Real Estate.
