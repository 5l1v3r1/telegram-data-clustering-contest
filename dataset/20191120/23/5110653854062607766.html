20 Nov 2019, 23:22 by Matt Lutovsky

If youlistento only one fantasy football podcast all year and it happens to be the Week 12 Fantasy Football Show (podcast available on iTunes, SoundCloud, and YouTube), you would walk away from the 2019 season thinking Ryan Griffin is the most important player in all of fantasy football. (He's not, by the way, but we really like him as both a waiver pickup and obvious 'start' if he's on your start 'em, sit 'em bubble.)

The good news is we talk about some other players, too, includingDak Presscot,Tom Brady, andAaron Rodgers...whoever they are. We also break down ifBo Scarbrough is for real, whetherJordan Wilkins orJonathan Williamsis the better Colts RB play, and the likelihood of fantasy owners benching Amari Cooper this week. On top of that, we hit you withBrian Hill regret,DeVante Parker appreciation, andJeff Driskel optimism.
