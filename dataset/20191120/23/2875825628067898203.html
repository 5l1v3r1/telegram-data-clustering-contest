Fuming Ian Wright explodes in foul-mouthed meltdown at I'm A Celebrity campmates

I'm A Celebrity viewers blasted Ian Wright for his angry reaction to being offered extra crocodile feet despite complaining about being hungry just hours before

20 Nov 2019, 23:09 by Irishmirror.ie

Fuming Ian Wright exploded tonight in a foul-mouthed meltdown at his fellow I'm A Celebrity campmates.

I'm A Celeb viewers were shocked with the former footballer's angry reaction to being offered extra crocodile feet.

Just hours before, Ian confessed he was on the brink of leaving the jungle because he was struggling with the lack of food.

So it came as a massive surprise when he started swearing at Andrew Maxwell, telling the comedian he didn't want anymore "f***ing crocodile".

Ian was labelled a 'drama queen' and 'diva' by viewers who condemned his rude behaviour.

Ian was in a happy mood as he teased Kate Garraway by trying to high five the GMB presenter with a crocodile claw.

But there was a tense atmosphere in camp when Ian refused to accept another croc foot when offered by Jacqueline Jossa.

Ian said: "I don't want no more. I really honestly don't want no more."

Andrew and Jacqueline insisted he could have another one as James Haskell had already had more.

When Ian refused again, frustrated Andrew said: "Oh Jesus the drama."

Losing his rag, Ian replied: "I said I don't want anymore f***ing crocodile bruv. I don't want no more."

In the Bush Telegraph, he said: "There's nothing more frustrating than when you say no to something and someone continues to ask you. It's annoying."

Kate admitted it had been a "hard day" for everyone in camp, while Jacqueline said people were getting more "agitated" and were "at logger heads".

Could Ian be the first campmate to crack and leave?

I'm A Celebrity viewers took to Twitter to slam Ian for his antics.

One viewer said: "Solid conclusion after tonight’s episode.. Ian is a bit of a d*** really, complains about not eating enough, complains when they get a decent meal"

"Someone get Ian a snickers , quick #diva #hungry," added another.

A third said: "Had Ian Wright down as the winner in the beginning, not anymore, turns out he’s a bit of a moaner"

"Ian Wright is such a f***ing drama queen! Whinge whinge whinge about being hungry blah blah and someone offers him seconds and he gets all bent outta shape? Idiot!," said a fourth.
