So Cute! Madelaine Petsch's New Puppy Is "Like a Ferocious Little Cotton Ball"

20 Nov 2019, 23:06 by Chanel Vargas

My fellow dog-lovers will agree that few moments in life surpass the joy you experience when you get a new puppy to call your own, and Madelaine Petsch is definitely among the crowd. In a YouTube video on Wednesday, Madelaine introduced us all to the new baby in her life: her dog, Olive! "When I found Olive, I was told she's most likely a Maltese-Shih Tzu mix, but I don't think that's true anymore because her body is looking very Chihuahua-ish," Madelaine said as Olive excitedly nibbled on her face. "We're still working through the teething phase clearly."

Madelaine has no idea what kind of dog Olive is, but she says the puppy has already doubled in size in the past three weeks, growing from one and a half pounds to three pounds. "She's a gremlin sometimes," Madelaine added. "She bites a lot!" Still, she seems pretty dang lovable.
