The ‘Christmas Prince 3’ Trailer Somehow Combines A Royal Baby & A 600-Year-Old Curse 

20 Nov 2019, 23:02 by By Steffanie Tan

It just really isn’t the festive season without this very bad movie series, apparently, because Netflix has churned out another – ANOTHER – A Christmas Prince movie.

It looks dumb as hell but will I somehow end up watching it on Christmas Day? Well, yeah.

This time Amber (Rose McIver) is pregnant, and she along with hubs Prince Richard (Ben Lamb) invite royals from a faraway kingdom to renew an ancient truce. BUT, all is thrown into the deep end when the priceless 600-year-old treaty suddenly disappears. It’s up to Amber to find the treaty “before the clock strikes midnight on Christmas Eve” otherwise her baby will be cursed. I shit you not, that’s the deal.

A Christmas Prince 3: The Royal Baby will hit Netflix December 5.
