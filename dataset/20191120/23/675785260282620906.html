Superstar Kim Kardashian leads the celebrity fashion scene in luxury faux fur

Kim Kardashian and other well-known faces are re-cycling their wardrobes and bringing back past fashion including tweed, fur and mum jeans

20 Nov 2019, 23:14 by Rachael Bletchly, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

Elasticated waists are back in fashion... and ­millions of women of a certain age are ­breathing a sigh of relief.

And they are not the only former style duds to ­reappear as the height of chic. Mum jeans, tweed suits, jelly shoes, big knickers and headscarves have all had their moment.

Stylist Martine Alexander said: “Even when fashion comes full circle, there will always be a modern twist.”

So it’s time to have a rummage in your wardrobe. Here’s what else is hot again...

It comes after their half-sister sister Kylie Jenner bagged a huge deal with Coty.

Khloe Kardashian celebrated herself and sisters Kim and Kourtney Kardashian as the "OG trio."

The sassy nickname was unveiled on Instagram on Tuesday night in light of younger half-sibling Kylie Jenner , 22, inflating her personal bank balance to around the tune of $1.6 million - following a huge business deal.

Not one of Kylie's older sisters made reference to the huge achievement but Khloe, 35, used to opportunity to remind fans that she Kim, 39, and Kourtney, 40, were the original Kardashians.

She illustrated the caption with a photo of the threesome posing in pastel coloured bodysuits from a recent photoshoot for their perfume collaboaration.

On her stories Khloe did post footage of unboxing Kylie's Christmas make-up collection as well as Kim's cosmetic kit that was a partnership with her long-time make-up artist Mario Dedivanovic.

Kylie is selling off a majority 51 per cent share in her company Kylie Cosmetics to beauty giant Coty in a deal worth around $600 million.

The daughter of Caitlyn Jenner's deal with Coty was announced on Monday and means she'll be able reach more customers across the world thanks to the company's international infrastructure.
