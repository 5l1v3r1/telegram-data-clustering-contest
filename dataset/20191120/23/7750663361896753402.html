Liverpool pair Mohamed Salah, Andrew Robertson ruled out of Crystal Palace game

Liverpool have been dealt a double blow after each of Andrew Robertson and Mohamed Salah were ruled out of Saturday's Premier League fixture with Crystal Palace.

20 Nov 2019, 23:51 by Darren Plant

Having suffered ankle injuries before the international break, the pair missed out on representing Scotland and Egypt respectively over the past week.

Jurgen Klopp would have hoped that the time on the sidelines would have been sufficient for the pair to be available for the trip to Selhurst Park.

However, multiple outlets have revealed that the left-back and winger will play no part of the game against the Eagles.

The duo will be keen to be available for the Champions League meeting with Napoli, which takes place four days later.
