Cardi B Wants To Mold Baby Kulture Into A Successful Businesswoman

20 Nov 2019, 23:00 by AllHipHop Staff

The Bronx rapper already has some ideas for the career path baby Kulture may take.

(AllHipHop News) Cardi B has high hopes for her daughter Kulture.

Cardi B hitmaker shares the 16-month-old with rapper husband Offset, and despite having just celebrated her first birthday, the star revealed she's already thinking ahead to her daughter's future.

Speaking during a "73 Questions" segment with Vogue U.S., the rapper, 27, revealed her three greatest wishes for Kulture, as she opened up on motherhood.

“I wish for her to be a successful businesswoman,” Cardi expressed, adding she also hopes the young girl will grow up to be “independent” and “confident.”

The Grammy Award-winning star went on to share some words of advice for the tot based on her own life experiences, adding: “Don’t listen to what people say and to dream big and follow it.”

Meanwhile, Cardi also revealed what being a mom has taught her so far, explaining: “The biggest lesson is that you’re never ready on time… things don’t go as you plan, never."
