Rangers get an early jump on Friday's toy drive

It's in support of the Grand River Hospital Foundation

20 Nov 2019, 23:15 by KitchenerToday Staff

NEWS RELEASE
KITCHENER RANGERS
*************************
The Wawanesa Insurance Toy Drive in support of the Grand River Hospital Foundation (GRHF) is this Friday!

Since the players will be on the ice looking for a win against the rival London Knights, co-captains Riley Damiani and Greg Meireles along with Jonathan Yantsis wanted to kick-start the collection early.

On the team’s behalf, they made a donation this afternoon to Paul Murphy, Sr. Business Development Representative with Wawanesa Mutual Insurance Company.

You can also join our players and place a smile on a child’s face this holiday season by donating a new and unwrapped toy or making a cash donation at the game this Friday at 7:30pm.

The Wawanesa event team will be in the concourse collecting toy and cash donations while also selling Wawanesa stuffed bears for $10 – which can then be donated to the toy drive. Some requested donation items include:

  • Colouring, activity, and reading books
  • Crayons, pencil crayons, and markers (ideally in small packages to take home)
  • Bubbles, Crayola Model Magic, and Hasbro Play-Doh
  • Craft kits or board games
  • New small to medium-sized stuffed animals
  • Tactile and fidget toys
  • Small dolls or Hot Wheels/Matchbox cars
  • Lego and Lego Kits
  • Puzzle games
  • Gift cards for emergency needs
  • Gift cards for movies and music for teenage patients (Amazon, iTunes, Google Play, Cineplex etc.)

Please note that dollar store toys cannot be accepted due to the low-quality plastics not meeting health and safety standards/regulations of the hospital.

Fans attending the game will also have a chance to win a trip to the CHL Memorial Cup, presented by Kia! Contest rules can be found at wawanesa.com/toydrive
