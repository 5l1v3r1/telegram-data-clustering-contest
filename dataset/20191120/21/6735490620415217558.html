Statue Of Baby Jesus In Mexican Church Looks Just Like ’80s-Era Phil Collins And Twitter Cannot Handle It

20 Nov 2019, 21:12 by Brent Furdyk

A church in the Mexican state of Zacatecas is making unlikely headlines thanks to a 22-foot-tall statue of baby Jesus, created by sculptor Roman Salvador, which is being lauded (according to the New York Post)  as “the biggest baby Jesus statue in the world.”

While the size of the 2,000-pound statue is impressive, that’s not the most notable thing about it; what’s getting people talking is the fact that it just happens to be the spitting image of 1980s-era Phil Collins, circa “Sussudio”.

One Twitter user pointed that out by sharing photos of both Collins and the statue, and it’s tough to argue there’s an uncanny resemblance.
