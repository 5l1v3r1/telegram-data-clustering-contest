The science of Numerology works in the same way as the science of Astrology. The astrology deals with understanding and predicting the personality and happenings through the signs, whereas numerology deals with understanding the personality and happenings through the numbers. Numerology helps understand how the person's day is likely to go by assessing their ruling number. Here is the daily numerology predictions for individuals with ruling number 7.

Do you give yourself the same care and support as you give your closest companion? Are you as mindful of your health as you are for your children or friends? How to calculate your daily number?

Ruling number of a person is based on their birth date. The addition of the digits of your birth date will be your ruling number. For example, if your birthday is on October 16, your ruling number will be 01 + 06 = 07. If your birthday falls on October 25, your ruling number will be 02 + 05 = 07.  A person with a one-digit birth date like October 07 or October 08 will have their ruling number as 07.

Personality Traits

Individuals with ruling number 07 have a burning desire to gain knowledge and this makes them wiser. They are known to be introspective and analytical. This makes them great problem solvers, and they search for all the reasons for a problem. They enjoy working by themselves and do not prefer working in teams. They are highly intuitive individuals who find peace in pursuing their own spiritual path. You will see these individuals running a very orderly life where all the plans and schedules are organized timely. They possess an outstanding ability to heal others with their compassionate nature.
