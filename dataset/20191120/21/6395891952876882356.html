7-time NASCAR series champion Jimmie Johnson to retire after 2020 season

20 Nov 2019, 21:17 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -

Seven-time NASCAR Series champion Jimmie Johnson announced Wednesday that 202 will be his final season of full-time stock car racing.

Johnson will race for a 19th season next year in his No. 48 Chevrolet, ESPN reported.

In a black-and-white video posted on Twitter, Johnson said he was "so thankful for 18 incredible years" on the NASCAR circuit.
