First look at Coleen Rooney's adorable family Christmas album

Coleen used a Liverpool photographer to get the festive photo

20 Nov 2019, 21:14 by Sophie McCoid

Coleen Rooney has shared magical photos of her four sons - just in time for Christmas.

Coleen shared several adorable snaps of her sons Kai, Kit, Klay and Cass-Mac dressed in personalised Christmas pyjamas from Pretty Personal Gifting.

In the photos they boys look like they've taken a trip to Santa's workshop - with the big man even making an appearance  in several of the shots.

The photos were captured by Liverpool-based Gracie Kellie Photography, which also gave the ECHO permission to share them, with Coleen using the same photographer for her Christmas photoshoot last year.

Sharing them to Instagram Coleen said: "In Love @gracekelliephotography  these are amazing!!! Thank you so much, you are brilliant with the kids. Thanks for the gorgeous pj’s  @prettypersonalgifting ."

And her followers were quick to comment on the adorable photos.

lauraclegg1979 said: "So beautiful, all the boys are gorgeous."

mrslrod said: "This is so lush."

shields53 said: "Wow stunning pics."

And angelarankinestevens said: "Omg cute overload."

Coleen is enjoying being back in the UK, after previously relocating to Washington DC for husband Wayne's footballing career.

But the family have now moved back to to England, with Coleen loving being surrounded by her family and friends again.

The family recently went trick or treating round Liverpool, dressed in cute costumes.

And Coleen and Wayne enjoyed a date night with their friends , with Coleen saying it had "been a while."
