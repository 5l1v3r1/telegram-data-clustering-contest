RuPaul's Cheryl Hole begs 'icon' Cheryl to team up with her in 2020

The RuPaul's Drag Race UK star met her idol when she was a guest judge on the hit show, and now she wants them to work together

20 Nov 2019, 21:25 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

RuPaul's Drag Race UK star Cheryl Hole has reached out to her idol Cheryl in the hope of teaming up on a duet.

The drag queen, who was the seventh to be eliminated from the BBC Three series, had the chance to meet the Girls Aloud star when she appeared as a guest judge.

And after her dream came true, she's hoping they could end up working together in 2020.

The 25-year-old even went as far as to brand Chezza the "best guest judge of the season".

She told MailOnline : "We have crossed paths since filming and she’s an absolute icon. Who knows, 2020 we might be delivering some Cheryl and Cheryl!

"I’m putting feelers out there so Chez if you’re listening, not that she’d be listening in on our phone call, but, yea, I’m definitely up for it."

Geordie star Cheryl delighted fans when she appeared on the UK version of the hit US contest.

Her drag namesake said: "She loves drag, she’s been surrounded by drag her entire life, that came across so well on the show and you could tell she was living her best life."

She went on to say that the singer and Little Mix star Jade Thirlwall were the best guest judges on the season, which will conclude tomorrow night.

Drag queens Baga Chipz, The Vivienne and Divina De Campo are the only three contestants left.

Tomorrow's finale will see them fight it out for the crown and to be named the UK's first Drag Race winner.

The victor will head to Los Angeles to film their own web series with the creators of RuPaul's Drag Race.

Cheryl Hole said being part of Drag Race was a "surreal experience" and something she never thought would happen.
