Ezekiel Elliott Reveals How Dak Prescott Reacted To His TD Celebration

20 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Matt Hladik

After catching a touchdown pass from Dak Prescott in Sunday’s win over the Detroit Lions, Dallas Cowboys running back Ezekiel Elliott broke out his best impression of the star quarterback’s “hip-whip” pregame routine.

Apparently, Elliott did a pretty solid impersonation. Good enough to impress Prescott, in fact.

Zeke told reporters today that Prescott told him he’s “nice” at the move. He also joked that he should switch positions.
