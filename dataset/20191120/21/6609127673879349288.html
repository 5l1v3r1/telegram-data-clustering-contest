DRUG CHECK

Glasgow minister urges police to probe behind bars ecstasy dealing of scumbag who supplied MDMA that killed girl, 16

20 Nov 2019, 21:22 by Ruth Warrander

A MINISTER has urged cops to probe whether the scumbag who supplied the ecstasy that killed teen Chelsea Bruce is still dealing from jail.

Rev Brian Casey called for police to act after we revealed caged Lewis Reid’s Twitter and Snapchat continues to tout cocaine for £80 a gram.

The clergyman, who led the funeral of tragic Chelsea, 16, at Springburn Parish Church in Glasgow, said: “The police need to investigate. It’s worrying.

"More focus should be put on the dealers than the addicts — it would be far more productive.

"Drugs are a massive problem in our area.”

We told how a pal of Reid, from Paisley, is thought to be running his 'Gucci' social media but it’s not known if he too is involved.

A message posted this week read: “Prop their £80 a gram. More weights call number if need about Glasgow/Pollok/Paisley all night. Save number.”

And a message on Twitter from Reid, of Paisley, advertises the Snapchat account and offers “1g for 40, 2 for 70, 3 for 100, 2g of prop £140.”

He even mocked cops with a crude “F*** the CID” spelled out in white powder.

Reid - nicknamed Gucci - is behind bars after admitting 22 offences between August 2018 and July.

Chelsea, from Glasgow, died in July after taking MDMA supplied by Reid.
