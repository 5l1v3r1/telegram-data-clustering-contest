Ghanaian Actor Emmanuel Armah Speaks For The First Time After Suffering Stroke

20 Nov 2019, 21:03 by BB Jones

Veteran actor, Emmanuel Armah has started talking bits by bits after he was ‘taken down’ by stroke few years ago.

The stroke according to report kept him bedridden and invariably kept him out of the screens.

The illness has left the actor suffering from speech impairment, a condition in which the ability to produce speech sounds that are necessary to communicate with others is impaired.

In his first interview granted to One Ghana TV, the ailing was full of praise for Mr Samuel Nyamekye, CEO of Miracle Film Production for his pivotal role played in Ghana Movie industry.
