Oman weather: Aqbat Baushar road closed

20 Nov 2019, 21:41 by Times New Service

Muscat: The Royal Oman Police has closed Aqbat Baushar road on both directions due to heavy rainfall and overflowing wadis. ROP has advised road commuters to use the Amerat-Wadi Adi road.

The Royal Oman Police has called on citizens and residents to stay at home with their families to ensure their safety. “If you dont have to go out in rain, we advise you to stay with your family. Your safety is our top priority,” ROP added.
