Cristiano Ronaldo, Maurizio Sarri 'set for clear-the-air talks'

Cristiano Ronaldo will allegedly hold clear-the-air talks with Juventus boss Maurizio Sarri.

20 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Darren Plant

After his early withdrawal against AC Milan before the international break, Ronaldo was said to have been left unhappy with the decision of his head coach.

However, Ronaldo has been able to come through two international fixtures with Portugal unscathed, putting him in a position to feature for Juventus at the weekend.

According to Tuttosport, the pair are scheduled to hold discussions before the meeting with Atalanta BC in a bid to resolve any lingering differences.

Despite a number of inconsistent performances from both Ronaldo and Juventus, the Italian champions have remained at the top of the Serie A standings.
