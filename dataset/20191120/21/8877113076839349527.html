Schedule Change: 'Days of Our Lives' to Air at 3 a.m.

20 Nov 2019, 21:31

Due to NBC News' coverage of the Impeachment Hearings, Wednesday's episode of "Days of Our Lives" did not air at its regularly scheduled time.

The Nov. 20, 2019 episode will now air at 3 a.m. Thursday, Nov. 21.

If you are setting this in a DVR, we recommend building in some extra time at the end to make sure you receive all of the broadcast.
