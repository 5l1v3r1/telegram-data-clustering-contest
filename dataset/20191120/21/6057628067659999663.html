Ana samun karuwar muggan laifuka a Agadez

20 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Michael Kuduson

Rahotanni daga yankin Agadez na  Janhuriyar Nijar na cewa ana samun karuwar kazaman hare-hare wanda Hukumomin yankin ke cewa suna iyakacin kokarin su domin magancewa.
Wakilinmu Omar Sani na dauke da wannan rahoto daga Agadez.
