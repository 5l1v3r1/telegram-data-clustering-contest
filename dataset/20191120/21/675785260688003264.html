Caitlyn Jenner's post-surgery sex life after 'uncomfortable' intimacy with Kris Jenner

I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here star Caitlyn Jenner - who is now 'dating' Sophia Hutchins - said sex with her ex, Kris Jenner, was awkward

20 Nov 2019, 21:30 by Frances Kindon

When Caitlyn and Kris Jenner tied the knot in 1991, their sex life was "good and frequent".

But it wasn't long before the gender dysphoria issues that had plagued I'm A Celebrity star Caitlyn from the age of 10 resurfaced and put a dampener on their love-making.

While their intimacy was always “imbued” with love, Cait - who was called Bruce before her transition - admitted the experience was an awkward one.

"My attitude is no different than it was in high school: I'm just not entirely comfortable with it," she wrote in her autobiography, The Secrets of My Life.

Indeed, before Kris, the Olympian had slept with just two other women - wives Chrystie Crownover and Linda Thompson.

And after her 2015 transition started, she remained decidedly on the fence about whether she would sleep with men, women or both.

"Of the most important things in my life, sex is beyond the bottom; it has been that way for a long time," she explained.

“A future female companion? I think about that. A future female sexual companion? Not happening, at least for now, and perhaps not ever,” Caitlyn, 70, wrote in her memoirs.

At the time, she hinted she may actually have been more open to meeting a man.

“A future male sexual companion? I have never had the inclination. But maybe that attitude might possibly change if I have the Final Surgery," she continued.

She was later linked to transgender actress Candis Cayne, with the pair reportedly spotted kissing at a salon (claims Cait denied) before the so-called 'Final Surgery' to remove her penis took place in 2017.

“The surgery was a success, and I feel not only wonderful but liberated," she wrote, insisting the body part was 'just a penis' that was of no 'use'.

"I just want to have all the right parts. I am also tired of tucking the damn thing in all the time," joked the reality star, who also underwent a nose job, boob job, tracheal shave, jaw reshaping and beard removal as part of her transition.

Then reports claimed she was dating transgender model Sophia Hutchins, 23.

And in 2017, Caitlyn hinted that she was once again enjoying intimacy, telling The Times she'd previously been "no good" at sex because it meant “giving", adding, "[but] I believe I am better now."

Meanwhile, Caitlyn and Sophie wasted no time moving in together and have spent the last two years jet-setting across the globe.

Acknowledging their reported relationship, Caitlyn's step-daughter Khloe Kardashian described Sophia as, "really sweet."

"She’s younger, but she’s not bothering anyone. She’s super sweet," she said in a Divorce Sucks! podcast.

And while neither Cait or Sophie have officially confirmed their relationship status, the latter described them as "partners in everything we do".

"We’re inseparable, we’re business partners, we live together, we share a dog, we share family, we share a life together," she said.
