‘SNL’ has Jennifer Lopez, Eddie Murphy and Lizzo on deck for December

20 Nov 2019, 21:42 by Marisa Dellatto

“Saturday Night Live” is giving us the gift of hosts just in time for the holidays — Jennifer Lopez is hosting “SNL.”

The “Hustlers” star and soon-to-be Super Bowl co-headliner will take the stage at Rockefeller Center on Dec. 7, along with musical guest DaBaby.

J.Lo previously appeared on the show in 2001 and 2010, both times doing double duty as host and musical guest.

DaBaby will be making his inaugural appearance, fresh off news that his song “Suge” was nominated for a Grammy Award Wednesday.

In a tweet Tuesday, NBC’s iconic sketch comedy series also announced that Scarlett Johansson will host with musical guest Niall Horan, formerly of One Direction, on Dec. 14.

Horan’s former boy bandmate Harry Styles did double duty as host and musical guest duty this past weekend on the Nov. 16 show.

The holiday show, on Dec. 21, will be hosted by former cast member Eddie Murphy. Lizzo will make her “SNL” premiere that night as the musical guest. The singer-rapper star led 2020’s Grammy nods, receiving eight nods.
