Person wounded in shooting near school in Brooklyn

20 Nov 2019, 21:29

BEDFORD-STUYVESANT, Brooklyn -- Police say one person was wounded in a shooting near a school in Brooklyn Wednesday.

It happened around 3 p.m. near the intersection of Patchen Avenue and Marion Street in Bedford-Stuyvesant.

The victim was transported to a nearby hospital and is expected to survive.

There is no word yet on what led to the shooting.
