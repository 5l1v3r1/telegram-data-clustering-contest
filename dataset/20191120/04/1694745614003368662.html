16 Cheeses You Have to Get From Costco

20 Nov 2019, 04:20 by Anna Monette Roberts

Costco's cheese selection is truly impressive, but it's hard to know what to grab when you have so many options. And if you're shopping for snacks to serve at an event, you surely don't want to go with the wrong variety. Rest assured, just like there are healthy Costco go-tos and even Keto favorites from the big box store, there are cheeses that won't disappoint. We've done the legwork and tried each of these cheeses for you (it's a tough job, but somebody's got to do it!) so you can be sure you're picking up a crowd-pleaser.

Prices and products reflect what is available via Instacart in the San Francisco/Bay Area.

1 Yancey's Fancy Smoked Gouda ($9)

Yancey's Fancy Smoked Gouda has a velvety texture and salty-smoky flavor that will appeal to even the pickiest of cheese eaters. Try it in Guy Fieri's Super Melty Cheese Sauce.

2 Tillamook 2013 Makers Reserve ($11)

One of my favorite snacking cheddars is this Tillamook 2013 Makers Reserve. It's not sharp in the slightest and has a nutty, delicate flavor.

3 Kirkland Signature Fresh Goat Cheese ($8)

Baked and on a charcuterie board, Kirkland Signature Fresh Goat Cheese has infinite possibilities.

4 Marin Petite Cheese Variety ($13)

Marin Petite Cheese Variety lets you try four styles of French cheeses. Petite Breakfast doesn't have a rind and offers a mild flavor. The other three have a white rind with gooey interiors. I like to let them ripen in the fridge a week or two to accentuate the flavors.

5 BelGioioso Crumbled Gorgonzola Italian Blue Cheese ($9)

One of my favorite blue cheeses is BelGioioso Crumbled Gorgonzola Italian Blue Cheese. Its tangy taste pairs well with veggie burgers and you can eat it on the keto diet.

6 Dutch Tradition Gouda Cheese Wedge ($14)

Dutch Tradition Gouda Cheese Wedge is nothing like smoked gouda. Its caramelized, savory flavor is sort of reminiscent of parmesan. Prepare to eat the entire block in no time.

7 Kirkland Signature Isigny French Brie ($7)

Buttery and ripe, this double-cream Kirkland Signature Isigny French Brie pairs oh so nicely with fresh baguette. However, you can also bake it for a warm, gooey appetizer.

8 Kirkland Signature Pecorino Romano Cheese ($14)

You can't make cacio e pepe (Italian "mac" and cheese) without Kirkland Signature Pecorino Romano Cheese. However, slices of this cheese taste incredible solo too.

9 Kerrygold Dubliner Irish Cheese ($13)

If Kerrygold butter gives you life, then you must try its Dubliner Irish Cheese, essentially a bold cheddar.

10 Kirkland Signature Imported Manchego ($17)

This Spanish sheep's milk cheese, Kirkland Signature Imported Manchego, and a fresh, crusty loaf of bread are all you need for an excellent app.

11 Emmi Roth Le Gruyere Cheese ($13)

Sometimes, you just can't beat a hunk of Emmi Roth Le Gruyere Cheese . Let it be known, it's a product of Switzerland â€” not some knockoff.

12 Valbreso Feta French White Cheese ($9)

Salads aren't complete without a block of Valbreso Feta French White Cheese. Or drizzle honey, olive oil, mint, and orange zest over the top for an enticing appetizer to pair with crackers.

13 Leerdammer Original Emmentaler Cheese ($8)

Leerdammer Original Emmentaler Cheese offers a nutty but mild dairy flavor. Excellent on a cheese plate, the emmentaler tastes excellent melted into a grilled cheese too.

14 El Cazo Mexicano Queso Fresco Casero ($10)

Beyond crumbling El Cazo Mexicano Queso Fresco Casero on top of tacos or salads, it tastes great on top of guacamole and beans too.

15 BelGioioso Cheese Mozzarella Fresh Slices ($10)

BelGioioso Cheese Mozzarella Fresh Slices are ready to be served in a caprese salad or melted into a comforting casserole.

16 Garcia Baquero Spanish Cheese Selection ($12)

You don't have to settle on one flavor of Spanish cheese with this trio pack: Garcia Baquero Spanish Cheese Selection . Manchego, Tipsy Goat, and Iberico to keep a cheese plate interesting.
