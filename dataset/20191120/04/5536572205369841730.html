Seahawks Defender After Failing To Catch & Tackle Lamar Jackson: “That Muthaf*cka Fast” (VIDEO)

20 Nov 2019, 04:17 by Darrelle Lincoln

Lamar Jackson is freakishly fast. There aren’t too many defenders in the league that can catch him from behind. You’d be lucky to grab a foot and hope he trips as full speed.

Such was the case when the Baltimore Ravens faced off against the Seattle Seahawks a few weeks ago when Jackson got loose out of the pocket and was tripped up, because a few Seahawks defenders couldn’t catch him.

One of them could be heard saying, “That motherf*cka fast” while on the bench.
