Videos: boyfriend Rohman Shawl throws Sushmita Sen a surprise birthday bash

20 Nov 2019, 04:34 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Yesterday, Bollywood actress and former Miss Universe, Sushmita Sen turned a year older as she celebrated her 44th birthday. Yes, you heard it right, the diva is aging gracefully like a fine wine and after looking at her we must say 40s is the new 30s. She is one of those actresses, who could still make your hearts beat faster.

As we all know, Sushmita shares a special bond with her daughters and boyfriend Rohman Shawl. Her Instagram posts majorly speak about her family, bonding and love. Continuing the trend, she treated her fans with yet another overwhelming event that took place in her life, yesterday. She posted a few videos of her surprise birthday party, which was secretly organised by her boyfriend Rohman and her daughters Renee and Alisah. The surprise bash happened to be one of the memorable moments of her life as the actress was in complete surprise and happiness after learning about it.

In the video, we can see the terrace area was shining with some decorative lighting while the colorful balloons were all over the venue. The decoration also included some heartwarming notes that were left hanging. A very stylish cake was specially customised for the actress and was placed in specially made tent.

Sush posted the videos and expressed her happiness with a long caption which reads, "What a magical #birthday EVERYTHING I COULD’VE WISHED FOR & MORE❤️❤️❤️😁💃🏻🌈 Thank you jaan @rohmanshawl for this ALL HEART Birthday Surprise!!! I love you😍💋Everyone acted sooooo well...I really had no idea!!!😅👏 And there it was...a magical terrace with lights, balloons, tent, yummy cake & heartfelt notes suspended all over...How simply loved you make me feel Alisah, Renée, @rohmanshawl @pritam_shikhare @nupur_shikhare & Rajesh!!!🤗❤️😊Even my other baby, My puddle called #darling came to surprise me!!!😀😇🤗 #sharing #cherished #happiness #love #family #friends #celebrations #44yrs #birthdaygirl 😄💃🏻❤️🎵 I love you guys!! #duggadugga 💃🏻💃🏻💃🏻."
