Love Island reunion as Amber Gill parties with pals, but Molly-Mae Hague steers clear

Her reported £1million deal with MissPap is double that of Love Island rival Molly-Mae who is said to have made £500,000 to promote her Pretty Little Thing collection

20 Nov 2019, 04:17 by Sam Elliott, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sam-elliott/

There was a Love Island reunion as Amber Gill shone as she showed off her new collection at her big launch in London.

The star didn't disappoint at the Amber Rose Gill X MissPap party dinner at trendy London hot spot Restaurant Ours on Tuesday night.

She was joined by Anna Vakili as well as former contestants India Reynolds, Francesca Allen, Yewande Biala and Kaz Crossley.

As expected it was the 2019 winner who stole the show as her gold mini dress wowed everyone.

Her pals were on hand to support her as the 22-year-old got down to work after she secured the big money contract.

The Love Island winner, who was cruelly dumped by Greg O'Shea via text, landed the £1million fashion deal weeks after leaving the show.

Molly-Mae Hague was nowhere to be seen as her old rival steered clear.

The runner up only scooped a £500,000 deal with her Pretty Little Thing collection.

A source close to Amber told The Sun Online : "Amber is on cloud nine - she signed the £1million deal and couldn't be happier.

"She's had every major brand in the UK fighting to sign her - it's been insane. Amber couldn't be more excited about the future and what else is in store."

Amber is clearly excited and told her fans: "Oh hey it's just me your NEW MISS PAP babe.

"I honestly cannot believe it!! Soooo excited to be part of the fresh Miss Pap brand...

"It's been a long anticipated announcement by me and I've been dying to tell you all...

"I cannot wait for you to see what I've been working on you know it's going to be GOOOD."
