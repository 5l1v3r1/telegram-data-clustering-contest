The third season of The Crown hits the tip of the iceberg that is Prince Charles and Camilla Parker Bowles’ tumultuous relationship. It shows how the eventual couple met, the fact that they were dating while Camilla was on a break from her future first husband, Andrew Parker Bowles, and how Princess Anne was actually dating Andrew for a quick sec while Camilla and Charles were together. It’s...a lot!
