Marjaavaan Box Office Day 5 Early Trends: Witnesses A Further Drop!

With the initiation of the weekdays, the movie witnessed a routine drop, but now things are turning serious as it further dropped yesterday.

20 Nov 2019, 04:37 by Koimoi.com Team

Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh, and Tara Sutaria starrer Marjaavaan is edging towards the end of its first week, and the movie has added decent numbers to its box office collections. With the initiation of the weekdays, the movie witnessed a routine drop, but now things are turning serious as it further dropped yesterday.

Post adding collections to a total of 28.57 crores till its Day 4, the movie as per the early trends flowing in, has added another 3-4 crores to its kitty. This is a further drop, and now things seem to be turning serious as the movie should have at least maintained its momentum in its first week.

The overall collections of Marjaavaan now will stand in the range of 31.57-32.57 crores. One can only hope for the coming weekend to work in favour of the movie as it will majorly determine the lifetime collections of this Milap Zaveri directorial.

On the release day, makers of Marjaavaan included a new song, ‘Raghupati Raghav Raja Ram’ and it got the viewers even more excited for the movie.

After the track of ‘Marjaavaan‘ was released on various platforms, it has been lapped up by the netizens. Since its release, it has already crossed 2.3 million views on YouTube. Tweeples have started pouring in their love for it.

Social media was flooded with millennials talking about it. Some netizens spoke about Palak Muchhal’s lovely voice and some were talking about the stars.

‘Raghupati Raghav Raja Ram’ is sung by Palak Muchhal and the music is given by Tanishk Bagchi. The lyrics of ‘Raghupati Raghav Raja Ram’ is penned by Manoj Muntashir.
