Ryan Giggs says great things to come after Wales qualify for Euro 2020

Aaron Ramsey scored twice as the Dragons beat Hungary 2-0 in Cardiff.

20 Nov 2019, 04:35

Ryan Giggs believes qualifying for Euro 2020 marks a bright start for Welsh football.

Aaron Ramsey scored twice as Wales beat Hungary 2-0 in Cardiff on Tuesday to reach their second successive European Championship.

Wales made the semi-finals at Euro 2016 – their first major tournament for 58 years – but followed that up by failing to qualify for the 2018 World Cup in Russia.
