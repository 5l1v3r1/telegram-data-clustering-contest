There’s A Dumb Theory Kylie’s Sisters, Already Rich, Are Jealous Of Her $600M Payday

20 Nov 2019, 02:01 by By PEDESTRIAN.TV

There’s a silly, silly theory going around about the Kardashian / Jenner family following the news of Kylie Jenner‘s latest business movie.

If you’re unaware, Kylie recently became USD$600 million richer after she sold a majority stake in her makeup company, Kylie Cosmetics, to beauty beasts Coty Inc., which is a big power move.

So then today, the Daily Mail published a yarn about how Kylie’s sisters including Kim, Kourtney, Khloé, and Kendall have apparently snubbed her big business move because they’re not posting about it on Instagram. Yes, really.

In the last day or so, Kim has used her IG Story to give a shoutout to the family’s loyal as hell friends Malika and Khadijah, who just released their own reality TV show. She also promoted her Solutionwear, the one that used to be called Kimono.

Khloé used her clout to plug perfume by “The OG trio” on her feed. But she supported Kylie’s latest Christmas collection on her IG Story.

Kourtney promoted her latest blog posts on her website Poosh as well as the perfume.

And Kendall posted a cute piccie sponsored by Proactiv, as per usual.

Mum / manager Kris Jenner also shared some promotional pictures of Kylie’s holiday collection.

With the internet being the internet, the DM’s yarn copped a lot of comments and the theory flew… and turned ugly. For example, this comment: “They’re all jealous of one another, big fallouts in the future and I cannot wait.”

Christ.

But to be honest, the family probably celebrated the big win privately or they’re gearing up for a public celebration, like what they did when Kendall landed the coveted September Vogue issue cover.

Or, ya know, maybe they’re just waiting for Kylie to actually acknowledge it on Instagram because right now, she’s knee deep in promoting her Christmas collection.

Also, they’re all already fucking rich.
