Pariya’s death sad, says Tinubu - The Nation Newspaper

20 Nov 2019, 02:47

ALL Progressives Congress (APC) National stalwart Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu has described the death of Umar Pariya as sad.

In a tweet on his handle on Tuesday, he said: “I’m deeply saddened by the death of Umar Pariya. I knew him very well. He was very cerebral.

“An excellent human being, Umar Pariya had a welcoming and accommodating spirit. May his soul rest in the bosom of the Lord.

“My condolences to Vice President Atiku Abubakar over the loss. May VP Atiku, family, friends and associates of the late Pariya find the strength to live through this sudden and untimely demise.”
