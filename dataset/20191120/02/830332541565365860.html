Memorial's Kapinus picks Penn State

Will play college basketball for the Nittany Lions

20 Nov 2019, 02:43 by Jay Wilson

MADISON - Madison Memorial basketball star Leilani Kapinus announced Tuesday she'll be playing college basketball at Penn State.

Kapinus was ranked as the 30th prospect in the country for the Class of 2020 by ESPN.

Kapinus missed all of last season with a knee injury, but is recovered and competing in her senior year for Memorial. 

She's a 5-11 guard who chose Penn State over Wisconsin, Minnesota, Arizona and LSU.
