Badgers up to 12th in CFP Rankings

Up two spots from last week

20 Nov 2019, 02:46 by Jay Wilson

MADISON - The University of Wisconsin football team moved up two spots to 12th in the latest College Football Playoff rankings released Tuesday night. 

The Badgers (8-2 overall, 5-2 Big Ten) host Purdue in their final home game of the season Saturday at 3 pm at Camp Randall.
