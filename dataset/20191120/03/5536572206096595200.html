Dez Bryant Blasts Colin Kaepernick For Starting Movement After He Got Benched (TWEETS)

20 Nov 2019, 03:45 by Darrelle Lincoln

Dez Bryant had something on his mind on Tuesday, so he took to Twitter to get some answers.

The free agent wide receiver posted a long rant to Colin Kaepernick that asked him numerous questions about what his next step was as far as actions after he started kneeling during the Anthem in 2016. He also wanted to know why he wanted to get back into a league that does not agree with his views and how him getting a job will change anything.
