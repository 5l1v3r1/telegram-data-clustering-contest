If You Loved The Skywhale, There’s Now A Giant Floating Skywhale Daddy

20 Nov 2019, 03:54 by Joseph Earp

I mean I don’t really know where to start with this story about the unveiling of the multi-boobied Skywhale balloon’s kid, so we might as well start right at the multi-boobied beginning.

In 2013, artist Patricia Piccinini designed a hot air balloon to fly above Canberra as part of its centenary celebrations. There’s no easy way to describe the balloon, but I guess I’ll just tell you that it’s very big, looks kinda like a greenish, dead-eyed whale, and that it is pretty much covered in boobies.
