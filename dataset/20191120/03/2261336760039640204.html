UFC heavyweight contender Francis Ngannou teases ‘big news coming soon’

20 Nov 2019, 03:00 by Mookie Alexander

After a mostly disastrous 2018, Francis Ngannou’s 2019 has perhaps set him up for a massive 2020.

The UFC heavyweight contender has been vocal about wanting the next title shot, accusing the promotion of treating him like a gatekeeper. Well after voicing his displeasure in media interviews, Ngannou has since tweeted of his recent meeting with Dana White and Chief Legal Officer Hunter Campbell, teasing a major announcement is on the horizon.
