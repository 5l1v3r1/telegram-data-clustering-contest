First Borderlands 3 expansion Moxxi’s Heist of the Handsome Jackpot out December

20 Nov 2019, 16:00

Gearbox has revealed the first campaign add-on for Borderlands 3, featuring Mad Moxxi and a space casino designed by Handsome Jack.

The first major campaign add-on for Borderlands 3 is called Moxxi’s Heist Of The Handsome Jackpot and it’ll be out on 19 December.

Whatever else you say about them, the Borderlands games have always been known for their imaginative and generously large DLC packs.

This new one will involve taking control of a derelict casino space station built by Borderlands 2 bad guy Handsome Jack.

That seems to mean a mixture of retro enemies and brand-new ones, as well new Legendary weapons and gear, cosmetic items, side missions, and crew challenges.
