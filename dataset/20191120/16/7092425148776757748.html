Ducati's latest additions..

20 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Dave Humphreys

The onslaught of new models from Ducati for 2020 shows no sign of letting up — as the company introduced the latest additions to its line-up.

Following the Streetfighter V4, the Panigale V4 gets some of the trick bits from the range-topping V4 R.

These will include larger side vents for better cooling, lateral wings and a Plexiglas screen, while the Front Frame design gains more stiffness to improve front-end feel.

Below that is the new Panigale V2, which gets a whole new look while also becoming more comfortable for sports riders thanks to a new seat design and a more compliant suspension setup.

It gets a single-sided swingarm and a neat under-engine exhaust with a side-mounted silencer. Power from the 955cc Superquadro motor comes in at 155hp and 104Nm of torque.

For the trendy folk around town, the Scrambler range gains a new Icon Dark model serving as the entry-point to the line-up.

Ducati also gave a preview of two Scrambler concepts that are due to debut at EICMA 2019 later this month.

New colour schemes for the Diavel 1260 gives it an ever sexier look than ever before.

A mean-looking Dark Stealth is available on the standard version and is undoubtedly on Batman’s Christmas list.

Ducati has also added a new Red with white rims colour scheme for the sportier S model.

For the globetrotters, the Multistrada 1260 S Grand Tour is ideally kitted out to let you comfortably and quickly cover some far-flung adventures.
