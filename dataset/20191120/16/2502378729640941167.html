Billie Eilish and Lizzo Lead The 2020 Grammy Award Nominations

20 Nov 2019, 16:06 by Allison Stubblebine

See all the nominations, here

The 2020 Grammy Awards are almost upon us but until the big show next year, the nominations will be enough to stew over until then. It's been a long year of music; from Ariana Grande's breakup album to end all breakup albums thank u, next to Lil Nas X's viral fame with "Old Town Road," this Grammy season is sure to be unlike any other. Ahead of the big show on January 26 at 8pm EST, scour the list below to see who is up for awards in every major and minor category, and start concocting theories as to who win it all.
