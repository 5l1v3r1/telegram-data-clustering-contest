The Late Late Toy Show 2019 trailer is finally here

The Late Late Toy show was the most watched Irish television programme last year

20 Nov 2019, 16:25 by Mark Corcoran

The official trailer for the Late Late Toy Show has been released, with the greatest night in Irish television almost upon us.

In the video, marshmallows, Christmas jumpers and popcorn angels are the order of the day, as one little boy and his family prepare to settle in to watch Tubridy showcase some of the hottest toys from this year.

In 2018, The Late Late Toy Show  was the most watched television programme of the year, with over 1.5 million people tuning in to watch the show on RTÉ One.

The show, which had the theme of The Greatest Showman, was watched by an average audience of over 1.3 million live on the night.

This year's theme is a closely-guarded secret but is sure to thrill audiences.

Kids will get to stay up late next Friday, November 29th as the Toy show kicks off at 9.35pm.
