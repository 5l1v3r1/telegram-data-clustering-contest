Arsenal team news: Injury, suspension list vs. Southampton

Arsenal host Southampton in their first game after the international break hoping to end a dire run of form.

20 Nov 2019, 16:18 by Sean Wilson

The Gunners are without a win in their last five games across all competitions and lost 2-0 last time out against Leicester City.

Pressure is beginning to grow on Unai Emery as Arsenal are now eight points adrift of Manchester City in fourth, and have half the points of Premier League leaders Liverpool.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest injury and suspension news for Arsenal ahead of this fixture.

Dani Ceballos

Status: Minor doubt
Type of injury: Thigh
Possible return date: November 23 (vs. Southampton)

The on-loan midfielder suffered a hamstring problem that forced him to miss the defeat against the Foxes, but following a period of rest over the international break, he could be fit to return to action against Southampton.

ARSENAL'S SUSPENSION LIST

Arsenal have no players who are absent through suspension for the game this weekend.
