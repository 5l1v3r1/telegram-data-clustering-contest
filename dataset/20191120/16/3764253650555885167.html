Deontay Wilder dons bare-chested outfit ahead of rematch with Luis Ortiz

20 Nov 2019, 16:45

- Deontay Wilder wears a bizarre bare-chested garment to Las Vegas ahead of his Ortiz rematch

- The American boxer will face the Cuban champion on Saturday, November 23, 2019

- The WBC heavyweight champion shows no sign of worry as he looks forward to having another victory

Deontay Wilder has shown no sign of worry ahead of his heavyweight fight against Cuban boxer Luis Ortiz after the American pugilist arrived La Vegas in a bare-chested garment that is now a subject of internet discussion.

The WBC heavyweight champ, 32, will take on the 40-year-old Cuban fighter on Saturday, November 23, in another famous rematch.

But the highlight of the pre-fight show was the 32-year-old's bizarre cloth suggesting he is full of confidence of another win over Ortiz.
