Initial Betting Line Released For Alabama-Auburn Game

20 Nov 2019, 16:29 by Andrew McCarty

Over the weekend, Alabama star quarterback Tua Tagovailoa suffered a season-ending injury that put his career in jeopardy. As Tua attempted to escape two Mississippi State defenders, he was tackled and dislocated his hip.

He also suffered a fracture, which will cost him the rest of the season. Initial reports suggested the injury could be career-threatening, but recent reports suggest he will make a full recovery.

With Tua gone, the Crimson Tide turn to backup quarterback Mac Jones. Despite losing Tagovailoa for the season, Alabama remained at No. 5 in the most recent College Football Playoff rankings.

However, Las Vegas thinks Jones is a significant downgrade at the quarterback position. The Crimson Tide are just 2.5-point favorites against Auburn in the final week of the regular season.
