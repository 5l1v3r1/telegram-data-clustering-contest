Shark launches Black Friday sale with £200 savings on vacuum cleaners

The site is already seeing products sell out in its Black Friday sale

20 Nov 2019, 16:46 by Jonathan Yates

Shark has unveiled its Black Friday deals early with big discounts on vacuum cleaners.

If you want to snap up a brand new super-powered vacuum ahead of Christmas there's a number to choose from in the sale.

Customers can purchase exclusive offers on the website which comes with free additional filter kits.

Currently the best discount is on the 'NZ801' model which is priced at £199.99 instead of £379.99.

Others include cordless vacuums 'IF200' for £199.99 instead of £349.99 and 'IC160' for £279.99 instead of £499.99.

Plus there's a number of TruePet models included in the sale ideal for pet owners to help remove pet hair from carpets, stairs and sofas.

This includes 'NZ801' which is priced at £199.99 instead of £349.99 in the Black Friday sale.

When purchasing a vacuum cleaner that's included in the sale you won't need a coupon code as the discounts will automatically be applied.

The hugely popular retailer has already sold out of a number of products including a £149.99 and a £169.99 item which shoppers have snapped up.

Shark is also a favourite of the likes of Mrs Hinch who often recommends the products to her millions of followers.

Previously Amazon has cut the price of a Shark vacuum cleaner   to £169.  The saving of £130 is one of the biggest price drops on the vacuum cleaner this year.
