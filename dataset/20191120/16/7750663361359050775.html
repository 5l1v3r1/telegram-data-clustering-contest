Chelsea team news: Injury, suspension list vs. Manchester City

Chelsea make the trip to the Etihad Stadium holding a narrow advantage over Manchester City in the Premier League standings.

20 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Darren Plant

As a result of a six-match winning streak in the top flight, Chelsea sit one point ahead of the defending champions ahead of Saturday evening's showdown in the north-west.

However, the treatroom room at the Blues continues to be busy, and Frank Lampard has several selection issues ahead of the return to another of his former teams.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest injury and suspension list for Chelsea as he prepare for their first outing after the international break.

Christian Pulisic

Status: Minor doubt
Type of injury: Hip
Possible return date: November 23 (vs. Man City)

Before being substituted against Crystal Palace two weeks ago, Pulisic sustained a problem with his hip. However, while the issue kept him out of international duty with USA, the winger could be involved at the weekend.

Antonio Rudiger

Status: Out
Type of injury: Groin
Possible return date: December 4 (vs. Aston Villa)

Before the international break, it was revealed that Rudiger had undergone surgery on a groin injury. The German is not expected to be back in contention until December.

Ruben Loftus-Cheek

Status: Out
Type of injury: Achilles
Possible return date: Unknown

Loftus-Cheek has been out since May with a major Achilles injury. However, the midfielder is currently hoping to make his return before the end of the year.

Ross Barkley

Status: Out
Type of injury: Ankle
Possible return date: November 23 (vs. Man City)

Having been sidelined since October 19 with an ankle injury, Barkley subsequently missed out on representing England last week. However, the playmaker is closing in on a return to the squad ahead of the visit to Manchester.

CHELSEA'S SUSPENSION LIST

With Jorginho having served a one-match ban, Chelsea have no players who are suspended for this encounter.
