New variant TA-1165 of the Nokia smartphone with a Unisoc processor passes Bluetooth certification

20 Nov 2019, 16:01 by Nayan

We had earlier reported about a Nokia Phone/smartphone with TA-1208 variant appearing in the BIS certification in India and later passing the Bluetooth certification. Now, another variant TA-1165 of this smartphone has passed Bluetootth certification and it confirms that we are looking at a smartphone.

The smartphone may be the first Nokia smartphone to be powered by a Unisoc processor has passed Bluetooth certification. The Bluetooth certification reveals the Bluetooth 4.2 specs, LTE support and also the fact that it is powered by a Unisoc processor that is generally used on entry-level smartphones.

So far nothing else is known about this smartphone other than the fact that is an India-bound device, as its appearence in BIS certification revealed.
