Praye applaud Nana Akuffo Addo for bringing back Ghana Airways

20 Nov 2019, 16:53 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Music group Praye has applauded the Akuffo Addo led government for bringing back the country’s airline service thus the Ghana Airways.

A member of the group which pledge their support to the NPP towards the build-up to 2016 elections Cartel Big J aka Praye Tietia who spoke on behalf of the group comment comes after the Government signed a deal to acquire six new aeroplanes in Dubai.

He wrote: “Yaaaaayyyy ??FINALLY. LAUDABLE move Mr. President @nakufoaddo ?????”

The government of Ghana on Tuesday, November 19, 2019, signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with The Boeing Company and De Havilland Aircraft of Canada for the purchase of aeroplanes as part of efforts to re-launch an international airline at the Dubai Air Show.

“There is a growing demand for air travel to and from Ghana and we believe the advanced 787-9 Dreamliner gives us an efficient and flexible machine to launch a regional network and eventually serve international destinations in the future.

“The 787 has an excellent reputation for its operational performance, fuel efficiency and passenger experience and we are confident that we have the right partner for our new carrier,” the Minister of Aviation, Mr Kofi Adda who signed the deal on behalf of the country stated.
