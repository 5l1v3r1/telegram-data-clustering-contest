Coronation Street SPOILERS: David faces rapist Josh Tucker in court

Josh raped David last year

20 Nov 2019, 16:52 by Charlotte Rodrigues

David Platt is set to have one last face off with rapist Josh Tucker in court in upcoming Coronation Street scenes.

In the episodes David will have to stand against Josh, who is currently on remand for sexual assault.

In pictures obtained by The Sun, it shows David and Josh actors Jack P. Shepherd and Ryan Clayton filming on location.

It looks like Josh is dressed for court as he wears a suit, but the publication suggests David could have a meltdown.

The haidresser is seen approaching his attacker in the street and the ensuing argument looks more than a little heated.

Will David lash out? And could it jeopardise his case against Josh?

The last time David saw Josh was in prison.

David, who was inside for stealing money from his gran Audrey, was encouraged by his fellow inmates to attack his rapist during the prison riots.

However David decided to help Josh instead, and tried to get him away from the other prisoners.

But Josh went on to frame David for attempted murder when he was stabbed during the riots, meaning David had to spend longer in prison.

However, he soon came clean after being pressured by Paul Foreman to tell the truth and David was released from prison.
