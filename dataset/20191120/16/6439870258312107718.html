Two calves killed in Iredell County barn fire

20 Nov 2019, 16:22

IREDELL COUNTY, N.C. (WBTV) - A fire broke out at a barn in Iredell County Wednesday morning, killing two calves inside.

The fire broke out around 9 a.m. off of Tabor Road near Tatum Road.

No people were hurt in the blaze. Smoke was seen pouring from the area.
