Bucks introduce new 'Cream City' uniform

20 Nov 2019, 16:59 by Logan Rude

MILWAUKEE - The Bucks introduced a new "Cream City" uniform for the 2019-2020 season in a news release Wednesday.

According to the release, the cream colored uniform is a reference to to bricks that many of Milwaukee's early buildings were built with, giving the city the nickname "Cream City" The uniform features green and blue striping with the words "Cream City" displayed across the front of the jersey.

The Bucks will wear the new uniform 20 times throughout the 2019-2020 season, with the first appearance on Nov. 30, when the team plays the Charlotte Hornets at Fiserv Forum, according to the release.

Fans can now buy the new jersey on the team's website or at the Bucks Pro Shop inside Fiserv Forum.
