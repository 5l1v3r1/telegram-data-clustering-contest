Letter of the Day: City should get rid of flag poles

20 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Reader Letters

Letter to the editor from Olga E. Coates

Let’s just get rid of the flag poles. And, sooner would be better than later, before there is another new department with numerous new people hired at city hall to deal with the issue of which flag is deemed suitable and which is not.

There are more serious issues to be dealt with by our city than flags.

Olga E. Coates

Saskatoon
