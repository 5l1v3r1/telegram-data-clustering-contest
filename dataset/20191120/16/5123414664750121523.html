The Chemical Brothers, RÜFÜS DU SOL Lead 2020 Grammy Dance Nominations

Bonobo, Soulwax and Tycho also get nominated for the 2020 Grammys.

20 Nov 2019, 16:54 by Ryan Middleton

The Grammys revealed the nominees this morning and it seems as though they really looked to new artists with the main categories. Lizzo garnered eight nominations; Billie Eilish had six, as did Lil Nas X. In terms of dance and electronic, RUFUS DU Sol and The Chemical Brothers both get nominations in Best Dance Recording and Best Dance / Electronic Album.

The smash hit “Piece Of Your Heart” by Meduza gets a nomination for Best Dance Recording, while Dog Blood’s collab with Ty Dolla $ign gets the nod as well. Magnetic favorite Bonobo gets a nomination for “Linked.”
