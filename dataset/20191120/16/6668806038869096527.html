Tiger Shroff looks hot as hell in these pictures from the sets of Baaghi 3

20 Nov 2019, 16:47 by Filmfare

Tiger Shroff has never shied away from flaunting his chiselled abs and body.  The actor, who was last seen in War along with Hrithik Roshan, recently took to Instagram and shared pictures from his next. The actor looks hot as hell in the pictures from Serbia where he is currently shooting for Baaghi 3.

In the pictures, Tiger is seen in a rugged look flaunting his tanned muscular body in a sleeveless beige coloured jeans and shirt. He captioned the picture, “#Baaghi3 #Actionday2”. Check out the picture below. 

Helmed by Ahmed Khan and produced by Sajid Nadiadwala the film is a continuation to the Baaghi series. The movie is a hard-core action flick from the looks of it. It also stars Shradha Kapoor, Ankita Lokhande and Riteish Deshmukh in substantial roles. Baaghi 3 is said to hit the theatres on March 6, 2020. Tiger’s last movie War which was from the same genre crossed about 300 crore at the box office hence becoming the highest grossing action thriller of all time.
