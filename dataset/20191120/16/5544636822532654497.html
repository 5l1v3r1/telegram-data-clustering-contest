Babcock University Reveals What It’ll Do About Trending Sextape (Exclusive)

20 Nov 2019, 16:19 by Ridwan Yusuf

As a leaked video of two young persons having sex surfaced on social media platforms and got people talking on Wednesday, the authorities of Babcock University, Ilishan-Remo, Ogun State, Nigeria have exclusively told Concise News they are coming up with a statement soon.

The ‘performers’ in the clip, believed to be students of the missionary school were seen having sex in where appears to be a clinic. The video hit the cyber space on Tuesday and instantly went viral.

Although there are many narratives being pushed around as regards the source of the video, it has since dominated the conversation on Twitter, where a number of users have been registering their diverse opinion.

“We are still in a meeting. We are coming out with a Press Release in a short while. Just be patient, please,” an official of the institution who prefers not to be named disclosed to this online news medium via phone.
