I'm A Celebrity viewers complain the show is on too late

The show usually starts at 9pm

20 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity have complained that the show is on too late.

The episodes usually start at 9pm on ITV and run until either 10pm or 10:30pm.

But for some fans, it's on too late and they've asked ITV to put the episodes on at an earlier time.

One person said on Twitter: "Think it’s a bit selfish of ITV to assume everyone will be awake at 9 to watch I'm A Celeb."

Another wrote: "As much as I’m loving I’m A Celeb being back on, I’m so glad it finished at 10pm tonight, going to bed at almost 11 was a struggle for someone who’s in bed sleeping for half 9 every night."

A third added: "Why can’t @imacelebrity start at 8.00/8.30 rather than 9? It’s far too much of a commitment to stay awake until 10pm when you have 2 kids up at the crack of dawn!"
