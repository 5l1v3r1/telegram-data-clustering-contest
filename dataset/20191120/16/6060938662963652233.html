Priyanka Chopra congratulates Jonas Brothers for Grammy 2020 nomination; captions, "I love you Nick Jonas"

20 Nov 2019, 16:32 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Grammy 2020 nominations are out and Jonas Brothers' 'Sucker' has been nominated in the Best Pop Duo/ Group Performance category.

And now, Priyanka Chopra took to her Twitter congratulate the band and band members her hubby Nick Jonas, Joe Jonas and Kevin Jonas. Sharing a short clip of the song, she captioned the post, "So proud of you @jonasbrothers !! I love you @nickjonas @joejonas @kevinjonas

@RecordingAcad #GRAMMYs"
