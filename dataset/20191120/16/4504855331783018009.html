Police searching for missing Florida mother, infant daughter

20 Nov 2019, 16:54 by Johari Canty

WINTER HAVEN, Fla. (WSVN) — Police are searching for a missing Florida woman and her baby daughter.

Investigators said 32-year-old Heather Cunniff and her infant daughter, 3-month-old Destiny, were last seen in Winter Haven on Nov. 6.
