Shatta Wale indirectly gets Grammy Award nomination

20 Nov 2019, 18:40 by Adu Sarpeah

Shatta Wale has indirectly garnered Grammy Award’s nomination. Y’all know that Beyonce employed the services of Shatta Wale on “The Lion King Album” with regard to “Already” song.

The album has been nominated at Grammy Awards 2020 so logically, Shatta Wale is also a nominee.

The Lion King Album was nominated in the Best Pop Vocal Album category. It’s worth knowing that Beyonce’s “The Lion King Album” is competing with albums such as When we all fall asleep, where do we go? by Billie Eilish, Thank U, Nextb y Ariana Grande, NO.6 collaborations project by Ed Sheeran and lover by Taylor Swift.
