Jimmie Johnson: 2020 final year as full-time driver

20 Nov 2019, 18:07

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- Jimmie Johnson is the latest NASCAR superstar to climb out of his car, with the seven-time champion announcing Wednesday that 2020 will be his final season of full-time racing.

The winningest driver of his era will have a 19th season in the No. 48 Chevrolet and once again chase a record eighth championship. Johnson made the announcement in a video on social media.
