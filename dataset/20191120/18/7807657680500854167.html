Adobe Photoshop Elements 2020 is 40 percent off in the Microsoft Store

Windows 10 users now have another way to grab Adobe Photoshop Elements 2020.

20 Nov 2019, 18:04 by Sean Endicott

Source: Adobe

What you need to know

  • Adobe Photoshop Elements 2020 is now available through the Microsoft Store.
  • Photoshop Elements 2020 came out last month but was not available through the Microsoft Store until today.
  • Photoshop Elements 2020 is on sale in the Microsoft Store for 40 percent off.

Adobe Photoshop Elements 2020 is now available through the Microsoft Store. To celebrate its launch in the store, the app is 40 percent off, bringing the price down to $60. Photoshop Elements 2020 launched last month, but was only available through Adobe's website until today.

Photoshop Elements 2020 brings several new features to Adobe's photo editing software. Many of the features center around easy editing that looks clean and professional, including automatic colorization, one-click subject selection, and automatic skin smoothing. The app also includes a guided mode that can help you remove unwanted objects and eliminating distracting features like power lines.

Photoshop Elements 2020 uses Adobe Sensei technology to power intelligent features like object removal.

Unlike the full version of Adobe Photoshop, Photoshop Elements 2020 does not require a subscription.
