Shatta Wale Reacts After He Technically Gained A Grammy Nomination

20 Nov 2019, 18:55 by Ghbase New Writer

Before you dismiss this report as false, let me take my time to explain why you should not do so in a hurry.

The nominees for the 2020 Grammy Awards were announced earlier this morning on CBS by president/CEO Deborah Dugan and Academy Chair of the Board of Trustees and producer Harvey Mason Jr., along with host Alicia Keys and Bebe Rexha.

Beyoncé’s Lion King album gained four nominations in different categories and since Shatta Wale contributed to the album on the song ‘Already’, he’s technically in line for consideration.

This also means if Queen Bey wins the award, all the artists she featured which includes Shatta Wale are winners too.

The Lion King: The Gift album was nominated in Best Compilation Soundtrack For Visual Media & Best Song Written For Visual Media.

An overly excited Shatta Wale who is also aware of the information espoused above expressed his excitement in a tweet.

He wrote: At long last my name has entered #GRAMMYs ,thanks to the Beehive team !!!! Patience is truly the key !!! Pah pah pah pah
