Stonebwoy thanks Shatta Wale for gifting him his award

20 Nov 2019, 18:16 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

President of Bhim Nation Stonebwoy has finally responded to Shatta Wale after the latter willingly gifted him his award last weekend.

During the 4Syte Music Video Awards last weekend, Shatta Wale after winning the award for the most popular video called a representative from Stonebwoy on stage and gave him the award saying its a sign of their newfound friendship.

Well, Stonebwoy earlier today went to the 4Syte mansion and officially received his awards from the organizers.

Stonebwoy after taking his awards, the ‘Ololo’ hitmaker thanked his fans for voting for him to win the awards and also thanked his former enemy Shatta Wale for dashing him his award on the night.
