The truth on Alfredo Morelos and the Aston Villa transfer rumour that just won't go away

Rangers have ruled out a January exit for their top scorer but that hasn't stopped speculation from the English media.

20 Nov 2019, 18:51 by Liam Bryce, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/liam-bryce/

If you've bashed Alfredo Morelos' name into Google any time over the last month then chances are you'll have seen it alongside the words " Aston Villa".

Amid the Rangers star's prolific goalscoring form he's been linked with a host of clubs both in England and across Europe.

Eintracht Frankfurt, Saint-Etienne and Crystal Palace are just a selection.

But the link to Villa has been especially prominent, so much so that boss Dean Smith was asked about it earlier after Morelos netted a stunner to beat Porto in the Europa League.

In a play straight from page one of the manager's media handbook, Smith paid Morelos a compliment before remaining decidedly coy on a potential transfer.

He said: "I certainly saw his goal last night.

"It was a very good finish.

"He's a player who is contracted to another football club and we aren't a club that talks about them."

Smith refused to open the door on a transfer but he didn't exactly slam it shut either.

However, Record Sport understands the manager was simply being true to his word on not discussing other teams' players, and that there is no interest on Villa's part in the Colombian.

Morelos may well move on from Ibrox to the Premier League, but seemingly not to the West Midlands.

The Ibrox favourite has bagged 22 club goals already this term, followed up with a maiden international strike last week as well.

Steven Gerrard has continually ruled out a January exit for the striker, with the Rangers boss targeting a sustained Premiership title tilt and an extended run in Europe.

He said earlier in November: "I'm not confident, I'm sure, I'm 100 per cent sure he's not going anywhere."
