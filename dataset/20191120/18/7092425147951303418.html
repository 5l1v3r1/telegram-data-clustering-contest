Here's what is coming to Netflix over December and Christmas

20 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Nick Fitzgerald

December is the month the highly anticipated The Witcher is being released

A fantastic line-up of Netflix original movies, series and Christmas flicks for you and yours is coming to the streaming giant this December.

If you are running out things to watch, fear not, Netflix will release some absolute crackers in the lead up to Christmas.

Without further ado, here is the complete list:
