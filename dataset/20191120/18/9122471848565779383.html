Injury Update For Chicago Bears QB Mitchell Trubisky

20 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Matt Hladik

A season of struggles continued for Chicago Bears quarterback Mitch Trubisky on Sunday as the third-year pro was pulled late in a loss to the Los Angeles Rams with what is being labeled a hip pointer.

Trubisky had played poorly up until that point in the game, continuing what has been a disappointing year for him. As of now, there’s no guarantee he’ll be on the field this Sunday against the New York Giants.

According to Bears head coach Matt Nagy, Trubisky is day-to-day. He may practice today, though that has yet to be determined.

If Trubisky can’t play Sunday, backup Chase Daniel will take over the reins of the offense.
