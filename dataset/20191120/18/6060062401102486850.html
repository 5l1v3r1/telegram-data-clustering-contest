A Washington Post photo is gaining traction on social media for capturing the drama of President Donald Trump's impeachment hearings.

The photo shows Ambassador Gordon Sondland smirking at the camera, as a sea of photojournalists angle for a shot behind him.

One Twitter user compared it to famous photo of President Donald Trump, smiling to himself during a dinner at Mar-a-Lago.
