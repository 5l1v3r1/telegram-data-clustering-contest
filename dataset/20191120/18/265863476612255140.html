Kenya Moore Becomes The Baby Quest Foundation’s Ambassador – Check Out Her Announcement

20 Nov 2019, 18:45 by Ron Collins

Kenya Moore has an announcement for her fans, and she proudly posted it on her daughter’s social media account. Here’s what she had to say on baby Brooklyn Daly’s social media account.

‘As the newest ambassador for @babyquestgrants, I realize Infertility is difficult enough without having to worry about the financial burden it creates. Please join me in spreading kindness and giving financial help to those battling the high costs of fertility treatment. I encourage each of my followers to donate $5 (or more if you’d like) to Baby Quest Foundation,” Kenya began her post.

She continued and said: ‘Funds will be awarded to future grant recipients to help them pursue expensive procedures such as IVF. For those of us who have been blessed with children, let’s pass this along so that others may also experience the joys of parenthood.’
