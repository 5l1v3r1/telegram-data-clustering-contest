Sada zumunta: Jamus za ta fafata da Spain, Belgium da Portugal

20 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Michael Kuduson

Kocin Jamus coach Joachim Loew ya bayyana tabbacin cewa tawagarsa za ta fafata da ta Spain a watan Maris na shekara mai zuwa, sannan ya bayyana yiwuwar haduwa da Belgium da Portugal duk a matsayin kintsawa don tinkarar gasar cin kofin nahiyar Turai ta Euro 2020.

Tawagar Jamus din dai ta samu gurbin fafatawa a gasar Euro bayan ta lallasa Ireland ta Arewa da ci 6-1, wasan da dan wasan Jamus din Serge Gnabry ya zuba kwallaye 3 rigis a raga.

Loew ya ce hukumar kwallon kafar Jamus na daf da tabbatar da batun wasan sada zumunta tsakanin kasar da Spain, wasan da kafafen yada labaran Spain suka ruwaito cewa a bir4nin Madrid za a gwabza.
