Tomorrow: Watch Machine Head Unleash Chaos In A Tiny Dive Bar

Machine Head turn London upside down at The K! Pit, in association with Rizla

20 Nov 2019, 18:00

Tomorrow night, something huge is happening. It’s the biggest ever K! Pit, in association with Rizla. Why? You ask. Well the mighty Machine Head kicked seven shades of shit out of our favourite London dive bar. And they just wouldn’t stop! They played for over an hour, whipping the 50 deranged Head Cases into a sweaty frenzy.

What starts as a hellacious tribute to Burn My Eyes turns into a joyous round of covers – from Sepultura to Black Sabbath – as Blondies’ resident crowdsurfers and mosh goblins hurl themselves around the bar.

This is so far removed from what we’ve seen Machine Head do lately. In fact, it took place less than 24 hours after they played to 5,000 people at Brixton O2 Academy. This is a 100th of the size. Machine Head just don’t do intimate gigs any more, but what can we say, that’s the K! Pit for ya!

To watch the action for yourself, head to to our Facebook page tomorrow, November 21, at 7:30pm BST (2:30pm EST / 11:30am PST), for an exclusive livestream of Machine Head’s London K! Pit performance.
