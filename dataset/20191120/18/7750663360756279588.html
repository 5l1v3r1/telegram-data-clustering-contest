Real Madrid defender Sergio Ramos considering China offers?

Sergio Ramos is allegedly open to leaving Real Madrid for the Chinese Super League in 2020.

20 Nov 2019, 18:54 by Darren Plant

The 33-year-old has enjoyed a legendary career at the Bernabeu, making 621 appearances since signing for the club in 2005.

However, over the past 12 months, there has been speculation regarding the veteran's future, including the possibility of a lucrative switch to Asia.

According to reports in Spain, Ramos is not ruling out penning a lucrative contract with a team from China's top flight at some point next year.

With Ramos having just over 19 months left on his contract, Real are likely to make an effort to extend the Spaniard's stay.
