Prince Andrew stops royal duties with Queen's approval over Jeffrey Epstein friendship

The Duke of York released a statement addressing criticism over his friendship with sex offender Jeffrey Epstein

20 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Jenny Kirkham

Prince Andrew was given permission from The Queen to step back from public duties over the ongoing Jeffrey Epstein controversy.

The Duke of York issued a statement to say he would  be taking a break "for the foreseeable future” amid criticism over his relationship with Epstein.
