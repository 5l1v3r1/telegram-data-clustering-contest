Power shutdown in Kilpauk on Friday

20 Nov 2019, 18:58 by Special Correspondent

Power supply in Kilpauk will be suspended on Friday (November 22) owing to the maintenance work undertaken by Tangedco. Power disruption, in force from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m., will be undertaken in Kilpauk area including Sastri Nagar 1st to 12th Streets, Kilpauk police quarters, Pandaram Lane, Flowers Road, Audiappa Street, Saravana Perumal Street, Perumal Street, Diwan Rama Road, Lawders Gate Street, GE Koil Street, Pankajammal Colony, Ramasamy Street, Latangs Road, Gengureddy Road, Aramuddan Garden, Nehru Park, and parts of P.H. Road, Barnaby Road, Vellala Street and Dr. Alagappa Road.
