ESPN Analyst Releases Score Prediction For Georgia-Texas A&M

20 Nov 2019, 18:56 by Matt Hladik

Georgia and Texas A&M will meet this weekend in Athens with plenty at stake. The Bulldogs are currently the No. 4 in the College Football Playoff rankings.

Georgia has a stranglehold on the SEC East, but must win its final two regular season contests and the conference championship game to reach the playoff. Texas A&M is the next obstacle in their path.

The Aggies are 7-3 and all three of their losses are of the “good” variety. Alabama, Auburn and Clemson are the only teams to beat A&M, which has won four games in a row.

ESPN’s Bill Connelly has unveiled his score prediction for the game, using his SP+ formula.

He likes the Bulldogs by two touchdowns.
