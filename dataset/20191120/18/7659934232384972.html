Saudi Crown Prince Receives South Korean Defense Minister

20 Nov 2019, 18:00

Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense, received in Riyadh on Wednesday South Korean Defense Minister Jeong Kyeong-doo.

Discussions focused on bilateral cooperation, especially in the defense field.

They underscored the importance of the cooperation between Riyadh and Seoul in serving regional and international security and stability.

The meeting was attended by Prince Khalid bin Salman bin Abdulaziz, Deputy Minister of Defense; a number of senior officials and the South Korean minister's accompanying delegation.
