Apple Releases iOS 13.3 Beta 3 [Download]

20 Nov 2019, 18:13 by iClarified

Apple has released iOS 13.3 beta 3 and iPadOS 13.3 beta 3 to developers. The build number is 17C5046a.

iOS 13.3 introduced Communication Limits for Screen Time letting you limit who can be contacted during Screen Time and Downtime. This applies to Phone, FaceTime, Messages, and iCloud contacts.

Additionally, Safari now supports NFC, USB, and Lightning FIDO2-compliant security keys.
