Cody Simpson Says Finding Love With Miley Cyrus Helped Him Get Out Of His ‘Rebelling’ Stage

20 Nov 2019, 18:13 by Aynslee Darmon

Cody Simpson is opening up about his relationship with Miley Cyrus.

The singer, 22, recently chatted with WHO magazine, confessing his relationship with Cyrus pulled him out a dark place.

“I was kind of an idiot,” he admits. “I was rebelling. I just burnt through everything possible, really quickly. And I’m really glad about that, actually, because I kind of got through it.”

Thankfully, the “Slide Away” singer, 26, came into his life at the perfect time.

“[I had to] party a bunch and get all that out of me and just the minute that I felt like I was done with that, we found each other again,” he says.

And now, Cyrus and Simpson both focus on their health.

“I try to eat well five days a week and then the other two I just see whatever,” he explains.

Adding, “And that seems to be the most long-term sustainable thing. If I can go Monday, Friday and like eat really well yeah. And then Saturdays and Sundays do what, literally eat anything, whatever I want.”

Simpson is mid-way through writing and recording a fourth studio album, his first in five years, which could feature Cyrus.

“I don’t want to say too much about the new album because it’s a surprise,” he reveals.

Following their month-and-a-half long whirlwind romance, rumours of Cyrus and Simpson’s split has made headlines this week, but according to E! News, everything is fine.

“Miley and Cody saw each other last week when he visited her in Tennessee. They are fine and are still dating,” a source tells the outlet. “Miley has been resting from her vocal surgery but has been in touch and seen Cody recently.”
