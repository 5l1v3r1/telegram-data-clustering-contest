Man Utd Risk Losing Seven Stars For Free

20 Nov 2019, 18:30 by SL International

As the Premier League season nears its half-way point, record 20-time champions Manchester United face the increased risk of losing no fewer than seven stars for free.

Click through the mini-gallery below for images of the seven players whose contracts are expiring.

The transfer window re-opens in January and will come with the expected flurry of activity, but it also opens the opportunity for clubs to enter into pre-contract agreements with players entering the final six months of their existing deals.

For the Red Devils, according to the Manchester Everning News, that means that seven of their players could start talking to other clubs in a bid to move on as free agents at the end of the current campaign.

Chief among those names is club captain Ashley Young who has been a regular in Ole Gunnar Solskjaer’s side this season. The 34-year-old signed a one-year extension last year but is yet to put pen to paper on a new deal this time around.

The former England international is joined by fellow veterans Nemanja Matic and Lee Grant, the latter of whom has served as United’s third-choice goalkeeper behind David De Gea and Sergio Romero.
