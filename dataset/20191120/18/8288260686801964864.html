Newcastle United have said this ahead of Aston Villa clash - and Steve Bruce will love it

Latest Aston Villa news | Steve Bruce returns to Villa Park as manager of Newcastle on Monday

20 Nov 2019, 18:11 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Newcastle United have hinted that they could be boosted by the return of two players against Aston Villa.

The Magpies visit Villa Park on Monday evening for what will be Steve Bruce’s first return to his former club since his sacking last October.

Bruce, 58, was relieved of his duties as Villa boss 13 months ago after a poor run of form saw them plummet down the Championship table.

A 3-3 draw with Preston North End, who were then bottom of the league, proved to be the breaking point for Villa after an evening which also saw a fan hurl a cabbage in Bruce’s direction.

Bruce will be hoping for a happier night on his upcoming visit and he is set to have two defenders back at his disposal.

Posting on social media on Wednesday, Newcastle indicated that Florian Lejeune and Fabian Schar are set to return to action.

 

Lejeuene is a long-term absentee having been sidelined for most of the year with a knee injury, while Schar missed the last Premier League match as well as Switzerland’s most recent Euro 2020 qualifiers also with a knee problem.

But the north-east club shared images of the duo in the gym with emojis which said “back soon”.

Their potential return is a timely boost for Newcastle who saw captain Jamaal Lascelles suffer a serious injury against Bournemouth prior to the international break.

The centre-back is likely to be out for the rest of 2019 having been hurt during a tussle with Callum Wilson during the 2-1 win.
