'He's had a Rolls Royce upbringing in football': Dundalk sign England underage international

20 Nov 2019, 18:44 by By Sports Desk Staff

SSE Airtricity League Premier Division champions Dundalk have signed England underage international midfielder Will Patching.

The Manchester native was a member of the Manchester City Academy for 14 years, from 2004 to 2018. The 21-year-old was at Notts County last season where he made 10 appearances before departing Meadow Lane in the summer.

The creative midfielder, who has penned a two-year-deal at Oriel Park, becomes Dundalk's second off-season signing after Greg Sloggett.
