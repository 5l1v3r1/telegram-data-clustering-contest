Gears 5 is Getting a Double Horde Character XP Event this Weekend

By Sean Carey, 20 Nov 2019
Comments
This weekend Gears 5 is getting a Double Horde Character XP bonus weekend, and players can also get free Boost to go along with it. This weekend should help you on your way with levelling up your Horde characters and make unlocking a few of the more grind-heavy achievements in Gears 5, slightly more bearable.

Starting this Friday from 2:00 AM PT/5:00 AM ET/10:00 AM GMT until 10:00 AM PT/1:00 PM ET/6:00 PM GMT on Monday 25, Boost will get you 4XP which is double the usual Boost rate for levelling up your Horde characters. With previous events, players needed to have Boost to take advantage of Double XP events; thankfully The Coalition is giving away three days of free Boost this weekend. All you have to do is log in each day from Friday to Sunday to claim each day's free boost. The times you need to log in by are listed below:

  • Friday Free Boost: 2:00 AM PT/ 5:00 AM ET/ 10:00 AM GMT – 1:59 AM PT/ 4:59 AM ET/ 9:59 AM GMT(Saturday)
  • Saturday Free Boost: 2:00 AM PT/ 5:00 AM ET/ 10:00 AM GMT – 1:59 AM PT/ 4:59 AM ET/ 9:59 AM GMT (Sunday)
  • Sunday Free Boost: 2:00 AM PT/ 5:00 AM ET/ 10:00 AM GMT– 1:59 AM PT/ 4:59 AM PT/ 9:59 AM GMT (Monday)

This Boost Bonus Weekend could help out with a few achievements. The Who's Your Horde Main achievement which requires you to reach character level 15 with a launch Horde character and the Compulsive Horder achievement which requires you to reach level 15 with Kait, JD, Del, Marcus, Fahz and Jack. The Compulsive Horder achievement ties in with Seriously 5.0 - Chapter 1. This weekend's event could make a sizeable dent in these achievements if you're willing to grind them out for the full three days.

The Who's Your Horde Main? achievement in Gears 5 worth 66 pointsReach Character Level 15 with a launch Horde Character

The Compulsive Horder achievement in Gears 5 worth 352 pointsReach Character Level 15 with Kait, JD, Del, Marcus, Fahz and Jack

The Seriously 5.0 - Chapter 1 achievement in Gears 5 worth 8829 pointsInsane Campaign, Master Horde & Escape Launch Maps & Characters, 20 Re-ups, General in Tour
