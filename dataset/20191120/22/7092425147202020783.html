Tupac 'alive' conspiracy rages with new video 'showing rapper on phone in 2019'

20 Nov 2019, 22:56 by Buzz Contributor

The footage captured a Tupac Shakur look-a-like shooting a smile, while seemingly smoking and drinking a canned drink

Tupac Shakur has been filmed talking on his phone while wandering outside, conspiracists claim.

The US rapper was murdered in a drive-by shooting in Las Vegas, Nevada, US, on September 13, 1996.

But conspiracy theories have circulated ever since wildly claiming that the California Love star survived the shooting and went into hiding.

Various supposed sightings of the legendary rapper, as well as mysterious prison records, have all been used as "evidence" a cover-up was in place.

And in a new explosive clip, a man who bears a striking resemblance to Tupac has been filmed walking around outside.

The footage, captured by a cameraman who attempts to remain hidden, recorded the man talking on his phone.

He is seen sporting similar facial hair and a bald head.

The footage captured the lookalike shooting a smile, while seemingly smoking and drinking a canned drink.

After being uploaded to YouTube, captioned: "A video Of Tupac alive in 2019, age 48."

Hundreds have since flooded in to watch the bizarre clip.

One person said: "Pac has risen. Hallelujah."

Another added: "Probably the best lookalike out there same head shape teeth even scar on top right from holes in the quad studio."
