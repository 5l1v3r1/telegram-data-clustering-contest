Tua Tagovailoa Has A Message For Alabama Fans Before Saturday’s Game

20 Nov 2019, 22:19 by Andrew McCarty

Over the weekend, Tua Tagovailoa suffered a gruesome season-ending injury. The Alabama star quarterback dislocated his hip and suffered a fracture during the team’s game against Mississippi State.

Initial reports suggested Tua’s playing career could be in jeopardy. However, after he underwent surgery, doctors announced he is expected to make a full recovery.

He won’t play for the rest of the season and likely won’t play for Alabama ever again. However, he still has love for the university and its fans.

Tagovailoa had a message for fans before Saturday afternoon’s game against Western Carolina.

“Hoping to see everyone this Saturday to support the team for senior day at BDS! See you Saturday Alabama football. ROLL TIDE,” Tua said.
