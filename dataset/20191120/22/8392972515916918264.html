I'm A Celebrity viewers elated as Cliff Parisi and Andy Whyment announced as new campmates

They will join camp tomorrow

20 Nov 2019, 22:15 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity are over the moon as TWO new campmates have been announced.

Former EastEnders and Call The Midwife star Cliff Parisi and Coronation Street actor Andrew Whyment will be entering the Aussie camp tomorrow night (November 21).

At the end of Wednesday's show, hosts Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly told the campmates they will be joined by two new faces.

"Come on you spiders! Come on your crocodiles!"

I don't think I do have any fears or phobias but watch me now when I get in there.
