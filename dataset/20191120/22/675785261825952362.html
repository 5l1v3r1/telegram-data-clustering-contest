Simon Cowell skinnier than ever on date night with Lauren Silverman after losing 20lbs

The X Factor: Celebrity judge stepped out with his girlfriend Lauren Silverman looking slimmer than ever on their date night

20 Nov 2019, 22:03 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Simon Cowell looked slimmer than ever as he stepped out for a date night with his stunning girlfriend Lauren Silverman.

The X Factor : Celebrity judge has recently dropped 20lbs thanks to a strict vegan diet.

He shocked fans when he returned to screens looking much skinnier than the last time he'd been on TV.

And it seems his weight loss is continuing.

Simon was looking very trim when he stepped out tonight with Lauren.

The couple, who share son Eric, five, headed to London's Shaftesbury Theatre to watch musical & Juliet.

Simon, 60, looked smart as a dart in a fitted grey suit with a navy blue jumper underneath.

Lauren, 42, braved the cold as she bared her enviable pins in a stunning black velvet off-the-shoulder dress.

Simon recently revealed he'd achieved his slender physique with a vegan diet.

The music mogul spoke candidly about making changes to his lifestyle after he was hospitalised in October 2017 after he fainted due to low blood pressure .

Speaking to Extra TV in June, he said: "I went to see this guy who's very well-known. And he said to me, after doing some blood work, 'You can't have red meat, dairy, sugar, bread or gluten'."

Simon said the first thing he asked about was whether or not he could drink alcohol.

He said: "And he said, 'Maybe not quite as much as you had before'. And I did it and I stuck to it and it's easy."

The star joked he was on the same diet as his little boy, saying: "Apart from ice-cream, he actually likes water, eats all his raw vegetable. I'm on the Eric Cowell diet."

Simon recently confirmed that the celebrity version of his ITV talent contest will be returning for a second series, but that he may not stick with the broadcaster.

He told new magazine: "As an idea we would be crazy not to do a second series.

"Funnily enough, I've had an offer from another network to take it. So, to answer your question, it will be on next year."
