Exclusive: Goldy lockS Band fights child abuse with 'Black' video 

The Goldy lockS Band, helmed by rocker, former pro wrestler and now singer Goldy Locks, premieres its new music video for her song "Black" exclusively on Digital Journal.

20 Nov 2019, 22:32 by Markos Papadatos

"Black" is by Smith and Hay, and it features the Goldy lockS Band. Most impressive about this Pearl Jam cover tune is that it is meant to bring awareness to the epidemic of child abuse.
