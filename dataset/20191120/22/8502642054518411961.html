Trump says he doesn't think China is 'stepping up' in trade talks

20 Nov 2019, 22:26

AUSTIN, Texas, Nov 20 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump said on Wednesday he has not made a trade deal with China yet because Beijing is not "stepping up" in negotiations.

"I don't think they're stepping up to the level that I want," Trump told reporters after touring a plant in Austin that assembles Apple Inc computers. (Reporting by Steve Holland; Writing by Eric Beech; Editing by Sandra Maler)
