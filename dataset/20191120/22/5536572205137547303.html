Bettor Banks $17K After Putting Down Just $100 On Crazy 8-Team Parlay (PICS)

20 Nov 2019, 22:56 by Darrelle Lincoln

While it feels good to hear about people winning insane amounts of money betting as little as possible, it sucks knowing that it hasn’t happened to you yet.

One Bettor recently struck it big when he walked into ARIA Race & Sports Book in Las Vegas and put down a $100 bet on 8 teams during college football Saturday.

He picked all the favorites and everybody who was favored came out on top.

That bettor walked away with $17,220.00 when it was all said and done.
