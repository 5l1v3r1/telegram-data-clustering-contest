Ozzy Osbourne Shares Teaser Of New Song, Straight To Hell

Listen to the first 13 seconds of the second single from Ozzy’s upcoming solo album, Ordinary Man.

20 Nov 2019, 22:07

If Ozzy Osbourne’s plan was to keep us hanging on his every move, it’s definitely working. Only late yesterday, the metal godfather tweeted a cryptic postcard inviting his listeners to go “Straight To Hell.” Now, Ozzy has tweeted a teaser of the track featuring 13 seconds of new music.

As you can hear in the tweet, Straight To Hell takes an eerie, almost clerical tone, similar to first single Under The Graveyard. This time around, the Ozzman seems less focused on coming off tough or rebellious and more ready to creep out his listeners. It’s an appropriate elder statesman role — the king of shadows, rather than, say, the organizer of Ozzfest.
