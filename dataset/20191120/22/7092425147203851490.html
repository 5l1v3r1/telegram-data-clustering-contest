Nadine Coyle brands rumours of feud with Cheryl Cole as "fake news"

20 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Nick Fitzgerald

The former Girls Aloud singer had an open conversation with fellow I'm A Celeb campmates Jacqueline Jossa and Adele Roberts

It's something ITV bosses certainly hoped for when they picked the former Girls Aloud singer to partake in I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here.

Nadine Coyle has finally spilled on her relationship with Cheryl Cole. In tonight's show of I'm A Celeb, Nadine sat by the Jungle creek with fellow campmates Jacqueline Jossa and Adele Roberts.

Talk quickly turned to Girls Aloud and it was Jacqueline who asked Coyle what it was like being in a girl band.

Nadine responded by saying:

“Really good, really good, I like being in a girl band.  I am one of three sisters and went to an all-girls grammar school so I’m used to being around girls."

Later in the conversation Jaqueline asked the Northern Irish beauty if she got along with everyone in Girls Aloud to which Nadine said "Yes".

Jaqueline then went on to quiz Nadine on her relationship with Cheryl by offering "I’d heard you didn’t get on with Cheryl."

The Derry native shot down the rumours, branding them as "fake news" and said "It wasn't a thing."

Girls Aloud, who rose to fame in 2002, split for good in 2013. It was believed that Nadine and Sarah Harding had a falling out with former bandmates Cheryl, Kimberley Walsh and Nicola Roberts.

It was only recently Kimberley admitted they had "drifted apart" over the years. However, she did say she will be keeping up with I'm A Celeb and wants Nadine to "do well".

Let sleeping dogs lie.
