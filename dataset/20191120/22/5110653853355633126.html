Kawhi Leonard injury update: Clippers star will return vs. Celtics, team up with Paul George for first time

20 Nov 2019, 22:55 by Jordan Greer

For the first time this season, the Clippers will be able to play their All-Star forwards together.

Kawhi Leonard is expectedto take the floor against the Celtics on Wednesday night, according to ESPN's Adrian Wojnarowski and Ohm Youngmisuk, after missing the Clippers' previous three games with a left knee contusion. Barring any last-minute setbacks, Los Angeles coach Doc Rivers will finally be able to pair Leonard with Paul George in the starting lineup.

"It'll be great," Clippers center Ivica Zubac said of both Leonard and George being available (via The Athletic's Jovan Buha)."We're really good with only Kawhi. We're really good with only PG. You put them two together, it's gotta be good. We'll see it tonight. But I'm really excited."
