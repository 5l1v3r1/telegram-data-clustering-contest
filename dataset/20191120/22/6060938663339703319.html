Not Mouni Roy and Ankita Lokhande but Krystle D'souza to replace Kriti Kharbanda in 'Chehre'; read details

20 Nov 2019, 22:21 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi's 'Chehre' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. Recently, the makers announced that Kriti Kharbanda who was also a part of the film is no longer working on the project. Taking to their social media, they stated that Kriti and makers have parted their ways mutually.

Soon, reports surfaced that Mouni Roy and Ankita Lokhande are in the race to bag the role. However, according to the latest report, Krystle D'Souza has managed to bag the role and she will be replacing Kriti in the film. Reportedly, the acterss auditioned for the role and she was brought on board for the film.

It is also said that Kriti and the makers had issues due to which they parted their ways.

Meanwhile, the film is helmed by Rumi Jaffery and has been slated to release in April 2020.
