Oman weather: Schools closed due to heavy rain

20 Nov 2019, 22:15 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Ministry of Education has suspended classes for all government and private schools on Thursday due to heavy rainfall in many governorates

“The Ministry of Education, in coordination with the Early Warning Centre has decided to halt teaching at local government and private schools on Thursday 21st of November,” a statement from Ministry said.

Schools in governorates of Muscat, North AlBatina, South AlBatina, Al Dakhiliya, and North Al Sharqiya will closed due to heavy rainfall and overflowing wadis.
