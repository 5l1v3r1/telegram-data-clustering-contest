Everything coming to — and leaving — Netflix in December

20 Nov 2019, 22:06 by Libby Torres
  • Netflix is adding lots of new titles in December, including buzzy films like “Marriage Story,” starring Adam Driver and Scarlett Johansson, and “The Two Popes.”
  • New seasons of shows like “Terrace House: Tokyo” and “Full House” spin-off “Fuller House” will also be added to the streaming service, as well as numerous Netflix original holiday movies.
  • But some fan favorites will be removed from the site in December as well, such as “Pulp Fiction” and seasons seven through 11 of Anthony Bourdain’s travel show “Parts Unknown.”
  • Visit Insider’s homepage for more stories.

Netflix just revealed the movies and TV shows that will be coming to – and leaving – the site in December.

New seasons of “You” and “Terrace House: Tokyo” will be added to the streaming service at the start of the month, as well as eagerly anticipated films like “Marriage Story” and “The Two Popes.”

But some titles will be leaving the streaming service this month too. Classics like “Jackie Brown,” all of the “Rocky” movies, and “Schindler’s List” will be removed from Netflix at the end of the month.

Here’s everything that’s coming and leaving Netflix in December.
