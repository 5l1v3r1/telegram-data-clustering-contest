Resident Advisor to host live Exchange with Kelly Moran at MUTEK MX

The interview will go down at Casa MUTEK on Thursday evening.

20 Nov 2019, 22:38 by Matt McDermott

Kelly Moran will give a live RA Exchange interview at MUTEK MX tomorrow.

The Warp Records artist, whose experiments for prepared piano and electronics have earned her a devoted following, will speak with RA staff writer Matt McDermott at Casa MUTEK, the site of the festival's Digi Lab programming. The talk is free and open to the public and will be held between 6 and 7 PM at Av. México 200, Hipódromo Condesa. Moran will speak with McDermott ahead of her performance at A/Visions 2 on Sunday, November 24th.

Resident Advisor will host a stage at MUTEK MX featuring DJ Lag, Deadbeat and more. Check out the full lineup for MUTEK MX 2019, featuring Tim Hecker, John Talabot, Caterina Barbieri and many others, in the event listing below.
