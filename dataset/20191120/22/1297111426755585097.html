Man dead after highway collision with refrigerated truck

The two men in the refrigerated truck were not injured but the sole occupant of the pickup, the 46-year-old driver, was transported by STARS Air Ambulance to hospital in Regina.

20 Nov 2019, 22:38 by Regina Leader-Post

A 46-year-old man is dead after a highway collision near Yellow Grass.

Members of the Milestone RCMP, Yellow Grass Fire Department and Weyburn EMS responded to a scene on Highway 39, two kilometres northwest of Yellowgrass at 8:30 a.m. Nov. 18.

According to police, a pickup truck heading northwest collided head on with an international refrigerated truck going southeast. The two men in the refrigerated truck were not injured but the sole occupant of the pickup, the 46-year-old driver, was transported by STARS Air Ambulance to hospital in Regina. He succumbed to his injuries a few hours later.

Alcohol is not being considered as a factor in the collision.

The cause of the collision is still under investigation.

Yellow Grass is about 90 kms southeast of Regina.
