OU vs. Maryland Eastern Shore men's basketball: How to watch, things to know, projected starters

20 Nov 2019, 22:04 by Abby Bitterman

Maryland Eastern Shore at OU

When: 7 p.m., Thursday

Where: Lloyd Noble Center, Norman

TV: FSOK (Cox 37)

Radio: KRXO-FM 107.7

Three things to know

• Senior Kristian Doolittle and redshirt junior Austin Reaves are leading the Sooners’ offense through the first four games. Reaves leads the team with an average of 17.3 points per game, and Doolittle has scored a team-high 19 points in each of the last two games.

• This will be the fourth ever meeting between the Sooners and Hawks. Oklahoma is 3-0 all-time against Maryland Eastern Shore, and the Sooners have won every game in the series by 24 points or more, including the last meeting between the two in 2011.

• This game is the second of two games Oklahoma will play in the Hall of Fame Classic campus rounds in Norman. Next week, the Sooners will play two more Hall of Fame Classic games, including one game against Stanford, which beat Maryland Eastern Shore 76-55 earlier this week. Those games will be played in Kansas City, Missouri.
