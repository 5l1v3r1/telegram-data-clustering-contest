Report: Decision On Myles Garrett’s Appeal Could Come Today

20 Nov 2019, 22:07 by Chris Rosvoglou

Myles Garrett was in New York earlier today to appeal his indefinite suspension from the NFL. His punishment from the league quickly came after he struck Mason Rudolph over the head with his own helmet.

ESPN’s Dianna Russini reported today that Garrett would learn his fate very soon. It turns out that he actually might find out in the coming hours.

Mary Kay Cabot of cleveland.com is reporting that a decision on Garrett’s appeal could come as early as today.

Garrett’s appeal process is being handled by James Thrash, who was a former wide receiver for the Philadelphia Eagles and Washington Redskins.
