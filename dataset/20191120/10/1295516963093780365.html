Steven Universe Future Debuts First Trailer, Poster and Premiere Date

20 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Tim Adams

Steven and the Crystal Gems are back in the first official trailer and poster for Steven Universe Future, the retitled sixth season of the Cartoon Network animated series.

The trailer kicks off with an ensemble rendition of "Here We Are in the Future," before displaying shots of the fan-favorite cast of characters (and villains). Along with the trailer comes a new poster that reveals the epilogue limited series will debut December 7 on Cartoon Network.
