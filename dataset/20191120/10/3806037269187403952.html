Garbstore Remixes Crescent Down Works Outerwear for FW19

Releasing in black and olive.

20 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Jack Stanley

Fresh from delivering the latest collection from its The English Difference label, London-based retailer Garbstore has collaborated with Crescent Down Works on a new capsule. The Made in the USA jackets are constructed from two contrasting fabrics, with a nylon shell on the top and a 60/40 mix of cotton and nylon used on the bottom.

Additionally, the jackets — releasing in either black or olive — feature 14 ounces of goose down, a removable hood and utility-inspired cargo pockets. The winter-ready jackets are then finished with the Crescent Down Works logo, which appears on the front left pocket of each piece.

Take a look at the two Garbstore x Crescent Down Works collection in the gallery above, with both jackets available now from the Garbstore web store.
