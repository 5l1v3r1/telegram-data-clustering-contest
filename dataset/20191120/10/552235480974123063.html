Rangers star Glen Kamara rubs shoulders with world pass masters in key Euro 2020 stat

The Finland midfielder was instrumental as he helped his side qualify for their first ever major tournament.

20 Nov 2019, 10:03 by Dailyrecord.co.uk

Rangers midfielder Glen Kamara was named in Euro 2020 qualifiers' top five pass masters after he helped Finland to a historic qualification.

The Ibrox star has been touted for moves down south after becoming a key man for Steven Gerrard's side this season.

And the former Dundee playmaker helped his country qualify for their first ever major tournament last week to spark off huge celebrations.

And after the final qualifiers were completed this week, Kamara's worth to his country was illustrated by official UEFA figures on the stats for the matches.

Kamara made 720 completed passes in Finland's ten games - that made him the fifth best passer of the ball in Europe.

He was behind Bayern Munich and Germany star Joshua Kimmich who was the best with 897 passes, followed by Belgium's Toby Alderweireld, Italy's Jorginho and Spanish hardman Sergio Ramos.

Kamara and Rangers will return to domestic action this weekend as they face Hamilton Accies in the Premiership.
