Israel Launches Retaliatory Strikes Against Syria, Defense Minister Warns: “The Rules Have Changed”

20 Nov 2019, 10:48 by David Sidman
Behold, Damascus shall cease to be a city; It shall become a heap of ruins.Isaiah 17:1 (The Israel Bible™)

After rockets fell in Israel’s northern Golan region on Tuesday, Israel retaliated by hitting Iranian and Syrian installations inside Syria early Wednesday morning. The IDF reportedly published a map of the attacks.
