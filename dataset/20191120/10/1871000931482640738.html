While you might have had all the butter chickens and kadhai chickens of the world, but here we have a delightful chicken dish that is not just delicious but super easy to prepare at home. Nimbu Hari Mirch ka Murgh is a quick and easy chicken dish that has aromatic flavours of a myriad of spices and chillies. It has fried chicken pieces sautéed with ginger-garlic paste, onion, curd, lemon pickle and a host of spices. This chicken curry is quite different from the other curries since it has an addition of lemon juice that brings tanginess to the chicken while the curd and cream gives a creamy and rich look to the dish. It is served with a generous garnishing of coriander leaves and green chillies.
