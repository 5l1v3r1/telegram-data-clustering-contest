Nysa Devgn looks like a spitting image of mom Kajol in this viral photo

20 Nov 2019, 10:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kajol and Ajay Devgn’s little girl Nysa Devgn is all grown up and is now looking like a spitting image of her gorgeous mom. All thanks to her large fan-following on social media, a photo showing the similarities between the mother-daughter duo, has been doing the rounds online.

The click not only sees the lovely ladies flashing their best smiles, but also sees them donning matching red caps.

It was only early Wednesday morning that we saw the mother-daughter-duo at the Mumbai airport. While Nysa kept her look cool, comfortable and trendy, Kajol was spotted in a simple salwar khameez.

On the work front, the actress is awaiting the release of her next ‘Tanhaji: the Unsung Warrior’, starring hubby Ajay in the lead along with Saif Ali Khan as the antagonist. The film opens in theatres on January 10, 2020.
