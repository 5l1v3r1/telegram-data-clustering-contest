Caitlyn Jenner's mum hits out at Kardashians for making daughter look spineless

20 Nov 2019, 10:27

Caitlyn Jenner’s mum has lashed out at the Kardashians and Kris Jenner, claiming her daughter was made to look spineless by her family.

The 70-year-old is currently winning new fans on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here!, making friends with campmates including Adele Roberts, Ian Wright and James Haskell.

But when it comes to the stars of Caitlyn’s other big reality show, her mother Esther isn’t so keen.

Esther, 93, told the Mail Online: ‘[Kris] made [Caitlyn] look like a milquetoast man!’ – milquetoast meaning spineless and unassertive.

She continued: ‘I wasn’t fond of that programme [Keeping Up With The Kardashians], there was no plot, and why it was such a hit I’ll never know, and I guess it still is.’

Caitlyn – who had daughters Kylie and Kendall with wife of 24 years Kris – starred on the reality show from 2007, and while she doesn’t star all the time alongside her ex-wife, daughters and stepchildren Kim, Kourtney, Khloe and Rob, the former Olympian continues to have cameos.

The star went on to have her own E! series, I Am Cait, which Esther appeared on – but when it comes to I’m A Celeb, Caitlyn’s mum won’t be tuning in.

Esther said: ”That’s not entertainment to me, to see somebody frightened. I think she could spend a lot more pleasant times on the golf course!

‘I have no idea [why she’s doing this]. I don’t ask her questions to get into her mind or how she feels about this or that. My big worry is [she may] get bitten by a snake or a poisonous insect.’

On last night’s edition of I’m A Celeb, Caitlyn began to spill the tea on her famous family in a chat with Adele Roberts.

Talking about how Kim got the business bug, Caitlyn recalled: ‘Kimberly never went to college. The minute she left high school she started businesses. She had her first business out of the closet.

‘Kimberly would go into the ladies’ closets. Some of these are like 2,000 square feet – they’re huge. Beverly Hills ladies.

‘They figure out what they want to get rid of and Kimberly has an eBay site… Kimberly sells them for them and gets 30% commission.’

And speaking about son-in-law Kanye West, the athlete added: ‘I like Kanye. Kanye was very, very good to me.’
