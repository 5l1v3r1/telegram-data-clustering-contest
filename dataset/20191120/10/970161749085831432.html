Tottenham star 'likes' Jose Mourinho dig after Mauricio Pochettino sacking

20 Nov 2019, 10:54

Tottenham striker Troy Parrott has not got off to the best start with Jose Mourinho after ‘liking’ a post on Twitter which criticised the new Spurs manager.

Mourinho has now officially replaced Mauricio Pochettino as manager after the Argentine was surprisingly sacked by Spurs on Tuesday evening less than six months after guiding the team to a Champions League final.

Mourinho had been out of work since he was axed by Manchester United nearly a year ago and left Old Trafford under a cloud after he was involved in bust-ups with several players including Anthony Martial and Paul Pogba.

The Portuguese was also heavily criticised for his playing style towards the end of his reign at Old Trafford, and having spent nearly £400 million on signings he left United 19 points behind Liverpool, who were the Premier League leaders at the time of his departure.

And judging by his social media activity, Parrott might be sceptical over Mourinho’s arrival as he ‘liked’ a post from a supporter who urged him to join Manchester United because Spurs are ‘f*****’ under the Portuguese.

Parrott subsequently removed the Twitter post from his likes but not before Spurs fans had spotted it.

The 17-year-old striker was given his Tottenham debut by Pochettino earlier this season in their Carabao Cup defeat to Colchester United in September.

Parrott has made only one Premier League squad this season – the 1-1 draw against Everton earlier this month – but he was left on the bench by Pochettino.

Mourinho, meanwhile, will take charge of Spurs for the first time on Saturday as his side travel to West Ham.
