Accor appoints new General Manager at Novotel Athenes

20 Nov 2019, 10:25 by Vicky Karantzavelou

After 14 consecutive years at Novotel Athenes, Mr Evripides Tzikas a long experienced Manager in the Greek hospitality industry, has assigned duties to Mr Kritikopoulos with effect as from 10th October 2019.

Novotel Athenes, a 196-room property located in the city center of Athens and member of Accor Group, has appointed Mr Georgios Kritikopoulos as its General Manager.

After 14 consecutive years at Novotel Athenes, Mr Evripides Tzikas a long experienced Manager in the Greek hospitality industry, has assigned duties to Mr Kritikopoulos with effect as from 10th October 2019.

Mr Kritikopoulos after a Master Certificate in Hospitality Management started his career in Celebrity Cruises in the USA where he developed his career for 9 years before he arrived in Canada. The succeeding 15 years, he joined Best Western Dorval as Front Office Manager then in Fairmont Le Chateau Montebello as Room Divison Manager and in Fairmont Hotels and Resort as Operation Manager until the role of General Manager in Marpunta Resort by Santikos Collection since March 2019.

Mr Daniele Morniroli, Accor Invest VP Asset Management for Italy & Greece, stated: “I would like to welcome Georgios and wishing him great success in his new role. I want to thank Evripides Tzikas for the precious work done for Novotel Athenes and wish him great fulfillment in his new professional adventure.”
