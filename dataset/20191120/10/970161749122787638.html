Katie Price 'threatens to fly out to Oz over Emily Atack's Extra Camp dig

20 Nov 2019, 10:21

Katie Price has clapped back at Emily Atack’s dig and has threated to fly out to the I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! jungle to confront her.

During Tuesday’s Extra Camp, the former Inbetweeners star threw shade at Katie and Peter Andre by joking that presenters Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas needed to get tested for diseases after plunging into the same pool that the former couple first kissed in.

‘This is where Katie Price and Peter Andre had fondles,’ Joel proudly boasted to his co-host. But back in the studio, Emily teased: ‘I hope you boys went to the clinic after being in that pool.’

Unsurprisingly, Katie wasn’t best pleased by Emily’s comments and, according to reports, made it clear that the actress would have a ‘problem’ if she wanted one.

An insider said: ‘Katie says she’s not watching the show, but when Emily Atack mentioned her on Extra Camp all her friends told her what she said.

‘She was telling people that she can’t believe Emily would say something so snide and if she “wants a problem, I’ll give her a problem”.’

They added to The Sun: ‘At one point Katie was saying she’d fly out to Australia and confront her. She’s not going to, obviously, but she was asking around to see if anyone had Emily’s number.

Katie and Peter starred in the third series of I’m A Celebrity in 2004 and it was where their relationship first began.

The pair married the following year but divorced back in 2009. They share two children together, Princess, 12, and Junior, 14.

Back in July, Katie unearthed her rotting backpack from the ITV series containing a crumpled uniform and a water bottle – and she drank the 15-year-old liquid.
