Pakistan, Australia Test series trophy unveiled in Brisbane

20 Nov 2019, 10:34 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

BRISBANE (Dunya News) – Pakistan and Australia Test series trophy has been unveiled at the Brisbane Cricket Ground – commonly known as the Gabba – ahead of their first clash at the aforementioned venue on Thursday (tomorrow).

Captains Azhar Ali and Tim Paine

The hosts conducted practice session as chief selector and head coach Misbah-ul-Haq guided the players. Bowling coach Waqar Younis gave tips to the bowlers. Abid Ali took wicket-keeping practice as Mohammad Rizwan got injured during practice match against Australia A.

Pakistan Group Photo

It is pertinent here to mention that Pakistan have not won a Test match in Australia since 1995. Furthermore, the Kangaroos have not lost any game at the Gabba – where the first contest is scheduled – since 1988.

Let it be known that the second and final match of the series will be hosted by Adelaide Oval from November 29 to December 3. Earlier, both three-day tour matches between the Pakistanis and Australia A were drawn.
