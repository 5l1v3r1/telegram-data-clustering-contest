Epworth Villa seniors help others prepare for winter chill

20 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Staff reports

With winter weather approaching, the residents of Epworth Villa held their annual fall clothing drive.

The biannual event allows residents to clean out their closets and give their clothing items to Skyline Urban Ministries.

“I had a closet of good coats, and I only need one," said Jan Neel, Epworth resident. "I can help keep others warm this winter with the coats that I am not wearing.”

Each year in the fall and spring there is a clothing drive at Epworth Villa. Skyline workers said they love receiving donations from Epworth Villa because of the quality and care that goes into providing items. One Skyline volunteer said, “We also like the clothes Epworth Villa donates for our 'Dress for Success' patrons.”

This fall, Epworth residents gathered 845 pounds of warm clothing and coats for Skyline.

Epworth Villa is a nonprofit, Life Plan Retirement Community in north Oklahoma City.
