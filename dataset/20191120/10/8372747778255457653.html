Wade Wilson being held on no bond for Cape Coral murders

20 Nov 2019, 10:25 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- The man accused of murdering two Cape Coral women last month made his first appearance on murder charges Wednesday in Lee County.

The state requested that Wade Wilson be held on no bond, and the judge agreed.

His next court date is scheduled for November 25th.

The judge also said that if he does somehow get out, he should have no contact with ex-girlfriend, MeLisa Montanez.

On Tuesday, a grand jury indicted Wade Wilson for the murders of Diane Ruiz and Kristine Melton.
