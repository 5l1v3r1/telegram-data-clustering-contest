RADA Electronic Ind. (NASDAQ:RADA) Releases Quarterly Earnings Results, Meets Expectations

20 Nov 2019, 10:16 by Doug Wharley

RADA Electronic Ind. (NASDAQ:RADA) issued its earnings results on Tuesday. The aerospace company reported ($0.02) EPS for the quarter, hitting the Thomson Reuters’ consensus estimate of ($0.02), Fidelity Earnings reports. The business had revenue of $11.26 million during the quarter. RADA Electronic Ind. had a negative return on equity of 2.16% and a negative net margin of 3.39%.

RADA traded up $0.07 during trading on Wednesday, reaching $5.23. 150,900 shares of the stock traded hands, compared to its average volume of 100,052. RADA Electronic Ind. has a fifty-two week low of $2.50 and a fifty-two week high of $5.80. The stock’s 50-day simple moving average is $5.17 and its two-hundred day simple moving average is $4.23.

A number of analysts recently issued reports on RADA shares. ValuEngine cut RADA Electronic Ind. from a “hold” rating to a “sell” rating in a research report on Wednesday, October 2nd. Alliance Global Partners reissued a “buy” rating and set a $7.50 price objective (up previously from $6.50) on shares of RADA Electronic Ind. in a research report on Thursday, September 19th.

About RADA Electronic Ind.

RADA Electronic Industries Ltd. develops, manufactures, markets, and sells defense electronics to various air forces and companies worldwide. The company offers military avionics systems, including flight data recorders for fighter aircraft; digital video/audio/data recorders with data transfer functions; high-rate data recorders for aircraft and airborne pods; video recorders and airborne data servers; high definition digital video/audio/data recording for fighter and trainer aircrafts; a range of head-up-displays color video cameras for fighter aircraft; and various ground debriefing solutions.
