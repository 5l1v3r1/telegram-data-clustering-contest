Russian businessman Potanin says to continue investing in Nornickel

20 Nov 2019, 10:03 by Reuters

MOSCOW — Russian businessman Vladimir Potanin, the biggest shareholder in Norilsk Nickel, said on Wednesday he would continue investing in the Russian mining giant when the opportunity arises.

Potanin made the comment in an interview with RBC TV. He currently owns 34.6% of Nornickel, the world’s largest palladium producer and one of the world’s largest nickel producers.
