Put a respect on my name – Akuapem Poloo tells Archipalago

20 Nov 2019, 10:26 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Video vixen turned actress Rosemond Brown aka Akuapem Poloo has taken a swipe at social media sensation turned musician Dennis Anane known to many as Archipalago to put some respect on her name because she has been verified on social media.

In a video shared on her Instagram page a few hours after her verification, Akuapem Poloo stated that Archiapalago has recorded a song and dissed her saying because of internet Akuapem Poloo is a celebrity.

But Akuapem Poloo who is still celebrating her verification has asked that Archipalago go back and change that line in his upcoming song because her name is now verified.
