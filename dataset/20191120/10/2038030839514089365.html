Local stars are sharing their baby pics on Twitter and it's adorable

20 Nov 2019, 10:13

Nikita Coetzee

Johannesburg – We love ourselves a good throwback pic, and our local stars delivered this week!  

Donovan Goliath, Claire Mawisa and Coconut Kelz were just a few of the famous faces who had us going, "aaaww", when they shared pictures of themselves as kids on Twitter. 

The challenge was simple – post a photo of yourself alongside a picture of yourself as a child. 

From cute hairstyles to questionable fashion choices, we saw it all.
