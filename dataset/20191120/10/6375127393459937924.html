2 Chinese, 3 Pinoys shot in Angeles City

20 Nov 2019, 10:07 by Bernard Galang

ANGELES CITY – Two Chinese nationals and three Filipinos were injured after they were shot at by two armed robbers inside the California Hotel in Barangay Malabanias here recently.

Major Aries Gonzales, Police Station 4 commander, identified the shooting victims as Zhan Cheng, 21; Lin Yun Ting, 45; Anna Grace Faalam Gobrez, 42; and hotel security guards Donald Agustin, 28, and Jimmy Doctolero, 43.

The other day, police filed robbery and frustrated murder cases against one Russel Matthew Cantil and one alias “Tawing” who were identified as the perpetrators based on the footages obtained by investigators from closed circuit television cameras. The two suspects are still at large.

Investigation showed that around 1:40 a.m. last November 13, two helmet-wearing men entered the California Hotel and fired their guns at the security guards and hit the abovementioned victims.

The suspects then forcibly took the cellular phones of the hotel staff and guests at the lobby and immediately went outside to board a motorcycle and flea.

The two Chinese nationals, Gobrez and Agustin were brought to the Angeles University Foundation Medical Center while Doctolero was taken to the Ospital Ning Angeles for treatment of their gunshot wounds.

Personnel of the Scene of Crime Operatives found two deformed fired bullets and four fired cartridges for a cal. .45 gun at the crime scene.

The city police is conducting manhunt operation for the arrest of the suspects.
