Kiara Advani shares adorable throwback pictures to wish cousin Ishita on her birthday

20 Nov 2019, 10:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kiara Advani might be basking in the success of her last release 'Kabir Singh' but the diva is all set to entertain the audience once again in Raj Mehta's 'Good Newwz' this year along with Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan and Diljit Dosanjh.

Meanwhile, the buzz around the trailer of the film still going strong on social media, Kiara took to social media to share a series of adorable throwback pictures to wish her cousin Ishita on her birthday.

While the first one shows us the bond between the two, Kiara captioned it as "To the best of the best. Happy Birthday squishyy." In the other one, she wrote, "Always eating your birthday cake." showing off her witty side. With more than eight million followers on social media, Kiara surely enjoys connecting with her audience.

On the professional front, the 27-year old actress has her kitty full with some really interesting projects like 'Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2', 'Laxmmi Bomb' and 'Shershaah' and 'Good Newwz'.
