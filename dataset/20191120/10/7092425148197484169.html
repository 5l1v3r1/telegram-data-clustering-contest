Ryan Tubridy shares FIRST GLIMPSE of The late Late Toy Show 2019

20 Nov 2019, 10:17 by Ciara Walsh

The excitement is building for the biggest TV event of the year. 

Ryan Tubridy has shared the very first glimpse of The Late Late Toy Show 2019.

With just 10 days to go before the most watched show of the year, the presenter said that things are getting very exciting in RTE ahead of the annual show.

He revealed that the official promo clip will go live across television, online and social media channels this week.

Ryan said: "It's getting very exciting now. This just got real, toyswise of course because look this is a jumper I have been asked to wear, and I wasn't even asked to wear this, I volunteered to wear it for the Toy Show promo which you're going to be seeing in the next day or two on TV and online and on the air."

He then joked: "And in the sky written in the clouds and peppered with Smarties and Skittles and glitter, environmentally friendly glitter."

Tubridy went on to tease the possible theme of The Late Late Toy Show and he dropped a massive hint as to what it could be by saying that there are two sides to every story.

In the video, he added: "The beautiful thing about this jumper is, that like every story, there's two sides, so all I'll have to say to you is watch the promo, start getting Christmassey and we'll see you on the other side."

The Late Late Toy Show will air on RTE One on Friday November, 29 at 9.35pm.
