St. Louis Blues welcome back Patrick Maroon, present forward with Stanley Cup ring in emotional pregame tribute

20 Nov 2019, 01:44 by Sammi Silber

Patrick Maroon may no longer be with the Blues, but he's always from St. Louis.

The 31-year-old forward, who grew up in the town but theleft the Blues to sign with theTampa Bay Lightning over the offseason, was honored before the Lightning's game against St. Louis, receiving not only a tribute on the big screen but something much more significant: his Stanley Cup ring.
