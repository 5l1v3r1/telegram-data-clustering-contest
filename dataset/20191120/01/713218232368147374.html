Latest on road closures due to fires

20 Nov 2019, 01:39 by Janene Carey

Waterfall Way remains closed in both directions between Ebor and Wollomombi due to the fire that police allege was deliberately lit on Thursday.

The advice is to use the Gwydir Highway between Grafton and Glen Innes as an alternative route.

The Guyra Road between Ebor and Aberfoyle is also closed because of the same fire.

Another bushfire has closed Armidale Rd between Clouds Creek and Coutts Crossing but Live Traffic notes that residents may be able to access townships under the discretion of emergency services.

Summerland Way is closed in both directions between Casino and Mountain View near Grafton due to a bushfire..

Motorists are advised to avoid the area. Motorists who need to travel can use the Pacific Highway instead.

Bungawalbin-Whiporie Road remains closed between Whiporie and Bungawalbin.

Other road closures remain in place due to bushfires including:

  • Colo to Milbrodale - Putty Road is closed
  • St Albans to Webbs Creek - St Albans Road is closed
  • Webbs Creek to Leets Vale - Bicentenary Road is closed
  • Walcha to Long Flat - the Oxley Highway remains closed
  • Ebor to Wollomombi - Waterfall Way is closed
  • Ebor to Aberfoyle - Guyra Road is closed
  • Buccarumbi to Newton Boyd - Old Glen Innes Road is closed
  • Clouds Creek to Coutts Crossing - Armidale Road is closed
  • Nowendoc to Rookhurst - Thunderbolts Way is closed
  • Nowendoc to Knorrit Flat - Nowendoc Road is closed
  • Comboyne to Killabakh - Comboyne Road/Wingham Road is closed
  • Comboyne to Upper Lansdowne - Koppin Yarratt Road is closed
  • Telegraph Point - Red Hill Road is closed
  • Lower Creek to Bellbrook - Kempsey Road / Armidale Road is closed
  • Upper Fine Flower to Baryulgil - Clarence Way is closed
  • Grevillia to Rathdowney (across the Queensland border) - Summerland Way and Mount Lindesay Highway are closed

Motorists are reminded that conditions can change quickly. Motorists should follow directions of emergency services and traffic crews.
