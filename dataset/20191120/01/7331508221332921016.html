Back at it, Champ: NBA players celebrate Carmelo Anthony's return

20 Nov 2019, 01:22

After a 376-day absence, Carmelo Anthony returned to the NBA, starting for the Portland Trail Blazers on Tuesday against the New Orleans Pelicans. Anthony was wearing the unfamiliar No. 00 -- No. 15 is retired in Portland for Larry Steele, and No. 7 has been held out of circulation since Brandon Roy last wore it in 2011 -- but the sight of Anthony on an NBA court was enough to get his friends and colleagues from around the association excited on social media.

Not surprisingly, the first player to tweet about Anthony's comeback was longtime friend and fellow draft class of 2003 member LeBron James, hours before he took the court for his own game Tuesday.
