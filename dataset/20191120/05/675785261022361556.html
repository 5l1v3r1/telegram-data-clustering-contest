Celebs Go Dating’s line-up revealed as stars spotted arriving at the agency

Amy Childs, Dean Gaffney and James Lock are just some of the names hoping to find love as the next series of the show gets closer with filming soon to start

20 Nov 2019, 05:08 by Sam Elliott, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sam-elliott/

The next batch of unlucky-in-love celebrities are about to begin their search for 'the one'.

Celebs Go Dating’s line-up has been let out of the bag as the show gets ready to return.

EastEnders legend Dean Gaffney will be trying his luck after a number of failed relationships. He and his soon-to-be co-stars were spotted with filming starting.

Love Island's Amy Hart, 29, will also be leaning on the agency for tips - as will TOWIE royalty Amy Childs.

Malique Thompson-Dwyer, the Hollyoaks star, has also signed up.

 

Made In Chelsea's Olivia Bentley, 24, also turned up as they stars were put through their paces.

James Lock, who has recently split from Ellie Mackenzie, was also seen outside the show's London base.

Experts Anna Williamson and Paul Carrick Brunson will once again be dishing out their relationship wisdom - with flamboyant  Tom Read-Wilson also snapped on set.

 

The pros will hope few have heard the news that Geordie Shore legend Charlotte Crosby and Love Island hunk Josh Ritchie have called it a day.

They appeared on the show to fix their love life after a number of rows.

But it's all over after she made the shock announcement on Instagram.

"Me and Josh have decided to part ways," said the star.

"Sadly the relationship seems to have broken down. Hoping we can still remain civil and wish him the best for the future."

The split came months after she moved from the north east to Bolton to live with her boyfriend.

No start date for the show has been announced.
