Bala Box Office Day 12: Stays Good On Tuesday, To Now Cross 100 Crores In 3rd Weekend

The film should go on to accumulate around 3.25-3.50 crores more before the end of week

20 Nov 2019, 05:24 by Joginder Tuteja

Yet another record has been surpassed by Bala. In 12 days flat, the film has surpassed the lifetime numbers of Luka Chuppi, one of the earliest superhits of 2019. That film had collected 95 crores in its lifetime and now Bala stands at 95.04 crores.

On Tuesday, the film collected 2.05 crores and that was a nominal drop when compared to Monday numbers of 2.25 crores. The film should go on to accumulate around 3.25-3.50 crores more before the end of week and then enter the 100 Crore Club in the third weekend.

For producer Dinesh Vijan this is good news indeed as Luka Chuppi as well as Bala are his productions. Even former had the potential to cross the 100 crores milestone but then Badla too was running alongside and that film did business on the same lines. In case of Bala, there would be competition in the form of Pagalpanti next week, so it has to be seen where does it eventually land up.

The film is a Super Hit nonetheless.

Note: All collections as per production and distribution sources
