CBSE CTET admit card 2019 released: How to download at ctet.nic.in

CBSE CTET Admit Card 2019: CBSE has released the admit card for the Central Teacher Eligibility Test. The candidates can download the admit cards from the official website-ctet.nic.in.

20 Nov 2019, 05:23 by Careers Desk

CBSE CTET admit card December 2019: The Central Board of Secondary Education has released the admit card or the exam hall ticket for the Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) examination. The candidates who are appearing for the CTET can download the admit cards from the official website — ctet.nic.in.

The Central Teacher Eligibility Test will be conducted on Sunday – December 8, 2019. Paper-I will be held from 9:30 am to noon and Paper-II from 2 pm to 4:30 pm.

CBSE CTET admit card 2019: Steps to download

Step 1-Visit the official website-ctet.nic.in

Step 2- On the homepage, click on the CTET admit card link that will be flashing.

Step 3: A new tab will open on the screen.

Step 4: In the space provided, enter the details asked and click on submit.

Step 5: The CTET admit card will be displayed on the screen.

Step 6: Download the admit cards and take a print out for future reference.

The candidates must take the admit card along with them to the examination hall. The CTET Part-I exam will be conducted for the candidates who will teach classes 1 to 5, while Part-II exam will be conducted for candidates who will teach classes 6 to 8.

The candidates will have to report 90 minutes before the commencement of the exam at the examination centre. They will not be allowed to enter the examination centre after 9:30 am for Paper-I and after 2 pm for Paper-II.

The scores secured by the candidates in Central Teacher Eligibility Test are valid up to seven years from the date of the declaration of results.
