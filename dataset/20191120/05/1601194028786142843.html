CM Punk dislikes blue WWE Universal Title

20 Nov 2019, 05:44 by Karan Bedi

When CM Punk was hired by Fox to be on WWE Backstage, it was hinted that he wasn't going to hold back on his opinions.

Well, according to wrestlingnews.co, it looks like Punk doesn't care for the new look of the WWE Universal Title, which Bray Wyatt revealed recently on WWE SmackDown.

It's been interesting to see how fans have reacted to the recent designs of WWE Championship belts. Some wrestling fans have liked the look of it while others wish that WWE would go back to the old look of the titles.

It's suffice to say that in terms of belt designs, nothing comes close to the "Winged Eagle" belt but that's beside the point.
