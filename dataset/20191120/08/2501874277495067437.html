Three Injured in Head-on Collision Near Orcutt

20 Nov 2019, 08:45 by Edhat Staff

Photos: SBCFD

By edhat staff

Three people suffered injuries following a head-on collision on Highway 101 near Orcutt early Wednesday morning.

At 5:44 a.m., Santa Barbara County firefighters responded to the scene in the southbound lanes near Clark Road with a CalStar helicopter and ground ambulance.Â 

Upon arrival, fire crews discovered a head-on traffic collision between two vehicles with one landing on its side. There were no subjects trapped or needing extrication.

Three people were injured, one with minor injuries and two with moderate injuries that were transported to Marian Medical Center via ground ambulance.

California Highway Patrol is investigating the incident.
