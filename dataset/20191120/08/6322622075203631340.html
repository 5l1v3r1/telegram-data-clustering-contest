Anwar at Istana Negara message fake, says PKR leader

20 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 20): A PKR leader has denied having circulated a message saying PKR president Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim had gone to Istana Negara today for an audience with the Yang di-Pertuan Agong.

Ahmad Fahmi Mohamed Fadzil, the PKR communication director, said the message circulating on social media since this morning is fake and intended to embarrass him.

“I wish to confirm that I did not circulate the message. That is a despicable, dirty and irresponsible act,” he said.

Ahmad Fahmi said he will lodge a police report because his name has been used by irresponsible people for a political purpose.

The message circulated over WhatsApp went like this: “PKR communication director Fahmi Fadzil confirms that Anwar is at Istana Negara for an audience with YDP (Yang di-Pertuan Agong). Media friends are asked to be in front of the palace for coverage.”

Anwar was at Parliament early today to attend the Dewan Rakyat sitting. He arrived at 9.50am, and immediately confirmed that the message was fake.
