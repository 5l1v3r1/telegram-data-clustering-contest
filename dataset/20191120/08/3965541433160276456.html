Elderly woman struck by car, critically injured on Bellevue Way

20 Nov 2019, 08:26 by KOMO News Staff

BELLEVUE, Wash. - An elderly woman suffered life-threatening injuries when she was struck by a car Wednesday morning at a busy Bellevue intersection, police said.

Police and medics responded to the scene, on Bellevue Way at NE 24th Street, at about 6:30 a.m. after receiving reports of a serious injury crash invoving a pedestrian.

The pedestrian, an 85-year-old woman, was found with severe life-threatening injuries. She was rushed to Harborview Medical Center in Seattle.

The driver of the car stayed at the scene to help and is being cooperative, police said.

The circumstances leading up to the accident are under investigation.

The southbound lanes of Bellevue Way are closed at the scene during the investigation.
