This is who celebrities are voting for in the 2019 general election

The first TV debate occurred last night, on ITV1, with Jeremy Corbyn, from Labour, vying with Boris Johnson, the Conservative Party leader

20 Nov 2019, 08:38 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

The General Election is fast approaching - with Labour and the Tories set to do battle, and a string of other parties also looking for your votes.

The first TV debate occurred last night, on ITV1, with Jeremy Corbyn, from Labour, vying with Boris Johnson, the Conservative Party leader.

YouGov reckons the debate ended in a virtual tie.

The chances are, you've already made your mind up over who you're voting for on December 12.

After all, a slew of famous faces have already had their say - from outspoken right-wing activist Katie Hopkins to musicians Liam Gallagher, Slowthai and Stormzy.

So, who are they backing - the Brexit Party, the Tories or Labour? Or maybe the Lib Dems or Greens?
