Three arrests after gardaí seize two guns in Limerick City

20 Nov 2019, 08:37 by Newstalk

Gardaí have seized two guns after searching a house in Limerick City.

The discovery was made in Ballynanty, on the north side of the city, at around 7:30pm yesterday evening.

The search involved detectives from Mayorstone Garda Station, assisted by members of the Divisional Drug Unit and Regional Armed Support Unit.

Gardaí say three males - a teenager, one man in his 20s and another in his 50s - were arrested at the scene.

They were taken to Henry Street Garda Station and Roxboro Road Garda Station, where they are being detained under Section 30 Offences Against the State Act.
