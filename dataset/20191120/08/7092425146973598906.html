WATCH: The second trailer for Cats has landed and it's just as bonkers as the first one

20 Nov 2019, 08:24 by Ciara Walsh

The second trailer for Cats has landed. 

You might remember the intense reaction to the first trailer for Cats earlier this year - it pretty much left everyone speechless.

With Idris Elba, Judi Dench, James Corden and many more A-list celebs, it has the makings of a great film, however the famous faces attached to cat bodies is a bit too bonkers for some.

Tom Hooper, director of the King's Speech and Les Miserables, is the mastermind behind the highly-anticipated film with one of the main questions related to the use of CGI animation.

You'll recognise some of movie land's most revered names including Sir Ian McKellen but it must be said that seeing them in cat form takes a bit of getting used to.

There's been a strong reaction to the latest trailer featuring Macavity the Mystery Cat (Idris Elba), Grizabella (Jennifer Hudson) and Old Deuteronomy (Judi Dench) to name but a few.

It lands on December 20 and judging by the hype so far, you're going to hear a lot more about this film release over the next few weeks.
