How Jose Mourinho’s Tottenham could line up after Portuguese appointed new manager

The change offers a fresh start for some of Spurs’ players who were out of favour under Mauricio Pochettino

20 Nov 2019, 08:12 by Jack Rathborn

Jose Mourinho has been appointed as the new manager of Tottenham Hotspur following the sacking of Mauricio Pochettino.

The Portuguese will take his first training session on Wednesday afternoon.

His first match is against West Ham on Saturday, handing a fresh start to the squad and opportunities to prove themselves and win a starting role.

Players such as Christian Eriksen, Jan Vertonghen and Toby Alderweireld could all be back in favour.
