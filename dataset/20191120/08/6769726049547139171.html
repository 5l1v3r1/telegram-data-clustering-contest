Emergency services at Girrawheen crash

20 Nov 2019, 08:21 by Staff Writer

EMERGENCY services are at the scene of a serious crash in Girrawheen this afternoon.

There were initial reports of a car crashing into a house but authorities have since been confirmed the incident to be a car striking a tree.

The car has rolled over near a house, around the intersection of Marangaroo Drive and Girrawheen Ave, at about 2.40pm.

A WA Police spokesperson said a person appeared to have received significant injuries.

Marangaroo Drive westbound at Girrawheen Ave have been closed off.

Motorists are advised to avoid the area.
