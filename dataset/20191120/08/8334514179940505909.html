Russia says Israeli air strikes on Syria a wrong move: Ifax

20 Nov 2019, 08:09

MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Mikhail Bogdanov said on Wednesday that Israeli air strikes on Syria were a wrong move, and Moscow had contacted its allies about the incident, Interfax reported.

Israel has said its aircraft struck dozens of Iranian and Syrian military targets in Syria on Wednesday in retaliation for rockets fired toward Israel a day earlier.
