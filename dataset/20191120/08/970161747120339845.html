Martin Kemp gets revenge on son Roman by voting him into I'm a Celeb trials

20 Nov 2019, 08:29

You know how revenge is a dish best served cold?! Well, for Martin Kemp it’s a dish best served with a side order of fish eyes and kangaroo testicles.

The Spandau Ballet star proudly declared he’s nominated son Roman for the latest I’m a Celebrity Bushtucker Trial to allow the radio DJ to put his money where his mouth is.

Roman may have well and truly put his foot in it with dear old Pops after mocking his singing ability on Monday night’s show.

So when it came time to vote…

Sensing the prime opportunity for payback, Martin wrote on Twitter: ‘Just voted for my boy to eat Kangaroo B****ks .. That’ll show him!!’

Turns out Martin’s cheeky little ploy worked out – and now Roman is joining forces with Adele Roberts for his first ever trial – aptly called Just Desserts.

After previously bragging he would eat anything put in front of him, it’s time for Roman to prove he’s not all bark and no bite (…literally).

The star has been proudly talking about his family heritage which includes his 80s legend parents Martin (who performed in Spandau Ballet) and Shirlie (a backing singer for Wham!), and his godfather George Michael.

But during a chat he joked that 58-year-old Martin ‘couldn’t even sing or play an instrument’ when Spandau Ballet was formed.

Roman laughed: ‘He still can’t. He still hasn’t realised his mic wasn’t on for Live Aid!’

But he also praised Martin, who also appeared on EastEnders, as his ‘hero’ after fighting through a brain tumour and rebuilding himself from scratch.

‘How can my dad not be my hero through all of that. We’re a proper unit,’ he added.
