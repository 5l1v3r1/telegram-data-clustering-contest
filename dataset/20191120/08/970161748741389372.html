Camila Cabello has had feelings for Shawn Mendes since 2015

20 Nov 2019, 08:49

Camila Cabello has admitted she’s had feelings for her boyfriend Shawn Mendes since 2015, despite only dating him since earlier this year.

Oh, you guys.

The Havana hitmaker, who was pictured looking chill in gym gear and giving cameras a cheeky pose as she arrived at her vocal coach studio, explained that she ‘bonded with him’ when they collaborated on their track I Know What You Did Last Summer.

‘During I Know What You Did Last Summer I really bonded with him as more than a friend,’ the 22-year-old said. ‘I think he did, too, but we were both really young, and he was experiencing the pressures of his career.’

She added to Rolling Stone: ‘I don’t think we knew what to do with those feelings. It was this awkward thing where we both liked each other, but we weren’t together. It was just weird.’

Fast-forward to 2019, when they reunited for their hit Senorita, and apparently all the feels came flooding back.

‘Our paths just didn’t cross in that way romantically until we started hanging out again and writing. For me, it just brought it back,’ Camila said.

The star also melted everyone’s hearts by saying that her Canadian beau feels like home to her.

Camila and Shawn have only been dating since July 2019, but it seems that things are moving pretty quickly between the pair, with them getting tattoos together.

Both chose to get a new design etched onto their body, with Camila choosing a quote from Shakespeare In Love along the inside of her little finger and boyfriend Shawn opting for the letter A behind his ear.

Meanwhile, they’re no strangers to a spot of the ol’ PDA, with one of their most recent dates seeing them all over each other at a basketball game.

They were papped snuggling up, holding hands and kissing while sitting in the audience at the big game.

And it seems we can always count on them for cracking date ideas.

In a recent Q&A in Kuala Lumpur, Shawn said: ‘Camila and I will basically wake up and find the nearest coffee we can find.’
