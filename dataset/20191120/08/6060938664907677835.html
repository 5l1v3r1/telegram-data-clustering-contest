'Chehre': Ankita Lokhande or Mouni Roy, who will step into Kriti Kharbanda's shoes?

20 Nov 2019, 08:17 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After Kriti Kharbanda exiting the movie 'Chehre', the names of new actresses replacing Kriti have started doing the rounds. Reportedly, Anand Pandit has almost finalized the leading lady.

According to report in a tabloid, on Tuesday, popular actresses Mouni Roy and Ankita Lokhande were seen leaving the residence of the producer but nothing has been confirmed yet regarding who has been roped in for the film.

Meanwhile, it was earlier reported that Kriti's 'starry tantrums' compelled director Rumy Jafry and producer Anand Pandit show her the exit door. The makers had expressed their displeasure about her behaviour and had also warned her of action, but she did not pay any heed.

The film 'Chehre' which is a mystery thriller featuring Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi in lead roles is slated to release on April 24, 2020.
