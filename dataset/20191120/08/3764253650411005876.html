Plus-sized reality show star prevented from travelling to Dubai because of her size

20 Nov 2019, 08:05

- A Ghanaian plus-sized reality star, Precious Mensah, has shared the reason behind her not travelling to Dubai

- Precious popularly called PM won the Atinka FM’s plus-sized show, Di-Asa, and was meant to travel to Dubai as part of the prize

- PM was, however, unable to travel due to her big size

Popular Ghanaian reality show star, Precious Mensah also known as PM, recently won the Atinka FM’s plus-sized show, Di-Asa, to the joy of fans on social media.

Mensah was also meant to travel to Dubai with the runner-ups on the show as part of the prize but unfortunately missed out on the lifetime opportunity.

According to reports, Mensah had already checked in her luggage at the airport but was prevented from boarding the flight due to her big size.

In an interview with Yen.com.gh, Mensah revealed that she felt really bad when she could not travel with her friends and that if she had made the trip, it would have been her first time outside the country.
