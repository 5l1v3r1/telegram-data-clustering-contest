Manchester United fans want out-of-job Pochettino to take over from Solskjaer

20 Nov 2019, 08:56

- Manchester United fans believe Pochettino is the right man for the job

- Pochettino had previously been tipped for the United job even before Solskjaer's appointment

- After Pochettino left Spurs, United fans trooped to Twitter to call for replacement of their current manager with the Argentine

- Both Pochettino and Solskjaer have had poor starts in the 2019/2020 season

News of the sacking of Mauricio Pochettino by Tottenham has been met with excitement from a section of Manchester United fans who would prefer to have him as their manager instead of Ole Gunnar Solskjaer.

Some believe dismissing Pochettino after a five-year stint at Tottenham was probably a bad move, one that could also be costly for the club in the long run.

Aside from reaching the Champions League final, Pochettino established the London side as one of the regulars in Europe’s elite competition, sometimes at the expense of bitter rivals Arsenal.
