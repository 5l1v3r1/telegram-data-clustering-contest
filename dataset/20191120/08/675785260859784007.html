New Tottenham boss Jose Mourinho gushes over Spurs transfer target Bruno Fernandes

Bruno Fernandes was a transfer target for Tottenham and Manchester United over the summer but remained at Sporting Lisbon - and Jose Mourinho is a big fan

20 Nov 2019, 08:10 by James Whaling, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/james-whaling/

New Tottenham boss Jose Mourinho is a huge fan of Bruno Fernandes - who was strongly linked with a move to Spurs over the summer.

Tottenham appeared to be in a straight fight with Manchester United for the services of Fernandes - but he remained at Sporting where he established himself as one of Europe's hottest properties.

The Portuguese international plundered 31 goals from midfield in just 52 appearances last term and already has eight strikes in 14 games this season.

He is set to be the subject of yet more interest when the transfer window re-opens in January - and Mourinho has spoken of his admiration for the 25-year-old.

 

“I was surprised by the season of Bruno Fernandes," he told Eleven Sports in the summer.

“It was easy to predict quality, he is a very good player, but scored a very high number of goals for a midfield player.

 

“It shows that you need to have instinct and qualities to score and he has it all.

“He strikes the ball well, heads well, cool when he is one-on-one with the goalkeeper. He finds space in the penalty area. It’s all there.”

Mourinho has penned a three-and-a-half-year deal in north London after Spurs sacked Mauricio Pochettino on Tuesday night.

It is his first managerial role since being sacked by Manchester United last December.
