How to get free fries at McDonald's this Friday

Medium portions of fries will be completely free

20 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Molly Holt, Victoria Jones

For one day only, McDonald's is giving away a free portion of fries.

The golden arches restaurant has decided that on Friday, November 22, medium portions of chips will be completely free via the McDonald's app.

Customers can claim one pack each, the Mirror reports , with no purchase necessary, but you just need to make sure you place your order via the McDonald's app.

The offer is available to all new and existing McDonald's app customers, in selected branches.
