Harry Kane sends message to Mauricio Pochettino after Tottenham sacking

20 Nov 2019, 08:38

Harry Kane has paid a special tribute to Mauricio Pochettino after the Argentine was sacked by Tottenham on Tuesday evening.

Less than six months after guiding the team to the Champions League final, Pochettino has been axed by Spurs following extensive talks with the club’s chairman Daniel Levy over the past week.

Jose Mourinho has now replaced Pochettino as manager and has signed a three-and-a-half year deal with Tottenham.

Kane, meanwhile, has played a key role at Spurs since Pochettino took over in 2014 and has thanked the Argentine for his support during their time together.

‘Gaffer. I’ll be forever thankful to you for helping me achieve my dreams,’ said Kane.

‘We’ve had some amazing moments in the last 5 and a half years that I will never forget.
