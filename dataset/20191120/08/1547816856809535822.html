Singer-actor Jennifer Lopez, one of the most successful stars in Hollywood reportedly has a net worth of a staggering 400 million dollar! Converted into rupees, it is almost 2,800 crore and the number has sent us reeling!

Singer-actor Jennifer Lopez, most famously known for her pop and dance chartbuster hits like On The Floor, Dance Again, Papi and several others, is one of the most successful stars in Hollywood. She started with dancing on the TV show In Living Color, but her first big break was in 1997, when she was chosen to play the lead role in Selena. Her performance was lauded and since then, there has been no looking back for this 50-year-old star. Well, as she is one of the most popular stars globally, we’re sure you are curious about her net worth, and after knowing the figure, it is surely gonna send you reeling!

 As reported by Celebrity NetWorth, Jennifer Lopez has a net worth of a staggering 400 million dollars. Converted into rupees, it is almost 2,800 crore and the number is a lot! Recently, Jennifer Lopez revealed in GQ’s Men of the Year issue that she didn’t get paid for Hustlers, a crime drama about New York strippers. She revealed that she did it for free and produced it. However, the report in Celebrity NetWorth did mention that she does make a salary of 40 million dollars per year. She had been on the judging panel of the music reality show American Idol, for which she was paid 12 million dollars per year.
