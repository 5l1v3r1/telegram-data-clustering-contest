Commission-Free Trading App Robinhood to Debut in the UK

20 Nov 2019, 08:04 by Rachel McIntosh

Robinhood’s commission-free trading app is making its debut in the UK (the land of its namesake), according to an official blog post from the company posted on November 19th. The move comes  after the company achieved a $7.6 billion valuation with the completion of a $323 million fundraising round in July.
