2020 Hyundai Creta India Launch At Delhi Auto Expo In February

20 Nov 2019, 20:12 by Team GaadiWaadi

The upcoming next-gen Hyundai Creta gets the new design theme along with luxurious interior design

Hyundai commenced works on the next generation Creta last year based on the same platform from Kia Seltos that launched recently and becomes India’s best selling SUV with monthly sales crossing 12,000 units mark. Early this year, the company has revealed the iX25 at the Auto Shanghai and launch recently in the Chinese market. It is basically the Creta for our market which due for launch at upcoming 2020 Delhi Auto Expo with BS6 petrol & diesel engines.

Here are the top 5 changes expected on the next-generation Creta.

1. Front Design

Hyundai has completely redesigned the 2020 Creta as it gets the latest design language and the major changes are at the front as the signature Hyundai grille is big. The 2020 Creta gains split headlamp setup in which LED DRL is placed on top and projector headlamps are positioned below it with a new bumper along with the fog lamps at the lower part. The side turn indicators are placed on the hood along with the DRL’s.
