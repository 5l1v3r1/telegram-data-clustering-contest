How tall is Declan Donnelly and is he taller than Ant McPartlin?

20 Nov 2019, 20:10

Ant and Dec have been reunited on I’m A Celebrity – and that means we can expect the return of jokes about Dec’s height.

The presenter’s stature has long been the subject of gags on the show, peaking in 2018 when Holly Willoughby – who co-hosted the show while Ant was taking a year off – coined the catchphrase: ‘He’s loads of fun and he’s five foot one.’

All of which led to his Wikipedia page being changed accordingly – with that exact phrase – but how tall is Dec really?

Here’s what you need to know…

How tall is Declan Donnelly?

Dec is actually 1.68m tall – or 5 ft 5in in feet and inches.

Dec’s height came into question last year when he was hosting with Holly, who is 1.7m tall – making her only marginally taller than Dec.

However her choice of shoes could make her look a lot taller than him, and she appeared to be towering over him in the promotional photos for the show – leading to the jokes.

Holly’s quip on the season opener in 2018 led to Dec’s Wikipedia page being changed, to read: ‘Declan Joseph Oliver Donnelly OBE (born 25 September 1975) is an English television presenter, producer and actor who is loads of fun and only 5’1.’

The joke was picked up by former I’m A Celebrity winner Georgia Toffolo who quipped: ‘OMG someone changed Dec’s Wikipedia already.’

How tall is Ant McPartlin?

By contrast to Dec, Ant is 1.73m tall, making him 5ft 9in.

Gags about height have never been far from I’m A Celebrity, with Ant and Dec describing 2017 contestant Dennis Wise as ‘a very small man’, even though he is actually 5ft 6.

All of which was ultimately settled when Dennis was voted out of the jungle – and was revealed to actually be that little bit taller than Dec.

Awkward.

I’m A Celebrity continues on ITV on Wednesday night at 9pm.
