Akon plans to run for president to move US forward in 2024 with Kanye West

20 Nov 2019, 20:13

- Evergreen worldwide singer, Akon, said there is hope he will run in the US 2024 presidential race with Kanye West

- The Smack That crooner said he feels so unempowered and unsafe under Trump's presidency and said he feels the race is going to be a fight

- According to Akon, though he does not have the same political belief with West, he believes the difference could make for a great team

Legendary singer, Akon, is gunning for the 2024 US presidential run and might even collaborate with popular American rapper, Kanye West, as reports suggest.

Yahoo reports that the I Wanna Love You crooner on Monday, November 18, spoke about his stepping back from the 2020 presidential race.

“I know what the issues are. And as a person, as an immigrant, as a black man, as whatever you want to call it, I kinda know or feel like I know that I can take the country and move it forward. I really believe so,” he said.

He also spoke about how he has been watching closely and how he feels so much unsafe and unempowered under President Trump’s administration, and how running might be a tough fight.

“I feel a lot more unsafe. I feel unempowered. Honestly, I feel it's gonna be a fight. I've been thinking about running for 2020 very seriously,” he said.

When asked what he thought about Kanye West, who has also shown interest in running, he said that he does not see it as a competition.
