I'm a Celebrity star 'destroyed' by Gabby Allen over relationship chat

I'm a Celeb star Myles Stephenson spoke about looking for a "meaningful relationship" - but Gabby hit back

20 Nov 2019, 20:33 by Sophie McCoid

I'm A Celebrity star Myles Stephenson has been savagely taken down by his Love Island ex Gabby Allen.

Gabby and Myles were together for just over a year, before they split after Rak-Su singer Myles reportedly cheated on her.

Last night Myles was chatting to his I'm a Celebrity campmates about looking for love.

Myles said he worried that he found it hard to work out if a girl liked him for him as person or for who he was.

Ian Wright said: "You have to go to places that are more obscure, you can’t be going to places where the girls all like your music. 

"My missus didn’t know anything about football.  I met her in a pub in Notting Hill, it was the biggest kind of mugging off in respect of meeting someone.

"I’m not saying I‘m Brad Pitt but she mugged me off so beautifully I knew she was the one.”

And now Gabby has responded, after being told about the conversation by her fans.

Taking to her Instagram stories Gabby shared a photo of a cheetah with Myles face stuck on it.

She said: "Omg I'm dead, I'm not even watching the show but people are sending me things like this. Saying meet "meaningful milo"

"Apparently he was on about 'meaningful relationships' I'm not sure what the guy knows what he's on about tbh.

"I didn't know cheating, lying and a whole load of other (poo emoji) came under 'meaningful' who knows? Each to their own."

Earlier this year there had been rumours that things were not right with the pair, after they stopped posting pictures together.

And in August there were reports that Gabby, 27, had split with Myles after she discovered he'd been messaging other girls.

Gabby has confirmed the news in an emotional Instagram story post.

She wrote: "The newspaper articles are true.

"I am no longer in a relationship.

"I hope youse have all had a better week than me.

"If anyone else has anything they need to tell me, please do. x."

Gabby and Myles were initially inseparable and Myles moved into Gabby's flat earlier this year.

But she had grown suspicious of his behaviour in recent months and he moved back in with his mum to give the pair space.

Gabby, starred in the last ever series of Celebrity Big Brother and was rumoured to be seeing Myles during her time on the show.

Scouser Gabby was linked to Dan Osbourne, with CBB viewers accusing the pair of flirting during the show, but Gabby told the girls she was seeing someone on the outside - which turned out to be Myles - as she posted photos of a night out together when she walked out of the house.

Captioning a video of Myles drinking and dancing in a club "Happy Birthday Baby", Gabby then posted a video of the pair snuggling up to each other, as she kissed him on the cheek.

Speaking about Gabby's new man to OK! Magazine, Gabby’s mother Paula said: “It’s really early days.

"Gabby is selective about who she goes out with now because she doesn’t want to get hurt again.

“She’s not jumping into a relationship, but she deserves to be with someone who’s nice to her.”
