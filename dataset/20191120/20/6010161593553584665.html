Soulcalibur 6 Season 2 arrives next week on November 26

20 Nov 2019, 20:51 by Stephany Nunneley

Season 2 for Soulcalibur 6 kicks off next week with new characters, battle mechanics, and balance adjustments.

Soulcalibur 6 Season 2 kicks off on November 26 and brings with it new paid DLC characters, along with an update featuring improvements to fighters’ balance, and new moves for all characters.

An update for all players will also include new Battle Mechanics such as the Soul Attack, which is a strengthened strike, and the Resist Impact which is a  defense move “capable of repelling any attack” including currently unblockable attacks.

Two new stages will be included with the update.

The new DLC character is Hilde, and later Haohmaru from Samurai Shodown and another character will be added for the season.

Notes for the Ver. 2.00 update are below. Once the patch is applied, replay data from previous versions can no longer be replayed due to gameplay fixes and improvements.
