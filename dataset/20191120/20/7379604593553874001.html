General election news: Tories accused of misleading voters after renaming one of their Twitter accounts ‘FactcheckUK’

20 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Natasha Clark

THE Tories have been accused of misleading voters after renaming one of their Twitter accounts “FactcheckUk”.

FullFact, the fact-checking charity, described the move as "inappropriate and misleading" and other critics called for Twitter to remove the party’s press account’s ‘verified’ status.

The Tories made the move after the leadership debate between Boris Johnson and Jeremy Corbyn on ITV last night.
