A nostalgic trailer for The Late Late Toy Show has been released

20 Nov 2019, 20:37 by Nick Fitzgerald

Viewers won't have to wait too long for one of the most anticipated festive shows on RTE

Ah, The Late Late Toy Show. It's not hard to think back to when you were a little nipper gathered together with the fam in front of the telly in the lead up to Christmas.

A nostalgic feeling for many, watching The Late Late Toy Show is something we remember with fondness. Many still watch the show no matter what age they are.

Ahead of the much loved festive show on RTE, the national broadcaster has released a warm and heartfelt trailer.

Featuring marshmallows, Christmas jumpers and popcorn angels, the trailer also sees one little boy, along with his family preparing to settle in to watch the show.

A special appearance is made by The Late Late Show owl, named Gabriel, after the late Gay Byrne.
