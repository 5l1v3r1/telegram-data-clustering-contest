Actor Emmanuel Armah Speaks For The First Time After Suffering From Stroke (+Video)

20 Nov 2019, 20:45 by vanessa nordzi

Veteran Ghanaian actor, Emmanuel Armah who has been battling stroke for sometime now has finally spoken.

Emmanuel Armah was reportedly struck with a stroke which affected his movement leading to him going off the screen for some time now.

The illness  reportedly left Mr Armah speech impaired,  a condition in which the ability to produce speech sounds becomes difficult.

In his first interview with One Ghana TV, the ailing actor eulogized Mr Samuel Nyamekye, CEO of Miracle Film Production for his pivotal role played in Ghana Movie industry,

In a video available to GhBase.com the veteran actor is seen struggling to talk indicating that he is still gradually recuperating.

Known for his  exceptional performance in movies, Emmanuel Armah has featured in movies like Every Woman Wants Me, Fresh Trouble, Beyond Love, THINGS MEN DO, Shadows from the Past among others.
