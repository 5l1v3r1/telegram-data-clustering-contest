'Kamma Rajyam Lo Kadapa Reddlu' Trailer 2: More direct & controversial - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

20 Nov 2019

The second trailer for 'Kamma Rajyam Lo Kadapa Reddlu' is more direct and more controversial.  True to his style, Ram Gopal Varma peddles propaganda using lookalikes of Andhra Pradesh's reigning political deities.

It's an extension of the first trailer, but what is striking is that a few elements are beyond parody.  Mahesh Kathi, who plays a CBI officer, thinks that arresting Pawan Kalyan will be the right thing to do.  A Lokesh lookalike commits a gaffe and says that Gangaveeti's father, Ganga, was murdered by his party. Towards the end, Ajmal Amir, who plays a Jaganmohan Reddy lookalike, announces that he is resigning.  All the while, Chandrababu Naidu lookalike is shown as a cunning, wily fox.  In Delhi, a Modi lookalike and an Amit Shah lookalike are shown to have their own Kautilyan plans.  

Penned by RGV and Karun Venkat, this one has music by Ravi Shankar. Jagadeesh Chikati and Sirasri have done cinematography and lyrics, respectively.

The political fiction will hit the screens on November 29.
