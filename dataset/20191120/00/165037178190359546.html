Bayern after 2 Premier League starlets

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Bayern Munich are looking for changes to the team and they seem to have a special interest in the Premier League. According to the 'Daily Mial', the Bavarians are after 2 starlets in the Premier League: goalkeeper Dean Henderson and forward Troy Parrott.

There weren't many incomings at the Allianz Arena in the summer, but Bayern Munich are trying to make a move in the next few months to give a breath of fresh air to their team. In particular, they want youthfulness and are looking for it in English football, where they have got their eye set on 2 Premier League starlets.

According to 'Daily Mail', the Bavarians are after the goalkeeper Dean Henderson, who is on loan at Sheffield United form Manchester United, and the Tottenham striker Troy Parrott.

Henderson is 22 years old and is an England youth international. He is on loan at Sheffield United from the Red Devils, where he is the first choice keeper playing all 12 matches.
