Man’s faced burned in gas explosion at Adelaide Airport industrial business

20 Nov 2019, 00:36

A man has serious burns to his face after a gas explosion at an industrial business near Adelaide Airport.

Emergency services were called to the Butler Boulevard premises following reports of an explosion just before 9am on Wednesday.

A man in his 50s is being treated at the scene for serious facial burns. He and will be taken to Royal Adelaide Hospital with non life-threatening injuries.
