United to renew the injured Bailly's contract

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

His contract ends soon, but Manchester United want to tie down his future as soon as possible, despite him still being injured and hardly being able to play in the last two seasons.

After hard, long and serious injuries, the African CB has hardly been able to play in the last two campaigns. His last official match was in April 2019.

But United took a gamble on the player and at 25, he still has a lot to give the Old Trafford club. They paid almost 40 million for his services.

According to 'The Sun', the ex Espanyol and Villarreal defender will be offered a contract renewal soon. From 1st January he could sign with any other club, but United have plans for Bailly.
