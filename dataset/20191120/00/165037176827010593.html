Lampard ready to battle with Klopp for Berge

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Chelsea are interested in Genk's Norwegian player Sander Berge. The player has been linked with teams in Italy and also Liverpool.

Sander Berge's season hasn't gone unnoticed from big clubs. The Norwegian who plays at CM is convincing with his solid football at Genk and lots of big clubs have shown an interest in signing him.

Liverpool, who are in Genk's Champions League group, join Napoli in the teams interested in the player.

However, according to the newspaper 'Metro', Frank Lampard's Chelsea are also keen and are ready to battle with Liverpool to sign him.

Berge is in his 4th season at Genk and signed when he was just 18. Before, he started at minnows Asker, where he moved to Valerenga in the Norwegian capital.

Despite his young age, he has played 165 matches as a professional player and has 20 caps for Norway.
