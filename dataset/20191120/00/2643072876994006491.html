Nasarawa State First Lady Struggles To Read Prepared Speech

20 Nov 2019 by Olawale Adeniyi

The wife of Nasarawa state governor, Salifat Sule seems to have difficulty in reading.

Saliat, who is the first wife governor Abdullahi Sule was struggling to read a prepared speech during the inauguration of a programme in the state.

She mispronounced the words like “flag off” as “flag up”, and seem to skip some words that she couldn’t pronounce.

The first lady also pronounced the word “development” as “department” and was quickly corrected by one of her aides.
