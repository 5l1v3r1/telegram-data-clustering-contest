The first acts for Sónar Istanbul are here

Things are shaping up nicely for the 2020 edition

20 Nov 2019 by Rebecca Maguire

The first lineup announcement has been made for the fourth edition of Sónar Istanbul.

Acts set to perform at the festival include Skee Mask, DJ Python and Umfang.

Sónar Istanbul presents a mix of international and local talent from the electronic music scene, and this year's festival is no exception, bringing together talent from all corners of the world to combine music, technology and creativity across the two-day festival.

The hotly-anticipated festival will take place at the Zorlu Performing Arts Center, a venue known for bringing different artists and genres together in the heart of Istanbul.

The festival has sold-out every year with a reported 10,000 tickets sold annually.

Early Bird Tickets for Sónar Istanbul are available now.
