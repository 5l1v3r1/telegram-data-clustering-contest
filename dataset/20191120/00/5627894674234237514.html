Students found under the influence of Xanax

20 Nov 2019

CHINO  (INT)  The prescription drug Xanax is finding its way onto school campuses.

Four students at Don Lugo High School and Ramona Junior High showed up under the influence of the anti-anxiety drug over a 3-day period. Each required hospital treatment.

Police mounted an investigation and arrested three students for possessing Xanax for sale. A 19-year-old Ontario youth was arrested for supplying the drug to the students.

Xanax is a controlled addictive drug that is illegal to obtain without a prescription from a medical doctor.
