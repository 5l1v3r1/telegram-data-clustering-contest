I wasn't hurt when I was knocked down - Andy Ruiz to Anthony Joshua

20 Nov 2019 by Navitalai Naivalurua

World Heavyweight Champion, Andy Ruiz Jnr has ominously told Anthony Joshua that he wasn’t that hurt when he caused one of the greatest heavyweight shocks of the generation.

Joshua had previously claimed that he lost to a “lucky punch sent by the gods” but Ruiz Jr has now responded by denying that he was never hurt, even when he was knocked down.

Ruiz says it was a lucky shot and it did not hurt him as he wasn’t wobbling after the knockdown.

He said that he had the will and fire inside him to keep him fighting and win the fight.
