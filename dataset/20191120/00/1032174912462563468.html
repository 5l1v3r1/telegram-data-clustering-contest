Sydney Sales Wrap: Best of the Week, November 21, 2019

Shop 80 per cent off Zimmerman, Anine Bing and Rag & Bone; 80 per cent off Rebecca Vallance and Christopher Esber; 75 per cent off homewares; plus a huge Mud sample sale.

20 Nov 2019 by Stephanie Vigilante

The Outnet
Online
Discount designer retailer The Outnet is taking up to 80 per cent off labels including Zimmerman, Ganni, Chloé, Oscar de la Renta, Valentino, Alexander Wang, Dolce & Gabbana, Anine Bing, Rag & Bone, Alexander McQueen, Marc Jacobs, Herve Leger, The Row, Balmain and The Outnet’s in-house label Iris & Ink. This Jil Sander clutch is on our list.

Until Dec 2

Net-a-Porter
Online
Retail giant Net-a-Porter is taking 50 per cent off a range of top-tier brands. Prices are slashed on everything from skirts to jumpsuits, from brands such as Balmain, Balenciaga, Ellery, Vetements and Roland Mouret.

While stocks last

Mud
In-store
Australian porcelain maker Mud Australia is throwing a one-day seconds and samples sale, with a minimum of 40 per cent off its handmade homewares. Tableware, bakeware, lighting and planters are all up for grabs, with seconds stock in some of Mud’s latest shapes and colours. The sale is at the company’s production studio, so you can check out the manufacturing process while you shop. All of Mud's porcelain is microwave and dishwasher safe. BYO bag or box.

Sat 9am–4pm

Rebecca Vallance
In-store
Score up to 80 per cent off past-season and one-off sample cocktail dresses, suits, beautifully tailored pieces, silk styles, premium knitwear and statement gowns at Rebecca Vallance’s warehouse sale. This Australian designer blends structured tailoring with luxurious fabrics, which basically means you can’t miss this sale.

Thu to Sat
Thu & Fri 8am–6pm
Fri 9am–2pm

Christopher Esber
In-store
Your minimalist summer wardrobe is in for a treat thanks to the Christopher Esber sample sale. Shop lightweight silks, lace and signature deconstructed knits at up to 80 per cent off. Expect archive samples and current and past-season stock.

Kas
In-store
Take up to 75 per cent off bedding, table settings, bath and beach towels, and more at Kas’s three-day clearance sale. Expect colourful patterns and bold designs from this online Aussie homewares label. A good excuse to jazz up your home.

Fri to Sun 9am–5pm

Matches
Online
Shop up to 50 percent off men’s and women’s clothes, accessories, bags, homewares and shoes at luxury online retailer Matches Fashion. Labels include Givenchy, Saint Laurent, Balenciaga, Isabel Marant, Rochas, Etro, Lemaire, Erdem, Jimmy Choo, Valentino and Marni. Give your wardrobe a spring refresh.
