Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Women’s Swimwear For Winter Vacations

20 Nov 2019 by Adrienne Jordan

Warm-weather vacations are right around the corner and its time to break out the suitcase. Save space in your luggage for some new and fresh swimwear: suits that are practical for active sports and adventure, or cute and sexy styles best made for poolside lounging. Here are 8 women’s swimwear styles for gifting someone special (or yourself) this holiday:

1.    O’Neill Salt Water Solids Strappy One Piece

Available in 8 colors, the O’Neill Salt Water Solids Strappy One Piece is sexy, but functional (think lounging by the pool, but also jumping in the ocean for some snorkeling). The one piece has over the shoulder straps; removable bra pads; and medium coverage in the back.

2.    Aerie Wide Strap Scoop Bikini Top

American Eagle’s Aerie brand has a fun-loving vibe for the youthful spirit. The Aerie Wide Strap Scoop Bikini Top is made from comfortable nylon and has the scoop cut that is popular these days. The 20% elastane helps to hold the stretch in the top so that it hugs the torso with ease. You can choose from black, olive, and red as well as pair with the matching High Cut Cheeky Bikini Bottom in several colors.

3.    Anna Kosturova Seashore Angel Bikini Top And Bottom

The designer, Anna Kosturova is known for having many of the label’s crochet-style swimwear featured on Sports Illustrated models. For a swimsuit that is a neutral color, but still pops because of the sexy cut, you should choose the Crochet Seashore Angel Bikini Top and Bottom. The pieces have authentic cowrie shells that are placed in linear rows on top of one another. The triangle top and bottom are held together by strings.

4.    O’Neill Natasha Lace Up Top and Natasha Hipster Bottom

Surfer girls will gravitate towards this sporty but chic two-piece look: the O'Neill Natasha Lace Up Top and Natasha Hipster Bottoms. The blue and gold striped swimsuit has cute tie-up laces in the middle and pairs well with the hipster bottoms that are lined and have medium coverage.

5.    Krahs Selena 2.0 Top

Designed by Selena Gomez, the limited edition Selena 2.0 Top by Krahs is available in both red and cow, depending on how playful you want to get. The top has a cinched center for support, as well as underwire, and adjustable shoulder straps. Pair the top with the Selena 2.0 bottom, which is high waisted and comes with a belt.

6.    Lululemon Salt-Lace One-Piece

Available in three colors, honeycomb multi yellow, Almafi blue, and black, Lululemon does sporty and sexy right with the Salt-Lace One-Piece. If you’re about to jump on a paddle, scuba dive, or snorkel on vacation, this suit has quick drying Esca fabric and was engineering for swim

.

7.    Sweet Paradise Club Reversible Milo Bandeau and Oskar Bottom

Designed in Hawaii, and manufactured in Hawaii, the Sweet Paradise Club swimwear is aimed at providing feminine and practical looks. The Sweet Paradise Club Reversible Milo Bandeau gives you the option of going out in hot pink or almond. Pair with the Oskar Bottom that has clever cutouts on the top and sides.

8.    Aerie V Cut High Bikini Bottom

The henna color of the Aerie V Cut High Bikini Bottom might remind you of cranberries or light-colored red wine. The V-pattern consists on suns and stars and pops of starbursts on the swimsuit. You can pair this cut with several other tops, ranging from the Longline V Scoop Bikini Top or the Wide Strap Bandeau.

9.    Tori Praver Pull Over Bralette Top

Comprised of 80% nylon, and 20% spandex, the Tori Praver Pull Over Bralette Top comes only in black, but is sleek and sexy, and has under bust seaming detail to keep the lines of the suit elegant. Be sure to pair with the high-cut Savannah Bottom, which gives a nice, partial coverage in the back.
