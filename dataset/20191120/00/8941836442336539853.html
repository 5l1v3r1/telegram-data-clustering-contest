LETTERS TO THE EDITOR: Stop making up crimes

20 Nov 2019 by THE WASHINGTON TIMES

ANALYSIS/OPINION:

So Roger Stone was convicted of lying to Congress (“Roger Stone criminal trial attended by a parade of ‘alt-right’ personalities,” Web, Nov. 17). Wait a minute — I thought a “liar” who was convicted of lying to liars was forgiven for 1,000 years.

After the years of lying during the Obama administration, we had to deal with the liars on the Mueller investigation — and now we must deal with the lying “whistleblower.” I can’t believe the American people will give the Democrats any credibility or votes in 2020.

Roger Stone should be pardoned by President Trump, along with Paul Manafort, Michael Flynn and anyone else who was railroaded by the socialist Democrats.

The victims should be paid for their time in jail and their legal-fee expenses. I’m sick of these manufactured crimes.

MICHAEL A. PACER

Paso Robles, Calif.
