Ray Stevenson, Alison Doody as lead villains in 'RRR' - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

20 Nov 2019

The 55-year-old Irish actor of 'Thor' series fame, RayStevenson, has been shortlisted to play the main villain in 'RRR'.  He will have scenes with either NTR or Ram Charan or both.  

"It’s a pleasure to have you play the lead antagonist Scott in our movie.  We can't wait to begin shooting with you," the makers of the multi-starrer today said.

Besides him, Irish actress Alison Doody will play the film's female villain. This will be her Indian debut.  "Had a wonderful time shooting for your first schedule... We are glad to have you play lead antagonist Lady Scott in our movie," the makers noted.  

As for NTR's heroine, Rajamouli has chosen Western theatre artiste Olivia Morris.  "Welcome aboard #OliviaMorris! We are happy to have you play the female lead #JENNIFER. Looking forward for the shoot," DVV Danayya's production house today said.
