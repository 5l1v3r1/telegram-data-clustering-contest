Boots launch new Black Friday 2019 deals with massive price cuts on electrical beauty

High street chain Boots has slashed the price of Oral B electric toothbrushes, Braun IPL hair removers, Fenty Beauty palettes and more in its newest Black Friday deals

20 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Chantelle Symester, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

Boots Black Friday deals are now live for shoppers desperate to bag some beauty bargains ahead of Christmas.

Beauty fans can now pick up some huge better than half price savings on a number of products, including expensive electrical beauty items, in stores and online at boots.com.

The chain previously announced it was rolling out price cuts ahead of 29 November at the beginning of the month, giving customers plenty of time to shop hundreds of deals that will be going live daily as we all countdown to Black Friday.

Top offers worth snapping up now include a whopping £220 off the RRP for the Oral B Genuis X electric toothbrush, now £120 in the sale and half price for the Stylpro rose gold makeup brush cleaner and dryer set, now £24.99.

But it's not just electricals that are massively reduced in the sale, there's also plenty of make up and skincare from premium brands like Fenty Beauty and Benefit too.

 

We have spotted the Fenty Moroccan Spice Palette for £21 and the Benefit Superstars set, worth £80, on sale for £32.50.

Fans of Boots own brand make up line No7 won't miss out either, the chain has slashed gifts sets like the No7 The Beauty Wardrobe to half price too in the savings.

   

Shoppers who have an Advantage card can maximise their savings when shopping in store or online as Boots are running a free £10 points event between 21 November and 24 November.

All customers need to do is spend £50 or more in store or £60 or more online.
