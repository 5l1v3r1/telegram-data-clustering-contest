Your Success Depends On Your Attitude – Abeiku Santana

20 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Radio and TV presenter Abeiku Santana has advised individuals on what they can do to be successful in life.

According to him, success doesn’t depend on your level of education or the skills you have but rather your attitude. Attitude has a great impact on your life and one of your greatest potentials in life.

He wrote on his twitter page what one can do to be successful and what goes into it; adding how he has categorized it.

” How far you can go largely depends on your attitude and relationship with people. I define success with the acronym ASK ,” he tweeted.

Attitude = 60 %

Skills = 25 %

Knowledge = 15 %


Attitude is everything
