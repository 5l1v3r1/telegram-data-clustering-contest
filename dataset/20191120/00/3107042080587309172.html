'No 1080' in dead rats on beach

20 Nov 2019

Toxicology shows no presence of 1080 in dead rats that washed up along with other animals on North Beach near Westport earlier this month.

  • This story was first published by RNZ

About 680 rats, an octopus, a calf and other animals were found washed up on the West Coast beach on Saturday, November 9.

The rats were collected and most were buried following advice from West Coast Regional Council.
