Alibaba prices Hong Kong share offer at HK$176/share, raising $12.9 billion

20 Nov 2019 by Reuters

(Reuters) - Alibaba (NYSE:BABA) has priced its shares at HK$176 each for institutional and retail investors in its Hong Kong listing, according to a statement from the company.

The Chinese e-commerce giant has confirmed the deal for 500 million shares, which will raise up to $12.9 billion.
