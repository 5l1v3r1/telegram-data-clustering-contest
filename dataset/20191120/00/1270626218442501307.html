Isoof who's facing 5 counts of murder and 1 count of attempted murder further remanded by Lautoka High Court

20 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

Muhammad Raheesh Isoof who is facing five counts of murder and one count of attempted murder has been further remanded in custody by the Lautoka High Court.

This is in relation to the alleged murder of the three adults and two children in the Nausori Highlands in August this year.

The bodies of the family members were found in the Nausori Highlands on the 26th of August.
