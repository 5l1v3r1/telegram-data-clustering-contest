The XI that Mourinho could play at Tottenham... With Dybala, Bruno and Bale!

20 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The newspaper 'The Sun' has already published a possible eleven with which José Mourinho could work in Tottenham soon. Several illustrious names would make up some new signings...

Mourinho has landed in London to take the Spurs' reigns, but he has done so with a few signings in mind. Those footballers who Mauricio Pochettino asked for time after time but who could not be brought in because of the spending in the new stadium ...

Also, although 'The Sun' does not make specific mention of Gareth Bale, there are many voices calling for the return of the Welsh striker. Bale had already impressed the Portuguese coach as a Spurs player and Mourinho wanted him in Manchester United.

While Eriksen ends his contract and seems to be wanted at other destinations (he dreams of Madrid), Tottenham has shown interest in players like Youcef Atal (Nice) and others of very high quality (and not cheap) like Paulo Dybala (Juventus) and Bruno Fernandes (Sporting CP).

The Sun's eleven includes Heung-min Son, who could alternate his position with a Bale whose return is contemplated more than ever by Mourinho's arrival to Spurs. According to 'AS', Mourinho adores Bale as a footballer and will propose him to Daniel Levy, president of the entity.

Here is the XI that Mourinho dreams of:

Hugo Lloris (goalkeeper)

Youcef Atal (central defender)

Davinson Sánchez (central defender)

Juan Foyth (central defender)

Ryan Sessegnon (winger)
