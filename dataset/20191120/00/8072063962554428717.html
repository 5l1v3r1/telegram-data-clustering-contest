Stakeholders to discuss cultivating a safe and equitable cannabis industry

20 Nov 2019 by ISL

(PRESS RELEASE) – The Saint Lucia Cannabis Commission will host a press launch on Friday.

The launch will be held under the theme “Cultivating a Safe and Equitable Cannabis Industry.” The event will start at 10 a.m., on Nov. 22 at the Government Information Service’s NTN studios, in Point Seraphine, Castries.
