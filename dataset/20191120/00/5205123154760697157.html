Everyone is falling in love with this Irish bartender’s poignant rendition of ‘Rare Auld Times’

20 Nov 2019 by Jack Beresford

AN IRISH barman is winning hearts and minds online with his stirring rendition of a familiar favourite from the Emerald Isle.

Take a trip to The Celt Bar on Talbot Street in Dublin and you could be in for a rare treat.

You might find bar worker Donncha entertaining patrons with his distinctive singing voice.

An example of his soulful vocal style surfaced online this week, courtesy of a new video shared by the good folk over at the Celt Bar on social media this week.

The perfect showcase of his impressive vocal talents, the video has gone down a storm back in Ireland and across much of the world.

It features Donncha performing a particularly memorable version of the old Irish classic Rare Auld Times.
