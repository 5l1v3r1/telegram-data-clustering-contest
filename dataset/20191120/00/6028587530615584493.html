Gift Guide 2019: Unique Gift Ideas For The Person Who Has It All

20 Nov 2019 by Felicity Carter

We all know someone who seems to have it all, and every gift-giving occasion proves to be a challenge. Instead of scratching your head, take some inspiration from these unique gifting ideas, which include everything from new beauty must-haves to personalized bottles of Champagne and Basquiat-inspired snowboards.

Slip Pink Marble Travel Set

For those who travel, a silk pillow and matching sleep mask is the perfect flight companion. The Slip team actually special-commissioned and developed the fabric over ten years to achieve the right mix of shine, thickness, softness and durability. The pillow is intended to be anti-aging while protecting against sleep creases and bed head.

AERIN Shagreen Bar Set

Aerin Lauder, granddaughter of Estée Lauder, established her namesake label in 2012 after spending 20 years at the family firm. With exceptional taste, she looks to bring elegance and function to objects, including this stylish bar set. It contains a Fausto jigger and shaker with four julep cups, four coasters, a bottle opener, a corkscrew and a bottle stopper, along with four Mattea cocktail picks and four napkins. Bring on a chic evening.

SHOP NOW

THE SKATEROOM+ Jean-Michel Basquiat Set Of Three Printed Skateboards

Everyone loves the late American artist Jean-Michel Basquiat, and these oh-so-cool skateboards feature his designs on the front. Thanks to THE SKATEROOM, they’ve put Basquiat's original “Horn Players” print on this triptych. The trio have been produced in Canada from maple wood and, of course, come with a mounting kit so the recipient can display in the home.

SHOP NOW

Balmain Black Logo Towel

The person who has everything just may not have a designer towel, and this one comes from French fashion house Balmain. In the brand’s signature black, it features the Balmain logo right in the middle and is made from 100% cotton terrycloth. Practical and stylish for winter getaways and will surely upgrade any bathroom.

SHOP NOW

Fortnum's Personalized Blanc de Blancs Champagne

The British institution, Fortnum and Mason are known for being the Queen’s grocers and so their personalized Champagne is rather special. Laurent Hostomme produces their Blanc de Blancs Champagne, located within the Grand Cru region of Chouilly. It is 100% Chardonnay which has been aged for three years “sur latte’”for extra complexity to give a light fizz with floral notes and a hint of acidity. Make it even more special by customizing the label as you wish.

SHOP NOW

Stephanie Windsor Antiques Diamond-Embellished Oval Locket

Stephanie Windsor’s love for jewelry began ever since she saw her grandmother's antique necklaces. Exclusively curated for Barneys New York, her vintage pieces include this locket. Oval in shape, it has been crafted in 18k yellow gold and features a raised cross motif. From the Victorian era, it has been adorned with deluxe oval and round white diamonds arranged in a starburst pattern.

SHOP NOW

Jacquemus Grained-Leather Coin-Purse Bracelet

Simon Porte Jacquemus is known for playing with proportions, whether it’s oversized clothing or minuscule accessories. This is a super cute, totally unique boxy purse-cum-bracelet made from textured leather. It features a wide band, specially designed to be wrapped around the wrist, and a micro wallet that will fit the essentials: house keys and card.

SHOP NOW

LaCalifornienne Cartier Watch

Someone who has it all probably has an elegant watch, but the ones by laCalifornienne are different, fun and completely covetable. The Los Angeles-based firm draws inspiration from the pink and blue magic hour skies of California and translates this onto classic timepieces such as this Tank by Cartier.

SHOP NOW

FORNASETTI Ordine Architettonico Scented Candle Set

Piero Fornasetti was a Milanese painter, known for changing 20th-century art with his classical drawings. This set of three candles are housed in hand-painted ceramic pots that feature his classical façade drawings, making them perfect for all of those who are into art, architecture and elegant fragrances. Made from vegetal wax, it is mixed with their signature “Otto” scent of ultra-relaxing lavender and thyme along with orris and cedarwood.
