Emirate Airline Sends Woman Off Plane For Being Too Overweight

20 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

One Precious Mensah, the 2019 winner of Atinka TV’s plus size women reality show ‘Di Asa’ in Ghana, has allegedly been refused entry into the Emirates Airlines’ plane to Dubai.

Naija News understands that she was not allowed in after the airline complained that her body size is bigger than a single seat on Emirates Airlines’ flight.

Precious alongside two runners-up, Maafia and Gaza, were given a sponsored trip to Dubai, as a price for coming on top during the competition.

The runners-up, however, were able to go with the flight.
