Mumbai get its new premium travel merchandise store

20 Nov 2019 by Nisha Shroff

Travellers looking for some premium travel gear options can check out the new store Trunk House in Mumbai, offering a fine curation of travel accessories from across the globe.

Located on the third floor of the Palladium mall, this high-end multi-brand travel merchandise store features smart luggage, business cases, and handbag options.

On offer are some new collection from luxury brands such as Calvin Klein and DKNY. Ideal for all business travellers, smart luggage by Heys from Canada feature bags that are inbuilt with integrated weighing scale and Bluetooth lock. The Italian brand Roncato offers bulletproof bags and other travel accessories by brands like Trussardi and Cerruti from Italy.

The highlight also includes cabin bags that have a laptop add on space, which helps the travellers to store their laptops conveniently and travel hassle-free.

Founder Amrit Uttam said, “The concept of having a multi-brand store that showcases premium travel gear under one roof is relatively new in the Indian market. Trunkhouse has established its presence in this area with the launch of our store. We are excited to see how this new journey turns out for us and all the brands we’ve given a luxurious home through this venture.”
