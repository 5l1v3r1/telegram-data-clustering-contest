Burna Boy Pulls Out Of ‘Africans Unite’ Concert In South Africa

20 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

African hip hop star Burna Boy has finally pulled out from the upcoming ‘Africans Unite’ concert due to death threats by some South African artists and online trolls.

Recall that despite his comments during the xenophobic attacks, Burna Boy still announced his intention to perform at the concert which was earlier scheduled to hold in South Africa on the 23rd of November.

But after a series of meetings with his team, Phambili and Play Media network have decided to withdraw the artiste from performing.

Also, the ‘Africans Unite concert’ which was scheduled to hold on the 23rd has also been canceled.

On this note, provisions have already been made for fans who already bought tickets to be refunded.
