3 Reasons To Visit NYC This Month

20 Nov 2019 by J.Q. Louise

Savor the last few weeks of Fall in the center of it all in New York City. Shopping, leaf peeping, fine dining, and more await those who head down to the Big Apple this time of year. Here are a few hotel specials that may just be enough to encourage you to pull the trigger on a quick big city escape to NYC.

1)   Fall Leaf Peeping Package at Le Méridien Central Park

The Fall Leaf Peeping package is available at the Le Méridien Central Park through November 30th. The package includes: a stay in a suite room with the best Central Park views for enjoying the colorful autumnal view, access to the ‘leaf peeping concierge team’ who gives guests the best tips for way to enjoy leaf peeping, in and around the hotel, a bottle of champagne in-room, complimentary WiFi and complimentary breakfast. The Le Méridien Central Park is conveniently located in Midtown Manhattan and has some amazing views of both Central Park and the skyline surrounding the park.

While visiting the hotel, be sure to also dine at the lobby-level restaurant, Kingside, which serves up both sushi and new American classics. It is a convenient and tasty spot either for a quick lunch before exploring Central Park or for a nice cocktail before dinner.

2)   Life’s a Picnic at Moxy Chelsea

If you would rather stay further downtown, head to the Moxy Chelsea and be sure to take advantage of their “Life’s a Picnic” package. Also available now through November 30th, the package includes: accommodations, complimentary Sole Bikes with baskets for two and a picnic that features—two San Daniele Prosciutto and imported mozzarella focaccia, two cacio and pepe chips, two slices of torta della nonna cake, two Polara imported sodas and two bottles of Panna water. While on your visit be sure to also head to the rooftop to check out the Fleur Room for some craft cocktails and amazing views. Located in the always cool neighborhood of Chelsea, the Moxy is a fun spot for any visit the NYC. Break out of your November routine and pop downtown before the end of the month!

3)   Thanksgiving Dinner at the Loews Regency New York

Located on the Upper East Side, the Loews Regency New York is a fabulous place in the city to spend any holiday. But particularly, this Thanksgiving, the team has something special in store. Guests and visitors can enjoy a full Thanksgiving meal from noon to 11pm on November 28th at the Regency Bar & Grill. The menu includes lots of shareable starters, such as: Roasted Butternut Squash Soup and Crab Cakes. And when it’s time to move on to the main event, guests will enjoy a classic American Thanksgiving meal. The meal includes: Bourbon Red Heritage Breed Turkey, stuffing, mashed potatoes, sweet potatoes and green beans.

When planning your visit you’ll also want to check out their “Shop & Stay” package, which features: Luxury accommodations, $50 Bloomingdale’s gift card, $100 Food & Beverage credit for The Regency Bar & Grill, complimentary personal shopping and cosmetic makeover services, complimentary delivery of purchases to the hotel for purchases over $300, complimentary gift wrapping and shipping for holiday gifts from Bloomingdale’s loyalists. Start the holiday shopping season in style with a little help from Bloomingdales and the Loews Regency New York!
