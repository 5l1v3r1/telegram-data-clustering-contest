T-Mobile handing out ShopRunner memberships and Reebok discounts next Tuesday

20 Nov 2019 by Alex Wagner

Next Tuesday, T-Mobile is rolling out deals that’ll help you save on some new clothes.

On Tuesday, November 26th, T-Mobile customers can get a free one-year membership to ShopRunner. This membership will get you free two-day shipping and free returns to more than 100 retailers, including Fanatics, MLBshop.com, Peet’s Coffee, 1-800-Flowers.com, NBA Store, Calvin Klein, Kate Spade, and Adorama.

Also coming next Tuesday is a Reebok deal that’ll get you up to 50% off your purchase plus free shipping, $0.10 off per gallon of gas at Shell, and you choice of one free Taco Bell item from the following three options: Chips and Nacho Cheese Sauce, Crunchy Taco Supreme, and a Mountain Dew Baja Blast.

Rounding out next week’s deals are Live Nation ticket offers and up to 40% off hotels from Booking.com.

T-Mobile will be handing out American Eagle gift cards to winners of its contest. Five grand prize winners will each receive a $500 American Eagle gift card and 50 first prize winners will each get a $200 gift card to American Eagle. Finally, 750 second prize winners will each get a $50 American Eagle gift card.
