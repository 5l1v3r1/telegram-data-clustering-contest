The Game Awards 2019 Nominee List Revealed as Voting Opens

20 Nov 2019 by Sillicur

It is that time of the year again when we reflect back on a year of awesome gaming and what better way to do it than to vote for your favourite games, developers, esports teams and much more. That’s what The Game Awards hosted and produced by Geoff Keighley is all about. The Game Awards 2019 will take place on 12 December 2019 (13 December at 03:30 for South Africans) and now, the voting has opened and the nominee list for The Game Awards 2019 have been revealed.

RELATED: Detroit: Become Human PC Release Date and Details Revealed

There are 29 categories to vote in over on the official website and you can register and cast your vote for your favourites from The Game Awards 2019 nominee list by heading on over to this link. Death Stranding leads the way, being nominated in nine categories, including GOTY, which isn’t all that surprising since we gave it a 10/10 in our review. Remedy’s Control, which we gave a 9.2/10 in our review, has been nominated in eight categories.

The Game Awards 2019 nominee list was picked by “a jury of 80 media and influencer outlets” and the choices, for the most part, look very solid, with a possible exception in the esports player of the year category where there is no player from OG, a team that made esports history this year.

Check out The Game Awards 2019 nominee list below and don’t forget to vote for your favourite in each category. Scroll down to the bottom of the article if you don’t like reading to watch the video announcement.
