Caroline Flack's beau Lewis Burton meets her at airport after South Africa trip

This 24-hour whistle stop trip marks the beginning of Caroline's chaotic Love Island schedule which will run her life in January

20 Nov 2019, 14:27 by Irishmirror.ie

Caroline Flack was greeted at the airport by her toyboy beau Lewis Burton.

The Love Island host, 40, had been in Capetown for 24 hours to shoot steamy scenes for promotion ahead of the new winter series in 2020.

Lewis got himself boyfriend brownie points when he made a beeline to the airport so he could be reunited with his girlfriend as soon as she got off the flight.

Caroline wasted no time in snapping pictures of her other half while they were stuck in traffic on the way back from the airport.

Lewis looked less than impressed as he crossed his arms firmly across his chest.

She wrote over the picture: "He should be the face of traffic 'Addison law'."

Caroline loves her job and it shows because she was watching the Love Island reunion on her British Airways flight home.

This 24-hour whistlestop trip marks the beginning of Caroline's chaotic Love Island schedule which will run her life in January.

The Surjury star is used to flying back and forth from the UK to Mallorca for eight weeks over the summer while Love Island is on.

But early next year, her schedule is about to get a whole lot busier.

Love Island has launched its first ever winter series and Caroline signed on the dot to present the show.

Instead, the winter version is set in Capetown which means the Love Island host will have to catch 13-hour flights to and from South Africa to the UK.

However, it seems Caroline is prepared for the madness.

She said: "24 hours in Capetown best time ever makes me so excited for next year."

Caroline crossed paths with her boyfriend Lewis at the start of the summer after she wrapped up her stint on Love Island.

The lovebirds even sparked engagement rumours in recent weeks but it seems the couple are happy just dating for now.
