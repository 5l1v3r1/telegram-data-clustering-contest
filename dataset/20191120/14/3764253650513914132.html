Jose Mourinho claimed in 2015 that he would not take Tottenham job

20 Nov 2019, 14:44

Jose Mourinho who is the new manager of Premier League side Tottenham reportedly claimed in 2015 that he would never join Spurs before making the move four years later.

The former Chelsea and Manchester United manager replaced Mauricio Pochettino who was sacked at Spurs for poor run of form.

Mourinho had been without a coaching job since he was sacked at Manchester United in December 2018.

The 56-year-old Jose Mourinho had two stints at Chelsea where he made the statement that he would never join Tottenham because of the Blues' fans.
