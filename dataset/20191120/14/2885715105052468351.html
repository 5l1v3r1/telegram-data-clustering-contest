Railways RRB JE document verification, medical exam date preponed, check revised dates

RRB JE document verification, medical exam: The same was scheduled to be held on December 20, as per the prior notice, however, it has now been preponed to be held on December 18, beginning from 8:30 am.

20 Nov 2019, 14:41 by Careers Desk

RRB JE document verification, medical exam: The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has rescheduled the document verification (DV) and medical exams to be conducted to hire at the post of junior engineer (JE). The same was scheduled to be held on December 20, as per the prior notice, however, it has now been preponed to be held on December 18.

Only the candidates who failed to report for document verification (DV) on the assigned date will be eligible to appear for this round. Candidates will also have to bring proof of the reason behind not attending the exam in the first attempt.
