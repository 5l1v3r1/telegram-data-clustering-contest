Spark for iOS gains inbox avatars, Dark Mode, iPad productivity enhancements & more

20 Nov 2019, 14:48 by Christian Zibreg

In its biggest redesign in years, Readdle’s beautiful Spark email client for iPhone, iPad and iPod touch has been redesigned from scratch with new features such as inbox avatars, support for iOS 13’s Dark Mode, multiple window support on iPadOS and other perks.

Right off the bat, I went to my Spark settings and tapped Personalization → Color Scheme in order to enable the new avatar option to make browsing my inbox visually easier with people’s photos and company logos next to emails. Spark’s personalization settings now come with the option to choose between Dark Mode and truly Black Mode, which the company says was created to reduce eyestrain and improve battery life.
