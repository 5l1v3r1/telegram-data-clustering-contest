Burna Boy Nominated For 2020 Grammy Awards

20 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Michael Bamidele

Burna Boy has finally proven that he is indeed the African giant with a Grammys nomination in the Best World Music Album category for his critically acclaimed album, The African Giant.

On the global scene, Lizzo leads the nominations for the 62nd Grammy Award with eight nods, while Billie Eillish and Lil Nas X follow closely with six nominations each. The three musicians are all are first-time Grammy nominees.
