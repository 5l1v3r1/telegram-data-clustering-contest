Puppies in training bring early festive cheer at airport

20 Nov 2019, 14:24

Puppies in Santa hats and Christmas outfits are brightening the days of air travellers in Ireland.

The festive pooches are on a training session in Dublin Airport today – and holiday makers are being encouraged to ‘come over for a quick pet’.

One, Luna, was filmed looking a little sheepish in a Christmas outfit, while sitting down and looking around.

Passengers appeared to be big fans of the adorable visitors, with the group being labelled ‘gorgeous’, ‘so cute’ and ‘absolutely precious’ on Twitter.

Wearing seasonal jumpers decorated with sequins, holly and pom poms, the young dogs are visiting Terminal One in the Irish capital, with the charity My Canine Companion.
