ESPN Updates Its Bowl Projections After New Playoff Rankings

20 Nov 2019, 14:42 by Matt Hladik

The College Football Playoff committee unveiled its new rankings last night, and the top of the list was unchanged.

LSU, Ohio State, Clemson and Georgia remain the top four, followed by Alabama and Oregon. Utah, Penn State, Oklahoma and Minnesota round out the top 10.

With the rankings out, ESPN’s Heather Dinich released the following bowl projections, based on the updated playoff standings.

Below are what the New Year’s Six matchups would be if the regular season ended today.

College Football Playoff Semifinal at the PlayStation Fiesta Bowl: No. 1 LSU vs. No. 4 Georgia

College Football Playoff Semifinal at the Chick-fil-A Peach Bowl: : No. 2 Ohio State vs. No. 3 Clemson
