Just In:Burna Boy Gets Grammy Award Nomination

20 Nov 2019, 14:44 by Ghbase New Writer

Burna Boy’s album, African Giant has been nominated in the Best World Album category at the next edition of the Grammy Awards slated for January 26, 2020.

He was nominated in the category alongside Altin Gün for Gece, Bokanté & Metropole Orkest Conducted By Jules Buckley for What Heat, Nathalie Joachim With Spektral Quartet for Fanm D’Ayiti, and Angelique Kidjo for Celia.
