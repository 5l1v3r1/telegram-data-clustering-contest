Army Sports Awards: All The Atmosphere Live From Sandhurst

We will be live at the inspirational Army Sports Awards at RMA Sandhurst where the top Army athletes will be honoured

20 Nov 2019, 14:43

It has been an impressive year for Army sport, with athletes, coaches, teams and leaders all working together on the path to victory during 2019. 

On Thursday, November 21, their phenomenal sporting achievements are being honoured in a glittering ceremony at Royal Military Academy Sandhurst. 

Over the course of the evening, guests will receive a mouthwatering three course meal and a chance to congratulate and be congratulated for their achievements. 

Categories for the awards include: Rising Star, Team Of The Year, Sportswoman Of The Year, Sportsman Of The Year and Lifetime Achievement Award.

The award ceremony starts at 9.50pm and is set to be a resplendent affair with winners being announced to a talented room of sportsmen and sportswomen, and guests. 

There will be some high profile sporting guests: Joe Townsend, a former Royal Marine Commando, 2014 Invcitus Games gold medal winner Steph Twell, who represented Great Britain at the 2008 and 2016 Olympics, and Millie Bright, a footballer who competed at the 2019 World Cup on the England team. 

Good luck to all those up for awards.
