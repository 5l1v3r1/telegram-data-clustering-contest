Ayr man left with ‘serious injuries’ after horror stabbing as police launch probe

20 Nov 2019, 14:18 by Justin Bowie

A 39-YEAR-old Ayr man was left with “serious injuries” after being stabbed in the stomach last night.

The victim was rushed to hospital after the horror attack which took place in a close on Glenmuir Road at 7.30pm.

Police say his condition is currently stable.

And now cops have launched an appeal for witnesses as they make a bid to hunt down the attacker.

They are keen to speak to anyone who may have seen anything suspicious at the time of the stabbing.

The male suspect is described as white, with a local accent.

He was wearing dark clothing with a baseball cap.

Anyone with information should contact Police Scotland via 101, quoting reference number 3254 of November 19.

Alternatively, you can contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
