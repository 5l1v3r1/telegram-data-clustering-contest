There are Chocolate Orange Twirls available in Derby and this is where

The elusive chocolate has been hard to find

20 Nov 2019, 14:07 by Noah Martin

Chocolate Orange Twirls are still available to buy from a shop in Derby but the stock is running out.

The limited edition treat has been spotted at the newsagent inside the King's Treatment Centre.

Situated at the Royal Derby Hospital, the shop had two half-full boxes left this morning (November 20).

Staff at the shop, which is next to the Delimarché café, said the Twirls had been there "for a while".

But, there were only around 15 bars remaining and those were the last of the stock.

Other newsagent and convenience stores are still struggling to get a new delivery, so these may be the last Chocolate Orange Twirls in Derby.
