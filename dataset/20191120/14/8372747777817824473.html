New park along Myakka River in Charlotte County to be discussed at meeting

20 Nov 2019, 14:45 by WFTX Digital Team

CHARLOTTE COUNTY, Fla. -- Charlotte County Parks and Recreation will be holding a public input meeting next month to discuss the development of Myakka Park in the Gulf Cove community.

The meeting will be at 6 p.m. on December 3rd at Tringali Park Community Center (3460 North Access Road, Englewood).

The final draft for the master plan will be discussed.

The park is located off Gallagher Boulevard, along the Myakka River just up river of El Jobean.
