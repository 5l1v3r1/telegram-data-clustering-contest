Nas, Snoop Dogg, Rick Ross, Jay Rock & More Attend 'The Irishman' Dinner

20 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Justin Ivey

West Hollywood, CA – Martin Scorsese’s highly anticipated film The Irishman is scheduled to premiere on Netflix on November 27. Prior to its streaming debut, Nas hosted a dinner inspired by the movie at the AGO Restaurant in West Hollywood on Sunday (November 18).

Snoop Dogg, Rick Ross, Jay Rock, ESPN’s Cari Champion, Karen Civil, BJ The Chicago Kid, Maverick Carter and Terence J were among the notable names in attendance at the event.
