Holly Willoughby gets eyeful as Chris Evans randomly strips down to underwear

20 Nov 2019, 14:32

Chris Evans gave everyone a right shock as he stripped down to his underwear live on This Morning and to say Phillip Schofield and Holly Willoughby had no idea where to look would be an understatement.

The radio DJ was on the sofa to discuss his latest charity work and car festival, but it didn’t take long for things to take a surreal turn.

Showing off the bruises and injuries from having the varicose veins in his legs stripped, the former Top Gear presenter gave everyone’s favourite duo a shock.

‘Are you actually going to take your trousers down?’ Phillip cautiously asked, before the all-important question: ‘What pants have you got on?’

‘I don’t have any teeny pants,’ the Evans jokes. ‘Do you have any teeny pants?’

And poor Holly got a right eyeful.

‘I’ve had my varicose veins microwaved,’ the 53-year-old revealed. ‘Emma Thompson said it’s up there with childbirth, having your veins stripped out.’

Unfortunate viewers watching at home managed to see the very bruised legs of the Big Breakfast presenter, which we’re sure is a sight none of us want to see again anytime soon.

As the interview concluded, Evans admitted: ‘I don’t know how to get my trousers back on.’

Former Gladiator Ace also appeared on the This Morning sofa, opening up about his failed engagement to Katie Price in the 1990s.

The bodybuilder admitted that the pair came from ‘similar backgrounds’, and bonded over their newfound fame.

This Morning continues weekdays from 10.30am on ITV.
