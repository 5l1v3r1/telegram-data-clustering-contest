Katie Price threatens to fly to Australia for Emily Atack showdown

20 Nov 2019, 14:48 by James Cox

Katie Price has threatened to fly to Australia to confront I'm A Celeb: Extra Camp host Emily Atack over the presenter's on-air STD jibe.

The former glamour model, 41, was furious after Atack's dig on the ITV2 spin-off show.

During the show, presenter Atack suggested that her co hosts Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas needed to get tested for sexual health diseases after they took a dip in the same pool where Katie and ex-husband Peter Andre first canoodled.

She said: "I hope you boys went to the clinic after being in that pool."

It's safe to say Price was not impressed.

A source told The Sun Online: "Katie says she’s not watching the show, but when Emily Atack mentioned her on Extra Camp all her friends told her what she said.

"Loads of fans were messaging her on social media too – and Katie was furious.

"She was telling people that she can’t believe Emily would say something so snide and if she ‘wants a problem, I’ll give her a problem’."

Price even considered flying over to confront Atack, and asked her pals for the former Inbetweeners star's number.

The source added: “At one point Katie was saying she’d fly out to Australia and confront her.

"She’s not going to, obviously, but she was asking around to see if anyone had Emily’s number.

"She thinks she wouldn’t be so brave to say it to her face.

“Katie reckons she’s due a public apology from Emily. She’s never had any issue with Emily so she’s really shocked by her joke.”

Price has reportedly refused to watch the latest series of I'm A Celeb after being snubbed by show bosses after asking to appear on the show for a third time.

She starred in the ITV reality show in 2005 and 2009.
