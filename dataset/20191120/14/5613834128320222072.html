Compulsion Games Releasing a Making-of Doc For ‘We Happy Few’ December 19

20 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Mike Wilson

With the final DLC for Compulsion Games’ We Happy Few entitled “We All Fall Down” now out in the wild, Compulsion have decided to celebrate a bit with the announcement of a new documentary about the making of the game.

The Cost of Joy: The Compulsion Games Story will be released on December 19th, and will “take viewers along the We Happy Few journey, providing a raw, behind-the-scenes look at the game as well as the plucky bunch of creatives behind it.”

We Happy Few and its expansions are out now on PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.
