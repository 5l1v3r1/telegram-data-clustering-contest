Jose Mourinho: Tottenham new boss names Joao Sacramento as his new assistant

20 Nov 2019, 14:43

- Jose Mourinho names Sacramento as his assistant at his new club

- The 56-year-old tactician was recently appointed new Spurs boss

- The north London side sacked their former manager Pochettino after five years in charge

Newly appointed Tottenham Hotspur boss Jose Mourinho has been ranked second most paid manager in the world.

The Portuguese tactician signed a deal that will see him represent the interests of the club until 2023 - almost one year after staying jobless.

It was earlier revealed that the former Chelsea and Manchester United gaffer will earn about £15million-a-year at the north London club.

And the figure, according to Daily Mail, ranks him just one step behind Manchester City boss Pep Guardiola who currently earns £20 million at the Etihad Stadium.
