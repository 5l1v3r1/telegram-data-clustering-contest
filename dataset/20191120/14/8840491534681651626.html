Funding boost for Mayo regional sport projects

20 Nov 2019, 14:27

THE Minister for Rural & Community Development, Michael Ring, has confirmed approval of €405,123 to two regional sports projects in County Mayo under the 2019 Sports Capital Programme.

Mayo GMIT Campus is getting €225,000 for an all-weather rugby playing facilities in Castlebar while €180,120 is going to Lake District Athletic Club, Ballinrobe, for an athletics track and facilities.

Said Minister Ring: “The Sports Capital Programme is the most important way in which the government assists in the development and refurbishment of sporting facilities and in the purchase of non-personal sports equipment.”

“The regional projects in Mayo being funded will provide better equipment and facilities for athletes to train and compete to the best of their ability.

"The funding announced today follows on from the €1,143,493 approved to 36 local sports projects in Mayo last week.”
