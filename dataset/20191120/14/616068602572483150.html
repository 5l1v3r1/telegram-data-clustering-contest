Show of Olympic proportion

20 Nov 2019, 14:57 by John Lawless

The Light Keeper, a show directed and created by Vernon Olympian Camille Martens, will be hitting the stage at the Vernon & District Performing Arts Centre next week.

Performed by members of Canada's National High Performance Team, the show will feature a fusion of circus arts, drama, dance, acrobatics and gymnastics. The cirque-like storytelling has been crafted to entertain audiences of all ages.

The show will be performed three times over the course of two days. There will be one show on Friday, Nov 29 at 7 p.m. There will be two shows on Saturday, at 2:30 p.m. and 7 p.m.

If you are interested in purchasing tickets, you can do so online.
