UPTET 2019 registration to close today, applicants complain of ‘slow server’

Uttar Pradesh Teachers’ Eligibility Test will consist of 2 papers-Paper 1 for the candidates who will teach from classes 1 to 5 and paper 2 for the ones will teach from classes 6 to 8.

20 Nov 2019, 07:51 by Education Desk

The UP Pariksha Niyamak Pradhikari, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh will close the application process for Uttar Pradesh Teachers’ Eligibility Test today, November 20, 2019. The candidates can pay the application fee till November 21, 2019. Interested candidates, who have not applied yet, must apply on the official website-updeled.gov.in by today.

Many applicants are unable to register due to technical issues. Some have taken to Twitter and are complaining slow server issue to the UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath.
