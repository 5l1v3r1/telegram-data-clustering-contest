Surprise! Death Stranding leads the 2019 Game Awards nominations pack

20 Nov 2019, 07:30 by Darryn Bonthuys

Arguably the biggest video game award show of the year, the titular Game Awards have evolved over the years to be an annual event that’s actually well worth a watch not only due to the kudos thrown at deserving winners but also for sneak peeks at upcoming titles. Last year saw Mortal Kombat 11, Rage 2 and the Outer Worlds all steal the spotlight with trailer debuts, while games such as God of War, Forza Horizon 4 and Fortnite cleaned up nicely in the awards section.

This year though? It’s going to be another tight race to the top as 2019 was another stellar year for new releases. The 2019 nominees have been announced, with Death Stranding unsurprisingly grabbing nominations in eight of the categories. Game Awards show host and boss Geoff Keighley apparently knows Hideo Kojima, according to my sources.
