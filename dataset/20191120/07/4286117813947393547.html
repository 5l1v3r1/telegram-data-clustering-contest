IndiGo flight from Coimbatore makes emergency landing in Chennai after smoke alarm goes off

An IndiGo flight from Coimbatore made an emergency landing at the Chennai airport after a smoke alarm went off in the cargo hold of the aircraft

20 Nov 2019, 07:55

An IndiGo flight made an emergency landing at the Chennai airport Wednesday after a smoke alarm went off on board the plane. The IndiGo flight was flying from Coimbatore and made its emergency landing after a smoke alarm went off in the cargo hold of the aircraft.

News agency PTI, quoting unnamed authorities, reported that all passengers and crew were safe.

The IndiGo flight, a Coimbatore-Chennai service, was carrying 168 passengers. The pilot of the flight requested priority landing after detecting a smoke alarm in the cargo area of the plane.
