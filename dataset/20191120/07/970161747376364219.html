Stormzy thought his Glastonbury set was so bad he 'cried for an hour' afterwards

20 Nov 2019, 07:49

Stormzy’s Glastonbury headlining set was a major cultural moment and one of the most memorable festival performances this decade.

But at the time, the grime star himself didn’t think so.

In fact, Stormzy was so upset with the show that he cried ‘for an hour’ after leaving the stage.

The 26-year-old, real name Michael Omari, has revealed that his in-ear monitors ‘blew’ 20 minutes into his Friday night headlining stint on the Pyramid Stage this year, leaving him without sound for the rest of the set.
