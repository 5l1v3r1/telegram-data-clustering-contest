Why #FartGate was trending and continues to be a hot topic of debate in the US

During a live interview on news channel MSNBC, a show's host and a lawmaker Eric Swalwell were discussing developments in the latest impeachment hearing. But viewers were more intrigued by a strange sound that sounded a lot...like a fart.

20 Nov 2019, 07:36 by Trends Desk

The on-going impeachment hearings against US President Trump may have divided people in the US, but one thing united everyone on Twitter on Tuesday night — an alleged fart. #FartGate dominated trends on Twitter, starting as a whodunnit and turning into a meme-fest.

What happened

During a live interview on news channel MSNBC’s Hardball show, host Chris Matthews was discussing the latest developments in the impeachment hearing with US representative Eric Swalwell. But viewers were baffled by a strange sound, that sounded a lot…like a fart.

The Democrat from California seemed to pause briefly, and continued talking unfazed. There was no mention of the sound, or any other clues offered, as the interview continued.

However, in minutes, the clip was on Twitter.
