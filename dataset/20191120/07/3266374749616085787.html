Transfer News: Dortmund to battle Barcelona for Karim Adeyemi

20 Nov 2019, 07:54 by Abhisek Bajaj

Borussia Dortmund are reportedly looking at Red Bull Salzburg wonderkid Karim Adeyemi, who is also a Barcelona target, as a potential replacement for Jadon Sancho.

According to an exclusive report from transfermarkt.de, Red Bull Sazburg prodigy Karim Adeyemi, who is currently on loan at FC Liefering, is on the radar of Borussia Dortmund. The Bundesliga giants are scouring the youngster as a potential replacement for Jadon Sancho, should he end up leaving in the coming transfer window(s). Adeyemi is also being tailed by Arsenal, Liverpool and Barcelona.

English international Sancho is being heavily linked to a return to the Premier League, with reports stating that Manchester United are set to launch a double swoop for Sancho and Erling Haaland. There are other suitors with a keen interest in Sancho and regardless of where he ends up, Dortmund would need to replace Sancho if and when he leaves.

Karim Adeyemi is in the second year of his two-year loan at FC Liefering. The first season saw him grab a healthy return of 6 goals and 4 assists in 20 league games in the second division in Austria. The current season though, has seen Adeyemi improve on his average, with 5 goals and 4 assists in just 11 league appearances so far.

His form for FC Liefering at the senior grade and in the UEFA Youth League has caught the attention of the European bigwigs, with Barcelona, Liverpool, Arsenal and Dortmund named as potential suitors.
