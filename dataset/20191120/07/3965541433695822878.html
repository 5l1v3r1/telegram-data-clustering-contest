1 person killed in car fire at Federal Way park

20 Nov 2019, 07:57 by KOMO News Staff

FEDERAL WAY, Wash. - One person was found dead after a car erupted into flames Wednesday morning in a Federal Way park, officials said.

Firefighters and medics responded to the scene, inside Celebration Park, at around 7:15 a.m. after receiving reports of a vehicle fire, said Lt. Brad Chaney of South King Fire & Rescue.

The car was engulfed in flames when the first crews arrived. The fire was extinguished and one person was found dead inside the vehicle, Chaney said.

The cause of the fire was not immediately known.

The area is currently blocked off while an investigation is underway.
