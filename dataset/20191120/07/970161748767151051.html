Ant McPartlin sings Kylie Jenner's Rise and Shine to Caitlyn on I'm a Celeb

20 Nov 2019, 07:49

Ant McPartlin is definitely back on form in I’m a Celebrity, and may have just won the internet by singing Kylie Jenner’s Rise and Shine right in front of Caitlyn Jenner.

Hey, it’s an opportunity too good to miss, to be fair!

The Geordie presenter and co-star Declan Donnelly were seen at the end of Tuesday night’s episode heading into the camp to reveal who was doing the latest bushtucker trial.

So seeing as the campmates had just got up…the joke was there for the taking.

Confirming it was deliberate, Ant later responded to a fan who was cracking up at the moment, and had written online: ‘ANT DID NOT JUST SING RISE AND SHINE IN FRONT OF CAITLYN JENNER OMG STOP.’

‘Yes I did!!!!,’ he sent with some cry-laugh emojis. ‘Twitter is LOVING it!’

You’ve got to shoot your shot when you can, we guess!
