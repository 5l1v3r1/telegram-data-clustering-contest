Bigg Boss 13: Sidharth Shukla Fans Say #EvictAsimRiaz After He Blasts Shukla, 'Ladkiyon Par Gussa Nikalta Hai'

20 Nov 2019, 07:45 by Divya Ramnani

With Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz’s friendship going for a toss, their fans too have parted ways. Now, Sidharth’s fans want Asim to be thrown out of the Bigg Boss 13 house!

Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz’s friendship during the first month of Bigg Boss 13 had been the talk of the town. The two always stood beside each other and were hailed as the ‘Jai-Veeru’ of the house by host Salman Khan. However, in the last few episodes, viewers have been constantly coming across their ugly clashes on the pettiest of issues. The most recent fight between Sidharth and Asim got nastier, as the duo was this close to getting into a physical brawl and the housemates had to intervene and separate them.

Asim Riaz and Sidharth Shukla’s enmity has also divided their fans on social media, so much so, that all the Shukla fans demanded Asim’s eviction from the Bigg Boss 13 house. Yes, you read that right! The trend-list on Twitter is currently seeing #EvictAsimRiaz as one of the top hash-tags. As per them, Asim crossed all the limits and was getting violent with the housemates. “Immediate action needs to be taken because this person is harmful and violent for all people be it a friend or an enemy. #EvictAsimRia #StayStrongSidharth,” wrote a user, while another wrote, “So much of arrogance and overconfident #EvictAsimRiaz.” (ALSO READ - Bigg Boss 13: Sidharth Shukla-Asim Riaz Turn Enemies; 'Stay Strong Sid Shukla' And 'We Stand By Asim' Trends)
