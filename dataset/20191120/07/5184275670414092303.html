Sujoy Ghosh Says Kahaani 3 Will Be About Vidya Balan’s Character Searching For Vodafone Network, Parineeti Chopra Can’t Stop Laughing

Even Parineeti Chopra couldn't stop herself from laughing over Sujoy's witty tweet.

20 Nov 2019, 07:10 by Koimoi.com Team

Kahaani director Sujoy Ghosh is one of the most active Bollywood celebs on Twitter. If you love to spend time on the microblogging site, you’ll often see him putting up hilarious tweets and replying in his own funny way to other industry friends.

Recently Sujoy took a hilarious dig on the telecom company Vodafone for the network issue he was facing. Sujoy took to Twitter and tweeted that the 3rd installment of his Kahaani franchise will be about the lead character searching for Vodafone network and it will easily go on in 4th installment too.

His tweet read as, “kahaani3 will be bidya bagchi finding vodaphone network. it might run into kahaani4 also…”
