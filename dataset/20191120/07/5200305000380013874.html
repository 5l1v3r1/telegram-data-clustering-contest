New Rajshri Productions’ show to replace ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ on Star Plus

20 Nov 2019, 07:37 by BizAsia Correspondent

With news that Star Plus is set to wrap up fantasy series ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jin Ka’ in February, its being reported that Rajshri Productions is working on a new show called ‘Dadi Amma Dadi Jaag Jao’, which is expected to take its place.

‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka’ launched in October and has been produced to run for 100 episodes. If all goes well, the series will end in February 2020 by the new show.

‘Dadi Amma Dadi Jaag Jao’ stars Sheen Dass and Anagha Bhosle, along with Seema Biswas, who plays the dadi on the show. Khalid Siddiqui, Shilpa Tulaskar, Mohan Joshi complete the casting, according to IWM Buzz.

The Rajshri Productions show has completed its opening Indore schedule. A source told the publication, “It is very good to be part of the limited series with a possibility of Season 2. It has a very good story and every middle class family will identify with.”

The source added, “At first this show which was in the work for long was supposed to have come on Star Bharat but now apparently has shifted base to Star Plus.”
