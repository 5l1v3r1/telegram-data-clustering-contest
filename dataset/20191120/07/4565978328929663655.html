The workers of the Colombia sugar mil finalize details to be incorporated into the harvest in the next few days, after carrying out the test of the machinery, without cane, which proved that the conditions to produce the planned amount of sugar are created, and -at the same time- rescue its nickname of Las tunas Clock that had identified it in previous contests.

"Colombia", Las Tunas.- At the end of the exercise, the director of the Sugar Company in Las Tunas, exchanged with the workers, to whom he recognized the favorable progress of the enlistment of the factory, the most advanced in the province.

In addition, he called them to make an efficient harvest, since this year the sugarcane growers will be paid for the quality of the raw material delivered, which requires its rigorous analysis, and in the end it will result in the increase of workers' wages.
