'Housefull 4' box office day 26: Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh and Kriti Kharbanda's film continues its impressive run

20 Nov 2019, 07:41 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The fourth instalment of the popular 'Housefull' franchise seems to be going strong at the domestic box office as the film manages to allure the audience to the theatres in the fourth week as well. The Farhad Samji directorial which entered the 200 crore club stars Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol and Pooja Hegde.

Housefull 4 Movie Review : Not so full of comedy

According to the latest report on Boxofficeindia.com, the multi-starrer comic caper has gone on to rake Rs 30 lacs on its fourth Tuesday which has helped the film reach a total of Rs 3.25 crore in its fourth week (five days). With a total of Rs 203.38 crore in four weeks, 'Housefull 4' is the fourth highest-earning film of the year after 'Kabir Singh', 'War', 'Uri: The Surgical Strike'.

The only thing that remains to be seen is if the Akshay starrer can surpass Ajay Devgn's 'Golmaal Again' nett collections which seems to be a tough task with 'Pagalpanti' coming this week.

The film which revolved around the concept of reincarnation left the audience in splits and Akshay is now all set to weave his magic once again with 'Good Newwz' which would release next month.
