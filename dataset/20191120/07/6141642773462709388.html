Golden State Warriors snap seven game losing streak to beat Memphis Grizzlies

Pelicans beat Trail Blazers in New Orleans and Kings beat Suns in Sacramento.

20 Nov 2019, 07:43

Alec Burks scored 29 points as the Golden State Warriors beat the Memphis Grizzlies 114-95 to snap a seven game losing streak.

Glenn Robinson III added 20, while Marquese Chriss and Eric Paschall added 17 each for the Warriors in a game where the result was rarely in doubt.

Memphis reduced the deficit to single digits late in the third quarter, but Golden State were untroubled by their opponents in the fourth.
