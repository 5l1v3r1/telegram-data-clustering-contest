Kamal Haasan visits Indian hockey team in Bhubaneswar

20 Nov 2019, 07:49 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

NEW DELHI: Visiting Odisha capital Bhubaneswar to receive an honorary doctorate for his contribution to Indian cinema, veteran actor and founder of the Makkal Needhi Maiam political party in Tamil Nadu, Kamal Haasan, made a special stop on Tuesday. The veteran actor took time out of his busy schedule to meet the Indian men's hockey team and the Odisha FC team, currently training at the Kalinga Stadium.

Haasan met the players and was also presented with official jerseys of the two teams. Captain of the Indian hockey team, Manpreet Singh, and his senior teammate PR Sreejesh could be seen clicking selfies with Haasan, who also toured the extensive facilities in the Kalinga Stadium complex.

Earlier during his visit, Haasan also met the Chief Minister of Odisha, Naveen Patnaik, to seek his advice on politics.
