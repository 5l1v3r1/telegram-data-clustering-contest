Transfer Rumour: Manchester United, Arsenal interested in Brad Young

20 Nov 2019, 07:14 by Abhisek Bajaj

Manchester United and Arsenal are reportedly interested in and keeping tabs on young goalkeeper Brad Young, who is currently plying his trade for Hartlepool.

As per The Sun, Brad Young is already garnering attention from the English top-flight. Premier League giants Manchester United and Arsenal are interested in the Hartlepool youngster and are keeping a close eye on the goalkeeper’s form and improvement over the games.

Brad Young has earned a England youth call-up after impressing at club level for Hartlepool. Having only signed a professional contract with the National League club, Young stands 191 cm tall and is an imposing presence in the box. Yet to have his senior debut for Hartlepool, the 17-year-old is being chased by the likes of Arsenal and Manchester United.
