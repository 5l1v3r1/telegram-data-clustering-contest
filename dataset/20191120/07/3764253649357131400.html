Halima Abubakar shares throwback video of her face as she addresses bullies

20 Nov 2019, 07:06

Nollywood actress, Halima Abubakar, recently left social media users stunned, after she took to the platform to share a throwback photo of when she suffered from a skin condition that affected her face.

With the luxuries and advantages celebrities have, it appears many people often forget that they are humans and with problems of their own too.

Halima recently shocked her fans and social media users after she took to her Instagram account to share a throwback video of herself, revealing the skin condition she had to battle with on her face.

She wrote: "Good morning beautiful people. I decided to post this video today, without shame or afraid of being ridiculed. I woke up in July last year to this. I don’t need any pity, cos some of you think you can break me. I own my sh*t. I lost friends, lover and was devastated. But this was last year. Only few people knew."
