Middleton woman arrested on suspicion of 8th OWI

20 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Jaymes Langrehr

MIDDLETON, Wis. - A Middleton woman is facing her 8th OWI charge after being stopped by police Tuesday night and admitting to drinking while driving.

The Middleton Police Department says 56-year-old Dawn Jessie was pulled over at 5:32 p.m. Tuesday after they received a traffic complaint. The officer reported a strong smell of alcohol and noticed an open container in the car. Jessie reportedly admitted to drinking alcohol and having open intoxicants in the car.

Jessie was arrested after field sobriety testing and faces a felony charge of 8th offense OWI and tickets for driving with a revoked license and open intoxicants in a motor vehicle.
