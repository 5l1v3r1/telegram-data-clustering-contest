Lauren Goodger's brutal jibe at I'm A Celebrity's Adele Roberts over Jane McDonald photo

TOWIE's Lauren has weighed in on Adele choosing a photo of Jane McDonald as her I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! luxury item

20 Nov 2019, 09:48 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Lauren Goodger has taken a savage swipe at I'm A Celebrity's Adele Roberts over her Jane McDonald photo.

TOWIE's Lauren has weighed in on Adele choosing a photo of Jane McDonald as her I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! luxury item.

Adele, 40, left viewers baffled on Monday night when she received her gift from home — a framed photo of Jane McDonald

Celebrities on the outside world are also now weighing in, with former The Only Way Is Essex star Lauren sparking out.

The former TOWIE star posted on her story to her 763,000 followers: "Who is this Adele?!

"What a weirdo with a pic of Jane McDonald ?! No offence .... but hats off to Kate for the concealer as that is exactly what I'd have as my Luxury item 'make up' I see my fam when I'm out lol  [sic] ".

Emmerdale's Charley Webb, meanwhile, wrote: "Is her luxury item a picture of Jane McDonald? I absolutely love JM but I’m so confused… #imacelebrity."

 

She then wrote: "I’m only half listening so could be wrong but…" before posting a video in which she shakes her head with confusion and says: "My reaction to the fact I heard it absolutely correctly…"

Roman Kemp butted heads with Adele over her decision on Tuesday.

Kemp, 26, the son of Spandau Ballet star Martin Kemp, teased Radio 1 DJ Roberts, 40, about her choice.

The pair clashed after Capital FM DJ Kemp challenged members of the camp in Australia to name one of McDonald's songs before lampooning Roberts's decision as "just random".

Singer McDonald, 56, shot to fame on fly-on-the-wall TV series The Cruise.
