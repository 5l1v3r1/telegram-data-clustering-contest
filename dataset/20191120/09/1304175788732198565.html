From medieval landmarks to museums of international art, London is one of the most diverse cities in the world. London attracts millions of visitors every year. With so much on offer, it can be difficult to decide what you want to visit this time around. Why not check out the most popular attractions and see what all the fuss is about?

Tate Modern 
The Tate Modern was the most visited London attraction in 2018 with six million visitors. This attraction specialises in contemporary and international modern art. It has exhibitions, events, artist rooms, performer arts and collections from all over the world. 

Currently, the Tate Modern is featuring art surrounding queer lives, wellbeing, women and black identities. Take a deep dive into modern art and explore your inner art critic at this attraction! 

British Museum 
Next up, is the British Museum with more than two million years of human history and culture. Catch a free tour, talk or organise a group visit for you and your family. 

London Eye 
The London Eye is an iconic British tourist attraction. Did you know it is the world’s tallest cantilevered observation wheel? You can see the infamous London landmarks while overcoming your fear of heights. 

Make sure to book ahead so you can skip the queues. 

Harry Potter: Platform 9 and 3/4s 
Everyone loves Harry Potter. Whether you’re a lover of the books, movies or both, grab a picture with the Harry Potter trolley in Kings Cross Station. 

Kings Cross is a tourist attraction in itself. You can catch a train from Kings Cross to Kings Lynn to explore even more British history.  

Madame Tussauds
Every tourist secretly hopes to catch a glimpse of the royal family when visiting London. While the Queen does not take personal visits from tourists, there are wax works of the royals in Madame Tussauds.

Take an Instagram shot with politicians, Shakespeare, Lady Gaga and many more! 

Shard
After a long day of sightseeing, order a cocktail at the top of the Shard. You can see St Paul’s Cathedral, Wembley Stadium, the London Eye and much more from this epic attraction. 

Tower of London 
Are you a history geek? Take a tour around London’s prison, place of execution and jewel house. There is over nine hundred years of history here. Let’s get sightseeing!
