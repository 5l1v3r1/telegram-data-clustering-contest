We carried out strikes on Iran's Quds Force, Syrian targets: Israel army

20 Nov 2019, 09:48

Israel's military had said on Tuesday its missile defence system had shot down four rockets fired from Syria.

After Syria's state news agency reported the country's air defences destroyed missiles fired from Israel, the Israeli army confirmed it was aimed at Syrian and Iranian "terror targets" near Damascus on Wednesday morning.

"We just carried out wide-scale strikes of Iranian Quds Force & Syrian Armed Forces targets in Syria in response to the rockets fired at Israel by an Iranian force in Syria last night," the Israel Defense Forces tweeted.
