Vegan sues US fast food chain Burger King for ‘meat byproducts’ in vegan Impossible Burger

20 Nov 2019, 09:27

A vegan has sued US fast food chain Burger King for allegedly misleading customers by cooking vegetable-based patties for their ‘Impossible Whoppers’ on the same grill as their meat products.

The lawsuit was filed by a self-proclaimed vegan in a U.S District Court in Florida and aims to become a class-action.

According to the suit, the man ordered the burger at an Atlanta drive-through and was shocked to find out the plant-based patty was grilled on the same surface as traditional meat burgers - meaning they were potentially contaminated with ‘meat byproducts.’

While Burger King’s ‘Impossible Burgers’ are described as “100% Whopper, 0% Beef”, the website also states “for guests looking for a meat-free option, a non-brioler method of preparation is available on request.”

The news sparked a debate about vegan options in fast food restaurants.
