Vicky Kaushal to go through drastic changes physically for his next

20 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Filmfare

Actor Vicky Kaushal and director Aditya Dhar are collaborating once again post the massive success of Uri:The Surgical Strike for their next film titled The Immortal Ashwatthama. The film is being produced by Ronnie Screwvala who also backed Uri.  Aditya talked about the project in a interview with a leading daily and said that it’s mounted on a big scale and features Vicky in a never-seen-before role.

Aditya revealed that it’s a superhero film that will go on floors in mid-2020. The Immortal Ashwatthama will be shot at Greenland, Tokyo, New Zealand and Namibia after which the VFX of the film will be done in the United States.

Talking about the film, director Aditya Dhar said, "Ashwatthama is one of the most powerful and mysterious characters from the Mahabharata. Being Drona’s son, he is a brilliant warrior but also arrogant and egoistic because he is immortal. In comparison to other warriors like Arjuna and Karna, not much is written about him in various versions of the Mahabharata."

Moving on to Vicky Kaushal's look in the movie, director Aditya Dhar stated, "Like Uri, Vicky has to gain an incredible amount of weight, but this time, he will also have to lose it all during the course of the filming. His look will come as a complete surprise." Well, we can't wait for this hit duo to create magic again, what about you?
