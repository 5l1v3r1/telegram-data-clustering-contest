Cyclone Idai like disaster: Zim govt warns of heavy rains, flooding this week

20 Nov 2019, 09:42

Zimbabwe could experience a disaster worse than Cyclone Idai which claimed at least 300 lives and hundreds more still reported missing, the government has warned.

In a statement issued today, the government said people should stay away from danger due to impending floods, strong winds, hail storm and lightning as heavy rains are predicted in many parts of the country this week .
