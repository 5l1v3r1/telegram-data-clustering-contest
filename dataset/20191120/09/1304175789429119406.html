Marina Fiesta named Resort of the Year in Travel & Hospitality Awards

CABO SAN LUCAS, MEXICO – Mexico Grand Hotels’ Marina Fiesta Resort & Spa has been named Resort of the Year 2019 in Baja California Sur by Travel & Hospitality Awards, which recognize hard work and accomplishments across the travel industry. Travel experts along with a panel of trusted judges, identify the best hotels, tour companies, spas and restaurants throughout each continent and recognize long-standing customer satisfaction.

Mexico Grand Hotels’ nautical-themed resort, Marina Fiesta is conveniently located in the heart of the Marina Golden Zone, walking distance from the destination’s bustling nightlife and restaurant scene. Guestrooms are equipped with kitchenettes, balconies or patios. The resort is a go-to option for families seeking adventure and authenticity in one of Mexico’s premier destinations.

“We’re honored that Marina Fiesta’s signature blend of familial approach and cultural authenticity has resonated with voters and guests,” said Gabriel Ibarra, director of sales & marketing of Mexico Grand Hotels.

Following the recent opening of the group’s Vista Encantada Spa Resort & Residences, this award reflects Mexico Grand Hotels’ longstanding reputation for regional excellence and standout resort features.
