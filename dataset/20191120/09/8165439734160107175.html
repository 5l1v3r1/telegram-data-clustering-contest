WATCH: Netflix Release Trailer For Ian Somerhalder's New Vampire Series V Wars

20 Nov 2019, 09:32 by Tara Walsh

V Wars

Netflix have just released the first full official trailer for V Wars starring Ian Somerhalder.

The upcoming vampire series drops this December 5th and we cannot wait.

It's giving us some serious The Vampire Diaries flash backs!

The trailer sees a young boy woken up by a loud crashing noise in his home.

Presumably his sibling, appears at his bedroom door and asks, "Did you hear that too?"

They walk downstairs with a flashlight only to find a murderous creature feeding on something.

The scene skips to a clip of three men in a garage.

Their attention turns to a broadcaster on the TV reporting a series of vampire attacks in the country.

We're then shown a clip of Ian Somerhalder's character Dr. Luther Swann emerging from his car.

The authorities ask the doctor for help, "We need you doctor. All the victims have been missing a lot of blood."

Ian's character addresses an audience, "This is modified, a virus causing DNA transformations."

Swann soon discovers that his best friend Michael has been turned into one of these murderous predators.

He promises Michael, "Together we will find a cure for this."

However, the authorities warn him to stay away, "The man you knew is gone."

We're later shown a clip of Michael addressing the country in a live broadcast.

He speaks, "My people and I are not terrorists. And have as much right to live as anyone else."

"Horrible as it will be, this war is also necessary. Blood nation will prevail."
