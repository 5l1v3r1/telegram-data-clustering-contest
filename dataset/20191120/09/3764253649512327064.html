Asa Asika praises Davido, calls him the hardest worker in the game

20 Nov 2019, 09:59

- Nigerian singer, Davido Adeleke, has received worthy recognition from perhaps one of his biggest fans

- The singer’s manager, Asa Asika, recently took to social media to celebrate Davido’s work ethic

- According to Asika, Davido is the hardest worker in the game

Son of Nigerian billionaire and talented musician, David Adeleke, popularly called Davido, is no doubt a young man many have described as hardworking.

Despite being from a home where he can decide not to lift a finger to work, Davido has been known to sweat it out in the entertainment industry with other top shots as he continues to remain relevant.

Just recently, Davido received a shout out on social media from perhaps one of his biggest fans, his manager. Asa Asika had posted a photo of the singer on his Instastory as he hailed him.

According to Asika, Davido is one of the hardest workers in the game. See a screenshot of his post below:

Just recently, Davido made the news after he took all his squad members designer shopping in Dubai.

Obviously touched by the gesture, another singer who was also a recipient of the goodwill, Zlatan Ibile, took to his Instagram page to praise Davido.

Zlatan who was clearly overwhelmed with emotions shared a photo of them with their shopping bags at what appeared to be the designer store. He captioned the photo: "BaBa Ifeanyi, you no go kill us @davidoofficial thanks my G".

Earlier on, Legit.ng reported the news of how Davido also gifted Zlatan, a 40K necklace from the ones on his neck. Davido shared the video of the touching moment on his Instagram page as he explained the reason behind his action.
