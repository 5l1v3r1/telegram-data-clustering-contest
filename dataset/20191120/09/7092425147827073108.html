I'm a Celebrity Get Me Out of Here reveals two brand new campmates

20 Nov 2019, 09:35 by Ciara Walsh

The jungle just got a little bigger. 

The I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here camp just got a little bigger.

Two additions have entered the Australian outback, and they're both soap stars.

Coronation Street star Andrew Whyment and EastEnders actor Cliff Parisi have joined the show as latecomers, it was revealed on Tuesday night.

Andy is best known for playing Kirk Sutherland on Coronation Street, and joined the soap in 2000.

Meanwhile, Cliff is best known for his role of Minty Peterson in EastEnders, which he played from 2002 to 2010.

He's also starred in Hollyoaks as Walt, and has most recently been in Call the Midwife as Fred Buckle.

The pair were previously rumoured to be entering the jungle, however neither appeared in the main line-up.

They will be joining current campmates Caitlyn Jenner, Roman Kemp, Nadine Coyle, Ian Wright, Kate Garraway, James Haskell, Myles Stephenson, Jacqueline Jossa, Adele Roberts and Andrew Maxwell.

Although we're only into the first few days in camp, there is already some friction. Roman and Adele clashed in Tuesday's highlight show over a photo of Loose Women presenter Jane McDonald - if you can believe it.

I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here continues tonight from 9pm on Virgin Media One and ITV.
