When is the Tunnock's Caramel Wafer Cup semi-final draw?

Rangers Colts became the final side to reach the last four after defeating Wrexham at Ibrox.

20 Nov 2019, 09:25 by Liam Bryce, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/liam-bryce/

We're edging closer to discovering which team will be the first ever to hold the Tunnock's Caramel Wafer Cup Champions moniker.

The Challenge Cup , as it was originally named, has been around for years but it's the first iteration of its new confectionary-based branding.

Partick Thistle , Raith Rovers, Inverness and Rangers Colts are the final four sides battling it out for silverware after the weekend's fixtures.

The Ibrox youngsters have been the surprise package in this year's tournament, with around 5000 fans turning out to watch Graeme Murty 's side defeat Wrexham on Saturday.

But they'll have to overcome another two vastly experienced sides to lift the trophy.
