Lisbon airport to close during night for construction work

20 Nov 2019, 09:45

National airport managers ANA will be starting improvement works at Lisbon airport at night from January

The work aims to create two fast exit-ways from the runway.

The airport will be closed during the night between 11.30pm and 5.30am January to June for the work to take place.

ANA said the intervention aims to improve operational efficiency, airplane traffic flow, and reduce CO2 emissions.
