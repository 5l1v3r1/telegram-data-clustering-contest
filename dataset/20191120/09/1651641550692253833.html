Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite specs leak

20 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Surur

It has been more or less confirmed that Samsung is working on a slightly cheaper version of the Samsung Galaxy S10, and now we have a lot more detail on the Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite specs.

According to reliable Samsung leaker Ishan Agarwal the Samsung Galaxy S10 Lite (SM-G770F) will feature a Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 processor and the following camera specs: 48MP (Main) + 5MP (Depth) +12MP (Wide angle) & 32MP (Selfie)
