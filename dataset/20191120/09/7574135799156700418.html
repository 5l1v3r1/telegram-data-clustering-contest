KISS 2020 North American tour on presale

20 Nov 2019, 09:32 by BrooklynVegan Staff

Tickets for all 2020 North American dates of KISS‘ continuing End of the Road farewell tour, go on KISS Army fan club presale at 10 AM local time, with a Citi card member presale beginning at noon local. There are also KISS Meet & Greet Experiences on sale now.

Dates include stops in Atlantic City, NJ; Canandaigua, NY; Hartford, CT; Bangor, ME; Mansfield, MA; Allentown, PA; Buffalo, NY; Peoria, IL; Fort Wayne, IN; Los Angeles, CA; El Paso, TX; Atlanta, GA; Raleigh, NC; Tinley Park, IL; Dayton, OH; Milwaukee, WI; Phoenix, AZ; Austin, TX; and many other cities. KISS’ final show, which will be in their hometown of NYC in 2021, has not been revealed. All announced dates are here.

If you miss out on the presales, tickets for the tour go on sale to the general public on Friday (11/22) at 10 AM.
