Bale Insults Real, Nears Exit Door

20 Nov 2019, 09:34 by SL International

Gareth Bale's relationship with Real Madrid has soured even further after the Wales star seemingly insulted the LaLiga club in public while on international duty.

In recent times, Bale's future with Los Blancos has been called into question after the 30-year-old fell out of favour with manager Zinedine Zidane and the club's fans. 

Despite playing a starring role during his time in Madrid, the former Tottenham Hotspur forward has been widely criticized for his frequent golf days and his reluctance to learn Spanish, as well as the amount of time he spends in the injury room.

Bale was linked with a transfer to an unnamed Chinese club during the previous transfer window but the deal collapsed following Real's injury crisis, and the Wales international now appears to be doing all in his power to force a move away. 

Following his nation's 2-0 win over Hungary on Tuesday night, Bale was photographed smiling while holding a Welsh flag with the words "Wales. Golf. Madrid. In that order.".
