How adults can pay kids prices at Walt Disney World and Universal Studios

This could make for some very tempting savings

20 Nov 2019, 09:22 by Julie Delahaye, Victoria Jones

Florida's Walt Disney World and Universal Studios are on many people's bucket lists.

But they are pricey holidays, particularly when you have to pay for a family.

It might interest anyone considering a holiday that adults can now bag tickets to both resorts for the same price as kids' tickets.

That's because Attraction Tickets Direct has brought back its popular 'Adults pay kids prices' deal for theme parks including Walt Disney World and Universal, the Mirror report.

The best part is that there's a wide variety of ticket options included as part of the offer, whether you're after a 14 Day Ultimate Ticket to Disney or a three-park ticket to Universal Orlando.

Oh, and there are even combination tickets to both resorts if you're planning to explore a whole host of the parks.

There's more good news too as the deals are valid for trips in both 2019 and 2020, ideal if you've been thinking of a last minute trip or you're looking to plan ahead for next year.

You can already book the deals on the Attraction Tickets Direct website , but you'll need to do so by midnight on Friday, November 29 (Black Friday itself) when the deal expires.

It's also worth noting that tickets are subject to availability.

It's not the only deal that the website has running on Orlando getaways.

In fact, as part of its Black Friday offerings - which it is actually running all through November - families can bag discounts of five per cent off Walt Disney World hotel stays and park tickets, as well as five per cent off Universal Hotels packages .
