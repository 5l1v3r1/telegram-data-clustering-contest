Masters of the Multiverse Features One of the Most Beloved He-Men

20 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Samuel Gelman

The new series, He-Man and the Masters of the Multiverse, will bring together the various iterations of the beloved hero from the last three decades. Together, they will take on Anti-He-Man, the villainous incarnation of He-Man from Anti-Eternia, after he begins an assault on the multiverse. However, there is one particular He-Man that stands out from the rest.

Speaking to CBR, writer Tim Seeley and artists Dan Fraga and Richard Friend revealed that a beloved He-Man will appear in the very first issue of the series: Dolph Lundgren He-Man.

"You got him in the first issue!" Fraga said. "100%," echoed Seeley."

Fraga continued: "When I was a kid, there was a muscle magazine by Joe Weider [Muscle & Fitness] and they had He-Man on the cover and I remember thinking 'Oh my gosh, it's [Lundgren's Rocky character] Ivan Drago as He-Man!' Like, I didn't call him Dolph. But it was Ivan Drago as He-Man so I needed to see it. Yeah, man, I love that look and the movie. And I'm a big fan of Gwildor, I enjoy drawing Gwildor."

He-Man and the Masters of the Multiverse #1 is written by Tim Seeley and illustrated by Dan Fraga and Richard King. It is on sale now from DC Comics.
