Bigg Boss 13: After The Physical Brawl, Vindu Dara Singh Criticizes Asim Riaz For Provocating Sidharth Shukla

20 Nov 2019, 09:47 by Lubna Khan

Recently, on Bigg Boss 13 we saw Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz getting into a nasty fight which turned violent and other BB housemates had to intervene. Now, Bigg Boss 3 winner Vindu Dara Singh has criticized Asim for using the same provoking tactic as others

Bigg Boss 13 recently saw a huge fight between once-best friends Asim Riaz and Sidharth Shukla. The two, who were once hailed as the ‘Jai-Veeru’ of the BB house by Salman Khan, are now at loggerheads with each other. While they have also had fights earlier, the duo had patched up. Or so it seemed, until now. Recently, during a task, Asim and Sidharth got into an ugly fight with soon turned violent and the two were seen shoving each other. It had escalated to a point that the housemates had to intervene and separate them. While a lot of people have a lot of opinions about this physical brawl, BB 3 winner Vindu Dara Singh took to Twitter to express his.

Vindu Dara Singh took to Twitter and sided with Sidharth Shukla. He said that when Sidharth fought with other contestants over Asim’s issues, Asim and his fans didn’t find him aggressive then. He also criticized Asim for using the same provocative tactics that the others used in the house. His Tweet read, “When Sid was at log-heads with co-contestants over personal & Asim’s issues than Asim & his fans didn’t find him aggressive In the end what did Asim do? He used the same provoking tactics used by the rest! Sid is playing solo inside & so are his fans outside! #StayStrongSidharth.” ALSO READ: Bigg Boss 13: Sidharth Shukla Fans Say #EvictAsimRiaz After He Blasts Shukla, 'Ladkiyon Par Gussa Nikalta Hai'

Not just this, Asim Riaz and Sidharth Shukla’s nasty brawl has also divided their fans on social media platforms. In fact, Shukla’s fans even demanded Asim Riaz’s elimination from Bigg Boss 13.
