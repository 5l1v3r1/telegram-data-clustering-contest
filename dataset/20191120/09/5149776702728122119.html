McDonald's is giving away FREE fries this week - here's how you can claim yours

It's only available in select stores

20 Nov 2019, 09:28 by Molly Holt, Christopher Harper

Who doesn't love free food?

No-one and it's even better when it's McDonald's.

It appears that the restaurant has been on a streak of giving away free food as of late.

In a one-off generous giveaway, McDonald's has decided that on Friday 22 November, medium portions of the brand's famous chips will be completely free via the McDonald's app.

Each customer can claim one pack each, with no purchase necessary, but rather than heading up to the counter or self-serve screens like normal, to get the freebie, you'll need to make sure you place your order via the McDonald's app, reports the Mirror.

The offer is available to all new and existing McDonald's app customers, in selected branches. We've got the full list of all the branches that aren't participating below, which includes certain branches in Croydon, Llandudno, Dublin and a few Asda stores.

As usual, to complete registration, new users must go into the restaurant for their first order – otherwise this offer works for click and collect, through the Drive Thru and from this week, for orders placed and delivered directly to your table thanks to the nationwide introduction of Table Markers.

Table Markers, as the name suggests, means that customers can find a table, order through their phone and have their food delivered straight to where they’re sat.
