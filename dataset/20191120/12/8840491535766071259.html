Outcry over Mayo Christmas tree draws swift response

20 Nov 2019, 12:38

MAYO County Council is to replace a Christmas tree after an outcry over how it looked.

When the tree was erected yesterday at Market Square in Castlebar ahead of the switching-on of the Christmas lights in the town on Friday, locals took to social media to give out about its condition.

They shared the view that it looked bad and was simply not good enough.

"It look like it's from last year," one business person said.

Following representations made to him, local Councillor Ger Deere said he contacted Mayo County County about the concerns.

"I am delighted to be able to report that it will be replaced tomorrow. Thanks to the council for swift action," he added.
