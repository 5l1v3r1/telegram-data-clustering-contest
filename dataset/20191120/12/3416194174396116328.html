Paul Butcher Confirms A 'Zoey 101' Reunion Is Happening This Week!

20 Nov 2019, 12:00

Paul Butcher is putting all those Zoey 101 reunion rumors to rest – and CONFIRMING them!!!

The 25-year-old singer and actor opened up what is going on with the cast.

“I’m not going to say exactly what it’s for, but things are definitely happening,” he told Seventeen.

Jamie Lynn Spears posted a cryptic photo of a script, blocking out all of the words except, “Gimme that bacon!” and the name Sean, which likely refers to Sean Flynn, who played Chase Matthews on the series.

In case you missed it, the cast got rumors swirling when they all reunited over the summer, sans Jamie Lynn, who was filming a new show for Netflix.

While the cast of Zoey 101 is not doing a full on reunion series, we can’t wait to see what they are working on!!!
