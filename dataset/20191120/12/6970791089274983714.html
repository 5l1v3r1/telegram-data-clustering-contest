POWER Imagines Tommy Taking Out Ghost For Good: “Did He Finally Settle The Score”

20 Nov 2019, 12:22 by Biz Jones

The “Power” marketing team is out here playing with people’s emotions. The STARZ series has teased the idea of character Tommy Egan being the mystery gunman in the mid-season finale.

This week, the show shared a promo clip suggesting Egan had reason enough to take out James “Ghost” St. Patrick.

Recently, rap artist Pete Rock flamed “Power” character Tasha St. Patrick with some major trolling.

Over the past few days, “Power” has pitched the idea of everyone from Tasha and other key characters possibly taking out James in the mid-season finale.
