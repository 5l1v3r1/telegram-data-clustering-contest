RRR: Theatre Actress Olivia Morris To Romance Jr.NTR; Irish Actors Ray Stevenson & Alison Doody To Play Antagonists In Period Action Drama

“RRR” will release worldwide on July 30, 2020, in 10 Indian languages.

20 Nov 2019, 12:29 by Koimoi.com Team

Cine goers and Jr.NTR fans have been waiting for this moment from the past number of weeks to know whom their favourite star has been paired opposite in the much talked and much-awaited, RRR. It was yesterday evening when the makers of RRR took on Twitter to give an update that they will be announcing the lead actress in the period action drama today.

The makers this evening took the official twitter handle of RRR to announce the name of the actress. Their tweet read: Welcome aboard #OliviaMorris @OliviaMorris891! We are happy to have you play the female lead #JENNIFER. Looking forward to the shoot. #RRRMovie #RRR.
