Ideas to celebrate Universal Children’s Day

It is a day that is unique and gives you and your child the opportunity to make lifelong memories.

20 Nov 2019, 12:42 by Supplied

The United Nations’ (UN) Universal Children’s Day, that was established in 1954, is celebrated on November 20 each year to promote international togetherness and awareness among children worldwide.

It is a day that is unique and gives you and your child the opportunity to make lifelong memories.

Universal Children’s Day was first announced by the General Assembly of the United Nations in 1954. Originally, there were two goals this day was to help achieve: to encourage children of all races, creeds and religions to spend time together, getting to know each other and appreciating each other’s differences, and to prompt governments worldwide to pay more attention to the welfare of their youngest citizens.

Although Universal Children’s Day takes place on 20 November, each country that participates in the festivities has its own date set aside for this purpose.

Here are some fun ideas to celebrate with your children:

  1. Have a picnic
  2. Have a marathon movie session
  3. Go for a walk
  4. Bake a cake or cupcakes with them
  5. Have a pizza party
  6. Hold a treasure hunt
  7. Or simply go for a long walk together.
