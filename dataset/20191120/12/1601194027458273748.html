Next week's AEW Dark episode to feature AAA Mega Championship match

20 Nov 2019, 12:03 by Shubham Roy

Kenny Omega is set to defend his AAA Mega Championship against Jack Evans on next week's episode of AEW Dark. The announcement was made by AEW on their official Twitter account and it is mentioned that their match will be taped on November 20 in Indianapolis, Indiana after the conclusion of AEW Dynamite.
