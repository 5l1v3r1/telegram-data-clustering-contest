Kamya Panjabi’s Beau Shalabh Slams A Troll For Saying She’ll Sell Her Daughter; Says ‘Have B*LLS For Real Pic’

20 Nov 2019, 12:55 by Gretel Sequeira

Recently, Kamya Panjabi’s beau Shalabh Dang took to his social media to pour out his love for his fiance. Replying to Dang’s post, a social media troll says Kamya is a ‘2 rupees woman’ and she’ll sell her daughter, in anger, Shalabh lashed out the person for passing derogatory remarks.

Bigg Boss ex-contestant Kamya Panjabi is an avid Sidharth Shukla fan and constantly extends her support towards the Bigg Boss 13 contestant. Recently after Asim Riaz and Sidharth’s fight in the house, Kamya pointed out that Asim deliberately started the fight with Sidharth and that he should have not done it. Meanwhile, Kamya’s beau Shalabh Dang took to his social media to share a picture with his ladylove. While Dang shared the picture, Kamya Panjabi received a backlash from a troll. Enraged by the ill-spoken against his ladylove Kamya, Shalabh lashes out at the troll on social media.

 Kamya Panjabi’s beau Shalabh Dang took to his Twitter to post a love soaked picture with his ladylove. The tweet shared by Dang read, “I promise that you would wear this content expression on your face, always @iamkamyapunjabi #love #trust #care,” pouring out his love on the beautiful Kamya. Meanwhile, this particular post shared by Shalabh didn’t go down well for an Anti-Sidharth Shukla troll on social media.
