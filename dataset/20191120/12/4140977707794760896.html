New Zealand vs England 1st Test Live Streaming: The SportsRush present before you the live streaming details of Mount Maunganui Test.

The first Test of the ongoing England’s tour of New Zealand will start from tomorrow in Mount Maunganui. With England winning a tightly-contested T20I series 3-2, the onus is on the hosts to stand tall on their potential now.

New Zealand, who are considered to be a formidable Test side at home, would be eager to beat England given all that has happened between the two teams this year. Having missed the five-match T20I series, New Zealand captain Kane Williamson is back leading the side.

It is worth mentioning that the two-match series is being played outside of the ongoing ICC World Test Championship. Despite that, both teams have stayed away from resting their premier players for the series.

While New Zealand haven’t handed a Test debut to fast bowler Lockie Ferguson, England have included debutant opening batsman Dom Sibley for the first Test.

In the recently concluded County Championship Division One season, the right-hand batsman from Surrey ended the season as its highest run-scorer on the back of amassing 1,324 runs in 13 matches at an average of 69.68 including five centuries and half-centuries each.
