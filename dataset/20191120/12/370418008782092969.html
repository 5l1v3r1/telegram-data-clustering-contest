Hot Snakes Release New Track, Checkmate

Listen to Hot Snakes’ new song Checkmate, their only new music this year

20 Nov 2019, 12:48

California punks Hot Snakes have released brand new single Checkmate.

The track is the first new music since Hot Snakes’ 2018 album Jericho Sirens, and will be available to buy on 7” at the band’s upcoming shows. You can also listen to it below.

Frontman Rick Froberg describes Checkmate as “big, fatty content freshly extruded from the Hot Snakes sausage machine. Same ingredients, new flav.”

What more could you possibly want?
