watch Lana Del Rey & Hamilton Leithauser cover Bob Dylan in Nashville

20 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Andrew Sacher

Lana Del Rey keeps bringing out exciting guests on her Norman Fucking Rockwell tour, and the latest is The Walkmen frontman Hamilton Leithauser, who joined her on stage at Nashville Municipaal Auditorium last night (11/19). Together, they covered Bob Dylan‘s “Don’t Think Twice, It’s Alright” (the same song that Joan Baez sang on stage at Lana’s show in Berkeley last month), and then Hamilton sang “When the Truth Is…” from his 2016 album with Rostam on his own. Watch video of the Dylan cover below.

Lana also welcomed Nikki Lane, Lukas Nelson, and Lissie to the stage in Nashville, which was the final night of her fall US tour. Full setlist below.
