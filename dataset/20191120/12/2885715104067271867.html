Viral video: These ‘aliens’ in a home are not what they seem

The video has been viewed over 12 million times and the 14-second viral clip features two creatures --with large eyes and elongated faces -- who seem to be standing in a corner.

20 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Trends Desk

A viral video features two mysterious creatures has left many spooked on the internet. The video has been viewed over 12 million times and the 14-second viral clip features two creatures –with large eyes and elongated faces — who seem to be standing in a corner of a roof.

However, on further observation, it’s quite clear that the two creatures are not extraterrestrials, but baby owls.

“I’m now positive that people who claim to have seen aliens have actually just seen baby owls,” wrote Twitter user Daniel Holland, who shared the clip.
