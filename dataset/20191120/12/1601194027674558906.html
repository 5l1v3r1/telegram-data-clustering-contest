Charlotte Flair warns the NXT roster ahead of WWE Survivor Series

20 Nov 2019, 12:32 by Arunava Ghoshal

This week's WWE RAW kicked off with a tag team match between The IIconics and Becky Lynch and Charlotte Flair. As soon as The Man and The Queen picked up the win over the former WWE Women's Tag Team Champions, NXT's Shayna Baszler, Marina Shafir and Jessamyn Duke showed up and attempted to take them out. Unfortunately, they couldn't get the job done and had to retreat for the night.

Charlotte Flair's comment on the invasion

Charlotte Flair, who is the captain of the women's team for RAW, was interviewed backstage following the NXT invasion and The Queen sent out a loud and clear message for the NXT Superstars.

So I'm NXT homegrown. I'm from NXT, I was a former NXT Women's Champion. I know what it's like to be at NXT and wanna come to RAW and SmackDown and make a name for yourself and takeover, have that eagerness. But there's one thing they need to know, they will find out quickly, they are just a bunch of little kids playing with a Queen.
