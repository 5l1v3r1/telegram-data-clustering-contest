I’m A Celebrity fans predict Adele Roberts will be first to leave after clash with Roman Kemp

She caused a stir last night

20 Nov 2019, 12:23 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity think Adele Roberts will be the first celebrity to be voted out.

On Tuesday's show, the radio DJ clashed with campmate Roman Kemp when he claimed "no-one knows" who Jane McDonald is after it was revealed her luxury item was a photo of the former Loose Women star.

Adele was one of only two campmates to successfully retrieve their luxury item on Monday's episode but many of the campmates were confused.

The photo was then tied up to a tree in camp.

On last night's show, Kate Garraway said: "Jane McDonald is looking over us, can you think of a song of hers?"

Adele confessed: "I know her off Loose Women, I don’t know her actual songs but I know her from the cruising show when she does covers.

"She’ll cruise around the world, for example she’d go to Iceland and then sing a Bjork or would go to the USA and sing Proud Mary."

Roman and Rak-Su singer Myles Stephenson started to laugh and Adele became upset.

Capital FM DJ Roman said: "It’s just random mate."

Adele hit back: "Why though? I don’t understand. If I had brought in a picture of Ru-Paul would you have the same reaction?

"That’s prejudice, I’m allowed to like what I want. It’s like you’re saying 'why would I like Jane McDonald?'"

At least we know Adele is getting voted off first.

Roman realised Adele was getting upset and apologised.

He told her: "Adele it’s not, the situation is funny. I wasn’t saying that at all, I’m sorry, I didn’t mean to."

Giving her a hug, Roman added: "I like the picture being there."

Viewers were divided over the clash, with many predicting Adele will be the first celebrity to leave the jungle.

One person said on Twitter: "Prejudice? At least we know Adele is getting voted off first."

Another wrote: "Adele to be the first one out. Accusing Roman of being prejudice because he, like the rest of the nation, has no bloody idea why she has a framed photo of Jane Mcdonald?!"

A third tweeted: "Can Adele be the first one voted out please. She's just as annoying as she was when she was on big brother."
