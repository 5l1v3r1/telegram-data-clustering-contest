Half-price food at West Bridgford restaurant and takeaway - for one day only

Make a note of the date

20 Nov 2019, 12:04 by Lynette Pinchess

It's that time of year when cash is tight, what with all that Christmas present buying and festive nights out, so anything where you can save a few quid is not to be sniffed at.

A West Bridgford restaurant and takeaway's six-month anniversary, with the promise of cut-price food, is perfect timing.

Customers can bag themselves a half-price lunch, tea or post-drinks' bargain.

For one day only, Eva's Grill in Radcliffe Road will be serving pitta gyros for just £2.50 and shish wraps for £3.25.

Owner Julian Koraqe said: "We want to celebrate being open six months and say thank you to all our customers."

The deal will be available from 11.30am to 11.30pm, on Monday, December 2.

Since opening, the business has increased its menu. "We are strong in the market with rotisserie products like a whole lamb, offal, chicken and pork.

"We also offer Greek home-made feta cheese pies, spinach pies, ham pies, sausage, cakes and coffee," said Julian.
