Molly-Mae Hague debuts natural look in white bikini in totally unairbrushed pictures

Love Island star Molly-Mae Hague looked incredible as she posed in swimwear at a photoshoot in Barbados

20 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Louise Randell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-randell/

Molly-Mae Hague has embraced a stunning new natural look by posing in a white bikini in totally unairbrushed pictures.

The Love Island star, 20, looked incredible as she modelled a range of swimwear at a photoshoot in Barbados.

Molly-Mae's natural beauty shines through in the snaps and is a dramatic changed from her social media snaps, which usually show her preened to perfection and heavily edited.

In the new snaps, Molly-Mae is seen posing in a number of white bikinis at the ruins of Farley Hill house in Farley Hill National Park.

One picture shows the star on a break, relaxing in a white dressing gown as she clutched her phone.

 

During the trip, the influencer has been busy working and is believed to have been shooting new campaigns for PrettyLittleThing.

During the trip, she was pictured sunbathing topless on a yacht and got viciously abused by fat-shaming trolls.

She was defended by her boyfriend Tommy Fury last night, who shared a video of himself raging against a punch bag.

He captioned it: "Me when I see haters on my girl."

Molly-Mae been the face of PrettyLittleThing since getting out of the Love Island villa, while she's also scored lucrative deals with companies including Beauty Works and Tatti Lashes.

After shooting to fame on the hit ITV dating show, Molly-Mae has swelled her Instagram following to more than 3.5 million.

She's now one of the most in-demand influencers both on Insta and YouTube, where she posts videos promoting her deals and brands.

Despite coming second in the show, losing out on the title to Amber Gill and Greg O'Shea, Molly-Mae has become the 2019 series' most successful star.

Her PrettyLittleThing deal is said to be worth around £500,000 and she's been pulling in plenty of cash from her post-villa deals.
