After Selena Gomez, Jennifer Lopez Too Becomes The Face Of Coach

Now there’s one more good news for Jennifer’s fans as she has become the new face for luxury brand Coach.

20 Nov 2019, 12:28 by Koimoi.com Team

Jennifer Lopez who recently appeared in Hustlers opposite Constance Wu, Cardi B and Lili Reinhart is world-known for her fit personality and style. The film did very well at the box office and Jlo received huge appreciation for her character from the film by not just her fans but critics as well.

Now there’s one more good news for Jennifer’s fans as she has become the new face for luxury brand Coach.

Celebrities like Selena Gomez, Michael B. Jordan, Yara Shahidi have already collaborated with the brand in the past and now Jennifer Lopez is a new addition to the Coach family. Jennifer on Tuesday took to her Instagram to announce the collab and shared, “Thank you @coach! Exciting things coming soon!!!🖤 #Repost Get ready to be swept away. #JLo is the new face of Coach. More to come in 2020 #CoachxJLo #CoachNY 📸: @benjaminrosser”.

Coach acknowledged by commenting on Jlo’s post and wrote, “Welcome to the Coach fam. We can’t wait for 2020. ❤️💥”

Jlo is already associated with makeup brand Inglot and has her own line of collection. Jennifer in an interview said, “I’m so excited for this collaboration with Coach, It is a timeless brand that I’ve always been a fan of and the upcoming collection really speaks to my personal style— an uptown-downtown mix.”

Jennifer recently walked the ramp for her designer friend, Donatella Versace and killed it. Her pictures went viral on the internet in no time.

Seems like this woman isn’t aging at all!

Jennifer along with international pop star Shakira will be opening at Super Bowl 2020 and we are hell lotta excited for her!
