MAD Paris Crafts $110K USD Rolex Daytona Rainbow Sapphire Watch With Ornate Floral Details

Feast your eyes on the head-turning timepiece.

20 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Eric Brain

MAD Paris has released a new iteration of its much-loved Rolex Daytona Rainbow Sapphire watch, this time around featuring a smooth leather strap and an engraved buckle.

It follows on from the extremely-limited Daytona Rainbow Sapphire that released back in April with a metal bracelet. This time around, the customized Rolex adopts a slicker look with its new smooth leather strap, which contrasts the detail-heavy body of the watch itself.

MAD Paris has not only covered the bezel in a sea of rainbow-hued sapphires, but has also engraved the stainless steel body all over with an ornate floral pattern. The case sports embellishments at the top and the bottom of the dial, which then wraps around the rear of the case and is also implemented on a new, heavy-duty buckle. This pattern blends into the Rolex crown emblem, which is raised rather than engraved.

The face is given the archetype black MAD Paris treatment and sports a set of clear jewels around the dial. Black hands, stainless steel face dials and an exhibition back case showcasing the automatic movement with 44 jewels, come together to finish this unique, head-turning timepiece.
