The Top Goalscorers In Euro Qualifiers Revealed

20 Nov 2019, 12:30 by David Kappel

Cristiano Ronaldo has been in superb form during the qualifiers for the 2020 UEFA European Championship, but one player scored even more than the Portugal superstar – here are the top goalscorers in Euro qualifiers.

Click through the gallery above to see the top goalscorers in 2020 Euro qualification.

The 34-year-old netted four goals during the recent international break, taking his overall tally to an impressive 99 goals for Portugal.

Eleven of his 99 strikes came in this year’s Euro qualification, and Ronaldo managed to score them in just eight appearances.

However, England striker Harry Kane netted one more during the current qualification campaign. While Ronaldo failed to score in two of his games, Kane netted in all of his eight appearances.

Serbia’s Aleksandar Mitrovic and Finland’s Teemu Pukki follow with 10 goals respectively.
