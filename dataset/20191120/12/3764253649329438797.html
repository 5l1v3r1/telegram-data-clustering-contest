Unai Emery: Arsenal boss likely faces sack after Mauricio Pochettino

20 Nov 2019, 12:34

- Unai Emery is believed to be the next manager whose position is threatened and could be sacked

- The Spaniard has failed to meet the towering expectations of the Gunners since taking over the London side

- Emery has been reportedly issued with ultimatum by disgruntled Arsenal chiefs

Arsenal manager, Unai Emery, seems to be the next manager whose seat in Premier League is threatened after the disgraceful sack of Mauricio Pochettino's by Tottenham.

The Argentine manager was relieved of his position on Tuesday, November 19, with Jose Mourinho taking over the affairs of the North London side in what signals his surprising yet sensational return to the Premier League.
