Frank and Barbara King have celebrated 70 years of marriage

Family party at Mickleover British Legion

20 Nov 2019, 12:48 by Jane McFarlane

A couple from Mickleover have celebrated their platinum wedding.

Frank and Barbara King were married at St Werburgh's Church in Derby in 1949 when they were both just 22 years old, and enjoyed a big celebration of their 70 year milestone with all their family at Mickleover British Legion.

Frank, who is now 92, was born in Derby and spent his whole career with the British Railway as a mechanic and electrical engineer, while Barbara, also 92, was born in Belper and worked for the National Coal Board as a secretary.

They met at the London School of Dancing in Derby, and have spent nearly all of their married life at Nelson Close in Mickleover - a house they bought as a new-build in 1959.
