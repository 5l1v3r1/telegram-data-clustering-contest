'Ruined my childhood memories', Twitterati is up in arms against the 'Ankiyon Se Goli Maare' song from 'Pati Patni Aur Woh'

20 Nov 2019, 12:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The much-awaited song 'Akhiyon Se Goli Maare' from 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' is out. The reloaded version featuring Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar has apparently not gone down well with the netizens. As soon as the song got released reactions have started pouring in from all over. Although some die-hard fans of the actors have given a thumbs up to the song, others are up in arms against the reprised track.

The song is a remix version of the Govinda and Raveena Tandon's hit track from the 1998 release ' Dulhe Raja'. The song has been crooned by Mika Singh and Tulsi Kumar. While the song has music by Tanishk Bagchi, it has been penned by Shabbir Ahmed.
