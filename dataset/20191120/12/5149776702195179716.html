Man to appear in court following pub burglary in Chilwell

He has been charged with burglary and assault occasioning actual bodily harm

20 Nov 2019, 12:40 by Joseph Locker

A man has been charged following a burglary at a pub in Chilwell which resulted in the landlord getting bitten on the stomach.

Nicholas Jackson, of Kilsby Road in Clifton, will appear at Nottingham Magistrates' Court on Wednesday, November 20.

He has been charged with burglary and assault occasioning actual bodily harm.

It follows an incident at the Chequers Inn, in High Road, Chilwell, where a man was found attacking a fruit machine before being detained by the landlord.
