TWICE Takes a Trip to DisneySea Japan

20 Nov 2019, 12:53 by HelloAnon

TWICE was spotted at DisneySea Japan earlier today having fun and spending time together after participating last night at the 8th ABU International Song Festival to represent South Korea.

The girls have dropped their latest Japanese album "&TWICE" and are set to appear on Asahi Music Station on the 22nd of November making it their 11th time (the most number of invitation that a Kpop girl group has ever achieved) to guest on the popular Japanese show.
