Better Call Saul Delivers Speedy Justice With Season 5 Teaser, Release Date

20 Nov 2019, 12:11 by Tim Adams

Saul Goodman is delivering speedy justice in the first teaser trailer for AMC's Breaking Bad spinoff, Better Call Saul.

The trailer starts with a close-up of Saul Goodman's business card, which reads, "Saul Goodman, Attorney At Law. "Speedy Justice For You!" Beneath that information is Saul's phone number of (505) 842-5662. The camera then spins to reveal a handcuffed gentleman holding the business card, with the short teaser ending with the return date of "2.23."
