Ilana Glazer, Phoebe Robinson, and Michelle Buteau add shows

20 Nov 2019, 12:53 by Bill Pearis

Comedian and former 2 Dope Queens podcaster Phoebe Robinson has spent the fall on her “Sorry, Harriet Tubman” tour, and she’s still got a few dates to go, including San Francisco shows this weekend, and a Philly show in December. She’s also just announced a new Brooklyn show at The Bell House on January 16 which is a campaign benefit for Ohio State Representative Phil Robinson (who is her brother!). Other “special guests” are promised and tickets for that are on sale. All Phoebe dates are listed below.

Phoebe’s pal and fellow podcaster Michelle Buteau has two shows at The Bell House on December 1. The early show is sold out but you can still get tickets to the late show.

Speaking of comedy and The Bell House, The Janelle James Comedy Festival happens there from December 5-7 and there have been a couple additions to the lineup. Ilana Glazer (Broad City), Daniel Simonsen and Kristen Toomey have been added to “Comedians That Don’t Owe Me Money” on 12/15, which also features Hadiyah Robinson, Abi Sanchez and more (tickets). The Lucas Brothers, Casey Balsham, Ethan Simmons-Patterson have been added to “Comedians Who Flewed Out” on 12/7 that also features Ayo Edebiri, Jaboukie Young-White, and hosts Sydnee Washington and Marie Faustin (tickets).

You can also catch Ilana at four shows in Miami this weekend.
