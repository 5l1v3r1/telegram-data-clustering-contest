The War On Drugs announce 2nd annual Philly holiday benefit shows

20 Nov 2019, 12:13 by Andrew Sacher

Philly indie heartland rockers The War On Drugs have announced their second annual “Drug-Cember To Remember” hometown holiday shows, which happen 12/29 & 12/30 and benefit The Fund For The School District of Philadelphia. They write:

Philadelphia fans and friends!
Please join us this holiday season for our SECOND Annual “DRUG-CEMBER TO REMEMBER”- two shows in our beloved city with proceeds benefiting The Fund for the School District of Philadelphia. There’ll be two nights of music around the city, hopefully some big surprises and more ideas are definitely ‘brewing.’ We hope you’ll be a part of it with us. Thanks for your support and STAY TUNED FOR DETAILS!!!

– XO TEAM DRUGS
