Liam Payne Is 100% Down To Do A Song With Harry Styles!

20 Nov 2019, 12:54

While One Direction‘s official reunion might be way off, Liam Payne is very open to reuniting with Harry Styles for a new song.

During his appearance at Hits Radio Live over the weekend, the 26-year-old singer revealed that he and Harry would probably drop the song of the year.

“From the first song off his album, I feel like me and Harry could do a really cool song together,” Liam shared. “There could be a good mix between the two things, because the first song was kind of funky, so I feel like we could break down into some sort of hip-hop R’n'B thing half way though, and have like a little mega mix.”

We area here for this!
