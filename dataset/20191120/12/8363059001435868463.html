20 Nov 2019, 12:20 by Shabana Arif

You've only got until Monday to take advantage of EE's early Christmas offer of a free console or TV.

Your choice isn't just limited to the P30 Pro - there's a bunch of Samsung and Huawei smartphones to choose from - and once you've landed on your final pick, you can nab a Nintendo Switch, or a Toshiba 4K 43-inch TV for free. Eligible handsets include:

  • Huawei P30 Lite from from £34 per month
  • Huawei P30 Pro from £59 per month
  • Huawei P30 Pro 512GB from £69 per month

You'll have to wait out the 14 day cooling off period before you can go about claiming your freebie, and you only have until Monday, November 25 to partake before it disappears. You can keep up with all of the Black Friday shenanigans right here so you won't miss a deal.
