Internet users accuse Tacha of faking the video call with Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey

20 Nov 2019, 12:43

Some internet users have taken to social media to accuse BBNaija reality star, Tacha, of faking the video call to Twitter CEO, Jack Dorsey, which she posted to celebrate him on his birthday.

Earlier on, Legit.ng reported the news of how Tacha left social media users stunned after she shared a recording of the video call she made to Jack, in order to celebrate him on his birthday.

The post went viral on various social media platforms as it not only left her fans excited, it also gave them bragging rights and more reasons to keep supporting her, seeing as she was rolling with the big guns.
