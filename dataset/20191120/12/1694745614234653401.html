When Sofia Richie emerged from the Nine Zero One hair salon in the LA neighborhood of West Hollywood, paparazzi stopped to take her picture, which should come as no surprise. But in this outfit, my first thought was, admittedly, "watch out Victoria Beckham." I hate to say it, but it's true. I mean, if Victoria is the queen of the LBD, then consider Sofia her protégé. Sofia shows us exactly how the young woman is going to be wearing her minidress in 2020: with a sleek, square-toe bootie (Sofia's are Alexander Wang), dark visor sunglasses, hoop earrings, and — but of course! — Bottega Veneta's The Pouch, which Sofia owns in the neutral Cammello shade.

The "new Bottega" is fast becoming the ultimate fashion trend, and Sofia's full look provides the ultimate way to put your luxe, oversize bag on full display. Sofia topped off her ensemble with a dainty diamond chain and chunky rings, looking every bit the glamorous babe we know she is. This outfit is easy, effortless, and, considering Sofia's spaghetti-strap tank dress can be picked up anywhere, it provides the perfect opportunity to mix high and low. Keep reading to check out a few different angles of Sofia's street style moment, then shop the essentials.
