Chris Evans stuns Holly and Phil by pulling his trousers down on This Morning

Radio host Chris, 38, appeared on This Morning today and opened up about a medical procedure he'd undergone to heal a leg injury

20 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Irishmirror.ie

Chris Evans left Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield stunned today after pulling his trousers down live on This Morning today.

The radio host, 38, appeared on the ITV daytime show today and opened up about a medical procedure he'd undergone to heal a leg injury, which is putting his usual fitness routine on hold.

But when hosts Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield probed him about his recovery, he offered to show them his wound.

However both the presenters and viewers were left stunned when he stood up to unbuckle his trousers.

Phil joked: "What set of pants do you have on? Do you have teeny pants?"

The car enthusiast insisted that he didn't but told Phil to "fess up" to owning his own pair of "teeny pants".

Chris quipped: "Now we know what to get each other for Christmas!"

He then sat back down on the sofa in his boxers and showed off his bruised leg, saying that he had his "varicose veins microwaved".

However Chris, Holly and Phil laughed about how bizarre he must look with his trousers down during the interview.

Phil laughed and clarified the situation to those "tuning in now".

*This Morning airs weekdays at 12.30pm on ITV
