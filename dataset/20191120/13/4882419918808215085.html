Gareth Bale mocks Real Madrid with Welsh flag

Wales, golf, Madrid, in that order, read the flag that he stood grinning behind like a Cheshire cat

20 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Sport EN

Gareth Bale has mocked Real Madrid in public. After helping Wales reach Euro 2020 with a 2-0 win over Hungary, he posed in a photo which has sent Madridismo into a rage.

The Welshman stood grinning with his team-mates behind a banner of the Welsh flag with the words "Wales, golf, Madrid, in that order", something that the fans sang through the game too.

Bale played 88 minutes after not playing in any recent Madrid matches with an injury, and his professionalism and dedication to the club has been called into question.

Golf has been his favourite past-time and always caused trouble at Madrid, because it seems sometimes he prioritises the sport over football.
