The latest Newcastle United Premier League odds after the November international break

Premier League football returns this weekend after another international break

20 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Josh Challies

Newcastle United are still 11/4 to be relegated from the Premier League - despite their recent upturn in results.

Wins over Manchester United, West Ham and Bournemouth have left the Magpies in 13th place heading into the return of top-flight football this weekend.

In fact Steve Bruce's side are just two points off second place in a congested league table, but the bookies still don't have high hopes for the Magpies.

They don't, however, think they will go down, with four sides at shorter odds for the drop.

While there is tonnes more football to play and plenty more twists on the cards, the latest odds from The Pools have provided an insight into how the battles in the Premier League are expected to finish.

Unsurprisingly, it is Liverpool who are favourites for the title at 2/5 ahead of Manchester City at 9/4, while the top-four race favours Leicester City at 1/2 and Chelsea at 1/5.

At the bottom, Norwich City are 1/5 to suffer relegation and Watford are evens, with Southampton at 6/4 the favourites to join them in the second-tier next season.
