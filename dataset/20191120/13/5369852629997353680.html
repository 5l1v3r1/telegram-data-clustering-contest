Bale's 'Wales. Golf. Madrid. In that order' flag causes latest problem for Zidane

The Real Madrid star appeared to mock his employers after qualifying for Euro 2020 last night.

20 Nov 2019, 13:50 by AFP

ZINEDINE ZIDANE FACES another crossroads in his handling of Gareth Bale, who appeared to mock Real Madrid after Wales’ 2-0 victory over Hungary last night. 

After sealing qualification for Euro 2020 with a wild win in Cardiff, an ecstatic Bale celebrated next to his team-mates behind a flag that read “Wales. Golf. Madrid. In that order”.
