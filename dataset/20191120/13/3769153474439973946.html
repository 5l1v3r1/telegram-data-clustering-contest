MassFX v3 Update! [$]

20 Nov 2019, 13:50

Alberto Gonzalez writes:

MassFX for Blender 2.8 is finally here.

Since the first release for Blender 2.79, everything has been re-worked completely. The add-on now operates as a brush where you can switch between two modes: Drop & Paint:
Storing your items has never been this easy. You now have a collection manager. Where you can store a group of objects & switch between the collection you want to drop on the fly.

Many new features have been included in this release. Here are the list of features:

● New Physics Painter
● Particle based brush
● Simplified, User-friendly UI
● Options at hand, both brush & physics settings

You can find more information about the tool on Blender Market. I have also put together a video explaining the recent changes made to the add-on.

Happy Blending & Thanks for your support!
