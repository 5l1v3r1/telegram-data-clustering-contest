Grammys nominations: Lizzo, Billie Eilish and Taylor Swift lead nods

20 Nov 2019, 13:45

Billie Eilish, Lizzo, Taylor Swift are among the stars who are leading the Grammy nominations ahead of the anticipated 2020 awards ceremony.

Alicia Keys helped do the honours of announcing the list of nominations on Wednesday, as she’s set to host the awards for the second year in a row at the Staples Center in Los Angeles on 27 January.

Gayle King, Bebe Rexha were also on-hand.

Billie Eilish, Ariana Grande and Lizzo are among the acts competing in the album of the year category, while Lewis Capaldi has received a nod for song of the year with Someone You Loved.

It was confirmed earlier this month that Alicia, 38, would reprise her hosting duties for the second year running after earning praise for her efforts earlier this year.

Reacting to the news, Alicia said in a statement: ‘At first I did think last year was a one-time thing but when the opportunity came back around there was no question about returning as host of the GRAMMY Awards.

‘Last year was such a powerful experience for me. Not only did I feel the love in the room, but I felt it from around the world and it confirmed the healing and unifying power of music.’

Grammys president and CEO Deborah Dugan added: ‘From best new artist recipient to Grammy Award host, Alicia Keys has been a beacon of artistry within the music community for more than two decades.

‘Last year was a testament to her compelling creativity and we are thrilled that she will return to the Grammy stage as our host. Anything can happen, you don’t want to miss it.’

The Grammys 2020 will be taking place at the Staples Center in Los Angeles on 27 January.
