Here are the secret Netflix codes that unlock the full list of Christmas movies

20 Nov 2019, 13:41 by By Anna O'Donoghue

Nothing says Christmas like cosying up on the couch with a festive flick although finding something for the whole family can turn into the nightmare before.

Thankfully, Netflix has a hidden way that can help you find the right Christmas movie for everyone with a little bit of ease.

Introducing Netflix codes …

The list itself opens you up to a world of yuletide sub-genres you never knew exsited - making those ‘what to watch on’ family discussions a whole lot easier.

To access, just log in to your Netflix account and enter the url: http://www.netflix.com/browse/genre/

After that, simply add the number code below to the end of that URL to access each category.
