Japan: Latest Famitsu review scores

20 Nov 2019, 13:01 by My Nintendo News Admin

The latest edition of Japanese gaming publication Famitsu has arrived with subscribers and with it comes a number of reviews. While this issue lacks the big hitters of recent weeks, there’s a few niche titles that may be of interest to some of you. The highest scoring title this week is 13 Sentinels: Aegis Rim for the PlayStation 4 which received an outstanding 38/40. Here’s all the review contained within the latest edition of Weekly Famitsu:

  • 13 Sentinels: Aegis Rim (PS4) – 9/9/10/10 [38/40]
  • A Plague Tale: Innocence (PS4) – 9/8/8/7 [32/40]
  • Guns of Mercy (Switch) – 8/8/7/8 [31/40]
  • Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon? Infinite Combate (PS4, Switch, PS Vita) – 6/6/6/6 [24/40]
  • SD Gundam G Generation Cross Rays (PS4, Switch) – 8/8/8/7 [31/40]
  • Tokyo Dark: Remembrance (Switch) – 8/7/8/8 [31/40]
  • Your Toy (PS4, Switch) – 6/6/6/6 [24/40]
