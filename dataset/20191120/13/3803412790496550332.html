What even is a Max Raid Battle?

I have my friends. How do we battle?

  1. First, you and each of your friends need to go into the Wild Area.
  2. Next, open up your Y-Comm Menu by pressing Y.
  3. Within the Y-Comm Menu, verify that you're connected to Local Communication. If not, you will have a prompt to connect to Local Communication. Source: iMore/ Casian Holly
  4. Return to the Wild Area overmap.
  5. One of you, whichever will "host" the Battle, needs to find a Max Raid Battle Den. These are the Dens that aren't just glowing, but have a beacon of light shining out the top and into the sky. Source: iMore/Casian Holly
  6. The host presses X on the Max Raid Battle Den and follow through the text prompts.
  7. A new menu will open and the host will select Invite Others. Source: iMore/ Casian Holly
  8. The other players can now open their Y-Comm Menus again by pressing Y.
  9. The other players can now press X to load new stamps.
  10. The Max Raid Battle Stamp should now be visible on the other players' screens. They can now click on the stamp, and select Join in. Source: iMore/ Casian Holly
  11. One by one, the other players will show up on the roster. Once everyone has joined, the host selects Ready to Battle. Source: iMore/ Casian Holly
  12. Kick that Dynamaxed Pok√©mon's butt and good luck catching!

And it's just that easy. Now you and all your local friends can join in for some co-op fun!
