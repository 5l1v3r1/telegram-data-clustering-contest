Man prints AirPod stickers and puts them across San Francisco to prank people

As the prank tweet went viral, people demanded to see if citizens really fell for it, and he furnished proof that it worked, leaving people in splits. He also provided the sticker template for all, in case someone else too wants to prank others.

20 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Trends Desk

Digital artist Pablo Rochat decided to have some fun with the fear that all Apple AirPod owners have of losing the device. He printed life-size AirPod stickers and stuck them across the city, documenting the process on video.

Rochat, who also owns AirPods, said he fears losing the wireless headphones since he drops them often.

“I often see people drop their AirPods, so I thought it would be funny to make it look like people are dropping their AirPods all over the city and see if people would try to pick them up,” he told Mashable.
