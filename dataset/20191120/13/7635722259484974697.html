Former Chelsea prospect Walter Figueira joins Derry City

20 Nov 2019, 13:57 by Will O'Callaghan

Derry City have signed English striker Walter Figueira.

The former Chelsea youth player makes the move to the Brandywell after impressing with Waterford in the second half of last season.

Figueira scored five goals before his spell at the RSC and Derry boss Declan Devine says they've secured a "quality player".

“Derry fans who saw him play for Waterford against us in the Brandywell will know exactly the type of player we’ve brought here.

“He wants to play every minute of every match and that’s what we need at this club. He had a host of offers from clubs in Ireland and Scotland so we are delighted to have secured his signature.”
