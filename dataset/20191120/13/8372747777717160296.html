FGCU to break ground on new Water School building Thursday

20 Nov 2019, 13:53 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Florida Gulf Coast University will break ground on the new building for their Water School on Thursday

The Water School started in March.

Academic Building Number Nine will be the Water School's own facility. It’s actually the largest building to date on the FGCU campus.

The Water School focuses on the environmental sciences, and draws in the social sciences, health sciences, business, and engineering.

The groundbreaking will be at 10 a.m. Thursday.
