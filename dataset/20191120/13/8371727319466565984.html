Android 10-Based ColorOS 7 Is Now Official: Here’s the List of Devices Getting It

20 Nov 2019, 13:14 by George Kamau

OPPO has finally launched the next version of its user interface, ColorOS 7 that’s based on Android 10, following Huawei’s EMUI 10 and Xiaomis’s MIUI 11. This is the latest Android user interface from the Chinese based company. ColorOS 7 brings with it a completely redesigned interface and being Android 10 based, expect to see a lot of changes influenced by Google’s operating system.

Features of ColorOS 7

For the homescreen, OPPO went ahead and used a more comfortable visual design that will let users change the shape and size of its icons and also offered new Art+ wallpapers to spice up your home screen.
