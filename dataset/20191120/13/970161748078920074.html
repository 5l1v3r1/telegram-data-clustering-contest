Kate Beckinsale is very proud of her hip bones at home gym launch

20 Nov 2019, 13:35

Kate Beckinsale was keen to show off her hip bones as she celebrated MIRROR’s home gym launch.

The 46-year-old attended the grand opening of the California store, which describes itself as ‘the world’s first nearly invisible, interactive home gym.’

And while posing for pictures in a mustard coloured suit, the actress was spotted pulling down her trousers and exposing her abs.

Honestly girl, you should have opted for low waisted jeans at this rate.

Taking to Instagram, the Underworld star celebrated the lavish event by sharing a string of photos from the night where she was joined by 90210 star Sara Foster.

She wrote to her 3.5million followers: ‘Beautiful,clever,funny girls @sarafoster and @erinfoster are bringing us @getthemirror at this chic little store @westfieldcenturycity. It’s an interactive home gym inside a mirror.
