Kodak Black Plans To Enter Prison Rehab Program To Cut Some Time Off The Sentence

20 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Alexis Stone

Kodak Black just asked for approval from a judge to enter a drug rehab program while he is serving his Florida prison sentence. The Shade Room has more details on the matter, and you can read them below.

TSR cites the info from TMZ and says that ‘Kodak is asking the court to send him to a prison that offers the Residential Drug Abuse Program, @tmz_tv reports.’

TSR continues and notes that ‘The 500-hour program, which lasts nine months and would involve group therapy, could possibly shorten Kodak’s 46-month sentence.’
