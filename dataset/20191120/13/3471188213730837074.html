Jabra Elite 75t wireless earbuds are now available

20 Nov 2019, 13:36 by Duncan Jaffrey

They’re here, after what felt like an aeon of waiting following their launch at IFA this year Jabra’s latest fully wireless earbuds have been released, say hello to the Jabra Elite 75t wireless earbuds. While there’s some dissent amongst the Ausdroid writers as to what the ‘best’ wireless earbuds are, there’s no doubt that Jabra’s top offering is always in contention.

The Elite 75t are a fairly significant upgrade on the previous Elite 65t model featuring a much smaller design meaning better fit for more people, 50% increase in battery life, up to 28 hours from 15 hours, fast charging over USB C and a much smaller carrying case. The Elite 75t will include Bluetooth 5.0 with up to 8 devices paired and 2 simultaneous connections.

The Jabra Elite 75t are now on sale in Australia for $299 AUD in Titanium Black. You can pick them up today from Jabra Online, JB HiFi, Harvey Norman and Amazon.
