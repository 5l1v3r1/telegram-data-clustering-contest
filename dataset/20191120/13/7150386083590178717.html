President Gotabaya Rajapaksa Names Brother Mahinda as Prime Minister of Sri Lanka

The development comes after incumbent PM Ranil Wickremesinghe announced his resignation from the post following the defeat in the recently concluded election.

20 Nov 2019, 13:15

New Delhi: Two days after taking oath as the 7th President of the country, Sri Lankan President Gotabaya Rajapaksa on Wednesday named his brother Mahinda Rajapaksa as Prime Minister of the island nation, local media reported. The development comes after incumbent PM Ranil Wickremesinghe announced his resignation from the post following the defeat in the election.
