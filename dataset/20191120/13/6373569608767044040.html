Hit and run BMW driver in crash involving children is banned from road

Jonathan Southern was described as an idiot before accident at busy Plymouth junction

20 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Stuart Abel

A hit and run BMW driver has been banned from the road after a crash with a car containing young children at a busy Plymouth junction.

Jonathan Southern, aged 37, was branded an idiot by police for undertaking, overtaking, tailgating and making obscene gestures.

He drove away after the accident with a Vauxhall Zafira at the junction of Outland Road and Western Park Road.

Another officer said that it looked like the BMW jumped a red light just after 4pm last month.

The Force Incident Manager added that two women and two children were “shaken” after the accident. He added the youngest child was just two or three years old.

Three ambulances attended and a fire crew made the scene safe.

Witnesses got the BMW’s registration Southern was arrested within hours but refused to take a breathalyser test.
