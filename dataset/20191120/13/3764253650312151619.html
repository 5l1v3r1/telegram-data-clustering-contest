Joseph Akpala, ex-Super Eagles star, re-joins KV Oostende until end of the season

20 Nov 2019, 13:06

- Joseph Akpala has re-joined Belgian top league side KV Oostende on a short term deal

- The 33-year-old could bag one extra year if he impresses on his second stint at the club

- Joseph spent last season with Saudi Arabian top side Al-Faisaly

Former Super Eagles star Joseph Akpala has returned to struggling Belgian League club KV Oostende until the end of the season.

The 33-year-old spent the 2018-19 league season in Saudi Arabia topflight side Al-Faisaly where he scored 12 goals in 62 meetings.

He has however been re-signed by the Versluys Arena outfit to ensure they turn their struggles in the league around.
