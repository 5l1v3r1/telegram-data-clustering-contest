Rajasthan Food Safety Officer admit cards 2019 released, check steps to download hall ticket

Rajasthan Food Safety Officer admit cards 2019: Rajasthan Public Service Commission will conduct the Food Safety Officer examination on November 25, 2019 in the Ajmer office from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

20 Nov 2019, 06:55 by Education Desk

Rajasthan Food Safety Officer admit cards 2019: Rajasthan Public Service Commission has released the Rajasthan Food Safety Officer admit card 2019 for the recruitment examination. The candidates who will appear for the examination can download the admit cards from the official website-rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in using their application number and date of birth.RPSC will conduct the Food Safety Officer examination on November 25 in the Ajmer office from 9 am to 12 pm for the TSP and Non TSP posts. The total number of vacant posts for the recruitment examination are 30 out of which 26 are for Non-TSP and 4 are for TSP areas.

Rajasthan Food Safety Officer admit card 2019: Steps to download

Step 1: Visit the official website-rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in

Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘Admit card link for Food Safety Officer 2019’

Step 3: On the page that opens, click on the link that says, ‘Get admit card’

Step 4: On the next page, enter the details asked like their application number and date of birth and click on submit.

Step 5: The admit cards will be displayed on the screen. Download the admit cards and keep them safe for future reference.

The candidates must carry the admit cards along with them to get entry in the examination hall. The applications for the above recruitment examination was accepted from August 9 to September 8, 2019 and the exam will be conducted on November 25, 2019.
