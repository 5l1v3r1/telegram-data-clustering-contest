Brexit Party to hold two Black Country meetings this week

The Brexit Party is holding two public meetings in the Black Country to hear people's views ahead of the general election.

20 Nov 2019, 06:30

Wolverhampton candidates Raj Chaggar and Vishal Khatri will chair a meeting at the Old Chainyard pub, Castle Street, Coseley, tomorrow from 7.30pm.

And on Friday Walsall South candidate Gary Hughes is holding a meeting at Lyndon House Hotel, Upper Rushall Street, at 7.30pm.

Both events are free to attend.

Mr Hughes, said: "We are holding these public meetings because we passionately believe in listening to the people.

"I urge people to come along to our meetings and have a say local and national issues."

The party had been due to hold two major rallies in the Black Country last Friday.

Nigel Farage scrapped one in Dudley after his candidate Rupert Lowe pulled out, then cancelled his appearance at a later event in Willenhall at short notice.
