Koobee to intro phone with 108-megapixel camera in 2Q20

20 Nov 2019, 06:11 by Max Wang, Taipei; Steve Shen, DIGITIMES

Following in the step of Xiaomi, which unveiled recently its high-end CC9 Pro handset featuring a 108-megapixel camera, Koobee Communication Equipment has also unveiled a comparable model, F3R, with plans to begin marketing the product in the second quarter of 2020.

In addition to featuring a 108-megapixel main camera, the F3R will also come with a 6.3-inch display, MediaTek Helio G90T processor, supporting ArcSoft image solutions and 34x zoom, according to company sources.

Koobee, a subsidiary of China-based Coosea Group, has been selling its smartphones under Koobee brand in the domestic market and mostly under Coosea brand in over 30 markets outside China. It currently ships about 6-8 million handsets a year.

However, Koobe has shifted its focus to overseas markets recently from the saturating smartphone market in China, said the sources, adding that the proportion of overseas sales will see a significant growth in 2019.
