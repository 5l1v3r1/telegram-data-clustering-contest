Didi is stable: Madhur Bhandarkar after meeting Lata Mangeshkar in hospital

Director Madhur Bhandarkar on Tuesday visited veteran singer Lata Mangeshkar and informed that her health is getting better.

20 Nov 2019, 06:44 by ANI

New Delhi: Director Madhur Bhandarkar on Tuesday visited veteran singer Lata Mangeshkar and informed that her health is getting better.

The ' Padma Shri,' director shared a picture of the playback singer in an Instagram post that read, "Visited the Hospital to see @lata_mangeshkar didi glad to inform that she is stable and responding positively to the treatment. Thanks everyone for countless blessings & prayers for her speedy recovery #latamangeshkar."
