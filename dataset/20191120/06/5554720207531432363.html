Fans left speechless by rare photo of Kareena Kapoor Khan in Amitabh Bachchan’s arms, just like Taimur is held by Saif Ali Khan

20 Nov 2019, 06:40 by JKR Staff

Kareena Kapoor Khan may have acted opposite Amitabh Bachchan in several films including Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham, but little did her fans know that the Bollywood megastar once held her like Saif Ali Khan carries son Taimur. This was revealed after a fan asked Bachchan about the identity of a little baby being carried in his black and white photo.

A fan tagged Bachchan on Twitter and asked, “Who are you holding @SrBachchan Ji?” She also pointed out to the presence of Kareena’s sister Karisma Kapoor and his daughter Shweta Bachchan Nanda in the photo. Pat came the reply from the Bollywood megastar, “bebo .. Kareena Kapoor Khan.”
