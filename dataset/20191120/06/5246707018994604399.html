Rumour: Crash Bandicoot Might Be Returning In A Completely "New" Game

Comeback king

20 Nov 2019, 06:30 by Liam Doolan

At the start of this year, Activision revealed the Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy had shifted more than 10 million units worldwide. Crash Team Racing Nitro-Fueled followed this and managed to finish in first place on multiple occasions.

The success of these classic games led to Activision's CEO Robert "Bobby" Kotick stating how there were "a lot of opportunities" to grow existing IPs such as Crash and Spyro, and even consider "new content" after the remasters. It should come as no surprise then to hear that there's potentially another Crash Bandicoot game in the works.
