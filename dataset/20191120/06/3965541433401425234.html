Dirt truck crashes down I-5 embankment in Lynnwood

20 Nov 2019, 06:02 by KOMO News Staff

LYNNWOOD, Wash. -- A semi hauling dirt crashed off the northbound lanes of I-5 early Wednesday morning.

Troopers were called to the crash just before 1:30 a.m. near 164th Street SW, according to officials. They arrived to find the large semi had veered off the freeway and was down a short embankment off the shoulder.

The driver of the semi suffered minor injuries and some diesel fuel spilled at the scene, prompting a response from the Department of Ecology.

Troopers are still looking into what caused the crash, but it appears the truck was the only vehicle involved.
