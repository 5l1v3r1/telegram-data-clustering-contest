Qualcomm expects fast 5G adoption

20 Nov 2019, 06:30 by David Manners

Yesterday, at its analyst day, Qualcomm said there will be between 175 million and 225 million 5G smartphones in 2020,  more than 450 million in 2021 and over 750 million in 2022..

5G adoption will be faster than 4G, says Qualcomm, because of demand from China.

Qualcomm is expected to have an integrated 5G modem in 2020.

Qualcomm sees its growth coming not only from wireless chips but from 5G-associated markets like automotive, computing and IoT.

The Serviceable Available Market (SAM) which the company is addressing is worth $65 billion in 2019, and will be worth $100 billion in 2022, said Qualcomm.
