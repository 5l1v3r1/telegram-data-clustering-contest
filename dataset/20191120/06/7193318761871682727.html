OKLAHOMA CITY

Santa Market comes to fairgrounds Saturday

The annual Santa Market to benefit the Oklahoma Alzheimer's Association will be Saturday, from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. in the Pavilion building at the OKC Fairgrounds.

About 196 vendors will be selling items that include handmade clothing, toys, jewelry, home décor, holiday and food items. Grab coffee and other beverages and food will be available to patrons while they shop. Children may get a free photo with Santa and the first 1,000 shoppers receive a free shopping bag filled with goodies.

In 2010, Joan Clarke, her daughter Megan Nye, and Megan’s mother-in-law, Molly Nye, founded Santa Market after Clark lost her husband to early onset Alzheimer's.

For more information, call or send a text to 405-823-4993.
