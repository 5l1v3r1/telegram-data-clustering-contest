Bofowaa Ciara flaunts her beautiful sister as she celebrates her birthday

20 Nov 2019, 06:44 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

The wife of one of Ghana’s rich men of God, Rev. Obofour, Ciara Antwi popularly known as Bofowaa Ciara has taken to social media to show off her beautiful sister as she celebrates her birthday.

Sharing a photo, Obofour’s wife hailed her big sister as a beautiful woman and wished her joy and many surprises.

“Mirror mirror on the wall Who’s the most beautiful of them all? It’s my sister. Didn’t you know She’s the best, I don’t need to show. The most important thing that I want to say is that today is her birthday I wish that the path of happiness she treads brings her joy and surprises in the year ahead. Wishing you a Happy Birthday dearest sis blood God bless you more ,” Ciara stated.
