Meet Apple Developer app, the new primary destination for Apple developers and designers

20 Nov 2019, 06:30 by Pradeep

Apple yesterday released the new Apple Developer app, the new primary destination for Apple developers and designers. Apple is updating the existing WWDC app as Apple Developer app. This app will allow you to stay up to date with the latest developer news, informative videos, WWDC content, and more. In the US, developers can also use this app app to enroll in the Apple Developer Program on iPhone or iPad. Apple will bring this support to more countries and regions in the future.

  • Browse news, developer stories, and informative videos.
  • Catch up on videos from past events and download them to watch offline.
  • During WWDC, access the conference schedule, sign up for labs, and watch session live streams.

For iOS 13 users, this app supports Dark Mode as well.

You can download the app here from Apple App Store.
