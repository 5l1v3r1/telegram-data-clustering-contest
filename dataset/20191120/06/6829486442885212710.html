Pro-Black Lives Matter Official Forced To Stand Down From The Kumanjayi Walker Inquiry

20 Nov 2019, 06:19 by Rachael Conaghan

The Northern Territory’s anti-corruption commissioner will no longer investigate the police shooting of Indigenous teenager Kumanjayi Walker after being criticised for publicly supporting the Black Lives Matter movement.

Apparently, believing that people of colour are valuable members of society, and being able to impartially investigate a police shooting are mutually exclusive.
