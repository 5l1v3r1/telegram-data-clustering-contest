Alibaba to exercise full retail tranche in Hong Kong listing: sources

3 sources told Reuters the Chinese e-commerce giant would raise up to $12.9 bn in the listing.

20 Nov 2019, 06:12 by Reuters

HONG KONG: Alibaba has exercised its full retail tranche, from 2.5 per cent to 10 per cent, in its Hong Kong secondary listing, thanks to oversubscription, three sources with direct knowledge of the matter told Reuters.

Earlier, three sources told Reuters the Chinese e-commerce giant would raise up to $12.9 billion in the listing.
