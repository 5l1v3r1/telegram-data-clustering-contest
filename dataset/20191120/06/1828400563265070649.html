Competition: Win tickets for Blackpool v Scunthorpe United in the Leasing.com Trophy

Blackpool’s second-round tie in the Leasing.com Trophy at home to Scunthorpe United has been fixed for next Wednesday, November 27, and we have 20 tickets to give away for the 7.45pm kick-off.

20 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Andy Moore

The Gazette has teamed up with Leasing.com, official title sponsor of the Leasing.com Trophy, to offer five lucky fans the chance to win four tickets to Blackpool v Scunthorpe.

Blackpool topped their qualifying group and will welcome the Iron to Bloomfield Road in the round of 32 as Simon Grayson’s side look to take a step closer to Wembley.

For your chance to win, simply answer the following question:

Blackpool finished top of their group in the Leasing.com Trophy but which other team qualified from that group as runners-up?

Email your answer to tango@blackpoolgazette.co.uk

Please write ‘Blackpool FC competition’ in the email subject field and include your name, full address and a daytime telephone number.

Entries must reach us by noon this Friday, November 22.

This competition is for Blackpool supporters only as the prize tickets are in a home section of the stadium. For full terms and conditions see below
