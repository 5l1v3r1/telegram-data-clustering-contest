Post Malone's Hooters bash - Entertainment News

20 Nov 2019, 06:56 by WENN

Rapper Post Malone celebrated the final leg of his tour on Monday by renting out Hooters on Hollywood Boulevard, California for a private party.

The sports bar and grill chain usually opens at 11 a.m., but according to TMZ the star rented out the venue prior to opening and had been at the location since 8 a.m. that morning.

The publication spoke to the musician outside the eatery, as he drank beer and smoked a cigarette, where he told reporters: "I like to party."

When asked how the impromptu gathering came to be, he explained he and his pals were hanging out at his house "trying to find something to do" when they decided to call the restaurant and bar and see if they could open up the venue.

The Sunflower hitmaker, real name Austin Richard Post, added there was no real cause for the party, quipping it was a, "celebration of life... and of two shows left".

As the musician wraps up his jaunt, he's also gearing up to play the American Music Awards on Sunday, where he'll perform a new track with Black Sabbath rocker Ozzy Osbourne, rapper Travis Scott and WATT.
