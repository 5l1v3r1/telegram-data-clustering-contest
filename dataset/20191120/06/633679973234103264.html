Local mini-series shatters M-Net viewership records

20 Nov 2019, 06:26 by Algoa FM

M-Net has proudly announced via Twitter and its website that Trackers, the riveting five-part adaptation of Deon Mayer’s internationally renowned crime novel, has shattered viewership records to become the most popular show on M-Net in 2019.

And, as stated on their website,” this tremendous success story for M-Net and the African television industry hasn't even completed its run! Trackers' final episode will be screened on M-Net 101 on Sunday 24 November at 20:00”.

They went on to say that “average viewer numbers per episode have outshone not only local shows in all genres on M-Net Channel 101 but also international audience favourites such as Game of Thrones, Big Little Lies, Chernobyl, The Big Bang Theory and The Enemy Within”.

For those who have DSTV the good news is that you have not missed out, you can binge-watch the mini-series on DStv Catch Up and Showmax.
