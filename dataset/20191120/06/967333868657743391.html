Netizens Astonished by 'Worst Songs for Having Sex' Playlist

20 Nov 2019, 06:50

The story dates back to February 2018 when a handful of audio tracks were reportedly added to the list. Currently, the playlist, dubbed "worst songs to have sex to" comprises 13 melodies which are not recommended by netizens for lustful pleasures.

Mashable drew attention to the playlist earlier this week, originally created on Spotify. The playlist, what netizens describe as containing 'anti-sex' music, instantly went viral.
