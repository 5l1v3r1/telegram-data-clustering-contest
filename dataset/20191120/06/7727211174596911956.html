FUT Rewards: FIFA 20 FUT Champions Weekend League reward tiers in full and release time

Here are the confirmed rewards you can get from competing in the FIFA 20 Ultimate Team FUT Champions Weekend League competition.

20 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Nathan Bliss

The FIFA Ultimate Team game mode is extremely popular, with players from all over the world competing to the get the best squad, then going head-to-head in the infamous FUT Champions Weekend League.

In the weekly competition, gamers play a total of 30 games and the more games you win within the time limit, the better the reward.

FUT Champions is the basis of all competitive FIFA play, where amateur players can even become professionals, if they do well enough in the competition and become FUT verified that is.

To become FUT verified, you need to win 27 out of 30 games in the FUT Champions Weekend League, which will allow to you enter professional events and build your career as a professional FIFA esports player. But, that is a ridiculous amount of games to win, and only the very best manage it.

But, for most players, the Weekend League is about rewards, because the better you perform, the better rewards you get.

And, there are some changes to the reward tiers from FIFA 19, so pay attention.

So, want a low down on the Weekend League rewards? Here's all you need to know.

FIFA 20 Ultimate Team Weekend League

Weekend League beings on Friday mornings at 8am and ends on until Monday mornings at 8am every weekend.

You can play a maximum of 30 games and the rewards you get are calculated by the number of wins you manage to achieve. The more wins you get, the better your rewards will be.

FUT Rewards: What time are Weekend League rewards released?

Earned rewards are released at 08:00 UTC, that's 09:00am GMT in the UK, on Thursdays.

Rewards are distributed to players who have completed the minimum 5 matches that were required to be ranked.

These rewards are for players on PC, Xbox or PlayStation.
