Hina Khan starrer Hacked gets a release date

Hacked explores the dark underbelly of the web and deals with the issue of cybercrime. From the looks of it, Hina Khan seems to be playing the role of a modern woman in the movie.

20 Nov 2019, 06:45 by Entertainment Desk

After ruling hearts as Komolika in Ekta Kapoor’s popular daily Kasautii Zindagi Kay, Hina Khan will be seen in Vikram Bhatt’s thriller titled Hacked. The film will release in theaters on January 31, 2020.

The makers announced the release date of Hacked on Tuesday. Vikram Bhatt’s daughter and actor Krishna Bhatt shared stills from the movie and wrote on Twitter, “Get ready to step into the deep, dark digital world! Presenting #Hacked, starring Hina Khan and directed by Vikram Bhatt. In cinemas on 31st January 2020 @eyehinakhan @TheVikramBhatt @krishnavbhatt @Rohaan_ #SidMakkar @mohitmalhotra9.”
