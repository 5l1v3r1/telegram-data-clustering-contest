Conor McGregor 'cleared of fathering love child' following DNA test

Terri Murray claimed she slept with UFC superstar Conor McGregor but he has continually denied the allegations

08 Nov 2019, 11:49 by Liam Prenderville, http://www.facebook.com/mirrorfootball

Conor McGregor is not the father of a Liverpool woman's child, it has been reported.

Mum Terri Murray claims that her daughter Clodagh was born nine months after she allegedly slept with the UFC star, 30, after a night out in the city.

She filed an “initial enquiry form” for child maintenance in 2018, naming McGregor as the “respondent”.

The Irishman has continually denied he was the father and said he was willing to take a DNA test to prove it.

The Irish Herald has today claimed that he has passed the test with a  'zero percent chance' he is Clodagh's father according to documents the newspaper have seen.

The Herald claim swabs were taken from the Irish UFC star and the little girl for the DNA test

The tests, obtained by McGregor's solicitors Michael J Staines and Company, reportedly stated there was a 'zero percent chance' that he fathered the child, who will turn two in January.

The samples were said to have been tested by a private laboratory company.

They are alleged to have not shown the biological markers that would need to be present if McGregor was the father.

 

McGregor met partner Dee Devlin when he was a teenager with the couple having two children together.

Their son Conor Jr. was born in 2017 and they announced the arrival of daughter Croia earlier this year.

McGregor made his MMA debut in 2007 and has since shot to stardom.

He has not fought since his defeat to Khabib Nurgamoedov in October 2018.

The Irishman announced last month that he would be making a return to UFC on January 18 2020.
