The Australian Pink Floyd Show is returning to Birmingham this weekend and you can still get tickets

The world famous tribute act are bringing the All That You Love tour to Arena Birmingham tomorrow night

08 Nov 2019, 11:05 by Becky Weaver, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/becky-weaver/

The Australian Pink Floyd Show is embarking on a brand now tour, and Birmingham is the next stop!

Dubbed as one of the world's greatest tribute bands, they'll be bringing the 'All That You Love World Tour' to Arena Birmingham tomorrow (Saturday, November 9, 2019).

Since forming, they've sold more than four million tickets to their concerts across the globe, which celebrates the music of the iconic Pink Floyd.

What started as a tribute to one of the biggest bands of all time turned into a global phenomenon.

In fact, Pink Floyd's David Gilmour was so impressed with their performance, that he invited them to perform at his 50th birthday.

The band will kicked off the 28-date leg of their UK tour at Harrogate Convention Centre on Friday, November 1, 2019, and will be taking it to cities far and wide right through to December.

A full list of dates for the tour can be found here.

Fans can expect to hear all of the classics including Dark Side of the Moon, Wish You Were Here, Animals, The Wall and many more.

Tickets for the All That You Love World Tour are on sale now.
