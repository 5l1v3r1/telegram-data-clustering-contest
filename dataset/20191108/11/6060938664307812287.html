India to host Men's Hockey World Cup in 2023

08 Nov 2019, 11:29 by PTI

Highlights

  • India will host the 2023 Men's Hockey World Cup from January 13 to 29
  • Spain and the Netherlands have been named as the co-host of the 2022 Women's World Cup scheduled to be held from July 1 to 17
  • The venues of both the men's and women's World Cups will be announced by the host nations at a later date

LAUSANNE: India will host the Men's Hockey World Cup for the second consecutive time after the country was picked to host the game's showpiece event in 2023 by the International Hockey Federation (FIH) here on Friday.

According to the FIH, the Men's Hockey World Cup will be held in India from January 13 to 29.

In another decision made by the Executive Board during the FIH's last meeting of the year, Spain and the Netherlands were named as the co-hosts of the 2022 Women's World Cup scheduled to be held from July 1 to 17.

The venues will be announced by the host nations at a later date.
