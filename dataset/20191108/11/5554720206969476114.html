Sony TV’s extraordinary apology for ‘inaccurate reference to Chhatrapati Shivaji’ on Amitabh Bachchan’s show after #Boycott_KBC_SonyTV trends

08 Nov 2019, 11:50 by JKR Staff

Sony TV has sensationally apologised for what it said was an ‘inaccurate reference to  Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj during Wednesday’s KBC episode hosted by Bollywood megastar Amitabh Bachchan. The channel’s extraordinary apology came after Twitterati trended hashtag #Boycott_KBC_SonyTV on the microblogging site.

The channel took to Twitter to issue a public apology. It wrote, “There was an inaccurate reference to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj during Wednesday’s KBC episode, due to inadvertence. We deeply regret the same and being mindful of the sentiments of our viewers have carried a scroll expressing regret during our episode yesterday. #KBC11.”
