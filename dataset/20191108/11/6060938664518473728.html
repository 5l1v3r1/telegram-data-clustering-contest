Natasha Dalal reveals she will get married to Varun Dhawan eventually

08 Nov 2019, 11:59 by TNN

There have been rumours since a long time that Varun Dhawan is all set to tie the knot with his long-time girlfriend Natasha Dalal. The two, who have been dating for several years now, have often been spotted together on lunch or dinner outings and social gatherings. It had been reported in recent weeks that Varun and Natasha would have a a royal Jodhpur wedding and last year, Varun had even admitted that while he wants to tie the knot, he doesn’t know when it would happen. In a recent interview with Hello! Magazine, Natasha Dalal revealed that while the two are in a serious relationship, marriage will have to wait. Natasha said that the two studied in school together and were friends for a long time before they started dating in their mid-20s. She added that just after they started seeing each other, she had to move away. She said that will they both thought that marriage was on the cards eventually, it wasn’t on the agenda right now.

On the professional front, Varun has ‘Street Dancer 3D’ opposite Shraddha Kapoor and ‘Coolie No 1’ alongside Sara Ali Khan.
