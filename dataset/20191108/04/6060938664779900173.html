Watch: Shikhar Dhawan thanks fans for support after India defeated Bangladesh in 2nd T20I

08 Nov 2019, 04:17 by ANI

NEW DELHI: India's opening batsman Shikhar Dhawan thanked fans for cheering the team after the hosts defeated Bangladesh in the second T20I in Rajkot on Thursday.

FULL SCORECARD

India won the match comfortably by eight wickets as stand-in skipper Rohit Sharma played a knock of 85 runs off 43 balls while Dhawan scored 31 runs.

"All happy faces after a good game. Thank you for all the support. Kudos to @ImRo45 for his great knock," Dhawan captioned the video he tweeted, with a sea of Indian fans in the background.
