MSCI drops four constituents and adds nine to its Global Small Cap Indexes' Malaysia index

08 Nov 2019, 04:51 by Wong Swee May

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 8): MSCI has dropped Boustead Plantations Bhd, Eastern & Oriental Bhd, Hong Leong Industries Bhd and UOA Development Bhd from the MSCI Global Small Cap Indexes' MSCI Malaysia Index, following MSCI Inc's November 2019 semi-annual index review.

Meanwhile, nine other securities which are Alliance Bank Malaysia Bhd, Ekovest Bhd, Frontken Corp Bhd, Leong Hup International Bhd, MMC Corp Bhd, Pentamaster Corp Bhd, Sime Darby Property Bhd, S P Setia Bhd and Syarikat Takaful Malaysia Keluarga Bhd were added to the index.

As a result of the same semi-annual index review, three out of the nine securities that are being added into the MSCI Global Small Cap Indexes' Malaysia index— Alliance Bank Malaysia Bhd, Sime Darby Property Bhd, S P Setia Bhd— will be deleted from the MSCI Global Standard Indexes'Malaysia index.

In a statement on Thursday (Nov 7), MSCI said changes in constituents for the MSCI Global Small Cap Indexes and theMSCI Global Standard Indexes will take place as of the close ofNov 26, 2019.

At Bursa Malaysia today, Boustead Plantations fell 1 sen or 1.61% to 61 sen at 11:55am, while Eastern & Oriental dropped 0.5 sen or 0.73% to 68 sen.

At the same time, Ekovest rose 0.5 sen or 0.65% to 77.5 sen, while Hong Leong Industries added 0.75% or 8 sen to RM10.70.
