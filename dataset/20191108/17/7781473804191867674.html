Image Gallery - Royal Enfield Twins FT showcased at EICMA 2019

08 Nov 2019, 17:12 by Aditya Jadhav  

Royal Enfield showcased six custom-made motorcycles at EICMA 2019 along with the Himalayan with three new colour options. The custom motorcycles were Nought Tea v1.0, Nought Tea v2.0, Two Smoking Barrels (TSB), MJR Roach, Gallinella and the Twins FT. The Twins FT is a custom-built flat track motorcycle by Royal Enfield, built in collaboration with Harris Performance and inputs from the Flat track racers.

We take a closer look at the newly imagined Twins FT in the official pictures by the manufacturer.
