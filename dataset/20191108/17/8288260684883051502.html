WWE Live will come to Arena Birmingham - here’s how to get tickets

The biggest brawl in the UK will take on Birmingham on Tuesday, 5 May 2020

08 Nov 2019, 17:48 by Scott Dickinson, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/scott-dickinson/

WWE is set to bring some of the (literally) biggest wrestling talent to Birmingham next year for a live spectacular.

WWE Live comes to Arena Birmingham on Tuesday, 5 May 2020 and promises a ring packed full of entertainment for the entire family.

Bursting with all the drama and energy of a rock concert, in a fun, lively and exciting environment, this show offers one of the most unique experiences to ever hit the stage.

As one of the longest standing companies in sport, WWE has proudly served some of the best wrestling action of all time.

Tickets for WWE Live go on sale Thursday, 14 November 2019 at 9am, and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

Branching into action figures, trading cards and home video, the prestigious brand has become one of the most familiar and reputable names in households across the world.

Now the media giant is set to hit the arenas, boasting huge spectacle and production value to draw in audiences of all walks of life.

Wrestling fans can also look forward to a host of extras when attending the event with premium tickets and packages.

With The Superstar Meet and Greet Experience and Ramp Walk package, you’ll be sitting in the front two rows around the ring, meeting your favourite wrestlers and walking down the entry ramp as if you were one yourself!

With the premium fan package you can experience all the action in rows 3-5 around the ring.

Not to mention you can look forward to a range of exclusive goodies such as an authentic mini-title, commemorative 3D event ticket and hard stock poster when ordering your tickets as well.
