Lauren Goodger has injections in her face after fans begged her to stop

The reality TV personality reassured her followers it was not Botox or filler after posting footage of her face being injected with a needle

08 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Sara-Aisha Kent, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/sara-aisha-kent/

Lauren Goodger has uploaded footage of a needle and syringe being injected into her face after fans begged the former TOWIE star to ditch cosmetic treatments.

Sharing her latest beauty procedure with her Instagram followers, the Essex babe, 33, posted clips that showed her having needles pumped with solution being injected into her jaw and chin as she looked into her camera.

Shortly after sharing the clips, the curvy star then reassured her fans it was not a tweakment she was having but a skin hydrating treatment.

She wrote: "Thank you @essexcosmetics for my @profhilo_uk treatment! It’s not Botox and it’s not a filler it’s so good for hydrating skin and other benefits which is a much better alternative."

Profhilo is synthetic hyaluronic acid that is injected by a into the face and promises to boost natural collagen production to give users a dewy and plumped- up complexion.

It comes after Lauren's fans begged her to cut back on fillers after she posted a airbrushed snap alongside a confident and seemingly boastful caption: "They fall in love before they know my name" onto social media.

Eagle-eyed fans instantly fled to the comment section to blast the Celebs Go Dating hopeful's constantly increasing lip size.

One social media user wrote: "Why all the silly fillers? Your pics aren’t real. You don’t need them!"

While another one harshly questioned: "What have you done to your face?"

A third commented: "It literally looks like a blow up doll filter."

One person pointed out: "You've overdone it with the collagen."

Meanwhile dedicated supporters of the TV star stood up for her but begged her to slow down with cosmetic procedures.

One wrote: "Lovely face and eyes, but all I see more and more these days are the comedy lips unfortunately."

While another shared: "I wish u knew how beautiful you were without all the cosmetic procedures xx."

Lauren previously claimed in June that she had not had fillers in two years.

However, Lauren appeared to have changed her mind last month as she expressed her desire to have "full-blown surgery" .

In the past, the star admitted to undergoing a nose job, boob job, liposuction to rid her of 'lumps and bumps' and fillers, shortly after rising to fame on The Only Way Is Essex in 2010.

Lauren continued to surgically sculpt her image when she had a boob job to take her from a 34D cup to a 34E cup in 2014.

In September, the former TOWIE icon confessed to Closer magazine that she still "hates" her body image and opened up about the pressure to look good all the time.

She told the magazine: "I hate my body, I hate it. I want to get surgery.

"I want full-blown surgery. I want it all sucked out and I want to be little. I want to be skinny."
