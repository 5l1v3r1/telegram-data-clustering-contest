'Massively difficult' - What Hull City fans have said about West Brom

The Baggies head to the East Riding of Yorkshire on Saturday as the two most in form clubs in the whole of the Championship face off before the international break

08 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Joseph Chapman, https://www.facebook.com/josephchapmanBM/

When Hull City and West Brom cross swords at the KCOM Stadium this weekend, it’ll be a meeting of the division’s top two - that’s based on the form table, at least.

Albion swept aside rock bottom Stoke City on Monday evening as the Sky Sports cameras broadcast to the world their qualities.

Two days earlier, though, the Tigers managed the most eye catching result of the weekend in the Championship when they blew expensively assembled Fulham away 3-0 at Craven Cottage.

Grant McCann’s side are sitting down in 11th but have won their last three - including a victory at Albion’s promotion rivals Nottingham Forest.

The Baggies head north prior to the international break looking to retain their grasp on the top spot - this is what the opposing fans this Saturday have been saying about Slaven Bilic’s boys.
