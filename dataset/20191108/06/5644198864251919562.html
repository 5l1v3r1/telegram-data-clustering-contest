Cardinal Health Inc wins US$2.25 billion US defense contract: Pentagon

08 Nov 2019, 06:45

WASHINGTON: Cardinal Health 200 LLC, a unit of Cardinal Health Inc , has been awarded a maximum US$2.25 billion modification for a contract with the Defense Logistics Agency for ordering and distribution of medical surgical supplies, the Pentagon said on Thursday.

(Reporting by Mohammad Zargham)
