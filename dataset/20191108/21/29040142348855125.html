Defend your Android

Whether you have a Pixel 4 or 4 XL, Google phones can last a long, long time — the original Pixel got 4 years of OS updates — but that long support life only matters if you keep your Pixel 4 XL safe. Safe phones are happy phones, and heavy-duty cases like the affordable Tudia Merge Series can help you ensure that your Pixel 4 XL lasts you as long as possible.

On a clearer note, the Ringke Fusion-X allows me to show off the distinct Google design while also adding some extra edge and style with the colored bumpers. Space Blue will look great with the black and white versions, and the Black and Camo Black will look great with any color Pixel 4 XL.

If you prefer not to bulk up your brand-new and already-large phone, the Tech21 Studio Colour and its super-slim profile offer scratch protection and grip without adding any bulk at all. Despite being an ultra-thin case, the Studio Colour is still rated for drops up to 8 feet — not that I'd want to test that claim — and has anti-microbial protection, which is great for those of us who use our Pixels quite literally everywhere.

Navigating Black Friday case deals

Cases will see Black Friday deals and discounts like every other electronic accessory under the sun, but you're going to have to pay a little more attention to them to get the best value you can. Seeing $20-30 off a Pixel 4 XL case doesn't necessarily mean you're getting a better deal for a case that debuted at $60; it just means you're not getting ripped off as the folks who bought that case on Day One. Apart from first-party cases like the Google Fabric Case and name brand cases like Bellroy, Lifeproof and Otterbox, it's rare to see a case being sold at list price in 2019.

Of course, for those high-quality, high-priced name-brand cases, Black Friday can mean a rare discount on a case that normally never sees a discount, such as the custom-printed Skinit Clear cases, which are already 30% off for Veterans Day and will likely see a similar sale for Black Friday/Cyber Monday. You'll want to compare case prices between the manufacturer and big retailers like Best Buy and Amazon to see who has the better price, since Black Friday is one of those rare times that casemakers will likely have better discounts.

We'll post the best case deals as we come across them in Android Central's Black Friday deals roundups, so check back early and often. Don't forget that a great number of cases tend to pop up in lightning deals during major shopping events like these, so you might want to add cases you like to your wishlist so you'll get a notification when a Lightning deal on them goes live.
