Brazil's Lula calls for Saturday rally, national tour

08 Nov 2019, 21:23

CURITIBA, Brazil (Reuters) - Brazil’s former president Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva plans to hold a gathering of supporters at a metal workers union in greater Sao Paulo on Saturday before embarking on a national tour, he told a crowd after leaving prison on Friday.
