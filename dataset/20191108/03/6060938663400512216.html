Netizens rib Ananya Panday as she compares father Chunky Panday’s new look to Tony Stark

08 Nov 2019, 03:44 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ananya Panday, who is active on the social media, commands quite a fan following. While her fans love and appreciate all her social media updates, her latest post did not go down well with many social media users. She recently took to her Instagram handle to share a picture of herself with her father Chunky Panday and Farah Khan. Comparing Chunky’s new look to Tony Stark, aka Robert Downey Jr. from Iron Man and Avengers franchise. While comments from the B-town actors poured in appreciating Chunky’s new look, netizens were not so happy about the same.
