Teacher and boyfriend arrested on suspicion of raping student and forcing her into threesome

08 Nov 2019, 03:21 by Coconuts Bali

Police in Bali’s Buleleng regency have arrested a 29-year-old honorary teacher and her boyfriend for allegedly forcing one of her students to watch them have sex and then raping her. 

In a statement, police identified the teacher as Ni Made Sri Novi Darmaningsih and her 36-year-old boyfriend as AA PT Wartayasa, and said that the alleged crime took place on Oct. 26. The victim, who is identified by her initial V, is 15 years old. 

According to authorities, Novi invited V over to introduce her to Wartayasa that day.

“The victim was promised new clothes and prepaid phone credit. She was taken to her teacher’s house so they could buy the clothes and so she agreed, because it was her own teacher so she agreed,” Buleleng Police spokesman, I Gede Sumarjaya, told Detik yesterday.

They went to Novi’s boarding room, where she forced V to sit on the mattress and watch them have sex. All this while, police said that Wartayasa held V’s hands, before later starting to kiss her.

Wartayasa and Novi allegedly forced V to have a threesome with them, which constituted raping the teenager. Sumarjaya said they were driven to the crime after watching porn. 

V’s parents later reported the couple to the police after finding out what had befallen their daughter. Wartayasa and Novi were charged with sexual abuse of a minor, which is a crime punishable by up to 15 years in prison under Indonesia’s Child Protection Law.
