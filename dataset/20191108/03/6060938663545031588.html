THIS is what Sooraj Pancholi has to say about father Aditya Pancholi’s link-up with Kangana Ranaut

08 Nov 2019, 03:27 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After his last film, ‘Hero’ with Athiya Shetty, Sooraj Pancholi is all set to impress the audience once again with his upcoming film, ‘Satellite Shankar’. The actor, who is currently on a promotion spree, opened up recently about his father Aditya Pancholi’s affair with Kangana Ranaut. Speaking about the entire episode and how it affected his life and his parents’ relationship, Sooraj reportedly said that the issue was theirs and he doesn’t get into that. The actor said that he has spent most of his time with his grandparents. According to Sooraj, his father’s affair with Kangana was a personal matter between his parents and they have sorted it out. Although he is not too happy about the things that have happened he cannot help as it is their life.

Sooraj also reportedly believes that his mother Zarina Wahab is his greatest pillar of strength. The actor also added that she is the strength of the entire family and his father knows that. According to Sooraj, though his father is tall, well-built and aggressive, his mother is still stronger than him.

Aditya's wife Zarina Wahab had earlier stated that Aditya and Kangana were in a relationship for four and a half years.

An Irfan Kamal directorial, ‘Satellite Shankar’ will also star Megha Akash in lead roles. The film is slated to hit the theatres on November 15, 2019.
