Coronation Street fans fear Shona Ramsey will die after disturbing discovery

Coronation Street fans are worried that a miserable fate for Shona Ramsey is inevitable after marrying David Platt

08 Nov 2019, 23:04 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

Shona Ramsey is set to leave the cobbles but after a happy reunion with David Platt, Coronation Street viewers fear that her exit could be tragic.

Last night, viewers watched as David was released from jail and married Shona in a sweet ceremony.

But, considering she will be leaving as actress Julia Goulding goes on maternity leave, it looks like the couple won't have a happy ending.

This prompted fans to flock to Twitter to speculate.

One tweeted: "With @juliagoulding set to go off on Maternity leave sooner rather than later & David & Shona finally married what possible reason could she have for leaving? Under threat from the bloke's from the Prison? Death? #Corrie."
