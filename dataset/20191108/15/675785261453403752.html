Rebekah Vardy shows off her bump as she steps out after Coleen Rooney drama

Rebekah Vardy, 37, sported a tight brown dress that clung to her growing belly as she hung out with friends in London

08 Nov 2019, 15:28 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Rebekah Vardy seemed in high spirits as she showed off her baby bump while out and about in London today.

The mum-to-be, 37, sported a tight brown dress that clung to her growing belly as she hung out with some friends in the capital.

She teamed her frock, which reached down to her ankles, with a pair of rust coloured boots and a cream coat.

Rebekah, who is pregnant with her fifth child, carried a pale pink handbag and wore her long brunette locks in a side plait for the casual outing.

Despite a difficult couple of months, she looked cheerful and was seen laughing with her pals as they hung out.

Rebekah and her footballer husband were forced to cut their recent holiday to Dubai short when she was accused of selling stories on fellow WAG Coleen Rooney, something she denies.

Coleen and Rebekah have been at loggerheads ever since her accusations.

Wayne Rooney's wife was nicknamed Wagatha Christie in the wake of the scandal after unveiling her detective work.

She said she had planted false stories on her private Instagram account and revealed in an epic statement that she'd blocked every account but Rebekah's from viewing her Stories to test whether or not the information would leak.

And when they did, she told the world what had happened in a bombshell statement that went viral.

Rebekah has vehemently denied leaking stories about Coleen and said in her own statement that she was "disgusted" that she even had to deny it, especially when she's heavily pregnant.

She was so upset that she cut her Dubai holiday short and flew back home.

Becky claimed she has hired a forensic IT expert to prove her innocence and is considering legal action.

But representatives for Coleen say she has irrefutable evidence that the leaks came from Rebekah's account.
