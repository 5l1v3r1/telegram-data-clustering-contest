Restaurant of the Year accolade comes to Mayo

08 Nov 2019, 15:13

Flannery's Bistro, Ballinrobe, has been named Restaurant of the Year at the 2019 Food Awards Ireland.

The event, held in Dublin, recognised the achievements of local suppliers, wholesalers, restaurants and chefs - among others involved in the industry.
