Prosecutor Vacancy At Ministry Of Corporate Affairs

08 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Manav Malhotra

Union Public Service Commission invites online application for the post of Prosecutor in Ministry of Corporate Affairs, for two years subject to successful completion of mandatory induction training of two weeks duration, as prescribed and sponsored by SFIO.

Name of the Post: Prosecutor

No. of Post: 01 (One)

Age Limit: 30 Years

Pay Scale: Level 8 (Rs.47,600-1,51,100/-) in the Pay matrix as per 7th CPC

Essential Qualification and Experience

  • Graduate in any discipline plus bachelor's degree in Law from a recognized University or Institute; OR
  • Integrated graduate in Law (5 years duration) from a recognized University or Institute.
  • For Graduate in any discipline plus bachelor's degree in Law Holders- One-year experience in handling litigation & court matters/administration of Law in a Government organization; OR
  • For Integrated graduate in Law (5 years duration) Holders 2 years' experience in handing litigation & court matters/administration of Law in a Government organization.
