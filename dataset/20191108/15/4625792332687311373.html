‘Maybe if we got on our knees’: Jane Fonda planned to bring 'attractive' women to convince Trump on climate change

08 Nov 2019, 15:19 by Madison Dibble

Jane Fonda asked Ivanka Trump if she could bring attractive women to the Oval Office to convince President Trump to battle climate change.

In an interview with Vice, Fonda said she planned to bring beautiful women and scientists to feed Trump’s ego.

“I got this idea that you know, maybe if I went into his office with several extremely attractive, very, very smart women who know and care about the climate, and a few scientists, and maybe if we got on our knees, and we said to him, ‘Donald, you will be the global hero for all-time if you do what’s right about [the climate],” Fonda said.
