Robert Moreno: Ansu Fati could play for Spain at European Championships

The Spain coach has left the door open for the Barcelona forward, who's currently with the U21s

08 Nov 2019, 15:14 by Jordi Gil

Robert Moreno spoke in a press conference on Friday after naming his Spain squad for the upcoming games against Romania and Malta.

Among other things, he spoke about Barcelona's Ansu Fati, who could go the European Championships with the senior squad next summer despite currently being with the U21s.

Ansu Fati at Euro 2020: "He's on the pre-list although he's not with us now. He's a special player who could be called up, why not?"

Pique and the Olympic Games: "It's something I can't control (U21 coach Luis de la Fuente will make the decision). He's already announced his retirement from Spain, which we respect."

Gabriel Paulista: "I'm aware they're applying for a passport, he's still can't be (called up). It's welcome if it is an option."

Dani Olmo: "He's doing really well in his league and above all in the Champions League. We're happy to be able to have him with us."
