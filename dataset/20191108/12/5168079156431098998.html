National and Armed Forces Museums in Oman to open doors to public

08 Nov 2019, 12:26 by Times News Service

Muscat: The National Museum and the Armed Forces Museum will open their doors to the public on the holiday to mark the Prophet's birthday.

The National Museum said in a statement online, "The Museum will open its doors to the public on Sunday November 10, 2019 from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm."

The Sultanate's Armed Forces Museum will also be open during the holiday.
