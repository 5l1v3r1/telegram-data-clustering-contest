BREAKING Michael O'Neill appointed Stoke City manager - but will still oversee Northern Ireland

O'Neill has been tasked with the job of turning Stoke City's fortunes around in the Championship with the side sitting in the relegation zone

08 Nov 2019, 12:39 by Aaron Flanagan, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/

Michael O'Neill has been appointed Stoke City manager.

The Northern Ireland boss will however still take charge of any Euro 2020 play-off matches for his country, should they qualify for them.
