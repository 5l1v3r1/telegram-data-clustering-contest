Three young Mayo people celebrate Gaisce silver awards

08 Nov 2019, 12:52

Three young Mayo people - Abdul Abdullatef, Mohamad Fahed Abdullatef and Amran Dalihamad - have been presented with Gaisce President Silver Awards.

They were honoured for successfully completed 26 weeks across three different challenge areas and undertaking a three-day adventure journey incorporating either a 50km walk or a 190km cycle.

They were accompanied at the ceremony by their friend Declan Gaughan from Youthreach Ballinrobe

Gaisce is the most prestigious youth award in Ireland and is a challenge to young people from the President of Ireland, Michael D. Higgins, to dream big and realise their potential.
