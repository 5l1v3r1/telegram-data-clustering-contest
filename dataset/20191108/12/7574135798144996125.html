Travis Scott’s Astroworld ’19 lineup: Migos, Marilyn Manson, Pharrell, Rosalia & more

08 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Andrew Sacher

Travis Scott‘s 2019 Astroworld Festival is tomorrow (Saturday, November 9) in Houston’s NRG Park, and the lineup was just revealed today. Travis headlines and he’ll be joined by special guests, and the lineup also includes Migos, Marilyn Manson (!), Pharrell, Gucci Mane, Rosalia, Young Thug, Megan Thee Stallion, DaBaby, Sheck Wes, Young Dolph + Key Glock, Pop Smoke, Tay Keith, Don Toliver, and the Houston All Stars.

Tickets are still available.
