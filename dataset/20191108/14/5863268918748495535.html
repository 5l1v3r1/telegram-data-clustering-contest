Dadu: PPP candidate wins PS 86 by-election

08 Nov 2019, 14:45 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

DADU (Dunya News) - PPP candidate has won the PS-86 by election, according to unofficial results.

PPP’s Salahuddin Jilani got 42254 votes while the PTI’s Imdad Leghari got 25555 votes. PPP workers celebrated the win by dancing on the roads.

Polling completed on 5 PM to elect a new Sindh Assembly representative from Dadu’s PS 86. The polling continued without any break till 5 PM.

The Election Commission set up 158 polling stations in PS-86 for the more than 199,000 people. 1300 police while 1100 Rangers officials performed duties for secure election.

The PS-86 seat was left vacant after the previous MPA, the PPP’s Syed Ghulam Shah Jilani, passed away on September 13.
