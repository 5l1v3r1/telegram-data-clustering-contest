Leicester v Arsenal: match preview

08 Nov 2019, 14:38 by Guardian Staff

The contrast between these clubs goes way beyond a six-point gap in the table. Arsenal are close to disarray, with a woeful away record, a dispute over the captaincy and doubts about the future of Unai Emery. At Leicester, harmony reigns and Brendan Rodgers is bringing the best out of an exciting young team. Rodgers says it is far too early to talk about a top-four finish but a win would increase those prospects and pile pressure on Emery. Paul Doyle
