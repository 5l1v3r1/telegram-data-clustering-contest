SGX reports 9% m-o-m higher total securities turnover value for October as risk appetite returns

08 Nov 2019, 14:02 by PC Lee

SINGAPORE (Nov 8): Singapore Exchange says total securities market turnover value rose to S$21.6 billion in October, up 9% against September.

In a noon filing today, SGX said a rebound in Asian stock market activity had led to a return of investor risk appetite.
