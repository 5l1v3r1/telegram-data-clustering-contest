Magnitude 5.9 Earthquake Strikes Off Tonga Coast - USGS

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - A 5.9 magnitude earthquake was registered on Friday off the coast of the Polynesian kingdom of Tonga in the southern part of the Pacific Ocean, the US Geological Survey (USGS) said.

08 Nov 2019, 14:16

Initially, seismologists reported about a 6.2 magnitude earthquake.

The quake was registered at 09:41 GMT about 60 miles to the east from Hihifo town at the depth of 6.2 miles.
