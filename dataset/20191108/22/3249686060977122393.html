Former 'Produce 101' Contestant Lee Suhyun to debut as a solo artist

08 Nov 2019, 22:14 by honeycider

Former 'Produce 101' contestant Lee Suhyun has released video and photo teasers of her upcoming solo debut under HG Entertainment.

Previously Suhyun was a member of project group I.B.I and was a pre-debut member of the girl group DAYDAY before they disbanded before making a debut.

She will be debuting with the song "Blah Blah" (쉿) and will be going under the stage name "Luri" (루리).
