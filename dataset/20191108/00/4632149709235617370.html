Fake News Buster: Old foam

08 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
Several photos of devotees offering prayers during the Chhat puja, while being surrounded by toxic foam in the Yamuna River, are being circulated on social media.


SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
While Delhi is embroiled in a public health emergency and there are recent photos of devotees surrounded by toxic foams, many of the photographs shared are from over a year ago.
