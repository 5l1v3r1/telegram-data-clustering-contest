Shipping Cigarettes

08 Nov 2019, 00:19 by BRIEF

NEW YORK – UPS should pay $18.8 million in unpaid taxes and $78.8 million in penalties for illegally shipping cigarettes into New York, the Second Circuit ruled, modifying a district court’s determination that had the company paying $9.4 million in unpaid taxes and $237.6 million in penalties.
