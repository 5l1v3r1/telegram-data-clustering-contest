FoundPac Group may rebound higher, says RHB Retail Research

08 Nov 2019, 00:31 by theedgemarkets.com

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 8): RHB Retail Research said FoundPac Group Bhd may rebound higher after it continued holding above the 45.5 sen support.

In a trading stocks note today, the research house said given that it has formed a positive candle for the second consecutive day, the positive sentiment has been enhanced.

“A positive bias may emerge above the 49 sen level, with an exit set below the 45.5 sen threshold.

“Towards the upside, the immediate resistance is at the 54 sen mark. This is followed by the 56.5 sen level,” it said.
