Barcelona vs. Celta Vigo: Junior and Suarez return to the squad

Suarez is fit again and Junior's back due to Alba's injury

08 Nov 2019, 20:46 by Sergi Capdevila

Barcelona have the chance on Saturday to move on from winless games against Levante and Slavia Prague and the sensations those games left.

Ernesto Valverde's side host Celta Vigo before the international break with the chance to get back to winning ways.

With Jordi Alba missing through injury, Junior Firpo returns to the squad having been left out for the last three games.

Luis Suarez also returns from a calf problem, whole there is no room for Carles Perez, Carles Alena or Jean Clair Todibo.

The full squad is: Ter Stegen, Lenglet, Umtiti, Piqué, Sergi Roberto, Semedo, De Jong, Busquets, Arthur, Arturo Vidal, Leo Messi, Luis Suárez, Dembélé, Griezmann, Ansu Fati, Wague, Rakitic, Junior and Neto

With 19 players summoned for the game at Camp Nou against Celta, who will be coached by new boss Oscar Garcia for the first time, one will have to be cut before kick-off.
