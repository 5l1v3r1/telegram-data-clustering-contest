Hrithik Roshan's 'Krrish 4' to start rolling in January 2020? Read details

08 Nov 2019, 20:59 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Hrithik Roshan's last two releases scored huge at the box office and also received a lot of appreciation from the masses. Now, it is being reported, the actor has been roped for Farah Khan's next which is said to be the remake of 'Satte Pe Satta'.

The actor will also star his prep for his superhero flick 'Krrish 4'.

Now, according to the latest report, Hrithik and his father Rakesh Roshan will be making the official announcement about 'Krrish 4' next month. The report further stated that the film will start rolling in January 2020.

It was supposed to be announced last month but Roshan is working on the script to perfect it. Reportedly, it will be out of the box concept this time which no one has seen before. Also, Rakesh Roshan ensured that he is involved in every phase of production.

Reports of Sanjay Gupta helming the film were doing rounds the town, however, Sr. Roshan will be back as the director of the film.
