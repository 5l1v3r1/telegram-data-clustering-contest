Thugs ‘blow up’ homeless man with firework in evil prank in city centre

The sick footage recorded in Liverpool shows the man in a wheelchair surrounded by two thugs who light what appears to be a rocket firework and force it into the homeless man's hand

08 Nov 2019, 20:56 by Mirror.co.uk

Four men 'blew up' a vulnerable homeless man in Liverpool in a vile prank.

The sick footage shows a man in a wheelchair surrounded by two hooded thugs who light what appears to be a rocket firework and force it into the homeless man's hand.

After lighting the firework the grimacing thug turns and runs.

The homeless man can be seen leaning forward from his wheelchair - in what appears to be a move to protect himself from the blazing rocket.

Shortly after, the firework explodes in the man’s hand.

 

The shocking footage has recently been shared on social media after initially emerging on Snapchat, with the owner of the video commenting ‘I can’t believe he blew himself up’.

The attack took place in central Liverpool close to the city’s main station Liverpool Lime Street.

Michelle Langan runs the Papercup project that works with the man in the wheelchair regularly.

She said: "It’s so sad to see a vulnerable person being used for laughs like this.

"Our team sees this man every week, and its horrifying to see him put in danger like this...

"Its not funny, its not a joke. This prank on the video could have easily ended in tragedy - homeless people should not be treated as objects to laugh at or be taken advantage of.

Ms Langan raised concerns over the younger generation taking extreme risks for the sake of gaining popularity on social media.
