Exclusive: Alibaba planning launch of US$15 billion HK listing for last week of November - sources

08 Nov 2019, 16:01

Alibaba Group Holding Ltd plans to launch a Hong Kong share offering to raise US$10 billion to US$15 billion in the final week of November, two people with direct knowledge of the matter told Reuters.

REUTERS: Alibaba Group Holding Ltd plans to launch a Hong Kong share offering to raise US$10 billion to US$15 billion in the final week of November, two people with direct knowledge of the matter told Reuters.

The U.S.-listed Chinese e-commerce giant is due to seek approval from Hong Kong's listing committee on Thursday, the people said, for a deal that Dealogic data showed will be the world's biggest-ever cross-border secondary listing.
