Bride uses bouquet toss at wedding to help her brother propose to his girlfriend

08 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Alexandra Deabler

Luckily she said yes.

A wedding came to a halt during the bouquet toss when, instead of throwing the flowers as is traditional, the bride turned around and calmly walked over to her brother’s girlfriend and handed them to her directly. The guest then turned around where she was met with the sight of her boyfriend down on one knee.
