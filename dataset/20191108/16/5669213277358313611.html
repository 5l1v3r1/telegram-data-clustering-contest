Cargo ship grounded, refloated, Miami

08 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Erofey Schkvarkin

Cargo ship BETTY K VI ran aground in Government Cut, Fisher island, Miami, at around 2200 UTC Nov 7, while leaving port, bound for Nassau. She was refloated late same day according to track, taken back and berthed at around 1600 UTC Nov 8.
Cargo ship BETTY K VI, IMO 8801216, GT 1457, built 1988, flag Panama, manager BETTY K AGENCIES LTD, Bahamas.
