Bags involving school learning aids distributed to Myanmar students

08 Nov 2019, 05:30 by Wai Lin

 

PYI-With the aim to promote China-Myanmar Friendship, the bags involving school learning aids are being distributed to some Basic Education High Schools.

The Alibaba Philanthropy is now sponsoring the China Foundation for Poverty Alleviation (CFPA) for donating 50,000 school bags since October.

“We aim to donate the school bags and stationeries for the students who can conduct their study without having difficulties. Secondly, we helped the reforming education sector and third also aimed for long-lasting relationship between Myanmar and China,” said Pyae Phyo Kyaw, CFPA’s Project Officer.

The ceremony to donate the school bags was held at the Basic Education High School in Pyi Township, Bago Region, yesterday morning and totaling 5,014 school bags were donated to the 161 schools, according to the Pyi Township Education Office.

There are 18 kinds of items in the school bags. For 2019 academic year, tt is the first time donation launched in Pwint Phyu, Yanangchuang, Chuak Townships in Magway Region, Nay Pyi Taw, Meikhtila in Mandalay Region, Shan State and Bago Region.

The CFPA will be donating 70,000 school bags to Dedaye, Dala and Shwepyitha Townships in Yangon Region, in December.

CFPA was formally registered in 2015 with Ministry of Home Affairs to operate in Myanmar as an INGO.

CFPA Myanmar Office will always stand by with Myanmar people, and envisage to link big ideas with bold action to impact and empower individuals, and to generate cohesion of intelligence, will and power towards a progressive and prosperous Myanmar society.
