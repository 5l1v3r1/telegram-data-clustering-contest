Best places to have Christmas dinner in Birmingham in 2019

Find out what's on the Christmas menu in Birmingham, how much they'll cost per head

08 Nov 2019, 05:30 by Sanjeeta Bains, https://www.facebook.com/sanjeetabainsbm/

Whether you're planning the office Christmas lunch or party, or sorting out your annual friends festive get together, it doesn't need to be a headache.

Birmingham is renowned for its vibrant food and drink scene with a variety of restaurants catering for various budgets and tastes so you can find something to please everyone.

So what’s on the Christmas dinner table in our city? Here’s our guide to Brum's festive menus for 2019.

Eat, drink and be merry!

Gas Street Social

Thanks to their cosy Nordic theme, Gas Street Social is one of the best places in Brum to celebrate Christmas. The canalside Mailbox venue offers a three-course lunch and dinner menu.

For starters choose from ham hock bon bon, pork, apple, mustard and deep fried camembert, roasted and pureed beetroot with pea shoots.

The mains dishes include turkey with bacon, chestnut and cranberry stuffing, roast potatoes. The veggie option is individual homemade leek, mushroom, lentil and cranberry pie.

Desserts include homemade apple strudel, custard and Black Forest cheesecake.

How much? A two-course lunch menu costs £19.95. A three-course lunch menu costs £25.95.  A two-course dinner menu costs £25.95 and three-courses for dinner for £29.95.

Available from Tuesday November 19 to Tuesday December 24 from 12-5pm for lunch and 5- 9pm for dinner.

The Mailbox

 

Miller and Carter

For starters choose from baked cheddar mushrooms - mushrooms in creamy cheddar and spinach sauce and potted smoked salmon pate served with ciabatta and rye breads.

Mains include hand carved turkey breast served with dauphinoise potatoes, pork and mustard duvets, pomegranate glazed roasted parsnips, carrots and sprouts with a rich gravy. Veggie option is  butternut squash and emmental roulade - served with a savoury granola, garlic and cheese sauce, parsnips, carrots and sprouts and baby potatoes.

There are four desserts on offer include chocolate and salted caramel gold bar and  sticky toffee pudding.

How much? Two courses for £22.95 and three courses for £26.95, or three-courses with a glass of prosecco for £30.95.

Available from November 21 to December 24 available all day from Sunday - Friday and from 12- 5pm on Saturday.

Mailbox Birmingham, 178-180 Wharfside Street B1 1RN

 

 

 

Lost and Found

 

 

This beautiful venue offers a Christmas cracker of a menu and well priced too!

Enjoy a choice of four starters such as spiced carrot and ginger soup, chicken liver parfait, grilled smoked mackerel with a beetroot and horseradish purée, goats’ curd and a pickled apple & dill salad.

For mains, there are four choices including roasted turkey breast , chestnut and apricot stuffing, roast potatoes and a pig in a blanket and slow cooked treacle cured beef brisket served with horseradish mash. There are two veggie choices including chestnut mushroom and lentil pie topped with cauliflower and potato mash.

Dessert choices include sticky toffee pudding and cookie loaf cake with red berry coulis and vegan vanilla flavour ice cream.

Lost and Found also offers a separate three-course vegan menu too. 

How much? A three-course festive menu costs £29.95 per person.

 8 Bennetts Hill, B2 5RS

 

Ju Jus

 

 

This cute canalside cafe is the place for relaxed social gatherings. For starters, enjoy chicken liver pate laced with Scotch whisky and served with homemade bread or cream of chestnut & parsnip soup with croutons.

For mains there is a choice of roast turkey with a pork chestnut & herb stuffing served with ‘pigs in blankets’ and all the other trimmings, veggie nut roast and Irish fish chowder.

Dessertwise, there's Christmas pudding or sticky toffee pudding, followed by mince pies for everyone.

How much? £29.99 for three courses plus mince pies.

Watermarque, Canal Square, 100 Browning Street, B16 8EH

Marco Pierre White steakhouse bar and grill

 

If you’re looking for a touch of sophistication and glamour, than the rooftop restaurant at The Cube is the place to dine.

The dinner menu starters include smoked duck and pork terrine - pickled baby onions and gherkins, sun dried tomato bread and baked camembert fondue with red onion marmalade with sourdough bread.

For mains,  there is turkey roast with all the trimmings.

Other choices include roasted lamb saddle, kale creamy potato, pumpkin puree, baby leek, red wine jus and garlic and dill baked cod with herb and lemon crushed potatoes, crayfish, caper butter and salty fingers.

For dessert who could say no to  Christmas pudding with brandy sauce? If that doesn't take your fancy there are three other choices including a cheese plate.

The restaurant will be transformed into a magical winter wonderland, with snowy Christmas trees, heaters and twinkling lights to create the perfect festive backdrop.

How much? The lunch menu starts from £28 per person.

Festive dinner starts from £45 per person depending on what day you dine.

Available November 25  - December 30.

The Cube, 200 Wharfside Street, B1 1PR

Chung Ying Central

Popular Chinese restaurant on Colmore Row offers a very fabulously priced menu at £15 per person.

Enjoy a Christmas starter platter -  with salt and chilli ribs, Thai style chicken wings, sesame toast, king prawn toast, king prawn tempura and crispy seaweed.  For main course choose from a range of five dishes including festive lemon and honey turkey and crispy chill beef. For £10 extra,  you can toast lunch with a festive Bellini.

On the Christmas dinner menu, there are a choice of four starters - crispy duck with pancakes and ribs and wings. For mains choose from five dishes include chicken katsu curry, beef fillet in black pepper sauce and king prawns in spicy Sichuan sauce. There is also a separate vegan menu.

How much? Lunch menu is available for a minimum of four people and is priced £15. Dinner is available for a minimum of four people and costs £30 per person.

 

The Cube, 200 Wharfside Street, B1 1PR

Tattu

With a range of dining options suitable for various sized groups including the stunning Yang private dining room and rose covered booths on the lower ground floor, Tattu should make for a very memorable and glam Christmas party.

The three-course set Christmas menu is available for parties for nine or more.

For starters, choose from lotus chip mess kimchi, bacon and sriracha aioli, Peking duck bon bons, chilli salt baby squid sweet chilli and spring onion.

Main courses include sweet and sour Iberico with pineapple, banana shallots and dragon fruit, pan fried Szechuan sea bass chilli bean and spring onion and special fried sweet potato with Szechuan, spring onion and sweet sour salt.

Or how about Asian pear sticky toffee pudding with cinnamon, vanilla and almond with cinnamon, vanilla and almond for dessert?

How much? £45 per person.

18 Barwick Street, B3 2NT

 

 

Hotel du Vin

Hotel du Vin has an epic Christmas offering 12 choices of dishes on their three-course Christmas menu this festive season.

For starters choose from dishes including goat's cheese, pear and pecan salad with cranberry dressing and salmon gravadlax - cucumber salad with sweet mustard dressing.

Mains choices include roasted free range turkey ballotine with traditional trimmings,  daube of beef provencale, chantenay carrots and celeriac, herb crusted cod white bean and San Marzano tomato stew.

Veggie choice is butternut squash cavolo nero and sage risotto.

On the Christmas desserts list, you can enjoy a cheese board with biscuits, chutneys and fruit.

They will also throw in mince pies and crackers!

How much? Prices start from £34.95 for a three-course celebration.

Celebration drinks package costs from £20 for a drinks package to include a welcome glass of fizz, half a bottle of mineral water and half a bottle of Hotel du Vin Selection wine per person.

Christmas lunch and dinner menus are available from November 27.

Hotel du Vin also offers a festive afternoon tea option.

25 Church Street Bthree 2NR

25 Church Street B3 2NR

 

Pint Shop

 

This pub chain's cosy and beautiful restaurant is a wonderful setting for festive gatherings.

Their three-course menu includes sharing starter starters- with a choice of three snacks to share - choose from pulled ham croquettes, gin cured salmon on crackers, baked camembert and thick cut chips.

Mains choices include roast turkey, with all the trimming, nut roast, braised tomato sauce, vegan ‘Pigs’,  Camden Ink braised pork belly and beef and mushroom wellington.

Pudding choices include brown sugar meringues, mango and coconut and nut crumb, and gin float!

How much? Three courses for £29.50 available from November 21 to December 24.

38 Bennetts Hill, B2 5SN

 

Gino D'Acampo - My restaurant

 

Brum's latest glam restaurant chain offers a fabulous looking festive lunch and dinner menu. Choose from starters including calamari and tomato bruschetta, Mains include Italian meatballs, seabass and turkey escalopes. Desserts include Tiramisu of course and chocolate fondant.

The festive party menu (available all day and evening) includes starter dishes king prawn bruschetta, pear, parma ham and blue cheese salad.

Mains include crispy duck, cherry and red wine sauce, sea bass and gnocchi margherita.

Dessert options include a Baileys panna cotta and cheese board.

How much? The festive lunch menu costs £24.50 available from 12pm -4pm. The party menu costs £33.40 and is available all day.

 

Temple Row, B2 5DJ

 

Malmaison

 

Malmaison offers a lunch and dinner celebrations menu.

For starters choose from dishes including smoked ham hock and dried cranberry roulade, watercress, apple and shallot salad and sweet English mustard  or celeriac truffle soup.

For mains  - dishes include daube of beef, roast parsnip and Beetroot Wellington - roast beetroot, mushroom duxelle, vegan pastry and jus.

Desserts include 'Le Fromage' - a selection of artisan cheeses, chutney, celery, quince crackers and crispbreads.

How much?  Lunch and dinner menu priced from £29.95 per person.

Available from Wednesday November 20 to Monday December 23.

1 Wharfside Street, B1 1RD

 

Gaucho

The festive offering at Gaucho is described as an "informal menu with an abundant selection of dishes. Designed to share." Sounds good to us! Starter dishes include empanadas and chicken liver parfait. There are three main steak dishes.

Desserts include Dulche De Leche flan All served up in a low lit and truly gorgeous setting.

How much? Priced £55 per person.

55 Colmore Row, B3 2AA

 

 

 

Purecraft bar and kitchen

Purecraft - co-owned by Simpsons chef director Andreas Antona - stocks a range of more than 75 bottled beers from around the world so if you’re not interested in the German beers on offer at the nearby market, you must try here.

And with a new chef at the helm - their festive food offering sounds pretty good too.

On the set Christmas set menu, starters include Purecraft scotch egg with purity beer ketchup or beef and onion soup with smoked cheese rarebit.

For mains enjoy a choice of four mains -  including turkey schnitzel, old Winchester mayo, watercress pesto and skin on fries and smoked bacon Ribeye with sauerkraut, charred potato, Purity cider and wholegrain mustard sauce.

Festive dessert on offer includes profiteroles and Bird's custard and sattleback chocolate sauce, Maravilla raspberry beer Eton mess.

Includes sharing bottle of Purity Limited edition beer with dessert.

How much? two-courses for £27.50 or three-courses for £32.50. There is also a buffet menu available at £11 per person.

30 Waterloo Street, B2 5TJ

Maribel

 

Maribel is offering a taste of Christmas taster menu. If you fancy an fine dining festive extravaganza, Maribel is offering a ten-course menu for £75.

Start with cheddar and onion cracker and work your way through to venison tartare with mushroom and other dishes including duck liver, plum and walnut and Monkfish cauliflower and yeast. And end of course with a mince pie.

How much? Ten courses for £75. 

Available for lunch and dinner from Tuesday December 3 to Tuesday December 24.

6 Brindley Place, Birmingham B1 2JB
