Listen to rare Prince track ‘Don’t Let Him Fool Ya’

08 Nov 2019, 13:34

It will feature on forthcoming '1999' reissue

Prince‘s estate has shared rare track ‘Don’t Let Him Fool Ya’ for the first time.

  • Read More: 10 things we learned at the playback of Prince’s new album, ‘Originals’

Recorded in 1982 at the late icon’s home studio on Kiowa Trail in Chanhassen, Minnesota, the track will appear on a new reissue of ‘1999’, which is out on November 29.

The track has been shared among fans for years but this is the first time it has officially been released. You can listen to it below.
