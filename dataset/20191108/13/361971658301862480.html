ASAP Rocky Announces First Show in Sweden Since Assault Conviction

The rapper was freed from Swedish jail in August with a suspended sentence

08 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Noah Yoo, Jazz Monroe

A$AP Rocky will perform in Sweden next month, returning to the country for the first time since he spent a month behind bars for assault. He’ll perform at Stockholm’s Ericsson Globe on December 11. A portion of proceeds will go to local charity FARR, the Swedish Network of Refugee Support Groups.

Rocky was detained in Stockholm this past July charged with assault, following a physical altercation on June 31. After missing several shows, he was eventually freed to return home, before being found guilty and handed a suspended sentence.
