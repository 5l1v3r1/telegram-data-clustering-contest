Kangana Ranaut stuns in gold at her brother’s engagement ceremony

08 Nov 2019, 13:31 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

All that glittered was gold for actress Kangana Ranaut as she attended her brother, Aksht Ranaut’s grand engagement ceremony. Looking effortlessly regal in a golden silk sari, the beauty channelled some old-world charm by donning a vintage pearl neckpiece.

The actress, who has long had a love affair with saris, gave us another look we can’t wait to try out during the upcoming wedding season. According to reports, Kangana picked a special Benarasi sari that she paired with a powder pink coloured blouse, which added contrast to her look.

Going by the photos shared online, Kangana also played the perfect sister by raising a toast to the couple and popping the Champaign bottle to get the celebrations started.

Sister, Rangoli Chandel also took to her Twitter handle to share a handful of special photos from the happy day that also saw the entire family posing together with the newly engaged couple.'
