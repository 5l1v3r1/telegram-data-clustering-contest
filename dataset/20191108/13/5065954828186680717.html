Want more data allocation, long validity? Go for Cherry Prepaid’s Ganda Data promos

We, Filipinos, are known for “tingi” personality which for us affords savings and value for money. And even if we are heavy internet or mobile data users, most of us, still prefer the prepaid load. We subscribe to various promos, which we believe, afford us more savings and longer validity of our mobile data.

Cherry Prepaid knows exactly what we need with its newly launched Ganda Promos, a “leveled-up” version of the Cherry Prepaid (CM) Surf.

Powered by Globe Telecom and Cherry Mobile, Cherry Prepaid’s Ganda Promos are the company’s most affordable data, text, and call promos.

“Since Cherry Prepaid’s inception in 2015, we have activated more than 5 million subscribers nationwide. Our goal for Cherry Prepaid is to provide Filipinos with a diversified prepaid mobile service from data, call and text packages at affordable prices allowing them to be connected every day of the year. Our CMSURF packages data connectivity given is long validity. Now with new “Ganda Promos”, we are able to further enhance data experience Cherry Prepaid subscribers by providing even more data allocation with the same long validity Cherry Prepaid promos are known for,” shared Jacy Chua, Chief Finance Officer of Cherry Mobile Communications Incorporated’s (CMCI).

The Ganda Promos are said to be the upgraded versions of their top data promos. It is comprised of three packages: Ganda15, Ganda30 and Ganda60.

Stay updated with the latest happenings on Facebook with Ganda15 which has 250MB for 3days for only Php15.

Users can also browse through the internet and answer emails and messages on the go with Ganda30 that comes with 550MB consumable in 7 days for just Php30.

Lastly, they can watch the latest trending video on YouTube, stream their favorite songs on Spotify, or play day and night on Mobile Legends when they register to Ganda60 which has 1.3GB worth of data valid for 15 days for only Php60.

Also introduced during the launch is Cherry Prepaid Ganda Data promos ambassador, the well-loved TV host and comedian Vice Ganda.

“I am happy to be part of Cherry Prepaid family. The three promos were specifically created for the Filipinos so they can enjoy surfing the internet longer at very affordable prices. Sulit di ba? Vice Ganda quipped.

“Cherry Prepaid users can now enjoy bigger data and longer validity at an affordable price. We are extremely excited to have Vice Ganda as Cherry Prepaid’s newest ambassador as we feel Vice best embodies the value that the “Ganda Promos” represents, which is SULIT!”, Chua concluded.
