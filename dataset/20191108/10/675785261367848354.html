BREAKING Germany mine collapse: Up to 30 workers trapped underground after explosion

A major rescue operation is underway after the explosion left about 30 workers trapped - including two who are injured - at the Teutschenthal facility, near the town of Halle, Germany

08 Nov 2019, 10:16 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

About 30 workers are trapped in a coal mine following an explosion.

Two people have been injured in the blast at the Teutschenthal facility, near the town of Halle in eastern Germany.

The trapped workers are believed to be in a safety area underground.

A major rescue operation is underway.

Halle is about 95 miles south-west of Berlin.
