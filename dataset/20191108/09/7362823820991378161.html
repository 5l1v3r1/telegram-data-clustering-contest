California homeowner finds dead woman inside house, bomb threat written on wall: police

08 Nov 2019, 09:49 by Danielle Wallace

A gated community in southern California was evacuated Thursday night after a homeowner discovered a dead woman in a pool of blood inside his house along with a message written on the wall indicating there was a bomb somewhere inside the residence, authorities said.

Officials said the home was in a gated community in Simi Valley, located in Ventura County, Fox 11 Los Angeles reported. The woman, whose identity has not been released, was pronounced dead at the scene.
