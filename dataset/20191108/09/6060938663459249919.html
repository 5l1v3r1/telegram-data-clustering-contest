Nick Jonas opens up about his special plans for his first wedding anniversary with Priyanka Chopra

08 Nov 2019, 09:23 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Power couple Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas, who got married last year at the Umaid Bhawan Palace in Jodhpur in a lavish fashion on December 2 and 3 are all set to complete one year of togetherness. While several pictures from the fairytale wedding which took place according to Christian and Indian traditions made their way on the internet leaving the fans astonished, the couple has been giving us all some major relationship goals.

Recently, during the promotions of his film 'Midway', Nick opened up about his special plans for wife Priyanka. Talking about his wedding anniversary the 'Sucker' singer revealed that he has something special planned for their first anniversary but chose not to reveal anything more in order to keep the surprise from getting spoiled.

Terming the past year like a crazy year for both of them, Nick found it hard to believe that a year has passed by so soon. Furthermore, he added that they had settled into the roles of a husband and wife naturally and called it a beautiful year. He also revealed that he wishes to spend more time with PeeCee in the coming year.

The couple has been giving us major relationship goals since the time they have come out in open about their relationship. And soon they'll be completing a year of marriage.
