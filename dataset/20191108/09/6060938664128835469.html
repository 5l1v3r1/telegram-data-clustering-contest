Watch: Salman Khan wishes Sooraj Pancholi the best for his latest film ‘Satellite Shankar’

08 Nov 2019, 09:59 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Four years after making his Bollywood debut with ‘Hero’, Sooraj Pancholi is all set to release his next film 'Satellite Shankar'. The film that has been receiving the backing of several B-town biggies, including Salman Khan, has had fans buzzing with excitement about its release in theatres today.

To share an added wish for the young hunk who he launched under his banner, Salman posted a special video to send out his best wishes to Sooraj and the team. From saying that he wished the actor could be touring with him, to coming up with a jingle and even wishing him the best, Salman played the perfect cheerleader. Joining him were actresses Sonakshi Sinha, Jacqueline Fernandez, Daisy Shah, brother Sohail Khan and brother-in-law Aayush Sharma.

The film reportedly revolves around a young soldier played by Sooraj and his special journey connecting India.

'Satellite Shankar', directed by Irfan Kamal, also stars south actress Megha Akash making her Bollywood debut.
