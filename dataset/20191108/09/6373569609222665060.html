Firefighters called to major fire involving three houses in Plympton

Smoke and flames were seen coming from the building

08 Nov 2019, 09:24 by Rom Preston-Ellis

Firefighters are at the scene of a major house fire in Plympton.

Smoke can be seen for miles around following the blaze which is reported to involve three properties in the Merafield Road area.

Devon and Somerset Fire and Rescue Service has been contacted for more information.

Police and ambulance are also on scene although there are not reported to be any injuries.
