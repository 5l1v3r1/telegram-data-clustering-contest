Oscar-winning actress Helen Mirren was quite 'flattered' when people thought she was Keanu Reeves' girlfriend

08 Nov 2019, 07:25 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Keanu Reeves recently turned up on a red carpet with his girlfriend Alexandra Grant and sent his fans into a frenzy. While fans couldn't keep calm and were all praise for their favourite star and his lady love, many thought the 'John Wick' star to be dating Oscar-winning actress Helen Mirren due to the uncanny resemblance with Grant.

Recently in an interaction with a Hollywood portal, the 74-year-old actress said that she was quite flattered as she found Grant to be lovely and even called them to be lucky to be with one another.

Reeves has always kept his personal life away from the limelight and had been working with Grant professional since a while now. The 'Matrix' star now wants to be quite open about his relationship with Grant, therefore, he didn't shy away from flaunting his love in front of the media on the red carpet recently. The couple was also spotted having dinner together in California last month.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Reeves is all set to reprise his role of Neo from 'The Matrix' in the upcoming instalment of the popular franchise. Apart from that, he is also reported to join the cast of Vin Diesel and John Cena starrer 'Fast & Furious 9'.
