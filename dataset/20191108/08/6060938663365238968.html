'Dabangg 3': The makers of Salman Khan starrer share a fun BTS video- 'Making of Chulbul Pandey' from the sets

08 Nov 2019, 08:10 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha starrer highly anticipated film 'Dabangg 3' is slated to hit the theatres in a couple of weeks. Recently, the makers released the trailer of the film and now they have dropped in a ‘Making of Chulbul Pandey’ BTS video from the sets.

The one minute forty-nine seconds video gives a glimpse of all the efforts that have gone into the making of the most beloved cop and how Salman brings Chulbul to life.
