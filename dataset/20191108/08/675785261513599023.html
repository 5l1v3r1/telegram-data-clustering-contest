Man who spent £80,000 to look like Katie Price now thinks she's 'gone too far'

Nathan Thursfield, from Stoke on Trent, now wants his face changed so he looks like Kylie Jenner instead

08 Nov 2019, 08:36 by Hayley Parker

A young man who spent £80,000 to look like his idol Katie Price is now considering tweaking himself so he resembles Kylie Jenner .

Nathan Thursfield has had numerous procedures on his face in a bid to capture the striking look of former glamour model Katie.

Although he has irreversibly changed his look and no longer resembles the fresh-faced teenager he once was, the 24-year-old has dropped Katie as his official inspiration, Stoke Sentinel reported.

Nathan feels Peter Andre's ex has tweaked herself too much, leading him towards American reality TV's Jenner instead.

 

"I used to admire Katie’s surgery journey now I fear she is going a little too far especially with her face, but she definitely looks fabulous still," he said. "I know my limits.

"I do think Kylie Jenner looks fab. Maybe I will change my face to look like hers. That would be the next outrageous thing."

 

Nathan and Katie have crossed paths before.

When Nathan appeared on Loose Women with Katie, she said he looked perfect.

Recently Nathan set off on a trip to Turkey where he was booked in to the same clinic Katie uses.

 

There he will fork out £7,000 for a smile makeover.

It will be the latest in a long list of bodily tweaks Nathan has opted for included chin fillers, cheek fillers, lip fillers, botox and liposuction on his arms and back and three nose jobs - despite medics warning it could fall off.

"I’ve had my teeth shaven down to start the treatment - they look like little baby shark teeth," he explained.

 

"It took four hours and I've got to come back for another five hours for fitting and more shaving.

"I speak to Katie, so she gave advice and told me her experience, so that’s why I chose to come here."

Nathan's advice for those considering going down the plastic surgery route is likely to ruffle the feathers of those who promote body positivity and acceptance.

 

"I must admit if there is something I dislike about myself or my body, I will change it," he said.

"I advise anyone the same when they ask me. If it bothers you that much then change it, however make sure you research surgeons and companies.

"The majority just want your money then provide the worst aftercare."

 

The health care worker raises money for his operations through TV appearances and magazine deals, including selling footage of his Las Vegas wedding to now husband Michael Gibbons for £12,000.

"I work hard so I use my own money," Nathan said.

"I don’t ask for anything of anyone, never have done. It’s my money so I spend it on what I want, I have a gorgeous home and live a comfortable lifestyle.

"I did get in debt when I was younger and naive and that funded my first nose job however I owe nobody anything now, I’m sensible with money."

In the next year Nathan will appear in a documentary about cosmetic surgery and a fitness series on MTV.
