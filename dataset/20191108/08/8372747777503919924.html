Is FSU ready for 'Prime Time'?

08 Nov 2019, 08:32 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- Potential big news involving Fort Myers native Deion Sanders.

According to sources at the NFL network, “Prime Time" is being considered for the head coaching job at Florida State.

The NFL Hall of Famer and North Fort Myers High grad played three sports at FSU in the 80s before turning pro.

Sanders has no head coaching experience at the professional or college level, but has coached high school.

The Seminoles fired Willie Taggert last weekend after just 21 games.
