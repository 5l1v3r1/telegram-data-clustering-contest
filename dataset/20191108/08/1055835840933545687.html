Spring into spring in the Snowy Mountains

08 Nov 2019, 08:20 by Edited by Nicholas Fonseca, Stellar Magazine

Winter isn’t the only time to enjoy this stunning region of Australia’s Alps. Visit during the warm-weather months for wildflowers, locally made beverages and a peaceful retreat. And what better way to see the glorious sights than with the timeless Mazda CX-3? Timeless in style, the Mazda CX-3 fits seamlessly into the modern classic backdrop of the Snowy Mountains. Zoom, zoom!  

WHEELS AND WALKS

Many travellers to the region start their journey in nearby Canberra. It’s a leisurely two-hour drive from NAMADGI NATIONAL PARK, a scenic and historically rich stretch of land boasting a wealth of Aboriginal sites (including paintings at Yankee Hat), scenic views and an arboretum. The Mazda CX-3 is the perfect car to take you from city to countryside and everywhere in between. 

If you want to make it a weekend of exploring, zoom south to Kosciuszko National Park near Thredbo and stretch your legs along ILLAWONG WALK, a 5km return trek that hugs the Snowy River valley and is popular with swimmers and birdwatchers. And with space for all of your gear, including your swimmers and binoculars, the Mazda CX-3 is the perfect companion for any road trip with advanced safety and tech features.

REST EASY 

After a busy day exploring with the Mazda CX-3, head to TINKERSFIELD, a sprawling and secluded private retreat tucked in a quiet valley at Crackenback, couples and groups are catered for across a range of accommodation, with all the 21st-century amenities you need – Nespresso machines, free wi-fi and Netflix on demand, rain showers and feather-topped king beds.

The venue also holds a regular roster of makers’ artisan classes, COOKING CLASSES and workshops throughout the year. Rooms from $250/night; visit tinkersfield.com.au to book and for more information.

RAISE A GLASS (OR A TOAST)

The Snowy Mountains is home to a selection of cold-climate vineyards, microbreweries and distilleries. Drop into WILDBRUMBY DISTILLERY for cafe meals, schnapps tastings, and a walk through the on-site sculpture gardens, which feature the work of venue founder and local artist Brad Spalding.

Drive your Mazda CX-3 about 10km up the road, and a block from gorgeous Lake Jindabyne, is KOSCIUSZKO BREWERY, which sits inside the Banjo Patterson Inn. Tours are on offer if you want to see how their signature pale ale is brewed. Just ask a staffer.

TRAVEL NOTES

Planning a camping excursion? The ever-stylish and spacious Mazda CX-3 has room to hold all your gear and is available in FWD and AWD. We drove the flagship Akari, a top-of-the-range choice with plush interiors and a 360-degree view monitor.
