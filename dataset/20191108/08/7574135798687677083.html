Crowbar’s Kirk Windstein announces new solo album, shares title track

08 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Andrew Sacher

Crowbar frontman and only constant member Kirk Windstein has officially announced his new solo album, titled Dream In Motion and due January 24 via eOne Music, and he shared its title track. Kirk recently said on Jamey Jasta’s podcast that the new album is “not an acoustic record. It’s mellow, dark music,” and added that it’s “my version of a mellow Crowbar basically.” You can definitely hear that on “Dream In Motion,” where “mellow” is being used very relatively. It pretty much sounds like if Crowbar went in a doomer direction, and it’s pretty awesome. Listen below. Also check out the tracklist, which includes a cover of Jethro Tull’s “Aqualung.”
