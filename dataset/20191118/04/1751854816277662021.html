Today, Lenovo has started taking pre-orders for the Lenovo Z6 Pro 5G smartphone on its official website and has revealed that the shipping of the smartphone will being next month, from 12th December.

The smartphone is priced at 3,299 Yuan, roughly $471 for the 8GB RAM and 256GB internal storage model. Apart from aggressive pricing, it is also the cheapest 5G smartphone model in the Chinese market.

Lenovo Z6 Pro 5G smartphone supports full 5G Netcom from three major network operators in China and supports 5G./4G dual SIM dual standby. The smartphone features a 6.39-inch Full HD+ screen resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels.

The notched screen offers an aspect ratio of 19.5:9. The display is integrated with a sixth-generation in-display fingerprint reader which even works with wet fingers. Lenovo Z6 Pro’s display is equipped with features like DCI-P3 wide color gamut, HDR10, blue light filter and full-range DC dimming.

Under the hood, the device is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 octa-core chipset, which is coupled with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of internal storage. On the front side, the device comes with a 32-megapixel AI powered selfie camera with f/2.0 aperture.

There’s AI Hyper Video quad camera setup on the back that consists of a 48-megapixel primary sensor with f/1.8 aperture, a 16-megapixel super wide-angle lens with 125-degree field-of-view, an 8-megapixel telephoto lens with f/2.4 aperture and a 2-megapixel super video camera with f/1.8 aperture, 2.9micron pixel size and 6-axis OIS.

The smartphone comes with L1 + L5 dual-frequency GPS technology for accurate location tracking. Connectivity options on the device include dual-SIM support, 5G, 4G VoLTE, Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth 5.0, and 3.5mm headphone jack.

It runs Android 9 Pie operating system out-of-the-box with the company’s own ZUI custom user interface on top. It is also optimized for better gaming experience and comes with features like Game Turbo game acceleration. There’s also Coldfront liquid cooling technology regulates the internal temperature of the device. The device is powered by a 4,000mAh battery with support for 27W fast charging through USB-C port.
