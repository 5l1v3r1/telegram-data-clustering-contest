PBOC sets Yuan reference rate at 7.0037 vs. Friday’s 7.0091

18 Nov 2019, 01:16 by Dhwani Mehta

On Monday, China’s central bank, the People's Bank of China (PBOC), set the Yuan reference rate at 7.0037 versus Friday’s fix at 7.0091.

The PBOC injected CNY 180bn via 7-day Reverse Repo while no Reverse Repos matured today.

Meanwhile, the Chinese central bank announced a small cut to its 7-day Reverse Repo rate to 2.5% vs. 2.55% previous. This marked the first reduction in the short-term liquidity tool in more than four years.
