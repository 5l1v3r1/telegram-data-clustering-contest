WATCH: King Willliam’s Town’s Luyolo Yiba takes Idols SA crown

18 Nov 2019, 05:24 by Algoa FM

The Eastern Cape’s Luyolo Yiba emerged triumphant at the finale of the 15th season of Idols SA at Carnival City yesterday.

The Kings William's Town youngster beat Snenhlanhla Msomi for the top spot during a spectacular finale.

Yiba has walked away with prizes worth over R1.5 million and a record deal to get his career off to the best start possible.

Congrats Luyolo Yiba – you have done the Eastern Cape proud!
