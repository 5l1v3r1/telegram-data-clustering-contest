'Pagalpanti': The new teaser of John Abraham and Ileana D'cruz starrer will surely tickle your funny bone

18 Nov 2019, 05:37 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ahead of the film's release, the makers of 'Pagalpanti' are sharing a few teasers from the past couple of days to mark the countdown. The film will be hitting the theatres this Friday. Recently, Ileana D'cruz took to her Twitter profile to share another funny teaser of the film.

The actress shared the teaser and wrote, "Inki shakal pe mat jaao, jitne seedhe dikhte hai yeh... utne hai nahi! Karne aa rahe hai aapko bhi pagal, 4 dino mein!"
