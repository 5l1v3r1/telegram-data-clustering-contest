Giant, inflatable poo attracts crowd in Portland, will stay through World Toilet Day

18 Nov 2019, 05:49 by KATU News

PORTLAND, Ore. – If the arrival of the Christmas Tree was the biggest thing happening in Pioneer Courthouse Square this weekend, you could say this was “number two.”

A giant, inflatable poo emoji was set up in Portland’s Living Room while the company Poo-Pourri made a stop in Portland on its tour around the U.S.

On its tour, the company is raising money for World Toilet Day, an official United Nations observance day that raises awareness for the global sanitation crisis.

Anyone could brave an entry into the inflatable poop for free. For every visitor, Poo-Pourri will donate $2 toward safe sanitation.

Tuesday is the last day to visit the inflatable poo in Portland.
