Pope Skinny Believes The Award Shatta Wale Gifted Stonebwoy Has A Hidden Motive

18 Nov 2019, 05:55 by BB Jones

One of the highlights of the 2019 4Syte Music Video Awards was when Shatta Wale gifted his Most Popular Song award to his former rival, Stonebwoy.

His move has widely been applauded because it goes a long way to foster the cordial relationship they have established.

Stonebwoy was also impressed with the gesture as he also got down from the stage to hug Shatta Wale when he was performing.

This excited the audience and the internet is still talking about it. However, rapper Pope Skinny is desperately reading between the lines of that harmless gesture.

To him, there could be a hidden agenda behind Wale’s move and has therefore (well, subtly) warned Stonebwoy to be careful whiles addressing Blade and Bone, two Militants who were sacked by Wale.

Pope Skinny wrote;

He wrote:

“Success delayed is not success denied. Blade and Bone, I wish you guys well. Very soon Good over Evil and Stone go join you guys. May God grant us long life Itz a movie. The world go watch am live.”
