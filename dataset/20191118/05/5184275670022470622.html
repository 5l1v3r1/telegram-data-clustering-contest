Good Newwz NEW Poster: Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh & Kiara Advani ‘Goofed Up’ With A Sperm

The new poster follows a similar colourful theme as the earlier ones.

18 Nov 2019, 05:35 by Koimoi.com Team

Good Newwz starring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani is one of the most awaited comedy-dramas. While the makers are all set to release the trailer of the film today (November 18), they have released a new poster of the film that features the four bounded by a sperm and you cannot miss it.

The new poster follows a similar colourful theme as the earlier ones. Dressed in vibrant clothes, the actors can be seen sharing a group hug while they are being huddled by a sperm. The catch has to be how the CGI sperm is given a devil look with thorn-like year and Dracula teeth and an evil grin. This is only adding to the anticipation.

Another thing to notice amongst the posters is Akshay and Kareena’s confused expressions which have also left the audience confused about what the goof-up is exactly. Diljit and Kiara are all smiles and beautiful.

The posters that had come out before had Akshay and Diljit stuck between the two bellies have already given rise t many speculations about the plot of the film.

Karan Johar shared the poster and wrote, “Intertwined by the biggest goof-up & there’s no escape! #GoodNewwzTrailer out at noon.”
