Let me preface this by saying that I bloody love summer and I love the beach even more. I’m very Aussie about it too. I’m down for all the barbecues and all the Gaytimes and all the late afternoon sunburn, all of it. Count me in. Australian summer – what a time to be alive. 

What I’ve never been a big fan of, however, is the whole “it’s almost summer, better get your beach body ready” mentality. Nup. No thanks. The best, most perfect beach body is the one you’ve got. Just like the best camera is the one in your hand and the best lover is the one holding your other hand (so stop thinking about your ex already, geez). 

I’m done with dreading the annual swimwear shop. And the thing is, we don’t need to dread it anymore. Whether you call ’em swimmers or bathers, cozzies or togs, there’s a style or fabric out there to fit every body type. Jiggly bits and all.
