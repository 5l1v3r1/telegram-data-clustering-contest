KAPITAL Drops Classic Leather Bandana Slip-On

A sleek footwear essential.

18 Nov 2019, 03:17 by Charlie Zhang

After dropping its half-quilted cardigans recently, KAPITAL has returned with its latest set of its bandana prints, this time set on classic slip-on loafers.

Coming in colorways of red and black, the shoes are glossed in an intricate bandana pattern that covers all of the uppers. Edged with thin tonal piping, it’s slip-on construction begins at the upper collars where a slight arch at the tongue has been made. The back of the shoe sees rich paisley patterns, while the front is emblazoned with other traditional shapes and motifs. Complementing its raised heel, the silhouette sees an angular almond toe that gives the footwear piece a sleek finish. As a final touch, the insole has been embossed with gold-trimmed “KAPITAL” branding alongside a spread of tiny paisley motifs.

The KAPITAL Leather Bandana Slip-Ons are currently available at www.kapital-webshop.jpfor $422 USD.
