Joaquin Phoenix starrer Joker is cited as one of the best films ever made. Directed by Todd Phillips, the released in October this year and opened to rave reviews from the audience and the critics. While a lot of people have many things to say about Joker, Friends show actor Matthew Perry has his own take, a hilarious one.

Matthew Perry shared a meme which showed the similarity between Joker and Chandler’s dance steps. The post had ‘Cinematic Parallels’ written on it. The Friends actor shared the meme and captioned it, “You’re welcome.”
