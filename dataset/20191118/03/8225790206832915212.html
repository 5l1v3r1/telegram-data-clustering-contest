Flood advisory canceled for Windward Oahu; flash flood watch still posted

18 Nov 2019, 03:36 by Ben Gutierrez

HONOLULU, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) - The National Weather Service has canceled a flood advisory that was posted for portions of Windward Oahu from Kahana through Waikane and Waiahole to Heeia.

The Weather Service office said at 5:08 p.m., radar indicated heavy rain near Kualoa Ranch, with rain falling at a rate to 1 to 2 inches per hour.

The advisory was canceled shortly after 7 p.m. after the heavy rain ended.

The threat of more heavy rain still exists. A flash flood watch remains posted statewide through 6 p.m. Monday.
