Faiz Festival’s final day all about dissecting genres of poetry

18 Nov 2019, 03:15 by Muhammad Shahzad

LAHORE: Marsiya, a genre of Urdu poetry can trace its origins to the subcontinent, said an expert on Urdu language, literature, and poet Dr Arfa Zehra.

She expressed these views during a session titled, ‘Rahnai sai jo main karta kaghaz pe qalam – Shairi ki Asnaf’ at the final day of Faiz International Festival 2019. The session was moderated by Qasim Jafri.
