Aparshakti Khurana Reveals His Birthday Plans & It’s Going To Be Hectic!

Aparshakti Khurana, who turns a year older today reveals his birthday plans.

18 Nov 2019, 03:22 by IANS

Actor Aparshakti Khurana, who turns a year older on Monday, will be spending his birthday shooting for his upcoming film. The Stree actor revealed his birthday plans and it is a busy day for him with a little bit of celebration with one of the important people in his life.

“I believe that birthdays should be spent doing what you love and I love working. My wife Aakriti will be coming to Ludhiana on the shoot location, so that makes my birthday perfect. I will be doing what I love and being with people who I love. I cannot ask for more,” the Luka Chuppi actor said.

Aparshakti Khurana will also be seen in “Pati Patni Aur Woh“, which is scheduled to release on December 6. The film stars Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday in the lead roles. The film is helmed by Muddassar Aziz and produced by T-Series.

He was most recently seen in “Kanpuriye“, which is an anthology of three fast-paced stories capturing the struggles of three smalltown boys, who are chasing big city dreams. Aparshakti received a lot of praises for his performance in Kanpuriye and people got to see a different side of the actor in it.

He also had a cameo in his brother Ayushmann Khurrana starrer Bala which released recently and is doing quite well at the box office. Directed by Amar Kaushik, Bala also stars Yami Gautam and Bhumi.

Well, we wish the actor a very Happy Birthday and hope that he does more and more films and keeps entertaining us!
