Don’t come to my shrine for money rituals if you’re below 18 years – Nana Agradaa warns

18 Nov 2019, 08:02 by Adu Sarpeah

Living in Africa with Ghana not being an exception to the reality can be linked to the illusion believers have about hell as the politicians and all those in authority continue to make things difficult for the citizens.

As a result, majority of the citizenry especially the youths have devised ways and means to get financial freedom with money rituals being part of the short cuts to financial freedom. In Ghana for instance, Nana Agradaa is one of the well-known traditional priests who help people have financial freedom.

But according to Nana Agradaa, irrespective of the deplorable financial status of a person, whoever is below Ghana’s voting age is not required to come to her shrine for financial assistance. To the best knowledge of the powerful traditional priestess, under 18 people don’t need a whopping amount of money for anything.

Nana Agradaa issued this strong warning in the studios of Thunder television yesterday, November 17, 2019 when she was responding to the popular perception that she’s being duping those who come her shrine at Sowutoum for help.
