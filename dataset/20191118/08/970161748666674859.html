EastEnders admission, Corrie burglary and 8 more soap spoilers

18 Nov 2019, 08:00

It’s another intense week of drama in soapland, as Max Branning (Jake Wood) readies himself to propose to Ruby Allen (Louisa Lytton) in EastEnders, while Daniel Osbourne (Rob Mallard) bids farewell to his beloved Sinead Tinker (Katie McGlynn) in Coronation Street.

In Emmerdale, David Metcalfe (Matthew Wolfenden) faces a horrifying reality, while in Hollyoaks, Ste Hay (Kieron Richardson) faces a terrifying ordeal.

Ben makes an admission in EastEnders

When Martin learns that Callum wants to join the police, he proceeds to wind Ben up about it. As a result, Ben ends up admitting to Callum that he’s not sure if they could remain together if Callum was to become an officer of the law — a revelation which leaves Callum upset.

One to watch: Monday 18thNovember at 8pm on BBC One

Daniel is devastated after burglary in Coronation Street 

Daniel returns to Weatherfield just in time for Sinead’s funeral. However, after the service he decides to heads off back to the flat and watch the videos she recorded for Bertie — but little does he know he’s being burgled, and the thief makes off with the laptop featuring Sinead’s videos.

One to watch: Monday 18thNovember at 7:30pm on ITV

Lydia receives devastating news in Emmerdale

Lydia’s devastated when long-lost mum Agatha reveals that her father died of Huntington’s disease, which means that there’s a chance Lydia could have the same thing. After giving it some thought, Lydia shares this news with her family, but she remains unsure of whether or not she should take the Huntington’s test.

One to watch:

Stuart plans to kill Ste in Hollyoaks

Sid is left speechless when he overhears Stuart admitting that he has a plan to kill Ste. Later, Jonny takes Ste to a taxi that’s waiting in The Dog carpark, but he’s horrified when she realises that the driver of said vehicle is none other than Stuart.

One to watch: Friday 15th November at 7pm on E4.

Max proposes to Ruby in EastEnders

 Max decides that he’s going to propose to Ruby in an attempt to prove just how serious he is about her. However, when Ian gets wind of the situation, he ensures everyone is there to witness Max’s big moment, which leaves him with no other choice to carry on and propose. But what will Ruby have to say?

One to watch: Friday 22ndNovember at 8pm on BBC One

Paul reveals the truth in Coronation Street

When Bernie and Gemma do some investigating in Kel’s flat, they find not only Daniel’s laptop but they also find photos of a younger Paul. Paul is horrified to discover what they’ve found, but he ends up discussing his memories of Kel, before revealing how he sexually abused him when he was a teenager. Bernie and Gemma are shocked at what Paul’s said, and Gemma encourages him to go to the police.

David learns that Maya has been released in Emmerdale

David and Leyla are left shell-shocked when they learn that Maya has been released from prison. They deliberate over what to do for the best, but they eventually agree to inform Jacob. He’s composed upon learning the news, but when he disappears, David and Leyla are worried he’s gone to meet with Maya.

One to watch:

Jack is forced to intervene in EastEnders

 Jack finds himself caught in the middle of a high-octane situation in a courtroom, and is forced to make an arrest as a result. But just who is it that he puts in cuffs?

One to watch: Thursday 21stNovember at 7:30pm on BBC One.

Ollie is left heartbroken in Hollyoaks

Ollie’s left heartbroken when Brooke informs him that she wants Sienna to attend the next scan with her instead of him. However, Sienna knows that something is up — but she’s later distracted with joy when she realises that the baby is a boy.

One to watch: Wednesday 20th November at 7pm on E4. 

Michael’s secret comes to light in Coronation Street

When James doesn’t return home, Ed thinks he’s spent the night with Bethany — much to Aggie and Michael’s dismay. However, when James does show up, he reveals to Michael that he spent the night with a man named Dan — and that he’s lost his phone. Michael agrees to help him find it, but upon doing so, he encounters his ex, Grace — and his daughter!

One to watch: Wednesday 20thNovember at 8:30pm on ITV
