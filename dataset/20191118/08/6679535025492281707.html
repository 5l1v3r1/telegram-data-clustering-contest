Youth arrested for stabbing young woman near Chidambaram

The 18-year-old victim, who is undergoing treatment in hospital, had turned down the man’s proposal

18 Nov 2019, 08:14 by Special Correspondent

An 18-year-old young woman was stabbed in the neck by a young man after she turned down his proposal for marriage in Keezha Veethi near Chidambaram on Saturday.

The accused, R .Sakthivel, 22, a resident of Kalamarudhur, has been arrested and remanded to judicial custody. The young woman, a resident of Kattandikuppam near Panruti was working along with the accused in a chips company in Keezha Veethi and the two had been friends for a long time, police said.

According to police, the accused barged into the house where the young woman was staying with her grandmother near Chidambaram, and picked up an argument with her. He then stabbed her in the neck after she rejected his marriage proposal.

The young woman belongs to the Vanniyar community and the accused to the Dalit community, police said.

The young woman is undergoing treatment at the Raja Muthiah Medical College and Hospital. Her condition is stated to be stable.
