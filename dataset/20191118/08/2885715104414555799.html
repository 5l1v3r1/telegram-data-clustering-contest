Watch: Spinner Gregory Mahlokwana bowls left and right handed to take wickets

During a T20 match between Cape Town and Durban Heat in the ongoing Mzansi Super League, Gregory Mahlokwana surprised everyone by picking up wickets with each hand. 

18 Nov 2019, 08:26 by Sports Desk

Days after Sri Lankan leggie Kevin Koththigoda grabbed eyeballs with his bizarre bowling action, Cape Town Blitz’s spinner Gregory Mahlokwana is taking the internet by storm with his ambidextrous bowling skills. During a T20 match between Cape Town and Durban Heat in the ongoing Mzansi Super League, Mahlokwana surprised everyone by picking up wickets bowling with his left and right hand.

The all-rounder first dismissed opener Sarel Erwee on 16 in the eighth over while bowling with the right-hand. He then dismantled the stumps of Durban skipper Dane Vilas in the tenth over, while bowling with left.
