Heroin worth €195,000 seized in Co Limerick

18 Nov 2019, 08:50 by Newstalk

Heroin worth an estimated €195,000 has been seized in Co Limerick.

Yesterday officers searched a derelict property in the Ardkilmartin area of Kilmallock.

The drugs were discovered during the operation and have now been removed for a technical examination.

No arrests were made as part of yesterday's search operation, and gardaí say their investigations are continuing.

Anyone who has any information in relation to the seizure is now being asked to contact Bruff Garda Station on 061-382-940 or the Garda Confidential Line on 1800-666-111.
