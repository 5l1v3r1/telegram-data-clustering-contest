Bello inches closer to second term - The Nation Newspaper

18 Nov 2019, 08:52 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

Barring any last-minute exigencies, Governor Yahaya Bello is set to be declared winner of the governorship election in Kogi state.

The governor has extended his lead, winning the remaining two local government areas of Ibaji and Lokoja that were announced some moments ago.
