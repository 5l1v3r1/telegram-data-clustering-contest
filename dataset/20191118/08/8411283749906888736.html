Cleanroom constructor UIS posts profit surge

18 Nov 2019, 08:01 by Jessie Shen, DIGITIMES, Taipei

Taiwan-based United Information Systems (UIS) has reported net profit surged 44.3% from a year earlier to NT$2.03 billion (US$66.7 million) in the first three quarters of 2019. EPS for the nine-month period came to NT$10.67.

UIS disclosed its cumulative 2019 revenues through October climbed 46.7% from a year ago to NT$18.86 billion, which already surpassed the NT$18.13 billion posted for all of 2018.

UIS is set to generate record-high revenues in 2019, according to market watchers. TSMC and Micron Taiwan are reportedly among the cleanroom constructor's major customers.

In other news, UIS had NT$58.77 billion worth of orders on hand as of the end of September 2019, as much as 127% higher than that of the orders received during the same period last year, according to a report by Taiwan's Central News Agency (CNA). UIS is expected to enjoy another year of record-high sales in 2020, the report quoted market watchers as indicating.
