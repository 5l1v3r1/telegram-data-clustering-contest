Rob Thomas Honours Late INXS Frontman Michael Hutchence With Surprise Appearance By Bandmate Andrew Farriss

18 Nov 2019, 08:12 by Corey Atad

It’s been almost 22 years since INXS frontman Michael Hutchence passed away, and on Saturday night, singer Rob Thomas found a beautiful way to pay tribute to him.

At his show in Sydney, Australia, the Matchbox Twenty singer surprised fans by bringing out INXS band member Andrew Farriss.

The two performed a duet of INXS’ classic hit “Never Tear Us Apart”, leading the audience in a singalong, Variety reported.
