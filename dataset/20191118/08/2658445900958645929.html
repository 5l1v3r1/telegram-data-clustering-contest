Kogi: Power outage disrupts collation of results  - The Nation Newspaper

18 Nov 2019, 08:30 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

There was power outage at the INEC headquarters in Lokoja where the final collation of results for the governorship election is taking place.

The generating set being used suddenly went off when collation of the results from Lokoja was going on.

The engineers are battling to get it working, while collation is put on hold.
