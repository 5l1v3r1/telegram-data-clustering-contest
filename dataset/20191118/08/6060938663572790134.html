'Housefull 4' box office collection day 24: Akshay Kumar's film continues to battle it out with Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Bala' and Sidharth Malhotra's 'Marjaavaan'

18 Nov 2019, 08:55 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The fourth instalment of the popular 'Housefull' franchise has been making headlines since its inception and now that the film has released and is in its fourth week, it still continues to weave its magic.

The Farhad Samji directorial which stars Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Bobby Deol, Kriti Sanon, Pooja Hegde, Kriti Kharbanda and others in pivotal roles has managed to mint another Rs 1.40 crore on its fourth Sunday, taking its total fourth-weekend collection to Rs 2.60 crore.

With a staggering figure of Rs 135.86 crore in the first week itself, 'Housefull 4' has gone on to collect Rs 48.07 crore in its second week, Rs 16.25 crore in the third week and has reached a total of Rs 202.78 crore in twenty-four days.

'Housefull 4' has now become Akshay's highest nett grosser surpassing the collections of '2.0' and Mission Mangal. With a drop of more than 70% since last weekend, the report further suggests that the film may reach Rs 205-207 crore but would depend on how 'Pagalpanti' fares as well.
