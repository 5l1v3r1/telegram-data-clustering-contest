Coronation Street fans stunned by Adam Barlow's new haircut

Adam has had several very different styles this year

18 Nov 2019, 20:53 by Sophie McCoid

Coronation Street fans were stunned tonight as Adam Barlow showcased another new haircut.

This year Adam, played by Sam Robertson, has had several different styles - with viewers liking some more than others.

Tonight Adam had a very different look, with shaved sides and his dark hair in a quiff style, as he spoke to Michelle about "taking Robert to the cleaners."

And fans had a lot to say about Adam's new style in tonight's episode.

Some fans loved Adam's new look and took to social media in their droves to comment on it.

Ryan Love said: "Adam Barlow had a haircut, THANK YOU LORD."

Kelly Ford said: "Adam Barlows new haircut !! he’s bloody beautiful!"

And Jess said: "I’m sorry but Adam Barlow with that hair cut (drooling emoji)

But others thought the new style was a mistake. 

Tracy Davidson said: "What has Adam done to his hair? No no no!"

Fairylight said: "What on earth has Adam done to his hair."

And Laura said: "I think adam is gorgeous but what the hell is that hair."
