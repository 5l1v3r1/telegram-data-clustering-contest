How old is Kate Garraway, who is she married to and what is her GMB salary?

18 Nov 2019, 20:10

Kate Garraway has got off to a tricky start in I’m A Celebrity 2019 after being chosen along with Caitlyn Jenner for the first Bushtucker trial of the series.

Having already been visibly terrified after facing the show’s opening ‘walk the plank’ challenge, Kate will now have to survive a box load of cockroaches in her bid to win meals for her fellow campmates on Monday night.

It’s all a far cry from her usual job as Good Morning Britain presenter, who reportedly almost pulled out of taking part – not to mention her life away from the screen.

Here’s what you need to know about Kate.

How old is Kate Garraway?

Kate was born on 4 June 1967, making her 52 years old.

She made reference to her age as she was introduced to the viewers on Sunday night’s I’m A Celeb, saying: ‘I am 52, and I just want to do everything I can, try everything, be a bit braver and just push myself a bit more.’

Kate began her career as a presenter on local news on ITV before joining Sky News Sunrise and then GMTV (the forerunner of Good Morning Britain), co-hosting alongside the likes of Eamonn Holmes and Ben Shephard among others.

She also appeared on ITV breakfast show Daybreak, while her other TV appearances have included Lorraine, The National Lottery Draws, Wall Of Fame and The Biggest Loser – and she’s a regular face on the panel of Loose Women.

Who is Kate Garraway married to and does she have children?

Kate is married to former lobbyist and political advisor Derek Draper, whom she wed in 2005.

Derek is her second husband, with the presenter previously being married to her former ITV Meridian boss Ian Rumsey from 1998-2002.

Kate and Derek have two children, daughter Darcey who was born in 2006, and son Bill, who was born in 2009.

She recently revealed that it was her children who spurred her on to take part in I’m A Celebrity, telling Hello!: ‘It’s all their fault. Darcey really got into the last couple of series and kept saying: “You should so do this Mum,” and things like “You should eat that slug from the garden, or jump off of that wall to practise.”. It’s been a war of attrition.’

What is Kate Garraway’s Good Morning Britain salary and net worth?

According to the website Celebworth.net Kate’s Good Morning Britain wage is around £540,000.

She’s also estimated to have a net worth of around £1.5m.

According to reports she’s also pocketing a tidy £75,000 for her part in I’m A Celebrity.
