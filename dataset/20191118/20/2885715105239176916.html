Mumbai: Mother, her son held for killing 23-yr-old woman

Police said the victim, Nirmala Waghela, was in a relationship with a man who her mother Paapu and brother Aakash did not approve of.

18 Nov 2019, 20:44 by Express News Service

The Mumbai Police arrested a 40-year-old woman and her son for allegedly killing her 23-year-old daughter in their hutment in Phydonie on Sunday.

Police said the victim, Nirmala Waghela, was in a relationship with a man who her mother Paapu and brother Aakash did not approve of.

An officer said there have been past confrontations wherein Nirmala was asked to leave her boyfriend. “But on Sunday, when Nirmala packed her clothes and was about to leave with her boyfriend, there was a fight between the mother and daughter, and the two (mother and son) strangulated Nirmala with a dupatta,” an officer said.

After Nirmala collapsed, the two accused realised she was dead, following which they went to the police station
to surrender.
