Bahawalpur: Two Indians arrested for entering Pakistan without travel documents

18 Nov 2019, 20:29 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

BAHAWALPUR (Dunya News) – Authorities on Monday have arrested two Indian citizens from Bahawalpur’s Yazman Mandi for illegally entering Pakistan.

According to details, the Indians, identified as Parshant and Vari Lal, were apprehended from dessert area in a charge of crossing the border without passports and other travel documents.

Moreover, investigation into the matter has been launched after filing first information report (FIR) against the detainees.
