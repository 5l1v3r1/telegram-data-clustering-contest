Lawyers resume work at courts

Protest over shifting of High Court will continue, says bar association secretary

18 Nov 2019, 20:07 by Special Correspondent

Lawyers who launched an indefinite strike on October 21, seeking the establishment of permanent High Court in the city resumed their works at courts on Monday.

The general body of Visakhapatnam Bar Association took a decision to call off the strike to prevent inconvenience caused to the litigants. However, The association has resolved to continue the strike in a peaceful manner.

Relay fast tomorrow

“We will launch a relay fast on Wednesday as per the decision taken at the general body meeting of the association. The lawyers will also stage dharnas and take out rallies to press for the demand,” VBA secretary A. Kondababu said.
