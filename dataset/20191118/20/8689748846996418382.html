I Never Had Intercourse With Akuapem Poloo – Bro Sammy Debunks Rumors

18 Nov 2019, 20:05 by vanessa nordzi

Controversial Ghanaian gospel musician, Brother Sammy has denied allegations that he had sex  with Akuapem Poloo when they were on set for the “Baby Mama” movie in Kumasi.

Speaking on the matter during an interview with Zion Felix on Radio Univers’ ‘Brunch2Lunch Entertainment’ show, he quizzed why he didn’t have an affair with any of the characters but only Akuapem Poloo clearly stating that he never had any intimacy with her.

“Everybody wake up and say whatever they want in Ghana. There were many women on the set so why will they make this allegation of me sleeping with only Akuapem Poloo? Somebody is making this up and if you don’t take care, you will commit a sin. There is no truth in that,” he stated.

Brother Sammy is currently preparing towards upcoming launch of his  ‘Aduro Biaa Nkadem’ album at the Dichemso Assemblies of God Church in Kumasi on Sunday, November 24, 2019.
