Scott Frost Not Happy With Nebraska’s ‘Leadership’ This Season

18 Nov 2019, 20:06 by Matt Hladik

Nebraska’s potential breakout season never materialized. The Huskers are 4-6 on the year and need to win their final two games to reach a bowl.

There have been several reasons for Nebraska’s struggles. Today, head coach Scott Frost took aim at his team’s leadership, or lack thereof.

While meeting with the media this afternoon, Frost made some pointed remarks about leadership not being “where we needed it to be” because a few people (presumably players) are “working against that.”
