Airstrikes kill 21 Taliban, ISIS militants; destroy car bombs in 6 provinces

18 Nov 2019, 20:42 by Khaama Press

A series of airstrikes killed 21 militants of Taliban and ISIS Khurasan militants and destroyed two car bombs in six provinces in the past 24 hours.

The military officials said earlier today an airstrike killed 6 Taliban militants and destroyed a weapons cache in Zurmat district of Paktiya.

The officials further added that a similar airstrike killed 6 ISIS Khurasan militants in Achin district of Nangarhar while another airstriked 2 ISIS Khurasan militants in Deh Bala district of the province.

Separate airstrikes killed 3 Taliban militants in Baraki Barak district of Logar and Sharan district of Paktika, the officials said, adding that an airstrike killed 3 more Taliban militants in Marjah district of Helmand.

The security forces also conducted airstrikes in Sherzad district of Nangarhar and Daymirdad district of Wardak which destroyed two car bombs belonging to Taliban and ISIS Khursan groups, the officials added.
