The Grey Part Of Blue: Suhana Khan With Her Acting Debut Proves Her Magnificent Acting Roots Come Straight From Dad Shah Rukh Khan

From her expressions to her accent, the star kid manages it all effortlessly and isn’t that the sign of a star?

18 Nov 2019, 09:51 by Koimoi.com Team

While star kids always remain under the radar of the netizens, Shah Rukh Khan’s children Aryan Khan and Suhana Khan have been often in the news regarding their Bollywood debuts. That may still remain unknown, but Suhana just made her acting debut with a short film titled as The Grey Part Of Blue. All we can start of is to talk about the in-built acting roots coming straught from father Shah Rukh Khan.

The short film, The Grey Part Of Blue, which spans over a period of 10 minutes and 4 seconds witnesses Suhana in the character of Sandy. Sandy can be seen travelling in a car along with boyfriend and is super excited to introduce him to her parents. However, things take a toll when they gradually realize that their relationship has met a roadblock and cannot be worked upon any further.
