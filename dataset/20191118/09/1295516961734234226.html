The Boys Showrunner Announces End of Filming on Season 2

18 Nov 2019, 09:11 by John Ridlehoover

The Boys showrunner Eric Kripe has confirmed that filming on Season 2 has ended.

"2:15 am. That’s a wrap on #TheBoysTV #season2," Kripe posted on Twitter with an image of himself holding a beer. "Exhausted but triumphant. Fucking love this cast & crew. Wait till you see the madness. Coming Summer 2020."
