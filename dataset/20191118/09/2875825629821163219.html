I'm A Celeb fans joke Kris Jenner will vote ex Caitlyn to do Bushtucker Trials

I'm A Celeb memes of Kris Jenner rushing to vote for Caitlyn to do all the Bushtucker Trials exploded on social media

18 Nov 2019, 09:39 by Irishmirror.ie

I'm A Celebrity fans were amused over the idea of Kris Jenner voting for ex Caitlyn to do the Bushtucker Trials.

Viewers at home were roaring with laughter creating memes of the Kardashian rushing to put in not one - but five - allotted votes online for her ex to do the grisly trials.

Some fans were in stitches about Kris' reaction to being unable to vote over the phone because she is based in the US.

It really tickled fans' sense of humour as they picked out a number of different Kardashian clips to best suit their tweets.

"Kris Jenner using her 5 free votes to vote Caitlyn for every single trial going on #ImACeleb ." One fan wrote, joining in on the social media fun.

Others added: " #ImACeleb Kris Jenner finding out that she can't vote for Caitlyn because she's in the USA."

"Kris Jenner calling ITV to vote Caitlyn for the trials every night #ImACeleb ."

During Monday's episode, Caitlyn is up for the first Bushtucker Trial.
