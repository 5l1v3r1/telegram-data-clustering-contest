Roxy Cinemas is hosting Frozen II fun activities for Disney fans

Frozen II is in cinemas across the UAE this week

18 Nov 2019, 09:12 by Kelly Johnstone

With the highly-anticipated release of Disney movie Frozen II across the UAE this week, Roxy Cinemas is embracing the sequel and inviting little Disney fans to do the same.

Roxy Cinemas seems to be as excited as we are here at Time Out Kids HQ about Frozen II coming to cinemas on Thursday November 21.

The boutique cinema chain is celebrating the launch of the follow-up film to the epic story about royal siblings, Anna and Elsa with a frosty line-up of fun for all the family.

Do you want to build a snowman? OK, maybe not quite literally, but Roxy Cinemas is transforming into a winter wonderland to celebrate the launch of the new animated film.

For every ticket to see Frozen II purchased, Roxy Cinemas is giving away a pass to Mattel Play! Town absolutely free.

But that's not all, anybody enjoying an afternoon trip to the cinema, before 4pm to be precise, will get a free Frozen-themed ice cream cake – sweet – between Thursday November 21 and Thursday November 28.

Plus, little birthday boys and girls can celebrate their big day in Frozen style with a themed party for super fans of all ages.

Enjoy a party with Frozen II goodies and decorations, plus little party-goers get to watch a screening of the sequel, and until Wednesday January 15 they can also tuck into tasty treats including a huge blue ice-cream cake, complete with a picture of their choice of Queen Elsa or hilarious little snowman Olaf on top.

Make sure your little ones are prepared for their cinema visit with these Frozen II must-haves.

Frozen II is in cinemas from Thu Nov 21. Let the countdown begin.
