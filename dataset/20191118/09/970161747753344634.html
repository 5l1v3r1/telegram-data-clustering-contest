My Chemical Romance seen for first time since announcing reunion gig

18 Nov 2019, 09:23

My Chemical Romance has revealed the first glimpse of the band back in the rehearsal space after announcing an anticipated reunion show.

The group – made up of Gerard and Mikey Way, Frank Iero and Ray Toro – surprised fans when they revealed they’d got the band back together for their first gig since 2012.

And no, we’re still not over it.

Now the group has whet our whistles and given us even more reason to not be ok as they shared a shot of themselves just chilling in the studio.

Taking five as they prepared for their concert, the band members could be seen chilling on the floor as they sat in a circle.

Are they mulling over adding more dates to their big comeback? Some tunes perhaps? Lyrics? Are they talking about lyrics?

The group was pretty hush hush about what was going down in this particular shot, as the photo was simply captioned: ’11.15.19.’

Still, a picture tells a thousand words and we’re lapping up every last detail of the black and white shot.

Before the group announced their reunion last month, Joe Jonas teased at their return earlier this year, and claimed that while the Jonas Brothers were rehearsing in New York, MCR were apparently there rehearsing right next to them.
