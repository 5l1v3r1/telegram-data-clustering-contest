Florida gas prices hit a 9-month low

18 Nov 2019, 09:14 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- You may want to fill up your car soon. Gas prices in Florida right now are at a 9-month low.

Florida drivers are now paying 13 cents less than we were a month ago.
