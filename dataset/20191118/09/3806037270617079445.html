UNDERCOVER Applies Intricate Details Over Gold & Silver Bangles

Available in slim and wide options.

18 Nov 2019, 09:46 by Charlie Zhang

After releasing its NOISE LAB lineup, UNDERCOVER has returned with a slew of intricately detailed jewelry for FW19.

Featured are two silhouettes: a wide bangle with the label’s signature GIZ motif and a slim bracelet that twists at the bridge. The former is etched with a double-serrated “GIZ” pattern that runs from one end of the bangle to the other. Coming in two colorways, the gold version applies shiny tones over the pattern, separated by a thin black line that runs through the middle. Its silver counterpart is debossed, colored instead with dark oxidization.

The other silhouette is the thin bracelet. Also coming in gold and silver iterations, the pieces are rounded at each end and gradually twists midway for a slim bridge. “UNDER COVER” branding is stamped at the center, finished off by “SILVER 925” engraved directly under it.

The UNDERCOVER Bangles are currently available at undercoverism.com, ranging from $318 – $433 USD.

Elsewhere, BEAMS Japan has immortalized “Tora-San” in a 50th-anniversary capsule for Otoko wa Tsurai yo.
