Authorities seize 31 kilo of ICE in Myawady

18 Nov 2019, 09:45 by Kyi Naing

 

Anti-drug squad seized over Ks2.4 billion worth of 31 kilo of ICE in No.5 Ward in Myawady on November 16, according to announcement from Myanmar police force on November 17.

According to a tip-off, the police pretended to buy the narcotics drugs from Tangyi, who live in Thingannyinaung Ywarthit, Myawady, and he took them to a house owned by Lu Maw in Myawady. The police didn’t find the house owner and they found 31 kilo of ICE in the house.

The police are making further investigation in concerned with the seizure of the narcotics drug.
