Male fans of Efia Odo rush on her for pictures in her see-through dress

18 Nov 2019, 09:27 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

The much-awaited 4stye Music Video awards came off last Saturday where Shatta Wale, Stonebwoy and other acts won big on the night with electrifying performances to accompany it.

On the night, most of our Ghanaian celebrities were spotted rocking their best attire for the awards night but actress cum television presenter Efia Odo made most of the patrons especially guys at the event looked on in shock.

Efia appeared on the red carpet in a see through dress but before her appearance, some guys believed to be area boys rushed on her to take photos with her.

In the video, all the guys wanted to touch Efia Odo or to stand by her side.
