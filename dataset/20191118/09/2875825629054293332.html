Denise Welch celebrates Katie Hopkins' downfall as disgraced star loses £1m home

Denise didn't hold back when she offered no sympathy up to the humiliated Katie Hopkins following her downfall

18 Nov 2019, 09:10 by Irishmirror.ie

Denise Welch triumphed in Katie Hopkins' fall from the top.

Outspoken Katie was forced to sell off her £1million Devon home and now rents following a humiliating court case.

The far-right activist was obliged to sell up after she slapped with a £500,000 legal bill when writer Jack Monroe sued her for libel and won.

Her controversial views, which have seen her sacked from two lucrative newspaper columns and LBC radio show, have made her unpopular.

In the light of the news, Denise couldn't resist digging the knife in further with a scathing tweet.

Thick with sarcasm, the telly star was unable to offer up any sympathy to Katie.

Sharing The Mirror's article, she wrote: "Poor old Katie Hopkins. Couldn't have happened to a nicer person."

It comes after Katie confessed speaking her mind had lost her career in an interview with The Sunday Times .

"I live in a rented, modern, four-bedroom detached house with my husband Mark and our three children, aged 15, 14 and 10," she said.

"It's tucked out of the way for security reasons - our last home was rigged up to the local police station and had panic alarms fitted because of threats I received to my life.

"I was forced to sell it for £950,000 in early 2018 to pay off a legal bill in excess of £500,000."

Katie didn't apologise for the circumstances leading up to the 2017 legal case.

The personality had falsely accused anti-poverty campaigner Jack of vandalising a war memorial.

Hopkins did express regret she hadn't acted sooner to settle the case out of court.

Asked what her worst business decision had been, she said: "Arguably, not settling the legal case brought against me.

"I could have settled by giving £5,000 to a migrant charity but chose not to, and the ensuing court action and legal bills forced me to apply for an individual voluntary arrangement ."

Hopkins found herself in hot water in September when Little Mix's Jesy Nelson cited her fat-shaming tweet as the point at which her depression leading to a suicide attempt.
