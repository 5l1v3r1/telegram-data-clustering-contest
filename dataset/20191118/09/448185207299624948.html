Biker dies at Athgaon Flyover on Saturday Night

18 Nov 2019, 09:44

STAFF REPORTER

GUWAHATI: One Akash Boro (22) died while another Papu Saikia (19) sustained serious injuries when the bike (AS-01-DQ-3411) in which they were riding collided head-on with a dumper at Athgaon flyover on Saturday night. The youths were riding towards Bharalumukh from Paltan Bazar end when the accident took place. Boro from the Rupnagar area was declared dead at GMCH. Saikia from Kahilipara is currently undergoing treatment at a private hospital.
