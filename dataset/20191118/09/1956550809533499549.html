Buddha-Bar Dubai to host acclaimed Turkish DJ

Expect slick tunes at the popular Dubai Marina spot

18 Nov 2019, 09:18 by Reema Rahman

Forget booking that flight for the upcoming UAE National Day 2019 long weekend – Buddha-Bar Dubai is promising to bring a slice of Europe and Western Asia to Dubai Marina.

On Friday November 29, DJ Serdar Ayyıldız is set to take over the chic nightlife institution for its World Music Sessions with DJ Mario playing the closing set.

The Turkish DJ started his career in 1995 as a radio DJ and has since performed at nightclubs and festivals all around the world.

Known for his cool beats and remixes in different styles so needless to say, it’s going to be a cracking night with slick tunes.
