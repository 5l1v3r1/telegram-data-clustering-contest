Ariana Grande puts herself on vitamin drip after tearfully cancelling tour show

18 Nov 2019, 09:56

Ariana Grande has taken extreme measures to overcome her nasty illness, by hooking herself up to a vitamin IV drip hours after cancelling her latest Sweetener tour show.

The Thank U, Next singer has been battling a mystery illness since August and it got the best of her this weekend, prompting her to scrap her performance at Lexington’s Rupp Arena on Sunday evening.

In a tearful Instagram video, Ari told her followers: ‘It hurts so bad to swallow.

‘I unfortunately don’t think I’ll be able to push through tonight, and I’m so … sorry.’

Bless her heart.

Fans and Ari’s celebrity peers have been equally concerned about her wellbeing, including Barbra Streisand who tweeted some suggestions for the singer to overcome the illness once and for all.

‘Take a strong Vitamin C drink with some Manuka honey and of course get some chicken soup! Bx,’ Barbra told the popstar.

Ari was a little starstruck by the iconic singer’s kind tweet and replied: ‘My goodness. love you so so much. thank you my sweetest.’

Barbra wasn’t the only celebrity dishing out advice for Ari as British singer FKA twigs provided a soup recipe.

‘Love you, sweet caring angel @fkatwigs this recipe is the one,’ Ariana wrote alongside a clip of her stirring the pot.

The 26-year-old then cosied up in bed to binge on episodes of Gossip Girl.

Sounds like the perfect evening to us – nasty colds aside, of course.

On Saturday, the 7 Rings hitmaker warned fans that there was a chance she’d have to cancel her next concert, revealing her head was ‘splitting’ while performing in Charlottesville the night before.

Ariana explained: ‘So I’m still very sick. I’ve been sick since the last London show. I don’t know how it’s possible but my throat and head are still in so much pain.

‘I sound okay I’m just in a lot of pain and it’s difficult to breathe during the show. I am seeing my doctor and trying my v vest to get better for tomorrow’s show.’

She continued: ‘Please take this as a gentle heads up…

‘I don’t want anyone to feel blindsided tomorrow if God forbid I can’t make the show happen. ‘I’m on all the meds. Getting IV drips, doing everything I possibly can to pull through. Mum and friends are taking good care of me. Just a scary feeling.’
