Manchester City fans react to Kingsley Coman reports

18 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Tom Procter

Some Manchester City fans have reacted to reports the club could consider replacing Leroy Sane with Kingsley Coman.

Sky Sports report the Citizens are eyeing the Frenchman in case Sane departs for Bayern Munich next summer ‘as expected’.

The injured 23-year-old’s current deal runs out in the summer of 2021, and the club appear to have had difficulty tieing him down to a new contract.

Sane has regularly been linked with a move to German champions Bayern, after failing to nail down a regular starting role under Pep Guardiola at the Etihad Stadium.

Coman has been with Bayern since 2015, spending two seasons on loan at the Allianz before making a €28 million / £24 million permanent move from Juventus in 2017 (Goal).

The star has won league titles in France, Italy and Germany, and also has several domestic cups to his name.

For Bayern so far, he has scored 28 goals in 139 appearances.
