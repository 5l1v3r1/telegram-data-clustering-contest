Manish Malhotra’s father passes away

18 Nov 2019, 09:29 by BizAsia Correspondent

The father of fashion designer, Manish Malhotra has passed away. He was 90.

A number of Bollywood stars headed to Malhotra’s residence to pay their condolences. Mid-Day reports that his best friend, Karan Johar, along with Shabana Azmi, Urmila Matondkar and Mohsin Akhtar Mir, David Dhawan, Boney Kapoor and Sophie Choudry led the tributes.

Malhotra’s nephew, Punit Malhotra was clicked while carrying the mortal remains of the designer’s father to the cremation.
