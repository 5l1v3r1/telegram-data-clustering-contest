An Official Look at Star Wars x adidas UltraBOOST "X-Wing" & "Millennium Falcon"

Set to release this week.

18 Nov 2019, 09:44 by Jack Stanley

After leaks of an X-Wing-themed sneaker emerged back in September, adidas has now revealed its two collaborative Star Wars UltraBOOST colorways. As well as the X-Way sneaker, the pack is set to include another design inspired by the iconic Millennium Falcon vehicle owned by Han Solo.

For the X-Wing UltraBOOST S&L, adidas has adorned the sneaker’s upper with a range of X-Wing graphics, as well as a “Sesame/Active Orange/Carbon” colorway inspired by the ship. The Star Wars x adidas UltraBOOST 19 “Millennium Falcon” comes in a darker colorway with a tonal grey base offset with “Bright Cyan.” The sneaker is then finished with graphics across its black BOOST sole unit, and “Never Tell Me The Odds” written on the heel tab.

Take a look at the two sneakers in the gallery above, and expect them both to launch via stockists including Sneakersnstuff on November 21.

As well as working with Star Wars, adidas recently launched an UltraBOOST collaboration with Wood Wood.
