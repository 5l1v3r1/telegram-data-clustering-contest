Prem Alakananda, a Collection of Poetry released on Sunday

18 Nov 2019, 09:11

STAFF REPORTER

GUWAHATI: Prem Alakananda, a collection of the poem written by Swapna Dutta was released at Guwahati Press Club on Sunday. Renowned scientist & poet Bipul Jyoti Saikia formally released the book in the presence of a dignified gathering.

Saikia said that a true poet is the one who has the ability to depict known and comprehensible things in a mysterious and incomprehensible manner. Relating poetry to science, Saikia enlightened the gathering with his brilliant speech.

Swapnil Baruah, a retired ACS officer whose photograph is the Cover of the book said that the balance between profession and passion is necessary. He said, “We the government officials often suppress our desires amidst work pressure. But we must try to balance between these two. The other dignitaries including historian Rajen Saikia and Janardan Goswami also praised the works penned by Swapna Dutta.
