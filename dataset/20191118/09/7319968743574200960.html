Irish Dad found sleeping with family dog battling cancer

18 Nov 2019, 09:49 by IrishCentral Staff

A young Irish man has revealed how he found his father asleep next to the family’s cancer-ridden dog.

Jack Graham, 23, from Northern Ireland, got up in the middle of the night to find his father Mark asleep next to the 17-year-old dog’s cushion.

Graham filmed the “sad” but “heart-warming” moment and shared the video to social media on November 9, the Daily Mail reports.

He said: “I was walking to go to the bathroom and I walked past my father's room and his door was open which isn't normally like him, I looked in and seen he wasn't lying in his bed.
