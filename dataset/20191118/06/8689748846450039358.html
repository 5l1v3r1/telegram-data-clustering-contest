Stonebwoy Subtly Throws New Shots At Kelvynboy During His Performance At The 2019 4Syte Music Video Awards

18 Nov 2019, 06:30 by BB Jones

During Stonebwoy’s performance at the just 2019 4Syte Music Video Awards which was held at the Kempinski Hotel in Accra on Saturday, he launched new shots at his former signee, Kelvynboy.

Stonebwoy paused to acknowledge Samini during his beautiful stagecraft but at the same time he made a comment which was subtly directed at Kelvynboy.

He said:

“Big up to Samini, my godfather, and the upcoming wey dem no dey respect, dem for sit well.”

All of the big names including Sarkodie, Stonebwoy, Shatta Wale, R2bees were all at the award ceremony and it was an unforgettable night as loads of fun just filled the atmosphere endlessly.
