Mickey Mouse turns 91

18 Nov 2019, 06:33 by Gary Detman

Happy birthday to Mickey and Minnie Mouse.

The pair turns 91 years old today.

Both made their debut in the animated movie "Steamboat Willie" on Nov. 18, 1928.

Mickey and Minnie have a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

Walt Disney admitted privately in 1933 the two are married but it's never been officially stated.
