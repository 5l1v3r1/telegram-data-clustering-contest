Manager (Legal & Corporate Affairs) Vacancy At Punjab Cricket Association, Mohali

18 Nov 2019, 06:36 by Manav Malhotra

Punjab Cricket Association, Mohali invites application for the post of Manager (Legal & Corporate Affairs).

Name of the Post: Manager (Legal & Corporate Affairs)

Age Limit: 40 Years

Essential Qualification and Experience

  • Should have a valid B.A. L.L.B Degree
  • 5 years of experience post-qualification
  • Candidate needs to have qualified graduation or post-graduation in Law (LLB or LLM) from a recognized institution or university. A candidate must have registered with Bar Council of India as an Advocate and 5 years of working experience of legal works in any Company/Firm/Court.
