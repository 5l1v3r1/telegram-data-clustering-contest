Stock market update: 60 stocks hit 52-week lows on NSE

Among the stocks that touched their 52-week lows were Apollo Micro Systems and Gujarat Fluorochemicals.

18 Nov 2019, 06:01 by ETMarkets.com

NEW DELHI: Around 60 stocks fell to touch their 52-week lows on NSE in Monday's session.

Among the stocks that touched their 52-week lows were Apollo Micro Systems, Gujarat Fluorochemicals, Force Motors and Sterling And Wilson Solar.

Apcotex, Bajaj Electricals and Everest also featured among the stocks that touched their 52-week lows on NSE.

Domestic benchmark index NSE Nifty was trading 21.70 points down at 11,873.75, while the BSE Sensex was trading 107.24 points down at 40,249.45.

In the Nifty 50 index, Bharti Airtel, BPCL, Tata Steel, Zee Entertainment and Grasim Industries were among the top gainers on the NSE. However, YES Bank, Bajaj Auto, M&M, Asian Paints and Hero MotoCorp were among the top losers.
