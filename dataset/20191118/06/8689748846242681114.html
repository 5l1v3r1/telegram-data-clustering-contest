‘I’ve The Best Fanbase In Africa, I’m Not Bragging’- Sarkodie (+Video)

18 Nov 2019, 06:15 by BB Jones

Sarkodie was one of the biggest winners at the 2019 MTN 4Syte Music Video Awards after bagging 3 awards on the night which was held at the Kempinski Hotel in Accra.

The Ghanaian rap King won the Best Male Video, Achievement Award, and Best Hip Hop Video.

When he came up on stage to collect his Achievement Award, Sarkodie delivered a short speech which was very motivational and partially self-praising.

Sarkodie asked all up and coming artists to keep pushing hard and not be distracted by anything whatsoever in their quest to reach the top.

He then proceeded to thank his fans for the continuous support, saying they are the best in Africa.

“Not to brag but I have the best fanbase inna whole of Africa.”
