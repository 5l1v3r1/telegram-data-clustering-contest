Laal Singh Chaddha First Look: Turban Clad Aamir Khan With His Innocent Eyes Is Here To Impress

The film is the remake of Hollywood film Forrest Gump starring Tom Hanks and is currently being shot in Punjab.

18 Nov 2019, 06:03 by Koimoi.com Team

Aamir Khan’s Laal Singh Chaddha is more than a year away from its release, but like every film of the superstar, it is too making news already. The film also starring Kareena Kapoor Khan in lead features Aamir in the role of a Sikh and his turban clad look which got leaked recently has already made the fans excited.

Aamir who plays the title role in the film has now shared his first look on social media. In the poster, Aamir can be seen holding a “mithaai ka dabba” in his hand while sitting on the side of a train’s seat.

He captioned the pic, “Sat Sri Akaal ji, myself Laal…Laal Singh Chaddha.”
