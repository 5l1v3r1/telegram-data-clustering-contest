Punjab: Capt Amarinder Singh Ensures Investigation & Trial For The Death Of Dalit Man

18 Nov 2019, 06:50 by Varsha Chavan

General News

The Cheif Minister of Punjab Captain Amarinder Singh has ensured the family of Jagmael Singh that proper investigation and trial will be carried out by the govt

Written By Varsha Chavan | Mumbai | Updated On: November 18, 2019 06:50 IST

The Chief Minister of Punjab Captain Amarinder Singh on Sunday, November 17, ensured the family of Jagmael Singh that proper investigation and trial will be carried out by the government. Taking to Twitter Captain Singh also assured that the Punjab government is committed to protecting the Dalits and strict action will be taken against the accused. Earlier on November 9, a 37-year-old man identified as Jagmael, a native of Changaliwali village was allegedly thrashed by some men with whom he had a dispute.
