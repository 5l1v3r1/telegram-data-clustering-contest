Total Tanzania CEO pledges quality products, services to consumers

18 Nov 2019, 06:43 by The Guardian Reporter

TOTAL Tanzania will continue to provide not only the best quality fuels with Excellium and lubricants, but also superior services to its customers

The company’s Managing Director, Jean Francois Schoepp said in Dar es Salaam on Friday during the launch of a top customer service week that Total observes the week annually because it wants to be closer to customers.

“We want to get an opportunity to listen to them, to hear from them and it’s also an opportunity for us to improve our products and services,” Schoepp said while noting that during the week, the company staff flocked to its network of service station to offer quality services to customers.

“Total customer week focuses on customer centricity where we see our customers at the center of our attention; it is an opportunity for all our staff to meet our customers and set a good platform for Total to promote its best quality fuel the Total Excellium and lubricants,” he added.

Total Tanzania’s Network Director, Marieme Sow said during the week they wish to make sure they demonstrate how to handle customers throughout the year by talking to them and get their valuable feedback on what they want.

“Once again as Total, we demonstrate how we can be innovative and it has been a pleasure discussing with our customers as they visited our stations and got a chance to be services by our employees and management teams,” Sow said.

Total Tanzania Limited is a petroleum market and service company, incorporated in Tanzania since 1969. Total business focuses on the marketing, supply and service of petroleum products, HFO and lubricants, which are marketed through its general trade, service stations and specialty departments within its organization.
