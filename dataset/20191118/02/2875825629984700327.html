Dec picks out bad tempered I'm A Celeb camp-mate after noticing telling sign

The presenter thinks former footballer Ian Wright may turn out to be this year's hot-head after day one Down Under as the stars meet for the first time as they prepare for the Jungle

18 Nov 2019, 02:32 by Irishmirror.ie

Declan Donnelly has already made a big prediction about this year's series of I'm A Celebrity .

The 44-year-old isn't talking possible winners just yet - but he has picked out who could be the most bad tempered camp-mate.

The stars said hello for the first time on Sunday night as things finally got under way.

Dec usually says what he sees - and Ian Wright is in his eye-line early on.

The former Arsenal striker was the first to hit our screens as he pulled up in a red Ferrari - but will he be seeing red as the days go on?

"I think there could be one or two spiky characters," the host predicted.

"He might blow, he might just go - if he gets hungry..... He's also got quite a short attention span."

I’m A Celeb viewers were distracted by Declan during the launch night.

He appeared on the show with painful looking marks on his arm - and it left those at home guessing.

While the marks looked like they could either be bruises or insect bites, viewers had a range of wild ideas about what they could be.

“What’s up with Dec’s arm? That’s a nasty bruise,” one concerned fan commented on Twitter .

“What’s up with Dec’s right arm? Big bruise!!” another said.

Meanwhile, his co-host Ant McPartlin has had a poke at controversial Katie Hopkins. He admitted she wasn't his favourite of the bunch of previous I'm A Celebrity campmates.

Katie, 44, shot to fame on The Apprentice in 2007 and starred in that year's series of I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!

But Ant, 43, told The Sun : "Katie Hopkins was in I'm A Celebrity but you wouldn't really remember her doing it, as she didn't really shine."
