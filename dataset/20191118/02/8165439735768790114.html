WATCH: Caitlyn Jenner's First Bush Tucker Trial In Teaser For Tonight's I'm A Celeb

18 Nov 2019, 02:00 by Tara Walsh

I'm A Celeb teaser

The teaser for tonight's episode of I'm A Celeb is here and it sees Caitlyn Jenner take on her first bush tucker trial.

Kate Garraway joins her for the trial which is called Bugged Off.

The new series kicked off last night.

The clip shows Caitlyn and Kate lying down in two small glass boxes high above ground.

Ant shouts, "Okay best of luck. The trial starts... Now!"

A handful of insects pour over Kate who begins to panic, "No, no, no, no, no...."

Dec shouts, "10 minutes for 10 stars. Take your time."

We then see a pot floating in the water below their boxes.

The celebs must drop the red balls from their containers into the pot below as it floats past.

Caitlyn misses her first shot.

Insects begin to pour over her as she exclaims, "Oh, we've got lots of friends."

Ant responds from the ground, "That's right, lots of friends in there with you now."

He tells them that they're joined by, "30,000 cockroaches."
