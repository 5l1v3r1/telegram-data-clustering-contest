Karnataka Congress MLA Tanveer Sait stabbed at wedding in Mysuru, attack captured on camera

18 Nov 2019, 07:40 by JKR Staff

A Karnataka MLA and former state minister was stabbed at a wedding in Mysuru on Sunday night. Tanveer Sait, an MLA from Narasimharaja constituency, was taken to a nearby hospital.

His attacker has been identified as one Farhan Pasha, who was later captured by people present at the wedding and handed over to police.
