HEARTBREAKING:Inside Parirenyatwa Hospital after 3 Months Doctors Strike

18 Nov 2019, 07:24 by Philip Mangwiro

Inside Parirenyatwa Hospital after Doctors Strike, Heartbreaking Video

Zimbabwean Doctors have been on strike for over 3 months now demanding better pay and improved working conditions. Negotiations between the government and doctors failed leading to The Ministry of Health firing over 200 Doctors. Impoverished Zimbabweans have been left in a precarious situation where they are now keeping their sick loved ones at home leading to death from curable diseases.

Below is a video shared by veteran journalist Hopewell Chin’ono on the sad state of the country ‘s major referral Hospital Parirenyatwa. He said:

Empty & Ghostly, this is Parirenyatwa Hospital this evening!Only tired army doctors who are providing an auxiliary service.
Ward after ward, it was empty!Not only doctors are off work, even the hard-working nurses have left their stations due to wage incapacitation!
