Bigg Boss 13: Evicted Contestant Arhaan Khan Confesses Being In LOVE With Rashami Desai; Wants To Go Back And PROPOSE To Her

18 Nov 2019, 07:07 by Divya Ramnani

Aww! Recently-evicted Bigg Boss 13 contestant Arhaan Khan reveals that he is in love with Rashami Desai and that he wants to go back and propose to her

What has been happening inside the Bigg Boss 13 house is as it is unpredictable, and now, it looks like that the situations outside the house, too, are on the similar lines. It was yesterday night during the latest Weekend Ka Vaar episode that Salman Khan announced Arhaan Khan’s eviction due to lesser amount of votes, as reported by SpotboyE exclusively. Well, his elimination didn’t go down well with his rumoured girlfriend Rashami Desai, who broke into tears as she bid him a farewell.

While inside the house, Arhaan Khan suggested Rashami maintain a safe distance from him; however, on his eviction, it looks like the actor is regretting it, at least his latest statements suggest so. In a conversation with Bombay Times, Arhaan Khan confessed that he is in love with Rashami and that he wishes to go back inside the Bigg Boss 13 house, only to propose her for marriage.
