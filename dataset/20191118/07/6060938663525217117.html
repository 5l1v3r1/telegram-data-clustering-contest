Good Newwz Trailer: Akshay Kumar-Kareena Kapoor Khan's baby dream involves a 'sperm' goof-up

18 Nov 2019, 07:26 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Akshay Kumar has been raising the excitement for ‘Good Newwz’ with several posters and now that the trailer is out, it has been totally worth the wait. Akshay and Kareena Kapoor Khan reuniting for the silver screen has come as a blessing for fans of the two. Playing a couple eager to turn parents in ‘Good Newwz’, Akshay and Kareena give a fresh perspective to parenthood and IVF, but with a hilarious twist.
