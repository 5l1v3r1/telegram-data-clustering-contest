B&Q tell Rangers fan ‘keep us out of it’ after he highlights ‘EBT’ product they sell

18 Nov 2019, 07:21 by Gary Taylor

B&Q have jokingly told a Rangers fan 'keep us out of it' after he highlighted an 'EBT' product they sell.

Scottish football was rocked earlier this week after The Times reported that Oldco Rangers' final tax bill could be massively reduced.

HMRC later hit back at those claims and insisted it had not made any mistakes in how they calculated the company's tax liabilities for its EBT scheme.

But one Rangers fan saw the lighter side of the row earlier as he claimed DIY store B&Q were wading into the debate.

Sharing a picture of cans of Sikaflex EBT sealant on the shop's shelves, he tweeted: "FFS there's B&Q at it now."

B&Q then joined in with the joke, replying: "Keep us out of it."

Some fans loved the company's response.

@Tomtom1872 said: "Too sticky even for B&Q bud."

@Ben0517_ added: "You should be okay as long as the HMRC staff member isn't a Homebase supporter."

While @Stuart723 showed his appreciation by tweeting a picture of a box of Quality Street.

Meanwhile, Rangers star Glen Kamara has admitted he partied all night following Finland's historic Euro 2020 qualification.

The Finns sparked huge celebrations in their homeland after they swept aside Liechtenstein to qualify for a major tournament for the first time ever.
