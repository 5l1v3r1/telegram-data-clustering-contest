SEVENTEEN brings home 2 Major Awards at Asian Music Festival 2019 in China

18 Nov 2019, 07:57 by jaehaedae_seojeo

SEVENTEEN just bring home 2 Grand Prize awards in Asian Music Festival 2019!

Seventeen wins the Best Album of the year with their 3rd full album'An Ode' and Asia’s Best Group of the Year in Asian Music Festival which was held in Shenzhen, China on November 17.

Seventeen Jun and The8 attended the awards ceremony as the group's representatives.

The8 stated that, "I wished my members were here with us for this glorious moment, although they are not here, I'm sure that they will be really happy when they hear it. I'm really proud that I'm a member of Seventeen and that my fans are Carats."
