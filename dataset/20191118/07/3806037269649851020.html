BROCKHAMPTON Drops First Episode of 'Keeping The Band' Web Series

An in-depth look at the boy band’s “Heaven Belongs to You Tour.”

18 Nov 2019, 07:57 by Sophie Caraan

BROCKHAMPTON has shared the very first episode of their web series Keeping The Band, created by Kevin Abstract. The full 10-minute episode starts off with an in-depth look at the boy band’s preparations for their GINGER-supporting tour (“Heaven Belongs to You Tour”), taking fans behind-the-scenes during rehearsals, their stint on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon and their frustrations during the tour’s first show in New York City. The group’s creative director Henock “HK” Sileshi, frequent stage design collaborator Alex Reardon, Jimmy Fallon, “SUGAR” singer Ryan Beatty, creative team member Robert Ontenient and manager Jon Nunes also appear in the inaugural episode alongside the boy band.
