Kim Kardashian demonstrates DIY boob lift after incredibly painful duct tape fails

The KKW Beauty founder treated her followers to a tutorial and discussed her previous painful experiences with industrial tape

13 Nov 2019, 18:59 by Tiffany Wallis

Kim Kardashian demonstrates how her new SKIMS body tapes work with a tutorial on social media on Tuesday.

Using a topless model, the Kardashian shows fans how to use her collection of nude-coloured body tapes for tricky outfits that boast plunging necklines and threaten to expose more than what you bargained for.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians alum, 39, also discusses her previous very painful experiments with other types of sticky tape including gaffer tape and industrial shipping tape.

To avoid revisiting her famous gaffer tape bra from 2008, Kim has introduced a more comfortable version for her solution-focused shapewear brand.

She announces: "Hey guys, I’m in my closet and I’m going to show you how to tape up your boobs with the boob tape because it’s something that I have used for so many years."

But she admits: "I would use gaffers tape and shipping tape and it would burn me and rip me.

"This material is super soft and super stretchy — but not too stretchy because you want it to hold. It’s like the perfect amount of stretch."

Promising to create a "mini-boob lift", Kim reveals she wanted to share her "lifesaving secret" and made the tutorial to show how easy SKIMS tape are to use.

The tape works as a DIY lift when women choose to go braless and want to create the ultimate hold on their cleavage.

SKIMS tonal body tape has a slight stretch to it to allow ultimate hold underneath and comes in three shades of nude.

In the social media video, Kim is filmed cutting the tape and peeling the back label off before sticking it onto the topless model.

After securing the tape smoothly on the skin, the businesswoman dresses the model with a chic blazer to prove the invisible appearance on the tape that lies underneath.

Afterwards Kim encourages the model pull the tape off of her body to show how easily it comes off, pain-free, and without leaving any marks on her skin.

The global icon then shares a series of throwback images of when she used either duct tape or gaffer tape to strap her boobs down in some of her most memorable outfits.

The snaps show Kim wearing fashion ensembles with very low necklines and plunging designs as she captions each with an explanation of her painful wardrobe hack.

In one photo, Kim jokes she was in such a rush that her cleavage looked lopsided due to uneven tape application.

Meanwhile in another shot, she reveals she used black tape, but admits it flattened the center part of her boob.
