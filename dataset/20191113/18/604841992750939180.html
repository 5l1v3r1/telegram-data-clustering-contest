Tidy Towns blunder sees Dunboyne moved to County Dublin!

13 Nov 2019, 18:43 by John Donohoe

Last week, Ryan Tubridy was at the receiving end of a barrage of calls and texts when he mentioned on radio that Dunboyne was in County Dublin, when talking about the Sean Cox 'You'll Never Walk Alone' CD.

Now, the Department of Rural and Community Development, Tidy Towns, and SuperValu have made the same mistake. Dunboyne Tidy Towns has posted a photo of a poster on a local bus stop - congratulating Dunboyne Tidy Towns in Co Dublin.

Is it any wonder we can't even win a Leinster title - next thing they'll be taking our players!
