SBK, Aragon test: all the photos of the first day at Motorland

PHOTOGALLERY - Lowes with Kawasaki, Caricasulo with Yamaha, Camier with Ducati and Razgatlioglu with Yamaha, many news at the first Spanish release

13 Nov 2019, 18:43 by Riccardo Gugliemetti

There was no shortage of waiting at Aragon for what was the first day of testing in view of the new season. In addition to the first direct confrontation on the track, there was also the curiosity to see all those riders who have changed the saddle since last season.

This is the case of Toprak Razgatlioglu, who in Yamaha has collected the witness of Alex Lowes, the latter passed in Kawasaki alongside Rea. And what about Leon Camier, for the first time on the Panigale V4 of Barni team. Also new at GRT with the Italian-American couple formed by Caricasulo and Gerloff. It is forbidden to forget Scott Redding in the Panigale V4, even though the latter had already tried three weeks ago in Valencia.
