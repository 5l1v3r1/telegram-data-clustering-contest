21-year-old charged with murder for setting man on fire after losing card game in Pasco County

13 Nov 2019, 18:17 by Associated Press, WFTS Digital Staff

HUDSON, Fla. (AP) — A 21-year-old Pasco man was so angry about losing several thousand dollars in a card game that he set the other man’s car on fire with him inside.

RELATED: Body found in burned vehicle in Pasco County

A Pasco County Sheriff’s arrest report says the victim was burned beyond recognition after playing cards with Michael Psilakis, 21, in October. Investigators haven’t released the victim’s name, citing Marsy’s Law, a state constitutional amendment designed to protect crime victims.

The sheriff's office says the body and vehicle were found in the woods Friday afternoon east of Baillies Bluff Road near Key Vista Nature Park.
