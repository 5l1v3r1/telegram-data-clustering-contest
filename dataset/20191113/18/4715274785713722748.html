Pinoy dance troupe wins gold in South Korea

13 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Manila Standard Showbiz

Philippine Representative Saint Benilde Romançon Dance Company 

(SBRDC), the resident dance team of the De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde (DLS-CSB), conquered the world stage and took home the much-coveted gold from the recently concluded All Generation Championships (AGC) held at the SK Olympic Handball Stadium in Seoul, South Korea. 

The AGC is a gathering that unites urban movers from different age groups across the region through the ever-growing growing hip hop scene. It recognizes rhythmic skills under their respective groupings, namely, Super Kidz, Highteen, Top University, Adult, Over 40, Silver Star, Pay Back, and All Style Solo Battle. 

AGC Founder Austin Jiseong Heo, specially invited the SBRDC to contend in their country after they met at the US-organized Hip Hop International held in Manila, of which he is the Affiliate Director for Saint Benilde Romançon Dance Company
