‘Last Christmas’ premieres in London

13 Nov 2019, 18:10 by AFP

English actress Emilia Clarke was the picture of glamour as she attended the UK premiere of Last Christmas. She was in good company as she arrived arm-in-arm with her co-star Henry Golding (Crazy Rich Asians), who plays her love interest in the film. The premiere was held at the BFI Southbank in south-east London on Monday evening.

In the film, written by Emma and her husband Greg Wise, Emilia’s character Kate, who has often been unlucky, finds work as a Santa’s elf for a department store and unexpectedly finds love with Tom, played by Henry.
