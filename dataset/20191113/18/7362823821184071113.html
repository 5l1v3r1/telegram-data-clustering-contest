Boris Johnson shows off tea-making skills, stirs outrage when he adds milk with bag still in mug

13 Nov 2019, 18:03 by Stephen Sorace

British Prime Minister Boris Johnson’s tea-making skills stirred some controversy Tuesday after an election video showed the Brexit leader pouring milk into his tea without first taking the bag out of the mug.

The apparent faux-pas was spotted as Johnson answered a wide range of questions, from how he starts his day to the challenges facing Britain and why parliament has called for a general election on Dec. 12. His answers, however, were overshadowed by a cup of tea.
