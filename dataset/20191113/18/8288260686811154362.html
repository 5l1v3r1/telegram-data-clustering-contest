Mac Demarco announces Birmingham O2 Academy show - how to get tickets

These five UK shows include his biggest gig to date, at London's Alexandra Palace, a week before he plays Birmingham's O2 Academy on November 27

13 Nov 2019, 18:27 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Mac Demarco is set to appear in Birmingham on his latest tour later this month.

The hugely talented musician will bring his band to the UK and Europe in November with five shows across Britain.

These five UK shows include his biggest gig to date, at London's Alexandra Palace, a week before he plays Birmingham's O2 Academy on November 27.

The UK leg of the tour will also call at Manchester, Glasgow, and Liverpool.

Mac has announced Orville Peck, the leather-fringe-masked rising country star as the special guest for London's Alexandra Palace, and today announces support for the rest of the UK and European run, courtesy of the Cumbia-infused instrumental act, Los Bitchos.

Tickets are available now via  SeeTickets here.

The upcoming shows follow celebrated appearances at London's All Points East, Glastonbury and headline shows in Glasgow, Halifax and Margate; as well as extensive tours of North America and Europe earlier this year.

Mac’s fourth full-length album,  Here Comes The Cowboy , is out now via Mac’s Record Label (distributed in the UK via Caroline International).

The record, which received four star reviews in Uncut, Q, MOJO and The Evening Standard, was written, tracked and mixed at DeMarco’s Jizz Jazz Studios in Los Angeles.

Nearly every instrument on the album was played by DeMarco, aside from keyboards on select tracks by touring member and close friend Alec Meen.
