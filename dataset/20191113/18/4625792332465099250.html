'Schiff can't do his own questions': Trump knocks Democrats for using lawyer during impeachment hearing

13 Nov 2019, 18:11 by John Gage

President Trump commented on the ongoing House impeachment hearing while he answered reporters' questions sitting next to President Recep Tayyip Erdogan of Turkey.

"[Adam] Schiff can't do his own questions," Trump said Wednesday, taking a dig at the House Intelligence chairman for using a lawyer to question witnesses. House Democrats have tapped Daniel Goldman to question witnesses. Republicans have their own lawyer, Steve Castor, who they can call upon to ask questions during their portion of the impeachment hearing.
