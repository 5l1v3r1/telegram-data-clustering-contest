T-Mobile Tuesdays will include deals from Puma, Taco Bell, and more next week

13 Nov 2019 by Alex Wagner

Here’s what T-Mo has in store for next week’s edition of T-Mobile Tuesdays.

On Tuesday, November 19th, T-Mobile customers can get a $20 dining credit for Dining Advantage and 40% off at Puma.com. Also available will be $0.10 off per gallon of gas at Shell.

T-Mobile’s free Taco Bell offer will be available again next Tuesday, but the free items are being changed up a bit. You’ll be able to choose one free item from the following three: Crunchy Taco Supreme, Chips and Nacho Cheese Sauce, and a Mountain Dew Baja Blast.

Rounding out next week’s deals will be up to 40% off hotels from Booking.com and Live Nation ticket offers.

T-Mobile hasn’t announced any info about the sweepstakes next week.

Source: T-Mobile Tuesdays
