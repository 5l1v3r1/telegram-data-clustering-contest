Edelweiss Standalone September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 35.38 crore, down 52.9% Y-o-Y

13 Nov 2019

Reported Standalone quarterly numbers for Edelweiss Financial Services are:

Net Sales at Rs 35.38 crore in September 2019 down 52.9% from Rs. 75.12 crore in September 2018.
