Funniest Comedy Wildlife Photography Awards 2019 Photos Prove Nature Is A Joker

13 Nov 2019 by Thanussha Priyah

Be prepared to quack up.
With the hustle and bustle of city life, The Comedy Wildlife Photography Awards returns this year to interject the noise with some of nature’s most hilarious moments.
Hearty chuckles are abound, with this year’s winning photographs showcasing Mother Nature’s diverse antics ranging from a surfing penguin to a laidback chimpanzee. Founded by Paul Joynson-Hicks and Tom Sullam, the photography competition offers giggles along with a meaningful message of wildlife conservation.
The overall winner of 2019 is Sarah Skinner for her cheeky photograph aptly titled as Grab life by the …!. Skinner’s work was selected out of 4,000 entries from 68 different countries and 40 finalists.
With its partnership with The Born Free Foundation, the competition has provided some tidbits for the rest of the world to play their part.
It takes a few steps to help out in conservation, such as avoiding single-use plastics and packaging, as well as opting for recyclable products and cutting down water usage at home. However, if you’d like to reach out to do more for the environment, you can become a “Wild Influencer.”
