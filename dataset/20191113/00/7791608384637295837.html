Peter Parker And Tony Stark’s ‘Avengers: Endgame’ Reunion Has Alternate Scene

13 Nov 2019 by Yimin Huang

The famous reunion between Iron Man played by Robert Downey Jr. and Peter Parker played by Tom Holland in Avengers: Endgame is revealed to have an alternate scene.
The different version is now released on the streaming service Disney+, where Downey Jr. and Holland’s characters embrace like the original version, only this time he introduced Spider-Man to Pepper Potts.
The alternate version has been shared without CGI, giving viewers an interesting look into how the scene is actually portrayed.
Actors are seen running around in ping-pong ball suits instead of the full gear seen in the theaters, per Complex.

Editor Jeff Ford told Slash Film that the introduction of Potts was deleted in the actual film as it was deemed “too lighthearted” for the reunion scene.
He added that when the scene was first filmed, Potts was introduced in a “nice to meet you” scene in the midst of a battle that made it seem more like a “comedy.” Thus, it did not fit into the big picture.
