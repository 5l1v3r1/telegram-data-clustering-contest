Heads Up: There Might Be A ‘Friends’ Reunion Special On The Horizon

13 Nov 2019, 00:27 by Joseph Earp

In our oversaturated world of streaming, Friends is one of the last big nostalgic sitcoms that has yet to be rebooted. But, if the entertainment stars line up, that might all change sometime very soon.

Yep, the long rumoured, long anticipated reunion might be hitting our screens in the form of one last episode. According to The Hollywood Reporter, who got their information from “sources close” to the situation, a bunch of the key players are talking about inking a deal for a special.

Those players include Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc, Matthew Perry, and David Schwimmer. So, ya know, the whole gang.
