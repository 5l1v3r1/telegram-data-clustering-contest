Mona Road a cow pasture at night

13 Nov 2019

Dear Editor,

Every night Mona Road is transformed into a cow pasture for a herd of cows.

As some sections of the road are dimly lit, often these cows seem to just appear out of nowhere.

This is a disaster waiting to happen.

These cows need to be removed before there is a serious accident.

It is a most selfish act on the part of the owner who is putting motorists at risk.

It is also rather careless for the authorities to allow this to continue for so many years.
