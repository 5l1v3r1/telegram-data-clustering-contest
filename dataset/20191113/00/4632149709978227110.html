Fake News Buster: Rich man in casket not from Kuwait

13 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
The picture of a man lying in a casket with gold jewellery on is being shared on Facebook with the claim that he is Nassi Al Kharki, the richest man of Kuwait. Various posts carrying the picture have got thousands of shares on social media, with many of them also containing pictures of his ‘other riches’, such as bullion and cash.


SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
The man is actually real estate mogul called Sheron Sukhedo from Trinidad and Tobago. According to British newspaper reports, the millionaire was shot dead in March 2018 and was laid to rest in a gold casket with his gold pendants and jewellery.
