Black Friday is the busiest shopping day of the year, and it’s only getting bigger and bigger. Retailers are aware of that -- and as a result, they’re announcing incredible deals earlier and earlier. Amazon, as usual, is a great place to get some of the best online deals -- and the retailer has announced some of the better deals on TVs and computers currently available. TVs

TVs have evolved at a staggering rate in recent years. Even the highest-end HDTVs from a few years ago seem almost outdated already. The good news is that this evolution has resulted in some of the latest, greatest tech on the market is available at introductory rates. If you've been looking for a chance to get your hands on a Smart TV or a UHD TV console with massive savings, this year could be your year!

TCL 55S425

If you’re looking for a great TV on a budget, then TCL offers some of the best options out there. The TCL 55S425 offers a 55-inch display, supports a 4K resolution and HDR, and has Roku built right into it, so you’ll easily be able to install all your favorite streaming apps and services. Sure, the TV may not match LG’s OLED TVs or Samsung’s QLED TVs in terms of image quality -- but they still have a ton to offer, especially in this price range. 

Samsung 55-inch Q60R

As mentioned, Samsung’s QLED TVs offer among the best TV image quality currently on offer -- and the 55-inch Q60R is a great way to get a taste of that without spending thousands of dollars. This TV boasts bright, vivid colors, along with Samsung’s Tizen operating system, which is pretty smart. It’s compatible with Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa, and can even upscale older 1,080p content to look great on the 4K panel.

Sony 65-inch A8G

LG may be famous for its OLED TVs, but Sony leverages the amazing tech too. This particular TV offers among the best of the best in terms of image quality, including the super-deep black levels and incredibly vivid colors. The TV has Android TV built into it too, meaning you’ll easily be able to install your streaming apps and services, and use the ultra-smart Google Assistant to find out information from the web, control smart home devices, and even control aspects of the TV. While the TV normally comes at $3,499.99, it’s currently available for $1,998 -- which is a discount of an incredible $1,501.99.

Computers

Even though we use our phones for practically everything, these days, there's so much to be said for having a reliable computer setup, no matter what your tech needs. If you're looking to upgrade your technical capabilities, getting a more powerful system for either work, school, or fun, Amazon's offering you numerous opportunities to do so!

Apple MacBook Air

The MacBook Air recently got a major redesign, making it look even more modern than before. The laptop comes in at only 0.6 inches thick, and has a 13-inch display -- which is more than big enough for watching videos and shows on Netflix. The laptop is relatively powerful under the hood too, offering an Intel Core i5 processor, 8GB of RAM, and 128GB of storage, which is more than enough for most professionals and students. The laptop normally comes at $1,099, and is currently available for $999.

HP Pavilion All-in-One

If you’re in the market for a Windows 10 all-in-one, this computer by HP is a great choice. It’s relatively powerful, meaning that it can be used for most productivity tasks, and offers a relatively nice design. Under the hood, you’ll get an AMD Ryzen 5 processor, with 8GB of RAM and a massive 1TB solid-state drive, and on the front, you’ll get a massive 23.8-inch display. Normally, the computer comes at $1,349 -- but you can get it now for only $1,044.40.
