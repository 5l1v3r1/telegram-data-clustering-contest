'The Batman': Kristen Stewart is eager to see Robert Pattinson step into the shoes of caped-crusader Batman

13 Nov 2019, 11:51 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kristen Stewart is all set to make her presence felt on the silver screen as she essays the character of Sabina Wilson in Elizabeth Banks third instalment of 'Charlie's Angels'.

In a recent interview with a Hollywood portal, Stewart revealed that she is excited to see her ex-flame Robert Pattinson step into the shoes of Batman in Matt Reeves directorial 'The Batman'. The leading trio of 'Charlie's Angels' were quizzed about their favourite Batman, replying to which Stewart said that they are yet to see Robert Pattinson as Batman yet but she has all faith in him.

Furthermore, talking about Robert and Zoe Kravitz, who plays the Catwoman in 'The Batman', she revealed that she cannot wait to see them on the celluloid as she has known them for a long time.

While Stewart is joined by Naomi Scott and Ella Balinska in Banks directorial 'Charlie's Angels', Pattinson is all set to feature in Reeves' 'The Batman' which is slated to release on 25th June 2021.
