Credit card fraud suspects sought in Collier County

13 Nov 2019, 11:28 by WFTX Digital Team

NAPLES, Fla. -- The Collier County Sheriff's Office is requesting help in identifying two men involved in credit card fraud in Naples.

It happened at Lotus Vape & Smoke Shop at 4396 Tamiami Trail North in Naples.

If you have any information regarding their identity please contact CCSO at 239-252-9300.

If you wish to remain anonymous you can call our tips line at 1-800-780-TIPS (8477) or SWFL Crime Stoppers 1-800-780-TIPS (8477).
