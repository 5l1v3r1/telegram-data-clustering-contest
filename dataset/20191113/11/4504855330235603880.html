Germany hails Tesla plan to build new factory near Berlin

13 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Associated Press

BERLIN (AP) — Germany on Wednesday hailed Tesla’s announcement to build its first European factory in the country, days after the government said it would boost subsidies for buyers of electrics cars.

Tesla CEO Elon Musk said during an award ceremony in the German capital Tuesday evening that “we’ve decided to put the Tesla Gigafactory Europe in the Berlin area.”
