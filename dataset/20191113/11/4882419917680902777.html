Real Madrid's Rodrygo: I prefer PSG's Neymar to Barça's Lionel Messi

The young Brazilian spoke from the national team camp pre-Argentina friendly

13 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Alex Muñoz

Rodrygo's impressive performances for Real Madrid, including a hat-trick in the Champions League win over Galatasaray, have seen him earn a place in the latest Brazil squad.

He spoke to the press ahead of Friday's game against Argentina in Saudi Arabia and was asked various questions, including if he prefers Neymar or Lionel Messi.

The former Santos man said that between the Barcelona No.10 and his international teammate, "I say on the side of Brazil, with Neymar."

Rodrygo's form has earned comparisons with Cristiano Ronaldo, who Real Madrid have struggled to replace since he joined Juventus in 2018.

"I am too young to say that I am filling the space that he left," Rodrygo said. "I am at the beginning of my career at Madrid, I hope to one day be a great player there, but I'm not putting myself under pressure."
