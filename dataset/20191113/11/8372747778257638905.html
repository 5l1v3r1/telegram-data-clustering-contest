Images released of burglary suspect at Fort Myers convenience store in August

13 Nov 2019, 11:15 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Fort Myers Police are seeking assistance identifying a male involved in a vehicle burglary.

According to Fort Myers Police, it happened at 5150 Martin Luther King, Jr. Boulevard on August 13, 2019. The incident took place at the RaceTrac convenience store on the corner of Six Mile Cypress Boulevard where he broke into the vehicle and stole cash.

The suspect was in a newer model black Lincoln SUV and was accompanied by an unidentified white SUV.

If identified, please contact FMPD Detective Cierra Carter at (239) 321-8036 or email ccarter@fmpolice.com.

Tipsters can also remain anonymous and be eligible for a cash reward of up to $3,000 by contacting Crime Stoppers at 1-800-780-TIPS (8477) or online at www.southwestfloridacrimestoppers.com
