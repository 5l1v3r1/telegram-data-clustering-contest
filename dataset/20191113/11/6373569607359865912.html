Man 'punched in the face' in Plymouth road rage incident

Police want anyone with dashcam footage to come forward

13 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Rom Preston-Ellis

A man was punched in the face during a road rage incident in Plymouth.

Police have now launched an investigation and have appealed for information.

The incident took place in a layby off Forder Valley Road on the B3413, near to the Liegham roundabout, between 5.50pm and 6pm on Thursday, November 7.

Occupants of two vehicles had pulled into the layby following a minor road traffic collision before an argument ensued between two men.  

The victim, aged in his 40s, alleges that he was punched in the face. He suffered a facial injury.

A Devon and Cornwall Police spokesperson said: "Police are appealing for witnesses, or anyone with anyone with dashcam footage, to come forward."

Call 101, or email 101@dc.police.uk, quoting crime reference number CR/100889/19.
