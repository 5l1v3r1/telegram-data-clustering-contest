Come And Join BitMart’s Thanksgiving Whole Network Trading Competition!

13 Nov 2019, 11:54

It’s Thanksgiving time again! In order to give a special “thank you” note to all users, BitMart launched a Whole Network Trading Competition. This time, they are giving away over 400,000 USD worth of tokens! 

Campaign Period: 11/11/2019 10:00 AM to 9:00 AM 12/02/2019 10:00 AM (EST)

Campaign Details: 

Part I – Whole Network Net Trading Competition

Net Trading Volume = |Buy – Sell| (absolute value of “buy minus sell”)

  1. USDT Market Net Trading Competition

During the campaign period, users will be ranked according to their total net trading volume in their BitMart accounts across all USDT Market trading pairs (e.g. USDT acts as the “Quote Currency”). The top 10 traders with the highest net trading volume will be rewarded with the following prizes:

  • 1st: 30,000 USDT + 500,000 BMX
  • 2nd: 10,000 USDT + 100,000BMX
  • 3rd: 5,000 USDT + 50,000BMX
  • 4th-10th: 2,000 USDT + 20,000 BMX each

What is “Quote Currency” & “Base Currency” ?

“Quote Currency” is the cryptocurrency used to buy other digital assets. “Base Currency” is the cryptocurrency that you wish to buy. Take BMX/USDT trading pair as an example, USDT is the “Quote Currency” while BMX is the “Base Currency”.

  1. BTC Market Net Trading Competition

Users will be ranked according to their total net trading volume in their BitMart accounts across all BTC Market trading pairs (e.g. BTC acts as the “Quote Currency”). The top 10 traders with the highest net trading volume will be rewarded with the following prizes:

  • 1st: 5 BTC + 500,000 BMX
  • 2nd: 3 BTC + 100,000 BMX
  • 3rd: 1 BTC + 50,000 BMX
  • 4th-10th: 0.5 BTC + 20,000 BMX each

What is “Quote Currency” & “Base Currency” ?

“Quote Currency” is the cryptocurrency used to buy other digital assets. “Base Currency” is the cryptocurrency that you wish to buy. Take BMX/BTC trading pair as an example, BTC is the “Quote Currency” while BMX is the “Base Currency”.

Part II – BMX Trading Competition

Trading Volume = Buy + Sell

During the campaign period, participants will be ranked according to their total BMX volume traded in their BitMart account across BMX/BTC, BMX/ETH, and BMX/USDT. The top 10 users with the highest trading volume will be rewarded with the following prizes:

  • 1st: 10 BTC + 1,000,000 BMX
  • 2nd: 5 BTC + 500,000 BMX
  • 3rd: 3 BTC + 300,000 BMX
  • 4th-10th: 1 BTC + 200,000 BMX each
