UK weather forecast: Met Office issues severe weather warning for ice

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

13 Nov 2019, 11:27 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

The Met Office has issued an ice warning before temperatures plunge on one of the coldest nights of autumn.

It said: "Scattered showers will affect much of the east coast of Scotland this evening and overnight, falling onto freezing surfaces.

"These will be heavy at times and will lead to a risk of icy surfaces where any treatment is washed off.

"Showers will be wintry above 200m away from the coast across Aberdeenshire and Moray, with 1-2cm of snow possible."
