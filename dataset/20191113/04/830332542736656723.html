Reedsburg city officials approve ordinance that bans vaping in public places

13 Nov 2019, 04:14

REEDSBURG, Wis. - The city of Reedsburg has approved a new ordinance that bans vaping and e-cigarettes from a number of public places in the city. 

The Common Council approved the measure unanimously at Monday night's meeting. 

A city clerk said e-cigarettes are no longer allowed anywhere normal cigarettes are not allowed.
