Photos: Gauri Khan lets her creativity run wild in the city of Jaipur

13 Nov 2019, 04:39 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Apart from being a doting mom, wife and businesswoman, Gauri Khan has made a name for herself as an ace interior designer. Known for her simple, sophisticated yet exquisite taste in décor, style and class, the star has become one of the go-to designers for Bollywood stars looking to make their swanky houses feel a little more homely.

Gauri, also makes it a point to travel the globe looking for inspiration and to curate stunning décor pieces. This time around, her travels lead her to the vibrant city of Jaipur. Taking to social media, she shared a few photos of herself posing amidst colourful corridors and stunning arches.

“Design is all about inspiration ....from architecture and culture of cities , just like the city palace in Jaipur,” she captioned the click.

Her post prompted good friend Farah Khan to leave a comment that read, "I thought ur redesigning the city.. can’t put it past u."
