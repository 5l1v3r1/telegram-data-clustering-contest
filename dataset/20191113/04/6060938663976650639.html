'Bala' Box office collection day 5: Ayushmann Khurrana starrer mints a total five day business of Rs.60 crore nett

13 Nov 2019, 04:37 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Amar Kaushik directorial comedy entertainer 'Bala' starring Ayushmann Khurrana in the lead role is running successfully at the box office. The film which opened to a good start has managed to maintain to earn very good business in the past few days. The film has been well received with positive reviews and praises have been showered upon the actor.

According to the latest report of BoxofficeIndia.com, the film got the benefit of a partial holiday for Guru Nanak Jayanti on Tuesday as it earned a good business up in the North and some other parts. The Ayushamann starrer went up 15-20% on day 5 as it collected 9.50 crore nett as per early estimates. The total five day business of the film was around 60 crore nett.

Meanwhile, the Monday figure of the film was better than Ayushmann's previous film ' Dream Girl' which had collected Rs 7.20 crore. 'Bala' is expected to do better than 'Dream Girl' and 'Chhichhore' in its first week.

'Bala' is Ayushmann's biggest release to date. The film released across 3000 screens in India and more than 550 screens in the overseas markets.
