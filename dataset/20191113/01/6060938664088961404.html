Katy Perry joins the ranks of Beyonce, Ed Sheeran, Coldplay, Justin Bieber and other singers who performed in India

13 Nov 2019, 01:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Popstar and international singing sensation Katy Perry landed in Mumbai during the early hours of Tuesday ahead of her big performance at a music festival, set to take place over the weekend.

She will also be joined on stage by British sensation, Dua Lipa.

This isn't the first time that the 35-year-old singer has visited India. She vacationed in the country back in 2010 along with her then-beau Russell Brand. During their trip, they visited the Taj Mahal, Agra, where Brand reportedly popped the question. They even tied the knot at a tiger reserve in Rajasthan. Sandly the couple pulled the plug on their marriage in two years.

After seven long years, the singing sensation is back in the country, this time around to perform some of her biggest hits in her debut show in Mumbai city. As KatyCats get ready for a smashing performance, we take a look at the long list of International singers who performed in India.

Beyonce

Although not a pubic concert, Beyonce put on quite the show at Billionaire Isha Ambani and Anand Piramal's wedding celebrations. The songstress treated the guests at the high profile event to a spectacular performance, glimpses of which the rest of us got to see through social media posts.
