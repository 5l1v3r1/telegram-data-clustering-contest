Dad 'shot 14-year-old son to death because he hated the fact he was gay'

Giovanni Melton's foster mum said his father Wendell Melton would "rather have a dead son than a gay son" as friends and family remember the teen who 'brought smiles to people's faces'

13 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Danya Bazaraa, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/danya-bazaraa/

A father allegedly shot his 14-year-old son to death because "he hated the fact that he was gay".

Wendell Melton, 53, is accused of opening fire on Giovanni Melton in his apartment in Henderson, Nevada, US, on Thursday afternoon.

Giovanni's former foster mother Sonja Jones claims she was told by a family member that the argument which led to the shooting was partly about the teenager's sexual orientation.

Sonja said the dad had 'problems with it'.

She told News 3 Las Vegas: "He hated the fact that his son was gay.

"I'm sure that inside of his mind, he would rather have a dead son than a gay son."

Melton is due in a Henderson court next week.
