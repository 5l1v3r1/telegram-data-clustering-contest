Win Dhs3,000 at Time Out Dubai’s pub quiz at Nezesaussi Grill Dubai Marina

Think you know sports? Prove it to win big

13 Nov 2019, 09:57 by Time Out Dubai staff

Massive fan of sports? It’s time to show off your knowledge at Time Out Dubai’s Big Pub Quiz at sports bar Nezesaussi Grill Dubai Marina– as there’s Dhs3,000 up for grabs.

For the first time we’re running an entirely sporting pub quiz – so get down on Wednesday November 27 to prove you know your cricket from your croquet.

The quiz will run over seven rounds – and there are loads of goodies and prizes up for grabs.

As well as the whopping Dhs3,000 to the winning team, we’re also giving away brunches, massages, dinners and more.

There will also be deals available on the night, including a main course and three drinks for Dhs169, so get your chops around a burger or fish and chips while you put that brain to good work.

Rounds will all be sports related so get swotting up on your news, history and trivia today.

Starting at 8pm sharp, head down Nezesaussi Grill Dubai Marina for a fun-packed quiz with a few surprises thrown in.

The quiz entirely free to enter, but be warned – previous editions of have been fully booked days in advance, so be sure to call up and reserve your table in plenty of time.

Quiz teams can have up to a maximum of six members, or as few as one (if you’re not keen on splitting those grand prizes…).

Doors open at 6pm so get down early to Nezesaussi Grill in Dubai Marina and make the most of the evening.

Think you know sports? Prove it and win big.
Free to enter. Wednesday November 27, doors open 6pm, quiz starts 8pm sharp. Nezesaussi Grill Dubai Marina (04 888 3444).
