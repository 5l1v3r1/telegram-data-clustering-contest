House destroyed by fire in Buckingham Tuesday morning

13 Nov 2019, 09:08 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- A house was totally destroyed by a fire Tuesday morning, but thankfully nobody was in the house when it happened.

It happened at a home off Cemetery Road near Buckingham Road at approximately 7 a.m.

According to Tice Fire, no residents were home at the time, but there are reports that a dog managed to escape unharmed.

The fire remains under investigation.
