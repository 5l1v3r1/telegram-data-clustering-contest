Lorraine claims 'tension' between Love Island exes Amy and Curtis during reunion

Amy Hart and Curtis Pritchard had a chat on the red carpet at the ITV Palooza, months on from their Love Island split

13 Nov 2019, 09:27 by Charlotte Tutton

Amy Hart and Curtis Pritchard's split on Love Island 2019 was the talk of the series at one point, before she left the villa.

The tearful islander vowed to remain friends with Curtis, who later moved on with co-star Maura Higgins - with the two still together now.

On Tuesday, AJ Pritchard's brother did the red carpet presenting gig for Lorraine with the coverage airing on the morning show the following day.

Lorraine Kelly praised the dancer for his efforts, before making a claim about Curtis and Amy's reunion on the carpet.

This isn't the first time they've come face-to-face since Love Island, and while things might obviously be awkward both have said in the past that there are no hard feelings.

This didn't stop Lorraine claiming there was "tension" between the former lovers though.

In a clip shown on Lorraine, Amy and Curtis hugged it out before having a little chat on camera.

Lorraine's fashion presenter Mark Heyes introduced the clip, claiming things were "awkward"between the two stars.

He said: "There was an awkward moment though when he met his ex Amy on the red carpet. Here's what happened."

Amy told Curtis there was something "she needed to ask him later in secret", before he put her on the spot and asked her to define the term "palooza".

After she made her prediction she claimed she was "always correct" as they continued to talk.

Lorraine then claimed they "styled it out" but suggested things were tense.

She said: "They styled it out quite well, but there's obviously a little bit of tension but it was okay."

Both hosts then laughed off the moment, before changing the topic.

Lorraine airs weekdays at 8:30am on ITV.
