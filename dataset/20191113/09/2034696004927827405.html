Billie Eilish shares a clip of her new single, ‘Everything I Wanted’ – listen now

13 Nov 2019, 09:45

New music is coming...

Billie Eilish has announced that she’s releasing new single ‘Everything I Wanted’ later this week and now the musician has shared a snippet of the new song. You can listen below.

  • Read more: The Big Read – Billie Eilish: the most talked-about teen on the planet

It comes after the young pop star teased last week that she was releasing new music. In an Instagram story shared on November 6, she told her fans that a new Vanity Fair piece about her wouldn’t be out for a few weeks, but as a “compromise” she’d be releasing a new song next week.

Taking to Twitter last week (November 10), the ‘bad guy’ singer shared the name of the new single and that fans can expect it on Wednesday, November 13, at 4pm Pacific Time.

Titled ‘everything I wanted’, the new song comes after Eilish released her debut solo album ‘When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?’ earlier this year.
