Nando’s customers can get free food all month - just by using their phone

And, remarkably, all you need to do is show the Nando's till assistant that your phone’s predictive text shortlists “Nando’s” when you type in the words “I’m craving”

13 Nov 2019, 09:38 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

We all love a cheeky Nando's - be it macho peas, creamy mash and half a chicken, or a halloumi and mushroom wrap.

The Portuguese inspired eatery has multiple outlets across Birmingham, the Black Country and throughout the wider Midlands region.

And Nando's, which is widely loved by chicken lovers, is giving you the chance to get a free meal.

Customers can get a free Nando's meal every week this month.

And, remarkably, all you need to do is show the Nando's till assistant that your phone’s predictive text shortlists “Nando’s” when you type in the words “I’m craving”.

Customers have to spend £7 to capitalise on the deal, which runs from Monday to Wednesday.

Week one sees Nando's customers get any regular side worth £2.60, with week two giving you three wings.

From November 18 to November 20, week three, you can get any vegetarian burger - worth £6.45 or £6.95 depending on which you choose.

Week 4 (November 25 to 27) gives you ¼ chicken worth £4.10.

 

The promotion is valid when you eat in and when you take away, and is available at all UK and Republic of Ireland Nando's restaurants.

Chicken-lovers shared the mouth-watering deal on  Hotukdeals .
