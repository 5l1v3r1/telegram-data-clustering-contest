'Messi Is The Greatest Of All Time'

13 Nov 2019, 07:30 by Kurt Buckerfield

Following Lionel Messi's sublime hat-trick this past weekend, a Premier League star and Germany international has hailed the FC Barcelona superstar as the greatest player he has ever seen.

The Argentine has been excellent for the Catalan club since his return from injury, having already scored nine goals, of which four have come from direct free-kicks.

Messi was on hand to put three past Celta Vigo in the Blaugrana's 4-1 win on Saturday night, while rival Cristian Ronaldo continues to struggle at Juventus in Serie A.

Weighing in on the ever-lasting debate, Manchester City's Ilkay Gundogan has now revealed his belief that Messi is the greatest player he and the rest of the world has ever witnessed.

"Lionel Messi (is the best player in the world)," he said, according to BeSoccer.

"In my opinion, he has very special abilities. He has talent and he always manages to show it on the pitch. He always has the instinct to make the right decision.

"He uses his speed well against the opponents he plays. He has a strong finish. All in all, no one else has as much quality as Lionel Messi.

"He is the star player of my generation and the greatest footballer of all time. I hope we get to watch him play for a few more years."
