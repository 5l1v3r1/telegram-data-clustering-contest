'Bala' box office collection Day 5: Ayushmann Khurrana's film collects Rs 9.25 crore, continues to weave its magic

13 Nov 2019, 07:39 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushmann Khurrana is back and once again the impressive actor has taken the audience on one humorous joy ride. With Amar Kaushik's comedy-drama, 'Bala' revolving around a man suffering from premature balding turning out to be Ayushmann's biggest opener of his career is continuing to weave its magic at the box office.
Bala Movie Review : Bald is beautiful, indeed

According to the latest report on Boxofficeindia.com, the film has managed to mint another Rs 9.25 crore on its first Tuesday which has taken the total of the film to a staggering figure of Rs 59.50 crores.

The film which also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles apart from Ayushmann is set in Kanpur and showcases how premature balding affects a man's life but in an entertaining fashion.

Talking about how she feels about bald men in real life, Yami opened up saying "I feel bald men are really cool. They look really cool. The idea of the film is to tell people that they should love themselves first, and only then can you expect others to love you," while interacting with the media after a special screening of 'Bala' in Mumbai.

The Guru Nanak Jayanti holiday on Tuesday helped the film and showed a 15 per cent jump in collections from Monday mostly in Punjab and Delhi regions. The first-week collections of 'Bala' is also expected to surpass the total of Rs 70 crore of Ayushmann's last release 'Dream Girl' making 'Bala' his career's best opening film.
