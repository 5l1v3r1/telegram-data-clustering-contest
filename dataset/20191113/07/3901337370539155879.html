Pirates Match To Speed Up Polokwane’s Coach Search

13 Nov 2019, 07:45 by Soccer Laduma

The upcoming match against Orlando Pirates could be one of the reasons Polokwane City are speeding up the process of appointing a new coach.  

Rise and Shine will be hosted by the Buccaneers in an Absa Premiership match at Orlando Stadium on November 26, after the FIFA international break.

The Limpopo outfit is currently in search of a coach to replace Serbian mentor Zlatko Krmpotic, who was relieved of his coaching duties at the club last Friday, following a string of poor results by the team in recent matches.

Bernard Molekwa took charge of the team’s match against Baroka FC – the 1-0 loss in the Limpopo derby on the weekend on caretaker basis. 

Polokwane have lost all of their last five matches in the League. The team was also booted out of the Telkom Knockout by Golden Arrows in the Last 16 stage of the competition.
