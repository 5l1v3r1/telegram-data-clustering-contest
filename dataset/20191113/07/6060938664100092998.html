Juhi Chawla shares a birthday memory with Aamir Khan and it is sure to crack you up!

13 Nov 2019, 07:36 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood actress Juhi Chawla is celebrating her 52nd birthday today. While the internet is flooding with wishes from her fans, the actress recently walked down the memory lane and shared how she celebrated her special day during her childhood and teenage days. Speaking about the same, Juhi reportedly said it is the gifts that she used to be the most excited about on her birthdays. She also added that during her teenage days, she would have day parties and to make it look like night, she would put up black papers all over the window or black curtains and would dance to loud music.

Recalling a funny incident with her co-star and friend Aamir Khan, she shared that Aamir had once came over to her birthday party. He told her he’s got a gift for her which got her all excited. Aamir’s special birthday gift was nothing but a small chocolate. Juhi confessed that although it disappointed her, it has remained with her as a special memory.

Elaborating more on what she loves to do on her special day; Juhi stated that now she likes to celebrate her birthday by give back to the needy people.

On the work front, she was last seen in ‘Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga’. The film also starred Anil Kapoor, Sonam K Ahuja and Rajkummar Rao in lead roles. The film received positive reviews from both the audience and the critics alike.
