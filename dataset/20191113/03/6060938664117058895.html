This day in 2014, Rohit Sharma scored highest individual score in ODIs

13 Nov 2019, 03:23 by ANI

NEW DELHI: It was on November 13, 2014, when opening batsman Rohit Sharma, popularly known as the 'Hitman', registered the highest individual ODI score.

Rohit went on to score 264 runs against Sri Lanka at the Eden Gardens in Kolkata, and till date the knock remains the highest score by any batsman in 50-over internationals.

The 32-year-old was given a reprieve when he was on just four and went on to make the most of it as he scored 260 more runs to make the Sri Lankan bowlers toil on the field.

"#OnThisDay in 2014, Rohit Sharma went big! The Indian opener smashed 264, the highest ever ODI score. The worst part? Sri Lanka dropped him when he was on 4," ICC tweeted.
