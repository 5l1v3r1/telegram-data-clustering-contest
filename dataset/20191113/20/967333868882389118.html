Rapper Kodak Black Sentenced to 3 Years and 8 Months in Prison on Federal Weapons Charges

13 Nov 2019, 20:31 by by Tim Korso

The US rapper earlier pleaded guilty to lying on multiple occasions on forms for firearm background checks as he sought to obtain additional guns for his arsenal, a crime punishable by up to 10 years in prison.

Rapper Kodak Black was sentenced to three years and eight months in prison out of a possible maximum of 10 by a Miami federal judge for violating gun regulations on several occasions.
