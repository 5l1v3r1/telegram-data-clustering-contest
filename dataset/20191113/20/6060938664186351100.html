Kiara Advani shares a sweet post for her 'Kabir Singh' co-star Shahid Kapoor

13 Nov 2019, 20:48 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kiara Advani and Shahid Kapoor have worked together in Sandeep Vanga's 'Kabir Singh' which scored huge at the box office. The film was a remake Vijay Deverakonda's Telugu hit drama 'Arjun Reddy'.

And today, the duo met each other at an event in the city. For the occasion, Shahid was in a black and white suit and he looked dapper in it! Kiara looked stunning in a shimmery thigh-high slit gown.

And Kiara was happy to meet Shahid at the event and she expressed the same on her Instagram story. She captioned the picture, "Met this lovely one after a long time @shahidkapoor".
