3D-Printed Guns

13 Nov 2019, 20:11 by BRIEF

SEATTLE – A federal judge in Washington state ruled Tuesday that the Trump administration violated federal law when it decided to allow a Texas company to publish files on the internet showing how to use a 3-D printer to create guns and gun components.
