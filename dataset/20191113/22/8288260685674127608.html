Liverpool face competition from Manchester United and Arsenal for West Brom starlet - report

The young striker has been catching the eye with his performances for the Baggies this season

13 Nov 2019, 22:57 by Chris Watson

Liverpool face competition from Manchester United and Arsenal in their bid to land West Bromwich Albion starlet Jovan Malcolm, according to reports.

The Reds are said to have stepped up their efforts to sign the 16-year-old striker after he scored four times against their under-18 side last weekend.

Now The Mirror claims that Manchester United and Arsenal are also monitoring the youngster's progress.

Malcolm has scored seven goals and provided three assists in nine games for Albion 's youth team this season.
