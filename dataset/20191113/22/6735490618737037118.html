Little Big Town Shines A Light On Rising Female Artists In ‘Girl Crush’ Performance

13 Nov 2019, 22:04 by Katie Colley

Little Big Town is showing their “Girl Crush” for country music’s up-and-coming female artists.

During the 53rd annual CMA Awards, Kelsea Ballerini took to the stage to perform a stripped-down version of her emotional new single “Homecoming Queen?”, which perfectly segued into an all-female performance of Little Big Town’s 2014 hit “Girl Crush”.
