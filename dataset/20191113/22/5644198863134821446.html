Greta Thunberg sails to Europe, hopes to reach UN climate summit

13 Nov 2019, 22:57

HAMPTON: Swedish teen climate activist Greta Thunberg departed the United States on Wednesday (Nov 13), hitching a ride to Europe aboard a catamaran to attend a UN climate summit in Madrid.

Carrying a large backpack, the 16-year-old and her father Svante boarded "La Vagabonde", a 14m catamaran belonging to an Australian couple that will be her home for the next two to three weeks.

The Swedish activist, who became world famous for founding the "school strikes for the climate," is ending a hectic 11-week North American sojourn that made headlines at every turn as she criss-crossed the United States and Canada.
