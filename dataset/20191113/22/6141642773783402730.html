Zlatan Ibrahimovic thanks LA Galaxy after leaving MLS club

The Swedish striker has spent two seasons with the club.

13 Nov 2019, 22:13

Zlatan Ibrahimovic has thanked LA Galaxy for making him feel “alive again” after mutually parting ways with the MLS club.

The Swedish striker’s two-season spell with the Galaxy ends after defeat to Los Angeles FC in the MLS Cup play-offs conference semi-finals.

Ibrahimovic scored 52 goals in 53 starts in all competitions for Galaxy after leaving Manchester United in March 2018.
