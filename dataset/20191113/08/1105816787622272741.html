Sunny Deol overwhelmed with all the love he received in Pakistan

13 Nov 2019, 08:07 by Entertainment Desk

Bollywood veteran and Indian minister, Sunny Deol, recently visited Pakistan on the inauguration of the Kartarpur Corridor.

The actor, in a recent chat with the media, has revealed that he is overwhelmed with all the love Pakistan and its people have showered over him.

Deol was part of the delegation from across the border that had crossed over to attend the inauguration of the Kartarpur Corridor, earlier this week on the occasion of the 550th birth anniversary of Guru Nanak.

He was all praises for Pakistan whilst speaking to an Indian channel after returning from the inaugurationtion. “It was a great visit to Pakistan. People in Pakistan showered so much love. This is the new beginning for peace and I want everyone to stay together with it.”
