'Housefull 4' box office collection day 19: Akshay Kumar and Kriti Sanon multi-starrer inches closer to Rs 200 crore mark

13 Nov 2019, 08:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The fourth instalment of the popular 'Housefull' franchise continues to weave its magic at the box office in its third week as well. The film which stars an ensemble cast comprising of Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Bobby Deol, Kriti Sanon, Pooja Hegde, Kriti Kharbanda and others is inching closer to the Rs 200 crore mark at the box office.

According to the latest report on Boxofficeindia.com, the Farhad Samji directorial has managed to rake in another Rs 2.50 crores at the box office on its third Tuesday which has taken its total third-week collection to Rs 13.75 crore.

'Housefull 4' which released along with Rajkummar Rao's 'Made In China' and Taapsee Pannu-Bhumi Pednekar's 'Saand Ki Aankh' managed to collect Rs 135.86 crore in its first week which was followed with Rs 48.07 crore in its second week, taking its overall total Rs 197.68 crore.

With Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Bala' dominating the domestic box office currently, experts expect 'Housefull 4' to surpass the 200 crore mark by the end of this week.
