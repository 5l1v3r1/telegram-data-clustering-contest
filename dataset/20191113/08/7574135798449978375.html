Jenny Lewis opening Harry Styles’ 2020 tour, including MSG

13 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Andrew Sacher

One Direction member turned classic rock impressionist Harry Styles tends to bring cool openers on tour, and he just announced a 2020 tour supporting his upcoming album Fine Line which will once again see him joined by some cool opening acts. The US and Canada dates are all with Jenny Lewis, while the Mexico shows have support from rising reggae artist Koffee and the Europe shows have alt-pop breakout King Princess.

The shows with Jenny include NYC’s Madison Square Garden on July 7 and 8. Tickets for those shows go on sale Friday, November 22 at 11 AM with Verified Fan and AmEx presales starting Monday (11/18) at 11 AM. All dates are listed below.

Harry’s new album Fine Line comes out December 13 via Columbia, and he’ll be both the host and musical guest on SNL this Saturday (11/16). King Princess will be the musical guest on SNL the following week (11/23).

Meanwhile, Harry wasn’t the only boy band/girl group member turned solo pop star to announce a tour that hits Madison Square Garden today. Former Fifth Harmony member Camila Cabello (who played SNL last month) did as well, and her tour hits MSG on September 18. All dates and tickets (on sale 11/21 with presales beforehand) here. Camila will also be at that same venue earlier (12/13/19) for Z100’s Jingle Ball alongside Taylor Swift, Lizzo, and more. Camila’s new album Romance comes out December 6 via SYCO/Epic. She’ll also be on former Harry Styles tourmate Kacey Musgraves’ Christmas special.

Speaking of Taylor Swift, she just released a remix of her song “Lover” featuring Camila’s “Señorita” collaborator Shawn Mendes, and you can listen to that here if that’s something you might be interested in.
