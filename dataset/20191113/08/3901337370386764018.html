Sterling To Be Punished Further For Bust-Up?

13 Nov 2019, 08:29 by Kurt Buckerfield

After getting into a physical altercation with one of his Premier League rivals in the England camp, Manchester City superstar Raheem Sterling could reportedly face further punishment for his actions.

Click through the gallery above to see images of Sterling's bust-up with Joe Gomez on Sunday.

On Monday, England boss Gareth Southgate announced that Sterling would play no part in his team's 2020 European Championship qualifier against Montenegro on Thursday, following his in-camp spat with Liverpool defender Joe Gomez.

Sterling and Gomez had clashed in City's 3-1 loss to the Reds at Anfield this past Sunday, and their spat seemingly continued when they crossed paths while reporting for international duty.

The 24-year-old forward accepted his punishment, and released a statement in which he explained that he and Gomez had moved on from what had happened, before pictures emerged of the Liverpool defender training with a scratch below his right eye.

Southgate has stood by his decision to leave Sterling out of the Montenegro match, and it has since come out that the manager first considered sending the player home from the camp, but Gomez reportedly pleaded with the manager to keep the City attacker in the squad.

According to a report in the Daily Mirror, Sterling could still be charged with misconduct by the FA, despite the two players being spotted getting along in training after the incident.

It has been suggested that Sterling's jewellery may have caused the scratch under Gomez's eye before they were pulled apart by their England colleagues in a canteen where the altercation took place.
