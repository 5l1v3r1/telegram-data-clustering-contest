The brilliant advert that Nottingham Forest fans - and Tom Hanks - will love

A blockbuster of a football club and a blockbuster of a film!

13 Nov 2019, 08:39 by Sarah Clapson

One put together a run so phenomenal it took them all the way to the pinnacle of European football; the other simply ran.

A blockbuster of a football club and a blockbuster of a film.

The link between Nottingham Forest and the 1994 Tom Hanks classic Forrest Gump is perhaps not an obvious one to make.

But Reds fans will surely love how the two have been given top billing on a new advert.

A poster from the bank HSBC, which is on display in the city and is part of their localised campaign, has united the two.

It reads: “Nottingham gave us Forest. Alabama told him to run.”

Underneath is the bank’s tagline: “We are not an island. We are part of something far, far bigger.”
