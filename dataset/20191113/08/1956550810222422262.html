Mattel Play! Town launches a weekend brunch with Barney

Food, fun, singing and dancing with the loveable purple dinosaur

13 Nov 2019, 08:30 by Kelly Johnstone

Looking for a new brunch to try that the kids will enjoy as much as you? Mattel Play! Town has introduced a weekend brunch with Barney and all his friends.

The all-day brunch takes place every Friday and Saturday from 10am, and as well as an unlimited feast to satisfy little appetites, everyone's favourite purple dinosaur will be on hand for love and hugs.

From Dhs165 for those who book in advance online, or Dhs175 on the door for those who don't get around to that, little fun-seekers will get access to every single magical experience that the edutainment centre has to offer.

In addition, the indoor play zone City Walk Dubai will be serving up a tasty buffet for all mini brunchers with all their top dishes to tuck into, as well as some sugary party treats too... shhh, don't tell mum.

Barney will of course be making an appearance along with all his Jurassic besties and will be singing and dancing for your little one's pleasure, not to mention encouraging them to join in.

It's Dhs50 for access to the mums and dads zone, which includes the aforementioned tea and cookies, or Dhs75 if you want something more substantial to whet your appetite.
Dhs50 (kids under two), from Dhs165 (kids), from 50 (adults with access to the lounge). Fri 10am-8pm & Sat 10am-7pm. Dubai City Walk, Al Safa Street, playtowndubai.com.
