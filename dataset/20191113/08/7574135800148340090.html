Elton John adds several more 2020 dates to farewell tour

13 Nov 2019, 08:24 by Andrew Sacher

Elton John has once again expanded his Farewell Yellow Brick Road goodbye tour, with 24 new dates added for 2020. According to the announcement, these will be the last dates he’s adding for 2020, and the tour will conclude with TBA shows in 2021.

The newly-added dates happen in Toronto, Montreal, Hershey, Columbus, Tampa, Orlando, Miami, St. Paul, Chicago, New Orleans, Houston, St. Louis, Kansas City, and more. Tickets for all newly-added dates go on sale Friday, November 22 at 10 AM local with an AmEx presale starting Thursday, November 14 at 10 AM local time.

As previously mentioned, Elton plays an NYC-area show at Nassau Coliseum this Saturday (11/16), and he’ll be back in the area in 2020 for shows at Madison Square Garden on April 6 & 7, Barclays Center on April 10 & 11, Prudential Center on April 15, and Nassau Coliseum on April 17 & 18 (tickets).
