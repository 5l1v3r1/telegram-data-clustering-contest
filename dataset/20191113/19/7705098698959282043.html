The Witcher Season 2 Has Already Been Ordered By Netflix

13 Nov 2019, 19:40 by Blair Marnell

Andrzej Sapkowski’s popular fantasy novel franchise, The Witcher, is heading to Netflix next month for its first season. However, Netflix isn’t waiting around before diving into the further adventures of Geralt of Rivia. Lauren S. Hissrich, the showrunner of the series, has announced that The Witcher season 2 is already a go at Netflix. She also expressed her pride for what her team has already accomplished with the series.
