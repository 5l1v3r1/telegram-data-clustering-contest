Kamfanin hakar Uranium ya kaddamar da shirin bunkasa noma a Nijar

13 Nov 2019, 19:34 by Michael Kuduson

Kamfanin Hakar uranium mallakar kasar Faransa na Orano ya kaddamar da wani shirin bunkasa harkar noma a Jamhuriyar Nijar wanda akalla mutane 500 zasu ci gajiyar sa a Yankin Agadez da zai lakume Cefa miliyan11.
Daga Agadez ga rahoton Oumar Sani.
