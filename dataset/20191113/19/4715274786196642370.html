Regional meeting

13 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Jessica M. Bacud

Labor Secretary Silvestre Bello III (left) and Cagayan Gov. Manuel N. Mamba share thoughts as they preside over the meeting of the Provincial Task Force to End Local Communist Armed Conflict at the Sangguniang Panglungsod Session Hall in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan last Thursday. They also met with the Cabinet Officers for Regional Development and Security, Tuguegarao Mayor Jefferson Soriano, and Directors of the Regional Peace and Order Council (inset), which Mamba chairs.
