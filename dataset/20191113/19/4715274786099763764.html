Leaders' forum

13 Nov 2019, 19:08

The 2019 Top Leaders Forum organized by SM Prime Holdings inc. once again gathers senior government officials, top-level executives from the private sector and local chief executives to discuss the country’s status on disaster risk reduction, preparedness, resiliency and sustainability. Joining the forum are (from left) ARISE Philippines board member and Kokusai Kogyo Co. Ltd. chairperson and chief executive officers Sandra Wu, ARISE board member and SM Prime Holdings executive committee chair Hans Sy, United Nations Office for Disaster Risk Reduction Special Representative of the Secretary-General Mami Mizutori, Defense Secretary Delfin Lorenzana and Leyte Rep. (4th District) Lucy Torres-Gomez.
