It's Hard to Be a Grown-Up: Cat Plays With a Kitten

13 Nov 2019, 19:00

You can often see how a cat bites its kittens, hits them with its hind legs or drags them by the scruff, all while the kittens are squeaking. Don't rush to save the little ones, as this could be an educational moment for them.

A cat has been captured on video playing with a kitten. The funny little feline is apparently trying to get his fellow cat's attention, even though the elder one doesn't seem to be very interested or amused. The older feline just knocks the kitten down with its paw several times.
