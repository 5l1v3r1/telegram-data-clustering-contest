Top 50 All-Time WIAA Swimmers / Divers named

50th anniversary Swimming and Diving Meet

13 Nov 2019, 02:26 by Jay Wilson

MADISON -

This week, the 50th Annual  WIAA State Girls Swimming and Diving Meet will take place at the UW Natatorium.

To honor the history of the event, a committee of swimming coaches, media and administrators selected the top 50 girls swimmers and divers in WIAA state history.

Below is the list including the athletes, their high schools, their graduation years and the colleges they attended.
