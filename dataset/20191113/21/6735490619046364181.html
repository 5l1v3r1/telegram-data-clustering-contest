Chris Stapleton And Pink Take The Stage For Moving Performance Of ‘Love Me Anyway’ At CMA Awards

13 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Katie Colley

Chris Stapleton and Pink sure know know how to rock the stage.

The two joined forces inside Nashville’s Bridgestone Arena for the 53rd annual CMA Awards to deliver a powerful performance of their duet “Love Me Anyway”.

The piano ballad, which is featured on Pink’s latest album Hurts 2B Human, was their first televised performance of the song — and they did not disappoint.

Backed by a piano and string instruments, the duo delivered flawless harmonies, making the performance as heartfelt as the track’s emotional lyrics.

Kacey Mugraves, Nicole Kidman, Reese Witherspoon and the rest of the crowd were all brought to their feet as they joined the standing ovation.

This may have been their first televised performance of their collaboration, but back in June the two performed “Love Me Anyway” during Pink’s New York City concert at Madison Square Garden.

Ahead of their performance, Pink posed for pictures on the red carpet with her with her husband Carey Hart and their two kids, daughter Willow, 9, and son Jameson, 2.

Stapleton is up for three awards tonight, including Entertainer of the Year, Male Vocalist of the Year and Single of the Year.
