Violent Soho Are Back With Mournful New Track ‘A-OK’, Their First In Three Years

13 Nov 2019, 21:40 by Joseph Earp

Violent Soho, one of the most exciting acts in the alternate rock scene, have been quiet for some time now.

The last we heard from the band, they were releasing Waco, their big, filthy, heartbreaking collection of hooks, a rock record with an uncommon amount of emotional maturity.

That was way back in 2016. A lot has happened since then, both in Australia and abroad. Things have gotten scarier in the world of politics, the environment has started to fall apart, and our mounting sense of apathy has begun to solidify.
