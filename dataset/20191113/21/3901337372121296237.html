Ronaldo 54, Messi 52

13 Nov 2019, 21:01 by David Kappel

Lionel Messi may have equalled Cristiano Ronaldo's record for the most hat-tricks in LaLiga history last weekend, but his longstanding rival still has more overall.

Click through the gallery above to see the active players with the most hat-tricks in their careers.

The FC Barcelona captain scored a treble in his side's 4-1 win over Celta Vigo last Saturday, with two of his goals coming from direct free-kicks, and the other from the penalty spot.

That hat-trick was his 34th in LaLiga, equalling Ronaldo's record. However, overall, the Portuguese superstar still has two more trebles to his name.

Messi has scored 34 hat-tricks in LaLiga, eight in the UEFA Champions League, three in the Copa del Rey, one in the Spanish Super Cup and six with Argentina.

Ronaldo, meanwhile, netted 34 trebles in the Spanish top flight, eight in the Champions League – seven of them in the colours of Real Madrid and one with Juventus – two in the Copa del Rey, one at the FIFA Club World Cup, one during his time at Manchester United and eight for Portugal.

Messi's treble against Celta Vigo was his first hat-trick in the 2019/20 campaign, while Ronaldo's most recent triple was part of a four-goal haul against Lithuania with Portugal in September.
