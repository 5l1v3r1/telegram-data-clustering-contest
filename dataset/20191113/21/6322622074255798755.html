WeWork’s Quarterly Loss Doubled to $1.3 Billion as IPO Faltered

13 Nov 2019, 21:47 by Bloomberg

(Nov 14): WeWork reported a net loss of $1.25 billion in the third quarter, eclipsing its sales and more than doubling its loss from the same period last year. The quarter coincided with a spending spree in anticipation of the initial public offering, which failed.

Revenue in the quarter was $934 million, up from $482 million a year earlier, according to a financial document that was presented to bondholders Wednesday and reviewed by Bloomberg. A spokeswoman for WeWork parent company We Co. declined to comment on the report. - Bloomberg
