Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, Alia Bhatt, Ayushmann Khurrana, Vijay Deverakonda and other Bollywood actor recently came together for an event. And now, a group picture featuring all them have surfaced over the internet.

Deepika opted for a red ensemble while Alia was in a multicoloured outfit. Ranveer decided to go for a funky look and Ayushmann stayed classy in an all-black look. Fans of the actors are going gaga after seeing them in a single frame!
