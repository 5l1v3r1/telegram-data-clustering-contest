Emmerdale fans accuse soap of turning all kids 'horrible'

Arthur has been bullying Archie

13 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Fans of Emmerdale have accused the soap of turning all kids 'horrible' as they get older.

Over the last few weeks, viewers have seen Arthur Thomas become increasingly jealous of Archie Sharma.

After Archie's mum Rachel died, he came to live in the village with his dad, Jai, who is going out with Arthur's mum, Laurel.

Although Arthur and Archie seemed to get on well at first, Arthur soon turned on the little boy.

In last night's episode (November 12 2019) after Archie ran off from Ashley's memorial picnic, Arthur was furious and accused Archie of ruining his dad's birthday.

When Laurel asked Arthur to take Archie to play on the pirate ship, he took the little boy back to his house instead.

After Arthur said some cruel words to Archie, he called Arthur mean and in return, Arthur punched Archie and he fell unconscious.

All kids in these soaps are horrible brats.

However, viewers have pointed out that this isn't the first time that a soap child had suddenly turned.
