ESB Networks say power to be restored in Ballina by 7 p.m.

13 Nov 2019, 17:46

ESB Networks has stated that a power supply is expected to be restored to over 4,723 customers in the Ballina area by 7 p.m. this evening.

Crews have bene working over the past number of hours to repair a significant power cut in and around the town.
