Alia Bhatt's character to be mellowed down in Gangubai Kathiawadi?

13 Nov 2019, 15:39 by Filmfare

Alia Bhatt is finally working with Sanjay Leela Bhansali. The actress has been waiting to do a film with the ace director and has gotten the opportunity to do so in Gangubai Kathiawadi. The story of the film is based on a book by Hussain Zaidi, Queens of Mumbai, which talks about the lives of the sex workers of Kamathipura of which Gangubai was a drug-peddler and a killer. According to latest reports in a leading daily, SLB is keen on cutting out a few facts about Gangubai in the film and the character will apparently be whitewashed.

According to reports, Alia's character as a prostitute will be glossed over. The actress will soon begin working on the film which is currently in the pre-production stage, we can't wait to witness the magic that this actor-director duo will create for the audience, what about you?
