Did you know there are only three black female billionaires in the world?

13 Nov 2019, 15:30 by Theodora Aidoo

There are several billionaires in our world today and sadly, out of the 2,153, according to Forbes, only 13 of them are Blacks representing 1% of the total sum

Surprisingly, only three of them are Black women. Women are building more wealth but racial gap still persists. Across the various sectors, Black women are excelling but it seems breaking into the billionaires league is a great deal of a challenge.
