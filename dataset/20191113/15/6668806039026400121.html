Here's how Ranveer Singh is prepping for his anniversary

13 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Filmfare

It feels like only yesterday when Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh announced their marriage on social media with some beautiful pictures from their wedding. But believe it or not, the power couple is only a day away from their first anniversary and we’re counting hours before their big day.

To commemorate the day, our beloved DeepVeer, along with their families is headed to Tirupati for a holy vacation. After that, the couple will head to Amritsar to visit Golden Temple. But before the couple rings in their first wedding anniversary, they spent their day prepping for celebration. Deepika Padukone shared a picture on Instagram of Ranveer Singh wearing a face pack. She captioned the picture, “Ranveer Singh in preparation for his first wedding anniversary.”

After a day of spa and some pampering, the two have flown to Tirupati. Well, we’re not surprised Ranveer Singh has a beauty routine before his vacation. After all, it’s not easy being the most gorgeous couple in B-town, right?
