Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone off to Amritsar to celebrate their first wedding anniversary

13 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Kajol Mathur

Exactly this time last year, lovebirds Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh tied the knot in the dreamy Lake Como in Italy. Soon after the couple tied the knot, pictures of them from their big day went viral on the internet and fans couldn't stop obsessing over it. All set to celebrate the big day yet again, Ranveer and Deepika are off to Chennai and Amritsar with their families to celebrate their first year wedding anniversary. On 14th November last year, the couple tied the knot in the Konkani wedding followed by a Sindhi wedding on the 15th. 

Excited to celebrate the special day, Ranveer and Deepika were snapped by our shutterbugs at a Mumbai airport where they were accompanied by Singh's father Jagjit Singh Bhavnani. Looking effortlessly stylish, DP opted for a high neck cardigan which she paired with blue denim and sunnies. Ranveer, on the other hand, looked oh so dapper in a black tee with brown trousers. He rounded off his look with a snapback and a pair of sunnies. The good looking stars were seen busy having some conversation before they entered the airport.
