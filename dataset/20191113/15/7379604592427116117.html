Arsene Wenger appointed Fifa Chief of Global Football Development as he lands first job since leaving Arsenal

13 Nov 2019, 15:28 by https://www.facebook.com/TheSunFootball/

FIFA has hired Arsene Wenger in a full-time role leading its global work developing soccer.

The former Arsenal manager accepted Fifa's offer more than two months after it was first reported, and one week after talks with Bayern Munich about the German champions vacant head coach job.
