13 Nov 2019, 15:31 by Linda Howard

Essex hairdresser Mrs Hinch has single-handedly persuaded a nation to karate chop their cushions, store their spare cleaning products in a cupboard called Narnia and refer to housework as “Hinching”.

She names her sponges, dusters and household appliances and updates her 2.9 million Instagram followers on what she and Kermit have been up to.

Stores have been quick to leap on her bandwagon, with her beloved Home Bargains grouping a selection of her favourite Zoflora disinfectant and Vanish carpet spray in a special ‘Hinch’ corner.

Her book, Mrs Hinch: The Activity Journal , was released last month and instantly became a number one bestseller, topping the charts in stores and online.
