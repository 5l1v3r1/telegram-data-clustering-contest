Pakistan's Hajra Khan lambastes organisers for mismanagement in National Games

13 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Sports Desk

KARACHI: Pakistan football team captain Hajra Khan took to Twitter to voice her displeasure over mismanagement in the 2019 National Games.

Veteran footballer, with three Guinness World Records to her name, criticised organisers for unprofessional behaviour and inadequate facilities provided to athletes.

In a Twitter post, she said revision of timings was not being communicated to players directly after a match was rescheduled.
