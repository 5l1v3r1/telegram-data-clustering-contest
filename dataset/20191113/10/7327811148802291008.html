3-alarm fire burns through Jersey City home

13 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Eyewitness News

JERSEY CITY, New Jersey (WABC) -- A three-alarm fire is burning through a home in Jersey City.

The fire broke out in a home on Neptune Avenue at around 4:50 a.m. Wednesday.

So far, no injuries have been reported.

The cause of the fire is under investigation.
