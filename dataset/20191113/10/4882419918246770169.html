Man City's Gundogan says "Messi is the greatest footballer of all time"

The German international made his opinion on the game's best clear

13 Nov 2019, 10:48 by SPORT.es

Manchester City midfielder Ilkay Gundogan says Barcelona star Lionel Messi is the greatest player of all time.

Despite the debate, which has now existed for a decade, about whether Messi or Cristiano Ronaldo is the best, the German international made his opinion quite clear.

"Lionel Messi [is the best player in the world]," he said. "In my opinion, he has very special abilities. He has talent and he always manages to show it on the pitch. He always has the instinct to make the right decision.

"He uses his speed well against the opponents he plays. He has a strong finish. All in all, no one else has as much quality as Lionel Messi. He is the star player of my generation and the greatest footballer of all time. I hope we get to watch him play for a few more years."
