Latifi looking forward to experiencing another weekend in Brazil

13 Nov 2019, 10:36 by Tom Mason

Nicholas Latifi is once again being allowed by Williams to drive in FP1, his third in succession and he has admitted that he's looking forward to the experience in Brazil.

There has been much talk that the Canadian will drive for Williams next season after Robert Kubica announced that he won't be driving for the team next season.

  "I am excited to get another opportunity to jump back in the car in Brazil. It will be my third FP1 in a row, in such a short period of time, so that’s good for the rhythm," Latifi told Williams.

  "I drove in FP1 there last year, so I know the track and that will help me get up to speed. It is a unique circuit, and an older style compared to the more modern venues. 

 "It is smaller, tighter, but the track has a lot of character and has been on the calendar for a while.

  "I’m looking forward to getting out there and experiencing it once again.

 The season has been a real struggle for Williams, picking up just one point to date.
