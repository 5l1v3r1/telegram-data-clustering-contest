Road cleared after crash between car and HGV

13 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Will Jefford

A busy Suffolk road has been cleared after crash between a car and a lorry this morning.

The incident was reported to the police at around 8.30am today on the A140 at Earl Stonham.

Officers arrived to find a two vehicle collision between a lorry and a car at the junction between the A140 and the A1120.

The road was partially blocked by the vehicles.

The incident caused delays on both the A140 and the A1120 with cars queuing back towards junction 51 of the A14 at Needham Market.

Officers are reporting that there are not any injuries as a result of the crash.

The vehicles have now been recovered and the road is now clear.
