Dubai Fitness Challenge 2019 finale to be celebrated with massive fireworks display

Don’t miss the huge show at Dubai Festival City this weekend

13 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Amy Mathieson

Dubai Fitness Challenge 2019 is drawing to a close this weekend (Saturday November 16) and in celebration of a massive month of exercise there’s set to be a huge fireworks display.

A spectacular fireworks display is going to take place at Dubai Festival City at 7.15pm on Saturday. And the show will also combine with the final fitness IMAGINE show, so it’s going to be an event you don’t want to miss.

Even better, it’s free to attend so make sure you take all your family along.

Looking to squeeze in a few workouts before Dubai Fitness Challenge 2019 draws to a close? Tough Mudder Dubai rounds off the Dubai Fitness Challenge this weekend, while there’s also a vertical stair run. And it’s the final chance to get your free TEPFactor challenge.

The Dubai Fitness Challenge encourages Dubai residents to do at least 30 minutes of exercise, every day for 30 days, whether it’s walking, Pilates, HIIT or boxing.

The initiative was launched in 2017 by His Highness Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed Al Maktoum, Crown Prince of Dubai and Chairman of the Executive Council for Dubai Government, with the aim of making Dubai the most active city in the world.

This year there’s been everything from fitness villages to a massive Sheikh Zayed Road run.
