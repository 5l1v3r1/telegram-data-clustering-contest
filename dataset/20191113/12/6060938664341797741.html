THIS actor suggested 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' remake

13 Nov 2019, 12:12 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan, Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday's remake of 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' has been in the headlines since its announcement. The energetic star cast is the major reason why fans are eagerly waiting for the film to release. It's super impressive trailer has just added up to the excitement of the fans.

As we all know, the film is a remake of an old classic film, featuring Sanjeev Kumar, Vidya Sinha and Ranjeeta, here we bring you some super exciting update about the film, which you may not know. Did you know one of the Bollywood's A-listers have suggested the film's remake? Yes, you heard it right.

In an exclusive chat with Filmfare, producer Juno Chopra revealed, “The idea to remake the film was suggested by Akshay Kumar. He had come home to meet us one day. That’s when he asked us why we were not remaking Pati Patni Aur Woh, which was made under our own banner.” When we asked him that was Akshay his first choice for the film? Juno replied, “No, we never thought of him casting in the film. Kartik was our first choice always.”

'Pati Patni Aur Woh' is directed by Mudassar Aziz and is slated to release on December 6th, this year.
