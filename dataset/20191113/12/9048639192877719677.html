Sewage rates going up in Sterlington

13 Nov 2019, 12:51 by Perry Robinson

STERLINGTON, La. (KNOE) - The Town of Sterlington is about to see a big increase in the sewage rate.

The bills are going up to $68 a month for residential customers.

The Board of Alderman approved the hike which is up from $47 a month.

The mayor says the town's fiscal administrator recommended the increase.

The money will go toward payroll, equipment, and maintenance for the sewer department.

This is the second sewage rate increase for Sterlington in the last few months.
