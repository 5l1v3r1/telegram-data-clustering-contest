A$AP Mob announce Yams Day 2020

13 Nov 2019, 12:53 by Andrew Sacher

A$AP Mob‘s annual Yams Day — their big annual, guest-filled NYC concert that happens in honor of the late A$AP Yams — returns in 2020 on January 17 at Barclays Center. Tickets go on sale Friday (11/15) at 10 AM with artist, official platinum, and VIP presales starting today (1/13) at 1 PM. Presale password = YAMMYGANG. A portion of the proceeds goes to the Always Strive and Prosper Foundation, which was established by Yams’ mom and is “dedicated to fostering greater awareness of drug and substance abuse, as well as providing support for alternatives promoting prevention and education.”

As usual, the guests aren’t announced yet, but they’re usually pretty great. Last year had Soulja Boy, Meek Mill, Flatbush Zombies, Young M.A, Joey Bada$$, and more.
