Overwatch’s Mercy’s Recall challenge is now live

13 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Aran Suddi

Blizzard has updated Overwatch with a new challenge for players to tackle and that is Mercy’s Recall challenge in which players can get a new player icon, new sprays and a Dr Ziegler skin. There are two ways to earning rewards and they include either playing Overwatch or watching streamers on Twitch play Overwatch.
