Priyanka Chopra congratulates Nita Ambani on being elected to the board of Met Museum

13 Nov 2019, 12:39 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Today, honorable Nita Ambani, wife of Mukesh Ambani, who is the chairman of refining-to-telecom giant Reliance Industries Ltd., was elected to become an honorary trustee of the museum, today. Nita, the wife of Asia's richest man, has been promoting Indian art and culture around the world along with sports and development programs inside the country as the head of the conglomerate’s foundation.

With this development, Nita turned to be the first Indian Trustee to be Elected to Board of New York’s Metropolitan Museum of Art. Global star, Priyanka Chopra extended her heartfelt wishes for the lady. She took to her social media handles and wished Nita for her achievement. She wrote, "Congratulations Nita ma’am on being elected to the board of @metmuseum. Your passion for Indian art & seeing it on a global platform is admirable.Very proud. #NitaAmbani."
