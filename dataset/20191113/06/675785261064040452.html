This Morning’s Alison Hammond ‘signing up for Celebs Go Dating’

This Morning favourite Alison Hammond is expected to 'sign up for Celebs Go Dating' after an extended spell as a single mother and now she wants to find love

13 Nov 2019, 06:04 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Alison Hammond has reportedly agreed to join the next series of Celebs Go Dating.

The 44-year-old This Morning presenter has established herself as a favourite among viewers and it has been reported that she is talking to the producers the E4 show, in the hope of finding love.

It has been two years since Alison split from her ex-boyfriend Jamie Savage.

A source told Mail Online : "Alison has been single for some time now. She's always prioritised her career and being a mum to her son.

"But now she's keen to meet someone as he's getting slightly older and she'd love to be romantically involved with a partner.

"Alison is the perfect singleton to take part, she's always laughing and up for having a good time."

Alison has a son, 13-year-old Aidan, from her relationship with taxi driver Noureddine Boufaied.

She is expected to be joined by TOWIE star James Lock, who is single again after splitting from Yazmin Oukhellou.

It would mark another slice of TV action for Alison, who is expected to be hosting her own chat show as well.

The hour-long programme, which will be handily titled Alison Hammond’s Happy Hour, will be a mix of celebrity interviews, feature makeovers, exercise regimes and fun lifestyle segments.

It is expected that among the guests there will be her friends from This Morning, along with a selection of her fellow competitors from her time on Strictly Come Dancing and I'm A Celebrity.
