Ayushmann Khurrana all set to battle his own film as 'Bala' and 'Dream Girl' contest for box office records

13 Nov 2019, 06:02 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The Amar Kaushik directorial 'Bala' which revolves around a young man in Kanpur who suffers from receding hairline features Ayushmann Khurrana in the lead role. The film which turned out to be the National Award-winning actor's biggest opener is competing with his own film 'Dream Girl' at the box office.

According to Boxofficeindia.com latest report, the comic-caper which also features Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar as well has managed to rake in another Rs 9.50 crore on its fifth day and is inching closer to the collection of Ayushmann's 'Dream Girl' which had minted Rs 72 crore in its first week. Not only that but the Raaj Shaandilyaa directorial had opened up at Rs 9.43 core and 'Bala' managed to surpass it with ease on the first day itself.

With a 15-20 per cent growth on Tuesday, Ayushmann's latest release looks adamant to create new records at the box office. The talented actor has been showcasing his versatility from the very first day in the industry and has never feared to experiment with his on-screen roles as well.

Talking about the film, Ayushmann had said, "With 'Bala', we are sending out some really strong and powerful social messages in the most entertaining manner possible and I'm delighted that the film has instantly connected with audiences on day one."
