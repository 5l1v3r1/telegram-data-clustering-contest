Harry Styles announces 2020 tour

13 Nov 2019, 14:19 by WFTS Digital Staff

The star announced his 2020 world tour, "Love On Tour," on Wednesday. The tour is to support his upcoming album, "Fine Line" which comes out Dec. 13.

The tour begins in the UK in April and travels across Europe and North America before ending in Mexico in October.

Styles will be at AMALIE Arena on Monday, Aug. 3, 2020 at 8 p.m.

Tickets go on sale to the general public Friday, Nov. 22.

For the US and Canada dates, and select European dates, American Express Card Members can purchase tickets in select markets before the general public. That begins Monday, Nov. 18 at 10 a.m. local time and lasts through Thursday, Nov. 21 at 10 p.m. local time.

Styles will be joined by King Princess in Europe from April 15 to May 31, Jenny Lewis in the US and Canada from June 26 to September 5 and his final guest will be Jamaican reggae singer Koffee in Mexico from September 29 to October 3, according to a press release.
