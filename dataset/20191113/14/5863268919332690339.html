Plan B: JUI-F issues directions to block Faizabad Interchange, other areas

13 Nov 2019, 14:58 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam-Fazal (JUI-F) has on Wednesday issued directions to its workers to block Faizabad Interchange in Islamabad, Rawalpindi’s T-Chowk and other areas to implement Plan B of Azadi March.

Details suggest that JUI-F and Pashtunkhwa Milli Awami Party blocked Quetta-Chaman road in Qilla Abdullah for all types of traffic, resulting in long queues of vehicles.

The passengers faced a lot of trouble following the blockades. On the other hand, the government has put the police on alert to deal with the situation.
