iKONICS CONTINUE TO TREND HASHTAGS TO SHOW SUPPORT TO B.I AND iKON

13 Nov 2019, 14:53 by shining-light131

155 days has passed yet fans are still fighting for both Hanbin and his six brothers who are still continuing to work under the name of iKON despite everything that has went down. 

For the past 155 days fans continued to show their support by trending a hashtag everyday. It may not be seen by others but everyday thousands of tweets coming from fans all around the world are being sent to shown the never ending support. To remind Hanbin as well as Jinhwan, Yunhyeong, Jiwon, Donghyuk, Junhoe and Chanwoo that there's no single day that they don't think of them and include them in their prayers.

The fandom iKONICS which is called by many a small fandom has done their best to fight for their beloved seven boys. Despite being small has managed to trend several hashtags worldwide and most of all who could forget the iconic #CreditHanbin in which fans called out YG for not crediting Hanbin in his own work, only to find out later that he's not credited for not just one but two songs he worked hard on. Not just that, to add on fans celebrated Hanbin's birthday in the grandest way they can. 

Fans aren't giving up despite being tired, how could they when they're Hanbin's and iKON's seawall? Time and time again they have proven that they're not just fans who are their through the good times but rather a family that the boys could always lean on. Indeed one of the most loyal fandom out there. 

Hang in there iKONICS, the storm will be over soon and seven-colored rainbow will come through beautifully.
