Make Wednesday your movie night at home with these 3 flicks

13 Nov 2019, 14:35

Cape Town – Pop some popcorn and get your favourite drink for movie night from the comfort of your couch.

From comedy, to a stylish crime thriller, to a South African Western – here are our top movie recommendations.

Instant Family

21:50 Movies Premiere (DStv 103)

Mark Wahlberg and Rose Byrne star as a couple who are yearning for parenthood. Looking to adopt a small child, the couple enrol in a foster care class to prepare themselves for the next step in their lives parenthood. After meeting three siblings, they decide to adopt the trio without realising the whirlwind of family life they’ve just let themselves in for.
