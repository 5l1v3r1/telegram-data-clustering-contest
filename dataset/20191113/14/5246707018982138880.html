Japanese Charts: Luigi's Mansion 3 Still Can't Grab Number One As Death Stranding Takes Over

Ring Fit Adventure surpasses 200,000 sales

13 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Ryan Craddock

Famitsu's Japanese chart figures are now in for 4th - 10th November, revealing that Luigi's Mansion 3 has missed out on finishing in top spot once again.

Despite selling more than 150,000 copies in its opening week last week, the game couldn't compete with the new launch of Persona 5 Royal. This week it's Death Stranding's turn to ruin Luigi's party, with 185,000 sales securing top spot.

Elsewhere, Ring Fit Adventure is still performing well, with its total sales now topping 200,000 in the region.

Here are the top 10 (first numbers are this week's estimated sales, followed by total sales):

1) [PS4] Death Stranding (Sony Interactive Entertainment) {2019.11.08} - 185.909 / NEW
2) [NSW] Luigi's Mansion 3 (Nintendo) {2019.10.31} - 54.680 / 205.329
3) [NSW] Ring Fit Adventure (Nintendo) {2019.10.18} - 52.240 / 235.925
4) [PS4] Persona 5 Royal (Atlus) {2019.10.31} - 20.113 / 221.561
5) [PS4] Need for Speed: Heat (Electronic Arts) {2019.11.08} - 16.306 / NEW
6) [PS4] Call of Duty: Modern Warfare (Sony Interactive Entertainment) {2019.10.25} - 16.179 / 171.754
7) [NSW] Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games: Tokyo 2020 (Sega) {2019.11.01} - 14.276 / 33.714
8) [NSW] Minecraft (Microsoft Game Studios) {2018.06.21} - 8.321 / 995.523
9) [NSW] Mario Kart 8 Deluxe (Nintendo) {2017.04.28} - 7.866 / 2.519.528
10). [NSW] Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (Nintendo) {2018.12.07} - 7.693 / 3.324.998
