Leeds have irons in the fire over swoop for midfield star worth over £8m

13 Nov 2019, 14:57

Leeds are interested in signing Rangers midfielder Glen Kamara, according to reports, with two options available to the club over a potential swoop.

Kamara, who moved to Ibrox for £50,000 in January, has become a key part of Steven Gerrard’s side having made 20 appearances for his team already this season, including a solid performance in the 2-0 win over Porto last Thursday.

Football Insider report that Leeds scouts were impressed by what they saw of Kamara in that Europa League tie and have identified him as a potential player Marcelo Bielsa could use in his squad.

However, it is claimed that the Whites will put him on a list of summer targets, given that the West Yorkshire outfit are only planning to spend in January if some of their loan stars are recalled by their parent clubs.

Furthermore, the report states that Rangers have set an asking price of more than £8m for Kamara’s services, but have not given the actual figure it will cost to bring him south of the border.

Kamara, formerly of Arsenal’s academy, left North London for Aberdeen in July 2017 before being snapped up by the Gers last summer.
