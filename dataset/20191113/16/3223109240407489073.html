‘Dumb’ Matt Damon Still Regrets Losing Massive ‘Avatar’ Payday

13 Nov 2019, 16:20 by Jordan Minor

At this point, jokes about how no one remembers anything about Avatar are more memorable than anything actually in Avatar. Despite making nearly $2.8 billion (the highest-grossing movie ever until Avengers: Endgame dethroned it this year) when you ask anyone about James Cameron’s sci-fi nature epic they’ll shrug and say, “Alien cat people?”

Someone who will never forget Avatar though is Matt Damon. The actor is still kicking himself when thinking about how much money he lost by turning down the film’s starring role of…checks notes… Jake Sully. You could say he’s feeling pretty blue.
