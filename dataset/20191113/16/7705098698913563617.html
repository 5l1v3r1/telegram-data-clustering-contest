New The Witcher Stills Highlight Yennefer’s Stunning Beauty

13 Nov 2019, 16:23 by Tudor Leonte

A new set of promotional photos from the upcoming The Witcher has made its way online. The set features a closer look at Anya Chalotra in her role as the lovely sorceress Yennefer. We also got some shots of Henry Cavill’s Geralt of Rivia as well as the first look at Lars Mikkelsen as the mage Stregobor and at Jodhi May as Queen Calanthe.

You can check the new Netflix’s The Witcher stills in the gallery below.

Also starring alongside Cavill’s Geralt of Rivia and Chalotra’s Yennefer are 17-year-old Freya Allan as Ciri, Jodhi May as Queen Calanthe, Mimi Ndiweni as fledgling sorceress Fringilla, Sabrina as Therica Wilson-Read, Millie Brady as outcast Princess Renfri, Adam Levy as the druid Mousesack, as well as Björn Hlynur Haraldsson as Queen Calanthe’s husband, the knight Eist, and MyAnna Buring as Tissaia, head of the magical academy.

Based on the best-selling fantasy series, The Witcher is an epic tale of fate and family. Geralt of Rivia, a solitary monster hunter, struggles to find his place in a world where people often prove more wicked than beasts. But when destiny hurtles him toward a powerful sorceress, and a young princess with a dangerous secret, the three must learn to navigate the increasingly volatile Continent together.

Schmidt Hissrich will serve as executive producer and showrunner for The Witcher. Sean Daniel is set to executive produce under the Sean Daniel Company together with his partner Jason Brown. Additionally, Tomek Baginski and Jarek Sawko of Platige Image will executive produce as well.
