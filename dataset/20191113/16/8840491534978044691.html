Mayo call for action to halt footpath cyclists

13 Nov 2019, 16:25

AN appeal has been issued to An Garda Siochana in Mayo to clampdown on cyclists on footpaths.

It follows an incident in which an elderly woman was knocked down at Linenhall Street in Castlebar.

Councillor Ger Deere said the cyclist entered the street from the archway into SuperValu before the accident occurred.

He hit out: “We need byelaws in this town to prevent this kind of thing from happening. Cyclists should not be on footpaths unless they are pushing their bikes.”

Councillor Michael Kilcoyne said the location in question is particularly dangerous for pedestrians already without the added peril of a cyclist appearing from nowhere.
