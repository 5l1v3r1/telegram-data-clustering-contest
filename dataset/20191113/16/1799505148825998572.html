Wild Horses

13 Nov 2019, 16:01 by BRIEF

SACRAMENTO — In the never-ending battle over wild horses, the Friends of Animals sued the U.S. Bureau of Land Management in federal court, fighting a 10-year plan to drug and castrate wild horses in Lassen County, California.
