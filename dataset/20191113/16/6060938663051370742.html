Deepika Padukone opens up about life post her marriage with Ranveer Singh

13 Nov 2019, 16:26 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh will be celebrating their first wedding anniversary tomorrow and for the same, the jetted off to Tirupati Balaji. During an interview, Deepika opened up about her life post marriage and how they deal with their hectic schedules. The actress went on to say that they are quite busy with their work and also added that it will be like that for a while now.

However, she also stated that even after all of this, they ensure to spend a good time with each other whenever possible and value their time together.

Talking about her work life post marriage, she said that it hasn't been changed from earlier times. Deepika added that as they are obsessed with their work, they do not think about their personal relationship. They stop being husband and wife whenever camera starts rolling. All they think is about the characters and nuances of the scenes of the film.

Meanwhile, the duo will be sharing screen space in Kabir Khan's upcoming sports drama '83'. The film has been slated for April 2020 release.
