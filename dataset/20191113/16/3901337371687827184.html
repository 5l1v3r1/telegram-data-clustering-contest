Breaking: Telkom Knockout Final Venue & Date Announced!

13 Nov 2019, 16:03 by Soccer Laduma

The Premier Soccer League has confirmed the venue and date for this year’s Telkom Knockout Final.

Last season, the final of the prestigious competition between Baroka FC and Orlando Pirates took place at the Nelson Mandela Bay Stadium in the Eastern Cape.

Golden Arrows, Mamelodi Sundowns, Kaizer Chiefs and Maritzburg United are the four teams that will be competing for the two spots in this year’s final. 

The PSL has confirmed that this year’s TKO Final will be hosted at Durban’s Moses Mabhida Stadium on December 14.
