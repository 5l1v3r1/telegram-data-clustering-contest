UK Gamers Can Get Their Hands On Pokémon Sword And Shield Early Thanks To GAME's Midnight Launch

65 stores taking part

13 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Damien McFerran

Based in the UK? Itching to get your hands on Pokémon Sword and Shield? Well, you'll be pleased to know that high street chain GAME is conducting a series of midnight openings on Thursday night/Friday morning across 65 of its stores, which means you can get your grubby hands on the latest title in the series a few hours earlier than planned.
