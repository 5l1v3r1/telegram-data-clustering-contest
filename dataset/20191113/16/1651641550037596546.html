Motorola’s foldable Razr phone leaked ahead of official launch

13 Nov 2019, 16:39 by Pradeep

Earlier this year, it was reported that Motorola is planning to revive the old RAZR brand with a limited edition foldable phone. Later some of the specs of this foldable phone got leaked. Last month, Motorola sent invites to the press for a November 13 launch event in Los Angeles. The invitation mentioned “highly-anticipated unveiling of a reinvented icon.” Last month, several official renders of Moto razr got leaked online.

Motorola razr is expected to launch with the following mid-range specs:

  • sdm710, likely Qualcomm Snapdragon 710
  •  Main display: 6.20? 2142×876 by BOE
  • Closed display: 2.69? 800×600 also by BOE
  • 4/6GB RAM
  •  64/128GB storage
  •  Now believed to have 2510mAh battery
  •  SKUs: Verizon (US) and ROW

Today, the FCC listing of this upcoming Motorola razr device went public and it has revealed several images and details about the device. The new razr device will have an USB-C port for charging and it will come with a fingerprint sensor for easy and quick authentication.

Motorola is expected to officially announce this device at a press event later today.

Source: FCC
