This is how Max Verstappen prepares for the Brazilian Grand Prix

13 Nov 2019, 16:23 by Matt Gretton

The 2016 Brazilian Grand Prix had it all. Rains showers, red flags, crashes and restarts. Max Verstappen managed to survive all the drama to finish on the podium. According to his Twitter account, Verstappen uses this race to give motivation for the 2019 instalment.
