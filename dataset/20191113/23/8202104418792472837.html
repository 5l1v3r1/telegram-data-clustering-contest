Saskatoon police investigate death of 13-year-old girl

13 Nov 2019, 23:33 by Saskatoon StarPhoenix

The girl was reportedly "in medical distress" when first responders found her at a home on Avenue S South. The death is not currently being treated as suspicious.

Saskatoon police are investigating the sudden death of a 13-year-old girl.

Officers and Medavie Health Services responded on Nov. 10 to a home in the 400 block of Avenue S South after a report of a 13-year-old girl “in medical distress,” Saskatoon police said in a news release issued Wednesday.

The teen was transported to hospital, where she died on Tuesday.

Investigators are not treating the death as suspicious at this time, according to a police spokeswoman.

The provincial coroner and the city police major crimes unit are involved in the ongoing investigation, police said.
