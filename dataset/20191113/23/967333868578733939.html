White House Says Star Witnesses in House Impeachment Never Spoke With Trump

13 Nov 2019, 23:37

WASHINGTON (Sputnik) - Evidence in the US House impeachment probe thus far is based on second-hand information from bureaucrats who have never spoken with President Donald Trump, White House spokeswoman Stephanie Grisham said in a statement on Wednesday.

“Dems star witnesses can’t provide any first-hand knowledge of any wrongdoing by POTUS”, Grisham said via Twitter. “These are essentially two bureaucrats with a foreign policy gripe”.
