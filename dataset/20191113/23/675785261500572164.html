Liam Payne and Maya Henry look seriously loved-up as they enjoy glitzy event

Liam and Maya looked happier then ever when they tread the red carpet among the great and the good of the showbiz world

13 Nov 2019, 23:36 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Liam Payne and Maya Henry were the picture of true love.

And the couple took their love to the glitzy Bvlgari launch celebration for the Save The Children 'Give Hope' campaign, in London on Wednesday.

The singer, 26, looked typically suave in a white collarless shirt unbuttoned to tease his hunky physique while he paraded his beautiful girlfriend, 18, on his arm.

Maya was impossible to miss in a red suit which had a daring cut to reveal her cleavage.

The hot pair wrapped their arms around each other when they put on an affectionate display for everyone to see.

They watched on with admiration as they saw the switching on of the twinkling Christmas light installation.

Liam has previously talked about how happy Maya has made him.

They have grown close in recent months and have been flaunting their romance all over London.

Also the former One Direction star has been name checking his ex Cheryl in recent weeks.

The father-of-one enthused sincerely that he hoped his former flame was happy.

Liam lathered on the praise as he hailed Cheryl as a "great mum" who deserves "to find happiness" in a discussion on Straight Talking With Ant Middleton.

He elaborated: “I hope she’s happy, she deserves it, she’s a great mum, she works hard, she deserves to find happiness.”

The exes amicably raise their two-year-old son Bear together after their romance come to an end.
