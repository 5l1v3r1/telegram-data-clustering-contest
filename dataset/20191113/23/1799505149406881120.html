Reality TV

13 Nov 2019, 23:43 by BRIEF

DALLAS – A federal court in Texas dismissed a woman’s suit against Carnival in which she claims that the company stole her idea for a reality television show featuring customers vacationing on cruises. A letter of intent did not establish that both parties entered into a valid contract.
