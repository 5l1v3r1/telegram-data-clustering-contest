East Ayrshire Primary school and village suffer power cut

Power outages have been reported in the KA2 and KA11 area.

13 Nov 2019, 13:25 by Ross Dunn, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/ross-dunn/

A number of homes and a primary school in the KA postcode area have suffered a power cut this afternoon.

East Ayrshire Council say that the school will remain open as normal.

Scottish Power Energy Networks has said that they hope to restore the power by 2.15pm.

 

East Ayrshire Council said: "We're aware that there is a power cut in the Crosshouse Area that is affecting Crosshouse Primary & ECC.

"Lunches are still being provided for the pupils and it's hoped that the situation will be resolved within the next two hours.

"The school and ECC are remaining open as normal."

 

Scottish Power Energy Networks' website states: "There is a power cut affecting the KA2 and KA11 postcode area of Kilmarnock and Irvine.

"We did not know in advance there would be a power cut, and became aware of this at 12.10PM.

"Our control centre will attempt to reset the network remotely to restore power in stages, and our emergency response team are on their way to site to investigate and repair the fault.

 

"Our team will work to get your power back on as quickly and as safely as possible by 2.15PM.

"Once they arrive, or when we receive any update regarding the cause, we will be able to provide you with more information.

"We apologise for the inconvenience and thank you for your patience."
