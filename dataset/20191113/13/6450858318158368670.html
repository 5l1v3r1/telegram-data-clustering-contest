OnePlus 6T price & availability

The OnePlus 6T is no longer available directly from OnePlus but you can still find some models on Amazon if you want to buy SIM free. The phone sells for £529 with 8GB RAM and 128GB storage. That's still a bit much to pay for a year-old phone though, especially with newer models now available, though we're hoping prices might drop as we get closer to Black Friday.

The phone is a bit tricky to find in part because it's not the latest model any more. If you'd rather grab one of the more recent phones, then see our round up of the best OnePlus 7 and 7 Pro deals or check out the even newer OnePlus 7T.
