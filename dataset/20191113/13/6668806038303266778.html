Bala continues to have a great run at the box-office during weekdays

13 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Vedanshi Pathak

Ayushmann Khurrana, Yami Gautam and Bhumi Pednekar's latest release Bala is creating a stir at the box-office. Directed by Amar Kaushik, the film hit the theatres last Friday and revolves around Bala (Ayushmann Khurrana) who faces trouble in his love life because of premature balding. Bala also gives a message about self-love and its importance in ones life. The film has been receiving praise from all ends and has been doing great at the box-office too.

On its opening day, Bala made Rs 10.15 crore, followed by Rs 15.73 crore on Saturday, Rs 18.07 crore on Sunday, Rs 8.26 crore on Monday and Rs 9.52 crore on Tuesday making the grand total an enormous Rs 61.73 crore in just five days. The film is sure to enter the Rs 100 crore club by this weekend, we congratulate the team on their success.
