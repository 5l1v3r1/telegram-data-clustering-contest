Arjun Kapoor unveils Nawab Shah and Mantra's first-look from 'Panipat'

13 Nov 2019, 13:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ashutosh Gowarikar's dream project, 'Panipat' is now all set to hit the silver screens in the coming weeks. This Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon starrer has been in the news of late post their trailer release. The makers are treating the masses with unveiling first look posters of the prominent characters from the film. It started with revealing Arjun, Kriti and Sanjay Dutt's first look poster followed by the rest of the prominent star cast.

Today, Arjun Kapoor took to his social media handles and shared Mantra and Nawab Shah's first look posters. While Nawad portrays Ibrahim Khan Gardi, Mantra can be seen as Najib-Ud-Daula.

Talking about the characters, Ibrahim, is a loyalist, he served Peshwa of the Maratha Empire and was captured and killed by the Afghan Pathans during the Third Battle of Panipat in 1761. On the other hand the cunning, Najib-Ud-Daula joined hands with Ahmad Shah Abdali, against the Marathas during the war.

Both Mantra and Nawab Shah look convincing and also give the needed justice to the characters.
