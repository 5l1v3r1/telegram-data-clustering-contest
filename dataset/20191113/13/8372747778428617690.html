Public invited to Census presentation in Port Charlotte

13 Nov 2019, 13:15 by WFTX Digital Team

CHARLOTTE COUNTY, Fla. -- A U.S. Census Bureau representative will be giving a public presentation on the 2020 Census next month in Port Charlotte.

It will be held at 3 p.m. on December 3rd in Room B106 of the Charlotte County Administration Center, located at 18500 Murdock Circle.

The meeting is open to the public. No reservations are required.
