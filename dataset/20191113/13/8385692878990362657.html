Xiaomi Planning To Release Another Camera-Centric Smartphone

13 Nov 2019, 13:48

It seems like Xiaomi is planning to release yet another camera-centric smartphone. As most of you probably already know, the Mi CC9 Pro aka Mi Note 10 can be referred to as a camera-centric smartphone.

Xiaomi included five cameras on the back of this phone. The device comes with a 108-megapixel main camera, and a separate camera for telephoto, ultrawide, and macro shots. It actually includes two telephoto units, and two dual-LED flash sensors.

The Mi CC9 Pro / Mi Note 10 managed to hit a really high score on DxOMark. The device scored 121 overall, and it matches the Mate 30 Pro in that regard.

Xiaomi may already be planning to release a new camera-centric smartphone

This phone may not retain the status of Xiaomi's best camera smartphone. If a Chinese tipster is to be believed, Xiaomi is preparing something else for us, soon. That device will, allegedly, trump the Mi CC9 Pro / Mi Note 10 in the camera department.

More info regarding the device will be revealed during Xiaomi's AIoT event which is supposed to occur in the near future. This information was published by the tipster's official account on Weibo, but take it with a grain of salt.

It is also rumored that Xiaomi is once again partnering with Samsung. As a reminder, that Bright HMX sensor that is included in the Mi Note 10 is actually an ISOCELL sensor. A camera sensor that was developed by both Samsung and Xiaomi.
