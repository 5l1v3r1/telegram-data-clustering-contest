Supergirl Guest Actor Rahul Kohli streams on Microsoft’s Mixer

13 Nov 2019, 13:43 by Lewis White

British actor Rahul Kohli has joined a host of video game streamers in the move to Microsoft Mixer.

Joining the likes of Ninja, Shroud and King Gothalion, Kohli has launched his very own Mixer channel. The actor, who has expressed his wish to be referred to as Supergirl Guest Actor Rahul Kohli, started his Mixer career with a stream of Star Wars Battlefront 2.

Kohli’s Mixer channel is Star Wars focused at the moment. The channel description currently reads: “That really unknown actor who decided to exclusively play and stream as many LucasArt’s Star Wars titles as possible.” You’re not that unknown, man. We’ve seen you on Funhaus.
