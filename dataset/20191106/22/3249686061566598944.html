Rainbow reveals MV teaser for 'I Dream Of You'

06 Nov 2019, 22:22 by sl278

Rainbow surprised fans yesterday with the announcement that they were making a comeback and the group has revealed their first MV teaser for their song "I Dream of You". The girls are seen emotionally standing in the recording room, preparing to sing to a beautiful ballad. 

The video is set to release today at 6 pm KST so stay tuned for more updates!
