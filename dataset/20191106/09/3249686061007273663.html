1Team join galactic army in 'Make This' MV

06 Nov 2019, 09:47 by germainej

1Team have revealed the futuristic music video for "Make This"!

In their new MV, 1Team join a galactic army as they travel through space and prepare for a futuristic battle. "Make This" is a trendy, hip hop track from the group's third mini album 'One', which also includes the sweet confession song "Love It" and the emotional ballad "Not Afraid".
