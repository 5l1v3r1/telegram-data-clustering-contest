ZE:A's Dongjun reveals emotional MV for 'Alone'

06 Nov 2019, 09:49 by germainej

ZE:A's Dongjun has revealed his emotional music video for "Alone".

The MV follows a man who ends a lonely day in tears. "Alone" is the title song of Dongjun's new mini album, and it's about being okay despite being all by yourself.
