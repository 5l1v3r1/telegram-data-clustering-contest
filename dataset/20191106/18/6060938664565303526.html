Photos: Varun Dhawan, Shraddha Kapoor, Ileana D'Cruz, Sara Ali Khan and other Bollywood celebs attend 'Bala' screening

06 Nov 2019, 18:16 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushman Khurrana's 'Bala' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. The film also got involved itself involved in several controversies.

The film's story revolves around a person who suffers from premature balding played by Ayushmann. The film stars Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam as the leading ladies.

Now, ahead of the release, the makers of the film hosted a special screening for the friends from the fraternity. Celebs like Varun Dhawan with his girlfriend Natasha Dalal, Ileana D'Cruz, Shradhha Kapoor, Sara Ali Khan, Janhvi Kapoor, Varun Sharma, Shashank Khaitan, Homi Adajania, Dinesh Vijan and more attended the screening held in the city.

All of them posed for some pictures for the paparazzi and were all smiles for the cameras.
