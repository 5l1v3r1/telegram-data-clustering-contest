Mapeza: There's Been A Massive Improvement

06 Nov 2019, 23:01 by Soccer Laduma

Chippa United head coach Norman Mapeza feels there has been a massive improvement from the side since his arrived at the club.

Despite falling yet another loss, Mapeza insists the side has improved over the past few weeks.
