US House Committee to Hold First Open Hearings in Impeachment Inquiry Next Week - Schiff

WASHINGTON (Sputnik) - The US House Intelligence Committee will hold its first public hearings in the Trump-Ukraine impeachment inquiry next week, Chairman Adam Schiff said in a statement.

06 Nov 2019, 20:59
"Next week, the House Intelligence Committee will hold its first open hearings as part of the impeachment inquiry", Schiff said via Twitter. "On Wednesday, November 13, 2019, we will hear from William Taylor and George Kent. On Friday, November 15, 2019, we will hear from Marie Yovanovitch".

Schiff said more public hearings in the impeachment inquiry will be announced soon.
