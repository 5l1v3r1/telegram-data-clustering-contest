Photo: Priyanka Chopra enjoys paan as she tackles the cold weather of Delhi in a sweater

06 Nov 2019, 20:27 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Priyanka Chopra has been juggling between India and the US for her work commitments. Now, the actress is back in her home country and enjoying her time in Delhi.

The actress took to her Instagram to share a picture of herself enjoying a paan along with fighting the capital city's winter by wearing a sweater. In the photo, the actress looked cute in a black outfit with a brown hoodied sweater over it. She captioned the picture, "Set life. Paan tasting!" and tagged the location as New Delhi.
