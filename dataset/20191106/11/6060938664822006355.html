'Marjaavaan' new song 'Kinna Sona': Sidharth Malhotra and Tara Sutaria's innocent love story will tug at your heartstrings

06 Nov 2019, 11:42 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

With the trailer of 'Marjaavaan' already leaving the audience on the edge of their seats, the makers of Sidharth Malhotra, Tara Sutaria, Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh starrer are making sure that they leave no stone unturned in promoting the film.

While the leading cast of the film is busy with promotional events and interviews, the makers dropped another song on the internet on Wednesday evening which is titled as 'Kinna Sona'.

The song which is crooned by Meet Bros, Jubin Nautiyal and Dhvani Bhanushali is a remake of a popular Ustad Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan song 'Kinna Sona Tenu Rab Ne Banaya'. This is the third song in the film which is a remake of an old classic track after 'Ek To Kum Zindagani' and 'Haiya Ho'.
