What channel is Man Utd vs Partizan Belgrade on? TV and live stream information

It has been a positive campaign in the Europa League so far for United in terms of results and they can qualify for the next round with victory

07 Nov 2019, 07:20 by Sam Fletcher

Manchester United's Europa League journey continues as they take on Partizan Belgrade.

Although The Red Devils’ Premier League campaign has been underwhelming, they top Europa League Group L on seven points and a win in this tie confirms their progression to the knockout stages.

Despite having 61 percent possession against Partizan two weeks ago, Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's men only managed to muster one shot on target in the entire 90 minutes.

Anthony Martial’s penalty was the difference between the sides last time out, but United fans will be hoping the game is more of a formality on home soil.

Here’s everything you need to know before the match...

When is the game?

The game is on Thursday 7 November.

Kick-off is set for 8pm.

Where is the game being played?

Old Trafford is the venue for the game.

Man United have not lost a Europa League home match since a 3-2 defeat against Athletic Bilbao in 2012.

What TV channel is the game on?

For UK viewers the game is on BT Sport 2.

Coverage is scheduled to start at 7:30pm.

Can you live stream the game?

If you are a BT Sport subscriber and are based in the UK, you can stream the action through their website and the BT Sport app.

Alternatively, you can follow all of the action on the Mirror Football live blog.

Manchester United team news

United have a host of players on the treatment table including star-man Paul Pogba as he recovers from an ankle injury.

Manager Solskjaer is not expecting him back till December, the French international has only made six appearances for the Red Devils this season.

Also sidelined are Diogo Dalot, Eric Bailly, Axel Tuanzebe, Timothy Fosu-Mensah, Luke Shaw and Nemanja Matic.

Partizan Belgrade team news

Belgrade manager Savo Milosevic boats a fully-fit squad ahead of the match with no injury concerns.
