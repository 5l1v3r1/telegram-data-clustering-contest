Video: Miss BumBum Suzy Cortez Shows Off Her Ball Control in Thong and Barcelona T-Shirt

07 Nov 2019, 05:33

In the video clip, posted on her Instagram, the Brazilian model is seen jumping in slow motion into the air and spectacularly bouncing the ball off her chest, while wearing only a Barcelona T-shirt - knotted at the front - and a purple thong.

Miss BumBum, the 29-year-old athletic model, also posed attractively using a goalpost to perform her captivating "keepie-uppies".

In September, the eager internet celebrity made a video in which she took off all her clothes as a form of honouring of her idol, football star Lionel Messi, after he won Best FIFA Men's Player.
