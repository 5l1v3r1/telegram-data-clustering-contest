Throwback Thursday: Sara Ali Khan's adorable childhood pictures prove that she was born to be a star

07 Nov 2019, 10:16 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ever since her debut in Abhishek Kapoor's 'Kedarnath', Sara Ali Khan has come a long way in her career and established herself as one of the most talented actresses on the block. The young starlet left everyone's stunned in Rohit Shetty's 'Simmba' and is currently gearing up for his next film.

The 24-year-old actress has been quite active on social media and keeps on sharing pictures from her personal life from time to time apart from her photoshoots and glimpses from her film's sets.

Taking to Instagram on Thursday afternoon, Sara shared a series of throwback pictures from her childhood days with a caption which read as "Waiting for my shot since 2000 ⏰ 🎥 🎬🔌🔜🙇🏻‍♀️#apnatimeayega #tbt #sarakadrama"

The overloaded cuteness and that starry attitude is quite evident in these pictures which prove that Sara was born to become a star. She can also be seen dressed in traditional outfits and looks cute as a button in these pictures.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Sara will soon be seen opposite Kartik Aaryan in Imtiaz Ali's next and is currently shooting with Varun Dhawan for the remake of 'Coolie No. 1'.
