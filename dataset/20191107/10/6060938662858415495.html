Watch Video: Akshay Kumar and Katrina Kaif recreate their 'Namastey London' magic on the sets of 'Sooryavanshi'

07 Nov 2019, 10:41 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Akshay Kumar is collaborating with Katrina Kaif once again and the duo are all set to weave their magic on the silver screen in Rohit Shetty's 'Sooryavanshi'. The film which has been making headlines since its inception has already grabbed the audience's attention due to Ranveer Singh and Ajay Devgn's presence in it along with the Khiladi of Bollywood.

Meanwhile, Akshay recently made his first music video appearance opposite Kriti Sanon's sister Nupur in B Praak's soulful track 'Filhall' and the teaser has already got his fans excited. In response to the fan comments on the video saying that the teaser reminded them of Namastey London, Akki replied to them in his own style and it's sure to win them over.

Akshay and Katrina recreated their 'Namastey London' magic on the sets of 'Sooryavanshi' and the superstar took to social media to share the video.
