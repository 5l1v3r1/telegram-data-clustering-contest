Plymouth man accused of bleach attack on mum and two-year-old daughter

Defendant allegedly threw corrosive fluid over woman and toddler, court told

07 Nov 2019, 13:22 by Stuart Abel

A Plymouth man has appeared in court charged with throwing bleach at a woman and her two-year-old daughter.

The 33-year-old faced city magistrates accused of attacking the mum and toddler intending to seriously injure them.

He answered two charges of throwing a corrosive fluid with intent to burn, maim, disfigure or disable the pair on March 24.

The man, who cannot be named for legal reasons, is also accused of assaulting the mother causing actual bodily harm.
