Saudi King Salman Receives Phone Call from Jordanian King

07 Nov 2019, 06:15

The Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Salman bin Abdulaziz, received on Wednesday a telephone call from Jordan’s King Abdullah II.

During the conversation, the King of Jordan congratulated the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques on the success of Saudi Arabia's efforts in signing the Riyadh agreement between the Yemeni legitimate government and the Southern Transitional Council.

The Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques thanked the Jordanian King, confirming that Saudi Arabia would always stand by the side of Yemen and remain keen on its stability and prosperity.
