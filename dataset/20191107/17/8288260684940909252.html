'Perfect' - West Ham fans are loving January transfer hint involving West Brom

Latest West Brom transfer news | West Ham fans are all in agreement about Grady Diangana's future

07 Nov 2019, 17:16 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Manuel Pellegrini has hinted Grady Diangana will be staying at West Bromwich Albion beyond January – and West Ham United fans are loving it.

The 21-year-old has been a revelation for Albion since joining from Premier League West Ham on a season-long loan in August.

He’s scored four goals and set up another four to help push Slaven Bilic’s side to the top of the Championship.

That kind of form understandably hasn’t gone unnoticed and with West Ham having the option to recall Diangana when the window reopens, Manuel Pellegrini was asked about his plans for the winger.

The Hammers boss indicated he’s happy to let Diangana continue his progess at The Hawthorns, saying: “I think that Grady is doing what we wanted for him, same as Josh Cullen. He’s a player without any doubt will be part of West Ham in the future.

“I trust a lot in Grady and I think it was better for him to go and play 40 games this season and be ready for next season, than to play here just some games. I think he must finish his season where he is.”
