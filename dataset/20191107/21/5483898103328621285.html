Report The Adelaide Crows Have Picked Their New Footy Boss

07 Nov 2019, 21:38

Adelaide FC’s new football manager could be announced as early as today, says FIVEaa’s Stephen Rowe.

Rowey told FIVEaa Breakfast on Friday:

“Today could be an announcement day for the Adelaide Crows. We’re hearing they’ve got their man, the GM of football -- I think it’s Adam Kelly, SANFL GM of footy.

“Matty Nicks needs to sign it off. Obviously these two are going to deliver that footy program together so a super important role. I’m hearing today. If it’s next week it will be what it is.”
