Jennifer Lopez Persistently Wows Fans With A Revealing Black & White Playsuit

07 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Image via DFree / Shutterstock.com
Jennifer Lopez performed at the iHeartRadio Fiesta Latina in Miami last Saturday, impressing the crowd with her brilliant performance and a stunning, futuristic black and white outfit.
Lopez put on a black and white latex bodysuit that showed off her curves as a black belt cinched at her waist.
She donned fishnet tights and thigh-high black boots with silver detailing to complete her edgy ensemble. Lopez also wore her long blonde tresses down and had them parted in the middle.
The singer delivered upbeat tracks during her concert, including her new hit single Baila Conmigo.
