IU posts adorable childhood photos on Instagram to celebrate her brother's birthday

07 Nov 2019, 22:21 by sl278

IU has revealed some adorable pictures from her childhood.

She uploaded a series of family pictures on November 7 with a caption stating: "My beloved younger brother's birthday." The pictures show IU as a child, and it's clear that her adorable and beautiful looks have stayed with her since then.
