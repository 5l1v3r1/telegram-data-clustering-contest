Wi-fi sa Caloocan

07 Nov 2019, 11:13 by Edd Reyes

CALOOCAN City Mayor Oscar “Oca” Malapitan yesterday announced the launching of free WiFi connection he earlier promised to fulfill before the end of the year.

Mayor Oca, represented by Councilor Enteng Malapitan, City Administrator Engr. Oliver Hernandez and InfiniVan President Enrique Uy led the launching of free WiFi kiosks at the New Caloocan City Hall Complex and Caloocan City People’s Park that will provide residents of two-hours free internet connection per day

Prior to this, students of the University of Caloocan City, for almost one and a half year were given free wifi internet connection that rendered great help for their studies.
