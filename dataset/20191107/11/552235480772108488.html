Jacqueline Ullmer was pulled from the water on October 22 after she disappeared from her home in bad weather in Carrbridge, Cairngorms.

07 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

The body of a missing mum has been found in the River Spey almost two months after she vanished.

A major search was launched, with cops drafting in a Coastguard helicopter and mountain rescue crews to track her down.

Officers confirmed the grim discovery is Jacqueline and send their thanks to those involved in the effort.
