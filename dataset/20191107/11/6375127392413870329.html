Druggie nabbed on mom’s tip

07 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Melnie Ragasa-Jimena

A jobless man was arrested after his mother reportedly tipped off the police about his alleged illegal drug activities.

Acting Quezon City Police District (QCPD) Director, Police Colonel Ronnie S. Montejo,  identified the suspect as Anthony Alvarez alias Tonyo, 44, married and resident of Block 9 Lot 8, Dayap St., Palmera Subdivision, Bgy. Sta Monica, Novaliches.

Alvarez was arrested by the Station Drug Enforcement Unit (SDEU) of Novaliches Police Station (PS 4) under PLtCol. Rossel Cejas at about 11:45 a.m., Wednesday following a text message from Alvarez’s mother regarding his illegal drug activities.

Responding operatives seized from the suspect two sachets of shabu, aluminum foil strips with traces of shabu, disposable lighter, and a cellular phone.

According to the mother, her son has been in and out of a drug rehabilitation center, apparently his drug addiction treatment did not work as he is still hooked into drugs prompting his mother to  seek help from the police.

The suspect was charged with violation of RA 9165 or The Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002.
