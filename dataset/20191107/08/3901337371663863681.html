City’s New Coach Bringing Swag to the PSL

07 Nov 2019, 08:43 by Delmain Faver

Cape Town City have announced the appointment of Jan Olde Riekerink, after the club terminated the contract of Benni McCarthy.

Not only is the Dutchman bringing his vast experience to Mzansi, he is also bring his swag and style, with the likes of Rulani Mokwena and Steve Komphela having competition both on and off the pitch.

The 56-year-old has an impressive CV, and has been at the helm in some of the biggest dugouts in world football.
