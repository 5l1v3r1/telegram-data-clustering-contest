Lottery Winner

07 Nov 2019, 02:38 by BRIEF

ATLANTA – A Georgia appeals court ruled that the state wrongfully refused to issue lottery prize money to a winning ticket-holder, finding that the ticket is an “express written contract” and sufficiently waives sovereign immunity.
