For-Profit Universities

07 Nov 2019, 02:55 by BRIEF

WOODBURY, Minn. – The Minnesota Supreme Court ruled that two for-profit universities were properly ordered to pay restitution to former students who claim they were misled about the value of their criminal justice degrees.
