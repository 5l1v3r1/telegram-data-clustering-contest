His Majesty Sultan Qaboos congratulates UAE President

07 Nov 2019, 09:44 by Times News Service

Muscat: His Majesty Sultan Qaboos Bin Said has congratulated Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, President of the United Arab Emirates, on the occasion of renewing confidence in him as Chairman of the Supreme Council of the UAE.

His Majesty the Sultan expressed his sincere congratulations and best wishes to Sheikh Khalifa, praying to Allah the Almighty to grant him good health, well-being and a long life, and to help him to continue to achieve further progress and development for the brotherly Emirati people.
