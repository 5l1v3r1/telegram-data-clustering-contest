Saudi ambassador to Britain calls on tourists from around the world to visit the Kingdom

07 Nov 2019, 15:32

RIYADH: Saudi Arabia’s King Salman hosted the Central Intelligence Agency’s director Gina Haspel on Thursday.
The king and Haspel “discussed a number of topics of mutual interest,” Saudi Press Agency reported.
The meeting in the capital was attended by a number of Saudi officials including Foreign Minister Prince Faisal bin Farhan and intelligence chief Khalid Al-Humaidan.
