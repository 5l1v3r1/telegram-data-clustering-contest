The main specifications of this device are;

  • Screen size: 6.39 inches, 100.9 cm2 (~83.9% screen-to-body ratio)
  • Screen resolution: 1080 x 2316 pixels (~400 PPI density)
  • Weight: 180 g (6.35 oz)
  • OS: Android 9.0 (Pie); Android One

Nokia X71 hardware

What are the hardware features of this device?

Design

This gadget has a fantastic design with glass and metal as the primary build materials. It has a punch-hole camera in its top-left corner, which is considered to be a convenient feature. Its overall design suggests that the manufacturer is adapting its smartphones to the latest trends.

However, if you expected multiple Nokia X71 colors, there are no varied options for this one. You can only get your device in black.

Display

This device has a 6.39-inch LCD capacitive touchscreen with a 1080 x 2316 screen resolution. Nokia X71 display is equipped with the modern technology, Pure Display, which provides enhanced colours, sharpness, and high contrast. In addition, there is an HDR10 video playback function that provides the best experience to users who enjoy watching videos on their phones.

Another interesting feature of this phone is an LED power button, which glows white every time you have an unchecked notification or a missed call. With this feature, the user will know about any call or message that has come in.

Processor, storage, and performance

The processor, Qualcomm SDM660 Snapdragon 660, is one of the weaker points of this device because it is not the newest one. However, for those who want a reliable gadget, this may not be the problem, as it still works quite well.

There is 128 GB of internal memory and 6 GB of RAM, which is relatively adequate for storing large files and data. If you want to expand your memory, there is a chance to do so using a microSD card of up to 256 GB.

Overall, it performs well and does not have heating issues or severe lagging. Avid phone gamers would be satisfied to know that there is a high GPU - Adreno 512, which will run all of their favourite games smoothly.

Battery

The phone is powered by a non-removable Li-Po 3500 mAh battery, which guarantees a relatively okay long Nokia X71 battery life. In addition, there is an 18W fast charging support. Compared to other devices in the same range, this battery capacity is lower.

Speaker and call quality

The device has a loudspeaker and a 3.5 mm jack for those who love listening to music through wired headphones. There is also a function of active noise cancellation with a dedicated microphone. The call and audio quality is clear.

Nokia X71 software

What are the software features of this device?

OS and features

The operating system of this device is Android 9.0 (Pie). A lot of modern phones are supported by this system. Some of the additional Nokia X71 features include the rear-mounted fingerprint sensor, as well as a few other sensors, including compass, accelerometer, proximity, and gyro.

Camera, video and image quality

Nokia X71 camera is a strong feature of this gadget. At the rear, there are triple cameras, which are a 48 MP wide, 8 MP ultrawide, and 5 MP depth sensor, so you can go wild with taking beautiful pictures at any time. The colours will look natural both in daylight and low-light modes. Some features like Zeiss optics, HDR, panorama, and dual-LED flash will make your photo shoots fantastic. You can even make videos of a 2160p quality at 30fps.

The 16 MP selfie camera will also let you take high-quality pictures, as well as 1080p quality videos at 30 fps.

Verdict

Generally, Nokia X71 can be classified as an affordable device. Some may, however, feel that its price is a little bit high judging by the processor it comes with. Compared to its competitors, Infinix Hot 8 and Realme 3 Pro, the device comes with better rear cameras. However, these competitors have better battery capacities.

Nokia X71 is a strong competitor to many modern phones. It sports a stylish look and is relatively affordable. Although it does not come with the latest processor, it is a reliable device that is worth purchasing.
