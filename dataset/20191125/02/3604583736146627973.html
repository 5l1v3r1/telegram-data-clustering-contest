Ballarat police disappointed by deliberately lit fires

25 Nov 2019, 02:50 by Ben Hopkins

Police have taken to social media to voice their frustration following a number of alleged deliberately lit fires across the city including Victoria Park.

A passerby on a Victoria Park walking track, south-east of the corner of Quercus and Plane avenues, noticed a fire lit in pine needles under large trees shortly before 7.30pm on Sunday.
