Shaq Lawson Went Off On Denver OL Connor McGovern After Game ‘You Remember Me Now’ (VIDEO)

25 Nov 2019, 02:04 by Darrelle Lincoln

With all the talk about certain winning teams around the league, not many people are paying attention to the Buffalo Bills.

The team sits comfortably at 8-3 following a 20-3 victory over the Denver Broncos on Sunday.

During that game, Shaq Lawson had two of Buffalo’s four sacks and he wanted somebody on the Denver offensive line to know it.

During the post-game handshake, Lawson approached Denver o-lineman Connor McGovern after the lineman stated he had never heard of him.

Lawson made sure he knew about him now.
