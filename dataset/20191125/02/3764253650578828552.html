Infinix Hot 8 lite specs, features, review, price

25 Nov 2019, 02:27

Infinix Hot 8 Lite is an entry-level device that was recently launched by Infinix Mobile. It is the latest smartphone in the Hot series and is packed with amazing features. Most of its features are similar to its predecessors, except for the processor. It has enough internal storage for all your documents, images, and videos. It is available in both online and offline stores in Nigeria.

The device has a stylish design. The large screen is an amazing feature as it allows you to use the phone for watching videos and playing games without strain. Infinix Hot 8 Lite price in Nigeria is N32,000. The device was announced and availed for retail in October 2019.

Hardware

Check out the phone's hardware details;

Design

The device comes with an elegant design, made with a plastic back. It is a large device with measurements of 165 x 76.3 x 8.7 mm and weighs 175 g. The back is made of premium plastic that has a glassy effect. It features a single color varied with light intensity and a diagonal line running from the top to bottom. Infinix Hot 8 Lite all colours are Quetzal Cyan, Cosmic Purple, Shark Grey, and Midnight Black.

Display

The phone comes with a 6.6-inch HD + waterdrop display. It has an IP LCD capacitive touchscreen with a color depth of 16M. It has narrow bezels, thus offering an impressive 83.5 % screen-to-body ratio. This allows you to watch videos and play games at a much larger frame. It has a screen resolution of 720 x 1600 pixels and a 266-pixel density. With an aspect ratio of 20:9 and a brightness of up to 450 nits, your graphics and images appear bright and sharp.

Processor and storage

This phone model runs on a Quad-core 1.3 GHz processing unit and a Mediatek MT6580 chipset. For storage purposes, it comes with a 32 GB / 2 GB internal memory. It also has a dedicated slot for your microSD (up to 128 GB).

Battery

Infinix Hot 8 Lite battery is one of the most impressive features of this model. Its 5000 mAh non-removable battery can last you up to 4 days and 23 hours talk time without recharging it. It also features a battery lab adopting AI intelligence feature, which optimizes power conservation. It takes approximately 3 hours until full charge as it does not come with a fast charger.

Speaker & call quality

The speakers, located on the lower side of the device, are enhanced with the DIRAC mode to offer you rich and clear sound. You can also sync several devices together for a converged immersive sound. Through the 3.5 mm audio jack, you can connect your headphones and other external audio devices. The call quality is also good.

Software

What software features does the device come with?

OS and features

The device runs on Android 8.1 (Go edition). The Infinix Hot 8 Lite android version is supported with the XOS 5.0 Cheetah.

Infinix Hot 8 Lite camera, video and image quality

This gadget comes packed with a dual 8 MP rear camera and a QVGA (low light sensor). The camera has an f/1.8 aperture, enhanced with the PDAF feature. Pictures taken have impressive detail and sharpness thanks to the infused AI software technology. The 8 MP selfie camera with LED flash takes decent portraits, especially in daylight. The video resolution is 1080@30fps.

Verdict

Based on its features, the device is quite decent for its price range. It is an affordable device that performs quite well and with enough storage. For a better processor, one might want to consider the Xiaomi Redmi Note 3.

The Infinix Hot 8 Lite is a low budget device with impressive features. It has a big and elegant display with enough storage, and that might be a reason for you to buy the device. However, its performance is a bit disappointing.
