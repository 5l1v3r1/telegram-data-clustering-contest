Firefighters battle blaze at Guildford warehouse

25 Nov 2019, 02:46

Flames at a warehouse fire in Guildford are soaring up to 40m in the air as firefighters battle to bring the large blaze under control.

Around 130 firefighters are at the scene on Hyne Road, a warehouse believed to be owned by waste management company Cleanaway.

Workers at neighbouring businesses have evacuated, according to reports on radio station 6PR.

It is believed Cleanaway workers were inside the warehouse when it caught alight but had to flee when their efforts to douse the flames were unsuccessful.

Thick plumes of black smoke have darkened the sky of surrounding suburbs of South Guilford, Ashfield, Bayswater, Morley, Eden Hill and Kiara and the Department of Fire and Emergency Services has issued a smoke alert.

Air quality in the area is being monitored.
