‘Playboy’ Model Lexi Wood Goes Topless In Fuzzy Sea Green Hat On Instagram

Trisha Faulkner

Playboy model Lexi Wood turned up the heat for her 664,000 Instagram followers just a few hours ago when she shared a sizzling topless snap of herself rocking nothing more than a fuzzy sea green hat.

While the 22-year-old Canadian model was clearly lacking a top in the revealing photo, the bottom of the image cut off right at the top of her bosom, leaving her exposed chest to the imagination of her followers.

Teasing her social media following even more, Lexi censored any hint of her exposed bosom by draping her arm and hand across her breasts.

Because the snapshot was an up-close selfie there wasn’t much to be seen behind the young model. The brunette bombshell appeared to be reclining on her back on a bed with her head resting on white pillows that were adorned with black stripes.

The fuzzy sea green hat was one of the highlights of the snap, as it matched her sparkling eyes that looked to be the very same shade of green.

Despite the fact that she was “hangin'” in bed without a top, Wood did have a full face of cosmetics including a thick medium pink lip color and a dark smoky eye shadow. Lexi also accessorized with a pair of silver hoop earrings that were slightly visible behind her straight, brunette tresses. She had a gorgeous diamond ring on her finger, an addition to a fresh manicure.

Published to her profile just five hours ago, Lexi’s Instagram followers flooded the snapshot with over 22,000 likes and just shy of 200 comments.

Many gushed over how adorable her hat was.

“You’re an actual angel,” one follower penned.

“You are literally the prettiest ever,” a second chimed in.

“Made by heaven,” a third added.

“Oh my god what a beautiful woman flawless,” a fourth chimed in.

The comments section of the photo also contained the usual array of heart and fire emoji. Some opted for single word complements. Some admitted they were jealous of how incredible the young Playboy model looked.
