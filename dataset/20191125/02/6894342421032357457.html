The 2019 MacBook Pro with 15-inch Retina Display Now Starts At RM 9299

25 Nov 2019, 02:18 by Chief Chapree

A few weeks ago, Apple has introduced the brand-new 16-inch MacBook Pro which subsequently replaced the 15-inch model that the company has introduced earlier this year. Despite not being made available through Apple Malaysia’s website, you can still obtain a unit from local Apple Authorised Resellers (AAR).

A quick check at a number of Apple Premium Resellers (APR) including Machines, Switch (shown below), and Mac City showed that the 2019 15-inch MacBook Pro can now be obtained for a starting price of RM 9,299. Prior to this, the MacBook was priced at RM 10,499 which as you might know is now the starting price for the newly announced 16-inch model instead.

At that price point, you able to obtain the notebook with 2.6GHz Intel Core i7-9750H 6-core processor alongside 16GB DDR4-2400 RAM, 256GB solid state drive, and AMD Radeon Pro 555X graphics. From there, you can also choose to have the higher-end variant which includes the more powerful 2.3GHz Intel Core i9-9880H chip and AMD Radeon Pro 560X graphics as well as a larger 512GB solid state drive.

The higher-end model is now being priced at RM 10,999 as opposed to its original price tag of RM 12,199. To learn more, check out the sources below or reach out of your favourite AAP and APR outlets.
