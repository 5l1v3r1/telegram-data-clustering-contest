Jerry Jones Calls Out Coaching Staff After Cowboys Loss: “I Shouldn’t Be This Frustrated”

25 Nov 2019, 02:23 by Darrelle Lincoln

Jerry Jones came into the 2019 season hoping for a different season from his franchise, but the more things change, the more they stay the same.

Even though the Dallas Cowboys owner gave head coach Jason Garrett a vote of confidence for the remainder of the season, his anger was evident after the Cowboys suffered a 13-9 defeat to the New England Patriots on Sunday.

Cowboys was upset at the special teams and specifically called out the coaching staff.
