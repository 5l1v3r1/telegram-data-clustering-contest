UGREEN Wired & Wireless Car Charger Are Up For Grabs On Amazon

25 Nov 2019, 02:55

If you're spending a lot of time in your car, charging your smartphone or some other device while driving may be a good idea. Well, UGREEN is offering both a wired and wireless car charger on Amazon, and they're both quite compelling.

The UGREEN car mount is a 10W Qi wireless charger at the same time

Let's focus on the wireless one first, shall we. This device is both a smartphone mount and a wireless charger at the same time. Unlike the wired charger, this one has been designed to charge your smartphone.

You can, technically, charge something else if you want, as this is a Qi charger we're looking at here. Considering the way it'll be set up in your car, however, you'll need a device that fits in that mount.

Advertisement

In any case, this car mount can hold pretty much any phone out there, and charge it. This charger uses 10W Qi charging, in case you were wondering. Do note that you'll need a Quick Charge 3.0 car adapter as its power source for fast charging, that adapter is not included.

This UGREEN mount / fast charger comes with silicone rubber grips that auto-retract in order to fit your phone. It also has a built-in sensor that will automatically close those arms when it recognizes your phone is in place.

You can even charge phones which are in a case, as long as that case is not over 3mm thick. It also shouldn't be made out of metal, of course. This charge has various charging security measures in place. It comes with temperature control, over-current protection, over-charge prevention, and so on.

UGREEN will also provide you with a 24-month warranty for the device. This mount / charger can be yours for $21.99, the purchase link is included below.

The UGREEN 36W car charger offers fast charging via two USB-A ports

The UGREEN 36W car charger is also available on Amazon. This device supports Quick Charge 3.0, and it's a dual USB car charger.

Advertisement

It can charge two devices simultaneously, up to 18W at a time. If one device is charging, you'll get a full 36W output, presuming the device needs it.

This charger comes with a smart chip that will adapt its current to the device it's charging. In other words, you don't need to worry about overcharging, overcurrent, or anything of the sort.

You can charge a wide variety of devices using this car charger, it's not limited to smartphones. You can charge pretty much anything that can be plugged into its USB-A port.
