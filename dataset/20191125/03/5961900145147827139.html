FEATURE JOBS: Acne Studios, Edwards And Co, By Charlotte + More

25 Nov 2019, 03:05 by By Yasmin Adhami

Acne Studios, a multidisciplinary fashion house known for its ready-to-wear lines, footwear collections, accessories and denim, are looking for a part time Stock Associate (SYD) to join their Kings Street studio! Acne Studios is independently owned and has over 45 stores over the world including flagship stores in Paris, London, New York City, Los Angeles and Tokyo. Working in Acne’s King Street studio, the successful candidate would be responsible for tasks such as stockroom maintenance, inventory control, providing excellent customer service and receiving and processing deliveries. To be considered for this role, candidates must be passionate about Acne Studios products and brand, reliable and punctual, fluent in English and highly motivated. To be eligible for this role, applicants must have permanent residency or Australian citizenship. If you have previous retail experience and fit the job criteria, then apply today!

Edwards and Co, Australia’s leading hair agency, housing the countries most innovative and trend setting hair stylists and makeup artists, are on the hunt for a full time Marketing Manager (SYD)! Edwards and Co have worked with celebrities and influencers such as Lara Worthington, Jasmine Howell, Samara Weaving, Charlotte Best and many more! The roles and responsibilities include strategising and implementing marketing and advertising campaigns with strong digital focus, creating loyalty programs for clients, tracking sales data and managing website content. To excel in this role, applicants must have 5+ years experience within a similar marketing role, strong organisational skills and a can-do attitude. Apply here to be a part of a rapidly growing organisation within the beauty industry!

Spirited jewellery label By Charlotte are seeking a part time PR & Marketing Manager (SYD) to join their team for a 7 month period as a Maternity Leave cover. The role involves developing, managing and executing sales and promotional based events both online and in-store, executing the organisation and management of campaign shoots and managing the overall communications of the brand. The ideal candidate has a minimum 4 years experience in PR/Marketing within the fashion/retail field, experience in digital marketing, brilliant verbal and written communication and great working relationships with key Australian media and stylists within the fashion industry. Apply now for this great opportunity!
