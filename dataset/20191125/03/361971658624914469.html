Watch Taylor Swift Perform a Career-Spanning Medley at the 2019 AMAs

She played “Shake It Off,” “Love Story,” “I Knew You Were Trouble,” and more despite recent controversy over Swift’s old material

25 Nov 2019, 03:52 by Allison Hussey, Madison Bloom

Following reignited controversy in mid-November, Taylor Swift took the stage at the American Music Awards earlier tonight to perform a career-spanning medley of hits celebrating her title of Artist of the Decade. Tay was introduced by legendary songwriter Carole King, before she performed “Love Story,” “I Knew You Were Trouble,” “Shake It Off,” and more. Watch her performance and acceptance speech below.

It was uncertain whether or not Swift would be able to perform any of her pre-Lover material tonight, as she took to social media earlier in the month stating that her ex-label boss Scott Borchetta and Scooter Braun were preventing her from doing so. As it turns out, Taylor was able to perform songs old and new.

Her full setlist included “Lover” and “The Man” from Lover, “Love Story,” from Fearless, “I Knew You Were Trouble” from Red as well as “Blank Space” and “Shake It Off” from 1989. Halsey and Camila Cabello joined Swift for “Shake It Off,” while Tay performed “Lover” solo on a gilded piano, accompanied by ballet dancers.
