Browns fans use helmet to take swings at Mason Rudolph pinata

25 Nov 2019, 03:17 by  Bradford Arick, WPXI.com

The game may be over and the fines and suspensions handed down after a brawl erupted in the final seconds in a match up between the Steelers and Browns. However, the anger and frustration is apparently still simmering for some fans.

>> Read more trending news

During a tailgate Sunday, Cleveland Browns fans were swinging a Steelers helmet at a Mason Rudolph pinata, imitating the infamous fight in which Myles Garrett smashed Rudolph with his own helmet.

Garrett was fined and suspended indefinitely. Rudolph was accused of using a racial slur and fined $50,000 by the NFL. A total of 33 players were fined as part of the incident.
