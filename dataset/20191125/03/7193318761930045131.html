Photos, video and winners list: Carrie Underwood tearfully accepts two trophies at 2019 American Music Awards

25 Nov 2019, 03:27 by Brandy McDonnell

Carrie Underwood was among the big winners at tonight's American Music Awards, snagging two trophies at the star-studded show.

As previously reported, the Checotah native, who was among the presenters on the ABC broadcast, was nominated for Favorite Album - Country for "Cry Pretty" and Favorite Female Artist - Country. She won both - at the same time as it turns out.

After announcing that Underwood had earned her sixth career trophy in the Favorite Album - Country category, Cobie Smulders and Michael Ealy, stars of the TV series "Stumptown," surprised the Oklahoma native with news that she had also been named Favorite Female Artist - Country.

"So, it's a good surprise. OK, I was worried for a second," Underwood said with a laugh.
