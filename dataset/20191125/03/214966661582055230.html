Toddler hospitalised with burns after Logan house fire

25 Nov 2019, 03:24 by Monique StClair

Investigations are underway after a toddler was burnt in a house fire in Logan this morning.

The blaze broke out at a home on Meredith Street, Loganlea, around 11.00am.

Three fire crews were called to the scene where they were able to extinguish the fire within 30 minutes.

Paramedics treated a male toddler for burns to his legs, before taking him to the Queensland Children’s Hospital for further treatment.

His mother gave him first aid treatment at the scene before paramedics arrived, though she wasn’t injured in the incident.

Both police and fire investigators are working to determine the cause of the blaze.
