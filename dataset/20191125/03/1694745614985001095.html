Taylor Swift Looked Like a Freakin' Goddess at the AMAs

25 Nov 2019, 03:25 by Kelsie Gibson

Taylor Swift was the guest of honor at the American Music Awards on Sunday night. The "Lover" singer, who received the artist of the decade award at the show, looked absolutely stunning as she hit the red carpet in sparkly green dress and thigh-high boots. Perhaps she and BFF Selena Gomez planned their outfits together?

In addition to her big award, Taylor passionately thanked her fans for their dedication and performed a medley of her biggest hits from the past decade alongside friends Camila Cabello and Halsey. See more of Taylor's monumental night ahead.
