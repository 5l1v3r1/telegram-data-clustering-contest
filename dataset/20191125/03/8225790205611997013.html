Big Island police identify man killed in single-vehicle crash in Kau

25 Nov 2019, 03:14 by HNN Staff

HAWAII ISLAND, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) -A 35-year-old man died after a crash in Kau on the Big Island on Sunday morning, police said.

The man has been identified as Paul Ray Roby.

Emergency crews were dispatched to Kamaoa Road, about a half mile east of the South Point Road intersection, just before 10 a.m.

They found a wrecked convertible overturned off the roadway in brush and trees.

Police later said the car went down an 8-foot embankment and struck several trees before overturning.

No other vehicles were involved.

The victim was transported to the Kona Community Hospital, where he was later pronounced dead.
