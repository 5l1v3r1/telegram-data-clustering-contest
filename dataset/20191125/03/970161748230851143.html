Halsey throws shade at the Grammys during AMAs winner's speech

25 Nov 2019, 03:48

Halsey might not have been nominated for any awards at the 2020 Grammys, but she did pick up a prize at the American Music Awards on Sunday night.

The 25-year-old won the award for Favourite Song Pop/Rock with her track Without Me and on thanking fans for voting for her, Halsey pretty much threw shade at the Grammys for their snub.

On picking up her gong, she launched into a huge speech about how she used to think winning awards gave artists validation for all the hard work they put in to writing music, but actually she doesn’t think they’re ‘what they seem’ now she’s older.

She explained: ‘The truth is I am older now and I’m also an artist… most of these awards really aren’t what they seem at all. But that’s OK and I’m up here right now and I’m so thankful for the AMAs because they are the world’s largest fan voted award show.’
