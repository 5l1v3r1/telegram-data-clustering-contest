Looking for a forever home; Vernon SPCA pet of the week

25 Nov 2019, 03:00 by Parker Crook

Mirielle, a five-year-old spayed cat who came to the SPCA as a stray, is looking for her new home.

"This girl is a spunky calico cat who's looking to find a family as adventurous as she is," said Chelsea Taylor, Vernon SPCA branch manager. "Affectionate and charming, Mirielle is one of a kind. She's not a big fan of other cats and would much rather be the only queen of the castle."

For more information about Mirielle and other animals available for adoption, visit the Vernon SPCA online.
