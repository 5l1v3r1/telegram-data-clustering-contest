Man who fled from hospital now in custody, Port McNeill RCMP say

25 Nov 2019, 18:33

A suspect is in custody following a manhunt that sent three schools into lockdown Monday afternoon.

Port McNeill RCMP said the incident started while officers were investigating a motor vehicle incident and found a firearm.

The suspect, who had been taken to hospital, fled before police arrived, sparking a manhunt.

Police asked residents to secure their homes and vehicles and report any suspicious activity.

The Vancouver Island North School District issued a notice about 3:15 p.m. Monday saying that the RCMP had directed all public schools in Port McNeill to go into lockdown “due to an incident that was occurring in the community.”

At about 3:50 p.m., RCMP allowed a controlled release of students into their parents’ care or onto buses or ferries.

“All students and staff were safe and followed procedures as expected,” the district said.

At about 6:20 p.m., police said the man had been taken into custody.
