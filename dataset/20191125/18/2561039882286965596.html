Apple Posts Its 2019 Holiday Ad: 'The Surprise' [Video]

25 Nov 2019, 18:07 by iClarified

Apple has posted its touching ad for the 2019 holiday season entitled, 'The Surprise'.

Sometimes, the best gifts can come from the most unexpected places.

In the ad, two children are inspired to make an interactive presentation on iPad as a gift for their grandfather who was mourning the loss of his wife. The presentation features photos and videos of their grandmother.
