Smash between lorry and car on M74 causes rush hour chaos as emergency crews race to the scene

25 Nov 2019, 18:07 by Paul Smith

A SMASH closed part of the M74 motorway northbound at Hamilton Services earlier.

Police, fire and ambulance teams were called to J6 after the accident at 5:45pm.

A Police Scotland spokesperson confirmed a car and lorry were involved.

There have been no serious injuries.

Drivers reported huge delays while lanes one, two and three were locked down.

We told you earlier a man has suffered serious injuries after a crash in Aberdeen this afternoon.

Emergency services were called to Powis Terrace at around 2.25pm following reports of a person being hit by a car.

Roads in the area have been as emergency crews tackle the incident.
