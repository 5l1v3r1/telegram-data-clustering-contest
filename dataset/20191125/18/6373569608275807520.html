All the best Santa's Grottos in Plymouth this Christmas

If you're still looking for somewhere to take the kids to see Santa this year, look no further

25 Nov 2019, 18:50 by Jess Morcom

At the most magical time of the year, there is nothing quite like a visit to see Santa to get you or your family into the festive spirit.

With most of this year's Grotto's now open and in full swing, Old Saint Nick himself is stopping at a few places around the city to spread the Christmas cheer far and wide, as well as handing out gifts to visitors.

If you're still stuck on where to book, here is a round up of some of the best Santa's Grottos around the city this Christmas.

Tamar View Nurseries Garden Centre

Tamar View's Grotto has been gaining great reviews for years and this year opening dates and times are extended.

Get into the festive spirit and visit their spectacular Winter Wonderland, including white snow, animated reindeer and polar bears, along with a trip to see Santa.

There is an advanced prepaid booking system, so booking in advance is recommended.

The Grotto is £6.99 per child and includes a gift from Father Christmas himself.

To book over the phone call on 01752 847366.

Drake Circus

Once again Drake Circus are bringing the magic of Christmas with Santa's grotto and his team of elves.

The Grotto is now officially open, and each child that visits will receive a special gift from Santa and his helpers.

Pre-booking is available now or you can attend by visiting on the day between now and Christmas Eve, although the grotto is expected to be busy throughout the Christmas period, so arrive early to avoid disappointment.

Chaplins

 

Chaplins award-winning Grotto is back again for another year, and promises to be bigger and better than ever.

Open from now right through until Christmas Eve, Chaplins' amazing winter wonderland experience promises to be magical for people of all ages.

At just £7.50 per child and 50p per adult, Chaplins even welcomes dogs, and you can bring your pooch for £4.99.

Boringdon Hall

Feel the magic of Santa's Grotto at Boringdon this year with a fun and festive day out for all the family.

All visitors and families will be dazzled by a dance performance, will get to enjoy a diving Festive Afternoon Tea, get creative with Christmas activities including gingerbread decorating and also get to meet Santa Claus himself to receive a Christmas present.

Choose from a 12:30pm or 4pm sitting on selected dates throughout December. The events are very popular so pre-booking is essential.

Find out more here.

Dartmoor Zoo

Santa Claus is coming to Dartmoor Zoo and bringing his special Elves with him on the weekends of 14th-15th & 21-22nd December.

The Grotto will be open from 11am-12pm on these days and all children will get a time frame with Santa.

Tickets cost £3.50 and children will also receive a small present from Santa.

Endsleigh Garden Centre

 

Santa's Grotto at Endsleigh is a wonderful experience for all ages. Prepare to be dazzled by twinkling lights on the Blue Diamond Express, before visiting Santa Claus himself.

You can even book to spend the morning or evening with Santa too, with the chance to enjoy a cooked breakfast with him, or even an afternoon tea.
