Aoibhin Garrihy welcomes second child

Amazing news!

25 Nov 2019, 18:39 by Kendra Becker

Aoibhin Garrihy and her husband John Burke have welcomed their second child.

The former Fair City star gave birth to a baby girl at the University Hospital in Galway, after going into labour early.

Posting a photo of tiny baby shoes on Instagram, the new mum-of-two wrote: “What a crazy 24 hours it’s been…one minute you’re painting your nails with notions of an awards night, the next you’re on the labour ward at 2am…”

”The result? The ULTIMATE reward, a perfect little sister for Hanorah, another beautiful Burke baby girl arrived today at 12:36pm and we are simply in love ❤️.”

Aoibhin and John are already parents to their one-year-old daughter Hanorah, who they welcomed in June 2018.

The couple have been married since 2016, after tying the knot in Co. Clare.
