No Red Alert Siren, But Rocket Fired By Gaza Terrorists

25 Nov 2019, 18:44 by Hana Levi Julian

Although no Red Alert incoming rocket alert siren was triggered, Gaza terrorists fired a rocket at southern Israel shortly before 6:30 pm Monday evening, according to a report by Israel’s Channel 12 news.

The rocket reportedly landed in an open area in southern Israel. There’s been no report of property damage or injuries.

The Red Alert incoming rocket alert siren may not have activated because the projectile’s trajectory was not headed towards a populated area.
