Aoibhin Garrihy and hubby John Burke welcome second child - a baby girl

She admitted the birth had come as a surprise

25 Nov 2019, 18:51 by Blathin de Paor

Aoibhin Garrihy and husband John Burke have welcomed their second child together - a baby girl.

The health and wellness influencer was due before Christmas , but took to Instagram on Monday to reveal their second child had made an early arrival.

Aoibhin confirmed she had welcomed another baby girl, admitting it had come as a big surprise, RSVP Live reports.

Sharing a picture of her daughter's first pair of tiny bootees she wrote: "What a crazy 24 hours it’s been...one minute you’re painting your nails with notions of an awards night, the next you’re on the labour ward at 2am...the result?

"The ULTIMATE reward, a perfect little sister for Hanorah, another beautiful Burke baby girl arrived today at 12:36pm and we are simply in love."

Aoibhin and hotelier husband John Burke, are already parents to adorable daughter, Hanorah, the popular couple are looking forward to expanding their family.

She admitted it will be a busy household when they become a family of four, and feels lucky to have help at home.

"I feel blessed and very lucky [to be expecting again], but a little bit petrified as well and that is where nana's come in. And we are very lucky to have them as well," the former Fair City actress previously told us.

"Hanorah is a pure rogue; she is just like Doireann [Aoibhin's sister] which I am very worried about. She is full of craic and she makes us laugh every single day."

Speaking about getting the discipline right with a toddler, Aoibhin joked: "Where do you get tips on that because she is ruling the roost.

"Hanorah is not taking me seriously and it is becoming a real stress because she just laughs when I reprimand her, it is a disaster.

"She is all fun and games at the moment but we will get there."
