Pre-order the OPPO Reno 10x Zoom 12GB RAM and get gifts worth RM1446

25 Nov 2019, 18:32

OPPO has announced that the OPPO Reno 10x Zoom 12GB RAM edition will be available for pre-order from today, until 6 December 2019. Those who pre-ordered the device on 29 November 2019 or before, will be eligible for free gifts worth up to RM1446 which includes products such as a FujiFilm Instax Mini printer, Enco Q1 as well as VOOC Powerbank.

The aforementioned smartphone, as well as free gifts, can be collected during the official launch of OPPO’s Flagship Store in The Gardens Mall. Do note that you are required to pay an RM100 non-refundable deposit, if you are interested in making a pre-order.
