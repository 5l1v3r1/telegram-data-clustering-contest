Why did Ellis leave Emmerdale and is he returning with a new actor?

25 Nov 2019, 18:01

Emmerdale is set to see the return of a familiar character very soon as Ellis Chapman returns to the Dales.

Viewers were told that he was leaving to stay with mum Jessie (Sandra Marvin) in Dubai following her split from Marlon Dingle – with fans assuming that he wouldn’t be seen again.

However Ellis comes back into focus on Monday night’s episode as tensions rise between his dad Al (Michael Wildman) and Billy (Jay Kontzle)

Does this mean he’s heading back to the soap – and why did he leave in the first place?

Here’s what you need to know…

Why did Ellis leave Emmerdale?

Ellis made his exit from Emmerdale after Asan N’Jie, the actor who played him, was sacked from the soap.

He was sacked following a fight with Hollyoaks actor Jamie Lomas at the TV Choice Awards in September which was caught on video.

Asan later said in a statement: ‘My behaviour at the TV Choice Awards was completely unacceptable and very much out of character.

‘I sincerely apologise to everybody who has been affected especially Jamie Lomas, the whole Emmerdale team, our audience, ITV, my family, and the organisers of the TV Choice Awards.

‘I am devastated, accept full responsibility for my actions and I am determined to learn from this.’

While Asan left the soap immediately, Ellis continued to appear on screen for a while after scenes which had already been filmed were aired.

Those showed him getting to work on his new business and growing close to Belle Dingle (Eden Taylor-Draper) but his exit to Dubai was then explained in a series of scenes between other characters discussing where he had gone.
