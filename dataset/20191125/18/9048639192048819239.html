Suspect wanted for murder in Madison Parish

25 Nov 2019, 18:05 by KNOE

MADISON PARISH, La. (KNOE) - A search is underway for a murder suspect wanted in Madison Parish.

The Madison Parish Sheriff's Office is looking for Johnson Combs.

He's wanted in connection with a shooting that happened outside the P.E.G. Centre in Tallulah over the weekend.

Authorities say a fight broke out and the suspect fired into a crowd. One person was killed and six people were injured.

Combs is now wanted on second-degree murder and attempted second-degree murder charges.

If you know where to find him, contact the Madison Parish Sheriff's Office at 318-574-1831.
