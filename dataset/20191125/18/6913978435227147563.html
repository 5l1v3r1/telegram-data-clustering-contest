VIVA partners with Intigral over Jawwy TV Home in Kuwait

25 Nov 2019, 18:17

KUWAIT — VIVA, a world-class digital leader providing innovative services and platforms to customers and enabling the digital transformation in Kuwait, and a subsidiary of STC Group, announced its collaboration with Intigral to launch their state-of-the-art Set top box, Jawwy TV HOME for the first time through the agent in Kuwait NHE Group, available with VIVA Postpaid plans.

The launch was made following a strategic partnership with Intigral, a leading provider of digital entertainment, sports and related solutions in the MENA and the developer and provider of Jawwy TV which forms a leading innovative OTT service in the region.

Jawwy TV is a class leading entertainment platform, including a selection of the best Arabic, Western, Turkish and kids’ entertainment and sports content, both as live TV and on demand. The Jawwy TV box gives you access to titles from StarzPlay, Wide Khaliji, BluTV, Cartoon Network, Fox+, as well as Jawwy TV exclusives and originals, in addition to live TV from networks such as MBC, Rotana and Fox channels. In fact, with over 10,000 titles available on demand there is no end of binge worthy entertainment at your fingertips. Jawwy TV offers features such as parental control and rewind TV, and it is available in both English and Arabic.

Additionally, Jawwy TV recently started broadcasting highly-acclaimed original productions that achieved high ratings and wide regional viewership. Its original series so far include “Galabet Jad” and “Studio Almashaher”, with more content to be coming soon. Viewers can enjoy these productions exclusively via Jawwy TV Home on television, and Jawwy TV app on smartphone and tablet.

In this regard, Mrs. Haneen Al-Fulaij, Chief Consumer Officer at VIVA, said: “This strategic partnership with Jawwy TV is a key step in the delivery of our vision for a new era in entertainment across Kuwait to deliver market leading OTT products and services and a world-class customer experience. We look forward to continuing to provide our customers with cutting-edge products and services that cater to their needs and aspirations.”

Hamoud Al Rumayan, Chief Executive Officer at Intigral, said: “We are very excited to be entering into this partnership with VIVA, giving audiences in Kuwait access to thousands of hours of movies and TV series. Jawwy TV offers something for everyone and will serve to strengthen the already amazing line up of products that VIVA offers today. This is a step in the right direction for Intigral’s regional expansion strategy, aiming to position Jawwy TV at the forefront of the digital entertainment sector.”

To find out more, customers can follow VIVA’s official platforms on social media, visit one of VIVA’s stores, VIVA app, VIVA’s official website at www.viva.com.kw, or access round the clock assistance from a team of specialists available on the VIVA’s customer care line 102. — SG
