Mourinho's Three-Man Transfer Wish List Revealed?

25 Nov 2019, 18:30 by David Kappel

Jose Mourinho, who is known for splashing the cash, has reportedly already given Tottenham Hotspur chairman Daniel Levy a three-man transfer wish list, and it includes one of his former players.

The Portuguese tactician got his Tottenham Hotspur career off to a winning start after beating West Ham United 3-2 on Saturday.

He had taken over the London club's hot seat from Mauricio Pochettino, who was sacked last week Tuesday after Spurs fell to 14th on the Premier League log.

During the Lilywhites' win on Saturday, though, they concede two late goals, proving some of the shortcomings in Mourinho's new squad.

As a consequence, the 56-year-old wants to invest in the upcoming two transfer windows, and has reportedly handed chairman Levy a transfer wish list, according to the Daily Mirror.

Benfica centre-back Ruben Dias is said to be one of Mourinho's priority targets, while attacking midfielder Bruno Fernandes is also reportedly on the list.

In addition, Nemanja Matic's name has been mentioned, with the Manchester United midfielder falling down the pecking order under Ole Gunnar Solskjaer.
