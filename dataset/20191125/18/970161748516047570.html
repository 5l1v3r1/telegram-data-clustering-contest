This Is Us star Justin Hartley hangs out with daughter after filing for divorce

25 Nov 2019, 18:20

This Is Us star Justin Hartley stepped out for lunch with his daughter without his wedding ring after filing for divorce from wife of two years Chrishell Stause.

The actor made his first public appearance since filing for divorce to hang out with his daughter Isabella whom he shares with first wife and Passions actress Lindsay Korman.

Justin was dressed in an all-black ensemble but gave his outfit a little colour with white trainers.

Isabella, 15, kept to the theme of black with a leather jacket and black jeans but we’re shook at how fast she’s growing up.

His daughter is the spitting image of her mother and All My Children’s star Lindsay.

Justin filed the paperwork to divorce his wife Chrishell on 22 November.

The 42-year-old stated the reason for divorce was ‘irreconcilable differences’ and ‘dissolution without minor children’, according to Us Weekly.

Although Justin has listed their split as 8 July, the two have been seen together on a number of occasions, and more recently at a pre-Golden Globes party just last week.

The actor also took the time out to wish his now ex-wife well on her birthday at the end of July.

‘Let’s all wish this young gem of a human a very happy birthday!’ he wrote on Instagram. ‘To many more years of love and laughs!!! HAPPY BIRTHDAY @chrishellhartley!!!!!!!!’

Justin and Chrishell had begun dating in January 2014, with Justin proposing to his future wife in July 2016, before the two wed the following year in October 2017.

‘People close to Justin and Chrishell are completely shocked by Justin filing for divorce. The couple has always been incredible with no signs of things being on the rocks,’ a source told ET.

‘Friends are speculating this was an impulse on Justin’s side due to something that happened, and are hopeful the couple can work things out.’

Metro.co.uk have contacted reps for comment.
