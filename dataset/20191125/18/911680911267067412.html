Diamondbacks to offer tickets to home games for 3 months for $99 this Black Friday

25 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Cydeni Carter

PHOENIX â€” Attention Arizona Diamondbacks fans: we may have found the stocking stuffer you've been looking for.

The D-backs announced Monday the "D-backs Holiday pass," which is good for Opening Day at Chase Field (that's March 26 by the way) and ALL 31 Chae Field home games in March, April, and May.

Just a few of the best match-ups during that time are:

  • Atlanta Braves (March 26, opening day)
  • Houston Astros (April 28-29)
  • Chicago Cubs (April 30-May 3)
  • Washington Nationals (May 14-17)
  • Detroit Tigers (May 29-31)

The Holiday Pass will go on sale on Black Friday, Nov. 29 through Cyber Monday, Dec. 2. The pass can be purchased via the MLB Ballpark app.

The D-backs will also have Black Friday deals at the Chase Field Team Shop from 10:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. with special deals. Some deals include: every purchase receives a free shaker bottle with koozie, 50 percent off select styles, fit seven select items in a stocking for $40, and more.

Also, if you wear D-backs gear to the store on Black Friday, you will receive a raffle ticket for a prize.
