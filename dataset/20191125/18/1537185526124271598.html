Search for missing worker in partial building collapse downtown now ‘recovery’ effort, city manager says

Four released from hospital, Turner says

25 Nov 2019, 18:15 by Sarah Hager

CINCINNATI (FOX19) - Cincinnati Police Department sources tell FOX19 NOW one person is dead in the partial collapse of a building in downtown Cincinnati.

Crews remain at the scene on Fourth Street early Tuesday, continuing their night-long searching for a worker who remains unaccounted for.
