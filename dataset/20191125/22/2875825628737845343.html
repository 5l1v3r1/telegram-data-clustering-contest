Robbie Williams' wife Ayda Field KO's Liam Gallagher fight plan as 'embarrassing'

The former X Factor judge has slammed her husband Robbie Williams' fight suggestion against his music rival and told her hubby to 'grow up'

25 Nov 2019, 22:31 by Irishmirror.ie

Ayda Field has KOd her husband Robbie Williams ’ plan to fight Liam Gallagher , telling him to grow up.

Singer Robbie, 45, last week said he had approached boxing promoter Eddie Hearn to fix up a fight with Liam, 47.

The rivalry dates to the mid-1990s when Liam’s brother Noel dubbed Robbie the “fat dancer from Take That”.

In October, Robbie said he wanted to settle their differences once and for all.

But Ayda said: “I think it is embarrassing.”

She told Robbie: “I am going to knock the hate out of you so you may even send Liam a Christmas card.”

Last week, Robbie said: “If there’s anybody that I want to box, it’s Liam Gallagher. Eddie Hearn’s up for it”.

But on their new podcast, At Home With The Williamses, Ayda stormed: “You are 45, man. You are going to go in a ring and smack each other. It is lame.

“You are not in your 20s. I am going to have to bring you back from the dead after this. I don’t understand it.”

Robbie got the message. He said: “This is the festive season to all men, even Liam Gallagher. If you need me to smack anybody I am not gonna. I am now the spirit of Christmas too, like you.”

The two musicians have a long running feud, and Williams wanted to settle his differences with the Oasis frontman in a KSI vs Logan Paul-style bout in the ring.

Williams admitted back in October he hoped to battle it out with the Oasis frontman in a bout similar to that of Youtube stars KSI and Logan Paul .

He told GQ at the time: "I’d love it. But I’d want it to be a professional fight. I’ve seen how much KSI and Logan Paul made from theirs and I think we could trounce that."

He added: "But, yeah, I think last time in the noughties Liam wanted to go down to an abandoned railway track or something. I don’t want to do that. It’s got to be f****** whistles and bells if it happens."

During a recent chat on the Jonathan Ross show, Robbie revealed that he made contact with Eddie Hearn over staging a bout with Gallagher.

He claims that Hearn - who promoted the KSI vs Logan Paul fight - is up for it, although Gallagher is yet to reply.

The pair were good friends at the height of Williams' fame with Take That in the nineties, before a bitter falling out.

The singer - who has children Teddy, seven, Charlie, five, and Coco, 14 months, with wife Ayda Field , 40 - as a busy month coming up with the release of his Christmas album 'The Christmas Present', featuring an unusual collaboration with boxer Tyson Fury .
