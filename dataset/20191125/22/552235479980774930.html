Police appeal to trace OAP whose car was last seen in parts of North Ayrshire

Robert Dunn, 71 is believed to be driving a blue Ford C-Max motor vehicle, registration mark SC17RXD.

25 Nov 2019, 22:49 by Eric McGowan, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/eric-mcgowan/

Police are appealing for the public’s help to trace a pensioner last seen in North Ayrshire this evening.

Robert Dunn, 71,  from the Stranraer area, was in the Ayr area earlier today to to collect his motor vehicle.

The car was last seen on the A737 in the area of Beith and Lochwinnoch at 8.25pm this evening (November 25).

Robert is believed to be driving a blue Ford C-Max motor vehicle, registration mark SC17RXD.

Anyone who may have seen Robert or his vehicle, or who has any information on his whereabouts, is urged to contact Police Scotland via 101 quoting incident number 3725 of 25th of November 2019.
