Man facing impaired charge following crash in Waterloo

Three people suffered minor injuries

25 Nov 2019, 22:00 by KitchenerToday Staff

A Waterloo man is facing an impaired related charge following a three-vehicle collision in the city.

It happened on King Street North near University Avenue East on Sunday, shortly after 7 p.m.

Police say two drivers and a passenger were taken to hospital with minor injuries.

A 32-year-old man has been charged with operating a conveyance while impaired.

Any possible witnesses are asked to call the Traffic Services Unit at 519-570-9777 ext. 8856.
