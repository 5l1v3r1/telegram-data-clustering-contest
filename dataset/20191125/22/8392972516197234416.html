I'm A Celebrity: Jac Jossa elated to escape next trial

25 Nov 2019, 22:07 by Karen Hyland

Headlines about Jacqueline Jossa's struggles in I'm A Celebrity have dominated the press coverage so far this year.

The young mum of two has struggled with fear over the trials, although performing in them really well.

She has also been devastated to be put up for them time after time.

And it appeared she would face her fears again tonight as Ant and Dec revealed who would be facing Arch of Agony.

It was down to her and Andrew Maxwell... and luckily for the former Easties star the comedian was headed to the task this time.

Viewers were relieved for her...
