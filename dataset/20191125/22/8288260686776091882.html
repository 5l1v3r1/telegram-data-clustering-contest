'So easy' - Aston Villa fans have their say after comfortable win over Newcastle United

Aston Villa 2-0 Newcastle | First half goals from Hourihane and El Ghazi earned AVFC a comfortable win over the sorry Magpies

25 Nov 2019, 22:41 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Aston Villa ended their three-match losing run with a resounding 2-0 win over Newcastle United at Villa Park.

The home side dominated the Magpies and could have scored countless more, but had to settle for two, first half strikes.

Conor Hourihane, who was recalled by Dean Smith, opened the scoring with a clever free-kick. Before the Irishman created the second, this time, his set piece was turned home by Anwar El Ghazi.

Newcastle had chances either side of half time, but after Heaton stood strong, they were able to cruise towards their fourth Premier League win of the season.

Delighted fans belted out 'Sweet Caroline' at full time and supporters on social media were similarly as jubilant:
