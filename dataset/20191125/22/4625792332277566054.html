Journalist temporarily suspended on Twitter for response to Chelsea Clinton about transgender violence

25 Nov 2019, 22:19 by Mike Brest

Journalist Andy Ngo said he was temporarily suspended from Twitter for his contributions to a conversation pertaining to violence against transgender people.

Last week, the Human Rights Campaign honored Transgender Day of Remembrance on Twitter. Chelsea Clinton quote tweeted the HRC and commented, "Since 2013, more than 150 trans people have been murdered in the U.S., the majority Black transgender women. On #TDoR2019, we remember and honor the lives lost, hold their loved ones in our hearts and must commit to doing all we can to end this epidemic of violence and hate."

Ngo, who is currently an editor at The Post Millennial, responded to Clinton by tweeting, “The US is one of the safest countries for trans people. The murder rate of trans victims is actually lower than that for cis population. Also, who is behind the murders? Mostly black men.”
