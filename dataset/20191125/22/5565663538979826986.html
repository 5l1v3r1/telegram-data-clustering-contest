WATCH: Ammara Brown drops Tichichema video

25 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Mandla Ndlovu

Award winning musician Ammara has released a video of her song Tichichema which is an ode to the abused and the abusers.

The video chronicles the life of a woman who is enduring domestic abuse at the hands of her lover.

She is forced to fake a smile when neighbors pass by.

The video was directed by Director Teekay and produced by Edible Elevens
