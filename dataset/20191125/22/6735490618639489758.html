The Weeknd Registers ‘Like Selena’ Songtitle

25 Nov 2019, 22:20 by Shakiel Mahjouri

It looks like Selena Gomez is inspiring at least one of The Weeknd’s new songs.

The Weeknd recently registered the song title “Like Selena” according to ASCAP website. Gomez and The Weeknd started dating in Jan. 2017 before splitting 10 months later.

On Monday, the Canadian “Starboy” singer got fans hyped after teasing an upcoming project.

“The fall starts tomorrow night,” The Weeknd captioned a blurry black-and-white Instagram photo. Moments later, he posted the same picture to his Instagram Story with the caption, “tomorrow night we start again.”

On Sunday, The Weeknd’s lead single from his fourth studio album was officially announced. “Blinding Lights” is expected to drop soon followed by a song titled “Heartless”.
