X Factor contestant Ariel Burdett reportedly dies aged 38

25 Nov 2019, 22:01 by Buzz Contributor

X Factor contestant Ariel Burdett has passed away at the age of 38, according to reports.

The Daily Star report Ariel died earlier this month but a cause of death is yet to be revealed.

The singer was most famous for her audition on the 2008 series of X Factor, which saw Alexandra Burke win the series.
