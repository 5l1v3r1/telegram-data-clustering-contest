NZ Cricket to apologise to Archer after racial abuse

England star was subjected to "disturbing" racial insults by a spectator during the first Test against the Black Caps

25 Nov 2019, 22:15 by Louis Cameron

New Zealand Cricket (NZC) will apologise to Jofra Archer on Tuesday after England's pace sensation was racially abused by a fan during the first Test in Mount Maunganui.

Archer claimed he'd been subjected to "disturbing" racial insults while he batted during the series-opener as England tried, in vain, to hold on for a draw on the Test's fifth day.
