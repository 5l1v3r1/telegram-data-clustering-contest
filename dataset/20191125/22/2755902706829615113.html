PHOTO: A Zimbabwean Civil Servant will not afford this

Following the removal of subsidies on maize, Zimbabweans have found themselves in the doldrums as the price of Mealie meal, the country’s staple food has shot up beyond the reach of many.

In a bid to make ends meet and forgo bread which is now a luxury, Citizens had resorted to mealie meal dishes 3 times a day namely porridge in the morning, sadza in the afternoon and evening. The latest price hike has left many in low spirits. If you are in the civil service, A quarter of your salary buys 50kg of mealie meal and that is before rent and school fees.
