Hourihane spearheads Aston Villa win over Newcastle

25 Nov 2019, 22:10 by Raf Diallo

Ireland's Conor Hourihane scored an excellent opener for Aston Villa as they beat Newcastle United 2-0 in Monday night's Premier League encounter.

On a good night for the 28-year-old, he also set up the second goal of the night.

The Birmingham club took the lead on 32 minutes through the Cork-born midfielder.

Harbinger of Joy?

Villa captained Jack Grealish teed him up at the edge of the Newcastle box and Hourihane curled an excellent free-kick into the back of the net inside Martin Dubravka's near post.
