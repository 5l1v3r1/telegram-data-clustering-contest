American rapper blasts Ghanaian Media for discrediting Shatta Wale’s Grammy nomination

25 Nov 2019, 10:40 by Nana Yaw

American female rapper, Mariahlynn Araujo who stated categorically that Shatta Wale is second to none when making the list of Ghanaian artistes after announcing a collaboration with him has descended heavily on Ghanaian media stations and personalities who have downplayed Shatta Wale’s Grammy nomination.

The rapper responding to Yvonne Nelson’s earlier tweet about how Ghana entertainment industry doesn’t support their own in regards to discrediting Shatta’s Grammy nomination wrote on Twitter, describing those media people as dumb.

MariahLynn who is a member of Shatta Movement quizzed why anyone on earth would want to downplay Shatta Wale’s contribution to the “Lion King; the Gift” album which has been nominated for the 62nd Grammys.

“Wait wth did I miss? Who is trying to discredit the king & are they dumb? #Shattamvmtforlife,” MariahLynn’s tweet read.
