Samuel Chukwueze scores 3rd league goal in Villarreal's 3-1 defeat to Celta Vigo

25 Nov 2019, 10:13

- Samuel Chukwueze scored his third La Liga goal in their 3-1 defeat to Celta Vigo

- The Nigerian international powered home a superb strike in the second half

- Chukwueze's goal was however not enough to rescue his side from outright defeat after regulation period

Nigerian star Samuel Chukwueze took his Spanish League goals tally to three after 13 matches in Villarreal's 3-1 defeat to Celta Vigo at the El Madrigal.

The Super Eagles winger fired home from close range after running into space to connect with Xavier Quintilla's pass from the left flank.

Although, his goal was not enough to rescue a point for the Yellow Submarines after two goals from Iago Aspas and one from Pione Sisto sealed the win for the visitors.
