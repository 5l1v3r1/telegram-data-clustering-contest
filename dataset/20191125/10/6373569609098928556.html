Pictures predict what the Royal babies will look like in 20 years' time

A team of designers have created images of what they could look like when they're older

25 Nov 2019, 10:19 by Jamie Hawkins

Concept images which show how the Royal babies could look in 20 years' time have been released.

As the new generation of the royal family expands, public demand is at its peak. Whether it’s a royal wedding or the birth of a royal baby, the whole nation is at a standstill wanting to know more. 

With Meghan Markle giving birth to baby Archie earlier this year, all eyes and ears are on the latest royal babies, especially those closest in line to the throne. But what will the royal babies look like in 20 years’ time? Whose features will they take?

A team of designers at beauty discovery website  www.cosmetify.com  have gazed into their crystal balls and created some concept images that depict what the royal babies could look like when they’re older. The images are of Prince George, Princess Charlotte, Prince Louis, Prince Archie and Savannah Phillips.

Savannah Phillips

 

Age: 30

Savannah has got extremely fair hair like her mother, and is wearing it in a tight bun. Her nose and eyes take after her mother, whilst the mouth is closer to Phillip’s.

Handbag essentials:  Hair grips, lip balm and perfume.

Prince George

 

Age: 27

George has been envisaged with a slicked-to-the-side blonde quiff, with hair beginning to recede, like William’s. With a nose similar to Kate’s, he also has a solid jaw and round, brown eyes.

Grooming essentials:  Travel-sized hair wax, mints and spare contact lenses.

Princess Charlotte

 

Age: 25

Charlotte has been imagined to sport dark hair like her mother but dyed darker. Her slim nose and piercing eyes remain the same, though her jaw is slightly more chiselled

Handbag essentials:  Longwear matte lipstick, multi-purpose eight hour crème, waterproof eyeliner and eye serum.

 

Prince Louis

 

Age: 22

Louis has got fairer hair like William, with a squarer jaw than his brother, similar to Prince Andrew. He has slightly chubby cheeks and a similar nose shape to William, with eyes similar to Kate.

Grooming essentials:  Tweezers, anti-ageing moisturiser and tooth floss.

Prince Archie

 

Age: 21

The team has imagined that Archie will take Harry’s hair colour but slightly darker, in tight curls. The nose takes after Harry’s, whilst the eyes are more similar to Meghan’s. Archie has freckles and a light beard.

Grooming essentials:  Hair spray, sensitive face moisturiser and facial wash.
