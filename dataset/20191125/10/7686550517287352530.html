Ebay launches Black Friday sale on laptops, TVs and vacuum cleaners

The auction website has released the first of its Black Friday sales

25 Nov 2019, 10:34 by Neil Bennett

Online auction site eBay have launched their Black Friday sale today with over half-price savings on leading market brands.

Tech items down to fashionwear have all been discounted in the lead up to the big day at the end of this week, Friday November 29.

Along with the millions of items listed by verified sellers, Ebay – as ever – is also hosting the Black Friday discounts from many of the UK's biggest retailers including Currys PC World , Argos and ShopTo .

Ebay's deals page offers a “stress-free” money-back guarantee as well as free delivery on millions of products across their website.

Among what they describe as Ebay's Best Buys, shoppers can now save up to 51% on items like the Google Home Hub.
