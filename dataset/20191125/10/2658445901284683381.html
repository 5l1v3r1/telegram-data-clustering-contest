VIDEO: Suspected internet fraudster, Mompha arrives in court

25 Nov 2019, 10:50 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

Robert Egbe

Suspected internet fraudster, Ismaila Mustapha, also known as Mompha, has arrived the Federal High Court in Lagos for his scheduled arraignment by the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission.

EFCC filed a 14-count charge bordering on alleged money laundering and other related offences against him.
