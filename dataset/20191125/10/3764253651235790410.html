Medical doctor narrates how a patient paid him back for his kindness

25 Nov 2019, 10:21

- A Nigerian doctor has taken to social media to narrate how a patient fled the hospital without footing her bills

- According to the doctor, he carried out a scan and stood surety for her when she was rushed to the hospital after she suffered a stroke

- The patient, however, fled the hospital the following day without footing her bills

A Nigerian doctor identified as Urchilla Dean left social media in shock after he took to the platform to recount how a patient took advantage of his kindness. In the series of tweet he made, he narrated how the patient was rushed to the hospital after suffering a stroke.

However, money was needed to carry out a CT scan but the family members claimed they didn't come with cash but stated that they would pay the following day.
