Harbhajan Singh hopes Sourav Ganguly to change selection panel after Sanju Samson snub

Harbhajan Singh was reacting to wicketkeeper-batsman Sanju Samson being dropped from the Indian T20 squad without getting a game in the recent home series against Bangladesh.

25 Nov 2019, 10:00 by PTI

Veteran Indian off-spinner Harbhajan Singh is hoping that BCCI President Sourav Ganguly will “change” the current selection panel and bring “strong people” on board.

The 39-year-old was reacting to wicketkeeper-batsman Sanju Samson being dropped from the Indian Twenty20 squad without getting a game in the recent home series against Bangladesh.

“I guess they r testing his heart #selectionpanelneedtobechanged need strong people there.. hope dada @SGanguly99 will do the needful,” he tweeted.
