Peugeot driver's tyres so bald they had 'cords exposed' in wet conditions

Officers say they were 'asking for trouble'

25 Nov 2019, 10:08 by Ben Waldron

A Peugeot driver was caught by cops in the rain with bald tyres and cords exposed while driving through a South Derbyshire village.

It happened in Castle Gresley on the evening of Thursday, November 21.

The silver car was pulled over by police officers of Derbyshire Police in the pouring rain.

It had two bald tyres with cord exposed, according to the force's dedicated policing unit in a message on social media.

The driver received a ticket and the car was recovered.

A tweet from the Derbyshire Roads Policing Unit issued the following afternoon, at around 3.30pm, reads: "Castle Gresley, Swadlincote.

"Driving about in the pouring rain on two bald tyres with cords exposed. Asking for trouble. Ticket and vehicle recovered pending repair/disposal. #PG9 #Traffic #ConstructionAndUse"
