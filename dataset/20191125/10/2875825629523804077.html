Amber Davies reminisces over old Love Island bikinis and wearing them with Kem

Amber posted a throwback picture of her with ex Kem Cetinay as she reminisced over Love Island memories

25 Nov 2019, 10:34 by Irishmirror.ie

Love Island alumni Amber Davies has taken a trip down memory lane as she picked out her old bikinis from when she was on the show.

Amber, 23, won the show in 2017 with her then boyfriend Kem Cetinay.

She has since become a star on stage, performing in Dolly Parton musical 9-5 alongside Louise Redknapp .

But Amber reminisced over her reality TV roots as she cleared out some old swimsuits from her time in the villa.

Explaining to her fans which bikinis were her favourite, she posted photos of herself in them, including one of her and ex boyfriend Kem.

She has previously said she no longer speaks to Kem, who she split with soon after leaving the Love Island villa.

Amber landed a £500,00 contact with Motel Rocks and performed in nightclub Cirque Le Soir when she left the ITV show.

She was linked to Liam Payne when he split from Cheryl, but she maintained they were just friends.

She told OK!: "Liam is a genuinely lovely guy and very down to earth considering how successful he is.

"If I messaged him and asked him for advice he would give it to me, but I’m not dating him. Liam is lovely but we’re just friends!"

Amber did however date James Hawkins between April and June 2018.

Meanwhile Kem appeared on shows including Good Morning Britain, This Morning, and You vs. Chris and Kem.

He competed on Dancing On Ice in 2018 and will be returning to the upcoming series as a backstage presenter.

He has had clothing deals with Boohoo and Primark, and at the beginning of this year said that he hadn't liked anyone since Amber - and even refused to rule out a reunion between them.

He told The Sun : "I haven’t been on a date with anyone I’ve actually liked since I was with Amber and that’s honestly the truth.

"A lot has happened and we both only have nice things to say about to each other – you never know.

"Never say never."
