Fans think Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello are OVER after awkward AMAs moment

The couple won an award for their hit song Senorita

25 Nov 2019, 10:53 by Clodagh Meaney

Last night the American Music Awards took place at the Microsoft Theatre Los Angeles, which saw a host of musicians come together to celebrate their achievements.

Naturally, mixing celebs with live TV, the AMA ceremony, which was hosted by Ciara, saw some very awkward moments.

From Halsey taking swipes at the Grammys to Taylor Swift subtly shading Scooter Braun there was already drama on the night.

But the one that Twitter won’t let go of, is Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello’s awkward as hell award acceptance.

From the moment they won, to their duet, fans couldn’t help but notice something was amiss.

The couple were heading to the stage to accept their award for Collaboration of the Year when Camila tried to plant a kiss on her beau.
