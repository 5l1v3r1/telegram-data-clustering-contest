Parkway Drive Announce Viva The Underdogs 2020 Tour Support

Here’s who will be joining Parkway Drive on the road next year.

25 Nov 2019, 10:21

Aussie metal heroes Parkway Drive have always brought along killer bands as support on the road – with last year’s epic UK run featuring the mighty Killswitch Engage and Thy Art Is Murder. And, unsurprisingly, next year’s huge Viva The Underdogs tour is no exception.

The Byron Bay mob’s forthcoming UK and European arena tour – its full name being the Viva The Underdogs - European Revolution 2020 Tour – will see hardcore heavyweights Hatebreed, Cali hardcore-punks Stick To Your Guns, and UK favourites Venom Prison joining Winston McCall and co. for what is surely going to be one of the best heavy shows of the year.

The Viva The Underdogs - European Revolution 2020 Tour will kick off at Hamburg’s Sporthalle on April 1, and culminate at London’s iconic SSE Arena Wembley on April 18, with tickets on sale now. Don’t miss out.
