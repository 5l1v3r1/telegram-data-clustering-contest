All trains cancelled between Dundee and Edinburgh due to broken rail

Trains between Dundee and Edinburgh have been cancelled after the discovery of a broken rail at Leuchars on Monday morning.

25 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Claire Warrender

Scotrail, LNER and Cross Country services are all affected and replacement buses have been put in place.

Disruption is expected to last until noon.

Scotrail announced at 9.30am that a safety inspection was taking place near Leuchars and that no southbound trains would run.
