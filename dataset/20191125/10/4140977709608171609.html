Sourav Ganguly’s daughter Sana Ganguly trolls him: The President of the BCCI got a couple of hilarious replies from his daughter.

Former India captain and current BCCI (Board of Control for Cricket in India) President Sourav Ganguly has allured plentiful limelight in the last few days for successfully conducting India’s first day-night Test match with the pink ball at Eden Gardens in Kolkata.

While the Indian team had expressed apprehensions regarding playing the day-night Test in the past, Ganguly’s appointment as BCCI President soon brought the change and a good one at that.

“Just take a look around [fans with their camera lights on to click pictures]. Do you see this? Have you seen this in Test cricket? When was the last time you had a capacity crowd watching a Test match? Feels like it is a World Cup final,” Ganguly was quoted as saying to news agency IANS during the historic Test.

Sourav Ganguly’s daughter Sana Ganguly trolls him on Instagram

In a hilarious development, Ganguly was seen getting trolled by his own daughter on social media platform Instagram. It all started after Ganguly posted a photo of himself from the post-match presentation ceremony after the second Test against Bangladesh in Kolkata.

Ganguly’s daughter Sana Ganguly soon commented on the post. “What is it that you’re not liking ?,” read Sana’s comment.

While Ganguly pointed out Sana becoming disobedient as something he doesn’t like, she replied back that she is learning from her father.
