Dayton man charged in Piqua carjacking

25 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Breaking News Staff

PIQUA — Minutes after a reported carjacking in Piqua late Saturday morning, a Troy Police Officer spotted the car traveling southbound on I-75 at Troy, got it stopped and got the suspect in handcuffs.

According to information obtained by News Center 7’s Steve Baker, Piqua Police charged Brian Grissom, 33, of Dayton with robbery and driving on a suspended license.

The car was stolen from the 800 block of South Downing Street in Piqua. 

Grossom was arrested without a struggle and booked into the Miami County Jail.
