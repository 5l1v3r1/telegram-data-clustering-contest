BPSSC releases results for Stenographer, ASI, Excise SI; check cut-off

BPPSC stenographer ASI, excise SI cut-off 2019: The candidates can check their score and cut-off marks at the official website, bpssc.bih.nic.in

25 Nov 2019, 10:27 by Careers Desk

BPPSC stenographer ASI, excise SI cut-off 2019: The Bihar Police Sub-ordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the result along with cut-off marks for the post of assistant sub-inspector and excise sub-inspector (ASI). Candidates can check their score and cut-off marks at the official website, bpssc.bih.nic.in.

The link to check individual marks and cut-off will remain active till December 5, 2019 after which it will be deactivated. Candidates need to check and download the documents for their reference within the given time frame.

For the sub-inspector category, the final selection cut-off was at 153 for males and 134.6 for females in the general category. For ASI, the final selection cut-off was recorded at 78.4 marks for general category males and 75.6 marks for general category females.
