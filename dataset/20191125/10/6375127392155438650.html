Quezon bgy chief held for drugs

25 Nov 2019, 10:05 by Gemi Formaran

GUMACA, QUEZON— A barangay captain is now behind bars after policemen found illegal drugs in his possession during a search operation at his residence in Bgy. San Diego Poblacion, here.

Peter Vermont Noscal, 39, barangay captain of said village, did not resist when placed under arrest by the raiding police team, according to Quezon police director, Col. Audie Madrideo.

Madrideo said the search operation was carried out at 3:15 a.m. by elements of Gumaca police station led by its commander, Major Rodelio Calawit on the strength of a search warrant issued by Lucena City Regional Trial Court Judge Dennis Orendain.

During the search, Calawit said they found  heat-sealed transparent plastic sachets containing white crystalline substance believed to be shabu weighing 0.5 gram with DDB value of P925 and two strips of aluminum foil.

Calawit said Barangay Councilors Antonio Arqueza and Delia Aeganda witnessed the operation and the accounting of seized evidence.

Noscal was charged with violations of Sec. 11 and 12 of Comprehensive Dangerous Drug Act of 2002 after the laboratory results of the seized evidence turned positive.
