Flash sale: Swoop offers flights for as little as $59 from Abbotsford

25 Nov 2019, 10:48 by Elana Shepert

While it often costs less to fly to Paris from Vancouver than to Manitoba, Swoop is offering a number of extremely low fares as part of its latest sale.

Swoop’s Black Friday X Cyber Sale Monday Sale kicked off today with a smorgasbord of ultra-low domestic, transborder, and international fares. The sale runs until Dec. 3 at 11:59 p.m. or until the deals are sold out. Sale fares are available through Jan. 7 to Mar. 10, 2020.

Currently, the sale offers travel out of Abbotsford to three major destinations: Mazatlan, Puerto Vallarta, London, Ontario, Las Vegas, and Winnipeg. One-way fares start for as little as $59 CAD including all taxes and fees, such as the flight below, from Abbotsford to Winnipeg direct.

There are also a number of other great deals, such as Mazatlan for $99 and Las Vegas for $61, as well as Puerto Vallarta for only $118.63 CAD including all taxes and fees (see below).

Discover Puerto Vallarta

World-famous tequila, delectable Mexican cuisine, beautiful beaches, and beaming sunshine are just a few of the reasons why people visit Puerto Vallarta. And, while it can be fun just to kick and back and relax at a resort, this beautiful city is a great place to stay in. What’s more, there are a number of ancient ruins nearby that are fascinating to explore.

Flying Swoop: What to know before you go

Since Swoop flies out of Abbotsford, Vancouverites have to make a trip into the Fraser Valley in order to catch their flights. As a result, many locals skip the low-cost option because they don’t want to spend the extra time or money getting to the airport.

For those who are looking to save, but don’t want to spend big bucks on a cab or leaving their car at the airport, there is an alternative: Reliable Bus. Find out more information about the cost-effective transit option here.

Note: While the low-cost carrier is an extremely affordable alternative, it is worthwhile to note that there are a number of additional costs. For one, luggage will cost extra, as will selecting your own seat or even speaking to an operator. As such, it is worthwhile to factor in what you need to bring, or if you want to sit in a particular spot.
