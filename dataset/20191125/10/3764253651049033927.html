Survivor Series 2019: NXT dominate RAW SmackDown in entertaining event

25 Nov 2019, 10:13

Survivor Series 2019 for the first saw the combination of RAW, SmackDown and NXT superstars do battle in the squared circle

The build-up to the event in the past weeks had seen the invasion of WWE next rated stars invade Monday Night RAW and Friday Night SmackDown events.

And the collection of the young wrestlers led by Triple H won key events on the night as the NXT had four wins ahead of SmackDown and Raw, who recorded fewer victories.
