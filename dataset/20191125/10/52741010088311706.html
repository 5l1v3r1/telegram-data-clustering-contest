Regional police looking for missing 82-year-old woman

She was last seen in the Baden area

25 Nov 2019, 10:10 by Phi Doan

Regional Police are asking the public for help in locating a missing 82-year-old woman.

Elizabeth Ann Peters was last seen in Baden. 

Police say she's believed to be driving a gold Toyota Camry, and may be wearing a navy blue coat.

There is concern for her well being. Anyone with information is asked to reach out.
