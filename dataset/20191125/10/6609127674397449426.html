HAVE YOU SEEN HIM?

Kyle Hay missing: Govan man disappears after work night out as pals issue desperate plea

25 Nov 2019, 10:14 by Ewan Mowat

A GLASGOW man who disappeared after a night out has sparked social media appeals to find him.

Kyle Hay was last seen out with his work mates over the weekend.

But the Govan man has not been in contact with friends or family since then.

And now his worried pals have taken to social media to issue a desperate plea in a bid to trace him.

Jamie Bey Jnr shared a picture of his friend on Facebook, appealing for anyone with any information to get in touch.

In the comments, he told one of Kyle's relatives that he was on a work night out when he vanished.

Jamie added that Kyle's colleagues had been made aware of his disappearance.

The post has been shared more than 250 times as locals do their bit to help track him down.

The Scottish Sun has contacted Police Scotland for comment.
