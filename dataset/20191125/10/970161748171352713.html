AMAs: Selena Gomez marks comeback with 'shaky' performance as fans defend her

25 Nov 2019, 10:29

Selena Gomez can’t seem to catch a break these days, as some fans criticised her ‘shaky’ opening performance at the American Music Awards last night.

After hitting the red carpet looking fierce in neon, the 27-year-old singer took to the stage and performed her two new songs, Lose You To Love Me and Look At Her Now.

She rocked a disco ball-like body suit in her fun and upbeat performance of Look At Her Now in Los Angeles’ Microsoft Theater, but it’s her other new song’s vocal performance drawing criticism.

One fan wrote on social media: ‘I love Selena Gomez to the bones but her performance of Lose You To Love Me was absolutely shaky.’

While another tweeted: ‘I have a feeling she was just nervous.’

Then one fan critiqued: ‘Yikes.. I love Selena, but this just really wasn’t it.’

But then fans of the Back To You singer rushed to her defence.

One fan shot back at the haters: ‘The Selena Gomez performance was a bit shaky at first but that’s okay since she was probably really nervous.’

Another simply tweeted: ‘Proud of you!’

While another reasoned: ‘For everyone saying that Selena’s performance was shaky, please remember that she hasn’t done a performance like this in years. She was probably a bit nervous and she still killed it.’

It’s true Selena’s not had the easiest time in the limelight lately.

She recently opened up about the vicious trolling she received during her battle with Lupus in 2015.

The star was diagnosed with the condition – which is an autoimmune disease that occurs when your body’s immune system attacks your own tissues and organs, causing inflammation that affects different body systems – and let her fans know in 2015.

She later went on to undergo a secret kidney transplant after complications from the disease, sharing the news on Instagram in September 2017.

Speaking on the hurtful comments she received online about her weight at the time, she said: ‘That got to me big time.

‘That really messed me up for a bit.’

She also revealed she went ‘through a depression’ after her kidney transplant.
