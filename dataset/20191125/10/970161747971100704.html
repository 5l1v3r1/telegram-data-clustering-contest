Price explains heated start to Players Championship final with Van Gerwen

25 Nov 2019, 10:31

Gerwyn Price has cleared up what was bothering him during the Players Championship final on Sunday, blaming a moving stage for his bad mood at the start of the game.

Price was beaten 11-9 by Michael van Gerwen in a superb final in Minehead, with the two players showing absolute respect for each other at the end of the game.

However, there appeared to be some tension between the two men early in the game, with the Iceman clearly bothered by something.

The pair have slightly differing accounts of the incident, but both involve something to do a moving Minehead stage.

‘I could hear clicking on the stage,’ Price told Live Darts. ‘I wasn’t blaming Michael in any way, but there was some movement.

‘I mentioned it but I thought it’s pointless dwelling on it, just try and blank it out and move on.’
