Slight oil price rollback

25 Nov 2019, 10:23 by Edd Reyes

OIL firms will implement a slight rollback in pump prices effective today.

Pilipinas Shell, Petron Corporation and Chevron Philippines will cut prices of  gasoline products by P0.20  per liter while diesel and kerosene by P0.10 effective at 6 a.m. today.

Independent oil firms PTT Philippines, Phoenix Petroleum, Unioil Philippines, Seaoil Philippines, Eastern Petroleoum, Total Philippines, Petro Gazz and Flying V will also implement the same price adjustments.

Earlier, Cleanfuel rolled back  gasoline prices by P0.20  per liter and of diesel by P0.10 which took effect Sunday at 4 p.m.

The Department of Energy (DOE) said the rollback of oil prices reflected the rates in the international oil market.
