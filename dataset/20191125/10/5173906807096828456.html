Government injects €10m into VIP air route

25 Nov 2019, 10:30

Portugal’s minister of economy and digital transition said has revealed that the government will bolster the VIP.pt programme to attract air routes, with a budget of €10 million.

Among areas targeted by the programme will be a boost of marketing campaigns for new operations in the Azores and Madeira and searching for new players to improve flights to the Algarve during the winter season.
