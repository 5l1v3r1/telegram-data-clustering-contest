Class Of ’80s: Mohanlal, Nagarjuna, Venkatesh, Jackie Shroff & Others Have A Gala Time At Chiranjeevi’s Reunion Bash

The get-together bash was attended by stalwarts from Telugu, Malayalam, Tamil, Kannada, and Hindi film industry.

25 Nov 2019, 10:39 by Koimoi.com Team

Tollywood megastar Chiranjeevi last night hosted the 10th annual reunion of the 1980’s actors aka ‘Class Of 80’s’ at his residence in Hyderabad. The get-together bash was attended by stalwarts from Telugu, Malayalam, Tamil, Kannada, and Hindi film industry. Present at the bash were Akkineni Nagarjuna, Jackie Shroff, Venkatesh Daggubati, Mohanlal, Revathy, Jayaram, Sarath Kumar, Radhika Sarathkumar, Jagapathy Babu, Jaya Pradha, Prabhu, Amala Akkineni, Suhasini Maniratnam among others.
