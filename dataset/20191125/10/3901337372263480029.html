Real Star Talks Up Mbappe Amid Transfer Links

25 Nov 2019, 10:32 by SL International

PSG wonderkid Kylian Mbappe has been strongly linked with a move to Real Madrid, and one Los Blancos star has seemingly turned on the charm in an attempt to make it happen.

"In a few years, he will be the best player in the world. He is a great talent," the former Chelsea star said.
