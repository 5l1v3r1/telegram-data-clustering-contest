Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly hit back as viewers claim I'm A Celebrity live trial was 'fixed'

Some thought one of the challenges wasn't fair on the campmates

25 Nov 2019, 10:54 by Rebecca Carter

TV's Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly have hit back after I'm A Celebrity viewers branded last night's live trial a "fix".

James Haskell, Roman Kemp, Ian Wright, Caitlyn Jenner, Andrew Maxwell, Jacqueline Jossa and Adele Roberts took part in a Love Island themed trial to win a roast dinner for each campmate.

The stars managed to sail through each challenge until the final one which saw James and Roman lying in a bed filled with snakes.

They had to try and unscrew three stars in the blue boxes, which were on James' side and filled with a crab, spiders and cockroaches, and move it to the corresponding yellow box on Roman's side, which were also filled with critters.

The pair had just 90 seconds to try and get the final three stars but didn't manage to complete the task, meaning Ian, Cliff Parisi and Andy Whyment weren't going to be getting their roasts.

Viewers were unimpressed with the challenge, with many saying it was "impossible" to complete in 90 seconds.

One person said on Twitter: "Time on last task was totally ridiculous. Thought it was 90 sec per star not 90 sec altogether. Impossible."
