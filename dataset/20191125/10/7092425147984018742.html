United fans slam Ed Woodward after pic of him 'arguing' with Alex Ferguson

25 Nov 2019, 10:13 by James Cox

Manchester United executive vice-chairman Ed Woodward isn't exactly the most popular figure with the club's supporters.

Since Alex Ferguson and former CEO David Gill retired in 2013, the club has suffered an alarming slide.

Woodward has become a figure of blame in United's demise as he's presided over a number of failed managerial appointments and botched transfers.

While he's more than capable in terms of the business side of things, noodle partners don't quite match up to the feeling of winning trophies.

In contrast, Ferguson is a club legend whose reputation is pretty much untouchable.

With this in mind, a photo of the pair in heated discussion in the stands during United's 3-3 draw with Sheffield yesterday has not gone down well.

The picture in question appears to show Woodward shouting at Ferguson and it has not gone down well with fans.
