BREAKING: Suspected internet fraudster, Mompha arrives in court

25 Nov 2019, 10:39 by Oladimeji Ramon

Oladimeji Ramon

Suspected internet fraudster, Ismaila Mustapha, alias Mompha, has arrived the Federal High Court in Lagos for his scheduled arraignment by the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission.

The EFCC charged him with 14 counts bordering on cyber fraud and money laundering.
