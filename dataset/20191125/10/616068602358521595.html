Highway 97 slow down

25 Nov 2019, 10:59 by John Lawless

If you're travelling on Highway 97 this week you should expect some slow traffic due to maintenance.

The highway will be narrowed down to one lane between 15th & 48th Avenues while crews do some routine storm catch basin cleaning.

Expect some delays between 9 a.m. and 3 p.m. from Nov 26 to Nov 29.
