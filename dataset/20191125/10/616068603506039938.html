Stars On Ice set to align

25 Nov 2019, 10:38 by Jon Manchester

The stars will align at Prospera Place as some of the biggest names in Canadian figure skating come to Kelowna May 8.

The 2020 Stars On Ice tour will feature some of Canada's all-time greats in the sport.

Combining athleticism and artistic grace, the tour cast includes: four-time world champion and four-time Canadian champion Kurt Browning; two-time Olympic silver medalist, three-time world champion and seven-time Canadian champion Elvis Stojko; Olympic gold and silver medalist, three-time world champion and 10-time Canadian champion Patrick Chan; Olympic gold, silver and bronze medalists, two-time world champions and six-time United States Dance Champions Meryl Davis & Charlie White; Olympic gold, silver and bronze medalist, 2018 world gold medalist and three-time Canadian champion Kaetlyn Osmond; three-time Canadian dance champions, three-time world medalists and 2018 Olympians Kaitlyn Weaver & Andrew Poje, and more.

Numerous guest skaters will also include many of the top upcoming names in the sport.

Tickets go on sale Friday, Dec. 6 at 10 a.m. www.starsonice.ca, www.selectyourtickets.com, the Prospera Place Box Office, and by phone at 250-762-5050.

Prices start at $27.50, and group discounts and special on-ice seating are available. 

Star On Ice was founded in 1986 by skating icon and Olympic gold medalist Scott Hamilton. The changing touring troupe has performed more than 300 shows over the past 25-plus years.
