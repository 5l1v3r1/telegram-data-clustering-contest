Baaghi 3: Cuts And Scrapes Won't Slow Down Tiger Shroff; Shares His Bruised Body Picture On Instagram

25 Nov 2019, 10:55 by Saheli Maity

Tiger Shroff is all set to raise Baaghi 3’s action level 3X! Recently, the actor shared a picture of his back to showcase the ‘first set of scrapes and minor cuts from the battlefield’. The actor has been currently in Serbia sweating it out for the third installment of the hit franchise.

Tiger Shroff is leaving no stone unturned in his preparation for Baaghi 3. The actor is currently shooting for the movie in Serbia and shares pictures to keep the fans updated. Since the actor is said to be choreographing his upcoming film, we were pretty damn sure that we are in to see some death-defying stunts and action sequences in the film, knowing that he is a terrific martial artist. However, the actor recently shared a picture of his back and we are convinced about the same. He shared a picture to show his ‘first set of scrapes and minor cuts from the battlefield.’

The picture shared by him has is back, showing all the minor cuts and scrapes he had during the shooting. While sharing the picture, Tiger captioned the picture as, "First set of scrapes and minor cuts from the battlefield..and hopefully last. doesn't look or feel like much until I take a shower #baaghi3."
