They’ll steal my man – Emelia Brobbey on why she keeps her relationship private

25 Nov 2019, 10:51 by MyNewsGH

Actress, Emelia Brobbey has been reported to have said she will not make public the man she’s dating.

According to her, Ghanaian ladies are sharp and will chase him immediately she discloses hi identity.

She made this known when she spoke to Sammy Kay Media in an interview.

The actress said she’s not in search of a man.

“I will not say whether I am dating or not because the two has its own consequences but I am not searching.”
