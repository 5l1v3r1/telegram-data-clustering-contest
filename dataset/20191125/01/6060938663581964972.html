Slaying in style: Kriti Sanon looks alluring in THIS little black dress

25 Nov 2019, 01:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kriti Sanon is one of the most sought-after stars in Bollywood. After making her big Bollywood debut opposite Tiger Shroff, there was no looking back for the actress. She has worked in some amazing big banner films till now. Currently, she is gearing up for her next outing 'Panipat' co-starring Arjun Kapoor.

The actress who enjoys a huge fan following on social media is also often seen sharing her pictures and videos with her followers on Instagram. Recently she took to her Insta handle to share an alluring picture of herself donning a pretty little black dress.

In the photos, we can see Kriti wearing a short collared black dress, held together by a large magenta-pink drape, tied at her waist. She accessorised her look with a pair of dazzling earrings. Kriti was spotted wearing this dress at Kartik Aaryan's birthday bash a day ago.

Meanwhile, currently she is shooting for Laxman Utekar’s ‘Mimi’. Kriti will be seen playing a young surrogate mother onscreen for the first time. The film also stars Pankaj Tripathi and Sai Tamhankar in pivotal roles.

Kriti will be next seen in Ashutosh Gowariker's period drama 'Panipat'. In the film, Kriti will be playing the role of Parvati Bai, wife of Sadashiv Rao Bhau played by Arjun Kapoor. Sanjay, on the other hand, will portray Ahmad Shah Abdali in the film. It is slated to hit the theatres on December 6.
