Ringgit opens slightly lower against US dollar

25 Nov 2019, 01:41 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 25): The ringgit opened slightly lower against the US dollar today as traders remained on the sidelines waiting for more positive signs before the new tariffs by the United States on Chinese imports kicks off on Dec 15, 2019.

At 9.03 am, the ringgit was quoted at 4.1750/1780 against the greenback from Friday’s close of 4.1700/1750.
