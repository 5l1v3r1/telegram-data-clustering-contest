Jerry Jones’ Reaction To The Cowboys’ Loss Is Going Viral

25 Nov 2019, 01:41 by Andrew Holleran

Jerry Jones has reacted to the Dallas Cowboys’ loss to the New England Patriots on Sunday night. If Jason Garrett wasn’t worried about his job security before, he should be now.

The Cowboys fell to the Patriots, 13-9, in an ugly game at Gillette Stadium. The weather was bad and Dallas simply made too many mistakes to get the win (a terrible “tripping” penalty call didn’t help, either).

Garrett’s team dropped to 6-5 with the loss. The Cowboys are still in decent position, leading the NFC East, but there’s too much talent on this roster to be just one game above .500 late in November.

Jones knows this.

His postgame quote on the Cowboys’ loss has quickly gone viral:

“With the makeup of this team, I shouldn’t be this frustrated.”
