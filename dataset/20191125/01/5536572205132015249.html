KJ Wright Claims Seattle Knew Most of Eagles’ Plays Before They Ran Them

25 Nov 2019, 01:29 by Darrelle Lincoln

This is just sad.

On Sunday, the Philadelphia Eagles offense never showed up at home and that might have something to do with the fact that the Seahawks defense knew what was coming most of the time.

After the 17-9 loss, Seahawks star defender KJ Wright claimed post-game that they actually knew most of the offensive plays the Eagles were calling.
