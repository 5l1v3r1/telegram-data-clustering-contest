Moment raging domestic abuser is shot dead by his battered ex-girlfriend's dad

25 Nov 2019, 20:13

Dramatic home surveillance footage captured the moment a furious domestic abuser was shot dead by his ex-girlfriend dad.

Brian Ingham, 39, arrived full of aggression at his former partner’s house in Lakeland, Florida, on November 21 to pick up their two year-old son as part of a custody agreement.

He was filmed breaking his way into the family’s home through a door, before clutching his chest in pain after being shot.

Ingham was then seen stumbling away while oozing blood, before collapsing and dying in the garden moments later.

No charges have been filed against his killer, who suffered minor injuries during the confrontation.

It began when Ingham’s mom Patricia went to the door to collect her grandson. Ingham’s ex has a restraining order in place preventing him from contacting her directly.

The dad who later shot Ingham explained that his daughter was out on an errand, and asked Patricia Ingham to wait inside until she returned.

Patricia Ingham was happy to do so, but the delay infuriated her son, who also dialed 911 to claim he was being barred from accessing his child.

He continued to break his way into the home despite his ex’s dad warning him that he would shoot if he failed to do so.

The man who shot Ingham, who has convictions for crimes including assault and battery, remained in the yard, and cooperated fully with sheriff’s deputies.

Polk County Sheriff’s Office say his version of events lines up exactly with what the video shows, with criminal charges unlikely to be filed.

Ingham’s son was uninjured in the commotion. His ex arrived home shortly after he was shot and killed.
