Op-Ed: Do we really want a president that is all-omnipotent?

Energy Secretary Rick Perry, in an interview released by "Fox & Friends" this past weekend that he believes President Trump is the “chosen one.” But after hearing Trump "ordered" the Pentagon to allow Gallagher to keep his trident - I think not.

25 Nov 2019, 20:53 by Karen Graham

"God's used imperfect people all through history. King David wasn't perfect. Saul wasn't perfect. Solomon wasn't perfect,” Perry said in the clip, according to The Hill.

“I said, 'Mr. President, I know there are people that say you said you were the chosen one and I said, 'You were.’ Perry then added, "I said, 'If you're a believing Christian, you understand God's plan for the people who rule and judge over us on this planet in our government,’ ” he added.

Perry was talking about a conversation he had with Trump about a month ago, and it seems that his omnipotence, Trump, is behaving exactly like the ruler and judge over the American people Perry was referring to - and with glee.
