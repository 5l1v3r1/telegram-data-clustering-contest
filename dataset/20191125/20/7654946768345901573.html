Remains believed to be Aniah Blanchard, UFC star’s missing stepdaughter, found

25 Nov 2019, 20:57 by Jackie Salo

Remains were discovered in the search for Aniah Blanchard, the missing stepdaughter of UFC heavyweight Walt Harris, officials said.

The remains were found Monday in a rural area in Shorter, Ala., as authorities looked for the 19-year-old, who was reported missing Oct. 24, news station WRBL reported.

“I can confirm that human remains have been found and we have good reason to suspect they are that of Aniah Blanchard,” Lee County District Attorney Brandon Hughes told the outlet.

It’s unclear what brought authorities to the remote road in Macon County, which is around 35 miles from where Blanchard was last seen on Oct. 23.

Authorities arrested two people in Blanchard’s disappearance after her abandoned car was found in Montgomery with her own blood. They were identified as Ibraheem Yazeed, 29, and Antwain Shamar Fisher, 35.

Police received a tip that Yazeed was allegedly seen forcing Blanchard into her car on Oct. 23 at an Auburn convenience store.
