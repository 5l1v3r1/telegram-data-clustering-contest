Tesla Cybertruck Defeats Ford F-150 in Tug-of-War Battle

Experts are skeptical, however.

25 Nov 2019, 20:30 by Emily Engle

Elon Musk recently posted a 16-second video on Twitter that shows Tesla‘s Cybertruck duking it out with a Ford F-150 in a tug-of-war match. Musk has been taking numerous digs at the F-150, including in a tweet that simply states the Cybertruck is a “better truck than an F-150.” In the video, The Cybertruck pulled away victorious almost immediately, leading to some skepticism from the experts over at Autoblog.
