Home Bargains is selling Christmas snowman hot chocolate toppers for just 99p

Perfect for getting shoppers in the Christmas spirit, discount retailer Home Bargains is selling a pack of six snowman hot chocolate toppers for only 99p

25 Nov 2019, 20:08 by Howard Lloyd

There isn't much better at warming you up on a cold winter's evening than a mug of hot chocolate.

But, thanks to Home Bargains, you can now pimp yours up another notch by selling snowman hot chocolate toppers to get you in the Christmas spirit.

The discount retailer's Hot Chocolate Mallow Toppers come in a pack of six and cost only 99p according to the Mirror, meaning you can stock up for the festive season without hurting your wallet.

They provide a delightfully festive snowman design that's sure to spread some Christmas cheer at any time of the day.

A member of the Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group on Facebook shared a picture of the toppers, claiming it's "the best £0.99 spent".

The post has been hugely popular with users, gaining almost 8,000 likes.

Another post about the toppers by the money-saving group has wracked up 2,800 likes and over 4,000 comments.

One user commented: "My little girl loves them," while another added "Will have to get some."
