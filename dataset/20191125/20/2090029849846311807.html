Tonto Dikeh leaks private chats with blogger, Stella Dimorkokorkus

25 Nov 2019, 20:16 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

The drama between popular blogger, Stella Dimokokorkus and Nollywood actress Tonto Dikeh, has taken another dimension as the screen diva has leaked private chats between the both of them. The fight has led to some alleged secrets being revealed to the public.

Recall that before the fight turned to a dispute of direct shots, Tonto had threatened that she will expose a particular blogger who she claimed was writing false news about her.

She alleged that the blogger begged her for money, which she refused to give, hence the reason for the defamatory stories about her from the blogger. She was actually referring to Stella in that post.

Now, Tonto Dikeh has released the alleged private chat between her and Stella where she alleged Stella was begging her for money, but disguised and came to her through a lady who had sickler children. Tonto Dikeh claimed that the woman was Stella and she needed the money for herself.
