Westlife answer who'd survive an apocalypse, who's naked the most and other silly questions

EXCLUSIVE: As the Irish boy band celebrated 20 years in the business and their new No1 album, Mirror Online quizzed them with a bunch of silly questions

25 Nov 2019, 20:33 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Westlife took on a series of silly questions as they were quizzed by Mirror Online.

We put 20 questions to the lads to mark the two decades since the release of their debut album when they popped into the offices for an exclusive chat.

We asked everything from who's the funniest to who farts the most and who's the loudest to who's the most likely to be abducted by aliens.

We also quizzed Mark Feehily, Kian Egan and Nicky Byrne - without Shane Filan who was too poorly to attend - on who'd survive the longest in an apocalypse and which one of them is manager Louis Walsh's favourite.

The lads agreed that Nicky was the loudest, but couldn't agree on which one of them is the best looking.

Although Nicky, who admitted to being a big fan of himself, proclaimed he was the answer for any question that made him better than the others.

When asked who farts the most, they all chose Shane, especially today when he's at home with a tummy bug.

When the question about who's the best dancer came, Nicky, who took part in Strictly Come Dancing in 2012, immediately answered himself.

But Kian said: "Listen, just because you do Strictly doesn't mean you're a good dancer, right."

When asked who'd survive the longest in a post-apocalyptic world, Nicky said: "Definitely Kian. Only because he won the jungle and he fed the camp that year and he was the daddy of the camp."

When ask which of them is Louis' favourite, Nicky and Mark both held up pictures of Kian, while Kian answered Shane.

Nicky leaned into Kian, shouting: "Are you kidding me?"

Kian said: "It's changed. Back in the day it was me but not any more."

Nicky then told us: "Ask that question again and say 'least favourite'."

When we did as we were told, all three of the boys immediately held up a picture of Nicky, and Mark also held up a picture of himself.

Impersonating Louis, Kian said: "There was three in the Bee Gees."

When asked about who they'd eat first if they were ever stranded on a deserted island, Mark warned the boys this "could go really wrong".

Nicky said he was too lean, so you wouldn't get much meat off him.

He said: "I'd be gone in a second. If you ate me you wouldn't get fat."

When asked which of them is the most likely to be abducted by aliens, they all agreed it was Shane, with Kian impersonating him to say: "They thought I was one of them."

Mark said: "Shane would be abducted by them because they'd want to study him to see how he's so amazing."

Nicky added: "He'd say, 'My voice is so unique that the aliens wanted to hear me in outer space'."

When we asked who spends the most time naked, Nicky quickly held up his own picture, and the others agreed.

Kian said: "I reckon Nicky walks around naked at home all the time."

Nicky jumped in: "You've got to let it hang out, haven't you? You've got to just be free as a bird."
