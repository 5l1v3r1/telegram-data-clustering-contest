Is Burlington Stores' Uptrend Still in Stock?

25 Nov 2019, 20:39 by BRUCE KAMICH

Burlington Stores (BURL) is due to report its latest quarterly earnings numbers on Tuesday. Let's see what this retailer is up to on the charts ahead of the fundamental numbers. 

  In the daily bar chart of  Action Alerts PLUS holding BURL, below, we can see a pattern of higher lows from March and higher highs from April. Prices are just above the rising 50-day moving average line and above the rising 200-day moving average line.   The On-Balance-Volume (OBV) line has been moving up since March and it should soon make a confirming new high of its own. The trend-following Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) oscillator is in a bullish configuration rising from the zero line.   

    In the weekly bar chart of BURL, below, we can see a bullish picture. Prices have been in an uptrend for the past three years and BURL is above the rising 40-week moving average line.   The weekly OBV line has been in an uptrend from October 2017 and is not far from making a new high. The MACD oscillator is bullish too.   

    In this first Point and Figure chart of BURL, below, we can see that prices have met an upside price target but we can also see that a trade at $211.69 will refresh the uptrend.    

In this second Point and Figure chart of BURL, below, we used weekly close-only data and a price target of $236 is indicated.   

  Bottom-line strategy: I have no special knowledge of BUR:'s earnings tomorrow but the charts look positive and a trade at $211.69 should refresh the uptrend.
