National Defense Days Prove Effectiveness

25 Nov 2019, 20:06 by Luz Marina Reyes Caballero

The National Defense Days, held over the weekend, began in Las Tunas with the activation of the Provincial Defense Council and the municipal ones, with the aim of preparing the governing and command bodies for its complete combative disposition.

Las Tunas, Cuba.- In addition to the preparation of the Provincial Defense Council, the missions of the working groups and subgroups, as well as the updating of the objectives and tasks of the factors that integrate the territorial defense system were pointed out on Saturday.

During Sunday, all the structures of the territorial defense system and the population participated, when the activities of each group to be fulfilled, along with the companies and entities that have precise missions in case that an exceptional situation is decreed, were carried out.

The realization of these exercises guarantee the continuity of the services to the population in the territory, in addition to show the organization, direction, preparation and command of the State administration agencies and other structures of the territorial defense system.

On these National Days, the tactical exercises of the defense zones and the actions of the production and defense brigades for protection and security of the objectives of the companies constituted key factors.

The president of the Provincial Defense Council, Manuel Pérez Gallego, and its Vice President, Jaime Chiang Vega, discussed with the working groups issues that are also in the strategic objectives of the country, such as food production, the development of the sugar harvest and the supply of water to hard-to-reach areas.
