Motorcyclist thrown from bike in Brooklyn crash

25 Nov 2019, 20:48 by Daniel Cassady

A Brooklyn driver slammed into a motorcyclist on Monday, sending the rider flying through the air and landing him in the hospital in critical condition, cops said.

The 19-year-old biker was riding south on Pennsylvania Avenue in East New York at around 9 a.m. when a person traveling the opposite direction in a Hyundai Sonata smacked into him while making a left turn onto Hegeman Avenue, police said.

The rider, who was thrown from his bike and landed in the street, was rushed to Brookdale University Hospital Medical Center in critical condition, according to police.
