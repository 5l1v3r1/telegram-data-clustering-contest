Electronic Theatre - Project Breakdown

25 Nov 2019, 20:05 by Louis du Mont

Louis du Mont writes:

A video going over a recent project creating an intro sequence for Electronic Theatre, a co-op interactive experience. The experience envelopes the participants in projected imagery, covering all for walls of room and relied on a quick iterative approach, while developing the animation. The video covers optimised wireframe rendering, previewing in VR and creating a lcd/crt character face.
