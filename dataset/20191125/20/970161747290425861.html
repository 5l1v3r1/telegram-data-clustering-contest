2020 PDC World Championship draw in full

25 Nov 2019, 20:06

The draw for the 2020 PDC World Championship is out as the first round is set, and the 32 seeds know who they may face in round two.

The top 32 players in the PDC Order of Merit enter in the second round where they face the winner of a contest between a ProTour and an international qualifier.

Defending champion and world number one Michael van Gerwen will face either Jelle Klaasen or Kevin Burness in round two, while in-form Grand Slam champ Gerwyn Price will meet William O’Connor or Marko Kantele.

Raymond van Barneveld is playing in his last World Championship and enters at the first round against American Darin Young, with the winner taking on Jeffrey de Zwaan.

The World Championship gets underway on Friday 13 December at Alexandra Palace, with the final on New Year’s Day.
