Dublin businesses to pay more in rates as budget passes

25 Nov 2019, 20:27 by Dyane Connor, https://www.facebook.com/rtenews/

Business owners in the capital will have to pay almost 3% more in commercial rates next year after Dublin City Council passed its budget for 2020 tonight.

Local authority rents will not be increased, as was previously warned, but there will be hikes for on-street parking and the East Link Toll.
