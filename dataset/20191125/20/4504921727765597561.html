Hompa Kudumo Eugen Siwombe calls on all eligible voters to vote in big numbers

25 Nov 2019, 20:01

Hompa Kudumo Eugen Siwombe of the vaKwangali Traditional Authority has called on all eligible voters countrywide to go out in numbers and vote in the upcoming elections.
In an interview at his homestead, Hompa Siwombe says, he has been following various activities pertaining to the elections including various campaigns and he is happy that, no incidences of violence and intimidation were recorded in his area of jurisdiction.
