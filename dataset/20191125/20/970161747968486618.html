Jessica Biel spotted after Justin Timberlake caught cosying up with co-star

25 Nov 2019, 20:33

Jessica Biel was spotted in public for the first time since her husband Justin Timberlake was caught getting cosy with Palmer co-star Alisha Wainwright.

In the photos, the 37-year-old actress can be seen sporting a pair of sunnies and wearing a long coat, plain white tee and casual trousers.

But, peculiarly, she was holding a giant pillow, as well as a Transformers toy.

Perhaps their four-year-old son Silas had a sleepover?

Whatever it is, she’s looking unfazed and unbothered by recent rumours swirling around her husband’s antics last week.

(She’s also still wearing her wedding ring, if that’s anything to go by).

The rumours started after photos emerged of Justin and Alisha partying on a wild night out at The Absinthe House in New Orleans this weekend.

Even more worryingly, Justin wasn’t wearing his wedding ring.

Alisha, 30, can be seen with her hand on 38-year-old Justin’s leg. While in another photo, the pair hold hands under the table.

Wearing a cap, Justin looks up at Alisha as she, at one stage, got up from her seat holding a bottle as she draped herself over a railing.

The pair were sitting on a balcony, surrounded by other party-goers, who all appeared to be having fun as they joked with Justin, who was in great spirits.

Justin also appeared in footage that showed him being helped up and dragged inside by a friend at the party.

Neither Justin or Jessica have spoken publicly about the incident.
