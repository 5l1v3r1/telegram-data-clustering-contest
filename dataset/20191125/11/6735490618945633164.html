Elon Musk Gets A Very Punny Cameo On ‘Rick And Morty’

25 Nov 2019, 11:31 by Corey Atad

The name is Tusk. Elon Tusk.

On the latest episode of Adult Swim’s “Rick and Morty”, Tesla entrepreneur Elon Musk had a very special cameo appearance.

Instead of appearing as himself, Musk played Elon Tusk, a businessman with tusks coming out of his mouth who heads the company Tuskla.

According to Comicbook.com, the naming pun is a reference to a tweet from several years ago in which Musk changed his name to Tusk.

On Twitter, fans took a lot of joy in the Elon Tusk character.
