Stonebwoy’s wife congratulate him on winning Reggae/Dancehall Artiste of the Year for the 3rd time at 2019 AFRIMA’s

25 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Nana Yaw

Wife of Stonebwoy, Dr Louisa Ansong has sent a congratulatory message to her husband after he won Reggae/Dancehall Artiste of the Year at the 2019 AFRIMA’s.

Ghanaian dancehall artiste, Livingstone Etse Satekla, aka Stonebwoy on Saturday added to his cabinet the Best Artist, Duo or Group in African Reggae, Ragga or Dancehall award at the 6th All Africa Music Awards, also known as AFRIMA 2019.

Congratulating her husband, Dr Louisa in a post on Instagram wrote: “HATRICK! Congrats on winning Reggae/Dancehall Artiste of the year for the 3RD time at the All Africa Music Awards(AFRIMA) in Lagos on Saturday night. We’re all very proud of you. #NationalAsset #BhimnationToTheWorld #GhanaToTheWorld #BHIM”

Stonebwoy, R2bees, King Promise, Kuami Eugene, MzVee, Becca, Kwesi Arthur, Sarkodie, Diana Hamilton, Bethel Revival Choir, Medikal, Shatta Wale the Ghanaian artists who made the nominees lists.
