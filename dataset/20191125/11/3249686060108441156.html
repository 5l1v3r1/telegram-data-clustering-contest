BTS’ Jungkook's merch continue their no 1 best selling streak on the biggest retailers in the world

25 Nov 2019, 11:07 by dose-nel

BTS released a new series of mini dolls and not surprisingly Jungkook’s doll was the best seller among the individual dolls and now among all 158 BTS related goods in "Barnes & Noble". Jungkook also achieved a "triple kill" with his mini doll, prestige doll and funko pop at no 1 within the respective categories. His merch ranks no 1 and no 3 among BTS in the games and collectibles category. Jungkook's insane brand value continues to stay unrivalled!
