Bigg Boss 13: Mahira Sharma-Vishal Aditya Singh’s OH-SO-ROMANTIC Jig Makes Us Wonder If They Are A Thing- VIDEO

25 Nov 2019, 11:15 by Divya Ramnani

Mahira Sharma and Vishal Aditya Singh’s super-sizzling performance inside the Bigg Boss 13 house is soaring temperatures, making fans wonder if they are a couple!

Remember when Vishal Aditya Singh had just entered the Bigg Boss 13 as wild card contestant and his closeness with that of Arti Singh had got the internet buzzing. Well, it looks like that chapter is now thing of the past because a recent promo shared by the official account of Colors hints at his blossoming romance with Mahira Sharma instead. Adding to your excitement, it looks like Mahira’s close friend Paras Chhabra, too, seems to have approved of them.

The clip sees Paras turning director and cameraman for Mahira and Vishal, who performed to the super-romantic Aye Udi Udi Udi. It started with Paras holding the phone and directing the duo on how to start and where to perform. And, let us tell you, both Mahira and Paras didn’t really disappoint because right from their sizzling chemistry to some of the most romantic dance moves; their performance was a treat for all the viewers. For a second, you might just think that they are a couple.
