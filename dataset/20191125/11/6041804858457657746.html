Why Pawan Kalyan Deleted His Election Tweets?

25 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Sridhar Raavi

Janasena President Pawan Kalyan is very active on Twitter for the last month or so tweeting against the decision of the YSR Congress Government to eliminate Telugu Medium completely from Government Schools. On the other side, Pawan Kalyan’s team has deleted all his tweets between March 19th and August 21st.

These are precisely the tweets during the Election Season and the question is why he had to delete them now. Rumors are abuzz in the Social Media that Pawan Kalyan may have deleted the traces of him bashing BJP during the election season. They say it has to something to do with Pawan Kalyan’s recent Delhi Trip.

Pawan Kalyan spent two days in the National Capital and there are no updates about what has happened there. Rumors in the Social Media are that Pawan Kalyan may be gearing up for the Merger or alliance with BJP. Meanwhile, Some party supporters say that the 2019 election is a dreadful memory for Pawan Kalyan and he may want to delete it totally from his mind as well as the Twitter.

Janasena had just managed to open its account in the recent elections with a Single Seat in the recent elections. Even Pawan Kalyan lost from both his seats – Bhimavaram and Gajuwaka leading to doubts over Janasena’s future.
