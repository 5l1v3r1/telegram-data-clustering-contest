Nigerian arrested over alleged possession of illegal drugs in India

25 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Oluwakemi Abimbola

Bengaluru police have arrested a Nigerian identified as Nonso, 36, over alleged possession of illegal drugs.

According to ANI, the City Crime Branch of the Bengaluru Police arrested Nonso on Monday and seized a sizable amount of drugs from him.

The CCB seized 28 grams of cocaine and 10 slips of Lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD) from him.

Other items recovered from Nonso include were Rs 10,000 in cash, an electronic weighing machine, a Nokia mobile phone, and a Toyota Corolla automobile.
