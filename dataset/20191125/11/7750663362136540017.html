NFL Roundup: San Francisco 49ers see off Green Bay Packers

The San Francisco 49ers returned to form to record a 37-8 victory over the Green Bay Packers in a one-sided clash of the National Football Conference big guns.

25 Nov 2019, 11:53

The Niners had experienced close calls in their three previous matches and never looked back after forcing an Aaron Rodgers fumble deep in Packers territory early on to produce a turnover that turned into seven points one play later.

Jimmy Garoppolo clocked up 176 yards and claimed two touchdowns, both of which went right down the middle of the field to open receivers.

A season-low return of eight points wiped out Green Bay's lead in the NFC North and they are now level with the Minnesota Vikings with five games left.

The New England Patriots demonstrated why they have the best defence in the NFL with a 13-9 victory over the Dallas Cowboys, who were restricted to just three field goals.

A New England blocked punt in the first quarter set up the game's only touchdown as the Patriots stay top of the AFC.

Will Lutz nailed a 33-yard field goal with time running out to help the New Orleans Saints fend off a comeback from the Carolina Panthers and secure a 34-31 win.
