New Zealand vs England 2019: Claims of racial abuse by Jofra Archer; investigation underway as NZC issue apology

25 Nov 2019, 11:23 by Aakash Arya

England fast bowler Jofra Archer has claimed that he was subjected to racial abuse on the last day of the first Test in Mount Maunganui. Archer took to twitter to come out with serious allegations of racial abuse from an anonymous member of the crowd after getting out to Trent Boult.
