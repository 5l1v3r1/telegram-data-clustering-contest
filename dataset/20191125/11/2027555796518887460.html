Body of a newborn baby was found in a Mysuru drain

25 Nov 2019, 11:59 by Ranjith Kandya

The body of a newborn baby was found at a drain in Giriyabhovipalya in the city, on Monday morning.

The civic workers, who were cleaning the drain near Government Higher Primary School found the body and alerted the Police.

Nazarbad police visited the spot and investigating the case.
