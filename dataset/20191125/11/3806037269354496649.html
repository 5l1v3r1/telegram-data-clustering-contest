Supreme x Nike Fall 2019 Collection

With a leather and gold focus.

25 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Marc Wong

After Supreme‘s Vol. 2 Hardcover Monograph release with Phaidon Publishings, a 351-page tome documenting the skateboarding label’s history from 2010-2018, Supreme is back with its latest collaborative drop with Nike.

For Fall 2019, Supreme and Nike have worked together on a collection spotlighting leather and gold. Made exclusively for Supreme, its collection with Nike features a Leather Anorak made with premium cowhide leather with fill, quilted nylon lining and removable hood, and a 14K Gold “Nike” Logo Ring. Other cattle-crafted goods include a Leather Baseball Jersey, Leather Warm-up Pant and Leather Duffle Bag, all produced using premium cowhide leather with two-layer leather appliqué logos. And if full leather is not your thing, the drop also includes a Hooded Sweatshirt of cotton fleece with a subtle two-layer premium cowhide leather appliqué logo, as well as Sunglasses and Bandana.

The Supreme x Nike Fall 2019 collection will be available in Supreme stores and online on November 29, and in Japan on November 30.

In other fashion news, Prada and adidas officially unveil a Superstar and bowling bag collaboration, all made in Italy by Prada.
