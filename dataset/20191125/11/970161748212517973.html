The Walking Dead spin off trailer teases Rick Grimes helicopter connection

25 Nov 2019, 11:01

The Walking Dead spin off World Beyond is set to expand the zombie-ravaged universe we’ve come to know and love.

The first trailer, which aired revealed that it will delve into CRM, the mysterious group that whisked away Rick Grimes (Andrew Lincoln) to an unknown destination in season nine.

Briefly we can see the three circle symbol on the side of a black helicopter, and the coat of the powerful woman who looks like she means business right at the end.

Through the clip we can hear the woman saying: ‘Once upon a time the end of the world was our beginning. We are the last light of the world. We are the last hope.’

We can also see a group of young characters wandering around their desolated town

World Beyond is set roughly 10 years after the start of the zombie outbreak that introduced us to Rick, Michonne, Daryl and the rest of the survivors.

Recently CRM featured in Walking Dead prequel Fear The Walking Dead when Althea (Maggie Grace) was introduced to member Isabelle (Sydney Lemmon).

In the show’s fourth episode from season five, Skidmark, the survivors are seen search for Althea who was abducted after a crash near a nuclear power plant. Before she was abducted, Althea was seen investigating the corpse of a heavily armoured soldier and found a map with the same symbol previously seen on the helicopter carrying Rick.

This helicopter was later sighted by survivor Max (Ethan Suess) as it flew away from the site, taking an abducted Althea along with it.

Fear The Walking Dead’s fifth season takes place roughly eight months before Rick’s capture, so it’s possible to pull off the timeline.

The Walking Dead: World Beyond is set to air on AMC in 2020.
