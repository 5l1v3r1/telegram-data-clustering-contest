DJ Cuppy believes she's the most followed DJ in Africa

25 Nov 2019, 11:33

Nigerian billionaire daughter, Florence Otedola, popularly known as DJ Cuppy, has taken to social media to claim the title for the most followed DJ in Africa and her fans are here for it.

It is no news that DJ Cuppy is one of the most talked about Nigerian disk jockeys in recent times. Judging by her latest post, it may appear that her social media following has earned her some bragging rights.

The Gelato crooner whose enthusiasm for music is constantly displayed in her love for disc jockeying and songwriting has a following of 4.2 million on Instagram and 831,000 on Twitter.

Perhaps with this in mind, she recently took to the Twitter platform to declare herself the most followed DJ in Africa. Sharing a cute photo of herself with her equipment, she wrote: "Most followed DJ in Africa"
