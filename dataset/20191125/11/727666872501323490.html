Cardi B Wins 2019 American Music Award For Favorite Artist - Rap/Hip-Hop

25 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Post Malone and Lil Nas X pick up Rap/Hip-Hop trophies too.

(AllHipHop News) Belcalis "Cardi B" Almánzar added another industry award to her shelf last night. The "Money" rapper won Favorite Artist - Rap/Hip-Hop at the 2019 American Music Award.

Cardi was competing against Drake and Post Malone in the Favorite Rap Artist category. She also recently won the Rhythm & Bars Award at the 2019 Soul Train Music Awards. 

"THANKFUL for MY AMA AWARD & Soultrain award. I love you BARDIGANG and everybody that root for ME It’s very much appreciated ❤️❤️❤️❤️," tweeted Cardi early Monday morning.

Cardi also won Favorite Artist - Rap/Hip-Hop at the 2018 American Music Awards. In addition, this year has seen the Bronx-bred entertainer take home a Grammy, two BET Awards, and six Billboard Awards.

Post Malone (Hollywood's Bleeding) won the AMA for Favorite Album – Rap/Hip-Hop. Favorite Song – Rap/Hip-Hop was awarded to Lil Nas X featuring Billy Ray Cyrus ("Old Town Road").
