Anushka Sharma couldn’t stop smiling as Virat Kohli returned from Kolkata

25 Nov 2019, 11:29 by Vedanshi Pathak

Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma are undoubtedly one of the most popular celebrity couples of today. They are always seen having a great time in each other’s company and notwithstanding their hectic work schedules, they make it a point to find time for each other. Virat, who was in Kolkata to play the first-ever pink ball test match in India, was snapped at the airport as he returned to Mumbai earlier this morning. Virat hit a century during the match and it was reason enough to make his wife Anushka Sharma beam with elation.
