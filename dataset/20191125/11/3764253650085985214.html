Unai Emery: Arsenal players reportedly demand for the sacking of Spanish boss

25 Nov 2019, 11:33

- Unai Emery could be asked to leave Arsenal as reports claim players have lost faith in him

- The Gunners have been winless in six consecutive matches in all competitions so far

- Reports also claim Jose Mourinho's appointment as Tottenham boss has sparked reactions

Unai Emery could be frustrated out of Arsenal by players after less than two years in charge of the north London club.

The Gunners were forced to a 2-2 draw at the Emirates by Southampton and sources claim the players have lost faith in the Spanish manager.

According to Telegraph, players have now joined fans who have been calling for the sack of Emery and do not believe that he is the man to save their season.
