PHOTOS of when Mahama visited Dumelo’s family

25 Nov 2019, 11:34 by MyNewsGH

Former President of Ghana, John Dramani Mahama paid a visit to John Dumelo’s residence to see the actor and politician’s son.

In Photos and videos on social media you can see the former President very happy to see the young John, first son of the National Democratic Congress'(NDC’s) Parliamentary Candidate for Ayawaso West Wuogon

John Dramni Mahama has been an integral part of the political growth of John Dumelo and played a key role in his wedding.
