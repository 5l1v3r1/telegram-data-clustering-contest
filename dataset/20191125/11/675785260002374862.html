Molly-Mae Hague defended by fans after vicious trolling over 'orange' foundation

Fans have hit out at trolls who accused Molly-Mae of 'blackfishing' for wearing a shade darker than her natural skintone

25 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Louise Randell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-randell/

Molly-Mae Hague has been defended by fans after she was targeted by vicious trolls who mocked her "orange" foundation.

The Love Island star posted a video online showing her doing her make-up, and she was seen using a base darker than her natural skin tone.

Molly-Mae explained she uses a darker shade to stop her looking so pale in winter, but was bombarded with abuse from trolls.

She said: "I’ve really been enjoying Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk but this is too dark for me so I’m going to be mixing it with my studio fix fluid from MAC which is a holy grail of mine.

"I use it all the time and this is in the shade NC42 which is still very dark.

"I often get lots of comments on how my face is too dark.

"I go very pale in the winter time and obviously now it’s getting colder, it’s getting wintery.

"So I’ve gotten quite pale. ‘Once my foundation is on, and the whole world has hated me for how orange my face is…"

One troll wrote: "Is this satire? There’s no way she actually believes it’s a match… It’s like 10 shades too dark."

Another added: "he uses nc42 which is the LIGHTER bottle she’s holding… I’m punjabi and i’m NC30.

"I know black women and asian women who are NC42. lol.

"You can defend this all you want but it’s actual madness & FYI it doesn’t look believable for one second. you are all orange blud."

However, Molly-Mae has been defended by fans who have called the trolling "madness".

One tweeted: "Can’t lie Molly-mae’s Nc42 foundation isn’t really that dark. I wear NC44 and I’m light skinned.

"It has a tanned/orange undertone so it goes well with fake tan."

Another wrote: "I wish people would actually watch the video of molly-mae doing her makeup because they’d realise after concealer, powder etc her foundation matches her tan perfectly and doesn’t look “too dark”

A third added: "Poor molly-mae Hague never gets a break, first for a photo where she looks like she’s got a hairy arse, now Cos she’s wearing shade NC42 foundation she’s accused of black fishing...

"Hold the door, I wear the same shades - someone lock me up and through away the key."
