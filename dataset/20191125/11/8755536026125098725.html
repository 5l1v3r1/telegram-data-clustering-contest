Rosanna Davison prepares to return home with new baby Sophia

Rosanna shared that she is delighted to be a "family of three"

25 Nov 2019, 11:32 by Sarah Magliocco

Rosanna Davison and Wes Quirke have welcomed their first child Sophia Rose into the world.

The loved-up couple shared that they left the maternity hospital last night with their baby girl.

The move is the couple’s first step on preparing to return to Ireland with their daughter, who they welcomed via surrogate in an unknown country abroad.

Taking to Instagram, Rosanna shared the first family photo of the trio.

“Leaving the maternity hospital today to begin our new life together as a family of three,” she wrote.

“But Leo & Ted make five – can’t forget about the fur babies,” she joked.

Rosanna shared that she was the one to cut the umbilical cord at the birth.

Rosanna and Wes announced the birth of their daughter last week. Baby Sophia Rose was born on November 21st 2019 at 11.57am, weighing a healthy 6lbs8oz.

The couple turned to surrogacy to have a child after struggling with pregnancy loss.
