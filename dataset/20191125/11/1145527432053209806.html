Aadhaar app update: UIDAI rolls out new features; users can lock biometrics, create virtual ID

25 Nov 2019, 11:50 by BusinessToday.In

The new mAadhar, after the latest update, has features like sharing Aadhar details with the others. Users can also get a new Aadhar card directly from the app and check Aadhar authentication history

Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) has recently launched a new update to its mAadhaar app for iOS and Android users. This new update enables all the Aadhar cardholders to access their Aadhar ID details on a single-window for all Aadhar related online services on their smartphones.

UIDAI, in a tweet, said, "Uninstall any previously installed versions of the mAadhaar app from your mobile. Download and install the New mAadhaar App."
