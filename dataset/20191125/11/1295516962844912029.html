The Mandalorian: Disney Reverses Baby Yoda GIF Takedown Campaign

25 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Mike Bloom

The Mandalorian GIFs of Baby Yoda are no longer in a galaxy far, far away.

GIPHY announced that GIFs of Baby Yoda, a breakout character from the first-ever live-action Star Wars series, will return to its filebase. The news comes in the wake of the revelation last week that GIFs of the adorable youngster had seemingly disappeared from social media.

"Last week, there was some confusion around certain content uploaded to GIPHY and we temporarily removed these GIFs while we reviewed the situation," GIPHY's spokesperson told ComicBook.com. "We apologize to both Disney and Vulture for any inconvenience, and we are happy to report that the GIFs are once again live on GIPHY."

The Vulture reference refers to an article posted by the publication last week, which highlighted only the scenes featuring the green creature that has taken the universe by storm. In lieu of direct GIFs being available at the time, Vulture persisted by partnering with illustrator Martin Gee, who brought the scenes to life with his own hand-drawn animations.

Created by Jon Favreau, The Mandalorian stars Pedro Pascal, Gina Carano, Carl Weathers, Giancarlo Esposito, Emily Swallow, Omid Abtahi, Werner Herzog and Nick Nolte. The first three episodes are streaming now on Disney+.
