Post mortem to be carried out on body found in Dublin

25 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Paul Reynolds, https://www.facebook.com/rtenews/

Gardaí have said they are currently treating the death of a man whose body was found in a container in Dublin this morning as a personal tragedy.

However, a post-mortem examination is due to be carried out tomorrow to confirm the cause of death.

The discovery was made at around 10.50am at Balbutcher Lane in Ballymun.

The man in his 40s was pronounced dead at the scene and his body has since been removed to the Dublin City Morgue where a post-mortem examination will be carried out tomorrow 

All the circumstances surrounding the discovery of the body are being investigated but, pending the outcome of the post-mortem, gardaí are treating the case as a personal tragedy.
