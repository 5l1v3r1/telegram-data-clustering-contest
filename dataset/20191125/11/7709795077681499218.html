BBC Sports Personality of the Year nominees: Ben Stokes & Dina Asher-Smith nominated for 2019 award

25 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Press Association

England all-rounder Ben Stokes and athlete Dina Asher-Smith were the first two nominees announced for the 2019 BBC Sports Personality of the Year award.

Stokes was announced as the first contender by the BBC, as an obvious nomination after starring in England's Cricket World Cup triumph.

In the final against New Zealand, he hit 84 not out - including two sixes in the final over - to tie the game, and then batted in the super over with Jos Buttler to help England to a dramatic victory.
