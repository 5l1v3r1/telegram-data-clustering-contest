Bigg Boss 13: High Chances Of Sidharth Shukla Or Asim Riaz Winning BB13? Celebs On The Panel Think So

25 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Amruta Patel

With the TV reality show Bigg Boss 13 finale still a long way to go, family members and friends of the housemates, have high hopes on Sidharth Shukla or Asim Riaz, winning this season. The two have proved to be the strongest contestants on the show with their strong foothold and growing popularity

The latest episode of Weekend Ka Vaar from the ongoing reality TV show Bigg Boss 13 saw Salman Khan host the family members and friends of the housemates. The episode saw small screen couple Jay Bhanushali-Mahhi Vij supporting Rashami Desai,  Paras Chhabra’s GF Akansha Puri coming out in support of her BF, Shefali Jariwala’s husband Parag Tyagi and Asim Riaz’s brother Umar Riaz on the show. While the episode saw the celebs supporting their family members and friends in the house, host Salman Khan also asked them, a “who could win the show”? Question! 

And the reply was but obvious. Almost all present on the show had Sidharth Shukla and Asim Riaz’s name on their minds. Well, it’s too early to predict the winner of this season, the fact of these contestants becoming a household name due to their stronghold on the show, too can’t be ignored. Once upon a time, buddies in the Bigg Boss house, have turned foe after their repetitive fallouts over the period of time in the house.  So who do you think will take home the BB13 trophy?
