Yvonne Okoro releases stunning photo to celebrate 35th birthday

25 Nov 2019, 11:37 by Nana Yaw

Ghanaian actress, Yvonne Okoro is 35 years today and to mark the day, she has released a stunning photo to show to the world, she’s still young and beautiful at that age.

In a post on Instagram, the beautiful actress noted she’s old but she’s always identified as an 18-year-old.

“I identify as 18 but my Mama and birth certificate says i am 35years old TODAY..🥳🥳🥳🍾🎂..(haters)..happy Birthday to me i guess,” she captioned alongside the beautiful released photo.
