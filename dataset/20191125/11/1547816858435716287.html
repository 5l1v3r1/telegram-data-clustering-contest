Genelia D’Souza Pens An Emotional And An Encouraging Post For Her Son Riaan On His 5th Birthday

25 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Amruta Patel

After a star-studded birthday bash hosted by Genelia D Souza and Riteish Deshmukh for their son Riaan, comes in an emotional post penned by the actress for her son as he turned 5. Riaan is the couple’s firstborn post their wedlock. Sharing an adorable picture on her Instagram handle, she penned a long post blessing and thanking him

Actress Genelia D’Souza has been on a sabbatical post she tied the knot with actor Riteish Deshmukh and then welcoming her sons Riaan and Rahyl in the year 2014 and 2016 respectively. Now making a comeback to the glamour world, the actress is not failing to fulfill her paternal duties of being a wonderful mommy to her sons. After hosting a star-studded birthday bash for her elder son Riaan, as he turned five, she penned down an emotional and encouraging post for him on her Instagram handle.

Wishing him on his birthday, her post had all the encouraging words a child would get from his mother and ultimately making them strong enough to face the world. Her post further mentions of a promise to her son, of walking with him and having her back throughout his life. Concluding her post, she thanked Riaan, as he is the best thing ever happened to her. Just a day ago, the star couple had hosted a lavish birthday bash for their son in town. It was attended by many star kids along with their parents; the list included Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Aaradhya Bachchan, Mira Rajput and Misha Kapoor and many more.

Unlike mommy Genelia, proud daddy Ritiesh Deshmukh had shared a picture on his Instagram story from Riaan’s birthday bash. The duo is seen happily posing for the paps, with their sons at the birthday bash venue.
