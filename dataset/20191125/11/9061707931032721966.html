Loose horses cause danger for drivers on South Derbyshire road

They've had an absolute mare

25 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Beth Pridding

Police have shared a picture of loose horses who were found wandering around a country lane in Barrow-on-Trent.

Officers from Derbyshire Police had just started a night shift last night, November 24, when they came across the three horses.

A spokesman for Swadlincote Response Unit wrote on Twitter: "Group two's night shift started off with a mare of a job!"

All the horses were returned to where they had come from and are safe and well, police confirmed.

Police have been asked in which road, and what time, the horses were found, but said they could not find that information.
