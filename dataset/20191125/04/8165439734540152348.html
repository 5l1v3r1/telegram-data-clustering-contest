Ricky Gervais Confirms Season 2 of After Life Release Date

25 Nov 2019, 04:40 by Tuathla Lucey

Something to get us through to the New Year.

Ricky Gervais has confirmed when season 2 of After Life will drop on Netflix.

If you haven't seen it, the show revolves around Tony (Gervais) and his seemingly perfect life. However, after his wife suddenly dies, Tony changes.

After considering taking his own life, he decides instead to live long enough to punish the world by saying and doing whatever he likes from now on.

He thinks it's like a super power - not caring about himself or anyone else - but it turns out to be tricky when everyone is trying to save the nice guy they used to know...
