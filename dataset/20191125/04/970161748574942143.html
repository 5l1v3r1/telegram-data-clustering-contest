AMAs arrivals: Taylor Swift, Selena Gomez and Lizzo nail the red carpet

25 Nov 2019, 04:36

The American Music Awards took place on Sunday night in Los Angeles and the stars of the music biz were out in full force.

It was definitely going to be a night to remember with the likes of Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello performing, as well as Lizzo, Post Malone, Travis Scott and Ozzy Osbourne taking to the stage and Taylor Swift being honoured with the Artist of the Decade prize.

But, before all the action took place on stage, it all went down on the red carpet where Swifty was just one of the famous faces leading the glamour.

She totally slayed on her arrival in thigh-high boots and a glittering green dress that had a NSFW slit to one side.

Brushing off her ongoing feud with Scooter Braun – the latest saw Taylor accuse Scooter of banning her from performing her old songs at the AMAs after buying her back catalogue, Taylor favoured natural make up and her hair styled to one side.

She was joined by her BFF Selena Gomez, who recently marked her musical comeback with new track Love You To Lose Me – a break up ballad which is heavily rumoured to be about her ex Justin Bieber.

And Gomez definitely came to play, as she stepped out sporting a chic bob hair cut and a neon yellow strapless mini dress.

Selena had opened the AMAs with a powerful performance of her new hit and another track called Look At Her Now and it marked her first live TV performance in two years.

Also performing on the night had been Lizzo, who rocked up to the red carpet with what you could probably say is the world’s tiniest handbag.

The singer casually posed for pics on her arrival holding her teeny tiny accessory which arguably outshone her one-shouldered ruffled orange number. Seriously, what can she fit in it?

Advertisement Advertisement

During her red carpet interview, Lizzo revealed there are only three of the little handbags in the world.

As for what she packed for the evening, the singer joked: ‘I got tampons in here, a flask of tequila, condoms.’

Lols.

Post Malone was flying the flag for the male music stars and chose to attend the AMAs in a bold, two-piece checked suit and cowboy hat.

Looking sharp, he later picked up the award for Favourite Album Rap/Hip Hop for his release, Hollywood Bleeding.

Other red carpet arrivals included a very glam Heidi Klum, Dua Lipa and her boyfriend Anwar Hadid and show host Ciara, who dazzled in blue.

Christina Aguilera and Billie Eilish fought it out for most bizarre outfit choice on the night with their striking get-ups and Jenna Dewan looked simply divine as she showed off her baby bump in a fuchsia pink floor-length dress.

She is expecting a baby with her boyfriend of one year, Steve Kazee.

Real life lovebirds Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello decided to shun a PDA-filled arrival to the AMAs and instead rocked up separately, with Shawn looking sharp in a purple suit blazer and trousers, while Camila looked like a princess in a nude gown.

So much glam. So much talent. If only we could have bagged an invite, eh?
