DMRC recruitment 2019: Pay scale at Rs 1 lakh, vacancies for Manager posts

DMRC recruitment 2019: For the posts of Manager, the candidates will get a monthly salary of Rs 90,200, while Rs 70,180 for the posts of Assistant Manager. The candidates can apply through the website- delhimetrorail.com

25 Nov 2019, 04:41 by Careers Desk

DMRC recruitment 2019: The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has invited applications from eligible candidates for recruitment for the posts of Manager. There are 12 vacancies for the posts of Manager, and Assistant Manager.

The interested candidates can apply through the official website- delhimetrorail.com.

The selected candidates for the posts of Manager will get a remuneration of Rs 90,200 per month, while for the posts of Assistant Manager, the candidates will be in the pay scale of Rs 70,180 per month.

DMRC recruitment 2019: Vacancies

Total vacant posts: 12

Name of the posts: Manager, Assistant Manager

Eligibility criteria:

Education qualifications:

The candidates will hold a degree or diploma in Electrical Engineering with minimum 60 per cent marks from a government recognised university or institute.

Experience: The candidates should have work experience in the field of maintenance, erection and testing.

Age Limit:

The upper age limit of the candidates should not cross 61 years. The candidates belong to reserved category will get an age relaxation of 1 year.

Pay scale:

Assistant Manager: The candidates will get a monthly salary of Rs 70,180

Manager: The candidates for the posts of Manager will get a remuneration of Rs 90,200 per month.

Selection process:

The candidates will be selected on the basis of Personal Interview (PI), physical ability, knowledge, skills.

The candidates can apply through the website- delhimetrorail.com.
