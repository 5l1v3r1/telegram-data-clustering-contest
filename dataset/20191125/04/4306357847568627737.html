‘Exceptional’: Jurgen Klopp sends message to Liverpool FC fans

Jurgen Klopp has his say on Liverpool FC's brilliant start to the new Premier League season

25 Nov 2019, 04:45 by The Sport Review staff

Jurgen Klopp has admitted that he has been thrilled by his side’s electric start to the new season in the Premier League.

The Reds have been in sensational form this season and they continued their brilliant start to their new Premier League campaign when they sealed a 2-1 win at Crystal Palace on Saturday.

That win was their 12th in 13 Premier League games this season and the Reds are currently the heavy favourites to win the title following their brilliant start.

Indeed, Liverpool FC have only dropped two points so far this season in what has been a sensational start to the new campaign for Klopp’s men.

The Reds are aiming to go one step further than they did last season as they bid to win the Premier League title this term.

And Klopp has admitted that he has been thrilled by the way his side have started the new Premier League campaign.

Speaking at his post-match news conference on Saturday, Klopp said: “I have no clue what people will say. But I know that 37 points after 13 matchdays is exceptional.

“You cannot have them with winning only brilliant games. We had a couple of really brilliant games – and we won them, thank God – but we cannot only win them, so that’s how it is.

“We are not out there to send messages, we actually just want to get results.”

Next up for Liverpool FC is a Champions League clash against Napoli on Wednesday night.

They will then switch their attentions back to Premier League affairs and their home clash against Brighton and Hove Albion next weekend.
