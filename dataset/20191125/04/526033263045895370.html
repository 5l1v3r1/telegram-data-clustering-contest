'Start Here': Navy secretary fired over SEAL case and Bloomberg enters 2020 race

25 Nov 2019, 04:59

It's Monday, Nov., 25, 2019. Let's start here.

1. SEAL fight

Secretary of Defense Mark Esper forced out Navy Secretary Richard Spencer Sunday for his handling of a case involving Chief Petty Officer Edward Gallagher, a Navy SEAL accused of war crimes in Iraq who was granted clemency by President Donald Trump.

Esper was not aware Spencer had privately told White House officials that Gallagher would be allowed to keep his SEAL status if they did not intervene to stop a disciplinary proceeding, according to a statement from Chief Pentagon Spokesman Jonathan Hoffman.
