Selena Gomez Reportedly Had a Panic Attack Before Her AMAs Performance

25 Nov 2019, 04:49
  • Selena Gomez performed “Lose You to Love Me” at the 2019 AMAs last night, and it was not received well by some viewers.
  • A source now says Selena had a panic attack before the show, which impacted her performance.

The AMAs may have ended last night, but people are (unfortunately) still critiquing Selena Gomez’s performance of “Lose You to Love Me.” Why? Well, because some people online thought Selena was really off-key, and some went a step too far by joking about how the song’s lyrics literally say “sang off-key in my chorus.” The internet can be so mean!

Selena’s fans chalked it all up to nerves, and as it turns out, they were pretty spot on. An E! News source came forward today and said Selena’s performance struggles last night happened because she had a bit of a breakdown before going onstage—poor Selena. 😓

“Selena definitely had anxiety and a panic attack before she went out and was putting a lot of pressure on herself,” the source said.
