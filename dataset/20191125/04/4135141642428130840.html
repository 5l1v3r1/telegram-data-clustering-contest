Survivor Series 2019 results

25 Nov 2019, 04:00 by WWE.com

For Survivor Series results, photos and video highlights, stick with WWE.com throughout the day.

The action begins at 5 ET/2 PT with a special two-hour Kickoff, which streams live on the award-winning WWE Network and can be seen on a free stream on WWE.com, the WWE App, YouTube, Twitter, Facebook and Twitch. The action includes a Triple Threat Match for the NXT Cruiserweight Championship and a Champion vs. Champion vs. Champion Triple Threat Match between Raw Tag Team Champions The Viking Raiders, SmackDown Tag Team Champions The New Day and NXT Tag Team Champions The Undisputed ERA.

Then, Raw, SmackDown and NXT battle in a head-to-head competition for brand supremacy, streaming live on WWE Network at 7 ET/4 PT. The show features “The Fiend” Bray Wyatt vs. Daniel Bryan for the Universal Championship, two 5-on-5 Survivor Series Triple Threat Matches and two Champion vs. Champion vs. Champion Triple Threat Matches, including Becky Lynch vs. Bayley vs. Shayna Baszler.

As the action unfolds on the ultimate battle for brand supremacy, WWE.com will update this story with live results.
