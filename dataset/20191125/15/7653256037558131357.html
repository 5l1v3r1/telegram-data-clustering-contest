Saudi Aramco IPO retail subscription at $5.8 billion, says lead manager

Aramco, the world's biggest crude oil producing company, is looking to sell 1.5% of its stock in the IPO.

25 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Reuters

RIYADH: Retail subscription for Saudi Aramco's initial public offering (IPO) reached 21.77 billion Saudi riyals ($5.8 billion) on Monday, lead manager Samba Capital said.

Aramco, the world's biggest crude oil producing company, is looking to sell 1.5% of its stock in the IPO.
