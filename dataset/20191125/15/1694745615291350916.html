Halsey Thanked Her Fans For "Keeping the Magic Alive" in Her AMAs Acceptance Speech

25 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Chanel Vargas

Image Source: Getty / Jeff Kravitz

It looks like major congratulations are in order for Halsey! The singer took home the American Music Award for favorite song for her hit "Without Me" on Sunday, and her heartwarming acceptance speech has me beyond proud to be a fan. "When I was a kid, I used to believe that this award was the ultimate validation . . . but most of these awards really aren't what they seem at all," the "Graveyard" singer said. "But I am so thankful to the AMAs because they are the world's largest fan-voted award show."
