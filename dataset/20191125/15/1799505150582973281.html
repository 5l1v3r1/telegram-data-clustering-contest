Kaiser

25 Nov 2019, 15:59 by BRIEF

OAKLAND, Calif. — Sutter Bay Hospitals, et al., sued Kaiser Foundation Health Plan in Alameda County Court, claiming it has dramatically reduced reimbursements for emergency and trauma services, using a “secret new methodology” that is not legal in California.
