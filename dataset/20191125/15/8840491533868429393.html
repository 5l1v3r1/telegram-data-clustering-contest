Funding approved for works at 26 Mayo primary schools

25 Nov 2019, 15:30

THE Minister for Rural and Community Development, Michael Ring, has confirmed that significant improvement works will take place 22 primary schools and four post primary schools in Mayo next summer as part of the government’s Summer Works Scheme.

Improvement works will take place at the following schools in the county:
