Hardcore porn retweeted from Welsh Office Twitter account

They say the account was hacked

25 Nov 2019, 15:51 by Katie-Ann Gupwell

Hardcore porn has been tweeted from a Welsh Office Twitter account.

An extremely graphic screenshot was shared on social media after being re-tweeted on Monday afternoon from the UK Government in Wales Twitter account.

The message was quickly deleted and a spokesman has since alleged that the account had been the victim of a "cyber-attack".

Stace Williams Tweeted: "That awkward moment when the UK Government in Wales account tweets porn into your timeline."

Another social media user posted: "Biggest faux pas of the day goes to whomever is running the UK Government in Wales @UKGovWales Twitter account who r/ted this."

Following the incident, UK Government in Wales posted on Twitter: "We are aware that our account was subject to a cyber-attack earlier.

"We apologise for any offence caused by the material shared.

"We are confident the issue has now been resolved.

"If you find yourself in a similar situation please see: https://ncsc.gov.uk/section/information-for/individuals-families "
