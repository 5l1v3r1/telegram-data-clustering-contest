ESPN Analyst Thanks Tom Herman For Not Taking The LSU Job

25 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Andrew Holleran

LSU pursued then-Houston head coach Tom Herman back in 2016 after firing Les Miles. The Tigers expressed interest in Herman, though he said no to a potential meeting, as he was leaning toward the Texas job, which he ended up getting.

Three years later, LSU is being led toward the College Football Playoff by Ed Orgeron. Texas is 6-5, barely making a bowl game.

ESPN analyst Ryan Clark, an LSU alum, had a message for Herman following Saturday’s game. The message is a simple one: Thank you.

“As a LSU alum I would like to thank you Tom Herman. A few years back you made the decision I never knew I wanted you to make. You chose Texas & for that I am grateful,” he tweeted.
