Best Black Friday software deals for Mac and iOS users

25 Nov 2019, 15:46 by William Gallagher

It's not just hardware you can get a good deal on during Black Friday and Cyber Monday, there are significant savings to be had on the best software around. Check out our continually updated guide to the best app deals available.

Here are the most significant deals on the best software for Mac, iPhone and iPad, that are available during this year's prime shopping season. While Black Friday itself is officially November 29, the majority of these deals start early and continue late. But all of them will vanish at some point soon, so if you see software you need, be certain that the price is going to go back up shortly so you should buy right away.

Frankly, we're happy paying full price for the software that we rely on, but that doesn't mean we'll ignore a bargain. Nor does it stop us scouring for deals, so keep checking back here as we find new ones.

40% off Adobe Creative Cloud

By the size of the discount, this one is very good. But by the size of what it applies to, it's incredible. Adobe is discounting Creative Cloud plans and that means the price of every single app is cut —until November 29.

You can currently get a 40% discount off Adobe's entire collection of apps for both Mac and iOS. That includes industry-standard Mac apps like Photoshop, Illustrator and Premiere Pro —and the new iPad version of Photoshop.

Creative Cloud is sold in different versions, but the discount offer applies to what's called the All Apps plan. It's now $29.99 per month when you sign up for an annual plan, or $359.88 when you buy the whole year in advance.

20% off Parallels Desktop

Parallels has also issued its Black Friday deals early, with a 20% off instant discount on Parallels Desktop. New licenses are $63.99 after the markdown, while upgrades are $39.99 (reg. $49.99). These discounts, which only happen once a year, are valid through Cyber Monday.

Fantastical, Cardhop and Chatology

Flexibits is running a 20% discount sale from 22:00 ET on November 24 to the same time on December 9. That means, amongst other bargains, that one of the calendar apps AppleInsider most recommends is now forty bucks on the Mac instead of fifty.

Fantastical 2 for Mac is $39.99, but you'll also want the separate versions for iPhone and iPad. Fantastical 2 for iPhone is now just $3.99, and for iPad it's $7.99.

Flexibits is also the developer of Cardhop, a kind of bionic contacts app that we've enthused about on the Mac and on iOS too.

Cardhop and Fantastical are both built to make it fast to find the contact or the appointment you need, and Flexibits's Chatology does the same for searching Messages. It's now just $15.99 for the Mac.

Check out the details of all these apps on the Flexibits site.

Affinity

The maker of Affinity Photo, Affinity Designer and the new Affinity Publisher has a sale of 30% off each of those apps —plus the company's workbooks, content packs.

This means that Affinity Publisher is now $33.99 instead of $49.99, and so too are both Photo and Designer. Affinity Designer and Affinity Photo are also available on iPad, and there the 30% discount makes them just $13.99 each.

DEVONtechnologies

You could regard DEVONthink as an Evernote- or Apple Notes-style app, but that would be missing the real point of this extremely powerful research tool which uses AI to find connections between your notes.

For the Black Friday to Cyber Monday weekend, all versions of DEVONthink —and all upgrades —are 25% off. That means the standard edition of DEVONthink 3 is $75 instead of $99. DEVONthink Pro 3 is $149 instead of $199. And the upgrade from standard to pro is also 25% off.

So are the company's companion apps, including DEVONagent, which is a web search app whose pro version will now cost $38. And also DEVONsphere Express, a desktop search app that organizes and indexes the documents on your Mac, which will be $7.50.

The 25% sale runs from Friday to Monday on the DEVONtechnologies site, and the discounts are applied during checkout.

Roxio

Roxio is offering discounts of up to $40 on its Roxio Creator and Toast apps —plus a bonus gift. The sale is only on from November 25 to November 29, so you have to buy before this weekend.

Roxio Toast is the long-standing app for creating and burning DVDs on your Mac, while Roxio Creator adds video and photo editing.

Check out both on the discounts of up to $40.

Corel

You can save up to 60% off the cost of Corel software, including such major apps as Corel Painter, PaintShop Pro and WordPerfect Office. But note that the sale ends on Friday November 29

Visit the Corel site and enter discount codes at checkout.

The code 10CORELSAVE gets you 10% off the majority of apps, although not CorelDRAW. PSP2020SAVE10 is also a 10% discount off the likes of PaintShop Pro, and CD2019SAVE5 takes 5% off the cost of CorelDRAW Graphics Suite 2019.

Other items on the store have had their price lowered directly, such as Painter 2020 which is 30% off.
