Man cited for second OWI after rear-ending Madison Metro bus

25 Nov 2019, 15:53 by Logan Rude

MADISON, Wis. - A Sun Prairie man was cited for his second OWI after he rear-ended a Madison Metro bus with a truck Friday morning, according to an incident report.

Police said no one on the bus was injured, but the driver suffered a broken nose. According to the report, the man was driving erratically when he hit the bus on High Crossing Boulevard and East Springs Drive.
