Photos of Shatta Bandle in his JHS days hits online

25 Nov 2019, 15:16 by Qwame Benedict

Richman of the moment Shatta Bandle is really enjoying his celebrity status after his video went viral some months ago.

His antics on social media has got many to love him though people refer to him as an illiterate who is using the fame acquired to earn some money.

Well, Shatta Bandle is not as illiterate as one woman once described him in a viral video some months ago.

A new photo has surfaced and it sees Shatta Bandle in his school uniform seated in a classroom with his colleagues.
