Monday night on TV: Music and comedy

25 Nov 2019, 15:00

Cape Town – From an English family taking on the American dream, to the American Music Awards, to an award-winning tragicomedy – here are our top picks for your Monday night viewing.

Living the Dream

20:00 - ITV Choice (DStv 123)

Philip Glenister and Lesley Sharp reunite with another series, Living the Dream, a comedy that follows a British family leaving Yorkshire for the dream life in Miami, Florida. However, things don’t turn out as planned, and the Pemberton family must navigate their new environment. When the family arrive in Miami, they take up the challenge of running a trailer park, but the residents aren’t too keen on their plans. (READ MORE HERE)
