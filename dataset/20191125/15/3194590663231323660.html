ICYMI: Chimamanda becomes first Nigerian to receive UN’s Global Leadership Award

25 Nov 2019, 15:32 by Stephen Charles Kenechukwu

Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie, Nigeria’s award-winning novelist and feminism activist, has been honoured with the United Nations Foundation Global Leadership Award.

The 42-year-old author was honoured with the prestigious prize at ‘We the Peoples’, the UN Foundation’s 2019 global leadership dinner held in New York.

The award celebrates outstanding individuals whose work “brings to life the goals enshrined in the UN Charter to promote peace, justice, and fundamental human rights for all people.”
