Ntshumayelo Surfaces At New Club

25 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Soccer Laduma

Thandani ‘Bibo’ Ntshumayelo has surfaced at a new club as he attempts to revive his career.

Having finished last season with Baroka FC, who signed him on a short-term deal in March after serving his ban from sport, Ntshumayelo has struggled in his search for a new club.

The midfielder trained with SuperSport United during their pre-season, though there was no deal offered to him. 

According to sources, the 29-year-old has surfaced at JDR Stars in the GladAfrica Championship for training with the team.

“Thandani Ntshumayelo is at JDR Stars. He arrived today for training with the team,” said a source.  

JDR, who are currently second on the log, defeated Jomo Cosmos 5-1 at the weekend. The side looks to add experience to the squad in their chase for promotion to top-flight football.
