Coronation Street SPOILERS: Fiz gets rid of Jade?

Jade has a new job

25 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Charlotte Rodrigues

In next week's episodes of Coronation Street, it looks like Jade is moving onto a new job.

In preview pictures for on the ITV website Evelyn quizzes Jade about the role.

However Jade remains tight-lipped and shows an apparent lack of emotion when saying goodbye to Fiz.

Is this the last we'll see of Jade?

This week, Fiz's daughter Hope pushes Fiz and Tyrone to breaking point.

When the little girl, who has behaviour problems, asks for a firework party, Fiz puts her foot down worrying that her daughter's love of flammable objects is getting out of hand.

Although Tyrone is also worried he suggests they have the party to keep Hope happy.

But things soon get out of hand when Fiz notices the back garden is on fire and Hope is standing nearby watching the flames in amusement.

Fiz and Tyrone are horrified to realise Hope must've started the fire, but Jack soon reveals the fire had already started when he and Hope entered the back garden.

Did Hope start the fire, or could it of been home tutor Jade?

With the evidence pointing to Hope, Fiz admits to Tyrone she thinks her daughter was behind the fire in the factory building site.

Tyrone is shocked when his girlfriend admits she's been thinking the worst about Hope for weeks but didn't want to admit the truth out of fear he would leave her.

The pair eventually make up and take a stand against the little girl, deciding to be tougher on her.

But Jade is already stirring drama and suggests to Evelyn that Fiz's parenting skills aren't up to scratch.

And Jade's point seems to be confirmed when Fiz smashes Hope's iPad in a fit of rage after finding a lighter hidden in her doll.

However it's not all bad as Fiz soon receives a letter from Hope's old school saying there's space for her and she can return.

But with Hope returning to school, it means the family no longer need Jade.

Fiz is forced to break the news to the live-in tutor that her services are no longer needed. How will she take the news?

Coronation Street airs on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays at 7.30pm and 8.30pm on ITV.
