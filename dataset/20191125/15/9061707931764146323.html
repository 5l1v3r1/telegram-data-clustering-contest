Son of former Derby County man linked with Premier League move

The 20-year-old winger has apparently been catching the eye with his performances this season

25 Nov 2019, 15:39 by Chris Watson

The son of former Derby County midfielder Lars Bohinen is being linked with a move to the Premier League.

Emil Bohinen has apparently been catching the eye with his performances this season.

According to Sky Sports, Brighton & Hove Albion are "among a number of Premier League clubs" keen on the 20-year-old winger.

Bohinen junior currently plays for Stabaek JF in the top flight of Norwegian football.
