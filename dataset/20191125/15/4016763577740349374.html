Here Are All The Winners Of The 2019 American Music Awards

25 Nov 2019, 15:25 by Chris Mench

The 2019 American Music Awards went down on Sunday night, attracting a small army of celebrities and some major performers. Taylor Swift came away as the night’s biggest winner, taking home Artist of the Year on top of her Artist of the Decade honor, plus awards for Favorite Music Video, Favorite Female Artist – Pop/Rock, Favorite Album – Pop/Rock, and Favorite Artist – Adult Contemporary. She surpassed Michael Jackson’s record in the process, becoming the artist with the most AMA wins in the history of the show.

Other major winners included Halsey, BTS, Billie Eilish, Lil Nas X, Khalid, Post Malone, Beyoncé, Marshmello, Cardi B, Shawn Mendes, Camila Cabello, and Carrie Underwood.
