Taylor Swift Reminds You (and Scooter Braun) She’s ‘The Man’ With AMAs Medley

25 Nov 2019, 15:09 by Halle Kiefer

After winning Favorite Pop/Rock Album and ahead of receiving this year’s American Music Artist of the Decade Award from Carole King, Taylor Swift took to the AMAs stage Sunday to remind you that she’s Taylor Swift. While the set might not have been specifically referencing her ongoing feud with Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta, it’s hard not to read into Taylor’s choice to start her set with “The Man” from Lover in a button-down adorned with the titles of all the albums Braun now owns. She also sang “I Knew You Were Trouble,” “Blank Space,” “Lover” (with a performance from Misty Copeland), and “Shake It Off” with Camila Cabello and Halsey, among others, but still. “I’m so sick of them coming at me again/’Cause if I was a man, then I’d be the man!” Come on now.
