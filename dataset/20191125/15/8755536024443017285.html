Meghan Markle and Prince Harry share baby Archie’s first adorable milestone

The couple are taking a hands on approach to the milestone moment

25 Nov 2019, 15:37 by Sarah Magliocco

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry have reached a special milestone with their son Archie.

The mini-royal is currently being weaned on to solid food by the Duke and Duchess of Sussex.

Speaking to The Royal Story, royal commentator Omid Scobie claimed the six month old is moving on to real food.

He said: “Meghan says that Archie’s currently being weaned at the moment.”

“He’s moving on to, or I guess at some point, moving on to solids.”

Omid also claimed to have insight into the royal’s diet, via the former-royal chef to Prince Charles and Princess Diana.

The chef highlighted that baby Archie’s diet would be wholly “organic.”

It is also said to be “seasonal and very simple” with “everything made fresh.”

“There’s so much pre-made food available but I’m sure they would like doing everything themselves” they added.
