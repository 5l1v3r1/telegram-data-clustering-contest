Inside pictures from the big South film industry reunion hosted by Chiranjeevi

25 Nov 2019, 15:34 by Filmfare

South megastar Chiranjeevi who has a huge mass-following recently hosted a party at his new house in Hyderabad. The bash was not a regular house-warming gig. The superstar made it special by calling all his co-stars and industry folks from the ’80s. The party was attended by the who’s who of the South industry including Venkatesh Daggubati, Mohanlal, Nagarjuna Akkineni, Balakrishna, Ramesh Aravind, Suman, Jayaram, Khushbu Sundar, Jackie Shroff, Ambika, Menaka, Radha, Saritha, Bhagyaraj, Revathy, Jayasudha, Radhika, Lizzy Priyadarshan and Sumalatha. Moreover, the actor had a colour theme for the bash and everyone was clad in black and gold.

The pictures from the party left the fans quite excited. However, everyone surely missed the presence of Thalaiva, Rajnikanth, who couldn’t attend the party as he was shooting in Chennai for his next film.
