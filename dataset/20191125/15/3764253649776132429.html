Jose Mourinho prepares Tottenham stars ahead of first home game as club boss

25 Nov 2019, 15:00

- Jose Mourinho will be playing his first home game as Tottenham manager on Tuesday night, November 26

- Tottenham will be facing Olympiacos in the Champions League tie in which a win will send them to last 16

- Mourinho was spotted drilling some of his players ahead of the Champions League tie

Jose Mourinho has been spotted preparing his players for the club's next game which will be in the Champions League against Olympiacos.

The former Chelsea manager recently replaced Mauricio Pochettino at Tottenham and he started his reign as Spurs' boss with a 3-2 win over West Ham United.

Known as the 'Special One', Jose Mourinho is now preparing to play his first home game with Tottenham where he will face the home fans.

At the training, Jose Mourinho spent time with all three groups of players - those who played at the weekend, those who were not involved and the goalkeepers.

He was also seen talking with some foreign reporters and sharing jokes with Tottenham staff ahead of the Champions League encounter.

Mourinho will be desperate to win his first home game in charge of Tottenham because a victory will send Spurs to the last 16 of the Champions League.
