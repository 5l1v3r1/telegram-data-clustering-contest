Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos Donates $98.5 Million to Homeless Organizations

25 Nov 2019, 15:48 by VladTV

Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos has donated $98.5 million to organizations that help the homeless population via his Bezos Day One Fund that was founded in 2018. The fund has a commitment of $2 billion to give to existing non-profit organizations that help the homeless and also to create tier-one preschools in low-income areas of the country. Last year, the fund donated $97.5 million.
