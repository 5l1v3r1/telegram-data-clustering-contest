Oman College president wins Distinguished Leadership Award

25 Nov 2019, 09:10 by Times News Service

Muscat: The International Academic Accreditation Services Authority has granted the Distinguished Leadership Award for Higher Education Institutions to Dr. Moudhi Al Ghelani, the President of the Executive Council of Al Bayan College.

A statement issued online by Oman News Agency (ONA), said: “Dr. Moudhi Al Ghelani, the President of the Executive Council of Al Bayan College, received the Distinguished Leadership Award for Higher Education Institutions by the International Academic Accreditation Services Authority accredited by the British Government during the annual international conference held by the Authority in the Malaysian capital of Kuala Lumpur.”
