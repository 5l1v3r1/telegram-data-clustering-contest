Strictly's Kelvin Fletcher has viewers in tears telling romantic story about how he met his wife

The former Emmerdale star was in tears and so were viewers as he talked about Eliza

25 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Katie Fitzpatrick, Jasper King

Strictly Come Dancing fans were left in tears after Kelvin Fletcher told viewers the story about how he met his wife Eliza, reports the Manchester Evening News.

Kelvin explained how the pair met and welled up as he paid tribute to his wife Eliza, who is also the mother of his two children Milo and Marnie.

Kelvin played Andy Sudgen in Emmerdale and met Eliza at school and she was the first girl he ever kissed.

However, the pair lost contact when she went to university because Kelvin was working on Emmerdale. But their future together appears to have been mapped out.

The Oldham actor was in a bar in Manchester when by remarkable chance he found Eliza again.

"I'm in a random bar in Manchester and who of all people appears in the crowd dancing towards me?" he said.

Kelvin married Eliza in 2015, and they welcomed daughter Marnie in August 2016 and son Milo last December.

During the weekend's Strictly performance, he performed to their first dance song Do I Love You (Indeed I Do) by Frank Wilson with his dance partner Oti Mabuse.

"It will mean so much," he said ahead of their performance.

"When she walked down that aisle she took my breath away. Best moment of my life, it was just amazing," he said about their wedding day.

"I just want Liz to know how lucky I feel. Thank you for giving me two amazing incredible children."

And Eliza said about her husband: "He's everything, everything."
