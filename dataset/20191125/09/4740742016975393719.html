I'm A Celeb fans demand justice for James Haskell and Roman Kemp after 'impossible' trial

But Ant and Dec said the stand-ins were able to do it

25 Nov 2019, 09:58 by Emma Flanagan

Last night saw the I'm A Celebrity camp mates take part in their first live Bushtucker Trial and it was certainly a challenge.

With a roast dinner for everyone up for grabs the celebs were determined to collect all 12 stars, and were on track for success after successfully winning their first nine.

But, the final challenge proved too difficult for former rugby-player James Haskell and radio host Roman Kemp.
