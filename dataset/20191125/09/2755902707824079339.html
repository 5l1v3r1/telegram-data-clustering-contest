For the love of beer……2 drinkers gang up & bash security guard for closing bar

Excessive love of beer landed two Victoria Falls behind bars after they violently ganged up on a  security guard for closing a bar while they were still drinking.

Kasanga Ndlovu (32) and Clive Ndlovu (22)beat up Mr Daniso Ncube (32) at Chinotimba bar.

This follows after Mr Ncube ordered them out as it was time to close the bar but the furious duo bashed him with fists and broken empty beer bottles.

Kasanga will serve  an effective seven months in jail after the magistrate invoked another suspended month from a previous case where he bashed his wife,

The magistrate sentenced Clive to six months in prison before suspending two months on condition of good behaviour.
