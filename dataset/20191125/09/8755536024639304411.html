Caitlyn Jenner began gender ressignment in the ’80s but stopped for Kris Jenner

The reality star opened up about her surgeries on I'm A Celeb

25 Nov 2019, 09:18 by Clodagh Meaney

Caitlyn Jenner has revealed that she “grew breasts” in the ’80s, but removed them to marry her ex-wife, Kris Jenner.

The 70-year old is currently starring on ITV’s I’m A Celebrity, Get Me Out Of Here.

Last night, she opened up to her fellow campmates, Jacqueline Jossa and Nadine Coyle about her transition, by confessing she began gender reassignment in the ’80s.

When Jacqueline asked her about her surgeries, Caitlyn said she had a lot over the years.

“Over many many years I had a lot but then near the end, it went pretty quickly because you can do your face and your chest all at the same time which they did.”

“Actually for guys, if they go on oestrogen, they will grow boobs ok.”

“And in my case, I was on oestrogen back in the ’80s and had a little 36/38B and then I met Kris and had them chopped off,” she confessed to Jaqueline.

“I’m so confused in my life, and then the second time around 25 years later I was back on oestrogen obviously and they [breasts] came back some but not much.”

Caitlyn said that she had her breasts enhanced once they grew back.

“So I really had to have them done so it is kind of past the point of no return.”
