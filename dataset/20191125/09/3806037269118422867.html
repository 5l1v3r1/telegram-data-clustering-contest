Gucci's Dublin Sneakers Get a Cozy Seasonal "Beige Wool GG" Makeover

Fresh from its Cruise 2020 runway.

25 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Eric Brain

Gucci has released a pair of Dublin Sneakers in a “Beige Wool GG” makeover from its Cruise 2020 runway. The slip-on silhouette is made from brushed wool and features buffed leather trims around the vamp and heel, along with elasticated gussets in black on the sides.

Gucci’s iconic “GG” logo is repeated across the sneaker’s entire upper, and is finished with embossed branding on the heel, a grosgrain pull-loop heel tab, and a rubber sole unit that lifts the sneaker by 5mm.

Take a look at the Gucci Dublin Sneaker in “Beige Wool GG” in the gallery above, and pick up a pair via SSENSE now for $580 USD.
