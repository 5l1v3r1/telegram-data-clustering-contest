‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’ set for a minor leap

25 Nov 2019, 09:44 by BizAsia Correspondent

Rajan Shahi’s popular series ‘Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke’, the sister show of ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai’, is set to undergo a major change, with the introduction of a leap.

According to sources, the show will be going for a few month’s time leap, with the look of every character set for a new look. The youth is loving Abir and Misthi (Shaheer Sheikh and Rhea Sharma) and Kunal and Kuhu (Ritvik Arora and Kaveri Priyam) and they are sure to love them more in their new avatar.

Producer Rajan Shahi has given a fresh look and perspective to the show. Not only will there be a short leap, but with the addition of actor Vatsal Seth’s character, the dynamics of interpersonal relationships are sure to change.

Now we need to wait and watch what will happen next!
