Chiefs Fall, Sundowns Book Final Spot, 10 Images Of TKO Action

25 Nov 2019, 09:20 by Soccer Laduma

The stage has been set for the upcoming 2019 Telkom Knockout final and Soccer Laduma takes a look at some images of the past weekend in action.

Mamelodi Sundowns were the first team to book their spot in the Telkom final after a 2-1 victory over Golden Arrows at the Sugar Ray Xulu stadium on Saturday afternoon.

Read more: City Confirm Progress Of One More Midfielder

On Sunday, Kaizer Chiefs were made to eat some humble pie, as their unbeaten run came to an end this season, following a 2-1 loss to Maritzburg United at the Mbombela Stadium.

Now, Maritzburg will play hosts to Sundowns on December 14, when the two sides lock horns at the Moses Mabhida Stadium in the final of the competition.

This will be Maritzburg’s first Telkom Knockout final, while Sundowns have last won the competition in the 2015/16 campaign.
