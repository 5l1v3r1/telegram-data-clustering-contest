Hideo Kojima hints next game could be ‘the scariest horror game’

25 Nov 2019, 09:46

The follow-up to Death Stranding could end up being a horror game in the vein of P.T. and Silent Hills, according to a new hint form Kojima.

It’s very common that when a developer leaves a larger company to set up their own studio they end up making something very similar to the games they’re already known for. But while Death Stranding did have a similar open world to Metal Gear Solid 5 the two games don’t really have much else in common.

But the game Hideo Kojima was working on before he left Konami wasn’t anything to do with Metal Gear, but was instead horror game revival Silent Hills, which got as far as the infamous P.T. demo.

He was even working on it with Death Stranding stars Norman Reedus and Guillermo del Toro, and now it seems his thoughts may be returning to making a new survival horror game of his own.
