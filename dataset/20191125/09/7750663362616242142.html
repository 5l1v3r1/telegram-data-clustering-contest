Paris Saint-Germain 'to prioritise new Kylian Mbappe deal'

Paris Saint-Germain have allegedly prioritised attempting to agree terms over a new contract with Kylian Mbappe.

25 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Darren Plant

While the forward still has over two-and-a-half years remaining on his existing deal, interest in the 20-year-old's signature means that PSG feel the need to speed up negotiations.

According to L'Equipe, sporting director Leonardo is focused on trying to reach a positive conclusion in discussions with the France international.

Given his desire to take on a new challenge, there have been previous claims that the World Cup winner intends to reject any offers from the Ligue 1 leaders.

Real Madrid are currently regarded as the favourites to sign Mbappe, while Liverpool and Juventus have also been linked with a big-money approach for his signature.
