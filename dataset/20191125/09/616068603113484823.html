Heavy snow hitting Hwy 3

25 Nov 2019, 09:35 by Colin Dacre

Heavy snow is falling on Highway 3 in Manning Park, with Environment Canada issuing a snowfall warning for the area Monday morning.

A total of 15 to 25 centimetres is expected to fall on the Allison Pass as several bands of moisture move over the area.

“Between these bands the snow will taper off. Periods of heavy snow are expected on and off through today and tonight,” the weather alert states. 

As of 8 a.m., nine centimetres of snow had already fallen at Allison Pass. 

“Prepare for quickly changing and deteriorating travel conditions,” Environment Canada says. “Rapidly accumulating snow could make travel difficult over some locations.
