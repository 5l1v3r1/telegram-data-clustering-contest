Water playground to open in time for summer

25 Nov 2019, 09:14 by Staff Writer

THE dinosaur-themed water playground in Madeley is set to open to the public this weekend.

The City of Wanneroo has confirmed the Kingsway Aquatic Play Space will open to the public from 2pm on November 30, encouraging people to bring swimming

There will be a van onsite selling coffee and ice-cream for the official opening.

With temperatures expected to be 30 degrees and above this weekend, the long-anticipated facility could provide cool relief to families this summer.

A Health Department requirement to install a shower delayed the original opening date.
