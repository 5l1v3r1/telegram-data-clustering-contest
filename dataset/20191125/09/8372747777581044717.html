12-year-old in critical condition after ATV crash in Collier County

25 Nov 2019, 09:23 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- A 12-year-old girl is in critical condition following an ATV accident in Collier County.

Florida Highway Patrol says it happened on Platt Road near Corkscrew Lane on Sunday afternoon.

They say that 12-year-old Kaeli McCormick was riding the ATV when it suddenly flipped and landed on top of her.

She's in Lee Memorial Hospital with critical injuries.
