iPhone XR price

You can choose between 64GB and 128GB of built-in storage, priced as follows, if you buy directly from Apple:

  • 64GB: £629/$599 (Was previously £749/$749)
  • 128GB: £679/$649 (Was previously £799/$799)

With the 128GB iPhone XR just costing a few pounds (or dollars) more than the 64GB model we'd suggest that you choose the model with greater capacity.

Keep these prices in mind when looking at deals because some third-party resellers may quote the price from before Apple's discount was applied.
