Harry Potter’s Dudley Dursley actor surprises fans with His Dark Materials role

Harry Melling, 30, is starring on His Dark Materials and The War Of The Worlds which are both airing on TV now

25 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Irishmirror.ie

Harry Potter’s Dudley Dursley actor shocked fans with His Dark Materials role.

Harry Melling, 30, has had a surge of acting work as he stars on His Dark Materials and The War Of The Worlds - and fans sure didn't miss him.

Both hit TV shows are airing concurrently on our television screens at the moment.

Social media was awash with eagle-eyed Harry Potter fans spotting the actor in both television series.

"Feeling like IMDB when spotting Dudley Dursley in both ‘His Dark Materials’ AND ‘The War of the Worlds’."

His role in His Dark Materials has similarities with the part which made him famous in the Harry Potter saga.

Harry plays Sysselman in the North who could turn out to be as annoying as Harry Potter's cousin Dudley.

His Dark Materials is another epic fantasy based on a book series which sees two children go on a spell-binding adventure.

Also based on the book of the same name, The War Of The Worlds tells the narrative of martians who wage war against the human race.
