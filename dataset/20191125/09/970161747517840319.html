Louis Walsh addresses on/off friendship with Simon Cowell

25 Nov 2019, 09:58

X Factor: Celebrity’s Louis Walsh has addressed his rollercoaster friendship with Simon Cowell ahead of the show’s hotly-anticipated final on Saturday.

Joined by his only remaining act Megan McKenna on Lorraine, the infamous judge explained that he often falls out with the music mogul in public, but right now they’re in a good place.

‘Your relationship [with Simon Cowell] intrigues me,’ began Lorraine. ‘You’re like best pals and you fall out and you fall back in again…’

‘Yeah,’ Louis agreed. ‘We do it in public. He’s been really good to me. He’s a great, great person and he’s got his mojo back, big time.’

Louis, who recently revealed Simon ‘paid him off’ to leave X Factor, then pointed at Megan and added: ‘And he loves this one.’

‘And I love Simon,’ Megan enthused. ‘He’s a really nice guy.’

‘He can make or break people, he really can,’ Louis continued. ‘He usually makes them, if he likes them.’

Last month, the 67-year-old presenter claimed that Simon paid him to leave the X Factor judging panel in 2012.

Speaking to Daily Star Online, Louis explained: ‘I was paid off if you really want to know the truth.

‘I’m back, listen, you would take it if you were offered it… it was a lot.’

Louis also claimed that Simon ‘needs’ him on the X Factor: Celebrity judging panel.

‘Simon has tried this before and it didn’t work but this feels like a completely new show and he may not want to admit it but deep down he knew he needed me back,’ the star said.

‘He always realises in the end. I think he doesn’t like the fact I’ve had less plastic surgery than him, and me being on the panel is a constant reminder.’

Louis has returned to the small-screen for X Factor: Celebrity along with Simon and Nicole Scherzinger.

The final set to air on Saturday with Megan, Max and Harvey, The Chase’s Jenny Ryan and V5 battling it out for a recording contract.

X Factor: Celebrity final airs on Saturday at 7.10pm on ITV.
