Kendall Jenner's pals and family beg her to have babies with rumoured boyfriend

Kendall Jenner maintains she is just good friends with Fai Khadra, but asked her Instagram followers if they should have babies together

25 Nov 2019, 09:48 by Irishmirror.ie

Kendall Jenner is the only one in the Kardashian/Jenner brood without any children, but according to her latest Instagram post, she's got baby fever.

She posted photos with her two cute nephews, Kim Kardashian's sons Saint, three, and Psalm, six months, who she shares with husband Kanye West .

She posed with model and musician Fai Khadra, who she has taken as her date to events including Justin and Hailey Bieber, but maintains they are just friends.

Fai, 28, has accompanied Kendall to lots of glitzy events, but she has maintained it's a platonic relationship, telling a fan: "He's my date but we don't date".

However, in her Instagram caption, she asked her 119 million followers whether they thought she should have babies with Fai.

"Everyone in favor of Fai and i starting a family say 'I'", she penned.

Her sister Kylie, who has one-year-old Stormi with ex Travis Scott, replied "I".

Excited sister Kim replied with a series of exclamation marks, while her mum Kris Jenner wrote "I" alongside a love heart emoji.

Kendall's pal Hailey Bieber was beside herself, writing "IIIIII" "MEEEEE" "I SAY YES" "I DOOO" in a series of comments.

It seems Fai is also game, as he replied with the family emoji.

Meanwhile fans of Kendall responded in droves begging her to have babies with Fai.

Kylie's sister Khloe Kardashian has one-year-old True Thompson with ex Tristan Thompson.

Kourtney Kardashian has Mason, nine, Penelope, seven, and Reign, four, with ex Scott Disick.

And their brother Rob Kardashian has three-year-old Dream with ex Blac Chyna.

Notably, none of the Kardashians have been supporting their step parent Caitlyn Jenner, who was married to Kris until 2015.
