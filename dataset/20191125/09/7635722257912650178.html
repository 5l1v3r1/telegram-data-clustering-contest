Spanish police intercept submarine with 3,000 kilos of cocaine

25 Nov 2019, 09:33 by Jack Quann

Police in Spain have arrested two people after intercepting a submarine off its north-west coast.

The submersible contained around 3, 000 kilos of cocaine, Spanish state broadcaster TVE reports.

The submarine had come from a country in Latin America and arrived in Spain on Sunday.

A third person who was on-board is reportedly on the run.

Authorities are trying to re-float the submarine, which is 20 meters long.

Investigators are also trying to clarify whether the submarine had sailed from South America to Spain with the drugs - or whether they picked up the cargo en-route.
