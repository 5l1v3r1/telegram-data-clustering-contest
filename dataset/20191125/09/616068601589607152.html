Urban to play Rockin' River

25 Nov 2019, 09:47

Country superstar Keith Urban will headline this summer's Rockin' River Music Fest in Merritt.

Live Nation announced the lineup for the 2020 outdoor festival this morning. Along with Urban, other heavy hitters in the country world include Jon Pardi, Chris Janson, and Travis Tritt.

The annual festival is set for July 30 to Aug. 2 along the banks of the Coldwater River.
 
It will mark the first appearance at the event for Urban, a Grammy Award winning star whose popularity has crossed over into the pop realm. Urban will be joined by two-steppin’ radio chart topper Jon Pardi, "red-hot fireball of a performer" Chris Janson, and member of the world’s famous Grand Ole Opry, Travis Tritt.

Also on the bill are Randy Houser, Chase Rice, Lauren Alaina, and more.
 
In total, more than 30 acts will perform across multiple stages.

Camping and festival passes go on sale at 10 a.m. this Friday at www.rockinriverfest.com/tickets.

General admission weekend passes start at $149 plus service charges. Weekend camping starts at $79, and weekend Party Zone passes start at $299. Early bird pricing ends Jan. 6.
