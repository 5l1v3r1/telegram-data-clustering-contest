ROAD HORROR

Serious smash involving lorry locks down A74(M) near Lockerbie for FOUR HOURS

25 Nov 2019, 09:48 by Blair Meikle

A MAJOR Scots road has been locked down for FOUR HOURS after a serious smash involving a lorry.

Emergency services raced to the scene at junction 18 on the A74(M), near Lockerbie, at around 5.50am this morning.

The northbound carriageway remains closed and diversions are in place.

It's not clear if anyone has been injured in the crash.

A spokesman for Police Scotland said: "We were called at 5.50am to reports of a road crash involving one lorry on the M74 northbound near junction 18, Lockerbie.

"Officers are at the scene and the road is closed northbound.

"Diversions are in place."
