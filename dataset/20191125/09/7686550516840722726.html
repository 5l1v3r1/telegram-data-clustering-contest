The best Amazon Black Friday sales starting today

Amazon has launched more Black Friday sales with just five days to go until the biggest shopping day of the year

25 Nov 2019, 09:46 by Stephen Hurrell

Amazon has launched big Black Friday discounts with just five days to go until the biggest shopping day of the year.

The online retailer's Black Friday sale has been running for three days already and each day a host of new discounts are revealed, with the biggest savings set to take place on Black Friday itself on 29 November.

On Monday the retailer cut the price of a number of items including ghd Orginal Ceramic Hair Straighteners, which have been reduced from £109 to £81.99.

Amazon has also brought back one of the most popular deals of last week by cutting the price of Apple Air Pods from £159.99 to just £129.99.

As usual on Black Friday it has also reduced much of its own technology including the Amazon Echo Dot. The voice-controlled smart home device is now just £22, down from £50. The Amazon Echo Show 8 is down from £119.99 to £59.99 and the Amazon Fire TV Stick 4K is now £29.99.

The other highlights from the Black Friday sale include:

Amazon Music Unlimited - 99p

Over 50 million songs for four months at just 99p. This deal is designed to compete with Spotify and at a fraction of the price it is a tempting one.

Sony Wireless Bluetooth Noise Cancelling headphones - £79

Down from £100, the Sony noise-cancelling headphones are reduced by £20. The headphones have Amazon Alexa built in to connect to your other Alexa devices.

Yankee Candle Large Jar Scented Candle - £21.05

The Shea Butter Yankee Candle has been reduced from £23.99 to £21.05. Only a small discount but it represents good value for Amazon customers.

Talisker 10 year old single malt whisky - £31

Whisky fans will know Talisker is one of the best around. The Isle of Skye whisky rarely falls below £30 but a reduction of £13 to just £31 represents good value.

XBox Game Pass Ultimate - £16.49

You can get three months of XBox Live Gold and over 100 games to play over multiplayer using the XBox Game Pass deal. This should be a popular one with gamers.
