EXCL: Infinite Crisis Shows How Blue Beetle Survived in the Dark Multiverse

25 Nov 2019, 09:11 by L.D. Nolan

The original Infinite Crisis saw Ted Kord/Blue Beetle -- in one of the darkest moments in DC history -- murdered by Max Lord. However, Tales from the Dark Multiverse -- a series of one-shots set in the titular place first introduced in Dark Nights: Metal -- imagines a world in which Blue Beetle survived.

DC has given CBR an exclusive preview of Tales from the Dark Multiverse: Infinite Crisis #1 in which Kord and Lord face-off. In the original, Blue Beetle refused to even entertain the notion of joining Lord and Checkmate. However, in this new retelling, Beetle pretends to join Lord, though a previous first look revealed that the hero goes on to turn the tables and kill his attempted murderer.

You can see CBR's exclusive preview of Infinite Crisis #1 below along with solicitation information for the book.
