Girl, 2, dies at Christmas market after being crushed by falling ice sculpture

The youngster died while visiting the Place Guillaume in Luxembourg City this afternoon after the sculpture fell and broke into pieces

25 Nov 2019, 09:36 by Neil Murphy

A young child has died after being crushed by an ice sculpture at a Christmas market in Luxembourg.

The two-year-old girl was struck while visiting the Place Guillaume at about 8pm yesterday.

Eyewitnesses say the ice sculpture collapsed near an ice rink and shattered into pieces, fatally injuring the child.

It is understood that the toddler died at the scene despite paramedics' desperate efforts to resuscitate her at the scene.

Place Guillaume II was evacuated and closed off in the aftermath of the tragedy.

Detectives have launched an investigation  and are have asked eyewitness to come forward with more information.

Luxembourg's Prime Minister - Xavier Bettel  - wrote on Twitter that 'words cannot describe how tragic the incident was'.
