Hotunan bikin daurin auren matashin da ya auri yan mata uku rana daya

25 Nov 2019, 12:32

A makon da ya gabata, mun kawo muku rahoton wani matasahi mai suna, Osman Hafiz, wanda ke shirin auren yan mata uku a lokaci daya ranar 23 ga Nuwamba, 2019.

A karshen makon da ya gabata, an daurawa Hafiz aure da amarensa, Yidana Sikena, Ali Karima da Yakubu Huzaima a garin Walewale, Arewa maso gabashin kasar Ghana.
