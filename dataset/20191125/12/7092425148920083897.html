Gardaí investigating after body found in north Dublin

25 Nov 2019, 12:07 by James Cox

Gardaí are investigating after the body of a man was found in north Dublin.

The body was discovered in the Balbutcher Lane area of Ballymun at around 11am this morning.

A Garda spokeswoman said: "Gardaí are currently at the scene of an incident at the Balbutcher Lane area of Ballymun. A scene is currently in place. Further updates will issue."
