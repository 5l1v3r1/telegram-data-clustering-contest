Palace Ultimo 2019 Tracksuits

Filled with Winter-ready fabrics & cozy fits.

25 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Jack Stanley

Palace has revealed its full range of tracksuits for the Ultimo 2019 collection, with weather-ready items coming in GORE-TEX and Polartec featuring in the range. The stand-out tracksuit in the capsule is the Polartec Lazer set, which is releasing in a range of colors spanning from muted “Cobalt Blue” to the more eye-catching “Hi Vis Yellow” and “Hi Vis Pink.” Additionally, the set will release in three different camouflage options, referencing a theme that runs throughout the collection.

Other stand-outs include a full GORE-TEX tracksuit in a different camouflage pattern, as well as the “PALLAS” shell suit and the “Catch Up” tracksuit which comes in four different three color designs.

The first Ultimo 2019 pieces will arrive in store and online on November 29. For more from the collection, make sure to check out the range in the following categories -

- Lookbook
- Outerwear
- Hoodies & Sweatshirts
- Tees
- Hats
- Accessories & Hardware
