Selena Gomez Was Sizzling in a Sheer Versace Slip Dress at the AMAs

25 Nov 2019, 12:10 by Kara Kia

After a two-year hiatus from performing, Selena Gomez gave us a glamorous fashion show at the 2019 American Music Awards that was definitely worth the wait. Gomez started the night by rocking a lime green Versace minidress on the red carpet of the Microsoft Theater. Then, she headed to the stage in an elegant black gown to sing "Lose You to Love Me" before quickly changing into a crystal bodysuit to perform "Look at Her Now." After her performance, Gomez rejoined the audience in another green Versace dress â€” but this one gave us a very sexy lesson in layering for the festive season.

For her boudoir-inspired fourth look of the night, Gomez's Versace Fall/Winter 2019 slip dress featured an empire-waist design with a silk chartreuse bust and sheer, embroidered green lace from the waist down. The partially see-through dress was layered over orange lace tights, which gave the look an avant-garde lingerie vibe. Gomez completed the look with highlighter-green satin Versace pointed toe pumps, a wavy, wet-look lob, and black graphic winged liner.
