Vicky Kaushal & Katrina Kaif set for New Year’s vacation?

25 Nov 2019, 12:30 by BizAsia Correspondent

Rumours about Vicky Kaushal and Katrina Kaif have been doing for a number of months. While the rumoured couple are yet to comment on their status, it seems they’re allegedly planning a New Year’s getaway in December.

According to Mumbai Mirror, Kaushal and Kaif are planning to spend New Year’s Eve together in an undisclosed destination.

Previously, it was reported that the pair have been approached by filmmaker Rajkumar Santoshi for a new project. However, it seems they are yet to give a nod to the script.
