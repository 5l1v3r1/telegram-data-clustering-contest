Naira Marley is the biggest artiste in Nigeria – Ycee

25 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Sanya Agunbiade

Nigerian Rapper, Oludemilade Martin Alejo, popularly referred to as Ycee has stated that Naira Marley is currently the biggest artiste in Nigeria.

In a post he made on Twitter, Ycee mentioned that even though many people in the music industry know this, it will be hard for them to acknowledge such.

Ycee tweeted;

“Hope say una know say Naira Marley na the biggest Naija artiste, but based on ‘industry’ una go wan deny”.

Naira Marley recently reportedly sold out a near 5,000 capacity arena in just 3 minutes. This was according to an announcement made by popular London based OAP, Adesope Olajide popularly known as ShopsyDoo via his Instagram page.
