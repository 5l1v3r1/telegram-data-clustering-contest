Spoilers: Fiz gets rid of Jade in Corrie - but pays the price

25 Nov 2019, 12:25

Jade Rowan (Lottie Henshall) has had Coronation Street viewers on edge since she arrived in Fiz Stape’s (Jennie McAlpine) household – from the off, it was clear that she was up to something sinister and had ulterior motives for offering to be Hope’s tutor and nanny.

She will soon get bad news from Fiz, which won’t go down too well – brand new pictures revealed by ITV’s preview site show the moment that Fiz tells Jade that her services are no longer required. But you can be that this isn’t the last that she and Tyrone Dobbs (Alan Halsall) will hear from her.

The picture captions tease: ‘Evelyn quizzes Jade about her new job but she remains tightlipped, and shows an apparent lack of emotion when saying goodbye to Fiz. Is this the last we’ll see of Jade?’

Well, in short, not a chance!

Jade has got her feet under the table and tonight, viewers will be no doubt suspecting foul play when a fire spreads in the back yard of the house. When Hope is found staring at the flames hungrily, Fiz realises her pyromaniac youngster must have been behind it – even though Jack Webster says that the fire had already started when they arrived.
