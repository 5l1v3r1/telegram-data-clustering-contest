The Top Goalscorers This Season So Far

25 Nov 2019, 12:28 by David Kappel

Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi's combined league goals this season are less than what the current leading scorer in Europe has, while ex-Manchester United striker Romelu Lukaku has climbed in the top 10.

The two superstars were once again unable to add to their tallies on the weekend, with Messi staying on eight strikes in LaLiga after failing to score in FC Barcelona's 2-1 win at Leganes on Saturday, while Ronaldo did not have a chance to score his sixth Serie A goal in 2019/20 after sitting on the sidelines for Juventus' 3-1 victory over Atalanta.

Bayern Munich striker Robert Lewandowski has stayed at the top of the ranking despite failing to score in a Bundesliga game for the first time in this campaign. He remained on 16 strikes after drawing a blank in his side's 4-0 win over Fortuna Dusseldorf on Saturday.

As a consequence, Lazio's Ciro Immobile used the chance to make up ground on the Poland forward, netting his 15th strike in Serie A on the weekend.

Leicester City's Jamie Vardy and Red Bull Leipzig's Timo Werner remained in joint-third spot after adding their 12th goals of the campaign.
