Saints' Cam Jordan appears to strike Panthers' Kyle Allen in stomach, explains actions

25 Nov 2019, 12:33 by Ryan Gaydos

New Orleans Saints defensive end Cam Jordan took some heat Sunday against the Carolina Panthers when he appeared to strike quarterback Kyle Allen after a play had been blown dead.

The play occurred in the second quarter. Allen had been sacked by Saints linebacker Demario Davis when the referee blew the play dead -- just as it appeared Jordan had punched Allen in the stomach. Davis was celebrating as if he completed the sack, but the Saints were penalized for Jordan’s action.
