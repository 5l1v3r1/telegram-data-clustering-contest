'Our Systems Are up Now', Tweets IndiGo After Fixing Major Network Issue

IndiGo, one of the largest carrier flights in India, on Monday resolved an issue affecting all its systems across the network.

25 Nov 2019, 12:06

New Delhi: India’s largest carrier airline IndiGo on Monday solved a major network issue affecting all its systems. “Our systems are up now. Thanks for your understanding and patience,” tweeted IndiGo after resolving the technical glitch on November 25.

Earlier in the day, IndiGo tweeted, “#6ETravelAdvisory : Our systems are down across the network. We are expecting the counters to be crowded more than usual. Please bear with us as we try to solve the issue asap. For assistance, contact us on Twitter/Facebook or chat with us at https://bit.ly/2Gp7SIF.”
