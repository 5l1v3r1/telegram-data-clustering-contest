WWE Smackdown Responds to Survivor Series Rout with Infinity War Meme

25 Nov 2019, 12:11 by Steve Russell

The official WWE on FOX Twitter account responded to a critical SmackDown loss during Sunday night's Survivor Series pay-per-view, referencing Peter Parker's poignant farewell during Avengers: Infinity War. 

The tweet shared the brand scores at that time, with NXT leading the charge at three and both Raw and SmackDown tied at one, along with the caption, "Mr. McMahon, we don't feel so good." The image showcased NXT Superstar Roderick Strong, his NXT North American Championship slung over his shoulder. However, his opponents, Raw's AJ Styles and SmackDown's Shinsuke Nakamura, are nowhere to be found, having faded to dust a la Parker's emotional goodbye to Tony Stark.
