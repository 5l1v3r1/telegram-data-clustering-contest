Wolves U23s game now taking place at England's training base

Wolves Under-23s' Premier League International Cup clash with Athletic Bilbao on Wednesday has been moved to England's St George's Park training base.

25 Nov 2019, 12:17 by Joe Edwards

The game was supposed to be taking place at the New Bucks Head, home of AFC Telford United, but has been changed because of problems with the pitch.

It will still kick off at 7pm, and 350 tickets have been made available priced at £4 for adults and £2 for children.

Mark Kennedy's side drew 1-1 against Paris Saint-Germain in the competition last week, in front of more than 5,000 fans at Molineux.
