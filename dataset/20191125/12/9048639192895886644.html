Ruston man arrested after Rayville man stabbed to death

25 Nov 2019, 12:00 by KNOE

RUSTON, La. (KNOE) - The Ruston Police Department says an arrest has been made in connection with a stabbing death.

According to police, Garrett Carreira was arrested in Richland Parish on a charge of second-degree murder.

Police say the investigation into the murder began on Sunday, Nov. 24, after Ruston PD assisted state police with a vehicle pursuit entering the city limits of Ruston on US Highway 80 East. After the vehicle stopped in a parking lot on East Georgia Avenue, it was discovered that one of the men inside had been stabbed. He was identified as 36-year-old Joshua Tibbs, of Rayville. Tibbs eventually succumbed to his injuries.

Police say Tibbs was stabbed in the 300 block of E California Avenue. Witnesses were interviewed and evidence was gathered. Police say their investigation led them to suspect Carreira of the crime. An arrest warrant was obtained for him on Nov. 25. Shortly thereafter, Carreira was arrested in Richland Parish. He will be extradited to Lincoln Parish to be booked into the Lincoln Parish Detention Center.

Police say the investigation is ongoing.
