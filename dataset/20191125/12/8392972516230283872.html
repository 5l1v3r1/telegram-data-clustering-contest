Dunelm selling teddy bear fleece onesie to keep you warm this winter

It comes in two colours - pink and charcoal

25 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Rebecca Carter

Dunelm is selling a teddy bear fleece onesie to keep you nice and toasty this Christmas.

The home furnishing store is offering the comfy all-in-one in pink and charcoal grey for £20.

According to Dunelm, the onesie is made from "Teddy fabric with a super soft feel for extra warmth and comfort".

The product description reads: "Perfect for keeping you warm and cosy, this Teddy Bear onesie comes in an on-trend and stylish blush pick colourway and makes for an ideal gift to give this Christmas.

"Crafted from Teddy fabric with a super soft feel for extra warmth and comfort, this machine washable onesie is perfect for a relaxing evening."

The onesie comes in two sizes - small/medium or large/extra large.
