What does TfL's ban mean for UberEats?

25 Nov 2019, 12:54

News of Uber’s licence being scrapped in the capital has left many hungry Londoners worrying about the future of their takeaways.

Transport for London announced on Monday that it has not renewed the taxi app company’s licence to operate in London, citing concerns over security breaches.

It found that Uber allowed unauthorised drivers to pick up passengers in at least 14,000 trips, after drivers uploaded photos of themselves to the app linked to cars which they were not registered to drive.

This meant that passengers were being picked up by drivers not named or authorised by the company, putting them at risk.

Following the news, many took to Twitter questioning whether the move would affect UberEats, the app’s food delivery service.
