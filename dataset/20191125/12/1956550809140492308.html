Dubai's Secret Parties to throw a special New Year’s Eve brunch

Ring in the New Year in Dubai at this massive Palm Jumeirah bash

25 Nov 2019, 12:56 by Reema Rahman

If spending New Year’s Eve in Dubai at a party brunch followed by a four-hour post-brunch bash sounds like your kind of night, read on.

Secret Parties (you know, the guys behind some of Dubai’s top party brunches) are throwing a special edition of the Secret Brunch at The Palace to help end the year in style.

The brunch will be thrown from 8pm until midnight at miX by Alain Ducasse at Palm Jumeirah’s palatial Emerald Palace Kempinski Dubai.

Expect a four-course dinner, music from international DJ Chris Wright, a live band starring a singer, a saxophonist and a drummer performing hit tunes, break dancing performances and a magician. Packages start at Dhs700 for soft drinks.

Brunchgoers will be seated on the outside terraces so you’ll be able to catch all the firework action from Jumeirah Beach Residence, Palm Jumeirah, Burj Al Arab and even the Burj Khalifa.

The night continues with an after-party until 4am and entry comes in at Dhs200, which includes two house drinks and if you’re looking to flash the cash, table packages are available from Dhs2,500.
