Ex-Livingston and Rangers keeper Liam Kelly battling injury setback at QPR

Scotland squad member hopes to return in time for the festive fixtures.

25 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Fraser Wilson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/fraser-wilson/

Liam Kelly is facing another fortnight on the sidelines at QPR after suffering a thigh tear in training.

The 23-year-old former Rangers and Livingston keeper had cemented his place in the Loftus Road first team with a string of impressive displays when he was struck down a day before the Championship draw with Middlesbrough.

Kelly had been in contention for a return to the Scotland squad for the Euro 2020 qualifiers against Cyprus and Kazakhstan.
