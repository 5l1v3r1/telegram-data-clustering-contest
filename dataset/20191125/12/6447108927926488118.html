Black Friday Deals – Get a PS4 Pro or Xbox One X bundle for £299.99

25 Nov 2019, 12:39 by Stefan L

Black Friday sales and offers are well and truly underway, and today is seeing Sony and Microsoft slashing the RRP of their premium 4K capable consoles, the PlayStation 4 Pro and Xbox One X. Whichever platform you’re looking at, you’ll be able to pick one up for £299.99 with a game bundled in.

That obviously means that Microsoft have cut their prices much harder than Sony. The PS4 Pro’s regular RRP is £349.99, while the more powerful Xbox One X regular price is £449.99.

We’re still waiting to see just what Sony and Microsoft are going to do with the base model consoles, the PlayStation 4 Slim and Xbox One S – US pricing has already been confirmed by both – but adverts for the Xbox One S All-Digital has pinned the disc-less console at a mere £129.99 for this Friday.
