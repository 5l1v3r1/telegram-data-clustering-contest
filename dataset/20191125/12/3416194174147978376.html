'Duck Dynasty' Star Sadie Robertson Ties the Knot With Christian Huff

25 Nov 2019, 12:39

Sadie Robertson is a married woman!

The Duck Dynasty star and former Dancing with the Stars contestant and Christian Huff said “I do” in front of friends and family on Monday (November 25) in Sadie‘s parents’ house in West Monroe, La., The News-Star reports.

Sadie wore a wedding dress from Kleinfeld Bridal before changing into a more comfortable dress by Sherri Hill for the reception, E! News reports.

“I have been looking forward to the dance party at my wedding for my whole life,” Sadie told the outlet. “I got a reception dress so I can do my dancing, it’s really fun and pretty and has more details than my wedding dress.”

Sadie‘s sisters were reportedly her bridesmaids, and her brothers were some of the groomsmen.

Celebrity friends in attendance included Touched by an Angel‘s Roma Downey, her husband Mark Burnett, and Alfonso Ribeiro.

Sadie Robertson also told the outlet ahead of the event that the wedding was at night “because we want to have Christmas lights everywhere, it’s going to be very magical.”

Congrats to the happy newlyweds!
