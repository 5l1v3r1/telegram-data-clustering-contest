Sara Ali Khan and Karan Johar meet up in New York

25 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Filmfare

Sara Ali Khan is currently busy holidaying in New York and she is surely having the time of her life. The actress took to her Instagram to share pictures from her trip to NYC and they look mesmerising. Karan Johar also headed to New York recently for work and Sara and Karan caught up with each other in NY and even clicked a cool selfie. Karan posted the selfie which was later also shared by Sara Ali Khan. 

Meanwhile, on the work front, Karan Johar is all set to direct his multi-starrer Takht which will go on floors next year, while Sara Ali Khan will return to shoot Coolie no. 1 with Varun Dhawan soon.
