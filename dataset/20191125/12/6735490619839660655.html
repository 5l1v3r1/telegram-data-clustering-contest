Wendy Williams Slams Taylor Swift’s Artist Of The Decade Title At The AMAs

25 Nov 2019, 12:25 by Corey Atad

Taylor Swift had a huge night at the 2019 AMAs on Sunday, but not everyone was here for it.

On Monday, Wendy Williams took some time on her show to called out the singer for winning Artist of the Decade and breaking the record for most AMA wins.

“Taylor Swift made history, which is, like, unbelievable to me,” Williams said, according to toofab.

“And so, good for her. She won six awards, now she has a total of 29 awards,” she continued. “That sounds unbelievable, but what sounds unforgivable, she’s got more awards than Michael Jackson!”

Williams added, “And Artist of the Decade?! Is she who’s been firing you up for the last 10 years?!”

The TV host then lamented the general state of public taste.

“But you know what? I think that taste has cheapened through the years in a lot of things,” she said. “The quality of clothes we buy, they get tattered and torn real quick. The quality of food we buy, we’re being poisoned by a lot of crap. Just life in general I think has cheapened and I don’t mean this in a bad way to Taylor, but it’s unbelievable … I appreciate her, but Artist of the Decade?”
