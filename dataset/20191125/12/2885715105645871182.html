Viral video: 107-year-old mother gives candy to 87-year-old daughter

The 107-year old woman had reportedly attended the wedding reception, where she had got some candy, and she decided to give one to her daughter.

25 Nov 2019, 12:37 by Trends Desk

A video of a 107-year-old handing over a piece of candy to her 87-year-old-daughter, who is visibly happy to receive it, is going viral on several social media platforms.

In the video, the mother can be fishing out candy from her jacket pocket which she then gives her daughter. The delighted daughter smiles at the camera on receiving the candy.

The video was shared by the official Twitter handle of Chinese media outlet People’s Daily, who described the 87-year-old as “the happiest child in the world!”
