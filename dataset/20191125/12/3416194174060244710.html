Lauren Alaina Line Dances For Her Freestyle DWTS Season 28 Finale - Watch Here!

25 Nov 2019, 12:20

Lauren Alaina is a complete winner in our eyes after her freestyle performance with partner Gleb Savchenko on the Dancing with the Stars season 28 finale tonight (November 25)!

The 24-year-old singer and Gleb hit the dance floor for the final night of the season, and performed to “Country Girl” by Luke Bryan.

They earned 30 points. Their total is 57 points for the night.

“I feel great! I feel really excited,” Lauren shared earlier about her performance. “I was nervous. I didn’t know what we would do with the Freestyle, but it is literally the perfect dance for me. I’m like line dancing? Sure! Let’s do it. Bring it.”

Lauren added, “The dance is really, really good and showcases me perfectly. Regardless of what happens, we’re gonna have fun doing it and hopefully connect with a bunch of people. And I get to sing!”

This week, the live vote (via abc.com, the ABC app and SMS/text) will be combined with the judges’ scores and tabulated in real time, determining the “Dancing” champion. To help Lauren and Gleb win, text LAUREN to 21523!
