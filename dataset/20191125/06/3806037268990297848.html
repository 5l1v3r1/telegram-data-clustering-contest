Prada & adidas Officially Unveil Superstar & Bowling Bag Collaboration

Limited to 700 pieces.

25 Nov 2019, 06:57 by Nicolaus Li

Following a series of teasers, Prada and adidas have now come together to officially unveil their unprecedented collaboration. Centered around a clean “Optic White” base accented by black detailing, the collaborative effort from the Italian luxury fashion house and German sportswear label is led by a take on the classic Superstar footwear model.

Made in Italy by Prada, the sneaker features a simple premium full-grain leather construction with a rubber shell toe and matching rubber cupsole. Contrast co-branding appears on the tongue, alongside Prada’s recognizable logo placed above “MADE IN ITALY” debossing on the lateral side, edition number debossing on the medial side and Trefoil logos at the rear. Accompanying the shoe is an enlarged version of Prada’s bowling bag tote that matches the footwear and draws on the functionality of adidas gym bags.

The Prada x adidas Superstar and Bowling Bag Set will be limited to just 700 pieces and available December 4 on adidas.com and prada.com alongside a release at select Prada and adidas locations. Stay tuned for pricing details.
