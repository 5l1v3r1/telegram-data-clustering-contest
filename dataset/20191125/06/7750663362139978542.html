Monday's Liverpool transfer talk news roundup: Xherdan Shaqiri, Jadon Sancho, Rhian Brewster

The transfer window may be closed, but rumours and speculation continue to swirl as Liverpool eye possible January reinforcements.

25 Nov 2019, 06:50 by Andy Brent

Jurgen Klopp's title contenders were not particularly busy during the summer and are unlikely to make any major moves in January either, but that has not stopped the rumour mill from linking them with additions.

Here, Sports Mole rounds up the latest news on possible comings and goings from Anfield.

Monday morning's headlines:

Jadon Sancho 'feels like scapegoat at Borussia Dortmund'
Winger Jadon Sancho allegedly feels that he was made 'a scapegoat' after Borussia Dortmund's 4-0 defeat to Bayern Munich before the international break. Read more.

Aston Villa to rival Crystal Palace for Liverpool youngster Rhian Brewster?
Aston Villa are allegedly prepared to rival Crystal Palace in the race to secure a loan deal for Liverpool forward Rhian Brewster. Read more.

Crystal Palace to make loan move for Liverpool forward Rhian Brewster?
Crystal Palace reportedly make contact with Liverpool regarding the potential loan signing of striker Rhian Brewster. Read more.

Xherdan Shaqiri 'given assurances over Liverpool future'
Manager Jurgen Klopp suggests that Xherdan Shaqiri has a future at Liverpool, despite the winger's lack of game time during 2019. Read more.
