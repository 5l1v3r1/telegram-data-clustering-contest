Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly get sassy after fans brand I'm A Celeb trial impossible

Fuming fans had vented their frustrations on social media as they labelled the trial "impossible"

25 Nov 2019, 06:43 by Irishmirror.ie

Ant McPartlin and Dec Donnelly rubbished claims the I'm A Celeb trial was impossible.

Social media was in meltdown as viewers complained the Bushtucker Trial was too difficult for the campmates to complete in the time slot.

But the I'm A Celeb presenters weren't having any moaning as they shot down claims it was "unfair" in a sassy tweet.

The duo penned on their joint account: "The stand ins did it when we tested it."

They added a sassy emoji of a little man and the I'm A Celeb hashtag.

Fuming fans had vented their frustrations on social media as they labelled the trial "impossible" and "completely out of order".

"That last trial on I’m a celeb was IMPOSSIBLE #imacelebrity ," one fan wrote, leading the troops.

"Thought the last game on I'm a celeb was apart from being impossible in the time was unfair. Must have a sporting chance at these trials surely."
