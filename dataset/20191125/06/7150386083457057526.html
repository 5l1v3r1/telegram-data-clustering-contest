Supreme Court Reserves Order on Maharashtra Crisis, Final Judgement at 10:30 AM on Tuesday

While the Sena-Congress combine urged the SC to order a floor test either today itself or for Tuesday, the Maharashtra BJP, on the other hand, argued that the apex court could not direct the Governor and the reasonable time to carry out a floor test was seven days.

25 Nov 2019, 06:43

New Delhi: A three-judge bench of the Supreme Court on Monday reserved its final order on the ongoing political situation in Maharashtra and will deliver its verdict in the case at 10:30 AM on Tuesday.
