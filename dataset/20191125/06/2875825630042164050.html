American Music Awards full winners' list as Taylor Swift smashes Michael Jackson's record

The Bad Blood singer now has 29 awards to her name as she overtakes the King of Pop following a golden night in Los Angeles after getting her hands on six more gongs

25 Nov 2019, 06:20 by Irishmirror.ie

Taylor Swift cleaned up at the American Music Awards picking up no fewer than six gongs - overtaking icon Michael Jackson.

She is now the most decorated artist in AMA history on a night where she celebrated victory over her former record label who appeared to attempt to block her performing her classic hits.

The megastar now has 29 awards to her name, overtaking MJ's 24-award haul before the night was even half way through.

She claimed not only Artist of the Year and Artist of the Decade, but Favourite Pop/Rock Album for Lover and Favourite Female Artist as well.

You Need to Calm Down meanwhile won Favourite Music Video.

It was a great night too for her pal Halsey, her hit Without Me landing the Favourite Pop/Rock song accolade.

Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello's seductive song Senorita won Collaboration of the Year with Cardi B taking the Rap/Hip-Hop's Favourite Artist crown.

Teenager Billie Eilish continued a 12 months to remember, picking up the prize for New artist of the Year.

Favourite Female Artist (soul/R&B) went to Beyonce, with Post Malone grabbing the Favourite Rap/Hip-Hop Album.

The night's big winners:
Artist of the year - Taylor Swift
Artist of the decade - Taylor Swift
New artist of the year - Billie Eilish
Collaboration of the year - Shawn Mendes & Camila Cabello (Senorita)
Favourite alternative rock artist - Billie Eilish
Favourite pop/rock song - Halsey (Without Me)
Favourite rap/hip-hop song - Lil Nas X and Billy Ray Cyrus (Old Town Road)
Favourite pop/rock album - Taylor Swift (Lover)
Favourite rap/hip-hop album - Post Malone (Hollywood's Bleeding)
Favourite country song - Dan + Shay (Speechless)
Favourite country album - Carrie Underwood (Cry Pretty)
Favourite female artist (country) - Carrie Underwood
Favourite duo or group (country) - Dan + Shay
Favourite duo or group (pop/rock) - BTS
Tour of the year - BTS
Favourite music video - Taylor Swift (You Need to Calm Down)
Favorited social artist - BTS
Favourite male artist (pop/rock) - Khalid
Favourite female artist (pop/rock) - Taylor Swift
Favourite male artist (country) - Kane Brown
Favourite artist (rap/Hip-Hop) - Cardi B
Favourite male artist (soul/R&B) - Bruno Mars
Favourite female artist (soul/R&B) - Beyonce
Favourite album (soul/R&B) - Khalid (Free Spirit)
Favourite song (soul/R&B) - Khalid (Talk)
Favourite artist (adult contemporary) - (Taylor Swift)
Favourite artist (Latin) - J Balvin
Favourite soundtrack - Bohemian Rhapsody (Queen)
