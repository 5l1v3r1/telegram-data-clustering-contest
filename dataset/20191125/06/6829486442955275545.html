David Leyonhjelm Forced To Pay $120,000 To Senator Sarah Hanson-Young Over Defamatory Comments

25 Nov 2019, 06:55 by Rachael Conaghan

During his time in parliament, David Leyonhjelm seemed to revel in saying as many outrageous, offensive things as possible.

Now he’s being forced to put his money where his mouth is, after losing his defamation case against Greens Senator Hanson-Young.

He has been ordered to pay $120,000 for sexist, defamatory comments he made about her last year in parliament, and in subsequent interviews.
