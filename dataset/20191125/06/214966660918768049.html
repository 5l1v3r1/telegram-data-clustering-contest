SEQ dam levels are dropping.

We can’t count on the rain to top them up, so Seqwater is counting on us to start saving water.

Share your best tip for saving water with us, and every week from next week for six weeks we’ll share the best tip we come across and the tipster will win a Super Wash from Hoppy’s Handwash Cafe!

Hoppy’s water usage rates are more economical than all other wash types. They also recycle and reuse water and harvest rainwater.

When it comes to our water, everyone and every drop counts!

SUBMIT A TIP NOW

Seqwater is counting on you to start saving water.

How? By taking shorter showers, not leaving the tap running and only using your toilet’s half-flush, when possible.

When it comes to our water, everyone and every drop counts. Check out www.seqwater.com.au/water-wise for more water-saving tips.
