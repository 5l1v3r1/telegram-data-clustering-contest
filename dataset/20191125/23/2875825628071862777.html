The former glamour model teased an insight into her new two-part special show as she shared the explosive trailer with Kris Boyson addressing recent cheating scandal

25 Nov 2019, 23:32 by Irishmirror.ie

Katie Price has shared the first look at her upcoming two-part special reality show My Crazy Life.
