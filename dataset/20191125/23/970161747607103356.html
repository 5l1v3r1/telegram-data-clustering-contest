Spoilers: Paul shares his abuse trauma in Corrie after row with Sean

25 Nov 2019, 23:58

Paul Foreman (Peter Ash) has had an incredibly difficult couple of months in Coronation Street, as — after Billy Mayhew (Daniel Brocklebank) questioned him about his past — he came to the horrifying realisation that Kel (Joe Alessi) had sexually abused him when he was a teenager. With him having revealed the truth about what happened to him to both Gemma and Bernie Winter (Dolly-Rose Campbell and Jane Hazelgrove), he begins to open up about his abuse to others.

With the festive season approaching, Sean (Antony Cotton) is excited about the prospect of decorating the flat.

However, Billy has decided against Christmas decorations this year, as he’s come to understand that they trigger bad memories for Paul from when he was a teenager.

Sean’s none too pleased at the lack of decorations, and he makes his opinion on the matter known.

Meanwhile, Paul is incredibly nervous about speaking to the police in regards to his abuse, but — after a conversation with David (Jack P Shepherd) in which he spoke about his own ordeal with Josh (Ryan Clayton) — he’s put at ease.
