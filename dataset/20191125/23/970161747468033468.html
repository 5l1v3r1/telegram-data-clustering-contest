Spoilers: Robert murders Vicky and his unborn baby in Corrie?

25 Nov 2019, 23:58

Robert Preston (Tristan Gemmill) has fought long and hard in Coronation Street to ensure that his wedding to Michelle Connor (Kym Marsh) goes ahead, and with him having been leading a double life and cheating on Michelle with Vicki Jeffries (Kerri Quinn), he’s lucky he’s got this far. However, Vicky proves to be a thorn in his side as the week of the wedding arrives, but will he resort to murder to ensure she remains out of the picture?

In the coming episodes, pre-wedding celebrations get underway at the bistro, but Robert is left horrified when Vicki shows up and interrupts proceedings.

However, he quickly manages to get rid of her, and lies to Michelle about it — claiming that he’s simply attempting to help out Tyler. As a result, Vicky scolds him, and promises to take him for every penny.

With the wedding in sight, Michelle orders Robert to ban Vicky from the ceremony, so he takes a trip to visit her to do just that. However, during his visit, a neighbour of Vicky’s hears the two of them shouting — that is, until there’s a resounding noise, followed by total silence.

Robert returns to the cobbles and ensures Michelle that Vicky won’t be a problem.

Later in the week, the wedding arrives, and Tyler barges into the ceremony, and demands that Robert tell him what he’s done to Vicky.
