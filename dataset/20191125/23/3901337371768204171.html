Villa End Newcastle's Unbeaten Run

25 Nov 2019, 23:56 by SL International

English Premier League

Result: Aston Villa 2-0 Newcastle United

Date: 25 November 2019

Venue: Villa Park

Aston Villa ended a three-match winless run in the Premier League with a 2-0 triumph over Newcastle United on Monday evening.

Paul Dummet earned a recall for the Magpies, replacing the injured Jamaal Lascelles as Jack McGrealish entered as one of the home sideâ€™s three key changes.

Long range shots from outside the area and a few half-chances marred the opening minutes of the encounter, as either side struggled to get clear openings through.

The Magpies though threatened the opener with a curling free-kick from Dummet on the far side of the penalty area, but the attempt was of no trouble to Tom Heaton following the 26th minute.

Conor Hourihane changed the overall context six minutes later when he curled home a free-kick from the far side of the area after a one-two with McGrealish.

Villa doubled their cushion four minutes later when Anwar El Ghazi raced onto an in-swinging free-kick from the left to rifle into the roof of the net.

Newcastle came quick out of the starting blocks in the second half and had Heaton at full stretch following a low drive outside the area from Allan Saint-Maximin. Moments later, Joelinton went close to increasing the hosts tally when his header from a corner forced Martin Dubravka to go down and parry the effort.

Newcastle went for a change following the 62nd minute and introduced Andy Carrol who entered for Dummet, as Steve Bruce threw caution to the wind.

However, the Claret and Blue remained in the ascendancy and forced the visitors to thorough defending after threatening deliveries and attempts close to the area.

This brought an end to Villa's three-match losing run in the league, as the Magpies continue their inconsistent run of results.
