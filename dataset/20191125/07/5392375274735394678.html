Oppo Reno Ace & Reno 10x Zoom Edition are ColorOS 7 early adopters -

25 Nov 2019, 07:59 by Efe Udin

Rate this post

ColorOS official said today that Oppo Reno Ace and Reno 10x zoom version is the  “early adopters” recruit for the ColorOS 7. Reno now has a recruitment quota, which the official will push later today. ColorOS 7 evolves with a borderless concept and is lightweight. It comes with a softer color scheme, redrawn icons, and Art + wallpapers. This in association with the new quantum animation engine provides more visual interaction. It also has an all-weather dark mode, global theme, rich personalization.

The UI can fully optimize the system while also improving the application startup speed. It also improves the system resource utilization efficiency. The official said that the overall startup performance increased by 25%; RAM utilization increased by 40%; system fluency increased by 30%; game frame rate increased by 19%; touch response speed increased by 15%. This is what this system will offer the Oppo Reno Ace and Reno 10x zoom version.

ColorOS image algorithm comes with Soloop recording video editor. This ensures that from shooting to editing to sharing gives users a convenient experience beyond imagination. In addition, there is also the Breeno sleep mode for a relaxing sleep. It also comes with the flashback button for scene switching and the automatic filling of the input codebook. At the press conference, OPPO announced that ColorOS has more than 300 million users worldwide, covering more than 40 countries and regions. It is also translated into more than 80 languages.
