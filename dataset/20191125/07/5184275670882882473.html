Darbar Update: First Single ‘Chumma Kizhi’ From Rajinikanth’s Action Thriller To Release On This Date

The action thriller has Rajinikanth as a cop.

25 Nov 2019, 07:40 by Koimoi.com Team

Megastar Rajinikanth’s fans have got a very exciting reason to celebrate. The makers of Thalaiavar’s much-anticipated release Darbar has officially confirmed that fans and cine-goers will soon be receiving a treat in the form of the first single track which has been titled Chumma Kizhi.

It was yesterday when the makers took the film’s production company Lyca’s twitter account to announce the news, as they along with a video featuring the film’s music composer tweeted: “Kannungala #ChummaKizhi’ka ready ya? Thalaivar’in #DARBAR 1st single from
27th Nov ROCKSTAR @anirudhofficial musical sung by the legendary #SPB sir & lyrics by @Lyricist_Vivek”
