Everlight to expand LED automotive device capacity by 50%

25 Nov 2019, 07:53 by Siu Han, Taipei; Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES

LED packaging service provider Everlight Electronics has disclosed it will expand monthly packaging capacity for LED automotive lighting devices by 50% from 100 million chips at present to 150 million chips in 2020.

Everlight said it will also expand monthly packaging capacity for ultraviolet (UA) and infrared (IR) LED devices by 80 million chips and SMDs (surface mount devices) by 50 million chips in 2020.

The expansion is estimated to require spending of NT$1.0 billion, about half of its annual capex budget in recent years.

Everlight said it expects growth in 2020 consolidated revenues to come from UV LED lamps used in sterilization and curing, LED horticultural lamps, LED lamps used in poultry farming and fish culture as well as high-end LED backlighting.

Everlight began small-volume shipments for mini LED backlight units (BLUs) for LCD TVs, gaming LCD monitors and LED automotive displays in fourth-quarter 2019.

Everlight expects LED devices for consumer electronics to account for 35% of its 2019 consolidated revenues, UV and IR LEDs for 25-30%, LED BLUs for nearly 20%, LED lighting for 10-20% and LED automotive devices for 6-7%.
