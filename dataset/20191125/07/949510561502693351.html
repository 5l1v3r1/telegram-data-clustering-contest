New Jersey man killed when ATV crashes in Pennsylvania

25 Nov 2019, 07:50 by Associated Press

PENN FOREST, Pa. (AP) — Authorities say a New Jersey man injured in an ATV crash in Pennsylvania has died from his injuries.

The crash in Penn Forest occurred shortly after 8 p.m. Saturday.

Authorities say 40-year-old Jason Borys, of Clifton, was driving the ATV when it veered off the road and crashed. He was pronounced dead a short time later.

The cause of the accident remains under investigation. Authorities say “adverse” weather conditions and alcohol may have played a role in the crash.

No other injuries were reported in the accident.
