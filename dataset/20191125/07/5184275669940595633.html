Akshay Kumar On Reuniting With Kareena Kapoor Khan In Good Newwz: “Making Movies With Bebo Is Like Going On A Wild Picnic”

As Akshay Kumar gears to reunite with Bebo in Good Newwz, he has all things good to say for the Heroine actress.

25 Nov 2019, 07:18 by Koimoi.com Team

After doing movies like Aitraaz, Tashan and Ajnabee, Akshay Kumar and Kareena Kapoor Khan are all set to reunite with a twisted comedy revolving around the theme of surrogacy with their next release, Good Newwz!

The pair has been much loved for their chemistry and were last seen together in Gabbar Is Back. And now, as Akshay Kumar gears to reunite with Bebo he has all things good to say for the Heroine actress.

Speaking to Hindustan Times in a recent interview, Akshay has been quoted saying, “Making movies with Bebo is like going on a wild picnic. She is fabulous at everything she does. Even when she goofs up, Kareena is enigmatic. With the sound of one clap, she switches from being a mummy, friend, and co-star to a professional queen of cinema. I know fans have been waiting for us to reunite, and nothing could be more perfect than this film for both of us, right now!”

Meanwhile, the first song from Good Newwz is soon to be released and fans can’t wait for the two stars to be seen on the screen together. Also, this is Diljit and Kareena’s second film together after Udta Punjab and that has also left fans excited.

Directed by Raj Mehta, Good Newwz features Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani in pivotal roles. The film also features Adil Hussain and Tisca Chopra in pivotal roles. The film is slated for a 27th December, 2019 release.
