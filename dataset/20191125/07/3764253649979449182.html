Femi Otedola says people think when they die, they take their money with them

25 Nov 2019, 07:29

Popular business mogul and father to sensational DJ and musician, Cuppy, Femi Otedola, has recently revealed why he donated the sum of N5 billion to a charity organisation in Abuja.

The Nation reports that earlier in November the businessman gave out the sum to the Save The Children organisation in its aim to intervene in the northeast.

It should be noted that the charity outfit is a 100-year-old United Kingdom organisation and is the biggest organisation ranked after UNICEF.

In his comment on why he made the donation through his daughter, Otedola said: "A lot of people think when they die, they take their money with them, we’ll see. In my journey of life, I have taken the risk, I’ve done the chase, I’ve enjoyed the thrill and I have achieved success and recognition. What next? You give it back."

Meanwhile, Legit.ng earlier reported that Otedola promised to provide a full-ride scholarship to the university level for a little girl.
