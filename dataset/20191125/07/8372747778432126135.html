Mother and son reported missing Sunday night in Collier County

25 Nov 2019, 07:56 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- A young mother and son have been reported missing from North Naples, according to the Collier County Sheriff's Office.

25-year-old Mackenzie Stallmer and 2-year-old Jaylen Stallmer were last seen around 5 p.m. Sunday night in North Naples.

Stallmer is 5 feet 1 inches tall and 130 pounds with brown hair and brown eyes.

Her family is concerned for her safety.

If you have any information on the location of these two, please contact the Collier County Sheriff's Office at 239-252-9300.
