Natalie Horner missing: Concerns growing for Barrhead woman who disappeared over the weekend

25 Nov 2019, 07:20 by Chelsea Rocks

POLICE are searching for a missing Scots woman who disappeared over the weekend.

Natalie Horner, 29, from Barrhead, near Glasgow, was last seen at around 6.05am on Saturday and was reported missing at 12.50pm the same day.

Officers in East Renfrewshire are appealing for information in a bid to help trace Natalie.

She has links to Glasgow, Paisley and Ayrshire and access to a Black Vauxhall Corsa reg BL11 AJX.

Concerns are growing for her welfare as police said it's "unusual" for Natalie not to be in contact with her family for this length of time.

Natalie is described as around 5ft5in with long, dark hair and was last seen wearing a blue jacket and dark leggings.

Inspector Michelle Grant, of Cathcart Police Station, said: “It’s unusual for Natalie to not be in contact with her family for this length of time.

"We are appealing for anyone who has any information as to her whereabouts to get in touch.”

Anyone with any information should contact police via 101, quoting incident number 1881 of Saturday, 23 November, 2019.
