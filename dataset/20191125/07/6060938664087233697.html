Did you know Kangana Ranaut took hormone tablets to get into the skin of Jayalalitha for 'Thalaivi'

25 Nov 2019, 07:43 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Yesterday, Kangana Ranaut amazed one and all with the first look which was followed by the teaser of her upcoming biopic on late CM and actress Jayalalithaa, titled, 'Thalaivi'. B-town was all praise for the actress and social media was abuzz with congratulatory wishes. The first look went viral in no time and her fans poured in love and praised the actress. Kangana is one of those actresses who have proved her acting craft and has paved a way into the Bollywood film industry. She is one of those who walk the extra mile to get the character right and she has done it again for this biopic.

The actress surely has been through a lot of hard work to finally get into the character and talking to a media house, the actress shared some details about her transformation and struggle to get the look of the character right. Kangana divulged that she had put on six kilos of weight for the role. She further revealed that the reason for her weight gain, she shared that looking voluptuous was needed for the role, especially around the belly and thighs and since Kangana is tall and skinny, she had to take mild doses of hormone pills to look different and start eating goods that promote weight gain.

Kangana further revealed that she had to further take help from Jason Collins, who had worked on the prosthetics to make her face look fuller. Moreover, different kind of pads were used for different parts of the body to get the final look apt.

Directed by Vijay, 'Thalavi' is being shot simultaneously in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi. It is scheduled to hit the theatres on 26th June next year.
