Romanian cult figure Pavel Florin has had another successful performance while representing Victorian suburban cricket club Surrey Hills on Sunday.

Florin took 3-10 in two overs and backed it up with three catches in the field.

In his first match for the club last week, he took one wicket and asked questions of the opposition batsmen with his unique bowling style, which initially saw him rise to fame.
