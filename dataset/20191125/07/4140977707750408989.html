Eden Hazard adds fuel to Kylian Mbappe Real Madrid transfer rumours

25 Nov 2019, 07:06 by Archie Blade

Eden Hazard adds fuel to Kylian Mbappe Real Madrid transfer rumours. The Belgian claimed that the young Frenchman would go down as one of the greatest.

There’s no doubt that French sensation Kylian Mbappe will one day become the best footballer in the world. Even Eden Hazard agrees, to the point where he has said if he can, he will do everything to bring him to Real Madrid.

Mbappe has long been rumoured to join the Spanish side and there are constant talks that he is Real Madrid’s number one transfer target. However, they have so far failed to bring him into their fold. However, new recruit Eden Hazard has revealed that he wants to see the 20-year old in a Real Madrid Jersey.

Eden Hazard adds fuel to Kylian Mbappe Real Madrid transfer rumours

“In a few years he will be the best player in the world. He is a great talent,” remarked the Belgian winger to Le Parisien.

“If he continues like this, Kylian will go down as one of the best players in history. A footballer always dreams of playing with the best and if tomorrow I was able to help bring him to Real Madrid, I would try.

“Although, I don’t think anyone will ask me for an opinion,” he humbly added.

Eden Hazard was also asked his opinion on who would bag the coveted Balon D’Or this term. The Belgian ignored Lionel Messi over Van Dijk and insisted that if not the defender, the winner should still come from Liverpool.

“I would give it to a Liverpool player. Mané, Salah or Van Djik deserve it,” he said. “If Sadio had won AFCON with Senegal, there would have been no debate. It would be beautiful if he won it. Or Momo (Salah), who is one of my friends.”
