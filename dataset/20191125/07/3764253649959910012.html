Wedding photos of man who married three wives on the same day

25 Nov 2019, 07:49

- A Ghanian man identified as Osman Hafiz has married three women on the same day

- The man and his wives tied the knot on Saturday, November 23

- Photos from Hafiz's wedding to his wives surfaced on social media

A man identified as Osman Hafiz, a native of Walewale in the North-east region of Ghana, and three ladies have done something strange in the name of love. The man tied the knot with the three women on the same day.

The three wives, Yidana Sikena, Ali Kamara and Yakubu Huzaima married their husband on Saturday, November 23.

According to Ghana's Ghsplash.com, the husband and his three wives released flyers some weeks ago to announce their forthcoming marriage.
