The Jonas Brothers Perform at the AMAs For the First Time in Over a Decade

25 Nov 2019, 07:05 by Monica Sisavat

After 11 long years, the Jonas Brothers returned to the American Music Awards on Sunday. OK, technically they were in Boston on their Happiness Begins tour, but their show was telecasted at the ceremony, marking the first time the band's performed at the show since 2008. The Jonas Brothers sang "Only Human," and we couldn't stop dancing along to the high-energy performance.

In addition to their performance, the band was nominated for favorite duo/group and favorite pop/rock song for "Sucker." Check out the performance ahead.
