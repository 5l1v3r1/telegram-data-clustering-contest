Nita Ambani’s daughter Isha Ambani leaves fans mesmerised with ‘Romance of Blossoms’ look even as video of playful daughter-in-law Shloka Mehta goes viral

25 Nov 2019, 07:53 by JKR Staff

The last few days have seen media attention shifting to the Ambani ladies as Mukesh and Nita Ambani hosted pre-wedding functions of their nephew Arjun Kothari at Antilia. As expected, the topic of intense discussion on social media was the outfits chosen by Shloka Mehta, Isha Ambani and Radhika Merchant for the various occasions related to Arjun’s wedding. While Nita Ambani’s daughter-in-law Shloka Mehta rocked in her red-hot avatar at the Ambani residence, daughter Isha and to-be bahu Radhika Merchant were no less stunning in their designer outfits. Even though the wedding is now over, fresh photos of Isha’s have gone viral soon after designer-duo Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla shared her regal look with ‘Romance of Blossoms!’ caption.

While sharing Isha’s photos, Abu Jani and Sandeep Khosla wrote, “Isha Ambani Piramal is absolute grace in custom Abu Jani Sandeep Khosla. Her powder pink Floral ghagra is exquisitely hand-embroidered in intricate floral bursts formed with crystals, silk threads and sequins. Paired with a short jacket blouse with floral corsages and finished with a light as air, organza Stole to create deliciously delicate romance.”

In another photo, Isha looked utterly radiant in her 3D floral embellished couture lehenga. Her custom made ivory lehenga choli with her lehenga being embossed with several floral designs was no less a stunner. The highlight of her look was that she wore absolutely no jewellery except for earrings and a statement ring on her left hand. Needless to say, Isha looked absolutely stunning.

As expected, fans were seen drolling over Isha’s regal look as one wrote, “Crushing over her floral lehenga.” “This look for her is so mesmerising,” commented another fan. One fan wrote, “She is looking beautiful, I just fell in love with her look.”

Arjun Kothari is the son of Mukesh Ambani’s sister Nina Kothari. He got married last week.
