Richa Chadha Finds Her Inspiration In Ranveer Singh & HERE’s Why!

The is one thing that Richa has realized in her decade long career is that one should never get too complacent in the industry if they desire to keep themselves relevant.

25 Nov 2019, 07:18 by Koimoi.com Team

Even though Richa Chadha made her debut over a decade ago in Bollywood, with Oye Lucky Lucky Oye! It was her role in Gangs Of Wasseypur that gave her the recognition that she craved for as an actor. And from there on there is no turning back for the actress.

The is one thing that Richa has realized in her decade long career is that one should never get too complacent in the industry if they desire to keep themselves relevant. And it is for this reason that Richa has revealed that she admires the spirit and approach to work of actors like Ranveer Singh.

Richa has been quoted by PTI saying, “Bollywood has taught me that if you have talent and are ready to work without massive ego, complications, if you can keep sane and keep working, then you’re in it for a long haul. I’m very inspired by people like Ranveer Singh who, in his mega-stardom, still goes out and works as hard as he did in his first. That hunger to do better shouldn’t ever die. The minute someone gets complacent it reflects.”

On the professional front, Richa will return to the digital world with Inside Edge where she will be seen reprising her character of Zarina Malik from the Amazon Prime series, Inside Edge 2.

Inside Edge 2 also stars Vivek Oberoi, Sayani Gupta, Angad Bedi, Sapna Pabbi among others and two will stream from December 6.
