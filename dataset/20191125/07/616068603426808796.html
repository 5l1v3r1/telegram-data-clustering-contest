Taylor Swift rules AMAs - Entertainment News

25 Nov 2019, 07:08 by WENN

It was Taylor Swift's night at the American Music Awards on Sunday as she picked up Artist of the Year and the Decade.

The Me! singer also scored the Album of the Year for Lover, Video of the Year for You Need to Calm Down, Female Artist - Pop/Rock, and Favorite Artist - Adult Contemporary to lead all winners with six gongs.

Taylor's hits medley was also the performance highlight of the 2019 ceremony at Los Angeles' Microsoft Theater, where Khalid and BTS picked up a triple each and Carrie Underwood, Billie Eilish, and Dan +Shay were among the double winners.
