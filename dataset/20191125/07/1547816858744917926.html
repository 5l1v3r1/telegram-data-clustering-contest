Bigg Boss 13: Salman Khan Likely To QUIT The Show Before Season 13 Winner Is Announced?

25 Nov 2019, 07:07 by Garema Khurana

Salman Khan’s upcoming film Radhe requires him to commence work asap and reportedly it could be difficult for him to juggle both the movie and his hosting duties on Bigg Boss 13. The show’s telecast schedule has been extended by 4 weeks

There are strong rumours that superstar Salman Khan might quit Bigg Boss 13 mid-season. The superstar has been associated with the show for the 10th time now and online reports claim that he might step down. This season is replete with drama, controversies, verbal and physical fights and a dash of romance, thanks to it’s contestants – Sidharth Shukla, Rashami Desai, Asim Riaz, Shehnaaz Gill, Himanshi Khurana, Devoleena Bhattacharjee, Paras Chhabra, Hindustani Bhau and others.

A few days back we reported that the show that has been garnering great TRPs will now be extended by another 4 weeks. Which means, Salman Khan will also need to make the required changes in his film calendar, to alot the show extra time. According to a report in Pinkvilla, the superstar might not be able to do so and hence is likely to exit the show before Season 13’s winner is announced.
