Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa first property in Malta to be invited into Signature’s Hotels & Resorts Collection

25 Nov 2019, 07:57 by Vicky Karantzavelou

Signature Hotels & Resorts is a curated collection of five-star luxury and four-star deluxe properties.

The five-star Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa is the first property in Malta to be invited into Signature’s Hotels & Resorts Collection. Located in the heart of the island of Malta, adjacent to the Presidential Palace, the 147-room Corinthia Palace, Corinthia’s founding hotel, exudes grace and historic charm.

Signature Hotels & Resorts is a curated collection of five-star luxury and four-star deluxe properties. Each hotel applicant is reviewed by Signature's Hotel Committee and invited to join based on top product quality, market demand, and Member Agencies' needs. 

"We are thrilled to welcome Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa in Malta into the Signature Hotels & Resorts Collection,” said Jordin Greene, Vice President Hotel Partnerships. “Signature has had a strong and longstanding relationship with the Corinthia brand, including being the first network in the USA to feature their London and Budapest hotels. Malta is such a unique and in-demand destination, and we are excited to now offer this top product, with exclusive program benefits, to our Membership." 

Adrian Attard, General Manager, Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa, said “at a time when Malta is trending in the American market, especially in the luxury space, we are honored to be the first Maltese property to become part of the Signature Hotel collection. The timing is excellent. In spring 2020 Corinthia Palace Hotel & Spa will be launching its new-look Athenaeum Spa which will be one of a kind in Malta. We are also refreshing our food and beverage offering and introducing initiatives such as curated itineraries for guests to experience the real magic of Malta.”
