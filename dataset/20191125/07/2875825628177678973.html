Jacqueline Jossa's hubby Dan Osborne confesses I'm A Celeb star is 'dramatic and screams a lot'

The dad-of-three didn't mind pointing out his love got "emotional", "dramatic" and "screamed a lot"

25 Nov 2019, 07:46 by Irishmirror.ie

Dan Osborne said his wife Jacqueline Jossa was "emotional, dramatic and screams a lot" on I'm A Celeb.

The former TOWIE star, 28, has been documenting his wife's I'm A Celeb journeys on his Instagram stories as he tunes in to watch every night.

In a honest post, the dad-of-three didn't mind pointing out his love got "emotional", "dramatic" and "screamed a lot".

However, the reality star also drew attention to the fact Jacqueline always gets what she wants done.

He said: "She's doing great. It's well out of her comfort zone. I said in an interview after the first episode, she'll shock everyone.

"She'll do good. She do it. She might be an emotional person, she might be a little dramatic, she might scream a lot but she's getting it done and that's what matters."

Dan had watched on in fear as the mother-of-two took part in the cave trial where she had to crawl into boxes to collect stars passed up to her by Ian Wright.

However, there were a few nasty surprises in there including snakes and spiders.

Incredibly impressed, Dan gushed his wife had made the Bushtucker Trial look "easy" when she performed it alongside Ian.

"Jac and Ian did amazing in that trial," he enthused. "Went in there. Smashed it. Made it look easy.

"There was a lot of screaming going on from both people but they absolutely nailed it."

It comes after Dan made a sly dig at Jacqueline for crying ahead of the I'm A Celeb trials.

He said: "I think it's good, if you go there, you're not going to enjoy the trials. There's a part of you wanting to do the trial.

"You want to get out of camp and do as much as you can."
