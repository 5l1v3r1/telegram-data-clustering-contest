Shed Seven celebrate Rick Witter's birthday at Rock City

His band present Rick with a birthday cake

25 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Phil Viles

It’s Rick Witter’s birthday, but it’s hard to tell if he’s in a good or a bad mood from the early exchanges with the crowd. After rousing opener Room in my House wets the whistles of the sold out, predominantly male audience, first Rick has a pop at someone for holding up a sign and then lambasts someone else for asking for an autograph during the set.

Is it banter? Is Rick ok with being 47? The crowd’s chants of Happy Birthday don’t land well either, and the band make ‘slitting throat’ gestures in a bid to stop the chants.

Despite this, he sounds great. In this two-hour set there’s a real mixture of old and relatively new, and the songs are bolstered by an organ and a brass section. Then, prior to the first chord of On Standby, his band present Rick with a birthday cake and thankfully there are wide smiles.
