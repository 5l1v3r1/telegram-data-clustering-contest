Camila Cabello and Shawn Mendes set the stage on fire with yet another performance of their hit song “Señorita” at the 2019 American Music Awards on Sunday night.

In another steamy session from the couple, who have reportedly been dating for months, the audience yet again waited with anticipation for an onstage kiss that never came.

The best part of the performance, however, just might have been Taylor Swift‘s expression in the crowd.

It started like any other “Señorita” performance, with Cabello looking muy caliente in a red dress, Mendes in a sleeveless vest, and a lot of intimate singing.

As the song was introduced, Mendes showed off his muscles while strumming the guitar as Cabello walked over to him seductively showcasing some serious vocals.

The two got closer and closer until they were literally face to face and singing into the same microphone.
