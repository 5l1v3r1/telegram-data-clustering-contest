Video: Garth Brooks returns to 'Words With Friends' with custom tile

25 Nov 2019, 13:36 by Brandy McDonnell

Zynga Inc., a global leader in interactive entertainment, announced today a new, holiday-themed activity in its ongoing collaboration with Oklahoma native and Country Music Hall of Famer Garth Brooks and its flagship mobile game, "Words With Friends."

Starting today and continuing through Dec. 8, players of the game will be treated to special in-game and real-world giveaways as part of this two week event from country music superstar, award-winning entertainer and longtime "Words With Friends" player.

In celebration of the holidays, Brooks and "Words With Friends" are releasing the first collaboratively designed tile style, featuring a vinyl record. Representing his record-toppling career and the release of his career-spanning, comprehensive "Legacy" box set, Brooks' vinyl record tile style features his familiar "g" logo overlaid on a classic vinyl LP, according to a news release.
