PHOTOS: VP Osinbajo, Dolapo mark 30th wedding anniversary

25 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Agency Report

Vice-President Yemi Osinbajo and wife, Dolapo, mark their 30th wedding anniversary on Monday (today).

Mrs. Osinbajo made this known in romantic posts on her Instagram handle, which she titled, ‘Hand in hand.’

She had posted, “Hand in hand on the 25th November 1989. It’s our 30th wedding anniversary to the glory of God.”
