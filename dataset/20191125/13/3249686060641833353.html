KIMTAEHYUNGBAR, BTS V's China Fansite unveils "City of a Thousand Stars" the 2nd of Project Vionysus for BTS V's grand birthday bash

25 Nov 2019, 13:39 by fanpop

BTS V's China Fan Site,@KIMTAEHYUNGBAR_is making history again.

On November 23, Baidu V Bar revealed the second part of the lavish birthday support they have planned for BTS V from the Project Vionysus series,called "City of a Thousand Stars"  and the mammoth-scale event is absolutely jaw dropping. 

The project involves a total of 1,899 individual birthday supporting ads for BTS V  that will completely cover ALL of Seoul's Subway systems-and all of these will light up like thousands of stars in December where millions of passengers will be able to see and resonate with the feeling of the celebration and, just like their previous projects, will be done for the first time in history. 

The Seoul Subway is the heart of the city having 9 major lines  which possesses one of  the world's largest railway systems with maximum capacity of passengers; 400 shops, and nearly 2,000 screens that will display  their heartfelt wishes  for the multi-talented artist's birthday.
