Video: Nick Diaz refuses to groin kick a guy for money, reveals he’s ‘fighting again soon’

25 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Anton Tabuena

Nick Diaz’s Instagram has been very active — and pretty crazy — during his hiatus from mixed martial arts. What he shared Sunday night was just a tad bit weirder, as the former Strikeforce champion was roaming the streets of New York.

It started out from Diaz selling four jiujitsu gis online for $50 each, to eventually being asked to kick a fat guy in the balls for money.

He refused, and instead paid part of the sum to a bouncer, who would do the kicking instead. In the middle of all the madness, Diaz revealed that he is going to fight again soon.
