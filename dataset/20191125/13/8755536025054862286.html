Fans turn on I’m A Celeb’s Andrew Maxwell amid ‘shouting’ row

He clashed heads with Ian Wright yet again

25 Nov 2019, 13:02 by Clodagh Meaney

Irish fans of I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here were thrilled when not one, but two Irish contestants were announced for the brand new series of the ITV show.

Derry’s sweetheart Nadine Coyle was joined in the line-up with Irish comedian, Andrew Maxwell.

Last week, Ian Wright snapped at the 44-year-old comic and fans on Twitter quickly jumped to the funnyman’s defence.

On last night’s show, he came to blows his fellow campmate once again, and fans opinions changed very fast.

One viewer even branded the star “the first Irish reality TV participant to let the whole country down.”

During a task, campmates were asked to create a queue system for who would get to eat first.

During this task, Andrew accused Ian of “shouting” at him.

“You keep shouting at me Ian,” he said, standing up to leave the campfire.

“Who is shouting, when did I shout?” asked Ian.

Andrew quickly told him it happened “just then” to which Ian claimed: “I wasn’t even talking to you, what did I say?”

Even though the pair quickly made up, with Andrew saying “I’m sorry, I’m really tired, I don’t know, I’m really tired,” fans were quick to take to social media to express annoyance at his antics.
