Moment a jihadist base is blown up in southern Idlib: video

25 Nov 2019, 13:32 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (5:20 P.M.) – A jihadist base belonging to Hay’at Tahrir Al-Sham was blown up in the southern countryside of the Idlib Governorate this week.

In a short video released on Monday, the HTS headquarters can be seen being targeted by the Syrian Air Force in the town of Banin.
