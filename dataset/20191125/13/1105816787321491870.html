Qureshi discusses economy, social development with Queen Maxima

25 Nov 2019, 13:42 by News Desk

Queen Máxima of the Netherlands, who landed in Islamabad earlier in the day on a three-day visit, called on Foreign Minister Shah Mehmood Qureshi.

She is visiting Pakistan in her capacity as the United Nations Secretary-General’s Special Advocate (UNSGSA) for Inclusive Finance for Development.

During the meeting, matters pertaining to different aspects of economic and social development came under discussion, according to Radio Pakistan.
