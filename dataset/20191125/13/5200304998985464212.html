Subhash Chandra resigns as Chairman of ZEE Entertainment Enterprises Limited

25 Nov 2019, 13:59 by Raj Baddhan

Subhash Chandra has resigned as Chairman of ZEE Entertainment Enterprises Limited, with immediate effect.

In a filing with the Bombay Stock Exchange, the company said, “In light of the changes in shareholding, Mr. Subhash Chandra has expressed his intention to resign as Chairman of the Board with immediate effect. The Board accepted his resignation and noted that this is in line with the requirements of Regulation 17 (lB) of SEBI Listing Regulations, which inter alia mandates that the Chairperson of the Board shall not be related to the Managing Director or the Chief Executive Officer of the Company.”

Chandra will continue in the capacity of non-executive director of Essel Group.

Essel Group has been in the news of late over its finances. The company said it is planning to sell 16.5 per cent stake in its flagship brand, ZEEL to financial creditors to meet its loan repayment obligations. Earlier, the group had sold 11 per cent stake in ZEEL to Invesco-Oppenheimer fund for over Rs. 4,224 crore and then retired debt worth Rs. 4,000 crore.
