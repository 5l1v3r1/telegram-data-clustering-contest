Tottenham Hotspur have released a team news update ahead of tomorrow’s match against Olympiacos.

Victory against the Greek side in North London would book their place in the knockout stages of the Champions League.
 
Spurs trained at Hotspur Way earlier this morning where a portion of the session was opened up to the media to watch as part of the usual UEFA protocol.
 
Club captain Hugo Lloris once again misses out with his long-term elbow injury while fellow goalkeeper Michel Vorm, who recently returned to the club suffered a calf problem in training and won’t return until next month.
 
Ben Davies picked up an ankle injury in the 3-2 win against West Ham United on Saturday and is unavailable along with Erik Lamela who still has a hamstring issue.
 
Tanguy Ndombele and Jan Vertonghen have both returned to first team training.
 
Olympiacos drew against Spurs in match day one, but have failed to have registered a further point since after losing three matches in a row.
