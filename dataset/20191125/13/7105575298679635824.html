Jenna Dewan Was Caught on Camera Calling Camila Cabello "Always Extra" at the AMAs

25 Nov 2019, 13:04 by Mehera Bonner
  • Jenna Dewan was caught on camera saying "She's always extra!" about Camila Cabello during her AMAs performance with Shawn Mendes.
  • Fans wondered if it was shade, but Jenna hit Instagram Stories to deny it, saying she has nothing but love for Camila and "Señorita."

Last night, Camila Cabello and Shawn Mendes hit up the American Music Awards and performed—you guessed it—their hit song "Señorita." The performance was about as sexy as you'd anticipate (read: very sexy!), but things got a little awkward when the cameras flashed to Jenna Dewan. Jenna leaned over to her friend and stylist Brad Goreski, rolled her eyes, and appeared to say "She's always extra."
