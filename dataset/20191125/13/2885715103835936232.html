‘Man with a golden heart’: Ratan Tata’s appeal to adopt abandoned dog gets praise online

Requesting people to find a home for a 9-month old Labrador, Ratan Tata wrote, "Of all the different sufferings that animals today have to go through, my heart truly breaks for the ones that get abandoned by families."

25 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Trends Desk

Owner of two pet dogs, industrialist Ratan Tata has often been vocal about his love for animals. While last year he won hearts after dedicating a part of his Mumbai residence to street dogs, recently his attempt to find a home for an abandoned dog has left netizens emotional.

Requesting people to find a home for a 9-month old abandoned Labrador, Ratan Tata wrote, “Of all the different sufferings that animals today have to go through, my heart truly breaks for the ones that get abandoned by families.”

“I cannot imagine what must go through their minds when one day they have a home, and the next they don’t. The kindness in 9-month-old Myra’s eyes still stays after being abandoned, and I could really use your help in finding her a family,” Tata further said while empathising the pain the animal must be feeling after being left alone.

Along with his heartfelt post, Tata also shared two pictures of the canine. With a following of over six lakh, it did not take long for the post to go viral and be flooded with reactions.
