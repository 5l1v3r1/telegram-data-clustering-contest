Lizzo's tiny purse won the American Music Awards red carpet

Lizzo nominated for three awards

25 Nov 2019, 13:10 by Lisa Respers France, CNN

LOS ANGELES (CNN) - Oh how we love you, Lizzo.

The singer showed up on the American Music Awardsred carpet Sunday sporting the teeniest of purses.

Seriously small.

And true to Lizzo style, there was a reason for it.

"@maisonvalentino bag big enough for my f***s to give," she wrote in the caption on her official Instagram account. "Big body b***h in a Valentin-HO custom look for @amas."

Lizzo was nominated for a three awards, including new artist of the year.
