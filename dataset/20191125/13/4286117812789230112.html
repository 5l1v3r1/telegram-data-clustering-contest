Encounter underway in J&K's Pulwama, 1 militant killed

The area has been cordoned off.

25 Nov 2019, 13:49

An encounter between security forces and militants is underway in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir.

Two to three militants are believed to be trapped in Pulwama's Drabgam area. According to sources, one local Jaish commander has been killed. He has been identified as Irfan Ahmed.

The area has been cordoned off.
