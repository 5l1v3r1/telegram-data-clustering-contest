Man dies in tragic incident on A38 in Derby

The road was closed for five hours

25 Nov 2019, 13:07 by Nigel Slater

A man has died in an incident on the A38 in Derby.

Police officers were called to reports that a man had fallen from a footbridge across the A38, just north of Markeaton roundabout, at around 8.20pm yesterday (Sunday, November 24).

The road was closed for five hours and did not reopen until around 2am this morning.

While in the carriageway, the man was struck by a vehicle, a statement from Derbyshire police says.

The statement says: "There are not believed to be any suspicious circumstances regarding the incident.

Officers want to speak to the driver of the Ford, as well as any other drivers who were in the area at the time who may have information that can assist in the investigation.

Anyone with information should call Derbyshire police on 101 or contact the force via its socia media platforms quoting reference number 19000630443.
