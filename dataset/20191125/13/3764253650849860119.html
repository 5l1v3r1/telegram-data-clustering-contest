Jose Mourinho reportedly names Bournemouth's Nathan Ake as first target in January

25 Nov 2019, 13:54

- Jose Mourinho has reportedly identified Bournemouth's Nathan Ake to be his first signing in the January window

- The Netherlands international played under Mourinho at Chelsea but was demoted to the reserve team

- But the 24-year-old is currently enjoying life with Cherries following his £20m move from Chelsea in 2017

Jose Mourinho is reportedly plotting a January move for Bournemouth defender Nathan Ake.

It is understood that Tottenham are looking to sign two centre-backs to help bolster their team in the next two transfer windows.

According to Football Insider, the Netherlands international would become an ideal replacement for Jan Vertonghen whose contract expires next summer.
