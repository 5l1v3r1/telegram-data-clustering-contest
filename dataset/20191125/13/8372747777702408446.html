Two lottery jackpots top $100 million this week

25 Nov 2019, 13:48 by WFTX Digital Team

TALLAHASSEE, Fla. -- The jackpot in both the Mega Millions and PowerBall lottery games continue to grow this week.

First up this week is the Mega Million drawing on Tuesday night, woth an estimated $226 million, according to the Florida Lottery .

The last winner was on September 24th, when an anonymous Texas player won $225 million.

The following night, the PowerBall drawing on Wednesday night will be worth an estimated $100 million.

Both drawings will take place at 11 p.m. each night, but tickets must be purchased by 10 p.m. the night of the drawing.

And as always, you can catch these lottery drawings live on Fox 4 at 11 p.m.
