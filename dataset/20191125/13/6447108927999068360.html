One Piece Pirate Warriors 4 release date confirmed for March

25 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Aran Suddi

Bandai Namco has confirmed that One Piece Pirate Warriors 4 will be releasing on March 27th 2020 with the game coming to PS4, Xbox One, Switch, and PC. To mark this announcement the publisher released a brand new trailer which shows a lot of the characters from the manga being quite shouty. The game will also feature an original ending due to the fact the manga itself is still ongoing.
