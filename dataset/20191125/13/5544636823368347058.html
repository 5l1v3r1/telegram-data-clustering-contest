Throwback Photos Of Osinbajo, Wife As They Mark 30th Wedding Anniversary

25 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Oyindamola Olubajo

Vice President, Yemi Osinbajo and his wife, Dolapo are celebrating their 30th wedding anniversary on Monday, November 25.

In celebrating their special day, Dolapo shared some rare throwback photos on her Instagram handle, some of which are their wedding photos in 1989.

She attributed the success of their marriage to God’s grace and praised Him for leading them this far.

She wrote;“Hand in hand on the 25th November 1989. Its our 30th wedding anniversary to the glory of God.”

The couple is blessed with three children.

Dolapo is a grandchild of the late Nigerian nationalist, Obafemi Awolowo.
