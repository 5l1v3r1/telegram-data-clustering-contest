UPSC CDS results 2019 declared: Steps to download results, check merit list

UPSC CDS result 2019: A total of 2726, 1609 and 643 candidates had qualified in the written test for admission to the Indian Military Academy, Indian Naval Academy and Air Force Academy respectively.

25 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Careers Desk

UPSC CDS results 2019: The Union Public Service Commission has declared the final results of UPSC Combined Defence Services. The candidates can check their results from the official website, upsc.gov.in. A total of 129 students have qualified the final  CDS examination. A total of 2726, 1609 and 643 candidates had qualified in the written test for admission to the Indian Military Academy, Indian Naval Academy and Air Force Academy respectively.

The UPSC had advertised for 100 vacancies 100 for Indian Military Academy, 45 for Indian Naval Academy and 32 for Air Force Academy, Hyderabad. As per the official notification, there are some common candidates in the three lists for various courses.

UPSC CDS results 2019: Steps to check

Step 1: Visit the official website- upsc.gov.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on the link that says, ‘Final results-Combined Defence Services 2019’
Step 3: A PDF containing the results will open on the screen
Step 4: Check for your name and download for future reference

Candidates should note that the verification of date of birth and educational qualifications of these candidates is still under process and therefore their selection is provisional.

While the result is available on the official website marks of the candidates will be available only after the declaration of the final result of Officers’ Training Academy for Combined Defence Services Examination (I), 2019.
