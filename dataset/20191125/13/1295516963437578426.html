Thanos Wears Different Armor in Rejected Avengers: Endgame Concept Art

25 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Berek Bigos

Avengers: Endgame concept artist Jerard Marantz has unveiled new concept art showing off several unused versions of the Mad Titan Thanos' armor.

"Here’s my early design pass on Warrior Thanos for Avengers Endgame and Infinity War," Marantz wrote. "In this design I went a bit more spartan and got some good traction with the Russo Brothers. Though I do like this version, I am very proud of the final armor design. I feel like it was a good call and that the armor I designed in film matched Thanos’s character much more than in this pass. You can catch this work and more in the art of Endgame book. So many incredible pieces in there from so many insanely talented artists."

In a separate post, Marantz revealed updated armor with a bulkier look, writing, "Close to final design for Thanos’s warrior armor in [Endgame] and [Infinity War]. From the waist up the design is what showed up in the film. I changed up the armor on the legs and the loincloth and swapped out the weapon. This was an incredible challenge."

Thanos made his Marvel Cinematic Universe debut in 2012's The Avengers. Following minute appearances in both Guardians of the Galaxy and Avengers: Age of Ultron, the Mad Titan would face off against Earth's Mightiest Heroes during Infinity War, where he would achieve his destiny and eliminate half of all life. However, Thanos and his alien army would inevitably be defeated by Tony Stark at the conclusion of Endgame.

Directed by Joe and Anthony Russo, Avengers: Endgame stars Robert Downey Jr. as Iron Man, Chris Evans as Captain America, Mark Ruffalo as Bruce Banner, Chris Hemsworth as Thor, Scarlett Johansson as Black Widow, Jeremy Renner as Hawkeye, Brie Larson as Captain Marvel, Paul Rudd as Ant-Man, Don Cheadle as War Machine, Karen Gillan as Nebula, Danai Gurira as Okoye and Bradley Cooper as Rocket, with Gwyneth Paltrow Pepper Potts, Jon Favreau as Happy Hogan, Benedict Wong as Wong, Tessa Thompson as Valkyrie and Josh Brolin as Thanos. The film is available on Digital HD, Blu-ray and 4K UHD.
