Star Wars: Rise of Skywalker Director Confirms Film's Final Runtime

25 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Jon Arvedon

The saga of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker's runtime is nearly as long as the Skywalker Saga itself. However, we now have confirmation of the film's official runtime from the most reputable source possible: director/co-writer J.J. Abrams.

While speaking with EW, Abrams said the final cut of the film is 141 minutes, adding an "I believe" at the end to possibly account for a few extra minutes of credits.

In August, percussionist Don Williams, the brother of longtime Star Wars composer John Williams, hinted that the film may have clocked in at 135 minutes. Then, when tickets went on sale in October, AMC listed the film at 155 minutes, which would have made Episode IX the longest entry in the franchise, beating out The Last Jedi's previous record of 152 minutes.
