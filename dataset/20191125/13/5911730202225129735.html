Whole Foods Raspberry Cheesecake Italian Gelato recalled over undeclared allergen

25 Nov 2019, 13:00 by WFTS Digital Staff

Whole Foods is recalling one lot code of their raspberry cheesecake Italian gelato because it contains egg that isn't listed on the product label.

The product was sold nationwide and was packaged in 1-pint plastic containers with the UPC 9948248024 and a best by date of 6/11/2021.

The FDA says there have been no reactions from the product reported and all affected product has been taken off store shelves.

Customers who purchased this product at can bring a valid receipt into stores for a full refund.

If you have additional questions call 1-844-936-8255 between the hours of 7 a.m. and 10 p.m. CST, Monday through Friday, or 8 a.m. and 6 p.m. CST Saturday through Sunday.
