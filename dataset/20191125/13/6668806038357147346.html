Video: Shahid Kapoor and Mira Kapoor have a blast jumping on a trampoline

25 Nov 2019, 13:21 by Filmfare

Shahid Kapoor and Mira Kapoor are one of the most loved B-town couples. The two manage to give us couple goals with everything they do – be it heading out for fancy dinners and lunches to attending starry B-town bashes and even working out together. No wonder, the couple always manages to look so fit and glamorous together.

Giving us a glimpse of their fun weekend, Mira shared a lovely boomerang video on her Instagram. In the video, she can be seen jumping on a trampoline with husband Shahid. One look at the boomerang and you know that these two sure know how to make their workout fun. Check it out here.
