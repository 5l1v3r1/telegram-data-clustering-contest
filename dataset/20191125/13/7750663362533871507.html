Manchester United's Paul Pogba 'trying to force Real Madrid move'

Manchester United midfielder Paul Pogba is allegedly trying to secure a transfer to Real Madrid in January.

25 Nov 2019, 13:02 by Darren Plant

However, according to journalist Paco Gonzalez, the World Cup winner has requested not to play in an attempt to force through a switch to the Bernabeu.

Speaking to Tiempo de Juego, Gonzalo has claimed that Pogba, who has admitted that the wants to leave Old Trafford, has already recovered from his ankle issue.
