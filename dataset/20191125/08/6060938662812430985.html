'Chahal TV' host Yuzvendra impressed with new anchor Rohit Sharma

25 Nov 2019, 08:26 by IANS

KOLKATA: Indian leg-spinner Yuzvendra Chahal on Monday took a funny jibe at his teammate Rohit Sharma who interviewed pacers Ishant Sharma and Umesh Yadav following India's emphatic victory in their maiden pink-ball Test against Bangladesh at the Eden Gardens here.

Chahal usually interviews Team India players in a show 'Chahal TV' for the Board of Control for Cricket in India.

"Good job by new anchor Rohit Sharma. Keep it up youngster," Chahal said while replying on the video tweeted by BCCI.

"Hitman in conversation with Ishant Sharma and Umesh Yadav. Rohit Sharma dons the anchor's hat and quizzes the pace duo who steered India to a historic victory in the pink-ball Test," BCCI had tweeted the video on Sunday.
