Apostle Suleman Reveals ‘Time’ God Is Not Happy (Video)

25 Nov 2019, 08:42 by Ridwan Yusuf

President of the Omega Fire Ministries Worldwide Apostle Suleman Johnson says when a believer is broke, God is “not happy”.

Concise News reports that at a church service recently, the fiery preacher stressed that God promises the believers riches.

“Scarcity worries God. Your scarcity troubles him. When your are broke; you are stranded, God is not happy. When that Landlord is throwing out your things because you have not paid your rent, God is grieved.

“You can’t serve The Most High and be most low,” Suleman states.
