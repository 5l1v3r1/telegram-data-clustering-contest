Marine Serre & SSENSE Team for Black & Pink Shadow Moon Long Sleeve T-Shirt

Featuring the brand’s signature crescent moon print.

25 Nov 2019, 08:35 by Torsten Ingvaldsen

Marine Serre and SSENSE have teamed up for an exclusive take on the fashion imprint’s famed crescent moon print, crafting a Black & Pink Shadow Moon Long Sleeve T-Shirt. Featuring a stretch nylon jersey base, the T-shirt features the brand’s signature graphic printed in pink and white throughout. This is met by a mock neck collar and raw edges at the cuffs and hem.

Marine Serra has become one of the most talked-about designers in recent memory following the establishment of her namesake label. In 2017 she won the LVMH Prize for Young Fashion Designers, since launching a menswear line for the Spring/Summer 2019 season. She also recently collaborated with Nike and Converse, further helping to sculpt out her genderless approach to fashion.

You can view the SSENSE-exclusive Black & Pink Shadow Moon Long Sleeve T-Shirt via the gallery above. The piece is currently available for sale via SSENSE’s webstore with a retail price set at $330 USD.
