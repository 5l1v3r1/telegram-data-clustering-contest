Jofra Archer says he was racially abused during England's defeat to New Zealand

25 Nov 2019, 08:11

Jofra Archer says he was subjected to racial insults from a spectator during England’s defeat to New Zealand in Tauranga.

Archer scored 30 off 50 balls but was unable to prevent England from slipping to an innings-and-65-run defeat in the first Test against New Zealand.

The Barbados-born fast bowler says that during his innings he was racially abused by a fan.

‘A bit disturbing hearing racial insults today whilst battling to help save my team,’ Archer tweeted on Monday morning. ‘The crowd was been amazing this week except for that one guy. The Barmy Army was good as usual also.’
