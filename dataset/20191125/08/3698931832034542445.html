Oppo offers the Reno 10X special edition with RM1,446 worth of freebies

25 Nov 2019, 08:04 by Alexander Wong

The OPPO Reno 10X Zoom special edition with 12GB RAM will be arriving in Malaysia on 30th November. If you plan getting on, Oppo Malaysia is bundling freebies worth RM1,446 during its first day sale at The Gardens Mall this coming Saturday.

The OPPO Reno 10x 12GB RAM Special Edition will be bundled with a Fujifilm Instax Mini Link Smartphone Printer worth RM548, an Oppo Enco Q1 Wireless Noise-cancelling headphones worth RM599 and a VOOC Powerbank worth RM299.

Customers who place their pre-order before 30 November can collect your phone in advance and walk-in customers can also purchase on-the-spot.

The new Oppo Flagship store is located at Lot T-230 on the 3rd Floor, The Gardens Mall. At the moment, the official price isn’t revealed yet but we reckon it should be priced above RM3,000. The Reno 10X Zoom with 8GB RAM was originally launched in Malaysia at RM3,399 but the price is now reduced to RM2,999.
