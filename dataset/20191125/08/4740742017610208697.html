Little Mix to play Kingsholm Stadium in Gloucester in July 2020 - how to get tickets

It's part of their 21-date Summer 2020 tour

25 Nov 2019, 08:55 by Jasper King

One of the world's biggest girl band's, Little Mix, will perform at Gloucester's Kingsholm Stadium on Thursday, July 9, 2020.

It forms part of their 21-date Summer 2020  tour, which sees the pop superstars taking their unmissable live show on the road next year.

Tickets will go on sale at 9am on Thursday (November 28) and you can get them from ticketmaster.co.uk, musicplussport.com and tickets.gloucesterrugby.co.uk.

The tour will see Little Mix perform some of their greatest hits including 'Woman Like Me', 'Touch', 'Shout Out to My Ex', 'Black Magic' and 'Wings'.

Speaking about the Summer 2020  tour coming to Gloucester's Kingsholm Stadium, Lance Bradley, Chief Executive Officer at Gloucester Rugby said: "It's great to welcome Little Mix back to the home of Gloucester Rugby, Kingsholm Stadium.

"When Little Mix came to Kingsholm in 2017, the concert sold out within a couple of days of going on general sale so we do advise everyone to get their tickets quickly.

"We really look forward to welcoming Little Mix back in Gloucester!"

Speaking about the Summer 2020  tour, the group said: "Performing live is our favourite thing to do as a band, we love it.

"Our last Summer tour in 2018 was one of our favourites ever, so we can't wait for some more brilliant outdoor shows next year.

"We want everyone to come party with us in the sunshine."

Little Mix have established their reputation as one of the biggest girl band's around.

The group have just completed a massive LM5 Arena Tour that took place in cities across Europe, the UK and Ireland including an amazing five sold out dates at London's 02 Arena.
