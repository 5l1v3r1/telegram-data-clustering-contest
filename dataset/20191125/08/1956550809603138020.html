Mega R&B stars Boyz ll Men to perform in Dubai

The US group will be playing at Dubai's Coca-Cola Arena

25 Nov 2019, 08:56 by Darragh Murphy

One of the most iconic R&B groups are making their way to Dubai for their first-ever performance, and you can be sure it’ll be "one sweet day".

Hip-hop stars Boyz ll Men are set to rock the Coca-Cola Arena in Dubai, and if you already know you want to be there, you can nab yourself a ticket today.

Tickets will be available today (Monday November 25) from noon at www.coca-cola-arena.com, with prices starting at Dhs200.

Set to perform at Dubai's Coca-Cola Arena on Thursday December 19, it’s not a performance to be missed, seeing as the trio hold the title for being the best-selling R&B group of all time.

Some of their biggest tunes include End of the Road, One Sweet Day, Motownphilly (which they recently were nominated for a CMT Music award for) and plenty more.

Over the past two decades, Boyz II Men have won themselves four Grammy Awards, nine American Music Awards, three Billboard Awards and a star on the Walk of Fame.
