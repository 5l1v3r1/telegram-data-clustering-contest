IndiGo says 'systems down across the network'

25 Nov 2019, 08:50 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Highlights

  • The airline said it is trying 'to solve the issue asap (as soon as possible)'
  • IndiGo had faced a similar issue earlier this month

NEW DELHI: India's largest airline IndiGo said on Monday that its "systems are down across the network" and the carrier is trying "to solve the issue asap (as soon as possible)".

"#6ETravelAdvisory : Our systems are down across the network. We are expecting the counters to be crowded more than usual. Please bear with us as we try to solve the issue asap," IndiGo said in a Twitter post.
