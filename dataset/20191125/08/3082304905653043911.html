WATCH: Malaysia come from behind to level the match after Myanmar had taken shock lead in SEA Games 2019 opener

25 Nov 2019, 08:48 by Harigovind

On Monday, Malaysia took on Myanmar in the opening match of the SEA Games 2019 Men’s football tournament – and in the 13th minute, Myanmar’s Soe Moe Kyaw stunned Malaysia with a goal in the 13th minute itself, only for the 2017 tournament finalists to equalize just eleven minutes later.
