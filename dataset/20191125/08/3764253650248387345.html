Nigeria vs Oman: Super Sand Eagles crash out of World Cup after defeat

25 Nov 2019, 08:19

- Nigeria vs Oman's Beach soccer World Cup game ended in 6-5 defeat for Sand Eagles

- The Super Sand Eagles have now been thrown out of the 2019 Beach soccer World Cup

- They have lost their first two games and will face Brazil in their final group game

Nigeria's Super Sand Eagles have crashed out of the 2019 FIFA World Cup after losing their second game of the tournament 6-5 against Oman on Sunday night, November 24.

But their performance against Oman was better than their first game which they lost 10-1 against Portugal.

Super Sand Eagles wanted to get a win against Oman in their second game which could have given them a chance ahead of their last match against Brazil.

The first period of this encounter ended 2-2 but Oman led 2-1 after the end of the second period before sealing the victory with a 2-2 scoreline after the third period.
