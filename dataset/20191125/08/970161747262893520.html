Mini Piers Morgan and Susanna Reid audition to replace OG presenters

25 Nov 2019, 08:54

Mini Piers Morgan and Susanna Reid joined Good Morning Britain today and met grown-up Piers Morgan and Susanna Reid and they were seriously cute.

Millie Peake, aged six, and Jack starred in the show’s viral Christmas advert to rival John Lewis and melted hearts as they confessed to the OGs that they wanted to take over from the presenters as stars of the show.

‘Do you want my job, Jack?’ asked Piers, to which the youngster shamelessly replied: ‘Yes’.

Susanna then asked: ‘And what about you, Millie. What do you want to be?’

‘I want to be on Good Morning Britain,’ she enthused.

Susanna and Piers then challenged their mini-double act to a live audition by getting them to read the auto-cue.

‘There is a possibility that Piers might not be here in the New Year,’ Susanna began. ‘Should we do an on-air audition?’

The pair then read through the lines without hesitation, much to the delight of Pies who branded them ‘naturals’.
