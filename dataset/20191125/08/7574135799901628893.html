Archers of Loaf teasing “The Return of the Loaf,” announce tour dates

25 Nov 2019, 08:21 by Andrew Sacher

Back in summer 2018, Eric Bachmann said Archers of Loaf were planning new music, earlier this year they played their first show since 2015, and now they have released a new teaser video that just says: “The Return of the Loaf: Coming Soon.” Archers of Loaf put a live album of a 2011 show in 2015, but if this teaser means a new album, it’d be Archers of Loaf’s first since 1998’s White Trash Heroes. Stay tuned.
