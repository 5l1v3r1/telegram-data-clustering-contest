Dezeen Weekly newsletter now has over 300,000 subscribers

25 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Karen Anderson

More than 300,000 people are now signed up to receive Dezeen Weekly, our curated newsletter sent every Thursday.

A huge thank you to our subscribers who have supported us. To celebrate this milestone, collage artist Sebastian Jordahn has created this illustration.

If you haven't already, sign up to Dezeen Weekly for your weekly dose of the most popular stories on Dezeen, including catchy reader comments, competitions and updates on exciting events.

You can also sign up to Dezeen Daily to get all the latest news on architecture, interiors, design and technology, Dezeen Day newsletter to be the first to hear what our conference has in store, as well as Dezeen Awards and Dezeen Jobs for updates on our awards programme and job listings within the industry.
