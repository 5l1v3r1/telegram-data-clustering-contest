Taliban Shadow District Governor Killed in Faryab

Three other Taliban members were killed in the attack, and seven others were wounded, MoD said.

25 Nov 2019, 08:20 by TOLOnews.com

Mawlawi Abdul Ghafar, the Taliban-designated shadow district governor of Almar district in Faryab, was killed in an airstrike in the province, the Ministry of Defense (MoD) said in a statement on Sunday.

The airstrike took place in Khawja Gauhari village of Almar district after Afghan air forces targeted a Taliban’s haven in the district, the statement said.

“Six vehicles and some weapons were also destroyed during the airstrike,” it said.

The Taliban have not commented on the incident.
