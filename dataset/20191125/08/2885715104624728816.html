Third Walking Dead series gets official title

Set to arrive next year during spring, The Walking Dead: World Beyond will feature two young female protagonists and focus on the first generation to come-of-age in the apocalypse.

25 Nov 2019, 08:30 by PTI

The third spin-off in The Walking Dead franchise has been officially titled The Walking Dead: World Beyond.

The title was revealed on Sunday in a new teaser on the show’s verified Twitter account.

“Get ready for the #TWDUniverse to expand. #TWDWorldBeyond coming this spring,” read the caption of the video.
