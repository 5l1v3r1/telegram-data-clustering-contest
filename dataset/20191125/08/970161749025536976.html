Post Malone dancing to Shania Twain at the AMAs is pure joy and relatable AF

25 Nov 2019, 08:29

Is there any bigger mood than Post Malone living his best life to Shania Twain?

Because if so, we’ve yet to find it.

The rapper was serving majorly relatable vibes as he owned his prerogrative to have a little fun at the American Music Awards while country legend Shania performed an epic medley.

Marking her upcoming Las Vegas residency – side note, booking flights to Sin City ASAP – Shania, 54, returned to the AMAs stage for the first time since 2003 to bless us with a medley of her own classics and other chart hits.

The pop-country star first delivered a medley of Twenty One Pilots’ Stressed Out, Taylor Swift’s Shake It Off and Drake’s God’s Plan – played on a glittering pink guitar – before seguing into her slow-set classic, You’re Still The One.
