PSG leading transfer race for Wolves teen ahead of Liverpool and Arsenal - report

Latest Wolves transfer news | Owen Otasowie is being linked with a number of clubs

25 Nov 2019, 08:22 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Paris Saint-Germain are leading the race to sign Wolverhampton Wanderers youngster Owen Otasowie, according to reports.

The centre-back, who can also play in midfield, impressed against the French side in the Under-23 Premier League International Cup last week.

The 18-year-old hasn’t been a regular for the U23s this season but looked assured in possession and defended resolutely in the 1-1 draw at Molineux.

Liverpool and Arsenal are said to be admirers of the former USA U18 international who has played twice in the EFL Trophy this season, featuring against Blackpool and Morecambe.

According to the Sun, there is also interest from Germany in the form of Schalke, currently managed by former Huddersfield Town boss David Wagner.

Otasowie is out of contract in the summer and Wolves would only be due training compensation following a move to Europe.

The former Mass Elite Academy player also qualifies for England and Nigeria at international level.
