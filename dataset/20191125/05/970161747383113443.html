BTS win big at the AMAs after missing out on Grammys nod

25 Nov 2019, 05:06

BTS found themselves winning big at the American Music Awards on Sunday night, as they took home all three awards they were nominated for.

The K-pop group, made up of V, J-Hope, RM, Jin, Jimin, Jungkook and Suga, were the second biggest winners on the night, along with Khalid, who also won three awards.

They took home the gongs for Tour of the Year, Favourite Social Artist and Favourite Duo or Group Pop/Rock.

Not bad boys, not bad.

Of course, the BTS Army were thrilled with the boyband’s wins and Twitter was flooded with congratulatory messages cheering the group on.

It will no doubt come as a nice confidence boost for the band, after they were noticeably snubbed by the Grammys.

The nominations for the 2020 Grammys were released last week and BTS missed out on nod, despite their success over the last year which included three number one albums in 11 months.

It’s safe to say their fans were not happy with the Recording Academy, with fellow singer Halsey commenting on the snub on Twitter and stating that BTS deserved not just one, but many Grammy nominations.

She too wasn’t included in the Grammys list and tweeted in support of BTS: ‘deleting and ignoring all negativity. BTS deserved many nominations. I am however, unsurprised that they weren’t acknowledged. the US is so far behind on the whole movement. the time will come.’
