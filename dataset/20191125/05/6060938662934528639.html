Fitspiration! Janhvi Kapoor is slaying her Pilates workout in this video along with her trainer

25 Nov 2019, 05:02 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The young brigade of Bollywood is very much conscious when it comes to fitness and healthy living. Many millennial stars like Ishaan Khatter, Kartik Aaryan, Sara Ali Khan, Alia Bhatt, Janhvi Kapoor are often snapped outside their gym in the city. Each actor have their own style of working out, some prefer pilates while some opt for weight lifting. They not only themselves follow a workout regime but also motivate their fans and followers to adapt to a healthy lifestyle.

One of the fittest stars is Janhvi Kapoor who never fails to impress us with her amazing Pilates workout. Just a few minutes ago her trainer Namrata Purohit has shared a video where we can see the 'Dhadak' star slaying the fluid movement exercise like a pro.

Her trainer shared the video on Instagram and wrote, "Fluid movement with @janhvikapoor 🥰 one of our favourite series! 💪🏼🔥 Our glutes are on fire! Haha #MondayMotivation #MondayMood #PilatesGirls" In the past also, Namrata has shared a appreciative posts for Janhvi.

On the work front, Janhvi Kapoor has her plate full. She will be seen playing the titular role in ‘Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl’, and then she will be seen essaying a pivotal part in ‘Takht’. Not just this, she also has ‘Dostana 2’ and ‘Roohi Afza’ in her list of upcoming movies.
