Lady and her friends denied entry into Abuja Lounge – See reason

25 Nov 2019, 05:37 by Sanya Agunbiade

A lady has taken to social media to call out a certain Abuja Lounge where she and her friends were denied entry imply because they might not be able to afford the expensive drinks in the lounge.

According to her, they were initially refused entrance into the space because they had no male companion to accompany them in, only for the bouncer to later reveal that the action was taken simply because management felt they wouldn’t be able to pay for their drinks.

A man however came to their rescue, she wrote:

I’m so livid!!!! My friends and I walked into a lounge and they refused to let us in cause there’s a policy that doesn’t let women in without a male champion. This is freaking 2019!!

Women with striving careers can’t hang out and have fun in Abuja Without a man? Wow!

A gentleman came to our rescue and assured the bouncer we weren’t “street girls.”Lmao why should street girls be banned from having a good time with male counterparts ffs? Streets girls are human!

The bouncer’s argument was that we can’t afford a drink in the lounge …My friend brought out her business card
