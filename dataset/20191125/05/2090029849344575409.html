Full list of winners at the Future Awards 2019

25 Nov 2019, 05:55 by Sanya Agunbiade

Winners have emerged at the 2019 Future Awards, which was held yesterday in Lagos.

The event, which was hosted by reality TV star Khafi and media personality Taymesan, saw a host of winners, including the rave of the moment in the entertainment industry, Burna Boy, who copped the awards in two categories.

Find below the full list of the winners at the Future Awards.
