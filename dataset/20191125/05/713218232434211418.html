Despite smoke and dust, bull rides provide a great spectacle at Dorrigo Show

25 Nov 2019, 05:46 by Vivian Hoskins

Bull riding, the opening feature of Dorrigo Show, drew a record crowd on Friday night.

Competitors from schoolboys to open riders came from as far as Townsville especially for the evening event.

Many of them, juniors and seniors, have achieved major success in national events in Australia and overseas.

The program featured "professional" bulls and mini bulls from the Matthew Doak Bucking Bulls, Bundarra.

The bull rides have proved to be a popular family event for Dorrigo Show with crowd numbers increasing during the last three years.
