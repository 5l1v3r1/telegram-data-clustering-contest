Gas leak Port Melbourne: Multiple people ‘overcome by fumes’

25 Nov 2019, 05:04

A gas leak in Port Melbourne has seen several people fall ill when they were overcome by fumes with hundreds evacuated and at least one person taken to hospital.

Emergency crews were called to the incident in Lorimer St about 1.20pm.

The Metropolitan Fire Brigade said 250 people were evacuated while three people were found to be “overcome by fumes”.

Paramedics treated five people at the scene and one person was taken to the Royal Melbourne Hospital in a stable condition.

The leak was believed to be a refrigerant gas however crews had not yet determined the exact substance.

Specialist MFB Hazmat Technicians were investigating the cause of the leak.

Police were at the scene.
