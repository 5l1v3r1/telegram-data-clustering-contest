Vince McMahon's backstage reaction to Bayley's heel turn [Watch]

25 Nov 2019, 05:26 by Abhilash Mendhe

On the October 11 edition of SmackDown Live, Bayley debuted a new look and destroyed the Bayley Buddies while coming out for her SmackDown Live Women's title match against Charlotte Flair, signifying a turn to the dark side. The latest edition of "WWEDay Of" takes a look at Bayley's heel turn, and shows Vince McMahon's reaction to the same.

The September 2, 2019 episode of RAW saw Bayley joining forces with Sasha Banks and attacking RAW Women's Champion Becky Lynch. Around a month later, Bayley met Charlotte Flair in a match on SmackDown Live, with Flair's title on the line.

Bayley shed her old persona on this night, and came out donning a completely new look. She eventually won the match, and berated the WWE Universe to complete her heel turn.
