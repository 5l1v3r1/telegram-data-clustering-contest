Is Nana Aba Anamoah Leaving GHOne To UTV?

25 Nov 2019, 05:00 by BB Jones

Poaching and defection are very common in the media landscape and it’s something that will continue for a long time.

Just recently, the likes of Berla Mundi, Giovanni Caleb, Vim Lady, Captain Smart and other great media personalities have crossed carpet to the opposing end, a situation which was trailed by so much controversies.

And it’s like Nana Aba Anamoah, who is one of the hottest cakes in the media fraternity, could follow suit.

How is it possible that Nana Aba Anamoah who is still with GHOne TV congratulated a competitor for winning an award over them?

That’s what she did and although some might think it’s harmless, it sends loads of signals from our perspective.

She sent a congratulatory message to United TV (UTV) for emerging as the TV station of the year (Akan) at the just ended 24th Ghana Journalists Association Awards on her Instagram page.

She wrote;

“Congratulations to the @utvghana team too for emerging as the TV station of the year (Akan). Mo y3 gam!”
