WATCH: Taylor Swift sweeps the boards at 2019 American Music Awards

25 Nov 2019, 05:58 by Algoa FM

It was Taylor Swift’s night at this year’s AMAs. The superstar walked away with five competitive awards, including the top accolade of Artist of the Year, bringing her total tally of AMA wins over the course of her career to 28 (she had 23 career wins prior to Sunday night's show).

This also means that she has broken Michael Jackson's record of most AMA wins of all time, the late King of Pop won 24 AMAs in total.
