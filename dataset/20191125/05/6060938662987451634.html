#AmericasBestGroupBTS: BTS fans laud K-Pop band for winning all three AMAs they were nominated for

25 Nov 2019, 05:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

K-Pop band BTS and their fans are on cloud nine after the band picked up all three awards they were nominated for at the American Music Awards.

The seven-member boy band beat out the likes of the Jonas Brothers and Panic! At The Disco to win the Favorite Duo or Group—Pop/Rock. They even performers won against fellow nominees Billie Eilish, EXO, Ariana Grande and Shawn Mendes to win Favourite Social Artist.

And scoring them the hattrick, was the award for Tour Of The Year, beating out music heavyweights including Ariana Grande, Elton John, P!nk and Ed Sheeran. This has reportedly made them the first Asian act to win the prestigious award.

Though the boys couldn't be there to accept the awards in person, they did record a message for their devoted fans - BTS Army.

They said, "Thank you so much. We are really honoured to receive such an important and meaningful award. BTS has been a group for six and a half years and, throughout those years, we've had so many of our dreams somehow manifest into reality and Army, you guys are the ones who make all this possible and we really couldn't have done it without your love and support from all over the world."

They concluded, "We will try our best to live up to it, to strive high and work hard and return your love back to you."

Fans across the globe soon began trending ‘#AmericasBestGroupBTS’ on social networking sites to laud the singers for the wins.
