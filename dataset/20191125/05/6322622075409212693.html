Bursa's top gainer Master-Pack jumps on profit rise

25 Nov 2019, 05:20 by Chong Jin Hun

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 25): Master-Pack Group Bhd's share price rose as much as 48 sen or 23.88% to RM2.49 so far today before paring gains at Bursa Malaysia's afternoon break after the corrugated paper packaging manufacturer said on Friday third quarter net profit rose to RM5.99 million from RM1.26 million a year earlier.

At Bursa's 12:30pm break today, the counter settled at RM2.35 as the bourse's top gainer. The stock saw 6.11 million shares traded.

On Friday, Master-Pack said in a Bursa filing revenue climbed to RM55.48 million in the third quarter ended Sept 30, 2019, from RM37.13 million.

For cumulative nine months ended Sept 30, Master-Pack said net profit increased to RM12.57 million from RM3.95 million a year earlier.
