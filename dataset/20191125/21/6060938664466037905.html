Photos: Janhvi Kapoor looks absolutely beautiful as she teams a blue shirt with denim shorts!

25 Nov 2019, 21:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Janhvi Kapoor marked her debut in Shashank Khaitan's 'Dhadak' co-starring Ishaan Khatter in 2018. She received appreciation and love from the audience for her performance in the film. And now, she has several films lined up ahead.

The actress often gets snapped in the city post her workout sessions and on various occasions. On Monday, the young starlet visited her brother Arjun Kapoor's house and was clicked while leaving the same. For the night, she opted for a sky blue shirt teamed with denim shorts and flip flops. The actress looked beautiful as she left her hair open as she stepped out for the same.
