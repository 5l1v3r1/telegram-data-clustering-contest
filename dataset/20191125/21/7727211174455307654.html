"It is worrying to say the least."

25 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Rebecca Koncienzcy

Soap fans were left disturbed this evening as Coronation Street's Hope Stape's face lit up with delight as a fire broke out in her back garden.

Hope's spiralling behaviour has become a huge cause for concern for mum Fiz Stape, played by, Jennie McAlpine, and Tyrone Dobbs, played by Alan Halsall.

But on ITV tonight it also started to concern viewers as they watched her get eerily excited as a firework party got out of hand and a fire, sending two foot flames into the air, started.
