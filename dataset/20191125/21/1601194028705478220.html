WWE makes a big announcement for Royal Rumble weekend

25 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Shruti Sadbhav

WWE have confirmed the network special event Worlds Collide 2020 will take place during the Royal Rumble weekend in January. As per the official announcement, the WWE Universe will see the Superstars from NXT and NXT UK engage in a battle for brand supremacy on January 25, 2020.

NXT picked up a historic win at Survivor Series 2019 after defeating RAW and SmackDown in their first-ever appearance at the PPV. The Black and Gold brand dominated the entire show with commendable performances.

Now, both divisions of NXT will prepare to lock horns at the Toyota Center in Houston, Texas to kickstart the Royal Rumble weekend.

Soon after the conclusion of Survivor Series 2019, Triple H took to Twitter to announce the next event that will see NXT and NXT: UK Superstars head into a war to determine the superior brand.

Triple H's tweet read, "What will happen when @WWENXT battles @NXTUK? Worlds. Will. Collide. #WorldsCollide @ToyotaCenter Saturday, January 25th during #RoyalRumble weekend LIVE on @WWENetwork... Tickets go on sale next Friday, December 6th at 10am CT at https://NXTTickets.com! #WeAreNXT"
