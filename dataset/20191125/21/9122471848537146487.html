Indianapolis Colts Release Injury Update For RB Marlon Mack

25 Nov 2019, 21:45 by Alek Arend

The AFC South is shaping up to be one of the most hotly contested division battles in the NFL this season. The Colts and Titans are each 6-5 and just one game behind the Texans for the division lead.

Indianapolis hosts the Titans on Sunday in a pivotal division contest. According to the latest report from ESPN’s Adam Schefter, the Colts will once again be without running back Marlon Mack.

Mack missed Thursday’s game with an injury and will be out again this week, Schefter tweeted earlier today.
