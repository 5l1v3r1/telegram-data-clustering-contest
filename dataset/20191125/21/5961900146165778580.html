HEY MA: Bon Iver Are Doing A Full Aussie Tour Next Year And Headlining Dark Mofo

25 Nov 2019, 21:51 by By Alex Bruce-Smith

Bon Iver are heading back to Australia for a stadium tour in 2020, including a casual headlining gig at Dark Mofo in Hobart.

Led by Justin Vernon, it’s Bon Iver’s first national tour since 2012 – and first visit since headlining Vivid in 2016 – following the release of their critically acclaimed fourth album i,i.

The tour is promising to be “unlike anything fans have seen before”, hitting up Perth, Adelaide, Melbourne, Brisbane and Hobart mid-next year.

Pre-sale tickets will be available from Dec 3, with the rest of them available on Dec 5. Get on board here.
