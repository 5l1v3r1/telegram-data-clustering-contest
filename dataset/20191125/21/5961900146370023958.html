Jenna Dewan Swears She Wasn’t Shading Camila Cabello & Shawn Mendes At The AMAs & Sure, Sis

25 Nov 2019, 21:23 by By Matt Galea

Witches of East End star Jenna Dewan has been forced to publicly deny throwing a heap of shade at Camila Cabello at the American Music Awards yesterday.

During Camila and Shawn Mendes’ ~steamy~ performance of ‘Señorita’, Dewan was reportedly spotted saying, “She’s always extra,” to stylist friend Brad Goreski while rolling her eyes, according to People.

Goreski later defended his friend, stating: “Guys, we were bumping and grinding so hard to ‘Señorita.’”
