I’m A Celebrity’s Ant McPartin cheekily adjusts his ‘care package’ on live TV

25 Nov 2019, 21:48

I’m A Celebrity host Ant McPartlin gave viewers an unexpected eyeful during tonight’s episode as he cheekily adjusted his ‘care package’ live on-air.

Ant, 44, and his sidekick Declan Donnelly were giving their commentary after the campmates carried out their latest Bushtucker trial, which saw them abseiling down the side of a cliff in an attempt to win care packages.

For the male campmates likes Myles Stephensons and Andy Whyment, it was a particularly tricky challenge to navigate in regards to their manhood and Ant just couldn’t resist a cheeky joke.

‘I thought Andy and Myles did a great job too although those packages took quite a bashing on the way down,’ the TV host observed.

Dec chimed in: ‘The harnesses were very tight.’

Poor fellas.

As he introduced the next segment, Ant began hoisting up his trousers prompting Dec to ask what was on all of our minds.

‘What are you doing there?’ he enquired to which Ant replied: ‘Just adjusting the old care package.’

And the words of Phoebe from Friends echoed in all our ears: ‘My eyes. MY EYES.’

Reacting to Ant’s cheeky quip, one ITV viewer tweeted: ‘I’m distracted by Ant’s care package,’ although we’re not sure if they were horrified or entertained.

Another advised: ‘Ant I’d be carful sorting your care package on live television.’

One viewer shared a meme of them spitting out their drink and, frankly, the other tweets are just too NSFW for publication.

The campmates were abseiling in a bid to win their special packages from home and Emmerdale actor Andy, Kate Garraway, Nadine Coyle and Rak-Su singer Myles were able to successfully grab hold of their parcels meaning all 12 campmates would receive their care packages.

I’m A Celebrity airs each night at 9pm on ITV.
