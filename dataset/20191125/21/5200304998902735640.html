Deepika Padukone confirms Ranbir Kapoor & Alia Bhatt’s wedding?

25 Nov 2019, 21:32 by BizAsia Correspondent

Rumours about Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt’s big day have been doing the rounds for several months. It now seems the news about the couple tying the knot is public knowledge.

Recently, Deepika Padukone appeared in a discussion on the 100 greatest performances of the decade with ‘Film Companion’, along with Bhatt, Manoj Bajpayee, Vijay Deverakonda, Ayushmann Khurrana and Ranveer Singh.

During the conversation, Vijay opened up about the crush he had on Padukone and Bhatt. He said, “Without shame a lot of people on this table have been massive crushes, like been in love with some of them like these two (pointing to Deepika and Alia). She (Deepika) got married but…” Deepika interrupted and said, “She (Alia) is getting married.” Vijay continued, “She (Alia) is getting married.”

Bhatt was cited in Hindustan Times responding, “Excuse me, why have you made this declaration?” Padukone added, “Guys, I made that up. I just wanted to see his reaction.”
