What Each Zodiac Sign Should Let Go Of In 2020 

By January Nelson Updated November 25, 2019

Adrien King By January Nelson Updated November 25, 2019

Adrien King

Aries

You should let go of your anger at people who have wronged you. You should accept they screwed up and accept they’re out of your life for good.

Taurus

You should let go of your cynicism. You should let yourself believe in love again, let yourself believe in happiness again.

Gemini

You should let go of your guilt. You should stop blaming yourself for mistakes you made years ago, mistakes you cannot fix now.

Cancer

You should let go of your desire to please everyone. You have to accept that you aren’t in charge of everyone else’s happiness, only your own.

Leo

You should let go of your addiction to social media. You should put down your phone and stop comparing yourself to people you haven’t seen since high school.

Virgo

You should let go of your desire to be right all the time. Embrace the fact that you don’t know everything.

Libra

You should let go of your self-doubt. You should stop holding yourself back because you’re acting as your own biggest critic.

Scorpio

You should let go of your grudges. You should put your past in the past.

Sagittarius

You should let go of your fears. You should break free from your comfort zone and take more chances.

Capricorn

You should let go of your expectations. Stop assuming you need to reach a certain milestone by a certain point in time.

Aquarius

You should let go of your toxic friendships. It doesn’t matter whether you’ve known this person for years, because if they’re unhealthy for you, they’ve got to go.

Pisces

You should let go of your worry about the future. You should focus on what today holds instead of jumping too far ahead.
