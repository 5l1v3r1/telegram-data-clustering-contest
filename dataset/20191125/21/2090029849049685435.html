Burna Boy unfollows everyone on Instagram except girlfriend, Stefflon Don

25 Nov 2019, 21:12 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Nigerian singer aka African Giant, Burna Boy has taking to his Instagram page to unfollow all leaving just British rapper, and girlfriend Stefflon Don.

Such move is not new with celebrities as some other celebrities like Wizkid, Tiwa Savage, Falz have made similar moves in the past with either unfollowing followers or deleting all pictures on social media.

The African Giant who recently bagged the Young Person of the Year award and got a Grammy’s nomination for his album “African Giant,” unfollowed everyone he follows including his entire family and colleagues in the music industry but left his girlfriend, Stefflon Don.

The reason for this sudden move has not been revealed but it appears fans are not too happy about it.
