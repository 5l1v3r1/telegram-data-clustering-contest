Photos: Disha Patani opts for a no-makeup look and flashes her million-dollar smile as she gets snapped outside Mohit Suri's office

25 Nov 2019, 21:45 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Disha Patani is one of the beautiful actresses of Bollywood and the title 'National crush of India' given to her by the netizens is proof of the same. The actress often gets snapped in the city on various occasions, from dinner dates with Tiger Shroff to brunch with her friends and other events.

And on Monday, the diva was clicked outside Mohit Suri's office. For the occasion, she was wearing a white crop top with a pink jacket over it and blue loose denim. She left wavy hair open and decided for a no-makeup look. Disha flashed her million-dollar smile while leaving the filmmaker's office.

On the work front, the actress will be next seen in 'Malang' helmed by Mohit Suri. The film stars Aditya Roy Kapur, Anil Kapoor and Kunal Kemmu along with Disha. The film will hit the theatres on 14th February 2020.

She will also feature in Salman Khan starrer 'Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai' directed by Prabhu Deva. The film has been slated for Eid 2020.
