Efia Odo Angrily Blasts A Popular Blogging Site For Spreading Rumor That She Is Pregnant (+Screenshots)

25 Nov 2019, 16:09 by vanessa nordzi

Controversial Screen goddess Andrea Owusu popularly known as Efia Odo has taken a swipe at a popular blogging site known as Celebritiesbuzzgh for saying she is pregnant for Dancehall King Shatta Wale.

It would be recalled that Efia Odo recently posted on her Twitter page that she missed her monthly period and might be pregnant for Shatta Wale.
She probably posted it just for fun but a lot of blogging site took the hint and broadcasted it on major social media platforms.

Efia Odo who was obviously unhappy about the post was just waiting for the right time to vent out her displeasure and anger towards the parties involved in the publication.

Well, as expected, the Kwese TV presenter posted a picture on her Instagram page and captioned it ” Calling God to tell her I’m in the garden of Eden and there’s no apple”.

The blogging site “Celebritiesbuzzgh” decided to look for trouble for themselves by commenting on it.

Efia Odo who obviously has been waiting for the opportunity replied in rather harsh manner. She wrote “fuck you and your stupid fake ass stories u dey promote bout me! Now people think I’m pregnant!”

When it comes to insults and the use of the “F” word, Efia Odo has no contender.
