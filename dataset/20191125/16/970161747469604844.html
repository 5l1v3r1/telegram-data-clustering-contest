Donald Trump 'is the chosen one sent by God to do great things', aide claims

25 Nov 2019, 16:00

Donald Trump is ‘the chosen one’ who has been sent to Earth by God as part of a ‘divine plan’, a top aide claims.

The President of the United States’ outgoing Secretary of Energy Rick Perry made the glowing comments on Fox News Sunday.

Perry began by explaining his belief that God has ‘used imperfect people all through history,’ before naming Biblical examples King David, Saul and Solomon.

He said he’d given Trump a brief note on those Old Testament kings last month, and added: ‘I shared it with him and said, “Mr. President, I know there are people that say you said you were the chosen one and I said, ‘You were'”.
