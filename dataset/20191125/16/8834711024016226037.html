Top Social Tech Startups To Battle It Out For R5 Million Seed Funding

25 Nov 2019, 16:18 by NT Editor

Now in its second year, the Social Tech Startup Challenge (STSC) is a 12-month programme, created by the Diageo Empowerment Trust South Africa – DETSA, empowering tech-enabled start-ups through access to industry experts, design professionals, relevant business training and funding to help scale up their innovations from idea to viable business.
