Bloomberg Officially Running for President

'I'm running for president to defeat Donald Trump and rebuild America,' he said.

25 Nov 2019, 16:05 by Shelby Talcott

Former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg officially announced the start of his presidential campaign Sunday.

Bloomberg, 77, has been toying with the idea of joining an already-crowded Democratic field, with a report earlier in November noting he was preparing to file for a Democratic presidential primary in Alabama. The multibillionaire officially joined the race Sunday with an advertisement and the launch of his campaign website.

“I’m running for president to defeat Donald Trump and rebuild America,” Bloomberg tweeted.
