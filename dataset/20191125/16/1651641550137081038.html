Xiaomi’s Mi Note 10 with 108MP camera to launch in India soon

25 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Rahul

Xiaomi had officially announced the Mi Note 10 smartphone, which is also known as Mi CC9 Pro in China. What makes the smartphone unique is the camera — Mi Note 10 features a huge 108MP camera on the rear. After China and Europe, the Mi Note 10 is confirmed to be coming to Indian markets.

Xiaomi India’s Global VP & MD Manu Kumar Jain took to Twitter to announce that Xiaomi is planning to launch its 108MP smartphone to India. However, he didn’t reveal the date of release and price of the Mi Note 10.
