PSL Confirm 13 Suspensions

25 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Soccer Laduma

The Premier Soccer League confirmed that 13 players are set to miss this week’s Absa Premiership action due to suspension.

Orlando Pirates’ Happy Jele will miss out his side’s clash against Polokwane City tomorrow, with it being his second match suspended after he watched the Soweto Derby from the stands.

Eric Mathoho, who missed his side’s Telkom Knockout semi-final fixture also due to a red card suspension, will miss Kaizer Chiefs trip to the Western Cape on Wednesday to face Stellenbosch FC.

Also on a red card suspension is Gaston Sirino, who received the booking on Saturday afternoon, with the player set to miss two of the Brazilians’ domestic fixtures, starting on Tuesday evening when they face off with AmaZulu.

Here are the 10 other players suspended for this week’s league action:

Amazulu FC - Tapelo Xoki

Baroka FC - Goodman Mosele and Matome Kgoetyane

Cape Town City - Thabo Nodada and Thato Mokeke

Golden Arrows - Brandon Parusnath and Danny Phiri

Highlands Park - Marks Munyai

Kaizer Chiefs - Erick Mathoho

Mamelodi Sundowns - Gaston Sirino

Maritzburg United - Keagan Buchanan

Orlando Pirates - Happy Jele

Stellenbosch FC - Morgan Gould
