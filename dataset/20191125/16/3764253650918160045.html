Don Jazzy commends Fireboy, Joeboy, Remyboy and Burna Boy for an amazing 2019

25 Nov 2019, 16:01

- Popular music producer Don Jazzy has come out to commend some artistes in the Nigerian music industry

- The Mavin boss shared a post on Twitter celebrating Fireboy JoeBoy, Remyboy and Burna Boy

- Don Jazzy’s funny post was received with mixed reactions from his followers

The year has indeed been an amazing one for a number of relatively new artistes in the Nigerian music industry, and ace producer and Mavin Record boss, Don Jazzy, recently took time out to celebrate the young talents.

In a post shared on micro blogging platform, Twitter, Don Jazzy hilariously listed out a number of artistes that have the suffix ‘boy’ attached to their stage names.

Don Jazzy congratulated Fireboy, Joeboy, Remyboy and the biggest of them all, Burna Boy, for all they have achieved in the course of the year.
