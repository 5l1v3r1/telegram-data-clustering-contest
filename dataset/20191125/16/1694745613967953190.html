As Expected, Camila Cabello and Shawn Mendes Brought the Heat in Their AMAs Performance

25 Nov 2019, 16:05 by Mekishana Pierre

Is it hot in here, or is it just Camila Cabello and Shawn Mendes's sultry performance at the American Music Awards? The couple performed their swoon-worthy duet "Señorita" on Sunday night, and they really brought the love to the AMAs stage. During the intoxicating performance, Mendes was on the guitar, while Cabello danced around him up until the very end when the singers teased a kiss and ended things with a cute little nuzzle.

The pair took home the award for best collaboration for the song, and damn, they definitely earned that honor with their fiery performance! Watch it ahead.
