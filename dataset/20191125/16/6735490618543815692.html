Blake Lively Shares Hilarious Hospital Video From 2017 Following Her ‘The Rhythm Section’ Injury

25 Nov 2019, 16:07 by Aynslee Darmon

Thanks to Ryan Reynolds, the aftermath of Blake Lively’s “The Rhythm Section” on-set injury was caught on tape.

The “Gossip Girl” alum shared a hilarious video – taken by her loving husband – from her hospital bed, after hurting her arm on set of the upcoming film.

“Literally four weeks after I said, ‘I’d give my right hand to be in this movie!’ (Good thing no one told me to ‘break a leg’).” she captioned the video. “My husband somehow knew ‘Wild Thing’ would be my version of smelling salts. I’ve never felt so seen. Or high. Also… I have zero recollection of this.”

Lively also credited Reynolds as director and DJ on the short clip.

The upcoming drama will be released in 2020, but the majority of the film, except for the short break while Lively healed, was filmed in 2017.

Lively recently wiped her Instagram clean to get ready to promote “The Rhythm Section”, leaving just a clip of its trailer.

“The Rhythm Section” hits big screens in January.
