Windows 10 Theme: Download Microsoft’s new theme pack Painting in Light PREMIUM from Microsoft Store

25 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Rahul

Microsoft has added a new theme pack to the Microsoft Store for Windows 10 users. Titled Painting in Light PREMIUM, the new theme pack contains 12 high-quality 4K wallpapers, the description of which reads as follows:

Follow the fluid stream of liquid light in these 12 long-exposure 4k premium images. These images are free for Windows 10 Themes and are to be used as desktop wallpaper only.

After downloading the collection, go to Start > Settings > Personalization > Themes and click on the theme you prefer.

To apply and change to the most appropriate color, based on the current wallpaper, go to Customize > Colors > Automatically select a primary color from the background.

You can download the Painting in Light PREMIUM theme pack from this link.
