Glory Osei is back on Twitter, sends strong message

25 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Earlier last month, the social media space especially Twitter was hot on account of founders of Divergent Enterprises, Glory Osei and Muyiwa Folohunso with allegations of scam business ventures and poor treatment of former staffs as well as maintenance of a toxic work environment.

Reacting to the allegations Glory Osei said they were the handiwork of disgruntled ex-staff trying to bring down.

“This is a ludicrous statement. Everything we have done from when we started till now is to maximize the resources at our disposal, making sure to not take more than what we need to achieve what needs to be done.”

In the wake of the allegations that have since trailed Divergent Enterprises, Glory Osei is back on Twitter and in a series of tweets sent messages to all who care to read.
