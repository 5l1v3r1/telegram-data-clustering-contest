Devil May Cry 3 Special Edition for Nintendo Switch coming 20th February 2020

25 Nov 2019, 16:55 by My Nintendo News Admin

Capcom has announced today that the impressive third entry in the Devil May Cry series Devil May Cry 3 will be heading to the Nintendo Switch next year. You will be able to get hold of Devil May Cry 3: Special Edition on Nintendo’s latest platform on 20th February 2020 and it is priced up at £15.99.

As this is the special edition, a few of the bonuses are guaranteed. People will have a playable Vergil. Also, the Bloody Palace mode is present. The mode here has 9,999 floors and lets people earn the Super Costumes for Dante or Vergil.
