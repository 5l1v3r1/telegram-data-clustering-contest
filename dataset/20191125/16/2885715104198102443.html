UP Police Constable phase II admit card released for 49,568 posts: Check how to download

UP Police Constable phase II admit card: The exam is scheduled to be conducted on January 27 and 28. The written exams have been conducted before and this is the physical fitness examination.

25 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Careers Desk

UP Police Constable phase II admit card: The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPRPB) released the admit card for the recruitment exam to be conducted for the post of constable. Candidates can download their hall ticket from the official website, uppbpb.gov.in. Of the total posts, 31,360 are for the post of civil constable while 18,208 are for PAC. The notification for these recruitments was released in 2018. The written exams have been conducted before and this is the physical fitness examination.

The exam is scheduled to be conducted on January 27 and 28. A total of 49,568 posts are to be filled through this exam. This is the second phase of the recruitment exam.  As per the official notification, the number of candidates shortlisted for document verification will be 2.5 times the number of vacancies.

UP Police Constable phase II admit card: How to download

Step 1: Visit the official website, uppbpb.gov.in
Step 2: On the homepage, click on ‘link to download admit card’ next to ‘recruitment for the post of reserve civil police, reserve PAC scrutiny’
Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page
Step 4: Log-in using credentials
Step 5: Admit card will be available at the dashboard, download

No candidate will be allowed inside the exam hall without a valid admit card and government-approved identity proof. Candidates need to download and take a print out of these admit cards for future use.

UP Police Constable: Salary

Those who clear the document verification round will have to appear for a physical efficiency test (PET). Selected candidates will get a salary in the pay grade of Rs 5200 to Rs 20,200 an additional grade pay of Rs 2000 will also be granted.
