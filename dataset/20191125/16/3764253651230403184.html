Sergio Aguero doubtful for Manchester derby next month due to thigh injury

25 Nov 2019, 16:46

- Sergio Aguero is set to miss Manchester derby next month

- The Argentine striker picked up a thigh injury in City's 2-1 win over Chelsea in tough EPL clash last weekend

- Pep Guardiola confirmed the injury during his post-match conference after the game

Manchester City’s all-time goalscorer Sergio Aguero might miss the Manchester Derby slated for December 7, manager Pep Guardiola has confirmed.

Guardiola confirmed that Aguero picked up an injury in the City’s win against Chelsea on Saturday, November 23, and would be “out for a few weeks”.

The 31-year old hobbled off the game in the 77th minute whilst holding what looked like his groin or upper thigh as City went on to beat the Londoners.
