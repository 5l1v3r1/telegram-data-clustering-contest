‘Schitt’s Creek’ Fans Are Leaving 5 Stars Google Reviews For The Fictional Rosebud Motel In Honour Of The Show’s Final Season

25 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Aynslee Darmon

While the Rosebud Motel isn’t a real place, “Schitt’s Creek” fans are honouring the iconic, fake landmark.

After news that the beloved comedy series would wrap after their sixth season, fans took to Google Maps to leave the best five-star reviews, full of “Schitt’s Creek” references, for the Ontario building where exterior shots of the motels were filmed.

The official Twitter account for the show shared a few of their favourites, writing, “Omg these Google Maps reviews of the ‘Schitt’s Creek’ motel are the best, you sweet, sweet strangers.”
