Dan Osborne slams James Jordan for accusing wife Jacqueline Jossa of being 'fake' on I'm A Celebrity

Jacqueline has had a hard time in the jungle

25 Nov 2019, 16:40 by Rebecca Carter

Dan Osborne has slammed James Jordan for accusing his wife Jacqueline Jossa of being "fake" on I'm A Celebrity.

The former EastEnders actress has had a tough time in the jungle after being voted to do the last couple Bushtucker trials.

Before each trial, Jacqueline has broke down in tears despite going on to absolutely smash them.

Read more: I'm A Celebrity: Jacqueline Jossa fears she'll 'break' if public keep voting for her to do the trials

During last night's episode, former Strictly Come Dancing pro James tweeted: "I’m normally a great judge of character I don’t know if it’s just me but I feel Jacqueline is acting the whole time on @imacelebrity.

"Everything she does seems a little fake & trying to be what she thinks the public want to see. Only my opinion guys."

Dan, who has two daughters with Jacqueline, hit back: "Coming from someone who is married to her and lives with her, she isn’t being fake at all mate & she isn’t acting.

"She is dramatic and can be emotional but she would say that herself... but fake, ABSOLUTELY not."
