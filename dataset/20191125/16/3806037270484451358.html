Take a Look Inside The Kinship Issue

#27 from HYPEBEAST Magazine.

25 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Vanessa Lee

Released last month, HYPEBEAST Magazine Issue 27: The Kinship Issue features a collaborative cover between longtime friends Futura and Jun Takahashi. Take a look inside our print publication’s latest, featuring in-depth conversations with Futura, Jun Takahashi, Cleon Peterson and many more on the unique relationships which shaped their work.

Brothers Arthur and Daniel Chmielewski from HAVEN and the now-defunct intelligence Magazine offer a glimpse into the Canadian brand’s beginnings. Angelo Baque interviews close friends Shaniqwa Jarvis, James Gilchrist from DSMNY, Jon Gray from Ghetto Gastro and June Canedo from Chroma, giving an intimate insight into the creative community of New York City. Along with culinary collective Ghetto Gastro, New York-based label Goodfight, and the self-proclaimed “world’s first teen movie studio” Illegal Civ, HYPEBEAST Magazine Issue 27: The Kinship Issue is a nod to our adopted families and the ones we call our kin.

HYPEBEAST Magazine Issue 27: The Kinship Issue is now available at HBX.com along with selected fashion retailers, bookshops and newsstands worldwide.
