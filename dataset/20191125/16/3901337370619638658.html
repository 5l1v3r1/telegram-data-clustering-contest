Modise's High Praise For Ex-Chiefs Midfielder

25 Nov 2019, 16:56 by SL Social

As one of the greatest footballers South Africa has ever produced, Teko Modise recently heaped praise on a former Kaizer Chiefs player. Did you see who?

Amakhosi took on Maritzburg United in a Telkom Knockout semi-final clash this past weekend, with the Glamour Boys succumbing to a 2-1 loss on the day.

The fixture also saw one of Chiefs' former players take to the field and inspire the Team of Choice to victory, much to Modise's delight.

The player in question is Keagan Buchanan, and the former Bloemfontein Celtic star pulled the strings in the middle of the park for the Pietermaritzburg outfit.

After the match, Modise took to Instagram Stories to post an image of the player known as "The Fixer" along with a caption that read: "As they say, form temporary, class is permanent. Great to see him do what he loves. The comeback from setback is well-documented."
