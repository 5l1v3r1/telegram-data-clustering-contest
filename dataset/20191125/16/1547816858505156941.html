Anupam Kher Talks About The Horrific 26/11 Attack, Taj Chef Hemant Oberoi And Hotel Mumbai- EXCLUSIVE

25 Nov 2019, 16:35 by Vickey Lalwani

As Anupam Kher's film The Hotel Mumbai is nearing its release date, we caught up with the actor for a quick chat about the film, his character of Hemant Oberoi and also the horrific 26/11 terror attack that hit Mumbai

Anupam Kher’s rich repertoire of films over the past few decades proves that he’s unstoppable with his fab performances. As his upcoming film Hotel Mumbai is slated to release on Nov 29, we interact with the actor to know what went into its making.

Hotel Mumbai is based on the 2008 Mumbai attacks that happened at Taj Mahal Palace hotel. It ain’t easy to recreate a dastardly act done by terrorists that still sends shivers down our spine. And for Anupam Kher too, it wasn’t easy to immediately say a yes when he was approached for the film by Australian filmmaker Anthony Maras.

But, he was blown away by the director’s research and gave a go ahead. Anupam Kher essays the real life role of Taj Hotel Chef, Hemant Oberoi in the film. Speaking about his character, the actor said, “I'm the only person who is playing a real character Hemant Oberoi who was part of the tragedy. He was the chef at that time.” Well, the other characters in the film are fictional creations like Dev Patel plays the role of Arjun, a fictional character who’s working under Hemant Oberoi at the hotel.
