Louis Tomlinson supports Vinnie Jones after X Factor: Celebrity exit

25 Nov 2019, 16:36

Louis Tomlinson lent his support to Vinnie Jones after the actor lost out on a place in the X Factor: Celebrity final.

Saturday’s semi-final saw a star-studded audience watch the likes of Megan McKenna and girl group V5.

Louis, a former X Factor judge himself, was spotted leaving the show with Vinnie and mucking about in front of people stood outside.

The singer was in good spirits as stopped to take pictures with fans, and posed next to the former footballer who had his arm around Louis’ shoulders.

After his exit from the live shows, Vinnie thanked Simon for helping him get through a difficult time after the death of his wife.

In a video shared on social media, the Snatch star said:‘Been a fantastic journey, absolutely enjoyed it,’ Vinnie began. ‘Simon, thank you so much for getting me through my grief, helping me a hell of a lot with it. ‘I will always be indebted to you my friend. Thank you very much.’

He added: ‘All the fans out there, I’ve had a terrific time.’

Megan and V5 also had big smiles, understandable as they are both now X Factor: Celebrity finalists along with Max and Harvey and Jenny Ryan.

Love Island stars Megan Barton Hanson and Georgia Steel were also seen leaving the live shows in London.

The main man himself Simon Cowell and his girlfriend Lauren Silverman headed out in the night together following the show.

He must be feeling pretty pleased with himself having two acts – V5 and musical.ly personalities Max and Harvey – in the grand final.

Louis and Nicole are still in with a fighting chance as their respective acts, Megan and Jenny, will be singing to win on Saturday night.

Last Saturday Try Star were also sent home, made up of rugby players Thom Evans, Ben Foden and Levi Davis.

The X Factor: Celebrity final is Saturday night on ITV at 7.10pm.
