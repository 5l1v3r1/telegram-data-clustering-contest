Hiplife musician Nana Kontihene survives terrible accident

25 Nov 2019, 16:21 by Lizbeth Brown

Veteran Ghanaian hiplife musician Emmanuel Nana Appiah Boateng popularly known as Nana Kontihene has been involved in a gory accident.

The “Aketesea” hitmaker in an Instagram post revealed how he had a dream about the accident.

According to Nana Kontihene, he had a dream about a woman he knew chasing him which he informed his pastors about to pray against it.

He shared pictures of the accident on Instagram with the caption; “I had a dream few days ago, I saw this woman that I know and she was chasing me in the dream with two others. This revelation came around 4 am – I quickly gave her name out to all the prophets that God has surround me with. We fast and wage warfare prayers. Just 7 days after the fast a strange woman chased me physically on a highway and cut in my lane. I wasn’t moved nor scared…
I have escaped the fowler’s trap and the snare has been broken. Psalm 124:7 ???. I declare and degree as an Oracle of God, on this day, I exchange her soul for the evil scheme she’s been plotting against my life #kingdom“
