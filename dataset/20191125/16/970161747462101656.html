Woman 'groped by Prince Andrew at Epstein's home' pictured for first time in dec

25 Nov 2019, 16:32

A woman who claims Prince Andrew groped her breast at Jeffrey Epstein’s home has been pictured for the first time in 10 years.

Johanna Sjoberg, 39, alleged that the Duke of York touched her chest at the convicted paedophile’s mansion in New York when she was 21.

She is one of only two women who have claimed to have sexual contact with the prince.

It is believed she is now willing to testify under oath against the duke.

A source told The Mirror that her testimony over ‘how she was recruited and abused’ by Epstein is ‘vital’ to the agents working on the case.

They continued: ‘They are ­determined all those who enabled [Epstein’s] crimes will face justice. It is a promise they made to the victims.

‘Andrew has had serious allegations made against him and agents are determined that a person’s standing in life does not make them immune from being ­questioned.’

Ms Sjoberg’s allegations were part of a defamation case brought by Virginia Giuffre against Ghislaine Maxwell.

It was claimed that Epstein had flown both Ms Sjoberg and Ms Giuffre, then Roberts, to Manhattan to help entertain Andrew in 2001.

The girls claimed they presented the prince with a Spitting Image puppet of himself, with Ms Maxwell then putting the dummy’s hand on Ms Giuffre’s breast.

Ms Sjoberg alleges that Andrew then touched her breast while she was sat on his knee.

When asked for comment, Ms Sjoberg stated that her lawyer is currently handling her case.

The Duke of York is now standing back from all of the organisations of which he is a patron, following a fierce backlash from his Newsnight interview.

Andrew faced criticism for showing a lack of empathy towards Epstein’s victims, at one point describing his former friend’s actions as ‘unbecoming’.

He has also been stripped of his £249,000 Sovereign Grant ‘salary’ and all duties after the disastrous interview.

It has also been reported that the Queen cancelled a party she was to host for Andrew’s 60th birthday on February 19.
