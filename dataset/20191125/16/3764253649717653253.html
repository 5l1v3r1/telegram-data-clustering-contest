BBNaija Tacha shares photo from her first ever photo-shoot at age 17

25 Nov 2019, 16:29

- One of BBNaija 2019 most talked about housemates, Tacha Akide, is in the news again

- The 23-year-old recently got tongues wagging after she shared a photo from her first ever photo-shoot at the age of 17

- Tacha’s appearance looked very different from how her fans see her now

On the BBNaija 2019 reality TV show, one of the housemates, Natacha ‘Tacha’ Akide, knew how to get Nigerians reacting on social media with her controversial ways.

The 23-year-old no doubt earned herself the title of one of the most talked about housemates on the show and even after her disqualification, she has still got people reacting on social media.

Just recently on Twitter, Tacha shared a throwback photo of herself as she said that it was from her first ever photo-shoot and was taken when she was just 17 years old.

Well, from the photo, it is obvious that the young lady’s looks have changed drastically as she appeared way darker and younger than she is now.
