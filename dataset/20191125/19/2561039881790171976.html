Apple Posts First Look Featurette for 'Truth Be Told' [Video]

25 Nov 2019, 19:35 by iClarified

Apple has posted a first look featurette for 'Truth Be Told', an original series coming soon to Apple TV+.

When new evidence compels podcaster Poppy Parnell (Octavia Spencer) to reopen the murder case that made her a national sensation, she comes face to face with Warren Cave (Aaron Paul), the man she may have mistakenly helped to put behind bars. Her investigation navigates urgent concerns about privacy, media and race.

Truth Be Told is created by Nichelle Tramble Spellman and stars Octavia Spencer, Aaron Paul, Lizzy Caplan, Elizabeth Perkins, Michael Beach, Mekhi Phifer, Tracie Thoms, Haneefah Wood and Ron Cephas Jones. It will air December 6th, 2019.
