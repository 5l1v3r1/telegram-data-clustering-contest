Hardap, //Kharas to share 12 cars to conduct elections

25 Nov 2019, 19:36

The //Kharas and Hardap regions only have 12 cars to use during the elections for more than 100 000 people on Wednesday.
The Electoral Commission of Namibia's regional co-ordinator in Hardap says the commission will use private vehicles rented from individuals to address the shortfall.
