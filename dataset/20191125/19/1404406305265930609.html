Tekashi 6ix9ine Texas Assault Case Reportedly Dismissed

25 Nov 2019, 19:44 by VladTV

TMZ reports that Tekashi 6ix9ine's assault charge stemming from a 2018 incident where 6ix9ine choked Santiago Albarran at a mall for filming him without his permission. On Monday, a Texas judge threw out the case completely after citing several reasons, which are not surprising. 

For starters, Albarran told the judge to drop the case during the initial court hearing and later posed in a photo with Tekashi after the two left court. Lastly, the judge cited 6ix9ine's current legal situation that has him federal custody.
