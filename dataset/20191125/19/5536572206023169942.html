Cowboys Fans Beg Team to Fire Jason Garrett After Patriots Loss (TWEETS)

25 Nov 2019, 19:14 by Darrelle Lincoln

Cowboys owner Jerry Jones was rightfully disgusted that his team lost yet another game against a team above .500 and he made sure to call out the coaching staff for more blunders that has cost them 2 games in the last 3 weeks against quality opponents.

Jerry is unlikely to part ways with head coach Jason Garrett during the 2019 season, and that alone has the fan base clawing its eyes out.

Following the 13-9 loss to the Patriots on Sunday, Cowboys fans took to Twitter and called for the team to part ways with Garrett by starting a #FireGarrett hashtag.
