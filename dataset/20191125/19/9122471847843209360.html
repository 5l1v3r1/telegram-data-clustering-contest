Urban Meyer Releases Thank You Video For Ohio State Fans

25 Nov 2019, 19:33 by Matt Hladik

Urban Meyer returned to Ohio State for last weekend’s game against Penn State. Meyer is part of FOX’s “Big Noon Kickoff” pregame show.

Naturally, the Buckeye fans welcomed their former head coach with open arms. Meyer returned the favor over the weekend and today on Twitter.

This afternoon, Meyer thanked the OSU fans who turned out and provided the atmosphere for he and his colleagues on Saturday.

“On behalf of the @CFBONFOX Big Noon Kickoff team, THANKS for your hospitality. You guys are the best. Come see us in Ann Arbor,” Meyer wrote.
