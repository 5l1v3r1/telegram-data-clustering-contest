Spoilers: Shady Al's secret exposed after brutal Emmerdale attack

25 Nov 2019, 19:26

Emmerdale has confirmed that shady businessman Al Chapman (Michael Wildman) is out of his depth and owes a lot of money to some very dangerous people – and now that Dawn Taylor (Olivia Bromley) has overheard an incriminating exchange, she could expose him to Kim Tate (Claire King) and also Billy Fletcher (Jay Kontzle).

Al has been on edge recently and taking his frustrations out on Billy and, after he was attacked from behind with a metal pipe and left out cold, he told Jai Sharma (Chris Bisson) and Dawn that Billy must have been responsible.

Kim was wary about being connected with Al as she feared he was a liability but as he insisted that this was a family member but also refused to involve the police, Dawn waded in and confronted a shocked Billy.

It soon became clear that Billy had no idea what she was talking about and, determined to get to the bottom of Al’s deceit, she followed him to the scrapyard, where he was sitting in his car, looking anxious.
