Lisa Fowler (Lucy Benjamin) is showing signs of going dramatically off the rails again in the wake of Mel Owen (Tamzin Outhwaite)’s death.

And with Lisa determined to learn the truth of what happened to her friend, her daughter Louise is becoming concerned for her, especially after she becomes concerned over whether she is taking her medication.
