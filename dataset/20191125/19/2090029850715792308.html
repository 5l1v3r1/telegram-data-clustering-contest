Davido pictured with J.Cole, Trey Songz and little Noah (Photos)

25 Nov 2019, 19:56 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

DMW boss, Davido is without doubt living his best life as he keeps popping up in the news for good reasons. From goodwill to having his photo on the New York Times square to breaking record with his recently released album. David Adeleke is in the news again for the right reasons.

The Nigerian singer, was recently pictured posing with American superstars, J.Cole and Trey Songz, who also brought his 7-month old son, Noah to the Madison Square Garden

Davido was at the Madison Square Garden where he watched the Knicks vs Nets basketball game when he met the entertainers and they all posed together for a picture.
