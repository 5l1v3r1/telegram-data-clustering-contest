Urban Meyer Named Favorite To Land The Dallas Cowboys Job

25 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Andrew Holleran

Urban Meyer addressed the Dallas Cowboys job earlier this fall during an appearance on Colin Cowherd’s radio show. The former Ohio State head coach said it was the kind of job you almost have to take if you’re offered it.

“That’s the New York Yankees, that’s the Dallas Cowboys, that’s the one. Great city, you got Dak Prescott, you got Zeke Elliott, you got a loaded team, and I can’t speak for [Lincoln Riley] obviously—I hate to even speculate—because I don’t know him, that’s really not fair, but to me, that’s the one job in professional football that you kind of say, ‘I gotta go do that.’”

Meyer said he would consider an offer if he got one.

“Absolutely. Absolutely,” he said. “That one? Yes.”

That was a month ago. Fast forward to the present and Urban Meyer coaching the Dallas Cowboys might be a legitimate possibility.

Jason Garrett likely needs to reach the NFC Championship Game or better to stick around in 2020. Jerry Jones made it very clear after Sunday’s loss in New England that he’s not happy.
