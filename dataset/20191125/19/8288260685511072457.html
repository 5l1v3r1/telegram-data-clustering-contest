Wolves boss Nuno Espirito Santo tipped to replace Unai Emery at Arsenal

The pressure has been mounting on the Gunners boss following a disappointing run of form

25 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Chris Watson

Wolverhampton Wanderers boss Nuno Espirito Santo has emerged as the bookies' favourite to become the next Arsenal manager.

Pressure has been mounting on current Gunners chief Unai Emery following a run of only two league wins in 11 Premier League games.

Arsenal were fortunate to grab a last-gasp equaliser against struggling Southampton at the Emirates Stadium on Saturday.

The 2-2 draw left them eighth in the table, 19 points behind leaders Liverpool.

With Emery's future apparently in the balance, the bookmakers have been taking bets on who might replace him at the helm.

And Wolves fans will not be pleased to learn that, at the time of writing, Nuno has been installed as the 6/4 favourite by Sky Bet .

Former Arsenal player Mikel Arteta is next on the list at 7/2, followed by Massimiliano Allegri and Rafa Benitez at 5/1.

Mauricio Pochettino, sacked by Tottenham Hotspur last week, is at 6/1, with Freddie Ljungberg 8/1.
