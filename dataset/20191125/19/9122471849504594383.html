Michigan State Football Legend Has Passed Away At 85

25 Nov 2019, 19:03 by Andrew Holleran

Michigan State’s football program announced some unfortunate news this afternoon, as an all-time Spartans legend has passed away.

Henry Bullough, an iconic figure in the history of the Michigan State program, has died at 85.

“We are deeply saddened to learn that MSU Athletics Hall of Famer Henry Bullough has passed away. The Spartan football family has lost a truly great Spartan. We send our sincere condolences to the Bullough family,” Michigan State football announced.

Bullough was a starting lineman for Michigan State in the 1950s. He led the Spartans to a Rose Bowl win in 1954.
