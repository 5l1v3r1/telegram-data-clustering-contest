Archer claims he was subjected to 'racial insults' from a fan during NZ Test

25 Nov 2019, 19:47 by Julia Montesano

England paceman Jofra Archer has claimed he was subjected to racial abuse by a member of the crowd while at the crease on Day 5 of his side’s Test loss to New Zealand.

Archer, who made 30 during England’s second innings as the visitors went down to the Black Caps by an innings and 65 runs, claimed he was the victim of racist taunts.

While he praised the majority of the supporters at Mount Maunganui, Archer called out one particular member of the crowd on Twitter.
