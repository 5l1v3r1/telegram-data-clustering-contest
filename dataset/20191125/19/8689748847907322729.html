Before I Quit Smoking I Used To Smoke 4 Packs A Day – Hammer Discloses

25 Nov 2019, 19:32 by vanessa nordzi

Renowned Ghanaian song producer and CEO of A1 bread, Edward Nana Poku Osei also popularly known as Hammer has revealed the number of cigarette packs he used to smoke before he quit smoking.

According to Hammer, his business partner in the bread business, Kwaku Boateng helped him quit the hazardous act.

Hammer revealed that he quit smoking in 2014 after encountering several health issues.

Hammer disclosed in a recent interview that he started smoking since first year in PRESEC (Presbyterian Boys’ Secondary School) and it was difficult for him to quit.

“I was deteriorating. I woke up tired each morning instead of being rejuvenated and this was constant.” Hammer recounted.

Hammer further revealed that he used to smoke at least 4 packs a day. He however advised  the youth to not indulge in the dangerous act.

“I wouldn’t be here if I were still smoking”, he reiterated.
