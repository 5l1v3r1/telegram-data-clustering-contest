Man crushed to death on theme park ride at Berlin Christmas market

25 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Ruairi Scott Byrne

A man has been crushed to death by a rollercoaster in a tragic incident at Berlin Christmas market.

The incident was reportedly witnessed by a family-of-four who had been riding the rollercoaster when the accident occurred just after 7pm on Saturday.

Fire Department spokesman Jens-Peter Wilker said the man is believed to have been a market employee and was working when he was hit by the carriage and crushed beneath it.

German broadcaster Deutsche Welle reports the man died at the scene after several attempts to resuscitate him.

The Berlin Spectator said several visitors suffered post-traumatic stress disorder and received treatment.

Counselling has also been offered to those affected.
