Media personality Stephanie Coker welcomes first child with husband

25 Nov 2019, 19:55

- Popular TV personality, Stephanie Coker, has joined the leagues of new mothers in the entertainment industry

- The celebrity has welcomed her first child with her husband, Olumide Aderinokun

- Stephanie shared a video showing different snaps of her baby bump as fans trooped to congratulate her

Being a mother is something many ladies look forward to as they wonder if they would be able to rise up to the task and raise a little human to the best of their abilities.

Despite all these fears it is still a moment many ladies hope to experience and media personality, Stephanie Coker, has now welcomed her own bundle of joy.

Stephanie and her husband, Olumide Aderinokun, recently welcomed the birth of their first child together to the joy of fans on social media.

The news broke out after the media personality shared a short video showing various snaps of her heavily pregnant. Also, film maker, Kemi Adetiba confirmed the news of the birth with her comment under the post.
