XFL Reportedly Blocked NFL Team From Signing Their QB

25 Nov 2019, 19:07 by Alek Arend

The XFL is getting ready to kick things off in late winter of 2020. The football league will try and garner a viewership while the NFL and college football are in the off-season. It’ll certainly be interesting to see how many tune in to watch the new football league.

While the XFL doesn’t get started until next year, the league is already starting to try and compete with the NFL.

With Lions QB Matthew Stafford dealing with a back injury, the Detroit Lions are trying to find a quarterback to help out with depth issues. The Lions reportedly attempted to sign XFL quarterback Josh Johnson, but the XFL has blocked the Lions from doing so.
