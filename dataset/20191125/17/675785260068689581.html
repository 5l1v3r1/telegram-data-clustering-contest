Premier Inn's latest sale has rooms for £35 and under for the Christmas period

Premier Inn has launched a sale on December stays including the Christmas period with rooms going for £35 or less across heaps of its UK hotels

25 Nov 2019, 17:25 by Julie Delahaye, https://www.facebook.com/juliedjourno

Can't quite face another air bed on the floor as you stay with relatives this Christmas ?

Then you might want to check out Premier Inn 's latest sale. That's because the hotel chain has slashed prices on 100,000 rooms to £35 or less for December stays - including the Christmas dates.

The company is offering up the cheap rooms for stays between the 1st December 2019 through to Monday 6th January 2020, so it's perfect if you're planning to be away from home for Christmas or even looking for somewhere to stay for New Year's Eve.

Oh, or of course it can also be a great excuse to book in a mini break to explore some of the UK's best Christmas markets .

The deals are already live on the Premier Inn website but they are subject to availability, so if you do spot the right stay for you it's worth booking in to secure the deal.

There are hotels to be found in destinations from Birmingham to Glasgow, while the sale also includes hotels in London with rooms for £79 and under up for grabs.

There are two rates you can opt for; there's the Non-Flex rate which is often the best value, but if you do need a bit of flexibility, then you may want to opt for the Flex Rate option (prices may vary).

Don't be disheartened if you can't quite make the offer work for you.

We've rounded up the best Premier Inn deals and discounts that need to be on your radar no matter what time of the year you're looking to book - and our guide also includes a few top tips on how to bag the best deals.

For example, families may want to make use of the hotel chain's Kids Stay and eat Breakfast for Free offer , in which kids aged up to 16 years old can stay for free in Premier Inn hotels.

That's not the only saving to be had for parents. In fact, for every adult who orders a full Premier Inn Breakfast or Meal Deal, up to two under 16s can eat breakfast for free, to help you stretch that budget.
