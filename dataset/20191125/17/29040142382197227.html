Get more daily use out of your Galaxy S9 or S9+

If you find that you Galaxy S9 or S9 Plus just isn't lasting as long as you need it to, a battery case is a great solution so that you don't have to stop and wait for it to charge. There's a good number of options available, and you'll want to consider the different battery capacities available and how each will affect the physical size and shape of your phone.

The ZeroLemon Slim Power is the best battery case for most people, since it's fairly affordable and can reliably charge your phone at least once fully. If bulkiness is no issue and you want the most extra battery life that money can buy, you should absolutely get the ZeroLemon ZeroShock case that offers a whopping 8,000mAh of juice.

However, if you've gone through this list and decided that you don't want a battery case after all, there are plenty of other caes for both the Galaxy S9 and the S9+ that are available.
