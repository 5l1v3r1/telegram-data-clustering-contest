T.I. claims he 'exaggerated' about checking daughter's hymen

25 Nov 2019, 17:19

T.I. has now backtracked on his controversial admission that he takes his daughter Deyjah to the doctor to have her hymen checked, claiming he ‘exaggerated’ the story.

The 39-year-old, real name Clifford Harris Jr, sparked outrage when he said that he took his 18-year-old daughter to the gynaecologist every year to check whether her hymen was still intact.

It was his way of making sure Deyjah hadn’t had sex.

But in his new Red Table Talk interview with Jada Pinkett Smith, T.I. says his words were ‘misconstrued’.

‘I came to clear up any misconceptions there have been surrounding how we parent and what is appropriate and inappropriate,’ T.I. explained to Jada, Willow Smith and Adrienne Banfield-Jones.

‘I think all of this comes from a conversation I was having in a very joking manner when asked how I deal with parenting in this day and age.’

The Live Your Life rapper continued: ‘From a place of truth, I began to embellish and exaggerate and I think a lot of people took it extremely literal because if you put any of my reputation, who I am as a father, who I’ve been, I honestly thought people knew me better than that.’
