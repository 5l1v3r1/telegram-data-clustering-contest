GOT7 open up about their hardships and growth as a group in interview with StudioK

25 Nov 2019, 17:37 by greenbird

StudioK released an interview with GOT7 where the members got the opportunity to open up about their hardships and growth as a group.

The veteran group is known to be really funny and energetic but they explained how it has been hard for them since their debut with Yugyeom saying ''Our path wasn't easy for us. We walked step by step with our fans (whether we are suffering or happy), so I think our story is not just joyful and super bright, it's more like we've grown up together''.

They also sang some parts of the songs 'You Calling My Name' and 'Thursday' in acapella and explained the meaning behind the lyrics.
