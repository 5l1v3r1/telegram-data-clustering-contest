How to shop at John Lewis on Black Friday

If you're not brave enough to elbow your way through the crowds in store, don't worry: you can get the same (and often, better) deals by shopping online from the comfort of your bed. 

But it's not quite as easy as it sounds. Systems can time out, and you can lose out on deals if you're not fast enough. Below, therefore, are our top tips for shopping online at John Lewis on Black Friday.

  • Get online early. Last year, the busiest shopping hour on the website was between 6am and 7am on Black Friday itself, so you might need to set your alarm a little earlier if you want to get the best deals.
  • Use your Bookmarks. It's worth adding your wish-list products to your Bookmarks (if you create a new folder, this will make your life even easier). Then, on the day, you can quickly find out whether your dream purchases are on sale.
  • Register as a member. It can be worth registering as a member before Black Friday, as you will get email alerts from the retailer (which could include any deals from that day) and it will speed up your check-out process.
  • Follow on social media. If you're poised for flash deals, you may want to follow John Lewis on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram so you can find out about new deals first.
  • Read The Telegraph's live coverage. We will be publishing our pick of the best Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals as soon as they become available, so be sure to follow our Black Friday page for live updates.
