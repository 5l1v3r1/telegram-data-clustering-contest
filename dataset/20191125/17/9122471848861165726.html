The Week 4 AP Poll Top 25 Has Been Released

25 Nov 2019, 17:13 by Andrew Holleran

As the 2019 college football season heads into the final week of the regular season, the college basketball season is starting to heat up. Several big-time Thanksgiving Week tournaments are taking place this week, including the Maui Invitational, which begins this afternoon.

There’s been a shakeup inside the top five, as Maryland has jumped North Carolina, which has struggled in recent contests.
