Watch: Stormtroopers ‘Fly’ in New ‘Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker’ Clip

25 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Genevieve Scarano

Rey and the gang are fleeing from a group of “high-tech” stormtroopers in a new Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker clip.

When Rey, Poe, Finn, C-3PO, Chewbacca, and BB-8 are on the run in the desert, a bunch of stormtroopers detect the “fugitives,” and they immediately go after them. This time, the squad of stormtroopers has jetpacks…and that could be a MAJOR problem for Rey & Co.
