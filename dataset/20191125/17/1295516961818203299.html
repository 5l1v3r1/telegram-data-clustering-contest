Lil Nas X Knows You Think He Dressed like a Jojo Character for the AMAs

25 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Jacoby Bancroft

The American Music Awards aired Sunday night, and although artist Lil Nas X won for Favorite Rap/Hip-Hop Song for his hit "Old Town Road (Remix)," it was his bold, bright fashion choice that has most people talking.

With a lime green tuxedo and a zebra-print top with matching green gloves, it wasn't long before Twitter users started drawing comparisons between Lil Nas X's outfit and JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, the popular anime series with a very distinct color palette and style.
