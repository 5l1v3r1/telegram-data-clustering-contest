Nintendo Switch Bundles and Deals

The Switch dominated a good portion of the Black Friday sales last year, and came out as one of the best sellers on sites like Currys PC World. We're already starting to see a few Switch deals appear, with plenty of bundle offers already available.

The new edition to the Switch family is the Switch Lite, a slimmer, less expensive version of the standard Switch, and one of the best handheld systems to date. Lacking any docking capabilities, the Switch Lite is purely a handheld system, and a great one at that.

At only £199, getting this console for any cheaper would be a great deal, and we'll have to wait and see what happens. If you do see either console discounted, snap it up quick, because stocks never last for long.

Xbox One Bundles and Deals

UPDATE: Xbox UK recently announced that Black Friday deals on the Xbox Store will start on November 22.

Xbox One hasn't had the easiest ride in this console generation, but has made back some ground with the Xbox One S and One X. The Xbox One X is the most powerful consoles on the market, with stunning HDR and 4K capabilities, while the Xbox One S is a great entry point and very cheap at this stage in the console cycle, normally selling for less than £225 on most sites.

As previously mentioned, this will be the last year that Microsoft focuses on the Xbox One in Black Friday. Sure, next year the console will definitely get plenty of discounts and will be incredibly cheap, but the focus will be on Project Scarlett.

Microsoft has also released its new Xbox One Elite Controller in November, so the older model may get a discount on Black Friday. Xbox also has its very own 'Design Lab' for its controllers, so there could be a deal on the cards there as well.

PlayStation 4 Bundles and Deals

Sony has recently released details of the next PlayStation console, and it will be called PlayStation 5. Sony, unlike Microsoft, like to keep it simple.

Similarly to Microsoft however, this will be the last year that the PS4 gets any focus during the Black Friday sales. It will likely be even cheaper next year, but that's simply because the PS5 will be getting all the attention.

PlayStation has less consoles to decide from in comparison to Xbox, and you can either pick up the PS4 Slim or the PS4 Pro at the moment. The Pro has 4K capabilities, and is only slightly less powerful than the Xbox One X. Specs aside, the Pro is a great console will plenty of incredible games to back it up.

We expect more bundle deals will occur during Black Friday, and we could see the DualShock 4 controllers get partnered with games, or discounted alone.
