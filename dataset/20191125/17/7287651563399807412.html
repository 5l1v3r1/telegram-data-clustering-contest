AMD Ryzen 9 3950X Processor available starting today (16c/32t)

25 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Hilbert Hagedoorn

You've been able to read our Ryzen 9 3950X processor review last week already, however, that was the announcement followed by the reviews. The availability of what I deem one the nicest processors of 2019 starts today.  

Man, that's a lot of AMD news today. It is (very) fast, feisty, agile and albeit a lot of money, affordable. Ryzen 9 3950X (16c/32t) is priced at 749 USD, you can read our 28 PAGE review here.

-- AMD -- The Ryzen 9 3950X, available for sale today, brings 16-cores and AMD’s industry-leading 7nm process technology together to provide gamers and enthusiasts with the fastest consumer CPU on the market. The 3950X offers exceptional single- and multi-threaded performance for a unique mix of HEDT and consumer-friendly value, delivering incredible gaming experiences and powerful performance for encoding, editing and more.
