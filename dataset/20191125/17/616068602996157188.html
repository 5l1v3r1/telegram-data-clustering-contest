House fire claims life

25 Nov 2019, 17:09 by Jon Manchester

A house fire in Surrey claimed the life of an elderly woman Monday morning.

Flames tore through the two-storey home on 135A Street.

After dousing the blaze, firefighters found the woman on the upper floor of the home, CTV News reports. Paramedics pronounced her dead at the scene.

The cause of the fire is under investigation.
