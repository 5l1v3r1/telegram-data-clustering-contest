Iceland's Luxury Christmas range unveiled - including whole lobsters for £5.50

From whole Canadian lobsters, to clams, and sprouts with blue cheese and bacon, Iceland has unveiled what's in their Luxury Christmas range for 2019 - and we want it all

25 Nov 2019, 17:23 by Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/mollyjaholt/

Christmas isn't really Christmas without a huge festive feast, and if you're wanting to indulge in the crème de la crème of Christmas food... wait until you see what Iceland has in store.

From fancy whole lobsters and clams, to mouth-watering rump steak joints, and an array of stunning desserts, Iceland has just unveiled its Luxury Christmas 2019 range - and we want absolutely everything.

We've picked out some of our favourite items from the luxurious selection below, with some things already available on the Iceland website and in stores.

Despite prices start from just £1 and most of the range being frozen, it wouldn't look out of place at a restaurant, making it perfect for those that want a slightly more swanky Christmas to impress their friends and family with.

Luxury Canadian Whole Cooked Lobster, £5.49

If you want a fish platter to impress, things don't get much more extravagant than an entire lobster - the perfect Christmas centrepiece.

Plus, it's very easy to serve, as you won't need to do much to it a part from pairing it with a selection of dipping sauces and some lemons for that extra zing.

Luxury Wholeshell clams with a Chilli & Garlic butter, £3

Full of flavour yet exceedingly fancy, serve these up as a simple starter by pairing them with some nicely toasted bread, or add them to pasta for a super easy dinner once all your guests have gone home on Boxing Day.

Iceland Luxury 12 Month Matured Christmas Pudding, £4

Taking top spot in Good Housekeeping's annual Christmas taste test, Iceland's £4 Christmas pudding beat the likes of M&S and Waitrose.

The traditional offering was applauded for its heady festive spices, and almond and pecan pieces, containing brandy, sherry AND cognac.

Luxury Sprouts with Chestnuts, Bacon, Stilton & Gorgonzola, £2.50

Just because it's a side dish, doesn't mean it should get less attention, with these brussel sprouts surely convincing even the most avid of haters thanks to their diced chestnut, smoked bacon and Blue Stilton and Gorgonzola sauce, all topped off with breadcrumbs.

Luxury Cod Lion Wrapped in Bacon, £10

For those opting for a fish main or something a little different for their Boxing Day buffet, this intriguing Mediterranean number is a real centrepiece. The sweet cod is complimented by a layer of salty bacon, before being drizzled with an olive oil and parsley dressing to maximise the flavours.

Iceland Luxury Five Gold Rings, £7 (was £8)

On the fifth day of Christmas, my true love gave to me... five gold rings (in pudding form).

A must-have for chocolate lovers alongside the obligatory Christmas pudding, this show-stopping dessert features a dark chocolate gold shell, filled with a Belgian dark chocolate mousse, salted caramel sauce and a dark chocolate cookie base and of course, some dark chocolate pouring sauce to finish. Wow.

Luxury British Easy Carve Extra Tasty Turkey Crown, £16

What’s Christmas without a turkey? And Iceland have done half the work for you with this huge 2kg option, which is already infused with seasoning to enhance the full flavour of the meat for the most succulent main.

Opting for a boneless crown instead of the full shebang also allows feasters to easily carve and serve, reducing waste and promising everyone gets the best bits.

Luxury Chocolate and Orange Gateau, £8

If you're looking for something that looks like you've made it yourself, you might just about be able to get away with this one.

Featuring a layered chocolate flavoured sponge cake with chocolate and orange flavoured frosting and an orange filling, topped with orange flavoured chocolate segments and chocolate vermicelli. Now just to remember all that before we start telling everyone it's homemade...

Luxury Perfect Aberdeen Angus Rump Joint with Mustard & Peppercorn, £16

While turkey is certainly traditional, it's not everyone's bag, so if you'd prefer to tuck into a prime, tender, juicy beef with a mustard and peppercorn crust instead at Christmas, pop this on the table instead and carve it in front of the guests for some extra flair..

Luxury Lamb Cushion with Mint Stuffing, £12

A must for the Boxing Day spread when nobody can stomach any more turkey, this British lamb shoulder is encased around a lamb, rosemary and mint stuffing, with an extra mint butter, meaning all we need to do is shove it in the oven. Well it is Christmas, after all...
