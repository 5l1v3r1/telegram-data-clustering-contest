Dez Bryant Had A Subtle Rip On Jason Garrett During Cowboys-Patriots Game (TWEET)

25 Nov 2019, 17:50 by Darrelle Lincoln

Dez Bryant took a swipe at Jason Garrett late in the fourth quarter as the Dallas Cowboys tried desperately to come back against the New England Patriots at Gillette Stadium.

Immediately after Garrett elected to kick a field goal instead of going for it on 4th down with about six minutes to go in the game, Bryant took to Twitter and casually ripped Jason Garrett and the entire coaching staff.
