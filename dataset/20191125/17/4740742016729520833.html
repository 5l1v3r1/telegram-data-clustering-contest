How to send your friends free chocolate with Cadbury Secret Santa postal service

The Cadbury bus has rolled into the city centre

25 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Emma Grimshaw

Forget the Coca Cola truck because tonight the Cadbury bus has rolled into Bristol offering chocolate fans a chance to send a surprise gift to a special someone for free.

The Cadbury Secret Santa Postal Service has come to our city as part of its UK tour covering 10 places.

Lines of people are queueing in the city centre, on St Augustine's Parade, for their chance to post a treat.

People can pick between Cadbury Dairy Milk, Cadbury Dairy Milk Whole Nut, Cadbury Dairy Milk Caramel, Cadbury Dairy Milk Fruit and Nut or Cadbury Dairy Milk Oreo.

But you'll have to rush because the service is only there until 7pm tonight (November 25).

A spokesperson said: "Cadbury Secret Santa Postal Services will be springing up to spread festive cheer across the country. Pop along to post a free Cadbury treat to your favourite person at our Cadbury Secret Santa Postal Service."

For anyone who misses out today, if you purchase a Cadbury bar from WHSmith you will be able to post the gift for free.
