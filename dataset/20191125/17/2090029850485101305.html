See reactions as Glory Osei returns to Twitter

25 Nov 2019, 17:25 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Nigerians have taken to the micro-blogging platform, Twitter to express themselves over the return of business woman, Glory Osei on the platform after awhile from the heat of last month’s allegations.

Recall, Osei was in October attacked on social media by some former employees of Divergent and accused alongside Muyiwa Folorunsho of fraud and maltreatment of employees.

At the time, Osei denied the allegations but added that she was responding via an “unofficial press release”.

However, she returned to Twitter on Monday, November 25 to respond fully to her critics.

She began by saying that she thought of making her lengthy write-up into a book, referring to her attackers as a mob.
