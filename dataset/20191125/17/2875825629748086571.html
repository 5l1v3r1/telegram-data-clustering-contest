Nicole Kidman shares rare snap of daughter Sunday Rose as they enjoy family time

The award-winning actress shared a rare glimpse at her family life as she posted a sweet snap of her 11-year-old daughter

25 Nov 2019, 17:12 by Irishmirror.ie

Nicole Kidman shared a rare photo of her 11-year-old daughter Sunday Rose over the weekend.

The Oscar-winning actress, 52, tends to keep her personal life away from social media, but took to Instagram on Sunday to share a glimpse at her spending quality family time with her daughter.

The adorable snap shows the mother and daughter enjoying some quality bonding time during a cold, wintry walk through the park.

The sun appeared to be gleaming through the trees as Nicole and Sunday Rose explored the wintry outdoors and colourful, falling autumnal foliage.

She captioned the sweet photo: "Sunday walk with Sunday #MotherDaughter #Happiness."

Now living in Nashville, Tennessee, Nicole is married to country singer Keith Urban and the pair have two biological daughters - Sunday Rose, 11, and eight-year-old Faith Margaret.

The Australian actress also has two adopted Isabella, now 26, and Connor, now 24, with her ex-husband Tom Cruise .

The 52-year-old also had minimal contact with Isabella, who is now married and living in Croydon, South London.

Nicole married Mission Impossible star Tom in 1990, a year after the pair met on the sets of ‘Days of Thunder’.

They split in 2001, with their adopted children choosing to stay with Tom and within the Scientology faith.

Tom is an outspoken advocate for the Church of Scientology, having become involved with the faith through his first wife, Mimi Rogers.

Nicole last spoke of her two eldest children back in 2018, amid rumours of a rift after she snubbed them in an Emmys acceptance speech thanking her family, when she addressed their commitment to Scientology.

A-lister Nicole has a busy work schedule at the moment with new movie Bombshell set to hit cinemas in December and three other acting projects which Nicole is producing.

Nicole stars in upcoming film Bombshell alongside Margot Robbie and Charlize Theron.

It was recently announced that she will serve as a producer for another Liane Moriarty adaptation, Truly Madly Guilty.
