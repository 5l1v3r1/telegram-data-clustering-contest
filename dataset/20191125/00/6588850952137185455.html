Wedding PHOTOS Of The Man Who Married Three (3) Wives On Same Day

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

A Ghanaian man identified as Osman Hafiz has tied the knots with his 3 fiancees.

The wedding ceremony of Hafiz and his wives Yidana Sikena, Ali Karima and Yakubu Huzaima was held over the weekend (November 23) at Walewale in the North East Region of Ghana. The event reportedly lasted for about an hour and a half.
