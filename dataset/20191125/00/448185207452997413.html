Taurus : (April 21 – May 21)

 

Today you will be getting suitable marriage proposals from persons known to you. You will be initially reluctant but eventually your family will be able to convince you. And you will give your consent. This will make everyone happy and there will be cheer and joy in your family. You will also have plans to settle abroad and will take steps in that directions.
