RGV announces 'Enter The Girl Dragon' teaser, trailer dates - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

25 Nov 2019

Ram Gopal Varma has been silently making a movie besides the political fiction 'Kamma Rajyamlo Kadapa Reddlu'.  An Indo-Chinese action flick, it's titled 'Enter The Girl Dragon'.  Calling it India's first martial arts movie, RGV also says that it is his "most ambitious movie" ever.  

The film's teaser will be released on Bruce Lee’s 80th birth anniversary (November 27 at 3.12 pm).  

On the other hand, the trailer will be out on December 13 in Bruce Lee's hometown Foshan City in China. 

RGV has always been a fan of Bruce Lee.  The film seems to be his tribute to the legend.
