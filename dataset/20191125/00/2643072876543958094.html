President Buhari Meets With UNGA’s President, Muhammad-Bande

25 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

President Muhammadu Buhari and the President of the United Nations General Assembly, Prof Tijjani Muhammad-Bande, met in Abuja on Monday.

Naija News recalls Muhammad-Bande was elected on June 4 as the President of the 74th session of UNGA for a year service term.

The meeting between the duo is taking place at the Presidential Villa, Abuja. This is Muhammad-Bande’s first working visit to Nigeria.
