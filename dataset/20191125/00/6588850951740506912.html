I've No Plans To Fall In Love...I Only Want To Sleep With The Women When I Touch Down In Ghana - Jamaican Dancehall Act

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Popular Jamaican artiste, Kemar Donaldson who is popularly known as Kranium who will be in Ghana for a week this December has sent a special message to Ghanaian women ahead of his visit.

The ‘Nobody Has To Know’ hitmaker who will be performing at Mr Eazi’s DETTY RAVE and AFRONATION in a recent tweet revealed he has no plans to fall in love with any Ghanaian woman but only coming to enjoy their company sexually.
