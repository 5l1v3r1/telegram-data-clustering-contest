Douglas Lima reacts to ‘MVP’s’ walk-off KO, wants to see Page beat ‘ranked opponents’ before he accepts rematch

25 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Lewis Mckeever

Bellator welterweight champ Douglas Lima was impressed with Michael ‘Venom’ Page’s performance last night at Bellator London II, but the Brazilian would like to see ‘MVP’ beat some ranked opponents before he accepts a rematch.
