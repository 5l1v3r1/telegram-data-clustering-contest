Eight Teenagers Have Been Charged Following A Police Chase

25 Nov 2019, 00:45

Eight teenagers will face court following a pursuit in a stolen car at Port Augusta on Saturday.

SAPOL reported on Monday:

About 8pm on Saturday 23 November, Police received reports of a grey BMW SUV doing burnouts in Cook Street and Paringa Road.

Checks on the car’s registration revealed it had been stolen during a house break-in at Marino two days prior.

Police patrols searched the area and spotted the stolen BMW on Stirling Road where they attempted to stop it. However, the driver refused to stop and the car was pursued briefly by patrols before being terminated for safety reasons.

The stolen car was spotted a short time later on Carlton Parade and followed onto Racecourse and Footner Road’s where the car had its tyres spiked.

The car continued driving and was found abandoned on Forster Street a short time later. Patrols searched the area but were unable to locate the occupants.

As a result of investigations, Port Augusta police yesterday (24 November) arrested eight youths over the pursuit.

All eight youths were charged with –

- Two 16-year-old girls from Port Augusta West charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle. Bailed to appear in the Port Augusta Youth Court on 11 December.

- A 15-year-old boy from Elizabeth South charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle, drive dangerously to escape police pursuit and drive unlicenced. Refused bail to appear in Port Augusta Youth Court today.

- A 15-year-old boy from St Agnes charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle, drive dangerously to escape police pursuit and drive unlicenced. Refused bailed to appear in Port Augusta Youth Court today.

- A 14-year-old girl from Glenelg North charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle and drive unlicenced. Refused bailed to appear in Port Augusta Youth Court today.

- A 13-year-old boy both from Glenelg North charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle and drive unlicenced. Refused bailed to appear in Port Augusta Youth Court today.

- A 13-year-old boy from Ingle Farm was charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle, state false details, drive dangerously to escape police pursuit and drive unlicenced. Refused bailed to appear in Port Augusta Youth Court today.

- A 12-year-old boy from Glenelg North charged with illegal use of a motor vehicle. Bailed to appear in the Port Augusta Youth Court on 11 December.

Police would like to thank all road users who call police about Fatal 5 offences they see on the road.
