Former Revenue Collector with Rakiraki Magistrates Court, Saleshni Lata sentenced to 15 months suspended for 2 years

25 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

A former Revenue Collector with the Rakiraki Magistrates Court who is charged with a count each of Obtaining a Gain and Falsification of Documents has been given a suspended sentence by the Lautoka Magistrates Court.

Saleshni Lata received a sentence of 15 months which has been suspended for 2 years.

Lata was charged by FICAC in December 2017

Lata whilst being employed as Revenue Collector at the Rakiraki Magistrates Court, she falsified the Judicial Department Trust Fund receipts and the Maintenance Payment card and as a result, obtained the sum of $100.00 in April 2017.
