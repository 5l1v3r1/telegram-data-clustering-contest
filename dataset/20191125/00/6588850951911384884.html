Prang Manhene Praises Prez Akufo-Addo Over Development

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

The Paramount Chief of Prang Traditional Council in Pru West District in the Bono east region Nana Kwadwo Nyarko III and his chiefs have commended Npp government led by President Akufo-Addo over massive projects at their traditional area.

According to Nana Kwadwo Nyarko III who was Speaking during 2019 Prang Nkyifie yam festival celebration explained that their traditional area have seen massive developmental projects within just two and half years under President Akufo-Addo administration.

He said, their community water supply tank which was abandoned since Gold Coast time is now almost complete by president Akufo-Addo and now the water crisis at the area has gone down.

Moreover, they have also gotten their share of One district, one factory and also Pru west district Administration block is ongoing, planting for food and job has also helped farmers within their traditional area.


He appealed to government to upgrade their health center to fit the status of District hospital since Prang is now the district capital for Pru west.

Again he called on the government to put up more school dormitories and classrooms for Prang SHS to reduce congested.


Afterwards Minister for Employment and labour relations/Mp for Sunyani West Hon. Baffour Awuah who represented President Akufo-Addo also assured the chiefs and the people at Prang that Npp will upgrade their health center to hospital and also put up more school dormetries for Prang SHS to help to reduce accommodation problems facing the school.

He appealed to the chiefs to be always united so that they can develop their new district.
