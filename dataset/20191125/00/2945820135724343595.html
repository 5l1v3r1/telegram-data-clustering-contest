Moto2™ and Moto3™ riders make their debuts in Valencia

After a rain-affected test in Jerez, a handful of riders finally completed laps in their brand-new 2020 colours

25 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

The first day of testing for the Moto2™ and Moto3™ classes in Valencia took place today with a handful of riders in both the intermediate and lightweight classes getting their first opportunity to lap in their new 2020 colours after a rain-affected two-day test at the Circuito de Jerez - Angel Nieto last week.
