Premier League top scorers 19-20

25 Nov 2019 by Aidan Shearer

Jamie Vardy is currently the top scorer in the Premier League after having a fine start.

Jamie Vardy leads the way with 12 goals so far this season. His latest one came in his side's 2-0 away win against Brighton which sees them sit 2nd in the table. He also bagged a hat-trick in the Foxes' 9-0 win over Southampton.

Chelsea's Tammy Abraham is 2nd on the list. He has also had a great start to the season and Frank Lampard has made the most of their transfer ban by playing the youth. He is followed by Sergio Aguero who has 9.

Aubameyang is 4th with 8 goals, whilst leaders Liverpool's top scorer Saido Mané also has also got 8 goals this season.
