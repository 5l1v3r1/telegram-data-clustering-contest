Stonebwoy Flaunts His 2019 AFRIMA Awards Plaque

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Stonebwoy is a proud winner of yet another AFRIMA award 2019. He won in the Best African Reggae Dancehall Artist category.

This is his third time winning this coveted award. Stonebwoy is the ONLY artist to win the Best Reggae Dancehall Artiste of the year more than once.

He set a record last year by winning it more than once and he has now increased it to 3.

His first award was bagged in 2015. The second in 2018 and the third in 2019.

In new photos, Stonebwoy and his supposed new manager were seen proudly showing off the award.
