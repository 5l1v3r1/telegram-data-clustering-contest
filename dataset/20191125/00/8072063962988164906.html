Dennery shopping centre launches first Black Friday Clearance Sale

25 Nov 2019

(PRESS RELEASE) – The Community of Dennery celebrated the opening of it’s first shopping centre, located conveniently along the Castries – Vieux Fort highway earlier this year in January.

The Centre now is celebrating it’s first Black Friday Clearance Sale on the 29th of November 2019 to facilitate new stock arrivals in 2020.

The Centre offers clearance of up to 50% off , opening it doors to the public at 6:00 am on the Friday to benefit those shoppers who would need to return to work the same morning. It is a great opportunity to find amazing Christmas deals and gifts before the arrival of December. The Centre will close at 4pm two hours earlier than the normal closing time. The following day opening hours will resume as normal from 8am to 6pm.

For further details of the sales details please follow on https://www.facebook.com/groocentre/.
