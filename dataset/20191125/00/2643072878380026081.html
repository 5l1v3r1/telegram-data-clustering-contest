PDP Congratulates Atiku On His 73rd Birthday

25 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

The Peoples Democratic Party has sent a congratulatory message to its 2019 presidential candidate, Atiku Abubakar, as he marks his 73rd birthday.

The main opposition party took to its official Twitter account to celebrate the former vice president.

PDP wrote, “We congratulate the former VP of Nigeria and the @OfficialPDPNig Presidential Candidate in the February 23, 2019, Presidential Election, H. E. @atiku, on the occasion of his 73rd Birthday. We wish you good health and many years of service to our fatherland. Happy birthday, Sir”.

Some other notable individuals have sent their congratulatory message to the political leader, the former Anambra state governor, Peter Obi, described Atiku as a great leader who remains a beacon for selflessness and service.
