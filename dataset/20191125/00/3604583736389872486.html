Ballarat man allegedly flees police, found hiding under car in retirement village

25 Nov 2019, 00:44

A 29-year-old Ballarat man has been charged after allegedly fleeing from and assaulting police officers.

Just before 5.30pm on Friday, Dareton Traffic and Highway Patrol Command officers stopped the Ballarat man in a Holden Commodore on Beverley Street in Wentworth - north of Mildura on the NSW/Victoria border - for a random breath test.

He was arrested for an outstanding warrant before allegedly fleeing on foot.

He allegedly ran to a retirement village and damaged property before hiding under a vehicle in the yard. He was then found by a female officer.
