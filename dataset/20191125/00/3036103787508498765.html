PlayStation South Africa unveils Black Friday 2019 deals

25 Nov 2019 by Bradley Prior

PlayStation South Africa has unveiled its Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals for 2019.

These deals will be available from 27 November until 2 December 2019 and include discounts on consoles, games, controllers, and VR equipment.

“Whether it’s for existing fans who are looking to pick up another game or controller, or for newcomers who are looking to join the PlayStation family, or the world of VR, we’ve got you covered,” said PlayStation.

Prospective buyers will be able to purchase these deals from various popular local stores including BT Games, Dion Wired, Game, Hi-Fi Corporation Incredible Connection, Loot, Makro, Raru, and Takealot.
