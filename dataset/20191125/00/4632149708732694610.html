Fake News Buster: Condom hair-tie not from JNU

25 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
With JNU students’ protest against the hostel fee hike being widely discussed on social media, many pictures with false claims are being circulated online. One such picture shows a girl’s hair tied with a condom and has been shared with the claim that the girl in the photo is a protesting JNU student.

SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?

The picture is misleading and was first shared on the web in 2016 and is an old meme. It was clearly not clicked during the ongoing JNU protests.
