Gov't Procures 54,400 Beds For Free SHS

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

A total of 54,445 beds, 108, 890 mattresses and 36,240 staff common room chairs have been procured by the government of Ghana, as part of the implementation of the free Senior High School (SHSH) programme.

This was revealed by the Public Relations Officer for the Ministry of Education, Mr Ekow Vincent Assafuah, in a statement on Monday, 25 November 2019.

The statement said the items are to boost furniture supplies in secondary, technical, vocational and agricultural schools.

The “beneficiary schools include Ejisuman SHS, Achinakrom SHS, Sakafia Islamic SHS, Oppong Memorial SHS, Gyaama Pensan SHTS, Dadease Agric SHS, Bechem Presby SHS”.

“The rest are Odomaseman SHS, Bole SHS, Walewale SHS, Bawku SHTS, Sandema SHTS, Three Town SHS, Aveyime-Battor SHS, Vakpo SHS, Have Technical Institute, Half Assini SHS, among others”.
