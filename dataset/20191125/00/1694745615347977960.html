Camila Cabello Is a Bohemian Princess in Her Oscar de la Renta Dress

25 Nov 2019, 00:43 by Sarah Wasilak

Camila Cabello stepped onto the American Music Awards red carpet in a gorgeous Oscar de la Renta dress with a mesh bustier bodice and sweeping tulle overlay, which was finished with origami-like appliqué. The gown might've been nude, but it was certainly voluminous enough to make a statement. Stars like Selena Gomez and Lizzo were there at the ceremony in shades of neon, and Halsey was essentially a walking rainbow bouquet, but Camila took it upon herself to prove color isn't everything, and it certainly isn't required to look utterly romantic. We're sure her SO, Shawn Mendes, who arrived in his own stylish outfit, took serious notice. Scroll to check out Camila's full outfit details, from her Djula hoop earrings to her gold platform shoes. Also, can someone tap Camila to star in a new Disney movie stat? It's almost as if she's the bohemian princess who never was — but should be.
