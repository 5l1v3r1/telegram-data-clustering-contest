The three teams who are after Mauricio Pochettino

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The extensive, historic and successful period of Mauricio Pochettino as Tottenham manager came to an end and Jose Mourinho took over as the new coach of the English side. Teams of the size of Real Madrid, Bayern Munich and Arsenal have thought about the Argentine on more than one occasion.

It is not a secret for anyone that Real Madrid have thought on more than one occasion about the possibility of signing Mauricio Pochettino as coach. In fact, many of the media outlets assure that Real Madrid launched an offer for the Argentine, but they were rejected as Pochettino pledged to stay on at Tottenham. 

The arrival of the ex-Spurs coach to Bayern Munich as also been talked about. Bayern are now managed by Hans-Dieter Flick after the sacking of Niko Kovac as coach. 

But in reach hours, a new rumour has started to link the 47 year-old coach with Arsenal. Specifically, it's 'The Sun' who are speaking of the possible arrival of Pochettino because of the poor results Unai Emery has been getting.

Robert Pires, the ex-Villarreal player and Arsenal legend, admitted in comments givenn to 'Omnisport' that the Spaniard is not going through a good spelll: "The Arsenal target is to end in the top four because it's fundamental for the team to be in the Champions League. The situation is not good, but both he (Emery) and the players need to find a solution."

Arsenal are in eighth position with 18 points, while Tottenham are tenth with 17 after the victory over West Ham in Mourinho's first game in charge.
