Troy Aikman Calls Out Cowboys’ Coaching During Patriots Game

25 Nov 2019, 00:14 by Tzvi Machlin

The Dallas Cowboys only narrowly avoided disaster in the middle of the fourth quarter against the Patriots today. After giving up a field goal to make it 13-6 New England, the ensuing kickoff could have all but ended the game.

Return man Tony Pollard lined up out of position and the kickoff hit the ground and sailed behind him. Pollard was able to jump on the ball – well behind the 15-yard line.

FOX commentator Troy Aikman noticed Pollard in the wrong position from the get-go and was almost unsurprised when the play went south.

After it happened, Aikman called out the coaching staff for letting that happen.

“That’s poor coaching,” he said at the start of the drive.

The Cowboys ultimately fell a few yards short of a touchdown, and had to settle for a field goal that made the game 13-9.
