Steve Bruce Admits 2018 Cabbage Incident Left 'Unsavoury' Taste in His Mouth

25 Nov 2019 by Tom Gott

​Newcastle manager Steve Bruce has admitted that having a cabbage thrown at him during his time with Aston Villa was one of the lowest moments of his career.

Towards the end of his ill-fated spell with Villa in 2018, Bruce had attracted plenty of criticism for his side's struggles, but one fan opted to take it one step further and launch an entire cabbage at Bruce during a 3-3 draw with Preston North End – possibly remembering the ancient Greek tradition of using the large-headed leafy vegetable as a laxative as Preston ran through Villa.
