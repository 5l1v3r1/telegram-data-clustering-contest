Punjab University announces M.A/M.Sc Part 1 and Part 2 results | Pakistan - Geo.tv

25 Nov 2019 by Web Desk

LAHORE: The University of Punjab (PU) has announced the results of M.A./M.Sc Part-I and Part-II annual examinations, 2019.

The result was scheduled to be announced on November 22, 2019, however, owing to delayed compiling it was announced today, according to the statement.
