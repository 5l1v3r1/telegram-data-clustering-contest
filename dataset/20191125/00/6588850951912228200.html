‘I Saw a Pregnant Woman Being Buried Alive With John Mahama’s Picture & NDC Flag’ – Prophet Cosmos

25 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Popular Ghanaian pastor, Cosmos Walker Affran has disclosed that former President John Dramani Mahama and the National Democratic Congress (NDC) should pray fervently against evil machinations being targeted at them spiritually.

The Fante speaking prophet who has correctly made some predictions in the past before, said he saw in the spiritual realms that John Mahama’s photo has bee buried along with that of a pregnant woman.

This means his main rivals in the incumbent government are doing everything possible to stop his second comeback.

He made the prophecy via his Facebook page.

“I saw a pregnant woman be buried alive with John Mahama picture and NDC flag, NDC should pray against it. ” thou saith the Lord. Prophecy by Prophet Cosmos Walker Affran,” – Prophet Cosmos wrote.
