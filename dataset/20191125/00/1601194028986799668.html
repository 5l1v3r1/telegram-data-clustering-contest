The Viking Raiders win Triple Threat Tag Team Match at WWE Survivor Series; Get the first point for RAW

25 Nov 2019, 00:08 by Shubham Roy

This year's WWE Survivor Series features WWE NXT going toe to toe against WWE RAW and WWE SmackDown as they all compete for brand supremacy. The pre-show for WWE's Fall Classic featured a Triple Threat tag team match between WWE NXT Tag Team Champions The Undisputed Era, WWE SmackDown Tag Team Champions The New Day and WWE RAW Tag Team Champions The Viking Raiders.

Earlier in the night, Dolph Ziggler and Robert Roode won a Tag Team Battle Royal to score a point for SmackDown and Lio Rush retained his NXT Cruiserweight Champion to get the point for NXT. The Viking Raiders won the triple threat match to score the first point for WWE RAW and even the scoreboards.
