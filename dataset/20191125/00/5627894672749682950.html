Downhill action begins at local ski resorts

25 Nov 2019

WRIGHTWOOD  (INT)  Mountain High is the first Southern California ski resort to open for the winter season.

Man-made snow and colder temperatures have made it possible. Mountain High reportedly had a sell-out crowd over the weekend.

Bear Mountain and other resorts in the Big Bear area plan Thanksgiving weekend openings. The opening of tubing began earlier this month.

The resorts are gearing up for an incoming storm that could dump up to 2-feet of snow.
