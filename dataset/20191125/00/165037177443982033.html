Suárez's advice to Griezmann to rediscover his best version

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Antoine Griezmann isn't performing at the level that was expected of him at Barcelona, but his teammate Luis Suárez has given him the recipe to be the star player that Barcelona signed.

"Griezmann is one of the best players in the world, but imposing yourself at Barça requires time and patience to adapt to their particular style, it also happened to me in my first months here", Suárez commented in an interview collected by the German magazine 'Kicker'.

The Uruguayan said that if Griezmann wants to rediscover his best version, he must be calm and serene, and avoid becoming anxious.

Griezmann knew how to deal with Atletico Madrid's demands on more than one occasion, but it seems that this season, at Barcelona, that is surpassing him. We'll see if Suarez's advice works.
