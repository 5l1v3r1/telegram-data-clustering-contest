Governor fast tracks fire recovery efforts

25 Nov 2019

SACRAMENTO  (INT)  Governor Newsom has renewed his executive order that streamlines recovery efforts in Octobers fire zones.

In the Inland Empire, it applies to the Reche, Sandalwood, 46, Hill and Hillside fires.

The order helps displaced residents with housing needs with the use of manufactured homes and mobile home parks. Fees are being waived to replace documents lost in the fires such as drivers licenses and birth certificates.
