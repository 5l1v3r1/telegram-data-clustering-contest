Pune: 29-year-old cop hangs self, ‘husband blamed’

Officials from Dehu Road police station said that Waghmare left her house at 9 pm for night duty, saying she would return by morning. However, in the morning, she was found hanging from the ceiling of her bedroom, police said.

25 Nov 2019, 00:12 by Express News Service

A 29-year-old police constable attached to Talegaon Dabhade police station allegedly committed suicide by hanging at her house on Dehu Road.

The deceased was identified as Saraswati Waghmare, a resident of Vikasnagar. Based on the complaint filed by her parents, police have booked her husband, Vikas Zodage (35). Waghmare is survived by her six-year-old son, husband and parents.

Officials from Dehu Road police station said that Waghmare left her house at 9 pm for night duty, saying she would return by morning. However, in the morning, she was found hanging from the ceiling of her bedroom, police said. Her husband was sleeping in the drawing room at the time.

Sub-inspector Chhaya Borkar of Dehu Road police station said, “We have found a suicide note, suspected to have been written by the deceased. She has said in the note that she was ending life because of mental harassment. She has not named anyone. However, based on her parents’ complaint we have booked her husband.”

Borkar denied reports that the deceased was allegedly being harassed by her superiors, saying, “Those allegations are not true. There is a complaint that she was harassed by her husband, and we have filed accordingly.”
