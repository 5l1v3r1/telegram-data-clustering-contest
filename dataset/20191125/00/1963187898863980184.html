Micho settles on Zamalek starting line-up against Enppi

25 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

Zamalek coach Milutin Sredovic "Micho" is struggling to put his squad against Enppi in the sixth round of the Egyptian league, only in the midfielder's positions.

Tarek Hamed and Mahmoud Abdel Aziz are out for injury, and Ferjani Sassi is suspended, so Micho is expected to turn to Ahmed Sayed Zizou in midfield.

Micho prefers the logical solution: Mohamed Hassan, who has played for Zamalek only three times since joining him last season, and calling Hazem Imam, the right-back to participate in the midfield next to Zizo.

Thus, the formation of Zamalek is expected ..

Goalkeepers: Mohamed Awad

Defense: Hamdi Nagguez - Mahmoud Alaa - Mahmoud Hamdi "Al Wench" - Mohammed Abdel Shafi

Center: Ahmed Sayed "Zizo" - Mohamed Hassan (Hazem Imam) - Karim Bambu - Imam Ashour - Mohamed Onagem

Attack: Omar Said

Zamalek is in the second place with 10 points from four matches, while Enppi are bottom of the table with one point from five games.
