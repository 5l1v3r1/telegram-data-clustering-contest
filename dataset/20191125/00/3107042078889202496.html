Much-loved ODT journalist dies

25 Nov 2019

Long-serving Otago Daily Times journalist David Loughrey died on Saturday, aged 58, of cancer.

Mr Loughrey was a city council, religion, fashion, tourism and infrastructure reporter for the ODT, as well as stints at the Oamaru Mail and Channel 39.

He also penned the "A Strictly Local Matter" column, which appeared regularly in the ODT.

Mr Loughrey was a much-loved colleague, and the thoughts of all ODT staff are with his partner, Lesley, and son, Ben, at this time.
