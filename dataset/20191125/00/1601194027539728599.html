Rhea Ripley leads the women's NXT team to victory

25 Nov 2019, 00:54 by Arunava Ghoshal

For the very first time in WWE history, RAW, SmackDown and NXT collided with one another at this year's Survivor Series pay-per-view. One such match featured the women's team of all the three brands engage in a triple threat elimination match to establish brand supremacy.

Captained by the 10-time Women's Champion Charlotte Flair, Team RAW included Natalya, Sarah Logan, and the current WWE Women's Tag Team Champions Asuka and Kairi Sane. Team SmackDown had Nikki Cross, Dana Brooke, Carmella, Lacey Evans, with Sasha Banks as their captain. The Black-and-Gold brand presented Io Shirai, Candice LeRae, Toni Storm, Bianca Belair in their team under the leadership of Rhea Ripley.
