8-pack abs hero Bellamkonda to work with Santosh Srinivas - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

25 Nov 2019

Bellamkonda Sai Srinivas, after scoring a hit with the thriller 'Rakshasudu' earlier this year, has teamed up with 'Kandireega' and 'Rabhasa' director Santosh Srinivas for his next.  This untilted flick will be launched on November 29.

To be shot from December, this one casts Srinivas in a "never seen before avatar".  

"Srinivas has undergone a physical makeover and attained 8-pack abs for his role in this movie," the makers say.  Going by this, we are in for an action movie.  

G Subrahmanyam of Sumanth Movie Productions will produce the movie.  Dudley is the cinematographer, while Devi Sri Prasad will compose the music.  Art Direction is by Avinash Kolla.
