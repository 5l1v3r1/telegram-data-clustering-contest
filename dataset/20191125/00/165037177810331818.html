Getafe want Aleñá on loan

25 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Carles Aleñá's lack of minutes at Barcelona could lead him to go out on loan. Getafe have always shown an interest in signing him and could do so in the January window.

Carles Aleñá has hardly played a Barcelona match this season. He started on matchday 1 of the league but didn't play the 2nd half at the San Mamés.

He didn't play again for Barcelona until the November international break when he started again in the friendly match against Cartagena.

With this scarce participation, it is understandable that the young youth academy graduate is looking for alternatives. And one could be going on loan to Getafe in January.
