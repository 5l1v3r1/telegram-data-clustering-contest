Refs Seemingly Screw Cowboys With Horrible ‘Tripping’ Call In 4th QTR vs. Patriots (VIDEO)

25 Nov 2019, 00:44 by Darrelle Lincoln

Controversial calls in the NFL have been quite the norm in 2019.

That was the case on 3rd-and-one late in the 4th quarter when Cowboys offensive lineman Travis Frederick was called for a tripping penalty by refs that cost them 10 yards and ultimately a turnover on downs on the next play.
