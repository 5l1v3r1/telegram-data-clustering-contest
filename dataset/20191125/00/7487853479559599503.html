Unpacking Baffling Sam Allardyce Comments on British Managers in Viral Clip

25 Nov 2019 by Robbie Copeland

There are a few inevitabilities around this time of year. The trees will go bare, Mariah Carey will come on the radio and Sam Allardyce will pop up with some audacious, controversial nonsense in the press to remind us all that he still exists. 

What's he been saying to it this time? Well, trophies are irrelevant apparently (unless you're Mauricio Pochettino). He also trotted out one of his greatest hits that name and nationality are the only factors that stopped him landing the Manchester United job.
