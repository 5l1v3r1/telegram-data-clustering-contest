SRVD in The Lab LDN

Radio Slave & Patrick Mason showcase their collaborative project in The Lab LDN

25 Nov 2019 by In association with Coors Light

The Lab LDN is Mixmag and Coors Light's weekly live stream, powered by Void Acoustics, where we showcase the finest DJs direct from our world famous office party.

This Friday, November 29, we welcome two titans of the Berlin club scene as Radio Slave & Patrick Mason throw it down as SRVD. Get guestlist here.

The pair first met inside Panorama Bar, and since 2018 have been collaborating on weighty sets and productions that ignite dancefloors as SRVD.

Three releases have arrived via Rekids, merging Patrick Mason’s club kid energy with Radio Slave’s floor-focused expertise into hot ‘n’ heavy bangers. Standout sets at the likes of Pacha’s Transmoderna, Pornceptual and the beloved Berlin spot where they met have further marked out SRVD as a powerful force in clubland.

Catch the live stream from 5pm BST this Friday, November 29. Tune in live over on our Facebook page and re-watch later on MixmagTV

SRVD play Afterlife at Printworks on November 30
