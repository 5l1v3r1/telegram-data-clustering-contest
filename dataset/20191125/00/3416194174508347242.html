Taylor Swift Sings Her Greatests Hits for AMAs 2019 Performance Medley

25 Nov 2019, 00:18

Taylor Swift performed a medley of her greatest hits at the 2019 American Music Awards to celebrate her Artist of the Decade Award and she did not disappoint with the set list!

The 29-year-old singer was even joined by some special guests during the buzzworthy moment on Sunday (November 24) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

Taylor kicked off the set with her Lover album song “The Man” while wearing a shirt featuring the names of her first six albums. She then shed the shirt and effectively shed her former record label away in a nod to the feud she’s in with them.

The superstar then performed “Love Story,” “I Knew You Were Trouble,” “Blank Space,” “Shake It Off” with special guests Camila Cabello and Halsey, and finally “Lover.”

While she sang “Lover,” the title track from her new album, ballet dancers Misty Copeland and Craig Hall did a dance performance!
