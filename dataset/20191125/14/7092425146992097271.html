Roz Purcell praised for candid post on body confidence

25 Nov 2019, 14:31 by James Cox

Roz Purcell has been praised by followers after sharing a candid post on body confidence.

In a lengthy post accompanied by a picture, Purcell encouraged her followers not to obsess over their bodies and diet culture.

The former Miss Universe has previously spoken about about suffering from a binge-eating disorder.

Purcell shared the following message with the unfiltered picture.

"I was not born to diet my way through life and then die ~ something I had to remind myself of last night when I felt kind of down.

"Coming into a day where usually I would have felt the need to feed my negative image of myself by restricting, over training and putting myself down. NOT TODAY.

"I have spent far too many years tied up in diet culture. Today's #contentcontent is a bit different but it's probably more honest to what I felt like I needed myself last night & I'm posting unusually early to catch any of ye who are feeling sh*t about your body today.

"**ck it, don't be Dick to yourslef, don't restrict your food today, don't punish yourself & let's not waste our time on something so irrelevant to WHO WE ARE."

One follower wrote: "You are a beautiful young woman with a strong beautiful body ...young people and us more mature ladies need this kinda honesty in our world."

A second added: "Thank you for sharing this and making me and so many others feel normal!!!!  All our bodies are so amazing!"
