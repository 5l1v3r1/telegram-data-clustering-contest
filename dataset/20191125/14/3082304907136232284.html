Hilarious video of two toilets in one cubicle from athletes village goes viral during SEA Games 2019

25 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Harigovind

The organisers of the SEA Games 2019 are likely to be in more trouble soon, as the video of a poorly-planned women’s toilet at the Rizal Memorial Stadium has emerged online. In the video, a single cubicle can be seen housing two toilets, with no barrier in between both of them.
