Kimberley Walsh admits she cried as she watched Nadine Coyle on I'm A Celeb

25 Nov 2019, 14:03

Kimberley Walsh revealed she was left ‘streaming with tears’ as she watched fellow Girls Aloud bandmate Nadine Coyle jump out of a helicopter for I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here.

Appearing on Loose Women, the Sounds of the Underground singer said Nadine is a ‘scaredy-cat’ when it comes to heights and thrills.

She said: ‘I couldn’t actually believe the reaction that I had. I suddenly started filling up and I’m streaming with tears.

‘You realise how much you care for people in those moments because I knew how much of a big deal that moment was for her.’

The start of I’m A Celeb saw Nadine and Caitlyn Jenner jump from a helicopter at 10,000 feet and even we were scared.

On whether Nadine is a ‘scaredy-cat’, Kimberley said: ‘She genuinely is. She hates rides, like when we used to have to go to theme parks and get filmed on rides she was terrified.

‘So when I saw she was going to [jump] I was like, “Aww”. I’m so impressed she did it.’

Kimberley, 38, was also over the moon that she got to witness Caitlyn Jenner practise the Girls Aloud The Promise dance.

Nadine taught the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star the elaborate moves, and we lost our minds.

Kimberley beamed: ‘Oh my gosh, that was amazing. I was really excited about that.’

Caitlyn, Nadine, Adele Roberts and Jacqueline Jossa all stood up to form their own girl band and Nadine taught them the moves to the song.

Talking in the hut afterward Nadine explained: ‘I taught the girls a little bit of The Promise routine, Jacqueline and Adele knew most of it already.

‘Caitlyn just threw herself into it, we’ll teach her Sound of the Underground next.’

Now we’re fully expecting Caitlyn to leave the jungle a Girls Aloud super fan.
