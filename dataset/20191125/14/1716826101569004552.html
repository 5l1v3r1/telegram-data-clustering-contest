Pau Gasol seeking treatment in Spain

25 Nov 2019, 14:29

Free agent Pau Gasol is in Murcia considering further options on the recovery from his left foot injury.

By John Askounis/ info@eurohoops.net

A few days after being waived by the Portland Trail Blazers, Pau Gasol returned to Spain. He made his way to Murcia to gather further options on his recovery, per Marca. He went through left foot surgery last May, but his rehabilitation did not go as planned.

After visiting many foot specialists in USA, he decided to seek diagnoses from his homeland as well. The 39-year-old Spaniard big hopes to get back on the courts as soon as possible with his main goal being the 2020 Olympic Games with the Spanish national team.
