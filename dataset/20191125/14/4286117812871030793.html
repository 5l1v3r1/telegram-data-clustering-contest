Terror attack averted as security forces diffuse 2 IEDs in Kashmir

The operation was jointly executed by J&K Police and the Army. 

25 Nov 2019, 14:03

A major terror attack has been averted by security forces in Kashmir.

The security forces were successful in diffusing two Improvised Explosive Devices(IED) in south Kashmir today.

The IEDs were located 800-meters away from the Jammu-Srinagar National Highway near Qazigund area.

The IEDs weighed 13 kg each.

The operation was jointly executed by J&K Police and the Army.
