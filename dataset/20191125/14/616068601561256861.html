Ram on the lam

25 Nov 2019, 14:16 by Rob Gibson

A group of local residents and Central Saanich police teamed up to recapture a ram that was on the lam for several hours on Sunday morning.

The ram managed to escape its farm enclosure and got out onto the road in the area of Benvenuto Ave. and West Saanich Rd, causing traffic delays at approximately 10:30 a.m. Sunday.

RCMP tell CTV News Vancouver Island that the managed to corral the ram with the help of, "some really good neighbours and Good Samaritans".

It took officers and their helpers about 45 minutes to wrangle the ram before its owner arrived and took it back to the farm where it lives.
