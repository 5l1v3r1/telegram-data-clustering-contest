Sebastian Stan, Christopher Plummer Lead An Ensemble Cast In Military Drama ‘The Last Full Measure’

25 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Rachel West

Watch Video

Sebastian Stan and Christopher Plummer head up an ensemble cast in your first look at the military drama “The Last Full Measure”.

Based on a true story, Stan stars as Pentagon investigator Scott Huffman, a man whose quest to award Airman William H. “Pits” Pitsenbarger the posthumous Medal of Honour, 53 years after his death. It is told through flashbacks of Pitsenbarger’s (Jeremy Irvine) time in the Vietnam War, where he saved the lives of 60 marines during the bloody Operation Abilene. As he meets with surviving veterans to get the full story, Huffman encounters a present-day cover-up investigation into the mission.

The film’s all-star ensemble cast includes Canadian icon Plummer as the late Pitsenbarger’s father, Diane Ladd, Bradley Whitford, Samuel L. Jackson, Ed Harris and William Hurt. Due in theatres on Jan. 17, “The Last Full Measure” also features the last performance of Peter Fonda, who died on August 16, 2019.
