Best GAME Black Friday 2019 deals include £150 off Xbox One X

25 Nov 2019, 14:42

GAME has announced its first wave of Black Friday deals, including both consoles and games, with £50 off a PS4 Pro and £10 off a Switch Lite.

As the days tick closer to Black Friday itself, GAME has officially started their discounts, with some decent savings on a PlayStation 4 Pro and some particularly good bargains for the Xbox One X.

As ever, the saving on Nintendo consoles and games are considerably less extreme, but there is at least £10 off most hardware.

GAME are promising to unveil additional deals daily, especially on Thursday 28, and you can see some of those greyed out on their website, including FIFA 20 and Need For Speed Heat.

So far the game deals aren’t particular good though, so it remains to be seen if they’re saving the best till last.
