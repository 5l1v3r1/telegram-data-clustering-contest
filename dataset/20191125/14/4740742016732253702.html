'Waste of a wage' - Bristol City and Derby County fans on Jamie Paterson's Ashton Gate return

He looks set for a January return

25 Nov 2019, 14:57 by David Byrom

Bristol City and Derby County fans have been reacting to the news that Jamie Paterson looks set to return to Ashton Gate in January.

Paterson moved to Derby on loan from City in August but has so far struggled for regular football, and the arrival of Wayne Rooney at Pride Park only lengthens the queue for the first team.

It means that both player and club may agree to cut their losses in January to facilitate an early return to the Robins.

Here are what fans of the Robins and the Rams have to say about the news.

Bristol City

 

Breathe Robins: Defo want him back. Offers that work rate from the bench. Has quality in him. Need to unlock it. Done reasonably well at Derby, albeit hasn’t had much game time.

Sam: Weird that we let him go in the first place in my opinion.

Matt Street: Runs into culdesacs. Not much end product. No thanks. If he played the ball quicker. Maybe.

Matt: Good decision in my opinion, he’s been doing alright for Derby.

Kingswood Red: Not good enough before and still not good enough. We have better midfielders now than what he was competing with.

Max Rawlings: Would have him in the squad!! Proven Championship quality and maybe give Szmodics a chance to go out on loan and get some football!!

Chris Hall: A lot needs to move on. With Nagy, Brownhill, Massengo, Korey, Szmodics and Palmer already as our centre midfield options and now Paterson coming back. And with Morrell signing a contract and Walsh still at Coventry.

Keith Farrow: Personally, I rate him higher than COD.

Edward Waddell: Thank god. No idea why he was allowed to leave in the first place.

Tom Fielding: It was a bit of a strange move in the first place given his connections with Forest. Not surprised it hasn’t worked out.

Derby

Andy Buckley-Taylor: So Paterson is going back, can we send Dowell back too?

Danny: We must be getting someone in then.

Joel Moore: Would be very strange. I've really liked him when he's played. Has made much more of an impact for us than PL loanee Dowell, and we missed him against Forest

Matthew Jones: Need to terminate all the loans depending on Clarke’s injury. They are a waste of wages at the moment. We have an academy that are Champions of England!

Mr Sifter: Makes sense. We can’t afford any passengers.

Stephen: Don't think that would be a terrible idea. We certainly need better.

Jimmy: Sack him off. Sack Dowell off. Get Rooney in. Get Jordan Ibe in. Get promoted.
