Dabangg 3 Song Yu Karke Making: Salman Khan, Sonakshi Sinha And The Crew Are Hooked On To Mumbaiya Kiss Fever

25 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Amruta Patel

After treating the audience with a fun and naughty number titled Yu Karke from Dabangg 3, makers released the making of the song on the social media platform. Along with the lead pair Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha, the entire crew is getting goofy as they practice this typical Mumbaiya Kiss as seen in the BTS video. Movie releases on December 20, 2019

With Dabangg 3 inching closer to its release, makers are making sure the audience is thoroughly entertained, resulting in massive footfalls in the cinemas from December 20, 2019, onwards. After releasing the naughty and fun number from the movie titled ‘Yu Karke’, now makers treat Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha fans with the making video of this fun number on the social media platform. The behind the scenes video has the entire cast and crew, attempting the Mumbaiya kiss, and it’s fun to watch.

In the video, Sonakshi is seen praising choreographer Vaibhavi Merchant for the mind-blowing and unique dance moves. And to top it all Chulbul Pandey’s Rajjo goes on to praise the director and ace choreographer PrabhuDeva for the uniqueness he adds to the song. The music director duo Sajid-Wajid are also seen justifying these fun lyrics. Costume designer and Salman’s personal stylist Ashley Rebello, giving his inputs, says that the mood of the song is also lifted because of fun and vibrant coloured outfits sported by the lead pair.
