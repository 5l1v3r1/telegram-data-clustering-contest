Celebrate the season at Holidays on the Green in Sarasota

25 Nov 2019, 14:59 by Teyonna Edwards

SARASOTA, Fla.—’Tis the season to celebrate at Holidays on the Green! This family-friendly and free event takes place at The Mall at University Town Center (UTC) from now until January 6.

“Nothing will get you in the Christmas spirit like UTC,” said Lauren Clark, marketing director for The Mall at UTC. “Holidays on the Green is the absolute number one experience that we have to offer.”

Check out more than 4 million lights that are strung up throughout the UTC District. Catch Holiday Movies on the Lawn every Friday and Saturday at 7p.m. and the firework show every Saturday at 9 p.m.

Holidays on the Green also offers Florida's largest outdoor ice-skating rink, in addition to ice sledding on real ice. Enjoy a carousel ride, Christmas carrollers and other activities. To learn more about what Holidays on the Green has to offer, click here.
