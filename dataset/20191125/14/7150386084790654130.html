J&K: Exchange of Fire Underway Between Terrorists & Security Forces in Pulwama

Terrorists and security forces on Monday exchanged fire in Jammu and Kashmir's Pulwama district, stated news agency ANI.

25 Nov 2019, 14:49

New Delhi: An exchange of fire is underway between terrorists and security forces at Tachwara village in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir, stated news agency ANI.

Meanwhile, on late Monday evening, the police and security forces defused an improvised explosive device (IED) in Gasigund of J&K’s Anantnag district, added the report.
