School bus driver shortage causes Miamisburg to change routes, fill jobs

25 Nov 2019, 14:05 by Nick Blizzard - Staff Writer

Miamisburg schools are seeking to hire bus drivers after the district said a shortage has led to reduced routes.

The district is offering paid training for contracted bus drivers, according to an advertisement dated Thursday.

The bus driver shortage has caused the district “to assess our routes and our numbers,” Miamisburg Superintendent David Vail said in email.
