James Maddison fires Leicester title warning to Premier League leaders Liverpool

Brendan Rodgers' Leicester side reeled off their sixth straight win in all competitions with 2-0 win at Brighton on Saturday

25 Nov 2019, 14:40 by James Nursey, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/james-nursey/

James Maddison reckons Leicester have title-winning momentum.

Brendan Rodgers' Foxes reeled off their sixth straight win in all competitions with 2-0 win at Brighton on Saturday.

It was their ninth win in the Premier League this season and fifth in a row to stay second behind leaders Liverpool .

The last time Leicester bagged five successive wins was under Claudio Ranieri in 2015-16 when they famously went on to win the league.

And City's England ace Maddison admits they are riding a wave ahead of struggling Everton's visit on Sunday.

Maddison said: “Momentum is such a key thing, that’s why teams always win titles and they’re right up there.

“They always go through spells where the momentum is with them, they take advantage of that and they get wins in consecutive games like we’re doing at the minute.

“If we want to be considered a top team, we have to keep doing that.

"We know that we’re not going to take our foot off the gas because every game is a tough game in this league.

“We’ll get back to the training pitch and we’ll keep working hard to try and stay up there.”

Jamie Vardy scored his 12th of the season at Brighton where Maddison also impressed.

Maddison didn't net on his 23rd birthday but can still boast four goals and made two assists in 12 top-flight appearances this season.

He added: “Away from home, it’s always tough to get three points at any place in this division, and I think we came away as deserved winners. It’s the best birthday present I could ask for.

“We had to grind through a little stage at the start of the second half and the end of the first half, but I think we came out as deserved winners.”
