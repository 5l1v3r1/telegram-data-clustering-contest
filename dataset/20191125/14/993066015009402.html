Have you seen Helena McElhennon?

25 Nov 2019, 14:56 by Digital Staff

Police are appealing for information on missing person Helena McElhennon.

The 42-year-old was last seen yesterday morning and her car has been found in a lay-by close to Foyle Bridge on the city side of Derry.

PSNI Inspector John Moore said: “Helena is described as being five feet four in height, of medium build with short, dark hair and possibly wearing a maroon coloured cardigan or long, brown dress."

He said Ms McElhennon was last seen in her black Mitsubushi Warrior at around 10:30am yesterday and the vehicle was found in the lay-by at around the same time.

“Prior to this, Helena was seen in the Draperstown area yesterday morning at around 9:30am heading towards Derry/Londonderry," he added.

“We would appeal to anyone who was walking or driving along the Foyle Bridge yesterday morning, who may have seen Helena or her vehicle in the area to contact police on 101 quoting reference number 771 24/11/19.”
