‘State of Decay 2’ Coming to Steam in 2020

25 Nov 2019, 14:45 by Mike Wilson

The wait for the Steam version of State of Decay 2 now has an approximate release date. The Undead Labs zombie epic not only has a Steam page, but lists the game having an “Early 2020” release date.

The game will apparently support crossplay between Xbox One and Steam, but there’s no word on any of the already-released DLC for the Xbox One version of the game being included in the Steam version.

More info on the Steam version is expected later today when Undead Labs hosts a livestream beginning at 3 pm PT/6 pm ET on Twitch and YouTube.
