Nanaimo RCMP say the manager was smashed over the head with a shopping bag carrying bottles on Saturday.

It happened after a man allegedly left the store without paying for some merchandise, and the manager confronted him outside.

The manager was taken to hospital with a serious head injury, but it is not believed to be life-threatening.

Police are now looking for the shoplifter. He is described as white, between 35 to 40 years old, with a slim build.

Witnesses are asked to contact RCMP at 250-754-2345 or through Crime Stoppers or 1-800-222-8477
