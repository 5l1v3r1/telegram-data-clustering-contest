Antoine Griezmann: Frenchman talks about partnership with Messi, Suarez at Barcelona

25 Nov 2019, 14:23

- Antoine Griezmann says he is still adapting to life at Barcelona

- The Frenchman sealed a move to the Spanish giants from Atletico Madrid in summer

- Griezmann also hails his Camp Nou partnership with Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez

Barcelona forward, Antoine Griezmann, has opened up on his relationship with Lionel Messi since joining the Spanish giants during the last summer transfer.

The Frenchman sealed a sensational move to the Catalans after a 4-year stint with the league's top rivals Atletico Madrid.

Griezmann was a central figure in Diego Simeone's formidable Atletico team before mutually parting ways with the club in 2019.
