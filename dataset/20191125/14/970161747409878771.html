Dame Barbara Windsor ‘emotional’ after EastEnders’ touching tribute to Peggy

25 Nov 2019, 14:05

Dame Barbara Windsor was left ‘quite emotional’ after she learned that EastEnders will be paying tribute to her.

Viewers of the BBC One soap will know that Louise Mitchell (Tilly Keeper) — Peggy Mitchell’s granddaughter — is carrying Keanu Taylor’s (Danny Walters) baby, and she’s due to give birth in the coming episodes. Upon the child’s arrival, Lou will name the baby after her grandmother, Peggy.

According to Barbara’s husband Scott Mitchell, the legendary actress was rather emotional after learning the news. He told The Sun newspaper: ‘She thought what a lovely thing it is Peggy Mitchell will live on in a family member. What a lovely tribute.’

‘It made her quite emotional and I have been reminding her each time we watch the show every night. Each time her whole face lights up.’

Longtime EastEnders viewers will know that Barbara played Mitchell matriarch Peggy from 1994 to 2016.
