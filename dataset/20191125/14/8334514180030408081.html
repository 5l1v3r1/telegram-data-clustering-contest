Revolutionary Guards: Iran's enemies will be destroyed if they cross our red lines

25 Nov 2019, 14:08

DUBAI (Reuters) - Iran will destroy the United States, Israel and Saudi Arabia if they cross Tehran’s red lines, the head of the elite Revolutionary Guards Hossein Salami said in a televised speech on Monday.

“We have shown restraint ... we have shown patience towards the hostile moves of America, the Zionist regime (Israel) and Saudi Arabia against the Islamic Republic of Iran ... but we will destroy them if they cross our red lines,” said Hossein Salami.

Writing by Parisa Hafezi
