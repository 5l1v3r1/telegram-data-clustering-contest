See what elderly couple did for grandson

25 Nov 2019, 14:19 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

The relationship between grandparents and their grand kids sometimes can be very intense one might mistake that the actual parents have little or no love for their kids.

That is the case of these adorable couple who stood under the sun to protest the punishment laid upon their grandson, Caleb.

A Twitter user with the handle @kjohnathank took to the micr-blogging platform to share the moment her grandparents took to the open space to protest the punishment her parents gave her brother, Caleb.

In the photo she shared, her grandparents, apparently old were seen holding placards, with protest words that read;

“Please give Caleb a shorter punishment, 3 weeks,” another reads, “Please give Caleb a lesser punishment release his cell phone”

Her grand parents were not in the best of shapes, as a walking aide could be seen before the elderly man however it did stop them from protesting on behalf of their grandson Caleb.
