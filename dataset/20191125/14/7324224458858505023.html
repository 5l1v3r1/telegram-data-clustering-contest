Wolves chief Nuno a bookies' favourite to become next Arsenal boss

Wolves boss Nuno Espirito Santo is a bookmakers' favourite to become the next permanent Arsenal boss.

25 Nov 2019, 14:44 by Joe Edwards

Although the Gunners have not sacked Unai Emery, the Spaniard is under intense pressure at the Emirates and Sky Bet have Nuno at 5/2 to take his place.

Former Arsenal midfielder Mikel Arteta is 3/1, ex-Liverpool, Chelsea and Newcastle chief Rafa Benitez 4/1 and Mauricio Pochettino – recently relieved of his duties as Tottenham gaffer – 6/1.

Nuno's Wolves are currently fifth in the Premier League – three places above Emery's side.
