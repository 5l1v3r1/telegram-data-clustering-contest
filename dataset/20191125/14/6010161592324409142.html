Don’t expect GTA Online content to stop coming anytime soon

25 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Sherif Saed

Despite its age, and the release of Red Dead Online, Rockstar is not yet done making GTA Online content.

GTA Online has been a massive, enduring success for Rockstar. What started out as a novelty add-on evolved into its own game that continues to break player records.

Many assumed things would start to wind down for GTA Online when Read Dead Online first became available. We’re now a year into that, and both games continue to be developed in tandem.

According to Rockstar North co-studio head, Rob Nelson, this is intentional, and things will continue down that path for the foreseeable future. In fact, Rockstar has plans for at least another year of content for GTA Online.
