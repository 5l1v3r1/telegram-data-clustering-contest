Saidi Balogun announces death of mum

25 Nov 2019, 14:44 by Ifeanyi Ngerem

Veteran Yoruba actor, Saidi Balogun has announced the death of his mother earlier today. The actor took to his social media page to make the announcement.

Saidi Balogun did not disclose the time of death nor the age of his mother as he simply announced on his Instagram page:

“GOOD NIGHT MAMA MI JOLADE OMO OLOMU APERAN.Rest in peace”.

The post has attracted the sympathetic reactions of actors in Nollywood and the entertainment industry.
