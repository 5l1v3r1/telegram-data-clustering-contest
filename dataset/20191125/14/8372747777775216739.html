Worker injured in ladder fall in Naples Monday

25 Nov 2019, 14:53 by WFTX Digital Team

COLLIER COUNTY, Fla. -- A worker was trauma alerted to the hospital Monday morning after a serious fall.

According to Naples Fire-Rescue, the person was injured falling off a ladder.

They shared these photos of the patient being transfered to the medical helicopter.

The patient was taken to a Lee County trauma center.

No further details are available at this time.
