Kelvin Fletcher shocks fans with painful bruises from Strictly rehearsals

He made it through this weekend

25 Nov 2019, 14:36 by Rebecca Carter

Kelvin Fletcher has showed off his painful-looking bruises from his Strictly Come Dancing rehearsals.

The former Emmerdale actor, 35, shared a photo to Instagram at the weekend showing the bruises on his thighs.

Standing in the mirror, Kelvin sported a pair of stripey pants as his muscly thighs were covered in huge red marks.
