Strictly's Lisa Armstrong seeks energy hit with Lucozade

25 Nov 2019, 14:07

Lisa Armstrong is taking a break from all of the drama in her life by having a ‘me’ moment featuring a cigarette and downtime on her phone.

The 43-year-old Strictly make-up artist and ex-wife of Ant McPartlin, was spotted on the streets of central London with a cigarette in hand and a Lucozade in the other.

She wore a big black fur coat, with trousers and black heeled boots.

In the photos, she appeared to light the cigarette and then start to peruse on her Louis Vuitton-covered phone, while taking occasional drags and scrolling along.

Lisa then reached into her matching Louis Vuitton bag and pulled out a make-up bag.

After, she grabbed her Lucozade and walked back into an office building in the heart of Soho.

What a moment.

The couple first announced they were filing for divorce in January last year, after 11 years of marriage.

They officially divorced in October last year, but they’re still in the process of dividing up their assets.

She recently hit out at people who claimed she thought £31million was not enough in her divorce settlement.

Someone on Twitter asked her: ‘How can 31million, not be enough for you?! It’s enough for both me and my family put together for life. Just sayin’.

Lisa then responded: ‘Don’t believe the lies #justsaying’.

Their lawyers are said to be preparing for a hearing in the High Court next year.

It could mean intimate details of their marriage and Ant’s battle with drink and drug addiction could be made public as Lisa feels he ‘got away with a lot’ after their split.
