Ohio State's Chase Young returns from suspension, sets school single-season sack record

25 Nov 2019, 14:17 by Ryan Gaydos

Ohio State defensive end Chase Young returned from a two-game suspension with a vengeance Saturday.

Young recorded three sacks in the Buckeyes’ 28-17 victory over Penn State and set a school single-season record for sacks with 16.5. He broke the original 14 mark set by Vernon Gholston in 2007.
