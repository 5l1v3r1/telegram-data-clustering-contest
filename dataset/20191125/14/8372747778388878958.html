Tow to Go service offers a safe ride and a tow home this Thanksgiving weekend

25 Nov 2019, 14:15 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- If you have too much to drink this Thanksgiving weekend, you have an option to make it home safe, thanks to AAA.

Under the Tow-to-Go program , a tow truck will take your car and up to two people anywhere within 10 miles for free.

It starts Wednesday, November 27th and goes through 6 a.m. on Sunday December 1st. Just call (855) 2-TOW-2-GO.

Tow to Go is designed as a safety net for those that did not plan ahead. Citizens are urged to always choose a Designated Driver before having a first drink.

The Tow to Go service is available during several major holidays. Future dates include Christmas and New Year's Eve, Super Bowl, St. Patrick's Day, Cinco de Mayo, and Memorial Day.

The service is provided in Florida, Georgia, Iowa, Michigan, North Dakota, Nebraska, Tennessee, Wisconsin and Indiana (Fort Wayne and South Bend only).
