NDC Deputy National Youth Organizer Edem Agbana captured in student loan default

25 Nov 2019, 14:59 by MyNewsGH

Deputy National Youth Organizer of the opposition National Democratic Congress (NDC), Eric Edem Agbana has been captured as part of hundreds of Ghanaians who have defaulted on the payment of their student loans years after leaving school.

Mr. Agbana was captured with student ID 10339654 from the University of Ghana where he took the loan to support his education. The NDC youth leader joins the likes of loud-mouthed Awal Mohammed of the NPP who have been captured.

The list which captures defaulters as far back as 2010 features loan defaulters from the University for Development Studies, University of Ghana, University of Cape Coast, the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology ; Ho, Bolga, Tamale, Sunyani, Koforidua, Kumasi Takoradi Polytechnics; among many others.
