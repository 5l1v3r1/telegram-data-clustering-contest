ESPN’s Power Rankings Top 25 Heading Into Rivalry Week

25 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN’s college football power rankings have been updated heading into the biggest week of the 2019 regular season: Rivalry Week.

Week 14 will be a huge one, with several massive games – Ohio State vs. Michigan, Alabama vs. Auburn, LSU vs. Texas A&M, Oklahoma vs. Oklahoma State – taking place.

ESPN’s college football power rankings have been updated heading into the week.

Here’s the latest top 15:

  1. Ohio State
  2. LSU
  3. Clemson
  4. Georgia
  5. Utah
  6. Alabama
  7. Oklahoma
  8. Florida
  9. Oregon
  10. Baylor
  11. Minnesota
  12. Penn State
  13. Wisconsin
  14. Michigan
  15. Notre Dame

You can view the full top 25 here.

There’s a clear top three, obviously, in LSU, Ohio State and Clemson. The No. 4 spot will likely come down to one of Alabama, Oklahoma and Utah (assuming Georgia loses to LSU in the SEC Championship Game).

The new College Football Playoff rankings will be out on Tuesday night.
