EastEnders SPOILERS: Max proposes to Ruby

Max has been dating Ruby Allen for a few months

Max wants to prove to Ruby he's serious about them, but what will she say when he pops the question in next week's EastEnders?

Viewers know Max and Ruby have been dating for the past several months but the age gap has become more and more obvious to the businessman (and the viewers at home!)
