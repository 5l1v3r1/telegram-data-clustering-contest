When is Rick and Morty season 4 released on Netflix UK?

We have known for some time that Rick and Morty would be back for a fourth run - but when exactly does the Adult Swim series arrive on Netflix UK?

12 Nov 2019, 16:35 by Lewis Knight, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lewis-knight/

We need new Rick and Morty in our lives, stat!

The popular animated sci-fi comedy has five episodes already made and ready to go, with US fans getting the new season this month via Adult Swim.

The hilarious show has already revealed the episode titles too: Edge of Tomorty: Rick Die Repeat, The Old Man and the Seat, One Crew Over the Crewcoo's Morty, Claw and Hoarder: Special Ricktim's Morty, and Battlestar Ricklactica.

These episodes only make up one half of the fourth season, however, as the second half arrives on our screens in late 2020.

But when exactly does the first half of Rick and Morty season 4 arrive on UK screens?

Here is what you need to know.

Rick and Morty season 4 Netflix UK release date

The shocking truth is that Rick and Morty season 4 won't be available on Netflix UK for some time.

Instead, fans of the animated comedy will have to turn to E4 and its on demand service All 4.

The new episodes of Rick and Morty season 4 will arrive on E4 on November 20, 2019, with the episodes being on All 4 following their airing on E4

Fans will require a TV license to stream or watch the episodes live, but they will not need it to watch on Catch Up on All 4.

Rick and Morty season 4 arrives in the UK on E4 on November 20, 2019.
