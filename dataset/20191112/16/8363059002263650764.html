Selfridges' Unveils a Mini Mince Pie Advent Calendar to Scratch That Festive Itch

12 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Shabana Arif

If you're looking for an advent calendar that's filled with edibles (not that kind), Selfridges' festive mince pies might be just the ticket.

Stuffed with 24 mini mince pies, there's an array of fillings waiting to be snaffled up as you chow your way through December, including classic, citrus, almond, and chocolate. Behind the 25th door is a Christmas cake from artisan bakery, Meg Rivers.
