Shahid Kapoor shares a throwback picture from his Europe vacation; captions, "missing my boys"

12 Nov 2019, 16:07 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shahid Kapoor was last seen in 'Kabir Singh' which scored huge at the box office. Afterwhich the actor went on a vacation in Europe with his brother Ishaan Khatter and actor Kunal Kemmu. He also shared pictures from his vacay on his Instagram.

Shahid currently gearing up for his upcoming sports drama 'Jersey'. The actor has been sharpening his cricketing skills by net practising in the city.

Now, he took the photo-sharing app to post a throwback picture from his holidays in Europe. In the pic, the actor looked handsome in a light olive green t-shirt paired with black pants and biking shoes on! Also, don't miss the breathtaking view in the background. Shahid captioned the post, "Missing my boys. #throwback"
