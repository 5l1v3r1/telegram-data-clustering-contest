Best Buy Pre-Black Friday 2019: Today's Best Deals on Tech

12 Nov 2019 by Forbes Finds

Black Friday may not be here just yet, but Best Buy has already announced a series of deals on awesome products on tech, from TVs to smart home devices. One of the best places to hunt for discounts is Best Buy’s Deals tab, which includes Deal of the Day offers, the weekly ad and a round-up of the hottest current deals. Using this tab, we’ve put together a list of our favorite deals from Best Buy, which are all available right now, weeks before Black Friday. Hey, with deals like these, who even needs Black Friday?

Samsung 75-inch NU6900

Original Price: $1,099.99

Sale Price: $999.99

Samsung has been building some of the better TVs of the past few years, and the 6 Series is an excellent example of that. You’ll get a 4K resolution, with support for HDR and Samsung’s UHD Engine, which should be able to upscale even older content to look great. The TV is smart too — it has Samsung’s Tizen operating system built right into it, which allows you to install all your favorite streaming apps and services.

JLab Audio Epic Air Sport

Original Price: $149.99

Sale Price: $119.99

Perhaps you’re looking for a great pair of true wireless headphones, and JLab Audio has been building some great ones for a while now. The best thing about JLab Audio’s true wireless headphones is the fact that they have an ear hook to stay firmly on your ears. No more worrying about your earbuds bouncing down the drain. The headphones are also pretty comfortable and sound great — which is always helpful.

Simplisafe Entryway Kit

Original Price: $299.99

Sale Price: $229.99

If you’re looking for a way to better secure your home, then Simplisafe is an excellent way to go. This kit comes with a main hub, a keypad, a door sensor and a video doorbell — so you’ll be able to secure your entire entryway, which is always handy. The camera has a 1,080p resolution and supports two-way audio, so it should be more than capable of capturing whatever is going on around your front door. 

HP Envy x360 2-in-1 Laptop

Original Price: $829.99

Sale Price: $679.99

The HP Envy x360 is a great way to get a relatively powerful and stylish computer at an affordable price. The laptop comes with an Intel Core i5 processor, along with 8GB of RAM and 256GB of storage — plus it has a nice, big 15.6-inch display that’s perfect for watching movies and TV shows. Safe to say, this is a great laptop for professionals and students alike. 

Lenovo Yoga C940 2-in-1

Original Price: $1,699.99

Sale Price: $1,499.99

You might need something a little more powerful, in which case the Lenovo Yoga C940 is a great laptop. This laptop swaps out the lower-power specs for an impressive Intel Core i7 processor, 16GB of RAM and 512GB of storage.
