UFC heavyweight champ Stipe Miocic sends warning to Tyson Fury: ‘Be careful what you wish for’

12 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Lewis Mckeever

Earlier this week, MTK Global released video footage of heavyweight boxing champion Tyson Fury training MMA with UFC welterweight Darren Till, and ‘The Gypsy King’ didn’t look half bad.

In fact, Fury’s pad work with ‘The Gorilla’ soon caught the attention of UFC president Dana White, who seemed more than impressed with the 31-year-old’s in-cage athleticism and work ethic.

It wasn’t long before the video found its way to Stipe Miocic’s Twitter feed, and the UFC heavyweight champion had quite a different reaction than Dana White.
