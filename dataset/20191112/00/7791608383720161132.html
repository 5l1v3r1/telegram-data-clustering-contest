Bella Thorne Dives Into A Fresh Look With A Mermaid-Like Green Ombré Hairdo

12 Nov 2019 by Thanussha Priyah

Bella Thorne ringed in the Christmas season with a festive green hair color. The 22-year-old debuted her new ‘do on Instagram, which showed off a mermaid-like green ombré waves. Still keeping her brunette roots, with blonde streaks, her long hair ends with vivid hues that would soon have others green with envy.
The Instagram post was captioned as, “Yeah I love to do abnormal changes to my hair.” Thorne is known to switch up her hair colors every now and then, and her latest look received plenty of praises.
One user wrote, “Makes you look mermaid-y beautiful, Bella! You fit in all. So pretty.”
