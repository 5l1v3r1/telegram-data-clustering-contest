Japan Is In Love With These Gorgeous, Edible DIY Zen Garden Kits Made From Candy

12 Nov 2019 by Mikelle Leow

You’ve probably seen a number of DIY candy kits from Japan floating around the internet. These mini projects seem to magically turn powders and water into miniature meals, but are deceptively made out of candy.
The imaginative confectioneries are typically concocted for kids, but there now appears to be a couple of variations more suited for grownups who wish to calm down their minds.
Available on Amazon Japan by way of Japanese company Heart, the Zen Garden DIY candy kits let you construct your own “zen gardens” with the help of some food hocus pocus. Internet users in Japan are fawning over the adorable edible gardens, and for good reason.
One set depicts a miniature rock garden, with the “stones” being made of chocolate or candy, while its “sand” is actually powdered Ramune sweets, named after an iconic Japanese soda.
The other features a gummy-like koi pond and the same “sand candies.”
In both kits, you’ll even receive a mini “garden rake” to create ripples out of the faux sand. It’s relaxing yet visually realistic.
