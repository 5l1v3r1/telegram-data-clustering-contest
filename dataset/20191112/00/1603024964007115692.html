Indiabulls Real Consolidated September 2019 Net Sales at Rs 1,085.65 crore, up 4.35% Y-o-Y

12 Nov 2019

Reported Consolidated quarterly numbers for Indiabulls Real Estate are:

Net Sales at Rs 1,085.65 crore in September 2019 up 4.35% from Rs. 1,040.42 crore in September 2018.

Quarterly Net Profit at Rs. 301.17 crore in September 2019 up 296.7% from Rs. 75.92 crore in September 2018.
