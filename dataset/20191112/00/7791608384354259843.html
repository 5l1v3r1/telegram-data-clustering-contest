Meghan Markle Recreates Princess Diana’s Polished Royal Style In All-Black Look

12 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Left image via mark reinstein / Shutterstock.com, right image via Kathy Hutchins / Shutterstock.com
Meghan Markle turned to the late Princess Diana for outfit inspiration for a recent royal event.
The Duchess of Sussex rocked an all-black ensemble on Remembrance Sunday commemoration at the Cenotaph. Wrapped in a black wool Stella McCartney coat, Markle paired her chic outerwear with a black round-neck dress and wide brim hat with a leather ribbon detailing.
She placed a bright red poppy on her coat to pay respects to the fallen ones during the First World War. Her elegant outfit looked similar to the one Princess Diana wore to the same event back in 1991. Diana had worn the outfit with an oversized belt cinched at her waist and a dramatic black hat.
She paired her ensemble with pearl drop earrings, while Markle chose to appear without it.
