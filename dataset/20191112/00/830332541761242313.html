Khris Middleton out with thigh contusion

Will miss 3-4 weeks

12 Nov 2019, 00:11 by Jay Wilson

MILWAUKEE - Milwaukee Bucks forward Khris Middleton sustained a left thigh contusion during Sunday night’s 121-119 win at Oklahoma City. He underwent an MRI and subsequent examination today by team physician Dr. William Raasch of the Froedtert & the Medical College of Wisconsin health network.

The exam confirmed the injury as a left thigh contusion.

Middleton is expected to be sidelined for three-to-four weeks and his status will be updated as appropriate.

In 10 games (all starts) for the Bucks this season, Middleton is averaging 18.5 points, 5.7 rebounds and 2.9 assists in 30.8 minutes per game, while shooting a career best in field goal percentage (.486).
