Giant Waterspout Off the Coast of Genoa Scares Locals

A giant waterspout was captured on video by an eyewitnesses in the Italian city of Genoa; the water 'tornado' was directly approaching the coast of the Ligurian Sea.

12 Nov 2019, 14:12

The footage is spectacular, but obviously not for the locals. Their houses were separated by the vortex by only a few dozen meters.
