Several shops closed in Oman due to violations

12 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Times News Service

Muscat: An inspection by concerned teams of Muscat Municipality, of commercial shops and stores to ensure compliance with rules has resulted in the closure of several shops.

A statement published online by Muscat Municipality said, “Muscat Municipality has closed a number of shops and multi-purpose stores because of the practice of carrying on activities without municipal licenses, and some of them for expiry of the license to practice those activities.”

“The Municipality emphasises the importance of licenses as a protection for the legality of the activity and commitment to the requirements associated with the law," Muscat Municipality added
