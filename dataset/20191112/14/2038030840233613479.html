3 local TV shows to watch this Tuesday

12 Nov 2019, 14:15

Cape Town – South Africans had a lot to celebrate this past week. Continue the celebration with these world class local shows.

From a riveting regal drama, to family rivalries, to South Africa's first fictional women president – here are our top three recommendations.

Fynskrif

20:00 - kykNET (DStv 144)

Chris van Niekerk (7de Laan) is Ben Van Wyk, the now retired partner at the law firm Van Wyk and Partners. Legal drama isn’t the only thing the firm worries about, there is one personal issue that needs to be ironed out. Ben is retiring and handing over the reins to his prodigal son, and not to his daughter who’s been working at the firm for the past five years.
