Bitcoin.com exchange prepares initial listing of Universal Protocol Token

12 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 12): Bitcoin.com Exchange will provide an initial exchange listing to support the first interoperable mega-utility token, the Universal Protocol Token (UPT).

In a statement, Bitcoin.com Exchange said UPT would be listed on Nov 20.

UPT is backed by the Universal Protocol Alliance, a group of leading blockchain organisations including Cred, Bittrex Global, Uphold, Blockchain at Berkeley, CertiK and OmiseGO.

In addition to listing UPT, Bitcoin.com Exchange will also be listing Universal Protocol Stablecoins, including the Universal Dollar (UPUSD), Universal Euro (UPEUR), in addition to an ERC-20 version of Bitcoin, the Universal Bitcoin (UPBTC).

Bitcoin.com Exchange will also support interoperability between the Ethereum protocol and the Simple Ledger protocol, and work with UP to develop Universal Yen (UPYEN).

Founded in 2018 to accelerate mainstream adoption of blockchain technology, the Universal Protocol Alliance is committed to producing viable and pragmatic use cases that can benefit consumers, businesses and governments globally. — Bernama
