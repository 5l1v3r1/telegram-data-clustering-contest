Tuesday Trivia: Did you know Shah Rukh Khan owns a piece of land on the moon?

12 Nov 2019, 06:08 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shah Rukh Khan is one actor who has carved a niche for himself in Bollywood and his popularity surpasses boundaries now. With millions of fans across the world, SRK enjoys an immense fan following as a result of which he even receives unbelievable gifts every day.

According to a report, an Aussie fan purchases a piece of land on the moon every year for the actor and the 'Zero' actor receives certificates for it every year from the Lunar Republic Society.

Well, given SRK's down to earth approach towards his fans, the versatile actor has met this woman and even keeps in touch with her via emails.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, SRK recently promised fans on his birthday that he will definitely announce a film soon. As per reports making rounds on the internet, Shah Rukh is rumoured to collaborate with South filmmaker Atlee for his next project which would be titled 'Sanki'.
