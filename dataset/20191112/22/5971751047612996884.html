Nokia SmartTV to run Android 9, expected in early December

12 Nov 2019, 22:01 by Stipe

Times of India reports new details about the Nokia Smart TV that is expected to soon hit the Indian market. The sources of Times of India reveal some new details that weren’t mentioned in the official press release posted by Flipkart, which will be the exclusive Nokia brand partner for the TV segment in India.

From the press release we saw that the expected Smart TV will use JBL Audio technology and that Filpkart will be responsible for manufacturing and sales. At the same time, rumors appeared that the TV will have a 55″ 4K panel. Times of India source says the SmartTV will use an LED panel, with Intelligent Dimming technology. The TV is expected to run Android 9 Pie and have access to the Google Play Store.

Details about the price remain unknown, but the launch of Nokia Smart TV should happen in early December, per Times of India sources.
