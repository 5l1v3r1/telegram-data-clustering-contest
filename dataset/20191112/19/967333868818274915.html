Lizard Shocked as Sees Itself in Mirror

12 Nov 2019, 19:00

A lizard is an unusual animal that has provoked strong opinions and reactions in humans over centuries. It has been revered, hated, been printed on the body, painted on the walls of houses and worshiped.

Mirror Test is an experiment developed in 1970 by psychologist Gordon Gallup Jr. to determine if animals have the ability to recognize themselves in a mirror.

In this video lizard sees itself in the mirror and its reaction looks weird.
