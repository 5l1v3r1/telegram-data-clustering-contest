Liverpool fans fume as Mohamed Salah ruled out of Egypt matches ahead of Crystal Palace clash

The Liverpool winger aggravated his injury in the win over Man City and will miss the upcoming internationals

12 Nov 2019, 19:06 by Andy Ha

Mohamed Salah has been ruled out of Egypt's upcoming Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers against Kenya and Comoros with an ankle injury.

The Egyptian forward has been hampered by his ankle since limping off in the win over Leicester, but a tackle from Fernandinho during the 3-1 win over Man City on Sunday appeared to aggravate the problem.

According to ESPN, Liverpool were concerned by the severity of the injury and wanted Salah to remain on Merseyside for tests but the Egpytian national team had insisted that he reports for duty to be assessed.

Egypt have now confirmed that Salah is not fit enough to play and posted pictures of the forward wearing a protective boot on his left ankle.
