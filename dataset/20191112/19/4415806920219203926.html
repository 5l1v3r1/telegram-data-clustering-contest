Quiz: Can you match this sandwich to the country it’s from?

12 Nov 2019, 19:04

Few things are as universal as a sandwich, and every culture has their own variation on the simple formula of bread, stuffed with a filling.

However, that doesn’t mean you’re going to find a simple cheese sarnie all over the world. Instead, each country tends to champion their own sandwich, filled with ingredients that reflect the local cuisine.

The question is: Which sandwich comes from which country?
