Editor’s Pick: Guess Liberty Sherpa Moto Jacket

12 Nov 2019, 19:55 by The Daily Front Row

What: A classic faux-leather moto jacket with warm Sherpa detail and lining for cold days. Featuring a notched lapel and metal zipper hardware.

Who: GUESS was established in 1981 by the Marciano brothers, who traded the South of France for Southern California and used their European sensibility to reimagine American denim. Bloomingdale’s was the first department store to embrace the fledgling brand, ordering two dozen pairs of jeans, which proceeded to sell out in a matter of hours. Today, Guess is a global lifestyle brand creating everything from watches to fragrance to ready-to-wear.

Why: Although technically a men’s jacket, it looks just as cool, if not cooler, on a woman, especially when worn oversized  with an extra-long sleeve cuff. Buy it for your guy and then wear it whenever you want!

Where: Guess.com

How (much): $198
