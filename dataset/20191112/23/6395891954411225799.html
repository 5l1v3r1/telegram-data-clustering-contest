Fauria to broadcast for 25 straight hours on WEEI

12 Nov 2019, 23:53 by Butch Stearns

DEDHAM, Mass. - Christian Fauria is on a mission. His mission is to raise awareness about diabetes and also to raise $25,000 for the American Diabetes Association.

To do this, the former New England Patriots two-time Super Bowl champion and current WEEI co-host of OMF (Ordway, Merloni and Fauria) has launched a campaign called "25 for 25."

Fauria is set to broadcast for 25 straight hours on WEEI with the goal of raising at least $25,000 for his cause.

To donate, just go here: WEEI.com/25.
