Hilldale kicks off holiday season with tree lighting, photos with Santa

12 Nov 2019, 23:36 by Logan Rude

MADISON, Wis. - Hilldale is kicking off the holiday season Nov. 24 with a tree lighting, free photos with Santa and his reindeer, and live music, according to a news release.

Seasonal festivities will continue through December, including an event for people to get their photo taken with their dog and Santa, the release said. The Wisconsin Dickens Carolers will be at Hilldale from Nov. 29 through Christmas Eve and Santa will be there from Dec. 6 through Dec. 23.

All events at Hilldale can be found on its website.
