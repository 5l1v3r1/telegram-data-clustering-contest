Regina police investigating city’s eighth homicide of 2019

Regina police are treating the death of 27-year-old Jordan Gaiton Denton as a homicide.

12 Nov 2019, 23:08 by Regina Leader-Post

Regina police are treating the death of 27-year-old Jordan Gaiton Denton as a homicide.

On Saturday, Regina police responded to a report of a deceased male on the 800 block of Robinson Street at approximately 3:25 a.m. EMS also responded, and confirmed that the man was deceased.

On Tuesday, police issued a press release identifying Denton, and confirming his death is being treated as a homicide. Denton’s family has been notified of his death.

This is Regina’s eighth homicide of 2019. The Regina Police Service (RPS) Major Crimes unit and Coroners Service of Saskatchewan is continuing to investigate.

Anyone with information that could help police can contact the RPS at at 306-777-6500 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477 (TIPS).
