Indian Punjab CM Amarinder thanks PM Imran for opening Kartarpur Corridor

12 Nov 2019, 18:41 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – After the historic inauguration of Kartarpur Corridor, Indian Punjab Chief Minister Amarinder Singh thanked Prime Minister Imran Khan for making the Sikh community’s dream come true.

According to ANI, Indian politician expressed his gratitude towards PM Imran and Indian PM Narendra Modi saying, “I thank PM Modi and PM Imran Khan for realizing Sikh community’s dream of visiting Kartarpur Gurdwara.”
