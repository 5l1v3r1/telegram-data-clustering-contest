Oman welcomes Saudi efforts to settle Yemen crisis

12 Nov 2019, 18:49 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Sultanate welcomes the efforts by Saudi Arabia to reach a Riyadh Agreement and hopes that this would lead to an end to the current crisis in Yemen.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MoFA) Oman said in a statement online, "The Sultanate welcomes the efforts of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in reaching a Riyadh agreement between some Yemeni parties and expresses the hope that this will pave the way for a comprehensive political settlement to end the current crisis in the Republic of Yemen."
