LA CROSSE, Wis. - A big change could be on the way for City Brewery in La Crosse.

Pabst Brewing is slated to move the majority of production to City Brewery facilities which includes La Crosse, Latrobe, Pennsylvania and Memphis according to brewing industry website Brewbound.

The move will be completed by 2024.

The agreement lasts until 2040.

Information about how it may impact City Brewery in La Crosse has not been announced.

Officials with Pabst Brewing Co. did not immediately return a request for comment from News 3 Now's sister station, WKBT-TV.
