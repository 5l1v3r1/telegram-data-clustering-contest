Tony Stark Reunites With Adult Daughter in Deleted ‘Avengers: Endgame’ Scene

12 Nov 2019, 21:50 by Genevieve Scarano

Tony Stark’s death was one of the saddest moments in Avengers: Endgame. However, the water works are about to begin again, because the hero reunites with his grown-up daughter in a deleted scene that was just released by Disney.

On Tuesday, Disney+ officially launched, making many movies, including Avengers: Endgame, streamable to fans. Subscribers noticed the two-minute scene while they were watching the movie on the streaming service, INSIDER reported.
