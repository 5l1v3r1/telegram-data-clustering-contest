Protester shot at roadblock near Beirut: security source

12 Nov 2019, 21:47

BEIRUT (Reuters) - An armed man shot and critically wounded a protester who was blocking a road in the town of Khaldeh south of Lebanon’s capital Beirut on Tuesday night, a security source said.

The protester was shot in the head and the shooter was detained for investigation, the source said.
