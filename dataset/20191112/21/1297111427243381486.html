60-year-old man charged in death of 61-year-old Paul Brule

A 60-year-old Regina man is charged with manslaughter in the death of a 61-year-old man in Regina's Core neighbourhood last week.

12 Nov 2019, 21:19 by Regina Leader-Post

A 60-year-old Regina man is charged with manslaughter in the death of a 61-year-old man in Regina’s Core neighbourhood last week.

Douglas James Fletcher, 60, is expected to make his first appearance in Regina Provincial Court on Tuesday. Fletcher was taken into custody on Friday and subsequently charged.

The deceased man is Paul Richard Brule, 61, of Regina. His family has been notified of his death.

It is Regina’s seventh homicide of 2019.

Officers responded to the 1800 block of St. John Street for a report of a deceased male after 11 p.m. Nov. 7. EMS also responded and confirmed the person was dead.

Officers secured the scene and requested additional resources, including the provincial coroner, forensic identification and major crimes units.
