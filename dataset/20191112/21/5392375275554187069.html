Xiaomi Mi Wireless Mouse 2 released for only 59 yuan ($9)
2.5 (50%) 6 votes

In April 2017, Xiaomi released its first-generation wireless mouse and it came with a 69 yuan ($10) price tag. With 2.4G high-speed transmission, the use distance is up to 10 meters. This first version comes with a 1200dpi precise positioning and the side also has a navigation side button. This ensures that you can return to a previous web page with a single click.

After two years, Xiaomi Mi wireless mouse 2 is out and it has a 59 yuan ($9) price tag. The 2.4G transmission speed remains the same but the minimal design is much better than the previous generation.

The Xiaomi Mi wireless mouse 2 uses better design materials. The surface treatment is smoother with a better feel. The fluffy effect particles coupled with the spray paint coating gives it a delicate and skin-friendly touch.

It adopts an aluminum alloy roller and the surface is sandblasted and anodized. This gives it a better texture. It also has a mute button design that makes the mouse operation quiet, quick rebound and durable. Whether it’s in the bedroom, in the conference room, or in the library, it won’t bother others. It completely eliminates the clicking sound.

It comes with a magnetic adsorption top cover structure. This ensures that the battery and receiver can be easily removed and installed by opening the upper cover. According to the company, the battery can last for one year.

The Xiaomi Mi Wireless Mouse 2 has a 1000dpi photoelectric sensor that can be accurately positioned when moving quickly. At the same time, it has excellent over-surface properties and is available in two colours – black and white.
