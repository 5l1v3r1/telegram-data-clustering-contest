Photos: Ananya Panday and Shanaya Kapoor enjoys a movie night in the city

12 Nov 2019, 21:42 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ananya Panday and her BFF Shanaya Kapoor often get snapped in the city on various occasions. And on Tuesday night, the young starlets stepped out to watch a movie together.

For the outing, Ananya was in a black t-shirt paired with matching ripped jeans and cool shining shoes. While Shanya wore a red full-sleeve top teamed with blue ripped denim and flip flops. The duo was accompanied by Ananya's father and mother, Chunky Pandey and Bhavana Pandey. Paparazzi clicked them all while leaving the cinema hall.
