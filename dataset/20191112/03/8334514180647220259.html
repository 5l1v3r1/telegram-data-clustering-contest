Syrian army fires on 'hostile' target in skies west of Damascus: state TV

12 Nov 2019, 03:40

CAIRO - (Reuters) - The Syrian army fired on a “hostile target” in the town of Daraya, west of the capital, Damascus, Syrian state media said late on Tuesday.

The nature of the target was not immediately clear and the report did not say if it had been brought down in the action.
