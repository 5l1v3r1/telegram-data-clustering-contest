Meat Puppets & Mudhoney announce 2020 co-headlining tour

12 Nov 2019, 09:31 by Bill Pearis

Two legendary bands — Meat Puppets and Mudhoney — are teaming up for a 2020 co-headlining tour. So far only West Coast / Southwest dates have been announced, all of which are in May and hit San Francisco, Los Angeles, Tucson, Sante Fe, Denver and Salt Lake City. They do promise more shows will be announced — all dates are listed below.

Meat Puppets, who released Dusty Notes (their first album with the original lineup since 1995) earlier this year, have a couple shows lined up this month, too, playing Phoenix and Pioneertown, CA over Thanksgiving weekend. Those dates are listed below as well.

Mudhoney’s fall tour hit NYC back in October.
