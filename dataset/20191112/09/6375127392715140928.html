LTFRB accepts Yuletide special bus permit applications

12 Nov 2019, 09:55 by Jun I. Legaspi

THE Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board (LTFRB) announced it is now accepting applications for special permits for bus units that will be operating during the holidays from  December 23 this year to January 3, 2020.

The LTFRB said they started accepting applications since Monday and will end on November 22.

Based on the rules and regulations imposed by the LTFRB, only 25% of the total authorized units per franchise is allowed.

Units applied should not be more than 10 years old from the date of manufacture. Certificate of Public Conveyance (CPC) with 1 authorized unit is not allowed.

Indicate the specific address of the terminals at about end of the applied route.

Meanwhile, the Public Commuters and Motorists Alliance (PCMA) commended the leadership of the LTFRB for the early preparation.

“Mas maaga ang preparation mas maganda para maihanda ang lahat at maiwasan ang  unnecessary vehicular accidents,” said the PCMA.

Bus operators also thanked the LTFRB for the  early announcement.

“Maganda po ito  para ngayon pa lang exclusivelymaiayos namin ‘yung mga bus na dapat sa  long trips para sa safety ng commuters,” the bus operators said.
