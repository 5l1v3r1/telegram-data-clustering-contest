Bride-to-be, 25, puts engagement ring on Facebook for £200 after split with 'd***head'

Since Abbey Oxley, of Sheffield, South Yorkshire, ended her relationship with Steve Harvey, the mum-of-one listed the diamond ring on Facebook and labelled him as 'not worth the oxygen'

12 Nov 2019, 09:21 by Bradley Jolly, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/bradley-jolly/

A mum has put her engagement ring up for sale for £200 on Facebook after splitting with her "d***head" partner.

Abbey Oxley, 25, listed the diamond ring on the site this week with a message saying: "Had it on about 2 weeks. He's a d***head wasn't worth the oxygen."

She ended things with Steve Harvey 34, after a string of rows.

"Within minutes it was shooting off with people messaging me and bidding over the ring. I can't believe it," the mum-of-one, from Sheffield, South Yorkshire, said.

"I'd had enough of all the arguing so was like 'f**k it, might as well make something from this'.

"Hopefully it will sell and the offers are serious because I'd like the money and it's a bit of a silver lining then."

 

Abbey admitted her relationship with Steve was "rocky anyway".

They'd only been dating for a year and a half, according to Sun Online .

And Abbey, who works at a bookmakers, said Steve bought the ring from Warren James recently.

Now she intends to pamper herself with the cash.

The mum added: "He wasn't happy about the post and messaged me about it.

"If I get the £200, I'll spend it on a spa day for myself because I need to relax after getting out of that relationship. Basically a 'f**k you present' to myself."

But Steve, who is a tiler, defended himself.

"She knows I'm the best s**g she's ever had," he told the publication.
