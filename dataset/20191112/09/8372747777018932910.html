Mega Millions jackpot up to $163 million for Tuesday night's drawing

12 Nov 2019, 09:33 by WFTX Digital Team

TALLAHASSEE, Fla. -- It's been more than a month and a half since anybody has picked the winning numbers in the Mega Millions lottery, pushing the jackpot up to hefty size.

Tuesday night's jackpot is worth an estimated $163 million, according to the Florida Lottery.

The last winner was on September 24th, when an anonymous Texas player won $225 million.

The next drawing will be on Tuesday night at 11 p.m. But good luck; your chances of winning are 1 in 302,575,350.

Tickets must be purchased by 10 p.m. each night, and the drawing can be watched live on Fox 4 in Southwest Florida.

And if you miss out on the Mega Millions jackpot, you can try Wednesday night's Powerball drawing, with a $60 million jackpot on the line.
