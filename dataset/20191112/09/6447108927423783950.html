Crash Team Racing update quietly adds new Ring Rally mode

12 Nov 2019, 09:40 by Jim Hargreaves

With Crash Team Racing’s Neon Circus Grand Prix event well underway, developer Beenox has added a new racing mode some fans may have completely missed.

Amidst the game’s new wave of themed unlock (which include Pasadena O’Possum, Ebenezer Von Clutch and Koala Kong as playable racers) the developer has added Ring Rally to the list of events available in local play.
