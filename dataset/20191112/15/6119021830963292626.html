President's Rule in Maharashtra imposed under PM, Amit Shah's pressure, says Digvijaya Singh

Singh said that Maharashtra Governor Bhagat Singh Koshyari had been following the due process for the formation of the government in the state until the recommendation of the President's Rule

12 Nov 2019, 15:04 by ANI

Senior Congress leader Digvijaya Singh on Tuesday said that the decision to impose the President's Rule in Maharashtra was taken under pressure of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Home Minister Amit Shah.
