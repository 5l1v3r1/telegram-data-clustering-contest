Jazz Up Your Thanksgiving with a Mac and Cheese Turkey

12 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Johnny Thrash

Here’s one that’s bound to have the internet divided this holiday season.

Reynold’s Wrap has rolled out their annual Thanksgiving recipes, including a recipe for a mac and cheese turkey.

It’s a fairly simple recipe in which you coat the turkey with the powdered cheese mix prior to baking it. You then serve the cooked turkey on a bed of mac and cheese.

Of course, the recipe calls for the turkey to be wrapped in Reynold’s Wrap in order to “hold in the juices.”

If the mac and cheese turkey recipe isn’t up your alley, but you’re still feeling adventurous, check out their recipes for cool ranch turkey, Flamin' Hot Cheetos turkey, and Mountain Dew turkey.

While there’s no guarantee you’ll score a hit with any of the turkeys, you’ll definitely get people talking.
