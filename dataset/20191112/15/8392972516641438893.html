Fans beside themselves as they think John Lewis has dropped a teaser trailer for its new Christmas advert

It's on its way!

12 Nov 2019, 15:43 by Paul Hirons

We've already had the Sainsbury's and M&S Christmas adverts, and now it seems we're about to get the eagerly-anticipated John Lewis festive advert.

The legendary high-street brand has a reputation for producing the best and most magical Xmas commercials.

A new Twitter account has been created and posted a cryptic message, which fans think heralds this year's Yuletide advert from John Lewis.
