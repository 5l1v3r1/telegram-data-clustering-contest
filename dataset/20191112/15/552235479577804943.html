Chance to win free VIP entry and drinks for Coatbridge's 30s & Sugar nightclub

To be in with a chance to win, all you have to do is get your picture taken when you are partying in the club on a Friday or Saturday night.

12 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Airdrie and Coatbridge Advertiser, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/airdrie-and-coatbridge-advertiser/

The Advertiser has teamed up with Coatbridge’s premier nightclub – 30s & Sugar – to give four of our lucky readers free VIP entry to the Main Street venue each weekend plus a bottle of bubbly to get your night going.

To be in with a chance to win, all you have to do is get your picture taken when you are partying in the club on a Friday or Saturday night.

Then, watch out for it appearing in the paper the following week.

If your picture is chosen, then it will appear on the club’s Facebook page.

The clubber with the most likes will be awarded Clubber of the Week title and will win the fabulous prize from 30s & Sugar.

●To vote visit www.facebook.com/30sandSugar/

To claim your prize, take your winning image from the paper along to the club.
