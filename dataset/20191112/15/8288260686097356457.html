'Shine for us' Birmingham City fans react to star's four-word message

For the second time this season Birmingham City's Jefferson Montero came off the bench in the closing stages of a match and terrorised the visitors’ defence

12 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Brian Dick, https://www.facebook.com/blueswriterbriandick/?fref=ts

Jefferson Montero reminded Birmingham City fans what they had been missing with a thrilling cameo at the end of Saturday’s loss to Fulham.

The on-loan winger ended a spell of more than two months on the sidelines to make just his third appearance since his summer move from Swansea.

For the second time this season he came on in the closing stages and terrorised the visitors’ defence, setting up a chance for Lukas Jutkiewicz and sending over a series of crosses from the bye-line that suggested a way back for Blues .
