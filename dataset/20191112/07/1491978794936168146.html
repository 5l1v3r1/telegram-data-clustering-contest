Stylish pooches: Strutz canine fashion show – in pictures

12 Nov 2019, 07:30

More than 40 dogs took part in the Strutz canine fashion show in Chelsea, London, hosted by Love My Human, including rescued dogs from war-torn countries to raise money for the animal welfare charity PDSA and the Wild at Heart Foundation. Photographer Sophie Green went to meet some of the stylish dogs and their owners

Caitlin and her cockapoo Mabel

Maggie the Wunderdog was rescued from the streets of Beirut by Wild at Heart Foundation. She had been tied up and used as target practice, had both eyes pulled out and her ear cut off. Thanks to the generosity of donors she has found a happy home in Brighton with her human Kasey, and she spends her time spreading love as a therapy dog. In this picture she is wearing a handmade bandana by Love My Human

Mira with her cavapoochon Sashimi

Elly is a Wild at Heart rescue dog who has climbed Ben Nevis and Snowdon and even run marathons. In this picture she is wearing a hand knit Vivienne jumper by Max Bone and a Bartock and Boog black night lightning collar and lead set
