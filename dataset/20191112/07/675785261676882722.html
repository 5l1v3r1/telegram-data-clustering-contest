I'm A Celebrity's Myles Stephenson planning to 'embrace single life' after Gabby Allen split

The Rak-Su singer is single following his split from Love Island's Gabby Allen amid cheating claims - and says he has no plans to find romance in the jungle

12 Nov 2019, 07:21 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Myles Stephenson says he's planning to "embrace single life" during his time Down Under.

The Rak-Su singer, 28, has landed in Australia for I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here!

And the X Factor winner, who split from his girlfriend, Love Island's Gabby Allen , earlier this year amid cheating claims, insists he's not looking for romance in the jungle.

He said: "I am single. I have just got out of a relationship but I think I want to be single for a bit and embrace single life!"

In August, Gabby confirmed hr split from Myles after reports she'd caught him sexting other women.

The reality star was said to have dumped the singer after finding out he'd been exchanging flirty messages with other girls on  Instagram .

 

As she confirmed it was over, Gabby begged fans to pass on any information they have about his unfaithful behaviour.

She said in a statement on Instagram: "The news paper articles are true. I am no longer in a relationship. I hope youse have all had a better week than me!

"If anyone else has anything they need to tell me, please do x"

Her message seemed to suggest that someone has previously reached out to her on social media with information about her ex.

Gabby and Myles started dating last September, and were inseparable at first.

Myles moved into Gabby's North London flat earlier this year, but friends say she'd begun to grow suspicious of his behaviour in the last months of their relationship.

It was a big blow to Gabby after her ex Marcel Somerville, who she met on Love Island, cheated on her.

Gabby has made it clear she won't be watching Myles on the show.

She told  The Daily Mail  : "Hmmm. I don’t watch that show anyway."

 

Gabby stressed people can't get over their exes while they remain in contact with one another.

She added: "No [we don't talk]. I don’t wish him any harm though. I find it fascinating that people can be friends with their exes. You can’t get over someone when you’re still in contact."
