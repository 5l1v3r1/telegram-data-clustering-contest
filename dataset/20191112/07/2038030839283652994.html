Lamar Odom is engaged!

12 Nov 2019, 07:41 by Bashiera Parker (@bparks_)

Cape Town – Lamar Odom and Sabrina Parr are engaged!

The professional basketball player who made his relationship with the personal trainer public just three months ago, shared the news on Instagram on Monday writing, "Introducing my new fiancé!! Soon to be Mrs Parr-Odom.

"She the ONE!!!"

In the photo, Sabrina is wearing a pear-shaped ring she later showed off in a few videos of her own.

"I SAID YES!!!" she captioned it, adding the hashtags, "#mrsparrodom" and "#lamarandsabrina".

SEE THAT POST HERE:

Although Lamar and Sabrina haven't been dating for very long, the 40-year-old shared that they were dating in August, he wrote on Instagram, "What we have is much more than they can see..." Shortly after the soon-to-be-married couple also spoke openly about their relationship on Dish Nation.

Sabrina said, "You have to meet people where they are." When talking about Lamar's troubled past, touching on his relationship and very public split from Khloé Kardashian, she continued, "He was never in a position to be someone's husband, you know? He was sick, he needed to heal, he needed to grieve. He experienced a lot of losses. And he never had time to go through that."

Congratulations to the happy couple!
