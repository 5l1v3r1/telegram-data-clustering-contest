Dabangg 3 Badass dialogue: Salman Khan asks for a counter dialogue from his fans

12 Nov 2019, 07:14 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

As 'Dabangg 3' inches towards its much-awaited release, fans are all excited to see their favourite Chulbul Pandey back on big screens, delivering some ' Dhaasu' dialogues in this cop-drama. While the trailer is full of masala, it seems Salman Khan is still in search of a badass dialogue for his movie which he can inculcate in one of his action scenes.

Yes, you heard it right, today Salman Khan on his social media handles posted a video, wherein he is asking his fans for a kick-ass dialogue, which he can include in one of his action scenes. He urged his fans in Chulbul Panday style with a caption that reads, "Hum chup hain, intezaar mein sahi counter ke. Aur humein poora bharosa hai ki aap humein denge, ek jabardast counter. #Dabangg3BadassDialogue."
