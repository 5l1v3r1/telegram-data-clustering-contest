High School Gameday Game of the Week: Week 11

12 Nov 2019, 04:39 by Katherine Ellis

Division 4 North: Revere at Melrose
Two undefeated teams will face off Friday night at 7 p.m. in Melrose. The Patriots and the Red Raiders have both had perfect 9-0 records this season. Revere is coached by Louis Cicatelli and ranked 17th in the state. Melrose is coached by Tim Morris and ranked 8th in the state.

Division 2 South: Mansfield at Natick
Mansfield is looking to end Natick’s perfect 10-0 season on the road this Friday night at 7 p.m. in Natick. The Hornets have only lost once this season on the road and currently have an 8-1 record under Michael Redding's coaching. The Redhawks are coached by Mark Mortarelli and ranked 3rd in the state. 

Division 3 South: Duxbury at Hingham
Duxbury lost their first two games of the season and has not lost since, giving them an 8-2 record. The Dragons are coached by David Marimaron and are ranked 18th in the state. Hingham also has only two losses this season earning them a 9-2 record this year. The Harbormen are coached by Jim Connor. Duxbury and Hingham face off at 7 p.m. in Hingham Friday night. 

Division 5 South: Holliston at Canton
Holliston is looking to give Canton it's first loss of the season this Friday night at 7 p.m. in Canton. Holliston has an 8-1 record and is ranked 14th overall in the state. Canton has had a flawless 9-0 season and is ranked 23rd in Massachusetts. The Panthers are coached by Todd Kiley and the Bulldogs are coached by Dave Bohane.  With over 1,000 votes in just three hours, Revere at Melrose is currently in the lead with 51% of the votes as of Monday night.
