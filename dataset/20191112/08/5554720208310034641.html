Patanjali founder Ramdev’s crazy hug video involving Arnab Goswami sets internet on fire

12 Nov 2019, 08:15 by JKR Staff

Republic TV’s Arnab Goswami recently welcomed controversial guru and founder of Patanjali Ayurved, Ramdev, in his studio on the day the Supreme Court delivered its much-awaited verdict on the Ayodhya land dispute case. With a favourable Supreme Court ruling, Ramdev was unable to hide his excitement and decided to hug and lift Goswami in the air. The video of Ramdev hugging and lifting the Republic TV founder in the air has now triggered a wave of memes on social media,

Ramdev lifting Goswami in the air may have gone unnoticed had Congress spokesperson Pawan Khera not tweet the photo asking his followers to write a suitable caption. He wrote, “Caption contest. Intelligent captions will get RTd/liked.”
