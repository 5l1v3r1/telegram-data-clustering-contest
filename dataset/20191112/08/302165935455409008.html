Hong Kong stocks finish higher

12 Nov 2019, 08:36

HONG KONG, Nov 12 — Hong Kong stocks ended with gains today, clawing back some of the hefty losses from the previous day but traders remained on edge as the city was hit by fresh protests that blocked key roads and shut businesses.

The Hang Seng Index gained 0.52 per cent, or 138.73 points, to 27,065.28.

The benchmark Shanghai Composite Index added 0.17 per cent, or 4.85 points, to 2,914.82 and the Shenzhen Composite Index, which tracks stocks on China’s second exchange, also rose 0.17 per cent, or 2.75 points, to 1,614.19. — AFP
