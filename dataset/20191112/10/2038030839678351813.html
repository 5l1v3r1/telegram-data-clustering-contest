Kylie Jenner denies sending cease and desist letters over 'Rise and Shine'

12 Nov 2019, 10:21

Cape Town – Kylie Jenner has responded to reports that she sent cease and desist letters over her viral catchphrase turned pop culture bop.

A clip of her singing the words "rise and shine" quickly went viral a little while back.

Soon after reports came flooding in that the beauty mogul wanted to trademark her famous words.

But finding herself in a whole new social media frenzy, this time about the rumour she sent cease and desist letters to a family selling T-shirts that say "rise and shine" on it, according to East Coast Renaissance, Kylie has responded.
