The current claim

The royal commission into aged care has wasted little time, releasing an interim report describing "a sad and shocking system that diminishes Australia as a nation".

Labor immediately suggested the blame lay solely with the Coalition.

In an appearance on Channel Nine's Today Show, Deputy Leader Richard Marles was asked why Labor didn't address longer term "systemic failures" when it was in government.
