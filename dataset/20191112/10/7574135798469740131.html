Hall & Oates announce 2020 tour, dates w/ Squeeze & KT Tunstall (MSG included)

12 Nov 2019, 10:43 by Bill Pearis

Legendary hitmaking duo Daryl Hall and John Oates will be touring in 2020 and among their just-announced dates are a couple of big shows where Squeeze and KT Tunstall will also be on the bill: Hershey, PA’s Biant Center on February 26, and NYC’s Madison Square Garden on February 28. Tickets for both of those shows are on various presales now, and go on sale to the general public on Friday, November 15 at 10 AM.

Hall and Oates have shows in the spring in California and Connecticut, and John Oates has Solo shows in November and December. All dates are listed below.
