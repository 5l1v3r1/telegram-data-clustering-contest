'Messi Will Be Linked To Barca For The Rest Of His Life'

12 Nov 2019, 10:28 by SL International

As speculation surrounding Lionel Messi's future rages on, FC Barcelona president Josep Maria Bartomeu has made a bold statement about what lies in store for the Argentine superstar.

Read: 'Messi Is Uncontrollable'

With Messi having previously hinted at a return to his native Argentina, Bartomeu has now seemingly sought to ease Blaugrana fans' fears by making a bold statement about his star man's future.

"Leo Messi will still play with us for the next two, three, four or five years, I have no doubts about that," the 56-year-old said, as per the Daily Mail.

"Surely it will be the wish of all parties, if he (Messi) feels strong and ambitious, to extend this contract indefinitely.

"Ultimately it is he who must decide. He has earned the right to decide when he will stop playing football.

"But like he said a few weeks ago, he wants to finish his playing career at Barcelona.

"Over the next two or three seasons, our leader will continue to be Leo Messi. There's no doubt that he is still young, still strong. He is still ambitious."

Bartomeu also revealed that there will be a place at the club for 32-year-old Messi even after he ends his playing career.

"I have no doubt that after Messi finishes his career as a footballer, he will remain linked to this club for the rest of his life," Bartomeu added.
