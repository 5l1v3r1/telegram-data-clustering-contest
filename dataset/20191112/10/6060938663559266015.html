Photos: Deepika Padukone channels her inner diva as she sports a trendy yellow tracksuit at the airport

12 Nov 2019, 10:01 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh recently attended a friends wedding in Bangalore last week. While several pictures and videos of the couple have been making rounds on the internet, burning the dance moves with their impeccable chemistry, the 'Padmaavat' actress returned to the bay on Tuesday afternoon.

While Deepika had taken to social media yesterday to reveal that she had fallen sick, she was spotted at the Mumbai airport sporting a trendy yellow tracksuit. She completed her casual look with stylish white sneakers, white tank top and sunglasses.

The 'Bajirao Mastani' actress, who enjoys a huge fan following throughout the world, can be seen channelling her inner diva as she headed towards her vehicle.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Deepika will soon be seen in Meghna Gulzar’s 'Chhapaak'. She will also be seen with Ranveer in Kabir Khan's sports drama '83'. Not only that but Deepika will also be essaying the role of Draupadi in Madhu Mantena’s ‘Mahabharat’.
