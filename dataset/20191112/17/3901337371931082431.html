City Defender Latest Arrival In Egypt For SA

12 Nov 2019, 17:35 by Soccer Laduma

As players continue to join the camp of the South African Under-23 team, a Cape Town City defender has become the latest star to arrive for coach David Notoane and the team in camp in Egypt. 

It has been confirmed by the South African Football Association that City’s Keanu Cupido joined the team on Tuesday.

The 21-year old centre-back will be an important addition to Notoane’s side in their attempt to qualify for next year’s Tokyo Summer Olympic Games. 

“It truly is an honour to be here. I would also like to thank the coach (David Notoane) and his technical staff for their trust and belief in me. I will do everything in my might to help the team and contribute in whichever way I can,” said Cupido, as quoted on SAFA’s official website.

The SA U23s take on Ivory Coast today in their second Africa Cup of Nations Group B match at the Al Salam Stadium.
