Murder charges laid after man's stabbing death in Moose Jaw

Jeffrey McCaig has been charged with second-degree murder after a man died from stab wounds following an altercation Sunday night.

12 Nov 2019, 17:07 by Regina Leader-Post

Jeffrey McCaig has been charged with second-degree murder after a man died from stab wounds following an altercation Sunday night.

Police responded to the incident late Sunday evening, Nov. 10, when they were called to a disturbance in the 400 block of Stadacona Street East.

Kevin Hallock, 50, was transported to Dr. F.H. Wigmore Hospital, where he was pronounced dead.

McCaig is to appear in provincial court Tuesday afternoon.

During its investigation, Moose Jaw Police stated it believes that no further danger exists to the public surrounding this incident.
