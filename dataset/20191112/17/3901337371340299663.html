Tau & Co Arrive As Bafana Touchdown In Ghana

12 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Soccer Laduma

Bafana Bafana head coach Molefi Ntseki and the team have been joined in their camp in Ghana by five overseas-based players to continue their preparations for the upcoming Africa Cup of Nations qualifying match. 

Read: Chiefs Lose Analyst To Bafana 

The five players who joined the team on Tuesday afternoon are Percy Tau, Bongani Zungu, Darren Keet, Lebogang Mothiba and Lebogang Phiri. As confirmed on Bafana’s official Twitter account, Thulani Serero and Kamohelo Mokotjo are expected to arrive in camp this evening.  

The team that travelled from the O.R. Tambo International Airport arrived in Ghana earlier today (Tuesday).

The match between Bafana and the Black Stars has been scheduled for the Cape Coast on Thursday evening.
