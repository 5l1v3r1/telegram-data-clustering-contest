'Petrified' Geoff Horsfield lifts the lid on Birmingham City's legendary Aston Villa victory

Horsfield was part of Birmingham City team which won 3-0 at St Andrew’s in September 2002 and has recalled how Steve Bruce motivated his men with an Olof Mellberg interview

12 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Brian Dick, https://www.facebook.com/blueswriterbriandick/?fref=ts

Birmingham City great Geoff Horsfield has described how disparaging comments from Aston Villa’s Olof Mellberg inspired Blues to a legendary derby victory over Aston Villa.

In a superb interview with Undr The Cosh podcast the ex-Blues and West Brom striker discusses his relationship with Trevor Francis, the celebrations after winning promotion at the Millennium Stadium - and the first Second City clash in 16 years.

Horsfield, who scored in two games against Villa, was part of the team which won 3-0 at St Andrew’s in September 2002 and he has recalled how then boss Steve Bruce motivated his men following an interview given by the Villa defender.
