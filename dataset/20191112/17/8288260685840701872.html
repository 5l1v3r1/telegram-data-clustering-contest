'Outrageous' - Birmingham City fans pick their player of the season so far

Who has stood out the most for you in a Blues shirt during the 2019-20 campaign to date?

12 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Chris Watson

There have been both highs and lows for Birmingham City so far this season.

With just over a third of the campaign gone, Blues sit 13th in the Sky Bet Championship table.

They are six points adrift of a play-off spot and nine points clear of the relegation zone.

We asked Birmingham fans to pick their player of the season up to this point - and there were some interesting responses.

Maxime Colin and Ivan Sunjic received plenty of votes, with the likes of Dan Crowley, Jude Bellingham, Kristian Pedersen, Fran Villalba and Marc Roberts also getting an honourable mention.
