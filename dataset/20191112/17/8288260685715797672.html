Appeal after thugs raid Handsworth home and escape with Audi A4 - and 47 inch TV

Police have appealed for help to trace these men after the late-night burglary

12 Nov 2019, 17:37 by Charlotte Paxton, https://www.facebook.com/charlottepaxtonbm/

Thugs raided a city house in the early hours of Halloween - and escaped with a widescreen telly and pricey car.

Now police have appealed for help to trace these men, who were caught on a Ring doorbell CCTV camera.

 

The burglary happened on Windermere Road, Handsworth , at 4.30am on October 31.

A gang forced entry to the house, before making off with a 47" Panasonic TV and An Audi A4.

CCTV stills show a number of people who police are keen to find, but officers say it is unclear exactly how many men were involved.

The crystal clear images show various men, some with their hoods up and faces covered.

Some of the men left in a getaway car, while other members of the gang fled on foot.

 

In an appeal, West Midlands Police wrote: "Do you know who any of these men are?

"We are investigating a burglary on Windermere Road in the Handsworth area of Birmingham, at around 4:30am on Thursday, October 31.

"We want to speak to them after they were captured on a doorbell CCTV camera.

"The group forced entry to a house and made off with a 47" Panasonic TV and an Audi A4 car.

"It is unclear how many men were involved, but some of them left in a getaway car and some ran down the road.

"Enquiries continue and anyone with information is asked to contact us via Live Chat at www.west-midlands.police.uk between 8am and midnight, or call 101 anytime.

"To remain anonymous, call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111. Quote crime reference 20BW/257133R/19."
