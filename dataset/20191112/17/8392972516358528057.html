Marks & Spencer launches bright pink Percy Pig muffins and they only cost £1!

Will you be trying one?

12 Nov 2019, 17:17 by Nancy Brown

He's arguably the most famous pig of our time (sorry, Peppa), and now Percy Pig has been immortalised in a Marks & Spencer muffin.

Yes, fans of the jelly sweets can now enjoy a bright pink muffin topped with a Percy.

You'll be able to find the cakes in the in-store bakery.

A spokesperson for M&S said: "We’ve turned your favourite sweet into a fluffy Percy-flavoured muffin, topped with Percy-flavoured buttercream and filled with strawberry jam."
