Nugget Royale is my new lunchtime distraction

There's a simple joy to time-wasters

12 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Jordan Devore

I'm not above spending a sleepy lunch break on browser games, even when I could be out for a relaxing stroll, reading a book, meditating, or accomplishing something else – literally anything at all – instead.

And so I bring you this silly little game, Nugget Royale, in the hope that I'm not alone.
