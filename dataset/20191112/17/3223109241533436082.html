The Oculus Go HMD lets you break out of your home and away from your computer. The VR system is entirely contained inside of the headset, which means you can use it anywhere at any time without the need of a computer or any other equipment.

As a mobile friendly VR headset, the Oculus Go differs significantly from Oculus’s other VR headsets. Without the extra processing power of a computer the Oculus Go is incapable of playing the cutting edge VR games that were developed for the Oculus Rift. The Oculus Go is able to play some games, but these games are necessarily less graphically advanced and closer to the sort of games you would expect to see on your smartphone, but with VR.

The Oculus Go is also able to display VR video content, giving you the feeling of being inside of your favorite shows. You can also use it enter VR chatrooms and talk to people from all over the world. There are also several other VR experiences you can partake in.

The headset and VR content can be controlled using either a series of hand gestures or with a small controller that is included with the HMD. Currently there are two versions of the Oculus Go available.
