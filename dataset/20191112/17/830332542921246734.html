Driver falls asleep at wheel, crashes into guardrail

12 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Logan Rude

BOSCOBEL, Wis. - A Woodman man fell asleep at the wheel and crashed into a guardrail Friday evening while driving home from work, police said.

According to a news release, the man dozed off while driving home just after 6 p.m. He work up right before he crashed into a guardrail. Police said his 2003 Silverado spun around and blocked the westbound lane of Highway 133.

The man refused medical treatment at the scene, according to police.
