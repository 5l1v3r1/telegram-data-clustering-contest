Possible threat against Highland High School in Palmdale being investigated

12 Nov 2019, 20:41 by ABC7.com staff

LANCASTER, Calif. (KABC) -- The Los Angles County Sheriff's Department in Lancaster is investigating an alleged school shooting threat.

They said they received a tip from a concerned family member about a threat possibly made online Monday night about Highland High School in Palmdale.

Deputies are looking into the credibility of the threat.
