Shah Rukh Khan's son Aryan Khan's 22nd birthday: Fans pour in wishes over social media

12 Nov 2019, 20:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Superstar Shah Rukh Khan's Aryan Khan has not marked his debut in Bollywood, however, he enjoys a huge fan following over social media. The star kid is an internet sensation! Pictures and videos of the actor take over the internet in no time.

Today, Aryan turned a year older and his fans poured in love for him in the comment section. He will ring in his 22nd birthday today and fans dropped wishes over his posts on Instagram.

And due to Aryan's uncanny resemblance to his dad, the masses expect the boy to make his debut soon. However, recently in a talk show, SRK revealed that Aryan doesn't want to get into acting. He said that he doesn't think his son has what it takes to be an actor and according to the actor and Aryan also realises that.

Meanwhile, Aryan made a debut of sorts in Bollywood recently as he dubbed for the Hindi version of Hollywood film, 'The Lion King'. Aryan voiced for the character of Simba, while SRK had dubbed for Mufasa.
