Baby in hospital following Ashbourne incident

12 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Ann Casey

A baby is being treated in Temple Street Children's Hospital for serious injuries following an incident at a house in Ashbourne yesterday morning.

A man in his 20s was arrested and is currently being detained, following the incident in which a young woman also received minor injuries.

He is expected to be charged with an assault on the woman and to appear at Trim Court later today.
