Emmerdale SPOILERS: Pregnancy fears for Leanna as she sleeps with Jacob

Jacob was groomed by Leanna's ex-stepmother Maya

12 Nov 2019, 12:43 by Charlotte Rodrigues

It looks like Leanna could be expecting after she throws up the morning after pill after sleeping with Jacob Gallagher in next week's Emmerdale.

David reveals to Leyla that paedophile Maya Stepney, who groomed their son Jacob, is being released from prison leaving her worried she may try to contact the teen.

They make the decision to tell Jacob about Maya's release and they are surprised at how composed he is.

Later Leanna tries to be supportive but Jacob tries to leave quickly and quietly.

Realising he's gone, David and Leyla worry that he's gone to try to meet Maya. Will they manage to track him down before it's too late?

Later, back at the village Jacob tries to speak to Leanna and after a passionate kiss, they end up sleeping together.

Afterwards, Jacob begins to act distant and quickly leaves, leaving Leanna upset.

She later decides to tell her dad's fiancée Bernice about sleeping with Jacob, but the beautician is left conflicted when Leanna asks her not to tell her dad Liam.

However when Leanna throws up in front of Liam as a result of taking the morning after pill, Bernice is forced to come clean and tell her soon-to-be husband about Leanna and Jacob.
