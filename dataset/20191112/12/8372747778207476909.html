Women wearing Disney shirts are wanted for Cape Coral theft

12 Nov 2019, 12:56 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Two apparent Disney fans are wanted for a retail theft in Cape Coral.

According to Cape Coral Police, the two women pictured here are responsible for a theft at a Cape Coral Publix.

The women were seen wearing Simba and Stich t-shirts.

Police say the theft totaled more than $700 in merchandise.

Anyone with information on these women are asked to call Cape Coral Police at 239-574-3223.
