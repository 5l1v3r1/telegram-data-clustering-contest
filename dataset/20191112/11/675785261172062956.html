I'm A Celebrity always has secret star who stays in luxury hotel for entire time

There's always one extra celebrity who flies out to the jungle every year but doesn't get to participate in came life - unless something goes wrong

12 Nov 2019, 11:52 by Emmeline Saunders

Every year, 10 stars jet out to Australia to be the face of the I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! series.

Occasionally there'll be a bombshell arrival or two - Noel Edmonds was last year's surprise campmate - but for the most part, what you see on screen is what you get.

However, hosts Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly have revealed that there's always one secret celeb who jets out separately from the rest of the line-up and is never seen on camera.

That's because the secret star acts as an emergency back-up just in case one of the original campmates falls ill or pulls out before the launch show.

It's a great deal for the stand-in, who gets to stay in the lap of luxury at the Palazzo Versace hotel in the Gold Coast, eating and drinking on ITV's tab for the whole time the show is being filmed.

If they're lucky, not a single bull testicle or emu anus will pass their lips.

Ant and Dec uncovered the practice in their 2010 autobiography Ooh! What a Lovely Pair: Our Story.

In it, Ant recalled the first series of I'm A Celebrity in 2002, when they had no idea what to expect.

"When we arrived at the big hotel, we had the first of several thousand surreal experiences we'd face over the next few weeks," he wrote.

"We bumped into Keith 'Cheggers Plays Pop' Chegwin. Every year on I'm A Celebrity they fly stand-ins out in case any of the cast get ill or realise what they're getting themselves into and, in series one, Cheggers was that stand-in."

Ant went on: "One thing about Cheggers is that he's a very heavy smoker and, when we ran into him, he'd just got off the 24-hour flight from London. To combat his cigarette cravings on the plane, he'd come up with a carefully thought-out plan - he would wear nicotine patches, chew nicotine gum and drink copious amounts of Coke.

"All of this meant that when we saw him, he was completely wired. He was also keen on showing us some of the home-made footage he'd already taken around the hotel."

Ant finished: "When we bumped into Cheggers, he had just finished filming a cassowary [a type of Australian emu] attacking its own reflection in a car door. So there we were, jetlagged, standing in our second hotel of the day with wired children's TV legend Keith Chegwin showing us home-video footage of a giant bird attacking its own reflection."

This year's stand-in celebrity has - as always - been kept secret by tight-lipped ITV bosses.

The main line-up is made up of Nadine Coyle, Caitlyn Jenner, Ian Wright, Andrew Maxwell, Jacqueline Jossa, James Haskell, Roman Kemp, Kate Garraway, Adele Roberts and Myles Stephenson.
