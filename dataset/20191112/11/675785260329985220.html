BREAKING Brothers murdered by mum Sarah Barrass were strangled and had bin bags on heads

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

12 Nov 2019, 11:29 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

Two brothers who were murdered by their mum and a family member were strangled and had bin bags placed over their heads, a court heard.

Tristan and Blake Barrass, 13 and 14, both died of asphyxiation.

But it was unclear whether the strangling or the bin bags were the cause, Sheffield Crown Court heard as their mum, Sarah Barrass, and family member Brandon Machin were sentenced on Monday.
