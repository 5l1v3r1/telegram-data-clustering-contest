12 Nov 2019, 11:07 by Andrew Sacher

Are you so caught up in best of the decade lists that you almost forgot best of 2019 lists would be coming soon too? Well, here’s Rough Trade with your reminder. They’re always the first major list-maker out of the gate, and this year is no different. The full list of 100 is below, and you can read their commentary on each album in the top 10 here.
