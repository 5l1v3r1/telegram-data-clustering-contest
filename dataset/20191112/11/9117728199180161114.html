Vettel admits it was tough to watch Mercedes being dominant at start of the season

12 Nov 2019, 11:38 by Tom Mason

Sebastian Vettel found it tough at the beginning of this season and he has admitted that it was difficult to watch how good a start Mercedes had to the season.

Mercedes secured both titles after being utterly dominant in the opening races of the season, winning all of the first eight races.

Only Max Verstappen away from Mercedes was able to win a race before the summer break.

"At that moment you do not give up, but you realize that you are not in the position where you want to be," Vettel told Autosport.

"You fight with yourself first, because you are too far away to fight with others or fight with Mercedes, that race after race got stronger.
