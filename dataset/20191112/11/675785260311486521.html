Take Me Out fans can now book romantic holidays to the Isle of Fernando's

Take Me Out fans always see Paddy McGuinness sending hopeful romantics to the Isle of Fernando's and now you can book dedicated couple's holidays there too

12 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Julie Delahaye, https://www.facebook.com/juliedjourno

Take Me Out fans will know that there's one ultimate destination for romance; the Isle of Fernando's.

We can't help but be jealous when Paddy McGuinness sends two hopeful romantics off into the sunset for a date on the sun-soaked isle.

To celebrate the return of the hit ITV show to our screens, Travel Republic has launched a dedicated Isle of Fernando's holiday package for couples - and it's got everything you could want for a dreamy romantic break.

We're talking a 5* all-inclusive hotel, massages, sunset excursions and of course, heaps of sunshine on the Isle of Fernando's (which you may also know by the name of Tenerife).

In fact, the package includes seven nights at the 5* GF Gran Costa Adeje in a Superior room on an all-inclusive basis, your return flights, a pre-booked couple's massage and even a ride on the Mount Teide Cable Car at sunset.

Prices start from £1,030pp and you can already book the deal online with Travel Republic, although you'll need to do so by Monday 18th November when the offer expires.

There's no denying that the hotel makes for the ideal setting for a romantic getaway.

 

For a start it's right by one of those picture-perfect beaches which Tenerife is renowned for, El Duque Beach, not to mention it offers THREE swimming pools, a rooftop pool for adults, and a spa complete with sauna, hot tub and Turkish bath.

As for food? You'll be spoiled for choice thanks to the three restaurants and four bars to discover.
