Vivo S5 will officially arrive on November 14th
2 (40%) 1 vote

In a recent poster, Chinese singer, Cai Xukun, was spotted with the Vivo S5. The company also announced that Cai Xukun is the new spokesperson for the Vivo S5. The company has also sent out invites for the launch conference which will take place in Hangzhou on November 14th.

From the official posters, the Vivo S5 uses the gradient colour scheme and there will be a sky blue version. In addition, we may also have a light pink colour option. The colour options for this smartphone suggest that it targets young people and female folks.

The Vivo S5 comes with a 6.44-inch AMOLED punch-hole screen that has a resolution of 1080×2400 and a battery capacity of 4010mAh. It has a dimension of 157.9 × 73.92 × 8.64mm  and weighs 188g. According to the recent exposures, the Vivo S5 processor frequency clocks 2.3GHz. It will have up to 8GB of RAM and up to 256GB of internal storage. In the camera department, the quad rear camera and flash form a diamond shape. The sensors are 48MP + 8MP + 5MP + 2MP. For selfies, it uses a 32MP front camera. 

It is worth mentioning that the “5-fold super-quality and beauty” is a new beauty concept proposed by Vivo. This could be a major upgrade of this smartphone in the self-portrait beauty function. his phone also has features such as gravity sensor, distance sensor, light sensor, and on-screen fingerprint recognition.
