Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy: Deepak Chahar takes another hat-trick; well, almost

12 Nov 2019, 11:45 by Narayanan S

Highlights

  • Pacer Deepak Chahar almost did a repeat of his hat-trick feat against Bangladesh a couple of days ago
  • Playing for Rajasthan against Vidarbha, Chahar took three wickets off three consecutive legal deliveries but in between those wickets he bowled a wide
  • Despite Chahar's brilliance, Rajasthan went on to lose the game

Pacer Deepak Chahar almost did a repeat of his hat-trick feat against Bangladesh a couple of days ago. Playing for Rajasthan against Vidarbha in a Group B game of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy T20 tournament at Sports Hub in Thiruvananthapuram on Tuesday afternoon, Chahar took three wickets off three consecutive legal deliveries but in between those wickets he bowled a wide.

On Sunday in the third T20I between India and Bangladesh in Nagpur, Chahar had recorded the best bowling figures in T20I, taking 6/7, which included a hat-trick.
