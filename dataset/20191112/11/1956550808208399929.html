The Third Floor Sports Bar & Club opens in Dubai's DIFC

The new "luxury" sports bar will have weekly quiz nights, Friday brunches and live music

12 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Darragh Murphy

If you’re desperate to find a watering hole straight after work in DIFC to catch the biggest match, this new sports bar at the Capital Club Dubai may just be what you’re after.

The Third Floor Sports Bar & Club has now officially launched in Dubai, and it boasts plenty of screens, live music nights and other weekly events to get excited about.

Open daily from 11am to 3am, the luxury sports bar will feature major footy, rugby and cricket (you name it) matches on its array of HD TVs. The more TVs the better.

The bar isn’t just for chilled sporting action, as it will also host Friday brunches twice a month, along with weekly quiz nights and a line-up of live music acts every week.

As for the pub grub you can expect, The Third Floor is serving up pub-style gourmet burgers and sliders, along with classics dishes such as fish and chips, Angus rib-eye, butter chicken and nachos.

There will also be a special business lunch, with two courses costing Dhs90 and three courses for Dhs110. As for dinner from 8pm onwards, visitors can expect oysters and caviar, lobster rolls, foie gras hotdogs and wagyu beef burgers served until late.

Every hour is happy hour at the bar, too. Prices for short measures start at Dhs30, house bottles of grape are priced at Dhs180 and hops start at Dhs39.

Open daily 11am-3am. Capital Club Dubai, Building 3, Gate Village, DIFC, www.3rdfloordubai.com (04 364 0111).
