130 runners to take part in Oman Desert Marathon

12 Nov 2019, 11:55 by Times TV

Muscat: Around 130 runners from all over the world will take part in the seventh edition of Oman Desert Marathon that will begin on November 17.

According to Oman News Agency (ONA), the eight-day marathon will cover a distance of 165 kilometers in North Al Sharqiyah governorate.
