Dubai Design Week 2019: Deliveroo launches special interactive pop-up

Art installations, edible experiences and a walk-in oven at Design, Delivered

12 Nov 2019, 11:51 by Darragh Murphy

Ready for some artistic foodie snaps with larger-than-life food? This new, free pop-up at Dubai Design Week 2019 has the dishes you’re looking for.

Deliveroo is setting up the region’s first-ever interactive pop-up food experience named Design, Delivered, and it’s completely free to check out from today (Tuesday November 12) until Saturday November 16 from 10am onwards.

Inspired by the UAE’s creative art scene, Deliveroo’s interactive pop-up features a host of installations and edible experiences designed around food. Yes, one of them is a gigantic bowl of noodles.

There’s even a chance to experience how food is made thanks to a "walk-in oven", created by manipulating lighting and temperatures to give customers an insight into the cooking process.

“To celebrate our journey so far, we wanted to take our partners and consumers on a journey to their plate by Deliveroo,” said UAE Deliveroo GCC GM Anis Harb.

“Design, Delivered is a first of its kind experience that will not only engage visitors through engaging installations but will reiterate our belief that the entire Deliveroo ecosystem; our partners, suppliers, riders and customers, are part of a forever evolving journey to the ultimate food freedom.”
