Whenever they are not making money, these are what your black billionaires are probably doing

12 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Novieku-Babatunde Adeola

Although the world’s black billionaires are overly exposed to the media and public due to the nature of their jobs, their private lives still contain some tightly-held details.

In the midst of the life-changing decisions that they have to make, they still engage themselves in hobbies and passions, meant to ease the pressure of the job.

Whether it is fishing, boat riding, boxing or even watching cartoons, these habits just prove they are human after all. 

In this article, Face2Face Africa casts the spotlight on the hobbies of some of your favorite black billionaires.
