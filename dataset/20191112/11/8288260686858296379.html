Global phenomenon André Rieu returns to Birmingham with brand-new show

The 'King of the Waltz' will be bringing his Johann Strauss Orchestra to the Resorts World Arena on April 23, 2020

12 Nov 2019, 11:21 by Tom Bramwell

He's been credited with making classical music accessible to all and you can find out for yourself exactly why that is when André Rieu returns for a magical night in Birmingham on April 23, 2020.

Together with his 60-piece Johann Strauss Orchestra and many soloists, the man dubbed the 'King of the Waltz' will give his fans what they’ve come to expect from his performances - the maestro conducting his orchestra and playing his world-famous Stradivarius violin to create a colourful concert full of surprises.

The audience can look forward to fantastic melodies from film, musical, opera and operetta as well as traditional music, and of course they are invited to waltz the night away in an unforgettable evening of music and dance.

In 2018, Rieu performed for 100,000 fans in the UK and firmly believes that classical music is made for everybody to enjoy, not just for an elite. At his concerts you can see his devoted fans laughing, weeping, singing and dancing in the aisles.

Rieu has sold more than 40 million CDs and DVDs and his current albums are Happy Days (CD) and Love in Maastricht (DVD). He has reached 30 number one chart positions and has 500 platinum awards.

His YouTube videos have reached far over a billion views so far and five million subscribers follow the King of Waltz on Facebook.

Experience the magic of André Rieu at a festive and unforgettable evening at the Resorts World Arena in Birmingham on the 23 April 2020!

Tickets and Travel Packages are available at www.andrerieu.com .
