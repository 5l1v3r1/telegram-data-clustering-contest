Realme 2 Pro and Realme 5 updated with Dark Mode and more

12 Nov 2019, 13:09 by Marco Lancaster

Rate this post

Realme may have slowed its plans of developing custom software for its smartphones, but at least the company has been providing constant ColorOS updates for its smartphones. Just recently, the company has brought updates for the Realme X and Realme 3 Pro with October 2019 security patch and System-Wide Dark Mode. Now, the company is giving a similar treatment to the older Realme 2 Pro and the recently released Realme 5.

According to the company’s official software update portal, it has now rolled out ColorOS 6.0.1 (RMX1911EX_11_A.18) for the Realme 5 and ColorOS 6.0 (RMS1801EX_11_C.25) for the Realme 2 Pro. The ColorOS 6.0.1 release brings wide-angle lens video recording for the handset as well as the highly-anticipated Dark Mode. Moreover, it brings several improvements to the low-light captures. You can see the complete changelog below:

Realme 5 ColorOS 6.0.1 changelog:

  • Security

    • Android security patch: October 2019

  • Camera

    • Added wide-angle video shooting feature
    • Improved the brightness of the rear main camera
    • Optimized low light scene shooting of rear portrait mode
    • Improved preview clarity of rear camera
    • Optimized the issue of noise on the face with front and rear camera

  • Application

    • Updated font change supporting in Theme Store

  • Settings

    • Added Realme Laboratory
    • System-Wide Dark Mode
    • Added a switch of notification dot for updates

  • Notification Center and Status Bar

    • Removed the reminder after the hotspot enabled
    • Added fast switch toggles between SIM in the notification center
    • Swipe left or right to remove the message prompt while receiving
    • Added a switch to swipe down on the home screen for global search or notification center

  • Known issue fixed

    • Fixed low probability issue of the battery cannot be fully charged

Unfortunately, the update still packs October 2019 security patch. The Realme 2 Pro, on another hand, is receiving November’s 2019 security patch. In addition, the smartphone is getting the same System-Wide Dark Theme. Interestingly enough, ColorOS 6.0 update also introduces some new cool features for the 2 Pro. For instance, it adds App cloner support for more 3rd-party apps.

Realme 2 Pro ColorOS 6.0 changelog:

  • Security

    • Android security patch: November 2019

  • System

    • System-Wide Dark Mode
    • Added realme Laboratory
    • Added data switch in the notification panel
    • App cloner support with more third-party applications
    • Added a switch of notification dot for updates
    • Swipe left or right to remove the message prompt after receiving the message

  • User Interface

    • Redesigned the notification center style
