Galaxy launches a Christmas cracker full of CAKE as part of its festive range

There are also festive hot chocolate pods!

12 Nov 2019, 13:57 by Nancy Brown

Christmas is a time to pull a cracker – and they surely don't come much better than this launch from chocolatiers Galaxy.

Galaxy's limited-edition Cake Bar Cracker is on sale in Spar stores now and costs just £2.50.

It features a selection of some of the brand's festive favourites.

The cracker includes the Salted Caramel and Orange Festive Cake Bars, as well as the ever-popular Galaxy and Galaxy Caramel Cake Bars.

Elsewhere, fans can buy packets of the Festive Cake Bars – which feature five individually-wrapped light and spongey chocolate cakes topped with orange or salted caramel sauce.

Just tried a Galaxy hot chocolate pod. Most acceptable!

They're just £1 from Tesco, Asda, Co-Op, Morrisons and Sainsbury's.

And, if you've got a Nescafe Dolce Gusto machine then you're in for a real treat, as there's a new range of hot chocolate pods designed to fit the machines.

Chocoholics can enjoy their favourite chocolate bar in a warming drink this Christmas, with the new Twix, Milky Way, Galaxy and Maltesers hot chocolate pods.
