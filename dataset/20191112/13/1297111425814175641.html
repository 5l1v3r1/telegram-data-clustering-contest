Pizza, Fajardo and loud chants: Jay Onrait ready to Experience Regina

"You know, there's something unique about pizza on the Prairies .... The cheese looks like a 'before' picture of a Lactaid commercial."

12 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Rob Vanstone

Jay Onrait will be on-stage on Friday.

He will be in Regina later this week, alongside “SC at SportsCentre” cohort Dan O’Toole, for a recording of the popular “Jay and Dan Podcast” at the Conexus Arts Centre.

The TSN duo will also attend Saturday’s Regina Pats WHL home game against the Prince Albert Raiders for “Experience Regina Night.”

In advance of the visit, Onrait — a former Global Saskatoon sportscaster who has relatives in Balcarres — chatted with Regina Leader-Post sports editor Rob Vanstone.
