Match Of The Day X presenter Craig Mitch sparks outrage after branding Liverpool a 's***hole'

Craig Mitch slated the city and said people in Liverpool "still haven't got jobs" in a shocking video going viral on social media

12 Nov 2019, 13:45 by James Rodger, Max Clements, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

A host of BBC show Match of the Day X has issued a grovelling apology - after calling Liverpool a 's***hole'.

Craig Mitch slated the city and said people in Liverpool "still haven't got jobs" in a shocking video going viral on social media.

Mitch, who presents Match of the Day X, has sparked outrage with the comments.

The presenter has subsequently apologised, saying "sorry" after the YouTube video surfaced.

In the clip, the presenter made a series of derogatory slurs about the people who live in Merseyside.

Appearing on the YouTube show Filthy Fellas, Mitch said: "I went to Liverpool and it's still a s***hole.

"I went there, people still haven't got jobs."

 

He also said: "Things haven't changed. They (Liverpool FC) couldn't get the job done against Arsenal . Why? Because they don't know what a job is."

A second video has also surfaced in which again made derogatory marks about the city when discussing Steven Gerrard' s move from the Reds to LA Galaxy, reports Mirror Online.

"He's going to a developed area. That's all it is," says the presenter.

"He's leaving a deprived area and going to a very developed area where there's sun and low crime rates."

 

Mitch said: "There is a video of me making insensitive jokes about Liverpool from 5 years ago on Twitter.

"This was clipped from an old episode of a YouTube show. I apologise for the offence these comments could cause and just know, these are not my actual views about a place I have a lot of respect for.

"Sometimes as football fans, we take banter too far. Back then, I did and I apologise for that."
