Cape Coral ranks among worst cities for singles

12 Nov 2019, 13:55 by WFTX Digital Team

LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- If you're looking for love, you may want to think twice before searching for it in Cape Coral.

According to a new study by Wallet Hub, Cape Coral ranked 171st out of 182 cities on a list of the “Best Cities for Singles.”

Cape Coral was the only Southwest Florida city to be included on the list.

This ranking was determined using three categories: economics, fun and recreation, and dating opportunities.

The #1 city to find love in was Atlanta, and the top-ranked Florida city was Tampa at #19.

Other Florida cities on the list include:

#33 - Miami
#37 - Orlando
#44 - Tallahassee
#45 - Fort Lauderdale
#72 - Jacksonville
#101 - St. Petersburg
#162 - Port St. Lucie
#173 - Hialeah
#181 - Pembroke Pines
