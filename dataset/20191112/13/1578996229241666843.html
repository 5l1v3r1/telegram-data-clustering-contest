Kristen Stewart's Rainbow Sequined Cutout Dress Was the Glitziest Thing on the 'Charlie's Angels' Red Carpet

12 Nov 2019, 13:48 by Alyssa Bailey

Kudos to Kristen Stewart, who literally outshone everyone in the glitziest rainbow sequin cutout dress at the Charlie's Angels premiere in Los Angeles last night. Stewart wore the Germanier statement piece with navy Le Silla heels. Her co-stars Ella Balinska and Naomi Scott dressed elegantly, Balinska in a pretty nude Vera Wang gown and Scott in a sharp black Givenchy dress. Director Elizabeth Banks also incorporated some sparkle into her look with her two-toned dress. Really, everyone looked great:

Stewart's big premiere look comes days after she gave a very revealing interview to Howard Stern while promoting the film. Stewart talked to the host candidly about her romance with Robert Pattinson during her Twilight days.

Stern asked if she thought she'd marry Pattinson. "I don’t know, I wanted to…yeah, no, I think I’ve never been in—" she started. When Stern asked more plainly if she would've said yes if Pattinson proposed, Stewart eventually admitted that yes, she would've.

"I don’t know. I’m not a super duper traditionalist, but at the same time, like yeah," she said. "Every relationship I’ve ever been in, I thought that was it. I’ve never like casually—maybe one or two, okay, I’m not giving everyone that due. I’ve never really been the most casual person."

She confirmed that yes, they were a very real couple who were trying to balance privacy with fame during their heyday. "We were together for years, that was my first [love]," she said, adding that Pattinson is still "the best," even now.
