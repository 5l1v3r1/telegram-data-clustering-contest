Emirates launches live maps service in DXB and around the world

Breeze through Dubai International Airport with this new app update

12 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Darragh Murphy

Dubai International Airport is one of the most technologically advanced airports in the world – and it’s also absolutely gigantic, with a huge amount to do to help pass the time before or in between flights.

Emirates Airline has announced a new feature on its app to help users make the most of their time in DXB Terminal 3, as well as other major airports on its network – including London’s Gatwick and Heathrow Airports, as well as Hong Kong International airport.

The new feature will allow passengers to open up a real-time map providing location services and point-to-point navigation through the airport. So, whether you’re at check-in, or looking to find your way to a top restaurant in the terminal, the Airport Maps feature will help you find the right desk, lounge, shop, restaurant, bar (like, perhaps, the new Bottega) or ATM you’re after.

This is on top of Emirates also giving passengers on flights to ability to create a tailored playlist of movies, TV shows and music to listen to or watch in-flight. So, before you even get to the airport you can figure out how you’re going to pass the time by collating everything in the app.
