Elizabeth TAFE campus evacuated following series of arson attacks

19 Nov 2019, 04:32

A building at the Elizabeth TAFE campus has been evacuated following a series of “reckless” arson attacks this morning, concerning police ahead of catastrophic fire danger. Picture: 10 News

Emergency services arrived at the campus on Woodford Rd just after 10am, responding to a fire in an asbestos-laden toilet block.

MFS crews extinguished the fire, but the toilet block will need to be demolished.

Two other blazes were reported while emergency services were on the scene – one in a rubbish bin and another in a building.

No one was injured.

Police expressed their concern over the “reckless” attacks before a day of catastrophic fire danger.

Anyone who saw or heard any suspicious activity today, or in recent days, or who has any information that may assist the investigation is asked to contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000 or online.
