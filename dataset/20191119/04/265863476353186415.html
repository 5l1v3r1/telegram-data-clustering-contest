Tamar Braxton Apologizes A Second Time For Offensive LGBT Comments

19 Nov 2019, 04:30 by Ricki Mathers

Tamar Braxton speaks her mind at all times which has landed her in hot water again and again. The singer just apologized for the second time for some controversial comments that she made.

In an Instagram post that she made clear wasn’t referencing her current boyfriend David Adefeso, Tamar stated that if a man doesn’t want to sleep with his female significant other for multiple days it’s because he is gay.

‘The truth is ladies that these dudes out here really do be gay!! It ain’t enough money, beauty, hair, babies in the world to keep them!! They want D—!! Periodt!! It’s nothing wrong with you, but they will find EVERY reason in the world to make u not good enough!! If he lays with you for 3,4, 10 days and he don’t touch u, it’s NOT YOU!! HE WANT A MAN!! and that’s on my momma.’
