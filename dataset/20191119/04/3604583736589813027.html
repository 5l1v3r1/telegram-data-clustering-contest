Ballarat Summer Sundays 2020: Ali Barter, Meraki Minds to perform on opening weekend

19 Nov 2019, 04:00 by Ben Hopkins

A former Triple J unearthed winner is set to take to the stage in Ballarat for the opening weekend of Summer Sundays.

Melbourne's Ali Barter has been announced as the headline act for the first of the City of Ballarat's Summer Sundays events in the Ballarat Botanical Gardens on 5 January 2020.

Barter will be joined by Ballarat band Meraki Minds, who have also been announced for the opening weekend.
