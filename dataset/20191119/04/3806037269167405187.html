Google Stadia Increases Its Launch Titles to 22 Games (UPDATE)

The console releases November 19.

19 Nov 2019, 04:15 by Jeff Yeung

UPDATE (November 19, 2019): Just before Stadia‘s launch on November 19, Google has announced a whole list of new launch titles for the console, almost doubling the amount. Added to the launch lineup are the following games:

   •  Attack on Titan 2: Final Battle
   •  Farming Simulator 2019
   •  Final Fantasy XV
   •  Football Manager 2020
   •  Grid 2019
   •  Metro Exodus
   •  NBA 2K20
   •  Rage 2
   •  Trials Rising
   •  Wolfenstein: Youngblood

ORIGINAL STORY (November 12, 2019): With just one week to go before its launch, the long-awaited Google Stadia has finally announced its roster of launch titles, including games that will follow and release before the end of this year. Google reassures fans that the 12 launch titles have been “carefully chosen,” and that “there are more to come.” For a full list of the announced titles, read below:

Launch Titles

   •  Assassin’s Creed Odyssey
   •  Destiny 2
   •  Gylt
   •  Just Dance 2020

   •  Red Dead Redemption 2
   •  Thumper
   •  Tomb Raider
   •  Rise of the Tomb Raider
   •  Shadow of the Tomb Raider
   •  Samurai Shodown
2019 Titles

   •  Attack on Titan 2: Final Battle
   •  Borderlands 3

   •  Rage 2

Google Stadia will launch November 19 in North America and certain European regions, with the rest of the world to come in 2020.
