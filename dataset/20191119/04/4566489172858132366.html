Arianne Zucker talks 'A Mermaid for Christmas,' fans and success 

Emmy-nominated actress Arianne "Ari" Zucker ("Days of Our Lives") chatted with Digital Journal about the upcoming holiday film "A Mermaid for Christmas," which will be available on Amazon Prime Video on November 26.

19 Nov 2019, 04:47 by Markos Papadatos

Zucker plays the role of Tiffany Devereaux in A Mermaid for Christmas, which was written and directed by Michael Caruso. "It was a blast," she admitted. "What I love most about Tiffany is she is such an antagonist, and she is selfishly yummy. I do enjoy playing these kinds of roles. I had a lot of freedom with this character."

She praised Kathleen Gati, who plays the role of Connie Hunter in the film. "Kathleen is so wonderful. She is such a genuine woman. She has been doing acting for so long and she is just a fantastic person," she said.
