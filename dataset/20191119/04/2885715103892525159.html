RBI officer grade B prelims result declared: Steps to check merit list, Mains exam dates

RBI Officer Grade B Prelims 2019: The Phase-II online examination for grade-B DR (General) 2019 will be conducted on December 1 and for officers in grade-B DEPR and DSIM- 2019 will be conducted on December 2, 2019.

19 Nov 2019, 04:55 by Careers Desk

RBI officer grade B prelims result 2019: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) declared the result for the preliminary exam conducted for recruitment at the post of grade B level officer in the bank. Candidates who appeared for the test can check their result at the official website, rbi.org.in. The prelims was conducted on November 9.

The Phase-II online examination for grade-B DR (General) 2019 will be conducted on December 1 and for officers in grade-B DEPR and DSIM- 2019 will be conducted on December 2, 2019. The exams will be held in two shifts. Candidates will have to appear in both the shifts

RBI officer grade B Prelims 2019: How to check

Step 1: Visit the official website, rbi.org.in
Step 2: Move towards the end of the page and click at ‘opportunities@RBI’ under ‘more links’
Step 3: Click on ‘results’ under ‘current vacancies’
Step 4: Click on phase one result link
Step 5: Click on ‘roll number of candidates shortlisted for phase II’ link
Step 6: A PDF will open, check roll number

RBI Officer Grade B Prelims 2019: Salary

Selected candidates will draw a starting basic pay of Rs 35,150 per month, including the allowances the gross emolument will be approximately Rs 77,208.

A total of 199 vacancies are to be filled through this recruitment drive. Candidates will have to undergo three-phase online examinations to be eligible for the job. Those who clear phase I or prelims will have to appear for Mains in December.
