BART Shooting

19 Nov 2019, 23:19 by BRIEF

OAKLAND, Calif. – Excessive force claims against a Bay Area Rapid Transit police officer involved in a man’s shooting death may continue, a federal court in California ruled, because there are questions of material fact with respect to the man’s possession of a gun and his surrender.
