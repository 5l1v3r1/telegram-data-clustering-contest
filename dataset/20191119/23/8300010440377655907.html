Former Food Network star Debbie Lee offers Thanksgiving dinner delivery for those with dietary restrictions

19 Nov 2019, 23:26 by Lori Corbin

Chef Debbie Lee lost about 30 pounds while learning to cook food in a more healthy way. She learned the ins and outs of dietary restrictions such as low sugar, grain free, keto friendly, even vegan. Her Mind Body Fork company offers full Thanksgiving spread or a la carte sides.
