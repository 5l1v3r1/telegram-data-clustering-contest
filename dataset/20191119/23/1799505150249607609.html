Diabetic Supplements

19 Nov 2019, 23:33 by BRIEF

SAN FRANCISCO – A putative class action against GNC over labeling on diabetic supplements may continue, a federal court in California ruled. Consumers claim labels describing the product’s functions did not include a disclaimer required under the Federal Food, Drug and Cosmetics Act.
