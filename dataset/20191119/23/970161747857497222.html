Simon Thomas praises Mauricio Pochettino for writing to him when his wife died

19 Nov 2019, 23:04

TV presenter Simon Thomas confessed Mauricio Pochettino was the only manager to write personally write to him after his wife died.

His comments came after Tottenham released a statement announcing Pochettino had been ‘relieved of his duties’ after five years in the hot seat, along with his coaching staff Jesus Perez, Miguel D’Agostino and Antoni Jimenez.

The former Blue Peter host’s wife Gemma died of an aggressive form of blood cancer two years ago and the Argentine Football Manager reached out to him.

Taking to Twitter to share his thoughts, the 46-year-old tweeted: ‘When my wife died very suddenly two years ago, the one manager who took the time to write personally to me was Mauricio Pochettino and it was a heart felt letter. Class manager even better man.’

Gemma and Simon wed in 2005 and were married for 12 years before her tragic and sudden death.
