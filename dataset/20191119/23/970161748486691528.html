I'm A Celebrity's John Barrowman throws shade at Adele Roberts over 'Jane Gate'

19 Nov 2019, 23:09

John Barrowman has thrown shade at Adele Roberts on I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here as he claims we are starting to see her ‘true colours’.

The former campmate appeared on the Extra Camp sofa with Alison Hammond as they discussed ‘Jane Gate’.

Roman Kemp and Adele Roberts had the first clash of the series over the iconic Jane McDonald photo, which Adele brought in as her luxury item.

Alison started by telling Emily Atack and Joel Dommett that taking a photo of Jane into the jungle was ‘random’.

‘It’s a bit awks,’ Alison began.

‘It was random and he was just stating a fact maybe she is embarrassed or maybe she has been caught out a little bit.’

John Barrowman, who is not one to hold back accused Adele of bringing the photo in ‘to get a vote from a certain community’.

He continued: ‘She has been caught out a little bit because she used the word prejudice, and he was just a young man asking a question.

‘If you brought a picture of Jane McDonald on you should probably know the songs she sang.

‘She probably brought it on to get a vote from a certain community and it’s all backfired on her a little bit, I think you are seeing her true colours right now.’

Roman and Adele had a disagreement when Capital FM DJ made a joke about the Loose Women host, causing his radio DJ rival to get offended by his critique.

Talking together in the main camp, Kate Garraway and Roman are talking about the picture, which Adele brought in as her ‘luxury item’.

While Adele’s girlfriend Kate Holderness has since cleared up why she brought in the framed photo, the campmates where nonethewiser.

But Kate saw the plus side, as Kate asked: ‘Jane McDonald is looking over us, can you think of a song of hers?’

Admitting she didn’t know any, Adele said: ‘I know her off Loose Women, I don’t know her actual songs but I know her from the cruising show when she does covers.’

Adele then feels cornered by Myles and Roman who laugh at her, with Roman declared: ‘No one knows what she does.’

As Kate backed up Adele and Jane, Roman put her on the spot, and asked her: ‘She’s a legend is she? Name a song?’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! airs weekdays at 9pm on ITV.
