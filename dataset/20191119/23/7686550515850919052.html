Inside the Wales dressing room celebrations as players and Ryan Giggs enjoy Euro 2020 qualification

Absolute scenes

19 Nov 2019, 23:08 by Matthew Southcombe

The celebrations are likely to continue long into the night as Wales secured their place at Euro 2020.

Two goals from Aaron Ramsey fired Ryan Giggs' side to next summer's tournament and Cardiff went off.

There were wild scenes out on the pitch as players embraced each other and fans in the stands did the same.
