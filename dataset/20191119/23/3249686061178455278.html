8 Fashion Trends That Took K-Pop By Storm

19 Nov 2019, 23:53 by LorraineYe

Fashion trends are in one day and out the next, but these fashion trends managed to stick in popular culture thanks to fans from around the world and a few K-Pop idols. When you think of how K-Pop used to be a few years ago, you immediately are flooded with a ton of nostalgic memories of coordinated group outfits. Let's take a walk down memory lane with the 8 fashion trends that took K-Pop by storm:

8. Rose Quartz and Serenity Blue
