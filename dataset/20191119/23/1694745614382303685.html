Outdoor Voices makes some of the most flattering, functional leggings imaginable. Sometimes when we shop, though, it can be tough to pick the perfect pair; they're all so cute! If you're a Pilates pro, you're probably going to want something different than you would for a boxing class or a dance cardio party. So we've done the hard work for you and compared seven of Outdoor Voices' best leggings.

The brand's signature Textured Compression will keep you held in, in the best way possible. They're so flattering and perfect for low-impact activity. Or, if you want something sweat-wicking, the TechSweat will be your new best friend. It's buttery soft, and you'll never want to take them off. Now, all you have to do is click buy!

7/8 Springs Leggings

These Outdoor Voices 7/8 Springs Leggings ($85) are perhaps the most iconic of Outdoor Voices' leggings. They're made of OV's signature Textured Compression, meaning they're thick enough that you don't have to worry about them being see-through. Plus, they're super smoothing and hold you in, and come in the cutest colorways. If you've never tried Outdoor Voices before, these are a great place to start.

TechSweat 7/8 Zoom Leggings

These TechSweat 7/8 Zoom Leggings ($95) are buttery soft, and the colorblocking is insanely flattering. They're made for sweaty activities like running, dancing, and Spinning. Trust us when we say you won't want to take them off.

3/4 Warmup Leggings

If you're looking for a slightly cropped legging, reach for the 3/4 Warmup Leggings ($75). They're made of the same Textured Compression as the Springs Leggings, but they're all one color. We love them for things like Pilates, yoga, or hiking.

7/8 Flex Leggings

If you're doing something that really makes you sweat, you need these breathable 7/8 Flex Leggings ($75). They're beyond comfortable, hit just above the ankle, and were made for you to move.

FreeForm 7/8 Hi-Rise Leggings

If you want something comfortable and breathable, these FreeForm 7/8 Hi-Rise Leggings ($75) are the ones for you. They aren't as sweat-wicking as the TechSweat material, but they're probably the coziest and stretchiest pair of the bunch. We love these for dancing or yoga.

Hi-Rise 7/8 Warmup Leggings

Most of OV's leggings are mid-rise, but if you want something a little bit higher, reach for these Textured Compression Hi-Rise 7/8 Warmup Leggings ($75).

TechSweat Two-Tone Kneecap

Come summertime, we're into a cropped legging. The TechSweat Two-Tone Kneecap ($95) will make you feel comfortable, not constricted, and comes in the cutest colorways, like this minimal sandy pair. These were made for your next outdoor run.
