Teddy A reiterates his marriage vows as he shares wedding photo

19 Nov 2019, 17:27

BBNaija's Teddy A and Bam bam tied the knot in a glamorous ceremony in Dubai recently. Several spectacular moments were captured from the special moment and many fans had more than enough congratulatory messages for the two lovers.

While the couple are set to step into a new phase in their relationship, many social media users are convinced that the two are meant to be. Just recently, Teddy A affirmed that when he shared the first photo of himself and his bride, Bam bam from their glamorous Dubai wedding on his Instagram page.

In what appears to be Teddy A reiterating his marriage vows, he captioned the photo: "Riding till the wheels fall off' a phrase associated with promising to stick together to the very end, reflecting that the couple are more in love than ever.

Many people still find it hard to believe that what started as a simple strategic partnership in the BBNaija house has graduated to become a promised lifetime love affair. Many are excited for the couple who indeed found love and companionship in the rarest of places.
