Stormzy details second album, Heavy Is The Head

Featuring Headie One, Aitch and Burna Boy, the long-awaited LP lands December 13th.

19 Nov 2019, 17:38 by Carlos Hawthorn

Stormzy has confirmed details of his forthcoming full-length, Heavy Is The Head.

Out via Atlantic on December 13th, the 16-track LP is one of the most eagerly awaited albums of the year. Cameos come from Ed Sheeran, Burna Boy, Aitch, YEBBA, Tiana Major9 and UK drill star Headie One. Three singles from the LP have already been released: "Vossi Bop, "Crown" and "Wiley Flow."

Stormzy's debut album, Gang Signs & Prayer, was released in 2017, becoming the first grime album to top the UK charts.
