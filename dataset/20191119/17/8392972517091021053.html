EastEnders SPOILERS: Martin tries to kiss Ruby

Martin recently ended his relationship with wife Stacey

19 Nov 2019, 17:13 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Martin makes a move on Ruby in next week's EastEnders, but how will she react?

Ruby has a busy week when she helps Ian hold an event to plug his Walford Councillor campaign and he is desperate to impress. However his reputation is compromised when an unwelcome visitor turns up.

Ruby quickly becomes annoyed with Ian and takes matters into her own hands.

Meanwhile, Max is worried Ruby is avoiding him.

Later Ruby moves into The Vic with Mick and Ollie as part of the wife swap competition.

As she's in the pub, she's shocked when she overhears that Martin and Stacey's relationship is over.

As Ruby unwillingly continues to help Ian with his campaign, Ian coerces Martin into getting involved.

But it's not long before things get awkward as Ian winds up Martin by mentioning Stacey.
