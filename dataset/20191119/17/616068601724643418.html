Hot Chocolate Challenge

19 Nov 2019, 17:21 by John Lawless

If you have a nagging sweet tooth and want to fulfill those chocolate cravings, then you better get a move on.

The Hot Chocolate Challenge is coming to Vernon on Dec 7, and you get to be a taste tester. Tour over five downtown cafes and bistros competing to be crowned the best hot chocolate in Vernon.

You can join in the fun for free, just pick up your chocolate passport at the #DowntownVernon tent outside 3334 30th Avenue while tickets last.

The Hot Chocolate Challenge is part of Free Parking Day, which means free parking downtown to take in street performers and one-day shopping deals.
