Whataburger Unveils Christmas Inflatable for your Festive Yard

19 Nov 2019, 17:04 by Stryker

Whataburger will never stop making stuff with their logo on it.

Whataburger is the only fast food brand I know that puts their logo on stuff like this. I think it's because Texans have a lot of pride in Whataburger and love to show off that pride. Some of the more recent items are boots, jewelry and ugly Christmas sweaters. To go along with that sweater, you can now have a Christmas inflatable in your yard.
