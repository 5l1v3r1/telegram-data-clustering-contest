Listen: Latest Sporting Life Snooker Podcast with Ben Coley and Richard Mann

19 Nov 2019, 17:24 by Sporting Life

Host Ben Coley and snooker expert Richard Mann cover the latest snooker talking points following Judd Trump’s defeat of Ronnie O’Sullivan in last week’s Northern Ireland Open final.

Judd’s remarkable run of form comes under the microscope as the team wonder whether he is now the dominant force in snooker while Ronnie’s own return to form catches the eye ahead of the defence of his UK Championship crown in York.

Jimmy White and Reanne Evans earn special mention following their efforts at the Champion of Champions while Richard picks out three names to watch in the coming weeks and months.
