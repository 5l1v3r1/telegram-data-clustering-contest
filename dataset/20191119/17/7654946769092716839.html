Teacher busted in revenge porn case involving ex’s relatives

19 Nov 2019, 17:45 by Joshua Rhett Miller

An Indiana high school teacher was busted for sending nude snaps of his ex to the woman’s family, authorities said.

Jason C. Hohlt, who also coaches the boys’ and girls’ golf teams at Pike High School in Indianapolis, was busted Monday in a revenge porn probe that began when his ex told police he continued to harass her after she blocked his number last summer, according to a probable cause affidavit obtained by the Indianapolis Star.

Hohlt — who allegedly called the woman from other numbers and reached out to her relatives on social media — then took his ongoing torment to another level in July by sending nude images of his onetime companion to her brother via Facebook, the affidavit states.

Hohlt’s ex-girlfriend gave screenshots of the messages and images to investigators, authorities said.

Prosecutors claim they can prove with a warrant issued by a judge to Facebook that the messages were sent from an account linked to Hohlt’s phone number, the affidavit states.

An attorney for Hohlt, Chris Eskew, declined to comment when reached Tuesday by The Post.

The 42-year-old social studies teacher was placed on administrative leave following his arrest on a misdemeanor charge of distribution of an intimate image, WISH reports.

There’s “no reason to believe” that any students were impacted by Hohlt’s alleged behavior, Metropolitan School District of Pike Township officials said in a statement to the station.
