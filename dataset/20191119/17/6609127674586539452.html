BUS DRAMA

Man rushed to Glasgow Royal Infirmary after being hit by bus at Buchanan Bus Station

19 Nov 2019, 17:41 by Aidan Smith

A MAN has been rushed to hospital after being hit by a bus near Buchanan Station in Glasgow.

Emergency services were called to the scene after the collision around 4.40pm today on Killermont Street.

Police confirmed the man was taken by ambulance to Glasgow Royal Infirmary for treatment.

The man's age is currently unknown.

Killermont Street is currently closed in both directions.

Traffic leaving the bus station and the shopping centre are being sent towards the North Hanover Street junction.

The Scottish Ambulance Service has been contacted for comment.
