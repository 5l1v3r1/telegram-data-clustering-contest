Andy Ruiz Jr taunts Anthony Joshua over his 'lucky shot' knockdown

19 Nov 2019, 17:41

Andy Ruiz Jr claims Anthony Joshua didn’t hurt him in their first fight and believes the defeated champion caught him with a ‘lucky shot’ in the third round.

The Mexican hit the canvas in the third round after Joshua connected with an uppercut and hook, but rallied to drop his opponent and eventually win the fight in the seventh.

Joshua similarly claimed he was beaten by a ‘lucky punch sent by the gods’ and that verbal jab appears to have left its mark on Ruiz Jr.

‘I got lazy. I think I was feeling good the first three rounds and I got a little lazy,’ Ruiz Jr told Sky Sports about the third round.
