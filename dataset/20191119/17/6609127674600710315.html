CRASH DRAMA

Emergency services rush to Greenock street after police vehicle smashes into car

19 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Aidan Smith

EMERGENCY services raced to a Greenock street after a police vehicle smashed into a car.

The two vehicle crash occurred on Patrick Street this afternoon.

The incident took place around 2:15pm.

Cops and ambulance units were called in and pictures show the extent of the damage.

The police car can be seen with its front grill smashed in.

The other vehicle's bumper has been forced up due to the impact.

It is believed an ambulance was only drafted in as precaution and nobody was taken to hospital.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "We were called at 2.15pm to a crash on Patrick Street involving two vehicles.

"They are waiting to be uplifted."
