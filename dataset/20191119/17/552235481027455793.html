Rush-hour delays for A77 commuters in Kilmarnock

Motorists face being held up following a road traffic collision on the southbound carriageway.

19 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Colin Paterson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/colin-paterson/

A road traffic collision has led to rush-hour delays for commuters on the A77 in Ayrshire tonight.

Motorists heading southbound face being held up on the approach to the Bellfield Interchange at Kilmarnock.

Traffic Scotland reports that one of the lanes is blocked with traffic "very slow" on the approach to the busy interchange.

A tweet issued by the organisation a few minutes later confirmed that all lanes were restricted for up to 30 minutes.

The Kilmarnock Standard will update you on its Facebook page as more is learned about the incident.
