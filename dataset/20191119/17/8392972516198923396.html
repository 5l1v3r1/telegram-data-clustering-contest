B&M is selling a light-up glitter snow globe for just £10 this Christmas

The bargain store strikes again!

19 Nov 2019, 17:14 by Nancy Brown

The time to deck the halls with all things Christmassy is surely upon us and shoppers have gone crazy for the latest steal from bargain store B&M.

Now you can add some festive cheer to your home with this gorgeous light-up snow globe.

Oh, and did we say that it's also glittery?

Permanently knocked down in price from £14.99 to just £10, the Magical Light Up Glitter Globe is battery operated and available in two colours.

Pick from warm white or cool white, depending on the look you're going for.

We spent over £300 on Christmas decorations at B&M today and I don’t even feel guilty. It made me happy.

"Add shimmering colour to your home over the festive period with this Magical Light Up Glitter Globe. Ideal for bringing impactful light to your home at Christmas," said the blurb.

The globes were going down a storm with shoppers, according to The Sun, so it appears you'll have to move fast if you want to get one.

You'll have to brave the cold and head to your nearest store too, as sadly B&M doesn't offer online shopping.
