Family pay tribute to Preston mum, 32, who died after being hit by a bus in Fishergate Hill

19 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Matthew Calderbank

A mum-of-one, 32, has died after being hit by a bus in Preston.

Christina Watkins was struck by a Scania double-decker bus in Fishergate Hill at around 6.55pm on Friday, November 15.
