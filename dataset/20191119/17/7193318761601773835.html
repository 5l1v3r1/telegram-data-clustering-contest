Video: Critically acclaimed documentary 'For Sama' debuts tonight on OETA

19 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Brandy McDonnell

The Oklahoma Educational Television Authority (OETA) will present at 9 tonight the U.S. broadcast premiere of one of the year’s most critically acclaimed documentaries, "For Sama."

The documentary will air as part of the PBS series "Frontline."

"For Sama" tells the true story of filmmaker Waad al-Kateab’s life through five years of uprising in rebel-held Aleppo, Syria. During that time, she falls in love, gets married and gives birth to her daughter, Sama, all the while filming the conflict raging around her.
