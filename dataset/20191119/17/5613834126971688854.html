Thanks to Funko, ‘Gremlins’ PEZ Dispensers Now Exist for the First Time Ever

19 Nov 2019, 17:38 by John Squires

Countless Gremlins toys and collectibles have been released over the years, but one thing Joe Dante’s classic film has never been honored with is a set of PEZ dispensers.

Thanks to Funko, they’re finally on the way as part of the “POP! PEZ” line!

Three different dispensers will be included in the wave:

  • Gizmo
  • Gremlin
  • Gremlin Chase Variant

These are listed as “Coming Soon” on Funko’s website, so keep your eyes peeled!
