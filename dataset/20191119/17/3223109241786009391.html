‘Cats’ Movie Trailer Teases a Bizarre Feline Show

19 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Genevieve Scarano

A new trailer just dropped for the upcoming Cats film and it teases a strange, yet alluring storyline about a group of passionate felines.

The movie’s star-studded cast, including Idris Elba, Jennifer Hudson, and Taylor Swift, has received a “digital fur” makeover, thanks to some CGI tech. Even though the animation is a tad creepy, the movie looks like a lot of fun with dancing, singing, and glittery catnip.
