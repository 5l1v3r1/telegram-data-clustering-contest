Boy falls into sambar vessel, dies

19 Nov 2019, 17:39 by Staff Reporter

A family function turned fatal for a three-and-half-year-old boy who slipped into a vessel of piping hot Sambar in Sardar Nagar of Shabad mandal on Monday afternoon. P Arush succumbed to his burns in the wee hours of Tuesday at Osmania General Hospital here.

The boy’s father P Suresh told police that the tragedy took place around noon when the boy was playing near the cooking area and accidentally fell into the vessel. The victim hails from Sheripally in Sangareddy district and came to Sardar Nagar with his parents to attend a function at his aunt’s house.

Ayush was first taken to Shadnagar Government Hospital and later was shifted to OGH, where he died around 4 a.m., Shabad police said and added that the child suffered 45 % burns.
