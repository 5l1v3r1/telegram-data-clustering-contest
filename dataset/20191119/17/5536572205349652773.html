RB Mark Walton Reportedly Beat Pregnant GF After Finding Out He Was Going To Be A Father On Sunday

19 Nov 2019, 17:53 by Darrelle Lincoln

The Dolphins cut running back Mark Walton on Tuesday morning upon finding out he was accused of attacking a woman who was five weeks pregnant.

To make the matter even worse, the pregnant woman was his girlfriend and he had just found out that he was to become a father this past Sunday.
