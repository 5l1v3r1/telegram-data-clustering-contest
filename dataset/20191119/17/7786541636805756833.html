Woman paralyzed in Las Vegas mass shooting 2 years ago dies in California

19 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

A Southern California woman who was paralyzed two years ago during the mass shooting on the Las Vegas strip died Friday, according to a news release from the coroner's division of the San Berardino Sheriff's Department.

Kimberly Gervais, 57, of Mira Loma, California, died at a Redlands Community Hospital in Redlands, California, according to the news release.
