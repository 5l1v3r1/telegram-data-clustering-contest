What time is kick-off?

Birmingham will take on Huddersfield Town at the John Smith Stadium on 3pm on Saturday, October 23.

 

Is Huddersfield Town v Birmingham City on TV?

Saturday's game will not be available on TV in the UK.

This weekend Sky Sports have selected three LIVE television games, both shown on Sky Sports Football.

They have opted to show QPR's trip to face Fulham, Middlesbrough v Hull City and Charlton's clash with Cardiff City at The Valley.

But, those looking to watch the game outside the UK can watch LIVE on the club's website with an iFollow subscription.

Will the game be on Quest's EFL highlights show?

Besides Hull's trip to face Middlesbrough on Sunday, all the rest of the Championship action will be available on Quest on Saturday night.

It is part of a four year deal which will bring 90 minutes of match highlights, hosted by Colin Murray, at 9pm.

Quest are also broadcasting 10 hour-long highlights shows capturing the action from every round of midweek fixtures. 

In addition, they have been presenting eight hour-long highlights shows from the Carabao Cup and Checkatrade Trophy as part of the arrangement.

Quest is available in 27 million homes across Freeview, Freesat, Sky, Virgin Media and BT TV. With a monthly reach of 12.4 million, Quest is Discovery’s largest channel in the UK.

The EFL highlights will headline Quest’s line-up of sports events throughout the year which are delivered in conjunction with Eurosport. 

Each show will feature every goal from every Saturday and Friday night EFL fixture plus analysis from a selection of pundits.
