Lil Reese Updates Fans After Being Shot, Shares New Song: Listen

“I appreciate everybody keeping me in they prayers”

19 Nov 2019, 01:56 by Evan Minsker

On Tuesday, Chicago drill rapper Lil Reese was shot in the neck in an incident that took place in the Chicago suburb Country Club Hills. Hospital officials said at the time that Reese was in critical condition. Reese is now out of the hospital and has shared some updates with fans, as well as a brand new song called “Come Outside,” which you can hear below.
