Detroit: Become Human lands on PC next month

Quantic Dream's former PS4 exclusive hits the Epic Games Store in December.

19 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Jennifer Locke

Source: Quantic Dream

What you need to know

  • Detroit: Become Human originally released in 2018 as a PS4 exclusive.
  • It follows the story of androids living in the near-future, and features choices that will change the course of the game.
  • It will release for PC on December 12 through the Epic Games Store for $40.

We knew Quantic Dream's Detroit: Become Human was coming to PC sometime this year, we just didn't know when. It looks like the developer is ready to reveal when players can get their hands on a PC a copy, and it will indeed be squeaking in by the end of 2019.
