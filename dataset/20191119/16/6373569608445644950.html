Man arrested after lorry overturns in crash with car

The driver of the lorry was taken to hospital

19 Nov 2019, 16:29 by Rom Preston-Ellis

A man has been arrested after a crash led to a lorry overturning in a Plymouth street earlier today.

The lorry crashed with a Fiat Punto on Bartholomew Road at 11.15am today.

The road was closed for hours while a recovery operation took place.

The driver of the lorry has been taken to Derriford Hospital with a chest injury while the Fiat Punto's occupants were described as 'walking wounded'.

Police have confirmed that the driver of the Fiat has now been arrested on suspicion of driving while unfit through drink and drugs.

A spokesperson for Devon and Cornwall Police said: "Police and ambulance were called at 11.15am today [19 November] following a collision involving a Ford Transit van and a Fiat Punto on Bartholomew Road, Plymouth. The van ended up on its side.
