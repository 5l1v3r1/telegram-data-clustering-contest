Gardai appeal for witnesses after serious traffic accident on Cork Road in Waterford

A motorcyclist is in a serious condition following the accident

19 Nov 2019, 16:19 by Edel Hughes

Gardai are appealing for witnesses after a serious traffic accident that left a motorcyclist in hospital in Waterford.

The collision, which involved a car and a motorcyclist,  took place on the R680, Cork Road, at approximately 12.45pm on Monday afternoon, November 18.

The injured motorcylist, a man in his 30s, was taken from the scene to University Hospital Waterford and is in a critical condition.
