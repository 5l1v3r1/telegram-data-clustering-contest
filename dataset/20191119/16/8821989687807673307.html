King Diamond and Uncle Acid Haunt Cleveland: Photos, Set Lists

19 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Matthew Wilkening

King Diamond teamed up with Uncle Acid and the Deadbeats to extend Cleveland's Halloween celebration this past weekend with their wonderful spooky music at the Agora.

You can see exclusive photos and the set lists from the artists' performances below.

Performing on a new three-level stage set, Diamond and his sharp backing band delivered a strong 13-song set mostly focused on '80s albums like Abigail and "Them."

Bathed in red lights below a video screen that displayed psychedelic graphics and silhouettes of the band, Uncle Acid and the Deadbeats entranced the packed audience with an eight-song set centered on their 2011 album Blood Lust.

King Diamond, Uncle Acid and the Deadbeats and Idle Hands' 2019 North American tour is scheduled to conclude on Dec. 4 in Phoenix. The Institute, King Diamond's long-awaited follow up to 2007's Give Me Your Soul ... Please, arrives in 2020.
