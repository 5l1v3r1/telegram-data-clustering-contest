Darksiders Genesis trailer shows new gameplay and upgrade system

19 Nov 2019, 16:20 by Aran Suddi

A brand new trailer for Darksiders Genesis has been released by Airship Syndicate and THQ Nordic which shows the abilities and a system called creature cores. Players can play either as War or Strife through the campaign in the game and each has different abilities. As enemies are killed creature cores are dropped which allows for both characters to unlock new abilities including spawning hellhounds, increasing attack power, and adding lava trails to weapons.
