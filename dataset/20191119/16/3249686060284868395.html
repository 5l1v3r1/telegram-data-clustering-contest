Jin Hua gets into an accident in preview for 'Flavor of Wife'

19 Nov 2019, 16:35 by sl278

More trouble is brewing for the Jin Hua and Ham So Won couple. 

In the preview for next week's episode of 'Flavor of Wife', Jin Hua was reported to be involved in a car accident. 

The scene was shown where Ham So Won seemed distressed as the family tried to handle the crisis. Ham So Won appeared panicked while telling the staff members that her husband got into an accident.
