Salman Khan shares a post to wish everyone a Happy International Men's Day in 'Dabangg 3' style

19 Nov 2019, 16:59 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan's 'Dabangg 3' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. The trailer of the film received a lot of love and appreciation from Bhai fans. The 'Hud Hud' song also garnered a positive response from the audience.

And today on International Men's Day, Salman took to his Instagram story to wish all the men out there! Sharing a picture of his Chulbul Pandey avatar, the superstar captioned the post, "Celebrate International Men's Day with Chulbul Pandey." "#Dabangg3BadgeOfHonour."
