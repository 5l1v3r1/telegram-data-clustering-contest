Crypto Whales Emerge As Traders Track 28,521,383 XRP and 5,376 Bitcoin (BTC) on the Move

19 Nov 2019, 16:05 by Daily Hodl Staff

Crypto whale watchers are tracking a large XRP transaction.

On Monday, 28,521,383 XRP worth about $7.4 million moved from an unknown wallet to the South Korean crypto exchange Bithumb.

The move indicates the holder may be looking to sell some of the asset.
