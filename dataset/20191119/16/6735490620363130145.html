Taylor Swift Releases Holiday Capsule Clothing Collection

19 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Shakiel Mahjouri

This Christmas is all about Taylor Swift.

The “You Need to Calm Down” singer is getting everyone hyped for her new Holiday Collection. Swift, 29, announced the capsule clothing line’s release on Monday.

“It’s nice to have new merch,” the official Taylor Nation Twitter account wrote. “The Holiday Collection is available now!”
