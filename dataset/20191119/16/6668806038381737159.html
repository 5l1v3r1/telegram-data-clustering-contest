Tanhaji trailer: Fans react positively to Ajay Devgn’s 100th film

19 Nov 2019, 16:49 by Filmfare

Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior marks Ajay Devgn’s 100th film in Bollywood. He plays the great Maratha general Tahnaji Malusare while Saif Ali Khan portrays Uday Bhan, a Rajput working for Mughal emperor Aurangzeb.

Ajay Devgn is himself co-producing the movie along with Bhushan Kumar while Om Raut is directing it. Set in the 17th the period actioner also stars Kajol in a pivotal role as Savitribai Malusare, Tahnaji’s wife.
