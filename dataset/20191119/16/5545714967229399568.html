Project Sakura Wars video looks at Tite Kubo's character concepts

From Sketch to Screen

19 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Chris Moyse

Sega has taken a break from pumping out the music videos for its upcoming tactical RPG Project Sakura Wars, with a new video focusing on the sequel's eclectic cast and the artistic concepts that originally brought them to life for your gaming pleasure.

The two-minute video showcases some rough sketches of each of The Floral Division's heroes, masterfully conceived by Bleach creator Tite Kubo. Each character then segues off of the page and into their in-game counterpart - conversing with friends, strolling 'round town, or preparing for mech-assisted mayhem.
