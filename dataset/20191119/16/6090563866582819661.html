Lightning kills 3 ECD pupils in Goromonzi

19 Nov 2019, 16:41

Three Early Childhood Development (ECD) pupils at Rusike Primary School in Goromonzi died after they were struck by lightning on their way home from school on Tuesday afternoon.

Goromonzi District Civil Protection Department Chairperson, Prisca Dube confirmed the incident and said the fourth child who was injured was taken to Ruwa Rehabilitation Hospital for treatment.
