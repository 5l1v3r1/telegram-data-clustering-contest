Castlebar mourns passing of much-loved personality

19 Nov 2019, 16:28

THE death has taken place of Margaret Moran (née Roache), Westport Road, Castlebar, and late of Fisherhill, Parke, Castlebar.

A member of a popular and respected family, news of her passing is widely mourned in the community.

Wife of the late Pete, she is deeply regretted by her sons Peter, Noel and Paraic, daughters Ann Marie and Patricia, sister Annie (Reilly), daughter-in-law Brenda, grandchildren Sean, Kevin, Brianna and Eddie, sister-in-law Mai (Connor, Belcarra), nephews, nieces, relatives, neighbours and friends, to whom sympathy is tendered.

Her remains will be reposing at the Coady Funeral Home, Linenhall Street, Castlebar, this evening (Tuesday) from 5 p.m. with removal at 7 p.m. to the Church of the Holy Rosary, Castlebar.

Funeral Mass takes place on Wednesday at 11 a.m. with burial afterwards in the Old Cemetery, Castlebar.

May her gentle soul rest in peace.
