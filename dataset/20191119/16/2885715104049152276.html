Fan captured Joker’s iconic stair dance scene and netizens can’t keep calm

Viewed over two million times, the clip has triggered many reactions on Twitter, with many calling the person who recorded the video "lucky". "A lucky fan saw the ‘Joker’ stair scene happen in realtime," wrote a user while sharing the viral clip.

19 Nov 2019, 16:38 by Trends Desk

Can you imagine looking out your window one fine day to see actor Joaquin Phoenix performing the iconic stair dance scene at the Bronx from the movie ‘Joker’? That is exactly what happened with a fan, who was prompt enough to record the epic moment.

The video, which was shared on Reddit by a user azar0v, features Phoenix preparing for the scene before getting into his character and performing one of the defining moments of the film. “Filming of the most iconic scene ever,” read the post, which is being widely shared on several social media platforms.
