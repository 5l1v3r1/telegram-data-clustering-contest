Defamation

19 Nov 2019, 16:45 by BRIEF

MANHATTAN — Right-wing political commentator Gina Loudon, a member of the Trump campaign’s media advisory board, claims Mediaite LLC defamed her by accusing her of “pretending to be a psychology expert” and “pretending to be a lawyer,” in New York County Supreme Court.
