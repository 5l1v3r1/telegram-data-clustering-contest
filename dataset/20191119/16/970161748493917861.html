Girl, 13, jumped to her death 'after being gang raped by six boys'

19 Nov 2019, 16:39

A teenager allegedly who was allegedly held captive and repeatedly raped has killed herself.

The girl, identified only as Pinkie, 13, allegedly jumped from the top of a block of flats to her death in Bangkok, Thailand, on Friday night.

Moments before her death she sent a message that said ‘I’m about to go, I love you’, showing her legs dangling over the roof from where she apparently took her life.

Her mother, 45, reported six men for gang rape in a flat in the city less than a week before her death.

She said that her daughter had spiralled into a deep depression as a result of the sex attacks she suffered.

It has also been reported locally that she had argued with her mother who chased after her to try and stop her.

A Royal Thai Police spokesman said: ‘Suspects face charges for collaborating to sexually abuse the girl.

‘There were two girls, two boys on the night of the incident. One of the girls, Pinkie, is dead. Warrants have been issued for both boys.’

The girl’s friend was said to have been found at the scene by police.

The spokesman said that other suspects had been taken into custody after being arrested on a weapons charge.

They said: ‘When we raided the first suspect’s home, we found weapons in his house, so we detained him and charged him with illegally possessing firearms.

‘He is currently locked up. Meanwhile, the court just approved the warrant for the second suspect this morning.’
