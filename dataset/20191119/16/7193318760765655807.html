What to do in Oklahoma on Nov. 19, 2019: Hear Kinky Friedman at the Blue Door

19 Nov 2019, 16:18 by Brandy McDonnell

Today's featured event:

Texas music icon Kinky Friedman is bringing his "Merry Kinkster Tour" to Oklahoma City for a show at 8 tonight at the Blue Door, 2805 N McKinley.

The tour is supporting his new album, "Resurrection," which was released Oct. 25 on Echo Hill Records.

Friedman worked with superstar producer, multi-instrumentalist, and three-time Grammy Award winner Larry Campbell, finding a complement to his jagged-edged Texas Hill Country persona. Together with three-time Grammy-winning engineer Justin Guip, they have produced what's billed as one of the crowning achievements of the Kinkster’s career.
