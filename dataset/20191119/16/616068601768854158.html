Rocky Mountain High

19 Nov 2019, 16:51 by Jon Manchester

Castanet and SOFT 103.9 present A Rocky Mountain High Christmas Nov. 28-30 at Kelowna Community Theatre.

The concert features the music of the late John Denver, with Lee Holdridge, Denver’s award-winning original arranger-conductor leading the Okanagan Symphony Orchestra, an all-star band, and guests Melina Schein and Justin Moore.

?Sing along to Denver's hits Annie’s Song, Country Roads as well as some of his best known Christmas songs from Rocky Mountain Christmas and The Muppet Christmas special.  

Showtimes are 7:30 p.m. Nov. 28 and 29, with a matinee at 2 p.m. Nov. 30.

Partial proceeds support the Boys and Girls Club of the Okanagan and Central Okanagan Food Bank.

Tickets available at www.kelownatickets.com or by calling 250-862-2867.
