Liam Hemsworth in high spirits as he returns to LA amid Maddison Brown romance

19 Nov 2019, 16:53

Liam Hemsworth has been spotted arriving at LAX following his split from Miley Cyrus.

Bit rude of him to look that good getting off a flight, but okay.

The actor, who’s currently dating fellow Aussie actress Maddison Brown, looked in high spirits as he arrived at the LA airport, giving fans a thumbs up.

Rocking a casual look, the Hunger Games star looked chill as anything as he returned to his American home after his time in Australia.

Liam and Miley shocked fans earlier this year, when they confirmed their split after a decade together.

With the Wrecking Ball packing on some PDA with Cody Simpson, Liam has been getting cosy with his new beau Maddison.

Following his split from Miley, the Aussie actor was said to be hiding out in Byron Bay in his Aussie homeland, with his brother Chris providing him a shoulder to lean on.
