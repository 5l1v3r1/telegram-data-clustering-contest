Rubbish tip and recycling opening times in Newcastle & the North East

Up to your eyes in garden and household rubbish? Find out when recycling centres open in and around Newcastle

19 Nov 2019, 16:26 by Lesley Oldfield

It's that time of the year when we're about to create a lot of rubbish to clear.

So you might want to make the most of a trip to the local recycling centres. You will want to know the opening hours before making a trip so, to save you a job, we’ve done some of the checking for you.

And there are also ways to check on the day, as once a site is full, it may close early. Here is a round-up of opening hours at the main waste and recycling centres around Tyneside and Wearside. You’ll find you can find one (or several) to visit any day of the week - and that includes Bank Holidays.

Newcastle

Brunswick Industrial Estate, Sandy Lane, NE13 7BA

Byker, Glasshouse Street off Walker Road, NE6 1AH

Walbottle, Walbottle Road, NE15 8HY

Opening times: Brunswick and Byker are open daily 8am-6pm or 8am-8pm April 1 to September 30.

Walbottle is open from 10am-6pm from October 1 to March 31 or 10am-8pm from April 1 to September 30. You can also check for updates on Twitter .

All sites close Christmas Day, Boxing Day and New Year's Day and close at 4pm on Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve.

The rules: Newcastle residents only. There are restrictions on very large vehicles and a free permit scheme for residents who drive a van or a car towing a trailer, a vehicle with commercial branding or sometimes use a hire vehicle.

The vehicle restrictions include any vehicle over 2m high (including roof rack), over 6m long, exceeding 3.5 tonne weight and trailers exceeding 3m.

Gateshead

Campground waste, Springwell Road, Wrekenton, NE9 7XW

Cowen Road, Blaydon, NE21 5TW

Opening times: All the above are open daily 9am-6pm. From October 1 to March 31 hours are: Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm, Saturday and Sunday, 9am to 6pm. Closed Christmas Day.

North Tyneside

Household waste recycling centre, Wallsend Road, North Shields, NE29 7SH

Opening times: Daily between 8am and 8pm. Closed Christmas Day.

South Tyneside

Recycling Village, Middlefields Industrial Estate, Recycling Village, Throckley Way, NE34 0NU

Opening times: Daily from Monday to Sunday, 8am-6pm. Closed Christmas Day.
