MP HOPEFUL AXED

General Election 2019 – Scots Tory candidate Ryan Houghton axed over historic ‘anti-Semitic forum posts’

19 Nov 2019, 08:59 by Jonathan Whitelaw

A SCOTS Tory candidate has been axed by the party over alleged anti-Semitic comments made on a blog post.

Ryan Houghton had been running for the MP seat in Aberdeen North in next month's General Election.

But the party has now suspended him and withdrawn its support after alleged comments made online seven years ago.

Mr Houghton issued a statement and an apology on social media last night.

He said he had discussed freedom of speech and comments made by notorious Holocaust denier David Irving in one of the forums.

The ex-Tory candidate also apologised to the Jewish community of Aberdeen.
