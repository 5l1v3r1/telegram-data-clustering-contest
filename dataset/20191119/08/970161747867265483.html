Orange Is The New Black's Danielle Brooks welcomes 'perfect' baby girl

19 Nov 2019, 08:25

Orange Is The New Black’s Danielle Brooks has welcomed her first child, a baby girl, with her partner, whose identity is only known as ‘D’.

The star, who played Tasha ‘Taystee’ Jefferson on the show, shared an adorable snap of the little one on Instagram to announce the happy news.

‘11.16.19 [heart emojis],’ she wrote. ‘She’s perfect.’

Fans and friends have been quick to congratulate the star, with Amy Schumer telling her: ‘Yes she is,’ while Glee’s Amber Riley added: ‘Yay friend!!’

The 30-year-old revealed she was pregnant back in July, telling fans she was ‘elated’ with the news.

Along with her message, she shared a picture of herself beaming while holding up a positive pregnancy test. She also revealed on her Instagram story that she was already five months in to her pregnancy.

‘When one chapter ends, another begins. Super excited to share with you my new beginning,’ she said.

While she has shared the occasional photo of her partner, she’s been keen to keep her love life private, referring to him only as ‘D’.

And Danielle’s been glowing throughout her pregnancy, showing off her bump at the OITNB world premiere as she posed alongside her co-stars.

Meanwhile, she’s not been afraid to show off her body, recently sharing a gorgeous photo shoot and writing: ‘This moment is sacred. This moment is about birth and RE-birth.

‘This moment is discovering new parts of myself that I never knew existed before. This moment is about gaining clarity on what I will or will not except so that when my daughter watches me, she is clear on who she is and what she believes.

This moment is understanding what’s worth my energy and what is not.  This moment is unapologetically leaning on those that truly understand the definition of family and friendship.

This moment is about experiencing all that life has to offer me as a woman. I’m living and basking in the right now! And it feels damn good. #mothertobe.’
