I'm A Celebrity fans baffled by Myles Stephenson's 'pointless' luxury item

This year's I'm A Celebrity campmates requested some very unusual items to take in the jungle with them

19 Nov 2019, 08:53 by Irishmirror.ie

During the latest episode of I'm A Celebrity, the campmates were tasked with retrieving their much-longed for luxury items as part of a late night mini-challenge.

With the famous faces forced to live in basic al fresco conditions with no contact from their families for up to a month, home comforts are of vital importance when it comes to jungle survival.

On Tuesday night's show as each celebrity revealed what they had selected to bring with them for support, viewers were left double-taking when they heard Myles Stephenson's canny idea.

The Rak-Su singer announced that he had opted to take a disposable camera in with him, allowing him to 'make memories' of his time on the ITV entertainment show.

Fans of the show were left in hysterics by his decision which seemed to forget that his antics were being filmed around the clock by an ITV production crew.

"Can’t get over the fact Myles wanted a disposable camera for memories of something that’s on national television," said one viewer.

"If you ever feel stupid, just remember that Myles chose a disposable camera as his luxury item...in the jungle...on ITV...24/7," laughed another.
