The West Brom transfer rumour that Baggies fans won't want to hear

Latest West Bromwich Albion transfer news | The Baggies defender is enjoying his breakthrough season at The Hawthorns but his current deal is due to expire at the end of the season

19 Nov 2019, 08:11 by Mark Wakefield

West Bromwich Albion will reportedly be will willing to sell defender Nathan Ferguson, if a new contract is not agreed.

The 19-year-old’s current deal at The Hawthorns expires at the end of the season, and has impressed in his breakthrough campaign with Albion .

Express & Star claim that a five-year deal has been offered to Ferguson, but is yet to put pen to paper on the extension.

The report adds that Albion will be forced into selling Ferguson in the January transfer window, should the teenager not commit to a new deal at The Hawthorns.

 

It has also been claimed that a number of clubs both in England and abroad are keeping tabs on the situation, with a view to a potential offer for the young defender.

Premier League duo Tottenham Hotspur and Crystal Palace have been linked with Ferguson, along with Serie A Champions Juventus and La Liga outfit Atletico Madrid.
