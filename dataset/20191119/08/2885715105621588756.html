Uniform of Rajya Sabha marshals will be reviewed, says Chairman Venkaiah Naidu

As the winter session of Parliament commenced Monday, marshals donned a new uniform in the Upper House — said to be the first in five decades.

19 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Express Web Desk

Rajya Sabha Chairman Venkaiah Naidu Tuesday said the new uniform of marshals would be reconsidered, a day after it received widespread criticism. As the House began proceedings this morning, Naidu said he had asked the Rajya Sabha secretariat to review the new uniforms, which resemble that of Army officials.

Naidu added that he had received some observations regarding the dark military-style uniform and peaked cap from politicians, among others.
