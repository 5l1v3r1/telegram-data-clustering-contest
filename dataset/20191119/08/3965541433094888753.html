Woman finds armed robber in her backseat after leaving Texas mall

19 Nov 2019, 08:36 by SBG San Antonio Staff Reports

SAN ANTONIO (WOAI/KABB) – A woman leaving a store at the North Star Mall, in San Antonio, Texas had a shocking surprise when she returned to her car.

The woman left the mall around 9 p.m. Monday and got into her car. She saw a man in her backseat.

Police said he pulled out a gun, robbed her and made her drive him across the street. He then got out of the vehicle and ran to a nearby apartment complex.

Thankfully, the woman was not hurt but the suspect did take some personal items.

Police are searching for the suspect.
