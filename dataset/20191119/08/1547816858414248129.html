Tanhaji - The Unsung Warrior Trailer Review: One Word For Film's First Rushes Feat Ajay Devgn-Kajol - EPIC

19 Nov 2019, 08:44 by Divya Ramnani

The trailer of Ajay Devgn, Kajol and Saif Ali Khan’s Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior and it’s nothing short of a masterpiece, read our review here

It’s raining period dramas in Bollywood and we aren’t complaining at all! All hail the might Sanjay Leela Bhansali, who paved the way with some of his masterpieces like Bajirao Mastani and Padmaavat. At present, the internet is abuzz with Ajay Devgn’s upcoming magnum opus titled Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior, a film based on the life of Subedar Taanaji Malusare, the military leader in the army of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who lost his life in the battle at Fort of Sinhagad. The trailer of this film was unveiled today and it’s every bit impressive, grand and exciting! (ALSO READ - Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior Trailer Early Reviews: First Rushes Of Ajay Devgn-Kajol's Period Drama Suggest That It’s A Visual Treat)

The trailer boasts of some spectacular performances by the entire cast of Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior, including Ajay Devgn, Kajol, Saif Ali Khan, Sharad Kelkar and Luke Kenny. And a special mention to the highly-engrossing VFX and visuals that will take you to that particular era, quite literally. Real-life couple Ajay Devgn and Kajol’s chemistry, too, is unmissable, whereas Saif Ali Khan, as the merciless antagonist Udaybhan hogs the limelight, during certain scenes. In a nutshell, Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior is an EPIC saga that promises entertainment, knowledge and grandeur in abundance.
