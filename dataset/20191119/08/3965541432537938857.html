2 teens injured as car slams into loading dock of Auburn drug store

19 Nov 2019, 08:35 by KOMO News Staff

AUBURN, Wash. - Two teens are hospitalized after their car slammed into the loading dock of a Walgreens drug store in Auburn overnight, emergency officials said.

Police and medics responded to the scene, the back of the Walgreens store at 1701 Auburn Way South, at about 3:30 a.m. after receiving reports of a vehicle hitting a commercial building, says the Valley Regional Fire Authority.

A car was found by the loading dock with its front end caved in. The driver and passenger, both males in their late teens, were both injured. One was taken to Multicare Auburn Medical Center, and the other to Harborview Medical Center.

The circumstances leading up to the crash are under investigation.

The building itself sustained very little damage, and the store remains open for business.
