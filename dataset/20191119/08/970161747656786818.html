19 Nov 2019, 08:57

Evidently Pete Davidson and Kaia Gerber are in that stage of relationship where you can’t stay away from one another

Cute.

Pete Davidson and Kaia Gerber have only been rumoured to be dating for a hot minute, but right now the heat is well and truly on.

After the 18-year-old model took a birthday cake to Pete at Saturday Night Live on, er, Saturday night, it seems the couple can’t stay away from one another.

Yes, workplace visits and all, it appears Pete and Kaia are in that heady period of any relationship where you’re stuck to one another’s side.

Isn’t young love grand?

Now the pair are taking in concerts, as they headed to see Charlotte Lawrence perform in New York City on Monday night.

Wearing the classic model’s outfit of blue, straight-leg jeans, a white crop and jacket, Kaia walked ahead of Pete as he kept his head down.
