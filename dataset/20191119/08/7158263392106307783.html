19 Nov 2019, 08:14

News flash: You’re probably brushing your teeth wrong. “Most people aren’t holding their brush the right way and they’re definitely not brushing for two whole minutes,” says Michael Apa, DDS, cosmetic dentist at the Rosenthal Apa Group in NYC. Which is exactly why everyone (yes, even you) needs to switch to an electric toothbrush. If you brush with a manual toothbrush, you need to be brushing in circular motions while holding the brush at a 45-degree angle along the gum line to effectively clean your teeth (umm, who actually does this??). But if you use an electronic toothbrush, all you have to do is hold the brush at your gum line and let the vibrations do their thing until the two-minute timer goes off, says Dr. Apa. Easy, right?
