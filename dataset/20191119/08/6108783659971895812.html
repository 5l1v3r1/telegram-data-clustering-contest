Google Stadia launch library increases to 22 on launch day

19 Nov 2019, 08:50 by Steve Wright

Not that it matters to Aussies.

Google Stadia has launched in select markets today, surprisingly bumping its launch library from 12 games to 22.

Here’s the new list of launch titles available for Google’s streaming platform:

  • Destiny 2: The Collection (free with Stadia Pro)
  • Samurai Showdown (free with Stadia Pro)
  • Assassin’s Creed Odyssey
  • Attack on Titan: Final Battle 2
  • Farming Simulator 2019
  • Final Fantasy 15
  • Football Manager 2020
  • Grid
  • Gylt
  • Just Dance 2020
  • Kine
  • Metro Exodus
  • Mortal Kombat 11
  • NBA 2K20
  • Rage 2
  • Rise of the Tomb Raider
  • Red Dead Redemption 2
  • Shadow of the Tomb Raider
  • Thumper
  • Tomb Raider
  • Trials Rising
  • Wolfenstein: Youngblood

What do you make of the above list? It’s solid… but mostly dated, right?

Stadia isn’t available in Australia as yet; a release date has not been named.
