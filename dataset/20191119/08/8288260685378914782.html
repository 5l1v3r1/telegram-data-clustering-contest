'Don't forget' - John Terry taunts Celtic star with brilliant five-word message

Aston Villa latest news | Assistant head coach John Terry has playfully taunted Celtic's Scott Sinclair about his gym activity with the former Chelsea man posting online

19 Nov 2019, 08:25 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa assistant head coach John Terry has sent a brilliant five-word message to Celtic star Scott Sinclair.

The pair were team-mates at Chelsea, with Sinclair joining in 2005 from boyhood club Bristol Rovers.

Former Villa loanee Sinclair spent five years at Stamford Bridge, making just five League appearances, before heading to Swansea City.

At the Liberty Stadium was where he made his name, earning a move to Manchester City as a result of his good performances for the swashbuckling Swans.

 

Sinclair is now at Celtic, but he doesn't get a look in these days making just six appearances this season - just two of those coming in the Scottish Premirship.

Judging by his social media activity he is keeping himself in tip-top shape, though - he posted a picture on Instagram yesterday of himself with the caption: "No matter what the situation, I’m focused and ready..!"

While Sinclair was obviously trying to be serious, Terry couldn't help himself, replying: "don’t forget the legs geez."
