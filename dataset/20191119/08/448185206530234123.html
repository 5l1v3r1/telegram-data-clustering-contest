Silver jubilee celebration of Rajabari Junior College begins in Demow

19 Nov 2019, 08:36

A Correspondent

DEMOW: Preparation is on to celebrate the silver jubilee of Rajabari Junior College in Desang Rajabari near Demow. The three-day silver jubilee celebration will begin on December 20. A magazine titled Aarjasiyan will be published and for that, the alumni have been urged to submit their writings by November 27.
