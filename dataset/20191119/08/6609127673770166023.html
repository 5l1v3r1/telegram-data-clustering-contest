Celtic ‘seeking to extend’ Man City starlet Daniel Arzani’s loan deal

19 Nov 2019, 08:36 by David Fowler

CELTIC are keen on extending Daniel Arzani's loan deal from Man City, according to reports.

The Hoops are reported to be interested in keeping the young winger for a further season despite his lack of first-team action.

Australian football website The World Game claim Hoops boss Neil Lennon wants the 20-year-old to stay on at Celtic.

Discussions are reported to be in the early stages but it's claimed Arzani would be interested in another season in Glasgow.

Arzani last played for the Hoops first-team against Dundee 13 months ago.

He has only managed training and the rare reserve outing ever since suffering a tear to his ACL on his debut.

Meanwhile, Swansea are set to make a move for Jonny Hayes.

Championship rivals Stoke City are also eyeing a move for the Ireland international.

Hayes' current contract is set to run out in the summer with clubs beginning to circle.

But Celtic don't want to lose the utility player and want him to commit his future to Parkhead.
