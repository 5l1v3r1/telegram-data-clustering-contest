Rakul Preet Singh Becomes Another Leading Lady Of John Abraham & Jacqueline Fernandez’s Attack

The film that had Jacqueline cast already turned out to be a two heroine film and will have Rakul Preet Singh as well.

19 Nov 2019, 08:06 by Koimoi.com Team

John Abraham’s next titled Attack has been in the headlines every now and then since its inception. The actor announced the film earlier this year and later we saw Jacqueline Fernandez join the cast of the film. Today bringing in a new update is that Marjaavaan actress Rakul Preet Singh has also joined the cast and below is all you need to know about the same.

The film that had Jacqueline cast already turned out to be a two heroine film and will have Rakul Preet Singh as well. If reports in Pinkvilla are to be believed, a source close to the development said, “It’s a two-heroine project. Both the roles are really powerful and action-oriented. The team was scouting for two good looking actors who can also perform and do stunts really well. It’s then that they took the film to Rakul Preet Singh. She was their first choice for the role.”

Further giving details that Rakul has also greenlit the project. The source added, “She loved the script and her role in it. The final modalities are being worked out. Post that, the team will make an official announcement of the same soon. It goes on the floors in January next year.”

Attack is an action drama directed by Lakshya Raj Anand. It is being said that not just John but the two actresses will also have to indulge in some top-notch action.

Meanwhile, Rakul who was also seen in De De Pyaar De alongside Tabu and Ajay Devgn this year is been seen in Sidharth Malhotra and Tara Sutaria starrer Marjaavaan, which is in the theatres right now. She also has the cross border drama opposite Arjun Kapoor.
