Liam Payne arrives in Paris with Maya Henry amid confusion over model's age

19 Nov 2019, 12:33

Liam Payne’s relationship with Maya Henry is going places… including to the City of Love.

The couple took their relationship public in August, although they are believed to have been dating for months before that, and have been inseparable ever since.

And Maya, 19, was on hand to accompany her beau to Paris, as the pair were seen disembarking the Eurostar looking effortlessly cool.

Liam, 26, wore a yellow-trimmed tracksuit beneath a plaid coat, teamed with retro aviator glasses, for the journey, and was serving us Blue Steel vibes as he arrived at Gare du Nord.

Meanwhile, model Maya kept her head down beneath a baseball cap, while wearing a white top, an embroidered denim jacket and tracksuit bottoms.

The couple’s trip to France comes amid confusion about Maya’s real age – after internet sleuths reckoned the model is a year younger than reported.

One such detective tweeted last week: ‘Every article about Liam’s girlfriend says she’s 19 when she’s actually 18.

‘This is intentional because when fabricating this ridiculous PR campaign for her they said that they’ve been together for a year, which would mean that Liam was dating a 17 y/o. She’s a senior in HS.’

However, Strip That Down singer Liam – who has a two-year-old son Bear with ex Cheryl – hit back at the claims, tweeting: ’19, don’t believe everything you read on the internet.’

The confusion stems from a number of alleged birth dates floating about for Maya.

Various sources claim that Maya was born on 12 February 2001 and 15 June 2000 – although, according to Heat World, Maya herself said she was born in December.

In a clip from her family’s reality show Hanging With Los Henrys, from 2018, Maya said she was 17 and that she’d be 18 in December – meaning that Maya would now be 18, and turning 19 in a few weeks time.

In addition to this, in 2016, Maya’s father, Texan attorney Thomas J Henry, threw his daughter an epic quinceañera – a traditional Latin American celebration of a girl’s 15th birthday – worth a whopping $6 million (£4.6 million).

If the party, which saw Nick Jonas and Pitbull perform, was timely for Maya’s birthday, she would have been 15 in March 2016 – meaning she’d still be 18 now.
