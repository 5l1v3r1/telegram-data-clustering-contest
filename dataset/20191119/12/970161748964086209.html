Kevin Clifton no longer needs attention from other girls and that's nice

19 Nov 2019, 12:09

Kevin Clifton has admitted he’s swapped clubbing for cups of tea ever since shacking up with Stacey Dooley.

Seems he no longer needs attention from other women and good for him.

The pair made things official after winning last year’s Strictly Come Dancing title and now the pair are trucking along nicely Kevin loves the quiet nights in.

‘I used to be like, “When I’m in a bar I need to be the coolest one that can drink more than anyone else and I need the girls to fancy me”,’ he told The Sun.

‘I thought I needed it all to exist, when actually I didn’t need any of it to exist. I realised, “Do I really want to be in a bar until 4am trying to get someone’s approval?” Or would I rather [be] at home watching Match of the Day with a cup of tea?

‘You don’t have to be the cool party guy, getting everyone’s attention.’

The feeling is mutual, as Stacey herself cooed over her boyfriend, calling her romance with Kevin ‘amazing’ and insisting she is ‘happy’ and feels ‘very lucky’ to have him.

She previously said: ‘Kev and I have agreed not to go into too much detail about our private lives because you can’t really indulge that and then ask for privacy.

‘That’s why we’ve turned down interviews that have offered us tens of thousands of pounds. What I am happy to say is Kev’s amazing, I’m happy, life happens, I’ve got an amazing career and I’m very lucky.’

The pair celebrated Kevin’s 37th birthday recently, after Stacey praised her beau following his knock out from Strictly.

Kevin and dance partner Anneka Rice were booted off 2019’s season of Strictly Come Dancing in the second week.

After the announcement of their elimination, Stacey told fans on social media: ‘Kev and Anneka got knocked out this weekend and I just want to say I am so proud of both of them, but particularly Kevin because I just think he is the most incredible teacher.’
