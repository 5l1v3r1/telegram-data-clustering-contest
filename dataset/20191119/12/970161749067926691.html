Chloe Goodman confirms pregnancy amid fears of premature birth

19 Nov 2019, 12:04

Congratulations are in order for Chloe Goodman and Grant Hall – the couple are expecting their first child together.

Chloe, 26, confirmed her pregnancy as she told Closer magazine that she’s always wanted to have a baby: ‘I’ve wanted to be a mum for years.

‘I’m from a family of five girls and I’ve always had maternal instincts. When I met Grant, we agreed early on that we wanted to become parents, so it feels like everything has fallen into place.’

Chloe, who found fame on Ex On The Beach in 2014, which was followed by a stint on Celebrity Big Brother in 2015, has been hard at work preparing for the new arrival.

She explained: ‘Now that I’ve passed the three-month mark, I’ve completely dived into being pregnant.

‘We started the nursery, then Grant decided he wanted to renovate the whole house so it’s perfect and to make it baby friendly.’

The pregnancy hasn’t been entirely stress-free for Chloe and her Queen’s Park Rangers footballer beau – she revealed that treatment following an abnormal smear test result means she may not carry to full term.

Chloe had part of her cervix removed to get rid of the pre-cancerous cells, and explained: ‘I knew I needed to get rid of the abnormal cells and have the op, but it was stressful because I worried I wouldn’t be able to carry a child full-term.

‘It sparked me into trying sooner to give myself the best chance. There’s a plan in action and I’m being monitored.’

She added that she is avoiding any strenuous activity before she has a meeting with her consultant in December and a stitch put in her cervix to stop her going into labour early.

Chloe has previously suffered a miscarriage with her ex-partner, Jordan Clarke, and admitted the heartbreak of losing a baby made her even more fearful in the first few weeks of this pregnancy.

The couple are now planning to have a lavish gender reveal party on New Year’s Eve to celebrate the upcoming arrival in 2020.
