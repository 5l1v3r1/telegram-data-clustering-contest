Okanagan man who killed Belgian tourist gets life in prison

19 Nov 2019, 12:48 by Jonathan Szekeres

VANCOUVER (NEWS 1130) – The Okanagan man who plead guilty to the second-degree murder of a Belgian hitch-hiker will spend at least the next 23 years behind bars.

Castanet reports Sean McKenzie, from Oliver, has been sentenced to life in prison with no chance of parole for at least 23 years.
