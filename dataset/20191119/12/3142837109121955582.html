Goodfellow Inc. (GDL) To Go Ex-Dividend on November 21st

19 Nov 2019, 12:49 by Scott Moore

Goodfellow Inc. (TSE:GDL) declared an annual dividend on Thursday, November 21st, TickerTech reports. Investors of record on Friday, December 6th will be given a dividend of 0.10 per share on Friday, December 6th. This represents a dividend yield of 2.06%. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Thursday, November 21st.

Shares of Goodfellow stock traded up C$0.10 during trading on Tuesday, reaching C$5.00. The company’s stock had a trading volume of 3,500 shares, compared to its average volume of 2,256. The stock has a market capitalization of $42.20 million and a P/E ratio of 14.75. Goodfellow has a one year low of C$4.50 and a one year high of C$6.45. The company has a current ratio of 1.88, a quick ratio of 0.74 and a debt-to-equity ratio of 49.48. The stock has a 50-day moving average price of C$5.07 and a two-hundred day moving average price of C$5.23.

Goodfellow (TSE:GDL) last posted its quarterly earnings data on Friday, October 4th. The company reported C$0.29 earnings per share for the quarter. The company had revenue of C$130.59 million for the quarter.

About Goodfellow

Goodfellow Inc engages in the wholesale distribution of lumber products, building materials, and hardwood flooring products to the retail trade, industrial, and manufacturing sectors in Canada and the United States. It is also involved in the remanufacturing and distribution of lumber and wood products.
