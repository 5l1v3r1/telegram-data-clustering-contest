Bollywood superstar Arijit Singh to play Arena Birmingham

One of the voices of India will tour Europe and the UK with a blockbuster show in Birmingham next summer

19 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Josh Layton, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/josh-layton/

Arijit Singh is set for a blockbuster show when he touches down at Arena Birmingham on a tour of the UK and Europe next summer.

The Indian singer, much loved for his soulful voice and songs of romance and love, is following up a sell-out UK tour in 2018.

He is a giant of Bollywood with a huge catalogue of anthemic songs, such as the award-winning 'Tum Hi Ho' from movie Aashiqui 2.

Arijit will appear at Arena Birmingham on Friday, July 17, 2020.

The tour - announced on the back of a mammoth year of gigs including a '12 cities' tour of the US - also takes in London and Rotterdam in Holland.

The superstar's impressive versatility comes from a background in Indian classic music, appearances on reality TV music shows and an exhaustive collection of hit music from Bollywood films.

His only date in Birmingham is brought to fans by Rock On Music and TLC Events.
