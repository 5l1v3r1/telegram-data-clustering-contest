Newcastle United pair Miguel Almiron and Allan Saint-Maximin retain the backing of their team-mates despite failing to score this season.

In a combined 20 appearances this season - nearly 1,500 minutes of football - the duo are yet to register.

Indeed, Almiron is yet to score since his January arrival from MLS side Atlanta United.

However, their profligacy in front of goal hasn't coincided with a drop in form for Steve Bruce 's side..
