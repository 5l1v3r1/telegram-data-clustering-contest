When Anushka Sharma put Shah Rukh Khan in a sticky spot

19 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Filmfare

Anushka Sharma and Shah Rukh Khan have a really close bond as the duo has worked together in Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi and Jab Harry Met Sejal. Anushka is extremely fond of the superstar as she made her debut opposite him in Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi in 2008. Recently, Jab Harry Met Sejal director Imtiaz Ali got candid during an interview about SRK and Anushka.

The director recalled the time when they were shooting for the film in Portugal and Anushka and he had asked SRK to approach a girl.
