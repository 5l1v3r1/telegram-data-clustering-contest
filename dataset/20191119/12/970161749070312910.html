Are I'm a Celeb getting new arrivals tonight? This Morning digs for BTS info

19 Nov 2019, 12:12

I’m a Celebrity’s camp could be about to get bigger with the tease of potential new arrivals on This Morning.

Alison Hammond and John Barrowman have flown over to Australia for behind-the-scenes info and featuring on spin-off Extra Camp.

So naturally, Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield couldn’t resist asking the question on everyone’s lips – are we about to see

The usually chatty pair then went quiet before Allison teased: ‘To be honest with you, I’ve been trying to find out a little bit of gossip. I’ve been going into offices, going into the galleries…but everything is literally on lockdown.’

‘There are rumours but we don’t know anything, if I’m honest,’ she added. ‘I have tried.’

‘All the boards on the walls about what’s happening – you can kind of look, but it’s all kept very undercover, very secretive,’ John added. ‘So if it’s going to happen, it’s going to be a surprise not only to everyone in the camp, but to all of us watching.’

‘And everyone’s got codenames as well!’ added Allison.

It’s currently heavily rumoured that Andy Whyment (aka Kirk from Corrie) and Cliff Parisi (aka Minty from Eastenders) will be heading into the show as the final two campmates.

Show sources say that the pair will be joining the show together at some point in the next week.

The two actors will join a camp that has already been through a lot, after the opening episode saw the contestant face bugs, sweat (if it’s medically possible, some people can’t) and tears.

Tonight’s episode will see the luxury (if you can call it that camp) and the basic camp finally come together – after one more bushtucker trial that sees celebs have their heads trapped in globes.
