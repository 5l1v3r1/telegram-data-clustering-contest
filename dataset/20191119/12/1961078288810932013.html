Why Ben Affleck and Gal Gadot Are Tweeting #ReleaseTheSnyderCut

19 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Kyle Buchanan

The most talked-about movie on social media this week is one that may never come out.

Two years after the release of “Justice League,” a critically derided superhero team-up starring, among others, Ben Affleck as Batman and Gal Gadot as Wonder Woman, those stars took to social media to advocate for a different cut of the film, one made by its deposed director, Zack Snyder.

“#ReleaseTheSnyderCut,” Gadot tweeted on Sunday afternoon, echoed in short order by Affleck. Snyder, who was replaced by director Joss Whedon when Warner Bros. ordered reshoots for “Justice League,” quote-tweeted both stars approvingly.
