Varg releases first EP as Varg2™ after cease and desist threat

The Swedish artist was forced to change his name earlier this year.

19 Nov 2019, 12:05 by Mark Smith

Varg2™ is releasing an EP on Northern Electronics.

The five-track Fuck Varg references the Swedish artist's name change following a cease and desist claim by a German metal band earlier this year. While it's his first solo EP as Varg2™, he's released multiple tracks under the moniker in recent months.

The new EP was recorded in Copenhagen, Kyiv and Tbilisi this year, and also includes a remix by Polish producer VTSS on the digital version. It will be available on vinyl and digital formats from December 12th.

Fuck Varg is Varg2™'s third release on Anthony Linell's Northern Electronics this year, following the two Sky City EPs. Two more Varg2™ releases are due out before the end of the year: a collaboration with Croatian Amor on Posh Isolation, and an hour-long tape made with Ano Ton.
