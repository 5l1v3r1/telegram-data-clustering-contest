Baffled This Morning viewers all asking the same question after Phillip Schofield disappears

Every morning, from Monday to Thursday, Phillip Schofield presents the ITV1 daytime favourite with Holly Willoughby

19 Nov 2019, 12:53 by James Rodger, Rebecca Koncienzcy, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Baffled This Morning fans have taken to social media - and they're all asking the same question: "where's Phil?"

Every morning, from Monday to Thursday, Phillip Schofield presents the ITV1 daytime favourite with Holly Willoughby.

But today, fans of the show flocked to social media to have their say after a segment of the show left them puzzled.

Baffled viewers were quick to address one scene - after eagle-eyed fans spotted Phil's disappearing act.

The silver-haired presenter was missing from a key segment, forcing Holly, 38, to present alone.

The Liverpool Echo, our sister title, reports the 57-year-old was absent from a fashion item which saw Trinny Woodall talk through the best ways to 'power dress'.

There was an empty seat next to Holly as Phil was nowhere to be seen and fans of the ITV daytime show were quick to notice.

One viewer, taking to Twitter, wrote:: " Phil pulled a disappearing act."
