Overnights: 80s film ‘Naseeb Apna Apna’ surprises on Monday in UK

19 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Raj Baddhan

‘Naseeb Apna Apna’ (1986) proved a surprise ratings winner on Monday in UK ratings, according to exclusive data obtained by BizAsiaLive.com.

The film starring Rishi Kapoor and Farah, was telecast on ZEE Cinema at 14:00. It attracted a huge 25,800 viewers – peaking at 53,900 viewers. As well as ‘Naseeb Apna Apna’, the film ‘Roti Kapda Aur Makaan’ pulled in 15,000 viewers at 10:30.

Both the films gave ZEE Cinema a huge boost in share with 0.10%, putting on par with Sony TV in UK ratings on Monday, behind Star Plus, which garnered 0.15%. ZEE Cinema was overall the most watched Bollywood movie channel in UK on Monday.

Please note: Above data is overnight figures, which may change slightly after seven days when consolidated data has been accounted for. BizAsiaLive.com publishes consolidated figures every Monday.
