Eleven People Killed, 25 More Injured After Three Buses Collide in Mexico - Reports

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - At least 11 people have been killed and 25 more injured as three buses collided in the state of Mexico, the Milenio newspaper reported.

19 Nov 2019, 12:14

The incident occurred on the Mexico City-Pachuca highway at 20:00 local time on Monday (02:00 GMT on Tuesday), according to the outlet.

According to the media, the drivers of all the three of the buses fled the scene following the incident.
