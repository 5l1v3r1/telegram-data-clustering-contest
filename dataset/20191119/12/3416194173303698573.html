Shay Mitchell Says Partner Matte Babel Is a Pro Dad Already

19 Nov 2019, 12:36

Shay Mitchell is gushing about her partner Matte Babel‘s dad skills!

The 32-year-old Dollface actress recently opened up about how he’s doing as a parent, and even where daughter Atlas‘ first trip will be.

“[He's] awesome, but I always knew he was going to be an incredible dad. So, it’s really cool, and he’s like, such a pro,” she told ET over the weekend.

As for Atlas‘ first trip around the world – Canada, which is where both Shay and Matte are from.

“Vancouver to visit Grandma, so that is going to be the first place,” she said. “She is going to go see Canada.”
