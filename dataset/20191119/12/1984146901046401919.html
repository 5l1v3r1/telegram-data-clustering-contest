I'm A Celebrity elimination odds revealed and bookies think they know who will go first

The I'm A Celebrity first elimination odds have been revealed already with bookies naming their pick to leave first

19 Nov 2019, 12:54 by Simon Duke

This year's stars have barely had the chance to settle into jungle life but bookies have already revealed who they think will be the first to leave I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here this year.

I'm A Celebrity returned to our screens for its 19th series on Sunday night, with Ant & Dec once again at the helm.

In a big twist, this year's lineup, which includes Ian Wright, Caitlyn Jenner and Nadine Coyle, were immediately divided after the 'power pair' challenge, with those in the main camp enjoying relative luxury, with the unlucky celebs in the rough camp had to survive on basic rations and even sleep on the floor.

I'm A Celebrity odds are changing all the time, with a new favourite to win revealed on Tuesday and bookies have also opened betting on who will be the first famous face to be eliminated from the jungle.

We don't even know when the voting out will start yet, but bookmakers William Hill have revealed their odds on who will go first.
