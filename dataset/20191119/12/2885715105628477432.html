Bigg Boss 13 wild card Himanshi Khurana: I don’t need to take advantage of Shehnaaz Gill’s popularity

While Himanshi Khurana is safe this week, the nominated contestants include Sidharth Shukla, Rashami Desai, Devoleena Bhattacharjee, Arti Singh and Khesari Lal Yadav.

19 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Sana Farzeen

Himanshi Khurana entered Bigg Boss 13 with a bang. Her presence led to an emotional outburst by Shehnaaz Gill, as the two share an ugly past. As already revealed by the two ladies, their social media criticism developed into a major fight, with both of them also abusing each other’s family.

While the issue hasn’t been resolved yet, Himanshi and Shehnaaz have been civil to each other, though they are yet to become friends. But interestingly, in last night’s episode, we saw Shehnaaz saving Himanshi from nomination.

Before entering the show, Himanshi spoke to indianexpress.com about deciding to live with her rival under the same roof. When asked what made her take the offer, Himanshi said, “The controversy involving Shehnaaz and me had garnered national attention. I think this is why I got approached by the makers. The world wants to know the reality, and I hope through this show, people know what the truth is. Also, she might be showing a side of Punjab that many wouldn’t even relate to. I can project the real Punjabis to the audience.”

Himanshi has been accused of taking advantage of Shehnaaz’s popularity. The singer-actor said, “Why would I need to take advantage of her popularity? I have been working in the industry for 10 years, and I collaborate with only A-listers. Also, if you look at our social media following, you would know who is more popular in real. Also, not everyone can handle fame, I am mature enough to be grounded.”
