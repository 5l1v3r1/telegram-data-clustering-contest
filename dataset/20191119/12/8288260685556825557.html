Man taken to hospital after Oldbury smash

The crash happened on Churchbridge last night (November 18) at around 7.30pm

19 Nov 2019, 12:17 by Charlotte Regen, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/

A man has been hospitalised after a van and car crashed.

Medics rushed to the scene on Churchbridge, Oldbury, at around 7.30pm, last night (Monday, November 18).

The van driver was taken to Sandwell hospital as a precaution after he suffered minor injuries.

A man and a woman - who were in the car - were discharged at the scene.

A spokesman for West Midlands Ambulance Service said: “We were called at 7.30pm last night to Churchbridge in Oldbury to reports of a crash between a van and car.

"We sent an ambulance and two paramedic officers to the scene where they assessed three patients. One man, who was the driver of the van was taken to Sandwell Hospital as a precaution following treatment for minor injuries.

"Another man, who was the driver of the car, and a woman, who was the passenger in the same vehicle were discharged on scene.”
