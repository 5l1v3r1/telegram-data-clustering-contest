This Former Disney Channel Star Worked On 'High School Musical: The Musical: The Series'!

19 Nov 2019, 12:55

Former Disney Channel and Disney XD star Jason Earles helped show the ropes to the cast of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series!

The 42-year-old actor, who starred as Jackson Stewart on Hannah Montana and as Rudy Gillespie/Bobby Wasabi on Kickin’ It, revealed that he actually worked as a mentor and acting coach on the new Disney+ series.

“Spent 6 Months in Salt Lake working with this amazing group, as an acting coach and mentor of sorts. By the end, we were all just family,” he shared on Instagram.

“It was humbling to watch these insanely gifted people work to constantly be better, and never settle. I am so proud of how the show turned out. Heartfelt and funny, it’s totally original but respectful of its history, and filled with amazing new music,” Jason added.

“So happy the world can finally watch these guys on Disney+ and see what I see…the next generation of Disney Stars. Congrats! Love all you guys! Can’t wait for Season 2 😉,” he concluded.
