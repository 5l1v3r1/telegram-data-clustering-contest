Firefighters battle house fire in Midland

19 Nov 2019, 12:59

A Midland home has been engulfed by flames this evening, prompting the Department of Fire and Emergency Services to issue a smoke alert for people in the immediate area.

The fire broke out at a two by one along Victoria Street about 8.20pm, a sending plume of smoke into the eastern suburb.

The smoke is expected to clear in the coming hours.

Authorities still couldn’t confirm the cause of the blaze or if there were suspicious circumstances but said the matter would be investigated.

By 9pm, firefighters managed to get the blaze under control.

Police have also been at the scene this evening to direct traffic in the area for the attending fire and rescue units.

It’s understood no one was inside at the time and St John Ambulance we’re required to attend.

Drivers have been urged to be extremely careful when travelling through the area and told to watch for emergency services personnel.

People who have a respiratory condition and have been affected by smoke are urged to contact their local doctor or call Health Direct on 1800 022 222.
