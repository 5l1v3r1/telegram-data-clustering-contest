Camila Cabello Reveals The Most Rebellious Thing She's Ever Done

19 Nov 2019, 12:53

Camila Cabello heads into a super festive home after a workout at the gym in Los Angeles on Tuesday afternoon (November 19).

The 21-year-old singer was seen picking up a bite to eat and some juice after her workout before heading out for the day with her mom Sinuhe.

Speaking with Rolling Stone recently, Camila opened up about the most rebellious thing she’s ever done in her life and turns out, it happened while she was still in Fifth Harmony.

“When I was in the group [Fifth Harmony], me and one of the girls snuck into this club on the first floor of a hotel for a white party,” she revealed. “We were, like, 16 or something, and if we wanted to leave the room, we had to tell the tour manager so our security and our team at the time were looking for us. It’s not a great answer: The last rebellious thing I did was when I was 16 years old.”

However, it was a fun party.

Camila added, “It was fun because we weren’t allowed to be in it. But actually this is inspiring me!”
