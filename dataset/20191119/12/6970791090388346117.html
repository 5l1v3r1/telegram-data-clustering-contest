YBN Cordae Gives Dr. Dre Edge Over Kanye West, Swizz Beatz + Everyone Else: “Greatest Producer Of All Time”

19 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Rosario Harper

East Coast rapper YBN Cordae is giving major respect to music mogul Dr. Dre. The hip-hop star has called the Doc the greatest producer of all time.

Last night, Cordae went online to salute Dre and remind the world he’s the best hitmaker ever.
