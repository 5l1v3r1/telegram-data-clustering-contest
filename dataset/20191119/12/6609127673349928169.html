Lewis Capaldi to drop ‘most personal’ music yet with new single ‘Before you Go’ later today

19 Nov 2019, 12:50 by Aidan Smith

LEWIS CAPALDI is set to release new single 'Before You Go' tonight.

The hit Scots artist insists his latest release will be his most personal tune to date.

He also took the opportunity to thank his loyal fan base for his recent rise to fame.

Taking to Twitter, the 23-year-old announced: "The past year has been absolutely wild, didn't in a million years think all of this stuff would happen to me.

"Less than two years ago I was lucky enough to get to gig in small pubs and bars back home in Scotland and somehow I am writing this with a number one single in America and getting to play to and meet thousands of ya every night all over the world.

"Today at 5pm UK time I am releasing 'Before You Go' which is by far the most personal tune I've ever written.

"Can't wait for ya to hear it. Lewis x"

We told you last week how a Glasgow pizzeria created an incredible Lewis Capaldi pizza.

Chefs at Shawlands restaurant Oro Italian made the incredible doughy creation as they became the latest to hype up the Scots superstar.

We also reported how Capaldi joked he was Troy Bolton from High School Musical after he was gifted a snazzy red and white varsity jacket by the NRJ Awards.

His hit tune Someone You Loved - which recently topped the US charts - was nominated for International Song Of The Year at the NRJ Awards.
