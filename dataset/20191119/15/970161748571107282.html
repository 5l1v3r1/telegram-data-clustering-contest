Spoilers: Ben has a decision after a proposition from Callum in EastEnders

19 Nov 2019, 15:03

Ben Mitchell (Max Bowden) has had something of a wake up call – not to mention a throbbing cheek – after a showdown with his disgusted mum Kathy Beale (Gillian Taylforth) over his recent behaviour in EastEnders. And with his relationship with Callum Highway (Tony Clay) on shaky ground, he has some decisions to make.

As Ben always does when he feels he is getting too happy, he plays the hard man and pushes those close to him away – and he left Callum feeling mucked about when he dismissed his ambition to join the police force. Refusing to be pushed around, Callum walked out and Ben got steadily more drunk during a family meal.

When Kathy overheard Ben ordering Martin Fowler (James Bye) to take on a stolen cars job, or else, she was horrified at him for blackmailing a family member. Ben was disparaging and turned on Kathy, telling her that she was weak and was to blame for the abuse she had endured in her life.
