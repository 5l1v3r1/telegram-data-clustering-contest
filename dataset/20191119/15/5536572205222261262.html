Rob Gronkowski’s ‘Big Announcement’ Seemingly Closes Door On 2019 Return To Patriots (VIDEO)

19 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Darrelle Lincoln

Over the weekend, Rob Gronkowski, who retired from the NFL in March, took to Instagram and stated that he would be dropping a big announcement on Tuesday morning.

“I have a big announcement to make. I can’t wait to drop it on all of you. It’s gonna be fun, it’s gonna be wild, it’s gonna be a good time,” Gronkowski said. “Come check out my Instagram page this Tuesday at 9:00am to hear the news. Lets go.”

Most people thought it would be football related or even wrestling related. It was neither.

Gronk revealed plans for a music festival called “Gronk Beach” that will be held Saturday, Feb. 1 on Miami Beach.
