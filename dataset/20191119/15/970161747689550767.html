Strictly's dance routines revealed as Bruno Tonioli to give surprise performance

19 Nov 2019, 15:44

Strictly Come Dancing has revealed the dance routine line-up for this week’s show – but it’s judge Bruno Tonioli that has a surprise up his sleeve (and we literally can’t wait for it).

This Saturday Strictly fans will be excited to know that Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard will be dancing the Samba to Walking on Sunshine, while Kelvin Fletcher will be performing a street dance to Do I Love You (Indeed I Do) with partner Oti Mabuse.

But it’s judge Bruno that viewers won’t be able to take their eyes off (see what I did there?) – as he will be performing a live rendition of Can’t Take My Eyes Off You, accompanied by a dance from the show’s team of professionals.

Last weekend saw Michelle Visage eliminated from the show after landing at the bottom of the leaderboard after scoring 32 points – and Strictly fans were less than impressed.

And while we’d hoped that fan power could’ve saved the RuPaul’s Drag Race judge, it wasn’t to be, with the 51-year-old Michelle facing her second consecutive bottom two as she faced off against a teary eyed Saffron Barker in the dreaded dance off.

With both celebs having been pegged for the final, the audience was naturally shocked by the bottom two, while the judges unanimously chose – despite Craig Revel Horwood calling the choice ‘really difficult’ – to save 19-year-old Saffron.

However, despite saying goodbye to Strictly and her partner Giovanni Pernice, Michelle seemed not to regret her decision to bring voguing to Blackpool.

‘We wanted to take a risk for Couple’s Choice,’ she told Tess Daly.

‘We love dancing Ballroom, we love doing our Latin but we wanted to bring something different for Couple’s Choice and do a dance with a mission statement behind it and we’re really proud.’

What are the dance routines for this week’s show?

Alex Scott and Neil Jones will perform an Argentine Tango to Never Tear Us Apart, as performed by Bishop Briggs

Emma Barton and Anton Du Beke will dance a Quickstep to Sparkling Diamonds from the Moulin Rouge! soundtrack

Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard will dance the Samba to Walking On Sunshine, as performed by the Tame Me To Rio Collective

Chris Ramsey and Karen Hauer will perform a Paso Doble to Run Boy Run by Woodkid

Strictly Come Dancing returns next Saturday at 7pm on BBC One.
