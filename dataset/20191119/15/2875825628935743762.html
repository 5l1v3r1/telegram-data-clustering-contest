Mel B recalls 'intense but beautiful' long term relationship with a woman

The Spice Girl is the host of a new podcast but opened up to guest Megan Barton-Hanson on what she thinks are the big differences between men and women

19 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Irishmirror.ie

Spice Girl Mel B has opened up about a "beautiful" five year relationship with a woman.

She's the host of new podcast The Truth Flirts, and gave guest, Love Island's Megan Barton-Hanson, a break from the mic by chatting about her own love life.

Megan spoke about how she probably wouldn't date another man and would be sticking with women, prompting Mel- who's previously bragged about her "huge libido and great sex life" - to discuss the differences in dating men and women.

"I do think that dating women is very different to dating men," she told Megan bluntly.

The 44 year old continued: "I had a five year relationship with a woman and it was an intense five years but it was actually a really beautiful five years.

"We barely argued, because we both thought the same, wanted the same out of life. It was really interesting when I actually compare it to my other relationships."

While Mel never names the woman she's talking about, it's possible she could have meant US film producer Christine Crokos - their romance lasted between 2002 and 2006.

She told The Guardian : "I had a girlfriend. So what? I did have a four year relationship with a woman.

"People call me a lesbian, bisexual or heterosexual, but I know who's in my bed."

A year after she ended her relationship with Christine, she married her second husband Stephen Belafonte.

Mel also revealed her attitude to first dates, and how important looks are to her to make a good first impression.

"On that first date," she said, "Looks are everything because it's the first thing that you see."

Scary Spice made waves earlier this year when she claimed she'd bedded Ginger Spice, Geri Halliwell.
