BBNaija: Tacha shares video of herself video calling Twitter CEO

19 Nov 2019, 15:47

- Popular reality show star, Tacha Akide, has taken to social media to wish Twitter CEO, Jack Dorsey, a happy birthday

- The young lady showed off a video call she had with the Twitter founder

- Tacha also penned down a birthday message

BBNaija 2019 reality show star, Natacha Akide, better known by her fans as Tacha, has made another move on social media that has got her fans excited.

The 23-year-old star took to her Instagram page on November 19, 2019, to wish Twitter CEO and co-founder, Jack Dorsey, a happy birthday.

Tacha posted a short snippet of the video call she had with the tech giant and she seemed very excited to be on a call with him even though their conversation was muted.

The reality star also penned down a short birthday note for Jack on her page. She thanked him for making Twitter what it is today and also urged her fans to vote the app to win best application of the year.
