2019 UK Championship Snooker draw, schedule, odds, TV channel and prize money

19 Nov 2019, 15:07

The first Triple Crown event of the season starts next week as the UK Championship gets underway at the Barbican Centre in York.

There is the biggest prize in the tournament’s history on offer as the winner will walk away from Yorkshire with a cool £200,000.

World champion Judd Trump is the favourite to do just that, but defending champion Ronnie O’Sullivan will be attracting plenty of money in the bookmakers to win the title for an eighth time.

The Rocket faces Ross Bulman in round one, while the world number one takes on Amine Amiri.

When is the 2019 UK Championship and what TV channel will it be on?

The event runs from 26 November – 8 December at the Barbican Centre in York.

The BBC and Eurosport will be covering the tournament.
