Steve Earle playing intimate autism research benefit dinner

19 Nov 2019, 15:25 by Bill Pearis

As mentioned, Steve Earle and City Winery are hosting the fifth annual John Henry’s Friends benefit concert, which raises money for children diagnosed with autism, on December 17 at Town Hall with performances from Jason Isbell, Amanda Shires, The Mastersons, Josh Ritter, with Earl and his band The Dukes headlining. Tickets are still available.

To warm up for the Town Hall show Steve will be performing a rehearsal show at City Vineyard on December 16 that includes a five-course dinner with wine pairings and an event poster signed by Steve Earle and guests. Tickets are sold in pairs ($750 total) and proceeds go to The Keswell School, an educational program for children and young adults with autism.

Jason Isbell is also performing at the 2019 Ally Coalition Talent Show at Town Hall on December 12 with Dixie Chicks, Mike Birbiglia, and more; and Amanda Shires is touring with L.A. Edwards in 2020.
