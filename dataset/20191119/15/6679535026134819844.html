Water released for extension areas under 18th channel

19 Nov 2019, 15:52 by Staff Reporter

Water for irrigation of extension areas under 18th channel, was released on Tuesday. The district collector M. Pallavi Baldev, released 95 cusecs of water from the Sudhagangai Odai.

Water will be released for 15 days totalling to a quantum of 121 mcft.

The water will benefit seven tanks including Kattabommankulam, Kottaikulam, Kamarajabalasamudrakulam, Vannar oorani, Soolapuram kulam, Paaraikulam and Koppan oorani, apart from recharging 585 wells in Thevaram, Pottipuram, Rasingapuram, Silamalai, Melachokkanathapuram and Bodinayakkanur. A total of 4794.71 acres of land will receive irrigation through the release.
