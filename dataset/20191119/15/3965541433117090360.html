Sounders goal keeper picked for MLS Expansion Draft

19 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Bill Swartz | KOMO News

A goal keeper from the MLS Cup champion Seattle Sounders FC was taken in Tuesday's Expansion Draft. Don’t worry Rave Green fans, it was NOT Stefan Frei.

Inter Miami C F used its fifth and final draft pick to select 30-year-old Bryan Meredith, who has played for both Seattle and its USL club, Tacoma Defiance.

Meredith was originally signed by Seattle in the MLS 2011 Super Draft and returned to Sounders FC in the 2017 season.

Miami’s new technical director Kurt Schmid knows plenty about Meredith’s abilities because his late father Sigi Schmid was Seattle’s head coach.
