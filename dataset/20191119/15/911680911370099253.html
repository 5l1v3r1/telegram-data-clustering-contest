Amazon to open brick-and-mortar book store in Scottsdale

19 Nov 2019, 15:07 by Brandon Brown, Phoenix Business Journal

The Valley’s first brick-and-mortar Amazon Books store is set to open Tuesday morning in Scottsdale.

The store is in a 4,000-square-foot space in a newly constructed building at Scottsdale Quarter, next to where Fox Restaurant Concepts is building its new Culinary Dropout location.

The store will carry books, devices, toys and games and other customer favorites.

Amazon employs a team of curators that select items for stores that are either rated 4 stars and above, bestsellers in stores or on Amazon.com, or new releases.
