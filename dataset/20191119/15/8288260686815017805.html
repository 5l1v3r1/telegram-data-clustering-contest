Metcalfe's Popcorn Jumbles launch new flavours for Christmas - and they sound great

Available in two fabulous flavour combos, Toffee Apple and Eton Mess, Popcorn Jumbles brings together an assortment of tastes and textures

19 Nov 2019, 15:06 by Brett Gibbons, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/brett-gibbons/

With a host of Christmas movies already hitting TV channels and streaming services, it's time to start stocking up on the popcorn for those nights in front of the telly.

Snack specialist Metcalfe's has launched a new version of the treat called Jumbles that promises to be a new festive flavour experience.

Available in two fabulous flavour combos, Toffee Apple and Eton Mess, Metcalfe’s Popcorn Jumbles brings together an assortment of tastes and textures.

 

Reminiscent of the much-loved traditional treat, toffee apple lovers can now munch on delicious toffee and caramel-coated popcorn jumbled up with slices of dried apple, without a thought about calories.

Alternatively, snack aficionados can enjoy the Eton Mess flavour, bursting with delectable strawberry pieces and crunchy meringue shells and mingled with strawberry-coated popcorn replicating the British summer favourite dessert - but lighter.

 

Made with the very best butterfly popcorn, this new sharable snack is a lusciously light substitute to those calorific chocolate share bags, making them perfect for movie nights and Christmas gatherings.

The snack is available now at Sainsbury’s for £1.79.
