Chiefs, Downs Fans Welcome Possible Furman Addition

19 Nov 2019, 15:30 by SL Social

Highly-rated Bafana Bafana midfielder Dean Furman is fast entering the last six months of his contract with SuperSport United.

As a result, Kaizer Chiefs and Mamelodi Sundowns fans have eyed up the midfielder as a possible addition to their club should he leave Matsatsantsa.

Furman has been a midfield workhorse for Kaitano Tambo’s team. Something SL Supporter @tebohowaleSundowns recognizes, and feels the 31-year-old is the type of player needed at the defending PSL champions.

“Typical midfielder who is always passionate about his game. I admire him a lot. His style of play is suitable for Mamelodi Sundowns anyway, I won't be surprised if I see their interest in his services.”

He is not the only admirer of Furman, SL Supporter @kc-inferno has seen the possibility of Furman possibly replacing the ageing Katsande in the Chiefs midfield.

“Bobsteak must wake up, after missing out on Cole Alexander this is a great chance to send Katsande to the stands by signing this guy.”

Not one for the limelight, SL Supporter @ Taayah-shimooshili0 has hailed Furman as one of the hardest working midfielders in the country.

“SA’s most underrated footballer by far.”
