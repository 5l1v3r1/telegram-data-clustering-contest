Two get 12 years of jail term

19 Nov 2019, 15:59 by Staff Reporter

Madurai

The Madurai District Court on Tuesday sentenced two men to 12 years of imprisonment and imposed a fine of ₹ one lakh, under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act, for possession of over 400 kg of ganja.

I Additional Special Judge for exclusive trial of cases under NDPS Act R. Sridharan sentenced Wilson Gabriel and Karthikeyan. The Narcotics Intelligence Bureau- Crime Investigation Department (NIBCID) of Thoothukudi seized seven gunny bags containing ganja from the duo. They were hidden in a building.
