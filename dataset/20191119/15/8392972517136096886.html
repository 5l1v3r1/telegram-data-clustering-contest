I'm A Celebrity viewers complain about Kate Garraway's bunches

Some have even threatened to stop watching because of it

19 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity are complaining about Kate Garraway's bunches.

The Good Morning Britain presenter has sported the hairstyle over the past couple of days during challenges and her first Bushtucker trial last night.

However, the bunches aren't going down well with some viewers who told Kate to ditch the hairdo on Twitter.
