Bob's Burgers Movie Removed, Then Readded to Disney Slate

19 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Sundi Rose

Fans of the animated FOX series, Bob's Burgers, might be suffering a bit of informational whiplash today regarding the release date of the feature film. Although talk of a movie version of Bob's Burgers has been around for a while now, Disney recently left it off the schedule of upcoming releases, causing quite the uproar, but has since released an update with the movie still scheduled for a 2020 release.

The confusion over whether or not there will be a movie is probably a direct result of the Disney/FOX merger, but fans are justifiably confused. Fans took to Twitter to get some answers and got reassurance from Bob's Burgers creator, Loren Bouchard.
