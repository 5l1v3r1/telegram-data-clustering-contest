Georgia Photographer Shares Picture She Took Before Getting Hit At Auburn

19 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Andrew Holleran

Georgia photographer Chamberlain Smith was hit hard on the sideline of the Bulldogs’ game at Auburn on Saturday. Smith was taken off the field on a stretcher and transported to the hospital in a scary moment. Thankfully, she’s since been released.

Smith shared on Twitter this morning the photo she took right before the collision on the sideline with Georgia running back Brian Herrien.

“For those of you asking if I got ‘the’ shot, here it is!! The last thing my camera & I saw before being tackled,” she tweeted.
