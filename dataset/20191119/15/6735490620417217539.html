Mandy Moore To Embark On First Tour In A Decade

19 Nov 2019, 15:37 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Mandy Moore is going on tour in 2020.

Moore, 35, has announced her first concert tour in more than a decade. The “This Is Us” actress will kick off her U.S tour in Pittsburgh on March 20.

“It’s been over a decade since I’ve hit the road but I am thrilled to put together an intimate, elevated evening of new and old tunes, performed with a superb group of musicians,” she said in a statement published by Variety.

“I want the show to have threads of what the writing and recording process was like with this band, almost like you’ve been invited in our living room for an inside glimpse,” Moore added.

The three-month tour wraps in Denver on May 9. Tickets will go on sale on Friday.

“It just feels good to be sharing this side of me again in its purest form,” Moore concluded. “A real side of me I have been itching to put back into the universe.”
