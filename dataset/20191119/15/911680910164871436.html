Sprouts shakes up board of directors, adds Five Below CEO

19 Nov 2019, 15:13 by Brandon Brown, Phoenix Business Journal

Phoenix-based grocery chain Sprouts Farmers Market Inc. has shaken up its board of directors.

The company said Monday that Joel Anderson , CEO of retailer Five Below Inc., will join the board.

“It is an honor to welcome Joel to our board of directors,” Sprouts CEO Jack Sinclair said in a statement.

“Joel’s deep experience as a high-growth retail executive at companies of scale will be extremely valuable to our board as we continue to develop our strategy for long-term growth and value creation.”
