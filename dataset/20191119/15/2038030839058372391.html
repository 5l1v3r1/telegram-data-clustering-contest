From the great outdoors to the kitchen: These are the top 3 reality shows you should be watching tonight

19 Nov 2019, 15:00

Cape Town – Reality competition shows are not for the faint-hearted luckily, we can enjoy all the action and drama from the couch.

From foodies looking for their big break, to 12 strangers trying to survive on a deserted island, to interior designers trying to win a life-changing contract – here are our 3 recommendations.

The Taste Master

19:30 - SABC3 (DStv 193)

In this exciting new reality show aspiring, undiscovered foodies will be put to the test with a series of challenging tasks which will test their skills as they battle it out in the hopes of becoming the resident chef on the channel’s popular variety show Afternoon Express.
