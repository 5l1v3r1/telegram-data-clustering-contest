AMD Releases Athlon 3000G Processor for Mainstream Desktop Users

19 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Hilbert Hagedoorn

Before we begin, our review sample got shipped to what seems to be another dimension, ergo we'll publish our review on Athlon 3000G at a later stage. AMD released the new AMD Athlon 3000G, its newest processor for mainstream desktop users.

Harnessing the power of the “Zen” architecture and Radeon Graphics, the Athlon 3000G delivers the ability to take on a diverse range of tasks, from everyday productivity applications to high-definition gaming – all while coming in at an incredible price point. The Athlon 3000G is also the first “Zen” based Athlon processor to be unlocked, and the first in this segment, giving enthusiasts the potential to overclock their processors and leverage even more of its power.

Consumers can purchase the Athlon 3000G in the coming days from participating retailers for $49. Again, AMD is issuing a new sample due to shipping issues, which should arrive soon we hope.
