Czarface prep RSD Black Friday release, playing NYC in January

19 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Andrew Sacher

Czarface — the underground hip hop project of the Wu-Tang Clan’s Inspectah Deck and 7L & Esoteric — remain very prolific in the studio (among their 2019 releases are a collab LP with Ghostface Killah and an upcoming Record Store Day Black Friday release called The Odd Czar Against Us), but they don’t seem to play live very often. So it’s exciting to learn that they’ll play NYC’s Sony Hall on January 16. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday (11/22) at noon with an AmEx presale starting Wednesday (11/20) at noon.

It appears to be their only upcoming show at the moment, but stay tuned in case that changes. Watch a video from their collab LP with Ghostface below.
