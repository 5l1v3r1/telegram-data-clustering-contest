Jim Harbaugh’s Quote About His Michigan Team Is Going Viral

19 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Chris Rosvoglou

This season didn’t begin the way Michigan hoped that it would, but Jim Harbaugh and his coaching staff have finally found their groove in November.

Michigan has won three-straight games in convincing fashion since losing to Penn State on Oct. 19, which includes a dominant win over Notre Dame.

Last Saturday, the Wolverines dismantled the Michigan State Spartans in their annual rivalry game. Shea Patterson had an incredible performance, throwing for 384 yards and four touchdowns.

When asked about the team’s improved play, Harbaugh dropped the quote of the year.

“You can’t plant potatoes one day and expect to eat potato salad the next day,” Harbaugh said.
