How many episodes of Gold Digger are there BBC and are they all on iPlayer? Millions hooked on drama

Gold Digger is the latest much buzzed about drama on BBC1 with millions of viewers hooked on it

19 Nov 2019, 15:28 by Simon Duke

It is the latest BBC drama to get everyone tuning in and everyone talking and Gold Digger continues on BBC1 on Tuesday night.

Following hot on the heels of The Capture, which had millions gripped in early autumn, Gold Digger debuted on BBC1 on Tuesday, November 12 and continues tonight, Tuesday, November 19.

Gold Digger is centered around Julia, a wealthy woman who falls in love with mysterious younger man Benjamin. Her children worry he’s a gold digger.

At the heart of the story is a fractured family trying to recover from a violent past.

Episode 1 saw Julia and Benjamin meet by chance and start dating. But when Julia introduced him to her grown-up children, they questioned if Benjamin's intentions are financially motivated.

In episode 2 we'll see Julia realise she’s in love with Benjamin. Patrick and Della, who are suspicious of Benjamin, follow him and catch him in a compromising situation. Is he really who he seems?

Tune in to BBC1 at 9pm tonight to find out. However, you don't actually have to wait until then!

How many episodes of Gold Digger are there and are they all on iPlayer?

There are six episodes of Gold Digger in total, airing on consecutive Tuesday nights at 9pm.

They are called Her Boy, Her Daughter, Her Son, Her Husband, Her Baby and Her Love.

You don't actually have to wait until Tuesday nights to watch them all. Many viewers have binge watched them already and you can do the same as they are all available on BBC iPlayer right now.
