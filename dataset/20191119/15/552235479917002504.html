Countdown to Christmas in East Kilbride has begun with weekend of festive fun

A weekend of celebrations saw the Village sparkle with festive spirit while EK, East Kilbride launched their season of goodwill with a visit from the big man himself.

19 Nov 2019, 15:46 by Fraser N Wilson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/fraser-n-wilson/

The countdown to Christmas has officially started in East Kilbride after a weekend of celebrations.

EK, East Kilbride launched their festivities on Saturday with a very special visit from the main man.

 

Father Christmas hosted one of a number of Santa’s Grotto days, with Rudolph and the magical elves helping to create a winter wonderland.

Then, on Sunday, the Village was awash with fun, activity, well-wishes and anticipation as the East Kilbride lights were turned on.

 

And it was down to depute provost, Collette Stevenson to flick the switch, illuminating the skyline with colour.

Clyde 1’s Gina McKie hosted the festivities, with ocal dance and theatre groups regaling with song and dance.

Band ‘Ernest’ rocked the crowds with some classic festive hits, and Olaf and Kristoff from Frozen, along with some Minions helped spread festive cheer.
