Jacqueline Jossa's changing TV looks from her EastEnders days to I'm A Celebrity

The mother-of-two has overhauled her look as she has lost one stone and a half since she gave birth to her second child

19 Nov 2019, 15:55 by Irishmirror.ie

Jacqueline Jossa is kitted out in her jungle gear while she battles the bugs in the I'm A Celeb camp.

This is the former soap star's first stint on reality TV, although she is married to Dan Osborne of The Only Way Is Essex fame.

Jacqueline, 27, made her name in the spotlight on EastEnders' Albert Square when she looked youthful at the age of 18.

The stunning actress walked in the heels of character Lauren Branning for seven years.

Former soap star Jacqueline left behind her post on EastEnders in 2017 and two years later she now has a starring role on I'm A Celeb.

In recent months, the mother-of-two has overhauled her look as she admitted she was the biggest she ever was before she had her two daughters.

Jacqueline looks astonishing following her latest makeover which she heavily documented on social media with swimsuit snaps before making her jungle appearance.

The reality star transformed her figure after she gave birth to her second daughter Mia, now one, last year.

I'm A Celeb's Jacqueline shed one stone and a half as she credited a shake meal replacement plan for the incredible weight loss.

She previously told fans on social media: "Before I had Mia in between Ella and Mia I was the biggest I have ever been.

"Then I got pregnant and so I decided that I wanted to change as soon as I had Mia I thought I'm not going to let myself go. I thought I really want to get back to a comfortable weight where I feel good where I feel confident.

"And I'm still on my journey but I'm already feeling so much better I've lost a stone and a half.

"I do feel comfortable and I maybe want to lose a little bit more and I want to tone up as well. But I just feel so much better."

Husband Dan has been left in charge of their two daughters Ella, four, and Mia.
