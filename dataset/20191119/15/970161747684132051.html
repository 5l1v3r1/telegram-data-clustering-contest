Roman Kemp pokes fun at famous dad Martin Kemp over musical talent (or lack of)

19 Nov 2019, 15:43

I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here star Roman Kemp couldn’t resist a cheeky dig at his famous dad.

The radio DJ is the son of actor Martin Kemp, who is a member of the band Spandau Ballet which shot to fame in the 80s.

The 26-year-old revealed in the latest episode of I’m A Celebrity that Martin’s brother Gary was apparently ordered by their mum to include him in the group.

He joked that the 58-year-old ‘couldn’t even sing or play an instrument’ when Spandau Ballet was formed.

Roman joked: ‘He still can’t. He still hasn’t realised his mic wasn’t on for Live Aid!’

The campmate also recalled his dad’s health battle, revealed he had to ‘relearn’ the New Romantic band’s hits such as Gold and True.

‘When they went back on tour, post brain tumour and stuff like that, he forgot everything. So he forgot how to play and he forgot all the songs,’ he explained.

‘He knew how to play but he didn’t know any of the songs. So I had to learn them and then teach him. I learnt all the Spandau songs and then retaught them to him.’

Roman added that the difficult time in Martin’s life made them ‘super close’.

‘How can my dad not be my hero through all of that. We’re a proper unit,’ he added.

His fellow camper Kate Garraway, a Good Morning Britain presenter, confessed that she had a crush on Martin’s dad back in the day.

She told him: ‘He was my absolutely favourite.’

Roman went on: ‘My dad is my best mate. 100%. I speak to him every day.’

Last night’s episode saw Kate and Caitlyn Jenner take on the bushtucker trial Bugged Off, which saw them trapped in a suspended box that was gradually filled with insects and rats.

Kate ended up in the main camp after winning two stars while Caitlyn was sent back to Snake Rock.

I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here! continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
