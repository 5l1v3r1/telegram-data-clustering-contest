Shearer picks four Liverpool stars in his Team of the Season so far

19 Nov 2019, 15:41

Alan Shearer has included four Liverpool players in his Premier League team of the season so far, while only Kevin De Bruyne makes the cut from Manchester City’s squad.

After 12 games played, Jurgen Klopp’s side remain unbeaten and are eight points clear at the top of the Premier League.

City, meanwhile, find themselves nine points behind their title rivals following their 3-1 defeat at Anfield before the international break.

And as a result, Shearer believes only De Bruyne has been good enough to take a place in his Premier League team of the season so far.

Sheffield United currently sit fifth in the Premier League after an excellent start and two of their players are included – Dean Henderson and centre-back John Egan.

They are joined in defence by Liverpool’s star full-backs Trent Alexander-Arnold and Andy Robertson, while Leicester City’s Caglar Soyuncu makes up the back line.

In midfield, Liverpool’s midfield anchor Fabinho sits behind Sadio Mane, James Maddison and De Bruyne.

And in attack, Shearer has gone for a two-man front line as Tammy Abraham partners Jamie Vardy.
