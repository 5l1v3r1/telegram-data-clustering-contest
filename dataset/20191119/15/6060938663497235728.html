Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, hubby Abhishek Bachchan and their daughter Aaradhya pose for a perfect family photo!

19 Nov 2019, 15:43 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan got married in the year 2007 and since then the duo has been giving us major couple goals. Ash and Abhi love to share their pictures on respective social media accounts to keep their fans updated.

And today, Aishwarya took to her Instagram and shared a stunning picture of her with Abhishek and their daughter Aaradhya. In the photo, the 'Manmarziyaan' actor can be seen wearing a black sherwani while the actress looked beautiful in a red ensemble. Aaradhya was all-cute in a golden dress.

Fans poured in love in the comment section and called it a "perfect family picture". The caption of the post read with heart and star emojis.
