Alltimers Launches Heavily-Branded Holiday 2019 Collection

Featuring puffer jackets, hoodies, snapbacks & more.

19 Nov 2019, 21:01 by Gabrielle Leung

After traveling to rural Pennsylvania for its Fall 2019 collection, Alltimers returns with a Holiday 2019 lookbook featuring riders Etienne Gagne, Ben Blundell and Stafhon Boca. The capsule collection offers outerwear, brand staples and accessories for the colder months ahead.

Hoodies sport psychedelic logos, while heavily-branded T-shirts and crewnecks boast playful graphics. A highlight of the capsule includes a Farley nylon puffer jacket in two colorways, featuring a removable hood, contrasting lining and a snap front. Rounding out the collection are newly designed beanies, black and blue snapbacks with embroidered floral designs and a porcelain soap or lotion dispenser. Alltimers also includes a new skate deck covered with zoomed-in images of rainforest frogs.
