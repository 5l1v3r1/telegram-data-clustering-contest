I'm A Celebrity's Ant and Dec make dig at Prime Minister after clash on TV

19 Nov 2019, 21:27

Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly couldn’t help but make a dig towards Prime Minister Boris Johnson on tonight’s I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here.

The reality show followed a live debate held between the Prime Minister and Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn on ITV.

However the two men, who are most likely to lead the country after the December 12 vote clashed on Brexit, the NHS, the royal family, and the union – but shook hands at one stage.

Ant and Dec couldn’t help but make light of the situation by throwing some jokes in there about it as they introduced I’m A Celeb.

‘Two camps are about to come one, but first there’s work to be done,’ Ant began.

‘Which means five of them are about to face their deepest fears,’ Dec continued.

Ant joked: ‘Welcome to Australia and another TV show where two men like the sound of their own voices.’

‘Evening Prime Minister…for now,’ Dec laughed.

Ant then interrupted as he listened into his ear piece, he continued: ‘Hang on, hang on, I am just hearing that even this show must remain politically impartial.’

Dec responded: ‘You’re right, good evening Prime Minister for now, and good evening bloke who wants to be Prime Minister, although why anyone who would want that job is beyond me.’

The jokers continued as Ant said: ‘Caitlin proved there’s nothing worse than someone going on and on and on and not listening to a word you say.’

Dec interjected: ‘Again evening Prime Minister and bloke who wants to be Prime Minister.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! airs weekdays at 9pm on ITV.
