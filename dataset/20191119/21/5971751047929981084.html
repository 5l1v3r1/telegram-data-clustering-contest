Nokia secured a 50th 5G deal and over 120 private wireless network customers

19 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Marin

Nokia announced that it secured a 50th 5G deal thanks to the Spark New Zealand. This operator chose Nokia to deliver radio access network technology for the rollout of its 5G network. Nokia will be deploying its AirScale RAN solution at more than 200 sites so the Spark could offer new services to its customers.
