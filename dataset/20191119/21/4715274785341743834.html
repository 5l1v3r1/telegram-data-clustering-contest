Toilet day

19 Nov 2019, 21:53 by Norman Cruz

Department of Health and Manila Health Department celebrate World Toilet Day in Amadome Compound, Baseco, Port Area in Manila, with Senator Cynthia Villar (middle), Manila Vice Mayor Honey Lacuna (2nd from left) and officers from UNICEF. The aim of World Toilet Day this year is to practice hand washing properly to avoid polio which infects through fecal or oral transmission.
