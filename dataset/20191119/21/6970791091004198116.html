Joe Budden Gave Tory Lanez One Of His Top 5 All-Time Best Interviews: “Hands Down”

19 Nov 2019, 21:47 by Rosario Harper

New Jersey rapper Joe Budden is really looking like the new Stephen A. Smith of the hip-hop journalism world. R&B singer Tory Lanez has credited the veteran rap artist for putting together one of his most memorable interviews – ever.

On Tuesday, Tory jumped on Instagram with major salutations to Budden following the release of their “Pull Up” Q&A.
