Woman hit by car on the Gold Coast

19 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Monique StClair

A woman’s recovering from serious injuries in hospital this morning after she was hit by a car on the Gold Coast last night.

Emergency services rushed to Pacific Pines just after 9.30pm last night, following reports a car had hit a person.

They briefly treated a woman at the scene for an ankle injury, before rushing her to hospital for further treatment.

There were critical care paramedics on board.

She’s believed to be in a stable condition at the Gold Coast University Hospital.
