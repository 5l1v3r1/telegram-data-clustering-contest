Man, 60s tragically dies following collision between jeep and van in Co Sligo

The scene of the crash is preserved for examination by Garda forensic collision investigations

19 Nov 2019, 21:32 by Cormac Byrne

A man has tragically died following a two-vehicle collision in Co Sligo.

The incident occurred at around 7pm on Tuesday night when a jeep collided with a fan on the R284 at Jinks Cross, near Carraroe, Co Sligo.

The driver of the jeep, a man in his 60s was pronounced dead at the scene.

The driver of the van was taken to Sligo University Hospital with non-life threatening injuries.

The scene of the crash is preserved for examination by Garda forensic collision investigations.

The road is currently closed and local diversions are in place.
