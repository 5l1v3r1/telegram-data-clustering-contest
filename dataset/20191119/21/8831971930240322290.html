10 Tights To Complete Your Winter Look

19 Nov 2019, 21:01 by Constance Kalpakis

Although it’s literally 20 degrees out, mini skirts and dresses are an essential part of your winter wardrobe. Paired with your favorite pair of boots or heels makes a perfectly stylish look for the holiday season, but it’s still winter nonetheless. It’s freezing. Tights are a girl’s secret weapon. They give your outfit a little extra something and prevent you from completely freezing your ass off. Here are some fun looks to try out this winter!

Polka Dots

Polka dots are a subtle pattern that adds extra style to any basic and simple outfit you may throw on.

Plaid

Plaid makes a louder statement, but perfect for getting festive. Pair them with loafers as pictured above, or a pair of heels and you’ve created the perfect holiday look.

Leopard Print

Animal print is a huge trend this season. Tone down this eccentric style with some boots and solid colors, leaving room for these leopard print tights to be the focus of the look.

Glitter

Adding these shiny glitter tights to your outfit is a perfect way to make it fancier without making it over-the-top. Great for a holiday parties or any dressy event you have coming up, and perfect with a pair of open toed heels!

Sheer & Shiny

Use a pair of sheer tights that match your skin tone with a little extra shine to add some sparkle and elegance to your look! Great for sequined looks and adds the extra warmth you need.

Floral Net

These floral net tights are so cute. They add extra style to basic fishnet tights, and perfect to make subtle statement to any black and basic look.

Crystal Embellished Tights

These crystal embellished tights are another genius way to add some sparkle and fun to a fancy occasion. Be as dramatic or simple as you want with a dress and heels.

Opaque Colored Tights

Some colored tights make dressing up fun and chromatic. Style a pair that coordinates with your look to make yourself the most fashionably organized person in the room.

Prints

Be bold with an exotic printed pair of tights like these. Match it with some solid colored skirts and sweaters, and you instantly have a unique outfit within seconds.

Stripes

Vertical striped create the illusion of longer legs. With these pair you can easily fool everyone with this “leggy look” with a mini skirt and heels to work, parties, or any other holiday events coming up.
