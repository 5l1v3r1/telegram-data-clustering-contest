Miami Dolphins cut RB Mark Walton after he was charged with hitting pregnant girlfriend

19 Nov 2019, 21:46 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

Miami Dolphins running back Mark Walton was cut by the team Tuesday after he was accused of punching a woman who is five weeks pregnant with his child, team officials said.

It was Walton's fourth arrest this year. The 22-year-old was suspended by the team before his latest arrest, WPLG reported.
