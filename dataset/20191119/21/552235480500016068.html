Leigh Griffiths makes Celtic goalscoring return as he nets reserve double

The Hoops star is back among the goals ahead of a huge month for his club.

19 Nov 2019, 21:31 by Mark McDougall, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/mark-mcdougall/

Celtic striker Leigh Griffiths was back among the goals as he grabbed a double for the club's reserve team.

The Hoops youngsters took on Stenhousemuir in a friendly at Lennoxtown with Griffiths handed a striking role.

He hasn't played for Celtic's first team since a 12 minute cameo against Hearts in August.

But he made an instant impact for the second string by scoring after 11 minutes. The 29-year-old was picked out by David McKay and heÂ slipped the ball into the back of the net.

Griffiths added a second three minutes later and this time it was former Rangers youngster Liam Burt who provided the assist.

Burt moved across the divide in the summer and he played a stunning through ball to Griffiths who smashed it past the keeper.

Burt then grabbed a double of his own as Celtic cruised into a dominant lead before half time and he got his hat-trick 10 minutes into the second half. Cameron Harper added a sixth.

Also involved for the Hoops was Ireland U21 striker Jonathan Afolabi, who will be hoping to make his first team debut this season.

A return to form for Griffiths would be good news for Neil Lennon as he heads into some tough fixtures.

With 10 games over December before the winter break, the Hoops had looked likely to be relying on Odsonne Edouard.
