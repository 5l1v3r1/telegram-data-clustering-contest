I'm A Celebrity's Nadine Coyle reveals why she doesn't speak to the rest of Girls Aloud anymore

On Tuesday night's I'm A Celebrity, Nadine Coyle opened up about her relationship with her Girls Aloud bandmates

19 Nov 2019, 21:15 by Simon Duke

It's a subject that has been the topic of substantial speculation over the years and Nadine Coyle has revealed why she no longer speaks to her Girls Aloud bandmates.

Nadine is one of the famous faces in the jungle for the new series of I'm A Celebrity but is of course best known for being part of the group who shot to fame on Popstars: The Rivals.

They soared straight to the Christmas number 1 spot with debut single Sound of The Underground and hits including Love Machine, Jump, Call The Shot and The Promise swiftly followed before the girls decided to call it a day after their 2009 tour.

They came back with greatest hits album and tour Ten in 2012/3, before announcing they were calling time on the band again.

Cheryl has openly hit out at Nadine for suggesting she and bandmates Kimberley, Nicola and Sarah were behind the split and on Tuesday night's I'm A Celebrity, the Irish singer was put on the spot by Kate Garraway about why she doesn't speak to them anymore.

Nadine explained: “Everyone gets on with their lives, I was friends with them all, it was all nice and lovely.  But I was always in the gay bars with all the guys, I’d be out partying, me and 10 guys, I was having a laugh, I was 18-19 years old.”
