The science behind numerology works in the same manner as the science behind astrology. The astrology deals with understanding and predicting the personality and events through the signs, whereas numerology deals with understanding the personality and events through the numbers. Numerology helps one to understand how the person's day is likely to go with respect to their ruling number. Here is the daily numerology prediction for individuals with number 9.

How to calculate your number?

The ruling number of a person is based on their birth date. The addition of the digits of your birth date will be your ruling number. For example, if your birthday is on October 18, your ruling number will be 1 + 8 = 9. If your birthday falls on December 27, your ruling number will be 2 + 7 = 9.  A person with a one-digit birth date like January 9 will have their ruling number as 9.
