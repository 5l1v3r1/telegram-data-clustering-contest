I'm A Celebrity's Adele Roberts accuses Roman Kemp of 'prejudice' in heated row

The first argument of this year's I'm A Celebrity kicks off in dramatic style when Adele Roberts bickers with Roman Kemp over a very serious topic - Jane McDonald

19 Nov 2019, 21:08 by Irishmirror.ie

Adele Roberts and Roman Kemp were embroiled in the first heated row of this year's I'm A Celebrity.

The first argument of the series kick off in dramatic style when Adele started bickering with Roman over a very serious topic.

Brexit ? The upcoming election? Who will win the Premier League? No...they actually got into a vicious row over JANE MCDONALD.

An initial squabble over the cruise ship queen turned very sour when furious Adele accuses Roman of "prejudice" when he offended Jane.

Stunned Roman immediately apologised, but the tension remained as the pair get into a confrontation over a photo of the former Loose Women star.

The clash started when Roman admitted he was puzzled why Adele chose a picture of Jane McDonald as her luxury item.

When asked if she knows any of her songs, Adele said: “I know her off Loose Women, I don’t know her actual songs but I know her from the cruising show when she does covers.”

Giggling Roman caused offence when he claimed: "No one knows what she does."

Kate insisted Jane was a "legend", but confused Roman replied: "She’s a legend is she? Name a song?"

The Good Morning Britain presenter failed to name a song and Myles and Roman continued to laugh.

This angered Adele, who explained: "She’ll cruise around the world, for example she’d go to Iceland and then sing a Bjork or would go to the USA and sing Proud Mary."

When Roman repeated that it was "random", Adele asked: "Why though? I don’t understand."

"But if I had brought in a picture of Ru-Paul would you have the same reaction?"

Roman continued to claim it was "random", before being accused of "prejudice" by the former Big Brother contestant.

She strongly insisted: "That’s prejudice, I’m allowed to like what I want."

Realising he had upset Adele, Martin Kemp's apologetic son said: "I wasn’t saying that at all, I’m sorry, I didn’t mean to."

But the bickering did not stop there as Roman claimed he found the situation "funny".

Adele sarcastically asked: "I’m not allowed to like what I want?"

The DJ got Kate's backing and Roman apologised once again for winding Adele up.

She said: "It’s fine, you are just going on about it so much…"

With the tense atmosphere unsettling the rest of the campmates - the pair agreed to put their differences aside and hugged.

Kate awkwardly joked: "It’s just fun around camp."

Is this the start of a major rivalry in camp?
