Dele Alli speaks out on Tottenham's decision to sack Mauricio Pochettino

19 Nov 2019, 22:39

Tottenham star Dele Alli says he’s ‘grateful’ to Mauricio Pochettino for the impact the Argentine has had on his career.

The Argentine was sacked on Tuesday after Daniel Levy, Spurs’ chairman, blamed the club’s ‘extremely disappointing’ for the decision.

Pochettino spent five-and-a-half years in north London and Spurs qualified for the Champions League four times during that period.

His reign will be remembered for the side’s title push in 2015, as well as their run to the Champions League final in 2019.

But his legacy of work with young players was also a strong factor and Alli, one of the main winners of Pochettino’s era, has thanked his now former boss.
