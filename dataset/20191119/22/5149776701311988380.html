'Never given a chance' - Nottingham Forest fans' verdict on whether to bring midfielder back

Nottingham Forest latest | Liam Bridcutt is on loan at Bolton Wanderers until January - but could he be an option in the Forest midfielder after that? Reds fans have their say

19 Nov 2019, 22:13 by Josh Murray

Liam Bridcutt helped Bolton Wanderers to get their League One points tally into positive numbers at the weekend.

The Nottingham Forest midfielder is on loan with Bolton until January, and he returned from three matches out injured to feature in Saturday's 1-0 victory over MK Dons, which moved Keith Hill's side on to one point.

They began the 2019-20 campaign with a 12-point deduction for going into administration.

Since joining the Trotters on September 2, Bridcutt has made six appearances, including draws against Oxford United, Sunderland and Blackpool.

It remains to be seen whether Sabri Lamouchi chooses to bring the 30-year-old back to the City Ground in the new year to add to his extensive midfield options - or whether the deal could be extended.

Bridcutt has scored one goal in 31 appearances for Forest since signing in the summer of 2017.

Only one of his 28 Championship appearances came last season.

We took to our Nottingham Forest News Facebook page to ask Reds supporters: "would you bring him back in January?"
