Caitlyn Jenner risks Kim Kardashian's wrath by giving away secret from her youth

I'm A Celebrity star Caitlyn Jenner discussed step daughter Kim Kardashian's first business and how Kanye West treated her during an insightful chat with Adele Roberts

19 Nov 2019, 22:45 by Irishmirror.ie

Caitlyn Jenner has risked the wrath of the Kardashian clan by revealing some of their secrets on I'm A Celebrity.

The reality TV star has started to open up on the famous family during an insightful chat with Adele Roberts.

And some viewers joked that Kris Jenner, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West would be watching intently at home.

Caitlyn discussed step daughter Kim's first business, revealing she never went to college, and how Kanye treated her.

She told Adele: "Kimberly never went to college. The minute she left high school she started businesses. She had her first business out of the closet."

"Kimberly would go into the ladies' closets. Some of these are like 2,000 square feet - they're huge. Beverly Hills ladies.

"They figure out what they want to get rid of and Kimberley has an Ebay sight.. Kimberly sells them for them and gets 30% commission."

Adele then asked whether Caitlyn helped Kanye when he moved from music to fashion.

Cailtyn said: "I like Kanye. Kanye was very, very good to me."

The former Keeping Up With The Kardashians star then took time in the Bush Telegraph to reflect on the chat.

Caitlyn said: "I like Adele. She is quietly a very smart person."

I'm A Celebrity viewers can't wait for Caitlyn to spill more tea on the Kardashian clan.

Taking to Twitter , one fan said: "Kris getting mad because caitlyn is revealing all kim’s secrets"

"@kanyewest and @KimKardashian probably sat down right now watching," added another.

A third said: "Caitlyn is starting to spill some T I beg someone to bring up Kim’s sex tape!"

This isn't the first time that Caitlyn has mentioned her family in the jungle.

Emotional Caitlyn admitted she was missing her children and grandchildren, who are part of the famous Kardashian and Jenner clans, during an early morning cry.

In the Bush Telegraph, she said: "Being the first night in the Jungle my head was spinning. I’m kinda trying to figure out if I’m doing the right thing.

"I’m 70 years old now and is being in here the right way to use my time. Should I be home with my kids, playing with the grandchildren.

"I’ve never quit at anything I’ve ever done and I’m thinking maybe it’s time I learned how to do that."
