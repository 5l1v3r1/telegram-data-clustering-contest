Bough signs NLI to Niagara

19 Nov 2019, 22:36 by Jacob Seus

TOWN OF CHENANGO (WBNG) – On Tuesday, Emma Bough signed her national letter of intent to continue her soccer career at Niagara.

Bough played a key role on Chenango Forks state title run in 2018 and helped lead the team to a Section IV title this past season. She joins a Purple Eagles team that finished with a 9-8-2 record in 2019.

“Just like getting better and experiencing, pushing myself and playing at the next level is going to be a challenge but I’m excited for it,” said Chenango Forks senior Emma Bough.
