I'm A Celebrity's Nadine Coyle reveals why she's not friends with Girls Aloud

19 Nov 2019, 22:01

I’m A Celebrity star Nadine Coyle has revealed why she’s not friends with her former Girls Aloud bandmates anymore.

On tonight’s I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here, the singer admitted to Kate Garraway that being in the band ‘was fun’ but ‘lots of hard work’.

Kate asked Nadine why she wasn’t friends with the other members of Girls Aloud or saw them.

Nadine responded: ‘Everyone gets on with their lives, I was friends with them all, it was all nice and lovely.

‘But I was always in the gay bars with all the guys, I’d be out partying, me and ten guys, I was having a laugh, I was 18-19 years old.’

Girls Aloud – made up of Cheryl, Sarah Harding, Nadine Coyle, Nicola Roberts and Kimberly Walsh – were created through the ITV talent show Popstars: The Rivals in 2002.

After winning the show, the band became one of Britain’s biggest pop bands, earning 20 consecutive top 10 singles in the United Kingdom, including four number ones.
