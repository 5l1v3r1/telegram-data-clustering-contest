I'm A Celebrity host Emily Atack stuns fans with 'gorgeous' dress

Extra Camp fans commented in their hundreds on her outfit

19 Nov 2019, 22:23 by Sophie McCoid

Emily Atack stunned fans tonight as she wore a "gorgeous" green and white dress for tonight's Extra Camp.

Emily is hosting the spin off show to I'm A Celebrity , after being a finalist in last year's show.

And keen to impress on her first few days presenting, Emily pulled out all the stops with her outfit.

Wearing a midi dress with a leg split, Emily posted the outfit to her Instagram account.

She captioned the post: "Ready to Extra Camp the sh*t out of it!! dress  @reformation  and boots  @drmartensofficial  and we are LIVE in a mo on  @itv2 "
