Colin James coming to these Okanagan cities in 2020

19 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Ben Bulmer

Multi-award winning blues musician Colin James is heading to the Okanagan next spring and taking to the stage in both Vernon and Penticton.

Tickets go on sale Nov. 22 for Colin James Trio Tour 2020 for performances at Penticton's Cleland Theatre March 24 and the Vernon Performing Arts Centre March 26, 2020.

The Canadian artist got his first record deal in 1988 and since then has released 19 albums and been nominated and won dozens of music awards, including being nominated for 17 Juno Awards - winning six of them.

Over the course of his career, James has performed alongside Stevie Ray Vaughn, Carlos Santana, Lenny Kravitz as well as countless others and has had a string of commercial hits.
