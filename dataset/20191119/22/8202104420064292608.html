'Enough is enough': Province boosts fines for distracted drivers in Saskatchewan

19 Nov 2019, 22:25 by Dave Deibert, Saskatoon StarPhoenix

Currently, the cost of a first offence is $280 plus four demerit points. As of Feb. 1, 2020, a first-offence ticket will more than double, to $580; the four demerits will remain the same.
