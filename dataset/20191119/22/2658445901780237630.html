Lecturer, student die in accident - The Nation Newspaper

19 Nov 2019, 22:50 by https://www.facebook.com/alao.h.joshua

Adekunle Jimoh, Ilorin

A lecturer and a post graduate student of the Kwara State University (KWASU), Ilorin have reportedly died in a ghastly accident.

The crash, which involved one of the university’s buses carrying 15 workers and students, was said to have occurred on Monday.

Seven of the passengers were injured, while six escaped. The accident occurred on the Minna-Lambata road.

Governor AbdulRahman AbdulRazaq has commiserated with the bereaved families.
