Pregnant Danielle Armstrong announces gender of her first baby with childhood sweetheart

The former TOWIE star, 31, shared the news she's expecting a baby girl as she hosted a gender reveal party with her boyfriend

19 Nov 2019, 22:56 by Irishmirror.ie

Pregnant Danielle Armstrong has announced the gender of her first child.

The former TOWIE star is expecting a little girl with her childhood sweetheart.

Danielle, 31, shared the exciting news as she hosted a lavish gender reveal party alongside her boyfriend, whose name she has never publicly revealed.

The reality star, who was joined at the party by her famous pal and former co-star Ferne McCann, posed up with a giant pink balloon with the words "it's a girl" printed on the front.

Danielle announced earlier this month that she's expecting her first baby.

She shared the news on Instagram by sharing a series of loved-up pictures of her and her beau together, including one of her holding up an ultrasound scan.

She wrote alongside them: "The Best Part Of Us Will Be You."

The picture also revealed the baby is due in May 2020.

While Danielle hasn't publicly revealed the identity of her boyfriend, she said back in September that she had found love with her "best friend of 22 years".

Telling her Twitter followers about the change in her romantic life, she said: "Life has a funny way of working out just when I decided to give up on love my friend of 22 years swept me off my feet.

"You don’t find love it finds you, it’s got a little a to do with destiny & fate, and what’s written in the stars #mybestfriend #love #happiness."

Danielle isn't the only former TOWIE star expecting a baby next year.

Lydia Bright and Lucy Mecklenburgh also have little ones on the way.

Danielle was previously in a long-term relationship with Daniel Spiller but ended things as they'd become "more like friends".

In the aftermath of the split, she jetted off on a girls' holiday with pal Ferne and was seen in Marbella cosying up to a mystery man who later confirmed was her partner.
