TAT'S INK-REDIBLE

Celtic superfan shares incredible tattoo sleeve made up of club legends

19 Nov 2019, 22:02 by Connor Boylan

A CELTIC superfan living Down Under has shared his incredible tattoo sleeve made up of club legends.

Mark Dunn’s Hoops-themed ink has been a hit with Celts fans on social media.

It includes portraits of Parkhead idols like Jimmy McGrory, Danny McGrain and Paul McStay.

And it now covers most of his left arm following the two latest additions to the collection – Billy McNeill and Willie Maley.

The iconic images of the Hoops heroes were designed and tattooed by talented artist Damien Wickham at Ink Attack in Sunshine Coas, Queensland, Australia.
