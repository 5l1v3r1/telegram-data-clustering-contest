Return to Earth X with Marvels X Themed Variant Cover Gallery

19 Nov 2019, 22:11 by Jodi Odgers

In 1999, Earth XÂ gave readers a glimpse into an alternate future, where everyone on the planet has superpowers. In 2020, original writers Alex Ross and Jim Kreuger looks to return to this universe in Marvels X. In tandem with the series, a host of Marvel's flagship series will receive variant covers showing strange alternate version of the heroes, created by some of Marvel's leading artists.

The list of currently-announced variant covers is as follows:

Captain Marvel #14 Marvels X Variant by Alex Garner

Deadpool #3 Marvels X Variant by David Yardin, with colors by Morry Hollowell

Dr. Strange #2 Marvels X Variant by Oliver Vatine

Guardians of the Galaxy #1 Marvels X Variant by Skan

Iron Man 2020 #1 Marvels X Variant by Christian Ward

Spider-Man #5 Marvels X Variant by Javier Rodriguez

Thor #1 Marvels X Variant by Ryan Brown

Venom #22 Marvels X Variant by John Tyler Christopher

X-Force #5 Marvels X Variant by Greg Land, with inks by Jay Leisten and colors by Frank D'Armata

From a portly Spider-Man, to a female Dr. Strange, to a medieval knighted Deadpool, the Marvels X variant covers present a number of intriguing 'What if?' scenarios. With both the variant covers and Marvels X itself releasing in January, it won't be too long before we can once again venture to Earth X.

Marvels X will be available in January 2020.
