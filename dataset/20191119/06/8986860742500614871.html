‘Di Asa’ winner kicked off Emirates flight to Dubai for being too huge

19 Nov 2019, 06:59 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

2019 winner of Atinka TV’s ‘Di Asa’ known as PM according to reports was kicked out on an Emirate Airline flight en route Dubai for her huge size.

PM, and two runner ups, Maafia and Gaza as part of their award package is to embark on a sponsored trip to Dubai.

But, unfortunately for PM, she was disallowed to be onboard all because her body size was bigger than a single seat in Emirates Airlines’ flight.

She was the ultimate winner of the reality show but her dream of traveling abroad for the first time was quashed by Emirates Airlines.
