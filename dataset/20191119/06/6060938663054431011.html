Manish Malhotra shares a picture of his late father and fans says, “stay strong”

19 Nov 2019, 06:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Manish Malhotra lost his father, Suraj Malhotra on Monday morning. He had been unwell for a while and passed away at the age of 91 at their Bandra residence. Manish performed the last rites of his father at a Santacruz crematorium and several Bollywood celebrities attended the funeral to pay their last respects. From Karan Johar to Shabana Azmi and Boney Kapoor, several stars were snapped at Manish’s residence in the morning. Manish’s nephew, filmmaker Punit Malhotra also attended the last rites. Today the designer took to social media to share a picture of his beloved father. Manish’s post was soon flooded with condolences and a few fans even commented, ‘stay strong’.

In the evening, several celebrities visited the bereaved designer’s residence. Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Karisma Kapoor, Malaika Arora, Gauri Khan, Jaya Bachchan, Shweta Bachchan Nanda, Farah Khan, Raveena Tandon and Shilpa Shetty among others were snapped by the paparazzi.

A prayer meet for late Suraj Malhotra will be held today at a suburban hotel.
