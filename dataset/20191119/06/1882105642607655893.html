Typhoon Ramon stuck in same spot ahead of landfall

Typhoon Ramon (Kalmaegi) is still 120 kilometers east of Calayan, Cagayan, as of early Tuesday afternoon, November 19. Signal Nos. 1, 2, and 3 remain raised.
