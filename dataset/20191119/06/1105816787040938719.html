Twitter can't get enough of Harry Styles in a ballerina outfit

19 Nov 2019, 06:10 by https://tribune.com.pk/author/8823/asfa-sultantribune-com-pk/, Tribune.com.pk

In a recent turn of events, former One Direction singer, Harry Styles, hosted the Saturday Night Live (SNL) while donning a pink tutu and looking utterly graceful. The show came days after he announced his new album Fine Line, which will be released on December 12, 2019, and is rumoured to be proof of his apparent bisexuality.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B4-A1vXHT6s/

Appearing on stage in New York, Harry delivered one of SNL’s iconic monologues, which is tradition for every host to perform. According to Cosmopolitan, he used the opportunity to throw some playful shade at his ex-One Direction bandmates, saying “I love those guys, they’re my brothers. Niall, Liam, Louis and.. Ringo,” possibly referring to the supposed feud with Zayn after he left the band in 2015.

Receiving huge laughs from the crowd, Harry continued to joke, “I’m just so glad Simon Cowell grew us in those test tubes.” He went on to act in sketches throughout the show, sometimes appearing to be an irresponsible airline pilot, followed by a chihuahua-turned-human and a gangster.

Keeping the guests entertained, Harry constantly switched from host to musical guest, after which he took to the stage to perform his new single Lights Up. Fans are speculating the song could be a coming-out anthem, as well as a previously-unheard album track, Watermelon Sugar.

One of Harry’s acting roles saw him dress up as a ballerina and fans obviously can’t contain themselves. There’s no denying that they have been loving his new content unconditionally and have taken to Twitter to voice their excitement over his SNL appearance and especially his legs.
