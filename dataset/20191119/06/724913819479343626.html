6 ways Dynamics 365 Business Central can take your business to the next level

19 Nov 2019, 06:10 by First Distribution

What could you do with better insights that track what’s actually happening in your business versus what’s planned? Growing businesses face complexities, service-level challenges and spiralling costs. The right data drawn from every aspect of your business can deliver the insights you need to make better decisions, cut costs and deliver exceptional customer service. Growth doesn’t need to be complex and daunting — it can be simple and streamlined.

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is a cloud based, all-in-one business management solution designed specifically for the growth and operational challenges small and medium-sized businesses face.

Here are six ways Dynamics 365 Business Central can help you scale:

1. Grow your accounting capabilities

Use Dynamics 365 Business Central Accountant Hub to manage accounting tasks and processes. One simple and easy to use dashboard integrates your data, allowing you to easily access your client information.

2. Manage your financials

Because Dynamics 365 Business Central gives you an end-to-end view of your organisation, you can easily refine financial forecasts, streamline your accounts and automatically reconcile your numbers to report on your financials.

3. Keep projects on time and under budget

Real-time data on all available resources allows you to manage budgets and monitor the progress of your projects. Track customer invoices against quotes and orders to make better decisions based on real-time insights on each project’s status, profitability and resource usage.

4. Improve customer service and sell smarter

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central gives you the tools, data and insights you need to keep track of all customer interactions. This gives you and your sales team the guidance required to up-sell, cross-sell and renew contracts throughout your sales cycle. With the right data, you can also deliver the service your customers expect to promote loyalty and generate repeat business.

5. Automate and secure your supply chain

Gain greater control over your working capital and cash flow by purchasing only what you need, when you need it. Maintain the right amount of inventory by using dynamically updated inventory levels with Dynamics 365 Business Central.

6. Optimise your operations

Streamline your warehousing operations to deliver products on time while simultaneously reducing costs. Use sales forecasts and expected stock-outs to automatically create purchase orders as you need them.
