Watch: Yuvraj Singh laughs uncontrollably as West Indies’ Chadwick Walton speaks Punjabi

Yuvraj Singh has scored 20 runs in two innings playing for Maratha Arabians in the ongoing T10 league

19 Nov 2019, 06:17 by Sports Desk

Yuvraj Singh is teaching his Maratha Arabians teammate Chadwick Walton to speak in Punjabi in the ongoing T10 League 2019. Yuvraj posted a video on his Instagram account while having a short conversation with Walton in the Punjabi. The former Indian cricketer could not control his laughter as the West Indies cricketer managed to copy him well.
