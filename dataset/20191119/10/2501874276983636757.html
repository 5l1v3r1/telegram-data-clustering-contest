Cheryl Diamond 5k Schlep

19 Nov 2019, 10:55

Event Date: Sunday, January 12, 2020 - 10:00

The first annual Cheryl Diamond Santa Barbara 5K Schlep will take place on January 12, 2020 at Leadbetter Beach in Santa Barbara at 10AM.

Partially benefiting the Breast Cancer Resource Center, this event will bring together hundreds of participants from the county, ranging in age from 5 - 90 years old, bound together in solidarity to raise the hopes of those battling breast, ovarian and prostate cancer.

Current sponsors include The Jewish Federation of Greater Santa Barbara, American Self Storage, Edhat, El Al Airlines, Congregation B’nai B'rith, Power Of Your Om, Blenders in the Grass, UCSB Hillel, California Property Group, BCRC, the ADL, Fitness 805, Sol Wave Water, Patient Power, Bare Republic and more.

To register, please visit www.5kschlep.org
