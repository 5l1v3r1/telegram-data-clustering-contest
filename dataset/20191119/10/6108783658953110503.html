Half-Life Alyx VR game to be detailed this week

19 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Steve Wright

Big news for Half-Life fans.

Half-Life Alyx is a VR game that will be detailed later in the week, Valve has just confirmed.

“We’re excited to unveil Half-Life: Alyx, our flagship VR game, this Thursday at 10am Pacific Time,” Valve just said in a tweet. “Can’t wait to share it with all of you!”

That unveiling corresponds to 5.00 am AEDT on Friday, 22 November.

We don’t have much else to report on Half-Life Alyx at this time, but rest assured we’re as excited as you… even if we’re talking about a VR game and not a full-fledged sequel.
