Dangerous paedophile goes on the run - do not approach if you see him

19 Nov 2019, 10:48

A convicted paedophile is being hunted by police after he went on the run following his release from jail.

Cops have urged members of the public not to approach Valerijs Gordijs, 46, who was released from HMP Hull yesterday.

An arrest warrant was issued hours after he was released from prison, where he was serving a sentence for sexual offences against children.

Humberside Police said officers have carried out inquiries since his release but could not locate Gordijs.

A spokesman for the force said: ‘Officers are currently making enquiries to locate Valerijs Gordijs following a warrant issued for his arrest.

‘Gordijs, 46, was released from HMP Hull on Monday, 18 November, where he was serving a sentence for sexual offences against children.

‘Officers have carried out various enquiries since the prison reported his release and a warrant has now been issued for his arrest to return him to prison.

‘If you believe you know where he is, please call police on 101. If you see him, please do not approach him. In an emergency, always call 999.’
