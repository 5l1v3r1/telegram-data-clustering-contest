Gisele Bündchen and six-year-old daughter look identical in throwback photo

19 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Judy Philander

Cape Town - The apple doesn’t fall far from the tree for supermodel Gisele Bündchen and her six-year-old mini-me.

On Monday, the 39-year-old posted a side-by-side photo to Instagram, comparing a snap of herself from her childhood to a recent one of her daughter, Vivian Lake, People reports.

"Do you guys think my baby girl and I look alike?" she asked in the caption.  

Gisele and Vivian’s appearances are nearly identical, with the model and her daughter even sharing the same hairstyle, Fox News reports.

Fans were quick to point out the resemblance between the two – even Gisele’s husband, American football star Tom Brady, couldn’t help but chip in, Daily Mail reports.

"Girlie girls," he commented, along with two red heart emojis.   

"Completely like Mama," someone else commented.
