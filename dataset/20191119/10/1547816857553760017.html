Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke: Vatsal Sheth Comes Onboard For This Shaheer Sheikh, Rhea Sharma Show

19 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Divya Ramnani

Vatsal Sheth joins the star cast of Rajan Shahi’s popular show, Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke, read on for more deets on his character.

Actor Vatsal Sheth, who became a household name with his performance in Tarzan: The Wonder Car, has also been a part of several television shows. He was last seen in Colors’ popular horror show called Kaun Hai? As for his next, Vatsal is all set to make his television comeback on TV and he will be next seen in Rajan Shahi's Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke. While the internet was floating with the rumours about the same, Vatsal Sheth recently took to his social media and put an official stamp on this exciting news.

The picture shared by Vatsal saw him posing alongside producer Rajan Shahi and, in his caption; Vatsal expressed his elation on joining the cast of Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke. He wrote, “New Beginnings and New Rishteys... with the best in the business @rajan.shahi.543 #yehrishteyhainpyaarke." Earlier in the day, the actor opened up on his character in an interview with Times of India. Vatsal revealed how it would be a change for him, as in Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke will see him in a positive character. The actor was quoted as saying, “With this show, I am looking forward to getting back to my chocolate-boy image, the one I had in 'Just Mohabbat' (1996-2000) and the film, 'Tarzan — The Wonder Car' (2004). It feels great and different. I agree that it’s difficult to break the set image on TV, and people will expect me to do something crazy, but they will be shocked to see me in a different avatar.”
