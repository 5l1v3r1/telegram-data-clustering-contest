Isolated rain over parts of Oman

19 Nov 2019, 10:27 by Times News Service

Muscat: There are chances of isolated rain associated with thunderstorms, fresh winds and hail over a number of governorates of the Sultanate, the Public Authority for Civil Aviation (PACA) announced.

PACA said in a statement: "The weather today is cloudy over the governorate of Musandam, Al Buraimi, Al Dhahirah, Al Batinah North and the Hajar Mountains, with chances of isolated rain associated with thunderstorms sometimes, accompanied by fresh winds and hail."

"The aerial picture shows the presence of low clouds and fog over parts of the governorate of Al Wusta, Al Dakhiliyah and Dhofar, as the clouds spread on the coasts of the Arabian Sea," PACA added.
