'Greta Thunberg' spotted in 120-year-old photo

19 Nov 2019, 10:16

Conspiracy theorists are branding teenage climate activist Greta Thunberg a time traveller after spotting her in a 120-year-old picture.

The picture in question shows three children working at a gold mine in the Yukon Territory in Canada and was taken around 1898. It was found in the picture archives of the Univeristy of Washington, which you can see for yourself here.

While it’s true that one of the children does bear a striking resemblance to Greta, we have to point out the teenage activist has not, in fact, discovered the art of time travel.

It’s pretty much just a spooky coincidence.

But the internet hasn’t wasted any time in pointing out the similarity.
