4 soldiers killed, 7 hurt in road mishap

19 Nov 2019, 10:13 by Zaida Delos Reyes

FOUR soldiers were killed while seven others were wounded when a wingvan collided with a tricycle and a pick-up vehicle yesterday morning in Paranas, Samar.

The fatalities were identified as Sgt.Crisostomo A.Gabuay, SSgt.Sherwin V.Antonio,SSgt. Romel G.Ombrog and 2Lt Charlie M.Cabanog.

Taken to Samar Provincial Hospital were Ltc. Edgar Allan B.Orbito, Pvt Mat Raymond Barrantes and Sgt.Nixon Abadiano, both occupants of Toyota Hilux-pickup with plate number TU0962; Warren Dabuet, Janica Obidos Baco, Jella Bacay Baccol and Sheryl Sayasa Maurillo, both occupants of the tricycle.

Police arrested the suspects identified as Elvin Brillantes, 44 , driver of wing van and his helper Alex Dubria.

Reports reaching Camp Crame said that the incident occurred at around 9 a.m. along the national highway at Bgy Apolonia, Paranas, Samar.

Initial investigation showed that Brillantes was driving the wing van truck with his helper along the national road from Hinabangan, Samar going to Catbalogan City, Samar when he hit the tricycle driven by Dabuet and occupied by students.

Brillantes maneuvered the wing van to the oppossite direction and figured in a head on collision with the pick-up occupied by the soldiers .

Because of the strong impact of the accident, the tricycle and the pick-up dragged the house of a certain Marita Bacacaycay Abarratigue.

Fortunately no one from the house was hurt but the victims were rushed to the hospital.
