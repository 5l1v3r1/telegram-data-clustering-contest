Balenciaga Updates $995 USD Triple-S Clear Sole With "Neon Yellow" Highlights

Add a splash of color to your step.

19 Nov 2019, 10:48 by Eric Brain

Balenciaga continues its run of Triple-S sneakers — and most notably its introduction of the clear sole iteration — with the release of a “Black/Neon Yellow” offering.

The Demna Gvasalia-designed chunky sneaker maintains its status as the frontrunner in the elevated footwear world, towering above the rest with its triple-stacked technical sole unit, trail-inspired looks and contemporary translucent sole unit. This time around, Balenciaga gives the clear TPU-injected air bubble a “Neon Yellow” tinge, contrasting the lightly washed-out black leather-free upper.

Branding is embroidered along the mid-panel in contrasting white stitching, and the toe box sports the sizing of each shoe that’s now synonymous with Balenciaga’s sneakers. Athletic rope laces in white further contrast the shoe, while a grippy black outsole ties everything together.

Take a look at the Balenciaga Triple-S Clear Sole “Black/Neon Yellow” in the gallery above, and pick up a pair for yourself from Balenciaga boutiques and online for $995 USD.
