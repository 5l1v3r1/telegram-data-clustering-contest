'One of us!' - Birmingham City fans give their verdict on Blues starlet Jude Bellingham

Latest Birmingham City news | The talented Blues midfielder made his first-team debut at 16 years of age earlier this season, and appears to have a bright future ahead

19 Nov 2019, 10:36 by Mark Wakefield

Birmingham City are beginning to showcase their abilities in the Sky Bet Championship this season.

One of the players who has got Blues fans excited is young midfielder Jude Bellingham .

The 16-year-old broke onto the scene at the start of the campaign, making his first-team debut in the 3-0 defeat to Portsmouth in the Carabao Cup first round tie in August.

He has since gone on to make 12 appearances in the Championship, eight of which have come as a starter, and looks to have a bright future ahead of him.

 

Bellingham netted his first professional goal in Blues’ 2-1 win over Stoke City in August, and followed that up with another strike in their following game against Charlton Athletic.

Earlier this week, we asked Blues fans to describe the talented midfielder in just three words.

Here is a selection of what they had to say.
