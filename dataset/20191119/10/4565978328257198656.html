Day of Sports and Physical Culture: For a Healthier Life (+Photo gallery)

19 Nov 2019, 10:01 by Reynaldo López Peña

The celebration of the Day of Sports and Physical Culture on November 19 was the pretext for Las Tunas inhabitants of various ages to meet around the Ángel López Stadium over the weekend.

Las Tunas, Cuba.- There were several presentations of sports, gymnastic tables of various ages, participation games and other recreational activities.

Physical Education teachers and organizations that work together to promote these activities were recognized.
