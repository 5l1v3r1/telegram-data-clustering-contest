LEI Vs CCC Dream11 : Two regressing sides try to wrench out of their impasse when they clash heads against each other today

For each of Leeward Islands and Combined Colleges and Campuses, today’s match couldn’t have carried bigger ramifications. Slumping in form, both the sides are on a downward and wayward spiral as things stand, making the need to snap out of their losing spree an imminent and requite one.

After beginning the Super50  Cup in exemplary fashion with three successive wins, Leeward Islands have gone onto lose two matches in successions to not only slip from challenging from first place in Group A but also allow their counterparts to snap at their heels. The side were made a mockery of in their foregone contest, trounced by 9 wickets by Barbados who needed a mere 31 overs to get past the 185 run target set out infront of them.

And as the side enter the fray of today’s clash, they’ll be delighted at the identity of their opponents. In Combined Colleges and Campuses, they are going at loggerheads against the bottom placed side, a team which has been left beleaguered with horrid displays to emerge with the singular win from five matches.

The side come into this forthcoming encounter in sordid form, having registered the three defeats in succession with the most recent being a low scoring clash against Barbados. Despite the best efforts of their bowlers to take the match down to its closing stages, the 207 runs doled out by their bats were never going to be enough to mount a challenge, seeing the side be put down by 4 wickets at the end of the day.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

A travesty of a track for the batters, this is a track where the batsmen are going to endure a tepid time. The exodus of batsmen is going to be extremely quick and high in the first part of the match with the conditions massively favouring the pacers who’ll get the ball to frisk around, leading the side winning the toss opting to field first in this low scorer.
