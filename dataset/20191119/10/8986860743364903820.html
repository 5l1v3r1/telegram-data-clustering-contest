DJ Switch meets with Chinese billionaire Jack Ma and actor Jet Li

19 Nov 2019, 10:59 by ‌ Qwame Benedict

Ghanaian female disc jockey DJ Switch has met with Chinese billionaire and the co-founder of Alibaba Jack Ma and popular Chinese and Hollywood actor Li Lian-jie popularly known as Jet Li.

The two participated in the maiden edition of the Africa Netpreneurship Summit, that took place at the Accra International Conference, on Saturday, November 16, 2019.

The 11-year-old DJ Switch was one of the performers at the event and she got the opportunity to meet the Chinese icons.

In a video sighted, DJ Switch was captured shaking hands with Jack Ma showed excitement.

“How are you? I saw your video this morning. Perfect. How did you do that? Can you to teach me how to do that. I wanna learn that,” Ma shouted.
