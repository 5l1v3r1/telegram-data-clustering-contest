'Hey Glasto!': McCartney to headline Glastonbury's 50th anniversary

19 Nov 2019, 10:36 by The Jakarta Post

Paul McCartney is to headline the 50th anniversary of Glastonbury in June next year, the world's largest greenfield festival, organisers announced on Monday.

"Hey Glasto - excited to be part of your Anniversary celebrations. See ya next year!" the 77-year-old former Beatle tweeted.
