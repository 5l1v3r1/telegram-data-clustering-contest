Justice League Stunt Team Backs Snyder Cut With Epic Fight Choreography

19 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Ian Cardona

As the two-year anniversary of Justice League's release arrived, plenty of actors lent their support to the #ReleaseTheSnyderCut movement on social media. Stars Ray Fisher, Gal Gadot, Ben Affleck and Joe Manganiello offered support for the release of Zack Snyder's original version of the film. Even Warner Bros.' former president recently backed the movement.

And now, the Justice League stunt team has also offered their support of the Zack Snyder cut by releasing an epic fight choreography video from the DC movie.

The video, which was originally shared on Instagram, came with the caption: "Ideas, concepts, pitches...some may make it some don’t. Right direction or the wrong direction, you have to start with a direction. With a fight team and creators of myself, Mike Lambert and Matt Rugetti here is little behind the scenes of a reality that could have existed."
