Man accused of attacking family for not supporting his 'America's Got Talent' dreams

19 Nov 2019, 10:30 by WPXI.com

WARREN, Ohio - A West Virginia man is accused of attacking his brother and other family members at a home in Ohio after they said they were not going to support him entering "America's Got Talent," according to WFMJ-TV.

Gregory Rarey, 25, became irate and left the home in Warren, Ohio, near Youngstown, before he came back and started screaming, authorities said. Police said Rarey then punched his brother in the face.
