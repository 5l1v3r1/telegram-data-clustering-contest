Cannabis plants discovered at property on Bathurst Road in Weston-super-Mare

Police enquiries into the incident are ongoing after the fire service found the cannabis

19 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Jasper King

Avon and Somerset Constabulary have discovered cannabis plants at a property on Bathurst Road in Weston-super-Mare.

This follows a house fire at the same property which Avon Fire and Rescue attended this morning.

A spokesperson for the police issued this statement to Bristol Live.

“We were called by the fire service at 5.40am to a fire at a property on Bathurst Road, Weston-super-Mare.

"The fire service advised us they discovered cannabis plants in the address.

"We’re currently supporting the fire service by providing a cordon around the property and assisting with traffic control while they deal with the fire. Enquiries into the incident are ongoing.”
