Grab A Bargain In The Switch eShop Cyber Deals Sale Starting This Friday (Europe)

Over 150 digital discounts

19 Nov 2019, 10:30 by Gavin Lane

With Black Friday looming upon us, Nintendo has announced its Cyber Deals sale for 2019 and it looks to feature some choice cuts from the Switch's considerable library. We've already seen Nintendo's North American Black Friday sales plans and now Europe is getting in on the action.

Over 150 games, large and small, from the Switch eShop will be included in the sale which begins on Friday 22nd November and will run until Sunday 1st December. The full list of discounts will go live with the sale itself, but Nintendo has provided a peek at some of the juiciest deals. As you can see below, some of the biggest Nintendo first-party games will be getting 30% off, with discounts elsewhere going as high as 70% (Dragon Ball FighterZ looks particularly tasty):

Title Percentage Discount in Cyber Deals Sale
Super Mario Odyssey 30%
The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild 30%
Cadence of Hyrule – Crypt of the NecroDancer Featuring The Legend of Zelda 30%
New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe 30%
Dragon Quest Builders 2 30%
Xenoblade Chronicles 2 30%
FIFA 20 30%
DARK SOULS: Remastered 40%
Dragon Ball FighterZ 70%
Wolfenstein: Youngblood Deluxe Edition 50%

Also of note, the Nintendo Switch Online exclusive NES controllers will have a 20% discount for the duration of the Cyber Deals sale, so if you're a Nintendo Switch Online subscriber and have been eyeing those (but perhaps had trouble justifying their £49.99 / €59.99 price point), 20% off might be tempting if you enjoy playing all those NES games on Switch.
