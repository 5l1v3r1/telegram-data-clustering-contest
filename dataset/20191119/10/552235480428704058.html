New dad caught with sick images of children on computer after police raid

Graeme Rippey, 32, denied downloading photos depicting abuse of youngsters, but a jury unanimously found him guilty after trial.

19 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Wishaw Press, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/wishaw-press/

A new dad was caught with hundreds of indecent images of children on his laptop.

Graeme Rippey, 32, denied downloading photos depicting abuse of youngsters, but a jury unanimously found him guilty last Wednesday after trial at Hamilton Sheriff Court.

He was also convicted of possessing images showing bestiality.

Lorry driver Rippey was charged after an early morning police raid at his home in Ravenscliffe Road, Motherwell, on November 23 last year.

The laptop had been given to him by his stepdad, who was called as a witness and denied all knowledge of the images.

Rippey didn’t try to blame his stepdad, claiming the images must have been downloaded by accident. His partner wept as she recalled police arriving at their home with a search warrant.
