Man mistakenly drops ring in the sea while proposing to his girlfriend on surfboard

19 Nov 2019, 10:16

- A young man identified as Christopher Garth accidentally lost the engagement ring while proposing to his girlfriend

- The man had taken his girlfriend surfing and proposed to her on a surfboard

- During the romantic moment, Garth mistakenly dropped the engagement ring in the ocean

- However, it was discovered that the man had kept the real ring on land and brought a spare one with him to surf

38-year-old Christopher Garth and 32-year-old Lauren Oiye would have a good memorable story to tell their children and grandchildren about the day they got engaged.

The story probably starts with Garth losing the ring, well the fake one. Garth in an attempt to give his woman a unique and creative proposal decided to propose while surfing.

As a surfing couple, Garth took Oiye surfing in their native Honolulu, Hawaii, and proceeded to propose on his surfboard, but during the romantic moment, he accidentally dropped the engagement ring in the sea.
