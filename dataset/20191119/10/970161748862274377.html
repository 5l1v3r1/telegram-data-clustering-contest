Heavily pregnant mum goes missing with two young daughters

19 Nov 2019, 10:20

Fears are growing for a heavily pregnant mum and her two children who have been missing for more than two months.

Hajar Mohammed (Al-Shammani), 35, was last seen at a Travelodge on September 10 with daughters Reem, eight, and Marjam, six.

Police released CCTV images showing the family in a bit to track them down.

They said they were becoming ‘increasingly concerned’ for them.

They were last seen at the hotel in Lower Street, Newcastle-under-Lyme, Staffordshire, at approximately 5.30pm on September 10.

Despite enquiries being made by our Staffordshire Police’s Missing Person Enquiry Team, the family have not been found.
