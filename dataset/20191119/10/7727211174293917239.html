Primark's drink trolley that's identical to designer for £360 less is back in stock in Liverpool

Fans have been desperate for Primark to restock the item

19 Nov 2019, 10:33 by Catherine Murphy

Primark has announced its drinks trolley is finally back in selected stores - and Liverpool is one of them.

For anyone not familiar with the drinks trolley, it was initially released last month and sold out instantly after fans spotted it was practically identical to a designer one, but for much less.

The drinks trolley (or cart depending on what you prefer to call it) looks just like one by Oliver Bonas - for £360 less.

Taking to its Instagram feed, Primark announced the news on both its home account and its clothing one.

On its home account the post has received more than 12,000 likes while on its main brand Instagram the post has received more than 36,000 likes.

Primark’s trolley features two levels, a gold handle and wheels to make it easy to move about.

Oliver Bonas’ is similar in design, with its round shape and colour, though it does have more levels in the middle and doesn’t have a handle.

The design sold by Oliver Bonas, which  recently opened a store at Liverpool ONE  , costs £395, while Primark’s trolley comes in at £30.
