Coronation Street actress Jean Fergusson died days after telling friends she wasn't feeling well

Jean Fergusson was described as 'the life and soul of any gathering'

19 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Vicki Newman, Charlotte Hadfield, Victoria Jones

An actress who appeared in Coronation Street and Last of the Summer Wine died just days after telling her friends that she wasn't feeling well at a party.

Jean Fergusson, 74, described as "the life and soul of any gathering", died at home on Thursday morning.

With an acting career spanning more than 50 years, she was best known for playing Marina in the long-running sitcom from 1985 until it ended in 2010.

She was also known for appearing in Coronation Street as Dorothy Hoyle from 2010 until 2011, as Liverpool Echo report.

On Sunday, Jean had been with friends celebrating Su Pollard's birthday, when she posed with 'Allo 'Allo star Vicki Michelle for what could have been her final picture, reports the Mirror Online.

Jean looks glam with her blonde hair in a classic up do, wearing a white roll-neck jumper with an off-white jacket and chiffon scarf.

Jean posed up with Vicki for a smiley picture.
