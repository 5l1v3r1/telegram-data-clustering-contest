Microsoft will end support for Cortana on iOS and Android next year

19 Nov 2019, 07:28 by Shereesa Moodley

Microsoft on Friday announced that it will end support for the Cortana app on both iOS and Android as part of the virtual assistant’s “evolution”.

“To make your personal digital assistant as helpful as possible, we’re integrating Cortana into your Microsoft 365 productivity apps,” the company added on its support blog.

Following the change, users will no longer have access to reminders and lists via Cortana. Instead, the information will only be accessible via Cortana on Windows.

“Also, Cortana reminders, lists, and tasks are automatically synced to the Microsoft To-Do app, which you can download to your phone for free,” Microsoft continued.

Support for Cortana for iOS and Android will end on 31 January 2020.

Meanwhile, the company earlier this month also launched an all-in-one Microsoft Office app as well.
