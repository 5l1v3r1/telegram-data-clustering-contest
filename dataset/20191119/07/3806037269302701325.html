KAPITAL Drops Sleek Bandana-Clad Navy Marine Boots

Decorating the classic silhouette.

19 Nov 2019, 07:54 by Charlie Zhang

After just releasing its classic bandana slip-on loafers, KAPITAL has returned with a Chelsea boot iteration of the footwear concept â€” Leather Bandana Navy Marine Boots.

This time around, the shoes come with a high ankle construction and a long zipper opening on the sides that run all the way down to the sole. Like the previous release, the boots come in either black or red colorways, decorated with a series of rich bandana patterns. The high ankle panel is emblazoned with a contrasting pattern that cuts into the paisley pattern along the forefoot. To contrast the patterns at the back of the shoe, the front half is emblazoned with a spread of traditional motifs, wrapping over its angular almond toe for a sleek look. As a finishing note, the boots have been painted with decorative polka dots over the raised heels.

The KAPITAL Bandana Leather Navy Marine Boots are currently available at kapital-webshop.jp for $727 USD.
