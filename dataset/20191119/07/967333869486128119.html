Playful Golden Retriever Enjoying Snow Cave

Most dogs just love to wallow in the snow. Even adult dogs, seeing snowdrifts, turn into little puppies - they run and jump until snowflakes fly in different directions.

19 Nov 2019, 07:30

Dog owners annually observe the attitude of their pets to freshly fallen snow. It is amusing to watch a pet jumping into snowdrifts, making labyrinths and drowning in snow-white cold fluff.

This golden retriever seems to like its snow cave and could even live there.
