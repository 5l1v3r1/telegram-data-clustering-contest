Nokia 7.2 is a CES 2020 Innovation Award Honoree

19 Nov 2019, 07:39 by Nayan

In a great recognition for Nokia Mobile’s prowess with smartphones, Nokia 7.2 has been chosen as one of the CES 2020 Innovation Award Honoree. Talking about why Nokia 7.2 was chosen, the official CES page mentions,

Nokia 7.2 offers fans advanced tools to express their creativity with a powerful 48MP triple camera featuring ZEISS Optics. Nokia 7.2 combines PureDisplay technology, always-on HDR and Google Assistant into a device with a timeless Nordic design.

You can check the official CES award page for Nokia 7.2 by clicking here. Reacting to this good news, HMD CPO Juho expressed his happiness on Twitter.
