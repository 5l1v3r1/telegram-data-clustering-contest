Spoilers: Fire horror for Fiz as nightmare Corrie nanny Jade strikes

19 Nov 2019, 07:01

You’d have to be a pretty trusting Coronation Street viewer not to be suspicious of newcomer nanny Jade Rowan (Lottie Henshall), who has been seen secretly scheming against Fiz Stape (Jennie McAlpine) since arriving to help look after troubled Hope.

And in upcoming episodes, she will step up her manipulation, using Hope as a weapon to unsettle Fiz and Tyrone (Alan Halsall) – with dramatic consequences. And as another fire takes hold, is young Hope really to blame?

We have seen flame happy Hope cause trouble with fire before but since her behaviour began to improve, Fiz and Tyrone had hoped that the troubles were behind them and things would start to get calmer. But not with Jade around, whose mysterious vendetta brings chaos and fear to the household.

When Fiz refuses to throw Hope a fireworks party, Hope plays up and Tyrone worries that they should give in in case their refusal leads to her misbehaving again.
