Kanye West confirms he’s working with Dr Dre on Jesus Is King Part 2

19 Nov 2019, 07:52

Kanye West is already back in the studio working on a second Jesus Is King album and, somehow, he’s managed to persuade Dr Dre to join him for the ride.

Fans are still digesting the first installment of the gospel-inspired Jesus Is King, which was released just three weeks ago, but it seems Kanye’s creative juices are still flowing.

Announcing the album’s sequel, the Gold Digger rapper shared a photo to Twitter on Monday with him looking rather serious in the recording studio with Dr Dre beside him, looking pretty excited and pointing at Ye.

‘Ye and Dre Jesus is King Part II coming soon,’ Kanye captioned the image.

It’s a shame Kanye doesn’t visibly share Dre’s enthusiasm but he has made his feelings on the NWA producer clear in the past. In a piece for Rolling Stone, Kanye once said: ‘Dre feels like God placed him here to make music.’
