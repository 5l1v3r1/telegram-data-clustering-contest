US politician Eric Swalwell rips massive fart on live TV during Hardball interview

19 Nov 2019, 07:26

If you’ve been on Twitter today, you’ll probably have noticed an unusual hashtag has been dominating the Twitter-sphere for the best part of the last few hours.

#Fartgate is the newest political scandal to rock the US after Congressman Eric Swalwell ripped one live on international television.

In a live broadcast to Hardball host Chris Matthews, Rep. Swalwell was discussing the impeachment proceedings of Donald Trump when he let out what can only be described as an absolutely enormous fart.
