Aaron Carter seen for first time since leaving hospital after 'weight loss'

19 Nov 2019, 07:05

Aaron Carter has been seen in public for the first time since being admitted to hospital last week, as he headed back to Los Angeles from Florida.

The singer, 31, was seen still wearing his hospital wristband and appearing to have lost significant weight, which was why he had been admitted to the medical facility.

Aaron had travelled back to his home in LA, and was seen wearing his own merch – a baseball cap with his name on the front – as he picked up some bits at a petrol station.

As the photo of him in a hospital bed emerged, Aaron’s mother revealed she would be looking after his social media channels for him ‘until he recovers’, as well as being his new manager.

It’s claimed the reason for his hospital visit was due to his ‘alarming weight loss’ caused by family stress, according to TMZ.

The site reported that Aaron’s mum, Jane, took him in to see doctors because he weighed just 115lb (eight stones and two pounds) down from his previous weight of 160lb (11 stone four pounds).

Aaron was recently hit by restraining orders from his brother, Nick Carter, and his twin sister Angel Carter after they claimed to be ‘afraid for their family’s safety’ and wanted Aaron’s collection of guns to be removed from him.

Angel and Nick were said to be worried by Aaron’s ‘increasingly alarming behaviour’, with Angel writing in a statement on her Twitter account that he had confessed to having ‘violent thoughts towards babies and our family members’.

Aaron had also revealed that his family had called law enforcement out to his house to have his guns removed, but that the police said they wouldn’t remove his collection, which includes assault rifles, unless he was deemed ‘mentally unstable’.

He had also claimed he would be moving to Nova Scotia in Canada after buying a property there to get away from the ‘foul’ people who had treated him badly in America and so that he could be ‘making music 24/7’.
