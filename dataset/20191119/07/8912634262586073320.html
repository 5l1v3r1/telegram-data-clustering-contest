Imagine Dragons, Alan Walker, Clean Bandit added to Diriyah line-up

19 Nov 2019, 07:43

LONDON: Paul McCartney is to headline the 50th anniversary of Glastonbury in June next year, the world’s largest greenfield festival, organizers announced on Monday.

“Hey Glasto — excited to be part of your Anniversary celebrations. See ya next year!” the 77-year-old former Beatle tweeted.
