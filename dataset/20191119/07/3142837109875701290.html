Tristel Plc (TSTL) To Go Ex-Dividend on November 21st

19 Nov 2019, 07:51 by Renee Jackson

Tristel Plc (LON:TSTL) declared a dividend on Wednesday, October 16th, Upcoming.Co.Uk reports. Investors of record on Thursday, November 21st will be paid a dividend of GBX 3.50 ($0.05) per share on Friday, December 20th. This represents a yield of 1.19%. The ex-dividend date is Thursday, November 21st. This is a positive change from Tristel’s previous dividend of $2.04. The official announcement can be accessed at this link.

Shares of TSTL opened at GBX 322 ($4.21) on Tuesday. The stock has a market cap of $143.73 million and a PE ratio of 36.18. The stock’s 50-day moving average is GBX 308.59 and its two-hundred day moving average is GBX 297.90. Tristel has a 12 month low of GBX 210 ($2.74) and a 12 month high of GBX 338 ($4.42).

Separately, FinnCap restated a “corporate” rating on shares of Tristel in a research note on Wednesday, October 16th.

About Tristel

Tristel Plc develops, manufactures, and supplies infection and contamination control, and hygiene products in the United Kingdom, Germany, and internationally. The company operates through three segments: Human Healthcare, Animal Healthcare, and Contamination Control. It offers hospital infection prevention and control products under the Tristel brand; contamination control products for pharmaceutical and personal care industries under the Crystel brand; and animal health infection prevention and control products under the Anistel brand.
