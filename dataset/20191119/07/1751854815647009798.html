The Honor V30 series of flagship phones will be made official in China on Nov. 26. Honor’s parent firm Huawei is also gearing to launch the Nova 6 5G smartphone. Yesterday, the Chinese firm had released the first video teaser to reveal its front design. While the rumor mill has been speculating that the Nova 6 5G will go official in December, there was no confirmation on it yet. Huawei has now finally confirmed that the Nova 6 5G will be announced on Dec. 5.

The below shown official launch posters state that the Nova 6 5G will be the latest edition to its Nova series of smartphones. It will be arriving as a selfie-centric smartphone. The launch poster confirms that it will be holding an unveil event in Wuhan on Dec. 5 to announce the Nova 6 5G smartphone. The Nova 6 5G will be made official exactly 10 days after the MatePad Pro flagship tablet will go official in the country.

The video teaser that was revealed yesterday had revealed that the Nova 6 5G is equipped with an elliptical cutout at the upper-left corner of its screen for its two front-facing cameras. The exact size of the screen is not known yet. However, the company confirmed that the phone is capable at capturing 105-degree superwide angle selfie shots. The smartphone also features a side-mounted fingerprint reader integrated with the power button.

Through 3C certifications it was revealed that the Huawei Nova 6 will be arriving in 4G only (WLZ-AL10) and 5G ready (WLZ-AN00) variants. Both models may arrive with support for 40W fast charging. There is a possibility that the 5G edition of the Nova 6 could be fueled by Kirin 990 5G whereas the regular version may feature Kirin 990 or 980 SoC.

Huawei Nova 6 Specifications and Price (Rumored)

Rumors surrounding the Nova 6 phone have revealed it will be coming with a 6.44-inch full HD+ IPS LCD display with 1080 x 2340 pixels resolution support. The punch-hole on the display could be featuring a 32-megapixel + 12-megapixel dual front-facing cameras. The rear shell of the phone may feature a 60-megapixel + 16-megapixel + 12-megapixel + 2-megapixel quad camera system. The upcoming Sony IMX686 lens could be its primary sensor.

The Kirin 990 powered phone could be coming to the Chinese market in 8 GB and 12 GB RAM LPDDR4x RAM editions. The phone is expected to release in UFS 2.1 storage options like 128 GB and 256 GB. The device may come equipped with a 4,000mAh battery. The 8 GB RAM + 128 GB storage, 8 GB RAM + 256 GB storage and 12 GB RAM + 256 GB storage editions of the phone could be respectively priced at 3,499 Yuan (~$498), 3,799 Yuan (~$541) and 4,099 Yuan (~$583).  It may come in purple, black, blue and red colors.
