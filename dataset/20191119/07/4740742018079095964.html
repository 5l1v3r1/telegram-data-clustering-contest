Police appeal for help after man suffers serious facial injuries in Harbourside attack

A 20-year old man was injured after disorder broke out near a kebab van

19 Nov 2019, 07:42 by Jasper King

Avon and Somerset Constabulary are appealing for witnesses after a 20-year-old man suffered serious facial injuries following an attack at the Harbourside in Bristol.

The victim was punched in the face and then kicked in the head during the incident in Canons Road at 3.15am on Tuesday, October 8.

The assault followed a disorder which broke out near a kebab van.

As a result of this attack, the victim has suffered serious injuries to his face, which include a fractured jaw.

It's believed the two offenders were both wearing white t-shirts and skinny jeans.
