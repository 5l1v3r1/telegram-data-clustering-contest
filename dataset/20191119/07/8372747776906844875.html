Three teen girls reported missing in Lehigh Acres, all are found safe

19 Nov 2019, 07:34 by WFTX Digital Team

UPDATE (2:40 PM) -- The Lee County Sheriff's Office now says Williams has been found and is safe.

All three of the teens who were reported missing on Monday are now safe.
---------------------------------------
UPDATE (8:55 AM) -- The Lee County Sheriff's Office reports that Destiny and Kyera have been located and are safe. Cassy Williams is still missing.
-----------------------------------------
LEE COUNTY, Fla. -- There is a trio of missing teen girls in Lee County that deputies need your help finding.

16-year-old Destiny Aguilar was last seen wearing a black sweatshirt, black yoga pants, and a backpack.

13-year-old Kyera Dealmeida was last seen wearing a gray sweatshirt, gray sweatpants, and a backpack.

And 13-year-old Cassy Williams was last seen wearing a black sweatshirt, black yoga pants, and a backpack.

They were last seen early Monday morning and deputies believe they ran away together from their Lehigh Acres home.

If you have any information on where they are, please call the Lee County Sheriff's Office at (239) 477-1000.
