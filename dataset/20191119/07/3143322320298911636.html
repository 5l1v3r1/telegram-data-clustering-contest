WATCH: Shots fired in local parking area

WARNING: Viewer discretion advised. A shot rang out at City Centre in Mbombela in the middle of the night on Friday, November 15.

19 Nov 2019, 07:33 by Lowvelder

MBOMBELA – A video has been doing the rounds on social media of a fight that broke out in a parking area close to the city centre. In the video you can see one of the men pull out a gun and run after the other man. A few seconds later a shot is fired.

According to Brig Leonard Hlathi, no one was injured in the video. The police have opened an enquiry and have urged the man with the gun to step forward.
