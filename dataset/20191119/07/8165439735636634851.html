Man's Body Found In A Burning Car In Dublin Last Night

19 Nov 2019, 07:07 by Josh Crosbie

Found in burning car

An investigation has been launched after a man's body was found in a burning car in Dublin.

It's suspected he may have been shot.

Gardaí and emergency services were called to the Mount Andrew area in Lucan at around quarter past eight last night.

A car that was set on fire was extinguished and a man's body was discovered.

It is suspected he had been shot before it was set on fire.

The scene remains sealed off this morning and the man has yet to be identified.

Gardaí are appealing for anyone who might have any information to contact them.
