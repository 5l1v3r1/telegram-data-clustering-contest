Miley Cyrus and Cody Simpson 'split' after month of hot and heavy dating

19 Nov 2019, 07:35

Miley Cyrus and Cody Simpson’s romance has been blazing hot and heavy since they first hooked up back in October.

But it seems the pair are starting to cool things off.

According to reports, Miley, 26, wants to focus on herself and is backing off from her relationship with the Australian singer for now.

A source told Radar Online: ‘They are taking a break, but want to remain friends.

‘She said she just needs to focus on herself.’

Miley and Cody, 22, have been piling on the PDA across social media after first being pictured kissing in early October – from sharing pictures with Miley’s hands down her beau’s pants, to making out dressed as Billy Idol and Perri Lister for Halloween.

But it’s been claimed that all the oversharing was a plan to get a rise out of Miley’s ex-husband Liam Hemsworth, who she split from in August after eight months of marriage.

An insider added to Radar: ‘This romance with Cody…and constant “look at me” behaviour is all designed to push Liam’s buttons— and to be fair it’s always worked in the past because he’s a good guy who ultimately loves her.

‘But now he’s just rolling his eyes and washing his hands of her, even as a friend, which of course is driving Miley nuts.’

Even Cody’s mum reckons that the constant PDAs were all a bit of an attention-seeking mission, with Angie telling the Kyle and Jackie O Show: ‘I think that they’re doing it for a bit of fun and looking for exactly what the media is saying.

‘They’re like two peas in a pod. We were just on FaceTime with them this morning. They’re in Nashville having a bit of a holiday and I just think they like to play it up.’

The pair haven’t actually shared much about each other in recent weeks – Miley last mentioned Cody on 3 November on her Instagram story, while Simpson last shared a mention of the Hannah Montana star on 8 November.

If Miley is indeed taking some time off from their romance to focus on herself, it wouldn’t be too much of a surprise.

Since splitting from Liam, 29, three months ago, the Wrecking Ball singer has also had a romance with Kaitlynn Carter, who she was pictured kissing in Lake Como just after her divorce announcement.

However, 31-year-old Kaitlynn, who had just broken up with husband Brody Jenner, and Miley ended things after a month of dating.

Metro.co.uk has contacted Miley’s rep for comment.
