19 Nov 2019, 02:23 by Darrelle Lincoln

For yet another game in Mexico city, we have questions and concerns about the turf at Estadio Azteca Stadium for Monday night’s matchup between the Kansas City Chiefs and Los Angeles Chargers.

Basically, the field is terrible and the pictures and videos coming from the game prove that the league needs to stay away from this game every year for the safety of the players.
