FIFA 20 Ultimate Team TOTW 10 predictions Aston Villa star features alongside Cristiano Ronaldo

We predict Piemonte Calcio star Cristiano Ronaldo to be named in the FIFA 20 Ultimate Team TOTW 7 lineup alongside an Aston Villa star who performed excellently for his country in this international break, here are the full predictions for the FUT 20 TOTW 7 lineup.

19 Nov 2019, 02:00 by Nathan Bliss

Piemonte Calcio and Portugal forward Cristiano Ronaldo has a great chance of being included in the FUT20 TOTW 10 this week, after an outstanding hat-trick against Lithuania, which would earn him the first in-form card of the FUT20 season.

Ronaldo predicted to be named alongside Aston Villa midfielder John McGinn in the lineup, who scored Scotland's winning goal against Cyprus in the international break.

McGinn could join the likes of Eden Hazard, Raphael Varane, Christian Eriksen, Harry Kane, Toni Kroos and Manuel Neuer in the FIFA 20 Team of the Week 10 lineup, after some outstanding international performances.

This is just a FUT 20 TOTW prediction, so there is no guarantee that any players mentioned will be included.

The FUT20 TOTW line-up is picked on single performances only, so goals and assists are not added accumulatively within the TOTW period.

Here are our FIFA 20 Ultimate Team TOTW week 10 predictions.
