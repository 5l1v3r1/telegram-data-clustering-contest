Kanye West Buys Another $14 Million Mansion In Wyoming

19 Nov 2019, 02:59 by AllHipHop Staff

Chicago rapper Kanye West is planning to call Wyoming home, after he bought another massive ranch.

(AllHipHop News) Kanye West has been spending his $850 million fortune lavishly as of late, and his spending spree continues.

According to reports, Ye just plunked down another $14 million to buy a second ranch in his new hometown of Wyoming.

TMZ reports that Kanye is the new owner of a huge property called Bighorn Mountain Ranch.

The rap star's new piece of real estate sits on 6,713-acres is comparable to Monster Lake Ranch, the 9,000-acre property Kanye bought for $14 million in September.

Bighorn Mountain Ranch comes complete with lodges, cabins, two heated helicopter pads and sweeping views of the surrounding mountains.

Earlier this week, Kanye revealed he is building a 70,684-square-foot amphitheater called the “West Meditation Space Large Impact Structure" which will be an open-air facility.
