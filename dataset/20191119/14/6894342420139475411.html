Malaysian Flying Car Test Flight To Take Place This Thursday: Will Not Be Shown To Public Though

19 Nov 2019, 14:25 by Chief Chapree

The Minister of Entrepreneur Development, Mohd Redzuan Md Yusof has revealed that the test flight for a flying car made from a Malaysian company will take place this coming Thursday, 21 November. The remark was made during a Dewan Rakyat session at the Parlimen yesterday.

While the minister has extended the invite to his fellow MPs, he declined to publicly reveal the location of the test flight though. He insisted that the test flight was meant as a private demonstration and having it being done publicly could jeopardize the company that was behind the flying car.
