Gardaí appealing for information on movements of car after body found in Lucan

19 Nov 2019, 14:33 by James Cox

Gardaí are appealing for information on the movements of a Navy Toyota Corolla 05-CW-2225 following the discover of a man's body in a burning vehicle in Lucan.

The body has been removed to the Office of the State Pathologist, Whitehall, Dublin, where a post mortem will be carried out by Locum State Pathologist Dr. Heidi Okkers.

The Navy Toyota was sold on November 13, 2019, and gardaí are appealing to the public for any information on the vehicles movements from this date.

Gardaí are also appealing for any road users who travelled in the area last night and who may have dash cam footage available to contact Gardaí in Lucan Garda Station on 01 666 7300 or the Garda Confidential Line on 1800 666 111.
