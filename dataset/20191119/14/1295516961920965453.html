V Wars: Netflix's New Vampire-Fueled Series Debuts First Trailer

19 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Jon Arvedon

Netflix has released the first trailer for V Wars, the upcoming show based on Jonathan Maberry's book series of the same name.

The trailer begins with Dr. Luther Swann researching what the news describes as "vampire killings" in which the victims are missing large amounts of blood. What he soon discovers is a virus that transforms ordinary human beings into blood-sucking vampires.
