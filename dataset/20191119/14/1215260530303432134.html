Clarendon farmer charged after allegedly attacking, chopping man

19 Nov 2019, 14:44

The Clarendon police have charged 27-year-old farmer Bryan Thompson for wounding with intent after he allegedly attacked and chopped another man with a machete on Sunday, November 17.
