I'm A Celeb's Emily Atack throws shade at Katie Price and Peter Andre

19 Nov 2019, 14:24

Emily Atack has taken aim at Katie Price and Peter Andre as she joked about the first time they ‘fondled’ in the I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! pool.

When appearing on the competition’s sister show Extra Camp, the 29-year-old watched her co-hosts Joel Dommett and Adam Thomas take a dip in the iconic lake.

However, she didn’t seem too impressed when she was reminded about Katie and Peter’s smooch in the water, when they fell in love on the show back in 2004.

‘This is where Katie Price and Peter Andre had fondles,’ Joel proudly boasted to his buddy.

But back in the studio, Emily teased: ‘I hope you boys went to the clinic after being in that pool.’

Looking slightly lost for words, her new presenting pal quickly rose to the banter, as he named one of Pete’s songs, drum roll, please!

‘Oh yeah, we did,’ he told the former I’m A Celeb contestant: ‘And we tested positive for Insania.’

Emily giggled behind her flashcard at the joke as Joel showed there were no hard feelings by adding: ‘Love you, Peter.’

The ITV hit appears to be pushing the boundaries this year with their sly comments, seeing Ant and Dec take aim at Prince Andrew in the opening sequence.

When welcoming their fans back to the show, they made reference to the royal’s claim that he has a ‘peculiar medical condition which is that I don’t sweat.’

Ant chuckled: ‘10 celebrities, all of them very nervous, all of them sweating.’

‘Presuming they can sweat, not everyone can,’ Dec added with a cheeky smirk on his face.

Never ones to turn down a chance to milk a joke, the pair returned to the sweating puns, with Dec asking about the second team of celebs to arrive in the jungle.

‘Will those celebrities be A) confident, B) excited, or C) absolutely sweating?’ Dec asked, with his pal jumping in: ‘Presuming they can sweat.’

‘Presuming they can,’ Dec repeated: ‘Not everybody can.’

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out of Here! continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
