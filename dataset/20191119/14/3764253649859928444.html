Granit Xhaka: Arsenal to offer the Swiss footballer to Borussia Monchengladbach

19 Nov 2019, 14:07

- Granit Xhaka could be making a return to Borussia Monchengladbach

- But Arsenal want Denis Zakaria plus cash for them to release Granit Xhaka

- Xhaka has not played for Arsenal since he had a confrontation with Gunners fans

Premier League giants Arsenal are reportedly ready to offer German club Borussia Monchengladbach a chance to resign Granit Xhaka when the transfer window opens.

But the German club will have to give the Gunners their star man Denis Zakaria before luring Granit Xhaka back to Germany.

Granit Xhaka's days at Arsenal are now numbered after the confrontation he had with Gunners fans at the Emirates stadium in which he failed to apologize.

He was also stripped of the captaincy at Arsenal after his outburst against Gunners' fans as Unai Emery named Gabon international Pierre Aubameyang as the new captain.
