Cops probing 'indecent offence' at Scots H&M store release CCTV image of 6ft man

Police say the incident caused serious upset and have appealed for help in identifying the man they believe can help the investigation.

19 Nov 2019, 14:39 by Kathleen Speirs

Police probing an 'indecent offence' at an H&M store have released CCTV footage of a man they think may help with their enquiries.

The incident took place at around 4.45pm on Monday, August 5 at the Union Square shopping centre in Aberdeen .

PC Mark Brechin, who is leading the enquiry, said: "The incident caused serious upset to those involved.

"I would urge anyone who has any information relating to the man in the CCTV images to make contact with police and pass on that information.

"The man is described as approximately six foot tall with dark hair.

"He was wearing dark trousers and a light coloured top."

 

You can contact Police Scotland via the 101 non-emergency telephone number quoting reference number CF0198110819.

Those wishing to remain anonymous can call the registered charity, Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
