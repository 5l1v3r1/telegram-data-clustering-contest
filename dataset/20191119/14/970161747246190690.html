What Anthony Joshua whispered to himself during ring-walk before Ruiz Jr fight

19 Nov 2019, 14:03

Anthony Joshua muttered some final words of motivation to himself during his ring-walk to face Andy Ruiz Jr last June.

Ruiz Jr would inflict a shocking defeat on Joshua at Madison Square Garden, stripping the Brit of his three world titles with a seventh-round knockout.

The likes of Tyson Fury pointed to Joshua’s demeanor during the ring-walk as an indicator the defending champion wasn’t in the right frame of mind.

With his rematch with Ruiz Jr fast approaching, behind-the-scenes footage has been released of the first fight in New York.
