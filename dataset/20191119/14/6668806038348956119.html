Manav Kaul’s first look as Parineeti Chopra’s coach in Saina Nehwal biopic revealed

19 Nov 2019, 14:13 by Filmfare

There has been a lot of buzz around Saina Nehwal biopic starring Parineeti Chopra in the lead. Touted to be one of the most anticipated films of 2020, fans have loved Chopra’s look as badminton player in the film. Parineeti has been learning the nuances of the game for quite some time. Now, Manav Kaul, who plays her coach in the film, shared his first look from the film.

Sharing his look from the biopic, Manav wrote, “My first look as @nehwalsaina's Coach (respect) #SainaNehwalBiopic... hard working @parineetichopra as Saina Nehwal... Directed by #AmoleGupte Produced by @itsbhushankumarofficial @tseries.official @tseriesfilms

Take a look.

Saina Nehwal dropped a comment on his post which read, “Amazing.” Manav went on to thank her and wrote, “@nehwalsaina you have an amazing story to tell ... I feel very lucky to be part of #sainanehwalbiopic thank you ... respect.”
