Chiefs vs. Chargers final score: Kansas City's defense holds on to win in Mexico City

19 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Eli Boettger

The Chiefs defense preserved a much-needed 24-17 victory over the AFC West rival Chargers on "Monday Night Football" in Mexico City's Azteca Stadium.

In the final game of the 2019 NFL international series, Kansas City (7-4) avoidedfalling off the top spot in the divisional standings. The Chiefs allowed Los Angeles (4-7) to rack up 312 first-half yardsbut showed a much stronger effort in the final two quarters, particularly during the game's final drive.
