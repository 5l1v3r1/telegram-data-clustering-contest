Premier League rivals linked with move for Aston Villa target - report

Aston Villa transfer news | The top flight's transfer window re-opens in January with Dean Smith's side able to strengthen for their survival mission in the New Year

19 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Richard Cusack

Norwich City have been linked with a move for Aston Villa summer target Jack Butland.

Villa were believed to be vying with AFC Bournemouth for the England international's services, but both sides ended up going a different route.

Bournemouth promoted Aaron Ramsdale - signed from Sheffield United in 2017 - to number one and Villa signed Tom Heaton from Burnley in an £8 million deal.

Jed Steer stepped up from deputy to Orjan Nyland - who got injured last season - to help guide Dean Smith 's side back to the Premier League through the Championship's play-offs.

But Smith wanted a new stopper for his side's first campaign back in the top flight after three seasons in the Championship.

In the end he plumped for Heaton, balking at Stoke City's asking price of north of £20 million for Butland .

Butland is now set to be offered an escape route from the bet365 Stadium, report StokeonTrentLive , with the 26-year-old enduring a torrid few seasons with Mark Hughes, Paul Lambert, Gary Rowett, Nathan Jones and now Michael O'Neill all taking charge of Stoke since the 2017-18 season.
