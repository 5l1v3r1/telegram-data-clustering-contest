We have just picked up the Debris Achievement list.

There are 21 achievements worth a total of 1,000 Gamerscore, none of which are secret.

Reality Check Endure each delusion. 100

In Flow Make it through the dark fish delta in Flow without taking damage. 75

Jacques Cousteau Make your presence known in Shock. 20
An atmospheric first-person narrative adventure set in a surreal seascape locked between ice and rock. Following an incident, you and a friend must find power, defeat strange and deadly creatures, and decipher what mysterious forces are attempting to prevent your escape. Escape together or die alone.

Debris is developed and published by Moonray Studios.
