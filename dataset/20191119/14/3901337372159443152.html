In This Week's Edition Of Soccer Laduma

19 Nov 2019, 14:42 by Soccer Laduma

In anticipation of this week’s 1150th edition of Soccer Laduma, we preview some of the features included. The latest edition will be available at your nearest outlet tomorrow, Wednesday 20th November.

TKO Semi-finals Preview

In our TKO semi-final preview special, we caught with Mamelodi Sundowns defender, Motjeka Madisha, Golden Arrows left-back, Danny Phiri, Kaizer Chiefs legend, William Shongwe, and Maritzburg United forward, Gabriel Nyoni. Once the semi-final matches commence, this weekend is bound to spark into life. Find out the thoughts of the players who desire silverware and one who has been there and has done it before.

Wedson Nyirenda Interview

Current Baroka FC head coach and former Kaizer Chiefs forward, Wedson Nyirenda, has bags of coaching experience across the continent, which has served him well, for the most part. However, despite his reputation as a mentor who can make teams punch above their weight, life in Polokwane this season, hasn’t been as rosy as before. And with rumours surfacing of a possible return to coach his native Zambia, is it the end for Nyirenda at Bakgaga?

Thulani Hlatshwayo Interview

Captain of club and country, Thulani Hlatshwayo was the talk of the football fraternity not too long ago, with him expressing his desire to play for Orlando Pirates. Despite this, the move failed to materialize, with ‘Tyson’, as he is affectionately known, extending his contract with the Bidvest Wits and enjoying life and football in Braamfontein. Hlatshwayo opened up about the Orlando Pirates saga and life under new Bafana Bafana head coach, Molefe Nsteki.

 

Also…

Aubrey Modiba Interview

SuperSport United winger, Aubrey Modiba, has been one of the country’s standout players, adding goals, assists and consistent performances for both club and country. Although a move to Absa Premiership champions, Mamelodi Sundowns, didn’t materialize during the last transfer window, regular Bafana Bafana call-ups and winning the MTN8, has certainly put Modiba is in a great space in his career. Hopefully, his ambition to play abroad materializes, however, the left-footed player is firmly focused on his football in Matsatsantsa. 

GladAfrica Championship interview

Soccer Laduma’s Peter Raath caught up with Ajax Cape Town winger, Abednego Mosiatlhaga, as he shares scoring a hat-trick, his time at Stellenbosch FC, his loan deals and playing football, the Dutch way.

Barcelona feature

Current Barcelona manager, Ernesto Valverde, hasn’t really been taken to, by the Barcelona fan-base and his relationship with Lionel Messi hasn’t always been perfect. It’s clear that the Catalans have relied heavily on their talisman for results, and accusations that Valverde’s team is essentially a one-man show, have failed to dissipate. Soccer Laduma’s Kurt Buckerfield digs deeper to find out the real problem behind Barcelona’s ‘Messi’ reliance.

Revolving Column

The U23 AFCON for South Africa and head coach, David Notoane, has gone extremely well, as they find themselves in the semi-final stage of the competition. In this week’s revolving column, Notoane shares the problems they’ve faced during the tournament and some of the goals they’ve attained, in Egypt.

Siyagobhoza

In this week’s edition of our beloved Siyagobhoza, the crew reveals news concerning Chippa United tricky winger, Thabo Rakhale. With his contract ending at the after this season, one of South Africa’s big three clubs are keen on signing the former Buccaneers player, in an attempt to add some much-needed flair. Which team is it, find out in this week’s edition.
