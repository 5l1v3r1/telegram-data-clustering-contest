Guardians of the Galaxy Star Boards Mission: Impossible 7 & 8

19 Nov 2019, 14:11 by Renaldo Matadeen

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 star Pom Klementieff has officially joined the cast of Mission: Impossible 7 and 8.

"@PomKlementieff, How do you spell femme fatale? #MI78" director Christoper McQuarrie tweeted, to which Klementieff replied, "Mischief: Accepted."
