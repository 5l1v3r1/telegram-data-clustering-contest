Spoilers: Drugs danger for Emmerdale teen Sarah tonight

19 Nov 2019, 14:23

Sarah Sugden (Katie Hill) finds herself in a dangerous situation during an hour long episode of Emmerdale tonight – as she attends a party with her boyfriend Danny (Louis Healey), who gives her a pill, which she then takes.

With things tense at home following the split of Charity Dingle (Emma Atkins) and Vanessa Woodfield (Michelle Hardwick), Sarah has been spending more and more time with Danny and enjoying getting close to him. But his peer pressure on her steps up a gear tonight.

Noah (Jack Downham) is already suspicious of Sarah sneaking off and finds that she has gone to the party. He sees Danny and his mate Jayden with her and when she is offered a pill, she doesn’t want to feel shown up in front of Danny and so takes it.
