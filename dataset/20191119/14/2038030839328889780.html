Khloé Kardashian teases new reality show with True

19 Nov 2019, 14:44

Cape Town – On the latest episode of Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Kris and Khloé hinted at what could be a possible reality show spin-off in the works.

That's right, Khloé had producers over to speak about the possible mother-daughter duo getting their own show.

According to ET, the producers are heard saying they "want to shoot and put this on the Internet as soon as possible". They're also heard discussing "a five-to-seven-minute episode" in which fans will see "mom and daughter out and about... letting the world see just little snippets".

The reality show will most likely be a short-form series available online.

According to Us Weekly, the camera panned to a shot of presentation during the production meeting. There was a photo of Khloé and True on the screen with what we assume is the possible title of the show: Khloé and True Take the World.
