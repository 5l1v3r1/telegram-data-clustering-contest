Amazing Acro-Cat Having Fun

19 Nov 2019, 14:00

Fluffy, mewing, touching. Needless to say, many people adore cats. They are congenital acrobats, preferring to walk along thin balcony railings, forcing their owners to worry - and they can also do tricks.

Can cats be trained? Yes, but how? How can an owner persuade their cat to perform tricks?

This cat is doing tricks of its own free will, just for fun and without any commands.
