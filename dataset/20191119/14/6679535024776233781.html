Farmer attempts suicide at mandal revenue office

19 Nov 2019, 14:23 by Special Correspondent

Tension prevailed at the Dhanthalapalli Mandal Revenue Office as a farmer attempted suicide accusing officials of not giving him the land record.

The farmer I. Shankar, belonging to Kummarikunta village in the mandal, came to the office of the MRO and doused himself with kerosene and threatened self-immolation . He alleged that he had been cultivating eight acres of land for many years, but officials failed to update the records with his name.

The police who were present there foiled his suicide bid and counselled him. He was mentally upset and was handed over to his family members, the police said.

When contacted the MRO of Danthalapally, T Gowri Shankar said he had taken charge only recently and verified the records of the farmer . The land was in litigation and hence they were waiting for the decision of the court, he added.
