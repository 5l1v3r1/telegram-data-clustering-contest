Half-Life Is Back, This Time with Virtual Reality

After 12 years of break, Valve announced the new Half-Life game and it's going to be available on November 21.

19 Nov 2019, 14:55 by Nursah Ergü

If you like video games, you definitely know about Half-Life, which is a first-person shooter game. The first-ever Half-Life was released on November 19, 1998. The second game was released on November 16, 2004, and the last game, Half-Life 2: Episode Two was released on October 10, 2007.

And Half-Life fans have been waiting for Half-Life 2: Episode Three or Half-Life 3, but it's been a long time since the last Half-Life game was released.

On November 18, Valve announced on Twitter their new game, but it doesn't include any 3 in its name, it's called Half-Life: Alyx. Also, it's a VR (virtual reality) game.
