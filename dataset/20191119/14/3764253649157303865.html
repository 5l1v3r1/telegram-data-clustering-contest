Dad shares moments his daughter runs towards him when he picks her from school (video)

19 Nov 2019, 14:56

- A Nigerian dad has shared a heart-melting video compilation of his daughter

- The man identified as Lincoln Robinson shared a video compilation of his daughter always excited to see him after school

- In the video, the little girl always ran towards her dad excitedly, shouting the words my daddy

A Nigerian dad identified as Lincoln Robinson has warmed the hearts of many Nigerians with a video compilation of his 2-year-old daughter. In the video, the adorable girl is always seen running towards her dad.

In the video compilation shared on the popular micro-blogging platform, the little girl is always seen running towards her dad whenever she spots him at her class door after school.

The 2-year-old runs to him shouting the words my daddy. Every time she runs to him, she hugs him in an adorable way since she only measures up to his knee.
