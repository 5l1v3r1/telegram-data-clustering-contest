Cape Town - Former Skeem Saam actress Pearl Nikolic just joined the many celebrities who are expectant moms this year.

The actress recently revealed that she is expecting another baby while on holiday with her family in the Maldives.

Taking to Instagram, she shared multiple images of herself in a bikini – showing off her tiny baby bump while having fun in the sun.

This is not the only milestone Pearl has reached in 2019. In March, she married her now husband in a gorgeous ceremony, which was held in Bulgaria.

She previously played the role of Sonti Magongwa on SABC 1’s Skeem Saam for five years.

Her character Sonti left for Limpopo (Ga-Mashashane) after she lost everything she worked for and she hasn’t appeared on the soap-drama for almost a year now.
