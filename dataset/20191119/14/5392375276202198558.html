Today, at the Xiaomi Developers Conference 2019, Xiaomi Group founder and chairman Lei Jun said that under the “mobile +AIoT” dual-engine strategy, the company will continue to promote the next generation of “5G+AIoT”. “The super Internet has become a part of public life. As the leader of smart life, Xiaomi will give full play to the global leading edge of Xiaomi AIoT. Also, it will use core technology to help upgrade the home industry and promote the construction of smart homes.”

At today’s conference, Xiaomi Group released its own IoT industry interconnection solution. Lei Jun said that the Xiaomi AIoT platform has access to 2200+ products. Also, the number of connected devices reached 196 million, an increase of 69.5%. More than 3 million users have 5 or more IoT devices.

The Xiaomi executive said that the Xiaomi IoT platform will enter a new stage of development and move towards scene interconnection. The enterprise side of the scene interconnection is undoubtedly an important market.

In this sense, the Xiaomi solution consists of three parts: hotel, real estate, and IoT Enterprise Suite.

  • In the field of hotels, we offer Xiao Ai speakers, Xiaomi TVs, multi-function gateways, intelligent lighting, and other categories. This year, the company should deliver 5000+ hotels covering 34 cities;
  • In the field of real estate, we offer Xiao Ai speakers, Xiaomi TVs, Xiaomi routers, smart door locks, and other categories. This year, 2000 sets of cooperation with Zall, Greenland and Times Real Estate will be carried out, and there will be 14 inter-model projects.
  • In the IoT Enterprise Suite, it currently includes purifiers, switches, air conditioning temperature control, and sensors.
