Xiaomi’s succesful Mi Band series is about to win another member. That’s what suggests a new post shared by the company on Twitter. The post in question comes to promote the RunWithMi marathon with the words “Good thiiings come to those who run”. The three “i” are a perfect allusion to the Mi Band 3i which is rumored to go official on November 21.
