Helen Maider missing – Body found in search for Bishopbriggs pensioner who vanished amid freezing conditions

19 Nov 2019, 14:09 by Jonathan Whitelaw

A BODY has been found in the search for a pensioner who vanished amid freezing conditions earlier this morning.

An appeal was launched to help trace Helen Maider, 89, from the Bishopbriggs area after she vanished around 6am.

Now cops have confirmed that a body has been found as part of their efforts.

A spokesperson for the force said: "Police in East Dunbartonshire can confirm a body has been found in the search for missing woman Helen Maider in the Bishopbriggs area.

"The 89-year-old was last seen around 6am on Tuesday, 19 November, 2019 at the junction of Balmuildy Road and Stirling Drive.

"Formal identification has yet to be carried out however her family has been informed and the death is not being treated as suspicious".

Temperatures across Scotland plummeted overnight - with the mercury dipping to as low as -9C in parts of the Highlands and north east.
