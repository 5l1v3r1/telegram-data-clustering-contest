Chiranjeevi has hidden a secret from his wife! - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

19 Nov 2019

Megastar Chiranjeevi has always called his wife Surekha as Rekha at home.  If you thought it's innocuous, you are not alone.  Even the naive Surekha has always seen it as her hubby's way of calling her in a cute way!   

But 'Rekha' is a code, mind you!  

As far as the legendary Chiru is concerned, Rekha denotes the Bollywood actress Rekha, whose big favourite the 'Sye Raa' actor has been.  "I call my wife Surekha as Rekha.  It's because Rekha garu is one of my favourite actors.  My wife doesn't know this at all!  I hope this part of the speech is edited out so that she doesn't get to know the long-kept secret," the Megastar recently said at the ANR Awards ceremony.

Describing her as an ageless beauty, Chiru said that Rekha's stunning beauty would distract the attention of MPs in Rajya Sabha on the rare occasion she would visit the Parliament.
