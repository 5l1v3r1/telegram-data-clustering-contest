Huawei nova 5T receives EMUI 10 based on Android 10

19 Nov 2019 by Ro

After Huawei pushed the much-awaited EMUI 10 based on Android 10 for its Huawei P30 and Mate 20-series, the company is now rolling it out for one of its newest releases - the nova 5T.

And that's not the beta version of the software too. It's the stable release and those that participated in the beta testing are getting it worldwide - EMUI 10 10.0.0.168. The firmware is about 4.5GB big and it delivers tons of new features and a complete re-design of the UI.

Since it's a staged rollout, you should expect the OTA update to arrive in the following days if it hasn't already. You can always check for the new software manually in Settings > System > Software update > Check for Updates.
