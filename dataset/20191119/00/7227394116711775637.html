Xiaomi Redmi Note 8 Pro will get Android 10 any moment now

19 Nov 2019 by Ivan

The Xiaomi Redmi Note 8 Pro has been spotted running Geekbench with Android 10, signalling that Xiaomi is likely all but ready to release the Android 10 firmware for the public.

The score itself isn't interesting, rather the firmware on board the device. The Redmi Note 8 Pro is already running a Android 9-based MIUI 11 beta in China and is due to receive Android 10 in December.

The Redmi Note 8, on the other hand, should get Android 10 by the end of November.

In any case, Xiaomi could be closing in on a ready Android 10 firmware update for the Redmi Note 8 Pro and Redmi Note 8 a bit ahead of schedule.
