Italy Net Nine, Spain Score Five In Euro 2020 Qualifiers

19 Nov 2019, 00:10 by SL International

Spain 5-0 Romania

Spain ended their European Championship qualifying campaign in style after thumping Romania 5-0 at the Wanda Metropolitano.

Fabian Ruiz, a brace from Gerard Moreno and a Adrian Rus own goal put the hosts four goals ahead inside the first half, before Mikel Oyarzabal rounded off the scoring in second-half stoppage time.

Italy 9-1 Armenia

Italy were the biggest winners of the night after romping to a 9-1 triumph over Armenia in their final Group J qualifier.

The hosts were four goals ahead in the first through Ciro Immobile's brace and strikes from Nicolo Zaniolo and Nicolo Barella.

Zaniolo completed his brace after the hour mark, while Alessio Romagnoli, Jorginho, Riccardo Orsolini and Federico Chiesa completed the rout.

Gibraltar 1-6 Switzerland

Switzerland finished top of Group D after thumping Gibraltar 6-1 at the Victoria Stadium.

Cedric Itten bagged a brace, while Granit Xhaka, Ruben Vargas, Christian Fassnacht and Loris Benito also got on the score-sheet.

Sweden 3-0 Faroe Islands

Sweden finished second in Group F after defeating Faroe Islands 3-0 in their final qualifier at the Friends Arena.

Sebastian Andersson, Mattias Svanberg and John Guidetti guided the Swedes to victory on the night.
