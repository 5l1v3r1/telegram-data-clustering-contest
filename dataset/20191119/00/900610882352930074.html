11 Cadogan Gardens joins Relais and Chateaux

19 Nov 2019 by Mark Caswell

Chelsea hotel 11 Cadogan Gardens has joined the luxury hotel and restaurant collection Relais and Chateaux as its only London member.

The 56-room property is located close to Sloane Square, and was built in the late 19th century, originally comprising four Victorian townhouses.

Previously a private members’ club, it reopened as a luxury boutique hotel in 2012, and is part of the Iconic Luxury Hotels Group, along with sister properties and fellow Relais and Chateaux members Chewton Glen and Cliveden House.

Facilities include a library with log fire, two private outdoor terraces, self-contained apartments with private garages, and a gym.

Last year the hotel opened new restaurant Hans’ Bar and Grill, which opens out onto Pavilion Road.

Commenting on the news Philippe Gombert, president of Relais and Châteaux said:

“We are very happy to count 11 Cadogan Gardens as a member of our family. This is a perfect example of what Relais and Châteaux is: properties anchored in their region, ambassadors of their heritage, but also passionate about their craft. The hotel is perfectly located and will offer a delicious immersion into London for our guests.”

Relais and Chateaux was established in France in 1954, and now counts 580 hotel and restaurant members across over 60 countries.

The Stock Exchange Hotel in Manchester also became a member when it opened this month.
