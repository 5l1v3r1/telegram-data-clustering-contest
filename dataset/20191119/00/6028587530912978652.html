Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Most Luxurious Snack Baskets

19 Nov 2019 by Chelsea Davis

Everyone loves a gift basket full of delicious, sweet, salty and crunchy snacks to munch on. No matter what the occasion, these gift baskets are uneatable when it comes to wowing friends and family who love eating, and snacking, well. From imported caviar and black truffles to freshly baked cookies and chocolate-dipped fruits, these baskets have something enticing to satisfy everyone’s cravings.

Gourmet Entertainer’s Gift Basket: This massive gift basket is pilled high with both sweet and savory snacks that showcase the best of what Harry & David is famous for. Savory goodies include white cheddar and gouda cheeses, Thuringer and hickory smoked summer sausages, Milano salami, pepper and onion relish, lemon dill smoked salmon, antipasti, and more. To balance it all out, the basket features is an equally impressive assortment of sweet gourmet treats, including milk chocolate Moose Munch bark, a whole Chocolate Decadence Cake, premium chocolate truffles, clover honey, and more.

The Caviar Co. Cooler Gift Set: For your fanciest friend gift them a luxe caviar gift set from The Caviar Co. These exceptional gift sets come with everything from Mother of Pearl Spoons to blini and are sure to impress anyone who likes the finer things in life. The Ultimate Caviar Flight comes with three one oz. jars of any caviar of your choice; 30 French cocktail blini; Crème Fraîche; three Mother of Pearl Caviar spoons; and more. Enjoy trying out a variety of top domestic, imported and exported caviars. If you really want to go all out, gift a caviar subscription that deliveries between two and four shipments a year.

Cypress Grove Cheese, The Entertainer: This gift basket is the perfect party starter. With eight cheese plus Mango Habanero and Raspberry Habanero jams, flatbread crackers from three fantastic Northern California specialty food artisans, it’s a gourmet set that will have everyone pleased. For those with a sweet tooth there’s also a Madagascar Dark Milk Chocolate bar made of rich blend of Humboldt County dairy and Madagascar cacao.

Cured's Grand Cru Gift Box: This gift box is expertly curated with the finest, most decadent and indulgent treats from around the world. Some standout items you’ll be hard pressed to find anywhere else include handblown Zalto Bordeaux glasses with bowls shaped in accordance with the biodynamic angles of the earth; and a 30 gram black truffle, fresh out of the ground from Perigord, France. From Rodolfe Le Meunier Beurre de Barratte Cultured Butter and La Quercia Acorn Edition Jamon to truffle honey and Dick Taylor Toledo Belize Drinking Chocolate, this gift is overflowing with top notch goodies that will have anyone drooling.

Blue Diamond Deluxe Sweet & Savory: Enjoy wholesome, healthy almonds in all the most delicious ways with this gift basket! This show-stopper is filled with a variety of savory almond cans including Smokehouse, Sriracha, Wasabi & Soy Sauce, Sweet Thai Chilli and Habanero BBQ. On the sweet side enjoy a tasty selection of pastel jordan almonds and a selection of cream covered almonds in flavors like lemon and orange. Perfect for the when the snack cravings hit!

Grand Christmas Gift Basket: Get as festive as can be with this Christmas basket filled with sweets. Along with Harry & David’s famously juicy Royal Riviera Pears comes a lovely selection of holiday goodies that include milk chocolate Moose Munch Premium Popcorn, velvety signature truffles and chocolate cherries. Inside are also baked desserts that include an indulgent cinnamon swirl and dense, rich fruitcake.

Holiday Party Snack Gift: This festive basket comes with all things sweet, salty and delicious. Sweets featured include two types of hand-crafted popcorn in holiday flavors; a tin of traditional Italian Amaretti cookies; and chocolate covered cherries. The savory selection includes artisan cracker crisps; a smoked salmon fillet; olive and herb dip; vegan fig salami; cheese straws; and a trio of salty, sweet and spicy hazelnuts. This incredible selection feeds up to 20 people and is customizable if you want to add any surprises!

La Tavola Gift Box: This classy gift box features the best of Enzo Olive Oil's estate including organic olive oils, balsamic vinegar, jams, pestos and nuts. Everything featured is grown right on the property in California's San Joaquin Valley by 4th-generation farmers making this the ultimate gift for lovers of true farm-to-table fare. From almonds and dried nectarines to a variety of oils and spreads, it’s a great gift for your favorite host.

Pacific Pantry Set: Send the best of the Pacific Northwest with this luxury gift basket. Filled with a gourmet assortment of treats from the coast, this gift includes a selection of preserves and dips, plantains, hazelnuts, salami, salmon, coffee, tea and more. Each item is handcrafted in Oregon or Washington with all natural ingredients. This gift is also customizable in case you want to add any additional goodies to the set.

The Foodie Gift Box: This is a great gift for your foodie friend who loves to graze on classy bites. Inside this mouth watering box of treats is spicy maple syrup, bourbon caramel, nuts, cookies, honey, coasters and coffee beans making it a well rounded surprise for any gift giving occasion. Teak and Twine is a company that makes specially curated, thoughtful gifts based out of Northern Virginia.
