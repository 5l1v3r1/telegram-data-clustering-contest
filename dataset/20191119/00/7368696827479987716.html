Half-Life: Alyx Officially Announced – Valve Will Unveil it This Week

19 Nov 2019 by Sillicur

A few hours ago, at roughly 02:00 this morning (19 November) the official Valve Twitter account suddenly woke up and revealed that the developer’s next Half-Life game is their flagship VR title, Half-Life: Alyx. In October this year, we did report that Valve’s next Half-Life game could be a flagship VR title and now, we know for sure.

Finally, we will see a new Half-Life game from Valve and although a VR title is probably not what a lot of fans were hoping for, it is still quite exciting to know that something new is actually happening. You won’t have to wait long either, as Valve will reveal Half-Life: Alyx to the world this Thursday, 21 November at 20:00 SAST.
