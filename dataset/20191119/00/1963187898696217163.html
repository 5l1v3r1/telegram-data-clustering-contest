Know the countries qualified to the 2020 EURO

19 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today

There are 19 teams that have been confirmed to reach the 2020 EURO tournament, with five remaining to be known.

On Tuesday, we will see 10 matches in the final round of European qualifiers.

Euro 2020 will be held for the first time in 12 different countries, with the semi-finals and the final in July next year.

The teams that qualified for Euro 2020 are:

Austria

Belgium

Croatia

Czech Republic

Denmark

England

Finland

France

Germany

Italia

Netherlands

Poland

Portugal

Russia

Spain

Sweden

Switzerland

Turkey

Ukraine

Hungary, Wales and Slovakia are battling for second place in Group E.

Eight teams play the play-offs to see the quartet complete the 24 teams.
