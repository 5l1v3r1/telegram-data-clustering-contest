BoG boss loses dad

19 Nov 2019

The Governor of the Bank of Ghana, Dr Ernest Yedu Addison, is bereaved.

He has lost his dad.

Joseph Atta Addison died on Saturday, November 16 after a short illness.

He was 97 years.

Mr Addison was one time a Deputy Director and Engineer-in-Chief of the erstwhile Posts and Telecommunications Department.

He was also the father of renowned lawyer Philip Addison.

Funeral arrangements will be announced later, 3news.com gathers.
