Gift Guide 2019: Best Friend Gift Ideas For Every Type Of Personality

19 Nov 2019 by Felicity Carter

Holiday shopping for friends can be tricky, but here is some inspiration when it comes to buying a gift for your bestie, no matter their personality. From J. Crew’s cozy cashmere hats to Keith Haring-inspired candles, here are the top picks for your pals.

Charmed & Chained Rotary Phone Pendant

For all of those friends who need a little reminder to call, this sweet necklace from Charmed & Chained will do the trick.

Made from 14kt yellow gold, this pendant features a rotary phone which is topped off with round blue sapphires and diamonds. The pendant design was originally crafted in the ’60s, so it suits all those friends who are into vintage accessories, too.

FLOWERBX Flower Subscription

FLOWERBX supplies the stylish crowd with fashionable stems and, thanks to this subscription service, they can supply your friend, too. Designed to celebrate the best of the seasonal flowers, they offer various types and colors to suit any of your friends’ aesthetic. Subscriptions are available weekly, fortnightly or monthly. Set a scheduled delivery to your bestie as a routine reminder of how much you value their friendship.

Old Dutch International Duracopper Cocktail Shaker

This cocktail set consists of the five essential pieces to get the party started. Made of duracopper-finished stainless steel, the set is as stylish as it is functional and durable (it won’t tarnish). Perfect for cocktail connoisseurs, they’ll love how these look, whether they’re sitting in an office or on a cocktail trolley.

Alighieri Moonlight Dance Gold-Plated Hair Pin

If your friend has medium to long hair, then this 24k gold-plated hair pin by London-based brand Alighieri is ideal. A part of the “No More Tears” line, it is inspired by Dante’s fifth canto of Purgatorio–a nod to Montefeltro’s Roman tear catchers. Curved in shape, it has a textured bar to secure the hair in place. This piece is perfect for literature-loving pals with long locks.

Highgrove Cuvée Champagne & 2 Champagne Flutes Gift Set

For the regal friend, this is brought to you by Highgrove Estate, the home of The Prince of Wales. The beautifully presented set consists of a bottle of Highgrove’s crisp and elegant Cuvée Champagne, along with a pair of crystalline glasses.

The champagne is made in the Marne district of France exclusively for Highgrove by Laurent Perrier and is a combination of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir and Pinot Meunier grapes. As for the flutes, they are made specially for Highgrove from lead-free crystalline glass. They are also the glasses used at royal events at Prince Charles’s Gloucestershire home in England.

Anissa Kermiche Popotin Ceramic Vase

London-based jewelry designer Anissa Kermiche has launched a range of covetable homewares. This statement vase comes in the shade of dark gold and is Kermiche’s very own ode to womanhood with its silhouette that mimics the female form. It also features a textured finish and edges to add further dimension.

Ligne Blanche Running Heart Candle

This scented candle combines notes of cinnamon, caramel, apple and vanilla for the ultimate fall fragrance. Made in France by Ligne Blanche, it features American artist Keith Haring’s famous, red running-heart motif. The candle itself has been created by the renowned Swiss producer of fine fragrances, Givaudan for a gift that is both classy and cool.

J. Crew Cashmere Beanie In Heart Dot

Show your friend that you care with this super cozy cashmere beanie. Black all over with white hearts and dots it will be a sure-fire hit when the temperature drops. Pair it with J. Crew’s matching scarf and gloves to complete the full look.
