See Jailed Fraudster Who Coordinated $1m Internet Scam From Prison (Photos)

19 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

EFCC Makes Shocking Revelation

The Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) has accused an internet fraudster, Hope Olusegun Aroke of coordinating internet scams worth over $1m while serving his jail term.

The EFCC Lagos Zonal office said Aroke who is currently serving a 24-years jail term for internet fraud was still able to maintain contacts with other fraudsters outside the prison and pull off the massive fraud successfully.

The accomplices have also been declared wanted by the EFCC for various offences relating to computer fraud and money laundering.
