John Mayer Gushes Over Ex-Girlfriend Taylor Swift’s Music A Decade After Their Break Up – See The Clip!

19 Nov 2019, 00:21 by Ashley Mitchell

Even though their relationship was short-lived and ended a decade ago, it looks like there is only love between exes Taylor Swift and John Mayer – at least as far as he’s concerned! During an IG Live session with Shawn Mendes yesterday, John had only good things to say about his former girlfriend.

The man praised Taylor’s music in particular and the compliment comes at the best time considering the singer’s drama with Scooter Braun.

John started by sharing his great opinion of her latest single titled ‘Lover’ since Taylor and Shawn collabed on a remix of it.

But while he stressed that he simply ‘loved the song’ John could not help but poke fun at some of the lyrics.

It was more of a relatable joke, however, with no malicious intent on his part!

‘I get a little chuckle because she says, ‘We can keep the Christmas lights up until January,’ and I go…you’re insane. Everyone keeps their Christmas lights up until January!’ he exclaimed. He’s not wrong, though!
