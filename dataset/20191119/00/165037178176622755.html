Benítez a candidate to replace Pellegrini

19 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The 'Mirror' are stating that Benítez, who is currently in charge of Dalian Yifang in China, could succeed Manuel Pellegrini at West Ham.

West Ham's bad run of form has led the board to consider a replacement. Manuel Pellegrini has lot support and that's why the Hammers have possible replacements in mind.

According to the 'Mirror', the London outfit would look favourably on Rafa Benítez. The Premier League legend, who managed Liverpool and Newcastle, is currently managing in China after ending his spell with the Magpies.

The Spaniard currently earns around 15 million euros a year with Dalian Yifang, but he would accept a pay cut to move to the Premier League.

The English source recalls that in 2015 he almost joined the Hammers, but in the end, after his spell at Madrid, he ended up at Newcastle.

"I'm happy, it's not that I want to come back right now, but in the future, I will," insisted the Spanish manager.
