Visa launches festive season campaign

19 Nov 2019 by The Citizen Reporter

Dar es Salaam. Global payment technology, Visa has today launched a festive season campaign which will last for two month in Tanzania with special focus on Dar es Salaam shopping malls with the  aim of easing  shopping experience during the holidays.

This they say will relieve people from the burden of moving around with bundles of cash something that poses a security risk to them.

Speaking at the launch of the campaign, Visa country manager for Tanzania Olive Njoroge said the campaign aims to go beyond promoting the use of cards.

“The service is convenience and safety as they make purchase during the holiday,” said Njoroge

The campaign will run from November 8 to December 22 this year, whereby users are encouraged to participate so that they can win a wide range of prizes.  

“Customer that make payments using the visa card on mobile stand a chance of winning a range of prizes,” he said
