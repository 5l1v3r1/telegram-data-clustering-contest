Kawhi Leonard (knee) misses third straight game for Clippers

19 Nov 2019, 00:24 by ESPN

LA Clippers forward Kawhi Leonard was ruled out for Monday's game against the Oklahoma City Thunder, the team announced.

Leonard suffered a left knee contusion in the second half of the Clippers' loss to the Houston Rockets on Wednesday and did not play against New Orleans on Thursday or Atlanta on Saturday.

Leonard has missed five of the Clippers' 14 games this season and has yet to play alongside teammate Paul George, who made his Clippers debut on Thursday after rehabbing from two shoulder operations.

George has helped mitigate Leonard's absence, scoring 70 points in 44 minutes in the past two games.

Leonard has been effective as well, when he has been on the court, averaging 26.8 points per game to go with a career-high six assists.
