EastEnders spoilers: Lisa Fowler vows to avenge Mel's death and take down Sharon

EastEnders' Lisa Fowler vows to expose Sharon Mitchell in the fallout to Mel Owen's tragic death on the BBC soap

19 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Charlotte Tutton

EastEnders spoilers have teased Lisa Fowler will set out to avenge the death of her best friend Mel Owen, as she's disbelieved over her claims.

Lisa dropped the bombshell on Phil Mitchell last week that he wasn't the father of his wife Sharon's unborn baby.

Mel had told Lisa shortly before her death about Sharon having an affair - while she didn't tell her who the father was.

Viewers know that Keanu Taylor is the father of her unborn baby, with Mel blackmailing the pair over their secret right up until her death.

 

Next week, as it's revealed Phil disbelieves ex Lisa's claims about the baby, she sets out to avenge Mel.

Lisa is determined to expose Sharon's baby secret to her family, blaming this over Mel's tragic demise.

Mel was in a car chase with Sharon, both heading for the airport to get to Phil first - Mel wanted to tell him about the baby, with Sharon keen to silence her.

But Mel crashed her car, with Sharon managing to save her only for her to walk into the path of an incoming lorry.

Lisa accused Sharon of murder working out where they were headed and what the chase was about.

But Lisa, who has a history of battling mental illness, leaves her pregnant daughter Louise concerned fearing she is off her medication.

 

Sharon is left feeling guilty when Louise's fiancé Keanu heads round there and admits her concerns over Lisa being unwell again.

The pressure is on for the former lovers, as they remain desperate to hide their affair and baby secret knowing full well it's making Lisa appear unwell in the process.

Will one of them be forced to admit the truth, and what will it mean for Lisa?

Viewers will have to tune in to see how far the character will go to expose the truth for Mel and for her own sake.

EastEnders airs Mondays and Fridays at 8pm, and Tuesdays and Thursdays at 7:30pm on BBC One.
