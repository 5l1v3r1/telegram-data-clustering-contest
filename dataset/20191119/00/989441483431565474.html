Ethiopia shocked Cote d’Ivoire in African cup of nations qualifying match

19 Nov 2019

borkena

Walia, Ethiopian National Team, has shocked Cote d’Ivoire National team (the elephants) on Tuesday in African Cup of Nations qualifying match.

Cote d’Ivoire scored first but team Ethiopia scored after ten minutes when Surafel Bekele skillfully converted a free kick outside the penalty box to an amazing goal.

Team Ethiopia sealed the game in a 2-1 victory as team Ethiopia’s key player, Shimeles Bekele, managed to score after dribbling the ball and tricking Cote d’Ivoire defenders within the penalty box.
