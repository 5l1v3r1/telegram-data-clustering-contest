Line-up for Neighbourhood Weekender music festival announced

19 Nov 2019

The indie music festival will take place in Warrington in May next year.

Indie rock group Catfish And The Bottlemen and former Stone Roses front man Ian Brown will headline the Neighbourhood Weekender musical festival, it has been announced.

The Warrington-based festival marks a first major solo headline performance for Brown in almost a decade, following the release of his album Ripples earlier this year.

Brown will be the main attraction on day one of the festival while Catfish And The Bottlemen will headline day two.

Also performing on the weekend in May are acts including Sam Fender, Paul Heaton & Jacqui Abbott, Shed Seven, Miles Kane, The Coral and Tom Walker.

Well-known indie acts such as Sundara Karma, Reverend And The Makers and Pale Waves will also appear.

Neighbourhood Weekender is set to take place at Warrington’s Victoria Park on May 23 and 24 next year.

Tickets for Neighbourhood Weekender will go on sale on Friday.
