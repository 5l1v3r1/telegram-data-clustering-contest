Alabama 4-Star Freshman CB Will Enter Transfer Portal

19 Nov 2019, 00:58 by Alek Arend

Alabama football lost a four-star freshman on Monday night when four-star cornerback Scooby Carter announced he is entering his name in the NCAA transfer portal.

Carter was one of the top-100 prospects in the 2019 class, according to 247Sports’ Composite Rankings. He announced the news of his transfer on Twitter.

Here’s a bit of what he had to say in his announcement:

“First I would like to thank the University of Alabama and the coaches for helping me and allowing me to play the sport I love … But after talking with my family I believe it is best for me to rethink my decision and enter my name into the transfer portal.”
