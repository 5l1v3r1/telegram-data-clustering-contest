'Sarileru Neekevvaru': Teaser date unlocked - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

19 Nov 2019

The teaser for 'Sarileru Neekevvaru' is awaited like only a few teasers.  The date is finally out.  The much-awaited teaser will be unveiled on November 22.  The exact time will be revealed later this week.

The makers have revealed the date in a first-of-its-kind manner in Tollywood.  As the user hits on a tab on Twitter, the date gets unlocked, complete with a hashtag that comes ready-made automatically.

Directed by Anil Ravipudi, this Mahesh Babu comedy entertainer is a Sankranthi 2020 release.  Vijayashanthi and Rashmika Mandanna are playing key roles.
