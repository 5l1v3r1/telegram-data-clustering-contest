You need to see Ed Balls in this Christmas jumper immediately

19 Nov 2019, 00:02

Holly Willoughby and Ed Balls are leading celebrity support for Save The Children’s Christmas Jumper Day this year, and their efforts are stunning.

Some famous faces have slipped into something festive for a good cause.

This Morning presenter Holly modeled a Christmas pudding jumper for the campaign, complete with reindeer antlers.

Katie Piper, James Blunt, Great British Bake Off star Liam Charles and singer Kimberley Wyatt are also rocking some incredible outfits for Save The Children.

Singer James looked dashing in a red jumper covered with snowflakes

But special mention has to go to Ed Balls, whose Christmas jumper trumps all the others.

The Strictly Come Dancing star looked incredibly happy to be wearing a red jumper absolutely laden with multi-coloured baubles.

Meanwhile Good Morning Britain newsreader Kate Garraway, who is currently in the Australian jungle on I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here!, poses up in a jumper that reads ‘Sleigh whaaaat?’.

Love Island star Maura Higgins, DJ Nick Grimshaw, and singers Peter Andre and Una Healy also in on the act.

Maura looked cosy in an elf jumper while Nick accesorised his with Christmas lights.

Celebrity Juice host Keith Lemon’s jumper of course has his own face on it, wearing a Santa hat and a bandage around his hand just like the real thing.

Other Love Island stars Jack Fincham, Tommy Fury, Kem Cetinay, Eyal Booker and Ovie Soko also get in on the fun, and we’re loving Ovie’s ‘Santa 1’ hoodie.

Kate’s fellow I’m A Celeb camp mate Myles Stephenson gets a Christmas jumper shoot with the rest of his X Factor group Rak Su.

Michelle Heaton, Aston Merrygold, Vicky Pattison, Dr Ranj and Chris Hughes are also a part of the campaign.

Save The Children’s Christmas Jumper Day takes place on December 13. To donate, head here.
