Vietnamese Snack Food Cafe Restaurant

Chilled-out Vietnamese restaurant in JLT

19 Nov 2019 by Time Out Dubai staff

Time Out Says

If you want excellent Vietnamese food there’s really only one place to go in Dubai – and that’s JLT. Teaming with excellent indie restaurants the area is already home to Hanoi Naturally and Vietnamese Foodies, our Best Budget Restaurant 2019.

Vietnamese Snack Food Cafe Restaurant has a menu almost as long as its name. And there are loads of interesting dishes, all at reasonable prices, plus a decent lunch deal for Dhs49. Heads up vegans – there’s also a dedicated plant-based menu.

We can’t help but try the classic chicken pho as a benchmark. It comes in small and large portions, with the former a pocket-friendly Dhs21. The silky broth sings with star anise and spices – it’s fragrant and delicious.

Prawn skewers are equally tasty, marinated in a creative concoction of spices. However, our other choices don’t fare so well. The vegan summer rolls aren’t the fresh, healthy dish we imagine – the chewy rice paper stuffed with soggy boiled carrots, is akin to school dinners, while the coconut prawn clay pot is stodgy and greasy. Our advice? Stick to the stand-out pho.

Some dishes shine, but to challenge others in the area, it’ll need to up its game.
