Huawei Watch GT 2 and FreeBuds 3 will launch in India in two weeks

19 Nov 2019 by Peter

Along with the new Mate flagships, Huawei unveiled the Watch GT 2 and the FreeBuds 3 last month. These two will be launching in India soon – sources have told us that they will be available in the first week of December.

We reviewed the FreeBuds 3 and were pretty happy with them – they are competitively priced with ANC and USB-C wireless charging.

Anyway, according to leakster Ishan Agarwal the Huawei Watch GT 2 will be available in both versions – 46mm and 42mm.

By the way, Huawei had plans to bring the Kirin 990 chipset to India, but there’s nothing yet. The Mate 30 flagships seem unlikely, but perhaps one of the 990-powered mid-rangers may make an appearance (the rumor mill is tracking the Huawei nova 6 and the Honor V30).
