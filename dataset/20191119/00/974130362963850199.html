Where To Watch Wales Vs Hungary? Irish TV Details For Euro 2020 Qualifier

19 Nov 2019 by Balls Team

Wales play Hungary in Cardiff on Tuesday, November 19th in a game which will probably decide who Ireland face in the Euro 2020 playoffs in March.

If Wales win, it will likely result in Ireland playing away to Slovakia in the playoff semi-finals. If the game ends in a draw and Slovakia win away to Azerbaijan, then Ireland will play Wales.

If Hungary win this evening's game, it becomes far more complex. Bosnia & Herzegovina are another possible opponent for Mick McCarthy's side.

What channel is Wales vs Hungary on?

In Ireland, you can watch the game on Virgin Media Two.

Coverage begins at 7pm.

Tommy Martin will be joined in studio by Kevin Kilbane and Brian Kerr with commentary from Gerry Armstrong and Dave McIntyre.

What times does Wales vs Hungary kick-off?

The game kicks off at 7:45pm.

It has also announced the Euro 2020 Play-Off draw will be live on Virgin Media Sport, this Friday from 11am where the Republic of Ireland’s opponents will be confirmed as well as deciding which nations will receive home advantage in the Euro 2020 Play-Off finals.
