Valve Returns to ‘Half-Life’ With New VR Game, ‘Half-Life: Alyx’!

19 Nov 2019 by Mike Wilson

While Valve still can’t quite count to three, they will be returning to the Half-Life series in the form of their first VR game! Half-Life: Alyx will be revealed at 10 am PT/1 pm ET on November 21. On top of that, according to a leaked transcript from a new interview, Alyx will be shown at this year’s Game Awards on December 12!
