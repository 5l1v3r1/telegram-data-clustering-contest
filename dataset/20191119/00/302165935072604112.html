'Path of Exile 2' gears up for battle of the titans (VIDEO)

19 Nov 2019, 00:40

SAN FRANCISCO, Nov 19 ― After Diablo 4 was announced at its studio's BlizzCon expo on November 1, genre fellow Path of Exile 2 was unveiled at ExileCon on November 16. The combat-oriented role-playing adventure is free and due in 2020.

Impressive action role-playing game Path of Exile has never hidden its connection to the formative Diablo series.

Plunging heroes into a relentlessly grim world filled with horrible creatures and no small amount of resultant viscera, it's not just cursed beasts that fall to player actions, but, also in keeping Diablo urban myth, mouse or gamepad buttons too, it's said, such is the continued intensity of combat.

Not only has Path of Exile excelled visually, but it also gave players an incredibly broad array of skills to choose between. That helped keep players engaged, trying out new setups and sharing them among the enthusiastic community.
