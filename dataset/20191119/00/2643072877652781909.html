Breaking: Corpses Burnt As Fire Razes OAU Morgue

19 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

The fire incident which happened on Tuesday at the morgue of Anatomy Department of Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife, Osun State has left the environs in disarray.

According to report making rounds, about ten corpses were burnt in the fire incident which took place in the early hours of Tuesday.

The incident was confirmed by the spokesperson of the Osun Police Command, Folasade Odoro. She added that investigation has been launched to determine the cause of the fire.
