Tough Mudder announces Worlds Toughest Mudder and Toughest Mudder Locations for 2020

19 Nov 2019 by Bonnie Wilson

At the 2019 World's Toughest Mudder Awards banquet, it was announced that the 2020 edition of World's Toughest Mudder will take place on November 7th and 8th, 2020 in Dallas Texas at TexPlex Park.

WTM features athletes from around the world competing to see how many laps of a 5-mile course they can complete in 24 hours.

TexPlex Park is located half an hour outside of Dallas on US-67 and is home to a sprawling ATV Park that features 9 different tracks for Supercross, Off-Road, and ATV Vehicles. It has hosted Tough Mudder events in the past and will be hosting a Tough Mudder the weekend before WTM.

Registration opens November 19, 2019.

Tough Mudder also annouced the locations for the 2020 Toughest Mudder Series, in which athletes take on a 5-mile loop of 20 obstacles for 12 hours, from midnight to midnight.

Los Angeles: April 4th and April 5th, 2020

Philadephia: May 16th and 17th, 2020

Europe's Toughest Mudder @ Midlands: June 13th and 14th, 2020

Chicago August 22nd and 23rd, 2020
