Atiku Mourns Death Of Close Personal Aide

19 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Atiku Loses Close Personal Aide

Alhaji Umar Pariya, better known as “Mr. Pee” a longstanding aide to former Vice President of Nigeria, Atiku Abubakar is dead.

He died on Tuesday, November 19 and has since been buried in accordance with Islamic rite.

A statement by the Atiku Media Office which was signed by Paul Ibe describes the death of the former Vice President’s Personal Assistant as sad and painful.

“He (Pariya) was a committed loyalist, a strong pillar of support in (my) life’s journeys and struggles in good, rough and uncertain times.

“He gave a good account of his service to the nation and association with my humble self, right from our days in the Department of Customs and Excise spanning over three decades.

He showed love and solidarity to me and my family and in the process became a member of my family”, the statement quoted Atiku as saying.

The former Vice President takes solace in the reality that Allah gives life and determines when He calls whomever He created.

“I commiserate with his wives, children and his friends; people who have benefitted from his kind-hearted nature. He was dear to many in high and low places. I will greatly miss him!”, Atiku said.

He prays to Almighty Allah to forgive Umar Pariya his sins and grant him Aljannah Firdaus.
