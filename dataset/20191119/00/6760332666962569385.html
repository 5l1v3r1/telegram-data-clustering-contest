RGV announces 'Dadas Of Hyderabad' - Telugu News - IndiaGlitz.com

19 Nov 2019

Ram Gopal Varma has announced a new project.  It's a film titled 'Dadas Of Hyderabad'.  Sandeep Madhav of 'Vangaveeti' and 'George Reddy' fame will be the hero.  

"Just signed Sandeep Madhav for DADAS OF HYDERABAD.  After finishing the rowdies of Vijayawada and factionists in Rayalaseema, I am making a film on the DADAs of the ’80s in Hyderabad.  The story is based on a real-life character, which was my inspiration for the character of SHIVA," RGV said, reminding us of 'Shiva' for the millionth time in his career.

RGV awaits the release of the political fiction 'Kamma Rajyamlo Kadapa Reddlu' on November 29.
