Kamal Haasan's massive contribution for the youth! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

19 Nov 2019

Ulaga nayagan Kamal Haasan celebrated his 65th birthday on November 7, and this year he's also completing  60 years in the film industry and hence celebrated his birthday in a grand manner.

In a three day celebration, Kamal Haasan unveiled a statue of his father Srinivasan in his native Paramakudi, and his mentor K Balachander's statue in the new RKFI office and screened Hey Ram on eve of 150th year of Gandhi.

In the Ungal Naan event held on Sunday, Kamal Haasan contributed Rs 1 crore from the money collected from the event, for building a skill development center "Skill to Scale Tamilnadu" in Paramakudi. Superstar Rajinikanth and Ilaiyaraja were present in the occasion.
