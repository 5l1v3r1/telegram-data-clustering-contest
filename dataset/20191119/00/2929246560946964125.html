Australia v Pakistan: Key battles

Feature

19 Nov 2019

The two-match Test series between Australia and Pakistan begins on 21 November in Brisbane. Here are some of the key contests to look forward to.

Mohammad Abbas v David Warner

Warner had an Ashes series to forget in England this year, where he aggregated only 95 runs in 10 innings. He's now back to his fluent best, scoring a century in his first game for New South Wales in the Sheffield Shield last month, and following that up with 298 runs from six innings while being dismissed only once across two T20I series against Sri Lanka and Pakistan at home. He has a chance to extend that form against one of his favourite opponents, against whom he's scored 595 runs from nine innings, including three hundreds.
