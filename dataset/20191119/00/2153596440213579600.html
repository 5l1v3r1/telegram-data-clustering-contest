Save your business money by moving to VOIPconnect

19 Nov 2019 by Partner

Many South African businesses are still using traditional phone lines, which means they are losing out on the benefits of next-generation VoIP services.

VoIP and cloud communications offer companies high-quality voice services at a fraction of the cost of PSTN or mobile calls.

For companies looking to migrate from traditional Telkom lines, Connection Telecom’s VOIPconnect product is a logical choice.

Excellent pricing

VOIPconnect offers companies a reliable and high-quality voice service with competitive pricing and free inter-company and inter-community calls.

Apart from cost savings and convenience, companies also get a real-time view of call activity and can prevent potential call abuse.

This service works over numerous connectivity methods – including fibre, wireless, and fixed-LTE – and delivers exceptional voice quality.

Businesses will also enjoy excellent security and lower infrastructure costs thanks to Connection Telecom’s hosted platform in Teraco’s data centres.

To move your service to Connection Telecom is quick and easy. Furthermore, companies have the option to keep their existing numbers or use new non-geographic and geographic numbers.
