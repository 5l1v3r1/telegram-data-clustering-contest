Valve officially announces Half-Life: Alyx VR game, reveal coming Thursday

19 Nov 2019, 00:18 by Emily Gera

No longer just a rumour.

Valve has officially announced a new addition to its Half-Life franchise – unfortunately, it’s not the one you were hoping for. Half-Life: Alyx was confirmed by the company today in a tweet, after previously being mentioned by Valve’s Robin Walker within a leaked transcript.
