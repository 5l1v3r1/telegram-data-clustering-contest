New DC Dhruva Jyoti Das of Biswanath district assumes Office

19 Nov 2019, 09:03

A Correspondent

Biswanath Chariali: The newly-appointed Deputy Commissioner of Biswanath district, Dhruva Jyoti Das assumed office on Monday. Prior to this job, he had served as the Director of Inland Water Transport and also Additional Secretary to the Government of Assam, Transport Department. After assuming office, he sought help and cooperation from everybody in running the district administration smoothly.
