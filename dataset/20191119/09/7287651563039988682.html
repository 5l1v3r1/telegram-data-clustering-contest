Review: Fractal Design ION+ Platinum 660 and 860W PSU

19 Nov 2019, 09:40 by Hilbert Hagedoorn

We’re putting the new Fractal Design ION+ Platinum power supply on our test bench today. It’s a fresh series in the offer of this Swedish company, as it was introduced at the end of July this year. As the name suggests, it does have 80 Plus Platinum Certification, which means that it’s 92% efficient at 50% load @ 115 V, and even 94% efficient @ 230 V.
