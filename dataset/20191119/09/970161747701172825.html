Kanye West ‘drops $14million on Wyoming ranch’ but Kim Kardashian 'isn't a fan'

19 Nov 2019, 09:11

Kanye West has ‘splashed out $14.4million (£11.1m) on another ranch in Wyoming’ – despite wife Kim Kardashian ‘not being all that keen’ on moving down there.

Just months after shelling out $14million (£10.8m) on a huge property in the area, the Jesus Is King rapper is back at it again, and has reportedly dropped serious cash on another place.

Well, if you’ve got the money…

According to TMZ, the 42-year-old rapper has treated himself to Bighorn Mountain Ranch, which spans 6,713 acres, and comes complete with several log cabins and two heated helipads.

Because one heated helipad just won’t do?

Not only that, there are lodges dotted around and walk-in saunas, with the ranch surrounded by rolling hills, and deer, antelope and turkey casually roaming the grounds.

That’s going to be a housewarming party with a difference.

However, it has been claimed that Kim, 39, isn’t really that into the ‘simple housing’ that comes with the ranch, and won’t be making a permanent move from LA to Wyoming just yet.

‘Kim is definitely not moving and living full-time in Wyoming right now. Kim likes visiting Wyoming, but she sees it more like a vacation place,’ an insider claimed. ‘It’s very quiet and private.

‘Every time they go, they enjoy it. But they are usually comfortable in luxury housing. The ranch only has simple housing. It’s mostly cabins with no amenities at all.

‘Kim just isn’t a fan of the cabins. There are many things that need to happen before she and the kids can spend more time there. Kim has a whole list of things. They would need to build a house so everyone can be comfortable.

‘And now, when everyone knows Kanye bought the ranch, there needs to be an elaborate security system put in.’

So Ye’s just dropped $14million for a holiday home?

Kim hasn’t ruled out the idea of leaving LA for good, explaining that she could see herself and their brood – children North, Saint, Chicago and Psalm – in Wyoming in the next decade.

During a chat with the Gold Digger rapper, he asked where she wants to be in the next 10 years.

‘In my mind I’m already living in those 10 years. I see us living on a ranch in Wyoming, occasionally going to Palm Springs and our home in Los Angeles – and becoming a lawyer,’ she replied.

The Kardashian-West’s Take Wyoming, anyone?

Metro.co.uk has contacted reps for Kim and Kanye for a comment.
