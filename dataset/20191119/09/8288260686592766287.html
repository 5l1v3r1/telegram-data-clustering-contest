'It is time' - Manchester United handed major injury boost ahead of Sheffield United clash

Manchester United latest news | Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's side travel to Sheffield United and then host Aston Villa in their next two matches in the Premier League

19 Nov 2019, 09:38 by Richard Cusack

Former Aston Villa defender Axel Tuanzebe could return to fitness for Manchester United's Premier League clash with his old side next month.

Villa travel to Old Trafford on December 1 (4.30pm) in their next away trip in the top flight and will be hoping to end a run of three-straight away defeats on the road.

And last season's Villa loan star Tuanzebe has issued a fitness update as he edges close to a return from a hip injury.

Tuanzebe made 35 appearances for Dean Smith's side last season, helping the Villa Park side to promotion through the Championship's play-offs.
