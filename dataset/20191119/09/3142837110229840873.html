Pangaea Logistics Solutions Ltd (NASDAQ:PANL) Declares Quarterly Dividend of $0.04

19 Nov 2019, 09:04 by Teresa Graham

Pangaea Logistics Solutions Ltd (NASDAQ:PANL) announced a quarterly dividend on Thursday, November 7th, RTT News reports. Stockholders of record on Thursday, November 21st will be paid a dividend of 0.035 per share by the shipping company on Tuesday, December 3rd. This represents a $0.14 annualized dividend and a dividend yield of 4.44%. The ex-dividend date of this dividend is Wednesday, November 20th.

Shares of NASDAQ PANL opened at $3.15 on Tuesday. Pangaea Logistics Solutions has a 1-year low of $2.71 and a 1-year high of $4.35. The business has a 50-day moving average of $3.22 and a 200-day moving average of $3.33. The company has a debt-to-equity ratio of 0.62, a current ratio of 1.28 and a quick ratio of 1.08. The company has a market capitalization of $139.13 million, a price-to-earnings ratio of 5.94 and a beta of 0.30.

Pangaea Logistics Solutions (NASDAQ:PANL) last issued its earnings results on Thursday, November 7th. The shipping company reported $0.19 earnings per share for the quarter, missing the Thomson Reuters’ consensus estimate of $0.29 by ($0.10). Pangaea Logistics Solutions had a return on equity of 8.27% and a net margin of 4.03%. The company had revenue of $118.89 million during the quarter. Research analysts expect that Pangaea Logistics Solutions will post 0.6 EPS for the current fiscal year.

PANL has been the topic of several recent analyst reports. Zacks Investment Research upgraded Pangaea Logistics Solutions from a “sell” rating to a “hold” rating in a research note on Wednesday, October 16th. ValuEngine upgraded Pangaea Logistics Solutions from a “strong sell” rating to a “sell” rating in a research note on Wednesday, October 2nd.

About Pangaea Logistics Solutions

Pangaea Logistics Solutions, Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, provides seaborne dry bulk logistics and transportation services to industrial customers worldwide. The company offers various dry bulk cargoes, including grains, coal, iron ore, pig iron, hot briquetted iron, bauxite, alumina, cement clinker, dolomite, and limestone.
