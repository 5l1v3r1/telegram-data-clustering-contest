Guide: Cheapest Nintendo Switch And Switch Lite Console Deals

Update: ASDA and Currys kick off Black Friday early in the UK

19 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Nintendo Life Staff

So, you're in the market for buying a Nintendo Switch or Switch Lite are you? Well, you've come to the right place. If you're hoping to pick up a deal on a Switch console - Lite or otherwise - various bundles are beginning to crop up as the one-two punch of Black Friday and Cyber Monday creep up on us at the end of November.

In this guide we've assembled the best Nintendo Switch and Switch Lite bundles and deals we can find. It's slim pickings right now in the US, although that's sure to change in the coming days as retailers reveal their Black Friday deals. UK readers looking for a Switch bargain have a little more in terms of choice, with electronics retailer Currys offering a tasty Pokémon Sword or Shield console bundle which effectively throws in the game for free. Even better, ASDA has an in-store deal for a Nintendo Switch for just £239.99. Probably worth venturing into the real world for that one.

We'll be sure to update this page with the best Nintendo Switch and Switch Lite deals as and when they crop up.

One important thing to note is to make sure you're getting the Switch you want. The newer revision of the standard model has better battery life, and Nintendo itself has a Mario Kart bundle which contains the older model. If you're at all unsure about which is which, be sure to check out our guide which details the differences between the Switch models, including how to tell the consoles apart by their packaging (the newer one has more red on the box).
