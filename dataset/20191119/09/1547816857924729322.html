Bigg Boss 13: Sidharth Shukla Dances Closely With Arti Singh And Shefali Jariwala At The Pool Party, Shehnaaz Gill Seems Hurt

19 Nov 2019, 09:13 by Lubna Khan

Shehnaaz Gill and Paras Chhabra recently patched up on Bigg Boss 13 and are one of the most adorable duos on the show! Now, after watching Sidharth Shukla dance closely with Arti Singh and Shefali Jariwala at a pool party, Shehnaaz Gill seems majorly affected by it

Bigg Boss 13 has amped up its drama and entertainment factor and most of it revolves around Sidharth Shukla. He is always at the forefront of any fight or an important decision and is famously known for his fights with Rashami Desai in the house. Shehnaaz Gill and Sidharth Shukla, who wee great friends, had been fighting and drifting apart. However, they just recently patched up and were back to being friends again.  However, things are gradually changing inside the BB house. Now, after watching Sidharth Shukla dance closely with Arti Singh and Shefali Jariwala at a pool party, Shehnaaz Gill seems majorly affected by it.

On Bigg boss 3, a task was given where thieves of the house Devoleena Bhattacharjee and Shefali Jariwala had to rob the maximum number of other contestants' belongings. Shefali was crowned the winner of this task and as a reward, Bigg Boss announced a pool party for her, and she was allowed to choose five other contestants she would like to take with her. She chose Sidharth Shukla,Arti Singh, Asim Riaz, Himanshi Khurana and Hindustani Bhau.During the pool party, Sidharth enjoys dancing closely and flirting with Arti Singh and Shefali. Shehnaaz watches this from the other side of the house, and seems visibly upset and affected by it.
