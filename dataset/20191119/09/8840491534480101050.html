MAYO SPCA issues appeal over care of pets during cold snap

19 Nov 2019, 09:38

MAYO SPCA has issued an appeal after receiving complaints of pets being left outside during the cold weather snap..

It has called for pets to be kept in a warm place, protected from the freezing conditions.

The organisation said it was issuing the appeal as a result of concerns raised by members of the public who witnessed cases where this is not happening .
