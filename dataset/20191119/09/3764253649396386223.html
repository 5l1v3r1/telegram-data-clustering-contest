Techpreneur Victor Asemota shares personal experience as he speaks about forgiveness

19 Nov 2019, 09:14

- A Nigerian social media user, Victor Asemota, has used his platform to share words of encouragement with members of the online community

- The individual referenced some of his personal life struggles to drive home his point

- Asemota also approached the topic of forgiveness and the sacrifices he had to make before he married his wife

A Nigerian social media user with a verified account on micro messaging platform, Twitter, identified as Victor Asemota, recently flooded his timeline with several tweets, as he encouraged members of the internet community.

Asemota who is a techpreneur according to his Twitter bio, started his long ‘sermon’ by recounting how he was once in a bad financial situation, and how his life had taken a turn after he prayed to the lord.

He said his prayers had been answered by someone who had owed him some money in the past showing up, and paying his debt. This same individual also gave him a deal that turned his life around for good.
