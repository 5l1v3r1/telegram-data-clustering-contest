EastEnders arrest, Corrie fire and 18 more soap spoilers

19 Nov 2019, 09:00

There’s tough times ahead for some big soap characters, as Linda Carter (Kellie Bright) finds herself on the wrong side of the law in EastEnders, while Tyrone Dobbs (Alan Halsall) and Fiz Stape (Jennie McAlpine) worry about daughter Hope when she seemingly stars a fire in Coronation Street.

In Emmerdale, Bernice Blackstock (Samantha Giles) prepares for her wedding to Dr Liam Cavanagh (Jonny McPherson), while in Hollyoaks, Damon Kinsella (Jacob Roberts) is back in the village.

EastEnders

With Mick having to attend a doctor’s appointment, Linda agrees to to leave Ollie to school — in spite of the fact that she’s still a bit hungover. While at the school, she accidentally reverses her car into another, and refrains from telling anyone. However, unbeknown to her, someone was watching. Later, Mieko and Shelley wait on her — with two police officer’s in tow — and, after a breathalyser test, Linda is arrested.

Mick is determined to make his Wife Swap competition a success, and — as the day of said competition arrives — everyone awaits the outcome at The Queen Vic. However, Gray decides to take it upon himself to sabotage the results.

Martin’s problems continue, and when drama ensues in The Queen Vic, he makes a quick exit. However, Ruby is quick to follow him, and tells him that he can stay at her flat if he wishes. Martin’s emotional as a result, and her thanks her for being so supportive, and leans in to kiss her — however, he stops himself from doing so.

Coronation Street

Paul’s fuming with Bernie when he discovers that she’s reported Kel for sexual abuse. Kel’s arrested for burglary, but — during the interview — he’s shown the photos that were at his flat. As a result, and angry Kel later takes Bernie hostage at No. 5. However, Gemma and Paul come to the rescue, and Bernie promises to be a better mum.

Hope isn’t pleased when Fiz refuses to throw a fireworks party. However, with the young girl exhibiting worrying behaviour once more, Tyrone suggests that maybe they’d be better off doing as she wishes. Later, during the party, Kevin and Tyrone come to the realisation that the back yard is on fire — and Hope is evidently amused as she watches the flames.

Richard and Nina pay a visit to Weatherfield to see where Roy works. However, Richard’s tired and frail afterwards, and Nina blames Roy for the setback. Later, Roy looks after Richard — and realises just how hard it must be for Nina to do this on a regular basis.

Emmerdale

Determined to get revenge on Graham, Kim implies to Marlon that her ex-lover shouldn’t be around Leo. Rhona berates her for interfering, but little does she know Kim’s planning something big. Later, Graham receives a text from Kim saying she needs to speak to her employees in the Woolpack. However, when Graham arrives, he knows what’s about to happen. Kim reveals a shocking secret about Graham — and Rhona’s floored as a result.

Afterwards, Graham attempts to damage control, and thus he heads over to speak to Rhona. However, he’s not given much opportunity to do so, as Marlon intervenes and punches him as a result.

The week of Bernice and Liam’s wedding arrives, and Dee Dee informs her mum that she’s decided to start school in Emmerdale as a wedding gift. At the hen do, Bernice’s kids are mortified when a stripper shows up, but — during the celebrations — Bernice misses a call. Meanwhile Dee Dee takes a call — and she makes a shocking revelation.

Hollyoaks

Sylver and Grace spend quite a bit of time together, and Breda is disgusted when Grace enters the living room in nothing more than her dressing gown — with Sylver’s tool belt as an accompaniment. Worried about how Sylver’s actions will look to the police, Breda asks her son to choose between his family and Grace.

After Luke disappears without telling her where he’s off to, Cindy becomes suspicious and decides to check out his laptop. However, Joel spies an opportunity and thus hides the device, promising to return it to her if she helps him out in the soup kitchen.

Damon returns to the village — as does Maxine — and the pair set their sights upon one another for the first time since his sentencing. However, with Liberty flirting with Damon, Maxine begins to believe that they’re dating.
