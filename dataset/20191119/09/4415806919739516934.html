International Men’s Day: 6 celebs who’ve helped redefine masculinity in 2019

19 Nov 2019, 09:38

It’s International Men’s Day, 2019. A day to discuss important male issues – male mental health, damaging male stereotypes, the alarmingly high rate of male suicide – it’s also a day to celebrate the people doing so all year round.

These public figures are changing the conversation around masculinity, and redefining the phrase ‘man up’…

1. Gareth Thomas for his continuous openness and honesty about being a gay sportsman
