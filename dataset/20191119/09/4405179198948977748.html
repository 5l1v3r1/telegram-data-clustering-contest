Fancy a new Mac mini? You don't have to pay full price! We've scoured the web for the best deals on a Mac mini so you can save money on a new Mac.

When you are looking for a good deal on a new Mac, the first thing to do is to check what the price would be if you bought from Apple, rather than assuming that the reseller selling you a Mac is quoting the correct price. Also, remember that once a new model is launched Apple usually sells it for the same price as the previous generation, so, with the price of a new model in mind, a small discount on an old model may not look so attractive, this is why we always quote the price that Apple sells the equivalent model for today.

It's also worth noting how old a particular model is. It's more than a year since Apple updated the Mac mini, so we could be seeing an update soon, and if new models arrive you might find even better discounts on the older models.

However, we'd advise you avoid doing so right now as the previous range of Mac mini models dates back to 2014. Apple went for a number of years without updating the Mac mini.
