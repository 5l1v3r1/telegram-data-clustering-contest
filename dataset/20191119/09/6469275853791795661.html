Jeremy Corbyn pays tribute to Lancashire NHS campaigner who has died of cancer days after comparing Chorley Hospital to Beirut

19 Nov 2019, 09:34 by Matthew Calderbank

Jeremy Corbyn has paid tribute to a Labour campaigner who died days after likening Chorley Hospital to "Beirut".

Jayne Rae, 54, from Whittle-le-Woods, near Chorley, died on Monday morning after a year battling terminal breast cancer.
