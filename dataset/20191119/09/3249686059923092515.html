Cosmic Girls grant otherworldly wishes in 'As You Wish' MV

19 Nov 2019, 09:05 by germainej

Cosmic Girls are granting otherworldly wishes in their music video for "As You Wish".

In the whimsical MV, the Cosmic Girls receive wishes from the people of Earth and process them for granting. "As You Wish" is the title track of the girl group's seventh mini album of the same name, and it's about hoping someone's wish comes true.
