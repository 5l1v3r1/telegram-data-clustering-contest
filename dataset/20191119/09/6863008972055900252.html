Ukrainian soldier wounded amid 14 enemy attacks in Donbas on Nov 18

Since Tuesday midnight, Russian-led forces have been observing the ceasefire.

19 Nov 2019, 09:26

Russia's hybrid military forces on November 18 mounted 14 attacks on Ukrainian army positions in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, with one Ukrainian soldier reported as wounded in action.

"The armed forces of the Russian Federation and its mercenaries violated the ceasefire 14 times on November 18. One Ukrainian soldier was wounded in enemy shelling," the press center of the Joint Forces Operation Headquarters said on Facebook in a morning update on November 19.

The enemy opened fire from 82mm mortars, cannons installed on infantry fighting vehicles, grenade launchers of various types, heavy machine guns, and small arms.

Under attack were Ukrainian positions near the villages of Novomykhailivka, Lebedynske, Berezove, Talakivka, Hnutove, Shyrokyne, Krymske, Luhanske, Novotoshkivske, Travneve, and Hladosove.

Since Tuesday midnight, Russian-led forces have been observing the ceasefire.
