Home Depot (NYSE:HD) Posts Quarterly Earnings Results, Hits Estimates

19 Nov 2019, 09:34 by Jessica Moore

Home Depot (NYSE:HD) posted its earnings results on Tuesday. The home improvement retailer reported $2.53 EPS for the quarter, meeting the consensus estimate of $2.53, RTT News reports. Home Depot had a negative return on equity of 1,179.69% and a net margin of 10.18%. The company had revenue of $27.22 billion for the quarter, compared to the consensus estimate of $27.51 billion. During the same quarter in the prior year, the business earned $2.51 earnings per share. The firm’s revenue for the quarter was up 3.5% on a year-over-year basis. Home Depot updated its FY20 guidance to $10.03 EPS.

Home Depot Company Profile

The Home Depot, Inc operates as a home improvement retailer. It operates The Home Depot stores that sell various building materials, home improvement products, lawn and garden products, and décor products, as well as provide installation, home maintenance, and professional service programs to do-it-yourself and professional customers.
