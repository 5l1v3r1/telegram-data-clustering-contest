Transfer Rumour: Manchester United keeping tabs on Matheus Pereira

19 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Abhisek Bajaj

Manchester United are reportedly interested in Sporting Lisbon youngster Matheus Pereira, who is currently plying his trade for West Bromwich Albion on loan.

The Mirror claim that Manchester United are interested in a young Brazilian star. Matheus Pereira, who is currently on loan in England playing for West Brom in the Championship, has caught the eye of Manchester United. The Red Devils are contemplating making a move for the Sporting Lisbon star.

Matheus Pereira has been a journeyman so far, with Sporting CP loaning him out to different leagues and different clubs over the last three seasons. The 2018-19 season was a good learning curve for Matheus at Nurnberg, who were relegated at the end of the season.
