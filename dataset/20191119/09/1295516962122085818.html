Arrow: Mia Will Form an Unlikely Bond with Longtime Team Arrow Ally

19 Nov 2019, 09:11 by Samuel Gelman

As Oliver and Mia spend more time together, the young fighter and future heir to the hood will no doubt be meeting many of Oliver's friends and enemies from his past. And, as Arrow heads to Russia for what could be its final episode set in the Motherland, one character in particular will stand out.

In an interview with Entertainment Weekly, Katherine McNamara spoke about the Russia-centric episode and pointed to one character in particular that will take joy in meeting Oliver's daughter.

"I think Anatoly has quite an interesting interaction with Mia, given that he was so instrumental in Oliver becoming who he is," she said. "Seeing those qualities in Mia, it’s fun for him to not only watch Oliver try and be a parent but for him to see sort of an Oliver Jr. in Mia."
