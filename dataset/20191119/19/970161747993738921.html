Liam Gallagher tells Noel's wife to 'grow up' as he hits back at Twitter claims

19 Nov 2019, 19:59

Liam Gallagher has found himself in another online brawl with his brother Noel.

The High Flying Bird recently claimed that the only reason Oasis are not together is because of Liam’s Twitter account.

And the only way the younger sibling thought it was suitable to retaliate, was by taking it to social media.

Yes, addressing the claims in a tweet, the 47-year-old took aim at his sister-in-law Sara MacDonald as he told her to ‘grow up’.

‘Your wife argues with oasis fans on social media mate she needs to grow up she’s nearly 60,’ he wrote before adding: ‘We all get s**t on social media my family gets it as well so don’t be blaming me LG x’

Fans were quick to comment on the post, with one teasing the star that Sara only claps back because she ‘fancies’ him.

‘Leave it out I’m eating,’ the frontman quipped before sarcastically telling another follower that an Oasis reunion ‘is coming’.

His posts come after Noel claimed every message his brother posts to Twitter about the palaver is a ‘nail in the coffin’ of a potential revival of the Madchester band.

‘It’s strange behaviour for someone who is gagging for me to pick up the phone and say let’s do it,’ he told The Big Issue.

‘He’d put his whole life on hold to get Oasis back together. But every tweet he sends out its another nail in the coffin of that idea.’

Noel then addressed Liam directly in the interview, as he added: ‘If you think for one minute I am going to share a stage with you after what you’ve said you are f**king more of a moron than you look.’

The 52-year-old did admit that the feud between him and Liam has become ‘exhausting’.

‘He insults my wife a lot,’ he continued: ‘It is nothing to do with her at all. And he threatens my kids and it all gets a bid stupid. Liam has got how many kids? Officially? Four I believe.
