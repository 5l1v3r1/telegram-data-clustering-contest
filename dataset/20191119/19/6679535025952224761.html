GITAM to launch courses in tune with industry demands

Specialisations in AI, machine learning, IoT, robotics, cyber security will be offered

19 Nov 2019, 19:08 by Special Correspondent

GITAM Hyderabad, a deemed-to-be university, will introduce full-fledged B.Tech programmes with specialisation in artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), Internet of Things (IoT), VLSI Design, medical electronics, robotics and automation, cyber security and data sciences from this academic year.

Pro Vice-Chancellor N.Siva Prasad, who released the GITAM Admission Test (GAT-2020) notification here said the specialisations will be introduced in civil, computer science, electronics and mechanical branches in tune with the demands of the industry and future opportunities,. He said GITAM will be one of the few institutions to offer the specialisations at the graduate level and make students industry-ready as the new technologies are about to shape future businesses.

Similarly, new programmes like computer aided structural analysis and design; manufacturing technology and automation; and computer aided engineering are being offered in M.Tech. Admissions for the 19 B.Tech, 12 M.Tech and B. Pharm programmes will be based on GAT-2020 to be conducted from April 11 to 21 next year at 50 centres across India.
