Man rushed to hospital after being struck by bus near Buchanan Bus Station in Glasgow

Emergency services rushed to Killermont Street, close to Buchanan Bus Station, at around 4.40pm after receiving reports that a man had been knocked down.

19 Nov 2019, 19:05 by Lynn Love

A pedestrian has been struck by a bus in Glasgow City Centre .

Emergency services rushed to Killermont Street , close to Buchanan Bus Station, at around 4.40pm after receiving reports that a man had been knocked down.

He was rushed to Glasgow Royal Infirmary .

His injuries are currently unknown.

 

The busy road is currently closed.

A Police Scotland  spokesperson confirmed: "Police were called to Killermont Street, near John Lewis, at 4.40pm this afternoon after reports of an accident involving a male edestrian and a bus.

"An ambulance also attended and there are no updates on injuries."
