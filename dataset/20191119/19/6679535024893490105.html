Next Makkal Kural at Thirubhuvanai: Minister

19 Nov 2019, 19:40 by Special Correspondent

The next Makkal Kural, a public grievance programme of the government, will be held at Thirubhuvanai in December, Social Welfare Minister M. Kandasamy said here on Monday.

Public should make use of the forum to air their grievances related to revenue and social welfare measures. Spot decisions would be taken based on the nature of issues raised, he said. He was inaugurating the 19th unit of Puducherry Co-operative Building Centre at Thirubhuvanai.
