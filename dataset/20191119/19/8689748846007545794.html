Davido Shares First Video Of His Baby As He Plays With Him

19 Nov 2019, 19:11 by BB Jones

Nigerian singer, Davido has shared a video of his baby for the first time on social media as he tries to bond with him.

Well, he shielded the baby’s face from the camera though but even his back view shows he’s such a cute angel.

Davido and Chioma named their newborn son, David Ifeanyi Adeleke Jnr (Ifeanyi means ‘nothing pass God’) at a ceremony that took place in the UK on Saturday November 9.

The event was attended by family and friends of the couple. Renowned Entertainment Consultant and Artiste manager, Abisagboola Oluseun, popularly known as Bankulli was one of the popular faces at the naming ceremony.
