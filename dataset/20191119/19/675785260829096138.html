Alison Phillips: I bet arrogant, narcissist Prince Andrew is really sweating now...

Prince Andrew is facing the prospect of being drummed to the wilderness fringe of the royal family

19 Nov 2019, 19:25 by Alison Phillips, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/alison-phillips/

Sixteen years ago this ­newspaper sent reporter Ryan Parry to work undercover as a footman at ­Buckingham palace.

His reports stunned the nation. Not only did we discover the Queen kept her Cornflakes in a ­Tupperware container and two different types of marmalade were served at breakfast... we also discovered what our royalty were really like.

Ryan’s stories about Prince Andrew have stayed with me ever since.

The Queen’s ‘favourite’ son was infamous among staff for his abrasive and demanding attitude.

Even royal protection officers stood well back as he screeched through the main palace gates in his Aston Martin.

And each morning a footman woke him up at 7.30am with his ‘calling tray’ – a pot of tea with a china cup and saucer. One of them told Ryan: “The response can as easily be ‘f*** off’ as ‘good morning’.”

And that, dear readers, is ­everything we need to know about Prince Andrew.

And every explanation why today he is facing the prospect of being drummed to the wilderness fringe of the royal family.

He may have ‘apparently’ spent years unable to perspire but I bet he’s sweating like a pig on a skewer now as the claims against him mount, the financial backers melt away, ­charities back off and he’s left with few friends beyond Fergie and a bunch of dodgy Kazakh ­businessmen.

But what could he expect? His friendship with paedophile Jeffrey Epstein and utter lack of empathy for victims during ­Saturday’s farcical TV interview are inexcusable.

As for his ‘explanations’ for what went on between him and Virginia Giuffre?

I’ve seen more substantial toilet paper.

But it was all to be expected – because the root cause of all this is Prince Andrew’s boundless arrogance.

In his head he’s been a James Bond Prince gallivanting around the world doing mega-deals on behalf of the Queen and cavorting with a string of beautiful women.

To the rest of us he’s a gone-to-seed narcissist who’s never done a proper day’s work since the ­Falklands and spent the last few decades freeloading around the world with the deviant, disreputable or downright dangerous.

Characters such as Gaddafi’s son Saif, the son-in-law of Tunisia’s ousted president, and the son-in-law of the then president of Kazakhstan, who conveniently bought his Sunninghill Park home for £15million – a mere £3million more than he’d have got on Rightmove.

He’s the man who once said bankers’ bonuses were ‘minute’ and who was labelled ‘cocky’ and ‘rude’ in a leaked US ambassadorial report of an official lunch.

Now former Home Secretary Jacqui Smith has labelled him racist at another event.

He’s the man who once spent £32,000 in a year flying to Scotland to play golf.

His other hobbies ranging the entire spectrum from shooting geese in the home counties to shooting game in Africa.

All this... because when he woke up in the morning he could tell the world to f*** off just because he wanted too.

Unfortunately for Andrew, the world is now hurling the same ­expletive back at him.
