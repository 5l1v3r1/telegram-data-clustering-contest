Election Live: Johnson and Corbyn head to head in TV debate

19 Nov 2019, 19:58

The Prime Minister and Labour leader are taking part in the first leaders’ debate in this year’s General Election.

Prime Minister Boris Johnson and Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn have taken part in the first televised debate of the 2019 General Election campaign.

The hour-long ITV debate was cleared to go ahead after the High Court in London rejected a legal challenge by the Liberal Democrats and the SNP over the exclusion of their leaders.

Here’s the latest:

9.15pm

A YouGov snap poll suggested 51% of Britons believed Mr Johnson won the debate compared to 49% for Mr Corbyn.

Those who answered “don’t know” were removed from the result, with YouGov adding the figures are so close as to be within the margin of error.
