'Pedophile raped sisters aged 4, 6 and 8 while their mother ran an errand'

19 Nov 2019, 19:36

A pedophile raped three sister aged four, six and eight years-old while their mother was out on an errand, it is claimed.

George Lamar Sanders, 24, had charges against him upgraded to three counts of first-degree rape on Tuesday, four months after he was first arrested.

Sanders, of Prattville in Alabama, had previously been charged with one count of first-degree rape and three counts of sexual abuse of a child under 12.

He has also been charged with three counts of enticing a child to enter a room for immoral purposes.

Sanders, who worked as a firefighter and Burger King server before setting up a towing business, reportedly attacked the girls while their mother was out visiting a friend.

Afterwards, the children told her what had happened, and she called police.

The woman and her family have now moved out of the home where the attack took place.

Autauga County Sheriff Joe Sedinger says his deputies fear Sanders could have more victims.

He told the Montgomery Advertiser: ‘This is a very disturbing case, because of the ages of the victims.

‘We have continued to investigate since it was first reported.

‘Through our investigation, we have reason to believe other victims are out there.’

Sheriff Sedinger said his team were specially trained to help child victims of sex abuse, and that victims could speak to a female member of his staff.

Sanders has been held in Autauga Metro Jail since he was arrested in July, with his bond amount now increased from $90,000 to $270,000.
