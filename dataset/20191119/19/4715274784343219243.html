Gonzales takes loss in Beijing in stride

19 Nov 2019, 19:48 by Manila Standard Sports

Filipino strawweight Ramon “The Bicolano” Gonzales failed to build on his streak, but he remains motivated to become better. Last Saturday, 16 November, Gonzales failed to make it four wins in a row after dropping a unanimous decision loss against hometown bet “Wolf of the Grasslands” Hexigetu on the preliminary card of ONE: AGE OF DRAGONS at the Cadillac Arena in Beijing, China.
