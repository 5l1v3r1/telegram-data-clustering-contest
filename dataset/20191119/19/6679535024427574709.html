National Integration Day observed

CM, ministers pay floral tribute to Indira Gandhi

19 Nov 2019, 19:27 by Special Correspondent

Chief Minister V. Narayanasamy led the observance of National Integration Day coinciding with the birth anniversary of former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi.

Mr. Narayanasamy paid floral tribute to the statue of the former Prime Minister at the Indira Gandhi Square on the intersection of 100 Feet Road and Villupuram Road.

Speaker V.P. Sivakolundhu, M. Kandasamy, Minister for Welfare, M.O.H.F. Shahjahan, Revenue Minister R. Kamalakannan, Agriculture Minister, MLAs and other dignitaries paid floral tributes. At the Chief Secretariat, Chief Secretary Ashwani Kumar administered the Integration Day pledge to the staff of various departments.
