Spotted: Cop Duchess Meghan’s trench dress

19 Nov 2019, 19:10 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

The lovely khaki trench dress, which Meghan Markle, Duchess of Sussex, was recently seen wearing at The University of Johannesburg, is available here in the Philippines. 

Duchess Meghan announced a new series of gender grants from the Association of Commonwealth Universities wearing a double-breasted trench dress from Banana Republic. The trench dress is part of the brand’s fall 2019 collection, which highlights urban utility pieces. The trench dress is available at Banana Republic in Greenbelt 5 for P7,250.
