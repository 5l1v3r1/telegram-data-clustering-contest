BHU professor appointed Central University of Gujarat vice-chancellor

The order issued Tuesday by the Ministry of Human Resource Development states the appointment for a term of five years from the date on which he enters the office, or until he attains the age of 70 years, whichever is earlier.

19 Nov 2019, 19:11 by Express News Service

Rama Shanker Dubey, Professor, Department of Biochemistry, Institute of Science at Banaras Hindu University, has been appointed Vice-Chancellor of Central University of Gujarat (CUG). He will succeed Prof Syed Abdul Bari who joined as CUG V-C in August 2014.

The order issued Tuesday by the Ministry of Human Resource Development states the appointment for a term of five years from the date on which he enters the office, or until he attains the age of 70 years, whichever is earlier.
