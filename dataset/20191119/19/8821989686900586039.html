Wilco Plot North American Tour for Spring 2020

19 Nov 2019, 19:37 by Ryan Reed

Wilco announced a North American tour for spring 2020. The 17-date leg kicks off March 9 in Madison, Wis., and wraps April 5 in Los Angeles.

Tickets go on sale Nov. 22 at 10AM local time via the band's website. The newly announced dates follow the alt-rock act's ongoing fall trek, which continues Nov. 20 in Omaha, Neb., and runs through Jan. 25 in Mexico City; their hand-curated festival vacation event, Sky Blue Sky, takes place Jan. 18 through 22.

The concerts promote Wilco's recently issued 11th LP, Ode to Joy, which features lead single "Love Is Everywhere (Beware)." The sextet paired the tour announcement with an intimate video for that album's "Before Us"; in the black-and-white clip, director Zoran Orlic follows the band at their Chicago studio the Loft as they track the sparse, simmering folk-rock cut.
