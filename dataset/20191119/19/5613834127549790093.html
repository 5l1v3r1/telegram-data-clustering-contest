The Game Awards 2019 Nominees Revealed; ‘Death Stranding’ and ‘Resident Evil 2’ Up for Game of Year

19 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Mike Wilson

Well, this will be interesting. The nominees for The Game Awards 2019 have been revealed, and the competition for Game of The Year is going to be a tough one.

Genre fans will be in heaven (or hell, depending on your point of view), as Control, Death Stranding, Resident Evil 2, The Outer Worlds and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice are all up for Game of the Year, alongside Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. Action Game of the Year isn’t any easier, as Devil May Cry 5, Gears 5 and Metro Exodus have the nod.

For the Action/Adventure Game of the Year, things are once again complicated, as Borderlands 3 is up against Control, Death Stranding, Resident Evil 2, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice and The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening.

In all, Control and Death Stranding lead the nominations with eight and nine respectively, including multiple nominations in Best Performance.

So, what are you waiting for? Head on over to the official site and get voting! The Game Awards will air on December 12 at 5:30 pm PT / 8:30 pm ET via YouTube, Twitch, Mixer, Facebook and Twitter.
