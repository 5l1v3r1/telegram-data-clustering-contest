When is the first eviction on I'm A Celebrity 2019?

19 Nov 2019, 20:30

I’m A Celebrity has returned for its 19th series welcoming an array of famous faces into the Australian jungle.

The debut episode aired over the weekend and saw the 10 campmates divided into two teams; Camp Snake Rock and Camp Croc Creek.

Among the stars are former Olympian athlete and reality star Caitlyn Jenner, footballing legend Ian Wright and TV personality Kate Garraway.

When is the first I’m A Celebrity eviction?

The date of the first eviction has not yet been revealed but judging by previous seasons, it may be around the fortnight mark.

Last year saw Noel Edmonds become the first campmate to be given the boot on day 13, while the series prior saw Shappi Khorsandi evicted within the same timeframe.

How can I vote for Bushtucker trials?

Viewers can vote for their favourite – or least favourite – celebs to take on a challenge through the show’s official app, which can be downloaded from the Apple or Google Plays stores.

They can also choose who participates in the trials by texting or calling the contestants’ numbers which pop up throughout each episode.

Caitlyn and Kate were the first contestants of the series to take on a Bushtucker trial as they tried to win food for their campmates.

The pair were suspended in transparent boxes with 30,000 cockroaches while trying to throw balls into a bucket.

Caitlyn will be joined by Ian, comedian Andrew Maxwell, former soap star Jacqueline Jossa and rugby star James Haskell for the next trial.

In the history of I’m A Celebrity, the contestant to have faced the most Bushtucker trials was series 16’s Adam Thomas, who took on 12.

I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! continues at 9pm on ITV.
