Tamannaah Bhatia shares a sweet note for Karisma Kapoor as she meets the latter on a flight!

19 Nov 2019, 20:35 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

South star Tamannaah Bhatia who has also worked in several Hindi films loves to keep her fans updated about her day to day activities. And recently, she took to her Instagram to share a post to appreciate Bollywood actress Karisma Kapoor.

In her post, Tamannaah revealed that she met Karisma in flight and they had a good conversation. Sharing a selfie with Lolo, the 'Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy' actress wrote, "So happy to have met someone who I have idolised since forever, the dance of envy ( Dil toh paagal hain )is still the most iconic dance faceoff in Indian cinema for me .

Dearest @therealkarismakapoor

Interacting with you on board this flight was such a pleasant experience, your warmth is infectious , will always cherish this meeting ❤️❤️❤️".

Soon, Karisma dropped a heart emoji on her post.

Meanwhile, Tamannaah recently visited the Golden Temple to seek blessings before kick-starting her next film. The film will be helmed by director Sampath Nandi and will start rolling in this month.
