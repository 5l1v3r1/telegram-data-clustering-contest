Moscow Calls US Plan to Restore Sanctions Against Fordow Violation of Int'l Obligations

19 Nov 2019, 20:26

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - The US intention to restore sanctions against the Fordow Fuel Enrichment Plant from December 15, 2019, is a flagrant violation of international obligations, the Russian Foreign Ministry said in a statement Tuesday.

"Such decisions are another example of a flagrant violation of international obligations by the United States and its irresponsible attitude towards the agreements being signed and the decisions of the UN Security Council that it not only feels free to openly violate but also tries to impede the process of implementation by all other countries," the ministry said.

It said Moscow would continue close cooperation with Tehran on implementation of the project to reconfigure the Fordow facility.
