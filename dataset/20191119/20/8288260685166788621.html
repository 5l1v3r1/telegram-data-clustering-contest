'Congrats on the win Aston Villa' - Newcastle United fans are angry with this decision

Latest Aston Villa news includes Newcastle fans unhappy with Lee Mason appointment for Premier League meeting

19 Nov 2019, 20:22 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Newcastle United fans are fearing the worst after Lee Mason was revealed as the referee for next week’s clash against Aston Villa.

Steve Bruce returns to Villa Park for the first time since he was sacked in October 2018 after two years at the helm.

Having been appointed by Newcastle in July, Bruce has won four of his 13 matches in charge of the Magpies, who sit four places and four points above Villa in the Premier League table.

Villa are hoping a number of injury concerns will be resolved in time for the clash, with Jack Grealish and Jota in line to return.

Grealish is back in training having missed the last three games while Jota has been sidelined since the start of October.

Bjorn Engels was a late omission from the travelling squad who lost at Wolves before the international break while Tom Heaton also missed out.

 

Dean Smith said after that game: "What the international period will do will enable them to be fit for the Newcastle game.”

Matt Targett went off injured at Molineux and the defender is expected to be fit, but Jed Steer faces a longer period on the treatment table while Keinan Davis remains out.

But Newcastle fans have a big concern of their own following confirmation that Mason will be the man in the middle.

The Bolton-born official hasn’t taken charge of any Newcastle games so far this season but has been involved in controversies with VAR since the new technology was brought in.

 

One fan said: “Congrats on the win Villa.”

“Who's going to be rugby tackled this time?” asked another.

A third simply added: “Oh dear.”
