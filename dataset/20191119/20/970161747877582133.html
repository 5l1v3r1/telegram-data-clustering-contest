Is I'm A Celebrity contestant Adele Roberts related to Jane McDonald?

19 Nov 2019, 20:06

I’m A Celebrity is off to a cracking start this year as 10 famous faces get to grips with jungle life and the inevitably terrifying challenges.

One of the trickier ones has seen the campmates given the chance to win their teammates’ luxury items using oars – but it was far from easy, with only retrieved: TV personality Kate Garraway’s concealer and BBC Radio 1 DJ Adele Roberts’ sentimental picture..

However viewers were pleasantly surprised to see the treasured photo was that of Loose Women’s Jane McDonald.

The bizarre choice prompted fans to wonder if there was a deeper link between Adele and Jane – so could they possibly be related?

Here’s what you need to know…

Is Adele Roberts related to Jane McDonald?

Despite what some viewers were tweeting last night, Adele and Jane are not related – the Radio 1 DJ just appears to have immense admiration for the talk show panelist.

Ahead of the task to win the items, Adele said her luxury item was a picture of ‘someone really special [to me]’.

She later explained: ‘She’s my favourite Loose Woman, she’s going to get me through the jungle, I love her.

‘She is a national treasure, she is the queen of Wakefield,’ Adele told her pals. ‘Just think, whenever you’re going off to a trial, just give her a lucky rub and off you go.’
