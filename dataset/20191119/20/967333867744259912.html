Listen to Alien-Like Howl of Earth’s Magnetic Field in Unique Solar Storm Recording

The ‘song’, created by waves generated when Earth’s magnetic field reacts to solar interference, was recorded by European Space Agency Cluster II mission probes.

19 Nov 2019, 20:03

For the first time ever, the ‘sound’ made by electrically charged particles from the Sun’s atmosphere as they approach to pummel Earth’s protective magnetic field during a solar storm has been recorded.
