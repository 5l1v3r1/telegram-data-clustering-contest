Who's who in Caitlyn Jenner's family - from exes and kids to the Kardashians

19 Nov 2019, 20:10

Caitlyn Jenner is fast becoming one of the favourites to win I’m A Celebrity 2019, despite having only been in the jungle for a couple of days.

The 70-year-old – who opened up about transitioning to her fellow campmates in Monday night’s show – has proven popular both with viewers and her fellow campmates – who of course want to hear all about her very famous family.

And not only is the family very famous, but it’s also very large, with Caitlyn having been married three times before she transitioned (and was still Bruce), and being parent to six Jenner children and four Kardashian stepchildren.

But just who is who in her very large family?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who’s who in Caitlyn Jenner’s family?

Heres how it all breaks down…

Caitlyn Jenner’s wives

Christie Crownover

Caitlyn was first married in 1972 to Chrystie Crownover, with that marriage ending in 1981.

Linda Thompson

Cailyn was married to Linda Thompson from 1981 to 1986.

Kris Jenner

Caitlyn was married to Kris Jenner from 1991 until 2014.

Caitlyn Jenner’s children

Caitlyn has six biological children from her three marriages.

Her first marriage produced two children – son Burt Jenner who is now 41 and 39-year-old daughter Cassandra Marino.

Burt has appeared on a couple of episodes of Keeping Up With The Kardashians as well as Caitlyn’s series I am Cait – and is father to two of her grandchildren, William Behr and Bodhi.

Cassandra, meanwhile, lives live away from the spotlight, but is married and has three children – Isabella, Francesca and Luke.

Caitlyn went on to have two children with second wife Linda Thompson – Brandon Jenner, 38, and 36-year-old Brody Jenner.

Brandon is a musician, who was one half of the duo Brandon and Leah with his ex-wife Leah Jenner.

The couple, who were married in 2012 but divorced in 2018, are parents to three-year-old Eva Jenner.

Brandon is now set to become a dad to twins with girlfriend Cayley Stoker, who is due to give birth in early 2020.

Meanwhile Brody Jenner is well-known across the pond as a DJ, model and TV personality who starred in the reality series The Princes of Malibu alongside brother Brandon, as well as appearing in The Hills – and dating one of its stars, Lauren Conrad.

He is currently appearing in the rebooted series The Hills: New Beginnings.

However Caitlyn’s best-known children came from her marriage to Kris Jenner – daughters Kendall, 24, and 22-year-old Kylie Jenner.

Kendall is of course famous for her modelling career – and has been spotted enjoying some quality time with Caitlyn recently – and Kylie is renowned for her cosmetics line Kylie Cosmetics, as well as for having 151m Instagram followers.

Kylie is the mum of another of Caitlyn’s grandchildren, one-year-old daughter Stormi Webster from her relationship with Travis Scott.

Caitlyn Jenner’s stepchildren

The marriage to Kris Jenner also saw Caitlyn gain four Kardashian stepchildren – Kris’ daughters’ Kim Kardashian West, 39, Khloe Kardashian, 35 and 40-year-old Kourtney Kardashian, as well as Kris’ 32-year-old son Rob Kardashian.

And then there are the step-grandchildren to think about – with Kim Kardashian, who is married to Kanye West (Caitlyn’s son-in-law) and has four children with the rapper – daughters North and Chicago, and sons Saint and Psalm.

Khloe Kardashian, meanwhile, is mum to Caitlyn’s step-granddaughter True Thompson, from her relationship with Tristan Thompson – and Kourtney has three children with her ex-partner Scott Disick – sons Mason and Reign and daughter Penelope.

Finally, Rob Kardashian has also given Caitlyn a step-grandchild – daughter Dream, from his relationship with Blac Chyna, who was born in November 2016.

While things have previously been rocky between Caitlyn and the Kardashians – following the release of her memoir where it was perceived that she was making digs at Kris and the Kardashian lifestyle, it looks as though she has reconnected with family members of late.

Kim was spotted at Caitlyn’s 70th birthday celebrations in October, while Caitlyn and Kris appear to be on good terms.

Who is Caitlyn Jenner dating?

Caitlyn is rumoured to be dating 23-year-old Sophia Hutchins – who is also a transgendered woman.

Sophie said of Caitlyn: ‘We’re partners in everything we do, we’re inseparable, we’re business partners, we live together, we share a dog, we share family, we share a life together.’

Could she possibly become the fourth Mrs Jenner? Time will tell…

I’m A Celebrity continues on ITV on Tuesday night at 9pm.
