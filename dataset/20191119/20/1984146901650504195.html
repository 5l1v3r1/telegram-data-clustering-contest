Gillingham 0-0 Sunderland: James Hunter's half-time player ratings

James Hunter's verdict on the Black Cats from their FA Cup replay against Gillingham

19 Nov 2019, 20:39 by James Hunter

Sunderland are level at half-time against Gillingham in their FA Cup first-round replay following a thoroughly uninspiring first half.

Neither side were able to create a chance of note at Priestfield, with Luke O'Nien perhaps coming closest for the visitors.

At the other end, Brandon Hanlan has looked the most likely for the Gills, but Lee Burge has not been called into action

Here's how James Hunter rated the Sunderland players at half-time of the FA Cup replay:

LEE BURGE 6

Hasn’t had a shot to save

BRANDON TAYLOR 6

Youngster has looked solid playing on the right-hand side of a back three

ALIM OZTURK 6

Not 100 percent fit but has done a decent job organising the back three

LAURENS DE BOCK 6

A stand-in centre-back, but has generally done OK

LUKE O’NIEN 6

Offered a threat going forward down the right

MAX POWER 6

A miscued cross forced Bonham to claw the ball from under his bar

GRANT LEADBITTER 6

Has seen quite a bit of the ball, but some of his passes have gone astray

CHRIS MAGUIRE 5

Yet to make an impression

DENVER HUME 6

A couple of dangerous forays down the left, but with no end product

DUNCAN WATMORE 5

Has seen little of the ball

WILL GRIGG 5

Struggled to get into the game but has had no service whatsoever
