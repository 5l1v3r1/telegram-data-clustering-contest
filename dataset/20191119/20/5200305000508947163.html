‘October’ actress Banita Sandhu confirmed for Vicky Kaushal’s ‘Udham Singh’

19 Nov 2019, 20:42 by Raj Baddhan

After her debut Bollywood film ‘October’ with Varun Dhawan, British actress, Banita Sandhu has been away from the spotlight. However, it seems Sandhu has bagged a biggie in the shape of Shoojit Sircar’s ‘Udham Singh’ starring Vicky Kaushal.

Talking about her return, Sandhu was cited in Deccan Chronicle, “I am in Udham Singh. We shot for it in Punjab. Vicky Kaushal is amazing and it was great to be back working with the October team again.”

During her time away, Sandhu completed the Tamil version of Sandeep Vanga’s ‘Arjun Reddy’. About this, she said, “I haven’t watched the final film yet, so I wouldn’t be able to say how my role has shaped up. But Vikram sir (legendary Tamil actor Vikram whose son Dhruv made his debut in Adithya Varma) and I worked to give my character more agency and autonomy in the film. I hope that gets translated on screen.”
