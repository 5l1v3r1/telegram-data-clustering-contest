Shatta Wale Finally Explains Why He Gifted His Award To Stonebwoy

19 Nov 2019, 20:01 by Ghbase New Writer

Shatta Wale surprised everyone during the just ended MTN 4Syte Music Awards when he gifted his Most Popular Music Video award to Stonebwoy, his former rival who has now become his buddy.

His gesture impressed a lot of Ghanaians as they genuinely believed it symbolizes the end of their age long beef and ushering a new dawn as they embrace they new found friendship.

In a fresh with Joy Prime, the SM boss said his action that night was to encourage the young ones in the industry whiles adding that looking at their history, he thought it wise to make that move to foster a healthy relationship between them.

He further indicated that he loves Stonebwoy so much and has supported since day one.
