Daisy Ridley Says ‘Rey Is Driving Her Own Thing’ in ‘Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker’

19 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Genevieve Scarano

It’s the end of a saga, but fans are talking about Rey’s fate in Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker, which hits theaters in a month from today.

The Rise of Skywalker cast, including Daisy Ridley, and director J.J. Abrams, spoke about the film in an Entertainment Weekly interview. Shockingly enough, Rey may have other motives in mind, especially when it comes to her complicated relationship with Kylo Ren.
