NEON Acquires Bong Joon-ho’s 2003 Film ‘Memories of Murder’ for Theatrical and Blu-ray Release

19 Nov 2019, 20:10 by John Squires

Many years before The Host and this year’s Parasite, Bong Joon-ho co-wrote and directed 2003’s crime-drama Memories of Murder, a critically acclaimed film based on Korea’s first serial murders. The award-winning film is now set for big screen and home video re-release courtesy of NEON, Variety reports, in the wake of Parasite‘s box office success.

The site notes, “NEON recently bought the rights to Memories of Murder, one of Bong’s early works, and plans to re-release it in theaters and on Blu-ray.”

In Bong Joon-ho’s Memories of Murder…

“In a small Korean province in 1986, three detectives struggle with the case of multiple young women being found raped and murdered by an unknown culprit.”

Kang-ho Song, Sang-kyung Kim and Roe-ha Kim star.
