Oklahoma high school grad, reality star goes viral in cat lady meme

19 Nov 2019, 20:06 by www.tulsaworld.com

A meme that has gone viral on the internet features a former Tulsan and Union High School graduate.

"Woman Yelling at a Cat" is a meme featuring a screen grab of "The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" cast members Taylor Armstrong and Kyle Richards followed by a picture of a confused-looking cat sitting behind a dinner plate.

Armstrong grew up and was known in Tulsa as Shana Hughes and later changed her name.
