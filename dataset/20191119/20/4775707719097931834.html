We have just picked up the Immortal Planet Achievement list.

There are 11 achievements worth a total of 1,000 Gamerscore, none of which are secret.

Name Description Gamerscore
Hard Landing Die first time 90
Level Up Level up at least once 90
Silent Assassin Complete first room without taking any damage 90
Alien Commander Defeat Alien Commander for good 90
Forbidden Temple Reach the forbidden Temple 90
Prisoner One Defeat Prisoner One for good 90
Alpha Twin Defeat Alpha Twin 90
Temple Sentinel Defeat Temple Sentinel for good 90
Bad Ending Finish the game 90
Ultimate Challenge Complete the ultimate challenge 90
New Game Plus Finish the game in New Game Plus 100

Immortal Planet is developed by teedoubleuGAMES and published by Monster Couch.
