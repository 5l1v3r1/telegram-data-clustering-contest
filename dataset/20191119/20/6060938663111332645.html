Sonam Kapoor shares her look for the day as she enjoys her vacation in LA!

19 Nov 2019, 20:12 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sonam Kapoor is currently enjoying her vacation with her hubby Anand Ahuja in the US. The actress has been sharing pictures from her holidays over her social media accounts.

And now, she took to her Instagram to share her look for the day. The actress opted for a mustard yellow puff sleeve top teamed with blue denim and black heels. The actress tied her hair into a bun and sported cool sunglasses to complete her look. Sonam shared a series of posts to flaunt her outfit as she stepped out on a sunny day in Beverly Hills, California.
