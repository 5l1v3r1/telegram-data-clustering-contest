Spoilers: Returning Emmerdale star reacts to huge Maya baby twist

19 Nov 2019, 20:25

Jaws across the country dropped tonight as Emmerdale slotted in a surprise twist for returning villain Maya Stepney (Louisa Clein). The sex offender, who groomed teenager Jacob Gallagher (Joe-Warren Plant) into a relationship, was released after serving a portion of her sentence.

Sporting a heavily pregnant belly, she deleted Jacob’s number but Jacob refuses to give up getting in touch with her as he remains in love with her. He wants them to have a future together but it remains to be seen where the story goes from here.

Viewers have been reacting with shock as Emmerdale had played down suggestions that Maya would reappear in the show. It is unconfirmed whether Maya will come back to the show full time but it seems inevitable that the storyline will he revisited now that this extra baggage has been piled on.
