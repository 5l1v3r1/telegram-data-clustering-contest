Tamil Movie Stars Kamal Haasan, Rajinikanth Announce They Are Open To A Political Alliance To Fight Dravidian Parties

19 Nov 2019, 20:58 by Swarajya Staff

Responding swiftly to the political overtures made by Makkal Needhi Maiam (MNM) chief Kamal Haasan for forging a potential alliance, Tamil superstar Rajinikanth said that he was willing to consider the proposal for the betterment of people of Tamil Nadu.
