Call of Duty: Modern Warfare hotfix addresses duplicate killstreaks, tunes weapons, more

19 Nov 2019, 20:26 by Stephany Nunneley

A hotfix is now available for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare.

Infinity Ward has released an update for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare which addresses some progressions issues and a killstreaks exploit.

It also tunes the weapons AUG, 725, and Model 680.

The hotfix also applies fixes to various challenges and a couple of issues with Ground War were addressed.
