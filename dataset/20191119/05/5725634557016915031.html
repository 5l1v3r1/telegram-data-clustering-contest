Total cost of goods sold increased 26.1%. Volume correlated sorting and transportation costs grew 27.8% and 31.7% respectively as a result of scaled leverage and very effective cost control. The net effect is that gross margin rates decreased 1 percentage point from last year to 30.3%. Income from operations grew to 28.3% and SG&A excluding SBC as a percentage of revenues decreased to 5.3% compared to 5.6% last year. We continue to demonstrate sound corporate cost advantage.
