After treating the fans with some super quirky posters, the makers of Good Newwz have finally unveiled the teaser and it literally left us in splits. Starring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani in lead, the trailer is a mixture of comedy, emotions and some spectacular performances.

The Good Newwz trailer showcases the entire ‘goof up’ that took place when both the couples (Akshay-Kareena, Diljit-Kiara) visited the same doctor to opt for In-Vitro Fertilisation and their sperms instead exchange. The rest of the story reveals around the struggles and the amusing consequences of it!
