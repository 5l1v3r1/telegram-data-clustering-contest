Total decorates outstanding staff, salutes customer loyalty

19 Nov 2019, 05:27 by Correspondent

OUTSTANDING staff members of Total Tanzania Limited were decorated with certificates and awards by management while loyal customers were also highly spoken about as the company ended its customer services week.

Total Tanzania’s new Managing Director, Jean Francois Schoepp and Corporate Affairs Director, Marsha Msuya said hard working staff are the reasons behind its domination of the market while also thanking dedicated customers for their support.

“We are dedicating this week to you our valued customers, in order to get your feedback and suggestions about our products and services so that we can meet and satisfy your needs,” said Schoepp at a party to climax the week in Dar es Salaam.

He said his management appreciates Tanzanian customers for their support in buying products and services of the company while promising that the company will continue to provide the market with the best quality fuels with Excellium, best lubricants and superior services.

“We appreciate and value your support over the years. Total Tanzania celebrated 50 years in the country, and this has been possible only because your loyalty and support,” he added.

Backing her boss’ observation, Total’s Legal and Corporate Affairs Director, Marsha Msuya said, this year’s customer week theme was focused on customer centricity. “This means we are putting our customers at the center of everything. We want to listen and understand their needs and challenges so that we can serve them better,” Msuya said.

Total Tanzania Limited is a petroleum market and service company, incorporated in Tanzania in 1969 with Total business focusing on the marketing, supply and service of petroleum products, HFO and lubricants, which are marketed through its general trade, service stations and specialty departments within its organization.
