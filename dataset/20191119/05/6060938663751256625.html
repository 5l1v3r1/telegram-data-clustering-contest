India vs Bangladesh: Already dreaming about historic pink-ball Test, tweets Ajinkya Rahane

19 Nov 2019, 05:30 by IANS

KOLKATA: India vice-captain Ajinkya Rahane says he is already "dreaming" about the first-ever day-night Test in the country, which is starting from Friday against Bangladesh at Eden Gardens here.

"Already dreaming about the historic pink-ball test," Rahane said in a Twitter post along with a picture of him sleeping and a pink ball lying next to him.
