Health focused social enterprise boss excited Civil Society Excellence

19 Nov 2019, 05:24 by Correspondent

THAMINI Uhai which is a social enterprise working in improving women and babies health especially those based in rural areas through use of innovative technologies, has vowed to stay the course after winning a CSO Excellence-

-Award of Outstanding Impact in Social Services Delivery category.

Speaking in Dar es Salaam after receiving the award from Foundation for Civil Society, Thamini Uhai’s Executive Director, Dr Nguke Mwakatundu said the award only encouraged them to do even better in the serving the vulnerable groups in Kigoma, Morogoro and Pwani regions.

“We are happy to receive this award recognizing our work. Our team has been working tirelessly to improve maternal and newborn health outcomes of rural and marginalized communities and we are grateful to see the impact contributed by our organization being recognized,” Dr Mwakatundu said.

Dr Mwakatundu further noted that the Foundation for Civil Society organised awards are aimed at recognizing and honour achievements and contributions made by different institutions or individuals that have influenced development policy and practice in the country.

“We take the award as a motivation towards our commitment to further help our country to meet its maternal and newborn health goals,” the Thamini Uhai Executive Director added.

According to the organizers, 166 nominations of people and organizations that have had an outstanding impact in a number of areas were received this year.

The selection of the winner was made on the basis of the development impact, scale, sustainability, impact to marginalized or vulnerable groups, creativity and innovation. The CSO Excellence Award for Outstanding Impact on Social Services Delivery category was presented to Dr Mwakatundu by President of FCS, Dr Stigmata Tenga.

The CSO Excellence Awards 2019 were held over the weekend in Dodoma and were organized by the Foundation for Civil Society with the objective of celebrating, recognizing and honouring achievements and contributions made by different institutions or individuals in Tanzania.

The award ceremony gives an opportunity for organizations to showcase their activities and impact for the sake of their own public relations and fundraising activities. The awards also offer the larger group a chance to learn about the unique approaches being applied by different actors in the field of development for the sake of replication of change and innovation in the sector.
