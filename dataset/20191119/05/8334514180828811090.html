U.S. never raised issues of troop reduction or pullout from South Korea: Seoul official

19 Nov 2019, 05:59

SEOUL (Reuters) - The United States has never mentioned the issues of reducing or withdrawing troops from South Korea during talks on the sharing of defense costs, Seoul’s negotiator told a news conference on Tuesday.

Jeong Eun-bo said that although the latest round of negotiations collapsed because of a “considerable gap” between the positions of the two sides, there had never been mention of U.S. troops.
