Lenglet and Griezmann back in Barça training after France duty

Valverde made up the numbers with six Barça B players on Tuesday

19 Nov 2019, 18:18 by SPORT.es

Antoine Griezmann and Clément Lenglet returned to Barcelona training on Tuesday following France's games against Moldova and Albania.

Coach Ernesto Valverde oversaw the session which included six Barça B players due to the fact a number of internationals are still missing.

In addition to the available first team players, the Txingurri called up Barça B players Sergi Puig, Morer, Riqui Puig, Saverio, Marqués and Carles Pérez.

The Barcelona boss will have Luis Suarez and Lionel Messi back soon, too, after the duo both scored in Monday's 2-2 draw between Argentina and Uruguay.

Barça return to action on Saturday when they travel to Leganes in La Liga.
