ZP standing committee members

19 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Special Correspondent

The following were elected to the five standing committees of the Zilla Panchayat on Tuesday.

Finance: Meenakshi Shantigodu (panchayat president), K. Koragappa Naik, Vasanti, Shayana Jayananda, Sujatha K.P., Janardhan Gowda and S.N. Manmatha

General: Kasturi Panja (panchayat vice-president), K. Koragappa Naik, Jayashree Kodanduru, Pramila Janardhan, Vasanti, Sucharith Shetty and Harish Kanjipili

Social Justice: Vinod Kumar Bolluru, S.N. Manmatha, Mamata M. Shetty, Pushapavati Balila, Sujatha K.P., Kamalakshi K. Poojary and P. Dharanendra (from the Congress).

Education and Health: Sucharith Shetty, Janardhan Gowda, Kamalakshi K. Poojary, Dhanalakshmi, Jayashree Kodanduru, Shayana Jayananda and Asha Thimmappa.

Agriculture and Industry: M. Tungappa Bangera, Asha Thimmappa, K. Ravindra Kambali, Soumyalata, Harish Kanjipili, Vinod Kumar Bolluru and Pushpavati Balila.
