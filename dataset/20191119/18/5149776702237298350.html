Police name man and woman found dead at home in Bingham

Both were pronounced dead at the scene

19 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Phoebe Ram

Two people who died following an incident in Bingham have been formally identified.

They have been named as 57-year-old man Kulvinder Singh and 39-year-old woman Mandeep Singh, both of Porchester Road in Bingham.

The pair were both pronounced dead at the scene after Nottinghamshire Police were called to an address in Porchester Road just before 3.40pm on Wednesday, November 13.

Emergency services remained at the scene on Wednesday night and police launched an investigation into the circumstances of the incident.

Detective Chief Inspector Rob Routledge, who is leading the investigation, said: "I can confirm that formal identification has now taken place and we are continuing to investigate the circumstances surrounding the deaths.

"We are not looking for anyone else in connection with the deaths and a file is now being prepared for the coroner.
