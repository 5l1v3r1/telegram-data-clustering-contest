Man dies following M50 crash

19 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Jack Quann

A motorcyclist has died following a serious road crash on the M50 in Dublin.

It happened at the merging of the off ramp and M50 at Junction 5 Southbound at around 7:45am on Tuesday.

The 29-year-old man was taken to Connolly Hospital Blanchardstown, where he has since passed away.

A post mortem is due to take place at a later date.

The road has fully re-opened since.
