Two companies of 105 Battalion RAF deployed at Sabarimala

19 Nov 2019, 18:39 by Staff Reporter

Two companies of the 105 Battalion of Rapid Action Force (RAF) based at Mahalingapuram in Coimbatore have been deployed at Sabarimala to strengthen security cover at the hill shrine.

The RAF companies were deployed at Sabarimala on November 15 and they will continue security duty at the temple up to January 20.

Crowd control

A statement issued by the RAF said that approximately 240 personnel would take part in crowd control initiatives from Marakootam to Sannidhanam. They would also involve in activities to ensure cleanliness in and around Pamba and Sannidhanam.

The garbage elimination initiative is implemented in Sannidhanam and surroundings under the ‘Punyam Poongavanam’ project that was inaugurated by Kadakampalli Surendran, Minister for Devasom, Government of Kerala, in the presence of RAF Deputy Commandant G. Dinesh on Sunday.

Under this project, officials of different government departments and Ayyappa devotees together would work between 9 a.m. and 10 a.m. every day to clean the surroundings of Sannidhanam, after which conservancy workers would take over the work.
