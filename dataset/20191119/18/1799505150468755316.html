No Stay

19 Nov 2019, 18:05 by BRIEF

MANHATTAN – Rejecting arguments by President Donald Trump’s lawyers that a stay is necessary given “novel and important constitutional issues,” a New York judge kept a defamation case brought by “Apprentice” contestant Summer Zervos against the president on track for trial.
