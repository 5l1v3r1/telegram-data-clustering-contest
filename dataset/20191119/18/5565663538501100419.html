PROPHECY: Chiwenga to do a coup with China before April...war to erupt in Zimbabwe

19 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Mandla Ndlovu

International Bethel Ministries leader, Prophet S. Sibisi has warned Zimbabweans to pray against war and bloodshed that will happen before April.

The Prophet who is based in KwaZulu Natal South Africa said God showed him in a vision Vice President Rt General Constatino Chiwenga sitting down with the Chinese to plot against the country.

He added that he saw President Emmerson Mnangagwa being held captive by some soldiers who want him to resign. The soldiers will be divided into two camps and they will kill each other.

According to the seer, there will be war in Zimbabwe where too much bloodshed will happen and Zimbabweans will flee the country heading to other countries.

The Prophet further said the solution to avoid the war was for Zimbabweans to sit down and solve their problems.
