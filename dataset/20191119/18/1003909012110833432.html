Alexisonfire to rock the Saddledome in January

19 Nov 2019, 18:54 by Newsroom Staff

Canadian post-hardcore rockers Alexisonfire are heading to Calgary this January as part of a western Canada tour.

The group, fronted by singer Dallas Green (also known for his folk-rock project City and Colour), will be leading the group through stops in Winnipeg, Edmonton, Calgary, Vancouver, and Seattle.

In the post-album universe, the band recently released two new songs to streaming services, and played several sold-out shows in London U.K, New York, Los Angeles and Toronto.

For their Calgary show on Jan. 23, 2020 at the Scotiabank Saddledome, they’ll be joined by punk rockers The Distillers and NOBRO.

Tickets go on sale this Friday starting at 10 a.m. and start at $50.50. Visit livenation.com for more details.

One dollar from every ticket sold will go to organizations bringing dignity, equity, and access to communities who need it.
