Tourism, Medicine and Defence in North Korea: Rare Photos of Kim Jong-un Inspecting His Country

Although the life of North Korean leader Kim Jong-un is shrouded in secrecy, it is still possible to learn some details about him.

19 Nov 2019, 18:18

North Korea's Central News Agency released some photos recently showing Kim Jong-un inspecting different facilities which are under construction across the country.

Probably, the most interesting photos show the North Korean leader visiting a couple of tourist resorts. Kim Jong-un also inspected a medical appliances factory and watched some military drills.
