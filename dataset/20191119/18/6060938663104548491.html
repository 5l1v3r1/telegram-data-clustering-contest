Malaika Arora is on a date night, but it’s not with Arjun Kapoor!

19 Nov 2019, 18:15 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Malaika Arora and Arjun Kapoor have been rocking the headlines for their relationship. The duo even enjoyed a short vacation in New York and often gets snapped together in the city on various occasions.

And today, the diva went on a date night in the city but not with her beau Arjun. The 'Chaiyya Chaiyya' girl and her sister Amrita Arora enjoyed a dinner together post attending an event in Mumbai. For the night, Malaika opted for a crop top with a matching jacket and pants and she looked amazing in it! While Amrita decided to go for a white top and shimmery jacket on it paired with black pants.

Malaika posted a picture from the night on her Instagram. She captioned the post, "Date night @amuaroraofficial".
