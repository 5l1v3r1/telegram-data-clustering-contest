Xiaomi Redmi Note 8 Pro could get Android 10 by end of November 2019

19 Nov 2019, 18:37

The Redmi Note 8 Pro has recently been spotted on Geekbench, and it seems that it is running on a Google’s Android operating system. Based on a statement by gsmarena.com, we can expect the Android 10 update to be available on the aforementioned smartphone as well as the non-Pro variant by the end of November 2019.

However, do take note that there was no mention of whether it will be available globally or in selected countries. Do the information regarding the release date with a grain of salt, due to the fact that Xiaomi has not commented on it for now.
