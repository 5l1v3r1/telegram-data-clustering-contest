Ariel Winter Flaunts Cleavage In Low-Cut Pink Top In New Snap From The Netherlands

Naomi Kennedy

Ariel Winter wowed her Instagram followers again today with a new snap from her trip to the Netherlands that saw her looking absolutely stunning.

A geotag included with the post placed the Modern Family star in Zaanse Schans, a neighborhood in Zaandam that is just a short distance from Amsterdam. She posed in front of a cute emerald green house in the photo, which was surrounded by a small pond and luscious greenery.

It appeared to be the perfect weather for sight seeing in the small town, as Ariel opted to not even wear a coat, unlike yesterday, when she was bundled up underneath a black jacket to fight the cold. Her decision to ditch the extra layer revealed her gorgeous look for the day, and it certainly did not disappoint.

Ariel looked beautiful in a blush pink top that did clung tight to her torso, accentuating her flat midsection and killer physique. It boasted a trendy eyelet pattern and peplum sleeves, as well as a daringly low-cut scoop neckline that gave the number a bit of edge.

Plenty of cleavage was left well within eyesight as the stunner posed for the photo, which was only enhanced by the top’s bustier style that upped the ante of the look even more. Meanwhile, its cups featured a flirty scalloped edge that drew even more attention to the babe’s busty display – though it hardly appeared to need help earning recognition.

The camera cut Ariel off just below her waist, though it wasn’t a far search for fans looking for a look at her complete ensemble for the day. The brunette bombshell also shared a full-length snap to her Instagram stories today, revealing she paired her pink blouse with tight skinny jeans and black knee-high boots.

Ariel wore her hair down for the day in loose waves, which were gathered over to one side of her shoulder. She also donned a makeup look that consisted of a light pink lip, dusting of blush, full brows, and thick coat of mascara. The combination of products made for a subtle glam that allowed for Ariel’s striking features and natural beauty to shine.

It didn’t take long for fans of the sitcom star to begin heaping praise on her new Instagram post. The photo earned over 97,000 likes within its first hour of going live to the social media platform – and that number continues to grow by the minute. Hundreds flocked to the comments section of the upload as well, where many left compliments for Ariel’s jaw-dropping display.

“Stunning as always,” one person wrote, while another said that Ariel was “flawless.”

“You are perfect!!” commented a third.
