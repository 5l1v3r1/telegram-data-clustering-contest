Best travel backpacks for 2019

If you're off on your travels it's necessary to keep the essentials for your trip with you. A backpack can will provide a safe place for your worldly possessions as you explore the world

19 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Brijiena Lovelace, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/brijiena-lovelace/

Any avid traveller will know that a good backpack is essential, for keeping your essentials close and safe.

The kind of backpack you'll need will largely depend on the activities you'll be doing. If you know you're going to be somewhere rainy - a waterproof backpack is the best option. Likewise if you'll only be travelling short distances, you'll likely only need a small backpack.

Either way, you'll want to make sure your backpack is stylish, comfortable on your back and easily adjusted to suit your needs.

Wherever you're off to on adventures, we've rounded up 10 of the best travel backpacks you can buy now to help get you on your way.

Best travel backpack for 2019

The ventilated back system allows the body to sink into the hipbelt and lumbar area of the backpack, which not only improves the comfort level - but also provides a supportive carry for even the heaviest of loads.
