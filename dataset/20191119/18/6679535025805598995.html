Over 3,500 people participate in plog run

19 Nov 2019, 18:48 by Staff Reporter

Over 3,500 people participated in a plogging event held at Gandhinagar in Tiruppur city on Sunday.

District Collector K. Vijayakarthikeyan, Commissioner of Police Sanjay Kumar, and Corporation Commissioner K. Sivakumar flagged off the event, a release said.

CSR initiative

The event was organised by Dollar Foundation, the corporate social responsibility (CSR) wing of garment company Dollar Industries.

The participants were provided with utility kits comprising hand gloves, face mask, apron and waste collection bag at the event. The event comprised a five-km run for adults and three-km run for children, the release said.

For cancer patients

Pallavi Gupta, vice-president of Dollar Industries, said in the release that a part of the funds raised in the plogging event would go for the “treatment of cancer patients” and that the company would organise similar events in other States in future. Plogging involves runners or walkers picking up trash along the way and disposing it of responsibly.
