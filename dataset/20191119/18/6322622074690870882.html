Three policemen injured in explosion in Narathiwat, Thailand

19 Nov 2019, 18:12 by Bernama

NARATHIWAT (Nov 19): Three traffic policemen manning a roadblock in Resak here today were injured in a bomb explosion.

Resak assistant CID chief Kapt. Pol. Komsan Thikarn said the three were inspecting vehicles with another three policemen in Kampung Benae Jamong at 5.30pm (local time) when the incident happened.

“A 10kg homemade bomb, hidden in a steel box not far from the place, was set off, injuring three of them.

“The blast also damaged several motorcycles and houses nearby,” he said when contacted.

He said one of the victims was seriously injured and was flown to Hatyai, Songkhla for treatment at the Prince Hospital of Songkhla University, while the other two were admitted to Narathiwat Hospital. — Bernama
