6 New Movies Are Coming Out For Kids This Winter, So Snow-Day Plans Are a Go!

19 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Alessia Santoro

As lovely as it'd be for Winter to disappear after the new year begins, we're stuck indoors for quite some time after — so why not spend that time between December and March at the movies with your family? From a new Disney-Pixar film to a long-awaited film about everyone's favorite blue hedgehog, there are several family movies coming out this Winter for both little kids and big kids that you can put on your crew's social calendar during the snowy months (even if your Winter isn't snowy — lucky — these movies are still must-sees!).
