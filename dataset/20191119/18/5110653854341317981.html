Mark Walton arrested, charged with battery for allegedly punching pregnant woman; Dolphins cut RB

19 Nov 2019, 18:13 by Joe Rivera

The Dolphins on Tuesday waivedrunning back Mark Walton hours after hewas arrestedand charged with battery of a pregnant woman.

Walton, 22, allegedly pushed and punched thewoman in the head multiple times early Tuesday morning, per a police report, which also states the woman is pregnant with Walton's child.
