The Apprentice's Thomas Skinner reveals who the man lying on the floor was

19 Nov 2019, 18:43

The Apprentice viewers were left feeling rather confused following last week’s episode after noticing a mystery man lying on the floor of the boardroom.

Jemelin Artigas was the latest candidate to be fired from the BBC show, and as some of the contestants left the boardroom, fans couldn’t help but spot a man lying on the floor.

But who was he and why was he lying there?

And now we don’t have to wonder any more because Thomas Skinner has revealed who it actually was.

He took to Twitter to put those rumours to bed and informed his followers that it was Ryan-Mark Parsons – although we are not sure we believe the reason behind it – what do you think?

He tweeted: ‘People keep asking me who was the geeza laying on the floor under the table in last tasks boardroom! Well I can actually reveal it was @ryanmarkparsons
