‘OG Insta Golf Girl’ Paige Spiranac Wears The Tiniest Of Miniskirts While Golfing

Anna Harnes

Blond beauty Paige Spiranac, who gained fame on social media as the “OG Insta Golf Girl,” recently floored her followers when she posted a new picture wherein she modeled the tiniest of miniskirts while golfing. Though Paige works as a professional athlete, pictures like her most recent have allowed her to also lay claim to being an Instagram influencer with over 2 million followers.

Paige took this latest picture at Troon North Golf Club, a set of links located in Scottsdale, Arizona. Unsurprisingly, southwestern plants and cacti dotted the background, along with dusty ground and a large boulder.

That said, all eyes were likely on Paige, as she stole the show in a pink-and-white ensemble. The top boasted an incredibly tight, hot pink knit with three-quarter length sleeves. The color looked stunning against Paige’s sun-kissed skin, and the knit material was sure to hug her curves in all of the right places.

The top clung to her torso, showing off her incredibly slim waist. Her hourglass figure was accentuated further, with Paige having paired the top with a miniskirt. Said miniskirt almost looked like it was tennis attire, as it was short, white, and accented with pleats. In fact, the skirt was so short that it barely covered the blond bombshell’s perky posterior, and her legs were showcased by this wardrobe selection.

Paige coupled the outfit with a pair of simple white sneakers and a purple golf glove, but did not appear to be wearing jewelry. Her hair was arranged in a half-up, half-down style, with a few escaped wisps framing her face. Paige also kept her look simple in terms of makeup, with only a hint of eye shadow and a dusty rose lip color in evidence.

Befitting a social media star, Paige knew her angles well, posing sideways to show off her famous curves. With one hand on her golf bag, it seemed as if Paige was just about to grab a club to hit her next shot.

The post earned over 128,000 likes and 2,225-plus comments in less than a day.

“You look so beautiful in that color and outfit,” raved one fan.
