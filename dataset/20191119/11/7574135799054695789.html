Angel Olsen installation in Brooklyn this weekend; more tour dates added

19 Nov 2019, 11:48 by Bill Pearis

Angel Olsen in on tour now in support of this year’s All Mirrors, and she’s got three consecutive sold-out nights at Brooklyn Steel this week starting Thursday. She’s just extended the All Mirrors tour with a spring 2020 Southeastern run, including shows in Charlotte, Richmond, Birmingham and more. All dates are listed below.

Timed with her Brooklyn Steel run, there’s an immersive fan experience called “Behind All Mirrors” that’s happening at Brooklyn venue/events space Villain on November 23 from 3-8 PM. Presented by Spotify, the event “will walk fans through an experiential installation, immersing them in a labyrinth of otherworldly spaces and sounds celebrating the visual inspirations behind the ‘All Mirrors’ video and highlighting the sonic layers of the title track of her new album.” It’s free to attend but you do need to RSVP.
