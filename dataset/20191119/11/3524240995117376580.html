BREAKING: Corpses burnt as fire razes OAU morgue

19 Nov 2019, 11:16 by Bola Bamigbola

Bola Bamigbola, Osogbo.

About ten corpses got burnt on Tuesday, in an early morning fire that razed the morgue of Anatomy Department of Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife, Osun State.

Spokesperson of Osun Police Command, Folasade Odoro, confirmed the incident.
