Costa Coffee reveals when it will open new Derby branch

Good news for coffee lovers

19 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Ben Lyons

A new Costa Coffee store will open soon in a Derby suburb.

The chain has announced a new branch will open early next year at Park Farm Shopping Centre, in Allestree.

The branch will be located in the former Royal Bank of Scotland building which closed around a year ago and has remained vacant since.

A Costa Coffee spokeswoman said: “We’re delighted to confirm the opening of a brand new Costa Coffee store at Park Farm Shopping Centre in Allestree, Derby - coming early next year.

"We are looking forward to being an active part of the local community, getting involved with events and serving customers our delicious signature, handcrafted coffees.”

Costa already has Derby branches at Intu Derby, Meteor Centre, The Wyvern and Kingsway Retail Park.
