I’m A Celebrity viewers asking if the campmates are wearing make-up

Caitlyn Jenner and Kate Garraway raised some questions

19 Nov 2019, 11:26 by Rebecca Carter

Viewers of I'm A Celebrity were left wondering whether the campmates had access to make-up after last night's show.

On Monday night, Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway and Caitlyn Jenner took on their first Bushtucker trial, Bugged Out.

However, fans of the show began asking whether the pair had make-up on leading to questions whether the campmates are glamming up behind the scenes.
