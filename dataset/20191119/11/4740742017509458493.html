Police appeal to find wanted man from south Bristol who has breached his terms of license

Jamie Cockle is known to frequent Bishopsworth and Hartcliffe

19 Nov 2019, 11:21 by Jasper King

Avon and Somerset Constabulary are appealing to find a wanted man.

Jamie Cockle, a 25-year-old from south Bristol is wanted on recall after breaching his terms of license.

Cockle is described by police as being 6ft tall, of muscular build and typically has short hair and often has a beard.

He is known to frequent the Bishopsworth and Hartcliffe areas of south Bristol.

Avon and Somerset Constabulary are keen to hear from anyone who sees him or has any information about his whereabouts.

The public in Bristol are advised not to contact Cockle but instead to call them on 101, providing the call handler with the reference number 5219258907.
