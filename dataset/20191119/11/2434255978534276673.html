Want to make a difference with your Christmas presents this year? Check out Barnardos 'Gifts for Good'

Help provide practical assistance to kids and families this festive season

19 Nov 2019, 11:53 by Kayla Walsh

Barnardos has launched their 'Gifts for Good', an alternative gift for family or friends this Christmas.

By purchasing a 'Gift for Good' on behalf of a loved one you will not only make a child’s spirit bright this Christmas, you will contribute to the brighter future they deserve.

Each gift is based on items typically used by families coming to Barnardos. These gifts offer practical support at Christmas time and beyond. To purchase a ‘Gift for Good’ visit: https://shop.barnardos.ie/

Barnardos Gifts for Good:

  1. Pyjamas, bed socks and slippers – to provide warmth and wellbeing. 
  2. Food voucher - to feed children and families this Christmas and into 2020. 
  3. Filled soap bag for a family - for health and independence. 
  4. Books –to help open a world of wonder for a child’s mind.
  5. Duvet, pillow and covers - to cover a good night’s sleep.

For each 'Gift for Good' you buy, you will be sent an individual card with an envelope in which you can write your own special personal message to pass on to a loved one. 

Each 'Gift for Good' that is purchased this Christmas will directly benefit children and families attending Barnardos services. By choosing 'Gifts for Good' you will allow Barnardos to respond to a range of individual, and often very complex, needs in the most practical, efficient and impactful ways possible.

Your gift will support Barnardos' work with over 17,700 children and families in over 41 communities around Ireland, ensuring the best outcomes possible for each and every child, helping them to reach their potential now and into their adult lives.

Barnardos' 'Gifts for Good' are available today at https://shop.barnardos.ie/ or by calling 01 7080 429 to place your order.
