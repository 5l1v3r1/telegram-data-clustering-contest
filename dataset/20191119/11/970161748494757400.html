Lorraine Kelly addresses savage Jennifer Arcuri confrontation

19 Nov 2019, 11:26

Lorraine Kelly shocked the nation when she brutally confronted Jennifer Arcuri on Good Morning Britain yesterday for ‘not answering questions’ about her former relationship with prime minister Boris Johnson.

And now, the Scottish presenter has addressed the savage head-to-head with the businesswoman in conversation with Piers Morgan and Susanna Reid.

‘It doesn’t happen every day. It only happens now and again,’ Lorraine admitted. ‘It’s your fault.’

‘It’s your fault, Lorraine!’ Susanna replied. ‘You are extraordinary. You launched a missile into the show.’

Piers commented: ‘When I see the smile’s gone and I see this very severe expression and I see a slight shaking of the shoulders, I realise a salvo is coming.’

He then suggested a reality television show which sees Lorraine trapped on a desert island with only Jennifer and Esther McVey, and honestly, we’d love to watch it.

Lorraine didn’t seem too thrilled by the idea and served an almighty eye-roll before Piers asked whether there was anyone she wanted to ‘savage’ today.

‘No, no, not today,’ Lorraine replied.

Yesterday’s show saw Lorraine come for Jennifer in unbelievable scenes, as she expressed that she wasn’t satisfied by the guest’s answers.

‘That was crazy, wasn’t it?’ Lorraine began. ‘What’s the point? Coming down and not answering any questions. What’s the point in that?’

She continued: ‘It just doesn’t make any sense. What’s the point in you coming down and not saying anything? What’s the point in coming on and not saying anything?’

A confused Jennifer then asked: ‘I’m sorry?’ to which Lorraine repeated her question: ‘What’s the point in you coming on the TV to clear the air and then you don’t say anything?’

‘I believe I said a few things,’ Jennifer replied. ‘What was it that you were looking for that I didn’t say?’

We’re still gasping.

Good Morning Britain airs weekdays at 6am on ITV with Lorraine airing straight after at 8.30am.
