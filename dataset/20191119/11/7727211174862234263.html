Couple spend two years trying to use 'broken' plug socket finally find out why it didn't work

After two long years of frustration, Darren finally found out why

19 Nov 2019, 11:26 by Rebecca Koncienzcy

A couple who've been trying to use a 'broken' plug socket for two years finally found out why they couldn't use it.

Darren Steels and his partner were bewildered by the double plug socket in the kitchen of their home in Hucknall, Nottinghamshire.

They had tried a variety of electrical goods in the sockets since they moved into the property in 2017, saying it was in the perfect location to vacuum under the stairs, Nottinghamshire Live reports.

Darren said: "It's perfectly placed for vacuuming the stairs, instead of having the cord running across the kitchen floor."

The socket didn’t feel loose or anything, and when the couple had decorators in to redo the kitchen soon after they moved in, nothing was flagged up.

The 45-year-old added: "People have just painted around it."

Darren said: "I was just fed up with it. We had an electrician come round to do some other bits and bobs and I asked him to have a look at it."

The electrician confirmed there was no current to the socket, before trying to tackle the problem in a different way.

Darren, a restaurant manager, said: "He prised it open, and it came away from the wall."

And what was revealed was - a secret safe.
