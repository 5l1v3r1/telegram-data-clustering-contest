I'm A Celebrity: Caitlyn Jenner faces her fears in terrifying Bush Tucker trial

19 Nov 2019, 11:29

Caitlyn Jenner is going to undertake the hardest I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here! trial yet tonight as she, quite literally, prepares to face her fears.

In a clip from tonight’s episode, viewers can see the reality TV icon caught between a rock and a hard place, overrun with slithering snakes.

The Olympic gold medalist takes on Face Your Fears, where she has to un-screw the two stars she needs to win for her campmates.

The only problem? She has to get these stars whilst feeling through a box positively brimming with the slippery reptiles.

Oh, and she has a giant fish-bowl strapped over her head, blocking her vision. This is, of course, filled with snakes too. Not ideal, really.

Her campmates – Jacqueline Jossa, James Haskell, Ian Wright and Andrew Maxwell – can be seen cheering the 70 year-old on from the sidelines as Ant and Dec watch on.

‘Brush the snake off slowly otherwise you’re going to get a little nip,’ EastEnders actress Jossa advises.

Caitlyn seems to get in a bit of bother, however, as her vision being blocked means she struggles to actually un-screw the much-needed stars from their position in the box in front of her.

This comes just a day after the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star faced off with Good Morning Britain presenter Kate Garraway – which was clearly a nightmare for them both.

In that trial, Caitlyn kept a very calm composure as Kate…well…didn’t. And this impressive streak of nonchalance seems to continue in this new trial.

We don’t think we’d be quite so calm with an actual snake crawling by our face, however.

In other I’m A Celeb news, Capital FM DJ and fellow contestant Roman Kemp opened up last night on his late, great godfather George Michael, even telling a story about the Faith singer accompanying his mother and father – Martin and Shirlie Kemp – on their first date.

Viewers also seemed to be quite baffled when Radio 1 fixture Adele Roberts brought a framed picture of Jane Macdonald into the jungle with her. Yes, really.

But apparently, Jane herself is loving it.

i’m A Celebrity continues tonight at 9pm on ITV.
