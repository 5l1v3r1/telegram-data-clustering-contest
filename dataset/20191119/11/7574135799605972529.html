Soccer Mommy shares new single, touring in 2020 w/ Tomberlin & Emily Reo

19 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Amanda Hatfield

Soccer Mommy, the project of Nashville native Sophie Allison, signed to Loma Vista to release a new single, “lucy,” back in September, and now she’s shared another new song, “yellow is the color of her eyes,” which is accompanied by a seven-minute short film directed by Alex Ross Perry (Her Smell). “‘yellow is the color of her eyes’ is a song that is really important to me,” Sophie says. “The song was inspired by a time when I was on the road constantly and I felt like I was losing time – specifically with my mother. It’s also a song that I feel really showcases my writing when it comes to instrumentation, so it’s one that makes me really proud.” Watch the video below.

Soccer Mommy is opening for The Jesus Lizard in Nashville at the end of the year, and for 2020 she’s heading on an international headlining tour, starting March 26 in Atlanta, GA. She’ll hit Washington DC, Philadelphia, Brooklyn, Chicago, Salt Lake City, Portland, Seattle, Austin and more in March and April, with well-matched support from Tomberlin and Emily Reo on various dates.

The Brooklyn show happens at Brooklyn Steel on April 3 with Tomberlin, and tickets go on general sale Friday 11/22 at 10 AM, with various presales starting Wednesday 11/20 at 10 AM. See all of Soccer Mommy’s upcoming dates below.
