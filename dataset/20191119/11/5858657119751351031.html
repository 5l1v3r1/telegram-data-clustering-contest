SkyTrain workers holding strike vote

19 Nov 2019, 11:59 by Nikitha Martins

BURNABY (NEWS 1130) – CUPE 7000 is set to announce results of a strike vote Thursday following a bargaining impasse with B.C. Rapid Transit Company last week.

Eight days of mediated talks involving 900 workers are slated to start Nov. 28.
