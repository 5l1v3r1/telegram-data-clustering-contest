Ferne McCann has new boyfriend after laughing off Jordan Hames kiss

19 Nov 2019, 11:21

Ferne McCann has a new boyfriend, following her brief fling with Love Island’s Jordan Hames.

The I’m A Celebrity star recently opened up about looking for love, explaining that she was chatting to a string of guys in a bid to find the one.

And the 29-year-old – who shares daughter Sunday with ex Arthur Collins – has managed to narrow her search down from five men to one.

‘I’m dating someone new and he’s really cool. It’s really looking promising,’ she revealed.

‘I was talking to five different guys and now I’ve met someone who I think is very special. I’ve actually known him for a very long time.’

However, before you start trying to figure out who her mystery man is, she admitted she won’t be unveiling him anytime soon – as she doesn’t want to pile the pressure on.

‘I’m not going to say too much at this stage, because I want to give this new romance a chance. The minute I say who it is, it turns into a big deal and puts way too much pressure on things,’ she continued.

‘It’s nice to enjoy the early days of a relationship and see how things go.’

Despite not wanting force things with her new beau, Ferne already has her eye on their future together – and wants things to get serious soon.

‘I’m proud to be a single parent, because it’s not an easy gig, but I hope this relationship goes somewhere – I’m fed up of doing it on my own,’ she added to Closer magazine.

‘I’m all or nothing and I’m ready to get married.’

The Towie star was recently linked to Love Island’s Jordan, when they were spotted passionately kissing at a party back in September – after bonding over their mutual love of feet.

She swiftly shut down any romance rumours, insisting there was nothing more to their kiss.

Discussing the moment on her TV show, First Time Mum, she can be seen telling pal Vicky Pattison: ‘He’s not gonna be my one but it was a nice bit of fun…

‘He is not going to be my husband, definitely not going to be my boyfriend, we’re not going on a date. It was just a nice drunken smooch.

‘I am not here to have a fling or to be mucking around. I am looking for a partner!’

Well, it looks like her search is over…
