Batwoman Midseason Finale Promo Is All About Alice's Revenge

19 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Sam Stone

Jacob Kane will finally have to answer to his long-lost daughter in an upcoming episode of Batwoman.

The CW has released a promo for the series' eighth episode, ominously titled "The Mad Tea-Party," with Jacob face-to-face with his daughter Beth, who has since taken on the villainous mantle of Alice after being presumed dead for the past 11 years. With Jacob held captive in an abandoned theater, it's clear this family reunion won't exactly be a heartwarming one.
