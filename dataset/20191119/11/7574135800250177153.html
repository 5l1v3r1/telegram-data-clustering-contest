Post Malone announces 2020 tour w/ Swae Lee, playing the AMAs w/ Ozzy Osbourne

19 Nov 2019, 11:37 by Andrew Sacher

“Sunflower” collaborators Post Malone and Swae Lee (of Rae Sremmurd) are about to wrap up their “Runaway Tour” together with two nights at The Forum in LA this week, and they just announced that they’ll do a second leg of the tour together in 2020. Once again, Tyla Yaweh will open.

The newly-added dates include two NYC-area shows: Newark’s Prudential Center on February 18 and Long Island’s Nassau Coliseum on February 19. Tickets for those shows go on sale Friday (11/22) at 9 AM with Citi and VIP presales starting today (11/19) at 5 PM and a Live Nation presale starting Thursday (11/21) at 10 AM. All dates are listed on the tour poster below.

Meanwhile, Post Malone is set to perform his Ozzy Osbourne collaboration “Take What You Want” with the Ozzman himself at the Ciara-hosted American Music Awards, which go down this Sunday (11/24) at LA’s Microsoft Theater and air on ABC at 8 PM Eastern. Say what you will about Post Malone, but that collaboration apparently re-energized Ozzy’s creativity and led to him making his upcoming album Ordinary Man with Post Malone producer Andrew Watt.
