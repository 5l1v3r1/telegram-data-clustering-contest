Tuesday night's Mega Millions drawing worth $192 million

19 Nov 2019, 11:27 by WFTX Digital Team

TALLAHASSEE, Fla. -- The jackpot in the Mega Millions lottery game continues to grow, with the grand prize jackpot nearing the $200 million mark.

Tuesday night's jackpot is worth an estimated $192 million, according to the Florida Lottery.

The last winner was on September 24th, when an anonymous Texas player won $225 million.

The next drawing will be on Tuesday night at 11 p.m. But good luck; your chances of winning are 1 in 302,575,350.

If you miss out on that jackpot, Wednesday night's PowerBall drawing is worth an estimated $80 million.

And as always, you can catch these lottery drawings live on Fox 4 at 11 p.m.
