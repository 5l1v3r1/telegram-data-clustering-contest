Sarileru Neekevvaru Update: Teaser Release Date Of Mahesh Babu’s Action Venture To Be Announced Today

It was on Friday last week when the film's director Anil Ravipidi took on social media to share a glimpse of the teaser

19 Nov 2019, 11:14 by Koimoi.com Team

Fans of Tollywood heartthrob Mahesh Babu just can’t keep calm, as they have been keenly waiting for the makers of Sarileru Neekevvaru to release the teaser. Well, the wait for the teaser is soon to get shortened as the makers will be announcing the teaser release date today evening.

Today morning Mahesh Babu’s team took their Instagram handle to share the news that the release date of the teaser will be announced today at 6:03 PM.

“Here we are with a #SarileruNeekevvaru #MASSMB Pre-celebrations begins at 6:03 pm today with #UnlockSLNTeaserDate”

It was on Friday last week when the film’s director Anil Ravipidi took on social media to share a glimpse of the teaser. Talking about the glimpse, one gets to see Mahesh in Army attire loading his gun and on his way to kick some ass.
