Jane McDonald responds to being Adele Roberts' luxury item on I'm A Celebrity

The item has divided many viewers

19 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Rebecca Carter

Jane McDonald has revealed she was "absolutely thrilled" to be the inspiration behind Adele Roberts' luxury item on I'm A Celebrity.

On last night's show, Kate Garraway, Nadine Coyle, Roman Kemp, Myles Stephenson and Adele competed in a late night challenge to try and win all the stars' luxury items.

However, they only managed to retrieve two of the boxes - Adele's picture of former Loose Women star Jane and Kate's concealer.

After it was revealed Adele had chosen to bring a picture of Jane into the camp, the actress responded on Twitter.

Jane wrote: "Absolutely thrilled to see my picture in the jungle. @AdeleRoberts I flipping love you. Best of luck #I'mACeleb."

Jane also appeared on This Morning via a phone call and admitted she was "gobsmacked" by Adele's photo.
