Cats: James Corden, Taylor Swift Join the Felines in Purr-fect New Trailer

19 Nov 2019, 11:11 by Jon Arvedon

The newest trailer for Universal Pictures' Cats has arrived, doubling down on the uncanny valley of the footage that originally debuted in July.

Cats is an adaptation of T. S. Eliot’s Old Possum's Book of Practical Cat, a book of philosophical musings on the nature of cats. The story centers on a group of cats called the Jellicles. Every year, this tribe is forced to make the Jellicle Choice, deciding which cat will enter the Heaviside Layer and be reborn.
