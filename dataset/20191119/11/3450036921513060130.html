Get paid $3k a month to critique weed, CBD, edibles and more

19 Nov 2019, 11:04 by KARLY WILLIAMS, WKRC Staff

CINCINNATI (WKRC) - Lovers of writing, vlogging and marijuana can apply for a new gig up for grabs from a marijuana research magazine.

Candidates must be willing to review a plethora of products including weed, vapes, edibles and CBD oils each month and live in Canada or a region of the U.S. where medical marijuana is legal.
