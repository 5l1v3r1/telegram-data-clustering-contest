HOFF NOTE

Hasselhoff says he’d go to a Partick Thistle match..’if they ever come to the UK’

19 Nov 2019, 11:07 by David Fowler

DAVID HASSELHOFF insists he would love to take in a Partick Thistle match - if they ever come to the UK.

The former Baywatch and Knight Rider star made a gaffe as he didn't know the Championship side play in Scotland.

The Hoff struck up a fondness for the Thistle after making a joke about them during a panto appearance in Glasgow.

But he has clearly not brushed up on where the team play as he made an error talking about the club on Zoe Ball's Radio 2 breakfast show.

When reading out questions from listeners for Hasselhoff, host Zoe Ball asked if he would ever go to watch a Partick Thistle match.

And the Hoff was certain he would as he insisted he'd love to watch them in action.

He said: "If Partick Thistle comes to the UK I would definitely go and see them.

"I love Partick Thistle.

"I made a joke about them in a panto and I said 'the only thing lower than low is Partick Thistle'.

"I the said 'what's a Partick Thistle?' and they showed me the team and I fell in love with the guys because its been around for a long time.

"They actually won two or three games in a row when I supported them."
