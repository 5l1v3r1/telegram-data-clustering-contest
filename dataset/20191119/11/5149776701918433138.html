Man to appear in court in connection with series of house burglaries

The 28-year-old will also face charges of handling stolen goods and harassment

19 Nov 2019, 11:34 by Ben Reid

A 28-year-old man has been charged in connection with a series of house burglaries in Nottinghamshire.

Shaun Cobb, of Falcon Way, East Leake, was arrested on Sunday, November 17, in connection with a house burglary in Ruddington Lane, Wilford.

He has now been charged with the incident as well as a separate alleged house burglary and theft of a motor vehicle in Ruddington Lane in Wilford, also on October 24, a house burglary in London Lane, Willoughby-on-the-Wolds, on October 25 and a house burglary in Main Street, Kinoulton on October 28.

The 28-year-old will also face charges of handling stolen goods on November 5 and 7 and harassment on October 27.

He is due to appear at Nottingham Magistrates' Court on Tuesday, November 19.
