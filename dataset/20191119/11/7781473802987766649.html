''Gift yourself performance gear on International Men's Day'' - TVS India

19 Nov 2019, 11:07 by Team OD  

On the occasion of World International Men's Day, TVS India has taken the opportunity to revisit its newly launched line of motorcycle riding gear and apparels.

"This World International Men's Day, gift yourself the TVS Racing Performance Gear. Building on the ethos of 37 years of TVS Racing heritage, the range of products offered are proven and tested by experts. The gear combines premium build quality with stylish new-age urban design and is available for both men and women," states the release.

The performance riding gear and casual wear were launched last month at the first edition of MotoSoul in Goa.

The motorcycle riding gear includes ISI and ECE certified helmets, riding jackets made of 600D polyester fabric with Level 1 CE certified elbow and shoulder protectors. Further, Level 1 CE certified riding pants are included in the list as well. The riding jacket features waterproof pockets and suspenders to hang the jacket when not in use.

Further in the list are riding gloves that feature touchscreen-compatible fingertips and attached forefinger rubber visor cleaners. Riding boots are also a part of the newly launched safety gear line-up.

Following is the detailed price list of the TVS riding gears:

Riding jacket red - Rs 5,750
Riding pants red - Rs 4,950
Riding jacket green - Rs 5,600
Riding pants green - Rs 4,950
Riding gloves - Rs 1,600

The company is yet to announce the prices for the Riding boots and the TVS Racing helmet.

The riding gear will be available at select TVS dealerships pan India and can also be bought on the company's official website.

Along with the safety riding gear, TVS has also launched their own apparels and accessories. The apparels include an urban riding jacket along with urban riding pants, rain gear, T-shirts and casual boots. To complement the apparel, the company also offers accessories such as sunglasses, caps and bagpacks. The apparels and accessories will be available alongside the company's riding gears at select dealerships. Also, the apparels and accessories can be bought from the TVS's official website.
