Westlife's biggest fans wanted for the Late Late Show this Friday

19 Nov 2019, 11:28 by Ciara Walsh

It's going to be a cracking show this Friday. 

The Late Late Show is on the lookout for Westlife fans to join the boyband in the studio audience.

This Friday will see Shane Filan, Mark Feehily, Nicky Byrne and Kian Egan chat with host Ryan Tubridy about their stellar career and comeback to the music scene with the boys also performing some of their class hits live.

RTE want Westlife's biggest fans to be there on the night, so if that sounds like you, get in touch.

All you have to do is fill out the application form here and explain why you're Westlife's greatest fans and what the band means to you.

You must be over 18's to apply and need to be free to attend The Late Late Show this Friday, November 22.

The exciting news comes after Tubs confirmed Westlife would lead this week's line-up at the end of last week's show.

Also joining them will be legendary singer Christy Moore and former Irish rugby coach Joe Schmidt.

The Late Late Show airs on RTE One every Friday night at 9.35pm.
