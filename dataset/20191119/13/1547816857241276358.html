Manish Malhotra’s Father’s Prayer Meet: Alia Bhatt, Karan Johar, Kriti Sanon And Others

19 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Amruta Patel

Manish Malhotra’s father Suraj Prakash Malhotra left for heavenly abode, yesterday. As reported he was in his early 90’s; many Bollywood celebs and eminent figures were seen at his residence. Today the ace designer arranged for a prayer meet in the memory of his late father, which saw few Bollywood celebs coming by to pay their condolences
