Chennaiyin, JFC ISL fixture rescheduled

19 Nov 2019, 13:12

JAMSHEDPUR: The Indian Super League (ISL) fixture between Jamshedpur FC and Chennaiyin FC has been moved to December 9 with a 7.30 p.m. kick-off time. The tie was originally scheduled on Dec. 6 at the J.R.D. Tata Sports Complex, here. The rescheduling, the press release said, is done in conjunction with the city authorities and administration in view of the Jharkhand State Assembly Phase 2 Polling on Dec. 7. Sports Bureau
