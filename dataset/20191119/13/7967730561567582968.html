West Melbourne office fetches $7.6m

19 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Simon Johanson

SALES

West Melbourne

A freehold double-story office building of about 868 sq m has sold with a short-term lease in place for $7.6 million on a yield of 2.9 per cent. The office at 11-17 Jeffcott Street fetched a land rate of $16,230 per square metre, Allard Shelton’s Joseph Walton said.

Preston

A local investor has purchased 1/188 Plenty Road for $4.5 million. CBRE’s Jake George, Guy Naselli, Ben Quennell and Daniel Eramo negotiated the sale. Mr George said the purchaser planned a long-term lease. The 2360 sq m property has a large office showroom perfect for future development of up to eight levels, Mr George said.

Surrey Hills

Office suites 102 and 108 at 486 Whitehorse Road have sold for $750,000. The property sold on a building rate of $4000 per square metre through Gorman Commercial’s Tom Maule and Stephen Gorman. Mr Maule said a lack of office developments in the eastern suburbs was driving up demand.

Watsonia

A government-leased building at 17 Watsonia Road has sold for $2,201,000 on a yield of 5.16 per cent. The 327 sq m building sold at $6987 per square metre, Allard Shelton’s Joseph Walton said.

Richmond

A refurbished office/warehouse at 5/2 Bromham Place has sold for $1,412,500 through Cushman & Wakefield’s Andrew O’Connell and Robert Colaneri, in conjunction with John Camilleri and David Marks from Killen & Thomas. The 336 sq m building has a two-level office and a high clearance, open-plan warehouse. It was bought as an investment and will now be offered for lease.

Airport West

A property developer has swooped on 22-28 Hawker Street for $4,322,000. The property has a 2717 sq m building on 6131 sq m of land across eight individual titles, allowing for development flexibility. CBRE’s Daniel Eramo and Daniel De Sanctis negotiated the sale.

Moonee Ponds

A 215 sq m shop at 732-734 Mount Alexander Road sold for $910,000 to a local investor through ICR Property Group’s Raff De Luise and Julian Materia.

Thomastown

A vacant warehouse at 82 Lipton Drive sold at auction for $820,000 to an owner occupier. The final price was $70,000 above reserve from about 20 bids, Craig McKellar and Oliver Price from CVA said.

Dandenong South

Bidder numbers were up at the auction of 29 Stephen Road, which sold for $1,083,000. The 582 sq m factory had a long-standing tenancy in place, Cameron’s Angus Clark and Ivo Redmond said.

Hawthorn

Investors have ignored the retailing downturn, paying $1.32 million for a shop at 544 Burwood Road. The buyer had confidence in the location because of its proximity to Swinburne University and public transport, Gorman Commercial’s Tom Maule said. He and Stephen Gorman sold the shop in conjunction with Paul Evans and Tim Mitchell from Miles Real Estate.

Collingwood

A two-level office building at 3/86 Wellington Street has sold with vacant possession at a land rate just under $7250 per sqm. Tom Larwill from Morley Commercial handled the transaction. The 125 sq m site fetched $870,000 from a local investor looking to buy into the booming Collingwood office market.

Kilsyth

A cafe in the Merrindale business estate has sold on a 4.68 per cent yield for $900,000 before the sale campaign closed. CVA’s Jarrod Moran and Stan Dawidowski said 2/127 Merrindale Drive was leased to a retailer trading as Industrie Cafe, returning rental of $42,091.17 plus GST on a seven-year lease with three further three-year options.

Dandenong South

A rare, low-site-coverage property at 46-62 Fowler Road has sold for $5.7 million. Logistics firm Ladher Express bought the 2190 sq m building on a 26,080 sq m parcel just off the South Gippsland Highway. CBRE’s Lachlan Ferguson and Ned Murphy managed the off-market sale.

LEASES

Hawthorn

American giant Taco Bell has taken retail space in 650-652 Glenferrie Road. Gorman Commercial’s Tom Maule said Taco Bell would lease 148 sq m at rental of $150,000 per annum net. “The rental reflected a rate of in excess of $1000 per square metre, which is incredibly strong and promising for the current retail climate. Swinburne University is a major drawcard for any hospitality business looking to get into the strip.”

Brighton

Cycling apparel will ride into 516 Hampton Street after a bike-focused clothing retailer signed a lease on a 132 sq m shop. The property, in a new development, was leased on a five-year term with an option at $60,000 per annum plus outgoings and GST in a deal brokered by Teska Carson’s George Takis.

Box Hill

Eighth Quarter Real Estate Group will move into a standalone 625 sq m office with 24 car spaces at 38 Ellingworth Parade. Gorman Commercial’s David Minton and Tom Maule said the property was leased on a five-year deal for $173,000 a year, reflecting a rate of $277 per sq m. The deal was struck on a 3 per cent incentive.

Brighton

Yoga group CorePlus has leased a large ground floor commercial space at 439 Bay Street. The deal was brokered by James Glen from Nichols Crowder at a total rental of $85,000 a year plus outgoings for a floor area of 240 sq m.

Collingwood

Tom Maule and Nathan May from Gorman Commercial have leased an office to Moss Enterprises at 100 Langridge Street. The first floor space commanded a rental of $35,000 a year plus GST and outgoings reflecting a rate of $100 per sq m.

Campbellfield

A 2102 sq m clearspan warehouse at 5-220 Barry Road has been leased to an automotive tenant at a net annual rental of $125,000. Knight Frank’s Brent Glassford and Marco Sandrin, who brokered the deal, said the property appealed to the tenant because of its access to major arterial road networks including the Hume Highway and the M80 Ring Road.

Armadale

Absolute Health Performance has taken a lease over shops No 3 and 4 at 948-960 High Street. The 330 sq m new retail space on the ground floor of developer Bensons’ The Chronicle apartments was leased for $180,000 per annum or around $540 per square metre on a 10-year term. The deal was negotiated by Morley Commercial’s Tom Larwill.

MOVERS

Valuers and advisors m3property have appointed David Martin as chief executive officer. Mr Martin joins m3property after a successful stint at The Buchan Group.
