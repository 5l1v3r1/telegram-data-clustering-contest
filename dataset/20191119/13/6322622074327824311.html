Mahathir attends Sepang event as scheduled following nose-bleed incident

19 Nov 2019, 13:03 by Bernama

SEPANG (Nov 19): Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad arrived as scheduled for an event here this evening, none the worse for wear after having a slight nose-bleed at another event earlier in the day.

The prime minister came just after 5 pm to the Movenpick Hotel and Convention Centre, looking as healthy and cheerful as ever.

He had come to deliver a special address at and close the Multaqa Ulama Asia Tenggara 2019.

Dr Mahathir had had a slight nose-bleed during a press conference after opening the Malaysian Palm Oil Board International Palm Oil Congress and Exhibition at the Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre in the federal capital this morning.

The Prime Minister’s Office, in a message shared to the media then, said Dr Mahathir was in good health and had resumed normal duties.

It also said that news that he was rushed for medical attention was not true. — Bernama
