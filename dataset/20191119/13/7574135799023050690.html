Poison the Well celebrating ‘The Opposite of December’ 20th anniversary at first show since 2016

19 Nov 2019, 13:45 by Andrew Sacher

Florida metalcore vets Poison the Well‘s classic 1999 debut The Opposite of December… A Season of Separation (which we recently named one of 15 ’90s metalcore albums that still resonate today) turns 20 this December, and the band will celebrate the anniversary by performing the album in full at LA’s El Rey Theater on January 18. Tickets go on sale Friday (11/22) at 10 AM Pacific.

It’ll be the band’s first show since 2016 and it’s currently their only scheduled date. Here’s to hoping it goes well and they play the album in other cities too!
