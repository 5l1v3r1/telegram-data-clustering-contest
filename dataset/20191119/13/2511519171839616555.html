Tite calls for patience with Rodrygo

 
Rodrygo's stardom could shine brighter with an impact off the bench today.
 
Tite has called for patience with the Real Madrid starlet.
 
"Rodrygo has a lot of talent and he did well off the bench against Argentina, although we hadn’t planned to bring him on,” said Tite.

 

“We have to be patient and give him time so that his training is done in the right way. And for that we have to go step by step -- wait for right time to make the right decision. His time will come with the national team and we can’t rush ahead with things.”
