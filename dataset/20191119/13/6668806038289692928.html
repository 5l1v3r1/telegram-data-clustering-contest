Birthday Special! Here are some lesser known facts about Tara Sutaria

19 Nov 2019, 13:12 by Poonam Jain

Tara Sutaria who made her big Bollywood debut with Student of the year 2 is celebrating her 24th birthday today. The movie managed to get a good response from the audience and Tara stole many hearts with her performance. While the movie marked her debut, a lot of people do not know is that SOTY2 wasn’t actually the first time when Tara faced the camera. Tara has been a powerhouse of talent right from her childhood. As the actress celebrates her birthday today we bring to you some lesser known interesting facts about the gorgeous actress.

1. Tara Sutaria first faced camera in 2010 as a video jockey in Disney India's Big Bada Boom.

2.In 2012, she acted in Disney Channel’s sitcom The Suite Life of Karan and Kabir.

3. She later went on to bag the lead role in the serial Oye Jassie on Disney itself. 

4.Tara has a twin sister named Pia Sutaria who is a trained ballet dancer and a model.

5. Not a lot of people know that the actress along with her sister is a trained in classical ballet and Latin American dances from the Royal Academy of Dance and the Imperial Society for Teachers of Dancing, United Kingdom.

6.Tara was one of the two actresses who got shortlisted for the part of Jasmine in                    Disney’s Aladdin. She however lost the role to Naomi Scott.

7.  She has also been a professional singer since the age of seven. She has recorded and performed in solo concerts around the world.
