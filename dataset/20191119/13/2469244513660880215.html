Emergency services rescue woman stuck in mud under bridge

She was taken to hospital

19 Nov 2019, 13:26 by Jamie Hawkins

Emergency services rescued a woman after she became stuck in mud under a bridge in Barnstaple.

Police, fire crews and paramedics were all called to Barnstaple Long Bridge at 7.15pm on Monday following reports that a person was stuck in the mud.

On arrival, the woman in her 50s was quickly located and then assisted from the mud by two firefighters wearing dry suits, to the safety of a waiting ambulance.

She was taken to hospital after the rescue.

A police spokesperson said: "Police were called at around 7.10pm yesterday (Monday November 18) with a report that a woman had become stuck in mud under a bridge near the Seven Brethren Industrial Estate in Barnstaple.

"Officers attended the scene along with ambulance and fire services. The woman, aged in her 50s, was helped from the mud by firefighters. She was taken to hospital as a precaution."
