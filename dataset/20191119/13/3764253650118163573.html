Congo governor beaten up by his citizens for going on a shopping spree in France (video)

19 Nov 2019, 13:58

- A governor of a city in Congo has been humiliated by his people in France

- The governor identified as Gentiny Ngobila Mbaka reportedly went on a shopping spree in Paris

- Mbaka was reportedly cornered by Congolese citizen in France who threw objects at him

Congolese citizens based in France have humiliated one of their governors while he was on a visit to France. Governor Gentiny Ngobila Mbaka of Kinshasa city was attacked by some of the citizens based in the country.

It was alleged that the governor was attacked by the citizens of Congo after it was gathered that he went on a shopping spree in Paris.

In a video shot during the incident, the governor could be seen trying to escape from the citizens who mobbed him. The mob carried the flag of Congo as they attacked and threw items at the man.
