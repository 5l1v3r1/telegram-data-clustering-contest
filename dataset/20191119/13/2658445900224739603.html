JUST IN: Man arrested over wife's lover's death - The Nation Newspaper

19 Nov 2019, 13:31 by https://www.facebook.com/Cheerfulpraise

Osagie Otabor, Benin

 

Men of the Edo State Police Command have arrested one Iroghama Moshudi over the murder of one Etinosa Nowaghama aged 35.

Etinosa was reportedly shot dead on November 8, 2019 at the residence of one Happy Ikpomwosa, estranged wife of Iroghama.

It was gathered that the police arrested Iroghama because he was said to have earlier threatened to deal with the deceased.

Police sources said the woman, a mother of four, abandoned her husband for the deceased and refused all pleas to return home.

The threat to the deceased was said to have been reported to a traditional ruler in their area.

Edo Police spokesman, DSP Chidi Nwabuzor, who confirmed the incident, said operatives of the Ogida Police Division rushed the deceased to the hospital but he was confirmed dead on arrival.

He said investigation would unravel the killers.
