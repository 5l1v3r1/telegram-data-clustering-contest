Littleton Road changes approved in Cape Coral

19 Nov 2019, 13:30

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Changes are coming to a Cape Coral road.

Monday, Lee County commissioners voted on a contractor to realign and widen Littleton Road at Kismet Parkway, and to install a roundabout at Littleton Road and Corbett Road.

The project also includes sidewalks, bike lanes and drainage improvements.

The county awarded Thompkins Contracting, Inc. $2 million for the project. It is jointly funded with the City of Cape Coral.

The total cost is estimated to be $5.2 million.

Construction is expected to begin early next year.
