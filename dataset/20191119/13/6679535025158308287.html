Juliette and Enid Blyton catch the eye

19 Nov 2019, 13:02

Juliette and Enid Blyton caught the eye when the horses were exercised here on Tuesday (Nov. 19) morning.

Inner sand:

800m: Questionable (Chouhan) 51.5, 600/37.5. Moved well. Enid Blyton (Neeraj), Allez La Bleue (S. Amit) 49.5, 600/37. Former was superior. Soldier Of Fortune (Nazil) 53.5, 600/38. Moved freely. Turmeric Tower (J. Chinoy) 51.5, 600/38.5. Responded well. Rousseau (Kaviraj) 51, 600/38. Pushed. Golden Eclipse (Nazil) 51.5, 600/38. Urged in the last part.

1000m: Take It Easy (Nazil) 1-5, 800/51, 600/38. Moved well. Sandalphon (Parmar) 1-5, 800/50, 600/37.5. Slightly urged. Transform (Chouhan), Susie Q (S.J. Sunil) 1-7, 600/39. They were urged.

1200m: Juliette (Bhawani), Flashing Honour (Rupesh) 1-19.5, 1000/1-5.5, 800/51.5, 600/38.5. Former started three lengths behind and easily finished five lengths ahead. Note former.
