Supergirl Faces the Harsh Truth About Lena Luthor in New Promo

19 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Sam Stone

The majority of Supergirl Season 5 has followed Lena Luthor secretly orchestrating an epic betrayal of her estranged best friend Kara Danvers. Now, this plan comes to fruition in a new promo for the Arrowverse series.

The DCTV series has released a promo from Season 5's eighth episode, titled "The Wrath of Rama-Khan," as Supergirl and the DEO deal with the immediate fallout from Lena's betrayal in the previous episode. As Kara flies above the Earth, Lena arms Kryptonite cannons to destroy her once and for all.
