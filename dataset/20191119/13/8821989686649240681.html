Watch Hollywood Vampires’ New Video for ‘I Want My Now’

19 Nov 2019, 13:23 by Martin Kielty

The Hollywood Vampires have released a new video for their song “I Want My Now,” which features scenes shot on tour and in the studio.

The track appears on their most recent album, Rise, and arrives alongside a series of European tour dates that run from Aug. 15 to Sept. 10 next year, with further dates to be announced.

You can watch the video below.

The supergroup -- featuring Alice Cooper, Joe Perry and Johnny Depp -- released its second LP in June, and, unlike the debut, the album consisted mainly of original music.

“However, in the spirit of the Vampires' original mission, there are three covers of songs originally written and recorded by some fellow rockers who died far too young,” a statement explained, detailing “an intimate and intense version of David Bowie's 'Heroes,' beautifully performed by Johnny Depp; the late Jim Carroll Band's ‘People Who Died’; and Johnny Thunders’ ‘You Can't Put Your Arms Around a Memory,’ sung by Joe Perry.”

Cooper noted that "Rise is not only a totally different animal than the first Vampires album, it is unique to anything I've ever been a part of. Each of us – Joe, Johnny, Tommy [Henriksen] and myself – have written songs on this album. … Because each of us has different influences, the sound of this album is very cool. I think that with this album, we are establishing what the Vampires' sound really is, whereas with the first album, we were more tipping our hats to our fallen rock 'n' roll brothers.”
