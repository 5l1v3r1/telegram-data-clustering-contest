Ukrainian officer reveals enemy death toll in Donbas on Nov 18

Two invaders were killed on Monday.

19 Nov 2019, 13:24

Officer of the 53rd Separate Mechanized Brigade of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Anatoliy Shtefan has disclosed the enemy death toll in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, over the past day.

"On November 18, 2019, the losses of Russian occupying forces totaled four people, namely: two people from the 3rd separate motorized rifle brigade of the 9th separate motorized rifle regiment were killed, and two people from the 9th separate motorized rifle regiment were wounded," he wrote on Telegram.

As UNIAN reported earlier, Russia's hybrid military forces on November 18 mounted 14 attacks on Ukrainian army positions in Donbas, eastern Ukraine, with one Ukrainian soldier reported as wounded in action.
