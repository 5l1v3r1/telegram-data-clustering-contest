Dubai's Emirates Golf Club reveals Christmas Day brunch and golf deal

Kick-start Christmas morning with a round of golf

19 Nov 2019, 13:34 by Darragh Murphy

It’s that time of year to get into the festive swing of things here in Dubai, and this time we're talking literally.

Emirates Golf Club is celebrating the big day in Dubai with a morning of golf , along with its ultimate Christmas Day brunch straight after all those (hopeful) hole-in-ones.

On Wednesday December 25 from 7.30am onwards, golfers around Dubai can tee off at the club’s flagship Majlis Course, a challenging par 72, 7,301-yard layout. Start practicing that swing.

After spending the morning on the greens, head over to Emirates Golf Club for its big festive brunch from noon until 3pm, featuring traditional roast turkey buffet with all the trimmings and plenty more festive treats.

Fancy making a day of it? Prices for the brunch start at Dhs245 with soft drinks, and Dhs210 for members. Its house beverage package is priced at Dhs395, while members can get it for Dhs350.

To add all the golfing action on the Majilis Course, those who book the brunch can get a special rate of Dhs450 to play on the course, too.

That’s not all, as those who prefer to tee off on Boxing Day can take on the Fire Course on Thursday December 26. Kicking off at 12.45pm, after the round of golf the club will be throwing a Christmas dinner buffet with all the trimmings – with everything priced at Dhs695.
