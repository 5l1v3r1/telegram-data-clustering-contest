Kremlin comments on Putin-Zelensky meeting in Paris

The agenda of Putin's trip is not ready yet.

19 Nov 2019, 13:56

Russian Presidential Press Secretary Dmitry Peskov has commented on a possible one-on-one meeting between Russian President Vladimir Putin and his Ukrainian counterpart Volodymyr Zelensky in Paris, France, before a Normandy Four summit, which is scheduled for December 9.

"The agenda of this trip is in the pipeline now," Peskov told the Russian news agency TASS, commenting on a possible one-on-one meeting between the two leaders.

As UNIAN reported earlier, Peskov on Monday confirmed that the Normandy Four summit was scheduled for December 9.
