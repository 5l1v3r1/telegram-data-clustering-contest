Detroit: Become Human releases on December 12th, gets a new PC gameplay trailer

19 Nov 2019, 13:18 by John Papadopoulos

Quantic Dream has announced that Detroit: Become Human will come out on the PC on December 12th. Like its previous titles, Detroit: Become Human will be exclusive to the Epic Games Store. In order to celebrate this announcement, the team has also released a new PC gameplay trailer.

Detroit: Become Human promises to feature stunning graphics on the PC, with full 4K native support. The game will also come full integration of both mouse/keyboard and gamepad controls.

Detroit: Become Human will put the destiny of both mankind and androids in your hands. The game will take you to a near and not-so-impossible future where machines have become more intelligent than humans. Every choice you make affects the outcome of the game, with one of the most intricately branching narratives ever created.
