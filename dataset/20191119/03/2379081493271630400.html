Adya Announces Results for the Three and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2019

19 Nov 2019, 03:40 by Business Wire

TORONTO — Adya Inc. (“Adya” or the “Company”), (TSX-V: ADYA) today announces it has released its unaudited condensed interim consolidated financial statements for the three and nine months ended September 30, 2019 and 2018.
