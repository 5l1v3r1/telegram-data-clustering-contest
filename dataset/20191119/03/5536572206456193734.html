Alex Smith’s Wife Posts Uplifting 1-Year Anniversary Video of Him Running, Training After Suffering Broken Leg (VIDEO)

19 Nov 2019, 03:25 by Darrelle Lincoln

It’s been 365 days since Alex Smith’s world came crashing down when he suffered a gruesome broken leg against the Houston Texans during the 2018 season.

On the one-year anniversary of the Washington Redskins serious injury, the QB’s wife took to Instagram and posted an uplifting video showing the 35-year-old running and training.

Alex Smith remains on the Redskins’ Physically Unable to Perform list but has made it clear he wants to play football again.

“Stuff happens to everybody – life happens. I feel like for me this is a time for me to be tested and I have this challenge in front of me and how can I handle it,” Alex Smith told FOX 5 during the summer.

The video above shows Smith weight training, and working on his three-step drop and sprinting on a treadmill.

The Redskins were 6-3 when he Smith broke his right tibia and fibula on a sack by Kareem Jackson. The Redskins haven’t been the same since.
