Alibaba sees increased 3Q19 revenues

19 Nov 2019, 03:21 by Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES, Taipei

Alibaba Group has reported consolidated revenues of CNY119.017 billion (US$16.65 billion) for third-quarter 2019, increasing 3.56% sequentially and 39.78% on year.

The consolidated revenues consisted of CNY101.220 billion (85.05%) from core e-commerce business, CNY9.291 billion (7.81%) from cloud computing services, CNY7.296 billion (6.13%) from digital media and entertainment services, and CNY1.210 billion (1.02%) from innovation initiatives and others.

Of the core e-commerce revenues, China's retail market accounted for 74.87%; China's wholesale market for 3.24%; overseas retail markets for 5.93%; and overseas wholesale markets for 2.40%.

As of the end of September 2019, Alibaba had 693 active consumers and 785 million active mobile users in its domestic online shopping market.
