Some passenger trains between mainland China and HK suspended: Global Times

19 Nov 2019, 03:05

BEIJING (Reuters) - Some passenger trains between mainland China and Hong Kong have been temporarily suspended on Tuesday and Wednesday, China’s Global Times reported.

Global Times, published by the official People’s Daily newspaper of China’s ruling Communist Party, did not provide details in its tweet about the suspensions.
