Dorrigo Preschool for 2020

19 Nov 2019, 03:16 by Michele Kellett

It's not just Christmas that comes up on the countdown list during November. For many families, this time of year signals changes ahead as planning starts for a new school year.

And for the Dorrigo Preschool a new year of changes is just 10 weeks away with the first preschoolers set to start for 2020 on Thursday January 30.

For many parents, the idea of preschool can be daunting with the realisation that the little person in your family has grown quickly.

Forward planning at this stage of the year is important for everyone in the family as the transition to preschool is made.

Preschool helps to develop and broaden the way children experience education, encouraging them to become confident, eager and enthusiastic learners who are looking forward to starting big school.

During 2019 the Dorrigo Preschool has supported 41 families with placement of 48 children over the five-day week.

There is no "normal" or "average" attendance at the Dorrigo Preschool - educators work with families and caregivers to place children to best suit individual needs.

Preschool can be a step towards starting big school, for some it is a fun and safe place to be while Mum and Dad work, and for others it is a chance to have their own time around busy families with younger or older siblings.

Enrolments for the 2020 school year are underway with educators having already placed children across the week.

Programming is an important part of an educator's skill set with the learning program in preschools guided by the Early Years Learning Framework for Australia.

Traditional play is important too but programmes catering for 2 year olds through to 5 year olds are well planned.

The Dorrigo Preschool is a community, not-for-profit organisation run by a management committee of parent volunteers.

It goes without saying that everyone involved with this small preschool is proud of the wonderful memories that have been made over many years.

If your family is ready for preschool in Dorrigo, please contact the Dorrigo Preschool on 6657 2078 for an enrolment pack.

Handy hints for starting preschool can also be found online at https://education.nsw.gov.au/public-schools/practical-help-for-parents-and-carers/year-by-year/starting-preschool .
