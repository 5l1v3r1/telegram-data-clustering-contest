Update: Explosions reported near Damascus Airport

19 Nov 2019, 03:49 by News Desk

BEIRUT, LEBANON (5:30 A.M.) – The Syrian Arab News Agency (SANA) is now reporting explosions near the Damascus International Airport, which is located at the eastern outskirts of the city.

No further details were given at this time.

A source from the Syrian military said their air defenses were activated this morning after suspected Israeli projectiles entered western Damascus from the Jabal Sheikh (Mount Hermon) region.

The source could not confirm whether or not they managed to intercept the Israeli aircraft or projectiles.

Since the early claims of the attack, the Israeli media reported that at least four rockets fired from Syria were intercepted by the Israeli Defense Forces’ (IDF) iron dome system.
