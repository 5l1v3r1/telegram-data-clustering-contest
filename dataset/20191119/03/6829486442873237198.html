Craig David, DJ EZ And What So Not Lead Electric Gardens’ Massive 2020 Line-Up

19 Nov 2019, 03:59 by Joseph Earp

In the three and a bit years since it first came Down Under, Electric Gardens has slowly risen to become one of the stand-out events on the dance music calendar.

Each year the line-up grows more diverse and fascinating, showcasing the true range of pioneers working in electro and house today. And hey, guess what: the 2020 bill is no different.

Related

First off the rank is Craig David, who’ll be bringing his TS5 project to our shores. Next up is DJ EZ, hailed by many to be the best DJ working in the world today; UK-based electro titans Gorgon City; weirdo auteur Reinier Zonneveld; and the beloved and experimental What So Not. Better still, that’s only the start of the line-up — there’s still more announcements to come.

Tickets go on pre-sale this Thursday November 21 at noon, and then on general sale 24 hours later. Electric Gardens will hit Sydney’s Centennial Park on Saturday February 22.
