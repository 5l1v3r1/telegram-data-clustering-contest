Car hits wall after hailstorm leaves parts of Jersey blanketed in ice

MOTORISTS are being urged to take extra care after a car skidded on ice and struck a wall.

14 Nov 2019, 08:52 by Richard Heath

The accident happened on St John's Road in St Helier following a hailstorm overnight.

In a Facebook post, the States police said: 'With similar weather expected over the coming days take extra care on the roads and look out for vulnerable road users namely those on two wheels and pedestrians.'
