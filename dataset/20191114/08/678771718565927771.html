Market Pulse on November 13th

14 Nov 2019, 08:18 by Olivier Rigot, EMC Gestion de Fortune

S&P 500 3094,04, 0,07%.

A day of consolidation with not much to report, prices are resisting any selling pressure and bargain hunting is at work whenever prices drift somehow. Weekly sentiment indicators are for a second week on a row stretched as the bull to bear ratio stands at 164, historically an elevated ratio pointing out to some kind of compacency from private investors. The last time the ratio hit this level was at the end of July, just before the summer swift correction, a potential warning.
