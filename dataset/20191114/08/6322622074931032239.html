Hong Kong expected to announce weekend curfew — Global Times

14 Nov 2019, 08:23 by Reuters

BEIJING (Nov 14): The Hong Kong government is expected to announce a weekend curfew, China's Global Times, a tabloid run by the ruling Communist Party's official People's Daily, reported on its Twitter feed on Thursday, citing sources.

Anti-government protests in Hong Kong have grown increasingly violent in recent days.
