Venue announced and tickets go on sale for Abu Dhabi’s first Ultra Music Festival

The party is coming to du Arena on Yas Island

14 Nov 2019, 08:14 by Colin Armstrong

Excitement is building as Ultra Music Festival is coming to Abu Dhabi for the very first time.

The huge dance festival, founded in Miami in 1999, will be coming to the city on March 5 and 6 2020.

Tickets to the event go on sale today (Thursday November 14) for those who registered their interest on the site, as more details have been announced for the two-day dance festival.

If you have registered, prices to the festival start from Dhs480 for general admission and Dhs680 for premium general admission tickets for a two-day pass.

For non-registered users tickets will start from Dhs560 for general admission and Dhs700 for premium for both days.

The festival will take place on Yas Island at du Arena, home of the Yasalam after-race concerts .

The Abu Dhabi festival is part of the Ultra Worldwide franchise that has previously put on shows in South Africa, South Korea, Singapore, Brazil and Mexico.

This year in Miami performers included Carl Cox, The Chainsmokers, Deadmau5, Marshmello, Martin Garrix, David Guetta, Afrojack and others.

Acts coming to Abu Dhabi are being kept a close secret but when announced you’ll be the first to know.

Keep your eyes peeled to Time Out Abu Dhabi and we’ll share the latest news. We’re looking forward to March already.
