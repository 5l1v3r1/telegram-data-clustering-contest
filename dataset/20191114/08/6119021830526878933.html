One of the great architects of modern India: Rahul Gandhi on Jawaharlal Nehru

Along with Gandhi and Singh, former president Pranab Mukherjee, former vice president Hamid Ansari paid homage to Nehru at Shantivan in Delhi as well

14 Nov 2019, 08:01 by ANI

Congress leader Rahul Gandhi on Thursday paid tribute to India's first prime minister, Jawaharlal Nehru on his birth anniversary and called him "one of the great architects of modern India".
