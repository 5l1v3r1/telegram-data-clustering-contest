Excitable Edgar the star of joint John Lewis and Waitrose Christmas ad

14 Nov 2019, 08:36

An exuberant young dragon called Edgar who almost derails festivities in his fire-breathing excitement is the star of John Lewis’s first joint Christmas campaign with Waitrose.

The eagerly anticipated ad is a return to familiar territory for UK retailer John Lewis, which ended a decade of heart-warming tearjerkers featuring loveable animals and charming children last year with a star turn from Elton John.
