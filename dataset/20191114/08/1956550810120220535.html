Tickets to huge Iron Maiden show in Dubai now on sale

Don’t miss out on the legendary British heavy metal band’s massive gig at Coca-Cola Arena

14 Nov 2019, 08:48 by Darragh Murphy

You waited, and now they’re here. Heavy metal fans in Dubai can now buy tickets to see heavy metal legends, Iron Maiden in the emirate next year - tickets are on sale now.

Last week, Coca-Cola Arena announced Iron Maiden will be coming to Dubai to perform a huge gig as part of their Legacy of the Beast World Tour, on May 27, 2020.

Now, tickets are on sale. Prices start at Dhs250 for seated tickets, and from Dhs495 for regular standing tickets.

For those that want to be right next to Bruce Dickinson and co on stage, premium standing tickets are priced at Dhs995. Whatever your ticket choice, Iron Maiden are known for their absolutely electric, phenomenal live shows.

Tickets are available right now on www.coca-cola-arena.com. Get ready to book, pronto.

Expect them to go through their backcatalogue of massive tunes, including The Trooper, Run To The Hills, 2 Minutes to Midnight and plenty more.

They’ve recently travelled around the world on their brilliant Legacy of the Beast World Tour. It's their biggest production to date, which is saying something, and has been seen by nearly two million fans since the tour opened last year. We’re not surprised.

Don’t waste any more time, get booked.
From Dhs250. Wed May 27, 2020. Tickets on sale Thu Nov 14, 800tickets.com. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, www.coca-cola-arena.com.
