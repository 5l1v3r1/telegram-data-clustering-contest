This old letter by Abhishek Bachchan to Amitabh Bachchan is the sweetest thing you will come across today!

14 Nov 2019, 22:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Megastar Amitabh Bachchan is quite active on his social media. He loves to share pictures, videos and expresses his thoughts with his fans.

And now, he took to his Twitter to share a picture of an old letter sent to him by son Abhishek Bachchan while Big B was away for a shoot. The letter read, "Darling Papa,

How are you? We all are well. I miss you very much. Papa you will be home soon. Papa I am praying for you smile" and so on.

While, Sr Bachchan captioned the post, "T 3549 - Abhishek in his glory .. a letter to me when I was away on a long outdoor schedule .. पूत सपूत तो क्यूँ धन संचय ; पूत कपूत तो क्यूँ धन संचय"
