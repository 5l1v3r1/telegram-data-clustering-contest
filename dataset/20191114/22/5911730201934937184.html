Tampa Bay among teams to attend Colin Kaepernick's workout Saturday

14 Nov 2019, 22:04 by KJ Hiramoto

TAMPA, Fla. -- Could we see quarterback Colin Kaepernick in a Tampa Bay Buccaneers uniform?

According to NFL's communication department, Tampa Bay is among the 11 teams that are scheduled to attend the former San Francisco 49er's workout Saturday. The list of teams include the Arizona Cardinals, Atlanta Falcons, Cleveland Browns, Denver Broncos, Detroit Lions, Miami Dolphins, New England Patriots, New York Giants, New York Jets and the Washington Redskins.

"We expect additional teams to commit," said NFL's communication department in the tweet.
