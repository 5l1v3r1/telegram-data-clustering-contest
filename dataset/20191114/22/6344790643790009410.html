Red-Band Trailer for Aaron Taylor-Johnson's Drug Addiction Treatment Film A MILLION LITTLE PIECES

14 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Jessica Fisher

We’ve got a new red band trailer for the adaptation of the semi-autobiographical novel by James Frey, A Million Little Pieces. The film stars Aaron Taylor-Johnson as James, a 23 year old who has lived a drug and alcohol-addled life, sent to rehab by his brother as his last chance to save himself. The film is directed by Sam Taylor-Johnson, Aaron Taylor-Johnson’s wife, and co-scribe of the adapted screenplay.

The incredible cast also includes Billy Bob Thornton, Odessa Young, Giovanni Ribisi, Juliette Lewis, Charlie Hunnam, Dash Mihok, Charles Parnell, Ryan Hurst, DavidDastmalchian, and Tom Amandes.

An alcoholic and a drug addict, 23 year-old James has two options: treatment or death. After waking up on a plane with a smashed up face and no memory of the past few weeks, he heads to rehab where he discovers much more than detox and therapy. As James endures the white-knuckle journey of mending his broken body, he heals his broken soul by connecting with other kindred spirits who also yearn and fight for a better life.

This performance looks like a career defining one from Taylor-Johnson. Each trailer and clip are captivating and heartbreaking. I can’t wait to see it. Check out the trailer below, and watch A Million Little Pieces in theaters on December 6th.
