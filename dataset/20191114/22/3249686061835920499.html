Seventeen finally reserves Japan's Biggest Dome in their 2020 Dome Tour

14 Nov 2019, 22:29 by jaehaedae_seojeo

Pledis Entertainment announced that Seventeen will finally hold a dome tour with a total of 5 concerts in 3 cities, including Tokyo Dome, Fukuoka Yahuoku! Dome, and Osaka Kyocera Dome in May 2020 through their Seventeen Japan official site.

Seventeen made their debut in Japan in May 2018 which they also gathered a total of 120,000 spectators through SEVENTEEN CONCERT 'IDEAL CUT' in the same year, then a total of 200,000 attendees through  SEVENTEEN 2019 JAPAN TOUR 'HARU' and including their various fan meetings/activities. Seventeen successfully captured more Japanese fans with their unique versatility and colorful charms along with their local status popularity.

Finally, Seventeen will soon reach their special achievement by hosting a '3 Dome Tours' in May 2020. Holding Dome Tours have been the goal and dream of Seventeen since their debut in Japan and they achieve it for just 2 years.
