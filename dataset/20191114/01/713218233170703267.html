Body found in burnt bushland west of Kempsey

14 Nov 2019, 01:43

An investigation is underway after a man's body was found in burnt bushland north-west of Kempsey last night.

Local residents found the body on the southern end of the Kyuna Track at Willawarrin, about 34 kilometres from Kempsey, shortly before midnight on Wednesday November 13.

The person is yet to be formally identified; however, it's believed to be a 58-year-old man who lived in a shed nearby.

Officers from Mid North Coast Police District have been told the man was last seen on Friday November 8.

The Carrai East fire in the area where he was found has now burnt through more than 93,000 hectares and remains at 'watch and act'.

A post-mortem examination will be conducted to determine the cause of death and confirm identity.

A report will be prepared for the information of the Coroner.
