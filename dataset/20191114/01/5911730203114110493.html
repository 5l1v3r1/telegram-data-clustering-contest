Deputies investigate shooting in Pinellas County

14 Nov 2019, 01:02 by WFTS Digital Staff

PINELLAS COUNTY, Fla. --- Pinellas County deputies are investigating a shooting that happened Wednesday evening.

Officials said it happened around 7:01 p.m. Wednesday. Deputies were called to Floral Ave. and Coral Way in unincorporated Largo.

They said there are no fatalities at this time.

Deputies are on the scene and are conducting an investigation.
