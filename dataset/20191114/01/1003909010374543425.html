Deceased man found near Fort McMurray identified as a Calgarian

14 Nov 2019, 01:04 by Vincent McDermott, Fort McMurray Today

Wood Buffalo RCMP have identified Mahmudul Hasan Chowdhury, 26, of Calgary as the deceased man found near Tower Road earlier this week.

According to police, Chowdhury’s body was found at 7:30 a.m. on Nov. 11. Officers are treating the death as suspicious. An autopsy was completed Wednesday in Edmonton and results are pending.

The investigation is in preliminary stages, but police are asking anyone to contact them if they spoke with, or saw, Chowdhury prior to Nov.11

Police are also asking anyone who was travelling along Tower Road between the evening of Nov. 10 and the morning of Nov. 11 to contact them. Investigators would like to review all dash and trail camera footage of the area from those hours.

Chowdhury is described as an East Indian man standing 5’4″ and weighing 119 lbs.

Anyone with information about this incident or criminal activity in Wood Buffalo is asked to contact Wood Buffalo RCMP at 780-788-4040 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477 (TIPS). Tips to Crime Stoppers are always anonymous and can also be sent to tipsubmit.com.
