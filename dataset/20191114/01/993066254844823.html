Man arrested on suspicion of having extreme pornographic image

14 Nov 2019, 01:00

A 49-year-old man has been arrested in Derry on suspicion of possessing what police described as an "extreme pornographic image".

The man was arrested during searches of properties in the city's Creggan area carried out under the Criminal Justice and Immigration Act 2008.

A police spokesman last night said the suspect was being questioned at Strabane PSNI station.
