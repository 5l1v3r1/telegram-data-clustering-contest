We’ve Gotten A Preview Of Everything Coming To Disney+ in Australia, And Hoo Boy It’s A Lot

14 Nov 2019, 01:25 by Joseph Earp

The streaming wars have really ramped up over the last few months, but even that hasn’t been able to prepare us for the entrance of Disney+ into the game.

Yep, the biggest entertainment company on the planet is coming Down Under, and bringing with it a veritable slog of content. Fallen behind on that Netflix miniseries you told yourself you’d finish a few months ago?

Related

Well, prepare to fall behind even further, because there is just so much goddamn content on Disney+.

Hell, we’re overwhelmed and we’ve only been given a teaser, posted to the Disney Instagram account — more titles will be confirmed over the next week or two. And yes, we can confirm The Simpsons are included.

But till then, here’s the rundown of everything that Disney has teased is hitting Disney+.
