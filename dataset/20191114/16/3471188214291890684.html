Circles.Life is offering 100GB mobile data for $28 per month – for real [Extended to 17/11/19]

14 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Chris Rowland

Want some data? Perhaps a lot of it? Australia’s newest telco Circles.Life is offering a 100GB mobile plan for the bargain basement price of just $28 a month.

Correct. 100GB for just $28. That’s not a mistake.

What you’re looking at is a promotion from Circles.Life to bring new customers onto their plans. For the first twelve months, new customers will receive 100GB data each month, and after that, it reverts to a much less impressive 20GB per month.

However, there’s no contracts, so if you decide after 12 months to try your luck with a better deal somewhere else, then so be it – you can!

For a similar allowance, you’re looking at some big plans from the big three carriers, starting with Vodafone at $65 a month on a 12-month contract. Optus charges more again, and Telstra a $1 more again, but there are options there.

To take up the Circles.Life deal, you need to sign up online before 17 November, use the promo code 100GBFOR28, and activate your SIM card by December 31.

There are other plans, too, from Circles.Life, including a more standard 20GB monthly plan which is only $18 per month.

That’s a similar deal – for 12 months only – and requires the promo code 10FOR12. It’s valid until the end of November 2019.

Why take up these deals? Both come with what Circles.Life calls “bill shock protection data”, where you can add on 3GB for $6 if you manage to use up all 100GB in a month.

Unlike some other carriers – which charge monthly based on your sign-up date – Circles.Life aligns with calendar months, so you’ll pay a pro-rata fee in the first month, and also receive a pro-rata amount of data.

Don’t go nuts with 100GB in the first month or you may be in for a surprise.
