Akshay Kumar to co-produce Telugu film Bhaagamathie's Hindi remake

14 Nov 2019, 16:05 by Filmfare

Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar is surely on a roll. The actor's last release Housefull 4 was a hit and his December release Good Newwz is creating a mega buzz and recently, he announced his next project Bell Bottom in collaboration with Nikhil Advani. Now we hear that there is another big project in the making. Akshay Kumar will be bankrolling the Hindi remake of Telugu movie Bhaagamathie which starred Baahubali star, Anushka Shetty.

According to the reports, Akshay Kumar will be collaborating with producer Vikram Malhotra for the Hindi remake. The film will star Bhumi Pednekar and R Madhavan in the lead roles. Director G Ashok, who originally helmed Bhaagamathie will direct the Hindi remake as well.

It is also been reported that Akshay Kumar might have a cameo in the film. He will shoot for six to eight days. The film is expected to go on floors in the month of December.
