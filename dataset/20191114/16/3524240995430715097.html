PHOTOS: Fire destroys houses, shops in Ogun

14 Nov 2019, 16:27 by Oluwakemi Abimbola

Three buildings and four shops located on Oke-Itoku Road in Abeokuta South Local Government Area of Ogun State were on Thursday gutted by fire.

It was reported that properties and other valuables were lost in the fire.
