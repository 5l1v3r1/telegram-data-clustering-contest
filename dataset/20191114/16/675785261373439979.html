Best Christmas gifts for kids 2019 including Linkimals, Harry Potter LEGO and Rizmo

From personalised presents, to classic books, to this year's top-rated toys, we've picked out what we reckon are the best Christmas gifts for kids in 2019 - whatever their age

14 Nov 2019, 16:22 by Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/mollyjaholt/

Christmas always seems to have more meaning when kids are involved, but there's no need to spend a fortune on making it extra special.

Plus, it's important to keep them entertained over the festive period, so treating them to something special will at least keep them occupied for a fair few hours while you get some well-earned rest in.

Whether you're buying for a child that already has everything, want the top toy of the season, or are just looking for a unique and decent priced gesture that will warm your loved one's hearts this Christmas, we've rounded up our top picks of all the best Christmas gifts for kids below.

We've focused on little ones of all ages, whether they're just learning to hold things by themselves, or are fully active and ready to take on the world with their first ever scooter, to make sure that there's a little something for everyone.

1. Linkimals Smooth Moves Sloth, £25.43

Expected to be one of the best selling toys of the year, this is perfect for very little ones.

It claps its hands and bobs its head along to lights, songs and music, introducing babies to music and lights to keep them amused.

Get it from Amazon for £25.43

2. Personalised Christmas Eve box, £34.99

If you want to have a slightly more ethical Christmas this year, why not invest in a present that you can keep using for years to come.

Amazon sell an array of handmade boxes that you can fill with gifts for your little ones for Christmas Eve and personalise with your kids names. Plus, it saves you some money on wrapping paper...

Get it from Amazon for £34.99

3. Personalised micro scooter, £159.95

Let them zoom their way around the village with a personalised micro scooter, in which the board is emblazoned with their name.

Choose from mint green or black for your metal body, then pick the name and wrap it up under the tree.

Get it from Micro Scooter for £159.95

4. LEGO Harry Potter Hogwarts Clock Tower, £84.99

If you know someone that's obsessed with all things Harry Potter, let them reimagine the scenes themselves with their very own Clock Tower model

Young witches and wizards can visit Dumbledore's office, attend a Dark Arts lesson and dance at the Yule Ball, as the Hogwarts set comes complete with minifigures, who are all dressed in their Yule Ball outfits.

Get it from LEGO for £84.99

5. Rizmo, £49.99

Growing up alongside your little one, Tomy's new Rizmo toys are being dubbed the first ever "evolving toy" as overtime, as you engage, cuddle, sing and play with Rizmo, you’ll experience its magical evolution from baby to kid, and eventually adult mode.

It's all down to clever tech, apparently, with the plush toy combining innovative robotics to create a remarkably interactive creature.

Get it from John Lewis for £49.99

6. Le Toy van coffee machine, £29.95

Wooden toys are having a bit of a moment right now, and there's surely no better gifts for hipster kids than a wooden coffee machine for them to pretend to make lattes on.

Perfect for training up little baristas, it even comes with its own pods. So simple but so genius.

Get it from Scandiborn for £29.95

7. LEGO Friends Heartlake City Amusement Pier park, £75.97

Christmas should always involve some form of LEGO, with this amusement park keeping them entertained for hours thanks to a pirate ghost ship, chair swing carousel, ticket kiosk and snack stall, as well as a spin-the-wheel stall with treats.

The carousel ride even fully swings around, with three chairs that spin thanks to a twisting function.

Get it from John Lewis for £75.97

8. Tiger who came to tea book, £4

You can't beat this classic bedtime reading by the brilliant Judith Kerr, which is still a favourite amongst most families after fifty years.

It's Britain's best loved picture book for good reason!

Get it from Amazon for £4

9. Knitted Diplodocus, £9.99

Get your little ones interested in science before they're even able to talk with this adorable knitted dinosaur toy, that also doubles up as a rattle.

As well as being absolutely adorable, it's also practical too, as its knitted exterior means that it's machine washable!

Get it from Best Years for £9.99

10. Plop Trumps, £5.30

The age-old game of Trumps, but with a child's twist!

Each card has a different poo-based subject matter that talks about animals as varied as leopards, Locusts and lemurs.

Get it from Amazon for £5.30
