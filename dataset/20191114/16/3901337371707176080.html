Kante Reveals Future Plans Amid Reported Real Deal

14 Nov 2019, 16:17 by SL International

Chelsea star N'Golo Kante has dropped a massive hint about his future amid reports that Real Madrid had reached an agreement with the Blues over his potential transfer.

Kante has been a long-time target for Los Blancos boss Zinedine Zidane, and was previously the subject of a reported verbal agreement between his club and the LaLiga giants.

In terms of that agreement, Chelsea are said to have given Real first option to sign the 2018 FIFA World Cup winner, sparking speculation that a bid would be imminent.

However, the 28-year-old has now commented on his future, and revealed that he intends to see out his contract with the Blues.

"No, not really," Kante replied when asked by Telefoot if he could see himself playing in another league.

"… Today I am with Chelsea until 2023. So, in my head, I hope to continue to have great seasons with Chelsea until 2023."
