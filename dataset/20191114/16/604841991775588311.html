Award for Meath woman Mary Davitt

14 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Conall Collier

Meath woman Mary Davitt was today honoured at ‘Volunteers in Sport Awards’

Meath woman Mary Davitt was honoured when she received the Meath sports volunteer of the year award for her commitment to Navan Arch Club and Special Olympics Ireland. 

The award was presented by Minister of State for Tourism and Sport, Brendan Griffin TD.

The Federation of Irish Sport awards celebrated the 450,000 volunteers contributing to sport in clubs and communities across 
Ireland

Recognised were volunteers from each county, an ‘outstanding contribution’ award and a ‘team of  volunteers’ at the awards ceremony
