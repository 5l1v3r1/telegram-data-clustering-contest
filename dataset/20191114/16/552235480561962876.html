Wishaw named one of the luckiest places to play the lottery in the UK

“When I did and it told me that we’d won £1 million I thought the app must be broken."

14 Nov 2019, 16:15 by Wishaw Press, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/wishaw-press/

Motherwell and Wishaw residents could be quid’s in after being revealed as one of the luckiest in the UK.

According to the National Lottery, the ML postcode has seen 53 people become millionaires since it launched in 1994.

In total, The National Lottery has seen a whopping 270 top tier prizes of at least £50,000 banked by players in and around the Motherwell and Wishaw area.

 

In the last three years alone, 42 high tier prizes have been won.

This makes the postcode the fourth luckiest for big wins per head of adult population out of all 121 UK postcodes.

Local winners who have shared news of their win recently include The Winning Nurses syndicate who scooped £1 million in the EuroMillions UK Millionaire Maker draw in April 2018.

The group of 15 nurses, who all worked together at Cleland Hospital, each took home £66,666.

Syndicate member June Fraser was in charge of checking the tickets.

 

She said: “I checked the tickets using the app on Saturday morning.

“I scanned the first one and it wasn’t a winner – I actually thought that was the ticket for Friday night’s draw and very nearly didn’t scan the second one as I thought it was for a future draw.

“When I did and it told me that we’d won £1 million I thought the app must be broken.

“My first phone call was to my ward where I managed to get hold of two of the other members who were working. I phoned a few other members but I was just too hyper to call everyone at that point. I still went into work that afternoon!”

 

Andy Carter, Senior Winners’ Advisor at The National Lottery, added: “The Motherwell postcode has certainly enjoyed lots of luck with a staggering 270 prizes of at least £50,000 won across the area since we launched.

“The Winning Nurses are a fantastic example of a winning syndicate and how winning can transform your life. Over the last 25 years, The National Lottery has been making amazing things happen.

“Thanks to our players, 565,000 Good Cause grants have been given to projects big and small.”
