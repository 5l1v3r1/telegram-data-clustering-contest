Sushant Singh Rajput diagnosed with Dengue

14 Nov 2019, 16:19 by Filmfare

Post the super success of Nitesh Tiwari’s Chhichhore, Sushant Singh Rajput flew out of the country with alleged girlfriend Rhea Chakraborty for a break. It was recently when the actor returned from Paris and was all set to work again but then he was advised by the doctors to take some rest. Reason being the actor has been diagnosed with dengue.

According to a daily, Sushant had been unwell since past few days and the recent tests showed that he had contracted dengue, following which he had to reschedule his work commitments. He was supposed to travel to Abu Dhabi for an event this week, but the doctors advised him to rest and avoid travelling. The actor’s next is an adaptation of Hollywood film, The Fault in Our Stars. Produced by Fox Star Studios, Dil Bechara marks the directorial debut of casting director Mukesh Chhabra.

We wish him a speedy recovery and hope he gets back to work real soon.
