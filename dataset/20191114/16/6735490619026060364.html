Aaron Carter Hospitalized: ‘Momma’s Gonna Take Care Of Me’

14 Nov 2019, 16:48 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Aaron Carter has been hospitalized.

Carter, 31, posted a photo of himself on Thursday in a hospital bed. Ascension Sacred Heart Emerald Coast hospital in Destin, Florida was tagged. The “I Want Candy” singer did not reveal why he was hospitalized. The only statement he made was in the caption of the Instagram post: “Momma’s gonna take care of me.”

 

Carter’s mom, Jane, reportedly told TMZ that her son arrived at her house on Thursday looking alarmingly thin. He reportedly weighed only 115-pounds, way down from the 160-pounds Carter once weighed.

Jane is said to have immediately rushed her son to the emergency room. Carter’s condition reportedly stems from the stress he is experiencing over the restraining orders levied against him by his siblings, Nick Carter and Angel Carter.

There was an outpouring of fan support in the comments section of the post. “Hope you’re ok Aaron, get well soon,” wrote one fan. Another commented, “Praying for you, Aaron.”

Carter’s wellbeing has been the subject of media attention and fan concern over the past few weeks. Carter’s brother, Nick, took out a restraining order against the 31-year-old. Carter later accused his sister of raping him as a child.
