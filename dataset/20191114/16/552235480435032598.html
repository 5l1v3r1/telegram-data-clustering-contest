Police appeal after man assaulted in Craigneuk street in broad daylight

The 24-year-old man was assaulted at around 12.30pm on Wednesday, November 13, in Meadowhead Road.

14 Nov 2019, 16:52 by Ross Thomson

Police are appealing for information after a man was attacked in a Craigneuk street in broad daylight.

The 24-year-old man was assaulted at around 12.30pm on Wednesday, November 13, in Meadowhead Road.

He was taken to University Hospital Wishaw for a number of non-life threatening injuries.

Police say they are following a positive line of enquiry but have appealed to the public for more information regarding the incident.

Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
