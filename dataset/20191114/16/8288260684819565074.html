Drinks evening held for West Midlands Tech Awards finalists and sponsors

Birmingham city centre bar The Alchemist welcomed staff from our commercial partners and finalists ahead of the annual awards ceremony next month

14 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Tamlyn Jones, https://www.facebook.com/tamlynjonesbpm/?fref=ts

Finalists and sponsors of the 2019 West Midlands Tech Awards came together for a celebration drinks evening ahead of the award ceremony next month.

Birmingham cocktail bar and restaurant The Alchemist played host to the event which welcomed guests from sponsors NatWest, Bruntwood SciTech, Severn Trent, The Development Manager and Mills & Reeve.

They were joined by staff from a host of different tech and digital companies from across the West Midlands, all of which are vying to take home the spoils from our nine different categories.

Richard Parks, director of commercial banking for Coventry and Warwickshire with headline sponsor NatWest, outlined some of the work the bank does with the tech sector.

The awards, which are run by BirminghamLive and our sister website CoventryLive, will celebrate individuals, SMEs and large corporates in categories such as Young Tech Talent, Digital Development Team and Innovation in Education.
