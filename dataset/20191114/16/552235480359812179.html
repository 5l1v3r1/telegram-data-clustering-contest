Diversions in place after incident on Whitletts Road

Police have put divisions in place on Whitletts Road, Ayr, following a suspected road traffic incident.

14 Nov 2019, 16:34 by Paul Behan, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/paul-behan/

The road eastbound is closed near to the Tesco store and drivers re being advised to find alternative routes home.

It's unclear how long the road will be closed for.

Police are still at the scene.
