Marks & Spencer is now selling Christmas jumpers for dogs!

They're available in store or online

14 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Marks & Spencer is now selling Christmas jumpers for dogs.

The Chevron Stripe bone print jumper comes in a cream mix and grey mix colours.

It's available in three sizes and costs £19.50.

On the website, the description says: "Your pooch will look delightful and stay cosy in this novelty dog jumper.
