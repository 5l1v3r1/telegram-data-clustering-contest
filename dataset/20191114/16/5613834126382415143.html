Paradox Interactive Touches on ‘Bloodlines 2’ Development, Teases New Character in PDXCON Recap Video

14 Nov 2019, 16:46 by Mike Wilson

Those curious about what went on at PDXCON for Paradox Interactive and their upcoming RPG Vampire: The Masquerade – Bloodlines 2 need not worry, as the team themselves have uploaded a new video recapping their presentation of the game to the Berlin crowd last month.

The team shared their vision for making players feel like a vampire, building the city of Seattle and making it feel like a living world, and even teased a new character, Chip, players will meet in-game. In addition, Andy Kipling, Co-Founder and CEO of the game’s developer, Hardsuit Labs, put together a dev diary sharing his experience at the show (which you can find here).

Vampire: The Masquerade – Bloodlines 2 will hit PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One late next year.
