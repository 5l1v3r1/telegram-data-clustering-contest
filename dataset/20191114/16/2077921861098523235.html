M. Night Shyamalan's Servant Has Another (Equally Creepy) Trailer

14 Nov 2019, 16:07 by Rachita Vasandani  

M. Night Shyamalan’s much-anticipated Apple series Servant has another trailer, titled “Making Jericho.” Spoiler alert: It’s creepy.

The Apple TV+ exclusive premieres on Nov. 28 and tells the story of a Philadelphia couple in the midst of mourning the death of their baby, whom they replace with a disturbingly lifelike doll. Weirdness ensues, they have a creepy babysitter and Rupert Grint somehow doesn’t make it any less uncomfortable.

The latest trailer focuses on the process of making the little baby doll, set to a backing track of Debussy’s “Clair de Lune.” Sounds artistic, but when you see it in the context of the other trailer, it’s hair-raising.
