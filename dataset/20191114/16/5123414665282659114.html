Pet Shop Boys Announce New Album 'Hotspot,' Release Single "Burning the heather"

The Pet Shop Boys will release their new album early next year.

14 Nov 2019, 16:13 by Ryan Middleton

Legendary 80’s English synth pop duo Pet Shop Boys has announced their new album Hotspot. The best selling British group in UK history, the pair will follow up 2016’s Super with their 14th album Hotspot, out next year. In addition to the LP announcement, they have also released a new single “Burning the heather.”
