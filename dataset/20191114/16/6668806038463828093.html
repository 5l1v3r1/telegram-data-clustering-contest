Ayushmann Khurrana’s Dream Girl to release in Hong Kong

14 Nov 2019, 16:25 by Filmfare

As we all know Ayushmann Khurrana is riding on the success of his recent release Bala but there’s another piece of good news for the actor. His movie Dream Girl directed by Raaj Shaandilyaa is now set to release in Hong Dong. The rom-com also starred Nushrat Bharucha and Annu Kapoor in supporting roles and saw Ayushmann impersonating a woman named Pooja for a hotline company. The film garnered rave reviews from audience and critics alike.

Judging from the film’s performance in the country, we’re sure that it’ll be a success abroad as well. Talking about the film’s Hong Kong release, Vibha Chopra, Head-Global Syndication & International Film Distribution, Zee Entertainment, said: "The quirky tale of 'Dream Girl' transcends boundaries! A film that has emerged to be one of the biggest entertainers of the year marks 5th Bollywood release in this non-traditional market after 'Barfi', 'Dangal', 'Secret Superstar' and 'Hichki'. It's always a great feeling to take such films to the unconventional territories and witness the diaspora's reaction towards this film.”

Earlier this week there was news of Bala releasing in Saudi Arabia and now with Dream Girl’s release in Hong Kong, we can conclude Ayushmann is on course to be a global star.
