Multiple victims reported in shooting at California high school

14 Nov 2019, 16:27 by Zachary Halaschak

A number of people are reported injured in an early morning shooting at a California high school.

Early reports indicate that at least seven people were shot at Saugus High School in Santa Clarita, California, on Thursday morning. Reports of gunfire first came in around 7:40 a.m.

Police say the scene is still active and described the suspect as an Asian male wearing black clothing. People were seen being wheeled out of the school on stretchers.
