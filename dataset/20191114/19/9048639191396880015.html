President Trump Rally in Bossier City

14 Nov 2019, 19:13 by KNOE

BOSSIER CITY, La. (KNOE) - President Donald Trump is holding a campaign rally in Bossier City, Louisiana on Thursday.

The rally is scheduled to begin at 7 p.m. but could start late depending on the president's schedule.
