Castlebar mourns passing of popular personality

14 Nov 2019, 19:13

THE death has taken place of Jimmy Prendergast, 'Glencar', The Curragh, Castlebar.

In his 92nd year, the deceased was a former psychiatric nurse by profession and was held in very high regard in the community.

His remains will be reposing at his home on Friday evening from 4 until 8 p.m, arriving at the Church of the Holy Rosary, Castlebar, on Saturday at 12 noon for Requiem Mass at 12.30 p.m.

Burial takes place afterwards in the New Cemetery, Castlebar.

May his gentle soul rest in peace.
