Christmas Stocking Filled With Hidden Valley Ranch is a Whole Lotta Yes

14 Nov 2019, 19:05 by Johnny Thrash

The king of ranch dressing has come through with the perfect stocking stuffer for my fellow ranch lovers.

I kid you not – Hidden Valley has introduced a plastic stocking filled with 52 ounces of ranch complete with a spout on top so you can douse everything on you plate with creamy goodness.

It’ll set you back $35 and is currently available for preorder at hiddenvalley.com.

The stockings are scheduled to ship in early December.

Or if you’re on a budget, just by a plastic stocking a dump and bunch of bottles in it for a DIY solution.
