General Election 2019 - all the confirmed candidates for Devon and Plymouth

Residents in Devon will go to the polls on Thursday, December 12, for a General Election

14 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Daniel Clark

The names of those who are vying to become the 12 MPs in Devon at the upcoming General Election have been confirmed.

Residents in Devon will go to the polls on Thursday, December 12, for a General Election.

Candidates had until Thursday, November 14, at 4pm, to submit their nominations.

The list of the 58 candidates, in 12 seats, has now been published.

The Conservatives and Labour are standing in all 12 seats, while the Liberal Democrats and the Green Party are fielding candidates in 11, standing down for each other in Exeter and Totnes respectively.

The Brexit Party and UKIP each have two candidates, while there are eight Independents.

The public have until midnight on Tuesday, November 26, to register to vote.
