Oldham stabbing: Four teens seriously injured after being knifed outside leisure centre

Four teenage boys aged between 16 to 19 have been stabbed outside the Oldham Leisure Centre in Greater Manchester

14 Nov 2019, 19:56 by Chris Slater, Helen Johnson

Four teenagers have been wounded after a stabbing outside a leisure centre in Greater Manchester.

Police say they were called to the leisure centre in Oldham around 4.30pm this afternoon.

Greater Manchester Police said four teenage boys aged between 16 and 19 were hurt, but their injuries are not believed life-threatening, reports the Manchester Evening News.

No further details about the incident have so far been released.

Emergency services remain at the scene and the area around the leisure centre, but the cordon has since been lifted.
