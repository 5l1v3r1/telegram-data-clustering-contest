US rock band Tool announce first Australian tour in seven years

14 Nov 2019, 19:00

Fans had to wait 13 years for a new Tool album, now the seven-year wait to see the US rock band live in Australia is about to come to an end.

The American band will play Rod Laver Arena on February 22.

Tickets go on sale November 22 at 2pm from Ticketek, with a Frontier Touring presale starting 2pm next Wednesday.

The tour comes on the back of their ARIA No. 1 album Fear Inoculum, their first release in 13 years.

The record, which knocked Taylor Swift’s Lover from top spot, is being reissued on December 13 in an expanded book edition.

Tool will also play RAC Arena Perth on February 14, Qudos Bank Arena Sydney on February 17 and Brisbane Entertainment Centre on February 20.

Fronted by Maynard James Keenan, Tool are currently on a 25 date US tour.

Their last three albums – Fear Inoculum, 10,000 Days and Lateralus all reached No. 1 in Australia.
