Suspect in custody after school shooting near Los Angeles

14 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Agency Report

The suspect in a school shooting near Los Angeles that injured at least four people has been taken into custody, officials said Thursday.

The shooting took place at Saugus High School in Santa Clarita, 40 miles (65 kilometres) north of Los Angeles, on Thursday morning.

“Update regarding the shooting at #SaugusHigh, the suspect is in custody and being treated at a local hospital,” tweeted Los Angeles County Sheriff Alex Villanueva.
