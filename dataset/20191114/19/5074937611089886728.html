AWA Marketing Manager named among 50 most influential CMOs in Ghana

14 Nov 2019, 19:31

Accra, Nov. 14, GNA - Madam Victoria Abena Takyi, Sales and Marketing Manager of Africa World Airlines (AWA), has been named among top marketing, sales and customer service professional in the list of the 50 Most Influential Chief Marketing Officers in Ghana.

The inaugural list, which is not ranked, was compiled by Avance Media, Reset Global People and Afiba Consulting and seeks to recognize the works of the individuals, who are motivating performance, broadening their market, creating new trends and brands perception and an impressive financial performance in the Ghanaian market.

A statement issued by organisers said the comprehensive list chronicles the names of the 50 best minds in the sales and marketing field in the country.Â 

It said 21 out of the 50 professionals listed were women and the sectors cut across media, technology, banking and finance, insurance, food and beverage, telco, oil and gas, and insurance among others.

It said Madam Takyi had over a decade working experience in customer care and relations, tele-sales, direct sales, key account management, marketing and public relations in Telecommunications industry (MTN Ghana) and airline industry (AWA).

"Victoria joined AWA in 2013 as a marketing officer in charge of travel agency and corporate sales," it added.

Madam Takyi holds an MBA in International Business Management and Supply Chain Management with IIBM Institute of Management (India) and Bachelor Degree in Marketing and Computing from Regent University College of Science and Technology (Ghana).

She had her high school education at Nsaba Presbyterian Senior High School in the Central Region.

She also holds an International Air Transport Association Certificate in Dynamic Pricing and Revenue Management and Airline Leading Practices and Cost Reduction Strategies.
