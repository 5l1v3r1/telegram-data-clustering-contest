Avon Middle High School evacuated because of gas leak

14 Nov 2019, 19:36 by Mike Saccone

AVON, Mass. - Student and staff at Avon Middle High School were evacuated Thursday because of a small gas leak due to a heater problem, Avon Fire Chief Robert Spurr told Boston 25 News. 

Spurr said the problem has been fixed and the school will be open Friday for a normal day.

"Thank you to the Avon Schools and Avon Police Department for their help," Spurr added.

Boston 25 News also reached out to Avon Public Schools for comment.
