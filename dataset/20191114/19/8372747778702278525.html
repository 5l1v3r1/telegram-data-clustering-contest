Homeowner shoots, kills apparent intruder in Louisiana

14 Nov 2019, 19:38 by KATC Staff

VERMILION PARISH, La. - Deputies in Vermilion Parish, Louisiana, are investigating after a homeowner shot an intruder during an alleged home invasion.

A spokesman with the sheriff's office says 29-year-old Landon Hebert allegedly broke into a residence on around 2:15 this morning when he encountered the home owner.

Deputies say the home owner fired a weapon killing Hebert.

The investigation is ongoing, and the case will be forwarded to the District Attorney's office for further review.
