The all new Motorola RAZR foldable display smartphone will be unveiled later today!

14 Nov 2019, 12:49

Last night, we found out that Motorola’s Facebook account was replaced with new logos which seems to imply the arrival of the new Motorola RAZR 2019 folding phone. Although there’s no official announcement on it but the device live image and renders are exposed today!

From the exposed images, it shows a black design phone with a carbon fiber-like material on the rear. It also adopts the folding method just like the original Motorola RAZR flip phone. When it is unfolded, it has a tall screen display with a top-notch that contains the front-facing camera, earpiece for phone calls and a thick handle on the bottom.

In terms of the tech specs, the Motorola RAZR 2019 uses a 6.2-6.5-inch screen and a 2.7-inch secondary screen display. It is expected to be equipped with a Snapdragon 710 processor, that comes with either a 4GB or 6GB of RAM and an option of 64GB or 128GB storage capacity. With its slim design, the Motorola Razr may come with a smaller 2510mAh-2730mAh battery capacity.

As for the pricing, the Motorola RAZR 2019 is expected to be priced at $1500(~RM6230). Although there’s no official confirmation on the device yet but it is revealed that it will make its public appearance later today! So stay tuned for more updates on TechNave.com!
