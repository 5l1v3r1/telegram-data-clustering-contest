Stacey Solomon's son unveiled as Celtic fan as star shares Insta story on kid doing housework in Hoops strip

The former X Factor star filmed son Leighton trying to tidy up his Lego using a mop as he wore a hoops shirt and shorts.

14 Nov 2019, 12:40 by Jennifer Russell, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/jennifer-russell/

Stacey Solomon has posted a picture on social media of her son wearing a Celtic kit while helping her clean.

The former X Factor star, 30, filmed son Leighton trying to tidy up his Lego using a mop as he wore a hoops shirt and shorts.

She joked she had to resist taking over as his unique method of cleaning was beginning to stress her.

The Loose Women's first picture shows the youngster kneeling down, with Lego scattered all over the floor, as he slowly picked up the pieces.

She captioned it : "What is life?"

By the time she took the second photo, he had devised an unusual yet genius way of gathering it all up - using the mop.

Stacey said: "10 points for imagination. I'm getting slightly obsessed with how he could be doing it so much more efficiently.

"I want to get up and do it. Must resist."

In each of the pictures, Stacey's boy can be seen wearing the green and white hoops.
