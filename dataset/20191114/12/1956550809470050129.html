A new trampoline park is opening on Dubai's Palm Jumeirah

Trampo Extreme is coming to Nakheel Mall

14 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Kelly Johnstone

A brand-new indoor trampoline park is opening on Dubai's Palm Jumeirah.

Kids love bouncing and parents love how tired bouncing makes their kids so news of a brand-new trampoline park coming to the city will be music to everyone's ears.

With Nakheel Mall set to open any day now, Trampo Extreme will be welcoming energetic kids from Thursday November 28.

The high-octane park is almost 4,000 sq ft of bouncing fun with 16 different jump activities ideal for tots of three up to teens of 13.

Older kids will love the combative elements of Extreme Dodgeball and the challenge of the warrior obstacle course, while breaking their PB times on the interactive climbing wall will keep them occupied for hours.

A huge foam pit and free jumping areas are great fun for all the tribe to enjoy together, while the internationally themed Kids Zone takes little adventurers through a series of 12 cool activities across three levels of multi-fun for different ages.

A Western-themed shooting arena, electric-car race track, role play arena and toddlers only area are all waiting to be discovered.

But perhaps most excitingly, if you have inquisitive explorers on your hands, a brand-new attraction called Cave, is coming to Trampo Extreme in the new year.

Featuring the largest indoor caving unit in the world, children can set off to discover various hidden chambers, slides and secret treasures.

The new trampoline park also hosts parties in a private party room, equipped with its own dance floor for the little ones to boogie on down under the disco lights. Plus, if your little ones have a birthday in December, they can take advantage of a one month pass absolutely free – now that's a reason to celebrate.

And don't fret mums and dads, because there is an extra special treat for you too. While the smalls are off jumping around, you can grab a spot and a cup of coffee at the on-site Lime Tree Café. Trampoline parks have never looked so appealing for parents.

This Trampo Extreme is the third in the city. The other locations are in Al Ain Mall and in The Dubai Mall.
Dhs55 (kids aged two), Dhs75 (kids three-five), Dhs85 (kids six and above). Sat-Wed 10am-10pm, Thu & Fri 10am-midnight. Nakheel Mall, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai, www.trampo.com (04 580 4411).
