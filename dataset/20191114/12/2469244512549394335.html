Last chance to get your tickets for the Western Morning News Business Awards

The glittering event is just days away

14 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Charlotte Turner

One of the biggest nights to celebrate Devon and Cornwall’s fantastic businesses is now just one week away.

The Western Morning News Business Awards shines a light on the best of the South West, crowning the people and companies which make our region proud.

This year, the glittering gala held at the Plymouth Pavilions on November 21 will also celebrate 160 years of the Western Morning News.

Bill Martin, editor of the paper, said: “The Western Morning News has been part of the fabric of the Westcountry for 160 years – and in this age of change – I think this is something we should recognise and celebrate.

“The paper has deep-rooted links with communities all over the region. I hope as many people as possible will come and join us celebrate this great newspaper's birthday.”

From Micro Business of the Year to Exporter of the Year, every business has been thought of in the categories, and the awards allow those leaders to stand out.

With only five weeks until the event it’s wise to get your tickets secured now. The awards are also a fantastic opportunity to network with some of the best business minds in the South West.

Don’t miss out on one of the best nights in your business calendar this year.
