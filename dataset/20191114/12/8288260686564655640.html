GO Diving at the Ricoh Arena - the highlights?

Diving holidays are a hugely popular part of the growing adventure travel market, from exploring coral reefs and sunken warships in the Red Sea to swimming among whale sharks and manta rays in the Indian Ocean.

You can get a taste of the action at GO Diving at the Ricoh Arena from February 22 to 24 and here's just a few ways how.

'Trydive' pool

A giant 100 sq m ‘trydive’ pool will be opened up to beginners and seasoned explorers alike, so visitors can get to sample the surreal and enthralling activity of scuba during their visit.

Explore wrecks and underwater caves

Other immersive experiences at the three-day event include the chance to explore wrecks, navigate a vast underwater cave and swim alongside sharks, seals and wrecks though Virtual Reality.

Diving experts

Steve Backshall, a naturalist broadcaster and author, Andy Torbet, an underwater explorer, Monty Halls, presenter of BBC Great Escape, and Miranda Krestovnikoff, a natural history and archaeology broadcaster, will share their passion for diving on the Main Stage at the GO Diving Show.

They’ll be joined by a stellar line-up of familiar faces and newcomers, with three more stages entitled Tech, Underwater Photography and Inspiration. There will be something to draw the interest of visitors, no matter what their level of certification - or even if they have never dived before.

The latest diving kit

 

There is a veritable smorgasbord of equipment manufacturers showcasing their products, including Aqua Lung, Apeks, Shearwater Research, Otter Watersports, Maxshow, Mares, Beuchat, Nautilus Diving, Fourth Element, Suunto, AP Diving, Finnsub, xDeep, Poseidon Diving Systems, Nammu Tech, Santi Diving Equipment, Ocean Reef, and Scubapro.

Talk to retailers such as Simply Scuba, Ocean Leisure, Scuba Store, Robin Hood Watersports, and Andark.

Want to research your next dive course, or find out how to take your first ‘fin-steps’ into scuba?

Talk to training agencies including PADI, SAA, SSI, NAUI and RAID, and specialists such as DAN Europe.

There are a host of tour operators at the Go Diving Show, including Scuba Travel, Scuba Adventures, Caribbean Fun Travel, Regal Dive, Dive Worldwide, Great Escapes Diving, and Diverse Travel.

Prestigious photography competition

And as if all that wasn’t enough, underwater photography supremo Alex Mustard will be announcing the winners of the prestigious  Underwater Photographer of the Year  competition live on the Main Stage on the Saturday afternoon, with large prints of all of the finalists’ images on display over the entire weekend.
