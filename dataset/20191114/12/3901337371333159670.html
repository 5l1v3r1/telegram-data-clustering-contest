The Top African Goalscorers This Season So Far

14 Nov 2019, 12:29 by David Kappel

Mohamed Salah netted his sixth Premier League goal of the 2019/20 campaign against Manchester City on Sunday, but there are four African stars who have scored more league goals than the Liverpool winger has so far this season.

Click through the gallery above to see the leading African goalscorers in Europe's top five leagues so far in this campaign.

Salah’s return thus far has not been as prolific as in his previous two campaigns, but he is still the fifth-best African scorer in 2019/20.

Arsenal's Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang and Metz forward Habib Diallo lead the ranking with eight league strikes each, while Salah's teammate Sadio Mane and Lille's Victor Osimhen follow with seven goals to their names.

A group of six African stars complete the top 11, with all of them on five goals so far. The are: Schalke's Amine Harit, Saint-Etienne's Denis Bouanga, Villarreal's Karl Toko Ekambi, Genoa's Christian Kouame, Rennes' M'Baye Niang and AS Monaco striker Islam Slimani.
