New ‘Diablo IV’ Footage Shows Off Boss Fight

14 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Mike Wilson

Game Informer continues to release footage of Diablo IV, this time posting a four-minute clip showing off a boss fight with a unique monster named Ashava The Pestilent. As is the norm for the series, players will randomly encounter bosses scattered throughout the areas they’ll visit.

Diablo IV is set for release on PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. No release date has been announced as of yet.
