AEW Dynamite: Chris Jericho challenges for Tag Team Championship

14 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Wade Sheridan

Nov. 14 (UPI) -- AEW World Champion Chris Jericho wanted to bring more gold to his group The Inner Circle when he teamed up with Sammy Guevara to challenge SCU for the AEW Tag Team Champions on Dynamite.

Jericho and Guevara were accompanied to the ring by their fellow Inner Circle member Jake Hager for the main event match while Scorpio Sky and Frankie Kazarian of SCU were accompanied by their partner Christopher Daniels.
