'Awful' - Newcastle United fans in meltdown after major announcement

Aston Villa v Newcastle United | When the Premier League returns after the break Dean Smith's side will play host to the Magpies on November 25 in a Monday night showdown

14 Nov 2019, 12:06 by Richard Cusack

Newcastle United captain Jamaal Lascelles will miss his side's clash with Aston Villa on November 25 because of a knee injury that has ruled him out until the New Year.

Lascelles was hurt during a tussle with Callum Wilson during the 2-1 win over AFC Bournemouth on Saturday and was replaced by Paul Dummett just 20 minutes into the Premier League clash.

The Magpies have run the rule over the injury and it has been announced that the 26-year-old will miss at least six weeks of football - the first match of that spell the trip to Villa Park next up for his side.

Lascelles hadn't missed a single match for Steve Bruce 's side and had played all-but-eight minutes of his side's League season before the collision with England striker Wilson.
