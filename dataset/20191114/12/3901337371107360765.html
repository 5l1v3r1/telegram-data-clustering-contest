14 Nov 2019, 12:19 by SL Social

With Zlatan Ibrahimovic announcing his exit from LA Galaxy this week, we throw it back to when the Swede announced himself to Major League Soccer with an inch-perfect 40-yard volley. Check it out!

"Go back to watching baseball" is the message Zlatan left MLS fans as his time with LA Galaxy came to an end. On a personal note, the former Manchester United striker's time with the Los Angeles club was a prolific one, with the Swede having scored 53 goals in 58 games for the team.

In typical "Ibra" fashion, Zlatan announced himself to the Galaxy fans in the most Zlatan way possible by scoring a 40-yard volley 19 minutes into his debut for the club.

Facing rivals Los Angeles FC at the time, his strike ensured that the two sides were tied at 3-3 until the 90th minute. The ex-Barcelona man then scored another goal in added time to inspire Galaxy to a 4-3 win in the El Trafico derby.
