COMPETITION: C'mon home to Dundalk for Christmas!

Dundalk Credit Union

14 Nov 2019, 12:04 by Democrat Reporter

There is no place like home at Christmas, especially when Dundalk is your home! For over 50 years Dundalk Credit Union has celebrated Christmas with the people of Dundalk.

This year, the celebration will spread worldwide, as Dundalk Credit Union want to help bring one lucky person home for Christmas.
