Sindel’s ‘Mortal Kombat 11’ Gameplay Trailer Screams Ahead of Release

14 Nov 2019, 18:45 by Mike Wilson

As Ed Boon had teased yesterday on Twitter, the upcoming DLC release of Sindel as a playable character for Mortal Kombat 11 has received a gameplay trailer. Those who purchased the Kombat Pack will be gaining early access to Sindel on November 26. The rest of us will have to wait two weeks for her to be available.

Mortal Kombat 11 is currently available for PC, PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Nintendo Switch. The Google Stadia version will be made available on November 19th with the launch of the service.
