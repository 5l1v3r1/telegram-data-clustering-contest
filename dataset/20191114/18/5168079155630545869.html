Sayyid Fahd meets top Chinese official

14 Nov 2019, 18:28 by Times News Service

Muscat: His Highness Sayyid Fahd received in his office HE Wang Yang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Permanent Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee.

Oman News Agency said in a statement that: “On behalf of His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said, HH Sayyed Fahad receives HE Wang Yang, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and permanent member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee of the Party of the People's Republic of China.”
