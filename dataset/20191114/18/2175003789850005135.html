Huawei Y9s and Band 4 Launched in Kenya: See Specs and Price

Huawei is no doubt one of the top players in Kenya’s mobile industry. In a market where flagship smartphones cost not more than Ksh 40,000, it is clear that the mid-range segment is vibrant. I mean look at just how popular Huawei Y9 2019 is.

Today, Huawei is throwing yet another hat in the ring with the launch of the Huawei Y9s in Kenya.
