Eric Nam leaves a message for IZ*ONE and X1

14 Nov 2019, 18:05 by honeycider

Singer Eric Nam recently held a press conference for his upcoming full-english album.

During the conference, he was asked to comment on the ongoing vote-rigging controversies surrounding the 'Produce' series, as he also made his debut through an audition program.

To this, Eric Nam first shared that he does not know the details about the issue as he was too busy in preparation for his new album.

Instead, he left a message for the members of the final project groups of 'Produce 48' and 'Produce X 101'―IZ*ONE and X1.

He said, "Those kids would probably be extremely anxious, and they must be having a hard time now. I hope they can find strength and that everything gets resolved. I honestly don't know the details, but I really hope them to find strength."

(source: SBS News)
