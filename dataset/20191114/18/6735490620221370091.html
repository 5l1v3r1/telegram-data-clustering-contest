Goop Releases Their Holiday Gift Guide And It Is Extravagant As You Would Expect

14 Nov 2019, 18:27 by Jamie Samhan

Move over Oprah because Gwyneth Paltrow and her team at Goop have just released their Holiday Gift Guide.

The presents range from $9 up to $1.3 million depending on if you are on Santa’s naughty or nice list.

For those really into composting, $1,199 will get you the WLads Innovations food recycler and composter.

Filed under “ridiculous but awesome” gifts you can find a brass fire extinguisher, a modular prefab home, joint roller or flight suit training.

If the person on your list has those items already, try the Rosetta Getty shearling loafers or Goop ski pants.

The mother of two hasn’t forgotten about the littles on your list. A stunning convertible bassinet that can change into an ottoman later on might be worth the $2,600. If that is a little too much, try the Rosa Parks Barbie for $31.

The more practical items named include a voice translator, hairbrush or Nyajio de-puffing and firming eye cream.

If you are wondering what $1.3 million gets you, it is pretty awesome. The pricey gift is from Momentum Adventure and includes 12 endangered-species focused trips which could be “spread out or taken all at once.”

And there are even suggestions for man’s best friend like the Den Urban Dog Retreat for “wellness-centric doggy daycare.”
