Christmas dinner flavour Gyozas now exist filled with turkey, bacon and stuffing

Itsu has launched a new festive flavour gyoza that combines the Japanese dumplings with a traditional Christmas dinner, packed full of turkey, bacon, stuffing and even cranberry sauce

14 Nov 2019, 14:47 by Molly Holt, https://www.facebook.com/mollyjaholt/

Out of all the food hybrids out there, we never thought we'd see the day al the key ingredients of a traditional British roast dinner were crammed into a Japanese gyoza.

But now you can eat lots of little bite-sized roast dinners as you walk, as Itsu has created what's surely the world's first Christmas dinner gyoza.

A festive feast packed into a soft steamed dumpling, each gyoza is filled with turkey, bacon, stuffing and even cranberry sauce, working out at just 302 calories each.

As well as tasting unique, the dumplings are also bright red, thanks to a natural beetroot flavouring.

Set to launch next Wednesday (20 November) in Itsu stores all across the UK, a portion of eight 'Christmas cracker' gyoza will set you back £4.29.

If you are heading into an Itsu store, make sure to put on your biggest festive smile, as one special Christmas cracker is also being handed out to a customer each day.

Inside the so-called its’mas crackers are a joke, sticker, Christmas hat and exclusive prize, which range from free Christmas cracker gyoza portions to an itsu pink card, which gives you a year's worth of half price itsu. Please Itsu, we've been really good this year...
