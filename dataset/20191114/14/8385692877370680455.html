The Verizon Exclusive Motorola razr Doesn't Work On Verizon's 5G Network

14 Nov 2019, 14:21

The Motorola razr is going to be exclusive to Verizon, and for some reason it won't support its 5G network.

Actually, the reason is pretty understandable. With the razr being such a small and thin device, there wasn't much room for other bands (other than the three LTE bands that Verizon needs) for LTE. Let alone enough room for a 5G radio and 5G bands that would be needed to work on Verizon's 5G network.

So while it might sound odd that a Verizon Exclusive smartphone doesn't work on Verizon's shiny new 5G network, it makes sense.

Not all Verizon-exclusive smartphones run on 5G anyways

Verizon has a number of other exclusive smartphones on its network that don't use 5G. So this isn't a big deal breaker. But when you're looking at a smartphone that costs $1499, many might expect it to have 5G.

After all, the Galaxy S10+ 5G is the same price, and does sport 5G connectivity.

Motorola's Moto Z4 and Moto Z3 also have 5G connectivity through the 5G Moto Mod. So it's not like Motorola hasn't done a 5G phone before. Well technically they haven't, just a Moto Mod.

5G is still pretty early, so for Motorola to forego it on the razr is not a big surprise and it's probably for the better. After all, the razr already has plenty of first-generation tech inside.

Not to mention that Verizon is spending millions on advertising their 5G network. And their headlining exclusive smartphone isn't going to work on that network. Seems like a golden opportunity was missed here.

But it was likely for the better. As 5G is still very new and the battery consumption isn't great. The Motorola razr isn't going to have great battery life anyways, with a 2510mAh capacity battery and a 6.2-inch display.

Just remember that if you are looking to purchase the Motorola razr, it's not going to work on Verizon 5G.
