The Villains Take Center Stage in DC’s Upcoming Spinoff Miniseries ‘DCeased: Unkillables’!

14 Nov 2019, 14:52 by John Squires

The sixth and final issue of DC’s Tom Taylor-penned horror event miniseries DCeased was released last month, and we’ve learned today that a spinoff series is coming early next year.

Titled DCeased: Unkillables, the 3-issue miniseries will show us what DC’s villains were up to while the world was devolving into chaos during the events of DCeased!

The blockbuster DC series returns to answer this question: What did the villains do when the heroes failed and the world ended?

“Spinning out of the dramatic events of 2019’s smash hit, writer Tom Taylor returns to this dark world with a street-level tale of death, heroism and redemption. Led by Red Hood and Deathstroke, DC’s hardest villains and antiheroes fight with no mercy to save the only commodity left on a dying planet of the undead—life!”

Each issue will be 48-pages, releasing between February 19-April 2020.

In the meantime, the collected DCeased graphic novel releases on November 20, 2019.
