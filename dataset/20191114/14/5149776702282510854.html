Man arrested on suspicion of robbery after security guard threatened at Asda

Police were in the area at the time

14 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Matthew Bunn

Police have arrested a man on suspicion of robbery after an incident at an Asda in West Bridgford in the early hours of Thursday, November 14.

Police were called to the store in Loughborough Road at about 1am to reports of a man trying to take £300 of goods while 'threatening a security guard with a weapon'.

Nottinghamshire Police has confirmed a 46-year-old man has been arrested.

Detective Sergeant Charlotte Henson, of Nottinghamshire Police , said: “Officers happened to be in the area while this incident took place and their quick action prevented the suspect from fleeing the scene.

“Nottinghamshire Police takes all reports of theft and robbery seriously and will investigate all reports thoroughly."

Witnesses are asked to call 101 and quote incident number 20 of 14 November 2019
