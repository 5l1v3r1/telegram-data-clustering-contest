Workman in hospital after 'coming into contact with power cable' in Maryhill, Glasgow

There were reports of power cuts in the surrounding area following the incident at 12.35pm.

14 Nov 2019, 14:57 by Sally Hind, Julie Dunnett, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/sally-hind/, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/julie-gilbert/

A workman has been taken to hospital following an incident in the Maryhill area of Glasgow.

He is understood to have come into contact with a power cable while working in Shawpark Road around 12.35pm today.

Emergency Services rushed to the area and the man who's in his forties was taken to Glasgow Royal Infirmary.

There were reports of power cuts in the surrounding area following the incident.

 

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "Police Scotland attended at Shawpark Road in Glasgow around 12.30pm on Thursday, 14 November following a report of a man having suffered an injury while working in the area.

"The man has been taken to hospital for treatment. The Health and Safety Executive has been made aware."

A Scottish Ambulance Service spokesperson said: "We received a call at 12.35pm today to attend an incident in Maryhill.

"We dispatched our trauma team, our special operations team, an advanced practitioner, one ambulance and a paramedic response unit to the scene.

We transported one male patient in his 40s to Glasgow Royal Infirmary.”
