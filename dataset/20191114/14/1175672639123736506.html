Four companies with multiple roles on Dezeen Jobs including Büro Ole Scheeren and Starck Network Agency

14 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Amina Amber

This week we've selected four companies with plenty of job opportunities available on Dezeen Jobs, including a number of vacancies at architecture studio Büro Ole Scheeren and Philippe Starck's design studio.

Positions at Büro Ole Scheeren

Büro Ole Scheeren has a number of opportunities in Beijing, London and Berlin, including roles for visual artists and landscape architects. The firm won World Building of the Year 2015 for The Interlace, a "vertical village" of apartment blocks that have been arranged diagonally to frame terraces and communal courtyards.

Opportunities at Starck Network Agency

French designer Philippe Starck designed the interiors of Jean Nouvel's luxury hotel in Sāo Paulo, which features a palette of earthy suede tones and dark wooden dining tables. Starck's studio is looking for a number of designers in Paris, including roles for interior designers, furniture designers and FF&E designers.

Roles at Seymourpowell

Seymourpowell is hiring industrial designers, design engineers and transport designers to join its office in London. The studio has conceptualised an intelligent cosmetics service that uses AI to tailor packages of skincare and beauty products to individual users.

Vacancies at Ippolito Fleitz Group

Ippolito Fleitz Group designed a dining room for German magazine Der Spiegel's headquarters in Hamburg, which features satin-polished aluminium panels and large yellow discs that conceal electrical wiring and fittings on the ceiling. The design studio is seeking employees for various roles in Berlin and Stuttgart, including jobs for intermediate and senior designers.
