Photos of the day: Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone seek blessings at Venkateshwara temple, Priyanka Chopra lands in Mumbai, Akshay Kumar spotted at Hinduja hospital and other celebrity spottings

14 Nov 2019, 14:23 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Like we always say, there is no single day in B-town, which can be tagged as 'dull'. Today, apart from being a children's day, there were many events that took place around the city. Fans were eagerly eyeing on what, how and where Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh will spend their special day. Finally, the internet was hit by some alluring pictures of the couple along with the family seeking blessings at Venkateshwara temple, Tirumala on their first wedding anniversary.

Later in the day, team 'Pagalpanti' which is set to hit the screens soon, promoted their film with much pomp and funfair. Spotted were actors Anil Kapoor, John Abraham, Ileana D'cruz, Arshad Warsi, Urvashi Rautela, Pulkit, and others.

Today, there were not many airport spottings. Later in the day, 'The Sky Is The Pink' star Priyanka Chopra was spotted at the Mumbai airport and the actress was pretty much happy to see blue sky after spending time in Dehli. She was clicked at the airport flaunting her uber-cool style in semi-formal attire. PeeCee opted for a balloon sleeved white shirt which she teamed up with black denim and completed her look with golden stilettos and stylish sunnies.

On the occasion of Children's day, while other celebs opted to share their childhood pictures on social media, Tamannaah Bhatia celebrated with hearing and visually impaired children. Akshay Kumar in the evening was spotted at Hinduja hospital.
