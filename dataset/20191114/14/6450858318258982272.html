Currys Black Friday deal: Get Switch with free Pokemon Sword or Shield

Black Friday deals are here. Pick up a free copy of Pokemon Shield or Pokemon Sword with a brand new Nintendo Switch Neon console from Currys PC World.

14 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Somrata Sarkar

The Pokemon Sword and Shield games release globally on Friday 15 November, but you can get it for free with Currys PC World's early Black Friday bundle offer on the Nintendo Switch Neon console. That's right: pick up a Switch from Currys and get one of the latest Pokemon games for free, a £44.99 value otherwise.

Head to Currys to pick up the Switch console with Pokemon Sword or Pokemon Shield.

The Switch and Pokemon bundle costs £279 and also includes access to Spotify Premium for six months, which is worth nearly £60 alone. Now that's what we call great value for money.

The deal comes as a part of Currys' 'Why Wait?' sale, which kicks off Black Friday savings two weeks in advance. While Currys will separately host a Black Friday sale, which you can register for to get early deals notifications, the retailer does promise deal prices will not be lower then. If current deal prices are further reduced on Black Friday, Currys will refund customers the difference.

Black Friday is on 29 November this year, though like Currys' 'Why Wait?' event, sales from several retailers and brands have already started.
