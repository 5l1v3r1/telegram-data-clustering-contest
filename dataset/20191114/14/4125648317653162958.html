Russian choppers take off from base in northeast Syria: video

14 Nov 2019, 14:13 by News Desk

Three helicopters of Russia’s Aerospace Force have redeployed to the Qamishli airfield in northern Syria to provide cover for military police convoys, the TV Channel Zvezda reported on Thursday.

“The first group of helicopters of Russia’s Aerospace Force has redeployed from Hmeymim to the Qamishli airport. They flew to Syria’s north for about five hours,” says the footage posted on the TV Channel’s website.
