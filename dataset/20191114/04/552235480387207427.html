The 12 top toys of Christmas revealed

Toy Story 4, Frozen 2 and Star Wars film merchandise will bring £1billion to the UK toy market, alongside small-screen favourites Paw Patrol and Peppa Pig

14 Nov 2019, 04:30 by Jennifer Russell, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/jennifer-russell/

The 12 toys of ChristmasView gallery

Major Hollywood movies are set to drive this year’s Christmas must have toy list, experts reveal.

Toy Story 4, Frozen 2 and Star Wars film merchandise will bring £1billion to the UK toy market, alongside small-screen favourites Paw Patrol and Peppa Pig.

The annual Dream Toys list, issued by the Toy Retailers Association, is the most authoritative list of what will be hot this Christmas.

The list is selected by a panel of retailers and industry experts and is independent of toy manufacturers and makers.

The trend for "surprise" and "reveal" toys remains strong, with LOL Surprise! still the most popular collectable for a second year in a row. The cheapest on the list is the Blume Doll at under £10 with the Barbie Dreamplane Playset the most expensive.

 

Gary Grant, chair of the DreamToys selection committee said: "This year’s DreamToys top 12 selection is an exciting mixture of the year’s biggest brands and kids favourites that will delight children up and down the country this Christmas,”

“These toys will top the Christmas wish lists and with two-thirds of the toys on the list priced under £50, it’s also a list suitable to a range of family budgets.”

From the Harry Potter Knight Bus to Nerf Elite Fortnite, here’s the complete list of 12:

Barbie Dreamplane Playset £79.99

Ready to help kids’ imaginations take off, the Barbie Dreamplace comes with realistic touches like reclining seats, overhead compartments and a snack cart.

Children can push it on its wheels to taxi on the ground or open up the top and side to reveal and play inside the plane.

Blume Dolls £9.99

The must have fashion doll. Just add water and like magic a new friend will grow before your very eyes. Featuring imaginative and over-the-top-hairstyles, Blume dolls grow out of flower pots and come with surprises and accessories.

Frozen 2 Into the Unknown Elsa Doll £39.99

 

Anna and Elsa are back for another winter themed adventure and the new film merchandise coincides with the big budget release. The latest doll sings “Into the Unknown” and says 14 different phrases from the movie.

 

Harry Potter Knight Bus £34.99

Whether you are a muggle or a wizard everyone will want to get onboard the Harry Potter Knight Bus, complete with driver, conductor and a Harry figurine. As the vehicle races away, swerving around corners and bouncing over bumps, the bed slides back and forth and the chandelier swings from the ceiling - just like the real thing.

LOL Surprise! 2-in-1 Glamper £99.99

A firm favourite returns from last year with even more surprises. The 2-in-1 Glamper has over ten play areas with lights and sounds, a removable cab, an exclusive doll and more than 55 surprises to unbox.

Nerf Elite Fortnite SP-L £19.99

 

Inspired by the blaster used in Fortnite with a similar look and colour from the popular video game, kids can play the game in real life. The blaster has a detachable barrel to customize for different ways to play.

 

Owleez £49.99

These interactives birds act like real owls as you teach them to fly through the air making triumphant and happy noises. Owleez will let you know when they are ready to fly themselves and will take off from their nests - just like a real baby bird.

Paw Patrol Mighty Pups Super Paws Mighty Jet Command Centre £59.99

Take the Paw Patrol gang on new adventures with the 2-in-1 delux vehicle as it transforms from a jet into a mobile command centre for the Paw Patrol. On land, lift the handle on the jet to switch into mobile command headquarters and when it’s time to go on a rescue mission transform it back.

Peppa Pig Peppa's Stage Playset £39.99

 

Re-enact your favourite Peppa episode or create your very own show for family and friends with Peppa's Stage Playset. This interactive playset includes loads of props, as well as Peppa and George figures to help bring your production to life.

Rizmo £59.99

Your new favourite pet who’s crashed down from outer space looking for a new home. How fast Rizmo evolves is up to you. All you have to do is play, sing and love Rizmo to help them grow. Each Rizmo has their own characteristics and musical style, all influenced by how you play.

 

Ryan's World Super Surprise Safe £44.99

Kids can use the key to open the safe and discover cool surprises inside, such as articulated figures, mix and match build a characters, and treasure. Push and reveal, smash and surprise, decode and discover all of the amazing surprises inside.Each safe has over 30 fun surprises inside.

Toy Story 4 True Talkers £17.99

Forky is the newest character from the lovable family movie, this 7-inch spork come to life with moving facial features. Featuring more than 20 phrases from the film kids can have hours of fun recreating favourite scenes or thinking up new adventures with Buzz, Woody and Bo Peep.
