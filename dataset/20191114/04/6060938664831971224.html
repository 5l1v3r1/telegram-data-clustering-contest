Here's why Amrita Singh is not happy about Sara Ali Khan and Kartik Aaryan’s New Year plans

14 Nov 2019, 04:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sara Ali Khan is one of the most famous star kids in B-town. The star marked her big Bollywood debut with 'Kedarnath' opposite Sushant Singh Rajput and ever since then, there was no looking back for her. The star recently made headlines for her alleged break up with Kartik Aaryan.

Now, a report surfaced on the Internet which revealed that the couple is even planning to bring in the New Year together. This has left their fans excited to see their favourite couple getting back together. Rumours are at a buzz that Sara's mother Amrita Singh is not happy about the same. An entertainment portal reported that she feels that it is high time for Sara to concentrate on her career, as the 'Luka Chuppi' actor might turn out to be a distraction for her.

The source in the report stated that Amrita feels it is now the time for the 'Simmba' actress to concentrate on her career and not get distracted by matters of the heart. While Sara feels that it’s okay to mix work with personal life.

Meanwhile, on the work front, Sara and Kartik will be seen next in Imtiaz Ali's next titled 'Aaj Kal' (tentatively). She will also star opposite Varun Dhawan in 'Coolie no. 1'.
