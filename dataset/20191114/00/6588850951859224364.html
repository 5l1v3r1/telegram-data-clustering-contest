‘I Regret Engaging In Premarital Sex – Lady Prempeh

14 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Gospel musician Lady Prempeh says she regrets having premarital sex before she tied the knot.

Speaking in an interview with Hitz FM, the 'Aseda' singer condemned the act, says it’s ‘disgraceful’ although it is considered normal these days. “That’s something, to me, I felt I shouldn’t have done it. I should have waited till I got married,” she stated.

The singer added, “premarital sex looks normal now but if you know whom you are serving, that is a no go area for Him.”
Lady Prempeh, however, advised fans to follow the teachings of Christ especially in so-called contemporary times when premarital sex and other worldly life is considered the norm. “If you have to follow this path you must be on it. Don’t try to sway or go your path and believe God will be with you. No, you have to go his way,” she added.
