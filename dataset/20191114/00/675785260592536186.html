Kylie Jenner says she's 'made the naughty list' as she sparkles in huge red bow

Kylie Jenner is typically provocative in the latest pics for her new cosmetics collection as she enters the festive season with a firm eye on being on Santa's bad list

14 Nov 2019, 00:52 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Kylie Jenner has declared that she has "made the naughty list" this year.

The 22-year-old billionaire make-up mogul is once again in sparkling form as she teases the launch of a new cosmetic collection for the festive season.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians star revealed that there was something new from Kylie Cosmetics by posing in a sparkling red bow on Instagram .

Kylie shared with her 151 million followers that she had something for Christmas lists across the world, with a sizzling pic that had amassed over a million likes in its first hour.

Dressed in red, with a giant gift bow in hair, Kylie looked like the gift that would top many people's want list, as she made reference to a turbulent few months in her personal life.

The reality star has split from rapper Travis Scott, the father of her daughter Stormi Webster, and has since been linked with a romantic liaison with Drake.

She was seen at his side for much of his 33rd birthday party and there have been rumours that they have 'dated' since then.

This has all been denied by sources close to Kylie, especially as Drake has been a long-term friend of the Kardashian family.

Captioning the pic, Kylie wrote: "Made the naughty list this year..️ my HOLIDAY collection is around the corner. Who’s ready for the reveal?"

Kourtney Kardashian's ex Scott Disick's current beau, Sofia Richie, was among the first to comment, saying: "In love w you"

Meanwhile, Kylie has been forced to deny that she threatened a small company with legal action after she trademarked the popular saying 'Rise and Shine.'

She took to Twitter over the weekend to deny reports that her legal team contacted a small clothing business based in Australia - after they carried T-shirts with the well-known phrase emblazoned on.

In response to the reports, the mother-of-one wrote: "Guys, please don't believe everything you read. I have not sent any Rise and Shine cease and desist letters."

She explained: "Rise and Shine was an unexpected moment.. I had a lot of fun with it, and I have laughed so hard seeing everyone's memes since the video came out a few weeks ago."

The make-up mogul then followed up with a second Tweet that read: "There are no law suits, no cease and desist letters. Happy Sunday."
