PETA Raises The Bar For Ugly Xmas Sweater With Mutilated Sheep Design

14 Nov 2019 by Yimin Huang

Ugly Christmas sweaters are great for the holiday season, but PETA has taken the “ugliness” to a whole new level by selling a sweater design with 3D mutilated sheep stuffed toy. The bloodied front of the sheep protrudes out from the front of the sweater, while its rear sticks out at the back.
The sweater is part of an anti-wool campaign by PETA, with the words “wool hurts” in block letters featured on the garment. The PETA shop also ensured that the sweater is made of 100% acrylic yarn, without any wool, per Metro UK.
The product description on the website alleges that workers have been caught “beating,” “kicking” and even killing sheep in shearing sheds.

The organization also mentions that celebrities like Billie Eilish and P!nk have aided the movement against wool, and urges Forever 21 to halt any wool merchandise.
