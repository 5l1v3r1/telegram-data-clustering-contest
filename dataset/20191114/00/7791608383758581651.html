Hugh Jackman Teases Ryan Reynolds While Praising John Legend’s Sexiest Man Title

14 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Left image via Tinseltown / Shutterstock.com, middle image via DFree / Shutterstock.com, right image via Twocoms / Shutterstock.com
People magazine has announced its 2019 Sexiest Man Alive, and it is none other than singer John Legend.
Alumnus Hugh Jackman thus decided to congratulate Legend on his new title, while taking the opportunity to take a jab at Ryan Reynolds.
Jackman posted a video on Instagram showing Reynolds and him walking through a room before dropping a 2008 copy of the magazine announcing Jackman as the year’s Sexiest Man Alive. Reynolds then responds, “I have an issue somewhere, I have a few of yours.”
“Usually I cut the eyes out or the mouth, but that’s just me,” continues Reynolds, who was crowned with the same title back in 2010.

In his video’s caption, Jackman congratulated Legend before slyly throwing Reynolds under the bus.
“Dear John, massive congrats on joining the prestigious group. 99-percent of the time, People magazine gets it right,” he wrote. “But a word of caution. There is the one percent. Exhibit A. It’s important that you fall in with the right crowd,” he joked.
