Asian qualifiers: Today's scores and stats

14 Nov 2019 by FIFA.com

Several of Asian football's biggest names were in action today as the continent's second qualifying round for the 2022 FIFA World Cup Qatar™ continued.

Here are all of today's results and stand-out statistics.

Results

Myanmar 4-3 Tajikistan

  • Myanmar shock Tajikistan in a seven-goal thriller to pick up their first points of the campaign, ending a three-game winless run and dealing a heavy blow to their visitors' qualifying hopes.

Maldives 1-2 Philippines

  • Two goals in 16 second-half minutes secure the Philippines a key victory over Maldives, taking the visitors on to seven points in Group A.

Turkmenistan 3-1 Korea DPR

  • Turkmenistan register a surprisingly comfortable win over Korea DPR - erstwhile joint-leaders of Group H - and move on to six points, just one point below their beaten visitors.

Kyrgyz Republic 0-2 Japan

  • Japan retain their perfect record thus far. The Samurai Blue have now gone four games without dropping a point or conceding a goal.

Uzbekistan 2-3 Saudi Arabia

  • A stunning late comeback gives Saudi Arabia victory in this vital top-of-the-table clash and sees them leapfrog their hosts to seize top spot in Group D. This was the Saudis' 70th win in World Cup qualifying.
