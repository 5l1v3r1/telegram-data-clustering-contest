Tourism Tax

14 Nov 2019, 00:09 by BRIEF

GREENBELT, Md. – A federal court in Maryland dismissed RICO claims brought by a proposed class of Mexican nationals whose airfare purchases included a “Mexico Tourism Tax,” even though they were not supposed to be charged the tax as Mexican citizens.
