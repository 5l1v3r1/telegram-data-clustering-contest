Cheeky Gender Reveal Passes Through Bare Butt In The Form Of A Fart

14 Nov 2019 by Thanussha Priyah

Gender reveal parties have gotten crazier over the years and have caused detrimental effects on others. Last year, one such affair escalated to a car explosion. Coupled with conventional connotations tied to gender, some have criticized that gender reveal parties are doing more damage than good.
But the internet has discovered a new method for this tradition. Come to think of it, it doesn’t really do much damage—it probably just stinks. Just when it seems like there is one too many gender reveal party, this video might change your mind.
YouTuber Paige Ginn shared a clip on her Instagram and YouTube channel revealing her baby’s gender in an unusual way.
The revelation, this time, comes through her anus. The seven-second clip posted online shows Ginn lying on the floor, baring her behind as she concentrates on passing gas.
Viewers are then treated to Ginn’s fart, which shoots out in the form of blue-colored powder from her butt, revealing the baby’s gender.
It’s a boy, if you are wondering.
The video then pans over to her three fans who cheer in excitement as they find out the baby’s gender. The whole video has the internet split as some are left in stitches, while others regret ever chancing upon the video.
