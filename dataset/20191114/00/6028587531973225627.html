Samsung 65-inch Q70

Samsung has poured a ton of cash into developing its QLED TVs over the past few years, and they represent some of the best that TV technology currently has to offer. With a Samsung QLED TV, you’ll get bright, vivid colors with a natural look to them. Thanks to the fact that the TV has local dimming, you’ll have nice, deep black levels too -- which is always a handy touch. 

The TV is pretty smart too. The Q70 TV offers Samsung’s Tizen operating system, which allows you to install your favorite streaming apps and services. It also lets you integrate the device with your preferred smart home ecosystem, thanks to the fact that it’s compatible with Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa.

The 65-inch model of the TV normally comes at $2,197.99, but it’s currently available for $1,197.99 -- which is an impressive $1,000 off.

Sony 65-inch A8G

OLED TVs arguably offer an even better image quality than QLED TVs, and Sony makes some of the best. The Sony A8G not only offers an OLED panel, but it’s also super smart. That’s thanks to the fact that the TV has Android TV lt right it, including the much-loved Google Assistant. 

OLED panels look great for a number of reasons. For starters, they offer nice, bright, vivid colors. Beyond that, however, is the fact that OLED panels can switch on and off individual pixels -- meaning that in darker scenes, you’ll get super deep and natural black levels.

Vizio 65-inch P-Series Quantum

If you want the advantages of quantum dot technology without having to pay the high price tags, Vizio’s P-Series Quantum is the way to go. The TV offers beautifully bright colors that look just like they would in real life. As you would expect, you’ll get a 4K resolution and HDR support too, which means you’ll be able to enjoy all your favorite content in stunning detail.

This TV is also pretty smart. It has Vizio’s SmartCast operating system built into it, meaning you can easily watch your favorite content from your favorite streaming platforms. Not only that, but it works with Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, and Apple’s HomeKit -- which means it should work in pretty much any smart home. 

The 65-inch model of the TV normally comes at $1,399.99 -- but you can currently get it for only $899.99.

TCL 55S525

If you want a great TV at a super affordable price, then this TV from TCL has a lot going for it. Even at this price, you’ll get a 4K resolution and support for HDR, so all your movies and TV shows should look great. 

Not only does the image look pretty good, but the TV has the Roku operating system built into it too. Roku is relatively easy to use and supports most major smart streaming services, plus it supports Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa.

The TV normally comes at an already-affordable $699.99, but it’s now available for an even better $399.99.
