NBA-Owned Logo Design Contest Gets Trolled After Denying Creatives Of Cash Prize

14 Nov 2019 by Mikelle Leow

Designers will pull the satirical “graphic design is my passion” card when necessary, and this was the case when an esports team owned by NBA’s Milwaukee Bucks launched a logo design contest without a proper incentive for participants.
According to Insider, Bucks Gaming called on creatives on Twitter to design a new header for its team, adding that the winner would receive free merchandise and accreditation in its profile bio. The tweet has since been taken down, along with a number of insensitive comments from the team.
Graphic designers requested for a cash prize, and when one recommended that Bucks Gaming hired a professional for the job, he was reportedly told to “get a job then.”
