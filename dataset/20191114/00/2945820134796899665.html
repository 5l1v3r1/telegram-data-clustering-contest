Valencia: fine culture and sporting heritage

The traditional end of season finale is one not to be missed

14 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

The city of Valencia has a bit of everything – fantastic parks and public spaces, beaches, museums, galleries, fine architecture, great food, friendly people and superb nightlife. And as the traditional MotoGP™ season finale setting, it’s a weekend not to be missed.

The stadium-style nature of the stands around the track means the whole of the circuit can be viewed by the fans as the annual MotoGP™ fiesta reaches a crescendo, with the city boasting a fine cultural and sporting heritage, as well as being famed for fantastic fiestas. Barcelona and Madrid take up the main spotlight, but Valencia has its own intensity and story to tell.

Given its excellent Mediterranean climate Valencia is a pleasant place to be, with the historic city centre being a marvel in itself, districts such as the Barrio del Carmen providing great nightlife with its maze of winding streets, lined with trendy bars, cafes and restaurants tell you that.

Book your tickets to Valencia today and enjoy what promises to be a sensational weekend!
