Cold snap kicks off stranding season for sea turtles

14 Nov 2019

EASTHAM, Mass. (AP) - Several sea turtles have washed ashore along Cape Cod Bay since frigid temperatures hit the area.

The Massachusetts Audubon Society’s Wellfleet Bay Wildlife Sanctuary says 18 sea turtles have washed ashore, mostly in Eastham, and hundreds more cold-stunned turtles are expected to wash ashore in the next six weeks.

The New England Aquarium said in a statement that stranding season doesn’t occur anywhere else in the world on this scale. It happens when cold snaps immobilize turtles and strong winds strands them ashore due to the Cape’s hook-like shape.
