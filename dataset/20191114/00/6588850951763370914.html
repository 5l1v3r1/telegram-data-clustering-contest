The 2019 Sexiest Man Alive Steps Out

14 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

He is certainly living up to his new title.

John Legend looked handsome as ever while seen out and about in Los Angeles for the first time since being named 2019 People magazine Sexiest Man Alive.
The 40-year-old crooner looked stylish in a brown Fendi shearling-trimmed wool-fleece blouson jacket - which retails at $3,980 - on the solo outing Wednesday.
