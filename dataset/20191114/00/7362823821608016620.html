Michael Avenatti sees 2 conspiracy charges dropped in Nike case, 1 wire fraud charge added to indictment

14 Nov 2019, 00:19 by Bradford Betz

A federal case charging California lawyer Michael Avenatti with conspiring to extort millions from Nike has been rewritten to drop two counts of conspiracy.

Avenatti tweeted Wednesday that he was “extremely pleased” and expects to be “fully exonerated.”

He told Fox News, “The government’s case is falling apart as I knew it would.”
