Emily Ratajkowski Flaunts Her Brand’s Blazers By Wearing Them Shirtless

14 Nov 2019 by Thanussha Priyah

Emily Ratajkowski, model and now turned entrepreneur with her fashion label Inamorata, has teased the brand’s first-ever blazers by modeling them herself.
A photo posted on Inamorata’s Instagram account is captioned, “Inamorata Suits. Next week,” giving followers a glimpse of a pale blue blazer that Ratajkowski modeled without an inner shirt.
Another sees her dressed in burnt orange outerwear. “[You all] got so excited, we decided to put these babies on pre-sale. Watch this space for updates. What color are you the most into?” the post reads.
Fans can look forward to snagging these sleek blazers on the Inamorata site.

The brand predominantly retails swimwear, tops, bottoms, dresses and bodysuits, and has now added blazers to its range.
