New jobs on the way as 24/7 gym prepares to open in Whitley Bay town centre

Snap Fitness will open a club on Park Avenue, in Whitley Bay, in December, at a site previously occupied by a McColl’s store

14 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Sonia Sharma

A new 24/7 gym is preparing to open its doors in a town centre, creating around 10 jobs.

Snap Fitness is setting up a base on Park Avenue, in Whitley Bay, and is scheduled to open in December.

The site was previously home to a McColl’s store and a post office. They both closed towards the end of 2017, though the post office later re-opened nearby on Whitley Road.

Now, work is under way to turn the Park Avenue premises into a fitness club.

The 8,000sqm facility will include a range of equipment from Matrix Fitness including cardio machines, a training area with a sled track, battle ropes, core bags and plyo boxes, as well as a weights area with dumbbells from 2kg to 50kg.

The club will have three group exercise studios including a dedicated spin section offering over a hundred classes for members to choose from. There will also be private changing facilities, with individual showers.

Isaac Buchanan, CEO of Snap Fitness, said: "We're excited to be opening a new site here in Whitley Bay and to have the town as part of the growing Snap Fitness UK family.

"Our members enjoy the ease of having 24/7 access to world class equipment all with a community feel.

"So, if you're new to the gym or it’s been a while since you’ve worked out, we can help get you started on your fitness journey.

"Even if you're an experienced gym goer, our top-class personal trainers and variety in equipment will help keep your workouts fresh.

"We are passionate about making Snap Fitness Whitley Bay a happy and healthy place for all members young and old, at all levels of experience."

Memberships will be available on a rolling contract so that users are not locked in and will include group fitness classes and access to the premises 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
