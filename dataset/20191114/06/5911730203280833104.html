Deputies locate 3 missing girls last seen in Homosassa

14 Nov 2019, 06:28 by WFTS Digital Staff

UPDATE — The Citrus County Sheriff's Office says the children have been found safe. No other information has been released.

ORIGINAL STORY — Deputies in Citrus County are asking for the public's help in finding three missing girls, all of whom are 3 years of age or younger.

According to the Citrus County Sheriff's Office, the three girls were last seen leaving South Le Baron Drive in Homosassa Wednesday afternoon.

Here are the names of the missing girls:

  • Kaylee Nance, 9 months old
  • Julia Nance, 1 year old
  • Isabella Tardi, 3 years old

The three girls were last seen with 23-year-old Roxanne Alaimo, of Brooksville. Alaimo was last seen in a red Ford truck with a Texas tag #GPR8502.

Deputies believe the girls could be in Spring Hill.

Anyone with information on Alaimo or the three girls are asked to call 352-726-1121.
