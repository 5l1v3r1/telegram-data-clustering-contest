Fans go crazy as Ranveer Singh and Vijay Deverakonda come together for an unmissable selfie

14 Nov 2019, 06:29 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

While Ranveer Singh is currently in Tirupati with his wife Deepika Padukone and their families as they celebrate their first wedding anniversary seeking the divine blessings, a picture of the 'Gully Boy' actor with south sensation Vijay Deverakonda has surfaced on the internet.

The 'Arjun Reddy' star can be seen flaunting his infectious smile while he takes a selfie with Bollywood's Mr Cool, who seems to be in his elements as they strike a pose together. While the fans are going gaga over them on social media, both can be seen giving out major outfit goals as they flaunt their uber-cool side in their casual best.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Vijay is busy shooting for 'World Famous Lover' and also learning boxing for his next film 'Fighter'. On the other hand, Ranveer will soon be seen alongside his wife Deepika in Kabir Khan's sports drama '83'.
