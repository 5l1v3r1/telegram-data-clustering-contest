Children’s Day 2019: Ekta Kapoor shares the most adorable video of son Ravie Kapoor with grandfather Jeetendra

14 Nov 2019, 06:42 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ekta Kapoor is celebrating her son, Ravie Kapoor’s first Children’s Day and the filmmaker posted an adorable video of her kid with his grandfather Jeetendra. Ekta has never showed her toddler’s face in all the pictures and videos she posts and this time too the kiddo was a part of the frame but the clip was taken from behind. Jeetendra held Ravie Kapoor in his arms while the kid tried to pull his mother’s hair and this fun video made for an adorable Children’s Day memory.

Interestingly, Neena Gupta asked Ekta to show her son’s face and commented on the post, “Dikha do na shakal” which evoked a hilarious response from Ekta who replied, “neena_gupta maaam meri maa mjhe ghar se nikal degi.” Neetu Kapoor too dropped hearts and kisses on the adorable click. Ekta had announced the arrival of her son through surrogacy in January this year.

Ekta also shared a video of brother, Tusshar Kapoor’s son Laksshya Kapoor on Instagram. The three year old was snapped enjoying a ride with his grandfather.
