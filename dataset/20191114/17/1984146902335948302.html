Premier League rumours: Leicester not interested in Morelos; Spurs, Liverpool and Man United boost

The latest transfer news and gossip from the Premier League

14 Nov 2019, 17:10 by James Findlater

The international break might be the focus this weekend, but there’s still plenty of Premier League gossip to keep supporters interested.

With the January transfer window just around the corner, plenty of clubs will be looking to add to their squads in the New Year.

Here’s a round-up of the latest transfer news, rumours and gossip around the Premier League.

Foxes not in for Rangers striker

Leicester City are not preparing a bid for Rangers striker Alfredo Morelos in January, according to the Daily Record.

Reports had been speculating that Brendan Rodgers was lining up a move for the Colombian, but Record Sport understand that the Foxes are not interested.

They also report that Leicester have dismissed recent links with Celtic defender Kristoffer Ajer.

Trio handed Olmo boost

Tottenham, Liverpool and Manchester United have all been handed a January boost in the pursuit of Spanish midfielder Dani Olmo, say football.london.

The 21-year-old has impressed in Croatia with Dinamo Zagreb, scoring six times in 15 appearances – and Dinamo head coach Nenad Bjelica has alerted some interested parties to his availability.

"I think we are just watching his last matches at Dinamo. There are so many offers. In the end, the club and the player will decide together what is best for both sides. He is ready to play for the best clubs in Europe."

Chelsea name price for Giroud

Chelsea have reportedly stated their price for striker Olivier Giroud as Inter Milan weigh up a January bid, according to FourFourTwo.

The striker has spoken about his frustration at the lack of opportunities at Stamford Bridge this season, with Tammy Abraham and Michy Batshuayi ahead of him in the pecking order.

And it’s reported that the Blues are willing to let the World Cup winner leave for £6million in January, with Gazetta dello Sport reporting Inter could place a bid.

Man United leading race for teenage star

Manchester United are leading the race for teenage sensation Erling Braut Haaland, according to Mail Online.

The 19-year-old has scored 26 goals in just 18 games in all competitions this season for Red Bull Salzburg, and now looks to be a target for Ole Gunnar Solskjaer.

Haaland played under the United boss at Molde, and Solskjaer has reportedly instructed Man United to do everything within their power to sign the teenager.
