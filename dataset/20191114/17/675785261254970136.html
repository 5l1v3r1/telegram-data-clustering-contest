Barcelona star responds to claims about Lionel Messi and Antoine Griezmann relationship

Griezmann is yet to set pulses raising at the Nou Camp following his big-money move in the summer, prompting suggestions of a rift with captain Messi

14 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Darren Wells, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/darren-wells/

Barcelona defender Clement Lenglet has suggested the relationship between Lionel Messi and Antoine Griezmann is a work in progress.

Frenchman Griezmann was Barca's marquee signing in the summer from La Liga rivals Atletico Madrid for an initial Â£107 million.

But he's yet to make a real impression at the Nou Camp, netting just four times since his arrival.

Much has been made of how he would adapt from being the superstar talisman at Atletico to being under Messi's watch, and Lenglet has shed some light on the situation.

"The same thing that happened to me is happening to Antoine," Lenglet told a press conference. "Dialogue comes little by little.

"Messi has a strong nucleus around him, he gets on very well with Luis Suarez, but he is very open and contact will come with time."

However Lenglet was keen to dampen any suggestions of cagey behaviour between Messi and Griezmann.

He added: "You can talk about a lot of things with Messi, not just football, he helps us every day."

While Griezmann has struggled to make his mark, Messi has been in rampant form, scoring nine goals in his last seven appearances.

But Lenglet feels his teammate will only get better once he becomes more settled in his new surroundings.

"It is not easy to earn your place in a club like Barca because there are a lot of players that have as much experience as you," he said.

"Barca has a very particular style of play which Antoine is not accustomed to. He needs time to adapt to feel his best on the pitch."

Asked whether Griezmann plays better for his country than his club, Lenglet replied: "Different' is a big word, but when he's with the national team, he's having a lot of fun.

"He's been there for a long time, he played a few big tournaments with the team, and he won a really beautiful one. Of course, he knows this place very well, he's very happy to be here, he has a strong history with the team.

"But he's also very happy to be at Barcelona, even if you're saying otherwise. He's smiling every day and likes to be happy on and off the pitch."
