12 students taken to hospital after possible pepper spray incident at Boston school

14 Nov 2019, 17:08 by EndPlay

BOSTON - At least 12 students have been taken to the hospital after an incident at a Roslindale school.

A spokesperson for the Boston Public Schools tells Boston 25 News that pepper spray was possibly released inside the Washington Irving School in Roslindale.

Boston EMS said at least 10 students have been transported. Soon after they told us 12 were transported. Officials say the illnesses/injuries are minor. 

The school is at 114 Cummins Highway and holds grades six through eight.
