Marilyn Manson opening Ozzy Osbourne’s North American tour

14 Nov 2019, 17:35 by Amanda Hatfield

Ozzy Osbourne‘s 2020 North American tour, which was rescheduled from 2019 due to medical issues, was originally set to be opened by Megadeth, but now it has a new opener: Marilyn Manson. The dates kick off on May 27 in Atlanta, and run through July 31 in Las Vegas, including an NYC show at Madison Square Garden on June 22 (tickets). See all dates below.

This news comes shortly after Ozzy announced rescheduled Europe/UK dates, opened by Judas Priest, which will follow the North American tour, starting in October of 2020 and running through the end of the year.

Ozzy announced his first new album in ten years earlier this month, Ordinary Man, and it’s due in early 2020. Listen to lead single “Under the Graveyard,” which features Guns N’ Roses’ Duff McKagan on bass, and Red Hot Chili Peppers’ Chad Smith on drums, below.
