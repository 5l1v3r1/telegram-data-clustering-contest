Nokia's Black Friday Sale Knocks £20 Off a Couple of Handsets

14 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Shabana Arif

If you're after a new phone but don't want to break the bank, Nokia has slashed prices on the 7.2 and 6.2.

The Black Friday discount is live now and runs up until December 3, which means you don't have to splurge pre-payday to make the most of it, which is always a good thing. Admittedly, the offer isn't that generous, but then the handsets aren't that pricey to start off with.

First up is the Nokia 7.2 with a 48MP triple camera and and special Zeiss optics, offering three bokeh modes, reduced from £229.99 from £249.99. It's joined by the Nokia 6.2 which comes sans Zeiss, and with a cameras that isn't quite as good as the 7.2, but that's reflected in the price, which is usually £199.99 but has been dropped down to £179.99.

The devices are refreshed versions of the of the original Nokia 7.2 and 6.2 and only just launched last month.

You can pick up the 6.2 in Ceramic Black, and Ice, with the black variant being a Carphone Warehouse exclusive colour until December 3, which is when the offer ends. The 7.2 is available in Cyan Green, Charcoal, and Ice.
