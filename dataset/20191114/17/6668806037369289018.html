In pictures: Sara Ali Khan takes a vacation to New York with her friend

14 Nov 2019, 17:23 by Filmfare

Sara Ali Khan made her debut opposite Sushant Singh Rajput in Kedarnath and has become quite a name in Bollywood in just one year. While the actress has her hands full with movies like Coolie No. 1 and Aaj Kal on the work front, she has taken time out from her busy schedule to spend time with her friend.The actress is on a mini vacation in New York with her friend Kamya Arora. The actress took to her Instagram today and shared pictures from Soho in lower Manhattan.

In the pictures Sara is seen donning a maroon jacket beating the chilling cold of the Big Apple. She is seen wearing a pair of black pants and topped up her look with grey gloves.Sara surely seems to know how to balance her professional and personal life well.
