Mum catches nurse 'abusing' wheelchair-bound son with cerebral palsy on hidden cam

WARNING - DISTRESSING CONTENT: Mum Dawn Caldwells took the matter to the court after claiming her son was kicked, slapped and poked by the nurse

14 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Tiffany Lo

A shocked mother claims she caught a nurse kicking, slapping and shaking her wheelchair-bound son on a hidden camera set up in her living room.

Dawn Caldwell, from Tennessee, USA, was shocked when she witnessed the alleged abuse through a home security camera in July.

Her 20-year-old son has Cerebral Palsy and is unable to speak or defend himself.

Dawn told NBC News : "He was hollering out, smacking the tray on his chair for hours after she [the nurse] would get him out of bed."
