Posters out: Here’s your dose of Good Newwz for the day

14 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Vedanshi Pathak

2019 is set to end on a high note as Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani will be serving some Good Newwz to the audience on December 28. The film has been surrounded by a lot of buzz since the day it was announced and now with the release day nearing, the makers are stepping up their game to keep the audience on their toes. This morning, three posters from the film were released.

One with Akshay, the other with Diljit and the last one was with Kareena and Kiara flaunting their baby bumps along with Akshay and Diljit. 

Directed by Raj Mehta, this Dharma Productions’ venture promises to be an entertainer and today on Children’s Day the makers released the posters of the film. The film is about two couples who struggle with conceiving a child and when they finally do, the biggest goof-up of the year takes place or so the posters suggest. Well, we are super kicked  after seeing these, what about you?
