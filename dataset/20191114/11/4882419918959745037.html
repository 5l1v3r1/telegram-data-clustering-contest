Barcelona v Real Madrid rescheduled Clasico set for 8pm kick-off

La Liga announced the time and date on social media on Wednesday

14 Nov 2019, 11:13 by Sport EN

La Liga confirmed the Barcelona v Real Madrid rescheduled Camp Nou clasico will take place at 8pm on Wednesday December 18.

After the controversy around the suspension of the game, which was due to take place on Oct 26, La Liga wanted a midday kick off so Asia audiences could benefit.

However in the end it has been set for the evening.
