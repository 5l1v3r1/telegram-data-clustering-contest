Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday at Newcastle Racecourse - all you need to know

This event is not to be missed, so find out everything you need to know about Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday at Newcastle Racecourse

14 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Adam Gray

Your social calendar will be filling up fast in the run-up to Christmas, but Newcastle Racecourse is hosting an event not to be missed.

Enjoy a Saturday afternoon of great jump racing at the racecourse's Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday sponsored by Betfair.

But when exactly is Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday, how can you get tickets and what should you wear? Here's everything you need to know.

When is Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday?

Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday is on Saturday, November 30.

Head to the racecourse for a Saturday afternoon of great jump racing, featuring the historic Betfair Fighting Fifth Hurdle, the first Grade 1 hurdle of the 2019-20 national hunt season, and the Rehearsal Chase, a listed Chase.

The Fighting Fifth Hurdle celebrates its 50th anniversary this year. Since Mugatpura won the inaugural event in 1969, the roll of honour has added an impressive list of equine stars, including Comedy of Errors, Sea Pigeon, Kribensis, Dato Star, Harchibald, My Tent Or Yours, and most recently, Buveur D'Air.

How much are tickets for Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday?

Grandstand & Paddock Admission Ticket £16 (advance price £16, gate price £20).

Premier Enclosure Admission Ticket £21 (advance price £21, gate price £25).

Package prices: Raceday Essentials Package £30 and includes a Premier Enclosure Admission Ticket, raceday programme, food voucher and drink voucher; Three-Course Lunch £120 and includes a Hospitality Admission Ticket, raceday programme, three-course carvery lunch and reserved seating in the raceday restaurant for the duration of the fixture.

*All packages must be booked in advance. Please call 0191 236 2020 for further details. Under 18s are admitted free!

What time does Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday start?

The gates open at 10.00 with the race action starting at 11.45. There will be seven races in total with the last race at 15.20.

Is there a dress code for Fighting Fifth Hurdle Raceday?

The dress code in hospitality areas and the Premier Enclosure is smart casual. Sportswear, trainers and fancy dress are not permitted.

There is no dress code in the Grandstand. Fancy dress is allowed, providing it’s decent and would not cause offence to other guests.

How do you get to Newcastle Racecourse?

The nearest Metro is Regent Centre in Gosforth. If you’re planning on taking the car, there’s plenty of parking at the racecourse and the best bit is it’s free.
