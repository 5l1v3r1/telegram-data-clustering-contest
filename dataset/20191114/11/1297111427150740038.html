SHSAA volleyball provincials set for this weekend

The Saskatchewan High Schools Athletic Association's 5A volleyball provincials run Friday and Saturday in Regina (boys) and Weyburn (girls).

14 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Regina Leader-Post

The Saskatchewan High Schools Athletic Association 5A boys volleyball championship tournament is to be held Friday and Saturday at Winston Knoll and Riffel.

The championship match is slated for Saturday, 5:45 p.m., at Winston Knoll — where the semi-finals are to begin at 3:15 p.m.

The Campbell Tartans, LeBoldus Golden Suns and Winston Knoll Wolverines will represent the Regina High Schools Athletic Association. LeBoldus is the city champion.

In Weyburn, the SHSAA 5A girls volleyball provincials are to be held on Friday and Saturday.

Campbell, Winston Knoll and the Miller Marauders will compete on behalf of the RHSAA. Campbell was won six consecutive city titles.

The final is set for Saturday, 5:45 p.m., at Weyburn Comprehensive High School.

Saskatchewan won its first-ever medal — a silver — at the recent Canadian under-18 female hockey championship, held in the Manitoba communities of Winkler and Morden.

Ontario defeated Saskatchewan 3-1 in Saturday’s gold-medal game.

Ashley Messier of Wilcox was named the tournament’s most valuable player.
