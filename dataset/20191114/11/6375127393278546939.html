Rita nabuntis sa natural na paraan

14 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Aster Amoyo

ANG dating sexy star noong dekada 90 up to early 2000 na si Rita Magdalena (Geraldine Sarmiento in real life) na naka-base ngayon sa San Diego, California, USA ay nakatakdang magsilang ng kanyang magiging first baby with her American husband of eight years na si Christopher Bartleson. The couple is expecting a baby boy.

Sina Rita at Chris ay nagkakilala sa social media in 2009 at sila’y nagpakasal  sa Amerika on September 9, 2011. Rita got pregnant to twins in 2014 but lost the babies after eight weeks of her pregnancy and she was so devastated.

Sa pagnanais ni Rita at ng kanyang US navy husband na si Chris na magkaroon ng anak, they resorted to all sorts of treatments at maging sa pamamagitan ng modern medical procedures tulad ng Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) at IVF (in vitro fertilization).

“We did 6 rounds of IUI and 4 rounds of IVF pero wala ring nangyari.  Nag-gain din ako ng 60 lbs. because of the shots I was getting,” pahayag ni Rita.

Early this year, dumating ng Pilipinas si Rita to revive her showbiz career at habang siya’y narito ay patuloy siyang nagpapayat pero walang tigil ang kanyang pagdarasal na sana’y magka-baby na sila ni Chris.

"I visited a lot of churches tulad ng Manaoag sa Pangasinan, Baclaran, sa Quiapo church, Padre Pio, sa Sto. Niño de Cebu, sa Simala church at iba pa,” kuwento pa ni Rita.

Nung nakaraang March ay muling bumalik ng San Diego, California si Rita at nung April 8 ay nag-conceive na siya.

“Eight is my lucky number,” pahayag pa niya.

“God’s timing is so perfect.  I found out that I’m pregnant on Mother’s Day pa when I was already 7 weeks pregnant,” patuloy niyang kuwento.

Nung nakaraang October 20 ay binigyan si Rita ng baby shower ng kanyang mga kaibigan sa San Diego.

She got pregnant sa natural na pamamaraan and will give birth to a baby boy sometime in January 2020.

“Ang dala-dala ko ngayon ang greatest Christmas gift na bigay sa aming mag-asawa,” aniya.
