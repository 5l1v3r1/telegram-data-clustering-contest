14 Nov 2019, 11:16 by Melisha Kaur, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/melisha-kaur/

Black Friday is fast approaching on November 29th, but many retailers have been kicking off their sales ahead of the shopping event.

Now health and beauty retailer Superdrug has joined in by launching its early Black Friday sale for 2019, with discounts on everything from beauty electricals to Christmas gift sets.

The early deals are available in store and online at superdrug.com until Tuesday December 3rd.

What's more, Health & Beautycard members can get an exclusive discount on top of sale items until Tuesday November 26th.

If you're not a Superdrug Health & Beautycard member, you can sign up for free in store or online at Superdrug.com/BeautyCard.

This year there are huge reductions on beauty electricals from TRESemmé, Braun and BaByliss.

Shoppers can also find a selection of gift sets from brands including Sanctuary Spa, Revolution and Nivea - which are perfect for unwrapping on Christmas Day.

But you'll need to be fast, as sale items are likely to sell out quickly.
