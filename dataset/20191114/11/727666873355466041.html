Watch Cardi B, Chance The Rapper, Lizzo & More Read Mean Tweets

14 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

The ABC show 'Jimmy Kimmel Live!' once again tries to humble some celebrities.

(AllHipHop News) Jimmy Kimmel is back with the latest edition of his "Mean Tweets" segment. For this round, the late-night host got musicians to read what trolls had to say about them on Twitter.

Cardi B closed out the video with specific instructions for a person that complained about her being too loud. Chance The Rapper and Lizzo also show up in the clip.

The other participants were Billie Eilish, Midland, Luke Bryan, Perry Farrell, Green Day, Leon Bridges, Monsta X, John Mayer, Alice Cooper, and Luke Combs. The Jimmy Kimmel Live! audience laughed throughout the presentation.
