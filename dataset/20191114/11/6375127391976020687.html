HPG, Grab to make roads safer

14 Nov 2019, 11:52 by Alfred P. Dalizon

THE Philippine National Police Highway Patrol Group headed by Brigadier General Dionardo B. Carlos and Grab Philippines  yesterday agreed to further intensify their partnership to transform Metro Manila’s major thoroughfares and other roads specifically EDSA a much safer place for the public this coming Yuletide season.

Carlos said that the PNP will provide Grab partner-drivers lectures that will equip them with knowledge on how to avoid falling prey to road thieves and other con artists.

The PNP-HPG is embarking on a massive program to arrest incidents of motorcycle and motor vehicle theft in the country specifically those being perpetrated by members of ‘Rent Sangla, Rent Tangay’ syndicates in Metro Manila and the rest of the country.
