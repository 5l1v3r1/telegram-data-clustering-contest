Real Madrid Keeping Eye On Eswatini Youngster

14 Nov 2019, 11:28 by SL International

Africa could soon have another star playing in LaLiga after reports emerged that Real Madrid are keeping an eye on an Eswatini wonderkid.

On Wednesday, the BBC reported that Eswatini teenager Leon Manyisa had once again emerged as a target for Los Blancos.

The 18-year-old took part in trials with the LaLiga giants last year, but saw a second trial scuppered due to visa issues.

"When I got a call to go for trials at Real Madrid, I was so excited," he said.
