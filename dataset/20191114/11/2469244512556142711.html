The Apprentice's Lottie Lion backs calls to get her on I'm a Celebrity 2020

Fans have been pleading for her to enter the jungle

14 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Jess Morcom

She's still going strong as a candidate in this year's series of The Apprentice, and already fans are calling for Lottie Lion to star in I'm A Celebrity.. Get Me Out Of Here next year.

Lottie, from Devon, has been acknowledging a series of tweets written by fans calling for her to star in the next season of the jungle favourite, which returns to our screens on November 17.

The 19-year-old who was involved in a racism row earlier on in the series, has been retweeting some of the pleas from fans for her to go into the jungle, but is so far yet to directly air her views on the proposal.

One fan tweeted: "Lottie needs to be on I'm A Celeb next year"

When the tweet received a few likes, the user wrote "Looks like people want you in there @LottieLion1 #junglelion."

While another added: "Yes, Lottie in the jungle would be brill."
