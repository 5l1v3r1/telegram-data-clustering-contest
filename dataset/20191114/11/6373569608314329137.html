Ryan Lowe issues 'hands-off' warning over Plymouth Argyle goalkeeper Mike Cooper

The 20-year-old starred for the Pilgrims in a 1-1 draw with Everton Under-23s on Sunday

14 Nov 2019, 11:16 by Chris Errington

Ryan Lowe has issued a ‘hands-off’ warning to any clubs who have taken note of the potential of Plymouth Argyle’s 20-year-old goalkeeper Mike Cooper.

The Pilgrims’ prospect is currently the back-up to first choice shot-stopper Alex Palmer, the loan signing from West Bromwich Albion.

But when Cooper has played, such as in the 1-0 EFL Trophy defeat by Chelsea Under-21s at Home Park last month, he has been in fine form.

Cooper followed that up by making a series of excellent saves when Argyle drew 1-1 with Everton in the Premier League Under-23 Cup at Southport FC last Sunday.
