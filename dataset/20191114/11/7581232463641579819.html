How To Advance Your Trace Element Analysis in Environmental Samples

14 Nov 2019, 11:00 by James Hannan

Who's speaking at this Webinar?

Accurate and precise measurement of major, minor and trace elements in samples from environmental sources is a key requirement across the world. Samples such as surface waters, potable waters and soils must meet the concentration limits specified in a range of legislative standards, such as EPA Methods 6010, 6020, 200.7 and 200.8 and the European Water Framework Directive.

In this webinar, you will discover how the latest ICP-OES and ICP-MS instruments and software from Thermo Scientific meet and exceed analysis standards for different environmental sample types.

In this webinar you will learn about:
- Environmental analysis legislation
- Which trace elemental analysis instruments are best suited to different environmental measurements
- Analysis performance for selected environmental applications
