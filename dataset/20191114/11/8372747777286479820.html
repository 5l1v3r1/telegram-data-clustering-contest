Cape Coral woman reported missing after not returning from trip

14 Nov 2019, 11:56 by WFTX Digital Team

UPDATE (1:40 PM): A source tells Fox 4 that Leah's family has been contacted by a nurse who reports that Leah is in a hospital. We're working to find out more and will update as this story develops.
-------------------------------
CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Police are searching for a Cape Coral woman last seen Sunday leaving home for a trip to Orlando.

According to Cape Coral Police, 41-year-old Leah Labecki was last seen leaving her home in the 500 block of SE 5th Place at 8:15 p.m. on Sunday, reportedly headed to Orlando.

She was expected back on Tuesday but as of Thursday has not returned or been in contact with family.

She may be in a gray Hyundai 4-door with Florida tag 018-8UP.

She is 5'6" tall with brown hair and blue eyes, and 225 pounds.

Please contact the Cape Coral Police Department with any information at 239-574-3223.
