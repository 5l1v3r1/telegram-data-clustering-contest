AEW 'Dynamite' Results (11/13): Jon Moxley Speaks, Cody Rhodes Seeks Revenge, More

14 Nov 2019, 03:04 by Tom Clark

The November 13 edition of AEW Dynamite emanated from the Nashville Municipal Auditorium in Nashville, Tennessee. The show was a followup to the company’s Full Gear event, which took place on November 9.

The show began with a flashback of Full Gear, including highlights of Jon Moxley versus Kenny Omega. Moxley was shown with the trainer, who was examining his scarred back after the match.
