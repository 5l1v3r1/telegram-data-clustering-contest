Indecent assault inquiry images released

TWO men are being sought by the police in relation to the alleged indecent assault of a woman on Mulcaster Street.

14 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Jack Maguire

The incident happened between 11.30pm and midnight on Friday 25 October on the section of the road which runs between the side entrance to The Drift nightclub and two takeaways.

Images of the two men the police would like to speak to have been posted on the Caught on Camera section of the States police website.

Anyone with any information is urged to contact the force on 612612 or call Crimestoppers on 08000555111.

Disclosures can also be made by visiting the Caught on Camera section and using the online form provided.

Meanwhile, a police spokeswoman said a woman who the force wanted to speak to in connection with the alleged theft of a purse in Marks & Spencer in St Helier has been identified. Inquiries in that case are still ongoing.
