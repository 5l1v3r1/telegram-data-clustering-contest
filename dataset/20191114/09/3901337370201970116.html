Ibrahimović Signs Out In The Most Zlatan Way Possible

14 Nov 2019, 09:35 by SL Social

“Now go back to watching baseball,” these were the last words from prolific Swedish striker Zlatan Ibrahimović, who this week, bid farewell to the MLS.

The 38-year-old, who has had a prolific yet nomadic career, stayed true to his colorful nature in his farewell message to the league.
