Wundowie bushfire: Emergency warning downgraded to watch and act alert

14 Nov 2019, 09:02

UPDATE: The bushfire warning in Wundowie has been downgraded to a watch and act alert as firefighters battle to bring the blaze under control.

There is still a possible threat to lives and homes in the area, as volunteer and career firefighters are on the scene.

The fire started near the intersection of Werribee Road and Vineyard Road at 3.45pm, with homes in Hawke Avenue previously under threat.

Locals should leave now for a safer place if the way is clear.

If they cannot leave, they should get ready to shelter in their homes and actively defend them.

Residents should close all doors and windows and turn off evaporative air conditioners, but keep the water running through the system if possible.

Visit the Emergency WA website for the latest updates and advice.
