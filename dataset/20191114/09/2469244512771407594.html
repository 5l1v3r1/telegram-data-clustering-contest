Warning as car crashes after spinning on black ice on A361 North Devon Link Road

The road was partially blocked for three hours this morning

14 Nov 2019, 09:56 by Joel Cooper

Drivers have been warned about hazardous conditions on the roads after a car spun on black ice and crashed this morning.

The incident happened on the A361 North Devon Link Road near Knowstone at 5.15am.

Police were called to reports of a single vehicle collision in which icy conditions saw the car lose control.

Initial reports suggested there were two crashes, but police say they only have a log of one incident.

A spokesman for Devon and Cornwall Police said: "The car had spun on black ice on the road and sustained damage.

"It doesn't appear there were any injuries caused to those in the vehicle."

The road was partially blocked for more than three hours while the vehicle was reecovered.

The road was fully reopened by 8.55pm.

It comes the morning after snow fell on higher parts of Devon with police reporting hazardous driving conditions on the A30 at Okehampton and Sourton.

Gritters were out in parts of the county last night and weather warnings are in place at several locations around the south west.

Highways England has advised motorists to act with caution and ensure they are prepared for journeys.

In a tweet they said: "Please ensure you drive to the conditions, allow extra time for your journeys and have water and snacks with you.

"We will keep you updated throughout the morning. Safe Travels."
