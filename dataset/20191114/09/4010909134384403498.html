The new MacPro is customisable and will start selling in December starting from RM20778!

14 Nov 2019, 09:20

Apple continues to surprise its fans after unveiling the new 16-inch and 13-inch MacBook Pro today.  According to Bloomberg, the company announced the redesigned Mac Pro that is Made in-the-USA will start selling next month!

The MacPro can be customized according to its users’ preference which it can support up to 8TB of SSD storage space and also handle up to six 8K streams in Final Cut Pro with a higher price tag. But if you’re in a tight budget and needed a Mac Pro for your workflow, it does come with a basic model which sports an 8-core 3.5GHz Intel Xeon Processor, Radeon Pro 580 GPU, 32GB RAM and a 256GB of SSD storage capacity.
