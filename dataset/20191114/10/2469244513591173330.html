Police update following serious assault near Exeter University

The area was cordoned off for several hours

14 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Jamie Hawkins

Police say no arrests have been made following a serious assault in Exeter.

St Germans Road near the junction with Prince of Wales Road was sealed off by officers in the early hours of Wednesday morning following an incident involving a woman.

The cordons were lifted at 4.30pm yesterday after detectives completed examinations in the area.

A spokesperson on Thursday morning confirmed that no arrests have been made, and enquiries are continuing.

The victim is being supported by specially trained officers.

A police spokesperson on Wednesday said: "Police were notified of a serious assault which happened in the area of St Germans Road in Exeter in the early hours of Wednesday November 13.

"The female victim is being supported by specially trained officers.

"Enquiries remain ongoing at this time.

"If any member of the public has any information that maybe relevant to the investigation please call 101 quoting ref 75 13/11/19."
