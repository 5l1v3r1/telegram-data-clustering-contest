Pictures released after incident at Rock City left person with 'serious leg injury'

It happened on the main dancefloor

14 Nov 2019, 10:13 by Phoebe Ram

CCTV images of four men have been released after a person's leg was injured at a city centre night club.

Nottinghamshire Police want to identify and speak to the four men shown in the photos in connection with an incident at Rock City on October 5, 2019.

It happened on the main dance floor around 2.45am.

As a result of the incident an individual sustained a serious leg injury.

If you saw the incident or have any information please contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101 and quote incident 100 October, 5 2019.
