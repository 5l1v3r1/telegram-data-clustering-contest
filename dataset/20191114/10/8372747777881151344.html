Police investigating indecent exposure in Punta Gorda convenience store

14 Nov 2019, 10:43 by WFTX Digital Team

PUNTA GORDA, Fla. -- Police say they're conducting an indecent exposure report at a convenience store on Tamiami Trail recently.

According to Punta Gorda Police, it happened at the Circle K at the intersection with Aqui Esta Drive.

They hope to identify the man seen here as part of that investigation.

If you have any information, please contact Southwest Florida Crime Stoppers by calling 1-800-780-TIPS (8477). You may be eligible for a cash reward for information leading to an arrest in this case.
