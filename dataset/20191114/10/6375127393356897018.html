6 Pampanga gamblers held

14 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Bernard Galang

Six persons were arrested for illegal gambling in Barangay Duyong Concepcion, San Simon town in Pampanga the other day.

In a report to Criminal Investigation and Detection Group-Region 3 (CIDG-3) officer Col. Christopher Abrahano, CIDG-Pampanga head Lt. Col. Dederick Relativo said the operation was conducted after they received an information from concerned citizens about the rampant “color game” operation in the said barangay.

Arrested for violation of Presidential Decree 1601 were Margie Ancheta, 36; Jemarose De Guzman, 27; Mark Dexter Galvez, 36; Elmer Manaloto Jr., 24; Erwin Taburnal, 39; and Dominador Bagacay, 23.

Assisted by regional mobile force and municipal policemen, the CIDG-Pampanga operatives also confiscated tables, dices, funnels and related materials used in the illegal gambling and P3,300 bet money.

The arrested persons were brought to the CIDG-Pampanga office for interrogation and documentation. They are facing charges for conduct of illegal gambling before the Provincial Prosecutor’s Office in the City of San Fernando.

Abrahano said the CIDG-3 will continue to go after people engaged in illegal and criminal activities and those wanted by the law.
