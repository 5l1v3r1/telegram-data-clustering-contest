Indian expat in Sharjah arrested for abusing wife

Sultan also alleged that her husband took away their passports and all her gold jewellery which had been reported to the police in February

14 Nov 2019, 10:19 by IANS

An Indian ex-pat was arrested by Sharjah Police after his wife posted a video of herself on social media saying he violently assaulted and abused her, a media report said on Thursday. Sharjah Police immediately responded to the tweet and reached out to the Indian woman and arrested the 47-year-old man on Wednesday night, Gulf News reported.
