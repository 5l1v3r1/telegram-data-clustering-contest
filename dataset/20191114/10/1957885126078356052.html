Ninja Theory's Bleeding Edge gets March release date

Stealth drop.

14 Nov 2019, 10:41

News by Imogen Beckhelling, Reporter Intern Updated on 14 November 2019

The Bleeding Edge store page has gone live ahead of this evening's X019 stream, revealing the release date of Ninja Theory's upcoming online brawler.

The colourful four-versus-four class-based game was announced at E3, and had technical alphas earlier this year. Aside from these, the developer has been pretty quiet about it all, until now.

Bleeding Edge arrives on 24th March, and those who pre-order it on Xbox Game Pass will get access to a closed beta - the first starting on 14th February, and the second starting on 13th March.
