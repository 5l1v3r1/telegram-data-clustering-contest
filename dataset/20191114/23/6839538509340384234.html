Your Say SA 2020 Vision: Have your say on the big issues facing South Australia

14 Nov 2019, 23:54

After 4000 South Australians told us what mattered to them — Your Say SA 2020 survey has closed. We reveal the results of the state’s biggest survey on January 5, 2020.

The Sunday Mail’s annual Your Say SA survey is back.

While this year’s survey revisits some previous themes, it also focuses on South Australia’s “2020 Vision” — the big issues that will shape the future of our state over the next decade.

The 100-question survey spans topics including the economy, health, education, climate change and the cost of living, and should take 15-20 minutes to complete.

This year’s survey was filled out by more than 4300 respondents.
