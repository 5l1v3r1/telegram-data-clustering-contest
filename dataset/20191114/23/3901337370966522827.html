AFCON Champions Hit Five Past Zambia

14 Nov 2019, 23:34 by SL International

Algeria 5-0 Zambia

Algeria got their campaign off to a flying start after hammering Zambia 5-0 in their Group H opener at Stade Mustapha Tchaker.

Ramy Bensebaini, Youcef Belaili and Arbi Soudani were also on target in addition to Baghdad Bounedjah's second-half brace.

Egypt 1-1 Kenya

Egypt, who without Mohamed Salah, got their campaign off to a slow start after being held to a 1-1 draw by Kenya in their Group G opener at Borg El Arab Stadium.

Kahraba broke the deadlock just before the break for the Pharaohs but Michael Olunga silenced the home crowd with the equaliser in the 67th minute to earn Kenya a precious point.

Togo 0-1 Comoros

Comoros moved to the top of Group G after upsetting Togo 1-0 in their opener at Stade de Kegue.

After a goalless first stanza, Faiz Selemanie finally broke the deadlock for Comoros six minutes into the second stanza to secure the spoils.

Mozambique 2-0 Rwanda

Mozambique got their Group F campaign off to a positive start after beating Rwanda 2-0 in Maputo.

Telinho and Edson Mexer netted first-half goals to seal the maximum points for the hosts.

Democratic Republic of Congo 0-0 Gabon

Democratic Republic of Congo and Gabon opened their Group D campaign with a golless stalemate at the Stade des Martyrs de la Pentecote in Kinshasa.

Mali 2-2 Guinea

Mali and Guinea played to a 2-2 draw in their Group A opener at Stade 26 Mars in Bomako.

After a goalless first stanza, the game came to life 11 minutes inside the second stanza after Mali broke the deadlock and it became a see-saw affair, with Guinea equalising twice to earn a point on the road.
