Classic Kingdom Hearts games are coming to Xbox One in 2020

14 Nov 2019, 23:41 by Stephany Nunneley

Kingdom Hearts 1 and Kingdom Hearts 2 are coming to Xbox One in 2020.

Today, at the X019 event in London, Square announced Kingdom Hearts 1 and 2 were coming to Xbox One.

The titles will be released as Kingdom Hearts HD 1.5 + 2.5 ReMIX and Kingdom Hearts HD 2.8 Final Chapter Prologue, releases players of the series on PlayStation will be familiar with.

Both were released separately and then together for PS4 last year as Kingdom Hearts – The Story So Far.
