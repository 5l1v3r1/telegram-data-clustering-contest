Protesters dressed as handmaids blare Blasey Ford testimony at Kavanaugh dinner

14 Nov 2019, 23:25 by Caitlin Yilek

Protesters donned red hooded cloaks and played Christine Blasey Ford’s testimony on a big screen outside of a black-tie dinner featuring Supreme Court Justice Brett Kavanaugh.

Kavanaugh was slated to speak Thursday night at the conservative Federalist Society’s annual convention at Union Station in Washington, D.C.

Videos from outside the venue showed nearly a dozen people dressed as handmaids walking single file and a truck with a large screen showing Ford testifying during Kavanaugh’s confirmation hearings.
