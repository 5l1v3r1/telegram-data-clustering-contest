Sterling and Southgate stick up for Gomez after defender booed at Wembley

The Liverpool star had been involved in a disagreement with England team-mate Sterling.

14 Nov 2019, 23:52

Raheem Sterling and Gareth Southgate criticised the England fans that booed Joe Gomez against Montenegro.

The build-up to the Three Lions’ 1,000th match was overshadowed by a skirmish at St George’s Park on Monday.

Sterling made private and public apologies after emotions got the better of him less than 24 hours after clashing with Gomez towards the end of Manchester City’s 3-1 loss at Premier League leaders Liverpool.
