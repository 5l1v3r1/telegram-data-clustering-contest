The Closing Theme Song for MY HERO ACADEMIA Will Be Included on Sayuri's Upcoming EP

14 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Tommy Williams

Japanese singer-songwriter “Sanketsu-Girl” Sayuri is releasing About a Voyage (World Edition) later this month. One of the four tracks on the EP will be the ending theme song to the hit anime My Hero Academia, “About a Voyage.” This is sure to be something that many fans will want to pick up, especially if they enjoy Sayuri and/or J-pop music. Talking about the title track, the 2.5D-parallel singer-songwriters said:

To me, ‘My Hero Academia’ is like a lucky charm that turns my inner, little thoughts to a solid power. I wrote ‘About a Voyage’ out of my wish to sing a song like that. I hope you enjoy this ending theme song alongside the development of the story.

Fans will be able to listen to About a Voyage (World Edition) on November 29, and you can check out the music video for the title track below.
