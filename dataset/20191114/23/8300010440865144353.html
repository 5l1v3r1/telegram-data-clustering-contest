Photos reveal aftermath of deadly Saugus High shooting

14 Nov 2019, 23:26

SANTA CLARITA, Calif. -- At least two people were killed and several others were injured Thursday during a shooting at a Southern California high school and the student gunman was in custody, authorities said.

The shooting occurred around 7:30 a.m. at Saugus High School in the city of Santa Clarita, about 30 miles northwest of downtown Los Angeles.
