Pink confirms she's 'taking a break from music' on stage at CMA Awards 2019

Pink made the Country Music Association Awards a family night out as her kids joined her and husband Carey Hart on the red carpet before making her announcement

14 Nov 2019, 05:36 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Pink has confirmed that she will be leaving music and performing behind, for a year at least.

The 40-year-old singer has spent the past two years on the road with her successful Beautiful Trauma World Tour and she is now looking in a different direction for her future.

Pink arrived at the Country Music Association Awards for 2019 with her kids in tow as she and husband Carey Hart made the event a family affair.

Speaking about her future on the red carpet, Pink confirmed: "It’s time to do something else for a minute."

Ahead of the ceremony Pink posed for pics with Carey and their two kids, daughter Willow, 8, and son Jameson, 3.

While Pink was stunning in a burnt-orange dress and black hat, it was Jameson that stole the show.

The toddler wore a cowboy hat, studded jacket and cowboy boots and looked every inch the country music legend.

Willow was equally as adorable with a white dress that had detailing of two horses.

As well as giving music a rest for now, Pink hailed her husband Carey as they approached their 14-year-anniversary.

Pink said: "He deserves a trophy! It’s been a while. We used to make a big deal out of it. Now, we’re just lucky to be together.”

When asked what the secret was for their longevity, Pink joked: "We fight nicer."

Pink opened up recently to Billboard about turning 40 and how her husband and kids threw her a surprise party.

She said: "Forty was the first birthday I’ve had where I’ve been like, ‘F*** yeah, this is awesome! Yes! I know exactly who I am!’ And now I can just chill out a little bit.

“And then 60 is going to be like, ‘F*** that, turn it back up.’ I’m going to be on roller skates, with rollers in my hair, house plants everywhere."
