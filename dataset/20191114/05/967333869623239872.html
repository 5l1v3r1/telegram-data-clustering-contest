Security Officers in Bolivia Use Tear Gas, Batons Against Pro-Morales Protesters

14 Nov 2019, 05:43

MEXICO CITY (Sputnik) – Police and military personnel in La Paz, Bolivia have used batons and tear gas to disperse supporters of former President Evo Morales, who are protesting against interim President Jeanine Anez in the wake of Morales' resignation, footage released by the media showed.

A video published by Ruptly on Wednesday showed security officers suppressing protesters.
