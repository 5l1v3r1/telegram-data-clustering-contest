T7 Global bags umbrella contract from Vestigo Petroleum

14 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Surin Murugiah

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 14): T7 Global Bhd has secured an umbrella contract from Vestigo Petroleum Sdn Bhd.

In a filing to Bursa Malaysia today, T7 said its unit Tanjung Offshore Services Sdn Bhd has on Oct 10 this year received a letter of award from Vestigo Petroleum for the provision of integrated well services for well intervention, workover and abandonment.

It said the duration of the contract was from Oct 10, 2019 to Sept 19, 2024.

T7 said the contract value shall be as per work order request, which is at the full discretion of Vestigo Petroleum.

The contract is expected to contribute positively to T7's earnings for the financial year ending Dec 31, 2019, according to the group.

At the midday break today, T7 shares rose 1% or 0.5 sen to 50.5 sen, for a market capitalisation of RM253.29 million.
