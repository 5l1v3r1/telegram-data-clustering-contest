Planet Coaster is coming to Xbox One!!

14 Nov 2019, 20:40 by Lewis White

Frontier’s theme park management simulation Planet Coaster is coming to Xbox One! 

Be inspired and inspire others as you create, manage and share the world’s greatest parks with friends and content creators around the world. Surprise, delight and thrill your guests as you build your coaster park empire. Let your imagination run wild with the future of coaster park simulation games.

The spiritual successor to Roller Coaster Tycoon will allow players to create their very own theme parks. With creators able to perfectly tweak everything within their park through the use of unparalleled creative tools, players can make some truly amazing creations.
