Seaoil partners with AAP

14 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Manila Standard

Automobile Association of the Philippines Members will soon enjoy exclusive rewards through SEAOIL VIP Club cards.

SEAOIL Philippines has officially joined the National Auto Club’s growing list of partner establishments. The agreement was signed at the AAP Tower by  (from left) AAP Business Development Manager Kristine Leonardo, Chief Operating Officer Mark Desales, SEAOIL Philippines Vice President for Corporate & Consumer Marketing Jose Jaime dela Fuente, and Business Development Supervisor Jen Lemuel Co.
