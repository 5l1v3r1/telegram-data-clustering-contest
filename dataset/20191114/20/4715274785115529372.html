Church facelift

14 Nov 2019, 20:52 by Norman Cruz

Rev. Ricky Villar, OSA, director of San Agustin Museum, uses a Karcher high pressure washer in cleaning the facade of the San Agustin Church in Intramuros, Manila on Thursday. The church is scheduled for restorative cleaning as part of the cultural program that aims to preserve historic landmarks around the globe.
