Grounded is a brand new survival game from Obsidian

14 Nov 2019, 20:24 by Ash

Announced today during X019, Grounded is a survival game from Obsidian Entertainment that shrinks you down to the size of an ant, puts you in a garden, and makes you fight for your tiny life.

You can watch the announcement trailer for Grounded below. It shows off plenty of exciting features, including the shrunk-down player and their equally-miniature friends banding together to fight off some now-giant bugs.
