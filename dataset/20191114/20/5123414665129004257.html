Looking to go to Vegas but don’t know where to start? Don’t worry – Discotech’s got you covered. Discotech is a free mobile app that allows you to see where your favorite artists / DJs are performing, sign up for free / discounted guest lists, get discounted tickets & promo codes, and book table service at the hottest clubs in Las Vegas.

Upcoming Events:

With over 20 different clubs and pool parties, deciding where to go isn’t an easy task. Discotech lets you easily see upcoming events in Vegas, who’s playing on which dates, see where your friends are going, and browse pictures of each club with a few swipes on your phone.

Sign Up For Free Guest Lists

Looking to do Vegas on a budget? Almost all the clubs in Las Vegas have a guest list – free before a certain time. Some guest lists require even ratio of guys to girls. Some guest lists come with free drink tickets. All the details are on the app! 

Book Table Service

For those of you looking to drop some cash – bottle service in Vegas is definitely a fantastic option! Whether you’re looking for a Cabana at Encore Beach Club or a dance floor table at Omnia – Discotech’s got you covered every step of the way.

In addition, Discotech has a variety of Las Vegas nightlife guides to answer any questions you might have – for example: What are the best EDM clubs in Las Vegas? What are the best hip-hop pool parties? What are the best Vegas Nightswims?
