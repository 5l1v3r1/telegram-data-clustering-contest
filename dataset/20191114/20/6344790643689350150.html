HELLTUREL Is Another Unofficial D&D Companion to DESCENT INTO AVERNUS

14 Nov 2019, 20:30 by Tommy Williams

The Dungeons & Dragons community is having a lot of fun with Baldur’s Gate: Descent into Avernus, and James Introcaso has a companion to the popular hellish adventure. Hellturel features additional locations in Elturel complete with NPCs and plot hooks, as well as new magic items perfect for characters of level 5-6. I don’t want to say much for fear of spoiling things, but Introcaso does include an Owlbear Butcher Shop.

Elturel is in Hell. It’s up to you to save it.

You can purchase Hellturel from DMs Guild for $4.95.
