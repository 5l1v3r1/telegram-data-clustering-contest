OnePlus starts rolling out OxygenOS 10.0.4 to OnePlus 7T Pro and OxygenOS 10.0.6 to OnePlus 7T smartphones, here is what’s new

14 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Raka

OnePlus has started rolling out  OxygenOS 10.0.6 to OnePlus 7T smartphones and OxygenOS 10.0.4 to OnePlus 7T Pro smartphones. OxygenOS 10.0.6 brings improvement in system stability, Bluetooth connectivity, general bug fixes for the OnePlus 7T smartphones.

On the other hand, OxygenOS 10.0.4 brings the Android security patch to 2019.10, a fix for the black bar issue while charging or playing a video to OnePlus 7T Pro smartphones, while the rest of the changelog is the same as that of the OxygenOS 10.0.6.

OxygenOS 10.0.6 Changelog

System
   •  Optimized standby power consumption
   •  Improved system stability and general bug fixes
   •  Optimized the Bluetooth connectivity in automobiles
   •  Optimized the expanded screenshot feature

OxygenOS 10.0.4 Changelog

System
   •  Optimized standby power consumption
   •  Improved system stability and general bug fixes
   •  Updated Android security patch to 2019.10
   •  Fixed the black bar issue while charging or playing a video
   •  Optimized the Bluetooth connectivity in automobiles
   •  Optimized the expanded screenshot feature

This update will be rolled out in a phased manner, meaning that if you’re one of the few who are using the smartphone right now, you might not get it today. OnePlus, however, confirmed that it’ll begin a broader rollout in a few days.
