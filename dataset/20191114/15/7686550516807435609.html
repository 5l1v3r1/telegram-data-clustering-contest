Overturned car in Ystrad Mynach causes road closure

Gwent Police are at the scene following a collision in Lewis Street

14 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Philip Dewey

A car has overturned following a collision in Ystrad Mynach which has resulted in the closure of a road.

Officers from Gwent Police were called to reports of a car accident in Lewis Street at around 11am on Thursday.

The road was closed while police are removed the vehicle from the road.

It's not known whether there are any injuries or whether another vehicle was involved in the collision.

A spokeswoman from Gwent Police said: "We received a report of a road traffic collision in Lewis Street, Ystrad Mynach, today shortly after 11am.

"A car has overturned."

The Welsh Ambulance Service has been asked to comment.
