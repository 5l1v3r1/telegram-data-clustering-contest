Gunman who fled to Dubai after shooting man dead in street GUILTY of murder

Keanu Bedward, 24, dumped the revolver in a reservoir after killing victim Remal Hunt in Church Street, Erdington

14 Nov 2019, 15:37 by Ross McCarthy, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ross-mccarthy/

A gunman who fled to Dubai after shooting a man dead on a Birmingham street has been convicted of murder.

Keanu Bedward dumped the revolver in a reservoir after he shot Remal Hunt in the chest in Church Road, Erdington, on April 18 this year.

Bedward, 24, of Norfolk Road, Erdington, was convicted of murder by a jury at Birmingham Crown Court this afternoon, Thursday, November 14.

Both men had been in cars and after the shooting Bedward sped off in a Volkswagen Golf.

Paramedics went to the scene but Mr Hunt was pronounced dead shortly afterwards.

Police later found the Golf parked in Stonechat Drive. It had a laundry bag bearing the initials KB in the boot and a balaclava and gloves.

Bedward was captured on CCTV walking away from the car with a female and later that day she bought a one-way ticket to Dubai from Manchester Airport.

The defendant got on the flight and stayed there for ten days but was arrested on his return to the UK.

West Midlands Fire Service crews found a black Nike bag filled with stones as they conducted a training exercise near Bartley Green Reservoir a few days before Bedward returned to Britain.

Also inside was a pair of gloves, two cigarette ends and the revolver used in the shooting, which had four bullets still in the chamber.
