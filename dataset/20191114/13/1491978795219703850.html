Nature photographer of the year 2019 - in pictures

14 Nov 2019, 13:17

From mystical woodlands to majestic whales, here are the winners and runners-up of this year’s competition. They were recently announced at the Nature Talks photo festival in the Netherlands

Mammals category winner

Under the Snow by Stefano Quirini (Italy)

Mammals category runner-up

Tapir Diver by Marcio Cabral (Brazil)

Other animals category winner

Golden rain by Norbert Kaszás (Hungary)

Birds category winner

Greeting the Sun by Peter Čech (Czech Republic )

Plants and fungi category winner

Survivor by David Frutos Egea (Spain)

Landscape category winner

A Stormy Day by Ales Krivec (Slovenia)

Underwater category winner

Up in the Air by Alexey Zozulya (Russia)

Nature art category winner

Happiness Grows in Trees by Simone Baumeister (Germany)

Man and Nature category winner

Lonely by Luke Massey (Spain)

Man and Nature category runner-up

White Rhino Trophies by Britta Jaschinski (UK)

Overall winner and black and white category winner

Jump by Csaba Daróczi (Hungary)

Landscapes of the Lage Landen category winner

Galloways Under the Bridge by Karin de Jonge (Netherlands)

Youth category winner

Symphony No 5 by Giacomo Redaelli (Italy)

Fred Hazelhoff portfolio winner

Cuejdel My Lake by Gheorghe Popa (Romania)
