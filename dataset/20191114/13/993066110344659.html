Police say suspicious object found in Co Armagh was 'nothing untoward'

14 Nov 2019, 13:51 by SUZANNE MCGONAGLE

A SUSPICIOUS object found in Co Armagh has been declared as "nothing untoward" by police.

A security alert was sparked in the Newry Road area of Belleeks last night after an object was found shortly before 11pm.

PSNI Chief Inspector Nigel Henry said: "ATO also attended and examined the object, which has since been declared as nothing untoward".

The road has been re-opened.
