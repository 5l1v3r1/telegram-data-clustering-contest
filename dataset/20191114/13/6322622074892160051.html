SC opens applications for prestigious Fellowship in Islamic Finance

14 Nov 2019, 13:20 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 14): The Securities Commission of Malaysia (SC) is inviting applications for the ninth Visiting Fellowship at Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies (OCIS), a recognised independent centre of the University of Oxford, United Kingdom under the Scholar in Residence (SIR) Programme in Islamic Finance for 2020/2021 academic year commencing October 2020.

SC in a statement here today said applicants should have extensive experience in Islamic finance or a closely related discipline, and demonstrate competence in conducting independent academic research.

"The closing date for submissions is Feb 3 next year. The selected scholar will take up residence in Oxford to conduct the research and to participate in a range of academic activities at OCIS.
