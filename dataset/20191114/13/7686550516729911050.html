Man jailed after forcing dog to brutally maul cat to death

Price fled the scene after his dog viciously attacked Cleo the cat

14 Nov 2019, 13:52 by Sian Filcher

A man has been jailed after disturbing CCTV showed him forcing his dog to attack a defenceless cat.

Amos Price, 30, watched on as his lurcher-type dog brutally mauled the cat to death in Woodlands Crescent, Walsall.

Cleo the cat sadly died after the attack, with her distraught owner saying his loyal companion will be ‘very much missed’.

The horrific act was caught on CCTV outside of the house, showing Price approaching the home with his dog on a lead.

The sickening footage then shows Price using a torch to provoke his dog to attack Cleo, before the animal lurches at the cat.

Walsall Magistrates Court heard how Price fled the scene after the attack, returning a short while after to find his dog.

Cleo’s owner Gary Truefitt, who found the footage after Cleo failed to return home, said that he has had ‘many sleepless nights’ since the horrific discovery.

Price pleaded guilty to causing unnecessary suffering to a protected animal under the Animal Welfare Act.

The court was told that he was ‘ashamed and sorry’ for the attack and was intoxicated at the time.

The thug has been jailed for 18 weeks and banned from keeping animals for life.

In addition to his jail sentence, Price must pay a victim surcharge of £122 and compensation of £250.
