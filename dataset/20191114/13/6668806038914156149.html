Here's proof that Katrina Kaif and Vicky Kaushal are going strong

14 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Vedanshi Pathak

Bollywood is one place which is always buzzing with news. From link-ups to break-ups, B-towners are a part of a fast changing industry which tosses and turns every day. After several rumours doing the rounds about Bollywood hottie Katrina Kaif dating Bollywood's latest favourite Vicky Kaushal, the couple decided to feature together on a talk show. Soon after, they were seen sitting next to each other at an award show and before one could try to process it all, the rumoured couple decided to leave together post a hi-profile Diwali party recently adding fuel to fire.

The couple has apparently hit it off really well and are going strong. Katrina and Vicky look great together and just when we thought the couple would never come out in the open about it, a picture of the two dining out with each other at a hotspot in Mumbai has surfaced online. Vicky and Katrina were casually dressed for the date and even sported the same pair of sneakers. Well, this fresh couple is sure to grab all the eyeballs once they start making more public appearances.
