Kazuki Kazama Burns Opponents in ‘Samurai Shodown’ November 18

14 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Mike Wilson

SNK has announced that the release date for Samurai Shodown‘s next DLC character, Kazuki Kazama, will launch on November 18. Kazuki Kazama can be purchased individually for $5.99, or as part of the $19.99 Season Pass. Kazuki’s brother, Sogetsu, was accidentally leaked last month, but he has no release date as of yet.

Samurai Shodown is available now for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One worldwide, with a Google Stadia version being available on November 19th. A PC release is also being planned, while a Switch version is due out on December 12 in Japan.
