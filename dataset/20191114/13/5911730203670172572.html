Florida Strawberry Festival 2020 lineup announced

14 Nov 2019, 13:01 by WFTS Digital Staff

PLANT CITY, Fla. â€” The Florida Strawberry Festival on Thursday released the lineup of entertainers who will perform on the Wish Farms Soundstage this year.

The 11-day festival is set for February 27 to March 8 in Plant City. It celebrates the strawberry harvest of Eastern Hillsborough County.

This year's theme is "Our Perfect Vision." The theme centralizes around the Festival's "Perfect Vision" for fun which includes rides, food, entertainment, shortcake and much more.

Each year, over 500,000 visitors enjoy the festival's headline entertainment, youth livestock shows, exhibits of commerce and, of course, its strawberry shortcake.
