Visitor from China in critical condition after snorkeling in Maui waters

14 Nov 2019, 07:32 by HNN Staff

MAUI, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) -A visitor from China is in critical condition after she was pulled from the ocean at Honolua Bay just before noon Wednesday.

The Maui Fire Department says says the woman in her 40s had been snorkeling with traditional mask and snorkel.

She needed help in the water and was brought to shore.

Lifeguards performed CPR until emergency responders arrived and took her to a hospital.

No additional details were available.
