Oman face Bangladesh in 2022 FIFA World Cup qualifier

14 Nov 2019, 07:23 by Times News Service

Muscat: Oman will face Bangladesh in Muscat in the fifth round of the 2022 Fifa World Cup qualifier and 2023 Asian Cup on Thursday.

The match will be played at the Sultan Qaboos Sports Complex in Baushar at 7pm.
