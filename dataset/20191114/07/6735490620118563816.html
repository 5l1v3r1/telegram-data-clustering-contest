Justin Bieber Wants To Trademark The Phrase ‘R&Bieber’

14 Nov 2019, 07:51 by Becca Longmire

Justin Bieber is reportedly looking to trademark the phrase “R&Bieber”.

According to TMZ, Bieber’s team filed legal documents not long after the Canadian hitmaker posted the phrase on Instagram on October 27.

He also told fans he’d be releasing an album before Christmas if another post he shared reached 20 million likes.

TMZ claims Bieber wants to trademark “R&Bieber” for “entertainment services in the nature of live musical performances and production of sound recordings,” fuelling rumours this could be the name of an upcoming album.

The star is also said to want to use the phrase for merch and film.

ET Canada has contacted Bieber’s rep for comment.
