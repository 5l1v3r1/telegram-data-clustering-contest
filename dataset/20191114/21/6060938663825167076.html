Box office report: Hrithik Roshan's 'War' beats Salman Khan's 'Bharat'; here's how

14 Nov 2019, 21:22 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Hrithik Roshan and Tiger Shroff's 'War' received a lot of love and appreciation from the masses. The action-adventure scored huge at the box office.

Talking about single screen numbers, 'War' is the highest grosser film of the year 2019.

The number of single-screen theatres is decreasing day by day as they are getting converted into multi-screens. However, the business done by single screens is still a sign of stardom for the actors involved and also the reach of the film where the higher the business means the film has reached a larger audience.

There are only four films of Bollywood which crossed the 100 crore mark when it comes to single screen collections. 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan', 'Sultan', 'Bahubali: The Conclusion' and 'Tiger Zinda Hai'.

'Bahubali 2' is the greatest blockbuster but the rest three films are of Salman Khan.

However, this year, Hrithik and Tiger's 'War' has beaten Salman's 'Bharat'. The top ten highest earners at single screens across India in 2019 according to BoxOfficeIndia.com are as follows:

1. War - 74,72,00,000 (28 days)

2. Kabir Singh - 54,82,00,000

3. Bharat - 53,29,00,000

4. Housefull 4 - 44,56,00,000 (17 days)

5. Saaho (Hindi) - 43,89,00,000

6.Total Dhamaal - 38,93,00,000

7. Mission Mangal - 33,15,00,000

8. Kesari - 29,05,00,000

9. Uri - The Surgical Strike - 27,94,00,000

10. Super 30 - 20,17,00,000
