MIDNIGHT RAIN Is a New Series on Webtoon

14 Nov 2019, 21:30 by Tommy Williams

A new series recently launched on Webtoon called Midnight Rain. This story is written by Pani and looks to be a drama with a touch of romance. For a web comic, the art actually looks pretty good in my opinion.

Noemi’s parents are working for one of the richest family in the country and moved to live on their estate. There she meets Blade, the hot-headed prince and Soren, the illegitimate son. Enjoying simple childhood days and falling in love for the first time...

You can watch the first few episodes of Midnight Rain on Webtoon now with new episodes every Monday.
