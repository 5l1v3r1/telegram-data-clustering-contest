Age of Empires 4 set in medieval period, gameplay reveal trailer shows two factions

14 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Stephany Nunneley

We were finally given a look at Age of Empires 4 today during X019.

Age of Empires 4 is still in development, which means a release date was not provided.

What we do know, is it’s set during the medieval period and the factions shown in the trailer are the Mongols and English.
