Wasteland 3 release date set for May 2020, check out the new gameplay trailer

14 Nov 2019, 21:37 by Stefan L

The long-awaited RPG sequel from inXile Studio, Wasteland 3 has been given a 19th May 2020 release date at XO19. The game will be coming to PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC on that date, as well as being inducted into Xbox Game Pass.
