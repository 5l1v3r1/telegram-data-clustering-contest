The Witcher 3, Rage 2, Remnant: From the Ashes and more coming to Xbox Game Pass

14 Nov 2019, 21:45 by Stephany Nunneley

Microsoft has announced eight titles releasing today on Xbox Game Pass for PC and Xbox One. One is Remnant: From the Ashes.

During its Inside Xbox Live presentation at X019 it was announced The Witcher 3, and more would also be added to the service.

The Witcher 3 isn’t a surprise considering an advertisement for Xbox Game Pass Ultimate revealed the news earlier today. It’s slated for December and will feature all 16 free DLC.

The full list of games available today through the service and coming to the service later is below.
