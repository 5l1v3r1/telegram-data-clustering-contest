You Can Return to the Original ‘Jurassic Park’ in ‘Jurassic World Evolution’s Next DLC!

14 Nov 2019, 21:29 by Neil Bolt

Park-building sim Jurassic World: Evolution let you make your own dino theme park, and that was pretty awesome in itself, but with the game’s latest update, you’ll be able to go back to 1993 and run John Hammond’s Jurassic Park!

In a forum post on the game’s site, the developer detailed this big new update that arrives in a few weeks on December 10.
