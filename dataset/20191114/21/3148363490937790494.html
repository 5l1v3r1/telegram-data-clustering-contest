WATCH: Brits Bash Each Other with Exercise Equipment in Wild Gym Fight

14 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Dylan Gwinn

A group of gym members in England have found themselves banned for life, after they used exercise equipment to attack each other in a wild gym fight.

The incredible footage shows one fighter using a metal bar to smash a counter that another scrapper had just jumped over. In another scene, a man hits another man in the head with a metal bar.
