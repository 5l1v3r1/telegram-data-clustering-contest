'Couldn't happen to a nicer club' - Newcastle United fans are trolling Aston Villa

Latest Aston Villa news includes Newcastle United reaction to AVFC injury worries

14 Nov 2019, 21:35 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Aston Villa are sweating on the fitness of Tom Heaton and Jed Steer ahead of the visit of Newcastle United.

The Magpies, managed by Steve Bruce, travel to Villa Park a week on Monday after the final international break of the year.

Heaton and Steer are both nursing injuries during the break from domestic action.

Heaton was missing for the West Midlands derby defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers at the weekend with a calf problem that also forced him to withdraw from Gareth Southgate’s England squad.

Steer was subsequently handed his first Premier League start of the season at Molineux but lasted just eight minutes before limping off.

It was feared Steer had ruptured his Achilles and although his injury isn’t thought to be as serious as that, the 27-year-old is still facing up to two months on the sidelines.

Orjan Nyland made his first appearance since last December when he was brought on to replace Steer and he went on to concede twice as Wolves secured a deserved victory.

He said afterwards: “It's about being ready when the opportunity arises. They're betting on me to the extent that they want me to be there.

"They have clear goals for me, and I think I'm a Premier League 'keeper."

 

Villa will be hoping to have Heaton available as soon as possible but Newcastle fans are hoping their side can take advantage of the potential double 'keeper lay-off.

One said: "They had to used their 3rd choice last game."

"Couldn't happen to a nicer club," said another.

A third added: "We will beat them with or without a decent 'keeper anyway."
