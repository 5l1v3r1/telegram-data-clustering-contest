National Lottery rollover to £12.6million after Saturday's Lotto winning numbers revealed

The winning Lotto numbers were: 13, 16, 28, 34, 41 and 56, with the bonus ball of 40

03 Nov 2019, 08:14 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

No-one scooped the jackpot in Saturday's National Lottery draw, making Wednesday's must-be-won top prize an estimated £12.6 million.

The winning Lotto numbers were: 13, 16, 28, 34, 41 and 56, with the bonus ball of 40.

Set of balls six and draw machine Arthur were used.

One player matched five of six numbers plus the bonus ball to win £1 million, while 83 ticket-holders scooped £1,750 by matching five balls, and 3,959 people won £140 for matching four of the six balls.

There were no top winners for the Lotto HotPicks on Saturday, though five people won £13,000 for matching four of the five numbers.

There was also no top prize winner in the Thunderball competition, where the numbers were 05, 14, 21, 23 and 30, with the Thunderball number of 03.

Three people won £5,000 each by matching five numbers, and 76 won £250 by matching four and the Thunderball.
