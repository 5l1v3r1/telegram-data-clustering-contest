King Salman Receives a Message from Prime Minister of Malaysia

03 Nov 2019, 11:15

The Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud has received a message from Prime Minister of Malaysia Dr. Mahathir Mohamad.

The message was delivered to the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques by Minister of Education and Special Envoy of the Prime Minister of Malaysia Dr. Maszlee Malik during his meeting with the King in Riyadh on Sunday.

Dr. Maszlee Malik conveyed to King Salman the greetings of the Prime Minister of Malaysia, while the King sent his greetings to him.

The audience was attended by Prince Turki bin Mohammed bin Fahd bin Abdulaziz, Minister of State and Cabinet's Member; Prince Faisal bin Farhan bin Abdullah, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Minister of State and Cabinet's Member Dr. Musaed bin Mohammed Al-Aiban; and Saudi Ambassador to Malaysia Mahmoud bin Hussein Qattan.

On the Malaysian side, it was attended by a number of officials, the Saudi Press Agency reported.
