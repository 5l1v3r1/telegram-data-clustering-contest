Sony announces 'Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse' sequel

03 Nov 2019, 11:13 by Crystal Villarreal, The Atlanta Journal-Constitution

Viewers just can’t get enough of the Spider-Man franchise, and Sony hasn’t disappointed. The entertainment company just announced a date for a sequel to the popular animated feature film “Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse.”

The new movie is set to be released on April 8, 2022, but no other details have been announced.
