Get a free upgrade at this Brazilian restaurant in Dubai

Be among the first to book, and you’ll get an automatic free upgrade

03 Nov 2019, 17:53 by Tim Skinner

If you’re looking for things to do in Dubai this week, why not savour something from South America at a brand-new flash sale that’s been announced at one of the city’s Brazilian steakhouses.

From today until Saturday November 9, Chamas Churrascaria & Bar on Sheikh Zayed Road is running a deal that will save you pennies throughout the week, for one week only.

Head along to the fun-loving Brazilian restaurant on Sheikh Zayed Road, and if you’re one of the first 30 customers booked into the restaurant each night you’ll receive a free package upgrade.

Just make sure you quote “InstaChamas” when you call the restaurant to book, and you’ll be up for the free upgrade. For instance if you head to the Noche Latina night on Monday from 6pm until 11.30pm, you’ll get a free upgrade to the house package if you get in on the deal. It usually costs Dhs215 per person for soft and Dhs315 for house, so you’re saving yourself Dhs100 right there.

Wednesday has also got a samba night running all night, where you’ll get access to 15 different varies of grilled meats, plus an international buffet to peruse, too.

There’s live music and entertainment throughout the week, and to find out more call 800 276963.
Valid Nov 3-9. Crowne Plaza Dubai, Sheikh Zayed Road (800 276963).
