Man arrested in Brooklyn fatal stabbing

03 Nov 2019

NEW YORK (AP) - Police have made an arrest in the fatal stabbing of a 54-year-old man in Brooklyn.

Police say 44-year-old Norberto Lozada, of Brooklyn, was arrested on murder charges in the death of Ramon Marrero.

Marrero was found unconscious and unresponsive with stab wounds to his torso in an apartment building in Midwood Sunday morning.

Police did not give a motive for the stabbing. Information on Lozada’s lawyer wasn’t immediately available.
