IU's "Love Poem" Becomes 5th Song Of 2019 To Score Perfect All-Kill

03 Nov 2019, 16:15 by BijayaMagar

On November 4 at approximately 0:30 AM KST,IU achieved a certified perfect all-kill on iChart with her newest single, “Love Poem”! 

A certified perfect all-kill is achieved after a song score first place on major daily and real-time charts including:Melon, Genie, Bugs, and Soribada, as well as Flo, and the weekly and realtime chart of Instiz’s iChart.

“Love Poem” is the fifth song of the year to score a perfect all-kill, with the other songs being BTS’s “Boy With Luv” in April, BOL4’s “Workaholic” in September,”, AKMU’s “How Can I Love the Heartbreak, You’re the One I Love” in October and MC Mong’s “Fame” in October.
