Everton Deny Struggling Spurs Victory

03 Nov 2019, 20:44 by SL International

Everton 1-1 Tottenham Hotspur

Everton struck late in the second half to earn themselves a point against struggling Tottenham Hotspur at Goodison Park.

The first half failed to provide any goals, before Spurs eventually broke the deadlock through Dele Alli's neat finish in the 63rd minute.

There was a horrible moment after 80 minutes when Andre Gomes suffered an horrific leg beak after a challenge from Heung-min Son, who was sent off in the process.

Everton eventually secured the equaliser in the seventh minute of second-half stoppage time thanks to Cenk Tuson's fine header.
