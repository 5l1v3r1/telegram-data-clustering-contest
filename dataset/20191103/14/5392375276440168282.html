Xiaomi Mi Note 10 to arrive in Spain on November 6th
3.5 (69.09%) 11 votes

Chinese manufacturer, Xiaomi, has an event on November 5th in China where it will release a couple of products which include Xiaomi CC9 Pro, Xiaomi Watch and Xiaomi TV 5. The CC9 Pro is considered as a version of the Xiaomi Mi Note 10 and earlier reports claim that the Note 10 will arrive in Spain on November 11. However. a new report from GadgetsFlix claims that it will come earlier. In fact, just a day after the CC9 Pro launch in China – November 6th.
