New York City Marathon: Jepkosgei tops Keitany in debut, Kamworor wins for second time

03 Nov 2019, 15:32 by Travis Fedschun

The 49th running of the New York City Marathon on Sunday was marked by a major upset on the women's side and the second win in two years on the men's.

Joyciline Jepkosgei, 25, of Kenya came out on top Sunday, winning the 2019 TCS New York City Marathon in her debut running in the race with an unofficial finish time of 2 hours, 22 minutes, and 38 seconds, seven seconds off the course record.

Jepkosgei overtook the reigning champion and countrymate Mary Keitany in the last half of the race who was attempting her fifth title in six years at the New York City Marathon The Kenyan pulled away from countrymate Keitany with about three miles to go. Four-time winner Keitany collapsed after finishing 53 seconds later.
