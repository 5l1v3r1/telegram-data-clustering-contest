Official: Oscar Garcia appointed coach of Celta Vigo

The rumours were true and the ex Barca player has been named in charge

04 Nov 2019, 20:26 by FE

Celta Vigo appointed Oscar Garcia as coach on Monday to replace Fran Escriba.

Escriba was sacked on Sunday after a 1-0 defeat by Getafe and it left Celta 18th, on nine points from 12 games.

Former Barcelona player Oscar, 46, has managed several teams, including Premier League sides Watford and Brighton, most recently working with Olympiakos in 2018 - where he left in acrimony.
