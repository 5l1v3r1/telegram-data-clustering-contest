PIC: Boity sparkles on the MTV EMA red carpet in Spain

04 Nov 2019, 11:50

Nikita Coetzee

Johannesburg – Boity Thulo was sparkling in silver when she walked the red carpet at the MTV EMAs on Sunday. 

The awards were hosted in Seville, Spain and saw the likes of Halsey, Dua Lipa and Niall Horan in attendance. 

Boity walked the carpet in an Orapeleng Modutle design. The short silver jumpsuit featured beautiful embroidered details on the left side, while the right sleeve was accented with a giant ruffle.
