Arjun Kapoor shares the menacing first-look of Sanjay Dutt aka Ahmad Shah Abdali from 'Panipat'

04 Nov 2019, 05:37 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Arjun Kapoor is all set to collaborate with Sanjay Dutt and Kriti Sanon for his first periodic film which is being helmed by ace filmmaker Ashutosh Gowariker. The actor along with his co-star is leaving no stone unturned as they gear for the trailer release.

Taking to Instagram, on Monday morning Arjun shared the first look of Sanjay's menacing character Ahmad Shah Abdali from 'Panipat'. Dutt, who is popularly also known as Baba in the industry can be seen donning his battle outfit while sporting a thick beard and that fearsome look in his eyes.

Arjun captioned Sanjay's look as "Ahmad Shah Abdali - Death Strikes Where His Shadow Falls. Panipat Trailer Out Tomorrow. #PanipatLook" also revealing that the trailer of the film will be out tomorrow.

The film recounts the events that led up to the Third Battle of Panipat, one of the mightiest battles ever fought in the history of India. Veteran actress Padmini Kolhapure will also be seen after a long time on the silver screen. 'Panipat' will hit the screens on 6th December this year.
