Boustead Heavy Industries names Mohd Fakhrul Arifin as new CFO

04 Nov 2019, 05:31 by Justin Lim

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 4): Boustead Heavy Industries Corporation Bhd (BHIC) has appointed Mohd Fakhrul Arifin Adinan as its chief financial officer (CFO).

In a filing to the stock exchange today, BHIC said the appointment was effective Nov 1.

Mohd Fakhrul, 35, had joined the group since April 2014. Prior to this, he began his career in 2005 in accounting firm Ernst & Young.

At the noon break, BHIC shares were four sen or 3.17% higher at RM1.30, valuing the group at RM323 million.
