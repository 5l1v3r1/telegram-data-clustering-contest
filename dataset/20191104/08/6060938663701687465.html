Happy Birthday Tabu: Farah Khan calls her 'Jaan' while sister Farah Naaz shares a childhood picture

04 Nov 2019, 08:42 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Today, talented actress Tabu celebrates her 47th birthday and social media is abuzz with birthday wishes for the actress. Tabu has impressed one and all with her acting skills and continuous to do so with noteworthy performances in Bollywood films. She is one of those celebrated Bollywood actresses who with their conviction and acting craft are giving young actors a run for their money.

Tabu after giving a stellar performance in the National Award winning film, 'Andhadhun', was seen in Ajay Devgn's 'De De Pyaar De' and Salman Khan's 'Bharat'. She will be next seen in Saif Ali Khan's 'Jawaani Jaaneman' and a south film 'Ala Vaikuntapuramlo'.

Many celebs extended their best wishes for the actress. While dear friend Farah Khan addressed Tabu as 'Jaan', her sister Farah Naaz shared a childhood picture of the actress. Noted singer Anup Jalota also shared his wishes for the actress.
