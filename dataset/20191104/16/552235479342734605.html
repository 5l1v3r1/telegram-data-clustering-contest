Paisley Centre roof collapses as emergency services rush to scene

Scottish Fire and Rescue confirmed eight appliances are at the scene of the incident which took place just after 3pm on Monday November 4.

04 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

Emergency services have raced to a shopping centre in Paisley after its roof caved in.

Scottish Fire and Rescue were called to the scene of the collapse just after 3pm on Monday November 4.

Images shared on social media show the ground of the Renfrewshire shopping centre, at its High Street entrance, completely covered in large pieces of debris.

The fire service confirmed eight appliances are currently in attendance, with police helping to close the centre.

 

Reports online say there are no injuries, however this is yet to be confirmed.

@bycharlitteann_ said online: “Jesus Christ the roof of the Paisley centre collapsed!!”

A Scottish Fire and Rescue spokesperson said: "We are currently in attendance at the Paisley Centre having receiving a call at 1509 on Monday November 4.

"Eight appliances are currently in attendance."

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "Around 3.15pm police were made aware of structural damage at the Paisley Centre High Street entrance.

"There are no injuries with officers in attendance to assist with closing the centre."
