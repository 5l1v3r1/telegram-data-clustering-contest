'Housefull 4' is racing against 'Golmaal Again' to become highest-grossing comedy film!

04 Nov 2019, 15:36 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

'Housefull 4' has been scoring huge at the box office since the release of the film. The multi-starrer has hit the screens on the festive occasion of Diwali and registered a strong second weekend!

The film has collected a whopping amount of Rs 168 crore net at the ticket window. The film is racing against 'Golmaal Again' to become the highest-grossing comedy film in terms of nett collections.

According to BoxOfficeIndia.com reports, in terms of gross collections, it is a tougher task and the 'Housefull 4' will have to hold phenomenally well from here on. Akshay's film was released before Diwali while Ajay Devgn's film was released post-Diwali.
