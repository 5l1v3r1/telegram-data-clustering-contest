Gag Order

04 Nov 2019, 15:54 by BRIEF

MANHATTAN — The Kobre & Kim law office sued the Commodity Futures Trading Commission in federal court for information on its allegedly illegal gag order about a $16 million fine against Kraft Foods for manipulating commodities markets.
