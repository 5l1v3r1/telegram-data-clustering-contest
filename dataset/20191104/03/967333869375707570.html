Trump Hails UFC Figter Masvidal Who Dubbed President a 'Bad Motherf*cker'

Earlier in the day, President Trump triggered a mixed reaction from the audience as he attended a UFC fight featuring mixed martial artists Jorge Masvidal and Nate Diaz.

04 Nov 2019, 03:56
