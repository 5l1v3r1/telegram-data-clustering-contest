The Top Goalscorers This Season So Far

04 Nov 2019, 19:15 by David Kappel

Romelu Lukaku has now scored just one goal less than Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi combined this season, but three players are still ahead of him among the top goalscorers in Europe.

The former Manchester United striker took his tally to nine goals in Serie A in 2019/20 on Saturday, while Ronaldo and Messi have each scored five times so far in league action.

However, Jamie Vardy, Ciro Immobile and Robert Lewandowski have scored more in this campaign.

The latter still leads the ranking after netting his 14th strike for Bayern Munich this season on Saturday, followed by Lazio's Immobile with 13 goals.
