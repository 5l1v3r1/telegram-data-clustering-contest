Crash closes part of Highway 151 in Iowa County

04 Nov 2019, 18:46 by Stephanie Fryer

MINERAL POINT, Wis. - Part of southbound Highway 151 in Iowa County near Mineral Point is blocked because of a crash.

According to dispatchers in Iowa County, one vehicle was involved in the crash.

The left lane of Highway 151 is closed at Wisconsin 39, according to an alert by the Wisconsin Department of Transportation. 

The crash was reported around 12:10 p.m., the alert said.
