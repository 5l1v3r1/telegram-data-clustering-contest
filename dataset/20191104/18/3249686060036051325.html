Jaejoong's First Appearance at a University Festival in Japan, "You are all young, aren't you?" + Japanese Netizens React

04 Nov 2019, 18:38 by Suspiria77

On November 2, Jaejoong made a special guest appearance at the fashion show held at the Aoyama Gakuin University Festival in Omotesando, Tokyo. He told the audience, "You are so young, aren't you?" He said that he lived near the university about 10 years ago. "I miss it. At that time I didn't understand Japanese and I was talking to various people at a coffee shop to study Japanese.”
On this day, Jaejoong sang 2 songs -Future Forecast and Forget Me Not- from his cover album 'Love Covers'.
