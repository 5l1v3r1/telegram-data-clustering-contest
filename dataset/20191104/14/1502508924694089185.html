M&M recalls a batch of XUV300 units to fix faulty suspension component

It will be applicable to a batch of the models manufactured till May 2019

04 Nov 2019, 14:53 by BS Reporter

Mahindra & Mahindra is recalling a limited batch of its XUV3OO models, the company said in a statement on Monday. It will be applicable to a batch of the model manufactured till May 2019.
