BTS V crowned with the title of “Idol with Midas Touch” By Idol Chart

04 Nov 2019, 14:43 by ThisKpopismyreli

BTS V always always creates buzz for being a “selling out powerhouse” as things associated with his name gets sold out in the blink of an eye. Media houses has been calling him “Sold out King” and “hand of Midas” to acknowledge his effect informally. It’s about time, V got formally crowned with the title of “Idol with Midas Touch” by Idol Chart polls conducted on the 28th Oct to 3rd Nov.
