Salman Khan and Aamir Khan's 'Andaz Apna Apna' completes 25 years; fans term it as '90's best comedy film'

04 Nov 2019, 12:35 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan and Aamir Khan are two superstars who share a good camaraderie in personal life and their 1994 film 'Andaz Apna Apna' which left the audience in splits is an evergreen classic cult in its own.

The Rajkumar Santoshi directorial which brought the two Khan's together for the first time has completed 25 years today. Fans of the respective actors took to social media sharing their views about the epic film with a hashtag #25YearsOfAndazApnaApna.
