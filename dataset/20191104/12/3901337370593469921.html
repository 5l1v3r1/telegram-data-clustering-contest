Lorch To Miss Pirates' Next Match!

04 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Soccer Laduma

Thembinkosi Lorch will miss Orlando Pirates’ match against Maritzburg United tomorrow (Tuesday).

Lorch, who was cautioned for a foul on Kaizer Chiefs' Khama Billiat in the Telkom Knockout quarter-final, will serve a one-match suspension after picking up his fourth yellow card of the season.

The Buccaneers were knocked out of the Telkom Knockout by Amakhosi with a 4-2 penalty shootout result at Moses Mabhida Stadium on Saturday. 

Lorch has made eight starts in all competitions in the domestic league so far this season.

The 26-year-old will be back for the team for the weekend’s Soweto Derby against Chiefs. Lorch bagged two assists for Pirates in Saturday's match with the goals scored by Fortune Makaringe and Gabadinho Mhango.
