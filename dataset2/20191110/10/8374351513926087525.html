Timeline of John Gokongwei Jr.

10 Nov 2019, 10:39 by James Loyola

Business tycoon John Gokongwei Jr. passed away Saturday evening, he was 93.  Here’s a brief of a life of one of the Philippines’ original taipans who built a giant conglomerate from scratch.

August 11, 1926– John Gokongwei Jr. was born in Fujian Province, China

-moved to Cebu with his family at an early age
1942– dropped out of school, sold dry goods in market stall
1943– became a trader travelling between Cebu and Manila
1945 – started Amasia Trading, importer of US products
1957– set up Universal Corn Products
1963– formed Consolidated Foods Corporation to produce branded consumer foods
1961-enrolled in De La Salle University for his MBA
1963– established the Universal Robina Corporation which produced salty snacks under Jack n’ Jill brand
1987 – organized the Digital Telecommunications Inc.
1990 – organized the JG Summit Holdings Inc.
1993– JG Summit went public, listed at Philippine Stock Exchange
1996 – entered the aviation industry with Cebu Pacific Air
2003 – Digitel launched Sun Cellular
2006 – Put up Gokongwei Brothers Foundation
