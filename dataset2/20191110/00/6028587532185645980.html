Sadie Frost Shares Her Little Black Book Of London

10 Nov 2019 by Felicity Carter

Actress, producer and fashion designer Sadie Frost is the original naughty Brit girl and she’s going in a new direction, making her directorial debut with a documentary that showcases the life of 1960s fashion icon, Mary Quant. Known on the creative scene, who better to ask for the hottest of hotspots in London town.

Shop: Liberty London

I really don’t like shopping but at Liberty I know I can do a whizz around and get the best beauty products, clothes, and gifts without having to trawl through things to find something nice - as I have no time to waste - everything has style and is unique, plus the shopping experience is beautiful.

Stay: Claridge’s

It has such class and always feels very sexy, when I go it’s such a treat. I love the vegan breakfast and the Christmas decorations are the best in London.

Play: Everyman Cinema

I love going to the movies on my own or with mates. It’s my idea of a perfect evening seeing an old film, having a glass of wine and eating some hummus, that’s my idea of a fun night out these days.

Bar: Quo Vadis

It's old school and still a little naughty. It has a great crowd of actors, artists and chefs. going in there for dinner and drinks.

Everything has become the same or so serious in the last few years. I love how grown-up we all are, but I miss the chaos of years before now.

Eat: Triyoga Camden

I can go to a yoga class and then eat the tastiest healthiest food. I have always been a vegetarian and always wanted fresh healthy food, and here I can get it straight after a yoga class so I feel regenerated and so full of life!

Music: Royal Albert Hall

You can see some of the longest concerts in comfort here. I saw a 3 hour Cure gig for the Teenage Cancer Trust and it was great, I always look forward to gigs there.

Chill out: Khera-Griggs Cleanse Clinic at Urban Retreat

It's perfect place to go and get a detox. Get a cleanse in the centre of London, it’s just what you need in the crazy world we live in. I can have a juice, do a Deepak Chopra mediation, get a intestine cleanse and lymph drainage.

Hair/beauty: Teresa Tarmey Facials

Teresa is one of the best and has wonderful products, she makes everyone looks super. Her new place in Belgravia is stunning.

Culture: Victoria and Albert Museum

They always have the best exhibitions and I love the building. I have many memories of going there as child, it has such a wonderful heritage. At the moment they have an exhibition about the fashion designer Mary Quant, I am going there a lot as I am about to embark on my directorial debut making a documentary about her.

Book/magazine store: The Conran Shop

It has a great coffee shop at the back of the store where you can chill out after shopping for the delightful presents they have on offer. They have a great selection of wonderful art and travel books too.

Your favorite place: Primrose Hill or Hampstead Heath

I love green and nature, it is so revitalizing to lose your self in these wonderful London parks - there’s ponds to swim in, hills to run up and trees to climb, its always an adventure.
