Everything Coming To ‘Disney Plus’ At Launch

10 Nov 2019 by Erik Kain

Disney+ is almost here.Disney’s new streaming service promises tons of content from across its various catalogs. Star Wars, Marvel, Pixar, animated and live-action classics, National Geographic and the recently acquired Fox catalog will all be part of the new streaming service–not to mention a swath of Disney+ Originals.

While not every movie or TV show from each of these brands will be available at launch, there will be more than enough content to keep you (and the whole family) entertained.

  • Here’s every compatible device with Disney+
  • Here’s what time the streaming service goes live tomorrow .

Some highlights include:

  • LucasFilm’s new Star Wars live-action series The Mandalorian, which I will be reviewing weekly. Episode 2 comes out Friday the 15th, and forthcoming episodes will release on Fridays.
  • Also all the classic original Star Wars films, though likely in their Lucas-altered versions unfortunately. (What a cool surprise it would be if they were restored to their original form!)
  • The X-Men animated series from the 90’s. I haven’t watched those in a hot minute!
  • Avengers: Endgame is also coming to Disney+ at launch.
  • The first three Toy Story movies so you can get all caught up for Toy Story 4 if you haven’t seen that one yet. It’s very good.
  • James Cameron’s Avatar is another launch film–just in time for its tenth anniversary, as well as next year’s sequel.
  • One of my favorite animated shows, Gravity Falls, is another Disney+ launch title. If you haven't seen this one yet, please do yourself a favor and watch it ASAP. Fun for kids and grown-ups.
  • Willow! I love Willow and I really hope that along with the film, Disney greenlights a TV series.
  • All 30 seasons of The Simpsons will be available on day one.
  • The new “live-action” Lady and the Tramp movie is a day-one Disney+ exclusive. It’s gotten mediocre reviews but I bet kids will love it.
  • So many great animated classics like Beauty and the Beast, The Lion King, Aladdin and The Little Mermaid.

Suffice to say, there’s a ton of quality content landing on Disney+ this Tuesday, with a bunch more to follow. You can check out all the titles below. (P.S. there have been various announcements recently for new titles coming on launch day and it’s possible I missed something. If you notice anything missing, please let me know on Twitter or Facebook. Thanks!)

Disney+ launches on November 12th in the US, Canada and the Netherlands; on November 19th in New Zealand and Australia; and on March 31st, 2020 in the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy and Spain. The service costs $6.99 a month or $69.99 per year.
