The Beauty Luxe List For November

10 Nov 2019 by Felicity Carter

The best products and treatments to serve all your beauty needs from new palette curated by Mrs Rodial herself to the ultra-relaxing floral candle from FLOWERBX that’ll uplift your mood.

Mrs Rodial Palette by Rodial: There’s a new palette in town kids and it’s a handpicked edit from Mrs Rodial herself. The all-in-one limited edition palette features a bespoke collection of shades to create a sophisticated full face and eye look. Combining best-selling shades with 5 limited edition hues, the warm, autumnal tones are perfect for the season ahead.

Eau de Parfum Discovery Set by DIPTYQUE: First launched as a boutique on Paris’ Boulevard Saint-Germain, Diptyque offers a range of elegant scented candles, diffusers and Eau de Toilettes, and this set of 5 perfumes is designed with discovery in mind. It is made up of Fleur de Peau, Do Son, L'Ombre Dans L'Eau, Tam Dao and the aromatic Minthé.

False Lashes by Anastasia Beverly Hills: Makeup favorite, Anastasia Beverly Hills have extended their line in the form of false lashes. Available in 6 different styles that flatter any eye shape and suit any occasion, from every day right through to dramatic. What’s more, they’re cruelty free and provide up to 20 wears.

 

Drink It Up Facial by Kat Maconie: Kat Maconie Beauty Boutique offers a one-stop shop for the award-winning designer shoes along with a one-of-a-kind, fully-serviced beauty salon and their range of facials will keep you looking fresh. Try the ‘Drink It Up Facial’ which will restore your glow. Vacuum suction lifts impurities, while high-frequency ultra-sound speeds up cell renewal and promotes better blood circulation. Suitable for all skin types.

Peony Scented Candle by FLOWERBX: For all those flower lovers out there, FLOWERBX also serve up delicious candles as well as bouquets. It combines base notes of sandalwood, cedarwood and amber (it’ll burn for 50 hours) and is housed in a beautiful rich chocolate glass with their signature velvet and gold foil label that’ll cute on your mantlepiece.

One Evening Body Splash by Bamford: This fragrance is a light water scented with Vetiver, Cedar and Amberwood. To use, either add a few drops into a bath or splash on the body after bathing to feel refreshed and subtly scented.

Peace Balm of Serenity by Kalmar: Massage this solid fragranced balm onto pulse points (i.e. the wrist) and it will help the mind to focus quietly.

The Charlotte Darlin Makeup Kit by Charlotte Tilbury: New, right in time for Christmas give this to a loved one and they’ll be hooked on CT. The kit consists of the brand new Easy Eye Palette in Charlotte Darling, The Classic in Audrey, Full Fat Lashes mascara in Glossy Black, Beauty Light Wand in Peachgasm, Lip Cheat lip liner in Hot Gossip and then there’s also the Superstar Lips in Everlasting Kiss.

Tickle Powder Highlighter by Benefit: A brand new highlighter by Benefit, use the miniture brush to apply the fine powder along cheekbones for light pink-come-gold lustre.

Femme De Force Scent by Crabtree & Evelyn: It’s a punchy rose fragrance that conveys an attitude (hence the name) thanks to the Italian lemon, cassis and frankincense for a bold first impression.

Amplifier Lip Duo by Illamasqua: A new offering from Illamasqua, this Lip Duo includes an Antimatter Lipstick in the shade of Amplifier and Colouring Lip Pencil in the shade of Trance. It makes a perfect stocking stuffer for all those that have a lust for lippy.

Twin Cheeks Kitten & Bronzer Duo by Stila: Here you’ll find two complementary bronzer shades specially designed to contour, define and highlight but in a sun kissed kind of way. What’s more, it’s highly pigmented so it’s long lasting.

Sleep Dream Easy Pillow Mist by Sanctuary Spa: Simply lightly spriz your pillow before bed and thanks to the midnight flowers and lavender oil in the mist, you’ll unwind and get a restful nights sleep.

Atlantic Kelp And Magnesium Salt Anti-Fatigue Exfoliating Body Scrub by REN Skincare: An exfoliating body balm from REN, it makes use of fresh Sea and Magnesium (a.k.a. Epsom) Salts that both polishes and smooths the skin. Apply on the body and scrub in a circular motion and it will encourage the skin's natural cell renewal, while also relaxing the muscles.

Hangover Watermelon Edition by Too Faced: This is a 3-in-1 replenishing primer and setting spray, a must-have for the upcoming party season.
