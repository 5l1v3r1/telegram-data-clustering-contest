Merkel Calls on Europe to Develop New Weapons

BERLIN (Sputnik) - German Chancellor Angela Merkel called on European states to develop new weapon systems, including military aircraft and tanks.

10 Nov 2019, 13:46
"We will jointly develop weapons systems in the future, including a military aircraft project and a tank project. Germany and France are at the cutting edge here," the chancellor said in her traditional weekly video address, this time dedicated to the 64th anniversary of the founding the Bundeswehr.

According to Merkel, the conflict in Ukraine and "annexation of Crimea" once again actualized defense and cooperation within the framework of military alliances, especially in NATO.
