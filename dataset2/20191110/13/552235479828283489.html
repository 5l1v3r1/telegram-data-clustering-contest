What channel is Livingston vs Rangers? Live stream, TV and kick-off details

The Ibrox side are flying high off the back of their impressive win against Porto midweek.

10 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Chris Doyle, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/chris-doyle/

Rangers will do battle with Livingston in their quest to topple Celtic from the Premiership summit.

Steven Gerrard's trail their city rivals only by goal difference and can keep the heat on with a win in West Lothian.

Livi are no pushovers, however, defeating Celtic last month. They also made Rangers work for a 1-0 Betfred Cup success earlier in the season.

The Light Blues dispatched Porto in the Europa League in mid-week, and this clash has been given an extra edge by Gary Holt taking aiming at his counterpart Gerrard.

Here's all the details you need to know ahead of the Premiership clash.

When and where will the game take place?

The match will take place on Sunday, November 10 at the Tony Macaroni Arena.

Kick-off is at 3pm.

What channel is it on?

You can watch the game live on Sky Sports Football.

Coverage begins at 2.30pm.

Is it being streamed online?

Yes, subscribers can watch on the Sky Go app.

Where else can I follow the game?

You can follow it right here on dailyrecord.co.uk of course! We'll be running a live blog well ahead of the TV coverage, bringing you all the key moments as well as post-match reaction and analysis.

A match report and much, much more will also be available on our website following the final whistle.
