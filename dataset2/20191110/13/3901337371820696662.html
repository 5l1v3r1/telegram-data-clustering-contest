Starting XI: Arrows v Sundowns

10 Nov 2019, 13:56 by Soccer Laduma

Absa Premiership

Teams: Golden Arrows and Mamelodi Sundowns

Date: 10 November 2019

Venue: Sugar Ray Xulu Stadium

Mamelodi Sundowns will be eager get back to winning ways in the Absa Premiership when they visit Golden Arrows on Sunday afternoon.

The Brazilians have dropped five points in their last two matches after a 2-0 loss to Chiefs, which was followed by a goalless draw with Baroka FC in midweek.

As a result, Pitso Mosimane’s charges find themselves ten points behind the AmaKhosi, who defeated Orlando Pirates 2-0 on Saturday.

On the other hand, Abafana Bes’thende are not doing too badly under Steve Komphela, having qualified for the Telkom Knockout semi-finals by edging SuperSport United.

Arrows come into the game lying sixth on the table following the stalemate with SuperSport on Tuesday and they could leapfrog Bidvest Wits in fourth place if they manage to upset Sundowns.

Masandawana have been dominant over Arrows in their previous meetings in the league, after winning the last six matches between the sides.

The last time Abafana Bes’thende beat Sundowns in the league was back in September 2015.
