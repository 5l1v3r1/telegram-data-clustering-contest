Mallorca earned hard-fought win over Villarreal 3-1 at Estadi de Son Moix

Following this result, Vicente Moreno's team secured their 3rd win in this campaign

10 Nov 2019, 13:24 by SPORT.es

Mallorca sways pass Villarreal 3-1 on Sunday at Estadi de Son Moix. Mallorca faced the match with a desire to make up their league after losing 3-0 against Real Valladolid while Villarreal had to settle for a draw in the last match played of the competition. Following today's result, Mallorca is 16th in the LaLiga, having garnered 14 points from 13 matches, while Villarreal sits at 9th with 18 points after playing a total of 13 matches.

The first half of the game started in favour of Mallorca, thanks to a goal from Junior Lago 13 minutes in the game. Later, Mallorca scored once more and increased their lead 2-0 thanks to a goal from Dani Rodríguez in the 24th minute, and the first half ended 2-0.

The second half started in favour of Villarreal, with a good early penalty goal from Santi Cazorla in the 49th minute. However, Mallorca 53 minutes in the game increased their advantage thanks to a goal from Takefusa Kubo, which saw the second half to a 3-1.

As for substitutions, both coaches used full substitutions. For Mallorca, Josep Sene, Abdón and Xisco Campos came on for Takefusa Kubo, Ante Budimir and Lumor, while Villarreal brought on Samuel Chukwueze, Javier Ontiveros and Manu Trigueros to replace Vicente Iborra, Moi Gómez and Mario Gaspar.

The referee booked Dani Rodríguez and Aleix Febas from Mallorca, while for Villarreal Sergio Asenjo, Rubén Peña and Raúl Albiol saw a yellow card as well.

Mallorca with their next fixture against Levante at away stadium, while Villarreal will face Celta at home stadium.
