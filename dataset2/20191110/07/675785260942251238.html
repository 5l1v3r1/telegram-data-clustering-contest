What channel is Man Utd vs Brighton on? TV and live stream information

Manchester United have seen a little upturn in form but Brighton will fancy their chances of getting a result at Old Trafford

10 Nov 2019, 07:10 by Sam Fletcher

Manchester United take on Brighton in the Premier League as Ole Gunnar Solskjaer continues to fight off pressure on his job.

The Red Devils currently lie tenth in the table having only won twice at home all season.

But with both those wins coming against top four opposition, it’s teams outside the European places who United have struggled to break down.

As for Brighton , they’ve won three of their last four matches and have climbed above United and into the top half of the division.

After a slow start, Graham Potter has finally got the football and the results he wants from his team.

Here’s all you need to know ahead of the match...

When is the game?

The game takes place on Sunday 10th November.

Kick-off is set for 2pm.

Where is the game being played?

The game is being played at Old Trafford.

United have never lost against Brighton when playing at home.

What TV channel is the game on?

This game is not being broadcast on TV but highlights will be shown on Match of the Day 2 at 10:30pm.

Can you live stream the game?

With no live TV coverage, the game is unavailable for live streaming this weekend.

Manchester United team news

Solskjaer has an extensive injury list ahead of Sunday’s match with Axel Tuanzebe, Eric Bailly, Luke Shaw, Diogo Dalot, Paul Pogba and Nemanja Matic all set to miss out.

There’s yet more injury worry for the United boss: Harry Maguire, Scott McTominay and Victor Lindelof will all have late medicals to see if they can feature.

Captain Ashley Young misses out through suspension.

Brighton team news

The visitors will have to do without full-back Bernardo and winger Jose Izquierdo who both remain sidelined with an injury.

Adam Webster is also out having being stretchered off with an ankle injury in the Seagulls 2-0 win over Norwich last weekend.
