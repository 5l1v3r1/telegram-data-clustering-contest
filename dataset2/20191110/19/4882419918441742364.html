Osasuna drop points against Getafe: 0-0 at Coliseum Alfonso Pérez

Getafe failed to seal all three points against Osasuna 0-0 at Coliseum Alfonso Pérez on Sunday

10 Nov 2019, 19:34 by SPORT.es

Getafe was held down on Sunday at Coliseum Alfonso Pérez by Osasuna as the game ended 0-0. Getafe and Osasuna arrived at this challenge after previous wins. Getafe arrived with the intention of increasing their league score after winning their previous two games against Celta away and Granada at home stadium, by 0-1 and 3-1 respectively while Osasuna came from winning 4-2 against Alavés in the last match played. As of today, Getafe is 7th in the LaLiga, having garnered 20 points from 13 matches, while Osasuna sits at 10th with 19 points after playing a total of 13 matches.

The first half ended with little further action at either end and goalless.

There were no goals during the second 45 minutes and the game ended 0-0.

At the end of the match, both teams made 3 substitutions each. For Getafe, Jaime Mata, Francisco Portillo and Enric Gallego came on for David, Kennedy and Jorge Molina, while Osasuna brought on Sergio Herrera, Brandon Thomas and Fran Mérida to replace Rubén Martínez, Nacho Vidal and Marc Cardona.

The referee booked Jorge Molina and Allan Nyom from Getafe, while for Osasuna Nacho Vidal, Rubén García, Brandon Thomas and Oier saw a yellow card as well.

Getafe will play against Espanyol at away stadium, while Osasuna will face Athletic at home stadium.
