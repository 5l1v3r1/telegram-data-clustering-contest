Neil Lennon delivers firm Celtic response as John Kennedy is linked with Hibs job

The Parkhead assistant coach is reportedly on a five-man short list for the vacant post at Easter Road.

10 Nov 2019, 19:21 by Chris Doyle, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/chris-doyle/

Neil Lennon has delivered a firm response as Celtic assistant coach John Kennedy has been linked with the Hibs job.

The Parkhead no.2 is reportedly on a five-man short list for the vacant post at Easter Road.

Record Sport understands Jack Ross is the front runner for the position and will hold talks with Hibs chiefs on Tuesday.

But Lennon insists his right-hand man isn't going anywhere if he is approached by the Leith side.

He said: "I don't want him to go for a start.

"It doesn't surprise me, because he is a superb coach.

"It is a feather in a cap for John if that is the case but as far as I'm concerned he won't be going anywhere, he is very integral to what we are doing here."
