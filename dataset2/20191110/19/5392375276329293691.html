Xiaomi TV sells over 200,000 units in 10 minutes
5 (100%) 1 vote

Today, Chinese manufacturing giant, Xiaomi, took to Weibo to announce the sale performance of its TV brand. According to the company, its TV took just 10 minutes and 36 seconds to exceed 200,000 units in the Chinese 11.11 sales. Last year, it took 58 minutes and 17 seconds to hit the same mark. This shows how well the company’s TV brand has grown in just a year.

According to Li Xiaoshuang, general manager of the Xiaomi TV Department, over the last three quarters (Q1 – Q3 2019), Xiaomi TV has shipped about 2.3 million units per quarter.

Xiaomi TV 11.11 sale discount starts with the TV 4S 75 inches which are selling for 4599 yuan ($650). In addition, the TV 4X 65 inches sells for 1999 yuan ($283) while the TV 4X 55 inches is selling for 1399 yuan ($198). Furthermore, the TV 43-inch is selling for 1099 yuan ($155) and the 32-inch version goes for 699 yuan ($99). The Xiaomi full-screen TV 55-inch and TV 4C 40-inch are available for 1699 yuan ($240) and 999 yuan ($141) respectively. These prices will be much lower if there are additional coupons.

You can find these deals on Xiaomi Mall, Xiaomi Youpin, Xiaomi Home, Jingdong, Tmall, Suning Tesco and other major e-commerce platforms. In addition, the general manager of the company’s TV Department revealed that Xiaomi’s full-screen TV Pro 43-inch has hit the market. It comes with a 1499 yuan ($212) price tag.
