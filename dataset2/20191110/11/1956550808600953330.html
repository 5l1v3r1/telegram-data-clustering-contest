Kids eat free at Zoco every weekend

Don't spend a Dirham extra on the kids'meals

10 Nov 2019, 11:22 by Kelly Johnstone

Parents listen up. Taking the whole family out is now looking less daunting for the wallet with two days of free eating for little ones at Mexican diner ZOCO.

On Saturdays between 2pm and 7pm under tens can eat absolutely free when they choose from the special new kids'menu.

The options include Mexican favourites such as nachos, quesadillas, tacos, tostadas and empanadas to satisfy little appetites.

And then on Fridays kids under six eat absolutely free at the ARRIBA! ARRIBA! brunch.

Pop the kids in the dedicated play corner where mini brunchers can enjoy a heap of fun games and activities, while a kids menu featuring everything from popcorn to ice lollies, will keep their tummies happy.

Meanwhile mums and dads can sit back, relax and enjoy a refreshing beverage or two (or three, but who's counting?).
