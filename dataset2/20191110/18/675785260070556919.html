Liverpool 3-1 Man City player ratings as Reds extend lead at top of Premier League

Goals from Fabinho, Sadio Mane and Mohamed Salah secured a stunning victory for Liverpool

10 Nov 2019, 18:23 by John Cross, Andy Dunn, https://www.facebook.com/johncrossmirror/, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/andy-dunn/

Liverpool opened up a nine point lead on Manchester City in the first title showdown at Anfield.

City were furious with Liverpool's opener after Trent Alexander-Arnold appeared to handle in his own area.

Referee Michael Oliver waved play on and Liverpool broke before Fabinho lashed in from distance.

Liverpool then had a second on 13 minutes when Andy Robertson whipped in a stunning cross for Mohamed Salah to head in.

Sadio Mane then made it 3-0 early in the second half as he headed in from Jordan Henderson's delivery.

Bernardo Silva got one back for City

Here are complete player ratings from Anfield.
