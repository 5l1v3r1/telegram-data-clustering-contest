Oman's roads being cleared of fallen trees

10 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Public Authority for Civil Defence and Ambulance (PACDA) announced that it was supporting other competent authorities in clearing fallen trees from the road.

In an online statement, PACDA said, “Civil Defence and Ambulance Directorate of Al Buraimi Governorate supports other competent authorities to remove trees fallen on the road due to heavy rain and strong winds.”

"PACDA calls on citizens and residents to to take care and exercise caution."
