Barcelona set to turn to La Masia graduate

10 Nov 2019, 12:24 by Ernesto Hernandez

Moussa Wague could finally be set to feature for Barcelona following Nelson Semedo’s injury.

Lost in another sublime performance by Lionel Messi in Barcelona’s win against Celta Vigo was the injury picked up by right-back Nelson Semedo.

Semedo came off midway through the first-half. Sergio Busquets took his place and the camera caught him having a few words with Ernesto Valverde.

Initially, some assumed it might have been a tactical change but the words Semedo was having with Valverde were not of anger or annoyance. He was not playing poorly so an injury looked the most likely explanation.

According to Mundo Deportivo, Semedo suffered a calf injury that will leave the Portuguese right-back sidelined for approximately five weeks.

His injury means that Moussa Wague could finally be in line to play meaningful minutes for the Catalans. The 21-year-old has been discarded by the manager and has watched the majority, if not all, the games from the stands.

When Semedo was forced to come off, Sergi Roberto filled in at right-back. Roberto is likely to take up that spot until Semedo returns but Wague is likely to play the next game against Leganes.

The reason being is Sergi Roberto is suspended for yellow card accumulations. With Wauge being the only other natural right-back, logic dictates that he should play against Leganes.

The Senegalese player was the starting right-back for Barça B last season and featured in a couple of games at the end of the season with the first team. But he, alongside backup keeper Neto, are the only players yet to play a single minute this season.

Unless Ernesto Valverde decides to play an unorthodox line-up, there is no other option besides playing Wague.

It is worth remembering that Wague played well with the ‘B’ team and did not look out-of-place with the first team. The only question will be match fitness because he has not played at all this season.

With the international break coming up, Barcelona should have a bit more time to prepare and make sure that Wague is ready. He will finally get a chance to prove himself and will be eager to take full advantage of it.
