Adama Traore replaces Rodrigo Moreno in Spain squad

The former Barcelona winger Adama Traore is in after Rodrigo's injury

10 Nov 2019, 12:46 by Sport EN

Former Barcelona winger Adama Traore, currently at Wolverhampton Wanderers, is in the Spain squad after Rodrigo Moreno pulled out injured.

The Spanish-Mali winger played for the U21 before but this is his first international call-up.

Adama is 23 years old and was poised to play for Mali before Spain called him up. He left Barcelona in 2015 after coming through the youth academy.

He signed for Aston Villa, then Middlesborough, and finally joined Wolves on a five year deal, where he has played frequently this season.
