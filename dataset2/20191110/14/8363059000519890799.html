The Newest Teaser for Westworld’s Next Season Is an Ad for a Menacing Tech Company

10 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Julie Muncy

Incite, Inc. is here to propel you forward to the future. No, don’t think about the name too much. It’s probably not threatening.

The new Westworld ad is a mindbender, and has ARG written all over it. Released yesterday at a WIRED panel called “The Future of Data: The Promise and Peril”, the ad features a promotion for the fictional Incite, Inc., a company that (mysteriously!) also sponsored the real-world WIRED panel. Something mysterious is afoot!
