Aston Villa suffer another major injury blow during Wolves meeting

Wolves v Aston Villa | After suffering a triple injury blow before the game, Dean Smith's side suffered another injury only minutes in

10 Nov 2019, 14:12 by Ashley Preece, https://www.facebook.com/PreeceBirminghamLive/

Aston Villa have been dealt yet another injury blow - with Jed Steer being forced off just five minutes into the match with Wolves.

Dean Smith was unable to name any of Jack Grealish, Tom Heaton or Bjorn Engels in his squad for the visit to Molineux.

Only minutes into the game, the 27-year-old winced in pain and appeared in some discomfort after watching Matt Doherty's cross clip the crossbar.

 

Steer, who came in for the injured Tom Heaton today, immediately signaled to the bench and couldn't put weight on his right foot.

Orjan Nylaand, Villa's third-choice goalkeeper, comes in for his first bit of Aston Villa match action since Preston in the Championship in December 29, 2018.

Villa, meanwhile, have been put under pressure by Wolves in the early stages, with Frederic Guilbert booked.
