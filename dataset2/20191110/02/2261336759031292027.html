Bellator fighter Dillon Danis banned from attending KSI vs Logan Paul 2

10 Nov 2019, 02:15 by Carolyn Lee Adams

Welterweight fighter Dillon Danis, who recently improved his reputation by getting bullied kids into martial arts classes, is once again embroiled in drama outside the care. Danis has reportedly been banned from attending tonight’s KSI vs Logan Paul 2 at the Staples Center.
