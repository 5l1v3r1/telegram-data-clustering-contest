Messi Equals Ronaldo's Record After Hat-Trick Heroics

10 Nov 2019, 09:30 by SL International

Lionel Messi was in superb form for FC Barcelona on Saturday after bagging a hat-trick, which has now seen him equal a record by his long-standing rival Cristiano Ronaldo.

Click through the gallery the players with the most hat-tricks in LaLiga history.

After a disappointing result in the UEFA Champions League earlier this week, Messi took it upon himself to get Barcelona back on the winning trail as they defeated Celta Vigo at the Camp Nou.

The Argentine superstar bagged two free-kicks and penalty to claim his 34th hat-trick in the Spanish top fight, equalling the record of Ronaldo's 34 hat-tricks.
