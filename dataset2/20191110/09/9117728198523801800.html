Celebrating Ayrton Senna's life: Footage from the Brazilian F1 festival

10 Nov 2019, 09:42 by Matt Gretton

Formula 1 fans, current and past drivers came together to celebrate Ayrton Senna's life at the 2019 F1 festival in Senna's hometown of Sao Paulo! Lots of donuts, heritage runs and stunts took place.
