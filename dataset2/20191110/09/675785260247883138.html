I'm A Celeb's Ant McPartlin pictured back on set with Declan Donnelly for first time in two years

Ant McPartlin is back in Australia for the first time since taking last year off to deal with his addiction issues

10 Nov 2019, 09:02 by Louise Randell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/louise-randell/

I'm A Celebrity host Ant McPartlin has been pictured back with his co-host Declan Donnelly in Australia for first time since his drink-driving conviction.

Ant, 43, took last year off to deal with his personal demons following a car crash in London which forced him to address his addiction issues and head to rehab.

He was replace by Holly Willoughby on I'm A Celeb 2018, but he's now back for this year's show.

Ant arrived in Australia yesterday and has now reunited with Dec on their first trip to the jungle together in two years.

They shared a cute snap of themselves from the set as they prepare to get to work, captioning it simply: "G'Day."

Fans were thrilled to see the pair reunited Down Under, and praised Ant's bravery for getting his life back together.

One wrote: "It is always an amazing thing to see them together."

 

Another added: "Not long to go now.. so glad you are both back together where you belong.. my favourite show.."

A third commented: "So good to see the boys back together!! So excited."

Dec was the first of them to arrive Down Under for the 2019 series, bringing along his wife Ali and their daughter Isla.

Ant followed by landing in Oz last night and was seen walking through the airport with his girlfriend Anne-Marie Corbett.

The new stars of I'm A Celeb Extra Camp have also touched down in Brisbane, with Adam Thomas and Emily Atack joining Joel Dommett for this year's spin-off show.

The first contestant to arrive was Ian Wright.
