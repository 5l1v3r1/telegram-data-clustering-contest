'I am Virat Kohli', says David Warner's little daughter

10 Nov 2019, 09:38 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

NEW DELHI: Australia batsman David Warner's daughter, Indi Rae, wants to bat like India captain Virat Kohli when she grows up.

In a video posted by Warner on his Instagram account on Sunday, Indi could be seen playing cricket in the backyard of her house and saying "I am Virat Kohli" while playing a shot.

"I'm not sure about this one. Indi wants to be @virat.kohli Caption This??," Warner wrote while posting the video.

Kohli, who recently celebrated his 31st birthday, is currently on a break having been rested for the ongoing three-match T20I series against Bangladesh at home.

However, Kohli will return for the two-match Test series against the visitors.
