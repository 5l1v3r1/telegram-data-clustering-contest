New sports bar Vuvuzela opens on Palm Jumeirah

Expect pub grub classics and big screens

10 Nov 2019, 09:04 by Amy Mathieson

There’s a brand-new sports bar on the Palm, and if you’re looking for somewhere to watch the match this weekend, this could be one for you.

Vuvuzela is now open at Fairmont The Palm and aims to become “Palm Jumeirah’s most friendly neighborhood pub”.

And if you’re a Palm resident you’ll find it even friendlier, as you’ll get 25 percent off your bill until December 20.

The bar will be showing everything from football to cricket, rugby and more and boasts big screens, leather armchairs and a starring 120-in central projector.

There’s also a menu of classic pub grub including nachos, burgers, barbecue beef sandwiches, chilli cheese dogs, fish and chips, chicken wings and more.

Named after the musical instrument that’s used at sporting events, and traditionally to call in social gatherings in South Africa. Décor is South African-inspired with bright colours as well as signed shirts from Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez.

It’s not the only sports bar to open recently – with Black Goose at JBR and The Exit also setting up.

Plenty to check out this month, then.
