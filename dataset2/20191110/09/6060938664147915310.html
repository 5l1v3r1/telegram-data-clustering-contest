Photo: Sara Ali Khan looks mesmerising in a red princess outfit

10 Nov 2019, 09:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sara Ali Khan might be just two films old but the diva has already carved a niche for herself in the industry with her brilliant performances. Not only that but the millennial diva is also an avid social media user and keeps on sharing pictures and videos on Instagram.

With more than 15 million followers on the picture-sharing platform, Sara recently took to the Insta story and shared a picture of herself sipping a coconut drink while dressed in a red princess outfit.

Sara, who is the daughter of Saif Ali Khan and Amrita Singh has not only been making headlines due to her spectacular performances on the celluloid but even her relationship with co-star Kartik Aaryan grabbed a lot of attention.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Sara will soon be seen opposite Kartik Aaryan in Imtiaz Ali's next and is currently shooting with Varun Dhawan for the remake of 'Coolie No. 1'.
