SNP accuse Boris Johnson of 'shafting' Scotland after PM's Northern Ireland Brexit boast

Leaked footage shows Tory leader telling business chiefs in Ulster how great it is that they are getting to keep free movement and access to EU markets.

10 Nov 2019, 04:30 by John Ferguson, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/john-ferguson/

Boris Johnson has been accused of leaving Scotland “royally shafted” after being filmed boasting of Northern Ireland’s preferential Brexit deal.

Leaked footage has emerged of the Prime Minister telling business leaders in Ulster they were getting to keep free movement and access to EU markets under his revised plans for leaving the EU.

He said: “Northern Ireland has got a great deal. You keep free movement, you keep access to the single market but you also have unfettered access to the EU.”

 

The SNP has argued that Scotland should also be allowed to retain access to lucrative European markets after Brexit by remaining in the Customs Union and Single Market.

But under the Withdrawal Agreement Johnson hopes to implement, free market access will only be given to Northern Ireland to avoid a hard border with the Republic of Ireland.

SNP candidate for West Aberdeenshire and Kincardine Fergus Mutch said: “Despite voting overwhelmingly to remain in the EU, Scotland is being royally shafted by the Tories.

"Boris Johnson is holding Scotland in contempt, with leaked footage this week showing the rambling Tory leader boasting about the special deal handed to Northern Ireland.

 

“Scotland is the only country in the UK that’s being dragged out of the EU against our will and given no say over our future relationship with Europe.

“That is beyond shameful and frankly makes a complete mockery of claims the UK is a partnership of equals.

On December 12, voters have the chance to send Boris Johnson a message and escape this Brexit fiasco once and for all.”
