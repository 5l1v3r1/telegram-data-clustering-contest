Photos: Baby Trump Balloon Stabbed 'Beyond Repair' Amid POTUS Alabama Visit

10 Nov 2019, 04:40

This is not for the first time a balloon caricature of Trump wearing a diaper and holding a cellphone in his hand has been vandalized during protests. This time, however, observers suggest that the damage is irreparable.

US President Donald Trump appeared in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, on Saturday, attending a Alabama-Louisiana State football game alongside several local Republican lawmakers.

In contrast to reports of a warm welcome for POTUS at the game, his floating caricature - 'Baby Trump' balloon - was not so lucky, as it was knifed by a protester in a park located close to the site where the University of Alabama football game was held.
