See top local talent with Aloft Hotel Dubai

Sponsored: Get ready for a series of free shows from some of the UAE’s best talent thanks to the Live at Aloft Hotels Homecoming Tour

10 Nov 2019, 17:01 by Time Out Dubai staff

Live music fans, mark your calendars. Trendy hotel chain, Aloft Hotels has teamed up with some of the hottest names in the region for a series of intimate and free-to-attend shows across the UAE as part of the Live at Aloft Hotels Homecoming Tour.

The stellar line-up includes independent talent, as well as Universal Music Group MENA artists Danny Aridi, Mahdi Baccouch, Esther Eden, Ahmed Hassan, Angi, Carl & The Reda Mafia, Lea Makhoul and Egyptian band Almena.

“At Aloft Hotels we believe that music is our common language and brings people together. Through our global music program Live at Aloft Hotels we provide a platform for artists to showcase their talent and now, through the Homecoming Tour, we are continuing to support the artist community as they celebrate their local roots with UAE fans,” said Sandra Schulze-Potgieter, Vice President Premium & Select Brands at Marriott International, Middle East & Africa.

Aloft Hotels are already known for being located in hotspots for live music, and continuously support touring musicians with special discounted artist rates, the chance to use its W XYZ bar as a performance space for free and offering up tips ranging from where to grab a post-gig bite, the best places to purchase guitar strings and the most affordable recording studios nearby.

“Aloft Hotels have always been a great platform for creativity and music, our partnership will always aim to mutually grow the local music scene and to support talented emerging artists. The Homecoming Tour will be an incredible experience for the artists’ fans who will be given the access to a new generation of music introduced by the stars of tomorrow,” adds Patrick Boulos, CEO at Universal Music Group MENA.

The four-stop tour will take place at some of Aloft’s newest and most notable hotels across the UAE and the Live At Aloft Hotels Homecoming Tour kicks off at The Backyard in Aloft Me’aisam in Dubai Production City on Wednesday November 13.

Danny Aridi, Hadi and Mahdi Baccouch are all set to perform at what promises to be an evening of soulful tunes.

On Wednesday November 20, Carl & The Reda Mafia, Tara Rautenbach and Angi will be taking to the stage at Aloft Al Ain’s W XYZ bar.

Next up on the mega line-up is Fafa, Esther Eden and Ahmed Hassan who you can catch on Wednesday December 11 at Brick in Aloft Abu Dhabi.

And on Wednesday January 8. Head to W XYZ bar in Aloft City Centre Deira and get ready to be wowed by singers Lea Makhoul, Almena and Stephon LaMar.

We’re ready for it, are you?
