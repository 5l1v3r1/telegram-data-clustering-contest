India vs Bangladesh, 3rd T20I: Deepak Chahar's hat-trick hands hosts T20I series win over Bangladesh

10 Nov 2019, 17:48 by PTI

Highlights

  • India defeated Bangladesh by 30 runs in the third and final T20 International to pocket the series 2-1
  • Deepak Chahar (6/7) registered best ever figures in T20Is to guide India to a thrilling win
  • Chahar also became the first Indian to take a hat-trick in the shortest format of the game

NAGPUR: Pacer Deepak Chahar took a sensational six-wicket haul, including a hat-trick after entertaining half-centuries from Shreyas Iyer and K L Rahul, as India defeated a self-destructing Bangladesh by 30 runs in the third and final T20 International to win the series 2-1, here on Sunday.

SCORECARD

Pacers Deepak Chahar (6/7 in 3.2 overs), who registered the best figures in a T20I match on Sunday besides becoming the first Indian to take a hat-trick in the shortest format, and Shivam Dube (3/30) shared nine wickets amongst themselves to secure India's first T20I series win at home this season.

Heavy late evening dew rendered the spinners ineffective but Chahar and Dube rose to the occasion.
