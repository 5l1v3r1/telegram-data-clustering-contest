Messi hat-trick keeps him on Benzema's tail in race for the Pichichi

Benzema leads the way with nine but Messi is closing gap after injury problems

10 Nov 2019, 17:01 by Nino Vàzquez R.

Lionel Messi is in the race of the Pichichi once again after an injury-disrupted start to the season left him off the pace.

However, his hat-trick against Celta Vigo on Saturday -- a penalty and two free-kicks -- took him to nine in just six starts in Spain's top flight.

He's still one goal behind Real Madrid's Karim Benzema, though, who has scored nine after bagging a brace against Eibar earlier on Saturday.
