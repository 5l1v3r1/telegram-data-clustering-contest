Sinking suburban 'home' drifts down Thames in watery climate protest

10 Nov 2019, 21:58

LONDON: A floating mock-up of a typical British suburban home was seen slowly sinking into the Thames in central London on Sunday (Nov 10) in a protest organised by Extinction Rebellion to demand politicians fighting a Dec 12 general election act on climate change.

A white microwave oven bobbed in the water next to the large model house, complete with drainpipes and chimney stack, which wallowed half-submerged in the river's brownish waters not far from Tower Bridge.
