Six top things to do in Dubai this week

From free pool days to brand-new ladies' nights we bring you things to do in Dubai this week

10 Nov 2019, 08:25 by Amy Mathieson

It's Sunday, a fresh new week to make plans for. And where better to start than right here. We bring you the top things to do in Dubai this week, whether you're looking for cheap drinks, free pool days to dinners with celebrity chefs. Enjoy the week.

See supercars
Dubai International Motor Show is back, and we can’t wait to race down to Dubai World Trade Centre on Tuesday November 12. There are some exciting new innovations on display, plus mega supercars to admire.
From Dhs20. Tue Nov 12-Sat Nov 16, 1pm-9pm. Dubai World Trade Centre, Sheikh Zayed Road, www.dubaimotorshow.com.

Check out a new ladies’ night
The Jenny from the Block ladies’ night at Soul Street promises urban beats, gourmet street food and more. You’ll get five free drinks, but for Dhs140 you can get three courses from the street food menu. Gents can get in on the deal for Dhs190 including two free drinks.
Wed 7pm-2am. FIVE Jumeirah Village, JVC (04 248 9999).

Rooftop dining (with free bubbly)
Fancy trying out a new menu? Popular bar Mercury Lounge has a revamped look and new eats in-spired by the French and Italian Riveiras, so it’s time to check it out. Ladies, you’ll also get two free glasses of sparkling grape from 8pm to 10pm on a Wednesday.
Free. Wed 8pm-10pm. Four Seasons Resort Dubai at Jumeirah Beach, Jumeirah 1 (04 270 7777).

Free pool access
The swish pool club at Palazzo Versace Dubai is now offering free access to everyone on weekdays. From 10am until 1pm from Sunday until Thursday you can laze around for free.
Free. Sun-Thu 10am-1pm. Palazzo Versace Dubai, Al Jaddaf (04 556 8805).

Take a trip to Abu Dhabi
See a range of exhibitions, pop-up installations, in-store activations and window-displays from the world’s most admired French luxury house in Abu Dhabi at Flânerie Colbert Abu Dhabi, a showcase by Comité Colbert of French luxury, culture and art in partnership with the Department of Culture and Tourism – Abu Dhabi. It’s being held at The Galleria Al Maryah Islands
Nov 12-Dec 14. The Galleria Al Maryah Island, Abu Dhabi, www.thegalleria.ae.

Four hands dinner at Ronda Locatelli
What’s better than a standout dinner made by a Michelin-starred chef and MasterChef judge? One also made with a winner of the MasterChef Italia 2019. For two nights, Giorgio Locatelli and chef Vale-ria Raciti will host a five-course “Four Hands Dinner”. Expect dishes including pot-roasted octopus, beef tartare and more.
Dhs290. Nov 10-11, 6pm-10.30pm. Atlantis The Palm, Palm Jumeirah (04 426 2626).

Enjoy free drinks with an Asian dinner
Ladies, head to Karma Kafé to enjoy three free drinks every Tuesday from 6pm until 1am. Peckish? You can also choose between either two courses for Dhs159 or three for Dhs189.
Tue 6pm-1am. Souk Al Bahar, Downtown Dubai (04 423 0909).
