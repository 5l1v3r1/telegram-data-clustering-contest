'Bigger for them' - Raging Dean Smith rips into Aston Villa players after Wolves defeat

Wolverhampton Wanderers 2-1 Aston Villa reaction | The claret and blue boss didn't hold back when he was interviewed about his side's display in the defeat at Molineux

10 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Ashley Preece, https://www.facebook.com/PreeceBirminghamLive/

Raging Dean Smith boss let rip at his awe-struck Aston Villa players during half-time at Wolves, blasting "That’s as bad a first 45 minutes I’ve had since Wigan away last year."

Villa were well-beaten by their West Midlands rivals at Molineux and were fortunate to go in at the break just 1-0 down.

Speaking afterwards, Smith spoke to BirminghamLive about his team's dour display, and here's part of the transcript from the press room.

What annoyed you most about today, Dean?

"The first 45 minutes," he said.

 

You didn’t move an inch in the first 45 minutes...

"A bit like some of my players as well today in the first 45 minutes," he added.

"That’s as bad a first 45 minutes I’ve had since Wigan away last year. Very poor, didn’t start.

"It appeared the game was bigger for them than it was for us, we didn’t win any balls, duels, didn’t pass it well. The best thing I got out of the first half was it was only 1-0 at half-time.”

What did you tell them at half-time?

"It’s something probably not for you to print!" he said.

"Listen, we’ve got to go and compete, it doesn’t matter what game you’re playing.

"People aren't just going to let you pass the ball and I thought they put us under pressure and we didn't cope with the pressure they put us under today in the first 45 minutes. You can't give anyone a 45-minute head start, especially in the Premier League."

Ruben Neves and Raul Jimenez scored for Wolves, with Trezeguet replying a late consolation for the hosts.
