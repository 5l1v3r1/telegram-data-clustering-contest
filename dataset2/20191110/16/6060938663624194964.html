Ayushmann Khurrana expresses his gratitude towards the audience for the success of 'Bala'

10 Nov 2019, 16:33 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Continuing his dream run at the box office, Ayushmann Khurrana's latest offering 'Bala' has turned out to be his seventh straight hit in succession. The film which sees the actor playing a prematurely balding guy has been received well at the box office. Positive reviews and critical appreciation have been flowing in from all the corners for the film.

Just a while ago, Ayushmann took to his social media handles to express his gratitude for all the love and appreciation showered upon the film by the audience. Sharing his picture from the Ghats of Banaras, the actor wrote, "#Bala ki tasveer aapne banaayi. #Bala ki taqdeer aapne banaayi. üôèüèª #gratitude"

Directed by Amar Kaushik, 'Bala' is a film set in Kanpur that revolves around a man suffering from premature balding and the lack of confidence and societal pressure which runs with it.

The film also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles. 'Bala' hit the screens on November 8.
