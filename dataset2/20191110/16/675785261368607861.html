Michelle Visage's secret battle as disease from breast implants caused hair loss and panic attacks

The Strictly contestant has opened up about an illness she battled for years due to breast implants she had at 21

10 Nov 2019, 16:27 by Tiffany Wallis

Strictly contestant Michelle Visage has got candid about her secret battle with a rare disease.

The reality star, 51, opened up about the heartbreaking side effects caused by a rare disease she struggled with in secret for years due to her old breast implants.

Following a breast augmentation at 21-years-old, Michelle was diagnosed with the rare Hashimoto's disease which can cause weight gain and depression.

The auto immune disease, which attacks the thyroid gland, left the Drag Race judge with unexplainable symptoms so she was forced to carry out her own research.

She said: "My skin was crackly dry and my hair was falling out in clumps. I was also having hardcore panic attacks.

"My journey goes back to me getting breast implants at the age of 21. My body couldn't fight off the attacker because it was there 24 hours a day, seven days a week."

The telly star battled the secret health problem for decades which led to her hair falling out in clumps and her suffering with crippling panic attacks.

The Strictly star had the implants removed 30 years after getting them done and has since seen improvements.

She said: "Since having them removed, the thyroid nodule is going down, which is amazing."

The star also revealed how she discovered over 50,000 women with similar issues from their breast implants on Facebook .

The New Jersey native faced the gruelling 20 year battle with the disease alone, and even feared it was cancer at one point.

She recently told OK! magazine: "It's not cancer, thank God.

"I got the report that it was benign the day I was coming to England for Strictly."

The flamboyant star is currently competing hit BBC dance competition Strictly Come Dancing alongside her professional partner Giovanni Pernice.

The judge on RuPaul's Drag Race was the first contestant to reach a lofty score of 39 when she performed the foxtrot on Halloween .

In last weekend's show the couple once again flew close to the top of the leaderboard when they netted 34 for their stunning Paso Doble.

In last night's show , Michelle and Giovanni danced the American Smooth to I Just Want To Make Love To You by Etta James and received a standing ovation.
