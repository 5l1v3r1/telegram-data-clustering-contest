Michelle Keegan scoffs a McDonald's takeaway as she enjoys indulgent cheat night

Michelle showed she was just like one of us when she enjoyed a cheeky cheat night McDonald's over the weekend

10 Nov 2019, 22:52 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Even Michelle Keegan enjoys a McDonald's takeaway every now and again.

And the Our Girl beauty, 32, tucked into her late night treat on her cheat day and she broadcast her fast food cravings to her 4.1million followers on Sunday.

In the snap, the Corrie star curled up in the back of the car as she clutched her McDonald's close to her chest.

The Brassic star was holding not one but two big brown paper bags from the fast food chain.

Set out next to her were three sour cream dips to add extra flavour.

Michelle washed it down with a cold beverage which she was holding while she posed for the cheeky picture.

She wrote in the caption: "Keep what you love close, and your filet-o-fish even closer."

Michelle was once voted the TV star with the best body in 2014.

The former Coronation Street star beat curvy beauties Kim Kardashian and Beyonce to the hot trophy in 2014.

So it may surprise fans the actress often enjoys a Chinese takeaway on a Saturday night with her husband Mark Wright.

As well as her once-a-week cheat night, the brunette bombshell confessed one of her favourite food groups is cheese.

She previously said: “One of my favourite foods is cheese and I love having a Chinese on a Saturday night.

“I think it is healthy to have a relaxed approach and balanced outlook.”
