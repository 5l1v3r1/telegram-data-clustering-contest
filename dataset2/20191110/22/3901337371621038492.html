Bafana Star Nets Brace In France, Tau Returns For Brugge

10 Nov 2019, 22:38 by Soccer Laduma

France

Lebogang Mothiba ended his goal drought for Strasbourg, who romped to a 4-1 win over Amiens on Saturday.

The Bafana Bafana striker played a starring role after netting a brace as Strasbourg ran riot to get back to winning ways.

In Ligue 2, Lebogang Phiri played the full 90 minutes as Guingamp played to a 1-1 draw with Ajaccio on Saturday night.

England

Kamohelo Moktojo inspired Brentford to a 3-1 victory over Wigan Athletic in the Championship on Saturday afternoon.

Mokotjo opened his account for the season after scoring in the 70th minute, before he bagged the assist for Brentford's third goal.

Netherlands

Lars Veldwijk netted his fourth league of the season as Sparta Rotterdam suffered a 2-1 loss to Heerenveen on Saturday afternoon.

Belgium

Percy Tau returned to the Club Brugge starting line-up in a 2-1 defeat to Royal Antwerp on Sunday afternoon.

The Bafana attacker played 55 minutes and bagged the assist for Brugge's only goal on the day.

Lyle Foster played the full 90 minutes as Cercle Brugge claimed the first win of the season with 2-1 triumph over Sint-Trudien on Saturday night.

Portugal

Luther Singh and Thibang Phete went head-to-head on Saturday as Famalicao and Moreirense played to a 3-3 draw in the Portuguese top flight.

Singh played the full 90 minutes for Moreirense, while Phete was introduced in the 70th minute for the hosts.
