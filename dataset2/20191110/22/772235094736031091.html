2019-20 Holden Astra, Commodore recalled

An issue with the crossmember on the base of the rear seat frame has forced 131 cars back to the dealer.

10 Nov 2019, 22:40 by Scott Collie

- shares

Holden has recalled the 2019-20 Commodore and Astra, over concerns about a problem with the rear seat crossmember.

According to the company, the rear seat crossmember could have been improperly welded to the seat frame. If a car with the issue is involved in a rear-end accident, there's an increased risk passengers will be injured.
