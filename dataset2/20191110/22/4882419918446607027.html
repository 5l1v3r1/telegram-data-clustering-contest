Sevilla earned hard-fought win over Real Betis 1-2 at Benito Villamarin

After this win, Julen Lopetegui's squad has 24 points from 13 games

10 Nov 2019, 22:14 by SPORT.es

Sevilla sways pass Betis 1-2 on Sunday at Benito Villamarin. The two teams arrived at this challenge after drawing their last game. Real Betis faced the encounter with a desire to add more points to their locker after a draw against Real Madrid (0-0) while Sevilla scored a 1 draw against Atlético de Madrid, adding a point in the last match played of the competition. As of today, Real Betis is 17th in the LaLiga, with 13 points from 13 matches, while Sevilla sits at 4th with 24 points after playing a total of 13 matches.

The first 45 minutes was in favour of Sevilla, thanks to a goal from Lucas Ocampos in the 13th minute. However, Real Betis just before the end of the 45 minutes found an equaliser thanks to a goal from Loren Morón in the 45th minute, thereby seeing the first half to a 1-1.

The second half started favourably for Sevilla, thanks to a goal from Luuk de Jong 55 minutes in the game, finalising the game with a 1-2.

At the end of the match, both teams made 3 substitutions each. For Betis, Joaquín, Cristian Tello and Borja Iglesias came on for Zouhair Feddal, Marc Bartra and Andrés Guardado, while Sevilla brought on Franco Vazquez, Nemanja Gudelj and Joan Jordan to replace Oliver Torres, Nolito and Éver Banega.

The referee booked Zouhair Feddal and Emerson from Betis, while for Sevilla Oliver Torres, Lucas Ocampos and Fernando saw a yellow card as well.

Real Betis will be playing against Valencia at home stadium, while Sevilla will face Real Valladolid at away stadium.
