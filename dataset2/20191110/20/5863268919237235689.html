Nawaz Sharif to leave for London on Monday: Sources

10 Nov 2019, 20:13 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) - Former premier and PML-N supremo Nawaz Sharif is expected to travel to London for his medical treatment.

Sources said Nawaz will travel to London on Monday at 9:05 AM through Qatar Airways flight QR629.

Sources further said opposition leader Shehbaz Sharif and personnel physician of Nawaz Sahrif Dr Adnan would also be on board to London.

Arrangements for Nawaz Sharif’s medical treatment have already been made by his son Hussain Nawaz in London.

It is worth mentioning here that federal government on Friday decided to remove former prime minister Nawaz Sharif’s name from the Exit Control List (ECL) following his deteriorating health.
