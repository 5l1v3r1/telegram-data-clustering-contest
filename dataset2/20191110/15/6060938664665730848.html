Akshay Kumar’s to star in upcoming spy thriller 'Bell-Bottom'; to hit the screens on January 2021

10 Nov 2019, 15:49 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

With back-to-back hits at the box office, Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar is all set to collaborate with Vashu Bhagnani for the very first time for their next project titled 'Bell-Bottom'.

Directed by Ranjit M Tewari, 'Bell-Bottom' revolves around one of India’s forgotten heroes. The film will go on floors mid next year and will release on 22nd January 2021.

The first look poster of Akshay Kumar from the film has been unveiled. Akshay features in a mind-blowing avatar in the poster.

The story inspired by true events is written by Aseem Arora & Pervez Shaikhand and is touted to be a very high-end espionage thriller. Akshay Kumar will be portraying a never seen before avatar and the makers are riding high on the content.

The film is produced by Vashu Bhagnani, Jackky Bhagnani, Deepshikha Deshmukh in association with Monisha Advani, Madhu Bhojwani and Nikhil Advani. 'Bell-Bottom' is slated to release on 22nd January 2021.
