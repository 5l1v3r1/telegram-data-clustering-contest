Steven Gerrard gives curt Gary Holt response before Rangers face Livingston

The Ibrox boss didn't seem entirely happy with his counterpart in wake of his stinging comments in the Sunday Mail.

10 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Jonathan McFarlane

Steven Gerrard has issued a stark riposte to furious comments from Gary Holt accusing the Ibrox boss of "naivety" before today's match with Livingston.

Speaking to sister title The Sunday Mail , Holt blasted back at Gerrard for highlighting the need for protection from referee Nick Walsh in today's Premiership clash.

The last game between the two clubs in West Lothian was marred by a collision between Ricki Lamie and Joe Aribo that left the silky Nigerian international with 20 stitches.
