Bobrisky Refuses To Follow His ‘Bestie’, As Tonto Dike Gets Deported From Dubai

16 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

Tonto Dikeh has been deported from Dubai after she got into a fight with a security guard because she was denied entry at an event centre in Dubai.

Naija News understands that Tonto Dikeh arrived at the event alongside Bobrisky and the security guard refused to grant her entrance.

Tonto Dikeh who was apparently drunk attacked the guard and until Dubai police arrested her

She was then deported from Dubai and is reportedly on her way to Nigeria as we speak.

Naija News also understands that Bobrisky has refused to follow her bestie back to Nigeria.
