Get free entry or special offers at these UK attractions by showing a National Lottery ticket

The National Lottery is saying #ThanksToYou for the around £30million players raise for good causes every week as part of its 25th birthday celebrations

16 Nov 2019 by Dave Masters, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/dave-masters/

Your National Lottery ticket will get you free entry or special offers at National Lottery-funded venues across the UK between November 23 to December 1.

Hundreds of National Lottery-funded attractions are throwing open their doors or releasing special offers to say #ThanksToYou to players who contribute around £30million to good causes every week.

And with so many popular venues taking part, there really is something for everybody - no matter where you live.

Among those joining in the celebrations are the Eden Project in Cornwall (below) which is offering visitors free entry (max two adults, two kids), while Derby Arena is inviting guests to go for a spin on its velodrome cycle track.

Free medieval wall walks are taking place at Lincoln Castle (also below) where you can explore the towers and dungeons, or you can get discounted tickets to see A Christmas Carol at London's famous Old Vic theatre.

Other popular venues across the country also running special promotions to celebrate The National Lottery's 25th birthday include the popular V&A Dundee, iconic Murrayfield Stadium, historic Cardiff Castle and The Giant's Causeway, which is one of many selected National Trust properties taking part.

 

The fantastic offer is part of The National Lottery's 25th birthday and celebrates the incredible difference The National Lottery has made – and continues to make – to the lives of people and communities throughout the whole of the UK.

By simply showing your National Lottery ticket you can unlock exclusive deals including free entry, behind-the-scenes tours or discounted offers.

Since the first National Lottery draw 25 years ago, thousands of millionaires (more than 5,500 to date) have been created. National Lottery players have also raised an amazing £40billion for good causes all over the UK, an average of 200 grants in every UK postcode district.

We've listed some of the amazing venues taking part but there are hundreds of venues waiting to welcome players with their National Lottery tickets - simply use our finder tool to locate your nearest!

To qualify for the promotion, all you have to do is bring along a ticket to any National Lottery game - such as Lotto, Euromillions, Thunderball, Hot Picks, Set for Life, Instant Win Games and Scratchcards.

You'll then be able to gain free entry or special offers at hundreds of fantastic National Lottery-funded venues and attractions across the UK during those nine days.
