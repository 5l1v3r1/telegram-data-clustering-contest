EDD: Inland Empire employment surpasses 2-million

16 Nov 2019

INLAND EMPIRE  (INT)  For the first time, employment across Riverside and San Bernardino counties has eclipsed 2-million.

Over the past 12-months, employment rose 2.1 percent with the addition of 31,900 jobs. The State Employment Development Department (EDD) said Roughly 98 percent of the job growth was concentrated in healthcare and social assistance.

Six other sectors reported year-over job gains, but the most noteworthy came from government, trade, transportation, and utilities, leisure and hospitality and professional and business services.

Octobers jobless rate was 3.7 percent reflecting some of the lowest rates in the past 40-years.
