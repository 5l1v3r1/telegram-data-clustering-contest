Breaking: Yahaya Bello Defeats Natasha Akpoti In Her Polling Unit

16 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

As the results of the 2019 Kogi State governorship election held on Saturday trickles in, Naija News has learnt of the result from PU 019, Ward 01, Obeiba/Ihima, Okehi LG.

The polling unit is the unit of Natasha Akpoti, governorship candidate of the Social Democratic Party (SDP).

Incumbent and candidate of the All Progressives Congress, Yahaya Bello, defeated Akpoti at her polling unit by two votes.

Below is the result:

APC: 78

SDP: 76
