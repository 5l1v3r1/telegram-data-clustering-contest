Barca following young Dutch talent

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Calvin Stengs has caught the attention of Barcelona. The winger is 20 years old and is playing for AZ Alkmaar. According to 'Mundo Deportivo', Barcelona will send a scout to see him live in Holland's matches during this international break.

Calvin Stengs is not short of talent. In fact, he has been called up by Holland. And Barca want to take advantage to see him up close. 

'Mundo Deportivo' are saying that Barcelona will send scouts to Holland's matches. They do not want to miss any details of Calvin Stengs' play.

His numbers have caught the attention of Barca. So far this season, he has played 23 matches for AZ Alkmaar, scored nine goals and given eight assists. 

Koeman, the Holland coach, has included him in the squad for the matches against Northern Ireland and Estonia. Barca are preparing to send someone to keep an eye on Calvin Stengs. 

The player has shown has already shown his talent for Holland U21s. We will have to see whether Koeman opts to give him game time. Barcelona are praying he does.
