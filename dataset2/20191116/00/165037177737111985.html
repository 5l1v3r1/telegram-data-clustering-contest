It will be Deschamps 100th game in charge against Albania!

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The France national team coach, Didier Deschamps, will manage his 100th game in charge of 'Les Bleus' this Sunday, against Albania. The coach arrived in 2012 and six years later led France to become World Champions!

When he was put in charge of France, few could imagine that Didier Deschamps would make history leading his side, but the coach, with more national team games (99 at the moment, 100 against Albania) managed to make history.

His record as 'Les Bleus' coach is truly remarkable, with 64 wins, 18 draws and 17 defeats. Their goal difference is +111 (194 in favour and 83 against). Moreover, the team have also managed to get 44 clean sheets.
