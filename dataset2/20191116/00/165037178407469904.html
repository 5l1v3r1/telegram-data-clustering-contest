Video of Valverde's anger at Anfield comes to light

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

There are matches which sometimes hurt badly. Liverpool-Barcelona in the Champions League last season was one of them. The Reds overcame a three goal deficit to knock Barca out. Six months later, a video showing Valverde's reaction to the tough defeat at Anfield has come to light.

Only a few seconds of footage have been released, but it is enough to see how angry the Barca coach was at being left out of the Champions League. 

In the images, which will be broadcast on 'MATCHDAY', a series on 'Rakuten TV', Valverde can be seen throwing his jacket. It was an image showing his helplessness.

You can also see the players looking extremely down. Coutinho was covering his face, Busquets gazing into space...

On the other hand, the coaching staff were trying to cheer up the footballers. A night which Barcelona will not forget easily. 

In this series on 'Rakuten TV', the most intimate moments of the Barca dressing room have still not come to light. In the six seconds which 'Sport' have chosen, we can see the rollercoaster of emotions which are seen on the field of play.
