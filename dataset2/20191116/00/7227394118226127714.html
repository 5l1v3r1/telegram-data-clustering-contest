Huawei Mate 20 Pro receiving Android 10-based EMUI 10 in Europe

16 Nov 2019 by Sagar

Huawei released EMUI 10 for the P30 series in Europe a couple of days ago, and now it's seeding the update for the Mate 20 Pro as well in the Old Continent.

The EMUI 10 update is based on Android 10 and sports version number 10.0.0.154(C432E7R1P5). We got a tip it's currently seeding in Bosnia and Herzegovina but it'll likely expand to other countries soon.

Now that Huawei has rolled out EMUI 10 for the Mate 20 Pro, updates for the vanilla Mate 20, Mate 20 X, Mate 20 lite, and the Mate 20 RS Porsche Design edition shouldn't take too long to arrive.

If you've already received the EMUI 10 update on any of the aforementioned smartphones, let us know by dropping a comment below.
