Live Updates: 2019 Kogi Governorship Election Results

16 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

Voting ended officially by 2:00PM in the 2019 governorship election which held in Kogi State on Saturday, November 16.

As earlier reported by Naija News, there was a large turn out of voters across various poling units in the state to determine who will lead the state for the next four years.

The registered voters in the state are 1,646,350, about 1,485,828 (90.2%) collected their PVCs to participate in the election across various polling units in the 21 local governments of the state.

The governorship election in the state was keenly contested by a total of 24 candidates from different political parties.

Naija News will be on ground to give you the latest information as the results come in from the various polling units. Keep refreshing the page to get the latest updates.

However, please note the results below are not final as the official and final valid results can only be released by the Independent National Electoral Commission, (INEC), the only body constitutionally recognized to declare election results in Nigeria.
