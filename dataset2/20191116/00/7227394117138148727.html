Realme 5s will be powered by the Snapdragon 665 SoC

16 Nov 2019 by Sagar

The Realme 5s arriving on November 20 will be sold through Flipkart, and the Indian e-commerce giant has been drip-feeding us information about the device for the past few days.

Flipkart has already confirmed that Realme 5s will come with a 6.51" HD display, 48MP quad camera, and a 5,000 mAh battery. Now it has confirmed that 5s will be powered by the Snapdragon 665 SoC - the same chip that powers the Realme 5.

The image that Flipkart included on the landing page of the Realme 5s says the phone will have a Snapdragon 655 SoC under the hood, but that's a typo since the video embedded on the same page confirms it's the Snapdragon 665 SoC while also showing off the phone in three colors - Blue, Purple, and Red.
