Bayelsa: PDP Deputy Governorship Candidate Cast Vote

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The Deputy Governorship candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Senator Lawrence Erujakpo, has cast his vote in the Bayelsa Guber election.

Naija News reports that Ofoni community is the home town of Erujakpo.

Recall that the Governorship candidate of the PDP, Duoye Diri, is yet to cast his vote.

There are eight polling units in his Sampou/Kalama ward 6. But neither materials for the poll or the INEC officials have arrived.
