Lloris set to return in January

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Hugo Lloris injured his shoulder in a match against Brighton. Since then, he has been recovering. The British press affirm that it is his idea to return in January.

Lloris last played for Tottenham on 5th October. In a passage of play, Lloris gifted the ball to the opposition. But this wasn't the worst of it: he also got severely injured.

Lloris had to go to surgery because of a dislocated elbow. He is currently recovering from this.
