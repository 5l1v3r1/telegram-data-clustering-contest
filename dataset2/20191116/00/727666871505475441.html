Rapper Lil Reese Is Out Of The Hospital After Being Shot In The Neck

16 Nov 2019, 00:36 by AllHipHop Staff

Chicago rapper Lil Reese has been released from a local hospital after being targeted in a shooting in broad daylight.

(AllHipHop News) Chicago rapper Lil Reese popped up on Instagram Live and is apparently out of the hospital after being shot earlier this week.

Lil Reese, real name Tavares Taylor, was targeted in a shooting on November 11th in a Suburb of Chicago.

The rapper was reportedly struck in the neck and taken to a local hospital to be treated for a gunshot wound.

Earlier today (November 15), Reese took to social media but he remained silent on the shooting.

Although he kept his neck area covered, the rapper looked rather healthy after such a harrowing incident.
