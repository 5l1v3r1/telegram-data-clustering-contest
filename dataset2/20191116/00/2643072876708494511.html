Just In: APC Reveals Fresh PDP Plot In Bayelsa

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The All Progressives Congress (APC) has accused leaders of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) of plotting to frustrate collation of results in Bayelsa State.

The APC made the accusation in a statement on Saturday by the Vice-Chairman, Media, APC Campaign Council, Julius Bokoru.

Bokoru, in his statement, alerted security agencies especially the military and the police of an alleged plot by the PDP to unleash violence to subvert the will of the people in Bayelsa.

He said the PDP plan to attack APC strongholds and attack collation officers to stop the results from getting to the collation centres.

According to Bokoru, “We are monitoring what they are doing and we will continue to expose the evil plot of the PDP against the electoral process.

“We have most of the unit results and the people of Bayelsa have spoken and their wish must be respected.

“We are calling on security agencies to be vigilant and stay at alert so as not to be ambushed by unscrupulous and criminal elements hired by the PDP.

“We specifically called on them to pay attention to areas Governor Seriake Dickson has been complaining about.

“We can’t help the governor’s frustration because he is aware before now that the people of Bayelsa are willing and ever determined to end the misgovernance of this state.”
