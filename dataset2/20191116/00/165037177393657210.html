Conte sent a bullet through the post

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Inter Milan coach is receiving threats through the post and has alerted police. The latest was a bullet inside an envelope.

Inter Milan have only lost one and drawn one of the last 12 matches they have played so far in Serie A. They are currently just a point behind Juventus. However, some fans are not happy. 

According to 'Corriere della Sera', Antonio Conte has had to get in contact with the police and Inter Milan himself to inform them he is receiving threatening messages via the post.

The latest thing which he has received is apparently a letter containing messages and a bullet inside. That has alerted the coach who lives in Milan. 

The cited media outlet has indicated that security has ben stepped up outside Antonio Conte's house and that this is being investigated to see where the threat is coming from. 

No major measures have been taken so far, but that could change in the coming days.
