The moment Messi told Tite to shut up

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Brazil manager, Tite, recognised in the press room that Leo Messi told him to shut up. The television cameras captured the exact moment that this tense exchange happened.

Argentina won the 'Superclásico' friendly thanks to a Messi goal, who broke the deadlock after taking advantage of the rebound after Alisson's save.

Messi suffered a lot because of the hard tackles he received, but his actions didn't stop there. He was involved in a tense exchange with Tite during the match.
