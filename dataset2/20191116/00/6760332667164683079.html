Thala Ajith honoured in Vijay Sethupathi's Sanga Thamizhan! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

16 Nov 2019

Actor Vijay Sethupathi's Sanga Thamizhan directed by Vaalu and Sketch director Vijay Chandar, and released by Libra Productions had released yesterday night, after initial issues and now the movie is running successfully, with favorable reviews.

Sanga Thamizhan is produced by Vijaya Productions, one of the popular and oldest production houses of South cinema, and have made successful movies with MGR, Superstar Rajnikanth, Kamal Haasan and NTR, and in their return to production, made movies with Ajith, Vijay and Dhanush.

Now, the reputed production house has honoured Ajith, by placing his visuals from Veeram, in their production house logo clip, that previously had MGR from Enga Veettu Pillai and Superstar Rajnikanth from Uzhaippaali, and this has made Thala Ajith fans happy.
