Egypt to face South Africa at U-23 AFCON semis

16 Nov 2019 by Egypt Today staff

CAIRO – 16 November 2019: South Africa secured a goalless draw against the defending champions, Nigeria, to qualify to the semi-finals as group runner-up.

The Bafana Bafana will challenge the host nation, Egypt, for a spot at the final and a direct ticket to the Olympic Games in Tokyo.

The Egyptian team aims to avoid the agony of the first team who suffered early elimination from 2019 AFCON after losing to South Africa 1-0 at Cairo International Stadium.

Egypt defeated Cameroon 2-1 on Thursday night thanks to two goals from the tournament’s goal scorer Mostafa Mohamed to finish the group stage with 9 points from three straight wins to secure the top of the group.

Egyptian fans are expected to pack the stands of Cairo Stadium to support their players who produced strong performances at the group stage.

On the other hand, Ivory Coast defeated Zambia 1-0 to finish group B on top after gaining six points and they will face Ghana at the semis.
