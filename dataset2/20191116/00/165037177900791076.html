"We have faith in the team and they'll win us more titles"

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

The Real Madrid president, Florentino Perez, gave a speech prior to the handing out of badges to members who have been linked to the club for 25, 50 or 60 years. The president called for calm with Zinedine Zidane's team.

Real Madrid have handed out this Saturday morning more than 2400 silver badges, 337 gold ones and 127 gold and diamond ones. People like Butragueño, Amancio, Buyo, Hierro, Roberto Carlos, Solari and Arbeloa were present at the ceremony. 

In the president's speech, Florentino Perez went over the achievements of the club's football and basketball teams, mentioning all the changes that there have been during the past transfer market and with Zidane's return to the club. 

"Values like teamwork, humbleness and never giving up are signs of Real Madrid's identity. We have gone through one of the best phases at our club, we have enjoyed a winning cycle both in football and basketball. We are in a process of transformaton which will lead us to more titles. We have brought in new players with talent, with very great players and young talents, We have faith in them," Florentino said.
