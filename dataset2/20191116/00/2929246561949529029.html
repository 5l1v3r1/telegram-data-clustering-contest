Asad Shafiq warms up with back-to-back hundreds

Aus v Pak, news

16 Nov 2019

Asad Shafiq led another strong Pakistani batting performance in the second practice match on their tour of Australia on Friday, 15 November.

Shafiq followed up his century from the first match with another hundred against Cricket Australia XI , finishing the first day of the two-day game at WACA, Perth, unbeaten on 101. 

Shan Masood (76), Babar Azam (63) and Kashif Bhatti (56*) brought up half-centuries, while Imam-ul-Haq was also among the runs. The Pakistanis finished the day on 386/7, having scored especially briskly in the last hour.

Among the eight bowlers used from an inexperienced line-up for the hosts, leg-spinner Lloyd Pope was the standout, picking up five wickets.
