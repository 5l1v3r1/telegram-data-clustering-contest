Company of Heroes 2 is Free on Steam to Keep Forever

16 Nov 2019 by Marco Cocomello

Skipping Pokemon Sword and Shield and don’t feel like buying Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order? Well, how about a free game then? Company of Heroes 2 is currently free on Steam for a limited time starting last night and ending on Monday 17 November 2019. Once you have added this game to your library, you will own it forever and it won’t disappear out of your game’s library.

It just so happens that Steam is having a huge sale on the Company of Heroes 2 DLC so if you fancy yourself an RTS fan, then this could be something great to invest in. All the DLC is currently 75% or you can purchase the entire pack for just R156 which includes 7 DLC packs. The original game, however, does include the single-player campaign as well as the multiplayer armies.

Head over to Steam and add this free game to your collection before the offer expires;

About Company of Heroes 2

Powered by the Essence Engine 3.0, the Company of Heroes 2 series feature some unique mechanics rewarding thoughtful players. From the TrueSight™ system that emulates the units’ line of sight to the cover-system that encourages clever unit placement – not to mention the combined arms approach and the hard and soft counters gameplay that will make you think twice before trying to destroy a tank with a simple squad of riflemen – each game presents players with an uninterrupted stream of meaningful tactical choices that can turn the tide of war.
