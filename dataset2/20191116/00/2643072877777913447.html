Fake Policeman Arrested In Kogi State

16 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

The Nigerian Police Force at the Igala Unity Square area in Anyigba, Dekina local government area of Kogi State has arrested a fake police officer.

Naija News learnt that the fake policeman was dressed in black T-shirt and black trousers with a black jacket with SARS inscribed on it.

He was stopped by a team of actual Special Anti Robbery Squad officers who were on patrol in the area and upon inquiry, it was realized he was not a policeman as he claimed to be and was immediately arrested and taken away in the bus.
