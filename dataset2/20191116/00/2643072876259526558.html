Bayelsa Election: APC Shocks PDP, Wins Big In Yenagoa (Full Results)

16 Nov 2019 by Philips Sunday

The All Progressives Congress (APC), Nigeria’s ruling party is currently taking a massive lead in Yenagoa against the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Nigeria’s main opposition party, in the ongoing Bayelsa governorship election.

Naija News had reported earlier that the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC), commences the election at 8:00 am amidst tight security.

David Lyon of the APC and Douye Diri of the PDP are the main contenders in the Bayelsa election which has been marred by violence.

A peep into the results trickling in form Yenagoa shows that the APC which is the opposition party in the state, is currently winning in the Yenagoa Local Government Area.
