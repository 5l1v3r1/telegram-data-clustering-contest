How to Watch UFC Fight Night 164

16 Nov 2019 by Cole Shelton

Sign up for ESPN+ right here, and you can then stream UFC on ESPN+ live on your computer, phone, tablet or streaming device via the ESPN app.

The Ultimate Fighting Championship will journey to Sao Paulo, Brazil, this Saturday, Nov. 16 for UFC Fight Night 164.

In the main event, Jan Blachowicz will battle Ronaldo Souza, who is making his light heavyweight debut in the UFC. Blachowicz is looking to extend his winning streak, while “Jacare” is will atempt to get back into the win column. The three-round co-main attraction pairs Mauricio Rua against Paul Craig, who took this fight on short notice.

UFC Fight Night 164 streams live on ESPN+ with the main card at 8 p.m. ET/5 p.m. PT. The prelims also will stream on ESPN+ at 5 p.m. ET/2 p.m. PT.

205: Mauricio Rua vs. Paul Craig
