"In the future I will return to England"

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Rafa Benitez isn't hiding his desire to return to the Premier League. Benitez wants to return to England because he wants to be close to his family and because it is a league that he likes a lot".

He is currently the manager of Dalian Yifang in the Chinese Super League and despite being happy at the club, he is clear that his future lies in the Premier League again.

During a chat with 'BBC', Benitez made known his intentions. "I will return to England". The ex Inter and Madrid manager isn't hiding it, and also declares himself a "follower of the Premier League".

Finally, the Spanish manager has not overlooked some of the peculiarities of Chinese football. He said he had to use ping pong as a metaphor to "show the players how to kick the ball".
