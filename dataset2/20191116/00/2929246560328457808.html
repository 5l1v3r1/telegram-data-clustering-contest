Langer wants 'battle-hardened' Australia to beat India in India in 2022

Australia news

16 Nov 2019

Australia coach Justin Langer is aiming to get his side "battle hardened" and "ready" to knock India off their perch when his side tours the subcontinent in 2022.

India are the No.1 side on the the MRF Tyres ICC Test Team Rankings, and are enjoying a run of 11 undefeated home series in a row. South Africa were the last team to be swept aside by Virat Kohli's men, outplayed in all areas in a 3-0 defeat.

Beating India in home conditions has become one of the toughest tasks in world cricket, and Langer, who had stated his desire to do so soon after being appointed to the job last year, still has his mind set on the challenge.
