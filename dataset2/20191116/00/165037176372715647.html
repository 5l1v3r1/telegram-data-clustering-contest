Tottenham's January sales: Lo Celso!

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

According to 'Mundo Deportivo', Tottenham could make Giovani Lo Celso a permanent signing for a price less than what they agreed in the summer with Betis.

January sales for Tottenham? This is what Spurs want, to sign Lo Celso on a permanent deal, a player who is currently in their youth team and on loan from Benito Villamarin.

Both sides agreed that the Argentinian would play in London this year, on loan for 16 million. And, at the end of it, and if Spurs were put in Champions League, the player would become part of the English team in exchange for 40 million pounds.

However, 'Mundo Deportivo' assures that this amount would fall to 32 (plus variables) in case the purchase occurs in the winter market.
