Kogi: Akpoti Decries Irregularities Of Electoral Process As She Cast Her Votes

16 Nov 2019 by Gbenga Sijuade

The Social Democratic Party (SDP) Governorship candidate, Natasha Akpoti has lamented the level of irregularity in the electoral process as she cast her votes Ogbeiba 1 Ward, Adanos house 2, 012, Okehi Local Government of the state.

Naija News understands that Mrs. Akpoti questioned the INEC staff about the incident that occurred before her arrival at the pooling unit and her knowledge about the electoral process.

However, there are alleged reports that agents of the All Progressive Congress (APC) in Natasha Akpoti’s polling unit are coercing people to vote for the party

Naija News also gathered that SDP agents were beaten up as thugs disrupt voting in Okehi, Kogi before security operatives intervened and brought the situation under control.
