Firm int'l action required against Iran: Saudi paper

16 Nov 2019 by MENA

RIYADH, Nov 16 (MENA) - A strict international action should be taken against Iran's regime in order to put an end to its hostile practices, said Saudi newspaper Al Riyadh on Saturday.

The newspaper called for toughening up sanctions imposed against Iran.

Iran's attempts to destabilize the region are crystal clear, the newspaper said in its editorial, "Falling Apart from Inside", noting that the Iranian regime believes that an unsafe region ensures its remaining in power.
