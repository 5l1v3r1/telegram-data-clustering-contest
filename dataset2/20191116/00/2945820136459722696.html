Viñales tops FP4, Marquez second but crashes

Yamaha have now led every session leading up to qualifying in Valencia

16 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Maverick Viñales (Monster Energy Yamaha MotoGP) left it late to top FP4 with a 1:30.484 to oust Marc Marquez (Repsol Honda Team) – who had a small Turn 4 crash – from P1 at the Gran Premio Motul de la Comunitat Valenciana. Free Practice 3 pacesetter Fabio Quartararo (Petronas Yamaha SRT) completes the top three ahead of qualifying.
