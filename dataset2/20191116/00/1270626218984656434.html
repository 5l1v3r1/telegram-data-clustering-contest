Trial of man who allegedly raped and sexually assaulted daughter to be in March 2021

16 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

The trial of a 62-year-old man who allegedly raped and sexually assaulted his 15-year-old daughter will be in March 2021.

He is facing four counts of rape and two counts of sexual assault.

The alleged incidents happened in Lami last year.

The man has been further remanded in custody and the case will be called on the 17th of February next year.
