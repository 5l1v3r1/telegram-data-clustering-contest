Live Update: Kogi West Senatorial District Election Results

16 Nov 2019 by Enioluwa Adeniyi

The Kogi West Senatorial Election has officially ended at 2:00 pm, November 16, 2019.

Recall that Naija News had reported that the Independent National Electoral Commission, conducted the senatorial election today, November 16, 2019.

The senatorial election which was held today is been contested by twenty-four candidate with the candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Dino Melaye and Smart Adeyemi of the All Progressives Congress leading as the major candidates.

Results have started trickling in from some polling units, stay tune with Naija News and reload this page for results from various polling units.

Note that these results are unverified, and the authentic result will be released by INEC
