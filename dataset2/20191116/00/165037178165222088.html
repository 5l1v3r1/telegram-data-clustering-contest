Spain could lose up to six points in Euro 2020 qualifying table

16 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Spain, if it stays as it is, could lose up to six points because they are in a group of six. This would make all the groups have five teams so that no one has more or less points.

The Spain side thrashed Malta and won 7-0 in which seven different goalscorers, but it could be an insignificant victory. 

Spain's points against Malta may not count if they end last in the group so that all teams are equal when it comes to seeding as calculations would be done on if there were just five teams.
