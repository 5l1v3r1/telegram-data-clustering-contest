Police search for missing 27-year-old Ilisapeci Sili

16 Nov 2019 by Naveel Krishant

Police are searching for 27-year-old Ilisapeci Sili who has been reported missing at the Totogo Police Station.

Sili was last seen attending a family function in Matata Settlement in Lami on the 25th of October.

Several attempts have been made by her family to locate her which has turned out negative.

Please call Crime Stoppers on 919 if you have information on Sili's whereabouts.
