Special Election

16 Nov 2019, 00:43 by BRIEF

SACRAMENTO, Calif. – California Gov. Gavin Newsom on Friday set a March 3 special primary election to fill former U.S. Rep. Katie Hill’s seat, following Hill’s resignation amid scandal last month. The general special election will take place May 12.
