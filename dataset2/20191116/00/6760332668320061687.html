For the first time, this top comedian acts with Vijay Sethupathi! - Tamil News - IndiaGlitz.com

16 Nov 2019

Actor Vijay Sethupathi's latest movie Sanga Thamizhan which was supposed to release yesterday, faced several issues which lead to the shows starting from yesterday night, and the movie has been receiving good response. The actor is now acting in several movies one of them being Yaadhum Oore Yaavarum Kelir

Yaadhum Oore Yaavarum Kelir is directed by debutant Venkata Krishna Roghanth, and the movie is produced by Chandaraa Arts. Yaadhum Oore Yaavarum Kelir is a music based movie, which was being shot in Kodaikanal, and is in its final stages of shooting.

It has been revealed now that the latest addition to the cast is popular comedian Vivek who is acting with Vijay Sethupathi for the first time. Yaadhum Oore Yaavarum Kelir has Megha Akash as the female lead, director Magizh Thirumeni playing the villain and Kaniha in an important role.
