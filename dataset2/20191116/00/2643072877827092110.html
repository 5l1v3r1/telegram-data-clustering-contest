Kogi/Bayelsa Polls: Nigerians Reacts As Voters Troop Out To Cast Their Votes In Bayelsa

16 Nov 2019 by Faderera Falade

It would be determined who would take over from Governor Seriake Dickson and govern Bayelsa for the next four years,

Major contenders are David Lyon of the All Progressives Congress (APC) and Senator Douye Diri of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).

Others are; Accord Party, Ebizimo Diriyai; African Action Congress (AAC), Kalango Stanley Davies; Alliance for Democracy, Owei-Tongu Woniwei; Alliance National Party (ANP), Kunde Noah.
