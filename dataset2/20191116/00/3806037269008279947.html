Jack Harlow Opens up About Performing, Wiz Khalifa & More

The latest edition of our #ConversationsWith series.

16 Nov 2019, 00:34 by Patrick Montes

In our latest #ConversationsWith episode, we sit down with the Louisville rapper Jack Harlow to talk about learning on the go from DJ Drama, Wiz Khalifa and more. Harlow also talks about feeding off live energy from a crowd, becoming a better musician through touring, finding time to have fun in the demanding music industry and more.

“That’s one of the most inspiring things — to do shows or go to shows,” Jack Harlow shares with us. “Watch how it’s moving the people, cause you know it’s interesting for me is my music has more of a bounce to it. You gotta dance to a lot of it … Me personally, I like the smoother shit.”
