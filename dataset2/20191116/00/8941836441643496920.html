New Hampshire deer hunting season gets underway in earnest

16 Nov 2019

CONCORD, N.H. (AP) - It’s the first weekend of New Hampshire’s deer hunting season for those using regular firearms, and officials are reminding participants of some of the rules.

The season started Wednesday and runs through Dec. 1 in northernmost New Hampshire, and through Dec. 8 in the rest of the state.

The state Fish and Game Department is reminding hunters to avoid urine-based lures, which can potentially spread fatal Chronic Wasting Disease to deer and moose.
