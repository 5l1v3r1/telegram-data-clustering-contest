Bayelsa: INEC Officials Yet To Arrive PDP Guber Candidate’s Ward

16 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

The Governorship candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Duoye Diri, is yet to cast his vote in the Bayelsa Guber election.

Naija News reports that Douye Diri hails from Sampou Community.

There are eight polling units in his Sampou/Kalama ward 6. But neither materials for the poll or the INEC officials have arrived.

Residents in the community have expressed concern that election is yet to commence in their neighbourhood almost two hours after polls opened.
