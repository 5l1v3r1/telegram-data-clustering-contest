Tua Tagovailoa Reportedly Transported To Hospital In A Helicopter

16 Nov 2019, 20:19 by Andrew Holleran

Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa was seen getting into an ambulance early in the second half of the Crimson Tide’s blowout win over Mississippi State. The Crimson Tide’s QB is reportedly now getting transported to the hospital in a helicopter.

Tagovailoa, who appeared to suffer a hip injury, is reportedly getting taken to St. Vincent’s Medical Center in Birmingham, Alabama.

Alabama insider Cecil Hurt has the news.
