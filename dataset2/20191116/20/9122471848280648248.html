Scott Frost Has Brutally Honest Comment After Loss To Wisconsin

16 Nov 2019, 20:47 by Chris Rosvoglou

Before Nebraska took the field this afternoon against Wisconsin, the athletic department announced a two-year extension for head coach Scott Frost. Unfortunately for the Cornhuskers, they couldn’t follow through with a win this weekend.

Nebraska needed an upset win to really help its chances of making a bowl game this year.

The Cornhuskers came out strong and matched the Badgers score for score during the first quarter. However, the defense just couldn’t stop Jonathan Taylor as the game went on.

Following the loss to Wisconsin, Frost had a brutally honest comment about the team’s season.

“I’m tired of looking for silver linings,” Frost told the media.

He also said the team needs to figure out what it’s going to take in order to win.
