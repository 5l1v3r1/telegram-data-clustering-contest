Jonathan Drouin injury update: Montreal Canadiens forward placed on IR

16 Nov 2019, 20:04 by Tommy McArdle

The Montreal Canadiens lost one of their top performers Saturday when they placed forward Jonathan Drouin on injured reserve.

The team announced that Drouin sustained an upper-body injury during the third period of Friday's 5-2 win over the Washington Capitals.

Drouin took a huge open-ice hit from Alex Ovechkin in the second period of that game,but TVA Sports' Renaud Lavoie reported Saturday that the injury is not related in any way to Ovechkin's hit — which rules out a concussion, head or neck injury.
