Michigan State Player Ejected For Dirty Hit On Michigan QB Shea Patterson

16 Nov 2019, 20:11 by Andrew Holleran

This afternoon’s Michigan-Michigan State game has been ugly and we’re not just talking about the score. Dirty plays have arrived late in Ann Arbor.

Michigan is leading Michigan State, 34-10, at the start of the fourth quarter.

It’s been a chippy contest all afternoon and we just had an ejection for a dirty play.

Michigan State defensive lineman Jacub Panasiuk was ejected for a cheap shot on quarterback Shea Patterson.
