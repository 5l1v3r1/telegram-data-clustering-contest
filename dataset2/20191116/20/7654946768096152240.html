Kanye West’s Sunday Service teams with Joel Osteen’s megachurch

16 Nov 2019, 20:43 by Sara Dorn

Christianity will get a star-powered boost from two of the religion’s most famous disciples — when Kanye West’s Sunday Service comes to Joel Osteen’s Lakewood Church.

Osteen will interview the rapper-turned-evangelist Sunday at his 17,000-seat megachurch in Houston, a church representative told the Houston Chronicle.

The interview will air live on Sirius XM at 11 a.m. West will return at 7 p.m. for a performance with his gospel choir.

The rapper began dabbling in the religious arena earlier this year with his pop-up choir performances. The “Sunday Services” feature only music, and no preaching, but have a “Christian vibe,” West’s wife, Kim Kardashian, said in an April interview with Elle magazine. 

West is currently promoting his new album, “Jesus is King.”

The flamboyant televangelist is expected to ask him about his “journey to his faith” in the 15-20 minute Q&A, Lakewood representative Donald Iloff told the Chronicle.

“Kanye and Joel are friends,” Iloff said. “He’s been wanting to come out for some time and it just worked out for this weekend.”
