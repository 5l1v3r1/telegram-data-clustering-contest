Chris Ramsey opens Strictly Come Dancing to a standing ovation as he 'unclogs his hips'

South Shields comedian Chris Ramsey got a record score - on the week when he was favourite to leave the show

16 Nov 2019, 20:09 by Lesley Oldfield

Chris Ramsey and partner Karen Hauer were the first to dance on Strictly and boy did they open the show in style!

Dressed in fuchsia pink and accompanied by eight backing dancers, they did a funky salsa to Mark Ronson & Bruno Mars' Uptown Funk. The audience were on their feet - and so were judges Bruno Toniolli and chief judge Shirley Ballas.

Bruno said: "You really have started Happy Hour across the nation. You looked like you were in a boy band. Your hips have started to unclog - the Draino has worked. Much more serpentine."

Chris tweeted afterwards: "Well, what did you think of that? Holy Moly!"

The couple scored two nines - from Shirley and Bruno, an eight from Motsi and a seven from Craig who told him: "It lacked flow but the timing wasn't bad and you fitted in with the group which is no mean feat as they are all professional dancers. You have upped your game!"

Motsi described the dance as "slick, fun and energetic" and Shirley said: "You hadn't danced in front of anyone nine weeks ago and look at you now! You brought your groove. You were all and everything I expected to start the show for Blackpool."

The bookies had earmarked Chris as the one to leave this week as the BBC1 show moved to Blackpool's Tower Ballroom. Wife Rosie chose to stay home with son Robin for a big family Strictly party at her mum's house in South Shields - and she Instagrammed the whole room screaming and jumping about in excitement at Chris's performance.

And at South Shields Customs House the show had a free screening to support the home-town man of the moment. Dame Bella's tweeted: "Massive excitement here."

Viewer Shona Tyler tweeted: "Hats off to Chris Ramsey, that was a great performance! You brought the fun to Blackpool" and Mark Jowett agreed, tweeting: "Great start from @IAmChrisRamsey and @karen_hauer . Some brilliant lead work from Chris in that Salsa as well! In time!"

After the dance, which included him getting a lift as he went head over heels, Chris described the atmosphere as "electric" and said: "It's an honour to open the show. That was amazing, I always wanted to be in a music video and I feel like I have just lived it."

In the next dance veteran Strictly star Anton Du Beke got his first 10s after 17 series for his foxtrot with celebrity partner Emma Barton.
