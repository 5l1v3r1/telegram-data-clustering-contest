Bala maintains a steady hold at the box-office

16 Nov 2019, 20:21 by Filmfare

Ayushmann Khurrana is currently riding high on the success of Bala, his latest release. Bala has been making waves at the box-office and is now his seventh consecutive hit. That is without a doubt an outstanding feat.

Talking about the film’s box-office collections, Bala managed to collect a total of Rs 10.5 crore on its opening day. A good word of mouth and overall positive reviews from critics have helped the film pick up at the box-office with total collections now amounting to Rs. 76 crores. Despite facing competition from films like Marjaavaan and Motichoor Chaknachoor, it seems like Bala is still going strong at the box-office.

Along with Ayushmann, the film also stars Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam. The film is helmed by Stree director Amar Kaushik and produced by Dinesh Vijan. It also stars Javed Jaaferi and Saurabh Shukla in supporting roles.
