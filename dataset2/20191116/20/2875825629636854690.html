Strictly fans furious over Craig's score for Karim Zeroual's Charleston

Strictly Come Dancing viewers were left bitterly disappointed when Karim's stunning performance missed out on getting a 40

16 Nov 2019, 20:43 by Irishmirror.ie

Strictly Come Dancing viewers were utterly furious this evening over Craig Revel Horwood's score for Karim Zeroual's Charleston.

The other judges were on their feet after Karim and Amy Dowden's performance, with Motsi Mabuse even revealing that she would be scoring them 10 before it was officially confirmed.

However viewers were left bitterly disappointed when Karim's stunning performance missed out on getting a 40 after being scored nine by Craig.

Motsi waved her own 10 paddle in front of Craig's and the pair scored '39'.

But angry fans flocked to Twitter in fury over Craig's score.

One tweeted: "HOW DID CRAIG NOT GIVE THAT A 10????????? #Strictly."

While another commented: "Amy’s first Charleston’s, first time at Blackpool and first 39 (IT SHOULD HAVE BEEN 40) #strictly."

A third remarked: "Oh come on Craig. #strictly."

"Craig ruining the party! #strictly," another wrote.

Another quipped: "Has craig just lost his 10 paddle and is afraid to tell anyone #strictly."
