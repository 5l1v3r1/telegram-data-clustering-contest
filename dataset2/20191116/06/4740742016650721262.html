From organ donation to holiday pay

16 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Tristan Cork

We have had a parliament in gridlock, a Government with no working majority, two Prime Ministers leading a coalition of sorts and everything stopping for Brexit - but remarkably, there are new laws coming into effect in the next few months, and into 2020.

They will affect our every day lives, and one in particular, goes to the core of the meaning of life, what happens when we're dead and what rights we have over our bodies.
