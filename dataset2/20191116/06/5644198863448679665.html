BeiGene prices lymphoma drug at US$12,935 for a 30-day supply

16 Nov 2019, 06:42

REUTERS: China-based drugmaker BeiGene Ltd on Friday priced its drug, Brukinsa, to treat a rare form of lymphoma at US$12,935 for a 30-day supply.

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration on Thursday approved the drug, giving a boost to the company's strategy of largely using data from studies held outside the United States.
