VIDEO: Sidharth Malhotra and Riteish Deshmukh mobbed by fans for selfies outside theatre during ‘Marjaavaan’ promotions

16 Nov 2019, 06:33 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sidharth Malhotra starrer ‘Marjaavaan’ hit the screens yesterday and the film's core team decided to surprise fans at a theatre in the city. Such was the craze for Sidharth and co-star Riteish Deshmukh that they were thronged by a crowd of fans eager for a selfie outside the theatre. Producer Nikkhil Advani and director Milap Zaveri had accompanied the actors to gauge live audience reaction to ‘Marjaavaan’. With the help of guards, Sidharth and Riteish managed to safely wrap the post release promotions of the film.
