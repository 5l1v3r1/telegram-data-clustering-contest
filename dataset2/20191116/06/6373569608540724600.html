The glamorous life of Plymouth influencer Jess Hunt

Jess Hunt has a whopping 616,000 followers on Instagram, and enjoys a life of travel, photoshoots and brand collaborations

16 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Jess Morcom

In a world full of Instagram many young girls may dream of getting paid to pose in their favourite outfits and jetsetting around the world.

And for Jess Hunt, that is exactly what she does.

The 22-year-old is originally from Plymouth, and is one of the most followed people to ever come from our city.

She has built up a staggering 616,000 followers on Instagram and is slowly making herself known as one of the UK's most popular influencers.

The stunning natural blonde regularly posts photos of herself chilling in some of the worlds most breathtaking locations, from Mykonos, LA, Dubai and the Bahamas.

With a figure and fashion sense to die for, Jess also frequently models for some of the biggest women's clothing brands and is often pictured on clothes websites as one of the faces of brands such as Pretty Little Thing, Oh Polly and Boohoo.

Jess also gets to hang on with celebrities at some of the coolest events in the world, including the world famous Coachella festival, which she attended earlier this year.

Many recognisable faces interact with her on social media, including former Love Island contestants, reality stars, and other well known influencers.

Most recently, Jess has posted updates from Paris, Dubai and Las Vegas, where she has been pictured during brand collaborations.

Although Plymouth Live has been unable to grab a chat with Jess as she is currently in Dubai, she quite often shares updates with fans through Q & A sessions on Instagram.

For her most recent Q & A a few months ago, she told fans her favourite skincare products, her favourite places to shop as well as hair styling tips.

"I love to travel with my favourite people, so my boyfriend, my family and my friends, I've just been away with my mum," she wrote on Instagram.

"I do miss home, and get excited to get back to my home comforts, but I also love being able to travel," she added.

In addition to being an Instagram star, Jess has also ran a Youtube channel for the past two years, where she has over 18,000 subscribers.

Here she posts reviews of beauty products, brand try on hauls, hair and makeup tutorials, and she has even posted vlogs of some of her exciting trips away, as well as 'behind the scenes' videos of her photoshoots.

Jess has definitely taken the social media and fashion world by storm throughout the past couple of years, and we are proud to be able to say she is from Plymouth.
