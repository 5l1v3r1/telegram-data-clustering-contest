Star Plus’ ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka’ to end in February 2020

16 Nov 2019, 09:48 by Shyama Sudra

Only just have launched on Star Plus last month, news has it that the fantasy series ‘Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka’ is set to go off air in February 2020.

According to IWM Buzz, the show had always been planned to have a short air time. “Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka produced by 4 Lions Films is one of the many finite series that Star Plus is looking at. Hence the show has always been slated to have a start and end,” a source was reported stating.

The show, which sees the new pairing of Vikram Singh Chauhan and Aditi Sharma, has audiences hooked to their TV screens. However it seems they’ll have to make the most of these actors until the show ends.
