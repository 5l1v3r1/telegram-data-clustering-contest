‘Bala’ box office update: Ayushmann Khurrana will soon register his third 100 crore film!

16 Nov 2019, 09:36 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushmann Khurrana starrer ‘Bala’ is unstoppable at the box office. The film is not just entertaining the audience but is writing new records for its lead actor. ‘Bala’ is inching towards the coveted Rs 100 crore mark and will soon be Ayushmann’s third film to enter the club. According to a report on Boxofficeindia.com the film has raked in Rs 3.75 crore nett on its second Friday at the box office and marked a total collection of Rs 73 crore and will comfortably cross the 100 crore mark in the coming days.

With this big century at the box office ‘Bala’ will emerge as Ayushmann’s third film in the coveted club after ‘Badhaai Ho’ which made Rs 134 crore and ‘Dream Girl’ which collected Rs 139 crore.

If this positive trend continues for ‘Bala’ in the third week as well, then the film will emerge as the eighth biggest hit of 2019 after ‘Kabir Singh’, ‘Uri - The Surgical Strike’, ‘Housefull 4’, ‘Mission Mangal’, ‘Chhichhore’ and ‘Dream Girl’. Directed by Amar Kaushik the film also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in pivotal roles.
