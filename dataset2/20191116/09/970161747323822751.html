Why the Man City squad are convinced Leroy Sane will join Bayern Munich

16 Nov 2019, 09:27

Leroy Sane’s team-mates are ‘increasingly convinced’ that the winger is in his final season at Manchester City.

The German was the subject of two bids from Bayern Munich last summer but his knee injury in August ended the Bavarians’ hopes of signing him.

City’s initial diagnosis was that Sane would not return until late March, meaning he’d face a race against time to prove his fitness for Euro 2020.

But there is now optimism at the club that the winger could return in February to boost the side’s title bid.

Sane is a popular figure in the City dressing room but the Telegraph report that the squad feel his ‘head has been turned’ by Bayern and that it’s only a matter of time before he makes the move.

The winger has 18 months remaining on his current deal and will be down to just a year left next summer, when Bayern hope to sign him for a reduced fee.

It’s felt that the 22-year-old is keen on a return to Germany and City are already said to be looking at replacements.

Real Sociedad’s Mikel Oyarzabal is believed to be the frontrunner and has admirers on City’s board, such as director of football Txiki Begiristain.
