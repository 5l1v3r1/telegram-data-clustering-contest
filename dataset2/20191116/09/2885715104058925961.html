Kerala KMAT December 2019 admit card released, steps to download

KMAT Kerala December 2019 admit card has been released on the official website, kmatkerala.in. The MBA entrance exam will be conducted on December 1

16 Nov 2019, 09:03 by Education Desk

Kerala KMAT admit card December 2019: The Kerala University of Fisheries and Ocean Studies has released the admit card for the Kerala KMAT – entrance exam conducted to enrol students in MBA programmes across the state. Candidates can download the admit cards from the official website of KMAT Kerala-kmatkerala.in

The KMAT Kerala exam will be conducted on December 1, 2019. Those who clear the entrance examination will be selected for admissions to MBA course for the academic year 2020. KMAT is accepted by B-school across the state of Kerala.

Kerala KMAT admit card December 2019: Steps to download

The candidates should follow the steps give below to download the admit cards:

Step 1: Visit the official website-kmatkerala.in
Step 2: On the homepage, login using your details asked like application number and password and click on log-in
Step 3:  The admit cards will be displayed on the screen
Step 4: Check the details on the admit cards and download for future reference.

Candidates are required to download their admit cards and take a print out of it for future reference. They will not be given entry to the examination hall without the admit cards, as per the rules.

To clear the exam, candidates will have to score at least 108 marks, for reserved category candidates the minimum marks are 54, for SEBC category the minimum marks required to be declared passed is 72.
