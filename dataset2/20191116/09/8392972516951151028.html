Emmerdale fans horrified by Arthur's evil abuse of Archie

His evil behaviour has Archie and viewers terrified

16 Nov 2019, 09:40 by Entertainment Daily

Emmerdale fans have been left horrified at Arthur Thomas' evil abuse of little Archie Sharma.

Usually mild-mannered Arthur has been bullying Archie for weeks but last night he stepped up his campaign of abuse.

Viewers know Archie is struggling after the death of his mum Rachel but Arthur seems determined to make his life a misery.

After hearing Jai and Laurel rowing over his claims that Jimmy was responsible for hurting Archie, Arthur turned nasty.

He shouted vile things at little Archie before grabbing the phone he was playing games on and smashing it to pieces.

"You're so stupid," he said.

Tell on me and you'll be sorry.

"You're a pathetic little baby, you're not even old enough to understand that game properly, just give it to me."

With that he grabbed the phone and stamped on it, wrecking it in the process before quickly deciding to blame Archie for his own abuse and warning him not to say anything.
