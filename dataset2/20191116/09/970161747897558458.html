The Apprentice's Lottie Lion and Ryan-Mark tell fans they are 'dating'

16 Nov 2019, 09:48

The Apprentice stars Lottie Lion and Ryan-Mark have confirmed they are actually ‘dating’ after fans noticed how close they have gotten on the show.

A fan of The Apprentice tweeted Ryan-Mark after Wednesday night’s show to comment on their friendship.

They tweeted: ‘Just watched last night’s episode of the apprentice, another brilliant episode. My favourites so far are
@LottieLion1 @ryanmarkparsons and @ImLewisEllis

‘I’ve noticed Lottie and Ryan-Mark have quite the friendship.’

The candidate then added fuel to the fire by responding: ‘We’re actually dating. EXCLUSIVE.’

Lottie then weighed in confirming the news as she tweeted: ‘Public Service Announcement after much speculation’

Fans were quick to respond to the news – and it’s safe some people found it funny – and others were just a bit confused.
