Naturi Naughton Clap Backs At 50 Cent's Mortal Kombat Meme

16 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Paul Dachs

Naturi Naughton is done with 50 cent’s shade. The Power actress and movie star responded to Fiddy’s latest troll with a jab of her own, pointing out his apparent Instagram timeout.

In an Instagram post that shared her original response to 50’s reaction-garnering tweet, Naughton said, “there’s so much I wanna say but… you don’t even have an Instagram account so… why waste my characters or come out of my CHARACTER!”

50’s post features the Mortal Kombat character ‘Goro’, and is clearly intended to make fun of Naughton’s hairline. The tweet – which is just the latest in a long line of social media trolls by the Power executive producer – received serious backlash from fans.
