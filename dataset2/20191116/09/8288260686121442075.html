'Big mistake' - Latest Liverpool update has Aston Villa fans fuming

Aston Villa v Liverpool in the Carabao Cup | Jurgen Klopp's side will travel to Villa Park once again but this time it will be for a December quarter-final clash

16 Nov 2019, 09:54 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Aston Villa have been criticised for the ticket prices against Liverpool after details for the Carabao Cup quarter-final tie were released yesterday.

Villa take on the Reds in the final eight of the competition on Tuesday, December 17 at Villa Park.

The clash has been the subject of intense interest since the draw was confirmed, because of Liverpool’s involvement in the Club World Cup the same week.

Jurgen Klopp’s side play in the semi-final of the tournament in Qatar under 24 hours after facing Villa.

 

Most of Liverpool's regulars are expected to head to Doha with an inexperienced side likely to travel to the Midlands - while Dean Smith also has a packed schedule to contend with.

Villa beat Wolves at the end of October to set up the clash against Liverpool – in a match which was free to season ticket holders.

The most expensive tickets for Villa fans against Liverpool are £33 in zone 1, gradually decreasing to £20 for adults in zone 6.

This is in line with the pricing structure for category C Premier League matches at home but plenty of supporters think it’s too much to pay.
