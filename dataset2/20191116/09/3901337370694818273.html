PSL Return On The Cards For Johnson?   

16 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Soccer Laduma

Cavin Johnson could be back in the Absa Premiership coaching scene, with reports of interest in him dominating to local football fraternity. 

Johnson has been without a club since September, since he parted ways with AmaZulu, after the Durban-based outfit terminated his contract due to poor results.

With Polokwane City having also parted ways with Serbian coach Zlatko Krmpotic last week, an indication is that Johnson is one of the coaches considered for the role at Rise and Shine, should the club decide to appoint a local coach.

According to sources, the Limpopo outfit’s first preference would be a foreign coach, but would be open to hiring a local one on failure to get a suitable candidate from abroad, with the experienced Johnson believed to be on their radar.

Rise and Shine will restart their campaign with a match against Orlando Pirates on November 26.
