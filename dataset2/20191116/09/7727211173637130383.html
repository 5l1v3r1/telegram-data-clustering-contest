BBC Apprentice star Alan Sugar blasts contestant over 'horrible tattoos'

WARNING: MAY CONTAIN SPOILERS "When you're 50 on the beach you'll wear a T shirt. You will be an embarrassment to your kids"

16 Nov 2019, 09:21 by Max Clements

A heated twitter row erupted between Apprentice host Lord Sugar and one of the shows contestants over his chest tattoos.

This week the candidates were set the task of launching and advertising campaign rebranding Finland.

For the advert, contestant Lewis Ellis was pictured with his top off revealing his chest tattoos- much to the dislike of Lord Sugar.

While this week's episode was on air , the businessman was tweeting along and he did not take kindly to Lewis' tattoos.

In a tweet on Wednesday night, Lord Sugar said: "Lewis your tattoos are horrific. Tell me where I am missing the point. What does he look like?"

Lewis immediately replied back with: "Wow LAS how could you do me like this @Lord_Sugar."
