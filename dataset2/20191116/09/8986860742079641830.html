Big Gyamfi drops a new banger “Ohemaa” (Listen, Download)

16 Nov 2019, 09:41 by ‌ RASHAD

The young budding musician is up with it again with an Afropop joint titled OHEMAA. The dominant theme for this groovy banger is the celebration of women and how a man will go every length to protect his Queen (Ohemaa).

This is just the tip of the iceberg for this extremely talented musician. He is indeed destined for Greatness as there is more to come from this young lad.

His passion for music has always been present counting back to his childhood as he would write down and sing the songs from his favorite stars growing up.

His early influences were Rnb and Rap, but later grew much interest in highlife and Reggae music.

His style of music is very unpredictable as he is able to blend different styles of music into one genuine Art form.

His goal as a musician is to inspire every young person to follow their dreams and never give up.
