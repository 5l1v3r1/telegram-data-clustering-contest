Happy times for Kerry Lee at Cheltenham as Diva strikes Gold

16 Nov 2019, 16:20 by Sporting Life

Happy Diva made up for being brought down in the same race last year by winning the BetVictor Gold Cup at Cheltenham for Kerry Lee.

The mare was travelling well when brought down four out last year but she didn't suffer such misfortune this time around under Richard Patrick.

As the field dropped away one by one in testing conditions, Happy Diva travelled strongly into contention and jumped the last well to put herself in pole position.

Brelan D'As and Slate House emerged as major threats at the last, though, and while the latter fell when in contention the former laid down a stern challenge.

However, Happy Diva wasn't for passing up the hill and she held on to win by a neck from Brelan D'As.

There were eight lengths back to long-time front-runner Warthog in third, with Belami Des Pictons fourth.

Magic Saint and Count Merible were fifth and sixth.
