Video Of Michigan State Players Entering Michigan Stadium Going Viral

16 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Andrew McCarty

In just under an hour, one of the best rivalry games in the country kicks off from Ann Arbor.

No. 15 Michigan hosts Michigan State in a rivalry game that has produced some of the most-memorable moments in recent college football history.

The Spartans are in the midst of a down season. Mark Dantonio’s squad has lost four-straight including an abysmal performance against Illinois last weekend.

However, Sparty is looking to turn things around with a big win over their in-state rival. It looks like the Spartans are ready for battle this afternoon.

Michigan State’s players came off the bus wearing their helmets.
