The Crown's Olivia Colman raves about Gillian Anderson as Margaret Thatcher

16 Nov 2019, 16:58

Olivia Colman has given her verdict on Gillian Anderson’s performance as Margaret Thatcher in The Crown season 4 – and she’s got the royal seal of approval.

The Sex Education actress will be portraying the late Prime Minister in the next series of the show, which is currently filming.

Speaking to Entertainment Weekly, the Oscar-winning star spoke about seeing Anderson channel the Iron Lady at a table read, and she definitely left a positive impression.

‘The moment she opened her mouth in the read-through, you could feel everyone wanting to do a little silent “Yay!” because she was so brilliant,’ she said.

‘She’s amazing,’ she added, to really hit the point home;

We won’t see the X-Files icon as the Prime Minister, however, for quite some time as the third series of the programme (finally) drops this weekend.

It comes at a particularly important time for the show too, as it has just replaced all its major stars.

Colman and Helena Bonham Carter are taking over from Claire Foy and Vanessa Kirby respectively, whilst Matt Smith has passed the torch on to Game Of Thrones’ Tobias Menzies.

The actresses will be portraying royal sisters Queen Elizabeth II and Princess Margaret.

The next run of episodes will explore an ageing royal family, which will track the life of the queen from 1964 to 1970; with events including the Aberfan disaster in South Wales and the assassination of Lord Mountbatten.

We’ll also see a more prominent role for the Prince of Wales in the new series, as he’s now aged up considerably and is played by God’s Own Country star Josh O’Connor.

And the beginnings of Charles’s relationship with his now-wife Camilla Parker-Bowles will also be portrayed, although Princess Diana herself won’t be turning up until series four – which is filming now.

The iconic princess will be played by Grantchester star Emma Corrin.

The Crown season 3 drops Sunday November 17 on Netflix.
