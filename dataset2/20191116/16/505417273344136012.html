Stuart Hogg Executes Inch Perfect Kick That Leads To Exeter Try

16 Nov 2019, 16:02 by Michael Corry

Stuart Hogg is really making his presence felt with his new club.

The international full-back linked up with new side Exeter Chiefs following Scotland’s disappointing Rugby World Cup campaign and is today making his Champions Cup debut with the English Premiership side.

Exeter currently lead La Rochelle, led by Ronan O’Gara, 14-0 following a try from Dave Dennis. However, the score came as a result of one of the most perfect kicks you are ever likely to see on a rugby pitch from Hogg.
