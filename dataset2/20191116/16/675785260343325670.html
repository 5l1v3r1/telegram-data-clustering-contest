Lucas Torreira's agent admits he is unhappy with Unai Emery as he considers Arsenal exit

The midfielder has been linked with a move back to Italy previously and has failed to hold down a regular starting berth at the Emirates this season

16 Nov 2019, 16:08 by Darren Wells, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/darren-wells/

Lucas Torreira is unhappy with Arsenal manager Unai Emery, according to the players' agent.

The Uruguayan international has started just four Premier League games so far this season for the Gunners, and has seemingly been ousted from the defensive midfield role.

Torreira was one of few positives to come out of last season for Arsenal, after impressing at the base of his team's midfield.

However he is said to be annoyed at Emery for changing his role and moving him into a more advanced position.

The 23-year-old has been linked with a a move away from the Emirates previously, and his agent Raffaele Bentancur did little to dispel any rumours.

"Anything is possible in football," Bentancur told Radio Marte. "Torreira is happy at Arsenal, although the change in role has not made him happy.

"It must be said that this year the whole team is not doing well. But I believe Arsenal wants to keep him."

AC Milan are big admirers of Torreira, while Napoli tried to sign him in 2018 before he moved to England.

Speaking of his lack of game time, Torreira said recently: "I don’t know whether I’d say it’s frustrating.

“All players want to play, they obviously want to be in the starting XI – that’s completely normal.

“But that’s not something that depends on me.

“It’s down to the coach to make those decisions and really I have to respect that.

“Right now it’s about not giving up.

“You have to continue working hard and whether I’m playing in the starting XI or from the bench I want to give my all for the team."

Torreira's Arsenal future could be affected by teammate Granit Xhaka, with the former captain looking likely to leave the club.

Milan are interested in signing the Swiss star, which could put them off of a move for Torreira, but also open up more first-team opportunities for him.

Newcastle are also interested in offering Xhaka a Premier League lifeline and could make a loan bid for the midfielder in January.
