Fans At Mississippi State-Alabama Game Warned About Bats

16 Nov 2019, 16:37 by Chris Rosvoglou

As if facing an angry Alabama team wasn’t bad enough for Mississippi State, the football program will also have to deal with bats this afternoon. Yes, you read that correctly.

The Crimson Tide and Bulldogs will battle in a little less than a hour. While the biggest storyline on the field has to do with the health of Tua Tagovailoa, this recent development involving bats has become the topic of conversation in Mississippi.

Davis Wade Stadium shared an announcement that there are bats in the venue that are considered “high risk for rabies.”

Alabama reporter Mike Rodak announced the news on Twitter.
