Urban Meyer Gives Harsh Assessment Of Michigan State Football

16 Nov 2019, 16:56 by Andrew McCarty

The Michigan State football program is in the midst of a down season. The Spartans have lost four-straight games, including an embarrassing defeat against Illinois last weekend.

College football analysts from around the country have taken notice and called out Mark Dantonio and the coaching staff for their job this season.

Add former Ohio State head coach Urban Meyer to that list. Meyer, when asked about the state of the Michigan State football program, painted an ugly picture for the Spartans.

“You watch Michigan State play, how many NFL players do they got? That doesn’t look like the defenses I used to go against. They had first-round corners, Le’Veon Bell. I don’t see that. That’s coaches, recruiting development,” Meyer said.
