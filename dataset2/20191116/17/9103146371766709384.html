Uganda Cranes games are always a party for fans

16 Nov 2019, 17:57 by Charles Mutebi

Business booms, with food vendors, player portraits, whistles, among other items, going on sale in and outside the stadium

As Uganda’s foremost sports brand, the Uganda Cranes enjoy unrivalled support and their home fixtures bear testament to this. 

Roads leading to the Mandela National Stadium on Cranes game days are decorated with masses of people donning the national colours while the decibel levels are heightened with fan songs, vuvuzelas, car horns and whatever instruments can be employed for the occasion. 

Business booms, with food vendors, player portraits, whistles, among other items, going on sale in and outside the stadium. The dress code is as varied as it is creative although the common thread is in the colours. 

The atmosphere within the stadium is the highlight, of course, especially on those occasions when the stadium is full. And when the Cranes score and or win, the party goes into overdrive.
