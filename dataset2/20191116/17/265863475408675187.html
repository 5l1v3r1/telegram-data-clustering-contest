Porsha Williams Proudly Shares An Important Achievement With Her Fans

16 Nov 2019, 17:09 by Ron Collins

Porsha Williams could not be prouder these days. She shared the latest achievement in a message on her social media account.

‘I couldn’t be more excited that @MarchofDimes named me as one of their first national advocates on their new Celebrity Advocate Council! And I’m being joined by @AllyBrooke, @NickLachey, and @TatyanaAli!!’ Porsha began her post.

She continued and said: ‘Together, we’ll be raising awareness about the issues impacting moms and babies, and I’ll be fighting for more research and funding to address why black women and mothers are disproportionately affected by pregnancy complications. #ItsNotFine, but it can be! Learn more at marchofdimes.org! #HealthyMomsStrongBabies.’

Someone said: ‘Your ❤️ is as golden as you, congratulations you’ll do@great things.’

Another follower posted: ‘Congratulations Porsha! they couldn’t have chosen a better person than you, God Bless You!!!💕🎉💕’

Someone said: ‘Thank you for being a voice for us @porsha4real I lost my son when I went into labor at 8 months pregnant. I lost him 2 weeks b4 Christmas in 2017. I still have no answers and I still cry. Postpartum almost took me up out here in 2018 but I kept my faith and God is blessing my husband and I all over again🙌🏾🙏🏾 It really means a lot to us brown girls to see a familiar face! It lets us know that we’re not alone! And that it’ll be ok. I’m so happy that you’re the one chosen to represent us!! God bless you girl!!💯’

One other commenter wrote: ‘You have become a warrior and I am here for it very impressed and glad u use your platform to help others bravo!!!’

Other than this, Porsha just showed love to Diddy while he’s remembering late Kim Porter.
