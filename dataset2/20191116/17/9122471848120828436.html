Alabama Is Off To A Ridiculous Start vs. Mississippi State

16 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Andrew McCarty

The Alabama Crimson Tide lost their first game of the season last weekend in a battle against rival LSU. Ed Orgeron’s Tigers came out firing and quarterback Joe Burrow threw for nearly 400 yards and three touchdowns en route to a 46-41 win.

The loss left college football analysts everywhere wondering if this is the first year Alabama won’t be in the College Football Playoff. Nick Saban’s team needs to win-out and win with style points in the process.

Well, it seems that Alabama got the message. The Crimson Tide are off to an incredible start against Mississippi State so far.

Just two minutes and six seconds into the first quarter, Alabama jumped out to a 14-0 lead.
