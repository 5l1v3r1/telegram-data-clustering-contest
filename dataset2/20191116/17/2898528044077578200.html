UFC Sao Paulo Results: Blachowicz vs. Jacare

16 Nov 2019, 17:00 by MMA Fighting Newswire

MMA Fighting has UFC Sao Paulo results for the Jan Blachowicz vs. Ronaldo Souza fight card in Sao Paulo and live UFC Sao Paulo Twitter updates.

The scheduled main event features a light heavyweight showdown between title hopefuls Ronaldo “Jacare” Souza and Jan Blachowicz.

On tap for the ESPN+ event is a light heavyweight co-headliner between former champion Mauricio “Shogun” Rua and short-notice replacement Paul Craig.

You can also check out live blogs for Blachowicz vs. Jacare and Rua vs. Craig.

Follow UFC Sao Paulo results below.

Mauricio “Shogun” Rua vs. Paul Craig declared a split draw (29-28, 28-29, 28-28)

Charles Oliveira def. Jared Gordon by knockout (punches) at 1:26, R1

Wellington Turman def. Markus Perez by unanimous decision (30-27, 30-27, 30-27)
