The Pitch Invader, Awal Suleman Finally Released- Received As A Hero In Cape Coast (+Photos)

16 Nov 2019, 17:33 by BB Jones

The University Police Station in Cape Coast has released Awal Suleman, the man who has entered the record books as the first Ghanaian pitch invader.

Suleman was arraigned before court on Friday during which he was charged with disturbing public peace.

However, he was granted bail after the court proceedings but now he’s a free man.

Suleman ran to the pitch during the 20121 AFCON qualifier between Ghana and South Africa in the 83rd to disrupt the game as he was pursued by police officers and policemen.

He later revealed that he wanted to take a selfie with his idol, Thomas Party.
