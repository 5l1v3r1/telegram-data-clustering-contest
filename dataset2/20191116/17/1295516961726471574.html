Mean Gene Okerlund, Thuganomics John Cena Join Funko's WWE Pop! Line

16 Nov 2019, 17:11 by Adreon Patterson

A new set of WWE superstars will be cemented in pop culture infamy as Funko has announced new figurines for the Funko Pop! WWE line.

Funko has unveiled figures based on current and past WWE performers, including The Miz, "Mean" Gene Okerlund, Diesel with Kevin Nash Chase (Funko's rare variants), Naomi with a glow-in-the-dark Chase, and John Cena's Doctor of Thuganomics.

The Miz is decked out in his full Miz regalia while Okerlund offers his usual commentary in his tuxedo and red bowtie with a WWE microphone. Collectors get two for the price of one as Kevin Nash appears as himself in NWO and Diesel. Naomi takes a power stance in her black and neon green leotard and hair with a glow-in-the-dark variant. John Cena gets a throwback, appearing in his baseball cap, throwback jersey and jean shorts with "word" and "life" brass knuckles as the Doctor of Thuganomics.

The line also includes two exclusives. Amazon will have “The Fiend” Bray Wyatt, while Wrestlemania Hulk Hogan will be exclusive to Walmart.
