Inside Ant McPartlin and Lisa Armstrong's bitter divorce and why she wants her day in court

Lisa Armstrong is waiting for her day in court as she declines offers of a financial settlement from Ant McPartlin, it's claimed

16 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Molly Pike, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/molly-pike/

Ant McPartlin and Lisa Armstrong split up almost two years ago but their divorce still hasn't gone through.

The makeup artist, who works on Strictly Come Dancing , is reportedly waiting for her day in court as she declines offers of a financial settlement.

A friend of Lisa's told the Daily Mail : "It is not about the money, she is not a materialistic person. It is all about his appalling behaviour to her."

Lisa and Ant were together for 23 years and married in 2006 before announcing their separation in 2018.

Ant soon started a new relationship with her friend and former personal assistant Anne-Marie Corbett.

Lisa was left reeling when the couple went public and took to Twitter to blast Anne-Marie for breaking the 'Girl Code'.

Ant has reportedly made his ex a very generous offer of around half of his £63 million fortune.

But Lisa is standing firm, with the source adding: "She doesn’t need to settle, she can be a thorn in his side. She knows she is a stronger person than him. He lives in dread of seeing her in the corridor of a TV studio one day, but she would relish it."

Another point of contention in the divorce talks is her rumoured refusal to sign a non-disclosure agreement which means she won't be able to talk about their relationship.

Friends say she has no intention of doing a tell all book, but doesn't want to sign the NDA out of principle.

They added: "She has always been discreet about him to the point where people will often have their make-up done by her and not be aware of the connection. The point is that she just doesn’t want to give him the power over her."

After a hellish year of two stints in rehab, a public battle with prescription drugs and booze, and his shameful drink-driving crash in March 2018, Ant is no doubt hoping the public will continue to support him.

But without Lisa agreeing to sign the NDA, he's reportedly not signing off on a financial settlement until she complies.

The warring exes have also been butting heads over custody of their Labrador Hurley.
