After Akshay Kumar, Twinkle Khanna visits mother Dimple Kapadia at the hospital

16 Nov 2019, 08:36 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Akshay Kumar was recently snapped outside a hospital leading to fans speculating that the actor has had a health scare. However, according to a news portal Khiladi Kumar was actually visiting his mother-in-law, Dimple Kapadia who is unwell. The actor made a quick visit on Thursday and today it was Twinkle Khanna who was clicked outside the hospital. The actress was snapped by the paparazzi making her way to the hospital to visit her ailing mother.

Dimple Kapadia was recently in the news for her Hollywood film ‘Tenet’ with celebrated filmmaker Christopher Nolan. The veteran actress was snapped shooting for the film at Gateway of India in Mumbai. ‘Tenet’ is touted to be a thriller written and directed by Christopher Nolan. The filmmaker is also producing the film with Emma Thomas. ‘Tenet’ is said to be a high-octane action espionage drama and will be shot across seven countries. The film scheduled to hit the screens in July 2020.
