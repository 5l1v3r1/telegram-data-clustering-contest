'I'm not stupid' - Manager makes big Aston Villa transfer admission

Latest Aston Villa transfer news sees Coventry City boss respond to reported AVFC interest

16 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Coventry City boss Mark Robins has admitted Sam McCallum could leave the League One club in the January transfer window.

Aston Villa are among the sides keeping an eye on the 19-year-old full-back who has impressed for the Sky Blues this season.

Robins has previously told interested clubs it will take a ‘monumental’ bid to prise McCallum away but concedes there’s nothing he can do if the right money is offered.

Scouts from the Premier League and Championship have flocked to St Andrew’s to watch McCallum play this term as Coventry eye promotion.

 

Asked if his side can hold onto the teenage talent, Robins told CoventryLive: “I would like to think yes, but I am not stupid.

“Money talks and it’s not any different at any other club, regardless of where they are in terms of stature and division.

“If someone comes in with the right money then people will be available, there’s no two ways about it.

“But I am not looking to move anyone on. I am looking to maintain, first and foremost, and then add if I can."
