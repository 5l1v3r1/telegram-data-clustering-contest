Kogi election: Accreditation commences in Lokoja

16 Nov 2019, 08:41 by Agency Report

Accreditation at Ward A Polling Station, Growther/ Sabongari, Lokoja, was on at 8.35 a.m. when a correspondent of the News Agency of Nigeria visited the polling station.

Voters were seen checking their names on the register pasted on the walls of Growther Memorial College, Lokoja, while others lined up, orderly, to be accredited.

The exercise was peaceful and orderly.

Men and officers of the Nigeria Police, Federal Road Safety Commission, Nigeria Security and Civil Defence Corps, the Customs Service, among other security outfits kept vigil to ensure the peaceful conduct of the election.

(NAN)
