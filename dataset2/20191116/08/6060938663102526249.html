She is SEXY and she knows it! Shama Sikander looks smoking hot in her latest monochrome click

16 Nov 2019, 08:59 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shama Sikander perfectly knows how to turn up the heat on the internet, case in point being her latest monochrome click. The actress showed off her envious figure as she posed on a chair in an unbuttoned shirt. Adding a hat to her sultry avatar, Shama looked all things glamorous. Shama was recently seen in ‘Bypass Road’ and was happy with the film’s response. “I am overwhelmed and happy with the love that is coming my way post 'Bypass Road'. The audience and my fans have loved my performance and the portrayal of a strong character. Personally I met few fans at the screening and they praised the character. The people who matter in my life have loved my performance,” Shama told ETimes.

Shama began her career on the big screen with 'Prem Aggan' (1988) and went on feature in several movies and TV shows. Beyond the silver screen, Shama also entertains her many followers on Instagram.
