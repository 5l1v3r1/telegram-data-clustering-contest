'Support the guy' - What Nottingham Forest fans have had to say on Aro Muric

Nottingham Forest latest | The Manchester City loanee has seen Brice Samba replace him as first-choice goalkeeper following the opening month of the Championship season

16 Nov 2019, 08:44 by Josh Murray

Nottingham Forest goalkeeper Aro Muric came close to helping Kosovo closer to an unlikely European Championship finals place last night.

Kosovo suffered a dramatic 2-1 defeat to the Czech Republic in group A - the same group as England - in a result that secured the Czechs' qualification for Euro 2020.

But Muric's Kosovo had threatened to cause an upset when a goal from Sheffield Wednesday Atdhe Nuhiu headed them in front just after half-time.

At that stage, the visitors were set to boast a two-point lead over the Czech Republic with one fixture to go.
