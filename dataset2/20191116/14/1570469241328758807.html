Car bomb explosion kills nineteen in northern Syria's Al-Bab

16 Nov 2019, 14:54

It was not immediately clear who had carried out the attack.

At least nineteen people were killed and many others wounded in the town of Al-Bab in northern Syria.

Turkish-controlled town of Al-Bab is located 18 miles from the Syrian-Turkish border in the Aleppo province. 

The bomb was planted in a car which was parked on the side of the road before a garage door when it exploded.

The blast struck a bus and taxi station in the town, the Britain-based Syrian Observatory for Human Rights reported.
