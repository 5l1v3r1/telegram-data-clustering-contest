Who won the 2018 series of I'm A Celebrity and what are they doing now?

16 Nov 2019, 14:23

The latest round of celebrities entering the jungle for I’m A Celebrity 2019 will be steeled for similar ordeals to the Bushtucker Trials faced by the class of 2018, when viper pits and gore were among the themes of the 20 grim physical and mental tests.

Across 22 days, the powers that be granted the show’s stars Dingo Dollars which they could earn for achievements and trade for relative luxuries, often providing scant comfort in a series that compelled more than 14 million viewers to tune in at its peak.

The small screen hit also saw viewers tend to vote out familiar faces from TV, with presenter Noel Edmonds the first to be banished, swiftly followed by soap actors Malique Thompson-Dwyer, Sair Khan and Rita Simons.

Moments of warmth won the popular vote, with the surprise winner speaking of their matrimonial bliss. Here’s a recap of the 2018 I’m A Celebrity result and a look at what the winner is up to now…

Who won I’m A Celebrity 2018?

Although I’m A Celebrity had previously crowned a sporting winner as early as its second series, which was won by former cricketer Phil Tufnell in 2003, the odds were against 2019 winner Harry Redknapp in a show the veteran football manager admitted he had never watched.

Redknapp was the only contestant with a sporting background to enter the outback last year, but he soon charmed his newfound company and the audience, not least through his penchant for storytelling.
