Photo: Baylor Fan’s ‘Girlfriend’ Sign Goes Viral At College GameDay

16 Nov 2019, 14:56 by Andrew McCarty

The college football season rolled on with a full slate of games in the Week 12 window. ESPN’s College GameDay broadcast live from Waco, Texas ahead of a huge Big 12 showdown between No. 10 Oklahoma and No. 13 Baylor.

Before the game kicks off, the GameDay crew broke down all of the action coming up this season.

Every Saturday as the crew gives their picks and predictions, the crowd gets in on the fun with signs. Now and then a sign pops up that takes the cake as the best sign of the day. This one had nothing to do with the actual game, though.

No, one fan lamented the loss of their girlfriend.

“My girlfriend breaking up with me was a quality loss,” the sign read.
