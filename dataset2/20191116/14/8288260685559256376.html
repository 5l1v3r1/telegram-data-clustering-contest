Ricky Hatton slammed over X-rated fancy dress outfit at family festival in Benidorm

The former welterweight - who once fought Floyd Mayweather - got involved in the annual British Fancy Dress Day celebrations during his trip

16 Nov 2019, 14:00 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Ricky Hatton has been criticised for donning an X-rated fancy dress costume at a Benidorm family festival.

The former boxer is at the popular Spanish destination during its fiesta season.

The former welterweight - who once fought Floyd Mayweather - got involved in the annual British Fancy Dress Day celebrations during his trip.

But Hatton has sparked a backlash after donning a jockey costume for the event, with a giant, sequin laden penis hanging in front.

The post was shared on a Benidorm Facebook page, the Mirror reports , with many criticising the 'bad taste' outfit.

 

One said: "It's a bit much when there's kids around.''

Back at home, Ricky is currently working with Love Island star Tommy Fury on his return to boxing.

Tommy - the younger brother of Tyson - will make his return to the ring on December 12 and trains at Ricky's gym in Hyde, in Manchester.

 

The 41-year-old wasn't the only one having a great time as thousands of revellers descend on the resort every year to take part in the November celebrations.
