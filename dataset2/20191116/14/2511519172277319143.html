Roger Federer vs Stefanos Tsitsipas LIVE - ATP Finals 2019: Latest score, updates and tennis results

The Swiss maestro is looking to go one better than last year after his semi-final defeat to eventual champion Alexander Zverev

16 Nov 2019, 14:41 by Jack Rathborn

Roger Federer avoided a shock to advance from the groups and now Stefanos Tsitsipas awaits in the semi-finals.

The Greek battled hard in his final game vs Rafael Nadal but went down in three sets, losing his unbeaten record in this year’s tournament and giving up valuable energy in a needless losing effort.

The Swiss maestro instead rolled back the years to dump out rival Novak Djokovic to reach this point and gain some revenge for his Wimbledon heartache. Follow this intriguing semi-final live below.

Federer vs Tsitsipas Key Points
   •  Federer is aiming to go one stage further than last year when he lost in the semi-finals vs Zverev
   •  Tsitsipas is the youngest semifinalist of the ATP World Tour Finals since Juan Martin Del Potro in 2009
   •  Second semi-final tonight: Thiem vs Zverev
