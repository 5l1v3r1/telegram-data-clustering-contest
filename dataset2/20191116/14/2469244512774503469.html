Exeter City will host Oxford United in next round of Leasing.Com Trophy

The draw was made this afternoon on Soccer Saturday

16 Nov 2019, 14:23 by Daniel Clark

Exeter City will host high-flying Oxford United in the second round of theÂ  Leasing.Com Trophy.

The draw for the competition took place this afternoon on Soccer Saturday on Sky sports.

Hosted by Sky Sports presenter Julian Waters, former Crystal Palace forward Clinton Morrison and ex-England midfielder Sue Smith, were on hand to draw the teams out.

The Grecians were handed a home draw as a result of winning their group, with wins in all three clashes against West Ham United U21s, today's visitors Cheltenham Town, and Newport County.

In Round 2, Exeter City will host League One Oxford United, who finished second in their group.

The O's, managed by Karl Robinson, are currently 4th in the League One.

The two clubs have not met since August 2016 when Oxford ran out 4-2 winners in a Football League Trophy tie

Matches are provisionally set to take place in the week commencing December 2, 2019.
