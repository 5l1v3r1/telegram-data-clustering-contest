Paul Finebaum Predicts The Winner Of Baylor-Oklahoma

16 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Chris Rosvoglou

The biggest game on the schedule this weekend in college football is a Big 12 showdown in Waco, Texas. Baylor will try to keep its undefeated season alive when it hosts Oklahoma.

Matt Rhule has done an exceptional job rebuilding Baylor’s football program. A win this afternoon would solidify his status as one of the hottest commodities on the coaching market.

Even though Baylor has home-field advantage this Saturday, Oklahoma is the odds-on favorite to win this game.

Oklahoma has an outside shot at making the College Football Playoff if it doesn’t suffer another loss.

Earlier this morning, ESPN analyst Paul Finebaum was on SportsCenter to discuss the top games slated for this afternoon.

Finebaum has the Sooners defeating the Bears, but he believes there is a chance an upset happens in Waco.

Here’s what Finebaum had to say, via 247Sports:

“It starts today, an amazing opportunity for Matt Rhule,” Finebaum said. “Win today, you finish out and try to beat Oklahoma again. I would have a hard time imagining a committee turning them down if they run the table and finish undefeated. It would be an amazing accomplishment for Matt Rhule and the Bears.”

If the Bears finish the season undefeated, the selection committee would have to put them in the Playoff.

There have been several skeptics out there wondering if Baylor is a legitimate contender. An upset win today over Oklahoma would certainly silence those doubts.
