Pankaj Tripathi Makes His Instagram Debut; Kaleen Bhaiya Announces Mirzapur 2

16 Nov 2019, 14:20 by Saheli Maity

The digital wave has now hit the Sacred Games actor Pankaj Tripathi as he made his Instagram debut in order to mark the first anniversary of his hit series Mirzapur

2019 has become a digital world, almost every species has joined this digital world. Even Jennifer Aniston couldn’t resist getting into this world so the new entrant who has now got into this digital ocean is Sacred Games star, Pankaj Tripathi. Known for his acting chops and his dry quick sense of rustic humour, Pankaj Tripathi has finally taken a step towards getting closer to his fans by making a much-needed debut on Instagram.

While marking his glorious debut, Tripathi shared a small clip of Mirzapur that not only completed a year but also announced the second season of the web series, that’s right! His first post is the teaser of the second season of Mirzapur in which he reprises his part as Kaleen Bhai. In less than three hours, the post sent fans on a tizzy as they showered their love on his first post. His colleague Shweta Tripathi welcomed him on the digital platform with a wonderful comment on his post. Tripathi turns the narrator as he talks about life and death in this grim teaser. How excited are you guys to watch the second season?

The actor was quoted saying, “There’s a lot of anticipation amongst fans for the next season and I’m often asked when it will release. I too am excited about season two so it is just fitting for me to make my Instagram debut on the first birthday of Mirzapur and give my and the show’s fans a little tease into what is to come!”
