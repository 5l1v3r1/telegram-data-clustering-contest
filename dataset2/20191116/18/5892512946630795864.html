Highlights! Watch these equally insane elbow, flying knee knockouts from LFA and ONE Championship action

16 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Dan Hiergesell

Prior to UFC Fight Night 164 setting sail later tonight (Sat., Nov. 16, 2019) from Sao Paulo, Brazil, fight fans were treated to fabulous knockout finishes in two other prominent MMA promotions.

The first stoppage came last night at LFA 78 from Belton, Texas, as the promotion was making its broadcast debut on UFC Fight Pass. In the co-main event, bantamweight prospect Jose Johnson squared off with Rafael Costa.

During a Costa takedown attempt along the cage midway through the first round, Johnson landed an absolutely brutal elbow to the side of the head. Costa immediately went limp and fell to the canvas in violent fashion. The strike initially looked like it could have hit the back of Costa's head, but replays showed it was clean.
