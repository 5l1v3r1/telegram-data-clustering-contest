Ukrainian beauty Leanna Bartlett showed off her stunning figure in a racy outfit over the weekend. The blonde bombshell took to social media and posted a sweet photo on Instagram that showed off her voluptuous curves to perfection.

Leanna proved that even when she tries to cover up, she’s only emphasizing what her fans really want to see. In an outfit, which is rather demure for the 34-year-old, she wore a long-sleeved black lacy top and the cutest pair of leather daisy dukes.

In this image, Leanna chose not to draw any attention to her ample bosom. From the high-neck shirt and even the way she posed, it seemed as if the model wanted to emphasize her legs. Although the lacy top was very feminine and fans can see hints of her skin, her thighs wowed her audience.

Leanna wore a skimpy pair of black leather shorts. Its shiny texture looked smooth to the touch and highlighted the social media star’s tiny waist. The shorts had a small v-shaped cut along the outer seam that allowed for greater movement.

The stunner wore a full face of makeup. She wore a natural brow and lashings of mascara to show off her blue eyes. Leanna contoured her cheeks with some blusher and treated her perfect pout to a pretty shade of pink lipstick.

Leanna also styled her blonde hair in a more casual fashion. She opted for a side-path and allowed her bangs to fall over her face. She grabbed the rest of her hair into a messy ponytail that looked absolutely gorgeous as it flattered her features

Of course, the photo’s background was also rather scenic. With columns, a palm tree, and blue skies it seemed as if Leanna was truly in paradise. The social media influencer posed next to some beautiful pink roses.
