The upcoming Galaxy Fold 2 will be cheaper than the Galaxy Fold

16 Nov 2019, 18:19 by Rahul

Samsung is said to be working on version two of the original Galaxy Fold dubbed Galaxy Fold 2 and according to famous teenage leakster Ishan Agarwal, the Galaxy Fold 2 might be cheaper than its predecessor.

Galaxy Fold 2, which has a model number SM-F700F, will also be available in multiple markets. Users in the USA, Canada, China, India, Korea, EU are going to be able to buy the cheaper Galaxy Fold. Oddly enough, it’ll be available in one storage option — 256GB. The smartphone will be available in two color options — Black and Purple.
