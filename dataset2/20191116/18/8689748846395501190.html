Rev Owusu Bempah Pledges ¢5000 To Psalm Adjetefio For His Heart Issues

16 Nov 2019, 18:27 by BB Jones

Reverend Owusu Bempah has donated an of ¢5000 to the ailing Psalm Adjeteyfio who is currently battling a condition of the heart which demands surgery to correct it.

The Kumasi-based pastor heard the actor’s condition on Peace FM’s Entertainment Review program after which he pledged the amount.

It was announced that the money will be ready by Monday for it to be delivered to Psalm Adjetefio.

The actor had previously disclosed his heart cannot pump efficiently enough for blood to circulate oxygen-rich blood throughout the body.

Mr Adjeteyfio claimed for about one and a half year now, the actor said he has taken more than 5000 different drugs.
