CoD Mobile: Zombies Mode coming on November 22
4 (80%) 9 votes

Call of Duty Mobile ( CoD Mobile ) has come a long way in a short period of time and has achieved milestones that most games don’t in their entire lifetime. It currently goes head to head with PUBG Mobile, another game published by Tencent Games in the same genre. To maintain player retention, the Call of Duty Mobile team has now confirmed the long-anticipated Zombie Mode arrival date as November 22.
