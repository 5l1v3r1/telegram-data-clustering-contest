Take Me Out contestant lifts lid on what it's really like to be on the show

Author Meera Sharma opened up about her experience

16 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Sophie McCoid

A Take Me Out contestant has lifted the lid on what really goes on behind the scenes of the hit TV show.

Meera Sharma, 31, is currently appearing on this series of the ITV dating show fronted by Paddy McGuinness on Saturday nights.

The Ormskirk native impressed fans in the first episode when she dissed Peter Andre's classic hit 'Mysterious Girl.'

And while author Meera remained tight lipped about the casting process and whether she actually bags a date on the show, she did let slip some behind the scenes gossip.

Meera, who hoped to bag herself a Michael B Jordan lookalike on the show, said: "It was a brilliant, fun experience and I'd definitely do it again - but it could be very tiring filming for hours.

"It was filmed a year ago, so it's funny watching it back, because you're just running on adrenaline, I can't remember half of the things I've said.

"We were encouraged to bring our own wardrobe to show off our personality and I really liked that, because I have a distinctive style.

"I really wanted to show off my character and wear bright colours every week.

"We get our own glam squad on the show, who do your hair and make-up every week and that's great."

But Meera said the days could be tiring, with contestants expected to be at the studio from early morning until the evening.

Meera said: "We'd get there early in the morning and then go to hair and make-up before we did a rehearsal, just to check everything looked right on the cameras and the girls were in the correct position.

"Then we'd just film the episode in one go with a live audience in the early evening.

"It was really fun, but it was a long day and you could get tired, you're on your feet for a long time and you're on call all day, going in to get your make-up retouched or your hair checked.

"But it was really nice chatting to the girls and we all got on really well."

Meera said she was worried the other contestants would be "catty" but when she met the other girls she said they all bonded and still speak to each other now.

She said: "I met so many lovely girls, we all got on really well.

"I think sometimes when girls get together they can be a bit catty, but everyone was really lovely.

"That's the thing, even if you don't get a date you meet so many new friends, so it's worth going on just for that.

"We're all still in touch via social media and we meet up for drinks when we're in the same part of the country.

"With the show airing now we're all messaging each other every week about something hilarious we'd forgotten we'd said.

"And Paddy is just as nice as he appears on TV, he's so witty and funny and really bounces off us."

Since the show was filmed Meera has kept herself busy, launching a motivational platform for women and writing 'The little book of sass' featuring inspirational quotes to motivate women.

*Take Me Out airs on Saturday on ITV at 7:10pm
