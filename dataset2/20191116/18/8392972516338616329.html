Strictly fans gets an eyeful as Kelvin Fletcher poses in his pants for spray tan

Ex Emmerdale star loves to feel the glow

16 Nov 2019, 18:18 by Entertainment Daily

Kelvin Fletcher will no doubt send Strictly Come Dancing fans into a spin with this cheeky pre-spray tan snap.

Every week the former Emmerdale star gets a tan ready for the live show on Saturday. However, fans have never had a backstage photo - until now.

Taking to his Instagram Stories, the actor posted a shot of himself wearing nothing more than a pair of pants as he poses in front of the spray tan tent.

"I mean it's not really a Friday if you don't step into the tanning booth," he wrote alongside the photo.

Kelvin has talked about his weekly tan, but has never shared a flesh-baring behind-the-scenes pic like this.

Last month he even admitted he can't wait to get a fresh glow and said it instantly makes him look better.

"Spray tan always takes me from a 7/10 to an 8/10," he wrote on Instagram.

"Is it really weird that I look forward to the Friday spray tan session?!"

Kelvin is currently in Blackpool with the other six remaining contestants as he prepares to dance the Jive to Jailhouse Rock from Smokey Joe's Café.

Last week, Kelvin and partner Oti Mabuse performed an energetic salsa and won high praise from the Strictly judges.

However, fans at home thought they should have received higher scores after Craig Revel Horwood awarded them an eight, while Motsi Mabuse, Shirley Ballas and Bruno Tonioli each gave them a nine.

"I'm sorry but why is Kelvin not getting 10s for that please explain #Strictly," one fan wrote, while a second outraged viewer tweeted, "An EIGHT?! For Kelvin’s performance??? Are you joking?? #Strictly."
