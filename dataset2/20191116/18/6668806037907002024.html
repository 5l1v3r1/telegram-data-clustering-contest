Arjun Kapoor begins shooting for his next with Rakul Preet Singh

16 Nov 2019, 18:54 by Filmfare

Arjun Kapoor will be seen opposite Rakul Preet Singh in his yet untitled film. Interestingly, the project brings together John Abraham, Nikkhil Advani, and Bhushan Kumar as producers for the third time post Batla House and Satyameva Jayate. Announcing the project. Arjun took to his social media handle and wrote, “Enroute to start film number 14.”

Earlier today, Arjun shared a collage on his social media in which he was seen cutely posing with Rakul. He wrote, “Picture Shuroo... other information bhi upload hogi soon...” Check it out…

Excited about the project, Arjun had said in an official statement recently, “I have grown up in a joint family so I understood that it's the biggest pillar of love, strength, and support. That's the essence of our film, which has its heart in the right place.” From what we hear, the film is expected to be a cross border love tale.
