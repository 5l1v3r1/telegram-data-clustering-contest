Border Patrol agent shoots Russian man near Mexico border

16 Nov 2019, 18:40 by Caitlin Yilek

A Border Patrol agent shot and wounded a Russian man suspected of crossing the border illegally into Arizona, the agency said Friday.

The agent tried to arrest the man Thursday near the Arizona border with Mexico, and a physical altercation ensued, according to the agency. The agent shot the man, who was flown to a Phoenix hospital and is expected to survive. The Border Patrol agent was not seriously injured.

"As the agent attempted to arrest the subject, a physical altercation ensued, and the agent discharged his firearm, striking the subject," U.S. Customs and Border Patrol said.

The FBI is joining CBP's Use of Force Incident Team in investigating the shooting.

An agency spokesman did not immediately return a request for comment from the Washington Examiner.
