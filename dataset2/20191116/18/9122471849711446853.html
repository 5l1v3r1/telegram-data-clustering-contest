Nick Saban Catching Heat For Keeping Tua Tagovailoa In Too Long

16 Nov 2019, 18:50 by Chris Rosvoglou

Alabama had concerns entering this weekend about Tua Tagovailoa’s ankle. Sure, he played well against LSU last weekend, but he’s still less than a month removed from surgery.

Many analysts were begging Saban to start Mac Jones at quarterback this afternoon. The Crimson Tide thought Tagovailoa looked solid during warmups, so they ultimately let him start.

Tagovailoa was carted off the field and is reportedly getting an X-ray on his right hip. His helmet flew off during the play, which resulted in him receiving a bloody nose.

Since Alabama led 35-7 before the injury took place, the entire sports world is bashing Saban for leaving Tagovailoa in the game.
