Look around £23m student complex in Derby via new virtual tour

The cheapest apartment is £137 per week

16 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Noah Martin

With its state-of-the-art gym and en-suite bedrooms, University of Derby students are loving life at The Croft.

Launched in September, the £23m complex is the latest addition to the student accommodation on offer in Derby.

Inside the complex, there are 326 bedrooms with en-suites in every room, a free gym, games area and a small study space.

The privately-owned accommodation is made up of two tower blocks, each containing clusters of rooms.

Flat sizes range from four to eight bedroom properties and there are three room sizes for students to choose from.

Thanks to a new interactive tool, members of the public can now take a virtual tour of the building, which still has rooms available to rent.
