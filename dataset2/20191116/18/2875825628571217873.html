Strictly's AJ Pritchard finally makes Drag Race UK debut - but someone isn't happy

Strictly pro AJ, 25, appears on Drag Race UK's final with 'little brother' Curtis as they choreograph the drag queens' choreography

16 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Irishmirror.ie

AJ Pritchard finally makes his debut on Drag Race UK during next week's final - but not everyone is happy about it.

The Strictly pro, 25, appears on the show with "little brother" Curtis - aka, the one who liked to deliver everyone's coffees in the morning on Love Island.

Ahead of the big final, AJ is tasked with choreographing the drag queens' choreography, but it doesn't exactly go to plan.

Divina de Campo, Baga Chipz and the Vivienne have made it to the final three after Cheryl Hole got the boot last week.

In a preview for the last episode, the trio are told they need to complete a dance performance before lipsyncing for the crown.

The next clip sees them standing on the stage as they face AJ and Curtis.

AJ tells them: "I'm AJ and this is my little brother Curtis."

Running through the choreography with the Vivienne, AJ looks unimpressed.

A couple of times, AJ tells the Vivienne to "redo, redo".

The Vivienne looks frustrated and later tells the camera: "He keeps shouting at me!"

Tonight, AJ will compete at the famous Blackpool ballroom with partner Saffron Barker.

The pair have been achieving near-perfect scores from the judges - will they impress again tonight."

*Drag Race UK's final is available on BBC Three iPlayer at 8pm next Thursday
