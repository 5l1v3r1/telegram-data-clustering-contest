Big quake drill

16 Nov 2019, 18:08 by Orlan L. Mauricio

Personnel of the Provincial Disaster Risk Reduction Management Office, Bureau of Fire Protection and members of the Police Provincial Office of Bulacan pose after an earthquake drill at the Philippine Arena in Bocaue, Bulacan on Thursday, Nov. 14 as part of the 4th Quarter National Simultaneous Earthquake Drill. Central Luzon Police Regional Director Brig. Gen. Rodel Sermonia said it also tests the readiness of security and first-aid responders for the opening of the Southeast Asian Games at the same venue on Nov. 30.
