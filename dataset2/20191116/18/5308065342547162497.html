BLACKPINK’s Lisa tops most beautiful list

16 Nov 2019, 18:30

- Advertisement -

South Korean K-pop girl group BLACKPINK is not just talented but are also beautiful.

AsiaOne says two members of the girl group are listed in the top five of TCCAsia’s Most Beautiful Faces In Asia 2019. BLACKPINK’s Lisa is at the top of the list ranking No.1.

Also in the list are BLACKPINK’s Jennie at No.3 while Rose and Jisoo take number 13 and number 22, respectively.

- Advertisement -

Japanese actress Sakura Miyawaki sits at No.2 while Chinese actress Angelababy ranked No.4 and Chinese model Sui He takes the No.5 spot.

A couple of relatively famous names failed to make much of an impression with the selectors in the ranking of the top 100 faces.

Here is top ten in the ‘Most Beautiful Faces in Asia 2109’:

1. Lisa

2. Sakura Miyawaki

3. Jennie

4. Angelababy

5. Sui He

6. Im Yoon-ah

7. Satomi Ishihara

8. Gulnazar

9. Gloria Tang Tsz-Kei

10. Irene

Born as Pranpriya Manoban and later changed her name to Lalisa Manoban, Lisa, who is more commonly known is a Thai rapper, singer, dancer and model based in South Korea.

She is part of the South Korean girl group BLACKPINK under YG Entertainment.

Lisa was born in the Buriram Province Thailand and is the only child of her Thai mother and Swiss stepfather who works as a restaurant consultant and Thai food expert based in Bangkok.
