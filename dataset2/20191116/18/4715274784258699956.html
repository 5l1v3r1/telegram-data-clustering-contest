House guests

16 Nov 2019, 18:13

Forty former members of the Abu Sayyaf Group set foot inside the House of Representatives on Thursday, Nov. 14, as part of their reintegration program and an exposure trip in Metro Manila and Luzon to learn best practices in farming. With them is John Louie Balagot (center, brown jacket), Chief of Staff of Deputy Speaker and Basilan Rep. Mujiv Hataman. House Media
