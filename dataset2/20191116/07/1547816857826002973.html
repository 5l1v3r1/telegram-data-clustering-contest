Marjaavaan Box-Office Collection Day 1: Sidharth Malhotra-Tara Sutaria-Riteish Deshmukh Starrer Opens Well

16 Nov 2019, 07:54 by Oshine Koul

Sidharth Malhotra, Tara Sutaria and Riteish Deshmukh starrer Marjaavaan has had an encouraging box office figures on day 1 of release. The film has earned Rs 7.03 cr on Day 1. Marjaavaan clashed with Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Athiya Shetty’s Motichoor Chaknachoor

Ek Villain duo Sidharth Malhotra and Riteish Deshmukh are returning to the silver screen with Milap Zaveri’s Marjaavaan with newbie Tara Sutaria in the lead roles. The film, which hit the theaters yesterday and has largely been receiving negative reviews. But the film has encouraging box office figures on day 1 of release. The Tara-Riteish-Sidharth starrer, which opened on 2922 screens, earner Rs 7.03 cr. Marjaavaan clashed with Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Athiya Shetty’s Motichoor Chaknachoor.

Trade Analyst Taran Adarsh took to social media and Tweeted the numbers. His Tweet read, “#Marjaavaan posts respectable numbers on Day 1... Mass circuits / single screens very good, contribute to the total... Metro multiplexes ordinary... Multiplexes of Tier-2 + Tier-3 cities good... Should witness growth on Day 2 and 3... Fri ₹ 7.03 cr [2922 screens]. #India biz.” (ALSO READ: Motichoor Chaknachoor, Movie Review: BHEJA CHAKNACHOOR Would Have Been The Right Title For This Nawazuddin Siddiqui-Athiya Shetty Film)
