HTET 2019 allows religious symbols inside exam hall, check last minute instructions

For females, bindi’, ‘vermilion’ and religious belief symbols allowed inside the HTET exam. As many as 2,83,878 lakh candidates have applied for the HTET

16 Nov 2019, 07:07 by Careers Desk

As the Board of School Education Haryana (BSEH) has started to conduct the Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test (HTET) from November 16 and will conclude the same on November 17, 2019. Here is a list of important instructions for the candidates appearing for the teacher eligibility test. As many as 2,83,878 lakh candidates have applied for the HTET.

Reporting time: The candidates are to reach the examination centre two hours and 10 minutes before the start of the examination for the compulsory checking, as per the official instructions. The candidates should note that the entry to the examination hall will be closed an hour before the examination.
