Everything you should know about OnePlus Music Festival

International singers like Katy Perry and Dua Lipa will wow the audiences at the inaugural OnePlus Music Festival. Indian musicians like Amit Trivedi, Ritviz, The Local Train and Aswekeepsearching will also perform at the event.

16 Nov 2019, 07:33 by Entertainment Desk

The inaugural OnePlus Music Festival will kick off today. The one-day event will start from 2 pm. International singers like Katy Perry and Dua Lipa will wow the audiences at the event. Indian musicians like Amit Trivedi, Ritviz, The Local Train and Aswekeepsearching will also perform at the OnePlus Music Festival.

Talking about the OnePlus Music Festival, Katy had earlier said, “I am so happy to be returning to India and excited to headline my first-ever performance in Mumbai. I am looking forward to seeing and singing with all of my Indian KatyCats at the OnePlus Music Festival. I am also excited to watch some of the other people performing.”
