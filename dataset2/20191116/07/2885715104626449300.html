Lata Mangeshkar’s health is ‘progressing steadily’

Legendary singer Lata Mangeshkar was admitted to Breach Candy Hospital. As per the latest health update, the legendary singer's health is 'progressing steadily'.

16 Nov 2019, 07:23 by Entertainment Desk

Days after Lata Mangeshkar was hospitalised for breathlessness and lung infection, the singer is said to be “progressing steadily” as per a statement from her team.

On Saturday morning, a statement from her team read, “Lata di is fine and progressing steadily. Request members of the media to please not give vent to rumours. Thank You for standing by us.”

Another statement from her spokesperson Anusha Srinivasan Iyer read, “Lata Didi is doing good today.”

Another update by her team on Friday read, “Dear Friends. We are as happy as you are to inform you that with all your prayers and best wishes, Lata didi is doing much better. Thank You for being there. God is great.”

Lata Mangeshkar was admitted to Mumbai’s Breach Candy hospital in the early hours of Monday.

Hospital sources told PTI that the singer was admitted to the hospital after she complained of breathing difficulties.

While initial reports suggested Lata Mangeshkar is critical, her family dismissed the reports.
