Pakistan Opposition Party Protest on Major Highway

Protesters vowed to continue with their action until their demands were met.

16 Nov 2019, 07:39 by AP

Supporters of a hardline Islamist party on Friday blocked highways across the country in an effort to pressure Prime Minister Imran Khan to resign, accusing him of bad governance.

Members of the JUI (F) (Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam-Fazlur Rehman) party held the sit-in protest in the Hub area outside Karachi, organized by regional leader Rashid Soomro, which immediately brought the traffic to a halt.

Hub is the border city between Sindh and Balochistan, connecting Karachi to southern Balochistan via the RCD highway.
