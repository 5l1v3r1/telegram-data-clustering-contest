Woman fatally stabbed in Queens, suspect at large

16 Nov 2019, 07:37 by Joe Marino and Laura Italiano

A woman died after being repeatedly stabbed in Jamaica, Queens late Friday night, police said.

The attack came to light when a homeowner on 166th Street heard a woman crying for help outside, and called 911.

Arriving cops found the victim lying on the sidewalk with two stab wounds to her chest, a law enforcement source said.

The attacker remained at large Saturday morning, but was captured on sidewalk surveillance video fleeing the scene, the source said.

The victim was pronounced dead on arrival at Jamaica Hospital; her name had not been released by Saturday morning.
