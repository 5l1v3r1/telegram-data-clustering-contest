NBA wrap: Mills shines but Spurs still slump

16 Nov 2019, 07:15

Evan Fournier landed two late three-pointers as his 26-point haul led the Orlando Magic to a 111-109 NBA win over the San Antonio Spurs.

Australian guard Patty Mills' 13 points in 19 minutes could not save the Spurs, who led by 16 in the first half before Orlando regrouped.

The contest was tied at 104 apiece after a DeMar DeRozan lay-up with one minute and 32 seconds to play, before Fournier nailed his second key three-pointer to condemn the Spurs to a fourth-straight loss.
