10 young Premier League players you can expect to be a wonderkid in Football Manager 2020

Anyone can sign the world's best player with an unlimited budget, but convincing the next best thing in football to buy into your philosophy is the real challenge

16 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Jake Polden, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/jake-polden/

Finding wonderkids is what makes Football Manager such a challenge.

Anyone can sign the world's best player - if they're interested in your club, that is - with an unlimited budget, but unearthing the next best thing for a bargain price and transforming him into Lionel Messi is what it's all about.

We've been getting to grips with Football Manager's latest installment for 2020 and have found a smattering of wonderkids all over the globe.

We've also found a healthy amount in the Premier League, but for your convenience have whittled the number down to just 10 of the very best.

You'll have likely heard of most of these names, but they fit the wonderkid category given their age - and they just might revolutionise your side.

Mason Greenwood

Club: Manchester United

Position: Forward

Age: 18

Greenwood needs little introduction, despite his young age and the fact you'd expect him to be lost in the academy at this point in his career.

He's proven he knows where the back of the net is in the real world, and on the game he has the potential to turn into it's best forward.

Troy Parrott

Club: Tottenham

Position: Forward

Age: 17

Spurs supporters will know all about this young striker, who was recently called up to the Ireland national team.

In the game his stats are modest to begin with, but after a few seasons he'll be up there with the very best.

Eddie Nketiah

Club: Arsenal

Position: Forward

Age: 20

Currently on loan at Leeds, Nketiah is a proven goal scorer, but at 20 he's one of the older players on this list.

Due to being older, and the fact he plays for Arsenal, he has a bigger profile and will therefore be harder to sign. But it's worth the try.

Phil Foden

Club: Manchester City

Position: Midfielder

Age: 19

Foden is both well known and has won silverware in the real world, meaning he'll be very difficult to sign on the game.

If you can though, he'll transform into one of its best midfielders - perhaps even its best.

Hannibal Mejbri

Club: Manchester United

Position: Midfielder

Age: 16

He plays for Manchester United, but you may not have heard of this 16-year-old.

According to Football Manager, you will in the next few years, as the game has tipped the teenager to turn into a world-beater.

Harvey Elliott

Club: Liverpool

Position: Midfielder

Age: 16

Elliott is already remarkably well known for a 16-year-old, having represented both Fulham and Liverpool.

Reds fans have enjoyed watching the youngster in action in cup competitions this season, and you'll no doubt enjoy watching him flourish into your team's best player on FM.

Bukayo Saka

Club: Arsenal

Position: Forward

Age: 18

Another player who needs little introduction is Saka, who has burst onto the scene at Arsenal this season.

He'll cost a bomb, and the Gunners won't want to sell him, but he has some serious potential on the game.

Matteo Guendouzi

Club: Arsenal

Position: Midfielder

Age: 20

The player with the most Premier League experience on the list, and another man Arsenal will not want to part company with.

The good thing about Guendouzi, given how much football he has played at such a young age, is that you'll know exactly what he'll bring to your team from studying him in the real world.

Max Aarons

Club: Norwich

Position: Defender

Age: 19

London-born full-back Aarons doesn't play for a Big Six team - the shock.

But he's absolutely top drawer on Football Manager and a player you need to think about signing, especially as it's doable.

Gavin Bazunu

Club: Manchester City

Position: Goalkeeper

Age: 17

He plays for Manchester City, but you may not have heard of him.

City view the young Irish keeper as one for the future, so it might be a challenge trying to sign him. But if you can, he has the ability to stand between your sticks for the next 20 years!
