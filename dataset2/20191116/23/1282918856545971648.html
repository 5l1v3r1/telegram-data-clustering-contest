Aditya Roy Kapur's Birthday: Malang's First Glimpse Also Starring Disha Patani Unveiled

16 Nov 2019, 23:08 by Joel Kurian

Bollywood News

As Aditya Roy Kapur celebrated his 34th birthday, first glimpse of 'Malang' also starring Disha Patani was unveiled. The duo is seen enjoying a party in it.

Written By Joel Kurian | Mumbai | Updated On: November 16, 2019 23:08 IST

An announcement of a new film or a new poster/look is usually on the cards when a celebrity celebrates his/her birthday. More of a norm seen for stars down South, of late, even Bollywood is following this trend, making fans anticipate something special on their favourite stars’ special day. As Aditya Roy Kapur celebrated his 34th birthday, the first glimpse of his movie Malang was unveiled. The actor and co-star Disha Patani looked completely in love in the still, promising an interesting love story. 

READ: Disha Patani Wraps Up The Shoot Of Her Upcoming Movie 'Malang'

Trade analyst Taran Adarsh took to Twitter to share the glimpse. In the still, Aditya and Disha are seen enjoying themselves and dancing at what seemed like a beach party. Flaunting his six-pack abs, and wearing sunglasses, headband, and loose pants, Aditya is seen pointing to the sky with Disha, dressed in a white sleeveless top and blue floral skirt, in his arm.
