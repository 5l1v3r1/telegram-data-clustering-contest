Texas Fans Aren’t Happy With Tom Herman vs. Iowa State Tonight

16 Nov 2019, 23:32 by Dan Lyons

Texas wasn’t expected to beat Iowa State tonight. Jack Trice Stadium is a tough place to play, and Tom Herman’s Longhorns haven’t exactly separated themselves from the Big 12 pack so far this year.

ISU entered the game as a seven-point favorite. Through three quarters, the Cyclones led 20-7, though Texas just cut it to 20-14 to start the final frame.

With star quarterback Sam Ehlinger back, off of last year’s impressive season and Sugar Bowl win over Georgia, expectations were high for UT. Some probably underestimated what some of the other losses on the roster would mean.

Still, even with how good Matt Campbell has been in Ames, Texas fans expect to beat Iowa State every year.
