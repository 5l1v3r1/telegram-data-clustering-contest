Ewan McGregor faces handing ex-wife family’s £4million Hollywood mansion as part of £20million divorce deal

16 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Toby McDonald

EWAN McGregor faces handing his ex-wife the family’s £4million Hollywood mansion as part of their £20million divorce deal.

The Scots actor, 48, is cutting financial ties with Eve Mavrakis before their 23-year marriage is formally ended.

McGregor and Eve, 53, have been separating their British assets, with the Trainspotting star taking control of three jointly-owned companies.

But he is expected to sign over their six-bed LA home next door to Marilyn Monroe’s old house.

California Community Property laws state finances are split down the middle in a divorce, regardless of who earned the money.

US family law expert Kristina Otterstrom said the parent who usually looks after the children normally gets the family house.

The attorney said: “If you leave matters to a judge, the parent with custody of minor children will probably get to stay in the marital home.

“One exception may be if neither spouse can afford to keep the home. In that case, a court may come up with a different solution.”

McGregor has filed documents petitioning an LA judge to dissolve the marriage and declare the pair single before the divorce is finalised.

People magazine in the US claims he states in court papers “that a termination of the status of our marriage will assist in [the] resolution of the balance of this matter”.

The Star Wars actor is dating Mary Elizabeth Winstead, 34, his former co-star on TV show Fargo.

In October 2017 The Scottish Sun on Sunday exclusively revealed the pair were spotted kissing in a London cafe.

McGregor’s divorce has been contentious as he wants joint custody of he and Eve’s four children.

But former production designer Eve has responded by asking for sole custody with visitation rights for their superstar father.

The couple married in 1995 after meeting while working on legal TV drama Kavanagh QC.

They lived in a £6million house in St John’s Wood, London, before moving full time to the States, where they bought their 1930s LA property in 2002 for £1.5million.

Companies House records reveal that over the summer McGregor, from Crieff, Perthshire, became “a person with significant control” of three UK businesses the couple formed.

Eve admitted she was upset her husband has moved to end their marriage but said: “My main concern is our four children are OK.”

Last month McGregor admitted of his new relationship: “I am better. I am happier. I think that’s true.”

Eve has hired hotshot Hollywood lawyer Laura Wasser for the divorce.

The brief handled Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie’s split and wrote How to Divorce Without Destroying Your Family Or Bankrupting Yourself.

She has also represented screen idols including Ryan Reynolds, Heidi Klum and Ashton Kutcher, and says she tries to build a “new family” out of the divorce wreckage.

She says of her work: “We try to get them to a place where they are still a family, just not in a romantic relationship anymore — and to figure out a way of continuing to work together.”

McGregor’s publicist was contacted for comment.
