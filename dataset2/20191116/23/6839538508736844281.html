Man rescued after boat capsizes near Mornington

16 Nov 2019, 23:13

A man has been rescued from the water after his boat capsized near Mornington this morning. He has been taken to hospital for observation, with the boat yet to be salvaged.

Police responded to reports of the boat capsizing around 6:25am and were told “the boat went down” around 500m north east of the Mornington pier.

A man was found 300m offshore when he was rescued by a local boat operator.

He was not injured during the incident and was taken to hospital for observation – efforts will be made later today to salvage the boat from the water.
