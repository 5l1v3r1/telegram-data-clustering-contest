Hazard Stars As Belgium Thrash Russia

16 Nov 2019, 23:55 by SL International

Russia 1-4 Belgium

Belgium, who have already qualified, should Russia no mercy after romping to a 4-1 victory at the Saint-Petersburg Stadium.

Eden Hazard bagged a brace, while Thorgan Hazard and Romelu Lukaku completed the rout for the Red Devils.

Georgi Dzhikiya bagged the consolation for Russia, who have also already qualified for the tournament.

Northern Ireland 0-0 Netherlands

The Netherlands have secured their ticket to next year's tournament, despite playing to a goalless draw at Northern Ireland.

Germany's win over Belarus means the Dutch have dropped to second spot in Group C.

Azerbaijan 0-2 Wales

Wales kept their EURO 2020 hopes alive with a 2-0 victory away to Azerbaijan on Saturday evening.

Kieffer Moore opened the scoring after 10 minutes, before Harry Wilson got the second goal for Ryan Giggs' charges after 34 minutes.

Wales can qualify automatically if they beat Hungary on Tuesday evening.

Croatia 3-1 Slovakia

Croatia qualified for the European Championship after rallying from behind to defeat Slovakia 3-1 at the Stadion HNK Rijeka.

Nikola Vlasic, Bruno Petkovic and Ivan Perisic netted for Croatia after the visitors had taken a first-half lead through Robert Bozenkik.

Israel 1-2 Poland

Poland cemented top spot in Group G with a 2-1 triumph away to Israel on Saturday night.

With qualification already secured, Grzegorz Krychowiak and Krzysztof Piatek got the goals to secure victroy for the visitors. Munas Dabour got the late consolation for the hosts.
