Brian Herrien, Jake Fromm Said A Prayer For The Injured Photographer During Halftime

16 Nov 2019, 23:15 by Matt Lombardi

Just before halftime of the Auburn-Georgia game, we had a scary situation at Jordan-Hare Stadium. Bulldogs running back Brian Herrien, after a short pass, we pushed out of bounds into a Georgia student photographer. She was knocked unconscious.

She came to a few minutes later, but needed to be carted off the field and taken by ambulance to the hospital. It looked like she was able to speak when leaving the stadium.

The woman has since been identified as Georgia student Chamberlain Smith. CBS Sports sideline reporter Jamie Erdahl provided an update on her situation.

Erdahl also noted that at halftime, Herrien and teammate Jake Fromm said a prayer in the locker room for Smith’s recovery.
