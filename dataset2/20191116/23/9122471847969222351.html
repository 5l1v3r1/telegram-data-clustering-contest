Video: Chris Olave Makes Catch Of The Season vs. Rutgers

16 Nov 2019, 23:08 by Chris Rosvoglou

There are still a few weeks left in the season, but we may have witnessed the catch of the year during the Ohio State-Rutgers game. Chris Olave made such a great play that even the opposing fans were in awe of his playmaking ability.

Ohio State entered today’s matchup as overwhelming favorites. It didn’t take very long for the Big Ten powerhouse to jump out to a 35-7 lead before halftime.

Despite having such a big lead in the third quarter, head coach Ryan Day decided to keep his starters in the game.

Justin Fields threw an absolute bomb down the right sideline to Chris Olave, who somehow managed to catch the ball between his own legs.

What makes it such an impressive catch by Olave is that he caught it with two defenders in his vicinity.
