Raiders’ Vontaze Burfict Rips NFL, Calls Roger Goodell A ‘Total B-tch’ For 12-Game Suspension

16 Nov 2019, 02:58 by Darrelle Lincoln

Vontaze Burfict must want his career to be over, because calling out a commissioner is not a good way to get back into his good graces.

The Oakland Raiders linebacker was speaking to The Athletic’s Vic Tafur and he did not mince words when he referred to Roger Goodell as a “total b—h” when the two met in New York and the commissioner upheld his 12-game suspension for a helmet-to-helmet hit on Indianapolis Colts tight end Jack Doyle.
