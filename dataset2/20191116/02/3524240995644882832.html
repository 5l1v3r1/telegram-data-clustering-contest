Eagles future is bright –Enyeama

16 Nov 2019, 02:01 by Kelvin Ekerete

Kelvin Ekerete

Former Super Eagles goalkeeper, Vincent Enyeama, has wished Nigeria good luck when they face Lesotho in their 2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualifier on Sunday in Maseru.
The former Lille goalie was at the camp of the Eagles at the Ibom Golf Resort to motivate the goalkeepers ahead of the showdown with the Crocodiles.
Enyeama who is Nigeria’s most capped goal-keeper has disclosed that the future is bright for the team with the influx of talented youths in the team.
“What an honour to share this moment with the Super Eagles at the Ibom Golf Hotel and Resorts. Wishing them the very best in their next match. The future is bright for Nigerian football,” stated while a posting a picture of himself alongside the three goalkeepers as well as the goalkeeper trainer, Aloy Agu on his instagram handle.
Daniel Akpeyi, also disclosed his admiration for the former Enyimba goalkeeper, “Thanks for coming around Vincent Enyeama, you are always an inspiration. African best till date,” he wrote on Instagram.
