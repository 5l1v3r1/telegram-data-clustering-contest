ESPN’s Lee Corso Isn’t Changing His Prediction For Alabama

16 Nov 2019, 15:42 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday morning, ESPN’s College GameDay kicked off from Waco, Texas ahead of a huge came with College Football Playoff implications.

No. 13 Baylor hosts No. 10 Oklahoma in a battle of Big 12 programs looking for a conference title. Before the day’s action kicks off, the GameDay crew broke down which teams can make the playoff.

Lee Corso argued that, despite losing to LSU, Alabama will still make the playoff. He suggested a one-loss Pac-12 champion will still be behind Alabama in the final College Football Playoff rankings.

“A one-loss Alabama is still in the playoff. That’s my story and I’m sticking to it,” Corso said.
