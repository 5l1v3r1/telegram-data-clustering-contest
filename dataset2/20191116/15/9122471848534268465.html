Huskers Insider Has Update On Nebraska’s Wan’Dale Robinson

16 Nov 2019, 15:15 by Andrew McCarty

Wan’Dale Robinson, Nebraska’s talented freshman receiver, is one of the most electric players in the country. It wasn’t surprising to see him as a finalist for the Paul Hornung award – given to the nation’s most versatile player.

However, he hasn’t seen the field in recent weeks.

Robinson left Nebraska’s last game, the Week 10 loss to Purdue, with a leg injury. Earlier this week, offensive coordinator Troy Walters called him “very questionable” for this weekend’s game against Wisconsin.

The star wide receiver doesn’t appear to be on track to play this afternoon. According to a report from Nebraska insider Andy Kendeigh, Robinson was not on the team buses before today’s game.
