Barnstaple road closure set to cause town centre traffic chaos

A diversion will be in place for five days

16 Nov 2019, 15:47 by Joel Cooper

A road closure in the centre of a Devon town is likely to cause traffic and delays for a whole week.

Devon County Council has revealed the B3149 Rolle Street in Barnstaple will shut every day from Monday, November 18 to Friday, November 22.

The road, which is a main thoroughfare to Barnstaple from the Braunton direction, is being closed by to allow South West Water to renew a ataer pipe in the area.

The council says during the closure that "delays are likely".

A diversion will be in place taking motorists along Fair View and Pilton Causeway.

However, as many motorists have pointed out on social media, Pilton Causeway is already part of another diversion route due to the closure of nearby Derby Road - which is also undergoing work from South West Water until January 31, 2020.

North Devon Council took to Facebook to share the news - and many people were not happy.

One person said: "What an earth are Devon County Council thinking of to allow SWW to do this??!! Pilton Causeway is already on a diversion route because Derby Road is closed.

"Barnstaple fire station and the NDDH is at the end of the diversion route - how the heck is that going to work in a major emergency (or even a minor one) at a peak time?"

Another person added: "Fair view already like a car park at busy times. That'll be me walking too and from work then as the other road closure has caused this. That's ridiculous."

One commenter said: "Fantastic, more traffic past my house, what with Derby road closed for four months now this road!
