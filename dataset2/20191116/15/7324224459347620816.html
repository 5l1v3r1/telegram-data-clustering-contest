Two men arrested at huge cannabis factory in Wednesbury

Two men have been charged after police raided a huge cannabis factory in Wednesbury containing 417 plants.

16 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Jamie Brassington

The haul was uncovered at an address on Hollies Drive on Thursday.

West Midlands Police arrested Klodian Ndoca, aged 31, and Arjan Nikollaj, 25, inside the property.

They appeared at Walsall Magistrates Court on Friday, charged with the production of cannabis.
