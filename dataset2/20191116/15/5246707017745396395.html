Final Fantasy VII And VIII Remastered Physical Twin Pack Expected To Be Released Soon

The wait is nearly over

16 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Liam Doolan

The Final Fantasy VII and Final Fantasy VIII Remastered Switch twin pack physical release that was announced exclusively for Southeast Asia in September has reportedly been dated.

It's expected to become available on 29th November and is available for pre-order right now. In saying this, a listing over on Play Asia suggests it still won't be shipping until December. Either way, Square Enix Asia has said it's "coming in 2019" – so at the very least, consider this post to be a friendly reminder that it's arriving soon.

Below is a look at the physical version's artwork:

As previously revealed on the Square Enix Asia Facebook page, this two-in-one hard copy will contain English and Japanese language support. Here's the original announcement:

For Asia, ‘FINAL FANTASY VII’ and ‘FINAL FANTASY VIII Remastered’ are coming to Nintendo Switch in one physical package!

The physical announcement made in September follows on from the news in August when Square Enix's Japanese website and Play Asia reportedly confirmed VIII Remastered would be a digital-only release.
