WATCH: Kanye West performs in a Texas county jail with his 'Sunday Service' choir

16 Nov 2019, 15:36 by John Gage

Music superstar Kanye West performed with his "Sunday Service" choir at a Texas county jail ahead of his appearance at Joel Osteen's church.

The artist performed his new album Jesus is King on Friday in front of over 200 male prisoners before a second performance for a smaller crowd of female prisoners, according to CBS 11.
