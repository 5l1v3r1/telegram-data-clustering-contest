Odds Released For Which NFL Team Could Sign Colin Kaepernick

16 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Matt Lombardi

Colin Kaepernick, who has been out of the NFL for three years, has a workout in front of at least 25 teams on Saturday. There are now odds out regarding which teams could sign him in the aftermath.

BetOnline has odds for 17 different teams that could sign Kaepernick. The Cincinnati Bengals, the only winless team in the NFL, have the best odds at +350.

The Pittsburgh Steelers, who are currently starting backup Mason Rudolph because of an injury to Ben Roethlisberger, have the next-best odds at +400.
