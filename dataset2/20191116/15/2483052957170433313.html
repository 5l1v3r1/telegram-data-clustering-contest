NDP leader Notley addressing party members Saturday

16 Nov 2019, 15:32 by Laine Mitchell, CTV News Edmonton

EDMONTON – Alberta opposition leader Rachel Notley is set to address NDP members at a bi-annual council in Edmonton Saturday.

Notley’s address will take place at 10 a.m. at the Northern Alberta Institute of Technology.

Notley is expected to to discuss the NDP's first few months in opposition and a so-called UCP expense scandal.
