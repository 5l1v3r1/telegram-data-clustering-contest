India blow away Bangladesh inside 3 days to win first Test

The Virat Kohli-led unit crushed Bangladesh by an innings and 130 runs to go 1-0 up in the two-match series.

16 Nov 2019, 15:10 by Sports Desk

Continuing their winning momentum in the longest format of the game, India kicked off their two-match Test series against Bangladesh with an emphatic victory. Dominating the visitors in both the departments of the game, the Virat Kohli-led unit crushed their opponent by an innings and 130 runs to wrap the contest in just three days. With this win, India have now taken a 1-0 lead in the two-match series.

After piling a gigantic 493/6 in response to Bangladesh’s 150 on Day 2, skipper Virat Kohli declared the innings and invited Bangladesh openers Shadman Islam and Imrul Kayes to kick-off the proceedings on the third day. Starting the day 343 runs behind the hosts, Bangladesh got off to a dreadful start, losing both the openers early in the first hour.
