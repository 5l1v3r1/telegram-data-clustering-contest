Man arrested following reports of robbery in Lenton Abbey store

The shopkeeper was left shaken by the incident, police said

16 Nov 2019, 15:29 by Matthew Bunn

A man has been arrested in connection with a robbery at a shop in Lenton Abbey.

Police were called to a One Stop Express store in Woodside Road just before 8am on Friday, November 15.

A man went into the shop and tried to leave with items without paying. The man was challenged by the shopkeeper and a brief scuffle then took place before he left the store, according to Nottinghamshire Police .

The shopkeeper, who police say was shaken after the incident, sustained minor injuries.

A 33-year-old man was arrested on Friday on suspicion. He remains in police custody.
