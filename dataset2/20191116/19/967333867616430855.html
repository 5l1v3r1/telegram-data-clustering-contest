Dare to Play? Fearless Kids Use Dead Snake as Jump Rope

16 Nov 2019, 19:00

They say kids these days are only interested in mobile phones and other devices and cannot entertain themselves. Well, these kids in Vietnam definitely know how to have fun in a very ingenious (and a little bit scary) way.

Five kids from Vietnam have been filmed playing a jump rope. This sounds pretty normal, doesn't it? Well, it would be if it wasn't for the fact that the jump rope is a huge snake!

At least the poor serpent has already passed away, but it doesn't make the game the kids are playing any less terrifying.
