David Pollack Crushes Nick Saban For Keeping Tua Tagovailoa In The Game Up 35-7

16 Nov 2019, 19:21 by Matt Lombardi

Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa appeared to suffer a significant hip injury in the second quarter of his team’s game against Mississippi State. Many, including ESPN’s David Pollack, are criticizing Crimson Tide head coach Nick Saban for still having Tagovailoa in the game.

The injury came just a few minutes before halftime and Alabama leading 35-7. Pollack took to Twitter to publicly call out Saban for having Tagovailoa in the game. He doesn’t think the move made any sense.
