Alabama Suffers Another Major Injury vs. Mississippi State

16 Nov 2019, 19:05 by Andrew Holleran

Alabama is rolling against Mississippi State this afternoon, but today’s likely victory for the Crimson Tide is probably coming at a big cost. Nick Saban’s program has suffered a couple of major injuries.

Quarterback Tua Tagovailoa had to be carted off the field with an apparent hip injury at the end of the first half. It didn’t look good.

Tagovailoa isn’t the only standout Alabama player to be carted off the field, unfortunately.

Alabama star defensive lineman Raekwon Davis had to be carted off the field with an apparent leg injury.
