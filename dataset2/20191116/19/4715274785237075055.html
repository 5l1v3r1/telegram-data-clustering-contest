E-waste

16 Nov 2019, 19:38 by Manny Palmero

Residents of Barangay West Triangle in Quezon City bring their broken or discarded equipment to a collection event organized by the barangay council to promote awareness and support for the ‘Safe PCB and E-Waste Management’ project by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources-Environmental Management Bureau to protect public health and the environment against harmful chemicals in electronic waste.
