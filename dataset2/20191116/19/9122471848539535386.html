Video: It’s Getting Heated Between Michigan, Michigan State

16 Nov 2019, 19:11 by Chris Rosvoglou

Things are getting chippy in Ann Arbor as Michigan and Michigan State are halfway through their matchup this afternoon.

When two rivals meet in college football, it’s wise to expect tempers to flare. It’s not just about winning another conference game, the winner gets bragging rights for an entire year.

Even though Michigan State has struggled over the past four weeks, Mark Dantonio’s team is giving it all they have against Michigan.

Toward the end of the first half, Michigan defensive lineman Michael Dwumfour and Michigan State tight end Matt Seybert found themselves in a shoving match.

Nothing major came out of this exchange, but the officiating crew is now on edge for the rest of the game.
