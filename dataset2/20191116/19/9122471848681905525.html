Clemson QB Trevor Lawrence Takes Incredible Photo With Fan

16 Nov 2019, 19:15 by Andrew McCarty

A few weeks ago, a teenage girl went viral for a video she posted on TikTok.

Why? Well, she looks a LOT like star Clemson quarterback Trevor Lawrence. She posted a video mimicking Lawrence’s poses from a few different photos and the two showed a unique resemblance.

The two later spoke over a video chat, but hadn’t met in person.

Until today.

Lawrence and his female doppelganger caught up before Clemson’s game against Wake Forest on Saturday afternoon.
