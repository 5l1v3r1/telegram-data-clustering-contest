Mesmerizing photo of leaping rabbits wins top nature photo award

16 Nov 2019, 19:46 by Melissa Klein

These bunnies are top dogs.

A snap of a pair of leaping rabbits won top honors in an annual contest for Nature Photographer of the Year.

Csaba Daroczi took the winning photo at dawn near the Hungarian village of Bocsa.

He bested 14,000 entrants from 73 countries to get the prize of $3,300.
