'Frightened' - Birmingham City great reveals interesting story behind Blue's iconic win over Aston Villa

Latest Birmingham City news | Horsfield was part of Blues team which won 3-0 at St Andrew’s in September 2002 and has recalled how Steve Bruce motivated his men with an Olof Mellberg interview

16 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Brian Dick, https://www.facebook.com/blueswriterbriandick/?fref=ts, Mark Wakefield

Birmingham City icon Geoff Horsfield has revealed the impact comments made by former Aston Villa man Olof Mellberg had on Blues as they claimed a legendary victory over their city rivals.

Horsfield made 126 appearances for Blues between 2000-03, scoring 29 goals and helped the club secure promotion to the Premier League.

In a superb interview with Undr The Cosh podcast the ex-Blues and West Brom striker discusses his relationship with Trevor Francis, the celebrations after winning promotion at the Millennium Stadium - and the first Second City clash in 16 years.

Horsfield, who scored in two games against Villa, was part of the team which won 3-0 at St Andrew’s in September 2002 and he has recalled how then boss Steve Bruce motivated his men following an interview given by the Villa defender.
