Winners and Bushtucker trials - all the odds for I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here

Who is favourite to win, and to be picked the most for a trial

16 Nov 2019, 19:02 by Tristan Cork

Arsenal and England football legend Ian Wright has been installed as the early favourite to win I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here, as the programme launches this weekend.

The striker and TV pundit has been given short odds of 5/2 by bookies William Hill, making him the head-and-shoulders-favourite ahead of all those other celebs about to go into the Australian jungle with Ant and Dec.

The programme launches on Sunday evening, with bushtucker trials, stars, camps, waterfalls and arguments over cooking and flies in store.

The second favourite is giant former England rugby player James Haskell, at 5/1, with EastEnders actress Jacqueline Jossa - who played Lauren Branning - joint third favourite at 6/1 alongside radio presenter Roman Kemp.

Those William Hill odds in full are:

Ian Wright - 5/2

James Haskell - 5/1

Jacqueline Jossa - 6/1

Roman Kemp - 6/1

Caitlyn Jenner - 9/1

Kate Garraway - 11/1

Myles Stephenson - 12/1

Nadine Coyle - 14/1

Andrew Maxwell - 16/1

Adele Roberts - 25/1

But while Ian Wright is the favourite to win, he’s also the favourite to be voted to take part in the most Bushtucker Trials, at 2/1, just ahead of Nadine Coyle and Caitlyn Jenner.

The American athlete, model and trans pioneer is also favourite to win the first star in the first publicly voted bushtucker trial - hinting that bookies think the public are going to go for her and Ian Wright early on.
