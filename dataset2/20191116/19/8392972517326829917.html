Aldi shoppers reckon candy floss and marshmallow gin tastes like 'magic in a bottle'

It is shimmery too!

16 Nov 2019, 19:27 by Rebecca Carter

Aldi is selling a shimmery pink candy floss and marshmallow gin for £9.99.

Gin lovers out there will have to be quick to bag a bottle though as they are limited release from the supermarket.

One shopper shared a video of the gin to Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK and fellow fans are going wild.

One person commented: "Okay I need this," while another said: "I need to try this sometime!"

People who have tried the liqueur have been quick to share their thoughts on social media.

We recently revealed that Aldi is selling a gin selection box for Christmas this year.

Instead of the traditional bars of chocolate in your Christmas selection box, this year you can go boozy and unbox a selection of gins.

The 12 Gins Of Christmas Selection Box is limited edition and only on sale while stocks last.
