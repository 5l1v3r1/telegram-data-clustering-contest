Bill Belichick Isn’t Concerning Himself With Colin Kaepernick’s Workout: ‘I’m Focused On The Eagles’

16 Nov 2019, 19:07 by Darrelle Lincoln

I’m not sure why reporters keep trying to get answers out of Bill Belichick after all these years, but they keep trying and he keeps turning them down in the same way.

While the New England Patriots are among the teams committed to attending Colin Kaepernick’s workout on Saturday, the head coach of the team hasn’t given much thought to Kaepernick or his workout whatsoever.

“I don’t really have any comment on that,” Belichick said Friday morning. “We’re trying to get ready for the Eagles. That’s really where my focus is.”
