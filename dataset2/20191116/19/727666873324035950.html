Kanye West Goes To Jail In Houston - To Spread The Gospel

16 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Shirley Ju

The rap star took his Sunday Service to the Harris County Jail to spread his brand of Christianity.

(AllHipHop News) At this point, Kanye West is unpredictable.

Fresh off the release of his new album Jesus Is King, the Chicago native hasn’t missed a beat with his weekly Sunday Services.

On Friday morning, Ye brought his gospel choir to Harris County Jail in Houston to give prisoners an unforgettable show.

Law enforcement sources state the whole thing was a surprise to nearly everyone involved.

Kanye and his singers stayed for several hours and performed a wide range of cuts for the inmates and staff.

This comes just two days before he meets with Joel Osteen on Sunday at Osteen's Lakewood Church. Service will include a night performance of Kanye and his Sunday Service choir.

TMZ reports everyone on Osteen's team has been working relentlessly behind the scenes, partnering with Ticketmaster to ensure both fans and churchgoers have the opportunity to catch the show.

Kanye brought out 100 choir and band members and delivered two separate shows: one for the male prisoners and one for the female prisoners.
