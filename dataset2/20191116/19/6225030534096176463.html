High Velocity scoots onto Derby lists with Bob Hope Stakes score

16 Nov 2019, 19:12 by Jonathan Lintner

High Velocity went from maiden special weight winner to an unbeaten stakes horse to watch from the Bob Baffert barn on Saturday, prevailing in what was essentially a match race edition of the Grade 3, $100,000 Bob Hope.

Strongconstitution, the 4-5 favorite, pressed outside of High Velocity until those two hit the top of the stretch evenly. But under jockey Drayden Van Dyke, even-money High Velocity had full measure of his rival, edging away late.

“He handled everything well for his second out," Baffert said. "It’s always a tough test after you break a maiden, and he didn’t have a lot of time between races. But the way he worked, we thought he would run well.

"I liked the way that when that other horse came to him he dug in and took off again. And that’s a nice horse that ran second.”

With a final time of 1:23.16 for the seven furlongs, High Velocity positioned himself to hit the Kentucky Derby trail similar to Baffert’s 2018 Bob Hope winner, Mucho Gusto, who’s multiple Grade 1-placed.

Behind Strongconstitution, Zimba Warrior and Rager completed the running order.
