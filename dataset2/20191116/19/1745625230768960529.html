Blond bombshell Anna Nystrom tantalized her 8.4 million Instagram followers with another sizzling hot snap that showcased her insane curves.

In the picture, Anna leaned against a counter in an all-white kitchen. The kitchen had stark white cupboards and drawers, and a marble backsplash to finish off the luxurious space. The drawer pulls were strips of deep brown leather, and there were three drawers on either side of Anna in the shot.

The stunner from Sweden rocked a white mini dress that left little to the imagination. The dress was crafted from a ribbed material that clung to every inch of her curves, and had a slight ruffle detail along the neckline and hem. A row of buttons went down the front of the dress, adding an interesting visual detail to an otherwise fairly simple look. The dress had long sleeves that covered up Anna’s arms, but the short hem meant that her toned legs were on full display.

The fit of the dress flaunted her hourglass physique, and though no cleavage was visible in the relatively closed neckline, her ample assets were still visible and tantalized her fans. While Anna usually leaves her blond locks down in gentle curls, for this snap she had her hair done in two tight braids, with a few strands of blond hair framing her face.

She looked off into the distance in the snap, which she revealed in the caption was a paid partnership with online retailer Fashion Nova. Her followers couldn’t get enough of the sizzling hot look, and the post quickly racked up over 21,200 likes within just 35 minutes.

Many of her followers couldn’t seem to find the words to express how they felt about the look, and instead they opted to leave a string of emoji in the comments section. Several fans did leave praise for the blond bombshell, though.

“You are so so unbelievably gorgeous Anna. Love the outfit,” one fan said.
