Dirty tricks: Feces being smeared on bike handles across Washington DC

16 Nov 2019, 19:44 by John Gage

Washington, D.C.'s Capital Bikeshare is having to clean up some bikes after a vandal smeared poop under the handlebars for unsuspecting riders to find.

"Capital Bikeshare takes any incidence of vandalism very seriously," Capital Bikeshare told the DCist this week. "The affected bikes have been removed from service and thoroughly inspected and cleaned by staff who are trained in hazmat recovery and removal."

Several D.C. residents have reported poop being smeared on bikes in the U Street Corridor for the past two weeks, taking to social media to complain.
