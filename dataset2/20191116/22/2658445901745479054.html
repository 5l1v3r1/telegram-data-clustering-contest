Soludo, others for Afrinvest's 2019 banking sector report launch

16 Nov 2019, 22:24

Our Reporter

 

Professor Charles Chukwuma Soludo (Former CBN Governor and current member of the Economic Advisory Council to the President) will be the distinguished guest of honour at the launch of Afrinvest’s 2019 Banking Sector Report scheduled for Monday, 18 November 2019, at Lagos Oriental Hotel, Victoria Island, by 12noon.

The report, with the theme, ‘Beyond the Precipice…Pulling Back from the Brink,’ will focus on the structural reforms needed to achieve strong economic growth and prosperity as well as the reforms required to improve performance in key sectors of the Nigerian economy.
