Road blockades by JUI-F continue on third successive day

16 Nov 2019, 22:55 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

PESHAWAR (Dunya News) – The Plan B of Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam-Fazal’s (JUI-F) Azadi March has been ongoing as party workers continue to block roads on third consecutive day (Saturday) in parts of country.

According to details, JUI-F has staged a sit-in on Nowshera GT Road in Hakimabad. Traffic has been diverted on alternative routes following the blockage. Tents of the protesters have fallen due to rain in Malakand.

The sit-in has entered its third day on the Indus Highway in Bannu disrupting the traffic. Demonstrators have blocked the national highway in Kandhkot and are present in large numbers in Ghotki.

In Balochistan, the JUI-F workers have staged sit-ins in Zhob and other parts of the province. The traffic flow has been interrupted by the protests.
