Camera Lady Gets KO’d During Auburn-Georgia Game, Gets Stretchered Out (VIDEO)

16 Nov 2019, 22:25 by Darrelle Lincoln

With less than 30 seconds in the first half game between the Auburn Tigers and Georgia Bulldogs, a play that went out of bounds caught a camera lady unexpectedly and it ended badly for her.

She was immediately knocked out and didn’t move for several minutes before medical personnel put her on a stretcher and took her to an awaiting ambulance.
