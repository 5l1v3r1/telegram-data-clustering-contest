Celebrity solicitor Gerald Kean hints he has found love with mystery woman after split with ex Sonya Valadi

The TV personality, who was once engaged to socialite Lisa Murphy, has given the impression he is once again off the market

16 Nov 2019, 22:38 by Brenda Dennehy

Celebrity solicitor Gerald Kean has hinted he has found love with a mystery woman following his split with ex Sonya Valadi in September.

The TV personality, who was once engaged to socialite Lisa Murphy, has given the impression he is once again off the market.

He told the Irish Sunday Mirror: “There will be someone under the mistletoe this Christmas.”

Earlier this year, Gerald ruled out rekindling his romance with former fiancee Lisa and has now confirmed the couple are no longer on speaking terms.

He said: “Lisa and I no longer speak, we haven’t spoken in two years.” While the 62-year-old has a trusting relationship with most of his exes, he is more than aware when he is being pursued for his high profile.

Gerald added: “I am cautious when I am getting into a new relationship.

“I have met many women who approach me for all the wrong reasons. I cop on to it immediately.”

“Initially I go for looks, let’s be honest, they are the initial attraction.

“I love being in the company of a woman as they are so easy to talk to.

“They are sharper than men and more intelligent.

“All the people I trust in my business life are women.

“They are calmer, cooler – they see the whole picture and are more focused.”
