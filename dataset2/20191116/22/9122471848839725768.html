LeBron James Sends Message To Colin Kaepernick Before His Workout

16 Nov 2019, 22:33 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, ESPN NFL insider Adam Schefter revealed the location of Colin Kaepernick’s workout for NFL scouts changed last minute.

Initially the workout was set to happen at the Atlanta Falcons training facility. However, just over an hour before the workout was scheduled to begin, Kaepernick’s camp decided to move the location.

They released a statement revealing the decision for the change. The NFL did not want media to attend and film the workout, so Kaepernick changed the location to allow media and the fans to see the workout for themselves.

Before the workout start, NBA star LeBron James sent a message to Kaepernick.

“Best of luck to Colin Kaepernick in his workout today! Pulling for you over here Young King!!! Regardless the outcome you’re SPECIAL and I thank you for being TRUE!” James said in the tweet.
