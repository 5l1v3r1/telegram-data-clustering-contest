College football best of Week 12: Scores, Top 25 analysis, Gophers lose, Sooners stun, Georgia wins SEC East

16 Nov 2019, 22:33 by ESPN staff

Week 12 of the college football season brought with it a full slate of Top 25 games and plenty of drama, including the first loss of the season for both Minnesota and Baylor. The news, analysis and best moments of the day are covered right here.

Injury updates and latest news

  • Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa, was carted off the field with an injury in the second quarter of Saturday's game against Mississippi State. He has a dislocated right hip and will miss the rest of the season, according to sources.
  • Notre Dame's streak of 273 straight home sellouts, second nationally only to Nebraska's 373 straight, ended with Saturday's game against Navy.
  • South Carolina athletic director Ray Tanner gave his coach a vote of confidence, saying "I want to make it clear that Will Muschamp is our football coach and will be our coach going forward."

Best of the week

Like looking in a mirror

Bella Martina, a 16-year-old Georgia high school student who went viral with her re-creations of Trevor Lawrence photos, met the Clemson QB. "That one girl actually does look a lot like me so it's kind of crazy," Lawrence said previously. He was right.
