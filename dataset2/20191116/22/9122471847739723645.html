Michigan Releases Video Of Locker Room Celebration vs. Michigan State

16 Nov 2019, 22:48 by Chris Rosvoglou

The Michigan Wolverines made a statement this weekend as they dismantled the Michigan State Spartans. Following the game, Jim Harbaugh and his players threw quite the celebration.

Michigan received a dominant performance from Shea Patterson, who threw for 384 yards and four touchdowns.

All the criticism that Michigan dealt with earlier in the season is officially gone. Harbaugh has the team playing its best football in recent memory.

Even though Michigan State has struggled this season, Michigan was thrilled to pick up a win over its in-state rival.

While addressing his team in the locker room, Harbaugh yelled “Who’s got it better than us?”

Once the coaching staff was done speaking to the players, they brought out the Paul Bunyan Trophy and allowed the locker room to go crazy for a few minutes.
