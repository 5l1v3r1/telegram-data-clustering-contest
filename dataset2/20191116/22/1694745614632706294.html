Lili Reinhart Shares Some of Her Most Hilarious Drunk Thoughts With a Giggly Jimmy Fallon

16 Nov 2019, 22:50 by Chanel Vargas

A drunken night out with friends often results in some truly hilarious quotes (if you can remember them), and Lili Reinhart wasn't afraid to share a few of her own during an interview on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon on Thursday night. Reinhart — who was on the show to reflect on her time as Betty Cooper on the CW's Riverdale — started by recounting one of her first drunken nights out in LA when the show began. "I thought that I was being so philosophical, or saying all of these revolutionary things that I needed to remember," she recalled.

Fallon then went on to read some of her "not sober" thoughts, including, "If you have a pet goat and you didn't name in Billy, who the hell are you?"
