Victim in late-night stabbing identified as Queens teen

16 Nov 2019, 22:48 by Ruth Weissmann

A 17-year-old Queens teen died after being stabbed multiple times in a late-night attack, police said Saturday.

Responders were tipped off to the killing by a 911 caller who heard the teenager crying out for helping outside a house on 166th Street near 116th Ave in Jamaica later Friday night, authorities said.

When they arrived, they found Talasia Cuffie, of Queensbridge, lying on the sidewalk with two stab wounds to her chest, law enforcement sources said.

She was rushed to Jamaica Hospital, where she was pronounced dead.

Her attacker remained at large Saturday afternoon, and no arrests have been made.
