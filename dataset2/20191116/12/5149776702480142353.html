Arrest made after reports a man armed with a sledgehammer demanded cash from shop

The man remains in custody

16 Nov 2019, 12:55 by Matthew Bunn

An arrest has been made following reports a man armed with a sledgehammer went into a shop in Bulwell and demanded cash.

A police dog and its handler were sent to the Local 4U store in Hucknall Lane just after 9pm on Friday, November 15.

According to Nottinghamshire Police, a man threatened staff and demanded money from the shop till. He left with cash.

The police dog team tracked down a 33-year-old man and he was arrested on suspicion of robbery. A sledgehammer and cash were recovered.

A door was damaged during the incident but no shop staff were injured.

The man remains in custody.
