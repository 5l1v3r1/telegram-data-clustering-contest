Andy Cohen Announces New Addition To ‘Real Housewives’ Franchise: ‘Real Housewives Of Salt Lake City’

16 Nov 2019, 12:40 by Brent Furdyk

Get ready for another group of table-flipping, backstabbing “Real Housewives” with the announcement of a new addition to the franchise — from one of America’s least-likeliest locations.

As the Daily Mail reports, Andy Cohen — who once served as Bravo’s executive VP of development and talent and remains exec producer of all the “Housewives” shows — is hosting the first-ever Bravocon, currently underway in New York City.

Speaking to fans from the stage, the “Watch What Happens Live” host revealed the cable channel’s plans for “The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City”.

“We are very excited to travel to Salt Lake City, Utah! The Real Housewives of SLC!” he announced to fans.

“I have to tell you, in Utah, you’ve got the majesty of the mountains. you’ve got the majesty of the Mormon religion, you’ve got an exclusive community of people who have very successful businesses who live in their own universe,” he added. “It is gorgeous and I think you’re going to be really surprised and intrigued by the group of women we’ve found.”

Ahead of the announcement, Cohen and Bravo teased the news on Twitter.
