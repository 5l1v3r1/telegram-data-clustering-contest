Christmas may be more than a month away but this little girl’s long list of items for the festive day has left all quite shocked online.

Christmas wish-list put out by little children may be an innocent way to ask Santa for things they simply want as presents from parents, but this 10-year-old girl’s list with scores of expensive items has left people divided on Twitterverse.

Some of the requests were pretty standard for her age such as dolls and bunnies but things went a bit far when she included extravagant gifts like an iPhone 11 and a Macbook Air. And if that wasn’t enough she cheekily also included whopping $4,000 in her list of demands!

But what really baffled many online were items like “Gucci slides”, “Chanel purse” and a “Hydro Flask”!
