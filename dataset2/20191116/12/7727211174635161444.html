"My loves, if you’ve got it, flaunt it!" - and it seems she always has

16 Nov 2019, 12:58 by Rebecca Koncienzcy

Kim Woodburn has quite a cult following that has been amplified a little more this year when she signed up to Twitter and Instagram .

Fans were blown away recently when the 77-year-old posted a snap of her toned legs as she said: "My loves, if you’ve got it, flaunt it! (And I’ve got it in truck loads!)"

Many of her 22,000 followers commented under the photo of her in a white gown that she had hitched up top reveal her tanned pins.

One, @BeckijoArcher said: "That you have Kim, that you have! Flaunt it sexy lady."

The original cleaning queen has always liked to 'flaunt it' as is evident from her secret modelling past in Liverpool.

The How Clean Is Your House star was born in Portsmouth but later moved to Merseyside and is believed to have lived on Bagot Street in  Wavertree .

After numerous jobs working in children’s homes, in retail, and as a social worker helping teenage girls in the city, the star - then known as Patricia McKenzie - embarked on a modelling career, doing jobs for Littlewoods in the late 1960s.

In 1983, Kim appeared on TV show Pebble Mill at One, demonstrating knitwear, but her brush with fame was short-lived and she went on to work as a professional house cleaner in the UK and US.
