Gina Rodriguez, Ariana Greenblatt & Jessica Marie Garcia Celebrate Eva Longoria Foundation

16 Nov 2019, 12:59

Gina Rodriguez and Ariana Greenblatt get in a group photo with Eva Longoria and America Ferrera at the Eva Longoria Foundation’s Gala Dinner held at Four Seasons on Friday night (November 15) in Beverly Hills, Calif.

The actresses were also joined at the event by Jessica Marie Garcia and Anne Winters.

At the gala, Ariana met up with quite a few celebs, including Mario Lopez and Lana Parilla.

“Thank you @evalongoria for a great night and for all the inspiring work the @evalongoriafoundation does for Latina women,” Ariana captioned a pic with Eva on Instagram story.

“Had a beautiful night celebrating @evalongoriafoundation and their important work to empower Latinas through education and entrepreneurship. With special honorees @americaferrera and Howard G. Buffett! So proud to be part of this important work to celebrate, educate, and uplift our next generation of Latina leaders ♥️,” Jessica added on her own account.
