Nick Jonas Celebrates New John Varvatos Fragrance On Day Off In Miami

16 Nov 2019, 12:32

Nick Jonas holds up his new fragrance JVxNJ Silver Edition with John Varvatos at the Macy’s Aventura store on Thursday (November 14) in Miami, Fla.

The 27-year-old musician and actor collaborated with the fashion designer on their latest scent, which is out now.

Nick spent his evening off from the Happiness Begins Tour to celebrate the launch of their new fragrance, which is described as a “fresh, effervescent and exhilarating fragrance that transports you and captures the moment when you can truly let go and relax.”

In case you missed the news, it was recently announced that the Jonas Brothers will be performing on the 2019 American Music Awards!
