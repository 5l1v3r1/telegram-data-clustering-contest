TXT's Soobin Voted The Best Rookie IDOL 2019

16 Nov 2019, 11:13 by kfan1234

TXT Soobin was announced as 'The Best Rookie IDOL 2019' after he snatched first place in KingChoice's '2019 Best Rookie KPOP IDOL 2019' voting Poll. KingChoice is well known as a popular international KPop & Celebrity voting website.

Soobin is a South Korean singer under Big Hit Entertainment. He is the leader of the boy group TXT.

On January 13, Soobin was revealed as the second member of Big Hit Entertainment's newest boy group TXT. He made his official debut with the group on March 4, 2019 with their debut mini album, The Dream Chapter: Star.

The debut album Dream Chapter: Star according to Billboard 200 was at No. 140 in the United States, making it the highest-charting debut album by an all-male KPOP group.
