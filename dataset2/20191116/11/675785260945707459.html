Kate Beckinsale slams Jamie Foxx dating rumours with sassy comment

The Serendipity actress was rubbing shoulders (but nothing else) with Jamie at a glitzy Hollywood party on Thursday

16 Nov 2019, 11:23 by Mirror .co.uk

Kate Beckinsale has clapped back at rumours she is dating Hollywood superstar Jamie Foxx with a sassy remark that left fans in a tizzy.

The 46-year-old British actress attended the Moet & Chandon HFPA and The Hollywood Reporters Celebration of the 2020 Golden Globe Ambassadors at Catch in Los Angeles on Thursday.

There, the English beauty rubbed shoulders with a string of high profile stars - including Academy Award winner Jamie, 51, and fellow British actor Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje, 52.

With Kate recently single, she seemed quick to pour ice cold water on rumours she was now moving on with Ray star Jamie before whispers even began to circulate - by posting a witty remark on Instagram.

 

“Lads night at #hfpa party with @therealaladewale @iamjamiefoxx and @jsauluck,” she wrote as she shared photos of herself posing alongside the trio of men.

“I would like to point out that standing next to someone does not mean I am dating them,” she added.

She then jokingly quipped: “Currently in line at the dry cleaners and if that’s true I’ve got a hell of a problem.”

 

Fans were quick to comment on Kate’s amusing statement - with one asking if “dry cleaning guy not classy enough?” to which she quickly retorted: “12 ppl right here.”

Another speculated she inappropriately touched Jamie at the moment the photo was taken, writing: “It does look like you are grabbing his bits behind the glorious fruit bowl…”

But the actress slammed the comment, writing: “There literally isn’t even a fruit bowl. I think you need a moment to yourself.”

Kate is back on the dating scene after her divorce from Underworld director Len Wiseman was finalised this week following the pair splitting back in 2015.

She has been linked to comedians Pete Davidson, 26, and Matt Rife, 24.

Kate was previously in a long-term relationship with 50-year-old Welshman Michael Sheen between 1995 and 2003, with whom she shares a daughter, Lily, who was born in 1999.

Meanwhile, Jamie is said to also be single after his on-off romance with Dawson's Creek legend Katie Holmes, 40, ended this year.
