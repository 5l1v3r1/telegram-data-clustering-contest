'Good luck' - Manchester United sent emphatic transfer message by Aston Villa fans

Aston Villa transfer news | Jack Grealish and John McGinn have been linked with a move to Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's side in January when the transfer window re-opens

16 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans have been reacting to transfer speculation linking Jack Grealish and John McGinn with Manchester United.

According to ESPN, the Villa pair are on a transfer hit-list alongside six other players Ole Gunnar Solskjaer's side want to sign to beef up their squad in January.

United feel they need to replace Alexis Sanchez and Romelu Lukaku, who left for Inter Milan in the summer and Ander Herrera who joined paris Saint-Germain on a free transfer.

Grealish and McGinn are key components of Dean Smith 's side, the duo impressing last season as Villa clinched promotion back to the Premier League.

 

And they have seven League goals between them this season and are first-choice midfielders.
