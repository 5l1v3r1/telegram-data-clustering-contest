Imran Khan drops official, party engagements for two days

16 Nov 2019, 11:51 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prime Minister Imran Khan has dropped official and party engagements for two days, Dunya News reported on Saturday.

According to details, the premier will spend the weekend in Islamabad’s Bani Gala with his family. No government officials of party members will meet Imran Khan on Saturday and Sunday.

It is pertinent here to mention that the premier used to chair Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) meetings at his Bani Gala residence at the weekends and hold sessions with party leaders and government officials.
