Kyle Hines highlights Round 8 Top 10 Plays

16 Nov 2019, 11:14

Plenty of impressive efforts made out EuroLeague’s latest Top 10 installment.

By Eurohoops Team/ info@eurohoops.net

Kyle Hines in blocking fashion took down Jan Vesely and secured first place in the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague Regular Season Round 8 Top 10 Plays as well. Tonye Jekiri, Kostas Papanikolaou, Tarik Black, Greg Monroe, Shane Larkin, Jacob Wiley, Ognjen Dobric, Alexey Shved and Quino Colom also made the cut.
