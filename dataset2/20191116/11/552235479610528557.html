Cops probe unexplained death of man in 50s at Glasgow high rise

The man was found dead inside his flat in Linkwood Crescent in Drumchapel at 1.05am on Friday, November 15.

16 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

A man in his 50s has been found dead inside a Glasgow high rise flat.

Police rushed to the tower block on Linkwood Crescent, in the Drumchapel area, at around 1.05am on Friday, November 15.

The man inside one of the properties was pronounced dead at the scene.

 

Police Scotland have confirmed that his death is being treated as unexplained and enquiries are still ongoing.

An eyewitness told the Daily Record that officers remain in the area more than 24 hours later.

A spokesperson for the force said: “A report will be submitted to the Procurator Fiscal.”
