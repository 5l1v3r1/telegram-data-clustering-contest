Mad Inventor YouTuber Makes Real Junkrat RIP-Tire from Overwatch

Watch Colin Furze bring this tire weapon from a video game to life.

16 Nov 2019, 11:33 by Loukia Papadopoulos

When it comes to bringing fiction to life, no one does it better than mad inventor YouTuber Colin Furze. We have seen him make Wolverine's claws as well as wrist-mounted flame throwers.

Now he has built a real Junkrat RIP-tire weapon from Overwatch. The weapon was made at the request of some viewers which are big fans of the game.

And Furze did not disappoint. He started with a tire and put a chainsaw engine in it.

He cut and retrofitted the chainsaw engine to fit perfectly in the tire. He also fitted it with a remote control to control where it heads.

In the end, Furze took the tire out for a test run and watched it run over some grass. The question remains, can it behave like a weapon?

Luckily for all, that's a question Furze does not answer doing little more than watching the tire ride around. What do you think of this DIY project? A little bit of fun or a dangerous endeavour?
