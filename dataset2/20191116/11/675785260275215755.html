Police desperately hunt for little Niyah after girl, 8, vanished in East London

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

16 Nov 2019, 11:40 by Andrew Gilpin, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/andrew-gilpin/

Police in East London are desperately searching for a girl, 8, who has gone missing in the Leyton area.

Waltham Forest police are concerned for the safety of little Niyah, who has been missing for the last hour.

Anyone who sees her is asked to call 999 and quote the reference CAD 2648/16NOV19.
