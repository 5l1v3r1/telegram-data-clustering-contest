Laura Anderson goes topless in saucy snap hours after revealing she’s a Rangers fan

16 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Rachel Macpherson

LAURA ANDERSON has gone topless in her latest saucy snap - just hours after revealing she is a Rangers fan.

The Scots Love Island star "struck a pose" in jeans and set pulses racing in the social media pic.

Taking to Instagram, the star wrote: "Strike me a pose 📸 #loveourbodies

"Never let anyone tell you to fasten up those jeans because PASTA IS LIFE."

And fans couldn't get enough - with some hailing Laura as "stunning".

One fan said: "Drop dead gorgeous."

While another wrote: "No other words but stunning."

And fellow Love Islander Anna Vakili said: "Ahhh you are fireeee."

Towie star Courtney Green added: "Sexy."

It came after the former Love Islander put out an appeal for tickets to the Betfred Cup clash between Celtic and Rangers at Hampden next month.

Taking to social media, Laura said: "I'm back in Glasgow start of December, it's a long shot but can anyone help me out with a ticket for the cup final?

"For the good end obviously. Because I'm Simply The Best".
