‘Simply how good this Indian team is’: Hosts crush Bangladesh with an innings win

Winning the Indore Test against Bangladesh by an innings and 130 runs, Virat Kohli-led side drew first blood in the two-match Test series against Bangladesh.

16 Nov 2019, 11:35 by Sports Desk

Mohammed Shami and Ravichandran Ashwin led the way as India crushed Bangladesh by an innings and 130 runs on the third day of the first Test at the Holkar International Stadium in Indore on Saturday.

Bangladesh, which was dismissed for 150 in its first innings, was bowled out for 213 in its second after Tea. Mushfiqur Rahim made a gritty 64 before he fell to off-spinner Ashwin, who also took the last wicket of Ebadot Hossain. While he registered a 3/42, paceman Shami took 4/31. Earlier, India declared on its overnight score of 493/6.
