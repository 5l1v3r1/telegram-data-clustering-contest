ESPN’s Final Prediction For Michigan-Michigan State Game

16 Nov 2019, 13:46 by Andrew Holleran

One of the Big Ten’s best rivalries is set to be on display in Ann Arbor in a couple of hours, when Michigan hosts Michigan State.

The Wolverines come into this afternoon’s game as the favorite, though you never know what you’re going to get when they play the Spartans.

ESPN’s Football Power Index has made its final prediction for the game. It likes Jim Harbaugh’s squad.

The Worldwide Leader’s computer doesn’t think it’ll be close.

ESPN’s Football Power Index is giving Michigan a 84.2 percent chance of winning this game, giving Michigan State just a 15.8 percent chance of pulling off the upset.

Michigan and Michigan State are set to kick off at noon E.T.

The game will be on FOX.
