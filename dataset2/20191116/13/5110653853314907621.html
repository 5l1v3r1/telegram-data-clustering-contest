How Michigan-Michigan State evolved into college football's nastiest sibling rivalry

16 Nov 2019, 13:25 by Bill Bender

The FOX Sports film "Divided We Stand: Michigan vs. Michigan State" premieres this Thursday at 11 p.m. ET on FS1 — and it will take an in-depth look at the football and men's basketball rivalry between the Mitten State schools.

On the football side, it's difficult to find a nastier rivalry in the last decade. Better rivalries? Sure. Nastier? There aren't too many.

This rivalry has taken on an increasingly nasty tone since Michigan State's Eddie Brown grabbed Desmond Howard at the goal line on a 2-point conversion attempt in 1990. That increased tenfold with the arrival of Michigan State coach Mark Dantonio in 2007. The Spartans have an 8-4 record since in a rivalry previously dominated by the Wolverines, and that relationship will be explored in that documentary.
