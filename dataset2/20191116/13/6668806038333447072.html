Marjaavaan has a decent start at the box-office

16 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Filmfare

Marjaavan created a lot of hype ever since its trailer was released online sometime back. Judging from the film’s trailer, it seemed like Marjaavaan would be a mass action entertainer.

Coming to the box-office collections of the film, Marjaavaan managed to make approximately Rs 6.50 to 7 Crore on the first day. The film managed to perform well in tier 2 and tier 3 cities in centres like Maharashtra, Gujarat, CP Berar, Nizam, UP and Bihar. However, Marjaavaan didn’t manage to impress the audience in the metropolitan cities.

The Milap Zaveri starrer features Sidharth Malhotra, Tara Sutaria, Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh in lead roles. Marjaavaan also brings back Sidharth and Riteish’s pairing after Ek Villain (2014).
