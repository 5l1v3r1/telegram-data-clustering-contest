Inside picture: Aaradhya looks like a princess in her pink Cinderella birthday dress

16 Nov 2019, 13:41 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Today, the youngest of the Bachchans celebrates her 8th birthday. Like every year, this year as well Bachchans have organised a themed party to celebrate dear Aradhya's special day.

Celebs like Shah Rukh Khan, Gauri, Riteish Deshmukh, Karan Johar, Genelia, and their kids have been snapped as they made their grand entries at the venue. While browsing the internet, we stumbled upon a beautiful picture of Aaradhya with mom Aishwarya from the birthday party.

In the picture, we can see the pretty birthday girl, Aaradhya posing with her fellow friends from the industry. Going by the decorations in the backdrop, we can make out that it is a neon themed birthday party. While Aaradhya looked pretty in her pink cinderella dress which has some floral detailing over it, Ash looked stunning as always.
