What channel is Georgia vs. Auburn on today? Time, schedule for ‘SEC on CBS’ game of the week

16 Nov 2019, 13:30 by James Crabtree-Hannigan

Looking to remain in the College Football Playoff conversation, No. 5 Georgia faces a daunting road trip to face No. 13 Auburn.

The Bulldogs used their room for error last month with a double-overtime loss to South Carolina, likely necessitating perfection the rest of the way to have a chance at making the playoff. The Tigers represent the biggest threat to that before a potential SEC championship.

Auburn also has a (much more defensible) loss on its resume, dropping a game at Florida. The Tigers' road to a playoff involves beating the Bulldogs and Alabama before the conference championship game.

Below is all the information you need to watch Georgia vs. Auburn on Saturday, including kickoff time, TV channel and a full Week 12 college football schedule.

What channel is Georgia vs. Auburn on today? 

  • TV channel (national): CBS

Georgia vs. Auburn is the second game of a CBS doubleheader for Week 12. No. 11 Florida's trip to Missouri kicks off at noon in the early game. There are four other games between ranked teams on Saturday's schedule, including No. 10 Oklahoma visiting No. 12 Baylor.

Georgia vs. Auburn: What time is kickoff?

  • Date: Saturday, Nov. 16
  • Start time: 3:30 p.m. ET

The Bulldogs won each of their past two meetings with Auburn. All-time, Georgia leads the series 59-56-8. Brad Nessler and Gary Danielson will be calling the matchup for CBS.
