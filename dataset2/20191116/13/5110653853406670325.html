WWE Survivor Series 2019 date, start time, matches, PPV cost, location, rumors

16 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Brian Fritz

Next up on the WWE calendar is Survivor Series,long considered one of its “big four” events of the year, taking place on Nov. 24 at the Allstate Arena in Rosemont, Ill., just outside of Chicago.

This year’s annual event has a new twist featuring matches between not just “RAW” and “SmackDown” but also NXT, making this the first time that the black and gold brand will be represented on the show.

This year’s event will feature two big matches — one with men and the other with women — each brand having a team of five in an elimination-style match. Plus, the WWE championship will be up for grabs in an emotional grudge match with Rey Mysterio challenging Brock Lesnar for the title.

The card is coming together and should be solidified in the coming weeks. Until then, here’s all you need to know about WWE Survivor Series 2019.

What time does WWE Survivor Series 2019?

  • Date: Sunday, November 24, 2019
  • Time: 6 p.m. ET (preshow) | 7 p.m. ET (main card)
  • Location: Allstate Arena in Rosemont, Ill.
  • TV channel: WWE Network (PPV)
  • Live stream: WWE.com/livestream+

WWE Survivor Series 2019 will air live on Sunday, Nov. 24. The preshow is scheduled to start at 6 p.m. ET and the main card will begin at 7 p.m. ET.

How to watch WWE Survivor Series 2019, PPV cost

WWE Survivor Series 2019 will be available for purchase on pay-per-view or via the WWE Network.

The WWE Network is available at WWE.com or by downloading the app on certain smart TVs - including Samsung, LG, Sony, and Panasonic - as well as Apple TV, Amazon Fire TV, Kindle Fire, Roku, Google Play, and Chromecast. You can also watch the network on your Xbox 360, Xbox One, PS3, and PS4 by downloading the app through its stores.

It costs $9.99 per month to sign up to watch the WWE Network online.
