Comic Con Bengaluru 2019 is happening on November 16 and 17. Read on to know all the details about the events including the passes, special events and goodies.

Written By Tanvi Dhote | Mumbai | Updated On: November 16, 2019 13:06 IST

Comic Con Bengaluru 2019 is happening today on November 16 and tomorrow on November 17. Comic Con is one of the biggest celebrations of pop culture started by fans. So if you are finding it difficult to get information about all the events, timings, passes, and other important details about Comic Con Bengaluru we have got you covered. Read on to know all the necessary details about Comic Con Bengaluru 2019.

Comic Con Bengaluru 2019: All you need to know

Dates, timings and passes

The Comic Bengaluru is happening on November 16 and 17. The Comic Con events will start by 11 AM and will end by 8 PM. Comic Con Bengaluru has three types of passes. The first pass is for November 16. The booking for it will end on the day itself. The November 16 pass costs ₹599. The second pass, which is for November 17 is also at the same price and booking for it ends on the same day. Both these passes can be booked on November 16 itself. The third pass, which is ₹1999 is for both the days. The pass for Comic Con Bengaluru 2019 can be only booked via the official website of Comic Con India.

Goodies along with the passes

The single day passes for November 16, and November 17 will be accompanied by a goodie bag. The goodie bags for both the days are same. The Comic Con Bengaluru bag consists of an exclusive Comic Con comic book, a poster, and a limited-edition Marvel bag. These goodie bag items are same for the single day passes. But for the two-day pass some other items have been added. Along with the comic book, poster, and Marvel bag the two-day pass will also consist of a Thanos funko pop, a Marvel T-shirt, and a Captain America badge.

Special Events

Cosplay is an integral part of Comic Con. Hence Comic Con Bengaluru 2019 will be hosting the Indian Championship of Cosplay Qualifier- Bengaluru and Bengaluru Comic Con 2019 – Cosplay Contest. The rules of both the contests are available on the official Comic Con India website in the Comic Con Bengaluru section. International guests like Adam Ellis, A.K. Wirru, and entertainment guests like Sanjay Manaktala, Duality Copy and many others are all set to appear at Comic Con Bengaluru.
