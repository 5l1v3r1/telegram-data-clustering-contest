Being friend-zoned, cheated on or breaking up with their partner are among the things in life that can break a man.

Seriously.

Understanding how painful the ordeal must have felt for this guy, his bros bandied together in an effort to cheer him up – and they did it by belting love songs centered on the theme of moving on.

Here is the video of them serenading him through his heartbreak.

Shared by netizen @NikkiZamri, the video featured the amazing voice of his roommate singing at the top of his lungs cheering up a fellow roomie who remained laying on his bed, apparently on the verge of tears.

In his status he writes, “To all the girls out there, know that not all men are trash. I’m saddened to see how broken my friend is but at the same time is happy that another is trying to cheer him up.”

“You will find the right partner soon. Time will heal.”

The video which has garnered more than 33k retweets also witnesses the talent of his roommate singing Malay and English songs.

Here is another video of him singing Celine Deon’s My Heart Will Go On, Siti Nurhaliza and many more.

Truly, the best example of a friend in need.

We pray that our fallen brother will soon get over his grief and hope that he too will find the happiness that he is searching for.
