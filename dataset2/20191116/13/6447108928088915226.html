Don’t Even Think Wildest Hunter season one battle pass now available

16 Nov 2019, 13:15 by Aran Suddi

Look, Don’t Even Think is not a good battle royale game even it is free to play. That’s our opinion here but some people enjoy playing the game and that’s fine. People have different tastes. If you are one of those people then you might be happy to know that the Wildest Hunter season one battle pass is now live which adds new skins for both human and werewolf characters as well as weapon skins. There’s also a new level up system so you can get up the season ranks faster.
