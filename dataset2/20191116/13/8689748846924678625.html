Efia Odo Finally Admits She Sleeps With Shatta Wale But Not The Way We Think

16 Nov 2019, 13:49 by Ghbase New Writer

Kwese TV presenter, Andrea Owusu- known within the entertainment world as Efia Odo has added another layer of suspense to her relationship with Shatta Wale in a fresh interview.

After years of evading the question as to whether she sleeps with the multiple award winning artist, the TV personality has finally admitted she does, but only in her mind.

When Pulse Ghana asked her in an interview if she’s sleeping with Shatta Wale, she replied, “Of course I am, in my mind. No, I’m not.”

“We’re just friends, guys. I sleep with him in my mind because he’s just a great guy.”

The host asked for clarification on whether both are friends with benefits and she said “no benefits, just friends. In my mind there are benefits.”

On the other hand, Shatta Wale subtly admitted on Paul Adom Otchere’s Good Evening Ghana programme that he had had his way with Efia Odo, few weeks ago in an interview.
