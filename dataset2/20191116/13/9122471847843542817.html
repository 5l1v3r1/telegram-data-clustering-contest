Video: Kirk Herbstreit Shows Off “Insane” Scene At College GameDay In Waco

16 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Andrew Holleran

ESPN’s College GameDay is broadcasting live from Waco, Texas this morning in anticipation of tonight’s showdown between undefeated Baylor and Oklahoma.

It’s been a while since the college football pregame show was at Baylor. The Bears’ fan base appears to be excited.

ESPN college football analyst Kirk Herbstreit shared a video detailing the “insane” scene by the set minutes before the show began.

“Insane amount of CREATIVE signs here in Waco for College GameDay! Coming up LIVE at 9am ET!!!” he tweeted.
