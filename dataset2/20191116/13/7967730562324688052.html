Zoono Group's run continues as Shares Race heads into final week

16 Nov 2019, 13:00 by John Collett

Angie Ellis, of 20 20 Investments, has increased her lead in week five of the six-week Shares Race, with her pick of Magmatic Resources remaining the best-performing stock tip of all.

Ellis' original $10,000 in the gold and copper explorer, which is also held by the fourth-placed Dan Silver, co-chief executive of Stake, is worth $24,167.

Ellis is also doing particularly well with Zoono Group – the third-best performing tip – with her original $10,000 investment in the anti-microbial products company now worth $22,674, up from $19,767 last week.

Her total portfolio value of $127,489 puts her comfortably ahead of Varun Ratra, of the newsletter Eye between Bulls & Bears, whose portfolio is worth $115,357. Michael Gable, of Fairmont Equities, is third with $109,956.

Ratra's best-performing stock tip remains Oncosil Medical, with his original $10,000 in the medical device company that focuses on oncology now worth $23,077.

Darren Gray, business reporter at the SMH & Age, holds New Century Resources, the fourth best-performing tip of all. His original $10,000 in the zinc miner is now worth $14,200.

The sharemarket, as measured by the S&P/ASX 300 index, finished the week almost unchanged at 6,668 points on Thursday, the day the competition closed.

The Shares Race is a fantasy shares game that runs for six weeks. Players start with $100,000, divided evenly across 10 stocks. Their progress is reported weekly.
