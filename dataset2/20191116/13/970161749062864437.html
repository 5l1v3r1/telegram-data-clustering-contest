Stassie shows off tattoo of BFF Kylie Jenner’s daughter Stormi's name

16 Nov 2019, 13:54

Here’s hoping Kylie Jenner and Anastasia Karanikolau never fall out – because the pair have matching tattoos of Kylie’s daughter, Stormi’s name on their arms.

Anastasia, also known as Stassie, got matching tattoos with Kylie back in May, but she showed off the ink this week as she visited an art gallery in Los Angeles.

We’re just going to go out there and say it – it’s a little odd to have your bestie’s kid’s name tattooed on you, isn’t it?

While Kylie and Stassie are the best of friends now, their closeness only accelerated after Kylie’s former BFF, Jordyn Woods, was unceremoniously dumped from the Kardashian fold.

Kylie used to be as inseparable from Jordyn as she now is with Stassie, until rumours surfaced that Jordyn had kissed Tristan Thompson, who was dating Kylie’s sister Khloe Kardashian.

Given how quickly that friendship ended – and Jordyn had to move out of Kylie’s house, where she had been living – at least she didn’t have to get any friendship tattoos lasered off.

Oh wait – yes she did.

Jordyn and Kylie had both had tiny ‘m’ letters inked on the inside of their little fingers in red ink in May 2016. Awkward.

Kylie and Stassie had shared footage of them getting their matching Stormi tattoos in May, which was done at Travis Scott’s birthday party.

Celebrity tattoo artist JonBoy is the one who did the tattoos according to Page Six, and Kylie also has a matching butterfly tattoo on her ankle with Stormi’s dad, Travis.

Kylie and Travis also broke up two years into their relationship, so we think the lesson in all of this is that unless you’re a million per cent certain your connection to a person is unbreakable, maybe get some friendship bracelets rather than a tattoo…
