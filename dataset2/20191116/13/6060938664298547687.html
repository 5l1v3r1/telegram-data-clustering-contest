Aaradhya Bachchan's birthday bash pictures: Shah Rukh, Gauri with Abram, Riteish Deshmukh, Genelia with their kids, Karan Johar with Yash and Roohi and other celebs at the lavish party

16 Nov 2019, 13:16 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The youngest of Bachchans, Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's daughter, Aaradhya Bachchan celebrates her eighth birthday today. The entire family is all geared up to celebrate their little munchkin's special day and have organized a nice birthday bash at Juhu.

Of late, the eldest of Bachchans organized a lavish Diwali bash and spent a relaxed birthday this year. However, that's not the case with Aaradhya, as we all know the Bachchans never fail to throw a big birthday bash for their favorite kid, Aaradhya, and like last year, this year is surely going to be one happening night.

Last year, the Bachchan family, hosted a unicorn-themed party for their little munchkin. And we are sure this year too there is something interesting stored in for the birthday girl and the tinsel town to witness. Here we bring you pictures of celebs and kids who attended the Aaradhya Bachchan's big birthday bash this year.

So far, celebs like Shah Rukh Khan, Gauri Khan along with their younger kid Abram had made a stylish entry to the bash. Joining them, were KJo's twin kids Yash and Roohi. Riteish Deshmukh along with wife Genelia and kids too made it to the bash.
