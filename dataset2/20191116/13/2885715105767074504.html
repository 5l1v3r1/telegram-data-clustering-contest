From Pixar lamp to Shrek’s ginger bread: Amputee executes unique Halloween constume designs

From being the famous Pixar Lamp to the half-eaten gingerbread from Shrek, Sundquist comes up with creative costume ideas that make the most of his single leg.

16 Nov 2019, 13:08 by Trends Desk

Living life on your terms is something one should learn from Josh Sundquist. The Paralympian comes up with distinctive costume ideas for Halloween every year to celebrate his unique body.

Losing his legs to a rare form of bone cancer at the age of nine, the motivational speaker went on to become a successful writer and a comedian.

From being the famous Pixar Lamp to the half-eaten gingerbread from Shrek, the list of Halloween costume ideas executed by Sundquist is long.
