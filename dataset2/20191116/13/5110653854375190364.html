Diontae Johnson injury update: Steelers WR in concussion protocol after helmet-to-helmet hit

16 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Sara Jane Harris

Steelers rookie wide receiver Diontae Johnson is in concussion protocol aftersuffering a helmet-to-helmet hit from Browns defensive back Damarious Randall, ESPN's Adam Schefter reported.

Johnson was seen bleeding from his right ear while walking off the field in the third quarter of Thursday's game at FirstEnergy Stadium.
