Hornets Player Mocks Dak Prescott’s Hip Thrusting During Warmups (VIDEO)

16 Nov 2019, 03:23 by Darrelle Lincoln

Dak Prescott likely never knew his pregame ritual that he does before every game would turn into the new thing to do, but here we are…

The hip thrusting he displayed on Sunday Night Football before the game against the Minnesota Vikings has now made its way to the NBA.

On Friday night, Charlotte Hornets player Devonte Graham was spotted on the court mocking the Dallas Cowboys QB with some hip thrusting of his own.
