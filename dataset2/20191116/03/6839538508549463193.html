Police release CCTV image after Nedlands fishing store robber

16 Nov 2019, 03:27

Police have released pictures of a man they wish to speak to about a fishing store robbery in the western suburbs early Saturday morning.

CCTV images from inside the Compleat Angler on Stirling Highway in Nedlands show a man forcing entry through a window at about 4am before stealing several expensive fishing reels.

It is believed the man cut himself while entering the store.

Police believe the man pictured can help them with their inquiries and are urging him to come forward.

Anyone who can identify him is asked to call Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000.
