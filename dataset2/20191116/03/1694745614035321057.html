Obsessed With Joshua Bassett's Solo on the HSM Series? Watch the Rest of His Singing Videos

16 Nov 2019, 03:20 by Kelsie Gibson

The cast of Disney+'s High School Musical: The Musical: The Series is insanely talented, and Joshua Bassett is no exception. From the very first moment the 18-year-old sings "I Think I Kinda, You Know" during auditions in the first episode, you can tell he's truly Troy Bolton 2.0.

While we can't wait to hear his renditions of classic High School Musical songs on the series, Bassett also has quite a few original songs of his own. When he's not filming, the young actor is giving glimpses of his music on social media. In fact, he actually wrote an original song with his costar Olivia Rodrigo for the series.
