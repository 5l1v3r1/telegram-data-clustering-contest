Oklahoma Star WR Reportedly “Questionable” vs. Baylor Tonight

16 Nov 2019, 21:31 by Andrew Holleran

Oklahoma is a couple of hours away from kickoff against Baylor. The Sooners could reportedly be without a star offensive playmaker against the Bears.

OU Daily is reporting that star wide receiver CeeDee Lamb is “questionable” for tonight’s contest vs. Baylor.

Lamb reportedly missed part of practice this week.

It’s unclear why he was absent from practice.

Oklahoma junior wide receiver CeeDee Lamb is questionable for Saturday’s game against No. 13 Baylor after missing part of practice this week, sources close to Lamb have told The Daily. It’s unclear as to why Lamb is questionable.

Lamb is one of the best wide receivers in the country. The junior wideout has 44 catches for 983 yards and 13 touchdowns. He leads the Sooners in most receiving categories.

Oklahoma and Baylor are set to kick off at 7:30 p.m. E.T.

The game will be on ABC.
