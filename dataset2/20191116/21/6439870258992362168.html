Crews working on power outage in Huntersville

16 Nov 2019, 21:44 by WBTV Web Staff

HUNTERSVILLE, N.C. (WBTV) - Crews are working to restore power after an outage Saturday in Huntersville.

ElectriCities, the electric operator in Huntersville and Cornelius, tweeted that crews are still “looking for the problem.”
