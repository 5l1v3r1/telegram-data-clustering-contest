Ohio State Is Off To A Ridiculous Start vs. Rutgers Today

16 Nov 2019, 21:05 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, Ohio State and Rutgers took the field for what figured to be the most lopsided game of the college football season.

Just a few minutes into the action, it’s clear why Las Vegas decided the Buckeyes should be 53-point favorites.

Each of Rutgers’ first two possessions ended in a turnover and a subsequent Ohio State touchdown. The Buckeyes raced out to a 14-0 lead in the first three and a half minutes of the game.

Things are not going well for the Scarlet Knights this afternoon.
