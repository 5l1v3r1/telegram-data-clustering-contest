TRAGIC

Daughter of great-grandad assaulted in East Kilbride hospital claims it contributed to his death

16 Nov 2019, 21:00 by Oliver Norton

THE daughter of a great-grandad assaulted in hospital claims it contributed to his death.

Agnes Edgar, 32, is also “gutted” over fears her father Wully’s alleged attacker may dodge prosecution.

She revealed her agony after we told how a patient was said to have kicked and hit Wully, 69, over the head with a glass jug as he battled a chest bug in Hairmyres Hospital, East Kilbride, in October last year.

He passed away in January. And last night Agnes, of Cambuslang, said: “My dad wasn’t himself after he was attacked.

"He took constant panic attacks.

"We’ve been told the man that attacked him might not face any more prosecution because my dad’s deceased. It’s devastating.”

Police said: “A 54-year-old man was charged in connection with an assault.”

A Crown Office spokesman added: “The procurator fiscal has received a report in relation to an alleged incident. It remains under consideration.”

A hospital spokeswoman said: “Patient safety is paramount.”
