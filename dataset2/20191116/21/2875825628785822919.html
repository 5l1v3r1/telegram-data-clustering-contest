Football star Jamie Redknapp's home hit by masked burglars twice in one day

EXCLUSIVE: Audacious masked raiders try to raid Sky football pundit Jamie Redknapp's Surrey home TWICE in one day

16 Nov 2019, 21:03 by Susan Hill

Jamie Redknapp’s family were said to be shaken last night after his home was targeted by masked raiders TWICE in one day.

The Sky footie pundit, 46, was away at the time but his housekeeper was in the £6million pad as the gang made their first attempt to break in.

She made a frantic 999 call and the raiders fled the property in Oxshott, Surrey – which Jamie shared with pop star ex Louise.

But in a brazen move, the raiders returned in the early hours for another crack.

This time, Jamie’s alarm system flagged them up – but the gang managed to break into one of his cars and stole a number of valuable items from the boot.

Police arrived at 5.14am on Tuesday but the thieves had already scarpered.

Jamie and Louise, who divorced after 19 years and share custody of sons Charley, 15, and Beau, 11, remain close and a source said the incident has shaken the family.

They said: “The thinking is the gang had been monitoring Jamie’s social ­media and seen he was out of the country.

“Jamie has been targeted before and he had already stepped up his ­security.

“The property is surrounded by tall hedgerows and has a set of large electronic gates at the entrance as well as a state-of-the-art alarm. The kids were pretty shaken up by it all and were asking a lot of questions.

“Jamie and Louise have reassured them that everything would be OK but obviously it’s a pretty harrowing thing for them to all go through.”

Last night, Surrey Police said enquiries were still ongoing and they could not give any details of what was swiped.

A force spokesman said: “We were called to a report of a man breaking into a car in Oxshott. A number of items are believed to have been taken from the boot.

“An area search was undertaken and some items were recovered.

“Further enquiries established two men were seen acting suspiciously in the area earlier in the morning and another vehicle was entered but nothing was taken.”

When Jamie and Louise got divorced, he stayed on in the family home while Louise bought a pad around the corner.

She has recently revealed she poured her pain at their split into some of the songs on her new solo album Heavy Love – the former Eternal star’s first record in 18 years.

Jamie’s spokesman was contacted for comment.
