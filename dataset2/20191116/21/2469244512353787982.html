Crash closes road near Holsworthy in both directions

The incident happened after the car spun out of control

16 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Philippa Jenkins

A car crash has blocked a road near Holsworthy in both directions this evening after the vehicle spun out of control.

The incident happened just at 8pm on Trewyn Road at Soldon Cross, between Holsworthy and Bradworthy.

The crash involved just one vehicle, a Ford Fiesta, but the road has been closed in both directions by police.

The car was only occupied by the driver who has been left uninjured. It has been confirmed that no other vehicles have been involved.

A spokesman for Devon and Cornwall Police confirmed that a ambulance was called as a precaution.

The emergency services are now waiting at the scene for the vehicle to be recovered. It is not clear when the road will reopen.
