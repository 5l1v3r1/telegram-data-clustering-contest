Scott Frost’s Message To Nebraska Fans After Loss vs. Wisconsin

16 Nov 2019, 21:30 by Dan Lyons

Today got off to a good start for Scott Frost. Despite just eight wins through two seasons at Nebraska so far, he second-year head coach was given a contract extension.

The new deal keeps Frost at Nebraska through 2026. Of course, given the team’s struggles, this was a surprising move, especially ahead of a big game against Big Ten West power Wisconsin.

The game itself did not go so well for Frost. The Badgers ran for 320 total yards, with star running back Jonathan taylor going for 204 and two scores on 25 carries.

The Huskers moved the ball quite effectively, out-gaining the Badgers 493-to-482, but couldn’t quite get over the hump today.

Now, the team needs to beat Maryland and Iowa in back-to-back weeks to make a bowl. Scott Frost says he’s “tired of looking for silver linings,” but once again pledged to get the ship righted at his alma mater.
