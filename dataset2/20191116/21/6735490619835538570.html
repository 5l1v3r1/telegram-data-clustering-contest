Kim Richards Comes After Kelsey Grammer For Calling Ex Camille Grammer ‘Pathetic’

16 Nov 2019, 21:29 by Jamie Samhan

The ladies of “The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills” are sticking together after Camille Grammer and her ex-husband Kelsey Grammer started publically feuding.

In a recent interview on “In Depth with Graham Bensinger”, the “Fraser” star opened up about his split from Camille who he was married to from 1997-2010.
