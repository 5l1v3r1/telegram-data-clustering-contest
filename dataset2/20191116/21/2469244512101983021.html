A30 partially blocked after car crashes into central reservation

The incident happened at Ide near Exeter

16 Nov 2019, 21:42 by Philippa Jenkins

The A30 was partially blocked this evening after a car crashed into the central reservation.

Officers from Devon and Cornwall Police rushed the scene after a Fiat Punto crashed into the barrier at Ide.

The incident happened tonight at 7.20pm and involved just the female driver of the vehicle.

Police have confirmed that no other vehicle or persons were involved in the incident.

It is not known what caused the incident at this stage.

The driver sustained no injuries but an ambulance was called to the scene as a precaution due the speed the vehicle had been travelling at the time of impact. It is not known how fast the car was travelling at this stage.

The road was cleared by 9pm once the vehicle had been recovered from the scene.

These are all the details available on this incident at this time.

The last update on traffic website Inrix stated: "All lanes open, accident cleared on A30 Westbound from A377 (Alphington Junction, Exeter) to A382 (Whiddon Down, Whiddon Down). Lane two (of two) was closed."
