George Conway under fire for sharing fake photo of GOP congresswoman and calling her 'lying trash'

16 Nov 2019, 21:01 by Ellie Bufkin

Attorney George Conway, husband of White House counselor Kellyanne Conway, issued scathing criticism of GOP Rep. Elise Stefanik and shared a fake photo of her after she claimed she was repeatedly interrupted during Friday's public impeachment hearing.

George Conway, who has been a firebrand against his wife's employer, tweeted that Stefanik is "lying trash," including a call to action that asked for donations to the New York congresswoman's Democratic opponent Tedra Cobb.

"@EliseStefanik is lying trash," he tweeted Saturday morning. "Please give to her opponent, @TedraCobb." He later tweeted an image of a receipt for his maximum donation of $2,800.
