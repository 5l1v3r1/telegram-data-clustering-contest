Amber Alert canceled for 2 children from Fort Lauderdale

16 Nov 2019, 21:22 by WFTS Digital Staff

FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla. --- An Amber Alert has been canceled for two children out of Fort Lauderdale. They are both safe.

The alert for Lei-Loni McLendon, 4-months-old, and Devante McLendon, 1-years-old has been resolved.

The children were last seen in the 1300 block of NW 5th Ave in Fort Lauderdale. They may be in the company of Devante McLendon Sr., 23.
