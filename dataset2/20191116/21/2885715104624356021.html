BJP MLA’s visit to Modibaug raises eyebrows

Jaykumar Gore, however, denied that he came to Modibaug to meet Sharad Pawar.

16 Nov 2019, 21:06 by Express News Service

BJP MLA Jaykumar Gore on Saturday visited Modibaug area in Pune, where NCP chief Sharad Pawar has a residence, raising eyebrows in political circles.

Gore, however, denied that he came to Modibaug to meet Pawar. “I came for a personal visit. My family is with me. I didn’t even know that Sharad Pawar lives in Modibaug,” Gore, who was with Congress before he joined BJP before the state elections, said.

Pawar, meanwhile, said: “…Gore came to meet me? I don’t think so.”
