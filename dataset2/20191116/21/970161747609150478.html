Spoilers: Furious Martin lashes out at Dotty in EastEnders

16 Nov 2019, 21:58

Dotty Cotton (Milly Zero) has wasted little time in winding up several of Walford’s finest in EastEnders since her return, and it would appear as if Martin Fowler (James Bye) is the next on her list, as the young woman’s actions leave the fruit-and-veg stall holder less than pleased in the coming episodes, and he clashes with her as a result.

Martin has had an incredibly tough couple of months, what with him being at the beck and call of Ben (Max Bowden).

Yes, as a result of the young man’s demands, he’s been forced to do all sorts, and as a result, he lied to wife Stacey (Lacey Turner) about having slept with Sonia (Natalie Cassidy) in order to keep her away from Albert Square.

With all of that to contend with — not to mention getting over the fact that he was hung upside down from the railway bridge — it’s safe to say Martin could do without stress right now, but he’s about to get quite a bit more courtesy of Dotty.

In the coming episodes, Dotty is desperate for cash, and thus she comes up with a plan to make some money. However, Martin soon realises what she’s done — and he’s less than pleased, that’s for sure.
