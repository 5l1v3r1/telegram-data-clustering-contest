Hue Jackson Reportedly Decides To Leave Colin Kaepernick Workout

16 Nov 2019, 21:15 by Chris Rosvoglou

Shortly after Adam Schefter announced that Colin Kaepernick’s workout would be moved, former head coach Hue Jackson decided that he will no longer run the show this afternoon.

Kaepernick was asking for transparency from the NFL and everyone who covers the workout, which resulted in the location being changed to an alternative facility in Atlanta. That isn’t the only major announcement involving this workout.

It was being reported throughout the week that Jackson would lead the workout for Kaepernick.

According to NFL Network reporter Michael Silver, the workout will no longer be run by Jackson. Once he learned that Kaepernick’s team wanted the location changed, he backed out of his original position.
