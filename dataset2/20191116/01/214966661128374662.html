Woman critical after near drowning on Gold Coast

16 Nov 2019, 01:27 by Shanee Dobeson

A WOMAN is fighting for her life after nearly drowning in a pool on the Gold Coast.

Emergency crews rushed to a home in Merrimac just before 9.00pm last night, after receiving reports a woman had come into trouble while swimming.

The woman, aged in her 50s, was treated at the scene by Critical Care Paramedics.

She was then rushed to the Gold Coast University Hospital in a critical condition.

A Queensland Ambulance spokesperson said the High Acuity Response Unit was also on board for transport.
