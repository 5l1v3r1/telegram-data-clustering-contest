Directors of Derry hospitality firm banned for six years

16 Nov 2019, 01:00

TWO directors of a Derry hospitality firm have been banned from running restaurant businesses for six years.

Martin Peter Getty (40) of Carn Manor, Derry city and Kerry Scullion (46) of Edenvale, Limavady, ran Regent Hospitality Limited.

The company operated The Classic Wine Bar and The Classic Bistro on Main Street, Limavady and operated premises in the Springtown Industrial Estate in Derry.

Both have accepted disqualifications from acting as directors of a licenced restaurant business for six years.
