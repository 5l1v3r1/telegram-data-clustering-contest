Disney Announced Five New Marvel Movie Release Dates Through 2023

16 Nov 2019, 01:15 by Joey Paur

Disney has announced five new Marvel movie release dates that include one in 2022 and four in 2023. There are no titles for any of the release dates but there will now be four Marvel films released in 2022 and four of them in 2023!

There were already three release dates set for 2022 for February 18th, May 6th, and July 29th. May 6th is reserved for Black Panther. The new one added is dated for October 7th. Then there are the dates for 2023 which include February 17th, May 5th, July 28th, and November 3rd.
