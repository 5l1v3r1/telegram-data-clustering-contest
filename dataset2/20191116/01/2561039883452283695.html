You Can Change the Refresh Rate on the New 16-inch MacBook Pro

16 Nov 2019, 01:47 by iClarified

Apple has posted a support document revealing that it's possible to change the refresh rate on the new 16-inch MacBook Pro.

You may want to do this for certain professional workflows such as video editing. Apple suggests choosing a refresh rate that divides evenly into the frame rate of your content. For example, if the content you’re viewing is 24 frames per second, select the 48 Hertz refresh rate.

Available refresh rates
You can switch between these refresh rates on your Retina display:
● 60 Hertz
● 59.94 Hertz
● 50 Hertz
● 48 Hertz
● 47.95 Hertz

You can change the refresh rate from the Displays section in System Preferences.
