Man injured in horror quad bike crash in Hinterland

16 Nov 2019, 01:39 by Shanee Dobeson

A man has been injured after crashing his quad bike in the Gold Coast hinterland.

Emergency crews were called to a property in Numinbah Valley around 6.30pm on Friday.

Upon arrival, they found a man with abdominal injuries.

He was treated at the scene before being taken to the Gold Coast University Hospital in a stable condition.

A Queensland Ambulance spokesperson told myGC Critical Care Paramedics were on board for transport.
