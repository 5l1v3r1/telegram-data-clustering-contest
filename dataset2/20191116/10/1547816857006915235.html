Bigg Boss 13: Asim Riaz Is The Dark Horse Of The House, Say Netizens As #WeLoveAsimRiaz Takes Over The Internet

16 Nov 2019, 10:53 by Nikita Thakkar

Bigg Boss 13 is the most discussed topic on social media these days. With fan clubs of every contestant fighting their battles, it is Asim Riaz who has gained a lot of popularity through his antics

We are literally eating, breathing and sleeping Bigg Boss 13. Social media is abuzz with fan clubs of stars fighting their battles. Be it, Sidharth Shukla, Asim Riaz or any other contestants, all of them have loyal fans who are voicing their opinion on social media every given day. But we must say, Asim Riaz, is one of those who has risen from the dark. He has found ardent followers on social media who are supporting him by trending #WeLoveAsimRiaz on Twitter.

Ahead of Weekend Ka Vaar with Salman Khan, Asim Riaz's fans have gone all out and praised their favourite contestant for being such a strong contender in the house. Quite a few have noted that Asim was the weakest contender when the show kicked off and now, he is one of the strongest in the house. @BiggBoss 13 in just 45 days!! He is ruling all the hearts. Please RT if you also supporting him!! #WeLoveAsimRiaz." Another fan wrote, "Remember that no body knows him when he was entered in BB13 but now he is ruling like a king in BB13 keep supporting him guys.....#WeLoveAsimRiaz."
