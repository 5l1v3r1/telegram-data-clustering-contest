Henderson and Gomez withdraw from England squad for Kosovo game

16 Nov 2019, 10:14

The Liverpool duo will not be involved in Pristina.

Joe Gomez and Jordan Henderson are to miss England’s final Euro 2020 qualifier in Kosovo on Sunday due to illness and injury respectively.

The PA News Agency understands Gomez was hurt when clashing knees in an accidental collision with Kieran Trippier.
