BREAKING: Shooting at Dino Melaye polling unit

16 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Olufemi Atoyebi

Olufemi Atoyebi

There was heavy shooting at Ayetoro ward 1 unit 004 where Dino Melaye voted this morning.

Less than an hour after he left the voting unit, some hoodlums in two Hilux vans a Honda Civic stormed the place, shooting into the air and targeted the ballot box.

The ballot box was eventually taken away while some boys in the community chased the vehicles with no success.

A young man simply identified as Dami, who just left a local eatery and was passing through the polling unit was shot in the leg and rushed to the hospital.
