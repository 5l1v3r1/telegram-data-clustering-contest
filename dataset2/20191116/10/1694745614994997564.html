The 16 Best Italian Foods You Can Get at Trader Joe's

16 Nov 2019, 10:15 by Erin Cullum

Trader Joe's has some of the best hidden gems among its stocked aisles, including amazing Italian foods that rival products from artisanal markets. I take Italian food very seriously — garlicky pasta for life — and I swear by a handful of Trader Giotto's cheeses, pastas, and dried goods, not only for their quality but also for their price. Plenty of POPSUGAR editors agree with me, so I've crowdsourced all of our must-have Italian foods from Trader Joe's that you'll want to pick up when you've got plans for a wine and cheese night or you need a quick pasta dinner. Whether you're more of a gnocchi kind of girl or you live for the perfect frozen pizza, we've got you covered.

Prices and products may vary by Trader Joe's location.

1 Trader Joe's Sweet Potato Gnocchi With Butter and Sage ($3)

"I love the sweet potato gnocchi with butter and sage. The little nuggets of sweetness go so well with the butter sauce, and it's so easy to make — just pop it in the microwave." — Samantha Netkin

2 Trader Joe's Parmesan Reggiano ($12/pound)

"I top every bowl of garlic pasta with a generous dusting of freshly grated parmesan cheese, and I always get Trader Joe's Parmesan Reggiano. This type of cheese is pricey, but Trader Joe's version — which is imported from Italy — is always affordable. I snack on this on its own or add it to any number of Italian recipes." — Erin Cullum

3 Trader Joe's Penne Arrabbiata ($3)

"I eat the TJ's Penne Arrabiata at least once a week . . . it's so delish, and I add spinach and kale to it to make it a little healthier. It also doesn't have dairy (which I try to avoid)." — Meghan Tipton

4 Trader Joe's Italian Truffle Cheese ($12/pound)

"This semisoft cheese has just the right amount of truffle — it's earthy and aromatic without being overwhelming. It's also amazing melted into a grilled cheese!" — Mandy Harris

"My friends and I are OBSESSED with the truffle cheese! Whoever is in charge of the cheese plate is required to buy at least three." — Carla Hawkes

"I love all of the Italian cheeses, but especially the truffle one. It reminds me of when I studied abroad in Florence and used to snack on crackers with cheese and truffle honey." — SN

5 Trader Joe's Pizza Dough ($1)

"Homemade pizza dough is completely worth the effort, but the next best thing for me is Trader Joe's refrigerated pizza dough (plain, whole wheat, and garlic herb). The flavor is much better than other store-bought dough, and I love the way it sets up and stretches after resting at room temperature for 20 minutes. For a whopping $1, you really can't get a better deal." — EC

6 Trader Joe's Linguine With Clam Sauce ($3)

"They have a clam linguine that somehow is literally like $2, so it's basically free, and it's so good. And this is coming from an Italian food snob (re: my last name)." — Dominique Astorino

7 Trader Joe's Marinated Fresh Mozzarella ($5)

"I love, love Trader Joe's Marinated Fresh Mozzarella. The mozzarella balls are marinated in olive oil, basil, and other herbs, so they're perfect alone or paired with some bread and olives." — Nicole Yi

8 Trader Joe's Sliced Prosciutto ($4)

"I could make — actually, I have made — a meal out of Trader Joe's prosciutto, cheese, and a baguette. Prosciutto is hands down my favorite meat, but unnecessary additives like nitrates always turn me off from buying most store brands. I live for the fact that this addictive prosciutto only has two ingredients: ham and salt." — EC

9 Trader Joe's Gnocchi al Gorgonzola ($3)

"The frozen gorgonzola gnocchi is such an easy go-to. It's so fast and easy to heat up and is crazy delicious. I try to keep one in the freezer for emergency meals!" — Shannon Vestal Robson

10 Trader Joe's Burrata, Prosciutto, and Arugula Flatbread ($5)

"I'm completely obsessed with the Burrata, Proscuitto, and Arugula Flatbread because those three things separately give me life, and together, it's a whole different level." — SN

11 Trader Joe's Organic Tomato Basil Marinara ($2) and Roasted Garlic Spaghetti Sauce ($3)

"ALLLL the pasta sauce. I love TJ's pasta sauce WAY better than anything you can get at Whole Foods." — DA

12 Trader Joe's Tiramisu Torte ($7)

"The frozen tiramisu cake is one of my all-time favorite desserts! I've requested it for countless birthday celebrations over the years, preferring it to most made-to-order or bakery case versions." — Stephanie Nguyen

13 Trader Joe's Egg Pappardelle Pasta ($2)

"If I'm buying dried pasta rather than fresh from an Italian store, I only buy Trader Joe's. From orecchiette to fusilli to linguine and orzo, the selection totally meets my approval, and most of it (even the organic spaghetti) is $1. When I'm feeling fancy and making bolognese, I go for this egg pappardelle pasta, which has the perfect texture." — EC

14 Trader Joe's Breaded Mozzarella Cheese Sticks ($4)

"Hate to admit it, but I love the mozzarella sticks and always keep them in my freezer in case I feel like snacking." — SN

15 Trader Joe's Mushroom Ravioli With Mushroom Truffle Sauce ($3)

"They have a mushroom ravioli that is to die for!" — DA

16 Trader Joe's Wood Fired Naples Style Uncured Pepperoni Pizza ($5)

"I'm not usually a fan of frozen pizzas, but this one is legit. It cooks in less than 10 minutes, and the crust gets nice and browned while the cheese melts and the pepperoni develops just the right amount of grease." — EC
