Aston Villa provide update on Marvelous Nakamba racist chant investigation

Latest Aston Villa news | A group of fans were filmed singing a song about the the Zimbabwe midfielder which contained racial slurs

16 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Aston Villa are continuing a ‘comprehensive’ investigation into the racist Marvelous Nakamba chant that was heard earlier this season.

Footage shared widely on social media showed a group of fans singing a song about the Zimbabwe midfielder which contained racial slurs.

The incident happened in October, when Villa thrashed Norwich City 5-1 at Carrow Road.

Villa released a strong statement at the time saying they were ‘disgusted and appalled’ by the chant and promised that those found to be responsible will be dealt with ‘in the strongest manner’ and reported to the Police.

The club gave an update on the situation during the Fan Consultation Group meeting held this week.

Safety Officer and Head of Security Keith Wiseman said a “comprehensive, multi agency investigation” is ongoing to identify those involved.

He added that a further update will be given in conjunction with West Midlands Police.

 

Nakamba was asked about the chant while on international duty last month and he told the Zimbabwe Chronicle: “I’m not worried at all, all is well and God is in control.

“I just want to enjoy my game, keep on improving. I’m feeling the love from everyone at Aston Villa which makes my work even easier.”
