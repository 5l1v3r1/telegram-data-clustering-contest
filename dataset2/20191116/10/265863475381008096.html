Ashanti Leaves Little To The Imagination As She Plays On A Bed In New Photos — Diva Looks So Good That Fans Are Now Bashing Nelly For Letting Her Go

16 Nov 2019, 10:01 by Mel Walker

R&B diva Ashanti rocked social media recently with a very sexy outfit, and fans have Tory Lanez to thank for that.

Lanez recently dropped a Chixtape 5 project, which featured songs from the 2000s R&B era. The project includes Ashanti’s “Foolish,” T-Pain’s “I’m Sprung,” and Chris Brown’s “Take You Down.”

Lanez spoke about Chixtape to Billboard and said this: “Everything is 2000s-inspired. Everything is inspired by the times when things were golden for us. I think all those pieces and everything that we’ve come out with has been all about nostalgia. I’m about to take you on a whole journey musically.”

Asanti, who collaborated with the crooner, posted a fabulous picture of herself on Instagram, where she stripped down to her undies while sitting on a bed.

The singer also delighted her million followers with a shoot from a concert, where she can be seen standing on a stage and performing to an admiring crowd.

Ashanti’s look for the event seemed dazzling as she wore a fresh and colorful bodysuit, and even though the outfit modestly kept from revealing too much skin, it outlined the singer’s curvy figure perfectly.

The 39-year-old combined the colorful attire with huge sunglasses and simple, but oversized hoop earrings.

The stunning effect of the diva’s looks was completed by impeccable makeup and bright red lipstick.

Ashanti wore her hair in a playful voluminous ponytail that was set very high.

The bombshell captioned the picture by simply stating that it was a blessing for her to perform, and indeed she looked like she was having the time of her life.

In the photo, Ashanti’s skin was glowing, and she raised her hands up in the air, and it seems like she really enjoyed the moment.

Naturally, after the musician shared the snapshot, it did not take long for reactions from fans to follow.

Very soon, the image gathered a remarkable number of likes, and a lot of supporters commented on how fine Ashanti looked.

The memorable outfit also took its share of attention in the comment section, as many people reacted positively to the bodysuit.

It seems like Ashanti may be really into the trend of combining form-fitting clothes with big sunglasses since she wore the look a couple of days earlier in LA too.

Ashanti is getting better with time.
