New pet info database ‘ironed out by the end of the year’

16 Nov 2019, 10:00

The registration of pet dogs, cats and ferrets on a new database seems to be causing some confusion.

The team running the new SIAC Pet Information Database, the National Vets Union, said it receives an average of 1,000 emails a day regarding problems with registrations.

The union said teething issues will be ironed out by the end of the year.

One of the main issues appears to be the duplication of information on the new platform with existing databases.
