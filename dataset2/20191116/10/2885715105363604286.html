The Death and Life of John F Donovan trailer: Kit Harington starrer deals with celebrity culture

The Death and Life of John F Donovan is about the relationship between an actor named Rupert Turner and Kit Harington's TV star, the titular John F Donovan. The film releases on December 13.

16 Nov 2019, 10:01 by Entertainment Desk

The trailer of The Death and Life of John F Donovan is out. Starring Kit Harington, the film has been directed by Canadian filmmaker Xavier Dolan and is penned by Dolan and Jacob Tierney.

The film is about the relationship between an actor named Rupert Turner (Ben Schnetzer as older Turner and Jacob Tremblay as the younger one) and Harington’s TV star, the titular John F Donovan. The young Turner and Donovan shared correspondence with each other.

Donovan dies tragically and suddenly after a series of scandals. Turner grows up to be an actor and writes a book about the insights into the life of Donovan.
