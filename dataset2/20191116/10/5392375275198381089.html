16 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Efe Udin

Rate this post

A few days ago, Motorola released the Razr flip phone which comes with a foldable display. The Motorola Razr is more like the 2004 Razr V3 with new technology. Thus, this new phone brought a lot of memories back to users. In the early Android years, the likes of Motorola and HTC were on top of their game. These companies have really old devices that were a hit back in the day. With the successful launch of Motorola Razr, HTC wants to tow the same line – bring back an old device with new technologies.
