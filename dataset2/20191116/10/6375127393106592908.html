Woman trapped in fire

16 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Edd Reyes

A 39-year-old woman was killed after she was trapped inside their burning home yesterday before dawn in Taguig City.

Taguig City Fire Marshal Supt. Randolf Bides said the body of Rose Franco was found in the rubble of their burned home in Dimasalan Street, Bgy. Hagonoy while her parents and three children were able to go out before the fire totally engulfed their house.

The fire of still undetermined origin broke out at the house of the victim at around 1:10 a.m. and quickly spread to three houses mostly made up of light materials before it was finally put out at around 2:25 a.m. with the arrival of Taguig firefighters.
