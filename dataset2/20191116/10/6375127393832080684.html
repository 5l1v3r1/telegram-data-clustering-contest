Lotto bettor wins P22M

16 Nov 2019, 10:28 by Arlene Rivera

AROUND P22.1 million was bagged by a bettor of Mega Lotto 6/45 during Friday night’s draw, the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO) said.

According to PCSO General Manager Royina Garma, the lucky bettor listed the 19-07-09-06-2 -16 winning combination. It has a total jackpot prize of P22,163,563.60.

The winning ticket was purchased at an outlet in Makati City, the PCSO said.

The jackpot prize will be subjected to 20 percent tax as mandatrivered by the TRAIN Law.
