Bigg Boss 13 November 16 SPOILER ALERT: Hindustani Bhau Locks Horns With Vishal Aditya Singh During Weekend Ka Vaar

16 Nov 2019, 05:01 by Oshine Koul

In the promo of the upcoming episode of Bigg Boss 13 Weekend Ka Vaar, Vishal Aditya Singh gives Hindustani Bhau ‘Bol Bachchan’ tag which gave them a chance to lock horns. Salman Khan can also be seen bashing the housemates

Salman Khan is back to school Bigg Boss 13 pulling up contestants about their actions inside the house with other housemates. The controversial show is making headlines like never before. Right from Rashami Desai and Sidharth Shukla’s dushmani to Devloeena Bhattacharjee and Rashami’s dosti, contestants’ equation is seen changing every day. But looks like this Weekend Ka Vaar is going to be intriguing as contestants- Hindustani Bhau and Vishal Aditya Singh can be seen locking horns with each other in front of Salman Khan.

As seen in the latest promo, Salman Khan asks contests to tag them with different names given by the actor. First Devoleena is seen calling Shefali Jariwala - Dishaheen because according to her she had been biased in the post. Reacting to this, Shefali opposes the former’s tag and says she is not lost. Further, Vishal Aditya Singh gives Hindustani Bhau ‘Bol Bachchan’ tag and that was it.
