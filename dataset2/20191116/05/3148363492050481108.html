Dem Sen. Brown: 'Some Republicans' Will Vote to Remove Trump | Breitbart

16 Nov 2019, 05:13 by Ian Hanchett

On Friday’s broadcast of HBO’s “Real Time,” Senator Sherrod Brown (D-OH) predicted that some Republicans in Congress will vote to remove President Trump.

Brown said, “[N]ext year, impeachment will be over. There will be some Republicans that vote to remove him, I’ll bet you, by then. Because they’re nervous in their districts.”
