Broken But Beautiful 2 trailer: Vikrant Massey and Harleen Sethi emerge from heartbreak

The trailer of Broken But Beautiful 2 shows Veer (Vikrant Massey) and Sameera's (Harleen Sethi) character moving ahead in life. The series starts streaming on ZEE5 and ALTBalaji on November 27.

16 Nov 2019, 05:39 by Arushi Jain

The first season of AltBalaji’s popular web series Broken But Beautiful ended with the lead protagonists, Veer (Vikrant Massey) and Sameera (Harleen Sethi) choosing separate paths to discover themselves. Now, after one year, the makers are releasing the second season of the series and from the look of the trailer, its premise seems to have the potential to hold the interest of audience if given proper treatment.

The three-minute-long trailer of Broken But Beautiful 2 shows Veer and Sameera’s character moving ahead in life. While Veer, a widower, finally gets into a relationship after being commitment-phobic in the first season, Sameera starts living life to the fullest after grieving over the end of her relationship of five years. But as the two cross paths once again, they feel a strong connection with each other.
