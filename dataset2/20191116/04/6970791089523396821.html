Lil Reese Bounces Back After Near-Death Shooting Listening To Throwback Jon B

16 Nov 2019, 04:10 by Biz Jones

Chicago rapper Lil Reese is more than on the road to recovery. The hip-hop artist has shared a quick update to fans concerned about his well-being following a near-fatal shooting.

New footage surfaced online Friday of Reese appearing to drive around listening to vintage music by R&B singer Jon B.
