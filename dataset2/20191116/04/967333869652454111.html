Police Use Tear Gas Against Demonstrators in Central Santiago, Rally Continues

SANTIAGO, November 16 (Sputnik) – Chilean police have used tear gas to disperse protesters in central Santiago, media reported, adding that despite this, the rally continued on unabated.

16 Nov 2019, 04:33

Several hundred people have gathered on Plaza Italia Square in the centre of the Chilean capital, with many protesters wearing masks or respirators.

The manifestation was peaceful. Young people, who gathered there danced and played drums.
