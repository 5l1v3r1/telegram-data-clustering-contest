IPL Auction: Complete list of players released and retained by teams

16 Nov 2019, 04:08 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Highlights

  • Defending champions Mumbai Indians retained their core team including Lasith Malinga, while releasing 12 players.
  • Chennai Super Kings released Sam Billings and David Willey along with Mohit Sharma, Dhruv Shorey and Chaitanya Bishnoi.
  • Kings XI Punjab released David Miller after eight IPL seasons, but retained Chris Gayle.
