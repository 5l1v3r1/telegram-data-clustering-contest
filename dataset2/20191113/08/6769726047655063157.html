Sam Kerr joins Premier League heavyweights

13 Nov 2019, 08:30 by AAP

MATILDAS captain Sam Kerr has signed with English heavyweights Chelsea.

Kerr has ended weeks of speculation about her club future by announcing on Wednesday she’s joining Chelsea for the next two-and-a-half years.

The 26-year-old striker is taking a three-week holiday before linking with Chelsea for the second half of the English Women’s Super League season.
