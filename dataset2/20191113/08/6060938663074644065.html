Amyra Dastur looks regal in a glittering traditional lehenga-choli

13 Nov 2019, 08:53 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Amyra Dastur exactly knows how to carry herself with grace and beauty. The diva is known for her fashionable outfits and her beauty which adds all the needed oomph! to her look. After treating the masses with her alluring pictures and fashionable outfits, Amyra is here again to stun her fans with a traditional look.

Amyra took to her Instagram handle and shared a regal picture wherein she is dressed in a beige colored beautiful lehenga. The choli has a mirror embellishments all over and also the dupatta has some mirror detailing over it. With a pearl chokar and a kamarpatta around her waist, Amyra is looks majestic in this traditional lehenga.

After delivering a stunning performance in Mikhil Musale's recently released film 'Made In China' alongside the likes of Rajkummar Rao and Mouni Roy, Amyra was recently in Goa for a short vacation and pictures from her Goan holidays were all over the internet. The sexy siren often shares drool-worthy pictures on her social media handles.
