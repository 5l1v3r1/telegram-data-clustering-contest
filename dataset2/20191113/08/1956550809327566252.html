Cool tween fashion brand opens in Mall of the Emirates Dubai

Mini stylistas can find all the latest fashion and accessories at Justice

13 Nov 2019, 08:50 by Kelly Johnstone

Tween girl fashion brand Justice has opened a new shop in Dubai's Mall of the Emirates.

This is the tenth UAE store for the label, which is already in The Dubai Mall and Yas Mall in Abu Dhabi, and is popular with tween girls looking for the latest fashion and accessories.
