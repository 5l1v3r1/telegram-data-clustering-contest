Market Pulse on November 12th

13 Nov 2019, 08:24 by Olivier Rigot, EMC Gestion de Fortune

S&P 500 3091,84 +4,83.

The S&P 500 surpassed briefly the 3000 mark before ceding some ground towards the close, Breadth settled slightly negative: 1849 stocks advanced compared to 1915 that settled down. Although we haven’t depicted any clear signs of speculation, our risk profile index still stands at a high level and our preferred scenario still calls for a quick pullback to the breaking point in the area of 3000-3030. The saga of a potential trade deal between the US and China may well come back to the surface; beware to any kind of vengeful tweet from Mr Trump!
