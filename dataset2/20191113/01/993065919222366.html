Phil Coulter to perform one-man show at Culloden hotel in Co Down next week

13 Nov 2019, 01:00

PHIL Coulter will perform his critically acclaimed one-man show at the Culloden Estate & Spa near Holywood next week. The multi-award-winning Derry producer, songwriter and composer has been earning rave reviews for what's billed as an "intimate and entertaining evening of music, songs and stories" from his more than 50-year career to the five-star north Down hotel on Thursday November 21. One of Ireland's most prominent artists, Coulter has recorded and played with the best in the music business, had multiple hits and a catalogue of albums. He created two of the most successful Eurovision songs, Puppet on a String and Congratulations, and his writing credits include some of Ireland's best-loved songs.
