Happy Birthday Aryan Khan: From becoming an actor to his passion here's what dad Shah Rukh Khan has to say about Aryan

13 Nov 2019, 01:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Shah Rukh Khan and Gauri Khan's son Aryan Khan is one of the most popular star kids in Bollywood. Even before his debut, this young champ lent his voice for the character of Simba in the Hindi version of 'The Lion King'. This left his admirers even more excited for his official debut. he enjoys a massive fan following on social media with more than 1 million followers. Whenever he is in town, he makes sure to spend quality time with her family and friends. Aryan is often spotted in the city with sister Suhana Khan and her besties Shanaya Kapoor, Ananya Panday and Ahaan Panday. As the star kid turned a year older today, we bring you some of the best quotes of his papa SRK on him.

Career plan

Recently, when SRK celebrated his birthday with his fans, the 'Zero' actor opened up about his son Aryan. He talked about a conversation he had with his elder son Aryan last year. According to Aryan, when he and Suhana were growing up, they saw ‘Baazigar’ and Kuch Kuch Hota Hai’ but AbRam should get a good character in the commercial film that after watching he would feel that his father is a big star. So the actor should make such a great film that AbRam knows why so many people love him.

Follow in his footsteps ?

Shah Rukh Khan has always been supportive of his kids- Aryan, Suhana and AbRam. In an interview with a leading daily, SRK revealed that Aryan desires to be a film-maker and writer, whereas Suhana wants to be an actress.

Director not actor

Rumours are doing the rounds that Suhana will make her Bollywood debut soon. But there is a lack of information on his elder son, Aryan Khan's career choice. A few days back, at an event, SRK revealed Aryan’s career plan and said that he doesn’t want to become an actor, he rather wants to make films, become a director and is training for it in the US.

'Good kids'

A few days back, Gauri posted a cute family photo on her Instagram handle, which has all their three kids in one frame. SRK reacted to Gauri’s post saying, “Over years I made a good house...Gauri made a good Home but I really believe we are the best at making some really good kids!”.

SRK on why Aryan 'May Not Become An Actor'

In a recent talk show, King Khan talked about his son Aryan's career choice, He said that once his son came to him and said that he doesn't want to act and the reason for it that is every time he will be compared to his father.
