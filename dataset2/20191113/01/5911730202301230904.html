Man hospitalized after shooting in Tampa, police say

13 Nov 2019, 01:03 by WFTS Digital Staff

TAMPA, Fla. -- A shooting in the Robles Park neighborhood left a man hospitalized Tuesday night.

The Tampa Police Department responded to a call of a shooting a little after 7 p.m. near the intersection of North Avon Avenue and East Stratford Avenue. Upon arrival, officers found a man with an apparent gunshot wound.
