The ‘Scoob’ Trailer Is Here And People Are Nostalgic And Emotional

13 Nov 2019, 03:45 by Michelle Rennex

For a lot of us, Scooby-Doo was a big part of growing up.

I mean, boasting a whole bunch of different versions and spin-offs since 1969, it’s hard to not have seen the iconic show.

Following the supernatural crime fighting adventures of Scooby, Shaggy, Fred, Daphne and Velma, Scooby-Doo was loved by adults, teens and kids alike.

And yesterday the official trailer for Scoob, the newest animation film in the Scooby-Doo franchise, was released to the delight of eager fans.
