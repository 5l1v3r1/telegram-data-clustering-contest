I'm A Celebrity's Caitlyn Jenner 'hoping to lose weight for Kardashian party'

Jungle rations in I'm A Celebrity can often see the campmates shed pounds and reality star Caitlyn Jenner is hoping to be slimmer when she leaves the show

13 Nov 2019, 03:40 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Caitlyn Jenner is hoping to use her jungle experience to lose weight, especially with the Kardashian Christmas party on the horizon.

The 70-year-old reality star has indicated that rations in camp could kick start a slimming process that she hopes will put her in better stead as the festive season begins,

Speaking ahead of entering the camp for the launch of I'm A Celebrity on Sunday, Caitlyn indicated that weight loss was the main determining factor to her decision to to sign up.

In a video interview, Caitlyn said: "The only reason I'm doing it is to lose a few pounds.

"I gotta fit into that outfit when I come back. I have the family Christmas party to go to. I wanna look good."

Caitlyn joked ahead of entering the jungle camp that she didn't know that there wouldn't be stylists on hand to help keep her looking on point at all times.

She asked: "Do we get hair and make-up? Noooo?! We don't get hair and make-up? Oh my god!"

Despite the lack of hair help in the camp, Caitlyn has indicated that it will be her fellow campmates that might end up bugging her more than any insects.

Caitlyn said: "People are far tougher than any snake or spider," before adding that she would try to "keep the peace" and will act as the "parent" of the camp.

I'm A Celebrity bosses have paid big bucks to bring Caitlyn Down Under, with reports that she is receiving a£500,000 payday for her appearance.

The former Olympian has previous with shows like this as she has already taken part in the US version.

Speaking about her experiences in that show, back in 2003, Caitlyn said: "I did the US version of this and it was a very interesting experience last time I did it.

"Last time, I did it with a bunch of people from the States who I knew of. This time it’s a bunch of people from the UK who I potentially won’t know."

She joins Roman Kemp, Ian Wright, Andrew Maxwell, Jacqueline Jossa, James Haskell, Nadine Coyle, Kate Garraway and Myles Stephenson for the 2019 series.
