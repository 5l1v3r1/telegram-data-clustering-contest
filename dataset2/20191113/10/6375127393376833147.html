Dismissed soldier, 6 others nabbed in Ecija buy-bust ops

13 Nov 2019, 10:10 by Steve A. Gosuico

CABANATUAN CITY – A dismissed Army personnel headed the list of seven suspected drug personalities who were arrested by the police in separate buy-bust operations in Nueva Ecija on Tuesday. 

In Sta. Rosa, town police head Major Steven Dela Cruz identified the nabbed dismissed AFP personnel as Marvin Dionisio, 34, alias Melvin Dionisio, of Bgy. Mapalad, who was arrested along with alleged cohort Jennifer Cruz, 41, also of Bgy. Mapalad during a sting operation conducted in the same barangay at 5:30 p.m.

The duo yielded 10 plastic sachets containing shabu and P500 in marked money during the operation conducted by the joint personnel of the Sta. Rosa police and the 303rd Regional Mobile Force.

Also nabbed in Bgy. San Vicente, Gapan City was suspected drug peddler Dennis Estanislao, 43, of Bgy. Bulualto, San Miguel, Bulacan.

In Peñaranda, anti-drug operatives collared John Paul Cañeso, 31, service crew, of Tumana St., Bgy. Sinasajan. He is listed among the town’s suspected drug personalities.

In Lupao, arrested was tricycle driver Vicente Angfaina Jr., 31, alias Enteng, of Bgy. Catalanacan, Science City of Muñoz. He was caught while selling a plastic sachet of illegal drugs worth P500 to a police-poseur-buyer in Bgy. Mapangpang.

In San Isidro, police led by Major Ranny Casilla nabbed Beatriz Deaño, alias Bea, 26, of Bgy. San Fernando Norte, Cabiao and Anthony Gerard Aquilizan, 25, of Bgy. Malapit. They were collared in separate buy-bust operations conducted in Bgy. Poblacion.
