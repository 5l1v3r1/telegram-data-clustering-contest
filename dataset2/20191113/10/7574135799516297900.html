Ali Wong adds 2020 ‘Milk & Money Tour’ dates (3 NYC shows)

13 Nov 2019, 10:58 by Bill Pearis

Comedian Ali Wong, who has been all over Netflix lately between comedy specials, romcom Always Be My Maybe and the new season of Big Mouth, has been on her “Milk & Money” tour for most of this year, and she recently announced new 2020 dates. Those include two nights at NYC’s Beacon Theatre on March 31 and April 1, which are all but sold out. Ali’s just added a third Beacon show on April 2 and tickets for that show go on presale at 1 PM. Password for the two other Beacon shows was COBRA — try that?
