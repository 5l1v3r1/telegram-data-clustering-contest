Priyanka Chopra shares ‘then and now’ pictures of how she preps for her projects

13 Nov 2019, 10:34 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Priyanka Chopra is busy shooting for her next project in Delhi and recently the actress took to social media to share a post of how she preps for her projects. In the then and now click, the first picture shared by the actress featured her walking in a graveyard. The 2018 click was from Priyanka’s shoot for a funeral scene for her TV show. And her more recent 'now' picture saw the actress immersed in the script and prep for her next venture with Rajkummar Rao.

On the big screen, Priyanka was last seen in Shonali Bose directed ‘The Sky Is Pink’. The film also featured Farhan Akhtar, Rohit Saraf and Zaira Wasim. While PeeCee is busy with her professional commitments in India, beau Nick Jonas is in USA. Despite the distance, the couple often indulges in social media PDA which is too cute for words.

On December 1, Priyanka and Nick will celebrate their first wedding anniversary. The couple tied the knot in a lavish ceremony last year in Jodhpur.
