Windows 10 Theme: Download Microsoft’s new Paper Art PREMIUM theme pack from Microsoft Store

13 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Raka

Microsoft has added a new theme pack to the Microsoft Store for Windows 10 users. Titled Paper Art PREMIUM, the new theme pack contains 16 high-quality 4K wallpapers, the description of which reads as follows:

Colorful flowers, animals, birds, and more are folded, bunched, and quilled in this 16-image 4k premium set. These images are free for Windows 10 Themes and are to be used as desktop wallpaper only.

After downloading the collection, go to Start > Settings > Personalization > Themes and click on the theme you prefer.

To apply and change to the most appropriate color, based on the current wallpaper, go to Customize > Colors > Automatically select a primary color from the background.

Find the new Paper Art PREMIUM in the Microsoft Store here.
