BREAKING Boris Johnson heckled as he finally visits community devastated by flooding

The Prime Minister had "where've you been?" and "you took your time" shouted at him by angry locals as he finally put in an appearance six days after flooding hit

13 Nov 2019, 10:28 by Dave Burke

Boris Johnson was heckled as he finally visited areas, hit by devastating floods, forcing families from their homes.

The Prime Minister had "where've you been?" and "you took your time" shouted at him this morning.

Yesterday he came under fire from Labour for his slow response to the crisis.

It is his first visit to the South Midlands since the flooding hit six days ago.
