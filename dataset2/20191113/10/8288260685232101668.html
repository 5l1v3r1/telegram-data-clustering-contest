McDonald’s is selling a Snickerdoodle McFlurry for Christmas 2019

Customers who love the Golden Arches can acquire the Snickerdoodle McFlurry exclusively via McDelivery

13 Nov 2019, 10:50 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

McDonald’s is launching a Snickerdoodle McFlurry for Christmas.

The fast food giant operates more restaurants in Birmingham than any other UK city outside of London.

And the Snickerdoodle McFlurry, a festive twist on the popular dessert menu item, is the newest product being sold at McDonald's eateries.

Customers who love the Golden Arches can acquire the Snickerdoodle McFlurry exclusively via McDelivery.

McDonald's customers can find the products on UberEats from November 11 to 17.

 

The dessert is a mix of creamy vanilla soft serve, cinnamon cookie crumbles, white chocolate pieces, and a snickerdoodle topping.

McDonald’s is bringing back the mint Matchmaker McFlurry this month too - after fans clamoured for its return.

The popular flavour hasn't been seen in restaurants since 2013.

The McFlurry will be available to buy in both the mini version for 99p and regular size which cost £1.39.

NewFoodsUK has also reported that Cool Mint McFlurries will be available in stores from 20 November.

McDonald's has revealed its yuletide options for 2019.

They include the Millionaire's Latte, a delicious latte, swirled with a Caramel biscuit syrup and topped with chocolate cream, and the Toffee Latte, a smooth latte, blended with a sweet toffee syrup and topped with cream.

The Hot Chocolate - a silky treat made with a rich, chocolate syrup - is also back, alongside the Millionaire's Donut, an indulgent iced caramel doughnut with gooey caramel filling, topped with biscuit pieces and a dark cocoa drizzle.

Fans can also get a soft chocolatey brownie with melted Belgian chocolate and added chocolate chunks and a berry muffin filled with a sweet raspberry jam and sprinkled with a crunchy granola topping.
