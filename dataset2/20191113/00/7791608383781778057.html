Creatives Pay Homage To Stan Lee On His Death Anniversary With Powerful Artworks

13 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Image via betto rodrigues / Shutterstock.com
It has been a year since the passing of Marvel’s comic book legend Stan Lee.
For the one-year anniversary of his death, several fans paid tribute to the legendary creator. Artists whipped out their sketching materials and creative juices to immortalize Lee as part of the Marvel universe through their work.
Marvel Entertainment, too, looked back on Lee’s legacy with an illustration of him surrounded by the superheroes he drew.
“Remembering Stan ‘The Man’ Lee,” the company wrote on Twitter. “His legacy will live on in the Marvel Universe and the hearts of his fans.” The artwork sees Lee smiling among superheroes by the likes of The Incredible Hulk, Spider-Man, and Fantastic Four.
The Avengers: Endgame directors, the Russo Brothers, also honored Lee with a sweet snap of them smiling together, while he sat on the director’s chair in his signature aviators.
