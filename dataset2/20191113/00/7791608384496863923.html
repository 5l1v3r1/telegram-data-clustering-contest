Pamela Anderson Takes Trip Down Memory Lane With Newlywed Trumps In 2005 Photo

13 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Left image via Featureflash Photo Agency / Shutterstock.com, right image via mark reinstein / Shutterstock.com
Pamela Anderson took a trip down memory lane on Twitter.
The actress shared a series of photos dating back to 2005, in which she is pictured with Donald and Melania Trump who were newlyweds at the time. The couple had thrown a celebration at the now-defunct Trump Taj Mahal for Donald Trump’s birthday.
Anderson teased her cleavage in a low-cut white dress as she stood next to the now-president in a suit and signature red tie. As for his wife, she wore a plunging black midi dress. The three are shown smiling widely back at the camera.
Anderson tagged the couple in the photo, which she captioned as, “Once upon a time.”
The 2005 snap brought mixed reactions from social media users, who either called her “awesome” or just a “traitor.” However, in an interview with The Hollywood Reporter from 2018, Anderson claimed their apparent camaraderie was all for show.
She explained that she was hired to be at the party, and that she saw Donald Trump as “nothing special.” However, she has since expressed her support for the first couple.
