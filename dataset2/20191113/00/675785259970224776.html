Currys PC World kicks off Black Friday 2019 deals two weeks early

Tech giant Currys has officially kicked off it Black Friday event for 2019 - and these are all the top discounts now live on Nintendo Switch bundles, Dyson V8 vacuums and LG smart 4K HD TVs and more in the first wave of deals

13 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Chantelle Symester, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

 Currys PC World has officially kicked off its Black Friday discounts for 2019.

Today the tech giant unveils the first of many ‘Why Wait’ deals that are now available for shoppers to snap up online at currys.co.uk and in stores across the UK.

The price cuts include plenty of incredible products across all major categories like home entertainment, kitchen appliances and computing.

The early deals are perfect for anyone eager to get their Christmas shopping sorted and avoid the chaos of Black Friday, which falls on 29 November this year.

Top brands like Dyson, Nintendo, LG and more feature in the deals, which will continue throughout this month. Highlights for the first wave of discounts include £100 off the Dyson V8 Cord-free vacuum cleaners, now £299 and £45 off the best-selling Nintendo Switch with Pokemon Sword or Shield game bundles, now £279.

Speaking about the decision to go early with deals this year, Zeena Hill, Go To Market Planning Director for Dixons Carphone says: “Each year, Black Friday continues to prove itself as a key shopping date for customers. At Currys PC World, we’re pleased to give shoppers an opportunity to purchase amazing technology at great prices now, ahead of the rush on 29th November.”

Does the Currys Price Guarantee work over Black Friday?

Under the Currys Price Guarantee shoppers won't have to hold out until 29 November to get the best price for coveted items they purchase.

Under the pledge, Currys will refund any items bought now in the 'Why Wait' deals that go down further on Black Friday .

How it works?

Currys will price match any items before you buy against the price of any UK rival retailer - whether you spot it in store or online - and the chain will even price match against discount code offers.

Shoppers can pop into store or contact Currys customer service teams online or via telephone - check details online at currys.co.uk/price-promise.

You can also price match on items you've bought up to a week afterwards and the chain will refund the difference.

What happened in 2018?

 

Last year Currys shoppers snapped up 14 orders a second online.

The retailer rolled out massive price cuts across all categories, but the most popular items during the 2018 sale included the Nintendo Switch and Xbox One games consoles as well as the popular Apple iPad mini.

Other highlights included the Dyson V6 vacuum cleaners which was slashed to under £200 and the Sonos One Wireless Smart Sound Speaker which was reduced to £174.

For Black Friday 2018 Currys opened 327 of its stores nationwide from 7am for earlybird shoppers to browse discounted stock before work.

Most of the deal continued over the weekend and throughout Cyber Monday online and in stores for customers who still wanted to pick up more tech bargains.
