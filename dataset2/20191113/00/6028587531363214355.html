Holiday Gift Guide 2019: The Best Cannabis Gear For Travel

13 Nov 2019 by Katie Shapiro

While traveling with cannabis is still technically illegal—even when you’re going to or coming from a legal state, products like smell-proof containers and luxe leather cases are essential for travelers willing to risk transporting their stash with subtlety. Plus, TSA recently stated, "We’re cool and we’re not looking for your weed.” So for the jet-setting cannabis enthusiast in your life, here are the eight best gifts to make heading home for the holidays a little less stressful this season.

Another Room New Kit, $88

Designed and hand-sewn in Vancouver, British Columbia, Another Room’s New Kit is all you need to smoke weed wherever you land. The clear carrying case comes complete with a signature Doob Tube, Joint Locker, and smell-proof pouch for flower with tons of extra room for more supplies. Surfaceless? The door of the bag doubles as a makeshift rolling tray. anotherroom.io

SilverStick Wallet Dugout, $99

Sturdy, discreet, and sleek, SilverStick is the dugout of choice for the day-to-day and is so compact, fits in the palm of your hand. Designed in Boulder, Colo. and handcrafted in the United States from Horween leather and European veg-tanned hides, its just-released wallet version holds the signature SilverStick one-hitter pipe, flower container, lighter, poker, and filters with a flap on the back to hold your ID, cash, and a credit card. thesilverstick.com

Green Jay Travel Set, $55

Every on-the-go quiver should have one of these high-grade aluminum tubes in it, perfect for packing pre-rolls. Available in white, black, and rose gold, Green Jay’s Single Carrier is airtight, waterproof, and discreet with grooves in the cap to keep it in place on flat surfaces and to secure the joint tip on the inside. Pair it with the Smooth Grinder, a four-piece magnetic system and windproof E-Lighter for a complete travel kit. green-jay.com

Tetra Travel Kit, $90

Ready, set, roll anywhere with Tetra’s handpicked set of essentials: private-label unbleached hemp rolling papers, a white bronze Fog Pin for packing and fine-tuning, a flameless Slide lighter, airtight storage container, and a jewel-like bottle of Japanese eye drops—all encased in a leather zip pouch with keyring by Minor History. shop-tetra.com

Mister Green x Dangle Bong, $139

In collaboration with Mister Green and the introduction of its new outdoor GEAR line, Dangle Supply has created a limited-edition, custom painted titanium adventure bong that includes a rubber stopper, machined titanium bowl, and branded carabiner. Dubbed the Dangle Bong, the camp mug-esque construction is indestructible, non-corroding, and was developed by a professional mountain biker and a bicycle designer/engineer. green-mister.com

PAX 3, $249.99; PAX Era, $29.99

PAX provides the easiest and most packable portable vaporizers hands down. Its PAX 3 device is compatible with both flower and concentrates, has more than 60 temperature settings, and can be controlled through its Bluetooth functionality and mobile app. The slimmer PAX Era device is outfitted for its patented pods (with dispensary partners spanning all legal states), each containing 500 milligrams of pure cannabis oil. The new PodID addition to the PAX app addresses the cannabis industry’s transparency issues with batch number matching, lab test results, and terpene profiles so you know exactly what you’re inhaling. pax.com

Cannabolish Set, $29.99

Despite the many advances in accoutrements, the smell left behind when smoking flower is still unavoidable. Ditch the harsh chemicals of old school odor control products like Ozium in favor of Cannabolish—an all-natural spray and candle line to clear the air around you. Made from natural plant oils and water, it’s effective in removing the scent of smoke quickly and is completely safe for use around plants, animals, and people. cannabolish.com

Cannador Leather Toolkit, $49

Cannabis storage company Cannador tapped a 100-year-old family leather business to create a toolkit for organizing all of your accoutrements in five separate pockets. An interior flap keeps it from falling out and stays closed with an exterior button strap. cannador.com
