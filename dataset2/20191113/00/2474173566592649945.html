Our Top 5 Must-Try Products From Avon Distillery

13 Nov 2019 by Daniela Morosini

Clean, vegan and sustainable, these products are as kind to the planet as they are to your skin.

Whether you’re trying to make your beauty routine vegan to match your diet or you’re just trying to live a little more sustainably, you’ve probably noticed that ethical skincare can be a little bit difficult. Historically, plumping for the vegan option meant compromising on texture or efficacy, leaving you with the choice between a product your skin loved, and a product made with love. Happily, that’s all changing, as Avon has married together its cutting-edge skin science with vegan formulas, and impressive green credentials, like recyclable packaging and low-water formulations where possible to minimise waste in Avon Distillery.

Allergy and clinically tested, the range has the comfort of super-scientific formula (all your favourite heavy-hitters like Vitamin C feature) alongside an ethical commitment to cleaner living. Want it? These are the five products that went straight to the top of our want list…

Sun protection is often hard to find in vegan formulas, but not any more. Combining a 100% mineral sunscreen with skin-loving ingredients like plant-based glycerine, this is the ultimate vegan SPF day cream. The souffle-light texture melts into the skin, while also helping to soothe and calm the skin, it’s completely fragrance and alcohol free. Instead, it leaves the skin feeling supple, plump and crucially – protected. That glycerine also helps lock in moisture, too.

distillery Shade the Day SPF25 Day Cream, £18, available at Avon

Sometimes, you have to break out the big guns, and that’s where this bad boy comes in. Pure Vitamin C in a powder form (that’s to protect its potency, as when it comes into contact with water, it starts to degrade) fused with Vitamin E for a mega-dose of brightness, think of this like a vitality injection for the skin. It immediately imparts radiance and can be mixed with any serum or moisturiser for easy application. Obsession-worthy. And of course, it’s totally vegan and formulated in an eco-conscious way.

distillery Vitamin C-Shot Powder, £22, available at Avon

With fatty acid rich jojoba oil and Vitamin E, this lightweight facial oil offers instant hydration without the greasiness. Helping to lend a more even radiance to the skin, it’s ideal for all skin types, but works especially well for dull and congested complexions. You can use it morning and night for round-the-clock hydration, mix it with moisturiser to make the perfect make-up primer or even use it for targeted radiance, patting it over the high points of your face for a healthy glow. Cloudberry extract adds an antioxidant hit to protect your skin’s radiance in the long term, too.

Avon Purify Facial Oil, £22, available at Avon

Cleansing really can be the pleasure centre of your routine when you do it right. Really taking the time to massage in something balmy and hydrating can feel oh-so-decadent, and Clean Break Cleanser is perfect for that. Laden with apricot oil and mango butter, the uplifting natural scent and creamy texture effortlessly melts away makeup and leaves the skin feeling soft and plump. Cocoa butter adds an extra hefty hit of hydration, while the cucumber extract is super soothing, making it perfect for sensitive skin. It’s also perfect for giving a little slip to the skin so you can do a radiance-boosting facial massage. Delicious all around.

distillery Clean Break Cleanser, £15, available at Avon

This night cream is a serious overachiever. Vegan? Check. Pore-shrinking? Check (the niacinamide). Hydrating? Check (sodium hyaluronate and glycerin). 48 hours of hydration? Check! The last word in beauty sleep, this clear gel-cream is perfect for all skin types and works to address numerous skin woes at once. The gel texture means it won’t feel sticky, while niacinamide, aka vitamin B3, is a water-soluble vitamin that works with natural substances in your skin to help shrink the appearance of pores and fine lines, as well as having an effect on fine lines caused by dehydration. A must-try. 

distillery Sleep Potion Night Cream, £18, available at Avon
