‘The Witcher’ Renewed For A Second Season On Netflix

13 Nov 2019 by Erik Kain

Netflix must have faith in upcoming fantasy epic The Witcher. The streaming TV show, based on the books by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski, is already confirmed for a second season. That’s great news for fans and for star Henry Cavill who plays the lead, Geralt of Rivia.

Cavill is a big fan of the books and the video games, apparently, and stars alongside Freya Allan as Ciri, Anya Chalotra as Yennefer and Anna Shaffer as Triss–not to mention his trusty steed(s) Roach.

The Witcher looks terrific–a fantasy-horror-monster-hunting-epic that’s quite a bit different than something like Game Of Thrones, but should appeal to the same audience.
