Rihanna Says She’s Had An ‘Overwhelming’ Year, Asks Family And Friends To ‘Forgive’ Her

13 Nov 2019, 07:39 by Becca Longmire

Rihanna appeared to confirm she’d be taking a break from Instagram as she spoke about her “overwhelming” year in her latest social media post.

RiRi shared a fierce selfie alongside a message asking her family, friends, and co-workers to forgive her for not getting back to them.

She also said she was going to work on her work/life balance, ending the post with “Brb.”

It’s no surprise Rihanna needs a break, as she’s been hard at work with her Savage x Fenty lingerie collection and her luxury Fenty clothing line, among other projects.

She’s also thought to have been working on a new album, despite hinting it won’t be released for a while yet.

ET Canada has contacted Rihanna’s rep for comment.
