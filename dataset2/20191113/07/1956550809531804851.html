Nine top nights out in Dubai this weekend

International superstars, festivals, big DJ nights and more

13 Nov 2019, 07:52 by Reema Rahman

Making your plans for things to do at the weekend? Here are nine top nights to try.

Whether you're looking to catch a Grammy-award winning artist, hit a pumping party at a top club or spend the evening in fits of laughter, we've got you covered.

Drai’s Dubai
Get ready for two nights of star-studded performances at the award-winning club – find out who’s performing here.
Free (ladies), Dhs200 (gents). Thu Nov 14 & Fri Nov 15, 10pm-4am. Meydan Racecourse, Nad Al Sheba (052 388 8857).

Dua Lipa
The New Rules singer will be performing at the Palm Jumeirah.
From Dhs250. Fri Nov 15, 8pm. The Pointe, Palm Jumeirah, www.ticketmaster.ae.

Dubai Opera
UK soul singer Mica Paris is heading to Dubai for a touching tribute to Aretha Franklin.
From Dhs150. Thu Nov 14, 8pm. Dubai Opera, Downtown Dubai, www.dubaiopera.com.

Groove on the Grass
The dance festival is celebrating its eighth anniversary with a mega line-up – get all the details here.
From Dhs225. Fri Nov 15, 5pm-3am. Emirates Golf Club, Emirates Hills, dubai.platinumlist.net.

One Africa Music Fest
Festivalgoers can expect top tunes from Wizkid, Burna Boy, Teni, Tekno and more.
From Dhs200. Fri Nov 15, 6pm-3am. Festival Arena, Dubai Festival City, www.800tickets.com.

Soho Garden
DJ and producer Marco Carola will be hitting the decks at the revamped party destination.
Free. Fri Nov 15, 10pm-4am. Meydan Racecourse, Nad Al Sheba (052 388 8849).

The Irish Village
Scottish pop-rockers Deacon Blue will be hitting the open-air stage at the iconic live music venue.
Dhs175. Thu Nov 14, 9pm-11pm. Garhoud, www.theirishvillage.com (04 239 5000).

The Laughter Factory
The November edition stars comedians Brennan Reece, Rich Wilson and Adam Rowe.
From Dhs160. Thu Nov 14-Fri Nov 22. Various locations, www.thelaughterfactory.com.

Zero Gravity
The dynamic Lovely Laura and Ben Santiago are one of the most demanded duos in Ibiza. No strangers to Dubai and Zero Gravity, they’re back for a night of sax-infused tunes at the Tropical Brunch after-party.

Free (ladies), Dhs100 (gents). Fri Nov 1, 5pm-3am. Skydive Dubai Drop Zone, Dubai Marina (04 399 0009).
