Impeachment Eve

On Wednesday, the House will begin with public impeachment hearings into President Trump. Stephen Colbert celebrated “Impeachment Eve” on Tuesday night, decorating an “impeachment tree” with “all the traditional ornaments” including Trump’s “perfect phone call” transcript as a tree-topper and some “quid pro snow.”
