Four senior Immigration Officers kidnapped in Rivers

13 Nov 2019, 04:28 by The Eagle Online

Four senior officers of the Nigeria Immigration Service have been kidnapped in Rivers State, The Nation is reporting.

According to the newspaper, the Officers were kidnapped on Monday morning on Bonny waterway in Rivers State.

A Correspondent of the newspaper said the development was revealed to it by an indigene of the area.
