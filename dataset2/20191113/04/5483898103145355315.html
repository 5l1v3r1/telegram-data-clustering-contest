Reports A Police Operation Is Underway Near An Adelaide School

13 Nov 2019, 04:57

UPDATE, 4:02pm -- A Subaru stolen from Cowandilla this morning has been recovered by police.

According to SAPOL:

Just before 12pm today (Wednesday 13 November) police were called to a house in Bagot Avenue, after reports that two men and a woman assaulted the victim with an unknown weapon, stole property from his house and also stole his 1997 silver Subaru sedan from a nearby car park.

The victim suffered minor injuries and was taken to hospital for treatment.

Police spotted the stolen car in the Henley Beach area about 1.30pm.  The stolen car was seized by police and towed away for forensic examination.

A female suspect was taken into custody and taken by ambulance to the Queen Elizabeth Hospital for a medical condition.

A male suspect was spotted going into a house in Cheadle Street and police are working to apprehend him safely and without incident.

A nearby school was locked down as a precaution due to the proximity of the police operation.  Police deployed resources to the school to safely coordinate school pick-up and patrols will remain within the school grounds.

EARLIER...

3:27pm -- FIVEaa understands a significant police presence is on the scene near Fulham North Primary School in response to an incident in the area.

An eyewitness who was at the school to collect her children says police and paramedics are in attendance and Cheadle Street is blocked off.

According to the eyewitness police are in bulletproof vests and man is yelling in the street.

SAPOL has confirmed they are responding to an incident in the area.
