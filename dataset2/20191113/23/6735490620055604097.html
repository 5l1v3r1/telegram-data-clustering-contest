2019 CMA Awards: Complete List Of This Year’s Winners

13 Nov 2019, 23:42 by Shakiel Mahjouri

It’s a wrap for the 2019 CMA Awards, celebrating the year’s biggest and best in country music.

Wednesday’s awards show took a decidedly female focus, honouring women in country music with a star-studded array of performers, including the hosting power trio of Carrie Underwood, Reba McEntire and living legend Dolly Parton.

Among this year’s winners were Garth Brooks, honoured with Entertainer of the Year, while Luke Combs won for both Male Vocalist of the Year and Song of the Year, with Kacey Musgraves winning Female Vocalist of the Year.

Here’s the complete list of this year’s winners”

Musical Event of the Year

WINNER: Lil Nas X and Billy Ray Cyrus, “Old Town Road (Remix)”
Maren Morris and Brothers Osborne, “All My Favorite People”
Brooks & Dunn and Luke Combs, “Brand New Man”
Garth Brooks and Blake Shelton, “Dive Bar”
Brantley Gilbert and Lindsay Ell, “What Happens in a Small Town”
