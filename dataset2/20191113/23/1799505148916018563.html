Commercial Sale of Sex

13 Nov 2019, 23:54 by BRIEF

DU PAGE COUNTY, Ill. – There is no constitutional right to the commercial sale of sex, an appeals court in Illinois ruled. A woman convicted of prostitution classified her right to engage in such activity as a fundamental right to privacy.
