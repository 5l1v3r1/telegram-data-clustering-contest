Leeds United star makes interesting claim about Nottingham Forest's City Ground

Latest Nottingham Forest news | The Reds faced Leeds in the Sky Bet Championship at Elland Road in August, where they were held to a 1-1 draw

13 Nov 2019, 21:20 by Mark Wakefield

Leeds United captain Liam Cooper says the City Ground is one of his favourite away stadiums to visit in the Sky Bet Championship.

Nottingham Forest are yet to welcome the Whites to Trentside this season, but have already made the trip to Elland Road.

A late goal from Lewis Grabban saw the Reds secure a 1-1 draw with Marcelo Bielsa’s side in August.

The last time Forest faced Leeds at the City Ground was last season on New Year’s Day, when the Reds claimed a 4-2 win.

A brace from Jack Colback supported by goals from Daryl Murphy and Ben Osborn sealed with victory, as Leeds midfielder was sent off shortly before half time.

Cooper was recently speaking alongside Mateusz Klich and Ben White at a Q&A event at Elland Road, where he was asked for his favourite and least favourite away stadium in the Championship.

"Favourite (away ground) would probably be – I like the Forest ground", said Cooper.
