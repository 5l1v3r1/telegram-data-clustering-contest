KBS News states the police never considered summoning the 'Produce' trainees

13 Nov 2019, 21:19 by honeycider

KBS News recently released a statement saying that 'Produce 48' and 'Produce X 101' trainees were never considered for summons since the allegations are related to the staff members.

This comes after rumors of X1 members being summoned for questioning and X1's reps denying these rumors.

You can read and see a rough translation their statement below.

"The police also said that "the nature of the case is undermining the fairness of the crew," and the summons investigation, which is a reference for the trainees who appeared in the program, is not being examined at all."
