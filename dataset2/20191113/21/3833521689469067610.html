WeWork’s Q3 loss doubled to US$1.3B as IPO faltered

13 Nov 2019, 21:46 by Ellen Huet

WeWork reported a net loss of US$1.25 billion in the third quarter, eclipsing its sales and more than doubling its loss from the same period last year. The quarter coincided with a spending spree in anticipation of the initial public offering, which failed.

Revenue in the quarter was US$934 million, up from US$482 million a year earlier, according to a financial document that was presented to bondholders Wednesday and reviewed by Bloomberg. A spokeswoman for WeWork parent company We Co. declined to comment on the report.
