Deepika Padukone shares a glimpse of hubby Ranveer Singh’s preparation for their first wedding anniversary

13 Nov 2019, 09:44 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The power couple of the tinsel town Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh are all set to celebrate their one year of blissful marriage on November 14. Both the stars are very much excited to celebrate their special day. The preparations for the big day have already started. A while ago Deepika shared a glimpse of hubby Ranveer Singh prepping for their first wedding anniversary.

Taking to Instagram stories, the 'Padmaavat' actress shared a picture of Ranveer, getting done some beauty treatments to look his best on the special day. In the picture, we can see that Ranveer has put on a face mask and his hair is in the steamer. “In preparation for his first wedding anniversary,” Deepika captioned the click.

ETimes had reported recently that Deepika and Ranveer are going to celebrate their first wedding anniversary in the most divine way. It is going to be a family affair for the couple and they have decided to celebrate it in a most private manner. According to the plan, DeepVeer along with their respective families would be heading first to Tirupati and then to Amritsar in order to seek almighty's blessings.

Meanwhile, Deepika and Ranveer tied the knot in Italy’s Lake Como on November 14, 2018 in a highly private wedding affair.
