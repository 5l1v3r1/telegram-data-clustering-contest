Pensioner left terrified after being targeted by hooded pervert

The woman, 71, was sitting her her car in Renfrew when the man touched himself in front of her.

13 Nov 2019, 09:35 by David Campbell

A hooded pervert touched himself in front of a terrified pensioner as she sat in her car.

Cops have launched a manhunt for the weirdo who targeted the 71-year-old woman in Newmains, Renfrew.

The shocking incident, which happened around 8.30pm, came to light when the “badly shaken” victim reported it to police days later.

Police say the victim was sitting in her car when the creep approached her window and began rubbing his groin before making
off.

She was left badly shaken by the ordeal and now police are desperate to trace the fiend.

He is described as being a white male, wearing dark clothing, with his hood up.

Inspector Jim Cast, from Johnstone, Renfrew and the villages community policing team, appealed for witnesses to come forward to help identify suspect.

He told the Paisley Daily Express: “The victim was sitting in her car in Newmains, Renfrew at around 8.30pm on Saturday night when she was approached by an unknown male.

“This was clearly a very distressing incident for the victim who has been left badly shaken by what happened to her.

“We are offering her our full support.

“We are clearly very keen to identify who this individual is and we would appeal to anyone who may have seen what happened to come forward.”

He added: “We are carrying out a range of enquiries, including checking CCTV and speaking to potential witnesses.

“I would urge anyone who has CCTV in the area to speak to officers if they have not already done so.

“I would also ask any drivers who were in the area to check dashcam footage to see if they may have captured anything significant.”

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
