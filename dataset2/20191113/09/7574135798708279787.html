James Blake playing solo piano shows in NYC & LA

13 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Andrew Sacher

James Blake is once again coming to the US for some rare solo piano shows. He’ll play December 10 & 11 at the Theater at the Ace Hotel in LA and December 17 & 28 at BAM’s Harvey Theatre in Brooklyn. Tickets for LA and BK go on sale Friday (11/15) at noon local time.
