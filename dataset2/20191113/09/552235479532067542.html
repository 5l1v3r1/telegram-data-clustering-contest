Talented youngsters at Starr Studio Dance School receive top awards

Dance teacher Andrew Starr said: “The troupe have scooped silverware galore this year."

13 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Leona Greenan, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/leona-greenan/

It was another successful month for the young pupils from Blantyre-based Starr Studio dance school at their most recent performances.

First up was the popular dance competition, held at Maryhill Community Centre on Sunday, October 20, where all the girls received top awards for their routines.

Next up was the successful British Association of Teachers of Dancing showcase on Sunday, October 27 at Motherwell Civic Centre, which saw the pupils deliver an outstanding performance to a packed audience.

Dance teacher Andrew Starr said: “The troupe have scooped silverware galore this year and we are encouraging any youngster who wishes to get involved to pop along to St Andrew’s Church Hall, Blantyre, on a Thursday from 5.30pm to 6.30pm.

“We enter freestyle/street dance competitions locally welcome any young dancer wishing to join our successful dance troupe to get in touch via email at cstarr4563@gmail.com or call myself on 07743 368880.”
