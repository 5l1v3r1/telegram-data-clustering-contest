Currys PC World launches early Black Friday sale with big Nintendo Switch deal

Pokemon fans will be able to get a cheap console bundle for Black Friday from today

13 Nov 2019, 09:26 by Stephen Hurrell

Currys PC World has launched an early Black Friday sale on the Nintendo Switch, Dyson vacuum cleaners and laptops.

The retailer has cut the price of a Nintendo Switch with the new Pokemon Sword or Shield game by £45 in the headline deal of the day. the bundle is now £279 in what the retailer is calling a 'Black Friday price now'.

Currys PC World regularly takes part in Black Friday, naming its annual sale the 'Black Tag' event. This year Black Friday falls on 29 November but the store has launched discounts over two weeks early with a host of price drops across electrical, computing and kitchen appliances.

Zeena Hill, Go To Market Planning Director for Dixons Carphone says: “Each year, Black Friday continues to prove itself as a key shopping date for customers. At Currys PC World, we’re pleased to give shoppers an opportunity to purchase amazing technology at great prices now, ahead of the rush on 29th November.”

 

Other big discounts in the sale include discounts on HP and Lenovo laptops , with savings of up to £150. It has also cut the price of an LG American Style Fridge Freezer by £450 and a Huawei P30 Lite 128 GB by £50.

It is not the first retailer to cut prices in the run up to Black Friday. Very hit the headlines last week with a big saving on a Nintendo Switch , while Amazon's early Black Friday sale has been cutting prices of robot vacuum cleaners in a series of lightning deals in the run up to Black Friday.

The Currys PC World Black Friday sale will officially begin 24 hours before Black Friday, according to the retailer.

Currys PC World also says it will be holding a second big sale on Cyber Monday on 2 December, where more deals on home appliances and smart phones will go live.

In 2018 the retailer cut up to £700 off the price of TVs on Black Friday, as well as £240 off the price of HP and Lenovo laptops. It sold 72 items per minute during the day.
