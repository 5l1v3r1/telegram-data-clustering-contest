Police investigating two sexual assaults in Pryzm nightclub

Do you recognise this man?

13 Nov 2019, 09:33 by Matt Jarram

Police want to trace a man following reports of two sexual assaults at Pryzm nightclub.

Nottinghamshire Police have released CCTV stills in an attempt to locate a man.

The incidents happened at the Lower Parliament Street nightclub during the early hours of Wednesday, November 6.

It was reported that two women were touched inappropriately by a man.

If you recognise the man pictured or think you can help call Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 64 of 6 November 2019, or call Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
