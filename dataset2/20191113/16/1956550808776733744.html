QE2 Dubai to host massive ’80s-themed New Year’s Eve party

You can extend it into a staycation too

13 Nov 2019, 16:07 by Amy Mathieson

Dig out those shoulder pads and perm your hair, as it’s time for a New Year’s extravaganza.

Dubai goes all out at New Year and to welcome in 2020 the QE2 is hosting a two-night bash. And it’s ’80s-themed.

The QE2’s New Year’s Eve party will feature an international buffet, free-flowing drinks and a live DJ (spinning ‘80s tunes). There will also be a five-piece band playing 1980s hits for you to dance along to. Kids will also be kept entertained with a special kids’ buffet and kids’ area.

Plus, when the clock strikes midnight there will be a prime view of the Downtown Dubai and Burj Khalifa fireworks from the world-famous cruise ship-turned-hotel.

And if one night isn’t enough you can extend it into a two-night staycation. Book a hotel stay to include the New Year’s Eve Gala and you’ll get breakfast thrown in too. Prices start at Dhs2,799 per room.
