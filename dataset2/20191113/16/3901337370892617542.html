In The Boots Of Major: Majoro Launches Youtube Channel

13 Nov 2019, 16:55 by SL Social

In the latest development off the field, Amazulu striker Lehlohonolo Majoro has given fans a behind-the-scenes look into his life, by launching his own YouTube Channel.

The channel is called In The Boots Of Major and the 33-year-old has promised fans never seen before contents of his life.

Speaking on the first episode of the channel, the former Kaizer Chiefs, Bidvest Wits, Orlando Pirates and Cape Town City striker feels the need to be closer to the fans.

“The purpose of this channel is to bridge the gap between supporters and their idols, who are the soccer players,” the Ladybrand-born star can be heard saying.

Among the topics covered by Majoro is his time at Kaizer Chiefs, His first PSL contract as well as his life off the field.
