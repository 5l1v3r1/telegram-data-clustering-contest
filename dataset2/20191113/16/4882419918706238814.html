Time and date given for Catalan derby, Espanyol v Barcelona

La Liga have published for fixtures in the first weekend of January

13 Nov 2019, 16:53 by Sport EN

Barcelona and Espanyol know when they will face each other in the first Catalan derby of the season. La Liga published the fixtures for week 19, including the clash between the two local rivals on Sat Jan 4th. It is each side's first game after the Christmas break.

Fri Jan 3

  • 19.00 horas: Valladolid - Leganés (Movistar LaLiga)
  • 21.00 horas: Sevilla - Athletic (Movistar LaLiga)

Sat Jan 4

  • 13.00 horas: Valencia - Eibar (Movistar LaLiga)
  • 16.00 horas: Getafe - Real Madrid (Movistar LaLiga)
  • 18.30 horas: Atlético de Madrid - Levante (Movistar LaLiga)
  • 21.00 horas: Espanyol - Barcelona (Movistar LaLiga)

Sun Jan 5

  • 12.00 horas: Granada - Mallorca (Movistar LaLiga)
  • 14.00 horas: Real Sociedad - Villarreal (GOL)
  • 16.00 horas: Alavés - Betis (Movistar LaLiga)
  • 21.00 horas: Celta - Osasuna (Movistar LaLiga)
