With the diva, herself says Jacqueline Fernandez as she shares a fangirl moment with Katy Perry

13 Nov 2019, 06:01 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood actress Jacqueline Fernandez had a fan moment as she got a chance to meet the popular American singer-songwriter Katy Perry who has arrived in Mumbai ahead of the music concert.

The actress took to her social media handles to share an adorable selfie with the singer. In the picture, Jacqueline was seen wearing a fur-sleeved short dress while Katy was dressed in a pink dress with yellow polka dots.

Sharing her excitement with her fans, the 34-year-old actor wrote, "With the diva herself! @katyperry Are you guys ready for Nov 16th? "

Katy is going to perform on November 16. She will also be joined by another international sensation, Dua Lipa.

Meanwhile, yesterday the American songwriter attended a press conference along with Jacqueline. This is not the first time that Katy has visited India. Back in 2010, she had visited the country along with her then-boyfriend Russell Brand.
