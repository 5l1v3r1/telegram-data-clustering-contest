Idris Elba and The Rock react as John Legend is named world’s sexiest man

13 Nov 2019, 06:29

Idris Elba and Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson have congratulated John Legend after the US singer succeeded them in being named sexiest man alive by People magazine.

Legend, a 40-year-old multiple Grammy Award winner, graces the magazine’s cover alongside the caption “how a good guy got it all”.

He admitted to being “perplexed” at being honoured with the title of sexiest man alive.
