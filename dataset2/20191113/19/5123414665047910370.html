Premiere: Blumey Releases Slumped, Eerie New Track "Whale Talk" [NS001]

These articles will focus on specific EP premieres/reviews for certain artists/labels within the 140 scene and beyond. This one features the Blumey with his debut release on Kotei's own label "Natural Selection."

13 Nov 2019, 19:35 by Benjamin Oliver Lloyd

Blumey comes correct on his forthcoming EP via the label Natural Selection, roping in both Noble and King Monday to remix one of the track's "Whale Talk." A young producer that makes various sounds across the 140 spectrum, Blumey focuses on the more darker elements of dubstep & 140 music. We were given the chance to premiere the original track "Whale Talk," which you can listen too below.
