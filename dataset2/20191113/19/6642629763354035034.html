China's biggest automaker hopes to triple overseas sales by 2025

As China's overall market for automobiles continues to decline, SAIC Motor hopes to boost revenue with overseas sales.

13 Nov 2019, 19:26

China's largest carmaker, SAIC Motor Corp Ltd, wants to triple its overseas auto sales to one million units by 2025, a senior company official said on Wednesday, as more Chinese auto manufacturers seek sales growth globally.

The Shanghai-based company, which sold around seven million vehicles last year with its brands and through joint ventures with Volkswagen and General Motors, makes up just under a quarter of China's auto market, the world's largest.
