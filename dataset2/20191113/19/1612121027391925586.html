[Deal] Save up to £100 on these Honor smartphones ahead of Black Friday

13 Nov 2019, 19:01 by Peter Holden

If you are waiting for Black Friday to buy a new handset perhaps there’s a case for instant gratification because Honor has reduced a number of its bestselling smartphones by up to £100 in the UK. The deals include the Honor 20, Honor 20 Pro, Honor 20 Lite, and the Honor 10 Lite, with savings of £30-100 to be had when bought from a variety of retailers. We’ve got the details for you after the break.

Honor 20 Pro

First up is the Honor 20 Pro that boasts a quad-camera setup that includes a 48MP main sensor with Dual Optical Image Stabilization for blur-free images as well as the remarkable Super Night Mode. Powered by the Kirin 980 processor that we’ve seen in handsets such as the Mate 20 Pro and P30 Pro, the Honor 20 Pro also features 8GB of RAM and 256GB of built-in storage. Usually priced at £549.99, you can save £100 by grabbing the Honor 20 Pro for just £449.

Buy it Now: Amazon, Very, Carphone Warehouse, John Lewis

Honor 20

If the Honor 20 Pro is a little too much phone for you, then perhaps the slightly lower specced, and thus cheaper, Honor 20 (reviewed here) is the phone for you. With 128GB of storage and 6GB of RAM, the Honor 20 is still powered by the same Kirin 980 processor, and also features GPU Turbo 3.0. It has dual-SIM connectivity, a 6.26-inch FHD+ IPS display, and three rear cameras consisting of a 48MP main sensor, a 16MP wide-angle lens, a 2MP depth-of-field sensor, and a 2MP dedicated macro lens. The Honor 20 usually costs £369 but it has been discounted to just £329 in the run-up to Black Friday.

Buy it Now: Amazon, Very, Carphone Warehouse, John Lewis

Honor 20 Lite

The Honor 20 Lite is the third, and cheapest in the Series 20 lineup but still manages to pack a punch with its Kirin 710 processor, 4GB of RAM, 128GB of storage. The rear panel sports a trio of cameras consisting of a 24MP main sensor, 8MP ultra-wide lens, and a 2MP depth-of-field sensor, there is also a 24MP selfie camera present. The Honor 20 Lite has been discounted from £299 to just £199, giving you an impressive £100 saving.

Buy it Now: Amazon, Very, Carphone Warehouse, John Lewis

Honor 10 Lite

We were impressed by the Honor 10 Lite when we reviewed it at the beginning of 2019, and now the handset is available for just £149, giving a saving of £30. Featuring a 6.21-inch FHD+ display, the Kirin 710 processor, 3GB of RAM, and 64GB of storage, the 10 Lite also boasts dual-SIM connectivity, dual rear cameras, and a 3.5mm headphone jack.

Buy it Now: Amazon, Very, Carphone Warehouse, John Lewis
