3.9 earthquake hits outside of Trona, no damage reported

13 Nov 2019, 19:59

TRONA, Calif. (KABC) -- A 3.9 magnitude earthquake hit outside the community of Trona Wednesday.

The quake hit at 11:26 a.m. about 11 miles south of Trona at a depth of 4 miles, according to the USGS.

No significant damage was reported.
