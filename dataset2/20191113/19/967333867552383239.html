Going Downhill: Cycling’s Litany of Doping Scandals Over The Years

13 Nov 2019, 19:04

As the former head coach of British Cycling and Team Sky Shane Sutton stormed out of a medical tribunal in Manchester on Tuesday after being accused of doping, Sputnik decided to take a look at cycling’s long history of doping scandals.

Sutton became enraged by the line of questioning from Mary O’Rourke QC, a barrister representing Team Sky’s former doctor Richard Freeman, who is facing a fitness to practice tribunal.

Freeman, who is facing a charge of medical misconduct from the General Medical Council, could be struck off if found guilty.
