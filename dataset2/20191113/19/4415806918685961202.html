Love Island’s Maura Higgins leads the best-dressed celebs on the ITV Palooza red carpet

13 Nov 2019, 19:39

TV stars were out in force for ITV Palooza, the annual event which celebrates the channel’s showbiz successes.

Alongside presenters Holly Willoughby and Susanna Reid there was reality TV royalty in the form of Gemma Collins and fellow TOWIE alumni Jess Wright with brother Mark.

Plus, there was a major Love Island reunion, with contestants from the last two series of the dating show bringing plenty of glitz and glam to proceedings.

Here’s who wowed on the red carpet…

Maura Higgins

The outspoken Irish star of Love Island 2019 was a vision in lace, her plunging black gown accessorised with a statement belt, mini bag and strappy heels.

Holly Willoughby

TV presenter and fashion icon Holly Willoughby looked simply stunning in a black velvet midi dress with chain detailing by Christopher Kane, which she teamed with black Gina court heels.

Amber Gill and Yewande Biala

Love Island besties Amber Gill and Yewande Biala were the golden girls of the night, appearing on the red carpet together in sparky figure-hugging gowns.

Meghan McKenna

Former TOWIE star McKenna, who is currently taking part in Celebrity X Factor, was glamorous as ever in a sparkly black mesh gown with an open back.

Georgia Steel

We don’t know if she was inspired by Zoolander’s famous ‘blue steel’ pose but Love Island 2018 favourite Georgia Steel royal blue frock was a real show-stopper, the mini wrap dress featuring an extra-long train.

Zara McDermott

Steel’s Love Island BFF Zara McDermott also opted for a blue outfit, her navy satin gown accessorised with sparkly silver peep-toe heels.

Jess Wright

Black was a big trend on the ITV Palooza red carpet and Jess Wright was one of many celebs who choose a monochrome ensemble. The former TOWIE star teamed her simple black crop top and skirt with a cool art deco-style clutch.

Caroline Flack

While all the Love Islanders opted for skimpy or bodycon dresses, series host Caroline Flack stuck to her signature style, looking chic in a sequinned shorts suit worn with a black roll neck and an on-trend beaded bag.
