Maiden Teqball Tournament slated for 15th - 16th in Agogo

13 Nov 2019, 19:26 by Emmanuel Asante Attakora, GNA

Accra, Nov. 13. GNA - The first ever Teqball Tournament in Ghana aimed at creating fun and togetherness for the environs of the Asante Akim North Municipal District has been scheduled for Agogo from Friday 15th to Saturday 16th November, 2019.

The sport, which is played between two players as a single game or between three to four players as doubles game, and played on a curved table, with football hit by any part of the body except the hands is being introduced to the Asante Akim North Municipal district by former Member of Parliament for the and now Board Chairman for National Sports Authority (NSA) Mr Kwadwo Baah Agyemang.

According to Mr. Baah Agyemang, the game which is fast aiming for Olympics Games inclusion since its invention in 2014 in Hungary is a way of helping the Asante Akim and Utah environs to have a feel of how the game is played and if possible get youth who would be interested in the sport and nurture them.

He said the sport is very fun and entertaining to engage in, which also requires full attention, concentration and discipline.
