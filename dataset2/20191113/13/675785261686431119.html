Victoria Beckham posts touching video of Harper celebrating World Kindness Day

David Beckham made a small appearance in the video, which is one of several Victoria posted to honour World Kindness Day

13 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Verity Sulway, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/verity-sulway/

Victoria Beckham posted a heartwarming video of her eight-year-old daughter Harper Seven celebrating World Kindness Day.

The Spice Girl turned fashion designer has posted several videos today marking the day, urging her fans to be kind to themselves and to one another.

Her hubbie and Harper's dad David Beckham also makes a cameo appearance in the video.

His distinctive hands covered in tattoos clap in front of Harper's face like a Hollywood clapperboard.

"And...action!" he says.

Beaming in her cute purple school uniform, Harper says: Caring is sharing!

"Happy Kindness Day everyone!"

"Kisses from Harper Seven on #worldkindnessday X VB", said the caption.

VB's fans flocked to coo over Harper's sweet video.

"Lovely and she has the lucky tooth gap!", said one, with a four-leaf clover emoji, noting the wide gap in Harper's teeth, an exciting time for any child.

"She's the cutest", gushed another.

"Awwwee she's so adorable...I can't believe she's not a little baby anymore", said a third.

"Luv it becks is the greatest hype man ever, Harper is so adorable", said a fourth.

Taking to her Instagram story, Victoria also gave fans a glimpse into the home life of one of the UK's most famous power couples.

Doting dad David was slicing up Harper's school snack, an apple.

He went to great pains to carve the slices into a work of art, creating the letter H, number 7, and a love heart.

The artistic masterpiece didn't end there.

Victoria revealed he then tried his hand at carving stars out of the apples.

"Pretty sure she has the most impressive snack in the class!", Victoria boasted.

She also posted another two videos in honour of World Kindness Day, one of which showed her staff at her fashion line talking about what the day means to them.

Next, VB talked to the camera about the importance of being kind to ourselves and other people, in a video where she is sitting on a sofa in a silk gown.

"Being kind is the most important thing", she says.
