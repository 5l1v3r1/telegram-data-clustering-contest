Suspects sought for using stolen credit cards in North Fort Myers

13 Nov 2019, 13:40 by WFTX Digital Team

CHARLOTTE COUNTY, Fla. -- Police are working on identifying three people accused of using a stolen credit card in North Fort Myers last month.

According to Punta Gorda Police, the investigation started regarding several vehicle burglaries on October 29, 2019.

They say the people pictured here used a victim's stolen credit card to purchase a large number of items at the Walmart in North Fort Myers.

They were seen leaving in a black in color Dodge Charger.

If you have any information, please contact Southwest Florida Crime Stoppers by calling 1-800-780-TIPS (8477). You may be eligible for a cash reward for information leading to an arrest in this case.
