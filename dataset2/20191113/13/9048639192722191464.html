Monroe shelter opens 24 hours a day due to cold weather

13 Nov 2019, 13:18 by KNOE Staff

MONROE, La. (KNOE) - The Salvation Army shelter in Monroe is now operating all day and night due to recent cold temperatures hitting the area.

The Salvation Army is not turning anybody away during 24-hour operations. They have 20 cots in addition to the beds the shelter already has.

They say this operation will continue throughout the rest of the week.

The shelter is located at 105 Hart St, Monroe, LA 71201.
