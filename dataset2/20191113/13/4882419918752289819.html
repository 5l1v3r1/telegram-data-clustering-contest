Dutch coach Ronald Koeman speaks about taking over at Barça

There's a clause in his contract which would allow for Camp Nou move in 2020

13 Nov 2019, 13:28 by sport.es

Former Barcelona player Ronald Koeman, who is currently in charge of the Dutch national team, once again has had to respond to questions about his future.

It's been revealed recently that there's a clause in his deal which would allow him to leave his current job if Barcelona come in for him after next summer's European Championships.

This time, he was much more discrete than in previous questions, as if he's grown fed up of being asked about the same thing.

He has always been linked with Barcelona since he hung up his boots and he said that "moving there is a possibility but I will not think about it until after Euro 2020."
