Harry Styles announces UK tour for 2020 - and it starts in Birmingham

Love On Tour will begin at the Birmingham Arena on April 15 before visiting Sheffield, Dublin, Manchester and Glasgow

13 Nov 2019, 13:44 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Harry Styles has announced he will embark on a world tour in 2020 in support of his forthcoming solo album Fine Line.

Love On Tour will begin at the Birmingham Arena on April 15 before visiting Sheffield, Dublin, Manchester and Glasgow.

Harry, who is releasing his second solo album 'Fine Line' on Friday 13 December, will perform his biggest hits across the UK.

The lead single from the record, 'Lights Up', came out back in October.

The track reached an impressive No. 3 on the UK singles chart.

 

Styles shot to fame as one fifth of the chart-topping, record-breaking boyband One Direction.

The singer rose to fame on The X Factor alongside his 1D bandmates, who included Wolverhampton singer Liam Payne.

Tickets for Harry's 2020 tour go on sale at 10am on Friday November 22.

 

Harry Styles 2020 UK tour dates are listed in full below:

Wed 15 Apr – Arena Birmingham
Thu 17 Apr– FlyDSA Arena, Sheffield
Wed 22 Apr – The O2, London
Thu 23 Apr – The O2, London
Sat 25 April – Manchester Arena, Manchester
Sun 26 April – The SSE Hydro, Glasgow

Tickets for Harry's 2020 tour go on sale at The Ticket Factory from 10am on Friday November 22.
