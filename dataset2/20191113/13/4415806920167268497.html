Man due in court in connection with €90k cannabis seizure

13 Nov 2019, 13:49 by By Digital Desk staff

Gardaí arrested five people and seized €90k worth of cannabis and plants in a raid in Co Meath yesterday.

Local drugs detectives and armed officers carried out the search yesterday in Laytown.

Five men were arrested at the scene, and three of them were later released.

One man has been charged and is due before Dundalk District Court today.
