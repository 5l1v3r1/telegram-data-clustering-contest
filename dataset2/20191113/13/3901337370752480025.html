African Rankings: Chiefs Chase Sundowns

13 Nov 2019, 13:59 by Soccer Laduma

The latest African rankings have been revealed, which shows Kaizer Chiefs not too far off from catching up to Mamelodi Sundowns, despite not challenging in any CAF Competition.

Chiefs have extended their Absa Premiership unbeaten run this past weekend after a 3-2 victory over their neighbours, Orlando Pirates. 

Read more: ‘If Chiefs Want To Go All The Way With Us, They Can Try!’ 

This weekend’s result saw Amakhosi go 10 points clear at the top of the log, after Sundowns’ 3-2 loss to Golden Arrows. 

Bidvest Wits also took a knock this weekend after a 1-0 loss to SuperSport United. 

Here are the top four South African clubs on the African rankings according to footballdatabase.com: 

Sundowns - 14th place 

Chiefs - 18th place 

Wits - 22nd place 

Pirates - 25th place 

Elsewhere in Africa, Esperance de Tunis remains the number one club on the African continent, although Al Ahly is positioned in second place, are not far from catching up to the top spot.

Here are the top 10 African clubs on the continent according to footballdatabase.com:

  1. Esperance de Tunis
  2. Al Ahly
  3. TP Mazembe
  4. Etoile du Sahel
  5. Al-Merreikh
  6. Vita Club
  7. Al Hilal Omdurman
  8. Wydad Casablanca
  9. El Zamalek
  10. CS Sfaxien
