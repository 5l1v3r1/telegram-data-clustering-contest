Gallery: Barca Unveil Special Fourth Kit

13 Nov 2019, 13:29 by David Kappel

FC Barcelona have unveiled a fourth strip for the first time in their history, and it pays homage to the club's 120-year-old history.

Click through the gallery above to see some images of Barca's new fourth kit!

The Spanish giants had previously unveiled their home, away and third strips for the 2019/20 campaign, but have now added a fourth kit featuring diagonal red stripes over a yellow background.

"With the tagline 'Ho portem dins' ('It’s deep within us'), FC Barcelona is presenting a new kit for this season that features the Catalan flag, the senyera, in honour of the club's roots and history: over 120 years the club has grown as an entity that is open to the world, and which views sport as a phenomenon that can build bridges between people. However, this has not meant losing its sense of Catalan identity, culture and values," a statement on the club's website read.

Sergi Roberto, one of the club's four captains added: "It is an honour to wear the flag, the four stripes, on the shirt."

The fourth shirt will be worn by both the men’s and women’s side in selected games throughout the season, the club confirmed.
