Royal Navy carrier HMS Queen Elizabeth joined by missile cruiser in the Atlantic

"Welcome to the Queen Elizabeth Carrier Strike Group"

13 Nov 2019, 13:37 by Rom Preston-Ellis

Royal Navy aircraft carrier HMS Queen Elizabeth has welcomed two new ships to its 'strike group'.

USS Philippine Sea and USS Truxtun, the Ticonderoga-class guided missile cruiser and Arleigh Burke-class destroyer have joined us as UKF35 jet trials continue in the Atlantic off the coast of the USA.
