Did Bitcoin Creator Satoshi Nakamoto Just Move 50 BTC? Litecoin’s Charlie Lee Ponders Mysterious Transfer

13 Nov 2019, 22:30 by Daily Hodl Staff

Litecoin creator Charlie Lee is wondering if the legendary, anonymous creator of Bitcoin known as Satoshi Nakamoto just moved 50 Bitcoin.

The BTC was donated to the team developing Grin, a new cryptocurrency that is focused on confidential transactions, hiding transaction values and making it hard for third parties to track activity.

It uses a private transaction technology called MimbleWimble which is also being tested on Litecoin.

Developer David Burkett announced the receipt of the BTC.

Lee quickly traced the origin of the coins, saying they were mined back in 2010 and had been dormant for nine years.

Several crypto influencers, including Charlie Lee and Binance CEO Changpeng Zhao, have said that the real Satoshi can prove their identity by signing a message with the address from the very first Bitcoin block. Until then, any claims about “the real Satoshi” are likely bogus.
