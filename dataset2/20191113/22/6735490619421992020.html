Lady Antebellum And Halsey Deliver Powerful Genre-Bending Mashup At CMA Awards

13 Nov 2019, 22:34 by Katie Colley

Halsey has made her mark on the CMA Awards stage.

In a never-before-seen collaboration, Halsey joined Lady Antebellum at Nashville’s Bridgestone Arena to perform a genre-bending mashup, blending the trio’s “What If I Never Get Over You” with the pop singer’s “Graveyard”.
