Shah Rukh Khan kick-starts shooting for Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer 'Brahmastra'

13 Nov 2019, 22:23 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt's upcoming film 'Brahmastra' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. Pictures and videos from the sets of the film are all over the internet.

Recently, it was reported that Shah Rukh Khan will be making a special appearance in the film. And now, the superstar has started shooting for his scenes of the film. A tweet which confirms the same have been doing rounds the internet. The user shared a picture of a story post the caption of which read, "Night schedule day 11 with SRK Brahmastra".
