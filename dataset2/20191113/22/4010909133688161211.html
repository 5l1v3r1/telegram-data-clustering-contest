Yoodo Gank will be representing Malaysia in the PMCO Global Finals

13 Nov 2019, 22:17

The PMCO Global Finals will be happening at the Putra World Trade Centre in Kuala Lumpur, from 31 November until 2 December 2019. With that said, Yoodo will be sending their PUBG Mobile Team, Yoodo Gank, one of the highest-ranked Malaysian team to represent Malaysia in the grand finals where they will be among the 16 teams who will be duking it for their share of the RM10.5 million prize pool.
