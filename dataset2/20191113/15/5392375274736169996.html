After Apple released the iPhone 11 series, the iPhone 11 has been the most sort after. The sales of this smartphone have been quite good in China, many thanks to its “low price”. However, it appears that Chinese carriers are still keen on selling this device and they have special marketing strategies for doing so. Presently on Jingdong, the iPhone 11 5G contract version from China Mobile is currently on sale. The interesting part is that it is cheaper than the regular model.

However, the “5G Contract” is just a marketing gimmick as the iPhone 11 does not support the 5G network. What China Mobile means is that you get to pay the same amount and get the same data as 5G standards. So, if you buy this device, you will still be using the regular 4G network.

The network package discount includes 128 yuan ($18), 198 yuan ($28) and 298 yuan ($42). The iPhone 11 is selling for $668 ($18 package), $644 ($28 package) and $691 ($42 package).

In September this year, Apple released three iPhones, iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro and iPhone 11 Pro Max. It comes with its latest A13 bionic chip and it uses a dual rear dual camera. Nevertheless, the iPhone 11 Pro/Max upgrades to a triple rear camera setup
