Logging

13 Nov 2019, 15:54 by BRIEF

MEDFORD, Ore. — The Klamath-Siskiyou Wildlands Center brought a federal complaint over the U.S. Bureau of Land Management’s plan to expand logging in the Klamath Falls Resource Area to 111 million board feet of timber in an already fragmented landscape.
