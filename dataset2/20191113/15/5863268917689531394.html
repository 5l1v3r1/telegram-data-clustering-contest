'Making Nawaz Sharif's departure abroad conditional is unconstitutional'

13 Nov 2019, 15:54 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

LAHORE (Dunya News) – Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) spokesperson Marriyum Aurangzeb has on Wednesday said it is unconstitutional and illegal to make former prime minister Nawaz Sharif’s departure abroad – for medical treatment – conditional.

The PML-N leader maintained that the government is playing a dangerous game with Nawaz Sharif’s health as the erstwhile premier has been given high dose of steroids twice for travelling.

Marriyum Aurangzeb said it is extremely risky to administer high dose of steroids to Nawaz Sharif again as his health condition is very critical

The government has no realization of the PML-N supreme leader’s critical health despite the fact that all legal requirements have already been fulfilled, she said while urging the government to not create hurdles in the erstwhile premier’s treatment.
