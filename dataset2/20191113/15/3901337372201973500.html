Man Utd Have First Option To Re-Sign Dutch Star

13 Nov 2019, 15:30 by SL International

With Manchester United reportedly looking to improve their fortunes by bolstering their squad in January, it has now been revealed that the Premier League giants have a first option to sign a prolific Dutch star who was once on their books.

The Red Devils have endured an indifferent run of form in recent times, and manager Ole Gunnar Solskjaer is reportedly set to shake things up in the January transfer window.

There could be a surprise arrival at Old Trafford too, after it emerged that the club retains a first option to sign a former player of theirs who has been in prolific form for his current team.

Speaking to Olympique Lyonnais' official website, the French club's sporting director Juninho Pernambucano revealed that United had a priority deal in place for the signature of Netherlands star Memphis Depay.
