M&S shoppers delighted by Snow Globe Clementine Gin for Christmas

And it contains edible gold leaf!

13 Nov 2019, 15:43 by Nancy Brown

This is not just any gin, this is Marks & Spencer's Clementine Gin Liqueur Snow Globe – and it's here to make your Christmas festivities merry and bright.

For just £15, you can add some sparkle to your Christmas parties as this zesty clementine gin liqueur contains edible golf leaf.

It creates a magical winter scene when you shake the bottle.

"We need some of this for Christmas!" squealed one delighted shopper.

Can you think of anything more decadent? No? Neither could we, until we discovered the cocktails you can make with the gin.

"Whether served over ice or mixed with prosecco or tonic water, this is a delicious tipple with seasonal sparkle," said Jenny Rea, M&S product developer.

M&S recommends serving your gin liqueur with a splash of prosecco in a champagne flute to make The Bubbly One.

For The Fruity One, add a dash of clementine gin liqueur to a glass tumbler. Pour over a generous splash of tonic and serve with fruity pomegranate ice cubes, made by adding a few pomegranate seeds to your ice cube tray before freezing.

The Neat One does what it says on the tin – simply pour a shot of clementine gin liqueur into a cold martini glass and garnish with a twist of orange peel.

We need some of this for Christmas!

In other gin news, the store is also stocking gin baubles that look perfect when hung from your Christmas tree.

While the gin in a tin range has also got an update with the store's Christmas Dry Gin, blended with festive botanicals, now available in a can.
