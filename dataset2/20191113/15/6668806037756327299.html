22 pictures of Aryan Khan that prove he is a reflection of Shah Rukh Khan

13 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Vedanshi Pathak

Shah Rukh Khan is the Badshah of Bollywood and everything to do with him sells like hotcakes online. The limelight is not just on King Khan but his stardom is such that his family too is constantly being watched by millions. SRK has three children, Aryan, Suhana and AbRam who the media follows closely. Today is King Khan's eldest son Aryan's birthday and on his big day we at Filmfare thought it would be a good idea to treat our readers with pictures of the young gun who turns 22 today.
These pictures are special as Aryan looks extremely similar to his dad in these images. From his charming looks, his demeanor to his smile, Aryan is a reflection of his dad and there is no denying that. The 22-year-old is currently studying abroad and plans to take up direction once he complete his education. If acting is on the cards on not, only time will tell, but with those amazing looks, we wish he decides to come in front of the camera too.
