You Laugh, You Lose: Funniest Animals of 2019 in Pictures

These hilarious and unexpected wildlife photos by finalists in the 2019 Comedy Wildlife Photography Awards prove that animals also have a sense of humour.

13 Nov 2019, 15:30

The Comedy Wildlife Photography Awards is a free global online presentation of funny photos of amazing animals. The contest aims to emphasize the importance of wildlife conservation. Some 4,000 photos from 68 different countries participated in the contest. They were then reduced to 40 finalists.

The winner of the photo contest, Sarah Skinner with her "Grab Life By The..." photo of a little lion will receive a week-long safari in Kenya as a prize.
