Victoria Beckham shares adorable message from daughter Harper on World Kindness Day

Happy Kindness Day

13 Nov 2019, 15:33 by Richard Bell

Victoria Beckham has shared an adorable video featuring her daughter Harper to spread a message of positivity for fans.

To mark World Kindness Day on Wednesday (13.11.10), the former Spice Girl shared a clip that showed the eight-year-old telling followers: "Caring is sharing!"

In the video, dad David claps his hands in front of the camera like a clapperboard and calls out, "And... action" before Harper says with a big smile on her face, "Caring is sharing! Happy Kindness Day everyone!"
