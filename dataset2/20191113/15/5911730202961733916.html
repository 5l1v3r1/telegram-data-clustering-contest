Elton John is coming to Tampa in 2020

13 Nov 2019, 15:17 by Teyonna Edwards

TAMPA, Fla.—Elton John just added a stop in Tampa to his three-year Farewell Yellow Brick Road Tour. He’ll perform at Amalie Arena on May 26 at 8 p.m.

Tampa isn't the only new stop; 23 other cities have been added to the previously sold out tour. These new dates will complete the second year of the North American leg of this world-wide tour. In addition to hearing top hits like “Rocket Man” and “Tiny Dancer”, the audience will get a rare glimpse into Elton John’s life with never before seen photos and videos from his 50-year career.

Tickets go on sale for these new dates on November 22 at 10 a.m. American Express Card Members can purchase tickets in advance from November 14 at 10 a.m. to November 18 at 10 p.m. You can purchase tickets here.
