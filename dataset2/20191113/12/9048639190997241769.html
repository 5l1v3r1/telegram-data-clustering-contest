Trump, Edwards to be in Shreveport/Bossier City on Nov. 14

13 Nov 2019, 12:46 by KNOE

(KNOE) - President Donald Trump is expected to hold a campaign rally in Bossier City ahead of Saturday's election in Louisiana.

The rally is scheduled for 7:00 p.m. at the CenturyLink Center on 2000 CenturyLink Center Drive. Trump is expected to stump for gubernatorial challenger Eddie Rispone. Rispone made an appearance at the Trump rally in Monroe on Nov. 6.

You can get tickets for the Bossier rally here.

Meanwhile, incumbent Governor John Bel Edwards will make a trip to Shreveport to show his appreciation for supporters and volunteers. According to the Edwards campaign, Edwards will at 3924 Greenwood Road at noon on Nov. 14. He is also expected to address President Trump's visit to Louisiana.
