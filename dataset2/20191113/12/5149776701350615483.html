Nottingham bar showing Christmas films and there's unlimited booze with every ticket

A choice of burgers is also included in the price

13 Nov 2019, 12:20 by Rachel Gorman

The Yuletide season just got a whole lot merrier with one Nottingham bar screening Christmas classics - including Die Hard - accompanied by unlimited booze with every ticket purchased.

New venue The Wildlife (located above Billy Bootleggers, in High Pavement), is getting its punters into the festive spirit by hosting a trio of movie nights, where two hours of free-flowing beer or fizz and a burger is included in the Â£25 ticket price.

Kicking off proceedings will be the first two Home Alone movies on December 5, followed by Die Hard on December 12 and Elf on December 19.

Tickets can be purchased in advance on the bar's official Facebook page. One ticket is needed per person per night and includes two hours of unlimited prosecco or Bud as well as something to (sensibly) line everyone's stomachs.

Options from the Hank burger menu include:

The Mr! - a homemade bean burger covered in 'Sloppy Joe' veggie mince, nacho cheese sauce and buffalo sauce.

The Buffalo Chicken Burger - tender southern fried chicken breast with cheese, smothered in hot buffalo sauce on a bed of blue cheese sauce with beef tomato topped with spring onion.

The Black and Blue - seasoned premium beef patty on a bed of sweet onion jam and lettuce, covered in blue cheese sauce and black pudding chunks.

Following the screenings of both Elf and Die Hard, there will also be a late-night party at the quirky new nightspot. Opening last week the venue is decorated with stuffed birds and animals, elephants trumpeting, crickets chirping and projections of hedgehogs and monkeys.
