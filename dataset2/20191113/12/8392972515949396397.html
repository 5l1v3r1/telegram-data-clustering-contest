Teletubbies star Pui Fan Lee makes a guest appearance in EastEnders

She played Dr Fremantle

13 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Children's TV favourite Pui Fan Lee, who starred in Teletubbies, made a guest appearance in last night's EastEnders (November 12 2019).

The actress was in the BBC soap as Dr Fremantle, the doctor conducting assessments on Ollie Carter.

Although fans may not recognise her face, Pui played Po in Teletubbies from 1997 until 2001.
