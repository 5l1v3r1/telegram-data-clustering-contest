‘If Chiefs Want To Go All The Way With Us, They Can Try!’

13 Nov 2019, 12:08 by Soccer Laduma

Mamelodi Sundowns defender, Mosa Lebusa is still confident that Kaizer Chiefs won’t stop them in their pursuit for the Absa Premiership title.

Sundowns suffered their second league defeat at the weekend to Golden Arrows, seeing them remain in second place on the log with 18 points - 10 points behind Chiefs with a game in hand.

Read more: ‘Benni Is Qualified To Do The Job For Bafana...’

Speaking in an exclusive interview with Soccer Laduma this week, Lebusa, who’s in his second season with Sundowns, maintains that Chiefs are not their biggest challengers for the league title this season. 

“The biggest? I don’t think so, man. We run our own race and they also run their own race. We are just focusing on ourselves. If they want to go all the way with us, they can try. I mean, we’ve been doing this for how long now? Even in the season before I came, Sundowns had been doing this. I just came in and added to what they had been doing. As time goes, we will see where they end up, but we know what we want at the end of the season and nothing is going to stop us,” Lebusa said.
