Honor V30 teased in two short videos; triple-camera array confirmed

13 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Marco Lancaster

Rate this post

Huawei’s sub-brand Honor is gearing up to unveil a new smartphone on November 26. It will carry the Honor V30 moniker, and according to rumors, will be Honor’s last flagship of 2019. A couple of official teasers confirmed that this device is coming with dual-selfie cameras. Now, Honor has just published two short video clips that reveal the triple-camera array that is placed on the device’s back.

Along with the Triple-Camera setup, the company is also teasing the 5G connectivity. We can spot the triple camera array at 0:03 mark in the video below. Interestingly enough, the back of a phone bears a triple camera setup which is accompanied by LED flash. If you’re familiar with the Samsung Galaxy M30s, then you’ll probably notice the similarity.
