Tyson Fury sends message to Dana White urging him to get in the Octagon for UFC fight

Dana White has been called out by John Fury - and Tyson Fury wants to see them settle it in the Octagon in a UFC fight

13 Nov 2019, 20:20 by Rich Jones, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rich-jones/

Tyson Fury has told Dana White to "grow a pair" and fight his dad after their war of words.

John Fury launched an extraordinary rant directed at UFC chief White after he claimed his son would "get smashed' if he entered the cage.

He told iFL TV he could "probably smash Dana White with one punch" and told him "keep my son's name out of your mouth".

And the Gypsy King now wants to see the pair of veterans do battle in what he is already branding the "Battle of the baldies".

Fury told BT Sport: "Dana, if you're watching... Grow a pair and sign the contract to fight my dad!

"Battle of the baldies!"

Fury has been training with Darren Till prompting speculation he could make a switch to UFC.

A clash with Stipe Miocic could be on the cards - but in the ring rather than the Octagon.

"I saw Tyson Fury is coming into the mix now here,” Miocic said. “I like a new challenge.”
