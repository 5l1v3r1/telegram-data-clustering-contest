Chiefs Midfielder Could Be Followed To PSL…   

13 Nov 2019, 14:34 by Soccer Laduma

Kaizer Chiefs midfielder James Kotei could be followed to the Absa Premiership by his former teammate. 

As reported in the latest Soccer Laduma, edition 1149, Ghanaian striker Nicholas Gyan has attracted interest from Black Leopards, with indications that the 21-year-old could be headed to Mzansi’s top-flight in January.

Leopards coach Luc Eymael is believed to be showing an interest in the player’s services. Gyan is currently on the books of Tanzania club Simba SC.

“I spoke to Luc Eymael and he knows the player. He wants to see him at Black Leopards in January. The boy can score goals. The coach is willing to have a look at him,” confirmed the player’s Mzansi-based agent, Wellington Machaba.

Gyan played with Amakhosi midfielder and countryman Kotei at Simba.

It was recently reported by the Siya crew that Gyan’s countryman Edwin Gyimah, who recently signed with Leopards, is already attracting interest from other PSL clubs, with Polokwane City and Baroka FC believed to be leading the race for his services.
