Gemma Collins shows off her new hair on the ITV Palooza red carpet

Fringe benefits!

13 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Nancy Brown

Fresh from her trip to Dubai, Gemma Collins rocked a new hairstyle on the red carpet of ITV's Palooza.

Wearing a floor-length green sequinned dress from her own collection, Gemma showed off her curves – and her new bangs – at London's Royal Festival Hall.

Arriving at the party, the style chameleon appeared to have had a fringe cut in as she posed up a storm on the red carpet.

Smiling broadly, Gemma's wore her long flowing blonde locks in loose waves over her shoulders.

"You look beautiful Gemma," cooed one of the star's fans.
