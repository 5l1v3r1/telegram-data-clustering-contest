Campaign trails and the French Spiderman: Wednesday's best photos

13 Nov 2019, 14:05 by Natasha Rees-Bloor

The Guardian’s picture editors select photo highlights from around the world

Durham, England

Visitors admire the artwork ‘Cloud’, formed from 6,000 incandescent light bulbs, during a dress rehearsal for ‘Lumiere Durham’ light festival in Durham.

Photograph: Oli Scarff/AFP via Getty Images

North Carolina , US

A red-cockaded woodpecker is held by a biologist collecting data on the species at Fort Bragg. The bird was caught, measured and banded as part of an ongoing study of the endangered species.

Photograph: Robert F Bukaty/AP

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

China’s Yunqiang Dai and Thailand’s Putharet Khongrak in action during the Men’s 5000m T54 Final at the 2019 World Para Athletics ChampionshipsAthletics.

London, UK

Jo Swinson, the leader of the Liberal Democrats, campaigns at a boxing gym for young people

Byblos, Lebanon

An anti-government protester carries a Lebanese flag in front of burning tyres blocking the main highway linking Tripoli to Beirut. Protesters took to the streets after a televised interview by the president, Michel Aoun

Photograph: Joseph Eid/AFP/Getty Images

Adelaide, Australia

Photograph: Kelly Barnes/AAP

Glasgow, Scotland

The Labour leader, Jeremy Corbyn, wears gloves bearing the words ‘Pick Pam’ to support the candidate Pam Duncan-Glancy during a visit to the Heart of Scotstoun in Glasgow
