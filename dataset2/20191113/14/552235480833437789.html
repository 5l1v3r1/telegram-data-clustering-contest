Pensioner dies after being knocked down in Dundee

The driver of the vehicle was uninjured and police are appealing for information on what happened.

13 Nov 2019, 14:05 by Julie Dunnett, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/julie-gilbert/

A pensioner has died after being knocked down in Dundee.

Pamela Cochrane, 74, was struck by by a vehicle on Glamis Road, Dundee, on Tuesday, November 12.

She was rushed to hospital but medics were unable to save her.

The driver of the vehicle was uninjured and police are appealing for information on what happened.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: "A 74-year-old woman has died following a road crash in Dundee on Tuesday, 12 November, 2019.

"Around 5:10pm, police received a report of a woman being struck by a vehicle on Glamis Road, Dundee.
