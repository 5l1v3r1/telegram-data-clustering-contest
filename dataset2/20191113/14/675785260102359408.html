Katie Price splurges on shopping trip with Kris Boyson ahead of bankruptcy hearing

The former glamour model and her boyfriend were weighed down by shopping bags after snapping up plenty of designer goodies ahead of her hearing

13 Nov 2019, 14:02 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Katie Price seemingly won't let her upcoming bankruptcy hearing stop her from splashing the cash on a shopping spree.

The former glamour model was pictured last night, seemingly loading boyfriend Kris Boyson up like a packmule.

Kris was weighed down by multiple bags, using both hands to carry all the designer goodies they'd been buying from Prada and Gucci.

The mum-of-five, 41, is said to have spent six hours shopping with her 30-year-old beau at a retail outlet.

The shopping trip comes ahead of Katie's court appearance later this month, where she's facing a fresh bankruptcy claim.

The upcoming appearance and the possibility of being declared bankrupt didn't seem to put Katie off, and she happily splashed the cash with Kris.

Katie was dressed for her shopping trip in a camouflage jumper and matching jogging pants.

She wrapped up warm in a puffy coat with a fur-trimmed hood.

A designer Gucci handbag hung around her neck and she clung to her shopping bags.

Personal trainer Kris was also in his designer clobber, wearing jeans and a Kenzo jumper.

He was well prepared for downpour too, carrying a large umbrella that he slung over his shoulder when he wasn't using it.

One snap sees Kris carrying at least seven shopping bags, potentially stuffed with Christmas presents for his and Katie's loved ones.

The couple recently got back together for the second time and they've had a rollercoaster of a relationship.

Not long after they got together, she dumped him for younger man Alex Addison, with Kris talking to the Sunday Mirror about his devastation that the romance was over.

They got back together around the time of his 30th birthday and she announced her engagement to him on Loose Women, showing off her new diamond ring.

They split again when Katie hooked up with builder Charles Drury, who she met when he was working on her house.

And while she and Kris are back together once more, it doesn't appear that the engagement is back on just yet.

Kris recently dropped hints that he's ready to have a baby with Katie, and she's suggested it could happen in the next year.

She told Flex FM : "Next year, maybe a baby and a wedding."
