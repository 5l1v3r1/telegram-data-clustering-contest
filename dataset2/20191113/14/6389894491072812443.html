Royal Mail wins High Court injunction to prevent potential postal strikes

13 Nov 2019, 14:59 by Sebastian McCarthy

Royal Mail has secured a High Court injunction to block potential Christmas postal strikes.

The company argued that there were “irregularities” with the ballot which delivered an outcome overwhelmingly in support of industrial action.

The Communication Workers Union has denied Royal Mail’s claims.

Members had voted to support the walkout by roughly 97 per cent on a turnout of nearly 76 per cent.
