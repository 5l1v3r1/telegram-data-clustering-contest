Tragedy as young biker dies after horror crash in Glasgow's west end

Kieran McSorely's motorbike collided with a Vauxhall Vectra on October 31.

13 Nov 2019, 17:25 by Kathleen Speirs

A young biker has died following a horror crash in Glasgow.

Kieran McSorely was involved in the smash with a Vauxhall Vectra on Queen Margaret Drive in the west end of the city on October 31.

The 23-year-old was rushed to the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital .
