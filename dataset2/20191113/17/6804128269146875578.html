Former Arsenal star sends transfer message to Tottenham board amid Pochettino speculation

Ray Parlour has been speaking about Mauricio Pochettino, Tottenham's form and the January transfer window

13 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Paul Clarke

Former Arsenal man Ray Parlour has offered his verdict on Mauricio Pochettino's future at Tottenham.

Pochettino wanted an overhaul of his squad this past summer but didn't quite get the full backing of Daniel Levy despite some big-money deals.

Meanwhile, the likes of Christian Eriksen, Danny Rose and Toby Alderweireld are all unsure of their futures, which has led to more unrest in the camp.

Pochettino himself signed a new five-year deal last year but has come under increasing pressure amid a poor start to the season for Tottenham, who sit 14th in the Premier League.

Parlour, however, is adamant that Levy should give the Argentinian his full backing, especially in the transfer market.

"They got to the Champions League final last season and they just haven't really pushed on in the league," Parlour told talkSPORT.

"It doesn't help when players are running their contracts down and the dressing room is split at the moment.

"It's amazing to see that Spurs are 14th with the players and the squad that they have got.

"I think he needs to be backed up a little bit more. They know they need to reinvest.
