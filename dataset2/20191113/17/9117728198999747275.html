Haas F1 confirm extension of partnership with Richard Mille

13 Nov 2019, 17:04 by Matt Gretton

Swiss watchmaker Richard Mille and Haas F1 team have extended their partnership. The two will continue to work together into the 2020 Formula 1 season having been together since Haas first entered into the F1 world in 2016. 

“I’m delighted that Richard Mille will continue to partner with us in Formula One. We share a passion for detail and innovation, and I think it was the belief in doing things a little differently that attracted them to us in the first place. Our attributes are very much aligned, and I’m pleased to see our partnership further maturing into the 2020 season," team boss Guenther Steiner said in the statement.

Haas, who this time last year were chasing Renault for the 'best of the rest' position in the F1 world championship, find themselves neared the bottom. With two races remaining in 2019 season, only Williams sit below them. The American team must score at least eight points to beat Alfa Romeo.
