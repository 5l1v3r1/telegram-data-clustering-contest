10 Things You Can Get for Free on Amazon

13 Nov 2019, 17:30

Getting the stuff you want for free is a therapeutic experience in itself and thanks to the popularity of online shopping, it is now possible. You can now receive electronic items, gift cards, and more, absolutely free of cost. You just need to know where to look and keep your eyes peeled!

We at Bright Side have dug up 10 amazing ways you can receive free products from Amazon and would love to share the secrets with you.

1. Baby products

“I have too many baby supplies,” said no mother ever. If you have a baby on board or a toddler who loves getting gifts, you can get them super cool stuff, absolutely free! All you have to do is sign up at Amazon’s Baby Registry, make a list of items, and wait. When someone buys a product worth $10 or more from your selection, the company will send you a large baby basket filled with goodies!

2. Gift cards in exchange for old stuff

We’ll let you in on a little secret — Amazon has a special Trade-In program where you can sell your old unwanted stuff in return for gift cards. From old cellphones to books, you can exchange anything and get a store credit which you can use to buy anything from Amazon. Thank us later.

3. eBooks

To satisfy the bookworm in you, you can get the bestselling eBooks for FREE and keep the good read going! Amazon has a huge selection of free eBooks catering to almost all genres, priced at $0, and you can get them directly on your Kindle device.

4. Beauty samples

Wanna try before you buy? You can. Amazon offers curated boxes featuring men’s products, luxury items, women’s beauty products, snack boxes, and more, and all of them are free. How this works is, you need to place an order for the sample box of your choice and pay a minimum fee. After a successful purchase, you will get the same amount back as a store credit. It’s a win-win!

5. Shows and movies

Free shows and movies? Yes, please. In addition to the original and paid content on Prime Video, there are also tons of free movies and shows you can buy and add to your collection. You just need to know the right place to look. Check some of the free content out here.

6. New exclusive products before their launch

If the term “influencer” sounds good to you, you can become one for Amazon. The company runs a reviewing platform called Vine where it provides newly released or pre-sale items to reviewers free of cost. If you like trying out new gadgets and leaving reviews, you can check-out more details here and get started.

7. Music

If you are an Amazon Prime member, you can get Amazon Music for free and enjoy unlimited songs without ads! Or, if you are not a member, you can still enjoy the service for 30 days without paying a penny.

8. Amazon Prime

And, there’s a way to get Amazon Prime for free too, if you don’t know about it already. The company’s express shipping service is loaded with super cool benefits, but if you are a bit hesitant to pay the money without trying Prime first, you can get a 30-day trial for free.

9. Audiobooks

Audiobooks are the future and Amazon gets it. You can try Audible, the company’s platform for audiobooks, free of cost for a month. By simply signing up, you can get 2 free audiobooks that you can keep forever, regardless of the trial.

10. More free stuff through reviews and partner sites

If you want more free stuff, you should consider leaving reviews on the products you buy from Amazon. There are multiple groups on Facebook where sellers ask customers to leave reviews in exchange for a full refund. There’s also this site called Cashbackbase which does the same thing and gets people unlimited free stuff from Amazon. It’s worth a try!

Which of these free products are on your wish list? Have you ever received a free product from Amazon? Let us know in the comments!
