Voices on the Hudson 2019/2020: Chris Stamey, Holly Miranda, Wesley Stace, more

13 Nov 2019, 17:52 by Bill Pearis

City Winery is working on its new Pier 57 NYC home, but sister venue City Vineyard (at Hudson River Park’s Pier 26) is still going strong, and their annual Voices on the Hudson series is underway now. These shows are intimate — only 100 seats — and guests can have a seasonal meal before the show. This year’s lineup includes: Lucy Wainwright Roche (11/22 & 11/23), Holly Miranda (11/26), Ben Lee & Joanna Sternberg (12/1), “The Holiday Sounds of Josh Rouse” (12/8), The dB’s Chris Stamey and Ariel Pocock (12/28), Laura Gibson with Kip Berman’s The Natvral solo project (2/22), Wesley Stace: A Tribute to John Wesley Harding feat. Robert Lloyd (2/28), and more.

Tickets are on sale now and you can check out the lineup below.
