Emmerdale viewers fed up of Charity Dingle as she lashes out at her family

Charity left Moses with Cain!

13 Nov 2019, 17:31 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Viewers of Emmerdale are fed up with Charity Dingle after she has lashed out at her family, including cousins Cain and Aaron.

Last week, after the break down of their relationships, Cain and Aaron stole cars with the plan to sell them on, but things went haywire when Sean, the man who stole the cars originally, tracked them down.

In last night's episode (November 12 2019) Charity was also struggling after her fiancée Vanessa Woodfield ended their relationship due to her involvement in the casino scam.
