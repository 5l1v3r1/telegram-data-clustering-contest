Soap SPOILERS: Here's what's happening in Emmerdale and Coronation Street tonight

The net is closing in on Robert

13 Nov 2019, 11:48 by Carena Crawford

We're off to Emmerdale and Coronation Street twice on Wednesday November 13.

One family is at war, while another is reunited, someone is accused of something terrible, and one couple find their luck is in.

Here are the top seven spoilers from tonight's soaps.

Emmerdale, ITV, 7pm

1. Charity at war with Chas

Despite the fact Moses was returned safely, Charity is on the warpath.

Cain and Aaron apologise, but Charity insists saying 'sorry' isn't enough to make things right.

When Chas goes in to bat for Aaron and defends her son, Charity finds herself at war with her too.

After breaking up with Vanessa, it won't be long before Charity is left totally alone...

2. Jimmy is stunned by the accusations against him

Jimmy is utterly shocked when Laurel and Jai confront him over the accusations Arthur made.

Horrified he's being accused of child abuse, Jimmy doesn't know what to say.

Laurel is worried how her friendship with Nicola will survive the accusations.

Meanwhile, Arthur continues to persecute Archie.

3. Mandy helps Lydia reach out

Coronation Street, ITV, 7.30pm and 8.30pm

1. Things get worse for Robert

Michelle tells Carla her suspicions over Robert after he lied about his whereabouts, mumbled about babies, and she found his 'big daddy' necklace.

Robert is able to give her a plausible explanation to her questions, but things are about to get more complicated for him...

Vicky is at the hospital for a check up and one of the midwives recognises Robert, so feeds back to Vicky he's been in an accident.

Vicky rushes to his side, and Robert begings to panic.

As he tries to juggle both Michelle and Vicky seeing him and make sure they don't bump into each other, will he succeed?

2. Roy meets his brother

A care worker lets Roy and Carla in when they go back to see Richard, who is suffering from pulmonary fibrosis.

Nina isn't impressed to see them, but Roy simply says he's Richard's online Scrabble partner.

However, when he goes back later, Roy comes clean about who he really is, but how will Richard react?

And what will Nina say when Carla tells her she'd be lucky to have Roy in her life?

3. Emma sorts out Amy and Steve

Emma is fed up of Steve and Amy's constant sniping so suggests they take it in turns to pick something fun they can all do together.

Steve chooses first and it's a football match at Weatherfield County. Amy's less than impressed, but ends up enjoying the game more than she expected.

Steve's not so happy though when Amy chooses a Little Mix gig as her activity!
