Where to go for a festive afternoon tea in Nottingham this Christmas

Get in the festive mood with turkey sandwiches, cinnamon scones and popping candy snowballs

13 Nov 2019, 11:38 by Lynette Pinchess

Best frocks, vintage china, dainty sandwiches and exquisite little cakes aren't just for summer-time afternoon tea.

Cafes, restaurants and the spiritual home of Batman are giving the quintessential British tradition a festive makeover.

Instead of a roast turkey dinner with all the trimmings, it's an alternative way of spending quality time catching up with friends and family over a cup of tea or cheeky glass of prosecco and a delicious array of sandwiches, big fluffy scones and decadent cakes.

Make a date with these Notts cafes and restaurants which will be bringing tiers of festive joy.

The Pudding Pantry

Put some sparkle into your Christmas celebrations at the masters of sweet treats.

Always popular and likely to book up quickly, the Pudding Pantry cafes in the city and Sherwood are kicking the festivities off on November 29 with a selection of seasonal sandwiches, festive petit fours and orange and cranberry scones.

In addition to tea, customers can have a tipple - choose from bubbles, mulled wine or mulled cider.

Price: £16.95 per person or £21.95 with prosecco, etc

Location: Trinity Square, Burton Street, Nottingham NG1 4AF and 579, Mansfield Road, Sherwood NG5 2JN

Booking: Go to thepuddingpantry/afternoon-tea

Colwick Hall

Famed for its themed afternoon teas, the December spread takes inspiration from Charles Dickens' A Christmas Carol.

However, there's nothing 'bah humbug' about this delicious menu of Scrooge Sandwiches, Fezziwigs Fanciful Delights and Desserts of Christmas Past, Present and Future.

Chefs will be conjuring up Cratchit’s cucumber and cranberry cream cheese sandwiches, Mrs Cratchit’s beef pocket pie and sweet treats of cranberry and orange scones with a clementine curd and cinnamon clotted cream and Figgy pudding truffle.

And sitting by a cosy fire, what could be more Christmassy?

Price: £26.95 per person on Saturday and Sunday, with a weekday offer of £39 for two, Monday to Friday. Groups of 10+ will also benefit from ten per cent off. Children under 11, £13.50 each.

Location: Racecourse Road, Colwick NG2 4BH

Booking: 0115 950 0566 or reservations@colwickhallhotel.com

Hart's Kitchen

Afternoon tea at one of Nottingham's premier hotels will be given a festive twist during December. The two AA Rosette hotel restaurant will be serving a selection of dainty sandwiches with fillings such as French Brie and cranberry and roast Derbyshire beef and creamed horseradish.

Freshly-baked sweet treats, including mince pies, will follow, all washed down with a choice of Jing teas.

The Yuletide tea will be served Monday to Friday, from noon to 5pm, and Saturdays 2-5pm.

Price: £21.95 per person.

Location: Standard Hill, Park Row, Nottingham NG1 6GN

To book: Pre-booking is essential, 0115 988 1900.

Fables Coffee House

You'd be crackers to pass up this seasonal tea treat at the cosy cafe which used to be the village library in Edwinstowe.

The festive feast comes with a choice of sandwiches, soup, a savoury tart, freshly baked scones with jam or mincemeat, mince pies and chocolate goodies.

As well as hot drinks, mulled wine and prosecco will be served. As part of the war against plastic, the crackers are eco-friendly. They contain a paper hat but rather than a plastic toy, a donation will be made to homelessness charity Shelter, and everything is compostable.

Price: £45 for two.

Location: High Street, Edwinstowe NG21 9QS

To book: Booking in advance isn't essential but to reserve a table contact 01623 822881.

Tiffin Tea House

Celebrating eight years in business this December, the vintage tea house in West Bridgford will launch its Christmas afternoon tea on Friday, November 29.

On the menu will be sandwiches with home-cooked gammon, smoked salmon, and chicken with stuffing and cranberry sauce.

Classic cakes like Victoria sponge and coffee and walnut will be served with mini mince pies and chocolate and amaretto battenberg. Completing the feast will be fruit scones with clotted cream and jam.

Gluten-free and vegan versions will be available.

It comes with a vintage china pot of your favourite loose leaf tea or add a touch of winter spice with Ceylon cinnamon black tea or Kuchipudi Masala Chai.

Price: £14.95 per person, or £19.95 with prosecco.

Location: Abbey Road, West Bridgford NG2 5NG

Booking: Recommended but not necessary (unless a party of four or more). Telephone 0115 981 6224.

Wollaton Hall

Wayne Manor, where scenes from the Batman movie The Dark Knight Rises, provides the perfect festive backdrop for a sumptuous Christmas afternoon tea with savoury and sweet treats served on vintage china.

Take a regular afternoon tea or treat yourself to a sparkling afternoon tea with gin, prosecco or a glass of wine.

Available every weekend from Sunday, December 1 until Sunday, December 22 with slots from 11am to 3.30pm. Complete the outing with a walk around the deer park.

Price: £15 per person, sparking £20, and children, 3 to 15, £7.50

Location: Wollaton Road, Wollaton NG8 2AE

Booking: Book in advance by calling 0115 876 1057.

Tom Browns Brasserie

Have yourself a merry little Christmas at the award-winning restaurant in Gunthorpe with tea served with aplomb by the team who scooped the accolade for Outstanding Service award at the NottinghamshireLive Food and Drink Awards.

Think popping candy coconut snowballs, Christmas pudding brownies, cinnamon scones with cranberry jam and lots of other festive surprises.

Served every day, except Sunday, there will be the added bonus of live music from pianist and singer Craig Baumber on Saturdays.

Starting on November 30, Christmas themed tea will be served until December 21.

Price: Saturdays £25 per person and midweek £20 per person.

Location: Trentside, Gunthorpe NG14 7FB

To book: Booking is essential. Call 0115 966 3642.

Madhatters Tea Room

The Alice in Wonderland themed tea room will be celebrating the most wonderful time of the year with an afternoon tea full of festive flavours.

Customers will be able to tuck into savoury canapés, finger sandwiches, scones with jam and clotted cream and festive sweet treats.

The contents will vary daily and can be tailored to cater for dietary requirements. As well as tea and coffee in abundance the tea room is licensed, so a cheeky glass of fizz will get the Christmas celebrations off on the right note.

Price: £18.95 per person.

Location: James Street, Kimberley NG16 2LP

To book: Telephone 0115 938 3255.
