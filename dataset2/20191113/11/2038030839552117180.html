Khloé Kardashian reacts to Lamar Odom's engagement

13 Nov 2019, 11:33

Cape Town – Lamar Odom announced his engagement – and Khloé Kardashian couldn’t be happier for her ex.

Khloé and Lamar married in 2009 but split after four years before finalising their divorce in 2016. At the time Lamar was struggling with substance abuse and rumoured infidelity, reports People. It resulted in his near-fatal drug and alcohol binge in 2015 that left him unconscious for several days in hospital, breathing only with the help of machines.

Now, the professional basketball player seems healthy and happy, and confirmed his engagement to Sabrina Parr on Instagram on Monday writing, "Introducing my new fiancé!! Soon to be Mrs Parr-Odom. She the ONE!!!" on a photograph with him and his soon-to-be wife.

According to E! News, Khloé is "happy" for Lamar.

An insider told the publication, "Khloé wants Lamar to be happy. She has always had hope that he would find his way and live a long and healthy life.

"She's happy for him if he has found happiness again. The chapter of her life that she shared with Lamar is closed and she has moved on. She harbours no ill will or jealousy. She wishes him nothing but the best."
