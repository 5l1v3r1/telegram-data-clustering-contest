State police confirm one fatality in crash on Interstate 20 near Start

13 Nov 2019, 11:19 by KNOE

RICHLAND PARISH, La. (KNOE) - UPDATE: According to Louisiana State Police, one person is dead after a crash on Interstate 20 near Start.

According to LA DOTD, the crash happened at Mile Marker 130 Wednesday morning. The crash has led to lengthy delays in the area. The interstate was shut down for a time and traffic had to be rerouted from the interstate.

Here's the latest update from DOTD on Twitter. The update was posted at 2:49 p.m.

"The left lane has opened leaving the right lane blocked I-20 West at Mile Marker 130 (near Start) due to an accident. Congestion has reached approximately five miles in length."
