Poland's Play does not expect its key investors to sell shares

13 Nov 2019, 11:19

WARSAW (Reuters) - Polish mobile operator Play Communications does not expect key investors to sell any shares in the company, its chief executive said on Wednesday.

“Investors are very involved on a day to day basis ... I don’t predict that they will make any additional move, but I’m not in their shoes,” said Jean-Marc Harion.

Reporting by Anna Koper; writing by Alan Charlish
