Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas have spent $20 million for a 20,000-square-foot property

13 Nov 2019, 11:36 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

To start a family and have a lavish house was always on the to-do list of the power couple Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas. Much has been said and written about the couple is in plans to buy an expensive property. Today, a report suggests that Priyanka and Nick have spent $20 million which is Rs 144 crore for a 20,000-square-foot property in Los Angeles.

The report further said that Nickyanka's modern home has seven bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, high ceilings, and ample outdoor space. Meanwhile, Joe Jonas and his wife Sophie Turner have spent $14.1 million for a 15,000-square-foot home just three miles away which is comparatively smaller and has 10 bedrooms and 14 bathrooms.

For the uninitiated, Nick had sold his bachelor pad and was looking for a new home to share with Priyanka. The couple tied the knot on 1st and 2nd of December in India last year and shall celebrate their first wedding anniversary soon.
