John Cena reveals Tic Tac addiction from WWE days: 'It's pretty much 10,000 calories of straight sugar'

13 Nov 2019, 18:44 by Andy Sahadeo

John Cena recently confessed to having a Tic Tac addiction during his WWE days.

The wrestler-turned-actor would go through boxes of the mints before every match, he said during an interview earlier this month on “The Kelly Clarkson Show.”
