Takeaway CEO says he does not plan to improve bid for Just Eat

13 Nov 2019, 18:51

Takeaway.com NV CEO Jitse Groen said on Wednesday he does not plan to improve his company's 4.3 billion pound (US$5.50 billion) all-share bid to buy British rival Just Eat PLC, despite a higher cash offer from rival Prosus NV .

BARCELONA: Takeaway.com NV CEO Jitse Groen said on Wednesday he does not plan to improve his company's 4.3 billion pound (US$5.50 billion) all-share bid to buy British rival Just Eat PLC, despite a higher cash offer from rival Prosus NV .

Asked on the sidelines of the Morgan Stanley European Technology, Media & Telecom conference in Barcelona whether he planned to raise or sweeten the offer, he said "no".
