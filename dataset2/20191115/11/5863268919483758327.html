Delay in Nawaz's treatment could've serious adverse life consequences: Dr Adnan

15 Nov 2019, 11:35 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Ex-prime minister Nawaz Sharif’s personal doctor Dr Adnan on Friday has said that delay in Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) supremo’s London departure for medical treatment can further deteriorate his health.

In a post on social-networking website Twitter, the doctor said that Nawaz Sharif’s health is still critical.
