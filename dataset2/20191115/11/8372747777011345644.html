Cape Coral offices damaged by vehicle collision Friday morning

15 Nov 2019, 11:25 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- We're working to find out more about a vehicle versus a building in Cape Coral Friday morning.

It happened on Cape Coral Parkway near Vincennes Street.

That's where a vehicle crashed into the front of a financial services business that faces the street.

There is no word yet on whether there were any injuries, but a sign posted at the site indicates that two front offices are closed due to damages to the building.
