Dad buys rare chocolate that could be worth £75 for 49p at B&M

"I was just holding it and could feel there were in fact three fingers in it. One on top of the other two. It’s mad isn’t it?"

15 Nov 2019, 11:56 by Neil Shaw

A dad was stunned after he bought a 'Twirl' chocolate bar - which had three fingers.

Samuel May, 72, bought the Orange Twirl for 49p - and was expecting two bars of choc inside.

He noticed the wrapper was bigger than he thought and he found it had three fingers inside.

Samuel, of Swindon, Wiltshire said: "I’d been looking for these Orange Twirls for ages. Me and my partner absolutely love chocolate but we couldn’t find them anywhere.

He said: "They were by the tills when I got there. So I was quite excited to have found them and I picked up a couple for me and my partner.

He said: "I was looking at them and thought ‘that one is a bit bigger than the other’.

"I was just holding it and could feel there were in fact three fingers in it. One on top of the other two. It’s mad isn’t it?

"I feel like I just can’t believe it. I can’t find these bars for ages and then pick first one up and it’s got three fingers in,''

Samuel says he isn’t sure what he’ll do with the third stick of chocolate but said he may share it with his youngest daughter, Nalah.

He said: "I’ve got an eight-month-old who likes sucking on chocolate at the moment so I may give it to her to enjoy. It’s just one of those funny things.

"It would be nice if it was the only one in the world. Hopefully there’s more good luck to come, now is a good time to by a lottery ticket.''

A spokeswoman for Cadbury said: “The reaction to the launch of Cadbury Twirl Orange has been incredible and the product is in very high demand across the country.

"This guy has hit the jackpot by finding three fingers in a rare limited-edition Orange Twirl. We can’t promise there will be any more to come.”
