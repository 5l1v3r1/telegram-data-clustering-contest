Rui: This Is What Makes Ronaldo The Best In The World

15 Nov 2019, 11:28 by SL International

Following Cristiano Ronaldo's latest hat-trick performance on Thursday, a Portugal teammate has now revealed what he believes sets the global superstar apart from his competitors.

Ronaldo was at his prolific best for Portugal again on Thursday night, when he scored a hat-trick in the reigning European champions' comprehensive 6-0 2020 UEFA European Championship qualifying win over Lithuania.

The 34-year-old was lauded for his performance after the match, particularly by teammate Mario Rui, who revealed why he believes the Juventus star is the best player in the world.

"He is the best in the world, never tired of winning, wanting more, and it is this hunger that makes him what he is," the Napoli left-back said, via A Bola.

"We are happy to have him on the team and I hope he will continue to give us joy as he has done so far."

Ronaldo's hat-trick took his overall international tally for Portugal to 98 goals, just 11 shy of former Iran international Ali Daei's world record of 109.
