Becky Hill, High Contrast and Roger Sanchez announced for The Weekender Festival in Plymouth

A brand new two-day outdoor multi-genre music festival is happening in Plymouth next year - and an incredible line-up has just been announced

15 Nov 2019, 11:00 by Sarah Waddington

Becky Hill has been officially announced as the headline act for an incredible new two-day outdoor festival happening in Plymouth in May 2020.

Organised by Casa, the brand new The Weekender Festival will be held at Newnham Park in Plympton on the May Day Bank Holiday next year.

And an awesome first wave of acts has just been announced for the inaugural event.

Becky Hill, Roger Sanchez, wAFF, Danny Howard, High Contrast have all been announced as headline acts for the festival on Friday, May 8 and Saturday, May 9 as the May Day Bank Holiday is moved in commemoration of the VE Day anniversary.

Becky Hill joins The Weekender Festival off the back of a current Top 10 chart single and a sold out UK tour.

Other names announced include Benny L, Casa, Crazy P, DJ Hazard, Garage Nation, Jaguar Skills, Kanine, Riton, Shapes and Sosa.

Organisers Ben Gill-Carey, Ryan Platts and Charlie Gardner say they can't wait to bring the new music festival to Plymouth and say the time is right for an event like this to take place in our city.

"We're really excited to get this off the ground and improve the music culture and diversity in the city, especially with the current venue predicament," said Ben.

Charlie said: "I'm really excited to get this off the ground and improve the music culture and diversity in the city, especially with the current venue predicament.

"It's the biggest thing for Plymouth since sliced bread, it just feels right."

Ryan added: "There's nothing better than being able to bring this line-up to the people of our city, as well as giving our local DJs a huge platform to perform on supporting these world class artists."

The festival will be over two days (non camping) and gates will be open from midday - 10pm.

There will be five stages with 20+ of the biggest names in dance music and a selection of fully licensed bars and independent food vendors.

There will also be two huge after parties.

Weekend and VIP Weekend tickets are on sale now via Skiddle.
