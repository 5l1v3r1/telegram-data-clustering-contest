Latest pictures of Sara Ali Khan vacationing in New York

15 Nov 2019, 11:19 by Filmfare

Sara Ali Khan has been shooting for Coolie No 1 with Varun Dhawan for a while now. The young star took some time out to head to New York for a vacation with her friends recently. The actress is constantly keeping her fans updated through her social media account about what she has been doing during her trip. The first set of pictures had Sara posing in front of a store at the Big Apple, Soho.

And now, the new pictures that are going viral over the internet has the actress dressed in black athleisure, sporting a red cap showing off her tonned abs. In another picture she’s seen planting kisses on her friend’s cheek. She also shared a picture at Ladurée Paris sipping on some coffee. Pictures of the actress make it clear that the actress is having a gala time in New York.

Meanwhile, on the work front, she will be next seen in Imtiaz Ali's Aaj Kal with Kartik Aaryan. The film is slated to hit the theatres on Valentine's Day 2020 (February 14).
