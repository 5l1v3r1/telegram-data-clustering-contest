EastEnders viewers turn on Karen Taylor for telling Gray about Chantelle's miscarriage

Chantelle lost her baby a few weeks ago

15 Nov 2019, 11:27 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Viewers of EastEnders have turned on Karen Taylor after she told Gray about his wife Chantelle's miscarriage.

For months viewers have known that Gray is abusive towards Chantelle and has hit her on multiple occasions.

However, ever since finding out Chantelle is pregnant with their third child, Gray has promised to be better and has gone to therapy for his anger.

But sadly a couple of weeks ago, Chantelle found out she had lost her baby after discovering she was bleeding.

She kept it to herself for a while but eventually told her mum, Karen.

After some encouragement from Karen, Chantelle knew she had to tell her husband the truth.

Karen you idiot!

But as she walked home, she ran into Denise who told Chantelle about the fight Gray got into at a club a few weeks ago.

Chantelle confronted the solicitor about what she heard and he told her that he only got into a fight as the other man was starting on people and harassing girls.

As the pair argued on the street, Karen and Mitch came along with Chantelle and Gray's daughter, Mia.

Noticing the pair were obviously upset, Karen thought Chantelle had told Gray about the miscarriage and said: "It's not the end of the world, you know?

"You may have lost this baby but that don't mean you can't try again."

As Karen walked away, Chantelle looked terrified.
