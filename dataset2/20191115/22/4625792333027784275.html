'You're a disgrace': Don Trump Jr. claims George Conway 'routinely & publicly embarrasses his wife'

15 Nov 2019, 22:41 by John Gage

Donald Trump Jr. and George Conway tangled on Twitter the same day public impeachment hearings were taking place, attacking each other in connection to their family members.

The fight started after Trump tweeted Friday, "America hired @realDonaldTrump to fire people like the first three witnesses we’ve seen. Career government bureaucrats and nothing more."
