Here are some of the natural chemicals that can help you attain better sleep.

Tetrahydrocannabinol

THC is known for having the psychoactive component of the cannabis plant. With this, it becomes the main component to make someone feel high. According to studies, THC can help relieve pain and nausea to promote better sleep. It has a sedative effect that makes its users feel sleepy with ease.

However, despite its capacity to aid you in sleeping, it alters the normal sleeping stage and pattern. Its usage is often associated with a reduced REM that also limits your dreams. Thus, an altered and changed sleeping cycle is one of the disadvantages of using this compound.

Cannabidiol

CBD is currently one of the hottest and most accessible cannabinoids available in the market. Aside from its popularity, it can also be used as an effective and natural alternative to treat numerous health conditions. The treatment becomes possible once the CBD attach and interact with the user’s body cells and receptors. At the same time, CBD also can reduce pain and anxiety. With this, it helps promote a healthy mind and improve one’s sleep.

CBD offers more than its capacity to treat a sleeping disorder. Unlike THC, it doesn’t make a person feel high. For this reason, it is commonly used by many as medicinal purposes alone and not for recreational activities. Also, there are numerous ways to consume the CBD. Its form varies from oils, vape concentrates, pills, edibles, and even creams. Thus, it is the best cannabinoid option to go for if you encounter some troubles when sleeping.

Cannabinol

Also known as CBN, this chemical compound can be used to treat pain and inflammation. It can also help promote and stimulate one’s appetite. However, similar to THC, it has powerful sedative components that can significantly affect one’s sleeping cycle.

Another compound that can be found in the cannabis Sativa plant is Myrcene.
