Looks like the vivo S5 will be coming to Malaysia very soon

15 Nov 2019, 22:15

vivo has officially announced that the vivo S5, which was initially launched in China yesterday, will be coming to Malaysia very soon. While we’re not sure about the exact Malaysia release date, as well as the official local pricing of the said device. We can expect the smartphone to be available anywhere between the end of November to December 2019.

In terms of what the aforementioned device to the table, it comes with a 6.44-inch FHD+ OLED display with a punch-hole notch towards the top right. Not only that, but it also comes with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 712 chipset, 8GB of RAM and up to 256GB of internal storage. Camera-wise, it has a quad-camera setup with up to a 48MP main camera and a 32MP front-facing selfie camera.
