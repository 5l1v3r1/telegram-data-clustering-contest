The snap caused a stir on social media.

15 Nov 2019, 04:30 by Kathleen Speirs

A Scottish fish and chip shop chain is has created a Christmas-themed sausage supper in the build up to the festive season.

Blue Lagoon Fish and Chips is launching its first turkey sausage supper.

The dish, sports two battered turkey sausages, chips and gravy.
