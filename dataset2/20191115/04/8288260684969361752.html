Morelos' agent speaks out

Alfredo Morelos’ agent has told interested clubs to go and get the Rangers striker before his value skyrockets.

Aston Villa have been heavily linked with the Colombia international who has already rattled in 22 goals this season.

Villa are finalising their plans for the January transfer window and Morelos has done little to quell speculation about a potential move to the Premier League when asked about his future in interviews.

He is said to be valued at around £20m at Ibrox and the 23-year-old’s agent, Theofilos Karasavvidis, thinks a fee like that would represent a bargain.

VAR meeting

Aston Villa chief executive Christian Purslow says ‘grave concerns’ about video assistant referee (VAR) technology were raised at a Premier League meeting on Thursday.

Purslow was Villa’s representative at a conference held between top-flight clubs to discuss the use of VAR.

Referees' chief Mike Riley led the discussion over the technology, which has caused weekly controversy since its introduction in the league at the start of the season.

Purslow told Sky Sports: “I’ve got grave concerns, but so has everybody in the room. We had a very robust discussion."

Fans urge Villa to sign Giroud

Chelsea are reportedly putting striker Olivier Giroud up for sale at a cut-price fee.

And Aston Villa fans think their club should go straight in for the forward.

Giroud is out of the picture under new Chelsea boss Frank Lampard, starting just one match in the Premier League this season with former Villa loanee Tammy Abraham leading the line.

Antonio Conte, who brought him to Stamford Bridge in January 2018, is set to try and sign him again according to Italian media.

And Chelsea are thought to be letting him go for just £6 million.

Nyland interview

Orjan Nyland says he will fight Tom Heaton to become Aston Villa's number one goalkeeper.

The Norwegian made his first appearance since December 29 at Wolverhampton Wanderers on Sunday, coming on for the injured Jed Steer after just seven minutes at Molineux.

Nyland had previously been limited to Under-23s outings this season until Tom Heaton picked up a calf injury before the Wolves game and was treated to an appearance on the substitute's bench for the West Midlands derby.

He was Villa 's number one last season having been signed from Ingoldstadt in summer 2018 but he suffered an Achilles injury that ruled him out for the rest of the season.

Frustration

Aberdeen's Scott McKenna has told of his transfer frustrations at being denied a move away from Pittodrie.

The Scotland defender was an Aston Villa target last year and sought to leave the Scottish Premiership side in the summer for the Championship with Nottingham Forest and Queens Park Rangers interested.

Celtic were also said to be interested in the 23-year-old , who submitted a transfer request as he tried to force through a move south of the border.

In the end it didn't materialise, with Derek McInnes keen to keep hold of the impressive centre-back.

Phillips clause

Kalvin Phillips has a release clause in his contract allowing him to leave if Leeds United don’t win promotion, according to Noel Whelan.

Phillips, 23, has been linked with a move to the Premier League after becoming a mainstay of Marcelo Bielsa’s side at Elland Road.

Aston Villa were firmly interested during the summer transfer window when Phillips was a leading target after the play-off triumph in May.

But they couldn’t agree a fee with Leeds, who rejected bids, and the midfielder stayed put for another shot at promotion with his boyhood club.
