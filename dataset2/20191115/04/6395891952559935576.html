MSP investigating fatal car crash in Freetown

15 Nov 2019, 04:37 by Manoella Macedo

FREETOWN, Mass. - State Police troopers are investigating a car crash that killed at least one person on Thursday night.

Troopers responded to reports of a crash at around 8:40 p.m. on Route 24 northbound south of Exit 10 in Freetown.

At the scene, officials found three vehicles had been involved with multiple injuries. One of those injuries was considered life-threatening.
