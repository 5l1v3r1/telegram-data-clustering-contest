Sara Ali Khan doesn't like to skip her workout session; hits the gym during her New York vacation

15 Nov 2019, 18:22 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sara Ali Khan marked her debut with Abhishek Kapoor's 'Kedarnath' opposite Sushant Singh Rajput. The young starlet received a lot of appreciation and love for her performance in the film. Well, apart from acting, the actress is also known for her humble nature and great fashion choices.

And now, she took some time off from her busy schedule to enjoy a short vacation in New York. The actress took to her Instagram to share pictures from her trip to NYC.

In the latest pics, she can be seen flaunting her washboard abs as she works out at a gym. Sara's love for fitness is not hidden from anyone. The actress often gets clicked outside her gym in Mumbai. However, working out during a vacation, well, it seems Sara doesn't like to skip her session!
