Banana prices to remain between ₹12 and ₹ 27: TNAU

15 Nov 2019, 18:26 by Special Correspondent

The farm gate prices of banana would be between ₹12 and ₹27 a kg in the Coimbatore market in December and farmers could decide on selling based on the prediction, Tamil Nadu Agricultural University said in a price forecast report.

Coimbatore market received ‘poovan’ variety from Pudukottai, Thirukattupalli and Cuddalore. The price of this variety could be from ₹12 to ₹14 a kg. It received the ‘karpuravalli’ variety from Sathyamangalam, Gobichettipalayam and Mettupalayam. The price of this variety could be between ₹15 and ₹18.

The market received the ‘nendran’ variety from Mettupalayam and Kerala and the price of this variety could be from ₹25 to ₹27 a kg. The North East Monsoon and festival season would be the price influencing factors and farmers were advised to take appropriate selling decision, the report added.
