Reese Witherspoon admits she turned down Friends return and we're gutted

15 Nov 2019, 18:16

Reese Witherspoon turned down the chance to appear on Friends for a second time because she didn’t like performing in front of a live studio audience.

The Morning Show actress was being interviewed with her co-star (and one-time on-screen sister!) Jennifer Aniston when she revealed for the first time that she had been asked back for a second stint on the iconic sitcom.

In the sitcom, Reese played Jill Green, the spoiled younger sister of Aniston’s character Rachel.

Both actresses were discussing performing in front of live audiences when the Legally Blonde star made the shock confession.

‘She’s so good in front of a live audience,’ Aniston said of Witherspoon. ‘You forget it because you blacked out from fear.’

‘I’ve never done it again,’ Witherspoon confirmed, before adding: ‘Did you know they asked me back?
