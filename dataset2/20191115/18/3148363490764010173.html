John Dean on Trump's Yovanovitch Tweet: 'This Is Criminal' | Breitbart

15 Nov 2019, 18:43 by Pam Key

On Friday’s broadcast of CNN’s special coverage of the House Intelligence Committees public so-called impeachment inquiry hearings, John Dean, the ex-White House counsel for Richard Nixon, said President Donald Trump’s tweet about former Ukraine Ambassador Marie Yovanovitch was “criminal.”
