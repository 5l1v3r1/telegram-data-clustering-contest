Millie Bobby Brown, 15, wants to show off womanhood with more 'fitted' clothes

15 Nov 2019, 18:30

Millie Bobby Brown wants to wear more ‘fitted’ clothes and show off her womanly figure.

The 15-year-old Stranger Things actress said she’d like to adjust her style to show what it’s like ‘being a woman’ of her age.

‘I would really enjoy wearing more fitted clothes now,’ she said. ‘I feel like I’m becoming someone and being a woman so I just want to show that.

The young star – who plays Eleven in the Netflix series – said she’s conscious she’s a role model to a lot of young girls.

‘I think about the girls who are going to look at the picture – those young girls that are going to look at me and if they think, “Wow that’s what I should dress up as”.

‘They are my age, so those are the ones I think of.’

‘I want to make sure that I’m influencing being your age and being who you are in that time of your life.’

Millie believes it’s so hard to get the balance right when it comes to choosing what to wear on the red carpet.

She added to Harper’s Bazaar: ‘It’s always difficult to dress for a red carpet event because a lot of people have opinions and unfortunately you say you won’t listen to it, but you actually kind of have to.

‘For me, I sit there and think, I’m not going to listen to what they have to say, journalists or whoever wants to write badly about my inappropriate outfit.’

Millie made headlines recently when she met Mariah Carey’s twins, Moroccan and Monroe.

In the cutest picture ever, the Emotions singer’s kids are seen smiling with joy as Millie sweetly hugs them.

Mariah captioned the photo: ‘Roc and Roe’s best day of their 8 year old lives! Thank you @milliebobbybrown for this moment, we had a blast!’

Millie’s certainly got some young fans!
