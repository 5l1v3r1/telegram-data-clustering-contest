CP Holiday Train will be here in two weeks

It will stop in both Cambridge and Ayr

15 Nov 2019, 18:45 by Blair Adams

It's always a big draw when it rolls on through.

The CP Holiday Train will be in Waterloo Region on Friday, November 29.

It will first stop in Cambridge at the former Galt Station at 10 Malcolm Street at 4 p.m. that day - Alan Doyle and Beautiful Band will then perform from 4:15 to 4:45.

The train will then head to Ayr (Inglis Street and Willison Street crossing - with a performance by Alan Doyle and Beautiful Band happening between 5:30 and 6.

Cash and food donations are collected along the route for area food banks.

Since the inaugural journey back in 1999, almost $16 million has been raised and 4.5 million pounds of food has been collected.
