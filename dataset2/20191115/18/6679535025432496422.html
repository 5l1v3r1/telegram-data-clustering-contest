Teen, two minors face assault charge

15 Nov 2019, 18:11 by Special Correspondent

The police took a 19-year-old into custody and produced two of his minor associates before the Juvenile Justice Board on the charge of assault and hurting religious sentiments, here on Friday.

In a press release here, the district police said Mohammed Aftab, 19, along with two boys assaulted another boy who had come to offer prayers to Lord Anjaneya at a gym, Janata Vyayama Shale, at Doddanagudde here at 7 p.m. on Thursday.

Following a complaint by president of the Janata Vyayamashale, Ramdas Shetty, the police took all three into custody. A case has been registered at the Udupi Town police station, the police said.
