Jane Hazlegrove: The former Casualty star’s rise to fame before Corrie

15 Nov 2019, 18:30

Former Casualty star Jane Hazlegrove joined the cast of Coronation Street as Gemma Winter (Dolly Rose Campbell) and Paul Foreman’s (Peter Ash) mum earlier this year and she’s been a force to be reckoned with on the Cobbles ever since.

Playing mum Bernie, Jane was given character who was described as: ‘an old raver who still sees herself as a free spirit. She is not one to be tied down to a man, a job or any responsibilities, especially when it means hard work for her.’

Her early storylines saw her get accused of shoplifting and steal her daughter’s urine to sell fake, positive pregnancy tests.

However, things have taken a dark turn and it has since transpired that Bernie’s ex, Paul and Gemma’s stepdad, Kel (Joe Alessi) sexually abused Paul as a child.

Talking about the difficult storyline and Peter’s performance in the role, Jane said on Lorraine earlier today: ‘He was so brilliant, Peter, and it was a massive monologue and he really worked so hard; and it was quite contained in the studio and he’s just brilliant.’

‘For everybody when something like this is revealed it affects every single person,’ she continued. ‘Not just Paul and everything he’s buried, it’s every single person around them. It ripples through the family forever.’

After watching a clip of Paul telling Bernie that she failed him, a particularly proud Jane was visibly emotional.

But what was former Casualty actress Jane Hazlegrove in before she became a soap staple and who is her partner in real life?

What was Jane Hazlegrove in before Coronation Street and Casualty?

51-year-old Jane Hazlegrove was born in Manchester in July 1968 and made her first appearance on TV in 1984 at the age of 16.

Jane appeared in a TV movie called Threads, in which she starred as a character called Alison Kemp. In the following years she went on to appear in a number of other TV shows before getting her big break in the show How We Used To Live, when she won a recurring role as character Maggie Selby between 1984 and 1987.
