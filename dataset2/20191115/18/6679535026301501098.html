Palakkad leads on Day 3 of school arts fete

The sub-district is toppers in higher secondary and high school categories

15 Nov 2019, 18:09 by Staff Reporter

Students of Palakkad sub-district took a lead on the third day of the district school arts festival held at Desabandhu Higher Secondary School and St. Dominicâ€™s A.L.P. School at Thachampara, near Mannarkkad, on Friday.

They emerged toppers in both higher secondary and high school categories. In the higher secondary section, Palakkad was in the first position with 265 points, followed by Ottappalam (243) and Cherpulassery (241) in the second and third positions.

In the high school category, Palakkad was leading with 221 points, followed by Thrithala with 219 points and Cherpulassery with 211 points. In upper primary (UP) category, Mannarkkad was ahead with 140 points, when Pattambi (133) and Cherpulassery (128) were in the second and third positions.

In UP Sanskrit festival, Alathur, Cherpulassery, and Mannarkkad were in the first three positions respectively. In high school Sanskrit festival, Cherpulassery, Alathur, and Palakkad were in the first three positions.
