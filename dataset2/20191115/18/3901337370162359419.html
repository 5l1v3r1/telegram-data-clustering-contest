Two Buccaneers In Action During 5-0 Loss

15 Nov 2019, 18:58 by Soccer Laduma

The duo of Orlando Pirates forwards Augustine Mulenga and Justin Shonga were in action as Zambia lost 5-0 to Algeria on Thursday. 

Mulenga and Shonga were in the starting line-up as Chipolopolo suffered a huge defeat in their Africa Cup of Nations qualifier in Blida.

The Bucs duo, who have seen their team suffer some disappointing results in the PSL this season, will hope for better luck with the national team against Zimbabwe. 

Zambia are scheduled to host Zimbabwe in Lusaka on Tuesday in another Afcon qualifier.
