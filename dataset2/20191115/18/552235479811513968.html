Fire crews race to blaze at Glasgow flats

Residents have been evacuated from their homes at Leadburn Square in Barmulloch.

15 Nov 2019, 18:25 by Samantha Croal, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/samantha-croal/

A fire has broken out at a block of flats in Glasgow .

Fire crews are tackling the blaze at Leadburn Square in Barmulloch.

Emergency services have been evacuating residents from their homes.

It is understood no one has been injured.

Fire crews have requested Scottish Power to attend.

It is not yet known what caused the fire.

A Scottish Fire and Rescue spokesperson said: "We received a call today of a fire in the building at Leadburn Square in Barmulloch at 5.08pm on Friday night.

"Three appliances were dispatched to the scene.

"The incident is ongoing."
