Prince Harry Joined Meghan Markle And Archie At A Local Playgroup

15 Nov 2019, 17:24 by Jamie Samhan

A playgroup session near Prince Harry and Meghan Markle’s home at Frogmore Cottage has become a favourite of baby Archie.

While at the WellChild Awards last month, the Duchess of Sussex had previously mentioned that she takes Archie, 7 months, to a playgroup, telling people, “It was a lot of fun. He loved it.”

And now the Duke has had the chance to join his wife and son for the first time.

“There were a couple of other dads there — he wasn’t the only dad there!” a source told People.

They added, “Archie had a fantastic time. He was crawling around and was particularly taken with two other red-haired babies.”

Prince Harry reportedly responded, “Gingers stick together.”

The playgroup comes just before the trio will fly to the United States where they are spending time with Meghan’s mother Doria Ragland for both Thanksgiving and Christmas.
