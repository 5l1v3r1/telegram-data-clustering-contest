Barricade situation in Salt Lake ends with arrest

15 Nov 2019, 17:43 by HNN Staff

HONOLULU, Hawaii (HawaiiNewsNow) - A suspect is in custody following an hours-long barricade situation in Salt Lake on Friday.

Police shut down the intersection of Wanaka and Likini streets around 7:10 a.m. as they responded to the incident. Lanes were reopened by about noon.

Details are still developing.
