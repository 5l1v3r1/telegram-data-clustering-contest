Premiere: Vantage Records Enlists French Producer Argo With 4 Track EP Featuring "Don't Sleep"

These articles will focus on specific EP premieres/reviews for certain artists/labels within the 140 scene and beyond. This one features the French don Argo with his debut release on LX One's own label "Vantage Records."

15 Nov 2019, 17:54 by Benjamin Oliver Lloyd

After 2 very successful release's for 001 and 002, Vantage Records are back with their 3rd release. This one might actually be my favorite so far. The label's head honcho, LX One has enlisted the talents of French producer, Argo. He's put together a 4 track instrumental EP thats absolutely stacked full of weight. We were given the opportunity to premiere the track "Don't Sleep," which you can listen to below.
