Begin repairs in a week, PWD told

15 Nov 2019, 17:42 by Staff Reporter

The Sub-Divisional Magistrate Court at Ottappalam has ordered that the repair work of the broken roads in Ottappalam taluk begin within a week.

Sub Collector Arjun Pandyan, who is the subdivisional magistrate of Ottappalam, ordered on Friday that major roads such as Ottappalam-Cherpulassery, Vaniyamkulam-Kothakkurissi, and Lakkidi-Railway Gate should be repaired without delay.

PWD executive engineer here and assistant executive engineer at Shoranur were asked to appear before the magistrate on November 28 if they have reasons for any delay.

Citizen’s right

Mr. Pandyan said that good roads were a citizen’s right, and it was a matter of grave concern that human lives were being lost on roads because of the poor condition.
