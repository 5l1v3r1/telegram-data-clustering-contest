Pokémon Sword and Shield – Galar region evolution guide for Koffing, Farfetch’d and more

15 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Darryn Bonthuys

Pokémon Sword and Shield is out right Meowth, and while it may have dozens of new critters to find and capture, it’s the quirky reinvention of old favourites that many a long-time fan is after. After all, who would have ever thought that Koffing would undergo an industrial evolution that transforms into a roaming magnate who gets by on the tears of Sobble? NOT ME!

So if you’re looking for a new twist on old favourites, then here you go: A complete guide to evolving familiar faces into brand new forms in Pokémon Sword and Shield.

Zigzagoon, Linoone and Obstagoon

  • Zigzagoon evolves into Linoone at level 20.
  • Linoone evolves into Obstagoon at night, at level 35.

Yamask and Runerigus

  • Yamask evolves into Runerigus if it has 49 and above damage and you walk under the Stone Scultpure in Dusty Hill.

Mime Jr, Mr Mime and Mr Rime

  • Mime Jr evolves into Mr Mime after it learns the move Mimic at level 32.
  • Galarian region Mr Mime evolves into Mr Rime at level 42.

Meowth and Perrserker

  • Meowth evolves into Perrserker at level 28.

Corsola and Cursola

  • Corsola evolves into Cursola at level 38.

Koffing and Weezing

  • Koffing evolves into Weezing at level 35.

Ponyta and Rapidash

  • Ponyta evolves into Rapidash at level 38.

Darumaka and Darmanitan

  • Darumaka evolves into Darmanitan when an Ice Stone is used on it.

Farfetch’d and Sirfetch’d

  • Farfetch’d evolves into Sirfetch’d after it lands three critical hits on another Pokémon in battle.
