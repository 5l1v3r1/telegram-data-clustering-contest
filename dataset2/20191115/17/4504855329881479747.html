Man found murdered in Sunrise home

15 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Cristina Alexander

SUNRISE, FLA. (WSVN) - Sunrise Police need help investigating the murder of Johnnie Ridgeway, who was found deceased in his own home.

Ridgeway was found dead in his residence at 5861 NW 12th Court, at around 4 p.m., Tuesday.

He was shot multiple times.
