Roche buys U.S. drugmaker Promedior for up to $1.4 billion to get lung drug

15 Nov 2019, 17:24

ZURICH (Reuters) - Swiss drugmaker Roche is paying up to $1.4 billion to buy U.S. biotech company Promedior, whose main drug candidate against the lung disease idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis is set to begin late-stage trials, Promedior said on Friday.
