Who is Andrew Maxwell on I'm A Celebrity? Comedian on jungle lineup for this year

Andrew Maxwell is on the lineup for the new series of I'm A Celebrity which gets going on ITV on Sunday night

15 Nov 2019, 17:21 by Simon Duke

There are just days to go now until this year's I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! is back on our screens.

The rumour mill had been in overdrive as speculation mounted about who would be heading Down Under for this year's show - set to be made even better with the jungle reunion of Ant and Dec .

From soap stars to sports stars, a whole host of different celebrities had seen their names linked with a stint on I'm A Celebrity , all hoping to follow in the footsteps of 2018 winner Harry Redknapp.

And on Sunday night, we will see the confirmed I'm A Celebrity lineup head into the jungle to take on everything life in there throws at them.

And, as well as Girls Aloud star Nadine Coyle and football legend Ian Wright, one of the campmates this year is Andrew Maxwell.

Who is Andrew Maxwell?

The Irish comedian has a very successful stand up and TV career. And as well as appearing on screen in full view of everyone, the 44-year-old is also known as the voice over guy for hit MTV series Ex on the Beach.

He first started performing in 1992 and went on to find success in the industry by booking warm-up slots at gigs across the country.

He has since appeared on a string of popular panel shows, including QI, Never Mind the Buzzcocks, Mock The Week, Breaking The News and Comedy Central's Roast Battle.

Speaking about his phobias, Andrew said: "I don't like the look of any of those guys - the insects and creepy crawlies!"
