Kelly Osbourne Says ‘The Osbournes’ TV Series Is Close To A Comeback

15 Nov 2019, 17:18 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Fans of “The Osbournes” may be in luck.

Ozzy Osbourne, Sharon Osbourne, Kelly Osbourne and Jack Osbourne starred in MTV’s reality series “The Osbournes” from 2002 to 2005. The first of four seasons was cited as the network’s most-viewed series ever.

Kelly recently told People that the show is as close it has ever has been to making a comeback.

“I’m not joking, every single year about every three months or so someone else gives us another offer and we keep saying no,” she responded. “It keeps coming ’round. Right now there’s another offer on the table and I think this is the closest we’ve ever come to accepting one. But whether that will happen or not, I don’t know.”

“We’ve got other people to think about,” Kelly said. “My brother [Jack has] got three kids… Do we want that life for them without them being old enough to choose if they want to do it or not like my brother and I were?”

The reality series aired for four seasons.
