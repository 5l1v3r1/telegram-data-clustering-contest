Firefighters evacuate homes due to gas leak in Land O Lakes area

15 Nov 2019, 17:45 by WFTS Digital Staff

PASCO COUNTY, Fla. --- Residents will soon be able to return to their homes after evacuations due to a gas leak.

Pasco Fire Rescue said they were on the scene of a gas leak in the 8200 block of Mallow Mirror Ln. in Land O Lakes. Shortly after, Clearwater Gas arrived and stopped the leak.

Officials said residents will soon be able to return to their homes.
