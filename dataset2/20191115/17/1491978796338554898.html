Trevor Noah on impeachment: ‘Maybe we should call it a presidential boo-boo’

15 Nov 2019, 17:40 by Adrian Horton

Late-night hosts assess the media analysis of the first televised impeachment hearings and discuss yet another school shooting California

Trevor Noah

On The Daily Show, Trevor Noah examined coverage of the impeachment hearings, in particular the reviews on Fox News. In a series of clips, Fox News commentators stressed that Americans don’t care about Ukraine, don’t know anything about it, couldn’t even find it on a map.

“So according to Fox News, Americans are too dumb to follow these impeachment proceedings?” Noah asked, mocking the network anchors: “The word impeachment is very confusing – maybe we should call it a presidential boo-boo, would that help?”
