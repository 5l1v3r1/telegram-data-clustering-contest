'In a heartbeat' - Fans react to shock Aston Villa speculation

Aston Villa latest news includes Hearts fans enjoying John Terry being linked with manager's job

15 Nov 2019, 17:51 by Shane Ireland, https://www.facebook.com/ShaneIrelandJournalist

Hearts fans have given the green-light to a potential swoop for John Terry after the Aston Villa assistant was linked with the manager’s job.

The Scottish Premiership side are on the hunt for a new boss after sacking former Scotland chief Craig Levein last month.

And Terry is being suggested as a possible successor after rising up the betting.

The former Chelsea captain was fifth favourite at 11/1 this morning, but is now the 7/1 third favourite to take charge with SkyBet.

Terry, who captained Villa to the Championship play-off final in 2017/18, returned to the club as an assistant head coach to Dean Smith last October.

"He's got better and better if I'm honest," said Smith. "When he first came in he looked at me and Richard (O'Kelly) for a lot of advice, where as now he will just go out and take players aside.

 

"He does video work with them as well as bits on the training ground. He's become an excellent coach. He focuses more on the defensive side of it and Richard will focus on the forward play. I have more of a holistic view of all of it.

"He's enjoying what he's doing at the football club. He keeps telling me that he's enjoying learning from us so I've got to take his word for that."
