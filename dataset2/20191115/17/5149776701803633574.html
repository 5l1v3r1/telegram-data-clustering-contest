Warrant issued for Hucknall man's arrest

He is charged with having drugs at his home

15 Nov 2019, 17:22 by Rod Malcolm

A Hucknall man, accused of having cocaine and Valium at home, is at risk of immediate arrest.

A warrant without bail was issued by a judge sitting at Nottingham Magistrates' Court after Thomas Burney, 30, failed to attend.

He is charged with having the drugs at his home in Crown Street, Hucknall on September 29.

The application for the warrant was made by Sanjay Jerath, for the Crown Prosecution Service.
