Transfer Update: Mbappe

15 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Kurt Buckerfield

With Spanish giants Real Madrid reportedly preparing a move for Paris Saint-Germain superstar Kylian Mbappe, here is the latest on the Frenchman's future.

10 Players Who Left Real Madrid Because Of Ronaldo

Reports in Spain and France in recent weeks have suggested that Los Blancos are readying a big-money move for the 20-year-old, but there now seems to be a new development.

According to ESPN, to counter the threat of the 13-time UEFA Champions League winners, PSG have offered the striker a new contract in France.

The Ligue 1 giants are believed to have no intentions of letting the forward leave next season, which is when Real were said to be hoping to lure him to Spain.

It has also been claimed that Mbappe has given the French champions his word that he will not look to leave ahead of the 2020/21 season, although it remains to be seen whether or not he accepts the club's new deal for him.

The new contract would see the forward earn nearly as much as Neymar, whose future in France is still unclear amid constant speculation that he could return to FC Barcelona.
