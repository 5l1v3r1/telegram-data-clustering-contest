RCMP seek four suspects in Campbell River office break-in

15 Nov 2019, 17:15 by Jeff Bell

Campbell River RCMP are looking for four suspects after a break-in at Quinsam Communications early Wednesday.

Police said two people made their way into the 16th Avenue business between 1:15 and 1:30 a.m. by smashing the glass in the front door. A third person entered at 4:45 a.m., followed by a fourth at 6:03 a.m.

All were caught on surveillance video, but it is not clear if they were connected.

“They could be linked but they might not be,” said Campbell River RCMP Const. Maury Tyre.

He said the third and fourth people to appear at the store might have been walking by and seen a situation they could take advantage of.

Tyre said the intruders took “all sorts of random things,” including an iPad, cords and cellphone covers.

“Everybody did a quick grab.”
