DC Universe Reveals First Official Look at Ravager's Costume

15 Nov 2019, 17:11 by L.D. Nolan

DC Universe has released the first official look at Rose Wilson wearing her Ravager costume in Titans.

The image, which you can see below, is being used as the featured image for Titans Season 2, episode 12. Exactly how Rose will get her costume remains unknown at this time.

Created by Marv Wolfman and Art Nichols, Rose Wilson/Ravager first appeared in 1992's Deathstroke #15. She's the daughter of Slade Wilson/Deathstroke and the fourth person to use the name Ravager, following in the footsteps of Grant Wilson, Bill Walsh and Wade LaFarge. Chelsea Zhang joined Titans as the character this season, and she's since struck up a romantic relationship with Jason Todd.
