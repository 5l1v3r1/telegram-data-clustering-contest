6 facts about the young man who invaded the pitch during Ghana – South Africa match

15 Nov 2019, 17:47 by Adu Sarpeah

Yesterday’s pitch invader during Ghana vrs South Africa football match has become the talk of Ghana especially among social media users for obvious reasons.

Per our checks, ghbase.com has been reliably informed that the pitch invader is not any illiterate person roaming about the streets of Cape Coast and its environs but a well-informed being contrary to popular perception.

READ:–Pitch Invader Charged With Disturbing Public Peace In Court Today PLUS He Said He Wanted A Selfie With Thomas Partey

Besides, most people erroneously concluded that the weird act of the pitch invader might have been as a result of winning a huge bet on the night. However, our checks tell a different story altogether.

Below are some few facts we’ve been able to gather about the man of the moment.

  1. Awal Suleman London happens to be the real name of the pitch invader under discussion.
  2. He is a student nurse from the Nalerigu College of Nursing.
  3. Awal completed Apam Senior High School.
  4. He was one of the best 400m runners when he was a student of Apam Senior High School.
  5. Historically, Awal Suleman is the first person to invade a professional football match in Ghana.
  6. As it stands, Awal might be the first person to be prosecuted for invading a pitch per the nature of the charges (unlawful entry and offensive character) levelled up against him by police.
