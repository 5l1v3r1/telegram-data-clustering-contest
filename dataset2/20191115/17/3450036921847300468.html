Local care center employees cook up tasty chili

15 Nov 2019, 17:46 by John Garlock

KIRKSVILLE, Mo. — A local care facility celebrated the Saturday start of Missouri's Fall Firearms Deer Season with a little friendly competition for its staff.

Employees at Kirksville Manor Care Center took part in a chili cookoff Friday afternoon.

KTVO Human Resources Director Melissa Billington and KTVO’s John Garlock served as the judges for the contest.

There were six entries, and they were all delicious. Unfortunately, there could only be three winners.

Third place went to Adam Smith, second place went to Kathy Humphreys, and first place was awarded to Tara Craig.

All three were given a prize.

Manor Care Activities Director Nancy Price said this was the first year for the chili contest, but they will probably make it an annual event.
