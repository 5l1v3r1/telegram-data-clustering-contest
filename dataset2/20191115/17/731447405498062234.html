NAKED unveil second EP, Killed By Roses, in Halcyon Veil trilogy

The duo's next release on Rabit's label is out on January 24th.

15 Nov 2019, 17:08 by Marissa Cetin

Up next on Halcyon Veil is an EP from NAKED called Killed By Roses.

Agnes Gryczkowska and Alex Johnston return to Rabit's label for the second part of their trilogy, which started with the 2017 EP Total Power Exchange. The London- and Berlin-based duo aim for "psychological horror drama" with Killed By Roses, according to the label. They're currently collaborating with the London Contemporary Orchestra for the third and final part in the series and a theatrical live show.
