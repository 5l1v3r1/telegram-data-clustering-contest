Armed suspect taken into custody following police standoff in Ottumwa

15 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Romelo Styles

OTTUMWA, Iowa â€” An armed suspect is now in custody following an hour-long standoff with Ottumwa police.

Around 3:45 p.m. Friday, authorities received multiple reports of a man acting erratically near Christ's Church, just south of Ottumwa.

Officers approached the man along Highway 63 south, and learned that he was armed with a knife. When police ordered the man to put the knife down, officers say he would not comply with their orders.

A standoff ensued for about an hour.

The man eventually surrendered to officers, and he was taken into custody.

Police declined to release the man's name at this time. Charges against him are pending.
