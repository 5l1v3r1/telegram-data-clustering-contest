Sushant Singh Rajput and Rhea Chakraborty clicked in the city

15 Nov 2019, 15:49 by Filmfare

Although the two have outright denied it, rumours linking Sushant Singh Rajput and Rhea Chakraborty have constantly made their way to the headlines. The two have been friends from a long time now and have often been spotted together in the city which just further adds fuel to the fire. 

Earlier today, the alleged couple were snapped together as they were visiting a friend’s house. Sushant kept it cool and casual as he opted for a black tee and a pair of denims. Rhea on the other hand went for a traditional look and was seen sporting an embroidered Indian outfit.
