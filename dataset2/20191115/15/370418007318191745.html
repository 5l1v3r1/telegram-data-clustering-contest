Slipknot Announce Knotfest Cruise

Slipknot are bringing Knotfest At Sea to Barcelona in 2020

15 Nov 2019, 15:00

Calling all seafaring Maggots, Slipknot have announced their very first Knotfest cruise.

Yes, you read that right. Knotfest At Sea will take place off the coast of Barcelona, Spain, from August 10-14, 2020 and will feature two separate performances from Slipknot across the weekend.

Or, as Clown puts it in the video below, “Barcelona, a big-ass ship, and the sea. You, us, you’re not gonna want to miss it.”

The full line-up is yet to be announced, as are other “on board activities”. If it’s anything like the Knotfests we’ve seen on land, there could be a whole host of dark and twisted exhibitions from the mind of The Nine.

For more information, head to KnotfestAtSea.com.
