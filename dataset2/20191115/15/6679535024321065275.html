Kanaka Shri award conferred on B.N. Murthy

15 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Special Correspondent

The State government conferred the Kanaka Shri award on B.N. Murthy in Bengaluru on Friday. He is the brother of the late B.N. Vijay Kumar, former BJP MLA of Jayanagar in the city, and was associated with the Vishwa Hindu Parishad.
