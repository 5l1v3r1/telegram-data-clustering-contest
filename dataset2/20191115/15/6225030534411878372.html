Churchill Downs now selling Kentucky Derby 2020 tickets

15 Nov 2019, 15:41 by HRN Staff

The first Saturday in May is still 5 ½ months away, but fans can get an early jump on tickets for racing’s biggest event.

Tickets went on sale Friday for the Kentucky Oaks and 2020 Kentucky Derby, Churchill Downs announced. The Louisville, Ky., track will host the Derby’s 146th running Saturday, May 2, one day after the Oaks' 146th edition.
