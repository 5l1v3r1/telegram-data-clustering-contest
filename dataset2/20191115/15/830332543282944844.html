PHOTOS: A look at Starbucks Reserve Roastery Chicago, largest coffee experience of the company

New roastery opens Friday

15 Nov 2019, 15:40

Starbucks Coffee Co. opened the Reserve Roastery Chicago Friday on the Magnificent Mile. The new shop is the company's six global roastery and third in the U.S. The location is Starbucks' largest-ever immersive coffee experience with five floors and 35,000 square feet of retail space.
