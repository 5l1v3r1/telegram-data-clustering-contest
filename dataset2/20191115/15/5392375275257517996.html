Xiaomi TV 5 Sound Wave Recognition Can Register Voiceprint

15 Nov 2019, 15:10 by Argam Artashyan

Rate this post

Many know that the Xiaomi TV 5 is already out. Moreover, many of them are thinking this is another high-end TV and that’s all. But it’s not so. The new Xiaomi TV brings a lot of new features. One of them is the sound wave recognition function. Of course, it’s still in the testing stage, but we are sure this feature will come to the TV products via updates in the nearest future. Today, Xiaomi TV department general manager Li Xiaoshuang introduced how this function works. Though it’s in Chinese, we still recommend you to watch the video below.

Watch the video following the link.

The Xiaomi Mi TV 5 supports the voice recognition of Xiao Ai. Users can register voiceprints themselves, establish a voiceprint model, and identify everyone in the family. In addition, you can accurately view the playing history of each family member and personalize the recommended content for them.

As for the configuration, the Xiaomi TV 5 has many reasons to boast of. Say, it’s packed with a high-end ultra-thin full-screen display. It uses an integrated bent metal frame, which is as thin as 5.9mm. Under the hood, the TV carries a 12nm process chip, which supports 8K video playback.

As said, the TV comes with far-field voice control. There is also a big screen Xiao Ai speaker. The Xiaomi TV 5 Pro uses a 4K quantum dot screen, of which the color gamut is up to NTSC108%. It also has an HDR 10+ certification, and our protagonist is equipped with MEMC motion compensation. There is a 4GB + 64GB large storage combination.
