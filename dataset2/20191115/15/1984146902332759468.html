Sunderland - the perfect setting for the perfect gifts

15 Nov 2019, 15:56 by Evening Chronicle

THOUSANDS of twinkling lights pierce the winter skies of Sunderland, as the city’s magical Festival of Light is reborn for 2019. Inspire finds out how this stunning annual event is adding a sprinkle of enchantment to the city centre throughout the festive season...

AS THE cold winter nights draw in, and shoppers snap up the last of their Christmas goodies, on the edge of Sunderland’s city centre, Mowbray Park is aglow – alive with sparkling lighting installations.

Every Thursday to Sunday from November 21 to December 22, the Festival of Light is injecting magic and sparkle into beautiful municipal parkland that stands in the shadow of the Sunderland Museum and Winter Gardens. A host of lighting installations will adorn the trees and lawns of Sunderland’s award-winning Green Flag park. And family-friendly shows, taking place in a magical tipi, are also available to book for those who want to add some extra entertainment to their visit. Along with live music, street theatre and a host of children’s activities, the Festival of Light promises to be truly magical.

It is part of a packed programme of Christmas activity in the heart of Sunderland that is bringing plenty of festive cheer to residents and visitors alike. Alongside the city’s fantastic range of shops, restaurants and bars, Sunderland has welcomed back its popular outdoor ice rink, which lies on Keel Square.

The frozen rink will be open every day over the festive period, excluding Christmas and New Year’s Day. Other family-friendly festive activities on offer in Sunderland include a visit to Sunniside’s Empire Cinema to enjoy Frozen 2, a trip to see Cinderella as the Sunderland Empire, or there’s the chance to take part in the city’s Reindeer Dash on Sunday, December 8. For something a little bit different, Hadrian’s Tipi stands close to the city’s Keel Square. The Bridges shopping centre, just metres from the Tipi, is also playing host to a shimmering snow-scene complete with Santa’s Grotto. The Disney Store, The Entertainer and Debenhams are among the most popular retailers to tick off for those in pursuit of the latest must-have toys.

The scene is set for a scintillating Christmas in Sunderland. To book your tickets for Sunderland’s Festival of Light, visit www.christmasinsunderland.co.uk . Tickets are £3 per person (under-twos free) and discounts available for online bookings of three tickets or more.

The magical tipi, in Mowbray Park, will be playing host to Snow Queen and Superhero save Christmas, Elf Check List Training and Storytime with Santa shows – each of which are sure to get families in the mood for the festive season. After purchasing Festival of Light tickets these are £3 per child with one accompanying adult free.

And for beautiful gifts....

 

Debenhams

From stylish menswear, to something for the lady in your life, Debenhams is the ultimate one-stop-shop for Christmas gifts for all the family. From fragrances to make up, and children’s clothing to the top toys for 2019, look no further for all your presents. 

Hotel Chocolat

For sweet-toothed friends and family members, Hotel Chocolat is the perfect place to pick up a packet (or two!) of tasty cocoa morsels for a tongue-tingling treat. 

Designer Childrenswear

Pop to Olive Street in Sunderland’s Park Lane to pick up the latest collections of kids clothes from Gucci, Burberry and Moncler, among other luxury brands.

Flannels

If your loved one likes labels, check out Flannels in Fawcett Street for high-end clothing that will add some ‘je ne sais quoi’ to any fashion fan’s wardrobe.

The Disney Store

If you’re stocking up for the little ones, where else would you go but The Disney Store? You can pick up everything from toys and costumes to giftware featuring your all your Disney favourites. And it’s not just little kids who will like the treats... many a big kid will be delighted with their own Disney themed teacup or keyring this Christmas.

Lush 

Bath-bomb lovers rejoice. For the most stunning sensory treat, pop into Lush to pick up fresh hand-made cosmetics, that are a treat to the body, mind and... nose! 

Flying Tiger Copenhagen

Stockings just got fuller this Christmas! Flying Tiger Copenhapen is the ultimate place to find oodles of things you didn’t think you needed but can’t live without! There’s gadgets and gizmos, trinkets and treats – a must visit for little gifts that will surprise and delight!

Boots

Well-known for its fabulous 3 for 2 gift offers in the run up to December 25, Boots is always a shopper’s favourite at Christmastime. With unusual ideas for him and her, if you’re struggling for inspiration, look no further.

Harrison Brown

For the house-proud gift recipient, check out Harrison Brown. Based in Holmeside, they stock a range of interiors from some of the world’s top designers and manufacturers. There’s everything from large upholstered items to stunning furnishings.

Aphrodite

A firm favourite with Sunderland shoppers, this high-end men’s retailer stocks attire that will appeal to the most stylish gent. Pop over to Vine Street – there’re no doubt you’ll find something suitable.

Nomination

Nomination Sunderland is the city’s exclusive stockist of the Italian fashion jewellery brand Nomination Italy, standing on the corner of Crowtree Road, opposite Debenhams. Born from the art of Italian craftsmanship, Nomination’s purpose and passion is to provide good quality and well-priced fashion jewellery.
