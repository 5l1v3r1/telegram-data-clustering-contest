The first console Pokémon RPG is here, and thanks to it being on the Nintendo Switch, you can actually take it with you wherever you go. Pokémon Sword and Shield is set in the brand new Galar region, and features dozens of new ‘mons to catch and train. But it’s no walk in the park. Thankfully we’ve got you covered with a handful of guides, easing you through some of the trickier parts of the game. We’ll detail some of the harder gym battles, and who you should bring with you to battle them. We’ll cover the brand new Wild Area and what it means for catch the strongest Pokémon. We’ll even tell you how to make some awesome curry. Don’t worry, it’s not nearly as intimidating as it sounds. So what are you waiting for? It’s time to jump into the Galar region and start making some lifelong friends!
