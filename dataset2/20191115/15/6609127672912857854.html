CHAMPAGNE JILL

Rangers fan furious as stalker fan Jill Sharp boasts over Celtic cup final tickets – but others are convinced it’s fake

15 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Blair Meikle

RANGERS fans are furious that 'fantasist' fan 'Champagne Jill' Sharp appears to have snapped up a cup final ticket - but others are convinced it's a fake.

Punters fumed after convicted stalker Sharp, 32, posted on social media claiming she had tickets for the Betfred Cup showdown with Celtic.

She uploaded a screenshot of an email from Rangers appearing to confirm she had been allocated a brief, captioned: "Yeeeeeee haaaaaaaa. Let's gooooo."

Sharp served time after being blasted in court over her "deliberate and malicious" stalking campaign against a fellow Gers-supporting couple.

She admitted causing pal Margaret and Steven Paton fear and alarm between January 2014 and August 2017.

Fans who reckoned her past should have earned her a ban from Ibrox were furious she had managed to secure cup final tickets.

One said: "Jill Sharp getting a ticket for the final is wrong. She should have her season taken off her.

"When you think what Rangers ban people for (the Killie thing being the most recent) is a scandal.

 

 

"She terrorised a lovely couple through the Rangers connection."

Another wrote: "The couple were ST holders and no longer attend games because of fear of bumping into her.

"Rangers FC should revoke her ST, she’s taken the club into disrepute etc.

"Getting a ticket for the CF ain’t her fault, that’s down to the system. Ban her prob solved."

Another added: "Fly b*****d has probably had someone use her season book and away allocations while shes been away f*****g shockin either way."

But some poured cold water on the post by claiming the email had been photoshopped.

One said: "Look at the ‘Rangers Number’ column... Why is Mr Hale’s number in a red font. While Ms Sharp’s number is blue with a weblink line underneath it. PHOTOSHOPPED."

Another added: "It’s defo photoshopped."
