The 11 Best Movies You Can Stream On Disney+ Right Now

15 Nov 2019, 15:00

Fans of Disney’s extensive animation, Marvel, and Star Wars libraries already know about the wealth of content available for streaming on Disney+, the $6.99 monthly service that delivers most of the company’s content to your television. Over 600 titles were made available when the channel launched November 12. (Just don’t expect to see 1946’s controversial Song of the South or World War II-era animated shorts with problematic propaganda content.)

If you know your way around classics starring the likes of Mary Poppins and Iron Man, take a look at 11 other films on Disney+ worth checking out.
