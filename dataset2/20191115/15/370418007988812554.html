Listen To Weezer’s Song For The Frozen 2 Soundtrack

Weezer’s end-credits cover of Lost In The Woods for Frozen 2 is finally here.

15 Nov 2019, 15:55

Following the release of Panic! At The Disco’s spectacular take on Into The Unknown from the Frozen 2 soundtrack, Weezer have now unveiled their cover for the smash Disney franchise.

Rivers Cuomo and co.’s touching rendition of Lost In The Words is performed by Jonathan Groff (the friendly iceman Kristoff) in the movie, but the Weezer-ed up version actually appears alongside Panic! and Kacey Musgraves’ covers in the end credits. All three songs are available now as part of the Frozen 2 soundtrack, which you can grab right here if you’re a fan of the animated musical sensation.

“While we can’t wait for the world to hear these songs, we feel like we got a sneak peek of how people will react with how quickly our end credits artists signed on,” says executive music producer Tom MacDougall, who won a GRAMMY for the first movie’s soundtrack. “Their excitement for this franchise and these songs is represented in their wildly creative takes on each of their versions.”
