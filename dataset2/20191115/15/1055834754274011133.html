The Winter Tour in Mario Kart Tour is starting on 19th November

15 Nov 2019, 15:05 by My Nintendo News Admin

Nintendo has announced via Twitter that the Winter Tour will be the next tour in the free-to-play smartphone game, Mario Kart Tour. You’ll be able to sign up to the Winter Tour when it kicks off on 19th November and it is set to feature plenty of cosy snow scenes. You’ll need your snow tyres at the ready if you wish to embark on this one.
