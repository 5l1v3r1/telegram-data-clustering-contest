Shawn Mendes branded 'coward' by Taylor Swift over Scooter Braun row

15 Nov 2019, 14:54

Taylor Swift fans have been dragging Shawn Mendes, questioning the singer on why he’s staying silent after her statement about Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta.

Bit awkward.

In a statement that shocked her followers, Taylor claimed she was ‘not allowed’ to perform her own music on TV and that there’s a ‘question mark’ over her AMAs performance, concerning legal issues with her former record company.

She also pleaded with her fans to go after Scooter and Scott, saying: ‘The message being sent to me is very clear. Basically, be a good little girl and shut up. Or you’ll be punished.’

Halsey, Lily Allen, and Gigi Hadid are among the stars to speak out in support of the Lover singer – although others have kept quiet.

Despite Shawn recently collaborating with Taylor on a remix of her hit Lover, he simply took to Instagram to post a snap of his girlfriend Camila Cabello, and Tay’s fans aren’t having any of it.

‘I’m done we shawn… taylor let you open for her then chooses you to collaborate with, even allowed you to write your own awful lyrics & we know you see what’s happening but you post THAT? typical straight white boy who thinks he deserves a trophy for wearing nail polish. bye [sic],’ one fumed.

‘Shawn or ed probably support taylor in private”. THAT’S NOT THE POINT. the point is that they’re men and have a huge platform, but they won’t use it because they want to maintain a good reputation and avoid controversy,’ one more angry fan pointed out.
