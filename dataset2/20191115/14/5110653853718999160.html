Google Stadia release date, games, price and more you need to know for launch

15 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Jordan Heck

Google is entering the gaming world with itscloud gaming service Stadia later this month.

Stadia is different from other consoles like the Xbox One or Playstation 4 because it focuses solely on cloud gaming. With the Xbox and Playstation you'll need to either purchase a disc or download the game to your console. With Stadia, you're streaming the game much like you would a Netflix show. The game lives on a server rather than within your machine or a disc.

Because Stadia is focusing on cloud gaming, you can play pretty much anywhere:laptops, PCs with a Chrome browser, Pixel phones, or a TV with an HDMI port where you can plug in yourChromecast Ultra dongle.

Google Stadia release date

Google Stadia will release on November 19, 2019.

People who were early on the Stadia train were able to pre-order theFounder’s Edition, which includeda Night Blue Stadia Controller ($69 separately), aGoogle Chromecast Ultra($69 separately), 3 months of Stadia Pro and a 3-month buddy pass for giving Pro access to a friend.

But those sold out quickly.
