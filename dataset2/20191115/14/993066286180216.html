New arrival for Irish News columnist Lynette Fay

15 Nov 2019, 14:00

Irish News columnist Lynette Fay has given birth to a baby girl.

The well-known Radio Ulster personality revealed on social media yesterday that she had welcomed a new arrival.

In her tweet, the Dungannon columnist said: "Life changed forever yesterday when we welcomed our beautiful little girl, Neansaí Máire into the world.

"Gavin & I are over the moon, as are our families & friends. Thanks to the incredible staff of The Royal Maternity for their care & attention to detail for Neansaí Mhíle Grá".

Writing in the Irish News column in recent weeks, Ms Fay said she would be taking time off with her new arrival until the spring.
