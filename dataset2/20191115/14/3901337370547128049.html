Huge Blow In Arrows’ Plans For Downs?

15 Nov 2019, 14:27 by Soccer Laduma

In their planning to face Mamelodi Sundowns in their second successive match, Golden Arrows might have to tweak their line-up as one of the team’s regulars appears to be in doubt for the upcoming Telkom Knockout clash.

Abafana Bes’thende will host the Brazilians in their TKO semi-final match at the Sugar Ray Xulu Stadium next weekend.

The KwaZulu-Natal outfit defeated Downs 3-2 in an Absa Premiership match played at the same venue, ahead of the FIFA international break.  

News from the Zimbabwe senior national team is that Arrows defender Divine Lunga has picked up an injury. 

According to Zimbabwean media, the left-back has been ruled out of tonight’s match for the Warriors against Botswana in the Africa Cup of Nations qualifiers.

It is believed that the 24-year-old suffered a groin strain, with Zimbabwean website NewsDay reporting that team doctor Xolani Ndlovu has recommended an MRI scan to determine the extent of the injury. 

Lunga has been influential for coach Steve Komphela’s team, having started nine of the 11 league matches for the side this season, and has been impressive in the two TKO matches against Polokwane City and SuperSport United respectively.
