Trailer for ‘Dwellers’, Produced by Megadeth’s David Ellefson [Video]

15 Nov 2019, 14:49 by Brad Miska

It’s Blair Witch Project meets C.H.U.D. in Dwellers, written/directed by and starring Drew Fortier.

While shooting a documentary on the suspicious disappearances within the homeless community, a filmmaker and his crew go missing while uncovering a terrifying and vicious secret below the city’s surface.

The film is produced by Megadeth’s David Ellefson with Thom Hazaert via Ellefson Films.

Here’s the brand new trailer with distribution/festival news still forthcoming.
