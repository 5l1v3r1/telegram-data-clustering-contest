Nakuul Mehta to attend AVTA 2019

15 Nov 2019, 14:55 by Raj Baddhan

Television star, Nakuul Mehta will be attending the Asian Viewers Television Awards (AVTA) 2019, taking place this weekend.

Mehta, who is known for his work in shows like ‘Ishqbaaaz’ and Pyaar Ka Dard Hai’, is the latest name to be added to the star-studded line-up, making it the biggest AVTA event in history.

Mehta joins his ‘Ishqbaaaz’ pal Kunal Jaisingh, along with Harshad Chopda, Erica Fernandes and Bharati Kumar, who will all be attending the event. It will be hosted by Karan Tacker. This is the second time Mehta will be attending AVTA, after his appearance in 2017.

AVTA 2019 will once again take place at Hilton London Heathrow Airport Terminal 5 on Saturday 16th November. AVTA is the only event to solely celebrate the success of Asian television in the UK market. It is helmed by media veteran, Rajan Singh.

As well as sponsoring AVTA 2019, BizAsiaLive.com is the exclusive digital media partner of the Asian Viewers Television Awards.
