How Fans Reacted To Makola's Six-Month Ban

15 Nov 2019, 14:58 by SL Social

Cape Town City midfielder Mpho Makola bore the brunt of a strong sanctioning by the PSL after he was found guilty of assaulting a referee in a Telkom Knockout match last month. Here's how social media reacted to the news. Check it out!

Makola was charged after it was determined that he had assaulted match referee Abongile Tom during the Citizens' spicy fixture against Kaizer Chiefs at Cape Town Stadium in the Telkom Knockout last month.

The former Orlando Pirates man was reprimanded for pushing the official in the midst of a heated scuffled during the game, and got away with only a yellow card at the time.
