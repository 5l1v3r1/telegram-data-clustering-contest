Portugal team trains before travelling to Luxembourg

15 Nov 2019, 14:22 by TPN/Lusa

Portugal’s national football team is training on Friday in the Algarve, before travelling to Luxembourg, where they are to play their last UEFA EURO2020 qualifier on Sunday.

Hours after beating Lithuania (6-0) at the Algarve Stadium on Thursday, Portugal was training at the hotel where they are expected to spare the starters from Thursday's triumph and then travel to Luxembourg.

With a Group B encounter to play, Ukraine, who have already qualified, lead with 19 points, five more than Portugal and six more than Serbia.

In their final matchday, Portugal plays against Luxembourg, while Serbia host Ukraine, having to win or wait until Serbs do not win to secure second place and qualification.
