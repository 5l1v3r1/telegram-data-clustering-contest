Brazilian GP FP1 report | Alex Albon tops the time sheet and also crashes out!

15 Nov 2019, 14:16 by Louis Shaw

Alex Albon topped the timesheets in a wet FP1 session at the Brazilian Grand Prix. The Red Bull driver went out on the slick tyre at the end of the session and crashed into a wall causing the session to be Red Flagged and ended early. 

It was a wet and slow start to FP1 at the Brazilian Grand Prix. The rain meant that teams waited until the track dried out before heading out. Drivers started the session on the full wet tyre and after about 30 mins of running the swapped and used the inter tyre.
