Sky Sports is half price in massive Black Friday sale

Football fans can get their hands on half price Sky Sports in the new Black Friday sale

15 Nov 2019, 14:02 by Stephen Hurrell

Sky will give away half-price Sky Sports TV packages from today to celebrate Black Friday.

The TV and broadband provider has revealed its Black Friday offers two weeks before it takes place on the 29 November and users can sign up for half price sport as one of three discounted bundles on offer.

Sky is selling Sky Entertainment and Sky Sports, which includes Premier League football matches, for £37 a month, down from £52.

The package will give customers a chance to see Premier League games on Friday nights, 5.30pm on Saturday and Sunday, as well as selected Championship games for the rest of the season.

The 50% off Sky packages options also include a Cinema at Home bundle including Sky Entertainment and Sky Cinema for £31.40, down from £41.

The final option is an all-inclusive bundle featuring Sky Entertainment, Sky Sports, Sky Cinema, Sky Kids, Ultimate On Demand (including Netflix) and Sky Q for £64, down from £106.

The TV provider has launched the deals on the same day major rival BT announced it had secured the rights to European football including the Champions League and Europa League until 2024.

However, with the likes of the F1 and cricket for half price Sky has quickly hit back with its Black Friday deals.

It has also launched a series of phone and tablet discounts as well as Broadband deals from £29 per month as part of its Black Friday announcement.
