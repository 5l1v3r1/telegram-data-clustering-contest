iDKHOW Have Just Dropped A Christmas EP

Listen to iDKHOW’s brand-new three-track Christmas EP – featuring a cover of Slade’s Merry Christmas Everybody.

15 Nov 2019, 14:07

With just over a month to go until Christmas, iDKHOW are getting into the festive spirit nice and early with a brand-new Christmas Drag EP.

The duo – Dallon Weekes and Ryan Seaman – have unveiled a three-track release ahead of the big day on December 25, featuring a cover of Slade’s Merry Christmas Everybody, as well as Oh Noel, and an original song in the form of the title-track.

Read this next: Dallon Weekes on iDKHOW’s debut album

“I wrote Christmas Drag over a decade ago,” Dallon explains. “I’ve always been a sucker for Christmas music, and this was one of the first Christmas songs I ever wrote for myself. I’m happy I got a chance to give it a proper recording and release. Oh Noel was recorded in one take, on the first try. I recorded it with a fella named Stu Maxfield, who is this fantastic musician from Provo. After we did the first pass, the engineer asked us if we wanted another take. We looked at each other and said no at the same time. A pretty special studio moment.”
