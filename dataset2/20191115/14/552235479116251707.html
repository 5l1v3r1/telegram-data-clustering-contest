Confusion reigns as man in Celtic shirt goes on Rangers stadium tour

The bizarre situation has come to light after photos appeared online.

15 Nov 2019, 14:22 by Jonathan McFarlane

A mystery man has caused a social media buzz by visiting Ibrox to attend a tour of the famous old ground - in the Hoops of bitter rivals Celtic !

The individual was pictured filming outside the Govan stadium in a Celtic top adorned with the name of skillful midfielder Callum McGregor.

He was well equipped with a tripod and microphone to record his visit.

Later, he was also pictured in the Ibrox press room where Steven Gerrard give post-match press-briefings and in the iconic Blue Room where major announcements, like the appointment of new managers, are often made.

It is unclear whether the man is a slightly befuddled tourist, the subject of a colossal wind-up or part of some sort of bizarre video experiment.

With the preponderance of football bloggers now pumping out content on the likes of YouTube, he may simply be looking to report on the city's two biggest clubs.

Either way it appears the mystery man was welcomed warmly at Ibrox despite his unusual sartorial choice.
