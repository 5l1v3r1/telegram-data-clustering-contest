'Intimidation': Fury as Trump attacks impeachment witness in real-time

President Donald Trump's Twitter attack on an impeachment witness during her testimony on Friday drew a furious response from Democrats, who accused him of witness intimidation, and even some allies criticized him.

"Witness intimidation is a crime," US Senator and presidential contender Kamala Harris wrote on Twitter after Trump wrote that everywhere former US Ambassador Marie Yovanovitch went in her long career "turned bad."
