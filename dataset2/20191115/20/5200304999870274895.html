Ayushmann Khurrana on what success has taught him

15 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Amrita Tanna

Ayushmann Khurrana is having a dream run and it seems to not be slowing down. The actor has recently spoken about what he’s been taught from the success he’s seen.

Talking to Mid-Day, the actor expressed, “They say you learn a lot through your successes and this current phase has told me that I should always push the content envelope and pick and back edgy stories because somehow my brand of cinema has become synonymous with something that’s a little left of centre”.

Khurrana also said a few words to towards his fans, “It feels amazing that my films are being loved by audiences across the country”.
