Shocking video shows cop pin abandoned quadruple amputee, 15, to the ground

15 Nov 2019, 20:41

Shocking footage shows the moment a cop tackled a 15-year-old quadruple amputee and pinned him to the ground.

The incident was filmed by another teen inside a group home in Tucson, Arizona in September.

KOLD was obtained the video and said the boy, who has amputations on all four limbs, was in the home because he had been abandoned by his family.

The Pima County Sheriff’s Department deputy was reportedly called to the group home because the teen apparently knocked over a garbage can and verbally threatened a worker in the home.

At the beginning of the video, the deputy is seen on top of teen, pressing him into a refrigerator.
