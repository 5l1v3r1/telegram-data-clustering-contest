General Zod Is Going to Hunt Down a Major Batman Villain

15 Nov 2019, 20:11 by Sam Stone

While the World's Finest are currently locked in an epic showdown against the Batman Who Laughs, two familiar foes will haunt them this February in the pages of Batman/Superman.

DC Comics has released its advance solicitation information for titles going on sale February 2020, including Batman/Superman #7, teaming writer Joshua Williamson with artist Nick Derington. As the first in a two-part story, General Zod will return to Earth hunting the longtime Batman villain Ra's al Ghul for reasons involving the Kryptonian city of Kandor. The advance solicit information and standard cover illustrated by Derington are below.

Zod was last seen installing himself and his family as the dictators on a faraway world, reaching a tentative truce with Superman while nearly throttling Green Lantern within an inch of his life when he attempted to stop him. With the Kryptonian military officer active back on Earth, the fragile peace between him and the Man of Steel may soon come to an explosive end.

Batman/Superman #7 is written by Joshua Williamson and illustrated by Nick Derington. It is scheduled to go on sale Feb. 26 from DC Comics.
