'Glen Kamara is our Xavi' Finnish fans rave about Rangers star's performance as they qualify for Euro 2020 and first major tournament

Former Celtic striker Teemu Pukki grabbed a double, but it was the Ibrox midfielder who earned high praise from supporters as they made it a historic night for their country.

15 Nov 2019, 20:20 by Adam Thomas

Rangers midfielder Glen Kamara was influential in his side's 3-0 victory over Liechtenstein as they secured their place at Euro 2020 .

Former Celtic striker Teemu Pukki scored a double after Jasse Tuominen opened the scoring for the Finns, as they made it a historic night for their nation.

The result meant they now have a seven point lead over Greece in third place with just one more round of games to play, meaning they will compete in their first ever major championship next summer.

Kamara played the full 90 minutes for his country tonight, as the Rangers midfielder put in an assured display in the middle of the park.

He's been a key asset for Finland in the qualification campaign, helping his team earn wins over the likes of Greece and Armenia earlier this season.

The Ibrox midfielder played 90 minutes for Steven Gerrard's side against Livingston on Sunday, but that didn't affect the midfielder's performance tonight in the middle of the park.

Finnish fans were raving about his display on social media, comparing him to the likes of Barcelona's Sergio Busquets and Xavi.
