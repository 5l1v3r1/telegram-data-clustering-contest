DEADPOOL #2 and #3 Pushed Back a Month Each

15 Nov 2019, 20:06 by Chris Arrant, Editor

Marvel has notified retailers that Deadpool #2 and #3 have been postponed one month each. This follows the publisher previously pushing back the series' debut two weeks to November 20.

Deadpool #2 is now scheduled for January 8, with Deadpool #3 adjusted to come out February 19.

The publisher has not given a reason for these schedule changes.
