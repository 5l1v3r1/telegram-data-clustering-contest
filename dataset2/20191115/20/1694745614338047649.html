Whether You're an Anna or an Elsa, Colourpop's Frozen 2 Collection Is Fit For Any Queen

Colourpop is the latest brand to get in on the hype surrounding the long-awaited debut of Frozen 2. The affordable beauty brand just released an entire makeup collection inspired by the Disney film and its two main characters, Anna and Elsa. The limited-edition range comes with eight products — two eyeshadow palettes, two lipsticks, two lip glosses, and two glitter gels — that are split up into two parts matching both of the characters' personalities.

In Anna's collection, you'll find a handful of warm-toned items like a creamy, chestnut-hued lipstick, a peachy lip gloss, and a palette with a series of matte and metallic eyeshadows in purple and gold colors. Elsa's half of the collection, falls on the cooler end of the spectrum with a raspberry-hued lipstick, a sparkling gloss, and a palette with shimmery silvers and light colors.

All of the items range from $8 to $76, and customers can purchase each product separately or in the bundles created for each character. You can also buy the whole thing in full if you want to give yourself the best of both worlds. Read ahead to take a look at the Colourpop x Frozen 2 collection and to shop.
