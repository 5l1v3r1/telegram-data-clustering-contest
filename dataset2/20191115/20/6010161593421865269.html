Destiny 2: Xur location and inventory, November 15-18

15 Nov 2019, 20:15 by Stephany Nunneley

It’s Xur time again.

This week, Destiny 2’s vendor Xur is selling a trace rifle, two helmets and chest armor.

You can find him at Giant’s Scar over on Io.

Be sure to get what you want or need before the reset hits on November 19.

Xur inventory for November 15-18

  • Mask of the Quiet One [Exotic Titan Helmet] –  23 Legendary Shards
  • Ophidia Spathe [Exoitic Hunter Chest] – 23 Legendary Shards
  • Prometheus Lens [Exotic Trace Rifle] –  29 Legendary Shards
  • Skull of Dire Ahamkara [Exoitic Warlock Helmet] –  23 Legendary Shards

Our man is selling Prometheus Lens this week. The Exotic Trace Rifle’s Intrinsic Perk Prismatic Inferno fires a Solar trace beam and generates a damaging heat field that grows while the weapon continues to fire. Flame Refraction is its Exotic Trait, and kills will return a fraction of the ammo used to the magazine from reserves. This extends the beam’s duration.
