Read All The Lyrics To The ‘Queen & Slim’ Soundtrack

15 Nov 2019, 05:00 by Caitlin Kelley

The Queen & Slim soundtrack has arrived, and the tracklist is even more star-studded than the romantic thriller. The Lena Waithe-written and Melina Matsoukas-directed movie stars Daniel Kaluuya and Jodie Turner-Smith. Sturgill Simpson and Red Hot Chili Peppers’ Flea also make on-screen appearances. The accompanying score was composed by Dev Hynes.

Some of the biggest names in music appear on the soundtrack, including Megan Thee Stallion, VickeeLo, Bilal, Raphael Saadiq, Vince Staples, 6LACK, Mereba, EARTHGANG, Tiana Major9, Syd, Contra, BJ the Chicago Kid, Choker, Lil Baby, Lauryn Hill, Burna Boy, Blood Orange, Ian Isiah, and Jason Arce. Benny Cassette, BlaqNmilD, and Leken Taylor can be found in the production credits.

“I wanted the soundtrack for Queen & Slim to showcase the historical evolution of Black music, from its roots in blues and soul, to modern bounce, hip-hop, and R&B–much like we used to see in film soundtracks in the ‘90s,” Matsoukas said in a statement. “We partnered with Motown because of their legacy within Black music.”
