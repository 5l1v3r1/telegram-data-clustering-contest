The Killers announce 2020 UK stadium tour - here's how to get tickets

In support of new album, Imploding The Mirage, it's their first tour since headlining Glastonbury

15 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Jillian MacMath

The Killers have announced a 2020 UK stadium tour as they prepare to release their sixth studio album, Imploding the Mirage, this spring.

The best-selling band won't be coming to Wales - but will take to the stage at Bristol's Ashton Gate, just a short drive from Cardiff, on Tuesday, June 9.

The tour marks the first for the indie group since they headlined Glastonbury Festival this year, just a day after their sellout gig at Cardiff Castle .

Supported by Sam Fender, Blossoms and Manic Street Preachers, they'll perform just nine shows in the UK across May and June.

Where and when are they touring?

May 28 – Falkirk, The Falkirk Stadium – with Blossoms

May 30 – Manchester, Emirates Old Trafford – with Blossoms

June 1 – Norwich, Carrow Road Stadium – with Blossoms

June 3 – Southampton, St Mary's Stadium – with Blossoms

June 6 – London, Emirates Stadium – with Sam Fender

June 9 – Bristol, Ashton Gate Stadium – with Manic Street Preachers

June 11 – Coventry, Coventry Ricoh Stadium – with Manic Street Preachers

June 13 – Middlesbrough, Riverside Stadium – with Manic Street Preachers

June 16 – Dublin, Malahide Castle – with Sam Fender

How to get tickets

Tickets go on sale to the public from 9am on November 22, though they are expected to sell quickly.

The good news is, fans which which pre-order their new album will get early access to the sale, increasing their chances for snagging a seat.

A number of premium hospitality packages will also be available, starting from £199.

You can purchase them from Ticketmaster and SeeTickets .
