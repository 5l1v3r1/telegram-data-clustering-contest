Ayushmann Khurrana's Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan gets a new release date

15 Nov 2019, 10:42 by Filmfare

Ayushmann Khurrana’s latest release Bala is doing wonders at the box-office. The actor has impressed the audience yet again. Audiences are now eager to watch his next. Well, Ayushmann's next project is Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan. The project is jointly produced by Bhushan Kumar's T-Series and Aanand L Rai's Colour Yellow Productions. It’s directed by Hitesh Kewalya. Now we hear that the film's release date has been changed. First the film was about to hit the theatres on March 13, 2020. But now the makers have finalised February 21, 2020 as the release date.

Talking about this change, producer Anand L Rai said, "It's a complete family film and we want to meet the audience soon. We have now decided to release the film in the month of February, which is also the month of love." Keep watching this space for more updates on Bollywood.
