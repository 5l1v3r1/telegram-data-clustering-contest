Luis Suarez: Only at the Bernabeu do they whistle me more than at Camp

The former Barca Ballon d'Or winning forward spoke to TV3

15 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Sport.es

Former Barcelona forward Luis Suarez spoke to TV3, looking back at glory days and times of failure. Suarez left Barca as a record signing for the era, joining Inter Milan. He is now 84.

"Only at the Bernabeu they whistled me more than at Camp Nou," he said. He also denied there was a rivalry with Kubala. "After losing 6-0 against Barca at Les Corts, Kubala told the board they had to sign me."
