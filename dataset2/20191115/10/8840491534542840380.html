36 Mayo sports clubs share €1,143,493 in funding

15 Nov 2019, 10:19

THE Minister for Rural and Community Development, Michael Ring, has said that sports clubs and organisations across Mayo are set to benefit from €1,143,493 investment in 36 local projects through the 2019 Sports Capital Grants.

The following organisations in Mayo have been allocated funding:
