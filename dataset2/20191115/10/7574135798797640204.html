Ali Wong adds fourth Beacon Theatre show to ‘Milk & Money Tour’ (tix on sale)

15 Nov 2019, 10:36 by Bill Pearis

Ali Wong is gearing up for her 2020 ‘Milk & Money Tour’ which includes a few show at NYC’s Beacon Theatre. Her three announced shows are all but sold out, so she’s just added a fourth Beacon show on April 3. Tickets are on sale now.
