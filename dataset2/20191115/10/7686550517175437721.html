Waitrose launches new Christmas food range and it looks delicious

The supermarket favourite is even releasing a giant jaffa cake dessert and we NEED it

15 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Stacey Dutton, Chantelle Symester

The Waitrose Christmas food offering for 2019 is packed with showstopping desserts and quality meat and trimmings.

Shoppers who consider a trip to the family store will be able to get ready for the festivities with food orders now available until December 15.

This year's Waitrose Christmas campaign with high street giant John Lewis has already stolen our hearts which tells the tale of an excitable dragon called Edgar who unintentionally sparks chaos as he strives to be accepted by his village.

Now that the Christmas adverts are coming in fast, more of us will be visiting stores to get prepared for the Christmas rush.

Those wanting to order at Waitrose will have items delivered between 20 and 24 December.

The dishes available for 2019 will consist of some traditional flavours but with a Heston spin on one dessert which is set to be a showstopper- their Hesto's Fig and Port Cheesecake.

With a huge increase in searches for vegan roasts last year, Waitrose have released plenty of vegan options to please everyone around the dinner table.

There is also a new signature concoction hitting shelves in the form of their new plum, spiced ginger and honey flavour which we have spotted throughout a number of their new 2019 dishes.

Speaking about the festive combination, Natalie Mitchell, Partner and Head of Product Innovation and Own Brand said: "Each Christmas we create a blend of ingredients that sums up our inspiration. This year's Plum, Spiced Ginger & Honey combination brings tartness, spiced warmth and an aromatic sweetness that works with many classic dishes, from roast turkey to mince pies."

Signature Bacon Wrapped Turkey Breast

New for 2019 is a tasty sounding bacon wrapped turkey breast, which comes with a plum, spiced ginger and honey stuffing with tangy plum glaze. Perfect for anyone who doesn't want to cook a full turkey on the big day, but still wants the main event to be full of flavour.

Price: £25 (1.1kg, serves 5) - buy now on waitrose.com

Vegan Root En Croute

Non meat eaters can tuck into the chain's new vegan root en croute, which is filled with paprika and dill marinated carrots in a creamy spinach sauce come Christmas Day. There are four in a pack, so they make the perfect main if you have a few vegans to cater for.

Price: £14 (pack of 4) - buy now on waitrose.com

Vegan Festive Wreath

Another highlight for this year is the vegan festive wreath made with vegetarian "sausages" made from apple and cranberry and mushroom and leek, with a fruity stuffing and herby crumb. A lovely alternative to the meaty pigs in blankets usually served up on the big day.

Price: £5.99 - soon to be available on waitrose.com

Chorizo Paella Pastry Shells

Your guests will definitely do a double take whilst eating these delicate pastry shells filled with tasty paella. The very convincing shells are coloured with an edible vegetable colouring for an authentic look and the spicy filling is made with rich seafood stock and roasted peppers.

Price: £6 - soon to be available on waitrose.com

Pork and Pancetta Christmas Cracker

What's better than a normal sausage roll? Well probably, Waitrose's festive one. This gigantic version is backed with British pork, cranberries, smoky pancetta and sage and onion stuffing for that extra festive flavour. Perfect for nibbling on in front of the TV as you nestle in for the night.

Price: £7 (serves 10) - soon to be available waitrose.com

Heston Fig and Port cheesecake

If you're a fan of Heston's off the wall cuisine you'll love this cheescake as a show-stopping dessert for Christmas Day. The clever visuals play on what would traditionally be cheese and dessert courses at the end of the meal. The sour cream baked cheesecake comes with a gooey, sweet fig and port caramel centre, all on an edible shortbread cheeseboard base. Genuis.

Price: £12 (675g, serves 8) - soon to be available on waitrose.com

Giant Jaffa Cake Dessert

Nothing screams Christmas like chocolate and oranges - two things that come in abundance in Waitrose's Giant Jaffa Cake Dessert. Layers of light chocolate mouse and fruity clementine jelly and a orange candy crunch make this rich dessert that will please any of your sweet-toothed guests.

Price: £13 (650g, serves 10) - soon to be available on waitrose.com
