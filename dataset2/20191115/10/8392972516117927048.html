Coronation Street and Last of the Summer Wine star Jean Fergusson dies aged 74

Tributes have poured in on social media

15 Nov 2019, 10:51 by Rebecca Carter

Tributes have poured in as Jean Fergusson has died at the age of 74.

The actress was known for her role as Dorothy Hoyle in Coronation Street and Marina in Last of the Summer Wine.

'Allo 'Allo! actress Vicki Michelle has paid tribute to Jean on Twitter.
