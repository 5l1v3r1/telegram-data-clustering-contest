Plymouth Argyle boss Ryan Lowe on FA Cup scouting mission as analyst for BT Sport broadcast

The winners of the televised replay between Bromley and Bristol Rovers will host the Pilgrims in round two

15 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Chris Errington

Ryan Lowe will be a TV analyst for the FA Cup replay which will decide Plymouth Argyle’s second round opponents.

Graham Coughlan’s Bristol Rovers have to travel to National League leaders Bromley next Tuesday in a match which will be televised live by BT Sport.

The winners of the replay on the 3G pitch at Bromley’s Hayes Lane ground will host Argyle on the weekend of November 30-December 1.

Bromley scored a late equaliser when they drew 1-1 away to League One outfit Rovers on Sunday.

Lowe will now get the chance to get a proper look at both sides at the invitation of BT Sport in preparation for Argyle’s second round tie.

He said: “Bromley are going well in the National League and Graham is doing a good job at Bristol Rovers.

“Listen, we know it’s going to be tough but to get through to the third round has never been easy.

“People say ‘You want a lower-league team at home’ but sometimes that’s a scalp isn’t it?

“I will be down there doing the game at Bromley for BT Sport. I will get a good insight and a good watch of it.

“Whoever it is, it is. When that game comes around we will have to pit our wits against whoever it is.”

Lowe is used to appearing as a TV analyst. He did so for Sky Sports in their coverage of the League Two play-offs last season.

Then, last month, he was the studio guest for the midweek EFL highlights round-up on Quest with host Colin Murray.
