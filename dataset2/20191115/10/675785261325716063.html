Aldi launch first ever Friends collection and it includes homeware and pyjamas

The new Friends range at supermarket Aldi is an absolute must for fans of the iconic 90s sitcom

15 Nov 2019, 10:02 by Melisha Kaur, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/melisha-kaur/

After launching 25 years ago in the 90s, American TV sitcom Friends is still popular as ever.

Now Aldi is treating devoted fans to its first ever Friends collection and it includes heaps of products inspired by the iconic show - just in time for Christmas .

The supermarket giant is set to launch Friends-themed pyjamas (£8.99) as well as homeware items from Thursday, November 21st.

Friends lovers will be able to create the cosy vibes of the show's famous Central Perk hangout with a range of cushions (£5.99) and coffee mugs.

There's even a range of duvet sets for your bedroom - including designs that feature iconic moments from the show.

With prices starting from just £4.99, the wonderfully-themed collection is a must for any Friends fan.

But items are expected to sell out quickly, so you ought to head over to Aldi as soon as they launch.

Meanwhile New Look is selling an entire Friends range that includes homware, clothing and stationary.

Our particular favourite is the Black Christmas Turkey Friends T-Shirt for £9.99.

ASOS also has some Friends-themed items that make great gifts, such as a Central Perk cappuccino mug (£11.99) and a calendar for 2020.
