eBay kicks off Black Friday 2019 deals with 20% off using this discount code

Online marketplace eBay has unveiled the first of its Black Friday 2019 discounts, with price cuts on top brands including PlayStation, Converse, Samsung and Apple - here are the best deals so far

15 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Chantelle Symester, https://www.facebook.com/csymester/

Today, eBay has unveiled its first wave of Black Friday deals for 2019.

The online retail giant is giving shoppers 20% off selected big name brands, including PlayStation and Apple with a special discount code.

The code - PRIZE20 - can be entered at checkout online at ebay.co.uk from 10am today (15 November) until 11:59pm on Thursday 21st November.

There is a minimum spend of £25 when using the promo discount, which can only be used once, and shoppers will only be able to get a maximum of £75 off their purchases.

So pick your purchases wisely if you want to bag a real bargain ahead of Black Friday on 29 November.

There is plenty of choice for those buying Christmas gifts for friends and family, below are just a few of the best deals we've spotted so far that you can pick up in the promotions right now.

You can check the full list of brands and sellers included in the deal by visiting ebay.co.uk. Shoppers can also check all the latest deals by regularly visiting eBay's official Black Friday deals page.

During Black Friday shoppers can still take advantage of the online retailer's Price Match Guarantee too. The policy means shoppers get the lowest priced deals on coveted items during the sale or eBay will issue a voucher for the difference in price.

What happened in 2018?

Last year eBay offered price cuts to shoppers ahead of Black Friday, giving bargain-hunters plenty of time to pick up massive savings on coveted items.

For 2018 the online marketplace discounted thousands of items including branded home appliances from Hoover and Dyson, smart home devices from Google and gaming console bundles from Nintendo, Playstation and Xbox - and all were snapped up fast by keen shoppers as they went live.

The chain also featured savings across LEGO toys, Converse and Superdry fashion clothing and footwear and even price cuts on power tools.

Speaking about the deals last year, Rob Hattrell, UK VP of eBay, said: ‘‘98.4 million Brits are expected to visit our marketplace over the discounting weekend. In 2018 we’ve seen retail go through unprecedented change and, while shopping habits evolve, people still ultimately want quality products at great prices."
