Naturi Naughton Responds To Getting Trolled By 50 Cent Again

15 Nov 2019, 10:26 by Yohance Kyles (@HUEYmixwitRILEY)

Some social media users are calling the G-Unit Film & Television boss a cyber-bully.

(AllHipHop News) 50 Cent may be off Instagram at the moment, but that has not stopped him from continuing his trolling on Twitter. The latest target is his own Power co-star/employee Naturi Naughton.

Fiddy posted another meme making fun of Naughton's hairstyle by comparing her to the character Goro from Mortal Kombat. He wrote as a caption, "😠Who the f*ck did this, POWER is #1." This came after 50 apologized to Naughton earlier this month for posting the picture on IG.

The former 3LW singer responded to the Power executive producer's latest jokes on Twitter and Instagram. She tweeted, "@50cent Did we have a fight and I not know about it? 🤷🏾‍♀️🤦🏾‍♀️ so confused. #SoMuchForPowerFam #FinalSeason." 

Naughton also wrote on Instagram, "😔There’s so much I wanna say but... You don’t even have an Instagram account so... 🤷🏾‍♀️ why waste my characters or come out of my CHARACTER! But do pick up a copy of my upcoming book: #whatNOTtoDoWhenYoureABoss 🤦🏾‍♀️ #ImNotACharacter #IAmARealPerson #IAmAWoman #IAmABlackWoman."
