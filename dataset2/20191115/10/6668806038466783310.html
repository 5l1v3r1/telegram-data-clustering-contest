Photos: Ranveer Singh & Deepika Padukone look divine as they visit Amritsar

15 Nov 2019, 10:31 by Filmfare

Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh went on a spiritual retreat on the occasion of their first wedding anniversary. First, the Bollywood couple took time out from work to visit Tirumala Tirupati Temple in Andhra Pradesh to seek the blessings of the almighty. While the couple was there yesterday, this morning they reached Amritsar to offer prayers at the Golden Temple. 

After making their fans jump with excitement on seeing their pictures from Tirupati, the couple’s pictures from Amritsar went viral online. Deepika was seen dressed in a wine coloured suit, while Ranveer opted for a pastel printed kurta and churidaar with a chic Nehru jacket to complete the look when they visited the Golden Temple.

Deepika took to Instagram to share a picture from their visit there. She wrote, "As we celebrate our first wedding anniversary,we seek blessings at Harmandir Sahib.Thank You all for your love,prayers and good wishes! @ranveersingh."
