At the on-going Lenovo Innovation Technology Conference, the company is showcasing a couple of new technology. Today is the second day and Lenovo’s CEO, Yang Yuanqing showed Lenovo’s ThinkPad X1 foldable laptop. Interestingly, we also have some live images of this 13.3-inch foldable and it looks amazing. As early as May of 2019, Lenovo officially displayed the Lenovo ThinkPad X1 variant with a folding screen. Today, the company officially unveils this device in China.

Lenovo ThinkPad X1 foldable laptop uses a 13.3-inch OLED display with a 2K resolution of 2K. This laptop is quite easy to store and carry after folding. In addition,  the ThinkPad X1 seamlessly connects to a stand for easy operation. Furthermore, it can also connect with a docking station for users who need a keyboard and mouse to operate. Finally, you can use this product as a large-screen “tablet”.

Lenovo ThinkPad X1 foldable laptop price and availability

As of now, there is no official information on the price and availability of this device.
