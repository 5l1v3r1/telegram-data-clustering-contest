Disha Patani indulges in binge-eating after shooting a song for Salman Khan starrer 'Radhe'; view picture

15 Nov 2019, 09:20 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Disha Patani has been quite busy with these days due to her professional commitments as she has been shooting for two films simultaneously. She has been juggling from the sets of Salman Khan starrer 'Radhe' and 'KTina' in which she'll be seen in a Punjabi avatar.

Taking to Instagram, the mesmerising diva gave her fans a glimpse of her delicious food as she indulged in binge-eating after shooting a song for 'Radhe'. Disha can be seen having a pizza and doughnut on the sets of the high octane action film and she captioned this picture as "Post song shoot be like #radhe"

Disha will be collaborating with Salman for the second time after an impressive performance in 'Bharat' and this news has already got the fans excited. She had even shared a glimpse of her sore knees recently on social media which were due to her hard work and practice to ace her dance moves.

'Radhe' which is being helmed by Prabhudeva is slated to release on Eid next year. Apart from that, Disha has her kitty full with interesting projects like Mohit Suri's 'Malang' and Ekta Kapoor's 'KTina'.
