Kids' brutally honest reviews of 2019's Top 12 Christmas toys - from Nerf guns to Lego

Barbie, Blume Dolls, Frozen dolls, L.O.L, Lego and PAW Patrol all feature in the Dream Toys 2019 top toys for Christmas - but what do the kids think?

15 Nov 2019, 09:38 by Zoe Forsey, https://www.facebook.com/zoeforseymirror/

December is creeping closer and closer which means it's almost time to sit the kids down to write their Christmas lists.

And while this brings the benefit of every parent's favourite "well, Father Christmas won't come if you're naughty" threat, it also means it's time to start thinking about what to get them.

Dream Toys has put together its annual list of the top toys for Christmas 2019, including breakthrough pieces and a number of old classics.

Blume Dolls, L.O.L, Nerf guns, PAW Patrol and LEGO have all made this year's list.

Juliet Ward, head of toys at Argos was part of the committee that put together the list. She told Mirror Online: "It's a really lovely mix of tech and traditional. Some of the tech stuff is really educational but it's great to have the imagination in some of the traditional things as well.

"I think there is a really good selection, I think there is real range in terms of price - there is something for everyone."

 

The panel of experts know exactly what they're talking about when it comes to toys, but they are grown-ups - so what do the kids think?

We spoke to a load of kids to find out what they think of the top 12...

Blume Dolls - £9.99

Trilyna Mah, six, said: "They've got different hair. Their hair is good and has flowers and diamonds.

"It's like magic because it comes up really quickly and it's fun.

"You get to play with all of them when they're out."

Gwen McDonald, eight, added: "It is really fun, I like it when it opens up."

Frozen 2 'Into the Unknown' Elsa Doll - £39.99

Trilyna said: "She's got a really nice dress with a diamond on it.

 

"I like Frozen because there are lots of characters and Olaf is funny.

"You could get all of the dolls and play with them."

Charlie Bonner-Davies, 10, added: "It is pretty tall. It's pretty well made."

L.O.L Surprise! 2-in-1 Glamper - £99.99

Liana Pierce, seven, "My favourite bit is the pool. I like the mirror because it's the shape of a love heart and it's got bits around it.

 

"I would play with it with my sister.

"It's nice because it's big enough for everyone to play with it."

Nerf Elite Fortnite SP-L - £19.99

Peter Olunloyo said: "It is really fun because there is one bit where you can aim to hit what you want to shoot at.

"The shoots can bounce off the walls."

 

Charlie added: "My favourite toy was the Nerf gun. I just like it because of how far it shoots. The reload quality is good. It hardly gets jammed, only if you really squish it in."

Owleez - £49.00

Gwen said: "It was really cool when it flew in the air.

Fiona added: "I really like it because you have to teach it to fly. I like that because it flies, like actually flies in the air."

 

PAW Patrol Mighy Pups Super PAWs Mighty Jet Command Centre - £59.99

Charlie said: "I think it's a decent toy. First of all, you can get a lot of stuff in it as there is a lot of space. And it has all these little things. The legs are pretty cool.

"It's something special."

 

Mofe Akinsamni, six, is also a fan, adding: "I think this is a good toy because of the front bit. I don't know what it does but I like the windows.

"I like how high it is."

Ryan's World Super Surprise Safe - £44.99

Gwen said: "I think it's fun to play with because it's basically a magic trick. I think it's really cool."

Fiona Mah, 10, added: "I do really like it because it's like a safe and you keep keep stuff secret.

"I like the key because you can take it out and out it back in. It's a big one too."

Barbie Dreamplane Playset - £79.99

Liana Pierce, seven, said: "I like it because of the inside. I like the chairs because they can move. I like the Barbie because she's got a nice long dress and it's very sparkly.

"I like it a lot."

LEGO Harry Potter Knight Bus - £34.99

Harry Keogh, seven, said: "So it's very shiny. My favourite thing about it is that it's smoother than a normal bus.

"I like Harry Potter and the bus because it has magic."

Peppa Pig Peppa's Stage Playset - £39.99

Fiona: "I like it because you can change the scene because there are little things.

"I watched Peppa Pig a lot when I was little but not much now."

But Trilyna wasn't as impressed.

 

She said: "I don't like Peppa Pig because it's not really my type. I don't like the show so I don't like the toy."

Rizmo - £59.99

Gwen: "I thought she was really cool. I don't really know why, I just thought she was really cool.

"I like the colour light on her tummy."

Fiona added: "I really like it because it moves and it's really cute and funny."

Toy Story 4 True Talkers - £17.99

Charlie: "I think it's pretty good that you can change its faces. It would be good for babies.

"For me I would say it's a bit young for me but it would be fine for my friend.

"It makes difference voices.
