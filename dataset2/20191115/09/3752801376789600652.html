Two women killed while crossing a highway in Sebokeng

15 Nov 2019, 09:01 by Riaan Grobler

Two women in their 40s were killed while crossing the Golden Highway while seemingly trying to get to the Golden Gardens suburb of Sebokeng on foot on Wednesday evening.

They were struck by a BMW SUV and died on impact.

According to ER24, paramedics arrived on the scene at 19:40 to find the vehicle stationary in the road and the bodies of the two women on the centre median of the highway.

They both showed no signs of life and were declared dead on arrival.

The driver was shaken but uninjured.
