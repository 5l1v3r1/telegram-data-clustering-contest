‘Sign of things to come’ Many LFC fans swarm Twitter after seeing Sancho & Trent linkup for England

15 Nov 2019, 09:05 by Jordan Chamberlain

Trent Alexander-Arnold is going to get 100 caps for England, we reckon.

How Kieran Trippier started ahead of him in the last set of internationals is truly baffling.

Liverpool’s right-back, still only 21-years-old, is the best on the planet in his position already – so perhaps it’s no real surprise that the also ludicrously talented Jadon Sancho combined so well with him last night as Gareth Southgate’s side pummelled Montenegro 7-0.

Borussia Dortmund’s prodigy started on England’s right-wing in the absence of suspended Raheem Sterling and did very well.

He’s been linked with a potential 2020 transfer on countless occasions – most notably by the Mail yesterday – who did claim Manchester United and Real Madrid are also in the running.

But plenty of Reds on Twitter were convinced Sancho could fit in at Liverpool easily, especially with Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain also playing on that side of the field and scoring a goal…
