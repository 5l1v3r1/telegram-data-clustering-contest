BROOKLYN NINE-NINE Officially Coming Back for Season 8!

15 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Jessica Fisher

We’ve got great news for all you Brooklyn Nine-Nine fans! The series is officially set to return for an eighth season on NBC! I’m so happy because this is one of mine and my husband’s favorite shows. It always cracks me up, and has the best writing and characters.

The series stars Andy Samberg, Joe Lo Truglio, Melissa Fumero, Terry Crews, Andre Braugher, Stephanie Beatriz, Dirk Blocker, Joel McKinnon Miller, and Chelsea Peretti. The network’s Co-President Scripted Programming Tracey Pakosta delivered the news to the cast during a table read recently, and you can watch their reaction to the new in the video below.

According to Deadline, Brooklyn 99 “is believed to be the most popular off-network live-action comedy on Hulu.” It was also last season’s #1 most-social primetime comedy series across television, earning 9 million total interactions.

Watch the upcoming seventh season on Thursday, February 6th, 2020.
