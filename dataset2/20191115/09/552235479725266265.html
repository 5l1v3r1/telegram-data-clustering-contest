Woman struck by vehicle on Whitletts Road

The incident happened at 2.55pm on Whitletts Road, Ayr, yesterday (Thursday, November 14) near the Tesco supermarket.

15 Nov 2019, 09:45 by Paul Behan, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/paul-behan/

The woman was taken to Ayr Hospital by ambulance. Her age and condition are not known.

Both stretches of Whitletts Road were closed for a time in order for an investigation to take place.

A police spokesperson said: "We received a report of a lady involved in a road traffic accident on Whitletts Road, Ayr, at 2.55pm.

"Police and ambulance attended and the lady was taken to Ayr Hospital. Inquiries are continuing."
