The Rose's Woosung serenades from city to country in MV for 'Beautiful Girl' feat. Peniel

15 Nov 2019, 09:06 by germainej

The Rose's Woosung has dropped his music video for "Beautiful Girl" featuring Peniel.

The MV follows Woosung from 2015 to the present as he serenades someone from city to country, and fans get to see everything from his cute to romantic side. "Beautiful Girl" is a romantic, acoustic track about a beautiful girl who grabs your attention.
