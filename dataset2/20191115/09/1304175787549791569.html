Paradies Lagardere delivers MOD Pizza to Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport

15 Nov 2019, 09:15 by Stavros Andriopoulos

Paradies Lagardere, and the Wayne County Airport Authority (WCAA), operator of Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport, announced that MOD Pizza is open and ready to serve hungry travelers in the North Terminal. The Paradies Lagardere Dining Division, a true restaurateur, is passionate about connecting with travelers through inspiring design, delicious, top-notch culinary offerings, fun brands such as MOD Pizza, exceptional hospitality, and first-class customer service.
