Iliza Shlesinger gets new Netflix series, announces tour (Beacon Theatre included)

15 Nov 2019, 09:43 by Bill Pearis

Comedian Iliza Shlesinger will release Unveiled, her fifth stand-up special for Netflix, on November 19. In it, the Elder Millennial tells the “terrifying truth” about getting married. You can watch the trailer below. Get ready to see even more of her on the streaming service, as she’s getting her own sketch series, titled The Iliza Shlesinger Sketch Show, which is currently filming in New York and will be out next year.

Meanwhile, Iliza will also be on tour next year, playing shows around the world, including a stop at NYC’s Beacon Theatre on March 27. Tickets for that show go on sale today at 10 AM. All tour dates are listed below.
