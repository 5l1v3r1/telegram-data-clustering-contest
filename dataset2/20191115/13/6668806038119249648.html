Lata Mangeshkar’s family shares an update on the singer's health

15 Nov 2019, 13:43 by Filmfare

Since the past five days, there’s been a lot of speculation surrounding veteran Lata Mangeshkar’s health. The legendary singer was admitted to Breach Candy hospital on November 11 and was monitored by a team of doctors. Reports also began doing rounds that she’s being treated for pneumonia and will remain in the hospital for a few days as a precautionary measure.

However, fans of the singer can finally breathe a sigh of relief as the family of Lata Mangeshkar has confirmed that she’s on her way to recovery. The family issued a statement that reads, “Dear Friends. We are as happy as you are to inform you that with all your prayers and best wishes, Lata didi is doing much better. Thank you for being there. God is great."

Lata Mangeshkar, popularly known as India’s nightingale, has lent her voice to over 1000 songs in her illustrious career. The much-loved singerwas honoured with Padma Bhushan, the Padma Vibhushan and the Bharat Ratna.
