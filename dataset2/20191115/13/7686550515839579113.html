Fancy a flutter on the impending General Election? Here's what you need to know

The Pools have put together some odds to help decide who to put your money on.

15 Nov 2019, 13:46 by Danny Thompson

With yet another General Election around the corner, Britain's political parties are now fully on the campaign trail.

So far we've heard calls to save the NHS, how best to deal with Brexit, the potential for political alliances, and Prime Minister Boris Johnson making a meal of singing Wheels on the Bus while surrounded by parents and toddlers.

The polls will open on Thursday December 12 around the country as the country gets ready to decide who will represent them in their respective constituencies.

Former prime minister Theresa May called a snap election back in 2017 in an attempt to increase her majority, but the attempt failed and she lost 13 seats and her majority meaning a deal was needed with the DUP.
