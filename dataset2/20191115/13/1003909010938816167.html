Calgary's Jocelyn Alice finds strength in loss on defiant debut album, How Dare You

15 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Eric Volmers

Jocelyn Alice was not being subtle when she chose the name.

In the news release announcing her vibrant, 11-song debut album, How Dare You, Alice makes cryptic reference to the title being inspired by a conversation with a friend: “She just knows me. She knows you can’t tell me what to do,” Alice explains.

In broader strokes, the fierce moniker suggests frustration and defiance, both of which the Calgary-born singer confirms have been drivers in her career for the past five years.
