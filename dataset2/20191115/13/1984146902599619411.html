Newcastle hosts Christmas dog cafe parties for Frenchies, dachsunds and pugs

It's pawty time for you and your best friend at Revolucion de Cuba, with pupuccinos and pawsecco for them and cocktails for you

15 Nov 2019, 13:24 by George Whitehouse

Revolucion de Cuba will be hosting a Christmas paw-ty where customers can bring along their pups and enjoy the cafe surrounded by dogs.

The Cloth Market bar is holding a day of Christmas parties in Newcastle dedicated to Frenchies, dachsunds and pugs on Sunday, December 1. Dog owners can bring their pets along with them, and are encouraged to dress their pets in Christmas outfits. Non dog owners are welcome.

For humans, there will be cocktails, Cuban food, cake and coffee, while the four-legged guests will be able to enjoy treats too, including 'pupuccinos', 'pawsecco' mince pies and 'pupcakes'. Previous pop-up cafes for pugs and dachsunds have been very popular but this will be the first event for French bulldogs. The parties are split into time slots for the different breeds.

10am - Frenchie Cafe
11.15am - Dachsund Cafe
12.30pm - Frenchie Cafe
1.45pm - Pug Cafe
3pm - Dachsund Cafe
4.15pm - Pug Cafe

Tickets are available now through www.pugcafe.com

Ticket prices

French Bulldog, Dachsund, Pug owners & their guests £7

Dog Lovers (not bringing a dog in their booking)  £10

Children 16 or under in a dog owner booking - £5

Children 16 or under in a dog lover booking - £7

All dogs FREE
