Movistar: Atletico Madrid offer 40 million for Ivan Rakitic

The Croatian midfielder isn't getting much play at Barca and will move along

15 Nov 2019, 13:03 by Sport EN

Atletico Madrid will push for Ivan Rakitic. Per Movistar, the Rojiblancos are going to lodge a 40 million euro offer for Barcelona's Croatian central midfielder. He is not getting much game time at Camp Nou this season and will likely leave in the winter transfer window.

Rakitic is a player that Atletico coach Diego Simeone likes. Atletico will have to fight off Inter Milan, Juventus and AC Milan for Rakitic's services, with the Serie A sides interested in the midfielder too.
