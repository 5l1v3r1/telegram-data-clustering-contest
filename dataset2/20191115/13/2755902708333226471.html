WATCH: Selfless Mbare Woman, Gogo Gwena Delivers Over 100 Babies In 1 Week As Nurses Go On Strike

15 Nov 2019, 13:34 by Timothy Ndoro

Mbare Woman Delivers 100 Babies

Gogo Esther Gwena from Mbare has delivered well over 100 babies in the last week due to the ongoing nurses’ strike (Image: ZBC)
A selfless Mbare woman has come to the rescue of stranded expecting mothers, as she has delivered well over 100 babies in the last week.

The woman, many of whom are struggling and cannot afford the services of private doctors have turned to Gogo Esther Gwena as nurses at the council clinics are on strike over poor pay.

ZBC reports that Gogo Esther Gwena is a midwife with St Johns Apostolic Church of the whole world and stays only a few meters from the council-run Edith Opperman Clinic.
