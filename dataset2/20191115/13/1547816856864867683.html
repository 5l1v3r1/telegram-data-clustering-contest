Paras Chhabra's Girlfriend Akanksha Puri Stands Up For Her Alleged Ex Sidharth Shukla: 'Rashami Desai, Mahira Sharma, Devoleena Bhattacharjee Won't Succeed In Unsettling Sidharth'- EXCLUSIVE

15 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Vickey Lalwani

Akanksha Puri is not in favour of the women in Bigg Boss 13 house who have been attacking Sidharth Shukla verbally. She stands up for her rumoured ex and gives it back to Rashami Desai, Mahira Sharma and Devoleena Bhattacharjee

Akanksha Puri gave us an EXCLUSIVE INTERVIEW where she was extremely candid to say that her boyfriend Paras Chhabra has issues with Sidharth Shukla because she was linked-up with Sidharth long back. She spoke to us when Paras made the nasty comment to Sidharth, 'You don't 'under’ stand, isiliye toh tumhari wali mere paas hai'.  

The time is right to have Akanksha's POV on what's happening with Sidharth inside the house. The other day, Devoleena lashed out at him with choicest expletives (that needed to be beeped), and prior to that, Mahira Sharma called him a 'buddha' and his ex-girlfriend Rashami Desai called him 'naamard'.

Abu Malik, I think, was right when he said to us yesterday that one of the ways to make a man lose his confidence is to attack his manhood.

A few minutes back, we called up Akanksha, who like always, was frank and forthcoming. Said Akanksha, "These girls are trying various stunts to unsettle Sidharth. Now, let me tell you that they won't succeed. Sidharth is a very balanced guy who is very respectful towards women."
