THE MANDALORIAN - Video Points Out 61 Easter Eggs and References

15 Nov 2019, 13:30 by Joey Paur

By now, I’m sure most of you have seen The Mandalorian. So, with Episode 2 premiering today, I thought now would be a safe time to share this video from Screen Crush that points out 61 Easter eggs and references from the first episode.

As you’re aware, the episode was loaded with all kinds of stuff. Some of these are harder to spot than others and you may have missed some of them, but this helpful video is here to point them all out to you and explains what they mean and how some might be more significant in upcoming episodes of the series.

Of course, if you haven’t watched the show yet then you might want to skip this.
