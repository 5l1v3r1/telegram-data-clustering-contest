Vodafone Black Friday Offer Gives Customers a Free Google Nest Hub Max

15 Nov 2019, 13:40 by Shabana Arif

Vodafone is giving away a free Google Nest Hub Max worth £219 to new and existing broadband customers this Black Friday.

Which translates to right now, weeks before the fact, because that's how Black Friday sales work nowadays. Just select an option from one of the service provider's Vodafone Together plans before December 2, and you'll be eligible for your freebie.

There are two home broadband plans giving you guaranteed download speeds of 25Mbps (Superfast 1) or 55Mbps (Superfast 2), with prices starting from £47 per month for a combined 18 month SIM-Only and home broadband plan.

You'll have to wait until December 15 to actually claim your Nest Hub Max when the specific web page goes live and you've passed the 14 day mark. Then it's all yours.
