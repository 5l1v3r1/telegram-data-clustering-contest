Scots bridal shop selling wedding dresses for just £50 in huge discount sale

Hundreds of brides-to-be were delighted when they found out that Kudos Bridal is selling off stock at quite incredible discounts.

15 Nov 2019, 13:02 by Robert Fairnie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/robert-fairnie/

Wedding dresses worth over £1000 are on sale at a Dunfermline bridal shop for just £50.

Hundreds of brides-to-be were delighted when they found out that Kudos Bridal is selling off stock at quite incredible discounts.

Those at the store posted on Facebook on Thursday: "If you're looking for a bargain at the start of our Winter Extravaganza today then you'd best be quick!

"We have some gorgous ex-sample bridal gowns starting at just £50.

"ALL ex-sample occasion-wear is £50, too!"

The post is accompanied by a photo showing a sale sticker  which reveals a dress originally costing £1245 discounted to £50.

The Kudos Bridal Dunfermline website also reveals that wedding dresses worth £1600 are reduced to just £350, while others have had discounts of more than 50 per cent slapped on them, reports Edinburgh Live .

The store, which opened in 2007, is owned by Jackie Kelso and Michelle Scott. They can be found at Century House, 45 East Port, Dunfermline.

They also have a store on Edinburgh's Morningside Road.
