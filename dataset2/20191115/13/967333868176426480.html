‘Eggspensive’ Indian Five Star Hotels Amuse Bollywood Stars with Food Prices, Twitter Hysterical

New Delhi (Sputnik): India’s extravagant and luxurious five star hotels have begun to amuse loaded Bollywood biggies with their unreal prices for simple orders like eggs and fruits.

15 Nov 2019, 13:50

On Friday, country’s top singer and composer Shekhar Ravjiani took to Twitter and shared a picture of an “eggxorbiant” bill charging him $24 approx. (Rs. 1,672) for three egg whites at a fancy hotel.

An entire tray of 30 eggs usually costs around $3 (about Rs.200) in local Indian shops.
