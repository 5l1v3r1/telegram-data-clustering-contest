Epistar, Lextar report 3Q19 operating losses

15 Nov 2019, 06:43 by Siu Han, Taipei; Adam Hwang, DIGITIMES

LED epitaxial wafer and chip maker Epistar and vertically-integrated LED firm Lextar Electronics have reported net operating losses of NT$874.6 million (US$28.7 million) and NT$145.8 million respectively for third-quarter 2019.

Despite consecutive net operating losses since fourth-quarter 2018, Epistar said it managed to reduce inventory value from NT$4.4 billion at the end of second-quarter 2019 to NT$3.7 billion at the end of third-quarter 2019.

Epistar posted consolidated revenues of NT$1.367 billion for October, increasing 1.67% sequentially but decreasing 19.07% on year, and those of NT$13.321 billion for January-October slipped 25.08% on year.

Lextar's consolidated revenues of NT$710.2 million for October dropped 13.69% sequentially and 19.30% on year and those of NT$7.684 billion for January-October declined 19.26% on year.
