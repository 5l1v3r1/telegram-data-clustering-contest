Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan' release preponed; actor shares his first look

15 Nov 2019, 06:06 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

There seems to be no halt for National Award winner Ayushmann Khurrana as the actor is giving back to back blockbusters at the silver screen since the last few years. With 'Bala' becoming his career-best opener in the domestic circuit, Ayushmann took to social media to share the first look of his character from 'Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan'.

Not only that but the film is a sequel of 2017 blockbuster 'Shubh Mangal Saavdhan' which dealt with the topic of erectile dysfunction, this sequel will revolve around the love story of a same-sex couple.
