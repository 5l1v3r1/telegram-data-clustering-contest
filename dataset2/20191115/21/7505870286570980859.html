Forget Black Friday deals, Anker’s rival to the $50 Echo Dot is only $19.99

15 Nov 2019, 21:37 by Maren Estrada

Amazon’s Echo Dot normally costs $50, but it does goes on sale every now and then so you can get it for as little as $30 or even around $25 if you wait for Black Friday. What you might not realize, however, is that Anker makes a little Alexa speaker that’s even cheaper without having to go on sale. It’s called the Eufy Genie Wi-Fi Smart Speaker with Amazon Alexa, and it costs just $19.99 every day of the week. The audio quality isn’t quite as good as the Echo Dot so it’s not the best for streaming music. But if your main concern is having hands-free access to Alexa and all of its wonders, the Genie is perfect!
