PH 3rd best in Asian baseball

15 Nov 2019, 21:00 by Peter Atencio

The Philippines repulsed China, 11-1, Thursday night in the Super Round to settle for third place in the 2019 BFA Women’s Baseball Asian Cup at the Panda Stadium in China. 

The game was cut short at the end of the fifth inning because of the 10-run rule, allowing the umpire to call an end to the game once one team is ahead by 10 runs.

“Excellent work by everyone. Today, the Philippine women's team is third best in Asia,” said Philippine Amateur Baseball Association president Chito Loyzaga.

Earlier, Pitcher Nicole Estante struck out five as the Philippines came away with a 6-2 shocker over China on Wednesday night.

Then, they lost their in bid to reach the finals following a 2-18 setback at the hands of Japan.
