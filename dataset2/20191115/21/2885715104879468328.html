Lata Mangeshkar recovering

The 90-year-old singer was admitted to Breach Candy Hospital on Monday for treatment of pneumonia and lung infection.

15 Nov 2019, 21:18 by Express News Service

Four days after she was hospitalised for breathlessness and lung infection, veteran singer Lata Mangeshkar’s condition is reported to be recovering.

The 90-year-old singer was admitted to Breach Candy Hospital on Monday for treatment of pneumonia and lung infection.

A tweet from her official handle stated on Friday: “Lata didi is stable..and recovering… We thank each one of you, for your concern, care and prayers.”

Mangeshkar was initially put on ventilator support in the intensive care unit. Her condition remained critical for two days before she started recovering.
