Every movie and show coming to Hulu in December

Follow Polygon online:

15 Nov 2019, 21:20 by Petrana Radulovic

With so many streaming services out there and so little time, it’s hard to keep track of what’s currently on your subscription plan and what’s heading out. This is even more true when it comes to Hulu and the service’s many premium channel add-ons.

Now that Hulu is an easy add-on to Disney Plus (well, kinda), you may be wondering what to pair with your rewatch of High School Musical.

This December, the Kill Bill movies come to Hulu, along with some of the Fast and Furious franchise. Into the Dark gets two new episodes — one themed for the New Year — while Killing Eve and Younger’s latest seasons also arrive. How to Train Your Dragon’s holiday special, or rather its Snoggletog special, picks up after the events of the final film.

As is the circle of life, when things come to a streaming platform, others will leave. Titles departing from Hulu this month include Interview with the Vampire, Heathers, and Magic Mike.

Here’s everything you can look forward to this month and everything to watch one last time.
