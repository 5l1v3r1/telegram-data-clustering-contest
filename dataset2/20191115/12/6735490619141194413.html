Reese Witherspoon Reveals The Surprising Reason She Turned Down An Offer To Return To ‘Friends’

15 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Brent Furdyk

As Jennifer Aniston and Reese Witherspoon continue their promotional tour for their new Apple TV+ series “The Morning Show”, they sat down for an interview with AP Entertainment, with their former roles as sisters on “Friends” inevitably coming up.

Witherspoon praised Aniston’s ability to perform live in front of a studio audience.

“She’s so good in front of a live audience!” Witherspoon gushed, pointing at Aniston.

“You are too,” Aniston told her co-star. “You forget it because you blacked out… of fear.”

Witherspoon, however, remembered things differently.

“I’ve never done it again,” Witherspoon declared, adding a surprising revelation. “Did you know they asked me back and I said I can’t do it,” she said of being approached by producers to reprise her role as the little sister of Aniston’s Rachel.

The reason: “I was too scared!”

“You got scared?” Aniston replied in disbelief.

“They asked me to come back, and I was like, ‘I’m too nervous,'” Witherspoon admitted.
