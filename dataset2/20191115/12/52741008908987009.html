'Tis the season for Santa Claus Parades

A number are planned in Waterloo Region and surrounding communities

15 Nov 2019, 12:00 by Erin Anderson

While December 24 is Santa's 'big night', the jolly man in red will be busy over the next few weeks.

Annual Santa Claus Parades are happening in Waterloo Region and our surrounding communities.

The fun kicks off on November 16, with parades in K-W and Cambridge.
