Borderlands 3 hotfix tackles a couple of progression blockers

15 Nov 2019, 12:04 by Sherif Saed

There’s a new Borderlands 3 patch that aims to take care of a few player-reported issues.

Borderlands 3‘s latest hotfix is now available on all platforms. This update is entirely focused of addressing bugs that could block player progress, as well taking care of a few other miscellaneous issues.

Hotfixes are live updates the game automatically downloads in the main menu, so be sure to sit on that screen for a bit the next time you launch Borderlands 3 to get the update.
