New Blender Cloud workshop: Anglerfish [$]

15 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Bart Veldhuizen

Andy has posted a new workshop on his 'Anglerfish' artwork on the Blender Cloud.

This image was inspired by the amazing deep sea creature. Follow the step by step process from the first initial sculpting doodles to the final Eevee-rendered image. The workshop includes approximately 7 hours of commented time-lapse videos, the final renders and all the source files are included as well.
