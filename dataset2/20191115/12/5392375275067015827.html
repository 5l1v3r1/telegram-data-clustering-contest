OnePlus 7T series updated with fixes and improvements
5 (100%) 1 vote

OnePlus is back to update OnePlus 7T and 7T Pro, the two most recent smartphones from the Chinese manufacturer. Both software updates are part of the stable channel (OxygenOS 10.0.6 for OnePlus 7T and OxygenOS 10.0.4 for the Pro variant) and will be distributed on all devices over the next few days.

The features introduced, different for the two smartphones, include several general optimizations. The update for the 7T Pro also includes the October 2019 patches – they also arrived on the 7T with build 10.0.5.

OxygenOS 10.0.6 for the 7T Changelog

  • Optimized standby power consumption
  • Improved system stability and general bug fixes
  • Optimized the Bluetooth connectivity in automobiles
  • Optimized the expanded screenshot feature

OxygenOS 10.0.4 for 7T Pro Changelog

  • Optimized standby power consumption
  • Improved system stability and general bug fixes
  • Updated Android security patch to 2019.10
  • Fixed the black bar issue while charging or playing a video
  • Optimized the Bluetooth connectivity in automobiles
  • Optimized the expanded screenshot feature
