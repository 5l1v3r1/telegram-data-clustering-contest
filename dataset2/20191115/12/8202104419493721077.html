# THEATRE

The Incredible Adventures of Mary Jane Mosquito: Nov. 15-16 at Broadway Theatre. A Carousel Players production. Mary Jane is a mosquito who has no wings and doesn’t know how she will fit in without them. Told through song, story and puppetry. Suitable for ages four to 12. Tickets at 306-384-7727, persephonetheatre.org.

Saskatoon Soaps Improv Comedy: Nov. 15, 9:30 p.m., at Broadway Theatre. The improv comedy troupe brings laughter to the stage. Tickets at 306-652-6556 or at the door.

Moscow Ballet’s Great Russian Nutcracker: Nov. 17, 3 p.m., at TCU Place. The classic performance is set to Tchaikovsky’s score. Tickets at 306-975-7799, tcutickets.ca.

Playhouse Creatures: Nov. 20-30 at Greystone Theatre in the U of S John Mitchell Building. Set in the time of Charles II, four famous Restoration actresses, including Nell Gwynn, navigate their way through their precarious careers with wit, charm and spunk. Tickets at artsandscience.usask.ca/drama/greystone.

Girl in the Box: Runs to Nov. 24 at The Refinery, 609 Dufferin Ave. Presented by Live Five and produced by Scantily Glad Theatre. In 1968, grad student Ruth Eisemann-Schier made history as the first woman to end up on the FBI’s Most Wanted List. Tickets at 306-653-5191, ontheboards.ca.
