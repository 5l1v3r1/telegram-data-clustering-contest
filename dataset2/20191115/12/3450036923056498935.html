Revived: Motorola brings back mid-2000s phone favorite, the Razr

15 Nov 2019, 12:19 by Marah Brock, WTGS Staff

SAVANNAH, Ga. (WTGS) – Motorola announced the newest iteration of the beloved mid-2000s phone- the Razr.

No, it is not 2005. This new version takes the approached similarly to that of Microsofts Surface laptop. The Razr has a full "main" screen, but flips into a smaller version of itself, including a smaller outside screen.

According to the Motorola Razr website, "the touch-enabled Quick View display allows you to see, respond, and move on. Play your music, take selfies, see notifications and more—all without ever opening your phone."
