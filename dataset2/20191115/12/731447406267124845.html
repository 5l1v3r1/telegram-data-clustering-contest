Annie Mac's Lost & Found festival reveals first names for 2020

Helena Hauff, CC:DISCO!, Jayda G and many more are heading to Malta in April.

15 Nov 2019, 12:03 by Carlos Hawthorn

The first acts are in for Annie Mac's Lost & Found festival in 2020.

The sixth edition of the Malta event will take place from April 30th through May 3rd across various venues. DJ highlights include Seth Troxler, Jayda G, Helena Hauff, Special Request, Eris Drew back-to-back Octo Octa, Honey Dijon, Saoirse, Denis Sulta and Moxie.

A handful of UK rappers have also been tapped, with slots for slowthai, Aitch and UK drill kingpin Headie One. More names are still TBA.
