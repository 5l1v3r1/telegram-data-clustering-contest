African Network of Southwest Florida holding annual gala Saturday night

15 Nov 2019, 12:16 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- You are invited to the African Network of Southwest Florida's gala Saturday tonight.

The annual gala is at the Crowne Plaza in Fort Myers. There will be some entertainment celebrating cultures from various African countries.

The doors open at 5 p.m. and the program runs through 9:30 p.m.
