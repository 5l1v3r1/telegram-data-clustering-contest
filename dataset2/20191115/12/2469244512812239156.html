Photo shows incredible fossil discovered in Exeter

Richard Dalton, MD at UK Remediation, ended giving the wonderful discovery to his 11-year-old son

15 Nov 2019, 12:23 by Howard Lloyd

This photo shows the remarkable fossil found at a soil treatment facility in Exeter.

Richard Dalton, managing director at UK Remediation Ltd, made the remarkable find at the Pinbrook Road Recycling Centre.

The company specialises in checking soil for contamination and removing it from certain areas.

Richard said they were used to finding interesting items - including rings, old coins and even toy cars.

But he admitted this fossil in particular stood out - so much so that he gave it to his 11-year-old son, Eban.

"Impressive fossil found at our Exeter Soil Treatment Facility #fossilfriday #geology #soil #remediation #exeter," read their tweet revealing the discvoery.
