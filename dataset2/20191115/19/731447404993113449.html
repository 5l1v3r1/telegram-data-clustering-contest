Renaissance heads to Tulum, Mexico for party with Roman Flügel, Robag Wruhme, Charlotte de Witte

The day-into-night event will go down at Zamna Tulum next January.

15 Nov 2019, 19:09 by Andrew Ryce

Long-running UK promoter Renaissance is doing its first party in Tulum, Mexico on January 4th, 2020.

The two-room event at Zamna Tulum will run from 6 PM till late morning the next day, and features Roman Flügel, Robag Wruhme, Dubfire, Charlotte de Witte and Fort Romeau, plus back-to-back sets from Adam Beyer & Ida Engberg and Danny Tenaglia & Nicole Moudaber.

Jan 04 Renaissance Tulum, Zamna Tulum, South

Promoter: Renaissance
