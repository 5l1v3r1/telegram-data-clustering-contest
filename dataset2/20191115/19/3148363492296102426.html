Hillary Clinton Implies Trump's Yovanovitch Tweet Is ‘Witness Intimidation’

Hillary Clinton tweeted Friday about “witness intimidation” after President Donald Trump’s Twitter post about former U.S. Ambassador to Ukraine Marie Yovanovitch was read aloud during a House impeachment hearing.

15 Nov 2019, 19:10 by Penny Starr
