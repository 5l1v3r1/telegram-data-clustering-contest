Manchester City’s appeal to CAS over FFP rejected

15 Nov 2019, 19:56 by The Eagle Online

Manchester City have lost their attempt to block a UEFA investigation into financial fair play violations.

City had asked the Court of Arbitration for Sport [CAS] to throw out the financial fair play case that had been brought against the club by UEFA.
