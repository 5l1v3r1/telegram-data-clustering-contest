iKON becomes the 4th K-pop group to reach 4 million likes on YouTube with "Love Scenario" MV

15 Nov 2019, 19:17 by KonVIP

iKON achieved another milestone for their history. Their mega-hit song" Love Scenario"s MV has surpassed 4 million likes on YouTube on November 14, it's their first MV to achieve this.

iKON became the 4th k-pop group to achieve this success after BTS, BLACKPINK, and EXO.  EXO also got their first 4 million likes for the first time with "Love Shot" on November, 2.

"Love Scenario" was released on January 1, 2018, as the title track for their 2nd full album, "Return".

The song was no.1 in all 2018's domestic year-end charts and got 4 "Song Of The Year" awards included 2 daesangs. Love Scenario's MV was the most-watched music video in Korea in 2018.
