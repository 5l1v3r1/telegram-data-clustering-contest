Katie Cassidy's Crisis on Infinite Earths Character Isn't Who You Expect

15 Nov 2019, 19:11 by L.D. Nolan

Earth-1 Laurel Lance (Katie Cassidy) will be making her return in the upcoming "Crisis on Infinite Earths" crossover.

Asked on Twitter for any teases about Laurel Lance in the "Crisis on Infinite Earths" crossover or the Green Arrow and the Canaries spin-off, Arrow creator Marc Guggenheim replied, "Earth-1 Laurel in Crisis, Earth-2 Laurel in spin-off."
