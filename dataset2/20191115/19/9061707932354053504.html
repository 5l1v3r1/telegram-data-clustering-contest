The transfer bargains Derby County could look at in January

Latest Derby County transfer news | Would any of these players be of interest to Derby County as they look to plot a course to the Premier League?

15 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Josh Challies, Steve Wollaston, Mark Wakefield

The 2020 January transfer window opens in less than two months, and clubs across Europe will be looking at what business they can go.

Derby County are fighting to secure a good finish in the Sky Bet Championship, with a place in the play-offs still very much a possibility should the Rams have a good run of results.

After narrowly missing out on promotion to the Premier League last season, Phillip Cocu and his players will be hoping that this season can provide the club with success.

The growing prices in the transfer market are a cause for concern for Championship clubs, particularly with the financial fair play restrictions, but fortunately there are a number of bargains to be had.

Next summer, a wealth of Championship players see their contracts at their current clubs come to a close - and they may, therefore, be available for a cut-price fee when the window opens in a few months time.

Here, you can find every player in the Championship who sees their deal expire in 2020, according to TransferMarkt.

Would any of these players be of interest to the manager?
