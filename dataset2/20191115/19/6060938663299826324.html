Photos: Tara Sutaraia and Aadar Jain get snapped as they attend 'Marjaavaan' special screening

15 Nov 2019, 19:32 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Tara Sutaria had been busy with the promotions of her film 'Marjaavaan' co-starring Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh. And, the film has hit the theatres and received a mixed response.

Apart from her film, Tara Sutaria is rocking the headlines these day for her rumoured relationship with Aadar Jain. Paparazzi have snapped the couple together on various outings. The duo has never talked about it in open, however, their appearances together and social media PDA reveals everything!

And on Friday evening, Aadar and Tara were snapped as they attended 'Marjaavaan' screening. The actress looked absolutely stunning in a peach-coloured saree. She was in a happy mood as he posed for the pictures. While Aadar was in his casuals. He wore a white t-shirt and blue denim paired with brown shoes.
