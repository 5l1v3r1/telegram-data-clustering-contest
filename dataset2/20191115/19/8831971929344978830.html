12 Beautiful Decorations For A Holiday-Ready Home

15 Nov 2019, 19:02 by Sofiya Golovnya

It’s the middle of November, so feel free to start looking for that box of Christmas decorations you have lying around. If what you already have doesn’t look that good anymore, don’t worry. Here’s a roundup of twelve amazing ornaments to make your home ready for the festive season.

Pre Lit Christmas Garland

This handmade Christmas garland with pinecones and berries will add a holiday touch to your home. Hang it down the railing of the stairway, above the fireplace or even above the kitchen cabinets; it would look fantastic anywhere.

Flameless Candles

These flameless candles are so realistic they practically indistinguishable from regular candles. Flame simulation technology makes the candles flicker, making them very realistic. No fire hazards are a great feature when the house is full of other decorations.

Metal Star Wreath

Perfect for a lover of minimalism, this star-shaped metal wreath lets you add a celestial touch to your home. Crafted from thin iron wire, it looks great hung on its own or decorated with faux flowers, a string of lights, or foraged botanicals.

Balloon Garland Kit

A peppermint balloon garland will help make your party feel extra special. You can assemble this decoration yourself to customize it, as everything that you will need to create it is provided in the kit. This decoration is a must for anyone who loves holiday-themed events.

Knitted Tree Skirt

If you are serious about your Christmas tree, a knitted skirt with a luxury textured finish is something you need right now! A traditional heavy knit and wine red color will add a rustic feel, and your tree will look fantastic in photos.

Paper Snowflake String Light

Every day is a snow day if you hang these paper snowflake string lights in your house. Powered by batteries, this ornament can be hung anywhere you want an extra wintery glow. Put these on the walls, and you’ll have yourself a little indoor winter wonderland.

Cable Knit Stockings

Stockings are always a great way to bring the holiday cheer into the home, and these personalized ones are truly special. As they are often customizable, you can select the stocking color, font style and font color to make them unique.

Christmas Gnomes

Each of these gnomes is individually made, as they are originally designed by Swedish artist and handcrafted unique one by one. These can act as both decorations and dolls, which are an adorable tradition perfect for spreading the holiday spirit.

Pompom Christmas Ornaments

If you are not a fan of traditional Christmas tree decorations, these fluffy pompom balls are just what you need. Chunky multicolor ornaments are dyed and made by hand, and each one features a thread loop for easy hanging.

Snowflake Door Hanger

This snowflake is perfect for hanging either inside or outside for the holidays! It is made from Baltic birchwood and is unfinished to look rustic. You can also stain or paint it to personalize it. Grabbing a few of these is also a great idea, as you can put them around your house.

Rattan Ball String Lights

Regular string lights are everywhere. If you’re looking for something special, these wonderful rattan ball string lights are a great holiday decoration. You could wrap them around something or hang them somewhere to light up this winter.

Vintage-Inspired Glass Food Ornaments

All the foodies get ready; you are going to love an ornament set, inspired by some of the most iconic meals and snacks. Pizza, donuts, ramen and chips – these are only a few of the available designs. Your friends will ask where you got those from for sure!
