ICYMI: Kidnapped Appeal court judge regains freedom in Edo

15 Nov 2019, 19:59 by Oluwakemi Abimbola

Abducted Court of Appeal judge, Justice Chioma Nwosu-Iheme, has regained her freedom.

Nwosu-Iheme of the Benin Division of the Court of Appeal was kidnapped two weeks ago in Benin, the Edo State Capital.

The Commissioner of Police in the State, Danmallam Mohammed confirmed that the judge has been rescued from her abductors and has been reunited with her family, reports Channels Television.
