Police arrest suspect in killing of student in Kaduna

15 Nov 2019, 19:12 by The Eagle Online

Kaduna State Police Command has confirmed the arrest of a suspect in the killing of a student, Hassan Abdullah of Government Secondary Rigachikun, in Igabi Local Government Area, Kaduna State.

The incident occurred in the school premises on Thursday at about 11 am when the suspect, Sani Umar, who is not a student of the school, accosted Abdullahi and allegedly stabbed him with a knife.

The commandâ€™s Public Relations Officer, DSP Yakubu Sabo, told the News Agency of Nigeria on Friday in Kaduna that the victim died on the way to hospital.

Sabo said Umar had been arrested and is undergoing investigation.

According to him, the matter was transferred to the Criminal Investigation Department to unravel the circumstances that led to the incident.

The police spokesman assured that the suspect would be prosecuted after the investigation.
