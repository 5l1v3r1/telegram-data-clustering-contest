Company of Heroes 2 is free this weekend on Steam, and you get to keep it

15 Nov 2019, 19:18 by Stephany Nunneley

Company of Heroes 2 is free to download and keep this weekend on Steam.

If you’ve yet to play Company of Heroes 2, just head over to Steam and give it a download for free.

Once you do, it’s yours to keep as long as you grab it by Sunday, November 17.

During this time, Relic Entertainment is putting the entire Company of Heroes franchise and DLC on sale for 75% off until November 24.

For those who miss out on downloading CoH2 for free, it will be 75% off November 18-24.
