NAMCYA finals at CCP

15 Nov 2019, 19:40 by Manila Standard Lifestyle

It’s the last stretch of this year’s National Music Competitions for Young Artists, as the four-day finals round begins on Nov. 26. 

The National Finals Competition is happening at the Cultural Center of the Philippines. Semi-finals round started on August 30 in Metro Manila and continued in Visayas and Mindanao regions in September. 

Three concerts are slated after the finals. 

Concert for Solo Winners will be held at CCP on the Nov. 30, simultaneous to the Traditional Music and Rondalla Outreach Concert at Rizal Park Open Air Auditorium in Labs Kita Sabado program. Meanwhile, the Concert of Winners is scheduled on Dec. 1. 

The competition and concerts are open to the public and all free of charge. Tickets are available at the CCP lobby.

Contact NAMCYA Secretariat at (02) 8836-4928 for more information.
