Tiny alligator startles officers in Cape Coral

15 Nov 2019, 08:46 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Cape Coral Police deal with criminals, car accidents, and wildlife. But one gator call didn't go exactly as planned recently.

They posted a video to social media Friday highlighting a lighter incident involving a small, but feisty alligator.

Officers were attempting to lure an alligator from underneath a car, but when the reptile darted out from underneath, it sent everybody scrambling.

The gator was eventually taken away from the underside of the car, and moved to a more favorable environment.
