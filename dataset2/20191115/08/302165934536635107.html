Renamo’s appeal to have election result annulled dismissed, says Mozambique’s top court

15 Nov 2019, 08:31

JOHANNESBURG, Nov 15 — Mozambique’s Constitutional Council has dismissed an application by the country’s main opposition party Renamo to have the results of its Oct. 15 general election nullified, according to a written judgement posted on its website.

“The applicant has not provided sufficient evidence to support its claim,” the judgement, dated Nov. 11, said. — Reuters
