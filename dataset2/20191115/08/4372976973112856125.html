The C7 Chevrolet Corvette era ended with this black Z06

A very good boy — and its manual transmission — gets laid to rest

15 Nov 2019, 08:25 by Jonathon Ramsey

The end of the C7-generation Chevrolet Corvette era ended not with a bang, not with a whimper, but with a tweet. Chevy posted a picture on the little blue bird platform yesterday showing the last-ever C7 to be hoisted down the line at Bowling Green. The gloss black Z06 coupe with Adrenaline Red trim set a record before it was built, becoming the highest grossing vehicle donated by an automaker when it crossed the Barrett-Jackson auction block for $2.7 million in June. Purchased by Dan Snyder, who also owns the final production Dodge Viper and Dodge Demon, the Z06 gets the Z07 performance package with kit like upgraded aero and carbon ceramic brakes, 3LZ trim package, custom wrapped red interior, navigation and Performance Data Recorder.
