Ronaldo Hailed After Hat-Trick Performance

15 Nov 2019, 08:26 by Kurt Buckerfield

Following his incredible hat-trick in Portugal's 6-0 win over Lithuania in their 2020 European Championship qualifier on Thursday night, superstar Cristiano Ronaldo has earned some praise from those around him.

Click through the gallery above to see some images of Ronaldo in action on Thursday!

A lot was said about Ronaldo's form for Juventus going into A Selecao's clash with Lithuania after he had been substituted by Maurizio Sarri in consecutive matches.

The Italian boss suggested on both occasions that the 34-year-old was not at his physical best, but Portugal boss Fernando Santos insisted he had no doubts his star man was perfectly fine.

"He's fine. I had no doubt about that, as I said before the match," he told RTP.

"Other people did, but I didn't have any doubts."

Ronaldo's international teammate Bruno Fernandes also hailed his attitude and spirit, before saying scoring a hat-trick is just standard procedure for the five-time Ballon d'Or winner.

"What we have is a very motivated Cristiano, who always gives his best," he said.  

"He scored three times, which is normal for him."
