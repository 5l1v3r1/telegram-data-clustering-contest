EMUI 10 stable version/public beta arrives 14 Huawei/Honor phones
4.5 (90%) 2 votes

The smartphone industry is consistently evolving and we are now at the point where a regular update is a necessity. OEMs now use the regular release of updates or support for old models as a marketing strategy and its working. Huawei is quite consistent and regular with updates, especially for its flagship models. A few days ago, the company released the EMUI 10 stable system update to P30/P30 Pro European users. Now, 14 Huawei/Honor phones are currently getting the EMUI 10 update. While some of these devices are getting stable versions, others are getting the public beta.

Devices getting EMUI 10 stable version

A total of 8 (eight) Huawei and Honor phones are currently receiving the EMUI 10 stable update. These devices include

  • Huawei Mate 20
  • Huawei Mate 20 Pro
  • Honor Magic 2
  • Honor 20
  • Mate 20 RS Porsche Design
  • Huawei Mate 20 X (4G)
  • Honor 20 PRO
  • Honor V20

Devices getting the public beta update

However, for the public beta update, 6 (six) Huawei and Honor phones are eligible. These devices include

  • Huawei Mate 10
  • Huawei Mate 10 Pro
  • Mate 10 Porsche Design
  • Mate RS Porsche Design
  • Huawei P20,
  • Huawei P20 Pro

The stable update is arriving Over-The-Air (OTA) thus all devices may not receive the update at the same time. Nevertheless, it will eventually arrive at your device if it is not currently available.
