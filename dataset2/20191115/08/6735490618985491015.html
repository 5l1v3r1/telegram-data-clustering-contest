Céline Dion Sings Karaoke Version Of Her Own Song ‘Flying On My Own’

15 Nov 2019, 08:56 by Corey Atad

Who better to do a karaoke song than the actual singer.

On Thursday night, Celine Dion attended a karaoke and listening party in New York for her new album Courage.

During the event, a TV screen was set up to play the songs with lyrics to sing along, and in one video posted to social media, Dion herself led the crowd in a karaoke rendition of her new track “Flying On My Own”.
