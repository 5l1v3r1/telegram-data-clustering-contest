Last Night's 'Grey's Anatomy' Finally Gave Fans The Cry They Really Needed

15 Nov 2019, 08:57

Fans of Grey’s Anatomy have been personally victimized by Shonda Rhimes and her plot twists many times over the past 16 seasons, but on Thursday night’s new episode — its 350th — the show really pulled out all of the stops. Meredith’s medical license hearing ended up being the perfect opportunity to break out the tissues. Y’all, it was rough, but in the best way possible.
