Social media followers take up Fani-Kayode over Dakolo-Fatoyinbo rape tweet

15 Nov 2019, 08:46 by Agency Report

Social media followers of ex-Minister of Aviation, Femi Fani-Kayode, have taken an umbrage at his tweet on the outcome of the suit of Mrs. Busola Dakolo, alleging rape by the Pastor/Founder of the Commonwealth of Zion Assembly, Biodun Fatoyinbo.

Mrs. Dakolo had alleged that Fatoyinbo raped her in her parents’ house when she was a teenager.

In its judgment, a Federal Capital Territory High Court sitting in Bwari on Thursday dismissed Mrs. Dakolo’s suit, and also ordered her to pay N1m for wasting the court’s time.
