Modest Mouse share new song “Ice Cream Party”

15 Nov 2019, 08:09 by Andrew Sacher

Modest Mouse, who are on tour now with The Black Keys, released a two-song single for Record Store Day earlier this year, and now they’ve released a third new song of 2019, “Ice Cream Party.” It’s a haunting, eerie song, and it’s pretty cool to hear Modest Mouse making stuff like this at this point in their lengthy career.
