Power out in area west of Sooke

15 Nov 2019, 08:45 by Cindy E. Harnett

Power was knocked out for about 140 customers in the Sooke area this morning.

B.C. Hydro crews headed out to the area just before 6 a.m.

The power outage is east of West Coast Road, also known as Hwy. 14, and west of Brutus Road, according to B.C. Hydro.

The cause of the outage is under investigation.

B.C. Hydro expects the power to be back on by 11:30 a.m.
