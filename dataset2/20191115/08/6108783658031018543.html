All of Microsoft’s X019 Inside Xbox livestream announcements

15 Nov 2019, 08:33 by Steve Wright

Lots announced today.

Microsoft’s X019 event is in full swing and we’ve all the big announcements from the Inside Xbox livestream that’s accompanied it.

Strap in, everyone — there’s a lot to cover!

  • Project xCloud preview adds more than 50 new games
  • Project xCloud heads to PC, “other devices” in 2020
  • Project xCloud heads to Canada, India, Japan in 2020
  • Everwild is the newest IP to come from Microsoft’s Rare
  • Sea of Thieves The Seabound Soul coming next week
  • DONTNOD’s Tell Me Why will feature a transgender protagonist
  • Halo Reach Master Chief Collection release date set for 3 December
  • 50 new games were just announced for Xbox Game Pass
  • Grounded is a first-person, co-op survival game from Obsidian

What from the above has you most excited?
