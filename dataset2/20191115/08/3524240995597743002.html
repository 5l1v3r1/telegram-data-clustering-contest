Ex-Information minister, Alex Akinyele, is dead

15 Nov 2019, 08:16 by Agency Report

A former Minister of Information, Alex Akinyele, is dead.

A statement by one of his sons, Constantine Akinfolarin Akinyele, confirmed that the Ondo chief passed away Thursday night after a brief illness, Tribune Online reported.

The former minister earned a degree in the English language from then University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo University).
