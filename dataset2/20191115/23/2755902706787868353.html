Passion Java RUBBISHES Influential List, MOCKS Mtawarira, Mambo Dhuterere and Ginimbi

15 Nov 2019, 23:48 by Masikati Nyasha

Passion Java RUBBISHES  Influential List, MOCKS Mambo Dhuterere, Tendai Mtawarira and Ginimbi.

Garrulous, motormouth  Prophet Passion Java who was named second on the 100 Most Influential Zimbabweans Under 40 List For 2019 has rubbished the list questioning it’s authenticity because he wasn’t named number one!

The glitzy US-based youthful Prophet who has perfected a knack of hogging acres and acres of limelight in Zimbabwean Media wasn’t amused that Tendai ‘Beast’ Mtawarira bested him on the list.  He whinged, “Mbanje, mbanje, unoita list uchiti Twabam number two? Pane apisa here pa Zimbabwe sa Twabam? Munhu anomhanya kubata chikweshe(rugby) nekuti kwahwinwa ukoko…haa imbanje”

He also took a potshot at man of the moment, Mambo Dhuterere who has stolen the nation’s hearts with his Gospel songs, “Haa imbanje, hanzi Mambo webute uyo, hanzi mambo we chiiko, mambo bute something uyo, moti akunda Jah Prayzah na Winky, Enzo, Mangoma  Levels Ribe na Fantan…(laughs…) ”

Signing off he bludgeoned celebrity businessman Genius “Ginimbi” Kadungure, poking fun at his grasp of the Queen’s language, “Zviri nani kungosiya, ingosiya.. Mukuita chinhu chembanje mbanje.. Mukoma Ginimbi avo dai mavaisawokana pa number5, 6, 7, munhu ane influence hombe ka muZimbabwe, akarova vhet yake akarova chii chii, ndezvembanje zvirikuitwa apa,hazvimeke sense, ini zita rangu bvisai henyu, mungandiisa panumber two? Twaaabam, ha imbanje, Zimbabwe!”
