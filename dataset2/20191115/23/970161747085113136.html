Chelsea issue warning to Jody Morris after he mocked Jose Mourinho

15 Nov 2019, 23:07

Jody Morris has been warned by Chelsea as to his future social media conduct after the Blues coach trolled Jose Mourinho.

Frank Lampard’s right-hand man took two swipes at the former Chelsea boss following his comments about the current state of affairs at Stamford Bridge.

Although Mourinho admitted there had been great progress at Chelsea, the Portuguese said he was still ‘worried’ about their defending in the big Premier League games.

Morris, Chelsea’s assistant coach, gleefully responded to Mourinho by pretending to cry in an Instagram video with Bitter Sweet Symphony by The Verve playing in the background and ridiculing the comments on Twitter.
