Lionel Messi caught telling Brazil coach Tite to 'shut up' on return from ban

15 Nov 2019, 23:42

Lionel Messi was caught telling Brazil coach Tite to stay quiet during Argentina’s win over Brazil.

Argentina beat Brazil 1-0 in Saudi Arabia thanks to Messi’s first-half goal and Gabriel Jesus’ horror miss from the penalty spot.

The game had several flashpoints with two penalties awarded and Tite was furious Messi did not receive a yellow card at one point in the match.

It was Messi’s first appearance for Argentina since serving a three-month ban for saying the Copa America was ‘fixed for Brazil’ and the Barcelona superstar was clearly fed up with Tite’s complaints from the sidelines.
