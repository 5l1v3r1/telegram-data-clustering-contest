Crash near St. Clements sends three people to hospital

One person was taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries

15 Nov 2019, 23:55 by Maddie Demarte

Regional police have confirmed three people were taken to hospital after a collision Friday afternoon.

The crash happened in the area of Williams Hastings Line and Manser Road near St. Clements.
