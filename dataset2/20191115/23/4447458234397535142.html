SOPHIA Di MARTINO In Talks for LOKI Co-Starring Role - Report

15 Nov 2019, 23:19 by Chris Arrant, Editor

Sophia Di Martino is in talks for a co-starring role in Disney+'s Loki show, according to Variety. The actor reportedly beat out several other actors in what the Hollywood trade called a "highly contested" part.

Di Martino was last seen in the film Yesterday, but shares a connection with Loki star Tom Hiddleston in that they both appeared on the BBC One hospital drama Casualty - albeit two years apart.

Loki is scheduled to debut in 2021 on Disney+.
