Future & Lil Durk Chronicle the Struggles of the Streets on "Last Name"

Future’s latest single has arrived.

15 Nov 2019, 23:34 by Patrick Montes

Two weeks are appearing on OVO duo DVSN’s latest single “No Cryin,” Future is unveiling a new record with Lil Durk. Titled “Last Name,” Future’s new single features production by DY Krazy and finds both Future and Lil Durk wearily reflecting on the darkest aspects of the street life and the paranoia that comes with that chaotic lifestyle. “Last Name” is also rumored to be a part of the rollout for Future’s next major studio project.

“I can’t go to sleep, I’m paranoid and it’s way too many homicides / Bought a hundred guns, got my n***as slidin’ / F*ck the bitch once, now she wan’ cry / Snitches told the feds, I had an alibi,” Future croons during the chorus of “Last Name.” “Brokenhearted, f*ck my bitch every night / I don’t know her name, she wanna have my child / Laughin’ to the bank, got me runnin’ wild / Numb to the pain, so I keep a smile.”
