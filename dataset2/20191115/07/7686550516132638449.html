Man found seriously injured at Tonypandy train station

He was found in the early hours of the morning

15 Nov 2019, 07:19 by Cathy Owen

A man was found with serious injuries in the subway of a Welsh train station.

Police were called at 1am on Friday after the injured man was found at Tonypandy railway station.

A spokesman said he had been taken to hospital with serious injuries.

The subway is currently sealed off as police investigate.

Rail passengers using the station this morning are being told they need to use the Trealaw entrance.

One rail passenger said there was a "lot of police activity" at the station this morning.

Anyone with information should contact police on 101 quoting occurrence 1900421624.
