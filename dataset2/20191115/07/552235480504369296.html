Tickets to see Gerry Cinnamon play Hampden Park are on sale today - here's how to get them

How to get tickets for Gerry Cinnamon, Deacon Blue, Goldfrapp and A Question Of Sport Live plus details on a special two-for-one offer to see Jack Whitehall's shows in Glasgow and Aberdeen next week

15 Nov 2019, 07:56 by Linda Howard

Tickets to see Gerry Cinnamon at Hampden Park go on sale this morning at 9am through Ticketmaster .

The Glaswegian phenomenon will perform in front of more than 50,000 fans on July 18, 2020 at the famous venue which has previously  played host to shows from the Stone Roses, Rolling Stones, Coldplay, Oasis and Bruce Springsteen.

It will be the biggest show of his career and a real statement of just how big his famous “bonny” is burning.

Buy tickets to see Gerry Cinnamon at Hampden Park here

Tickets also go on sale today for Deacon Blue , Goldfrapp and A Question Of Sport Live .

And there's a special two-for-one ticket offer to see Jack Whitehall: Stood Up at The SSE Hydro, Glasgow on Tuesday, 19th and Wednesday 20th, November and at P&J Live, Aberdeen on Thursday, November 21 - get tickets here .

And if you're looking for something to do this weekend, there are still tickets available to see Emile Sande perform in Edinburgh on Sunday, November 17 and in Glasgow on Tuesday, November 19 - get tickets here .
