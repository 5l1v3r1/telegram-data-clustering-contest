Gauri Khan, Karan Johar and Kajol pose for a happy picture last night at the Katy Perry's welcome party

15 Nov 2019, 07:35 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

It was indeed a star studded bash last night at Kara Johar's residence. Who's who of Bollywood was present at the Katy Perry's welcome party hosted by KJo. Many actors are taking to their Instagram handles to share pictures from the fun night.King Khan's wife Gauri Khan also took to her profile and shared a sweet frame with Kajol and Karan Johar. The trio was all smiles as they posed for a selfie.
