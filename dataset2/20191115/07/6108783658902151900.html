Sea of Thieves Seabound Soul coming next week

15 Nov 2019, 07:21 by Steve Wright

A new Tall Tale (and firebombs) come 20 November.

Sea of Thieves The Seabound Soul will head to Windows PC and Xbox One next week, developer Rare confirmed as part of the X019 Inside Xbox livestream.

Players can expect the free content update from 20 November, Rare confirmed.

“This update features an all-new, lore-focused Tall Tale mission for players looking ot play their part in a thrilling story,” Rare advised. “Join Captain Pendragon to uncover the mystery of the feat=red ship, the Ashen Dragon, and reveal the secrets of a sinister new threat.”

The update will also bring firebombs to the game, ammo which is able to be thrown or loaded into cannons in both Adventure and in The Arena.

Sea of Thieves is available now on Windows PC and Xbox One and via Xbox Game Pass. Rare also announced new IP today in the form of Everwild.
