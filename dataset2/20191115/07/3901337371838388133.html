'Griezmann Must Give Everything To King Messi'

15 Nov 2019, 07:28 by Kurt Buckerfield

With Antoine Griezmann having had a difficult start to life at FC Barcelona, it has been suggested that he should have reconsidered signing for the Catalan club, mainly because of Lionel Messi.

The 28-year-old made a big-money move from Atletico Madrid to the Blaugrana in July, in a transfer that caused quite a stir due to the fact that he snubbed an approach from the Spanish giants just a year earlier.

Since his arrival, Griezmann has been deployed predominantly in a left-wing role, away from the central position he played in at the Rojiblancos and in which he plays for France.

The Frenchman has managed to score just four times in his first 15 matches for Barca, leading former France international Frank Leboeuf to believe the forward should perhaps should not have signed for Barca.
