Art in the Springs showcases art club’s work

15 Nov 2019, 03:00 by Aaron Kirby

THE Art in the Springs annual art showcase features work in a variety of mediums.

RAAFA Meadow Springs Art Club members have been busy preparing new artworks for the event.

The club consists of several accomplished artists who work in mediums such as watercolour, oils, acrylics, pastels and alcohol inks.

They meet every Friday for art sessions at the RAAFA village.

Throughout 2019 they have held a number of smaller exhibitions in local shopping centres.

The club regularly conducts workshops led by established artists from both interstate and the local area.

This keeps members engaged, stimulated and in touch with current trends.

WHAT: Art in the Springs annual showcase

WHERE: RAAFA Estate, Portrush Parade, Meadow Springs

WHEN: Friday, November 22 to 24
