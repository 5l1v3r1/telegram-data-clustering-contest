This heated mouse mat will keep your hands warm in the office this winter

Amazon is selling a USB-powered mouse mat that has a built-in warming pad

15 Nov 2019, 16:17 by Linda Howard

Temperatures have started to drop and we’re already seeing gritters out on the roads as the dark cloak of winter settles around Scotland’s broad shoulders.

Too dramatic?

Well, we can’t be the only ones sitting in an open-plan office with layers on wishing somebody would crank up the heating. 

Using a keyboard and mouse with cold fingers is not to be sneered at and no amount of hot cups of tea ever seem to make it any better.

Until now.

Amazon is selling a heated mouse mat.

 

The USB-powered mouse mat costs £16.66 and has a built-in warming pad - guaranteed to keep your hands toasty warm in the office this winter.

And a second reviewer added: "People at work call it my big pink slipper. I call it my haven for warm fingers!
