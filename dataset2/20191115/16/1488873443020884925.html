Cosmoprof Worldwide Bologna 2020 Presents: The Garden of Innovation (Sponsored)

15 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Thomas Ohr

Considering the beauty industry is more guided by trends than any other fast-moving consumer goods sector, it is no surprise brands are increasingly turning to technology to stay competitive. The global cosmetics market is expected to reach $863 billion by 2023, up from $532 billion in 2017 (Nielsen) and this growth is widely enables by new technologies.

That is why Cosmoprof has decided to dedicate the heart of the show, the Service Center area, to the BeautyTech companies and startups that are disrupting the entire industry.

These companies will be featured in an innovative space to drive brands, companies and investors to engage and explore potential opportunities for cutting-edge achievements: a unique opportunity to access the global beauty community at the world’s largest beauty event.

From AI and AR advancements to the latest applications and tools, the Garden of Innovation lets you experience emerging tech from the beauty industry’s best companies and startups.

Cosmoprof 2020 Garden of Innovation, this area of the heart of the Expo floor (Service Center) will showcase the latest technologies from the industry’s best beauty startups.

The Gallery of Innovation is designed to provide beauty tech companies with the opportunity to access the global beauty community at the world’s largest beauty event. For further information, visit the Cosmoprof website.
