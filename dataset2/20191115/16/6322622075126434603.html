Tekun making forays overseas, targets 10 bil renmimbi sales in Changsha

15 Nov 2019, 16:48 by Bernama

CHANGSHA (China) (Nov 15): The National Entrepreneur Group Economic Fund (Tekun Nasional) is making forays overseas by bringing Malaysian entrepreneurs to join trade missions to introduce their products to the global market.

Its deputy chief executive officer (management and corporate), Roshaimi Harun said the trade missions emanated from the creation of the International Division at Tekun Nasional in July 2019 to coordinate with the International Division of the Ministry of Entrepreneur Development.

He said the Tekun Nasional International Division has so far conducted four trade missions, firstly to Singapore, followed by Bosnia, Myanmar and fourthly to Hunan, Changsha, which is participated by 13 Tekun entrepreneurs out 46 Malaysian entrepreneurs.

The trade missions were conducted with the cooperation of the Malaysian Small Entrepreneurs Chamber of Commerce (DPUKM) and Malaysia Promas International Business Society (Promas), he said after the launch the One Town One Product (OTOP) International Commodity Fair 2019 here, Friday.

Promas president Datuk Chong Chong Tik said he regularly brought Malaysian entrepreneurs for trade missions and expects Malaysian entrepreneurs to record 10 billion renmimbi (RM1=1.69 renmimbi) in sales at OTOP 2019 Commodity Fair.

"Most importantly we get repeat orders from trade visitors at OTOP International Commodity Fair today and tomorrow, as well as from other visitors when it is open to the public in the last two days,” he said.

DPUKM president Datuk Seri Abu Hasan Mohd Nor said the collaboration between Tekun Nasional and the chamber enabled favourite Malaysian food products to be introduced at the fair.

“I brought 46 participants and covered all aspects of business, especially what China loves most — food like durian crepe, durian coffee and ginseng," he said.

He said Chinese consumers really like Malaysian food products not only due to their guaranteed halal certification but also because some of them are not found elsewhere except in Malaysia.

The four-day OTOP International Commodity Fair 2019 held at the Changsha International Convention Center gathered about 2,000 entrepreneurs and exhibitors from around the world.
