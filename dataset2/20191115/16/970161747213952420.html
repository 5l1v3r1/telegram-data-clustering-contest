Sleuthing mother-in-law exposes her daughter's husband as a pedophile

15 Nov 2019, 16:42

A mother-in-law successfully exposed her daughter’s husband as a pedophile after posing as a young boy who the pervert propositioned for sex.

Ryan Budde, 34, was sentenced to more than 10 years in prison on Tuesday on child pornography charges in Clinton, Iowa after his ex wife’s mother Kimberly Earnest provided corroborating evidence against him.

Budde’s wife, now named Keri Butler after she divorced him, became suspicious about her husband’s faithfulness and found he had been talking to underage boys online and asking them for pictures, according to the prosecutor.

He reportedly promised to stop exchanging the images online and chatting with the boys – but Butler and Earnest, discovered he was unable to keep that promise.

‘Budde’s mother-in-law took it upon herself to conduct an undercover investigation where she pretended to be a 15-year-old boy and began to communicate with Budde online,’ the assistant US attorney said, according to NBC News.

Budde quickly became interested in meeting the ‘boy’ for sex, prosecutors said. This led Butler to divorce her husband, according to the Des Moines Register. 

Meanwhile, authorities in Clinton had been investigating Budde since May 2017 after they received a tip that 27 child porn images showing boys’ genitalia were uploaded to the social media site Tumblr.

Police acted on the tip and seized two laptops belonging to Budde, which were found to have contained 365 images of child pornography.

Prosecutors said: ‘The investigation also revealed that Budde had chat sessions with young boys, where he pretended to be a 15-year-old virgin.’

Budde reportedly admitted to police that he had an ‘unhealthy attraction’ to teenage boys under 18 years old.

The interaction between Budde and the ‘boy’ Earnest had posed as was used as evidence in court.

He was sentenced to 130 months in prison followed by six years of supervised release and will have to register as a sex offender.

‘Budde is a danger and the community needs to be protected…’

‘Budde systematically integrated himself in areas within the community wherein he would have access to teen boys,’ the prosecutor said.
