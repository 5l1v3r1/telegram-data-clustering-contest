Samsung’s rumoured second Galaxy Fold to launch in Canada

Hopefully it's a lot cheaper than the original

15 Nov 2019, 16:35 by Dean Daley

It looks like Samsung will eventually bring a Galaxy Fold series phone to Canada.

16-year-old tech leaker Ishan Agarwal (@ishanagarwal24) says that the rumoured ‘SM-F700’ 4G-only Galaxy Fold device will launch in two colours, black and purple. Additionally, Agarwal states that this foldable smartphone will come to multiple countries, including Canada.
