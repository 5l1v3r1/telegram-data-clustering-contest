Realme is all set to launch the Realme X2 Pro premium flagship smartphone in the Indian market on 20th November. Thanks to the Flipkart landing page, it was hinted that the company could also launch the Realme 5s smartphone at the same launch event.

Now, ahead of the launch of this upcoming entry-level smartphone, some key details about the device have surfaced online. Through a teaser on Flipkart, it seems almost confirmed that the device will be launched on 20th November.

Further, the smartphone will come with quad-camera setup on the back, with the primary sensor being a 48-megapixel camera. The teaser image claims that the camera can deliver “ultra-detailed pictures that remain sharp even when zoomed in.”

The leaked image of the phone’s back panel reveals that in the quad-camera setup, the sensors will be aligned vertically and the setup will be placed in the top-left corner. Further, it also shows that there will be a rear-mounted fingerprint sensor. Also, the back panel has the company’s signature diamond-cut design with a glass layer.

It has been revealed that the smartphone will feature a 6.51-inch HD+ display and will come powered by a 5,000mAh battery, running Android Pie operating system with the company’s own ColorOS 6 on top.

The Realme 5s is expected to be driven by Snapdragon 665 and 4 GB of RAM. It may arrive with 64 GB of native storage and support for external storage. The smartphone is expected to come powered by the Snapdragon 665 SoC, along with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage.

On the back side, the phone could come with 48-megapixel + 8-megapixel + 2-megapixel + 2-megapixel quad camera system and on the front side, the device is expected to be equipped with a 13-megapixel snapper.

To know more, including its specifications, pricing and availability details, we’ll have to wait for a few more days.
