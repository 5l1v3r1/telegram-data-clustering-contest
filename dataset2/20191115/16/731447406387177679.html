Grimes has shared a new track, "So Heavy I Fell Through the Earth," from her upcoming album, Miss_Anthrop0cene.

The Canadian pop artist has also revealed the tracklist and release date for her fifth LP, due out on February 21st, 2020. Arriving via 4AD, the ten-track album sees features from i_o and 潘PAN.

The new song is the second single off Miss_Anthrop0cene, following the release of "Violence" in September. Grimes has described her forthcoming project as a "nu metal(ish)" concept album focused on climate change.
