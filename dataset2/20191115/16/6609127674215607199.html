LOST BHOY

Man in Celtic top does Rangers tour…then visits Parkhead in Light Blues jersey

15 Nov 2019, 16:09 by Aidan Scott

A MAN in a Celtic top was spotted doing an Ibrox stadium tour...before visiting Parkhead in a Rangers jersey.

Light Blues fans were stunned earlier today to see the man wearing a Hoops shirt with Callum McGregor's name on his back at Ibrox.

But all was not as it seemed as the same 'supporter' was later pictured at Celtic Park in a Rangers top.

The 'supporter', who had a camera and tripod with him on both tours, is actually the YouTuber behind the Ultimate Bucket List channel.

Ninh Ly has toured several grounds around Europe dressed in the kit of the home team's rivals for his videos.

These have included visiting Liverpool's Anfield and Manchester City's Etihad stadiums in a Manchester United kit.

And his trip to visit both sides of the Old Firm was just the latest trips on his list.

That didn't stop both Rangers and Celtic fans aiming digs at Ly, as well as each other in general, on social media however.

@Ritchie_bjk said: "I wouldn't have let him in with that top on."

@JamesyMc55 added: "Seeing what a real institution looks like...let them come after us, we welcome the chase."

@Windsorrocker commented: "Well we all hear how Celtic are 'everyone's second team' but I didn't think that meant EVERYONE..."

And @GrizzleyBear72 said: "That's a caption competition if ever I saw one...the look on that guy's face is a picture."
