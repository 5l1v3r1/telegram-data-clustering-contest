Fans purchase a star in the Star Register database for Ateez and Atiny

15 Nov 2019, 16:26 by bribaybay90

In celebration of Atiny's birthday, a couple of fans pitched in to purchase a star in the Star Register database called “Atiny (Aurora)” for all of Ateez and Atiny. 

Now, no matter where you are in the world you can look up and we will always be there.

Thank you fellow Atiny for always loving and supporting our boys. 💕
