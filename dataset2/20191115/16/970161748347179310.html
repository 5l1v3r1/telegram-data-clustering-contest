Robbie Williams wanted Fairytale Of New York duet with Britney Spears

15 Nov 2019, 16:47

Robbie Williams reached out to record a duet of Fairytale of New York with Britney Spears, and it would have been like Christmas come early if he’d pulled it off.

The Angels singer has blessed us all with a new album – The Christmas Present – full of festive covers and some original bops.

But the 45-year-old admitted he couldn’t coax the Toxic star into the studio with him for a collaboration.

Speaking about why The Pogues’ banger didn’t make the list, he explained: ‘I wanted to do it with Britney Spears, but she’s not working, unfortunately.’

While Brit might be missing from the album, Jamie Cullum, Rod Stewart, Bryan Adams and even Tyson Fury have all got in on the action.

And the Take That legend is adamant that the Christmas Present isn’t just a ‘stop-gap’ but something he has put a solid three years of his life into.

‘I wouldn’t call it a stopgap, because you don’t spend three years writing a stop-gap, but it’s nice to take the pressure off,’ he added to Vice.

‘That said, I’ve put a lot of time and effort into getting it right.’

This will be among our favourite Christmas presents this year…

As well as a festive album, Robbie recently confirmed he would be embarking on another Las Vegas residency, kicking things off next year.

Because who needs rest?

Ayda Field’s husband announced the news on Instagram, months after his first stint came to an end.

‘I’m so excited to be returning to Las Vegas next year x ”Robbie Williams Live in Las Vegas” returns to Wynn Las Vegas in March and April 2020,’ he told fans.

At this stage, he’ll perform eight shows from 24 March, going through to 4 April.

And we absolutely cannot wait.

Robbie Williams’ The Christmas Present is released on 22 November.
