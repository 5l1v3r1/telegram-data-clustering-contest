Ananya Panday wishes her 'Student Of The Year 2' co-star Tara Sutaria for her second release 'Marjaavaan'

15 Nov 2019, 16:33 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ananya Panday and Tara Sutaria made their debut with Punit Malhotra's 'Student Of The Year 2'. The duo received love from the audience for the performance in the film. Now, Tara's second film 'Marjaavaan' co-starring Sidharth Malhotra and Riteish Deshmukh have hit the theatres today.

Ananya took to her Instagram account to wish her former co-star for her second release. She posted a sweet picture with a background music. Both of them are all smiles in the photograph. She captioned the photo, "Good luck for Marjaavaan Taar @tarasutaria" She also added, "Dedicating ur fave song to u".

On the work front, Ananya will be next seen in 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' alongside Kartik Aaryan and Bhumi Pednekar. Helmed by Mudassar Aziz, the film will hit the theatres on December 06.

On the other hand, 'Marjaavaan' is helmed by Milap Zaveri and also stars Rakul Preet Singh.
