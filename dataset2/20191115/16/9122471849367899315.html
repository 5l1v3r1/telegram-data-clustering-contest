Florida State vs. Alabama State: Game Time, Spread, Channel, Announcers

15 Nov 2019, 16:51 by Tzvi Machlin

The Florida State Seminoles will look to get that coveted sixth win of the season when they take on the Alabama State Hornets.

To say that the 2019 season has been a disaster for Florida State might almost be an insult to disasters. And yet, despite firing head coach Willie Taggart, dealing with a multitude of injuries and troubles at quarterback, the Seminoles need just one win to secure bowl eligibility.

Alabama State have been a rather average FCS team this year. They lost their only other game against an FBS team, in their season opener against UAB. Though they’re 4-4 with a respectable offense, it’s hard to imagine the Hornets pulling off an FCS over FBS upset.

Given how often Power Five teams crush their FCS opposition, the ‘Noles have been listed as massive favorites over the Hornets. They’ll be favored by nearly six touchdowns at home.
