Fife woman, 97, found dead in home as police arrest man

15 Nov 2019, 16:39 by Paul Smith

COPS have arrested and charged a man after a pensioner was found dead in Fife.

Annie Temple, 97, was discovered within a property on West End, Kinglassie on Friday October 25 2019.

Officers were alerted to a body at around 11:10am that morning.

The man, 36, is expected to appear at Kirkcaldy Sheriff Court on Monday.

Detective Chief Inspector Kevin Jamieson, of Police Scotland's Major Investigation Team, said: "Thank you to the members of the local community who have provided information and assistance in relation to our investigation.

"Your help and support has been appreciated."

"Local officers have been and will continue to be in the local area to provide reassurance."
