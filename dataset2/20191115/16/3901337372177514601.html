Images Of Bafana’s Defeat To Ghana

15 Nov 2019, 16:02 by Soccer Laduma

After no television broadcast of last night’s Africa Cup Of Nations qualifying encounter, Soccer Laduma takes a look at seven images from Bafana Bafana’s defeat to Ghana.

Click through the gallery to view some images of Bafana in action last night

The South Africa national team suffered a 2-0 loss to Ghana in the Cape Coast, which was a disappointing start to their 2021 Africa Cup Of Nations qualifying campaign.

However, there will be no time to lick any wounds, as Bafana are expected to arrive in Mzansi at 17h25 today, where they will thereafter gear up for Sunday’s encounter.

The South African national team will host Sudan at the Orlando Stadium on Sunday, in their second qualifying fixture, hopeful to secure maximum points before resuming qualification action in August next year.

Bafana are in group C alongside, Ghana Sudan and São Tomé and Príncipe, with Ghana and Sudan having already secured their first three points.
