The 10 Most Amazing Red Lipsticks Of All Time

15 Nov 2019, 16:51 by Sofiya Golovnya

Red lipstick is a true staple in any girl’s makeup bag. It looks good if you wear it for special occasions or use it every day. As choosing a perfect product might be quite hard, it’s great to get some guidance. This list of 10 amazing lipsticks will help you find the red color most suitable just for you.

Maybelline Superstay Matte Ink Liquid Lipstick in Pioneer

Intensely perfect, this liquid lipstick inks your lips in up to sixteen hours. SuperStay Matte Ink features a unique arrow applicator for precise application. This product is also very affordable, so you don’t need to break the bank for that lusty red lip.

Revlon Super Lustrous Lipstick in Really Red

Revlon use microfine pigments in their lipsticks to make the shades vibrant and the feeling lightweight. The formula is infused with vitamin E and avocado oil to moisturize your lips. This lipstick does not cake, so you won’t notice it’s even there.

Stila Stay All Day Liquid Lipstick in Beso

This creamy-matte, full coverage Stila liquid lipstick is weightless and comfortable. The color-rich formula will not bleed or transfer, and the Vitamin E and avocado oil softens the lips to make them look and feel beautiful!

MAC Retro Matte Lipstick in Ruby Woo

MAC Lipstick is the iconic product that made the brand famous. The Retro Matte long-wearing formula features an intense colour payoff and a completely matte finish to result in a non-feathering and fade-proof true red color.

Fenty Beauty Stunna Lip Paint in Uncensored

Stunna Lip Paint has a lip-defining precision wand, which makes it easier than ever to put on this soft matte pigment. Its long-lasting formula is seriously weightless, has no quick feathering and stays kissably smooth.

Becca Ultimate Lipstick Love in Cherry

This Becca satin lip color lasts for up to eight hours and is infused with hyaluronic acid to hydrate and smooth the look of lips. Creamy pure pigments and light-reflecting oils deliver bold color effortlessly. Infused with avocado oil, this formula nourishes lips too.

Kylie Cosmetics Matte Lip Kit in Mary Jo K

The now-iconic Matte Liquid Lipstick has high intensity pigment for instant bold color. The product lipstick contains moisturizing ingredients for a comfortable feel that does not dry your lips out, and the lipliner from the kit adds precision.

Dior Rouge Dior Lipstick in 999 Red

The cult formula of Rouge Dior wears for up to sixteen hours, and the Rouge 999 shade will seriously boost your confidence. This lipstick is on the more expensive side, but the high quality and luxurious feel make it worth every cent.

Chanel Rouge Allure Ink Matte Liquid Lip Colour in 222 Signature

This Chanel liquid lipstick with a smooth texture glides on like a gloss and sets with a luminous, velvet-like finish. Many great ingredients smooth and nourish lips, and the precision applicator tip allows for comfortable and easy application.

Bobbi Brown Luxe Lip Color in Retro Red

Bobbi Brown’s boldest, most vibrant color ever looks like a movie star from the ’50s could use it. This rich formula nourishes the lips while intensely rejuvenating and conditioning them. A sleek gold tube adds to the luxurious vibe.
