When is the Prince Andrew interview on the BBC? Where to watch interview

Prince Andrew the Duke of York is due to answer questions regarding his links to deceased US financier Jeffrey Epstein

15 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Lewis Knight, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lewis-knight/

The Duke of York is set to answer questions in a televised interview with BBC Newsnight.

Prince Andrew, Duke of York, who is the third child of The Queen and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, will be interviewed by Newsnight host Emily Maitlis.

The focus of questioning is expected to cover the long-running scandal surrounding US financier Jeffrey Epstein, who was a known former associate of the Duke.

The interview was recorded on Thursday (November 14) in Buckingham Palace.

But when does the the interview air on British television? Here is what you need to know.

When is the Prince Andrew interview on the BBC? Where to watch interview

Prince Andrew & The Epstein Scandal: The Newsnight Interview will air at 9pm on Saturday, November 16, 2019 on BBC TWO and BBC iPlayer.

BBC Newsnight host Emily Maitlis described the interview to the BBC as "no holds barred".

The Duke is expected to answer questions about his relationship with US financier Jeffrey Epstein, who took his own life at the age of 66 earlier this year whilst awaiting trial for charges of sex-trafficking.

Accusations have also been levelled at the Duke himself by accusers of Epstein, but these are strongly denied by the Duke and Buckingham Palace.

The Duke has two daughters by his former wife, Sarah Ferguson: the recently engaged Princess Beatrice and the recently married Princess Eugenie.
