Trailer Review: The Body

15 Nov 2019, 16:33 by Akash Nihalani

Movie: The Body
Cast: Rishi Kapoor, Emraan Hashmi, Sobhita Dhulipala and Vedhika
Release Date: 13 December 2019
Trailer Rating: 3.5/5

The trailer of The Body starring Rishi Kapoor, Emraan Hashmi, Sobhita Dhulipala and Vedhika, known for her work in south Indian films, is out now and looking at the trailer it seems like the film will serve a healthy dose of drama and suspense.

The trailer revolves around a police officer’s search for a body gone missing from a morgue. The body that is being searched is of a successful business woman Maya (Sobhita Dhulipala). The prime suspect of the is her own husband which is played by Emraan. The investigating officer in the case is played by Rishi Kapoor, who makes his return to the big screen in a rather interesting role. Later, in the trailer we witness another twist in which it is shown that Emraan’s character had been cheating on his wife with another woman, played by Vedhika. What caught our attention in the trailer was that in the last scene, we. See Emraan trying to flush a piece of paper with ‘I love you jaan’ written over it. However, when he realises the police are on the lookout, he ends up swallowing it instead.
