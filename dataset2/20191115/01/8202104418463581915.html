Five places to shop this weekend in Saskatoon

15 Nov 2019, 01:21 by Erin Petrow, Saskatoon StarPhoenix

Warm weather is headed Saskatoon's way this weekend, so you might as well make the most of it and get some Christmas gifts checked off the list.

Warm weather is headed Saskatoon’s way this weekend, so you might as well make the most of it and get some Christmas gifts checked off the list.

1. Night market

Head over to the Remai Modern on Friday night from 5 p.m. to 10 p.m. for the Remai Modern’s Night Market. Taking place in the Riverview Room, the event will host more than 20 local vendors and showcase new seasonal items from the Art & Design Store. Admission to the night market is free.

2. Christmas show

Everything Christmas is headed to Prairieland Park on Saturday and Sunday during the Mega Snow Christmas Show. The show and sale exclusively features local businesses, from jewellers to bakers and everything in between. Admission is $5 for adults and free for kids 12 and under.

3. SaskExpo Holiday Market

It’s the perfect time to fill the stockings for the lovers of all things pop culture. The SaskExpo Holiday Market, held at the German Cultural Centre on Sunday from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m., will feature more than 50 artists, crafters and vendors offering a variety of pop culture items from Pokemon to Spiderman. Entry is $2.

4. International bazaar

If you’re looking for something a little bit different, head over to 359 First Avenue North on Saturday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. for the Saskatoon Open Door Society’s International Bazaar and Market. Artisans from more than 30 different countries will be out selling handmade jewelry, art, crafts — and of course some delicious ethnic foods. While you shop, make sure to enjoy the live entertainment. Entry to the sale is free.

5. Santa Claus Parade

If you’re not too tired from all the shopping over the weekend, finish it off right at the annual Saskatoon Santa Claus Parade. The parade will snake its way downtown on Sunday starting at 1 p.m. Check out its website for all the information and more details on Santa’s route.
