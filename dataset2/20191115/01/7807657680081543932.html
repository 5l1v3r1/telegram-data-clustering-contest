Wasteland 3 is now available to preorder

The fate of Arizona rests in your hands.

15 Nov 2019, 01:54 by Jennifer Locke

Source: Windows Central

What you need to know

  • Wasteland 3 is a post-apocalyptic strategy game.
  • It is set to release on May 19, 2020 for Xbox One, PS4, and PC.
  • You can preorder it on the Microsoft Store for $60.

During X019 in London a new trailer for Wasteland 3 was revealed alongside a release date. It looks like we'll be playing it shortly before E3 this year, and if you just can't wait for it, you can preorder it on the Microsoft Store right now. Those who preorder will receive in-game items like the Ranger General's Hat and Glasses, Polished Ranger Badge, and Snow Camo Weapons.
