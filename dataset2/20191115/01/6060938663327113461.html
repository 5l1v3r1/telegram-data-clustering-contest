Exclusive! Tara Sutaria on working with ‘crush’ Sidharth Malhotra: It was lots of fun

15 Nov 2019, 01:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Tara Sutaria had once admitted that she has a crush on an ex-student from the ‘Student of The Year’ franchise and the actress was soon linked to Sidharth Malhotra. The duo was cast together for revenge drama ‘Marjaavaan’ and Tara landed the opportunity to romance Sidharth on the silver screen. Speaking about working with her crush, Tara exclusively told ETimes, “It was great. We actually met while we were filming in Marcia and it was it was lots of fun. Of course, it was very serious and intense as well but I think everyone connected well on the sets. And yeah, it was fantastic.”
