Marvel, Hulu, ESPN Plus, Star Wars details

BURBANK, Calif. -- If you love all things Disney, you're probably counting down the hours until the Nov. 12 launch of Disney+, the Walt Disney Company's streaming service.

According to a countdown clock posted to several official Disney websites, the streamer will launch at 6 a.m. ET | 5 a.m. CT | 3 a.m. PT on Tuesday.

Click here to visit the official Disney+ website for pre-registration information. In the meantime, here's everything you need to know ahead of the big launch.

WHAT IS DISNEY PLUS?

Disney+ (pronounced "Disney Plus") is the streaming service that will be the home for much of the content created by The Walt Disney Company. It will include content from Walt Disney Studios, Twentieth Century Fox, Marvel Studios, Pixar, Lucasfilm, National Geographic and more. It will offer thousands of TV episodes and hundreds of movies.
