SNP May Add Scrapping Trident to Post-Election Deal "Wish-List" if Labour Wins 2019 Snap Elections

11 Nov 2019, 20:24 by by Demond Cureton

The leader made the comments whilst speaking on Sophy Ridge on Remembrance Sunday, suggesting that the SNP took "responsibilities for defence seriously" and that conventional defences were not given sufficient priority.

Trident may be included on his party's post-election "wish-list", Scottish National Party Westminster leader, Ian Blackford said on Sunday.

“We don’t believe that we should be investing in those weapons of mass destruction," Mr Blackford told Sky News.
