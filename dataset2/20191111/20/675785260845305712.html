Man 'tries to lure three-year-old girl into van with non-existent puppy'

The girl's mother told police a stranger approached her family in a parking lot in York County, Pennsylvania and claimed he had a dog to show her daughter

11 Nov 2019, 20:37 by Talia Shadwell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/talia-shadwell/

Authorities in the US have arrested a man accused of trying to lure a three-year-old girl into his van with the offer of a puppy.

The girl's mother told police a man in a silver van asked her for a cigarette in a Pennsylvanian parking lot.

She told officers she gave him a cigarette, then he asked her daughter if she wanted to see a puppy in the back of his van.

But the mum said there was no dog inside the vehicle, according to the New York Post .

She told police she had been with her husband and two daughters when the stranger approached them in the parking lot in York County, West Manchester Township.

She drove to another store and called police after the encounter.

Police told US media they found New York man Sixtus Chiemeka Udeke in the van, which had temporary New Jersey license plates, along with two other men.

Police arrested Udeke, 39, on Friday and pressed charges relating to luring a child into a motor vehicle.

The force said Udeke, also known as Azubuike Nuubisi Anodu, is in custody in the York County Prison.

He has been denied bail and is due in court later this month.
