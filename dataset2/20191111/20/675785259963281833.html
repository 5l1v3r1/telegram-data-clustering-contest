EastEnders viewers horrified over Mel's suicide confession to Lisa

Lisa Fowler confronted her friend Mel Owen over her increasingly disturbing behaviour on EastEnders - but was left stunned over her reaction

11 Nov 2019, 20:20 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

EastEnders viewers were left utterly horrified tonight over Mel Owen's comment to Lisa Fowler tonight.

Over the last week, Lisa has become increasingly disturbed by Mel's unhinged behaviour - which even resulted in her friend punching her.

Despite that, Lisa tried once again to get her friend to seek help, even booking her in for an appointment with her doctor.

Unfortunately, this had the opposite desired effect as Mel was left utterly furious.

Trapping Lisa in the club, office Mel asked her: "You know what you are, Lis?"

"Yes," Lisa nodded enthusiastically. "Your best friend."

"Some of the time," Mel agreed. "But most of the time you're just something we have to put up with. And we've had to do a lot of that, me and Louise, over the years."

Mel continued spitefully: "With you in and out of psychiatric wards... and i thought, one day, the doctors are gonna call us up and tell us you finally done it. Finally put yourself out of your misery."

Lisa struggled not to cry, saying that she could forgive a lot, but Mel interrupted her.

"I can't," Mel said. "I can forgive you being weak and I can forgive you acting stupid.

"I can forgive you not being able to cope as a mother and me step in, do it all for you. But what I can't forgive is you sticking your weak, stupid and foolish little nose into my business."

As Lisa remained silent, Mel said: "Now, Louise is moving to Portugal because of me, what I can offer her. Because of the life that I can give her. And you're the price we have to pay.

"But if you push it too far, it's like any deal - it collapses. We go and leave you behind."

*EastEnders returns tomorrow at 7.30pm on BBC One
