Rangers-daft WWE star Drew McIntyre dons Gers shirt and sends fans message ahead of Glasgow show

The wrestler is a die-hard Light Blues supporter and was pictured wearing a Ally McCoist Rangers jersey.

11 Nov 2019, 20:17 by Record Sport Online

WWE star Drew McIntyre has been proudly wearing his Rangers shirt ahead of tonight's show in Glasgow.

The wrestler is a die-hard Light Blues supporter and took the opportunity to send a message to the Ibrox faithful before he gets in the WWE ring at The Hydro this evening.

McIntyre was pictured wearing the home shirt with Rangers legend Ally McCoist on the back of it.

He said: "This is Drew McIntyre here and I'm not just at any show, I am home. I am in Scotland, there are thousand of Scots ready to get crazy tonight.

"You might have noticed I am wearing my Rangers jersey, I am a Rangers fan I think everybody knows that. More importantly I am a Scottish football fan.

"Rangers and my career over the past few years have been very similar. I got fired, Rangers relegated to the bottom.

"Both wouldn't give up, the fans wouldn't give up, my fans stood behind me the whole time.

"We both fought fought fought and now we are both back on top.

"Rangers and Celtic are being talked about the world, Scottish football is being talked about in the world.

"I am a happy man, I am on top of the moon. I am going to go out there and I am going to get mental. And if I see you down the line, get ready to get mental with me."
