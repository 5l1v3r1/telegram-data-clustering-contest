Comedian Jo Koy coming to Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena

The funnyman will stop off in Dubai as part of his Just Kidding world tour

11 Nov 2019, 19:20 by Amy Mathieson

Dubai is fast becoming a go-to tour stop for the world’s funniest comedians, and now there’s another huge naming coming to the city.

US comedian Jo Koy will be bringing his Just Kidding world tour to Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena on Monday January 20.

It will be the first time the comedy star has performed in Dubai, and tickets go on sale on Thursday (November 14) at 10am.

"This will be my first time performing in Dubai, and I’m so excited I get to do it at the Coca-Cola Arena,” said Koy. “This has been a dream of mine, and I can’t wait to get there and make you all laugh. Get ready Dubai!"

Tickets will start at Dhs195 and go up to Dhs695 and will be available to buy on Thursday (November 14) at www.coca-cola-arena.com.

Famed for his stand up shows, his podcast The Koy Pond with Jo Koy by Starburns Audio and his recent Netflix special Comin’ In Hot, the comedian is currently on his first leg of the Just Kidding Tour – which promises all new material. He will also be performing in Australia, New Zealand, the US and Philippines. You may also know him from US talk show Chelsea Lately, in which he’s appeared in more than 140 episodes.

Koy, who started out performing at a Las Vegas coffee house, now has shows around the world using material inspired by his family.

In 2018, Koy was named “Stand-Up Comedian Of The Year” at the Just For Laughs Comedy Festival in Montreal and this year 2019, the comedian reached number one on the Billboard Charts for his stand-up comedy album, Live From Seattle.

Don’t miss your chance to see him live in Dubai.
