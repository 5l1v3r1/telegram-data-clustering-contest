Parineeti Chopra opts out of 'Bhuj: The Pride Of India'; here's why!

11 Nov 2019, 19:25 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Parineeti Chopra has several films lined up ahead and she is currently working on biopic on ace badminton player Saina Nehwal. After completing shooting for the biopic, the actress will start filming the Hindi remake of Hollywood flick 'The Girl On The Train' remake.

And according to a report, Parineeti has backed out from her other project 'Bhuj: The Pride Of India'. Reportedly, the actress was keen to be the part of the film, however, she couldn't allot her dates for the projects due to her prior commitments.
