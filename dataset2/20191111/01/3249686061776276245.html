Sungmin brings on the Christmas spirit early in cover image for his solo debut mini-album 'Orgel'

11 Nov 2019, 01:19 by jennywill

The former Super Junior member will be debuting solo on November 22nd with his first mini-album 'Orgel'. The mini-album includes 5 songs include the title song "Orgel", and will feature a warm healing moment for listeners with Sungmin's soft voice.
