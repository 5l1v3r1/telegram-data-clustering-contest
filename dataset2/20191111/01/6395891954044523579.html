Hundreds of needles, medical supplies found along California beach

11 Nov 2019, 01:33 by Jared Leone, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

LOS ANGELES - Hundreds of pieces of medical supplies, including needles, were found Saturday along the shore of Venice Beach.

Lifeguards saw “a large amount” of medical supplies littered along the beach around 11:30 a.m., Los Angeles County Fire Department officials said. It is unclear where the items came from.
