Watch Plymouth Argyle goalkeeper Mike Cooper excel against Everton in Premier League Cup tie

The 20-year-old was in outstanding form as the Pilgrims drew 1-1 away to cup holders Everton

11 Nov 2019, 10:11 by Chris Errington

Goalkeeper Mike Cooper was in outstanding form as Plymouth Argyle battled out a 1-1 draw away to Everton in the Premier League Cup on Sunday.

The 20-year-old made a number of superb saves to frustrate cup holders Everton in the Group B game, which was played at Southport FC.

Although designated as an under-23s’ competition, clubs are allowed to play a maximum of three over-age outfield players.

Argyle made the most of that opportunity by including 31-year-old attacking duo Billy Clarke and Ryan Taylor in their team.
