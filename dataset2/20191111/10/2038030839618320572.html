Did Prince Harry just hint at baby no. 2?

11 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Bashiera Parker (@bparks_)

Cape Town – Did Prince Harry just hint at baby number two?

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex visited army families last week in Windsor where they spoke about the sacrifices made by servicemen and women, as well as what it must be like to miss your little ones growing up.

"I can't imagine what it's like to miss so much as they change so quickly," Harry said before revealing Archie is crawling, while Meghan told a little girl who handed her a rose that Archie's got two little teeth.

According to Forces Network, Prince Harry also seemed interested in what the family dynamics are like after introducing a second child.

Army wife Susie Stringfellow told the military website, "Harry was really quite interested in how things were with second children as well... We were trying to encourage him to have a second baby."

Well, chances are, they probably will.

Earlier in the year, Prince Harry also revealed to Dr Jane Goodall in their conversation for Meghan's Forces For Change Vogue issue how many siblings he's planning on giving Archie. Revealing that the climate crisis helped the couple make the decision together, he said, "Two, maximum."
