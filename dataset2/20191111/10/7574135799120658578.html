YBN Cordae touring in 2020

11 Nov 2019, 10:48 by Andrew Sacher

YBN Cordae is currently on the road opening for Logic (alongside J.I.D) in support of his very good debut album The Lost Boy, and he has now begun announcing headlining tour dates for 2020.

The newly-added dates include a Brooklyn show on February 11 at Music Hall of Williamsburg. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday (11/15) at 10 AM. More dates appear to be TBA, but all currently known dates are listed below.
