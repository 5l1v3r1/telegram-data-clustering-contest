The Chase's Mark Labbett slams 'snowflake' Piers Morgan after shocking performance

While appearing on The Chase, Piers managed to get three correct answers on the show, and then took the lower offer of £1000

11 Nov 2019, 10:31 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

A star of popular ITV1 show The Chase has branded Piers Morgan a word he will no doubt shudder at: "Snowflake."

Piers is a presenter of Good Morning Britain and routinely makes headlines with his controversial opinions.

Regularly involved in explosive debates and rows on GMB, Piers is a controversial former newspaper editor.

And he was today branded a ‘snowflake’ by The Chase's Mark Labbett, aka The Beast.

While appearing on The Chase, Piers managed to get three correct answers on the show, and then took the lower offer of £1000.

Meanwhile, co-host Susanna Reid bagged an incredible £80,000 on the show.

Mark joked: “I’m heartbroken.

“You’ve moulded yourself as the epitome of English manliness for years.

“You deal with princes and presidents.

“And then you absolutely crack like the snowflake you are!”

The GMB stars failed to defeat The Beast, who caught Piers, Susanna and Kate in the final round with 37 seconds to spare.

Viewers of the popular show were outraged with Piers’ performance, after the host slammed Richard Madeley for taking the lower offer.

 

One said: “Piers Morgan taking the lower offer on The Chase has cemented my hatred towards him. Colossal nonce.”

Another said: “So Piers moaned at Richard for going low and he’s gone low himself #numpty.”

And one viewer who had been on the show himself said: “At peace that I scored higher than Piers Morgan in the cash builder round of The Chase and I didn’t take the lower offer.”
