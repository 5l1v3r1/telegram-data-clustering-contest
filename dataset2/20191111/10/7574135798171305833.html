Wolf Parade announce 2020 North American tour (BrooklynVegan presale for NYC)

11 Nov 2019, 10:18 by Andrew Sacher

Wolf Parade‘s new album Thin Mind was announced earlier today, along with a stream of new single “Forest Green,” and now a 2020 North American tour has been announced too. The first leg has support from Land of Talk and the second leg is with Jo Passed.

The run with Jo Passed includes a NYC show on February 24 at Brooklyn Steel. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday (11/15) with a BrooklynVegan presale starting Wednesday (11/13) at noon. Chec back here Wednesday morning for the presale password. All dates are listed below.

Thin Mind comes out January 24 via Sub Pop.
