Masilela Opens Up On His Return

11 Nov 2019, 11:44 by Soccer Laduma

Tsepo Masilela made his debut for AmaZulu on the weekend as a late substitute in the 2-0 loss against Chippa United.   

The former Kaizer Chiefs defender was signed by AmaZulu on Saturday after he impressed the team’s head coach Jozef Vukusic in training.

The 34-year-old left-back has been without a club since leaving Kaizer Chiefs at the end of the 2017/18 season.  

“It is a great feeling to be given another opportunity to prove my worth... I have been here for a while now, getting to know everyone and seeing how things work. I have spoken to the coach and he has made it clear, what is expected of me.

"I know it won’t be easy to break into the first team but we have some good players and it shows on the pitch as we have been winning lately, so I look forward to pushing each other and hopefully we can have a successful season,” said the defender, as quoted on AmaZulu’s official website.
