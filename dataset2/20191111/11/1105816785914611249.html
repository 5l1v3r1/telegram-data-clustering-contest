Ranveer Singh looks exactly like Kapil Dev in upcoming film

11 Nov 2019, 11:12 by Entertainment Desk

In an upcoming Bollywood film called ’83, based on India’s victory in the 1983 Cricket World Cup, Ranveer Singh plays the former Indian cricket captain Kapil Dev.

On Monday, the actor shared a still from the film on his social media handles, in which the cricketer is looking at the ball after playing the famous Natraj shot.
