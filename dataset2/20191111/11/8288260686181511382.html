YouTube goes down across UK as video streaming site crashes in Europe and Japan

The problems on Monday, November 11, are affecting users in the UK, parts of Europe and Japan

11 Nov 2019, 11:11 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

YouTube has crashed for users across the UK this morning.

According to Down Detector the issues started at around 10:55 GMT.

The problems on Monday, November 11, are affecting users in the UK, parts of Europe and Japan.

Of those who reported issues, 48% said they couldn’t watch video.

47% had problems with the website, they said, and 3% couldn’t log-in.

YouTube has yet to comment on the outage.
