IKEA UAE celebrates Singles Day with Dhs1 breakfast

Tuck into four breakfast items for an incredibly affordable price this week

11 Nov 2019, 11:30 by Darragh Murphy

Who said you being single meant being lonely? IKEA UAE certainly doesn’t think so, as it wants all the singles out there to enjoy a bargain.

To celebrate Singles Day today (Monday November 11), IKEA restaurants across the UAE have two specially discounted meals on offer.

Until Tuesday November 12, shoppers around the UAE can dig into the Swedish furniture store's breakfast menu for just Dhs1. Better yet, you can choose four items.

From its sausages and scrambled eggs to its classic has browns and baked beans, pick four of what you want and give over a single dirham. IKEA’s meals we’re already a bargain, but this takes the cake.

That’s not all, as the IKEA restaurant will also be offering its famed salmon balls with a soft drink to wash it all down with for Dhs11. Not to worry, plant-eaters of Dubai, as there are also veggie options available at the same price.

The offer is available at IKEA Dubai in Dubai Festival City and Yas Mall in Abu Dhabi.
