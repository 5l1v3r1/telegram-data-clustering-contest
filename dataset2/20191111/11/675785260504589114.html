Daniel James' teammate recalls rise from "funny little fat kid" to Man Utd star

James, 22, has enjoyed an impressive start to his career at Old Trafford after arriving from boyhood club Swansea in a £18million summer switch

11 Nov 2019, 11:39 by Matt Maltby, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/matt-maltby/

Daniel James' Wales teammate and close pal Connor Roberts has given a fascinating insight into the Manchester United starlet's stunning rise to stardom.

James, 22, has enjoyed an impressive start to his career at Old Trafford after arriving from boyhood club Swansea in a £18million summer switch, netting three goals in his first four games.

Although his exploits in front of goal have taken a sudden dip - he has gone 12 games in all competitions without scoring - James is regarded as one of the most exciting talents in the Premier League.

He was so desperate to make an instant impact at Old Trafford that he jetted off for a warm-weather training camp in Dubai on his own - and eventually topped all the initial pre-season fitness tests.

 

But the Welsh wing wizard hasn't always been in tip-top shape, as revealed by Swansea defender Roberts.

Reminiscing about James' emergence for Swansea's U23s, Roberts told The Athletic : “My first thoughts were, ‘You wouldn’t think he is as quick as he is.’ I thought he was a funny little fat kid.

“He had the pace — that’s what he thrives on. We always used to joke that he’s the fastest chubby kid we’ve ever seen.

"In recent years, he has got more toned but when he first came, he almost had a little bit of a belly on him. I was like, ‘Well, he can’t be that fast.’ But obviously he is.”

“He is a proper professional. Nights before games, he was on the foam roller, stretching, getting himself ready."

Roberts also opened up on James heartbreak after his father Kevan tragically passed away days before he sealed his move to United.

He added: “Every time I shared rooms with him, he would be on the phone to his mum or dad.

"What happened with his dad is even more heart-breaking because he was so close to him.”
