K-Media Criticizes YG for Media Playing Blackpink's Nomination and Concert Attendance Numbers

11 Nov 2019, 11:25 by Sooold

source:http://www.mediaus.co.kr/news/...

YG Entertainment published a press release stating Blackpink won in 3 categories at E! People's Choice Awards.

The title of the press release said, "Blackpink Won in 3 categories at E!People's Choice Awards - Group of 2019, The Music Video of 2019 and The Concert tour of 2019."

However, YG had published the press release before E! People's Choice Awards announced the result. Then YG Entertainment changed the title from "Blackpink Won in 3 categories" to "Blackpink is nominated in 3 categories..." However a lot of people saw the article and K-Media already started to write about it.

K-media has also criticized YG for media playing Blackpink's dome tour concert attendance numbers. They stated, "YG also issued a press release on Blackpink's Japan Tokyo Dome tour and stated, 'Blackpink is expected to mobilize a total of 205,000 spectators through 3 dome concerts.' However, even TVXQ, BTS and TWICE were never close to mobilizing 200,000 spectators through 3 dome concerts."
