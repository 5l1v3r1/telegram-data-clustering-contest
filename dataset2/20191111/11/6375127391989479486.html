Man hangs self

11 Nov 2019, 11:41 by Edd Reyes

A STAY-IN maintenance man said to be suffering from a lingering pain in the stomach reportedly ended his life by hanging himself inside his rented room in Pandacan, Manila yesterday morning.

PMSG Jonathan Bautista of the Manila Police District-homicide section identified the victim as Alvin Guinondola, 29, of 1901 Anita Building, West Zamora Street, Pandacan.

Police said his body was discovered at  8:16 a.m. inside his room located at the second floor.

Authorities are now investigating his death, although it was gathered that the victim had complained of stomach pain at around 8 p.m., or hours before his death.
