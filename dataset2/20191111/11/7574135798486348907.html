Black Lips announce 2020 tour in support of upcoming album

11 Nov 2019, 11:34 by Bill Pearis

The Black Lips have announced a tour in support of their upcoming album, Sing in a World That’s Falling Apart. The tour kicks off a couple dates before the album’s January 24 release date and currently runs through mid-March. NYC-area stops include Asbury Lanes on February 21 and Music Hall of Williamsburg on February 24. Tickets for Asbury Lanes and MHOW go on sale Friday, November 15 at 10 AM with presales starting Wednesday (11/13) at 10 AM and noon, respectively.
