Laffor The Latest Star To Venture Into Fashion

11 Nov 2019, 11:22 by SL Social

Anthony Laffor has joined a host of PSL stars who have ventured in the world of fashion, when the Mamelodi Sundowns man recently launched his own brand.

Laffor who has been sidelined with a reccuring knee injury for a lenghthy spell has had some time to try his hand at an industry of a different kind.

The Liberian joins the likes of Teko Modise, Morgan Gould, Oupa Manyisa, Kermit Erasmus and Mokgakalodi Ngele in launching his own brand.
