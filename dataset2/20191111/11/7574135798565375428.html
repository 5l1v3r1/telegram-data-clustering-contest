They Might Be Giants add more ‘Flood’ dates, including 5th NYC show

11 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Bill Pearis

They Might Be Giants will be spending 2020 on tour celebrating the 30th anniversary of their third album, Flood. They’ve added more dates since our last post and have just announced a fifth NYC show, happening May 9 at Bowery Ballroom. (Their other four Bowery shows are sold out). Tickets go on sale Friday, November 15 at 10 AM with a Citi cardmember presale starting Tuesday (11/12) at 10 AM.

This weekend, TMBG will appear on Live from Here with Chris Thile from Town Hall on 11/16 along with Big Thief, Aoife O’Donovan, and Marina Franklin (tickets). Updated tour dates are listed, along with a video press kit for Flood from back in the day, below.
