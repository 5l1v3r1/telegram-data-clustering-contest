YouTube is down: Video streaming site crashes for users across the UK

BREAKING: According to Down Detector the issues started at around 10:55 GMT and are affecting users in the UK, parts of Europe and Japan

11 Nov 2019, 11:06 by Shivali Best, https://www.facebook.com/shivalibestjournalist/

It’s the go-to video streaming service for many people, but it seems that YouTube has crashed for users across the UK this morning.

According to Down Detector the issues started at around 10:55 GMT and are affecting users in the UK, parts of Europe and Japan.

While YouTube is yet to comment on the outage, of those who reported issues, 48% said they couldn’t watch video, 47% had problems with the website, and 3% couldn’t log-in.

Mirror Online has contacted YouTube for comment.
