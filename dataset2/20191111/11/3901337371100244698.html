Bundesliga Star Sparks Mass Brawl

11 Nov 2019, 11:33 by SL International

Chaos descended on the Bundesliga on Sunday, when a mass brawl between Eintracht Frankfurt and SC Freiburg resulted in one manager lying on the floor and two red cards being dished out.

Click through the gallery above to see some images of the brawl between Frankfurt and Freiburg.

Eintracht suffered a 1-0 league defeat to Freiburg on Sunday, courtesy of Nils Petersen's goal, in a match that was marred by ugly scenes just before the final whistle.

The incident occurred during the dying stages of the match, when Frankfurt captain David Abraham appeared to intentionally charge into Freiburg boss Christian Streich on the sidelines.

The 54-year-old manager was knocked to the ground, which sparked a mass brawl between players and staff of the two clubs.
