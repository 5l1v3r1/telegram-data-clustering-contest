1 killed, 39 arrested in Mindanao anti-crime raids

11 Nov 2019, 11:20 by Alfred P. Dalizon

AGENTS of the Police Regional Office 12 yesterday launched simultaneous anti-crime raids that resulted in the killing of an armed drug trafficking suspect and the arrest of 39 others, PRO12 director Brig. Gen. Alfred Corpus said.

The official said that PRO12 agents, backed  by operatives from the Philippine National Police Special Action Force, Criminal Investigation and Detection Group, Highway Patrol Group, Intelligence Group and the Army, simultaneously served 78 search warrants for illegal possession of drugs, firearms and explosives against the targets in different parts of General Santos City, Sultan Kudarat, South Cotabato and Sarangani provinces.

In a report to PNP Officer-in-Charge Lt. Gen. Archie Francisco Gamboa, Corpus said an armed drug target was killed in a gunfight with operatives.

Arrested were 39 other suspects.

Seized during the operations were 32 assorted unlicensed firearms, six hand grenades, 372 pieces of assorted live ammunition and an estimated P500,000 worth of shabu and dried marijuana leaves.

Corpus said that appropriate criminal charges are being readied against the  arrested suspects.
