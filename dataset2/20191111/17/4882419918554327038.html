Spanish Super Cup in Saudi Arabia: Barca to face Atletico, Madrid v Valencia

Ernesto Valverde's team were drawn to face Atletico in the tournament in January

11 Nov 2019, 17:29 by Ivan San Antonio

Barcelona face Atletico in the Spanish Super Cup semi-finals in Saudi Arabia, it was decided Monday.

The game will take place on January 9, with the final against Real Madrid or Valencia on January 12.
