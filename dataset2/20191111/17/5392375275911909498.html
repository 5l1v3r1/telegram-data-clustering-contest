11 Nov 2019, 17:34 by Argam Artashyan

Rate this post

Yesterday, VIVO published another poster of the upcoming VIVO S5. It turns out with the launch of this handset, the manufacturer has taken the selfie camera beauty algorithm a step forward. At the same time, as we can see on the poster, the appearance of the phone is consistent with the previous leaks. The back cover’s blue-green gradient effect brings a quite recognition rate to the phone.

As for the so-called ‘5-fold super-quality beauty’, this is the new beauty concept developed by VIVO. We can see that the VIVO S5 will have a major upgrade in the self-portrait beauty function. The number ‘5’ should mean that the VIVO S5 will beautify in many aspects to achieve a more textured effect and meet the strict requirements of young people who love self-portraits.

In fact, in terms of selfies, VIVO has always been in the leading position in the industry. From the first front soft light to the full scene selfie experience, to today’s ‘5-fold super-quality beauty’, VIVO has always been committed to optimizing the pre-selfie effect. The first generation of the VIVO S series, the VIVO S1 Pro, features a 32-megapixel lifting front camera with an upgraded AI beauty and light and shadow effect.

As for other selling points, the VIVO S5 will come with a unique gradient effect. It is worth mentioning that the handset uses a punch-hole design to achieve a larger screen ratio. As shown above, the hole’s diameter on the upper right area is extremely small.

We also believe this phone will bring more surprises. It’s set to come on November 14. So not that long is left.
