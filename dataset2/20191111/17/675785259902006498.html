Gary Neville says Man Utd still need up to five more transfers

The former Red Devils full-back pinpointed key areas where United need to strengthen although admitted he feels the club have "something to work with" among the current crop

11 Nov 2019, 17:39 by Darren Wells, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/darren-wells/

Gary Neville has claimed that Man Utd still need "four or five" new signings despite their recent spell of good form.

The Red Devils saw off Brighton 3-1 on Sunday to bump themselves up to seventh in the table.

They are now on a run five wins from their last six in all competitions, with the only blemish a 1-0 loss away at Bournemouth .

But even though United's new additions have all been performing well, Neville feels more transfers are imperative.

He told Sky Sports: "It's been a really good month for Manchester United , barring the Bournemouth defeat.

"That game was frustrating and took us back a few steps but generally it's been a better month than the one before.

"You can see some real excitement with Marcus Rashford, Anthony Martial, Mason Greenwood and Daniel James, there's something to work on there.

"Defensively, with Harry Maguire and Aaron Wan-Bissaka, there's something to work on there too.

"The young boy Brandon Williams gives me a great deal of hope."

Adding on his own podcast, Neville said: "Look, Manchester United need four or five players with real quality and experience.

"They really need another centre-back, they need a left-back, and a couple of central midfielders and a striker or a forward.

"That's not going to change and won't change entirely even after January. But there's something there."

United have been linked with a move for their former striker Zlatan Ibrahimovic, which would see him make a sensational return in January.

The Swedish veteran is out of contract at MLS side LA Galaxy where he has continued to bang in goals on a regular basis.

His experience would be invaluable for the club's youngsters, and reports in Italy suggest that United will battle AC Milan for his signature, despite speculation he has already agreed a deal at the San Siro.

United chiefs are also said to be weighing up a move for another of their former stars Wilfried Zaha.

The Crystal Palace winger came close to leaving Selhurst Park in the summer, with Arsenal and Everton both in talks.

The Sun now report that United will look to bring Zaha back to Old Trafford, where he had a spell from 2013-15.
