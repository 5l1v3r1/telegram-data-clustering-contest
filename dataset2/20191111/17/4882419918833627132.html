Guillermo Amor: Barca accept playing in Saudi Arabia

The club director spoke about the tournament in January 2020

11 Nov 2019, 17:48 by FE/Sport EN

Guillermo Amor, Barca's director of institutional relations, spoke about the Spanish Super Cup draw.

"It's different to other years," he said of the new set-up. "The destination is a bit further, but that's what the Federation decided and we accept that decision.

"It's true that it's after Christmas, but that's not a problem either, we have to be ready for it, do the best we can and win it, that is about mentality and thinking."

He said "it would be very nice" if the final was a Clasico but "you have to have a lot of respect" for Atletico, Barca's opponents and Valencia.
