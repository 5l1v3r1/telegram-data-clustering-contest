Tyson Fury claims he gets mistaken for Brad Pitt and George Clooney 'all the time'

Lineal heavyweight champion and WWE star says women hit on him everywhere he goes

11 Nov 2019, 05:39 by Gareth Bicknell

Tyson Fury has claimed women "hit on him" everywhere he goes - and not just because he's a world-famous boxer.

The lineal heavyweight champion says he is up there with the likes of heart-throbs Brad Pitt and George Clooney.

Fury has been raising his profile of late, making appearances in WWE as well as teasing a potential transition from boxing into MMA .

The self-styled 'Gypsy King' - who is married to Paris, mum to their five children - is famous on both sides of the Atlantic.

But he said he even gets plenty of female attention when he goes to countries where he is not known as a boxer.

“Do women hit on me? All the time!" said Fury in quotes reported by the Daily Star .

"Everywhere I go. And not just because they know who I am.

“Seriously, I could go to foreign countries where people don’t know me and they’re still hitting on me.

"Because I’m tall, dark and handsome. Good dress sense. They’re like: ‘Who is this guy? Is he Brad Pitt? George Clooney?’”

Fury also claims to wear a three-piece suit all the time - even at home when lounging around or doing night feeds and changing nappies.

He said: "I’ve created a monster, haven’t I? I’ve created this flamboyant character, so now if I don’t turn up dressed the part, people are disappointed. It’s the brand. It’s just part of the job.”

Meanwhile, Fury has hinted he may never again box in the UK - casting doubt on potential future fights with the likes of Deontay Wilder and Anthony Joshua.

Speaking from the US about the UK boxing scene, the Gypsy King told the Times : "They had their chance.

"They didn’t treat me well. Over here, I get treated like a superstar.”
