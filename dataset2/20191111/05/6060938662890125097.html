Exclusive: Karan Johar to host a party for Katy Perry ahead of music concert in Mumbai

11 Nov 2019, 05:23 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

American singer-songwriter Katy Perry will touchdown in Mumbai city this week, to perform at an upcoming music festival. But before she can get the show on the road, we hear that Bollywood director and producer, Karan Johar will host a grand party at his residence later this week.

KJo, who is known to host some of the biggest parties in town, will be hosting a cocktail and dinner for Katy and might just have his Bollywood brigade over to party the night away. Karan has partied it up with several Hollywood stars and even hosted some over at his residence. He most recently had a bash for actor Will Smith and even threw a party for director Darren Aronofsky on his visit to the city.

Katy, who is no stranger to India, will perform LIVE for the first time in the city of Mumbai. She expressed her excitement about returning to India in a statement that read, “I am so happy to be returning to India and excited to headline my first-ever performance in Mumbai. I am looking forward to seeing and singing with all of my Indian KatyCats.”

Katy will be sharing the stage with British Pop sensation Dua Lipa, who will also be performing at the big gig this coming weekend.

Although this will be her debut show in Mumbai, Perry previously performed at the opening ceremony of a T20 League in 2012 in Chennai. Prior to that, she had even visited the country on a personal trip with her then actor-comedian beau Russell Brand, who reportedly proposed to her at the Taj Mahal. They later tied the knot in a lavish ceremony in Rajasthan. They sadly got divorced two years later.
