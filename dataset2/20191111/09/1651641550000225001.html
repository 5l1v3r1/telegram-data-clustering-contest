New leak reveals the colour options of Samsung Galaxy S11 series

11 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Anmol

We recently reported that Samsung might be launching three S11 smartphones with support for LTE and 5G. The Samsung Galaxy S11 is expected to launch early next year. According to the rumours, we could see the device at MWC 2020 in Barcelona.

Now a new rumour has revealed the colours of the upcoming Samsung smartphone. According to the popular smartphone insider Ishan Agarwal (via MySmartPrice), Samsung Galaxy S11 will come in Black, Grey, and Blue colour options. The cheaper Galaxy S11e will be offered in Blue, Grey, and Pink colours.

Apart from that, Samsung is also working on Galaxy S10 and Note 10 Lite. The Galaxy S10 Lite will be offered in Black, Blue, and White while the Note 10 Lite will be available in Red, Silver and Black colours. There’s no word on when the Lite versions of Note 10 and S10 will be available.

Galaxy S11 series, on the other hand, will be launched around MWC 2020 and will be available around March of 2020. The device is expected to receive a significant bump in specs and is expected to come with a 108 MP camera.
