Yasiin Bey (Mos Def) doing another ‘Black On Both Sides’ show in NYC this month

11 Nov 2019, 09:13 by Andrew Sacher

Yasiin Bey (fka Mos Def) recently celebrated the 20th anniversary of his 1999 debut solo album at a sold-out Brooklyn Steel show with surprise guests Pharoahe Monch, Dave Chappelle, and Tiffany Haddish, and it must have went well because he already scheduled another Black on Both Sides NYC show happening in just a few weeks. It goes down Saturday, November 30 at Sony Hall, starting at 11:59 PM. Tickets are on sale now. Will any surprise guests show up at this one too?

Yasiin Bey also has shows in Philly and DC this month, and a Black Star show with Talib Kweli in Boston on 11/29. He’s got Europe/UK tour dates after that, all of which are listed here. His Negus listening installation opens at Brooklyn Museum on Friday (11/15).
