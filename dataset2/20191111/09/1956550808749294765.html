International superstar Maluma to headline Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena

Colombian megastar set to make his UAE debut in early 2020

11 Nov 2019, 09:19 by Tim Skinner

Hot off the heels of Iron Maiden are coming to Dubai for the first time in ten years, another absolutely massive act has confirmed a date at the city’s Coca-Cola Arena.

Maluma, the Latin Grammy Award-winning Columbian singer-songwriter will be heading to the UAE on Friday February 14 – so make it a date.

With billions of YouTube streams, and nearly 100 million subscribers and followers across social media, the 25-year-old is one of the biggest artists in the world right now. His 2017 Maluma World Tour sold more than one million tickets across 105 concerts around the world, cementing his status as the world’s top concert-selling Latin artist.

His 11:11 World Tour is promising a “state-of-the-art show”, full of cutting edge visual effects and technology, as well as a live band with 12 backing dancers.

Expect massive tunes like 11PM Qué Pena and No Se Me Quita (featuring Ricky Martin, who last year headlined the Emirates Airline Dubai Jazz Festival – check out pictures here and full details of tickets for 2019’s festival here, which will feature Lauryn Hill, Lionel Richie and OneRepublic).
