7 meal deals to try in Dubai this week

Eat, Sip, Save your way around the city

11 Nov 2019, 08:21 by Time Out Dubai staff

Burger and drink deal at Burger & Lobster
If you buy any burger and a house drink, you’ll get it all for Dhs99.
Open Sat-Wed noon-midnight, Thu-Fri noon-2am. Burj Daman Building, DIFC (04 514 8838).

Dhs89 menu at Paul
This popular, family-friendly French bakery has a brand-new set menu for just Dhs89.
Various locations across Dubai including The Dubai Mall and Mercato Mall, www.paularabia.com.

Meat platter at City Grill
Throughout November you’ll be able to get a meat platter full of ribs, steak, boerewors, lamb chops and chicken for Dhs140.
Open Sun-Fri 5pm-midnight, Sat 2pm-midnight. The Atrium, Al Habtoor City (04 437 0088).

Pairing menu at Masala Library by Jiggs Kalra
This fancy Indian restaurant is laying on a special menu, plus a grape pairing for Dhs315.
Open daily 6pm-midnight. JW Marriott Marquis Dubai, Business Bay (04 414 3000).

Pizza and mixed drink combination at Boardwalk
Enjoy a classic margherita (Dhs55) and you’ll get a Mexican mixed drink thrown in for free.
Open daily noon-midnight. Dubai Creek Golf & Yacht Club, Deira (04 205 4647).

Sandwich deal at The Blacksmith
Try the belly-busting Texas Brisket Sandwich a pint for Dhs69.
Open Sun-Wed 5pm-2am, Thu-Sat 5pm-3am. Wyndham Dubai Marina, Dubai Marina (04 407 8873).

Three-course set menu at Observatory Bar & Grill
This sky-high restaurant is serving a three-course menu for Dhs199.
Open daily noon-1am. Dubai Marriott Harbour Hotel & Suites (04 319 4000).
