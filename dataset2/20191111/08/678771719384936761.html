Market Pulse on November 8th

11 Nov 2019, 08:05 by Olivier Rigot, EMC Gestion de Fortune

S&P 500 3093,08 +7,90.

Over the last week, the stock market continued to creep higher with less momentum and deteriorating sentiment. The market is clearly overbought, according to various indicators we monitor and our global risk profile index is hitting the highs of its historical trading band. The best scenario would call for a quick pullback to the breaking point in the area of 3000-3040.
