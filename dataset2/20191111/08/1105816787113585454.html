Badminton star Mahoor Shehzad wins gold in 2019 Pakistan International Series

11 Nov 2019, 08:52 by Sports Desk

Pakistan’s best badminton player Mahoor Shehzad won the gold medal in single’s event of the 2019 Pakistan International Series.

Mahoor beat Iranian opponent Soraya 21-15, 16-21 and 21-16 in the final to bag the three-set match 2-1.

The badminton star took to twitter to announce her win and said: “I’m delighted to have won the only Gold medal for Paksitan in this tournament.”
