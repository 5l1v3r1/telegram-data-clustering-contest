The 12 Best Drugstore Shampoos Under $20

11 Nov 2019, 08:51

Here’s the cool thing about shampoo: You don’t need to spend a ton of money to get one that really, truly works. Seriously! Some of the best shampoos out there can be found at the drugstore. “However, you do want to make sure the shampoo you’re using, regardless of the price, is tailored to your hair’s specific needs, so you’re not wasting your time or money,” explains Giovanni Vaccaro, Glamsquad’s artistic director for hair. Ahead are 12 best drugstore shampoos—all under $20—that target specific hair types and textures to make your hair look (and feel!) freaking amazing.
