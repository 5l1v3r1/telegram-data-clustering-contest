Wolf Parade releasing new album ‘Thin Mind’ in 2020, share new song “Forest Green”

11 Nov 2019, 08:56 by Andrew Sacher

Wolf Parade recently released the new song “Against the Day” off their heavily-teased, John Goodmanson-produced fifth album, and today they shared a second single and officially announced the album. It’s called Thin Mind, and it’s due January 24 via Sub Pop. It’s the band’s second since ending hiatus (following 2017’s Cry Cry Cry), and first since they became a trio, following the departure of Dante DeCaro.

“Against the Day” was a very collaborative sounding song where Spencer Krug and Dan Boeckner traded vocals, but new song “Forest Green” is a classic-Boeckner-style synth-punk ripper, and both are good stuff. Listen to the new song and check out the tracklist below. That’s the album artwork above.
