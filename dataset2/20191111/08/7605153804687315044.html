MWINGI, Kenya, Nov 11 – Police in Mwingi are probing an incident where unknown assailants killed a tutor who also doubled up as a clinical officer attached the local Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC) campus.

The body of Eunice Kiema was found next to her car on Monday morning near a local market.

He eyes, according to police, were gorged out.

Alex Mutemi, a senior sub-county police officer said part of the victim’s legs had burns and several stumps of a sharp object.
