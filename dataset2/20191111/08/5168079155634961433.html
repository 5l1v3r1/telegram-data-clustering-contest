In Pictures: Muscat Municipality begins clean-up after heavy rain

11 Nov 2019, 08:39 by Times News Service

Muscat: Muscat Municipality is making a great efforts to deal with the effects of the weather by cleaning the dusty roads and disposing waste and open the paths for the accumulated water to flow.

In a statement issued the Municipality said. "Municipal teams continue to deal with the effects of climatic conditions, by disposing of waste, cleaning roads from dust, suctioning rainwater pools, and opening paths to cross the water collected from different places in the governorate wilayats."
