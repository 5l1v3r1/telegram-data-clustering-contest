Gallery: Germany, Spain & Belgium Unveil Euro 2020 Kits

11 Nov 2019, 12:28 by SL International

Record-sharing three-time UEFA European Championship winners Germany and Spain, as well as Belgium, have released their new kits for the 2020 edition of the competition.

Click through the gallery above to see Belgium, Germany and Spain's kits for the 2020 UEFA European Championship!

On Monday, adidas released the kits that Belgium, Germany and Spain will wear at Euro 2020, which will be played across various nations on the continent. 

Die Mannschaft's new round-neck strip bears white as its primary colour and is adorned with horizontal black pinstripes, while the sleeves feature the nation's colours of black, red and gold.

Belgium's kit is a traditional red with a diagonal black track, with the neckline and sleeves both sporting black and gold stripes. 

La Furia Roja's new playing strip celebrates the 100th anniversary of Spain's first federation shirt, and is presented as a red and burgundy abstract of the country's flag, with adidas' three stripes present in gold on the sleeves.
