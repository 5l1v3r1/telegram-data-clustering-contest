Lata Mangeshkar recovering after viral infection, fans say “Get well super soon”

11 Nov 2019, 12:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood singer Lata Mangeshkar was admitted to Breach Candy Hospital's Intensive Care Unit after she complained of breathing problems. The celebrated singer was in a critical condition and was admitted to the hospital in the wee hours of Monday. In a statement, Lata Mangeshkar’s niece said, “Lata didi suffered from a viral chest infection so we got her to the hospital. But now, she is on a path of recovery. Thank you for your concern.”

Soon after news of Lata Mangeshkar being admitted made headlines, fans of the 90 year old singer flooded social media with posts of ‘get well soon’.
