Salting process begins ahead of messy commute as major snowfall hits GTA

11 Nov 2019, 12:08 by Sean Davidson, Multi-Platform Writer, CTV News Toronto

Salting trucks were out on Toronto roads in full force ahead of a forecasted messy commute caused by the city’s first major storm of the season.

Around 11:30 a.m., city officials said that salt trucks were heading to Toronto’s expressways and that “spot salting” had started on main roads, hills and bridges.

The trucks started to sprinkle salt on local roads around 1 p.m.
