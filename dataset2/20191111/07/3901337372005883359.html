'Messi Is Uncontrollable'

11 Nov 2019, 07:34 by Kurt Buckerfield

Following Lionel Messi's remarkable hat-trick for FC Barcelona on the weekend, during which he scored two free-kick goals, it has been claimed the Argentine is someone you simply cannot plan for.

Click through the gallery above to see some pictures of Messi's triple on Saturday night.

In the Catalan club's 4-1 win over Celta Vigo in LaLiga on Saturday, Messi opened the scoring with a calmly-taken penalty before finding the back of the net with two superbly-struck free-kicks.

The Argentine has now scored as many LaLiga hat-tricks as Cristiano Ronaldo did during his time in Spain, and has also now scored four direct free-kicks in the past month of football.

Celta coach Oscar Garcia was full of praise for the 32-year-old after the match, insisting the game would have been completely different had he not played.

"With Messi, you never have the game under control," he said, according to ESPN.

"He's uncontrollable, he throws all plans up in the air.

"His efficiency from free-kicks is incredible, his percentages are so high. You can't avoid giving free-kicks away there, either. He alone changes games.

"If he didn't play, it would have been very different. We could even have won this one.

"He conditions everything. You know something could happen every time he has the ball, but when it's a set-play, you cannot control it."
