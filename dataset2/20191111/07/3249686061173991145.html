VICTON show off their style in their everyday clothes version of 'Nostalgic Night' dance practice

11 Nov 2019, 07:37 by jennywill

VICTON has dropped an everyday clothes version of "Nostalgic Night".

The boys are back for the first time as a 6-member group without leader Seungwoo. The song is the boys' first comeback in a year and a half, and they've dropped a special dance practice version. VICTON members are wearing their own clothes for the dance practice, so fans can see what their everyday fashion is like.
