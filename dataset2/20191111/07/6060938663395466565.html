'Bala' box office collection Day 3: The Ayushmann Khurrana starrer sees an excellent first weekend

11 Nov 2019, 07:03 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Amar Kaushik directorial comedy-drama 'Bala' starring Ayushmann Khurrana in the lead role has witnessed an excellent first-weekend collection at the box office. The film which opened to a good start maintained very good gains over the weekend with pretty good collections. The film has been garnering praises from all over with positive reviews and critical appreciation hailing the film.

As per the report in Boxofficeindia.com, on Sunday, the film has raked in a total of Rs 17.50 crore on its third day at the box office. The first-weekend collection of the film stands at a total of Rs 42 crore at the box office.

The film remains a little short of what Ayushmann Khurrana's previous film 'Dream Girl' did at the box office in its first three days. 'Dream Girl' managed to rake in a total of Rs 43.22 crore in its first weekend. 'Bala' has scored better numbers than 'Dream Girl' at most of the bigger multiplexes, but collections in mass centers and single screens were better for 'Dream Girl'.

'Bala' is Ayushmann Khurrana's biggest release till date. The film released across 3000 screens in India and more than 550 screens in the overseas markets.

Directed by Amar Kaushik, 'Bala' is a film set in Kanpur that revolves around a man suffering from premature balding and the lack of confidence and societal pressure which runs with it. The film also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles.
