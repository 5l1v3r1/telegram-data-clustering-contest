Missing endangered woman Elza Guerrier reported in Cape Coral

11 Nov 2019, 07:48 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- Police want you to be on the lookout for a missing Cape Coral woman who could be in danger.

67-year-old Elza Guerrier was last seen walking out of her home on NE 13th Avenue, around 4:40 in the afternoon Sunday.

Police believe they saw her walking on Diplomat Parkway and Del Prado Boulevard, shortly after that.

She was wearing a pink and orange outfit. She is possibly holding a Bible.

If you have any information, call Cape Coral Police at 239-574-3223.
