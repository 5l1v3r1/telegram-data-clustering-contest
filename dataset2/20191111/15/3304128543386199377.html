First Uganda Airlines Flight To Mombasa Lands At Moi International Airport

11 Nov 2019, 15:12 by Mercy Auma

Uganda Airlines has commenced its direct flights to Kenya, with the first flight landing at the Moi International Airport from Entebbe.

The maiden flight has successfully landed, with 76 passengers on board and a full working crew.

Additionally, the airline is expected to fly to Mombasa three times a week, on Monday, Friday and Sunday.
