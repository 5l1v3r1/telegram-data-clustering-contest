Teenager's death at Perthshire boarding school 'unexplained'

Emergency services were called to Strathallan School following the grim discovery at around 7.20am this morning.

11 Nov 2019, 15:58 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

Police have launched a probe into the unexplained death of a teenager at a Perthshire boarding school.

Emergency services were called to Strathallan School, near Forgandenny at around 7.20am this morning.

A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: “We were called to Strathallan School at around 7.20am on Monday morning.

“Enquiries into the teenager’s death are ongoing and we are currently treating it as unexplained.”

Strathallan School is an independent school for boy and girls aged from nine to 19, set on a 153-acre site south of Perth.
