Park Plaza slashes prices on UK hotels and you can bag free room upgrades too

Park Plaza has launched a huge sale on UK hotels including bed and breakfast deals, cheap London stays and you can even bag free room upgrades as part of the deals

11 Nov 2019, 15:04 by Julie Delahaye, https://www.facebook.com/juliedjourno

Park Plaza is giving Brits the perfect excuse to plan their next staycation with a brand new sale.

The hotel chain has slashed prices on heaps of its UK hotels by up to 25 per cent off, and there are plenty of extra perks to be had.

We're talking free breakfast deals and even free room upgrades to the next room category when you book.

Oh, and there's a very generous travel window too, with deals valid on stays throughout selected dates until the 30th December 2020.

Which means you've got a whole year to play with whether you're thinking of booking a mini break during the half terms or other school holiday dates, or want to plan a wintry getaway.

You can already book the deals on the Radisson Hotels website, but it's worth noting that the cheapest rates (and best room upgrades) are subject to limited availability.

 

You'll need to book by Saturday 30th November 2019 when the sale ends.

The deals are predominantly available for stays at Park Plaza's London hotels, although there are discounts for stays at the brand's properties in Nottingham and Leeds. (You can check out the list of participating hotels below).

There are some tempting offers to be found; for example, you can find rooms from £89.62pp at the Park Plaza Victoria London.

 

Don't be disheartened if you can't quite make the offers work for you. To give you a helping hand, we've rounded up more cheap hotels in London that need to be on your radar.
