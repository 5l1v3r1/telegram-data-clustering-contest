India vs Bangladesh: Bowlers won us the game, admits Rohit Sharma

11 Nov 2019, 02:49 by PTI

Highlights

  • Pacer Deepak Chahar took a sensational six-wicket haul, including a hat-trick in the third T20I against Bangladesh
  • Chahar became the first Indian to take a hat-trick in the shortest format
  • Shivam Dube claimed 3 for 30 in India's series victory

NAGPUR: India captain Rohit Sharma credited his bowling unit for their stupendous show in the third and final T20 International against Bangladesh, saying the bowlers won the game and the series for the hosts on Sunday.

ALSO READ: Deepak Chahar's hat-trick hands India T20I series win over Bangladesh

Pacer Deepak Chahar took a sensational six-wicket haul, including a hat-trick after entertaining half-centuries from Shreyas Iyer and K L Rahul, as India defeated a self-destructing Bangladesh by 30 runs in the third and final T20 International to win the series 2-1.
