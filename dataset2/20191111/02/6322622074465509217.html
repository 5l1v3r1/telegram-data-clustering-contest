Stock With Momentum: Tanco Holdings

11 Nov 2019, 02:48 by Asia Analytica

Tanco Holdings Bhd (+ve)

TRADING of shares in Tanco Holdings Bhd (fundamental: 0.35/3, valuation: 0.9/3) triggered our momentum algorithm for the first time this year last Friday. The last time the counter triggered the momentum algorithm was on March 31, 2017.

Tanco shares rose 1.5 sen or 27.27% last Friday to seven sen, valuing the company at RM58.13 million. The stock saw some 23.49 million shares traded, far exceeding its 200-day average trading volume of 956,434 shares.

For the fourth quarter ended June 30, 2019, the group posted a net loss of RM2.63 million, compared to a net profit of RM743,000 a year earlier. Revenue, however, surged to RM2.49 million from RM408,000. The group attributed the quarterly loss to a decrease in other income.

Full-year loss ballooned to RM15.54 million, from RM595,000 in the year prior, with revenue declining to RM3.04 million from RM15.72 million.
