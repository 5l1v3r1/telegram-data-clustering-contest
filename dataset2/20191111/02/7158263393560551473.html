Holy McDreamy: Check Out the ‘Grey’s Anatomy’ Cast Then and Now

11 Nov 2019, 02:00

In the nearly 15 years since Grey’s Anatomy premiered on ABC, there have been a lot of shake-ups in the show’s cast. After all, Grey Sloan Memorial is known for having a revolving door…even if many of those surgeon departures are due to a lot of untimely (and completely heartbreaking) character deaths. But what are the actors who play our favorite doctors, both current and former, up to these days? Here’s an update to the Grey’s cast, from their first appearance on the show to now.
