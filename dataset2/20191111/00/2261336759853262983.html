YouTube star KSI says Logan Paul should face CM Punk in MMA following split decision result in Los Angeles

11 Nov 2019 by Lewis Mckeever

After fighting to a draw in a highly successful amateur boxing match in 2018, YouTube giants Logan Paul and KSI returned to the ring last night for a rematch at Staples Center in Los Angeles, California.

Both men fought back-and-forth in a highly entertaining six-round contest, with KSI edging a split decision over Paul to win the fight. If Paul hadn’t been docked two points for landing an illegal strike in the fourth round, the American would have emerged victorious.

Speaking in the post-fight interview, Paul called for a trilogy bout with KSI, claiming his two fights with the Brit rank amongst the best moments in his life.

“Yeah, yeah I’d love to fight KSI. These are the best moments of my life, man. These are the best moments of my life,” Paul said.
