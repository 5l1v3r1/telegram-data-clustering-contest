Bella Hadid Flaunts Eye-Catching ‘Pearl Nipple Piercings’ At Vogue Fashion Event

11 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Image via BAKOUNINE / Shutterstock.com
Bella Hadid recently attended the CFDA/Vogue Fashion Fund Awards in New York City wearing a black top with a twist.
The supermodel arrived at the event donning an Alexander Wang black bodysuit that had a high neckline and long sleeves.
What made the velvet number more appealing was the strategically-placed pearls on the bodysuit that made her look like she had nipple and belly piercings.
Hadid kept the rest of the outfit understated, with a glittering handbag and black boots. The model wore teardrop earrings and lined her eyes with black kohl.
