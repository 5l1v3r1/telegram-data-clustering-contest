Adelaide Is Heading For A Big Change In Weather

11 Nov 2019

Adelaide is set for a 16C drop in temperature in one day, says the Bureau of Meteorology.

The bureau’s Simon Timke told FIVEaa Breakfast on Monday:

“It’s a real drop in temperature again… we’re expecting to get to 34 today -- so quite a hot day with fresh northerly winds -- and then a pretty significant cool change moving through the Adelaide area.

“Early this evening I think it will get here and quite a lot colder… even though there’s not much around in the way of showers we’ll only get to around a maximum of 18 tomorrow, so quite a big drop in temperature.”
