Fake News Buster: Videos of huge waves is in Oman, not Mumbai

11 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

THE CLAIM
Several videos of huge waves hitting a coast are being circulated on social media platforms with a claim that it occurred in Mumbai’s Marine Lines earlier this week.


SO, WHAT’S THE TRUTH?
The video was actually recorded in Muttrah in Oman. Structures and a signboard seen in the video indicated that the location was not in Mumbai. The video was documented during Cyclone Kyarr, which hit UAE and Oman’s coastal towns.
