After winning all categories at BreakTudo Awards, BlackPink also wins in all PCAs

11 Nov 2019, 06:24 by Kyle_Nakama

BlackPink has just achieved another historic achievement, becoming the first female k-pop group to top the list of People's Choice Awards 2019 music categories.  The girl group took home 'The Group of 2019', 'The Music Video of 2019' (with "Kill This Love"), and 'The Concert Tour of 2019'.

Last month the group won the international BreakTudo Awards 2019, taking 4 awards, K-pop Female Group, International Group, Music Video Of The Year (with "Kill This Love") and Boom Video Of The Year (also with "Kill This Love").

Another historic achievement will happen for the group soon, the DDU-DU DDU-DU video is about to hit the 1 billion view mark, the video already has over 999.5 million views on YouTube.
