Attempt to Dispose of Girl's Remains in River by Deranged St. Petersburg Professor Caught on Video

11 Nov 2019, 06:49

Russian investigators continue to probe a gruesome murder that occurred earlier this week of a former female student from a prestigious St. Petersburg University identified as Anastasia Eshenko, 24, although the perpetrator, renowned history professor Oleg Sokolov, 63, has confessed to the crime.

Russian media published on Sunday CCTV footage depicting a man - allegedly the murderous professor, dressed in a black jacket - dumping a heavy black bag into the river.
