Plymouth war memorials to rediscover on Remembrance Day

Throughout the city, embedded in our surroundings, are memories to those who lost their lives at war. We have visited some of the memorials dedicated to show how Plymouth remembers the fallen.

11 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Penny Cross

While the grand Royal Naval Memorial on the Hoe is Plymouth's most iconic of tributes to our fallen, the city has a rich collection of memorials - and some are hidden in plain sight.

The Hoe boasts a collection of the most celebrated - the new Merchant Navy Memorial, the National Armada Memorial, Plymouth Naval Memorial and Royal Air Force and Allied Air Forces monument.

But there are overlooked memorials which you can rediscover to mark Remembrance Day 2019 all over Plymouth.

Some we walk past every day, even in the centre of the city, while others are tucked away on the outskirts.
