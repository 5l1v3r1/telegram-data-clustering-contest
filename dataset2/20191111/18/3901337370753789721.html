Bafana’s Plans For Ghana As They Await European Contingent

11 Nov 2019, 18:17 by Soccer Laduma

The South Africa Football Association has confirmed that the European contingent will only join up with the rest of the Bafana Bafana camp later tonight.

The senior national team will jet out to Accra, Ghana this evening after their first training session at Sturrock Park this morning. 

Lebogang Phiri, Bongani Zungu, Lebo Mothiba, Thulani Serero, Percy Tau, Darren Keet and Kamohelo Mokotjo will join up with the team in Ghana this evening.

Molefi Ntseki and his boys will thereafter have a long bus drive to the Cape Coast. 

Bafana will lock horns with Ghana in the first match of the 2021 Africa Cup Of Nations qualifiers on Thursday, November 14, where they will thereafter drive back to Ghana and then board a flight back to Mzansi.

Bafana will then face Sudan at the Orlando Stadium on Sunday, November 17, in their second AFCON qualifier.
