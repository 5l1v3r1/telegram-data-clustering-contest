WHITE Dubai to launch new night

Sponsored: The award-winning club will be dedicating Tuesdays to the best of afro beats, dance hall and R&B

11 Nov 2019, 18:19

Stop what you’re doing party people – WHITE Dubai is launching a brand-spanking new night and it sounds like it’s going to be a banger.

After huge success across Europe with Starboy Fest, Wizkid (who’s one of Africa’s hottest pop stars) and Starboy Entertainment have curated a cultural experience with a new residency at WHITE Dubai.

STARBOY BOY will be launching this Tuesday (November 12) and it promises the best of afro beats, dance hall and R&B, with Teni, Skales and Ice Prince hitting the stage for its launch night.

Nigerian singer and songwriter Teni has been making waves after releasing hit singles Askamaya, Case and Uyo Meyo.

Skales is best known for his R&B chart-topper Shake Body and he’ll be joined by hip-hop star and actor Ice Prince who’s famed for his single Oleku.

What’s more, ladies get free entry and unlimited drinks all night as well as unlimited sushi from 10am until 1am.

So, what are you waiting for? Fire up that WhatsApp group with your best party mates and head on down for a cracking night.
Tue 10pm-4am. Ongoing. Meydan Racecourse Grandstand, Nad Al Sheba, www.whitedubai.com (050 443 0933).
