Furious The Chase fans fume 'evening is ruined' after quiz show is replaced by rival

Fans were not happy when they tuned into ITV at 5pm for The Chase - but saw that another Bradley Walsh programme was on instead

11 Nov 2019, 18:42 by Rose Hill, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rose-hill/

The Chase viewers were left raging after discovering their beloved quiz show was replaced by a rival programme.

Tonight, fans tuned in at 5pm - only to be bitterly disappointed after finding out that it wasn't on.

Instead, host Bradley Walsh was fronting new series Cash Trapped, which sees six contestants compete to win big money.

The aim of the game is to become the person who accumulates the most and has the chance to take the money and run.

But if they manage to succeed, the rest of the contestants leave empty-handed, meaning that everyone else needs to try and stop them.
