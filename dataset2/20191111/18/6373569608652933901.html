The Chase viewers furious at 'absolutely garbage' replacement Cash Trapped

"They should add Cash Trapped to the list of torture methods. I would admit to anything rather than being forced to watch this"

11 Nov 2019, 18:09 by Katie Timms

Viewers have been left outraged by The Chase replacement Cash Trapped.

Whilst Bradley Walsh is still the host of the substitute game show, fans say their 'evening is ruined' and many have begged for ITV producers to bring back the tea-time TV favourite.

Cash Trapped has been brought back after being last aired in 2017 and people have said they do not understand the concept of the show.

The programme sees six contestants go head to head by answering various questions in four different rounds until one of them "escapes" during the final stage.

One woman asked ITV producers if they were "crazy" for replacing The Chase.

Lucy tweeted: "Are you guys crazy? What is this total s**** on my screen instead of #TheChase

"Why do you people always scrap your best shows for utter rubbish? I honestly don’t get it? Cash trapped? Crap trapped more like.

"Bring back the chase!! I’m switching over."
