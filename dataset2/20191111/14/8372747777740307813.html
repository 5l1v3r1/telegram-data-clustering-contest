Cape Coral hosting public input meetings on two parks this week

11 Nov 2019, 14:04 by WFTX Digital Team

CAPE CORAL, Fla. -- This week the City of Cape Coral will host two meetings to get your input on the design of several community parks.

Tuesday, residents are invited to talk about Lake Kennedy Racquet Club.

And on Thursday, they'll discuss Festival Park and Yellow Fever Creek Preserve.

Each meeting will be held at the Cape Coral Yacht Club from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m.

Last year, voters passed a bond setting set aside $60 million to pay for seven new neighborhood parks.
