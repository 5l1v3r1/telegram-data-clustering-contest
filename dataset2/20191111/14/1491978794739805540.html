Armistice Day and Germany's fifth season: Monday's best photos

11 Nov 2019, 14:48

The Guardian’s picture editors select photo highlights from around the world

Paris, France

Veterans take part in a ceremony at the Arc de Triomphe as part of commemorations marking the 101st anniversary of the 11 November 1918 armistice

Photograph: Ludovic Marin/Reuters

Santa Cruz, Bolivia

Police officers join in celebrations following the resignation of the Bolivian president Evo Morales

Photograph: Daniel Walker/AFP/Getty Images

Wolverhampton, UK

The prime minister, Boris Johnson, holds a baby at the Lynch Gate Tavern as he campaigns ahead of the general election

Photograph: Ben Stansall/AFP/Getty Images

London, UK

British artist Steve McQueen’s work Year 3 at Tate Britain. The installation takes school photographs from across London to create a picture of a generation to come

Photograph: Neil Hall/EPA

Amritsar, India

A boy performs Gatkha, a traditional form of martial arts, during a procession on the eve of the 550th anniversary of the birth of Guru Nanak Dev, the founder of the Sikh faith

Photograph: Munish Sharma/Reuters

Bangkok, Thailand

Workers carry a statue of Pope Francis at Saint Louis hospital. The pontiff will visit Thailand from 20 to 23 November to mark the 350th anniversary of the founding of the Vatican’s Mission de Siam

Photograph: Rungroj Yongrit/EPA

Warsaw, Poland

People take part in celebrations on the country’s independence day

Photograph: Maciek Jaźwiecki/Agencja Gazeta/Reuters

Prague, Czech Republic

A dummy of a policeman sits at a table as part of an exhibition at the cold war-era nuclear shelter inside the Jalta hotel

Photograph: Martin Divíšek/EPA

Hong Kong

A demonstrator is detained in Central district on the day that a protester was shot by police

Photograph: Vincent Yu/AP

Port-au-Prince, Haiti

Police patrol the streets during a march to demand the resignation of the president, Jovenel Moïse

Photograph: Jean Marc Herve Abelard/EPA

Cologne, Germany

Celebrations on the first day of the the carnival season. Also known as the fifth season in German-speaking countries, it begins each year on 11 November at 11:11am and finishes on Ash Wednesday of the following year

Photograph: Thilo Schmülgen/Reuters

Srinagar, India

A resident walks through heavy snowfall in the Kashmiri city. There have been several fatalities as the winter weather arrives in the region

Photograph: Saqib Majeed/Barcroft Media

Marktoberdorf, Germany

Ice needles on an autumnal branch

Photograph: Karl-Josef Hildenbrand/dpa/AFP/Getty Images

Wytaliba, Australian

A resident holds her son as she waits to get back to her house at a roadblock near Glen Innes, New South Wales. Several people have reportedly been killed and 150 homes destroyed by raging bush fires

Photograph: Dan Peled/EPA

Cape Town, South Africa

Team captain, Siya Kolisi, holds the Webb Ellis Trophy during the Rugby World Cup winning team’s victory tour

Photograph: David Harrison/AFP /Getty Images

Guadalajara, Mexico

Followers of the Mexican-based Pentecostal church Luz del Mundo (Light of the World) participate in a baptism ceremony. The controversial church’s leader, Naasón Joaquín García, is imprisoned in the US awaiting trial on child rape allegations

Sidon, Lebanon

Demonstrators take to the water for an anti-government protest

Cartagena, Colombia

A dance troupe participates in the Cabildo de Getsemaní parade as part of independence celebrations

Photograph: Ricardo Maldonado Rozo/EPA

Sydney, Australia

A red rose on the Cenotaph during a Remembrance Day service
