3 officers injured, 2 suspects arrested after police chase ends in fiery crash in Pacoima

11 Nov 2019, 14:50

PACOIMA, Calif. (KABC) -- Three Los Angeles police officers were injured after a pursuit ended in a fiery crash in Pacoima Monday morning.

Officers initiated the pursuit of two robbery suspects in an SUV around 4:30 a.m. At some point during the chase, the suspects' vehicle went into an embankment where it overturned and was struck by an LAPD cruiser, which was then rear ended by a second police SUV, officials said.

The suspects' vehicle then caught fire on the 118 Freeway westbound on-ramp near Glenoaks Boulevard.

Multiple Los Angeles Fire Department units responded to the scene, where the on-ramp remained closed as of 6:30 a.m.

One of the three injured officers suffered a broken leg, and the two suspects were taken into custody.

It is unclear when the on-ramp is expected to reopen.
