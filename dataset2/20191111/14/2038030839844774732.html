Cape Town – We have some riveting shows lined-up for your Monday viewing. From bad mothers to life in the public eye – here are our top three recommendations.

Bad Mothers

20:00 - Universal TV (DStv 117)

This all-new Australian series follows a group of flawed and chaotic female friends caught up in a murder investigation that ultimately tests the boundaries of what it is to be considered a “good” mother and the demands placed on women in modern society.
