BlazBlue: Cross Tag Battle drops trailers for Celica A. Mercury and Susanoo

Getting the band back together

11 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Chris Moyse

We are just a little over a week out from the release of BlazBlue: Cross Tag Battle's Version 2.0 update, which brings a posse of new characters to to the crossover fighter. Arc System Works has released two new trailers looking at BlazBlue: Central Fiction's Celica A. Mercury and Susanoo, both of whom are getting set to join the rumble.

Celica A. Mercury, who made her playable debut in BlazBlue: Chrono Phantasma Extend, is the younger sister of Nine the Phantom, and was a kindhearted young nun who was murdered by BlazBlue villain Yuuki Terumi. Now regenerated as her younger self, Celica continues to espouse her will for good, taking the fight to the enemy alongside her "Drive," Minerva.
