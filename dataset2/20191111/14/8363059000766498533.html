A OnePlus 8 Pro Prototype Has Already Been Spotted in the Wild

11 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Tom Pritchard

The OnePlus 7T range hasn't been out for very long, but it's as though that opened up the floodgates for rumours and leaks regarding the OnePlus 8 series - due sometime in Spring 2020. In fact someone has been spotted carrying around what appears to be a prototype version of the OnePlus 8 Pro - complete with a holepunch camera.
