ITV's The Chase has been forced off air today

Viewers of the show will be furious to know it won't be appearing as usual at 5pm

11 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Miles O'Leary

The Chase has been taken off air to make way for another game show, it's been reported.

Fans will no doubt have been expecting the popular ITV quiz to appear as usual from 5pm today.

But it's being booted out of its usual slot by Cash Trapped - which also happens to be hosted by TV veteran Bradley Walsh.

It's not entirely clear when The Chase is expected to make a return, reports Examiner Live.

But popular Chaser and former I'm a Celeb star Ann Hegerty has assured fans it won't be gone for long.

And she also shared some exciting news for hardcore Chase fans.

In a tweet to one concerned viewer, she said: "CT is only on for five weeks, and this year we have TWO Christmas specials for you!!"

The fan then asked if the next series of The Chase would start in December or January and Anne replied: "I have no idea, transmission schedule and recording schedule are quite seperate."

Some fans are not happy about the scheduled change.

Sue Goldsmith said: "Not happy that we are losing The Chase for Cash trapped. As much as I think you're great Bradders, it isn't the same as The Chase."

Strictly Superfan said: "Please axe @CashTrapped , nobody likes it, and there will be a major uproar from the chase fanbase. I for one will not watch."

And Stephanie Treasure said: "Um... what is this #cashtrapped coming on Monday @ITV ? Where is my beloved #TheChase ?!"

ITV has been contacted for comment.
