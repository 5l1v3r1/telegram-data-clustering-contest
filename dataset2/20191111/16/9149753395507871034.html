This slick WQHD monitor has a 120Hz refresh rate and comes equipped with Nvidia's G-Sync technology, providing you with the same anti-tearing support that V-Sync provides with a reduced impact on performance. While the 4ms response time may be a deal-breaker for some, this is honestly more than sufficient for competing at most levels of play.
