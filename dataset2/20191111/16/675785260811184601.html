Jack Fincham throws shade at Kris Boyson for snap with Katie Price's daughter Princess

Kris Boyson come under fire from Jack Fincham when he shared his latest Instagram post with Katie Price's daughter

11 Nov 2019, 16:48 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Jack Fincham laid into Katie Price's toyboy Kris Boyson for his latest Instagram post.

The Love Island 2018 winner proved overly critical of the model's social media picture which he uploaded on Sunday.

In the snap, Kris beamed brightly into the mirror for his selfie while he was photobombed by his girlfriend's daughter Princess.

They have developed a close bond since he started dating her mother Katie.

He joked in the caption: "Photobombed!"

Clearly unimpressed, the pen seller left a very sarcastic remark on Kris' latest Instagram post he had shared with his fans.

He wrote: "Ooooooo k den."

Katie had no qualms with the photograph as she posted a gushing comment beneath the picture of her daughter and her toyboy boyfriend.

She wrote: "You look handsome here and so cute That princess drives you nuts all the time."

Katie and Kris had been due to join the Celebs Go Dating line-up when Jack starred on the show earlier this year.

But the couple pulled the plug on their hotly-anticipated appearance which caused much disappointment among the cast and fans alike.

It is not known why Jack has decided to launch a venomous attack on Kris.
