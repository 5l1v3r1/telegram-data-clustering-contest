WET Deck launches new Saturday ladies' pool day deal

Expect live and free entry for women at this new Dubai pool party

11 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Reema Rahman

No plans this Saturday? No worries.

W Dubai – The Palm is probably one of the coolest spots in Dubai right now and this brand-new pool day deal makes for a pretty sweet Saturday.

WET Deck, the trendy hotel’s poolside spot, has teamed up with 411 Saturdays for Dazed – a day packed full of laid-back vibes and the latest tunes from local stars DJ Bliss, DJ Kaboo, DJ Taya KruZz and Smokingroove.

Ladies, you’ll want to set your alarm for this one as there’s free entry between noon and 3pm and a Dhs200 minimum spend thereafter, while there’s a minimum spend of Dhs300 for gents.

On the hunt for more ladies’ days and pool day deals in Dubai?

Love Parties has also launched a new ladies’ day and Candypants is now throwing its longest day-to-night carnival party.
