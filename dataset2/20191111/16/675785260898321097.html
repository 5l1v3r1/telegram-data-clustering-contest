BREAKING Death of girl, 11, on Drayton Manor ride was accidental

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

11 Nov 2019, 16:25 by Talia Shadwell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/talia-shadwell/

The death of an 11-year-old schoolgirl on a Drayton Manor theme park ride has been ruled an accident.

Schoolgirl Evha Jannath drowned after falling from the Splash Canyon water ride at the the popular Staffordshire theme park.

The conclusion of accidental death was read out by the jury foreman in the inquest into the girl's death today, and also stated that Evha died of drowning.
