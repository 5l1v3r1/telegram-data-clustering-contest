Man rushed to Wishaw hospital as police believe he was victim of targeted attack

The 26-year-old man suffered serious but non-life threatening injuries during the incident in Greenbank Terrace, Carluke.

11 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Ross Thomson

Police in Lanarkshire are appealing for information after a man was seriously assaulted in Carluke.

The incident happened around 6.30pm on Sunday, November 10, within a property in the Greenbank Terrace area of the village.

The 26-year-old man suffered serious but non-life threatening injuries during the incident. He was taken to University Hospital Wishaw for treatment.

Detective Constable Lindsey McIntyre, of Lanarkshire CID, said: “We are carrying out inquiries to establish the full circumstances of this assault which we believe to have been targeted.

“We are interested in speaking to anyone who was in the area around 6.30pm on Sunday, particularly if they saw any suspicious individuals or vehicles.

“Anyone with information is urged to contact 101, quoting incident number 3236 of 10 November, or call Crimestoppers anonymously via 0800 555 111.”
