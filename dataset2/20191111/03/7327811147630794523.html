Police investigating after 2 men, 1 woman shot in Brooklyn

11 Nov 2019, 03:35 by Eyewitness News

FLATLANDS, Brooklyn (WABC) -- Police are investigating after three people involved in an apparent love triangle were shot in Brooklyn Sunday afternoon.

A 911 call was received at about 2 p.m. for a shooting on Utica Avenue in the Flatlands section.

Police say a 24-year-old woman and a 30-year-old man were both found shot in the head. A 33-year-old man was found with a bullet wound in his torso.

Officials believe the woman lives in the apartment,

Police are not looking for a shooter, and stress that no gunman is on the loose.

The man and woman shot in the head were pronounced dead at Kings County Hospital. Police say the 33-year-old man shot in the torso is recovering at Brookdale Hospital.

There is no word yet on what led to the shooting.
