Kylie Jenner takes daughter Stormi to festival as she supports ex Travis Scott

The pair called it quits on their relationship last month, but it seems they're on good terms as Kylie jetted to Houston so Stormi could cheer on her dad

11 Nov 2019, 03:11 by Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

Reality TV star Kylie Jenner and rapper Travis Scott may have gone their separate ways - but it seems the pair are still on good terms.

Kylie, who shares daughter Stormi with music sensation Travis, turned up in Houston, Texas, to cheer on her ex with their little girl.

The 22 year old headed to Travis' own Astroworld Music Festival, which he set up in his hometome.

At one point during the show, Travis announced to his legions of fans that his one year old was watching in the audience.

He also brought out Kanye West for a two song performance - Kanye is Kylie's brother-in-law, married to her half-sister Kim.

A source told People magazine that it's important to the two parents that they remain close for Stormi's sake.

The source said: "Kylie flew to Houston with a group of friends. She returns to LA this morning.

"It was important for her to support Travis' festival. They are still very close and often hang out with Stormi."

The Kylie Cosmetics guru has been in the spotlight even more so than usual recently, amid reports that she and rapper Drake were an item at one point.

They both deny that they were ever together.

The Sun reported they tried to keep their alleged romance a secret - especially from Kim, considering Drake's former feud with her husband Kanye.

The publication went on to claim that Kylie wanted something more serious, but that Drake refused to be tied down.

A source had previously told People: "He and Kylie have been spending time together recently.

"They've been friends for a long time and Drake is very close to the family."
