UPDATED: Overturned lorry at Dundalk roundabout

Roads

11 Nov 2019, 13:59 by David Lynch

UPDATED 17.41: The road (N52) heading into Dundalk off the roundabout is still blocked off at scene of incident this evening. Northbound traffic coming into Dundalk from the M1 should exit at Junction 15 for Castlebellingham. Southbound traffic should exit at Junction 17.

Emergency services are attending a scene at the roundabout at Junction 16 off the M1 motorway this afternoon after a lorry overturned.

According to eyewitnesses, traffic can still get past the stricken lorry at present.

There are no reports on injuries at present.
