Celtic fans given final opportunity to make Cluj trip with just 15 seats remaining on club flight

Neil Lennon's men travel to Romania in December in a game that could decide the group winners.

11 Nov 2019, 13:23 by Record Sport Online

Celtic have revealed they have just 15 seats left on their chartered flight to Romania for their Europa League clash with CFR Cluj.

The Hoops travel to Transylvania for the final match of their group and it could be a decider on who tops the table.

Celtic have already qualified from Group E and Cluj are likely to follow them into the last 32, currently sitting six points ahead of Lazio in third.

And supporters can be on the trip and guarantee themselves a ticket by booking one of their final 15 seats available on the flight.

The day trip will see them fly from Glasgow Airport on Thursday, December 12 before returning from Romania after the match.

The offer is only open to season ticket holders and the club say it gives a great opportunity to build your score for future away games in Europe.

The match comes four days after the Betfred Cup Final against Rangers at Hampden.

Neil Lennon's men guaranteed their place in the next round with a stunning 2-1 win over Lazio in Rome last week.

James Forrest cancelled out Ciro Immobile's opening goal before Olivier Ntcham added a second late on.
