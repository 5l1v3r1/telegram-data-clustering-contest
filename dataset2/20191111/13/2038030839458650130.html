Jordana Brewster shares new 'Fast & Furious 9' details

11 Nov 2019, 13:08

Cape Town – Fast and the Furious actress Jordana Brewster has given fans a sneak tease of what to expect in the upcoming film.

Fresh off the set of the ninth film the 39-year-old actress says the latest movie in the action franchise will be "bigger and better" than ever.

Speaking to the Associated Press, she shares: "Every time we outdo ourselves in some way. I was reading the script, and I was like how are they going to do it, and then I was like oh, wow, that's how."

Jordana also went on to say that it was wonderful to be back with everyone, and that they literally are a family.

While details of the upcoming film directed by Justin Lin remains under wraps, Vin Diesel, Michelle Rodriguez, Tyrese Gibson, Chris "Ludacris" Bridges and Nathalie Emmanuel will be reprising their roles.

Helen Mirren and Charlize Theron who were in Fate of the Furious will also be returning. 

New cast members include John Cena and Cardi B.
