Ajay Devgn in plans to make the remake of 'Phool Aur Kaante', deets inside

11 Nov 2019, 13:48 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

As Ajay Devgn is all geared up to mark his 100 film journey in Bollywood with the release of his forthcoming film, 'Tanhaji-The Unsung Warrior', B-town was all in praise for the actor and also congratulated him for crossing the milestone. The actor in an exclusive conversation with Filmfare announced that he is in plans to make the remake of his first film,'Phool Aur Kaante'. We are sure with this piece of news has raised the excitement of his fans.

Let's see what more details did the 'Tanhaji' star shared with the portal. Talking to Filmfare, Ajay Devgn confirmed, “I want to remake my first film, Phool Aur Kaante (1991). I’ve thought of a new way of presenting it in keeping with the changing times. I will co-produce it with someone. I’m looking for a fresh face for that. The approach will be different but the emotional texture will be retained.”

Ajay Devgn is currently gearing up for the release of his 100th film, Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior, which is directed by Om Raut, the period drama .
