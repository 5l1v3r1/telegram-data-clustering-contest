Good day everyone, I want to share my latest personal work "Colleen Wing"
Based on Netflix's Marvel Iron Fist character Colleen Wing played by Jessica Henwick. Modelled and sculpted in Blender. Base clothing created in Marvelous Designer. Textures painted in Photoshop and additional textures from Poliigon. Hair groomed in Blender. Rendered in Cycles.

Studio/Client: Character
Software: Blender, Marvelous Designer, Photoshop
Tasks:

● Modelling
● Sculpting
● Surfacing
● Rendering
● When: November 2019
