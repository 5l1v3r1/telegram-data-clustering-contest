Report: Gordon Hayward to have surgery on fractured hand Monday afternoon

11 Nov 2019, 21:22 by David Souza

BOSTON - Boston Celtics F Gordon Hayward will reportedly have surgery to repair his fractured left hand, according to a report from Adrian Wojanrowski, an NBA reporter for ESPN who was citing Hayward's agent, Mark Bartelstein.

The announcement came just after 3:30 p.m. on Monday afternoon.
