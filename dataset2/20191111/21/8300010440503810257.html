Krispy Kreme releases 3 pie-inspired doughnuts for Thanksgiving

11 Nov 2019, 21:36

WINSTON-SALEM, N.C. -- Through Thanksgiving, Krispy Kreme will offer three pie-inspired doughnuts.

The new doughnuts are inspired by favorite Thanksgiving desserts.

The new flavors are Dutch Apple Pie, Cherry Pie and Chocolate Kreme Pie.
