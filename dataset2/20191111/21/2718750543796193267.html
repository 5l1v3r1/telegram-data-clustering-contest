Michelle Wolf’s New Netflix Special Premieres Next Month

11 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Megh Wright

Comedian Michelle Wolf is returning to Netflix next month, only instead of hosting a late-night show, she’ll instead be starring in a brand-new stand-up special. The streaming network announced today that Wolf’s special, titled Joke Show, will debut on Tuesday, December 10. In the special, Wolf “dissects the differences between men and women, explores gender equality, explains why society should be a little ‘less’ woke and more, all the while, constantly defying expectations.” Her debut comedy special, Nice Lady, debuted on HBO in late 2017. She followed that up with an extremely busy — and controversial — 2018 in which she performed at the White House Correspondents’ Dinner and hosted her now-canceled Netflix show The Break.
