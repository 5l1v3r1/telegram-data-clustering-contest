Badger women's soccer back in NCAA Tournament

Fourth straight year Wisconsin qualifies

11 Nov 2019, 23:19 by Jay Wilson

MADISON - The Wisconsin Badgers women's soccer team is back in the NCAA Tournament.

The Badgers received a No.3 seed Monday and will host Milwaukee Friday afternoon at 5 at McClimonÂ Soccer Complex in the first round of the tournament..

The Badgers (14-3-2) won the Big Ten Conference regular-season championship but were ousted by Purdue in the first round of the playoffs.

It's the fourth straight year the Badgers will play in the NCAA Women's Soccer Tournament.

The NCAA Women's College Cup is in San Jose, California December 6-8.
