Mayo gardaì respond to social media reports of alleged child abduction

11 Nov 2019, 23:00

GARDAÌ in Mayo have responded to social media reports of an alleged child abduction in the Castlebar area earlier tonight.

When contacted by The Connaught Telegraph, a spokesperson said the force is aware of the reports.

"We are investigating an incident, but we are not investigating a child abduction," the spokesperson explained.

The garda spokesperson added no further comment is being made on the matter.
