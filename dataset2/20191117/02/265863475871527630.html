Channing Tatum Returns To Instagram To Share Pictures Of Adorable Six-Year-Old Daughter With Jenna Dewan, Everly

17 Nov 2019, 02:26 by Charisse Van Horn

Channing Tatum has returned to Instagram after taking a break from the social media platform on August 7, 2019. He’s not the first to feel bored and uninspired from social media. Channing Tatum explained in a lengthy post that he was taking a break from Instagram and other platforms including his phone. Though leaving social media isn’t unusual, he may be one of the few celebrities who returned to share pictures of their offspring. Channing returned to Instagram so that he could share pictures of his six-year-old daughter, Everly,  living it up in Las Vegas.

Channing Tatum took six-year-old Everly Tatum to a Vegas arcade where the father and daughter duo had fun playing games, spending time together, and just hanging out. Everly looked adorable as she wore a princess dress and there was no doubt she felt like a princess getting her dad’s full and undivided attention. The pair were all smiles and there were plenty of photos and videos from their excursion that made their way to social media.

Channing Tatum has 17.3 million Instagram followers and fans were thrilled that he returned. Within minutes of posting to his Instagram story, fans flooded his previous pictures with comments.
