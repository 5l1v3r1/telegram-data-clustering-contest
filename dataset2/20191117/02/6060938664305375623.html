Hollywood newsmakers of the week: Dwayne Johnson's Black Adam, Taylor Swift-Scooter Braun controversy, Kristen Stewart eager to see Robert Pattinson as Batman

17 Nov 2019, 02:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Not only Bollywood but it was a busy week in Hollywood as well, as several events like Taylor Swift-Scooter Braun controversy, Robert Downey Jr pays tribute to Marvel legend Stan Lee and others grabbed everyone's attention throughout the globe.

While Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone completed one year of togetherness and opted for a spiritual first wedding anniversary celebration, ex-flame Kristen Stewart and Robert Pattinson have been making headlines in the West.

Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson joins DC Universe's as Black Adam

Wrestler turned actor Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson took to social media to announce that he is joining the DC Universe's as Black Adam and makers have planned for a standalone film for the same. The film is a spin-off of 'Shazam' featuring Zachary Levi. In his post, Dwayne also revealed how he had always wished to play Superman on the silver screen. The film 'Black Adam' will hit the theatres on December 22, 2021.

Taylor Swift and Scooter Braun controversy

On Thursday, pop sensation Taylor Swift took to social media and opened up revealing that she may not perform at the American Music Awards and may even have to future projects on hold as she isn't allow her to play her songs by Scooter Braun and Scott Borchetta, who own her old records. This controversy soon spread across social media like wildfire and netizens started using #IStandWithTaylor hashtag on Twitter to show their support towards Taylor. Ed Sheeran, Halsey, Camila Cabello and many other singers too came up in her support.

Robert Downey Jr pays tribute to Stan Lee

Robert Downey Jr, who played the popular role of Tony Stark aka Iron Man in Marvel 'Avengers' franchise recently won an award for his stunning performance in 'Avengers: Endgame'. Downey Jr thanked everyone for their contribution and paid tribute to Marvel legend and the man who co-created Iron Man/Tony Stark, Stan Lee as he dedicated the award to him.

Kristen Stewart is eager to see Robert Pattinson as Batman

While Kristen Stewart was busy the last entire week promoting her film 'Charlie's Angel', in an interview with a Hollywood portal she revealed that she is eagerly waiting to see ex-flame Robert Pattinson as Batman in Matt Reeves directorial 'The Batman'. Talking about Robert and her friend Zoe Kravitz, who plays the Catwoman in 'The Batman', Stewart revealed that she cannot wait to see them together on the celluloid.
