Video: LSU QB Joe Burrow Trucks A Referee

17 Nov 2019, 02:23 by Dan Lyons

At 6-foot-4, 216 pounds, Joe Burrow is a pretty big dude. The Heisman favorite has put up some huge numbers with his arm, but he’s dangerous in the open field as a runner as well.

That was evident against Alabama last week, when some Burrow runs helped seal the game. A referee during tonight’s game against Ole Miss found that out all too well tonight.

Ona play during tonight’s game in Oxford, Burrow took off up the middle. Unfortunately for one ref, he was in harm’s way as Joe came barreling through.
