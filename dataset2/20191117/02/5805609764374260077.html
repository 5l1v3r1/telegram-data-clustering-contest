Top 5 Best Cartoon Avatar Maker Apps for Android

Creating your own cartoon avatar is never easy. On the computer, you need to be good at photoshop to create attractive cartoon avatars. Similarly, things are neither easy on Android also. Some users rely entirely on Android for creating and editing images. For those users, we are going to share a list of best Android apps that lets you create your cartoony avatar.

#1 Androidify

Well, Androidify is not precisely an avatar maker, but it lets you create, accessorize, and share Android characters that look exactly like you. So, its an app that can be used to create a bug droid version of yourself. For customizing the avatar, the app provides users multiple color options, shapes, sizes, and different accessories. So, Androidify is the best cartoon avatar maker 2019 that you can use right now.

#2 Bitmoji

Bitmoji is one of the best and top-rated avatar maker app that you can use on your Android smartphone. Millions of users right now use the app, and it allows users to create expressive cartoon avatars. The key thing is that Bitmoji creates avatars based on emotions. For instance, you can create a laughing version of yourself, crying ones, etc. So, Bitmoji is definitely the best cartoon avatar maker app that you can use on your Android smartphone.

#3 FaceQ

FaceQ is for those who have been searching for an easy to use, yet powerful avatar maker app for Android smartphones. The app has a great user interface that aligns all features in a straightforward way. Even if you have zero knowledge regarding how to draw, you can easily create your own expressive cartoon avatar through FaceQ. So, FaceQ is another best free cartoon avatar maker apps that you can use today.

#4 Cartoon Avatar Photo Maker

Cartoon Avatar Photo Maker is another best free and unique avatar maker app on the list that you can use to create cartoon avatars that looks like you. Unlike other apps, Cartoon Avatar Photo Make offers various avatar editing features like mouth style, face style, sunglasses, hair colors, etc. In short, you can customize almost everything while creating a custom cartoon avatar.

#5 SuperMii

SuperMii is not much popular, but it’s one of the best avatar maker app available out there. The app lets you create custom avatars whose every aspect can be edited. The Avatar app for Android closely follows the Japanese anime concept and tries to give an anime feel on the avatars. So, SuperMii is another best Android avatar app 2019 that you can use right now.
