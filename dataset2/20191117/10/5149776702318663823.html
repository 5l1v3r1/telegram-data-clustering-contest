Woman needed hospital treatment after 'disturbance' in Mansfield

The incident happened in Trowell Court

17 Nov 2019, 10:16 by Matt Jarram

Police have arrested a man after reports of a disturbance at a property in Mansfield.

Officers were called to Trowell Court just before 7.45pm Saturday, November 16.

A woman was taken to hospital for treatment. The extent of her injuries is currently unknown.

A 25-year-old man has been arrested on suspicion of grievous bodily harm and remains in police custody.

Detective Inspector Natasha Todd, from Nottinghamshire Police, said: "Our enquiries are ongoing to establish the circumstances of this incident and there will be a police presence in the area as officers continue with their investigations.

"We'd appeal for anyone who has any information which could help us with our enquiries to contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident number 821 of 16 November 2019, or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111."
