Do you fancy a job at Nottinghamshire Police? These roles are up for grabs

Constable, sergeant and inspector jobs are being advertised

17 Nov 2019, 10:42 by Matt Jarram

Ever thought about a career at Nottinghamshire Police?

Well, now is your chance, as a number of jobs at the force are up for grabs.

The roles range from just under £20,000 to £55,000 and the jobs include police officers and inspectors, as well as clerical positions.

The first roles up for grabs are for serving police officers - constables, sergeants and inspectors to transfer to Nottinghamshire Police .

Roles in fraud, financial investigations, cyber crime and public protection are available.

There is also a chance to join the firearms, dog handling and road policing unit.

Positions range from £24,177 to £55,767.

The police are also looking for a clerical assistant at the police headquarters in Sherwood Lodge and an information request administrator.

The force also wants to recruit a commercial officer and a volunteer cadet leader who will deliver the cadet scheme for Nottinghamshire Police.

Police Cadets are young men and women who volunteer their time to learn about modern day policing and take part in community-focused initiatives.

If you fancy applying for the force, visit the Nottinghamshire Police website.
