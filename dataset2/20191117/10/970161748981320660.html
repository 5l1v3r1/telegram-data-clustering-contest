NFL executive says Colin Kaepernick's arm is still 'elite' after public workout

17 Nov 2019, 10:59

Colin Kaepernick still has ‘elite’ arm talent according to an NFL executive who attended his public workout on Saturday after the 32-year-old put on his own session to allow the media to attend.

The NFL had arranged a private workout in Atalanta but denied access to the media, prompting Kaepernick – who last played in the NFL in 2016 when he began kneeling during the US national anthem to protest against racism – to arrange a session 60 miles away at Charles Drew High School in Riverdale.

The NFL said it was ‘disappointed’ with the venue change, which happened at just 30 minutes’ notice, and many of the 25 teams scheduled to watch him were unable to do so.
