17 small but beautiful Christmas markets coming to Liverpool

Interesting food stalls, grottos for the kids, holistic well-being treatments and much, much more

17 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Mark Johnson

Christmas is around the corner and festive markets offer a great way to enjoy the midwinter.

They are a great way to get together with family and friends and there is something for everyone.

Rare and unique gifts are on offer, along with interesting food stalls, grottos for the kids, holistic well-being treatments and much, much more.

Liverpool's popular Christmas Market makes its return to St George’s Hall - and there is a huge wheel to enjoy there, too - but there are also many other venues holding festive markets.

Here are some of the many Christmas Markets taking place on Merseyside ...

Liverpool Christmas Farmers Market

Some of the best local producers will assemble in one room.

On offer will be fresh bagels, cheese, bread, local honey, dog treats, pies and more.

This will sit alongside the permanent street food market where some of the best and most interesting food stalls will be available to see.
Note that although the farmers market ends at 4pm the market is open until 9pm with food on until 7pm.

Entry is free and it will be child-friendly and dog-friendly.

Where: Stanhope Street, Cains Brewery, L8 5XJ

When: Doors open at 11am and it will go on until 4pm

Bishop Martin School Christmas Fayre

There will be a Santa's Grotto, LFC Hospitality Raffle, Tombola, Games and Toys, Cakes, Hot Food and Drinks, Tuck Shop.

And much more.

Where: Bishop Martin School C.E. School

When: November 30 from 12pm to 3pm, Bishop Martin School

Halewood Christmas market 

There will be local stall holders, unique handmade gifts, food and drink, and raffle prizes

All funds raised go toward St Nicholas Centre, Halewood.

Where: Hollies Hall

When: Saturday, November 23 between 1pm and 3pm.

Christmas at Baltic Market 

From December 1 onwards this will be open five days a week.

Wednesdays and Thursdays it will be open from 5pm to 11pm, Friday it is open from 12pm to midnight, Saturday it is open 11am to midnight and Sunday 11am to 9pm.

It will be closed Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, Boxing Day, New Year's Day and every Monday and Tuesday throughout December.

Where: Baltic Market

When: December 1 to January 1

Bishop Eton school

There will be tombolas, raffle, mince pies, Santa's Grotto, face-painting, bar, craft corner, mulled wine, gift stalls, Child of Sparrow Interiors, and efytastic interiors.

Where: Our Lady's Bishop Eton School, Green Lane, Liverpool, L18 2EP

When: Sunday, December 8 between 1pm and 4pm.

Hunts Cross Christmas Craft Fayre

Go along to this festive Christmas Craft Fair and pick up some lovely handmade gifts.

Where: St Columbus Church Hall Craft Fayre

When: Saturday December 14 from 10am to 2pm

 

Bluebell's Christmas Gift, Craft and Local Produce Fayre

The Bluebell Team welcomes you to our Festive Christmas Fayre.

There is always a little something for everyone at Bluebell's.

There will be a host of locally independent businesses showcasing their stunning stalls of all gifts, crafts and local produce.

There is a holistic well-being room with two fully qualified therapists available.

Hot and cold drinks, and freshly prepared lunches and cakes will be served from Bluebell's very own cafe in store.

In the main hall, as well as the stalls, we open a Christmas themed children's area full of Festive activity and a chance to Meet Mrs Christmas and her Elves.

Stay and create some fabulous little Christmas decorations.

There is wheelchair access and baby changing facilities.

Where: Crosby United reformed Church, Eshe Road, Crosby, L23 3AR

When: Saturday, December 14 2019 from 11am to 4pm.

Christmas Market at Pier Head Liverpool

One of the city's biggest festive attractions is coming back.

There will be a Christmas Ice Festival, which also boasts a Christmas market, at Pier Head Liverpool.

You will find ice skating, an ice slide, fairground rides and games.

And there will be lots of food and drink available.

Where: Pier Head, Liverpool

When: November 9, 2019 to January 5, 2020 (excluding Christmas Day)

Wirral Christmas Craft Fayre Hoylake Parade Community Centre

At this Christmas Craft Fayre there will be more than 30 local stalls.

They will be showcasing handcrafted, handmade, local gifts for the perfect presents this Christmas.

Where: Hoylake Community Centre, Hoyle Road, CH47 3AG

When: Saturday, November 30 from 12 midday to 4pm

Wirral Christmas Craft Fayre Neston Civic Hall

On offer at this Christmas Craft Fayre are the finest local artisans with stalls such as plants, candles, ceramics and many more.

Where: Sunday, November 24 from 12 midday to 4pm

When: Neston Civic Hall, Neston, CH64 9PE

Wirral Christmas Craft Fayre Thornton Hough Village Hall

There will be stalls such as plants, candles, ceramics and much more available.

Where: Thornton Hough Village Hall, Manor Road, Wirral, CH63 1JB

When: Sunday, December 8 from 12 midday to 4pm

 

Christmas Fayre in aid of Rainbow House

In a bid to help raise much-needed funds for Rainbow House Charity, the unique Ladies Southport in partnership with H2 Events Management Service Ltd will host a Festive Christmas Market fun day.

Where: The Prince of Wales Hotel, Lord Street, Southport, PR8 1JS

When: Saturday, December 7, from 11am to 4pm

My stall at Churchtown Christmas Market

This is expected to be a beautiful event to stir up some Christmas vibes in the picturesque setting of Churchtown.

You can buy unique gifts for loved ones.

Where: Botanic Road, Southport

When: Friday, December 6 2019 at 2pm until 8pm.

Aintree Christmas Market

The increasingly popular Aintree Christmas Market is back for its third year.
More than 50 local businesses trade each day.

There will be fair rides and a food truck.

Where: Aintree Racecourse

When: November 30 at 11am to December 1 at 4pm.

Indoor Christmas Fayre

Go to the Indoor Christmas Fayre at Willowbrook - The Living Well on Saturday, December 7 between 11am to 3pm.

There will be a range of stalls selling everything from sweets and chocolate, homemade crafts and toiletries to books and Christmas wreaths.

There will also be tombola, raffles and children’s games.

And you can visit the Winter Wonderland and Santa’s Grotto - entry charge £5 for grotto.

Where: Borough Road, St Helens, WA10 3RN

When: Saturday, December 7 between 11am to 3pm

 

Crafty Christmas Fair St Helens Mansion House

There will be more than 50 stalls available and a grotto.

Crafty Christmas Fair at the Mansion House, St Helens.

Where: St Helens Mansion House, Victoria Park, City Road, St Helens, WA10 2UE

When: November 24, 2019

Chester Christmas Market

A handmade, historical, magical experience awaits you with 70 traditional wooden chalets which will surround the sparkling tree.

Discover unique, locally made produce and with handmade crafts, jewellery, accessories and stocking fillers.

Chester Christmas Market is open seven days a week and is located in the city’s beautiful Town Hall Square opposite Chester Cathedral.

Where: Town Hall Square, Northgate Street, Chester, CH1 2HJ

When: From November 16 to December 22: Sunday to Wednesday 10am to 6pm; Thursday, Friday and Saturday from 10am to 8pm.
