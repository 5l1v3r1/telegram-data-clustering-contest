Who left X Factor: Celebrity last night in the fourth live show of the series?

17 Nov 2019, 10:06

X Factor: Celebrity is inching closer to its final, with just seven remaining acts taking to the stage in Saturday night’s quarter-final.

With the show having lost two acts from each of its previous live shows, Saturday’s show saw just one act lose their place in the competition, with the bottom two having to sing to win their place in the semi-final next weekend.

With Simon having axed the ‘safe seats’ earlier in the show, nobody had a guaranteed passage to next week – but who ended up in the sing-off and who was sent home?

Here’s what happened on the night…

Who left X Factor: Celebrity last night?

Saturday night’s show saw Glee star Kevin McHale take on footballer-turned-actor Vinnie Jones in the sing-off.

And while Kevin performed Labrinth’s Something’s Got To Give in his bid to remain in the competition, Vinnie performed an acapella rendition of Irish shanty The Galway Shawl, which he dedicated to his late wife Tanya.
