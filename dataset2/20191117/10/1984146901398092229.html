Strictly Come Dancing fans furious after spoiler reveals potential finalists have been kicked out

Viewers blast exit news leaked on social media

17 Nov 2019, 10:49 by Sophie Doughty

Fans of Strictly Come Dancing have been left furious after a results spoiler revealed one of the favourites for the final will be booted off the show tonight.

Viewers branded the decision "totally wrong", after the results, due to be aired on television this evening, were leaked on social media last night.

One tweeted: "Absolutely gutted #StrictlySpoiler."

While another commented: "Noooooooo #StrictlySpoiler."

A third remarked: "Boooo my fave has gone #StrictlyComeDancing2019 #strictly #StrictlySpoiler."

"Ah come on..it was a complete set up #StrictlySpoiler," another fumed.

Another wrote: "I shouldn't have searched #StrictlySpoiler. My life is over."

Last night, the remaining seven couples took to the floor to perform at the famous Blackpool Tower Ballroom.

However, fans will have to wait until tonight to discover the remaining celebs' fate, as well as watch a huge Westlife performance.
