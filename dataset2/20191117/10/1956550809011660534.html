29 top Christmas brunches in Dubai

Book these crackers for Christmas Day

17 Nov 2019, 10:20 by Amy Mathieson

Christmas is coming, and it’s time to start planning the main event – Christmas Day brunch. Whip out those party hats, crack open the mince pies, throw on some Michael Bublé and peruse our top picks for your festive feast this year.

The Big Buffet

Accents

If you’re a Christmas party animal, We Brunch at Accents has your name all over it. Dress up in your best festive partywear and make your way to the Marina. Expect banging tunes, a lively vibe and a classic Christmas roast, all topped off with a boogie on the terrace overlooking the yachts.
Dhs425 (bubbly). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. InterContinental Dubai Marina (04 446 6669).

Boardwalk
A Christmas buffet with al fresco views of the Dubai Creek? Sign us up. Boardwalk will be putting on a festive feast on the big day, filled with classic Christmas dishes, including a roast with all the trimmings. Pass us those roast potatoes.
Dhs335 (soft drinks), Dhs495 (house beverages), Dhs595 (sparkling), Dhs185 (kids aged six-12), free (kids below six). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. Dubai Creek Golf & Yacht Club, Deira (04 295 6000).

Brasserie 2.0
One of the best buffet spreads in the city, you can’t go wrong at this glam bumper brunch. Serving up everything from a full roast dinner to a massive seafood counter, if you like the finer things in life this buffet could be a top choice for you and your gang.
Dhs525 (soft drinks), Dhs675 (house beverages), Dhs925 (bubbly), Dhs250 (kids aged five-12). Wed Dec 25, 1.30pm-4.30pm. Le Royal Méridien Beach Resort & Spa, Dubai Marina (04 316 5550).

Bubbalicious
If you’ve heard of Friday brunch, then you’ll have heard of this massive buffet. With more than ten live cooking stations across three new restaurants – Sui Mui, Baba and Mina’s Kitchen – there’s heaps to choose from. Mountains of cheese, rows of potatoes and a huge assortment of festive desserts. This one will cater for everyone, plus winter is the best time to sit out on the terrace and enjoy the live entertainment.
Dhs595 (soft drinks), Dhs795 (bubbly). Wed 1pm-4pm. The Westin Dubai Mina Seyahi Beach Resort & Marina, Al Sufouh (04 399 4141).

Caravan
There are few things that scream “Christmas in Dubai” than a beachside Christmas lunch. The Ritz-Carlton Dubai’s Caravan restaurant throws an excellent festive brunch and it’s all set in the pretty gardens of the hotel, with the beach and Arabian Gulf within your sights. There’s live entertainment on the terrace, heaps of dishes from chilled seafood and sushi to traditional roasts and a massive cheese counter – and don’t miss those desserts. It’s family-friendly, too as there’s a special buffet for the little ones as well as a bouncy castle, face painting, balloon animal making and games galore. Get ready for a visit from the jolly man in red, too.
Dhs595 (soft drinks), Dhs695 (house beverages), Dhs895 (bubbly). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. The Ritz-Carlton Dubai, JBR (04 318 6150).

Ewaan

Nothing says luxury at Christmas than at a hotel literally named Palace. The hotel’s all-day dining restaurant is throwing a massive gourmet buffet this year with turkey and traditional dishes, as well as modern cuisine – think mezze, curries and more – all set to a live band. Plus, if you book before December 15 you’ll get 15 percent off, which is not to be sniffed at.
Dhs445 (soft drinks), Dhs605 (house beverages), Dhs830 (bubbly), Dhs225 (kids aged six-11), free (kids aged five and under). Palace Downtown, Downtown Dubai (04 888 3444).

Flow Kitchen

This family-friendly brunch at Fairmont The Palm is offering whole smoked salmon and Christmas turkey with all the trimmings. And if you have a sweet tooth make a beeline for this (via your dentist) as there are mincemeat tarts, panettone bread pudding, festive gingerbread cookies and more. Plus there’s a visit from Santa to look forward to if you’re dining with the kids.
Dhs395 (soft drinks), Dhs198 (kids aged six-12), free (kids under five). Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-4.30pm. Fairmont The Palm, Palm Jumeirah (04 457 3457).

Jumeirah Al Qasr
This is one of the most famous brunches in Dubai, and with good reason. Jumeirah Al Qasr goes all-out every week, but no more so than at Christmas. And if you have visitors in town who like copious amounts of food, this will impress. Set across its own private beach, this brunch has a massive spread of Christmas treats with dishes coming from The Hide, Arboretum and Al Hambra. The hardest part will be choosing what to go for first, we recommend doing a full lap and choose wisely before you end up too full for pud.
Dhs695 (soft drinks), Dhs795 (house beverages), Dhs895 (bubbly). Free (kids under four), Dhs310 (four-11 years old), Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-4pm, Jumeirah Al Qasr, Umm Suqeim (04 432 3232).

Jeema
Fancy getting away from it all this year? Take a trip out of the city and head to Hatta for a family-friendly Christmas brunch. JA Hatta Fort Hotel is throwing a massive buffet with a carving station with traditional stuffed turkey, salmon dishes, a seafood bar with poached prawns, octopus and mussels and classic desserts. Santa will be around for a visit too, so don’t forget to be nice.
Dhs250 (soft drinks), Dhs350 (house beverages). Wed Dec 25, noon-4pm. JA Hatta Fort Hotel, Hatta (04 809 9333).

Manava
This all-day dining eatery’s name means welcome in Tahitian, and it’s greeting the festivities this year with a range of Christmas treats. Oysters? Tick. Caviar? Tick? Roast dinner? Tick. Live music comes from singer-songwriter Lisa Goldin, while there are also loads of kids’ activities to check out, including face painting, bake your own gingerbread and a meet-and-greet with the main man – Mr S. Claus. Plus book before December 20 and you’ll get 15 percent off.
Dhs485 (soft drinks), Dhs595 (house beverages), Dhs260 (aged 12-20), Dhs130 (kids aged five-11), free (kids under four). Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-4pm. Sofitel Dubai The Palm, Palm Jumeirah (04 455 5656).

QE2

Forget decking the halls, deck the, erhm, decks instead. The QE2 will be hosting a huge family-friendly Christmas Day brunch. Taking place on the indoor and outdoor decks, expect a festive buffet, live cooking stations and a host of entertainment from Santa and his helpers. Oh, and a live band playing classic Christmas tunes.
Dhs445 (house beverages), Dhs150 (kids below 12), free (kids below four). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. Port Rashid, Mina Rashid (04 526 8811).

Raffles Garden
Do you want to build a snowman? Sorry you can’t actually, but Raffles is transforming to a winter wonderland ,which is as close as you’re going to get in this weather. With heaps of cheese, loads of seafood, live cooking stations and of course a carvery, you’re all set for a super festive feast. Not a fan of Christmas pud? Just let it go.
Dhs395 (soft drinks), Dhs550 (house beverages), Dhs685 (bubbly), Dhs195 (kids aged seven-12), free (kids under six). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4.30pm. Raffles Dubai, Oud Metha (04 324 8888).

The Irish Village
Pub lunch for Christmas dinner? Yes, please. Time Out Dubai’s Pub of the Year 2019 has revealed its big Christmas Day celebration, with a visit from the jolly man in the big red suit, a festive lunch and live Christmassy tunes to get you grooving to that jingle bell rock. Santa will be coming to pay a visit to all families, along with handing out special gifts to little ones aged two to 12. Dig into a buffet including a full carvery.
Dhs199 (food only).  Dhs99 (kids aged two-12), free (kids under two). Wed Dec 25, noon-4pm. The Irish Village, Garhoud (04 282 4750).

The Market
Gather the gang as the Festive Family Feast will offer up three and a half hours of classic Christmas grub, plus drinks, a huge kids’ buffet and supervised area, plus there will even be a visit from Santa, who’s bringing gifts for the little ones. There are 40 different types of desserts, including what’s being described as Dubai’s “biggest panettone cake” as well as a huge Christmas buffet. There will also be a live band singing all of the classic Christmas tunes, so get ready to belt out a bit of Mariah Carey or Slade. A top choice for families as this picked up a Highly Commended gong at our Time Out Dubai’s Kids Awards Best Family Brunch 2019.
Dhs400 (soft drinks), Dhs550 (house beverages), Dhs750 (bubbly), Dhs150 (kids aged five-12), free (kids under five). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4.30pm. Hilton Dubai Al Habtoor City (04 435 5577).

The Restaurant
Roast turkey in the shadow of Burj Khalifa? Now you know you’re in Dubai (in case you didn’t before). For a traditional feast then the restaurant at Address Boulevard could be for you – especially if you’re a fan of beef Wellington. The venue is promising a tasty version of the classic dish, as well as roast turkey, live stations and more. Plus Santa will be on hand to entertain the kids.
Dhs295 (soft drinks), Dhs395 (house beverages), Dhs495 (bubbly). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-5pm. Address Boulevard, Downtown Dubai (04 561 8888).

Wanderlust

This party brunch has had a festive make-over for Christmas – and is set poolside at Aqua for an extra twist (just think of the ‘gram while your mates are freezing at home). There will be live entertainment from a DJ, a saxophonist and dancers to keep you bopping, while there will also be ten live cooking stations and loads of beverage stations so you won’t run out of choice. There’s even a post-brunch party at Vault for sky-high Crimbo views.
Dhs395 (house beverages), Dhs545 (bubbly). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. JW Marriott Marquis Dubai, Business Bay (04 414 3000).

Yalumba

It’s time to party, Christmas-style. One of Dubai’s most famous and longstanding party brunches has a whopping Christmas Day event to get you rocking around the Christmas tree. There will be loads of live cooking stations, free-flowing drinks and of course a live DJ to get you up and dancing. Stick around from 4om until 9pm as there’s a 20 percent discount on drinks during happy hour.
Dhs399 (sparkling), Dhs529 (bubbly). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. Le Méridien Dubai Hotel & Conference Centre, Garhoud (04 702 2455).

The À La Carte

BiCE Ristorante
If you love traditional Italian dishes, then this is a Christmas dinner for you. Head to this BiCE on JBR – where you’ll get a three-course sharing menu. Plus, kids under six get a free meal. Now that’s a present.
Dhs250 (soft drinks), Dhs325 (house beverages), Dhs450 (bubbly), half price (kids aged six-12), free (kids under six). Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-4pm. Hilton Dubai Jumeirah, JBR  (04 318 2520).

BOCA
This Mediterranean spot in DIFC is whipping up a special à la carte menu with three-hours of unlimited drinks. Tuck into plates of festive food for Christmas Day, Eve and any other day of the week until January 18, 2020.
Dhs295 (soft drinks), Dhs395 (house beverages), Dhs590 (premium grape), free (kids below eight). Until Jan 18, 2020. Daily 9pm-midnight. Gate Village 6, DIFC (04 323 1833).

COYA Dubai
A Time Out Dubai award-winner, Peruvian restaurant COYA Dubai is setting up a limited-time festive à la carte menu, with main courses including pollo asado, hombre de cordero, flan de tenera a la parilla. In fact, festive celebrators will have to pre-order the menu 72 hours in advance. Pretty special.
Dishes from Dhs280, Dhs640 (prix fixe menu). Tue Dec 24-Thu Dec 26, 7pm-1am. Four Seasons Resort Dubai at Jumeirah Beach (04 316 9600).

Marea
This Michelin-starred New York restaurant is serving up an Italian Christmas dinner, so if you have family in town and your skills in the kitchen are limited to chopping Brussels sprouts – then give this one a go. The meal includes ravioli in a rich chicken broth, roast turkey and lamb, and panettone – a classic Italian dessert. See page 34 for more.
Dhs350 (soft drinks), Dhs700 (house beverages). Wed Dec 25, noon-9pm. Gate Village 7, DIFC (04 583 6366).

Marina Social
At a restaurant from a Michelin-starred British chef, you’d expect nothing less than ten out of ten for potatoes, and you’re in lucky. Sure, you’ll feel a million miles away from Blighty as you sit on the al fresco terrace overlooking the Marina in your party hat, but you’ll be happy enough digging into your Christmas pud.
Dhs435 (soft drinks), Dhs595 (house beverages), Dhs695 (bubbly), Dhs895 (premium bubbly). Wed Dec 25, noon-4pm, 7pm-11pm. InterContinental Dubai Marina (04 446 6664).

Prime68 Steakhouse
Carnivore with a head for heights? You can’t really get better than this sky-high steakhouse then. Pick your steak and admire the view.
Dhs650. Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. JW Marriott Marquis Dubai, Business Bay (04 414 3000).

MasterChef, The TV Experience
You don’t need to be a fan of the global TV show to enjoy this one. The food here is excellent and you’ll be treated to a three-course set menu from top chefs.
Dhs249 (soft drinks), Dhs378 (two hours house grape), Dhs478 (two hours premium grape). Wed Dec 25, noon-3pm 7pm-11pm. Millennium Place Marina (04 550 8111).

Rockfish
If you’re a seafood fan then this Mediterranean seafood dining spot is throwing a festive fishy feast. With an à la carte menu crafted by Chef Marco Garfagnini and set in the shadow of Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, if you don’t like big buffets this is one to check out.
Prices vary. Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-3.30pm & 6.30pm-11.30pm, Jumeirah Al Naseem, Umm Suqeim (04 432 3232).

Wavehouse
This family-friendly restaurant wasn’t open last Christmas, but the idea of bowling and burgers, pool and pints on Christmas Day sounds top-notch to us. We’ll be the ones on the surf machine before lunch.
Prices and times vary. Atlantis The Palm, Palm Jumeirah (04 426 1000).

Splashing out

Al Mahara
It’s Christmas – it’s time to treat yourself. And where better to do that than at Burj Al Arab Jumeirah? You can celebrate the festive season at Al Mahara, set next to the massive aquariums. What’s more special than that? Present enough, we say.
Dhs995 (six-course degustation lunch and dinner menu). Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-2.30pm. Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, Umm Suqeim (04 301 7600).

Ossiano
Christmas dinner – under the sea. No you’re not Sebastian from the Little Mermaid but your family will be thrilled with this stunning meal. Splash out (ahem) for this?
Dhs540 (set menu), Dhs200 (kids). Wed Dec 25, 12.30pm-2.30pm. Atlantis The Palm, Palm Jumeirah (04 426 1000).

Park Hyatt Dubai

One of the classiest spots in the city, this chic hotel has a super-fancy brunch this Christmas. We’re not saying it comes cheap, however, it’ll set you back more than Dhs1,000 for premium bubbly, but if you’re looking for a stellar brunch you know where to go. With a room full of fresh seafood, loads of roasts and one of the most impressive cheese spreads in the city, there’s a reason it’s our Best Brunch 2019. One for the bucket-list
Dhs595 (soft drinks), Dhs895 (house bubbly), Dhs1,395 (premium bubbly), Dhs295 (kids aged six to 12), free (kids under six). Wed Dec 25, 1pm-4pm. Park Hyatt Dubai, Deira (04 602 1234).
