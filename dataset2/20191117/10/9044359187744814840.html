Mariel Padilla gives birth to her second girl!

17 Nov 2019, 10:37

Mariel Padilla is now a mom of two!

The celebrity has given birth to a bouncing baby girl on Saturday, husband Robin Padilla announced on Instagram.

"Today Maria Gabriela de Padilla was born. God is great!" Robin said as he posted a photo of Mariel on a hospital bed as her father watched over her. "My wife had her dream fulfilled again."

According to Robin, their new daughter was delivered by natural birth. "Everything was very smooth," he added, sharing Mariel's family in the US took care of his wife.
