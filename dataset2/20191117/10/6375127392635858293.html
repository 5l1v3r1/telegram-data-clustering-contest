2 rape suspects jailed by cops

17 Nov 2019, 10:22 by Alfred P. Dalizon

TWO men accused of rape were arrested by Lanao del Norte policemen in separate manhunt operations on Saturday, Police Regional Office 10 director, Brigadier General Rolando B. Anduyan said.

One of the suspects identified as Roder Rabago, a 26-year- old driver residing in Purok 3, Barangay Balili, Kapatagan, Lanao del Norte was arrested for raping  his neighbor around 4 a.m. Saturday.

An investigation showed that the 27-year-old victim was walking on her way home when she was followed by the suspect who later dragged her into a banana grove where he sexually abused her.

Anduyan said that after receiving the complaint, local policemen conducted a hot pursuit operation which led in the arrest of the suspect in his house. The suspect was positively identified by the victim as the one who raped her, the official said.

The second suspect identified as Allan Lobeban, 41, a resident of Purok 2, Tubod, Rosary Heights, Iligan City  was arrested by a team of policemen in Bgy. Tanguegiron at 7:30 a.m. Saturday on the strength of a warrant of arrest for rape issued by Judge Dennis Vicoy of the Tubod Regional Trial Court Branch 12.
