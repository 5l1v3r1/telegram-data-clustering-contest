Australia's Ashton Agar suffers laceration to his nose

17 Nov 2019, 10:55 by IANS

ADELAIDE: Left-arm spinner Ashton Agar on Sunday suffered a laceration to the bridge of his nose in Western Australia's Marsh One-Day Cup game against South Australia.

Ashton Agar grassed a catch hit by his own brother Wes at the Karen Rolton Oval, according to a report.

Ashton ran around to take a catch off his younger sibling Wes Agar at mid-on, but slipped and copped the ball right between his eyes, the report said.
