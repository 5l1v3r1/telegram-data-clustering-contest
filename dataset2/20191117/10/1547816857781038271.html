Taapsee Pannu Loves To See Movies In Theatres, Hrithik Roshan's War Is The Latest

17 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Amruta Patel

On Big B's quiz show Kaun Banega Crorepati, Taapsee Pannu was asked to guess the song Ghungroo from War. The actress quickly answered and added how she loved watching the film in theatre

The popular TV show Kaun Banega Crorepati has seen several celebs make an appearance to support noble causes or just to promote their upcoming projects. Joining this league after Sonakshi Sinha and TV actress Sakshi Tanwar is bankable star Taapsee Pannu. The Saand Ki Aankh actress graced her presence on the show to support social worker Karamveer Samanta belonging to Odisha. Since the actress and Big B share a great rapport thanks to their on screen projects, we expected some fun and candidness.  

 

During one of the show’s segment, when audio of the song Ghungroo was played and Taapsee was asked to guess the movie’s name, the actress just quickly replied saying ‘WAR’.  Further she said that she enjoyed watching the film in theatre. Taapsee’s performance in her recent release Saand Ki Aankh was highly appreciated. Her next film will be Anubhav Sinha’s Thappad that’s releasing in 2020.
