Hired gun caught after killing Bukidnon target

17 Nov 2019, 10:25 by Alfred P. Dalizon

A SUSPECTED member of a gun-for-hire syndicate operating in Bukidnon province was captured by the local police which pursued him shortly after he shot and killed a target in Valencia City at 6 p.m. Saturday, Police Regional Office 10 director, Brigadier General Rolando B. Anduyan announced yesterday.

Leovy Batistil, said to be a member of the Batistil Criminal Group operating in the province, was caught by members of the Valencia City Police Station intelligence division in a hot pursuit operation, said the Northern Mindanao police director.

According to Anduyan, the suspect was identified as the armed man who shot and killed one Romeo Tilen in Purok 3, Petition, Barangay Barobo, Valencia City for still unknown reasons.

Immediately after they received a call from concerned citizens regarding the murder case, the local police conducted a hot pursuit operation which led in the capture of the suspect.

Anduyan said the suspect is now undergoing investigation at the Valencia City police headquarters. He will be facing charges for murder and illegal possession of firearm and ammunition, the official said.
