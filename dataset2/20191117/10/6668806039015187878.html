In Photos: Pop sensation Katy Perry returns to the States after her Mumbai concert

17 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Kajol Mathur

Katy Perry last night took over Mumbai and raised the mercury at her first-ever concert here. She performed with pop star Dua Lipa and made the night a memorable one for everyone present. Katy touched down in Mumbai earlier this week and did several interesting activities. During her visit, she was seen bonding with Karan Johar, Alia Bhatt, Gauri Khan, Ananya Panday and several other Bollywood celebs.

After staying here for almost a week, Katy kissed India goodbye last night. She was snapped at the Mumbai airport wearing a grey tracksuit, a baseball cap and sneakers. Before entering inside, she was also seen giving a few autographs to fans.
