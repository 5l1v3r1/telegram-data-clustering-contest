AFGH vs WI Dream11 Team Prediction : Afghanistan Vs West Indies Third T20 Best Dream 11 Team

17 Nov 2019, 10:54 by Gautam Kapoor

AFGH vs WI Dream11 : Series precariously poised ahead of today’s decider in what looks set to be an intoxicating concoction of an encounter.

Afghanistan needed five matches to triumph over West Indies in white ball cricket but the side will be glad with the timing of that elusive win. Pulling off their first win over the visitors yesterday to draw parity in the ongoing three match T20 series, Afghanistan managed to not only keep their aspirations of salvaging something alive but keep the series competitive and fresh as well with the win.

Their win in the second match on Saturday fully falls to the side’s bowlers who ended up being hailed and serenaded with a slew of accolades for their vigour, resolve, determination and character to leave West Indies flummoxed. Despite heading into their defence of a mere 148 distraught and low on confidence with the score making for a sorry reading, Afghanistan pulled off a stunning 41 run win to halt the opposing at a mere 106.

Where the result saw Afghanistan manage to make amends for their previous results and finally unlock the true potential of their outfit, for West Indies the manner of the defeat doesn’t make for good reading ahead of the culminating tie. The side have little time to paper over the cracks and bridge over the gaps prevelant yesterday, a rhetoric which is bound to engross us in a riveting and an edge of the thriller come the series decider.

Pitch Conditions And Toss

As the matches have kept coming on this surface, the track has started waning and wavering to go on a downward spiral for the batters. With their being no time for the ground team to address the crumbling nature of the surface, the slower bowlers will flourish today as well with the side winning the toss set to come out and bat first today.
