Woman's ludicrously easy trick to clean mouldy shower without scrubbing

The woman has been lauded by social media users for sharing the hack - which achieves phenomenal results without scrubbing

17 Nov 2019, 10:27 by James Rodger, Courtney Pochin, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

A woman's tip for making her mouldy shower tiles look as good as new has gone viral.

The woman has been lauded by social media users for sharing the hack - which achieves phenomenal results without scrubbing.

She has shared the simple but very effective way she managed to remove the mould from the grouting in her bathroom.

And, remarkably, she achieved the feat without doing any scrubbing.

The unnamed woman revealed she'd found a simple hack - and it only cost around £2 to do.

Mirror Online reports the woman claims she soaked a pieces of the cotton wool in the cleaning product and packed them all around the edges of the tiles.

The cotton wool was left on the tiles overnight and by the morning, the difference was incredible.

She re-shared her original photo as well as a new one showing the results of the cleaning trick.

"Thank you to everyone who has commented," she said. "I used cotton wool and bleach and have attached photos of the results. Wow!"

Dozens of people liked her post, with one person commented: "This is amazing!"

 

Many people responded to her post, sharing ideas, including using toilet bowl cleaner and just re-sealing the tiles.
