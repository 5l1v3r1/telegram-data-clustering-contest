Angola president happy with election of main opposition leader

17 Nov 2019, 10:21 by Abdur Rahman Alfa Shaban Agencies

Angolan president Joao Lourenco has welcomed the country’s new opposition chief to the political playground with a message posted on his Twitter handle.

“I congratulate Adalberto da Costa Junior on his election as President of the largest opposition party. May this election represent the strengthening of opposition in the interests of democracy,” a translation of the message posted in Portuguese read.
