Omarion breaks silence on B2K's Fizz dating his ex Apryl Jones

17 Nov 2019, 10:58

Omarion has finally broken his silence on the controversy surrounding his B2K bandmate Fizz dating his ex-girlfriend Apryl Jones, and he’s taking the high road stating they deserve to be ‘happy’.

Everyone from B2K fans to Meek Mill have had an opinion on the scandal since Fizz, 33, and Apryl, 32, went public with their romance in an episode of Love & Hip Hop Hollywood last month.

Their relationship has caused a stir as Omarion, 35, shares two children with Apryl – son Megaa, five, and daughter A’mei, three. He and Fizz, real name Dreux Frederic, have also known each other since childhood after rising to super stardom with boy band B2K in the early noughties.

Since confirming their relationship, Apryl and Fizz haven’t been subtle about their affections sharing cosy photos together on Instagram and intimate videos.

Those gripped by the scandal have been waiting for Omarion to comment but he’s seemed completely unbothered by the situation, maintaining a wall of silence until now.

Omarion spoke out on the situation for the first time in an interview with Vlad TV this weekend, and said: ‘I don’t feel no ways, I don’t feel any way about it.

‘I think that if they’re happy, then they should be happy.’
