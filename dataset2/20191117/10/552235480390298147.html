Police 'concerned' for schoolgirl, 14, reported missing in West Lothian

A search is ongoing for teenager Tya Miller who was last seen in the Newton area at 3.30pm yesterday.

17 Nov 2019, 10:13 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

Police are growing concerned over the whereabouts of a missing West Lothian teenager.

Tya Miller was last spotted in the Newton area at 3.30pm on Saturday, November 16.

Police Scotland have put out an appeal for the missing 14-year-old as officers continue their search.

She has been described by the force as being 5ft 9ins tall, with long fair/blonde hair.

 

Inspector Simpson from Livingston Police Station said: “Tya has been missing for a number of hours now, given her age there is understandable concern for her.

“We have a number of police resources looking for Tya and I am now looking for the assistance from anyone who may know where she is.”

A spokesperson for Police Scotland added: “If you believe you have seen Tya or have any information please contact 101 quoting incident no. 2593 of the November 16.”
