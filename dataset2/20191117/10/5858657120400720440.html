VPD respond to reports of suspicious package in Chinatown

17 Nov 2019, 10:35 by Lisa Steacy

Summary

The VPD swarmed the area of Keefer Street between Columbia and Main Sunday afternoon

The package was a fake explosive, and the investigation is ongoing

VANCOUVER (NEWS 1130) — Reports of a suspicious package led to a heavy police presence and road closures in an area of Chinatown Sunday.

The Vancouver Police Department swarmed the area of Keefer Street between Columbia Street and Main Street, along with the Emergency Response Team.

The package turned out to be a fake explosive, and while the investigation is ongoing there is no threat to the public.

The road was taped off for a period of time and closed to cars and pedestrians, but reopened around 2 p.m.
