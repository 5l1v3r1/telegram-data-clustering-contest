LONDON CALLING

Celtic star Edouard on Crystal Palace’s transfer radar with Rangers’ Morelos

17 Nov 2019, 10:04 by Alan Nixon

ODSONNE EDOUARD is on Crystal Palace’s radar as they hunt for a new striker.

The Celtic hitman, has emerged as a possible January target, with Roy Hodgson also interested in Rangers’ Alfredo Morelos.

Palace have £50million in the bank from the sale of Aaron Wan-Bissaka to Manchester United.

But Hodgson knows it would be tough to nick either player in the middle of a title fight.

Edouard has been in electric form for the Hoops with 12 goals and 12 assists so far this season.

He joined Celtic in 2018 in a £9million move from French champions PSG after a successful loan spell at Parkhead.

Yesterday we told that Edouard could miss over half of the Hoops European qualifiers next season after revealing his Olympics dream.

The Tokyo 2020 Under-23 tournament runs from July 23 to August 8, with France among the favourites.

Edouard is desperate to make the squad for Japan — but that would rule him out of key qualifiers.

The in-form striker insists he’s determined to play a leading role in Sylvain Ripoll’s squad in the Far East next summer.

He said: “It’s a very important competition, one that every sportsman dreams of participating in.

“I have waited a long time to be called up by France at this level and I am keen to do well so I make an impression.”
