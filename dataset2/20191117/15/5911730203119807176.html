1 dead after one-vehicle crash on I-275 in Hillsborough County

17 Nov 2019, 15:39 by WFTS Digital Staff

HILLSBOROUGH COUNTY, Fla. --- A man is dead after a one-vehicle crash early Sunday morning.

Florida Highway Patrol said 32-year-old Cliff Pierre Jacques was traveling northbound on I-275, north of Bears Ave., at a high rate of speed.

Jacques failed to slow down before the curve of the roadway and left the east side of the road.

The vehicle ran into a tree and the rear caught fire.

Officials said the driver suffered fatal injuries at the scene of the crash.

FHP said the driver was not wearing a seatbelt and believe alcohol was a factor.
