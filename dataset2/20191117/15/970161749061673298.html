Nicole Scherzinger 'motorboats' Louis Walsh and everyone clutches their pearls

17 Nov 2019, 15:44

Nicole Scherzinger has been slammed by fans who accused her of ‘motorboating’ an unsuspecting Louis Walsh on X Factor last night.

The two X Factor: Celebrity judges were celebrating when Nicole grabbed Louis’ head and pressed it against her chest.

Wearing a strapless, black dress, as she let go she then did a little shimmy by his head.

In all honesty, no one on the judging panel looked uncomfortable by it – with Simon Cowell just chilling behind the pair, living in the moment.

Of course, we shouldn’t judge an act by how ‘comfortable’ people appear, though, should we?

Naturally, the world clutched their pearls as they called out the Pussycat Dolls star for the act – angry because she’s on ‘a family show’ while also pointing out the riot should the gender roles be reversed.

‘Nicole rubbing her tits in Louis face and jiggling them, imagine if he’d done that with his crotch to her face!!’ a viewer seethed on Twitter, following the show.
