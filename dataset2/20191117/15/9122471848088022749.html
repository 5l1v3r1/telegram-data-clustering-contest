Danny Kanell Posts His New Top 8 After Week 12

17 Nov 2019, 15:21 by Andrew Holleran

College football analyst Danny Kanell has updated his personal rankings following Week 12 of the 2019 regular season. As always, they’re a bit controversial.

Kanell continues to not have LSU ranked No. 1, despite the Tigers’ undefeated record and big win at Alabama. Ohio State isn’t No. 1, either, despite the Buckeyes’ dominance.

Clemson is No. 1. Kanell says he’ll continue to have Clemson at No. 1 as long as they’re undefeated because they’re the reigning national champions.

That’s a bit ridiculous, but hey, it’s his poll.
