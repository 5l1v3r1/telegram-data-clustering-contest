Sinitta smiles and laughs it out as she takes a tumble leaving X Factor studio

17 Nov 2019, 15:13

Sinitta smiled it out after taking a tumble while leaving the X Factor studios last night… and honestly, what else can you do but laugh?

The 56-year-old So Macho singer was leaving the studio after the latest X Factor: Celebrity live show wearing a Loius Vuitton scarf, high-waisted purple skinny jeans, brown coat with fur collar and adorable dog Scarlett O’Hara in tow.

She must’ve lost her balance in the platform black boots she was wearing as the next thing she knew, she was hitting the cold, hard floor.

The trip may have knocked her down, but it didn’t knock her positive attitude, as she smiled and laughed while lying on her back.

A friend then kindly offered to help her up.

It looked like Sinitta wasn’t hurt in the ordeal, but it’s unclear if fur baby Scarlett left the scene unscathed.

Let’s just hope they managed to pick themselves up and dust themselves off.

Sinitta made headlines earlier this month when she surprised ex-boyfriend Simon Cowell with a cake celebrating his 60th birthday when she hilariously brought Simon a huge treat in the shape of his own hairy torso.

Last night’s X Factor had been a tough one for Simon after Vinnie Jones pleaded with him to save Kevin McHale and send himself home in the sing-off.

‘Simon…I feel that I’ve had a fantastic time, I’ve really enjoyed it – it’s been everything I’ve needed since my wife’s passing,’ he said.

‘This guy is a real singer, I think he can win it, for his career, I would like to step down and let him go through.’

But Simon decided: ‘The last person in the world I would like to have a fall out with is Vinnie Jones, which means we’re going to have to fall out because I’m going to insist that you stay in the competition.

‘This is not disrespect to Kevin, I am voting with my heart…I think you have a chance of winning this competition still,’ he added.
