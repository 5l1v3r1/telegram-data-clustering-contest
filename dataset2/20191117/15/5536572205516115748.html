Eagles Fans Chant “You Can Shove Tom Brady Up Your A–” Ahead of Sunday’s Matchup (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019, 15:20 by Darrelle Lincoln

Eagles fans certainly know how to make anything into a fight song.

Just ahead of Sunday’s matchup against the New England Patriots — a couple members of the fan base seem to be out and about near some casinos and chanting a peculiar new fight song that involved the Patriots legendary QB.

“You Can Shove Tom Brady Up Your A–” is what they came up with.
