Gemma Collins and Arg are back on as they unite for tile shop birthday party

The couple have had more up and downs than a rollercoaster but appear to be back on for good

17 Nov 2019, 15:38 by Irishmirror.ie

It's been quite the rollercoaster ride for Gemma Collins and James 'Arg' Argent but it appears they're back on solid ground.

The on-off on-off on-off couple may have broken up more times than we can keep count of, but they seem destined to be together.

After a brief split, Gemma and Arg, 31, proved they were meant for each other as they reunited for an appearance at the one year birthday party of a local tile shop in Essex.

Not as glamorous as the red carpets of the ITV Palooza which Gemma recently attended, but clearly not below the reality stars who looked happily in love.

Gemma, 38, was the perfect mix of high street and designer as she dressed down in a lilac tracksuit and jacket, topped off with a Dior headband and Chanel handbag.

Meanwhile Arg sported his trademark cardigan and shirt in navy, teamed with a pair of cream trousers and bright red trainers.

He had a sheepish grin on his face as he walked behind a beaming Gemma.

The pair delighted fans after they went away together to Dubai for a romantic break.

They had a flirty exchange on Instagram after Arg uploaded a snap from their mini holiday and boasted about happiness.

He shared a snap of himself at The Cheesecake Factory with the caption: "The pursuit of happiness," with a heart and Gemma penned a comment back saying: "You was staring at cheesecake here!!!!"

His sweet caption could be a hint at Gemma, who didn't approve, and instead blamed him for eyeing up the cheesecake over her.

Followers found her witty remark funny, and one penned back to her: "Howling," followed by a line of laughing faces.

It seems their on-off romance could finally be back on track again following his recent 'overdose' drama.
