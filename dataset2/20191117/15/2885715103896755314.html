SSC releases final vacancies for CGL 2017

SSC CGL 2017: The candidates can check the vacancies through the official website- ssc.nic.in

17 Nov 2019, 15:18 by Careers Desk

SSC CGL 2017: The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) released the final vacancies for the Combined Graduate Level examinations (CGL 2017). The candidates can check the vacancies through the official website- ssc.nic.in.

The commission declared the results of the Combined Graduate-Level (CGL) exam 2017. A total of 4,143 candidates qualified after the CGL tier-1, tier-II, tier-III recruitment examinations.

The candidates can also check the marks through the website- ssc.nic.in.

SSC CGL results 2017: How to check

Step 1: Visit the official website- ssc.nic.in

Step 2: Click on the ‘download result link’

Step 3: A pdf file with roll number of candidates will appear on screen

Step 4: Download it, and take a print out for further reference.

The selected candidates will get a salary as per the 7th to 10th pay commission including the grade pay of Rs 4,800 per month.

In the stage II exam, thousands of candidates took to the streets demanding cancellation of the exam over an alleged leak. The SSC conducts seven exams every year in stages — Tier I, Tier II, Tier III and Tier IV where a candidate is tested for mathematics, English and computer proficiency. The next step is document verification, which happens together for Tier III and IV candidates.
