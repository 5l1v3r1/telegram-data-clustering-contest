Bangladesh cricketer Mushfiqur Rahim faced the heat from the Indore crowd during the first Test against India. Rahim, who was stationed near the boundary ropes, fans decided to roast him and they started chanting, “Do rupay ki Pepsi, Rahim bhai sexy”. The Bangladesh wicketkeeper-batsman turned a deaf ear to all the chants coming his way.
