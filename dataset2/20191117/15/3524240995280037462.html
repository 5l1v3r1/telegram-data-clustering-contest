Kogi election: INEC loses electoral officer to boat mishap

17 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Success Nwogu

Success Nwogu, Abuja

The Kogi State Resident Electoral Commissioner, Prof. James Apam, on Sunday said an unnamed ad hoc electoral officer has died in a boat mishap in Ibaji Local Government Area of the state.

He stated that the deceased was a student of the Kogi State Polytechnic, Lokoja.

He added that the student was contracted for the governorship election but died while on election duty on Saturday.
