Confirmed: The official starting grid for the Brazilian Grand Prix

17 Nov 2019, 15:59 by Matt Gretton

Max Verstappen starts his second race from pole position. The Dutchman has Sebastian Vettel next to him while Lewis Hamilton and Valtteri Bottas will be breathing down his neck. This is what the grid looks like for the Brazilian Grand Prix.
