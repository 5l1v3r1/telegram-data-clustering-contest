Lil Reese Wants $1 Million for Interview Following Shooting, Posts Photo of Wound

17 Nov 2019, 15:52 by C. Vernon Coleman II

Lil Reese recently survived a scary ordeal, after being shot in Chicago last week. Now he wants nothing short of $1 million to tell his harrowing life story in an interview.

Reese was released from the hospital on Friday (Nov. 15), after spending five days recovering. He broke his silence on Twitter on Saturday night (Nov. 16) when a follower told the rapper, "Vlad gonna try to get that lil reese interview by any means," referring to DJ Vlad.

Reese responded that Vlad would have to pay a hefty price for his story, which he claims is more entertaining than some of the biggest artists in hip-hop.
