Harry Styles takes us back to summer with new song Watermelon Sugar

17 Nov 2019, 15:30

Harry Styles has stirred all kinds of nostalgia for the summer with his sweet new track Watermelon Sugar, which could easily have been a bop for the warmer months.

Served up in November instead, the former One Direction singer dropped the latest song from his forthcoming album Fine Line just hours after co-hosting Saturday Night Live.

Watermelon Sugar sees Harry, 25, lusting after a former love with lyrics including: ‘Tastes like strawberries on a summer evenin’, and it sounds just like a song / I want more berries and that summer feelin’, It’s so wonderful and warm.’

If he had to summarise his long-lost love in a month, it would be June, apparently.

Harry had teased Watermelon Sugar last month with a tweet in reference to the track Kiwi from his self-titled debut album, writing: ‘Kiwi walked so Watermelon Sugar could run.’
