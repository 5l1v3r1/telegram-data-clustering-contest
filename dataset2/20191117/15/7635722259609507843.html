13-year-old girl critically injured after machete attack in Co Fermanagh

17 Nov 2019, 15:11 by Newsroom

PSNI detectives have launched an attempted murder investigation after a group of men entered a house in Co Fermanagh armed with machetes.

Police were alerted at around 9.15pm last night that a number of men had entered the home at Trasna Way in Lisnaskea.

They forced their way in and attacked two of the occupants, a 41-year-old woman and a 13-year-old girl.

The young girl sustained serious stab wounds and is in a critical but stable condition in hospital.

The older woman was stabbed in the leg but has since been released from hospital.

A baby who was also in the house was not harmed in the incident.

Detective Sergeant Keith Monaghan said: "This must have been a terrifying ordeal for those involved and we are determined to find the men responsible.
