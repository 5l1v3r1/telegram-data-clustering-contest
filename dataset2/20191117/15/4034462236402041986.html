Dominate The Road In This Armored Law Enforcement Vehicle

And yes, it is available for you to purchase.

17 Nov 2019, 15:45 by Jared Rosenholtz

When you get to be the CEO of a major corporation, your personal safety starts to become a legitimate concern. And with that CEO level paycheck, chances are you can afford a pretty luxurious vehicle. That is why Canadian armored vehicle specialist Inkas builds heavily armored versions of luxury vehicles like the Mercedes G-Class and Bentley Bentayga. Inkas will even create an armored vehicle using the faster G63 AMG model.

These armored vehicles can stand up to pretty heavy assault including CEN 1063 BR6 ballistics and grenades. But if this still isn't enough protection and you prefer a vehicle with a "SWAT" experience, Inkas has just released the new 2020 Sentry Civilian, an armored law enforcement vehicle built for civilian use.
