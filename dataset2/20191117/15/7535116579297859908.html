One man gave new meaning to the word hitchhiking as he latched himself to a moving trailer to get to his destination.

The unidentified man, dressed only in jeans was seen hanging on to the back end of a trailer nearby Jalan Kempas Lama, Johor.

The video of the man was however unclear in the beginning but as the video went on the man stepped down from the trailer and ran away from the trailer as it approaches a junction.
