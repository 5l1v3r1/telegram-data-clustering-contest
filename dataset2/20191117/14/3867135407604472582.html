Male found with gunshot injuries in Lynnwood area

Reports of gunfire sent deputies to the 3700 block of 164th Street SW, where the victim was found.

17 Nov 2019, 14:30 by Herald Staff

LYNNWOOD — A male was shot and injured Saturday nightin north Lynnwood.

Reports of gunfire prompted the Snohomish County Sheriff’s Office deputies to respond in the 3700 block of 164th Street SW, the Sheriff’s Office said. He was found and taken to the hospital.

The Sheriff’s Office Major Crimes Unit was investigating the incident.
