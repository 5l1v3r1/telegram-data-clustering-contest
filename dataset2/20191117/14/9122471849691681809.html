Urban Meyer Reacts To Ohio State Football’s 10-0 Start

17 Nov 2019, 14:13 by Andrew Holleran

Ohio State continued to roll on Saturday, as the Buckeyes went to Rutgers and came away with a blowout victory. Ryan Day’s team is now 10-0 on the regular season.

It’s been a pretty perfect regular season for the Columbus, Ohio program, which has not lost any steam in the wake of Urban Meyer’s retirement.

The former Ohio State head coach, who stepped down following the 2018 season, continues to watch the Buckeyes closely.

Meyer, now an analyst for FOX, reacted to the Buckeyes’ 10-0 start on Twitter.
