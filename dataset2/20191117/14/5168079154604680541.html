His Majesty pardons more than 300 prisoners

17 Nov 2019, 14:08 by Times News Service

Muscat: His Majesty Sultan Qaboos Bin Said has issued a Royal pardon to 332 prisoners, including 142 from different nationalities, convicted in different cases.

His Majesty's Royal pardon coincides with the advent of the 49 National Day in consideration of prisoners' families.
