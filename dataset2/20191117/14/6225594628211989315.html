The Top 5 Social Media Stories Of This Week

17 Nov 2019, 14:51 by Geoff Desreumaux

Welcome to this week’s top social media stories: A new dating app, a new TikTok clone, a new look for YouTube and no more likes on Instagram.

The day has come: Instagram has started testing removing likes globally. As you can imagine, feelings differ depending on if you are a user, a Creator or a marketer. While first results are showing a decrease in overall engagement, users seem to be welcoming of the new “like-free” feed.

But Instagram was also busy “cloning” TikTok this week, with the launch of the long-awaited “Reels” in Brazil.

Want more? Here are the top 5 most talked about stories this week:

  • Instagram Is Testing Hiding Likes Globally Now
  • YouTube Introduces A New Look On Desktop And Tablets
  • S’more Is A New Dating App That Is About “Something More” Than Physical Attraction
  • Instagram Launches Reels, A TikTok Clone
  • WhatsApp Introduces Catalogs For Small Businesses
