India vs Bangladesh 2nd Test tickets: How to book tickets for IND vs BAN day-night Test in Kolkata?

17 Nov 2019, 14:15 by Dixit Bhargav

India vs Bangladesh 2nd Test tickets: The SportsRush present before you full ticketing details for the second IND vs BAN Test at Eden Gardens.

The second Test of the ongoing Bangladesh’s tour of India will start from November 22 at Eden Gardens in Kolkata. With India 1-0 up in the series, another victory for them will register a hat-trick of whitewashes by them in Test matches.

The second Test will be the first day-night Test played in India and also a first-ever instance for both India and Bangladesh. The visitors, who are playing their first series in the ongoing ICC Test Championship, will be looking to at least draw the match to get off the mark in the tournament.
