Thousands turn out for Halesowen's Christmas lights switch-on - with VIDEO

Christmas was sparked into life in Halesowen as thousands of people attended the town's annual lights switch-on.

17 Nov 2019, 14:47 by Jamie Brassington

The honours were carried out by Line of Duty actor Gregory Piper, 21, who is from also Halesowen.

He was joined on stage by Dudley's deputy mayor Hilary Bills - a Halesowen ward councillor - and local school pupil Pheobe Glover, who won a competition to be on stage for the event.
