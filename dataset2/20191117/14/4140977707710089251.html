QAL Vs MAR Dream11 Team, Probable Playing 11, Toss, Pitch And Match Report: A win assures Qalandars of qualification into the semi-finals from Group B.

Sitting atop of Group B ahead of making an advent into the dramatic land of their culminating encounter of the group stages, Qalandars will be looking to affirm themselves of qualification into the ensuing round come today. Not only will a win take them into the semi-finals but also assure them of a place in the pinnacles of the group, a set of events which are set to see the side come hot out of the blocks for this one.

After drawing their league opener, the side ended up putting in a resounding result in their previous outing in the league as they ripped into Northern Warriors to cut short their work. In what was an as comprehensive as they come performance, the side outshone their opponents in all the departments of the game to register the biggest win of the league till now on that day.

Batting first to score 112, it was the Qalandars’ bowling unit which really won the day as they ended up cutting short their opponent’s run chase at a mere 46. The 66 run win not only gave them important points but massively propelled their net run-rate, making it untouchable for the rest of ther counters.

Elsewhere, their opponents of the day were not quite so fortunate with the team’s dependent batting unit being disparaged in their league opener. The side registered a mere 88 runs, a score which was never going to be enough and the writing was always on the wall in their defence as the side were defeated by 9 wickets with Northern Warriors chasing down the target in just  overs.
