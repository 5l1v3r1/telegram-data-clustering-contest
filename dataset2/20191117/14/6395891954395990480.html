Last survivor of Hindenburg zeppelin disaster dies at 90, family says

17 Nov 2019, 14:24 by Bob D'Angelo, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

Werner Gustav Doehner, the last survivor of the Hindenburg zeppelin disaster in 1937, died Nov. 8 in Laconia, New Hampshire, a family member said. He was 90.

Doehner died from complications of pneumonia, The New York Times reported. His death was confirmed by his son, Bernie Doehner, the Times and CNN reported.
