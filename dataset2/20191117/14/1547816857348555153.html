Black Eyed Peas Fame William Accuses A Flight Attendant Of Racism; Shares His Ordeal On Social Media

17 Nov 2019, 14:25 by Divya Ramnani

American rapper Will.i.am has accused a flight attendant of racism and aggressive behaviour during his recent flight to Sydney, full story inside

Though rarely, celebrities are bound to come across an unpleasant flight experience at least once in their lives. One such case is that of American rapper William Adams, popularly known as Will.i.am, who allegedly underwent racist attacks during his recent flight. Taking to his Twitter account, the Black Eyed Peas star has accused a Qantas flight attendant of racism during his flight from Brisbane to Sydney. In fact, he also publicly shamed the attendant by sharing a picture of hers.

As per Williams, the “aggressive” encounter took place when the Qantas employee accused him of not listening to the safety demonstration and upon his exit, the rapper was met by five police officials. Sharing his encounter on social media, he wrote, "I’m currently on a flight from Brisbane to Sydney. I’m sorry to say me and my group have experienced they worse service due to an overly aggressive flight attendant. I don’t want to believe she [is] racist, but she has clearly aimed all her frustrations only at the people of colour."
