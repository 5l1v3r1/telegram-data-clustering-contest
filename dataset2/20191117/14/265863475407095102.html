The Idina Menzel And AURORA Frozen 2 Collaboration On Into The Unknown Is The New Let It Go And Brings Changes For Norweigian Singer

17 Nov 2019, 14:47 by Charisse Van Horn

The Frozen 2 soundtrack is here and the upcoming movie has its answer to Frozen’s hit song “Let It Go.” The sequel brings back the same team that led Frozen to great success and husband and wife songwriters Kristen Anderson-Lopez and Robert Lopez are back at the helm, undoubtedly creating more Oscar-worthy songs — such as the powerhouse ballad “Into The Unknown.”

A collaboration between Idina Menzel and Norwegian singer AURORA, “Into The Unknown” brings AURORA to the attention of Disney fans and many hope that this will be the beginning of more work between the singer and the company.

The premise of Frozen 2 is that Elsa is being wooed by dark magic where she might turn against everyone and everything she loves in the quest of power.  “Into The Unkown” is almost a call and response song as  AURORA voices the secret siren who is calling to Elsa.

“Into The Unknown” begins with AURORA’s haunting and ethereal vocals calling repeatedly to Idina with a series of  “oohs and ahhs” while Idina sings her response as Elsa to the melodic and hypnotic lure of her call.

The beginning of the song’s lyrics and chorus are as follows.
