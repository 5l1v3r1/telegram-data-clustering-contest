Win the Ultimate Ferry Pass

17 Nov 2019, 14:01

myGC have teamed up with City of Gold Coast & Hopo to give away the Ultimate Ferry Pass which invites you and your family to be part of the Hopo’s maiden voyage.

The first passengers will travel to the Mayor’s Christmas Carols presented by Mercedes-Benz Gold Coast at Broadwater Parklands on Saturday 7 December.

You will enjoy all of the entertainment at the Mayor’s Christmas Carols presented by Mercedes-Benz Gold Coast in a reserved section (bring your blankets!) plus nibbles on arrival and a goody bag.

As well as being part of the historic maiden voyage, you will also receive:

  • A family day pass to Sea World
  • A family pass to The Big Wedgie at Broadwater Parklands
  • A family pass to Deadly Hearts: A celebration of Australian Indigenous Music at HOTA starring Jessica Mauboy
  • A $200 gift card for Paradise Centre in Surfers Paradise
  • A family day pass to use on the ferry service (valid for 12 months)

The new ferry service offers a unique travel experience to our residents and visitors and provides them with greater access to our waterways and popular destinations.

The new ferry trial is part of the Gold Coast City Transport Strategy 2031 to provide more travel alternatives to the car.

The City of Gold Coast has been working in partnership with the State Government, Gold Coast Waterways Authority, Maritime Safety Queensland, Water Police, Marina Mirage and Sea World to develop the trial ferry service.

The two-year Hopo ferry service trial will initially stop at five popular waterfront precincts:

  • Sea World at Main Beach
  • Broadwater Parklands at Southport
  • Marina Mirage at Main Beach
  • Home of the Arts (HOTA) at Surfers Paradise
  • Appel Park at Surfers Paradise

If the ferry trial is successful, additional ferry stops may be considered.

Timetabled ferry services will start on Sunday 8 December 2019 and you can buy a day ticket and hop on and off as much as you want within one calendar day for $25, or purchase a single ticket from as little as $4.

Tickets can be purchased online at hopo.com.au, on the ferry, or at a Sea World Cruises terminal.

The ferry will stop approximately every hour, with one ferry heading upstream and the other heading downstream.

Competition opens Monday 18 November and closes Sunday 1 December 2019
