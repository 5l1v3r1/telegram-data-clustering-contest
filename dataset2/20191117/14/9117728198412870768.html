Watch: Max Verstappen's flying pole position lap for Brazilian Grand Prix

17 Nov 2019, 14:53 by Matt Gretton

Max Verstappen recorded his 2nd career pole position as the Aston Martin Red Bull Racing driver flew around Interlagos. The Dutchman was 0.300 faster than nearest rival Charles Leclerc. His teammate will line-up in 5th after being beaten by both Ferrari drivers and F1 world champion Lewis Hamilton.
