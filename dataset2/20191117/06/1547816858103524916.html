Nick Jonas Gives Out Major Jack Skellington Vibes As Priyanka Chopra Can’t Stop Laughing At His Antics- VIDEO

17 Nov 2019, 06:20 by Lubna Khan

Nick Jonas took to his Instagram to share a video, where he can be seen channeling his inner Jack Skellington, dressed the same way in a pinstriped suit and his wife Priyanka Chopra approves of his look and finds his antics funny!

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas are giving us major relationship goals- right from their PDA filled posts with each other, or their cute videos to their fun social media banter! They don’t miss out on any opportunity to shower love on each other’s posts and are always among the first ones to comment. The couple’s first wedding anniversary is in a few weeks, on December 2, and according to a report in People, Nick Jonas will be taking a few days off from his music tour to be with his better half. Despite their work keeping them away from each other, the two are always making time to be there for special moments.

Now, Nick Jonas took to Instagram to share a video on his Instagram, in which we can see him giving out major Jack Skellington vibes. For those who are not aware, Jack Skellington is a character from the 1993 film The Nightmare Before Christmas and the character is a skeleton dressed in a black pinstriped suit.  Dressed similarly, Nick Jonas can be seen saying “Happiness begins, Happiness continues”, and while several people commented that he is drunk, Priyanka Chopra found it funny! She took to comment on the post and wrote, “Hahaha,” with a fire emoji!  ALSO READ: Nick Jonas To Take A Break From His Music Tour, To Bring In First Wedding Anniversary With Priyanka Chopra

In other news,  Priyanka Chopra recently bought a house with Nick Jonas in Los Angeles, California. According to a report in the Wall Street Journal, Chopra and Jonas have invested Rs 144 crore for a home in hilly Encino neighborhood, Los Angeles. On November 13, 2019, the couple bought their home together after Nick Jonas sold his house earlier in August.
