All the reported crimes on Uttoxeter streets in just one month

Does your road feature?

17 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Ben Waldron

All the crimes committed and reported across Uttoxeter and the nearby areas have been revealed.

According to policing website police.uk, which gives a monthly record of all crimes reported in a particular area, there were a number of crimes reported across the Uttoxeter area.

They all happened in September 2019 - the latest available month of figures.

Figures from Staffordshire Police state that across Uttoxeter and the surrounding villages there were 80 crimes reported. A total of 43 happened in the town.

In Uttoxeter, the highest reported type of crime was violence and sexual offences, with 10, while there were no reports of bicycle thefts, drugs, possession of weapons and theft from a person.
