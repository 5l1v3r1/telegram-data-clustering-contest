I wasn’t sleeping but mourning my mother during 2020 budget reading – Isaac Adongo

17 Nov 2019, 06:23 by MyNewsGH

National Democratic Congress ( NDC) Member of Parliament(MP) for Bolgatanga Central, Isaac Adongo has denied claims by mainly New Patriotic Party(NPP) members that he was fast asleep as the 2020 budget and economic policy statement was being presented to Parliament by the Finance Minister but mourning his mother who died a fortnight ago

In a tweet sighted by MyNewsGh.com, the legislator denied sleeping in Parliament during the budget presentation, shaming supporters of the NPP for failing to get even what he wore on the day right.

“For the apologists of NPP who are circulating a picture of me in grey suit and a red tie purporting to be sleeping in Parliament when the Minister for Finance was reading the 2020 budget, shame on you. You’re not even smart. I was in black Nigerian attire mourning my beloved mum,” he tweeted.

Pictures of Mr Adongo seemingly taking a snooze during the budget reading went viral on social media in what critics of Government see as an equalisation attempt due to the negative press President Akufo-Addo’s alleged sleeping at a summit in Russia got and the subsequent criticism some of his appointees have had due to allegations of them sleeping at important events.

Members of the main political parties, the National Democratic Congress and the New Patriotic Party have sharing pictures of their opponents allegedly sleeping during important events.

During the budget presentation, pictures of several government officials and NDC MPs were spread on social media, alleging that they were fast asleep.
