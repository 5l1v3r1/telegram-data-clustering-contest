From Ranveer Singh-Deepika Padukone celebrating their first wedding anniversary to Katy Perry partying with B-town stars - here's all that went viral this week

17 Nov 2019, 06:37 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Just like any other week, Bollywood celebrities filled our timeline with beautiful updates. While some stars shared throwback pictures to mark Children's Day, others were busy partying with Katy Perry, who arrived in Mumbai for her music concert. The week started with some lovely pictures of Anushka Sharma from Bhutan and ended with some stunning clicks of Deepika and Ranveer from their anniversary celebrations.

Katy Perry parties hard with Bollywood stars
Recently, Karan Johar hosted a grand bash to welcome International pop singer Katy Perry to Mumbai. The bash was attended by the who's who of Bollywood including Karisma Kapoor, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Shahid Kapoor, Anushka Sharma, Alia Bhatt, Ananya Panday, Kajol, Karisma Kapoor, Arjun Kapoor, Malaika Arora and others. Several inside pictures and videos from the bash went viral on the internet and gave fans a glimpse of the glamorous night.

Tim Tim enjoys ice-cream with sister Inaaya
On the special occasion of Children's Day, proud mother Soha Ali Khan took to her social media handle to share an adorable picture of daughter Inaaya Naumi Kemmu with cousin Taimur Ali Khan. Taimur relished a cone ice cream, but what caught our attention was Inaaya's adorable expression. She drooled at the sight of ice-cream and it was all things cute.

DeepVeer's one year of togetherness
This week was a special one for Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh as they celebrated their first wedding anniversary. The couple jetted off to Tirupati with their families to seek blessings from the almighty. On the next day, they flew to Amritsar to pray at the Golden Temple. The couple even shared some moments from their visit to the holy places and left their fans gushing over their amazing chemistry.
