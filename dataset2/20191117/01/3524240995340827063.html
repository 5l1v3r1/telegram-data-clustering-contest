Fayemi attends Fayose son’s wedding in Lagos

17 Nov 2019, 01:28 by Agency Report

Ekiti State Governor, Dr Kayode Fayemi, on Saturday joined other well wishers to celebrate with the family of the immediate past governor of the state, Ayodele Fayose, whose son, Tomiwa, got married.

The event drew many important persons to the Haven Event Centre, Ikeja, Lagos

The event saw the duo burying their political differences to savour the moment.
