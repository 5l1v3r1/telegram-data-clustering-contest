Mamamoo's Hwa Sa, (G)-Idle's Soyeon, and Girls' Generation Taeyeon top November's individual girl group member brand repuation

17 Nov 2019, 01:10 by Yanderedan

The Korea Institute of Corporate Reputation has released November's Individual Girl Group Idol Brand scores, and Mamamoo's Hwa Sa, (G)-Idle's Soyeon, and Girls' Generation Taeyeon take the top 3 with 4,142,830, 3,354,929, and 2,729,781 points respectively.
