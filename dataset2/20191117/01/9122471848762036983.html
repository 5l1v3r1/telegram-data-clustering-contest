Iowa Football’s Tweet After Win vs. Minnesota Is Going Viral

17 Nov 2019, 01:46 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, the Iowa Hawkeyes hosted one of the only remaining undefeated programs in college football.

Despite entering with an undefeated record – and a win over then No. 4 Penn State – No. 8 Minnesota entered as an underdog. Saturday’s game between the Hawkeyes and Gophers shows why Las Vegas is king.

Iowa was a three-point favorite over Minnesota and left with a 23-19 win – thanks to a missed extra point attempt.

After the game, Iowa football’s official Twitter account decided to take a shot at the Minnesota football program. The Hawkeyes used Minnesota’s “Ski-U-Mah” chant against it.
