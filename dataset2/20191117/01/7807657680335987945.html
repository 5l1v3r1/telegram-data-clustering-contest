How to beat AT-ST in Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order

Remember to dodge.

17 Nov 2019, 01:45 by Asher Madan

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is an Xbox One, PC, and PlayStation 4 game that features exploration and a lot of Dark Souls-like combat. There are a lot of challenging bosses in the game, but the AT-ST on Zeffo is one of the worst. It's a massive tank that shoots lasers and drops grenades.

Luckily, there's a great way to defeat the machine. The trick is to get close to it and keep on hitting its legs. However, be careful because the AT-ST drops explosives and stun grenades. You can see their area of effect so they're relatively easy to avoid. The following video shows you exactly what you need to do.
