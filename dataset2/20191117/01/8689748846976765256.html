4SyteTv Music Video Awards 2019: Shatta Wale Bags 5 Awards And Ayat Wins Overall Best Video-(Full List Of Winners)

17 Nov 2019, 01:56 by Sammy Tuga

The 4syte music video awards is the biggest and most recognized music video award scheme in Ghana.

Since its inception10 years ago, music video directors and artistes have taken their creative skills a notch higher since the market has become a very competitive one.
