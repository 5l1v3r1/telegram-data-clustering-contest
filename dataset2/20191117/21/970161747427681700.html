Ant and Dec open I'm A Celebrity with Prince Andrew sweating' dig

17 Nov 2019, 21:58

Ant and Dec kicked off I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! by making a dig at Prince Andrew and fans are thrilled to have the pair back.

‘Ten celebrities, all of them very nervous, all of them sweating,’ Ant said.

‘Presuming they can sweat, not everyone can,’ Dec added with a cheeky smirk on his face.

The joke comes in reference to Saturday night’s interview with Prince Andrew on Newsnight, in which the royal family member and close friend of Jeffrey Epstein claimed that he couldn’t have been the man to sleep with Virginia Giuffre, an underage girl, because the man she described was sweaty whereas he ‘couldn’t sweat.’

He claimed: ‘I have a peculiar medical condition which is that I don’t sweat or I didn’t sweat at the time.’
