I'm A Celebrity's Ant & Dec make dig at Prince Andrew within seconds of new series starting

I'm A Celebrity returned to ITV on Sunday night and Ant & Dec had a harsh dig at Prince Andrew within seconds

17 Nov 2019, 21:31 by Simon Duke

Ant & Dec made a vicious dig at Prince Andrew's expense within seconds of I'm A Celebrity returning on Sunday night.

On the same day that the hit ITV show returned, Prince Andrew made headlines with his much talked about Newsnight interview.

In the televised chat with Emily Maitliss, the Duke of York denied having sex with Virginia Roberts, the teenager at the centre of the Jeffrey Epstein scandal – saying he is in the clear because he 'can’t sweat'.

Ms Roberts claims the Prince was “sweating profusely” as he danced in a nightclub – and that they had sex that night and on two further occasions.

But during a 50-minute BBC interview Prince Andrew said he can not even remember meeting her and, with regards to the sweating claim, he added "There’s a slight problem with the sweating because I have a peculiar medical condition which is that I don’t sweat or I didn’t at the time."

Using this as ammunition for their gag on I'm A Celebrity , which you can watch in the video from ITV above , Ant & Dec "Ten celebrities all of them nervous...all of them sweaty...presuming they can sweat!"

The Geordie duo went onto crack a similar joke just minutes later.
