#Kogi/BayelsaDecide: INEC winds down Situation Room

17 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Adesola Ayo-Aderele

Chairman, Independent National Electoral Commission, Prof. Mahmood Yakubu, formally wound down the Situation Room at the Commission’s headquarters, after two days of intensive monitoring of the Kogi and Bayelsa governorship elections; and Kogi West Senatorial and Brass 1 state constituencies elections.

This was made known via the commission’s verified Twitter handle @inecnigeria on Sunday evening

Yakubu thanked national commissioners, the technical team, directors and other staff members for their dedication and overall efforts.
