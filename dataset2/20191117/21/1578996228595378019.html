Mariah Carey Hung Out With Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds

17 Nov 2019, 21:46 by Erika Harwood

Turns out Mariah Carey’s son Moroccan (or “Rocky,” as she affectionately calls him) is a big Deadpool fan. Luckily for him, his mom is Mariah Carey and she has connections. Carey posted a photo to Instagram showing her two children, including Moroccan in a Deadpool mask, alongside Blake Lively and Deadpool himself, Ryan Reynolds.

“Rocky snuck up on an unsuspecting couple wearing a Deadpool mask,” she wrote on Instagram. “Will they ever recover?”

Carey recently opened up to Cosmopolitan about raising her children, saying that she wants them to have the childhood she didn’t, specifically when it comes to Christmas.

“As a kid, I always hoped for great Christmases, and we didn’t have them. My brother and sister would come back to wherever I was with my mom, and they’d be fighting and ruining the holiday. I would still be like, You know what? I just want to have the best time,” she said. “We do everything that I always wanted to do as a kid.”

She also recently shut down the idea that she would reunite with her ex-husband and the father of her two children, Nick Cannon, after he said he would go back and marry Carey all over again.

“Remarry? This is him making this up. He’s being silly,” she told a TMZ cameraman, who then asked her if she liked the liked the single life. “What is he, my last hope?”

Queen of Christmas. Queen of comebacks. Queen of motherhood.
