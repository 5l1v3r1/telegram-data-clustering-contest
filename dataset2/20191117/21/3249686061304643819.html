Fans beg SM Entertainment to change NCT Dream’s rotational concept as 4 members graduate

17 Nov 2019, 21:02 by ratmonster

Popular NCT subunit NCT Dream recently held their first ever solo concerts, “The Dream Show”. The group played multiple nights at the Jang-Chung Arena in Seoul.

On the final night, the members started getting emotional on stage, and ended up breaking down in tears, knowing that it would be their last show as a full 6 member group.
