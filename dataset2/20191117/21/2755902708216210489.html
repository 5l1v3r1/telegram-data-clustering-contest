Users unable to buy airtime after Ecocash Upgrade

17 Nov 2019, 21:33 by Philip Mangwiro

Ecocash Upgrade leaves users unable to buy airtime

Ecocash was down for system maintenance for 24 hours since yesterday November 16 and upon resumption of the mobile payment platform service, users found themselves unable to purchase airtime.

Upon logging in, one was welcomed by the screen below and no option to purchase airtime or bundles.

Citizens were quick to air out their frustration on Twitter.

“@CassavaSmartech
so the system upgrade was so that we wont be able to buy airtime on ecocash? Unbelievable. Can’t believe that you would make such a lame move. It’s high time another business takes over from you. Thats serious rubbish.
@NetOneCellular
mukana uyo torai muite mari,” said one Exavier Mugasa
“@econet_support
so we can’t buy our airtime using ecocash well done for the upgrade,” said Natsai Chiriga
“@EcoCashZW
after your system upgrade on #ecocash how do we buy airtime and data using ecocash? It seem you forgot to put back that option.,”said Rick

Support Staff from Cassavasmartech have been working overtime assuring users that the system is still intermittent with full functionality is yet to be restored.
