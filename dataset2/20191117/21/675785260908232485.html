Three men arrested after woman raped as two men watched in 'despicable attack'

Greater Manchester Police have arrested three men aged 33, 25 and 24 on suspicion of rape in connection with the incident in Mesnes Park, Wigan

17 Nov 2019, 21:08 by Sophie Halle-Richards

Three men have been arrested after a woman was raped in a park in Greater Manchester in what police have described as a 'despicable attack'.

The woman, believed to be in her 30s, was subjected to the horrifying attack in a park in Wigan on Saturday night.

She told police one man attacked her, while another two men stood by and watched.

She had been walking alone at around 9.20pm when she began talking to three men, reports the Manchester Evening News.

The four of them walked to nearby Mesnes Park, where the woman was raped.

A passer-by called police and officers attended a short time later, and arrested three men aged 33, 25, and 24 on suspicion of rape.
 

 

They all remain in custody for questioning.

Detective Sergeant Sarah Bell of GMP’s Wigan District said: "This was a despicable attack on a lone woman who is currently being supported by specialist officers.

"While we have arrested three people in connection with this incident, we are continuing to appeal to the public for information.

"Were you in the area at the time and do you have any information that can assist us? If so, contact police immediately."
