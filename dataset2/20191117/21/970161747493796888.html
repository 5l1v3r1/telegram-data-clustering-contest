I'm A Celebrity: Ant and Dec make digs about Caitlyn Jenner's '£500k fee'

17 Nov 2019, 21:29

I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here! kicked off with some cheeky opening banter from hosts Ant and Dec, as they made digs about Caitlyn Jenner’s rumoured fee for appearing on the show.

Reports from earlier this month claimed that Caitlyn, 70, is set to pocket a reported £500,000 for going into the jungle, said to be one of the largest fees the show has ever handed out.

The presenters joked that while most celebs go into the jungle to lose pounds, Caitlyn will in fact be gaining ‘500,000 of them’.

Introducing this year’s line-up, Dec said: ‘We’ve got reality TV royalty Caitlyn Jenner. Most people come into the jungle to lose a few pounds…’

Ant added: ‘But she’s going to be gaining 500,000 of them according to the papers.’

Dec told him: ‘Don’t believe everything you read in the papers, Ant. You know that.’

Just before the camp mates were introduced, Ant told viewers: ‘I’m A Celebrity 2019 can only have one winner.’

‘Caitlyn Jenner’s agent by the sound of it,’ Dec joked.

‘Okay, two winners,’ Ant said.

A source told The Sun about Caitlyn’s signing: ‘It’s a real bolt from the blue. They’ve asked her every year for a long time, but she finally felt the time was right.’

Olympic winner Caitlyn has a famous brood of children, including four Kardashian step-children.

Most notable of her children are models and fashion moguls Kendall and Kylie Jenner, who is the world’s youngest billionaire at the age of 21.

She is heading into the jungle with footballer Ian Wright, ex EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa, former Girls Aloud member Nadine Coyle and Rak Su singer Myles Stephenson.

In the first episode she quickly becomes close with Nadine, comforting the teary singer before the first challenge – a skydive into the jungle.

I’m A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here! airs daily at 9pm on ITV.
