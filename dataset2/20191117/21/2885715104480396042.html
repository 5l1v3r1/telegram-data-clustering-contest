Gujarat: Metro work in Gandhinagar to begin next year

The Centre has granted approval to a proposal of the Gujarat government to sanction Rs 5,384.17 crore for the project in February.

17 Nov 2019, 21:38 by Express News Service

Work for the Ahmedabad Metro Rail project’s phase connecting Motera and Mahatma Mandir in Gandhinagar will begin from June 2020 and it will be completed by March 2024, said an official release. It added that planning of the project was presented before Chief Minister Vijay Rupani at a meeting recently.

The release said the tenders for the works related to the first phase of the 28.26-km long stretch will be issued shortly. The Centre has granted approval to a proposal of the Gujarat government to sanction Rs 5,384.17 crore for the project in February, the release added.

Apart from the Chief Minister, the review meeting was attended by top officials from the Chief Minister’s Office, Urban Development department and Gujarat Metro Rail Corporation (GMRC).
