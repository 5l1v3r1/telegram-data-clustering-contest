Photos: Lamar Jackson, Deshaun Watson Share Heartfelt Moment After Game

17 Nov 2019, 21:58 by Matt Hladik

Lamar Jackson and Deshaun Watson are two of the NFL’s brightest young stars at the quarterback position, and both have been mentioned in the MVP conversation this season.

Jackson and Watson faced off this afternoon when the Texans traveled to Baltimore to play the Ravens. Jackson and his teammates dominated the matchup, winning 41-7.

The second-year Baltimore quarterback threw for four touchdowns and compiled over 300 yards of total offense. Meanwhile, Watson struggled, turning the ball over twice and throwing for only 169 yards.

After the game, the two longtime competitors–they faced off multiple times in college–had a heartfelt moment at midfield.

The pair exchanged jerseys and posed for a couple of photos.
