Lil Reese Shows Off Insane Gunshot Neck Wound: “Life’s A Gamble I Got Hella Luck”

17 Nov 2019, 21:36 by Biz Jones

Chicago rapper Lil Reese really came ‘this’ close to death. The hip-hop artist went online this week to share an up-close look at his gunshot wound to the neck.

On Sunday, Reese hit up Instagram and Twitter with updates on his well-being.
