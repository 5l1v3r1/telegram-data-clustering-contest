HOOPY CHRISTMAS

Celtic stars will don Christmas outfits and sing adapted version of classic carol for this year’s festive video

17 Nov 2019, 21:53 by Paul Drury

CHRISTOPHER Jullien and Kristoffer Ajer play it cool in wool as they star in Celtic’s festive video.

The Hoops defenders wear sunnies and cheesy Christmas jumpers featuring polar bears in the green and white kit.

The tongue-in-cheek promo also features tuxedo-clad Neil Lennon conducting a choir, Scott Brown as a director and the team performing iconic carol We Three Kings.

Manager Lenny also calls action with a clapperboard while right-back Moritz Bauer dresses as an elf.

An insider said: “Neil really got into the spirit of it all and was waving his arms around like the great conductor Andre Previn.

“He looked like he was enjoying himself. There was a lot of laughter during the filming because all the players got into the swing of things too.”

Bosses hope the vid will raise a huge sum for charity.

The source added: “The club is known for its festive video and everyone appeared happy to be donning anything from elves’ outfits to Christmas jumpers and reindeer ears to play their parts.

“A couple of players are in angel wings and one is a big golden star.

"The feeling is this song and film have the potential to go viral.”

Hoops-daft songwriter John McLaughlin penned the new version of the Christmas classic.

Rejigged lyrics focus on players and hails the Lisbon Lions.

European Cup winner Bertie Auld, 81, was drafted in for a cameo.

The recording took place in secret over a day at the Mitchell Theatre in Glasgow city centre last week.

It will be launched at the end of the month.
