A First: Israel’s National Anthem, Hatikva, Plays in Abu Dhabi

17 Nov 2019, 12:16 by Video Manager

After Israeli martial artist, Alon Lebayev became the world champion at a Jiu-Jitsu tournament in Abu Dhabi. This is the first time Israel’s national anthem, the Hatikva, has been played at a state-sponsored event in a Muslim country.
