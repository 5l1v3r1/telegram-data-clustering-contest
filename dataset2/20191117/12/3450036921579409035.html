Enchanted Forest returns to Ottumwa for the holidays

17 Nov 2019, 12:09 by Romelo Styles

OTTUMWA, Iowa — The Enchanted Forest is returning to the Wapello County 4-H Expo grounds this year.

The fundraiser will feature Santa, carriage rides, a cookie walk, and craft and vendor shows.

The event is November 23 and 24th.

It will be free to everyone on Saturday. But for a $5 fee, you can go for a special preview and have a pick of the first items to buy on Friday.

There’ll also be a free-will donation soup dinner.

If you can’t make it this weekend, items will still be for sale through the 4-H.

All the money raised at the fundraiser goes to help the kids at the organization.
