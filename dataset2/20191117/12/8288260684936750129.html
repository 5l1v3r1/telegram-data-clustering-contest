"Which one of them is it?”

That's the bizarre question, Wolverhampton Wanderers legend Carl Ikeme has been asking after revealing a shock familial link with either Ant or Dec.

The iconic TV duo tracked their family tree on a recent ITV show, which discovered both had ancestral roots in Ireland, with the pair even finding themselves distantly related.

And now Ikeme, who has family in England and Nigeria, whom he represented at international level, has revealed he is a distant relative of either Anthony McPartlin or Declan Donnelly.

The former Wolves keeper posted on social media: “A bit random but bare with me ...Last night I’m watching @antanddec DNA story on @itv with my other half and we start talking about our ancestry dna that we did a few years ago.
