Bella Hadid Enjoys Peace & Quiet On Mom Yolanda's Farm

17 Nov 2019, 12:45

Bella Hadid adjusts her collar while heading out of the airport on Saturday (November 16) in New York City.

The 23-year-old model carried a Chanel messenger bag with her as she went to catch her ride.

After landing back in the States, Bella hopped on a helicopter and went down to Pennsylvania to visit her mom Yolanda on her farm. She shared some videos and photos on her Instagram story.

“laying in the grass,” she captioned a cute selfie on Sunday (November 17).

In case you missed it, Bella recently opened up about not feeling sexy on the runway, and seemingly threw shade at Victoria’s Secret.
