Canteen Alley under construction

17 Nov 2019, 12:13 by Romelo Styles

OTTUMWA, Iowa â€” Eating an iconic Ottumwa meal might be a little more difficult for the next few days.

The City of Ottumwa says that Canteen Alley in downtown Ottumwa will be closed starting Tuesday.

The alley provides access to the downtown parking ramp and the Canteen Restaurant.

This closure will allow for storm drainage retrofit work.

Motorists are urged to use extreme caution in the vicinity of the work zone.

Weather permitting; the work is expected to last one week.
