Big win for Assam in Women U-23 T20 Cricket

17 Nov 2019, 12:15

Our Sports reporter

GUWAHATI: Assam thrashed Bihar by nine wickets in the Women U-23 T20 Cricket tie in the city today. Chasing just 49 runs to victory host needed just 42 balls to reach the target. Ruhina Pegu remained not out on 38 (28 balls, 4X5, 2X6). Earlier Bihar managed to score only 48 runs losing eight wickets. Nibedita Baruah picked up three wickets for 10 runs.
