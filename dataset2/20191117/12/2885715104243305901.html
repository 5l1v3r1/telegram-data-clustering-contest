Amid power tussle with Sena, Fadnavis says ‘Bal Thackeray taught us self-respect’

On the occasion of his 7th death anniversary, Fadnavis tweeted in Marathi, "Salutations to our inspiration source, Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray on his death anniversary."

17 Nov 2019, 12:30 by Express Web Desk

Amid the ongoing feud with its ally, Maharashtra caretaker Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis on Sunday heaped praises on Bal Thackeray, saying the Shiv Sena founder taught the people the value of “self-respect”.

On the occasion of his 7th death anniversary, Fadnavis tweeted in Marathi, “Salutations to our inspiration source, Hindu Hriday Samrat Balasaheb Thackeray on his death anniversary.”

Sharing a video of a speech by Thackeray, he wrote on the micro-blogging site: “Balasaheb taught us the importance of self-respect.”
