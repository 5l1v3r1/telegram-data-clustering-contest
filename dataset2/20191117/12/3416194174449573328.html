Noah Cyrus Updates Fans After Cutting Her Set Short During Festival

17 Nov 2019, 12:00

Noah Cyrus is doing okay after running off stage in the middle of a set.

The 19-year-old singer fell ill while she was performing at the Corona Capital Festival on Saturday (November 16) in Mexico City, Mexico.
