Louis Tomlinson & Liam Payne Perform Separately At Hits Radio Live 2019

17 Nov 2019, 12:31

Louis Tomlinson performs on stage at the 2019 Hits Radio Live concert held at Manchester Arena on Sunday (November 17) in Manchester, England.

The 27-year-old singer sang his newest song “We Made It” among others.

Also performing at the event that night were Louis’ One Direction band mate Liam Payne, as well as HRVY, Anne-Marie, and Mark Ronson.

Earlier in the week, Louis reunited with another 1D member, Niall Horan, in Mexico, and sent fans in a frenzy when they sang “Little Black Dress” together at soundcheck for a concert event.
