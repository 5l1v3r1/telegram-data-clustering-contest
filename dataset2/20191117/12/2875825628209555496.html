The Apprentice star in Twitter row with Alan Sugar over his 'horrific' tattoos

Lewis Ellis put his tattoos on full display during this week's episode as part of a challenge, and after Lord Sugar tweeted his disapproval, Lewis has 'received lots of nasty DMs'

17 Nov 2019, 12:47 by Irishmirror.ie

It seems Apprentice supremo Lord Alan Sugar is far from sweet on tattoos.

The Amstrad bigwig made an online attack on contestant Lewis Ellis after he saw the hopeful’s inked torso on last week’s episode.

Lord Sugar – previously accused of a racist tweet – branded the wannabe “horrific” and said he’d be “embarrassed” in future.

The jibe didn’t go down well with digital marketing manager Lewis, who was hurt by the outburst.

“Lewis tattoos are horrific,” Lord Sugar, 72, vented to fans.

“Tell me where I am missing the point. What does he look like?”
