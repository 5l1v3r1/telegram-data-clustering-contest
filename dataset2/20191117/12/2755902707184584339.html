Zimra RELEASES List Of CARS IMPORTED Under UNCLEAR circumstances:Check for your NAME

The  Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) has RELEASED a list of nearly 500 cars that were imported under unclear/irregular circumstances and advised the names owners to visit ZIMRA Loss Control Offices for verification.

The people and organisations named on the list are being requested to bring the vehicle as well as all customs clearance documents pertaining to their vehicles no later than 23 November 2019.

Reads the notification, “The Commissioner General of the Zimbabwe Revenue Authority is hereby notifying the owners of the vehiles listed below to visit ZIMRA Loss Control Offices at ZB Centre corner First Street and Kwame Nkrumah in Harare for vehicle registration verification. The vehicle owners are instructed to bring the vehicle together with all customs clearance documents pertaining to their vehicles not later than 23 November 2019”
