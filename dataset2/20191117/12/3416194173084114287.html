Ariana Grande Feels Much Sicker, Cancels Kentucky Concert

17 Nov 2019, 12:48

Ariana Grande has unfortunately had to cancel her Sunday night (November 17) concert in Lexington, Ky.

The 26-year-old “get well soon” singer has been feeling under the weather for the past month and shared the latest on how she’s feeling.

“Okay, so I woke up kind of ten times worse and it hurts SO bad to swallow. I’m not really sure what’s going on, but I unfortunately don’t think I will be able to push through tonight and I’m SO upset and sorry,” Ariana says in the video through tearful eyes.

“Ugh. I’m so upset, but of course, obviously you’ll be refunded and etc. I’m just really devastated. Thank you for understanding and sending love, etc whatever. I’ll keep you posted as soon as I know what’s going with my body, but thank you for understanding, and I am SO sorry,” she added.

Ariana‘s fans also got #GetWellSoonAriana trending on Twitter, sharing their love and understanding, and sending get well wishes.
