10-year-old demands iPhone 11, Chanel purse and £3k in cash in Christmas wish list

Fellow Twitter users can't believe the little girl's demands

17 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Rebecca Carter

A very hopeful 10-year-old has asked for an iPhone 11, a Chanel purse and £3,000 in cash for Christmas.

The child's Christmas list has gone viral on Twitter - and people can't believe the list of items she's asking for.

While many kids ask Father Christmas for the latest toys, it seems this little girl has her heart set on much, much bigger things!
