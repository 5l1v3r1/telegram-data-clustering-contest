Learn More About Demi Lovato's Boyfriend Austin Wilson!

17 Nov 2019, 12:17

Austin Wilson squats down for a little break while out and about last week in Los Angeles.

The 25-year-old model wore a pair of white pants, a black shirt and hat, and converse shoes for his outing.

Austin recently made headlines after he and Demi Lovato made their romance Instagram official.

After their surprise announcement last week, many are wondering who Austin is, and we found out some things about him.

According to E! News, Austin is also dedicated to his sobriety, and “can relate to Demi on that level and has been very empathetic to her struggles.”

Austin‘s dad, George Wilson, was a famous skateboarder with the Z-Boys of Dogtown, and now designs his own line of jeans, Rivi Goods.

Demi and Austin were both good friends with Thomas Trussell, who sadly passed away last month.
