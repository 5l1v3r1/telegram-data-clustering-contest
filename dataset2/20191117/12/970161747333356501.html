Brooklyn Nine Nine's Melissa Fumero announces pregnancy with second child

17 Nov 2019, 12:18

Brooklyn Nine Nine is getting a tiny new addition to the squad, as Melissa Fumero is pregnant with her second child.

The actress, who plays fave Amy Santiago on the series, has been hiding her bump as she admitted she was getting self-conscious during this pregnancy.

However, sharing her happy news on Instagram, she decided to hell with it and shared her bump with the world on Saturday.

Posing to the side to reveal a bump she’s managed to hide for a months, the star pulled a face as she stated: ‘Oh yeah, I’m hella pregnant. To be honest, I haven’t felt like posting about it because this pregnancy has been way harder and… I don’t feel that cute?

‘But yesterday I got a really intense massage, an awesome chiropractic adjustment, and my hair feels thicker… so I feel a little bit cute today.’

She ended the news with the hashtags #HereWeGoAgain and #BabyNumeroDos.

Melissa and her husband David have been married since 2007, and together they have a three-year-old boy named Enzo.

Her announcement comes four years to the day since she revealed her first pregnancy.

Last time she was expecting, the team hilariously worked her baby bump into the plot – having Amy go undercover at a women’s prison as a pregnant inmate.

Deciding to give Amy a bump in a bid to make her less likely to be attacked, in one scene she’s seen moving her belly and sighing: ‘I don’t know…do I even look pregnant.’

On screen love interest Jake is then scene moving the ‘fake’ bump as well and in an unsure voice tells her: ‘I mean…I guess I can see it.’

So with season seven being wrapped up as we speak, we can’t wait to see the excuse they come up with this time!

Can we see a Jake and Amy baby on the horizon maybe…?

Brooklyn Nine-Nine seasons 1-5 are available on Netflix in the UK.
