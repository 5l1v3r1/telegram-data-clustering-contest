18 Healthy Meal-Prep Recipes You Can Make in Your Instant Pot

17 Nov 2019, 12:10 by Caitlyn Fitzpatrick

Meal prep can help keep you on track with your goals, in addition to saving you money, but if the idea of carving out hours on Sunday to cook a week's worth of meals sounds daunting, it's time to make use of your Instant Pot. The Instant Pot can help speed up meal prep by cutting down on prep and cook time, and by freeing up your stove and oven so you can tackle more dishes at once.

To get you started, we've rounded up a bunch of healthy recipes you can make in large batches and serve throughout the week. Keep reading for the list!

1 White Chicken Chili

It takes just 30 minutes for this delicious chili to cook in the Instant Pot, and with the recipe making up to 10 servings, you'll have plenty to sustain you throughout the week.

Get the recipe: white chicken chili

2 Beef Carnitas

These beef carnitas are ideal for meal prep because they're just so versatile. Throw them on a salad or in a lettuce wrap, or eat them with some simple, colorful vegetables.

This brown fried rice isn't actually fried at all, but you'll still love the flavor it adds to your meals throughout the week (and the way it satisfies those cravings for takeout).

Get the recipe: Chinese-style brown fried rice

4 Chicken Fajitas

Make a batch of these fajitas on Sunday, then serve them with whole-wheat tortillas or lettuce wraps during the week. If you want to make the guacamole ahead of time, simply pour a thin layer of water on top to keep it from turning brown.
