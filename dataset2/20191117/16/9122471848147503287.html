Rob Gronkowski Is Making A “Big Announcement” On Tuesday Morning

17 Nov 2019, 16:18 by Andrew Holleran

Rob Gronkowski remains retired from the NFL. Will that continue to be the case moving forward? We could find out on Tuesday morning…

The star NFL tight end, who retired following the Patriots’ Super Bowl win over the Rams, is teasing a “big announcement” this upcoming week.

“What’s up Gronk Nation. I have a big announcement to make,” he said. “I can’t wait to drop it on ya’ll this upcoming Tuesday. It’s going to be fun. It’s going to be wild. It’s going to be a good time. Come check out my Instagram page, this Tuesday, 9 a.m. E.T.”

Patriots fans are of course excited about the possibilities…
