Biggest Annual Music Event, Youth Power Praise, Scheduled For 23 Nov

17 Nov 2019, 16:51 by Guest Author

Youth Power Praise

Call To Worship has announced their annual music event will be held on the 23rd of November. An interdenominational Christian event that has been going on now for the past 11 years. The biggest interdenominational Christian event will be held at Harare Gardens and will be Free for all to attend.

Through the continued support of sponsors like Econet wireless, ZiFM and other key stakeholders the event has continually managed to reach new heights gathering some of the best talents the nation has had to offer. Gospel artists from across the continent have been performing at this event annually for years now. This year plans to be just as exciting with new gospel artists from South Africa and Nigeria expected while all the nation’s favourite gospel choirs and Christian bodies will be present at the event. The event will start with Youth Power Praise from 12 pm to 3 pm.

Youth power praise is an empowerment session focusing on empowering the youth. This event is a workshop and seminar being led by industry leaders and experts to speak to young people on how to build a successful career. Last year the event saw Honourable Kirsty Coventry speaking at the event. This year plans to be just as exciting with a new coaching program that will see young people stand the chance to be coached by industry experts in different fields from business development to music to digital media as well as finding spiritual life coaches. After the event, the main Concert will begin from 4 pm to 8:30 pm

Call To Worship Global is a part of Ebenezer world ministries (EWOM) which is an interdenominational ministry uniting Christian Churches worldwide. Call to Worship has done events in various countries in the world including South Africa, Zambia and Malawi and continues to expand to different nations in the world. After 11 years of operation, great years of joy and pain and thousands of hours of praise and worship, founders of Call to Worship Pastor Charlotte and Phillip Pike along with their board and a group of partners have begun working towards a promising new vision. Call To Worship seeks to bring unity in a country in such a desperate need for unity.

Pastor Charlotte Pike the founder and head of the Call to Worship Choir will be again leading the interdenominational Christian choir with a special emphasis on Worship this year. The theme Deep Calls onto Deep will be focusing on the need for the nation to get into a deep session of worship after what has been a very challenging year for the people of Zimbabwe.
