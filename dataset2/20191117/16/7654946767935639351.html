Man struck and killed by train at East Harlem subway stop: cops

17 Nov 2019, 16:57 by Joseph Konig and Craig McCarthy

A man using a walker was struck and killed by a train in East Harlem on Sunday morning, officials said.

The man was struck by a No. 2 train at the 116th Street station near Lenox Avenue around 8:30 a.m., cops said.

It was unclear how the man ended up on the tracks.
