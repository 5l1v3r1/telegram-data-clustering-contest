Compete in OVERWATCH's Mercy's Recall Challenge to Get a Legendary Skin

17 Nov 2019, 16:30 by Tommy Williams

Earlier this week, Blizzard launched a new event in Overwatch that focuses on Mercy. The story is entitled “Valkyrie” and in it, “Mercy must confront her past and wrestle with whether she should join the fight again.” The event is called Mercy’s Recall Challenge and as you complete matches and watch Twitch, you’ll be able to get some new goodies. If you win 3 games, you’ll unlock a player icon, 6 games gets you two new sprays, and winning 9 games gives you the legendary skin: Dr. Ziegler. You can get up to 3 additional sprays by watching 6 hours of participating broadcasters on Twitch.

Mercy’s Recall Challenge runs from November 12-December 2, so you have the rest of this month to get out there and win.
