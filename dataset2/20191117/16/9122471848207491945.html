Report: 8 Teams Attended The Colin Kaepernick Workout

17 Nov 2019, 16:37 by Tzvi Machlin

After days of craziness leading up to Colin Kaepernick’s workout in Atlanta yesterday, there were ultimately more than a handful of teams that attended it.

On Sunday morning, ESPN NFL analyst Adam Schefter announced that eight teams attended the workout.

Schefter’s initial list of teams consisted of:

  • Kansas City Chiefs
  • New York Jets
  • Philadelphia Eagles
  • Tennessee Titans
  • San Francisco 49ers
  • Washington Redskins
  • Detroit Lions
  • Buffalo Bills
