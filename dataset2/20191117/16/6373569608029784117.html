Kitchen bin fire leaves Cornwood home damaged

Firefighters called late Saturday night to fire at Cornwood house

17 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Carl Eve

Firefighters from Ivybridge, Plympton and Crownhill were called to tackle a blaze at a kitchen fire at a property in Cornwood late last night.

The call came in shortly before 10.30pm after fire alarms sounded and firefighters raced to the property in Fore Street to find smoke issuing from a chimney and windows.

On arrival crews confirmed a small fire had taken ahold within a ground floor kitchen. Firefighters, using two breathing apparatus, tackled the blaze with one hose reel jet and one safety jet.

Devon and Somerset Fire and Rescue Service stated that the fire started within a refuse bin inside the kitchen.

A spokesperson for the fire service stated: "Fire crews successfully extinguished the fire however the property was 90 percent damaged by smoke and 10 percent by fire."
