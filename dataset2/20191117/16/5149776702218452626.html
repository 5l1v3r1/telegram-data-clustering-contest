CCTV footage released after catalytic converter theft at park and ride

Anyone with information is asked to contact Nottinghamshire Police

17 Nov 2019, 16:32 by Jamie Barlow

Detectives have released CCTV footage of two men they'd like to speak to in connection with an incident at Clifton Park and Ride.

Police were called to a report of a stolen catalytic converter.

The incident happened at the park and ride, Clifton Lane, at around 2.30pm on August 6.

No arrests have been made.

Police would like to speak to two men, visible in the footage, in connection with the investgation.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Nottinghamshire Police on 101, quoting incident 470 of August 6.

Advice on how to protect your vehicle can be found on the force website.
