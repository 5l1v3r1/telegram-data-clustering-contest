College Football Fans Are Furious With CBS Announcer Gary Danielson

17 Nov 2019, 16:04 by Andrew Holleran

College football fans are furious with CBS analyst Gary Danielson for how he insensitively handled the Georgia photographer’s scary injury on the sideline of Saturday’s game at Auburn.

A Bulldogs photographer was knocked out on the sideline late in the first half of Saturday afternoon’s game at Auburn. The female journalist was down, motionless, for several minutes. She was eventually stretchered off the field and taken to the hospital.

Thankfully, the Georgia photographer has since been released from the hospital. She appears to be OK.

Danielson, meanwhile, continues to get criticized for how he handled everything.
