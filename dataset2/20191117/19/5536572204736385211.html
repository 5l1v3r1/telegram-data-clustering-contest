Free Agent WR Terrelle Pryor Begs The Steelers To Sign Him On Instagram (PICS)

17 Nov 2019, 19:12 by Darrelle Lincoln

Terrelle Pryor has not played in the NFL since he split time between the Buffalo Bills and New York Jets during the 2018 season.

The free agent wide receiver desperately wants to get back in the league, but will only do so for one team.

The 30-year-old took to Instagram and starting pleading his case as to why his hometown Pittsburgh Steelers should sign him.
