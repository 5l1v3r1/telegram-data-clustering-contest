Newly single Josh Ritchie hits the club as he gets over Charlotte Crosby split

The reality TV star appeared carefree as he spent the night partying up in Manchester after his recent split

17 Nov 2019, 19:17 by Irishmirror.ie

Newly single Josh Ritchie, 25, has wasted no time in getting out and about after his split from 29-year-old Charlotte Crosby.

Instead of nursing his broken heart on the sofa with a tub of ice cream, the Love Island star hit Liv nightclub in Manchester as he made his return to the dating scene over the weekend.

Josh openly promoted his night on the tiles on social media by uploading stories onto his Instagram account that showed him dancing in the night time venue post break-up.

The handsome lad happily sang into his camera phone in the footage and showed off the bottles of booze available to him on his VIP table in the club.

He also appeared on the venue's Instagram stories where he proudly lifted his T-shirt to flaunt his abs as music blared and he let loose.

Geordie Shore star Charlotte announced the news of her and Josh calling time on their romance via her Instagram stories on Sunday evening.

She admitted that the two year relationship she'd shared with the hunk had "broken down" and she hoped they could "remain civil" after calling it a day.

Charlotte wrote: "Me and Josh have decided to part ways. Sadly the relationship seems to have broken drown.

"Hoping we can still remain civil and wish him the best for the future."

Josh is yet to comment on their breakup.

The pair moved in together in August, with Charlotte leaving behind the Toon to be with Josh in Bolton.

Just days ago she told Mirror Online about how moving in together had made their relationship better.
