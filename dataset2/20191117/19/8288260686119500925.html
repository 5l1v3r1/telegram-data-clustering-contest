'Uncomfortable' - Mixed response to Aston Villa star's performance in England win

Aston Villa latest | Villa centre-half Tyrone Mings played his part as England made it seven wins from eight in their European Championship qualifying group with victory over Kosovo

17 Nov 2019, 19:50 by Josh Murray

Aston Villa defender Tyrone Mings earned his second senior cap as England secured a European Championship top-seed spot with victory over Kosovo today (Sunday).

Mings played the full 90 minutes of a 4-0 win in Pristina, partnering Harry Maguire at centre-half.

Harry Winks fired the Three Lions into a first-half lead, before Harry Kane, Marcus Rashford and Mason Mount struck in the final 20 minutes to secure England's seventh group A win from eight.

John Stones had made Gareth Southgate's starting XI for Thursday's 7-0 thrashing of Montenegro - but Mings was back at the heart of the defence in England's final competitive fixture before next summer's finals.
