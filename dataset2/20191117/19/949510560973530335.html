American Legion thanks members with annual Turkey Dinner

17 Nov 2019, 19:19 by Jack Arpey

BINGHAMTON (WBNG) — American Legion Post 1194 held their annual turkey dinner Sunday in Binghamton.

The dinner is organized each year as an opportunity for members to not only share a Thanksgiving meal with their families but also show their appreciation to the Legion Riders and the Auxiliary for the work they do to keep the post open and operating at a time when many posts around the country are closing.

“We want the sons and the auxiliary and the legion riders to know that we appreciate everything they do to keep us going,” said Post 1194 member Steve Illsley.

Illsley says he has been a member of the post for more than fifty years.
