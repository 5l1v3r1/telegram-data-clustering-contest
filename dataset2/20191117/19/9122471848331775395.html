Danny Kanell Says LSU Has A “Liability” vs. Clemson, Ohio State

17 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Tzvi Machlin

LSU climbed to 10-0 on Saturday following a hard-fought road win over SEC rival Ole Miss. With convincing wins over Texas, Florida, Auburn and especially Alabama, it’s clear that the Tigers are the team to beat.

But college football analyst Danny Kanell pointed to one problem that could ultimately doom them against the likes of Clemson and Ohio State.

Taking to Twitter on Sunday, Kanell asserted that LSU would win the SEC outright. But he’s leery of the Tigers’ defense, and called it “a liability against the big boys.”
