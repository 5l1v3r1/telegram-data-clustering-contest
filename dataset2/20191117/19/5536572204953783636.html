Colts Do A Keg Stand Celebration After Scoring TD That Was Reviewed & Taken Off The Board (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019, 19:57 by Darrelle Lincoln

This is quality touchdown celebration.

Offensive guard Quenton Nelson found his number called against Jaguars when he received a handoff to score a touchdown.

To celebrate, his teammates lifted his legs off the ground to do an epic keg stand celebration.
