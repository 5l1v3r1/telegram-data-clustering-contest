'Idols SA' season 15 winner crowned

17 Nov 2019, 19:00

Cape Town - After a gruelling competition and over 186 million votes cast the winner of Idols SA season 15 has been announced.

The grand finale took place at Carnival City where Luyolo Yiba was crowned as the winner during a spectacular live show.

The 24-year-old from Buffalo River was one of only two men to make it through to the top 10 and is the first male winner in three years.

His single, Sunshine Through the Rain is number 2 on iTunes.
