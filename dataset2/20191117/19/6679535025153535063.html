Two arrested for molesting woman

17 Nov 2019, 19:47 by Special Correspondent

The Vadapalani all-women police arrested two persons for molesting a married woman on Sunday. The two have been identified as Harishkumar and Ramesh Kumar.

The accused had taken photos of the victim in compromising poses and then blackmailed her to part with money and gold jewellery. Later, they molested her more than once.

They were arrested based on a complaint by the woman.
