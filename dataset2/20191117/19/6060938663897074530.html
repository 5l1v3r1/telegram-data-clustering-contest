Photos: Saif Ali Khan, Kareena Kapoor Khan opts for traditional outfits for their airport look!

17 Nov 2019, 19:02 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Saif Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan along with their little munchkin Taimur Ali Khan get snapped in the city on various occasions. Today, the trio was snapped at the airport as they arrived in Mumbai.

The actress was shooting in Chandigarh for her upcoming film and Taimur was with her at the union territory.

And for the airport look, Saif opted for a light pink kurta paired with white pyjama and black shoes. Kareena, on the other hand, looked pretty in a yellow kurta with grey pyjama while little Tim was in all-blue attire.
