Arhaan Khan eliminated from ‘Bigg Boss 13’

17 Nov 2019, 19:15 by BizAsia Correspondent

Recent wild card contestant, Arhaan Khan has been booted out of ‘Bigg Boss 13’ in the show’s latest eviction process.

The actor had been in a rumoured relationship with fellow celebrity, Rashami Desai, who was in the bottom three along with Himanshi Khurana and Khesari Lal Yadav. The eviction was rather surprising as the latter two had been regularly picked on for not being so active on the show.

Since his entry on ‘Bigg Boss 13’, Khan remained a close alliance to Desai, in her war of words with Siddharth Shukla.
