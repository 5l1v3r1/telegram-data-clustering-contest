Betting Line Released For Ohio State-Penn State Game

17 Nov 2019, 19:34 by Matt Hladik

Ohio State and Penn State will meet with the Big Ten East on the line next Saturday in Columbus. The Buckeyes are the favorite in that game.

Matter of fact, they are heavily favored. The betting line for Ohio State-Penn State has been released, and Ohio State is “giving” over three touchdowns worth of points to the Nittany Lions.

According to Vegas Insider and Circa Sports, Ohio State is a 19-point favorite over PSU.
