How many episodes of His Dark Materials are there on BBC1? Magical TV adaptation continues

His Dark Materials, the TV adaptation of Phillip Pullman's classic novels, continues on BBC1 on Sunday

17 Nov 2019, 19:43 by Simon Duke

After just two episodes His Dark Materials has already established itself as THE drama to watch in the run up to Christmas.

The TV adaptation of Phillip Pullman's classic novels of the same name, started on BBC1 in early November and continues on Sunday night.

Secrets and dangers lie ahead for young Lyra Belacqua in an epic tale of stolen children and the mysterious substance of Dust.

Episode 1 saw Lyra's world turned upside-down by her long-absent uncle's return from the north, while the glamorous Mrs Coulter visits Jordan College with a proposition.

How many episodes of His Dark Materials are there?
