Kareena Kapoor Khan to attend ‘Good Newwz’ trailer launch

17 Nov 2019, 19:38 by BizAsia Correspondent

Kareena Kapoor Khan will be attending the trailer release this coming Monday of ‘Good Newwz’, in which she stars as Shruti.

Khan has shot extra hours this weekend for another upcoming movie named ‘Laal Singh Chadda’, in order to be able to attend the event without causing delays to the production.

‘Good Newwz’ will be releasing this December.
