The latest pictures from Australia as I'm a Celebrity 2019 kicks off

Here are the latest pictures from Australia

17 Nov 2019, 19:25 by Liz Day

I'm A Celebrity is back.

This year's contestants are Roman Kemp, Andrew Maxwell, Nadine Coyle, Ian Wright MBE, Caitlyn Jenner, Jacqueline Jossa, Myles Stephenson, Adele Roberts, Kate Garraway and James Haskell.

On episode one they arrived down a red carpet, before "walking the plank" by jumping off a 32-storey building, diving off a boat and swimming to the beach.

The stars needed a head for heights as Nadine and Caitlyn were challenged to do a sky dive.

Here are the latest pictures from Australia.

The first episode of I'm A Celebrity will start at 9pm on Sunday, November 17 on ITV.
