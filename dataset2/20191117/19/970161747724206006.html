Ryan Reynolds meets Mariah Carey and her kids, naturally freaks out

17 Nov 2019, 19:13

Oh to be a fly on the wall as Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively met Mariah Carey and her kids, Moroccan and Monroe.

In a meeting we never even knew we needed to see until today, Moroccan (nicknamed Rocky), 8, dressed up as Deadpool as the family posed with the celeb couple.

Mariah posted the picture to her 8.6 million Instagram followers and wrote in the caption: ‘Rocky snuck up on an unsuspecting couple wearing a Deadpool mask. Will they ever recover?’

Then, as if things couldn’t get any better, Ryan replied: ‘Whoa. My vision board became a photo.’

Beautiful.

People then began flooding Mariah’s Instagram comments section with their excited reactions.

One fan wrote: ‘Dahhhling you are winning at life!’

While another replied: ‘So awesome you got to meet @mariahcarey and her kids. Roc will never watch Deadpool the same now.’

Then another suggested: ‘I’m a big Deadpool fan. Please get Mariah to sing the soundtrack for Deadpool 3. That would be amazing!’

This latest celebrity meeting comes just days after Roc and Roe met Stranger Things star Millie Bobby Brown.

Mariah wrote in the caption: ‘Roc and Roe’s best day of their 8 year old lives! Thank you @milliebobbybrown for this moment, we had a blast!’

Earlier today, Blake deleted every single post on her Instagram except for the trailer of her upcoming film, The Rhythm Section.

The 32-year-old actress did a similar stunt in May last year to promote her film, A Simple Favor.

Something tells us Blake won’t be reposting Mariah Carey’s pic of her anytime soon…
