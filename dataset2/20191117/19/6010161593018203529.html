Watch Chun-Li morph into a Power Ranger in Power Rangers: Legacy Wars

17 Nov 2019, 19:45 by Emily Gera

Street Fighter’s Chun-Li joins the mobile game Power Rangers: Legacy Wars.

A new Power Ranger has been revealed for mobile fighting game Power Rangers: Legacy Wars – Street Fighter’s Chun-Li is making her debut in full ranger form, developer nWay Games announced in a new gameplay trailer which shows off her moves.

Chun-Li has in fact been in the game since early 2018, along with her fellow Street Fighter alumni Ryu, Guile, M. Bison, Cammy, and Akuma. Now fitted with an alternative Morphin’ look, Chun-Li is shown kicking things off by setting baddies alight with blue Phoenix fire, using abilities like Volcano Kick, Phoenix Dance, and the mega powerful Thousand Phoenix Kicks.
