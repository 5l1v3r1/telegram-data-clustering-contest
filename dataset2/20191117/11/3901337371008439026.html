Ex-Pirates & CT City Striker Surfaces At New Club   

17 Nov 2019, 11:57 by Soccer Laduma

A former Orlando Pirates and Cape Town City player is currently in search of a club for the 2019/20 season.

Having finished last season with Cape Umoya United, Letsie Koapeng has been spotted at Swallows FC as he looks to join a new team.

Following an unsuccessful stint with Pirates in the 2014/15 season, Koapeng went on to have a spell with Royal Eagles in the National First Division, before joining Stellenbosch FC during their time in the second tier league.

From Stellenbosch, the forward was snapped up by the Citizens. Swallows, who have been one of the busiest GladAfrica Championship clubs in the market this season, have given the player a chance to impress the technical team in training.   

Koapeng has previously also been on the books of Bidvest Wits, Bloemfontein Celtic and Chippa United, among other clubs in his career.
