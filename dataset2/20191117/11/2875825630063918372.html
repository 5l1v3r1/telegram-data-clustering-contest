John Joe Nevin wins WBA Super Featherweight International title

Nevin defeated the Nicaraguan via a unanimous decision over 10 rounds at York Hall on Saturday night

17 Nov 2019, 11:56 by Robert Hynes

John Joe Nevin has extended his professional record to 14-0 after defeating Freddy Fonseca to claim the WBA Super Featherweight International title.

Nevin defeated the Nicaraguan via a unanimous decision over 10 rounds at York Hall on Saturday night.

Meanwhile in Glasgow, Paddy Donovan tasted victory in his second professional bout.

He won all six rounds against Danny Mendoza at the Emirates Arena in the east end of the city.

On the same card, Pierce O'Leary beat England's Chris Adaway over four rounds.
