Police called to investigate late night vehicle blaze in Chorley

17 Nov 2019, 11:03 by Brian Ellis

Police are investigating after a vehicle was found on fire in Chorley last night.

The incident happened at around 10:45 pm on Hoggs Lane.

Fire crews from Chorley out out the blaze using a hose reel.

A spokesman said: "Police were also in attendance and an investigation is now underway."
