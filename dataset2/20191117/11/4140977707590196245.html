Antonio Conte receives anonymous envelope with threats and a bullet inside, Inter responds

17 Nov 2019, 11:29 by Archie Blade

Antonio Conte receives anonymous envelope with threats and a bullet inside. He is under police protection while they launch an investigation.

Inter Milan manager Antonio Conte has received a letter in his post which contains a number of threats and a bullet according to Italian publication Corriere della Sera. The incident has left a cloud over the club and Conte has stepped up security at his family home and at the club’s training ground.

Police are currently investigating the matter in a bid to discover the identity and motive of the criminal. The envelope will be sent and tested for training. However, there is a belief that the act could simply be an attempt at seeking attention.

Regardless, both Conte and the club have been asked to remain vigilant in case they are proven to be wrong. Conte has unsurprisingly taken the decision to protect himself and his family in the aftermath of receiving the letter. He has stepped up security at his home and at the club’s training ground.

The plan is for video surveillance system on the road of Conte’s home to be analysed; as well as training ground in case a secondary delivery is made. However, both Inter Milan and Conte’s wife have reportedly denied that the letter was intended for Conte. Instead, the club claim that the letter arrived at the club.
