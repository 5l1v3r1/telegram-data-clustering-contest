Over 200 police personnel to go on marriage leave in Lucknow

There are two to three applications for wedding leave in every police station in the state capital. There are inter-force marriages between 24 couples

17 Nov 2019, 11:59 by IANS

In the ongoing wedding season, about 200 police personnel in Lucknow alone are gearing up to celebrate weddings in their families. Those who have applied for the leave include 24 to-be-married couples who belong to the police services, over 100 police personnel have marriages of either their own children or relatives and the rest are themselves either grooms or brides.
