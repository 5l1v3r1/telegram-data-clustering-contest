Watch: Gush Etzion Bus Windshield Smashed by Terrorists After Last Week’s ‘Daily Dozen’ Terror Attacks

17 Nov 2019, 22:49 by Hana Levi Julian

Palestinian Authority terrorists smashed the windshield of a bus in a stone-throwing attack early Sunday as it traveled the roads of Gush Etzion in Judea. Miraculously, no one was physically injured.
