Hillview Seventh Day Adventist Church to host Morisset Monthly Market on Sunday, November 24

17 Nov 2019, 22:39 by David Stewart

GET your car washed while you go exploring at the Morisset Monthly Market hosted by Hillview Seventh Day Adventist Church on Sunday, November 24, from 8am to 12.30pm.

Car washing will be available among the stalls offering everything from fresh local produce and jams and chutneys to bric-a-brac, plants, handmade craft items and skin care products.

It promises to be a great opportunity to pick up some unique Christmas gifts.

The market has been hosted by the church for six years and is held at the block near the church, at 95 Freemans Drive, Morisset.

On Sunday, December 15, Morisset Monthly Market will host its annual twilight Christmas markets featuring free entertainment, a petting zoo, Tower of Terror rock climbing wall, live band, food, and unique stalls.

Stall holders with family-friendly products are invited to contact organiser Corina Seemann 0414 931 400 to book sites.
