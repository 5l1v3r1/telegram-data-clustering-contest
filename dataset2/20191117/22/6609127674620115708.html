HELL'S DWELLS

Glasgow drug lord Stephen ‘Jimmy’ Jamieson shacked up in swanky £260k pad owned by taxi tycoon Stevie Malcolm

17 Nov 2019, 22:42 by Rebecca Gray

DRUGS baron Stephen “Jimmy” Jamieson is shacked up in a swanky house owned by millionaire taxi tycoon Stevie Malcolm.

The hood, 36 has been living in the £260,000 mortgage-free pad for over a year with his girlfriend and kids.

The four-bedroom gaff in Glasgow’s southside was bought by car boss Malcolm, 55, in 2016 but he has never lived in it.

Two new £100,000 Range Rovers with blacked out windows are regularly parked in the driveway.

Jamieson, of Paisley — previously jailed for his part in a £9million drugs gang — has no known source of income.

One local said: “They look like just a normal family. There are a lot of fancy cars in the area so theirs don’t particularly stand out.

“They do have a lot of people coming and going though.

“The police seem to drive through the area a lot on patrol.”

The house, built in 2013, has French doors, two en suite bathrooms and a garage.

Jamieson served half of his 8½-year sentence and was freed in 2013.

He is best pals with feared crime boss Jamie ‘Iceman’ Stevenson, 54.

Malcolm, nicknamed The Fat Controller, owns a string of properties including his £9million mansion near Gleneagles, Perthshire.

In 2018, Malcolm stepped down as boss of one of his biggest firms, Edinburgh City Private Hire.
