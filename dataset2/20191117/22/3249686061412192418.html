TWICE's Momo and Dahyun Followed by Unidentified Vehicle, Abrubtly Cuts Off VLive Broadcast

17 Nov 2019, 22:17 by HelloAnon

TWICE just concluded their 2-day concert at Miyagi, Japan last night, November 17, as part of TWICELAND World Tour 2019. 

After the event, members Momo and Dahyun went live via V App while on the way to their hotel but viewers got confused because just 5 minutes into their broadcast, the girls said their goodbyes abruptly.

When fans replayed the video, it was later found out that someone was following their vehicle.
