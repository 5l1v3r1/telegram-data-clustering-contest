Fans are loving Ant back on I'm A Celebrity but Holly Willoughby is missed

17 Nov 2019, 22:45

Viewers were absolutely loving Ant McPartlin rejoining Declan Donnelly as host of the new series of I’m A Celebrity… but they’re already missing Holly Willoughby.

The 38-year-old This Morning host replaced Ant for 2018’s I’m A Celeb, but he took back the reins this year.

Fans were happy Ant was back by Dec’s side in the jungle, but social media was divided as Holly proved to be popular the year before.

One fan wrote on Twitter: ‘So happy to see @antanddec back together again for @imacelebrity but I do miss @hollywills.’

And another tweeted: ‘I miss Holly Willoughby already, not gonna lie.’

While one mother wrote: ‘My little boy has just said the jungle is strange without Holly, he loved you last year.’

Not even one ad break in, however, and people began rushing to Ant’s defence.

One fan tweeted: ‘So glad Ant is reunited with Dec on I’m A Celebrity there (sic) a great pair together. [I] mean @hollywills done a great job last year but it’s always great when them two are together.’
