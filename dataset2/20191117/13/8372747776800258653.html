Oh deer!

17 Nov 2019, 13:49 by WFTX Digital Team

It's not something you see every day, a deer taking a stroll down the aisles at Walmart. The surprising sight happening in Wooster, Ohio. Witnesses say the deer walked into the store, knocked into some shopping carts, and took off, causing a panic.

The deer was tackled and held down until an experienced hunter stepped in to help get it out of the store's back door. Thankfully, no one was hurt during the ordeal, and witnesses say the deer took off without any apparent injuries.
