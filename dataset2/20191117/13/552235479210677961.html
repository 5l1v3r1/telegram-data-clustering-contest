Police launch investigation after man is struck by car in Bellshill

Emergency services descended on Hope Street at around 8.50pm last night.

17 Nov 2019, 13:24 by Daniel Morrow, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/daniel-morrow/

A man has been struck by a car in Bellshill .

Emergency services rushed to Hope Street on Saturday night.

The condition of the man involved in the collision is still unknown.

Police have launched an investigation into the incident as officers remain in the area.

 

A spokesperson for Police Scotland said: “Police are investigating following reports of a male being hit by a motor vehicle at around 8.50pm on Saturday. November 16 on Hope Street, Bellshill.

“Anyone with information is asked to contact police on 101 quoting incident number 3869 of November 16.”
