Janhvi Kapoor 'Kick-Starts Her New Business'; Actress Begins Accepting Orders For Manicure And Pedicure

17 Nov 2019, 13:42 by Gretel Sequeira

Janhvi Kapoor is having a gala time with her team during the shooting schedule of Dostana 2. While the actress is busy shooting, Kapoor simultaneously kick-starts her new business and has begun accepting orders for manicure and pedicure proving her nail-art skills, all in jest!

Janhvi Kapoor recently jetted off to Amritsar, Punjab to kick-start the shooting schedule of her film Dostana 2. Produced by Karan Johar, Dostana 2 is the second installment to the film Dostana starring Priyanka Chopra Jonas, Abhishek Bachchan and John Abraham. As the little Kapoor is enjoying her gala time shooting with her co-stars and team, Janhvi is treating fans with sneak-peek pictures from the sets of the shooting schedule. While the actress is busy shooting, Kapoor simultaneously kick-started her new business and is accepting orders for manicure and pedicure proving her nail-art skills on her team, all in jest!

The Dhadak star took to her Instagram story to share a sneak-peek into the ‘Masti’ and fun she indulges in with her team. While the actress has been constantly sharing glimpses from the shooting schedule of Dostana 2, Janhvi shared a video of the multi-colored painted nails of her team.
