You can audition for The Addams Family Musical in Kitchener

The show will run at the Conrad Centre for the Performing Arts in 2020

17 Nov 2019, 13:00 by Erin Anderson

If you used to watch the show or movies as a kid, and always wanted to be a part of The Addams Family, now's your chance!

K-W Musical Productions is accepting video auditions for its upcoming musical. 

All you need to do is film yourself singing a section from two pop or musical theatre songs. Then upload it to YouTube, and send in the link.

If you think you'll really shine in person, there are some live auditions happening on November 26 and 27.

If you're less about singing and acting, and more about the art of movement, a dance call is set for December 1.

The show will run at the Conrad Centre for the Performing Arts in Kitchener from May 27 to June 6, 2020.
