An advent calendar filled with jewellery is available from Liverpool's answer to Tiffany & Co

It's practically sold out but there's still a chance to get hold of one

17 Nov 2019, 13:56 by Catherine Murphy

Liverpool jewellery store Say It With Diamonds has released the ultimate advent calendar filled with some of its most popular pieces and gift vouchers.

The '24 Days of Say It With Diamonds' calendar is filled with jewellery such as earrings, necklaces and more.

Say It With Diamonds specialises in jewellery, particularly engagement rings, for all budgets.

It started back in 2016 and is owned by friends Charlotte Donoghue and Steph Porter, who initially ran things from Instagram before opening a city centre store last year.

Earlier this month, Say It With Diamonds relocated its Cavern Walks store to a new home inside the Metquarter.

Priced at £200, the calendar includes 24 draws with an item inside each one.

Demand has been huge for the calendar this year with the team announcing it was practically sold out - however fans can still sign up to a reserve list.

Images of the calendar, which have been shared across Say It With Diamonds' Instagram feed, have gained hundreds of likes and comments from jewellery lovers excited to buy one.
