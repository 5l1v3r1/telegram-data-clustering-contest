Three Indian players part of Bangladesh Premier League player draft

Three Indian players - Manvinder Bisla, pacer Manpreet Gony and former Rajasthan Royals player Kumar Boresa - are part of the player draft for the new season of the Bangladesh Premier League (BPL), which will begin on December 11.

17 Nov 2019, 13:19 by Sports Desk

Three Indian players – Manvinder Bisla, pacer Manpreet Gony and former Rajasthan Royals player Kumar Boresa – are part of the player draft for the new season of the Bangladesh Premier League (BPL), which will begin on December 11.

Bisla played a special knock in the final of the 2012 IPL as Kolkata Knight Riders claimed their first IPL trophy.
