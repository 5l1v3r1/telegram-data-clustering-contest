Gulf Cup mascot arrives in Oman

17 Nov 2019, 13:07 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Mascot for the Gulf Cup reached the Sultanate to promote the next tournament which will be held in Qatar.

Oman Football Association said in a statement online, "Sudaifi "Gulf 24 Mantra" visits the headquarters of OFA to promote the Gulf Cup in its 24th edition."
