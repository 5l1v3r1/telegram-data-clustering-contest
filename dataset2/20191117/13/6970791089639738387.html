Lil Reese Breaks Silence On Near-Death Shooting: “S**t Bigger Than 50 [Cent] Rick Ross”

17 Nov 2019, 13:05 by Biz Jones

Chicago rapper Lil Reese is ready to tell people what happened with his near-death shooting for the highest bidder. The hip-hop artist has announced he’s willing to reveal the truth behind his recent attack for $1 million.

Last night, Reese hopped on Twitter and said his situation is bigger than the past feud between 50 Cent and Rick Ross.
