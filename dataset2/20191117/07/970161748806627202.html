I'm A Celebrity 2019: Start date, time, line-up, hosts and who won last year

17 Nov 2019, 07:05

The wait for I’m A Celebrity 2019 to start is almost over as this year’s celebrity contestants prepare to head into the jungle.

The hit show is returning for a 19th season and follows a group of celebrities as they try to survive both the Australian jungle and the dreaded Bushtucker Trials designed to test their mettle.

As fans wait on tenterhooks for the series to begin, here’s a quick round-up of everything you need to know about this year’s I’m A Celebrity.

When does I’m A Celebrity 2019 start?

This 19th series of I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! is set to start tonight, 17 November, at 9pm on ITV.

The very first episode will be a special one hour and 45-minute long show, while subsequent episodes will go on for one hour and be on every night of the week.

Who’s in the I’m A Celebrity line-up for 2019?

This year, the celebrities going into the jungle are:

  • Caitlyn Jenner – reality TV personality and retired Olympian
  • Nadine Coyle – former Girls Aloud singer
  • Adele Roberts – Radio 1 DJ
  • Ian Wright – former footballer
  • Jacqueline Jossa – EastEnders alum
  • Kate Garraway – Good Morning Britain co-anchor
  • Andrew Whyment – Coronation Street actor
  • Andrew Maxwell – comedian
  • James Haskell – former rugby player
  • Myles Stephenson – Rak-Su member
  • Roman Kemp – Capital FM DJ

Who’s hosting I’m A Celeb this year?

I’m A Celebrity will be hosted by Ant and Dec, who have hosted the show together for all but the 18th season.

That year, Dec was joined by Holly Willoughby, as Ant took time away from the spotlight in 2018 to deal with personal issues and spend time in rehab after he faced drink-driving charges earlier that year.

At the time, he issued a statement saying: ‘My recovery is going very well and for that to continue having spoken to Dec and ITV, I have made the decision to take the rest of the year off.

‘I’d like to thank both Dec and ITV for their continued support and I look forward to getting back to work in the new year.’
