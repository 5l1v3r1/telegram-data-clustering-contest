Bigg Boss 13: Ex-Contestant Sambhavna Seth Hails Devoleena Bhattacharjee, Writes, ‘Acchai Ho Toh Dikhani Bhi Chahiye

17 Nov 2019, 07:17 by Divya Ramnani

Ahead of Weekend Ka Vaar, it looks like Sambhavna Seth has found her new favourite Bigg Boss 13 contestant in Devoleena Bhattacharjee

Bigg Boss 13 has kept a lot of people, including all the ex-contestants, hooked to all the drama and controversies. Take, for instance, Sambhavna Seth, who was seen in Bigg Boss 2 and one, can just not forget her antics inside the house. As for Bigg Boss 13, Sambhavna Seth is among the avid viewers and her Twitter feed serves as proof. Recently, the actress tweeted in favor in Devoleena Bhattacharjee of the Bahu Gang, stating how she has started liking her.

As per Sambhavna, Devoleena has been showing a positive attitude lately and that she is among one of the greatest players inside the house. Sambhavna wrote, “I am really liking @Devoleena_23 positive attitude..u go girl..acchai ho toh dikhani bhi chahiye @BiggBoss @ColorsTV #bb13.” As a response to the same, a lot of netizens agreed with Seth and stated how Devoleena’s behaviour post her re-entry has changed. A user wrote, “My perception for Devoleena is totally changed now.. the way she is dealing with Sidharth Shukla is so positive.. she brings out his positive side also.. I like how she come out from Rashmi Shadow without any fight or disagreement #BB13 #BiggBoss13.”
