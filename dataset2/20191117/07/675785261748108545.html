Stalker jailed for creeping into sleeping victim's home and kissing her

Ian Essom, 41, has been jailed for eight years and put on the Sex Offenders Register for life - just weeks after being caught gazing at another girl through a petrol station window

17 Nov 2019, 07:15 by Ellie Iorizzo

A convicted stalker has been jailed after creeping into a young woman's home and kissing her while she slept.

Just three months after Ian Essom was convicted for repeatedly stalking a girl half his age while she worked at a petrol station, he began obsessing over his next victim.

At about 1am on November 18, 2018, Essom crept into his new victim's home and kissed her while she slept in bed, before restraining her as she attempted to resist.

Only three months earlier, Essom, now 41, had been handed a two-year restraining order for repeatedly gazing at another young woman through a window as she worked late shifts at a petrol station.
