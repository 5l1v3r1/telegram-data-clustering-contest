(Video) Nicest man in football Sadio Mane invites lookalike fan on Senegal team bus

17 Nov 2019, 07:05 by Ste Carson

Sadio Mane has once again proved he may just be the nicest man in football, as he’s likely made some Senegalese lad’s dream come true.

A fan of the Lions of Teranga approach the Liverpool star by way of the team bus windscreen, before the real Sadio clocked that the young man had styled his hair to look like him – the fan’s high cheekbones also help him strike an uncanny resemblance from certain angles.

Obviously flattered, Mane asked security to bring him onto the team bus and meet the rest of the team. What an absolute fella.
