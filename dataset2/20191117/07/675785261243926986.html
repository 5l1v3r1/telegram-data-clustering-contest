Best party dresses to buy for the festive season from the high street

Are you still looking for a fabulously frock for those all those festive diary dates? Find your perfect Christmas party dress here no matter what your age, size or budget.

17 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Lauren Goodwin-Grafton, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lauren-goodwin-grafton/

Finding the perfect party dress can be as stressful as Christmas shopping itself.

So if you haven't got time to pace up and down the high street, and battle the sea of sequins you'll be fronted with, you'll be pleased to know we've done all the hard work for you.

That's right ladies, we've had a scout around for you and put together a selection of the hottest frocks on the high street.

Whether you love a classic little black dress, luxurious velvet, or dazzling sequins, come party season there's something to suit all tastes and eventualtites.

We also wanted our dress styles to cater for lots of different body shapes too, so if you are curvaceous you too can feel confident and fabulous this Christmas.

Plus if you need some glitzy extras, our outfits may inspire some new accessory purchases too...

Check out Zara for their catwalk worthy party bags, and River Island for their amazingly affordable heels.

New Look have nailed it with their luxe for less jewellery too.
