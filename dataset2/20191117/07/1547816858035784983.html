Actress Dimple Kapadia Quashes Rumours Of Being Hospitalized, Confirms Her Mother Being Unwell

17 Nov 2019, 07:40 by Amruta Patel

It’s not the veteran actress Dimple Kapadia but her mother and Twinkle Khanna’s grandmother, Betty Kapadia, who is confirmed to be hospitalized. Veteran actress confirming the news and requested for prayers for her mother, without diluting any more details of the same

As soon as paps snapped actress Twinkle Khanna and Akshay Kumar outside a hospital in Mumbai, several reports of her mother and veteran actress Dimple Kapadia being hospitalized started surfacing the internet. But before the news could spread like wildfire, the veteran actress herself quashed the rumours and gave the actual reason behind Twinkle’s hospital visit. It is not Dimple but her mother and Twinkle’s grandmother, Betty Kapadia who is hospitalized. Refraining to dilute furthermore details, she informed her mother being better now and asked people for prayers and wishes for her mother.  

When contacted by IANS, a news agency, putting the rumours to rest, Dimple Kapadia said, "I am alive and kicking. It's my mother who has been hospitalised. I don't want to talk about it. My mother is doing fine. She is better now. I need all the prayers and best wishes.” We too wish for actress’ mother a speedy recovery and prayer for her well being.
