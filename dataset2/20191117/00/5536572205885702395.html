Kobe Bryant’s Daughter Dribbles Into Triple-Team & Shoots Perfect Fadeaway Just Like Her Father (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019, 00:15 by Darrelle Lincoln

Kobe Bryant is a proud father, and he shows that off by being a very active dad in his kid’s life as he continues to transition from his life in the National Basketball Association.

The former Lakers legend posted a new video this week showing his 13-year old daughter Gigi playing basketball, and she pulled off a move we’ve seen her dad do hundreds of times during his career.

GiGi received the in-bounds pass, dribbled up the court as she took it to the baseline and shot a fadeaway over multiple defenders.

Not a pass in sight.

A computer couldn’t replicate such an accurate Kobe Bryant field goal attempt .
