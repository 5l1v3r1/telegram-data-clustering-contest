Marjaavaan Screening: Tara Sutaria-Aadar Jain, Krishna Shroff-Eban Hyams Together

17 Nov 2019, 00:45 by Joel Kurian

Bollywood News

At a screening of Marjaavaan, couples grabbed eyeballs by coming out together. Tara Sutaria-Aadar Jain, Krishna Shroff-Eban Hyams looked in love on arrival.

Written By Joel Kurian | Mumbai | Updated On: November 17, 2019 00:45 IST

The recent screening of Marjaavaan became much more than celebrities coming together and watching the film. The gossip mills also got interested with the guests at the event as ‘lovebirds’ arrived for the event, grabbing eyeballs. The lead actor of the movie, Tara Sutaria came out with rumoured boyfriend Aadar Jain and Krishna Shroff too arrived with her beau Eban Hyams. 

READ: Tara Sutaria And Aadar Jain's Social Media PDA Is Raising Eyebrows On The Internet

As per reports, Tara hosted a special screening for her recent release Marjaavaan on Friday. What caught the attention of the paparazzi was that the actor arrived with Aadar Jain. The rumoured lovers were spotted in the car, enjoying a few laughs together and even posing for the shutterbugs together. That was also the case with Tiger Shroff’s sister Krishna Shroff and Eban Hyams. Eban drove his ladylove to the screening. This couple was also seen smiling and posing for the camera persons gathered at the venue.  

Marjaavaan, also starring Sidharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh, released on Friday. It opened decently with collections of over Rs 7 crore. Tara will also be seen in the remake of RX 100 opposite Suniel Shetty’s son Ahan Shetty. 

Story till now 

Tara and Aadar have been painting the town red with their frequent appearances of late. After being spotted together a few times for ‘dates’, the couple made their first public appearance by posing at the Diwali bash held by Amitabh Bachchan. Earlier, Tara had also joined Aadar in celebrating Diwali with his family. The former had recently stated that the Qaidi Band star is ‘special’ for her. They even indulged in some social media PDA over some quotes.

On the other hand, Krishna Shroff keeps sharing adorable snaps with her basketball player beau Eban Hyams. Be it exploring new locales together, or enjoying some love-dovey moments, the couple looks very much in love. Krishna’s brother Tiger Shroff too has found love, if reports are to be believed. The rumours of his relationship with Disha Patani has been doing the rounds for long now. The duo is regularly spotted together for movie and restaurant ‘dates’. However, they have not confirmed till now that  they are in a relationship, and often stick to the ‘good friends’ tag.
