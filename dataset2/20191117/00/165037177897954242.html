Umtiti on PSG's radar

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

PSG are eyeing the market looking to sign a central defender. They are looking at Samuel Umtiti, who has lost importance at Barcelona because of his injuries.

Barcelona are running out of patience with Samuel Umtiti. A player who was destined to become a great central defender for France has had numerous injuries. 

The fact he has a contract at Barca until 2023 does not mean that his future at the club is not under question. 

He has not played two consecutive games for Barcelona since 2018 and his constant knee injuries have caused Barca to run out of patience with him.

But despite that almost endless list of injuries, there is a team who are willing to give him another chance, according to 'El Desmarque'. 

Nasser Al-Khelaifi is looking for the formula to freshen up his defence and the Frenchman would be an option who they could buy at a good price after not playing much. 

It would be a risky move PSG, but they need a replacement for Thiago Silva as soon as possible.
