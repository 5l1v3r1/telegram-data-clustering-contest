Goodluck Jonathan’s Kinsmen Celebrate PDP’s Defeat In Bayelsa (VIDEO)

17 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

Former President Goodluck Jonathan’s kinsmen in Kolo, Ogbia LGA, of Bayelsa State, on Saturday, trooped out to celebrate the defeat of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in Ward 9.

Members of Kolo community hit the streets immediately the results of the 13 units in Ward 9 were announced by officials of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) officials and the All Progressives Congress (APC) was declared the winner of the ward.

Jonathan’s kinsmen said they were given as much as N10,000 by agents of the PDP but rejected the money because they were united and determined to vote in APC.

“We are happy because we won the PDP in all the units,” one of them, who failed to mention his name out of excitement said.

Also in Eppie-Atissa in Yenagoa, residents took over the road celebrating over the defeat of the PDP in the area.
