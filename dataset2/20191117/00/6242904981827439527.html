Elizabeth Addo wins fourth title with Chinese side Jiangsu Suning FC

17 Nov 2019

Black Queens captain Elizabeth Addo has won her fourth title with Chinese side Jiangsu Suning FC.

On Sunday, midfielder helped Jiangsu Sunning to beat Dalian 2-0  to win the Super Cup.

Addo has already won the league, FA Cup and the FA Tournament trophies.

In her debut season, she scored five goals in 14 matches and provided ten assists.
