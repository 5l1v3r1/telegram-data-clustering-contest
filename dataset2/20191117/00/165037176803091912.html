The Griezmann Barca want: goal and assist in 30 minutes

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Antoine Griezmann showed once again his worth against France. The striker was the protagonist at the beginning of the clash with Albania, getting an assist and a goal in 30 minutes.

First place was up for grabs and France had it in their own hands, Griezmann took on responsibility and gave France the perfect start 

After eight minutes of the game, Tolisso ended up heading a great cross from Antoine to make it 0-1.
