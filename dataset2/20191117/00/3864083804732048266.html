Best of OCR Awards 2019 Nominations Open

17 Nov 2019 by kmho

The Mud Run Guide Best of OCR Awards, also known as The Muddies, are back for the sixth year. It’s time for you to nominate the best of 2019 – best race series, clubs, athletes, moments, influencers, and more. Relive all the best OCR moments of 2019 as the industry looks ahead to the 2020 season. You will be the ones to help us choose the most memorable moments, people, and events. Click here to submit your nominations.

Best of OCR Timeline

November 17 – November 27 at 12 pm EST – Nominations

December 4 – 12 pm until December 15 at 11:59 pm EST – Voting

December 18 – Winners Announced

Be sure to make your nominations for categories such as Best OCR Series, Best Race Director, Best New Obstacle, and more. Mud Run Guide has a diverse set of categories for you to nominate all your favorites. What was your favorite moment of 2019? Who is your favorite athlete to watch? Which race do you think was the best this year? We leave it to you, the incredible OCR community, to make the decision.
