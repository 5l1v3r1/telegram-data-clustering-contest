FiveSixZero fest: Rewind and play

17 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

There’s a lot more to the upcoming FiveSixZero fest than a concert. It will also host a two-day summit to discuss trends in the music industry. Under its ‘Masterclass’ property, listen to stalwarts share their secrets about production, mixing and mastering, composing & arranging, pioneer equipment features and more. Whereas, Pitch Demo is a space where music-makers can drop their demo to various record labels and get feedback from producers. Now, meet some of the speakers of the summit, such as Ricky Kej, who will deliver the keynote address.

Ricky Kej

Grammy award winner, US Billboard No 1 artist, UNESCO’s Global Ambassador for Kindness, and UNICEF’s Celebrity Supporter, Ricky Kej has many feathers to his cap. His repertoire includes 16 studio albums released internationally, over 3,500 commercials and four feature films, including the natural history documentary Wild Karnataka narrated by Sir David Attenborough. From Ladysmith Black Mambazo, Peter Gabriel, Peter Yarrow and Soweto Gospel Choir to Amitabh Bachchan, Shankar Mahadevan, Rick Allen and Vishwa Mohan Bhatt, he’s collaborated with all.

Miguel Pardo

He is the director of the legendary Pacha Group, Ibiza. For the uninitiated, Pacha is a lifestyle and nightlife entertainment brand, which has club franchises in Brazil, United States, Russia, Germany, England, Egypt, Portugal, Austria and several Spanish cities.

Brian Tellis

He has enjoyed a long career of almost 30 years in entertainment, criss-crossing
as a show host, performer, voice-over specialist, radio jockey, jury member, event business honcho, and consultant. In 1995, he co-founded one of the leading event management companies in India called Fountainhead Promotions and Events Private Limited. He’s also a founder member of EEMA, the top body for India’s events and experiential marketing industry. Given his vast body of experience, he’s been a jury member for many award functions.

Tessa Sprangemeijer


As a general manager of Envie Events in Dubai, she specialises in organising award-winning mega-festivals such as Sensation and Unite with Tomorrowland. Before this stint, she has held roles at ALDA, organising 1,00,000-visitor festivals such as The Flying Dutch and Kingsland, plus she’s been a tour manager for the DJ MAG TOP100 DJs world tour.
