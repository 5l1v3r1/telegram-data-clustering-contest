Matteo 'in incredible form' after brain tumour operation

17 Nov 2019 by Sarah Jackson

Former Liverpool and Leeds defender Dominic Matteo is doing well, according to his friend Simon Ross,who spoke out about Matteo's treatment.

The 45-year-old is said to be in good shape after recently undergoing surgery for a brain tumour. 

Close friend Simon Ross said that Dominic Matteo is looking bright and is 'in incredible form' following his recent surgery and has now been released from intensive care to carry on further treatment, after he was rushed to hospital last week.

Ross said on his Twitter page: "Just had a FaceTime call from him, he was on incredible form, chatting, talking football, joking and very positive,"
