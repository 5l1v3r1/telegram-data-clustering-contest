Video Of Auburn Fans’ Reaction To Missed 4th Down Pass Going Viral

17 Nov 2019, 00:44 by Dan Lyons

Time just ran out on the Auburn Tigers. After struggling with Georgia’s impressive defense early, Auburn clawed back into the game in the fourth quarter, cutting a 21-0 lead down to one touchdown.

The Tigers won’t be able to complete the job, though. Bo Nix and the offense had its best shot with the ball in UGA territory with just over two minutes left.

Auburn went for it on 4th-and-2 from the Georgia 34 yard line with 2:24 remaining in the game. Unfortunately for the War Eagle faithful at Jordan-Hare Stadium, Nix’s attempt to Harold Joyner fell incomplete.

Fortunately for those of us neutral fans at home, the reactions from those Auburn fans were incredible.

CBS Sports posted a quick video from the stands, which featured a distressed orange-suited fan, next to a dapper-looking Aubie, cutting to a girl doing a full Darth Vader in Revenge of the Sith dramatic “NO” yell.
