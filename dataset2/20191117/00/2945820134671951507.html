Manzi fastest in Moto2™ Warm Up

Italian less than a tenth quicker than poleman Navarro on Sunday morning in Valencia

17 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Fresh from securing his and MV Agusta’s maiden Moto2™ front row start, Stefano Manzi (MV Agusta Temporary Forward) has kicked off race day at the Gran Premio Motul de la Comunitat Valenciana by topping Warm Up. 0.070 separates the Italian from polesitter Jorge Navarro (MB Conveyors Speed Up) as Marcel Schrötter (Dynavolt Intact GP) completes the top three.
