300k persons from 196 countries register for WYF 2019

17 Nov 2019 by MENA

CAIRO, Nov 16 (MENA) - Nearly 300,000 persons from 196 countries have registered for the third edition of the World Youth Forum (WYF), compared to 122,000 young people from 194 countries in 2018.

The forum will be hosted by the Red Sea resort of Sharm el Sheikh on December 14 to 17, under the auspices of President Abdel Fattah El Sisi.
