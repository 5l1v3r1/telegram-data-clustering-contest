Top 10 trending phones of week 46

17 Nov 2019 by Chip

Week 46 brought us quite a few changes among the most popular smartphones in the database. Xiaomi no longer controls all of the top 6, but it still has the most phones in the chart.

This time it's the Redmi Note 8 Pro reclaiming the top, after the Mi Note 10 series lost some of the hype that came with their announcement.

In second we have a Samsung phone that still just a rumor - the Galaxy A51. Not quite shocking though, given that the Galaxy A50 is by far the longest serving member of our top 10 and it's still ranking as high as sixth.

The Mi Note 10 Pro is down from the top to the lowest rostrum step, edging out stablemates Redmi Note 8 and Mi Note 10.

In seventh we have the Chinese version of the Mi Note 10 - the Mi CC9 Pro, which is followed by the newly announced HTC Desire 19s. It's been a while since we had a phone by the Taiwanese company in the top 10 and it's good to see it back even if it's unlikely to stay very long.

The final two spots again go to the two Realme phones - 5 Pro and X2 Pro, which have reversed places this time around.

This means we are losing the vivo S5 and the Redmi Note 8T.
