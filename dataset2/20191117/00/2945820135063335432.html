Masia heads polesitter Migno on Sunday morning

Moto3™ Warm Up has kicked off the final race day of 2019

17 Nov 2019 by Dorna Sports

Mugen Race teammates Jaume Masia and Andrea Migno finished P1 and P2 respectively in Moto3™ Warm Up at the Gran Premio Motul de la Comunitat Valenciana. Masia set a 1:39.959 to beat the polesitter by 0.075, with home favourite Aron Canet (Sterilgarda Max Racing Team) rounding out the top three.
