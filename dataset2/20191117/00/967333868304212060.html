Three People Injured in Belgium After Being Attacked By Migrant - Reports

BRUSSELS (Sputnik) - Three people were injured in the centre of the Belgian city of Kortrijk in an attack by a native of Ghana, local media reported on Saturday, citing law enforcement agencies.

17 Nov 2019, 00:03

According to the Nieuwsblad newspaper, the offender attacked bystanders with a stick.

"Two of those injured are in serious condition", the public prosecutor of West Flanders Province said, as quoted by the newspaper.
