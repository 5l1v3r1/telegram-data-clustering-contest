PHOTOS: See What Yahaya Bello Is Doing As INEC Announces Kogi Election Results

17 Nov 2019 by George Ogbolu

Governor Yahaya Bello of Kogi State was on Sunday pictured enjoying a cup of tea along with his aides in Lokoja, the state capital.

Naija News reports that the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) yesterday, Saturday, held the Kogi State Governorship Election.

Kogi people voted from a list of 23 political parties and in the governorship election which was marred by violence on Saturday.

This online news platform understands that Governor Bello is winning in some part of the country. Wada is winning also in some places where elections took place.
