Things to do today in Bengaluru

17 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

Shop till you drop

It’s the last day of Rajasthan Grameen Mela. From traditional Pata Chitra paintings to cloth-based scroll paintings, from Rajasthani handicrafts to Kashmiri carpets, from Kalamkari prints to Bagalpuri sarees and silver jewellery, the flea market has these and more to offer. For instance, you can check out the collection of Jaipuri and Punjabi jootis and Kolkata Shantiniketan bags and
even Andhra leather puppets.

WHERE: Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath, Kumarakrupa Road
WHEN: 10am-7pm, November 17
CALL: 9841278707

Make it all about music

The first dition of Far Out Left music festival is here – to encourage inclusivity in the electronic music scene and promote genre-agnostic sounds. It will bring together both homegrown and international pioneers, stalwarts, and upcoming talent in alternative electronic music such as DVS1, Octave One, Roman Flügel, Volvox, Carissa, Daisho and Unnayanaa.

WHERE: Sarjapur Social, Sarjapur Main Road
WHEN: 12pm onwards, November 17
ENTRY: `999, faroutleft.in

Mix a drink

Chris Moore, a famous name in the UK cocktail industry, is in the city to conduct a masterclass in mixology. Since the drinks at Coupette, of which he is the creative director, are known to be both deceptively simple and effortlessly complex, so, yes, you will get to learn this art of balancing.

WHERE: The Whitefield Arms, B Narayanapura, Mahadevapura
WHEN: 12pm onwards, November 17 CALL: 959122006

Classically yours

Catch Day 2 of Whitefield Music Festival 2019, presented by Ananda Dhwani Music Trust. Today, Prabir Bhattacharya will perform a sitar recital with Kumar Bose on the tabla. This will be followed by a Hindustani vocal recital by Padma Shri Ustad Rashid Khan with Subhankar Banerjee on the tabla, Ustad Murad Ali on the sarangi and Vinay Mishra on the harmonium, with vocal support by Armaan Khan.

WHERE: Inner Circle Ground, Whitefield
WHEN: 5pm, November 17
DETAILS: anandadhwani.in

Paint like a pro

Let loose your inner Picasso and ditch the stress, because there’s a dreamy evening canvas painting party to go to. Experience the mesmerising world of colours at this workshop and take back a beautiful painting enhancing the aura of your homes.


WHERE: Loft Koramangala, 18th Main Road, 6th Block
WHEN: 3pm onwards, November 17
ENTRY: `1,400, instamojo.com
