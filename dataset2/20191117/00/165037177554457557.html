Pau Torres wanted by Arsenal

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

According to the newspaper 'The Sun', Pau Torres is in Arsenal's plans. They are following his performances at Villareal and his recent jump to the Spanish national team.

He is 22 and a big club like Arsenal are after him. We are speaking about Pau Torres, a player that has established himself in the Villarreal defence.

This is what 'The Sun' indicates, who also signal that the Gunners could have to spend between 48 and 50 million to get him.
