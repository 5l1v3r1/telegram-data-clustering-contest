Breaking: INEC Suspends Collation Of Kogi Governorship Election Results

17 Nov 2019 by Oladipo Abiola

INEC Suspends Result Announcement

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) on Sunday announced that it has suspended the announcement of results in the November 16 governorship election in Kogi State.

Naija News reports the announcement was made by the returning officer.

The officer, while giving reasons for the suspension and postponement noted that this is because results from the last two LGAs are not ready.

He, however, noted that the announcement of results will resume by 8am on Monday.

As at the time the result announcement was suspended, Yahaya Bello of the All Progressives Congress (APC) leads Musa Wada of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).

Governor Bello of the APC had 184,430 while Wada of the PDP had 176,803.
