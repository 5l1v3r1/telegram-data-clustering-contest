Video: Rutgers Cheerleader Makes Funny Mistake vs. Ohio State

17 Nov 2019, 00:03 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday afternoon, Ohio State and Rutgers took the field for what figured to be the most lopsided game of the college football season. Just a few minutes into the action, it was clear why Las Vegas decided the Buckeyes should be 53-point favorites.

Each of Rutgers’ first two possessions ended in a turnover and a subsequent Ohio State touchdown. The Buckeyes raced out to a 14-0 lead in the first three and a half minutes of the game.

But they didn’t stop there. Star running back J.K. Dobbins scored his second rushing touchdown of the game to give the Buckeyes a 21-0 lead with six minutes left in the first quarter.

Perhaps the best moment of the game came shortly after – and it had nothing to do with the players.

A Rutgers cheerleader was spotted clapping after Dobbins scored the touchdown. It looks like he accidentally cheered for the wrong team.
