Redskins-Jets matchup leads to $6 tickets available

17 Nov 2019 by Adam Zielonka

The attendance figures for Sunday’s game between the Washington Redskins and New York Jets might be record-breaking, in the wrong kind of way.

The late-season matchup at FedEx Field between two teams with a combined three wins had tickets available for as little as $6 on StubHub mere hours before kickoff Sunday.
