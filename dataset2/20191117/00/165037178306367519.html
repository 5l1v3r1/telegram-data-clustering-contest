Leicester interested in Morelos

17 Nov 2019 by BeSoccer

Alfredo Morelos has caught the eye of Leicester City. According to 'Leicester Mercury', the Foxes want to buy the Rangers striker.

Alfredo Morelos has aroused the interest of several English clubs. Leicester are the latest team to show interest. Crystal Palace and Aston Villa had already asked about him beforehand. 

'Leicester Mercury' reports that Leicester want Morelos to bolster their attack in January. 

The Foxes are apparently willing to present an firm offer to Rangers. But the Scottish outfit are not keen on letting him leave.
