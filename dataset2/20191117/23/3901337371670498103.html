Griezmann Ends Goal Drought As France Finish Top

17 Nov 2019, 23:46 by SL International

EURO 2020 Qualifier

Result: Albania 0-2 France

Date: 17 November 2019

Venue: Estadi Nacional

Antoine Griezmann ended his goal drought for France on Sunday evening, as the World champions defeated Albania 2-0 to finish top of Group H in the EURO 2020 qualifiers.

Les Bleus made five changes to the side that edged Moldova on Thursday, with goalkeeper Steve Mandanda getting a run , while Kylian Mbappe settled for a place on the bench with illness alongside N'Golo Kante.

The visitors got the opener with their first attack in the eighth minute through Corentin Tolisso's header at the near post from Antoine Griezmann's well weighted free-kick.

France were soaking up the pressure and going forward with pace and they doubled thelead in the 31st minute thanks to Griezmann, who turned home Leo Dubois' cross.

The early stages of the second stanza saw the roles reversed, with Albania doing the most defending as France were taking complete control of the match.

Olivier Giroud came close to making adding a third goal for the visitors in the 71st minute with a curling strike from the edge of the box that came off the inside of the post.

Deschamps finally made his first change with 15 minutes remaining, after bringing on Lucas Digne in place of Benjamin Mendy to tighten up at the back.

Nevertheless, Les Bleus, who had already qualified, held on to win 2-0 and clinched top spot in Group H, while Albania, whose hopes of qualifying had ended, finished in fourth place.
