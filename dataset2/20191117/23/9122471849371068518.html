Eagles Announce Injury Update For Lane Johnson

17 Nov 2019, 23:02 by Matt Hladik

The Philadelphia Eagles are going to have to beat the New England Patriots without the services of Lane Johnson, one of their top offensive linemen.

Johnson suffered a head injury in the first half of this afternoon’s game. He was carted off the field before walking into the locker room on his own.

Johnson is being evaluated for a concussion, and the Eagles already announced he won’t return to the game.

Halapoulivaati Vaitai has stepped in and replaced Johnson at right tackle.
