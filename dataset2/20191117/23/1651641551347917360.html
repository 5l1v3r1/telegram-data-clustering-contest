Bring stunning eyes to your desktop with this free 4K Wild Eyes Windows 10 wallpaper pack

17 Nov 2019, 23:00 by Surur

Bring a stunning look to your desktop with a new set of 4K premium wallpapers from Microsoft, free from the  Microsoft Store.

Titled Wild Eyes, the description of the high-quality wallpaper selection reads as follows:

Time for a closeup! Look deeply into the stunning eyes of these 14 animals and birds in this premium 4k image set, free for Windows Themes.

After downloading the collection, go to Start > Settings > Personalization > Themes and click on the theme you prefer.

To apply and change to the most appropriate colour, based on the current wallpaper, go to Customize > Colors > Automatically select a primary colour from the background.
