TWICE to be Featured on "Who? K-pop" Manhwa Book as Kpop's New Generation Representative Girl Group

17 Nov 2019, 23:12 by HelloAnon

"Who? K-pop", a manhwa (comic) book to be released on November 21, 2019, features best selling Kpop girl group TWICE.

The said comic series will talk about TWICE as Kpop's representative girl group who helped revive the Hallyu wave and advance Korea's culture in other parts of the world in the current generation. The authors also tackled TWICE's journey to success and how they became known as "Asia's Top Girl Group".
