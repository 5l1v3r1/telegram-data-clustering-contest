Former T-ara member Boram to return with music drama 'Shall We That's'

17 Nov 2019, 23:36 by olmal

Former T-ara member Boram is returning with music drama 'Shall We That's'. On November 18, NETSCAPE revealed the official poster of 'Shall We That's'. The drama will relay the message that life itself is hope though it may be hard for some people. 

The show will premiere on November 23 at 5 PM KST through SMILE TV PLUS.
