Israel Folau Is Under Fire After His Latest Controversial Comments

17 Nov 2019, 23:47

Sacked rugby union star Israel Folau is in hot water after suggesting the NSW bushfire crisis was a “taste of God’s judgment” for legalising same-sex marriage and abortion.

SCROLL DOWN FOR VIDEO

Folau made the comments -- which were streamed online -- during an address at a church in Sydney.

FIVEaa’s Stephen Rowe said on Monday’s FIVEaa Breakfast show:

“I heard you guys bring up Izzy Folau…

“Do we the media have to peddle and perpetuate Izzy’s views? I think 99 per cent of us -- your listeners said it earlier -- we don’t believe what Izzy’s saying and he’s irrelevant.

“He was a sporting star -- he’s not now. He’s a disgraced, sacked sporting star, so I think he’s irrelevant and so too should his views be… he can run his own race. None of us believe him.”
