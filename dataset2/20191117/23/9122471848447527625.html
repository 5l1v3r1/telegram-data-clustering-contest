Former Alabama Star Writes Heartfelt Message To Tua Tagovailoa

17 Nov 2019, 23:18 by Tzvi Machlin

Tons of Alabama fans and haters alike have come out in support of ailing quarterback Tua Tagovailoa, whose very career may be in danger after a serious hip injury he suffered against Mississippi State yesterday.

Among the many people to offer well-wishes to Tagovailoa is an Alabama legend: Former NFL MVP Shaun Alexander.

Taking to Twitter today, Alexander expressed full confidence that Tagovailoa would recover. He expressed his love and respect for the young quarterback, and declared “A legend is being made.”

Alexander’s post also included a picture of the two of them from a College Football Playoff press conference.
