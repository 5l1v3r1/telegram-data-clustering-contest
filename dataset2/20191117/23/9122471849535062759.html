Tom Brady’s Halftime Stats vs. Eagles Are Pretty Brutal

17 Nov 2019, 23:07 by Andrew Holleran

Tom Brady and the New England Patriots’ passing attack has seen better days. The Philadelphia Eagles have prevented the AFC East leaders from establishing any kind of consistency through the air this evening.

New England is trailing Philadelphia, 10-9, after two quarters of play. All things considered, the Patriots are lucky the score is that close.

Brady and the Patriots’ passing attack has been poor at best through one half.

New England’s star quarterback actually set a career record for incompletions in the first half.
