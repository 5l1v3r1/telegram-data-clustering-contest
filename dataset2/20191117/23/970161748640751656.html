Dec Donnelly explains mystery I'm A Celebrity injury

17 Nov 2019, 23:49

Declan Donnelly earned major dad points after admitting to having injured himself sterilising his one-year-old daughter’s bottles ahead of the I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here premiere.

With the presenter teaming up with Ant McPartlin to present the Australian competition show again, viewers were overjoyed to see the pair back on their screens together.

But after one fan spotted a nasty looking graze on Dec’s arm, the 44-year-old had to confirm that he hadn’t sustained his injury by crawling with insects, swimming with fish or taking part in Celebrity Cyclone like the I’m A Celeb contestants are about to.

No, Dec’s was far less, erm, glamorous.

‘Dec- what have you done to your arm? Looks like a nasty injury,’ the Twitter user asked, with Dec quickly clarifying that he was OK.
