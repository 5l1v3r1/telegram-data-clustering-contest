Nayanthara-Vignesh Shivan Bond With Khushi-Boney Kapoor In New York Ahead Of Special Day

17 Nov 2019, 23:19 by Joel Kurian

Bollywood News

Nayanthara-Vignesh Shivan bonded with Khushi-Boney Kapoor in New York ahead of the 'Lady Superstar's birthday. The four of them enjoyed a meal together.

Written By Joel Kurian | Mumbai | Updated On: November 17, 2019 23:19 IST

Ahead of her 35th birthday on Monday, Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivan are enjoying some lovely moments in New York. But before the couple mesmerised their fans with some adorable posts, they bonded with Boney Kapoor-Khushi Kapoor. A picture of the two pairs is going viral on social media.

It is unclear yet if Nayanthara-Vignesh Shivan bumped into the a Bollywood father-daughter duo, or it was a planned meeting, but the four of them had a great time together over dinner.  Sharing a snap of the four posing together, Vignesh wrote, “Pleasant dinner moments @taodowntown #newyork #manhattan @khushikapoor_official #boneykapoor #nayanthara #taodowntownnyc #taodowntown #newyorkcity.” Vignesh and Nayanthara are seen twinning in black, while Boney Kapoor is also seen in an all-black attire. Khushi Kapoor was all smiles in a furry white coat and blue jeans.

Later, Vignesh shared some super romantic snaps with his ladylove. In one of them, the duo is seen walking on the streets as the director captioned it, “A walk to remember 😌  #centralpark #newyork #newyorkcity #birthday #birthdaygirl #coldweather #freezing.”  He then posted an adorable selfie where there are looking into each other’s eyes amid the backdrop  of the skyscrapers. He captioned it, “This Sky & her smile —— surreal 😍😌🤩😇!! Bringing in her birthday 🎂 🎁 in #newyorkcity #nayanthara #love 💎🤩😍😌 
This city is so so so beautiful 😍! #freezing #nofilter #newyork #brooklynbridge #birthdaygirl #birthday #eve."

Meanwhile, Khushi Kapoor is currently studying at the well-known New York Film Academy.  Boney Kapoor has been in the USA for the past few days. Sharing some of the father-daughter duo’s pics on social media, Janhvi Kapoor had written how much she was missing them.

On the work front 

Interestingly, both Nayanthara and Boney Kapoor are bonding with people of each other’s industries. While the ‘Lady Superstar’ collaborated with Katrina Kaif for the launch of her new beauty line, the film producer too has been busy with South ventures. After producing Ajith’s Nerkonda Parvaai, he is set to collaborate with the South star for Valimai. Nayanthara had featured opposite Ajith in his hit Viswasam earlier this year. Boney is also planning the remakes of Badhaai Ho in South languages. Moreover, he is also remaking Tamil film Comali that will star Arjun Kapoor.

Nayanthara last featured in the period multistarrer movie Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy. She had played the role of the wife of the titular character, played by Chiranjeevi. The movie was a success at the box office.
