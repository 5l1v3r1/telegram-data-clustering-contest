Selfie Of Ishita And Raman Aka Divyanka Tripathi-Karan Patel Of Yeh Hai Mohabbatein, Get Fans Bubbling With Joy

17 Nov 2019, 08:34 by Amruta Patel

Karan Patel is back on the sets of the most popular TV show, Yeh Hai Mohabbatein. Taking a break from YTM, the actor was busy shooting for Khatron Ke Khiladi; now actress Divyanka Tripathi taking to her Instagram handle shared a selfie, that has got the duo’s fans excited

Fans can’t keep calm after a selfie of the most adorable on-screen couple Divyanka Tripathi and Karan Patel hits the internet. The actress who plays Ishita Bhalla onscreen, took to her Instagram handle to share a selfie after a long time, with Karan Patel. The selfie is nothing but a much-awaited reunion of the Ishita and Raman (played by Karan) after Karan had taken a break from the popular TV show, Yeh Hai Mohabbatein to participate in a reality TV show, Khatron Ke Khiladi.

Divyanka looking pretty in a pink saree and Karan in a multi-coloured pathani kurta, she captioned the image as, “For you... #Ishra #Divan #YehHaiMohabbatein# YehHaiChahatein#Promo”. As per the location mentioned in Divyanka’s post, the duo is shooting in Madh Island, Mumbai. Isn’t this refreshing enough to make your Sunday morning sunnier? While Karan’s absence from the show, the makers had roped in actor Chaitanya Choudhry to fit into Karan’s show, but with Karan’s comeback to the show, fans are rejoicing with happiness.
