West Brom loan star has made this key January decision- reports

West Brom latest news | The West Ham loanee has been in scintillating form since his summer switch to the Championship

17 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Grady Diangana is set on staying with West Bromwich Albion, despite West Ham having the option to recall him in January.

The 21-year-old has been an integral part of Slaven Bilic’s side as they have raced to the top of the Championship.

An injury to Hammers forward Manuel  Lanzini had raised the possibility of Manuel Pellegrini recalling Diangana, serving as a cruel reminder of the Harvey Barnes situation of last January.

But Pellegrini has hinted that he wants the youngster to stay with Albion’s promotion chasers and that feeling is mutual, according to Football Insider.

The report suggests that Diangana would rather stay in the Black Country and play a key role in a promotion push, rather than be a peripheral figure with his parent club.

 

It is claimed that he wishes to return to East London in the summer and making a concerted effort in breaking into the side, but until then wants to remain at The Hawthorns.

So far this season, he has scored four times and created a further five, dazzling Albion fans, pundits and neutrals alike.
