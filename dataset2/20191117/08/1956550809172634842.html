Sofitel Dubai The Palm launches Indian restaurant

Zoya by Maui is now open at the Palm Jumeirah hotel

17 Nov 2019, 08:09 by Amy Mathieson

If you’re staying at Sofitel Dubai The Palm there’s now one more dinner option to check out. Zoya by Maui is now open at the Palm Jumeirah hotel, serving up Indian dishes for dinner.

By day, poolside restaurant Maui Beach Restaurant dishes up salads, burgers and snacks, but come 7pm the spot transforms into an Indian eatery.

The menu comes from all across Indian including Goa and Kerala with dishes including Keralan fried chicken, lamb chops burrah, achari aloo (potato curry), paneer khurchanwala and meen moilee (a perch curry).

And along with the sea views and al fresco terrace there’s a live sitar player to entertain you.
