VIDEO: Ghanaian Leaders are ignorant and visionless – Kwaku Manu

17 Nov 2019, 08:06 by MyNewsGH

Actor, Kwaku Manu has described Ghanaian leaders as ignorant visionless who do not have the development of the country at heart.

According to him, it’s out of ignorance that Ghanaian leaders have failed to see the essence of the movie industry leaving it on the claws of death.

He made this known when he spoke on UTV.

He mentioned that local movies are used by other countries to sell their customs, tourist sites and other important aspects of their economy but Ghanaian leaders do not know how they can use movies for country branding.

He called on leaders in the country to undertake measures to help develop the music industry to ensure its growth for the betterment of the country.
