The Christmas and seasonal temp jobs in south Wales you can apply for now

From shops to warehouses, there's something for everyone

17 Nov 2019, 08:40 by Jillian MacMath

If you're stretched for cash over the festive period, you may be in luck.

There are a number of employers desperate for extra hands at this time of year.

From high street shops to restaurants and warehouses, there's a range of jobs available.

And with many not requiring previous experience, there's something for everybody.

We're rounded up some of the best opportunities available now across south Wales:
