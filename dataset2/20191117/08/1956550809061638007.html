Nelly to perform at Drai’s Dubai for New Year’s Eve 2019

The American superstar returns to ring in the New Year

17 Nov 2019, 08:32 by Darragh Murphy

You won’t just be hearing fireworks go off when the clock strikes midnight on New Year’s Eve, as the resounding beats from famed American rapper Nelly will be heard from Drai’s Dubai.

Time Out Dubai's Club of the Year will be hosting the hit rap and hip-hop star to ring in the New Year, and early-bird tickets to the event are now ready for the taking.

Kicking off from 9pm all the way to 2020 at 4am, celebrators can expect to dance away to some of his top tracks, including Ride Wit Me, Dilemma and Hot in Here.

This isn’t the first time Nelly has performed at the Meydan Racecourse superclub, and he’s put on a stellar show earlier this year, along with his first performance in Dubai at the Toy Room.

Already know you’re going to be there? Early bird tickets are out right now for the New Year's celebration on tickets.virginmegastore.me, with prices for ladies starting at Dhs300, including three drinks, and Dhs500 for gents with four drinks.

Once the early bird tickets run out (be fast), general admission for ladies will set them back Dhs500, with five drinks in return, and Dhs700 for gents, with six drinks.

New Year’s Eve is already kicking off to an amazing start, with Bruno Mars set to headline at Abu Dhabi’s huge Resolution by Night concert.
