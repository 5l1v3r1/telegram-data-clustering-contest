Deepika Padukone Mockingly Asks Fan Whom He Loves More – Her Or Ranveer Singh! See Video

The couple encountered a rather amusing situation involving a fan.

17 Nov 2019, 08:26 by IANS

Bollywood couple Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh celebrated their first wedding anniversary on Thursday, and the spirit of mush seems to have rubbed off on some of their fans, too.

During their visit to the Tirumala Tirupati Temple in Chittoor to seek the blessings of Lord Venkateswara on their special day, the couple encountered a rather amusing situation involving a fan.

A video posted on Instagram shows that, as Ranveer and Deepika arrived at the temple, a crazed fan couldn’t curb excitement.

“I love you,” he told Deepika first, which the actress acknowledged with a smile. The fan then addressed Ranveer and said: “I love you Bhaiyya.”

Deepika, sporting as ever, had a witty retort in place.

“But you love me more!” she quipped to the fan, and the actor-couple broke into laughter.

While the couple visited the Tirumala temple on November 14, they were at the Golden Temple in Amritsar the next day, to seek divine blessings.

Deepika and Ranveer will be seen together next on screen in Kabir Khan’s “83”, which accounts the inspiring story of India’s first-ever World Cup win in cricket in 1983. Ranveer plays Kapil Dev, who led the team to the famous victory, while Deepika essays Kapil’s wife Romi.
