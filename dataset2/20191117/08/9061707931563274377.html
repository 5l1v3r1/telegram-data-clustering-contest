Here's what £200,000 will get you in Burton's property market

On the move?

17 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Jane McFarlane

Every penny counts when you're looking for a new home.

And whether it's a flat, a terraced house or a semi-detached property you're looking for, it's important that you stick to your budget and get value for money.

But what will £200,000 get you in the Burton property market?

Quite a lot if truth be told, including bungalows and detached properties including ones in Stretton and Outwoods.

Some have been extended and include four bedrooms and even a conservatory.

We've rounded up 10 properties priced around £200,000 in Burton for sale on property website RightMove.

But don't forget, you'll also need to factor in other costs such as solicitor's fees, stamp duty and paying a removal firm to name but three.
