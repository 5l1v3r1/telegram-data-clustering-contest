Air India to fly from Chandigarh to Dharamshala six days a week now

The Air India flight that will operate six days a week, sparing Sunday, will take off from Gaggal Airport at 8 am and will land at Chandigarh Airport around 9.30 am.

17 Nov 2019, 08:08 by Express News Service

Flight operations from Chandigarh to Dharamshala began on Saturday with Air India’s flight taking off from Chandigarh. Tickets for the 70-seater flight was priced around Rs 1,712 per person.

The Air India flight that will operate six days a week, sparing Sunday, will take off from Gaggal Airport at 8 am and will land at Chandigarh Airport around 9.30 am.

From Chandigarh Airport, it will depart at 9.55 am and will reach Dharamshala at 10.55 am.

A passenger from Delhi, Eish, who was travelling in the Chandigarh to Dharamshala flight, said, “I am working with an IT firm, and I have to travel to Dharamshala many times. Earlier, it was difficult to reach Dharamshala and return to Delhi, but with the new flight operating between Chandigarh and Dharamshala, the journey has become convenient.”
