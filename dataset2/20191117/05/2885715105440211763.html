Watch: Charlotte Flair tries Bhangra on India visit, gets dance lessons from Varun Dhawan

On Friday, Charlotte Flair also met with Bollywood actor Varun Dhawan, who gave her a few desi dance lessons. Sharing the video on Twitter, Flair thanked Varun for preparing her for Bollywood.

17 Nov 2019, 05:37 by Sports Desk

WWE superstar Charlotte Flair was on a three-day visit to India and the former SmackDown Women’s Champion made sure that she doesn’t miss out on any fun. Flair landed in India on November 14 and celebrated the occasion of Children’s Day with kids from Special Olympics Bharat.

In the latest video shared by Flair, she was seen taking some Bhangra lessons from popular VJ Gaelyn Mendonca.
