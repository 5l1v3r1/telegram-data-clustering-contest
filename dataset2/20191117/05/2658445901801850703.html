Between Lai Mohammed and fashion designer daughter

17 Nov 2019, 05:41 by https://www.facebook.com/alao.h.joshua

Olushola Victor

Only a few people know that Lai Mohammed has a daughter who is doing well in the fashion industry and she prefers to keep it that way.

The Minister of Information’s daughter, Yeside Laguda, was one of the designers who showcased beautiful pieces at the just concluded GTBank Fashion Weekend.

When Mohammad stormed the venue of the event, Laguda maintained a distance. There was no hint that the creative director of MyQ Lifestyle Concepts is someone special to the politician. When her father was leaving too, she was not all over him.

Social Circuit learnt that she had worked seriously to maintain a low-profile as regards her family background. She likes to be called a designer, rather down Lai Mohammad’s daughter.
