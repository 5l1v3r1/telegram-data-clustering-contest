WWE Star Charlotte Flair Enjoys Her Trip To India; Getting ‘Bollywood Ready’ With Varun Dhawan

Charlotte is enjoying her 3-day trip to India.

17 Nov 2019, 05:19 by Koimoi.com Team

WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) superstars have always shared a bond of affection with India and visited the country to meet their fans. Wrestling stars like John Cena, Dwayne Johnson aka The Rock and Ryback have been quite vocal about their love for the culture and Indian audience. Now, joining the list of India’s admirers is the former champion and the daughter of legendary Ric Flair, Charlotte Flair.

Charlotte is enjoying her 3-day trip to India. Speaking about her experience in the country, she said, “I have been here (in India) twice and I feel like I was treated like royalty from the second I got here and that just goes to show how much they respect us as talent (wrestlers),” reports PTI.

She even said that WWE has a huge potential to grow in India.

The wrestling star was even spotted with Bollywood superstar Varun Dhawan and there’s one video, in which Varun is teaching some dance steps to her, going viral.

Charlotte took to Twitter for sharing the video. In the video, Varun is showing some moves to Charlotte on the song of his upcoming Street Dancer 3D.

Charlotte Flair wrote, “Not quite salsa lessons instead learning some Bollywood dance moves .. thank you @Varun_dvn getting me Bollywood ready! #Mumbai #India.”
