Two People Die in Explosion in Kiev Dormitory - Police

MOSCOW (Sputnik) - Two people have died in an explosion in a dormitory in Kiev, Ukraine's capital, the local police announced on late Friday.

17 Nov 2019, 05:13

On Saturday, at approximately 9 p.m. local time (19:00 GMT), the police received information about the explosion in the dormitory, located at Karl Marx Street.
