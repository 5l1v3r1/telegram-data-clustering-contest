Avenues Flat Hit by Mysterious Explosion

An explosion rocked an Avenues flat in Harare yesterday giving residents the biggest scare of their lives.

Circumstances leading to the explosion are still unclear however there are suspicions that it could be dynamite.

Windows were shattered but fortunately there were no injuries.

The police are yet to release an official statement on the matter.
