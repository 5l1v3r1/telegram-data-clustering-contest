Smiles at Liquor and Letterpress at WNY Book Arts Center

Printmaking might seem limited to newspapers and DIY-ers, but the WNY Book Arts Center makes it seem cool, thanks in part to its well-organized events. Liquor and Letterpress, an annual fundraiser, drew a crowd downtown on Saturday, Nov. 16, 2019.
