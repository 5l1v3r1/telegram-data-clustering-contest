Video: Ezekiel Elliott’s Touchdown Celebration Is Going Viral

17 Nov 2019, 20:55 by Matt Hladik

Ezekiel Elliott has had some entertaining touchdown celebrations in the past, and today he borrowed one from his quarterback’s pregame routine.

Before Dallas’ game against the Minnesota Vikings last Sunday night, Dak Prescott’s pre-game workout caught people’s attention. A clip of Prescott performing a drill designed to get his hips snapping around into his throws went viral.

Well, after Elliott scored a touchdown on a 17-yard reception from Prescott against the Detroit Lions today, he broke out the “Dak” as a celebration.
