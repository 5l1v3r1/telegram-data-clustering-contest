When is Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway coming back to TV?

17 Nov 2019, 20:30

Ant and Dec are returning to our screens tonight as the 2019 series of I’m A Celebrity kicks off.

The pair are reunited on the show this year after Ant took a year off in 2018 to focus on his health following his drink driving conviction and prescription drugs battle – leaving Holly Willoughby to step in and co-host alongside Dec.

They’ve already been back together fronting Britain’s Got Talent back in the spring, and it’s not too long before they’ll also be bringing Saturday Night Takeaway back to the screen after a two-year break.

But just when will that show be returning, following its year off?

Here’s what you need to know…

When is Ant and Dec’s Saturday Night Takeaway coming back to TV?

It seems like ages since we last saw Saturday Night Takeaway on our tellies, but the good news is we don’t have to wait too much longer for its return.

That’s because it’s been confirmed that the show will be back in 2020.

Although no start date has yet been announced for the next series, previous seasons have aired around February/March time.

So we’re keeping everything crossed that the new series will follow the same pattern – meaning it’s only a matter of months before it comes back.

Ant and Dec recently confirmed work had begun on the new series of the show, sharing a snap from the first production meeting as they said: ‘First meeting for Saturday Night Takeaway 2020…exciting times!’ along with details of how viewers can get involved.

‘There’s a real buzz around the show,’ a source teased about the show’s return back in August. ‘It promises to be bigger and better than ever.’

What time is I’m A Celebrity on ITV tonight?

The new series kicks off on ITV on Sunday night at 9pm.

Ant has recently opened up about how excited he is to be back on the show, telling The Sun: ‘It’s a brilliant place to work and a brilliant part of the world to be in.

‘Plus, I’m back with my best friend. I can’t wait.’
