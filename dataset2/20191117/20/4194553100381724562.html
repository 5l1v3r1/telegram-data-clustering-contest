(Video) Mane highlights reel shows he’s just as lethal with his weaker foot

17 Nov 2019, 20:42 by Ste Carson

Sadio Mane is one of the best players in the world – I don’t think anyone would argue with that statement, and these last two years he has proved himself as one of the finest finishers in the game.

The Senegal international finds himself on the left side of Liverpool’s attack, and sometimes he is forced to to strike the ball with his weaker, left foot.

Turns out he’s not too shabby with it! Reddit user Zagado890 has created a video compilation of Mane scoring with his weaker foot.
