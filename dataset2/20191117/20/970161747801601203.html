Woman raped in park while two men watched in 'despicable attack'

17 Nov 2019, 20:57

A woman was raped by a man in a park while two others stood and watched the ‘despicable attack’.

Three men have been arrested after the sexual assault in Wigan Greater Manchester.

The woman, in her 30s, was walking along Park Road, Wigan when she began talking to three men at around 9.20pm on Saturday 16 November.

The four all walked to Mesnes Park, where one of the men raped her while the other two men stood by.

A passer-by came across the incident and called the police.

Officers arrived at the park a short time later and arrested three men, aged 33, 25 and 24, on suspicion of rape.

They all remain in custody for questioning.

Detective Sergeant Sarah Bell of GMP’s Wigan District said: ‘This was a despicable attack on a lone woman who is currently being supported by specialist officers.

‘While we have arrested three people in connection with this incident, we are continuing to appeal to the public for information.

‘Were you in the area at the time and do you have any information that can assist us? If so, contact police immediately.’

Anyone with information should call police om 0161 856 1814/ 0161 856 7182 or the independent charity Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
