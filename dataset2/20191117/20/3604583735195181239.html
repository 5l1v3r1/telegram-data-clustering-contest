Ballan crash leaves man in critical condition

17 Nov 2019, 20:00 by Ben Hopkins

UPDATE: 10am

A man in his 40s who was airlifted to the Royal Melbourne Hospital in a critical condition following a crash in Ballan on Sunday night has had his condition improve.

A media spokesperson from the RMH told The Courier the man's condition has been downgraded from critical to serious.

EARLIER: 7am

A man has been flown to the Royal Melbourne Hospital in a critical condition after a crash on Sunday evening.

Emergency services were called to the Ballan-Daylesford Road in Ballan just after 7pm on Sunday to reports of a single-vehicle crash.
