Who is I'm A Celebrity comedian Andrew Maxwell, voice of Ex On The Beach?

17 Nov 2019, 20:20

This year’s I’m A Celebrity contestants have swapped their lives of luxury for a spot of jungle hardship as the new series gets underway on ITV.

And this year’s line-up looks distinctly promising, with Caitlyn Jenner and Ian Wright set to rough it in camp along with the likes of Girls Aloud’s Nadine Coyle and Good Morning Britain’s Kate Garraway.

Also joining the 10 campmates is comedian Andrew Maxwell – whose voice might be familiar to you even if his name isn’t.

Here’s what you need to know about the funnyman…

How old is Andrew Maxwell and where is he from?

Andrew is 44 years old, and could well celebrate his birthday in the jungle – as he’s due to turn 45 on 3 December.

He is from Dublin, where he also began his comedy career – having appeared at a local club run by Father Ted’s Ardal O’Hanlon before moving on to the stand-up circuit.

How is Andrew Maxwell best known?

Andrew has previously worked as the audience warm-up man for TV shows including They Think It’s All Over and Friday Night With Jonathan Ross – although has also been a familiar face in front of the camera.

He’s been on the likes of Never Mind The Buzzcocks, Mock The Week, Drunk History and Irish TV show The Panel – while as a stand-up he hosts his own regular London show, Fullmooners, and has made frequent appearances at the Edinburgh Festival.
