Westlife fans furious over 'ruined' Strictly Come Dancing performance

Excited Westlife fans were left furious as the band reunited for their first performance in years and accused the audience of messing it up

17 Nov 2019, 20:10 by Irishmirror.ie

Iconic boy band Westlife left fans annoyed as they took to the Strictly Come Dancing ballroom at Blackpool Tower to perform together for the first time in years.

Despite the big reunion, fans were left incredibly frustrated as they struggled to hear the band perform.

The cheering Strictly audience infuriated fans, who blasted their clapping for being too loud.

The British public took to Twitter to share their anger towards the cheering and clapping on tonight's results show.

One shared on Twitter : "Wish they audience would stop clapping so we could actually hear westlife sing, so bloody annoying every week #StrictlyComeDancing

Another person wrote: "If the audience would stop bloody clapping every SECOND that would be fantastic #Strictly ".

A third furiously penned: "#StrictlyComeDancing too much clapping over the music!!!! Getting grrrrrrrrr".

And a fourth added: "Brilliant seeing Westlife on Strictly even if the sound wasn't the best!"

Others complained they didn't know Westlife had reunited.

One quizzed: "When did #westlife get back together ... they dont look any older.. #StrictlyComeDancing ".

Another said: "Have I been sleeping under a bridge? When did Westlife get back together?" as one joked back: "I think when the money from their last reunion tour ran out!"

The results show saw Saffron Barker and AJ Pritchard compete against Michelle Visage and Giovanni Pernice in the dance off.

Michelle and Giovanni came bottom of the leaderboard last night as others earned themselves high scores and as a result, the pair were sent home tonight.
