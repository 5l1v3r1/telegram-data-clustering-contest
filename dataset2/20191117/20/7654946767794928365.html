Tires slashed on over 30 cars in Bay Ridge overnight

17 Nov 2019, 20:32 by Craig McCarthy

More than 30 car owners in Bay Ridge woke up to slashed tires Sunday morning, cops said.

Police were called to Shore Road and 72nd Street in Brooklyn just before 8 a.m. when they found 32 cars with punctured tires and scratched exterior, according to the NYPD.

No arrests have been made.
