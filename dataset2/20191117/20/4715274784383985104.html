PH baseball awardees

17 Nov 2019, 20:11

All-Star Whell Ghene Cagud Camral, 3rd base from Iloilo, and Best Pitcher Clariz Padernal Palma from Bacolod display their trophies in the 2019 BFA Women’s Baseball Asian Cup at the Panda Stadium in China, where the Philippines settled for third place in the Super Round.
