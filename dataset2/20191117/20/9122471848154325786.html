ESPN’s Prediction For Saturday’s Ohio State-Penn State Game

17 Nov 2019, 20:48 by Andrew Holleran

The biggest game on college football’s Week 13 slate is taking place in Columbus. The undefeated Ohio State Buckeyes will be hosting Penn State in what is essentially a play-in game for the Big Ten Championship.

Ohio State enters the game as a massive favorite. The Buckeyes are listed as a 19-point favorite by the sportsbooks. That’s a massive spread for a game between two top 10 teams.

ESPN doesn’t seem to think the game will be close, either.

The Football Power Index is extremely confident in Ryan Day’s team.

ESPN’s Football Power Index is giving Ohio State an 84.3 percent chance of winning this game, giving Penn State just a 15.7 percent chance of pulling off the upset.

Ohio State and Penn State will kick off at noon E.T.

The game will be on FOX.
