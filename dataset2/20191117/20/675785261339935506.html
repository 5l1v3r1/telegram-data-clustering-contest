Ian Wright admits he lost his virginity "late" due to being "scared of that situation"

Cheeky chappie Ian Wright is a contestant in this year's I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! - but according to the man himself, he wasn't always confident

17 Nov 2019, 20:31 by Jake Polden, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/jake-polden/

Ian Wright is the cheeky chappie of ITV Sport; the former Arsenal forward who was known for his commanding presence and ability to hit the back of the net.

But according to the Gunners legend, who is a contestant in this year's I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!, this wasn't always the case.

Wright has previously admitted that he lacked confidence with women when growing up, adding that he lost his virginity "late" as he was "scared of that situation".

The 56-year-old attributes feeling this way to not receiving a lot a love when growing up. But adds that things have now changed in his life, and he's hugs and kisses his sons to the point where it's "embarrassing".

"I was very insecure during my teenage and adolescent years. I lost my virginity very late," Wright said on the Fortunately with Fi And Jane podcast.

"I was very scared of that situation, it was literally just after my 18th birthday. I closed myself off. I've learned how to love I didn't get a lot of love when I was younger.

"I hug all the time now, to the point where it's embarrassing. I kiss my sons. I want to do it for myself. It's what I would have wanted.

"I knew I needed counselling when I was older to deal with aggression and the anger, I was lucky. My wife is full of love and her family is too."

Wright recently spoke out in a video ahead of entering the Jungle.

He explained that while he will miss football, he's looking forward to seeing the back of VAR for at least a few weeks.

"I'll miss football. I love football, it's my whole life," said Wright. "I will not miss VAR. I'm going in there to get away from VAR."
