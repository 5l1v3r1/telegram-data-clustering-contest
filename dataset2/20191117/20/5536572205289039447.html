Injured Jets LB Avery Williamson Went Grocery Shopping in Full Uniform (PIC)

17 Nov 2019, 20:27 by Darrelle Lincoln

While the rest of the Jets prepared for their Week 1 opener against the Buffalo Bills, Avery Williamson was getting ready to go under the knife.

The Jets linebacker had surgery back in September to repair his torn ACL and effectively end his season before it even began.

The 27-year-old got tired of not suiting up on game day’s so he grabbed his full uniform and pads, put them on….and went shopping.

Yes, he showed up at a local grocery store like this.
