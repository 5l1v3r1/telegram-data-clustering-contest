How long is I'm A Celebrity on for? New series of award winning show kicks off on ITV

A new mix of famous faces are bedding down in camp for the new series of I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here

17 Nov 2019, 20:57 by Simon Duke

There will be no more shouts of “let’s get ready to juuuungle” as the new series of I'm A Celebrity is now underway.

The ITV ratings winner, won by North East lasses Vicky Pattison and Scarlett Moffatt in 2015 and 2016 respectively returns to our screens on Sunday night for its 19th series - we’re all of a sudden feeling very old!

As ever, this year’s I'm A Celebrity lineup unites a very eclectic mix of famous faces with everyone from Ian Wright to Caitlyn Jenner.

Other celebs in the mix for 2018 include Nadine Coyle, Kate Garraway and Adele Roberts.

As we’ve come to realise, the return of I’m A Celebrity means that Christmas is just around the corner but how long will the new series last?

How long is I’m A Celebrity on for?

While the exact end date is still being kept under wraps, I’m A Celebrity traditionally runs for three weeks, meaning we should have our new King or Queen of the Jungle by December 7.

The series launches with a bumper episode on Sunday, November 17 and is set to run for either an hour or 90 minutes every night.

When do people start getting voted out?

Typically, the celeb campers have around two weeks to settle in before being given the boot, so expect the first elimination to come towards the end of next week.
