XMAS COUNTDOWN

Glasgow Christmas lights switch on brought to thundering close with spectacular fireworks display at George Square

17 Nov 2019, 20:50 by Conor Rafferty

GLASGOW’S Christmas lights were switched on with a bang thanks to a spectacular fireworks display.

Thousands of people flocked to George Square tonight for the annual festive event signalling the city’s official countdown to December 25.

The evening was hosted by Clyde 1 breakfast favourites George Bowie and Cassi Gillespie.

The pair were joined by a number of music acts who helped warm up the crowd.

And the switch on was brought to a thundering close with a huge fireworks show after the lights were turned on.

An incredible video shared by Glasgow Loves Christmas on social media shows dozens of rockets lighting up the sky over George Square.

They tweeted: “Christmas in Glasgow has arrived! The Christmas lights in George Square are on!”
