(Video) Brilliant clip emerges of an emotional Fabinho singing YNWA after famous win

17 Nov 2019, 20:26 by Ste Carson

Fabinho is Liverpool hard man, and perhaps the finest defensive midfielder playing in Europe at the moment. His contribution to our Champions League title will never be forgotten.

A video clip has emerged of the Brazilian lined up with the players, singing along to our famous anthem ‘You’ll Never Walk Alone’ and he appears to get a little teary-eyed.

By no means are we trying to shame the man, it’s just a beautiful moment to see such a powerful presence on the pitch touched by the club in this way.
