Woman sexually assaulted by hooded man as she walked down path in Newcastle

The woman, 23, was walking in Newbiggin Hall when a man grabbed her

17 Nov 2019, 20:17 by Sophie Barley

A woman was sexually assaulted as she walked down a path in Newcastle.

Police are appealing for witnesses to the incident which happened at 7.45pm on Thursday November 14.

The 23-year-old woman was walking down a path off Whittingham Road, Newbiggin Hall.

She was approached by a man she didn't know who then grabbed her.

Police say the woman fell to the ground and screamed loudly.

The man then made off down Whittingham Road towards Kielder Close.

He is described as white, 5’7”, of thin build with dark stubble.

He is believed to be in his 30s. He was wearing a blue jacket with the hood up, dark coloured trousers and shoes.

Enquiries to locate the man remain ongoing and officers are now appealing for anyone who made have seen or heard anything to come forward.

 The man, or anyone who knows his identity, is encouraged to contact officers on 101 quoting 1050 141119 or email  579@northumbria.pnn.police.uk .
