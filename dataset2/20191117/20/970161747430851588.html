Who is Roman Kemp's famous actor dad and how is he best known?

17 Nov 2019, 20:10

I’m A Celebrity 2019 kicks off on Sunday night, with 10 famous faces braving the elements and their fears as they swap normal life for jungle life.

And among those hoping to nab that King of the jungle title this year is Capital FM presenter Roman Kemp – who could be facing a hard time in camp if the BTS fans have their way.

The 26-year-old has followed in the famous footsteps of his dad by having a career in showbiz – but just who is Roman’s famous father and how is he best known?

Here’s what you need to know…

Who is Roman Kemp’s famous actor dad?

Roman Kemp is the son of former 80s pop star and actor Martin Kemp.

He is the younger of Martin’s two children with wife Shirlie Holliman, a former backing singer for Wham!

The couple, who were married in 1988, also have a daughter, Harley Moon, who was born in 1989 and works as a photographer.

How is Martin Kemp best known?

Martin originally found fame with the 80s band Spandau Ballet, which also featured his older brother Gary Kemp, along with members Tony Hadley (who was on I’m A Celebrity in 2015), John Keeble and Steve Norman.

He played bass in the band, who scored a string of hits during the decade including To Cut A Long Story Short, Chant No 1, Gold, Only When You Leave and the classic chart-topper True.
