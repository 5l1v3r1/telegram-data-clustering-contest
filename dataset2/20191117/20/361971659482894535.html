Lil Reese, Out of Hospital, Updates Fans After Being Shot: “I Got Hella Luck”

“I appreciate everybody keeping me in they prayers”

17 Nov 2019, 20:48 by Evan Minsker

On Tuesday, Chicago drill rapper Lil Reese was shot in the neck in an incident that took place in the Chicago suburb Country Club Hills. Hospital officials said at the time that Reese was in critical condition. Reese is now out of the hospital and has shared some updates with fans.

On Friday, he streamed an update of himself driving around via Instagram Live. Today, he wrote some updates and shared a photo showing his neck stitches. “Lifes a gamble I got hella luck,” he wrote. “I appreciate everybody keeping me in they prayers tho I can’t lie thank y’all no matter what you do out here always talk to god.” He also wrote that he’d need $1 million to tell his story.
