Tua Tagovailoa Issues Statement About His Season-Ending Injury

17 Nov 2019, 03:20 by Andrew Holleran

Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa has taken to Twitter to address his season-ending hip injury, suffered late in the first half of today’s blowout win over Mississippi State.

Tagovailoa, whose college career is likely over, wanted to thank everyone for the well wishes.

“Thank you all for the prayers and well wishes! God always has a plan,” he wrote.

Alabama announced earlier this evening that the Heisman Trophy contender would miss the rest of the season with his injury, though he is expected to make a full recovery.
