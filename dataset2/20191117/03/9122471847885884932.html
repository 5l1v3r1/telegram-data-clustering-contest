College GameDay Announces It’s Headed To Columbus For Ohio State vs. Penn State

17 Nov 2019, 03:22 by Matt Lombardi

Next Saturday, Penn State and Ohio State will meet in one of the biggest games of the 2019 college football season. ESPN College GameDay will be in town for the affair.

ESPN announced the news Saturday night. The show was in Waco, Texas for this week’s showdown between Baylor and Oklahoma.

Ohio State boasts a 10-0 record and is currently the No. 2 team in the College Football Playoff rankings. Penn State, after a close win over Indiana, is 9-1 and No. 9 in the rankings.

An Ohio State win would secure the Big Ten East title for the Buckeyes. A Penn State victory would give the Nittany Lions the tiebreaker – assuming they can also take care of business against Rutgers.
