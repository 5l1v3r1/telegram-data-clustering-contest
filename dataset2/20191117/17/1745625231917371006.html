Ariana James shared a gorgeous snapshot of Stephany Gonzalez on her Instagram page, and Stephany returned the favor.

Ariana James has gained an impressive fan following for sharing photos of her scantily-clad figure on Instagram. However, for her latest post, the Colombian born bombshell decided to cast the spotlight on another stunning social media star whose bikini photos are always a big hit, Venezuelan model Stephany Gonzalez.

On Sunday, Ariana took to Instagram to share a photo of Stephany with her 1.9 million followers. In the image, Stephany is rocking a pale blue bikini. The two-piece is constructed out of a satin-like material, and the top is trimmed with lace, giving it a lingerie-like appearance. It has thick straps that provide plenty of support for Stephany, who has a slightly curvier figure than Ariana. However, both women have similar slender waists, and Stephany’s is wrapped with thick, crisscrossed straps made out of the same fabric as the rest of her bikini. This detail draws even more attention to her flat stomach and hourglass shape.

Stephany’s bottoms feature similarly thick straps that sit up high on the model’s hips. The thick bands also run along the front of the garment, causing it to slightly pucker. This causes a small gap between the waistband and her flawless skin.

Stephany Gonzalez is wearing her straight dark hair down and parted to the side. Her gorgeous locks are so long that they reach the small of her back. For her beauty look, she’s sporting a rosy lip and minimal eye makeup.

Ariana James turned off the comments when she uploaded the photo of her fellow fitness enthusiast, but the snapshot of Stephany garnered almost 17,000 likes over the course of an hour. In the caption of her post, Ariana let her followers know that she thought Stephany’s snapshot was “perfection.”

Stephany Gonzalez returned the favor over on her own Instagram account by sharing a photo of Ariana James with her 1.1 million followers. In the image that she chose, her friend is wearing a pair of skintight skinny jeans and a black lingerie-inspired crop top. The sleeveless top is constructed mostly out of sheer lace, but opaque black cups have been added to the bust to protect its wearer’s modesty. The garment also features a cutout on the bust. Three black straps extend down from the neck and shoulder straps over the opening. They connect to a piece of underwire in the center of Ariana’s chest.
