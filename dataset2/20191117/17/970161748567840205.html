Van Gerwen sends classy message to Price after Grand Slam of Darts defeat

17 Nov 2019, 17:10

Michael van Gerwen suffered a shock 16-12 defeat to Gerwyn Price in the semi-finals of the Grand Slam of Darts, but has taken the disappointing loss on the chin.

The world number one had never lost to the Iceman in 19 previous meetings, but succumbed to the defending champion in Wolverhampton on Sunday afternoon.

MVG turned in an impressive performance, averaging 101.91, but missed double cost him and the Welshman’s fantastic doubling saw him down the world champ for the first time.

Van Gerwen was magnanimous in defeat, giving his opponent a hug on the stage, and then posting a classy tweet soon after the match.

‘All I can say is Gerwyn deserved to win,’ Van Gerwen tweeted. ‘I made too many mistakes and gave him too many chances. Today he took advantage and credit is to him for that.
