What channel is Patriots vs. Eagles on today? Schedule, time for Week 11 NFL game

17 Nov 2019, 17:45 by James Crabtree-Hannigan

The Eagles enter Week 11 tied for the lead in the NFC East, but a game against the Patriots makes a return to .500 likely for Philadelphia.

Both teams are coming off a bye. Last time out, New England took its first loss of the season to Baltimore. Still, the Patriots are tied for the best record in the league and are outscoring their opponents by more than 19 points per game. The last time a team had that a point differential that high over a full season was New England's undefeated 2007.

MORE: Full betting preview for Patriots vs. Eagles

Though they'll be on the road, the Patriots will be expected to beat Philadelphia. The Eagles have crept to 5-4 with wins over Buffalo and Chicago, but their body of work remains unimpressive, even if it's good enough to lead the division for now.

Below is all the info you need to watch Patriots vs. Eagles on Sunday, including the TV channeland kickoff time.

What channel is Patriots vs. Eagles on today?

  • TV channel (national): CBS
  • TV channel (Dallas): KYW
  • TV channel (Detroit): WBZ

Jim Nantz (play-by-play), Tony Romo (analyst) and Tracy Wolfson (reporter) will be on the call for Patriots vs. Eagles.

For those who can't get to a TV and wish to find Patriots vs. Eagles on the radio, the New England call can be heard on Sirius XM channel 820, and the Philadelphia call can be heard on Sirius XM channel 227.

Patriots vs. Eagles: What time is kickoff?

  • Date: Sunday, Nov. 17
  • Start time: 4:25 p.m. ET

The 4:25 p.m. ET kickoff will be the Patriots' second such kickoff and the Eagles' seventh so far this season.

Three of New England's past four games were night games, and they return to primetime in Week 13 on "Sunday Night Football" against the Texans. The Sunday night game in Week 12 will feature the Eagles, who host the Seahawks.
