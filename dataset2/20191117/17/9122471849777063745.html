The Week 13 Coaches’ Poll Top 25 Has Been Released

17 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Andrew Holleran

The Week 13 Coaches’ Poll top 25 has been released following a somewhat eventful Week 12 of the 2019 regular season.

Week 12 was highlighted, unfortunately, by the devastating season-ending injury to Alabama quarterback Tua Tagovailoa. He suffered a hip injury that will force him to miss the rest of the season.

The weekend also saw to undefeated teams – Minnesota, Baylor – losing. The College Football Playoff picture is starting to get a little clearer as a result.

There’s been some shuffling in the latest Coaches’ Poll top 25, which was just released by USA TODAY:

The truly meaningful rankings, the College Football Playoff rankings, will come out on Tuesday night. We’ll have a better idea of the College Football Playoff picture then.
