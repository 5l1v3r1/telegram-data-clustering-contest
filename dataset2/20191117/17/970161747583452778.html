Strictly's Claudia Winkleman vows to never compete in the show herself

17 Nov 2019, 17:51

Claudia Winkleman may be one of the leading ladies of Strictly Come Dancing – but don’t expect her to actually hit the dancefloor anytime soon.

The presenter has ruled out ever taking part on the series because she doesn’t think she has the rhythm to get far in the competition.

Speaking to the Daily Star, she said: ‘Me dancing on Strictly? That will never happen! I am 47. Never. Watch me. I don’t move. I am always lying down.’

‘I used to dance with Len Goodman when I presented It Takes Two,’ she continued. ‘But I am not sure that would be translated as busting a few moves.

‘I was standing there going, “What… Where?”.’

So we guess for now the star is more than happy in her Clauditorium and intends to stay there for the foreseeable.

Alongside Tess Daly, Claudia has been hosting the Saturday primetime favourite since 2014, taking over full-time from legend Bruce Forsyth.

The pair have formed a tight bond and have promised that they’ll only ever leave the show together, after becoming known as a unit.

Just recently, her co-host Tess opened up about her time on the show, and revealed some back stage secrets to Metro.co.uk.

Despite presenting more than 200 shows, the 49-year-old revealed she still has a contingency plan in place for if things were to go wrong.

This week, she joined the class of 2019 as they performed at the iconic Blackpool ballroom – offering them moral support and debrief interviews after each dance.

Now they only have 7 couples left in the competition and the heat is being turned up as they edge closer to the finale.

Strictly Come Dancing continues Saturday at 7pm with the results show on Sunday at 7.15pm on BBC One.
