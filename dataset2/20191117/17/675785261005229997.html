Body found in search for 26-year-old man who went missing after work

Jonathon Donaldson was last seen at around 4pm on November 11 but did not return home to Armadale, Scotland. Formal identification has yet to take place

17 Nov 2019, 17:32 by Robert Fairnie, Chiara Fiorillo

A body has been found during the search for a man who went missing after work earlier this week, West Lothian Police said.

Jonathon Donaldson, 26, was last seen leaving his workplace in Uphall at around 4pm on November 11, but did not return home to Armadale, Scotland.

Police issued an appeal for information in a bid to trace him, but today they said a body has been found during the search.

"A body has been found during the search for Jonathon Donaldson from Whitburn," police said.

"Formal identification has yet to take place, but his family have been made aware.

"Thanks to all who helped during the search."

In the appeal Mr Donaldson, from the village of Whitburn, is described as being around 5ft 2ins tall, of slim build and with light brown hair, Edinburgh Live reports.

When he disappeared, he was wearing a black jumper with a grey hood, dark coloured trousers, dark shoes and was carrying a black Adidas rucksack.
