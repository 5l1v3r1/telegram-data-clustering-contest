FORD v FERRARI Is An Exhilarating High-Speed Drama That Will Blow You Away - MOVIE REVIEW

17 Nov 2019, 17:00 by Kristian Odland

Ford v Ferrari is a based on the real corporate rivalry between two of the world’s most-renowned car makers. Directed by James Mangold (Logan), the movie stars Matt Damon and Christian Bale. Not only does the film showcase an era when Ford began to break out of the repetition of only making economy cars, but it’s also an inspirational story with great characters and mind-blowing visuals. But is it possible for this feature film to rank high on your list of favorites?
