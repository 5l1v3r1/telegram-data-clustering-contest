‘Gears Tactics’ to Make an Appearance at This Year’s Game Awards in December

17 Nov 2019, 17:20 by Mike Wilson

The Coalition and Microsoft have been focusing on Gears 5 over the past year (seeing as it was, you know, released earlier this year), but what about Gears Tactics? The game was largely absent from this year’s E3, though Microsoft is still very much at work developing it. In fact, the strategy game will make an appearance at this year’s Game Awards, as was announced on Twitter.
