3 Head Coaches On The “Hot Seat” After Week 12

17 Nov 2019, 17:34 by Matt Hladik

There’s only a couple of weeks left in the college football regular season. We’ve already seen three Power 5 coaches get fired, with more changes coming.

Rutgers fired Chris Ash in late September, while Florida State dismissed Willie Taggart earlier this month and Arkansas canned Chad Morris last weekend. Morris made our most recent list of “Coaches on the Hot Seat” prior to being removed from his post.

With that out of the way, let’s take a look at which coaches are still sweating after Week 12.

Michigan State head coach Mark Dantonio

Dantonio’s Spartans were demolished by rival Michigan yesterday, losing 44-10 in Ann Arbor. Michigan State is now 4-6 on the season and must win its last two games to become bowl eligible.

If it were up to many MSU fans, Dantonio would be gone after the season. But his situation is more complicated, as Spartan Nation’s Hondo Carpenter detailed yesterday.
