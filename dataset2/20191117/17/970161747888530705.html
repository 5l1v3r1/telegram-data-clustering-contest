Roy Keane delivers damning verdict on £80m Man Utd transfer target Declan Rice

17 Nov 2019, 17:42

Roy Keane has delivered a damning verdict on Manchester United transfer target Declan Rice.

West Ham and England midfielder Rice has emerged as a target for the Red Devils, who are reportedly eyeing a stunning £80m move ahead of the January transfer window.

United spent almost £150m on new signings last summer but failed to bolster Ole Gunnar Solskjaer’s midfield options despite the departures of Marouane Fellaini and Ander Herrera.
