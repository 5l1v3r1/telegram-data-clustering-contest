Kelsey Merritt Poses On Her Back & Lifts Legs In Flirty Black Dress

Mizuki Hisaka

Kelsey Merritt shared a flirty new Instagram photo yesterday, and she was seen posing on the ground. The Victoria’s Secret Angel lay on a tan carpet on her back, and lifted her legs up on the wooden dresser behind her. She bent her right knee and was seen adjusting the straps on her heels, while she extended her other leg and rested it on the side of the dresser. The model glanced at the camera with a sultry expression on her face, her lips slightly parted. She slicked her hair back in a middle part, and wore shimmery pink lipstick and dark eyeliner.

The dress that she wore was black, and featured a low V-neckline with oversized sleeves. The sleeves extended to the side several inches, and brought attention to her neckline. The piece also featured a sheer panel on her chest, as her cleavage peeked through. Kelsey accessorized with a gold bracelet on her right hand, along with a ring on her left pointer finger. She also wore dangle earrings with two, gold circles. She didn’t wear a necklace, however, and kept the attention on her dress.

Beside the bombshell was a floor-length mirror, which offered a glimpse of her legs in the reflection.

The captions revealed that she was heading to the Revolve Awards, and fans left plenty of messages in the comments section.

“Had to rotate my phone,” joked a follower.

“I’m putting my heels on this way from now on! haha,” exclaimed a fan.

“If u don’t win one of these silly revolve awards then I demand a recount,” declared an admirer.

“Excited to see you come back here in the philippines @kelseymerritt,” noted a fourth Instagram user.
