NFL Week 11 coverage map: TV schedule for CBS, Fox regional broadcasts

17 Nov 2019, 17:46 by Tadd Haislop

The NFL coverage map for Week 11makes clear what CBS and Fox considerthe best games Sunday's NFL TV schedule has to offer.

In the early Sunday afternoon time slot for CBS, the majority of the country will see Texans at Ravens. In CBS' late Sunday afternoon window, almost the entire country will be presented Patriots at Eagles, the network's game of the week. Only a few markets will get Bengals at Raiders.

As for the early and late Sunday afternoon games on Fox, most of the country will get Cowboys at Lions, a game scheduled to kick off at 1 p.m. ET.

Below are the full NFL coverage maps for Week 11, plus a list of major TV markets and the CBS and Fox games that will be presented in each on Sunday.
