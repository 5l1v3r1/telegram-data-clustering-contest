Heart Evangelista shares what she eats to maintain her weight without workouts

17 Nov 2019, 17:52

Heart Evangelista revealed her secret on how she maintains her weight even without exercise: She gets her diet food from Aivee Cafe.

"What I like about this place is even if it's like diet food, it's really good and I don't workout I just have the diet food and I've maintained my weight," said the Kapuso Queen of Creative Collaborations.

Heart said when she got married, she gained 20 pounds and now that she's back to her pre-marriage weight, Heart said it's easier to maintain her weight with Dr. Food, the meals prepared by Aivee's in-house nutritionists.
