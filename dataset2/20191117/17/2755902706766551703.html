JUST IN: Fuel Shoots Up Again As ZERA Announces New Prices

17 Nov 2019, 17:09 by Timothy Ndoro

The Zimbabwe Energy Regulatory Authority (ZERA) has announced yet another increase in the price of fuel effective Monday, 18 November 2019.

Diesel has gone up by 21 cents per litre to cost ZWL$17.74 per litre while Blended Petrol has gone up by 30 cents per litre to cost ZWL$17.07 per litre.

This week’s margin of increase is much bigger than last week’s increase which was only 10 cents and 11 cents per litre of diesel and petrol respectively.
