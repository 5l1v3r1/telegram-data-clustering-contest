Westlife tour dates for 2020 Summer in the Stadiums concerts and how to get tickets

Westlife, who have just released new album spectrum, will tour the UK in 2020 on their Summer in the Stadiums tour

17 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Simon Duke

They sold out venues across the UK on their most successful tour to date earlier this year and Westlife will hit the road again for a host of outdoor shows next year.

Fans of the group speculated another tour might be in the offing after they announced a huge concert at Wembley Stadium for next year and they were spot on as Shane, Mark, Kian and Nicky have announced a string of outdoor shows for next year, hot on the heels of their sold out Twenty tour, which delighted fans at Newcastle's Utilita Arena in June , as well as legions of fans all over the country.

  Westlife  – the UK’s top selling album group of the 21 Century –  have announced a 15-date Stadiums in the Summer Tour for 2020, with tickets on sale now from Ticketmaster.

The lads will perform on Sunday night's Strictly Come Dancing results show.
