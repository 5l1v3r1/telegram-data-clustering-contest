Kriti Kharbanda's tantrums cost her Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi starrer 'Chehre'? Read details

17 Nov 2019, 18:25 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kriti Kharbanda is currently enjoying the success of her recently released film 'Housefull 4'. She is also promoting her next 'Pagalpanti' on various platforms.

After 'Pagalpanti', the actress was supposed to star in 'Chehre' alongside Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi. However, according to the latest report, Kriti is no more part of 'Chehre'.

It is being reported that Kriti was showing tantrums and her team's mismanagement of dates were the main reasons for the same. The report further stated that the makers were trying to make changes according to her demands, however, in the end, it didn't work. Also, there was a confrontation between the director and the actress after which the producers decided to go ahead with the project with her. Now, the actress is no more part of the film and the makers are hunting for a replacement.

The actress was a part of the first schedule of the film. However, now, her part will be re-shot with the new actress. The report added that after the fallout, both the makers and actress mutually parted their ways.

'Chehre' helmed by Rumi Jafry has been slated for April 2020 release.
