What time is I'm A Celebrity on ITV tonight? Ant & Dec go back into the jungle

I'm A Celebrity has returned to our screens for a new series of ITV which kicks off on Sunday night

17 Nov 2019, 18:51 by Simon Duke

It's official...Christmas is coming and that's because I'm A Celebrity Get Me Out of Here is back on our screens.

It's hard to believe that it's been 17 years since I'm A Celebrity first hit our screens, with Tony Blackburn the ITV show's first ever winner.

Since then the likes of Kerry Katona, Dougie Poynter, Christopher Biggins, Vicky Pattison and Scarlett Moffatt have all triumphed in the jungle and before long, we now know who'll be following in their footsteps this year.

The I'm A Celebrity line-up for this year includes Girls Aloud singer Nadine Coyle, GMB host Kate Garraway and Radio DJ Adele Roberts who has a shocking Big Brother past.

The current I'm A Celebrity odds have football legend Ian Wright as favourite to win, but they're changing all the time.

If you love the show, make sure you don't miss any of the jungle action.

What time is I'm A Celebrity on?

The first episode of I'm A Celebrity will be a bumper edition, running from 9 - 10.45pm.

I'm A Celeb will then continue every night on ITV at 9pm and typically run for an hour, although Monday', November 18's will be on for 90 minutes.

And on Saturday, November 23, the show will start slightly later at 9.10pm because of X Factor Celebrity.
