Newcastle United's Arsenal transfer plan and other moves Aston Villa's relegation rivals could make

Aston Villa news | A look at the transfer rumours for teams at the bottom end of the Premier League heading into January as they battle for survival with Dean Smith's Villa

17 Nov 2019, 18:30 by Matt Davies

Aston Villa are in the lower reaches of the Premier League and looking to strengthen their survival credentials as January approaches.

When the new year comes around, every side in the top flight is likely to be aiming to bolster their ranks for the second half of the campaign.

Here, we take a look at some of the business the teams around Villa in the Premier League table are rumoured to be ready to do come January.

Newcastle want Xhaka

Newcastle United hope to land Granit Xhaka on loan from Arsenal in January.

 

Sky Sports say Newcastle defender Fabian Schar's friendship with his Swiss international teammate could persuade Xhaka to move north.

The midfielder has not played for Arsenal since his angry reaction to being booed by fans when he subbed off against Crystal Palace.

Xhaka was subsequently stripped of the captaincy and looks set to leave, although Newcastle would have to pay most of his wages.

Toney touted for £13m

Burnley and Bournemouth are being linked with £13m moves for Peterborough United striker Ian Toney.

The former Newcastle striker has scored 35 goals in 75 games since joining from Newcastle for just £350,000.

Posh chairman Darragh MacAntony said: "Toney will break the transfer record for a League One player when he goes. If he goes that is. He'll certainly smash the Posh record.

"We don't want to lose anyone in January, but you can't guarantee anything in football."

Kean tipped for Italy return

Moise Kean has reportedly been offered to AC Milan after a nightmare spell at Everton .

Kean, 19, joined Everton in a £27m deal from Juventus in the summer but is yet to score and was left out of the squad for the clash with Southampton after turning up late for a team meeting.

Top prospect signs new deal

Striker Joe Gerhardt, 17, who was linked with Everton and a number of other Premier League clubs, has signed a new contract at Wigan.
