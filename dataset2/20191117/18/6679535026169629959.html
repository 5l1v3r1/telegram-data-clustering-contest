Six held for vandalising temple

17 Nov 2019, 18:57 by Staff Reporter

Namakkal police arrested six persons for vandalising a temple near Sendamangalam recently.

The Periyasamy Temple at the foothills of Kolli Hills was vandalised by a group of persons on November 10. They also attacked the house of temple priest Raghu.

Police arrested Paramasivam from Nadukombai bus stand on Thursday. He was sent to judicial custody. On inquiry, police found that Paramasivam had issues with Raghu, his nephew, regarding rights to do rituals at the temple. So, he and the gang attacked the temple and Raghuâ€™s house.
