Bafana Match-Winner: It Was Important To Bounce Back

17 Nov 2019, 18:26 by Soccer Laduma

Lebogang Phiri scooped the Man of the Match accolade as Bafana Bafana claimed victory over Sudan in their 2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualifier.

Read: Wasteful Bafana Sink Group C Leaders In AFCON Qualifier

After losing to Ghana on Thursday, Bafana bounced back with a 1-0 win over Group C leader Sudan on Sunday afternoon.

Phiri got the goal for Molefi Nteski's charges and feels it was vital that three points were secured on the day.

"I think today we came out and proved that we want to win this game, it's an important one, of course we went down in the first game, we needed to rectify our mistakes and I think we played as a collective, that resulted in us winning today," Phiri told SABC.
"For us as players it proved that at the end of the first half we got a goal, so we don't look down on everything that was happening, we kept pushing. We knew that if we keep on working hard we'll get the goal eventually.
