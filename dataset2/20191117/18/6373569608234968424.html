Best paid temporary Christmas jobs in Plymouth revealed

We bring you a list of temporary jobs in the region for this festive season

17 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Ginette Davies

If you fancy boosting your income this Christmas then a temporary festive job may be just the thing.

There are plenty of companies looking for short term staff to boost their teams during some of the busiest days of the year.

New data shows there are more than 20,000 Christmas temporary jobs available in the UK right now.

A variety of roles are available at companies including Tesco, Sports Direct, Royal Mail and Superdrug.
