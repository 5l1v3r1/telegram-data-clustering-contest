Strictly's Michelle Visage posts iconic throwback to mark RuPaul's birthday

The Drag Race judge paid tribute to her pal and colleague RuPaul with a throwback snap on his birthday

17 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Irishmirror.ie

Michelle Visage treated fans to an iconic throwback snap as she sent well wishes to to RuPaul on his 59th birthday on Sunday.

The Drag Race judge, 51, took to Instagram to upload a image of RuPaul dressed in drag clad with a cowboy hat and suede waistcoat alongside a blonde wig and face of expertly applied make-up.

Next to RuPaul in the throwback image was Michelle who wore a similar western-style ensemble with a cow print shirt and hat.

Alongside the image, that had a strong 90s feel thanks to the pairs clothing, she penned: "HAPPY BIRTHDAY to my ride or die. This world is a better place because of your love and wisdom.

"Everyday I thank God for you. Thank you for all that you have done, for all that you continue to do and for what's to come. You are magical, Ru and I couldn't possibly love you more. X"

RuPaul and Michelle have pioneered the LGBTQIA+ community over the past 20 years and brought drag to the mainstream thanks to RuPaul’s Drag Race which began in the US and started in the UK this year.

Both Michelle and RuPaul are on the judging panel of the hit show.

It comes after Strictly Come Dancing fans are worried for Michelle as she has landed a place at the bottom of the leaderboard on Saturday's edition of the dancing show.

She performed to the popular 1990 Madonna hit Vogue with professional partner Giovanni Pernice, but her time left in the competition could now be at risk due to receiving the lowest score.

The dancing duo scored 32 points for their couples' choice routine but it put them below all the other couples in tonight's show.

One fan took to Twitter to share: "See if Emma makes it through to the #StrictlyComeDancing final over Michelle Visage I will be absolutely FUMING."

Another viewer penned: "How is Michelle behind Chris and Saffron? She did fantastic tonight".

A third begged: "Everybody vote now for Michelle & Giovanni please!! We can’t let them go now".

And a fourth explained: "Wait, Michelle & Giovanni came BOTTOM of the leaderboard??? WTAF?!"
