Blue tent in Lozells amid serious police incident - what we know so far

A tent was erected in a 999 alert on Lozells Street at around 4pm on Sunday, November 17

17 Nov 2019, 18:12 by James Rodger, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Police and paramedics are on the scene at a Birmingham street tonight amid reports of a serious incident in the city.

A blue tent was erected in a 999 alert on Lozells Street, Lozells, with West Midlands Police officers and emergency service workers spotted at the scene from around 4pm.

Officers were working yards from a chemist, pictures from the scene showed.

The road was fully sealed off with a police patrol car and a van at the junction. A group of onlookers were standing at the cordon.
