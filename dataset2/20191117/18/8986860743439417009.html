Shatta Wale & Stonebwoy jamming together as Efia Odo joins Shatta Wale on stage at 4Syte TV Awards

17 Nov 2019, 18:42 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

It was an exciting moment at the just ended 4Syte TV Awards as dancehall musicians Shatta Wale and Stonebwoy jammed together.

While the “Ololo” hitmaker was performing his hit song dubbed “Hero” on stage, he approached Shatta Wale as they dance along.

Stonebwoy also gave Shatta Wale a hearty hug as a form of appreciation after he (Shatta) gifted his “Best Video” award to him.

It’s obvious the beef between these two ‘big’ artists is over as they always express their love for each other at almost every occasion
