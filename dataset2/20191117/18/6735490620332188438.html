Ben Affleck, Gal Gadot And More Throw Their Support Behind #ReleaseTheSnyderCut

17 Nov 2019, 18:31 by Jamie Samhan

Gal Gadot is joining “Justice League” fans in their campaign for a new cut of the film.

Fans are calling for the “Snyder Cut”, which is an alternate version of the film by writer and director Zack Snyder.

A number of stars of the movie have shown their support, including Gal Gadot who posted a black and white image of herself from the film with the caption “#ReleaseTheSnyderCut”.
