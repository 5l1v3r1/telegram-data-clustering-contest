Jharkhand Assembly Election 2019: Congress Releases List of 3 Candidates

Jharkhand is all set to go to polls in five phases, with the first one on November 30 and the last on December 20.

17 Nov 2019, 18:03

Ranchi: Ahead of Jharkhand assembly election 2019, Congress on Sunday released another list of candidates for the upcoming polls.

In this list, the names of three candidates have been announced. Candidate Sona Ram Sinku is in the fray from Jaganathpur ST seat, while Suresh Baitha will be contesting from Kanke SC seat. From Mandar seat, Sunny Toppo is in the fray.
