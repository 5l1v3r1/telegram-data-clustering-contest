Photographer Injured At Georgia-Auburn Releases Statement

17 Nov 2019, 18:21 by Tzvi Machlin

There were a lot of shocking moments in Week 12 of college football. But a Georgia photographer who got run over by Georgia RB Brian Herrien and injured was among the scariest moments.

Chamberlain Smith, an intern for the Georgia Athletic Department, was accidentally knocked unconscious when Herrien was tackled out of bounds.

Smith was taken to the hospital and later diagnosed with a concussion and bruises.

Fortunately, Smith has since recovered enough from the injury to make a statement. She took to Twitter and announced that she would be resting and recovering for the next few days. But Smith also pledged to be “back on the sidelines soon!”

“I’m so glad to be walking away with nothing more than a concussion and some bruises,” she wrote on a Twitter post. “I will be home resting and recovering for the next several days, but look forward to being back on the sidelines soon!”
