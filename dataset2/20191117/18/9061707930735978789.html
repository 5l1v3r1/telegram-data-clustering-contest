The latest on Derby County's link to Luciano Acosta and what you need to know about the Argentine

Derby County latest - All the latest news and updates on the Rams' link to D.C. United's Argentine attacking midfielder Luciano Acosta.

17 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Jamie Kemble

THE LATEST

There has been an update on the reported interest from Derby County in D.C. United midfielder Luciano Acosta .

In an interview in Argentina , Acosta claimed his former D.C. United team-mate, Derby-bound Wayne Rooney , had told him to join him at Pride Park.

Rooney joins the Rams as a player-coach in January after spending nearly two years in the United States with the MLS outfit.

Acosta has also called time on his D.C. United career, and is looking for a new club.

Fresh reports in The Athletic claim that Acosta is “waiting for the call” from Derby over a potential move, but there has been no sign from the Rams that deal could take place.

ACOSTA PROFILE

The January transfer window is fast approaching - and Derby County have been linked with a surprise move for highly-rated D.C. United attacking midfielder Luciano Acosta.

The Argentine has spoken about how soon-to-be Derby player-coach Wayne Rooney has asked him to move to Pride Park , and how he has considered the offer.

Rooney will officially join the Rams staff at the start of January and could make his debut against Barnsley on January 2.

Acosta, meanwhile, does have his sights on a return to Boca Juniors, where he began his professional career - but whether he will turn his attention back to Derby should Boca not pick up the phone remains to be seen.

ACOSTA REVEALS ROONEY CHAT

Wayne Rooney apparently urged one of his D.C. United team-mates to join him at Derby County.

Rooney has left the United States after agreeing a deal to sign for the Rams as player-coach in January.

Before departing at the end of the Major League Soccer campaign, the former England star reportedly encouraged Luciano Acosta to follow him to Pride Park.

Acosta has also called time on his D.C. United career, and is looking for a new club.

Rooney and Acosta formed a partnership known as LuchoRoo during their time in the US capital.
