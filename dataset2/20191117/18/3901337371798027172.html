Ronaldo Moves To 99 Goals As Portugal Qualify For EURO 2020

17 Nov 2019, 18:01 by SL International

EURO 2020 Qualifier

Result: Luxembourg 0-2 Portugal

Date: 17 November 2019

Venue: Stade Jose Barthel

Cristiano Ronaldo moved onto 99 internationals as European champions Portugal defeated Luxembourg 2-0 to qualify for the 2020 European Championship on Sunday.

The task was quite straightforward for the European champions, as three points would book their spot at next year's tournament regardless of other results.

Portugal struggled to deal with the pitch in the early stages, which led to a lack of clear-cut chances as Ronaldo tried his luck from distance on a few occasions in the opening 20 minutes.

The deadlock was eventually broken in the 37th minute as Bernado Silva's ball over the top found Bruno Fernandes, who smashed the ball into the back of the net.

Silva nearly grabbed a goal of his own shortly before the halftime break, but the Manchester City star was unable to capitalise from close range after some good work by the goalkeeper.

The home side came out firing in the second half, with Fernandes going closest with a curling effort from the edge of the box in the opening 15 minutes.

In an attempt to make the result safe, Portugal coach Fernando Santos threw on a double change as Joao Moutinho and Diogo Jota replaced Pizzi and Andre Silva.

However, the win was sealed with three minutes remaining in the second half when Ronaldo poked the ball into an empty net after some good work by Jota the far post.
