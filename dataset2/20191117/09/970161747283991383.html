Snoop Dogg names himself People’s sexiest man to troll John Legend

17 Nov 2019, 09:03

Snoop Dogg has stolen John Legend’s glory as People’s sexiest man alive by giving himself the title and his very own magazine cover.

John, 40, earned the most flattering accolade of his career so far when People unveiled him as their eye candy for 2019 last week, following in the footsteps of Idris Elba, Chris Hemsworth and Dwayne Johnson.

What company to be in!

Well, People have never had a rapper as their special cover star – until now, that is, with Snoop, 48, declaring himself the sexiest man alive instead of John.

Snoop, real name Calvin Broadus, shared a brilliant doctored version of John’s cover with his head replacing the Ordinary People singer’s. While John’s suit is still in place, Snoop has replaced his undone navy bow tie with a bandana pattern.

The headline reads: ‘Snoop Dogg. How a crip took your chick.’

‘Thank. You,’ Snoop simply wrote on Instagram, graciously accepting his new honour.

Beauty is in the eye of the beholder, as they say.

John admitted he was ‘excited’ and ‘a little scared’ with the ‘pressure’ of being named sexiest man alive.

He added: ‘Everyone’s going to be picking me apart to see if I’m sexy enough to hold this title. I’m [also] following Idris Elba, which is not fair and is not nice to me!’

The All Of Me singer added on Twitter: ‘1995 John would be very perplexed to be following 2018 @IdrisElba as #SexiestManAlive. Hell, 2019 John is about as equally perplexed but thank you @People for finding me sexy. I’ll take it.’

But perhaps no one was more thrilled than John’s wife Chrissy Teigen, who joked: ‘My secret is out. I have fulfilled my dream of having boned @people’s sexiest man alive!! an honor!!!!! [sic].’

The former model added to People: ‘I cannot WAIT for people to get mad about John being the sexiest man alive. it’s my new Starbucks holiday cup.’

She even changed her Twitter bio to: ‘Currently sleeping with people’s sexiest man alive.’
