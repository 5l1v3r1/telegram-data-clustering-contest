Ford v Ferrari eyes the number one spot at the North American box office

The Christian Bale and Matt Damon starrer sports drama Ford v Ferrari is set to earn approximately 29 million dollars in 3,528 screens.

17 Nov 2019, 09:04 by Entertainment Desk

Christian Bale and Matt Damon starrer sports drama Ford v Ferrari is set to win at the domestic (North American) box office. According to Variety, the James Mangold directorial will earn approximately 29 million dollars in 3,528 screens.

Penned by Jez Butterworth, John-Henry Butterworth, and Jason Keller, Ford v Ferrari is about Carroll Shelby, an automotive designer who was given the task to build a car for Ford capable of defeating the reigning champion Ferrari at 1966 24 Hours of Le Mans, world’s oldest active sports car endurance race. This was supposed to be unthinkable back then.

Christian Bale dons the role of the crotchety Brit race car driver Ken Miles who was Shelby’s choice for testing the car.
