Razzmatazz: Entertainment bigwigs shut down Dubai

17 Nov 2019, 09:22 by Mobola Sadiq

Mobola Sadiq and Toluwalope Kareem

The bigwigs of the entertainment industry assembled at Dubai, United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, memorable tourist attractions and a lively nightlife scene.

Popular media personalities and new entries in the entertainment industry were not left out too as they all registered their presence at the annual One Africa Music Festival.

Since the international music scene has realised that Nigerian artistes are also setting new trends and breaking records, the festival witnessed a huge turn-up from fans.

Not only did Dubai dominate Twitter sphere, music enthusiasts’ who couldn’t turn up for the festival had a field day digesting all that went down from their mobile gadgets.

There were stunning performances from Davido, Teni, Tekno, Burna Boy, D’banj, DJ Spinall, Zlatan Ibile, among others.

One of the highlights of the Dubai outing was the public display of affection by Tiwa Savage and Wizkid. The celebrity pals rocked each other on stage while performing their hit collaboration titled “Malo.”

In the viral video Wizkid at some point grabbed her butt which elicited wide excitement from their star struck fans.

Asides the razzmatazz, some celebrities like Davido took his “30BG” crew on shopping spree in Dubai and posted the photo on his Instagram page.

Controversial actress, Tonto Dikeh, and popular cross dresser, Idris Okuneye better known as Bobrisky, also claimed they lavished money buying designer wears and living the “la vida loca” lifestyle.

One thing noteworthy from the #Dubai trip is that Nigerians don’t do things in small measure they’ll rather go the extra mile to stand out.
