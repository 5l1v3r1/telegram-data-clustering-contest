Cat of the Week: Streak

17 Nov 2019, 09:00

Source: Spark Rescue

Available Now: Streak is an incredibly sweet 12 week old male kitten, he was picked up in Riverside as a stray and dropped off at a vets office. When the owner was contacted to pick up Streak he said that he had no place to keep him and would release him in the bushes! We were contacted about this boy and arranged transport to pick him up.

Streak came to Spark Rescue a 3 weeks ago, he has one of the most loving personalities we have met and he gets along great with other cats too!

Streak is now fully vetted and ready for a home to call his own!

If you are interested in adopting this adorable boy please visit our website and submit a cat adoption application at www.sparkrescue.org. Thank you for choosing to adopt
