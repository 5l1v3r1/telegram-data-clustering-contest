Shah Rukh Khan meets Dua Lipa, says she's 'beautiful and charming'

17 Nov 2019, 09:08 by Entertainment Desk

Popular British singer Dua Lipa has made social media swoon after posting some sari pics on Instagram after landing in Mumbai.

However, another thing she wanted to add to her list was meeting Bollywood icon Shah Rukh Khan. The actor took to Twitter to welcome Lipa in India and wish her luck for her concert with Katy Perry on Saturday night.

“Have decided to live by ‘New Rules’ and who better to learn them from but Dua Lipa herself! What a charming and beautiful young lady, and her voice,” Khan wrote on his tweet.”Wish her all my love for the concert tonight. Dua if you can, try the steps I taught u on stage.”
