Salman Khan’s ‘Dabangg 3’ to have the widest Bollywood film release?

17 Nov 2019, 09:01 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Salman Khan, who is the box office king with 14 films that have crossed the 100-crore mark, will be ready to check in another record with the release of ‘Dabangg 3’.

The cop-drama that also stars Sonakshi Sinha and Kichcha Sudeepa will reportedly have the widest ever release in Bollywood. According to reports doing the rounds, The Prabhudheva directorial will release actress 5400 screens.

The third instalment in the blockbuster franchise will reportedly be only the second Bollywood film to try out this marketing strategy and have such a wide release. Not just that, the film will release in four different languages - Hindi, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada.

While the distributors are reportedly going all out to get the maximum screens in multiplex chains, Salman’s team is working on ensuring the film reaches small towns as well.

Taking a cue from the Hollywood release ‘Avengers: Endgame’, the makers are also reportedly making sure that the film has early morning shows that start by 9.30 am.
