Spoilers: Shock romance for Karen and Billy in EastEnders?

17 Nov 2019, 09:00

No matter what obstacle is thrown her way, Karen Taylor (Lorraine Stanley) always manages to get by in EastEnders, but lately she’s been struggling financially, and thus has been borrowing money from Ben (Max Bowden) and Tubbs’ money-lending operation, and she struggles to meet the re-payments in the coming episodes — which is where Billy Mitchell (Perry Fenwick) comes in.

With financial struggles aplenty, Karen borrows once more from Ben and Tubbs. Ben orders his mate to let Karen off on another payment — but Tubbs ignores such a request and demands that Karen pay up.

Later, Karen discusses the matter with Billy, and he suggests that — in addition to her job at the launderette — perhaps she could get a second job to help with the repayments. Never one to miss an opportunity, Karen uses Billy’s words against him and ends up securing herself a job at E20.

Naturally, Billy is rather worried about how Karen will cope but, as the night of her trial shift arrives, Karen delivers — and Billy is shocked. As a result, he begins to see her in a whole new light.
