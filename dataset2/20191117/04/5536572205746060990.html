Eric Reid, Stephen A. Smith Get Into Massive Twitter Argument Over Colin Kaepernick Criticism (TWEETS)

17 Nov 2019, 04:37 by Darrelle Lincoln

Carolina Panthers safety Eric Reid and ESPN personality Stephen A. Smith got into a huge argument on Twitter that followed Saturday’s workout for former San Francisco 49ers Colin Kaepernick.

It all started when Reid posted a video of a tap dancing duck and called out Smith for doing what the NFL tells him to do after Smith posted a video about how Kaepernick doesn’t want to play football.

“You don’t want to work, you just want to make noise,” Smith said. “It’s over — Colin Kaepernick’s aspiration in the NFL.”
