Week 11 Fantasy FLEX rankings

17 Nov 2019, 04:26 by Billy Heyen

The Week 11 fantasy FLEX rankings are a bit backward. Normally, the bottom is overloaded with wide receivers, but that's not quite the case this week. There are a ton of running backs with tricky matchups, along with injuries at the position. On the flip side, tons of WRs have good matchups. That means that the wideouts rank higher than normal, while some backs and tight ends have to fill in the bottom of the rankings.

What does that mean? Even moreso than normal, it's probably a good idea to rely on a wide receiver in your FLEX spot. Of course, we went to the trouble of ordering 120 guys so that you don't even have to think that much, but it's definitely a better week for receivers than it is for RBs.

WEEK 11 NON-PPR RANKINGS:
Quarterback | Running back | Wide receiver | Tight end | D/ST | Kicker

We actually haven't put as many tight ends in the top-120 FLEX rankings in any other week as we did for Week 11. For the first time, that's us suggesting that your best option at FLEX might be a tight end, even one available on some waiver wires like Darren Fells. It just feels better than a remarkably low-upside RB play like Peyton Barber.

It's probably best just to let you get right into it. Be aware that the Giants, Packers, Seahawks and Titans are on bye, so those players won't make up this list and you'll have to replace them in your lineups. Best of luck!
