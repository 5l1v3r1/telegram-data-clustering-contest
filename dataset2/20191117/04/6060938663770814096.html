Photos: Katy Perry thanks Karan Johar for all the love as she jets out of Mumbai city

17 Nov 2019, 04:55 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

American singer Katy Perry thanked Bollywood filmmaker Karan Johar for the "warm welcome" and a grand party he hosted as she jetted out of the city early Sunday morning.

Taking to her social media handle, Katy shared a photo of herself hugging it out with KJo and she captioned the photo saying, “Thanks for the warm welcomes."

The filmmaker hosted a grand bash for the Pop diva and called over his Bollywood gang including Alia Bhatt, Jacqueline Fernandez, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Abhishek Bachchan, Kajol, Malaika Arora, Ananya Panday, Kunal Kemmu and many others to party the night away. While several stars had already posted pictures with her on the same night, Perry too shared a glamorous click from the party and captioned it saying "Bollywood babes."

The 'Roar' crooner who was in India to perform at a music festival on Saturday night, enthralled fans with her power-packed and “sweaty” performance. She shared the stage with Grammy Award-winning star Dua Lipa, who also performed her biggest hits. This was the first concert for both ladies in the city.

On her way out of the city, Katy was seen in the same grey sweat pants and jacket she wore on her arrival into the city. Pulling her own bag, the singer smiled and waved to the shutterbugs as she bid adieu to the city.
