Jalen Hurts’ Reaction To Oklahoma’s Win At Baylor Is Going Viral

17 Nov 2019, 04:15 by Andrew Holleran

Oklahoma rallied from a 21-point halftime deficit at Baylor on Saturday night to pull off a crazy comeback victory and remain in the College Football Playoff race.

The Sooners, who trailed 31-10 at halftime, pulled off a wild comeback in the second half. Oklahoma ultimately took the lead with less than two minutes remaining.

Lincoln Riley’s team pulled off a 34-31 victory on a game-winning field goal. The win was sealed when the Sooners picked off a Charlie Brewer pass.
