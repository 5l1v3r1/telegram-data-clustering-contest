Jalen Hurts Had A Great Postgame Quote After Oklahoma’s Comeback

17 Nov 2019, 04:44 by Dan Lyons

Jalen Hurts had his worst game as Oklahoma’s quarterback, and did some serious damage to his Heisman Trophy chances tonight. Ultimately, he and the Sooners found a way to win tonight’s game at previously undefeated Baylor.

Hurts had a pair of fumbles and an interception on the day, helping Baylor jump out to a 28-3 lead in the second quarter. Any good football fan knows that particular lead isn’t a safe one, though.

The Sooners stormed back, outscoring Baylor 24-0 in the second half. Gabe Brkic hit a go-ahead field goal with 1:45 left in the game, and an interception of Charlie Brewer by Nik Bonitto sealed a 34-31 win.

Jalen Hurts was so nervous that he couldn’t bear to watch the end of the game. After he was flagged down by Maria Taylor for a post-game interview, he owned up to his in-game mistakes, but he was mostly happy with the team effort that allowed Oklahoma to come back and win the game.
