FiveThirtyEight’s Updated Playoff Prediction After Oklahoma’s Win

17 Nov 2019, 04:50 by Andrew Holleran

FiveThirtyEight’s prediction for the College Football Playoff has updated following Oklahoma’s win over previously-undefeated Baylor in Waco on Saturday night.

Week 12 has now seen two undefeated teams go down, as Minnesota lost to Iowa and the Bears fell to the Sooners.

The computer projections have updated as a result.

Here’s the latest top four, from FiveThirtyEight:

  1. Clemson
  2. LSU
  3. Ohio State
  4. Georgia

Oklahoma is now the first team out at No. 5, given a 35 percent chance. Oregon is next up at 32 percent, followed by Utah (27 percent), Alabama (13 percent) and Penn State (13 percent).

The new College Football Playoff rankings will be released on Tuesday night.

We’ll have a much better idea of the playoff picture then.
