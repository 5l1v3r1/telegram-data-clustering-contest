Video: Jalen Hurts, Maria Taylor Share Funny Moment After Win

17 Nov 2019, 04:28 by Andrew McCarty

On Saturday night, the biggest game of the weekend – at least for the Big 12 – kicked off from Waco as No. 13 Baylor hosted No. 10 Oklahoma.

The Bears jumped out to a 21-point halftime lead, but saw that lead evaporate over the final two quarters of play.

The Sooners, who trailed 31-10 at halftime, pulled off one of the best comebacks of the season en route to a 34-31 win. Lincoln Riley’s squad might not have the best defense in the country, but as long as the team has star quarterback Jalen Hurts, it has a chance.

Hurts called his own number to the tune of 27 carries and 42 pass attempts. He racked up over 400 yards of total offense and four touchdowns.

Baylor couldn’t stop the Alabama transfer, but there was one person who could.

ESPN reporter Maria Taylor made sure Hurts didn’t escape her grasp for a post-game interview.
