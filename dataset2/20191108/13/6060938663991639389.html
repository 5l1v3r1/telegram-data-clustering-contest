‘Chehre’: Amitabh Bachchan averts clash with son Abhishek Bachchan as his thriller gets new April 2020 release date

08 Nov 2019, 13:02 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

‘Chehre’, starring Amitabh Bachchan and Emraan Hashmi has averted its box office clash by postponing its release date to 24th April 2020. The much-awaited Rumi Jaffery mystery-thriller was initially set to lock horns with Vicky Kaushal's horror film 'Bhoot Part One: The Haunted Ship’ and Anurag Basu's yet-to-be-titled film which stars Abhishek Bachchan and Aditya Roy Kapur in lead roles.

This move has ensured that the film enjoys an undisputed run at the ticket windows. The other films slated to release in April 2020 include Ranveer Singh's '83 and the Janhvi Kapoor and Rajkummar Rao starrer 'Roohi Afza'.

According to reports, ‘Chehre’ is said to be a thriller that allegedly plays out in a bungalow in Shimla. The film has an intriguing story with Bachchan playing a lawyer and Emraan playing a business tycoon.

The film also stars Rhea Chakraborty, Siddhanth Kapoor, Dritman Chakroborthy, Raghubir Yadav and Annu Kapoor.
