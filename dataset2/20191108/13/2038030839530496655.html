Everything you need to know about the third annual Joburg Film Festival

08 Nov 2019, 13:34

Cape Town – The third annual Joburg Festival will kick off its six-day celebration from 19 - 24 November.

The South African horror film, 8, will premiere at the festival hub on Nelson Mandela Square in Sandton.

Starring Tshamano Sebe, Inge Beckmann, Garth Breytenbach and Chris April - it centres on an old man, fated to collect souls for eternity, who then seeks atonement after trading his daughter's soul.
