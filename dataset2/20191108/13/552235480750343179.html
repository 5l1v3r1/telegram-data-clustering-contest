Early bird offer on M&S Christmas food orders ends this weekend

You only have until Sunday to place your Christmas and New Year food order at Marks and Spencer to receive a fabulous free gift

08 Nov 2019, 13:02 by Linda Howard

Marks & Spencer recently revealed the festive delights they'll be serving up this December – and they're aiming to make it as stress-free as possible by catering for everyone.

From vegans and veggies, to those with dietary requirements and hosts who need some help with preparing the perfect turkey, M&S has got you covered.

The M&S Food Christmas lines are available to order online at marksandspencer.com with selected lines also on sale in stores nationwide.

nd if you spend £85 or more on Christmas Food to Order before Sunday, November 10, M&S will treat you to a free bottle of wine or box of chocolates.

This year marks the first full vegan dinner options available for customers including a tasty looking vegan root vegetable Tarte Tatin and Plant kitchen no pork pigs in blankets.

There are also plenty of gluten-free options too, with an extensive range of mince pies and party food products for shoppers to choose from.

For meat-lovers, M&S has spent a whole year coming up with foolproof perfect turkey (£60), so shoppers can avoid a stress-filled Christmas morning.

If you're looking for fast cooking and equally impressive dishes to show off on the big day, M&S has also got that covered too.

M&S have launched a variety of new products that tick both boxes and the selection includes a tear and share Brie En Croute made with an all-butter puff pastry and Sticky Toffee Trifle - the ultimate hybrid dessert everyone will love.

The Clementine Gin Liqueur Snow Globe is a tasty tipple with a festive flavour and comes complete with edible 23 carat gold leaf pieces.

And it gets even better - when the bottle is turned, the pieces float, creating a snow globe effect.

The drink will make for an amazing gift or as a showstopping centre piece.

 

Marks and Spencer's Clementine Gin Liqueur Snow Globe costs £15 and is avaialble in stores and online now.
