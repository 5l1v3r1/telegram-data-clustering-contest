Free Nationals announce guest-filled debut LP, share song ft. Syd

08 Nov 2019, 13:59 by Andrew Sacher

Anderson .Paak’s backing band The Free Nationals have been rolling out guest-filled singles for about a year now — including “Beauty & Essex” (ft. Daniel Caesar & Unknown Mortal Orchestra), “Time” (ft. Mac Miller & Kali Uchis), “On Sight” (ft. J.I.D., Kadhja Bonet & MiKNNA), and “Eternal Light” (ft. Chronnix) — and now they’ve shared another new single featuring Syd of The Internet, and revealed that those five songs and eight others will make up their self-titled debut album, which is due December 13 via via OBE, LLC / EMPIRE. Other guests on the album include Anderson .Paak, T.I., Conway, Westside Gunn, and more. Check out the new song and the full tracklist below.

Anderson .Paak and The Free Nationals also have some upcoming shows, including a sold-out benefit show in Brooklyn this month. Syd also just put out a new single with SebastiAn off his new album that came out today.
