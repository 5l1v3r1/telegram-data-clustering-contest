RuPaul's Drag Race Werq the World Tour is set to arrive in the UK next spring and tickets go on sale today.

The tour ill see famous drag queens from past and present seasons, including US season 11 winner Yvie Oddly, grace the stage at shows in major cities like London, Manchester and Birmingham.

The line up also includes main judge and current Strictly contestant Michelle Visage .

Tickets for the shows go on sale at 10am today (Friday 8 November) from Ticketmaster , AXS and theticketfactory.com .

Fans of the UK version of RuPaul's Drag Race will also be able to see the queens from the debut season at the Troxy in London this winter with Voss Events.

RuPaul will also bring his DragCon to the UK for the first time ever for a weekend of drag performances, meet and greets and more at the Olympia in London on January 18-19.
