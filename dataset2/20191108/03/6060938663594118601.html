Not a nurse but Anushka Sharma to play a glamorous teacher in Farah Khan's 'Satte Pe Satta' remake?

08 Nov 2019, 03:35 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The buzz about Farah Khan making a remake of a cult classic film, 'Satte Pe Satta' has already got everyone by surprise. Fans of the original film, 'Satte Pe Satta' are waiting for this film with bated breath and why not? the remake will see a fresh pairing in Bollywood with Hrithik Roshan and Anushka Sharma stepping into Amitabh Bachchan and Hema Malini's shoe. Today, we came across yet another interesting update about Anushka's character in the film.

According to a media report, while the original film featured Hema Malini playing a nurse's character, director Farah Khan has taken some liberties and have changed the profession of the leading actress from a nurse to a teacher. Accordingly, now Anushka will play a teacher. Moreover, Farah is taking inspiration from her own directorial debut film 'Main Hoon Naa'. She will showcase Anushka as a glamourous teacher similar to the lines of Sushmita's character Miss Chandni from 'MHN'. However, instead of sarees, Anushka will flaunt short jackets and summer dresses. Wow! sounds interesting.

It was also reported that the celebrated role of Ranjeeta's character will be portrayed by Bollywood's dimpled girl Preity Zinta. With this Hrithik and Preity will collaborate for a film after 'Mission Kashmir' and 'Lakshya'.

With all such interesting updates flowing in, we are already excited to see this Hrithik and Anushka's 'Satte Pe Satta' remake.
