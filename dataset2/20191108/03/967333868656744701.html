Trump Accuses NY Attorney General of 'Mischaracterizing' Settlement Over Foundation's Donations

08 Nov 2019, 03:14

The New York Supreme Court has ordered US President Donald Trump to pay a $2 million penalty for misusing funds from his charitable foundation for his own interests, including during the 2016 presidential campaign.

Trump said Thursday that New York Attorney General "is deliberately mischaracterizing this settlement for political purposes".
