OnePlus launched the OnePlus 7, 7 Pro and 7 Pro 5G back in May 2019, and all three phones are now available to buy.

Though since supplanted by the OnePlus 7T, the OnePlus 7 is still worth considering if you can find it, especially since it's now cheaper than ever.
