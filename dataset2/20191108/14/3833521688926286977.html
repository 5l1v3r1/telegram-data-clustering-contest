TSX edges higher while U.S. markets mixed at the open

08 Nov 2019, 14:53 by BNN Bloomberg

Canada’s main stock index open slightly higher Friday despite a report showing a surprise decline in jobs for the month of October.

At 9:35 a.m. ET, the Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX Composite Index was down 1.00 points, or 0.01 per cent, at 16,680.92. However, the Canadian dollar was trading slightly lower at 75.55 cents US, compared with Thursday's average of 75.90 cents US.

Meanwhile, U.S. stocks were mixed. The Dow Jones Industrial Average began down 5.41 points, or 0.02 per cent, at 27,669.89. The S&P 500 opened 3.08 points lower, or 0.10 per cent, at 3,081.10.  The Nasdaq Composite began down 17.30 points, or 0.21 per cent, to 8,417.21.
