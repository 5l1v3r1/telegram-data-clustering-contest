Twitter PWA soon to have a Reddit-like conversation tree feature

08 Nov 2019, 14:45 by Raka

In an effort to make it easier for users to keep track of conversations easily, the social media giant, Twitter is working on a Reddit-like conversation tree.

The company is currently testing the feature for its Twitter Web App, meaning that Twitter could roll out the feature to those who are using Twitter Lite, Twitter PWA and Twitter Web. Interestingly, the concept first appeared on its experimental Twitter iOS app, according to famous reverse engineer Wong. That means we could also see the feature in action in the Twitter app for Android and iOS in the coming days.
