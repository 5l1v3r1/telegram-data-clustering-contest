Tom Phillips from The Pools provides the betting preview and all you need to know about the YouTube feud and boxing match

The world of YouTube collides with the professional world of boxing after less than a handful of bouts of white-collar boxing.

YouTubers KSI - who made his name making FIFA videos - and Logan Paul - who initially made his name on Vine - fight for the first time on the professional scene.

The amateur bout last year turned a few heads, noticeably promoter Eddie Hearn who was amazed by the views and pay-per-view numbers it received.

Hearn was asked to promote the amateur bout last year but declined before seeing the fight drew 28million views and nearly £3million from ticket sales alone.

It is safe to say Eddie Hearn has delivered his promise in bringing a world championship title fight to the night too.

How did we get here?

It all started when KSI fought fellow YouTuber Joe Weller at the Copper Box Arena last year.

The fight was a success with 1.6 million live viewers and 21 million viewers with a day.

KSI called out the Paul brothers and initially challenged Logan’s younger brother Jake to fight before Logan stepped up.

Jake later agreed to fight Deji in a co-main event ticket.

Deji will not be fighting in the next fight - but that’s a whole other story worthy for EastEnders or Coronation Street or something.

How did the first fight go?

Well if the KSI-Weller fight was a success, KSI vs Logan Paul was huge!

The pre-fight promotional videos reached nearly 30million views alone including diss tracks reaching a combined 60 million from each of the four fighters - yes really.

The live viewership was over 2.25million including more than a million pay-per-view alone and 17million alone from the official YouTube channel.

During the promo for the current fight, Eddie Hearn admitted he was approached to promote the first event but said no.

He later said the views and pay-per-view numbers convinced him to promote the second fight in the USA.

Who is favourite to win this fight?

Despite having a majority draw in his favour, KSI is 8/5 to win while Logan Paul is the favourite at 8/13 with The Pools .

Will the fight go the distance?

Like the previous fight, KSI and Logan Paul will fight in a six-round match at three minutes long each.

They will be fighting at cruiserweight but, as it is pro, they will be fighting 10oz gloves.

Hearn has previously stated “someone will get knocked out” so I’m agreeing with him and saying it won’t go the distance ( 8/15 ).

How will it end?

The demand in an amateur bout is nothing when you compare it to a professional fight. Granted the fact they are considered pro’s sickens the purists out there, but they are when they enter that ring.

The lack of headguards will be something both fighters will be nervous about - whether their little YouTube personalities say otherwise.

Logan Paul had shocking stamina and couldn’t even hold his gloves up after two rounds and I’ll be surprised if he can last four rounds to be honest.

I’m backing KSI to win by TKO ( 7/2 ) in the fourth round ( 20/1 ).
