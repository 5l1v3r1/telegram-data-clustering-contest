It’s the First Week of November, and the Jonas Brothers Have Already Released a Christmas Song

08 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Justin Curto, Zoe Haylock

We hear you: Christmas sneaks up on us earlier and earlier every year. This year, it seemed like once the clock struck midnight on November 1, everyone was eager to trade their costumes for sweaters (except for the few weirdos who kept up the Halloween antics through the weekend). And the Jonas Brothers, inspired by Queen Christmas Mariah Carey’s own transformation, are no exception: They just released their original Christmas song “Like It’s Christmas.” It’s another stage of their reunion, after the boys already gave us new album Happiness Begins, a documentary, and a tour earlier this year. (Just don’t expect to find their forthcoming memoir under the tree — it’s now out next March.) With all this comeback success, every day feels “like it’s Christmas” for the brothers, even if it’s 82 degrees and sunny out.
