Chelsea v Crystal Palace: match preview

08 Nov 2019, 14:51 by Guardian Staff

Stamford Bridge has not been a fortress for Chelsea this season. They have won three of their nine home games in all competitions and they will miss the suspended Jorginho against Crystal Palace, who have picked up impressive results at West Ham, Manchester United and Arsenal.
