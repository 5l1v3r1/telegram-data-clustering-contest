Cricket: Balbirnie to succeed long-serving Porterfield as Ireland captain

08 Nov 2019, 21:00

DUBLIN: William Porterfield has stepped down as Ireland's test and one-day captain after more than 11 years at the helm with Andrew Balbirnie succeeding him, Cricket Ireland said on Friday.

Since taking over from former skipper Trent Johnston in 2008, Porterfield has led the senior team 253 times, including when Ireland debuted as a test nation in May last year.
