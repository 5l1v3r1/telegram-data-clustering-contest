New details about the display of the OnePlus 8 Pro leaked online

08 Nov 2019, 09:30 by Raka

OnePlus’s upcoming flagship smartphone dubbed OnePlus 8 Pro will feature a 120Hz display, according to famous leakster Max J. On the other hand, the OnePlus 8 is likely to feature a display similar to the OnePlus 7 Pro, meaning that the former is going to settle on a 90Hz display.
