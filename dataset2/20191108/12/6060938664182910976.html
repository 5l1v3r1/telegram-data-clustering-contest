Watch: Riteish Deshmukh transforms into a 3-foot dwarf for Sidharth Malhotra starrer 'Marjaavaan'

08 Nov 2019, 12:40 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Bollywood is all set to witness a high-octane face-off between the 'Ek Villain' duo Sidharth Malhotra and Riteish Deshmukh in the upcoming romantic drama 'Marjaavaan.' The film will see both the actors uniting after the 2014 film. Tara Sutaria and Rakul Preet Singh will play the female leads in the film.

For the first time, Riteish Deshmukh will be seen playing a dwarf antagonist. The versatile actor turns into the 3-foot tall scoundrel character Vishnu. The trailer of the film gives us a glimpse into the never seen before avatar of the actor which looks very cruel and terrifying. The makers of the film have revealed a video which shows the transformation of Riteish into Vishnu.
