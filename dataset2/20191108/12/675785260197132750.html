Mothercare launches huge 90% off clearance sale - and here are our top picks

The collapsed retailer is promising huge reductions on baby clothes, prams, car seats and toys - but only while stocks last as it clears out warehouses and shelves across the country

08 Nov 2019, 12:14 by Emma Munbodh, https://www.facebook.com/EmmaMunbodh

It's the end of the road for Mothercare after the company announced plans to close all of its remaining branches on Tuesday, on the back of its collapse.

The company has now gone into administration, 2,800 jobs are about to be axed, and it's no longer accepting certain gifts cards or returns.

And on Friday, three days after officially folding, the chain launched a big closing down sale in stores and online, in efforts to recoup as much money as possible, as part of the final audit.

It's promising big discounts, with baby clothes, prams, car seats and toys all heavily reduced as part of the 'while stocks last' clearance.

But are the deals any good - or can we expect another disappointing Toys R Us closing down sale, which saw just 10% slashed off products which were later sold to another major retailer?
