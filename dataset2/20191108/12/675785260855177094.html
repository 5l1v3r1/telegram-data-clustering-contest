BREAKING BBC confirms general election TV debates - including Corbyn clash with Johnson

Jeremy Corbyn and Boris Johnson will face off in a two-way clash on Friday 6 December

08 Nov 2019, 12:38 by Dan Bloom, https://www.facebook.com/mirrordanbloom

The BBC has confirmed a string of TV debates for the general election - including a showdown clash between Jeremy Corbyn and Boris Johnson .

Chiefs unveiled the schedule today after ITV and Sky News confirmed TV debates in the countdown to the December 12 poll.

The ITV debate on November 19 - between just Mr Corbyn and Mr Johnson - had enraged Lib Dems whose leader Jo Swinson accused the network of sexism.

But the BBC has also opted for a two-way debate between the PM and Leader of the Opposition. It will be held on Friday 6 December and come live from Southampton.

As well as that event, there will be a seven-way podium debate between leaders of the seven biggest parties on Friday 29 November.
