Revealed: The surprising Aston Villa captaincy decision

Aston Villa latest news | Claret and blue midfielder Marvelous Nakamba reportedly turned down the captaincy of the Zimbabwe national side according to reports in Africa

08 Nov 2019, 12:08 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa midfielder Marvelous Nakamba reportedly turned down the captaincy of Zimbabwe.

According to reports in Zimbabwean media, Warriors general manager Wellington Mpandare offered the 25-year-old the captain's armband but he didn't want it.

The summer signing from Club Brugge has been in fine form for Villa , playing in every Premier League game since his debut in September's goalless draw with West Ham United at Villa Park.

Nakamba was exceptional again in Saturday's 2-1 defeat to Liverpool and he has made that defensive midfield position his own.
