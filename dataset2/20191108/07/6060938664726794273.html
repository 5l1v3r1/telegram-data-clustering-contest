'Housefull 4' box office collection Week 2: The Akshay Kumar starrer records the third-best second week of the year

08 Nov 2019, 07:31 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Unable to cease from its successful run at the box office, Akshay Kumar starrer comedy-drama 'Housefull 4' has witnessed yet another exceptional week at the box office. The film which banked on the Diwali holiday week has witnessed record-breaking collections, especially at mass centers. The Farhad Samji directorial also held strongly in its second week, despite coming off a huge holiday week.

Housefull 4 Movie Review : Not so full of comedy

As per the latest report on the Boxofficeindia.com, on Thursday, the film has raked in Rs 3.50 crore on its fourteenth day at the box office. The second-week collection of the film stands at a total of Rs 48.75 crore. The second week saw a drop of 64% coming from the huge Diwali holiday week. The film has managed to collect a total of Rs 184.61 crore in two weeks at the box office.

'Housefull 4' has recorded the third-best second-week collections of the year after 'Kabir Singh' and 'Uri - The Surgical Strike.' The second numbers are also among the top 15 second weeks of all time.

'Housefull 4' looks sure to emerge as the highest grosser of Akshay Kumar and will probably achieve the feat in its third weekend. However, the film will face competition with Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Bala' from this week.

Directed by Farhad Samji, ‘Housefull 4’ boasts of an ensemble star cast that includes, Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde in the lead roles. The film is the fourth installment of the hit comedy franchise. The film hits the screens on October 25 and clashed at the box office with Rajkummar Rao's 'Made In China' and Taapsee Pannu-Bhumi Pednekar's 'Saand Ki Aankh'.
