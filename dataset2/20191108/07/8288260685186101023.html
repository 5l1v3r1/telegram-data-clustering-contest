'We know' - Liverpool star fires brutal warning at Manchester City

Aston Villa latest news | Jurgen Klopp's side turn their attentions to Pep Guardiola's back-to-back Premier League champions after dispatching of Dean Smith's Villans

08 Nov 2019, 07:32 by Richard Cusack

Liverpool midfielder Georginio Wijnaldum has fired a warning at Manchester City.

The two sides clash on Sunday (4.30pm) with Wijnaldum's side six points ahead of the reigning Premier League champions in the table after 11 matches.

Jurgen Klopp's Reds are gunning for the title and kept up their unbeaten run with a 2-1 win over Aston Villa at Villa Park on Saturday.

They showed their mettle by erasing a 1-0 deficit with just three minutes of normal time to play, Andy Robertson crashing home a header before Sadio Mane made sure of the win four minutes into injury time.

"We just create that thing that we know we always can turn things around," he told the Daily Express .
