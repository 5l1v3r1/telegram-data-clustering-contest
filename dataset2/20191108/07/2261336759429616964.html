UFC Moscow weigh in results and video: Zabit 146 lbs, Kattar 146 lbs

08 Nov 2019, 07:21 by Anton Tabuena

UFC is heading to Moscow, Russia with a card headlined by featherweights Zabit Magomedsharipov and Calvin Kattar. The fighters have tipped the scales, with the two main event fighters both coming in at 146 lbs.

As for the co-main event, Greg Hardy came right at the heavyweight limit of 265 lbs, while his opponent, Alexander Volkov weighed in at 251 lbs.

The rest of the fighters on the card made weight, and you can check out the full weigh-in results below. The ceremonial weigh ins will happen at 10 a.m. ET.
