'Bigil' box-office worldwide collection: The Thalapathy Vijay starrer all set to enter Rs 300 crore club

08 Nov 2019, 07:48 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Vijay starrer 'Bigil' has already grossed more than Rs 270 crores worldwide and is set to emerge as the highest grosser of Vijay by entering into Rs 300 crore club. The film is having many firsts, it was the first to be Tamil film to be showcased on IMAX screens, it was the first Tamil movie to release in Egypt and now it will be the first Tamil film to release in Jordan too.
Bigil Movie Review : Thalapathy Vijay starrer is an engaging entertainer

'Bigil' has already earned more than Rajinikanth's 'Petta' and Ajith's 'Viswasam' to become the highest-grossing Tamil film of the year 2019. 'Bigil' also featured Nayanthara, Jackie Shroff, Kathir, Vivekh, Yogi Babu, Indhuja and others in key roles. The film directed by Atlee is bankrolled by AGS Entertainment.
