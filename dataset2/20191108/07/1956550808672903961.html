Park Hyatt Dubai launches UAE residents’ hotel stay deal

Save money at this Deira staycation deal

08 Nov 2019, 07:11 by Amy Mathieson

Always wanted to stay at Park Hyatt Dubai? Now’s your chance as the longstanding Dubai Creek hotel has a special deal for UAE residents.

If you book to stay at the popular hotel before Sunday December 15 you’ll get ten percent off room rates, plus 20 percent off at Amara Spa and on the hotel’s restaurants.

The offer is available now, and runs on stays at Park Hyatt Dubai until May 31, 2020, so get booking fast to secure your 2020 trip.

Restaurants at the hotel include New England-inspired seafood spot NOÉPE, Mediterranean restaurant Brasserie du Park and top Thai place, The Thai Kitchen. And if you stay at weekends there’s the hotel’s famous Traiteur brunch – Time Out Dubai’s Best Brunch 2019.

Hotel guests also get access to the hotel’s massive infinity lagoon pool – which will normally set you back Dhs360 on weekdays and Dhs450 on weekends.
