Satwik-Chirag march into China Open semis

08 Nov 2019, 07:14 by M Ratnakar

Highlights

  • The Indians defeated former world champions 21-19, 21-15.
  • The World No. 9 Indian pair will now face top-ranked Indonesian pair of Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo and Marcus Fernaldi Gideon.
  • Satwik and Chirag are the lone Indians left in the fray in the ongoing tournament.

FUZHOU: Satwiksairaj Rankireddy and Chirag Shetty continued their fine run at China Open BWF Super-750 Tournament. On Friday, the Indians dumped the 2018 world champions Li Jun Hui and Liu Yu Chen in straight games 21-19, 21-15 in front of their home crowd in Fuzhou.

This is the second successive victory for the Indians against the 2018 world champions. The young Indian duo dominated the local shuttlers in the 43-minute quarter-final clash. In the semifinals on Saturday, world No.9 team will square up against world No.1 pair of Marcus Fernaldi Gideon and Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo of Indonesia, who ousted Germans Mark Lamsfuss and Marvin Seidel 21-14, 21-19 in the other last eight clash. Satwik-Chirag lost to the top seeds in the finals of the French Open last month. The Indonesians enjoy a 7-0 record against the Indians.
