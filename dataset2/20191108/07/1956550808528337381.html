New Boston-themed restaurant launches kids' party packages

O'Learys in Deira has some top deals

08 Nov 2019, 07:11

O'Learys at Hilton Dubai Creek has introduced cool birthday party packages for kids.

Can't face the thought of hosting 20 six-year-olds in your back garden? Hand the reins over to the team at O'Learys and sit back and watch from a safe distance - perhaps with a glass of grape to ease the stress.

The new Boston-themed restaurant and bar that serves up a range of all-American food and drinks is going one step further than decent food and is offering to host your child's birthday party. What parent could say no to that?

A dedicated party zone is the perfect place to let them unleash their excitement with the restaurant's very smiley mascot, Larry.

The friendly fox will be on hand (or should that be paw?) to entertain the little party animals as they enjoy the activities on offer.

There are three packages to choose from starting from Dhs50 per child, with a minimum of 20 needed to make a booking.

Each package includes party invitations, decorations, colouring mats and crayons, birthday hats, a kid's meal and an appearance from Larry.

The premium package also includes a party host (just what every hassle-free birthday party needs) who organises the games, party bags for all the little guests, a face-painter and, because no party is complete without one, a birthday cake.
