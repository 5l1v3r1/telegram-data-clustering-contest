'Quite serious' - Slovan Bratislava player sustains horrible injury against Wolves

Wolverhampton Wanderers 1-0 Slovan Bratislava reaction | Wolves' important Europa League win at Molineux was marred somewhat by a serious injury to Kenan Bajric in the second half of the clash

08 Nov 2019, 07:00 by Alex Dicken, https://www.facebook.com/alexdickenjournalist/?fref=ts

Slovan Bratislava defender Kenan Bajric spent last night in Wolverhampton after suffering a horrible injury at Molineux.

Bajric, 24, was rushed to hospital after being struck on the head by Raul Jimenez’s attempted overhead kick during Wolves’ 1-0 Europa League win at Molineux.

There was immediate concern from Jimenez and several of Bajric’s teammates after he hit the deck, with goalkeeper Dominik Greif racing to put him in the recovery position.

 

After lengthy treatment on the field from medics Bajric came round before being carried off on a stretcher and taken to hospital for further tests.

Slovan manager Jan Kozak said: “At the moment it looks like it’s a head injury and quite serious. He’s not going home with us, he will stay here overnight to be observed.”

 

The victory puts Wolves on the brink of qualification from Group K - but thoughts inside Molineux quickly turned to Bajric once the match had finished.

“I just spoke with the staff to wish him all the best,” added Wolves boss Nuno Espirito Santo.
