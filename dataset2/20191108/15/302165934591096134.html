Michael Bloomberg ‘will fail’ if he joins W.House race, says Trump

08 Nov 2019, 15:40

WASHINGTON, Nov 8 — US President Donald Trump mocked “little” billionaire and former New York mayor Michael Bloomberg today, saying his potential opponent for the 2020 election would fail if he joined the race.

“He doesn’t have the magic to do well... Little Michael will fail,” Trump told reporters at the White House as he headed to the southern state of Georgia to rally support.

“There’s nobody I’d rather run against than Little Michael.” — AFP
