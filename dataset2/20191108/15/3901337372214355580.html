10 Emotional Goal Celebrations Caught On Camera

08 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Soccer Laduma

The Absa Premiership has so far entertained us with goals galore this season and while we are hopeful of another action-packed weekend, Soccer Laduma takes a look at 10 emotional goal celebrations that have been photographed in the past weeks.

While their defence has been their biggest concern this season, Cape Town City are currently the highest-scoring team with 18 goals in the league, with their on-form striker, Kermit Erasmus also top of the players’ goalscoring charts.

However, the Gauteng sides are quickly catching up to the Citizens, with Kaizer Chiefs at second placed on the charts with 16 goals while SuperSport United have scored 14.

At the bottom of the charts is Chippa United, who have scored just three goals all season and while the highest goal-scoring team is nowhere near the top of the league standings, the Chilli Boys are certainly right at the foot of the table with just four points.

Here are the top five highest-scoring teams in the league:

City – 18 goals

Chiefs – 16 goals

SuperSport – 14 goals

Highlands Park – 13 goals

Bloemfontein Celtic – 13 goals
