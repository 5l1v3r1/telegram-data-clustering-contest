VS Angel Candice Swanepoel Models Her Collection’s Micro Leopard-Print Bikini

08 Nov 2019 by Yimin Huang

Victoria’s Secret Angel Candice Swanepoel has turned up the heat by wearing a tiny leopard-print bikini from her swimwear collection, Tropic of C.
The halter-top swimsuit features tiny triangle-shaped cups, with a tortoise ring in the middle connecting the two cups. The bottoms are high-cut, matching the overall design.
In the photo, Swanepoel stares fiercely and is covered in a green and yellow plaid shawl.
The bikini is part of the Tropic of C swimwear collection, and Swanepoel describes in her caption that it is the “new #lira” in “mini #mamaafrica print.”

Tropic of C is her own collection, and is largely influenced by nature.
