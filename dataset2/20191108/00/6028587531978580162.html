How Qorvo Stock Is Benefiting From 5G Related Growth

08 Nov 2019 by Trefis Team

Qorvo (NASDAQ: QRVO), a maker of radio-frequency semiconductor components, saw its stock price jump by over 20% on Friday. The rally was driven by stronger than expected Q2 FY’20 results (FY ends March), with adjusted EPS coming in at $1.52, compared to a consensus of $1.30 and also due to a strong forecast for the December quarter (revenue of $840 million to $860 million, compared to consensus of around $760 million). While the company is benefiting from growing 5G handset volumes, it also indicated that it saw new design wins with a major Korean smartphone maker (likely Samsung) and four Chinese phone makers. Separately, Qorvo also authorized a new $1 billion share repurchase program. How does Qorvo’s Revenue Growth in 2019 compare with that in prior periods and what’s the forecast?

Total Revenues for Qorvo slightly increased from $2.97 Bil in 2018 to $3.09 Bil in 2019; an increase of 3.93%.

This compares with Total Revenues growth of:

  • 49.0% in 2015
  • 52.6% in 2016
  • 16.2% in 2017
  • -1.95% in 2018

We expect Total Revenues growth to be 2.3% in 2020.

How does Qorvo’s Total Expense in 2019 compare with that in prior periods and what’s the forecast?

Total Expense for Qorvo marginally increased from $2.96 Bil in 2018 to $3.00 Bil in 2019; an increase of 1.43%.

This compares with Total Expense growth of:

  • 41.4% in 2015
  • 64.4% in 2016
  • 15.0% in 2017
  • -1.63% in 2018

We expect Total Expense to decline by 8% in 2020.

How does Qorvo’s EBT in 2019 compare with that in prior periods and what’s the forecast?

EBT for Qorvo increased dramatically by 435% from $17.1 Mil in 2018 to $91.8 Mil in 2019.

We expect EBT to increase to $400 Mil in 2020.
