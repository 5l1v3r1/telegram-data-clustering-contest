Diplo Takes Over Niall Horan’s Music Video In Official Remake Using Deepfakes

08 Nov 2019 by Yimin Huang

Deepfakes have become increasingly common and a potential problem, with even a readily-accessible app that allows you to place your face on celebrities.
Now, an official music video has emerged showing Diplo and Niall Horan’s faces morphed together through deepfakes.
The masterfully-made clip is for Diplo’s remix of the former One Direction member’s single, Nice To Meet Ya, producing an unsettlingly realistic “Diploran” with very few edit lines visible.
