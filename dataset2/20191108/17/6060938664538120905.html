Here's what Priyanka Chopra thought about Nick Jonas' moustache look in 'Midway'

08 Nov 2019, 17:11 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Nick Jonas' film 'Midway' has hit the theatres on November 08. And during the premiere of the film, the actor talked about his moustache look in the film.

During a conversation at the premiere, a reporter asked Nick to vote the greatest transformation in the film, his moustache and the other option was Woody Harrelson's hair. The singer quickly gave his vote to his moustache. He added that it was very French and needed some darkening in the film.

When the reporter asked what his wifey Priyanka Chopra thought about his moustache. Nick revealed that the Bollywood diva liked it as her father had one. The singer-actor went on to add that she was into it. Nick also stated that when he told her he will be taking it off after the wrap, Priyanka said that may she doesn't like it that much as she thought she did.
