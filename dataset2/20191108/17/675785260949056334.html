Gavin & Stacey's Mathew Horne goes public with new romance after splitting from fiancée

The British show's star Mathew Horne, 41, has confirmed his new relationship with Celina Bassili as they went public in London for the first time

08 Nov 2019, 17:52 by Brogan-Leigh Hurst

Gavin & Stacey superstar Mathew Horne has officially marked his romance with girlfriend Celina Bassili as they stepped out together for the first time.

Mathew, 41, was spotted kissing the set designer, 25, back in July seemingly confirming he had split with fiancée actress Evelyn Hoskins, 31.

He broke up with the Casualty star in December, just two months after popping the question.

Fans had even thought they'd got married as he was sporting a ring when he appeared on This Morning before the breakup.

Now Matt and Celina have attended their first public engagement together, cosying up for pictures to confirm they are a couple.

The pair attended the Night For Dreamers event by Chicken Shed Theatre while they were in London last night.

Celina met Matt when she worked on the set of Gavin & Stacey's upcoming Christmas special.

They looked like the ultimate power couple as they posed beside each other in smart attire.

 

Earlier this year, the couple were caught kissing in Burger King during a night out in Wales at the time of filming the one-off Christmas episode.

In the pictures, Matt was seen walking with his arm around his now-girlfriend.

 

Wearing an all-black outfit, complete with cap and sunglasses, Matt walked along with his arm wrapped around her shoulder.
