Comedian horrifies German version of Britain's Got Talent by eating sausages with her bum

WARNING: CONTAINS NUDITY AND GRAPHIC CONTENT - Comedian Duckie L'Orange shocked the judges on Das Supertalent with her X-rated act

08 Nov 2019, 08:46 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

A comedian appearing on the German version of Britain's Got Talent left judges horrified when she ate a string of sausages with her bum.

The X-rated act left the Das Supertalent judges and audience so shocked they turned away and covered their eyes.

People couldn't believe what they were seeing when act Duckie L'Orange dropped her trousers and turn round to reveal the face of a dog drawn onto her back and bottom.

She sat down on the stage and shuffled backwards to a string of sausages that she'd placed front and centre.

Duckie then appeared to gobble up the sausages with her bum - or the dog's face - while the judges screamed in horror and turned away, refusing to watch.

One of the judges, model Bruce Darnell, was so shocked he ran off from his desk and into the audience, muttering "Oh my God" repeatedly.

Fellow judge Sylvie Meis, a Dutch TV star, gasped in horror and covered her mouth, while musician Dieter Bohlen immediately turned his back before slipping off his chair and ducking under his desk.

Duckie L'Orange, describes herself on her website as "a Berlin based performance artist with a twist".

She also describes herself as a children's performer.

Her bio reads: "She does weird and wacky interactive performances for kids, and equally wacky but significantly dirtier shows for adults."

Duckie boasts skills including puppetry, children's entertainment and burlesque.

The German show is one of many international spin-offs of Simon Cowell's Britain's Got Talent.

Many contestants on the British version of the show have gone for the shock factor before, but an act quite like this has never made it onto the programme.

Among the most outrageous British acts have been topless burlesque performers and daredevils risking their lives for entertainment.
