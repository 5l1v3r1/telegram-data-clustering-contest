Your Phone app gets big emojis for some

08 Nov 2019, 08:08 by Surur

Microsoft is testing a new feature in their popular Your Phone app. The latest update is the addition of big emojis in the Your Phone desktop app.

Microsoft Program Manager Roberto Bojorquez announced the feature on twitter, saying if you reply to a message with a single emoji, this will now be of a much larger size.

The feature is currently rolling out to 50% Windows 10 Insiders who are also using the Your Phone app with Android handsets.

Microsoft has been rapidly polishing the Your Phone app, and besides big emojis in the Your Phone, Microsoft is also adding the ability to make and take phone calls using your desktop, the ability to preview pictures in full size and of course the ability to control your phone directly.
