All ICAI exams postponed in view of Ayodhya verdict, Aligarh Muslim University suspends classes and tests

08 Nov 2019, 20:17 by JKR Staff

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India has said that it has decided to postpone all ‘examinations of the ICAI namely Foundation Paper 1, Final Paper 5, IRM Paper1, INTT AT and DISA ET Paper’ in view of the planned Ayodhya verdict by the Supreme Court today. All these exams were scheduled for 9 November.

The notification by the ICAI said that these exams, scheduled for 9 November ‘on all India basis and abroad stand postponed to a later date which shall be announced separately.’
