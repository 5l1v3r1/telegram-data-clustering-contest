Japan's Sakurajima volcano erupts in miles-high plume of ash: Kyodo

08 Nov 2019, 20:22

TOKYO: Japan's Sakurajima volcano shot smoke and ash miles into the sky on Friday (Nov 8) in its biggest eruption in more than three years, Kyodo News reported.

The volcano, on the southernmost main island of Kyushu, spewed ash 5.5km high, the first time the ash cloud from an eruption has exceeded 5km since July 2016, Kyodo said, citing a local meteorological observatory.
