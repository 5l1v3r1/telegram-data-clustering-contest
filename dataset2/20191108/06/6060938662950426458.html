Top celebrity baby names that may become popular in 2020

08 Nov 2019, 06:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Selecting the baby's name is one of the most crucial and pressurising tasks for parents-to-be. New parents spend days and months consulting friends and families for suggestions, buy Baby Names books, search endlessly on the Internet. But when you come across names of your favourite characters in a web series, you surely get tempted. Arya Stark from Game of Thrones, Rachel from F.R.I.E.N.D.S, Chrissy Teigen's Luna, are all names that are becoming extremely popular among new parents.
