12 years of Ranbir Kapoor: Fans pour in love for the actor over social media

08 Nov 2019, 19:58 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ranbir Kapoor has come a long way in Bollywood and has made a place for himself in the industry. The actor marked his debut with Sanjay Leela Bhansali' 'Saawariya' alongside Sonam Kapoor.

Ranbir went on to become an A-list actor of Bollywood by delivering stellar performances in the films like 'Wake Up Sid', 'Rockstar', 'Barfi!', 'Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani', 'Tamasha' 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil' and more. He was last seen in 'Sanju', a biopic on Sanjay Dutt for which he also received the Filmfare Best Actor Award.

His first film as an actor was released on November 09, 2007 and today Ranbir has completed 12 years in the world of cinema. Fans poured in love for their favourite star over social media and #12YearsOfRanbirKapoor trend.
