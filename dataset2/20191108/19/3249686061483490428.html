Chinese Celebrity fan Chengcheng shares a photo with BLACKPINK’s Rosé and people are loving the visual explosion

08 Nov 2019, 19:56 by abbyloveskpop101

Chinese rapper Fan Chengcheng shared a photo with BLACKPINK‘s Rosé. It was taken on November 7 during Maison Valentino’s event in Beijing China.

He posted the photo onto his Instagram with a caption that reads, "Successfully met my idol."

They're both wearing Valentino in black in the photo. Many fans are complimenting their superior visuals.

Fan Chengcheng is the younger brother of Fan Bingbing. He competed in China's version of 'Produce 101' called 'Idol Producer' and ranked 3rd in the competition. He debuted in the group named NINE PERCENT.
