Exclusive Preview: Black Cat Annual #1

08 Nov 2019, 19:52 by Blair Marnell

Way back in the ’80s, Felicia Hardy and Peter Parker were inseparable lovers. However, they eventually drifted apart. Spider-Man reunited with his first love, Mary Jane, and also went on to marry her. Meanwhile, Black Cat carried a torch for Spidey that made things difficult for them. Even when Felicia returned to a life of crime, she still had feelings for her ex. Now, out of nowhere, Black Cat and Spider-Man are getting married! And that’s just in the first two pages.

In Superhero Hype’s exclusive preview for Black Cat Annual #1, Jed MacKay is joined by artists Joey Vazquez, Natacha Bustos, and Juan Gedeon as they reveal why Spidey is marrying Felicia. Naturally, the cover image was just a distraction, much like the wedding itself. The real story is about what comes after, because Felicia has a plan. And she needs Spider-Man’s help to pull it off.

Here’s the official description from Marvel.

“You are cordially invited to the wedding of Black Cat and Spider-Man! In lieu of gifts, please turn off all your security systems, laser grids and counterweight giant stone traps. All this AND a bonus story?!”

You can read the full preview in our gallery below. Black Cat Annual #1 will hit comic book stores on Wednesday, November 13.
