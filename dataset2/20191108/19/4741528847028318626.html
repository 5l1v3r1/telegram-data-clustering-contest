Stock market witnesses return of the bulls as ASI increase by 0.48%

08 Nov 2019, 19:03 by Samuel Oyekanmi

The Nigerian Stock Exchange rounded off trading on Friday on a positive note as the All-share index increased by 0.48% to close at 26,314.49 basis points. The total trade recorded was 432.5 million valued at N5.6 billion across 4,002 deals leaving the total equity market capitalisation at N12.81 trillion.
