Real Sociedad drop points against Leganés1-1 at Reale Arena

Real Sociedad failed to seal all three points against Leganés 1-1 at Reale Arena on Friday

08 Nov 2019, 22:14 by SPORT.es

Real Sociedad and Leganés draw 1-1 on Friday at Reale Arena. Real Sociedad arrived with the intention of increasing their league score after winning away by a 1-2 victory against Granada while Leganés lost by a result of 1-2 in the previous match against Eibar. At the moment, Real Sociedad and Leganés occupy position 1st (23 points) and 20th (6 points) respectively after playing a total of 13 matches.

Both sides were struggling to produce anything in the first half and the teams went to the dressing room locked in a 0-0.

Real Sociedad took the lead in the second half, thanks to a goal from Mikel Merino 63 minutes in the game. Leganés found an equaliser thanks to a goal from Youssef En-Nesyri in the 78th minute to establish the 1-1, which saw the second half to a 1-1.

As for substitutions, both coaches used full substitutions. For Real Sociedad Luca Sangalli Fuentes, Alexander Isak and Aritz Elustondo came on for Adnan Januzaj, Willian Jose and Joseba Zaldua, and Leganés brought on Youssef En-Nesyri, Guido Carrillo and Aitor Ruibal for José Arnaiz, Óscar and Martin Braithwaite.

The referee booked Nacho Monreal and Mikel Merino from Real, while for Leganés Recio, Unai Bustinza, Kevin Rodrigues, Kenneth Omeruo and Roberto Rosales saw a yellow card and Kevin Rodrigues (2 yellow cards) saw the red card.

Real Sociedad with their next fixture against Real Madrid at away stadium, while Leganés will face Barcelona at home stadium.
