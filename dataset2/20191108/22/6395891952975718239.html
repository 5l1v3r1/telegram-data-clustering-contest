All lanes reopened on 495 SB in Tewksbury following rollover crash

08 Nov 2019, 22:36 by Manoella Macedo

TEWKSBURY, Mass. - A rollover crash on 495 initially forced officials to close all southbound lanes while they cleared the scene.

All lanes have since been reopened.

State Police responded to reports of a crash at around 4:35 p.m. on 495 south just north of exit 39 in Tewksbury.
