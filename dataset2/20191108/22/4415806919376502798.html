KSI clarifies comments about ‘killing’ Logan Paul ahead of boxing match

08 Nov 2019, 22:29

YouTube star KSI has clarified comments he made ahead of his highly-anticipated boxing match with Logan Paul, saying that he does not want to kill him.

The social media star and amateur boxer had taken part in pre-fight banter with fellow YouTuber Paul at a press conference on Thursday, where he said to his rival: “You’re going to f***ing die.”
