Ringgit slightly lower against US dollar at opening

08 Nov 2019, 01:22 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 8): The ringgit opened slightly lower on lack of buying interest, weighed down by uncertainties surrounding the US-China trade negotiations, said a dealer.

At 9.06 am, the ringgit was quoted at 4.1260/1300 versus the US dollar from 4.1225/1255 on Thursday. - Bernama
