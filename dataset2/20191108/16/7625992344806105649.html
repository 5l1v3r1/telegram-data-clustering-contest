John Legend and Kelly Clarkson release new version of "Baby, It's Cold Outside" after critics said it promoted date rape

08 Nov 2019, 16:00 by Caitlin O'Kane

John Legend and Kelly Clarkson have taken an old holiday classic and revamped it for modern times. The stars changed the lyrics of "Baby, It's Cold Outside" after critics said the iconic tune promoted date rape. 

Legend posted his duet with Clarkson on Vevo Thursday, and the song was also made available for streaming. It appears on the deluxe edition of his Christmas album "A Legendary Christmas."
