The Foals and other trending shows that will come to Birmingham soon - here’s how to get tickets

Birmingham’s arenas are set to hold an astonishing line up of acts over the coming months - see highlights on trending shows, who's playing in the next few weeks and latest gigs on sale

08 Nov 2019, 16:21 by Scott Dickinson, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/scott-dickinson/

International talent will continue to flock to Birmingham both this year and next for a stunning set of shows.

Using Resorts World and Arena Birmingham’s data, we have produced a list of the hottest acts that will be coming to Birmingham soon.

Trending Shows

Evanescene & Within Temptation Worlds Collide Tour

The rock legends’ collaboration will electrify Arena Birmingham on Friday, 1 May 2020.

The European tour sees the female fronted band take on 18 arenas and promises to be a performance for the ages.

Evanescene have sold more than 17 million copies of their debut album ‘Fallen’ worldwide, whilst Within Temptation currently boast 7 studio albums and more than 3.5 million record sales. This combination of such notorious rockers will be a treat for fans and the bands alike.

Within Temptation's lead vocalist, Den Adel, commented: "It is beyond our greatest expectations that a lot of our shows with Evanescence would sell out so quickly! We are super grateful for this. Surprise!”

Tickets are priced from £52.25 and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official booking page here now.

 

Cirque Du Soleil Crystal

 

The dazzling show will skate into Resorts World Arena from Wednesday, 8 April - Sunday, 12 April 2020.

This unique performance has proven a huge success in prior years, combining circus acts, acrobatics and aerial feats in one ice coated spectacular.  

We’ll follow Crystal and her journey of self-discovery in a heart-warming tale that accompanies a gripping plot with immense production value.

With jaw dropping visual projections, the show weaves popular music and the sound of Cirque du Soleil to create a one of a kind experience for spectators of all ages.

Tickets are priced from £36.20 and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

 

Dido

 

Dido will bring her stunning UK tour to Arena Birmingham on Thursday, 28 November 2019.

Following the release of her smash hit album, Still on my Mind, the Oscar-nominated singer-songwriter is gracing us with a seven-date UK tour that will stop in Brum.

The tour is her first for 15 years and given that her debut record ‘No Angel’ racked up over 21 million sales, fans have been pining for more ever since.

Tickets are priced from £38.00 (including fees) and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

 

Foals

 

Indie rock band Foals will hit Arena Birmingham on Saturday 9 May 2020 as part of their UK tour.

The tour kicks off in Edinburgh and heads to London and Blackpool before their date here in Birmingham, and we can’t wait to see what they have in store for us.

Across their nearly 15 year existence, the Oxford based band have released five studio albums. Their latest record ‘Everything Not Saved Will Be Lost’ received widespread reviews from critics and fans alike.

Dave Everley of Q says the album "underlines why its creators have continued to prosper long after so many of their late-'00s contemporaries have been lost to the waves."

Tickets for their show cost from £33.50 and (including fees) and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

 

Shows playing soon

Liam Gallagher

 

Liam’s 11-date UK tour will stop at Arena Birmingham on Tuesday, 12 November.

The singer’s tours are renowned for selling out in minutes but his gig in Birmingham still has tickets left on The Ticket Factory website.

The tour rides off his second solo studio album ‘Why Me? Why Not’, a hit with critics and fans that subsequently debuted at number one on the UK Albums Chart.

The record’s lead single 'Shockwave' shot straight to the top of the Official Vinyl and Trending Charts.

Gallagher will keep the momentum up with a tour set to astound audiences on Tuesday night.

Tickets are priced from £41.75 and can be purchased here on The Ticket Factory's booking page.

 

Christina Aguilera

 

The American singer-songwriter announced her first US tour a decade back in May 2018, and now she’s hitting the UK.

Aguilera will come to Resorts World Arena on Thursday, 14 November 2019 for a world-class act that is set to take on Las Vegas shortly after.

Throughout her career, she has sold more than 43 million records worldwide. Aguilera has achieved five No. 1 singles on the Billboard Hot 100 chart making her the fourth female artist to top the chart over three consecutive decades (1990s, 2000s, and 2010s).

She has received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and holds the prestigious honor of being the only artist under the age of 30 included in Rolling Stone Magazine's list of the 100 greatest singers of all time

Tickets to her show are priced from £57.75 and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

 

Jack Whitehall: Stood Up Tour

 

The comedian is embarking on his largest stand up tour ever with three dates pencilled in at Arena Birmingham on 15,16,17 November 2019.

Jack Whitehall began his career at The Edinburgh Fringe Festival in 2007 and then became a regular on Sky One's A League of Their Own.

Since then the 31 year old’s career has gone from strength to strength, portraying the hilarious JP in Fresh Meat and Alfie Wickers in Bad Education.

Clearly funny bones run in the Whitehall family, as Netflix subscribers will have seen Jack’s misadventures with his dad ‘Travels with my Father’, as well as his stand-up show ‘At Large’.

Jack’s tour has already proved wildly popular, but brummies will be ecstatic to know that tickets are still available for his latest stand-up gig.

They can are priced from £35.25 and can be purchased on The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.

 

Motorcycle Live

 

Start your engines, Motorcycle Live is back for nine days of adrenaline-filled action.

 Running from November 16 - 24 2019 at the NEC, bike enthusiasts will be able to check out the latest bikes and kit from some of the biggest brands in the world.

You can catch thrilling live action shows, take a test ride, visit the Wonderful & Weird exhibition brought to the show by The National Motorcycle Museum and even take part in some exciting competitions, including your chance to win a ride with Carl Fogerty. There are plenty of fun experiences for younger ticket-holders too.

The biking exhibition, in association with Bikesure Insurance, proved incredibly popular last year pulling in 103,702 fans and over 40 of the world’s leading manufacturers.

A number of ticket options are available from The Ticket Factory. Click here to book yours now.

A-ha: Hunting High and Low 2020 Tour

 

The legendary icon of 80s culture that is A-ha will be hitting Arena Birmingham on Friday 13 November 2020.

They added the UK dates to their tour following phenomenal demand from fans, and with 10 albums and over 40 million record sales to the band’s name, we understand their outcry.

A-ha will dive into their back-catalogue of hits, with the show’s first half celebrating their debut album ‘Hunting and High and Low’, an incredible thrust to the band’s long-standing career.

Songwriter and guitarist Pål Waaktaar has affirmed that “There’s not one song on that album that we’re not looking forward to playing live”, and we certainly match their enthusiasm.

Trevor Noah Loud and Clear Tour 2020

 

The hilarious star of ‘The Daily show’, Trevor Noah, will be bringing his latest tour to Arena Birmingham on Saturday 23 May 2020.

The 35 year old has become somewhat of a legend in the comedy circuit after recently picking up the 2019 NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Writing in a Comedy Series, plus two other nominations.

But it’s his political viewpoints and progressive attitudes that fuel his new found status. In April 2018 he set up a youth development initiative to help young people prepare for work and education through Microsoft.

The comedian is set to provide a ground breaking show, pulling laughter from different corners of life.

Tickets will go on sale for his cutting-edge tour tomorrow at 10am, and can be purchased from The Ticket Factory’s official bookings page here.
