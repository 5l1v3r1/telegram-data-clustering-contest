Police believe Harley Quinn may help identify a suspect

Police are looking for Harley Quinn. Not Gotham Police — the Regina Police Service.

08 Nov 2019, 16:24 by Austin M. Davis

Police are looking for Harley Quinn.

Not Gotham Police — the Regina Police Service.

The RPS “is looking to identify a possible witness to an incident that occurred in the early morning hours of Oct. 27” outside a bar on the 600 block of Victoria Avenue.

Police did not provide any information about the incident in question.

However, police say the possible witness was dressed as DC Comics character Harley Quinn, but have no more specifics about the costume. Police believe she may have information to identify a suspect.

There is no indication police are looking for the Joker.

The character first appeared in Batman: The Animated Series and was most recently portrayed by Margot Robbie in the 2016 Suicide Squad live-action film.
