Reason behind Disha Patani's sore knees while shooting Salman Khan starrer 'Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai'

08 Nov 2019, 16:04 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After sharing screen space with superstar Salman Khan in 'Bharat', Disha Patani will be starring with him once again in their upcoming film 'Radhe: Your Most Wanted Bhai'.

And the actress has kick-started the prep for the film. She is also shooting for her film 'KTina' simultaneously. 'Radhe' will helmed be by Prabhu Deva who is also known for his dance moves.

And the filmmaker makes sure to depict his love for dance in the songs of his films. Shooting for a Prabhudeva song in the film has given Disha a case of #SoreKnees.

Also, Disha loves to put all her effort to get into the skin of her character and this time is no different! She is working hard to deliver another stellar performance in her upcoming films.
