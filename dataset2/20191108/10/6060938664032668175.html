Ayushmann Khurrana starrer 'Bala' leaked online by piracy website Tamilrockers

08 Nov 2019, 10:57 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Starting from where he left the last time, Ayushmann Khurrana's latest offering 'Bala' looks all set to be his seventh straight hit in succession. The film which sees him play a prematurely balding guy has opened to a great start, with positive reviews from every corner hailing the film. The film has also opened to a good occupancy and is expected to pick up by the evening and is most likely to register a double-digit opening day collection.

Read Also: 'Bala' celeb review: Varun Dhawan, Ileana D'Cruz and other Bollywood stars laud the Ayushmann Khurrana starrer

However, the film has met with a big setback. 'Bala' becomes the latest movie to fall prey to online piracy websites. The Ayushmann Khurrana starrer film has been leaked online and is available to download illegally. As per reports, the film has been leaked online by the infamous Tamilrockers.

This comes after recent Diwali releases, including 'Housefull 4', 'Made In China', 'Saand Ki Aankh', and south film 'Bigil' were leaked online hours after its release.

Major Bollywood, as well as regional releases, are being leaked online within the first day of release, eating up a major chunk of revenue. The illegal practice continues even after repeated orders from the judiciary and efforts made by the government and the media industry to curtail piracy.

Directed by Amar Kaushik, 'Bala' is a film set in Kanpur that revolves around a man suffering from premature balding and the lack of confidence and societal pressure which runs with it. The film also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles.
