Jemima Goldsmith pins Joaquin Phoenix as Oscar winner for 'Joker'

08 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Entertainment Desk

Jemima Goldsmith is in awe of Joaquin Phoenix’s performance in and as Joker. Jemima said she would be surprised if the actor didn’t win an Oscar for his prowess in the film.

She, on Friday, took to Twitter and wrote, How can Joaquin Phoenix not get the Oscar for that mesmerising performance in Joker?”
