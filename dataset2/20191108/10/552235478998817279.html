Police want to speak to this man over serious assault at Edinburgh nightclub

Cops released CCTV images of the male they are looking to trace in relation to the incident at Why Not nightclub.

08 Nov 2019, 10:17 by Nicholas Keyden, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/nicholas-keyden/

Police are wanting to speak to this man after a serious assault at a popular nightclub in Edinburgh.

Cops released CCTV images of the male they are looking to trace in relation to the incident at Why Not nightclub, George Street, around 2.30am on Tuesday September 10.

He is described as white, slim build, 5’8-6ft tall, short dark hair.  He is pictured wearing a navy round neck t-shirt, denim jeans and white trainers.

Detective Constable John Dunn of Gayfield CID said: "As part of the investigation, we are eager to identify the man in the images and would urge him, or anyone who recognises him, to get in touch with us immediately.”

Those with information can contact Police Scotland on 101, quoting incident number 0850 of 11 August 2019, or contact Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
