Wolves boss Nuno answers that big Aston Villa question

Wolverhampton Wanderers 1-1 Slovan Bratislava | Wolves moved a step closer to Europa League qualification after a late win over Slovan at Molineux - Raul Jimenez with the goal

08 Nov 2019, 10:23 by Alex Dicken, https://www.facebook.com/alexdickenjournalist/?fref=ts

Nuno Espirito Santo has told Ruben Neves to practice his penalties after his miss against Slovan Bratislava.

Neves missed a penalty in the second half of Wolves’ 1-0 Europa League win despite regular taker Raul Jimenez, who has notched three spot-kicks already this term, being on the field.

Neves surprisingly stepped up after Pedro Neto was tripped inside the box but Slovan goalkeeper Dominik Greif saved it.

Jimenez eventually spared his teammate’s blushes with a stoppage time winner - and he is certain to take if Wolves get a penalty against Aston Villa on Sunday.

“Ruben has to improve, he kicked it very bad,” said Nuno. “Very bad. For today it was Ruben, he kicked it so bad, so bad.

“You have to give credit to the goalkeeper but it was the perfect spot to stop it - high and slow, bad. He has to practice - a lot!”
