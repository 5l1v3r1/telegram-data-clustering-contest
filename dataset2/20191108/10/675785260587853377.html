Robbie Savage's Premier League predictions including Liverpool vs Man City

The BT Sport pundit has looked into his crystal ball ahead of the weekend's Premier League fixtures

08 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Robbie Savage, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/robbie-savage/

It is a big weekend of Premier League football, with huge games at both the top and bottom of the table.

Liverpool host Manchester City on Sunday knowing that they could go a massive nine points clear of their title rivals with victory.

And BT Sport pundit and Mirror columnist Robbie Savage believes they will do just that, backing the Reds to come out with a 2-1 win at Anfield.

Elsewhere under-pressure Arsenal manager Unai Emery faces a tough-looking trip to Leicester City, who are flying high and have won seven of their last eight games in all competitions.

And Savage can't see the Gunners ending their recent poor form, predicting a 2-1 win for the Foxes at the King Power Stadium.

Friday night sees a big game at the bottom of the table, with 19th place playing 20th as Norwich host Watford .

On Saturday, meanwhile, Chelsea will look to win their sixth Premier League game in a row when they host Crystal Palace and West Ham will look to end their recent rotten form when they travel to Burnley .

Tottenham will look to get back to winning ways when they host Sheffield United, while Newcastle play Bournemouth and strugglers Southampton and Everton meet.

Finally on Sunday Manchester United will look to build on their midweek Europa League win when they host Brighton and there is a midlands derby as Wolves take on Aston Villa.

Here are Sav's predictions in full...

Norwich 1-2 Watford

Chelsea 3-1 Palace

Burnley 2-1 West Ham

Newcastle 1-0 Bournemouth
