'Bala' celeb review: Varun Dhawan, Ileana D'Cruz and other Bollywood stars laud the Ayushmann Khurrana starrer

08 Nov 2019, 10:53 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushmann Khurrana is back in the spotlight for his latest comedy ‘Bala’. The film directed by Amar Kaushik that hit theatres today is not only tickling the audience’s funny bone but is also serving up some food for thought with its powerful message.

The comedy-drama promises a humorous ride with Ayushmann joined by Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in the lead. The film offers a quirky take on premature balding in men and is touted to possess all the ingredients of a true entertainer.

The quirky family entertainer, which is laden with several laugh-out-loud moments has not only got fans buzzy but earned some good reviews from Bollywood stars.
