Megabus with 53 passengers involved in an accident on I-75

09 Nov 2019, 15:03 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. â€” Florida Highway Patrol responded to an accident involving a Megabus with 53 passengers and a vehicle on I-75 at Daniels Parkway. The vehicle was traveling north on I-75 in the right lane as it entered I-75 from the entrance ramp of Daniels Parkway. The Megabus was traveling north on I-75 in the center lane.

The vehicle made an improper lane change in front of the bus. The front of the bus struck the rear of the vehicle. The bus came to a final rest in the center lane, unable to be moved due to the damage. Northbound I-75 center lanes were reopened at 2:19 pm.

The Megabus was towed away, and another bus was called in to pick up the 53 passengers. No injuries were reported.
