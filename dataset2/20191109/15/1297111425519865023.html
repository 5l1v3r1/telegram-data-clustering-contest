Police investigating death in Core neighbourhood as homicide

Police are releasing few details as they conduct an investigation into the death of a man in Regina's Core neighbourhood Thursday night.

09 Nov 2019, 15:54 by Regina Leader-Post

Police are investigating the death of a man in Regina’s Core neighbourhood Thursday night as a homicide.

Officers responded to the 1800 block of St. John Street for a report of a deceased male after 11 p.m. Nov. 7. EMS also responded and confirmed the person was dead.

Officers secured the scene and requested additional resources, including the provincial coroner, forensic identification and major crimes units.

Police say the identity of the deceased person has been confirmed but “his name will not be released pending further investigation.”

In a media release sent more than seven hours later police said, “At this early stage, many questions remain but additional investigation will add more information to clarify the circumstances of this individual’s death. ”

Anyone who has information to offer is encouraged to contact the Regina Police Service at 306-777-6500 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477 (TIPS).
