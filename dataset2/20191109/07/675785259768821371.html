What channel is Chelsea vs Crystal Palace on? TV and live stream information

Chelsea will look to restore a little bit of stability after their dramatic Champions League clash during the week

09 Nov 2019, 07:10 by Sam Fletcher

Chelsea return to Premier League action as they take on Crystal Palace in Saturday’s early kick-off.

Following their dramatic fight back on Tuesday night, the next test for Frank Lampard’s young Blues is a Palace side who have a mixed record away from home this season.

Despite wins over Manchester United and West Ham , the Eagles were beaten by newly-promoted Sheffield United and also received a battering by Spurs.

As for Chelsea , they’re on a five-match league winning streak since, scoring 13 goals in the process, as they eye a top-four finish in Lampard's first season.

Here’s everything you need to know ahead of the match...

When is the game?

The game is on Saturday 9 November.

Kick-off is set for 12:30pm.

Where is the game being played?

The game takes place at Stamford Bridge.

Chelsea have only lost once at home all season - against Premier League leaders Liverpool .

What TV channel is the game on?

The game will be shown on BT Sport 1 for UK viewers.

Coverage starts at 12:00pm.

Can you live stream the game?

UK subscribers to BT Sport can stream the game through their website and the BT Sport app.

Alternatively, you can follow all of the action on the Mirror Football live blog.

Chelsea team news

Lampard will have to do without Antonio Rudiger and Ruben Loftus-Cheek who remain sidelined through injury.

Mason Mount and Ross Barkley remain doubts with ankle injuries but could still pass a late fitness test to play against Palace.

Jorginho, who scored two penalties on Tuesday night, misses out through suspension.

Crystal Palace team news

James McArthur is a major doubt after suffering a back injury in last weekend’s defeat to Leicester .

Mamadou Sakho and Andros Townsend are also likely to miss out.
