Three new jazz nights to try in Dubai

Chill out with some live music

09 Nov 2019, 07:49 by Time Out Dubai staff

Analog Room
Head to the cool underground club’s new jazz night every Wednesday for experimental jazz and groove from some of Dubai’s best talent.
Free. Wed 6.30pm-2am. Ongoing. Ibis Al Barsha, Al Barsha (050 883 3172).

Level 43 Sky Lounge
Every Wednesday saxophonist Manuel Kev will be fusing jazz, classical and Latin tunes and the night continues with a live DJ.
Wed 7pm-11.30pm. Ongoing. Four Points by Sheraton, Sheikh Zayed Road (04 316 9888).

Roberto’s
Every Sunday famous Italian swing band Mark Zitti e i Fratelli Coltelli (try saying that out loud) will keep your toes tapping until the early hours.
Free. Sun 10pm-1am. Ongoing. Gate Village 1, DIFC (04 386 0066).
