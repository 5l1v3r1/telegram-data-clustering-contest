Greenply Industries Q2 net profit rises by 51%

Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) increased by 22.1 percent to Rs 45 crore, the company said in a statement.

09 Nov 2019 by PTI

Greenply Industries has reported a 51 percent increase in its consolidated net profit to Rs 26.4 crore during the second quarter of the current fiscal as compared to Rs 17.5 crore in the year-ago period.

Total consolidated income for the quarter ended September 30, 2019, stood at Rs 380.4 crore as against Rs 363.5 crore recorded in the corresponding period of the last fiscal.

Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) increased by 22.1 percent to Rs 45 crore, the company said in a statement.
