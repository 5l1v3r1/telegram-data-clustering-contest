UFC Fight Night 163 Main Event Preview: Stats, Facts And Numbers For Zabit Magomedsharipov Vs. Calvin Kattar

09 Nov 2019 by Trent Reinsmith

The UFC returns to Moscow on Saturday for UFC Fight Night 163, which streams in its entirety on ESPN+. In the main event, Zabit Magomedsharipov faces Calvin Kattar in a bout that could result in a shakeup in the official UFC featherweight rankings.Magomedsharipov, who is 5-0 in the UFC with three stoppage victories, who is currently the No. 5 ranked fighter at 145 pounds, is coming off a decision win over UFC veteran Jeremy Stephens in his most recent fight. Kattar, who is the No. 11 ranked fighter at featherweight is 4-1 in the UFC with three knockout victories.

Here are some stats, facts and numbers for the upcoming matchup between Magomedsharipov and Kattar.

UFC Fight Night 163 takes place at CSKA Arena in Moscow. The prelims begin at 11 a.m. ET on ESPN+, the main card follows on ESPN+ at 2:00 p.m. ET
