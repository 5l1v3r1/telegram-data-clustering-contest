Central Industrial Security Force (CISF), which is one of the largest government security forces in India provides security cover to the most important installations in the country. The force demonstrated their capability and the advanced level of training when it comes to terrorist attacks.

CISF demonstrated how they act in a situation when a terrorist attack happens at an airport. This happened during the celebration of Ekta Diwas at the Kevadia, Gujarat in presence of Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

The demonstration to the public and senior government officials showed a temporary airport terminal and used it as a mock terrorist attack. The CISF personnel can be seen doing all kinds of stunts and manoeuvres to extract the VIP from the airport terminal.

What caught our attention was the exit process that used a bunch of vehicles. After the VIP was extracted successfully from the airport terminal, he was made to sit in a Maruti Suzuki Gypsy. The video shows a terrorist vehicle following the Gypsy.
