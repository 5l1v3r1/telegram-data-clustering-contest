X Factor: Celebrity fans query fashion trend as Nicole and Jenny wear 'same dress'

The X Factor: Celebrity judge Nicole Scherzinger and her act Jenny Ryan have been wearing similar outfits on the show, and fans think they're sharing material

09 Nov 2019, 23:26 by Charlotte Tutton

The X Factor: Celebrity viewers have noticed a hilarious trend over the past couple of weeks, or so they claim.

Nicole Scherzinger brings the glam every week with her incredible frocks during the live shows.

She's not alone though, as her act Jenny Ryan has also been seen in some pretty extravagant outfits when taking to the stage.

But some viewers have noticed that for the past two weeks, Jenny and her mentor have donned very similar looks.

Now, some viewers are mocking the situation and joking the pair are sharing material, or even copying each other's show styles.

One viewer spotted: "Oh that’s where the other half of Nicole’s dress went."

Another said: "Jenny is wearing the leftovers from Nicole’s dress."

A third joked: "Can someone tell @NicoleScherzy that @jenlion has the rest of her dress?"

This was echoed by another viewer who asked: "Is Jenny wearing the other half of Nicole's dress..."

But jokes aside, some fans thought there was some sort of trend going on with the ladies.

A viewer commented: "Jenny wearing bits of Nicole's dress two weeks in a row."

Another added: "Quick question, why does Jenny always dress like Nicole?"

There was double elimination drama as No Love Lost and Martin Bashir were sent home.

But Simon Cowell quickly decided to introduce a new wildcard twist, announcing one eliminated act will return for the live final.

The X Factor: Celebrity continues next Saturday on ITV.
