S.E.S's Bada and Eugene show their lasting friendship at GIFF

09 Nov 2019, 23:10 by olmal

S.E.S's Bada and Eugene showed off their long-lasting friendship at Gangneung International Film Festival (GIFF). On November 9 KST, Bada posted two selfies with Eugene at GIFF on her Instagram with a caption, "Congratulations to the premiere of GIFF". 

Since the group's debut in 1997, these two beautiful ladies have been friends for over two decades and the relationship is still going strong.
