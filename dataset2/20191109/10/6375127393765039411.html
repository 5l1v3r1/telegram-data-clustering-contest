Porac’s most wanted arrested

09 Nov 2019, 10:31 by Bernard Galang

Pampanga police operatives arrested last Thursday night the no. 1 most wanted person of Porac town, who was wanted for murder and violation of the anti-drug law.

Brigadier Gen. Rhodel Sermonia, Police Regional Office 3 (PRO3) director, identified the arrested suspect as Daryll Dela Cruz, 32, of Barangay Tokwing in Porac town. 

Sermonia said Dela Cruz was one of the 24 suspects of various crimes that were arrested over the week: seven in Bulacan; four in Nueva Ecija; four in Pampanga; three in Bataan; three in Zambales; and one each in Aurora, Tarlac and Angeles City.

He said operatives from the Provincial Intelligence Branch of Pampanga police arrested Dela Cruz around 9:30 p.m. of November 7 at Jose Lapid District Hospital in Sacan, Porac by virtue of warrant issued by Third Judicial Region of Angeles City for murder and violation of the anti-illegal drug law.
