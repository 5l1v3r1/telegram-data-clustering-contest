Tesla's Musk, Greenlight's Einhorn taunt each other on Twitter

09 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Reuters

Tesla Chief Executive Officer Elon Musk and noted short-seller David Einhorn mocked each other in open letters published on Twitter on Friday, trading barbs clothed in polite language.

Einhorn on October 30 had told his investors at Greenlight Capital that his fund had lost money on Tesla. Greenlight bet against the electric carmaker, and shares rose in the second quarter.

Still, he told investors that Tesla “appears to continue to spin positive PR ahead of the safety and fair treatment of its customers.”

Musk, who has a history of being playful on Twitter, began an exchange by posting an open letter on Twitter addressing Einhorn as “Dear Mr. Unicorn (fabulous name btw)”. Einhorn means unicorn in German.
