BTS Jimin was featured in two different international shows on the same day!

09 Nov 2019, 10:45 by parkhaneul1013

On November 6th, BTS Jimin was seen being featured in two different international show which is Argentina edition of "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?" and TNT Latin America for their BTS special segment.

Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? is a famous international television game show where they test their contestant with multiple-choice questions to win large cash prizes. BTS Jimin along with BTS SUGA were spotted being  featured in one of the question in Argentina edition of "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?".

TNT or Turner Network Television is a well known American TV network for broadcasting all of the high-profile award shows including the Oscars and Grammys. TNT Latin America is one of TNT international versions and BTS Jimin was seen being featured for their BTS special segment. TNT Latin America uploaded an introduction video about BTS Jimin along with all of his achievements as an idol on their official YouTube channel.
