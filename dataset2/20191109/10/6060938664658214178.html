Salman Khan croons the peppy track ‘Yu Karke’ from ‘Dabangg 3’; check out the audio inside

09 Nov 2019, 10:22 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Chulbul Pandey a.k.a Salman Khan has given vocals to a peppy track ‘Yu Karke’ from ‘Dabangg 3’, and the audio of the same is out for the fans to enjoy. It’s an upbeat fun song with quirky lyrics which is expected to rule the charts in no time.

The Robinhood Pandey himself has taken to his social media handle to share the audio, and in the caption he has written: “'Dabangg 3' ka naya gaana, 'Yu Karke', suno humaari yaani ki aap sabhi ke favourite Chulbul Pandey ki aawaaz mein #YuKarkeSong”

Talking further of the credits, the duo Sajid-Wajid has composed the song, while Danish Sabri has penned it. Further, along with Salman Khan, it is Payal Dev who has crooned the track. Coming to the movie, directed by Prabhudeva, ‘Dabangg 3’ is slated to release on 20 December.
