Nikki Haley says Trump does not deserve 'death penalty' impeachment proceedings

09 Nov 2019, 05:25 by Danielle Wallace

Former U.N. Ambassador Nikki Haley says she does not believe President Trump will be impeached or removed from office, adding that her former boss does not deserve what she termed “the death penalty” for being elected commander-in-chief.

Haley, who announced her resignation from the Trump administration last October, made the remarks in an interview set to air this weekend on "CBS Sunday Morning." A portion of the interview was released late Friday.
