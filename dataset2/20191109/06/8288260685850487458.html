'Will be arrogant' - Wolves fans look towards Aston Villa clash

Wolves v Aston Villa | The two Midlands rivals meet at Molineux on Sunday looking for what will be a vital three points

09 Nov 2019, 06:00 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Wolverhampton Wanderers will be looking to make it seven matches unbeaten when they face Aston Villa on Sunday.

Nuno Espirito Santo's men haven't tasted defeat since they were hammered by Chelsea back in September. Wolves have drawn their last three though and will be looking to return to winning ways when they host Villa.

Villa meanwhile have lost two on the spin, including a heartbreaking 2-1 defeat to Liverpool last time out.

The two Midlands rivals are separated by only two points and will both be looking to move away from the relegation zone and towards the top half.

Dean Smith's men got the better of a much changed Wolves side in the Carabao Cup last month, but can exact some revenge in front of the Sky cameras.

We took a look on social media and popular fans' forum Molineux Mix, to get a flavour of how the old gold faithful are feeling:
