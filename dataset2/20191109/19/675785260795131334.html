Can you watch KSI vs Logan Paul for free? TV, YouTube and live stream information

Millions of people around the world will be tuning in to watch as KSI and Logan Paul settle their rivalry in the ring

09 Nov 2019, 19:00 by Aaron Flanagan, Martin Domin, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

KSI and Logan Paul will tonight look to establish themselves as the best YouTube boxer in the world when they clash in their rematch.

The social media stars first met in the ring last August when they fought to a controversial draw at a sold-out Manchester Arena.

The sequel has been a long time coming but the pair finally agreed terms earlier this year and were granted professional licences by the California State Athletic Commission.

That means they will get rid of the head guards and pull on 10oz gloves for the second instalment at the Staples Center in Los Angeles.

And there is plenty of star power on the undercard with world champions Billy Joe Saunders and Devin Haney in action.

Here is everything you need to know about the fights...

When is the fight?

The fight takes place on Saturday night, with the action taking place in the early hours of Sunday morning.

What UK time is the fight likely to start?

For those watching in the UK, KSI and Logan Paul are expected to step into the ring between 4am and 5am.

That is a local time of between 9pm and 10pm in Los Angeles, where the fight is taking place.

What TV channel is the fight being shown on?

If you are in the UK, the only place to watch the fight is on Sky Sports Box Office.

Will the fight be shown on YouTube?

No. Unlike their first meeting, the fight is only being shown on broadcasting channels.

Is there a way to watch the fight for free?

Unfortunately not. This is a completely pay-per-view event.
