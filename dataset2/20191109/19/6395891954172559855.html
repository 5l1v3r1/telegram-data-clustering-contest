Braintree Police seeking driver, car involved in hit-and-run

09 Nov 2019, 19:10 by EndPlay

BRAINTREE, Mass. - Braintree Police are searching for a driver and car involved in a hit-and-run on Thursday.

They said the car hit a woman in the area of 300 Ivory Street as she was crossing from the MBTA station to a parking lot around 7:45 p.m. She was struck and thrown to the ground and the car continued north on Ivory Street without stopping.

The woman was brought to a hospital with non-life threatening injuries.

The car was a dark-colored sedan, likely a 2008 to 2012 Honda Accord that is missing the driver's side-view mirror and likely has damage in the headlight area.

Anyone with information regarding the incident or the motor vehicle is asked to call the Braintree Police Department at (781) 794-8601.
