'Made an impact' - The three players dominating Birmingham City fans' thoughts - for very different reasons

Blues 0-1 Fulham | Mitrovic's goal was the difference as BCFC were beaten at St Andrew's

09 Nov 2019, 19:13 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

It was a tale of missed opportunities, refereeing errors and one big mistake as Birmingham City were beaten by Fulham at St Andrew's.

Blues put in a valiant performance against the Cottagers but were left to rue missed chances when Aleksandar Mitrovic capitalised on a Lee Camp error to poke home.

The Serbian scored after Camp dropped a cross right into the path of the striker, who didn't have to think twice about giving his side the lead.

Blues boss Pep Clotet admitted he would speak to their goalkeeping coach in order to establish whether it was an error or simply him taking the wrong option.

 

In more positive news, Jeremie Bela put in a bright performance on his debut only days after signing on the dotted line at the club.

Jefferson Montero, who has endured an injury ravaged start to his spell, also enjoyed a particularly bright performance in a losing effort.

They were three players dominating the chatter on social media following the game, highlighting the two attackers' good performances, whilst lamenting the crucial Camp error:
