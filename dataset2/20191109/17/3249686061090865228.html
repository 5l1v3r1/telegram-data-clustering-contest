Producer Primary releases MV for 'Bad High' featuring Jade

09 Nov 2019, 17:19 by olmal

Producer Primary has released a new single album, '3-PAKTORY01' on November 8 and subsequently released a music video for 'Bad High' featuring Jade on November 9. 

After a year and 7 months of hiatus, Primary returns with a brand new single album '3-PAKTORY01'. As a continuation from his numbered album series '2-1', '2-2', '2-3', and '2', his new album hopes to approach listeners with new sounds.
