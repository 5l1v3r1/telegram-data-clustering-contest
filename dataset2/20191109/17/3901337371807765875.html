10-Man Stellenbosch Hold Highlands

09 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Soccer Laduma

Absa Premiership

Result: Stellenbosch 0-0 Highlands Park

Date: 09 November 2019

Venue: Cape Town Stadium

Ten-man Stellenbosch managed to secure themselves a point after hold Highlands Park to a goalless draw in the Absa Premiership on Saturday afternoon.

Iqram Rayners and Waseem Isaacs retained their spot at the top of the Stellies attack, while Alan Robertson and Afoakwa were drafted into the line-up following the loss to AmaZulu this week.

Highlands have bit a of slump lately, which saw Mothobi Mvala, Thela Ngobeni and Sifiso Mbhele dropped to the bench, as Marlon Heugh, Mzava and Rodney Ramagalela were recalled.

However, Stellies coach Steve Barker was forced into an early change as Mobbie, on loan from Mamelodi Sundowns, sustained an injury and was replaced by Bergman.

After a slow start to the half, Highlands carved out the first chance at the midway point of the half but goalkeeper Boy de Jong produced a good save to keep the scores level.

While it was the visitors who enjoyed more of the chances, the home side were dealt a blow just before the halftime break as veteran defender Morgan Gould received his marching orders after picking up two yellow cards in the first half.

Despite being a man down, Stellenbosch held their own in the second half as the Lions of the North pushed for an opening goal in the first 15 minutes of the stanza.

With both sides struggling to get the breakthrough, changes were introduced on both ends as Solomons replaced Rayners for the home side while Highlands threw on Marks Munyai for Mbhele.

However, neither side could find an opening in the latter stages of the half as Stellenbosch find themselves in 13th spot, while Highlands remain in fifth spot.
