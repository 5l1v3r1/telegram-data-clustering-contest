Starting XI: Barca v Celta Vigo

09 Nov 2019, 20:55 by SL International

LaLiga

Teams: Barcelona v Celta Vigo

Date: 09 November 2019

Venue: Camp Nou

After being jeered by the own supporters this week, LaLiga leaders FC Barcelona aim to get back on the winning trail with the visit of Celta Vigo on Saturday evening - Here's how they line up.

The match is scheduled to get underway at 22h00.

The Catalan giants endured a frustrating evening on Tuesday after being held to a goalless draw by Slavia Praha in the UEFA Champions League, which left the home crowd unhappy.

The stalemate comes after a disappointing 3-1 loss at the hands of Levante last weekend, which saw Real Madrid and Sociedad move level on points with the Catalan outfit.

Celta Vigo, meanwhile, will be eager to back to winning ways as they come into the encounter on the back of a four successive loss in the Spanish top flight.

When these two sides last met, it was Celta who claimed a 2-0 victory at the Abanca-Balaídos although Ernesto Valverde's claimed the spoils in this fixture last season.

Here's how they line up:

FC Barcelona: Ter Stegen; Junior, Umtiti, Piqué, Semedo; Sergi Roberto, De Jong, Arthur; Ansu Fati, Griezmann, Messi.

Celta Vigo: Blanco, Juncà, Mallo, Aidoo, Olaza, Araujo, Beltrán, Diop, Lobotka, Aspas, Sisto.
