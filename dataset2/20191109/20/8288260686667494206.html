West Brom fans belt out resounding nine-word message at Hull City

These were the scenes at the final whistle as Albion kept their Championship promotion push rolling

09 Nov 2019, 20:26 by Joseph Chapman, https://www.facebook.com/josephchapmanBM/

"We are the Albion, we're top of the league..."

That simple nine-word message was belted out at full pelt at Hull City as West Bromwich Albion preserved their place at the Championship summit.

Jake Livermore's 28th minute striker proved to be the winner against his former club at the KCOM Stadium.

The 1-0 victory keeps the Baggies top of the pile going into the latest international break with 33 points from 16 matches, two ahead of nearest challengers Preston North End.

Slaven Bilic's league-leaders have a two-week wait for their next match, when they take on Sheffield Wednesday at The Hawthorns on Saturday, November 23.

But the buzz around the club will sustain them during the break with the Baggies faithful boinging their way back to the Black Country with a spring in their step.

They were certainly in fine voice up at Hull, with euphoric scenes as they added another three points to their promoton-chasing tally.
