14-man Cheetahs slip to third straight PRO14 loss

09 Nov 2019, 21:11

Cape Town - The Cheetahs slipped to a third consecutive PRO14 loss after going down to Welsh side Cardiff Blues at Cardiff Arms Park on Saturday evening.

As it happened | Cardiff Blues v Cheetahs

The hosts won 30-17, after leading 17-14 at half-time.

The Cheetahs lost No 8 Jasper Wiese to a red card early in the game after he performed a dangerous ruck clean-out on a Cardiff player.
