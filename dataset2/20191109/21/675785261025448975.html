Man and woman found dead in Birmingham home as murder detectives probe killings

We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story

09 Nov 2019, 21:50 by Chris Kitching, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/chris-kitching/

A man and woman were found dead in a house this evening.

Emergency services were called to a property in Moseley, Birmingham, just before 5.30pm.

Officers found a man and a woman inside, both with serious injuries.

Both were confirmed dead at the scene.

Homicide detectives are currently trying to establish how they came about their injuries.

At this stage they do not suspect anyone else to be involved and the couple were known to each other.

A cordon has been put in place at the property for forensic examinations and officers are speaking to neighbours.
