Skelton, Southern cruise to 58-7 win over Virginia Lynchburg

09 Nov 2019, 21:07 by The Associated Press

BATON ROUGE, La. (AP) — Ladarius Skelton scored on a keeper early in the first quarter and Southern cruised to a 58-7 win over Virginia Lynchburg on Saturday.

Skelton’s touchdown pass to Hunter Register with 5:54 left in the first quarter gave Southern (6-4) a 21-0 lead.

Virginia Lynchburg is a member of the United States Collegiate Athletic Association.
