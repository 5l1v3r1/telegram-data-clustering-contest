Woman killed, 3 others injured in car crash

09 Nov 2019, 21:42 by EndPlay

TAUNTON, Mass.Â (AP) - A 21-year-old woman has been killed and three others have been injured in a car crash in Massachusetts.

State Police say Lakeville resident Nicole Dondero had been a passenger in a car driving on Route 140 in Taunton that was struck by an oncoming vehicle at around 9:15 p.m. Friday.

Officials say the car appears to have been taking a left turn to enter the ramp for Route 24. Dondero died at the scene.

The driver of the car, a 22-year-old Acushnet man, was taken to Morton Hospital in Taunton with serious injuries.

The driver of the other vehicle and a passenger in that vehicle, were also taken to the hospital with minor injuries.

State Police haven't identified the others involved in the crash. The incident remains under investigation.
