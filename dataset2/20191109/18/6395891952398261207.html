Police ID woman, 21, who died in multicar crash in Taunton

09 Nov 2019, 18:56 by EndPlay

TAUNTON, Mass. - Police said Nicole Dondero, 21, of Lakeville, died at the scene in last night's multicar crash in Taunton that also injured three others.

State Police said that around 9:15 p.m., a Plymouth woman, 33, was driving on Route 140 northbound when her 2005 GMC Envoy collided with a 2013 Chrysler 200 that was turning left from Route 140 southbound to enter the ramp for Route 24 northbound.

The Plymouth woman and her 35-year-old male passenger were taken to Morton Hospital with minor injuries. The driver of the other car, a 22-year-old Acushnet man, was also brought to Morton Hospital with serious injuries.

Police are investigating the crash. No charges have been filed.
