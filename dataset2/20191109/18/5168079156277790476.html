09 Nov 2019, 18:43 by Times News Service

Muscat: A deep air depression that has been affecting weather over parts of the Sultanate has been named Haya. The Public Authority for Civil Aviation on Saturday warned that the depression would be accompanied by heavy rains and strong winds over certain parts.
