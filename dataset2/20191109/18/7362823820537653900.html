Pete Buttigieg eating cinnamon roll 'like a chicken wing' draws ridicule online

09 Nov 2019, 18:39 by Gerren Keith Gaynor

A photograph of Democratic presidential candidate Pete Buttigieg eating a cinnamon roll has gone viral on Twitter.

The seemingly innocent image shows the South Bend, Indiana, mayor enjoying a nice bite of a frosted cinnamon roll from Ruby’s Restaurant in Decorah, Iowa.

But when Twitter users caught wind of the photo, which many believed to be Buttigieg eating a chicken wing, it was quickly flagged as an apparent food faux pas.

“I don't care what your politics are, it is illegal to cut a cinnamon roll into pieces with a knife and then eat it like a chicken wing,” an outraged Twitter user wrote along with the now-viral picture.
