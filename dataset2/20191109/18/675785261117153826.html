Is KSI vs Logan Paul rematch on YouTube? Full TV and live stream information

KSI and Logan Paul face off at the Staples Center in Los Angeles in the early hours of Sunday morning

09 Nov 2019, 18:00 by Aaron Flanagan, Martin Domin, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

Two of the world's biggest social media stars clash in the ring tonight as KSI and Logan Paul meet in their long-awaited rematch.

The YouTube stars, who have more than 40million followers between them, will bid to settle their rivalry at the Staples Center in Los Angeles.

They fought to a controversial draw in Manchester last year and quickly agreed to a second fight.

But it took the involvement of promoter Eddie Hearn for the two men to agree terms and they have also turned professional for tonight's clash, ditching the head guards and pulling on 10oz gloves.

There are also plenty of big names on the undercard with world champions Billy Joe Saunders and Devin Haney in action.

Here is everything you need to know about the fights...

When is the fight?

The rematch between KSI and Logan Paul takes place in Los Angeles on Saturday November 9.

However, with the time difference, it will be in the early hours of the morning on Sunday November 10 for those in the UK.

What UK time is the fight likely to start?

The main event fight is not expected to start until between 4am and 5am in the morning.

It will be getting going between 9pm and 10pm local time.

What TV channel is the fight being shown on?

The fight is being shown on Sky Sports Box Office for those viewers in the UK.

It can be purchased for a price of Â£9.95.

Can you live stream the fight?

If you have purchased the fight from Sky Sports Box Office, you will be able to live stream the fight.

Please follow instructions from Sky as you complete your purchase.

Will the fight be shown on YouTube?

No.

Unlike their first meeting, this is going to be shown solely by the broadcasters who have the rights.

The first fight was shown on YouTube pay-per-view but they will not be following suit this time around.

However, there will be a pre-shown, Countdown to KSI vs Logan Paul 2, that will be shown on YouTube before the fight. It can be found on DAZN, KSI and Logan Paul's YouTube channels ahead of the bout.

Is there a way to watch the fight for free?

No.

The action is completely pay-per-view for this match.
