Chloe Lewis shows off incredible post-baby body transformation in scanty crop top

New mum Chloe took a night off her parenting duties when she flaunted her enviably-toned figure on the town

09 Nov 2019, 22:55 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Chloe Lewis stunned fans with her post-baby body transformation.

Her Instagram post almost marks a month since she welcomed her son into the world on October 13, yet Chloe has already snapped back into shape.

The new mum, who credited pilates training for her weight loss, proudly flaunted her astonishing figure for all to see on the gram.

In her latest snap, the reality star exhibited her enviably-toned physique in a lace crop top with a scalloped edge drawing attention to her bare stomach.

Chloe got her glam on for her night off her mummy duties to her newborn son.

The mum-of-one had done up her caramel-coloured tresses in springy curls and pulled on her heels.

She wrote: "The appreciation of a night out when your a mumma."

Chloe added the applause emoji to her caption.

The beauty is raising her son with her boyfriend Danny Flasher; he is the first child of the couple.

She is yet to reveal her son's name.

Previously, Chloe shared her weight loss secrets as she praised her pilates class.

She wrote: "It’s just MAGICAL how you can grow a perfect little human inside of you... they arrive & it’s as if they have been here forever #2weekspostpartum.

"(Big thank you to @zerogravitypilates for helping my bod bounce back with all the training I did before & while pregnant)."
