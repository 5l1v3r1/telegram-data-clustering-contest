David McGoldrick Controversially Denied First Premier League Goal By VAR

09 Nov 2019, 16:58 by Oisin McQueirns

Sheffield United and Ireland striker David McGoldrick was denied his first Premier League goal after VAR adjudged that his teammate John Lundstram was offside in the build-up.

The Blades were 1-0 down in their game against Tottenham away from home thanks to Son Heung-min’s 58th minute goal but looked as though they had grabbed an equaliser 20 minutes later when McGoldrick met Ireland teammate Enda Stevens’ cross from the left.

McGoldrick celebrated what would’ve been his first goal in the top flight, only for VAR to chalk it off for an offside in the build-up after over three minutes of deliberating.
