Police investigating after body found on Robinson Street

Officers were dispatched to the 800 block of Robinson Street just before 3:30 a.m. after receiving a report about a deceased man.

09 Nov 2019, 16:28 by Regina Leader-Post

Police are investigating the death of a man who was discovered dead on Robinson Street early Saturday morning.

According to a police news release, officers were dispatched to the 800 block of Robinson Street just before 3:30 a.m. after receiving a report of a deceased man.

When emergency crews arrived at the scene, paramedics confirmed the man was dead. Police then secured the scene and requested additional resources from the coroner as well as the forensic identification and major crimes units.

Few other details into the mans death were given but police expect more information to come to light as the investigation progresses.

Anyone with information that might aid in this investigation is asked to contact Regina police at 306-777-6500 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477 (TIPS).
