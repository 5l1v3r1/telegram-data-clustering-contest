Malta AI & Blockchain Summit Closing while BitTok Exhibits are Highly Welcomed

09 Nov 2019, 16:02

On November 7-8, 2019, Singapore time, the world’s third artificial intelligence and blockchain summit was held in the European island nation of Malta. Leaders from all continents, industry elites, and media representatives gathered to discuss the development of blockchain technology and digital assets. As a representative of global cryptocurrency exchange, BitTok was also invited to attend this summit.

Join hands with top experts, enterprises and investment institutions in the fields of financial technology and blockchain to collide with thinking sparks, explore technology and industry hotspots, and seek the future of the industry.

The total population of Malta, the host country of this summit, is only around 500,000, but it has a pivotal position in the digital asset trading market. As the first country to issue blockchain industry legislation and guidelines, and the first country with a sound regulatory system, Malta is seen as the Eden of the blockchain industry. It has attracted many internationally renowned digital asset trading platforms to settle here.

At this summit, Malta Prime Minister Joseph Muscat and Malta Parliament Secretary Silvio Schembri delivered a speech on behalf of the host government. Other guests also delivered wonderful speeches, including American well-known rapper Akon, famous Silicon Valley investor Tim Draper, blockchain investor Ian Balina, Bobby Lee and other influential peers.

Malta is fast becoming one of the best places in the world for blockchain companies. This time, BitTok will show all participants the ecological services, product and technology development progress, planning and so on. Taking this as an important time node, BitTok will actively raise funds to apply for financial licenses in the future, and at the same time aggressively enter the European market to further expand its global business scope.

At this summit, BitTok’s booth surrounded hundreds of participants and digital asset investors. They eagerly inquired about the staff of BitTok’s platform features. It is understood that BitTok is a cryptocurrency exchange headquartered in Singapore. The goal of the platform is to provide safe, convenient, professional and high-quality digital asset financial services to users worldwide.

At present, the core functions of the platform are storage, trade and appreciation. With the careful operation and continuous polishing since the launch, BitTok has become an indispensable digital asset “smart housekeeper” for individual and institutional investors.

Before attending the Malta Summit, the platform has brought together a large number of digital asset enthusiasts and believers all over the world to create an efficient, transparent, fair and win-win global ecosystem of digital asset trading. In the recent 2.0 international version of the App, the English version is more important than ever.

In addition to English, the platform also plans to support Japanese, Korean and other languages in the future, laying a solid foundation for building an international community.

At present, the application and innovation of blockchain technology has been booming, bringing a layer of vitality to the traditional financial market, and constantly promoting the growth and development of the digital asset market. BitTok will redefine digital assets as its mission.

Through professional technology and international operations, it will enhance the user service experience and spread the value of blockchain. Accelerate the pace of the digital asset industry and continue to move ahead in reshaping the value system of the digital economy era!
