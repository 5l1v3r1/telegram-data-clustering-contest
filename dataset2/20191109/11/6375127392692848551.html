Old man sleeping in store hurt in fire

09 Nov 2019, 11:31 by Itchie G. Cabayan

A SENIOR citizen was injured in a fire that hit a store of surplus appliances in Port Area, Manila early yesterday morning.

F/ Insp. John Joseph Jalique, chief investigator of the Manila Fire District, said victim Luis Yu, 78, suffered burns on the hands and body.

The fire broke out at around 3 a.m. at the New Port Area Commercial Complex in Port Area.

The victim was reportedly sleeping inside the store when the fire struck.  The blaze, which was contained at 3:44 a.m., damaged a parked car as well as six stalls selling refrigerators, aircondition units, washing machines, helmets and sewing machines.

The fire left an estimated P100,000 in damages.  Its cause is still unknown.
