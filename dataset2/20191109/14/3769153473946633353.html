New on Blender Artists: Events Calendar

09 Nov 2019, 14:35 by Bart Veldhuizen

Over on Blender Artists, we just added an Events calendar to Blender Artists to create a central place where you can find all upcoming things like meetups, workshops, conferences, challenges, etc! Feel free to add your upcoming events there to share them with the community.

I'm still figuring out the best way to promote these events on BlenderNation - either by a weekly post (like every Saturday or so), or by integrating a widget on the front page. What's your preference?
