Starting XI: Polokwane v Baroka

09 Nov 2019, 14:41 by Soccer Laduma

Absa Premiership

Teams: Polokwane City v Baroka FC

Date: 09 November 2019

Venue: Peter Mokaba Stadium

Limpopo will be divided on Saturday afternoon as Polokwane City host struggling Baroka FC at the Peter Mokaba Stadium.

View The PSL Table 

Baroka halted their five-game losing streak in all competitions following a 0-0 draw with Mamelodi Sundowns on Wednesday evening.

The draw did come at a cost as full-back Matome Kgoetyane was sent off in the latter stages after receiving a straight for a lunge on Tebogo Langerman.

Rise and Shine, meanwhile, have failed to build on their fine start to the season after they extended their losing run to five matches in all competitions on Wednesday after a 5-3 loss to Cape Town City in the Mother City.

Polokwane also have a suspension of their of their own for this afternoon's clash as Themba Chauke misses out after an accumulation of yellow cards.

The defeat to the Citizens also saw head coach Zlatko Krmpotić lose his job, as Bernard Molekwa takes charge this afternoon.

Rise and Shine did claim the double over their over provincial rivals after claiming 3-1 triumph in the last clash, while the reverse fixture ended 2-0.
