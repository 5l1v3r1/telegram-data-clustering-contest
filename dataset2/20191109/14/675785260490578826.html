What channel is KSI vs Logan Paul on? TV, YouTube and live stream information

The rivalry between KSI and Logan Paul has been brewing for the past year and they finally step into the ring again this weekend

09 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Aaron Flanagan, Martin Domin, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/aaron-flanagan/, https://www.facebook.com/mirrorfighting

KSI and Logan Paul return to the ring tonight for their eagerly-awaited rematch as the social media rivals look to finally settle their beef.

What began as a call out from KSI - real name Olajide William Olatunji - last year led to a sold-out first fight in Manchester which ended in a controversial draw.

The pair had planned to rematch early in 2019 but negotiations dragged on until promoter Eddie Hearn stepped in.

His involvement means both men have turned professional after being granted licences by the California State Athletic Commission,

And tonight at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, they will dispense with the head guards and pull on 10oz gloves.

They will also headline a show featuring world champions Billy Joe Saunders and Devin Haney.

Here is everything you need to know about the fights...

When is the fight?

The highly-anticipated rematch between KSI and Logan Paul takes place on Saturday November 9.

However, the main event won't be until the early hours of Sunday November 10.

What UK time is the fight likely to start?

If you are watching from the UK, the fight is likely to start between 4am and 5am.

That is about 9pm to 10pm local time in Los Angeles, where the fight is taking place.

What TV channel is the fight being shown on?

If you are in the UK, the fight is being shown on Sky Sports Box Office.

For those in the US, it is being shown on the DAZN streaming service.

Here is the full TV channel listing around the world:

  • UK and Ireland: Sky Sports Box Office
  • USA, Canada, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, Spain and Brazil: DAZN
  • Poland: TVP
  • Taiwan: Eleven Sport
  • Malaysia/Brunei: Astro
  • China: CCTV
  • New Zealand: Sky Network
  • Indonesia: Reddentes
  • Phillippines: Tap DMV
  • Latin America: Space
  • Rest of the World: FITE.tv

Can you live stream the fight?

In the UK, you can stream the Sky Sports Box Office coverage on their website if you have purchased it.

DAZN, for those in the USA, is a live streaming service for subscribers.

Will the fight be shown on YouTube?

It will not be shown on YouTube, unlike the first time that the fight took place.

Only on the channels listed above can the fight be watched.

Is there a way to watch the fight for free?

On this occasion, the fight is only being shown on a pay-per-view basis.

But you can follow the action as it happens on the Mirror Fighting live blog.
