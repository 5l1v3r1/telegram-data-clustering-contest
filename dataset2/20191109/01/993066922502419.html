Woman critical after Shore Road accident

09 Nov 2019, 01:00

A WOMAN in her 40s was taken to hospital after being knocked down on the Shore Road in Newtownabbey yesterday morning.

According to PSNI, the incident happened at around 6:50 close to the Regional College.

The woman was taken to hospital, where last night her condition was described as critical.

The Shore Road was closed for several hours following the accident but reopened yesterday afternoon.
