Lest we forget: Calgary Remembrance Day ceremonies listings

09 Nov 2019, 01:27 by Newsroom Staff

On Monday, Calgarians will pause at 11 a.m. to remember those who made the ultimate sacrifice in service of their country. Below are listings for some of the organized Remembrance Day services across Calgary.

Outdoor Services

Field of Crosses

A service among a field of over 3,600 memorial crosses representing the men and women of southern Alberta who died in service of their country.
10:30 a.m.;
Sunnyside Bank Park, off Memorial Drive

The Hangar Flight Museum

A service with a focus on Canada’s airborne contributions to peace and stability.
10:30 a.m.;
4629 McCall Way N.E.

Central Memorial Park

This ceremony will feature a parade with the Calgary Highlanders.
10:30 a.m.;
1221 2 St. S.W.

Battalion Park

Gather at what was once a First World War training camp known as Camp Sarcee, near the regimental numbers on Signal Hill, for a service of remembrance.
10 a.m.;
3001 Signal Hill Dr. S.W.

The Military Museums

Service to take place outside the museums, which will open after the ceremony. Admission is by donation.
10:30 a.m. arrival; Service at 11 a.m.;
4520 Crowchild Trail S.W.

Ogden Memorial (CP Railway Service)

This service pays tribute to the 33,000 Canadian Pacific employees who served in the two world wars. Trains will sound their whistles at the start of the moment of silence.
10:40 a.m.;
7550 Ogden Dale Road S.E.

Peacekeeper’s Park

A service with a focus on Canada’s many peacekeeping contributions during the Cold War.
10:30 a.m.;
3031 Dallaire Ave S.W.

North Glenmore Park

Hosted by the Canadian Forces, 40 members will conduct a military parade from the park’s west entrance starting at 10 a.m. They will march to the ceremony at the Canadian Expeditionary Force, 137th Battalion Memorial. The west entrance to the park will be closed from 10 a.m. to noon. Please use the east entrance.
10 a.m.;
7305 Crowchild Trail S.W.

Indoor Services

Southern Alberta Jubilee Auditorium

Doors open 9 a.m.; Ceremony begins at 10:30 a.m.;
1415 — 14th Avenue N.W., Calgary.

Fort Calgary

Doors open at 10 a.m.; Service begins at 10:30 a.m.;
(Free admission with a non-perishable food bank donation)
Fort Calgary’s Burnswest Theatre
750 – 9th Ave. S.E., Calgary.

Kerby Centre

Doors open at 10:15 a.m.; Ceremony and wreath-laying at 10:30 a.m.;
Refreshments to follow.
1133 — 7th Avenue S.W., Calgary.

Kensington Royal Canadian Legion

Doors open at 9:30 a.m.; Ceremony 10:50 a.m.;
1918 Kensington Road N.W.

Calgary International Airport

10:45 a.m.
Main Terminal, arrivals level by Door 17, beside Bistro on the Bow.
