Friday's Pats-Broncos game postponed due to ice issue

09 Nov 2019, 01:03 by Greg Harder

The Regina Pats announced about 20 minutes before game-time on Friday night that their scheduled WHL meeting with the Swift Current Broncos at the Brandt Centre was being postponed.

The last-minute decision was made due to “an ice-plant issue” which left the ice surface “unplayable.”

The game will be rescheduled at a later date and all tickets from Friday’s contest are redeemable. More ticket information will be released in the future.

Friday’s game would have been the first meeting of the season between the Pats (2-11-2-0) and Broncos (3-10-1-2).

Regina’s next action is a road game on Saturday night against the Winnipeg Ice. That’s followed by six scheduled days off before meeting the Prince Albert Raiders next Saturday at the Brandt Centre.
