Sexual Harassment

09 Nov 2019, 08:21 by BRIEF

TOWNSHIP, N.J. – A federal court in New Jersey ruled that a woman’s workplace sexual harassment claims against an IT consulting company should continue. She claims the CEO made constant sexually harassing comments, asked her to have a threesome and told her he only hired her because of her large breasts.
