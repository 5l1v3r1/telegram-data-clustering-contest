Student Loan Fraud

12 Nov 2019, 16:24 by BRIEF

SANTA ANA, Calif. — The FTC accuses American Financial Support Services, Arete Financial Group dba Arete Financial Freedom and a slew of other business and their operators of preying upon student debtors in a $43 million advance-fee debt relief scam, in federal court.
