Dhanush clinches three gold on debut, becomes India's first shooter with hearing impairment to win medals at open international meet

12 Nov 2019, 16:10 by Tushar Dutt

PUNE: Dhanush Srikanth couldn't have asked for a better international debut. Telangana boy clinched three gold medals at the ongoing Asian shooting championships in Doha on Tuesday.

With the medals, Dhanush, 16, became India's first shooter with hearing impairment to win medals at an open international meet. Dhanush secured top place in junior men's 10m air rifle individual, mixed-gender and team events.
