Kara Locks Horns With Leviathan in New Supergirl Episode 5.07 Promo

12 Nov 2019, 16:44 by Tudor Leonte

The CW has released a new promo for the upcoming Supergirl Episode 5.07, featuring the clash between Supergirl and Leviathan. The episode will air on November 17. The official synopsis of “Synopsis” reads as follows:

Supergirl (Melissa Benoist) finally learns the truth about Lena (Katie McGrath) while tangling with Leviathan. J’onn J’onnz (David Harewood) makes a discovery about his brother’s whereabouts.

Andi Armaganian directed the episode written by J. Holtham & Katie Rose Rogers.

Supergirl stars Melissa Benoist (Glee) as Kara Danvers/Supergirl. Mehcad Brooks plays James Olsen, with Chyler Leigh (Grey’s Anatomy) as Alex Danvers, as well as Jeremy Jordan as Winslow ‘Winn’ Schott, and Katie McGrath (Merlin) as Lena Luthor. The show also stars Jess Rath as Brainiac 5, and David Harewood as Hank Henshaw/J’onn J’onnz.

Based on the DC characters created by Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster, the series boasts executive producers Greg Berlanti, Sarah Schechter, as well as Robert Rovner, and Jessica Queller. Moreover, Berlanti Productions, in association with Warner Bros. Television, produces Supergirl.
