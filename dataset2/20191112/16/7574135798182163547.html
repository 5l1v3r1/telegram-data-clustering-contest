DaBaby on new song by Trippie Redd (who announced a 2020 tour)

12 Nov 2019, 16:29 by Andrew Sacher

The unstoppable DaBaby has hopped on a new Trippie Redd track, “Death,” which will appear on Trippie’s upcoming album A Love Letter To You 4. It comes with a psychedelic, strip club-themed video and you can check that out below.

Trippie has also announced a lengthy headlining 2020 tour, including a NYC show at Terminal 5 on February 23 (tickets on sale 11/15 at 10 AM). All dates are listed below.
