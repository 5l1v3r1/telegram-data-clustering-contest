Karachi: Effigy of Abhinandan placed in PAF museum

12 Nov 2019, 16:43 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

KARACHI (Dunya News) – A statue of Abhinandan, Indian pilot captured by Pakistan armed forces, has been placed in Pakistan Air Force (PAF) museum.

Remains of the Indian jet downed by PAF are also placed in the museum. Blueprints, ammunition, watch and uniform of captured Indian pilot are also placed in the museum.

Abhinandan’s MiG-21 jet was shot down by a Pakistani fighter during a clash over Kashmir. The plane crashed on the Pakistani side of the de facto border that separates the two sides of Kashmir, a Himalayan region that has been a source of hostility between the two countries since independence from Britain in 1947.
