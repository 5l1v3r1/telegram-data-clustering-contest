Check out Santa's Grand Arrival and Fireworks Spectacular at UTC Mall

12 Nov 2019, 16:14 by Teyonna Edwards

SARASOTA, Fla.—Help the big man in red kick-off the holiday season with Santa's Grand Arrival and Fireworks Spectacular. The free event will take place at The Mall at University Town Center (UTC) on November 15 from 6 to 7:30 p.m.

The celebration begins outside the mall’s West Grand Entrance. Enjoy shows and games from Circus Arts Conservatory and the Tampa Bay Lightning or partake in The Holidays on the Green’s ice skating and snow tubing.

Don’t forget to grab yourself some sweet treats and a light-wand before the dance party begins at 6:30! There will be some holiday characters out for you to meet and greet as well.

Santa will make his grand arrival at 7 p.m. to light up The Mall at UTC as well as more than 4 million Christmas lights throughout the UTC shopping districts. The night will end with a spectacular firework show!

The Mall at UTC is located at 140 University Town Center Drive in Sarasota. To learn more about this holiday event, click here.
