Mum who went missing found dead two days after being traced 'safe and well'

Emma Watson, 32, was found dead at her home on Sunday - after first being reported missing two days earlier

12 Nov 2019, 21:59 by Abigail O'Leary

A mum who went missing was found dead two days after being traced 'safe and well'.

Emma Watson was reported missing at around 3.30pm on Friday, November 8.

Later that night, police confirmed the 32-year-old from Wishaw, Scotland, had been 'traced safe and well'.

Sadly, Emma was found dead at her home just two days later on Sunday, November 10, reports the Daily Record.

There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.

Heartbroken loved ones have paid tribute to the mum on social media.
