'Flattering' - Aston Villa and Leeds United transfer target speaks out on rumours

Aston Villa transfer news | Also linked with Nottingham Forest, West Bromwich Albion and Nottingham Forest, the Coventry City starlet has addressed rumours linking him with a move

12 Nov 2019, 21:01 by Richard Cusack

Reported Aston Villa transfer target Sam McCallum has addressed rumours surrounding his future at Coventry City.

McCallum has been linked with Dean Smith 's side as well as Leeds United, West Bromwich Albion and Nottingham Forest.

The 19-year-old left-back has impressed this season as Mark Robins's side vie for promotion from League One.

McCallum has made 10 League appearances so far this campaign, with scouts flocking to Coventry's temporary home of St Andrew's - they are groundsharing with Birmingham City - to cast their eyes over the youngster.

He showed his versatility by playing on the left-hand side of midfield in Saturday's goalless draw with Accrington Stanley.

Robins has previously spoken of how the Sky Blues won't part easily with their prized asset and the man himself has had his say on the speculation.

“I have been aware of it but the gaffer has been keeping my feet on the ground and keeping my head at Coventry ," McCallum told CoventryLive .

“He just wants to keep my head down and progress at Coventry.

"It's flattering and shows I must be doing something right. It’s always nice to hear but I just want to do well for Coventry."
