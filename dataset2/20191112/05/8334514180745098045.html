Sirens sound in central Israel, warning of rockets from Gaza

12 Nov 2019, 05:32

TEL AVIV (Reuters) - Sirens sounded in central Israeli towns on Tuesday, the military said, warning of possible rocket launches from the Gaza Strip after Israel killed a top Palestinian militant there.

The Israeli news site Ynet said sirens were also heard in the commercial capital of Tel Aviv, but there was no immediate official confirmation.
