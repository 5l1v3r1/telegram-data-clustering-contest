Barca Stars To Be Paid Lifetime Salaries Thanks To Messi?

12 Nov 2019, 15:44 by Kurt Buckerfield

It has seemingly been revealed that, because of Lionel Messi's important goal against Manchester United in the 2008/09 UEFA Champions League final, all of the club's past and present winners of European titles are being paid a lifetime salary.

United were beaten 2-0 by the Catalan giants in Rome a decade ago, courtesy of goals from striker Samuel Eto'o and superstar Messi.

According to GiveMeSport, it was due to that game that then-president Joan Laporta set up a scheme that would see all of Barca's former and present players paid lifetime salaries, with more than 290 players believed to be on the payroll.

One of them is Juan Carlos Heredia, who spent five years with the Blaugrana between 1974 and 1979 and won the 1979 Cup Winners' Cup with the club, and he has since opened up about the agreement, one that he credits Messi for.
