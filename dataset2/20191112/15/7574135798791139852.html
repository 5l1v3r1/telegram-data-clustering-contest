Amanda Shires touring with L.A. Edwards in 2020

12 Nov 2019, 15:15 by Andrew Sacher

Amanda Shires released one of 2018’s best albums, and she’s been busy this year too, as both a solo artist and as one fourth of The Highwomen. Before the year ends, she’ll play Steve Earle‘s benefit concert at NYC’s Town Hall with Jason Isbell, Josh Ritter, and The Mastersons (tickets); then she’ll play Brandi Carlile‘s Girls Just Wanna Weekend in Mexico in Jan/Feb; and then she’ll go on a just-announced headlining tour with support from L.A. Edwards after that. It’s been dubbed the “Atmosphereless Tour.”

The tour with L.A. Edwards includes a Brooklyn show on April 19 at Music Hall of Williamsburg. Tickets for that show go on sale Friday (11/15) at 10 AM with an AmEx presale starting Wednesday (11/13) at noon.
