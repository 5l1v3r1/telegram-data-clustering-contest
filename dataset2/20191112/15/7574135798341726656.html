Caribou expand tour, add second NYC show

12 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Bill Pearis

Dan Snaith has taken Caribou out of hibernation, releasing “Home,” the project’s first single in five years, last month and prepping a 2020 tour. North American dates wrap up in March in NYC, and Caribou have just added a second show at Brooklyn Steel on 3/28. (The 3/27 show is sold out.) Tickets go on sale Friday, November 15 at 10 AM with an AmEx presale starting Wednesday (11/13) at 10 AM.
