Titans season 2 UK release date, cast, trailers & how to watch

Here's when season 2 of Titans arrives, along with all the trailers, and how to watch it outside the US - or without a DC Universe subscription

12 Nov 2019, 15:02 by Dominic Preston

The second season of the popular DC Comics TV show Titans is here - if you're in the US. Titans is based on the Teen Titans group, which includes Robin and a group of other young superheroes and sidekicks (in this instance Raven, Starfire and Beast Boy) teaming up to fight crime. 

Titans is a grim 'n' gritty take on the material (arguably better known for its light-hearted cartoons), and was the debut original show for DC Universe - the company's dedicated streaming platform. The show airs on DC Universe, which unfortunately is currently only available in the US at the moment. 

But there is a way to watch Titans in the UK and the rest of the world, and it's even possible to watch Titans without a DC Universe subscription in the US. Here's how, along with all the news, casting info, and trailers for the show so far.

When does Titans season 2 come to the UK?

Titans season 2 will has already aired in the US on the DC Universe streaming platform, which costs $7.99 per month, and also includes a selection of classic DC TV shows and films along with digital comics and bonuses like store discounts and special giveaways.

You can't get DC Universe outside of the US, but don't worry: Netflix has the global rights to Titans outside the US (and China) so as long as you have a Netflix subscription you can still watch it if you're not American. The only thing we don't know is when the show will air. However, season 1 of Titans first dropped on Netflix UK in December, so we'll likely see season 2 come out around a similar time, or maybe even early 2020.

If you're worried about waiting and want to be sure to watch the show immediately, but you're not in the US, check out our guide to accessing DC Universe from outside the US.

How to watch Titans without DC Universe

If you're in the US you might want to watch Titans season 2 but balk at the $7.99 monthly fee - which you'll have to pay for three full months if you want to watch the full show as it airs. The good news is that you can watch Titans without a DC Universe subscription - you'll just need a VPN and a Netflix account.

Since Netflix is distributing the show outside the US, anyone with a Netflix account can watch it - though you'll have to wait a bit, as it hasn't reached Netflix just yet. You just need to use a VPN and set the server to a country that has the show on Netflix - you might as well use the UK - and then you can access UK Netflix and watch Titans without paying extra for DC Universe.

How many episodes are there?

Each season of Titans includes 11 episodes, each of which release weekly on DC Universe, rather than the whole season arriving at once.
