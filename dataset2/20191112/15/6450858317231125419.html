The Outer Worlds Switch release date and news

Here’s all you need to know about Obsidian's sci-fi shooter RPG, The Outer Worlds, including when it's coming to the Nintendo Switch

12 Nov 2019, 15:08 by Lewis Painter

Published by Private Division, The Outer Worlds is an open-world shooter set in a corporate-run colony called Halcyon on the edge of the galaxy, but all is not as it seems. You play as a colonist that stumbles on a conspiracy threatening to destroy the colony, and in true Obsidian style, your actions will dictate the outcome of the game.

Interested? You should be, it's been one of the most eagerly anticipated titles of the year, and we gave it four stars in our review.

Here’s all you need to know about The Outer Worlds, from the latest gameplay news to the official launch trailer. And if you want to grab a copy, check out our guide to where best to buy the game.

When is The Outer Worlds coming out on Switch?

The Outer Worlds came out on PC, PS4 and Xbox One on 25 October 2019, but there's an extra wait for the Switch port.

First announced in July 2019, The Outer Worlds will arrive on Switch in early 2020, as publisher Private Division confirmed it's due in the financial year 2020, which ends on 31 March.

The Outer Worlds trailers

The official launch trailer for The Outer Worlds was unveiled just a week before release date, though Obsidian has been teasing the game for months. The trailer showcases the dangerous and wacky world the game is set in, and sets the tone for the game.
