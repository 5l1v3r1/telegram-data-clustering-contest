Coronation Street viewers want Gary, Robert and Geoff killed off

They're all liars and manipulators!

12 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Viewers of Coronation Street want Robert Preston, Gary Windass and Geoff Metcalfe to be killed off in the soap.

At the moment, all three characters aren't popular for different reasons.

Robert is currently living a double life, engaged to two women with one of them expecting his baby.
