Decibel’s Top 40 Albums of 2019

12 Nov 2019, 15:50 by Andrew Sacher

Best of 2019 season has begun! Earlier today, we got Rough Trade’s list, and now here’s Decibel, who always have the first major metal list of the year. As usual, it’s a mix of legends (Candlemass, Nocturnus AD, Abbath, Opeth, Darkthrone, Mayhem, Sacred Reich, Possessed), buzzed-about newer artists (Gatecreeper, Crypt Sermon, Tomb Mold, Lingua Ignota, Spirit Adrift, Devil Master), and plenty of the in-between (Baroness, Full of Hell, etc). And the #1 pick is an album that doesn’t come out until next week. As for albums you might argue are glaring omissions: no Tool or Alcest (who happened to be the only two metal — or at least formerly metal — bands on Rough Trade’s list). Check out Decibel‘s full list below, and pick up your copy of the magazine’s January issue here.
