Sbahle on regaining her memory: ‘I know who was responsible for my accident’

12 Nov 2019, 15:00

Nolusindiso Mgujulwa

Cape Town - Fitness influencer Sbahle Mpisane has been recovering after she was involved in a car crash in August 2018.

Speaking to DRUM, Sbahle said she has regained 90% of her memory and she remembers who was responsible for her car crash.

“I’ve regained 90% of my memory. I would often go to the scene where the accident happened to try torecall what happened.”

The 26-year-old said she remembers who is responsible for her crash but did not want to mention names.

“I am so angry because I want him to reach out to me or to at least ask me how I’m doing. I am 100% certain about what happened, I know who was responsible for my accident.

“The past three weeks I’ve been very emotional, not because of the guy but because I’ve been reflecting a lot on what has happened and I am grateful,” she told DRUM.

Sbahle promised her fans that she has a big announcement to make on 1 December.

“I don’t want to say what it is now, but on 1 December, I’ll be sharing what I’m working on, and when I do share, I know my fans will be happy for me,” she said.
