Two Buccaneers On Suspension Tightrope

12 Nov 2019, 08:06 by Soccer Laduma

Two Orlando Pirates players are walking a suspension tightrope after picking up yellow cards against Kaizer Chiefs at the weekend.

After captaining Pirates against Chiefs, defender Ntsikelelo Nyauza could soon be unavailable for coach Rulani Mokwena’s team, should he be booked one more time.
