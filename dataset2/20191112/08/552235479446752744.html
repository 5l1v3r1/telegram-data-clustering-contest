Pedestrian hit by car in Barrhead in rush hour crash

Police and ambulance services rushed to the scene.

12 Nov 2019, 08:49 by Julie Dunnett, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/julie-gilbert/

Emergency services rushed to Barrhead in Renfrewshire this morning after a pedestrian was hit by a car.

The accident happened just after 7am this morning.

Police were unable to provide an update on the condition of the person hit.
