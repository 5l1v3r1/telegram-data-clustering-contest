Indonesian teen singer Claudia Emmanuela Santoso crowned as ‘The Voice of Germany’ winner

12 Nov 2019, 08:25 by Coconuts Jakarta

One more Indonesian talent shines brightly abroad, and we’re here for it. Claudia Emmanuela Santoso, 19-year-old singer from the port city of Cirebon in West Java, was named winner of the ninth season of talent show The Voice of Germany on Sunday.
