Morbid Angel and Obituary were part of a question on ‘Jeopardy!’ last night

12 Nov 2019, 08:20 by Andrew Sacher

Florida death metal legends Morbid Angel and Obituary were part of a question on Jeopardy! last night (11/11). The category was “D.M.” and the answer was “Morbid Angel and Obituary are popular bands in this genre.” The guy seemed like he might have been taking a shot in the dark, but… he got it right!
