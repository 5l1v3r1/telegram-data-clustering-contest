Dubai's Coca-Cola Arena: all the confirmed shows so far

From One Direction stars to comedy greats, here’s what’s on the line-up

12 Nov 2019, 08:26 by Amy Mathieson

Concert-lover? Comedy fan? There are loads of top names coming to Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena in the coming months. Here’s what you need to be booking tickets for today.

November 15 – AR Rahman
Indian musician and composer AR Rahman is in town this week, playing at Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena on Friday November 15. The composer has won many awards, including two Academy Awards, two Grammies, four National Film Awards and a Golden Globe. "I always look forward to performing here," said Rahman. "Dubai is like a melting pot of cultures. Each time I come back, I get so much love from the people and it’s overwhelming. I am happy to announce my next concert and I'm accompanied by my talented band and singers. We all look forward to presenting a very special set of songs for you." Tickets start at just Dhs50, so get in quick if you’re a fan.
From Dhs50. Fri Nov 15. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, Al Safa Street, www.doneevents.com.

November 22 – Why Not! Mazzika
Massive Arabic music festival Why Not! Mazzika Festival is promising loads of top acts. Sharmoofers, Cairokee, Massar Egbari and more top artists are coming to town this month as part of music extravaganza Why Not! Mazzika Festival. Why Not! Mazzika Festival will be taking place at the huge new arena in City Walk on Friday November 22. Doors open at 6pm, with the concert taking place from 8pm. tickets start at just Dhs195, so book now.
From Dhs195. Fri Nov 22. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, Al Safa Street, Dubai, ae.bookmyshow.com, www.wearemcx.

December 20 – Lara Fabian
Having spent 30 years on stage performing some iconic tunes, French-Canadian-Belgian singer Lara Fabian has added Dubai as a stop on her world tour. Coming to the Coca-Cola Arena on Friday December 20, Fabian will be headlining the stadium as part of her 50 World Tour, to celebrate her 50th birthday. Famous for powerful ballads including Je T’aime and Je Suis Melade, Fabian will also be performing a number of tunes from her latest album Papillon, her 14th studio album.
Dhs295. Fri Dec 20. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, ae.bookmyshow.com

April 18, 2020 – Louis Tomlinson
One Direction’s Louis Tomlinson is coming to Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena next April, and tickets are now on sale here. Tickets start at Dhs295. The British singer will be stopping off in Dubai on April 18, 2020 as part of his world tour. Tomlinson will be playing Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena in City Walk on April 18 2020, and fans are sure to be thrilled with the news. As a member of One Direction, Tomlinson sold more than 100 million records, but he’s making it out on his own, and his debut solo album 'Walls' will be released on January 31 2020.
From Dhs295. Tickets on sale now. April 18, 2020. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, Al Safa Street, 800tickets.com.

January 20, 2020 – JO KOY
US comedian Jo Koy will be bringing his Just Kidding world tour to Dubai’s Coca-Cola Arena on Monday January 20. It will be the first time the comedy star has performed in Dubai. Tickets will start at Dhs195 and go up to Dhs695 and will be available to buy at www.coca-cola-arena.com. Famed for his stand up shows, his podcast The Koy Pond with Jo Koy by Starburns Audio and his recent Netflix special Comin’ In Hot, the comedian is currently on his first leg of the Just Kidding Tour – which promises all new material. He will also be performing in Australia, New Zealand, the US and Philippines. You may also know him from US talk show Chelsea Lately, in which he’s appeared in more than 140 episodes.
From Dhs195. Mon Jan 20. Coca-Cola Arena Dubai, City Walk, Al Safa Street, www.coca-cola-arena.com.

May 27, 2020 – Iron Maiden
Iron Maiden are set to perform their Legacy of the Beast world tour in May 27, 2020. With thirty-nine albums, which includes 16 studio albums, 12 live albums, four EPs, and seven compilations, the East London rockers have been rocking out since 1975. Yes, they’re pretty well known alright.
Expect them to perform some of their heaviest tunes, including The Trooper, Run To The Hills, 2 minutes to midnight and plenty more.
Prices vary. May 27. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, www.coca-cola-arena.com.

February 14, 2020 – Maluma
Maluma, the Latin Grammy Award-winning Columbian singer-songwriter will be heading to the UAE on Friday February 14 – so make it a date. With billions of YouTube streams, and nearly 100 million subscribers and followers across social media, the 25-year-old is one of the biggest artists in the world right now. His 2017 Maluma World Tour sold more than one million tickets across 105 concerts around the world, cementing his status as the world’s top concert-selling Latin artist. His 11:11 World Tour is promising a “state-of-the-art show”, full of cutting edge visual effects and technology, as well as a live band with 12 backing dancers.
Prices vary. Fri Feb 14, 2020. Coca-Cola Arena, City Walk, Dubai, www.800tickets.com.
