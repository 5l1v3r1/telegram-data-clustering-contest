I'm A Celebrity romances 2019: Single campmates most likely to couple up this year

One of this year's bunch has already boasted they're 'planning to embrace the single life'

12 Nov 2019, 08:58 by Lucy Needham, https://www.facebook.com/dailymirror/

There might be Bushtucker Trials, hunger pangs and creepy critters aplenty, but the I'm A Celebrity jungle has proved the unlikely setting for more than a few romances over the years.

Who could forget the early flirtations of Katie Price and Peter Andre who got hitched, had two children and transformed their careers after falling for each other on the ITV reality show.

So what are the chances of this year's bunch getting jiggy in the jungle?

While some are happily married (Kate Garraway, Jacqueline Jossa, James Haskell, Andrew Maxwell and Ian Wright) and others in living long-term relationships (Caitlyn Jenner and Roman Kemp ) ITV producers have thrown some stars into the mix who might be up for finding love down under.

Heading up the single bunch in camp this year is Myles Stephenson, 28.

The Rak-Su singer's last relationship was with Love Island 2017 star Gabby Allen , but the pair parted ways in mysterious circumstances back in August.

After confirming things were over between them, Gabby begged fans to pass on any information they had about Myles' unfaithful behaviour.

"The news paper articles are true. I am no longer in a relationship. I hope youse have all had a better week than me! [sic]

"If anyone else has anything they need to tell me, please do x" Gabby urged on Twitter .

While Myles has been very open about his relationship status.

After touching down in Australia this week, the former X Factor winner said he was planning to 'embrace single life' - whatever that means.

"I am single. I have just got out of a relationship but I think I want to be single for a bit and embrace single life!" he said.

Joining Myles on the singles bench is Girls Aloud legend Nadine Coyle.

The northern Irish star was married to professional american footballer Jason Bell. The pair have a baby girl together, Anaiya, who was born in 2014.

However, Nadine and Jason parted ways in September 2019 after 11 years together.

A source told the Sun the pair were 'not longer in a romantic relationship' and had been 'living separate lives'.

Before Jason, Nadine was linked to Desperate Housewives heartthrob Jesse Metcalfe.

I'm A Celebrity returns for its 19th series November 17 on ITV
