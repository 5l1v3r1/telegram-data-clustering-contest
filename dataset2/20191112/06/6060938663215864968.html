From Amitabh Bachchan to Rishi Kapoor, B-town celebs extend Guru Purab wishes

12 Nov 2019, 06:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Sikh Devotees around the globe are celebrating 550th birth anniversary of Sikhism founder Guru Nanak Dev. To mark this special day, People offered prayers and took a holy dip in the 'sarovar'. On this day, Gurudwaras are all decked up and seek blessings of Guru Nanak Dev ji.

Since midnight, social media is flooded with Guru Purab wishes. Our B-town celebs too took to their social media handles to wishes their followers. From Amitabh Bachchan to Rishi Kapoor, stars extended good wishes on Twitter.

Rishi shared pictures from his films in which he played the character of a Sikh man. He wrote, ''On the auspicious occasion of”Guru Pita Shree Guru Nanak Devjis” 550th birth anniversary. Prakash Purab di lakh lakh wadhaiyan!''. On the other hand, Big B wrote, ''“ਸਤਿਗੁਰ ਨਾਨਕ ਪ੍ਰਗਟਿਆ ਮਿਟੀ ਧੁੰਧੁ ਜਗਿ ਚਾਨਣੁ ਹੋਆ।” “SatGuru Nanak Pargateya Mitti

Dund Jag Chanan Hoya!”#GuruPurabGreetingsToAll#GuruNanakJayanti #550YearsOfGuruNanakDe''.
