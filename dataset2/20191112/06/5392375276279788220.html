Huawei P30 , P30 Pro get yet another EMUI 10 Beta update
4.2 (84.62%) 13 votes

Huawei is reportedly now rolling out a new EMUI 10 Beta for the Huawei P30, and P30 flagship duo. This comes mere hours after the Chinese variants of both devices started receiving stable EMUI 10 update. The new update comes in the form of EMUI 10 Beta update meant for users of the P30, and P30 Pro outside of China, and could be the final Beta before Huawei finally rolls out the stable EMUI 10 update.

According to HuaweiCentral, the EMUI10 beta update comes with the 10.0.0.168 build and weighs in at 600MB. It bumps the security level from October to November, while also addressing a number of issues. See the full changelog below;

EMUI 10.0.0.168 Beta update changelog

Applications:
Fixed an issue that caused some system and third-party applications to freeze or close unexpectedly.

Call:
Fixed call errors in certain situations.

User Interface:
Fixed an issue where some UI elements would not display correctly in some situations.

Security:
Improved system security with November 2019 security patch.

You can manually check for the update by going to Settings > System> Software update > Check for updates, otherwise you can check viaÂ HiCare app > tap on Update > check for updates.

We are not sure when the stable update will begin to roll out for users outside of China. But with this Beta update, and the stable EMUI10 already available in China, a global rollout is very much not far off.
