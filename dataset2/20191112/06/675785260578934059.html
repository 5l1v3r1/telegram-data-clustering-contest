Amanda Holden enjoys a 'school night' drink with Alesha Dixon and Kelly Hoppen

Amanda Holden has taken a break from her busy schedule to enjoy a glass of bubbly with fellow Britain's Got Talent judge Alesha Dixon and Dragon Den's Kelly Hoppen

12 Nov 2019, 06:34 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Amanda Holden paused her busy schedule to enjoy a 'school night' drink with two of her best friends.

The 48-year-old Heart Radio Breakfast show presenter took time out to relax and unwind in the company of her fellow Britain's Got Talent judge, Alesha Dixon and former Dragon's Den star, Kelly Hoppen.

The trio were all smiles in the pic that Amanda shared with her followers on Instagram , as they enjoyed what looks like a glass of bubbly as their 'school night' out got off with a sparkling start.

Amanda's scooter was out of sight as she leaned between the pair for the cheeky snap.

The radio presenter has been keeping mobile with the help of the three-wheeled contraption, after she broke her leg in two places in an accident while on holiday.

Amanda was looking as resplendent as ever, in a black halter neck outfit which featured a cut out.

Alesha meanwhile went for a bright yellow blazer and Kelly looked glamorous in a shimmering gold dress.

Amanda captioned the image of the trio on Instagram: "Out on a #schoolnight so wonderful to catch up with these two lovely friends @aleshaofficial @kellyhoppen."

Kelly also shared the fun and posted the picture, saying: "Girls night out with these two gorgeous friends always such a laugh #goodforthesoul @noholdenback @aleshaofficial."

Alesha was a little more refined when sharing the pic as she just titled it simply: "Ladies Night"

The threesome were also enjoying some time off from being mothers for the evening, with them sharing five children between them.

Amanda has daughters Alexa Louise Florence, 13, and Holly Rose, 7; Alesha's has newborn daughter Anaya Safiya and Azura Sienna, six; and Kelly has daughter Natasha Corrett.
