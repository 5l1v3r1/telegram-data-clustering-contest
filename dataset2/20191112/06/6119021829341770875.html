Two trains collide in Hyderabad, 16 injured

Commuters of the two trains were seen running out of the train after the mishap

12 Nov 2019, 06:58 by mid-day online correspondent

Hyderabad: In a shocking incident, two trains collided near Kacheguda railway station in Hyderabad on Monday, leaving 16 injured including the driver of a train who was trapped in the mangled remains of his cabin, officials told PTI.
