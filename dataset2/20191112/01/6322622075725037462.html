Ringgit opens higher against US dollar on US bond yield concerns

12 Nov 2019, 01:45 by Bernama

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 12): The ringgit opened higher against the US dollar today on concerns over US bond yields.

At 9.10 am, the ringgit was quoted at 4.1380/1420 versus the US dollar against 4.1421/1450 on Monday.

Axi Trader Asia-Pacific market strategist Stephen Innes said with tonnes of trade optimism baked into the US rates, the risk for the US dollar is that the explosion in US bond yields has run too far ahead of the economic realities. - Bernama
