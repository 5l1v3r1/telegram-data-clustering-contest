Orvilla Rae WhiteEagle

12 Nov 2019, 20:52

Orvilla WhiteEagle, age 53 of Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin walked on Monday, November 11, 2019 at her residence surrounded by her family and friends.

Funeral services will be held Thursday, November 14, 2019 at 11:00 a.m. at the Native American Church, N881 Highway 12 & 16, Wisconsin Dells with Greg Blackcoon officiating. Burial will be at Spring Grove Cemetery. Visitation will be held at the Native American Church on Wednesday from 3 to 5 p.m. and on Thursday from 9 a.m. until the time of service at 11 a.m.

Orvilla was born January 22, 1966 in Winnebago, Nebraska the daughter of David Walker, Jr. and June WhiteEagle. Orvilla enjoyed visiting the HoChunk Casino, taking care of her grandchildren and watching the Green Bay Packers.

Orvilla is survived by her daughter, Owenita (Glen) Lee; sons, Tyler (Samantha) Cloud, Travis (Elaina) Cloud and Ryan Cloud; sisters, Rose Walker and Susie Redfern; brothers, David (Carri) WhiteEagle, Taylor (Pandora) WhiteEagle, Henry (Joyce) Whitethunder, Leonard Walker and Richard (Anita) Walker and 12 grandchildren.

She was preceded in death by her parents; brothers, Alan and Kevin; and sisters, Darlene, Jenny and Lisa.
