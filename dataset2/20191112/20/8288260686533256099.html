'Be concerned' - Aston Villa fans send stark warning to Dean Smith

Aston Villa latest news | After Sunday's 2-1 defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers the claret and blue faithful have had their say on the injury situation at Villa Park

12 Nov 2019, 20:25 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa fans have given their verdict on the club's current injury situation.

Dean Smith saw his side depleted when Jed Steer and Matt Targett limped out of Sunday's 2-1 defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers .

Steer came off just seven minutes into the Premier League clash at Molineux, while Targett was substituted for Neil Taylor in first-half injury time.

First choice goalkeeper Tom Heaton missed out through injury, as did captain Jack Grealish and starting centre-back Bjorn Engels.

Ezri Konsa made his debut beside Tyrone Mings, while Steer was drafted in for his first League appearance of the season.

Smith has been handed a boost by the improving condition of Jota , who is back running after a hernia operation last month.

Fortunately, Villa have two weeks to get their injured bodies back up to full speed before the next game, a Monday night showdown with Newcastle United in B6.

And the claret and blue faithful have had their say on the situation on social media.
