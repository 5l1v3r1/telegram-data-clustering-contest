Shots fired reported during disturbance in Fitchburg; police find shell casing

12 Nov 2019, 20:08 by Brandon Arbuckle

FITCHBURG, Wis. - Police are investigating a shots fired incident that happened in Fitchburg Tuesday.

According to a news release from the Fitchburg Police Department, officers were sent to the 2000 block of Pike Drive at 10:52 a.m. for a report of shots fired.

During their investigation, police learned there had been a disturbance between multiple young men in the area. Shots were fired during the disturbance. 

Authorities found a shell casing on the roadway.

No injuries or property damage have been reported, and no one is in custody at this time. 

Anyone who witnessed the incident is encouraged to call the Fitchburg Police Department at 270-4300. Callers can stay anonymous.

An investigation is ongoing.
