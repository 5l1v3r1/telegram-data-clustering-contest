CBD products stolen during smash-and-grab burglary, Madison police say

12 Nov 2019, 20:34 by Tabatha Wethal

MADISON, Wis. - Burglars stole merchandise from a west Madison store Monday night, according to a news release. 

The Madison Police Department said officers responded to the Paradise Plus on Mineral Point Road at 11:30 p.m. 

Officers found a store window had been broken out. 

Police said surveillance images show two people retrieving a projectile before leaving with merchandise made from CBD, or cannabidiol, a compound found in hemp plants.
