Photo: Katy Perry arrives in Mumbai ahead of music concert

12 Nov 2019, 03:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

American singer-songwriter Katy Perry was all smiles as she touched down in Mumbai city, ahead of her debut gig at a music festival in the city, set to take place over the weekend. Rocking a de-glam avatar, the songstress was clicked at the airport, in grey sweatpants and a matching hoodie. With a cap on her head and dark glasses on, she put on her best smile for the cameras as she pushed her own bag to her waiting car.

While her big gig is still days away, Katy is sure to busy herself with some touristy stuff and as reported earlier, will party it up with Karan Johar and his Bollywood gang. The director is reportedly hosting a grand cocktail bash for the singer at his residence in Mumbai later this week.

Although this is her first visit to Mumbai city, Perry is no stranger to the country. She previously performed at the opening ceremony of a T20 League in 2012 in Chennai. Before that, she even visited the country on a personal trip with her then actor-comedian beau Russell Brand, who reportedly proposed to her at the Taj Mahal. They later tied the knot in a lavish ceremony in Rajasthan but sadly parted ways two years later.

Also expected to arrive in the city is British Pop sensation Dua Lipa, who will also be performing at the big gig.
