Rejoice: All Three Seasons of ‘Broadchurch’ Are Coming To SBS On Demand

12 Nov 2019, 03:12 by Joseph Earp

I was in the UK on the day that the last episode of Broadchurch season one aired on television, and I can safely say that I’ve never seen anything quite like it.

It is all that anybody talked about. People stopped each other on the street, grilling one another for clues about who they thought the killer might be. A man at the corner shop loudly complained about how his son had spent the last three days talking about nothing but Broadchurch. A ghastly pub I wandered into was playing the thing on television, like it was a sports match. The place was packed, and deathly silent.
