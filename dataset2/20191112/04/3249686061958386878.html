Fancam SuperM “Jopping” in Fort Worth, TX

12 Nov 2019, 04:38 by BTSARMY34342

SuperM’s first leg of their “We Are The Future” tour kicked off in Fort Worth, TX on November 11, 2019. Performing hits from their album as well as a few individual acts, SuperM proved that they may just be the Avengers of Kpop after all. 

SuperM closed their show with their title song, “Jopping”.
