Veterans Day: Pacoima honors veterans with largest parade in LA County

12 Nov 2019, 04:27 by Carlos Granda

PACOIMA, Calif. (KABC) -- Pacoima held its 16th annual Veterans Day parade on Monday, honoring veterans from various generations. The parade, the largest in L.A. County, drew nearly 20,000 people who came out to pay their respects to the men and women who serve our country.
