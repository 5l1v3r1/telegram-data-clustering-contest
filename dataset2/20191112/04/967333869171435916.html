American Airlines Flight Slides Off Snowy Runway, Over 1,000 Flights Canceled From Chicago - Video

The incident, during which luckily no one was hurt, was blamed on icy conditions, with even more heavy snow expected in Chicago in the coming days, according to meteorologists.

12 Nov 2019, 04:16

A plane belonging to American Airlines has pulled off the runway during landing at O'Hare International Airport in Chicago, the ABC broadcaster reported, citing American Airlines.

The video of the slide spread across social media, as it was posted by one of the American Eagle flight 4125 passengers.
