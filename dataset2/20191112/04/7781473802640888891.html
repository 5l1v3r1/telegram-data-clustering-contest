Image gallery: 2020 Skoda Octavia

12 Nov 2019, 04:24 by Team OD  

After many teasers, Skoda has unveiled the 2020 Skoda Octavia globally. This is the fourth generation of the car, and it is expected to make its India debut by the end of 2020. The new car is now larger, adds more tech, gets new engine options, a hybrid version and adds a more upmarket feel to the interior.
