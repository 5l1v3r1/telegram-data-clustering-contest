Sean Spicer eliminated from 'Dancing With the Stars'

12 Nov 2019, 04:44 by Nate Day

After a season of controversy, former White House press secretary Sean Spicer was eliminated from "Dancing With the Stars" on Monday.

The 48-year-old political aide latest dance with partner Jenna Johnson, set to Destiny's Child's "Bills, Bills, Bills," earned him only 26 out of 40 possible points from the judges.
