Letlotlo Surfaces At New Club, Next Stop Pirates?

12 Nov 2019, 10:50 by Soccer Laduma

Former Kaizer Chiefs youngster, Emmanuel Letlotlo, has surfaced at Orlando Pirates as he continues his search for a new club. 

Since leaving Chiefs at the end of last season, Letlotlo is yet to find a club in the Absa Premiership.

The 23-year-old striker was recently training with Bloemfontein Celtic, and although it was thought that he impressed in training, there was no agreement regarding a possible deal between his representatives and the Mangaung outfit. 

News reaching the Siya crew is that Letlotlo is training with Pirates’ ABC Motsepe League side Pele Pele FC.   

“Emmanuel Letlotlo is training with Orlando Pirates’ ABC Motsepe League team Pele Pele FC,” said the source.
