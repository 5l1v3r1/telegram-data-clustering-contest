Sting nets P6.8M shabu

12 Nov 2019, 10:55 by Alfred P. Dalizon

A CONTROLLED delivery operation in Makati City late afternoon yesterday led in the seizure of a kilo of shabu worth P6.8 million, Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency chair, Director General Aaron N. Aquino said.

Aquino said that arrested during the 4 p.m. operation at no. 8456 Motor Sports Bar located at Dona Carmen Street corner Kalawaan Avenue, Poblacion, Makati City were Edison Chua and Brian Bantegui.

Members of the PDEA National Capital Region and the Makati City Police Station made the arrest.

The suspects are now being held at the PDEA headquarters in Quezon City facing non-bailable drug charges, Aquino said.
