2 sisters held for drugs

12 Nov 2019, 10:54 by Melnie Ragasa-Jimena

TWO sisters, one of them already a senior citizen, were arrested in a buy bust operation in Quezon City on Tuesday morning.

A report from the Galas Police Station showed that the suspects identified as 61-year old Carmen Estrada, and her sister Lilibeth Estrada, 59, were arrested in a drug bust along with another suspect identified as Joe Vincent Realiza, 40, who was the main subject of the buy bust operation.

The suspects both residents of Bgy. Sto. Niño, Quezon City were arrested by operatives of Galas Police Station (PS 11) Station Drug Enforcement Unit (SDEU) at about 5:30 a.m., Tuesday at 76B San Isidro St., Bgy. Sto. Niño.  

PS 11 Officer-in-Charge PMAJ Bernardo Pascua Jr said the suspects were included in the barangay drug watch list and were also drug surrenderees.
