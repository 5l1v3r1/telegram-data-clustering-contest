Man, 22, in critical condition after being found unconscious under Whitleigh Bridge

A police cordon is in place

12 Nov 2019, 10:29 by Rom Preston-Ellis, Katie Timms

A man is in a critical condition in hospital after being found under Whitleigh Bridge earlier this morning.

Police have confirmed they were called at 7am this morning following concerns for the welfare of the 22-year-old man who had been seen under the bridge.

He was rushed to Derriford Hospital where he remains in a critical condition.

A police cordon remains in place below the bridge.

A Devon and Cornwall Police spokesperson said: "Police were called at around 7am with a report of concern for the welfare of a man who was found unconscious under a bridge in Whitleigh Way, Plymouth.

"The ambulance service also attended the scene and the 22-year-old man has been taken to Derriford Hospital, where he is in a critical condition.

"A police cordon is in place at the scene and officers are investigating the circumstances surrounding the incident."

A local resident said earlier that the area is "covered with police".

They said: "I was walking my dog and the area is covered with police and there is a massive police cordon underneath the bridge. There is a massive cordon directly under the bridge.

"There was about seven [police officers] and two ambulances."

A spokesperson for South Western Ambulance Service said: “We were called at 7.08am and attended with two ambulances.

“One patient has been taken to hospital for further treatment.”
