PIC: Sara Ali Khan visits Golden temple to seek blessing on Guru Nanak Jayanti

12 Nov 2019, 10:32 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Everybody is celebrating 550th birth anniversary of Sikhism founder Guru Nanak Dev. To celebrate the auspicious occasion, Gurudwaras are all decked up and seek blessings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji.

A while ago, Sara Ali Khan also shared a beautiful picture with her friend from the Golden Temple and wished her fans on the occasion of Gurupurab. In the picture, the actress is seen posing in front of the temple along with her BFF wearing simple casual outfits.
