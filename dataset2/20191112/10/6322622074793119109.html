Financial Times editor Barber to step down after 14 years in charge

12 Nov 2019, 10:09 by Reuters

LONDON (Nov 12): Lionel Barber will step down as the editor of the Financial Times in January, ending a 14-year tenure that was marked by the sale of the newspaper to Japan’s Nikkei and a huge push to online subscription.

“I expect my successor to be announced shortly,” he said in a statement. Barber has spent the last 34 years working at the salmon pink newspaper.
