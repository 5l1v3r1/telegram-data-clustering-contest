'I hope' - Chelsea star makes Aston Villa admission

Aston Villa latest news | The AVFC record signing was a surprise call-up to the Brazilian international side

12 Nov 2019, 09:13 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Chelsea star Willian has revealed he has been following Wesley's early Premier League performances ahead of his maiden Brazil call up.

The Aston Villa striker was a surprise name in Brazil's latest squad for the friendly fixtures against Argentina and North Korea.

Since arriving on B6 for a club-record £22m fee, Wesley has netted four times in 12 top flight performances, but still has his fair share of claret and blue doubters.

A number of fans continue to call for Dean Smith to look at other options to lead the line, including splashing the cash in the January window.

But Willian, who will join up with Wesley this week feels he has adapted well to English football.

“He’s doing great, I’ve been following his games a little,” Willian told Globo Esporte.

“He is a player who is very well adapted, looks like he’s been in England for a while, scoring goals. I hope he can be happy in the Brazilian team too.”

The decision has gone down controversially in his homeland, as the former Club Brugge striker has never played for a top flight club in Brazil.

Earlier this week, Wesley posted an emotional message, after the news emerged: “I am living a very special moment and I have no words to describe my happiness.

 

“I always dreamed of representing Brazil and I will have the possibility of realising this dream.

“I have had a hard time getting here and I will never forget the people who have helped me since the beginning of my career.

 

“I thank all those who have always believed in my football and the clubs that have trusted me. I can only thank my family, who are by my side in good times and bad times, and my managers.

“I always kept my feet on the ground and now it will be no different. I will do my best to prove that I can contribute to the National Team.”
