Doug's Gym in Dallas: a five-decade workout – in pictures

12 Nov 2019, 09:45

Doug Eidd opened his gym in 1962 in downtown Dallas, and over the years collected an eclectic membership. Photographer Norm Diamond photographed the last months of the gym, capturing the daily life and decay before its closure in 2018. Doug’s Gym: The Last of Its Kind is published by Kehrer
