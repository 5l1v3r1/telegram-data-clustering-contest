South Club get slapped in 'Twice' MV teaser

12 Nov 2019, 09:08 by germainej

South Club have dropped their music video teaser for "Twice"!

The MV teaser starts out with South Club vocalist Nam Tae Hyun being slapped in the face. "Twice" is a rock ballad about wanting someone's love to come around again, and it releases on November 15 KST.
