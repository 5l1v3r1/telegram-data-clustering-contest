Isolated rain over some parts of Oman

12 Nov 2019, 09:32 by By Times TV

Muscat: There is a chance of isolated rain in south Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta and Dhofar governorates, Public Authority of Civil Aviation PACA announced on Tuesday.

PACA said in a statement online: "The aerial picture shows the spread of scattered clouds on parts of South Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta , Dhofar and parts of Al Dakiliyah and Al Dhairah deserts.

“Tuesday’s weather forecast is clear to over most of Sultanate governorate with chance of isolated rain over south Al Sharqiyah, Al Wusta and Dhofar governorates,” the statement added.
