Video: Katy Perry says she wants to watch Salman Khan-Jacqueline Fernandez starrer ‘Kick’

12 Nov 2019, 09:19 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

American singer-songwriter Katy Perry is in Mumbai and will be performing at an upcoming music festival. But before she entertains fans in the city, Katy attended an event with Jacqueline Fernandez today. In a BTS video shared by Jacqueline, the Bollywood actress spoke about her massive fan moment while Katy went on to claim, “I am going home tonight to watch ‘Kick’.” One interaction later it seems like Katy has become a fan of Jacqueline and is eager to see her acting chops in the Salman Khan starrer.

Apart from Jacqueline, Katy will be meeting several other Bollywood celebrities too during her trip. Karan Johar, who is known for his lavish parties, will be hosting a cocktail and dinner for the singer. Who’s who of Bollywood is expected to attend the do and party the night away with Katy. Previously, KJo has hosted a bash for celebrities like Will Smith and filmmaker Darren Aronofsky.

Expressing her excitement ahead of the big act, Katy had said in a statement, “I am so happy to be returning to India and excited to headline my first-ever performance in Mumbai. I am looking forward to seeing and singing with all of my Indian KatyCats.”
