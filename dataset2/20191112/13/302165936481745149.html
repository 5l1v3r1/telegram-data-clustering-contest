Weezer shares new video for ‘California Snow’ (VIDEO)

12 Nov 2019, 13:58

LOS ANGELES, Nov 12 — California Snow will appear during the closing credits for the upcoming film Spell, which will arrive in select US theatres November 15 and on-demand services on November 19.

This dark comedy thriller stars Barak Hardley as a grieving American illustrator who goes on a soul-searching journey in the Icelandic countryside after the sudden death of his fiancee (portrayed by Jackie Tohn).

As he runs out of medication, he quickly wonders whether his compulsions are related to his mental disorder or if he is drawn into the supernatural world of ancient runes.
