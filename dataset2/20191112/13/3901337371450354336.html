PSL Club Coach Search In Progress…

12 Nov 2019, 13:53 by Soccer Laduma

The search for a new coach is ongoing for one of the Absa Premiership clubs that recently parted ways with their mentor.

Following a string of poor results in recent matches, Polokwane City relieved Serbian coach Zlatko Krmpotic of his coaching duties at the club last Friday.

Bernard Molekwa, who has been Krmpotic’s assistant and Rise and Shine team manager, took charge of the team’s match against Baroka FC in the Limpopo derby on the weekend.

Rise and Shine lost 1-0 against Bakgaga at the Old Peter Mokaba Stadium on Saturday. The indication from sources is that the club is seeking a replacement outside the borders of South Africa.

“The search for a new coach for Polokwane City is in progress. The club is looking for their man from outside of the country. The club is likely to appoint a foreign coach again if they succeed in their search,” said the source.

Krmpotic had replaced Slovakian coach Jozef Vukusic at Rise and Shine, when he took over the reins at the start of the season.
