'Difficult times' - Aston Villa hero's message for the man who took his place

Aston Villa latest news | Despite three losses on the spin, the January signing has earned plaudits for his recent performances

12 Nov 2019, 13:44 by Ben Husband, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/ben-husband/

Frederic Guilbert has been lavishly praised, from one of the men he has replaced.

The Aston Villa defender has been in fine form this season, after signing in January, the Frenchman ended the season at his beloved Caen as he tried, unsuccessfully to battle against relegation.

Alan Hutton was the man that occupied the right back slot at Villa Park for much of the promotion season, until an injury all but ended his final season as a Villa player.

The former Scotland international left the club in the summer, but has been able to watch his replacement flourish in his fledgling Premier League career.

Guilbert is walking a disciplinary tightrope in the coming weeks. After picking up his fourth booking of the season in the defeat at Wolves, he must now navigate the next seven fixtures without picking up a card, if he is to avoid a one-match ban.

It is an area of game that will be pinpointed for improvement, the 24-year-old has picked up a staggering 25 cautions since the start of the 2017/18 season.
