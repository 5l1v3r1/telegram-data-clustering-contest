Federal cabinet meeting underway to analyse political, economic situation

12 Nov 2019, 13:13 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prime Minister Imran Khan on Tuesday is presiding over a meeting of the federal cabinet in Islamabad to deliberate on a seven-point agenda.

Reportedly, the matter of removing former premier Nawaz Sharif’s name is not included in the today’s discussion.

The decisions made during the Economic Coordination Committee (ECC) meetings held on November 6 and 8 will be ratified.

The federal cabinet will approve appointment of OGRA members. Under the Islamabad Health Care Regulation Act, the appointment of board members of the authority will be approved.

The federal cabinet will also examine progress achieved on the earlier decisions taken by it.

It will be briefed on the resolution of complaints filed by overseas Pakistanis at the Pakistan Citizens Portal. Importantly, the cabinet will also analyse the current political and economic affairs of the country.
