The fishing ship Divina del Mar after it ran aground near Castro de Barona, in the town of Porto do Son, Galicia, northwestern Spain. One of the crew members died after he jumped from the ship during the accident

Santiago, Chile

Child model Chen Ruoxi has a head dress fitted backstage before the Van Monfe show during the China Fashion Week. It is just one of 35 shows dedicated to children’s fashion during the week

Venice, Italy
