Drake Drops Tongue-In-Cheek Response To Being Booed Offstage

12 Nov 2019, 23:56 by Joseph Earp

Drake is having a pretty tough time of it.

Sure, he might be the biggest rapper on the entire planet, a global megastar who tops all the streaming charts. But earlier this week, he suffered through about one of the most humiliating experiences it is possible for a performer to suffer through — he had to stand there onstage while an audience loudly booed him.

It all went down at Tyler The Creator’s Camp Flog Gnaw, where the audience expected to be serenaded by A Secret Performer who they had convinced themselves was to be Frank Ocean. When it was Drake instead, the crowd turned on him. In fact, things got so bad that Drake asked the audience if they wanted him to continue.

They did not.
