Rebekah Vardy believes her 'enemies will destroy themselves’ after Coleen row

Rebekah Vardy has posted a cryptic message to those she believes have crossed her as the 'Wagatha Christie' feud with Coleen Rooney rumbles on

12 Nov 2019, 23:51 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Rebekah Vardy has taken aim at those she believes have wronged her.

In a post on her Instagram story, the 37-year-old said her enemies will "destroy themselves" as her feud with Coleen Rooney refuses to die down.

The WAG shared with her followers a message which most commenters believe was a dig at Wayne Rooney's wife.

Rebekah wrote: "Allow your enemies their space to hate; they will destroy themselves in the process."

The rift between Rebekah and Coleen has not been healed since Rooney turned sleuth and accused her former friend of spreading stories about her, in a scandal that was dubbed 'Wagatha Christie'.

It has been reported that Coleen had been suspecting that Rebekah had been sharing gossip about her, a few months before she went public with her alleged findings.

At that time, 33-year-old Coleen is said to have blocked Rebekah on social media after she became suspicious of her activity on her account.

Speaking to the Daily Mail , Rebekah said she confronted Coleen about the move: "I sent her a message and asked her: 'Have I done something, is there a problem here? I seem to be blocked on Instagram.'

"And she said no, it was probably one of the kids messing about with her phone that had done it.

"She was completely convincing."

Sources have claimed that Coleen was completely convinced that Rebekah was the one that had been spreading the 'lies'.

Speaking to The Mirror , the source said: "Coleen was convinced Vardy was to blame, but had to get the evidence first. Blocking her wasn’t going to cut the mustard... she needed proof."

At the time, Coleen outed Rebekah's Instagram account as being the only one that could view a number of made up stories which eventually ended up in being reported in the media.

Rebekah has continuously denied having any involvement in the leaks and said that a number of people had access to her account.

A source told The Sun that she is trying to find out who is responsible.

They said: "Rebekah is determined to prove her innocence. She's waiting on the results from the forensic investigation and will prove to Coleen it wasn't her."
