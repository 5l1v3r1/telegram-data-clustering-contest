US Supreme Court Allows Lawsuit Against Gunmaker Over School Massacre

WASHINGTON (Sputnik) - The US Supreme Court green-lighted a lawsuit against the Remington gun manufacturer over the 2012 Sandy Hook grade school massacre, a court document revealed on Tuesday.
