— “La Dame Aux Camelias (La Dame Aux Camelias),” 1993 — Rene Gruau (1909-2004); watercolor and pencil on paper; signed with the monogram lower left. Measurement: 65 x 50 cm; 25 9/16 x 19 11/16 in. Featured in lot 200. Estimate: € 10,000-15,000 (11,000 - 16,500 USD)

— “Tout En Plumes, Etude Pour Affiche Du Lido (All in Feathers, Study for Lido Poster),” 1988 — Rene Gruau (1909-2004); gouache, watercolor and pencil on paper; monogram signature lower right. — “Profil De Femme Au Lisere Rouge (Profile of Woman with Red Light)” — Rene Gruau (1909-2004); watercolor, ink, and pencil on paper, signed with the monogram on the lower right. Measurement: 32 x 34 cm; 12 ⅝ x 13 ⅜ in. Featured in lot 202. Estimate: € 8,000-12,000 (8,800 - 13,200 USD)

— “Projet Pour “Rouge Baiser” (Project for “Rouge Baiser”)” — Rene Gruau (1909-2004); ink and gouache on paper, Signed with the monogram on the lower right. Measurement: 48 x 36 cm; 18 ⅞ x14 3/16 in. Featured in lot 203. Estimate: € 8,000-12,000 (8,800 - 13,200 USD)
