AirPods Pro’s Design Makes It Really Tricky Just To Remove Them From Their Cases

12 Nov 2019 by Mikelle Leow

Apple’s AirPods Pro might be US$100 more expensive than regular AirPods, but they come with a design complication that has users struggling to get them out of their wireless battery cases.
The latest wireless earbuds arrive with changeable eartips, making it trickier to slip them out of the cases.
Because of this added difficulty, AirPods Pro owners have been forced to find a way to easily remove them from the cases every time.
What 9to5Mac’s Guilherme Rambo has discovered is that, by holding the earbud in between your thumb and index finger, and then flicking the plastic part of the AirPods Pro’s head and catching it in your thumb, you’ll be able to quickly detach the AirPods Pro from the box.
Pro tip aside, Apple users have questioned if the AirPods Pro are really an advanced variation of the normal AirPods if simply taking them out of the wireless cases is already a chore.
“Can you [imagine] Steve [Jobs] tolerating such a finger dance to remove them? I think he would have screamed the meeting room down,” Apple enthusiast Grizzly Media tweeted in response.
