Donald Trump Jr. trolls ‘The View’ after his episode becom

12 Nov 2019 by Jessica Chasmar

Donald Trump Jr. trolled ABC’s “The View” on Tuesday after his heated episode with girlfriend Kimberly Guilfoyle last week fetched the show’s highest ratings in months.

On Twitter, Mr. Trump linked to a story by the Washington Examiner highlighting Nielsen Media Research’s latest figures, which showed that “The View” had an audience of slightly over 3 million people for its Nov. 7 episode, up from an average of 2.5 million viewers the week prior.

“Confirmed, America has been looking for a different View,” the first son tweeted. “You’re welcome.”
