Charlotte Crosby dishes on how living with Josh Ritchie has changed their relationship

EXCLUSIVE: The former Geordie Shore star recently relocated to Bolton to move in with beau Josh Ritchie - and she tells us it's going amazingly so far

12 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Vicki Newman, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/vicki-newman/

Charlotte Crosby says she feels closer than ever to boyfriend Josh Ritchie .

The former Geordie Shore star waved goodbye to the Toon to move in with Josh in Bolton, and things are going better than ever.

The couple's two-year romance has been through some rocky patches, with them arguing over when to have kids and about how often he goes on lads' nights out.

But Charlotte, 29, says moving in together has been bliss.

Charlotte opened up about how her relationship has changed during an exclusive chat with Mirror Online ahead of the new series of her MTV show Just Tattoo Of Us.

She said: "I've not spent much time [at the house], I was there for a week so I could get all the deliveries and furniture in but then after that I was back in Australia, came back yesterday and I'm away again so had one day there.

"I'm hardly at the house but I do see Josh all the time, we've just spent three weeks in Australia together, but I've never been in one place more than one week at a time, that's why I decided to rent my house out because it was a complete and utter waste of money, it was just sitting there empty."

She added: "We have learnt a lot more [since moving in together] and it's been really, really good. We're getting on so well, I think it's brought us closer together.

"We've had no arguments about cleaning and stuff, he's so tidy, always doing the washing, it's really been perfect."

Just Tattoo Of Us is returning for a fifth season.

And it's set to be wilder than ever thanks to a brand new twist.

Charlotte explained: "I'm very proud that it's got that far, a lot of shows will only have one series then not get recomissioned. I couldn't be prouder, I love its craziness and its completely unfiltered nature and this series is just taking it to a whole new level.

"Now we've got this stick or twist which is where people have got the option to stick with the tattoo their friend's given them or twist and get a completely random tattoo designed by one of the artists, and that element to it is a complete gamble and this tattoo could be worse or better.

"People get so p****d off when they choose to twist. It just opens up a brand new can of worms, there's all these new elements you could be angry about because your friend could have got you a nice one and you end up with a horrible one and they'll be p****d off because you didn't trust them.

"It makes the show even crazier and I didn't think it could get any crazier."

Again this series, Charlotte will be joined by guest hosts, including her boyfriend, Geordie Shore co-star Chloe Ferry and Love Island's Olivia Attwood .

But Charlotte admits she's ready for a regular co-host again.

She said: "I'm ready to have a regular co-host again, it's all over the place when there's guests. For me, it's quite hard because I'm getting a different person every couple of days and most of the time they don't know what they're doing and the producers rely on me to bring it back.

"If we get a guest host who isn't very good or doesn't know what they're doing I have to do their job for them too so I'm doing a lot more work, so I think it's time we had a regular."

Charlotte also confirmed the fourth series of The Charlotte Show will begin filming next year.

*Charlotte Crosby is back with Just Tattoo Of Us series five, which premieres Monday, November 18 at 10pm on MTV
