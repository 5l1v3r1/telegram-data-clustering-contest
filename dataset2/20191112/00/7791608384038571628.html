‘Bloomberg 2020’ Fan Campaign Logo Somehow Reminds Internet Of ‘Boobs’

12 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

An unofficial logo and slogan supporting Michael Bloomberg’s run for presidency in 2020 went viral last Saturday.
The logo, which can be seen on website Bloomberg2020.org, has been confirmed as a fake symbol that is not affiliated with the Bloomberg CEO’s campaign.
It is unclear who is behind the logo, but Mediaite reported that the wordmark is a parody of Michael Bloomberg’s official 2020 campaign and its tagline “Owning It Together!”
Bloomberg’s confidant Howard Wolfson has cleared up to supporters in a tweet, explaining that the team has nothing to do with the website and logo.
The bold logo features a sans-serif typeface with a blue and red color scheme. The numbers 2020 run vertically through the ‘O’s’ of Bloomberg, and its whole appearance has been described as being “busy.”
The logo has inspired some jokes, with one Twitter user saying that all she can read is “Boobs.” Another Twitter user says that the logo gives them trypophobia, while one has recreated the logo to read “OK BOOMER.”
