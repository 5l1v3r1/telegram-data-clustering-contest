Nigerian national arrested, 12 gm of cocaine recovered

12 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

A Nigerian national has been arrested for trying to sell cocaine. The CCB sleuths who made the arrest recovered 12 grams of cocaine from him.

The arrested has been identified as Makuka Chukwuka Muolokwu alias Miracle Chukwuka alias Henry, 38, a resident of Banaswadi.

Along with the cocaine, the police have also recovered five mobile phones and Rs 10,200 in cash.


The accused is alleged to have come to the country on student visa and was reportedly selling drugs to foreign national students.

The police have recovered his passport and visa and are further investigating the case.


The CCB sleuths have registered a case against him in the Banaswadi police station.
