Bella Hadid Risks Wardrobe Malfunction By Draping Leather Jacket Over Nipples

12 Nov 2019 by Izza Sofia

Pop Magazine recently tapped supermodel Bella Hadid for its September issue in dramatic fashion.
Photographed by Hugo Comte, Hadid risked a wardrobe malfunction in a photo she shared on Instagram. The behind-the-scenes image shows her rocking a pixie black wig while wearing a leather jacket that was draped over her nipples.
She flaunted her side boob and flat stomach for the shoot, while wearing a pair of tiny black shorts.
Hadid also let her natural beauty shine in the photos, as she was barely covered in makeup.
She had contoured cheekbones and slight freckles on her face, with a natural pink hue painted over her lips.
