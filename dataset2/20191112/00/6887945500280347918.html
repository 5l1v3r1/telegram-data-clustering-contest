Auction Preview: Irish Art at Sotheby's, London, November 19

12 Nov 2019 by BLOUIN ARTINFO

What: Irish Art

Where: Sotheby's, 34-35 New Bond Street, London, W1A 2AA, UK

When: November 19, 2:00 pm GMT

Public Viewing: November 15, 2019 | 09:00 AM - 04:30 PM, November 16, 2019 | 12:00 PM - 05:00 PM, November 17, 2019 | 12:00 PM - 05:00 PM, November 18, 2019 | 09:00 AM - 04:30 PM, November 19, 2019 | 09:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Top Lots at the Sale:

— “A Paris of the West/ A Paris Come to Judgment in the West” — Jack Butler Yeats, R.H.A. (1871-1957); oil on canvas, signed “Jack B. Yeats”. Painted in 1944. Measurement: 61 x 91.5cm (24 by 36in). Featured at lot 20. Estimate: 500,000 - 800,000 GBP (639,700 – 1,023,600 USD)

— “The Man in the Moon has Patience” — Jack Butler Yeats, R.H.A. (1871-1957); oil on canvas, signed “l.r.: Jack B. Yeats”; titled on the reverse, and further titled, signed and inscribed with the artist's address on an old label attached to the stretcher. Painted in 1943. Measurement: 46 x 61cm. (18 by 24in). Featured at lot 11. Estimate: 150,000 - 250,000 GBP (191,900 – 319,900 USD)

— “A Moonlit Breton Landscape” — Roderic O’Connor (1860-1940); oil on canvas, studio stamp “Atelier O’Connor” on the reverse. Painted circa 1898-1900. Measurement: 71 x 83cm (28 x 32½in). Featured at lot 33. Estimate: 120,000 - 180,000 GBP (153,500 – 230,300 USD)

— “The Stevedore” — Jack Butler Yeats, R.H.A. (1871-1957); oil on canvas, signed “l.l.: JACK B/ YEATS”; titled on the stretcher verso. Painted in 1947. Measurement: 35.5 x 53.5cm (14 by 21in). Featured at lot 34. Estimate: 120,000 - 180,000 GBP (153,500 – 230,300 USD)

— “The Massing of the Armies” — Louis le Brocquy, R.H.A. (1916-2012); tapestry, numbered 1/1 and signed “Le Brocquy” on the Tapisserie d'aubusson certificate of authenticity attached to the reverse. Measurement: 445 x 683cm (175 x 269in). Featured at lot 79. Estimate: 120,000 - 180,000 GBP (153,500 – 230,300 USD)

— “The Bar in the Hall-By-The-Sea, Margate” — Sir William Orpen, R.W.S., N.E.A.C., R.A., R.H.A. (1878-1931); oil on canvas, signed “l.l.: Orpen”. Painted circa 1907-08. Measurement: 91.5 x 71cm (36 x 28in). Featured at lot 38. Estimate: 100,000 - 150,000 GBP (127,900 – 191,900 USD)

— “Clare Island from Achill” — Paul Henry, R.H.A., R.U.A. (1876-1958); oil on canvas, signed “l.l.: Paul Henry”. Measurement: 51 x 61cm (20 x 24in). Featured at lot 57. Estimate: 80,000 - 120,000 GBP (102,300 – 153,500 USD)

— “The Bones” — Jack Butler Yeats, R.H.A. (1871-1957); oil on panel, signed “l.l.: Jack B Yeats”; titled on the reverse. Painted in 1936. Measurement: 23 x 35.5cm (9 x 14in). Featured at lot 69. Estimate: 80,000 - 120,000 GBP (102,300 – 153,500 USD)

— “The Sunlit Page” — Jack Butler Yeats, R.H.A. (1871-1957); oil on board, signed “l.l.: Jack B Yeats”; titled on the reverse. Painted in 1952. Measurement: 35.5 x 46cm (14 x 18in). Featured at lot 85. Estimate: 80,000 - 120,000 GBP (102,300 – 153,500 USD)
