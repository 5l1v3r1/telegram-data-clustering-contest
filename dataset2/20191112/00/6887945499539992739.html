Auction Preview: Oriental Arts at Tajan, Paris, November 13

12 Nov 2019 by BLOUIN ARTINFO

What: Oriental Arts

Where: Tajan, 37 rue des Mathurins, 75008, Paris

When: November 13, 5:30 pm

Public Viewing: November 8, 2019 | 10 am-6 pm, November 12, 2019 | 10 am-6 pm, November 13, 2019 | 10 am to 2 pm

Top Lots at the Sale:

— “Cavaliers Arabes (Arab Horsemen)” — Edy- Legrand (1892-1970); Oil on heavy cardboard, signed lower left. Measurement: 99 x 80 cm (38 x 31 ½ in). Featured in lot 156. Estimate: € 40,000 - 60,000 (44,200 - 66,300 USD)

— “Composition” — Charles Hossein Zenderoudi (Born 1937) Iranian School; Mixed technique and collage on kraft paper, signed, dated “12 May” and autographed “for my dear Bagherzadeh” in Farsi lower right. Dedicated “for Cher Firouz” in Farsi lower left. Measurement: 42 x 118 cm (16 9/16 x 46 7/16 in). Featured in lot 174. Estimate: € 40,000 - 50,000 (44,200 - 55,200 USD)

— “Dand L'attentes Des Instructions (Waiting for Orders, Algeria),” 1881 — Frederick-Arthur Bridgman (1847-1929) American School; Oil on canvas, signed and dated “1881” lower left. Measurement: 29 x 45 cm (11 ½ x 18 in). Featured in lot 86. Estimate: € 35,000 - 45,000 (38,700 - 49,700 USD)

— “Composition,” Circa 1965 — Charles Hossein Zenderoudi (Born in 1937) Iranian School; Mixed technique and collage on kraft paper, signed “Hossein” in Farsi in the composition. Measurement: 71 x 49 cm (27 15/16 x 19 5/16 in). Featured in lot 173. Estimate: € 30,000 - 40,000 (33,150 - 44,200 USD)

— “Parade De La Garde Royale (Parade of the Royal Guard)” — Hassan el Glaoui (1924-2018); Gouache on isorel, signed lower left. Measurement: 75.3 x 106 cm (29 ⅝ x 41 ¾ in). Featured in lot 166. Estimate: € 20,000 - 40,000 (22,100 - 44,200 USD)

— “Devant L’entree De La Mosquee De La Pecherie, Alger (Small Entrance to the Mosque De La Pecherie, Algiers)” — Frederick-Arthur Bridgman (1847-1929) American School; Oil on canvas, signed lower left. Titled on the back of the canvas. Measurement: 24 x 34 cm (9.5 x 13.5 in). Featured in lot 85. Estimate: € 18,000 - 22,000 (19,900 - 24,300 USD)

— “La Priere Devant Le Palais D'or Harmandir Sahib, Amritsar, Inde (Praying in Front of Harmandir Sahib Palace, Amristar, India)” — Antoine Druet (1857-1921); Oil on canvas, signed, located “Amritsar, India” and autographed “To my friend Pelissier “in the lower left. Measurement: 64.5 x 81 cm (25 ⅜ x 31 ⅞ in). Featured in lot 137. Estimate: € 15,000 - 25,000 (16,600 - 27,600 USD)

— “Guerrier Au Repos (Warrior at Rest)” — Orientalist School (late 19th century); Oil on canvas. Signed “G. Lem [...]” at the bottom left. Measurement: 65 x 80 cm (25 9/16 x 31 ½ in). Featured in lot 84. Estimate: € 12,000 - 18,000 (13,250 - 19,900 USD)

— “Chevres A Shobrah, Egypte (Goats in Shobrah, Egypt)” — Ludwig Deutsch (1855-1935); Oil on canvas, signed lower right. Measurement: 50 x 60 cm (20 x 24 in). Featured in lot 115. Estimate: € 12,000 - 18,000 (13,250 - 19,900 USD)
