Top Lots at the Sale:

— “Untitled XXII” — Willem de Kooning (1904 - 1997); oil on canvas, signed on the stretcher. Executed in 1977. Measurement: 70 x 80 in (177.8 x 203.2 cm). Featured in lot 13. Estimate: 25,000,000 — 35,000,000 USD

— “Blue over Red” — Mark Rothko (1903 - 1970); oil on canvas, signed and dated 1953 on the reverse. Measurement: 64 x 35 in. (162.6 x 88.9 cm). Featured in lot 26. Estimate: 25,000,000 — 35,000,000 USD

— “PH-399” — Clyfford Still (1904 - 1980); oil on canvas, signed twice and dated 1946; signed, dated 1946 twice, and numbered P-399 on the reverse. Measurement: 53 ¾ x 44 ¾ in (136.5 x 113.7 cm). Featured in lot 11. Estimate: 12,000,000 — 18,000,000 USD

— “Number Two” — Brice Marden (B.1938); oil on canvas, in twelve parts, signed, titled, dated 1983-84, and variously inscribed on the reverse. Measurement: 84 ¼ x 108 ¾ in (214 x 276.2 cm). Featured in lot 9. Estimate: 10,000,000 — 15,000,000 USD

— “YVES-Marie in the Rain” — David Hockney (B.1937); oil on canvas, signed, titled, and dated “1973” on the reverse. Measurement: 48 ⅛ x 60 ⅛ in (122.2 x 152.7 cm). Featured in lot 28. Estimate: 8,000,000 — 12,000,000 USD

— “Achrome” — Piero Manzoni (1933 - 1963); kaolin and wrinkled canvas. Executed in 1959. Measurement: 68 ⅛ x 51 ⅜ in (173 x 130.5 cm). Featured in lot 34. Estimate: 8,000,000 — 12,000,000 USD

— “Vignette 19” — Kerry James Marshall (B.1955); acrylic on PVC, signed with the artist's initials and dated 2014. Measurement: 71 ¾ x 60 in (182.2 x 152.4 cm). Featured in lot 19. Estimate: 6,500,000 — 7,500,000 USD

— “Untitled” — Christopher Wool (B.1955); enamel on aluminum, signed, titled, dated 1995, and numbered P219 on the reverse. Measurement: 90 x 60 in (228.6 x 152.4 cm). Featured in lot 21. Estimate: 6,000,000 — 8,000,000 USD

— “Pope” — Francis Bacon (1909 - 1992); oil on canvas. Executed circa 1958. Measurement: 77 ⅛ by 55 ⅞ in (195.9 x 141.9 cm). Featured in lot 23. Estimate: 6,000,000 — 8,000,000 USD

— “Encased Cakes” — Wayne Thiebaud (B.1920); oil on canvas, signed and dated 2011; signed and dated 2010/2011 on the reverse. Measurement: 72 x 48 in (182.9 x 121.9 cm) Featured in lot 30. Estimate: 6,000,000 — 8,000,000 USD
