Pearson carries Texas St. over Prairie View A&M 75-48

12 Nov 2019

SAN MARCOS, Texas (AP) - Nijal Pearson had 23 points as Texas State rolled past Prairie View A&M; 75-48 on Tuesday night for coach Danny Kaspar’s 100th win with the Bobcats.

DeShawn Davidson had 11 points with three 3-pointers for Texas State (2-1), which never trailed. Isiah Small added nine rebounds and Mason Harrell had six assists.

Lenell Henry had 10 points for the Panthers (1-2).

Gerard Andrus, whose 14 points per game heading into the contest ranked second on the Panthers, shot only 13 percent for the game (1 of 8).

Texas State takes on Baylor on the road on Friday. Prairie View faces Texas on the road on Friday.
