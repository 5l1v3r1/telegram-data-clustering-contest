Baltimore Ravens debut Heisman Package with three former college football stars

12 Nov 2019, 14:01 by Ryan Gaydos

The Baltimore Ravens gave the Cincinnati Bengals, and the rest of the NFL, a new look on offense when they debuted their so-called "Heisman Package."

Lamar Jackson lined up under center in the second quarter with running backs Mark Ingram on his right and Robert Griffin III on his left. Each of the players had won the Heisman Trophy when they were in college.
