Sonic the Hedgehog Makes His Second Debut in Ridiculously Fun Trailer

12 Nov 2019, 14:00 by Beth Elderkin

Sonic the Hedgehog is back – and this time, he’s adorable. The latest trailer for Paramount Pictures’ Sonic the Hedgehog has arrived, trading “badass” one-liners for silly gags and showing off the redesign that was worth the wait.
