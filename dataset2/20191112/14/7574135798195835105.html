Om announce tour with Wovenhand, as Sleep announce hiatus

12 Nov 2019, 14:47 by Andrew Sacher

OM have a Brooklyn show with “special guests” coming up on December 14 at Pioneer Works (tickets), and they’ve now also announced a lengthy 2020 tour with Wovenhand happening in February and March. There’s no NYC show on that tour, but there are other Northeast dates (like DC, Philly, Boston, Providence, and Pittsburgh) with some nights off around then, so maybe they’ll add a NYC show after the Pioneer Works date passes? Stay tuned to find out. All dates are listed below.

Meanwhile, OM frontman Al Cisneros’ band Sleep have their previously announced New Year’s run in Chicago coming up, and they recently revealed that they’d be going on an indefinite hiatus after that. At least that’s what we think “prep hypersleep facil for indef duration” means. See their announcement below.
