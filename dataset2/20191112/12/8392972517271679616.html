Former EastEnders star Harry Reid looks totally different as he reunites with soap legend Sian Webber

He has worked alongside the Ritchie Scott actress many times

12 Nov 2019, 12:29 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Former EastEnders actor Harry Reid looks completely different in his latest Instagram post.

The actor, who played Ben Mitchell before current actor Max Bowden, posted a picture to his social media alongside soap favourite Sian Webber, who plays Ritchie Scott on the soap.

Harry captioned the post: "Look who I was working with today. This woman knows how to COMMAND a scene.

"Ritchiescott #reunion #EastEnders #dodgybird."

Fans rushed to comment on the reunion.

One wrote: "Ritchie Scott loving it."

A second commented: "Aww, who doesn't love a reunion?"

A third said: "Looking good Harry."

Sian has played solicitor Ritchie on and off since 2005, representing Albert Square residents in multiple different cases.

Ritchie has represented the Mitchell family on more than one occasion.

In 2012 she helped Phil Mitchell's cousin Billy when he was arrested for the murder of Heather Trott.

Look who I was working with today...

Eventually Ben, who was played by Joshua Pascoe at the time, confessed to Heather's murder but refused to let Ritchie represent him.

In 2015 Ben, then played by Harry, was arrested for the murder of Lucy Beale.

Harry left EastEnders in 2018 after Ben left for a new life in France.

Ben returned earlier this year played by Max.

Since the character's comeback, Ritchie has made an appearance changing Phil's Will.

Phil made the decision to remove Ben from his will and put Arches' mechanic Keanu in it instead.
