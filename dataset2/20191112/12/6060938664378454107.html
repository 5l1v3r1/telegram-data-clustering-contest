Here's the sound track of 'Awara' from Salman Khan and Saee Manjrekar's 'Dabangg 3'

12 Nov 2019, 12:33 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After releasing the energetic foot-tapping song, 'Munna Badnaam' from Salman Khan's much-awaited cop drama, 'Dabangg 3', the makers today unveiled the soundtrack of their first romantic song, 'Awara', which will feature Salman Khan and Saee Manjrekar.

This song will put light on Chulbul Panday and Khushi's blooming love story. This is one of the soothing songs from 'Dabangg' franchise and is sure to be a hit one. Sung by Salman Ali and Muskaan, the lyrics of the song is penned by Sameer Anjaan and Sajid. The UPS of the song are the various instruments that play in a sync to bring out a beautiful track. Salman took to his social media handle and captioned the video as, "Pehle ishq ki baat hi kuch aur hoti hai. Suno Dabangg 3 ka naya gaana, 'Awara'."
