Florida man charged with molesting young child in Pasco County

12 Nov 2019, 12:43 by WFTX Digital Team

PASCO COUNTY, Fla. -- Florida investigators have charged a Brooksville man with molesting a young child, and are working to see if there are more victims in Florida or other states.

33-year-old Edgar Diaz-Colon was arrested in Pasco County on Friday and is facing charges of lewd and lascivious molestation, promoting sexual performance by a child, and possession of child pornography.

According to the Pasco County Sheriff's Office, after he was arrested, detectives found videos on his phone of him molesting a 3-year-old girl, in addition to other child pornography videos.

The investigation continues into whether there are more victims in Pasco, Florida, or other states.
