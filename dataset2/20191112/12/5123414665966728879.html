Premiere: JLSXND7RS - Marching (CASPA Remix)

These articles will focus on specific EP premieres/reviews for certain artists/labels within the 140 scene and beyond. This one features CASPA with his remix of JLSXND7RS "Marching."

12 Nov 2019, 12:57 by Benjamin Oliver Lloyd

The prominent Netherlands grime producer known as JLSXND7RS has finally decided to release his debut record on his new label Dark Knight. Having collaborated in the past with the likes of Wiley, Footsie, Rocks Foe, Discarda and more, its evident that JLSXND7RS takes his craft very seriously. 

He's teased certain aspects of the label and DK001 over the past few months, but finally, the time is here. With plenty of releases in the pipeline, Dark Knight is definitely a label you need to watch out for. 

For DK001 he roped in both CASPA & Jook to remix his 2016 instrumental "Marching." We were given the chance to premiere the remix from CASPA which you can listen to below:
