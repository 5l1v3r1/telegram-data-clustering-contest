Colvend Primary School holds successful coffee morning in Dalbeattie Town Hall

A raffle, tombola and candy stall were well supported as the parent council raised funds for new maths resources

12 Nov 2019, 12:45 by Stuart Gillespie, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/stuart-gillespie/

Colvend Primary School held its annual coffee morning at Dalbeattie Town Hall recently.

A wide selection of sweet treats were on offer to appreciative customers, served up alongside tea and coffee from parents, carers and staff from the school.

A raffle, tombola and cake and candy stall were well supported, helping to raise funds for the school which plans to purchase new maths resources with the proceeds.

The parent council would like to thank all who donated prizes, baked cakes and came along to the event.
