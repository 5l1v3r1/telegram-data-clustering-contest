This is when the summer holidays begin in Oman schools

12 Nov 2019, 12:32 by Times News Service

Muscat: Summer vacation for Omani school students will start in June and the new school year 2020-2021 will begin on August 23, according to the Ministry of Education.

The Ministry of Education announced the dates of study and holidays for the academic year 2019-2021, where the mid-term holiday will begin on January 19 for students from grades 1-4 and on January 30 for grade 12 students.

The second semester will begin on February 9, 2020, and the school year will end on June 2, 2020.
