Mallika Sherawat sets the temperature soaring with her 'Tuesday transformation' post from her latest photoshoot

12 Nov 2019, 12:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Mallika Sherawat might be away from the silver screen but the mesmerising diva who had set the celluloid ablaze with her bold performance seems to have aced her social media game as she shared a new post.

Taking to Instagram on Tuesday afternoon, the 'Murder' actress shared a delightful post of herself posing for the lens which she termed as #tuesdaytransformation in the caption. This picture of the sizzling actress from her latest photoshoot donning a beige dressed with heavy eye makeup and open tresses has set the temperature soaring.

Despite her absence from the silver screen, Mallika still manages to grab everyone's attention with ease and would give her contemporaries like Deepika Padukone, Ileana D'Cruz, Nargis Fakhri and others a tough competition on social media.

Mallika's social media handle has a mix of fitness posts, style and vacation goals. She has been away from Bollywood for quite a while now but the vegan actress recently featured in a web series.
