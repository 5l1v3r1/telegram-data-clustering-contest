Tommy Fury, Vicky Pattison and Amy Hart lead the hot red carpet glamour at ITV's Palooza

TOWIE's Georgia Kousoulou and Olivia Attwood also joined the line-up of stars heading to the event

12 Nov 2019, 18:37 by Tiffany Wallis

A host of ITV's biggest stars hit the hot red carpet in spectacular glamour at one of the broadcaster's biggest events tonight.

Former I'm A Celebrity jungle Queen Vicky Pattison and Love Island sweetheart Amy Hart were among the lovely ladies who made their way to ITV Palooza.

The event, formerly known as the ITV Gala, is a star-studded night of entertainment for commercial customers, colleagues and talent.

TOWIE babes Georgia Kousoulou and Olivia Attwood also struck a pose at the glitzy event.

Love Island runner up Tommy Fury stormed the glamorous carpet in an eye-catching fashion ensemble.

The professional boxer, 21, turned heads in an autumn-inspired plaid blazer and a crisp white shirt with multi-coloured button detailing.

The reality TV star, who is still going strong with blonde beauty Molly-Mae Hague, flashed a glimpse at his pearly whites as he posed for photographers.

Tommy ensured to show off the benefits of his Love Island fame as he donned a shiny Louis Vuitton belt and embellished black shoes.

Fellow Love Island star Amy Hart also wowed on the hot red carpet.

The blonde TV star, who famously walked out the Mallorcan villa this summer after suffering a heartbreak at the hand's of Curtis Pritchard, couldn't contain her smile as she strutted her stuff.

The former air hostess, 26, dazzled in a shimmering gun-metal grey dress with glitzy sequins and a tie-up belt feature which accentuated her stunning figure.

Also joining the stars of ITV2'S hit dating show was former Geordie Shore babe Vicky Pattison.

The Newcastle star, 31, looked sensational in a figure-hugging midi dress.

The star showcased her enviable physique as she made her way down the red carpet.

The brunette beauty looked incredible as she showed off a glamorous make-up look and wore her glossy long locks in perfectly blowdried waves.

Joining Vicky at the event was one of her closest showbiz pals, Ferne McCann.

The mother-of-one recently returned to screens with the fourth series of her show First Time Mum.

Cameras follow Ferne as she takes motherhood in her stride and juggles her work commitments with her parenting.

The telly star looked incredible as she shimmied down the red carpet tonight.

Ferne wowed in a floor-length black gown with sexy cut-out features.

The backless number was a glamorous addition to the red carpet's star-studded displays.
