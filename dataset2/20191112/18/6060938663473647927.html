Ranveer Singh's comments steal the limelight from wife Deepika Padukone's red hot look!

12 Nov 2019, 18:16 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Deepika Padukone is one of the most talented actresses of Bollywood. The beauty has won everyone's heart with her stellar performances in various films and established herself as an A-list actress in Bollywood. Apart from her acting talent, she is also known for her classic fashion statements!

And now, she took to her Instagram account to share her look for an event. The actress opted for a red gown teamed with matching heels for the night. She captioned the post, "RED- is the color that makes people hungry. Red also instantly attracts attention,makes people excited,energetic and increases heart rate!

All of the things I’m going for today!❤️"

Husband Ranveer Singh was quick to comment on her posts. On the first picture, he wrote, "Yes ! All of the above are happening to me right now."

On the second, the actor commented, "My spirit colour."

The comment on the third post read, "The colour of Passion hayye".

Meanwhile, after 'Padmaavat', the couple will be sharing screen space once again in Kabir Khan's sports drama '83'. The actress will be essaying the role of Kapil Dev's wife Romi Dev in the film while Ranveer Singh will be seen as Kapil Dev.
