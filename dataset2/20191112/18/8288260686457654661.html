Breaking: Aston Villa star withdraws from Wales squad due to personal reasons

Aston Villa latest news and views | Neil Taylor has pulled out of the Wales squad for European Championships qualifiers with Azerbaijan and Hungary later on this month

12 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Richard Cusack

Aston Villa left-back Neil Taylor has withdrawn from the Wales squad because of personal reasons.

Taylor was with Ryan Giggs' side for European Championships qualifiers with Azerbaijan on Saturday and Hungary the following Tuesday.

The 30-year-old will have been vying to make his 43rd and 44th appearances for his country.

Taylor came on for the second-half of the 2-1 defeat to Wolverhampton Wanderers on Sunday, missing the previous four matches in the Premier League.
