Man arrested after sickening dog attack which left cat dead

Police arrested a 30-year-old man at an address in Walsall this afternoon (November 12)

12 Nov 2019, 18:35 by Charlotte Regen, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/charlotte-regen/

A man has been arrested after a defenceless cat was mauled by a dog - who was 'encouraged' to carry out the sickening attack.

The 30-year-old was wanted for questioning about the dog attack in Woodlands Crescent, Pelsall on October 23.

He was found at an address in Walsall this afternoon (November 12) and arrested on suspicion of cruelty to animals and remains in police custody.

Chief Superintendent Andy Parsons, from Walsall police, said: "This was a horrifying act of cruelty on an innocent animal. 

"I want to thank the community and members of the public for sharing our appeal and providing information to the police. Your information has been invaluable to our investigation. "

Anyone with any information who has not already spoken to us is urged to contact police via Live Chat at  www.west-midlands.police.uk  between 8am and midnight or call 101 anytime. Crimestoppers can be contacted anonymously on 0800 555 111. Please quote crime number 20WS/255002G/19.
