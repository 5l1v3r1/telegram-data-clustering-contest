'Housefull 4' box office collection Day 18: The Akshay Kumar starrer remains steady on its third Monday

12 Nov 2019, 07:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Maintaining its box office dominance, Akshay Kumar starrer comedy-drama 'Housefull 4' has witnessed a steady run on Monday after an excellent third weekend. The Farhad Samji directorial which features an ensemble star cast is edging close to the ambitious Rs 200 crore mark and is all set to become the fourth film to achieve the feat this year. The film has also emerged as the highest Akshay Kumar grosser movie.
Housefull 4 Movie Review : Not so full of comedy

As per the latest report on the Boxofficeindia.com, on Monday, the film has raked in Rs 1.75 crore on its eighteenth day at the box office. The film has managed to collect a total of Rs 195.18 crore in eighteen days at the box office.

The film is expected to get some boost from the holiday of Guru Nanak Jayanti on Tuesday. The film is heading towards a good third-week collection.

Directed by Farhad Samji, ‘Housefull 4’ boasts of an ensemble star cast that includes, Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon, Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol, Pooja Hegde in the lead roles. The film is the fourth installment of the hit comedy franchise. The film hits the screens on October 25 and clashed at the box office with Rajkummar Rao's 'Made In China' and Taapsee Pannu-Bhumi Pednekar's 'Saand Ki Aankh'.
