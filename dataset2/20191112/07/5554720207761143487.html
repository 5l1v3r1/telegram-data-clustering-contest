Digvijay Singh of Congress dares Subramanian Swamy to expose names after BJP MP’s cryptic tweet

12 Nov 2019, 07:44 by JKR Staff

Congress leader Digvijay Singh has asked BJP’s Rajya Sabha MP Subramanian Swamy to expose those who, according to him, conspired against the listing of Babri Masjid-Ram Temple case in the Supreme Court for seven years. This was after Swamy posted a cryptic tweet implying that many within his own party may have conspired to stop the listing of the Ayodhya dispute case in the Supreme Court.

Swamy’s tweet read, “All those who now want to claim credit for the Ram Temple case should explain what they did from 2010 to 2017 to get the hearing the matter dates and matter listed? Zamindars can collect the crop revenue without planting the seeds.”
