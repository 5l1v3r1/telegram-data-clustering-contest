Half-price wedding up for grabs at Priory Hall or Thomas Robinson

Two of Dudley's most popular wedding venues are offering a half-price deal on wedding booked in the run up to Christmas

12 Nov 2019, 07:44 by Oprah Flash, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/oprah-flash/

Have you got your heart set on a winter wedding?

While tying the knot is an exciting occasion for you and your loved ones, it can also hit your bank balance pretty hard.

Fear not, if you've got plans for a wedding in the New Year there's a half-price bonus up for grabs

It’s not your average stocking-filler but brides and grooms could save money this Christmas by making a half-price booking at one of Dudley Council’s wedding venues .

Anyone who books a Saturday wedding at Dudley’s stunning Priory Hall or Stourbridge’s beautiful Thomas Robinson building to take place in January can bag themselves a half-price ceremony.

It means they have to be booked before Christmas, making the perfect festive gift.

Staff at the popular wedding venues have decided to trial this quirky deal to help people beat the winter blues and save money in an unusual way.

The offer relates to the Priory Suite at Priory Hall and the Wollescote Suite at Thomas Robinson.

"Penny Fulford, superintendent registrar at Dudley Council, said: "January is a tricky time for household finances. We wanted to do something to help people rise above this and focus on what’s important to them, so we decided to try out this special winter sale.
