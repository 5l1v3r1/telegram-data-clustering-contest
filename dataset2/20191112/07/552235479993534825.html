Charlie Nicholas responds to Rod Stewart's Celtic prediction pop as he delivers Rangers one-liner

Sir Rod had a pop at the former Celtic star but the pundit is hoping the pair can rekindle their friendship.

12 Nov 2019, 07:54 by Graeme Young, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/graeme-young/

Surprised Charlie Nicholas admits he was caught on the hop after Rod Stewart took him to task over his Lazio vs Celtic prediction.

The 57-year-old is known for going against Scottish sides away from home in Europe and he didn't see the Hoops' storied win in the Stadio Olimpico coming after tipping the Italians.

Hotline regular Sir Rod then called in on Friday to tell Nicholas he "should know better" than to bet against Neil Lennon 's side.

And now Nicholas is looking to mend fences with the legendary crooner who was in Rome to enjoy one the club's thrilling Europa League triumph.

That victory has Celtic in the knockouts with two games to spare with Lennon and his players earning widespread acclaim for their display.

But Nicholas insists he remains a big fan of the 74-year-old but has joked Stewart's Greatest Hits collections might no longer be on repeat.

He said in his Daily Express column: "I had a little bit of a chuckle when Sir Rod Stewart had a pop at me last week.

"Rod was unhappy with me for saying that I didn't expect Celtic to win away to Lazio.

"I hold my hands up and admit I got it wrong, because Celtic produced a great performance on the night.

"I also got the Rangers result wrong but I didn't see Rod having a go at me for that one!

"In all seriousness, I haven't seen Rod for years, but he is a great friend and always will be.

"I am in the business to give my opinion and I know people will have a pop back.

"I am more than happy to take things on the chin - whatever one they want to pick!

"So, while I might not be singing Baby Jane as much as I used to, I still love Rod."
