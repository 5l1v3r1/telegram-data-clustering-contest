'Bala' box office collection Day 4: The Ayushmann Khurrana starrer crosses Rs 50 crore mark

12 Nov 2019, 07:29 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

With back-to-back successes at the box office, Ayushmann Khurrana has added yet another hit to his name with his latest offering 'Bala.' The Amar Kaushik directorial comedy-drama has crossed Rs 50 crore mark at the box office in just four days of its release. The film had opened to a good start and showed excellent growth over the weekend. The film has been greeted with positive reviews and praises have been showered upon the actor.

As per the report in Boxofficeindia.com, on Monday, the film has raked in a total of Rs 8 crore on its fourth day at the box office. The film dropped around 15% from Friday. The film has managed to collect a total of Rs stands at a total of Rs 50.25 crore at the box office.

The Monday figure of the film is better than Ayushmann's previous film 'Dream Girl' which had collected Rs 7.20 crore. With a holiday in North on Tuesday, the film is expected to catch up with 'Dream Girl.' The film is expected to do better than 'Dream Girl' and 'Chhichhore' in its first week.

'Bala' is Ayushmann Khurrana's biggest release till date. The film released across 3000 screens in India and more than 550 screens in the overseas markets.

Directed by Amar Kaushik, 'Bala' is a film set in Kanpur that revolves around a man suffering from premature balding and the lack of confidence and societal pressure which runs with it. The film also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles.
