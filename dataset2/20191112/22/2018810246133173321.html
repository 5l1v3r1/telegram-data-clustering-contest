See The New Sonic In The Latest Sonic: The Hedgehog Trailer

12 Nov 2019, 22:52 by Alex Walker

The movie still looks a touch nuts, but Sonic's design is definitely a huge improvement.

The latest trailer, Trailer D, dropped late Tuesday evening and showed off everything you need to see from the new Sonic design. The blue hedgehog is a little less life-like in its dimensions, and he's a little more palatable now. The teeth aren't so freakish either, and Sonic has much shorter legs now.

So Sonic looks more like a cartoon character now than someone in a suit, which is nice. How James Marsden and Jim Carrey are going to carry this one through.
