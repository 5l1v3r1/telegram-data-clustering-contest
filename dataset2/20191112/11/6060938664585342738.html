Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Bala' beats Akshay Kumar's 'Kesari' and Kartik Aaryan's 'Luka Chuppi' to create this record

12 Nov 2019, 11:33 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ayushmann Khurrana's 'Bala' which also features Bhumi Pednekar and Yami Gautam in lead roles has been dominating the domestic box office this week. The film which crossed the Rs 50 crore mark in just four days even turned out to be Ayushmann's biggest release till date.

According to Boxofficeindia.com latest report, the Amar Kaushik directorial comedy-drama which minted Rs 8 crore on its first Monday managed to surpass the collections of Akshay Kumar's 'Kesari' and Kartik Aaryan's 'Luka Chuppi' to grab a spot in 2019's highest-earning films on first Monday.

With a total collection of Rs 50.25 crore, the film is another example of Ayushmann's versatility on the celluloid which has helped him earn the National Award for the best actor. The plot of 'Bala' which revolves around a man suffering from premature balding and the impact it has on his life is on the tenth spot of the highest-earning films on first Monday in 2019.

Meanwhile, Ayushmann, who is currently enjoying the success of 'Bala' is also gearing up for the release of his next films. With some really interesting projects like 'Gulabo Sitabo' and 'Shubh Mangal Zyada Saavdhan' both scheduled to release next year, Ayushmann looks adamant to weave his magic once again.
