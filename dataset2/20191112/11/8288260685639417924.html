Lionel Richie announced as first headliner for Blenheim Palace concert series

The singer is confirmed for the Nocturne Live concerts at the historic country manor in 2020

12 Nov 2019, 11:23 by Josh Layton, https://www.birminghammail.co.uk/authors/josh-layton/

Lionel Richie will be a headliner at the Nocturne Live series of summer concerts at Blenheim Palace.

The legendary singer will perform his timeless array of hits at the resplendent country residence on Saturday, June 20, 2020. It should be a dream ticket as he performs tracks from his 30-year plus career including hits 'All Night Long', 'Dancing on the Ceiling' and 'Hello'.

Since his 1982 solo debut he's sold more than 90 million records worldwide and won four Grammy Awards. This includes Album of the Year and Song of the Year, for his collaboration with Michael Jackson on 'We Are the World', which Richie co-wrote.

He's also won an Academy Award for Best Original Song with 'Say You, Say Me' as well as being inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame. More recently he's acted as a judge on American Idol alongside Katy Perry and Luke Bryan for the past three seasons.

In such fine surroundings, and joined by special guests, it promises to be a truly memorable show.
