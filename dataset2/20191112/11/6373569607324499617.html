Student, 21, found dead by police at city centre block

The young man's death is not being treated as suspicious but police say their inquiries will continue

12 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Carl Eve

Police say they are not treating the death of a student as suspicious after they were called to a city centre property yesterday afternoon.

Paramedics and police were seen at the location in Gasking Street, a narrow lane which runs between the St Thomas Court student accommodation building and the Plymouth Options on Exeter Street.

Police have said they were called around 5.40pm yesterday (November 11) following concern for the welfare of a man at an address inÂ Gasking Street.

On arrival they located a 21-year-old man deceased at the address.

Police are not treating the death as suspicious and a file will be prepared for the coroner. Police have said the young man's next of kin have been informed.
