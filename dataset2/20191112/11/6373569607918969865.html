The 20 worst baby names of 2019 - including Danger, Xxayvier and Chardonnay

Are you brave to name your child any of these?

12 Nov 2019, 11:58 by Jamie Hawkins

Game of Thrones, a type of wine, and a holiday destination are the worst baby names of the year, according to research.

Parenting site Parents.Com ranked the worst names children have been given in 2019.

HBO show Game of Thrones concluded earlier this year, and the popularity has spread worldwide, with Khaleesi making the list.

Named after the dragon queen, Khaleesi has been named the fifth worst name for a girl in 2019.

Also making the cut include named like Manson, the surname of notorious cult leader Charles Manson.

Here's the list of the worst boys and girls names this year:

Top ten worst baby boy names in 2019

1. Kingmessiah

2. Yugo

3. Cub

4. Axis

5. Manson

6. Pinches

7. Xxayvier

8. Cletus

9. Danger

10. Stylez

Top ten worst baby girl names in 2019

1. Shy

2. Mattel

3. Cyncere

4. Chardonnay

5. Khaleesi

6. Starlett

7. Blaykelee

8. Any

9. Vegas

10. Pansy

At the other end of the scale, Olivia was named 2019's most popular girls name, whilst Oliver topped the list for boys.

Previously, the least popular baby names in Britain were revealed.

Girls names included Tammy, Justine and Tracy, with these monikers given to fewer than three babies each.

While for boys Jeff and Geoff, Melvyn, Greg and Graeme proved most unpopular.

Still looking for baby name inspiration?

Astrid, Margot and Thea are set to be favourites for baby girls in 2020.

While November baby names such as Millie and Eve are set to be popular this month.
