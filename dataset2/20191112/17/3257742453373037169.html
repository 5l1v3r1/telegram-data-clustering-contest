Giveaway: Enter to win a free pair of Apple AirPods Pro

12 Nov 2019, 17:12 by Amber Neely

Now through November 26, those of you who follow AppleInsider on Twitter will have a chance to win a pair of Apple AirPods Pro, valued at $249.

One lucky winner will receive a brand-new pair of Apple AirPods Pro with Wireless Charging Case sealed in box. These are Apple's newest AirPods, which feature active noise cancellation, a customizable fit and seal, adaptive EQ, quick charging, and the highly praised transparency mode.

Entering the giveaway is easy. Simply follow @AppleInsider on Twitter, like the giveaway tweet, and retweet the giveaway while tagging two friends. Additional chances for entries will pop up periodically on Twitter, so keep your eyes peeled!
