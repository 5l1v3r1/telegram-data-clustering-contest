Clothes worn by Sienna Miller and Kate Moss for sale in Plymouth for cheap this week

Emma Hanbury, founder of Marosa Ltd is selling her designs in a closing down sale

12 Nov 2019, 17:26 by Jess Morcom

A range of designs that have been worn by the likes of Sienna Miller and Kate Moss are being sold in Plymouth this week for cheap.

Emma Hanbury started Marosa Ltd in 2000 when she began selling her own designed clothes and accessories.

For many years she had a shop on the Kings Road in London as well as a successful online business.

Now, Emma is closing Marosa to pursue other ventures and all remaining stock will be available to buy at Wembury House this week.

"I've been living in Plymouth for around 30 years," Emma told Plymouth Live.

"I started Marosa around 20 years ago when my children were reasonably grown up.

"I've always had a love of India and embroidery as well as the colours they used, so this theme has been the basis for my designs.

"Marosa started organically and slowly, and peaked around ten years ago when I had a shop in London.

"But, I decided I needed to live more full time in Devon, so that coupled with London's high rent and the fact I would have to employ someone to work in the shop, meant that I worked mainly as an online business.

"Now, my attentions have moved to interior design, and I feel like this is the right time to close Marosa.

"This week, everything must go, and it would be amazing if it did."

Coats, jackets, skirts, shawls, stoles, boots, jewelery and more will be on sale this week.

Dates and times are Thursday, November 14, 6pm- 9pm, Friday, November 15, 10am - 5pm and Saturday, November 16, 10am - 1pm.
