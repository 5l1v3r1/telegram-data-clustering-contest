Magnetic Mix 064: Pinz & Pelz

Sydney/Berlin duo, Pinz & Pelz drop in for a drum and bass mix.

12 Nov 2019, 17:35 by Ryan Middleton

Sydney/ Berlin electronic music duo Pinz & Pelz released their new EP A Purer Life at the end of September. The six-tracker covers a lot of ground with some breakbeats, house music, ambient electronica and a touch of drum and bass. We asked them to make us a Magnetic Mix in honor of the new EP and they did something different. They pulled on the drum and bass thread that they rarely get to work with and made a full drum and bass mix.
