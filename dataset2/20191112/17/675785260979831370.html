I'm A Celebrity: How Katie Price seduced Peter Andre with lewd act at jungle reunion

Peter Andre wasn't sure if he saw a future with Katie Price when they reunited after I'm A Celebrity... but he didn't take much convincing

12 Nov 2019, 17:27 by Frances Kindon

When Katie Price and Peter Andre's paths crossed in the Australian jungle in 2004, it was lust at first sight.

The unlikely duo couldn't keep their hands off each other, driving their fellow I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here campmates to distraction with their constant flirting and hammock massages.

Katie - who was dating Scott Sullivan when she entered the show - ended up getting evicted as punishment for giving Pete the runaround.

But she was waiting for him on the outside and ready to take their romance to the next level... in more ways than one.

However, there was one hurdle standing in the way - and that was Peter's second thoughts.

"When I saw her for the first time with her hair done nicely and with full make-up on, do you know what I thought? Inside I went, ugh!" he wrote in his 2010 autobiography, All About Us - My Story.

"She was also a bit drunk and I didn't like that.

"She could tell I was staring at her face, which was so different from the one I'd become accustomed to in the jungle. All I could think to say that wouldn't be too unkind was, 'I think you looked better... in the jungle without the make-up and the hair off your face."

His concerned parents had already warned him to 'stay away' from the glamour model, but despite Katie's appearance, Pete was hooked.

Not wanting to waste any time, he says Katie virtually proposed right there and then, whispering, "There's one thing I have to say. It begins with M and it's a leap year."

Before he could answer, he was whisked off into isolation to protect those precious exclusive deals, but Katie managed to get a present to him - a recording of Phil Collins' Against All Odds.

That, Peter said, was the first time he realised they were meant to be.

"That was the moment I knew Katie and I were going to spend the rest of our lives together. Our love really was against all odds," he wrote.

A determined Katie then managed to weasel her way past Pete's security and infiltrate his lockdown by 'storming' into his room.

"She was wearing a horrible low cut top and a very short skirt. Her hair was all frizzy and looked a mess," Pete sniped, claiming she barged into the bathroom and again asked him to marry her.

"She was drunk and I couldn't take her seriously. Judging by the state of her, I doubted a relationship would last more than a week!" he said.

Then she started to cry before offering him a taste of the good stuff - a move Peter couldn't resist.

"No, you can't let me go. I've never felt his way about anyone," Pete said she wailed.

"Never, never, never. Look, I'll prove it to you... I'll never leave your side... every day... every night... do you want a little suck?"

Despite being in turmoil over whether to 'respect her or not', Pete admitted the lust too strong to resist and the "acorn was once again an oak".

From that moment on he was preoccupied by "what it would be like to be inside her," and Katie and Peter became a thing.
