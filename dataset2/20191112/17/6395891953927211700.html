Worcester Police asking for help to find missing man

12 Nov 2019, 17:49 by EndPlay

As part of its ongoing investigation and search, the Worcester Police Department is asking for public assistance in locating a 26-year-old man who has been missing for more than a month.

Paul Leite was reported missing on Sept. 28, 2019. It's believed he lived on or near Harlem Street in Worcester. He has ties to the Fall River area as well.

Police describe him as a 5'9" white man with dark hair weighing approximately 160 pounds.

Leite was last seen at a bank in Westwood, close to a construction site where he had been working.
