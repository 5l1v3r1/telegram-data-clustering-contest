5 Things to do this Weekend, November 15th to 17th in Southwest Florida

14 Nov 2019, 15:31

Make this weekend a great one! Here are some fun events around Southwest Florida to to help you do so.

Aviation Day at Page Field
Fort Myers, Fla. - This is a free community and family friendly event. Come see general aviation aircraft and helicopter exhibits. Enjoy plane and helicopter rides, bounce houses and face painting, demonstrations by airport police and fire departments, aviation-themed shopping, food trucks and more! Though parking and admission are free, we ask those coming to the event to help our local community by bringing a non-perishable food item. All donations will benefit the Harry Chapin Food Bank of Southwest Florida, Inc.

Event day and time: Saturday 11/16 from 10:00 am to 3:00 pm
