Wynonna Judd’s Daughter Released From Prison 6 Years Early On Parole

14 Nov 2019, 15:07 by Corey Atad

Wynonna Judd’s daughter is out of prison after serving two years.

According to People, Grace Pauline Kelley was released from her eight year sentence after being granted parole last week.

The 23-year-old will be under supervision, reporting to a parole office until the end of her sentence in 2024.

Kelley was originally sentenced for violating probation following a 2017 drug-related conviction. Kelley had reportedly left her court-mandated recovery program, leading to the warrant for her arrest.

In May 2017, Kelley pled guilty to meth manufacture, delivery, sale and possession on charges stemming from a 2015 arrest. She was originally sentenced to serve nearly 12 months in jail, but her sentence was suspended and she was placed on probation.
