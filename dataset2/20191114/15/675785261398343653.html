Arsenal 'send scouts' to watch Leicester defender Caglar Soyuncu

Arsenal are reportedly considering re-entering the transfer market for Caglar Soyuncu after his excellent performances for Leicester this season

14 Nov 2019, 15:32 by Rich Jones, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/rich-jones/

Arsenal have reportedly sent scouts to watch Leicester defender Caglar Soyuncu in international action.

Soyuncu has been a revelation for the Foxes this season since they sold Harry Maguire to Manchester United for a world record fee on the eve of the season.

He will be in action for Turkey against Iceland on Thursday and Turkish newspaper Star claim Gunners scouts will be in attendance to watch him in action.

They are also believed to be keeping an eye on Soyuncu's team-mate Merih Demiral, with Manchester United scouts believed to be joining them to watch the Juventus youngster in action.

Arsenal were linked with a move for Soyuncu when he played played for Germain outfit Freiburg.

He eventually opted to join Leicester in August 2018 on a five-year deal, playing a bit-part role last season with Maguire established as their first choice.

But he has been crucial to Leicester's stunning early season form, which has seen them move up to second in the Premier League table with the best defensive record in the league.

They have conceded just eight goals in 12 Premier League games, including a clean sheet in their 2-0 win over Arsenal last time out.

On the international stage, Soyuncu has, 23, has already won 27 caps.

Turkey sit top of their group in Euro 2020 qualifying, knowing a point against third-placed Iceland can secure their spot in next summer's tournament.
