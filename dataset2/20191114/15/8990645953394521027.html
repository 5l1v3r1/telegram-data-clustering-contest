What Thanksgiving Recipes are Texans Searching For the Most?

14 Nov 2019, 15:59 by Tony Kerns

With Turkey Day two weeks away, what recipes are you searching for? What do you think is the top recipe search for the state?

Using Google Trends, House Method compiled their list of the most searched main and desert dishes for the upcoming fall feast.

As the turkey is the main focal point for the meal, its understandable that it would be the most searched dish in the country, topping the list in nearly 1/5 of states, but not in Texas. For the Lonestar State, its all about the dressing.

 

On the desert side, it should come as no surprise that Texas and most of the states that searched for dressing were also searching for the best recipe for Pecan Pie.
