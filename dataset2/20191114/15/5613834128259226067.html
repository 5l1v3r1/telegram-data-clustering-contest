Like ‘RE2’, ‘Devil May Cry 5’ Also Receiving Additional DLC Content?

14 Nov 2019, 15:45 by Mike Wilson

It seems that Resident Evil 2 might not be the only one to be in line for more DLC.

After it was made known that Capcom added a hidden achievement in the latest update to their successful remake of the survival horror classic, Devil May Cry 5 has also received a similar update. According to the game’s Steam database page, a new changelist update includes entries for multiplayer, PvP and Co-op play. And like Resident Evil 2, Capcom has said nothing about any additional DLC for DMC5.

One could speculate that like RE2, Capcom is simply gearing up for a “Gold Edition” for DMC5, which has to date has sold over 2.5 million units worldwide. But, until Capcom says anything, we’ll just have to wait.
