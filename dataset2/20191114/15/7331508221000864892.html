Squirrels, cats, dogs ... bats? When animals ran loose in stadiums

14 Nov 2019, 15:00 by Kelly Cohen

Sports are great. Animals are great. So what is the general consensus when the two mix? Chaos -- and awesomeness.

Last week, a black cat ran onto the field during last week's New York Giants and Dallas Cowboys Monday Night Football game at MetLife Stadium, and last we heard, the furry friend was still on the loose.
