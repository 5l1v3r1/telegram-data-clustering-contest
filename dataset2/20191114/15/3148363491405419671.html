Intrusion Suspect Recovering After Being Shot by Homeowner

An intrusion suspect is recovering after being shot Wednesday morning by a Kansas City, Kansas, homeowner.

14 Nov 2019, 15:23 by AWR Hawkins

Fox4KC reports the homeowner woke to strange noises around 1 a.m., armed himself, and went to investigate.

KSHB reports the homeowner located “the suspect and shot him.”
