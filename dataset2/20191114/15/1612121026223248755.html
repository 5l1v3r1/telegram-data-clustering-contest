[Video] Check out the behind-the-scenes story of how the foldable Moto Razr came to be

14 Nov 2019, 15:46 by Peter Holden

After months of leaks and rumors, the Moto Razr folding smartphone has finally been unveiled as a Verizon exclusive (and EE in the UK) that will go on sale in January 2020. Unlike Samsung’s bodged Galaxy Fold and Huawei’s Mate X (that still hasn’t launched), the Moto Razr stays true to its iconic predecessor’s clamshell design and simply opens up to reveal what a display that is now classed as a normal-sized, a 6.2-inch smartphone display. Another notable feature is the $1,500 price tag that may just make your wallet weep a little. If you are curious as to how Motorola developed this new, modern take on the legendary Razr, you are in luck because Moto has posted a video showing what happened behind the scenes, and we’ve embedded it for your enjoyment after the break.
