A$AP Rocky playing Thanksgiving Eve show in Brooklyn

14 Nov 2019, 15:27 by Andrew Sacher

A$AP Mob just announced the 2020 edition of their annual Yams Day, which happens on January 17 at Barclays Center (presale tickets, passwords = YAMMYGANG or TESTING), but A$AP Rocky will play NYC sooner than that. He’s doing a Thanksgiving Eve (11/27) show at Avant Gardner in Brooklyn with DJ Spade, DJ Clue, DJ Moma, DJ Boof, and Odalys. Tickets are on sale.

Rocky’s only other upcoming US date at the moment is Rolling Loud LA. He also recently announced his first Sweden show since his Sweden arrest.
