All about Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas' majestic new home in Los Angeles

14 Nov 2019, 15:05 by Filmfare

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas were on a lookout for a new residence in Los Angeles for a while now. The couple had been shortlisting several properties since the past few months and latest reports around the couple confirm that they have bought a new abode in LA. Apparently, the couple has spent an enormous $20 million to buy the house which has seven bedrooms, eleven bathrooms and an outdoor space. Reports also suggest the Nick's brother Joe Jonas and his wife Sophie Turner have bought a house in the same neighbourhood.

Nickyanka, as they are lovingly called by their fans have spent around Rs 144 crore to buy their dream house and with this they have set new records in the Encino area of Los Angeles as it is one of the most expensive properties there. We congratulate the couple for finally striking their dream deal.
