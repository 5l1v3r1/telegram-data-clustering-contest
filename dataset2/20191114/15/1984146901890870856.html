Geordie Shore star Bethan Kershaw 'storms out of the house' and off the show after blazing row

Filming is underway on series 21 of Geordie Shore but one cast member is reported to have quit the MTV show

14 Nov 2019, 15:51 by Simon Duke

Geordie Shore is known for its shock departures - there have been plenty over the years - and it looks like the latest series has brought another.

While series 20 has just got going on MTV, filming for series 21 of the Newcastle-set reality show is already well under way.

Months before it's due to air, the forthcoming series has already given us plenty to talk about, from the shock casting of former X Factor star Amelia Lily to the arrival of two new lads, one of whom is from nowhere near Newcastle!

But this week has brought the most explosive moment to date, with the news that Bethan Kershaw has quit the show after a blazing row with boyfriend Beau Brennan.

Former Ibiza Weekender star and beautician Bethan and Whitley Bay lad and civil engineer Beau both joined the Geordie Shore cast as newcomers earlier this year for series 19 and sparks quickly flew between them.

They've already had an argument on screen in series 20 and, according to The Sun , tempers have flared again during filming for the new episodes, leading to Bethan storming out of the Geordie Shore house and off the show.

A source told the paper: "Bethan and Beau got into a heated argument and things escalated really quickly.

"It was one of the worst rows they have ever had - really explosive stuff. In the end, Bethan was very emotional and just walked. She's had enough.

"The bosses are panicking because she is a great cast member and they really don't want to lose her."
