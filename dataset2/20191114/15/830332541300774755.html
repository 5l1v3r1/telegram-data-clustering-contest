STOUGHTON/EDGERTON-Gerry H. Linnerud, age 62, of Edgerton, passed away unexpectedly on Monday, Nov. 11, 2019.

He was born on May 28, 1957, in Stoughton, the son of Erling and Mary (Lee) Linnerud.

Gerry graduated from Stoughton High School in 1975.Â  Gerry was a wonderful man with a generous heart. He loved spending time with his family and friends, especially with his grandsons. On the weekends, he could be found at the local short track cheering on his favorite driver, relaxing at his land up north, traveling to North Dakota, or fishing at one of his favorite spots.
