East Bridgewater Police looking for driver who hit 2 girls, 2 horses

14 Nov 2019, 02:10 by Mike Saccone

EAST BRIDGEWATER, Mass. - East Bridgewater Police are looking for a newer model white Ford SUV that allegedly hit two teenage girls and two horses before taking off, Deputy Chief Paul O’Brien told Boston 25 News. 

The crash happened Wednesday evening on Belmont Street. 

O’Brien said one of the girls suffered possible leg injuries and one of the horses was being checked out by a veterinarian for possible injuries.
