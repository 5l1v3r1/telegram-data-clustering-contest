AOA scheduled to have a comeback with 5 members.

14 Nov 2019, 04:03 by greenbaypackers

On, November 26th, AOA are scheduled to have their comeback, with the title track called "Come See Me"

What used to be a 8-member group has now been reduced to 5 following Youkyung's departure in 2016 and Choa's departure in 2017, and Mina's departure in 2019. The current group feature members Jimin, Yoona, Hyejung, Seolhyun, and Chanmi
