Bushfire survival guide in Friday’s The Courier-Mail

14 Nov 2019, 04:52

With Queensland experiencing unprecedented, catastrophic bushfire conditions across the state, we’ve put together a full survival guide to help you protect your family and property.

The guide, produced in conjunction with Suncorp, will be available in Friday’s paper.

It features information to prepare your bushfire survival plan, what to pack if you decide to leave early, and a survival kit checklist.

There are tips for safeguarding your home and property during bushfire season, protecting pets and how to check for danger signs when driving during a bushfire.

Understanding bushfire behaviour and how the terrain in your neighbourhood may influence it, is also explained in the Bushfire Survival Guide.

Don’t miss your copy in Friday’s The Courier-Mail.
