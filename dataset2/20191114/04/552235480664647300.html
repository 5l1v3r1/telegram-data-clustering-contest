How to incorporate retinol into your skincare routine

Retinol is a form of vitamin A found in over-the-counter products that helps reduce fine lines and wrinkles and increase collagen production

14 Nov 2019, 04:10 by Fiora Mcfarlane

The beauty world has been banging on about how good retinol is for quite some time now, so chances are you’re already familiar with this anti-aging superstar ingredient.

A form of vitamin A, retinol is an ingredient for reducing wrinkles and fine lines and helping prevent them.

It helps slow down the aging process by boosting collagen production - it’s also great for acne.

There really is a retinol product for everyone - from creams and serums to gels and oils, you can let your personal preference lead the way, or let your skin type guide you.
