Happy Anniversary DeepVeer: Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone dazzle in red and gold in these first few pictures from Tirupati

14 Nov 2019, 04:18 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

'Padmaavat' actors Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone are celebrating their first wedding anniversary today. The actors decided to go divine way to make the day even more special. Yesterday both the stars along with their families jetted off to Tirupati to seek almighty's blessings.

Here we bring you the first glimpse of DeepVeer dazzling in royal traditional outfits on their first anniversary. While surfing the microblogging site we got our hands on this gorgeous picture of the two posing for the camera from Tirupati. Deepika is seen wearing a heavily embellished red traditional saree. She accessorised her look with a traditional choker set, a gold necklace and adorned sindoor on her forehead while Ranveer was seen in an embroidered golden sherwani paired with brown specs.
