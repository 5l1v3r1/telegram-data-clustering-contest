Fairhaven Police investigating fatal one-car crash

14 Nov 2019, 04:34 by Manoella Macedo

FAIRHAVEN, Mass. - Police in Fairhaven are investigating a fatal single-car crash that happened on Wednesday evening.

At around 5:15 p.m., officers responded to the area of 475 Bridge Street after receiving 911 calls regarding the crash.

The car involved in the crash, a 2008 Ford F-150, veered off the road and struck a tree before becoming engulfed in flames.

The driver and sole occupant, 31-year-old Shane Pacheco, of North Dartmouth, was pronounced deceased on scene.

Authorities continue to investigate this incident.
