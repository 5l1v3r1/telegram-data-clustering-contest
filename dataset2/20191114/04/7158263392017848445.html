How to Get Bigger Lips: 9 Best Lip-Plumping Products and Tips

14 Nov 2019, 04:26

So you want bigger, fuller, holy-wow lips, eh? Maybe you’ve been inspired by the recent trend of EVERY SINGLE CELEBRITY getting lip injections or maybe you’ve always wanted to pump up your natural lips a bit. Either way, the question of how to make your lips bigger is a very common one (like, I get a text about it all too frequently). But here’s the thing: Unless you want to get lip injections—which are truly not as scary as they sound but do kinda hurt and can cost anywhere between $500 and $2,000—there’s no tried-and-true DIY way to get bigger lips that last.

Still, that doesn’t mean you need to go under the needle to get some immediate plumping effects. With the right combo of products and techniques, even naturally thin lips can look a little va-va-voom. And to help you out on your lip journey, I broke down every tip, trick, and product, below, to help you get bigger lips ASAP.

2 How to Get Bigger Lips: Lip Scrubs Burt’s Bees 100% Natural Conditioning Lip Scrub

Okay, I know this is the most basic-seeming tip, but you’d be surprised by how a little lip scrub can go a really, really long way. Why? Gently buff your lips each morning in circles with a lip scrub or a toothbrush to lift flakes.

6 How to Get Bigger Lips: Contour Kiko Milano Sicilian Notes Long-Lasting Lip Liner

ulta.com $9.00 SHOP NOW

Lip contouring? Yup, it’s a thing, and as someone who has personally tried this, I can attest that it really, truly works. Just grab a lip liner one shade darker than your natural lip color (or the gloss or lipstick you’re planning to wear), line your lips, scribble in the corner Vs of your mouth, and draw a line down the center of your bottom lip. Soften the lines by gently smudging them with your finger, then add your gloss or lipstick. Confused? Follow this lip contour guide.
