It’s Time To Admit Climate Denial Is Making Australia’s Bushfires Worse

14 Nov 2019, 04:01 by Elfy Scott

Extensive and devastating bushfires along the east coast have made this week horrifying for a lot of Australians.

At least four lives have been lost in the unprecedented blazes and more than 200 properties have been destroyed in NSW since Friday.

Koala populations have also been severely impacted, with at least 350 animals dying after fires swept through the Lake Innes Nature Reserve.
