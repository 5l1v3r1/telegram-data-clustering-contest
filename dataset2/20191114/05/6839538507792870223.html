Instagram-famous mummy blogger Caitlin Fladager claims smoking weed makes her a ‘better mum’

14 Nov 2019, 05:27

An Instagram-famous mummy blogger has been praised online after admitting to smoking weed daily, claiming it makes her a “better mum” and should be “just as acceptable as a glass of wine”.

In a post to her more than 288,000 Instagram followers, Canadian blogger Caitlin Fladager - known for her “mum truth” confessions online - claimed smoking marijuana has helped her, especially in caring for her two children Adriana (six) and Jack (four).

The 23-year-old confessed to smoking weed daily while her children slept, saying it aids her anxiety, allowed her to stop taking antidepressants and has assisted in curbing her dangerous under-eating and alcohol consumption.

“Marijuana is my glass of wine,” the mother-of-two said in the post.

“It’s so funny to me how frowned upon marijuana is.

“No one looks twice when a mum says she enjoys ‘mum juice’, aka wine, after her kids are in bed.

“But when a mum says she smokes weed, it’s a huge shock.”

The post, claiming to “bring awareness” to the the stigma around 420-friendly mums, has drawn massive support online, with more than 25,000 people “liking” it in 48 hours.

“Yes finally someone said it,” a mother-of-three commented on the post.

Another mum agreed with Fladager’s comments about the drug being frowned upon as opposed to drinking.

“It’s crazy how something that’s safer with less negative side effects than alcohol can be so looked down on,” she said.

One mum from Australia said she “felt this in her soul” as marijuana is frowned up for its illegality.

“I wish people would see the benefits rather than just swearing it’s bad because it’s what they’ve been told,” she wrote.

Fladager has been married to her high-school sweetheart Noah for more than a decade and describes herself as a mental health advocate.
