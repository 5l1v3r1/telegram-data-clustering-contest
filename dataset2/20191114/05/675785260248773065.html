Brooklyn Beckham dating Marvel star Nicola Peltz - the fourth woman in four months

Brooklyn Beckham is not hanging about when it comes to finding a new beau as he has been linked with his fourth woman in as many months as he looks for 'the one'

14 Nov 2019, 05:58 by James Brinsford, https://www.facebook.com/james.brinsford

Brooklyn Beckham is playing the field just like his father David would command a football pitch, as he goes official with his fourth woman in as many months.

The 20-year-old has found himself jumping from relationship to relationship but it seems that he may have found a keeper this time.

The son of former England football captain David and Spice Girl turned fashion designer, Victoria, has gone 'official' with 24-year-old Marvel actress Nicola Peltz.

For her part, Nicola has been busy leaving comments on Brooklyn's recent Instagram posts, adding an angel emoji to a couple snaps.

The pair are looking smitten with each other in a photo that was posted in social media and it has been reported that Brooklyn is already getting on well with her family.

A source told The Sun: “Brooklyn has already met her family and he gets on well with her brother, they even partied together.”

Brooklyn will be hoping that his parents will be more approving of Nicola after they were concerned about his recent dates.

It was reported that they were less than happy with his 'secret fling' with Kate Moss' half-sister Lottie and they were worried that he was gaining a reputation for being a 'ladykiller'.

Brooklyn split from model Hana Cross in the summer and Nicola is the fourth woman that he has been linked with since that time.

The wannabe photographer has also been dating actresses Natalie Ganzhorn and Phoebe Torrance.

The latter is the 27-year-old daughter of golfing legend Sam, and the strange twist in that relationship was that Phoebe used to work as a lookalike of his mum, Victoria.

Brooklyn is still looking to make his name as a photographer and was given a boost when he landed the opportunity to take part in a photoshoot for America's Next Top Model with Heidi Klum.

He was reportedly not as comfortable with the chance to snap a supermodel but will hope the experience will hold him in good stead for the future.
