Hour-by-hour weather forecast for Nottingham as city is hit by flooding

Rain will continue to persist throughout the day

14 Nov 2019, 14:57 by Hayley Watson

Nottingham residents have been grappling with difficult weather conditions today as heavy rainfall hit the city.

A yellow weather warning for rain is currently in place across the region until 10pm this evening.

Flooding caused by rainfall across Nottingham city centre has disrupted transport links, with more than a dozen bus routes affected.

Residents in parts of Nottinghamshire have been advised to be prepared for flooding as flood alerts remains in place around parts of the River Trent.

However if you were hoping for a drier evening, then you might not be so lucky.

Here is an hour-by-hour weather forecast for the city for the rest of the day, according to the Met Office.
