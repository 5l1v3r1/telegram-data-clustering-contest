Harry Styles has a new style icon – Princess Diana

14 Nov 2019, 14:24 by Bashiera Parker (@bparks_)

Cape Town – Harry Styles may have just made his boldest fashion move yet channelling Princess Diana.

The Lights Upsinger stepped out in New York City this week wearing navy slacks and a striped shirt, reports Harper’s BAZAAR, and completed his look with a quirky and familiar-looking sheep vest.

Princess Diana wore a wool jumper with the same print in the 80s. She was famously photographed wearing it at polo matches in Windsor.

And now, we can’t decide who wore it better.

According to Bustle, Harry Styles’ Lanvin sweater costs $1 190 (R17 631) and is part of the Spring 2020 collection.

Princess Diana’s sweater was designed by Muir and Osbourne, with one distinct difference: a lone black sheep.
