Planet Coaster is heading to Xbox One

I'm glad someone's bringing these sim titles over

14 Nov 2019, 14:39 by Jordan Devore

It's been slim pickings for console players who want to build and run their own theme parks, but that's changing soon, at least for one notable game. Today at Microsoft's X019 event, Frontier Developments announced it's bringing Planet Coaster over to Xbox One. Soak in these RollerCoaster Tycoon vibes.
