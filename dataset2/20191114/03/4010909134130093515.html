Apple introduces the new 16-inch Macbook Pro with improved keyboard and graphics from RM10499

14 Nov 2019, 03:37

Just recently, Apple officially unveiled the 16-inch and 13-inch MacBook Pro from RM12199 and RM10499 respectively. In terms of the tech specs,  it comes equipped with a 9th generation Intel Core i9 processor, up to 16GB of 2666MHz DDR4 RAM and 1TB of SSD storage.

But that’s not all, as the laptop also features a 16-inch Retina display with True Tone, a Touch Bar as Touch ID. Furthermore, the laptop also introduces a new and improved Magic Keyboard, which has a refined scissor mechanism that gives users a much better typing experience.

You won’t need to worry about the battery life of the laptop, as it also comes with a 100Wh battery, which is the largest ever in a MacBook Pro. Last but not least, it also utilizes up to an AMD Radeon Pro 5500M which brings up to 210% improvement in terms of performance depending on the software.
