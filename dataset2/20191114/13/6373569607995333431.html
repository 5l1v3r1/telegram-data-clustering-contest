Heartbroken family pay tribute to Shane Hedges after fatal crash in Plymouth

Shane died after a single-vehicle collision in the early hours of Sunday morning

14 Nov 2019, 13:12 by Katie Timms

A heartbroken family have paid tribute to their loved one after a single-vehicle collision in the early hours of Sunday morning.

Shane Hedges, aged 25, was driving a red Ford C-Max and hit a wall at the junction of Ferndale Road and North Prospect Road. Police have now formally named him.

Devon and Cornwall Police officers were called to the fatal crash at 1.38am on November 10 and requested firefighters to attend the scene.

Shane was taken to Derriford Hospital, where sadly he was pronounced dead.

His family have now issued an official tribute to him.

It reads: “In loving memory of our son, brother, and overall great guy. We will miss you dearly and never forget you.”

Dozens of people offered their condolences to Shane's family and friends at this difficult time.

Neil Williams described Shane as "one of the nicest lad you will meet".

He commented: "One of the nicest lads you will meet. Total gutted to read this today. Thoughts are with his family. R.I.P."

Kim Squance offered her "heartfelt condolences" to the young man's loved ones.

She said: "My heartfelt condolences to this young man's family and friends. I could see police and road shut but didn't know what was going on life is so very cruel god bless you young man. xx"

Sarah Burridge commented: "Heartbreaking.. thoughts are with his family and friends. RIP young man. Cherish your loved ones.. you never know what tomorrow may bring."

Sara Hawkins also offered her condolences to the man's family and friends.

She said: "So very sad for a family here in Plymouth today... literally a few minutes away from my home...sympathies with his family members xx"

Police are appealing for witnesses

Police are now appealing for witnesses and more information to help with their enquiries.

No other vehicles are thought to have been involved and officers are working to establish the cause of the collision.

A police spokesperson said: "At 01:38 hours on Sunday 10th November 2019, Police were notified of a single vehicle road traffic collision at the junction of Ferndale Road with North Prospect Road, Plymouth (OSGR 045973, 057381).

"The vehicle involved, a red Ford C-Max, was being driven by a 25-year old local male. No other vehicles are thought to have been involved in this collision.

"Police thank the public for their patience while they investigated the scene."

If you would like to report information to the police anonymously, you can use the charity Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.️

Members of the public who may have seen the vehicle in the Weston Mill area, or witnessed the collision, are asked to contact the police via telephone on 101, or email 101@dcpolice.co.uk, quoting log 84 of November 10, 2019.
