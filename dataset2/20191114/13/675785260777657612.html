Woman bites pet cat on the head to get back at boyfriend in sickening live video

Daria Turchina, from Dmitrov, Moscow, in Russia, threatened to crush the pet cat to death in the sinister video after having a heated argument with her boyfriend

14 Nov 2019, 13:01 by Tiffany Lo

A woman was filmed biting her boyfriend's pet cat on the head in a desperate attempt to get back at him after they had argued.

Daria Turchina, 38, streamed the animal abuse live on Telegram, a messaging app similar to Whatsapp.

At the start of the clip she can be seen smiling at the camera while holding the innocent moggie.

But she then holds the cat to her chest and says: "Do you know that I can kill you? I can kill you, crush you.

"Do you understand me?"

Daria, from Dmitrov, in the western Russian region of Moscow Oblast, called the cat a 'b****' and 'filthy little w*****'.

She then bites the pet which reacts by howling in pain.

In another clip she puts the cat on her lap and presses onto its paws strongly.

Local media reported that Daria had a heated row with her boyfriend and was trying to upset him by threatening and harming him the cat.

The details of the argument were not reported and it's unclear whether local authorities will investigate her for appearing to abuse an animal.
