M&S is selling Percy Pig muffins for £1 - and they look delicious

A yummy treat for people with a sweet tooth

14 Nov 2019, 13:54 by Melisha Kaur, Christopher Harper

Marks and Spencer has released a game-changer with Percy Pig muffins.

The sweet treats are very pink with a fluffy sponge and delicious raspberry jam, reports the Mirror.

Each is topped with fruit-flavoured buttercream and, of course, a Percy Pig sweet.

The best part? Shoppers can pick one up one of these tasty treats for just £1 - you don't need to tell us twice.

It hasn't been long since M&S launched its Percy Pig advent calendars for Christmas 2019 , but the new Percy Pig muffins were recently spotted in a post on Instagram that sent fans of the chewy sweet into overdrive.

One user commented: "Ooh you can always count on M&S to deliver the goods."

Another said: "I'm so getting myself a percy pig muffin."

If you're thinking of picking up a Percy Pig muffin (or two), the cakes can be found in the M&S bakery sections.
