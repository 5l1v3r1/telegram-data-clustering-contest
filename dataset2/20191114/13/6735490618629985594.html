Ellie Goulding Puts Her Twist On ‘River’ By Joni Mitchell

14 Nov 2019, 13:58 by Shakiel Mahjouri

Ellie Goulding is doing justice to a classic song.

Goulding shared her cover of “River” by Joni Mitchell Thursday on Twitter. The cover can be streamed exclusively on Amazon Music.

The English singer-songwriter does not try and reinvent the wheel with her cover. Goulding’s effort mostly stays true to the original in composition but has a slightly more restrained pace.
