This Morning: Vanessa Feltz tells caller it’s ok for heavy-drinker to get drunk in front of kids - and viewers aren’t happy

The advice guru told a caller that they should let her ex heavy-drinker partner and father-in-law get drunk 'for one day'

14 Nov 2019, 13:28 by Sian Filcher

This Morning’s Vanessa Feltz dished out her advice on some festive family issues this morning - sparking twitter outrage.

One caller explained why she didn’t want her father-in-law at their home on Christmas Day.

Lucy, the caller, said that her partner’s father ‘drinks a lot of alcohol’ and explained how her partner has cut down on heavy drinking.

She said: “I feel so guilty, but I don’t want my children to think it’s ok to drink so much.”

Revealing that her children are two and 11 months old, Vanessa said: “I don’t think they’re likely to come to a conclusion about whether he drinks too much or not when they’re so young.

“Even if they did, you could always explain that it’s a very very special occasion, it only happens once a year and lots of grown ups drink too much on that day.”

Lucy revealed how her partner’s father will encourage him to drink more, and says that he has ‘done so well’ in cutting back.

She said: “He wants his dad there.

“They know that they’re just a bad influence on each other.”

Vanessa suggested not having any alcohol in the house on Christmas Day, but the caller admitted that her father-in-law would turn up with his own.

Vanessa said: “It’s a difficult one, but on the other hand it’s just one day.

“So if they both do get drunk on one day, it’s only one day.

“And then the next day is another day.”

Viewers took to Twitter after the advice segment, with one branding the advice ‘ludicrous’.

She said: “Vanessa Feltz, telling a worried young mum its OK if her partner gets drunk on Christmas day, "it's only one day", utterly ludicrous advice!”

Another tweeted: “Woah did Vanessa just day that if a recovering alcoholic drinks for one day, it’s not a bad thing?”

Another fan said that the advice was ‘disgusting’, while one angry viewer claimed that Vanessa is ‘not qualified to give advice’.

A furious watcher said: “Vanessa have you ever had you Christmas day spoilt because of a drunk person let alone your children's day spoilt? I think if you’re supposed to be giving advice to people be more empathetic and actually listen!”
