Pet Shop Boys announce ‘Hotspot’, share “Burning the heather” ft. Bernard Butler

14 Nov 2019, 09:48 by Bill Pearis

Veteran synthpop duo Pet Shop Boys have announced new album Hotspot which will be out January 24. They’ve once again worked with producer/collaborator Stuart Price (Madonna, New Order), and the album includes this year’s single “Dreamland” that features Years and Years. They say it’s the final instalment in a trilogy of records with Price that started with 2013’s Electric and 2016’s Super. The new single from the album is “Burning the heather,” a dreamy electro ballad that nicely layers acoustic guitars — played by onetime Suede guitarist Bernard Butler — into the mix.
