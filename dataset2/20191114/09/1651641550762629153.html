Nokia is planning to launch a TV next month and here is what we know about it

14 Nov 2019, 09:00 by Raka

After OnePlus and Motorola, another major player who is all set to enter the smart TV business is Nokia. The company is planning to launch its first-ever smart television next month in India in partnership with e-commerce giant Flipkart. According to reports, Nokia’s smart television will only be available in India.

As per sources close to the development, Nokia’s upcoming smart TV will feature a 50-inch-plus 4K ultra-HD panel and powering the smart LED TV will be Android 9.0. This also means that the smart TV will have support for the Google Play Store.

Intelligent Dimming Technology, which is similar to call local dimming or micro dimming technology, is an interesting feature that you will find in the upcoming Nokia smart TV and as per sources, this will produce better contrast and deep Black Levels.

Nokia is ready to launch its first-ever smart TV in December this year and it is expected to be available only on Flipkart.

This smart television is going to be available only in India, at least for now, and is part of the Government’s ‘Make in India’ initiative, according to an official statement by Flipkart.
