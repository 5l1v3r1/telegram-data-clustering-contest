Kym Marsh's daughter Polly lands first film acting role alongside dad Jamie Lomas

Polly Lomas, eight, will be starring in a new film about homelessness called Lead Belly next to dad Jamie Lomas and ex-Emmerdale star Sammy Winward

14 Nov 2019, 09:21 by Emmeline Saunders

Kym Marsh's eight-year-old daughter Polly has scored her first film role after following in both her mum and dad's theatrical footsteps.

The little girl will star in a new film about homelessness called Lead Belly, alongside her actor dad Jamie Lomas and former Emmerdale star Sammy Winward.

Jamie plays lead character Mark, an armed forces veteran who is suffering from PTSD from his former career.

Proud mum Kym announced the news on Twitter , telling followers: "So very proud of my beautiful little girl being a part of such an important film. Huge well done to her daddy @jamielomas1 and for everyone involved in this project.

"Please everybody check out the link below and help support. Thank you," she added.

Polly is already familiar with the world of showbiz, having grown up with two soap star parents.

Kym, who left Coronation Street after 13 years to help her eldest daughter Emilie with her new baby, will be appearing on stage with Polly in her Elf: The Musical UK tour next month.

Polly will play the part of a naughty child in a scene with Santa, while Kym takes on the role of Buddy the Elf's girlfriend Jovie.

"It's amazing that I get to share the next part of my acting journey with Polly," Kym told the Loose Women panel in April.

"She loves acting and performing, so for her to land her first role aged eight is great, and we're all so proud of her.

"It's lovely to think she wants to follow in my footsteps and I know she'll do a great job. I think she was born to be in the spotlight â€” she's quite the little character."

Polly has been learning the trade at her Hollyoaks star dad Jamie's First Take School of Acting in Manchester, of which she "loves every second".

"I'm going to be some travelling around the country with the theatre tour and I don't want to be away from Polly," Kym wrote in her OK! magazine column earlier this year.

"I got told there was a small part for her playing one of the children, so it's worked out perfectly!

"She's been going to her dad's acting school and she's loved every second of it."

It comes after Jamie was embroiled in a fight with Emmerdale ex-star Asan N'jie at the TV Choice Awards in September.

Asan, who played Ellis Chapman in the ITV soap, was sacked after the Mirror's video showed him brawling with Jamie and threatening to stab him.
