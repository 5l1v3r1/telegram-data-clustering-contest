Wanted Quezon ex-cop falls

14 Nov 2019, 09:59 by Gemi Formaran

LUCENA CITY— Joint elements of Quezon Provincial Field Unit and Regional Special Operations Team of the Criminal Investigation and Detection Group (CIDG) and city police station on Wednesday arrested a former policeman wbo has been wanted for frustrated homicide.

CIDG-Quezon Provincial Field Unit chief, Major Eliot Eusebio, identified the nabbed felon as ex-cop Rodolfo Baldevieso Moreno, 54, and resident of Purok Dalungyan, Bgy. Isabang, here.

Eusebio said Moreno went into hiding after he was convicted for frustrated homicide on November 14, 2011.

But on the afternoon of Wednesday, Eusebio said they were able to arrest Moreno along Maharlika Highway of said  village by virtue of an arrest order.

He said the ex-cop is listed number 12 among Lucena City Police Station’s Most Wanted Persons-Manhunt Charlie.

He is presently under the custody of CIDG Provincial Field Unit office.
