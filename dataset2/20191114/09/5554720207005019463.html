Indian arrested in UAE for domestic violence after wife posts video with bruised and bleeding eye

14 Nov 2019, 09:15 by JKR Staff

The Sharjah Police have arrested an Indian man after his wife posted a video of herself with a bruised and bleeding eye while desperately seeking help.

A tweet by the Sharjah Police read, “A note of what is being shared through social media about the case of an Asian nationality women who was exposed to violence by her husband in the Emirate of Sharjah, the case was dealt with by the competent authorities.”
