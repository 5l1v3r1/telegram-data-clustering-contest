Build-A-Bear launches The Snowman and Grinch toys for Christmas

This Grinch stuffed gift bundle is guaranteed to get everyone in the festive spirit!

14 Nov 2019, 09:34 by Linda Howard

Build-A-Bear Workshop is selling a new range of The Snowman and The Grinch toys for Christmas.

The new collection includes a baby Grinch and an adult Grinch and the grown-up bear even comes with a three-piece Santa suit!

The little green monsters are available to buy in different bundles at the Build-A-Bear website here .

The adult Grinch , complete with his Santa outfit, costs £35.50 and comes with the option of dressed or undressed, so that recipients can do it at home themselves - Buy it here .

 

A voice insert featuring six of the character’s most memorable phrases including, “Ho, ho, no” and “Merry Christmas, Whoville” costs an additional £6 - Buy it here .

There’s even an option to make it personal and add your own voice recording or a beating heart inside the adorable toy - Buy it here .

The Young Baby Grinch is an online exclusive and available to buy separately online for £10 - but has already sold out.

And if you’re looking for more festive fun, Build-A-Bear is also selling the iconic Snowman stuffed toy for £18 - Buy it here .

The familiar Snowman figure comes complete with his matching green hat and scarf - it’s hard to believe this lovable character first came to life one winter’s night 40 years ago!
