Rainbow celebrate 10th anniversary with 'Aurora' MV

14 Nov 2019, 09:15 by germainej

Rainbow have revealed their music video for "Aurora"!

The MV follows the Rainbow members as a group of friends who go on to achieve their dreams. "Aurora" is a track from Rainbow's 10th anniversary special album, which also includes "I Dream of You".
