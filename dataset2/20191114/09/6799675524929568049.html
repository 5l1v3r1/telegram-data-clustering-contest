TytoHome medical exam kit now sold across US at Best Buy

Israel-invented comprehensive home telehealth solution is now available nationwide in Best Buy stores and on BestBuy.com.

14 Nov 2019, 09:25 by ISRAEL21c Staff

TytoHome, a home medical kit for examining body temperature, heart, lungs, skin, ears, throat and abdomen, and sending that data to a healthcare provider, is now available at Best Buy stores across the United States and at BestBuy.com.

The healthcare industry’s first all-in-one modular device and telehealth platform for on-demand, remote medical exams, TytoHome was invented by TytoCare of Israel. The company now is headquartered in New York with R&D in Netanya, Israel.

The nationwide US availability of TytoHome follows a successful initial rollout in the Midwest.

Using the data transmitted from home via the TytoHome platform, Tyto Care healthcare provider partners available 24/7 can diagnose ear infections, sore throats, fever, cold and flu, allergies, stomach aches, upper respiratory infections, coughs, rashes and more.
