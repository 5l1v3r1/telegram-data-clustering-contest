Heartbreaking deleted scene from 'Avengers: Endgame' released

14 Nov 2019, 09:30

Cape Town – Katherine Langford's deleted scene in Avengers: Endgame can now finally be seen.

Although the 13 Reasons Why actress was cast in the film fans were confused when she didn't appear in the theatrically released version earlier this year as her scene never made the final cut.

On Tuesday the film was released on Disney's streaming service with the addition of the deleted scene.

According to CoverMedia, it is a devastating goodbye between Tony Stark (Robert Downey Jr) and his daughter, Morgan (Langford).

The scene titled Tony at the Way Station takes place in a realm, not unlike the one in which Thanos finds himself during the end of Avengers Infinity War, explains CNN.

What follows is a conversation between Tony and Morgan, who is now an adult.

He questions whether he made the right decision to sacrifice himself; she reassures him, telling him she is proud of him.
