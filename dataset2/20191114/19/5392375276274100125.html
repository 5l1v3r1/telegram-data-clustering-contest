14 Nov 2019, 19:16 by Ying Hua

Rate this post

Energizer® Mobile announces today the launch of its store on Amazon to strengthen its online presence and deployment in the European market. More than 300 SKUs of smartphones, feature phones, charging and protection accessories are available. Delivery is free in Metropolitan France for all smartphones, and for mobiles and accessories from 25€ of purchase. Amazon Prime members benefit from one-day delivery on most items in the store. 

“The launch of a store dedicated to the Energizer® Mobile brand on Amazon is a key step in our development strategy on marketplaces in Europe. The French store is the first we are opening,” said Jean-Daniel Beurnier, CEO of Avenir Telecom, which holds the exclusive worldwide Energizer® license for mobile phones and accessories. 

“The choice to open Amazon online stores in France, Italy, Spain and Germany strengthens our online presence in the European market and allows us to offer a wide selection of products to our customers with extremely fast delivery and high quality customer service,” adds Jean-Daniel Beurnier. 

Long-lasting mobile phones and accessories 

Among the 300 references available in the store are the Energizer® smartphones and mobiles. Featuring large capacity batteries for hours of battery life, the range also includes rugged and waterproof phones as well as mobiles powered by the KaiOS operating system. Phone + SIM card bundles are also available for purchase. 

Energizer® also offers a wide selection of charging and protection accessories. It includes, among others, fast chargers, car chargers, cables, reinforced cases, screen protectors and USB-C hubs. The lifetime warranty range developed from robust materials, as well as a complete range of charging accessories certified «Made for iPhone, iPad» by Apple is available.

The online store on Amazon France is available on this link. The Energizer® Mobile stores on Amazon Germany, Italy and Spain are scheduled to open in 2020.
