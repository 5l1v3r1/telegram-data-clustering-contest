Social media users lambast ‘good girl’ eShun for releasing profane song

14 Nov 2019, 19:34 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Netizens have descended heavily on Ghanaian female singer and songwriter Ethel Esi Eshun popularly known as eShun for recording a ‘profane’ song.

Fans of the “Someone Loves Me” hitmaker are obviously not pleased with eShun’s new song dubbed “Koti Ma No”.

eShun recently released the video of her new song and her fans are not happy about it since she is not known for producing such profane songs.

Some fans advised the musician to desist from that and stick to her ‘good girl’ brand.

Most of the musician’s fans are disappointed in her for switching from her decent brand to something which they believe is unacceptable.

Recently, eShun revealed in an interview that she has switched to do danceable songs due to ‘hunger’.

According to the musician, its now time to cash in on her talent, therefore, she has joined the trend and has stopped doing sensible songs
