Jinbo, who’s worked with BTS on multiple albums over the years, talks about their good chemistry and working with them

14 Nov 2019, 19:43 by lbc

Jinbo is the man behind the song "여기봐 (Look Here)," "Anpanman," and "Pied-Pier." 

He also co-produced J-Hope's recent Single "Chicken Noodle Soup."

He also worked with f(x), SHINee, Red Velvet, and BoA. 

In this interview, he shares his story with Jung Kook and Jimin, how their talent and work ethic changed his views on the idol group.
