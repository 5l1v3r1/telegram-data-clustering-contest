West Lothian stops to pay respect to war heroes at Remembrance events

Remembrance Sunday and Armistice Day events are held around West Lothian

14 Nov 2019, 16:03 by Debbie Hall, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/debbie-hall/

Hundreds of people in West Lothian gathered to pay their respects at Remembrance events held around West Lothian on Sunday.

Veterans and their families also joined well wishers in towns and villages around the county to fall silent to remember those who gave the ultimate sacrifice in World War One and Two and in more recent conflicts around the world.
