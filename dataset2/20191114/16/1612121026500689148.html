Check out the specifications of the Moto Razr foldable smartphone

14 Nov 2019, 16:23 by Peter Holden

As folding phones go the Moto Razr stands apart in terms of its clamshell design when compared with Samsung’s Galaxy Fold and Huawei’s Mate X. But, if you compare the specifications in the cold light of day, the Moro Razr certainly appears to be a step below. With all the nostalgic, sentimental, gooey-eyed feelings towards the Razr, is it a case where the sum is greater than the parts? As in, is it worth paying the $1,500 price tag purely for the idea of the Moto Razr and its modern, foldable design? Join us below to check out the mid-range specs of the Razr handset.
