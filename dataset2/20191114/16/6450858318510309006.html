The best Currys PC World Black Friday 2019 deals

Black Friday hits later this month but Currys has already started deals with its 'Why Wait?' sale. Check out the best offers this month across smart home, tablets, laptops, speakers and more. Plus, find out how to get the best discounts from Currys as Black Friday approaches.

Black Friday arrives on 29 November, but Currys PC World is not waiting until then to unleash its mega deals with its (appropriately named) 'Why Wait?' sale, which launched on 13 November.

As one of the UK’s leading electrical retailers, you’ll find discounts on everything from TVs, laptops, cameras, smart home, vacuum cleaners and kitchen appliances.While Currys will have a separate Black Friday sale, it guarantees that deal prices won't be any lower than they are now, and if prices do go lower, customers will get a refund on the difference.

There are a couple of excellent deals on now worth highlighting. For instance, you can get a free copy of Pokemon Sword or Pokemon Shield worth £44.99 when you buy a Nintendo Switch console for £279. Head to Currys to see its Switch and Pokemon bundles.

Also worth noting is its bundle on the Fitbit Inspire HR with Google Nest Mini (formerly known as the Home Mini) that's currently £74.99, saving you £64. The incredible deal price is cheaper than the regular £89.99 RRP of the Inspire HR and also saves you on the Nest Mini's £49 RRP. See all Fitbit Inspire HR and Nest Mini bundles over at Currys right now.

Currys' prices often compete directly with Amazon so it's a great place to check before you make a purchase. In fact Amazon is mirroring Currys' deal with a similar one that includes an Echo Dot 3 with the Fitbit Inspire HR.

Right now you can also pick up 6 months of Spotify for free when you purchase any laptop, video game, gaming console, tablet, smart device – or any audio device over £49.

If you’re looking for something specific this Black Friday (or Cyber Monday) and want to do some bargain hunting of your own, our advice is clear: make sure you’ve checked other retailers and the manufacturer itself to be sure you’re getting the best price. 

Don’t be overly concerned about the size of the discount or the percentage; ensure that the price your paying is actually a good value for money. This is why we'd recommend looking up a product review before you buy, as you might be better off with a lower price on a previous model on certain products, like a Fitbit or iPhone, for example.

You’ll find deals in stores at Currys PC World as well as online, but we prefer to grab a cuppa and use the website instead. Currys features its strongest deals on the Home page, but there’s a Clearance section as well where you can often find other deals.

You won’t have to pay for delivery at Currys PC World as it’s free if you’re happy to wait three to five days for your item. If you’re in a hurry, you can click and collect for free from your local shop.
