Trailer For THE SPONGEBOB MOVIE: SPONGE ON THE RUN Features Keanu Reeves as Tumbleweed

14 Nov 2019, 16:45 by Joey Paur

Paramount Pictures has released a new trailer for Nickelodeon’s upcoming film The Spongebob Movie: Sponge on the Run, and it looks absolutely ridiculous in the best of ways! Hell, Keanu Reeves stars in the movie as a Sagebrush/Tumbleweed! I certainly wasn’t expecting that strange and wondrous madness! I don’t know what the hell is happening, but I’ll take it!

In this story, Gary's been snailnapped, and SpongeBob and Patrick embark on a hilarious adventure to find him and save him. Here’s the synopsis:

This Memorial Day weekend, SpongeBob SquarePants, his best friend Patrick Star and the rest of the gang from Bikini Bottom hit the big screen in the first-ever all CGI SpongeBob motion picture event. After SpongeBob’s beloved pet snail Gary is snail-napped, he and Patrick embark on an epic adventure to The Lost City of Atlantic City to bring Gary home. As they navigate the delights and dangers on this perilous and hilarious rescue mission, SpongeBob and his pals prove there’s nothing stronger than the power of friendship.

The movie hits theaters in May 2020. Watch the trailer and let us know what you think about this SpongeBob craziness!
