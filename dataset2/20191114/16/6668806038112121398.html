Exclusive: Team Good Newwz is planning the biggest goof-up of the year

14 Nov 2019, 16:41 by Vedanshi Pathak

Good Newwz is one of the most awaited films of the year. It stars Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Diljit Dosanjh and Kiara Advani in the lead. The film's posters were released this morning and they have created a stir online. Calling it the biggest goof-up of the year on its posters, the film's makers are definitely staying true to it. Just hours after the posters were released, Dharma Productions released a video saying that the trailer of the film will be out tomorrow. However, sources inform us that this is just an ace marketing strategy to stay true to the film's story which will have a big goof-up.

And it will be revealed tomorrow that it was a blunder from the side of the makers which is why the trailer will be released on some other day. We hear that Good Newwz's trailer will be out on Monday. Well, with so much buzz around the film, we are sure this strategy of the makers will work well too.

Good Newwz's story revolves around two couples who have a hard time conceiving a child and when they take the route of conceiving through IVF, the goof-up of the year takes place. We're excited to catch this one on the big screen on December 27 this year, what about you?
