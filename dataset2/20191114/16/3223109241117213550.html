Gary Gets ‘Snailnapped’ in ‘The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge on the Run’

14 Nov 2019, 16:39 by Genevieve Scarano

SpongeBob SquarePants and the Bikini Bottom gang are back in The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge on the Run, a new film coming to theaters in 2020.

In the movie, Gary, SpongeBob’s beloved pet, mysteriously disappears from his pineapple home. After a failed search operation, SpongeBob and his BFF, Patrick Star, go on an epic adventure to find the cute sea snail.
