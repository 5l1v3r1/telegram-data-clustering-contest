Doctor accuses her senior of raping, cheating her

14 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

A 29-year-old female doctor has filed a complaint against her senior, also a doctor, for allegedly raping her and cheating her after promising to get married to her.

The victim filed a complaint with the Konanakunte police on Tuesday against Dr Shridhar Srinivasan.

The complainant stated the two were working at the same hospital and on August 8, 2018, he called her to his apartment to talk about work.

There he forced her to drink alcohol. She said he raped her and locked her inside a room and did not let her go out the whole night.

He asked her not to tell this to anyone and promised to marry her.

The two were in a relationship since then and the accused used to take money from her.

She said on Tuesday he raped her again, when she refused to give him money.
