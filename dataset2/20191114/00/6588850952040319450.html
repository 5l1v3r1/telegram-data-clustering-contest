'You'll Eat Your Kenkey Without Pepper And Fish' If . . . - Bantama MP Tells Ghanaians

14 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

Member of Parliament (MP) for Bantama Constituency in the Ashanti Region, Daniel Okyem Aboagye has cautioned Ghanaians that they will 'eat their kenkey without pepper and fish' should they believe utterances made by members of the largest opposition party (NDC).

"You will eat your kenkey without pepper if you believe comments made by NDC members, especially concerning the 2020 budget," he emphatically stated.

Daniel Okyem Aboagye in an interview on UTV's 'Adekye Nsroma' political discussion pointed out that the government in power will be lying should they say they have fulfilled every promise made to Ghanaians.

According to the government's spokesperson on Finance, it is very necessary that as ruling party they sometimes make comparison to show the difference they have made from the previous government.
" . . NDC can never match or be of our standard in any part of governance and they know this for a fact," he said. Featured Video
