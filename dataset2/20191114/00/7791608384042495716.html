David Spade’s Look Of Utter Confusion At Noah Centineo Is Now A Relatable Meme

14 Nov 2019 by Thanussha Priyah

To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before actor Noah Centineo has become an internet meme from his baffling acceptance speech, but in one fell swoop, the 23-year-old has also inadvertently dragged David Spade down with him.
Centineo recently won the Favorite Comedy Movie Star category of this year’s People’s Choice Award. The actor hobbled to the stage with his crutches, which he had to use due to a recent knee injury, and gave a “cringe-worthily” confusing speech that ultimately turned into a meme.
He gave his thanks, but not without leaving everyone confused with his departing words, “It matters not what you’ve done but what you do with what you’ve done for others.”
If you’re equally confused by that sentence, don’t worry, you are not alone.
