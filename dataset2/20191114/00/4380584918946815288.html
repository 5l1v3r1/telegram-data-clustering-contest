Here are the Cy Young Award vote totals

14 Nov 2019, 00:15

Below are point totals for the balloting, as voted on by members of the Baseball Writers' Association of America. Ballots for each award were submitted before the start of the postseason from two writers in every city, based on the league covered.

• deGrom repeats, JV edges Cole for Cy honors

The system rewards seven points for first place, four for second place and three for third place, two for fourth place and one for fifth place.
