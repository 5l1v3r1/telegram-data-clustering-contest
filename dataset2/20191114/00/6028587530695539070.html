Shanghai’s Hottest New Hotel: Bringing Wellness To A Fast-Paced Metropolis

14 Nov 2019 by George Koutsakis

Shanghai's skyline is ever changing with bright, futuristic structures added to it constantly. Some add to city's character greatly, other serve as landmarks. The new JW Marriot Marquis Hotel Shanghai Pudong delivers a new concept with luxury, and more so, wellness at the forefront in a never-stopping city that needs it most.

Designed by famed architect Skidmore, Owings & Merill (SOM) who are responsible for some of the most iconic buildings in the world, the new JW Marriot Marquis stands out with a futuristic, calming design in the Pudong district by the Huangpu river. The hotel is the fourth addition to the JW Marriot's Marquis and the very first in Asia, and house 515 rooms throughout the building’s 39 stories.

The exterior and interior are brought together by water elements in the design, the first step to bringing mindfulness and wellness into the day of each guests. A small garden outside the hotel provides a moment of calm, while the herbs grown there are used in the hotel's three restaurants, Merchant Kitchen, Flint, and Le Manoir Ling. A rooftop garden will be launching soon when guests can source fresh ingredients, depending on the season, and have then cooked right then and there. The JW Lounge features an outdoor seating area where visitors can enjoy the creative, seasonal afternoon tea offerings. Seated outside, people exercising, and dogs scurrying past, one would never guess they were seated in the heart of one of the globes busiest hubs. Post-tea guests can visit the hotel's outstanding spa for an massage, sauna, or swim.

Meditation and yoga classes are available for that mind-clearing moment before starting off a busy day. In the afternoon, a walk along the calm river side allows guests to wind down after a long day. Less than a minute from the hotel stands the Modern Art Museum of Shanghai, currently exhibiting ‘Retrospectum Bob Dylan’, an exhibit featuring over 250 works by Bod Dylan, including painting, sculptures, and videos. Last but not least, the hotel works with a local yacht company to provide guests with evenings ride across the Huangpu River and the best view of Shanghai’s bright lights and vibrant skyline.

‘We are truly excited to expand our JW Marriott Marquis portfolio to four hotels globally and debut our newest addition in Shanghai, China,’ said Mitzi Gaskins, Vice President & Global Brand Leader, JW Marriott. ‘JW Marriott Marquis Hotel Shanghai Pudong is set to redefine luxury hospitality in Shanghai, with forward-thinking design inspired by the surrounding neighborhood, the city’s history and its unique culture. The hotel’s extensive offerings will encourage guests to live in the present, experience life to the fullest and promises an immersive experience that will leave guests feeling nurtured and recharged.’

With twelve multi-functional banquet rooms geared towards business meetings, events, and seminars, the strong focus on wellness is much needed and the hotel is doing an amazing job, with each offering helping guests relax, clear their minds, stay healthy, and focus. The JW Marriot brand has always focused on sustainability, environmental issues, and wellness, and the new, exciting Marquis Hotel Shanghai Pudong takes one step further in the brand’s continuous mission.
