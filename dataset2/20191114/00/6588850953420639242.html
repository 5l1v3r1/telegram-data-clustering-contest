Inside David Beckham's Lavish Mansion

14 Nov 2019 by Peace FM Online

They enjoyed a sunshine break stateside during half-term, sharing envy inducing snaps from their stunning rental home.

And an inside glimpse at the Beckham family's £20m hilltop home in Beverly Hills has been given, complete with indoor cinema, zen garden and infinity pool.
The Beckham brood - dad David, 44, and children Brooklyn, 20, Romeo, 17, Cruz, 14, and Harper, eight - stayed in the lavish home over the holidays while mum Victoria, 45, stayed at home to work on her make-up line.
