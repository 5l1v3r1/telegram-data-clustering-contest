Your chance to claim two free trees as part of our massive Climate Issue giveaway

We have teamed up with the Woodland Trust to give away 10,000 trees as part of our Climate Issue

14 Nov 2019, 00:01 by Richard Innes, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/richard-innes/

Right now we’re facing our gravest threat in thousands of years - climate change.

Scientists are clear urgent action is needed and what happens today and in the next few years will profoundly effect the next thousands of years.

Sometimes the scale of the problem can feel entirely overwhelming.

But we CAN all make a difference.

And today to help you do just that we are giving away 10,000 Birch or Rowan trees for you to plant in your garden, school, or outside your office, factory, church, mosque, scout hut or leisure centre.

We're working on this in conjunction with the Woodland Trust – the UK's largest woodland conservation charity.

You can plant the tree in memory of a loved one – or to mark a special moment in your family history.

Or you can just plant a tree to help make our world a better place.

To play your part and apply for the two free trees from the Woodland Trust, please enter your details into the form below.

1) This offer entitles the first 10,000 readers to claim two free trees (one Birch, one Rowan).

2) Five thousand readers can claim this offer through the online form in this article. 

3) This offer is available to UK residents only (including Northern Ireland). 

4) Readers who claim this promotion will be required to pay a postage fee of £3.81 and successful applicants will be contacted directly via email to arrange payment of the postage fee via cheque/phone. 

5)You will be required to give your full name, postal address and email address to apply for this offer. 

6) This offer is subject to availability and once the 5,000 requests have come in, this offer will no longer be available. 

7) This promotion closes at 5pm on Thursday November 21, 2019, however if the full allocation has been taken up before this date, the online form will notify you that the offer is no longer available. 

8) Only one application can be made per household. 

9) Trees will be delivered to the address you have submitted, so please ensure that this is the correct delivery address. 

10) By taking part in this offer you agree for your name, email and postal address to be shared with the Mirror and Woodland Trust. The Mirror will only use your details for the purposes of notifying you if you have been successful in qualifying for the free trees and fulfilling this offer (for the successful applicants). The Woodland Trust will only use your details to contact you as part of their aftercare policy for the two free trees. 

11) Your details will not be shared for any other purposes and you will only be contacted if successful in qualifying for this offer. The Woodland Trust are only permitted to contact you once with regard to this and you will have the option to opt in or out of further communication when receiving this.
