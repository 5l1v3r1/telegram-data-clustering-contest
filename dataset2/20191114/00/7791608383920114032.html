Adorable ‘Unicorn Puppy’ Has A Little Tail Growing On The Middle Of His Face

14 Nov 2019 by Thanussha Priyah

An abandoned puppy with two tails has gone viral online. The unusual thing is that the second tail is growing on the puppy’s forehead, per BBC.
The 10-week-old pup was first spotted in Kansas City, and was referred to Mac’s Mission, a shelter in Cape Girardeau that supports dogs with defects.
The owner of the special shelter, Rochelle Steffen, aptly named him Narwhal after the horned whale. After bringing him to the vet on Tuesday, she has updated on a Facebook post that the puppy is completely fine and “plays for hours.”
X-rays show that Narwhal’s extremity is all skin and no bone. Therefore, the tail on his face will not start wagging.
Steffen was also informed that Narwhal has “no medical need to remove” the tail, as he feels no pain or discomfort from it.
She also explained that the secondary tail is about a third the size of his actual tail, and no one really knows why he has it on his face but she thinks it does make him the “coolest puppy ever.”
However, Narwhal is not up for adoption yet as the center wants to let him “grow a bit more” to be really sure if the tail will not become a problem in the future.
As soon as the pictures of Narwhal circulated the internet, many users started expressing their heart-eyed adoration to the “unicorn” puppy.
