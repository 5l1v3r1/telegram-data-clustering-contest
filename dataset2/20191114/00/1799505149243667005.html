Kona Coffee

14 Nov 2019, 00:30 by BRIEF

SEATTLE – A federal court in Washington dismissed in part coffee farmers’ false advertising claims against several retailers, including Kroger, Amazon and Walmart. The farmers claim the retailers sell coffee products that are deceptively labeled as “Kona” coffee.
