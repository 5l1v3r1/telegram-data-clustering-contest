Investors value policy stability. Attracting investments will become difficult if decisions are reversed with the change in governments. This is exactly what has happened in Andhra Pradesh, and not for the first time. Some of the recent decisions of the Central government can also affect the investment climate. Business Standard Opinion pieces of the day highlight this and other important policy issues.
