The Apprentice fans rage as seventh BAME candidate fired and Lottie Lion remains

The racism row surrounding The Apprentice has deepened after Jemelin Artigas was axed from the show and all the while the allegedly racist Lottie Lion stays on

14 Nov 2019, 00:04 by Will Twigger, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-twigger/

The Apprentice racism row deepened during the latest show after the seventh BAME candidate in a row was fired by Lord Sugar.

Budding entrepreneur Jemelin Artigas, who hails from Argentina, was sent packing while a candidate accused of racism toward another contestant remains.

Lottie Lion has been surrounded by controversy since day one, and allegedly told the now-fired Lubna Farhan "shut up Gandhi" in a WhatApp message.

Fans took to Twitter to question why the seven fired candidates have all been from minority ethnic backgrounds.

One wrote: "At first I thought this was a coincidence but this apprentice racism is just too consistent now. And Iasha PM'd the task that made Â£1mil bu didn't do enough? naaaah."

Another penned: "Can the racism in the Apprentice be any more obvious? #theapprenticeuk."

"It's time to stop watching the apprentice now," a third insisted, "Just look at the pattern in the first seven weeks. Unconscious bias training would be a start! @BBC. Compounded by Lottie's racist rants and you've got to start asking questions. Someone? Anyone? #TheApprentice2019."

The first six weeks of the show saw the firing of Shahin Hassan, Kenna Ngoma, Souleyman Bah, Lubna Farhan, Riyonn Farsad and Iasha Masood.

Jemelin spoke to the Mirror Online about being fired, saying: "I felt like I could have stayed but I also felt I couldn't see myself as Lord Sugar's business partner.

"I don't come from a corporate world, I'm a designer. I've got high morals."

She also hit out at Lottie for her alleged racism, adding: "I just find it really sad. Because you've got 16 contestants, they have given their lives away, giving their jobs, family up....yet we are focusing on completely the wrong thing and I just feel saddened on it.

"We shouldn't just make it about one person. It's not fair on the other contestants.

"She did what she wanted to do and it made me really angry because we have other amazing contestants with amazing backgrounds."
