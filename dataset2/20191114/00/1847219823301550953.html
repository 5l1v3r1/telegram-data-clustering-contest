Corruption watchdog once again punishes VID officials for illegal information acquisition

14 Nov 2019 by LETA

Corruption Prevention and Combating Bureau (KNAB) has once again punished officials of the State Revenue Service (SRS) who were caught illegally acquiring information from systems.

Officials, driven by personal motives, were caught acquiring information from information systems that is not necessary for performance of their duties.

Two inspectors were applied fines of EUR 70 and EUR 80.

As previously reported, KNAB had found similar crimes in the actions of other institutions in the past. Most officials caught in the act were from VID.
