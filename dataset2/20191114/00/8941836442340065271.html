West Virginia basketball signs recruit Taj Thweatt

14 Nov 2019

MORGANTOWN, W.Va. (AP) - Forward Taj Thweatt has signed a letter of intent to play at West Virginia next year.

Coach Bob Huggins announced the signing Wednesday.

The 6-foot-7 Thweatt averaged 20.2 points and 10.7 rebounds per game last season at Wildwood Catholic High School in New Jersey. He made 27 3-point field goals and shot 36 percent from 3-point range.
