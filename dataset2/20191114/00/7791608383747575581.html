Nutella Will Make Your Chocolate Dreams Come True At Luxurious Hotella Nutella

14 Nov 2019 by Yimin Huang

Hotella Nutella is a pop-up site opened by Nutella, located in California’s Napa Valley, per The Hook Site. Nutella fans can look forward to the hotel’s opening a few weeks from today.
According to its official website, those who stay at the hotel can enjoy a “creative breakfast” that has been made with the help of “notable tastemakers.”
As the hotel design is all about the hazelnut chocolate sauce, its bedroom interior follows suit. There are Nutella rugs, plushes, curtains, patio furniture and even adorable pillows shaped like croissants, with Nutella wallpaper adding the finishing touch.
In order to get a chance to stay in such a fascinating hotel, fans have to join a simple competition where they have to record a video of 60 seconds or less sharing their love of Nutella.
Unfortunately for international fans, the competition is only open to those living in the United States, and can be signed up here.

Winners can also enjoy breakfast sessions with the “Nutella Weekend Breakfast Chef Team” over the weekend that consists of celebrity chefs Geoffrey Zakarian and Tanya Holland. Pancake artist DanCakes will also be there to teach the guests on how to create the best Nutella breakfasts.
