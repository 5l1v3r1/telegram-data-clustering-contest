Lee O'Connor hailed as Celtic kid provides impressive assist on Ireland debut

The youngster put in an brilliant performance for Mick McCarthy's side as he made his senior bow.

14 Nov 2019, 22:08 by Chris Doyle, https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/authors/chris-doyle/

Lee O'Connor might have not featured for Celtic yet but they could have another exciting full-back on their hands.

The youngster was handed his Ireland debut in a friendly against New Zealand as Mick McCarthy 's men ran out 3-1 winners.

The full-back signed for the Parkhead side on deadline day in the summer but has played only for the reserves.

But the 19-year-old might have provided his gaffer Neil Lennon with some food for thought after putting in an impressive performance on his senior bow for his country.

O'Connor didn't look out of place and showed his attacking flair as he whipped in a stunning ball to assist Ireland's third goal from Callum Robinson.

And Ireland , Celtic fans and the rest of social media were quick to praise the former Manchester United kid for his display.
