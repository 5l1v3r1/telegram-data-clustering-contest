The Bloody Beetroots & DAB Motors Reveal Very Limited Motorcycle Collaboration

The Bloody Beetroots is releasing just 10 of these motorcycles!

14 Nov 2019, 22:27 by Magnetic

Usually artists like to do fashion collaborations or work with some visual media artist, but The Bloody Beetroots is doing something quite different. DAB Motors and The Bloody Beetroots have teamed up on a new motorcycle collaboration.

The collaboration is extremely limited. Only 10 numbered bikes will be built and sold, each of them signed by The Bloody Beetroots’ Sir Bob Cornelius Rifo and Dab Motors’ Simon Dabadie.

It comes with a 500cc single-cylinder engine, Kayaba suspension and Brembo brakes. All 10 bikes are finished in a black out color scheme.
