TBT: Serge Djiehoua, The Fittest Player In The PSL

14 Nov 2019, 17:11 by SL Social

Former Kaizer Chiefs striker Serge Djiehoua will be remembered more for his muscular thighs than for his goal scoring prowess. The Ivorian was largely referred to as the fittest player in the league.

Djiehoua spent time at Chiefs between 2005 and 2007 where he scored five goals in 10 appearances for the Glamour Boys.
