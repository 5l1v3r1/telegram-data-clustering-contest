New Teaser for M. Night Shyamalan’s “Servant” Documents the Creation of a Creepy Fake Baby

14 Nov 2019, 17:25 by John Squires

Coming to the Apple TV+ streaming service in two weeks, M. Night Shyamalan‘s series “Servant” centers on a creepy fake baby, and a new teaser video has been released today.

It’s titled “Making Jericho,” documenting the creation of said creepy fake baby. Find it below.

Shyamalan produced the psychological thriller series and also directed select episodes.

The series was created by Tony Basgallop.

“Servant” follows a Philadelphia couple in mourning after an unspeakable tragedy creates a rift in their marriage and opens the door for a mysterious force to enter their home.

Lauren Ambrose, Toby Kebbell and Nell Tiger Free star.

“Servant” will premiere on AppleTV+ on November 28, 2019.
