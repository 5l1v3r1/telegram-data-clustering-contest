Pick up The Messenger for free on the Epic Games Store

'Be always brave'

14 Nov 2019, 17:30 by Jordan Devore

After a string of duos, the Epic Games Store is back to giving out a single video game this week, but it's one worth nabbing. The next time you're in a classic Ninja Gaiden mood, you'll be glad The Messenger is only a few clicks away. The retro action-platformer is free on PC until Thursday, November 21.
