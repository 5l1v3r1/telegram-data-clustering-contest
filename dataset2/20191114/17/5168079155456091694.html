Iran’s Qeshm Air to operate weekly flights to Sohar

14 Nov 2019, 17:53 by Times News Service

Muscat: The Public Authority of Civil Aviation (PACA) has approved the operation of Iranian carrier Qeshm Air to Sohar Airport.

Qeshm Air will fly seven-days a week which includes two from Asfahan, three from Shiraz, one from Mashhad and another from Ahwaz.
