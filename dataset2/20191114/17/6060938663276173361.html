Photos: Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Shahid Kapoor, Anushka Sharma, Alia Bhatt and other Bollywood celebs attend Karan Johar's party for Katy Perry

14 Nov 2019, 17:47 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

International pop singer Katy Perry is in Mumbai to attend the music festival which will be held this weekend. And now, ace filmmaker Karan Johar has hosted a bash for Katy at his residence which will be attended by a lot of Bollywood celebs.

Celebs like Aishwarya Rai Bachchan with Abhishek Bachchan, Shahid Kapoor with his wife, Aditya Roy Kapur, Shashank Khaitan, Anushka Sharma, Alia Bhatt, Jacqueline Fernandez, Aditi Rao Hydari, Malaika Arora, Kajol, Arjun Kapoor, Dino Morea, Dinesh Vijan, Punit Malhotra, Ananya Panday with his mother Bhavana Pandey, Karisma Kapoor, Neha Dhupia, Kunal Kemmu, Sanjay Kapoor with daughter Shanaya Kapoor, Mini Mathur, Gauri Khan, Salman Khan's niece Alizeh Agnihotri, and more.

For the night, Katy opted for a shimmery purple off-shoulder top teamed with golden-black skirt. She was all smiles as the paparazzi clicked her pictures.

Before the musical festival, she also attended an event in the city along with Bollywood beauty Jacqueline Fernandez. Katy will be performing at the fest where she will be joined by British pop sensation Dua Lipa along with Amit Trivedi, Ritviz and more.

Talking about her big gig, she stated, "I am so happy to be returning to India and excited to headline my first-ever performance in Mumbai. I am looking forward to seeing and singing with all of my Indian KatyCats".
