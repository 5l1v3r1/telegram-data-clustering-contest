Emmerdale's Natalie Ann Jamieson shares sweet picture with co-stars enjoying night out on Jonny McPherson's boat

The Emmerdale stars seemed to have a great time together

14 Nov 2019, 17:18 by Charlotte Rodrigues

Actress Natalie Ann Jamieson, who plays Amy Wyatt in Emmerdale has shared a picture with her co-stars enjoying a night out on Jonny McPherson's boat.

In the picture, Amy can be seen with Jonny, who plays Liam Cavanagh, Amy Walsh, who plays Tracy Metcalfe, Olivia Bromley, who plays Dawn Taylor and Ash Palmisciano, who plays Matty Barton.

Natalie captioned the post: "Oh what a night, late November back in 19... #jonnysboat #whatanight #tiffanyhat @amyvwalsh @oliviabromley_ @ash.p.x."

Fans rushed to comment on the picture.

One wrote: "Class."

A second commented: " Love it xx."

A third said: "Great photo."

Amy Walsh also shared a video to her Instagram Stories showing Jonny demonstrating wine tasting.
