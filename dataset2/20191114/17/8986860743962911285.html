John Dumelo celebrates his father on his 70th birthday

14 Nov 2019, 17:22 by ‌ Lizbeth Brown

Today marks the 70th birthday of the father of popular Ghanaian actor and politician John Dumelo.

The renowned actor who was full of joy shared a picture of his handsome father on social media to celebrate him.

John Dumelo prayed for good health, wisdom and God’s blessing on his father’s life and also expressed his love for him.

The actor wrote on Instagram; “Happy 70th Birthday Mr John Dumelo SNR. God bless your new age. God give you wisdom, hope, good health and love. Love you loads! #idey4u
