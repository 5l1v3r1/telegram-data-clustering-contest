Strictly Come Dancing stars share an epic selfie as they head to Blackpool together

They're off to the home of ballroom

14 Nov 2019, 17:44 by Nancy Brown

The cast of Strictly Come Dancing are off to Blackpool this weekend and the stars of the show shared an epic selfie as they headed north.

Michelle Visage – who recently hit out at claims she was "deeply unhappy after clashing with partner Giovanni Pernice – shared the selfie.

"Friends headed to BLACKPOOL! Yaaaaassss,"  she said, also posting the roller coaster dancing lady emoji.

The image showed Michelle and Giovanni travelling up to Blackpool by train.

Also in the picture were Karen Hauer and her partner Chris Ramsey and Neil Jones and Alex Scott.

The selfie comes after this week's songs and dances were revealed.
