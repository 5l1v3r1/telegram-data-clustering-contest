Amazon Music Unlimited offering 99 cents for four months deal

14 Nov 2019, 12:57 by BrooklynVegan Staff

With Black Friday right around the corner, Amazon Music Unlimited has launched a deal for new subscribers: the first four months for 99 cents, if you sign up by January 6, 2020. The deal doesn’t apply to Amazon’s new Neil Young-approved HD Streaming service, but it does cover the over 50 million songs available in standard definition (which, according to TechRadar, is more than either Spotify or Apple Music offer). The price goes up to $9.99/month after the first four months, or $7.99/month if you’re a Prime subscriber.
