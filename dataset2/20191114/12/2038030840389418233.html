Selena Gomez and Julia Michaels celebrate their friendship with a performance, matching tattoos and a kiss

14 Nov 2019, 12:29

Cape Town – Let's talk BFF goals real quick because Selena Gomez and Julia Michaels just celebrated their friendship in a big, permanent way.

On Monday night the Lose You To Love Me singer made a surprise appearance on stage at Julia's concert in Los Angeles. The two sat next to each other and sang their song, Anxiety, live for the first time, ET reports.

The Issues singer later shared a clip of their performance on Instagram writing, "Thank you @selenagomez for the most epic headline show I've ever played.

"I love you beyond words and I'm so happy we finally got to sing our baby together."

According to People, after their performance, Julia and Selena shared a kiss on stage, and both posted the image on their Instagram Stories.

After the show the two also got matching arrow tattoos on their hands, reports E! News. Selena shared a picture on her story with the words, "It's tatted... my arrow points to you forever."
