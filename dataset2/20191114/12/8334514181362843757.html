Lebanon's Aoun hopes a government is formed in the coming days

14 Nov 2019, 13:47

BEIRUT (Reuters) - Lebanon’s President Michel Aoun on Thursday said on his official Twitter account that he has hope in the possibility of the formation of a new government in the coming days.

The demands of the protesters will be among the first goals of the incoming government, Aoun said.
