Tabu roped in for Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani starrer Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2

14 Nov 2019, 12:34 by Filmfare

On the occasion of Dussehra this year, Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani started shooting for their upcoming film, Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2. The film which is the second installment in Bhool Bhulaiyaa franchise, is helmed by Anees Bazmee. The duo shot for three days and the next schedule will kick-start early next year.

As per the latest reports, after Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani, the makers of Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 have roped in another actress in a prominent role. Tabu has joined the cast of the Anees Bazmee directorial. The details of her character have been kept under wraps. According to a leading daily, the actress loved the role she was offered in Bhool Bhulaiyaa and is expected to join the cast for filming from January 2020.

Bhool Bhilaiyaa 2 is produced by T-Series and Cine1 Studios, helmed by Anees Bazmee and is set to hit the screens in July 2020.
