How much Philippe Coutinho costs Bayern Munich in each game

The Brazilian plays for the German side on loan from Barcelona

14 Nov 2019, 12:03 by sport.es

Philippe Coutinho costs Bayern Munich over half-a-million euros per match, per a report from German newspaper Bild.

On loan from Barcelona at Bayernm, the player cost the Bavarians 8.5m euros in terms of a loan fee, while he has a 25m euro a year salary. His total cost to Bayern is 33.5m euros.

So he will cost the club a minimum of 657,000 euros per match, and that is assuming he will play in every game, includng the DFB Cup and Champions League finals.

Coutinho was also recently named as the loan player with the most value in the five big leagues, per the CIES Football Observatory.

Their study reports that he is worth 96.5 million euros - a figure under the 120m euro option to buy that Bayern Munich have on the midfielder.

He is not having a great time in Germany where he is no longer essential after a promising start tot he season.

Coutinho is playing where he likes, in attacking midfield, when he does feature.

He is followed by Arsenal's Dani Ceballos (65.7m euros) and Martin Odegaard of Real Sociedad (57.3m), both on loan from Real Madrid.
