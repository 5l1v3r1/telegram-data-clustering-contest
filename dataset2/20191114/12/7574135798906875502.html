Treefort Fest 2020 lineup: Chromatics, Japanese Breakfast, AJJ, Torres, more

14 Nov 2019, 12:47 by Bill Pearis

Boise, ID’s Treefort Music Fest have announced the initial lineup for the 2020 edition of the fest, which happens March 25-29 at venues in the city’s downtown area. Among those scheduled to play: Chromatics, Japanese Breakfast, Omar Apollo, Christian Scott aTunde Adjuah, Larkin Poe, Peter Bjorn and John, Tennis, AJJ, Prefuse 73, Mikal Cronin, Torres, The Felice Brothers, Chad VanGaalen, Sinkane, Prefuse 73, Algiers, Great Grandpa, Ratboys, Becca Mancari, Vanishing Twin, Horse Jumper of Love, Potty Mouth, The Orielles, Magic Sword, Garcia Peoples, and lots more.

Five-day passes are on sale now and you can check out the first round of announced artists below.
