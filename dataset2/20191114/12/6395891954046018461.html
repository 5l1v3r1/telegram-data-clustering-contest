All Roanoke, Virginia, schools closed amid active police investigation, authorities say

14 Nov 2019, 12:27 by Michelle Ewing, Cox Media Group National Content Desk

ROANOKE, Va. -

Police in Virginia are "actively investigation a situation" near a Roanoke high school, authorities said early Thursday. As a result, all of the district's public schools are closed, WFXR and WDBJ are reporting.
