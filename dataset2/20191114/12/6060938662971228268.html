FAKE ALERT: Nehru’s picture with niece shared saying he was trying to kiss British woman

14 Nov 2019, 12:18 by Times Fact Check

Highlights

  1. A picture of Nehru in which a woman can be seen kissing him is being shared to portray him as a womanizer.
  2. The picture is being shared with the claim that Nehru couldn’t come out of the clutches of Britishers.
  3. The lady seen kissing Nehru in the picture is his niece Nayantara Sehgal and not a British woman.

CLAIM

A picture of India’s first Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru in which a woman can be seen kissing him is being shared to malign him and portray him as a womanizer.

The picture is being shared with the claim that Nehru couldn’t come out of the clutches of Britishers, thereby implying that he exchanged a kiss with a British woman.

A Twitter user shared the image with a caption in Hindi that loosely translates to, “Nehru was at the peak of freedom struggle, but the Britishers kept him captive via crooked means. Even after trying hard, Nehru couldn’t resist freeing himself. Here’s a photo that symbolizes his struggle.”
