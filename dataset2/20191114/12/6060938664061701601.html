Akshay Kumar and Kareena Kapoor's 'Good Newwz' trailer to be out tomorrow!

14 Nov 2019, 12:34 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Early today, On the account of Children's day, the makers of 'Good Newwz' unveiled the very interesting first look posters of the film featuring Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Kiara Advani and Diljit Dosanjh. The film which was in the news since its announcement is finally taking its baby steps toward the release.

This Akshay and Kareena's film tells the story of the year's biggest goof-ups. In the posters which are out, we can see, Akshay and Diljit's heads are squeezed between Kareena and Kiara's baby bumps. While both Bebo and Kiara look beautiful, Akki and Diljit are seen troubled by their expressions. Now, unveiling the posters, the makers have announced that without wasting much time, they have planned to drop the trailer tomorrow, which is on the 15th of November.

Akshay Kumar took to his social media handles and announced, "Ho chuki hai goof ups ki shuruvaat! Stay tuned, #GoodNewwzTrailer coming soon!." The video which is shared says the trailer will be out tomorrow.
