IPL: Rajasthan Royals trade Rahane to Delhi Capitals; get Markande and Tewatia in return

14 Nov 2019, 12:14 by PTI

Highlights

  • Rajasthan Royals traded their most-capped player Rahane to Delhi Capitals in exchange of Markande and Tewatia
  • Rahane was Royals' top run-getter having scored 2810 runs at an average of 34.26 and strike rate of 122.65
  • The trade announcement came on the final day of the IPL players' transfer window

NEW DELHI: Rajasthan Royals on Thursday traded their most-capped player Ajinkya Rahane to Delhi Capitals in exchange of leg-spinner Mayank Markande and bowling all-rounder Rahul Tewatia.

Rahane, who captained Royals in 24 of his 100 IPL games between 2011 and 2019, was also their top run-getter having scored 2810 runs at an average of 34.26 and strike rate of 122.65.

India's Test vice-captain also scored two centuries for the Royals besides 17 half-centuries.

The trade announcement came on the final day of the IPL players' transfer window.
