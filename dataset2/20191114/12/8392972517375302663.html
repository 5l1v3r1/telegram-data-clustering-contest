EastEnders, BBC One, 7.30pm

1. Is Mel dead?

On Tuesday (November 12) Sharon and Mel were racing to get to Phil first, with Mel dead set on revealing Sharon's baby secret to everyone.

But just as Mel was about to announce the news to the packed E2o over speakerphone, she smashed into a broken-down car, flipping her own vehicle over the road.

The last we saw the car was upside down and fuel was dangerously leaking out. Is Mel set to go the same way as her ex-husband, Steve, who also died in a car explosion while being chased by Phil?

As Sharon approaches the wreckage she looks horrified and is stunned to see the car and leaking petrol.

Will Sharon save Mel? Is the car going to explode? Will Sharon and her baby be okay?

2. Louise and Keanu wait for news

After hearing the crash on the loud speaker at her birthday party, Louise is concerned for Mel.

As she and Keanu wait for news back home, and look after distraught Lisa, what will the outcome be?

3. Chantelle confronts Gray

Chantelle confronts Gray about the fight he had and why he didn't tell her.

Gray claims he kept it hidden because of the baby, but Chantelle is unconvinced.

He pleads for forgiveness and promises he'll do all he can to put it right, but is it one lie too many?
