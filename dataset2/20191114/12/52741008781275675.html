You can help shape Waterloo Region's future

Open houses will be held and input can be submitted online

14 Nov 2019, 12:00 by KitchenerToday Staff

The Region of Waterloo is looking to the future - far into the future!

It wants to know what the game plan should be in the years to come.

By the year 2041, the Region's population is set to reach 835,000, with over 400,000 jobs. 

Officials are currently reviewing the Regional Official Plan, which will guide long-term growth and development. 

If you have any ideas or feedback, you can have your say.

A number of open houses will be held in the weeks to come and input can be submitted online.
