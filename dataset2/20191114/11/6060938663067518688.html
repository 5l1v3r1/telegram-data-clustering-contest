Photos: Priyanka Chopra returns to the bay; flaunts her uber-cool style in a semi-formal attire

14 Nov 2019, 11:49 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Global sensation Priyanka Chopra was busy shooting for her next professional project in Delhi and had been giving her fans an update about her days in Delhi through social media.

From raising awareness about air pollution which has been one of the biggest problems that the citizens of Delhi have been facing for a while now to sharing pictures from her family get together, PeeCee had quite a busy schedule in Delhi.

Now, the 'Baywatch' actress has returned to Mumbai and was snapped at the airport flaunting her uber-cool style in semi-formal attire. PeeCee opted for a balloon sleeved white shirt which she teamed up with black denim and completed her look with golden stilettos and stylish sunnies.

She even took to social media to share her happiness about returning to the clear skies of Mumbai. Sharing a glimpse of the blue sky she wrote, "The joy of seeing a blue sky after #delhi"

Meanwhile, Priyanka, who was last seen in Shonali Bose's 'The Sky Is Pink' is all set to celebrate her first anniversary with husband Nick Jonas on 1st December.
