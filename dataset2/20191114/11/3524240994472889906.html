Amosun sponsors South-West Development Commission bill in Senate

14 Nov 2019, 11:06 by Sunday Aborisade

Sunday Aborisade, Abuja

A bill seeking the establishment of the South West Development Commission scaled first reading on the floor of the Senate on Thursday.

The bill was sponsored by the Senator representing Ogun Central Senatorial District, Ibikunle Amosun in conjunction with all the South-West senators in the red chamber.

Amosun is the immediate past governor of Ogun State.
