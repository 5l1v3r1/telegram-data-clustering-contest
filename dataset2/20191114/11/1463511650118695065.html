Senate re-introduces South West Development Commission

14 Nov 2019, 11:11 by The Eagle Online

The bill for the establishment of the South West Development Commission passed into first reading in the Senate on Thursday.

The bill was sponsored by Senator Ibikunle Amosun.

It is titled: “South West Development Commission.”

It was first presented in eight Senate.

The bill is coming 24 hours after the South East Development Commission was passed into first reading.
