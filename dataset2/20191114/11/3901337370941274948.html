TBT: When Zongo Showed Chiefs Flames

14 Nov 2019, 11:04 by SL Social

Known to be one of the most technically gifted South African footballers in recent years, trickster Masibusane Zongo has made a return to football, signing for D'General FC. We remember when Zongo had one of his finest performances on the pitch, against Kaizer Chiefs of all teams. Check it out!

Despite causing havoc each time Zongo set foot on the pitch, it was off the field where the most damage was done. With controversies and drama crippling what was once a promising career in the game, the 29-year-old winger has amassed just 62 appearances in his footballing journey so far.
