Dubai celebrates UAE National Day with big free-to-attend concerts

Emirati stars including Hussain Al Jassmi will be playing at La Mer and Global

14 Nov 2019, 11:08 by Darragh Murphy

Some brilliant getaways, events and deals are already being set up for UAE National Day 2019, and now we’re getting not one, but four concerts to check out, and they’re all free-to-attend.

Throughout the long weekend, music fans can celebrate the 48th UAE National Day with Emirati musical stars at La Mer and Global Village. Just one more thing to add to your list of events.

Kicking off on Friday November 29 at La Mer, Emirati singer Eida Al Menhali will perform at popular beachfront destination La Mer.

Miss that? Not to worry, as the very next day at the same time, Emirati icon Balqees and Kuwaiti superstar Essa Al Marzouq are set to dazzle fans with their incredible tunes.

For National Day itself, award-winning Emirati vocalist Hussain Al Jassmi will wow fans at La Mer, while compatriot Hamad Al Amri will perform at the multicultural festival park Global Village on the same day.

The shows at La Mer will kick off at 8.35pm each day, while the Global Village performance on Monday December 2 will start at 9pm. For more info about each performance, head to www.dubaicalendar.com.
