‘Rock On!!’ actor Luke Kenny looks unrecognisable as Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb in Tanhaji poster!

14 Nov 2019, 11:01 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ajay Devgn revealed several character posters from his upcoming period drama ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’. He shared a striking poster of Sharad Kelkar as Chhatrapati Shivaji and Padmavati Rao as Chhatrapati Shivaji's mother Jijamata. However what caught our attention was Luke Kenny as he posed as Mughal emperor Aurangzeb. The caption of his character poster read, "Aurangzeb Mughal Emperor - Hum mukkamal Hindustan ko fateh karne ka irada rakhte hain." (We plan to conquer Hindustan).

Luke has transformed massively from his popular character in ‘Rock On!!’. The actor played a lively rock band member in the musical but going by his Aurangzeb avatar, Luke has undergone a massive makeover for the period drama. With an intense gaze and a focused vision, Luke makes an impact as the Mughal warrior.

'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior' will focus on the Sinhagad battle wherein Tanhaji sacrificed his life and bravely fought against Udaybhan, who was leading the Mughals. With Ajay Devgn playing the titular role, Saif Ali Khan as the antagonist and a promising supporting cast, there is a lot of expectation from this period drama. Directed by Om Raut and produced by Ajay Devgn, 'Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior' is scheduled to hit the screens on January 10, 2020 and will clash at the box office with Deepika Padukone starrer ‘Chhapaak’.
