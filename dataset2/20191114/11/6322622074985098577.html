Higher tariff boosts Gas Malaysia's 3Q earnings

14 Nov 2019, 11:10 by Wong Ee Lin

KUALA LUMPUR (Nov 14): Gas Malaysia Bhd's third quarter net profit grew 12.24% to RM46.48 million, from RM41.42 million a year earlier, on higher revenue.

Earnings per share for the quarter ended Sept 30, 2019 increased to 3.27 sen, from 3.2 sen previously.

Revenue rose 12.82% to RM1.76 billion from RM1.56 billion a year ago, thanks to higher volume of natural gas sold and higher natural gas tariff, Gas Malaysia said in a filing with Bursa Malaysia today.

For the cumulative nine months ended Sept 30, 2019, Gas Malaysia's net profit edged up 7.22% to RM138.8 million or 10.29 sen per share, from RM129.46 million or 10.07 sen per share last year, while revenue was up 15.87% to RM5.21 billion from RM4.49 billion.

On prospects, Gas Malaysia expects the group's profitability for 2019 to be in tandem with the level reflecting the prevailing tariff setting mechanism framework.

"The board anticipates that the yearly increase in natural gas sale volume and number of customers will sustain for the financial year 2019," the group added.

Shares of Gas Malaysia closed one sen or 0.35% lower at RM2.82 today, valuing the group at RM3.62 billion.
