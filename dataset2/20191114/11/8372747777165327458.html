Coast Guard calls off search for missing boaters

14 Nov 2019, 11:01 by WFTX Digital Team

MIAMI-DADE COUNTY, Fla. -- The U.S. Coast Guard called off the search Wednesday for the two boaters after their boat overturned days ago.

They were part of a trio who had left the Key Largo area last Thursday.

The Coast Guard says there was a third boater with them. That person was rescued Sunday night by a good Samaritan boater.

The survivor, 31-year-old Adnan Diaz, says he'd left the other two on the boat when he went to swim for help after their boat capsized near MIami on Friday.
