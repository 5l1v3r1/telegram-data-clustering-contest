Three-day holiday for Dubai school kids

The KHDA has announced a three-day holiday for Commemoration Day and UAE National Day

14 Nov 2019, 11:24 by Kelly Johnstone

Dubai's education regulatory authority has announced a three-day break for schools in the emirate.

The the Knowledge and Human Authority (KHDA) said schools will be closed on from Sunday December 1 to Tuesday December 3 to mark Commemoration Day and the UAE National Day.

Students will be expected back in school on Wednesday December 4.
