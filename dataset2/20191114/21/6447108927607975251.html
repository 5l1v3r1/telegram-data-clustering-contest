Age of Empires 4 gameplay revealed at XO19

14 Nov 2019, 21:04 by Stefan L

Alongside the launch of Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition today, Microsoft have revealed the next step for the franchise. Age of Empires 4 is coming, y’all. Teased absolutely ages ago, it’s being developed by newly founded and named studio World’s Edge, though you’ll note renowned RTS developer Relic Entertainment still get a mention in the trailer.
