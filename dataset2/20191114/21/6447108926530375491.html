Black Friday Xbox One deals have been confirmed

14 Nov 2019, 21:03 by Aran Suddi

At XO19 Larry Hyrb ran through the major Xbox One Black Friday deals that consumers will be able to get their hands on during the annual sale. From November 24th until December 2nd Xbox One X consoles will be discounted to $349.99. The Xbox One S All Digital edition will also be discounted to $149.99 and will be bundled with Minecraft, Sea of Thieves, and Fortnite: Battle Royale with the legendary rogue spider knight skin.

If you don’t need a console but want accessories then the Xbox Wireless controllers will be discounted by $20 while the Xbox Design Lab controllers will be discounted by $10. From today the Xbox Game Pass Ultimate 3 month subscription will be available for just $1/£1. A 24-month sub will be $19.99. Xbox All Access subscribers will be able to upgrade to Project Scarlett when that releases. This will give access to all the big titles including Gears 5 and The Outer Wilds. If you prefer buying games then all Xbox Game Studio titles will be up 50% off. The deals include:

  1. Gears 5 for 33% off
  2. Forza Horizon 4 for 50% off
  3. Sea of Thieves: Anniversary Edition for 50% off
  4. State of Decay 2 for 50% off
  5. Hellblade: Senua’s Sacrifice for 50% off.
