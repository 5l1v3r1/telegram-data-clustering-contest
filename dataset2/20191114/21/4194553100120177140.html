(Video) “Our Sadio” – LFC fans love Mane helping unload Senegal’s team coach

14 Nov 2019, 21:09 by Ste Carson

Sadio Mane is a very nice man. Remember that video going around of him helping some men clean out a mosque in Liverpool? And when he realised he was being filmed, he went all shy? He’s a proper top bloke.

Well now another video of the star winger doing something which shows he has a caring side has emerged online, as he’s on international duty with Senegal.

As he exited the team coach, he noticed that there was a load of stuff that needed taking inside, so he picked up a couple handfuls of items and made his way on.

It’s only something simple, but sadly not every world class footballer would do this.
