CrossfireX gameplay teaser shown during XO19

14 Nov 2019, 21:13 by Aran Suddi

During E3 2019 Crossfire X was announced to be coming to Xbox One with the free to play shooter’s predecessor Crossfire amassing 650 million players on PC. Today at XO19 a new gameplay teaser was shown of the game in action. The trailer showcased the standard multiplayer first person shooter kind of approach but there was confirmation of bonus weapons like incoming mortars and a tank. In addition, there appear to be destructible environments.
