Daisy Ridley Describes ‘Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker’ in 4 Words

14 Nov 2019, 21:24 by Genevieve Scarano

Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker is coming to the big screen next month and fans have no idea what to expect for Rey’s next mission.

Daisy Ridley, who plays the heroine, recently discussed the movie with Entertainment Weekly. The publication asked her to describe The Rise of Skywalker and of course, the actress didn’t leave room for details.
