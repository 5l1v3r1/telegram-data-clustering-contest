Prince Harry, Meghan Markle Wish Prince Charles A Happy Birthday, Share Adorable Photo Of Baby Archie

14 Nov 2019, 21:38 by Becca Longmire

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle led the Prince Charles birthday messages Thursday.

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex shared an adorable pic of Harry with Charles, who turned 71, and baby Archie on Instagram, wishing “Grandpa” a very happy birthday.

Prince William and Kate Middleton also posted a pic of Prince Louis and two more snaps of Charles with his two sons.

Charles’ parents, Queen Elizabeth and Prince Philip, had another cute shot of Philip holding up a baby Charles.
