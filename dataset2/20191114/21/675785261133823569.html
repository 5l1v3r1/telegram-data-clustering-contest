Kevin Hart's friends pictured for the first time making incredible recovery after crash

Kevin Hart's friends were seen for the first time in their recovery following the crash on September 1

14 Nov 2019, 21:36 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Kevin Hart's friends were seen for the first time in recovery snaps after terrifying crash.

The stand-up comedian and his pals were involved in a car smash where his 1970 Plymouth Barracuda come off the Mulholland Highway and plunged down a gully.

His pals Jared Black, who was driving at the time, and Rebecca Broxterman, who was sat with Hart in the back of the vehicle, have not been seen since the crash until now.

Heart-rendering pictures showed Rebecca feed her love Jared with a tube while they made their recovery together.

Other snaps taken in the hospital bed show the couple in high spirits in each other's company.

She wrote: "GRATEFUL and BLESSED. September 1, 2019 was the scariest day of my life. God was definitely watching over us that night and protecting us. Thank you, Lord, for wrapping your loving arms around us in our time of need."

In the days following the accident, Rebecca penned a lengthy message which she waited two months to share.

She told how she and Jared were airlifted to hospital from the scene of the crash.

In a loving manner, she gushed her love was the "strongest" out of the three of them as she reflected on the incident that unfolded on September 1.

She wrote: "Written 9/17/19: First of all, I want you all to know that ultimately, all 3 of us in the car wreck are alive and recovering and that’s what matters the most! It is going to be a long road to recovery but we have the best support team. The scariest part for Jared and I was being separated after both being airlifted to UCLA.

"Just a lot of rest and physical therapy ahead for both of us! Jared is the strongest person I know and he will get through this. We still have each other and that’s what matters the most. This could’ve had a completely different outcome and we feel so blessed that it was not any worse.

"From the bottom of my heart, THANK YOU for all of the prayers, phone calls, texts and messages. It means the world to us that we feel so much love and support from you all at this time! I will keep you all updated as we embark on this new journey!"

Two months on, Rebecca gave everyone an update on how she and her love were doing in the recovery process.

She said the couple were feeling "so much better" and were "healing" together which has brought them closer following the car diaster.

Rebecca added: "TODAY: Feeling so much better! We are both healing! Jared is well on the road to recovery after having major back surgery! We are doing physical therapy together a few times a week!

"Our relationship is stronger than ever and we have so much to be thankful for! Life can take you in many different directions and we are just so happy to be here for it all!

"We have felt so much love these past 2.5 months and that doesn’t go unnoticed! So thank you to each one of you! This is our journey... it will have ups and it will have downs but we will always have each other.  @thejaredstanton  I love you so much and am thankful that we are doing life together!"

Kevin had made a return to the public eye when he attended the People Choice Awards.

The comedian has also made a comeback to work and even returned behind the wheel of a car.
