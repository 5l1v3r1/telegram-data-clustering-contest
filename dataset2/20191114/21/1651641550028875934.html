More Project xCloud details and surprises unveiled at X019

14 Nov 2019, 21:35 by Ash

During X019, more information regarding Microsoft’s Project xCloud was revealed, including a larger game library, better controller support, and a list of regions it’ll be hitting next year.

xCloud now has over 50 games in its library for all testers to try out. You can find a conveniently compiled complete list of them all at the end of the article. Game streaming for Xbox Game Pass is also set to hit next year.

At the moment, there are over 3,500 games available to play on Xbox One that will eventually be available to play on xCloud. As a nice bonus, 1,900 games that are currently in development are already working on xCloud.

Project xCloud is also set to come to Windows 10 next year. That means that you’ll be able to stream Xbox games on your PC.
