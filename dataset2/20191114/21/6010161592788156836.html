Ten Final Fantasy titles are coming to Xbox Game Pass

14 Nov 2019, 21:58 by Stephany Nunneley

Final Fantasy fans rejoice: ten titles are coming to Xbox Game Pass.

Square Enix announced today during X019 it was bringing ten Final Fantasy games to Xbox Game Pass to both console and PC.

Here’s the list:

  • Final Fantasy 7
  • Final Fantasy 8: Remastered
  • Final Fantasy 9
  • Final Fantasy 10: HD Remastered
  • Final Fantasy 10-2: HD Remastered
  • Final Fantasy 12: The Zodiac Age
  • Final Fantasy 13
  • Final Fantasy 13-2
  • Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy 13
  • Final Fantasy 15

That’s quite a lot. And it’s pretty great for not only fans of the franchise, but Game Pass subs as well.

All of the aforementioned titles are slated for the service beginning this holiday and throughout 2020.
