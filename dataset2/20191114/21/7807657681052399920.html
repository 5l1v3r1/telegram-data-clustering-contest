Updated November 14, 2019: It seems like the Kingdom Hearts games are also coming to Xbox Game Pass. Xbox's Phil Spencer revealed this on stage at X019.

X019 is going strong, and the announcements keep on dropping. Today, the company revealed 50 new games coming to Xbox Game Pass soon. While some of them are incredible, older experiences, there are some truly shocking additions to the mix. You can read about all of them below.

If you haven't tried Xbox Game Pass Ultimate yet, you can get your first three months for $1. For a limited time, all eligible Xbox Game Pass Ultimate members get a month of EA Access, three months of Discord Nitro, and six months of Spotify Premium included with their subscription. This is just an amazing value! If you were dreading subscribing before this offer, fear not, this isn't just for new members. These perks are for all Xbox Game Pass Ultimate members.
