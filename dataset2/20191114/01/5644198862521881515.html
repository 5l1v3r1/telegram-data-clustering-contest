Disney+ streaming service reaches 10 million sign-ups, shares surge

14 Nov 2019, 01:26

Walt Disney Co on Wednesday said its new streaming service, Disney+, has reached 10 million sign-ups since launching on Nov. 12.

REUTERS: Walt Disney Co on Wednesday said its new streaming service, Disney+, has reached 10 million sign-ups since launching on Nov. 12, sending shares up 3.5per cent to US$143.53 on the New York Stock Exchange.

In April the company said it plans to reach 60 million to 90 million Disney+ subscribers globally by 2024.
