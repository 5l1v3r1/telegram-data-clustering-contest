North Lanarkshire Council's gift card appeal boosted by encouraging efforts of employers

Several companies have placed drop-off points in their premises

14 Nov 2019, 08:00 by Ross Thomson

Generous employers are supporting North Lanarkshire Council’s Give-a-Gift-Card appeal.

Amey, Indeglas, Engie and Sidey are some of the companies contributing to the campaign by placing drop-off points in their premises and encouraging employees to donate gift cards worth at least £10.

The aim of the appeal is to make sure no child or teenager in North Lanarkshire is left without a present this Christmas, but also to empower their families to choose the present that best suits the age and interests of their youngsters.

 

The council’s deputy leader Paul Kelly said: “We are grateful that so many companies in North Lanarkshire are getting behind our gift card appeal.

“Their backing is important to guarantee its success, and to prove that when the council, our local communities and businesses join forces great things can be achieved.”

 

Drop-off locations available to the public can be viewed at www.northlanarkshire.gov.uk/GiveaGiftCard
