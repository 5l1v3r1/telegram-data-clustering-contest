Rampaging gunman kills student and wounds three in horror college shooting

Daniil Zasorin, 19, is reported to have opened fire on fellow students at Amur college in the Russian city of Blagoveshchensk, near the Russian border with Mongolia

14 Nov 2019, 08:05 by Will Stewart, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/will-stewart/

A college student opened fire on fellow students killing one and wounding three before turning the gun on himself.

Daniil Zasorin has been named by Russian media as the teenager who gunned down students at the a college in Blagoveshchensk, near the Russian border with Mongolia.

The 19-year-old was armed with a pump-action shotgun and was wounded by police before turning the gun on himself at the Amur college of Construction, Housing Maintenance and Utilities.

The shooting was in a classroom on an upper floor.

The gunman shot three fellow students, killing one, according to reports.

Others fled the scene in “panic”.

Student Alexey Golubnichy, 19, died in the college massacre.

The wounded were named as Egor Stasyuk, 17, Kasiyan Kamanets, 19, and Vladislav Rozhkov, 20.

They were rushed to hospital and are undergoing surgery.

A traffic policeman ran into the college after hearing shots and he fired at the gunman, wounding the 19-year-old, who was holed up in a classroom, it was reported.

The attacker then turned his weapon on himself.

 

His body was shown in pictures and video.

Earlier a teacher had twice asked the attacker to leave a classroom due to his behaviour, said witnesses.

He returned with a gun and opened fire.

 

A law enforcement source said: “When police inspectors went up to the second floor of the college, they saw a citizen with a gun.

“He shot at the traffic police.

“A policemen opened fire in return.

“The criminal was blocked inside a classroom.

“For the sake of public safety, the police evacuated everyone from the building.

“At the scene there were found the dead bodies of two young men, including the shooter.

“According to preliminary information, three more were injured.”

Armed police pointed guns at a college window from the nearby street in the city in the Russian far east.

The gun used by the attacker belonged to him, and was officially registered by police to him,

In the toilet the police later found his bag with more bullets.

According to the witnesses, the scene was covered with bullets shells.

An Interior Ministry source said: “The perpetrator committed suicide, using his own gun.

“It was registered in this citizen's name.”
