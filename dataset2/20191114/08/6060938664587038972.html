India vs Bangladesh: R Ashwin picks 250th Test wicket at home; third after Anil Kumble, Harbhajan Singh

14 Nov 2019, 08:31 by ANI

Highlights

  • Ashwin had 249 Test wickets under his belt when he stepped out on the field for the first Test against Bangladesh
  • Ashwin dismissed Bangladesh captain Mominul Haque to register his 250th wicket

INDORE: India spinner Ravichandran Ashwin bagged his 250th Test wicket at home during the first match against Bangladesh on Thursday.

By doing so, Ashwin became the third India bowler to achieve the milestone after Anil Kumble and Harbhajan Singh.

Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) took to Twitter and wrote: "250 Test wickets at home for @ashwinravi99. He becomes the third Indian bowler to do so after @anilkumble1074 & @harbhajan_singh."
