Kuwait's emir has no intention of dissolving parliament: KUNA

14 Nov 2019, 08:52

DUBAI (Reuters) - The Emir of Kuwait has no intention of dissolving parliament, the parliament speaker said, in a report carried by state news agency KUNA.

A government spokesman said on Thursday that Kuwait’s prime minister has submitted his government’s resignation to the country’s ruler.
