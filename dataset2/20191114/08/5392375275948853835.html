The smartphone industry is consistently evolving and we are now at the point where a regular update is a necessity. OEMs now use the regular release of updates or support for old models as a marketing strategy and its working. Huawei is quite consistent and regular with updates, especially for its flagship models. Now, recent reports show that the company has released the EMUI 10 stable system update to P30/P30 Pro European users.

The update size is about 4.5GB and the version number is EMUI10.0.0.168. As usual, this is an OTA update and it is rolling out in batches. If it is not available for your device at the moment, you have to be patient. Based on the Android 10 system development, EMUI 10 has added Diablo mode and brought a colorful AOD screen display and upgraded magazine lock screen. It also introduces 6 colors of Morandi style. There are also golden ratio icons, new camera experience, dark mode, smooth motion and more.

Huawei said that EMUI 10 breaks the barriers between Windows and Android. The keyboard and mouse of the PC become peripherals for mobile phones. EMUI 10 adopts three key distributed technologies and adopts a distributed UI programming framework to realize multi-end adaptation of the application interface.
