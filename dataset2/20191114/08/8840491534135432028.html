South Mayo alert following series of house break-ins

14 Nov 2019, 08:37

GARDAÌ in south Mayo have urged householders to be security alert following a series of break-ins to homes within the past week.

The burglaries occurred in Claremorris, Ballindine, Knock, Meelickmore and Mayfield.

The incidents have spread fear across the area with gardaì in Claremorris appealing for information that may help them with their enquiries.
