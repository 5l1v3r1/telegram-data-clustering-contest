Find out the Bollywood actresses who can feature in the Hindi remake of 'Charlie's Angels'

14 Nov 2019, 07:10 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

With the third instalment in the Charlie's Angels film series all set to release tomorrow, the Kristen Stewart, Naomi Scott, and Ella Balinska are all set to entertain us as they take the baton in their hands. While fans are eager to see the young millennial stars as the Angels in the high octane action drama, we take a look at Bollywood actresses, who can nail the characters of Charlie's Angels in the Hindi remake.

Deepika Padukone
With the elegance and poise that she poses, Deepika Padukone has proved her mettle in Bollywood and Hollywood as well. The diva starred opposite Vin Diesel in 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' and performed some breathtaking stunts as well. When it comes down to her acting chops, Deepika's success graph seems to be going higher and higher with each film.

Katrina Kaif

Her well-toned body and those mesmerising eyes is what makes her a perfect choice to star in the Hindi remake of 'Charlie's Angels'. Katrina Kaif has come a long way in her career and established herself as one of the A-listers in the industry due to her popularity and stunning display of her skills on the celluloid. Not only grace but the diva also knows how to use the guns and has done it in the past as well in films like 'Bang Bang!' and many others.

Anushka Sharma
Anushka Sharma might be away from the silver screen as she enjoys some quality time with her husband Virat Kohli, fans are eagerly waiting to see their favourite actress on the big screen once again. Her elegant moves and sweet persona makes her lethal and loved at the same time. With a niche to experiment with her on-screen avatars, Anushka's angel character would really leave us all impressed as we all would love to see the diva flaunt some high octane action moves on the celluloid.

The popular franchise which is now in the hands of ace filmmaker Elizabeth Banks is all set to release in India on 15th November and owing to the response that the trailers of the film have received, 'Charlie's Angels' looks all set to make a mark at the box office.
