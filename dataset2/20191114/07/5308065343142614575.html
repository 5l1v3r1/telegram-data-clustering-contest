HyunA reveals she had a crush on Dawn for more than a year

14 Nov 2019, 07:10

- Advertisement -

HyunA revealed that she had a crush on Dawn for more than a year before they started dating.

The couple featured as guests on the upcoming episode of JTBC’s ‘Knowing Brothers’ show where HyunA said, “Knowing Brothers’ will be our first and last time appearing on a variety show together.”

Cast members were curious about their relationship and flooded questions for both HyunA and Dawn.

HyunA shared that she had a crush on Dawn for a year and a half while Dawn revealed that he had no idea she liked him.

- Advertisement -

The episode of ‘Knowing Brothers’ was aired 9 November, 9PM KST.

HyunA made her comeback with ‘Flower Shower’ while Dawn made his comeback with ‘Money’.

HyunA, born June 6, 1992, as Kim Hyun-ah is a South Korean singer-songwriter, rapper and model. She debuted in February 2007 as a member of girl group Wonder Girls.

HyunA joined girl group 4Minute after leaving Wonder Girls and left JYP Entertainment. 4Minute is managed by Cube Entertainment.

In June 2009, 4Minute was launched and it became one of the most popular K-pop girl group.

DAWN, formerly known as E’Dawn is a South Korean rapper, songwriter and composer under P Nation.

DAWN was formerly from boy group PENTAGON and co-ed group Triple H. On November 5, 2019, Dawn debuted his solo single, ‘Money.’

HyunA announced on August 3, 2018, that she has been dating E’Dawn since May 2016.

Cube announced that Dawn left the agency after his exclusive contract was terminated on November 14, 2018.

Dawn and his partner HyunA signed with PSY’s new agency P Nation on January 27, 2019.

PSY uploaded a photo of the duo on his Instagram account announcing that the singles of both were completed and since 18 October, Hyojong’s name is being mentioned as DAWN.

Three teaser photos of ‘Money’ his solo debut single were released on October 28.
