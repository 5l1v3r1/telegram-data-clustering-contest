What channel is England vs Montenegro? TV and live stream information

The Three Lions are hopeful of securing qualification to Euro 2020 as they host Montenegro at Wembley on Thursday in their penultimate qualifying match

14 Nov 2019, 07:20 by Darren Wells, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/darren-wells/

England can seal their path to Euro 2020 with a win over Montenegro in the qualifying clash on Thursday.

Gareth Southgate's side faced a difficult week during the last international break, after suffering a shock defeat to Czech Republic before becoming embroiled in a racism storm in Bulgaria .

They will be hopeful of a more positive result and experience all round at Wembley, against a Montenegro team who've collected just three points in the campaign so far.

The Three Lions made light work of their opponents when facing them back in March, with a Ross Barkley brace helping them to a 5-1 win.

And Southgate will no doubt be eager for his team to produce a similar effort in their penultimate qualifier.

Here's everything you need to know about the Group A match...

When is the match?

The clash is on the evening of Thursday November 14, with kick-off scheduled for 7:45pm UK time.

Where is the match?

As mentioned, it will be at the home of English football - Wembley - where England have scored 14 goals in their last four appearances there.

What channel is it on?

ITV are broadcasting all of England's qualifiers, with this one set to be screened on ITV1.

Viewers can also live stream the match via the ITV Hub.

Team news

Southgate has been hit by a blow to his squad as Barkley has pulled out through injury.

The Chelsea midfielder grabbed two against Montenegro in the away fixture, as well as two in England's win over Bulgaria last time out.

Tom Heaton has also withdrawn from the squad, to be replaced by Sheffield United stopper Dean Henderson.

He'll make his international debut should he play any part in the match, while Fiyako Tomori and James Maddison could also take their first England bows.
