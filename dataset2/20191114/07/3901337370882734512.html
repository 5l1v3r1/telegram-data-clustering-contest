Big Guns In Hunt For AmaZulu Top Goalscorer?  

14 Nov 2019, 07:45 by Soccer Laduma

It is believed that both the big guns of South African football Orlando Pirates and Kaizer Chiefs are keeping a close eye on AmaZulu’s Bonginkosi Ntuli.

Ntuli is AmaZulu’s leading goalscorer this season, having scored all of the team’s seven goals so far in their campaign.

As reported in edition 1149 of Soccer Laduma, indication is that the 28-year-old striker could get a second bite of the apple at one of the so-called Big Three of the PSL, following his unsuccessful spell with Mamelodi Sundowns, before he joined AmaZulu on a permanent deal. 

“Bonginkosi Ntuli’s form for AmaZulu and his knack for scoring goals resulted in him attracting interest from big clubs in the PSL. I can confirm that both Pirates and Chiefs are eyeing the player,” confirmed a source.

“The boy is among the leading goalscorers in the PSL early on in the season and it’s not surprising that there’s this huge interest in his services,” the source added.  

When Ntuli was signed by Sundowns in 2014, the Brazilians had edged the Buccaneers to his signature, with the 23-year-old at the time on the back of an impressive season with Golden Arrows.
