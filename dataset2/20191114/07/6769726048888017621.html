Man charged over alleged burglary spree in Perth coastal suburbs

14 Nov 2019, 07:27 by Laura Pond

POLICE have charged a Doubleview man over alleged burglary and stealing offences in nearby coastal suburbs.

Mirrabooka Detectives and Scarborough Police arrested a 29-year-old man on November 13 in relation to home burglaries, car break ins, stealing and fraud offences they believe were committed in Doubleview, Karrinyup, North Beach, Watermans Bay, Trigg and Embleton between August 17 and October 28.

In one of the incidents, $600 cash, a watch and a car were stolen from a Doubleview home.

The man will face five home burglaries, seven stealing and nine fraud charges.
