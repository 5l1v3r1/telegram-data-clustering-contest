Rockstar apologizes for problems with PC version Red Dead Redemption 2

14 Nov 2019, 07:48 by Hilbert Hagedoorn

Rockstar has apologized for the problems PC gamers are experiencing with Red Dead Redemption 2. A new update that should be released quickly should solve the problems. Problems occurred with the combination of certain Nvidia cards, Nvidia drivers and processors.

Last week two updates have been that should have solved the problem, however. Rockstar claims that a relatively small number of players still have to deal with the problems which are being worked on. Everybody will get a complimentary care package in the form of Prieto Poncho and an assortment of provisions and ammo.
