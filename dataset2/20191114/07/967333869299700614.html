Parental Patience: Tiny Golden Retriever Pup Wants to Play

14 Nov 2019, 07:30

Being a parent is a wonderful experience, but definitely not easy, it requires a lot of love, attention, and, most of all – patience. Especially, when your children are puppies and belong to a very prolific breed- where do these kids get their energy?

This adorable golden retriever puppy wants to play – but the grown-up doggo just sits there calmly and sighs. It is obviously waiting for the little guy to learn to appreciate the chance to catch some shut-eye, finally go to sleep and stop preventing others from doing the same.
