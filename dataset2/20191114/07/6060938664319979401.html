Shahid Kapoor's 'Kabir Singh' surpasses Hrithik Roshan's 'War', Akshay Kumar's 'Housefull 4' and Salman Khan's 'Bharat' to set this record at the box office

14 Nov 2019, 07:42 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

This year saw several Bollywood actor experimenting with their on-screen avatar and opting for films with a good narrative. Even the audience entertained with some stellar performances with B-town biggies like Salman Khan, Akshay Kumar, Hrithik Roshan came out with their respective films which managed to make a mark at the box office.

Shahid Kapoor, whose last outing 'Batti Gul Meter Chalu' which failed to impress the audience last year, joined hands with south director Sandeep Vanga for the Hindi remake of his blockbuster Telugu film 'Arjun Reddy'. The film which also featured Kiara Advani in lead role managed to entertain the audience and did exceptionally well in the domestic circuit.

According to a report on Boxofficeindia.com, 'Kabir Singh' which was touted as a dark horse by trade experts has managed to top the list of highest-grossing films in multiplexes of 2019. The film which did outstandingly well in metro regions managed to surpass the likes of Hrithik Roshan's 'War', Akshay Kumar's 'Housefull 4' and 'Mission Mangal', Ranveer Singh's 'Gully Boy', Salman Khan's 'Bharat' and others to become the highest-grossing film in multiplexes this year.

With a total collection of Rs 221.52 crore from multiplexes, Shahid's film is well ahead of Hrithik and Tiger Shroff's high octane action film 'War' which settled for the second spot with Rs 217.25 crore, followed by Vicky Kaushal's 'Uri: The Surgical Strike' and Akshay's films 'Mission Mangal' and 'Housefull 4'.

Meanwhile, on the professional front, Shahid is all set to star in the Hindi remake of Nani's sports drama 'Jersey'. This has already got the fans excited and all eyes are now set of Sasha's next film.
