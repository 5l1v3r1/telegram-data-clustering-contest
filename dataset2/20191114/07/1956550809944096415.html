Madness coming to Dubai in 2020

The popular UK band will be playing at Dubai Duty Free Tennis Stadium

14 Nov 2019, 07:59 by Amy Mathieson

Massive fan of Madness? Listen up as the British ska band are coming to town next year, and will be playing at Dubai Duty Free Tennis Stadium at The Irish Village.

Madness will be performing at Dubai Duty Free Tennis Stadium on Thursday March 5 – and tickets go on sale next week (Thursday November 21).

Get ready for a stream of hits including Baggy Trousers, House of Fun, Our House and One Step to get you singing along and jumping up and down.

The band has had 15 top ten hits over the years and they’re sure to put on a huge show in Dubai. They’ve even won an Ivor Novella Award for Outstanding Song Collection, so this is one gig you’ll want to see.

Tickets cost Dhs315 for standing and Dhs275 for seats so book now before it’s too late – as this is sure to be a popular gig.

Madness will be supported by UK newcomer Emily Capell, who has just released her first album Combat Frock as well as DJ Katie Owen, who is currently touring with Jo Whiley.

Meanwhile on Friday March 20, 2020, singer Midge Ure is also coming to The Irish Village. Tickets again go on sale next Thursday (November 21) so get ready for a mega ticket booking session.

The evening will celebrate his current ‘Vienna & Visage – 1980 Tour’, with the complete Vienna album live, with highlights of Visage throughout the set.
