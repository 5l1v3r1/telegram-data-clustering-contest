West of Dead is an Xbox game featuring Ron Pearlman

14 Nov 2019, 20:29 by Lewis White

Xbox has announced a new video game featuring Ron Pearlman: West of Dead.

Playing as a Ghost Rider rip-off that’s also just a cowboy, it would appear that the title is some form of roguelite. You’ll be able to shoot enemies, take cover behind tables and more.

Apart from starring Ron Pearlman, West of Dead also features some gorgeous cel shaded visuals in a gorgeous top-down environment.
