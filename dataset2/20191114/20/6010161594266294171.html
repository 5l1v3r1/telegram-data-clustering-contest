Project xCloud Preview: 50 new games added, coming to PC in 2020, will support DualShock controller

14 Nov 2019, 20:41 by Stephany Nunneley

50 new titles are being added to the Project xCloud Preview library, and in 2020, it will launch on Windows 10 PCs. Razer and Sony DualShock controller support is also coming.

Microsoft opened up Project xCloud testing to the public in select countries back in October, and today 50 new titles are playable.

Xbox Console Streaming was made available in preview for Xbox Insiders in the Alpha and Alpha Skip-ahead rings recently. Insiders are able to try owned and installed Xbox One titles on an Android device remotely from the Xbox One console. The games included in the preview also encompass Xbox Game Pass titles.

Additionally, the company announced the preview will open up to more markets in 2020 including Canada, India, Japan, and Western Europe.
