Kacey Musgraves Admits She’s Paying The Price For Partying Hard After CMA Win: ‘Can You Die From A Hangover?’

14 Nov 2019, 20:28 by Brent Furdyk

Kacey Musgraves had a big night Wednesday at the CMA Awards, winning Female Vocalist of the Year thanks to the success of her critically acclaimed album Golden Hour and hit single “Slow Burn”.

As if that wasn’t enough excitement for one night, she also took to the stage of Nashville’s Brdigestone Arena to perform a duet of the “Muppet Movie” classic “Rainbow Connection” with living legend Willie Nelson.
