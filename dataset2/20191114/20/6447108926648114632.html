(Updated) Bleeding Edge release date is confirmed for March 2020

14 Nov 2019, 20:31 by Aran Suddi

Update: During the XO19 special of Inside Xbox there was confirmation from Ninja Theory that Bleeding Edge would be releasing on March 24th, and the beta details are correct. Details below.

Original Story.

It looks like Ninja Theory’s Microsoft exclusive Bleeding Edge, the online brawler, will be out on March 24th according to a now deleted store page for the game from the Microsoft site. It’s very likely then that the release date is something that is going to be confirmed tonight at the XO19 conference. Alongside the release date there will be two closed betas as well, with one set to take place on February 14th and another one on March 13th.
