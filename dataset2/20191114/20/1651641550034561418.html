Rare announces Everwild, a brand-new IP

14 Nov 2019, 20:18 by Lewis White

Sea of Thieves developer Rare has announced an all-new IP at Xbox’s X019: Everwild. 

Described as a “unique and unforgettable experience” that takes place in “a natural and magical world”, Everwild is certainly one hell of a looker.

Inheriting the cartoon visuals of the developer’s previous pirate adventure, the title also manages to bring forth gorgeous lighting and visuals into this expressive new world.
