The Artful Escape re-emerges with a 2020 release date

14 Nov 2019, 20:46 by Aran Suddi

The Artful Escape first made an appearance during E3 2017 during Microsoft’s conference and may have kind of got lost in the shuffle at the time. Since then it’s been pretty quiet. The Artful Escape has appeared on lists to say it is enhanced for Xbox One X. Today, during XO19, The Artful Escape reappeared briefly as it was confirmed the game would be released in 2020.
