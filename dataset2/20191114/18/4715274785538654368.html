Holiday Merryland

14 Nov 2019, 18:08

The doors of Holiday Merryland at SM City Sta. Mesa are now open with a fun-filled launch on Nov.  9, made extra merry by special guests, Kelley Day as host, Alwyn and Jenica Uytingco with their lovely little ones (inset), and rising star Krystal Brimner. Also bringing gladness and cheer were the San Sebastian Chamber Singers and Party with Jive Theatre Group. Also present at the inauguration was Quezon City District IV Councilor Hero Bautista, as he played a part in opening the Instagram-worthy spectacle to the shopping public.
