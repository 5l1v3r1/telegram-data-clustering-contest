[Deal] TCL TVs are already on sale for up to 40% ahead of Black Friday

14 Nov 2019, 18:20 by Jared Peters

November is a great month for buying a TV. That’s typically when we see the best deals on them, and right now TCL has jumped on the bandwagon even ahead of Black Friday.

There are several options of TCL TVs discounted on Amazon, some of which are over 40% off. There’s a mix of 5-series and 6-series here, too, so you get some flexibility with what you want to pay for.

The cheapest of the bunch of a 32-inch TV down to just $149. This one’s much more basic, but would be great in a spare room or bedroom. If you want to step up into something for playing games or watching movies, they have a 43-inch and 55-inch Roku TV on sale for $299 and $399, respectively. Both of these TVs support Dolby Vision, which is really rare in this price point, and have Roku’s whole ecosystem built-in.

If you’re wanting to step up to something a little nicer, the TCL 6-series 55″ and 65″ TVs are down to $599 and $799. These are QLED TVs with Quantum Dot technology, so you’ll see a pretty nice improvement in color reproduction and contrast on these sets, and they still support Dolby Vision, too. The 75-inch version is on sale for $1399, which is definitely getting pricey, but hey, if you’re gonna go big, go all the way.
