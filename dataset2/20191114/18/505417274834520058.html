Zach Elbouzedi Goal Gives Ireland U21's Crucial Win Over Armenia

14 Nov 2019, 18:06 by Oisin McQueirns

Zach Elbouzedi’s second-half goal gave ten man Ireland a crucial victory away to Armenia to keep them top of their Euro 2021 qualification group.

Despite missing a host of starters, including suspended captain Jayson Molumby and the duo of Lee O’Connor and Troy Parrott, who are both with the senior side, Kenny’s men dominated proceedings throughout the first half but couldn’t quite find the opening goal.

In the second 45, Ireland continued to put the pressure on and eventually broke the deadlock thanks to Waterford’s Elbouzedi who found himself on the end of a ball into the box from Adam Idah and the 21-year-old steadied himself and finished impressively past the Armenian goalkeeper.
