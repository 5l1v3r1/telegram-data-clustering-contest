FaZe Ewok has joined Mixer

14 Nov 2019, 18:56 by Ash

Soleil “Ewok” Wheeler, a deaf 14 year old Fortnite pro, has become the latest streamer to leave Twitch in favour of Microsoft’s Mixer platform.

Following in the likes of Tyler “Ninja” Blevins, Michael “Shroud” Grzesiek, Cory “Gothalion” Michael, and Supergirl Guest Actor Rahul Kohli, Ewok has opted to stream exclusively on Mixer.

Ewok broke the news on Twitter with a short video that shows her painting the Mixer logo on a wall.
