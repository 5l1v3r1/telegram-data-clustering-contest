Amy Hart is friendly with Molly-Mae Hague amid cattiest Love Island girl feud ever

Amy stayed friendly with Molly-Mae despite the major rift which tore the Love Island girls apart from each other

14 Nov 2019, 18:56 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

Amy Hart has made no secret of her friendship with Molly-Mae Hague despite the other girls feelings towards the Pretty Little Thing ambassador.

The former cabin crew member never let it bother her Molly-Mae didn't get on with the likes of Anna Vakili and Amber Gill, even though she remained friends with them herself.

In the snap, Amy and Molly-Mae looked like BFFs while leaning in to capture the gorgeous selfie on camera for social media.

The blonde bombshells put on a friendly display when they were reunited for a work commitment, in London on Thursday.

Amy captioned the post: "With my girl."

The air hostess appears to catch up with Molly-Mae every so often when they catch up between the model's ever busy work schedule.

Amy has been vocal about her friendship with Molly-Mae in the past.

The air hostess broke her silence on the rift rumours during a cosy exclusive chat with The Mirror at Mayfair's Hotel Bar, in London.

Also, the former cabin crew star shared the real unspoken reason why Molly-Mae didn't make Anna Vakili's Love Island reunion sleepover.

Amy spat it was "out of order" when she come under the attack of trolls because they immediately assumed Molly-Mae, Maura Higgins and Lucie Donlan were not invited.

The bikini beauty also set the record straight as although she had chosen to share the photographs, she hadn't arranged the sleepover herself.

Amy said: "I got so many comments, where's Molly? Where's Maura? Where's Lucie? You lot [trolls] were out of order. It wasn't my sleepover. [It was Anna's.] I posted the picture.

"If you had a friendship group of 36 people or 18 girls, how often would you all get together? Say we're shift workers, we don't have 9-5 schedules."
