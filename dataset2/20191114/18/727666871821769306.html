FBI Says Kodak Black Was Under The Influence Of Drugs In Prison When He Seriously Injured A Guard

14 Nov 2019, 18:01 by AllHipHop Staff

Kodak Black added to his long list of legal troubles, after he grabbed a guard's testicles and sent him to the hospital with serious injuries.

(AllHipHop News) Kodak Black's time behind bars has already been eventful.

On October 29th, the rapper was involved in a fight with another inmate, when he attacked a guard who was attempting to break up the altercation.

The pepper-sprayed blasted at Kodak Black failed to have an immediate effect and he punched the guard twice.

Then the "ZeZe" rapper grabbed the guard's testicles and squeezed them so hard that "his abdomen and intestinal wall were breached.”

The prison official was subsequently hospitalized and had to undergo surgery as a result of the incident.

According to the Miami Herald, it took four guards to properly restrain Kodak Black, who has yet to be charged for the intitial fight, or the alleged assault on the guard.

The information was revealed yesterday (November 13th), when Kodak Black was formally sentenced to almost four years in prison for lying on a federal gun application in an attempt to buy three guns.
