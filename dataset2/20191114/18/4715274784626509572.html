Shopee wraps up outstanding 11.11 Big Sale with 70M items sold

14 Nov 2019, 18:33 by Manila Standard

Shopee, the leading e-commerce platform in Southeast Asia and Taiwan, wrapped up its largest Shopee 11.11 Big Sale with 70 million items sold on November 11.

There were also thrice more orders recorded in the 1st hour compared to 2018 Shopee also delivered on its promise to offer an engaging shopping experience at  11.11 Big Sale. Users returned daily to play and win prizes via Shopee’s collection of in-app games, including its first augmented reality game Shopee Catch. The games were played over 150 million times in three weeks.  In addition, Shopee brought non-stop entertainment to users with over 65 million views recorded across its 11.11 Big Sale streams. Local celebrities including Alden Richards, Maine Mendoza, and Jose Mari Chan, as well as superstars GFriend and Rainie Yang also joined special streams on November 10 and 11 to celebrate the finale of 11.11 Big Sale with users. “We are delighted to continue our robust year-end momentum with a successful Shopee 11.11 Big Christmas Sale. More sellers, brands, and partners joined us this year as we delivered an engaging and social experience for our users. Shopee will continue to localise this global 11.11 phenomenon for the people in our region and we look forward to even more success in the years ahead,” Martin Yu, Associate Director at Shopee Philippines said. Highlights of Shopee 11.11 Big Christmas Sale in the Philippines In the Philippines, Shopee 11.11 Big Christmas Sale treated users to three weeks of unstoppable promotions and entertainment, headlined by free shipping with no minimum spend. Shopee welcomed the peak sale day with the Shopee 11.11 Big Christmas TV Special on November 10 live on GMA and Shopee Live. The televised show featured performances from top celebrities including Alden Richards, Maine Mendoza, Heart Evangelista, and more. Brand ambassador Jose Mari Chan also gave an exclusive performance of Shopee’s latest Christmas jingle on-stage.  On November 11, brands and sellers on Shopee received a huge boost to their sales. Top performing brands recorded an average of 4,227 times increase in traffic and 1,899 times growth in sales, while shopping activity peaked at 12:10 a.m. Shopee continued to see more male users and older users shopping on November 11. Male users are shopping most often for Mobile & Accessories and Men’s Apparel products, with power banks, earphones, and jogger pants being the hottest items. In addition, users aged over 50 were looking for household necessities like water dispensers, portable fans, and outdoor grills. Finally, Shopee also observed the following trends in the Philippines at Shopee 11.11 Big Christmas Sale: Most Popular Brands: Maybelline, Pampers, Unilever, Globe, and Bosch Top Categories: Mobiles & Accessories, Women’s Apparel, and Home & Living Most Active City: Metro Manila Yu closed: “We are heartened by the continued support from all our users and partners. Stay tuned for the grand finale at our upcoming Shopee 12.12 Big Christmas Sale as Shopee gives back to users for their incredible support in 2019.” Download the Shopee app for free via the App Store or Google Play
