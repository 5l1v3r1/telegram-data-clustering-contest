Love Island's Amber Gill has fascinated the nation with her array of transformations.

And the 2019 winner wowed her fans once again when she unveiled another dramatically different look on her Instagram stories on Thursday.

Amber opted for a change of hairstyle entirely as she regrouped with her Love Island besties Anna Vakili and Yewande Bialas.

Typically known for her bouncy corkscrew curls, the Love Island star got everyone doing double takes when she arranged her locks in loose waves.

The glamorous MissPap ambassador kept her caramel-coloured style, although she decided to change up the style of her natural curls.

In a series of Instagrams, Amber drew attention to her new hairdo by filming a number of pieces to camera.

The stunning model touched her face with her hands, framing her face, and ensuring all eyes were on her.

Amber would have been almost unrecognisable if she hadn't been hanging out with her usual Love Island crowd.

Despite having a very distinctive look with her springy curls, Amber has unveiled a number of transformations since leaving the villa to wow her fans.

Most notably, the Love Island beauty had all the attention on her when she sported bouncy black waves that were nothing like her natural locks.

Amber had made the epic entrance at her hotly-anticipated launch of her clothing range with MissPap, in London at a bash attended by the great and the good of the Love Islanders.
