PML-N biggest enemy of Nawaz Sharif's life: Fawad Chaudhry

14 Nov 2019, 18:56 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Federal Minister for Science and Technology Fawad Chaudhry has on Thursday said Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) and the Sharif family are the biggest enemy of former prime minister Nawaz Shaarif’s life.

While talking to media, the federal minister said Nawaz Sharif was safe at hospital and the government is worried about his health since he has been shifted to his home.

Fawad Chaudhry maintained that the cabinet made a balanced decision, and even Shehbaz Sharif, Maryam Nawaz and Hussain Nawaz are not willing to submit indemnity bond.

PML-N is playing with the life of Nawaz Sharif and it apparently looks like he is not safe at Jati Umra, the federal minister said while condemning the politics that is being done over the former premier’s health.

He affirmed that the politics of Nawaz Sharif and former president Asif Ali Zardari has ended. A race has been ongoing in PML-N for the leadership, he added.

He announced to move privilege motion against PML-N leader Khawaja Asif and said it is regrettable to lie on the floor of the House.
