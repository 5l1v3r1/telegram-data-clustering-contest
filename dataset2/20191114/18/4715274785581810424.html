‘Your Moment’ captures viewers nationwide

14 Nov 2019, 18:10 by Manila Standard Showbiz

Your Moment, ABS-CBN’s two-in-one talent reality competition, recorded high ratings and wowed viewers nationwide with its electrifying local and foreign acts during its premiere.

The show’s world premiere garnered a national TV rating of 28.9 percent versus The Clash’s 16.4 percent last Saturday (Nov. 9), according to data from Kantar Media. Audiences continued to watch Your Moment on Sunday (Nov. 10), which recorded a national TV rating of 26.3 percent compared to The Clash’s 17.4 percent.

The first two episodes welcomed viewers into the state-of-the-art revolving set that offers the studio audience views of both the dancing and singing stages, as well as the emotion meter used by judges Billy Crawford, Nadine Lustre, and Boy Abunda to input their scores for each performance.

Winning dancing acts Ruthless Comrades of Taytay, Rizal and Kenyo Street Fam of Bulacan, meanwhile, showed how camaraderie and teamwork made their acts and performances more cohesive. Also capturing viewers’ attention and emotion were the winning singing acts Lez 2 Men, the lesbian trio from Cainta, Rizal, and Indonesian duo The Dens Gonjalez shaking up the stage with their rock and roll.

Meanwhile, Your Moment’s highly esteemed hosts, judges, and production value were also the talk of the town the whole weekend as positive comments continued to pour online during its world premiere. In fact, the show already trended on Twitter on the morning of its world premiere. 

Apart from watching TV, fans also tuned in to Your Moment’s online show which featured hosts Sharlene San Pedro, AC Bonifacio, Matty Juniosa of Idol Philippines, and Dawn Chang, who joined the show live via Skype from Australia.
