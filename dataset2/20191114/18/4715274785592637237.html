Flagship NEX 3 smartphone now available in Vivo stores, Lazada and Shopee

14 Nov 2019, 18:53 by Manila Standard

Vivo NEX 3, the leading global technology company’s latest flagship smartphone, is officially available in the local market starting today.

Smartphone enthusiasts who are looking to get their hands on the premium handset can purchase the NEX 3 for only P39,999 at authorized Vivo stores nationwide. The much-anticipated smartphone in the high-end NEX series, reputed to be the “device of the future”, is also available online at bit.ly/LazadaNEX3 and bit.ly/ShopeeNEX3. The NEX 3 is equipped with Snapdragon 855 Plus and 8GB RAM/128GB ROM, all powered by a 4500mAh battery.  For a “future beyond edges” experience, the NEX 3 boasts the world’s first Super AMOLED Waterfall FullView™ display and a seamless body without any notch, bezel or buttons. It also has a 64-megapixel primary camera, a 13MP wide-angle camera, and a 13MP telescopic camera in the rear and a 16MP elevating front camera for high-quality images. To know more about the Vivo NEX 3, visit www.vivoglobal.ph/product/Nex3​.
