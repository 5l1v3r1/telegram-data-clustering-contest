Andy Serkis joins Robert Pattinson starrer 'The Batman' as Alfred Pennyworth; Matt Reeves confirms

14 Nov 2019, 10:00 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

The makers of Robert Pattinson starrer 'The Batman' which is being helmed by Matt Reeves seem to be leaving no stone unturned as they have managed to rope in Andy Serkis after getting Paul Dano, Zoe Kravitz and Jeffrey Wright on-board.

Taking to Twitter, Reeves shared a witty gif revealing that Serkis has joined the cast as Batman's most trusted person Alfred Pennyworth. He wrote, "And here comes #Alfred !"
