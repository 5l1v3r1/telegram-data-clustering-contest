Lata Mangeshkar's health update: The singer is stable and the family looks forward to take her home soon

14 Nov 2019, 10:31 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Ace Lata Mangeshkar, who was admitted to the Intensive Care Unit of a hospital earlier this week, is showing positive health signs and the family is looking forward to taking her home as soon as she recovers.

Mangeshkar, 90, was admitted to the Breach Candy Hospital after complaining about difficulty in breathing. Reportedly, is now showing some signs of improvement but it will take time for her to recover. She has pneumonia and chest infection. Any person suffering from it takes time to recover from it.

We came across a statement sent by Lata's spokesperson. The statement is as follows: "Lata Mangeshkar ji Progress Statement:

Lata didi is stable. The progress is steady and good. We look forward to take her home as she gets well. Thank You for your prayers and support."

In her over seven-decade career, Mangeshkar has sung over 30,000 songs across languages. She is considered one of the greatest playback singers in Indian cinema. She received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour, in 2001.
