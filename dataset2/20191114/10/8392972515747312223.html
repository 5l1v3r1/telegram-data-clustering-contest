Harry and Meghan share unseen photo of Archie to celebrate Prince Charles' birthday

It's a photo from Archie's christening earlier this year

14 Nov 2019, 10:06 by Rebecca Carter

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex have shared a new photo of baby son Archie to social media.

The black-and-white picture shows doting dad Harry carrying Archie, who was wearing the traditional royal christening gown, as Charles gazed at his grandson.
