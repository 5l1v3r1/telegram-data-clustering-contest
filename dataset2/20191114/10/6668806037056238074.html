Sidharth Malhotra, Nora Fatehi, Aditya Roy Kapur and more attend Milap Zaveri’s birthday bash

14 Nov 2019, 10:03 by Kajol Mathur

Bollywood filmmaker Milap Zaveri who is currently gearing up for the release of his next – Marjaavaan starring Sidharth Malhotra, Tara Sutaria, Riteish Deshmukh and Rakul Preet Singh celebrates his birthday today. Making it super special for him were his close friends from the movie business who joined him last night to ring in the birthday. 

Present for the celebrations were several stars including Sidharth Malhotra, Nora Fatehi, Aditya Roy Kapur, Riteish Deshmukh, Genelia D’Souza, Rakul Preet Singh, Jackky Bhagnani along with Divya Khosla Kumar and Bhushan Kumar.

One look at the pictures, it’s easy to say they all had a blast together.
