The strange call from Florentino Perez to Gerard Pique

Pique revealed a phone call from the Madrid president as he appeared on Movistar

14 Nov 2019, 10:20 by Sport EN

Gerard Pique was a protagonist on La Resistencia on Movistar again. This time he appeared in conjunction with the start of the Davis Cup next week in Madrid. Among other things, Pique revealed a curious call from Real Madrid president Florentino Perez.

"It was not to talk about football," said Pique, after saying he had the Madrid president's number. "Not football or business," he said.

During the interview he also said that a worker at Real Madrid wrote to Pique when he published his telephone number.
