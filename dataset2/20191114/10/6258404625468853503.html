Walmart's Black Friday Deals Includes $98 40-inch Roku TV, $129 Apple Watch, And More

14 Nov 2019, 10:28 by Paul Lilly

The best way to attack Black Friday and get the best deals is to formulate a game plan ahead of time. Fortunately, it is easier than ever to do so, as companies no longer wait until the last minute to reveal what kind of bargains can be had. While there will inevitably be some surprises, Walmart, for example, is offering a preview of some the Black Friday deals to come.

On the front page of Walmart's Black Friday flyer, it lists three televisions, the least expensive of which is a 40-inch Onn-brand Full HD 1080p TV with built-in Roku functionality, for $98. It will be available online starting Wednesday, November 27 at 10:00 pm ET, and in stores on Thursday, November 28 (Thanksgiving) at 6:00 pm ET.

Some other TVs that will be on sale include...

  • Onn 50-inch 4K Roku Smart TV: $148
  • Samsung 50-inch 4K Smart TV: $278 (save $50)
  • Samsung 55-inch 4K Smart TV: $328 (save $50)
  • Onn 58-inch 4K Roku Smart TV: $198
  • Phillips 65-inch 4K Android Smart TV: $278
  • Vizio 65-inch 4K Smart TV: $398 (save $130)

There will likely be other TV deals as well, with the more heavily discounted ones selling out first. It also looks like Walmart is offering models only for Black Friday. That's why we have not highlighted the savings on some of them above—there's no regular listing price for the Onn 50-inch 4K Roku Smart TV, for example.

This is not uncommon—retailers try their best to lure shoppers to their stores, both online and in their actual brick and mortar locations. So, expect supplies to be highly limited on many of these items.

Outside of TVs, Walmart will be offering discounts a bunch of other stuff as well. One of them is an Apple Watch Series 3 for $129 (save $70), and other is a 7th gen iPad (32GB) for $249 (save $80).

Here are some other highlights...

  • Oculus Go VR Headset (32GB): $149 (save $50)
  • Sony PlayStation 4 1TB Bundle: $199
  • Beats Solo3 Wireless Headphones: $129 (save $70)
  • Lenovo Smart Clock: $39 (save $40)
  • HP Pavilion Laptop (i5-9300H, GTX 1050, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD): $499 (save $250)
  • HP Pavilion Desktop (i5-9400F, GTX 1660 Ti, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD): $579
  • Fitbit Inspire HR: $69 (save $30)
  • Nintendo Switch with Mario Kart 8 Deluxe: $299
  • Respawn 110 Racing Style Gaming Chair: $109 (save $60)

Some of the deals are obviously better than others. Nevertheless, there will be a whole bunch of items discounted. It's not limited to electronics, either. From clothes and toys, to home appliances and automotive products, there will be lots to choose from.
