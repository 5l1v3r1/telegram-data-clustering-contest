B&M's doormat sensors play Christmas songs when walked on and they cost just £4.99

The budget store operates multiple branches across Birmingham and throughout the wider Midlands region

14 Nov 2019, 10:40 by James Rodger, Sophie Roberts, https://www.facebook.com/jamesrodgerjournalist

Fairytale Of New York, Last Christmas, All I Want For Christmas is You... at this time of year, these tunes are inescapable.

And, just when you thought it couldn't get worse, B&M is selling £4.99 doormat sensors that play Christmas songs when walked on.

B&M has created the perfect product for Christmas addicts.

But, if you can't bear the sound of Wham, The Pogues or Mariah Carey at this time of year, you'll want to avoid it.

The budget store operates multiple branches across Birmingham and throughout the wider Midlands region.

Pictures of the product were posted to the Extreme Couponing and Bargains group on Facebook, reports Daily Star.

A savvy shopper revealed you can buy the door mat an sensor for a combined total of £7.98.

She captioned the images: “This is amazing for all you Christmas lovers… B&M, £4.99.

“You only get the sensor. The door mats are £2.99.”

The post has since racked up 1,600 likes and more than 4,500 comments.

By the looks of the response, many will be rushing out to buy one of the festive doormats.

One commenter remarked: “This is right up my street.”

Another said: “I saw this yesterday. Sooooo tempting lol.”

A third added: “I’m getting this.”

 

While many have been raving about B&M’s Christmassy product, some consumers have urged caution.

One responder joked: “Not good if you have kids you'll soon want to return it. It's good though!”

A second laughed: “This would annoy the hell out of my other half.

“He’s a Scrooge – I must get it!”

Another remarked: “I saw this and thought it might annoy my neighbours."
