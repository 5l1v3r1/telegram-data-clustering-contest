Amazon India announced Apple Days and Oppo Fantastic Days, three days of sale starting November 13 till 15 offering deals on a variety of products. On the Apple side, the e-commerce giant is providing discounts and exchange offers on iPhones, iPads, Apple Watches, Macbooks, and accessories. As for Oppo, the Reno 10X Zoom Edtion is available at ₹39,990, a whopping ₹10,000 less than its regular price along with exchange offers on a wide range of recently priced dropped smartphones.

Amazon India Apple Days

Amazon India is offering up to ₹23,000 off on exchanging eligible phones while buying iPhones. Also, HDFC Bank Debit and Credit cardholders can avail flat ₹7,000 and ₹6,000 instant discounts on iPhone 11 Pro Max and iPhone 11 / iPhone 11 Pro respectively. This offer is applicable on EMI payments as well.

Other than that, the last year’s iPhone XR is available at a discounted price of ₹42,990 and buyers can further lower the price by up to ₹7,450 by exchanging any eligible smartphone. The new iPad 7th Generation and Apple Watch Series 5 are also available for HDFC instant discount of ₹3,000 and ₹4,000 respectively.

Check out all the Amazon India Apple Days Sale including the ones of Macbooks and accessories which we haven’t mentioned here by visiting this dedicated page.

Amazon India Oppo Fantastic Days

As mentioned earlier, the Oppo Reno 10X Zoom Edition(8GB / 256GB) receives a ₹10,000 price cut for the sale and hence it now retails for ₹39,990 along with 9 months no-cost EMI option. Whereas, other popular models are on sale with exchange offers, even the ones who have recently received price cuts.

  • Oppo Reno 2 (₹3,500 on exchange)
  • Oppo Reno 2Z (₹3,000 on exchange)
  • Oppo Reno 2F (₹2,500 on exchange)
  • Oppo Reno A9 2020 (₹3,000 on exchange)
  • Oppo Reno A5 2020 (₹2,000 on exchange)

The Oppo A5 and A9 2020 editions are also available at 6 months no-cost EMI option. Check out all the deals from this dedicated page on Amazon India.
