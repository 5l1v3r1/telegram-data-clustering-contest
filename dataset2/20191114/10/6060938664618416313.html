Ayushmann Khurrana and Nushrat Bharucha's 'Dream Girl' is all set to release in Hong Kong

14 Nov 2019, 10:33 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

After winning the National Award for the best actor, Ayushmann Khurrana looks adamant to make a global impact as his film 'Dream Girl' which also featured Nushrat Bharucha is all set to release in Hong Kong.

According to a report, the makers of the film have now collaborated with a Hong Kong-based entertainment organisation with the help of whom they'll be releasing the film on 5th December this year.

While Ayushmann's recent release 'Bala' is already breaking several records in the domestic circuit, the versatile actor's 'Dream Girl' will be the fifth Bollywood film to release in Hong Kong.

The film which revolves around Ayushmann's character of a man with the talent of impersonating a woman's voice with ease and how he takes up a job which explores his talent.

The Raaj Shaandilyaa directorial which even featured the likes of Annu Kapoor, Manjot Singh, and Vijay Raaz in pivotal roles was a huge hit in India and managed to rake in huge numbers at the box office as well.
