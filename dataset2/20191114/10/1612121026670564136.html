Sony is preparing to launch the Xperia 3 with a curved back and 5G support

14 Nov 2019, 10:00 by Erik Slaven

Although Sony’s smartphone division isn’t performing well with sales down 32% in the third quarter, the company is moving full steam ahead with flagships. Following both the Xperia 1 and Xperia 5, the Xperia 3 will be its latest high-end device, sporting an interesting curved back with a flat screen. 5G support will also be onboard. The curved design is reminiscent of the Xperia Arc from way back in 2011.

The processor is expected to be a Qualcomm Snapdragon 856 and the OLED screen will have the CinemaWide 21:9 aspect ratio like their recent devices. Whether or not it’s upgraded to a 90Hz panel for faster refresh rates remains to be seen. There will be three cameras as well, but additional specs are still unknown.
