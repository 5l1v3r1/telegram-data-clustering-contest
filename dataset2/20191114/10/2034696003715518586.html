Eminem responds to track referencing Rihanna assault

14 Nov 2019, 10:15

The song is said to have been recorded a decade ago

Eminem has reportedly responded to a song leak in which he appeared to “side with Chris Brown” over assaulting Rihanna.

Earlier this week (November 11), a Reddit user uploaded a seven-second clip titled ‘(SNIPPET) Eminem – Things Get Worse (Rihanna Diss)’ last week, which is said to have been recorded during sessions for Marshall Mathers’ 2009 album, ‘Relapse’.

“Of course I side with Chris Brown, I’d beat a bitch down too,” spits Eminem in the track which went on to become B.o.B’s ‘Things Get Worse’ in 2011.

Despite the line being removed for the final version, the rapper does say he “murdered” Dakota Fanning – who was then 16 years old – and “put Natasha Bedingfield in a washer” in the release.

Now, former Interscope executive Dennis Dennehy, released a statement addressing the track to XXL. He said: “After Eminem recorded it, he scrapped it and rewrote it. He and Rihanna have a great relationship.”
