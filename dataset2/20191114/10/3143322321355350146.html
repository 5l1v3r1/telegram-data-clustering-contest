WEATHER WARNING: Damaging hail possibly heading for Mbombela

Make sure your car is under cover. Lower your speed and ensure your lights or hazards are turned on for increased visibility.

14 Nov 2019, 10:46 by Stefan de Villiers

Various insurance companies have warned that a hail storm, that already hit parts of the R40 towards Barberton a while ago, is possibly heading to Mbombela.
