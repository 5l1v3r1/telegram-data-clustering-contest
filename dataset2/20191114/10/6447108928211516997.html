The Crew 2 update 1.5.0 brings the Blazing Shots

14 Nov 2019, 10:44 by Aran Suddi

Ubisoft has released a brand new update for The Crew 2 which adds the Blazing Shots contents pack for free. The Blazing Shots pack adds 17 live summits over five weeks with these being focused around events such as The Mix Master, Ghost Road, H2O, and Street Fight. There are also twelve new vehicles including the Bugatti Veyron 16.4, the Citreon GT, and the Mazda RX8, which will all be rolled out gradually. 78 vanity items have also been added.

Some of the fixes include not being able to break the top speed of drag vehicles, and monster trucks won’t be glitching through the arena stairs. The full list of changes can be seen below.
