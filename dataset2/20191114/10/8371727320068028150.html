Huawei Y9s Officially Launched in Kenya

14 Nov 2019, 10:04 by George Kamau

Early today, Huawei officially launched the Huawei Y9s and announced the Band 4 smartwatch series. Huawei recently announced they’ll be bringing these two devices, the Huawei Y9s and the Band 4 to the Kenyan market and now they’re finally here.

The Huawei Y9s is the company’s first phone in Kenya to rock a side-mounted fingerprint sensor.
