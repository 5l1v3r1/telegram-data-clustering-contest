Portugal Star: Ronaldo Doesn't Look Injured!

14 Nov 2019, 10:34 by Kurt Buckerfield

Following Cristiano Ronaldo's recent tantrums and Maurizio Sarri's claims the Portuguese forward is struggling with an injury, one of the five-time Ballon d'Or winner's Portugal teammates has claimed the Juventus striker appears fully fit in national team training.

Is Ronaldo Really Injured Or Is Sarri Lying?

The 34-year-old has been substituted in two consecutive matches for the Bianconeri, with Sarri claiming, on both occasions, that he has been carrying a knock.

Ronaldo's reactions to being taken off has suggested otherwise, however, especially this past weekend, when he was replaced in the 55th minute against AC Milan.

The former Real Madrid man kicked up a fuss after Sarri's decision to withdraw him from the match, heading straight down the tunnel and leaving the stadium early, as per reports in Italy.

On Wednesday, a publication in Portugal claimed that Ronaldo had trained normally with his international colleagues, showing no signs of an injury.

Eintracht Frankfurt forward Goncalo Paciencia, 25, has since seemingly confirmed the report, insisting his teammate looks to be completely fine in national team training this week.

"I don't have a medical degree, but he's looked fine to me," he told Portuguese newspaper A Bola.

"The most important thing is that he's here because he's the best in the world."
