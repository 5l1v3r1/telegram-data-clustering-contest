3 PSL Clubs Track Ex-Matsatsantsa Defender

14 Nov 2019, 10:45 by Soccer Laduma

A former SuperSport United defender could be back in the Absa Premiership, with interest said to be pouring in for his services. 

Following a stint with Platinum Stars (Cape Umoya) in the 2017/18 season, having joined the club from Matsatsantsa, Luvolwethu Mpeta went on to team up with the University of Pretoria to revive his career this season.

News reaching the Siya crew is that the 29-year-old left-back is monitored by Bloemfontein Celtic, Bidvest Wits and Polokwane City. 

“Celtic, Wits and Polokwane City have been monitoring him,” confirmed the source.

The source also added that the player has recovered from the injury setback that hindered his progress earlier in the season. 

“He has recovered fully and is in contention for game-time with Tuks,” the source added.
