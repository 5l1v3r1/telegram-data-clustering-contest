14 seasons of 'Idols SA' winners - who will join them this year?

14 Nov 2019, 06:56

Cape Town – On Sunday the search for this year's South African Idol will come to an end when a new winner is crowned.

The top two Luyolo Yiba and Sneziey Msomi will face off against each other for the last time this season in the grand finale.

Ahead of the final episode we take a look back at all the Idols winners who have come before them.

Not only will the winner walk away with the title but also an awesome prize package consisting of a record deal with Gallo, R1 million in cash, Huawei Matebook X, GT watch, B525 Router, and 20 GB of Telkom Data, a Ford Fiesta 1.0 Ecoboost, R100 000 Truworths voucher, and R80 000 Yahama music instruments.

GET TO KNOW THE FINALISTS BETTER

Luyolo (24), was born in Buffalo River. He wishes he had superpowers and could multiply objects so that he could reproduce money and help those in need. Winning the show for Luyolo would be about showing appreciation to everyone who supported him.
