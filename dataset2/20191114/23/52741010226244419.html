Police locate stolen vehicle, then cocaine

The vehicle was stolen from York Region back in October

14 Nov 2019, 23:15 by KitchenerToday Staff

Waterloo Regional Police have made an arrest after recovering a stolen vehicle.

The vehicle was stolen from York Region back on October 24.

It was located on Chandler Drive in Kitchener on Thursday.

During their investigation, police also seized suspected cocaine.

A 22-year-old man is charged with possession of stolen property over $5,000 and possession for the purpose of trafficking.
