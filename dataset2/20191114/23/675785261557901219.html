Olivia Attwood shares magical moment she said 'yes' to fiancé Bradley Dack's proposal

The TOWIE bombshell celebrated one month of being engaged by sharing a glimpse at her romantic proposal in Dubai

14 Nov 2019, 23:47 by Tiffany Wallis

Olivia Attwood has teased an insight to her romantic proposal with footballer beau Bradley Dack from last month.

The TOWIE bombshell, 28, previously revealed that her boyfriend Bradley popped the question in October while they were holidaying in Dubai and she has now shared emotional footage from the big day.

The reality TV star, who rose to fame on Love Island , posted a series of clips to Instagram on Thursday, showing the moment Bradley, 25, got down on one knee during their romantic dinner on the beach.

The football star looked unable to contain his emotions as he declared his love for the blonde beauty.

He asked the all-important question: "I love you more than anything in the whole world.

"Will you marry me?"

Tears started streaming for Olivia as she hilariously questioned her beau.

She stuttered: "Brad, are you joking? Are you serious?"

"Why? Are you sure?!"

The loved-up couple embraced in a romantic hug as she emotionally accepted his proposal.

She captioned the heartfelt Instagram post with a loving caption dedicated to her new fiancé.

Olivia penned: "One month since this magical moment (still not over it). I never dreamed I would find a love like this. How content I feel in life right now is actually such an alien feeling, but I never want it to end.

"All thanks to you Bradley Dack. Thank you also for not letting me get totally s***faced, so I was able to remember this moment, you always got my back."

The star then joked about what the footballer was getting himself in for.

She continued: "Please note my absolute disbelief at the fact someone wants to be stuck with me for life haha.. He cried himself to sleep later when he realised what he done.. lol.

"(I cut it down to keep some parts private and also there is only so much ugly crying you need to see) love you all."

The video received nearly 110K likes, with tonnes of her friends and fans rushing to the comment section.

Fellow Love Island star Amber Davies wrote: "'Why? Are you sure?' Is exactly what I’d expect you to say after Brad getting on one knee Hahahah I love this vid and I love you both so much. Roll on the wedding."

Former TOWIE star Ferne McCann commented: "Oh this is everything!"

Olivia's close friend from The Only Way Is Essex, Joey Turner, also replied to her sweet snap.

He wrote: "The 'are u sure' never fails to make me scream, love you. You deserve so much happiness." (sic)

Even her new fiancé commented on the post: " 'Are you sure?' love you babe xx" (sic)

Olivia and Bradley announced their engagement last month with a series of adorable Instagram photos.

One snap showed Blackburn Rovers star Bradley sweetly holding onto Olivia's hand - complete with shiny new jewellery - as they shared a passionate kiss.

In the other pic, Olivia laid a hand on Bradley's chest as they looked into the lens with huge grins on their faces.

The couple have been together on and off for four years, and she had been seeing him before her stint on Love Island, where she met now-ex Chris Hughes.

Just a few months ago, the TOWIE star dropped some surprising home truths about her relationship, including that she didn't completely trust her beau.

But the blonde bombshell confessed she was happy with some of Bradley's attributes — namely his "massive c**k" and "amazing bum".

Olivia told new! magazine: "We row about little things, like house-related things. I'm very OCD and he's very messy.

"I've also got a long way to go with trust. That's my own issue more than his. I don't think you should trust any man 100 per cent."

The couple met in 2015 but split in 2017 when Olivia went into the Love Island villa.

They had also previously hinted that they'd get engaged this year. Looks like they were right!
