14 Nov 2019, 23:19 by Lily Waddell, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/lily-waddell/

X Factor: Celebrity's Nicole Scherzinger looked sensational as always.

The X Factor personality, 41, turned heads on her arrival at the twinkling Steve Madde x Winnie Harlow launch party, in London on Thursday.

Dramatic ruffled detailing on her shoulders drew attention to Nicole's hourglass shape while she partied the night away in her stunning LBD.

Most of her enviably-toned legs were on show when she stepped out in the thigh-grazing ensemble.

Silver shimmer outlined her eyes and made the pop star's dazzling appearance stand out as she hit the town.

The singer made a fashionable statement when she elevated her height with white heels to contrast with her LBD.

Nicole enjoyed a well-deserved night off after spending heaps of time filming for X Factor: Celebrity with music mogul Simon Cowell.

It comes as she prepares to reunite with The Pussycat Dolls in a spectacular comeback during a surprise performance on X Factor : Celebrity.

Rumours were swirling the band would make a return to the stage for their first gig in more than a decade, according to the The Sun .

A source claimed: “The X Factor ­producers approached Nicole about the idea of performing with the girls and she jumped at the chance.

“It’s going to be their first big ­performance of the reunion and will be extra special for Nicole because of her role in the show.

“Having the Pussycat Dolls perform is a real coup for Simon Cowell and he has told Nicole to pull out all the stops to make sure their routine gets people talking.”

The Pussycat Dolls, made up of Nicole, Meoldy Thornton, Kimberley Wyatt, Ashley Roberts, Carmit Bachar and Jessica Sutta, split officially in 2010.

They all went onto chase their dreams of solo singing careers.
